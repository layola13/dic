savagers:/ˈsævɪdʒərz/ | n. |野蛮人，未开化的人，粗鲁的人 |People considered to be uncivilized or violent.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying brutality, lack of refinement, and disregard for societal norms.  |例句：The explorers encountered a tribe of savagers deep in the jungle. = 探险家们在丛林深处遇到了一群野蛮人。

The ancient Romans considered the barbarian tribes to be savagers. = 古罗马人认为蛮族部落是野蛮人。


近义词：barbarians, brutes, thugs, savages, ruffians |反义词：civilized people, cultured people, refined people |名词
