causality:/ˌkɔːˈzæləti/| n. |因果关系；因果性 |The philosophical study of cause and effect, and the relationships between them.  It explores how events are connected and how one event can lead to another.  Causality is a fundamental concept in science, philosophy, and everyday life. |例句：The study explored the causality between smoking and lung cancer.  这项研究探讨了吸烟和肺癌之间的因果关系。

例句：Understanding causality is crucial for making informed decisions.  理解因果关系对于做出明智的决定至关重要。


近义词：cause and effect, consequence, correlation (Note: correlation does not equal causality) |反义词：randomness, chance, coincidence |名词
