effect's:/ɪˈfɛkts/ | n. |效果的，影响的 |The word "effect's" is a shortened form of "effect is" or "effect has," representing a possessive form of the noun "effect."  It indicates that the effect belongs to something or is related to something.  The usage is largely informal and often found in casual writing or speech. |The effect's impact was immediate. = 这个影响是即时的。| results, consequences, outcome |cause, reason |noun
