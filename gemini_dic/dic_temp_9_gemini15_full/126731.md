nana:/ˈnænə/| n.|奶奶，外婆|A grandmother, especially one's maternal grandmother.|例句：My nana used to tell me stories.  我的奶奶过去经常给我讲故事。|近义词：grandmother, grandma, granny|反义词:grandchild


USAGE->

My nana bakes delicious cookies. = 我的奶奶烘焙美味的饼干。
