rifting:/ˈrɪftɪŋ/| v.|裂隙；分裂；不和|The process of splitting or cracking, especially of rocks or landmasses; also refers to a growing discord or estrangement between people or groups.|例句：The rifting of the continents millions of years ago formed the Atlantic Ocean.数百万年前大陆的裂解形成了大西洋。|例句：The rifting between the two political parties is hindering progress. 两大政党之间的裂痕阻碍了进步。|近义词：splitting, fracturing, dividing, breaking apart, estranging|反义词：uniting, unifying, joining, merging, reconciling|
