duos:/ˈduːoʊz/| n.|二重奏；两人一组|A duo is a musical composition or performance for two performers, or a pair of people working together.  It can also refer to a pair of things or people. | Example: The piano duo played a beautiful piece.  钢琴二重奏演奏了一首优美的乐曲。The marketing duo came up with a brilliant campaign.  营销二人组想出了一个绝妙的方案。|近义词：pair, couple, team|反义词：solo, single
