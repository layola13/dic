choosiest:/ˈtʃuːziɪst/ | adj. | 最挑剔的，最难以取悦的 | Most selective or fastidious; extremely picky.  It is the superlative form of "choosy". | 例句：She's the choosiest eater I know; she'll only eat plain pasta. 她是我认识的最挑剔的食客；她只吃清淡的意大利面。

She is the choosiest customer in the store,  returning almost everything she buys. 她是店里最挑剔的顾客，几乎退回她买的所有东西。

| 近义词：fastidious, finicky, picky, particular, discerning | 反义词：undemanding, uncritical, accepting
