powertrain:/ˈpaʊərtreɪn/| n. |动力传动系统|A powertrain is the group of components that deliver power from an engine to the wheels of a vehicle.  It typically includes the engine, transmission, driveshaft, axles, and differential. |例句：The new car boasts a powerful and efficient powertrain. 这辆新车拥有一个强大而高效的动力传动系统。

例句：Modern powertrains are designed for fuel efficiency and reduced emissions. 现代动力传动系统的设计目标是提高燃油效率并减少排放。


近义词：drivetrain, drive system |反义词：无|词性:名词
