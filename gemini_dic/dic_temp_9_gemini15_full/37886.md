complainers:/kəmˈpleɪnərz/| n. |抱怨者，爱抱怨的人|People who frequently express dissatisfaction or discontent. They tend to focus on problems and negativity rather than solutions.  | Example: The manager had to deal with a group of persistent complainers about the new policy.  经理不得不处理一群对新政策持续抱怨的人。|近义词：critics, grumblers, whiners|反义词：praisers, complimenters
