likelier:/ˈlaɪkliər/| adj.| 更可能的；比较可能的 | More probable; more likely.  It suggests a higher degree of probability than "likely."|例句：Given the evidence, a guilty verdict seems likelier.  鉴于证据，有罪判决似乎更有可能。

例句：She is likelier to succeed if she works harder. 她如果更努力工作，成功的可能性更大。


近义词：more probable, more likely,  plausible

反义词：less likely, improbable, unlikely

词性：形容词
