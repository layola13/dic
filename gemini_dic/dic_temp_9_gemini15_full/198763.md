/ˈsɪknɪs/| n. |疾病，恶心；不适|The word "sickness" refers to a state of being ill; a disease or ailment.  It can also refer to a feeling of nausea or queasiness. |例句：Her sickness kept her from attending the party.  她的疾病使她无法参加聚会。


例句：The smell caused a sudden sickness in his stomach.  那股气味使他突然感到恶心。|近义词：illness, disease, ailment, malady |反义词：health, wellness|
