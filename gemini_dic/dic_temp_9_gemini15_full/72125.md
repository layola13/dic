flood:/flʌd/ /flʌd/| n., v.|洪水；泛滥；涌入 | A large amount of water covering an area that is usually dry.  |例句：The heavy rain caused a flood in the town. 大雨导致该镇发生洪水。|例句：The streets were flooded after the storm. 暴风雨过后，街道被淹了。|近义词：deluge, inundation, overflow |反义词：drought |词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river floods every spring. = 这条河每年春天都会泛滥。
Floods often occur in this area. = 这个地区经常发生洪水。


一般过去时:
The flood destroyed many homes. = 洪水摧毁了许多房屋。
The dam broke and flooded the valley. = 水坝决堤，淹没了山谷。


现在完成时:
The river has flooded three times this year. = 这条河今年已经泛滥三次了。
The area has been flooded several times in recent history. = 该地区在近代历史上曾多次被洪水淹没。


过去完成时:
The house had already flooded before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，房子已经被淹了。
The river had never flooded so high before. = 这条河以前从未泛滥到这么高。


将来时:
The forecast predicts that the river will flood next week. = 预报显示这条河下周将要泛滥。
If the rain continues, the town will be flooded. = 如果雨继续下，这个镇将会被淹没。


虚拟语气:
If the dam had not broken, the town would not have flooded. = 如果水坝没有决堤，这个镇就不会被淹了。
I wish the flood hadn't damaged our crops. = 我真希望洪水没有毁坏我们的庄稼。


被动语态:
The town was flooded by the rising river. = 这个镇被上涨的河水淹没了。
Many houses are being flooded right now. = 现在许多房屋正在被淹。


疑问句:
Has the river flooded yet? = 河水已经泛滥了吗？
Will the flood damage the bridge? = 洪水会损坏桥梁吗？


否定句:
The river didn't flood this year. = 河水今年没有泛滥。
The authorities prevented the flood from damaging the city. = 当局阻止了洪水对城市的破坏。


PHRASE->
flood of = 大量；涌入
flood the market = 充斥市场
flood victim = 受灾者
flash flood = 突发洪水
control the flood = 控制洪水
