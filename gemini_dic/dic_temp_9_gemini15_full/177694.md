recancel:/ˌriːˈkænsəl/| vt.|重新取消；再次取消|To cancel something that has already been canceled.  This often implies a reversal of a previous cancellation, or a second cancellation of the same thing.|例句：The flight was canceled, then re-canceled, leaving passengers stranded. 航班被取消了，然后又被重新取消了，导致乘客滞留。  The meeting was re-canceled due to unforeseen circumstances. 会议由于不可预见的情况再次被取消。|近义词：countermand, revoke, annul|反义词：confirm, reinstate, approve|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-cancel the order every week. = 他们每周都重新取消订单。
He re-cancels his subscription every month. = 他每月都重新取消订阅。

一般过去时:
She re-canceled her appointment yesterday. = 她昨天重新取消了她的预约。
They re-canceled the project last year. = 他们去年重新取消了该项目。

现在完成时:
We have re-canceled the trip three times. = 我们已经三次重新取消了这次旅行。
They have re-canceled the contract twice. = 他们已经两次重新取消了合同。

过去完成时:
He had re-canceled the booking before I called. = 我打电话之前他已经重新取消了预订。
She had re-canceled the flight before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，她已经重新取消了航班。

将来时:
They will re-cancel the event if the weather is bad. = 如果天气不好，他们将重新取消活动。
We will re-cancel the meeting if nobody shows up. = 如果没有人出现，我们将重新取消会议。

虚拟语气:
If they re-canceled the order, we would be in trouble. = 如果他们重新取消订单，我们会陷入困境。
I wish they hadn't re-canceled the concert. = 我真希望他们没有重新取消音乐会。

被动语态:
The order was re-canceled due to a lack of funds. = 订单因资金不足而被重新取消。
The reservation was re-canceled without explanation. = 预约未作解释就被重新取消了。

疑问句:
Was the meeting re-canceled? = 会议被重新取消了吗？
Has the flight been re-canceled? = 航班被重新取消了吗？

否定句:
The contract was not re-canceled. = 合同没有被重新取消。
They did not re-cancel their plans. = 他们没有重新取消他们的计划。
