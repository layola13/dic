maple:/ˈmeɪpl/| n.|枫树；枫木；枫糖浆|A maple is a tree with lobed leaves,  often producing a sweet sap used to make syrup.  The wood is also valuable for furniture and other uses.|例句：The autumn leaves on the maple trees were a stunning display of red and gold. 秋天枫树上的叶子呈现出令人惊艳的红金色。|例句：The table is made of beautiful maple wood. 这张桌子是用美丽的枫木制成的。|近义词：sycamore (某些种类)|反义词：无
