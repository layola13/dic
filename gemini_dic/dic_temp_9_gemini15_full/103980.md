juggernaut:/ˈdʒʌɡənɔːt/|/ˈdʒʌɡənɔːt/| n. |不可阻挡的力量；巨大而不可抗拒的事物|A juggernaut is a large, powerful, and overwhelming force that crushes everything in its path.  It can refer to a destructive force of nature, a powerful organization, or an unstoppable trend.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting something destructive or overwhelming. |例句：The company's marketing campaign was a juggernaut, quickly dominating the market. 这家公司的营销活动势不可挡，迅速主导了市场。

例句：The advancing army was an unstoppable juggernaut, crushing all resistance.  这支前进的军队势不可挡，摧毁了一切抵抗。


近义词： behemoth, colossus, leviathan, force, power, irresistible force |反义词：weakness, impotence, insignificance |名词
