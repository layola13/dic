nontheoretically: /ˌnɑnθiːəˈrɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 非理论上地；实际上 |  This adverb indicates something is true or done in practice, not just in theory.  It emphasizes the practical or real-world aspect in contrast to a theoretical or hypothetical one. | Example:  Nontheoretically, the system works well, even though the initial model predicted otherwise.  实际上，该系统运行良好，尽管最初的模型预测并非如此。 | 近义词: practically, in practice, empirically, actually | 反义词: theoretically, hypothetically
