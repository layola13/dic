checkwork:/ˈtʃɛkˌwɜːrk/| n.|检查工作，复核工作|The act of reviewing or verifying work already completed to ensure accuracy and completeness.  It often involves examining details, correcting errors, and confirming compliance with standards.|例句：The manager requested a thorough checkwork of the financial reports before submission. 经理要求在提交财务报表之前进行彻底的检查工作。|例句：The quality control team performs a rigorous checkwork on each product before it leaves the factory. 质量控制小组在每个产品出厂前都进行严格的检查工作。|近义词：review, inspection, verification, audit|反义词：neglect, oversight|名词
