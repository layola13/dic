condescending: /ˌkɒndɪˈsɛndɪŋ/| adj. | 屈尊俯就的；盛气凌人的；高傲的 |  Showing or characterized by a patronizingly superior attitude toward those considered inferior.  It implies a feeling of superiority and a lack of genuine respect. |


例句：

He spoke to me in a condescending tone, as if I were a child.  他用一种屈尊俯就的语气对我说话，好像我是个孩子。

Her condescending remarks about my work really hurt my feelings. 她那轻蔑的评论伤害了我的感情。


近义词：patronizing, superior, arrogant, supercilious, disdainful


反义词：humble, respectful, modest, deferential


词性:形容词
