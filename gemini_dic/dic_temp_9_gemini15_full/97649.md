inheritrix: /ɪnˈhɛrɪtrɪks/ | n. | 女继承人 | A female heir; a woman who inherits property or a title.  | Example:  She is the sole inheritrix of her father's vast estate. 她是她父亲巨额遗产的唯一继承人。 | 近义词: heiress, legatee | 反义词: testator, bequeather


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The inheritrix owns a large company. = 女继承人拥有一家大公司。
She inherits her grandmother's jewellery. = 她继承了她祖母的珠宝。


一般过去时:
The inheritrix received her inheritance last year. = 女继承人去年收到了她的遗产。
She inherited a large fortune from her uncle. = 她继承了她叔叔的一大笔财产。


现在完成时:
The inheritrix has inherited several properties. = 女继承人已经继承了几处房产。
She has inherited a title and a large estate. = 她继承了一个头衔和一大笔遗产。


过去完成时:
Before her father's death, the inheritrix had already planned her future. = 在她父亲去世之前，女继承人已经规划好了她的未来。
She had never expected to inherit so much money. = 她从未想过会继承这么多钱。


将来时:
The inheritrix will manage the family business. = 女继承人将管理家族企业。
She will inherit the family mansion next year. = 她明年将继承家族宅邸。


虚拟语气:
If she were the inheritrix, she would travel the world. = 如果她是继承人，她就会周游世界。
I wish I were the inheritrix of that fortune. = 我希望我是那笔财产的继承人。


被动语态:
The estate was inherited by the inheritrix. = 这笔遗产由女继承人继承。
The family business will be inherited by the youngest inheritrix. = 家族企业将由最小的女继承人继承。


疑问句:
Is she the inheritrix of the estate? = 她是不是这笔遗产的继承人？

否定句:
She is not the sole inheritrix of the property. = 她不是这处房产的唯一继承人。
She did not inherit anything from her parents. = 她没有继承父母任何东西。
