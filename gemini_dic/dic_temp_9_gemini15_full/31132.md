chanceries:/ˈtʃænsəriːz/| n.|外交部；政府部门；大法官法庭；文书保管处|The offices of an embassy or similar diplomatic mission.  A chancery is also the office where the official documents of a court of law are kept.|例句：The embassy's chancery was heavily guarded.  大使馆的外交部戒备森严。

The chancery issued a statement denying the allegations.  外交部发表声明否认这些指控。|近义词：embassy, consulate, office, department|反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would depend on the specific context (e.g., if referring to a court, the opposite might be a courtroom).|名词
