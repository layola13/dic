self-centred:/ˈsɛlfˌsɛntərd/| adj.|以自我为中心的，自私的|Self-centred people are primarily concerned with their own needs and desires, often at the expense of others. They may lack empathy and consideration for others' feelings.  |例句：He's so self-centred; he never thinks about anyone else's feelings. 他太以自我为中心了；他从不考虑别人的感受。|例句：Her self-centred behaviour alienated her friends. 她以自我为中心的举动疏远了她的朋友们。|近义词：selfish, egotistical, narcissistic, self-absorbed|反义词：altruistic, selfless, unselfish, considerate|形容词
