reformats:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmæts/ | vt. |重新格式化|To change the format of (something), especially a computer file or disk.|The software automatically reformats the document.|近义词： reformat, restructure |反义词：disorganize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The software reformats the document automatically. = 软件自动重新格式化文档。
He reformats his hard drive every six months. = 他每六个月重新格式化一次硬盘。

一般过去时:
I reformatted my hard drive yesterday. = 我昨天重新格式化了我的硬盘。
She reformatted the text to make it clearer. = 她重新格式化了文本以使其更清晰。

现在完成时:
I have reformatted my computer several times this year. = 今年我已经重新格式化了我的电脑几次。
She has reformatted the report to meet the new guidelines. = 她已经重新格式化了报告以符合新的指南。

过去完成时:
I had reformatted my computer before installing the new operating system. = 在安装新操作系统之前，我已经重新格式化了我的电脑。
They had reformatted the data before running the analysis. = 他们在运行分析之前已经重新格式化了数据。

将来时:
I will reformat my hard drive next week. = 我下周将重新格式化我的硬盘。
We will reformat the entire database. = 我们将重新格式化整个数据库。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reformat my entire hard drive. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新格式化我的整个硬盘。
It would be helpful if you reformatted the document for better readability. = 如果你重新格式化文档以提高可读性，那就很有帮助。

被动语态:
The hard drive was reformatted by the technician. = 硬盘被技术人员重新格式化了。

疑问句:
