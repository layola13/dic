keyboardists:/ˈkiːbɔːrdɪsts/ | n. |键盘演奏家，键盘手 | A keyboardist is a musician who plays keyboard instruments such as pianos, organs, synthesizers, or keyboards. They are often found in bands, orchestras, or as solo performers.  | Example:  The band's keyboardist played a stunning solo during the concert. 乐队的键盘手在音乐会上演奏了一段令人惊叹的独奏。  The talented keyboardists impressed the audience with their skills.  才华横溢的键盘手们以其精湛的技艺给观众留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：pianist, organist, synthesizer player |反义词：无
