ingrate:/ˈɪŋɡreɪt/| n.|忘恩负义的人|An ingrate is a person who shows no gratitude; someone who is unthankful for kindness received.  They are often characterized by their lack of appreciation and often act selfishly.|例句：He's a complete ingrate; after all I've done for him, he didn't even thank me. 他是个十足的忘恩负义的人；我为他做了这么多，他甚至都没感谢我。|例句：It's incredibly rude to be such an ingrate to those who have helped you. 对那些帮助过你的人如此忘恩负义，真是太粗鲁了。|近义词：ungrateful person, wretch, cad, scoundrel|反义词：grateful person, thankful person|名词
