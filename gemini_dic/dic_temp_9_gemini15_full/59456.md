earworm:/ˈɪəwɜːrm/| n.| 挥之不去的歌曲（旋律）|A song, tune, or phrase that repeatedly enters one's head.  It is an involuntary and often unwelcome phenomenon. |例句：That catchy tune has become a real earworm; I can't get it out of my head. 那首朗朗上口的曲子成了挥之不去的旋律；我脑子里一直挥之不去。
例句：The advertisement's jingle is such an earworm, it's driving me crazy! 广告的铃声真是个让人烦的耳虫，它快把我逼疯了！|近义词：stuck song, brainworm, mental jingle |反义词：silence, quiet
