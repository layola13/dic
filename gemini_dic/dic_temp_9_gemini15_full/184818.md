resuscitations:/ˌrɛzˌʌsɪˈteɪʃənz/| n. |复苏；抢救；复活|The act of reviving someone from apparent death or unconsciousness.  It involves restoring vital functions like breathing and heartbeat.  The plural form indicates multiple instances of such procedures.|例句：The paramedics performed several resuscitations before the patient finally regained consciousness. = 急救人员进行了数次抢救，患者最终才恢复意识。


例句：The hospital's success rate for cardiac resuscitations is very high. = 这家医院的心脏复苏成功率很高。


近义词：revivals, revivings, restorations


反义词：deaths, fatalities


词性：名词
