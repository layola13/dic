manner:/ˈmænər/ | n. | 方法；方式；态度；举止 | The way in which something is done or happens.  |例句：He spoke in a calm manner. 他以平静的方式说话。|例句：Her manner was quite abrupt. 她的态度相当唐突。|近义词：method, way, style, approach, bearing |反义词：disorder, chaos


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He speaks in a polite manner. = 他以礼貌的方式说话。
She handles the situation with a calm manner. = 她以冷静的方式处理这种情况。

一般过去时:
He addressed the issue in a professional manner. = 他以专业的方式处理了这个问题。
She behaved in a strange manner yesterday. = 她昨天举止古怪。

现在完成时:
They have always conducted business in a fair manner. = 他们一直以公平的方式进行业务。
She has approached the problem in a thoughtful manner. = 她以深思熟虑的方式处理了这个问题。

过去完成时:
He had completed the task in a timely manner. = 他已及时完成了任务。
She had responded to the criticism in a dignified manner. = 她以尊严的方式回应了批评。

将来时:
He will approach the negotiation in a firm manner. = 他将以坚定的方式进行谈判。
She will address the audience in a confident manner. = 她将以自信的方式向观众讲话。

虚拟语气:
If he were to behave in a more respectful manner, people would like him more. = 如果他表现得更有礼貌一些，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she had handled the situation in a more tactful manner. = 我希望她能以更巧妙的方式处理这种情况。

被动语态:
The task was completed in a satisfactory manner. = 任务已令人满意地完成。
The meeting was conducted in a formal manner. = 会议以正式的方式进行。

疑问句:
In what manner did he express his displeasure? = 他以什么方式表达他的不满？
How did she handle the situation, in what manner? = 她如何处理这种情况，以什么方式？

否定句:
He did not handle the situation in a professional manner. = 他没有以专业的方式处理这种情况。
She did not respond in a timely manner. = 她没有及时回应。


PHRASE->
in a manner of speaking = 可以这么说
in a similar manner = 以类似的方式
in this manner = 以这种方式
after a fashion/manner = 勉强；粗略地
in no manner = 决不
