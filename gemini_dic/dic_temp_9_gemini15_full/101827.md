irritatory: /ɪˈrɪtəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 刺激的；令人恼怒的 | Causing irritation or annoyance.  Something that is irritatory is likely to cause anger, frustration, or discomfort. | The constant dripping of the faucet was irritatory.  不断滴水的龙头令人恼火。 | annoying, bothersome, vexing | soothing, calming, pleasant | 形容词
