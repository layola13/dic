cocultured:/ˈkoʊkʌltʃərd/| adj.| 共同培养的；共培养的 |  Describes cells, tissues, or organisms that are grown together in a shared culture medium.  This technique is used to study cell-cell interactions and other biological processes. | Example: The researchers cocultured human and mouse cells to investigate the effects of a new drug.  研究人员共同培养人类和老鼠细胞以研究一种新药的效果。 |近义词：jointly cultured, mixed cultured | 反义词： monocultured
