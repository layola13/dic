prepractice:/ˈpriːpræktɪs/| n. |预先练习；预演|Preliminary practice or rehearsal, often done in preparation for a performance or event.  It emphasizes the preparatory nature of the practice.|例句：The orchestra held a prepractice session before the concert.  乐队在音乐会之前进行了一次预演。

例句：The actors engaged in prepractice to refine their lines and blocking. 演员们进行了预演以完善他们的台词和走位。


近义词：rehearsal, preparation, dry run


反义词：performance, final run, actual event


词性：名词
