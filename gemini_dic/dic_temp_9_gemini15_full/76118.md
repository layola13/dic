furthest:/ˈfɜːðɪst/| adj.|最远的，最远处的|The furthest point of the journey was the summit of the mountain.  旅途中最远的地方是山顶。|近义词：farthest, most distant|反义词：nearest, closest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The furthest point is marked on the map. = 最远点在地图上标明了。
This is the furthest I can walk today. = 今天我能走的最远距离就是这儿了。


一般过去时:
The furthest he ever travelled was to Australia. = 他去过最远的地方是澳大利亚。
She walked furthest in the race. = 她在比赛中跑得最远。


现在完成时:
I have walked the furthest distance this year. = 今年我走的最远。
They have travelled furthest among us.  = 他们是我们当中走的最远的。


过去完成时:
He had walked the furthest by the time the others stopped. = 在其他人停下来的时候，他已经走了最远。
We had already reached the furthest point before it got dark. = 天黑之前，我们就已经到达了最远点。


将来时:
We will travel furthest next year. = 我们明年将会走得最远。
He will go furthest in his career. = 他将在他的职业生涯中走得最远。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would travel furthest. = 如果我有车，我会走得更远。
I wish I could travel furthest in the world. = 我希望我能周游世界。


被动语态:
The furthest point was reached by the explorers. = 探险家们到达了最远点。

疑问句:

否定句:
