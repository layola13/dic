oneandone:/ˌwʌn ænd wʌn/ | n. | 一加一 |  Oneandone refers to the concept of 1 + 1, often used metaphorically to represent synergy, where the combined effect is greater than the sum of its parts. It can also be used literally to represent the mathematical operation of addition.  | Example:  Their collaboration was a true oneandone; together, they achieved far more than either could have done alone.  他们的合作是真正的“一加一大于二”；他们一起取得的成就远远超过任何一方单独所能取得的。 |近义词: synergy, collaboration, combined effect |反义词: antagonism, opposition, conflict | 名词
