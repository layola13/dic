leaves:/liːvz/ | n. | 叶子；树叶 | Leaves are the main organs of photosynthesis in plants.  叶子是植物进行光合作用的主要器官。 |近义词：foliage,leaflets |反义词：trunk,branch


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The tree leaves turn golden in autumn. = 秋天树叶变黄。
These leaves are falling from the tree. = 这些叶子从树上掉下来。


一般过去时:
The leaves changed color last week. = 叶子在上周变色了。
She raked the leaves from the yard. = 她把院子里的落叶耙走了。


现在完成时:
The leaves have fallen from the trees. = 叶子已经从树上落下来了。
The gardener has collected all the leaves. = 园丁已经收集了所有的树叶。


过去完成时:
The leaves had already fallen before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，叶子已经落光了。
We had swept the leaves away before it rained. = 下雨之前我们已经把叶子扫走了。


将来时:
The leaves will change color soon. = 叶子很快就会变色。
The wind will blow the leaves away. = 风会把叶子吹走。


虚拟语气:
If the leaves were green, the tree would be healthy. = 如果叶子是绿色的，这棵树就会很健康。
I wish the leaves hadn't fallen so early this year. = 我希望今年的叶子不要这么早就落下来。


被动语态:
The leaves are swept up by the wind. = 叶子被风卷走了。
The leaves were collected by the children. = 叶子被孩子们收集起来了。


疑问句:
Are the leaves changing color yet? = 叶子开始变色了吗？
How many leaves are on that tree? = 那棵树上有多少片叶子？


否定句:
The leaves aren't changing color yet. = 叶子还没有变色。
The leaves did not fall until late autumn. = 叶子直到深秋才落。

leave no stone unturned = 千方百计，不遗余力
