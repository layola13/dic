consummateness:/kənˈsʌmətnəs/ | n. |完美；圆满；极致 |The state or quality of being consummate; perfection; completeness.  It implies a high degree of skill, excellence, or completeness.  The word evokes a sense of finality and achievement, suggesting something brought to its ultimate form. |例句：The consummateness of her performance captivated the audience.  她的表演的完美征服了观众。

The consummateness of the ancient temple's architecture is breathtaking. 古庙建筑的完美令人叹为观止。


近义词：perfection, completeness, excellence, mastery, flawlessness


反义词：imperfection, incompleteness, deficiency, inadequacy


词性：名词
