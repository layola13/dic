calcareous:/kælˈkeriəs/| adj.|含钙的，石灰质的|Containing calcium carbonate; chalky.  The calcareous soil is rich in minerals.  |近义词：chalky, limy, calcitic |反义词：siliceous


例句：

The calcareous cliffs were breathtaking.
含钙的悬崖令人叹为观止。

The calcareous deposits in the cave were stunning.
洞穴中石灰质的沉积物令人惊叹。


词源:

From Latin *calx (genitive calcis), "lime,"  + -aceous, a suffix denoting "pertaining to".  The word ultimately derives from Proto-Indo-European *kel-, meaning "to burn". This refers to the process of burning limestone to produce lime.


USAGE->

(由于calcareous是形容词，没有动词变化，以下只展示形容词的用法)

一般陈述句:
The soil is calcareous. = 土壤是石灰质的。
These rocks are calcareous in nature. = 这些岩石本质上是石灰质的。

比较级:
This soil is more calcareous than that one. = 这块土壤比那块土壤更富含石灰质。
The limestone is less calcareous than the chalk. = 石灰石比白垩土含钙量少。

最高级:
This is the most calcareous region in the country. = 这是全国石灰质含量最高的地区。
Among these samples, this one is the most calcareous. = 在这些样本中，这个含钙量最高。
