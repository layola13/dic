dzeren:/ˈdʒɛrən/| n. |瞪羚|A species of gazelle found in central Asia.  It's known for its speed and graceful movements.  The word is of Mongolian origin.| 例句：The dzeren are a fascinating sight on the Mongolian steppe. = 蒙古草原上的瞪羚是一道迷人的风景线。

The dzeren population has been declining in recent years due to habitat loss. = 近年来，由于栖息地丧失，瞪羚的数量一直在下降。| 近义词： gazelle, antelope | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but perhaps) predator,  endangered species | 名词
