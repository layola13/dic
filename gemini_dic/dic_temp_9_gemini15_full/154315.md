/ˈpɛrɪtrəpəs/| adj. |倒生的，倒生的；胚珠倒生的| Relating to or characterized by inverted ovules. In botany, it refers to an ovule that is attached to the placenta at the base, with the micropyle pointing towards the placenta. This is in contrast to an orthotropous ovule, where the micropyle points away from the placenta.|The peritropous ovule is a type of anatropous ovule with a curvature.|倒生胚珠是一种类型的胚珠，具有弯曲的特点。|近义词：anatropous (often used interchangeably)|反义词：orthotropous
