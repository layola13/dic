on-your-part:/ˌɒn jɔːr ˈpɑːrt/|/ˌɑːn jʊər ˈpɑːrt/| adj. phrase|就你而言，你的方面|This phrase indicates responsibility or action taken by a particular person.  It emphasizes the individual's role or contribution in a situation.|例句：The delay was entirely on your part.  = 延误完全是你的责任。

例句：The misunderstanding was entirely on your part. = 误解完全出在你身上。


近义词：from your perspective, your responsibility, your fault, your doing


反义词：on my part, on their part (implying responsibility rests elsewhere)


词性: Adjective Phrase (形容词短语)
