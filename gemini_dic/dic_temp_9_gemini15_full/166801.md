prespecifying: /ˌpriːˈspesɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 预先指定；预先确定 |  The act of specifying something beforehand; determining or setting something in advance.  | Example: Prespecifying the parameters of the experiment ensured consistent results. 预先指定实验参数确保了结果的一致性。


The study prespecified the inclusion criteria for participants.  该研究预先指定了参与者的纳入标准。


近义词: predetermining, predefining, setting beforehand, establishing in advance


反义词:  postdetermining (less common), leaving unspecified, improvising


词性: 动词 (verb)


词源:  The word is formed by combining the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") with the verb "specifying" (from "specify," meaning "to state or name explicitly").  Thus, it literally means "specifying before."
