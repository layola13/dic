extrusive:/ɪkˈstruːsɪv/| adj.|喷发的；喷出的；外生的|Relating to or denoting igneous rocks that have solidified from lava that erupted at the Earth's surface, as opposed to intrusive rocks, which solidified beneath the surface.|例句：Extrusive rocks are formed from lava that cools quickly on the surface. 喷出岩是由在地表快速冷却的熔岩形成的。|例句：The volcano's extrusive activity created a vast field of basalt. 火山的喷发活动形成了广阔的玄武岩地带。|近义词：effusive, volcanic|反义词：intrusive
