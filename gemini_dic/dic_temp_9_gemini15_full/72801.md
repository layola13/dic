fold-in:/foʊldˈɪn/| vt.|折叠放入；掺入；包入|To incorporate (one substance into another) by gently folding, usually with a spoon or spatula, rather than stirring.|例句：Gently fold in the whipped cream.  轻轻地把鲜奶油拌入。|例句：Fold the egg whites into the batter. 将蛋白轻轻地拌入面糊中。|近义词：stir in, mix in, incorporate|反义词：remove, take out
