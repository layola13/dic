naturalize:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使归化；使适应；使自然化 | To grant someone the rights and privileges of a citizen.  To make something seem natural or normal. |例句：He naturalized in the United States after living there for five years. 他在美国住了五年后就入了美国籍。  The garden was carefully designed to naturalize the landscape. 花园经过精心设计，使景观看起来很自然。 |近义词：acclimatize, assimilate, adopt | 反义词：denaturalize, alienate | 动词
