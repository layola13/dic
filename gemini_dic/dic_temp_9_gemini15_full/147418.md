overnourishing: /ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃɪŋ/ | v. | 过度喂养；营养过剩 |  The act of providing excessive nourishment or nutrients, often leading to negative consequences.  It can refer to plants, animals, or even humans. Overfeeding can lead to obesity, while over-fertilizing plants can harm them. |


例句：

The farmer's overnourishing of the crops led to weak stalks and reduced yields. = 农民过度施肥导致庄稼茎秆脆弱，产量降低。

Overfeeding your pet can lead to health problems like obesity and diabetes. = 过度喂养宠物会导致肥胖和糖尿病等健康问题。


近义词：overfeeding, overfertilizing


反义词：undernourishing, malnourishing


词性:动词
