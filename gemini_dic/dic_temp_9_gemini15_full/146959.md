overirrigate:/ˌoʊvərˈɪrɪɡeɪt/| vt.|过度灌溉|To water (plants, soil, etc.) excessively.  This can lead to various problems, including root rot, nutrient leaching, and wasted water.|例句：Overirrigating your lawn can damage the grass and attract pests. 过度灌溉你的草坪会损坏草坪并吸引害虫。

例句：The farmer overirrigated his fields, leading to a significant loss of crops.  这位农民过度灌溉了他的田地，导致了大量的作物损失。


近义词：water excessively, flood, inundate

反义词：underwater, dehydrate
