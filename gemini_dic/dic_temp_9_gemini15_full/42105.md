cottage:/ˈkɒtɪdʒ/ /ˈkɑːtɪdʒ/| n. |小屋，村舍 |A small house, typically one in the country.|例句：They live in a charming cottage by the sea. 他们住在海边一间迷人的小屋里。|例句：The cottage was surrounded by a beautiful garden. 小屋周围环绕着美丽的庭院。|近义词：cabin, hut, chalet |反义词：mansion, palace


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He owns a cottage in the countryside. = 他在乡下拥有一间小屋。
The cottage rents for $1000 a month. = 这间小屋每月租金1000美元。


一般过去时:
We stayed in a cozy cottage during our vacation. = 我们在度假期间住在一间舒适的小屋里。
They visited a historic cottage in the village. = 他们参观了村里一间历史悠久的小屋。


现在完成时:
They have renovated their cottage. = 他们已经翻新了他们的乡村小屋。
We have spent many happy summers in that cottage. = 我们在那间小屋里度过了许多快乐的夏天。


过去完成时:
They had already left the cottage before we arrived. =在我们到达之前，他们已经离开了小屋。
She had lived in that cottage for twenty years before selling it. =她在卖掉那间小屋之前在那里住了20年。


将来时:
We will rent a cottage by the lake next summer. = 我们明年夏天将在湖边租一间小屋。
They will be building a new cottage on their property. = 他们将在他们的房产上建造一间新的小屋。


虚拟语气:
If I had a cottage, I would spend my weekends there. = 如果我有一间小屋，我会在周末在那里度过。
I wish I could afford to buy a seaside cottage. = 我希望我能买得起海边小屋。


被动语态:
The cottage was built in the 18th century. = 这间小屋建于18世纪。
The cottage is being repaired at the moment. = 小屋正在修理中。


疑问句:
