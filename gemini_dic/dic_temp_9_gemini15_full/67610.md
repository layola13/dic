extravenate:/ˈɛkstrəveɪneɪt/ | vt. | 血液外渗；血管外渗 | To escape or cause to escape from a vein or blood vessel.  The term often implies an unintentional or abnormal escape of blood. | Example: The trauma caused the blood to extravenate into the surrounding tissue.  创伤导致血液渗入周围组织。  The IV needle extravenated, causing bruising. 静脉注射针头渗漏，导致瘀伤。 | 近义词： extravasate, exude, ooze | 反义词：intravenate (inject into a vein) | 动词
