nonstellar: /ˌnɒnˈstɛlə(r)/ | adj. |非恒星的；非星状的| Not relating to or characteristic of stars.  The term refers to objects or phenomena in space that are not stars, such as planets, nebulae, galaxies, or interstellar matter. | Example:  Nonstellar objects often outnumber stars in a galaxy.  非恒星天体在星系中通常比恒星多。| 近义词： extrastellar, non-stellar | 反义词：stellar
