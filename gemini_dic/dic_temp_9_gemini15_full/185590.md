reveller:/ˈrevələr/| n. |狂欢者，寻欢作乐者 | A reveller is someone who is enjoying themselves in a lively and noisy way, especially at a party or celebration.  They are often characterized by their enthusiastic and sometimes boisterous participation in festivities. | The revellers danced and sang until dawn. 狂欢者们载歌载舞直到黎明。  Many revellers spilled out onto the street after the concert.  音乐会结束后，许多狂欢者涌上街头。 | merrymaker, partygoer, celebrant |  (反义词较难直接对应，可考虑：homebody, recluse, introvert  根据语境选择)|名词
