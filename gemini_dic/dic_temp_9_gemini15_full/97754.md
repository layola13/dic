initiated:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪtɪd/ | v. | 开始；发起；启动；介绍；使加入 | To begin or start something; to put something into action; to formally introduce someone into a group or organization. |例句：The company initiated a new marketing campaign. 公司发起了一场新的营销活动。|例句：He initiated the conversation by asking about her day. 他以询问她的一天来开始谈话。 |近义词：begin, commence, start, launch, introduce |反义词：conclude, end, finish, terminate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He initiates new projects every year. 他每年都启动新的项目。
She initiates conversations with strangers easily. 她很容易与陌生人开始交谈。

一般过去时:
They initiated the peace talks last month. 他们上个月启动了和平谈判。
The teacher initiated a class discussion on the topic. 老师就这个话题发起了一次课堂讨论。

现在完成时:
She has initiated several successful business ventures. 她已经启动了几项成功的商业项目。
He has initiated a dialogue with his colleagues on the matter. 他已经就此事与同事进行了对话。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had initiated a series of emails to clarify the agenda. 会议之前，他们已经通过一系列邮件来明确议程。
The police had initiated an investigation into the crime. 警方已经对这起犯罪事件展开了调查。

将来时:
The government will initiate a new economic policy next year. 政府明年将启动一项新的经济政策。
They will initiate contact with the potential clients next week. 他们下周将与潜在客户取得联系。

虚拟语气:
If they had initiated the project earlier, it would have been completed by now. 如果他们早些启动这个项目，它现在就应该完成了。
I wish I had initiated a conversation with her. 我真希望我当时和她聊了聊。

被动语态:
The project was initiated by the CEO. 该项目是由首席执行官启动的。
The new program was initiated last spring. 新项目是去年春天启动的。

疑问句:
Who initiated the complaint? 谁发起了投诉？
When was the program initiated?  该项目是什么时候启动的？

否定句:
They did not initiate any action. 他们没有采取任何行动。
He didn't initiate the contact. 他没有主动联系。
