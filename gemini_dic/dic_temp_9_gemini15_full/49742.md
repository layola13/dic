demographies:/dɛməˈɡræfiz/ | n. |人口统计学；人口结构 |The study of the characteristics of human populations, such as size, growth, density, distribution, and vital statistics.  It involves analyzing population trends and patterns to understand social, economic, and environmental impacts. | Example: The demographies of the city show a significant increase in the elderly population.  这座城市的人口统计数据显示老年人口大幅增加。 The changing demographies of the workforce are creating new challenges for businesses.  劳动力人口结构的变化给企业带来了新的挑战。 |近义词：population statistics, population dynamics |反义词：N/A
