cyclone:/ˈsaɪkloʊn/| n.|气旋，旋风|A cyclone is a system of winds rotating inward to a low atmospheric pressure, especially a tropical cyclone.|例句：The cyclone caused widespread devastation.  这场气旋造成了大范围的破坏。

例句：A powerful cyclone is heading towards the coast.  一个强大的气旋正在逼近海岸。


近义词：hurricane, typhoon, tornado, whirlwind|反义词：anticyclone
