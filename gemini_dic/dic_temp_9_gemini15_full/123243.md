monocultures:/ˈmɒnəʊkʌltʃəz/| n.|单一栽培；单一文化；单一作物种植|The practice of growing or raising a single crop or organism, such as a single plant species, in a field or area.  This can lead to increased efficiency in some aspects but also makes the area vulnerable to pests and diseases.|例句：The reliance on monocultures in agriculture has led to soil depletion and increased susceptibility to pests.  单一作物种植依赖导致土壤贫瘠和对病虫害的易感性增加。

例句：The lack of biodiversity in the monoculture forestry plantation is a concern. 单一栽培林场的生物多样性匮乏令人担忧。|近义词：single cropping, uniform cultivation|反义词：polyculture, biodiversity
