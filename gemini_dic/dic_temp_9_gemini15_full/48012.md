decipherment:/dɪˈsaɪfərmənt/| n. |破译；解读；解释|The act of deciphering something, especially a code or cipher.  It involves understanding and interpreting the meaning of something that is unclear or hidden.  |Example: The decipherment of the ancient script revealed the secrets of a lost civilization.  古代文字的破译揭示了一个失落文明的秘密。|近义词：interpretation, solution, explanation |反义词：encryption, encoding
