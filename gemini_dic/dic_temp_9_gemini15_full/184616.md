restraining: /rɪˈstreɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 抑制的；克制的；约束的 |  Preventing something from developing or expanding; holding back.  |例句：The restraining order prevented him from contacting his ex-wife.  这项限制令阻止了他联系前妻。

例句：He felt a restraining hand on his shoulder. 他感到一只制止的手放在他的肩膀上。


近义词：inhibiting, curbing, controlling, checking | 反义词：releasing, liberating, encouraging, stimulating | 词性：形容词


词源:  Comes from the verb "restrain," which is formed from the Old French "restreindre," meaning "to restrain, limit," ultimately derived from the Latin "restringere," a compound of "re-" (back, again) and "stringere" (to bind, tighten).  The "-ing" suffix turns the verb into a present participle, functioning as an adjective.
