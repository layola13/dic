nonvibration: /ˌnɒnvaɪˈbreɪʃən/ | n. |无振动，不振动 | The absence of vibration; a state or condition of not vibrating.  This term is used in various contexts, including physics, engineering, and medicine, to describe a lack of oscillatory movement. | Example: The engine's smooth operation ensured near-perfect nonvibration. 引擎平稳运行，确保几乎完全没有振动。 The new design minimized nonvibration, resulting in a quieter machine. 新的设计最大限度地减少了振动，从而使机器更安静。| 近义词：stillness, quietness, stability | 反义词：vibration, oscillation, tremor | 名词
