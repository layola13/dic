conglomerators:/kənˈɡlɒməreɪtərz/ | n. | 联合企业；企业集团；集团公司 | Companies that combine many smaller businesses under one corporate umbrella.  They operate in diverse industries, often acquiring smaller companies to expand their reach and diversify their portfolio.  | Example:  Berkshire Hathaway is a well-known conglomerate.  伯克希尔哈撒韦是一家著名的联合企业。  Many large conglomerates are multinational corporations. 许多大型企业集团都是跨国公司。 |近义词：multinationals, corporations, holding companies | 反义词：small businesses, independent companies |名词
