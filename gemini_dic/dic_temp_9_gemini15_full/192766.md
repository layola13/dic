seal:/siːl/| n./v.|印章；海豹；密封；盖章|A seal is an official mark or stamp, an aquatic mammal, or an act of closing something tightly.|The document was sealed with the company's official seal.  这份文件盖上了公司的官方印章。|The seals were basking in the sun on the ice floe. 海豹们在冰块上晒太阳。|近义词：stamp, emblem, close, shut|反义词：open, unseal


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He seals the envelopes every morning. 他每天早上封信封。
The company seals all important documents. 公司密封所有重要文件。


一般过去时:
She sealed the letter with wax. 她用蜡封信。
The workers sealed the crack in the pipe. 工人们封住了管道上的裂缝。


现在完成时:
They have sealed the deal. 他们已经达成了协议。
The government has sealed off the area. 政府已经封锁了该区域。


过去完成时:
He had sealed the package before he left. 他离开前已经封好了包裹。
They had sealed the windows before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临前，他们已经封好了窗户。


将来时:
She will seal the contract tomorrow. 她明天将签署合同。（seal在此处指签署）
We will seal the opening with concrete. 我们将用混凝土封住开口。


虚拟语气:
If he sealed the letter, it would be safe. 如果他封好信，它就安全了。
I wish I had sealed the documents earlier. 我真希望我早点把文件封好。


被动语态:
The documents were sealed by the notary. 文件由公证人盖章。
The room was sealed off by the police. 房间被警方封锁了。


疑问句:
Did you seal the package? 你封好包裹了吗？
Has the agreement been sealed yet? 协议已经签署了吗？（sealed在此处指签署）


否定句:
He didn't seal the envelope properly. 他没有正确地封好信封。
The package wasn't sealed securely. 包裹没有被牢固地封好。



PHRASE->
