simpletons:/ˈsɪmplɪtənz/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋，蠢人 |Simpletons are people who are considered foolish or lacking in intelligence.  They often make poor decisions or exhibit a lack of common sense.  The term carries a somewhat informal and often derogatory connotation. | Example:  The simpletons believed the con man's outrageous story.  这些傻瓜相信了骗子的荒谬故事。


The simpletons readily fell for the trick. 这些傻瓜轻易地上了当。|近义词：fools, idiots, dunces, nincompoops |反义词：geniuses, intellectuals, scholars |名词
