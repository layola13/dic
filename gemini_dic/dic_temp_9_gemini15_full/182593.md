reoffender:/ˌriːˈɒfɛndə(r)/| n. |累犯，惯犯|A person who commits a crime after having been convicted of a previous crime.  A reoffender is someone who has been punished for a crime and then commits another crime.|例句：The high rate of reoffending is a major concern for the justice system.  高比例的累犯是司法系统的主要担忧。|例句：He is a convicted reoffender with a long history of violent crime. 他是一个有暴力犯罪前科的累犯。|近义词：habitual offender, repeat offender, recidivist|反义词：first-time offender
