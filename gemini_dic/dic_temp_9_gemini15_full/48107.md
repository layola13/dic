decliner:/dɪˈklaɪnər/ | n. | 拒绝者；下降；衰退者；倾斜器 | A person who declines something; something that declines or slopes downward; a device for inclining something.  | Example: He was a decliner of all invitations. 他拒绝所有邀请。  The economic decliner is worrying. 经济衰退令人担忧。|近义词：refuser, denier, rejector, downturn, decline |反义词：accepter, approver, upturn, ascent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The economic indicator is declining. = 经济指标正在下降。
He declines all offers of help. = 他拒绝所有帮助。


一般过去时:
The patient declined rapidly. = 病人的病情迅速恶化。
She declined the invitation politely. = 她礼貌地拒绝了邀请。


现在完成时:
The number of students has declined significantly. = 学生人数显著下降。
He has declined to comment on the matter. = 他拒绝就此事发表评论。


过去完成时:
The company had declined before the merger. = 这家公司在合并之前就已经衰落了。
She had declined several job offers before accepting this one. = 在接受这份工作之前，她已经拒绝了几份工作邀请。


将来时:
The population will decline in the coming years. = 未来几年人口将会下降。
He will likely decline the promotion. = 他很可能会拒绝晋升。


虚拟语气:
If he declined the offer, he would regret it. = 如果他拒绝了这个提议，他会后悔的。
I wish he had declined the invitation. = 我真希望他拒绝了邀请。


被动语态:
The proposal was declined by the committee. = 该提案被委员会否决了。
His request for leave was declined. = 他的请假请求被拒绝了。


疑问句:
Will he decline the offer? = 他会拒绝这个提议吗？
Did she decline the invitation? = 她拒绝了邀请吗？


否定句:
He did not decline the invitation. = 他没有拒绝邀请。
The sales figures did not decline this quarter. = 本季度销售额没有下降。



PHRASE->
decline in = 下降
decline to = 拒绝
sharp decline = 急剧下降
steady decline = 稳定下降
gradual decline = 逐渐下降
