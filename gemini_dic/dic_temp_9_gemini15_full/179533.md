reel:/riːl/ | n., v. |卷轴；卷筒；（电影）胶片；旋转；眩晕；深深吸引 | a cylindrical container around which something is wound, such as thread, film, or cable;  to wind something around a reel; to rotate rapidly; to feel dizzy; to be strongly attracted |例句：The fisherman carefully reeled in his line.  渔夫小心地收起鱼线。  The film reel was damaged. 胶片卷坏了。近义词：spool, coil, spin, whirl, fascinate | 反义词：unwind, straighten
