hill:/hɪl/| n.|小山，丘陵|A hill is a naturally raised area of land, smaller than a mountain.|例句：We climbed the hill to get a better view. 我们爬上了小山以获得更好的视野。|近义词：knoll, mound, elevation|反义词：valley, plain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer tills the hill every spring. = 农民每年春天都耕种那座小山。
The hill overlooks the town. = 这座小山俯瞰着小镇。


一般过去时:
They walked up the hill yesterday. = 他们昨天爬上了那座小山。
The children rolled down the hill. = 孩子们从山上滚了下来。


现在完成时:
We have climbed that hill many times. = 我们已经爬过那座山很多次了。
She has lived on that hill for ten years. = 她在那座山上住了十年了。


过去完成时:
They had already climbed the hill before the rain started. = 在下雨之前他们已经爬上了那座山。
The farmer had tilled the hill before planting the seeds. = 农民在播种之前已经耕种了那座小山。


将来时:
We will climb the hill tomorrow. = 我们明天将爬那座山。
They will build a house on the hill. = 他们将在山上建一座房子。


虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I would climb that hill easily. = 如果我更强壮一些，我会很容易爬上那座山。
I wish I could climb that hill without getting tired. = 我希望我能爬上那座山而不会感到疲倦。


被动语态:
The hill is often used for picnics. = 这座小山经常被用来野餐。
The hill was covered in snow last winter. = 去年冬天，这座小山被雪覆盖了。


疑问句:
Is that a hill or a mountain? = 那是小山还是大山？
Which hill are we going to climb? = 我们要去爬哪座山？


否定句:
That isn't a hill; it's a small mountain. = 那不是小山；那是座小山。
They didn't climb the hill because of the bad weather. = 因为天气不好，他们没有爬山。
