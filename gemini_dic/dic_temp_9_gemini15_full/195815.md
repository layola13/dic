send-off:/ˈsɛndɒf/| n. |欢送会；告别；辞行；解雇|A send-off is an occasion when people gather to say goodbye to someone who is leaving, often for a new job or a long journey.  It can also refer to the act of dismissing someone from a job. |例句：The company threw a big send-off party for him before he moved to London.  这家公司在他搬到伦敦之前为他举办了一个盛大的欢送会。

The team gave their captain a rousing send-off after his final match. 球队在他最后一场比赛后为他送上了热烈的欢送。|近义词：farewell, departure, dismissal |反义词：arrival, welcome
