carefree:/ˈkerfriː/| adj. |无忧无虑的，轻松自在的|free from worry or responsibility; lighthearted.|例句：She had a carefree childhood. 她童年无忧无虑。

例句：He felt carefree and happy on vacation. 他在假期里感到轻松自在又快乐。


近义词：relaxed,easygoing, unconcerned, happy-go-lucky|反义词：worried,anxious, apprehensive, burdened


词源:
The word "carefree" is a compound word formed from "care" and "free."  "Care" in this context refers to worry or concern, and "free" means without. Therefore, "carefree" literally means "free from care" or "without worries."  Its origins trace back to Middle English.


用法说明:
carefree主要用作形容词，修饰人或其状态，表示无忧无虑、轻松自在的状态。它通常用于描述一种积极的情绪和心态。
