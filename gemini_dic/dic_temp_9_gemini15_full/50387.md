departure:/dɪˈpɑːrtʃər/| n. |离开，出发；背离；去世|The act of leaving or going away from a place;  a deviation from what is expected, normal, or accepted; death. |例句：The departure of the flight was delayed due to bad weather.  航班因恶劣天气延误出发。

The departure from tradition caused some controversy.  背离传统引起了一些争议。|近义词：leaving, going, exit, divergence, deviation |反义词：arrival, return, adherence
