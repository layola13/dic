eprint:/ˈiːprɪnt/| n. |预印本，电子预印本 | An eprint is an electronic version of a scholarly paper or preprint that is made available online before or instead of formal publication in a journal or other print medium.  Eprints are often available on open access repositories. |Example:  He uploaded his eprint to arXiv. 他将他的预印本上传到了arXiv。

The eprint was widely circulated before the journal publication.  这篇预印本在期刊发表前就已广泛传播。|近义词：preprint, electronic preprint |反义词：print version, published article |名词
