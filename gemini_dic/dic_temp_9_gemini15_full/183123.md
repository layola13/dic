reposing:/rɪˈpoʊzɪŋ/ | v. | 静止；休息；安放；寄托 |  The act of resting or being at rest; lying down; to place in a resting position; to place trust or confidence in. |例句：The cat was reposing peacefully on the sun-warmed stones.  猫平静地躺在阳光照射的石头上。|例句：She reposed her faith in her doctor's judgment. 她对医生的判断充满信心。 |近义词：resting, lying, relaxing, entrusting |反义词：agitating, bustling, active
