imaginary:/ɪˈmædʒɪnəri/| adj.|虚构的；想象的；假想的 | Existing only in the imagination; not real.  |例句：He had an imaginary friend when he was a child. 他小时候有一个想象中的朋友。

例句：The story is entirely imaginary; it's not based on fact. 这个故事完全是虚构的；它不是基于事实的。

近义词：fanciful, fictitious, unreal, hypothetical | 反义词：real, actual, true, factual
