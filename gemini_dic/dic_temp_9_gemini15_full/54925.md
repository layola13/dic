disposingly: /dɪˈspoʊzɪŋli/ | adv. |  处置地；处理地；具有处理能力地 |  In a way that shows readiness or willingness to deal with something; readily.  It suggests an efficient and decisive manner. |


例句：

She dealt with the difficult situation disposingly, quickly finding a solution. = 她有效地处理了这个棘手的情况，迅速找到了解决办法。

He disposingly arranged the furniture in the new office. = 他有效率地安排了新办公室的家具。


近义词：efficiently, effectively, decisively, readily, promptly


反义词：inefficiently, ineffectively, indecisively, hesitantly, slowly


词性：副词
