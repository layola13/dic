hold-forth:/hoʊld fɔːrθ/ | /həʊld fɔːrθ/ | v. | 大谈；夸夸其谈；滔滔不绝地讲 | To speak or write at length and forcefully on a particular subject.  | The professor held forth on the intricacies of quantum physics for over an hour.  教授滔滔不绝地讲解了量子物理学的复杂性，足足一个多小时。

The politician held forth on the importance of economic reform.  这位政治家大谈经济改革的重要性。|  discourse, expound, lecture |  listen quietly, remain silent | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He holds forth on politics every chance he gets. = 他一有机会就大谈政治。
She holds forth on her travels whenever she meets new people. = 她一遇到新朋友就滔滔不绝地讲起她的旅行经历。


一般过去时:
He held forth at the meeting for nearly an hour. = 他在会议上滔滔不绝地讲了将近一个小时。
The speaker held forth on the dangers of climate change. = 演讲者大谈气候变化的危险。


现在完成时:
He has held forth on this topic many times before. = 他以前多次就这个话题大谈特谈。
She has held forth extensively about her experiences in Africa. = 她已经广泛地讲述了她在非洲的经历。


过去完成时:
He had held forth on the subject for so long that everyone was bored. = 他就这个话题讲了太久，以至于每个人都厌烦了。
Before the debate, she had held forth on her views to her team. = 在辩论之前，她已经向她的团队阐述了她的观点。


将来时:
He will hold forth on his new business venture at the conference. = 他将在会议上大谈他的新商业项目。
She will hold forth on the importance of education at the graduation ceremony. = 她将在毕业典礼上大谈教育的重要性。


虚拟语气:
If he held forth less frequently, people might listen more. = 如果他不那么频繁地夸夸其谈，人们可能会更多地倾听。
I wish he wouldn't hold forth so much about his opinions. = 我希望他不要如此夸夸其谈他的观点。


被动语态:
His views were held forth by several prominent figures. = 他的观点被几位知名人士大肆宣扬。
The importance of environmental protection was held forth in the speech. = 演讲中大谈了环境保护的重要性。(虽然被动语态用在此处略显生硬，但语法上可行)


疑问句:
Will he hold forth on the topic again tomorrow? = 他明天还会就这个话题大谈特谈吗？
Does she often hold forth at social gatherings? = 她经常在社交聚会上夸夸其谈吗？


否定句:
He didn't hold forth on the controversial issue. = 他没有就这个有争议的问题大谈特谈。
She chose not to hold forth on her personal life. = 她选择不谈论她的私生活。



PHRASE->

hold forth vehemently  激烈地发表意见
hold forth passionately  热情地发表意见
