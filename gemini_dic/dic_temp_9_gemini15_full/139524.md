obtaining: /əbˈteɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 获得，取得 |  The act of getting or acquiring something.  It emphasizes the process of successfully getting something, often after some effort. | 例句：He is obtaining a PhD in physics. 他正在攻读物理学博士学位。  | 例句：Obtaining a visa can be a lengthy process. 获得签证可能是一个漫长的过程。 | 近义词：getting, acquiring, securing, achieving | 反义词：losing, relinquishing, forfeiting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are obtaining the necessary permits. = 他们正在获得必要的许可证。
We obtain our water from a well. = 我们从一口井里取水。

一般过去时:
She obtained a high score on the test. = 她考试得了高分。
He obtained a loan from the bank. = 他从银行获得了一笔贷款。

现在完成时:
They have obtained all the information they need. = 他们已经获得了所有需要的信息。
We have obtained funding for the project. = 我们已经为项目获得了资金。

过去完成时:
Before the deadline, she had obtained all the necessary signatures. = 在截止日期前，她已经获得了所有必要的签名。
By the time the meeting started, he had obtained the relevant documents. = 到会议开始时，他已经获得了相关的文件。

将来时:
They will obtain the results next week. = 他们下周将获得结果。
We will obtain the necessary equipment soon. = 我们很快就会获得必要的设备。

虚拟语气:
If I were to obtain a scholarship, I would go to university. = 如果我能获得奖学金，我就会去大学。
I wish I could obtain a better job. = 我希望我能找到一份更好的工作。

被动语态:
The information was obtained from various sources. = 信息来自各种来源。
The necessary documents are being obtained. = 必要的证件正在办理中。

疑问句:
How did you obtain that information? = 你是怎么得到那个信息的？
Where can we obtain the necessary materials? = 我们在哪里可以获得必要的材料？

否定句:
He did not obtain the position he applied for. = 他没有获得他申请的职位。
They were unable to obtain the required permits. = 他们无法获得所需的许可证。
