mumbler:/ˈmʌmblər/| n. |含糊不清地说的人；嘟囔的人| A person who mumbles.  Someone who speaks indistinctly and quietly, making their words difficult to understand. |例句：He's a mumbler, I could barely understand a word he said. 他说话含糊不清，我几乎听不懂他说了什么。|例句：The old man was a mumbler, his words lost in a low, indistinct drone.  这位老人说话含糊不清，他的话语淹没在低沉、模糊的嗡嗡声中。|近义词：mutterer, grumbler|反义词：articulator, enunciator
