crouch:/kraʊtʃ/| v.|蹲下，蜷缩|To bend down low to the ground, especially with the knees bent and the upper body forward.  |例句：The cat crouched low to the ground, ready to pounce. 猫低伏在地，准备猛扑。|例句：She crouched behind the bushes, trying to remain hidden. 她蹲在灌木丛后面，试图隐藏自己。|近义词：squat, stoop, hunker down|反义词：stand, straighten up|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He crouches behind the wall. = 他蹲在墙后。
The dog crouches when it's scared. = 狗害怕时会蹲下。

一般过去时:
She crouched down to examine the insect. = 她蹲下检查昆虫。
They crouched behind the rocks for cover. = 他们躲在岩石后面寻求掩护。

现在完成时:
The thief has crouched in the shadows for hours. = 小偷躲在阴影里几个小时了。
He has crouched here waiting for you. = 他一直蹲在这里等你。

过去完成时:
She had crouched there for a long time before he arrived. = 他到达之前，她已经在那里蹲了很久了。
They had crouched down before the explosion. = 爆炸发生前，他们已经蹲下了。

将来时:
I will crouch down to pick up the flower. = 我将蹲下摘花。
He will crouch behind the tree to observe the birds. = 他将躲在树后观察鸟类。

虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would crouch and wait. = 如果我是一只猫，我会蹲下等待。
I wish I could crouch lower without being seen. = 我希望我能蹲得更低而不被看见。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
The body was found crouched in a corner. = 尸体被发现蜷缩在一个角落里。(强调尸体状态)


疑问句:
Does he usually crouch when he's nervous? = 他紧张时通常会蹲下吗？
Why did she crouch behind the car? = 她为什么躲在车后面？

否定句:
He didn't crouch down; he stood tall and proud. = 他没有蹲下；他站得又高又骄傲。
She didn't crouch; she simply bent over. = 她没有蹲下；她只是弯腰。


PHRASE->

crouch down = 蹲下
crouch low = 低伏
crouch in fear = 害怕地蹲下
crouch in the shadows = 躲在阴影里
crouch over = 弯腰驼背
