herborization:/ˌhɜːrbəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |植物采集；植物标本采集 | The act of collecting and preserving plants.  It often involves identifying, documenting, and arranging plant specimens for scientific study or personal enjoyment. |例句：Her passion for herborization led her to explore remote mountain regions.  她的植物采集热情让她探索偏远的群山地区。 |例句：The museum's extensive collection was built up through years of dedicated herborization.  博物馆丰富的收藏是多年辛勤植物标本采集的结果。|近义词：plant collecting, botanical collecting |反义词：N/A
