multilaterally:/ˌmʌltiˈlætərəli/| adv.|多边地，多边地；|Involving more than two countries or parties.|例句：The issue was addressed multilaterally through international cooperation.  这个问题通过国际合作得到多边解决。|例句：Many countries are working multilaterally to combat climate change.许多国家正在多边合作以应对气候变化。|近义词：jointly, cooperatively|反义词：unilaterally, bilaterally


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They negotiate multilaterally on trade agreements. = 他们就贸易协定进行多边谈判。
We address global issues multilaterally. = 我们多边处理全球性问题。


一般过去时:
The countries acted multilaterally to resolve the conflict. = 各国采取多边行动来解决冲突。
The treaty was negotiated multilaterally. = 这项条约是多边谈判达成的。


现在完成时:
They have worked multilaterally to achieve sustainable development. = 他们一直通过多边合作来实现可持续发展。
The organization has addressed the issue multilaterally. = 该组织已通过多边方式处理了这个问题。


过去完成时:
Before the summit, they had already agreed to approach the matter multilaterally. = 在峰会之前，他们已同意采取多边方式处理此事。
The nations had cooperated multilaterally on previous occasions. = 各国此前曾多次进行多边合作。


将来时:
We will approach the problem multilaterally in the future. = 我们将来会以多边方式处理这个问题。
They will continue to act multilaterally on this issue. = 他们将继续就这个问题采取多边行动。


虚拟语气:
If they had acted multilaterally, the outcome might have been different. = 如果他们采取多边行动，结果可能会有所不同。
It would be better if we approached this multilaterally. = 如果我们采取多边方式处理这个问题会更好。


被动语态:
The problem was addressed multilaterally by the participating nations. = 这个问题由参与国以多边方式得到解决。
The decision was made multilaterally. = 决定是多边做出的。


疑问句:
Will the negotiations be conducted multilaterally? = 谈判会采取多边方式进行吗？
Was the agreement reached multilaterally? = 该协议是通过多边达成的吗？


否定句:
The country did not act multilaterally; it acted unilaterally. = 该国没有采取多边行动；它是单方面行动的。
They did not solve the problem multilaterally. = 他们没有以多边方式解决这个问题。
