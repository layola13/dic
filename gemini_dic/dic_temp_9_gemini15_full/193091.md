sectioning: /ˈsɛkʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 分割；分区；章节划分 | The act or process of dividing something into sections.  This term is often used in various contexts, including document formatting,  land surveying, and anatomy. |  例句：The sectioning of the report made it easier to read.  报告的分段使它更容易阅读。|近义词：division, partitioning, compartmentalization | 反义词：unification, integration


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The editor is sectioning the manuscript into chapters. = 编辑正在将稿件分成章节。
They section the land into plots for sale. = 他们将土地分割成地块出售。


一般过去时:
The surgeon sectioned the brain for examination. = 外科医生切开大脑进行检查。
We sectioned the garden into different areas for planting. = 我们将花园分成不同的区域用于种植。


现在完成时:
She has sectioned the orange into segments. = 她已经把橘子切成瓣了。
They have sectioned off a portion of the road for construction. = 他们已经封闭了部分道路进行施工。


过去完成时:
He had sectioned the document before submitting it. = 他在提交文件之前已经将其分段。
The team had sectioned the data before analyzing it. = 团队在分析数据之前已经将其分段。


将来时:
They will section the building into apartments. = 他们将把建筑物分割成公寓。
We will section the cake into slices. = 我们将把蛋糕切成片。


虚拟语气:
If I sectioned the pie correctly, everyone would get a fair share. = 如果我把馅饼切得正确，每个人都会得到公平的份额。
I wish I had sectioned the garden before the weeds grew so tall. = 我希望我在杂草长得这么高之前就把花园分好了区。


被动语态:
The land was sectioned by the surveyor. = 土地由测量员分割。
The document is being sectioned into parts. = 文件正在被分成几部分。


疑问句:
How many sections will the book be sectioned into? = 这本书将分成多少章节？
How should we section this project? = 我们应该如何划分这个项目？


否定句:
The document was not sectioned properly. = 文件没有被正确地分段。
They did not section the garden into different areas. = 他们没有将花园分成不同的区域。
