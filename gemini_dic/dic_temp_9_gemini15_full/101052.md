inveigling:/ɪnˈveɪɡlɪŋ/| v.|诱骗；巧妙地劝诱|The act of persuading someone to do something by using deception or flattery.  It implies a gradual and subtle process of manipulation.|例句：He inveigled his way into the exclusive club by flattering the members. 他通过奉承会员们巧妙地加入了那个独家俱乐部。

例句：She inveigled the information out of him with a series of carefully crafted questions. 她通过一系列精心设计的问题诱导他透露了信息。


近义词：cajoling, coaxing, enticing, wheedling, persuading, alluring

反义词：coercing, forcing, compelling

词源:
源自中古英语 *invegelen，由古法语 *enveogler演变而来，其词根可能与古法语 *veugle（“陷阱”）有关。  最终的词源可能与日耳曼语系相关，但具体联系尚不清晰。
