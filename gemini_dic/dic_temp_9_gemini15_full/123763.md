mooring:/ˈmɔːrɪŋ/| n. |系泊；系泊装置；停泊处 |The act of securing a vessel to a fixed point;  a system of ropes, anchors, etc., used for mooring a vessel; a place where a vessel can be moored.  |例句：The boat was securely moored to the quay.  船安全地系泊在码头上。

例句：The harbor offers excellent mooring facilities.  这个港口提供极好的系泊设施。


近义词：anchoring, fastening, tying up |反义词：unmooring, casting off


词源：源自古英语 *mōring，意为“系泊处”。  最终源自 Proto-Germanic *mōrijan，意为“系泊”。
