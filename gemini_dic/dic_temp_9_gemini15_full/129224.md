nock:/nɒk/| n. vt.|  弓弦凹槽；(使)卡住；|The notch in the bow where the arrow sits. 弓上放置箭的凹槽。|The arrow nocked perfectly onto the bowstring. 箭完美地卡在了弓弦上。|近义词：notch, groove|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nocks the arrow onto the bowstring. = 他把箭装上弓弦。
The machine nocks the parts together. = 机器把零件卡在一起。


一般过去时:
He nocked the arrow before aiming. = 他在瞄准之前把箭装上。
The carpenter nocked the wood together. = 木匠把木头卡在一起。


现在完成时:
He has nocked the arrow and is ready to shoot. = 他已经把箭装上，准备射箭了。
The mechanic has nocked the engine parts together. = 机械师已经把发动机零件卡在一起了。


过去完成时:
He had nocked the arrow before the signal was given. = 在发出信号之前，他已经把箭装上了。
The engineer had nocked the components before the test. = 工程师在测试之前已经把组件卡在一起了。


将来时:
He will nock the arrow and fire. = 他将会把箭装上并射出。
