pendants:/ˈpɛndənts/ | n. |吊坠；垂饰 |An ornament that hangs from a necklace or bracelet.  A pendant is a piece of jewelry that hangs from a chain or cord around the neck. They can be made from various materials, such as gold, silver, gemstones, or other decorative materials.  |例句：She wore a beautiful diamond pendant. 她戴着一个漂亮的钻石吊坠。

She received a silver pendant as a gift. 她收到了一件银质吊坠作为礼物。|近义词：charm, locket, amulet |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with items worn on the finger (ring) or ear (earring).
