由于题目中单词{mamed}拼写错误，我猜测您想询问的是"married"。以下是"married"的详细解析：

/ˈmærid/ | adj. | 已婚的；结婚的 |  Married describes the state of being legally united to a person of the opposite or same sex as a spouse.  |  He is married to a doctor. 他和一位医生结婚了。  She's been married for ten years. 她结婚十年了。 | single, wedded | unmarried, single, divorced | adj.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are married. = 他们结婚了。
He is married with two children. = 他已婚，有两个孩子。

一般过去时:
They got married last year. = 他们去年结婚了。
She was married when she was very young. = 她很年轻的时候就结婚了。

现在完成时:
They have been married for five years. = 他们结婚五年了。
She has been married twice. = 她结过两次婚。


过去完成时:
They had been married for twenty years when he died. = 他去世时，他们已经结婚二十年了。
She had been married before she met him.  = 在她遇见他之前，她结过婚。


将来时:
They will get married next spring. = 他们将在明年春天结婚。
We will be married in a small church. = 我们将在一个小教堂举行婚礼。


虚拟语气:
If I were married, I would have more responsibilities. = 如果我结婚了，我将会有更多的责任。
I wish I were married and had a family. = 我希望我已经结婚并组建了家庭。


被动语态:
The couple were married by a priest. = 这对夫妇由牧师主持婚礼。
They were married in a beautiful ceremony. = 他们在美丽的仪式中结婚。


疑问句:
Are you married? = 你结婚了吗？
Is she married to John? = 她和约翰结婚了吗？


否定句:
He is not married. = 他没有结婚。
They aren't married yet. = 他们还没结婚。


PHRASE->

get married =结婚
be married to = 与...结婚
married life = 已婚生活
newly married = 新婚的
happily married = 幸福的婚姻


注意事项：

音标使用了美式发音，英式发音近似。
