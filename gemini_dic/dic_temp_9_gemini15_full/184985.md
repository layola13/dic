retelevising:/ˌriːˈtɛləvaɪzɪŋ/| v.| 重播电视节目 | The act of broadcasting a television program again.  This often refers to re-airing a previously shown program, either on the same channel or a different one.  It can also refer to the process of converting a program to a different television format. | Example:  The network is retelevising the classic sitcom next week.  下周电视台将重播这部经典情景喜剧。


The network is retelevising the documentary on Sunday evening.  电视台将在周日晚上重播这部纪录片。


近义词：rebroadcasting, re-airing, replaying


反义词：首播，first airing


词性:动词
