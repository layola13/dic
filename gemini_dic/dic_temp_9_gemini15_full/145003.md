/ˌoʊvərəˈdɪkʃənz/ | n. |过度沉溺；过度依赖 | The term "overaddictions" refers to the excessive reliance on or engagement with multiple addictive substances or behaviors simultaneously or sequentially.  It represents a complex condition where individuals struggle with controlling their urges related to several different addictions.  This can significantly impact various aspects of their life, including physical and mental health, relationships, and work. The term is not a formally recognized clinical diagnosis but rather a descriptive term used to highlight the multifaceted nature of addiction in some individuals.  | Example:  His overaddictions to gambling, alcohol, and nicotine almost cost him his family and his job.  他的沉迷于赌博、酒精和尼古丁几乎让他失去了家庭和工作。 |近义词:poly-addiction, multiple addictions, compulsive behaviors |反义词:moderation, self-control, abstinence
