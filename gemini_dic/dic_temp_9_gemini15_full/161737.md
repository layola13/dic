powerboat:/ˈpaʊərbəʊt/| n. |动力艇，快艇|A boat propelled by a motor, typically used for recreation or sport.|例句：He owns a powerful powerboat capable of speeds exceeding 50 knots. 他拥有一艘强大的动力艇，速度超过50节。|例句：We spent the afternoon enjoying a thrilling powerboat ride. 我们花了一下午时间享受了一次惊险刺激的快艇之旅。|近义词：motorboat, speedboat|反义词：sailboat, rowboat
