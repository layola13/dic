chipping:/ˈtʃɪpɪŋ/| n. v.|碎裂声；凿；削；击球|The act of chipping away at something; a small piece broken off; a small piece of something.  Also, in golf, a short shot played with a lofted club.|例句：The sound of chipping stone echoed through the valley.  凿石的声音回荡在山谷中。

The birds were chipping away at the bark of the tree. 鸟儿们啄食着树皮。|近义词：pecking, breaking, fracturing, chipping away|反义词：building, constructing, repairing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is chipping away at the old paint. 他正在一点点地铲掉旧油漆。
The birds chip at the seeds all day. 鸟儿们整天都在啄食种子。


一般过去时:
She chipped a tooth while eating an apple. 她吃苹果时崩掉了一颗牙。
The artist chipped away at the marble for hours. 艺术家花了几个小时凿琢大理石。


现在完成时:
They have chipped away at the problem for weeks. 他们已经处理这个问题好几个星期了。
He has chipped several pieces off the statue. 他已经从雕像上凿掉了好几块。


过去完成时:
She had chipped the vase before she realized it was antique.  在她意识到那是古董之前，她已经把花瓶弄碎了一角。
The workers had chipped away at the rock face for days. 工人们已经凿了几天岩石了。


将来时:
He will chip the ice off the windshield. 他会把挡风玻璃上的冰块敲掉。
They will be chipping away at the debt for years to come. 他们未来几年将不断偿还债务。


虚拟语气:
If he had chipped the paint more carefully, he wouldn't have damaged the wood. 如果他更仔细地铲掉油漆，他就不会损坏木头了。
I wish I hadn't chipped my favorite mug. 我真希望我没有打碎我最喜欢的马克杯。


被动语态:
The paint was chipped by the falling branch. 油漆被掉落的树枝弄掉了。
The ice will be chipped off by the morning sun. 冰块会被早晨的阳光融化掉。


疑问句:
Is he chipping the wood? 他在凿木头吗？
Are the birds chipping at the window? 鸟儿们在啄窗户吗？


否定句:
He didn't chip the paint. 他没有铲掉油漆。
The hammer didn't chip the stone. 锤子没有凿碎石头。


PHRASE->
chip away at: 逐渐削弱；一点一点地去除
chip in:  捐款；插话
chip off: 敲掉；剥落
a chip on one's shoulder:  心存怨恨；愤愤不平
chip and put:  (高尔夫)短距离击球和推杆


词源:
From Middle English *chippen, from Old English *cippan, from Proto-Germanic *kippōn, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kep- "to cut, to chop."  The meaning has evolved from a general sense of cutting or chopping to the more specific sense of breaking off small pieces.
