/ˈmæstˌhaʊs/ | n. |瞭望塔；瞭望台 | A mast house is a structure, typically a small building or tower, erected on high ground to provide a vantage point for observation, especially at sea or in a port.  It often houses signaling equipment. | The mast house offered a panoramic view of the harbor. 瞭望塔提供了港口的全景视野。| lookout, watchtower |  (反义词无直接对应词，可考虑与其功能相反的词，例如：hidden shelter)
