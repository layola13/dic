nonindependent: /ˌnɒnɪnˈdɪpɛndənt/ | adj. | 非独立的，依赖的 | Not independent; dependent on something else.  This adjective describes something that cannot exist or function on its own; it requires support or connection to something else. |


例句：

The small business is nonindependent of its larger corporate parent. = 这家小企业依赖于其更大的母公司。

His nonindependent thinking led him to blindly follow the crowd. = 他不独立的思维导致他盲目地跟随大众。


近义词：dependent, reliant, subordinate, subject


反义词：independent, autonomous, self-sufficient


词性：形容词
