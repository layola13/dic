noncomplicity:/ˌnɒnkəmˈplɪsɪti/| n. |不参与，无共谋|The lack of involvement or complicity in a wrongdoing or crime.  It suggests a deliberate choice to remain uninvolved, often implying knowledge of the activity.  |例句：The investigation revealed his noncomplicity in the fraud.  调查显示他与诈骗案无关。
The prosecution needed to prove the defendant's noncomplicity in the robbery.  检方需要证明被告与抢劫案无关。|近义词：noninvolvement, disassociation, innocence|反义词：complicity, involvement, participation
