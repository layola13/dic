paraphrase:/ˈpærəfreɪz/ | vt. n. |释义，意译|To express the meaning of (something written or spoken) using different words, especially to achieve greater clarity.|例句：Can you paraphrase this sentence for me?  你能帮我解释一下这句话吗？|近义词：rephrase, restate, summarize |反义词：quote, repeat verbatim

一般现在时:
He paraphrases complex ideas into simpler terms. = 他将复杂的想法用更简单的术语来解释。
She paraphrases the author's main points in her essay. = 她在论文中用自己的话概括了作者的主要观点。

一般过去时:
The student paraphrased the poem for his assignment. = 学生为作业改写了这首诗。
He paraphrased the speaker's key arguments in his report. = 他在他的报告中改写了演讲者的主要论点。

现在完成时:
I have paraphrased the entire chapter for better understanding. = 我已经将整章内容改写以便更好地理解。
She has paraphrased several articles for her research paper. = 为了她的研究论文，她已经改写了几篇文章。

过去完成时:
He had paraphrased the original text before submitting his translation. = 在提交翻译之前，他已经改写了原文。
They had paraphrased the legal documents to make them more accessible. = 他们已经将法律文件改写，使其更容易理解。

将来时:
I will paraphrase the instructions for the audience. = 我将为听众解释说明。
She will paraphrase the complicated data into a visual representation. = 她将把复杂的数据改写成可视化的表示形式。

虚拟语气:
If I were to paraphrase the book, I would focus on its central theme. = 如果我要改写这本书，我会关注它的中心主题。
I wish I could paraphrase this complex equation more easily. = 我希望我能更容易地解释这个复杂的方程式。

被动语态:
The passage was paraphrased to make it more concise. = 这段话被改写得更简洁。
The original text has been paraphrased in several different ways. = 原文已经以几种不同的方式被改写。

疑问句:
Can you paraphrase that for me? = 你能帮我解释一下吗？
How would you paraphrase this complicated sentence? = 你将如何解释这个复杂的句子？

否定句:
I cannot paraphrase this technical document without some expertise. = 没有一定的专业知识，我无法解释这份技术文件。
He did not paraphrase the quote correctly. = 他没有正确地改写这个引用。
