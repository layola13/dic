renovate:/ˈrɛnəveɪt/| vt.|修复，翻新，革新|To repair and improve something old, such as a building or a system, making it more modern or efficient.|The old house was renovated and now looks brand new. 这栋旧房子经过翻新，现在看起来焕然一新。|restore, refurbish, modernize|destroy, ruin, demolish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They renovate old buildings. = 他们翻新旧建筑。
We renovate our kitchen every year. = 我们每年都翻新我们的厨房。

一般过去时:
He renovated his bathroom last month. = 他上个月翻修了他的浴室。
She renovated the entire house. = 她翻修了整栋房子。

现在完成时:
They have renovated the school. = 他们已经翻新了学校。
We have renovated our apartment twice. = 我们已经翻修了我们的公寓两次。

过去完成时:
They had renovated the hotel before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经翻修了酒店。
She had renovated the car before selling it. = 她卖车之前已经翻新了它。

将来时:
We will renovate the garden next spring. = 我们明年春天将翻修花园。
They will renovate the office building next year. = 他们明年将翻修办公大楼。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would renovate the house. = 如果我们有更多的钱，我们会翻修房子。
I wish we could renovate the kitchen. = 我希望我们能够翻修厨房。

被动语态:
The house is being renovated. = 房子正在被翻修。
The old factory was renovated last year. = 那家旧工厂去年被翻修了。

疑问句:
Are they renovating the museum? = 他们正在翻修博物馆吗？
Will they renovate the church? = 他们会翻修教堂吗？

否定句:
They didn't renovate the building. = 他们没有翻修那栋楼。
We won't renovate the bathroom this year. = 我们今年不会翻修浴室。
