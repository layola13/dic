shadows:/ˈʃædoʊz/ | n. |阴影，影子；幽灵；暗影；预兆 |The dark shadows lengthened as the sun went down.  日落时，黑暗的影子变长了。|The event cast a long shadow over the family.  这件事给这个家庭蒙上了一层阴影。|近义词：shade, gloom, darkness |反义词：light, brightness


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The tree casts shadows on the ground. = 树木在地面上投下阴影。
Shadows lengthen as the sun sets. = 太阳落山时，影子变长。


一般过去时:
Long shadows stretched across the field. = 长长的影子伸展在田野上。
The shadows deepened as night fell. = 夜幕降临，阴影加深了。


现在完成时:
The scandal has cast a long shadow over his career. = 丑闻给他的事业蒙上了一层阴影。
Years of hardship have left their shadows on his face. = 多年的艰辛在他脸上留下了痕迹。


过去完成时:
The shadows had already lengthened before we reached the house. = 我们到达房子之前，影子已经变长了。
By the time we left, long shadows had fallen across the valley. = 我们离开时，长长的影子已经落在了山谷里。


将来时:
The tall buildings will cast shadows across the street. = 高大的建筑物将把阴影投射到街道上。
Long shadows will be cast at sunset tomorrow. = 明天日落时分将投下长长的影子。


虚拟语气:
If there were no sun, there would be no shadows. = 如果没有太阳，就不会有阴影。
I wish the shadows of doubt would leave my mind. = 我希望疑虑的阴影能离开我的脑海。


被动语态:
The wall is shadowed by the tall oak tree. = 墙壁被高大的橡树遮挡住了。
His reputation was shadowed by the recent scandal. = 他的名誉因最近的丑闻而受损。


疑问句:
What are those shadows on the wall? = 墙上的那些影子是什么？
Do you see the shadows moving in the dark? = 你看到黑暗中移动的影子了吗？


否定句:
There were no shadows in the bright sunlight. = 明媚的阳光下没有阴影。
The room was free of shadows thanks to the bright lamps. = 多亏明亮的灯，房间里没有阴影。
