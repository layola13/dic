Oort:/ɔːrt/| n. |奥尔特云|The Oort cloud is a hypothetical spherical cloud of icy planetesimals that is believed to surround the Sun at a vast distance.|例句：The comets that we see in the sky are believed to originate from the Oort cloud. 我们在天空中看到的彗星据信起源于奥尔特云。|近义词：Kuiper belt（柯伊伯带，但两者位置和构成不同）|反义词:无
