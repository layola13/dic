lambswool:/ˈlæmzˌwʊl/| n. |羔羊毛，羔羊绒|Soft, fine wool from a lamb.  Lambswool is known for its softness and warmth, often used in clothing and other textiles.|例句：The sweater is made of 100% lambswool. 这件毛衣是100%羔羊绒制成的。|例句：The baby blanket is incredibly soft, made from the finest lambswool. 这条婴儿毯非常柔软，由最优质的羔羊绒制成。|近义词：lamb's wool, fleece|反义词：无
