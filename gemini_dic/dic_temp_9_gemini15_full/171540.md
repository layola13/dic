The word "pulling" does not exist as a standalone word in the English language.  It is the present participle (verb + -ing) form of the verb "pull."  Therefore, I will provide the analysis for the verb "pull."


pull:/pʊl/ | vt. vi. n. |拉，拖，拔；吸引力 | to exert force on something so as to cause movement towards oneself, or to oneself |例句：He pulled the door open. 他拉开了门。  She pulled the weeds from the garden. 她把花园里的杂草拔掉了。|近义词：tug, drag, haul, attract |反义词：push
