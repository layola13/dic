/ˌɛpɪˈzjuːksɪs/ | n. | 重复修辞 | The stylistic device of repeating a word or phrase for emphasis.  It's a form of rhetorical repetition used to create emphasis, highlight a particular idea, or evoke a certain emotion.  | Example:  "Alone, alone, all, all alone, Alone on a wide wide sea!" (Coleridge)  这句诗通过重复“alone”和“all”来强调孤独感。|近义词：repetition, reiteration | 反义词：None (it's a rhetorical device, not a concept with a direct opposite)

There is no grammatical usage for epizeuxis; it is a rhetorical device.  Epizeuxis is not a verb, noun, adjective, etc that can be conjugated or used in different grammatical tenses. It is a literary technique.
