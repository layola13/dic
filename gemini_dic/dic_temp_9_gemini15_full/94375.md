implate:/ɪmˈplɑːnt/ | n. | 植入物；人工植入体 | A medical device or substance that is surgically implanted into the body.  | Example: The surgeon successfully implanted a new heart valve.  外科医生成功地植入了一个新的心脏瓣膜。 |近义词：graft, implant, prosthesis |反义词：removal, extraction | 名词
