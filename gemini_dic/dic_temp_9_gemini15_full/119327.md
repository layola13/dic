microbiology:/ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |微生物学 |The study of microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, archaea, fungi, and protozoa.  Microbiology encompasses many sub-disciplines including virology, bacteriology, parasitology, mycology, immunology and microbial genetics. |例句：She is majoring in microbiology at the university. 她大学主修微生物学。|例句：The microbiology lab confirmed the presence of E. coli in the water sample. 微生物实验室证实水样中存在大肠杆菌。|近义词：microorganism study, microbial science |反义词:宏观生物学(macrobiology)
