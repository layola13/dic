nuttish:/ˈnʌtɪʃ/| adj.|有点疯癫的；古怪的；傻气的 |Slightly crazy or eccentric; foolish or silly.  |He's a bit nuttish, always doing unpredictable things. 他有点疯疯癫癫的，总是做一些不可预测的事情。|近义词：eccentric, quirky, wacky, daft |反义词：sane, rational, sensible

She acts nuttish when she's stressed. = 她压力大的时候行为有点古怪。
