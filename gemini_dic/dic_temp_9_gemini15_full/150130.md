pamphleteering:/ˈpæmf(l)ɪtɪərɪŋ/| n. |散发小册子；用小册子宣传|The act of distributing pamphlets, especially for political or ideological purposes.  It often implies a somewhat aggressive or persuasive approach.|例句：The political party engaged in extensive pamphleteering in the lead-up to the election.  这个政党在选举前进行了广泛的小册子宣传。

例句：His pamphleteering against the new tax law was met with fierce opposition. 他反对新税法的传单宣传遭到了强烈的反对。


近义词：leafleting, propaganda, agitation|反义词：silence, suppression|词性：名词
