menstruations:/ˌmɛnstruˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 月经，经期 | The monthly discharge of blood from the uterus of non-pregnant women.  It's a natural physiological process regulated by hormones. | Example:  She experiences painful menstruations every month. 她每个月都经历痛经。  The doctor explained the hormonal changes that occur during menstruations. 医生解释了月经期间发生的荷尔蒙变化。 |近义词:periods, menses | 反义词:N/A
