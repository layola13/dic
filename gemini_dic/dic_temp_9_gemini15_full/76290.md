gabbed:/ɡæbd/| v.|闲聊，喋喋不休地说|To talk casually and continuously, often about unimportant things.  |例句：They gabbed for hours about their favorite celebrities. 他们聊了几个小时关于他们最喜欢的名人。|例句：We gabbed on the phone for ages. 我们在电话里聊了很久。|近义词：chatted, gossiped, chatted away, jawed|反义词：kept silent, remained mum


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They gab about everything. = 他们什么都聊。
She gabs incessantly. = 她喋喋不休。

一般过去时:
We gabbed until late last night. = 我们昨晚聊到很晚。
They gabbed about the party all day. = 他们一整天都在聊派对的事。

现在完成时:
I have gabbed with my friends for hours. = 我和朋友们聊了好几个小时。
They have gabbed about the movie all week. = 他们整个星期都在聊这部电影。

过去完成时:
She had gabbed with her sister before the meeting. = 她会议前和她姐姐聊过。
We had gabbed for hours before we finally got to the point. = 我们聊了好几个小时才最终说到点子上。

将来时:
I will gab with my friends later. = 我晚些时候会和朋友们聊聊天。
They will gab about the news tomorrow. = 他们明天会聊聊新闻。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would gab with you. = 如果我有时间，我会和你聊聊。
I wish I could gab with my friends more often. = 我希望我能更经常地和朋友们聊天。

被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies active, informal conversation.)  被动语态很少使用，因为它暗示着主动的、非正式的谈话。

疑问句:
Did they gab about the test? = 他们聊考试了吗？
Were they gabbing during the meeting? = 会议期间他们在闲聊吗？

否定句:
They didn't gab much at the party. = 他们在派对上没怎么聊天。
I didn't gab with anyone today. = 我今天没和任何人聊天。

gab on: 继续闲聊
