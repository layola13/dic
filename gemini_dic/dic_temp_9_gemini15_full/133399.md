These are things that are irrelevant or unimportant in a specific context. | Example:  The cost of the materials was a nonfactor in their decision to proceed with the project. 他们的决定继续进行项目，材料成本并非考量因素。  The weather proved to be a nonfactor in the success of the outdoor event.  天气的因素并未影响户外活动的成功。|近义词：irrelevant factors, insignificant elements, inconsequential aspects |反义词：factors,influences,determinants |名词
