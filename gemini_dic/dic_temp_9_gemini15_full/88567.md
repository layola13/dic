hitchhiking:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪkɪŋ/| n.|搭便车；搭顺风车|The act of traveling by getting free rides in someone else's vehicle.  It often involves standing by the roadside with a thumb extended to signal to passing drivers.|例句：He's planning a hitchhiking trip across the country. 他计划进行一次横跨全国的搭便车旅行。

例句：Many backpackers rely on hitchhiking to get around cheaply. 许多背包客依靠搭便车来廉价地旅行。|近义词：thumbing, freeloading|反义词：paying for transport, using public transport|名词
