colloquium:/kəˈloʊkwiəm/| n.|学术讨论会；研讨会|A colloquium is a formal meeting or a series of meetings for discussion on a particular subject, typically in an academic setting.  It often involves presentations by experts and open discussion among participants.|The physics department held a colloquium on quantum computing. 物理系举办了一场关于量子计算的学术讨论会。|seminar, symposium, conference|lecture, class|名词
