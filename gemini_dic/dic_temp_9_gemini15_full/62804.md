enhalo: /ɪnˈheɪloʊ/ | vt. | 以光环环绕；使神圣化 | to surround with or as if with a halo; to glorify; to make sacred |  例句：The sunset enhaloed the mountains in a golden light.  日落给群山镀上了一层金色的光芒。


例句：The saint was enhaloed in religious art.  在宗教艺术中，圣徒被描绘成带着光环。


近义词：glorify, sanctify, surround, irradiate


反义词：defile, desecrate, tarnish


词性: 动词
