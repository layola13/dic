This is a serious crime, often involving attempts to sway the outcome of a trial through illicit payments or other forms of coercion.  It undermines the integrity of the judicial system.|Example: The judge sentenced the defendant to a lengthy prison term for his involvement in the embracery scheme.|近义词：jury tampering, jury bribery, corruption|反义词：justice, fairness, impartiality|名词
