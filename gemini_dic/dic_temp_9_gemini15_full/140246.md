offprinted:/ˈɒfˌprɪntɪd/ | adj. |  抽印的；单行本的 | Separately printed, typically from a larger publication, such as a journal or book.  An offprint is a copy of an article or chapter that has been reprinted separately from the larger publication it originally appeared in. |  例句：I received an offprint of his article from the journal. 我收到了他从期刊上抽印的论文。

例句：She gave me an offprint of her chapter from the textbook. 她给了我从教科书中抽印出来的章节。


近义词：reprint, separate print | 反义词：None.


词性:形容词
