overhauled:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːld/| vt.|彻底检修；大修；彻底改革；彻底检查|To thoroughly examine and repair something, especially a machine or system.  It implies a complete and detailed inspection and repair, often involving replacing parts.|例句：The mechanic overhauled the engine. 机械师彻底检修了发动机。

The company overhauled its marketing strategy. 公司彻底改革了其营销策略。|近义词：repair, renovate, revamp, revise|反义词：neglect, ignore


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The mechanic overhauls the engine every year. = 机械师每年都对发动机进行大修。
They overhaul their business plan regularly. = 他们定期检讨他们的商业计划。

一般过去时:
The engineer overhauled the system last week. = 工程师上周检修了系统。
She overhauled her wardrobe after losing weight. = 她减肥后彻底更新了衣橱。

现在完成时:
They have overhauled the entire factory. = 他们已经彻底检修了整个工厂。
We have overhauled our website to improve user experience. = 我们已经改版了我们的网站以改善用户体验。

过去完成时:
They had overhauled the machine before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经检修了机器。
The team had overhauled the software before launching the new product. = 团队在发布新产品之前已经更新了软件。

将来时:
