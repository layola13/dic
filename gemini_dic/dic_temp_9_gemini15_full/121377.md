mishandle:/mɪsˈhændl/| vt.| 误操作；处理不当；虐待 | To handle badly or improperly; to manage clumsily or inefficiently; to treat roughly or cruelly. |例句：He mishandled the delicate situation, causing further problems. 他处理棘手的情况不得当，导致了更多的问题。

例句：The police mishandled the investigation, leading to a wrongful conviction.警方处理调查不当，导致了错误的定罪。

近义词：mismanage, maltreat, abuse, botch

反义词：manage well, handle skillfully, treat gently


USAGE->
