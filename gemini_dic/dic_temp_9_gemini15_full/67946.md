facialists: /ˈfeɪʃəlɪsts/ | n. | 美容师（尤指面部护理专家） | A facialist is a skincare professional specializing in facial treatments. They are trained to assess skin conditions, provide customized treatments, and offer advice on skincare routines.  | Example:  I booked an appointment with a facialist for a deep cleansing facial. 我预约了一位美容师做深层清洁面部护理。  | 近义词: aesthetician, skin therapist, skincare specialist | 反义词:  (无直接反义词)
