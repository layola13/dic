shrewlike:/ʃruːlaɪk/| adj. |像鼩鼱的；泼辣的；尖刻的 | Resembling a shrew; characterized by shrewish qualities such as being bad-tempered, nagging, and aggressive.  |例句：Her shrewlike manner intimidated many of her colleagues. 她泼辣的举止吓倒了许多同事。

例句：He had a shrewlike gaze that could pierce through any pretense. 他眼神锐利，能够看穿任何伪装。


近义词：vindictive, spiteful,  sharp-tongued,  abrasive,  cantankerous

反义词：gentle, kind, amiable, sweet-tempered,  meek


词源:  The word derives from "shrew," which originally referred to a small, mouselike mammal known for its aggressive nature.  The suffix "-like" adds the meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."  The word's evolution reflects the association of the shrew's behavior with certain human personality traits.
