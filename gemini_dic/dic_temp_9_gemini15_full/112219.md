loopy:/ˈluːpi/| adj.| 傻气的；不稳定的；循环的；圈状的 |  Describes something that is silly, eccentric, or erratic; also describes a shape that is circular or looped.  | 例句：He's a bit loopy after that accident.  他那次事故后有点傻乎乎的。|近义词：crazy, wacky, erratic, circular | 反义词：stable, sensible, straight, linear

词源:
The word "loopy" ultimately derives from the Old English word "lūp" meaning "loop". Over time, the meaning evolved to encompass the senses of being eccentric or erratic, likely due to the visual association of loops with something "out of control" or "unpredictable".
