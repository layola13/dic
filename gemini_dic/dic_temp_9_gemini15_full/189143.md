sachemdom:/ˈseɪtʃəmdəm/| n. |萨切姆的统治；印第安酋长管辖的地区|The territory or domain ruled by a sachem, a Native American chief.  It refers to the area under the authority of a sachem and often implies a specific political or social structure within a Native American tribe.|Example: The sachemdom extended across several valleys and rivers, encompassing numerous villages.  萨切姆的统治范围横跨几座山谷和河流，包含众多村庄。|近义词：chiefdom, domain, territory |反义词：subject, colony
