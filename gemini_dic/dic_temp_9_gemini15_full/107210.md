laically:/ˈleɪɪkli/| adv.|世俗地；非宗教地；门外汉地 |In a manner characteristic of or relating to laypeople; without religious or professional expertise.  |例句：He discussed the theological issues laically, focusing on their practical implications. 他以世俗的方式讨论了神学问题，侧重于它们的实际意义。

例句：She approached the complex scientific problem laically, relying on common sense rather than specialized knowledge. 她以门外汉的方式处理了复杂的科学问题，依靠常识而不是专业知识。


近义词：worldly, secularly, non-professionally

反义词：clerically, professionally, religiously


词源：源于名词"layperson"或"layman"，指非专业人士，非宗教人士。"-ly"是副词后缀。  The word derives from the Old English word *lāic, meaning "lay," which itself comes from the Latin word *laicus, meaning "layman" or "of the laity."
