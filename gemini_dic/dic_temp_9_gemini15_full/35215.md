clucky:/ˈklʌki/| adj.|咯咯叫的；像母鸡孵蛋似的；焦虑不安的；|Describes a hen making clucking sounds, often associated with brooding or anxiety.  It can also describe a person behaving in a nervous or anxious manner.|例句：The hen was clucky over her chicks.  这只母鸡正在照看她的雏鸡。

例句：She got all clucky when her son announced his engagement.  当她儿子宣布订婚时，她变得非常焦虑不安。


近义词：broody, fussy, anxious, nervous|反义词：calm, relaxed, tranquil|词性：形容词
