redirect:/ˌriːˈdaɪrekt/| vt.|重定向，改道|To direct (something, especially a course, path, or flow) in a different direction.|The website redirects you to a login page.  网站会将你重定向到登录页面。|The flight was redirected due to bad weather.  由于天气恶劣，航班被改道了。|reroute, divert, switch|proceed straight|verb


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The system redirects users to the homepage. = 系统将用户重定向到主页。
He redirects his anger towards his work. = 他把怒气发泄到工作上。


一般过去时:
The police redirected traffic around the accident. = 警察将交通改道绕过事故现场。
She redirected her career path after graduation. = 毕业后她改变了自己的职业道路。


现在完成时:
They have redirected all incoming calls to the new number. = 他们已将所有来电重定向到新号码。
The company has redirected its marketing efforts towards younger consumers. = 公司已将营销工作重心转向年轻消费者。


过去完成时:
He had redirected the funds before the audit. = 他在审计之前已将资金重新定向。
They had redirected the river to prevent flooding. = 他们已经改道河流以防止洪水。


将来时:
The website will redirect you to a payment gateway. = 网站将把你重定向到支付网关。
They will redirect the resources to the most urgent project. = 他们将把资源重新定向到最紧急的项目。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would redirect my efforts to something more productive. = 如果我是你，我会把精力转向更有成效的事情。
It would be better if they redirected the funding to education. = 如果他们将资金重新定向到教育方面会更好。


被动语态:
Traffic was redirected due to roadworks. = 由于道路施工，交通被改道。
The email was redirected to the spam folder. = 电子邮件被重定向到垃圾邮件文件夹。


疑问句:
Will the link redirect me to the correct page? = 这个链接会把我重定向到正确的页面吗？
Where will the water be redirected? = 水将被重定向到哪里？


否定句:
The system did not redirect me to the expected page. = 系统没有把我重定向到预期的页面。
The company did not redirect its resources to research and development. = 公司没有将其资源重新定向到研发。


PHRASE->

redirect traffic = 重定向流量
redirect a request = 重定向请求
redirect stdout = 重定向标准输出
redirect to a new URL = 重定向到一个新的URL
redirect a call = 转接电话
