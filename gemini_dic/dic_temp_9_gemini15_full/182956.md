replacement:/rɪˈpleɪsmənt/ | n. |替换，替代品，更换 | A replacement is a person or thing that takes the place of someone or something else.  It refers to the act of replacing something or the thing used as a replacement. | Examples:  The mechanic installed a replacement part for the broken engine. (机械师安装了一个替换部件来修理损坏的引擎。) They hired a replacement teacher while Ms. Smith was on leave. (史密斯老师休假期间，他们雇了一位代课老师。) |近义词：substitute, replacement, stand-in,  alternative |反义词：original, primary
