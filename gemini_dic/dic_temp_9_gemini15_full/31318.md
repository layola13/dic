chaps's:/tʃæpsz/| n.|家伙们的；章节的；|The word "chaps's" is a possessive form of "chaps," which itself is a shortened form of "chap," meaning a fellow or a man.  It indicates possession or belonging to a group of chaps.|例句：This is chaps's car. 这是那些家伙的车。|近义词：fellows', men's, guys'|反义词：无
