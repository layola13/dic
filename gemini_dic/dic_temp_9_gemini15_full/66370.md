excuses:/ɪkˈskjuːzɪz/| n.|借口，理由|Explanations or reasons, especially those given to justify a fault or failure.  They can be valid or invalid, depending on the context.|例句：He made excuses for his lateness. 他为他的迟到找借口。|例句：Her excuses were unconvincing. 她的借口令人难以信服。|近义词：reasons, justifications, pretexts, alibis|反义词：truths, facts, realities|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes excuses all the time. = 他总是找借口。
What excuses do you have for missing the deadline? = 你有什么理由没赶上截止日期？


一般过去时:
She made excuses for not attending the meeting. = 她为没有参加会议找了借口。
They made many excuses to avoid responsibility. = 他们为了逃避责任找了很多借口。


现在完成时:
He has made numerous excuses for his poor performance. = 他为糟糕的表现找过很多借口。
She has always made excuses to avoid hard work. = 她总是找借口逃避辛苦的工作。


过去完成时:
He had made excuses before, but this time it was different. = 他以前也找过借口，但这次不一样。
She had made several excuses, but none were believable. = 她找了好几个借口，但没有一个可信的。


将来时:
I will make an excuse if I am too tired to go. = 如果我太累了，我会找个借口不去。
He will probably make excuses again. = 他很可能会再次找借口。


虚拟语气:
If I had a good excuse, I would tell you. = 如果我有一个好的借口，我会告诉你的。
I wish I had a better excuse for missing the party. = 我真希望我有更好的理由没去参加派对。


被动语态:
Excuses were made for his behavior. = 为他的行为找了借口。
No excuses will be accepted for this mistake. = 对于这个错误，任何借口都不会被接受。


疑问句:
What are your excuses? = 你的借口是什么？
Are there any valid excuses for your absence? = 你缺席有什么正当理由吗？


否定句:
I will not accept any excuses. = 我不会接受任何借口。
He offered no excuses for his actions. = 他对自己的行为没有任何解释。
