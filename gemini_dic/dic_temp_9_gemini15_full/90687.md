humbuggers:/ˈhʌmbʌɡərz/| n. |骗子，江湖骗子，吹牛者|A humbugger is someone who deceives or tricks others, often through exaggerated claims or false promises. They are characterized by their dishonesty and manipulative behavior.|例句：He's a humbugger; don't believe a word he says. 他是个骗子；别相信他说的任何话。  He was exposed as a humbugger after his fraudulent scheme was uncovered. 他的欺诈计划败露后，他被揭露为骗子。|近义词：fraud, swindler, charlatan, imposter, con artist|反义词：truth-teller, honest person
