cat:/kæt/| n.|猫|A small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, a short snout, and retractile claws.  It is widely kept as a pet. |例句：My cat loves to sleep in sunbeams. 我的猫喜欢在阳光下睡觉。
例句：The cat chased the mouse across the floor. 猫追着老鼠跑过地板。|近义词：feline, kitty|反义词：dog
