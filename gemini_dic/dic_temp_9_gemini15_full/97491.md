ingeny:/ˈɪndʒɪni/| n.|巧思，才智；发明；独创性 | ingenuity; inventiveness; cleverness; skill in devising;  a clever invention or device |  例句：The engineer showed great ingenuity in solving the problem.  工程师在解决这个问题上展现了极大的独创性。

例句：His ingenuity led to the invention of a new type of engine.  他的巧思导致了一种新型发动机的发明。


近义词：inventiveness, cleverness, creativity, resourcefulness | 反义词：stupidity, dullness, ineptitude | 词性：名词
