outloves:/ˌaʊtˈlʌvz/| v.|胜过…的爱，爱得超过…|to love more than someone or something else; surpass in love|例句：He outloves his brother in his devotion to their mother. 他对母亲的挚爱胜过他的兄弟。|近义词：surpasses, exceeds, outstrips|反义词：underloves


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She outloves her sister. = 她比她姐姐更爱。
He outloves all his friends. = 他比他所有的朋友都更爱。

一般过去时:
They outloved each other. = 他们彼此深爱。
She outloved him in the end. = 她最终比他更爱。


现在完成时:
He has outloved all his previous relationships. = 他对之前的感情都付出过真挚的爱。
She has outloved her past heartbreaks. = 她已经超越了以往的伤心恋情。


过去完成时:
I had outloved him by the time we broke up. = 我们分手时我已经比他更爱了。
They had outloved their initial feelings. = 他们已经超越了最初的感情。


将来时:
She will outlove him one day. = 她总有一天会比他更爱。
He will outlove her in the end. = 他最终会比她更爱。


虚拟语气:
If she loved him more, she would outlove him. = 如果她更爱他，她就会比他更爱。
I wish I could outlove my jealousy. = 我希望我能战胜我的嫉妒。


被动语态:
His love was outloved by her devotion. = 他的爱被她的奉献所超越。
Their love was outloved by time. = 他们的爱被时间所超越。


疑问句:
Does she outlove him? = 她比他更爱吗？
Does he outlove her? = 他比她更爱吗？


否定句:
He doesn't outlove her. = 他并不比她更爱。
She didn't outlove her ex-boyfriend. = 她并没有比她前任男友更爱。


PHRASE->
outlove someone completely = 完全胜过某人的爱
outlove someone deeply = 深深地胜过某人的爱
outlove someone passionately = 热烈地胜过某人的爱
outlove someone unconditionally = 无条件地胜过某人的爱
outlove one's fears = 战胜恐惧
