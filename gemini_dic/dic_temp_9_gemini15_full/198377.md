showgirls:/ˈʃoʊɡɜːrlz/ | n. |脱衣舞女；歌舞女郎 |Female performers, typically in a revue or similar entertainment, who often wear revealing costumes and may incorporate elements of striptease into their act.  The term often carries a connotation of being glamorous but also potentially exploitative.|例句：The showgirls' costumes were dazzling and extravagant.  脱衣舞女的服装华丽炫目。

The showgirls put on a spectacular performance. 歌舞女郎们上演了一场精彩的演出。|近义词：dancers, entertainers, performers |反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting modesty or demureness could be considered antonyms in contrast to the often provocative nature of showgirls.
