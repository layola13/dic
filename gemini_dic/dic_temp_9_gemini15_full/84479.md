haltered:/ˈhɔːltərd/| adj.|用缰绳套住的；受约束的|Having been fitted with a halter.  The word typically refers to an animal, but can be used metaphorically for a person feeling restrained.|例句：The horse was haltered and ready for the ride. 这匹马戴着缰绳，准备骑乘。The new regulations left many businesses feeling haltered. 新的规章制度让许多企业感到受约束。|近义词：restrained, controlled, bridled|反义词：unrestrained, free, liberated
