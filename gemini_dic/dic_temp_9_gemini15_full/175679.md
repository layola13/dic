rapaciously: /rəˈpeɪʃəsli/ | adj. | 贪婪地；掠夺性地；强夺地 |  Acting in a greedy and aggressive manner, especially in taking something.  |例句：The company rapaciously acquired smaller firms to expand its market share.  这家公司贪婪地收购小型公司以扩大其市场份额。

The empire rapaciously expanded its territories through conquest and colonization. 这个帝国通过征服和殖民贪婪地扩张其领土。


近义词：avariciously, greedily, voraciously, ravenously, aggressively | 反义词：generously, charitably, selflessly


词源:

The word "rapaciously" is derived from the Latin word "rapāx," meaning "greedy," "rapacious," or "plundering."  The suffix "-iously" is an adverbial suffix indicating manner.  Therefore, "rapaciously" literally means "in a greedy and plundering manner."
