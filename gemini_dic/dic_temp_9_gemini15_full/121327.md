misfunctioning:/ˌmɪsfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/| adj. | 故障的；功能失调的 |  Describes something that is not working correctly or as intended.  It implies a temporary or intermittent problem, rather than a complete failure. | Example: The computer system is experiencing some misfunctioning. 电脑系统出现了一些故障。The misfunctioning of the engine caused the car to stall. 引擎的故障导致汽车熄火。|近义词：malfunctioning, faulty, defective | 反义词：functioning properly, working correctly
