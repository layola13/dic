sighlike: /ˈsaɪlaɪk/ | adj. | 叹息似的，像叹息一样的 | Resembling a sigh; expressing or suggesting sadness, weariness, or resignation.  |例句：Her sighlike whisper barely registered above the din.  她的叹息般的低语几乎淹没在喧嚣声中。


例句：The painting evoked a sighlike feeling of melancholy. 这幅画唤起了令人叹息的忧郁之感。


近义词：pensive, mournful, wistful, resigned


反义词：joyful, exuberant, cheerful


词性:形容词
