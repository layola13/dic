milliard:/ˈmɪljən/| n.|十亿；百万（美式用法）|A milliard is a unit of measurement equal to one billion (1,000,000,000) in the long scale and one million (1,000,000) in the short scale.  The usage varies depending on region and context. In American English, "billion" is used instead.  In British English, "billion" refers to a million million (10^12), while "milliard" refers to a thousand million (10^9).|The company's annual revenue exceeded a milliard dollars. 这家公司的年收入超过了十亿美元。(美式用法下)|billion (in American English), thousand million|million (as a smaller unit) |n.
