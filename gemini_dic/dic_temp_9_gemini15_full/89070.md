holostei:/ˈhɒləstiː/| n. |全骨鱼类|A group of ray-finned fishes characterized by having a bony skeleton, a heterocercal caudal fin (meaning the upper lobe is longer than the lower), and often ganoid scales.  They are considered a more primitive group compared to teleosts (modern bony fishes).|The holostei represent an intermediate stage in the evolution of bony fishes.|近义词：暂无精确近义词|反义词：teleosts (in the context of fish evolution)|名词
