geospatial:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈspeɪʃəl/| adj. |地理空间的，地理信息的 | Relating to or denoting data that is associated with a particular location on the earth.  Geospatial data can include location information (latitude and longitude), spatial relationships between features, and attributes associated with those features.  It is used in various applications including GIS (Geographic Information Systems), mapping, and location-based services. |例句：The company uses geospatial technology to analyze customer locations. 这家公司使用地理空间技术来分析客户位置。

例句：The geospatial analysis revealed a correlation between pollution levels and proximity to industrial zones. 地理空间分析揭示了污染水平与工业区临近程度之间的相关性。


近义词：geographic, spatial, locational | 反义词：non-spatial, abstract


词源:
The word "geospatial" is a combination of "geo-" (from Greek "geō-", meaning "earth") and "spatial" (relating to space).  Therefore, it literally means "earth-space" related.  Its emergence as a distinct term reflects the growing importance of integrating geographic and spatial data in various fields.
