reed:/riːd/| n.|芦苇；簧片；竖笛|A tall, slender plant with hollow stems that grows in wet places, often used for thatching or making mats.  Also refers to a thin piece of cane or metal used in musical instruments like clarinets or oboes.|例句：The wind rustled through the reeds by the river.  河边的芦苇在风中沙沙作响。|例句：The oboe's reed needs replacing.  这支双簧管的簧片需要更换。|近义词：cane, rush, bulrush|反义词：None specifically contrasting|词性：名词
