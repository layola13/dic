sate:/seɪt/| vt.|使饱足；满足；使厌倦|To satisfy (a desire or appetite), especially to the point of satiety.  To satisfy fully; to quell. |例句：The hearty meal sated his hunger.  丰盛的饭菜满足了他的饥饿。

The endless repetition sated her interest.  无休止的重复让她厌倦了。|近义词：satisfy, gratify, appease,  quench|反义词：starve, deprive, frustrate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sates his hunger with a large breakfast. = 他用丰盛的早餐来满足他的饥饿。
The constant noise sates my patience. = 持续的噪音让我不耐烦。

一般过去时:
She sated her thirst with a cool drink. = 她喝了一杯凉爽的饮料解渴。
The movie sated his desire for action. = 这部电影满足了他对动作片的渴望。

现在完成时:
They have sated their curiosity about the mystery. = 他们已经满足了他们对这个谜团的好奇心。
I have sated my appetite for sweets. = 我已经满足了我对甜食的食欲。

过去完成时:
He had sated his ambition by achieving his goal. = 他通过实现目标满足了他的雄心壮志。
She had sated her desire for travel by visiting many countries. = 她通过游览许多国家满足了她对旅行的渴望。

将来时:
I will sate my craving for pizza tonight. = 我今晚将满足我对披萨的渴望。
We will sate our thirst at the water fountain. = 我们将在饮水机旁解渴。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would sate my love for reading. = 如果我有更多时间，我会满足我对阅读的热爱。
I wish I could sate my curiosity about the universe. = 我希望我能满足我对宇宙的好奇心。

被动语态:
His hunger was sated by the delicious meal. = 他美味的饭菜满足了他的饥饿。
Her thirst was sated by a glass of lemonade. = 一杯柠檬水解了她渴。

疑问句:
Will this meal sate your hunger? = 这顿饭能满足你的饥饿吗？
Does this book sate your curiosity? = 这本书能满足你的好奇心吗？

否定句:
The small snack did not sate his hunger. = 这点小零食不能满足他的饥饿。
The explanation did not sate her doubts. = 解释没有打消她的疑虑。


PHRASE->

词源:
源自古法语 *satier，源于拉丁语 *satiare，意思是“使饱足”。  最终与拉丁语 *satis（足够）有关。
