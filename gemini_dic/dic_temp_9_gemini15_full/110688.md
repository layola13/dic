lineal:/ˈlɪniəl/| adj.|直系的；线性的；直线的|Coming in a direct line of descent from an ancestor; according to the line of descent.  Lineage is a direct line of descent from an ancestor; something that is lineal is in a direct line.|例句：He is my lineal descendant. 他是我的直系后裔。

例句：The lineal progression of the argument was easy to follow.  论证的线性发展很容易理解。|近义词：direct, lineal, lineal descent|反义词：collateral, indirect
