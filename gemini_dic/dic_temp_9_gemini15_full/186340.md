rhumba:/ˈrʊmbə/ | n., v. |伦巴舞；跳伦巴舞 | A lively dance of Cuban origin, characterized by a rhythmical swaying of the hips.  It can also refer to the music associated with this dance. |例句：She learned to rhumba while on vacation in Cuba. 她去古巴度假时学会了跳伦巴舞。
The band played a sultry rhumba. 乐队演奏了一首性感的伦巴舞曲。|近义词：dance, cha-cha, samba |反义词：N/A
