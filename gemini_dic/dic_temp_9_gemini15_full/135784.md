nonprogressiveness:/ˌnɒnprəˈɡrɛsɪvnəs/ | n. |非进步性，不进步，停滞 |The lack of progress or development; a state of stagnation.  It refers to a situation or system that is not advancing or improving.  This can apply to various contexts, including social, technological, or economic spheres. |例句：The nonprogressiveness of the company's policies led to a decline in market share. 公司政策的不进步导致市场份额下降。

The nonprogressiveness of his thinking was evident in his refusal to adopt new methods. 他的思维停滞不前，从他拒绝采用新方法这一点就可以看出。|近义词：stagnation, inertia, standstill, retrogression |反义词：progress, advancement, development, improvement

The project's nonprogressiveness was a major concern.  = 项目的停滞不前是一个主要问题。
His speech highlighted the nonprogressiveness of current education reform. = 他的演讲突出了当前教育改革的停滞不前。

Complex Sentence:

PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "nonprogressiveness."  The concept is usually expressed differently.  For example:  Instead of saying "The meeting showed nonprogressiveness," one might say "The meeting was unproductive," "The meeting stalled," or "The meeting made no progress."
