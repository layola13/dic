nocturne:/ˈnɒktɜːrn/ /ˈnɑːktɜːrn/| n. |夜曲；夜景；夜间活动|A nocturne is a piece of music, typically for piano, that is characterized by its romantic and dreamy atmosphere, often evoking the feeling of night. It can also refer to a painting or literary work depicting a nighttime scene. |Example: Chopin's nocturnes are renowned for their beautiful melodies and melancholic mood.  肖邦的夜曲以其优美的旋律和忧郁的情绪而闻名。|近义词：night piece, serenade|反义词：diurnal piece|名词
