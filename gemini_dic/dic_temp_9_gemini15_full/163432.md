preconsumes: /ˌpriːkənˈsuːmz/ | v. |预先消费；提前消费 | To consume (something) before it is officially available or before it is needed.  This usually implies a form of borrowing or advance access. |例句：The company preconsumed its advertising budget by launching a major campaign early in the fiscal year.  公司在财政年度早期就启动了一场大型宣传活动，提前消耗了广告预算。


例句：Many consumers preconsumed their holiday bonuses by making large purchases before the actual payout. 许多消费者在实际发放奖金之前就进行了大额购买，提前消费了他们的节日奖金。


近义词：anticipate (in the context of consumption), advance, exhaust (in the context of resources), deplete


反义词：save, conserve, postpone (consumption)


词性:动词
