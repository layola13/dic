rehabbing:/ˌriːˈhæbɪŋ/| v.|康复治疗；修复；重建|The process of restoring something to its former condition or improving its condition.  It often refers to the rehabilitation of injured people or damaged buildings or structures.|例句：The hospital is rehabbing patients after surgery.  医院正在对术后病人进行康复治疗。
例句：They are rehabbing the old warehouse into a modern apartment complex. 他们正在将旧仓库改造成现代化的公寓大楼。|近义词：rehabilitating, renovating, restoring, repairing|反义词：destroying, demolishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rehabbing the old house. = 他们在修复那栋旧房子。
He is rehabbing his injured knee. = 他正在康复他受伤的膝盖。


一般过去时:
She rehabbed her ankle after the accident. = 事故后她康复了她的脚踝。
The city rehabbed the park last year. = 去年城市修复了这个公园。


现在完成时:
They have rehabbed several buildings in the neighborhood. = 他们已经修复了附近几栋建筑物。
He has been rehabbing for months. = 他已经康复了几个月了。


过去完成时:
The building had been rehabbed before they moved in. = 他们搬进去之前，这栋楼已经翻修过了。
She had rehabbed her arm before the competition. = 比赛前她已经康复了她的手臂。


将来时:

He will be rehabbing his shoulder for several weeks. = 他将要康复他的肩膀好几个星期。


虚拟语气:
