expulsions:/ɪkˈspʌlʒənz/ | n. |驱逐，逐出；排出 |The act of expelling someone or something.  It refers to the forceful removal of a person from a place, organization, or country, or the ejection of something from a space or system. |例句：The expulsions of students from school were due to repeated violations of the code of conduct.  学校对学生的驱逐是因为他们反复违反行为准则。|例句：The volcanic eruption caused expulsions of ash and lava into the atmosphere.  火山爆发导致火山灰和熔岩喷射到大气中。 |近义词：ejections, dismissals, removals |反义词：admissions, incorporations
