necking:/ˈnekɪŋ/| n. v.|脖子，亲吻；(金属)缩颈；(瓶颈处)变细|The act of kissing passionately;  the narrowing of a part, as in a bottle or a piece of metal.  |例句：The couple were necking in the back of the car. 这对情侣在车后座热吻。

例句：The metal was necked down to a smaller diameter. 金属被缩颈到较小的直径。


近义词：kissing (for the kissing sense), constricting, narrowing (for the metalworking sense)|反义词：widening (for the metalworking sense)|名词，动词
