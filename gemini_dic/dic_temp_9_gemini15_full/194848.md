semi-axis:/ˈsɛmiˌæksɪs/ | n. | 半轴 | A semi-axis is half the length of an axis, particularly in reference to ellipses and ellipsoids.  In an ellipse, there are two semi-axes: the semi-major axis (half the longest diameter) and the semi-minor axis (half the shortest diameter).  In an ellipsoid, there are three semi-axes. | Example: The semi-major axis of the Earth's orbit is approximately 149.6 million kilometers. 地球轨道的半长轴大约为1.496亿公里。 |近义词: half-axis | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but the full axis could be considered an antonym) | 名词
