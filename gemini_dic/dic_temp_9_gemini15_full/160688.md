portions:/ˈpɔːrʃənz/| n.|部分；份额；菜量 |A part or share of a whole.|The recipe is for four portions. 这个食谱是四份的。|近义词：parts, shares, sections |反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He divides the cake into equal portions. 他把蛋糕分成相等的份。
The restaurant serves large portions of food. 这家餐馆供应分量很大的食物。

一般过去时:
She gave each child a generous portion of pie. 她给了每个孩子一大块派。
They split the profits into equal portions. 他们将利润平均分配。

现在完成时:
We have already eaten our portions of the meal. 我们已经吃完了我们那份饭菜。
She has received her portion of the inheritance. 她已经收到了她继承的那一部分遗产。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had already discussed their portions of the presentation. 会议之前，他们已经讨论了各自的演示部分。
He had finished his portion of the work before the deadline. 他在截止日期前完成了他的那部分工作。

将来时:
They will divide the land into smaller portions. 他们将把土地分成较小的部分。
She will receive her portion of the bonus next week. 她下周将收到她那部分奖金。

虚拟语气:
If I had more portions, I would share them with you. 如果我有更多的份额，我会和你分享。
I wish I had a larger portion of dessert. 我希望我有一份更大的甜点。

被动语态:
The cake was cut into several portions. 蛋糕被切成几份。
The tasks were divided into manageable portions. 任务被分成易于管理的部分。

疑问句:
How many portions do we need? 我们需要多少份？
What portions of the report are you responsible for? 你负责报告的哪些部分？

否定句:
He didn't receive his portion of the inheritance. 他没有收到他继承的那一部分遗产。
They didn't want larger portions of food. 他们不想吃分量更大的食物。


PHRASE->
