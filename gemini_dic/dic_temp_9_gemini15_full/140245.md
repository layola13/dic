offprint:/ˈɒfˌprɪnt/| n. |抽印本，单行本 |A separate copy of an article or chapter, reprinted from a larger work, often sent to the author or other researchers.  |The author received an offprint of his article after the journal was published. 作者在期刊出版后收到了他文章的抽印本。| reprint, separate copy |  |n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He requests an offprint of the article. 他请求一份文章的抽印本。
She often sends offprints to her colleagues. 她经常给同事们寄送抽印本。

一般过去时:
The journal sent me an offprint of my paper. 期刊寄给我了一份我的论文抽印本。
They received offprints of the chapter last week. 他们上周收到了章节的抽印本。


现在完成时:
I have received several offprints of my latest publication. 我已经收到了我最新出版物的几份抽印本。
She has already distributed all the offprints. 她已经分发了所有的抽印本。


过去完成时:
He had already requested offprints before the conference. 他在会议前就已经请求了抽印本。
They had received the offprints by the time of the meeting. 他们在会议召开时已经收到了抽印本。


将来时:
The publisher will send you an offprint next month. 出版商下个月会寄给你一份抽印本。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
