overfastidiously:/ˌoʊvər fæˈstɪdiəsli/ | adv. | 过分讲究地；过于挑剔地 | Excessively meticulous or scrupulous; showing an excessive concern for detail.  | 例句：He overfastidiously arranged the silverware, spending an hour on the task. 他过分讲究地摆放银器，花了整整一个小时。

例句：She overfastidiously cleaned her apartment, leaving no speck of dust. 她过于挑剔地打扫她的公寓，连一点灰尘都没有留下。


近义词：excessively meticulously, hypercritically, overly carefully, meticulously, scrupulously

反义词：carelessly, sloppily, haphazardly


词性: 副词
