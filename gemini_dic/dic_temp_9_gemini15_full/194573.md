self-strengths:/selfˈstrɛŋθs/| n.|自身优势，个人优势 |Personal strengths or abilities.  These are positive attributes or skills that an individual possesses and can leverage to achieve their goals.  They can be talents, character traits, or learned abilities. |例句：Recognizing your self-strengths is crucial for career planning.  认识到你的自身优势对于职业规划至关重要。

例句：She leveraged her self-strengths in communication and teamwork to lead the project successfully. 她利用她在沟通和团队合作方面的自身优势成功领导了这个项目。

近义词：personal strengths, strengths, advantages, capabilities, talents, skills

反义词：weaknesses, shortcomings, disadvantages
