give-backs:/ˈɡɪvˌbæks/ | n. |让步；回馈；返还 | concessions or compromises made, often in a negotiation or agreement; something given back or returned.  |例句：The union made significant give-backs in the contract negotiations. 工会在合同谈判中做出了重大让步。|例句：The company offered give-backs to its employees in the form of bonuses. 公司以奖金的形式向员工提供了回馈。|近义词：concessions, compromises, returns |反义词：demands, concessions(in the context of not giving any)|词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company gives back a percentage of its profits to charity. = 公司将一定比例的利润捐赠给慈善机构。
These give-backs are crucial for maintaining employee morale. = 这些让步对于维持员工士气至关重要。


一般过去时:
The union gave back some of its demands to reach an agreement. = 工会放弃了一些要求以达成协议。
Management gave back some of the previously implemented cost-cutting measures. = 管理层放弃了一些先前实施的削减成本措施。


现在完成时:
The company has given back millions of dollars to the community. = 公司已经向社区捐赠了数百万美元。
They have given back significantly to the community through volunteer work. = 他们通过志愿者工作为社区做出了巨大贡献。


过去完成时:
The workers had given back their initial demands before the final negotiations. = 工人们在最终谈判之前已经放弃了最初的要求。
Before the strike, the employees had given back several benefits to avoid job losses. = 在罢工之前，员工们已经放弃了一些福利以避免失业。


将来时:
The government will give back tax revenue to stimulate the economy. = 政府将返还税收以刺激经济。
We will give back to our community by sponsoring a local event. = 我们将通过赞助当地活动回馈我们的社区。


虚拟语气:
If the company gave back more to its employees, morale would improve. = 如果公司向员工返还更多东西，士气就会提高。
I wish the company would give back some of the profits to its workers. = 我希望公司能将部分利润返还给员工。


被动语态:
Significant give-backs were made by the union during the negotiations. = 在谈判期间，工会做出了重大让步。
Some of the benefits were given back to the company in exchange for job security. = 一些福利为了换取工作保障而被返还给了公司。


疑问句:
What kind of give-backs are being offered by the company? = 公司提供了哪些类型的让步？
Will there be any give-backs in the next round of negotiations? = 下一轮谈判中会有任何让步吗？


否定句:
The company did not give back any profits to its shareholders. = 公司没有向股东返还任何利润。
The union refused to give back any of its initial demands. = 工会拒绝放弃任何最初的要求。
