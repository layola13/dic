egocentric:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. |以自我为中心的，利己的 | Self-centered; thinking only of oneself and one's own needs or desires.  |例句：He's become increasingly egocentric in his old age. 他上了年纪后变得越来越以自我为中心。 |例句：Her egocentric behavior alienated many of her friends. 她以自我为中心的举动疏远了她许多朋友。|近义词：selfish, self-absorbed, narcissistic, self-centered |反义词：altruistic, selfless, unselfish


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is egocentric and doesn't care about others' feelings. = 他很以自我为中心，不关心别人的感受。
The egocentric boss always takes credit for the team's success. = 那个以自我为中心的老板总是把团队的成功归功于自己。

一般过去时:
She was egocentric in her youth but matured later. = 她年轻时很以自我为中心，但后来成熟了。
He acted egocentrically during the negotiation, ruining the deal. = 他在谈判中表现得很自私，毁了这笔交易。
