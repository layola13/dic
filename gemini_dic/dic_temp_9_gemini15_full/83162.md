guttersnipes:/ˈɡʌtərsnaɪps/| n.|街童，小流氓|Children who live in poverty and often engage in petty crime or anti-social behavior in the streets.  They are typically characterized by their ragged clothing, poor hygiene, and aggressive behavior.  The term is considered derogatory and offensive.|例句：The guttersnipes were harassing passersby for money.  那些街童在骚扰路人要钱。

The police chased away the group of noisy guttersnipes.  警察驱散了一群吵闹的街童。|近义词：street urchin, street child, delinquent, hooligan|反义词：gentleman, lady, well-behaved child|名词
