processions:/prəˈseʃənz/| n.|队伍；行列；游行；(事件的)连续发生|A procession is a group of people or vehicles moving together in an orderly way, often as part of a ceremony or celebration.  It can also refer to a series of events or things happening one after another.|例句：The funeral procession moved slowly through the streets.  葬礼队伍缓慢地穿过街道。

The procession of seasons brings changes in weather. 季节的更替带来了天气的变化。|近义词：parade, march, train, sequence|反义词：disorder, chaos
