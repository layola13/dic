itemising: /ˈaɪtəmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 列举；逐条列明 |  To list or detail separately; to specify individually.  This is the present participle of the verb "itemize". |


例句：

He was itemising the expenses on his expense report. = 他正在他的费用报告上逐条列出各项开支。

The teacher itemised the students' mistakes on their papers. = 老师在学生的试卷上逐条列出了他们的错误。


近义词：listing, detailing, specifying, enumerating


反义词：summarising, generalising


词性：动词


词源:  From "item" + "-ise" (a suffix forming verbs).  "Item" ultimately derives from Old French "item," meaning "also," "likewise," evolving to mean a single thing on a list.  The "-ise" suffix indicates the act of making something into items or a list of items.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She itemises her expenses every month. = 她每月都逐条列出她的开支。
They itemise the contents of the package carefully. = 他们仔细地列出了包裹的内容。

一般过去时:
He itemised the costs involved in the project. = 他列出了该项目涉及的成本。
She itemised the ingredients for the recipe. = 她列出了食谱中的配料。

现在完成时:
I have itemised all the potential risks. = 我已经列出了所有潜在的风险。
They have itemised the contributions received. = 他们已经列出了收到的捐款。

过去完成时:
He had itemised the damage before the insurance adjuster arrived. = 保险理算员到达之前，他已经列出了损失情况。
She had itemised the purchases before leaving the store. = 离开商店之前，她已经列出了购买清单。

将来时:

They will itemise the tasks for the project. = 他们将列出该项目的各项任务。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
