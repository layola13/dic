nonobsolete:/ˌnɒnˈɒbsəliːt/ | adj. |非过时的；现行的；仍在使用的| Not obsolete; still in use.  The term refers to something that is still current and relevant, not outdated or replaced by something newer.  |例句：This software is nonobsolete and still widely used. 这个软件是非过时的，并且仍在广泛使用。

例句：The techniques described in this book are largely nonobsolete, making it a valuable resource. 这本书中描述的技术在很大程度上是非过时的，使其成为宝贵的资源。


近义词：current, up-to-date, modern, extant, functional

反义词：obsolete, outdated, archaic, antiquated, passé
