defunct:/dɪˈfʌŋkt/| adj.|已不存在的；失效的；停止运转的|no longer existing or functioning.  A defunct organization or system no longer exists or operates.|例句：The old factory has been defunct for years.  那个旧工厂已经废弃多年了。|例句：The defunct company's assets were sold off.  那家倒闭公司的资产被出售了。|近义词：obsolete, extinct, defunct, expired, terminated|反义词：active, functioning, operational


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old railway line is defunct. = 那条旧铁路已废弃。
This system is defunct. = 这个系统已失效。


一般过去时:
The company became defunct last year. = 这家公司去年倒闭了。
The project was declared defunct. = 该项目被宣布终止。


现在完成时:
The organization has been defunct for decades. = 这个组织已经不存在几十年了。
The law has been defunct since 1990. = 这条法律自1990年以来就失效了。


过去完成时:
The factory had been defunct before they bought the land. = 在他们买地之前，工厂就已经倒闭了。
The tradition had been defunct for generations. = 这个传统已经失传了好几代了。


将来时:
The program will become defunct next year. = 该项目明年将停止运行。
The website is likely to become defunct soon. = 该网站很可能很快就会关闭。


虚拟语气:
If the company hadn't been defunct, I would still have a job. = 如果公司没倒闭，我仍然会有工作。
If the law were not defunct, we could sue them. = 如果法律没有失效，我们可以起诉他们。


被动语态:
The system was declared defunct by the government. = 该系统被政府宣布失效。
The project was made defunct due to lack of funding. = 该项目由于缺乏资金而被终止。


疑问句:
Is the old telephone exchange defunct? = 旧电话交换机还运转吗？（是否已废弃）
Is the website still defunct? = 网站还处于关闭状态吗？


否定句:
The machine is not defunct; it just needs repair. = 机器并没有坏，只是需要修理。
The organization is not yet defunct.  = 该组织尚未解散。


PHRASE->
a defunct business = 一家倒闭的企业
a defunct volcano = 一座死火山
a defunct law = 一条失效的法律
a defunct empire = 一个已灭亡的帝国
a defunct tradition = 一个失传的传统
