hammer:/ˈhæmər/ | n., vt. | 锤子；锤打 | A tool used for hitting things, typically with a heavy head and a handle.  |例句：He used a hammer to drive the nail into the wood. 他用锤子把钉子钉进木头里。

例句：The blacksmith hammered the iron until it was shaped. 铁匠反复锤打铁块直到它成形。


近义词：mallet, sledgehammer | 反义词：None (锤子没有直接的反义词) | 词性：名词，动词


词源:
The word "hammer" has Old English origins, stemming from the Proto-Germanic word *hammarą.  This ancestral word is related to other Germanic words for the tool, and ultimately connects to words suggesting striking or beating.
