prestretch:/ˈpriːstrɛtʃ/| vt.|预拉伸，预张紧|To stretch (something) beforehand, typically to improve its properties or performance.  This often involves applying tension to a material before it is used in its final application.|例句：The rubber bands were prestretched before being packaged to ensure they retained their elasticity.  橡胶带在包装前经过预拉伸，以确保其保持弹性。
例句：The fabric was prestretched to reduce shrinkage after washing. 布料经过预拉伸以减少洗涤后的缩水。|近义词：pre-tension, strain, tense|反义词：relax, slacken|动词
