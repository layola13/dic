oversway:/ˌoʊvərˈsweɪ/| vt.|压倒；过度影响；使动摇|To influence (someone) too strongly; to persuade (someone) to do something against their better judgment. It also means to cause (something) to sway or lean excessively.|例句：The strong wind overswayed the small boat.  强风使小船摇晃过度。

例句：His arguments overswayed the jury. 他的论点过度影响了陪审团。


近义词：overwhelm, influence unduly, overpower, dominate

反义词：support, bolster, uphold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong wind oversways the small trees. = 强风使小树摇晃过度。
His charm oversways many people. = 他的魅力过度影响许多人。


一般过去时:
The pressure overswayed his decision. = 压力左右了他的决定。
The propaganda overswayed public opinion. = 宣傳影响了公众舆论。


现在完成时:
The evidence has overswayed the judge's opinion. = 证据已经影响了法官的意见。
Years of hardship have overswayed his spirit. = 多年的苦难已经让他意志消沉。


过去完成时:
The previous witness's testimony had overswayed the jury's initial impression. = 前一位证人的证词已经影响了陪审团的最初印象。
The initial reports had overswayed the public's understanding of the event. = 最初的报道已经影响了公众对事件的理解。


将来时:
The upcoming election results will likely oversway the government's policies. = 即将到来的选举结果可能会影响政府的政策。
I fear the temptation will oversway my resolve. = 我担心诱惑会动摇我的决心。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the risks, I wouldn't have let his words oversway my judgment. = 如果我知道风险，我就不会让他三言两语影响我的判断。
If only I hadn't let my emotions oversway my better judgment. = 要是我没有让情绪左右我的判断就好了。


被动语态:
His opinion was overswayed by the media. = 他的意见受到了媒体的影响。
The decision was overswayed by external pressure. = 这一决定受到了外部压力的影响。


疑问句:
Was the jury overswayed by the defendant's emotional plea? = 陪审员是否被被告的情感诉求所影响？
Could the public easily be overswayed by false propaganda? = 公众容易被虚假宣传所影响吗？


否定句:
He was not overswayed by their threats. = 他没有被他们的威胁所吓倒。
The judge remained unswayed and was not overswayed by the emotional testimony. = 法官保持冷静，没有被情绪化的证词所影响。
