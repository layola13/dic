pythagorean:/ˌpɪθæˈɡɔːriən/| adj.|毕达哥拉斯的；毕达哥拉斯学派的；|Relating to Pythagoras or his philosophical and mathematical teachings.  The Pythagorean theorem is a fundamental concept in geometry. |例句：The Pythagorean theorem states that the square of the hypotenuse is equal to the sum of the squares of the other two sides.  毕达哥拉斯定理指出，斜边的平方等于其他两边的平方和。


例句：Pythagorean triples are sets of three integers that satisfy the Pythagorean theorem. 毕达哥拉斯三元组是一组满足毕达哥拉斯定理的三个整数。


近义词：Pythagoric (less common)


反义词：None


词性：形容词
