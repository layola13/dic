carpeting:/ˈkɑːrpɪtɪŋ/ | n. |地毯，地毯铺设 | Covering a floor with carpet.  It also refers to the carpet itself as a material. |例句：The carpeting in the hotel was plush and comfortable.  酒店的地毯柔软舒适。

例句：They are installing new carpeting in the office.  他们正在办公室安装新的地毯。


近义词：carpet, floor covering | 反义词：bare floor


词源:  From "carpet" + "-ing," the present participle suffix indicating the action or process of carpeting. "Carpet" itself originates from the Old French "carpite," which comes from the Medieval Latin "carpeta," meaning "a woolen cloth."
