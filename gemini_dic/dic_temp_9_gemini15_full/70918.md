firebird:/ˈfaɪərbɜːrd/ | n. |火鸟，不死鸟|A legendary bird, often depicted as having brilliant plumage and the ability to be reborn from ashes.  It symbolizes rebirth, immortality, and power.  The term can also refer to specific species of birds or to a car model.|例句：The myth of the firebird is prevalent in many cultures.  火鸟的神话在许多文化中都很盛行。

The legend of the firebird inspired the design of the Pontiac Firebird car.  火鸟的传说启发了庞蒂克火鸟汽车的设计。|近义词：phoenix,  (depending on context)  legendary bird|反义词：none
