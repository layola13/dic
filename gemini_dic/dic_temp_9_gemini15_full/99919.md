internecine:/ˌɪntərˈniːsaɪn/| adj.|你死我活的；内部斗争的；互相残杀的|  characterized by conflict within a group or organization;  mutually destructive.|The internecine struggle for power within the company led to its downfall. 公司内部的权力斗争导致了它的垮台。|近义词：destructive, deadly, bloody, fratricidal|反义词：peaceful, harmonious, cooperative
