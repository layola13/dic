recontacts:/ˌriːˈkɒntækts/| v.|重新联系，再次联系 |To contact someone again after a period of time.|例句：I will recontact you next week. 我下周会再和你联系。|例句：She recontacted her old college friend after many years.  多年后，她重新联系了她大学的老朋友。|近义词：contact again, get back in touch with, reconnect with|反义词：lose contact with, sever ties with


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recontacts his clients regularly. 他定期与他的客户重新联系。
They recontact potential customers after the initial meeting.  他们在初步会面后会重新联系潜在客户。


一般过去时:
She recontacted her former boss to inquire about a job opening. 她联系了她之前的老板，询问是否有职位空缺。
He recontacted his old friend after seeing him on social media. 他在社交媒体上看到老朋友后，重新联系了他。


现在完成时:
I have recontacted all the participants in the study. 我已经重新联系了研究中的所有参与者。
They have recontacted several candidates for the position.  他们已经重新联系了几位应聘该职位的候选人。


过去完成时:
Before sending the final report, she had recontacted all the interviewees. 在发送最终报告之前，她已经重新联系了所有受访者。
He had recontacted his family before making the final decision. 在做出最终决定之前，他已经重新联系了他的家人。


将来时:
I will recontact you as soon as I have more information.  我一有更多信息就会重新联系你。
They will recontact the supplier to discuss the delayed shipment.  他们将重新联系供应商，讨论延迟的货运。


虚拟语气:
If I needed more information, I would recontact the author. 如果我需要更多信息，我会重新联系作者。
I wish I had recontacted him sooner.  我希望我早点重新联系他。


被动语态:
The participants were recontacted by email. 参与者通过电子邮件重新被联系。
All the applicants will be recontacted within a week. 所有申请者将在一个星期内重新被联系。


疑问句:
Have you recontacted the customer service department? 你重新联系了客户服务部门了吗？
Will they recontact us about the issue? 他们会就这个问题重新联系我们吗？


否定句:
I haven't recontacted him yet. 我还没联系他。
They did not recontact her after the interview. 他们面试后没有再联系她。
