hyperaggregate: /ˌhaɪpərˈæɡrɪɡeɪt/ | n. | 超级聚集体；高度聚集体 |  A mass or cluster formed by the aggregation of many smaller units or particles, often to an extreme or unusual degree.  It often implies a complex or hierarchical structure. |例句：The researchers observed a hyperaggregate of proteins in the diseased cells. 研究人员在患病细胞中观察到蛋白质的超级聚集体。


例句：The formation of hyperaggregates is a key characteristic of many neurodegenerative diseases.  超级聚集体的形成是许多神经退行性疾病的关键特征。


近义词：aggregate, cluster, clump, mass, conglomerate |反义词：dispersion, scattering, dissemination |词性:名词
