pitchforking: /ˈpɪtʃfɔːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 用干草叉翻动；混乱；| The act of using a pitchfork, or the state of being in a chaotic or disorderly state.  |例句：The farmer was busy pitchforking hay into the barn.  农夫正忙着把干草叉进谷仓。 The recent political events have led to a pitchforking of the public opinion. 最近的政治事件导致了公众舆论的混乱。|近义词：forking, raking, stirring, turmoil, chaos |反义词：order, organization, calm
