pinery:/ˈpaɪnəri/| n.|松树林；菠萝种植园|A pinery is a plantation of pine trees or a place where pineapples are grown.  The term often evokes a sense of seclusion and sometimes even gloominess, depending on the context. |例句：The hikers lost their way in the dense pinery.  徒步旅行者在茂密的松树林中迷路了。
The local economy relies heavily on its pinery exports. 当地经济严重依赖其菠萝出口。|近义词：pine forest, pineapple plantation|反义词：open field, desert
