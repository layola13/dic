electrotonize:/ɪˌlekˈtroʊtənaɪz/| vt.|电紧张|To subject (a nerve or muscle) to the passage of an electric current, especially a weak one, to alter its excitability.|例句：The researchers electrotonized the nerve fibers to study their response to electrical stimulation.  研究人员对神经纤维进行电紧张处理以研究其对电刺激的反应。|近义词：stimulate, polarize|反义词：depolarize
