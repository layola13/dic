provocativeness:/prəˈvɒkətɪvnəs/ | n. |挑衅性；煽动性；刺激性 |The quality or state of being provocative; the act or instance of provoking.  It refers to something that is intended to arouse anger, debate, or other strong reactions.  This can be through words, actions, or even appearance. |例句：The provocativeness of his speech led to a heated debate.  他的演讲具有煽动性，引发了一场激烈的辩论。

The provocativeness of her dress drew unwanted attention. 她穿着的衣服具有挑衅性，吸引了不必要的注意。|近义词：stimulation, incitement,  irritation,  aggressiveness |反义词：inoffensiveness, mildness, calmness
