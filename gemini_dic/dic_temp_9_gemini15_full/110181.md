lies:/laɪz/ | n. v. |谎言；说谎 | a statement that is not true.  |例句：He told a pack of lies. 他说了很多谎话。
The witness's lies were exposed in court.证人的谎言在法庭上被揭露了。|近义词：fabrications, falsehoods, deception |反义词：truth, facts | 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lies to his parents. = 他对他的父母撒谎。
She lies about her age. = 她谎报年龄。


一般过去时:
He lied to me yesterday. = 他昨天对我撒谎了。
She lied about her qualifications. = 她对自己的资历撒谎了。


现在完成时:
He has lied to me many times. = 他多次对我撒谎了。
She has lied about her whereabouts. = 她对自己的行踪撒谎了。


过去完成时:
He had lied to her before she found out the truth. = 在她发现真相之前，他已经对她撒谎了。
She had lied on her application. = 她在申请书上撒谎了。


将来时:
He will lie again if he gets the chance. = 如果有机会，他会再次撒谎。
She will lie under oath if necessary. = 必要时，她会在宣誓后撒谎。


虚拟语气:
If he lied, he would be in trouble. = 如果他撒谎，他会惹上麻烦。
I wish she hadn't lied to me. = 我真希望她没有对我撒谎。


被动语态:
The truth was eventually revealed, despite the lies told. = 尽管说了谎，真相最终还是被揭露了。
Lies were spread about his character. = 关于他的品格，有人散布谣言。


疑问句:
Did he lie to you? = 他对你撒谎了吗？
Is she lying to us? = 她正在对我们撒谎吗？


否定句:
He didn't lie to the police. = 他没有对警察撒谎。
She doesn't lie often. = 她不经常撒谎。


PHRASE->
tell a lie = 说谎
white lie = 善意的谎言
a pack of lies = 一堆谎话
live a lie = 过着虚伪的生活
lie detector = 测谎仪

词源:
The word "lie" comes from the Old English word *lēogan*, meaning "to lie, tell a falsehood".  Its various forms have existed in Germanic languages for centuries.  The noun form developed from the verb.
