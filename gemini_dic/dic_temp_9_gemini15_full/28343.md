caranx:/ˈkæræŋks/| n. |鲹属鱼|A genus of marine fish belonging to the jack family Carangidae, characterized by their streamlined bodies, forked tails, and often silvery coloration.  They are found in tropical and subtropical waters worldwide and are popular game fish.|例句：Many species of caranx are prized for their fighting qualities when hooked.|近义词：jack (general term), horse mackerel (some species)|反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it's a genus)|名词
