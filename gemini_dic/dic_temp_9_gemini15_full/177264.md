reasserts:/ˌriːəˈsɜːrts/ | vt. | 再次声明；重申；坚定地维护 | To state something again in a forceful way; to maintain or reaffirm something strongly. |例句：The government reasserted its commitment to economic reform. 政府再次声明其致力于经济改革。|例句：She reasserted her authority over the team. 她重申了她对团队的权威。|近义词：reaffirm, restate, reiterate, insist on |反义词：retract, deny, refute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reasserts his innocence every chance he gets. = 他抓住每一个机会重申自己的清白。
The company reasserts its market leadership annually. = 公司每年都重申其市场领导地位。

一般过去时:
