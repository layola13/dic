re-express:/ˌriːɪkˈspres/| vt.|重新表达；再表达|To express something again or in a different way.|The scientist re-expressed his theory in simpler terms.  科学家用更简单的术语重新表达了他的理论。| rephrase, reformulate, restate|obscure, confuse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-expresses his feelings regularly. = 他定期重新表达他的感受。
They re-express their concerns to the manager. = 他们向经理重新表达了他们的担忧。


一般过去时:
She re-expressed her gratitude for the gift. = 她再次表达了她对礼物的感激之情。
The artist re-expressed the theme in a new painting. = 艺术家在一幅新画中重新表达了这个主题。


现在完成时:
He has re-expressed his commitment to the project. = 他已经重申了他对项目的承诺。
We have re-expressed our opinions several times. = 我们已经多次重新表达了我们的意见。


过去完成时:
She had re-expressed her concerns before the meeting. = 她在会议前已经重新表达了她的担忧。
They had re-expressed their doubts about the plan. = 他们已经对计划重新表达了他们的怀疑。


将来时:

They will re-express their support for the candidate. = 他们将重新表达他们对候选人的支持。


虚拟语气:
If he re-expressed his apology, she might forgive him. = 如果他再次道歉，她可能会原谅他。
I wish he had re-expressed his intentions more clearly. = 我希望他更清楚地重新表达他的意图。


被动语态:
