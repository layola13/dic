reimbursable: /ˌriːɪmˈbɜːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可报销的，可偿还的 |  Capable of being reimbursed;  able to be repaid.  |例句：The travel expenses are reimbursable upon submission of receipts. 差旅费提交收据后可报销。

例句：  The company has a reimbursable expense policy for employees. 公司有员工可报销的费用政策。


近义词：refundable, repayable

反义词：nonreimbursable, unreimbursable


词源:  Comes from the verb "reimburse," which itself is from the Old French "reimburser," a compound of "re-" (again) and "bourser" (to put in a purse, to pay).  The "-able" suffix indicates capability or possibility.
