modeler:/ˈmɒd(ə)lər/| n. |模型制作者，建模者 |A person who creates models, often using computer software or physical materials to represent real-world objects or systems.  They might design and build scale models of buildings, create 3D models for video games, or develop statistical models for financial forecasting. | Example: The architectural modeler meticulously crafted a miniature replica of the skyscraper.  建筑模型制作者一丝不苟地制作了摩天大楼的微型复制品。  The fashion modeler used digital tools to create a virtual garment for the runway show. 时尚模型制作者使用数字工具为时装表演创建虚拟服装。|近义词：model maker, designer, simulator|反义词：无
