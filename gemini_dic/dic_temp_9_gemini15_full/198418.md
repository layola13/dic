shrank:/ʃræŋk/| v.|收缩；缩小；退缩|To become or make smaller in size or amount.  It is the past tense of "shrink".|The wool sweater shrank after I washed it. = 洗完之后羊毛衫缩水了。
The company shrank its workforce due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济低迷，公司裁员了。|decrease, contract, diminish|expand, enlarge, increase|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wool shrinks after washing. = 羊毛洗后会缩水。
He shrinks from responsibility. = 他逃避责任。


一般过去时:
The sweater shrank in the dryer. = 毛衣在烘干机里缩水了。
She shrank away from the scary dog. = 她躲避那条吓人的狗。


现在完成时:
The fabric has shrunk considerably. = 布料已经相当程度地缩水了。
My confidence has shrunk after the failure. = 失败后我的自信心下降了。


过去完成时:
The clothes had shrunk before I could wash them. = 衣服在我洗之前就已经缩水了。
He had shrunk from the challenge. = 他已经逃避了这个挑战。


将来时:
The material will shrink if you wash it in hot water. = 如果你用热水洗，材料会缩水。
I think the market will shrink next year. = 我认为明年市场会萎缩。


虚拟语气:
If I washed it in cold water, it wouldn't shrink so much. = 如果我用冷水洗，它就不会缩水那么多。
If I hadn't been afraid, I wouldn't have shrunk from the task. = 如果我不害怕，我就不会逃避这项任务。


被动语态:
The clothes were shrunk in the wash. = 衣服在洗涤过程中缩水了。
The workforce was shrunk by 10%. = 员工人数减少了10%。


疑问句:
Did the shirt shrink after washing? = 洗完后衬衫缩水了吗？
Has the company shrunk its operations? = 公司是否缩减了业务？


否定句:
The clothes didn't shrink in the wash. = 衣服在洗涤过程中没有缩水。
He didn't shrink from the responsibility. = 他没有逃避责任。


PHRASE->
shrink from (something):  逃避（某事）
shrink back: 退缩
shrink to: 缩小到
shrink away: 躲避
shrink in size: 尺寸缩小
