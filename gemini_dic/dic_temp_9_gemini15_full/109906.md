liassic: /laɪˈæsɪk/ | adj. | 利阿斯期的 | Of or relating to the Liassic Series, the lowest series of the Jurassic Period.  This geological period is characterized by specific fossil assemblages, particularly ammonites. | The Liassic strata are rich in fossils. 利阿斯系地层富含化石。  The discovery of these ammonites confirms the Liassic age of the rock formation. 这些菊石的发现证实了岩层的利阿斯纪时代。 |  Jurassic, Jurassic Period |  Cretaceous |  adjective
