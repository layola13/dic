saunters:/ˈsɔːntərz/| v.|闲逛；漫步；轻松地走|To walk at a slow, relaxed pace.|He sauntered down the street, enjoying the warm sunshine. 他沿着街道漫步，享受着温暖的阳光。| stroll, amble, wander | rush, hurry, sprint | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saunters through the park every morning. = 他每天早上在公园里闲逛。
She saunters along the beach, enjoying the sea breeze. = 她沿着海滩漫步，享受着海风。

一般过去时:
They sauntered home after the party. = 派对后他们轻松地走回家。
He sauntered into the room and sat down. = 他漫步走进房间，然后坐下。

现在完成时:
We have sauntered along this path many times. = 我们已经沿着这条小路漫步过许多次了。
She has sauntered through the market, buying some fresh produce. = 她漫步穿过市场，买了一些新鲜农产品。

过去完成时:
They had sauntered through the town before the rain started. = 下雨前他们已经在镇上漫步了。
She had sauntered along the riverbank before she saw him. = 在看到他之前，她已经在河边漫步了。

将来时:
We will saunter along the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海滩上漫步。
They will saunter through the forest after lunch. = 午餐后他们将在森林里闲逛。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would saunter through the museum. = 如果我有更多时间，我会在博物馆里慢慢欣赏。
I wish I could saunter through the countryside. = 我希望我能漫步在乡间。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The path was often sauntered upon by tourists. = 这条路经常被游客们漫步其上。(不太自然)
The park is seldom sauntered through at night. = 公园晚上很少有人闲逛。(不太自然)

疑问句:
Does he often saunter along the river? = 他经常沿着河边漫步吗？
Will they saunter to the cafe after work? = 他们下班后会去咖啡馆闲逛吗？

否定句:
He didn't saunter; he ran all the way home. = 他没有漫步；他一路跑回家。
They didn't saunter through the streets; they hurried along. = 他们没有在街上闲逛；他们匆匆走过。


PHRASE->
saunter along: 沿着...漫步
saunter through: 漫步穿过
saunter down: 沿着...走下去
saunter off: 闲逛离开
saunter over: 踱步走近
