oversnow:/ˈoʊvərsnoʊ/| n.|越雪，雪上行走|The word "oversnow" refers to the act of traveling over or across snow, often implying a journey or passage through a snowy area.  It can also refer to the snow itself that is being traversed.|例句：The expedition planned an arduous oversnow journey to reach the summit. 探险队计划进行一次艰苦的越雪之旅以到达山顶。


The oversnow route was treacherous due to the deep drifts.  由于积雪深厚，越雪路线非常危险。|近义词：snow travel, snow crossing, traversing snow|反义词：None specifically, but contrasts with  "under snow" (if such a phrase existed).|名词
