reshift:/riːˈʃɪft/ | v. | 再转移；重新调整；改变位置 | To shift again or anew; to change the position, arrangement, or emphasis of something. |例句：The company had to reshift its marketing strategy after the product launch failed. 公司产品发布失败后不得不重新调整其营销策略。

例句：We need to reshift the furniture to make more space.我们需要重新摆放家具以腾出更多空间。


近义词：realign, readjust, reposition, relocate | 反义词：stabilize, fix, maintain


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reshift the priorities every quarter. = 他们每个季度都会重新调整优先级。
The company reshifts its focus regularly. = 这家公司定期调整其关注重点。


一般过去时:
The team reshifted its approach after the initial setback. = 在最初的挫折之后，团队改变了方法。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
