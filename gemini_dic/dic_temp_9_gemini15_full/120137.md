It is used as a proper noun and doesn't have a verb or adjective form.  |例句：We visited the historic mining town of Minas in Uruguay. 我们参观了乌拉圭历史悠久的矿业小镇米纳斯。|近义词：mine (as a place), pit, quarry |反义词：N/A
