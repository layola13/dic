midsized:/ˈmɪdˌsaɪzd/| adj.| 中等规模的，中型的 |  Describing something of a moderate size, neither large nor small.  It is often used to describe businesses, cities, or vehicles. | 例句：The company is a midsized firm with around 500 employees. 这家公司是一家中等规模的公司，拥有大约500名员工。


例句：They drive a midsized sedan. 他们开一辆中型轿车。


近义词：medium-sized, moderate-sized, average-sized


反义词：large, small, gigantic, miniature


词性：形容词
