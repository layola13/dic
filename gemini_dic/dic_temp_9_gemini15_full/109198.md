legumen:/ˈlɛɡjʊmɪn/| n. |豆类；豆荚；荚果|A legumen is a dry fruit that develops from a simple ovary and usually dehisces (splits open) along two sutures (seams).  It contains several seeds. Examples include peas, beans, and lentils.|The farmer harvested a bountiful crop of legumes this year.|peas, beans, pulses|  (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with fruits that don't develop from a single ovary or don't dehisce.)|名词
