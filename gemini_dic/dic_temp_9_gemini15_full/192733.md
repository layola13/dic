seabird: /ˈsiːbɜːrd/ | n. |海鸟 | A bird that lives near or on the sea and often feeds on fish or other marine life.  Seabirds are adapted to withstand harsh marine environments and often have specialized adaptations for diving, flying over water, and locating prey. | 例句：Many seabirds migrate thousands of miles each year. 许多海鸟每年迁徙数千英里。  | 例句：Albatrosses are magnificent seabirds known for their long wingspans.信天翁是翼展很长的壮丽海鸟。| 近义词：waterfowl, seafowl | 反义词：landbird
