overmeddling: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛdliŋ/ | v. | 过度干涉；瞎掺和 |  Excessive interference in the affairs of others.  |例句：Her overmeddling in family matters caused a lot of friction.  她的过度干涉家庭事务引起了很多摩擦。

例句：His constant overmeddling in their relationship finally drove them apart. 他不断干涉他们的关系最终导致他们分手。


近义词：interference, intrusion, meddling, intervention | 反义词：non-interference, abstention


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix meaning "too much," "excessively," or "above." (来自古英语 *ofer-,  表示“过度”、“超过”的意思)
* **meddle**: verb meaning "to interfere in something that is not one's concern." (源自古英语 *maþel,  意为“谈话；干预”)
* **-ing**: present participle suffix, forming a gerund (动名词后缀).


用法说明:

overmeddling 主要用作动名词，作主语或宾语。它也可以用作形容词，修饰名词。


注意:  overmeddling 通常带有负面含义，暗示干涉是不必要的，甚至是令人讨厌的。
