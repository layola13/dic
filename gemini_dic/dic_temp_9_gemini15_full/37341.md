commendable:/kəˈmɛndəbəl/ | adj. |值得赞扬的，令人称赞的 | Deserving praise; praiseworthy.  |例句：Her dedication to the project was commendable.  她的奉献精神值得赞扬。 |例句：The team's performance was truly commendable, given the circumstances. 考虑到当时的状况，团队的表现确实值得称赞。|近义词：laudable, praiseworthy, admirable, exemplary |反义词：reprehensible, blameworthy, deplorable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The work is commendable. = 这工作值得赞扬。
Her efforts are commendable. = 她的努力值得赞扬。


一般过去时:
His actions were commendable. = 他的行为值得赞扬。
The decision was commendable. = 这个决定值得赞扬。


现在完成时:
Her contributions have been commendable. = 她的贡献一直值得赞扬。
Their work has been commendable. = 他们的工作值得赞扬。


过去完成时:
The results had been commendable. = 结果一直值得赞扬。
His efforts had been commendable. = 他的努力一直值得赞扬。


将来时:
Her future achievements will be commendable. = 她未来的成就将值得赞扬。
The project's outcome will be commendable. = 该项目的成果将值得赞扬。


虚拟语气:
It would be commendable if you helped. = 如果你帮忙就好了（这将是值得赞扬的）。
It would have been commendable if he had apologized. = 如果他道歉了就好了（这将是值得赞扬的）。


被动语态:
The effort was commended. = 这份努力受到了赞扬。
The performance was commended as commendable. = 这场演出被赞扬为值得称赞的。


疑问句:
Is her work commendable? = 她的工作值得赞扬吗？
Are their efforts commendable? = 他们的努力值得赞扬吗？


否定句:
His behavior was not commendable. = 他的行为不值得赞扬。
The response was not commendable. = 反应不值得赞扬。


PHRASE->
a commendable effort  = 一份值得称赞的努力
highly commendable = 非常值得赞扬的
most commendable = 最值得赞扬的
deeply commendable = 深深值得赞扬的
particularly commendable = 特别值得赞扬的

词源:
From commend (verb) + -able (suffix).  Commend itself comes from Old French *comander, from Latin commendāre "to entrust, recommend, praise," from com- "together" + mandāre "to order, entrust," from manus "hand."
