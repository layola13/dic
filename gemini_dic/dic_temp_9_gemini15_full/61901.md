empty-nesters:/ˈɛmpti ˈnɛstərz/| n. |空巢老人 |Parents whose children have grown up and left home.  空巢老人是指孩子长大成人并离开家后的父母。|Many empty-nesters find themselves with more free time and pursue new hobbies. 许多空巢老人发现自己有了更多空闲时间，并开始追求新的爱好。  The empty-nester syndrome can be challenging for some parents. 空巢综合征对一些父母来说可能是一个挑战。|近义词：senior citizens (老年人), aging parents (年迈的父母) |反义词：parents with young children (有年幼孩子的父母)
