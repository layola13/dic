controversies:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːsiz/| /ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrsis/ | n. |争议，争论 |A controversy is a serious public disagreement or debate about something.  It often involves strong opinions and heated arguments.  |例句：The new law has generated considerable controversy. 这项新法律引起了相当大的争议。

例句：The politician's comments sparked a major controversy.这位政治家的言论引发了一场重大争议。


近义词：disputes, debates, arguments, quarrels, conflicts |反义词：agreement, harmony, accord, consensus |词性: 名词
