outshone:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊn/| vt.|胜过，超过；光芒盖过|to surpass or exceed in brilliance, excellence, or achievement|The singer's performance outshone all the others.|近义词：eclipse, surpass, excel|反义词：be outshined by, be surpassed by


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun outshines the moon. = 太阳的光芒盖过月亮。
Her talent outshines her peers'. = 她的才能胜过她的同龄人。

一般过去时:
His bravery outshone his fear. = 他的勇敢盖过了他的恐惧。
The new design outshone the old one. = 新设计胜过旧设计。

现在完成时:
Her achievements have outshone her expectations. = 她的成就已超过她的预期。
The team has outshone its rivals this season. = 这个赛季球队已经胜过它的对手。

过去完成时:
His kindness had outshone his anger in the end. = 最后，他的善良战胜了他的愤怒。
Their performance had outshone all previous attempts. = 他们的表现已经超过了以往所有的尝试。


将来时:
This technology will outshine its predecessors. = 这项技术将超越其前辈。
His work will outshine all others in the field. = 他的作品将在该领域胜过所有其他作品。

虚拟语气:
If only my work could outshine his! = 但愿我的作品能超过他的！
I wish my performance had outshone the others. = 我希望我的表现能胜过其他人。

被动语态:
His achievements were outshone by his brother's. = 他的成就被弟弟的成就超越了。
The old design was outshone by the new one. = 旧设计被新设计超越了。

疑问句:
Does her beauty outshine everyone else's? = 她的美貌胜过其他人吗？
Will his new novel outshine his previous works? = 他的新小说会超越他之前的作品吗？

否定句:
His efforts did not outshine his colleagues'. = 他的努力没有超过他的同事们。
The small company was not outshone by the larger corporations. = 这家小公司没有被大型公司超越。
