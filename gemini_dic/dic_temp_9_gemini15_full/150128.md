pamphleteer:/ˌpæmfˈlɪtiər/| n. v.|小册子作者；散布小册子的人|A person who writes, distributes, or sells pamphlets, especially one who does so to promote a particular cause or opinion.|例句：He was a prolific pamphleteer, known for his sharp wit and controversial opinions. 他是一位多产的小册子作者，以其敏锐的才智和有争议的观点而闻名。  The government accused him of being a pamphleteer spreading dissent. 政府指控他是一个散布异见的传单作者。|近义词：writer, propagandist, agitator |反义词：censorship, silence
