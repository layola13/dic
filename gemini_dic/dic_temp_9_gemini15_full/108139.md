latinize:/ˈlætnˌaɪz/ | vt. | 拉丁化；使拉丁化 | To make something conform to Latin standards or usage, especially in terms of spelling, grammar, or vocabulary.  This often involves replacing words or elements from other languages with their Latin equivalents or adapting words to fit Latin grammatical patterns. |  He attempted to latinize the spelling of the place names. 他试图将地名拉丁化。 The botanist latinized the newly discovered plant's name. 植物学家将新发现的植物名称拉丁化了。 |  romanize, classicize |  de-latinize | 动词
