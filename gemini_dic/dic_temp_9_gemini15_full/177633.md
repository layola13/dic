rebury:/ˌriːˈbɛri/| vt.|重新埋葬；再次掩埋|To bury again or anew.  The act of burying something a second time. This often implies a previous exhumation or a change of burial location.|例句：They decided to rebury their ancestor in the family plot after the cemetery was damaged by a flood.  他们决定在墓地被洪水破坏后，将他们的祖先重新埋葬在家族墓地。

例句：The archaeologist reburied the artifacts after carefully documenting them. 考古学家在仔细记录文物后将它们重新埋葬。


近义词：inter,entomb|反义词：exhume, unearth
