pandoras:/pænˈdɔːrəz/| n.|潘多拉（复数）|The plural form of Pandora, referring to multiple instances of Pandora's box or its metaphorical implications.  It often signifies multiple sources of unforeseen troubles or widespread calamities. |例句：The release of those documents opened a pandoras box of political scandals.  释放这些文件打开了一个又一个政治丑闻的潘多拉魔盒。

例句：The new policy has unleashed a pandoras box of unintended consequences.  这项新政策引发了一系列始料未及的负面后果。


近义词：calamities, disasters, troubles, woes


反义词：blessings, boons, good fortune


词性:名词
