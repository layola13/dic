creaser:/ˈkriːzər/| n. v.|褶皱；起皱；弄皱的人或物|A creaser is something that makes creases, or a person who creases things.  It can also refer to a crease itself. |例句：The iron is a useful creaser for clothes. 熨斗是熨烫衣服的有用工具。

The constant folding created deep creases in the map.  不断的折叠在地图上造成了深深的褶皱。


近义词：wrinkler, folder |反义词：smoother, iron


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The iron creases my shirt. = 熨斗弄皱了我的衬衫。
He creases his trousers every morning. = 他每天早上都熨烫他的裤子。


一般过去时:
She creased the paper carefully. = 她仔细地折起了纸。
The accident creased the car's bumper. = 事故弄坏了汽车的保险杠。


现在完成时:
