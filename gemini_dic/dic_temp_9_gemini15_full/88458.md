histographical:/ˌhɪstəˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. | 史学的；史学方法论的 | Relating to the writing of history and historical methods.  It focuses on the methods, approaches, and interpretations used by historians in their work, rather than the historical events themselves. |  The article offers a compelling histographical analysis of the French Revolution. 这篇文章对法国大革命提供了一个引人注目的史学分析。 |近义词：historical (but with a focus on methodology, not just events) | 反义词：ahistorical (ignoring historical context)
