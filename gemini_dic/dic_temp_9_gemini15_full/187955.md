rotor:/ˈroʊtər/| n. |转子，旋转体|A rotating part of a machine, especially one that spins within a larger stationary part.  A rotor is a rotating component of a machine, often found in motors, generators, and pumps. It typically consists of a shaft with blades or coils that spin to produce motion or energy. |Example: The helicopter's rotor blades spun rapidly. 直升机的旋翼叶片快速旋转。|近义词：rotating part, impeller, spinner|反义词：stator
