conceive:/kənˈsiːv/ | vt. | 构想；怀孕；设想；相信 | To form an idea, plan, or belief in the mind.  |  例句：She conceived the idea for a new type of computer. 她构想出一个新型电脑的创意。

例句：  The artist conceived a masterpiece.  这位艺术家构思了一幅杰作。


近义词：imagine, invent, devise, create, understand, believe

反义词：misunderstand, disbelieve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I conceive of a better future. = 我设想一个更美好的未来。
They conceive a new marketing strategy. = 他们构想了一个新的营销策略。


一般过去时:
She conceived a child last year. = 她去年怀了孕。
He conceived a plan to escape. = 他设想了一个逃跑计划。


现在完成时:
They have conceived several innovative projects. = 他们已经构思了几个创新的项目。
Scientists have conceived a way to solve the problem. = 科学家们已经设想出解决这个问题的方法。


过去完成时:
I had conceived the idea before meeting him. = 我在遇到他之前就已经有了这个想法。
She had conceived the possibility of failure. = 她已经预料到失败的可能性。


将来时:
We will conceive a new product next year. = 我们明年将构思出一款新产品。
They will conceive of a solution to the crisis. = 他们将设想出一个解决危机的方案。


虚拟语气:
If I conceived a way to do it, I would do it. = 如果我能想出一个方法，我就去做。
I wish I had conceived the consequences earlier. = 我真希望我早点想到后果。


被动语态:
The plan was conceived by a brilliant engineer. = 这个计划是由一位杰出的工程师构思出来的。
The project was conceived in 2020. = 这个项目是在2020年构思的。


疑问句:
Did you conceive of any alternative solutions? = 你有没有想到任何替代方案？
How did he conceive of such a brilliant idea? = 他是怎么想到这么好的主意？


否定句:
I cannot conceive of such cruelty. = 我无法想象如此的残忍。
They didn't conceive of the risks involved. = 他们没有想到其中涉及的风险。



PHRASE->
conceive of: 设想，构想
conceive in: 在...中构想
conceive a child: 怀孕
conceive a plan: 制定计划
conceive an idea: 产生一个想法
