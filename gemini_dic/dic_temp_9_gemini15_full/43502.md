cravats:/kræˈvæts/| n.|领巾，围巾|A cravat is a piece of cloth worn around the neck, typically as a necktie or scarf.  It's often made of silk or other fine fabrics and can be tied in various styles.|He wore a silk cravat with his formal suit. 他穿着西装，系着丝绸领巾。|The cravat was a popular accessory during the 18th century. 18世纪，领巾是一种很流行的配饰。|necktie, scarf, ascot| (no direct antonym, but could be considered opposite of a bare neck)|名词
