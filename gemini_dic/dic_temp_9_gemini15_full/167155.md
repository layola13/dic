pretabulating: /ˌpriːˈtæbjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先制表，预先列表 | The act of preparing a table or list in advance.  It often refers to preparing data for later tabulation or processing, typically using a machine or computer. | Example: The company was pretabulating the survey responses to facilitate faster analysis. 公司正在预先制表调查回复以方便更快地分析。 | 近义词: pre-processing, preparing, organizing | 反义词: post-processing,  randomizing
