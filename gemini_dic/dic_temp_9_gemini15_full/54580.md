dismalest:/ˈdɪzməlist/| adj.|最凄凉的，最忧郁的，最沮丧的 | Most gloomy, depressing, or dreary.  It is the superlative form of "dismal".|例句：The dismalest day of the year was the day my cat died.  今年最凄凉的一天是我猫死的那天。  The dismalest weather discouraged us from going for a hike. 最糟糕的天气让我们打消了远足的念头。|近义词：gloomiest, dreariest, most depressing, most cheerless |反义词：brightest, cheeriest, most joyful, most exhilarating
