discouragement:/dɪsˈkɜːrɪdʒmənt/ | n. |气馁；沮丧；泄气 |The feeling of having lost confidence or enthusiasm; a loss of hope.  It can be caused by setbacks, failures, or difficult circumstances. |例句：Her repeated failures led to a deep sense of discouragement.  她屡屡失败导致她深感气馁。

例句：The constant criticism from her boss caused her much discouragement. 她老板不断的批评让她非常沮丧。


近义词：defeat, despair, despondency, frustration |反义词：encouragement, hope, inspiration
