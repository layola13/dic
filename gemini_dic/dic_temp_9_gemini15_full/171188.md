psychometric:/ˌsaɪkoʊˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. n. |心理测量的；心理测验的；心理测量学 |Relating to the measurement of mental aptitudes and abilities.  Psychometric tests are designed to assess intelligence, personality, and other cognitive functions. | Example: The company uses psychometric testing as part of its recruitment process.  这家公司在招聘过程中使用心理测验。  The study employed psychometric measures of anxiety. 这项研究采用了焦虑的心理测量方法。|近义词：psychological, mental |反义词：physical, somatic
