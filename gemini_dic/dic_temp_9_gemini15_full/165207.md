preinitialing: /ˌpriːɪˈnɪʃəlɪŋ/ | v. | 预先签名；预先签署 | The act of signing or endorsing something before it is officially presented or issued.  This often involves adding a signature or initials to a document before it is finalized or distributed.  | Example: The CEO preinitialed the contract before sending it to the legal team for review.  首席执行官在将合同送交法律团队审查之前进行了预先签字。 | 近义词：pre-signing, pre-approving | 反义词：finalizing, post-signing |
