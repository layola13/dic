figeater:/ˈfɪɡˌiːtər/| n.|无花果食者；（尤指）吃无花果的鸟|A fig eater is someone or something that eats figs.  The term is often used figuratively to describe someone considered to be foolish or simple. |例句：Some birds are fig eaters, relying on the fruit for a significant portion of their diet.  一些鸟类是吃无花果的，它们的食物很大一部分依赖这种水果。

例句：He called him a fig eater, implying he was naive and easily tricked. 他称他为“无花果食者”，暗示他天真易骗。|近义词：fruit eater, figivore (more formal)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms implying intelligence or shrewdness.
