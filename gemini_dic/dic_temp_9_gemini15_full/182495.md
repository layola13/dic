renovations:/ˌrɛnəˈveɪʃənz/| n. |装修，修缮，翻新 |The process of repairing and improving a building or other structure.  It often involves updating outdated features and systems.|例句：The renovations to the old house took six months. 老房子的装修花了六个月的时间。

例句： Extensive renovations are planned for the museum next year.  博物馆计划明年进行大规模的翻修。


近义词：repairs, restorations, improvements, refurbishments

反义词：demolition, destruction
