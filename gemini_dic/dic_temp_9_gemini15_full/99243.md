intercommune:/ˌɪntərˈkɒmjuːn/| v.|  互相交往；互相沟通 |To communicate or interact with each other.  The term often implies a close or intimate connection between communities or groups. |例句：The villages intercommuned through regular trade and social gatherings.  这些村庄通过定期的贸易和社交聚会互相交往。

例句：The two neighboring towns intercommuned frequently, sharing resources and cultural practices. 这两个相邻的城镇经常互相交往，共享资源和文化习俗。


近义词：interact, communicate, intermingle, associate


反义词：isolate, separate, segregate


词性:动词
