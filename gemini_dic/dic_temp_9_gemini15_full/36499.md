coleslaw:/ˈkoʊlslɔː/|/ˈkəʊlslɔː/| n. |凉拌卷心菜|A salad made of shredded cabbage, usually with a mayonnaise-based dressing.  It's a popular side dish in many cuisines.|例句：The restaurant served delicious coleslaw with the barbecue. 这家餐馆在烧烤时供应美味的凉拌卷心菜。|例句：I prefer a creamy coleslaw to a vinegar-based one. 我更喜欢奶油味的凉拌卷心菜而不是醋味的。|近义词：cabbage salad|反义词：无
