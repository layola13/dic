corporality:/ˌkɔːrpəˈrælɪti/| n. |物质性；肉体性；形体；实体性|The quality or state of being corporeal; the nature of being embodied in a physical body.  It refers to the physical body and its presence in the world, often contrasted with spirituality or immateriality.|例句：The artist's work explores the themes of corporality and mortality.  这位艺术家的作品探讨了肉体性和死亡的主题。

例句：Her performance emphasized the corporality of the character. 她的表演强调了人物的肉体性。


近义词：bodiliness, physicality, materiality | 反义词：immateriality, spirituality, incorporeality | 词性:名词
