nontobacco: /ˌnɒnˈtəbækəʊ/ | adj. | 非烟草的 | Not related to or containing tobacco.  This adjective describes things that are free from tobacco or are alternatives to tobacco products. | 例句：The company is focusing on the development of nontobacco nicotine products. 这家公司正专注于研发非烟草尼古丁产品。

The growing popularity of nontobacco vaping devices is concerning health officials.  非烟草电子烟设备越来越受欢迎，这引起了卫生官员的担忧。| 近义词：tobacco-free, smokeless | 反义词：tobacco-based


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb or noun forms that follow typical verb conjugations.  Therefore, tense considerations are not applicable in the same way as with verbs.  However, it can be used in various sentence structures:

Affirmative Sentences:
He uses a nontobacco alternative. 他使用非烟草替代品。
The store sells nontobacco products only. 这家商店只销售非烟草产品。


Negative Sentences:
That product is not nontobacco; it contains nicotine from tobacco. 那产品并非非烟草的；它含有来自烟草的尼古丁。
This isn't a nontobacco vape. 这不是非烟草电子烟。


Questions:
Are those nontobacco cigarettes? 那些是非烟草香烟吗？
Is this a nontobacco flavor? 这是非烟草口味吗？


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases specifically using "nontobacco."  Its usage is generally straightforward and descriptive.  However, it often appears in phrases like:

nontobacco nicotine  非烟草尼古丁
nontobacco products 非烟草产品
nontobacco alternatives 非烟草替代品
nontobacco vaping  非烟草电子烟
nontobacco flavors 非烟草口味


词源:

The word "nontobacco" is a compound word formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the noun "tobacco."  "Tobacco" originates from the Caribbean Taíno word "tabaco."
