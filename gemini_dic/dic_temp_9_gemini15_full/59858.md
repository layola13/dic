ectropium:/ˈɛktrəʊpiəm/| n. |眼睑外翻|  A condition in which the eyelid is turned outward, away from the eyeball. This can expose the conjunctiva and lead to dryness, irritation, and other complications.  | Example: The elderly patient presented with ectropium of the lower eyelid, causing significant discomfort and dryness.  老年患者出现下眼睑外翻，导致明显的疼痛和干燥。|近义词：eversion, eyelid eversion |反义词：entropion
