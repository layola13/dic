outlawer:/ˈaʊtˌlɔːər/| n. |歹徒，不法之徒，法外之徒 |A person who lives outside the law; an outlaw.  |例句：The outlawer was finally captured after years on the run.  这个亡命之徒最终在逃亡多年后被捕获。|近义词：bandit, criminal, fugitive, desperado |反义词：law-abider, citizen


USAGE->

The outlawer hides in the mountains. = 歹徒藏在山里。
He becomes an outlawer because of poverty. = 他因为贫穷而成为亡命之徒。


一般过去时:
The outlawer robbed the bank yesterday. = 歹徒昨天抢劫了银行。

The outlawer has evaded capture for years. = 这个亡命之徒多年来一直逃避逮捕。
