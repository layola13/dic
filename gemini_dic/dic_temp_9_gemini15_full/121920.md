misshape:/mɪsˈʃeɪp/| vt.  adj. |使变形；弄坏；畸形；丑陋的 |To spoil the shape or form of something; to deform.  Also, an adjective describing something deformed or misshapen. |例句：The accident misshaped his face.  事故使他的脸变形了。  The misshapen tree was struck by lightning. 那棵畸形的树被闪电击中了。|近义词：deform, distort, malform |反义词：beautify, perfect, reshape
