prime-the-pump:/praɪm ðə pʌmp/| v. | 刺激；启动；预先投入资金 | To stimulate or activate something, especially an economy, by providing an initial input or investment.  This idiom originates from the action of pouring a small amount of liquid into a pump to get it started. |例句：The government is using tax cuts to prime the pump of the economy. 政府正在利用减税来刺激经济。

例句：The charity primed the pump by donating seed money to start the project.  慈善机构通过捐赠启动资金来启动项目。


近义词：stimulate, boost, jump-start, kickstart

反义词：depress, stifle, hinder, impede

词性：动词
