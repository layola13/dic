composer:/kəmˈpoʊzər/ | n. |作曲家|A person who writes music, especially classical music.  |Example: Beethoven was a prolific composer. 贝多芬是一位多产的作曲家。|近义词:songwriter, musician, artist |反义词:performer, interpreter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The composer writes a new symphony. = 作曲家正在创作一首新的交响乐。
He composes music for films. = 他为电影创作音乐。


一般过去时:
The composer wrote a beautiful melody. = 作曲家创作了一首优美的旋律。
She composed a song for her daughter. = 她为女儿创作了一首歌。


现在完成时:
The composer has written many operas. =这位作曲家已经创作了许多歌剧。
He has composed several successful soundtracks. = 他创作了几部成功的电影配乐。


过去完成时:
The composer had already finished the score before the deadline. = 作曲家在截止日期前已经完成了乐谱。
She had composed the music for the play months ago. = 她几个月前就为这部戏剧创作了音乐。


将来时:
The composer will compose a new concerto next year. = 作曲家明年将创作一首新的协奏曲。
He will be composing music for a new ballet. = 他将为一部新的芭蕾舞剧创作音乐。


虚拟语气:
If he were a composer, he would write an opera. = 如果他是作曲家，他会创作歌剧。
I wish I could compose music as beautifully as Mozart. = 我希望我能像莫扎特一样创作出优美的音乐。


被动语态:
The symphony was composed by a young musician. = 这首交响乐是由一位年轻的音乐家创作的。
The music was composed for a documentary film. = 这首音乐是为一部纪录片创作的。


疑问句:
Who composed this beautiful piece of music? = 这首优美的乐曲是谁创作的？
Did he compose the music for the film? = 他是为这部电影创作的音乐吗？


否定句:
He did not compose the music for the film. = 他并没有为这部电影创作音乐。
The composer did not finish the symphony. = 作曲家没有完成交响乐。
