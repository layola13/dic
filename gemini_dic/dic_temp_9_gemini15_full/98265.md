insertion:/ɪnˈsɜːrʃən/ | n. |插入；嵌入；添加 | The act of inserting something; something that is inserted.  It can refer to the process of putting something into something else, or the thing that has been inserted. |Example: The insertion of a new paragraph improved the essay's flow.  插入一段新文字改善了文章的流畅性。The doctor performed a dental insertion. 医生进行了牙齿植入手术。|近义词：addition, inclusion, implant |反义词：removal, extraction, deletion |名词
