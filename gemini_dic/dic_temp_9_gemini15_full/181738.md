relics:/ˈrɛlɪks/ | n. |遗物；遗迹；圣物 | Objects surviving from an earlier time, especially one that is of historical or archaeological interest.  Relics can be tangible objects or intangible traditions. |例句：The museum houses a collection of ancient Roman relics.  这个博物馆收藏着许多古罗马遗物。

The ancient ruins are relics of a once-great civilization.  这些古老的废墟是曾经伟大文明的遗迹。|近义词：remains, remnants, vestiges, artifacts |反义词：new things, modern creations
