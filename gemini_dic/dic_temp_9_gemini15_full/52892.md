dimission:/dɪˈmɪʃən/| n.|辞职；免职；解职|The act of resigning or being dismissed from a position or office.  It often implies a formal relinquishing of a position, particularly one of authority or responsibility.|例句：He submitted his dimission to the board. 他向董事会提交了他的辞职信。|例句：Her dimission from the company was unexpected. 她离开公司的决定出人意料。|近义词：resignation, retirement, dismissal|反义词：appointment, hiring, employment|名词
