castle:/ˈkɑːsəl/ /ˈkæsl/| n. |城堡；堡垒|A large strong building, especially a castle, that is built to be defended against attack.  A castle is typically surrounded by high walls and towers and has a moat. |例句：The medieval castle stood proudly on the hill.  中世纪的城堡骄傲地矗立在山上。

例句：The king resided in his magnificent castle. 国王居住在他宏伟的城堡里。


近义词：fortress, stronghold, citadel|反义词：hut, shack
