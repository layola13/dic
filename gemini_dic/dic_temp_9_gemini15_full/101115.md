inverted:/ɪnˈvɜːtɪd/| adj.|倒置的；反转的；颠倒的 | Turned or placed in a position opposite to the normal one.  The word describes something that has been turned upside down, reversed, or otherwise changed to its opposite.  | 例句：The image is inverted. = 图像是倒置的。
The pyramid is an inverted cone. = 金字塔是一个倒锥体。

近义词：reversed,upside-down, reversed,  upturned

反义词：upright, erect, normal, right-side up


词源:  From Old French *inverter (past participle of invertir), from Latin *invertere, from in- (“in, into”) + vertere (“to turn”).
