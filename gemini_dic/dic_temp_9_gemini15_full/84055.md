half-forgiven:/ˌhæf fərˈɡɪvn/| adj.| 半被原谅的；部分得到宽恕的 |Partially forgiven; experiencing incomplete forgiveness.  The term suggests a state of being forgiven to some extent, but not entirely. It implies that there is still some lingering resentment or unresolved issue. |例句：His apology was accepted, but he felt only half-forgiven.  他的道歉被接受了，但他感觉只被部分原谅。

He was half-forgiven for his mistake, but the consequences still lingered. 他犯的错得到了部分原谅，但后果依然存在。|近义词：partially pardoned, conditionally forgiven |反义词：fully forgiven, completely absolved
