coating: /ˈkoʊtɪŋ/ | n. | 涂层，覆盖层 | A layer of material applied to a surface to protect or decorate it.  | The car had a new protective coating of wax. 这辆车涂了一层新的保护性蜡。 The cake was covered with a delicious chocolate coating. 蛋糕上覆盖着一层美味的巧克力涂层。 | layer, covering, film |  (无明显反义词，可根据语境使用“absence of coating”或类似表达) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory applies a protective coating to all its products. = 工厂在其所有产品上涂上保护层。
This machine coats the metal sheets with a special paint. = 这台机器用特殊的油漆涂覆金属板。


一般过去时:
They coated the furniture with varnish to protect it from damage. = 他们用清漆涂抹家具以保护它免受损坏。
The workers coated the bridge with a rust-resistant paint. = 工人们用防锈漆涂刷桥梁。


现在完成时:
We have coated the entire wall with wallpaper. = 我们已经用壁纸覆盖了整面墙。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
