capacitate:/kəˈpæsɪteɪt/| vt.|使有能力；使胜任；授权|To give someone or something the ability to do something.  To enable; to empower.|例句：The training program is designed to capacitate employees for the new roles. 培训项目旨在使员工胜任新的角色。

例句：The new software capacitated the team to handle much larger datasets. 新软件使团队能够处理更大的数据集。


近义词：empower, enable, qualify, authorize


反义词：disable, incapacitate, disqualify


词性：动词
