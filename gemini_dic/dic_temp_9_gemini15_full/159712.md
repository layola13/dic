polychaeta:/ˌpɒliˈkiːtə/| n. |多毛纲动物|Polychaeta are a class of marine annelid worms, characterized by having many bristles (chaetae) on each segment.  They are a diverse group, inhabiting a wide range of marine environments. |Example:  The researchers discovered a new species of polychaeta in the deep ocean. 研究人员在深海发现了新的多毛纲动物物种。

The deep-sea polychaeta exhibit remarkable adaptations to their extreme environment. 深海多毛纲动物对其极端环境表现出显著的适应性。
