/nɑːnˈrɪðəm/| adj.|非节奏的，无节奏的|Lacking rhythm or regularity; not rhythmical.|The non-rhythm of the machine's operation was unsettling.|近义词： arrhythmic, unrhythmical, irregular |反义词：rhythmic, regular


USAGE->

This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" does.  The usage focuses on its descriptive function.

Example Sentences:


The non-rhythmic tapping annoyed everyone.  =  机器不规律的敲打声让每个人都很烦躁。

His non-rhythmic speech made it hard to follow. = 他说话没有节奏，很难听懂。
