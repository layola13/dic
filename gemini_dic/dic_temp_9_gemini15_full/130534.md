nonaccumulatively:/ˌnɒnəˈkjuːmjʊlətɪvli/ | adv. | 非累积地；非积累性地 | Not accumulating; not adding up cumulatively.  This adverb indicates that something does not accumulate or build up over time.  It emphasizes the lack of a cumulative effect. | Example:  The bonuses were paid nonaccumulatively, meaning each bonus was a separate payment and did not add to subsequent bonuses.  非累积性地支付奖金，这意味着每次奖金都是单独支付的，不会累积到后续奖金中。|近义词：separately, individually, independently | 反义词： cumulatively, progressively
