phototype:/ˈfoʊtoʊtaɪp/| n. |照片原型；照相原型；铅字样本|A phototype is an early form of a photograph, often used as a prototype or model for later, improved versions. It can also refer to a sample or specimen of type used in printing, showing the size and style of the font.|例句：The photographer showed us the phototype before making final adjustments. 摄影师在进行最终调整之前向我们展示了照片原型。The printer showed us a phototype of the new font.  印刷商向我们展示了新字体的铅字样本。|近义词：prototype, model, sample|反义词：final product, finished version
