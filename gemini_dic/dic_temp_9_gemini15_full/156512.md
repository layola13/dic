piccoloist:/ˌpɪkəˈlɪst/| n. |长笛演奏者|A piccoloist is a musician who plays the piccolo, a small flute-like instrument.  The term refers specifically to someone skilled in playing this instrument, often professionally.|The piccoloist delivered a dazzling performance of the challenging solo.|近义词：piccolo player, flautist (if playing other flutes as well)|反义词：None directly applicable.
