exuding:/ɪɡˈzuːdɪŋ/| v. |渗出；散发；流露 |To discharge or emit gradually; ooze.  It implies a slow and often noticeable release of a substance. |例句：The wound was exuding pus.  伤口正在渗出脓液。|例句：His confidence exuded from him. 他散发着自信。|近义词：oozing, emanating, radiating, emitting|反义词：absorbing, retaining


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant is exuding a sticky sap. = 植物正在渗出粘稠的树液。
He exudes confidence in his work. = 他在工作中散发着自信。


一般过去时:
The cut exuded blood. = 伤口流出了血。
She exuded an air of mystery. = 她散发出一股神秘的气息。


现在完成时:
The vase has exuded a strange odor. = 花瓶已经散发出一股奇怪的气味。
The situation has exuded tension for weeks. = 这种情况几周来一直弥漫着紧张气氛。


过去完成时:
The wound had exuded pus for days before he sought medical attention. = 伤口几天前就开始渗出脓液，他才去看医生。
The cake had exuded a lovely aroma before it was even taken out of the oven. = 蛋糕还没出炉就散发出了诱人的香味。


将来时:

The new perfume will exude a captivating scent. = 新香水将散发出迷人的香味。


虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
