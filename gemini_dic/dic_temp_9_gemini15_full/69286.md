feature:/ˈfiːtʃər/| n. v.|特征，特色；以…为特色|A distinctive attribute or characteristic.|The house features a large garden. 这栋房子有一个大花园。|characteristic, trait, attribute|defect, flaw|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This car features a powerful engine. = 这辆车有一个强大的引擎。
The new phone features a high-resolution camera. = 这款新手机具有高分辨率的摄像头。


一般过去时:
The article featured the latest scientific discoveries. = 这篇文章介绍了最新的科学发现。
The film featured several famous actors. = 这部电影由几位著名演员主演。


现在完成时:
The museum has featured many important exhibitions. = 这个博物馆已经举办过许多重要的展览。
Their work has featured prominently in the industry. = 他们的作品在业界非常突出。


过去完成时:
The play had featured a surprise twist at the end. = 这出戏结尾处有一个令人意外的转折。
The news report had featured the politician's controversial statements. = 新闻报道曾重点介绍过这位政治家的争议性言论。


将来时:
The magazine will feature an interview with the author next month. = 下个月杂志将刊登对这位作者的采访。
The conference will feature presentations from leading experts. = 会议将邀请领先专家的演讲。


虚拟语气:
If the project featured more interactive elements, it would be more engaging. = 如果该项目包含更多互动元素，它将更具吸引力。
I wish the presentation featured clearer visuals. = 我希望演示文稿包含更清晰的视觉效果。


被动语态:
The product was featured in a recent advertisement. = 该产品最近在一个广告中被推荐。
The singer was prominently featured on the album's cover. = 歌手在专辑封面上占据显著位置。


疑问句:
What features does this software offer? = 这个软件有哪些功能？
Does the car feature all-wheel drive? = 这辆车是全轮驱动的吗？


否定句:
The design does not feature any unnecessary details. = 该设计没有任何不必要的细节。
The menu didn't feature any vegetarian options. = 菜单上没有任何素食选择。
