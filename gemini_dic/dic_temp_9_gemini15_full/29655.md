catfights:/ˈkætfaɪts/| n.|猫打架；女人之间的争吵|A catfight is a fight or argument, typically between women, characterized by screaming, scratching, and hair-pulling.  It often implies a petty or spiteful quarrel.|Example: The two women got into a nasty catfight over a man.|近义词：brawl, quarrel, squabble, spat|反义词：agreement, harmony
