movie:/ˈmuːvi/ /ˈmuːviː/| n. |电影，影片|A motion picture; a film.|例句：I went to the movies last night.  昨晚我去看电影了。|例句：The movie was very exciting. 这部电影非常精彩。|近义词：film, picture, motion picture|反义词:N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The movie starts at 7 pm. = 电影晚上7点开始。
We watch movies every weekend. = 我们每个周末都看电影。

一般过去时:
I saw a great movie yesterday. = 我昨天看了一部很棒的电影。
They watched a horror movie last night. = 他们昨晚看了一部恐怖电影。

现在完成时:
I have seen that movie three times. = 我已经看过那部电影三次了。
She has not seen many movies this year. = 她今年还没看过几部电影。

过去完成时:
I had already seen the movie before he told me about it. = 在他告诉我之前我已经看过这部电影了。
They had watched several movies before going to bed. = 他们睡觉前已经看过几部电影了。

将来时:
I will watch that movie tomorrow. = 我明天会看那部电影。
We will go to the cinema to see a movie next week. = 我们下周会去电影院看电影。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would watch that movie. = 如果我有更多时间，我会看那部电影。
I wish I could watch that movie again. = 我真希望我能再看一遍那部电影。

被动语态:
The movie was directed by a famous director. = 这部电影是由一位著名导演执导的。
The movie is being shown at several cinemas. = 这部电影正在几家电影院上映。

疑问句:
What movie did you watch? = 你看了什么电影？
Have you seen this movie before? = 你以前看过这部电影吗？

否定句:
I didn't like that movie. = 我不喜欢那部电影。
They haven't seen the new movie yet. = 他们还没看过新电影。
