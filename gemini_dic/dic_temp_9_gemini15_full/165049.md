preilluminated: /ˌpriːɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先照亮的；预先阐明的 |  Already illuminated; previously lit or explained.  The term combines "pre-" (before) and "illuminated" (lit up or enlightened). It suggests something that has been lit or explained beforehand. | 例句：The preilluminated display case showcased the jewels beautifully.  预先照亮的展示柜精美地展示了珠宝。

The preilluminated manuscript made it easier for the scholars to read. 预先阐明的稿件使学者们更容易阅读。| 近义词：pre-lit, beforehand illuminated, previously explained | 反义词：unilluminated, dark, unexplained
