categorize:/ˈkætəɡəraɪz/ | vt. |分类，归类 |To arrange or classify something into categories.  |Example:  We need to categorize these files before we can analyze them. 我们需要先对这些文件进行分类，然后才能进行分析。|近义词：classify, sort, group |反义词：jumble, mix, randomize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She categorizes her books by genre. = 她按类型分类书籍。
They categorize customer feedback by product. = 他们按产品对客户反馈进行分类。


一般过去时:
He categorized the data into three groups. = 他将数据分成三组。
The librarian categorized the new books. = 图书管理员对新书进行了分类。


现在完成时:
She has categorized all the emails. = 她已经对所有邮件进行了分类。
We have categorized the survey responses. = 我们已经对调查问卷的回复进行了分类。


过去完成时:
They had categorized the information before the presentation. = 他们在演示文稿之前就已经对信息进行了分类。
The researchers had categorized the samples by size. = 研究人员已经按大小对样品进行了分类。


将来时:
I will categorize the photos later. = 我稍后会对照片进行分类。
They will categorize the responses by age group. = 他们将按年龄组对回复进行分类。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would categorize all the items. = 如果我有更多时间，我会对所有物品进行分类。
It would be helpful if we categorized the data more effectively. = 如果我们更有效地对数据进行分类，将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The items are categorized by color. = 物品按颜色分类。
The data was categorized according to the date. = 数据是根据日期分类的。


疑问句:
How do you categorize these events? = 你如何对这些事件进行分类？
How are the applicants categorized? = 申请人是如何分类的？


否定句:
He didn't categorize the documents properly. = 他没有正确地对文件进行分类。
They haven't yet categorized the responses. = 他们还没有对回复进行分类。


PHRASE->
categorize by = 按...分类
categorize into = 将...分类到
broadly categorize = 广泛分类
easily categorize = 易于分类
difficult to categorize = 难以分类
