declarator:/ˌdekləˈreɪtər/| n. |声明者；声明部分；声明式|A declarator is a part of a C or C++ program that declares a variable, function, or other program element.  It specifies the name and type of the element being declared.  In some contexts, it might also include other information such as storage class specifiers (e.g., `static`, `extern`) or pointers. |Example: In the declaration `int x;`, `int` is the type specifier and `x` is the declarator.|近义词：declaration (as a noun), specifier|反义词：N/A
