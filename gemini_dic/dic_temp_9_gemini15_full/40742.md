conveyed:/kənˈveɪd/ | v. | 传达；运送；表达 | To communicate or make known; to transport or carry. |例句：He conveyed his feelings through poetry. 他通过诗歌表达了他的感情。  |例句：The truck conveyed the goods to the warehouse. 卡车把货物运送到仓库。|近义词：transmit, communicate, transport, carry |反义词：conceal, withhold, suppress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conveys his ideas effectively. = 他有效地传达了他的想法。
The bus conveys passengers to the city center. = 公共汽车把乘客送到市中心。

一般过去时:
She conveyed her message clearly. = 她清楚地传达了她的信息。
The ship conveyed the cargo across the ocean. = 船舶把货物运过海洋。

现在完成时:
They have conveyed their concerns to the management. = 他们已经向管理层表达了他们的担忧。
The company has conveyed numerous goods to customers. = 公司已经向客户运送了大量货物。

过去完成时:
He had conveyed the news before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他已经传达了这个消息。
The train had conveyed the passengers to their destination. = 火车已经把乘客送到了目的地。

将来时:
They will convey the package tomorrow. = 他们明天将运送包裹。
She will convey her appreciation in a letter. = 她将在信中表达她的感激之情。

虚拟语气:
If he had conveyed his feelings, things might have been different. = 如果他表达了他的感受，事情可能会有所不同。
I wish I had conveyed my gratitude properly. = 我希望我表达了我的感激之情。

被动语态:
The message was conveyed effectively. = 信息得到了有效的传达。
The goods were conveyed safely to their destination. = 货物安全地运送到了目的地。

疑问句:
How was the information conveyed? = 信息是如何传达的？
How are the supplies conveyed to the remote area? = 补给是如何运送到偏远地区的？

否定句:
He didn't convey his thoughts clearly. = 他没有清楚地表达他的想法。
The company failed to convey the goods on time. = 公司未能按时运送货物。
