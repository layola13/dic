godling:/ˈɡɒdlɪŋ/| n. |小神；半神；神之子|A godling is a minor deity, often the offspring of a major god or goddess, or a being with divine or semi-divine powers but lacking the full status of a major god.  They are often depicted as youthful and less powerful than their parents.|The young godling showed great promise, though still lacking the wisdom of his father.|近义词：demigod, minor deity|反义词：major god, supreme being
