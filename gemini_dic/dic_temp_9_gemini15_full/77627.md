gel:/dʒɛl/| n.|凝胶；胶状物|A gel is a colloid in which a liquid is dispersed throughout a solid.  It is a semisolid substance that is often transparent or translucent. |例句：The hair gel made his hair stiff.  发胶使他的头发变得僵硬。

例句：The scientist studied the properties of the new gel. 科学家研究了这种新型凝胶的特性。


近义词：jelly, jell


反义词：solid, liquid


词性:名词
