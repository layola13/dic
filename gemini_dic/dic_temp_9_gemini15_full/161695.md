poverty:/ˈpɑːvərti/| n. |贫穷，贫困，缺乏 |The state of being poor; lack of the means of providing material needs or comforts.  |例句：Millions live in abject poverty. 数百万人民生活在赤贫之中。|例句：Poverty is a major cause of crime. 贫困是犯罪的主要原因。|近义词：destitution, indigence, penury |反义词：wealth, affluence, opulence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Poverty affects millions globally. = 贫穷影响着全球数百万人。
He lives in poverty. = 他生活在贫困中。


一般过去时:
Poverty was widespread in the 19th century. = 19世纪贫困十分普遍。
The family fell into poverty after the father lost his job. = 父亲失业后，这个家庭陷入了贫困。


现在完成时:
Poverty has increased in many developing countries. = 在许多发展中国家，贫困有所增加。
She has overcome poverty through hard work. = 她通过努力工作克服了贫困。


过去完成时:
They had lived in poverty for many years before they received help. = 在得到帮助之前，他们已经贫困多年了。
The country had suffered from poverty for decades. = 这个国家几十年来一直遭受贫困之苦。


将来时:
Poverty will continue to be a major problem unless we take action. = 除非我们采取行动，否则贫困将继续成为一个主要问题。
Experts predict that poverty will decrease in the next decade. = 专家预测，未来十年贫困将会减少。


虚拟语气:
If there were no poverty, the world would be a better place. = 如果没有贫困，世界将会变得更好。
I wish poverty could be eradicated. = 我希望贫困能够被根除。

The problem of poverty is being addressed by many organizations. = 许多组织正在解决贫困问题。


疑问句:

否定句:
