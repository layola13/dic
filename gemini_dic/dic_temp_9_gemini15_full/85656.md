haustellate:/ˈhɔːstəleɪt/| adj.|吻吸式的；喙状的|Having a haustellum (a sucking proboscis, like that of a bug).  Describes a mouthpart adapted for piercing and sucking.  |Example: The haustellate mouthparts of the butterfly allowed it to feed on nectar.  蝴蝶的吻吸式口器使它能够吸食花蜜。|近义词：sucking, proboscidial|反义词：mandibulate (chewing)
