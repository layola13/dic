leisures:/ˈlɛʒərz/| n.|空闲时间；休闲；娱乐|Free time used for enjoyment.  The word typically refers to a period of time, or a collection of activities done during such a period.|例句：I enjoy my leisures by reading and hiking. 我喜欢在空闲时间阅读和远足。

例句：My leisures are often spent gardening and listening to music. 我的休闲时间通常用于园艺和听音乐。


近义词：free time, relaxation, recreation, pastime, hobby

反义词：work, toil, labor, occupation


词性：名词


词源：源自古法语“loisir”，意为“许可，允许”。  最终源自拉丁语“licere”，意为“被允许”。  其含义演变为指允许人们从事娱乐活动的自由时间。
