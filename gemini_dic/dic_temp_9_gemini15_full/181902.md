remainder:/rɪˈmeɪndər/| n. |剩余部分，余数，残余|The part that is left over after subtraction, division, or other operation.  It can also refer to something left behind or remaining.|例句：The remainder of the cake was eaten by the dog.  剩下的蛋糕被狗吃掉了。

例句：After dividing 17 by 5, the remainder is 2.  17除以5，余数是2。

近义词：residue, remnant, rest, leftover |反义词：whole, entirety

词源:
The word "remainder" comes from the Old French word "remanant," meaning "remaining."  This in turn derives from the verb "remaner," meaning "to remain."  The ultimate origin is the Latin verb "manere," meaning "to remain," with the prefix "re-" intensifying the meaning.
