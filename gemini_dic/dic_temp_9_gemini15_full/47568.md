debark:/dɪˈbɑːrk/| vt.|下船；下飞机；卸货 |To leave a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.  |例句：The passengers debarked from the ship.  乘客们下了船。|例句：The cargo was carefully debarked at the port. 货物在港口小心地卸下。|近义词：disembark, alight, unload |反义词：embark


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They debark the cargo every morning. = 他们每天早上卸货。
The passengers debark at the terminal. = 乘客们在航站楼下船/飞机。

一般过去时:
We debarked the ship at dawn. = 我们在黎明时分下了船。
The tourists debarked at the airport. = 游客们在机场下飞机。

现在完成时:
They have debarked all the passengers. = 他们已经把所有乘客都放下了。
The crew has debarked the goods safely. = 船员已经安全地卸下了货物。

过去完成时:
The passengers had debarked before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，乘客们已经下船了。
The workers had debarked all the crates before lunch. = 工人们在午饭前已经卸下了所有板条箱。

将来时:
We will debark in Miami tomorrow. = 我们明天将在迈阿密下船。
The plane will debark passengers at gate 5. = 飞机将在5号登机口下客。

虚拟语气:
If they had debarked earlier, they wouldn't have missed the train. = 如果他们早点下船，就不会错过火车了。
I wish I had debarked at the previous stop. = 我希望我在前一站就下车了。

被动语态:
The passengers were debarked quickly and efficiently. = 乘客们被迅速而有效地安排下船/飞机。
The goods were debarked with great care. = 货物被小心地卸下。

疑问句:
When will the passengers debark? = 乘客们什么时候下船/飞机？
Where will the ship debark its cargo? = 船将在哪里卸货？

否定句:
They didn't debark until the storm subsided. = 直到暴风雨平息，他们才下船。
The plane didn't debark the passengers on time. = 飞机没有按时下客。


PHRASE->
debark from a ship = 从船上下来
debark at a port = 在港口下船
debark the passengers = 让乘客下船/飞机
debark the cargo = 卸货
carefully debark = 小心地卸下


词源:
From Old French *desbarquer, from des- + barque "ship".  Ultimately of Germanic origin.
