commissaries:/ˈkɑːmɪsɛəriːz/| n.|军需处；伙食供应处；专卖店|A commissary is a store, often on a military base or in a remote location, that sells food and other essential goods.  It can also refer to a place where food is prepared and served, such as a mess hall.|Example: The soldiers visited the commissary to buy groceries.  士兵们去军需处买杂货。 The film crew relied on the commissary for meals during the long shoot. 电影摄制组在长时间拍摄期间依靠伙食供应处提供膳食。|近义词：store, shop, canteen, mess hall|反义词：N/A
