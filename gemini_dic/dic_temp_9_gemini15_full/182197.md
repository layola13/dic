remollifying: /ˌriːˈmɒlɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 使恢复柔软；使恢复柔顺 |  The act of making something soft or supple again; restoring softness or pliancy.  This word is a relatively rare and newly coined word, not found in standard dictionaries. It's formed from "re-" (again) + "mollify" (to soften). | 例句：The conditioner promised to remollify my dry, brittle hair.  = 护发素承诺能使我干燥易碎的头发恢复柔顺。  |近义词：soften, soothe, supple,  |反义词：harden, stiffen
