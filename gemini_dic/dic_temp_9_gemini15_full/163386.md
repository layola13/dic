preconsenting:/ˌpriːkənˈsɛntɪŋ/| adj.|预先同意；预先准许|Existing or done before consent is given; implying consent in advance.|The preconsenting agreement covered all potential risks.  预先同意的协议涵盖了所有潜在的风险。|近义词：prior consenting, advance consent|反义词：post-consent, objecting
