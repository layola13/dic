reintering:/ˌriːˈɪntəriŋ/| v.|重新进入，再次进入|The act of entering again; returning to a place or situation after having left.  It implies a previous entry.|例句：After a brief intermission, the performers were reentering the stage.  演员们短暂休息后重新回到舞台上。

例句：The astronaut, after his spacewalk, was reentering the spacecraft. 宇航员在太空行走后重新进入宇宙飞船。


近义词：re-entering, returning, going back, coming back|反义词：exiting, leaving, departing|动词
