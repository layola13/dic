self-confession:/ˌsɛlf kənˈfɛʃən/| n.| 自白，坦白 | A self-confession is a statement in which someone admits to having done something wrong or illegal.  It often involves revealing personal guilt or wrongdoing. |例句：He made a self-confession to the police. 他向警方做了自白。|例句：Her self-confession shocked everyone. 她的自白震惊了所有人。|近义词：confession, admission, avowal |反义词：denial, disavowal|名词
