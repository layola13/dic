fibber:/ˈfɪbər/| n.|说谎者，爱说谎的人|A person who tells lies, especially small, unimportant ones.|例句：He's such a fibber; you can't believe a word he says. 他是个说谎者，你一句都别信他。|例句：Don't be such a fibber, tell me the truth! 别再撒谎了，告诉我真相！|近义词：liar, prevaricator, storyteller (in a negative context)|反义词：truth-teller, honest person|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fibs about his age. = 他谎报年龄。
She fibs to her parents about her grades. = 她向父母撒谎隐瞒她的成绩。


一般过去时:
He fibbed about seeing the movie. = 他谎称看过那部电影。
She fibbed to avoid getting in trouble. = 她撒谎是为了避免惹麻烦。


现在完成时:
He has fibbed to me many times. = 他多次对我撒谎。
She has fibbed about her whereabouts. = 她对自己的行踪撒谎了。


过去完成时:
He had fibbed about his qualifications before the interview. = 他在面试前就对自己的资格撒谎了。
She had fibbed to cover up her mistake. = 她撒谎是为了掩盖自己的错误。


将来时:
He will fib again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他会再次撒谎。
She will fib to protect her friend. = 她会撒谎来保护她的朋友。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't fibbed, he wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果他不撒谎，他就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish she hadn't fibbed to me. = 我真希望她没有对我撒谎。


被动语态:
His lies were often described as harmless fibs. = 他的谎言常被描述为无害的谎话。
The truth was eventually discovered, despite his attempts to fib. = 尽管他试图撒谎，真相最终还是被发现了。


疑问句:

Was she fibbing when she said that? = 她当时说那话是在撒谎吗？


否定句:

She didn't fib this time. = 她这次没有撒谎。
