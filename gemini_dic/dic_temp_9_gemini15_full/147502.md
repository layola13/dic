overpackages:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒɪz/| n.|过度包装；包装过多 |The excessive packaging of products, often resulting in more waste than necessary.  It refers to the practice of using more packaging material than is actually needed to protect or contain a product. This can lead to environmental concerns due to increased waste and resource consumption.|例句：The company is criticized for its overpackaging, which contributes significantly to landfill waste.  这家公司因其过度包装而受到批评，这极大地增加了垃圾填埋场的垃圾。|近义词：excess packaging, superfluous packaging, excessive wrapping|反义词：minimal packaging, eco-friendly packaging|名词
