lambs:/læmz/ | n. | 小羊羔，羔羊 | Young sheep, especially those less than a year old.  They are often raised for their meat (lamb) or wool. | The farmer is shearing his lambs.  这位农民正在给他的小羊羔剪羊毛。| The lambs were playing in the field.  小羊羔们在田野里玩耍。| kids, young sheep | old sheep
