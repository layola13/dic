saut:/soʊ/ | v. |  快速翻炒；跳跃 | To cook (food) quickly in a small amount of fat over high heat, stirring constantly.  Also, to jump or leap. | 例句：He sautéed the onions and peppers before adding the chicken. 他在加入鸡肉之前快速翻炒了洋葱和青椒。  The deer sautéed across the field. 鹿跳跃着穿过田野。 | 近义词：stir-fry, fry, jump, leap | 反义词：simmer, stew
