reschools:/ˌriːˈskuːlz/| v. | 再教育；重新上学；对…进行再培训 | To educate or train someone again, often in a different way or for a different purpose.  It can refer to individuals returning to education or institutions changing their teaching methods. | 例句：The company reschools its employees annually to update their skills.  公司每年都会对员工进行再培训以更新他们的技能。| 例句：After a career break, she decided to reschool herself in computer science.  在职业生涯中断后，她决定重新学习计算机科学。| 近义词：re-educate, retrain, re-skill | 反义词：de-school, un-educate (informal)
