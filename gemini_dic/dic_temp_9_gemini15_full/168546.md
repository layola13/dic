procedures:/prəˈsiːdʒərz/ | n. |程序，步骤，手续 | A series of actions conducted in a certain order or manner.  |Example:  The procedures for applying for a visa are quite complex.  申请签证的程序相当复杂。


The company has strict procedures for handling customer complaints. 这家公司有严格的处理客户投诉的程序。|近义词：methods, steps, processes, protocols |反义词：chaos, disorder
