parricidal:/ˈpærɪsaɪdl/| adj.|杀父杀母的；弑亲的| Relating to or characterized by the killing of one's father or mother.  The term can also be extended to include the killing of other close relatives. |例句：The novel explores the dark themes of parricidal rage and familial betrayal. 这部小说探讨了杀父仇恨和家庭背叛的黑暗主题。

The ancient Greek myth of Oedipus Rex is a classic example of a parricidal act.  俄狄浦斯王的故事是弑父行为的经典例子。|近义词：patricide, matricide, homicidal|反义词：filial, pious
