congratses:/kənˈɡrætsɪz/| n.|祝贺，恭喜|Informal plural of "congrats."  It's a playful or slightly informal way of expressing congratulations.|例句：Give my congratses to the happy couple!  把我的祝贺送给这对幸福的夫妇！|近义词：congratulations, felicitations|反义词：condolences


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My congratses are with you. = 我祝贺你。
I send my congratses to the winners. = 我向获胜者表示祝贺。

一般过去时:
My congratses were for their success. = 我祝贺他们的成功。
I gave my congratses to them yesterday. = 我昨天向他们表示祝贺。

现在完成时:
I have sent my congratses. = 我已经表达了祝贺。
They have received my congratses. = 他们已经收到了我的祝贺。

过去完成时:
I had already sent my congratses before the announcement. = 在宣布之前我已经表达了祝贺。
They had received my congratses before the party. = 他们在派对之前已经收到了我的祝贺。

将来时:
I will send my congratses tomorrow. = 我明天会送上我的祝贺。
They will receive my congratses soon. = 他们很快就会收到我的祝贺。

虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have sent my congratses earlier. = 如果我知道，我会早点表示祝贺。
I wish I had sent my congratses sooner. = 我希望我早点表示祝贺。

被动语态:
Their success was greeted with many congratses. = 他们的成功受到了许多祝贺。
The winners were showered with congratses. = 获胜者收到了许多祝贺。

疑问句:
Have you given your congratses? = 你表达祝贺了吗？
Did they receive your congratses? = 他们收到你的祝贺了吗？

否定句:
I didn't give them my congratses. = 我没有向他们表示祝贺。
They didn't receive my congratses in time. = 他们没有及时收到我的祝贺。
