pre-empt:/priːˈempt/| vt.|抢先，先发制人；取代，占用；预防|To take action before someone else in order to prevent something from happening; to take the place of something else; to prevent something from happening by acting first.|例句：The government pre-empted the crisis by raising interest rates. 政府通过提高利率来预防危机。|例句：The new regulations pre-empt the need for further legislation. 新法规取代了进一步立法的需要。|近义词：anticipate, forestall, supersede, supplant|反义词：postpone, delay


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company pre-empts potential problems by regular safety checks. = 公司通过定期安全检查来预防潜在的问题。
He pre-empts his opponent's arguments in the debate. = 他在辩论中抢先回应了对手的论点。

一般过去时:
The army pre-empted the enemy's attack. = 军队先发制人，打退了敌人的进攻。
She pre-empted the inheritance by selling the family property. = 她通过出售家族财产来抢先继承遗产。

现在完成时:
The government has pre-empted a possible strike by offering better wages. = 政府通过提供更高的工资来预防可能的罢工。
They have pre-empted the market with their innovative product. = 他们凭借其创新产品抢占了市场。

过去完成时:
The police had pre-empted a riot by arresting the key organizers. = 警方通过逮捕主要组织者来预防暴乱。
He had pre-empted any questions by explaining everything beforehand. = 他提前解释了一切，从而避免了任何疑问。

将来时:
The company will pre-empt any future legal challenges by securing all necessary permits. = 公司将通过获得所有必要的许可来预防任何未来的法律挑战。
We will pre-empt the problem by addressing it proactively. = 我们将通过积极主动地解决问题来预防它。

虚拟语气:
If they had pre-empted the situation, the disaster could have been avoided. = 如果他们能提前预知情况，灾难本来是可以避免的。
It would have been better if he had pre-empted the criticism by addressing the issues directly. = 如果他能直接解决问题，本来会更好，而不是事后才面临批评。

被动语态:
The proposed legislation was pre-empted by a court ruling. = 提议的立法被法院裁决所取代。
The risk of a power outage was pre-empted by routine maintenance. = 通过例行维护来预防了停电的风险。


疑问句:
Did the company pre-empt the competitor's move? = 公司是否抢先一步行动，制止了竞争对手的行动？
Can we pre-empt this issue before it escalates? = 我们能否在问题恶化之前先发制人？

否定句:
The company failed to pre-empt the market downturn. = 公司未能预见市场低迷。
He didn't pre-empt her question, and she asked it anyway. = 他没有抢先回答她的问题，她还是问了。
