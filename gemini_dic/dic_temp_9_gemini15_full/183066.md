repolishes:/ˌriːˈpɒlɪʃɪz/| v. |重新抛光；重新擦亮|To polish again; to restore the shine or luster to something by polishing.|例句：He repolished the antique table to restore its original gleam. 他重新抛光了古董桌子以恢复其原来的光泽。|例句：She repolished her shoes before the important meeting. 她在重要的会议前重新擦亮了她的鞋子。|近义词：rebuff, refinish|反义词：scratch, damage
