rowans:/ˈroʊənz/| n. | 欧洲山楂树；山梨树| A rowan is a small tree or shrub belonging to the rose family, known for its clusters of bright berries.  They are also called mountain-ash. |The rowans in the garden were laden with bright red berries. 花园里的山梨树上挂满了鲜红的浆果。
The autumn leaves of the rowan trees turned a fiery red. 山梨树的秋叶变成了火红的颜色。| 近义词：mountain ash, quickbeam | 反义词：无
