conspicuous:/kənˈspɪkjuəs/ | adj. | 明显的；显著的；惹眼的 | Easily seen or noticed; readily visible.  It implies something stands out and attracts attention, often because of its size, brightness, or unusualness. |例句：Her bright red dress made her conspicuous at the party.  她鲜红色的裙子使她在派对上非常显眼。

Her bright red dress made her conspicuous in the crowd. 她鲜红色的裙子让她在人群中很显眼。


近义词：noticeable, prominent, obvious, striking, outstanding | 反义词：inconspicuous, unobtrusive, hidden, concealed


词源:  From Latin *conspicuus, from conspicere "to look at attentively," from con- "together" + specere "to look."  The word's roots highlight the idea of something being looked at and easily seen.
