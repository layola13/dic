luge:/luːʒ/| n. |雪橇（运动）|A winter sport in which a person rides a small sled down a track of ice.  It is a popular Olympic sport.|例句：Luge is a thrilling winter sport requiring speed and precision.  雪橇是一项令人兴奋的冬季运动，需要速度和精确度。|近义词：sledding (less specific)|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He luges down the icy track every weekend. = 他每个周末都在冰冷的赛道上玩雪橇。
They luge with great skill and speed. = 他们以高超的技巧和速度玩雪橇。

一般过去时:
She luged down the mountain yesterday. = 她昨天从山上玩雪橇下来了。
We luged for hours and had great fun. = 我们玩了几个小时的雪橇，玩得很开心。

现在完成时:
He has luged competitively for five years. = 他参加雪橇比赛已经有五年了。
They have never luged before. = 他们以前从未玩过雪橇。

过去完成时:
She had luged many times before she won the competition. = 在赢得比赛之前，她已经玩过很多次雪橇了。
They had already luged before the snowstorm hit. = 在暴风雪来临之前，他们已经玩过雪橇了。

将来时:
I will luge next winter. = 我明年冬天会玩雪橇。
We will luge at the Olympic training center. = 我们将在奥运训练中心玩雪橇。

虚拟语气:
If I had a luge, I would try it. = 如果我有一辆雪橇，我会试试。
I wish I could luge like a professional. = 我希望我能像专业人士一样玩雪橇。

被动语态:
