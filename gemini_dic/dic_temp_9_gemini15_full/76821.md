ganaches:/ɡəˈnɑːʃ/| n. |甘纳许，巧克力酱 |A ganache is a rich, creamy mixture of chocolate and cream, often used as a filling or topping for desserts.  It's prized for its smooth texture and intense chocolate flavor.  The consistency can vary depending on the ratio of chocolate to cream. |Example: The cake was topped with a decadent chocolate ganache. 这块蛋糕上淋着美味的巧克力甘纳许。|近义词：chocolate frosting, chocolate cream, truffle filling |反义词：N/A
