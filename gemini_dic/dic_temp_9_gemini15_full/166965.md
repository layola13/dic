prestructure: /ˈpriːstrʌktʃər/ | n. |预先结构，预先构造|A structure or framework that is established or created in advance of something else;  a preliminary structure that serves as a foundation for further development or implementation.  It often refers to a plan, outline, or framework that guides a subsequent process.| The team developed a prestructure for the project, outlining key milestones and deliverables before commencing the detailed work.  团队在开始详细工作之前，为项目制定了一个预先结构，概述了关键里程碑和可交付成果。|近义词：framework, outline, blueprint, scaffold, foundation |反义词：ad hoc structure, improvisation, impromptu
