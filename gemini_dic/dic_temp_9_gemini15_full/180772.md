regulating:/ˈreɡjuleɪtɪŋ/| v.|调节；管理；控制 |The act of controlling or directing according to rule, principle, or law;  to adjust or govern by a rule, method, or other controlling device. |例句：The government is regulating the price of fuel. 政府正在控制燃油价格。

例句：The body regulates its own temperature.  身体调节自身的温度。


近义词：controlling, governing, adjusting, managing, overseeing

反义词：deregulating, neglecting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government regulates the banking industry. = 政府监管银行业。
He regulates his diet carefully. = 他仔细地控制饮食。


一般过去时:
The company regulated its production costs. = 公司控制了生产成本。
She regulated her breathing before the race. = 比赛前她调整了呼吸。


现在完成时:
They have regulated the flow of traffic effectively. = 他们有效地调节了交通流量。
The committee has regulated the use of pesticides. = 委员会已经规范了杀虫剂的使用。


过去完成时:
The authority had regulated the water supply before the drought. = 干旱前，当局已经规范了供水。
He had regulated his spending habits before the trip. = 旅行前他已经调整了消费习惯。


将来时:
The new law will regulate online advertising. = 新法将规范网络广告。
They will regulate the temperature in the greenhouse. = 他们将调节温室的温度。


虚拟语气:
If the government regulated the market more effectively, prices would be lower. = 如果政府更有效地规范市场，价格就会更低。
I wish they regulated the noise pollution more strictly. = 我希望他们更严格地控制噪音污染。


被动语态:
The temperature is regulated by a thermostat. = 温度由恒温器控制。
The industry is being regulated by new government policies. = 该行业正在受到新的政府政策的监管。


疑问句:
How is the water supply regulated? = 供水是如何调节的？
Is the speed of the machinery regulated automatically? = 机器速度是自动调节的吗？


否定句:
The company doesn't regulate its employees' working hours strictly. = 公司没有严格规定员工的工作时间。
They didn't regulate the amount of chemicals used in the process. = 他们没有控制该过程中使用的化学品数量。


PHRASE->
self-regulating: 自我调节的
carefully regulating: 仔细调节
strictly regulating: 严格控制
properly regulating: 正确调节
effectively regulating: 有效调节
