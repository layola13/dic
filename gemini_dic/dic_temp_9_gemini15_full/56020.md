do-time:/ˈduː taɪm/| v. |服刑，坐牢|To serve a prison sentence.  |例句：He's doing time for armed robbery. 他因持械抢劫而服刑。|近义词：serve a sentence, be incarcerated, be imprisoned |反义词：be free, be released


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is doing time for grand theft auto. = 他因盗窃汽车而服刑。
They are doing time for drug trafficking. = 他们因贩毒而服刑。

一般过去时:
He did time for assault. = 他因袭击罪服过刑。
She did time in a federal prison. = 她曾在联邦监狱服刑。

现在完成时:
He has done time before. = 他以前服过刑。
She has done time for multiple offenses. = 她因多项罪行服过刑。

过去完成时:
He had done time before his latest arrest. = 在他最近一次被捕之前，他已经服过刑。
She had done time for fraud. = 她曾因诈骗罪服过刑。

将来时:
He will do time if he's convicted. = 如果他被判有罪，他将服刑。
They will likely do time for their crimes. = 他们很可能会因为他们的罪行而服刑。

虚拟语气:
If he were caught, he would do time. = 如果他被抓住，他将会服刑。
I wish I hadn't done time. = 我真希望我没有服过刑。

被动语态:
Time was done by many people for minor offenses. = 许多人因轻微罪行而服刑。(This sentence is awkward, but grammatically correct, highlighting the passive voice.)
A long sentence was done by him for the crime. = 他因该罪行服了很长的刑期。

疑问句:
How long will he do time? = 他要服刑多久？
Has he ever done time before? = 他以前服过刑吗？

否定句:
He didn't do time for that crime. = 他没有因为那项罪行服刑。
She hasn't done time in her life. = 她一生中从未服过刑。


PHRASE->

to have done time = 服过刑
