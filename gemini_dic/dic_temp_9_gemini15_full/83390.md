ha:/hɑː/| int.|哈，啊(表示惊奇、嘲笑等)|An interjection expressing surprise, amusement, or a similar emotion.  It can also be used as a filler word.|例句：Ha! That's funny! 哈！真有趣！|例句：Ha, I knew you'd say that. 哈，我就知道你会这么说。|近义词：huh, ah|反义词: 无


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and does not follow typical verb conjugation patterns.  It does not have tenses or voice.  Its usage is purely contextual.

PHRASE->
Ha ha =  哈哈 (表示大笑)
Ha! Ha! Ha! =  哈哈哈哈哈 (表示更加夸张的大笑)
Break a leg, ha! = 加油，哈！(表示鼓励，带些戏谑)
Go on, ha! =  来啊，哈！(表示鼓励或挑衅)
Oh ha! = 哦，哈！（表示轻微的惊讶）
