privations:/praɪˈveɪʃənz/| n.| 匮乏；贫困；苦难 | The state of lacking or being without something necessary or desirable.  It often refers to a lack of essential resources or comforts, leading to hardship. | Example: The refugees endured terrible privations during their long journey.  难民在漫长的旅程中忍受了可怕的苦难。  The family suffered severe privations during the war.  战争期间，这个家庭遭受了严重的贫困。|近义词：hardships, deprivations, sufferings, want |反义词：abundance, affluence, plenty
