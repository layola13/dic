operation:/ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən/ /ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən/| n. vt.| 操作；手术；行动；运转 |The process of performing a task or function; a surgical procedure; a military or police action; the working of a machine or system. |例句：The operation of the machine is simple. 这台机器的操作很简单。
The doctor performed a successful operation. 医生进行了一次成功的手术。


例句：The military operation was a success.  军事行动取得了成功。

近义词：procedure, process, function, action, undertaking

反义词：inaction, idleness, standstill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine operates smoothly. = 机器运转平稳。
He operates the crane skillfully. = 他熟练地操作起重机。


一般过去时:
The surgeon operated on the patient. = 外科医生为病人做了手术。
They operated the factory efficiently. = 他们高效地运营着工厂。


现在完成时:
They have operated this business for ten years. = 他们经营这家企业十年了。
The company has successfully operated in the market. = 公司在市场上成功运营。


过去完成时:
They had operated the equipment before the accident. = 事故发生前他们已经操作过设备了。
The factory had operated at full capacity last year. = 去年工厂曾满负荷运转。


将来时:
We will operate the new system next week. = 我们下周将启用新系统。
The company will operate in a new market. = 公司将在新市场运营。


虚拟语气:
If I operated the machine correctly, it would work. = 如果我正确操作机器，它就会工作。
If he had operated more carefully, the accident might not have happened. = 如果他操作更仔细一些，事故可能就不会发生了。


被动语态:
The machine is operated remotely. = 机器是远程操作的。

疑问句:

否定句:
