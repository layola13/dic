non-reductive:/ˌnɒn rɪˈdʌktɪv/| adj. |非还原的，非简化的|Not reducing or capable of being reduced to something simpler or more fundamental.  It implies a holistic or complex view that resists simplification. |Example:  The philosopher presented a non-reductive account of consciousness, arguing that it couldn't be explained solely in terms of brain activity.  哲学家提出了关于意识的非还原性解释，认为它不能仅仅用大脑活动来解释。

|近义词: holistic, comprehensive, complex, multi-faceted|反义词: reductive, simplistic, minimal|词性:形容词
