rubigo:/ˈruːbɪɡoʊ/| n. |锈；锈病；小麦锈病 | Reddish-brown discoloration caused by rust or a plant disease.  It often refers to rust on iron or a fungal disease affecting cereals, particularly wheat. |The old tools were covered in rubigo.  那些旧工具上覆盖着一层锈。|The farmer was worried about the rubigo affecting his wheat crop.  这位农民担心锈病会影响他的小麦收成。|rust, blight |clean, polished |名词
