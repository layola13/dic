mansionry:/ˈmænʃənri/| n. |大厦建筑；豪宅建筑|The word "mansionry" refers to the construction or style of mansions, often implying grandeur, opulence, and skillful craftsmanship.  It's less about the individual building and more about the collective body of work representing this architectural style.  |例句：The mansionry of the Gilded Age is characterized by elaborate ornamentation and vast scale.  镀金时代的豪宅建筑以其精美的装饰和巨大的规模为特征。|近义词：manorial architecture, palatial architecture|反义词：slum dwelling, shack construction |名词
