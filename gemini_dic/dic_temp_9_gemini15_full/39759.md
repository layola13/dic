consolers:/kənˈsoʊlər/| n. |安慰者，慰藉者 |People who offer comfort and support during times of grief or distress.  They provide solace and help those in need cope with difficult emotions. |例句：The consolers offered words of comfort to the grieving family. 安慰者向悲伤的家庭提供了安慰的话语。|例句：After the accident, her friends acted as consolers, offering support and understanding. 事故发生后，她的朋友们充当安慰者，提供支持和理解。|近义词：comforters, sympathizers, supporters |反义词：tormentors, abusers
