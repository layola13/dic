humanists:/ˈhjuːmənɪsts/ | n. |人道主义者，人文主义者 |Supporters of humanism, a philosophical stance that emphasizes human reason, ethics, and justice, often rejecting religious dogma.| 例句：Many humanists advocate for social justice and human rights. 许多人道主义者倡导社会正义和人权。  |近义词：humanitarian, liberal |反义词：religious extremist, dogmatist |名词

一般现在时:
Humanists believe in the inherent goodness of humanity. = 人文主义者相信人性本善。
Humanists often promote secular ethics. = 人文主义者经常提倡世俗伦理。


一般过去时:
Renaissance humanists rediscovered classical texts. = 文艺复兴时期的人文主义者重新发现了古典文本。
Many humanists fought for religious tolerance. = 许多人文主义者为宗教宽容而斗争。


现在完成时:
Humanists have significantly influenced Western thought. = 人文主义者对西方思想产生了重大影响。
Humanists have championed individual liberty for centuries. = 人文主义者几个世纪以来一直捍卫个人自由。


过去完成时:

Humanists will advocate for a more just and equitable society. = 人文主义者将倡导一个更加公正和公平的社会。
