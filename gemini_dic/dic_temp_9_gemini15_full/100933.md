inulin:/ˈɪnjʊlɪn/| n. |菊粉，菊糖|A naturally occurring polysaccharide composed of fructose units, found in many plants, particularly in the roots and tubers of dahlias, chicory, and Jerusalem artichokes. It is used as a dietary fiber and a prebiotic.  |Example: Inulin is a type of dietary fiber that promotes gut health.  菊粉是一种促进肠道健康的膳食纤维。|近义词：fructan (a broader term)|反义词：无|名词
