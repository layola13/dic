nonpredictable: /ˌnɒnprɪˈdɪktəbl/ | adj. | 不可预测的，难以预料的 |  Not able to be predicted; unpredictable.  The word combines "non-" (meaning not) and "predictable" (capable of being predicted). |


例句：

The weather in this region is notoriously nonpredictable.  这个地区的的天气出了名的不可预测。

The stock market's behavior is often nonpredictable, making investment risky. 股市的波动常常难以预测，使得投资存在风险。


近义词：unpredictable, erratic, capricious, volatile, uncertain


反义词：predictable, foreseeable, certain, reliable


词性：形容词
