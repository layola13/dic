radio-telemetry:/ˈreɪdioʊˈtɛləmɪtri/| n. |无线遥测|The science and technology of remotely measuring and transmitting data using radio waves.|例句：Radio-telemetry is used to monitor the heart rate of patients remotely. 无线遥测用于远程监测患者的心率。|近义词：remote sensing, wireless monitoring |反义词：direct measurement
