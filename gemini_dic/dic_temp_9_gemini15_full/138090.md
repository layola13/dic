/ˈnɔːrmənɪst/| n. |诺曼研究者；诺曼文学专家|A Normanist is a scholar who studies the Norman language, culture, history, or literature, particularly those related to the Norman conquest of England.  They might focus on aspects like the Norman-French language, the impact of Norman rule on England, or the literary output of Norman authors.|Example:  Professor Davies is a renowned Normanist, specializing in 11th-century Norman chronicles.|近义词: Medievalist (if focusing on the broader medieval period), Anglo-Norman scholar|反义词:  None directly applicable.
