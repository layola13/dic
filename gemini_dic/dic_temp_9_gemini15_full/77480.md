gazebo:/ɡəˈzeɪboʊ/ | n. |凉亭；六角亭 | A gazebo is an open-sided structure, usually with a roof, used as a shelter in a garden or park.  It often has a decorative design. |例句：We sat in the gazebo and enjoyed the beautiful sunset. 我们坐在凉亭里，欣赏美丽的日落。

例句：The gazebo in the park was a popular spot for wedding photos. 公园里的凉亭是拍婚纱照的热门地点。


近义词：pavilion, kiosk, rotunda | 反义词：None
