hexose:/ˈhɛksoʊs/| n. |己糖|A hexose is a monosaccharide (simple sugar) with six carbon atoms.  It has the general formula C6H12O6.  Hexoses are important sources of energy for living organisms and are involved in numerous metabolic processes. Examples include glucose, fructose, galactose, and mannose.|例句：Glucose is a common hexose found in many fruits and vegetables.  葡萄糖是许多水果和蔬菜中常见的一种己糖。|近义词：monosaccharide, simple sugar|反义词：N/A
