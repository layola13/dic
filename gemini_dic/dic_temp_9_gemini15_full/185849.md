revives:/rɪˈvaɪvz/ | vt. |使复苏；使恢复；使重新流行|To restore to life or consciousness; to renew or refresh; to bring back into use or fashion.  |例句：The doctor revived the patient with an injection. 医生用注射器使病人苏醒过来。

The old tradition has been revived recently.  这个古老的传统最近又被恢复了。

近义词：reanimate, revitalize, resuscitate, restore, resurrect |反义词：kill, extinguish, destroy, end


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revives the old car every weekend. 他每个周末都修理那辆旧车。
The company revives its marketing strategy annually. 这家公司每年都会更新其营销策略。


一般过去时:
The rain revived the thirsty plants.  雨水使干渴的植物恢复了生机。
She revived the flagging spirits of the team with a pep talk. 她用一次激励性讲话振奋了士气低落的团队。


现在完成时:
The economy has revived after a period of recession.  经济在经历了一段时间的衰退后复苏了。
They have revived their interest in learning a new language. 他们重新燃起了学习一门外语的兴趣。


过去完成时:
The old song had revived memories of his childhood. 那首老歌唤起了他对童年的回忆。
She had revived the business by introducing new products. 她通过推出新产品使企业恢复了生机。


将来时:
The government plans to revive the local economy with new investments. 政府计划通过新的投资来振兴地方经济。
They will revive the play next season.  他们将在下个季度重新上演这出戏。


虚拟语气:
If they revived the old factory, many people would have jobs. 如果他们恢复了旧工厂的运作，许多人就会有工作。
I wish they would revive the old traditions. 我希望他们能恢复那些古老的传统。


被动语态:
The old building was revived by a group of architects. 这座旧建筑物被一群建筑师修复了。
The company's image was revived by a successful marketing campaign. 这家公司的形象通过一次成功的营销活动得到了提升。


疑问句:
Will the government revive the project? 政府会恢复这个项目吗？
Can we revive the old furniture? 我们能修复这些旧家具吗？


否定句:
They couldn't revive the old machine. 他们无法修复那台旧机器。
The doctor failed to revive the patient. 医生没能使病人苏醒过来。
