chuted:/tʃuːtɪd/| v. (过去式)|用降落伞下降；（使）滑下|The parachutist chuted safely to the ground.  跳伞员安全地降落到地面。|近义词：descended, dropped, slid |反义词：ascended, climbed


USAGE->

He chutes out of the airplane. 他从飞机上跳伞。
The water chutes down the mountain. 水从山上滑下。

一般过去时:
She chuted into the valley. 她滑入山谷。
The pilot chuted the supplies to the disaster area. 飞行员用降落伞空投物资到灾区。

The team has successfully chuted all the equipment.  团队已经成功地空投了所有设备。

He had chuted many times before this mission. 在这次任务之前他已经跳伞很多次了。
