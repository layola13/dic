peppiness:/ˈpepɪnəs/| n. |活力，精力充沛；活泼 |Liveliness and energy;  a feeling of being full of energy and enthusiasm.  It suggests a cheerful and energetic disposition.|

例句：

Her peppiness was infectious, brightening the whole room.  她的活力感染了所有人，照亮了整个房间。

The children's peppiness was exhausting but delightful. 孩子们的活力令人筋疲力尽，但也令人高兴。


近义词：energy, vitality, vivacity, spirit, zest, enthusiasm

反义词：lethargy, sluggishness, apathy, lassitude, debility


词源:  Derived from "pep," meaning vigor or energy.  The suffix "-iness" adds the quality or state of being.  The word "pep" itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to a dialectal variant of "pip," meaning a seed or kernel, suggesting the idea of something small but potent.
