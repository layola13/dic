gratulate:/ˈɡrætʃuleɪt/| vt.|祝贺，庆贺|To express pleasure to (someone) on a happy occasion, typically a success or achievement.|例句：I congratulate you on your success. 我祝贺你成功。|例句：We congratulated her on her wedding. 我们祝贺她的婚礼。|近义词：congratulate, felicitate|反义词：condemn, censure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I congratulate you on your graduation. = 我祝贺你毕业。
They congratulate the winners every year. = 他们每年都祝贺获胜者。

一般过去时:
He congratulated me on my promotion. = 他祝贺我升职。
She congratulated them on their engagement. = 她祝贺他们的订婚。

现在完成时:
I have congratulated him on his new job. = 我已经祝贺他找到新工作了。
They have congratulated the team on their victory. = 他们已经祝贺球队取得胜利了。

过去完成时:
I had already congratulated her before she received the award. = 在她获奖之前，我已经祝贺她了。
They had congratulated the couple before the reception began. = 在招待会开始之前，他们已经祝贺这对夫妇了。

将来时:
I will congratulate him when he wins the race. = 当他赢得比赛时，我会祝贺他。
We will congratulate them on their anniversary. = 我们将在他们的周年纪念日祝贺他们。

虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would congratulate him. = 如果我看到他，我会祝贺他。
I wish I could congratulate her in person. = 我希望我能亲自祝贺她。

被动语态:
He was congratulated by his colleagues on his achievement. = 他因其成就而受到同事们的祝贺。
The team was congratulated for their hard work. = 球队因他们的辛勤工作而受到祝贺。

疑问句:
Did you congratulate her on her birthday? = 你祝贺她生日快乐了吗？
Will you congratulate them on their success? = 你会祝贺他们的成功吗？

否定句:
I didn't congratulate him on his win. = 我没有祝贺他获胜。
She didn't congratulate them on their new house. = 她没有祝贺他们拥有新房子。


PHRASE->
congratulate someone on something = 祝贺某人某事
offer congratulations = 表示祝贺
give someone congratulations = 向某人表示祝贺
extend congratulations = 致以祝贺
warmest congratulations = 最热烈的祝贺
