penitentiary:/ˌpɛnɪˈtɛnʃəri/| n. |监狱；感化院 |A penitentiary is a prison, especially one for long-term or serious offenders.  It is often used to emphasize the seriousness of the institution and the crimes committed by its inmates. |He was sentenced to ten years in a penitentiary. 他被判处十年监禁。|prison, jail, correctional facility |freedom, liberty |名词
