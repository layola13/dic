retouch: /ˈriːtʌtʃ/ | vt. | 修饰；润色；重新着色 | To improve a photograph, piece of writing, etc. by making small changes.  | 例句：I need to retouch these photos before I send them to the client. 我需要在把这些照片发给客户之前润色一下。


例句：The artist retouched the painting to enhance its colors.  艺术家润色了这幅画以增强其色彩。


近义词：refine, improve, enhance, amend | 反义词：ruin, spoil, damage


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retouches the images every day. 他每天都修饰图片。
She retouches her photos before posting them online. 她在网上发布照片之前会修饰照片。


一般过去时:
I retouched the photograph to remove the blemishes. 我修饰了照片以去除瑕疵。
The editor retouched the article before publication. 编辑在发表前润色了文章。


现在完成时:
She has retouched all the photos for the wedding album. 她已经为婚礼相册修饰了所有照片。
They have retouched the painting several times. 他们已经润色过这幅画好几次了。


过去完成时:
He had retouched the image before he showed it to the professor. 在他向教授展示图片之前，他已经修饰过它了。
The artist had retouched the portrait before the exhibition.  艺术家在展览前已经润色过肖像画了。


将来时:
I will retouch the photos tomorrow. 我明天将修饰照片。
She will retouch the document before submitting it. 她将在提交文件之前润色它。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would retouch the pictures. 如果我有时间，我会修饰这些照片。
I wish I had retouched the image before printing it. 我真希望在打印之前已经修饰过那张图片。


被动语态:

否定句:
