griskin:/ˈɡrɪskɪn/| n. |猪腰子，猪肾；(尤指)腌制猪肾|A griskin is a pig's kidney, especially one that has been cured or pickled.  The term is primarily used in British English.|例句：He ordered a plate of grilled griskins. 他点了一盘烤猪腰子。

例句：The butcher had some delicious looking griskins on display.  屠夫展示了一些看起来很美味的腌猪腰子。


近义词：pig's kidney, kidney


反义词：无


词性：名词
