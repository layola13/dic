firelighter:/ˈfaɪərˌlaɪtər/ | n. |打火机，火柴；助燃物 | A firelighter is a tool or substance used to start a fire, often made of compressed wood shavings, wax, or other easily combustible materials.  |例句：He used a firelighter to get the bonfire going. 他用打火机点燃了篝火。

例句：The firelighter was soaked and wouldn't light. 打火机湿透了，点不着。


近义词：tinder, kindling, fire starter | 反义词：fire extinguisher


词性:名词
