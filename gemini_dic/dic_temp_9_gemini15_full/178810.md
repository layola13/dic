recursion:/rɪˈkɜːʒən/| n. |递归；循环；回溯|The process of a function calling itself directly or indirectly.  This technique is commonly used in computer science to solve problems that can be broken down into smaller, self-similar subproblems.  It involves a base case that stops the recursion, preventing infinite loops. |Example:  The factorial function is often implemented recursively.  The calculation of 5! (5 factorial) can be expressed as 5 * 4 * 3 * 2 * 1, or recursively as 5 * (4!).|近义词：iteration, repetition|反义词：iteration (in some contexts, as iteration can be an alternative to recursion)|
