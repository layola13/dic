degenerated:/dɪˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪd/| adj.| 退化的；堕落的；衰退的 | Having declined or become worse in quality, condition, or character.  It suggests a process of deterioration from a previous, better state. |例句：The quality of life in the town has degenerated over the years.  多年来，这个城镇的生活质量一直在下降。

The once-thriving industry has degenerated into a shadow of its former self.  曾经繁荣的产业已经衰败成其昔日辉煌的影子。

近义词： deteriorated, worsened, declined, decayed, corrupted | 反义词：improved, enhanced, advanced, progressed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building is degenerating rapidly. = 这栋建筑正在迅速恶化。
His health is degenerating. = 他的健康状况正在恶化。

一般过去时:
The Roman Empire degenerated after the death of Marcus Aurelius. = 马可·奥勒留死后，罗马帝国开始衰落。
The neighborhood degenerated into a slum. = 这个街区沦为了贫民窟。

现在完成时:
His moral character has degenerated over time. = 他的道德品质随着时间的推移而堕落了。
The environment has degenerated significantly due to pollution. = 由于污染，环境已经严重恶化。

过去完成时:
The social fabric had degenerated before the revolution. = 在革命爆发之前，社会结构就已经衰败了。
By the time he retired, his eyesight had degenerated significantly. = 到他退休的时候，他的视力已经严重下降了。

将来时:
If we don't act now, the situation will degenerate further. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况将会进一步恶化。
Without proper care, his condition will degenerate quickly. = 如果不加护理，他的病情会迅速恶化。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been so lazy, his skills wouldn't have degenerated. = 如果他不那么懒惰，他的技能就不会退化了。
I wish the political situation hadn't degenerated so quickly. = 我希望政治局势没有恶化得这么快。

被动语态:
The area was allowed to degenerate into a wasteland. = 该地区被允许荒废成一片荒地。
The negotiations were allowed to degenerate into a shouting match. = 谈判演变成一场喊叫比赛。

疑问句:
Has the situation degenerated beyond repair? = 局势是否已经恶化到无法挽回的地步？
Is his mental state degenerating? = 他的精神状态是否正在恶化？

否定句:
The quality of the product hasn't degenerated. = 产品质量没有下降。
The relationship didn't degenerate into a fight. = 这段关系没有恶化成一场争吵。
