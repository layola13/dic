conine:/ˈkoʊnaɪn/| n. |毒芹碱，锥子碱|A highly toxic alkaloid found in hemlock (Conium maculatum), a poisonous plant. It is a neurotoxin that can cause paralysis and death.  Conine is historically significant due to its association with the death of Socrates.|Example: The ancient Greeks used conine, extracted from hemlock, as a poison.|近义词：hemlock poison (referring to the source)|反义词：antidote (to its effects), safe substance|名词
