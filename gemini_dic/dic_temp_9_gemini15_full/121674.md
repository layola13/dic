misplanted:/ˌmɪsˈplæntɪd/| adj.| 种植错误的；错位的 |  Describes something that has been planted in the wrong place or incorrectly. It implies an error in the planting process. |例句：The gardener realized he had misplanted the tomatoes, putting them in the shade instead of the sun. 园丁意识到他把西红柿种错了地方，把它们种在了阴凉处而不是阳光下。

例句：The seeds were misplanted and failed to germinate. 种子种错了地方，没有发芽。


近义词：improperly planted, wrongly placed, misplaced


反义词：correctly planted, properly placed


词性：形容词
