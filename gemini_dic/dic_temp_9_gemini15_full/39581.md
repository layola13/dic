conscriptions:/kənˈskrɪpʃənz/ | n. |征兵；征募；征用 |The compulsory enlistment of people for service in the armed forces.  The act of conscripting; drafting. |例句：The conscriptions were met with widespread protests. 征兵受到了广泛的抗议。

例句：The government announced new conscriptions to bolster the army. 政府宣布新的征兵以增强军队实力。


近义词：enlistment, drafting, recruitment | 反义词：discharge, release


词源：

From Middle French *conscription, from Latin conscriptio, from conscribere "to enroll, levy," from com- "together" + scribere "to write."  The original sense was "enrollment," which evolved to include the military context.


用法说明:

conscriptions 主要用作名词，通常指强制征兵或征募。它可以指单次征兵，也可以指一个时期内的多次征兵。  它也偶尔用于非军事领域，指强制征用资源或人员。
