rattleweed:/ˈrætlˌwiːd/| n. |响铃草，响板草|A plant of the genus *Baptisia*, having pea-like flowers and rattling seed pods.  The name refers to the sound made by the dried seed pods when shaken.|The rattleweed grew abundantly along the roadside.|近义词：Indian paintbrush (depending on the specific species)|反义词：无
