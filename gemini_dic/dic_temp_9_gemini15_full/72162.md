floorcloths:/ˈflɔːrklɔːθs/ | n. |擦地板的布，拖把布 |Floorcloths are pieces of absorbent material used to clean floors. They are typically made of cotton or other absorbent fabrics and are often used with a cleaning solution. |例句：She cleaned the kitchen floor with a damp floorcloth. 她用湿拖把擦厨房地板。


例句：The floorcloths were wrung out and hung to dry after cleaning the entire house. 清洁完整个房子后，拖把拧干并挂起来晾干。


近义词：cleaning cloths, mops, dusters |反义词：None
