nonactive:/ˈnɒnˈæktɪv/ | adj. |非活跃的，不活动的，无效的 | Not active; not currently operating or functioning.  It describes something that is not currently in use or operation, or something that lacks energy or vitality. |例句：The volcano is currently nonactive, but geologists believe it could become active again. 这座火山目前处于非活跃状态，但地质学家认为它可能会再次活跃起来。

The account has been marked as nonactive due to inactivity. 由于不活跃，该账户已被标记为非活动状态。

近义词：inactive, dormant, quiescent, latent |反义词：active, functioning, operational
