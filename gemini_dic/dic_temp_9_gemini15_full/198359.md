showed: /ʃoʊd/ | v. | 显示；展示；表明 | To reveal, display, or demonstrate something.  |例句：He showed me his new car. 他向我展示了他的新车。
例句：Her actions showed her true feelings. 她的行为表明了她真实的想法。 |近义词：displayed, demonstrated, revealed, exhibited | 反义词：concealed, hid, obscured | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shows his work to his friends. = 他向朋友们展示他的作品。
They show great enthusiasm for the project. = 他们对这个项目表现出极大的热情。


一般过去时:
She showed me the way to the station. = 她指给我去车站的路。
They showed their support by attending the rally. = 他们通过参加集会来表达他们的支持。


现在完成时:
He has showed remarkable improvement. = 他已经展现出显著的进步。
The evidence has showed that he is guilty. = 证据表明他是有罪的。


过去完成时:
She had showed signs of illness before the trip. = 旅行前她就表现出生病的迹象。
They had showed little interest in the topic before the discussion. = 讨论之前他们对这个话题几乎没有兴趣。


将来时:
He will show us his paintings tomorrow. = 他明天将向我们展示他的绘画作品。
They will show their new product at the trade show. = 他们将在贸易展会上展示他们的新产品。


虚拟语气:
If he showed more effort, he would succeed. = 如果他付出更多努力，他就会成功。
I wish he showed more understanding. = 我希望他能表现出更多的理解。


被动语态:
The results were showed to the public. = 结果公之于众。
The painting was showed in a prestigious gallery. = 这幅画在一间著名的画廊展出。


疑问句:
Did he show you the photos? = 他给你看照片了吗？
Will they show the film tonight? = 他们今晚会放映这部电影吗？


否定句:
He didn't show any signs of fear. = 他没有表现出任何恐惧的迹象。
They didn't show up for the meeting. = 他们没有参加会议。


PHRASE->
show off = 炫耀
show up = 出席；露面
show around = 带领参观
show one's hand = 摊牌；亮出底牌
show mercy = 怜悯；仁慈

词源:
源自古英语 *sceawian，意为“看，观察”。  它经历了漫长的演变，最终发展成现代英语的 "show"。
