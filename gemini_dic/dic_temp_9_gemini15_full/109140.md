legislation:/ˌlɛdʒɪsˈleɪʃən/| n. |立法；法律；法规|The act of making or enacting laws; the laws enacted.  Legislation is the process of creating laws, and it also refers to the laws themselves.  It can cover a wide range of topics, from criminal justice to environmental protection. |例句：The new legislation aims to reduce pollution.  新的立法旨在减少污染。

例句：Congress passed important legislation this year. 国会今年通过了重要的立法。


近义词：law, statute, enactment, ordinance, act

反义词：deregulation


词性：名词
