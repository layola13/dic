footstep:/ˈfʊtˌstɛp/| n.|脚步声；足迹；脚步；步伐|The sound made by a foot or feet when walking; a mark left by a foot; a step; pace. |例句：I heard footsteps approaching. 我听到脚步声靠近了。

例句：He followed in his father's footsteps and became a doctor. 他追随父亲的脚步成为了一名医生。


近义词：track, footprint, stride, pace|反义词：silence (lack of sound)|词性：名词


词源：源自古英语 *fot-step,  由fot (foot)  和 step 组成。
