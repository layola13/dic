comparative:/kəmˈpærətɪv/| adj. |比较的；相对的；比较级的|  Relating to or involving the comparison of two or more things or people;  of, relating to, or expressing comparison.  It can also refer to the comparative degree in grammar (e.g., "bigger," "better").|例句：He made a comparative study of the two languages. 他对这两种语言进行了比较研究。

例句：The comparative form of "good" is "better". "good"的比较级是"better".


近义词：relative, comparative analysis, contrastive


反义词：absolute, unqualified


词性：形容词
