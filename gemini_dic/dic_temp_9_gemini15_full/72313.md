flourishingly:/ˈflʌrɪʃɪŋli/| adv.|繁荣地；兴旺地；茂盛地|In a way that shows great success or growth.  It describes an action or state characterized by vigorous growth, success, or prosperity.|例句：The business flourished incredibly; it expanded flourishingly.  这家企业发展得非常好；它蓬勃发展。
The plants were flourishingly growing in the fertile soil. 植物在肥沃的土壤中茂盛地生长。|近义词：thrivingly, prosperously, exuberantly|反义词：poorly, weakly, miserably
