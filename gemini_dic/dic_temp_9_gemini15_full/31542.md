charting:/ˈtʃɑːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 绘制图表；规划；记录；标绘航线 | The act of making a chart or graph;  plotting a course; recording progress.  | 例句：They are charting the progress of the disease. 他们正在绘制该疾病的进展图表。

They are charting a new course for the company. 他们正在为公司规划一条新航线。| 近义词：plotting, mapping, graphing, plotting a course | 反义词：erasing, removing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are charting the stock market's performance. = 我们正在绘制股市的表现图表。
She charts her daily calorie intake. = 她记录她每天的卡路里摄入量。

一般过去时:
The sailors charted their course across the ocean. = 水手们绘制了他们横渡大洋的航线。
The doctor charted the patient's vital signs. = 医生记录了病人的生命体征。

现在完成时:
They have charted the growth of the company over the past decade. = 他们在过去十年中绘制了公司的增长图表。
We have charted a new strategy for success. = 我们已经规划了一个新的成功策略。


过去完成时:
The captain had charted a safe course before the storm hit. = 船长在暴风雨来临之前已经规划了一条安全的航线。
They had charted the area's geological features before starting the construction. = 他们在开始建设之前已经绘制了该地区的地质特征图。

将来时:
We will chart the progress of the project regularly. = 我们将定期绘制项目的进度图表。
The explorers will chart the unmapped territory. = 探险家们将绘制未绘制地图的区域。


虚拟语气:
If we had charted a different course, we might have succeeded. = 如果我们规划了不同的路线，我们也许会成功。
I wish I had charted my investments more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地规划我的投资。


被动语态:
The patient's progress is being charted daily. = 病人的进展每天都在被记录。
The new territory was charted by the expedition team. = 新领土被探险队绘制了地图。


疑问句:
Are you charting the data correctly? = 你正在正确地绘制数据吗？
How will we chart the unknown waters? = 我们将如何绘制未知水域的地图？


否定句:
They did not chart the course accurately. = 他们没有准确地绘制航线。
We haven't charted a course yet for our next trip. = 我们还没有为我们的下一次旅行规划路线。
