consultative:/kənˈsʌltətɪv/| adj.|咨询的；协商的；顾问的| Relating to or involving consultation; characterized by consultation.  It suggests a process of seeking advice or opinions before making a decision. |例句：The company adopted a consultative approach to management.  这家公司采用了协商管理的方式。

例句：The government held consultative meetings with various stakeholders before implementing the new policy. 政府在实施新政策之前与各利益相关者举行了协商会议。


近义词：advisory, deliberative, participative

反义词：dictatorial, autocratic, unilateral

词性:形容词
