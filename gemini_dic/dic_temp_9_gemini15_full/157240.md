pinnate:/ˈpɪneɪt/| adj.|羽状的，羽裂的|Having parts arranged on either side of a common axis, resembling a feather.  The term is commonly used in botany to describe leaves and veins, and in zoology to describe certain structures. |例句：The fern has pinnate leaves.  这蕨类植物有羽状叶。|例句：The bird's feathers are beautifully pinnate. 这只鸟的羽毛是美丽的羽状的。|近义词：feathery, plume-like|反义词：palmate, simple
