inferno:/ɪnˈfɜːrnoʊ/ | n. |地狱，炼狱；人间地狱；大火 | A violent and uncontrolled fire.  It can also refer to a chaotic and destructive situation. |例句：The city was an inferno after the bombing.  爆炸后，城市变成了一片火海。

例句：The political situation descended into an inferno of accusations and counter-accusations. 政治局势演变成一场相互指责的人间地狱。

近义词：hell, pandemonium, chaos, conflagration

反义词：paradise, heaven, peace, tranquility

词性:名词
