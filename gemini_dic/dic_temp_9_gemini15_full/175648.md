ransom:/ˈrænsəm/| n. |赎金；赎价| Money or other valuable things demanded for the release of a person or property.  |The kidnappers demanded a large ransom for the safe return of the child. 绑匪要求支付一大笔赎金才能安全地归还孩子。|近义词：redemption, price,  |反义词：free gift


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The family pays a ransom to the kidnappers. = 这个家庭付给绑匪赎金。
He demands a ransom for the stolen artwork. = 他要求支付赎金来赎回被盗的艺术品。


一般过去时:
They paid a huge ransom to free their son. = 他们付了一大笔赎金来解救他们的儿子。
The company refused to pay the ransom. = 公司拒绝支付赎金。


现在完成时:
The police have recovered the stolen car, but no ransom has been paid. = 警方已经找回了被盗的汽车，但没有支付赎金。
They have already paid the ransom. = 他们已经支付了赎金。


过去完成时:
The kidnappers had already received the ransom before the police arrived. = 在警方到达之前，绑匪已经收到了赎金。
The family had prepared the ransom for days. = 这家人已经准备了几天赎金。


将来时:
They will pay the ransom tomorrow. = 他们明天将支付赎金。
The criminals will demand a higher ransom. = 罪犯将会要求更高的赎金。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would pay the ransom. = 如果我有钱，我会支付赎金。
I wish I hadn't had to pay such a large ransom. = 我真希望不必支付这么高的赎金。


被动语态:
The ransom was paid anonymously. = 赎金是匿名支付的。
A ransom was demanded for the release of the hostages. = 要求支付赎金才能释放人质。


疑问句:
What was the amount of the ransom? = 赎金数额是多少？
How much ransom did they demand? = 他们要求多少赎金？


否定句:
The family refused to pay the ransom. = 这家人拒绝支付赎金。
The company did not pay the ransom. = 公司没有支付赎金。


PHRASE->
demand a ransom 要求赎金
pay a ransom 支付赎金
negotiate a ransom 协商赎金
collect a ransom 收取赎金
a ransom note 赎金信
