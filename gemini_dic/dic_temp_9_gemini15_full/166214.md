prequalification:/ˌpriːˌkwɑːlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |预选资格，预先资格审查|The process of determining if a prospective bidder or contractor meets basic requirements before being allowed to bid on a project or contract.  It involves verifying qualifications such as financial stability, experience, and technical capabilities.|例句：The company underwent prequalification before bidding on the government contract.  这家公司在竞标政府合同之前进行了预选资格审查。|例句：Many companies were disqualified during prequalification. 许多公司在预选资格审查中被取消资格。|近义词：preliminary screening, initial assessment |反义词：final qualification, full assessment |名词
