emeraud:/ˈɛmərɔːld/ | n. |祖母绿；翠绿色 |A precious stone of a vivid green color, a variety of beryl.  Emerald is highly valued for its intense green color and clarity.  |例句：The ring featured a stunning emeraud. 这枚戒指上镶嵌着一颗令人惊叹的祖母绿。

例句：The artist used a vibrant emeraud green in his painting.  这位艺术家在他的画作中使用了鲜艳的祖母绿绿。


近义词：jade, green gemstone | 反义词：无


词源:The word "emerald" comes from the Old French "esmeraude," which ultimately derives from the Latin "smaragdus," and  from the Greek "smaragdos" (σμάραγδος).  All these words refer to the green gemstone.
