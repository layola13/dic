immodestly:/ɪˈmɒdəstli/| adv.|不谦虚地；放肆地；无耻地|In a way that shows a lack of modesty or humility; shamelessly or brazenly.|例句：She immodestly boasted about her achievements. 她不谦虚地吹嘘自己的成就。|例句：He immodestly displayed his wealth. 他无耻地炫耀他的财富。|近义词：shamelessly, brazenly, audaciously|反义词：modestly, humbly, shyly|副词
