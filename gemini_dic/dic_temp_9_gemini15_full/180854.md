reheat:/ˌriːˈhiːt/| vt.| 再加热，重新加热 | To heat (food) again after it has been cooked.  |例句：I reheated the leftovers for lunch. 我把剩菜热了当午餐吃。

例句：Let's reheat the pizza.  让我们把披萨再热一下。


近义词：heat up, warm up | 反义词：cool down, chill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reheats his coffee every morning. 他每天早上都把咖啡再热一下。
She reheats the soup before serving it.  她在上菜前把汤热一下。


一般过去时:
I reheated the pasta last night. 我昨晚把意面热了一下。
They reheated the casserole for dinner. 他们把砂锅菜热了当晚餐。


现在完成时:
We have reheated the leftovers twice today. 我们今天已经把剩菜热了两次了。
She has reheated the tea several times. 她已经把茶续了好几次了。


过去完成时:
I had reheated the soup before the guests arrived. 客人来之前我已经把汤热好了。
They had reheated the pizza before watching the movie. 他们在看电影之前已经把披萨热好了。


将来时:
I will reheat the dinner before we eat. 我们吃饭前我会把晚饭热一下。
She will reheat the coffee in the microwave. 她会用微波炉把咖啡热一下。


虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would reheat the rice. 如果我饿了，我会把米饭热一下。
I wish I had reheated the vegetables. 我真希望我把蔬菜热一下。


被动语态:
The food was reheated in the oven. 食物是在烤箱里加热的。
The leftovers are being reheated in the microwave. 剩菜正在微波炉里加热。


疑问句:
Do you usually reheat your lunch? 你通常会把午餐热一下吗？
Should I reheat the stew before serving? 我应该在盛之前把炖菜热一下吗？


否定句:
I didn't reheat the potatoes because they were still warm. 我没有把土豆热一下，因为它们还很暖和。
She doesn't usually reheat her coffee. 她通常不会把咖啡再热。


PHRASE->
reheat leftovers  重新加热剩菜
reheat in the microwave 在微波炉中重新加热
reheat until hot 加热至热
reheat gently  轻轻地加热
reheat thoroughly  彻底加热
