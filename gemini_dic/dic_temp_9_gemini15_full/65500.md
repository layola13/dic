europhobic:/ˌjuːrəˈfoʊbɪk/ | adj. | 恐欧的，害怕欧洲的 |  Fearful or prejudiced against Europe or Europeans.  This term is relatively recent and less common than other forms of xenophobia. It describes a specific phobia or prejudice centered on Europe and its people.  | Example:  His europhobic views were evident in his refusal to travel to Europe.  他的恐欧观点在他拒绝前往欧洲的举动中显而易见。 | 近义词: xenophobic, anti-European | 反义词: philhellenic (specifically pro-Greek, but can be used more broadly as a contrast)
