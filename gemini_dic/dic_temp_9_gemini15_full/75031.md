frieze:/friːz/| n.|  饰带；带状装饰；壁饰；(建筑物外墙上的)雕饰带|A frieze is a broad horizontal band of sculpted or painted decoration, especially on a wall or building.  It often depicts scenes or patterns.|The museum's frieze depicted scenes from ancient mythology. 这个博物馆的壁饰描绘了古代神话的场景。|ornament, molding, decoration|plain, undecorated|名词

词源:
From Old French *frese, from Vulgar Latin *frisia, from Latin frisium, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old High German friso "curled hair",  ultimately of uncertain origin.  The meaning developed from the idea of a curled or wavy band.
