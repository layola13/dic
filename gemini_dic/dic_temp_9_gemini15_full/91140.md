hybridized:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzd/ | adj. | 杂交的；混合的 | Combined or mixed with something else, especially different species or varieties;  having the characteristics of two or more different things.  | 例句：The new rose is a hybridized variety, combining the best traits of two different species.  这朵新的玫瑰花是杂交品种，结合了两个不同物种的最佳特性。


The hybridized engine offers both power and fuel efficiency.  这台混合动力发动机兼具动力和燃油效率。|近义词：crossbred, mixed, blended, composite | 反义词：purebred, unmixed, homogeneous |

词源：
