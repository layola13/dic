inherit:/ɪnˈhɛrɪt/ | vt. | 继承，遗传 | To receive (property, a title, etc.) from a predecessor, especially by legal right.  To possess a characteristic that is passed down genetically. | 例句：She inherited a fortune from her grandmother. = 她继承了祖母的一笔巨款。

She inherited her mother's beautiful eyes. = 她遗传了她母亲漂亮的眼睛。|近义词：succeed to, receive, acquire | 反义词： bequeath, leave (to), disinherit | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inherits his father's business. = 他继承了他父亲的事业。
They inherit a strong work ethic from their parents. = 他们从父母那里继承了强烈的职业道德。


一般过去时:
She inherited a large house. = 她继承了一栋大房子。
The child inherited his father's red hair. = 这个孩子遗传了他父亲的红头发。


现在完成时:
I have inherited my grandfather's watch. = 我继承了我祖父的手表。
They have inherited a valuable collection of paintings. = 他们继承了一批珍贵的绘画收藏。


过去完成时:
Before his father's death, he had inherited several properties. = 在他父亲去世之前，他已经继承了几处房产。
She had inherited the family business before turning 30. = 她30岁之前就继承了家族企业。


将来时:
He will inherit his uncle's estate. = 他将继承他叔叔的遗产。
She will inherit a significant amount of money. = 她将继承一大笔钱。


虚拟语气:
If I inherited a million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我继承了一百万美元，我会环游世界。
I wish I had inherited my grandmother's artistic talent. = 我真希望我继承了我祖母的艺术天赋。


被动语态:
The property was inherited by his nephew. = 这处房产被他侄子继承了。
The family fortune was inherited by the eldest son. = 家族财产由长子继承。


疑问句:
What will you inherit from your parents? = 你将从父母那里继承什么？
Did she inherit anything from her grandfather? = 她从她祖父那里继承了什么吗？


否定句:
He did not inherit any money from his parents. = 他没有从父母那里继承任何钱。
She didn't inherit her father's musical abilities. = 她没有遗传她父亲的音乐才能。


PHRASE->
inherit a fortune = 继承一大笔财产
inherit a trait = 遗传特征
inherit the earth = 继承地球（指后代继承前人的遗产）
inherit a title = 继承头衔
inherit the mantle = 继承衣钵


词源:
Old French *inheriter, from Latin *hereditare, from heres "heir".  The meaning has evolved from simply "to obtain as an heir" to encompass both legal succession and genetic transmission.
