It implies a healthy detachment and balanced perspective. |  Her nonobsessiveness about her work allowed her to enjoy a fulfilling personal life.  她的工作并非事事亲力亲为，这让她拥有了充实的生活。  |近义词： detachment, equanimity, calmness, composure |反义词： obsession, fixation, preoccupation
