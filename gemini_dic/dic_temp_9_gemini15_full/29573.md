catchweight:/ˈkætʃˌweɪt/| n.|约定体重；议定体重|A catchweight is a weight agreed upon by two boxers or other competitors before a fight or competition, rather than a standard weight class.  This is often done when the fighters' weights are close but not within the same weight class. |Example: The fight was scheduled for a catchweight of 160 pounds. 这场比赛被安排在160磅的约定体重进行。|近义词：agreed-upon weight, negotiated weight |反义词：standard weight class|名词
