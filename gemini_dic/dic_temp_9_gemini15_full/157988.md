plankton:/ˈplæŋktən/ | n. | 浮游生物 | Plankton are the diverse collection of mostly microscopic organisms that drift or float in the sea.  They form the base of the marine food web. | Example:  The ocean's plankton population fluctuates seasonally.  海洋浮游生物的数量随季节变化。

Many types of plankton exist, including phytoplankton (plant-like plankton) and zooplankton (animal-like plankton).  各种各样的浮游生物存在，包括浮游植物（类似植物的浮游生物）和浮游动物（类似动物的浮游生物）。|近义词：drifting organisms, marine microorganisms |反义词：nekton (actively swimming organisms)
