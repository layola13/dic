malthouses: /ˈmɔːlthaʊzɪz/ | n. | 麦芽厂；酿酒厂 | A malthouse is a building where barley is malted.  Malting involves steeping barley in water, allowing it to germinate, and then drying it to stop the germination process. This malted barley is then used in the brewing of beer.  | Example: The old malthouse has been converted into apartments.  这座古老的麦芽厂已被改造成公寓。


The village boasts a historic malthouse, still producing high-quality malt.  这个村庄拥有一个历史悠久的麦芽厂，仍在生产高质量的麦芽。| 近义词：brewery (if also brewing beer) | 反义词：N/A
