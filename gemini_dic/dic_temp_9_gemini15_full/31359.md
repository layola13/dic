characterlessness:/ˈkærəktərləsnəs/ | n. |缺乏个性；无特点；软弱 |Lack of character, strength, or individuality; moral weakness.  It refers to a person or thing lacking distinct or interesting qualities, often implying a lack of moral strength or firmness. |例句：His characterlessness made him easily swayed by others.  他的缺乏个性使他很容易被别人左右。

例句：The design displayed a striking characterlessness, lacking any unique features.  这个设计显示出惊人的缺乏个性，没有任何独特的特点。

近义词：weakness, spinelessness, insipidity, vapidity

反义词：strength, individuality, fortitude, character

词性：名词
