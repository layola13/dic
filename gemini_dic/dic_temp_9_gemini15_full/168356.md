pro-Venezuelan: /ˌproʊˌvɛnəˈzweɪlən/ | adj. | 亲委内瑞拉的 | Supporting or favoring Venezuela or its government.  This term describes individuals, policies, or movements that express support for Venezuela and its interests.  It can be used neutrally or with a positive or negative connotation depending on context.

例句：

The pro-Venezuelan demonstrators marched through the streets.  亲委内瑞拉的示威者走上街头。

His pro-Venezuelan stance alienated many of his former supporters. 他亲委内瑞拉的立场疏远了他许多以前的支持者。


近义词：Venezuelan-supporting, pro-Chavista (if referring to Chavismo),  supportive of Venezuela


反义词：anti-Venezuelan, opposed to Venezuela


词性:形容词
