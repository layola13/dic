prebendaryship:/ˈprɛbəndəriʃɪp/| n. |教区牧师的职位；教区牧师俸禄|The right to hold a prebend; the office or benefice of a prebendary.  A prebendaryship is a position within a cathedral or collegiate church, carrying with it an income (prebend) derived from church lands or endowments.  |例句：He inherited a prebendaryship at Canterbury Cathedral. 他继承了坎特伯雷大教堂的一个教区牧师职位。

The prebendaryship provided him with a comfortable income.  这个教区牧师职位为他提供了舒适的收入。|近义词：benefice, canonry|反义词：lay position
