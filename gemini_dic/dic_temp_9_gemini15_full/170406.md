prowess:/ˈpraʊəs/| n. |熟练；本领；英勇；技艺|Exceptional skill or ability; expertise.  It often implies bravery and daring, especially in battle.|例句：Her prowess as a negotiator is well known.  她的谈判技巧非常出名。

例句：The boxer's prowess in the ring was undeniable. 拳击手在拳击台上的实力不容置疑。

近义词：skill, expertise, mastery, ability, talent, dexterity

反义词：inability, incompetence, clumsiness


词源：源自古法语 * prouesse，意为“英勇行为，勇敢”，最终源于拉丁语 * probus，“正直的，优秀的”。
