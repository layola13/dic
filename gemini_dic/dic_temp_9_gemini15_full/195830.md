senecio:/səˈniːʃioʊ/| n. |千里光属植物；(一种)千里光|A genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, Asteraceae.  Many species are known as ragworts or groundsels.  They are often found in diverse habitats and exhibit a wide range of growth forms.|The senecio plant is known for its vibrant yellow flowers. = 千里光植物以其鲜艳的黄色花朵而闻名。

Many senecio species are used in traditional medicine, although some are toxic. = 许多千里光物种用于传统医学，尽管有些是有毒的。


近义词：groundsel, ragwort (depending on the specific species)|反义词：无
