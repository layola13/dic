preremit:/priːˈrɛmɪt/| v.|预先汇款，预付|To send or pay money in advance.|The company preremits funds to its suppliers to ensure timely delivery. 公司预先向供应商汇款以确保及时交货。|近义词：prepay, advance, remit in advance|反义词：postpay, pay later


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preremit the payment for the goods. = 他们预先支付货款。
The company preremits funds to its vendors. = 公司预先向供应商汇款。


一般过去时:
We preremitted the fees last week. = 我们上周预先支付了费用。
She preremitted the down payment for the house. = 她预先支付了房屋的首付款。


现在完成时:
They have preremitted the full amount. = 他们已经预先支付了全款。
We have preremitted the travel expenses. = 我们已经预先支付了差旅费。


过去完成时:
The client had preremitted the deposit before the project started. = 项目开始前，客户已经预先支付了押金。
They had preremitted the money to secure the contract. = 他们预先支付了款项以确保合同。


将来时:
We will preremit the funds next Monday. = 我们下周一将预先支付资金。
They will preremit the payment upon receiving the order. = 他们将在收到订单后预先支付款项。


虚拟语气:
If we preremitted the payment, they would start the work immediately. = 如果我们预先支付款项，他们会立即开始工作。
I wish we had preremitted the money earlier. = 我真希望我们早点预先支付了这笔钱。


被动语态:
The payment was preremitted last Friday. = 款项已于上周五预先支付。
The funds were preremitted to the contractor. = 资金已预先支付给承包商。


疑问句:
Did they preremit the money? = 他们预先支付了钱吗？
Has the payment been preremitted? = 款项已经预先支付了吗？


否定句:
We did not preremit the fees. = 我们没有预先支付费用。
They haven't preremitted the funds yet. = 他们还没有预先支付资金。
