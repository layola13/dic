guildships:/ˈɡɪldʃɪps/| n.|行会船只；行会制度下的船只|Guildships refers to ships owned or operated by a guild, a medieval association of craftsmen or merchants.  The term highlights the collective ownership and operation of vessels, often for trade or other guild-related purposes.|Example: The merchant guild's guildships dominated the port, their sails a testament to their economic power.  = 商会的行会船只占据了港口，它们的帆见证了它们的经济实力。|近义词:guild vessels, association ships|反义词:individual ships
