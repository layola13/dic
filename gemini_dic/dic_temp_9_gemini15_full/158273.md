play-with:/pleɪ wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 玩耍；摆弄；戏弄 | To engage in recreational activities with something or someone; to manipulate or experiment with something. | 例句：The children were playing with their toys. 孩子们在玩他们的玩具。

The artist played with light and shadow in the painting.艺术家在绘画中运用光影。


近义词：play, toy with, fiddle with, tamper with | 反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They play with their pets every day. = 他们每天都和他们的宠物玩耍。
We play with ideas to find solutions. = 我们尝试不同的想法以寻找解决方案。


一般过去时:
She played with her doll all afternoon. = 她整个下午都在玩她的洋娃娃。
He played with the idea of quitting his job. = 他考虑过辞职。


现在完成时:
They have played with this game for hours. = 他们玩这个游戏已经好几个小时了。
I have played with the software settings, but it still doesn't work. = 我已经调整了软件设置，但它仍然不起作用。


过去完成时:
They had played with the dog before going to bed. = 他们在睡觉前已经和狗玩过了。
She had played with the concept for years before writing the book. = 在写这本书之前，她已经构思这个概念多年了。


将来时:
They will play with their friends tomorrow. = 他们明天将和朋友一起玩耍。
We will play with different scenarios to prepare for the meeting. = 我们将演练不同的场景以准备会议。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would play with my children more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地和我的孩子们玩耍。
I wish I had played with that idea earlier. = 我真希望我早点考虑过那个想法。


被动语态:
The toys were played with by the children. = 玩具被孩子们玩耍了。(很少用被动语态)
The software settings cannot be played with without technical knowledge. = 没有技术知识，就不能随意更改软件设置。(被动语态用于强调限制)


疑问句:
What are the children playing with? = 孩子们在玩什么？
What are you playing with in your free time? = 你在空闲时间玩什么？


否定句:
They don't play with fire. = 他们不玩火。
I didn't play with that idea seriously. = 我并没有认真考虑过那个想法。


PHRASE->
play with fire = 玩火自焚
play with words = 玩弄文字
play with an idea = 构思一个想法
play with numbers = 随意更改数字
play with someone's emotions = 戏弄某人的感情
