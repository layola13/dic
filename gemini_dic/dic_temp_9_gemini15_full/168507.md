problems:/ˈprɒbləmz/ /ˈprɑːbləmz/| n.|问题，难题，麻烦|difficulties encountered; a matter or situation regarded as unwelcome or harmful and needing to be dealt with and overcome.|例句：We need to find a solution to these problems. 我们需要找到解决这些问题的方法。

例句：There are many problems associated with this project.  这个项目有很多问题。


近义词：issues, difficulties, troubles, challenges|反义词：solutions, answers, resolutions|词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project presents many problems. = 这个项目存在许多问题。
What problems are you facing? = 你面临着什么问题？


一般过去时:
We had several problems last year. = 我们去年遇到了一些问题。
What problems did you encounter during the trip? = 你在旅途中遇到了什么问题？


现在完成时:
We have encountered many problems this month. = 这个月我们遇到了很多问题。
Have you solved all the problems yet? = 你已经解决所有问题了吗？


过去完成时:
They had already solved most of the problems before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经解决了大部分问题。
What problems had they faced before the merger? = 在合并之前他们面临着什么问题？


将来时:
We will face many problems in the future. = 我们将来会面临许多问题。
What problems will we encounter in the next phase? = 在下一阶段我们会遇到什么问题？


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we could solve these problems easily. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们可以很容易地解决这些问题。
I wish we didn't have so many problems. = 我希望我们没有这么多问题。


被动语态:
The problems were discussed at the meeting. = 这些问题在会议上进行了讨论。
Many problems are being addressed by the team. = 团队正在处理许多问题。


疑问句:

Are there any problems with the machine? = 机器有什么问题吗？


否定句:
