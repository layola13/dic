reiterant: /ˈrɪtərənt/ | adj. | 重复的；反复的 | Repeatedly saying or doing something.  Characterized by repetition. | The reiterant nature of the instructions made it difficult to follow.  反复的指示让人难以理解。 | 近义词：repetitive, recurrent, repeated | 反义词：unique, original, novel


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He reiterates his point every meeting. = 他每次会议都重复他的观点。
The machine reiterates the same pattern. = 这台机器重复着同样的图案。

一般过去时:
She reiterated her concerns to the manager. = 她向经理重申了她的担忧。
The speaker reiterated the key findings. = 演讲者重申了主要发现。

现在完成时:
He has reiterated his commitment to the project. = 他已经重申了他对项目的承诺。
They have reiterated their support for the cause. = 他们已经重申了他们对这项事业的支持。

过去完成时:
She had reiterated her request several times before he finally agreed. = 在他最终同意之前，她已经多次重申了她的请求。
The government had reiterated its position on the matter. = 政府已经重申了其在该问题上的立场。

将来时:
He will reiterate his arguments in the debate. = 他将在辩论中重申他的论点。
The company will reiterate its commitment to customer satisfaction. = 公司将重申其对客户满意度的承诺。

虚拟语气:
If he reiterated his offer, I would accept. = 如果他重申他的提议，我会接受。
I wish he would reiterate his support for our plan. = 我希望他会重申他对我们计划的支持。

被动语态:
The company's position was reiterated in a press release. = 公司的立场在新闻稿中被重申了。
His concerns were reiterated by several other employees. = 他的担忧被其他几位员工重申了。

疑问句:
Will he reiterate his statement tomorrow? = 他明天会重申他的声明吗？
Did she reiterate her position during the interview? = 她在面试中重申了自己的立场吗？

否定句:
He did not reiterate his earlier statement. = 他没有重申他之前的声明。
The company has not reiterated its earlier decision. = 公司没有重申其之前的决定。
