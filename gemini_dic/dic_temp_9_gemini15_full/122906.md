molt:/moʊlt/ | /mɒʊlt/ | v. |蜕皮；脱落；更换(羽毛、皮毛等) | To shed or cast off, as feathers, skin, horns, or an insect exoskeleton.  |The snake molted its skin.  蛇蜕皮了。| The bird is molting its feathers. 这只鸟正在换羽。 | shed, cast off, slough | retain, keep

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The snake molts its skin once a year. = 蛇每年蜕皮一次。
Birds molt their feathers seasonally. = 鸟类会季节性地换羽。

一般过去时:
The caterpillar molted its skin before pupating. = 毛毛虫在化蛹前蜕皮了。
The lizard molted its skin last week. = 蜥蜴上周蜕皮了。

现在完成时:
The snake has molted twice this year. = 蛇今年已经蜕皮两次了。
The dog has molted heavily this spring. = 这条狗今年春天脱毛很厉害。

过去完成时:
The bird had molted before it migrated south. = 这只鸟在南迁之前已经换羽了。
The insect had molted several times before reaching adulthood. = 这只昆虫在成年之前已经蜕皮好几次了。

将来时:
The snake will molt again in the spring. = 蛇春天还会再蜕皮。
My cat will molt heavily in the fall. = 我的猫秋天会大量脱毛。

虚拟语气:
If the bird molted sooner, it might have migrated earlier. = 如果鸟早点换羽，它可能就早点迁徙了。
If the snake had molted successfully, it would be healthier. = 如果蛇成功蜕皮，它会更健康。

被动语态:
The skin was molted by the snake. = 蛇蜕掉了皮。(很少用被动语态)
The feathers are being molted by the bird. = 鸟正在换羽。(很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Does the snake molt its skin easily? = 蛇容易蜕皮吗？
When do birds usually molt? = 鸟类通常什么时候换羽？

否定句:
The snake didn't molt its skin yet. = 蛇还没蜕皮。
The bird is not molting currently. = 鸟目前没有换羽。


PHRASE->
