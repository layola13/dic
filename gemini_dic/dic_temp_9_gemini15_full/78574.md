giambeux:/ʒɑ̃ˈbœ/| n.|腿甲，护腿|A giambeux is a piece of medieval armor that protected the leg, typically extending from the knee to the ankle.  It was often made of metal plates or lames and was worn over other leg armor like chausses.  |例句：The knight donned his giambeux before mounting his steed. =  骑士穿上他的护腿甲后才骑上他的坐骑。

The tournament required participants to wear full plate armor, including giambeux. = 锦标赛要求参与者穿戴全套板甲，包括护腿甲。|近义词：leg armor, greaves, cuisses (if referring to the thigh portion)|反义词：无|名词
