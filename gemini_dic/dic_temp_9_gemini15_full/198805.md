side-effect:/ˈsaɪdɪˈfekt/| n. |副作用，意外影响|An unintended and usually undesirable effect of a drug or treatment.  It can also refer to an unexpected consequence of an action or event.|例句：The medication has some unpleasant side effects, such as nausea and drowsiness. 这药有一些令人不快的副作用，例如恶心和嗜睡。

例句：The economic sanctions had unforeseen side effects on the country's infrastructure. 经济制裁对该国的基础设施产生了不可预见的影响。

近义词：adverse effect, undesirable effect, unintended consequence, secondary effect|反义词：intended effect, desired outcome|词性：名词
