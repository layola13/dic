词性：形容词 (participle of the verb "reprimand")


词源:  The word "reprimanding" is derived from the verb "reprimand," which comes from the Old French word "reprimander," meaning "to reprove," "to rebuke."  The prefix "re-" indicates "again" or "back," while the root is related to the Latin word "prehendere," meaning "to seize" or "to grasp," suggesting the act of seizing or grasping someone's attention to correct their behavior.
