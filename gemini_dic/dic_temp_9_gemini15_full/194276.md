self-offered:/ˌsɛlfˈɔːfərd/| adj.|主动提供的；自愿提供的| Voluntarily offered or given; not requested or demanded.  This adjective describes something that is given or presented without being asked for. |例句：He received a self-offered promotion. 他得到了一次主动申请的升职。

例句：The company received many self-offered suggestions for improvement. 公司收到了许多主动提供的改进建议。


近义词：voluntary, unsolicited, spontaneous, unrequested

反义词：requested, demanded, required, obligatory

词性:形容词
