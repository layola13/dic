retrials:/ˈriːtraɪəl/| n.| 重审；再审；复审 | The process of having a trial again, typically after an initial trial has been deemed unfair or flawed.  It involves reviewing the evidence and legal arguments, and potentially leading to a different outcome. |例句：The defendant was granted a retrial after new evidence emerged.  被告在出现新的证据后获得了重审的机会。

例句：The judge ordered a retrial due to juror misconduct.  法官由于陪审员行为不当而下令重审。


近义词：new trial, rehearing


反义词：final judgment, conclusive verdict


词性:名词
