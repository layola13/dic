profitable:/ˈprɒfɪtəbl/ /ˈprɑːfɪtəbl/ | adj. |有利可图的，赚钱的 |Profitable describes something that makes or is likely to make a profit.  It refers to activities or ventures that generate more revenue than expenses. |例句：The new marketing campaign proved highly profitable. 新的营销活动证明非常有利可图。

The investment turned out to be more profitable than we had anticipated. 这项投资的结果比我们预期的更有利可图。


近义词：lucrative, rewarding, advantageous, remunerative |反义词：unprofitable, losing, uneconomical

词性：形容词
