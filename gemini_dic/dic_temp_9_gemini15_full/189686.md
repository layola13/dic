saloon:/səˈluːn/| n.|酒馆，沙龙，轿车|A saloon is a public house or bar, especially one that is large and elegant.  It can also refer to a large, luxurious passenger car.  Historically, it referred to a large room for social gatherings.|例句：He spent the evening in a lively saloon. 他在热闹的酒馆里度过了晚上。
The family drove across the country in their new saloon car.  这个家庭开着他们的新轿车横跨了全国。|近义词：bar, pub, tavern, sedan|反义词： None specifically.
