sarcophage:/ˈsɑːrkəfeɪdʒ/|/ˈsɑːrkəfædʒ/| n. |石棺；石棺材|A sarcophagus is a stone coffin, often ornately decorated, typically used for the burial of important persons.  Sarcophagi were commonly used in ancient Egypt, Greece, and Rome.|例句：The museum houses a magnificent sarcophagus from ancient Egypt.  博物馆收藏着一个来自古埃及的华丽石棺。

The ancient pharaoh was laid to rest in a massive sarcophagus.  这位古代法老被安葬在一个巨大的石棺里。|近义词：coffin, tomb|反义词：urn (for ashes)
