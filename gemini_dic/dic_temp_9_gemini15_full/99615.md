interjects:/ˌɪntərˈdʒɛkt/ | vt. | 插嘴；突然插入；打断 | To interrupt a conversation or activity by saying something.  | Example: "He interjected a rude comment into the discussion." 他在讨论中插了一句粗鲁的话。 | 近义词: interrupt, butt in, break in | 反义词:  listen attentively, remain silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He interjects witty remarks during lectures. = 他在讲座期间插话一些俏皮话。
She frequently interjects opinions in meetings. = 她经常在会议上插话发表意见。


一般过去时:
The teacher interjected a correction to the student's answer. = 老师打断学生回答，进行了纠正。
He interjected a question during the presentation. = 他在演示过程中插了一句问题。


现在完成时:
She has interjected herself into the argument several times. = 她已经几次插手这场争论了。
He has interjected his thoughts throughout the report. = 他在报告中穿插了自己的想法。


过去完成时:
Before I could finish my sentence, he had interjected a sarcastic comment. = 我话还没说完，他就插了一句讽刺的话。
They had interjected several times before the speaker finally finished. = 演讲者最终完成演讲之前，他们已经插话好几次了。


将来时:
I know he will interject during the debate. = 我知道他会在辩论中插话。
She will likely interject with a different perspective. = 她可能会插入不同的观点。


虚拟语气:
If he had not interjected, the meeting would have ended earlier. = 如果他没有插话，会议就会早点结束。
I wish he hadn't interjected with that irrelevant detail. = 我真希望他没有插进那个无关紧要的细节。


被动语态:
A comment was interjected by a member of the audience. = 观众中的一位成员插了一句评论。
His opinion was interjected into the conversation. = 他的意见被插入到谈话中。


疑问句:
Did anyone interject during your presentation? = 在你的演示过程中有人插话吗？
Will someone interject if I make a mistake? = 如果我犯错，有人会插话吗？


否定句:
He didn't interject during the entire meeting. = 他在整个会议期间都没有插话。
She chose not to interject, even though she disagreed. = 即使她不同意，她也选择不插话。


PHRASE->
interject a remark = 插一句评论
interject an opinion = 插话发表意见
interject a question = 插问一句
interject a comment = 插话评论
rudely interject = 粗鲁地插话
