out-shaping:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪpɪŋ/| v. |胜过，超越，在外形上超过|To surpass or exceed in shape or form; to excel in design or appearance.|The new building out-shapes all the others in the city. 新建筑在城市中胜过其他所有建筑。  The sculpture out-shapes its predecessors in terms of artistry. 这座雕塑在艺术性方面超越了它的前辈。|outperform, excel, surpass|underperform, fall short|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The athlete out-shapes his competitors in terms of physique. = 运动员在体格方面胜过他的竞争对手。
This design out-shapes all previous models in elegance. = 这个设计在优雅方面超越了所有之前的型号。


一般过去时:
The artist's latest work out-shaped his previous creations. = 艺术家的最新作品超越了他之前的作品。
That strategy out-shaped the opponent's plan. = 那个策略胜过了对手的计划。


现在完成时:
The company has out-shaped its rivals in market share. = 这家公司在市场份额上已经超越了竞争对手。
This technology has out-shaped all previous attempts at innovation. = 这项技术已经超越了之前所有创新的尝试。


过去完成时:
By the end of the game, their team had out-shaped the opposing team in skill. = 到比赛结束时，他们的团队在技巧方面已经胜过了对方团队。
The artist had out-shaped his mentor’s earlier works by then. = 到那时，这位艺术家已经超越了他导师早期的作品。


将来时:
Our new product will out-shape the competition in terms of functionality. = 我们的新产品将在功能方面超越竞争对手。
They predict that this design will out-shape current market offerings. = 他们预测这个设计将超越目前的市场产品。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, we might have out-shaped our competitors. = 如果我们投资更多，我们可能会超越竞争对手。
I wish our product had out-shaped the others on the market. = 我希望我们的产品能够在市场上超越其他产品。


被动语态:
The old design was out-shaped by the new model. = 旧设计被新模型超越了。
Their marketing campaign was out-shaped by their competitor's innovative approach. = 他们的营销活动被竞争对手的创新方法所超越。


疑问句:
Does this design out-shape the previous version? = 这个设计是否超越了之前的版本？
Will our new strategy out-shape the competitor's plan? = 我们的新策略是否会超越竞争对手的计划？


否定句:
The small company did not out-shape the industry giant. = 这家小公司没有超越行业巨头。
Our efforts did not out-shape the challenges we faced. = 我们的努力没有克服我们面临的挑战。
