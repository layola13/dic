repressions:/rɪˈprɛʃənz/ | n. |压制；压抑；抑制 |The act of suppressing or restraining, especially of thoughts, feelings, or desires.  It often refers to the psychological process of keeping unacceptable or threatening thoughts and emotions out of conscious awareness.  Repressions can manifest in various ways, including anxiety, depression, and physical symptoms.|例句：The repressive regime stifled all dissent. 这个压制性政权扼杀了所有异议。

例句：His childhood traumas led to years of emotional repressions. 他童年的创伤导致了多年的情感压抑。


近义词：suppressions, inhibitions, constraints, restraints |反义词：expressions, releases, outbursts


词性:名词
