devotes:/dɪˈvoʊts/| vt.|献身；致力于；专心于|to give one's time, effort, or attention earnestly to something:|She devotes herself to her work. 她致力于她的工作。|近义词：dedicates, commits, consecrates |反义词：neglects, ignores


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He devotes his weekends to gardening. = 他把周末都用来园艺。
She devotes much of her time to charity work. = 她把大部分时间都用于慈善事业。


一般过去时:
They devoted their lives to serving others. = 他们一生致力于服务他人。
He devoted the entire afternoon to writing the report. = 他把整个下午都用来写报告。


现在完成时:
She has devoted years to studying ancient history. = 她已经多年致力于研究古代历史。
He has devoted his life to helping the poor. = 他毕生致力于帮助穷人。


过去完成时:
They had devoted many hours to the project before it was finally completed. =  在项目最终完成之前，他们已经投入了大量时间。
She had devoted her entire youth to mastering the violin. = 她把整个青春都献给了小提琴的学习。


将来时:
I will devote more time to my studies next semester. = 下学期我将把更多时间投入到学习中。
We will devote the next meeting to discussing the budget. = 我们将在下一次会议上讨论预算。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would devote it to learning a new language. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把它用来学习一门外语。
I wish I could devote more time to my family. = 我希望我能多花些时间陪家人。


被动语态:
Much time was devoted to the preparation of the event. =  大量时间用于活动准备。
His entire life was devoted to the pursuit of knowledge. = 他的一生都致力于追求知识。


疑问句:
How much time do you devote to exercise each week? = 你每周花多少时间锻炼？
What aspects of your work do you devote the most time to? = 你在工作中把最多时间花在哪些方面？


否定句:
He doesn't devote enough time to his family. = 他没有花足够的时间陪伴家人。
She didn't devote sufficient attention to detail in her report. = 她在报告中没有足够注意细节。


PHRASE->
devote oneself to = 献身于
devote time to = 投入时间于
devote attention to = 专注于
devote energy to = 投入精力于
devote resources to = 投入资源于
