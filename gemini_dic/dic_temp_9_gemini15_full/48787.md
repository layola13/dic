deflecting: /dɪˈflɛktɪŋ/ | adj. | 使偏斜的；转移的 |  Causing something to change direction or deviate from a course; turning aside. |


英文解析:The present participle of deflect.  It describes something that is in the process of changing direction, diverting, or turning aside something else.  It can refer to physical objects or abstract concepts.


例句:

The shield deflected the blow. =  盾牌挡住了打击。

His attempts to deflect attention from the scandal failed. = 他试图转移人们对丑闻的注意力，但失败了。


近义词: diverting, turning aside, warding off, averting


反义词:  straightening, directing, confronting


词性: adjective (形容词)
