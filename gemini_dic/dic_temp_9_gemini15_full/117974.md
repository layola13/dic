memorabilia:/ˌmɛməˈrɪliə/| n. |纪念品，珍藏品 |Things kept as reminders of a past event, person, or period.  These items often hold sentimental value.|The museum has a fascinating collection of Beatles memorabilia.|近义词：souvenirs, relics, keepsakes |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "ordinary items" or "disposable items".|名词
