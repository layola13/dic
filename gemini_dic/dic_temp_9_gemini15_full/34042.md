city:/ˈsɪti/| n. |城市，都市 |A city is a large and densely populated area, usually with a defined administrative area. Cities are typically characterized by a high concentration of buildings, infrastructure, and economic activity.|例句：London is a bustling city. 伦敦是一个繁华的城市。|例句：Many people move to the city to find work. 许多人搬到城市找工作。|近义词：town, metropolis, urban area |反义词：countryside, rural area, village
