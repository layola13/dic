donkey:/ˈdɒŋki/ /ˈdɑːŋki/| n.|驴；顽固的人|A donkey is a domesticated hoofed mammal used for carrying loads and riding.  The term can also be used informally to refer to a stubborn or foolish person. |例句：The farmer used his donkey to carry hay.  这个农民用他的驴来驮草料。

例句：He's such a donkey; he never listens to anyone's advice. 他真是个倔驴，从来不听别人的劝告。|近义词：ass, mule|反义词：horse (in terms of strength and capability), intelligent person (in the figurative sense)
