mind-boggling: /ˈmaɪndˌbɒɡlɪŋ/ | adj. |令人难以置信的；令人费解的；令人震惊的 | Extremely surprising or difficult to understand; bewildering.  |例句：The complexity of the problem was mind-boggling.  这个问题的复杂性令人难以置信。

例句：The sheer scale of the universe is mind-boggling. 宇宙的浩瀚规模令人难以置信。


近义词：astounding, baffling, bewildering, breathtaking, confusing, incredible, perplexing, staggering, stunning, unbelievable

反义词：understandable, comprehensible, clear, simple
