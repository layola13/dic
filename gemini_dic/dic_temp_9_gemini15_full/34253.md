clape:/kleɪp/| n.|碎石，瓦砾堆|A clape is a pile of stones or rubble, often found at the foot of a cliff or on a mountainside.  It is formed by the accumulation of rock fragments that have broken off from the surrounding rock formations.|例句：The path was blocked by a large clape of rocks.  路被一大堆碎石挡住了。|近义词：scree, rubble, debris|反义词：none
