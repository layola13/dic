reawaken:/ˌriːəˈweɪkən/| vt.|重新唤醒；重新激起；使复活|To awaken again; to rouse from sleep or inactivity; to revive or rekindle.|例句：The music reawakened her sleeping emotions. 音乐重新唤醒了她沉睡的情感。

例句：The news reawakened his political ambitions. 这则新闻重新燃起了他的政治野心。


近义词：rouse, revive, reanimate, awaken, stir

反义词：put to sleep, lull, quiet, subdue

词性：动词
