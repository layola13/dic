renowmed:/rɪˈnaʊnd/| adj.|著名的，享有盛名的|renowned means famous and respected for a particular skill, achievement, or quality.|例句：He is a renowned expert in the field of artificial intelligence. 他是人工智能领域著名的专家。|例句：The city is renowned for its beautiful architecture.  这座城市以其美丽的建筑而闻名。|近义词：famous, celebrated, distinguished, illustrious|反义词：obscure, unknown, infamous

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef is renowned for his innovative cuisine. =这位厨师以其创新的烹饪而闻名。
This university is renowned for its rigorous academic standards. = 这所大学以其严格的学术标准而闻名。

一般过去时:
The artist was renowned for his bold use of color. =这位艺术家以其大胆的色彩运用而闻名。
The company was renowned for its high-quality products. =这家公司以其高质量的产品而闻名。

现在完成时:
She has become renowned for her humanitarian work. =她因其人道主义工作而闻名。
The author has become renowned for his captivating storytelling. =这位作家因其引人入胜的故事而闻名。

过去完成时:
He had already become renowned in his field before he won the Nobel Prize. =在获得诺贝尔奖之前，他已经在自己的领域享有盛名。
The musician had been renowned throughout the country before his international debut. =这位音乐家在国际首秀之前就已经在全国享有盛名。

将来时:
She will be renowned for her groundbreaking discoveries. =她将因其突破性的发现而闻名。
This invention will surely become renowned in the future. =这项发明将来一定会闻名于世。

虚拟语气:
If he were renowned, he would have more opportunities. =如果他很有名，他就会有更多机会。
I wish I were renowned for my writing. =我希望我的写作能够闻名于世。

被动语态:
He is renowned throughout the world. = 他在全世界都很有名。
His work is renowned for its originality. = 他的作品以其独创性而闻名。

疑问句:
Is he renowned in his field? = 他在这个领域很有名吗？
Is this museum renowned for its collection? = 这个博物馆以其收藏而闻名吗？

否定句:
He is not renowned for his kindness. = 他并不以他的善良而闻名。
The product is not renowned for its durability. = 该产品并不以其耐用性而闻名。


PHRASE->
