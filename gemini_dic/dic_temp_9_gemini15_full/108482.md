lay to rest: /leɪ tuː rɛst/ | v. |  安葬；使…平静；结束；解决 | To bury (a deceased person); to put something to an end; to quiet or calm something. |


例句：

They laid their father to rest in the family plot.  = 他们在家族墓地安葬了他们的父亲。

We need to lay this argument to rest once and for all. = 我们需要一劳永逸地解决这场争论。


近义词：bury, inter, put to rest, settle, resolve


反义词：exhume, resurrect, revive, agitate


词性：动词短语


USAGE->

一般现在时:

They lay their loved ones to rest with respect. = 他们怀着敬意安葬他们的亲人。

We try to lay our anxieties to rest before bed. = 我们试图在睡觉前让我们的焦虑平静下来。


一般过去时:

The family laid their grandmother to rest yesterday. =  家人昨天安葬了他们的祖母。

She laid her fears to rest after a long conversation. =  在长时间的谈话之后，她平息了她的恐惧。


现在完成时:

They have laid their differences to rest and are now friends. = 他们已经化解了分歧，现在是朋友了。

We have laid the rumor to rest by presenting the facts. = 我们通过陈述事实平息了谣言。


过去完成时:

Before the trial, they had laid their suspicions to rest. = 在审判之前，他们已经打消了他们的怀疑。

After the investigation, they had laid the mystery to rest. =  调查结束后，他们解开了这个谜团。


将来时:

They will lay their mother to rest next week. = 他们下周将安葬他们的母亲。

We will lay this matter to rest as soon as we have all the information. = 一旦我们掌握了所有信息，我们将解决此事。


虚拟语气:

If I could lay my worries to rest, I would be much happier. = 如果我能消除我的担忧，我会快乐得多。

I wish I could lay this problem to rest quickly. = 我希望我能很快解决这个问题。


被动语态:

The deceased was laid to rest with full military honors. = 逝者以全军礼仪下葬。

The argument was finally laid to rest after much debate. = 在经过多次辩论之后，争论最终得到了解决。


疑问句:

Where will the victim be laid to rest? = 受害者将被安葬在哪里？

When will this issue be laid to rest? =  这个问题什么时候才能解决？


否定句:

They haven't laid their grievances to rest. =  他们还没有放下他们的怨恨。

We cannot lay this matter to rest until we find the culprit. = 在找到罪犯之前，我们不能解决这个问题。
