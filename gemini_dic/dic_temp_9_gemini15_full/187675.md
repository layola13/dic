rooibos:/ˈrɔɪbɒs/| n. |南非国宝茶，红灌木茶|A type of herbal tea made from the leaves of the rooibos plant (Aspalathus linearis), native to South Africa. It is naturally sweet and caffeine-free.  |Example: I prefer rooibos tea to black tea because it's less bitter and caffeine-free. 我更喜欢南非国宝茶而不是红茶，因为它不苦而且不含咖啡因。|近义词：herbal tea, redbush tea |反义词：caffeinated tea, black tea
