retwisting: /ˌriːˈtwɪstɪŋ/ | v. | 重新扭曲；再次扭曲 | The act of twisting something again, often implying a reversal or undoing of a previous twist.  It can refer to physical objects or metaphorical situations. |例句：The rope needed retwisting after it became frayed.  绳子磨损后需要重新拧紧。

The sculptor was retwisting the wire to achieve a more delicate shape. 雕塑家正在重新扭曲金属丝以获得更精细的形状。


近义词：rewinding, rebraiding, re-twisting


反义词：Untwisting, straightening


词性:动词
