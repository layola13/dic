matzohs:/ˈmɑːtsoʊz/| n.|无酵饼| Unleavened bread eaten by Jewish people, especially during Passover.  It is typically crisp and cracker-like. |例句：We ate matzohs with our Seder dinner. 我们在逾越节晚餐吃了无酵饼。|例句：The box contained several packages of matzohs. 盒子里装着几包无酵饼。|近义词：cracker (in the sense of a thin, crisp bread)|反义词：leavened bread|名词
