dickerson:/ˈdɪkərsən/| n. |迪克森（姓氏）|A surname of English origin.  It is a locational surname, derived from various places named Dickerson in England, which in turn are derived from the Old English personal name Dic(c) and the Old English word *sunu*, meaning "son".
