manias:/ˈmeɪniəz/| n.|狂热；癖好；痴迷|A mania is an excessive enthusiasm or obsession with something.  It often implies an uncontrollable urge or passion.  The term can be used to describe both positive and negative obsessions, but it often carries a negative connotation, suggesting an unhealthy preoccupation.|例句：His mania for collecting stamps filled his entire house. 他收集邮票的狂热爱好塞满了他的整个房子。
His gambling mania led to financial ruin. 他沉迷于赌博导致了经济破产。|近义词：obsession, craze, fascination, passion|反义词：apathy, indifference, calmness
