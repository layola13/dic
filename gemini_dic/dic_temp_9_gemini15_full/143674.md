outlaugh:/ˌaʊtˈlæf/| vt.|大声笑过；笑得比…大声|To laugh louder or more heartily than (someone).  It implies a competitive or surpassing aspect of laughter.|例句：He outlaughed all the others at the comedian's joke. 他比其他人更大声地笑了，听了喜剧演员的笑话。

例句：The children outlaughed their parents at the silly movie. 孩子们看着这部滑稽的电影笑得比他们的父母还大声。


近义词：outdo, surpass, exceed|反义词：weep, sob, cry
