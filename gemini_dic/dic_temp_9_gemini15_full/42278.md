counter-elite: /ˈkaʊntərɪˈliːt/ | n. | 反精英；对抗精英 | A group or class of people who oppose or challenge the established elite, often advocating for alternative values and power structures.  They may be from the working class, intellectual circles, or other marginalized groups.  They often challenge the authority, influence, and policies of the dominant elite. | Example: The rise of populist movements can be seen as the emergence of a powerful counter-elite challenging the traditional political establishment.  反精英势力的兴起可以被看作是挑战传统政治体制的有力反精英力量的出现。|近义词：opposition, dissidents, rebels, counterculture |反义词：elite, establishment, mainstream
