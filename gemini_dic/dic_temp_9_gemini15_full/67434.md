extinguished:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt/ | adj. v. |熄灭的；使熄灭的 | extinguished means having been put out; no longer burning.  It can describe a fire, a light, or even a feeling or hope. |例句：The fire was quickly extinguished by the firefighters. 消防员迅速扑灭了大火。

The hope in her eyes was extinguished. 她眼中的希望破灭了。|近义词：put out, quenched, suppressed |反义词：ignited, kindled, lit
