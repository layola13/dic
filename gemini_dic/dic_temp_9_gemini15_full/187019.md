ritornello: /ˌrɪtɔːrˈnɛloʊ/ | n. | 重奏，回旋曲 | A short musical phrase that is repeated at intervals in a piece of music, especially in a Baroque concerto or sonata.  It typically returns after a solo section or episode.  | Example: The ritornello in the Vivaldi concerto provided a grounding theme amidst the virtuosic violin solos.  | 近义词: refrain, recurring theme | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与之形成对比的概念，例如：solo, independent section) | 名词


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't have verb or adjective forms that follow standard grammatical conjugations.  Therefore, tense and voice examples are not applicable.  The usage is almost exclusively descriptive.


PHRASE->
ritornello form:  回旋曲式
vocal ritornello: 声乐回旋
instrumental ritornello: 器乐回旋
repeated ritornello: 重复的回旋
thematic ritornello: 主题回旋


词源:
From Italian *ritornello*, diminutive of *ritorno* "return," from *ri-* "again" + *torno* "return," from Latin *tornare*.  The musical term reflects the return of the musical phrase.
