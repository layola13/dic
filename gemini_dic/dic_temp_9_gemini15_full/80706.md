gorilla:/ɡəˈrɪlə/| n. |大猩猩 |A large, powerful ape with dark hair, found in the forests of central Africa. Gorillas are the largest living primates.  They are known for their intelligence and social structures. | 例句：The gorilla beat its chest in a display of dominance. 这只大猩猩捶胸以示其统治地位。

例句：We saw a family of gorillas in the rainforest.我们在雨林里看到了一群大猩猩。

近义词：ape

反义词：None (no direct antonym)

词性:名词

词源:
The word "gorilla" comes from the Greek word "gorillai," which was used by the ancient Greek writer Hanno to describe a type of hairy, wild woman or ape encountered during his voyages to West Africa.  The modern spelling and usage evolved over time.
