penumbrae:/pɛˈnʌmbrē/| n. |半影；半暗部 |The penumbrae are the partially shaded outer regions of a shadow. 半影是阴影部分遮挡的外围区域。|例句：During a partial solar eclipse, the penumbrae are visible as a gradual dimming of the sun. 在日偏食期间，半影表现为太阳逐渐变暗。  The penumbrae of the trees cast dappled shadows on the ground. 树木的半影在地面上投下斑驳的阴影。|近义词：partially shaded area, shadow, shade |反义词：umbra (本影)
