reexamined:/ˌriːɪɡˈzæmɪnd/| v.|重新检查；再审查|To examine again; to reconsider.|例句：The committee reexamined the evidence and reached a different conclusion. 委员会重新审查了证据，得出了不同的结论。The scientist reexamined his data to ensure accuracy. 科学家重新检查了他的数据以确保准确性。|近义词：reviewed, reassessed, reinvestigated|反义词：ignored, overlooked|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reexamine the data every year. = 他们每年都重新检查数据。
We reexamine our strategies regularly. = 我们定期重新审查我们的策略。

一般过去时:
She reexamined the contract before signing. = 她在签字之前重新检查了合同。
He reexamined the evidence and found new clues. = 他重新审查了证据，发现了新的线索。

现在完成时:
They have reexamined the case several times. = 他们已经多次重新审查了此案。
We have reexamined our initial findings. = 我们已经重新审查了我们的初步调查结果。

过去完成时:
She had reexamined the documents before presenting her report. = 她提交报告之前已经重新审查了文件。
They had reexamined the evidence before changing their verdict. = 他们改变判决之前已经重新审查了证据。

将来时:
We will reexamine the policy next month. = 我们下个月将重新审查这项政策。
They will reexamine the application carefully. = 他们将仔细重新审查申请。

虚拟语气:
If they reexamined the evidence, they might change their minds. = 如果他们重新审查证据，他们可能会改变主意。
I wish they had reexamined the data before publishing the results. = 我希望他们在发表结果之前重新审查了数据。

被动语态:
The evidence was reexamined by the judge. = 证据被法官重新审查了。

疑问句:

否定句:
