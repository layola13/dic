peace-keeping: /ˈpiːsˌkiːpɪŋ/ | n. | 维持和平；和平行动 | The action of keeping peace, typically through the intervention of a third party.  This often involves deploying peacekeeping forces to a conflict zone to monitor a ceasefire, disarm combatants, and protect civilians.  | 例句：The UN peacekeeping mission in the region has been successful in reducing violence. 联合国在该地区的维和行动已成功地减少了暴力。

例句：Peacekeeping efforts often require international cooperation and significant resources. 维持和平的工作通常需要国际合作和大量资源。


近义词：peacemaking, conflict resolution, mediation | 反义词：warfare, aggression, conflict escalation | 词性：名词
