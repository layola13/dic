pathway:/ˈpæθˌweɪ/| n. |路径；途径；方法；通路 |A pathway is a route or track, often one that is made by people walking along it. It can also refer to a series of steps or processes involved in achieving something.  |例句：The pathway through the woods was overgrown with weeds.  林间小路长满了杂草。

The pathway to success requires hard work and dedication.  成功的道路需要努力和奉献。


近义词：route, road, track, course, method, approach, process |反义词：dead end, impasse
