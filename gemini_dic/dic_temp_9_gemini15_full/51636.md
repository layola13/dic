devexity: /dɪˈvɛksɪti/ | n. | 凸度，弯曲度 | The degree to which a curve deviates from a straight line;  specifically,  the reciprocal of the radius of curvature.  It is a measure of how sharply a curve bends.  In mathematics and engineering, it's used to describe the curvature of a surface or line. | 例句：The devexity of the road made driving difficult. 这条路的凸度使得驾驶变得困难。 The high devexity of the lens resulted in a distorted image. 透镜的高凸度导致图像失真。| 近义词：curvature, convexity | 反义词：concavity
