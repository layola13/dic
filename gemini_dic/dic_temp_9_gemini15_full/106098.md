knifeful: /ˈnaɪfˌful/ | n. | 一刀的量；满满一刀的量 | A knife full of something.  It refers to the amount that can be held on the blade of a knife.  This word is less common and more informal.  |例句：He spread a knifeful of butter on his toast. 他在吐司上抹了一刀黄油。


例句：She took a knifeful of jam and put it on her scone. 她取了一刀果酱涂在她的烤饼上。


近义词：knife's worth,  a blade full of


反义词：N/A (there isn't a direct opposite)
