exogamies:/ˌɛksəˈɡæmiz/ | n. |异族通婚(复数) |The practice or custom of marrying outside a community, clan, or tribe.  Exogamies are often enforced by social rules or traditions. |例句：Many cultures throughout history have practiced exogamies to strengthen alliances and prevent inbreeding.  许多历史文化都实行异族通婚以加强联盟并防止近亲繁殖。


例句：The study examined the prevalence of exogamies in different societies. 这项研究调查了不同社会中异族通婚的普遍性。


近义词：outmarriage, intermarriage (if referring to marriage between different groups) | 反义词：endogamies


词性：名词
