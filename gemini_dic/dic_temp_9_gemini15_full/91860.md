hyperimmunizes:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmjuːnʌɪzɪz/ | vt. | 高度免疫；过度免疫 | To produce a much higher than normal level of immunity to a specific antigen. This often involves the administration of multiple doses of an antigen or the use of specialized techniques to boost the immune response.  |  The researchers hyperimmunized the mice with the bacterial toxin to study the immune response.  研究人员用细菌毒素对老鼠进行了高度免疫以研究免疫反应。 The repeated exposure to the allergen hyperimmunized her, leading to a severe allergic reaction. 反复接触过敏原使她高度免疫，导致严重的过敏反应。 | immunize, vaccinate | weaken immune system  |  动词
