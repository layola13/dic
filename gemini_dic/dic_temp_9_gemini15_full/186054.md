rewrapped:/ˌriːˈræpt/| v. |重新包裹；重新包装|To wrap something again.  This usually implies that the original wrapping was insufficient, damaged, or needed improvement.|例句：I had to rewrap the gift because the original paper tore.  我不得不重新包装礼物，因为原来的包装纸撕破了。
例句：The company decided to rewrap its products to appeal to a younger market. 公司决定重新包装产品以吸引更年轻的市场。|近义词：repackage, repack|反义词：unwrap, unpack|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewraps presents carefully. = 他仔细地重新包装礼物。
They rewrap the products before shipping. = 他们在发货前重新包装产品。

一般过去时:
She rewrapped the damaged package. = 她重新包装了损坏的包裹。
The store rewrapped all the discounted items. = 商店重新包装了所有打折的商品。

现在完成时:
I have rewrapped the gifts for my nieces. = 我已经为我的侄女们重新包装了礼物。
They have rewrapped the presents multiple times. = 他们已经多次重新包装礼物了。

过去完成时:
He had rewrapped the present before giving it to her. = 他在送给她之前已经重新包装了礼物。
They had rewrapped the goods before putting them on display. = 他们在陈列商品之前已经重新包装了货物。


将来时:
I will rewrap the cake before the party. = 我会在派对前重新包装蛋糕。
She will rewrap the presents tomorrow. = 她明天将重新包装礼物。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rewrap all the presents. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新包装所有礼物。
I wish I had rewrapped the fragile items more carefully. = 我真希望我更仔细地重新包装那些易碎物品。


被动语态:
