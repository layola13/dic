hipper:/ˈhɪpər/| adj.|时髦的，现代的，赶时髦的|More fashionable or modern;  Someone who is hip is considered to be aware of and participating in current trends, especially in music, fashion, and culture.  It often implies a certain coolness or sophistication.|例句：Her style is so hipper than mine.她的风格比我的时髦多了。

例句：That new coffee shop is really hipper than the old one. 那家新的咖啡店比老的那家时髦多了。


近义词：trendy, fashionable, stylish, chic, cool

反义词：unfashionable, outdated, old-fashioned, passé


词源:源于"hip",  "hip"最初指的是"了解情况的，有见识的"，后来逐渐演变成"时髦的，酷的"的意思。"hipper"是"hip"的比较级形式，表示程度更高。
