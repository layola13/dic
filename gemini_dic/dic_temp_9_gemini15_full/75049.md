fright:/fraɪt/| n.|惊吓；恐惧|A sudden and intense feeling of fear.  It often refers to a sudden, unexpected scare.|例句：The sudden noise gave me a fright.  突如其来的噪音吓了我一跳。|例句：She was filled with fright at the thought of being alone in the dark. 她想到独自在黑暗中就充满了恐惧。|近义词：fear, terror, scare, alarm|反义词：calm, peace, composure|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud thunder frightens the children. = 响亮的雷声吓坏了孩子们。
That scary movie frightens me. = 那部恐怖电影吓到我了。


一般过去时:
The dog's barking frightened the cat. = 狗的叫声吓到了猫。
The sudden appearance of the snake frightened her. = 蛇的突然出现吓了她一跳。


现在完成时:
Nothing has frightened me as much as that experience. = 没有什么比那次经历更让我害怕了。
That haunted house has frightened many visitors. = 那座鬼屋吓坏了很多游客。


过去完成时:

I had never been so frightened in my life. = 我一生中从未如此害怕过。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
