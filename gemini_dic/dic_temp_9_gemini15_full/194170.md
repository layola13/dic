self-interview:/ˌselfˈɪntərvjuː/| n. | 自我面试，自我评估 | A self-interview is a process of self-reflection and introspection where an individual critically examines their skills, experiences, and qualifications in preparation for a job interview or to assess their own career progress.  It involves answering common interview questions as if one were being interviewed by another person. | Example:  I conducted a thorough self-interview before applying for the management position. 我在申请管理职位之前进行了彻底的自我面试。|近义词：self-assessment, introspection, self-reflection | 反义词：N/A
