humored:/ˈhjuːmərd/ | adj. | 幽默的；心情好的 | Good-humored means cheerful and pleasant.  It describes someone who is generally happy and friendly.  The word often implies a tendency towards humor and amusement. | Example:  He was in a good-humored mood after receiving the good news. 他在收到好消息后心情很好。  She delivered the bad news with good humor. 她以幽默的方式传达了坏消息。|近义词：cheerful, jovial, merry, pleasant | 反义词：irritable, grumpy, ill-tempered, sullen | 形容词
