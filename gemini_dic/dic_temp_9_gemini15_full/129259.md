nodded off:/ˈnɒdɪd ɒf/ | v. | 打瞌睡，睡着 | To fall asleep unintentionally, especially while sitting up.  | Example:  I nodded off during the lecture. 我在讲座上打瞌睡了。 |近义词:dozed off, fell asleep, drifted off | 反义词:stayed awake, remained alert

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nods off during long meetings. 他在冗长的会议上打瞌睡。
She often nods off in front of the TV. 她经常在电视机前打瞌睡。

一般过去时:
I nodded off while reading. 我看书时睡着了。
He nodded off in the car on the way home. 他在回家的路上在车里睡着了。

现在完成时:
I've nodded off several times this week. 我这周已经好几次打瞌睡了。
She's nodded off during every meeting this month.  这个月她每次会议都打瞌睡了。

过去完成时:
I had nodded off before the movie ended. 电影结束前我已经睡着了。
They had nodded off before the speaker finished. 演讲者结束前他们已经睡着了。

将来时:
I think I'll nod off if I don't get some coffee.  如果我不喝点咖啡，我想我会睡着的。
He'll probably nod off during the flight. 他在飞行途中可能会睡着。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't had so much coffee, I would have nodded off. 如果我没有喝那么多咖啡，我就会睡着了。
I wish I hadn't nodded off during the important part of the presentation. 我真希望我没有在演示的重要部分睡着。
