quasi-refused:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ rɪˈfjuːzd/| adj.|几乎拒绝，似是而非地拒绝|The proposal was quasi-refused, meaning it wasn't explicitly rejected but neither was it wholeheartedly accepted.  提案几乎被拒绝了，这意味着它没有被明确拒绝，但也没有被完全接受。|近义词：nearly refused, almost rejected, virtually refused|反义词： wholeheartedly accepted, enthusiastically embraced|
