handing-in:/ˈhændɪŋ ɪn/| v.|提交，递交|The act of giving something to someone in authority or in charge.  This usually implies a formal submission, such as handing in homework or a resignation.|例句：I'm handing in my resignation tomorrow. = 我明天要提交辞呈。

例句：The students are handing in their essays today. = 学生们今天要交他们的论文。


近义词：submitting, delivering, presenting, surrendering

反义词：receiving, taking, withdrawing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hands in his homework every week. = 他每周都交作业。
She hands in her report to the manager. = 她把报告交给经理。


一般过去时:
I handed in my application last week. = 我上周提交了我的申请。
He handed in his keys before leaving. = 他离开前交了钥匙。


现在完成时:
She has handed in all her assignments. = 她已经提交了所有作业。
They have handed in their proposals on time. = 他们按时提交了他们的提案。


过去完成时:
I had handed in my essay before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经交了论文。
She had handed in her resignation before the meeting. = 她在会议前已经提交了辞呈。


将来时:
I will hand in my project next Friday. = 我下周五将提交我的项目。
We will hand in our forms tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将提交我们的表格。


虚拟语气:
If I had handed in my assignment earlier, I wouldn't have failed. = 如果我早点交作业，我就不会不及格了。
I wish I had handed in my application sooner. = 我希望我早点提交申请。


被动语态:
The assignment was handed in late. = 作业迟交了。
The report must be handed in by Friday. = 报告必须在周五前提交。


疑问句:
Have you handed in your homework yet? = 你交作业了吗？
When will the report be handed in? = 报告什么时候提交？


否定句:
I didn't hand in my essay on time. = 我没有按时交论文。
He hasn't handed in his application yet. = 他还没有提交申请。


PHRASE->
hand in one's notice = 递交辞呈
hand in a report = 提交报告
hand in an assignment = 交作业
hand in one's work = 交作品
hand in the answer sheet = 交答卷
