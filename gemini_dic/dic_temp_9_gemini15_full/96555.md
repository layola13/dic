indorsement:/ɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 背书；认可；赞同 | The act of signing the back of a check or other negotiable instrument to transfer ownership or to guarantee payment.  It can also refer to an expression of approval or support. | Example: The bank requires your indorsement on the check before it can be cashed.  银行要求你在支票上背书才能兑换现金。

The athlete received many endorsements from major brands.  这位运动员获得了许多大品牌的代言。 |近义词：approval, sanction, ratification, support | 反义词：rejection, disapproval, condemnation |  名词
