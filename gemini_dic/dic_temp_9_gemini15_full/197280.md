sharply:/ˈʃɑːrpli/ | adv. | 锐利地；突然地；严厉地；清晰地 |  Sharply means in a sudden and noticeable way, often implying intensity or precision. It can describe a physical action, a change, or a manner of speaking. | 例句：The wind shifted sharply to the north.  = 风突然转向北方。


The teacher sharply criticized his student's work. = 老师严厉批评了学生的作业。


近义词：abruptly, suddenly, severely, distinctly, clearly | 反义词：gradually, gently, mildly, vaguely, unclearly
