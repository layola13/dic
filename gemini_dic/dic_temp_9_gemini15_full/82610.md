guarish:/ˈɡɛəriʃ/| adj.|俗丽的；过于鲜艳的；花哨的| excessively bright or showy in a tasteless way.  The decor was garish and clashed horribly with the antique furniture.  |近义词：gaudy, flashy, loud, flamboyant|反义词：subtle, understated, muted, tasteful


例句：

The clown's costume was garish, with bright pink and orange stripes.  = 小丑的服装很俗丽，有着亮粉色和橘色的条纹。

Her makeup was too garish for a job interview. = 她的妆容对于求职面试来说过于艳丽了。
