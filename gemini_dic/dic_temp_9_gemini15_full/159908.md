polygon:/ˈpɒlɪɡɒn/ /ˈpɑːlɪɡɑːn/| n. |多边形|A polygon is a closed two-dimensional figure that is made up of straight line segments.  It has three or more sides.  Examples include triangles, squares, pentagons, and hexagons. The name indicates the number of sides and angles; for example, a polygon with five sides is called a pentagon.|The architect designed a building with a unique polygon-shaped roof. 建筑师设计了一座屋顶形状独特的建筑物。|
