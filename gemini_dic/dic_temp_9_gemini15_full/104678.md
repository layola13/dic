kart:/kɑːrt/| n.|小型四轮车，卡丁车|A small, open, four-wheeled vehicle, typically for racing.  |例句：He loves racing his kart on the track. 他喜欢在赛道上驾驶他的卡丁车。|例句：The children enjoyed riding in the go-kart. 孩子们喜欢乘坐卡丁车。|近义词：go-kart,  buggy|反义词：None.
