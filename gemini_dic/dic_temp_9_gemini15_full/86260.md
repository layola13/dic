heavenly:/ˈhɛvənli/ | adj. | 天上的；天堂的；极好的；美好的 |  Of or relating to heaven; divinely beautiful or blissful.  | 例句：The heavenly choir sang beautifully. 天上的唱诗班唱得优美动听。  The sunset was a heavenly sight. 日落景象美极了。 | 近义词：divine, celestial, angelic, delightful, superb | 反义词：hellish, infernal, earthly, terrible
