reaccumulation:/ˌriːəkjuːmjuˈleɪʃən/| n. |重新积累，再次堆积|The process of accumulating something again after it has been dispersed or lost.  It often implies a gathering or collection of something that was previously present but then diminished or removed. |例句：The reaccumulation of snow after the blizzard made travel impossible.暴风雪过后积雪再次堆积，使得交通瘫痪。

例句：The reaccumulation of capital after the economic downturn took several years.经济衰退后资本的重新积累花费了几年时间。|近义词：recollection, reacquisition, regeneration|反义词：dispersion, dissipation, depletion|词性：名词
