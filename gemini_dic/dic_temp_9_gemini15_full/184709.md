resubmit:/ˌriːsəbˈmɪt/| vt.|重新提交，再次提交|To submit (something) again, typically after it has been rejected or found to be unsatisfactory.|例句：The professor asked me to resubmit my paper with the corrections. 教授让我修改后重新提交我的论文。


例句：The application was rejected, so we had to resubmit it with the missing information.  申请被拒绝了，所以我们不得不补充完整信息后重新提交。


近义词：re-present, submit again, refile


反义词：withdraw, reject


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They resubmit their work every week. = 他们每周重新提交他们的工作。
He resubmits the form when there is a mistake. = 当有错误时，他重新提交表格。


一般过去时:
She resubmitted the proposal after making revisions. = 她修改后重新提交了提案。
They resubmitted their application because it was incomplete. = 他们因为申请不完整而重新提交了申请。


现在完成时:
I have resubmitted my application three times. = 我已经三次重新提交我的申请了。
She has resubmitted the report with the necessary changes. = 她已经用必要的修改重新提交了报告。


过去完成时:
He had resubmitted the assignment before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经重新提交了作业。
They had resubmitted their papers multiple times before receiving approval. = 在获得批准之前，他们已经多次重新提交了论文。


将来时:
We will resubmit the document once the corrections are made. = 一旦修改完成，我们将重新提交文件。
He will resubmit his application next week. = 他下周将重新提交他的申请。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would resubmit my application. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新提交我的申请。
If I were you, I would resubmit the report with the updates. = 如果我是你，我会用更新后的内容重新提交报告。


被动语态:
The application was resubmitted after the corrections were made. = 修改完成后，申请被重新提交。
The proposal was resubmitted by the team after careful review. = 经过仔细审查后，提案由团队重新提交。


疑问句:
Will you resubmit the report today? = 你今天会重新提交报告吗？
Should we resubmit the application? = 我们应该重新提交申请吗？


否定句:
They did not resubmit the documents on time. = 他们没有按时重新提交文件。
I will not resubmit the assignment unless I have made all the corrections. = 除非我做了所有的修改，否则我不会重新提交作业。


PHRASE->
resubmit a document = 重新提交文件
resubmit an application = 重新提交申请
resubmit a proposal = 重新提交提案
resubmit a report = 重新提交报告
