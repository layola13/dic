hydrosome:/ˈhaɪdrəˌsoʊm/| n. |水体；水生生物群落| A hydrosome is a body of water, often referring to a specific aquatic ecosystem or community.  It can also refer to a hypothetical, self-contained aquatic ecosystem. The term is less common and often used in specialized contexts like theoretical ecology or certain types of scientific research.| Example: The research team studied the hydrosome's unique biodiversity.  研究团队研究了该水体的独特生物多样性。  The proposed space habitat included a self-sustaining hydrosome. 提议的太空栖息地包括一个自给自足的水生生态系统。|近义词：aquatic ecosystem, water body|反义词：terrestrial ecosystem
