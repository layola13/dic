hangs:/hæŋz/ | v. |悬挂；绞死；(使)悬而未决|To be suspended or to suspend something.|The picture hangs on the wall. 图片挂在墙上。The criminal was hanged for murder. 罪犯因谋杀被绞死。The decision hangs in the balance. 决定悬而未决。|dangles, suspends| stands, supports|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lamp hangs from the ceiling. = 灯从天花板上垂下来。
The fruit hangs heavy on the branches. = 果实沉甸甸地挂在树枝上。

一般过去时:
He hanged the painting in the hallway. = 他把画挂在走廊里。
She hung her coat on the hook. = 她把外套挂在钩子上。

现在完成时:
The painting has hung in this room for ten years. = 这幅画在这间屋子里挂了十年了。
They have hung the decorations for the party. = 他们已经挂好了派对的装饰品。

过去完成时:
The thief had hung the stolen goods before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经挂起了偷来的赃物。
She had hung the laundry out to dry before the rain started. = 下雨之前，她已经把洗好的衣服晾出去了。


将来时:
I will hang the new curtains tomorrow. = 我明天会挂上新的窗帘。
The artist will hang his latest work in the gallery. = 艺术家将在画廊展出他的最新作品。


虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger wall, I would hang more pictures. = 如果我有一面更大的墙，我会挂更多的画。
I wish I could hang that beautiful tapestry. = 我希望我能挂上那幅美丽的挂毯。


被动语态:
The clothes were hung carefully on the line. = 衣服被小心地挂在绳子上。
The artwork was hung in the most prominent place. = 艺术品被挂在了最显眼的地方。


疑问句:
Where does the bell hang? = 铃铛挂在哪里？
How is the painting hung? = 这幅画是怎么挂起来的？


否定句:
The picture doesn't hang straight. = 图片挂得不直。
He didn't hang the poster properly. = 他没有把海报正确地挂好。


PHRASE->

hang in there:坚持下去
hang on:抓紧；坚持
hang out:出去玩;消磨时间
