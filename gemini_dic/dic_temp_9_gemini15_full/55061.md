disrecommend:/ˌdɪsrɛkəˈmɛnd/| vt.|不推荐，劝阻|To advise against something; to express disapproval of something.|例句：I disrecommend this restaurant; the food is terrible.  我不推荐这家餐馆；食物很糟糕。|例句：The doctor disrecommended the surgery due to the patient's age. 由于病人的年龄，医生不建议进行手术。|近义词：advise against, dissuade, discourage|反义词：recommend, endorse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I disrecommend that course; it's too difficult. = 我不推荐这门课程；太难了。
They disrecommend smoking to everyone. = 他们劝告所有人不要吸烟。


一般过去时:
He disrecommended the investment due to market instability. = 由于市场不稳定，他不建议进行投资。
The committee disrecommended the proposal after careful consideration. = 委员会经过仔细考虑后否决了该提案。


现在完成时:
I have disrecommended that approach several times. = 我已经多次不建议采用这种方法了。
She has disrecommended him for the position. = 她已经不推荐他担任这个职位了。


过去完成时:
They had disrecommended the plan before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始，他们就已经否决了这个计划。
The professor had disrecommended the book due to its inaccuracies. = 教授由于这本书的不准确而没有推荐它。


将来时:
I will disrecommend this product to my friends. = 我将不推荐这款产品给我的朋友们。
The expert will disrecommend the use of that chemical. = 专家将不建议使用这种化学物质。


虚拟语气:
I would disrecommend going there at night. = 我建议晚上不要去那里。
If I were you, I would disrecommend such a risky investment. = 如果我是你，我不会建议进行如此冒险的投资。


被动语态:
This approach was disrecommended by the experts. = 这种方法被专家否决了。
The project was disrecommended due to budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，该项目被否决了。


疑问句:
Would you disrecommend this medicine to a child? = 你会不建议给孩子服用这种药吗？
Does the doctor disrecommend strenuous activity? = 医生是否建议避免剧烈运动？


否定句:
I don't disrecommend the book; it's actually quite good. = 我并没有不推荐这本书；它实际上相当不错。
They didn't disrecommend the candidate despite the controversy. = 尽管存在争议，他们并没有不推荐这位候选人。


PHRASE->
disrecommend strongly = 强烈建议不要
disrecommend against = 反对
disrecommend the use of = 不建议使用
disrecommend on grounds of = 因为...而不推荐
disrecommend for consideration = 不建议考虑
