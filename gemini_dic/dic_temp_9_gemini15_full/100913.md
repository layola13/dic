intune:/ɪnˈtjuːn/ | adj. | 协调的；和谐的；合拍的 | In tune means in agreement or harmony;  functioning well together;  correctly adjusted or pitched. | The orchestra was perfectly in tune.  乐队演奏非常合拍。 The two companies are in tune with each other's goals. 这两家公司目标一致。|近义词：harmonious, accordant, synchronized |反义词：discordant, out of tune, disharmonious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The instruments are in tune. = 乐器调好了。
The singers are in tune with each other. = 歌手们彼此合拍。


一般过去时:
The piano was in tune after the tuning. = 钢琴调音后音准了。
The band was perfectly in tune during the concert. = 乐队在音乐会期间演奏非常合拍。


现在完成时:
The choir has been in tune all evening. = 唱诗班整个晚上都非常合拍。
They have finally gotten their processes in tune. = 他们终于使他们的流程协调一致了。


过去完成时:
The orchestra had been in tune for the previous performance. = 乐队在之前的演出中一直很合拍。
The gears had been in tune before the malfunction. = 齿轮在故障前一直运转良好。


将来时:
The instruments will be in tune for the performance. = 乐器将为演出做好调音。
The team will be in tune with the new strategy by next week. = 团队下周将与新战略保持一致。


虚拟语气:
If the instruments were in tune, the concert would sound better. = 如果乐器调好了，音乐会听起来会更好。
I wish my team were more in tune with the company's vision. = 我希望我的团队更能与公司的愿景保持一致。


被动语态:
The instruments were carefully tuned to be in tune. = 乐器被仔细调音以达到协调。
The company's strategies are being adjusted to be in tune with market trends. = 公司的战略正在调整以适应市场趋势。


疑问句:
Is the guitar in tune? = 吉他调好了吗？
Are the two departments in tune with each other's objectives? = 两个部门的目标一致吗？


否定句:
The orchestra was not in tune during the rehearsal. = 乐队在排练时没有合拍。
The management team was not in tune with the employees' concerns. = 管理团队没有注意到员工的担忧。


PHRASE->
in tune with = 与...协调一致
get in tune = 协调一致
out of tune = 走调的，不协调的
stay in tune = 保持协调
