lopper:/ˈlɒpə(r)/| n. v.|大枝剪, 修枝剪；砍伐树枝的人|A lopper is a tool used for pruning branches, typically with long handles for leverage.  It can also refer to a person who cuts branches.|例句：He used a lopper to trim the overgrown branches. 他用大枝剪修剪了过度生长的树枝。

The gardener is a skilled lopper, able to shape trees with precision. 园丁是一位熟练的修枝工人，能够精确地修剪树木。|近义词：pruner, shears, secateurs|反义词：planter (反义词较为宽泛，指种植者，与修剪动作相对)|名词, 动词
