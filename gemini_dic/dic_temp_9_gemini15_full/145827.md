overdeliberated:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪd/| adj.|过度考虑的；反复斟酌的|Excessively considered or deliberated upon; excessively careful in considering or discussing something.  The plan was overdeliberated and ultimately missed its deadline. 这个计划考虑过度，最终错过了最后期限。|近义词：overthought, overanalyzed, meticulously considered|反义词：hasty, impulsive, rash|
