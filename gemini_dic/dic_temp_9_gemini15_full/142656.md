ostracizer: /ˈɒstrəsaɪzər/ | n. |排斥者，冷落者 | One who ostracizes others; someone who excludes or shuns others from a group.  |例句：He became an ostracizer after his controversial opinions alienated his friends. 他因为有争议的观点疏远了他的朋友，变成了一个排斥者。

例句：The ostracizer's behavior created a hostile environment in the workplace. 排斥者的行为在工作场所营造了敌对的环境。


近义词：shunner, outcast, rejector, excluder


反义词：welcomer, includer, accepter


词性: Noun
