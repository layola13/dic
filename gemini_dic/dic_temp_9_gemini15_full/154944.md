pesters:/ˈpɛstərz/ | v. | 纠缠；烦扰；打扰 | To annoy someone persistently.  |例句：The children pestered their mother for sweets. 孩子们缠着妈妈要糖果。|例句：He kept pestering me with questions. 他不停地问我问题。|近义词：annoy, bother, harass, nag | 反义词：soothe, appease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He constantly pesters his sister. = 他不断地烦他妹妹。
She pesters her parents for a new phone. = 她缠着父母要一部新手机。


一般过去时:
The salesman pestered me all afternoon. = 销售员整个下午都在烦我。
They pestered their teacher with questions. = 他们不停地问老师问题。


现在完成时:
He has pestered me for weeks about this. = 关于这件事他已经烦了我好几个星期了。
She has pestered him until he finally agreed. = 她一直缠着他，直到他最终同意。


过去完成时:
I had pestered him for days before he finally gave in. = 我缠了他好几天他才最终让步。
She had pestered her boss for a raise for months. = 她几个月来一直缠着老板要求加薪。


将来时:
I know he will pester me about it again tomorrow. = 我知道他明天还会再来烦我这件事。
She will probably pester him until he gives her what she wants. = 她可能会一直缠着他，直到他给她她想要的东西。


虚拟语气:
If he pestered me any more, I would get angry. = 如果他再烦我，我会生气的。
I wish he wouldn't pester me so much. = 我希望他不要再这样烦我了。


被动语态:
He was pestered by the reporters all day. = 他一整天都被记者纠缠。
She was pestered into agreeing to the deal. = 她被缠着同意了这笔交易。


疑问句:
Is he pestering you again? = 他又在烦你吗？
Are they pestering you about the project? = 他们关于那个项目一直在烦你吗？


否定句:
He doesn't pester me anymore. = 他不再烦我了。
She didn't pester him about it this time. = 这次她没有为此烦他。


PHRASE->
pester someone relentlessly = 不停地纠缠某人
pester someone for something = 缠着某人要某物
pester someone into doing something = 缠着某人做某事
pester someone with questions = 不停地问某人问题
pester someone about something = 为某事烦扰某人
