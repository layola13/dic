orthoscopic:/ɔːrθəˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. | 正视的；正像的；正射的 |  Relating to or employing an orthoscope; producing a non-distorted image.  Orthoscopic lenses or instruments provide a true, undistorted view, free from perspective distortion. | Example: The orthoscopic lens on the microscope provided a clear, undistorted view of the specimen. 正视显微镜上的透镜提供了标本清晰、无失真的视野。 |近义词： undistorted, true-to-life | 反义词： distorted, skewed | 词性：形容词
