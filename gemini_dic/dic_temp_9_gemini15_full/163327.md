preconfirmed:/ˌpriːkənˈfɜːrmd/| adj.| 预先确认的，已预先确认的 |  Already confirmed in advance; previously confirmed.  This adjective describes something that has been confirmed before a particular time or event. | 例句：The preconfirmed reservations guarantee your spot on the tour.  预先确认的预订保证你在旅行中的位置。


The flight was preconfirmed, so we didn't need to worry about it. 航班已预先确认，所以我们不必担心。


近义词：confirmed, assured, guaranteed | 反义词：unconfirmed, unverified

词源:
The word "preconfirmed" is a combination of the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before" or "in advance") and the verb "confirm" (meaning "to verify or establish the truth or certainty of").  Therefore, "preconfirmed" means "confirmed beforehand".
