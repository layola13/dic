overeagerness:/ˌoʊvərˈiːɡərnəs/ | n. | 过度热切；急切 | Excessive eagerness or enthusiasm.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the eagerness is excessive or inappropriate. |例句：Her overeagerness to please sometimes led to mistakes.  她的过度讨好有时会导致错误。

例句：His overeagerness in the interview backfired, making him seem desperate. 他在面试中的过度热切适得其反，让他显得非常渴望得到这份工作。

近义词：excessive enthusiasm, undue zeal, impatience, precipitateness

反义词：apathy, indifference, calmness, composure
