despises:/dɪˈspaɪzɪz/ | vt. |鄙视；蔑视；厌恶 |To regard with contempt; look down on; scorn.  |例句：He despises liars and cheats. 他鄙视说谎者和骗子。|例句：She despises hypocrisy. 她厌恶虚伪。|近义词：scorn, contemn, disdain |反义词：admire, respect, esteem |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He despises her arrogance. = 他鄙视她的傲慢。
They despise dishonest behavior. = 他们鄙视不诚实的行为。

一般过去时:
She despised his cruelty. = 她鄙视他的残忍。
They despised the dictator's regime. = 他们鄙视独裁者的政权。

现在完成时:
I have despised his actions since the incident. = 从那件事起，我就一直鄙视他的行为。
We have despised their lies for years. = 多年来，我们一直鄙视他们的谎言。

过去完成时:
She had despised him long before they met. = 在他们相遇很久之前，她就鄙视他了。
They had despised his methods for a long time. = 他们早就鄙视他的方法了。

将来时:
He will despise her betrayal. = 他将鄙视她的背叛。
They will despise any attempt to cheat. = 他们将鄙视任何作弊的企图。

虚拟语气:
If I knew he was such a person, I would despise him. = 如果我知道他就是这样的人，我会鄙视他。
I wish I had not despised him so much. = 我真希望我当时没有那么鄙视他。

被动语态:
His actions are despised by many. = 他的行为受到许多人的鄙视。
His ideas were despised by the establishment. = 他的想法受到当权者的鄙视。

疑问句:
Does he despise her? = 他鄙视她吗？
Do they despise his work? = 他们鄙视他的作品吗？

否定句:
I don't despise him, although I disagree with him. = 虽然我不同意他的观点，但我并不鄙视他。
She doesn't despise everyone who is different. = 她并不鄙视所有与众不同的人。
