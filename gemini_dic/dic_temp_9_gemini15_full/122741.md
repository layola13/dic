moisteners:/ˈmɔɪsənərz/ | n. | 加湿器；湿润剂 | Substances or devices that add moisture.  Moisteners are used to increase the humidity in the air or to add moisture to a substance.  | 例句：The air in the room was dry, so we used a humidifier as a moisener.  房间里的空气很干燥，所以我们用加湿器来加湿。

The dry skin needed a moisener to become soft again. 干燥的皮肤需要润肤剂才能恢复柔软。| 近义词：humidifiers, moisturizers, humectants | 反义词：dehydrators, desiccants | 名词
