carried-forward: /ˈkæriːd ˈfɔːrwərd/ | adj. |结转的；递延的|Transferred to the next accounting period.  This term is primarily used in accounting and finance to describe amounts, balances, or losses that are not settled in the current period and are moved to the next. |例句：The company carried forward its losses from last year.  公司将去年的亏损结转到今年。

The uncollected debt was carried forward to the next quarter's accounts.  未收回的债务被结转到下一季度的账目中。|近义词：deferred, transferred, postponed |反义词：settled, closed, resolved |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company carries forward its losses. = 公司结转其亏损。
They carry forward the remaining balance. = 他们结转剩余余额。

一般过去时:
The company carried forward its losses last year. = 公司去年结转了其亏损。
We carried forward the project to the next phase. = 我们将项目推进到下一阶段。

现在完成时:
The company has carried forward its losses for three years. = 公司已经连续三年结转亏损。
They have carried forward the discussion to the next meeting. = 他们已将讨论推迟到下次会议。

过去完成时:
The company had carried forward its losses before the audit. = 公司在审计之前已结转了其亏损。
They had carried forward the project before the funding was secured. = 在资金到位之前，他们已经将项目推进。

将来时:
The company will carry forward its losses to next year. = 公司将把亏损结转到明年。
We will carry forward the research to the next project. = 我们将在下一个项目中继续研究。

虚拟语气:
If the company had more capital, it would not have carried forward its losses. = 如果公司有更多资金，它就不会结转亏损。
If we had more time, we would have carried forward the discussion further. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会更深入地讨论。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
