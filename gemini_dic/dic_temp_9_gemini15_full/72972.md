foolahs:/ˈfuːləz/| n.|傻瓜，蠢人|Fools; foolish people.  The term carries a connotation of being somewhat naive or silly, rather than deeply stupid.|例句：Those foolahs went hiking in a blizzard without proper gear. 那些傻瓜在暴风雪中徒步旅行，却没有合适的装备。


例句：The foolahs believed his outrageous lies.  那些傻瓜相信了他的荒谬谎言。


近义词：idiots, simpletons, buffoons, dolts


反义词：geniuses, sages, intellectuals


词性:名词
