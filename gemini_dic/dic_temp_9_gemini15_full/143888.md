outport:/ˈaʊtpɔːrt/ | n. |外港，偏远港口 |A smaller port, often located away from the main city center.  It might serve a more specific purpose or industry. |Example: The small fishing village had a bustling outport where the daily catch was brought in.  这个小渔村有一个繁忙的外港，每天的渔获都运到这里。|近义词：minor port, subsidiary port |反义词：main port, central port
