roll-ups:/ˈroʊlˌʌps/| n.|卷状物；卷起来的物品|A roll-up is something that is rolled up, often for easy storage or transport.  This can refer to many things, depending on the context.  It might be a map, a poster, a sleeping bag, a piece of fabric, etc.|例句：He carefully rolled up the map and put it in his backpack. 他小心地卷起地图放进背包里。

例句：The artist displayed her paintings as roll-ups, making them easy to transport. 艺术家将她的画作做成卷状，便于运输。


近义词：roll, wrap, scrolls


反义词：unrolled, unfolded


词性：名词
