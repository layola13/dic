fluorine:/ˈflɔːriːn/| n. |氟|A pale yellow, highly reactive, toxic gaseous chemical element; the most reactive of all the elements. It is used in the manufacture of refrigerants, plastics, and uranium hexafluoride.|例句：Fluorine is a highly reactive element and must be handled with extreme care. 氟是一种高反应性元素，必须小心处理。|例句：The compound is used in the production of fluorocarbons for refrigeration. 该化合物用于生产用于制冷的氟碳化合物。|近义词：halogen|反义词：无
