hotfoot:/ˈhɑːtfʊt/| adv. v.|匆忙地，飞快地；急忙赶路|To go or move quickly and hurriedly, often in a state of urgency or excitement.|例句：He hotfooted it back to the office. 他飞快地跑回办公室。  例句：I hotfooted it to the hospital when I heard the news. 我听到这个消息后急忙赶往医院。|近义词：hurriedly, swiftly, rapidly|反义词：slowly, leisurely
