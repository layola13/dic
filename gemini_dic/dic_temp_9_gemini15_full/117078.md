meadowing:/ˈmɛdoʊɪŋ/| n.|草地；牧场；放牧|The act of grazing livestock in a meadow or the process of a meadow growing.  It can also refer to the state or appearance of a meadow. |例句：The sheep were enjoying the meadowing in the sun-drenched field.  绵羊在阳光普照的田野里享受着放牧的时光。

The picturesque scene of meadowing reminded me of childhood summers.  放牧的优美景色让我想起了童年的夏天。|近义词：pasturing, grazing|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to actions opposite to pastoral scenes, such as urban development or industrialization.
