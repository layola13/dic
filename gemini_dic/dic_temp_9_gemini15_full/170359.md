provisionally:/prəˈvɪʒənəli/| adv.|暂时地，临时地|For the time being, or until something more permanent is arranged.  It suggests that the arrangement is not final and may be subject to change. |例句：The agreement was provisionally accepted, pending further negotiations.  协议暂时被接受，等待进一步谈判。|例句：The bridge is provisionally open, but repairs are still ongoing. 这座桥暂时开放，但维修仍在进行中。|近义词：temporarily, tentatively, conditionally|反义词：permanently, finally, definitively|副词
