psalmographer:/sælˈmɒɡrəfər/ | n. | 诗篇作者，圣诗作者 | A psalmographer is a composer or writer of psalms or sacred songs, particularly those used in religious worship.  | Example: The psalmographer skillfully wove themes of praise and lament into his work.  诗篇作者巧妙地将赞美和哀叹的主题融入他的作品中。 | 近义词：psalmist, hymn writer, composer of sacred music | 反义词：None specifically;  one might consider an author of profane or secular music as a contrast.
