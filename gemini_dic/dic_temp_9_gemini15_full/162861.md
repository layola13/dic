precede:/prɪˈsiːd/| vt.|在…之前；先于；领先|To come before something or someone in time or order.  It implies a chronological or sequential relationship.|例句：The appetizer preceded the main course. 开胃菜在主菜之前上。  The storm preceded the earthquake. 暴风雨先于地震发生。|近义词：antecede, forerun, precede|反义词：follow, succeed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The introduction precedes the main body of the text. = 介绍在正文之前。
The parade precedes the festival. = 游行在节日之前举行。


一般过去时:
The announcement preceded the launch of the new product. = 新产品的发布之前有公告。
A period of silence preceded the explosion. = 爆炸之前有一段时间的沉默。


现在完成时:
The rain has preceded the storm. = 雨水已经先于暴风雨到来。
Many difficulties have preceded this success. = 许多困难先于这项成功。


过去完成时:
The preparations had preceded the arrival of the guests. = 来宾到达之前已经完成了准备工作。
He had preceded me in applying for the job. = 他在我之前申请了这份工作。


将来时:
The meeting will precede the dinner. = 会议将在晚餐之前举行。
A short speech will precede the presentation. = 一个简短的演讲将在演示之前进行。


虚拟语气:
If the meeting preceded the dinner, we would have more time to chat. = 如果会议在晚餐之前举行，我们将有更多时间聊天。
I wish the rain had preceded the sunny weather. = 我希望雨水曾在晴天之前。


被动语态:
The speech was preceded by a moment of silence. = 演讲之前有一段时间的沉默。
The concert was preceded by an opening act. = 音乐会之前有一个开场表演。


疑问句:
What preceded the accident? = 事故之前发生了什么？
Does the introduction precede the chapter summary? = 介绍在章节总结之前吗？


否定句:
The dessert did not precede the main course. = 甜点不在主菜之前。
The game did not precede the practice. = 比赛不在练习之前。


PHRASE->
precede in time = 按时间顺序排列
precede in importance = 在重要性上领先
precede in rank = 在级别上领先
precede in order = 按顺序排列
to be preceded by = 在...之前
