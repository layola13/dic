secure:/sɪˈkjʊr/ | adj., v. | 安全的；安全的；牢固的；可靠的；获得；保护 |  To protect from harm or loss; to obtain or achieve something desired. |例句：The building is secure against intruders. 这栋建筑物防范入侵者很安全。


The company secured a large contract. 这家公司获得了一份大合同。|近义词：safe, protected, obtain, acquire |反义词：unsafe, vulnerable, insecure, lose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He secures the door every night. 他每晚都锁门。
They secure funding for their project. 他们为他们的项目争取资金。

一般过去时:
She secured a loan from the bank. 她从银行获得了一笔贷款。
He secured the package with rope. 他用绳子捆扎包裹。

现在完成时:
We have secured a place to stay. 我们已经找到住处了。
They have secured a major victory. 他们已经取得了一场重大胜利。

过去完成时:
She had secured a job before graduation. 她在毕业前就找到了一份工作。
He had secured the documents in a safe. 他已将文件锁在保险箱里。

将来时:
We will secure the perimeter before nightfall. 我们将在夜幕降临前确保周边安全。
They will secure the necessary permits. 他们将获得必要的许可证。

虚拟语气:
If we secured more funding, we could expand our business. 如果我们能获得更多资金，我们就能扩大业务。
I wish I had secured a better job offer. 我希望我得到更好的工作机会。

被动语态:
The building was secured by guards. 这栋建筑物由警卫守护。
The contract was secured after lengthy negotiations. 合同在经过长时间谈判后才签订。

疑问句:
Is the area secure? 这个区域安全吗？
Have they secured the necessary resources? 他们已经获得必要的资源了吗？

否定句:
He couldn't secure a meeting with the CEO. 他无法与CEO会面。
They failed to secure enough votes to win the election. 他们未能获得足够的选票赢得选举。


PHRASE->
secure a job  获得一份工作
secure a loan 获得贷款
secure a victory 取得胜利
secure funding 获得资金
secure a contract 获得合同

词源:
源自古法语 *secur,  最终源自拉丁语 *secūrus,  意为“没有担忧的, 安心的”。
