reversionally:/rɪˈvɜːʒənəli/| adv.|  反向地；回复地；逆转地 |  In a manner that relates to or involves reversion; in a way that reverts to a previous state or condition.  |例句：The property was held reversionally, meaning it would revert to the original owner upon the death of the current tenant.  该房产以返还的方式持有，这意味着它将在现有租户去世后返还给原业主。


例句：The system reversionally returned to its default settings after the error. 系统在错误后反向地恢复到默认设置。


近义词：conversely, inversely, reciprocally


反义词：progressively, forwardly


词性：副词
