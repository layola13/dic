crewmates:/ˈkruːmeɪts/| n. |船员；同事；组员|A group of people working together, especially on a ship or spacecraft.  It can also refer to a team or group of colleagues.|例句：The crewmates worked together to navigate the stormy seas. 船员们齐心协力，在暴风雨中航行。

例句：My crewmates and I celebrated the successful completion of our project. 我的同事们和我一起庆祝项目成功完成。


近义词：teammates, colleagues, companions


反义词：None specifically, but words like "rivals" or "opponents" could be considered antonyms in a competitive context.


词性：名词
