overenthusiastic:/ˌoʊvərɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/| adj. |过分热情的，过于热心的| excessively enthusiastic; showing too much enthusiasm.  |例句：His overenthusiastic approach alienated some of his colleagues.  他的过分热情的态度疏远了一些同事。
例句：The overenthusiastic fans rushed the stage after the concert.  演唱会结束后，过分热情的粉丝冲上了舞台。|近义词：exuberant, zealous, fervent, passionate|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic, lukewarm |词性：形容词
