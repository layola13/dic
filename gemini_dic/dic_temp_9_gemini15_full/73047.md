footlicker: /ˈfʊtlɪkər/ | n. | 谄媚者，拍马屁的人 | A person who shows excessive flattery or subservience to someone in a position of power or authority.  They are often characterized by their willingness to do anything to please the person they are flattering, even if it means compromising their own values or principles. | Example:  He was considered a footlicker for always agreeing with the boss, even when the boss was clearly wrong. 他总是附和老板，即使老板明显错了，也被认为是谄媚者。 | 近义词：flatterer, sycophant, yes-man, toady | 反义词：rebel, dissenter, independent thinker | 名词
