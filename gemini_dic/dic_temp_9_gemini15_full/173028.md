quarrelling: /ˈkwɒrəlɪŋ/ /ˈkwɔːrəlɪŋ/ | v. | 争吵，吵架 | The act of engaging in an argument or disagreement.  It often implies a heated or prolonged dispute. | 例句：The children were quarrelling over a toy. = 孩子们为了一个玩具而争吵。


The neighbours were constantly quarrelling about noise. = 邻居们不断因为噪音而争吵。| 近义词：arguing, bickering, disputing, wrangling | 反义词：agreeing, concurring, harmonizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are quarrelling loudly. = 他们在大声争吵。
We are constantly quarrelling about money. = 我们一直在为钱争吵。

一般过去时:
They quarrelled fiercely yesterday. = 他们昨天激烈地争吵了。
We quarrelled about the best way to proceed. = 我们就最佳方案发生了争吵。

现在完成时:
They have quarrelled several times this week. = 他们本周已经争吵了好几次了。
We have quarrelled over this issue for years. = 我们就这个问题争吵了好几年了。

过去完成时:
They had quarrelled before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们就已经争吵了。
We had quarrelled many times before finally resolving the issue. = 在最终解决这个问题之前，我们已经争吵过很多次了。

将来时:
They will quarrel again, I'm sure. = 我确信他们还会再吵。
We will likely quarrel if we don't find a compromise. = 如果我们找不到妥协方案，我们很可能会争吵。

虚拟语气:
If they quarrelled less, they would be happier. = 如果他们少争吵一些，他们会更快乐。
I wish they wouldn't quarrel so much. = 我希望他们不要吵那么多架。

被动语态:
The matter was being quarrelled over. = 这件事正在被争论。
The issue has been quarrelled over for years. =这个问题已经被争论多年了。

疑问句:
Are they quarrelling again? = 他们又在吵架了吗？
Were they quarrelling when you arrived? = 你到的时候他们在吵架吗？

否定句:
They didn't quarrel at all. = 他们根本没有吵架。
We haven't quarrelled recently. = 我们最近没有吵架。


PHRASE->
quarrel with someone = 与某人争吵

a bitter quarrel = 一场激烈的争吵
a family quarrel = 家庭纠纷


词源:
The word "quarrelling" comes from the Old French word "quereller," meaning "to complain," "to grumble," or "to quarrel."  This ultimately derives from the Vulgar Latin word "querela," meaning "complaint" or "cause of complaint."
