obliques:/oʊˈbliːks/| adj. n.|倾斜的；斜线；斜肌|Slanted or inclined from a vertical or horizontal line.  The term can refer to a line, a surface, or an object.  As a noun, it often refers to diagonal lines or muscles. |例句：The oblique lines in the painting create a sense of movement.  绘画中的斜线营造出动感。

The building has an oblique roof. 这栋建筑有一个倾斜的屋顶。


近义词：slanting, sloping, inclined, diagonal|反义词：vertical, horizontal, perpendicular|词性：adj. n.


词源:  From Latin *obliquus ("oblique, sideways"), from *ob- ("toward, against, near") + *liquare ("to be bent").
