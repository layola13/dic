disintegrator:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtər/| n. |崩解器；解体器|A device or process that causes something to break down or disintegrate into smaller parts.  It can refer to machines that pulverize materials, or processes that break down substances at a molecular level.|例句：The disintegrator pulverized the waste into fine particles.  崩解器将废物粉碎成细小的颗粒。

The nuclear disintegrator was used to break down the radioactive material. 核崩解器被用来分解放射性物质。|近义词：pulverizer, destroyer, decomposer |反义词：integrator, assembler, constructor
