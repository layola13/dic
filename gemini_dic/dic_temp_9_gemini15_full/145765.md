/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊrz/ | v. |过度治疗；矫枉过正|To treat (a disease or condition) to an excessive degree, often resulting in unintended negative consequences.  This often refers to medical treatment, but can also apply to other situations.|例句：The doctor overcured the patient, leading to further complications.  医生过度治疗了病人，导致了进一步的并发症。|例句：His attempts to improve the situation actually overcured the problem, making things worse. 他试图改善局势的尝试实际上矫枉过正，使情况变得更糟。|近义词：overtreat, overdo, overmedicate|反义词：undertreat, neglect
