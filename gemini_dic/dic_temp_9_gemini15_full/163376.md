preconscious:/ˌpriːˈkɒnʃəs/| adj.|下意识的，潜意识的 | Relating to or denoting the level of consciousness that is not fully conscious but readily available to consciousness; the part of the mind containing thoughts and feelings that can readily be brought into conscious awareness.  |例句：The preconscious mind holds memories and feelings that can easily be recalled.  潜意识储存着容易回忆起来的记忆和情感。

例句：He accessed his preconscious memories to find the answer. 他调动了潜意识里的记忆来寻找答案。


近义词：subconscious, latent, underlying | 反义词：conscious, aware


词性:形容词
