curricle:/ˈkʌrɪkl/| n. |双轮轻便马车|A two-wheeled, usually light, carriage, drawn by one or two horses.|Example:  The curricle, drawn by a pair of spirited ponies, sped down the lane. 这辆由两匹精力充沛的小马拉着的双轮轻便马车，飞快地沿着小路驶去。|近义词：gig, chaise, cart|反义词：carriage (in the sense of a large, heavier carriage)
