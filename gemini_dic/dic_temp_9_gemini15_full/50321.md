deplorate:/dɪˈplɔːreɪt/| vt.| 谴责；对…表示遗憾 | To express strong disapproval of; to regret deeply.  |例句：We deplore the violence in the city. 我们谴责城市里的暴力行为。

例句：I deplore the lack of funding for education. 我对教育经费不足深感遗憾。


近义词：condemn, denounce, regret, lament | 反义词：approve, commend, praise | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deplores the current political situation. = 他谴责当前的政治局势。
She deplores the use of violence to settle disputes. = 她谴责使用暴力来解决纠纷。


一般过去时:
They deplored the decision to close the hospital. = 他们对关闭医院的决定表示遗憾。
The committee deplored the lack of transparency. = 委员会对缺乏透明度表示谴责。


现在完成时:
We have deplored this injustice for years. = 多年来我们一直谴责这种不公正行为。
They have deplored the government's inaction on climate change. = 他们一直谴责政府在气候变化问题上的不作为。


过去完成时:
She had deplored the situation before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始她就对这种情况表示遗憾了。
The public had deplored the company's unethical practices for months. = 公众几个月来一直谴责该公司的违反道德行为。


将来时:
He will deplore the consequences of his actions. = 他将会对自己的行为造成的后果表示遗憾。
We will deplore any attempt to suppress free speech. = 我们将谴责任何试图压制言论自由的企图。


虚拟语气:
I would deplore such a decision if I were in charge. = 如果我负责的话，我会谴责这样的决定。
They would have deplored the outcome had they known the full facts. = 如果他们知道全部事实的话，他们会对结果表示遗憾。


被动语态:
The government's actions were deplored by many. = 许多人谴责政府的行为。
The decision was deplored by environmental groups. = 环境保护组织谴责了这一决定。


疑问句:
Do you deplore the violence? = 你谴责暴力吗？
Does she deplore the lack of opportunities? = 她对缺乏机会感到遗憾吗？


否定句:
He does not deplore the changes. = 他并不谴责这些变化。
She did not deplore the outcome of the election. = 她并不对选举的结果感到遗憾。


PHRASE->
deplore the loss of = 对…的损失表示遗憾
deplore the fact that = 对…这一事实表示遗憾
deplore the situation = 对目前的状况表示遗憾
deplore the lack of = 对…的缺乏表示遗憾
deeply deplore = 深感遗憾


词源:
From Old French *deplorer (to weep, lament), from Latin deplorāre (to weep, bewail), from de- (intensive prefix) + plorāre (to weep).
