deforestation:/ˌdiːˌfɒrɪˈsteɪʃən/| n. |森林砍伐；滥伐森林|The clearing or removal of forests for other land uses.  Deforestation is a significant environmental problem, contributing to climate change and biodiversity loss. |The Amazon rainforest is experiencing alarming rates of deforestation. 亚马逊雨林正面临着惊人的森林砍伐速度。  Deforestation has led to the extinction of many plant and animal species. 森林砍伐导致许多动植物物种灭绝。|reforestation, afforestation|preservation, conservation|名词
