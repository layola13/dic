reconvincing: /ˌriːkənˈvɪnsɪŋ/ | adj. | 再说服；使再次相信的 |  Persuading someone again; making someone believe something once more, especially after they have doubted it.  | The evidence was so strong that it proved reconvincing even to the most skeptical jurors.  证据如此确凿，以至于连最怀疑的陪审员都相信了。 | persuasive, convincing | unconvincing, unpersuasive | 形容词
