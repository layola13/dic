sanctifies:/ˈsæŋktɪfaɪz/| vt.|使神圣；使圣洁；认可；净化|To make or declare holy; to consecrate.  The act of sanctifying something often involves a ritual or ceremony, making it sacred or set apart for religious purposes. It can also refer to the act of purifying or cleansing something, making it morally pure.|例句：The priest sanctified the new church building.  牧师为新建的教堂举行了祝圣仪式。

The experience sanctified her life.  这段经历使她的生活变得神圣。

近义词： consecrate, hallow, bless, purify|反义词： profane, desecrate, defile|动词
