persulphide: /pɜːˈsʌlfaɪd/ | n. | 多硫化物 | A compound containing more than one sulfur atom per molecule, especially one with a chain of sulfur atoms.  This term often refers to inorganic persulfides, but can also encompass organic compounds.  | Example:  Hydrogen persulfide (H2S2) is a highly reactive molecule.  氢多硫化物(H2S2)是一种高反应性分子。  | 近义词: polysulfide | 反义词:  monosulfide
