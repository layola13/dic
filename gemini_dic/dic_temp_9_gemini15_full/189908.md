sampling:/ˈsæmpliŋ/| n. v.|抽样；取样；采样|The process of selecting a subset of a population to make inferences about the entire population.  It's used extensively in statistics, research, and quality control to gather data efficiently and cost-effectively.|例句：The researchers conducted a random sampling of the population to determine the prevalence of the disease.  研究人员对人口进行了随机抽样，以确定这种疾病的患病率。|例句：We need to take a sampling of the soil to test for contamination. 我们需要采集土壤样本以检测污染。|近义词：sampling, example, specimen, selection|反义词：census, totality


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They sample the wine before bottling it. = 他们在装瓶前品尝葡萄酒。
We sample the customer's opinions regularly. = 我们定期收集客户的意见。


一般过去时:
The scientist sampled the water for pollutants. = 科学家采集水样以检测污染物。
The company sampled the opinions of its employees. = 公司调查了员工的意见。


现在完成时:
They have sampled the new product and found it satisfactory. = 他们已经试用了新产品，并认为它令人满意。
The researchers have sampled data from multiple sources. = 研究人员已经从多个来源采集了数据。


过去完成时:

The team had sampled the opinions of experts before making a decision. = 团队在做决定之前已经征求了专家的意见。


将来时:
