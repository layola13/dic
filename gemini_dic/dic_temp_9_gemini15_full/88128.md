hiked:/haɪkt/| v.|徒步旅行；远足|To walk a long distance, especially in the country.  Hiking is a popular outdoor activity.|例句：We hiked through the forest to reach the summit. 我们徒步穿过森林到达山顶。|例句：They hiked for miles to find a water source. 他们走了好几英里去寻找水源。|近义词：trek, ramble, wander|反义词：drive, ride


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hikes every weekend. = 他每个周末都去远足。
She hikes in the mountains. = 她在山里远足。

一般过去时:
We hiked to the lake yesterday. = 我们昨天徒步去了湖边。
They hiked ten miles. = 他们徒步走了十英里。

现在完成时:
I have hiked that trail before. = 我以前走过那条小路。
She has hiked many mountains. = 她爬过许多山。

过去完成时:
They had hiked for hours before they found shelter. = 他们在找到庇护所之前已经徒步走了几个小时。
We had hiked all day and were exhausted. = 我们徒步走了一整天，筋疲力尽了。

将来时:
We will hike in Yosemite next month. = 我们下个月将在约塞米蒂国家公园远足。
They will hike to the top of the mountain. = 他们将徒步登上山顶。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would hike the Appalachian Trail. = 如果我有更多时间，我会徒步走阿巴拉契亚山脉小径。
I wish I could hike the Inca Trail to Machu Picchu. = 我希望我能徒步走印加古道到马丘比丘。

被动语态:
The trail is rarely hiked in the winter. = 冬天很少有人走这条小路。
The mountain was hiked by many people last year. = 去年许多人爬了这座山。

疑问句:
Have you ever hiked in the Alps? = 你曾经在阿尔卑斯山徒步旅行过吗？
Will you hike with us tomorrow? = 你明天会和我们一起远足吗？

否定句:
I didn't hike yesterday. = 我昨天没有远足。
They don't hike often. = 他们不经常远足。


PHRASE->
hike up:  向上攀登
go for a hike: 去远足
hike through: 穿过...远足
backpacking hike: 背包远足
day hike:  一日徒步旅行
