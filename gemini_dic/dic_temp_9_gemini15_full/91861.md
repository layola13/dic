hyperimmunizing:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmjuːnaɪzɪŋ/| v. |超免疫化；高免疫 |The process of inducing a higher than normal level of immunity, often through repeated exposure to an antigen or by administering a concentrated dose of antibodies.|例句：The researchers are exploring new methods for hyperimmunizing individuals against influenza. 研究人员正在探索新的方法来对流感进行超免疫化。|近义词：immunizing, boosting immunity|反义词：immunosuppressing
