kilostere:/ˈkɪləʊsteər/| n. |立方千米，千立方米|A kilostere is a unit of volume equal to one thousand cubic meters (1000 m³).  It's primarily used in forestry and related fields to measure the volume of timber or wood. |The forest yielded 10 kilosteres of lumber this year. 今年森林出产了10千立方米的木材。|cubic kilometer, cubic metre|  (no direct antonym, but smaller units like cubic meter could be considered a relative opposite)|名词
