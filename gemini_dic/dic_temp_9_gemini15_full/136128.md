nonreflecting: /ˌnɒnˈrɪflektɪŋ/ | adj. | 不反射的，无反射的 | Not reflecting light or other radiation.  A nonreflecting surface absorbs or transmits light rather than bouncing it back. |  The  nonreflecting coating on the lens reduced glare significantly.  不反射涂层显著减少了眩光。


近义词：antireflective, transparent (in the context of light)

反义词：reflective, mirror-like

词性：形容词

词源:
The word "nonreflecting" is a compound word formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the present participle "reflecting" (from the verb "to reflect").  The meaning thus directly indicates a lack of the property of reflection.
