liposome:/ˈlaɪpəˌsoʊm/| n. |脂质体|A liposome is a microscopic spherical vesicle consisting of a lipid bilayer enclosing an aqueous core.  They are used in drug delivery and other biomedical applications. |Example: Researchers are investigating the use of liposomes to deliver cancer drugs directly to tumor cells.  研究人员正在研究使用脂质体将抗癌药物直接输送到肿瘤细胞的方法。|近义词：vesicle (更广义)|反义词：无
