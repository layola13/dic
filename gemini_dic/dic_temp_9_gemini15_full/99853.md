intermodification:/ˌɪntərˌmɒdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |相互修饰；交互修改|The process by which two or more things mutually influence and change each other's characteristics or properties.  This often occurs in genetics, linguistics, and other fields where interacting elements affect one another. | Example: The intermodification of genes led to a novel phenotype. 基因的相互修饰导致了一种新的表型。|近义词：mutual modification, reciprocal alteration, interaction|反义词：unilateral change, independent development|名词
