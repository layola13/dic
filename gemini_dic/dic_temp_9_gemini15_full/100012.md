interpenetrating: /ˌɪntərˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |相互渗透的，互相交织的 |  Describes two or more things that are interwoven or permeate each other, occupying the same space simultaneously.  | Example: The two cultures exhibited interpenetrating influences, resulting in a unique hybrid.  两个文化展现出相互渗透的影响，最终形成了独特的混合体。  |近义词:intertwined, interwoven, overlapping, permeating | 反义词:separate, distinct, disjoint | 形容词
