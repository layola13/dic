intercondenser: /ˌɪntərˈkɒndɛnsər/ | n. |中间冷凝器，中间冷凝器|A device used in a refrigeration or air conditioning system to cool and condense refrigerant between two stages of compression.  It improves efficiency by allowing for a two-stage compression process. |Example: The intercondenser helped to significantly improve the efficiency of the industrial refrigeration system.  中间冷凝器帮助显著提高了工业制冷系统的效率。|近义词: secondary condenser, intermediate condenser |反义词: evaporator |
