nourished: /ˈnɜːrɪʃt/ | adj. | 滋养的；营养良好的；被培养的 |  Well-fed; strengthened or stimulated;  carefully developed or fostered.  | 例句：The plants thrived in the nourished soil.  = 这些植物在肥沃的土壤中茁壮成长。

The children were well nourished and healthy. =孩子们营养良好，身体健康。


近义词：fed, nurtured, cultivated, fostered | 反义词：starved, malnourished, neglected


词源:  Comes from the Old French word *nourrir* meaning "to nourish, feed, suckle."  This in turn derives from the Latin *nutrire*, meaning "to nourish, nurture."
