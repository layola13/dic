panning:/ˈpænɪŋ/ | v. | 平移；淘洗；全面检查 | To move a camera slowly across a scene to follow action or show a wide view; to wash gravel or sand in a pan to separate gold or other valuable minerals; to examine something thoroughly. |例句：The cameraman panned across the battlefield.  摄影师将镜头缓缓扫过战场。 |例句：He spent the afternoon panning for gold in the river. 他一下午都在河里淘金。 |近义词：sweep, scan, scrutinize |反义词：zoom, focus
