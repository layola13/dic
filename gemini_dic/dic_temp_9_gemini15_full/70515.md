filer:/ˈfaɪlər/| n. v.|归档者；（电脑）文件；提交；归档|A person or thing that files;  a computer file; to submit (a document, etc.) formally; to arrange documents in order|例句：He is a filer in the law firm. 他是这家律师事务所的档案管理员。|例句：She filed the documents with the court. 她向法院提交了文件。|近义词：submitter, arranger, record-keeper |反义词：disorganizer, scatterer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He files all the documents carefully. = 他仔细地整理所有文件。
She files her taxes every year. = 她每年报税。


一般过去时:
I filed my tax return last week. = 我上周报了税。
They filed a lawsuit against the company. = 他们对该公司提起诉讼。


现在完成时:
He has filed all the necessary paperwork. = 他已经提交了所有必要的文书工作。
She has filed three reports this month. = 这个月她已经提交了三份报告。


过去完成时:
They had filed the appeal before the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前提交了上诉。
She had filed the complaint with the authorities. = 她已向当局投诉。


将来时:
I will file the report tomorrow. = 我明天将提交报告。
He will file for bankruptcy next week. = 他下周将申请破产。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would file a complaint. = 如果我是你，我会投诉。
I wish I had filed the report earlier. = 我希望我早点提交报告。


被动语态:
The documents were filed correctly. = 文件已正确归档。
The lawsuit was filed on Monday. = 诉讼于星期一提起。


疑问句:
Have you filed your taxes yet? = 你报税了吗？
Where did he file the document? = 他把文件存放在哪里？


否定句:
He did not file the report on time. = 他没有按时提交报告。
