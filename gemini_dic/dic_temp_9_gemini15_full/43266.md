crafter:/ˈkræftər/| n. |工匠，手艺人，创造者 |A crafter is a person who makes things by hand, often using traditional methods and materials.  They are skilled in their craft and take pride in their work. |例句：She's a skilled crafter, known for her intricate pottery. 她是一位技艺精湛的工匠，以其复杂的陶器而闻名。|例句：He's a talented crafter who makes beautiful wooden toys. 他是一位有才华的手艺人，制作精美的木制玩具。 |近义词：artisan, artist, maker, constructor |反义词：destroyer, wrecker
