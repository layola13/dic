self-worshipers: /ˈsɛlf ˈwɜːrʃɪpərz/ | n. | 自我崇拜者 | People who excessively admire and praise themselves; narcissists. They are often characterized by egotism, vanity, and a lack of empathy.  | Example: The self-worshipers in the online forum constantly boasted about their achievements, ignoring the contributions of others. 自我崇拜者们在网络论坛上不断吹嘘自己的成就，忽略了别人的贡献。 |近义词: narcissists, egotists, egomaniacs | 反义词: altruists, humble people | 名词
