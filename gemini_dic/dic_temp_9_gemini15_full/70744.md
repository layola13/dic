fine-tuners:/ˈfaɪnˌtuːnərz/| n.|微调器，精调器|People or things that make small adjustments to improve something.|例句：The engineers used sophisticated fine-tuners to calibrate the machine's performance. 工程师们使用复杂的微调器来校准机器的性能。
例句：Machine learning models often require fine-tuners to optimize their accuracy. 机器学习模型通常需要微调器来优化其准确性。|近义词：adjusters, calibrators, optimizers|反义词：粗调器(coarse tuners)|名词
