hydrogenate:/ˈhaɪdrədʒəneɪt/| vt.|使氢化；使饱和|To combine (a substance, especially an unsaturated organic compound) with hydrogen.|The manufacturer hydrogenates vegetable oil to make it solid at room temperature. 制造商将植物油氢化使其在室温下呈固态。|近义词：saturate|反义词：dehydrogenate
