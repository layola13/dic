hell-bent:/ˈhɛlˌbɛnt/ | adj. | 坚决的；一心一意的；执意要…的 | Determined to do something, even if it is dangerous or wrong;  obstinately determined.  |例句：He was hell-bent on revenge. 他一心想要报仇。

He's hell-bent on proving his innocence. 他一心想要证明自己的清白。


近义词：determined, resolute, adamant, single-minded, obsessed


反义词：uncertain, hesitant, irresolute, ambivalent


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are hell-bent on finishing the project. 他们一心想要完成这个项目。
She is hell-bent on getting a promotion. 她一心想要升职。


一般过去时:
He was hell-bent on winning the race. 他一心想要赢得比赛。
They were hell-bent on leaving the country. 他们一心想要离开这个国家。


现在完成时:
She has been hell-bent on this idea for years. 她多年来一直坚持这个想法。
He has been hell-bent on success since he was young. 他从小就一心追求成功。


过去完成时:
They had been hell-bent on their journey, even though the weather was bad. 即使天气不好，他们也执意要继续他们的旅程。
She had been hell-bent on getting married before turning 30.  她一心想在30岁之前结婚。


将来时:
He will be hell-bent on finding the truth. 他将会不遗余力地寻找真相。
They will be hell-bent on achieving their goals. 他们将会一心一意地实现他们的目标。


虚拟语气:
If he were hell-bent on it, he would have succeeded. 如果他一心一意去做，他就会成功。
If she had been hell-bent on finishing the book, she would have stayed up all night. 如果她一心想要完成这本书，她就会熬夜。


被动语态:  (The adjective "hell-bent" is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Is he hell-bent on ruining his life? 他一心想要毁掉自己的人生吗？
Are they hell-bent on causing trouble? 他们一心想要惹事生非吗？


否定句:
He wasn't hell-bent on causing any harm. 他并非一心想要造成任何伤害。
She wasn't hell-bent on going, but she went anyway. 她并非一心想要去，但她还是去了。
