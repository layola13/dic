perturbed:/pərˈtɜːrbd/| adj.|不安的；扰乱的；烦乱的|agitated or disturbed; showing anxiety or nervousness.|The news left him deeply perturbed.  这个消息使他深感不安。|He was perturbed by the unexpected visitor. 他被意外的访客弄得心烦意乱。|disturbed, agitated, upset, anxious|calm, tranquil, peaceful, composed|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is perturbed by the noise. = 他被噪音扰乱了。
She is always perturbed about her future. = 她总是为自己的未来感到不安。


一般过去时:
I was perturbed by her strange behavior. = 我被她奇怪的行为弄得不安。
They were perturbed when they heard the news. = 他们听到这个消息后感到不安。


现在完成时:
I have been perturbed by this problem for days. = 我几天来一直被这个问题困扰。
She has been perturbed by the lack of communication. = 她一直为缺乏沟通而感到不安。


过去完成时:
He had been perturbed by the rumors before the meeting. = 会议之前，他已经为那些谣言感到不安。
We had been perturbed by her silence for a long time. = 我们很久以来一直为她的沉默感到不安。


将来时:
I will be perturbed if they don't show up. = 如果他们不出现，我会感到不安。
She will be perturbed by the results of the test. = 她会为考试的结果感到不安。


虚拟语气:
If I were not perturbed, I would have gone. = 如果我不不安，我就去了。
I wish I were not so perturbed by this situation. = 我希望我不那么担心这种情况。


被动语态:
He was perturbed by the unexpected results. = 他被意想不到的结果弄得心烦意乱。
The audience was perturbed by the actor's poor performance. = 观众对演员糟糕的表演感到不安。


疑问句:
Are you perturbed by the changes? = 你对这些变化感到不安吗？
Is she perturbed about the upcoming exam? = 她担心即将到来的考试吗？


否定句:
He was not perturbed by the criticism. = 他并没有被批评所扰乱。
She was not perturbed by the difficult task. = 她没有被这项艰巨的任务吓倒。
