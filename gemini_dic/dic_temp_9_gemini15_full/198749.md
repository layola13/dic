sick-bed: /ˈsɪkbed/ | n. |病床，病榻 | The state or condition of being confined to one's bed due to illness.  A sickbed is the bed of a person who is ill.  It can also refer to the period of time spent ill in bed. |例句：He was confined to his sickbed for weeks after the surgery. 他手术后卧床数周。

He spent his last days in a sickbed, surrounded by loved ones. 他在病床上度过了最后的日子，周围是亲人。


近义词：sickbed, bed of sickness, bed of affliction | 反义词：无


词性:名词
