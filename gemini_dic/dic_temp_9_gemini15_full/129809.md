non-governmental: /ˌnɒnɡʌvərˈmentəl/| adj. |非政府的|Not run or controlled by a government.  Organizations that are not part of a government and are usually run by private individuals or groups.  They often focus on specific issues or advocate for particular causes.|例句：Many non-governmental organizations (NGOs) work to protect the environment. 许多非政府组织（NGO）致力于保护环境。

例句：The non-governmental sector plays a vital role in development. 非政府部门在发展中发挥着至关重要的作用。


近义词：private, independent, voluntary


反义词：governmental, state-run, public


词性: adjective
