counsels:/ˈkaʊnsəlz/| v. n.|劝告；建议；商议；律师|To give advice to (someone); to advise.|例句：The doctor counsels patients on healthy eating habits.医生建议病人养成健康的饮食习惯。  The lawyer counsels his client on legal matters. 律师就法律事务向他的当事人提供咨询。|近义词：advise, recommend, suggest|反义词：misguide, dissuade|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He counsels his clients wisely. = 他明智地劝告他的客户。
She counsels people facing difficult decisions. = 她为面临艰难抉择的人们提供咨询。

一般过去时:
The therapist counseled the patient about their anxiety. = 治疗师就病人的焦虑症向其进行了咨询。
My father counseled me to finish my education. = 我父亲劝我完成学业。

现在完成时:
She has counseled hundreds of people throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经为数百人提供了咨询。
He has counseled me on several occasions. = 他多次劝告过我。


过去完成时:
The judge had counseled the defendant before sentencing. = 法官在宣判前曾劝告过被告。
She had counseled him to reconsider his decision. = 她曾劝他重新考虑他的决定。

将来时:
The counselor will counsel the student about their college options. = 辅导员将就学生的大学选择问题对其进行指导。
I will counsel you on this matter. = 我将就此事给你提供建议。


虚拟语气:
If I had counseled him earlier, things might have turned out differently. = 如果我早点劝告他，结果可能就不一样了。
I wish I had counseled her to be more cautious. = 我真希望我劝她谨慎一些。


被动语态:
The students were counseled by the teachers on their academic progress. = 学生们就学业进步问题得到了老师的指导。
He was counseled to seek professional help. = 他被建议寻求专业帮助。


疑问句:
Does the school counsel students on personal issues? = 学校是否就个人问题对学生进行指导？
Will you counsel me on this important decision? = 你会就这个重要的决定给我提供建议吗？


否定句:
The judge did not counsel the defendant to plead guilty. = 法官没有劝告被告认罪。
They did not counsel him against taking the risk. = 他们没有劝他不要冒险。
