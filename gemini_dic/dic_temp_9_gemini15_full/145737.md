overcriticizes:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪz/| v. |过度批评，吹毛求疵|To criticize someone or something too harshly or excessively.  It implies finding fault with excessive detail and often unfairly.|The teacher overcriticizes her students' work, discouraging their creativity.|近义词：hypercriticize, faultfind, nitpick |反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overcriticizes every detail of the project. = 他对项目的每一个细节都过度批评。
She overcriticizes her children's drawings. = 她对孩子的画过度批评。


一般过去时:
The manager overcriticized the team's performance last week. = 经理上周过度批评了团队的表现。
He overcriticized my essay, making me feel discouraged. = 他对我的论文吹毛求疵，让我感到灰心。
