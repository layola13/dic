prolapsion:/proʊˈlæpsɪən/| n. |脱垂；内脏下垂|The downward displacement of an organ or tissue from its normal position, often through an opening or weakened area.  This can affect various organs, including the uterus, rectum, bladder, and stomach. Symptoms vary depending on the location and severity of the prolapse.|Example:  She experienced uterine prolapsion after childbirth. 她分娩后出现了子宫脱垂。|近义词：prolapse, descent, hernia|反义词：None specifically, but  "normal positioning" could be considered an antonym.|名词
