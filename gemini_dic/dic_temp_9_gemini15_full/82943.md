gun-down:/ɡʌnˈdaʊn/| vt.|枪杀，击落|To shoot and kill (someone) or shoot down (something, such as an aircraft).  It implies a deliberate and often violent act.|例句：The police gunned down the suspect.  警察击毙了嫌疑犯。

例句：The fighter jet gunned down the enemy aircraft.  战斗机击落了敌机。

近义词：shoot, kill, assassinate, execute

反义词：save, rescue, protect
