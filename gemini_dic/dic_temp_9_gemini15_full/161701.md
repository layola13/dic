powder-keg: /ˈpaʊdərˌkeɡ/ | n. |火药桶；一触即发的局势|

英文解析：A powder keg is a container of gunpowder, but it is also used figuratively to describe a situation that is highly volatile and likely to erupt into violence or conflict at any moment.  The term evokes the image of a barrel of easily ignited explosive material.

例句：

The political situation in the region is a powder keg, ready to explode at any time.  这个地区的政治局势如同火药桶，随时可能爆炸。

Tensions between the two countries have made the border region a powder keg.  两国之间的紧张关系使边境地区成为一个火药桶。


近义词：time bomb, flashpoint, tinderbox, volatile situation

反义词：peaceful situation, stable environment, calm
