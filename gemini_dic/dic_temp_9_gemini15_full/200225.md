skippering: /ˈskɪpərɪŋ/ | v. | 担任船长；指挥；领导 |  The act of acting as a skipper or captain, especially of a ship or boat; also, to lead or manage something with authority and skill. |


例句：

He's skippering the yacht in the regatta. = 他正在指挥帆船参加帆船赛。

She's adept at skippering teams through challenging projects. = 她擅长带领团队完成具有挑战性的项目。


近义词：captaining, commanding, leading, managing


反义词：following, obeying


词性:动词

She skippers the project team effectively. = 她有效地领导项目团队。

词源:
From "skipper," originally a term for a captain or master of a small vessel, possibly derived from a Dutch word meaning "captain".  The "-ing" suffix forms the present participle, functioning as a gerund or noun.
