decore:/dəˈkɔːr/| n. |装饰，装潢 |The act or process of decorating a space; also, the style of decoration used.  It refers to the aesthetic aspects of a room, building, or space, encompassing furniture, fabrics, artwork, and other decorative elements. |例句：The decore of the hotel was luxurious and elegant.  酒店的装饰豪华典雅。|例句：She spent months perfecting the decore of her new home. 她花了几个月的时间完善新家的装饰。|近义词：decoration, ornamentation, embellishment |反义词：plainness, simplicity
