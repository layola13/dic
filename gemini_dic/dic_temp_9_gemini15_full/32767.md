chock:/tʃɒk/| n., v.|楔子；阻止；塞满；|A chock is a wedge-shaped piece of wood, metal, or other material used to block or support something, preventing movement. As a verb, it means to wedge something firmly in place or to fill something tightly.|例句：The wheel was chocked to prevent it from rolling. 车轮被楔子卡住以防止它滚动。  The box was chock-full of toys. 箱子里塞满了玩具。|近义词：wedge, block, jam|反义词：unblock, release
