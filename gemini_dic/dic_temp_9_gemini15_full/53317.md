disabling: /dɪsˈeɪbliŋ/ | adj. |使人丧失能力的；致残的；使无效的 | causing or likely to cause a person to be unable to function normally;  incapacitating;  making something ineffective. |

例句：

His disabling injury prevented him from working.  他的致残伤势使他无法工作。

The virus had a disabling effect on the computer system.  病毒对电脑系统产生了破坏性影响。


近义词：crippling, incapacitating,debilitating,invalidating

反义词： enabling, empowering, strengthening


词性：形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:

The heavy rain is disabling many roads.  大雨正在使许多道路无法通行。

This software is disabling the firewall. 这个软件正在禁用防火墙。


一般过去时:

The accident disabled him for life.  事故使他终身残疾。

The power outage disabled the entire system. 停电使整个系统瘫痪。


现在完成时:

The storm has disabled communication lines.  暴风雨已经切断了通讯线路。

Years of hard work have disabled his body.  多年的辛勤工作已经损害了他的身体。


过去完成时:

They had disabled the alarm system before entering the building.  他们在进入大楼之前已经破坏了警报系统。

The heavy snow had disabled the transportation system.  大雪已经使交通系统瘫痪。


将来时:

The coming storm will disable the power grid.  即将到来的风暴将使电网瘫痪。

The planned maintenance will disable the website for a few hours.  计划中的维护将使网站瘫痪几个小时。


虚拟语气:

If the storm were to disable the airport, we would have to postpone our trip.  如果暴风雨使机场瘫痪，我们将不得不推迟我们的旅行。

I wish the virus hadn't disabled my computer.  我希望病毒没有使我的电脑瘫痪。


被动语态:

The car was disabled by the accident.  这辆车在事故中被损坏。

The system was disabled remotely.  系统被远程禁用。


疑问句:

Is the software disabling the internet connection?  这个软件是否正在禁用互联网连接？

Will the strike disable public transportation?  罢工会使公共交通瘫痪吗？


否定句:

The injury didn't disable him completely.  这伤并没有完全使他丧失能力。

The virus did not disable the important files.  病毒没有损坏重要文件。


PHRASE->

disabling condition:  致残病症

disabling effect:  破坏性影响

mentally disabling:  精神上使人丧失能力的

physically disabling:  身体上使人丧失能力的

emotionally disabling:  情感上使人丧失能力的
