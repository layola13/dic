laveer:/ləˈvɪər/ | v. | 改变航向；反复改变主意；犹豫 | To change direction, especially of a ship by turning its head to windward; to tack.  It also implies wavering or indecisiveness. |例句：The captain laveer to avoid the iceberg. 船长为了避开冰山而改变了航向。

The politician laveer on the issue of tax reform, failing to give a clear answer.  这位政治家在税制改革问题上反复改变主意，未能给出明确的答案。|近义词：tack, zigzag, veer, vacillate, hesitate |反义词：persist, persevere,  continue straight

词源:
