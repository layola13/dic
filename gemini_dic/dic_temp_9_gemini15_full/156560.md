pickers:/ˈpɪkərz/| n.|采摘者，采摘工，拣选器|People who pick fruits, vegetables, or other agricultural products.  A tool used for picking something.|例句：The apple pickers worked tirelessly throughout the day. 苹果采摘工整天辛勤工作。

The automated pickers sorted the mail efficiently. 自动化的拣选器有效地分拣邮件。|近义词：gatherers, harvesters, selectors|反义词：planters (in the context of planting, not picking)|名词
