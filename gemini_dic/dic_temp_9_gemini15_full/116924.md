maypoles:/ˈmeɪpoʊlz/| n.|五月柱，五朔节柱|A maypole is a tall pole, often decorated with ribbons, around which people dance on May Day or other spring festivals.  The tradition is associated with ancient spring festivals and celebrations of fertility.|The children danced around the maypole, their ribbons twirling in the breeze. 孩子们在五月柱周围跳舞，他们的彩带在微风中飞舞。
