northeastward:/ˌnɔːrθˈiːstwərd/| adv.|向东北方向|Moving or directed toward the northeast.  It describes the direction of movement or orientation.|例句：The ship sailed northeastward across the ocean.  船向东北方向驶过海洋。

例句：The wind blew northeastward, carrying the scent of pine. 风从东北方向吹来，带着松树的香味。


近义词：north-eastward, towards the northeast|反义词：southwestward, south-westward


词源:north + east + -ward (suffix indicating direction)  东北方向+方向后缀


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  Its usage is always to indicate direction.


PHRASE->
head northeastward: 向东北方向前进
move northeastward: 向东北移动
travel northeastward: 向东北旅行
sail northeastward: 向东北航行
fly northeastward: 向东北飞行
