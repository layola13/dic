simper:/ˈsɪmpər/| v.|傻笑，假笑|To smile in a self-conscious or affected way.  A simper is a smile that is insincere or affected, often suggesting nervousness or a desire to please. It is typically a weak, nervous, or affected smile.|例句：She simpered at the compliment. 她对赞美傻笑了一下。
She simpered nervously as he approached.  当他走近时，她紧张地傻笑起来。|近义词：grin, smirk, giggle|反义词：frown, scowl
