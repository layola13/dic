garish:/ˈɡæriʃ/| adj.|俗丽的；过于鲜艳的；刺眼的；过分装饰的|extremely bright and showy in a tasteless way|The garish colours of the painting were unpleasant to look at.  这幅画俗丽的色彩令人不悦。|The hotel's decor was rather garish. 这家酒店的装饰相当俗气。|gaudy, flashy, loud, showy|subtle, understated, muted, tasteful


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The colours are garish. = 颜色过于鲜艳。
His clothes are too garish for a job interview. = 他的衣服对于求职面试来说过于鲜艳。


一般过去时:
The decorations were garish. = 装饰过于鲜艳。

They have made the design too garish. = 他们把设计做得过于鲜艳了。

虚拟语气:
