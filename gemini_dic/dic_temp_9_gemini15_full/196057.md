separate:/ˈsɛpəreɪt/| adj., v., n.| 分开的；分离的；单独的 | To divide or keep apart; to be or become divided or distinct.  |例句：Please separate the whites from the yolks. 请把蛋清和蛋黄分开。

例句：The teacher separated the children into groups.老师把孩子们分成几组。


近义词：divide, detach, isolate, distinct | 反义词：unite, combine, join, merge | 词性: adj., v., n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He separates his work and his personal life. 他把工作和私人生活区分开来。
They separate the recycling from the trash. 他们把可回收垃圾和普通垃圾分开。


一般过去时:
She separated from her husband last year. 她去年与丈夫分居了。
We separated the wheat from the chaff. 我们把麦子与糠分开。


现在完成时:
They have separated the winning team from the losing team. 他们已经把胜出队和败北队分开了。
The judge has separated the two disputing parties. 法官已经将两方争议当事人分开。


过去完成时:
They had separated before the accident happened. 事故发生前他们就已经分开了。
The children had separated before the teacher could stop them.  老师阻止之前孩子们就已经分开了。


将来时:
We will separate the good apples from the bad ones. 我们将把好苹果和坏苹果分开。
They will separate the components before assembling the machine. 他们将在组装机器之前将部件分开。


虚拟语气:
If they separated, they would both be happier. 如果他们分开，他们都会更快乐。
I wish they had separated earlier. 我希望他们早点分开。


被动语态:
The clothes are separated by color. 衣服按颜色分开。
The students were separated into different classes. 学生们被分到不同的班级。


疑问句:
Are the eggs separated? 蛋清蛋黄分开了吗？
Do they separate the waste?  他们会分类垃圾吗？


否定句:
Don't separate the two groups. 不要把两组分开。
They didn't separate the items properly. 他们没有正确地分开那些物品。
