dress-the-house: /dres ðə haʊs/ | phrasal verb |  布置房屋，装饰房屋 | To decorate or prepare a house, typically for a special occasion or event.  |  例句：We're dress-the-house for the Christmas party. 我们正在为圣诞派对布置房屋。


例句：They dress-the-house with festive lights and decorations. 他们用节日灯饰和装饰品布置房屋。


近义词：decorate, adorn, prepare, furnish, outfit


反义词：strip, dismantle, empty, clear


词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->  (由于dress the house本身是一个短语动词，没有单独的时态变化，而是依赖于其组成部分的动词 "dress" 的时态变化。以下例句展现了如何通过改变 "dress" 的时态来表达不同的时间概念。)


一般现在时:
We dress the house for every holiday. = 我们每个节日都布置房屋。
They usually dress the house with flowers. =他们通常用鲜花装饰房子。


一般过去时:
We dressed the house for the party last night. = 昨晚我们为派对布置了房屋。
She dressed the house beautifully for her daughter's wedding. =她为女儿的婚礼漂亮地布置了房屋。


现在完成时:
We have dressed the house several times this year. = 今年我们已经布置了几次房屋。
They have dressed the house for the upcoming festival. =他们已经为即将到来的节日布置了房屋。


过去完成时:
We had dressed the house before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，我们已经布置好了房屋。
They had dressed the house in a minimalist style before the photoshoot. =在拍摄之前，他们已经以极简风格布置了房屋。


将来时:
We will dress the house tomorrow for the open house. = 我们明天将为开放日布置房屋。
They will dress the house with elegant furniture for the auction. =他们将用优雅的家具来布置房屋以便拍卖。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would dress the house more elaborately. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会更精心布置房屋。
I wish we could dress the house with a more modern theme. =我希望我们可以用更现代的主题来布置房屋。


被动语态:
The house was dressed for the celebration. = 房屋已被布置好以进行庆祝。
The house is being dressed by professional decorators. =房屋正在由专业的装饰师布置。


疑问句:
Will you dress the house this weekend? = 你这个周末会布置房屋吗？
How are you going to dress the house for the event? = 你打算如何为活动布置房屋？


否定句:
We didn't dress the house last year. = 去年我们没有布置房屋。
They won't dress the house themselves; they'll hire a decorator. = 他们不会自己布置房屋；他们会雇佣一个装饰师。
