rosebud:/ˈroʊzbʌd/| n. |玫瑰花蕾，含苞待放的玫瑰|A rosebud is an unopened rose flower.  It symbolizes youth, innocence, and potential. |例句：The garden was filled with the delicate fragrance of rosebuds. 花园里充满了玫瑰花蕾的芬芳气息。|例句：She wore a rosebud in her hair. 她头上戴着一朵玫瑰花蕾。|近义词：bud, flower bud |反义词：full-blown rose, withered flower
