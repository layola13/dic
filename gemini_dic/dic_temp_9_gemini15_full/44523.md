crossposted:/ˈkrɒsˌpoʊstɪd/ | adj. |  交叉发布的，转发发布的 |  Posted to multiple online forums or platforms simultaneously.  | Example:  The article was crossposted on Reddit and Twitter. 这篇文章在Reddit和Twitter上交叉发布。 | 近义词：reposted, shared, disseminated | 反义词：exclusively posted, uniquely posted
