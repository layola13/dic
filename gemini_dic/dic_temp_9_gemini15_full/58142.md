dross:/drɒs/| n. |废渣；劣质品；糟粕|Waste or unwanted material, especially that which is left after a process of refining or purification; worthless or inferior material.  |例句：The dross from the smelting process was discarded. 冶炼过程中的废渣被丢弃了。

例句：His latest novel is pure dross. 他的最新小说完全是垃圾。


近义词：refuse, rubbish, waste, trash, garbage, detritus

反义词：treasure, gold, gem, essence, quintessence

词性:名词
