reimporting:/ˌriːɪmˈpɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. |重新导入，再导入 | The act of importing something again, often after it has been previously exported or removed.  This can refer to data, goods, or other items. | Example:  The company is reimporting the faulty components for repair. 公司正在重新进口有缺陷的部件以进行修理。


The company is reimporting its products from a different supplier after facing supply chain issues. 公司在面临供应链问题后，正在从不同的供应商处重新进口其产品。


近义词：reintroducing, bringing back


反义词：exporting, removing


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reimport textiles from China.  他们从中国重新进口纺织品。
We reimport the surplus goods to another market. 我们将剩余货物重新进口到另一个市场。


一般过去时:
The government reimported the essential medicines last year. 政府去年重新进口了必需药品。
The company reimported the parts after discovering a defect. 公司在发现缺陷后重新进口了零件。


现在完成时:
They have reimported the software after fixing the bugs. 他们修复错误后重新导入了软件。
We have reimported several batches of goods this month.  这个月我们已经重新进口了几批货物。


过去完成时:
They had reimported the data before the system crashed. 系统崩溃之前他们已经重新导入了数据。
The factory had reimported the raw materials before the strike. 罢工前工厂已经重新进口了原材料。


将来时:
They will reimport the coffee beans next month.  他们下个月将重新进口咖啡豆。
We will reimport the components once the quality is improved. 一旦质量得到改善，我们将重新进口零部件。


虚拟语气:
If they had enough capital, they would reimport the machinery. 如果他们有足够的资金，他们就会重新进口机器。
If we were to reimport the product, we would need to update the labeling. 如果我们要重新进口该产品，我们需要更新标签。


被动语态:
The data was reimported successfully. 数据成功重新导入。
The goods are being reimported from overseas. 货物正在从海外重新进口。


疑问句:
Will the company be reimporting the products soon? 公司很快会重新进口产品吗？
Are they reimporting the parts after the recall?  他们在召回后是否正在重新进口零部件？


否定句:
They did not reimport the goods due to high tariffs.  由于关税高昂，他们没有重新进口货物。
The company will not reimport the product this year. 公司今年将不会重新进口该产品。
