hacked:/hækt/ | v. | 侵入；黑客攻击；砍；劈；乱砍 | To gain unauthorized access to data in a system, typically a computer system.  |例句：The hackers hacked into the company's database and stole customer information. 黑客入侵了公司的数据库并窃取了客户信息。|例句：The sculptor hacked away at the block of marble. 雕刻家猛砍那块大理石。|近义词：intruded, breached, cracked, attacked |反义词：secured, protected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hacks into systems for fun. = 他为了好玩入侵系统。
They hack the website regularly. = 他们定期黑客攻击该网站。

一般过去时:
The thief hacked the lock. = 小偷撬开了锁。
She hacked down the tree with an axe. = 她用斧头砍倒了树。

现在完成时:
He has hacked several accounts this year. = 他今年已经入侵了几个账户。
They have hacked the code and found the bug. = 他们已经破解了代码并找到了错误。

过去完成时:
They had hacked the server before the security update. = 他们在安全更新之前已经入侵了服务器。
She had hacked the ice before the skating competition. = 她在滑冰比赛之前就已经劈好了冰。

将来时:
He will hack the problem soon. = 他很快就会解决这个问题。
They will hack the password and get the information. = 他们将破解密码并获取信息。

虚拟语气:
If I hacked into their system, I could access their data. = 如果我入侵他们的系统，我就能访问他们的数据。
I wish I had not hacked into that system. = 我真希望我没有入侵那个系统。

被动语态:
The system was hacked last night. = 系统昨晚被黑客攻击了。
The tree was hacked down by the storm. = 那棵树被暴风雨刮倒了。

疑问句:
Was the account hacked? = 账户被黑客攻击了吗？
Have they hacked the system yet? = 他们已经黑客攻击系统了吗？

否定句:
He didn't hack into the system. = 他没有入侵系统。
They haven't hacked the code yet. = 他们还没有破解代码。
