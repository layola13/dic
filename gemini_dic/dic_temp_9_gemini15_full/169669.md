proportioner: /prəˈpɔːʃənər/ | n. | 比例器；配比器 | A device or system that controls or regulates the proportions of different ingredients or components in a mixture or process.  It often involves precise measurement and control to achieve a desired ratio or blend.  For example, a proportioner in a cement mixer ensures the correct ratio of cement, sand, and gravel. | The baker used a precise proportioner to ensure the correct ratio of flour and water in his bread dough. |  doser, mixer, blender |  (No direct antonym, but the opposite concept would be "uncontrolled mixing") | 名词
