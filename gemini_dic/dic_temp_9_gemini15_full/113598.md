macerate:/ˈmæsəreɪt/| vt.|浸软；捣烂；使软化|To soften or disintegrate by soaking in a liquid, often to extract flavor or medicinal properties.|The chef macerated the strawberries in Grand Marnier before adding them to the cake.  厨师在将草莓加入蛋糕之前用君度酒浸泡它们。|The herbalist macerated the plant material to create a potent tincture. 草药师浸泡植物材料以制成有效的酊剂。|soak, steep, soften|harden, solidify
