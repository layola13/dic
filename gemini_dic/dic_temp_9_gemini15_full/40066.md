contactor:/kənˈtæktər/| n. |接触器；联络人；承包商|A contactor is a device that makes and breaks electrical circuits. It can also refer to a person who contracts to do work or provide services, or someone who makes contact.  |例句：The electrician installed a new contactor to control the motor.  电工安装了一个新的接触器来控制电机。

The project manager hired a contactor to build the new office. 项目经理雇佣了一名承包商来建造新办公室。


近义词：switch (as a device), contractor, intermediary, liaison


反义词：insulator (as a device)
