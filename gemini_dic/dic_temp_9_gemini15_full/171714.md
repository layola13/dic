pumpkin:/ˈpʌmpkɪn/| n.|南瓜|A large, round orange or yellow fruit with thick skin and many seeds, used as food.  Pumpkins are commonly used in pies, soups, and other dishes, especially during autumn.  They are also often carved into jack-o'-lanterns for Halloween.|例句：We carved a pumpkin for Halloween. 我们雕刻了一个南瓜灯来庆祝万圣节。

例句：She baked a delicious pumpkin pie. 她烤了一个美味的南瓜派。


近义词：squash (某些品种)|反义词：None
