sanctioner:/ˈsæŋkʃənər/| n. | 制裁者，实施制裁者 | A person or entity that imposes sanctions.  This could be a government, an international organization, or even a private company imposing restrictions. |The sanctioner imposed strict trade embargoes on the offending nation.|近义词：punisher, enforcer |反义词：benefactor, supporter |名词
