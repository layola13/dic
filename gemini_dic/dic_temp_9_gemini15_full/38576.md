concomitants:/kənˈkɑːmɪtənts/ | n. |伴随物；同时发生的事物 | things that happen at the same time as something else, often as a result of it.  |The concomitants of aging include decreased muscle mass and bone density. 老化的伴随物包括肌肉质量和骨密度的下降。 |近义词：accompaniments, correlates, consequences |反义词：antecedents, precursors


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The treatment has several unpleasant concomitants. = 这种治疗有一些令人不快的伴随症状。
These symptoms are concomitants of the disease. = 这些症状是该疾病的伴随症状。


一般过去时:
The project had several unforeseen concomitants. = 这个项目有一些无法预料的伴随事件。
The war's concomitants included widespread famine and disease. = 那场战争的伴随事件包括大范围的饥荒和疾病。


现在完成时:
The economic crisis has had many negative concomitants. = 经济危机带来了许多负面影响。
Researchers have identified several concomitants of climate change. = 研究人员已经确定了气候变化的几个伴随因素。


过去完成时:
By the time the project finished, several unexpected concomitants had emerged. = 到项目结束时，已经出现了一些意想不到的伴随事件。
They had not anticipated the many political concomitants of the decision. = 他们没有预料到这个决定带来的许多政治后果。


将来时:
The new policy will likely have some unforeseen concomitants. = 新政策可能会有一些无法预料的影响。
The technological advancements will have significant environmental concomitants. = 技术进步将产生重大的环境影响。


虚拟语气:
If the plan were implemented, there would be many undesirable concomitants. = 如果实施该计划，就会有很多不良后果。
I wish there weren't so many negative concomitants to this decision. = 我希望这个决定没有这么多负面影响。


被动语态:
Many negative social concomitants were observed in the study. = 在研究中观察到许多负面的社会伴随现象。
The positive concomitants of the program are being carefully studied. = 该项目的积极伴随效应正在被仔细研究。


疑问句:
What are the likely concomitants of this new technology? = 这种新技术的可能伴随效应是什么？
Are there any unforeseen concomitants we should be aware of? = 我们应该注意是否存在任何无法预料的伴随事件？


否定句:
There were no significant concomitants to the treatment. = 治疗没有明显的伴随症状。
The study found no evidence of negative concomitants. = 该研究没有发现任何负面影响的证据。
