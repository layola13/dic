marblelike: /ˈmɑːrblˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 大理石似的；像大理石的 | Resembling marble in appearance; smooth, hard, and often cold.  The word combines "marble" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance. | 例句：The smooth, cold surface of the countertop felt marblelike.  光滑冰冷的台面摸起来像大理石。

The statue had a marblelike gleam in the sunlight.  雕像在阳光下发出大理石般的光泽。


近义词：marbly, stony, cold, smooth, hard


反义词：rough, soft, warm, porous


词性:形容词
