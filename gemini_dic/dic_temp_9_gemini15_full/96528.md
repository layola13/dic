indolence:/ˈɪndəʊləns/ | n. |懒惰；倦怠；懒散| Habitual laziness; inactivity resulting from a lack of energy or motivation.|His indolence prevented him from finishing the project on time.他的懒惰使他无法按时完成项目。  |近义词：laziness, sloth, lethargy, idleness|反义词：diligence, industry, activity
