nurtures: /ˈnɜːrtʃərz/ | vt. | 培育；培养；滋养；关爱 | To care for and encourage the growth or development of.  |例句：She nurtured her children with love and care. 她用爱和关怀抚养她的孩子。

例句：The teacher nurtured the students' talents. 老师培养学生的天赋。


近义词：cultivate, foster, cherish, nourish | 反义词：neglect, ignore, abandon | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She nurtures her plants daily. = 她每天都细心照料她的植物。
He nurtures his ambition to become a doctor. = 他培养自己成为医生的志向。

一般过去时:
The gardener nurtured the seedlings carefully. = 园丁细心地培育幼苗。
My parents nurtured my interest in music. = 我的父母培养了我对音乐的兴趣。

现在完成时:
She has nurtured her business for over a decade. = 她已经经营她的生意超过十年了。
They have nurtured a strong sense of community. = 他们已经培养了强烈的社区意识。

过去完成时:
He had nurtured his resentment for years. = 他多年来一直怀恨在心。
She had nurtured her dream of traveling the world. = 她一直怀揣着环游世界的梦想。

将来时:
We will nurture our relationship through thick and thin. = 我们将风雨同舟，维系我们的关系。
The company will nurture young talent within the organization. = 公司将培养组织内的年轻人才。

虚拟语气:
If she had nurtured her talent, she might have become a famous artist. = 如果她当初培养了她的天赋，她可能会成为一名著名的艺术家。
I wish I had nurtured my friendships better. = 我希望我当初能更好地维护我的友谊。

被动语态:
The plants were nurtured with great care. = 植物得到了精心照料。
His potential was nurtured by his supportive teachers. = 他的潜力得到了他那些支持的老师的培养。

疑问句:
How do you nurture your creativity? = 你如何培养你的创造力？
What kind of environment is best for nurturing children? = 什么样的环境最有利于孩子的成长？

否定句:
He didn't nurture his relationships. = 他没有维系他的关系。
She failed to nurture her potential. = 她没有培养她的潜力。

词源:
The word "nurture" comes from the Old French word "nurture," meaning "to nourish,"  itself deriving from the Latin word "nutrire," meaning "to nourish" or "to suckle."  The connection to nourishing and caring for young is evident in its historical usage.
