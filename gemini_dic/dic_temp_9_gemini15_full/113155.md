lupin:/ˈluːpɪn/| n. |羽扇豆，鲁冰花|A lupin is a plant with tall stems, usually with many colourful flowers. Lupins are often grown as ornamental plants.  They are also known for their nitrogen-fixing properties, making them valuable in agriculture.|例句：The field was ablaze with the vibrant colours of the lupins.  田野里盛开着色彩鲜艳的羽扇豆，一片绚丽。

The lupins added a touch of elegance to the garden. 羽扇豆为花园增添了一丝优雅。|近义词：bluebonnet (in some contexts)|反义词：无|词性:名词
