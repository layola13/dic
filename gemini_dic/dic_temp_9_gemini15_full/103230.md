jetting:/ˈdʒɛtɪŋ/ | n. v. | 喷射；喷射流；喷嘴 | The act of emitting a fluid in a forceful stream; a forceful stream of fluid; a nozzle for emitting a fluid. |例句：The jetting of water from the fountain was spectacular.  喷泉喷出的水非常壮观。


The powerful jetting of the volcano sent ash high into the air. 火山的强烈喷发将火山灰喷射到高空。


近义词：spouting, spraying, squirting | 反义词：dripping, trickling


词源：源自 jet (n.) “一股液体”，最终源自古法语 jet，“喷射”，可能源自拉丁语 jactare，“投掷”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine is jetting fuel into the combustion chamber. = 发动机正在将燃料喷射到燃烧室。
Water is jetting out of the broken pipe. = 水正从破裂的管道中喷射出来。

一般过去时:
The geyser jetted hot water high into the air. = 间歇泉喷射出热水，直冲云霄。
He jetted the paint onto the canvas. = 他将油漆喷到画布上。

现在完成时:
The volcano has jetted ash and lava for days. = 火山已经喷发了几天火山灰和熔岩。
They have jetted the concrete into the foundation.  = 他们已经将混凝土喷射到地基中。

过去完成时:
The pipe had jetted water all over the floor before we fixed it. = 在我们修理管道之前，管道已经把水喷洒得到处都是。
The machine had jetted the coating onto the surface before it malfunctioned. = 机器在发生故障之前已经将涂层喷涂到表面。

将来时:
The rocket will jet fuel during its ascent. = 火箭在上升过程中将喷射燃料。
The sprinkler system will jet water onto the lawn. = 喷灌系统将把水喷洒到草坪上。


虚拟语气:
If the nozzle were larger, it would jet more water. = 如果喷嘴更大，它就会喷出更多的水。
I wish the paint sprayer would jet a more even coat. = 我希望喷漆器能喷涂更均匀的涂层。


被动语态:
The paint was jetted onto the car body. = 油漆被喷涂到车身上。
The concrete was jetted into the mold. = 混凝土被喷射到模具中。


疑问句:
Is the fountain jetting water properly? = 喷泉喷水正常吗？
What is jetting out of that pipe? = 那根管道里喷出的是什么？


否定句:
The engine isn't jetting fuel efficiently. = 发动机没有有效地喷射燃料。
The sprinkler system isn't jetting water at all. = 喷灌系统根本没有喷水。
