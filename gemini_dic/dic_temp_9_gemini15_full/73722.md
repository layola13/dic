fork-over:/fɔːrkˈoʊvər/|/fɔːkˈəʊvə(r)/| v. |交出；付清；拿出|To hand over something, especially money or a sum of money.  The term often implies reluctance or a feeling of being forced to do so.|例句：He reluctantly forked over the $500 fine. 他不情愿地交出了500美元的罚款。

例句：She finally forked over the missing documents. 她最终交出了丢失的文件。


近义词：hand over, surrender, pay up, shell out, cough up

反义词：keep, retain, withhold

词性:动词
