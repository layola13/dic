declaimers:/dɪˈkleɪmərz/| n.|免责声明；声明；否认|A formal statement denying something; a statement that something is not true.  It often appears in legal contexts to limit liability.|例句：The website contains disclaimers about the accuracy of the information. 网站包含关于信息准确性的免责声明。|例句：He issued a disclaimer denying any involvement in the crime. 他发表声明否认参与了这起犯罪。|近义词：denial, repudiation, disclaimer|反义词：affirmation, confirmation
