longs:/lɒŋz/| v.|渴望，极度想念|To have a strong desire or wish for something or someone.  |例句：I long for the days of summer. 我渴望夏日时光。
例句：She longs to see her family again. 她渴望再次见到家人。|近义词：yearn, crave, desire |反义词：dislike, hate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He longs for a better life. 他渴望过更好的生活。
They long to travel the world. 他们渴望环游世界。

一般过去时:
I longed for a peaceful evening. 我渴望一个平静的夜晚。
She longed to be with him. 她渴望与他在一起。

现在完成时:
I have longed for this moment for years. 我渴望这一刻已经很多年了。
We have longed to hear from you. 我们一直渴望收到你的消息。

过去完成时:
She had longed to visit Paris her whole life. 她一生都渴望去巴黎旅行。
He had longed for freedom before his escape. 他在逃脱之前一直渴望自由。

将来时:
I will long for this place when I leave. 我离开后会想念这个地方。
They will long for their childhood home. 他们会怀念他们的童年家园。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would long for nothing. 如果我有钱，我就不会渴望任何东西。
I wish I could long for something else besides this. 我希望我能渴望除这之外的其他东西。

被动语态: *(Longs doesn't typically use passive voice)*


疑问句:
Do you long for anything in particular? 你特别渴望什么吗？
Does he long for his homeland? 他渴望他的祖国吗？

否定句:
I don't long for fame. 我不渴望名利。
They didn't long for trouble. 他们不渴望麻烦。


PHRASE->

long for sth: 渴望某物
long to do sth: 渴望做某事
long after:  久久思念

long live: 万岁
