infortune:/ɪnˈfɔːrtʃən/| n. |不幸，厄运 | misfortune is bad luck.  It refers to an unfortunate event or circumstance.  |例句：His misfortune was losing his job and his house in the same year.  他一年的不幸是丢了工作又丢了房子。|例句：The misfortune of the family was compounded by the death of their only son.  这个家庭的不幸因为他们独子的去世而加剧了。|近义词：mishap, adversity, calamity, ill luck |反义词：fortune, good luck, prosperity
