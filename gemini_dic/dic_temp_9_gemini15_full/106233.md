know-alls: /ˈnoʊˌɔːlz/ | n. | 万事通，自以为是的人 | A person who acts as if they know everything.  They often interrupt and correct others, displaying an air of superiority. |例句：He's such a know-all; he never listens to anyone else's opinion. 他是个十足的万事通，从不听取别人的意见。


例句：She's become a real know-all since she started that course.自从她上了那个课程后，她变得像个万事通。


近义词：smart aleck, know-it-all, wiseacre, egghead


反义词：ignorant, unlearned, unassuming, humble


词性: Noun
