hospital:/ˈhɒspɪtl/ /ˈhɑːspɪtl/| n. |医院|A place where sick or injured people are given medical treatment and care.  |例句：He was taken to the hospital after the accident. 他事故后被送往医院。|例句：She works at a children's hospital.  她在儿童医院工作。|近义词：clinic, infirmary |反义词：home


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The hospital provides excellent care. = 这家医院提供优质的护理。
She goes to the hospital for regular checkups. = 她定期去医院体检。


一般过去时:
He stayed in the hospital for a week. = 他在医院住了一个星期。

将来时:
