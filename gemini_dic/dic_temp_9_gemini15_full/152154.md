patio:/ˈpætioʊ/| n. |露台；天井；庭院|An outdoor area, typically paved, adjacent to a house and often used for dining or relaxing.|例句：We had dinner on the patio. 我们在露台上吃了晚饭。|近义词：courtyard, terrace, deck|反义词:interior


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The patio is paved with bricks. = 这露台是用砖铺成的。
They often have breakfast on the patio. = 他们经常在露台上吃早餐。

一般过去时:
We sat on the patio and talked. = 我们坐在露台上聊天。
The patio was crowded during the party. = 聚会期间，露台挤满了人。

现在完成时:
We have furnished the patio with comfortable chairs. = 我们用舒适的椅子布置了露台。
They have spent many summer evenings on the patio. = 他们已经在露台上度过了许多夏夜。

过去完成时:
We had already cleaned the patio before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，我们已经打扫干净了露台。
They had decorated the patio for the special occasion. = 他们已经为这个特别的场合装饰了露台。

将来时:
We will have a barbecue on the patio tomorrow. = 我们明天将在露台上举行烧烤。
They will relax on the patio after work. = 他们下班后将在露台上放松身心。

虚拟语气:
If we had a larger patio, we could have more guests. = 如果我们有一个更大的露台，我们就能招待更多客人。
I wish we had a covered patio. = 我希望我们有一个有顶棚的露台。

被动语态:
The patio is being cleaned. = 露台正在被清洁。
The patio was designed by a famous architect. = 这个露台是由一位著名的建筑师设计的。

疑问句:
Is there enough space on the patio for everyone? = 露台上够大家坐吗？
What kind of plants are on the patio? = 露台上有什么样的植物？

否定句:
We don't have a patio at our apartment. = 我们公寓没有露台。
They didn't use the patio much last summer. = 他们去年夏天很少使用露台。


PHRASE->
covered patio: 有顶棚的露台
patio furniture: 露台家具
patio umbrella: 露台伞
patio heater: 露台加热器
patio doors: 露台门

词源:
From Spanish patio, from Old Spanish patiu, from Latin patium "courtyard", from pateo "open space".  The word ultimately derives from Proto-Italic *pat-yo-, related to Latin patēre "to lie open".
