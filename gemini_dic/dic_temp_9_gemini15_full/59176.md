dysfunctions:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənz/| n.|功能障碍；机能失调；故障|The term refers to the impaired or abnormal functioning of a body part, system, or process.  It can also refer to a failure in a system or process.  Dysfunctions can be physical, mental, or social.|例句：The patient's symptoms suggest a dysfunction of the nervous system.  这位病人的症状表明神经系统功能障碍。

The team's poor communication led to several dysfunctions in the project. 团队沟通不畅导致项目出现多处问题。|近义词：malfunctions, disorders, impairments, breakdowns|反义词： proper functioning, efficiency, effectiveness|名词
