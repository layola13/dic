kerfuffle:/ˈkɜːrfʌl/| n.|小争吵；混乱；骚动 |A kerfuffle is a commotion or fuss, typically a minor one. It suggests a brief burst of noisy disagreement or confusion.  |例句：There was a bit of a kerfuffle when the celebrity arrived.  名流到来时引起了一点小骚动。

例句：The announcement caused quite a kerfuffle among the students.  这个公告在学生中引起了一阵骚动。


近义词：commotion, fuss, ado, brouhaha, rumpus, to-do|反义词：calm, peace, quiet, tranquility | 名词
