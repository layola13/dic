meltwater:/ˈmɛltˌwɔːtər/| n. |融水，冰雪融水 |Water formed by the melting of snow or ice.|The meltwater from the glaciers feeds the river.  融化的冰川水滋养着这条河。 The rapid meltwater runoff caused flooding in the valley.  迅速融化的雪水导致山谷洪水泛滥。|近义词：thaw water, snowmelt |反义词：frozen water
