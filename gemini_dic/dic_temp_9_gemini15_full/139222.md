objurgate:/ˈɒbˌdʒɜːɡeɪt/| vt.|严厉斥责；痛骂|To rebuke severely; to scold vehemently.|例句：The teacher objurgated the student for his disruptive behavior.老师严厉斥责学生扰乱课堂秩序。|例句：The manager objurgated the employee for their repeated mistakes.经理严厉斥责员工反复犯错。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, scold, chide|反义词：praise, commend, applaud


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He objurgates his employees frequently. 他经常斥责他的员工。
She objurgates her children for misbehaving. 她因为孩子行为不端而斥责他们。


一般过去时:
The principal objurgated the student for cheating. 校长因为作弊而斥责学生。
My father objurgated me for coming home late. 我父亲因为回家晚了而斥责我。


现在完成时:
He has objurgated me several times this week.  本周他已经几次斥责我了。
She has objurgated her assistant for the project delays. 她因为项目延误而斥责了她的助理。


过去完成时:
The teacher had already objurgated the student before the principal arrived. 校长到达之前，老师已经斥责了学生。
He had objurgated her many times before she finally understood.  在他多次斥责她之后，她终于明白了。


将来时:
I will objurgate him if he does it again. 如果他再这样做，我会斥责他。
She will objurgate her son for breaking the vase. 她会因为儿子打破花瓶而斥责他。


虚拟语气:
I would objurgate him if I were you. 如果我是你，我会斥责他。
If he had studied harder, his teacher wouldn't have objurgated him. 如果他学习更努力，老师就不会斥责他了。


被动语态:
The student was objurgated for his poor performance. 学生因为表现差而受到斥责。
The employee was objurgated by the manager for his negligence. 员工因为疏忽而被经理斥责。


疑问句:
Will you objurgate him for his mistake? 你会因为他的错误而斥责他吗？
Has he ever been objurgated by his boss? 他有没有被老板斥责过？


否定句:
He did not objurgate the child for the small mistake. 他没有因为那个小错误而斥责孩子。
She will not objurgate him unless he repeats the behavior. 除非他重复这种行为，否则她不会斥责他。


PHRASE->
objurgate vehemently  强烈斥责
objurgate harshly  严厉斥责
objurgate publicly 公开斥责
objurgate severely  严厉斥责
objurgate relentlessly  不停地斥责


词源:
From Latin *objurgāre "to scold, rebuke," from ob- "against" + *jurgāre "to swear, brawl," from *jūris "oath."
