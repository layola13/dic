milldam: /ˈmɪlˌdæm/ | n. | 水磨坝；水磨堤坝 | A dam built to create a reservoir to power a watermill.  A milldam is a dam built across a stream or river to create a head of water to power a watermill.  | The old milldam was crumbling, a testament to time's relentless march.  古老的水磨坝正在崩塌，这是时间无情流逝的证明。 The milldam provided a constant source of power for the mill. 水磨坝为磨坊提供了持续不断的动力。 | dam, weir |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：destroyed milldam, broken dam) | 名词
