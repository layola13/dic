glycerite:/ˈɡlɪsərɪt/| n. |甘油剂|A glycerite is a solution of a medicinal substance in glycerin.  It's a type of pharmaceutical preparation where the active ingredient is dissolved in glycerin to form a liquid or semiliquid product. Glycerites are often used topically or internally, depending on the specific medicinal substance and its intended use.|Example:  This glycerite of boric acid is used as an antiseptic. 这个硼酸甘油剂用作防腐剂。|近义词：glycerogel, extract (in some contexts)|反义词：N/A
