duplicate:/ˈdjuːplɪkeɪt/|/ˈdjuːplɪkət/| n., vt., adj.|复制品；副本；复制；重复|To make an exact copy of something;  a thing that is identical to another.|例句：The police have found a duplicate of the stolen painting.  警方找到了被盗画作的复制品。  He duplicated the experiment to verify the results. 他重复了实验以验证结果。|近义词：copy, replicate, reproduce, clone|反义词：original, unique
