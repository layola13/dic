pro-Alaskan: /ˌproʊ əˈlæskən/ | adj. | 支持阿拉斯加的；拥护阿拉斯加的 |  Supporting or favoring Alaska or its interests.  This term typically refers to individuals or groups advocating for Alaskan autonomy,  development, or specific Alaskan policies.  It's a compound word combining "pro-" (in favor of) and "Alaskan" (relating to Alaska).|

例句：

The pro-Alaskan senator championed legislation to protect the state's natural resources.  支持阿拉斯加的参议员大力支持旨在保护该州自然资源的立法。

Pro-Alaskan groups rallied against the proposed pipeline route. 支持阿拉斯加的团体集会反对拟议的管道线路。


近义词：Alaskan-supporting, pro-statehood (in the context of Alaskan statehood)


反义词：anti-Alaskan


词性：形容词
