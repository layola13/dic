grapple:/ˈɡræpəl/ | n., v. | 抓住； grapple with sth.  努力解决（难题）|

英文解析：As a noun, a grapple refers to a tool with hooked arms used for seizing or grasping something. As a verb, it means to seize something firmly, especially in a struggle.  It can also mean to struggle with a problem or difficult task.


例句：

He used a grapple to pull the sunken car from the lake. 他用抓钩从湖里拉起沉没的汽车。

The firefighters grappled with the raging fire. 消防员与肆虐的大火搏斗。

The team grappled with the complex problem for weeks.  团队几个星期都在努力解决这个复杂的问题。


近义词：noun: hook, clasp; verb: wrestle, struggle, contend with, tackle


反义词：  release, abandon (verb);  (noun - no direct antonym, but the opposite concept would be something smooth and non-grasping)


词性：noun, verb
