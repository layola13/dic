darker:/ˈdɑːrkər/| adj.|较暗的，更暗的；更阴暗的，更险恶的 |More dark; having less light.  It can also refer to something more gloomy or sinister. |例句：The room was darker than I expected.  房间比我想象的要暗。

The future looks darker after the recent economic downturn.  最近的经济衰退后，未来看起来更加黯淡。|近义词：dimmer, blacker, gloomier |反义词：brighter, lighter

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sky is darker today. = 今天的天空更暗。
This color is darker than that one. = 这种颜色比那种颜色更深。


一般过去时:
The room was darker after the lights went out. = 灯灭后房间更暗了。
The news made her mood darker. = 这个消息使她的心情更加低落。


现在完成时:
The situation has become darker since the incident. = 自从那件事发生后，情况变得更糟了。
It has gotten darker much faster than I expected. = 天黑得比我预料的要快得多。


过去完成时:
The forest had become darker as the sun set. = 太阳下山后森林变得更暗了。
The atmosphere had grown darker before the storm. = 暴风雨来临前气氛变得更加紧张。


将来时:
The sky will be darker later tonight. = 今晚晚些时候天空会更暗。
The future will be darker if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，未来将会更加黯淡。


虚拟语气:
If the room were darker, it would be more romantic. = 如果房间更暗一些，会更浪漫。
I wish the future were not darker. = 我希望未来不要那么黯淡。


被动语态:
The room was made darker by closing the curtains. = 通过拉上窗帘使房间变暗。
The painting was made darker with the addition of more black paint. = 通过添加更多的黑色颜料使绘画变得更暗。


疑问句:
Is the coffee darker than usual? = 咖啡比平时更浓吗？
Is the future darker than we think? = 未来比我们想象的更黯淡吗？


否定句:
The room isn't darker than I like. = 房间没有暗到我不喜欢的程度。
The situation isn't as darker as the media portrays. = 情况并没有媒体描绘的那么糟糕。
