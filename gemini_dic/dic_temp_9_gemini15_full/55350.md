/ˈdaɪəɡræm/| n. |图表，简图|A diagram is a simplified drawing showing the main features of something.  It is often used to explain how something works or is structured. |Example: The teacher drew a diagram on the board to illustrate the concept.|近义词：chart, graph, illustration|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The diagram shows the process clearly. = 这个图表清楚地显示了这个过程。
This diagram illustrates the structure of the atom. = 这个图表阐述了原子的结构。


一般过去时:
The engineer presented a diagram of the bridge. = 工程师展示了一张桥梁的示意图。
They used a diagram to explain the system. = 他们用图表解释了这个系统。
