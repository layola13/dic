fuscation:/fʌsˈkeɪʃən/| n. |模糊；使模糊；使混乱|The deliberate obscuring of something, especially the meaning of something.  It often involves making something difficult to understand.  This can be done for various reasons, including protecting intellectual property, enhancing security, or simply being deliberately unclear.|例句：The fuscation of the legal document made it nearly impossible to understand. 文件的模糊措辞使其几乎无法理解。

例句：The politician's speech was full of fuscation, avoiding any direct answers to the difficult questions.  这位政客的演讲充满了含糊其辞，回避了对难题的直接回答。|近义词：obscuration, obfuscation, confusion, ambiguity|反义词：clarity, lucidity, transparency
