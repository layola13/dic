mechanizer:/ˈmɛkənaɪzər/| n. |机械化者；机械制造者|A person or thing that mechanizes something.  Someone or something that introduces or implements machinery or mechanical processes.|The mechanizer of the factory improved efficiency by introducing new robots. 工厂的机械化者通过引进新的机器人提高了效率。|近义词：automaton, mechanist |反义词：manual laborer
