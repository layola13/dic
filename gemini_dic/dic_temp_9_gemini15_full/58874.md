durango:/dəˈræŋɡoʊ/| n.|杜兰戈（地名，美国科罗拉多州城市；墨西哥州名）|Durango is a city in southwestern Colorado, known for its stunning mountain scenery and outdoor recreation opportunities.  It's also the name of a state in Mexico.|例句：We spent a week in Durango, Colorado, hiking and enjoying the beautiful scenery. 我们在科罗拉多州的杜兰戈度过了一周，远足并欣赏美丽的景色。

例句：Durango is a significant agricultural region in Mexico. 杜兰戈是墨西哥重要的农业地区。|近义词：Silverton (similar mountain town),  |反义词：无|名词
