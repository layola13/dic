USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't have tenses like verbs do.  It simply modifies verbs to indicate a lack of electrical operation.

Example 1: The pump operated nonelectrically, using a hand crank.  =  泵是非电动的，使用手摇曲柄。

Example 2: The gate opened nonelectrically, requiring a manual push. =  大门是非电动的，需要手动推动。


PHRASE->
There are no common multi-word phrases using "nonelectrically".  The word itself is sufficient to convey its meaning.  Phrases would typically focus on the method of operation instead (e.g., "by hand," "mechanically," "manually").
