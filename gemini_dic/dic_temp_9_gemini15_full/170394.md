provoking:/prəˈvoʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 挑衅的；煽动的；刺激的 |  Something that is provoking is likely to make someone angry, annoyed, or upset. It can also describe something that stimulates interest or thought. |

例句：

His provocative remarks angered the audience.  他的挑衅性言论激怒了观众。

The painting was provocative, causing much debate. 这幅画很煽动，引起了很多争论。


近义词：irritating, infuriating, stimulating, challenging, exciting

反义词：soothing, calming, unexciting, bland


词源：

From the verb "provoke," ultimately from the Latin "provocare," meaning "to call forth," "to challenge," or "to summon."  The suffix "-ing" transforms the verb into an adjective.
