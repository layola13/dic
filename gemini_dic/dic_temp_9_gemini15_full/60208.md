efflorescence:/ˌɛfləˈrɛs(ə)ns/| n. |开花；繁荣；（盐类的）风化 |The process of flowering; a blossoming or flourishing.  It can also refer to the powdery deposit formed on the surface of a substance due to the crystallization of soluble salts. |例句：The efflorescence of the roses was breathtaking. 玫瑰花的盛开令人叹为观止。

The efflorescence of the Renaissance saw a great flourishing of arts and sciences. 文艺复兴的繁荣时期见证了艺术和科学的蓬勃发展。


近义词：flowering, blossoming, flourishing, bloom, prosperity |反义词：decay, decline, withering, deterioration |词性：名词
