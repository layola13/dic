inbred:/ˈɪnˌbred/ | adj. |近亲繁殖的；遗传的；天生的|  resulting from or characterized by inbreeding; having or showing the characteristics of inbreeding; innate; inherent | The inbred strain of mice showed increased susceptibility to disease.  这只近亲繁殖的小鼠表现出对疾病的易感性增加。The inbred prejudice against immigrants hampered progress.  对移民根深蒂固的偏见阻碍了进步。|近义词：consanguineous, congenital, inherent |反义词：outbred, acquired


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The farmer practices inbred breeding with his cattle. = 这个农民对他的牛实行近亲繁殖。
This plant shows the characteristics of inbred lines. = 这种植物表现出近交系的特征。


一般过去时:
They studied the effects of inbred lines on crop yields. = 他们研究了近交系对作物产量的影响。
The scientists inbred several generations of mice. = 科学家们对几代老鼠进行了近亲繁殖。


现在完成时:
Researchers have discovered several problems associated with inbred populations. = 研究人员已经发现了与近亲繁殖种群相关的几个问题。
The effects of inbreeding have been documented in many animal species. = 近亲繁殖的影响已在许多动物物种中得到证实。


过去完成时:
Before the study, the researchers had inbred the mice for several generations. = 在研究之前，研究人员已经对老鼠进行了几代的近亲繁殖。
Scientists had already observed a decline in genetic diversity in the inbred population. = 科学家们已经观察到近亲繁殖种群中遗传多样性的下降。


将来时:
They will continue to inbreed the plants to maintain desirable traits. = 他们将继续对植物进行近亲繁殖以保持理想的性状。
The experiment will demonstrate the negative effects of inbreeding on the animals' health. = 实验将证明近亲繁殖对动物健康的负面影响。


虚拟语气:
If they had not inbred the dogs, they might not have developed this genetic defect. = 如果他们没有对狗进行近亲繁殖，他们可能就不会产生这种基因缺陷。
I wish we had not inbred those plants; their yield is quite low. = 我真希望我们没有对那些植物进行近亲繁殖；它们的产量很低。


被动语态:

The effects of inbreeding are being studied by scientists worldwide. = 全世界科学家正在研究近亲繁殖的影响。


疑问句:

否定句:
