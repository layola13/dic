edulcorate:/ɪˈdʌlkəreɪt/| vt.|使变甜；使愉快；润色|To make (something, especially a statement or piece of writing) less harsh or unpleasant; to sweeten.  |例句：He tried to edulcorate the bitter truth. 他试图使痛苦的真相变得不那么尖锐。

例句：The writer edulcorated the harsh realities of war in his novel.  作家在他的小说中淡化了战争的残酷现实。


近义词：sweeten, mitigate, soften, palliate

反义词：embitter, exacerbate, worsen, aggravate

词性:动词
