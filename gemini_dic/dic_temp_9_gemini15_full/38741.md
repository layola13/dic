condole:/kənˈdoʊl/| vt.|慰问，吊唁|To express sympathy to (someone) on the death of a relative or friend.  It implies sharing the sorrow and grief of the bereaved.|例句：We condoled with the family on their loss. 我们对他们家人的损失表示慰问。

例句：I offered my condolences to her on the death of her father. 我向她表示了父亲去世的慰问。


近义词：sympathize, commiserate, empathize

反义词：rejoice, celebrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He condoles with the grieving family. = 他慰问悲伤的家庭。
We condole with those who have lost loved ones. = 我们慰问那些失去亲人的。


一般过去时:
She condoled with her friend after the accident. = 事故后，她慰问了她的朋友。
They condoled with the bereaved family at the funeral. = 他们在葬礼上慰问了失去亲人的家庭。


现在完成时:
I have condoled with several families this year. = 今年我已经慰问了好几个家庭了。
Have you condoled with your neighbor on their loss? = 你有没有慰问你的邻居他们的损失？


过去完成时:
She had condoled with him before he left for the funeral. = 在他去参加葬礼之前，她已经慰问了他。
They had already condoled with the family before the service began. = 在仪式开始之前，他们已经慰问了这个家庭。


将来时:
I will condole with her when I see her next. = 下次见到她的时候我会慰问她。
We will condole with them at the memorial service. = 我们将在追悼会上慰问他们。


虚拟语气:
If I knew them better, I would condole with them more directly. = 如果我更了解他们，我会更直接地慰问他们。
I wish I could condole with them in person. = 我希望我能亲自慰问他们。


被动语态:
The family was condoled with by many friends and neighbors. = 这个家庭受到了许多朋友和邻居的慰问。
Their grief was condoled with by the entire community. = 他们的悲伤得到了整个社区的慰问。


疑问句:
Did you condole with them? = 你慰问他们了吗？
Will you condole with the family? = 你会慰问这个家庭吗？


否定句:
I didn't condole with him, as I didn't know what to say. = 我没有慰问他，因为我不知道该说什么。
They did not condole with the family publicly. = 他们没有公开慰问这个家庭。


PHRASE->
condole with someone = 向某人表示慰问
offer condolences = 表示慰问
express condolences = 表示慰问
send condolences = 发送慰问
deepest condolences = 最深切的慰问
