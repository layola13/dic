overconcentrated: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度集中的；过于浓缩的 |  Describes a substance that has a higher concentration than is desirable or necessary, or a situation where too much focus is placed on a single thing, leading to imbalance or negative consequences.  | Example: The overconcentrated solution damaged the equipment.  The overconcentrated effort on one project led to neglect of others. | 近义词: supersaturated, excessive, concentrated (to a fault) | 反义词: dilute, dispersed, balanced
