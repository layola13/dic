centimeter:/ˈsɛntɪmiːtər/| n. |厘米|A unit of length equal to one hundredth of a meter.|The table is 100 centimeters long.|近义词：cm|反义词:无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ruler measures 30 centimeters. = 这把尺子长30厘米。
This book is 2 centimeters thick. = 这本书厚2厘米。


一般过去时:
The plant grew 5 centimeters last week. = 植物上周长了5厘米。
He measured the fabric in centimeters. = 他用厘米测量了布料。


现在完成时:
The plant has grown 10 centimeters this month. = 这个月植物已经长了10厘米。
She has already cut the cloth to the correct number of centimeters. = 她已经把布裁剪到正确的厘米数了。


过去完成时:
The tree had grown 20 centimeters before the storm. = 在暴风雨来临之前，这棵树已经长了20厘米。
We had already measured the room in centimeters before starting the project. = 我们在开始项目之前已经用厘米测量了房间。


将来时:
The seedling will grow several centimeters in a few days. = 这棵幼苗几天后会长出几厘米。
They will measure the distance in centimeters. = 他们将用厘米测量距离。


虚拟语气:
If the line were only 1 centimeter longer, it would be perfect. = 如果这条线再长1厘米，就完美了。
I wish the painting were only 10 centimeters wider. = 我希望这幅画再宽10厘米。


被动语态:
The length was measured in centimeters. = 长度是用厘米测量的。
The fabric was cut to the specified number of centimeters. = 布料被裁剪到指定的厘米数。


疑问句:
How many centimeters long is the rope? = 这根绳子有多长（厘米）？
What is the height of the building in centimeters? = 这座建筑物的高度是多少厘米？


否定句:
The distance is not 10 centimeters. = 距离不是10厘米。
The box is not long enough, it needs to be 5 more centimeters. = 这个盒子不够长，还需要5厘米。
