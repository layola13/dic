inhibitable: /ɪnˈhɪbɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可抑制的，可阻止的 | Capable of being inhibited or prevented.  Something that is inhibitable can be restrained, suppressed, or prevented from occurring or developing. | The growth of the fungus was inhibitable by the application of this chemical.  这种化学物质的应用可以抑制真菌的生长。|  suppressible, preventable, controllable | unsuppressible, unstoppable, irreversible | 形容词
