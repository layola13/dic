chatoyance:/ʃæˈtɔɪəns/| n. |猫眼效应，变彩效应 |A chatoyance is a phenomenon where a gemstone or other material shows a band of light that seems to move as the stone is turned. This effect is caused by the internal structure of the material, typically fibrous or parallel arrangements of inclusions.  |The emerald showed a beautiful chatoyance in the light. 这颗祖母绿在灯光下展现出美丽的猫眼效应。|iridescence, sheen |dullness|名词
