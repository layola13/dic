/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ | vt.  | 传达；沟通；交流 | To convey information or express oneself in a way that is understandable to others.  It involves the exchange of information, ideas, or feelings. | He communicated his concerns to the manager. 他向经理表达了他的担忧。|  convey, transmit, express | obstruct, hinder, silence | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He communicates effectively in meetings. 他在会议上有效地沟通。
They communicate regularly via email. 他们定期通过电子邮件沟通。

一般过去时:
She communicated her decision to the team yesterday. 她昨天向团队传达了她的决定。
We communicated poorly and there was a misunderstanding. 我们沟通不畅，造成了误解。

现在完成时:
He has communicated his intentions clearly. 他已经清楚地表达了他的意图。
They have communicated with the client several times. 他们已经与客户沟通了几次。

过去完成时:
She had communicated her concerns before the meeting started. 会议开始前，她已经表达了她的担忧。
They had communicated their plan to the stakeholders. 他们已经向利益相关者传达了他们的计划。

将来时:
He will communicate his findings in the report. 他将在报告中传达他的发现。
They will communicate the updates to all staff. 他们将把最新情况告知所有员工。

虚拟语气:
If he communicated better, there would be fewer misunderstandings. 如果他沟通更好，误解就会少一些。
I wish he communicated more openly. 我希望他更坦诚地沟通。

被动语态:
The information was communicated effectively to the audience. 信息有效地传达给了听众。
The decision was communicated to the employees yesterday. 决定昨天传达给了员工。

疑问句:
How does he communicate with his colleagues? 他如何与同事沟通？
Did she communicate her needs adequately? 她是否充分表达了她的需求？

否定句:
He didn't communicate his feelings well. 他没有很好地表达他的感受。
They failed to communicate the importance of the deadline. 他们未能传达截止日期的重要性。


PHRASE->

communicate with sb. 与某人沟通
communicate a message 传达信息
means of communication 沟通方式
