glimmering: /ˈɡlɪmərɪŋ/ | adj. | 微弱闪光，闪烁的 | 发出微弱而闪烁的光的；隐约可见的 | The glimmering lights of the city were visible from afar. 城市的微弱灯光从远处可见。 | 近义词：shining, sparkling, twinkling, shimmering | 反义词：dull, dark, opaque


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The stars are glimmering in the night sky. = 星星在夜空中闪烁。
The distant lights glimmer faintly. = 远处的灯光微弱地闪烁。


一般过去时:
The candles glimmered softly in the darkness. = 蜡烛在黑暗中柔和地闪烁。
The gold glimmered in the sunlight. = 金子在阳光下闪闪发光。


现在完成时:
The city has been glimmering with festive lights. = 城市一直闪烁着节日的灯光。
The memory has glimmered faintly in my mind. = 这段记忆在我的脑海中若隐若现。


过去完成时:
The sun had glimmered through the clouds before the storm broke. = 暴风雨来临之前，阳光曾透过云层闪烁。
The distant lights had glimmered, promising a town ahead. = 远处的灯光闪烁着，预示着前方有一个城镇。


将来时:
The stars will glimmer brightly tonight. = 今晚星星将会明亮地闪烁。
The city will glimmer with excitement during the festival. = 节日期间，城市将因兴奋而闪烁。


虚拟语气:
If the moon were glimmering, we could see the path. = 如果月亮在闪烁，我们就能看到路。
I wish the stars were glimmering more brightly. = 我希望星星能更明亮地闪烁。


被动语态:
The distant hills were glimmered with snow. = 远处的山丘闪烁着白雪。  (Note: This usage is less common; active voice is preferred)
The lights were glimmering throughout the night. = 灯光整夜闪烁着。(Note: This is a more natural passive construction)


疑问句:
Are the stars glimmering tonight? = 今晚星星在闪烁吗？
Is the distant light glimmering? = 远处的灯光在闪烁吗？


否定句:
The stars weren't glimmering brightly last night. = 昨晚星星没有明亮地闪烁。
The distant lights didn't glimmer at all. = 远处的灯光根本没有闪烁。


PHRASE->
a glimmer of hope = 一线希望

glimmering surface = 闪烁的表面
