potful:/ˈpɒtfʊl/ | n. | 一满锅；满满一锅 | A potful is the amount that fills a pot.  It often implies a large quantity. | Example: She made a potful of delicious stew. 她做了一大锅美味的炖菜。 |近义词:  a pot's worth, a large quantity | 反义词: a small amount, a little


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It only takes singular and plural forms.

PHRASE->
a potful of soup 一大锅汤
a potful of flowers  一满瓶花
a potful of money  一大笔钱
a potful of problems  一大堆问题
a potful of trouble  一大堆麻烦
