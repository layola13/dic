Episcopalianism emphasizes the historical continuity of the Church, the authority of Scripture and Tradition, and the importance of sacramental life. |例句：His family's deep commitment to Episcopalianism shaped his religious views.  他的家庭对圣公会教义的坚定信仰塑造了他的宗教观点。|近义词：Anglicanism, high church |反义词：Nonconformity, dissent
