morphological:/ˌmɔːrfəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌmɔːrfəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 形态学的；形态的 | Relating to the form and structure of things.  The study of morphology examines the forms and structures of words and their components.  It deals with the internal structure of words, how they are formed, and how they relate to other words. | Example:  The morphological analysis of the word "unbreakable" reveals its prefixes and suffixes. = 对单词“unbreakable”的形态学分析揭示了它的前缀和后缀。


The morphological analysis of the sentence revealed interesting patterns. = 对句子的形态学分析揭示了有趣的模式。


近义词：structural, formal | 反义词：semantic (in the context of linguistic analysis)


词性：形容词
