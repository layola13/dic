rewagering: /ˌriːˈweɪdʒərɪŋ/ | v. | 再下注，重新下注 | The act of placing a bet again, often on the same event or outcome.  It typically implies a previous bet has already been placed. | Example:  The casino encouraged rewagering of winnings by offering bonuses.  赌场通过提供奖金鼓励将奖金再次下注。 | 近义词：rebetting, re-staking | 反义词：cashing out, collecting winnings
