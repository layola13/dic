romekin:/ˈroʊməkɪn/| n. |小烤盘，小砂锅|A small, shallow, oven-safe dish, typically made of ceramic or stoneware, used for baking individual portions of food, such as gratins, soufflés, or custards.|例句：I baked individual apple crumbles in small ramekins. 我在小烤盘里烤了单个的苹果碎。
例句：She served the mousse in elegant little ramekins. 她用精致的小烤盘盛放慕斯。|近义词：baking dish, oven dish, gratin dish|反义词:large baking pan.
