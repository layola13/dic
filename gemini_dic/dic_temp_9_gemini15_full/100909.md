intumesce:/ɪnˈtuːmɛs/| v. |膨胀；肿胀；隆起 |To swell or become swollen.  The word often implies a gradual or progressive swelling, rather than a sudden one. |例句：The injured limb began to intumesce after the accident. 事故后，受伤的肢体开始肿胀。

The bread dough intumesced in the warm oven.  温暖的烤箱里，面包面团膨胀起来了。


近义词：swell, bulge, distend, inflate |反义词：deflate, shrink, contract |词性：动词
