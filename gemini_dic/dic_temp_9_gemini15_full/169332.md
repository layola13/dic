promotional:/prəˈmoʊʃənəl/ | adj. | 促销的；推广的 | Relating to or intended for promotion or publicity.  It describes something designed to advertise or increase sales of a product or service. | Example: The company launched a promotional campaign to boost sales. 这家公司发起了一场促销活动来提升销量。  |近义词：advertising, publicity, marketing |反义词：unadvertised, unpublicized


USAGE->

The store offers many promotional discounts. = 这家商店提供许多促销折扣。
This product enjoys significant promotional activities. = 这款产品享有大量的促销活动。
