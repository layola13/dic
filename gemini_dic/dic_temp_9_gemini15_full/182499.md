renovel:/rɪˈnɒvəl/| n.|翻新，修缮；小说|The act of renovating something, especially a building or other structure; a new or renewed version of something;  a novel.|例句：The renovation of the old house took six months. 老房子的翻修花了六个月时间。|例句：This is a novelization of the popular movie. 这是一个热门电影的小说改编。|近义词：renovation, refurbishment, restoration, novelisation|反义词：destruction, demolition


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel renovates its rooms every year. = 这家酒店每年都会翻新客房。
He renovates old cars as a hobby. = 他把翻修旧车作为爱好。


一般过去时:
They renovated the kitchen last summer. = 他们去年夏天装修了厨房。
She renovated her old apartment. = 她翻修了她旧的公寓。


现在完成时:
We have renovated our bathroom twice. = 我们已经两次装修了我们的浴室。
They have renovated several buildings in the city center. = 他们已经翻新了市中心几座建筑。


过去完成时:
They had renovated the house before they moved in. = 他们搬进去之前已经装修了房子。
She had renovated her store before the grand opening. = 她在大张旗鼓开业之前已经翻修了她的商店。


将来时:
We will renovate the garden next spring. = 我们明年春天将翻修花园。
They will renovate the entire building. = 他们将翻修整栋建筑。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would renovate the whole house. = 如果我们有更多的钱，我们会翻修整栋房子。
I wish we could renovate our basement. = 我希望我们能装修我们的地下室。


被动语态:
The building is being renovated at the moment. = 这栋建筑正在被翻修。
The rooms were renovated last month. = 房间在上个月被翻修了。


疑问句:
Are they renovating the library? = 他们正在翻修图书馆吗？
When will the house be renovated? = 房子什么时候会翻修？


否定句:
They didn't renovate the bathroom. = 他们没有装修浴室。
We haven't renovated the house yet. = 我们还没有装修房子。
