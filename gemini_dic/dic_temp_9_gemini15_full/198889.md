sidewinder:/ˈsaɪdwɪndər/| n. |侧行蛇；响尾蛇的一种|A sidewinder is a type of rattlesnake that moves by throwing its body into a series of S-shaped curves, pushing off the ground with its lower body and pulling its upper body forward.  They are found in deserts of southwestern North America.| Example: The sidewinder slithered silently through the sand.  侧行蛇无声地穿过沙地。 The desert was home to many sidewinders and other reptiles.  沙漠是许多侧行蛇和其他爬行动物的家园。|近义词：rattlesnake (in the context of a specific type)|反义词：无|名词
