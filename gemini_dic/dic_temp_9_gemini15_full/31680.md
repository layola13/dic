chauffeured:/ʃoʊfərd/| adj.|有司机的；配备司机的|Having a chauffeur; provided with a chauffeur.  The adjective form of "chauffeur." It describes something that has or is accompanied by a chauffeur.|例句：He arrived in a chauffeured limousine. 他乘坐一辆有司机的豪华轿车到达。

例句：They enjoyed a chauffeured tour of the city. 他们享受了一次有司机导游的城市之旅。


近义词：driven, with a driver


反义词：self-driven, driverless


词性：形容词
