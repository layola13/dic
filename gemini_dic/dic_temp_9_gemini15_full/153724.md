pepper-up: /ˈpɛpər ʌp/ | vt. | 使振作；使兴奋；改进；增强 | To make someone or something more lively, energetic, or effective.  | 例句：We need to pepper-up the presentation to make it more engaging. 我们需要改进演示文稿，使之更引人入胜。


例句：The coach gave a pep talk to pepper-up the team before the game. 教练在比赛前发表了一番鼓舞人心的讲话来激励球队。


近义词：invigorate, enliven, stimulate, boost, enhance


反义词：depress, discourage, dampen, weaken, subdue


词性：动词
