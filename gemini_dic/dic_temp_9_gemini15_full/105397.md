kiddo:/ˈkɪdoʊ/| n. |孩子，小子，小家伙 |A term of endearment for a child.  It's an informal and affectionate way to address a child or young person. |例句：Come here, kiddo, let's have some ice cream. 来吧，孩子，咱们吃点冰淇淋。  Hey kiddo, how was school today?  嘿，小子，今天上学怎么样？|近义词：child, youngster, kid, little one|反义词:adult, grown-up
