codevelops:/ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləps/| v.|共同开发，合作开发|To develop something jointly or in collaboration.  It implies a shared effort and responsibility in the development process.|例句：The two companies codevelops a new software application. = 两家公司共同开发了一个新的软件应用程序。
The researchers codeveloped a novel treatment for the disease. = 研究人员共同开发了一种治疗该疾病的新疗法。|近义词：collaborate on, jointly develop, co-create|反义词：develop independently, separately develop|动词
