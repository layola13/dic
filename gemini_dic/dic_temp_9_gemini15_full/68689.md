farcing:/ˈfɑːrsɪŋ/ | n. | 填料；填充物 | Stuffing or filling used in cooking, often made of bread, herbs, and meat.  | Example: The turkey was stuffed with a delicious sausage and herb forcing. 这只火鸡塞满了美味的香肠和香草馅料。 |近义词：stuffing, filling | 反义词：emptying

词源:
The word "farcing" comes from the Old French word "farcir," meaning "to stuff."  This, in turn, has roots in the Latin word "farcire," also meaning "to stuff."
