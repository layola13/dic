phyllo:/ˈfɪloʊ/| n. |菲洛面团，千层酥皮| Thin, flaky pastry used in many cuisines, especially in the Middle East and the Mediterranean.  It's made from layers of very thin dough brushed with oil or butter.  |例句：The baklava was made with delicate phyllo pastry. 这块巴克勒娃是用精致的菲洛面团做的。

例句：She carefully layered the phyllo sheets for her spanakopita. 她仔细地将菲洛面皮一层层叠起来做菠菜馅饼。

近义词：pastry, dough (in the context of thin, layered pastry)

反义词：无 (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与厚实的、不层状的面团相对比)

词性：名词
