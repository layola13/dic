marls:/mɑːrlz/| n.|泥灰岩，泥灰土|A marl is a type of soil or sediment that is composed of a mixture of clay and calcium carbonate.  It is often used in agriculture as a soil amendment because it improves soil structure and fertility.|例句：The farmer added marl to the soil to improve drainage and increase its fertility.  这位农民往土壤里添加了泥灰岩以改善排水并提高肥力。|近义词：clay, loam, calcareous soil|反义词：sandy soil, gravelly soil
