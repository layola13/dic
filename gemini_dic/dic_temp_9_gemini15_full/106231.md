know:/noʊ/ |/nəʊ/| v.|知道，了解，认识|To have knowledge or information about something; to be aware of something.| I know the answer. 我知道答案。|She knows him well. 她很了解他。|understand, comprehend, recognize|ignore, forget, misunderstand|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I know the answer. = 我知道答案。
Do you know her name? = 你知道她的名字吗？

一般过去时:
I knew the answer yesterday. = 昨天我知道答案。
Did you know him before? = 你以前认识他吗？

现在完成时:
I have known him for ten years. = 我认识他十年了。
Have you known her long? = 你认识她很久了吗？

过去完成时:
I had known him before the incident. = 在那件事发生之前我就认识他了。
Had you known the truth before? = 你以前知道真相吗？

将来时:
I will know the results tomorrow. = 我明天会知道结果。
Will you know the answer by then? = 到那时你会知道答案吗？


虚拟语气:
If I knew the answer, I would tell you. = 如果我知道答案，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what to do. = 我希望我知道该做什么。


被动语态:
The answer is known to everyone. = 答案众所周知。
It is known that he is honest. = 他是诚实的，这是众所周知的。


疑问句:
Do you know him? = 你认识他吗？
What do you know about this? = 你对此了解多少？


否定句:
I don't know the answer. = 我不知道答案。
She didn't know what to say. = 她不知道该说什么。



PHRASE->
know better = 应该知道好坏
know-how = 专业知识；技能
know for sure = 确切知道
in the know = 了解内情的人
let me know = 告诉我
