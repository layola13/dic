reconvicted:/ˌriːkənˈvɪktɪd/ | adj. | 再定罪的，再次被判罪的 |  Having been convicted again after a previous conviction.  This term describes someone who has been found guilty of a crime after previously being convicted of another crime. | Example:  He was reconvicted on similar charges five years later. 他五年后因类似罪名再次被判罪。  | 近义词:  re-condemned,  re-sentenced | 反义词: acquitted, exonerated
