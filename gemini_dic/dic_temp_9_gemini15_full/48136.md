decollation:/ˌdiːkəˈleɪʃən/| n. |斩首；处决|The act of beheading; decapitation.  It often refers to a formal or ritualistic beheading, particularly as a form of capital punishment.|Example: The decollation of the king marked the end of the dynasty.  国王的斩首标志着王朝的终结。The prisoner faced decollation at dawn. 囚犯面临黎明时的斩首。|近义词：decapitation, beheading |反义词： None directly opposite, but perhaps "preservation of life"
