sills:/sɪlz/| n.|窗台；门槛；基石；（尤指）地基|A sill is a horizontal piece of wood or stone that forms the bottom of a window or door frame, or a similar piece at the base of something.  It can also refer to the lower part of a dam, or a geological threshold. |例句：The cat was sitting on the window sill.  猫坐在窗台上。

The old house had deep window sills perfect for growing herbs. 这栋老房子有很深的窗台，非常适合种植药草。


近义词：threshold, ledge, base, foundation|反义词：top, summit, apex|名词
