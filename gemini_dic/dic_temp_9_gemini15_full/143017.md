outbirth:/ˈaʊtbɜːrθ/| n. |诞生，起源；产生|The outbirth of a new star is a spectacular event.| 近义词：origin, birth, emergence | 反义词：death, end


英文解析:Outbirth refers to the act or process of something coming into existence or being born, especially in a striking or significant way. It emphasizes the emergence or beginning of something new.


例句:

The outbirth of modern physics was marked by Einstein's theory of relativity.  = 现代物理学的诞生以爱因斯坦的相对论为标志。

The novel explores the outbirth of a new social movement. = 这部小说探讨了一个新的社会运动的产生。


近义词：origin, birth, emergence, genesis, creation


反义词：death, demise, end, termination, extinction


词性：名词 (noun)
