damnations:/dæmˈneɪʃənz/| n.|诅咒；谴责；下地狱；毁灭|The act of damning or the state of being damned; condemnation to eternal punishment; utter ruin or destruction.  |例句：His actions brought upon him the damnations of the entire community.他的行为招致了整个社区的谴责。

例句：The damnations of war are far-reaching and devastating.战争的毁灭性影响深远而具有破坏性。


近义词：condemnation, damnation, perdition, ruin, destruction

反义词：blessing, salvation, redemption


词性:名词
