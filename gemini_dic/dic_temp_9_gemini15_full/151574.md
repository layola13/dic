partaking:/pɑːrˈteɪkɪŋ/|/ˈpɑːrteɪkɪŋ/| v.|参与；分享；享用|The act of taking part in something;  sharing in something, especially food or drink.|例句：She is partaking in the festivities. 她正在参加庆祝活动。

例句：We partook of a delicious meal together. 我们一起享用了一顿美味的晚餐。


近义词：participating, sharing, enjoying, consuming

反义词：excluding, abstaining, refusing


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He partakes in many extracurricular activities. 他参加许多课外活动。
They partake of the holy communion. 他们领圣餐。


一般过去时:

They had partaken in numerous discussions before reaching a decision. 在做出决定之前，他们进行了多次讨论。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
The food was partaken of by everyone.  食物被大家享用掉了。 (Note: Passive voice is less common with "partake")

疑问句:

否定句:

PHRASE->
partake in: 参加
partake of: 分享；享用
partake fully: 全力以赴地参与
partake sparingly: 节制地享用
partake freely: 自由自在地享用


词源:
The word "partaking" comes from the Old French "part," meaning "part," and the verb "taken," ultimately deriving from the Proto-Germanic "*taukan," meaning "to touch, take."  The combination reflects the act of taking part or a share in something.
