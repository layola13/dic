inhumanly: /ɪnˈhjuːmənli/ | adv. | 不人道地；残忍地；极其；非常 |  In a manner that is cruel, brutal, or lacking in human compassion or feeling.  It can also be used to describe something done to an extreme degree. | 例句：The prisoners were treated inhumanly. 囚犯们受到了不人道的待遇。

The dictator ruled inhumanly, showing no mercy to his people. 独裁者统治残暴，对人民毫无怜悯之心。


近义词：cruelly, barbarously, savagely, brutally, terribly, exceedingly, extremely | 反义词：humanely, kindly, compassionately, mercifully
