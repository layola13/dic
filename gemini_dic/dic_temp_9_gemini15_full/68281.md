falafel:/fəˈlæfəl/ | n. | 鹰嘴豆泥炸丸子 | Deep-fried balls or patties made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, herbs, and spices.  Falafel are a popular Middle Eastern street food, often served in pita bread with various toppings. |  I had a falafel sandwich for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个鹰嘴豆泥炸丸子三明治。


The falafel were crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.  这些鹰嘴豆泥炸丸子外酥里嫩。| 近义词：chickpea fritter, bean patty | 反义词：无
