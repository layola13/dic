retreatingness:/rɪˈtriːtɪŋnəs/| n. |退却；撤退；退缩；衰退 |The state or quality of retreating; a tendency to retreat or withdraw.  It often implies a gradual or hesitant withdrawal, rather than a sudden or decisive one.  The word suggests a lack of assertiveness or confidence. |例句：The general's retreatingness surprised his troops.  将军的退却让他的军队感到意外。

The company's retreatingness in the face of competition led to its eventual downfall. 公司面对竞争时的退缩导致了最终的倒闭。|近义词：withdrawal, recession, shrinkage, backpedaling, hesitancy|反义词：advancement, aggression, assertiveness, boldness
