common:/ˈkɑːmən/ /ˈkɒmən/ | adj. | 普通的；常见的；共同的 |  Common refers to something that occurs frequently or is widely shared. It can describe things that are ordinary, usual, or prevalent.  | This is a common problem. 这是一个常见的问题。|The common cold is a viral infection. 普通感冒是一种病毒感染。| usual, ordinary, frequent, prevalent | rare, unusual, uncommon, exceptional |形容词
