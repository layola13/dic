oldish:/ˈoʊldɪʃ/| adj.|有点老的，稍显老的|Slightly old; somewhat aged.  The word suggests a degree of age beyond youth but not necessarily very old. It often implies a subjective judgment rather than a precise chronological age.|例句：He's getting a bit oldish, but he's still very active. 他有点老了，但他仍然非常活跃。

例句：Her dress is a bit oldish for this party. 她的衣服对于这个派对来说有点老气了。


近义词：elderly, aging, mature, senescent

反义词：young, youthful, juvenile


词性:形容词
