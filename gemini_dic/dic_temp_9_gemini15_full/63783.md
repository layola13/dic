epicurean:/ˌɛpɪˈkʊəriən/| adj. n. |享乐主义的；美食家的；精致的；| relating to or characteristic of Epicurus or Epicureanism; devoted to pleasure, especially to the enjoyment of good food and drink.|例句：He led an epicurean lifestyle, indulging in fine wines and gourmet meals. 他过着享乐主义的生活，沉迷于美酒佳肴。  He is known for his epicurean tastes in food. 他以其对美食的精致品味而闻名。|近义词：hedonistic, sybaritic, luxurious|反义词：ascetic, austere, frugal
