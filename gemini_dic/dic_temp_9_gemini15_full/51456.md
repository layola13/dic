detonated:/ˈdɛtəneɪtɪd/ | v. |引爆；使爆炸；突然爆发|To cause (something, especially an explosive device) to explode.  It can also refer to a sudden and violent outburst.|The bomb detonated at noon.  炸弹在中午爆炸了。 The news of the disaster detonated a wave of grief across the country.  灾难的消息在全国引发了悲伤的浪潮。| exploded, triggered, ignited | imploded, defused |
