organizer:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzər/ | n. |组织者，主办者，整理者 | A person or thing that organizes something.  It can refer to a person who plans and manages an event, a system or device used for organizing things, or a person who keeps things tidy and in order. | Examples:  She is the organizer of the annual charity gala. 她是一位年度慈善晚会的组织者。 He uses a digital organizer to manage his appointments. 他用电子记事本管理约会。 |近义词：planner, coordinator, manager, arranger |反义词：disorganizer (less common), chaotic person
