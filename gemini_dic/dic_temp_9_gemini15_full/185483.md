reuniters:/ˌriːˈjuːnɪtərz/| n. |重聚者，使重聚的人或事物 |People or things that reunite others.  The reunion itself can also be considered a reuniter. This word is less common and often used in a figurative or specific context.  |Example: The old photographs served as reuniters for the long-lost siblings.  老照片成了失散多年的兄弟姐妹重聚的媒介。


The family trip was a wonderful reuniter for the whole family.  这次家庭旅行让全家人开心重聚。 |近义词:reconcilers, connectors|反义词:separators, dividers|名词
