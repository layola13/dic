oversoaking:/ˌoʊvərˈsoʊkɪŋ/ | n. |过度浸泡；浸泡过度 |The act of soaking something for too long, resulting in damage or undesirable consequences.  |例句：Oversoaking the seeds led to their rotting. 过度浸泡种子导致它们腐烂。

例句：The oversoaking of the soil caused the plants to wilt. 土壤过度浸泡导致植物枯萎。


近义词：waterlogging, saturation, drenching |反义词：underwatering, drying


词性：名词
