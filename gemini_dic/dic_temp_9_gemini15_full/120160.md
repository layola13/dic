mind-blowingly: /ˈmaɪndˌbloʊɪŋli/ | adv. |令人震惊地，令人难以置信地 | Extremely surprising or impressive; astonishing.  | 例句：The special effects in the movie were mind-blowingly good.  这部电影的特效好得令人难以置信。

The view from the mountaintop was mind-blowingly beautiful. 山顶的景色美得令人叹为观止。


近义词：amazingly, incredibly, astonishingly, unbelievably, spectacularly | 反义词：unremarkably, underwhelmingly, predictably
