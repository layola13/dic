reweakening:/ˌriːˈwiːkənɪŋ/| v. | 重新削弱；再次减弱 | The reweakening of the economy is a serious concern. 经济的再次衰弱令人担忧。|近义词：weakening, deterioration, decline |反义词：strengthening, improvement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is reweakening its stance on immigration. = 政府正在重新软化其关于移民的立场。
This policy is reweakening the national defense. =这项政策正在削弱国防力量。


一般过去时:
The storm reweakened the already damaged bridge. = 暴风雨再次削弱了已经受损的桥梁。
The company's profits reweakened after a period of growth. = 公司在一段时间的增长后利润再次下降。


现在完成时:
The repeated storms have reweakened the coastline. = 反复的暴风雨已经削弱了海岸线。
Years of drought have reweakened the agricultural sector. = 多年的干旱已经削弱了农业部门。


过去完成时:
The economy had reweakened before the new measures were implemented. = 在新措施实施之前，经济已经再次衰弱。
His health had reweakened after a period of improvement. = 他的健康状况在好转了一段时间后再次恶化。


将来时:
Analysts predict that the market will reweaken next quarter. = 分析师预测市场将在下个季度再次走弱。
The prolonged conflict will likely reweaken the region's infrastructure. = 长期的冲突很可能会再次削弱该地区的基础设施。


虚拟语气:
If the trade war continued, it would reweaken the global economy. = 如果贸易战继续下去，它将会再次削弱全球经济。
I wish the government hadn't reweakened environmental regulations. = 我真希望政府没有再次削弱环境法规。


被动语态:
The treaty was reweakened by the recent political turmoil. = 最近的政治动荡削弱了该条约。
The foundation of the building was reweakened by the earthquake. = 建筑的地基在地震中再次被削弱。


疑问句:
Has the recent scandal reweakened public trust in the government? = 最近的丑闻是否削弱了公众对政府的信任？
Will the new economic policies reweaken the competitiveness of domestic businesses? = 新的经济政策会削弱国内企业的竞争力吗？


否定句:
The new regulations did not reweaken the company's market position. = 新规并没有削弱公司的市场地位。
The government's efforts did not reweaken the opposition's influence. = 政府的努力并没有削弱反对派的势力。
