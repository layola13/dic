overqualifies: /ˌoʊvərˈkwɑːlɪfaɪz/ | v. |  使过度胜任；资历过高 | To be excessively qualified for a particular job or position.  This implies that the person's skills and experience far exceed the requirements of the role, potentially leading to boredom, dissatisfaction, or a mismatch between the individual and the job. |


例句：

He overqualifies for the entry-level position; his resume boasts years of experience in the field. = 他资历过高，不适合这个入门级职位；他的简历上炫耀着多年行业经验。

She overqualifies for the administrative assistant role, having previously managed a department of twenty. = 她拥有二十人部门管理经验，担任行政助理职位资历过高。


近义词：excessively qualified, overskilled, hyper-qualified


反义词：underqualified, lacking experience


词性: 动词
