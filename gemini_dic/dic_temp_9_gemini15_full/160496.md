porchless: /ˈpɔrtʃləs/ | adj. | 无门廊的 | Lacking a porch.  The term describes a building or house that does not have a porch or veranda.  It is formed by adding the negative prefix "porch-" to the suffix "-less".

例句：
The porchless house looked stark against the backdrop of the lush garden. =  这栋没有门廊的房子与茂盛的花园形成鲜明对比。

The architect designed a porchless modern home to maximize natural light. = 建筑师设计了一栋没有门廊的现代化住宅，以最大限度地利用自然光。


近义词：verandaless, stoa-less (less formal)

反义词：porched, verandaed


词性：形容词
