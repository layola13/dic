pseudoconfessional:/ˌsuːdəʊkənˈfɛʃənəl/| adj.|似忏悔的；伪忏悔的 | seemingly confessional but not genuinely so; giving the impression of a confession but lacking sincerity or genuine self-revelation.  |例句：His pseudoconfessional memoir glossed over his more unsavory actions.  他的伪忏悔回忆录轻描淡写地处理了他更不光彩的行为。


例句：The novel's pseudoconfessional style felt manipulative and ultimately unconvincing. 这部小说的似忏悔风格感觉具有操纵性，最终令人难以信服。


近义词：affected, feigned, disingenuous, insincere, self-serving


反义词：genuine, sincere, heartfelt, authentic, truthful


词性：形容词
