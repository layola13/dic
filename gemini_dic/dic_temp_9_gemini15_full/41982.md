cost-cutting:/ˈkɒstˌkʌtɪŋ/|/ˈkɑːstˌkʌtɪŋ/| n. |削减成本；节约开支 |The process of reducing expenses in a business or organization.  This often involves identifying areas of waste and inefficiency and implementing strategies to eliminate them.  |例句：The company implemented a strict cost-cutting program to improve profitability. 公司实施了严格的成本削减计划以提高盈利能力。

The government's cost-cutting measures led to widespread protests. 政府的削减成本措施引发了大规模抗议。|近义词：budget cuts, economy measures, expense reduction |反义词：increased spending, extravagance
