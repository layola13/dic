handrail:/ˈhændreɪl/| n.|扶手，栏杆|A handrail is a rail that people can hold onto for support, typically found along staircases, ramps, or other elevated areas.  It provides stability and prevents falls.|例句：The handrail was cold and metallic to the touch.  扶手摸起来冰冷而坚硬。

The old house had a beautifully carved wooden handrail on the staircase. 老房子楼梯上有一个雕刻精美的木制扶手。|近义词：banister, railing, balustrade|反义词：None
