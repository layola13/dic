penalty:/ˈpɛnəlti/| n.|处罚，惩罚|A penalty is a punishment imposed for a violation of a rule or law.  It can be a fine, imprisonment, or other forms of punishment.|例句：He received a penalty for speeding. 他因超速而受到处罚。|例句：The penalty for cheating on the exam was expulsion. 考试作弊的惩罚是开除。|近义词：punishment, sanction, fine|反义词：reward, prize

一般现在时:
The penalty for lateness is a deduction from your pay. = 迟到的惩罚是从你的工资中扣除。
A heavy penalty awaits those who break the law. = 违法者将受到严厉的惩罚。

一般过去时:
The judge imposed a severe penalty on the criminal. = 法官对罪犯处以严厉的惩罚。
They paid a penalty for their mistakes. = 他们为他们的错误付出了代价。

现在完成时:
He has already paid the penalty for his actions. = 他已经为他所做的行为付出了代价。
She has incurred a penalty for non-payment of taxes. = 她因未缴税而受到了处罚。

过去完成时:
By the time he finished the race, he had already accumulated several penalties. = 等他完成比赛时，他已经累积了好几项处罚了。
They had incurred a penalty before the game even started. = 比赛还没开始，他们就已经受到处罚了。

将来时:
You will face a penalty if you violate the contract. = 如果你违反合同，你将面临处罚。
They will likely receive a penalty for their unethical behaviour. = 他们可能会因为不道德的行为而受到处罚。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
