provabilities:/ˌprɒbəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |可能性；概率|The word "provabilities" is not a standard English word.  It seems to be an attempt to make the word "probability" plural. The correct plural form is "probabilities." Probabilities refer to the chances or likelihood of something happening.  It is a measure of the likelihood of an event occurring. |例句：The probabilities of winning the lottery are extremely low. 彩票中奖的概率极低。The probabilities of success are high if we work hard. 如果我们努力工作，成功的概率很高。|近义词：chances, likelihoods, possibilities|反义词：impossibilities, certainties
