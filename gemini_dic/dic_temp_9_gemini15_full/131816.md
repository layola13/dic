nonconsequentiality: /ˌnɒnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃiˈælɪti/ | n. | 非必然性；不必然性；无关性 | The lack of a necessary connection between events or ideas; the state of being inconsequential.  It refers to a situation where one thing does not logically follow from another, or where there's a lack of causal relationship. | The nonconsequentiality of his actions baffled everyone; there seemed to be no logical connection between his decisions and their outcomes.  他的行为的非必然性让每个人都感到困惑；他的决定和结果之间似乎没有逻辑上的联系。 | inconsequentiality, irrelevance, randomness | causality, consequence, relevance | 名词
