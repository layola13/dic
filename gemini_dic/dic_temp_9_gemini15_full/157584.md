pitchstone:/ˈpɪtʃstoʊn/| n.|沥青岩|A dark-colored, glassy volcanic rock with a smooth, conchoidal fracture. It is usually composed of volcanic glass and small crystals.  It's often obsidian with a slightly less glassy appearance.|The ancient tool was made from a dark grey pitchstone.|近义词：obsidian, volcanic glass|反义词：无
