reemitted:/ˌriːɪˈmɪtɪd/| v. |再发射；重新发出；再次散发|  To emit again; to send out or release again.  This word describes the process of something being emitted a second time, after an initial emission.  It often refers to light, radiation, or other forms of energy. |例句：The satellite reemitted the signal after a brief interruption.  卫星在短暂中断后重新发出了信号。

The infrared light was reemitted by the surface of the object.  该物体的表面重新发射了红外线。


近义词：re-radiated, re-released, emitted again | 反义词：absorbed, retained|  动词
