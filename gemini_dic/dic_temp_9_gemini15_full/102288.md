itwin:/ˈaɪtwɪn/| n.|数字孪生|A digital twin is a virtual representation of a real-world object, process, or system.  It uses data from various sources to create a dynamic and interactive model that can be used for simulation, analysis, and prediction.|Example: The engineers created a digital twin of the aircraft engine to test different operating conditions. 工程师们创建了一个飞机发动机的数字孪生模型来测试不同的运行条件。|近义词：virtual model, simulation, replica|反义词：physical object, real-world counterpart|名词
