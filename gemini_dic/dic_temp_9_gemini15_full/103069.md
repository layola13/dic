jenneting:/ˈdʒenɪtɪŋ/| n. |珍妮丁苹果|A variety of early-season apple, known for its sweet and slightly tart flavor.  It is typically harvested in late summer or early autumn.  The name is believed to be derived from the French "genette," referring to a small, agile animal, possibly alluding to the apple's quick ripening.|例句：The jenneting apples are ready for picking; they're perfect for pies. 珍妮丁苹果已经可以采摘了；它们非常适合做馅饼。|近义词：early apple, summer apple|反义词：late apple, winter apple|名词
