goofily:/ˈɡuːfɪli/| adj. |傻乎乎地；滑稽地；笨拙地 |In a silly or clumsy way.  The word implies a charming or endearing silliness, rather than outright foolishness. |例句：He goofily grinned at her. 他傻乎乎地对她咧嘴一笑。

例句：She goofily tripped over the rug. 她笨拙地绊倒在地毯上。


近义词：sillly, clumsily, awkwardly,  dorkily


反义词：seriously, gracefully, skillfully, smartly


词性：形容词
