caricaturing:/ˈkærɪkətʃəriŋ/| v.|漫画式地描绘；讽刺地模仿；歪曲|The act of creating a caricature; representing someone or something in a way that exaggerates their features or characteristics, often for humorous or satirical effect.  It can involve a distorted or exaggerated representation of a person's appearance, personality, or ideas.|例句：He's caricaturing the politician's speech, making fun of his pompous style. 他正在漫画式地描绘这位政治家的演讲，嘲笑他傲慢的风格。|例句：The cartoon caricatures the mayor's policies. 这幅漫画讽刺地模仿了市长的政策。|近义词：satirizing, lampooning, parodying, burlesquing|反义词：realistically portraying, accurately representing
