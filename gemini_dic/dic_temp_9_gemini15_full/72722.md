fodders:/ˈfɒdəz/| n. |饲料，牲畜饲料 |  Food given to livestock. Fodder is typically plant material, such as hay, straw, or silage.  |The farmer stored enough fodder to feed his cattle through the winter.  农民储存了足够的饲料以供牛过冬。| feed, forage |  — |

The farmer feeds his cattle fodder every day. = 农民每天都喂牛饲料。
This type of grass makes excellent fodder. = 这种草是极好的饲料。

They fed the sheep a lot of fodder last week. = 他们上周喂羊很多饲料。
