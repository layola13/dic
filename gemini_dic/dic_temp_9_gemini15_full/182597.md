reoiled:/ˌriːˈɔɪld/ | v. | 再加油；重新加油 | To oil something again.  This word is the past participle of the verb "reoil". It describes an action completed in the past. |例句：The mechanic reoiled the engine after the leak was repaired. = 修理工在修理完漏油后又给发动机加了油。 The machine ran smoothly after being reoiled. = 机器重新加油后运行平稳。| 近义词：re-lubricated, lubricated again | 反义词：drained, depleted | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He re-oils the machine every week. = 他每周都给机器加油。
They reoil the car engine regularly. = 他们定期给汽车发动机加油。

一般过去时:
She reoiled the hinges yesterday. = 她昨天给铰链加了油。
The technician reoiled the equipment before use. = 技术员在使用前给设备加了油。

现在完成时:
I have reoiled the bicycle chain. = 我已经给自行车链条加了油。
They have reoiled the sewing machine several times this month. = 这个月他们已经给缝纫机加了好几次油了。

过去完成时:

The farmer had reoiled the tractor before the long day's work. = 农民在一天的长工之前已经给拖拉机加了油。
