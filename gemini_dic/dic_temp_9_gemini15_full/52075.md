dials:/ˈdaɪəlz/ | n. v. |拨号；刻度盘；表盘；|to turn a dial to select a radio or television channel or a telephone number; a graduated scale or plate indicating a reading or measurement.|例句：She dialed the number and waited for an answer. 她拨了号码并等待接听。 He carefully adjusted the dials on the machine. 他仔细地调整了机器上的刻度盘。|近义词：call, ring, select |反义词：hang up, disconnect |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dials the number carefully. = 他仔细地拨号。
She dials the TV channel every evening. = 她每天晚上都拨电视节目频道。


一般过去时:
I dialed the wrong number. = 我拨错了号码。
They dialed 911 for help. = 他们拨打911求助。


现在完成时:
She has dialed that number several times. = 她已经拨打过那个号码好几次了。
He has dialed in to the conference call. = 他已经加入了电话会议。


过去完成时:
I had dialed the number before realizing it was wrong. = 我在意识到拨错号码之前就已经拨了。
She had dialed the emergency services before the ambulance arrived. = 在救护车到达之前，她已经拨打了急救服务电话。


将来时:
I will dial the number again later. = 我过会儿会再拨打这个号码。
He will dial into the meeting from his office. = 他将从办公室拨入会议。


虚拟语气:
If I had dialed the correct number, she would have answered. = 如果我拨对了号码，她就会接电话。
I wish I had dialed the police sooner. = 我真希望我早点报警。


被动语态:
The number was dialed incorrectly. = 号码拨错了。
The wrong channel was dialed by mistake. = 错拨了频道。


疑问句:
Have you dialed the number yet? = 你拨打过号码了吗？
What number did you dial? = 你拨打了什么号码？


否定句:
He didn't dial the number correctly. = 他没有正确地拨打号码。
She didn't dial the emergency services. = 她没有拨打急救服务电话。


PHRASE->
dial a number = 拨打电话号码
dial in = 拨入（电话会议等）
