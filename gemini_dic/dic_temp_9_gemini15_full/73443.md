foremost:/ˈfɔːrmoʊst/| adj.|最重要的，最主要的；最先的|most important or leading; first in place, order, or importance.  |例句：He is foremost among the world's experts on this subject. 他是世界上关于这个主题最主要的专家之一。

例句：The foremost priority is to ensure the safety of the passengers. 最重要的优先事项是确保乘客的安全。


近义词：leading, principal, chief, primary, main, first, topmost


反义词：least important, last, secondary


词性：形容词
