genitive:/ˈdʒɛnɪtɪv/| adj.|属格的，所有格的| Relating to or denoting a grammatical case that marks a noun or pronoun as modifying another noun or pronoun, typically indicating possession, origin, or other relationship.|The genitive case is used to show possession or a close relationship between two nouns. For example, in the phrase "the dog's bone," "dog's" is in the genitive case. |近义词：possessive|反义词：nominative


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat's toy is under the table. = 猫的玩具在桌子底下。
The book's cover is damaged. = 书的封面坏了。


一般过去时:
The dog's collar was dirty. = 狗的项圈脏了。
The car's engine made a strange noise. = 汽车的引擎发出奇怪的声音。


现在完成时:
The child's drawing has been framed. = 孩子画的画已经装裱好了。
The team's performance has improved. = 球队的表现有所提高。


过去完成时:
The student's assignment had been submitted. = 学生的作业已经提交了。
The company's profits had decreased. = 公司的利润下降了。


将来时:
The project's deadline will be next week. = 项目的截止日期是下周。
The artist's exhibition will open soon. = 艺术家的展览很快就会开幕。


虚拟语气:
If the house's price were lower, I would buy it. = 如果房子的价格低一些，我会买它。
I wish the team's strategy were different. = 我希望球队的策略有所不同。


被动语态:
The building's design was criticized. = 建筑的设计受到了批评。
The company's future is being discussed. = 公司的未来正在被讨论。


疑问句:
Whose book is this? (Whose is the genitive case here) = 这是谁的书？
What is the dog's name? = 狗叫什么名字？


否定句:
The house's location wasn't ideal. = 房子的位置并不理想。
The company's plan wasn't successful. = 公司的计划并不成功。
