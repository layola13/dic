nonsurvival: /ˌnɒn.sɚˈvaɪ.vəl/ | n. |  非生存；死亡；不能生存 | The inability to survive; death or failure to survive.  This term is usually used in a scientific or technical context, often referring to the failure of an organism, system, or process to persist.  | Example: The high mortality rate indicates a high rate of nonsurvival.  高死亡率表明高非生存率。 The experiment demonstrated the nonsurvival of the bacteria in acidic conditions. 实验表明细菌在酸性条件下无法生存。 | 近义词：death, mortality, demise, perishing, extinction | 反义词：survival, persistence, endurance, viability |  n.
