gleaned:/ɡliːnd/ | v. | 收集，拾取（信息等）；了解，获悉 | To gather information or material bit by bit. | He gleaned valuable information from old documents. 他从旧文件中收集了宝贵的信息。 | gather, collect, acquire | discard, ignore | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gleans information from various sources. = 他从各种来源收集信息。
She gleans insights from her experiences. = 她从她的经验中获得见解。

一般过去时:
The researchers gleaned data from the survey. = 研究人员从调查中收集数据。
He gleaned a few facts from the newspaper. = 他从报纸上收集到一些事实。

现在完成时:
I have gleaned some useful tips from this book. = 我从这本书中收集到一些有用的技巧。
She has gleaned much wisdom from her travels. = 她从旅行中获得了许多智慧。

过去完成时:
They had gleaned all the necessary information before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经收集了所有必要的信息。
He had gleaned little from the cryptic message. = 他从神秘的信息中几乎一无所获。

将来时:
We will glean more details as the investigation progresses. = 随着调查的进展，我们将收集到更多细节。
They will glean valuable insights from the experiment. = 他们将从实验中获得宝贵的见解。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would glean more information from the archives. = 如果我有更多时间，我会从档案中收集更多信息。
I wish I could glean the meaning behind his cryptic remarks. = 我真希望我能理解他含糊其辞的话语背后的含义。

被动语态:
The information was gleaned from a variety of sources. = 信息来自各种来源。
Valuable lessons were gleaned from the experience. = 从这次经历中汲取了宝贵的教训。

疑问句:
What can be gleaned from this data? = 从这些数据中可以看出什么？
What did you glean from the meeting? = 你从会议中学到了什么？

否定句:
He could glean nothing from the confusing report. = 他从混乱的报告中什么也没得到。
They gleaned little useful information from the interview. = 他们从采访中获得的信息很少。
