gnathite:/ˈnæθaɪt/| n. |颚肢|A gnathite is an appendage of the mouthparts of arthropods, such as insects and crustaceans.  They are involved in manipulating food and feeding.  Different types of gnathites exist, adapted for various functions. |Example: The mandibles are a type of gnathite used for biting and chewing.|近义词:mouthpart appendage|反义词:None.
