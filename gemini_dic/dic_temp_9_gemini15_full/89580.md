hooch:/hʊtʃ/| n. |劣酒，廉价酒|A strong, often homemade alcoholic beverage of uncertain quality.  It is typically made from fermented fruits, grains, or other materials. The term often carries a connotation of being illicit or low-quality.|例句：The moonshiners produced a potent hooch in their hidden still.  山区酿酒者在他们隐藏的酿酒厂生产烈性劣酒。|近义词：moonshine, rotgut, booze, liquor|反义词：fine wine, premium liquor
