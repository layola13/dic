para-transit: /ˈpærəˌtrænzɪt/ | n. | 辅助交通 |  A form of transportation that supplements regular public transit, typically providing door-to-door service for people who have difficulty using fixed-route systems (such as buses or trains).  It often serves individuals with disabilities, elderly people, or those in rural areas with limited access to conventional public transportation. | Example:  The city provides para-transit services for individuals with mobility impairments.  这个城市为行动不便的个人提供辅助交通服务。|近义词：supplementary transport, specialized transport | 反义词：regular public transit, fixed-route transit |
