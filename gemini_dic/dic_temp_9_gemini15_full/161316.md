postponement:/pəˈspoʊnmənt/ | n. | 推迟；延期 | The act of delaying or putting something off to a later time.  It refers to the state of being delayed or the action of delaying something. |例句：The postponement of the meeting was announced due to unforeseen circumstances.  会议由于不可预见的情况而被推迟。

例句：Due to the bad weather, the game's postponement was inevitable.  由于恶劣天气，比赛延期是不可避免的。


近义词：delay, deferment, adjournment, deferral | 反义词：acceleration, advancement, hastening


词性：名词
