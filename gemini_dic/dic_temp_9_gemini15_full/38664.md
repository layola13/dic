concussions:/kənˈkʌʃənz/ | n. |脑震荡（复数） | A concussion is a type of traumatic brain injury caused by a bump, blow, or jolt to the head or body that causes the head and brain to move rapidly back and forth. This sudden movement can cause the brain to bounce around or twist in the skull, resulting in stretching or damaging of brain cells and blood vessels.  | Example:  The football player suffered multiple concussions throughout his career.  这位橄榄球运动员在职业生涯中遭受了多次脑震荡。  The doctor confirmed that the child had a mild concussion after the fall. 医生确认孩子摔倒后轻微脑震荡。|近义词：brain injury, head injury |反义词：无 |词性：名词
