cohosts:/ˈkoʊhoʊsts/| n.|联合主持人|Two or more people who host a show or event together.  It refers to a group of people sharing hosting duties.|例句：The two cohosts had great chemistry and made the show enjoyable.  两位联合主持人默契十足，使节目非常精彩。|例句：The conference will feature several prominent cohosts from different industries.  此次会议将邀请来自不同行业的几位知名联合主持人。|近义词：joint hosts, presenters, emcees|反义词：solo host, single presenter
