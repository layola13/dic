eventuate:/ɪˈvɛntʃueɪt/| v.|最终发生，结果是|To happen or result as a consequence; to come about.  The verb implies a process leading to a final outcome.|例句：Their efforts eventuated in the successful launch of the project. = 他们的努力最终导致项目成功启动。
例句：Years of hard work eventuated in his promotion to CEO. = 多年的努力最终使他晋升为首席执行官。|result, culminate, conclude|prevent, avoid, avert|动词
