capsules:/ˈkæpsjuːlz/ | n. |胶囊；太空舱；(尤指药片的)胶囊剂 |A small, usually cylindrical container, often made of gelatin, for holding a dose of medicine.  Also refers to a compartmentalized vehicle, like a spacecraft. |例句：The doctor prescribed a course of antibiotics in capsules. 医生开了几盒胶囊抗生素。|例句：The astronauts landed safely in their capsule. 宇航员安全地降落在他们的太空舱里。|近义词：container, pill, vessel |反义词：bulk (as in medication)
