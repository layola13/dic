mandrills:/ˈmændrɪlz/| n.|山魈|A mandrill is a large, colorful primate found in the rainforests of equatorial Africa.  They are known for their brightly colored faces and rumps.  Mandrills are highly social animals, living in large groups with complex social structures.|例句：The male mandrills are easily recognized by their vibrant red and blue faces. 雄性山魈很容易被它们鲜艳的红蓝相间的脸认出来。

The troop of mandrills moved through the dense undergrowth.  一群山魈穿过茂密的灌木丛。|近义词：baboon (狒狒), primate (灵长类动物)|反义词：无
