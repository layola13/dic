cul-de-sacs:/ˈkʌl dəˈsæk/| n. |死胡同；尽头；无出口的街道|A cul-de-sac is a street or passage that is closed at one end, so that traffic cannot pass through.|例句：Our house is at the end of a cul-de-sac. = 我们的房子在死胡同的尽头。


例句：Many residential areas are built with cul-de-sacs to reduce through-traffic. = 许多住宅区都建有死胡同以减少交通流量。

近义词：dead end, blind alley|反义词：through street, thoroughfare

词源:
