by:/baɪ/| prep.|凭借；通过；在…旁边；被；由|英文解析：The preposition "by" indicates means, agency, proximity, or passing.  It can express the agent of a passive action, the manner of doing something, the time or position near something, or the measure or extent of something.|例句：The book was written by Jane Austen.  这本书是简·奥斯汀写的。

The thief escaped by climbing over the wall. 小偷翻墙逃跑了。

近义词：through, via, near, beside, with|反义词：without, independently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I travel by bus. = 我乘公共汽车旅行。
He communicates by email. = 他通过电子邮件交流。


一般过去时:
She went to school by bike. = 她骑自行车去上学。
They arrived by train. = 他们乘火车到达。


现在完成时:
We have travelled extensively by air. = 我们已经广泛地乘飞机旅行了。
He has learned English by immersion. = 他通过沉浸式学习英语。


过去完成时:
They had arrived by the time the meeting started. = 会议开始时他们已经到了。
She had already left by the time I called. = 我打电话时她已经走了。


将来时:
I will travel to London by plane. = 我将乘飞机去伦敦。
We will send the package by courier. = 我们将通过快递公司寄送包裹。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have gone by train. = 如果我知道的话，我就坐火车去了。
I wish I had arrived by five o'clock. = 我希望我在五点之前到达。


被动语态:
The building was designed by a famous architect. = 这座建筑是由一位著名的建筑师设计的。
The problem was solved by a simple method. = 这个问题通过一个简单的方法解决了。


疑问句:
How did you get here, by car or by bus? = 你怎么来的，开车还是坐公共汽车？
Do you prefer to travel by train or by plane? = 你更喜欢乘火车还是乘飞机旅行？


否定句:
He didn't get to work by car today. = 他今天没开车上班。
She didn't send the letter by post. = 她没有邮寄信件。
