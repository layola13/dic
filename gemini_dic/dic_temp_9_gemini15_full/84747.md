handles:/ˈhændlz/ | n., v. |把手；柄；控制；处理 |

英文解析：As a noun, "handles" refers to a part of an object designed for grasping or carrying. As a verb, it means to manage, control, or deal with something.

例句(Noun):
The door handles are made of brass. = 门把手是用黄铜做的。

The suitcase has comfortable handles. = 这个手提箱有舒适的手柄。


例句(Verb):
She handles stress very well. = 她处理压力非常好。

He handles the company's finances. = 他负责管理公司的财务。


近义词(Noun): grips, knobs, shafts
近义词(Verb): manages, controls, deals with, operates


反义词(Verb): neglects, ignores, mishandles


词性：Noun, Verb
