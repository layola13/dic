dischargees: /dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒiz/ | n. | 获释者，出院者 |  Those who have been released from a duty, obligation, or confinement, especially from military service or imprisonment.  It is the plural form of "dischargee". | 例句：The dischargees from the army were given a hero's welcome.  军队中的退伍军人受到了英雄般的欢迎。

The dischargees from prison were required to attend regular meetings with their parole officer.  监狱中的释放人员被要求定期与他们的假释官会面。

近义词：ex-servicemen, ex-convicts, released prisoners | 反义词：prisoners, inmates, convicts | 名词
