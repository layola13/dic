remanded:/rɪˈmændɪd/| v. | 遣送，还押，提交 | To send (a case, prisoner, etc.) back to a lower court or to custody.  | The judge remanded the case to the lower court for further investigation.  法官将此案发回下级法院进一步调查。| The suspect was remanded in custody pending trial. 嫌疑人被还押候审。|  commit, refer | release, acquit |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The court remands cases frequently. = 法院经常将案件发回。
He remands prisoners to the county jail. = 他将囚犯送回县监狱。

一般过去时:
The judge remanded the defendant. = 法官将被告还押。
They remanded the case for a new trial. = 他们将此案发回重审。

现在完成时:
The court has remanded several cases this month. = 本月法院已将几起案件发回。
They have remanded him to custody until the hearing. = 他们已将他拘留至听证会为止。


过去完成时:
The appeal court had remanded the case twice before. = 上诉法院之前曾两次将此案发回。
The police had remanded the suspect before the evidence was found. = 在证据被发现之前，警方已将嫌疑人拘留。

将来时:
The court will remand the prisoner to a mental health facility. = 法院将把囚犯送交精神卫生机构。
They will remand the case to the juvenile court. = 他们将把此案发回少年法庭。

虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, the judge would have remanded the case. = 如果证据更充分，法官就会将此案发回。
If I were the judge, I would have remanded the defendant for further investigation. = 如果我是法官，我会将被告送回进行进一步调查。

被动语态:
The case was remanded to a lower court. = 此案被发回下级法院。
The prisoner was remanded in custody for questioning. = 囚犯被拘留接受讯问。


疑问句:
Was the case remanded? = 此案被发回了吗？
Will the defendant be remanded? = 被告会被还押吗？

否定句:
The judge did not remand the prisoner. = 法官没有将囚犯还押。
The court did not remand the case. = 法院没有将此案发回。
