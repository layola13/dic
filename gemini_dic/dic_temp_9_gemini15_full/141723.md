oraison:/ˈɔːreɪzən/| n. |祈祷，恳求|A formal or solemn prayer; a devout expression of prayer or supplication.  It often refers to a set prayer or one said as part of a religious service. |例句：The priest led the congregation in a heartfelt oraison.  牧师带领会众作了一次发自内心的祈祷。

The monks began their day with an oraison of thanks.  僧侣们以感恩的祈祷开始他们的一天。|近义词：prayer, supplication, invocation |反义词：curse, blasphemy
