一般过去时:

现在完成时:
Researchers have distinguished several new species. = 研究人员已经区分出几种新物种。
He has distinguished himself in his field through hard work. = 他通过努力工作在自己的领域里脱颖而出。


过去完成时:

将来时:
The judges will distinguish the winners from the losers. = 评委将区分胜者和败者。

虚拟语气:
If I could distinguish the truth from lies, I would. = 如果我能分辨真假，我会的。
I wish I could distinguish the subtle differences in the music. = 我希望我能区分音乐中细微的差别。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

She failed to distinguish the important information from the irrelevant details. = 她未能将重要信息与无关细节区分开来。
