misleading:/ˌmɪsˈliːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 误导的；欺骗性的 | Giving the wrong idea or impression; deceptive.  | 例句：The advertisement was misleading, making false claims about the product's effectiveness. 这则广告是误导性的，对产品的有效性做出了虚假的声明。

The instructions were misleading, and I ended up assembling the furniture incorrectly. 说明书误导了我，结果我组装的家具不对。| 近义词：deceptive, deceptive, confusing, ambiguous | 反义词：clear, accurate, truthful, unambiguous
