liposomal:/laɪˈpoʊsəməl/| adj. |脂质体的，包裹于脂质体的|Adjective describing something encapsulated within liposomes. Liposomes are artificial vesicles composed of lipid bilayers, similar to cell membranes.  They are used to deliver drugs, cosmetics, and other substances.|Example: Liposomal drug delivery systems are becoming increasingly popular.  脂质体药物递送系统正变得越来越流行。|近义词：vesicular (if referring to the structure) |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms like "free" or "unencapsulated" if discussing drug delivery.
