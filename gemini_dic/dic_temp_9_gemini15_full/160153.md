pompous:/ˈpɒmpəs/ /ˈpɑːmpəs/| adj.| 傲慢的；自大的；夸夸其谈的；浮夸的| Characterized by excessive self-importance; arrogant and pretentious.  It suggests an inflated sense of one's own importance and a tendency to display it ostentatiously. |例句：He delivered a pompous speech filled with meaningless jargon. 他发表了一篇充满无意义术语的夸夸其谈的演讲。

例句：Her pompous manner alienated many of her colleagues. 她傲慢的态度疏远了她许多同事。


近义词：pretentious, arrogant, haughty, supercilious, self-important, inflated

反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, down-to-earth
