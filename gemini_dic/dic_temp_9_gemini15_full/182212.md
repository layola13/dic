remonstratingly:/rɪˈmɒnstreɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 规劝地；抗议地；反对地 |  In a manner that expresses strong disapproval or protest.  It suggests a forceful and often argumentative expression of objection. |例句：He remonstratingly shook his head at the suggestion. 他反对这个建议，摇摇头表示抗议。

She spoke remonstratingly, her voice filled with disapproval. 她规劝地说，语气中充满了不满。

近义词：protestingly, objectingly, disapprovingly | 反义词：approvingly, supportively
