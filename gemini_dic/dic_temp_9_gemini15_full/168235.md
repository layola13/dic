pro-Greeks:/ˌproʊˈɡriːks/| adj.|亲希腊的，支持希腊的|Favorable or supportive of Greece or the Greeks.|The pro-Greek sentiment was strong in the region after the war.  战后，该地区对希腊的同情情绪强烈。|近义词：philhellene, pro-Hellenic|反义词：anti-Greek

一般现在时:
He is pro-Greek in his political views. = 他在政治观点上亲希腊。
She supports pro-Greek initiatives. = 她支持亲希腊的倡议。


一般过去时:
Many intellectuals were pro-Greek during the independence movement. = 在独立运动期间，许多知识分子是亲希腊的。
