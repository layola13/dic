lute:/luːt/ | n. | 鲁特琴 | A lute is a stringed musical instrument with a pear-shaped body and a long neck.  It was popular in Europe during the Renaissance and Baroque periods.  | Example: The musician played a beautiful melody on his lute.  演奏家用他的鲁特琴演奏了一首优美的旋律。| 近义词：oud (a similar instrument from the Middle East)| 反义词：None (no direct antonym)| 名词
