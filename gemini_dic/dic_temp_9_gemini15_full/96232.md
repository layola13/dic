Indians:/ˈɪndiənz/| n. |印第安人，美洲土著居民 |The term "Indians" refers to the Indigenous peoples of the Americas.  The name originated from a historical misunderstanding by European explorers.  The term is now considered by many to be outdated and potentially offensive, with preference given to more specific tribal affiliations. | Example:  Many different tribes of Indians inhabited North America before European colonization. 许多不同的印第安部落在欧洲殖民之前居住在北美洲。  The history of the Indians is complex and varied. 印第安人的历史复杂而多样。|近义词：Native Americans, Indigenous peoples, First Nations (Canada) |反义词：colonizers, settlers
