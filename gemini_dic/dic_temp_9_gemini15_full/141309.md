open-plan:/ˈoʊpənplæn/| adj.|开放式平面设计的；开放式办公空间的|An open-plan design is one in which there are few or no internal walls, creating a large, undivided space.|例句：The office adopted an open-plan design to foster collaboration.  这家办公室采用了开放式设计以促进协作。|近义词：unpartitioned, open-concept |反义词：closed-plan, compartmentalized
