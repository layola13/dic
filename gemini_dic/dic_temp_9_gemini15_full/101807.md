irritable bowel syndrome:/ˌɪrɪtəbəl ˈbaʊəl ˈsɪndrəʊm/| n. |肠易激综合征|Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) is a common disorder that affects the large intestine.  Symptoms include cramping, abdominal pain, bloating, gas, and diarrhea or constipation. The exact cause of IBS is unknown, but it may be related to problems with the digestive system, gut bacteria, or the nervous system. Treatment focuses on managing symptoms and may involve dietary changes, medication, and stress management techniques.|She suffers from irritable bowel syndrome and experiences frequent abdominal pain.|近义词：spastic colon, functional bowel disorder|反义词：None.
