sarmatic:/sɑːrˈmætɪk/| adj.|萨尔马提亚的；萨尔马提亚人的；|Of or relating to the Sarmatians, a group of nomadic Iranian peoples who lived in the Pontic-Caspian Steppe from the 3rd century BC to the 4th century AD.  Their culture and language significantly influenced the development of several Eastern European cultures.|The Sarmatian culture is characterized by its distinctive weaponry and burial practices.  Many artifacts have been unearthed from Sarmatian settlements.|近义词：Scythian (related but distinct)|反义词：无
