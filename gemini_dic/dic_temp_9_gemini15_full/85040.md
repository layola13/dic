harbour:/ˈhɑːbə(r)/|/ˈhɑːrbər/| n. |港口；避风港；藏匿处|A sheltered place on a coastline where ships can safely dock.|The ship sailed into the harbour. 这艘船驶入了港口。|The small fishing village had a picturesque harbour.  这个小渔村有一个风景如画的港口。|port, dock, haven|open sea, storm|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The harbour provides shelter for many boats. = 港口为许多船只提供庇护。
The ships are currently docked in the harbour. = 船只目前停泊在港口。


一般过去时:
The harbour was damaged during the storm. = 港口在暴风雨中受损。
Many ships sought refuge in the harbour last night. = 许多船只昨晚在港口寻求避难。


现在完成时:
The harbour has been improved significantly. = 港口得到了显著的改善。
They have used the harbour for centuries. = 他们已经使用了这个港口几个世纪了。


过去完成时:

词源:
Old English *heafod, from Proto-Germanic *haubudą (“head”), from Proto-Indo-European *(h₁)k<sup>w</sup>ep- (“head”).  The modern spelling “harbour” developed through various spelling changes over time.  The original meaning referred to a sheltered place, like a headland offering protection.
