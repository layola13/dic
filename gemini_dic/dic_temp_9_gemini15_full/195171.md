semidestruction:/ˌsemiˈdɪstrʌkʃən/| n. |半破坏，部分破坏 |Partial destruction; the state of being partly destroyed.  The building suffered semidestruction in the fire.  |The earthquake caused semidestruction to the city infrastructure. |近义词：partial destruction, devastation (in a more severe context)|反义词：complete construction, full preservation

例句：

The bombing caused semidestruction to the city's infrastructure.  炸弹袭击对城市的基础设施造成了部分破坏。

The old house was in a state of semidestruction, with parts of the roof collapsed. 这栋老房子处于半毁状态，部分屋顶坍塌了。


词源：

* **semi-**:  A prefix meaning "half," "partly," or "partially."  源自拉丁语“semi-”，意为“半的”、“部分的”。
* **destruction**:  The act of destroying something; the state of being destroyed. 源自法语“destruction”，意为“破坏”。  最终源自拉丁语“destructio”。


用法说明：

semidestruction主要用作名词，描述某种东西部分被破坏的状态。它不强调破坏的程度，只强调破坏的不完整性。  与complete destruction相对。
