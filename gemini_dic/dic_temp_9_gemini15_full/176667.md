reacidified:/ˌriːˈæsɪdəfaɪd/| adj. | 再酸化过的；重新酸化的 |  Having been made acidic again.  This term describes a substance that has undergone a process of becoming acidic once more after a previous neutralization or reduction in acidity. | The soil was reacidified after the addition of lime. 土壤在添加石灰后重新酸化了。 The solution was reacidified to a pH of 3.  溶液重新酸化至pH 3。 |  re-acidified,  re-acidulated | neutralized, alkalinized | 形容词
