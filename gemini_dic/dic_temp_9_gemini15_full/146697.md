overheats:/ˌoʊvərˈhiːts/| v.|过热，热得过度|To become excessively hot; to reach a dangerously high temperature.|例句：The engine overheated and stalled. 引擎过热并熄火了。|例句：My computer overheats when I run too many programs at once. 当我同时运行太多程序时，我的电脑会过热。|近义词：superheats, scorches|反义词：cools down


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine overheats when it's overloaded. = 引擎超载时会过热。
He often overheats his food in the microwave. = 他经常在微波炉里把食物加热过度。

一般过去时:
