ravining: /ˈrævɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 贪婪的；狼吞虎咽的；（河流）汹涌的 |  Voracious; greedy; characterized by excessive desire or appetite; (of a river) raging, violent. |


例句：

The ravining beast devoured its prey whole.  =  那只贪婪的野兽整个吞下了它的猎物。

He ate with a ravining appetite, finishing his plate in seconds. = 他狼吞虎咽地吃着，几秒钟内就吃完了盘子里的食物。


近义词：voracious, greedy, ravenous, rapacious, gluttonous


反义词：moderate, restrained, temperate


词性：形容词


词源：源自古法语 *ravine，意思是“掠夺，抢夺”，最终源自拉丁语 *rapīna，“掠夺”。  The sense shifted from "plundering" to describing a greedy and insatiable appetite.
