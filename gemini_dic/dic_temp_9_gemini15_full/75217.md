frogling:/ˈfrɒɡlɪŋ/| n. |小青蛙；青蛙幼体|A small frog; a young frog.  The term often implies a diminutive or endearing size.|例句：The children delighted in catching the froglings in the garden.孩子们喜欢在花园里捉小青蛙。


例句：We found several tiny froglings near the pond.我们在池塘边发现了几只小小的青蛙幼体。


近义词：tadpole (especially if very young), small frog


反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting large size or adulthood could be considered antonyms (e.g., bullfrog).


词性:名词
