clairvoyances:/ˈklɛrˈvɔɪənsɪz/ | n. | 预知能力；透视力 | The ability to see things that are not present to the normal senses, especially the future.  It often implies a supernatural or paranormal ability. |例句：Her clairvoyances allowed her to predict the earthquake.  她的预知能力让她预言了地震。

例句：Many people attribute his successful business ventures to his uncanny clairvoyances. 许多人认为他成功的商业冒险归功于他不可思议的预知能力。


近义词：precognition, foresight, intuition, second sight | 反义词：blindness (to the future), ignorance
