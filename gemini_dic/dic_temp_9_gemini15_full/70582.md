filling-stations:/ˈfɪlɪŋˌsteɪʃənz/| n. |加油站|Places where motor vehicles can be refueled with gasoline or other fuels.  They often also sell convenience items. |Example:  I need to stop at a filling-station to fill up my car. 我需要停在一个加油站把我的车加满油。|近义词：gas station, petrol station, service station|反义词：无
