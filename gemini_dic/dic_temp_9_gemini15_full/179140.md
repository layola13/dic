redialled:/ˌriːˈdaɪld/| v. |重拨（电话）|To dial a telephone number again.  This usually implies a previous unsuccessful attempt.|例句：I redialed the number, but still got no answer. 我重拨了号码，但仍然没有得到回应。

I redialed the number several times before finally getting through. 我重拨了好几次号码才终于接通。|近义词：redid, called back, tried again |反义词：disconnected, hung up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redials the number every few minutes. 他每隔几分钟就重拨号码。
She redials numbers until she gets the right person. 她一直重拨号码直到拨通了想找的人。


一般过去时:
I redialed the number after it went to voicemail.  号码转到语音信箱后，我重拨了号码。
They redialed the number but the line was still busy. 他们重拨了号码，但线路仍然占线。


现在完成时:
He has redialed that number countless times. 他已经重拨了无数次那个号码。
She has redialed the number since this morning. 从今天早上开始她就一直在重拨这个号码。


过去完成时:
I had redialed the number three times before giving up. 我重拨了三次号码后才放弃。
They had redialed the number several times before realizing it was incorrect. 他们重拨了好几次号码才意识到号码错了。
