mansa:/ˈmɑːnsɑː/| n. |曼萨王, 曼萨王朝|The word "mansa" is a title used by the rulers of the Mali Empire, a powerful West African kingdom that flourished from the 13th to the 15th centuries.  It translates roughly to "sultan" or "emperor." The most famous Mansa was Mansa Musa, known for his extravagant pilgrimage to Mecca.|例句：Mansa Musa’s reign saw the Mali Empire reach its zenith. = 曼萨·穆萨的统治时期见证了马里帝国的鼎盛。|近义词：sultan, emperor, king|反义词:subject, vassal
