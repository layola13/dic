petalless:/ˈpɛtəlɪs/| adj.|无花瓣的|Lacking petals.  This term describes flowers or plants that do not have petals, the often brightly colored modified leaves that surround the reproductive parts of a flower.|The petalless flower was still beautiful in its unique way.  这朵无花瓣的花以其独特的方式仍然很美丽。|petaliferous, petaled|petaled
