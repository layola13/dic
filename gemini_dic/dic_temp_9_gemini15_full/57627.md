draughting: /ˈdrɑːftɪŋ/ | n. |制图；绘图；草图绘制|The act or process of creating technical drawings.  It involves using various tools and techniques to produce precise and detailed representations of objects, systems, or structures.  This is often done using computer-aided design (CAD) software. |例句：The architect spent weeks on the draughting of the building plans. 建筑师花了数周时间绘制建筑图纸。


例句：The engineer's careful draughting ensured the bridge's structural integrity. 工程师细致的绘图确保了桥梁的结构完整性。


近义词：drawing, sketching, designing


反义词：None directly opposite, but "improvisation" could be considered a contrasting concept.


词性: Noun
