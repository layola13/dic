neurosis:/nʊˈroʊsɪs/ | n. |神经症，神经官能症 |A relatively mild mental illness that is characterized by anxiety, depression, or other psychological distress but without the loss of contact with reality.  Neuroses are often caused by unresolved emotional conflicts or stressful life experiences. |例句：Her neurosis manifested as extreme anxiety and sleep disturbances. 她的神经症表现为极度焦虑和睡眠障碍。

例句：He suffered from a neurosis that stemmed from childhood trauma. 他患有源于童年创伤的神经症。


近义词：psychoneurosis, nervous disorder, mental disorder |反义词：psychosis, normalcy, sanity


词性: 名词
