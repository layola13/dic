montages:/mɒnˈtɑːʒ/| n. |蒙太奇；剪辑；组合 |A montage is a quick succession of images or shots in a film or video, often used to condense time or convey a particular mood or idea.  It's a technique of combining different shots into a sequence to create a particular effect. |The film used a powerful montage to show the passage of years. 这部电影用了一个强大的蒙太奇来展现岁月的流逝。The director created a montage of images to evoke a sense of chaos. 导演创作了一个图像蒙太奇来唤起一种混乱的感觉。|collage, medley, assemblage |disunity, separation|名词
