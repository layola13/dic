odelet:/ˈoʊdəlɛt/ | n. |小颂诗，短歌 | A short ode; a little poem, typically lyrical and expressing a single, strong emotion.  | Example:  She composed a delicate odelet about the beauty of the sunset. 她创作了一首关于日落美景的精致小诗。 |近义词：short poem, lyric, sonnet (if structured as such) |反义词：epic, long poem
