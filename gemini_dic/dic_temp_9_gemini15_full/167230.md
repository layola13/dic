preterit:/ˈprɛtərɪt/| adj. n.|过去式；过去时态的|The preterit is a grammatical tense used to describe actions or states that occurred in the past.  It's often referred to as the simple past tense. |The preterit tense is used to describe completed actions in the past.  He walked to the store.  She ate dinner. |past tense, simple past|future, present|adjective, noun

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He walks to school every day. = 他每天步行去上学。
She speaks three languages fluently. = 她流利地说三种语言。


一般过去时:
He walked to school yesterday. = 他昨天步行去上学。
She spoke to the manager this morning. = 她今天早上和经理谈话了。


现在完成时:
He has walked to school many times. = 他多次步行去上学。
She has spoken to several potential clients. = 她已经和几个潜在客户谈过话了。


过去完成时:
He had walked to school before the rain started. = 下雨开始之前，他已经步行去上学了。
She had spoken to him before the meeting. = 会议之前，她已经和他谈过了。


将来时:
He will walk to school tomorrow. = 他明天将步行去上学。
She will speak at the conference next week. = 她下周将在会议上发言。


虚拟语气:
If he had walked faster, he would have caught the bus. = 如果他走得快一些，他就能赶上公共汽车了。
If she had spoken up, her concerns would have been addressed. = 如果她当时说出来，她的担忧就会得到解决。


被动语态:
The letter was written yesterday. = 这封信是昨天写的。
The problem was discussed extensively. = 这个问题被广泛讨论了。


疑问句:
Did he walk to school? = 他步行去上学了吗？
Did she speak to the boss? = 她和老板谈话了吗？


否定句:
He did not walk to school. = 他没有步行去上学。
She did not speak to anyone. = 她没有和任何人说话。
