magniloquence:/mæɡˈnɪləkwəns/| n. |夸夸其谈；豪言壮语；浮夸的言辞 |The use of high-sounding language; pompous or bombastic speech.  |例句：His speech was full of magniloquence, but lacked substance. 他的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，却缺乏实质内容。  |例句：The politician's magniloquence failed to impress the skeptical audience.  这位政治家的夸夸其谈未能打动怀疑的听众。|近义词：bombast, grandiloquence, rhetoric, eloquence (in a negative sense)|反义词：understatement, modesty, simplicity|名词
