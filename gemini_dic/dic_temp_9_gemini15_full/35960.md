codec:/ˈkoʊdɛk/| n. |编解码器|A codec is a device or program that encodes and decodes a digital data stream or signal.  It's a combination of a coder (encoder) and a decoder. Codecs are essential for compressing and decompressing audio and video files, allowing for efficient storage and transmission.  For example, MP3 uses a codec to compress music files. |Example: The video codec used determines the quality and file size of the video.  这个视频使用的编解码器决定了视频的质量和文件大小。|近义词：encoder, decoder, transcoder |反义词：None
