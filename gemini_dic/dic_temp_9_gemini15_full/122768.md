mojo:/ˈmoʊdʒoʊ/ | n. |魔力，魅力，运气，自信 |Magic, charm, a feeling of confidence and energy;  a magical charm or spell.  It often refers to a person's charisma or the special quality that enables them to succeed. |例句：He's got the mojo to win this election. 他有赢得这次选举的魔力。

例句：She lost her mojo after the breakup. 分手后她失去了魅力。


近义词：charisma, charm, magic, luck, confidence, power, oomph |反义词：incompetence,  lack of confidence,  bad luck,  ineffectiveness


词源：The word "mojo" originates from the Kongo language of Central Africa, where "moxo" referred to a magical charm or spell. It entered English slang in the early 20th century, initially referring to a magical charm or talisman.  Over time, its meaning broadened to encompass the general sense of charm, charisma, and good luck.
