rerental: /ˌriːˈrɛntəl/ | n. | 再租赁，再次出租 | The act of renting something out again after it has been rented before.  It refers to the process of leasing something a second (or subsequent) time.  | Example: The rerental of the apartment was surprisingly quick.  这套公寓的再次出租出乎意料地快。 | 近义词: re-letting, subletting (if applicable) | 反义词: initial rental, first rental
