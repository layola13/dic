salwar:/ˈsæl.wɑːr/| n.|沙尔瓦尔裤|A loose, typically cotton, pair of trousers worn by men and women in South Asia, particularly in Pakistan, India, and Bangladesh.  They are often worn with a kurta (long shirt) or kameez (a tunic).|Example: She wore a vibrant salwar kameez to the wedding. 她穿着鲜艳的沙尔瓦尔卡米兹参加婚礼。|近义词：pyjama (loose sense), trousers|反义词：None|名词
