prebaked:/ˌpriːˈbeɪkt/| adj.| 预先烘烤的；预先烘焙的 | Already baked before being sold or used.  Prebaked goods are convenient because they can be quickly heated and served. | 例句：The prebaked pizza crust saved me a lot of time. = 预先烘烤的披萨饼皮节省了我很多时间。

The prebaked bread rolls were perfect for the party. = 预先烘烤的面包卷很适合派对。


近义词：precooked, prepared | 反义词：unbaked, raw | 词性：形容词
