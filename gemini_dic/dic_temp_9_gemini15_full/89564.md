honourary: /ˈɒnərəri/ /ˈɑːnərɛri/ | adj. | 荣誉的；名誉的 |  Given as an honor; not holding the responsibilities of the position.  An honorary position is one given in recognition of merit, without the usual obligations or salary. |

例句：He received an honorary doctorate from the university. 他获得了该大学的荣誉博士学位。

例句：She holds an honorary position on the board of directors. 她在董事会担任名誉职位。


近义词：honorific, titular


反义词：actual, official


词性：形容词
