contravening:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːnɪŋ/ | v. |违反，违背 | To act against or in defiance of a law, rule, or agreement.  |例句：The company was accused of contravening environmental regulations.  该公司被指控违反环境法规。

例句：His actions contravened company policy. 他的行为违反了公司政策。


近义词：violating, infringing, transgressing, breaching | 反义词：obeying, complying, conforming

词源:
The word "contravening" comes from the Latin word "contravenire," which means "to come against," "to oppose," or "to violate."  The prefix "contra-" means "against," and the verb "venire" means "to come."
