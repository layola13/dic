microbrewery:/ˈmaɪkroʊˈbrʊəri/ | n. |小型啤酒厂 |A small brewery, typically independently owned and operated, that produces a limited amount of beer, often specializing in craft beers.|例句：The local microbrewery offers a wide variety of seasonal beers. 当地的小型啤酒厂提供各种各样的季节性啤酒。|近义词：small brewery, craft brewery |反义词：large brewery, mass-production brewery
