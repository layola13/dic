over-articulate: /ˌoʊvərɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪt/ | adj. | 过分清晰地发音的；过于强调的 |  Excessively clear and precise in speech; emphasizing pronunciation to an unnatural degree.  It often suggests a deliberate or affected style of speaking. |


例句：

He over-articulates his words, making him sound somewhat pompous. = 他说话过于清晰，听起来有点自大。

Her over-articulate speech pattern felt unnatural and slightly irritating. = 她过分清晰的说话方式听起来不自然，略微令人恼火。


近义词：precise, distinct, enunciate clearly, affected, pedantic


反义词：mumbled, slurred, indistinct, unclear


词性：形容词
