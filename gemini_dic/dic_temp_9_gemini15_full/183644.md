/ˌriːˈredʒɪstərɪŋ/ | v. | 重新注册；再次登记 | The act of registering again; to register something or someone again.  This involves the process of formally recording or listing something or someone a second time, often after an initial registration has lapsed or become invalid. |  例句：He is reregistering for the fall semester. 他正在重新注册秋季学期。

例句：The company is reregistering its trademark to protect its intellectual property.  该公司正在重新注册其商标以保护其知识产权。


近义词：re-enrolling, re-recording, registering anew | 反义词：unregistering, deregistering | 动词
