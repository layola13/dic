fashioned:/ˈfæʃənd/ | adj. | 时髦的；流行的；制作的；形成的 |  Having a particular style or design; made or done in a particular way.  |例句：The house was fashioned in the Victorian style. 这房子是维多利亚风格的。 |例句：The clay was fashioned into a pot.  黏土被塑造成一个花盆。|近义词：stylish, trendy, designed, formed |反义词：old-fashioned, outdated, unstylish

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dress is fashioned with lace. = 这件衣服是用蕾丝制作的。
His ideas are fashioned after those of his mentor. = 他的想法是效仿他的导师的。

一般过去时:
The sculptor fashioned the statue from marble. = 雕塑家用大理石雕刻了这尊雕像。
They fashioned a raft from logs. = 他们用原木制作了一个木筏。

现在完成时:
The company has fashioned a new marketing strategy. = 公司已经制定了一个新的营销策略。
She has fashioned a unique career path for herself. = 她为自己塑造了一条独特的职业道路。

过去完成时:
The tools had been fashioned long before the settlers arrived. = 这些工具早在定居者到来之前就已经制作好了。
The plans had been carefully fashioned over many months. = 这些计划经过好几个月的精心策划。

将来时:
The artist will fashion a new masterpiece. = 艺术家将创作一件新的杰作。
They will fashion a new approach to the problem. = 他们将想出一个解决这个问题的新方法。

虚拟语气:
If the material were fashioned differently, it would be stronger. = 如果材料的制作方式不同，它会更结实。
I wish the furniture were fashioned in a more modern style. = 我希望家具的款式更现代一些。

被动语态:
The vase was fashioned by a skilled artisan. = 这个花瓶是由一位技艺精湛的工匠制作的。
The story was fashioned to appeal to a younger audience. = 这个故事是为了吸引更年轻的观众而编造的。

疑问句:
How was the sculpture fashioned? = 这尊雕塑是如何制作的？
What materials were fashioned into the building? = 建筑物是用什么材料建造的？

否定句:
The product was not fashioned to last. = 该产品并非为了耐用而设计的。
The argument was not fashioned logically. = 论证缺乏逻辑性。


PHRASE->
well-fashioned = 做工精良的
poorly fashioned = 做工粗糙的
newly fashioned = 新式的
custom-fashioned = 定制的
fashionably fashioned = 时髦的


词源:
源自古法语 *faconner，意为“塑造，制作”。  最终源自拉丁语 *facere，意为“做，制作”。
