shoplift:/ˈʃɑːplɪft/| v.|偷窃（商店里的商品）|To steal goods from a shop without paying.|例句：He was caught shoplifting a chocolate bar. 他因偷窃一块巧克力棒而被抓住。|近义词： pilfer, steal, thieve |反义词：pay, purchase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They shoplift regularly from that store. = 他们经常从那家商店偷东西。
He shoplifts small items to support his habit. = 他偷窃小物品来维持他的恶习。


一般过去时:
She shoplifted a dress last week. = 她上周偷了一件裙子。
The teenager shoplifted several expensive items. = 那名青少年偷窃了几件昂贵的物品。


现在完成时:
He has shoplifted many times before. = 他以前偷窃过很多次。
They have never shoplifted from this store. = 他们从未从这家商店偷过东西。


过去完成时:
She had shoplifted before, so she was careful this time. = 她以前偷过东西，所以这次很小心。
The police discovered he had shoplifted from several stores. = 警方发现他从几家商店偷过东西。


将来时:
I think he will shoplift again. = 我认为他会再次偷窃。
She will likely shoplift if she's desperate. = 如果她走投无路，她很可能会去偷东西。


虚拟语气:
If he shoplifted, he would go to jail. = 如果他偷窃，他会进监狱。
I wish he hadn't shoplifted that expensive watch. = 我真希望他没有偷那块昂贵的表。


被动语态:
Several items were shoplifted from the store last night. = 昨晚从商店偷走了几件物品。
He was caught shoplifting and was subsequently arrested. = 他被抓到偷窃，随后被捕。


疑问句:
Did he shoplift that expensive necklace? = 他偷了那条昂贵的项链吗？
Has anyone ever shoplifted from your store? = 有人从你的商店偷过东西吗？


否定句:
He didn't shoplift; he simply forgot to pay. = 他没有偷窃；他只是忘记付款了。
She wouldn't shoplift; she's too honest. = 她不会偷窃；她太诚实了。
