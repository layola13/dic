fiercer:/ˈfɪərsər/| adj.|更凶猛的，更猛烈的，更激烈的 | More fierce; more intense in feeling or action.  It is the comparative form of the adjective "fierce".|例句：The wind became fiercer as the storm approached.  暴风雨来临时，风变得更猛烈了。  The competition is becoming increasingly fiercer. 竞争越来越激烈了。|近义词：more intense, more ferocious, more violent, more aggressive |反义词：gentler, milder, calmer
