newsletters:/ˈnuːzˌletərz/ | n. |新闻通讯，简报 | Regularly issued bulletins, often electronic, containing news and information of interest to a specific group.  |例句：The company sends out monthly newsletters to its employees. 公司每月向员工发送新闻通讯。|例句：I subscribed to several gardening newsletters to learn more about growing roses. 我订阅了几份园艺简报，以了解更多关于种植玫瑰的信息。|近义词：bulletins, circulars, updates |反义词：无


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company sends out newsletters every month. = 公司每月发送新闻通讯。
We receive newsletters about new products. = 我们收到关于新产品的新闻通讯。

一般过去时:
They sent out a newsletter last week. = 他们上周发送了一份新闻通讯。

现在完成时:
I have received several newsletters this month. = 这个月我已经收到了几份新闻通讯。

过去完成时:
