cotyledon:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/| n.| 子叶 | A cotyledon is a significant part of an embryo within the seed of a plant. It acts as a food store for the developing seedling and is often the first leaf to emerge.  | Example: The bean seed has two cotyledons, while the maize seed has only one.  豆类种子有两个子叶，而玉米种子只有一个。|近义词：seed leaf|反义词：无
