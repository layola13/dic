objectifier:/ɒbˈdʒektɪfaɪər/ | n. | 物化者；使…物化的人 | A person who objectifies others.  Someone who treats a person as a mere object, disregarding their inherent worth and individuality. | Example: He was accused of being an objectifier, reducing women to mere sexual objects in his art. 他被指控为物化者，在他的艺术作品中将女性贬低为单纯的性对象。 | 近义词: dehumanizer, exploiter | 反义词:humanizer,  empathiser
