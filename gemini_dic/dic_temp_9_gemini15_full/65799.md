evidenced:/ˈɛvɪdənst/ | v. | 提供证据；证明 | To show something to be true or to support a claim with evidence.  | The study evidenced a clear link between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究证明了吸烟和肺癌之间存在明确的联系。 | demonstrated, proved, showed, illustrated | refuted, disproved | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The experiment evidences a significant improvement. = 实验表明有显著的改进。
His actions evidence his guilt. = 他的行为证明了他的罪责。


一般过去时:
The data evidenced a decline in sales. = 数据显示销售额下降。
The witness evidenced the suspect's presence at the scene. = 目击者证明了嫌疑犯在场。


现在完成时:
Recent studies have evidenced a rise in global temperatures. = 最近的研究表明全球气温上升。
The findings have evidenced the effectiveness of the new treatment. = 研究结果证明了新疗法的有效性。


过去完成时:
The investigation had evidenced a pattern of fraud. = 调查已经证明存在欺诈行为。
Previous research had evidenced similar results. = 先前的研究已经证明了类似的结果。


将来时:
Further testing will evidence the safety of the product. = 进一步的测试将证明产品的安全性。
The results will evidence the success of the project. = 结果将证明项目的成功。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence evidenced his innocence, he would be released. = 如果证据证明了他的清白，他就会被释放。(较少用evidenced在此虚拟语气句型中)
If the results had evidenced a different outcome, we would have altered our approach. = 如果结果显示不同的结果，我们会改变我们的方法。


被动语态:
The link between diet and health was evidenced in the report. = 报告中证明了饮食与健康之间的联系。
His guilt was evidenced by his own confession. = 他自己的供词证明了他的罪责。


疑问句:
Does this evidence the effectiveness of the drug? = 这是否证明了该药物的有效性？
What evidence the defendant's claim? = 什么证据支持被告的主张？


否定句:
The study did not evidence any significant differences. = 这项研究没有证明任何显著差异。
The results did not evidence a causal relationship. = 结果没有证明因果关系。



PHRASE->

be evidenced by = 由…证明
evidence of = …的证据
clear evidence = 明确的证据
strong evidence = 强有力的证据
substantial evidence = 大量的证据
