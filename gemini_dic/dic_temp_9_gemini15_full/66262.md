exclusively: /ɪkˈskluːsɪvli/ | adv. | 仅仅；专门地；独家地 | Only; solely; to the exclusion of others.  |例句：This offer is exclusively for our subscribers.  这个优惠只针对我们的订阅用户。|例句：The hotel is exclusively for adults. 这家酒店只接待成年人。|近义词：solely, only, entirely, solely, uniquely |反义词：inclusively, generally, commonly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exclusively uses organic products. 他只使用有机产品。
The company exclusively manufactures high-end goods. 这家公司专门生产高端商品。

一般过去时:
She exclusively wore designer clothes. 她过去只穿名牌服装。
They exclusively used local ingredients. 他们过去只使用当地食材。

现在完成时:
We have exclusively focused on customer satisfaction. 我们一直专注于客户满意度。
The team has exclusively used this method.  团队一直只使用这种方法。

过去完成时:
They had exclusively relied on traditional methods. 他们之前一直依赖传统方法。
She had exclusively studied French literature. 她之前只学习法语文学。

将来时:
We will exclusively promote the new product online. 我们将只在线推广新产品。
He will exclusively work from home. 他将只在家工作。

虚拟语气:
If we had exclusively used that material, the project would have been more successful. 如果我们只使用那种材料，项目会更成功。
I wish I had exclusively focused on my studies. 我希望我当时只专注于学习。

被动语态:
The product is exclusively sold online. 该产品只在线销售。
The event was exclusively attended by VIP guests.  活动只由贵宾出席。

疑问句:
Is this offer exclusively for employees? 这个优惠只针对员工吗？
Does the company exclusively use renewable energy? 这家公司只使用可再生能源吗？

否定句:
The restaurant doesn't exclusively serve seafood. 这家餐厅不只供应海鲜。
The shop doesn't exclusively sell imported goods. 这家商店不只销售进口商品。
