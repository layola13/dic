missus:/ˈmɪsɪs/| n.|太太，夫人|A term of address for a married woman.  It is a shortened form of "mistress."|  例句：The missus is expecting a call from the doctor.  太太在等医生的电话。  例句：Did you ask the missus if she needs anything from the shop? 你问太太是否需要从商店买什么东西吗？|近义词：wife, madam, woman|反义词：mister, sir
