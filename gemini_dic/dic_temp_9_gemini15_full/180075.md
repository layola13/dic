reflexions: /rɪˈflɛkʃənz/ | n. | 反射；反省；沉思 |  The act of reflecting or the state of being reflected;  serious thought or consideration.  | 例句：Her reflections on her childhood were poignant.  她的童年回忆令人心酸。

例句：The lake reflected the mountains in its still waters. 湖面平静地反射着山峦的影像。


近义词：considerations, thoughts, meditations, musings | 反义词：actions, deeds


词源:  From Old French *reflexion, from Latin reflexio, from reflectere "to bend back," from re- "back" + flectere "to bend."

过去完成时:
