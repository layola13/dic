cobber:/ˈkɒbə(r)/| n.|好朋友，伙伴，老兄|A close friend or mate, typically a male.  The term is primarily used in Australia and some parts of the UK.|例句：He's a good cobber; always ready to lend a hand. 他是个好朋友，总是乐于助人。|例句：I went to the pub with my cobbers last night. 昨晚我和我的伙伴们去了酒吧。|近义词：mate, pal, buddy, chum|反义词：enemy, foe
