mahonia:/məˈhoʊniə/| n. |十大功劳属植物|Mahonia is a genus of evergreen shrubs in the family Berberidaceae, known for their spiny leaves and clusters of yellow flowers.  They are native to Asia, North America, and Central America.  Many species are cultivated as ornamentals for their attractive foliage and flowers.|例句：The garden featured a vibrant mahonia bush, its yellow flowers a striking contrast to the dark green leaves. 花园里有一丛鲜艳的十大功劳，它的黄色花朵与深绿色的叶子形成了鲜明的对比。|近义词：Oregon grape (for some species)|反义词:|
