methicillins:/ˌmɛθɪˈsɪlɪnz/| n. |甲氧西林类抗生素|A group of semisynthetic penicillin antibiotics derived from 6-aminopenicillanic acid.  They are effective against a wide range of gram-positive bacteria, but resistance is a growing problem.|例句：The patient was treated with methicillins for a severe staphylococcal infection.  这位病人因严重的葡萄球菌感染而接受了甲氧西林类抗生素治疗。|近义词：penicillins, antibiotics|反义词：无
