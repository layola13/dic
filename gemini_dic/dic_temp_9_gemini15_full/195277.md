semiglaze:/ˈsɛmiɡleɪz/| n. |半釉，半光泽|A finish or coating that is partly glazed, giving a semi-gloss sheen. It's less shiny than a full glaze but more glossy than a matte finish.  |例句：The pottery displayed a beautiful semiglaze finish.  这件陶器呈现出美丽的半釉光泽。  The semiglaze on the tiles made them easy to clean.瓷砖上的半釉使其易于清洁。|近义词：partial glaze, satin finish|反义词：full glaze, matte finish, unglazed
