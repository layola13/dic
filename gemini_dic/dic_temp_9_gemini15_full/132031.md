noncorroboratory: /ˌnɒnkəˈrɒbəreɪtəri/ | adj. |未经证实的；不支持的| Not supporting or confirming a statement, theory, or finding.  Lacking corroboration; not tending to confirm. |例句：The witness’s testimony was noncorroboratory, casting doubt on the defendant's alibi.  证人的证词未经证实，使被告的不在场证明受到质疑。


The police found noncorroboratory evidence at the crime scene.  警方在犯罪现场发现了未经证实的证据。


近义词：unsubstantiated, unconfirmed, unsupported, unverified |反义词：corroborative, confirmatory, supporting, verifying
