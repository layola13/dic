semiherbaceous:/ˌsɛmiˈɜːrb(eɪ)ʃəs/| adj.| 半木质的，半草本的 | Partially herbaceous; having some woody characteristics but predominantly herbaceous.  Semiherbaceous plants typically die back to the ground in winter, but unlike fully herbaceous plants, they retain some woody stems or bases. | Example:  The semiherbaceous perennial thrived in the partly shaded garden.  这个半木质的多年生植物在半阴花园里茁壮成长。|近义词：subwoody, partly woody |反义词：herbaceous, woody
