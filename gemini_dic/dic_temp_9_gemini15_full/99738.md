intermarrying: /ˌɪntərˈmæriɪŋ/ | v. |通婚；异族通婚 | The act of marrying between different groups, typically ethnic or racial groups.  This often refers to a pattern or practice rather than a single instance. |例句：Intermarrying between different religious communities is becoming increasingly common in this region.  在这个地区，不同宗教社群间的通婚正变得越来越普遍。


例句： The study explored the social consequences of intermarrying between the two previously isolated tribes. 这项研究探讨了这两个先前隔离的部落之间通婚的社会后果。


近义词：cross-marrying, marrying across racial/ethnic lines


反义词：endogamy (marrying within one's own group)


词性：动词
