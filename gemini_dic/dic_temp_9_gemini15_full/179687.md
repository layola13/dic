reenlisting: /ˌriːˈɛnlɪstɪŋ/ | v. | 再次应征入伍；重新入伍 | The act of re-enlisting in the military;  signing up for another term of service after one's current enlistment has ended. | Example: He's considering reenlisting in the army after his tour of duty. 他正在考虑服役期满后再次参军。 | 近义词: re-enrolment, re-engagement | 反义词: discharge, leaving the service | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reenlists every five years. = 他每五年重新入伍一次。
She reenlists to gain more experience. = 她为了获得更多经验而重新入伍。

一般过去时:
He reenlisted last year. = 他去年重新入伍了。
She reenlisted after a short break. = 她在短暂休息后重新入伍了。

现在完成时:
He has reenlisted twice. = 他已经重新入伍两次了。
She has reenlisted in the same unit. = 她已经重新加入了同一个部队。

过去完成时:
He had reenlisted before the war started. = 在战争开始之前，他已经重新入伍了。
She had reenlisted before her promotion. = 在晋升之前，她已经重新入伍了。

将来时:
He will reenlist next year. = 他明年将重新入伍。
She will reenlist after her leave. = 她休假后将重新入伍。

虚拟语气:
If he were younger, he would reenlist. = 如果他年轻一些，他会重新入伍。
I wish I could reenlist, but I'm too old. = 我真希望我能重新入伍，但我年纪太大了。

被动语态:
He was reenlisted in the same regiment. = 他被重新编入同一个团。
Many soldiers are being reenlisted for the upcoming mission. = 许多士兵正在被重新征召参加即将到来的任务。

疑问句:
Will he reenlist? = 他会重新入伍吗？
Is she going to reenlist soon? = 她很快会重新入伍吗？

否定句:
He did not reenlist. = 他没有重新入伍。
She will not reenlist after this tour. = 她这次服役结束后将不会重新入伍。
