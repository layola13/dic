outrival:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvəl/| vt.|胜过，超过，比…强 |To surpass or exceed in excellence, achievement, or ability.  |例句：His latest novel outrivals all his previous works. 他的最新小说超过了他以前的所有作品。|例句：The company's new product outrivals its competitors in terms of functionality and design.  这家公司的新产品在功能和设计方面都胜过竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, excel, exceed, outdo, overshadow |反义词：be inferior to, be surpassed by |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new restaurant outrivals all others in the city. = 这家新餐厅胜过城里所有其他的餐厅。
Her talent outrivals that of her peers. = 她的天赋胜过她的同龄人。

一般过去时:
Last year's sales outrivaled those of the previous year. = 去年销售额超过了前一年。
His performance outrivaled all expectations. = 他的表现超出了所有预期。

现在完成时:
This technology has outrivaled all previous attempts. =这项技术已经超越了之前所有的尝试。
The team has outrivaled its opponents in every game this season. = 这个赛季球队在每一场比赛中都战胜了对手。

过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outrivaled all the other runners. = 比赛结束时，他已经超过了所有其他跑步者。
The company had outrivaled its competitors in the market before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经在市场上超越了其竞争对手。

将来时:
We expect that our new product will outrival the competition. = 我们预计我们的新产品将胜过竞争对手。
Next year's model will undoubtedly outrival this year's. = 明年的车型无疑将胜过今年的车型。

虚拟语气:
If only I had worked harder, I might have outrivaled him. = 如果我当时更努力工作，我或许就能胜过他了。
If she had trained more diligently, she would have outrivaled her rivals. = 如果她训练更刻苦，她就会胜过她的对手。

被动语态:
His work was outrivaled by a younger artist. = 他的作品被一位年轻的艺术家超越了。
The company's strategy was outrivaled by a more aggressive competitor. = 该公司的战略被一个更具侵略性的竞争对手超越了。

疑问句:
Does his new painting outrival his previous masterpieces? = 他的新画作是否胜过他之前的杰作？
Will this software outrival the existing options available in the market? = 这个软件是否会胜过市场上现有的选择？

否定句:
His efforts did not outrival those of his colleagues. = 他的努力没有超过他的同事。
The team's performance did not outrival the expectations of the fans. = 球队的表现没有超过球迷的预期。


PHRASE->
outrival in quality = 在质量上胜过
outrival in performance = 在性能上胜过
outrival competitors = 胜过竞争对手
outrival the best = 胜过最好的
outrival expectations = 超出预期
