euthanises:/ˈjuːθənətaɪzɪz/| v.|安乐死；实施安乐死|To kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to end suffering.|例句：The vet euthanised the suffering dog. 兽医对这只受苦的狗进行了安乐死。|例句：The family decided to euthanise their terminally ill pet.  这个家庭决定对他们患有绝症的宠物实施安乐死。|近义词：put down, dispatch|反义词：save, heal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The vet euthanises animals every day. = 兽医每天都对动物实施安乐死。
He euthanises pets that are suffering. = 他对受苦的宠物实施安乐死。

一般过去时:
The doctor euthanised the patient. = 医生对病人进行了安乐死。
They euthanised the horse after its accident. = 他们在马发生事故后对它进行了安乐死。

现在完成时:
The clinic has euthanised hundreds of animals this year. = 诊所今年已经对数百只动物进行了安乐死。
She has never euthanised a healthy animal. = 她从未对健康的动物实施过安乐死。

过去完成时:
The owner had already decided to euthanise the cat before the vet arrived. = 在兽医到达之前，主人已经决定对猫实施安乐死。
They had euthanised the dog earlier that day. = 他们当天早些时候已经对狗进行了安乐死。


将来时:
The vet will euthanise the dog tomorrow. = 兽医明天将对这只狗实施安乐死。
They will euthanise the animal if its condition worsens. = 如果动物的病情恶化，他们将对其进行安乐死。

虚拟语气:
If the animal were suffering greatly, I would euthanise it. = 如果动物遭受极大的痛苦，我会对它实施安乐死。
I wish I hadn't euthanised the pet so quickly. = 我希望我没有这么快就对宠物实施安乐死。

被动语态:
The animal was euthanised by the vet. = 动物被兽医实施了安乐死。
Many animals are euthanised each year in shelters. = 每年都有许多动物在收容所被实施安乐死。

疑问句:
Should the animal be euthanised? = 动物是否应该被实施安乐死？
Will the patient be euthanised soon? = 病人很快会被实施安乐死吗？

否定句:
The vet did not euthanise the cat. = 兽医没有对猫实施安乐死。
They refused to euthanise the dog. = 他们拒绝对狗实施安乐死。


PHRASE->
mercy killing (仁慈的杀戮):  often used synonymously with euthanasia
compassionate euthanasia (慈悲安乐死): emphasizes the humane aspect
assisted suicide (协助自杀): related but distinct, focuses on the individual's own action
veterinary euthanasia (兽医安乐死): specifically for animals
euthanasia debate (安乐死辩论): discussions surrounding the ethical and legal aspects
