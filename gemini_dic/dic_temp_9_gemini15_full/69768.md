ferm:/fɜːrm/| n. |农场；(尤指)耕地；(微生物的)培养基|A farm, especially one used for growing crops.  A nutrient medium used to cultivate microorganisms.|例句：He owns a large ferm in the countryside. 他在乡下拥有一家大型农场。|例句：The scientists are studying the growth of bacteria in a liquid ferm. 科学家们正在研究细菌在液体培养基中的生长。|近义词：farm, ranch, plantation|反义词：city, urban area|名词
