repay:/riːˈpeɪ/| vt. |偿还；报答；报复|To give back something that has been borrowed or received; to give something in return for a service, kindness, etc.; to retaliate or take revenge.|例句：I need to repay my student loan. 我需要偿还我的学生贷款。|例句：She repaid his kindness with a generous gift. 她以一份慷慨的礼物来报答他的好意。|近义词：return, reimburse, reciprocate|反义词：borrow, owe, receive|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I repay my debts regularly. = 我定期偿还债务。
They repay their employees for their hard work. = 他们报答员工的辛勤工作。

一般过去时:
He repaid the money he borrowed last week. = 他偿还了上周借的钱。
She repaid her friend's favor by helping him move. = 她通过帮助朋友搬家来报答朋友的恩情。

现在完成时:
I have repaid all my loans. = 我已经偿还了所有贷款。
She has repaid the kindness shown to her. = 她已经报答了对她表现出的善意。

过去完成时:
He had repaid the debt before the deadline. = 他在截止日期之前已经还清了债务。
She had repaid the favor long ago. = 她很久以前就报答了这份恩情。

将来时:
I will repay you soon. = 我很快就会还给你。
They will repay the investors for their contributions. = 他们将报答投资者的贡献。

虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would repay all my debts. = 如果我中了彩票，我会偿还所有债务。
I wish I could repay her for her help. = 我希望我能报答她的帮助。

被动语态:
The loan must be repaid on time. = 贷款必须按时偿还。
His kindness was repaid with gratitude. = 他的好意得到了感激的回报。

疑问句:
How will you repay the loan? = 你将如何偿还贷款？
When can I repay you for the dinner? = 我什么时候可以报答你请我吃饭的恩情？

否定句:
He didn't repay the money he owed. = 他没有偿还他欠的钱。
She hasn't repaid the favor yet. = 她还没有报答这份恩情。


PHRASE->
repay a debt = 偿还债务
repay a loan = 偿还贷款
repay a kindness = 报答恩情
repay a favor = 报答恩情
repay someone's trust = 辜负某人的信任 (负面含义，并非简单的“偿还”)
