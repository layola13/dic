electrons: /ɪˈlɛktrɒnz/ | n. | 电子 | A stable subatomic particle with a charge of negative electricity, found in all atoms and acting as the primary carrier of electricity.  Electrons orbit the atom's nucleus. | Example:  The flow of electrons creates an electric current.  电流是由电子的流动产生的。|近义词: negatron |反义词: proton (in terms of charge)
