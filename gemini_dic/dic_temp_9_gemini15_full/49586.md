demerit:/ˈdiːmɛrɪt/| n. |缺点；过失；处罚|A demerit is a fault or failing, or a penalty for a fault.  It often refers to a mark against someone's record, especially in school or the military. |例句：The student received a demerit for being late to class. 这个学生因为上课迟到受到了处分。

例句：His driving demerits included speeding and reckless driving. 他的驾驶缺点包括超速和鲁莽驾驶。


近义词：fault, flaw, defect, shortcoming, penalty

反义词：merit, advantage, virtue


词性:名词
