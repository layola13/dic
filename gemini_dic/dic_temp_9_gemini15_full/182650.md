reorganizing:/ˌriːˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪŋ/| v. |重新组织；改组；改编|The act of restructuring or rearranging something, typically a system, organization, or structure, to improve efficiency or effectiveness.  It implies a significant change to the existing arrangement.|例句：The company is reorganizing its sales department to improve customer service. 公司正在重组其销售部门以改善客户服务。

例句：They are reorganizing the library to make it more user-friendly.  他们正在重新整理图书馆，使其更方便用户使用。


近义词：restructuring, rearranging, reforming, reorganising (BrE)

反义词：disorganizing, dismantling


词性：动词


USAGE->

They are reorganizing the office layout. = 他们正在重新安排办公室布局。
The company reorganizes its structure annually. = 公司每年都会重组其架构。

将来时:
