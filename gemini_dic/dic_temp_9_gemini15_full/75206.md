frog: /frɒɡ/ /frɔːɡ/ | n. | 青蛙 | A small, tailless amphibian with smooth skin and long, powerful legs for leaping.  Frogs are usually found near water. | Example: The frog leaped into the pond. 那只青蛙跳进了池塘。| Near synonyms: toad, amphibian | Antonyms:  (There aren't direct antonyms, but contrasting creatures could be bird, snake) | noun
