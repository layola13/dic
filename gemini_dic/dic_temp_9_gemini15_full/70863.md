finlike:/ˈfɪnlaɪk/| adj.|鳍状的，像鳍的|Resembling a fin in shape or appearance.  The word combines "fin" (a limb of a fish) and "-like" (a suffix indicating resemblance).|例句：The creature had finlike appendages.  那个生物有鳍状的附属物。

The newly discovered species possessed finlike protrusions on its back. 新发现的物种背部长着鳍状突起。


近义词：fin-shaped, flipper-like


反义词：non-finlike


词性:形容词
