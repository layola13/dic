prohibitions:/ˌprɒhɪˈbɪʃənz/ | n. | 禁止；禁令 | The act of prohibiting or forbidding something; a rule or law that forbids something.  | Examples:  There are strict prohibitions against smoking in public places. (在公共场所吸烟是被严格禁止的。)  The government imposed new prohibitions on the import of certain goods. (政府对某些商品的进口施加了新的禁令。) |近义词：bans, restrictions, interdictions | 反义词：permissions, allowances


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The law prohibits driving under the influence of alcohol. = 法律禁止酒后驾车。
Many countries have prohibitions on the sale of certain drugs. = 许多国家禁止销售某些毒品。


一般过去时:
The company imposed prohibitions on the use of personal mobile phones. = 公司禁止使用个人手机。
Ancient societies often had prohibitions against certain foods. = 古代社会常常禁止食用某些食物。


现在完成时:
The city has implemented new prohibitions on parking in certain areas. = 该城市已实施了在某些区域停车的新禁令。
Several countries have recently introduced prohibitions on plastic bags. = 最近有几个国家出台了禁止使用塑料袋的禁令。


过去完成时:
Before the war, there had been prohibitions on the sale of weapons. = 战争爆发前，曾禁止销售武器。
They had already established prohibitions against unauthorized access. = 他们之前已经设立了禁止未经授权访问的禁令。


将来时:
The government will introduce stricter prohibitions on pollution. = 政府将出台更严格的污染禁令。
They will likely implement prohibitions on the use of drones in certain areas. = 他们很可能会在某些地区实施禁止使用无人机的禁令。


虚拟语气:
If there were no prohibitions, people would do whatever they wanted. = 如果没有禁令，人们就会为所欲为。
I wish there were fewer prohibitions on creative expression. = 我希望对创意表达的限制更少。


被动语态:
The sale of alcohol was prohibited during the festival. = 节日期间禁止销售酒精。
Several activities were prohibited due to safety concerns. = 出于安全考虑，禁止了几项活动。


疑问句:
Are there any prohibitions on photography inside the museum? = 在博物馆内部拍照是否有任何限制？
What prohibitions apply to visitors entering the country? = 进入该国的游客有哪些限制？


否定句:
There were no prohibitions against bringing food into the park. = 带食物进入公园没有限制。
The regulations did not include any prohibitions on public speaking. = 该规章制度不包括任何关于公开演讲的限制。


PHRASE->
impose prohibitions = 实施禁令

violate prohibitions = 违反禁令
subject to prohibitions = 受……限制
under prohibitions = 在禁止之下
