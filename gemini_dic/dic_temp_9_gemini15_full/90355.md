hoven:/ˈhoʊvən/| v.|悬停，盘旋|to remain in one place in the air without moving|The helicopter hovered above the building.|近义词：float, hang, drift|反义词：plummet, fall


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bird hovers above the flower. = 鸟在花朵上方盘旋。
The helicopter hovers over the accident scene. = 直升机盘旋在事故现场上空。

一般过去时:
The hummingbird hovered near the feeder. = 蜂鸟在喂食器附近盘旋。
The drone hovered silently above the crowd. = 无人机在人群上方静静地盘旋。

现在完成时:
The hawk has hovered for several minutes. = 鹰已经盘旋了几分钟了。
The helicopter has hovered in the same spot since noon. = 直升机从中午起就一直停留在同一位置。

过去完成时:
The eagle had hovered before it swooped down. = 鹰在俯冲下来之前一直在盘旋。
The plane had hovered briefly before landing. = 飞机在降落前短暂地盘旋了一下。


将来时:
The helicopter will hover while they rescue the people. = 直升机将在他们营救人员时盘旋。
The drone will hover above the field for surveillance. = 无人机将盘旋在田地上空进行监控。

虚拟语气:
If the helicopter hovered longer, we could get a better view. = 如果直升机盘旋得更久一些，我们可以看得更清楚。
I wish the butterfly would hover a little closer. = 我希望蝴蝶能再靠近一些。


被动语态:
The camera was hovered above the stage. = 相机被悬停在舞台上方。
The drone is hovered by remote control. = 无人机通过遥控器进行悬停。


疑问句:
Does the bee hover before it lands? = 蜜蜂在降落前是否会盘旋？
How long will the helicopter hover? = 直升机将盘旋多久？


否定句:
The plane didn't hover long enough for a clear picture. = 飞机盘旋的时间不够长，无法拍到清晰的照片。
The bird did not hover; it flew directly to its nest. = 鸟没有盘旋，而是直接飞到它的巢穴。


PHRASE->
hover over: 盘旋于...之上

hover in place: 悬停在原地
hover anxiously: 焦急地盘旋
