patchworkers:/ˈpætʃˌwɜːrkərz/| n. |修补匠；拼缝工人；（尤指）从事零工的人|People who do patchwork, often implying a temporary or makeshift nature to their work. They may take on various small jobs or tasks, lacking a consistent or specialized occupation.  |The patchworkers were busy mending the torn sails before the next voyage.|近义词：handymen, odd-jobbers, freelancers|反义词：specialist, professional
