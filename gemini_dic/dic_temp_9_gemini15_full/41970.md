cosset:/ˈkɒsɪt/| vt. n.| 宠爱；溺爱；娇惯 | To treat with excessive care and attention; pamper. | 例句：She cosseted her only child. 她娇惯她唯一的儿女。| 例句：The cosseted child grew up spoiled.  被娇惯长大的孩子变得骄纵。| 近义词：pamper, spoil, indulge | 反义词：neglect, ignore, discipline

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cosset their grandchildren. = 他们溺爱他们的孙辈。
He cossets his pet dog. = 他娇惯他的宠物狗。

一般过去时:
My parents cosseted me when I was young. = 我小时候父母很宠爱我。
She cosseted him throughout his illness. = 在他生病期间，她一直悉心照顾他。

现在完成时:
She has cosseted her son for too long. = 她溺爱儿子太久了。
They have cosseted the project for years. = 他们多年来一直对这个项目过度呵护。

过去完成时:
They had cosseted the child so much that he became spoiled. = 他们把孩子宠坏了，以至于他变得骄纵。
She had cosseted him for years, leading to his lack of independence. = 她多年来一直娇惯他，导致他缺乏独立性。

将来时:
They will cosset their new baby. = 他们将会溺爱他们的新生儿。
We will cosset ourselves with a relaxing weekend. = 我们会在轻松的周末好好犒劳自己。


虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would cosset myself with luxuries. = 如果我有钱，我会用奢侈品来犒劳自己。
I wish I could cosset my family more. = 我希望我能更宠爱我的家人。

被动语态:
The child was cosseted by his grandparents. = 这个孩子被他的祖父母宠爱着。
The project has been cosseted by the management. = 这个项目一直受到管理层的过度保护。

疑问句:
Do they cosset their children too much? = 他们是不是太溺爱孩子了？
Does she cosset her plants? = 她是不是很娇惯她的植物？


否定句:
They don't cosset their children. = 他们不溺爱他们的孩子。
He didn't cosset her when she was sick. = 他在她生病的时候并没有特别照顾她。
