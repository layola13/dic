misjudgers:/mɪsˈdʒʌdʒərz/| n. |误判者，错估者 |Those who make incorrect judgments or assessments.  People who consistently misjudge situations or individuals. |例句：The misjudgers of the market were caught off guard by the sudden surge in prices. 市场误判者被价格的突然飙升打了个措手不及。


The misjudgers of his character failed to see his inherent kindness. 那些误判他性格的人没有看到他内在的善良。|近义词：erroneous judges, miscalculators |反义词：wise judges, shrewd assessors |名词
