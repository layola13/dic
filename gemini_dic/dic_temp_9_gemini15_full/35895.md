cocooner:/kəˈkuːnər/| n. |茧居者；茧蛹| A person who spends a great deal of time alone, often avoiding social interaction.  It can also refer to an insect in its cocoon stage. |Example:  He became a cocooner after a series of personal setbacks. 他在经历一系列挫折后变成了茧居者。|The caterpillar in its cocoon is a cocooner.  在茧里的毛毛虫是一个茧蛹。|近义词： recluse, hermit, shut-in |反义词：social butterfly, extrovert |名词
