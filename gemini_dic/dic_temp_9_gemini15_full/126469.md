mythologers:/maɪˈθɒlədʒərz/ | n. |神话学家，神话研究者 |Those who study mythology, creating or interpreting myths.  They might be academics, writers, or artists focusing on myths and legends. |例句：Many mythologers believe the story of Icarus reflects a fear of hubris.  许多神话学家认为伊卡洛斯的传说反映了对傲慢的恐惧。

例句：The conference brought together leading mythologers from around the globe.  这次会议汇聚了来自世界各地的顶尖神话学家。


近义词：mythologists, mythographers, folklore scholars


反义词：无


词性：名词
