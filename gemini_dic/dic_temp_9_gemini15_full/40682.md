conversationist:/ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənɪst/| n. | 健谈者，擅长交谈者 | A person who is skilled in or enjoys conversation.  Someone who is a good conversationalist is able to engage others in stimulating and enjoyable conversations. They are often articulate, witty, and knowledgeable. | Example:  He's a delightful conversationist; I could listen to him talk for hours. 他是个令人愉快的健谈者；我可以听他说话几个小时。|近义词：chatty person, talker, conversationalist |反义词：mute, taciturn
