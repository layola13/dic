pilings:/ˈpaɪlɪŋz/| n.|桩基；桩列|Large, strong posts or beams driven into the ground to support a structure, such as a bridge, pier, or building.|例句：The bridge was built on sturdy pilings driven deep into the riverbed. 这座桥建在深深打入河床的坚固桩基上。 The construction workers are installing the pilings for the new wharf. 建筑工人正在安装新码头的桩基。|近义词：piles, supports, foundations|反义词：N/A
