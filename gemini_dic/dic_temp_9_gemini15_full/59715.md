eclecticists:/ɪˌklɛkˈtɪsɪsts/ | n. | 折衷主义者，兼容并蓄者 |  Those who adopt eclecticism, selecting elements from diverse sources. Eclecticists often draw from multiple philosophies, styles, or approaches without adhering strictly to any single one.  | The art critic praised the painter for being an eclecticist, incorporating elements of Cubism, Surrealism, and Impressionism into his work.  | synthesists, integrators | purists, traditionalists | 名词
