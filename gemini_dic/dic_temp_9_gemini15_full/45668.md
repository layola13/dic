curtainless:/ˈkɜːrtənləs/| adj. | 无窗帘的，没有幕布的 | Lacking curtains; without curtains.  The term describes a state or condition of absence of curtains.  It can be used to describe rooms, windows, or stages that are devoid of curtains. | Example: The stage was curtainless, revealing the backstage area to the audience.  舞台上没有幕布，让观众看到了后台区域。 The hotel room was surprisingly curtainless, offering little privacy.  令人惊讶的是，酒店房间没有窗帘，几乎没有隐私。 | 近义词：uncurtained, bare | 反义词：curtained
