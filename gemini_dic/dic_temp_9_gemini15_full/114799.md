malvaceous:/mælˈveɪʃəs/| adj.|锦葵科的；似锦葵的|Of or relating to the Malvaceae, a family of flowering plants including cotton, hibiscus, and hollyhock.  The term often describes characteristics similar to those plants, such as having mallow-like flowers.|Example: The malvaceous plant had large, showy flowers.  这株锦葵科植物开着大型艳丽的花朵。|近义词：mallow-like|反义词：无
