exceed:/ɪkˈsiːd/ | vt. |超过；胜过；超出 | To go beyond the limits of; surpass.  |例句：The costs exceeded the budget. 成本超过了预算。|例句：Her performance exceeded all expectations. 她的表现超出了所有人的预期。|近义词：surpass, surpass, outstrip, transcend |反义词：fall short of,  underperform


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The demand exceeds the supply. = 需求超过供应。
His achievements exceed expectations. = 他的成就超出了预期。

一般过去时:
The temperature exceeded 100 degrees Fahrenheit. = 温度超过了华氏100度。
The project exceeded its deadline. = 项目超过了截止日期。

现在完成时:
The company has exceeded its sales targets this year. = 公司今年已超过了销售目标。
The book has exceeded all expectations in terms of sales. = 这本书的销量超出了所有预期。

过去完成时:
The damage had exceeded our worst fears. = 破坏程度已经超过了我们最坏的担忧。
They had exceeded their authority. = 他们滥用了职权。

将来时:
We expect that the cost will exceed the budget. = 我们预计成本将超过预算。
The population is projected to exceed one billion by 2050. = 预计到2050年人口将超过十亿。

虚拟语气:
If the temperature exceeded 100 degrees, the experiment would have failed. = 如果温度超过100度，实验就会失败。
I wish my salary exceeded 100,000 dollars a year. = 我希望我的年薪超过10万美元。

被动语态:
The speed limit should not be exceeded. = 不应该超过限速。
The budget was exceeded by a significant margin. = 预算被大幅度超支。

疑问句:
Does the speed exceed the limit? = 速度超过限速了吗？
Did the cost exceed the budget? = 成本超过预算了吗？

否定句:
The profit did not exceed the expectation. = 利润没有超过预期。
The project did not exceed its budget. = 项目没有超出预算。
