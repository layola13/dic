quaking:/ˈkweɪkɪŋ/| adj.|颤抖的；发抖的；摇晃的|shaking violently; trembling.|The ground was quaking after the earthquake. 地震后地面在颤抖。The leaves quaked in the strong wind. 叶子在强风中颤抖。|近义词：trembling, shaking, shivering|反义词：still, calm, steady


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ground is quaking. = 地面在颤抖。
He is quaking with fear. = 他吓得发抖。

一般过去时:
The house quaked during the storm. = 房屋在暴风雨中摇晃。
She quaked at the sight of the snake. = 她看到蛇吓得发抖。

现在完成时:
The earth has quaked several times this year. = 今年地球已经震动了几次。
My hands have been quaking since I heard the news. = 自从听到这个消息以来，我的手一直在颤抖。

过去完成时:
The building had quaked violently before collapsing. = 建筑物剧烈摇晃后倒塌了。
She had quaked with fear before she spoke. = 她说话前吓得发抖。

将来时:
The volcano will quake again soon. = 火山很快会再次喷发（伴随地震）。
I think I will quake if I see him. = 我想如果我看到他我会害怕得发抖。


虚拟语气:
If the ground quaked again, we'd be in serious trouble. = 如果地面再次震动，我们将陷入严重的麻烦。
I wish my hands wouldn't quake so much when I'm nervous. = 我希望我紧张的时候手不会抖得这么厉害。

被动语态:
The building was quaked by the explosion. = 建筑物被爆炸震动了。(被动语态较少用在此词)
The picture was almost quaked off the wall. = 画几乎被震落墙了。(被动语态较少用在此词)

疑问句:
Is the ground quaking? = 地面在颤抖吗？
Are you quaking in your boots? = 你害怕得发抖吗？(习语)


否定句:
The ground is not quaking. = 地面没有在颤抖。
He did not quake in the face of danger. = 他面对危险没有害怕。
