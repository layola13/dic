词源:
The word "rageful" is derived from the Old French word "rage," meaning "madness, fury," ultimately from the Latin word "rabies," meaning "madness, rage."  The suffix "-ful" is an Old English suffix meaning "full of". Thus, "rageful" literally means "full of rage".
