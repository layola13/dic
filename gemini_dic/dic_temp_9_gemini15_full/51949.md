diabolo:/ˈdaɪəbəʊləʊ/| n. |空竹，抖空竹|A diabolo is a type of toy consisting of two cups connected by an axle, spun using strings.  It is often used in juggling and street performances.  |例句：He skillfully manipulated the diabolo, making it spin and dance in the air. 他熟练地操控着空竹，让它在空中旋转飞舞。

例句：My son loves to play with his diabolo; it's a fun and challenging toy. 我的儿子喜欢玩他的空竹；这是一个有趣且具有挑战性的玩具。|近义词：spinning top, juggling toy|反义词: None
