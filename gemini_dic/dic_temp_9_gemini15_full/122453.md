mobiles:/ˈmoʊbaɪlz/| n. |移动电话；手机；移动装置|A mobile is a device that can make and receive telephone calls over a radio link, while being able to move around.  Mobiles have become ubiquitous in modern society.|例句：I need to charge my mobile phone. 我需要给我的手机充电。|例句：Many mobiles now offer high-resolution cameras. 许多手机现在都提供高分辨率的相机。|近义词：cell phone, cellphone, handphone|反义词：landline
