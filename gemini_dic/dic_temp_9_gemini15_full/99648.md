interlays:/ˌɪntərˈleɪz/ | v. | 交错；层叠；穿插 | To interweave or interpose between layers; to put (something) between layers of something else.  | 例句：The artist interlays the fabric with gold thread.  艺术家用金线交错织入织物。

例句：The baker interlays layers of sponge cake with cream and fruit. 面包师将海绵蛋糕层层叠加，中间涂抹奶油和水果。


近义词：interweave, interpose, intersperse | 反义词：separate, divide
