profane:/proˈfeɪn/| adj.|亵渎的；不敬的；世俗的；粗俗的|To treat something sacred with contempt or disrespect; to violate or desecrate something holy;  to be commonplace or vulgar.|例句：It is profane to use God's name in vain.  滥用上帝之名是亵渎的。|例句：His language was profane and offensive. 他的语言粗俗且冒犯。|近义词：sacrilegious, blasphemous, irreverent, vulgar, obscene|反义词：sacred, holy, reverent, respectful
