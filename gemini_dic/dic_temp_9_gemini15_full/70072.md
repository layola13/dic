feudalize: /ˈfjuːdəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使封建化；使具有封建制度的特征 | To organize or govern according to feudal principles; to introduce or impose feudal practices or characteristics upon.  | The Norman Conquest feudalized England. = 诺曼征服使英格兰封建化了。 The king attempted to feudalize the newly conquered territories. = 国王试图使新征服的领土封建化。 | 近义词：feudalise (British spelling) | 反义词：modernize, democratize


USAGE->

He feudalizes his company's management. = 他使公司的管理具有封建色彩。
