redisburses:  /ˌriːdɪsˈbɜːrsəz/ | v. | 再分配，重新分配 | To distribute again; to redistribute.  The word is a combination of "re-" (again) and "disburse" (to distribute). | 例句：The company redisbursed the funds to its various departments. 公司将资金重新分配给各个部门。


例句：The government redisbursed the aid packages to ensure equitable distribution. 政府重新分配援助物资以确保公平分配。


近义词：redistribute, reallocate, reallot


反义词：centralize, concentrate


词性：动词
