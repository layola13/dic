guessed:/ɡɛst/| v.|猜测，推测|To form an opinion or make a judgment about something without sufficient evidence; to suppose something to be true.|I guessed the answer correctly. 我猜对了答案。 He guessed that she was upset. 他猜到她不高兴了。|assumed, speculated, surmised|knew, confirmed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I guess the answer. = 我猜答案。
She guesses the meaning of the word. = 她猜单词的意思。

一般过去时:
I guessed the riddle. = 我猜中了谜语。
They guessed wrong. = 他们猜错了。

现在完成时:
I have guessed the password. = 我已经猜到密码了。
She has guessed correctly several times. = 她已经猜对好几次了。

过去完成时:
I had guessed the outcome before it happened. = 这件事发生之前我就猜到了结果。
He had guessed that she would leave. = 他早猜到她会离开。

将来时:
I will guess the number. = 我将猜这个数字。
They will guess the winner.  = 他们会猜赢家。

虚拟语气:
If I guessed correctly, I would win the prize. = 如果我猜对了，我就能赢得奖品。
I wish I had guessed the answer. = 我真希望我猜对了答案。

被动语态:
The answer was guessed correctly. = 答案被猜对了。
The winner was guessed by many people. = 很多人都猜到了赢家。

疑问句:
What did you guess? = 你猜什么？
Did you guess the answer? = 你猜到答案了吗？

否定句:
I didn't guess the answer. = 我没有猜到答案。
She didn't guess his intentions. = 她没有猜到他的意图。


PHRASE->
guess what = 你猜怎么着

educated guess = 估计，推测
rough guess = 粗略估计
