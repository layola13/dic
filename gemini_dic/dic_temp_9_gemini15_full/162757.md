prebestowing: /ˌpriːbɪˈstoʊɪŋ/ | v. | 预先给予；提前授予 |  The act of bestowing something beforehand; granting or conferring something in advance.  This word is extremely rare and largely found only in specialized or archaic contexts. It combines the prefix "pre-" (before) with "bestowing" (giving generously). |例句：The king prebestowed the land upon his loyal subject before his official coronation. 国王在正式加冕之前就将土地预先赐予了他的忠诚臣民。  The generous benefactor prebestowed a scholarship on the promising student.  慷慨的捐助者预先为这位有前途的学生提供了奖学金。| 近义词:  precede,  antecede,  precede with | 反义词: withhold,  defer,  postpone | 动词
