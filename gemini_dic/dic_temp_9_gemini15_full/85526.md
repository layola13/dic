hatbands: /ˈhætˌbændz/ | n. |帽带；帽箍| A band worn around a hat, often for decoration or to hold the hat in place.  Hatbands can be made from various materials, including ribbon, fabric, leather, or metal. They are often used to add a personal touch to a hat or to complement an outfit.|The woman wore a hat with a beautiful velvet hatband.  这位女士戴着一顶带有漂亮丝绒帽带的帽子。|  Many antique hats feature elaborate hatbands. 许多古董帽都带有精美的帽带。|  ribbon, band | (近义词无明显反义词)| 名词
