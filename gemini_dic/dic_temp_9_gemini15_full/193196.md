sedition:/sɪˈdɪʃən/| n.|煽动叛乱；叛乱；暴动|The incitement of discontent or rebellion against a government.  Sedition is often expressed through speech, writing, or actions aimed at undermining authority.|例句：The government cracked down on sedition after the protests.  政府在抗议活动后镇压了煽动叛乱的行为。

例句：Several individuals were arrested for their involvement in the seditious plot.  有几个人因参与煽动叛乱的阴谋而被捕。


近义词：insurrection, rebellion, mutiny, revolt, subversion|反义词：loyalty, obedience, allegiance|名词
