institutionalize:/ɪnˌstɪtjuːʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使制度化；使成为制度；收容于机构 | To establish something as a norm or standard practice within an institution or society.  It can also refer to the act of placing someone in an institution, often a mental hospital or similar facility. | 例句：The company institutionalized its new safety procedures. 公司将新的安全程序制度化了。

The government decided to institutionalize the mentally ill patient. 政府决定将这位精神病人送进疗养院。| 近义词：formalize, systematize, establish, commit | 反义词：abolish, dismantle, deinstitutionalize
