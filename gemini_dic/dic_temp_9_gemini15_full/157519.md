pistilliferous:/ˌpɪstɪˈlɪfərəs/| adj.|具有雌蕊的；有雌蕊的 |Bearing pistils; having pistils.  The term refers to flowers or plants that possess pistils, the female reproductive organs of a flower.|例句：The pistilliferous flowers of the squash plant are responsible for producing fruit.  南瓜植物的雌花负责结果实。

The pistilliferous plants were easily identified by their large, swollen ovaries. 那些具有雌蕊的植物很容易通过它们大而膨大的子房来识别。|近义词：pistillate|反义词：staminate
