shirtless:/ˈʃɜːrtləs/| adj.| 赤裸上身的；不穿衬衫的 | Without a shirt.  Describes a person who is not wearing a shirt. |例句：The man was shirtless on the beach.  那个男人在海滩上赤裸着上身。

例句：He felt comfortable and relaxed being shirtless in the hot weather. 他在炎热的天气里赤裸上身感觉很舒服自在。


近义词：bare-chested, topless


反义词：shirted, clothed
