perron:/ˈpɛrən/| n.|台阶，平台 | A perron is a grand staircase, especially one leading to the entrance of a building.  It often implies a sense of formality and grandeur. |例句：The palace was approached by a magnificent perron of white marble.  宫殿由一个壮丽的白色大理石台阶通向。

The guests ascended the perron to enter the grand ballroom. 客人们拾级而上，进入宏伟的舞厅。|近义词：staircase, steps, platform |反义词：ground level, flat surface
