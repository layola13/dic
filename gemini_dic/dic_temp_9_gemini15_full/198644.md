shute:/ʃuːt/| n. |滑槽；滑道；（矿井的）倾斜巷道|A chute is a sloping channel or trough used to convey something (such as grain, coal, or rubbish) from a higher to a lower level.|例句：The workers loaded the grain into the chute. 工人们把谷物装进滑槽。The garbage chute was blocked. 垃圾滑道堵塞了。|近义词：slide, trough, channel|反义词：lift, elevator
