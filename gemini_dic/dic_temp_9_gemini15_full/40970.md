cooping:/ˈkuːpɪŋ/ | v. |关在鸡舍里；限制；欺骗 | to confine (someone or something) in a coop or small space; to restrict or confine; to cheat or swindle | 例句：The farmer is cooping up the chickens for the night.  农民正在把鸡关进鸡舍过夜。


They're cooping up the children indoors all day. 他们整天把孩子们关在室内。

近义词：confine, restrict, cage, defraud | 反义词：liberate, release, free
