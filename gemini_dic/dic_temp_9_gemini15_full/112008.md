long:/lɒŋ/ /lɔːŋ/| adj., adv., n.|长的；长时间；长音|Long describes something that has considerable extent from end to end.  It can refer to physical length, duration, or even abstract concepts. As an adverb, it modifies verbs to indicate a duration. As a noun, it refers to a long period of time or a long sound.|例句：The road is very long. 这条路很长。|The meeting lasted a long time. 会议持续了很长时间。|近义词：extended, lengthy, prolonged|反义词：short, brief, concise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river is long. = 这条河很长。
This meeting is long. = 这个会议很冗长。


一般过去时:
