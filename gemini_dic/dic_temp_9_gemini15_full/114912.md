manacling: /məˈnæklɪŋ/ | v. | 束缚；戴上手铐 | The act of restraining someone's hands with handcuffs or shackles.  This verb implies a restriction of movement and often suggests a forceful or coercive act. | 例句：The police manacled the suspect before transporting him to the station. = 警察在押送嫌疑犯到警局之前给他戴上了手铐。


The prison guards manacled the riotous inmates to prevent further violence. = 监狱警卫给闹事的囚犯戴上手铐以防止进一步的暴力行为。


近义词：handcuffing, shackling, restraining, binding | 反义词：freeing, liberating, releasing | 词性：动词
