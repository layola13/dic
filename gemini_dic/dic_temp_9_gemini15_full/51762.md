devotee:/ˌdɛvoˈtiː/| n. |热心崇拜者；信徒；忠实拥护者 |A devotee is a person who is very enthusiastic and loyal in their admiration or support of someone or something.  They are often deeply committed and passionate. |例句：He is a devotee of classical music. 他是古典音乐的爱好者。

She's a devoted devotee of yoga and meditation. 她是瑜伽和冥想的忠实信徒。

|近义词：admirer, enthusiast, follower, aficionado, fan |反义词：critic, opponent, detractor |名词
