pedlars:/ˈpedlərz/| n.|小贩，货郎|A pedlar is a person who travels from place to place selling small goods.|例句：The pedlar walked from village to village, selling his wares. 货郎走村串户，兜售他的货物。
The old pedlar had a cart full of trinkets. 老货郎的板车上装满了小饰品。|近义词：hawker, vendor, itinerant trader|反义词：retailer (in a fixed location)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pedlar sells his goods in the market. = 小贩在市场上卖他的货物。
He peddles his wares from door to door. = 他挨家挨户地兜售商品。

一般过去时:
The pedlar sold all his goods yesterday. = 小贩昨天卖掉了所有的货物。
They peddled their wares at the fair. = 他们在集市上兜售商品。

现在完成时:
The pedlar has sold most of his stock this week. = 小贩本周已经卖掉了大部分存货。
She has peddled her goods across the country. = 她已经在全国各地兜售她的商品。

过去完成时:
The pedlar had already sold his best items before the festival. = 节日前小贩已经卖掉了最好的商品。
They had peddled their wares for many years before they retired. = 他们退休前已经兜售商品很多年了。

将来时:
The pedlar will travel to the next town tomorrow. = 小贩明天将前往下一个城镇。
He will peddle his goods at the upcoming market. = 他将在即将到来的市场上兜售他的货物。

虚拟语气:
If the pedlar had more goods, he would make more money. = 如果小贩有更多货物，他会赚更多钱。
I wish the pedlar would come to our village. = 我希望小贩能来我们村子。

被动语态:

His wares are sold all over the town. = 他的商品在整个城镇都有售卖。

疑问句:

否定句:

He doesn't peddle his goods on Sundays. = 他星期天不兜售商品。
