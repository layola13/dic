lo:/ləʊ/| interj.| 瞧，看|An exclamation used to attract attention or express surprise or disapproval.|例句：Lo, and behold! 瞧，看！|近义词：look, see, behold|反义词:None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Lo, the sun rises in the east. = 瞧，太阳从东方升起。
Lo, a ship appears on the horizon. = 瞧，地平线上出现了一艘船。


一般过去时:
Lo, the king rode through the streets. = 瞧，国王骑马穿过街道。
Lo, the storm raged throughout the night. = 瞧，暴风雨肆虐了一整夜。


现在完成时:
Lo, the birds have built their nests. = 瞧，鸟儿们已经筑好了巢。
Lo, the flowers have bloomed at last. = 瞧，花儿终于开了。


过去完成时:
Lo, the battle had already been won. = 瞧，战斗早已结束。
Lo, the thief had already escaped. = 瞧，小偷早已逃跑了。


将来时:
Lo, the comet will soon appear. = 瞧，彗星很快就会出现。
Lo, the ship will sail tomorrow. = 瞧，船明天就要启航了。


虚拟语气:
Lo, if the king were here, all would be well. = 瞧，如果国王在这里，一切都会好起来。
Lo, if I had known, I would have acted differently. = 瞧，如果我知道，我会采取不同的行动。


被动语态:
Lo, the city was destroyed by the earthquake. = 瞧，这座城市被地震摧毁了。
Lo, the truth was concealed from the public. = 瞧，真相被隐瞒了公众。


疑问句:
Lo, what is that sound? = 瞧，那是什么声音？
Lo, where did he go? = 瞧，他去哪儿了？


否定句:
Lo, the treasure was not found. = 瞧，宝藏没有找到。
Lo, he did not see the danger. = 瞧，他没有看到危险。


PHRASE->
Lo and behold! = 瞧！看！
Lo, the poor fellow! = 瞧，这个可怜的家伙！
Lo, how the time flies! = 瞧，时间过得真快！
Lo, here it is! = 看，在这儿！
Lo, what a sight! = 瞧，多么壮观的景象！
