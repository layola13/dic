cross-division:/krɒs dɪˈvɪʒən/ | adj. n. |跨部门的，跨部门合作|Relating to or involving more than one division or department within an organization.  It can refer to a project, collaboration, or a problem that spans multiple departments.|例句：The new marketing campaign requires cross-divisional collaboration. 新的营销活动需要跨部门合作。


The company implemented a cross-divisional project to improve efficiency. 公司实施了一个跨部门项目以提高效率。|近义词：interdepartmental, multidepartmental |反义词：intradepartmental
