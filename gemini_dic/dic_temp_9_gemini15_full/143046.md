outblundered: /ˌaʊtˈblʌndərd/ | v. | 技高一筹地击败，以愚笨的错误战胜 |  To surpass someone in making blunders; to defeat someone by making more or greater mistakes than they did.  |  例句：He outblundered his opponent in the debate, making so many mistakes that the opponent looked competent by comparison. 他在辩论中以愚笨的错误战胜了他的对手，犯了那么多错误，以至于对手相比之下看起来很能干。

例句：Despite his clumsy strategy, he outblundered his more experienced rival and won the game. 尽管他的策略笨拙，但他还是以愚笨的错误战胜了他更有经验的对手，赢得了比赛。


近义词：outsmarted (but with a connotation of cleverness, not blundering), outmaneuvered, outwitted


反义词：outperformed (implies superior skill and strategy),  defeated skillfully


词性：动词
