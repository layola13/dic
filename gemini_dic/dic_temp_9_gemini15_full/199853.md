sits:/sɪts/| v.|坐，位于|To rest one's weight on one's buttocks; be in a seated position.|例句：He sits at his desk all day. 他整天坐在办公桌前。|例句：The house sits on a hill overlooking the ocean.房子坐落在俯瞰大海的山丘上。|近义词：rests, perches, squats|反义词：stands, rises


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sits quietly. = 他静静地坐着。
The cat sits on the mat. = 猫坐在垫子上。

一般过去时:
She sat down and rested. = 她坐下来休息了。
They sat through the entire movie. = 他们看完了一整部电影。

现在完成时:
He has sat here for hours. = 他已经在这里坐了几个小时了。
She has sat patiently waiting for her turn. = 她耐心地坐着等待轮到她。


过去完成时:
I had sat there for a while before she arrived. = 在她到达之前我已经在那里坐了一会儿了。
They had sat down to eat when the phone rang. = 他们刚坐下来吃饭，电话就响了。


将来时:
I will sit here and wait for you. = 我将坐在这里等你。
They will sit together at the ceremony. = 他们将在仪式上坐在一起。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would sit down and relax. = 如果我是你，我会坐下放松。
I wish I could sit by the beach all day. = 我希望我能整天坐在海滩边。


被动语态:
The chairs are usually sat on by many people. = 这些椅子通常被很多人坐。 (This is a less common usage)
This seat should be sat on only by authorized personnel. = 这个座位只有授权人员才能坐。 (This is a less common usage)


疑问句:
Where do you sit in class? = 你上课坐在哪里？
Does he usually sit in the front row? = 他通常坐在前排吗？


否定句:
He doesn't sit still for a minute. = 他一刻也坐不住。
She didn't sit down during the whole meeting. = 整个会议她都没有坐下。
