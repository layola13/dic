sevenths:/ˈsɛvənθs/| n. |七分之一；七个中的一个|The word "sevenths" is the plural form of "seventh," which refers to one of seven equal parts of a whole.  It can be used as a fraction (1/7, 2/7, etc.) or to describe the position of something within a sequence of seven.|Example:  Two sevenths of the cake were eaten.  蛋糕的七分之二被吃掉了。  The seventh runner crossed the finish line.第七个跑步者冲过了终点线。|近义词：parts, fractions|反义词：wholes
