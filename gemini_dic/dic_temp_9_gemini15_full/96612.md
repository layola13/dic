indulged:/ɪnˈdʌldʒd/| v.|沉溺；纵容；满足；使沉迷|To allow oneself to enjoy the pleasure of something.  To give someone whatever they want. |例句：He indulged in a large piece of chocolate cake. 他吃了一大块巧克力蛋糕。（纵容自己）


例句：She indulged her children's every whim. 她满足了孩子们每一个愿望。（纵容他人）


近义词：pamper, spoil, gratify, humor|反义词：deny, restrain, restrict|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I indulge myself with a hot bath every evening. = 我每天晚上都用热水泡澡来犒劳自己。
He indulges in expensive hobbies. = 他沉迷于昂贵的爱好。


一般过去时:
She indulged her craving for chocolate. = 她满足了她对巧克力的渴望。
They indulged in a long conversation. = 他们进行了长时间的交谈。


现在完成时:
I have indulged in too many sweets lately. = 我最近吃太多甜食了。
She has indulged her artistic talents for years. = 她多年来一直培养自己的艺术才能。


过去完成时:
He had indulged in several glasses of wine before driving home. = 他在开车回家之前喝了几杯酒。
They had indulged in a lavish celebration after their victory. = 他们胜利后举行了一场奢华的庆祝活动。


将来时:
I will indulge myself with a vacation next month. = 我下个月要给自己放个假。
They will indulge in some well-deserved rest after the project. = 项目结束后，他们将好好休息一下。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would indulge in my hobby of painting. = 如果我有更多时间，我会沉迷于我的绘画爱好。
I wish I could indulge in a long holiday. = 我希望我能享受一个长假。


被动语态:
His desires were indulged by his parents. = 他的愿望得到了父母的满足。
Her every whim was indulged. = 她每一个愿望都被满足了。


疑问句:
Do you often indulge in unhealthy snacks? = 你经常吃不健康的零食吗？
Does he indulge his children too much? = 他是不是太溺爱孩子了？


否定句:
I don't indulge in gossip. = 我不参与八卦。
She didn't indulge her anger. = 她没有放纵自己的怒火。


PHRASE->
indulge in = 沉溺于；纵容
indulge oneself = 放纵自己
indulge a whim = 满足一时兴起
give in to indulgence = 屈服于放纵
indulge one's passion = 满足自己的热情
