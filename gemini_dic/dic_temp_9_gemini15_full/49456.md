deltohedron:/ˌdeltəˈhiːdrən/| n. |三角形多面体|A polyhedron whose faces are all triangles.  Deltohedra are a class of polyhedra with triangular faces, and each face shares an edge with exactly two other faces. They are not necessarily regular.  |Example:  A tetrahedron is a simple example of a deltohedron.|近义词:polyhedron|反义词:None
