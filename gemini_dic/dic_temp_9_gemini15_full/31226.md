chant:/tʃænt/| n., v.|吟唱，反复吟诵|A repetitive rhythmical utterance of sounds, especially one used in a religious ceremony or by a group of people to express their feelings or support.|例句：The monks chanted prayers all night.  僧侣整夜诵经。|例句：The crowd chanted the team's name.  人群高喊着队名。|近义词：sing, recite, hum, carol|反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chant slogans at every rally. = 他们在每次集会上高喊口号。
The choir chants hymns during the service. = 合唱团在仪式上吟唱赞美诗。

一般过去时:
The protesters chanted angrily outside the parliament building. = 抗议者在议会大厦外愤怒地高喊口号。
She chanted a lullaby to her baby. = 她给婴儿哼唱催眠曲。

现在完成时:
The fans have chanted their support for their team throughout the season. = 粉丝们整个赛季都在高喊支持他们的球队。
They have chanted this mantra for years. = 他们多年来一直念诵这个咒语。

过去完成时:
The crowd had chanted for hours before the speaker arrived. = 在演讲者到达之前，人群已经高喊了几个小时。
They had chanted the same song repeatedly. = 他们反复地唱着同一首歌。

将来时:
The supporters will chant their team's name during the match. = 支持者将在比赛期间高喊他们球队的名字。
We will chant the victory song if we win. = 如果我们赢了，我们将高唱胜利之歌。

虚拟语气:
If they chanted louder, they might be heard. = 如果他们喊得更大声，他们可能会被听到。
I wish they would chant less loudly. = 我希望他们不要喊那么大声。

被动语态:
The chant was heard throughout the stadium. = 整个体育场都能听到吟唱声。
The ancient hymn is still chanted in some ceremonies. = 在一些仪式上仍然吟唱着古老的赞美诗。

疑问句:
What are they chanting? = 他们在吟唱什么？
Do they often chant during their rituals? = 他们在仪式中经常吟唱吗？

否定句:
The crowd did not chant during the speech. = 演讲期间人群没有吟唱。
They don't usually chant at these events. = 他们通常不会在这些活动中吟唱。
