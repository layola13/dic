panentheism:/ˌpænɪnˈθiːɪzəm/| n. |泛神论|A philosophical concept asserting that God is both immanent in and transcendent to the universe.  God encompasses and interpenetrates the world, but is also greater than and beyond it.  Panentheism distinguishes itself from pantheism (God is everything) and theism (God is separate from the universe).|Example:  Some theologians argue that panentheism offers a more nuanced understanding of God's relationship to creation than traditional theism.  一些神学家认为，泛神论比传统神学提供了对上帝与创造物之间关系更细致的理解。|近义词: pantheism (partially), immanentism |反义词: deism, atheism
