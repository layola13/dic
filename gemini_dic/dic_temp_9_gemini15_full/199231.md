sillimanite:/ˈsɪlɪmənˌaɪt/| n. |硅线石|A hard, metamorphic mineral, an aluminosilicate of aluminum, consisting of aluminum silicate (Al2SiO5), used in the manufacture of high-temperature materials such as refractory bricks and porcelain.|例句：Sillimanite is a valuable mineral used in the production of high-temperature ceramics. 硅线石是一种用于生产高温陶瓷的宝贵矿物。|例句：The presence of sillimanite indicates a high-grade metamorphic environment. 硅线石的存在表明这是一个高级变质环境。|近义词：kyanite, andalusite|反义词：无|名词
