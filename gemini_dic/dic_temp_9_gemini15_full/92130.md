hyperscrupulousness:/ˌhaɪpəˈskruːpjʊləsnəs/| n.|过分谨慎；一丝不苟；过于小心|Excessive conscientiousness; an extreme degree of carefulness and precision, often to the point of being impractical or counterproductive.  It suggests a level of meticulousness that goes beyond what is necessary or reasonable.|His hyperscrupulousness in preparing his tax returns led to an unnecessary audit.|近义词：meticulousness, fastidiousness, punctiliousness, overconscientiousness|反义词：carelessness, negligence, sloppiness
