quartan:/ˈkwɔːrtən/| adj.|第四的；每隔一天的；(疟疾)第四日发作的|Relating to or denoting a fever, especially malaria, that recurs every fourth day.  A quartan fever is a type of malaria characterized by fever recurring every 72 hours (every fourth day, counting the first day of the fever as day one).|Example: He suffered from quartan ague, a particularly debilitating form of malaria.|近义词：quarterly (in the sense of recurring every three days)|反义词：daily, continuous
