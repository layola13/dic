extrapunitive:/ˌɛkstrəˈpjuːnɪtɪv/ | adj. | 外罚的；将惩罚转向外界的 |  Relating to or characterized by the tendency to displace aggression or punishment onto others.  It implies a redirection of anger or frustration outwards, rather than inward self-blame or self-punishment. | Example:  He exhibited extrapunitive behavior, blaming his colleagues for the project's failure instead of accepting responsibility. 他表现出外罚的行为，将项目的失败归咎于同事，而不是承担责任。 | 近义词：aggressive, blaming, accusatory | 反义词：intrapunitive, self-blaming, self-punishing |  词性：形容词
