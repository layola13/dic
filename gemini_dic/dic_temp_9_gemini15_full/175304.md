rainstorms:/ˈreɪnstɔːrmz/ | n. |暴风雨；大雨 | A rainstorm is a storm with heavy rain.  It often includes strong winds and sometimes thunder and lightning. |例句：The rainstorm caused flooding in the streets.  暴风雨导致街道洪水泛滥。

The severe rainstorm delayed our flight.  严重的暴风雨延误了我们的航班。|近义词：storm, downpour, tempest |反义词：calm, fair weather
