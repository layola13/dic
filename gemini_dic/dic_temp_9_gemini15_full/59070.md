dwindle: /ˈdwɪndl/ | v. | 逐渐减少；衰落 | To gradually decrease in size, amount, or strength. |例句：His savings dwindled as he paid for his child’s education.  他的积蓄在他支付孩子的教育费用时逐渐减少了。

例句：The number of students in the class dwindled throughout the year.  这班的学生人数在这一年中逐渐减少。


近义词：decrease, diminish, lessen, shrink, fade |反义词：increase, grow, expand, augment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His savings dwindle steadily. = 他的积蓄稳步减少。
The company's profits dwindle each quarter. = 公司的利润每个季度都在减少。

一般过去时:
Our supplies dwindled during the long journey. = 在漫长的旅途中，我们的补给减少了。
The crowd dwindled as the concert ended. = 音乐会结束后，人群散去了。

现在完成时:
The population has dwindled significantly in recent years. = 近年来，人口数量显著减少。
Her energy has dwindled after a long illness. = 她大病一场后，精力大不如前。

过去完成时:
By the end of the war, their resources had dwindled to almost nothing. = 到战争结束时，他们的资源几乎消耗殆尽。
The team's morale had dwindled after several defeats. = 经过几次失败后，球队的士气低落。

将来时:
His influence will dwindle as he gets older. = 他年纪大了，影响力将会下降。
The supply of oil is expected to dwindle in the coming decades. = 预计未来几十年石油供应将减少。

虚拟语气:
If the funding dwindled further, the project would be cancelled. = 如果资金进一步减少，项目将被取消。
I wish my patience wouldn't dwindle so quickly. = 我希望我的耐心不会这么快就耗尽。

被动语态:
The team's strength was dwindled by injuries. = 球队的实力因伤病而削弱。
The forest has been dwindled by deforestation. = 森林因砍伐而减少。

疑问句:
Has the amount of rainfall dwindled this year? = 今年的降雨量减少了吗？
Are our resources dwindling too quickly? = 我们的资源消耗得太快了吗？

否定句:
The problem didn't dwindle; it grew worse. = 问题并没有减少，反而恶化了。
The support for the candidate didn't dwindle; it actually increased. = 对这位候选人的支持并没有减少，反而增加了。


PHRASE->

dwindling numbers = 减少的数量

词源:
The word "dwindle" comes from the Old English word "dwīnian," meaning "to grow weak or feeble."  It is related to the words "wane" and "win."
