rotting:/ˈrɒtɪŋ/|/ˈrɑːtɪŋ/| v. adj.|腐烂；腐朽|The process of decaying or decomposition, especially of organic matter.  It can also describe something declining in quality or condition.|例句：The fruit was rotting on the vine.  水果在藤上腐烂了。

The old building is rotting away. 这座旧建筑正在腐烂。


近义词：decaying, decomposing, putrefying|反义词：fresh, thriving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The apple is rotting.  苹果正在腐烂。
The wood is rotting slowly. 木头正在慢慢腐烂。


一般过去时:
The meat rotted in the heat. 肉在高温下腐烂了。
The vegetables rotted quickly. 蔬菜很快腐烂了。


现在完成时:
The food has rotted. 食物已经腐烂了。
The leaves have rotted on the ground. 叶子已经腐烂在地上了。


过去完成时:
The fruit had rotted before we could eat it.  在我们能吃之前，水果已经腐烂了。
The timber had rotted completely. 木材已经完全腐烂了。


将来时:
The food will rot if you don't refrigerate it. 如果你不冷藏食物，它就会腐烂。
The leaves will rot in the autumn. 叶子将在秋天腐烂。


虚拟语气:
If the food had not been refrigerated, it would have rotted. 如果食物没有冷藏，它就会腐烂。
I wish I hadn't left the bread out; it's rotting now.  真希望我没有把面包放在外面；它现在已经腐烂了。


被动语态:
The wood was rotted by the rain. 木材被雨水腐蚀了。
The fruit is being rotted by the insects. 水果正在被昆虫腐蚀。


疑问句:
Is the meat rotting? 肉在腐烂吗？
Are the vegetables rotting? 蔬菜在腐烂吗？


否定句:
The fruit isn't rotting yet. 水果还没腐烂。
The vegetables didn't rot because they were fresh. 蔬菜没有腐烂，因为它们很新鲜。


PHRASE->
rot away:腐烂掉
rot down:腐烂分解
rot off:烂掉
rotten to the core:烂到根里了
advanced rotting:高级腐烂


词源:
The word "rotting" comes from the Old English word "rotian," meaning "to rot."  This, in turn, has roots in Proto-Germanic and ultimately Proto-Indo-European.
