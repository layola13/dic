overthink:/ˌoʊvərˈθɪŋk/| vt.|过度思考，想太多|To think too much about something, often causing unnecessary anxiety or stress.  It implies dwelling on details or possibilities excessively, leading to negative consequences.|例句：I overthink every decision, which makes it hard to make any progress. 我总是过度思考每一个决定，这让我很难取得任何进展。
例句：She overthinks her relationships, imagining the worst-case scenarios. 她对自己的感情想太多，总是想象最坏的情况。|近义词：ruminate, worry, obsess, dwell on|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I overthink every little thing. = 我对每一件小事都过度思考。
He overthinks his work performance. = 他过度思考自己的工作表现。

一般过去时:
I overthought the situation and made a wrong decision. = 我对这种情况想得太多，做出了错误的决定。
She overthought her presentation and forgot her notes. = 她对她的演示过度思考，忘了她的笔记。

现在完成时:
I have overthought this problem for days. = 我已经考虑这个问题好几天了。
She has overthought the consequences of her actions. = 她已经过度思考了她行为的后果。

过去完成时:
I had overthought the entire project before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经过度思考了整个项目。
They had overthought the risks and missed a great opportunity. = 他们过度考虑了风险，错过了绝佳的机会。

将来时:
I will probably overthink this invitation. = 我可能会过度考虑这个邀请。
He will overthink the exam and do poorly. = 他会过度思考考试，考得不好。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overthought it, I would have taken the job. = 如果我没有想太多，我就已经接受了那份工作。
I wish I hadn't overthought my response. = 我真希望我没有对我的回应想太多。

被动语态:  (Overthink is rarely used passively)
The situation was overanalyzed, leading to unnecessary complications. =  这种情况被过度分析了，导致不必要的复杂化。(This uses a related word, not directly passive "overthink")
The problem was overconsidered, resulting in delayed action. = 这个问题被过度考虑了，导致行动延迟。(This uses a related word, not directly passive "overthink")

疑问句:
Do you overthink things often? = 你经常想太多吗？
Does he overthink his decisions before making them? = 他在做决定之前会过度思考吗？

否定句:
I don't overthink things as much as I used to. = 我不像以前那样想得那么多了。
He doesn't overthink small details. = 他不会对小细节想太多。
