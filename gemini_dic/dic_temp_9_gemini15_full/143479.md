outgambling:/ˌaʊtˈɡæmblɪŋ/| v.|  胜过（某人）赌博；在赌博中胜过 | To win more money than someone else in gambling.  It implies a significant and skillful victory. | 例句：He outgambled his opponent, leaving him penniless. 他在赌博中赢过了对手，让对手身无分文。


He consistently outgambles his rivals at the poker table. 他在扑克桌上总是赢过他的对手。


近义词：outsmart, outwit, outplay (in the context of gambling)


反义词：be outgambled, lose (at gambling)


词性:动词
