mechanicalize:/məˈkænɪkaɪz/ | vt. | 使机械化；使自动化 | To make (a process, industry, etc.) mechanical; to introduce machinery into (a process, industry, etc.).  | The factory has been mechanized to improve efficiency.  这家工厂已经实现了机械化以提高效率。  The assembly line was mechanized to increase production. 装配线实现了机械化以提高产量。 | automate, robotize | manualize, demechanize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mechanize the process to save labor costs. = 他们使这个过程机械化以节省劳动力成本。
We mechanize our farming practices every year. = 我们每年都使我们的农业生产机械化。

一般过去时:
The company mechanized its operations last year. = 这家公司去年将其业务机械化了。
Henry Ford mechanized the automobile production. = 亨利·福特使汽车生产机械化。

现在完成时:
They have mechanized the entire production line. = 他们已经使整条生产线机械化了。
Many factories have mechanized their processes in recent years. = 近年来，许多工厂都使其流程机械化了。

过去完成时:
By 1900, they had mechanized most of their textile production. = 到1900年，他们已经将其大部分纺织品生产机械化了。
Before the war, they had already mechanized some aspects of farming. = 在战争爆发前，他们已经将农业的某些方面机械化了。

将来时:
The government will mechanize more aspects of the postal service. = 政府将使邮政服务的更多方面机械化。
We will mechanize the entire warehouse next year. = 我们明年将把整个仓库机械化。

虚拟语气:
If we mechanized the process, we would save a lot of money. = 如果我们将此过程机械化，我们将节省大量资金。
I wish we had mechanized the system years ago. = 我希望几年前我们就将该系统机械化了。

被动语态:
The entire process was mechanized by the new management. = 整个过程由新管理层机械化。
The factory is being mechanized gradually. = 工厂正在逐步机械化。

疑问句:
Has the factory been mechanized yet? = 工厂已经机械化了吗？
Will the new system be mechanized? = 新系统将会被机械化吗？

否定句:
They did not mechanize the process completely. = 他们没有完全使这个过程机械化。
The company has not yet mechanized its distribution system. = 这家公司尚未将其分销系统机械化。

词源:
mechanical (adj.) + -ize (suffix)  机械的 + 使…化
The suffix "-ize" is used to create verbs from nouns or adjectives, indicating "to make or become something."
