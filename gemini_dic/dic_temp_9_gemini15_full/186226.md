rhinestones:/ˈraɪnstoʊnz/| n. |莱茵石，人造宝石|Imitation gemstones, typically made of glass or plastic with a faceted surface to create sparkle.  They are often used in jewelry and clothing as a decorative element.|The dress was sparkling with rhinestones.  这件裙子闪耀着莱茵石的光芒。|近义词：faux gems, imitation diamonds, crystals|反义词：genuine gems, real diamonds
