payrolls:/ˈpeɪroʊlz/ | n. |工资单；工资总额 |A payroll is a list of employees who are paid by a company, along with the amount of money each employee is to receive. It also refers to the total amount of money paid to employees. |The company's payroll is processed every Friday. 这家公司的工资单每周五处理。  The payroll for this month is unusually high. 这个月的工资总额异常高。 |salaries, wages |  | n.
