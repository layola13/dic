chloridize:/ˈklɔːrədaɪz/| vt.|氯化；用氯处理|To treat (something) with chlorine or a chloride.  The process often involves adding chlorine to a substance to achieve a specific chemical reaction or to disinfect.|例句：The water was chloridized to make it safe to drink.  水经过氯化处理以确保饮用安全。


例句：The metal was chloridized to prevent corrosion. 金属经过氯化处理以防止腐蚀。|近义词：chlorinate|反义词：dechlorinate
