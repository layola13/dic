/əˈreɪdʒəs/ | adj. | 令人震惊的；令人愤怒的；极度令人不快的 | Outrageous means shockingly bad or excessive. It implies a strong feeling of disapproval or anger.  | Example:  The price of that hotel room was outrageous!  那家酒店的房间价格真是离谱！

The judge's decision was outrageous and unfair.  法官的判决令人震惊且不公平。


| 近义词：shocking, scandalous, appalling, offensive, monstrous | 反义词：acceptable, reasonable, tolerable, mild |  形容词
