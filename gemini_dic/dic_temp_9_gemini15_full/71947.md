flies:/flaɪz/| n. v.|苍蝇；飞；飞行|The plural of "fly," referring to insects or the act of flying.  It can also be the third-person singular present tense of the verb "to fly."|例句：Several flies buzzed around the garbage can. 几只苍蝇在垃圾桶周围嗡嗡飞。

The birds flies high above the mountains. 鸟儿飞翔在高山之上。|近义词：insects (n.), soars, takes flight (v.)|反义词：walks, crawls (v.), stays grounded (v.)|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird flies high in the sky. = 鸟儿在高空中飞翔。
He flies to London every week. = 他每周都飞往伦敦。


一般过去时:
The plane flew smoothly. = 飞机平稳地飞行。
The birds flew south for the winter. = 鸟儿飞往南方过冬。


现在完成时:
The plane has flown over the Atlantic. = 飞机已经飞越了大西洋。
I have flown many times. = 我已经飞过很多次了。


过去完成时:
The bird had flown away before I could see it. = 我还没看到它，鸟儿就已经飞走了。
They had flown to Paris before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经飞往巴黎了。


将来时:
I will fly to New York next month. = 我下个月将飞往纽约。
The plane will fly at 30,000 feet. = 飞机将在三万英尺高空飞行。


虚拟语气:
If I had wings, I would fly. = 如果我有翅膀，我会飞。
I wish I could fly like a bird. = 我真希望我能像鸟一样飞。


被动语态:
The plane was flown by a skilled pilot. = 这架飞机由一位技术娴熟的飞行员驾驶。
The kite was flown in the strong wind. = 风筝在强风中飘扬。


疑问句:
Does the bird fly south in winter? = 鸟儿冬天飞往南方吗？
How many times have you flown in a plane? = 你坐飞机飞过多少次了？


否定句:
The plane didn't fly because of the bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，飞机没有飞行。
The bird couldn't fly because its wing was broken. = 鸟儿因为翅膀断了而无法飞行。


PHRASE->

fly the coop = 逃走
