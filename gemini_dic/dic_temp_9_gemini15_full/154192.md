periods:/ˈpɪəriədz/| n.|句点；时期；经期|A period is a punctuation mark (.) used at the end of a sentence.  It can also refer to a length of time or a stage in history.  In women, a period refers to menstruation.|例句：Please put a period at the end of the sentence. 请在句尾加个句号。

例句：The period of the Roman Empire lasted for centuries. 罗马帝国时期持续了几个世纪。

近义词：point, dot, era, epoch, menstruation, cycle

反义词：None directly applicable, depending on context (e.g.,  "beginning" for the "end" sense, "continuity" for the "period" sense of time).

词性: noun
