misstep:/ˈmɪsˌstɛp/| n.|失误，错误的一步|A misstep is a mistake, especially a small one that is easily corrected. It can refer to a wrong step, a wrong decision, or a minor blunder.|例句：His misstep in judgment cost him the election.他的判断失误让他输掉了选举。

例句：That was a minor misstep; we can easily recover.那是一个小错误；我们可以很容易地恢复过来。

近义词：mistake, blunder, error, slip, faux pas|反义词：success, triumph, achievement
