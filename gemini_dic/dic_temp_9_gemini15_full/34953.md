clocklike:/ˈklɒklaɪk/| adj.|像钟一样的；精确的；有规律的|Resembling a clock in its regularity and precision.  It often describes something that operates with predictable and consistent timing.|例句：The factory ran with clocklike precision. 工厂运作精准如同钟表一般。


例句：His schedule was clocklike; he woke up, ate, and worked at the same time every day. 他的作息像钟表一样规律；他每天都在同一时间起床、吃饭和工作。


近义词：precise, regular, punctual, dependable, consistent


反义词：irregular, erratic, unpredictable, haphazard, unreliable

词性:形容词
