custom-mades:/ˈkʌstəmˌmeɪdz/| adj. |定制的，特制的|  Items made to a customer's specific requirements or specifications, rather than mass-produced.  These are often higher quality and more expensive than mass-produced items. |例句：She ordered some custom-made shoes. 她订购了一些定制的鞋子。


例句：He bought a custom-made suit for his wedding. 他为婚礼买了一套定制西装。


近义词： bespoke, tailor-made, made-to-order, personalized


反义词： mass-produced, ready-made, off-the-shelf


词性:形容词

USAGE->
