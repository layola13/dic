non-resistance:/ˌnɒn rɪˈzɪstəns/| n.|非暴力抵抗；顺从；不抵抗主义|The philosophy or practice of not resisting evil or injustice, often by passive means.  It's a principle emphasizing peace and avoiding violence.|例句：Gandhi advocated non-resistance as a means of achieving independence. 甘地提倡非暴力抵抗作为争取独立的手段。|例句：His non-resistance to the bully's taunts only emboldened the bully. 他对恶霸的嘲讽不作抵抗反而助长了恶霸的气焰。|近义词：passivity, submission, acquiescence|反义词：resistance, defiance, opposition|名词
