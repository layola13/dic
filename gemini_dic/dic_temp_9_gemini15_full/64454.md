erinys:/ˈɛrɪnɪz/| n.|厄里倪厄斯（复数）；复仇女神|One of the three avenging goddesses in Greek mythology.  They pursued and punished those who committed crimes against natural law, especially murder.  They were often depicted as winged, terrifying figures with snakes in their hair.|例句：The Erinyes relentlessly pursued Orestes for the matricide he committed.  厄里倪厄斯们无情地追捕犯下弑母罪的俄瑞斯忒斯。|近义词：Fury, avenging goddess|反义词：none|名词
