quasi-spatial: /ˈkwɑːzaɪˈspeɪʃəl/ | adj. | 准空间的，似空间的 |  Relating to or resembling space or spatial properties, but not fully or strictly so;  partially spatial or having characteristics suggestive of space. It often refers to abstract or conceptual spaces, not physical ones.  The term implies an approximation or analogy to spatial concepts. | Example: The model uses a quasi-spatial representation of the data, mapping abstract relationships onto a two-dimensional plane.  该模型使用数据的准空间表示，将抽象关系映射到二维平面。| 近义词:  semi-spatial, virtual spatial, representational | 反义词:  truly spatial, purely physical
