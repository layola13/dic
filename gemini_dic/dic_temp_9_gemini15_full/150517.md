pantomimist: /ˈpæn.tə.maɪ.mɪst/ | n. |哑剧演员| A pantomimist is a performer who uses mime, gestures, and facial expressions to tell a story or convey a message without speaking. Pantomimists often use exaggerated movements and expressions to create a humorous or dramatic effect.  They rely heavily on physicality and nonverbal communication to engage their audience. | The pantomimist captivated the audience with his expressive movements and hilarious facial expressions.  哑剧演员以其富有表现力的动作和滑稽的表情吸引了观众。| mime, mimer |  speaker, vocalist | 名词
