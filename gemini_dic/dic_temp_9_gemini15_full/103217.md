jetsoms:/ˈdʒɛtsəm/| n.|漂流物；（尤指）海难后漂浮在海面的货物|Goods or wreckage that have been thrown overboard from a ship and found floating on the sea.  The term often implies that the discarding was intentional, to lighten the load of a distressed vessel.|例句：The divers recovered much jetsam from the wrecked ship.  潜水员从失事船只中打捞出许多漂流物。
The coastline was littered with jetsam after the storm. 暴风雨过后，海岸线上散落着许多漂流物。|近义词：flotsam, wreckage, debris|反义词：cargo (intact), treasure (recovered)|名词
