promissory:/ˈprɑːmɪsɔːri/ | /ˈprɒmɪsəri/ | adj. | 承诺的；约定的；有担保的 | Relating to or containing a promise, especially one to pay a debt.  A promissory note is a written promise to pay a specific sum of money at a certain time. | The promissory note was due next month.  这张期票下个月到期。

The bank requires a promissory note before granting a loan. 银行在发放贷款之前需要一张期票。


近义词：obligatory, binding, guaranteed


反义词：uncertain, unreliable, unsecured


词性：形容词
