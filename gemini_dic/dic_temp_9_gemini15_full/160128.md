pompadour: /ˌpɒmpəˈdɔːr/ /ˌpɑːmpəˈdɔːr/ | n. |蓬巴杜式发型；隆起的发型 | A pompadour is a hairstyle where the hair is styled upwards and away from the forehead, often creating a high, voluminous look.  The style is named after Madame de Pompadour, a mistress of King Louis XV of France, who was known for her elaborate hairstyles. |Example: He slicked back his hair into a perfect pompadour. 他把头发梳成一个完美的蓬巴杜式发型。  She wore her hair in a high pompadour for the gala. 她为了参加盛典，把头发梳成了高高的蓬巴杜式发型。|近义词：high-top, quiff |反义词：close-cropped, slicked-down |名词
