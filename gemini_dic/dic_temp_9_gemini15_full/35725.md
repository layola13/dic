coccosphere:/ˈkɒkəʊsfɪər/| n. |可可球层，可可球，碳酸盐球|The coccosphere is a microscopic, spherical structure formed by coccolithophores, a type of phytoplankton. It's composed of calcium carbonate plates called coccoliths. These structures play a significant role in the global carbon cycle and marine ecosystems.  |Example:  The ocean's coccosphere contributes significantly to the global carbon sink.|近义词：coccolithophore aggregate (不完全同义，coccosphere指结构，coccolithophore aggregate 指聚集体)|反义词：无
