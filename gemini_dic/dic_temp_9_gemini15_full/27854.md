canonized:/ˈkænənaɪzd/ | vt. | 封圣；正式承认；尊为经典 | To declare (a person) a saint; to officially recognize (something) as authoritative or canonical.  | The Pope canonized the long-dead missionary. 教皇封这位已故多年的传教士为圣徒。 |  declared a saint, sanctified, beatified |  uncanonized


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The church canonizes saints after a rigorous process. = 教会经过严格的程序后才封圣。
They canonize certain texts as authoritative interpretations. = 他们将某些文本尊为权威性的解释。


一般过去时:
The council canonized her as a martyr. = 理事会将她封为烈士。
