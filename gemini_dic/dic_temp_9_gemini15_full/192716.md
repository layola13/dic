/ɛsdiːkeɪz/ | n. | 软件开发工具包 | Software development kits (SDKs) are collections of software development tools that are used to create applications for a specific platform or operating system.  They typically include libraries, compilers, debuggers, and documentation.  | Example:  Many companies provide SDKs to help developers integrate their services into apps.  许多公司提供SDK来帮助开发者将他们的服务集成到应用程序中。 |近义词:development tools, software libraries |反义词:  N/A
