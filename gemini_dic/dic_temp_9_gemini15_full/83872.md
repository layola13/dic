It's often used to describe the size of a small plot of land, such as a residential lot or a small farm.|例句：Their new house sits on a half-acre lot. 他们的新房子坐落在半英亩的地块上。  The farmer planted vegetables on his half-acre plot.  这位农民在他的半英亩土地上种植蔬菜。|近义词：0.5 acre, 2023.4 square meters|反义词：acre, multiple acres|名词
