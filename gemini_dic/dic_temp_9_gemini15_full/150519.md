pantophagist:/ˈpæn.tə.fə.dʒɪst/| n. |杂食动物|An animal that eats both plants and animals.  A pantophagist has a varied diet, consuming both plant and animal matter.  This dietary flexibility allows them to survive in a wider range of environments and adapt to changing food availability. |Example:  Bears are excellent examples of pantophagists, consuming berries, fish, and other animals depending on the season and availability.  Many humans are also considered pantophagists. |近义词:omnivore|反义词:herbivore, carnivore|名词
