midwifed: /ˈmɪdwɪft/ | vt. | 接生；促成；帮助…诞生 | To assist (the birth of something), especially by guiding its development.  | 例句：The project was midwifed by a team of experienced investors.  这个项目是由一个经验丰富的投资者团队促成的。

She midwifed the birth of her grandchild. 她接生了她孙子的出生。


近义词：assisted, facilitated, helped, delivered (in the context of birth) | 反义词：hindered, obstructed, prevented | 词性:动词
