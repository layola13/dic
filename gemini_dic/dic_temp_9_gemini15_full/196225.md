ser:/sɜːr/| /sɝː/ | n. |先生，阁下|A title of respect used to address a man.  It is often used in a formal context, or when speaking to someone older or in a position of authority. |例句：Yes, sir. 是的，先生。|例句：May I help you, sir? 先生，需要帮忙吗？|近义词：mister, gentleman, sir|反义词：madam, miss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a kind sir. = 他是一位善良的先生。
The sir always helps people. =这位先生总是帮助别人。


一般过去时:
The sir helped me yesterday. =这位先生昨天帮助了我。
He was a respected sir in the community. = 他是社区里一位受人尊敬的先生。


现在完成时:
He has been a helpful sir to many. = 他对许多人来说一直是一位乐于助人的先生。
The sir has served his country well. =这位先生很好地为国家服务。


过去完成时:
The sir had already left when I arrived. = 我到达时，先生已经离开了。

将来时:

否定句:
