repaneled:/ˌriːˈpænld/| v.|重新镶板；重新装上镶板 |To replace or reinstall paneling.  The process involves removing old panels and installing new ones, often to improve the appearance or functionality of a surface. |例句：The old kitchen was repaneled with modern, sleek materials. 老厨房重新安装了现代时尚的镶板。

The house was repaneled after the fire damaged the original wood.  火灾损坏原木后，房子重新安装了镶板。|近义词：repanel, refit, renovate |反义词：remove paneling, strip
