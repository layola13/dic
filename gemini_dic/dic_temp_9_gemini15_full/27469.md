campaigns:/kæmˈpeɪnz/| n.|战役；竞选活动；宣传活动|A series of actions intended to achieve a particular aim.  Campaigns can be military, political, or social in nature, involving organized efforts to achieve a specific goal.|例句：The military launched a major campaign to recapture the lost territory.  军队发起了一场大规模的战役以收复失地。

The presidential campaign was intense and highly publicized.  总统竞选活动非常激烈且备受瞩目。|近义词：drives, crusades, movements|反义词：ceasefires, truces


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company campaigns actively for environmental protection. = 这家公司积极开展环保宣传活动。
She campaigns for better working conditions. = 她为改善工作条件而奔走呼号。

一般过去时:
They campaigned tirelessly for social justice. = 他们为社会正义不知疲倦地奔走呼号。
The army campaigned across the desert. = 军队在沙漠中作战。

现在完成时:
We have campaigned against poverty for many years. = 我们多年来一直在为消除贫困而奔走呼号。
The organization has campaigned successfully for stricter gun control laws. = 该组织成功地为更严格的枪支管制法而奔走呼号。

过去完成时:
They had campaigned for months before the election. = 他们在选举前已经奔走呼号了几个月。
The general had campaigned in several countries before the war. = 将军在战争前曾在几个国家作战。

将来时:
We will campaign for a new school in our neighborhood. = 我们将在我们附近地区为新建学校而奔走呼号。
The opposition party will campaign vigorously in the upcoming elections. = 反对党将在即将到来的选举中积极竞选。

虚拟语气:
If we campaigned harder, we might win. = 如果我们更努力地竞选，我们可能会赢。
I wish they had campaigned more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地开展宣传活动。

被动语态:
The issue was campaigned for by several organizations. = 这个问题得到了几个组织的宣传。
The new policy is being campaigned against by many citizens. = 许多公民正在反对这项新政策。

疑问句:
What are they campaigning for? = 他们在为争取什么而奔走呼号？
How long have they been campaigning? = 他们已经奔走呼号多久了？

否定句:
They did not campaign for that candidate. = 他们没有为那个候选人奔走呼号。
The company does not campaign against animal testing. = 该公司不反对动物实验。
