phrenetic: /frɪˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 狂热的；极度兴奋的；精神错乱的 | Extremely agitated; frantic; wildly excited.  Characterized by or exhibiting frenzy. |  例句：The phrenetic energy of the concert was infectious.  这场音乐会的狂热能量极具感染力。

例句：His phrenetic pace of work led to burnout. 他疯狂的工作节奏导致了倦怠。


近义词：frantic, frenzied, manic, feverish, agitated


反义词：calm, tranquil, peaceful, composed, serene


词性：形容词


词源：源自希腊语“phren-” (意为“心灵”、“精神”) 和“-ētikos” (意为“与……有关的”)，暗示着精神上的狂热状态。  The word ultimately derives from the Greek word "phrēn," meaning "mind" or "diaphragm," suggesting a connection to the emotional and mental state.
