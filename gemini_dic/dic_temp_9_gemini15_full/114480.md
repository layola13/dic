malachites:/ˈmæləkaɪts/| n. |孔雀石|A green copper carbonate hydroxide mineral, often used as an ornamental stone.  It's valued for its vibrant green color and banded patterns. |例句：The necklace was made of polished malachites, showcasing its beautiful green hues. 这条项链是用抛光的孔雀石制成的，展现出它美丽的绿色色调。

The ancient Egyptians used malachites in jewelry and cosmetics. 古埃及人用孔雀石制作珠宝和化妆品。
