plainclothes: /ˈpleɪnˌkloʊðz/ | n. |便衣，便服 |Plainclothes refers to ordinary clothing worn by police officers or other law enforcement personnel when they are not in uniform.  It allows them to blend in with the public and conduct undercover operations. |例句：The plainclothes officers were observing the suspect from across the street. 便衣警察正在街对面观察嫌疑人。

例句：She recognized him immediately, even though he was in plainclothes. 她立刻认出了他，即使他穿着便衣。


近义词：civilian clothes, everyday clothes, street clothes

反义词：uniform


词性：名词
