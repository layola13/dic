overwhelmment:/ˌoʊvərˈwelmɪnt/ | n. | 难以承受的压力；压倒；泛滥 | The feeling of being overwhelmed by a task or situation;  a state of being overpowered or inundated.  It often implies a feeling of being burdened or unable to cope. |例句：The sheer volume of work led to a feeling of overwhelmment.  大量的作业导致了难以承受的压力。

The constant demands of her job caused overwhelmment and burnout.  她工作的持续需求导致了难以承受的压力和倦怠。|近义词：overburden, inundation, deluge, stress, pressure |反义词：calm, peace, tranquility, ease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The workload overwhelms me. = 工作量让我难以承受。
The amount of information overwhelms him. = 信息量让他难以承受。

一般过去时:
The news overwhelmed her. = 这个消息让她难以承受。
The crowd overwhelmed the police. = 人群冲垮了警察。


现在完成时:
I have been overwhelmed by the support I've received. = 我收到的支持让我难以承受。
The system has been overwhelmed by the traffic. = 系统已经被流量压垮了。


过去完成时:
She had been overwhelmed by grief before she even received the news. = 在她收到消息之前，她就已经被悲伤压垮了。
The city had been overwhelmed by the flood before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，城市已经被洪水淹没了。


将来时:
I think I will be overwhelmed by the beauty of the landscape. = 我想我会被这美丽的风景震撼到。
The server will be overwhelmed by the number of requests. = 服务器将会被大量的请求压垮。


虚拟语气:
If I were to take on that project, I would be overwhelmed. = 如果我承担那个项目，我会不堪重负。
I wish I hadn't been overwhelmed by the pressure. = 我真希望我没有被压力压垮。


被动语态:
He was overwhelmed by the situation. = 他被当时的局势压垮了。
The small town was overwhelmed by the tourists. = 小镇被游客挤满了。


疑问句:
Are you overwhelmed by your responsibilities? = 你被你的责任压垮了吗？
Does this amount of work overwhelm you? = 这么多的工作让你难以承受吗？


否定句:
He wasn't overwhelmed by the criticism. = 他并没有被批评压垮。
She wasn't overwhelmed by the success. = 她并没有被成功冲昏头脑。
