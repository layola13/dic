remigrates:/ˈriːmaɪɡreɪts/ | v. |重新移民；再次移民 | To migrate again; to return to a former country of residence after having migrated elsewhere. | Example:  Many families remigrated to their home countries after the war ended. 许多家庭在战争结束后重新移民回他们的祖国。  |近义词：relocate, return, repatriate | 反义词：emigrate, immigrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remigrates to his homeland every summer. = 他每年夏天都重新移民回他的祖国。
She remigrates to the USA after living in Canada for five years. = 她住在加拿大五年后重新移民到美国。


一般过去时:
They remigrated to Europe after spending several years in Asia. = 他们在亚洲待了几年后重新移民到欧洲。
My grandparents remigrated to Italy after the Second World War. = 我的祖父母在二战后重新移民到意大利。


现在完成时:
She has remigrated to the Philippines three times. = 她已经三次重新移民到菲律宾。
He has remigrated to his native village after a long time abroad. = 他在国外待了很长时间后重新移民回了他的家乡。


过去完成时:
They had remigrated to the UK before the political situation worsened. = 政治局势恶化之前，他们已经重新移民到了英国。
The family had remigrated to Argentina before the revolution. = 在革命爆发之前，这个家庭已经重新移民到了阿根廷。


将来时:
We will remigrate to Australia next year. = 我们明年将重新移民到澳大利亚。
They will remigrate to France once they retire. = 他们退休后将重新移民到法国。


虚拟语气:
If they had the money, they would remigrate to their old country. = 如果他们有钱的话，他们就会重新移民回他们的老家。
I wish I could remigrate to my childhood home. = 我真希望我能重新移民回我的童年故乡。


被动语态:
Many refugees were remigrated to their original homes after the conflict ended. = 冲突结束后，许多难民被重新安置到他们的家乡。
The family was remigrated to their country of origin by the government. = 这个家庭被政府遣送回他们的原籍国。


疑问句:
Will they remigrate to their home country? = 他们会重新移民回他们的祖国吗？
Have they ever considered remigrating? = 他们是否曾经考虑过重新移民？


否定句:
They did not remigrate to the United States. = 他们没有重新移民到美国。
She will not remigrate to Brazil this year. = 她今年不会重新移民到巴西。


PHRASE->
remigrate back = 重新移民回
remigrate to = 重新移民到
remigrate after = 之后重新移民
remigrate from = 从...重新移民
remigrate because of = 因为...而重新移民
