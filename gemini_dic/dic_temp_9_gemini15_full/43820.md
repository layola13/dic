creolising:/ˈkriːəlaɪzɪŋ/| v.|克里奥尔化；混合；融合|The process by which a pidgin language evolves into a creole language, characterized by the development of a more complex grammatical structure and a wider vocabulary.  It also refers to the broader cultural mixing and adaptation that occurs when different groups come into contact.|例句：The creolising of the local dialect resulted in a unique language with influences from several sources. 当地方言的克里奥尔化导致了一种具有多种来源影响的独特语言。|例句：The island's culture shows clear signs of creolising, blending African, European, and Asian traditions.  这个岛屿的文化显示出明显的克里奥尔化迹象，融合了非洲、欧洲和亚洲的传统。|近义词：hybridization, mixing, blending, fusion|反义词：purification, isolation, segregation|动词
