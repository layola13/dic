human-resourceses:/ˈhjuːmən rɪˈsɔːrsɪz/|/ˈhjuːmən rɪˈsɔːrsɪziːz/| n. |人力资源（复数形式）|Human resourceses is the plural form of human resources, referring to the people who make up the workforce of an organization.  It's often used informally or in situations where a more emphatic plural is needed, although "human resources" is generally preferred in formal contexts.|The company's human resourceses are its most valuable asset.|近义词：personnel, workforce, staff |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
This word is unusual.  The standard term is "human resources," which is treated as a singular uncountable noun, not a plural. Therefore, standard grammatical tenses and forms do not apply to "human resourceses" in the same way as for regular nouns.  The examples below demonstrate usage of the singular form, "human resources".
