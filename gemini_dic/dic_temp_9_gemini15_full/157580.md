pitching:/ˈpɪtʃɪŋ/| v.|投球；推销；倾斜；搭建|The act of throwing a ball, especially in baseball;  the act of trying to sell something; the act of causing something to slope or lean; the act of erecting something. |例句：The pitcher's fastball was impressive.  那位投手快速球很厉害。|例句：He spent the afternoon pitching his new business idea. 他下午都在推销他的新商业理念。|近义词：throwing, selling, angling, erecting|反义词：catching, buying, leveling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pitches the ball really fast. = 他投球很快。
She pitches her products effectively. = 她有效地推销她的产品。

一般过去时:
He pitched the ball to the catcher. = 他把球投给了接球手。
They pitched their tent in the field. = 他们在田野里搭起了帐篷。

现在完成时:
He has pitched many winning games this season. = 这个赛季他投了很多胜场球。
The company has pitched the new product to several clients. = 公司已经向几家客户推销了新产品。

过去完成时:
He had pitched a great game before his injury. = 他受伤前投了一场精彩的比赛。
They had pitched the idea to the board before the meeting. = 他们在会议前向董事会提出了这个想法。

将来时:
He will pitch the next inning. = 他将投下个局。
She will pitch her ideas to the investors next week. = 她下周将向投资者推销她的想法。

虚拟语气:
If he pitched better, they would win. = 如果他投球更好，他们就会赢。
If she had pitched the idea earlier, it might have been approved. = 如果她早点提出这个想法，它可能就获批了。

被动语态:
The ball was pitched perfectly. = 球投得完美无缺。
The idea was pitched to the wrong audience. = 这个想法向错误的受众推销了。

疑问句:
Who pitched the winning run? = 谁投出了致胜分？
When will they pitch the new product? = 他们什么时候会推销新产品？

否定句:
He didn't pitch well today. = 他今天投球表现不佳。
They didn't pitch the idea convincingly. = 他们没有令人信服地提出这个想法。


PHRASE->
pitch a tent = 搭帐篷
pitch in = 帮忙；贡献
pitch an idea = 提出想法
pitch black = 漆黑一片
pitch a fit = 勃然大怒
