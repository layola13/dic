puddler:/ˈpʌdlər/| n. |搅泥工；(尤指炼铁厂的) 搅拌工；(鸟) 水洼鸟|A puddler is a worker who stirs or mixes mud, especially in the process of iron production in a puddling furnace.  The term also refers to a type of bird that frequents puddles.|例句：He worked as a puddler in the steel mill for twenty years. 他在钢铁厂做了二十年的搅泥工。|例句：The puddler bird skillfully foraged for insects in the shallow puddle. 水洼鸟熟练地在浅水洼里觅食昆虫。|近义词：ironworker,  (for bird) wader|反义词：None specifically applicable.
