pierce:/pɪərs/|/v.|刺穿；穿透；刺痛；触动|To make a hole through something with a sharp pointed object. To affect someone deeply, emotionally.|例句：He pierced his ear with a needle. 他用针刺穿了他的耳朵。

例句：Her words pierced his heart.  她的言语刺痛了他的心。

近义词：puncture, penetrate, perforate

反义词：seal, close, mend
