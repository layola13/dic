contradistinction:/ˌkɒntrədɪˈstɪŋkʃən/| n. |对比，对照|The act of distinguishing by contrasting; a difference or contrast.  It emphasizes the differences between two things to highlight their distinct characteristics.  |例句：In contradistinction to his brother, he was quiet and reserved. 与他兄弟形成对比的是，他安静而内敛。|例句：The essay explores the contradistinction between theory and practice. 这篇文章探讨了理论与实践之间的区别。|近义词：contrast, difference, opposition |反义词：similarity, resemblance, agreement |名词
