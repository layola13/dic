sanitising:/ˈsænɪtaɪzɪŋ/| v.|消毒，净化|The act of making something clean and free from germs or bacteria.  It involves removing or killing harmful microorganisms to prevent infection or contamination.|例句：We need to sanitise the kitchen surfaces before preparing food. 我们需要在准备食物之前对厨房表面进行消毒。|例句：The hospital uses a special solution to sanitise its equipment. 医院使用特殊的溶液来消毒其设备。|近义词：disinfecting, sterilising, cleaning|反义词：contaminating, infecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We sanitise the equipment regularly. = 我们定期对设备进行消毒。
The staff sanitise their hands before entering the operating room. = 工作人员在进入手术室前洗手消毒。

一般过去时:
