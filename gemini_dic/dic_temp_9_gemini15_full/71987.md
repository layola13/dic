flinching:/ˈflɪntʃɪŋ/| v.|畏缩；退缩；退避|To make a sudden, involuntary movement as a reaction to something unpleasant or frightening.|He flinched at the loud noise. 他被巨响吓了一跳。|近义词：shrink, recoil, wince|反义词：brave, bold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flinches at the sight of blood. = 他看到血就畏缩。
She flinches when someone raises their voice. = 当有人提高嗓门时，她会畏缩。

一般过去时:
He flinched when the doctor gave him the injection. = 当医生给他注射时，他畏缩了一下。
She flinched away from the hot stove. = 她从热的炉子旁畏缩地躲开了。

现在完成时:
He has flinched from difficult conversations all his life. = 他一生都回避艰难的谈话。
She has never flinched in the face of adversity. = 面对逆境，她从未退缩过。

过去完成时:
He had flinched away from the blow before it landed. =  在他挨打之前，他已经畏缩开了。
She had flinched so many times before that she was desensitized. = 她以前已经畏缩过很多次了，以至于她已经麻木了。


将来时:
He will flinch when he hears the bad news. = 当他听到坏消息时，他会畏缩。
She will probably flinch if you touch her injured arm. = 如果你碰她的受伤的胳膊，她可能会畏缩。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't flinch from the challenge. = 如果我是你，我不会回避这个挑战。
I wish I hadn't flinched when he asked me that question. = 我真希望在他问我那个问题时我没有畏缩。


被动语态:
(被动语态在此处使用较少，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Did he flinch when you told him the truth? = 当你告诉他真相时，他畏缩了吗？
Does she flinch easily? = 她容易畏缩吗？


否定句:
He didn't flinch when he saw the snake. = 他看到蛇时并没有畏缩。
She didn't flinch at the sight of the accident. = 她看到事故时并没有畏缩。


PHRASE->
flinch away = 畏缩躲开
flinch from = 逃避；回避
flinch at = 对...畏缩
without flinching = 毫不畏缩地
flinch and dodge = 躲闪和躲避
