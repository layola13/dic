nondebilitative: /ˌnɒnˌdɪˈbɪlɪteɪtɪv/ | adj. |非衰弱性的，不致衰弱的 | Not causing debilitation or weakness; not debilitating.  This word describes something that does not weaken or impair someone's health or ability to function.  |例句：The treatment was nondebilitative, allowing her to maintain her daily activities.  这种治疗不会削弱她的身体，让她能够保持日常活动。


例句：The doctor prescribed a nondebilitative course of medication. 医生开了不会使人衰弱的药物疗程。


近义词：harmless, innocuous, mild, strengthening


反义词：debilitating, weakening, enervating, debilitating
