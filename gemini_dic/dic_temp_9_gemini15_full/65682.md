eveners:/ˈiːvənərz/| n.|平衡器，均力器|A device or mechanism used to equalize or distribute weight or force evenly.  It often refers to a type of hitch or coupling used in agriculture or transportation to distribute the load between two or more animals or vehicles.|例句：The farmer used eveners to distribute the load between his two oxen. 农民用平衡器来分配他两头牛的负重。|例句：The eveners on the wagon ensured a smooth ride.  马车上的平衡器确保了平稳的行驶。|近义词：equalizer, balancer, distributor|反义词：unbalancer, disbalancer
