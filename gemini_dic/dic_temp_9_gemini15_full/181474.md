rejoining:/ˌriːˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/| v. |重新加入，重新结合|The act of joining again; returning to a group or activity.  It can also refer to the act of two or more things coming together again.|例句：After a long break, she's rejoining the team.  长时间休息后，她重新加入了团队。

例句：The separated pieces of the vase were carefully rejoined. 花瓶分离的碎片被小心地重新粘合在一起。


近义词：reuniting, rejoining, recombining, resuming


反义词：leaving, separating, parting


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rejoins the choir every Sunday. 他每个星期天都重新加入合唱团。
They rejoin the conversation after a brief pause. 他们短暂停顿后重新加入谈话。


一般过去时:
She rejoined her friends after the concert. 音乐会结束后，她重新加入了她的朋友们。
He rejoined the army after a period of leave. 休假一段时间后，他又重新参军了。


现在完成时:
I have rejoined the gym after a long absence. 长期缺席后，我又重新加入了健身房。
They have rejoined their families after the evacuation. 疏散后，他们已经与家人团聚了。


过去完成时:
Before the final act, the lead singer had rejoined the band. 在最后一幕之前，主唱已经重新加入乐队。
After he had rejoined the project, progress accelerated. 他重新加入项目后，进展加快了。


将来时:
She will rejoin the company next month. 她下个月将重新加入公司。
He will rejoin his unit after completing his training. 他完成训练后将重新加入他的部队。


虚拟语气:
If he rejoined the team, we would win the championship. 如果他重新加入团队，我们将赢得冠军。
I wish he had rejoined the project sooner. 我希望他早点重新加入这个项目。


被动语态:
The broken pieces were rejoined with glue. 破碎的碎片是用胶水粘合起来的。
The separated families were rejoined after the war. 战争结束后，分散的家庭团聚了。


疑问句:
Will he rejoin the project? 他会重新加入项目吗？
Has she rejoined the club yet? 她已经重新加入俱乐部了吗？


否定句:
He did not rejoin the discussion. 他没有重新加入讨论。
She will not rejoin the company. 她不会重新加入公司。
