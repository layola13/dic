salaryman:/ˈsæləriˌmæn/ | n. | 工薪族，上班族 | A salaried worker, especially a white-collar worker in a corporation.  This term often implies a life dedicated to working for a company, often with long hours and a relatively inflexible schedule.  The term often carries connotations of routine, conformity, and a certain lack of individual expression. | Example: He's a typical salaryman, commuting to work every day by train. 他是一个典型的上班族，每天乘火车上下班。|近义词：office worker, company employee, white-collar worker | 反义词：entrepreneur, freelancer, self-employed
