frisky:/ˈfrɪski/| adj.|活泼的；嬉戏的；顽皮的|Playful and full of energy; mischievous.|例句：The frisky kittens chased each other around the room.  顽皮的小猫在房间里追逐嬉戏。|例句：The frisky puppy was difficult to keep still.  这条活泼的小狗很难安静下来。|近义词：playful, lively, spirited, mischievous, frolicsome|反义词：lethargic, sluggish, subdued, quiet, docile

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The kittens are frisky this morning. = 小猫今天早上很活泼。
He is always frisky when he plays with his friends. =他和朋友玩耍时总是很活泼。

一般过去时:
The dog was frisky after its walk. = 这只狗散步后很活泼。
The children were frisky during the party. =孩子们在派对上很活泼。

现在完成时:
The horses have been frisky all day. =这些马整天都很活泼。
She has been feeling frisky since she got some good news. =自从她得到好消息后，她就一直感觉很活泼。

过去完成时:
The lambs had been frisky all afternoon before they finally settled down. =羊羔们整个下午都很活泼，最后才平静下来。
They had been frisky as children and remained so as adults. =他们小时候很活泼，长大后也依然如此。

将来时:
The puppies will be frisky when they are older. =这些小狗长大后会很活泼。
I think the colts will be very frisky in the spring. =我认为这些小马驹春天会非常活泼。

虚拟语气:
If the horse were frisky, it would be hard to ride. =如果马很活泼，骑起来会很困难。
I wish my dog was more frisky. =我希望我的狗更活泼一些。

被动语态: (Frisky is rarely used in passive voice)
The children were made frisky by the exciting game. =孩子们被激动人心的游戏弄得很活泼。(较为生硬的表达)
The atmosphere was made frisky by the music. =音乐使气氛变得活泼。(较为自然)


疑问句:
Is the puppy frisky today? =小狗今天活泼吗？
Are the children feeling frisky? =孩子们感觉活泼吗？


否定句:
The cat is not frisky today; she seems tired. =猫今天不活泼，她看起来很累。
The old horse was not frisky anymore. =老马不再活泼了。


PHRASE->

feeling frisky = 感觉活泼
frisky demeanor = 活泼的态度
