lightens:/ˈlaɪtənz/| v.|使变轻；使变亮；使轻松；(脸色)变得红润|To make or become lighter in weight, color, or intensity; to make or become less serious or burdensome; to brighten or illuminate.|例句：The rain lightened the mood.  雨水冲淡了沉闷的气氛。|例句：The sun lightened the room. 太阳照亮了房间。|近义词：brighten, alleviate, ease|反义词：darken, aggravate, burden


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun lightens the sky every morning. = 太阳每天早晨都照亮天空。
He lightens the heavy boxes with a crane. 他用起重机减轻沉重箱子的重量。


一般过去时:
The news lightened her heart. = 这个消息使她心情轻松。
The storm lightened after several hours.  暴风雨持续数小时后减弱了。


现在完成时:
The load has lightened considerably since we started. = 自从我们开始以来，负荷已经大大减轻了。
His face has lightened with happiness. 他脸上露出了幸福的表情。


过去完成时:
The situation had lightened before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，情况有所好转。
The clouds had lightened, revealing a clear sky. 云层散开了，露出了晴朗的天空。


将来时:
The burden will lighten once the project is completed. = 项目完成后，负担就会减轻。
The sky will lighten as the sun rises.  随着太阳升起，天空将会变亮。


虚拟语气:
If the rain lightened, we could go out. = 如果雨小了，我们就能出去了。
I wish the burden would lighten soon. 我希望负担能很快减轻。


被动语态:
The mood was lightened by the music. = 音乐使气氛轻松起来。
The room was lightened by the newly installed lamps.  新安装的灯照亮了房间。


疑问句:
Does the workload lighten on weekends? = 周末工作量会减少吗？
Will the sun lighten the dark room? 太阳会照亮昏暗的房间吗？


否定句:
The rain didn't lighten the mood at all. = 雨根本没有冲淡沉闷的气氛。
The heavy boxes didn't lighten the burden. 沉重的箱子并没有减轻负担。
