grannies:/ˈɡræniz/| n.|奶奶们，老奶奶们|A granny is a grandmother, or a term of endearment for an elderly woman.  The word is often used in a plural context to refer to multiple grandmothers or elderly women.|例句：The grannies were knitting in the park.  奶奶们在公园里织毛衣。|例句：My grannies always bake the best cookies. 我的奶奶们总是烤最好吃的饼干。|近义词：grandmothers, old ladies, elderly women|反义词：young girls, children
