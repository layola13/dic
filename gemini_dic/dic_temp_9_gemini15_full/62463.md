endoscope:/ˈɛndəskoʊp/ | n. | 内窥镜 | A medical instrument consisting of a long, thin, flexible tube with a light and a camera on the end, used to examine the interior of a body cavity or organ.  | Example: The doctor used an endoscope to examine the patient's stomach. 医生用内窥镜检查了病人的胃。 |近义词: endoscope,  fiberscope |反义词: None.
