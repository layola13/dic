cheerlessly:/ˈtʃɪərləsli/| adv.| 闷闷不乐地，无精打采地 | Without cheer or enthusiasm; dejectedly.  |例句：He ate his breakfast cheerlessly. 他无精打采地吃了早餐。

例句：She answered cheerlessly, her voice lacking any warmth. 她闷闷不乐地回答，声音毫无温暖。


近义词：dejectedly, despondently, glumly, sadly, dismally

反义词：cheerfully, happily, joyfully, merrily, brightly
