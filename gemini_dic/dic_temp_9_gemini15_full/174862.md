racists:/ˈreɪsɪsts/| n. |种族主义者|A person who is prejudiced against or antagonistic toward a person or people on the basis of their membership in a particular racial or ethnic group, typically one that is a minority or marginalized.|例句：He is a racist who believes that his race is superior to others. 他是一个种族主义者，认为他的种族优于其他种族。|近义词：bigot, xenophobe, chauvinist |反义词：egalitarian, integrationist
