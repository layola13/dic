proofed:/pruːft/| v. |校对过的；校正过的 |To have checked and corrected a text or other piece of work for errors.  |例句：The manuscript has been proofed and is ready to be printed.  手稿已经校对完毕，可以印刷了。|例句：I've proofed the document three times, but I'm still not completely satisfied. 我已经校对过这份文件三次了，但我仍然不太满意。|近义词：corrected, edited, revised |反义词：uncorrected, unedited


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He proofreads the documents every day. 他每天都校对文件。
She proofreads the articles before submission. 她在提交文章前会校对。

一般过去时:
I proofread the report last night. 我昨晚校对报告了。
They proofread the book carefully. 他们仔细地校对那本书。

现在完成时:
I have proofread the entire manuscript. 我已经校对了整份手稿。
She has proofread the document twice. 她已经校对了两次文件。

过去完成时:
They had proofread the document before submitting it. 他们在提交文件之前已经校对过了。
He had proofread the chapter several times. 他已经校对过好几遍那章了。

将来时:
I will proofread the essay tomorrow. 我明天会校对这篇论文。
She will proofread the document before sending it. 她会在发送文件之前校对。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would proofread it more carefully. 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地校对它。
I wish I had proofread the letter before sending it. 我真希望在寄出信之前能校对一下。

被动语态:
The document was proofread by the editor. 这份文件由编辑校对。
The manuscript is being proofread at the moment. 手稿正在校对中。

疑问句:
Have you proofread the contract yet? 你已经校对合同了吗？
Will you proofread my thesis? 你会校对我的论文吗？

否定句:
I didn't proofread the document carefully enough. 我没有足够仔细地校对文件。
She hasn't proofread her essay yet. 她还没有校对她的论文。
