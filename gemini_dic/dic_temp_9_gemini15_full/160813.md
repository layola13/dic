无法按照要求解析"{possess-of}"，因为"possess-of"本身并非一个独立存在的英文单词。  "possess" 是一个动词，"of" 是一个介词。"possess of" 这样的组合在标准英语中并不常见，且语法上不正确。  要解析的是 "possess" 这个单词。


possess: /pəˈzɛs/ | vt. |拥有；具有；支配|To have or own something.  To have a particular quality or characteristic.|例句：He possesses a rare talent. 他拥有罕见的才能。She possesses great courage. 她拥有巨大的勇气。|近义词：own, have, hold, control |反义词：lack, want


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He possesses a large collection of stamps. = 他拥有一大批邮票收藏。
They possess the necessary skills. = 他们拥有必要的技能。

一般过去时:
She possessed a strong sense of justice. = 她具有强烈的正义感。
He possessed a valuable painting. = 他拥有一幅名贵的画作。

现在完成时:
I have possessed this book for many years. = 我拥有这本书很多年了。
They have possessed that land for generations. = 他们世世代代拥有那块土地。

过去完成时:
He had possessed the property before the war. = 他在战争前就拥有这处房产。
She had possessed the secret for years. = 她多年来一直保守着这个秘密。

将来时:
She will possess the necessary qualifications soon. = 她很快就会具备必要的资格。
They will possess a new house next year. = 他们明年将拥有一栋新房子。


被动语态:
The land was possessed by the family for centuries. = 这块土地世世代代为这个家族所有。
The house was possessed by evil spirits, they said. = 他们说这房子闹鬼。


疑问句:
Does he possess the required experience? = 他拥有所需的经验吗？
Do you possess a valid driver's license? = 你有有效的驾驶执照吗？


否定句:
He does not possess the necessary funds. = 他没有必要的资金。
I do not possess the skills to complete this task. = 我没有完成这项任务的技能。
