cribration:/kraɪˈbreɪʃən/| n.|筛分；筛选；[解剖]筛状结构|The process of separating materials of different sizes using a sieve or screen.  It can also refer to the sieve-like structure in anatomy.|例句：The cribration of the ore yielded high-quality particles.  矿石的筛分产生了高质量的颗粒。
The cribriform plate is a part of the ethmoid bone.筛板是筛骨的一部分。|近义词：sieving, screening, sifting|反义词：aggregation, clumping|名词
