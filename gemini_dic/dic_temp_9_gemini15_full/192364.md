script:/skrɪpt/| n. v. |剧本；手稿；脚本；笔迹；书写；编写|a written text for a play, film, broadcast, etc.|The script for the movie was excellent.  这部电影的剧本很棒。|The doctor carefully examined the patient's script. 医生仔细检查了病人的处方。|manuscript, screenplay, text|improvise, ad-lib|noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The script calls for a dramatic scene here. = 剧本在这里要求一个戏剧性的场景。
This script needs more dialogue. = 这个剧本需要更多对话。

一般过去时:
She scripted the entire play herself. = 她自己编写了整部戏剧。
They scripted a new commercial for television. = 他们为电视编写了一个新的广告。

现在完成时:
He has scripted several award-winning films. = 他已经编写了几部获奖电影。
The playwright has scripted countless plays throughout her career.  这位剧作家在其职业生涯中创作了无数戏剧。

过去完成时:
They had scripted the scene before the actors arrived. = 在演员到达之前，他们已经编写了那个场景。
The director had scripted the ending many times before settling on this version.导演在最终确定这个版本之前，已经多次修改过结尾。

将来时:
She will script the next episode of the series. = 她将编写该系列剧的下一集。
They will script the advertisement in the next few days. 他们将在接下来的几天内编写广告。

虚拟语气:
If I had scripted it differently, the ending would have been more impactful. = 如果我写得不一样，结局会更有影响力。
I wish I had scripted that scene more carefully.  我希望我更仔细地写那个场景。

被动语态:
The script was written by a famous playwright. = 剧本是一位著名剧作家写的。
The scene was scripted to be emotionally powerful.  这个场景的编写是为了使其在情感上更有力。

疑问句:
Who scripted this amazing film? = 谁写的这部精彩的电影？
Is the script ready for the next rehearsal? = 剧本准备好进行下一次排练了吗？

否定句:
The play wasn't scripted as originally intended. = 这部剧没有按照最初的意图编写。
He didn't script the dialogue well enough. 他没有把对话写好。
