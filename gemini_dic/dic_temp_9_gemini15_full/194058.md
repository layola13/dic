self-hates: /ˌsɛlfˈheɪts/ | n. | 自我厌恶 |  A feeling of intense dislike or disgust towards oneself. This can manifest in various ways, including negative self-talk, self-destructive behaviors, and low self-esteem.  It's often a symptom of underlying mental health conditions like depression or anxiety. | Example:  Her self-hates stemmed from years of bullying and criticism.  她的自我厌恶源于多年的欺凌和批评。  |近义词: self-loathing, self-contempt, self-deprecation | 反义词: self-love, self-acceptance | 名词
