freezing-point:/ˈfriːzɪŋpɔɪnt/| n.|冰点，凝固点|The temperature at which a liquid turns into a solid.  It's the temperature at which a substance changes from a liquid state to a solid state. For example, the freezing point of water is 0 degrees Celsius or 32 degrees Fahrenheit.|The water in the lake reached its freezing point overnight.|近义词：melting point (in reverse),凝固点|反义词：boiling point
