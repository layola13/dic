leapt:/lɛpt/| v.|跳跃；跃过；骤增|The past tense of "leap".  It describes a sudden, energetic jump or a rapid increase. |例句：The deer leapt over the fence.  鹿跃过了栅栏。

The frog leapt into the pond. 青蛙跳进了池塘。


近义词：jumped, sprang, bounded|反义词：fell, dropped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leaps over the hurdle. 他跳过障碍物。
The company leaps to new heights. 公司跃上新的高度。


一般过去时:
She leapt for joy. 她高兴地跳了起来。
The stock price leapt significantly. 股价大幅上涨。


现在完成时:
The rabbit has leapt into the burrow. 兔子已经跳进了地洞。
Profits have leapt dramatically this quarter. 本季度利润大幅增长。


过去完成时:
He had leapt to his feet before I could speak.  在我开口之前，他已经跳了起来。
The sales had leapt by 20% before the promotion ended. 促销结束前，销售额已增长了20%。


将来时:
The athlete will leap over the high jump bar. 运动员将跳过高跳杆。
The demand will leap in the coming months. 未来几个月需求将会激增。


虚拟语气:
If he leapt, he would have reached the other side. 如果他跳过去，就能到达对面。
I wish I had leapt at the opportunity. 我真希望我当时抓住那个机会。


被动语态:
The hurdle was leapt with ease. 障碍物很容易就被跳过去了。(很少用被动)
The record was leapt by the athlete.  运动员打破了纪录。(很少用被动)


疑问句:
Did he leap the fence? 他跳过栅栏了吗？
Will the prices leap next year? 明年价格会暴涨吗？


否定句:
He did not leap over the stream. 他没有跳过小溪。
The number of cases did not leap this year. 今年病例数量没有激增。


PHRASE->
leap of faith: 冒险的举动  a leap of faith into the unknown. 向未知领域迈出的冒险一步。
leap year: 闰年  The next leap year is 2024. 下一个闰年是2024年。
leap forward: 飞跃；突飞猛进  a leap forward in technology. 技术的飞跃。
leap to conclusions: 轻易下结论  Don't leap to conclusions; let's wait for the facts. 不要轻易下结论；让我们等待事实。
take a leap: 冒险；尝试  She decided to take a leap and start her own business. 她决定冒险创业。
