emphatics:/ɪmˈfætɪks/| n.|强调，重音；强调词|Words or phrases used to add emphasis or stress to a statement or idea.  They often serve to highlight a particular point or feeling. |例句：Her emphatic denial of the allegations convinced no one.  她断然否认指控，但这并没有说服任何人。|例句：The emphatic rhythm of the music was infectious. 音乐强烈的节奏极具感染力。|近义词：stress, emphasis, force, intensity|反义词：understatement, downplaying
