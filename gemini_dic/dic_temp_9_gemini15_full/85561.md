hate:/heɪt/| vt. n.|憎恨，讨厌|To feel intense dislike or aversion for.  It can also refer to the feeling of intense dislike itself.|例句：I hate spiders. 我讨厌蜘蛛。

例句：He hates being told what to do. 他讨厌被别人指使。


近义词：detest, loathe, abhor|反义词：love, adore, cherish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hate Mondays. = 我讨厌星期一。
She hates loud noises. = 她讨厌大声的噪音。

一般过去时:
I hated school when I was young. = 我小时候讨厌上学。
He hated the way she treated him. = 他讨厌她对待他的方式。

现在完成时:
I have hated this job for years. = 我已经讨厌这份工作好多年了。
She has hated him ever since he lied to her. = 从他对她撒谎后她就一直讨厌他。

过去完成时:
I had hated him long before I met his wife. = 在我遇见他妻子很久以前我就讨厌他了。
She had hated the food even before she tasted it. = 她甚至在尝之前就讨厌那食物了。

将来时:
I will hate this even more tomorrow. = 明天我将更加讨厌这个。
She will hate leaving her friends. = 她将讨厌离开她的朋友们。

虚拟语气:
I would hate to see you go. = 我不想看到你走。
I wish I didn't hate him so much. = 我希望我不那么讨厌他。

被动语态:
His actions are hated by many. = 他的行为被许多人憎恨。
Her behavior is hated by everyone in the office. = 她的行为被办公室里的每个人都讨厌。

疑问句:
Do you hate broccoli? = 你讨厌西兰花吗？
Does he hate cats? = 他讨厌猫吗？

否定句:
I don't hate him, I just dislike him. = 我并不讨厌他，我只是不喜欢他。
She doesn't hate her job, she just finds it boring. = 她并不讨厌她的工作，只是觉得它很无聊。


PHRASE->
hate to say/do something = 不得不遗憾地说/做某事
hate to think = 不敢想象
can't stand to hate someone = 无法忍受讨厌某人
hate the thought of  = 讨厌想到
hate doing something = 讨厌做某事
