playspace:/ˈpleɪspeɪs/| n. |游戏空间，游乐场|An area designed for children to play in, often with equipment such as swings, slides, and climbing frames.  It can also refer to a less structured area where children can engage in imaginative play.|例句：The new playspace at the park is very popular with the local children.  公园新的游戏空间很受当地孩子们欢迎。|近义词：playground, play area, recreation area |反义词：workplace, study space
