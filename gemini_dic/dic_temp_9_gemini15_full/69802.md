ferns:/fɜːrnz/| n.|蕨类植物|Ferns are spore-producing vascular plants that reproduce via spores rather than seeds. They are characterized by their feathery or frond-like leaves.  They thrive in moist, shady environments.| 例句：The forest floor was covered with lush ferns. 森林地面覆盖着茂密的蕨类植物。| 例句：Many different types of ferns grow in the rainforest. 热带雨林生长着许多不同种类的蕨类植物。|近义词：fern plants, pteridophytes|反义词：flowering plants (in terms of reproduction)|名词
