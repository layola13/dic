cephalopod:/ˈsɛfəlɒpɒd/| n. |头足类动物|A marine mollusk of the class Cephalopoda, characterized by having the head surrounded by tentacles.  Cephalopods include octopuses, squids, cuttlefish, and nautiluses.|例句：The octopus is a fascinating cephalopod known for its intelligence.  这只章鱼是一种以其智力而闻名的令人着迷的头足类动物。|例句：Scientists are studying the unique camouflage abilities of various cephalopod species.  科学家们正在研究各种头足类动物独特的伪装能力。|近义词：mollusk (general term)|反义词：无
