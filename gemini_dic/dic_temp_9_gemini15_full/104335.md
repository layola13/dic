juvenescent:/ˌdʒuːvəˈnɛsənt/| adj.|青春的；返老还童的；恢复青春的 |Becoming or appearing young again; showing renewed youth.  The word implies a regaining of youthfulness, often after a period of decline or aging.  It suggests a revitalization or rejuvenation, not simply the appearance of youth. |例句：The garden, with its vibrant flowers, had a strangely juvenescent quality. 花园里鲜花盛开，呈现出一种奇特的青春活力。

The fountain of youth, a legendary source of juvenescence, is a recurring theme in literature and mythology. 青春之泉，一个传说中的恢复青春的源泉，是文学和神话中反复出现的主题。


近义词：youthful, rejuvenated, revitalized, youthful-looking

反义词：aged, senile, decrepit, elderly


词源:  From Latin *iuvenēscēns, present participle of iuvenēscere "to grow young," from iuvenis "young."  The suffix "-escent" indicates a process of becoming or tending toward a state.
