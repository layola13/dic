polystyrenes:/ˌpɒliˈstaɪriːnz/ | n. |聚苯乙烯（复数） |Polystyrenes are synthetic aromatic polymers of styrene.  They are commonly used in a variety of applications due to their lightweight, insulating, and inexpensive nature.  The plural form of polystyrene, referring to multiple instances or types of the material. |Example:  Many disposable cups and food containers are made from polystyrenes. 许多一次性杯子和食品容器都是用聚苯乙烯制成的。|近义词：synthetic polymers, plastics |反义词：无 |  名词
