denigratory:/ˈdɛnɪɡrətɔːri/| adj. |贬低的；诋毁的；诽谤的 | Tending to belittle or disparage; derogatory.  It suggests an attempt to damage someone's reputation or undermine their worth. |例句：His denigratory remarks about her abilities were completely unwarranted. 他对她能力的贬损言论完全没有道理。
例句：The article contained several denigratory comments about the competitor's product. 这篇文章包含几条贬低竞争对手产品的评论。|近义词：disparaging, derogatory, belittling, defamatory, slanderous |反义词：laudatory, complimentary, praising

词源:
From Latin *denigrātor, from denigrārī "to blacken," from de- "down, off" + niger "black."  The sense evolved from literally blackening someone's reputation to figuratively disparaging them.
