securing:/sɪˈkjʊərɪŋ/| v. | 获取；保护；使安全 |  The act of making something safe or obtaining something.  It implies taking steps to ensure something is protected from harm or loss. |

例句：
The company is securing new funding for its project. = 这家公司正在为其项目筹集新的资金。

They are securing the perimeter with barbed wire. = 他们正在用铁丝网保护周边地区。


近义词：obtaining, protecting, safeguarding, guaranteeing

反义词：endangering, jeopardizing, risking, compromising


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is securing the documents. = 他正在保护文件。
She secures a new client every week. = 她每周都能获得一个新客户。

一般过去时:
They secured the building against intruders. = 他们保护建筑物免受入侵者侵害。
He secured a loan to buy a house. = 他获得贷款买了一套房子。

现在完成时:
We have secured a contract with a major supplier. = 我们已经与一家主要供应商签订了合同。
She has secured a place at university. = 她已经获得了一个大学名额。

过去完成时:
They had secured the evidence before the trial. = 审判前他们已经保护了证据。
He had secured a good job before he got married. = 他结婚前已经找到了一份好工作。

将来时:
We will secure the premises tonight. = 我们今晚将保护该处所。
They will secure funding for their research. = 他们将为他们的研究获得资金。

虚拟语气:
If we secured more funding, we could expand the business. = 如果我们获得更多资金，我们就能扩大业务。
I wish I had secured a better deal. = 我希望我当时能达成更好的交易。

被动语态:
The building was secured overnight. = 该建筑物整夜都处于保护状态。
The funds were secured through a government grant. = 资金是通过政府拨款获得的。

疑问句:
Have you secured the necessary permits? = 你已经获得必要的许可证了吗？
Will they be able to secure enough votes to win the election? = 他们能否获得足够的选票赢得选举？

否定句:
They failed to secure a visa. = 他们未能获得签证。
The company did not secure enough investment. = 该公司没有获得足够的投资。


PHRASE->
secure a job = 找到一份工作
secure a loan = 获得贷款
secure a contract = 签订合同
secure funding = 获得资金
secure the future = 保障未来

词源:
The word "securing" comes from the Old French word "securer," meaning "to make safe,"  which ultimately derives from the Latin word "securus," meaning "free from care," "safe," or "secure."
