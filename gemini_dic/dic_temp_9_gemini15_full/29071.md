carvel:/ˈkɑːrvəl/| n.|平底船；龙骨外板|A carvel is a type of boat construction where the planks are laid edge to edge, creating a smooth, flush surface.|例句：The old fishing boat was built using the carvel method. 这艘旧渔船是用平底船法建造的。  The carvel hull is known for its sleek lines. 平底船船体以其流畅的线条而闻名。|近义词：flush-planked, smooth-planked|反义词：clinker-built
