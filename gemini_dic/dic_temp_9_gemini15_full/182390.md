renegated:/rɪˈneɪɡeɪtɪd/| adj.|背叛的；否认的；放弃的 | Having renounced or abandoned a commitment or allegiance.  It describes someone or something that has been rejected or abandoned. |例句：The renegated treaty left both sides vulnerable.  (被废弃的条约使双方都变得脆弱。) The renegated plan was never implemented. (被放弃的计划从未实施。) |近义词：rejected, repudiated, forsaken, abandoned |反义词：accepted, confirmed, ratified, upheld
