leghorn: /ˈliːɡɔːrn/ | n. |利古里亚鸡；莱格洪帽；(意大利)利沃诺| A breed of chicken, originally from Italy, known for its white eggs and prolific laying.  It can also refer to a type of straw hat. |例句：The farmer raised Leghorn chickens for their abundant egg production.  这位农民饲养利古里亚鸡以获得大量的鸡蛋产量。

例句：She wore a stylish Leghorn hat to the garden party. 她戴着一顶时尚的莱格洪帽参加花园派对。

近义词： (for the chicken)  laying hen, Mediterranean chicken | (for the hat)  straw hat, boater

反义词：  None (There are no direct antonyms for either meaning)


词性：名词
