pelfish:/ˈpɛlfɪʃ/| adj.|贪财的，唯利是图的|Selfish and greedy, especially in the pursuit of money.  The word implies a base or sordid greed.|例句：He was a pelfish man, always looking for ways to increase his wealth, even at the expense of others. 他是个贪财的人，总是想方设法增加自己的财富，即使是以牺牲他人为代价。

例句：The pelfish actions of the corporation led to widespread public outcry.  这家公司的贪婪行为导致了公众的强烈抗议。


近义词：greedy, avaricious, covetous, mercenary, rapacious


反义词：generous, altruistic, charitable, unselfish


词性：形容词
