pennated:/ˈpɛneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 羽状的；羽裂的 | Having barbs or side branches, resembling a feather.  The term is often used in botany and zoology to describe structures with a feather-like appearance.  | Example: The pennated leaves of the fern unfurled in the spring breeze.  蕨类的羽状叶在春风中舒展开来。 The bird's pennated tail feathers were iridescent in the sunlight.  鸟的羽状尾羽在阳光下呈现彩虹色。 | 近义词：feathery, plumose | 反义词：unbranched, simple
