med's: /medz/ | n. | 药品；药物（med的缩写） |  Med's are used to treat illnesses.  药品用于治疗疾病。| 近义词：medicines, drugs | 反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor prescribes meds for the patient. = 医生为病人开药。
She takes her meds every morning. = 她每天早上服药。


一般过去时:
He took his meds before bed last night. = 他昨晚睡前服药了。
The pharmacist filled the med's prescription. = 药剂师完成了处方药的配药。


现在完成时:
I have taken all my meds this week. = 我这周已经服完了所有的药。
She has not finished her course of meds. = 她还没有完成疗程。


过去完成时:
He had taken his meds before the doctor arrived. = 医生到达之前他已经服药了。
She had forgotten to take her meds that morning. = 那天早上她忘了服药。


将来时:
I will take my meds after dinner. = 我晚饭后会服药。
The doctor will prescribe stronger meds if needed. = 如果需要，医生会开更强的药。


虚拟语气:
If I had taken my meds, I wouldn't feel so bad. = 如果我服了药，我就不会感觉这么难受。
I wish I had taken my meds earlier. = 我希望我早点服药。


被动语态:
The meds were administered by the nurse. = 药物是由护士给的。
The prescription meds are being prepared. = 处方药正在准备中。


疑问句:
Have you taken your meds today? = 你今天服药了吗？
What kind of meds are you taking? = 你正在服用什么药？


否定句:
He didn't take his meds on time. = 他没有按时服药。
She hasn't taken her meds yet. = 她还没服药。
