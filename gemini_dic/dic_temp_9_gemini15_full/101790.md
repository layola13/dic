irrevocably:/ɪˈrevəkəbli/ | adj. |不可撤销地；不可改变地 | incapable of being revoked or reversed; impossible to change.  | The decision was irrevocably made.  这个决定是不可更改的。  |  unalterably, permanently, finally |  revocable, changeable, reversible |  形容词
