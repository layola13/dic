cetology:/siˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. |鲸豚学|The scientific study of whales, dolphins, and porpoises.  It encompasses their biology, behavior, ecology, and conservation.  |例句：Cetology is a fascinating field that combines zoology, oceanography, and conservation biology.  鲸豚学是一个迷人的领域，它结合了动物学、海洋学和保护生物学。

例句：Researchers in cetology are working to understand the impact of climate change on whale populations. 鲸豚学的研究人员正在努力了解气候变化对鲸鱼种群的影响。
