calumnied:/ˈkæləmniːd/| vt.|诽谤；中伤；污蔑 |To make false and defamatory statements about; slander.  |例句：He was calumnied by his rivals for years.  他多年来一直被他的竞争对手诽谤。|例句：The newspaper article calumnied the politician's reputation. 这篇报纸文章诽谤了这位政治家的名誉。|近义词：slander, defame, libel, vilify |反义词：praise, commend, extol

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They calumniate their opponents constantly. = 他们不断地诽谤他们的对手。
He calumniates his colleagues behind their backs. = 他在背后诽谤他的同事。

一般过去时:
The newspaper calumnied the mayor's actions. = 报纸诽谤了市长的行为。
She calumnied her neighbor to the police. = 她向警察诽谤她的邻居。


现在完成时:
The company has been calumnied by its competitors. = 这家公司一直被竞争对手诽谤。
He has calumnied many people throughout his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中诽谤过很多人。


过去完成时:
They had calumnied him for months before the trial. = 在审判前几个月，他们就一直在诽谤他。
The politician had been calumnied repeatedly before the election. = 在选举之前，这位政治家已被反复诽谤。


将来时:
They will calumniate him again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他们还会再次诽谤他。
The media will likely calumniate the candidate before the next election. = 媒体可能会在下次选举前诽谤这位候选人。


虚拟语气:
If they were to calumniate me, I would sue them. = 如果他们诽谤我，我会起诉他们。
I wish they hadn't calumnied my family name. = 我真希望他们没有诽谤我的家族声誉。


被动语态:
He was calumnied by the press for his unpopular policies. = 由于他的不得人心政策，他被媒体诽谤。
The innocent man was calumnied and imprisoned. = 那位无辜的人被诽谤并监禁。


疑问句:
Were they calumnied by the media? = 他们被媒体诽谤了吗？
Has he ever been calumnied in the past? = 他过去有没有被诽谤过？


否定句:
They did not calumniate the witness. = 他们没有诽谤证人。
The article did not calumniate anyone. = 这篇文章没有诽谤任何人。
