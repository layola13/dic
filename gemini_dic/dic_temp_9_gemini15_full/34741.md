cliental:/klaɪˈɛntəl/| n. |顾客，客户群 |A clientele is the group of people who regularly use the services of a business, especially a professional one such as a lawyer or a hairdresser.  |The restaurant boasts a loyal clientele. 这家餐馆拥有忠实的顾客群。|customer base, patronage|  unpopular, undesirable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The salon attracts a diverse clientele. = 这家沙龙吸引了各种各样的顾客。
Our company relies on a stable clientele for success. = 我们公司依靠稳定的客户群来取得成功。

一般过去时:
The boutique lost much of its clientele after the owner retired. = 店主退休后，精品店失去了很多顾客。
The hotel's clientele changed significantly after the renovation. = 装修后，酒店的顾客群体发生了显著变化。

现在完成时:
The law firm has built a strong clientele over the past decade. = 这家律师事务所在过去十年中建立了强大的客户群。
The spa has cultivated a loyal clientele through excellent service. = 水疗中心通过优质的服务培养了一批忠实的顾客。


过去完成时:
Before the expansion, the business had a much smaller clientele. = 在扩张之前，这家企业拥有小得多的客户群。
The restaurant had already established a significant clientele by the time of its anniversary. = 到周年纪念日的时候，这家餐厅已经拥有了相当多的顾客。


将来时:
The new marketing campaign hopes to attract a wider clientele. = 新的营销活动希望吸引更广泛的客户群。
We anticipate a growing clientele as the economy improves. = 我们预计随着经济好转，客户群将会增长。

虚拟语气:
If the company had a larger clientele, it would be more profitable. = 如果公司拥有更多的客户，它将更有利可图。
I wish we had a more discerning clientele. = 我希望我们有更挑剔的客户。

被动语态:
The business's reputation is built on its loyal clientele. = 这家企业的声誉建立在其忠实的客户群之上。
The restaurant's success is largely attributed to its dedicated clientele. = 这家餐馆的成功很大程度上归功于其忠实的顾客。

疑问句:
What kind of clientele does the hotel cater to? = 这家酒店服务于什么样的客户？
What is the profile of your target clientele? = 你们目标客户群体的特征是什么？

否定句:
The shop doesn't have a very large clientele. = 这家商店的顾客不多。
The new product hasn't yet attracted a significant clientele. = 新产品尚未吸引大量的顾客。
