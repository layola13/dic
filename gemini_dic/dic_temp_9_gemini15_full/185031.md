rethatches:/ˌriːˈtætʃɪz/ | v. | 重新盖屋顶；重新铺盖 | To cover (a roof, etc.) again with thatch.  This word is a relatively uncommon verb, formed by combining "re-" (again) and "thatches" (the present tense of the verb "thatch").| 例句：The farmer rethatched the barn roof after the storm damaged it. =暴风雨过后，农民重新给谷仓屋顶盖上了茅草。 The old cottage needed rethatching to withstand the harsh winter. = 老房子需要重新盖上茅草才能抵御严冬。| 近义词：re-roof, repair (a roof) | 反义词：unroof, demolish
