interparty: /ˌɪntərˈpɑːrti/ | adj. |党际的；党内的 | Relating to or involving more than one political party.  |The interparty negotiations broke down without an agreement.| 近义词：inter-party, bipartisan (if referring to only two parties) | 反义词：intraparty (within one party)
