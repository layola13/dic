competitress: /kəmˈpɛtɪtrɛs/ | n. | 女竞争者，女对手 | A woman who competes, especially in business or sports.  A female competitor. |例句：The competitress displayed remarkable skill and determination throughout the race. 这名女竞争者在整个比赛中展现出非凡的技巧和决心。

例句：She proved to be a formidable competitress in the business world.  她在商界证明自己是一位强大的竞争对手。


近义词：competitor, rival, contender | 反义词：collaborator, ally


词源:  Derived from the word "competitor," adding the suffix "-ess" to indicate the feminine gender.  The word "competitor" itself comes from the Latin "conpetitor," combining "com-" (together) and "petere" (to strive for, seek).
