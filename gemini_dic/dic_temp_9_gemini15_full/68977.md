fatigable:/fəˈtɑɪɡəbəl/ | adj. | 易疲劳的，易倦的 | Easily tired; subject to fatigue.  | The long hike left him feeling fatigued and irritable. 漫长的徒步旅行使他感到疲惫不堪且易怒。  He is fatigued after a long day of work. 他一天的工作后感到疲惫。| tiresome, tiring, wearied | tireless, energetic
