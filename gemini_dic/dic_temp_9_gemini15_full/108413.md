lawn:/lɔːn/| n. |草坪，草地 | A piece of land covered with short, mown grass, especially one near a house.  |例句：The children were playing on the lawn. 孩子们在草坪上玩耍。  |例句：We had a picnic on the perfectly manicured lawn. 我们在一个修剪整齐的草坪上野餐。|近义词：turf, sward |反义词：desert, wasteland


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lawn needs mowing. = 草坪需要修剪。
He waters the lawn every morning. = 他每天早上都浇草坪。

一般过去时:
We played croquet on the lawn yesterday. = 昨天我们在草坪上玩槌球。
She cut the lawn last Saturday. = 她上周六割了草坪。

现在完成时:
They have just finished mowing the lawn. = 他们刚刚结束修剪草坪。
We have had a lot of rain, so the lawn is very green. = 我们下了很多雨，所以草坪很绿。

过去完成时:
They had already mowed the lawn before the party started. = 派对开始前他们已经修剪了草坪。
The lawn had been dry for weeks before the rain came. = 下雨前草坪已经干旱了几个星期。

将来时:
I will mow the lawn tomorrow. = 我明天将要修剪草坪。
They will have a barbecue on the lawn next weekend. = 他们下周末将在草坪上举行烧烤。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would tend the lawn better. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更好地打理草坪。
I wish the lawn were greener. = 我希望草坪更绿一些。

被动语态:
The lawn is mowed weekly. = 草坪每周修剪一次。
The lawn was damaged by the storm. = 草坪被暴风雨破坏了。

疑问句:
Is the lawn big enough for a tent? = 草坪够大能搭帐篷吗？
Whose lawn is this? = 这是谁家的草坪？

否定句:
The lawn isn't very well-maintained. = 草坪维护得不太好。
He didn't mow the lawn last week. = 他上周没有修剪草坪。
