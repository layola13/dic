quarters:/ˈkwɔːrtərz/| n.|四分之一；宿舍；地区；季度|A quarter is one-fourth of something.  Quarters can also refer to living accommodations, a specific area or region, or a period of three months.|例句：I'll pay you in quarters. 我会用四分之一美元付给你。
例句：The soldiers were quartered in the old schoolhouse.士兵们住在旧校舍里。


近义词：fourth, lodging, district, trimester|反义词：whole, entirety, single
