neckerchiefs:/ˈnekˌtʃɑːrfz/| n.|围巾；领巾|A neckerchief is a piece of cloth worn around the neck, often tied in a knot.  It can be used for warmth, fashion, or as part of a uniform.|She wore a colorful neckerchief with her summer dress. 她穿着夏装，戴着一条色彩鲜艳的围巾。 He tied his neckerchief neatly around his neck. 他把围巾整齐地系在脖子上。|scarf, kerchief|——|
