change-color: /ˈtʃeɪndʒ ˈkʌlər/ | v. | 变色，换颜色 | To alter or modify the color of something.  This verb describes the action of something shifting or being shifted to a different hue. It can refer to a gradual or sudden change. | 例句：The chameleon changed color to match the leaves.  变色龙变色以匹配树叶。

The leaves changed color in the autumn. 秋天的树叶变色了。


近义词：alter, shift, transform, modify | 反义词：remain the same, stay constant | 词性: verb
