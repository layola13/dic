rationalizable: /ˈræʃənəlaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可合理化的；可解释的 |  Able to be explained or justified in a rational or logical way.  It suggests that an action or decision, even if seemingly illogical or questionable, can be given a reasonable explanation. |

例句：

His aggressive behavior, while unsettling, was rationalizable in light of his traumatic childhood.  他的侵略性行为虽然令人不安，但考虑到他痛苦的童年，是可以解释的。

The company's decision to lay off workers, though unpopular, was rationalizable as a necessary cost-cutting measure. 公司裁员的决定虽然不受欢迎，但作为必要的成本削减措施是可以解释的。


近义词： justifiable, explainable, defensible

反义词：unjustifiable, inexplicable, indefensible


词性：形容词
