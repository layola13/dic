delightingly:/dɪˈlaɪtɪŋli/| adv. |令人愉快地，令人高兴地 |In a delightful manner; pleasingly.  It describes something that is very pleasant and enjoyable. |例句：The children played delightfully in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩得很开心。


例句：She sang delightfully, captivating the audience. 她唱得令人愉快，迷住了观众。


近义词：pleasantly, charmingly, enjoyably, sweetly


反义词：unpleasantly, unhappily, disagreeably
