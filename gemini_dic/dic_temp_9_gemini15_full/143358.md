outfacing:/ˈaʊtfeɪsɪŋ/| adj. |朝外的；外向的；面向外部的 |Facing outward; exposed to the outside.  It refers to something that is directed or oriented towards the exterior or outside.  |例句：The outfacing walls of the building were made of brick. 建筑物的朝外墙壁是用砖砌成的。

例句：The outfacing windows offered a stunning view of the ocean. 朝外的窗户提供了令人惊叹的海洋景色。


近义词：external, outward, exposed


反义词：inward-facing, internal


词性：形容词
