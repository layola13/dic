ladybug:/ˈleɪdiˌbʌɡ/|/ˈleɪdiˌbʌɡ/| n. |瓢虫，七星瓢虫|A ladybug is a small, round beetle with a hard shell, usually red or orange with black spots.  They are beneficial insects that eat aphids and other plant pests.|例句：I saw a ladybug crawling on the rose bush. 我看到一只瓢虫在玫瑰花丛上爬行。|例句：The children were delighted to find a ladybug in the garden. 孩子们很高兴在花园里发现一只瓢虫。|近义词：ladybird, lady beetle|反义词: N/A
