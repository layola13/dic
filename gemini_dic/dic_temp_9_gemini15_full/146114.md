overembroiders:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈbrɔɪdərz/| vt.|过度绣花；过于装饰|To decorate (something) excessively with embroidery or other ornamentation.  It implies an excess of decoration that may be considered gaudy or tasteless.|例句：She overembroidered the dress, obscuring the delicate fabric underneath. = 她把裙子绣得太过分了，遮盖住了下面精致的面料。

例句：The cake was overembroidered with sugary decorations, making it look cluttered and unattractive. = 蛋糕上装饰了过多的糖霜，看起来凌乱不堪。


近义词：overdecorate, overadorn, embellish excessively, gild the lily


反义词：underdecorate, simplify, minimize


词性：动词
