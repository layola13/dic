recurrence:/rɪˈkɜːrəns/| n. |复发，重现，再现 |The recurrence of the problem is worrying.  这个问题的反复出现令人担忧。|The recurrence of the theme throughout the novel is striking. 小说贯穿始终的主题令人印象深刻。|近义词：repetition, reappearance, return |反义词：uniqueness, singularity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem recurs frequently. = 这个问题经常复发。
His headaches recur every few days. = 他的头痛每隔几天就复发一次。

一般过去时:
The symptom recurred after a week. = 症状在一周后复发了。
The earthquake recurred in the same region. = 地震在同一地区再次发生。

现在完成时:
The disease has recurred several times. = 这种疾病已经复发了数次。
The pain has recurred since the surgery. = 手术后疼痛又复发了。

过去完成时:
The rash had recurred before the doctor's visit. = 在看医生之前皮疹已经复发了。
The error had recurred in the previous version. = 错误在之前的版本中已经出现过。

将来时:
The doctor fears the illness will recur. = 医生担心疾病会复发。
He anticipates the problem will recur next year. = 他预计这个问题明年还会出现。

虚拟语气:
If the symptoms recurred, I would see a doctor immediately. = 如果症状复发，我会立即去看医生。
I wish the problem wouldn't recur. = 我希望这个问题不会再发生。

被动语态:
The disease is known to recur in some patients. = 已知这种疾病在某些患者中会复发。
The error was found to have recurred in the new software. = 发现错误在新软件中再次出现。

疑问句:
Does the pain recur often? = 疼痛经常复发吗？
Will the problem recur after the fix? = 修复后问题还会复发吗？

否定句:
The problem did not recur after the treatment. = 治疗后问题没有复发。
The error hasn't recurred since the update. = 更新后错误没有再次出现。


PHRASE->
recurrence rate = 复发率
recurrence interval = 复发间隔
recurrence relation = 递推关系
risk of recurrence = 复发风险
recurrence prevention = 复发预防
