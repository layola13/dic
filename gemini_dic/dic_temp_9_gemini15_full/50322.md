deploration:/ˌdɛpləˈreɪʃən/| n. |痛惜；悲叹；谴责 |The expression of grief or sorrow;  a strong expression of disapproval.  It often involves a formal or public expression of sadness or condemnation. |例句：The ceremony was filled with deploration for the victims of the accident.  仪式充满了对事故受害者的悲痛。

例句：His deploration of the government's policies was widely reported in the media. 他对政府政策的谴责在媒体上被广泛报道。


近义词：lament, bewailing, condemnation, denunciation, reproach

反义词：celebration, praise, commendation
