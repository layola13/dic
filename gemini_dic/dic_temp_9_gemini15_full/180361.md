refurbisher:/rɪˈfɜːrbɪʃər/ | n. | 翻新者；修理者 | A person or company that refurbishes something, especially buildings or equipment.  | The refurbisher completely transformed the old office building.  翻新者彻底改造了旧办公楼。 | renovator, restorer, repairer | destroyer, ruiner | 名词


USAGE->
