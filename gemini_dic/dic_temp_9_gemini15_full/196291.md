serfage:/ˈsɜːrfɪdʒ/| n. |农奴身份；农奴制度 |The state of being a serf; the system of serfdom.  Serfage was a characteristic of feudal societies.  |例句：The abolition of serfage in Russia led to significant social and economic changes.  俄罗斯废除农奴制导致了重大的社会和经济变革。|近义词：serfdom, bondage, servitude |反义词：freedom, liberty |名词
