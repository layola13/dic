chewed:/tʃuːd/| v.|咀嚼；反复考虑|To crush food in the mouth with the teeth to prepare it for swallowing.  It can also refer to the act of thinking deeply or repeatedly about something.|例句：The dog chewed the bone until it was splintered.  这条狗把骨头嚼碎了。
He chewed on the problem for days before finding a solution. 他几天来一直在反复思考这个问题，才找到解决办法。|近义词：masticate, gnaw|反义词：swallow (whole)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chews his food thoroughly. 他细嚼慢咽。
She chews gum all day. 她整天嚼口香糖。

一般过去时:
I chewed my nails nervously. 我紧张地咬指甲。
The cow chewed its cud. 牛在反刍。

现在完成时:
He has chewed through the entire book. 他已经把整本书都看完了。(比喻意义)
The dog has chewed up my shoes. 狗把我的鞋子咬坏了。

过去完成时:
They had chewed over the proposal for hours. 他们已经讨论这个提案好几个小时了。
She had chewed the end of her pen nervously. 她紧张地咬着钢笔笔头。

将来时:
I will chew on this idea later. 我稍后会仔细考虑这个想法。
He will chew through this challenge. 他将会克服这个挑战。(比喻意义)

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would chew over the contract carefully. 如果我有时间，我会仔细审阅合同。
I wish I had chewed over my options more thoroughly. 我希望我当时更仔细地考虑了我的选择。

被动语态:
The food was chewed thoroughly by him.  食物被他细嚼慢咽。
The problem was chewed over by the team.  这个问题被团队反复讨论。

疑问句:
Has she chewed the gum yet? 她嚼口香糖了吗？
Did he chew the food properly? 他把食物嚼烂了吗？

否定句:
He didn't chew his food properly. 他没有好好咀嚼食物。
She doesn't chew gum in public. 她不当众嚼口香糖。
