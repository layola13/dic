disorderedness: /dɪsˈɔːrdərnəs/ | n. |紊乱；无序；混乱 | The state or quality of being disordered; lack of order or regularity.  It refers to a condition characterized by a lack of organization, system, or regularity.  This can apply to physical arrangements, systems, or even abstract concepts. |

例句：

The disorderedness of his room was shocking.  | 他房间的凌乱不堪令人震惊。

The economic disorderedness led to widespread poverty. | 经济混乱导致了普遍贫困。


近义词：chaos, confusion, disarray, disorganization, turmoil, messiness

反义词：order, organization, regularity, system, neatness
