copaiva:/koʊˈpaɪvə/| n. |古巴香脂，科派瓦香脂|A resin obtained from various species of Copaifera trees, used medicinally and in perfumes.  It is a viscous, yellowish-brown oleoresin with a characteristic odor.|Example: The ointment contained copaiva balsam for its antiseptic properties.  这种药膏含有古巴香脂，因为它具有防腐性能。|近义词：balsam, resin|反义词：无
