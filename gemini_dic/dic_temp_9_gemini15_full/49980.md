denier:/dɪˈnaɪər/ | n. | 否认者；怀疑者 | A person who denies something, especially a fact or truth.  | Example: He's a denier of climate change. 他是一个气候变化怀疑论者。 |近义词：disbeliever, doubter, skeptic | 反义词：believer, supporter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The denier refuses to accept the evidence. = 否认者拒绝接受证据。
She denies the accusations against her. = 她否认对她提出的指控。


一般过去时:
The denier denied the existence of the problem. = 否认者否认了这个问题的存在。
He denied having seen the accident. = 他否认见过那场事故。


现在完成时:
The denier has consistently denied the scientific consensus. = 否认者一直否认科学共识。
She has denied all knowledge of the incident. = 她否认对该事件有任何了解。


过去完成时:
The denier had already denied the reports before the meeting. = 在会议之前，否认者已经否认了这些报告。
He had denied everything up until that point. = 直到那时，他都否认了一切。


将来时:
The denier will continue to deny the truth. = 否认者将继续否认真相。
She will deny any involvement in the crime. = 她将否认参与犯罪。


虚拟语气:
If he were a denier, he would not accept the facts. = 如果他是一个否认者，他不会接受事实。
I wish the denier would acknowledge the evidence. = 我希望否认者能承认证据。


被动语态:
The evidence was denied by the denier. = 证据被否认者否认了。
The accusations were denied by the suspect. = 指控被嫌疑人否认了。


疑问句:
Is he a denier of the established facts? = 他是否否认既定事实？
Who are the main deniers of this theory? = 这一理论的主要否认者是谁？


否定句:
He is not a denier of climate change. = 他不是气候变化怀疑论者。
She doesn't deny the importance of the issue. = 她不否认这个问题的重要性。


PHRASE->
