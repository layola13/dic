old-world: /ˈoʊldˌwɜːld/ | adj. | 老世界的；旧世界的；古色古香的；古典的 |  Relating to or characteristic of Europe, especially before the modern era; often suggesting a sense of tradition, elegance, or romance.  It can also refer to a style or aesthetic reminiscent of past eras. |


例句：

The hotel boasted old-world charm with its antique furniture and elegant décor.  这家酒店以其古董家具和优雅的装饰，拥有旧世界的魅力。

He preferred the old-world craftsmanship of handmade shoes to mass-produced footwear. 他更喜欢手工制作鞋子的旧世界工艺，而不是批量生产的鞋类。


近义词：antique, traditional, classic, vintage, archaic


反义词：modern, contemporary, new, futuristic


词性：形容词
