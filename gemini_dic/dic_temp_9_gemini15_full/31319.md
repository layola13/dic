chapter:/ˈtʃæptər/ | n. |章节，篇章 | A chapter is a main division of a book, typically numbered.  It's a self-contained section of a larger work, often dealing with a particular aspect of the subject.  | Example: This chapter deals with the history of the Roman Empire.  这个章节讲述了罗马帝国的历史。|近义词：section, part, division |反义词：None
