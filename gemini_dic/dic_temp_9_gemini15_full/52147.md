diaphanie:/daɪˈæfəni/| n.|透光性，透明度，半透明|The quality of being diaphanous; translucence.  It refers to the property of a material that allows light to pass through it, but not necessarily clearly.  This can apply to various materials, from fabrics to liquids. |例句：The diaphanic quality of the silk allowed the light to filter through beautifully. 丝绸的透光性使光线能够美丽地透过。
The artist used diaphanies of colour to create a dreamy atmosphere. 艺术家运用色彩的透光性创造出梦幻般的氛围。|近义词：translucency, transparency, sheerness|反义词：opacity, impenetrability|名词
