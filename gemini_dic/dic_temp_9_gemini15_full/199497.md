sinecureships:/ˈsaɪnɪkjuəʃɪps/| n.| 闲职；无需努力就能获得的职位；肥缺 | A sinecureship is a position that requires little or no work but provides a salary or other benefits.  It often implies a position obtained through favoritism or patronage rather than merit.  | The scandal involved several high-ranking officials awarded sinecureships for their political loyalty. 这起丑闻涉及几名因政治忠诚而获得闲职的高级官员。 |近义词：easy job, plum job, cushy job |反义词：demanding job, strenuous job, grueling job
