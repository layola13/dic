indazole: /ˈɪndəzoʊl/ | n. | 吲唑 | Indazole is a bicyclic organic compound consisting of a benzene ring fused to a pyrazole ring. It is a heterocyclic aromatic compound with a nitrogen atom in both rings.  It is used as a building block in the synthesis of various pharmaceuticals and agrochemicals. |  Indazole derivatives are found in many pharmaceuticals, acting as inhibitors of various enzymes. For example,  the indazole derivative,  Sunitinib, is used in cancer treatment. | 近义词: (lacks direct synonyms due to its specific chemical structure) | 反义词: (lacks direct antonyms due to its specific chemical structure) | 名词
