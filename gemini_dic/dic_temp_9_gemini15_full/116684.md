maturations:/ˌmætʃəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |成熟；成熟期；发育|The process of becoming mature; the period of development.  It refers to the process of growth and development to a state of full functional capacity. This can apply to biological organisms, processes, or even ideas.|例句：The maturation of the fruit takes several months. 水果成熟需要几个月的时间。|例句：The maturation process of cheese requires careful monitoring of temperature and humidity. 干酪的成熟过程需要仔细监控温度和湿度。|近义词：development, ripening, growth|反义词：immaturity, underdevelopment
