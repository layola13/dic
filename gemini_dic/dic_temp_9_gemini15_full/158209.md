platycephalic:/ˌplætɪsɛˈfælɪk/| adj.|扁平头的；宽头的|Having a broad, flat head. The term is often used to describe a skull shape characterized by a relatively short distance between the forehead and the back of the head, and a relatively wide head.  It's often associated with certain genetic conditions or syndromes.|The brachycephalic skull is platycephalic.|近义词：brachycephalic (although brachycephalic emphasizes breadth more than flatness)|反义词：dolichocephalic (long-headed)
