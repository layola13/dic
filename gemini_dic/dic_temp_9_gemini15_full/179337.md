redoubt:/rɪˈdaʊt/| n. |防御工事；堡垒；避难所 | A fortified place, typically a small one, used as a defense.  It can also refer to a refuge or place of safety. | The soldiers took refuge in the redoubt during the attack.  士兵们在袭击中躲进了防御工事。|The besieged city's redoubt held out for weeks. 被围困城市的堡垒坚持了几周。| fortification, stronghold, bulwark | open field, unprotected area |名词
