nontraining: /ˌnɑːnˈtreɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 非培训的；非训练的 | Not related to or involving training.  This adjective describes something that is outside the scope of training, education, or practice. | 例句：The nontraining staff members are not involved in the new employee orientation.  非培训人员不参与新员工培训。

The nontraining activities during the conference included sightseeing and social events. 会议期间的非培训活动包括观光和社交活动。

近义词： untrained, unschooled, noneducational | 反义词：training, educational
