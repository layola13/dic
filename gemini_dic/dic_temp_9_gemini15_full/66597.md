exhumes:/ɪɡˈzjuːmz/| vt.|掘出；发掘出；使（事情）重见天日|To remove (something, especially a body) from a grave.  The verb often implies a careful and deliberate process, rather than simply digging.|例句：Archaeologists exhumed the ancient artifacts from the burial site. 考古学家从墓地中挖掘出古代文物。

例句：The police exhumed the body to perform a further autopsy.警方掘出尸体进行进一步尸检。


近义词：unearth, disinter, dig up|反义词：bury, inter, conceal|词性:动词
