overfertilize: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrtɪlaɪz/ | vt. |施肥过多；过度施肥 | To apply more fertilizer to (something) than is needed or beneficial.  |例句：Overfertilizing your lawn can damage the grass and pollute nearby water sources.  过度施肥你的草坪会损害草坪并污染附近的水源。

例句：The farmer overfertilized his crops, leading to stunted growth.  这位农民给庄稼施肥过多，导致生长迟缓。


近义词：overmanure, excessively fertilize | 反义词：underfertilize


词源:  over- (prefix indicating excess) + fertilize (from Latin *fertilis "fertile")
