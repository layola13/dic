colonialist:/kəˈloʊniəlɪst/| n. adj.|殖民主义者；殖民主义的|A person who supports or practices colonialism.  Someone who believes in or supports the policy of a nation seeking to acquire and maintain colonies.  The adjective form describes things relating to colonialism or colonial practices. |例句：The colonialist policies of the 19th century had a devastating impact on indigenous populations.  十九世纪的殖民主义政策对土著居民产生了毁灭性的影响。

The colonialist government imposed its laws on the native people. 殖民政府强加其法律于当地人民。|近义词：imperialist, expansionist|反义词：anti-colonialist, independence advocate
