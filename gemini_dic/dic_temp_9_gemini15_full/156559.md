pickering: /ˈpɪkərɪŋ/ | n. |皮克林（姓氏；地名）|  Pickering is a surname of English origin, and also the name of several places, most notably a town in North Yorkshire, England.  The name derives from the Old English elements "pic" (meaning "peak" or "point") and "ing" (meaning "belonging to" or "of").  Therefore, it likely referred originally to someone who lived near a pointed hill or peak. | The Pickering family is well-known in the area.  |近义词: (as a surname, no direct synonyms) |反义词: (as a surname/place name, no direct antonyms) | 名词
