nonvaporous: /ˌnɒnˈveɪpərəs/ | adj. |不蒸发的；不挥发的| Not producing or in the form of vapor; not evaporating easily.  The term describes a substance that does not readily turn into a gas or vapor under normal conditions. | The nonvaporous nature of the oil made it suitable for use in high-temperature applications.  这种油的不挥发性使其适合在高温应用中使用。|近义词：nonvolatile, unevaporative |反义词：vaporous, volatile
