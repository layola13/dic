shalwars:/ʃælˈwɑːrz/| n.|沙尔瓦尔裤|Loose, baggy trousers, typically worn in Central Asia, South Asia, and the Middle East.  They are often gathered at the ankles and are typically worn by men.  They can be made from various fabrics and are often paired with a long shirt or tunic.|Example:  He wore traditional shalwars and a kurta to the wedding. 他穿着传统的沙尔瓦尔裤和库尔塔去参加婚礼。


|近义词：loose trousers, baggy pants, pajamas (in some contexts)|反义词：tight trousers, skinny jeans|名词|
