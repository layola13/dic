sincerer:/sɪnˈsɪərər/| adj.|真诚的；真挚的|Someone who is sincere is honest and genuine in their feelings and intentions.  They are truthful and straightforward in their dealings with others.  This word emphasizes the authenticity of one's emotions and actions.|例句：He offered a sincere apology for his mistake. 他为自己的错误真诚道歉。

例句：Her sincere concern for others was evident in her actions. 她对别人的真挚关怀在她的行动中显而易见。|近义词：genuine, heartfelt, honest, truthful|反义词：dishonest, insincere, deceitful, hypocritical|词性：形容词
