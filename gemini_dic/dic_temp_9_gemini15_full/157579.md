pitchiness:/ˈpɪtʃɪnəs/| n. |音调；音高；黑暗；沥青味|The quality of being high or low in pitch; the degree to which something is high or low in pitch.  It can also refer to the darkness or gloominess of something, or the smell of pitch (a black sticky substance).|例句：The pitchiness of his voice made it hard to understand him.  他的声音音调太高，很难听懂。|例句：The pitchiness of the night made it difficult to see. 夜晚的黑暗使得视线变得模糊。|近义词：darkness, gloom,  resonance (in the context of sound)|反义词：brightness, lightness, clarity
