outlaughing: /ˌaʊtˈlæfɪŋ/ | v. | 大笑，笑声盖过；笑得比…厉害 | To laugh louder or more heartily than (someone else).  |例句：The children outlaughed their parents at the clown's antics. 孩子们被小丑的滑稽动作逗得比父母笑得更厉害。  The comedian's jokes were so funny that the audience outlaughed each other.  喜剧演员的笑话太好笑了，以至于观众们互相争着大笑。|近义词：outdo, surpass, exceed | 反义词： sob, weep


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outlaughs everyone in the room. = 他比房间里每个人都笑得更大声。
They outlaugh their opponents after winning the game. = 赢得比赛后，他们比对手笑得更开心。


一般过去时:
She outlaughed her brother during the movie. = 看电影的时候，她比她哥哥笑得更厉害。
The audience outlaughed the performer during his act. = 表演期间，观众的笑声盖过了表演者。


现在完成时:
He has outlaughed every comedian he's ever seen. = 他比他看过的每一个喜剧演员都笑得更厉害。
They have outlaughed their worries with a hearty laugh. = 他们开怀大笑，把烦恼都抛诸脑后了。


过去完成时:
She had outlaughed him before he even told the joke. = 他还没讲笑话，她就先笑起来了，笑得比他还厉害。
They had outlaughed the previous record for audience laughter. = 他们打破了之前的观众笑声记录。


将来时:
The children will outlaugh their parents at the circus. = 孩子们在马戏团里会比他们的父母笑得更厉害。
I think she will outlaugh everyone at the party. = 我想她会在派对上笑得比任何人都厉害。


虚拟语气:
If she had told a funnier joke, she would have outlaughed him. = 如果她讲个更滑稽的笑话，她就会笑得比他更厉害。
I wish I could outlaugh my anxieties. = 我希望我能用笑声来战胜我的焦虑。


被动语态:
His laughter was outlaughed by the entire crowd. = 他的笑声被全场观众的笑声盖过了。 (Note: This usage is less common)
The comedian's jokes were outlaughed by the audience's uproarious laughter. = 喜剧演员的笑话被观众的哄堂大笑声盖过了。(Note: This usage is less common)


疑问句:
Did he outlaugh his friends at the party? = 他在派对上笑得比朋友们更厉害吗？
Will she outlaugh him when he tells his joke? = 当他讲笑话的时候，她会笑得比他更厉害吗？


否定句:
He didn't outlaugh her; she had the better joke. = 他没有笑得比她更厉害；她的笑话更好笑。
They couldn't outlaugh the booming laughter of the crowd. = 他们笑声盖不过人群中震耳欲聋的笑声。

outlaugh someone into silence = 笑得让某人哑口无言
outlaugh someone's attempts at humor = 笑倒某人试图搞笑的尝试
