pentamera:/pɛnˈtæmərə/ | n. |五聚体|A pentamer is a molecular complex consisting of five subunits.  These subunits can be identical or different, and the arrangement can vary depending on the specific molecule. Pentamers are found in various biological contexts, including proteins and nucleic acids.|Example: The influenza virus hemagglutinin protein is a trimer or a pentamer, depending on the viral strain.|近义词：oligomer (more general term)|反义词：monomer (single subunit)
