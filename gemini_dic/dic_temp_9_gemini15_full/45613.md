/ˈkʌriːz/ | n. |咖喱（复数）|Curries refers to multiple dishes containing curry powder as a key ingredient.  These dishes vary widely in taste and preparation depending on the region and specific recipe. | Examples:  I tried several curries at the Indian restaurant. 我在印度餐厅尝试了几种咖喱菜。 The chef specializes in South Indian curries.  这位厨师擅长制作南印度咖喱。|近义词：spiced dishes,  sauces|反义词：无
