handicrafts:/ˈhændɪkræfts/| n. |手工制品；手工艺品 |Items made by hand, especially those made using traditional methods.  Handicrafts often involve skilled craftsmanship and artistry.  They can range from simple items to complex and intricate works of art. |例句：She sells beautiful handcrafted jewelry. = 她卖漂亮的纯手工珠宝。

The museum showcased a stunning collection of Native American handicrafts. = 博物馆展出了令人惊叹的美国原住民手工艺品收藏。


近义词：handmade items, artisan crafts, handwork |反义词：mass-produced goods, factory-made items


词源:  The word "handicrafts" combines "hand" (referring to the use of hands in making something) and "craft" (meaning a skill or occupation requiring dexterity and skill).  The suffix "-s" indicates plurality.  The origin of "craft" can be traced back to Old English "cræft," meaning "strength, power, skill," ultimately related to the Proto-Germanic *kraftaz.
