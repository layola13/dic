hike:/haɪk/| n. v.|远足，徒步旅行；(价格，数量等)上涨|A hike is a long walk, usually in the countryside.  It can also refer to an increase in price or amount.|例句：We went on a hike in the mountains. 我们在山上远足。
The price of petrol has had a significant hike recently. 近期汽油价格大幅上涨。|近义词：walk, trek, climb, increase, raise|反义词：decrease, drop, fall


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hike every weekend. = 他们每个周末都去远足。
The cost of living hikes steadily. = 生活成本稳步上涨。

一般过去时:
We hiked to the summit yesterday. = 我们昨天徒步走到山顶。
The company hiked its prices last month. = 上个月公司提高了价格。

现在完成时:
They have hiked the Appalachian Trail. = 他们已经徒步穿越了阿巴拉契亚山脉。
Inflation has hiked the cost of goods. = 通货膨胀抬高了商品的价格。

过去完成时:
They had hiked for hours before they reached the lake. = 他们在到达湖边之前已经徒步走了几个小时。
The government had hiked taxes several times before the economy improved. = 在经济好转之前，政府已经几次提高了税收。

将来时:
We will hike to the waterfall tomorrow. = 我们明天将徒步去瀑布。
They will hike the interest rates next quarter. = 他们将在下个季度提高利率。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would hike to the top of the mountain. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会徒步走到山顶。
I wish the government hadn't hiked taxes so much. = 我真希望政府不要提高这么多税。

被动语态:
The trail is often hiked by many tourists. = 这条小路经常被许多游客徒步旅行。
The prices were hiked by the company without warning. = 公司未经警告就提高了价格。

疑问句:
Do you like to hike? = 你喜欢远足吗？
Will they hike the fees again? = 他们还会再次提高费用吗？

否定句:
We didn't hike yesterday because of the rain. = 因为下雨，我们昨天没有去远足。
The company did not hike the salaries this year. = 公司今年没有提高工资。


PHRASE->
go for a hike = 去远足
a long hike = 一次长途远足
hike up = 攀登；提高
hike through = 穿越…徒步旅行
day hike = 当日往返的徒步旅行
