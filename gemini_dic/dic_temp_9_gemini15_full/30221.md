cellars: /ˈsɛlərz/ | n. | 地窖；酒窖 | A cellar is an underground room, typically used for storage.  It is often found in houses or other buildings. |  例句：We keep our wine in the cellar. 我们把酒放在地窖里。

例句：The cellar was damp and dark. 地窖潮湿而黑暗。


近义词：basement, underground storage


反义词：attic, loft


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cellar stores many old bottles of wine. = 地窖储存着许多年份久远的葡萄酒。
He checks the cellar for leaks every month. = 他每个月检查一下地窖是否有漏水。


一般过去时:
They found some old documents in the cellar. = 他们在地窖里发现了一些旧文件。
The cellar flooded during the last storm. = 上次暴风雨期间，地窖被淹了。


现在完成时:
We have stored potatoes in the cellar all winter. = 我们整个冬天都把土豆储存在地窖里。
They have never cleaned the cellar before. = 他们以前从未打扫过地窖。


过去完成时:
The water had already reached the cellar before we could pump it out. = 我们还没来得及抽水，水就已经漫到地窖了。
They had used the cellar as a workshop for years. = 他们多年来一直把地窖用作工作室。


将来时:
We will renovate the cellar next year. = 我们明年将翻新地窖。
They will store their harvest in the cellar. = 他们将把收成储存在地窖里。


虚拟语气:
If we had a larger cellar, we could store more wine. = 如果我们有一个更大的地窖，我们就能储存更多的酒。
I wish the cellar was drier. = 我希望地窖更干燥一些。


被动语态:
