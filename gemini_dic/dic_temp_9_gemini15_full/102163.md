isthmus:/ˈɪzməs/ | n. |地峡 | A narrow strip of land connecting two larger land areas, usually with water on either side.  |Example: The Panama Canal cuts across the Isthmus of Panama. 巴拿马运河横穿巴拿马地峡。|近义词：neck, strait |反义词：continent, ocean

词源:
From Ancient Greek ἰσθμός (isthmós), meaning "neck," "narrow strip of land."  The word reflects the visual similarity of an isthmus to a narrow neck.
