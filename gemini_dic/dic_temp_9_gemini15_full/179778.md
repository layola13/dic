reevaluating:/ˌriːɪˈvæljuˌeɪtɪŋ/| v. |重新评估；重新评价 |To reconsider and assess (something) again.  It implies a more thorough or critical examination than a simple review. |例句：The company is reevaluating its marketing strategy after a disappointing sales quarter. 公司在一个令人失望的销售季度后正在重新评估其营销策略。

例句：We need to reevaluating our options in light of the new information.  鉴于新的信息，我们需要重新评估我们的选择。


近义词： reassessing, reconsidering, reappraising, reviewing

反义词： accepting (without further consideration), ignoring
