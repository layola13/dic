enamelist:/ɪˈnæməlɪst/ | n. |搪瓷工，珐琅工匠|A person who makes or works with enamel.  An enamelist is skilled in applying enamel to metal, glass, or other materials, creating decorative or functional objects. They are proficient in the techniques of enameling, such as cloisonné, champlevé, and basse-taille.|例句：The enamelist carefully applied the vibrant blue enamel to the silver brooch.  这位珐琅工匠小心地将鲜艳的蓝色珐琅涂在银质胸针上。|近义词：enameler, artisan (in enamel)|反义词：无
