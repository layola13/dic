poignantly:/ˈpɔɪnjəntli/| adv. | 令人心酸地；深深地；深刻地 | In a way that evokes a keen sense of sadness or regret; deeply moving.  |例句：He spoke poignantly of his lost childhood. 他深情地谈起了他失去的童年。

例句：The photograph poignantly captured the moment of their farewell. 这张照片深刻地捕捉到了他们告别的时刻。


近义词：movingly, touchingly, affectingly, deeply

反义词：unfeelingly, callously, indifferently


词性: 副词
