foraminifer:/ˌfɔːræmɪnɪfər/ | n. |有孔虫 |A single-celled protist that secretes a shell of calcium carbonate or other materials.  Foraminifera are found in diverse marine environments and their fossils are important in stratigraphy and paleoclimatology. |例句：The ocean floor is teeming with foraminifera, their tiny shells accumulating over millennia. 海洋底部充满了有孔虫，它们微小的外壳积累了数千年。

例句：Scientists study foraminifera fossils to reconstruct past climates.科学家研究有孔虫化石以重建过去的气候。

近义词：protozoan (a more general term)

反义词：None (no direct antonym)

词性：noun
