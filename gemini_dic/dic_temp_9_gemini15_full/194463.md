self-reputations:/ˌsɛlfˌrɛpjuˈteɪʃənz/| n. |自我评价；名誉；名声 |Self-reputations refer to the opinions individuals hold about themselves, often reflecting their self-worth and perceived social standing.  These internal assessments can significantly impact behavior and choices.  They are subjective and may not align with external perceptions. |例句：Her self-reputations as a diligent worker were well-deserved.  她作为勤奋工人的自我评价是当之无愧的。

例句：His inflated self-reputations led him to make poor decisions. 他过高的自我评价导致他做出错误的决定。


近义词：self-image, self-esteem, reputation (when referring to the external perception of one's character)


反义词：self-deprecation (downplaying one's accomplishments), low self-esteem


词性:名词
