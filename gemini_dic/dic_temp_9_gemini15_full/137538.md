It refers to the absence of acts of treachery or disloyalty;  it signifies instances of loyalty, faithfulness, and adherence to one's commitments.  Due to its length and complexity, it's rarely used in everyday conversation or writing. A simpler alternative would almost always be preferred. |  The sheer volume of nontraitorousnesses displayed by the soldiers during the siege was remarkable.
