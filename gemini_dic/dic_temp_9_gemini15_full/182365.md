rendezvous:/ˈrɑːndeɪvuː/ (美式)/ˈrɒ̃deɪvuː/ (英式)| n. v.|约会，会合；集合地点|A rendezvous is a meeting at an agreed time and place, typically between two people.  It can also refer to the place where this meeting occurs.  As a verb, it means to meet at an agreed time and place.|例句：They arranged a rendezvous at the park.  他们约定在公园见面。

They rendezvoused at the old oak tree. 他们在古老的橡树下会合。|近义词：meeting, appointment, assignation|反义词：separation, parting|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We rendezvous at the cafe every Friday. = 我们每周五都在咖啡馆会合。
They rendezvous before every mission. = 他们每次任务前都会会合。


一般过去时:
The spies rendezvoused in a dark alley. = 间谍们在一个黑暗的小巷里会合。
We rendezvoused at the agreed-upon time. = 我们在约定时间会合了。


现在完成时:
They have rendezvoused several times this week. = 他们本周已经会面几次了。
We have never rendezvoused in person before. = 我们以前从未见过面。


过去完成时:
They had rendezvoused before the attack began. = 袭击开始前他们已经会合了。
She had rendezvoused with her contact several times that month. = 那一个月她已经和她的联络人会面过几次了。


将来时:
We will rendezvous at the airport tomorrow. = 我们明天将在机场会合。
They will rendezvous at dawn. = 他们将在黎明时分会合。


虚拟语气:
If we had rendezvoused earlier, we could have avoided the traffic. = 如果我们早点会合，就能避免交通堵塞。
I wish we had rendezvoused at a more secure location. = 我希望我们在一个更安全的地方会合。


被动语态:
The meeting was poorly planned; the rendezvous was a failure. = 会议计划得很糟糕；会合以失败告终。
A secret rendezvous was arranged by the agents. = 特工们安排了一次秘密会合。


疑问句:
Where will we rendezvous? = 我们将在哪里会合？
When is the rendezvous scheduled for? = 会合计划在什么时候？


否定句:
They did not rendezvous at the specified location. = 他们没有在指定地点会合。
We couldn't rendezvous due to the storm. = 由于暴风雨，我们无法会合。
