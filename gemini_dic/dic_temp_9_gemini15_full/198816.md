sidebar:/ˈsaɪdbɑːr/ | n. |侧边栏，侧栏 | A sidebar is a secondary column of text or graphics on a web page, typically located to the side of the main content.  It often contains supplementary information, advertisements, or navigation links. | Example: The website's sidebar contained links to related articles and a search bar.  网站的侧边栏包含了相关文章的链接和一个搜索栏。 |近义词：side column, margin, panel |反义词：main content, central area
