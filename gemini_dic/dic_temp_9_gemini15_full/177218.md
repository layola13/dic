reascending: /ˌriːəˈsɛndɪŋ/ | v. |重新上升；再次上升 |  The act of ascending again; rising again after a descent or pause.  This word is a relatively rare and less common verb formed by combining "re-" (again) and "ascending" (rising). It describes a renewed upward movement.  |  The hot air balloon reascending after a brief dip. = 热气球在短暂下降后再次上升。 The stock market is reascending after a period of decline. = 股市在经历一段时间的下跌后正在重新上涨。 | 近义词：re-ascending, rising again, climbing again | 反义词：descending, falling, dropping | 动词
