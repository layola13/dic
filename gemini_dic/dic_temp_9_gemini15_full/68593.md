fantasias:/fænˈteɪziəz/| n.|幻想曲；幻想作品|A fantasia is a musical composition, typically for a solo instrument, that is characterized by its free and imaginative form, often incorporating improvisation and virtuosic display.  It's not bound by strict formal structures like sonatas or concertos. | 例句：The pianist played a beautiful fantasia on a Bach theme.  钢琴家演奏了一首以巴赫主题为基础的优美的幻想曲。

The orchestra premiered a new fantasia based on folk tunes. 乐团首演了一部以民歌为基础的新幻想曲。

|近义词：improvisation, fantasy, composition |反义词：sonata (in terms of strict form) |名词
