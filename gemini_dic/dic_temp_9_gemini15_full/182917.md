repetitiously:/rɪˈpɛtɪʃəsli/ | adv. |重复地，反复地 |  The word "repetitiously" describes the manner in which something is done repeatedly. It emphasizes the repetitive nature of the action and often suggests a monotonous or tedious quality.  |例句：He repetitiously tapped his fingers on the table, a sign of his growing impatience. 他反复地敲打着桌子，这是他越来越不耐烦的迹象。

The music was played repetitiously throughout the entire event.  整场活动都反复播放着同一首音乐。|近义词：repeatedly, over and over, again and again |反义词：uniquely, once, singularly |词性：副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and its usage is straightforward.  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed. There aren't specific tense variations for the adverb itself, as its meaning remains consistent.  The tense is determined by the verb it modifies.

词源:

The word "repetitiously" is derived from the word "repetition," which comes from the Latin word "repetitio," meaning "repetition."  The suffix "-ously" is an adverbial suffix, transforming the adjective "repetitive" into the adverb "repetitiously."
