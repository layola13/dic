philanthropist: /fɪˈlænθrəpɪst/ | n. |慈善家，乐善好施者 | A philanthropist is a person who donates time or money to improve the lives of others and/or support charitable causes.  They are often wealthy individuals but can also be people with modest means who dedicate themselves to helping others. | Example: Bill Gates is a well-known philanthropist, donating billions to various charitable organizations. 比尔·盖茨是一位著名的慈善家，向各种慈善机构捐赠了数十亿美元。  |近义词：benefactor, donor, humanitarian | 反义词：miser, curmudgeon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He donates a significant portion of his income to charity. 他把他收入的很大一部分捐给了慈善机构。
She volunteers at a local soup kitchen every week. 她每周都在当地的一家施粥所做志愿者。


一般过去时:
Andrew Carnegie established many libraries across the country. 安德鲁·卡内基在全国各地建立了许多图书馆。
The Rockefeller Foundation funded numerous scientific research projects.  洛克菲勒基金会资助了许多科学研究项目。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
