carcass:/ˈkɑːrkəs/|/ˈkɑːrkʌs/| n. |尸体；残骸；躯壳 |The dead body of an animal, especially one slaughtered for meat;  a dead body.  Often used to refer to the remains of something that has been destroyed or ruined. |例句：The carcass of the deer lay in the forest.  鹿的尸体躺在森林里。

例句：The wrecked car was just a twisted carcass of metal.  那辆报废的汽车只是一堆扭曲的金属残骸。

近义词：corpse, remains, wreck, hulk

反义词：living body, intact structure

词性：名词
