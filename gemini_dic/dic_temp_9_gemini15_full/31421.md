charioted:/ˈtʃæriətid/| v. |乘坐战车；被战车载着|  Past participle of "chariot".  Describes something or someone that has been transported or carried in a chariot.  It implies a sense of grandeur, speed, or perhaps even a triumphant journey.|例句：The victorious general was charioted through the streets.  凯旋的将军乘坐战车穿过街道。

The hero, charioted by his loyal steed, raced towards the finish line. 英雄由他忠实的坐骑拉着战车，飞奔向终点线。|近义词：carried, conveyed, transported|反义词：walked, went on foot|动词
