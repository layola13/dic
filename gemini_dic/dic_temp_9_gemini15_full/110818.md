linnet:/ˈlɪnɪt/| n. |红雀(一种小型鸣禽)|A small, seed-eating bird, typically with reddish-brown plumage.|例句：The linnet sang sweetly in the hedgerow.  红雀在树篱里婉转地歌唱。|例句：We saw a flock of linnets feeding on the seeds. 我们看到一群红雀在吃种子。|近义词：finch, redpoll|反义词：无
