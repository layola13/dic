inhumanity:/ˌɪn.hjuːˈmænɪti/| n. |不人道；残酷；残暴|The deliberate infliction of suffering on others.  Lack of compassion or humanity. |例句：The war crimes trial exposed the sheer inhumanity of the regime.  战争罪审判揭露了该政权的残酷无情。

The inhumanity of the prison conditions led to many deaths.  监狱环境的恶劣导致许多人死亡。|近义词：barbarity, cruelty, brutality, savagery|反义词：humanity, kindness, compassion, mercy|词性：名词
