carryon:/ˈkæriɒn/ /ˈkæriɑːn/| n. v.|继续；手提行李；随身行李 |To continue; a small bag used to carry personal belongings on an aircraft or other form of public transport. |例句：Please carry on with your work. 请继续你的工作。

例句：I've packed everything I need in my carry-on bag. 我已经把所有需要的东西都装进我的手提行李中了。


近义词：continue, proceed, luggage, handbag

反义词：stop, cease, halt


词性：Noun, Verb

词源:
The word "carryon" originates from the verb "carry," meaning to transport something from one place to another.  The "on" indicates continuation or progression. As a noun, "carry-on" is a relatively recent addition to the lexicon, specifically referencing the small bag carried onto planes.  Its development reflects the modern practice of air travel.
