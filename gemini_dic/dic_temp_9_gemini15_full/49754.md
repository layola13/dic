demolish: /dɪˈmɒlɪʃ/ | /dəˈmɑːlɪʃ/ | vt. |拆除；破坏；推翻；彻底击败 | to destroy a building or other structure completely:  to completely destroy something, especially a building | 近义词：destroy, raze, wreck, ruin | 反义词：construct, build, erect, establish


英文解析:
To demolish something means to destroy it completely, usually a building or structure. It can also refer to the complete destruction of an idea, argument, or reputation.


例句:
They're going to demolish that old factory. = 他们打算拆除那家旧工厂。

The hurricane demolished several houses. = 飓风摧毁了几座房屋。


词源:
From Old French *de- + mollir ("to soften").  The original meaning involved the weakening or breaking down of something, before evolving to the more complete destruction we understand today.
