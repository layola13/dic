semibouffant: /ˌsemiˈbuːfɑːnt/ | n. | 半蓬松发型 | A hairstyle that is partly bouffant, typically having some volume at the crown but not as much as a full bouffant.  It often involves teasing or backcombing the hair at the roots to create lift and volume. | Example: She wore her hair in a semibouffant, giving her a sophisticated yet playful look.  她梳了一个半蓬松发型，让她看起来既精致又俏皮。 | 近义词：partially bouffant, half-bouffant | 反义词：flat hairstyle, sleek hairstyle |  名词
