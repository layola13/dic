shoat:/ʃoʊt/| n.|小猪；乳猪|A young pig, especially one that is still suckling.  |例句：The farmer raised a litter of shoats.  这位农民养了一窝小猪。|例句：The butcher sold fresh shoats for the holidays.  屠夫在节日期间出售新鲜的乳猪。|近义词：piglet, young pig|反义词：sow (成年母猪), boar (成年公猪)
