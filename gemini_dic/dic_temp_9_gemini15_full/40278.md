continuations:/ˌkɒntɪnjuˈeɪʃənz/ /ˌkɑːntɪnjuˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 继续；延续；后续部分 |  Continuations refer to the act of continuing something, or the thing that continues something else.  This can refer to a process, a story, a series, or any other sequence.  It emphasizes the ongoing nature of something, extending beyond a previous point. |例句：The story's continuations will be published next month. 故事的续集将于下个月出版。

The negotiations are ongoing; we await further continuations. 谈判正在进行中；我们等待进一步的后续。


近义词：sequels, follow-ups, extensions, prolongations | 反义词：interruptions, conclusions, terminations | 词性: noun
