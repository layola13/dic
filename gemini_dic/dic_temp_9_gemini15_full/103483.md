jobster:/ˈdʒɒbstə(r)/| n. |求职者；工作狂|A jobster is a person who is actively seeking employment or who is intensely focused on their career.  The term often carries a slightly informal or even negative connotation, suggesting someone might be overly ambitious or obsessed with work.|Example: He's a real jobster, always networking and applying for new positions.|近义词：job hunter, careerist|反义词：slacker, idler|名词
