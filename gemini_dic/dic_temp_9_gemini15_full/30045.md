cbt:/ˌsiːbiːˈtiː/| n.|认知行为疗法|Cognitive Behavioral Therapy; a type of psychotherapy used to treat a wide range of mental health disorders.  It focuses on changing negative thinking patterns and behaviors.|例句：My therapist recommended CBT to help me manage my anxiety. 我的治疗师建议我进行认知行为疗法来帮助我控制焦虑。|近义词： psychotherapy, talk therapy |反义词:None.
