hitchhikers:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪkərz/| n.|搭便车的人；|People who travel by getting free rides in vehicles.|例句：The hitchhikers were picked up by a friendly truck driver. 搭便车的人被一位友好的卡车司机搭载了。|例句：Many hitchhikers rely on the kindness of strangers. 许多搭便车的人依靠陌生人的好心。|近义词：travelers, riders|反义词：drivers
