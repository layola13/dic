moderators: /ˈmɒdəreɪtərz/ | /ˈmɑːdəreɪtərz/ | n. |主持人；版主；调节器 |  A person who presides over a meeting, discussion, or online forum; a device that regulates or controls something. | 例句：The moderators ensured a civil discussion.  主持人确保了文明的讨论。


The moderators of the online forum quickly removed offensive posts.  该在线论坛的版主迅速删除了攻击性帖子。


近义词：chairperson, facilitator, umpire, regulator, controller | 反义词：disruptor, instigator


词源:  Derived from the verb "moderate," which comes from the Latin word "moderatus," the past participle of "moderari," meaning "to restrain," "to keep within bounds," or "to regulate."  The suffix "-or" indicates the agent or doer of the action.
