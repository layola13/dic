expatiate:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/| v.|详述，详细解释|To speak or write at length on a subject.|The professor expatiated on the intricacies of quantum physics. 教授详细解释了量子物理学的复杂性。|He expatiated at great length about his travels. 他冗长地详述了他的旅行经历。|elaborate, detail, enlarge upon|condense, summarize|动词

He expatiates on the benefits of exercise every chance he gets. 他抓住一切机会详述锻炼的好处。
She expatiates on her childhood memories frequently. 她经常详述她童年的回忆。
