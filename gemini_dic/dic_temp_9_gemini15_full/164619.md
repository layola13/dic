preexhibiting: /ˌpriːɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预展，预先展出 |  Showing or exhibiting something beforehand.  It implies a display or presentation before the main or official event. | The museum is preexhibiting some of its new acquisitions before the grand opening. = 博物馆在盛大开幕之前预展了一些新的藏品。

The gallery is preexhibiting selected works from the upcoming exhibition. = 画廊正在预展即将举行的展览中的精选作品。


近义词：previewing, showcasing beforehand, presenting in advance

反义词：postponing, delaying
