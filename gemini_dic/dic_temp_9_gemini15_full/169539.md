propenyl:/ˈproʊpɪnəl/|/ˈprɒpɪnɪl/| n. |丙烯基|A propenyl group is an unsaturated hydrocarbon group with the formula C₃H₅, derived from propene by removing one hydrogen atom.  It's an alkenyl group with a three-carbon chain containing a double bond.  There are two isomers: 1-propenyl and 2-propenyl (allyl).|The synthesis of propenyl groups is important in organic chemistry.|近义词：allyl (for 2-propenyl isomer)|反义词：无
