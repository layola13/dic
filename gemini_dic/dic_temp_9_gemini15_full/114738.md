malnourishment:/ˌmælˈnʌrɪʃmənt/| n. |营养不良 |A condition resulting from insufficient or unbalanced intake of nutrients.  It can lead to various health problems, including stunted growth, weakened immunity, and increased susceptibility to diseases. |Example:  Malnutrition is a serious problem in many developing countries.  营养不良是许多发展中国家面临的严重问题。  The child's malnourishment was evident in his thin frame and lack of energy.  孩子的营养不良从他瘦弱的身材和缺乏活力中显而易见。|近义词：undernutrition, nutrient deficiency |反义词：good nutrition, well-nourished |名词
