showeriness:/ˈʃaʊəriˌnɛs/ | n. |阵雨性，阵雨天气|The quality or state of being showery; characterized by frequent showers.  It refers to weather conditions with frequent, short bursts of rain. |例句：The showeriness of the afternoon made it difficult to plan an outdoor picnic.  下午阵雨不断，很难安排户外野餐。

例句：The forecast predicted showeriness for the rest of the week. 预报显示未来一周将有阵雨天气。


近义词：showers, intermittent rain,  sporadic rainfall

反义词：drought, dryness, continuous rain


词性：名词
