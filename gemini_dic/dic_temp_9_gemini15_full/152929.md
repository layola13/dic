pedestrians:/pəˈdɛstriənz/ | n. |行人 |People traveling on foot, especially in a city or town.  Pedestrians have the right of way at crosswalks. 行人在斑马线上享有通行权。|近义词：walkers, foot travelers |反义词：drivers, motorists


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Many pedestrians cross the street at the corner. = 许多行人在拐角处过马路。
Pedestrians often ignore traffic signals. = 行人经常忽视交通信号。
