precharts:/ˈpriːtʃɑːrts/| n. |预先图表，预先制图|Precharts are charts created before an event to predict or plan for outcomes.  They are often used in business or data analysis to visualize potential scenarios.|Example: The marketing team prepared precharts to illustrate projected sales figures for the new product launch.|近义词: preliminary charts, advance charts|反义词: final charts, post-event charts
