conceits:/kənˈsiːts/| n.|奇想；自负；夸张的修辞手法|An unusual or eccentric idea; an excessively high opinion of oneself; a literary device involving far-fetched or extravagant metaphors.|例句：His conceits about his own genius were legendary.  他的天才自负是出了名的。|例句：The poem is full of elaborate conceits, comparing love to a ship sailing on a stormy sea. 这首诗充满了精心设计的比喻，将爱情比作在暴风雨中航行的船只。|近义词：fancies, whims, affectations, pretenses|反义词：humility, modesty, simplicity
