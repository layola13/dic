furthered:/ˈfɜːðərd/ | v. | 促进；推进；增进 | To help something to develop or progress.  | The new policy furthered the cause of social justice.  这项新政策促进了社会正义的事业。 | advanced, promoted, aided | hindered, obstructed, impeded | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He furthered his career by taking on new challenges. = 他通过承担新的挑战来提升自己的事业。
The research furthered our understanding of the disease. = 这项研究增进了我们对这种疾病的了解。


一般过去时:
The discovery furthered scientific knowledge significantly. = 这一发现极大地促进了科学知识的发展。
The meeting furthered the negotiations between the two countries. = 会议促进了两国之间的谈判。


现在完成时:
His hard work has furthered his company's success. = 他努力的工作促进了公司成功。
Recent advancements have furthered our ability to treat the illness. = 最近的进步提高了我们治疗这种疾病的能力。


过去完成时:
They had furthered their research before publishing the findings. = 他们在发表研究结果之前已经进一步研究了。
The previous government had furthered economic growth considerably. = 前一届政府已经大大促进了经济增长。


将来时:
This project will further the development of renewable energy. = 该项目将促进可再生能源的发展。
The new technology will further the efficiency of the manufacturing process. = 新技术将提高制造工艺的效率。


虚拟语气:
If they had furthered their education, they might have had better job opportunities. = 如果他们继续深造，他们可能会有更好的工作机会。
I wish they had furthered the discussion before making a decision. = 我希望他们在做决定之前能进一步讨论。


被动语态:
The project was furthered by a generous grant. = 该项目得到了慷慨的资助而得以推进。
His reputation was furthered by his numerous awards. = 他因获得无数奖项而名声大噪。


疑问句:
How did the program further the students' learning? = 这个项目如何促进学生的学习？
What steps can be taken to further the cause of peace? = 可以采取哪些措施来促进和平事业？


否定句:
The lack of funding did not further the research project. = 资金不足并没有推进该研究项目。
His actions did not further the company's goals. = 他的行为并没有促进公司的目标。



PHRASE->
further the cause of = 促进…的事业
further one's education = 深造
further development = 进一步发展
further research = 深入研究
further discussion = 进一步讨论
