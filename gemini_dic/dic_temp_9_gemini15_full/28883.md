carpoolers:/ˈkɑːrpuːlər/| n. |拼车者，合乘者 |People who share a car for transportation, especially to and from work or school.  |例句：The carpoolers arrived on time for the meeting. 拼车的人准时到达了会议。|例句：Many carpoolers save money on gas and reduce traffic congestion. 许多拼车者节省了汽油钱，并减少了交通拥堵。|近义词：commuters (if referring to work travel), riders (in a broader sense) |反义词：solo drivers
