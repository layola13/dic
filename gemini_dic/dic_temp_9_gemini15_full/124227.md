mosquitoes:/məˈskiːtoʊz/| n. |蚊子（复数）|Mosquitoes are small insects that bite and suck blood. 蚊子是会叮咬和吸食血液的小昆虫。|例句：Many mosquitoes were buzzing around my head.  很多蚊子在我头上嗡嗡叫。

例句：We need to use mosquito nets to protect ourselves from mosquitoes. 我们需要使用蚊帐来保护自己免受蚊虫叮咬。


近义词：gnats (较小的飞虫)

反义词：无


词源:  The word "mosquito" comes from the Spanish word "mosca," meaning "fly," and the diminutive suffix "-ito," meaning "small."  Therefore, "mosquito" literally means "little fly."  The plural form adds the standard English plural "-es".
