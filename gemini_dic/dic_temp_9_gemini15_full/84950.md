haphazardness:/ˌhæpˈhæzədnəs/| n. |随意性；偶然性；杂乱无章|Lack of order or planning; randomness.  The haphazardness of the city's growth led to a chaotic infrastructure. 城市发展中的随意性导致了混乱的基础设施。| randomness, disorder, chaos, confusion | orderliness, organization, planning, system | 名词


例句：

The haphazardness of his approach to the project resulted in missed deadlines. 他做事随意，导致项目错过最后期限。

The haphazardness of the book's organization made it difficult to follow.  这本书组织混乱，难以理解。


近义词：

randomness, disorder, chaos, disarray, confusion, messiness

反义词：

orderliness, organization, system, planning, method, precision
