remake:/ˌriːˈmeɪk/| vt. n.|重拍；翻拍；重做；改造|To make something again, often in a new or improved form.  It can refer to films, products, or even processes. |例句：They're remaking the classic film "Casablanca". 他们正在重拍经典电影《卡萨布兰卡》。

例句：The company decided to remake its flagship product to improve sales. 公司决定重新设计其旗舰产品以提高销量。


近义词：redo, revise, refashion, reconstruct|反义词：destroy, demolish|动词
