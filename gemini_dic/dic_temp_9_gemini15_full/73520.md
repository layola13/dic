foreshores: /ˈfɔːrʃɔːrz/ | n. |前滨，前滩|The foreshores are the areas of land that are alternately covered and uncovered by the tide.  They are often characterized by mudflats, sandflats, and salt marshes.|例句：The foreshores were teeming with life after the tide receded. 退潮后，前滩上充满了生机。The development plans included significant restrictions on building near the foreshores. 开发计划包括对靠近前滩的建筑物施加重大限制。|近义词：shoreline, beach, coast|反义词：inland, offshore
