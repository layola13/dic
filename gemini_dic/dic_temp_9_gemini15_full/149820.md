paleolith:/ˈpæliəˌlɪθ/| n. |旧石器时代 |The Paleolithic, also called the Old Stone Age, is a long period in human history characterized by the use of knapped stone tools.  |The discovery of these tools provides valuable insights into Paleolithic life.|近义词：Old Stone Age|反义词：Neolithic (新石器时代)
