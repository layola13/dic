seceder:/sɪˈsiːdər/| n. |脱离者；退出者|A person who secedes, especially from a political state.|The seceder from the union faced harsh penalties.  脱离联盟者面临严厉惩罚。|近义词：defector, deserter |反义词：loyalist, conformist|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He secedes from the group. 他脱离了这个团体。
She secedes from the political party. 她退出该政党。


一般过去时:
The states seceded from the Union. 各州脱离了联邦。
He seceded from the organization last year. 他去年退出了该组织。


现在完成时:
They have seceded from the main body. 他们已经脱离了主要团体。
Several members have seceded from the club.  好几个成员已经退出了俱乐部。


过去完成时:
The colony had seceded before the war broke out.  战争爆发前，殖民地就已经脱离了。
He had seceded from the company long before the scandal.  丑闻发生很久以前，他就已经离开了公司。


将来时:
More members will secede if the conditions don't improve. 如果情况没有改善，将会有更多成员退出。
The smaller faction is likely to secede in the coming months.  小派系可能会在未来几个月内脱离。


虚拟语气:
If they seceded, they would face severe consequences. 如果他们脱离，将会面临严重的后果。
I wish they hadn't seceded so hastily. 我真希望他们不要这么匆忙地脱离。
