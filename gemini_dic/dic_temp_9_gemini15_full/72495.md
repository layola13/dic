fluorescents:/ˌflɔːrˈɛsnt/| n. |荧光灯；荧光物质|Substances or objects that emit light when exposed to radiation, typically ultraviolet light.  Fluorescent lights are a common example, converting electricity into light through the excitation of phosphor-coated tubes.|例句：The artist used fluorescent paints to create a vibrant effect.  艺术家使用荧光颜料来创造鲜艳的效果。

The lab was lit by bright fluorescent lights. 实验室由明亮的荧光灯照明。|近义词：phosphorescent, luminescent|反义词：opaque, non-luminous|名词
