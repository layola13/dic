dellacruscan:/ˌdeləˈkrʌskən/| adj.| 德拉克鲁斯卡的；矫揉造作的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Accademia della Crusca, a Florentine academy that promoted the purification of the Italian language; hence, often used to describe writing or style that is overly refined, artificial, or affected.  | The Della Cruscan style of poetry was criticized for its excessive use of ornate language and sentimental themes.  |  affected, artificial, precious | natural, simple, unaffected |  形容词
