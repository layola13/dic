complies:/kəmˈplaɪz/| v.|遵从；服从；符合|To act according to a rule, command, or wish; to be in accordance with.|The building complies with all safety regulations. 这座建筑符合所有安全条例。|近义词：obeys, conforms, adheres to|反义词：disobeys, violates, contravenes|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He complies with the rules. = 他遵守规则。
She complies with her doctor's orders. = 她遵从医生的嘱咐。


一般过去时:
He complied with the request. = 他照办了请求。
They complied with the law. = 他们遵守了法律。


现在完成时:
He has complied with all the requirements. = 他已经满足了所有要求。
She has complied with the instructions. = 她已经按照指示做了。


过去完成时:
He had complied with the regulations before the inspection. = 他在检查之前已经遵守了规章制度。
They had complied with the order before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经遵守了命令。


将来时:
He will comply with the new regulations. = 他将遵守新的规章制度。
She will comply with the court order. = 她将遵守法院的命令。


虚拟语气:
If he complied, he would avoid punishment. = 如果他服从，他就能避免惩罚。
I wish he would comply with my request. = 我希望他能答应我的请求。


被动语态:
The request was complied with immediately. = 请求立即得到了满足。
The rules must be complied with. = 必须遵守规则。


疑问句:
Does he comply with the rules? = 他遵守规则吗？
Will she comply with the demands? = 她会答应要求吗？


否定句:
He does not comply with the rules. = 他不遵守规则。
She did not comply with the order. = 她没有遵守命令。


PHRASE->
comply with = 遵守；服从
comply fully = 完全遵守
comply in good faith = 善意遵守
comply strictly = 严格遵守
fail to comply = 未能遵守


词源:
From Old French *complir, from Latin complēre "to fill up, complete," from com- "together" + plēre "to fill."  The sense shifted from "filling" to "fulfilling" (a requirement, etc.), leading to the modern meaning of obedience and conformity.
