prepositionless:/ˌpreːpəˈzɪʃənles/| adj.| 无介词的 | Lacking prepositions; not containing or using prepositions.  This word describes phrases, sentences, or styles of writing that omit prepositions.  It's a relatively uncommon word used primarily in linguistic analysis. |例句：The telegram contained a prepositionless phrase, making it quite terse.  电报中包含一个无介词的短语，使其非常简洁。


例句：His minimalist style often resulted in prepositionless sentences, which some found jarring. 他的极简风格经常导致无介词的句子，有些人觉得刺耳。
