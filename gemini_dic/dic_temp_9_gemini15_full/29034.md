cartoonishness: /ˌkɑːrˈtuːnɪʃnəs/ | n. |卡通风格，夸张性，滑稽性 | The quality of being like a cartoon;  exaggerated or unrealistic representation.  It often implies a lack of seriousness or realism.  |例句：The cartoonishness of the villain's portrayal made him less frightening.  卡通反派人物的夸张表现使其不那么可怕。

The cartoonishness of the political satire was effective in highlighting the absurdity of the situation.  政治讽刺漫画的夸张风格有效地突显了局势的荒谬性。


近义词：exaggeration, absurdity, silliness, caricature, buffoonery |反义词：realism, seriousness, sobriety, naturalism

词性: 名词
