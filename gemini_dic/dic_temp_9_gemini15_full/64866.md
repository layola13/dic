espresso:/ɛˈsprɛsoʊ/ | n. |浓缩咖啡|A strong black coffee brewed by forcing hot water under pressure through finely-ground coffee beans.  Espresso is the base for many other coffee drinks. |例句：I ordered a double espresso to wake me up. 我点了一杯双份浓缩咖啡来提神。|例句：The barista expertly pulled a perfect espresso.  咖啡师熟练地制作出一杯完美的浓缩咖啡。|近义词：coffee, shot |反义词：Americano (in terms of strength and concentration, Americano is weaker and less concentrated)|名词
