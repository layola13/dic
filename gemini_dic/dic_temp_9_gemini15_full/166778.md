presolicited:/ˌpriːˈsɒlɪsɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先征求意见的；预先请求的 |  Previously solicited; obtained or sought before the usual or expected time.  This word is relatively rare and often appears in specialized contexts, particularly legal or business settings, implying a proactive approach to obtaining something. | Example: The presolicited bids ensured a smooth and timely auction process. 预先征求的投标确保了拍卖过程顺利且及时。  The presolicited feedback was overwhelmingly positive. 预先征求的反馈非常积极。 | 近义词：pre-emptive, anticipatory, advance | 反义词：unsolicited, unexpected
