lifts:/lɪfts/| n. |电梯；举起；情绪高涨|A lift is a device for moving people or goods vertically between floors of a building.  It can also refer to the act of raising something or someone.  Figuratively, it can mean a sudden improvement in mood or spirits.|例句：We took the lift to the tenth floor. 我们乘电梯到了十楼。

例句：He helped her lift the heavy box. 他帮她抬起了那个沉重的箱子。

近义词：elevator, hoist, raise, boost | 反义词：lower, drop, depress | 词性：n. v.
