interparticle: /ˌɪntərˈpɑːrtɪkəl/ | n. |颗粒间，粒子间|  Relating to or occurring between particles.  The term often appears in the context of interactions, forces, or spaces between individual particles within a system. For example, in materials science,  "interparticle forces" refer to the forces acting between individual particles within a material. |Example: The interparticle forces significantly influence the material's rheological properties. 颗粒间的力显著影响材料的流变特性。|近义词: intergranular (when referring to grains) |反义词: intraparticle (within a particle) |名词
