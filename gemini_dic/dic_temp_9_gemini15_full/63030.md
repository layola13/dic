enrage:/ɪnˈreɪdʒ/| vt.|激怒，使愤怒|To make someone extremely angry.|The driver's reckless actions enraged the passengers. 司机的鲁莽行为激怒了乘客。|近义词：infuriate, anger, provoke, irritate|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His behavior enrages me. = 他的行为让我很生气。
The noise enrages the neighbors. = 噪音激怒了邻居们。

一般过去时:
The news enraged her. = 这个消息让她很生气。
His rudeness enraged his boss. = 他的粗鲁激怒了他的老板。

现在完成时:
This situation has enraged me for days. = 这种情况已经让我生气好几天了。
His constant complaints have enraged everyone in the office. = 他不断的抱怨激怒了办公室里的每个人。

过去完成时:
I had been enraged by his lies before he apologized. = 他道歉之前，我已经被他那些谎话激怒了。
She had been enraged by his behavior for weeks before she finally confronted him. = 在她最终与他对质之前，她已经被他的行为激怒了好几个星期了。

将来时:
His actions will enrage many people. = 他的行为将会激怒许多人。
This decision will enrage the public. = 这个决定将会激怒公众。

虚拟语气:
If he knew the truth, it would enrage him. = 如果他知道真相，这会让他很生气。
I wish that wouldn't enrage my father. = 我希望那不会激怒我的父亲。

被动语态:
He was enraged by the injustice. = 他被这种不公正激怒了。
The audience was enraged by the poor performance. = 观众被糟糕的演出激怒了。

疑问句:
Does this situation enrage you? = 这种情况让你生气吗？
What enrages you the most? = 什么事最让你生气？

否定句:
The news didn't enrage him. = 这个消息并没有让他生气。
This problem doesn't enrage me anymore. = 这个问题不再让我生气了。


PHRASE->
enrage someone to the point of...= 激怒某人到...的地步
be enraged by sth = 因某事而愤怒
enraged at/by = 对…感到愤怒
enraged outburst = 愤怒的爆发
feel enraged = 感到愤怒
