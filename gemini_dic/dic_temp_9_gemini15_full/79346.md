gleen:/ɡliːn/| vt.|收集，拾取；(尤指少量地)获得， glean information from a book 从书中收集信息 |To gather (something, especially information or material) gradually or bit by bit.| He gleaned information from various sources. 他从各种来源收集信息。|近义词：gather, collect, acquire|反义词：scatter, disperse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They glean wheat in the fields. = 他们在田里收割麦子。
She gleans information from newspapers. = 她从报纸上收集信息。


一般过去时:
The farmers gleaned the remaining grain. = 农民们收集了剩下的谷物。
He gleaned useful data from the experiment. = 他从实验中收集到了有用的数据。


现在完成时:
We have gleaned much valuable knowledge from this project. = 我们从这个项目中获得了许多宝贵的知识。
She has gleaned a great deal of information from her research. = 她从她的研究中收集到了大量的信息。


过去完成时:
They had gleaned all the information they needed before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经收集了所有需要的信息。
The researchers had gleaned some interesting results from their study. = 研究人员们从他们的研究中收集到了一些有趣的结果。


将来时:
We will glean the results of the survey next week. = 我们下周将收集调查结果。
The scientists will glean important data from the satellite images. = 科学家们将从卫星图像中收集重要数据。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we could glean more information. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们就能收集更多信息。
I wish I had gleaned more details from the report. = 我希望我从报告中收集到更多细节。


被动语态:
The information was gleaned from several sources. = 信息来自多个来源。
The results will be gleaned from the experiment later. = 结果稍后将从实验中获得。


疑问句:
What did you glean from the meeting? = 你从会议上获得了什么？
Can we glean any useful data from this experiment? = 我们能从这个实验中收集到任何有用的数据吗？


否定句:
They didn't glean much from the discussion. = 他们从讨论中没有获得多少东西。
He couldn't glean any meaning from the cryptic message. = 他无法从神秘的信息中解读出任何含义。
