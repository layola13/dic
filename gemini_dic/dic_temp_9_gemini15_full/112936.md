lugged:/lʌɡd/ | vt. |用力拖；费力地搬运 | to carry or pull something heavy with difficulty |例句：He lugged the heavy suitcase up the stairs. 他费力地把沉重的行李箱搬上楼梯。

例句：She lugged the groceries home from the supermarket. 她费力地把杂货从超市搬回家。


近义词：dragged, hauled, pulled, heaved | 反义词：pushed (轻推), carried (轻松地搬运)


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lugs the heavy box every day. 他每天都搬运那个沉重的箱子。
She lugs her backpack to school. 她把背包背去学校。


一般过去时:
He lugged the heavy box to the car. 他费力地把沉重的箱子搬到汽车上。
She lugged her suitcase through the airport. 她拖着行李箱穿过机场。


现在完成时:
He has lugged all the furniture upstairs. 他已经把所有的家具搬到楼上了。
She has lugged the bags all the way here. 她把包一路拖到这里。


过去完成时:
He had lugged the piano up three flights of stairs before he realized he needed help. 他在意识到需要帮助之前已经把钢琴搬上了三层楼梯。
She had lugged the heavy shopping bags for miles. 她已经拖着沉重的购物袋走了几英里。


将来时:
He will lug the equipment to the site tomorrow. 他明天将把设备搬运到现场。
She will lug her books to school tomorrow. 她明天将把书搬到学校。


虚拟语气:
If he had a cart, he wouldn't have to lug all those boxes. 如果他有一辆手推车，他就不用费力地搬运所有那些箱子了。
If she had asked for help, she wouldn't have had to lug the sofa herself. 如果她寻求帮助，她就不用自己费力地搬沙发了。


被动语态:
The heavy box was lugged upstairs by him. 沉重的箱子被他搬上了楼。
The groceries were lugged home by her. 杂货被她搬回家了。


疑问句:
Did he lug the piano all by himself? 他一个人搬钢琴了吗？
Will she lug all those bags on her own? 她会独自搬运所有那些袋子吗？


否定句:
He didn't lug the boxes himself; he asked for help. 他没有自己搬箱子；他寻求了帮助。
She didn't lug the heavy bags; she got a trolley. 她没有搬运沉重的袋子；她拿了一个手推车。



PHRASE->
lug around: 随身携带 (Lugging around this heavy backpack is tiring.)
lug something out:费力地拿出（He lugged a heavy book out from under the pile.）
lug something in:费力地搬进（They lugged the refrigerator in through the back door.）
lug something off:费力地搬走（She lugged the old sofa off to the curb.）
lug something up:费力地搬上去（He lugged the suitcase up the stairs.）
