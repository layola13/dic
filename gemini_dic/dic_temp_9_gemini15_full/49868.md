demotivated:/ˌdiːˈmoʊtɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. |丧失动力的；消极的；缺乏积极性的 |Lacking motivation or enthusiasm; discouraged.  |例句：After the setback, he felt demotivated and unsure of his abilities.  |近义词：discouraged, disheartened, disillusioned, apathetic |反义词：motivated, enthusiastic, inspired


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feels demotivated by his current job. = 他对目前的工作感到没有动力。
She is demotivated by the lack of progress. = 她因为缺乏进展而感到沮丧。

一般过去时:
The team was demotivated after losing the game. = 球队在输掉比赛后士气低落。
He felt demotivated after receiving negative feedback. = 他在收到负面反馈后感到灰心。

现在完成时:
I have been demotivated since the project failed. = 自从项目失败以来，我一直都缺乏动力。
She has felt demotivated for months. = 她已经感到沮丧好几个月了。

过去完成时:
They had been demotivated for weeks before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，他们已经消沉了好几个星期。
He had already felt demotivated before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始，他就已经感到灰心了。

将来时:
If the situation doesn't improve, he will be demotivated. = 如果情况没有改善，他会失去动力。
She will likely be demotivated if she doesn't get a promotion. = 如果她没有得到晋升，她很可能会感到沮丧。

虚拟语气:
I would be demotivated if I were in his shoes. = 如果我是他，我会感到沮丧。
If she had received more support, she would not have been so demotivated. = 如果她得到更多支持，她就不会这么沮丧了。

被动语态:
The employees were demotivated by the company's poor management. = 员工们因为公司糟糕的管理而士气低落。
The students felt demotivated by the difficult exam. = 学生们因为考试太难而感到灰心。

疑问句:
Are you demotivated by your work? = 你对你的工作感到沮丧吗？
Is he demotivated because of the criticism? = 他是因为批评而感到沮丧吗？

否定句:
He is not demotivated by challenges. = 他不会因为挑战而失去动力。
The employees were not demotivated by the delay. = 员工们并没有因为延误而感到沮丧。
