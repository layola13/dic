recrimination:/ˌrɛkrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |反责；互相指责；反控|The act of accusing someone in return; a counteraccusation.  It often implies mutual blame and a heated exchange. |例句：The meeting descended into a bitter exchange of recriminations.  会议演变成一场激烈的互相指责。|例句：Their marriage ended in a torrent of recriminations.他们的婚姻以一连串的互相指责告终。|近义词：counteraccusation, accusation, reproach, denunciation|反义词：praise, commendation, exoneration
