repatterns:/ˌriːˈpætərnz/| v.| 重塑模式，重新塑造，改换模式 | To establish a new pattern or system; to change the existing pattern or form of something.  It implies a more thorough and fundamental change than simply adjusting or modifying. | 例句：The company repatterned its marketing strategy after a series of unsuccessful campaigns. 公司在连续几次营销活动失败后重新调整了其营销策略。

例句：The therapist helped the patient repattern their negative thought patterns. 治疗师帮助病人重塑其消极的思维模式。


近义词：reshape, restructure, reform, reorganize, reconfigure

反义词：maintain, preserve, retain


词性:动词
