femininity:/ˌfɛmɪˈnɪnɪti/ | n. | 女性气质；女人味 | The quality of being feminine; the traits associated with women.  It encompasses a range of characteristics, often including gentleness, nurturing behavior, emotional expressiveness, and an emphasis on beauty and grace. However, the concept of femininity is complex and varies across cultures and time periods.  | Example:  Her femininity was undeniable, evident in her graceful movements and soft voice.  她的女性气质是不可否认的，体现在她优雅的举止和温柔的声音中。

Example: The film explores different facets of femininity in modern society.  这部电影探讨了现代社会女性气质的不同方面。
