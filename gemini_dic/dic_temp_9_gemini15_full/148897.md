overwhelms:/ˌoʊvərˈwɛlmz/ | vt. |压倒；淹没；使不知所措 | To defeat completely; to overcome by superior force; to submerge; to overpower.  | 例句：The amount of work overwhelmed her.  工作量压倒了她。

The floodwaters overwhelmed the village. 洪水淹没了村庄。


近义词：submerge, inundate, swamp, defeat, crush

反义词：support, assist, encourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The workload overwhelms me. 工作量让我难以承受。
The beauty of the scenery overwhelms the tourists.  风景之美让游客们惊叹不已。

一般过去时:
The news overwhelmed him. 这个消息让他不知所措。
The sudden storm overwhelmed the small boat. 突如其来的暴风雨让小船难以承受。

现在完成时:
The intensity of the emotions has overwhelmed her. 强烈的感情已经让她难以自控。
The problems have overwhelmed the company's resources. 问题已经耗尽了公司的资源。

过去完成时:
The sheer volume of data had overwhelmed the system. 巨大的数据量已经压垮了系统。
She had been overwhelmed by grief after losing her parents.  失去父母后，她一直沉浸在悲痛之中。

将来时:
The project will overwhelm us if we don't get more help. 如果我们得不到更多帮助，这个项目将会压垮我们。
The demand for tickets will overwhelm the box office.  门票的需求将会让售票处不堪重负。

虚拟语气:
If the task overwhelmed me, I would ask for help. 如果任务让我难以承受，我会寻求帮助。
I wish the noise wouldn't overwhelm me.  我希望噪音不会让我难以忍受。

被动语态:
She was overwhelmed by the kindness of strangers. 她被陌生人的善意所感动。
The city was overwhelmed by the flood. 城市被洪水淹没了。

疑问句:
Does the task overwhelm you? 这项任务让你难以承受吗？
Is she overwhelmed by the pressure? 她被压力压垮了吗？

否定句:
The challenge did not overwhelm him.  这个挑战并没有让他难以承受。
He wasn't overwhelmed by the news.  他并没有被这个消息压垮。


PHRASE->
be overwhelmed by/with: 被…压倒；被…淹没
overwhelmed with emotion: 情绪激动
overwhelmed by grief: 悲痛欲绝
overwhelmed with work: 工作繁重
feel overwhelmed: 感到难以承受
