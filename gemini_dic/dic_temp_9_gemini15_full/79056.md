givenness:/ˈɡɪvənəs/ | n. |既成事实；既定性；给予；赠予|The state or fact of being given; something that is given.  It often refers to something that is assumed or presupposed, rather than explicitly stated.  It can also refer to the act of giving itself.|例句：The givenness of the situation made it difficult to find a solution.  (情势的既定性使得难以找到解决方法。) The givenness of the data influenced the results of the experiment. (数据的既定性影响了实验结果。) |近义词：fact, reality, assumption, premise |反义词：uncertainty, unknown, possibility, contingency |名词
