groves:/ɡroʊvz/ | n. |树丛；小树林 | A grove is a small group of trees growing closely together.  |例句：We walked through a shady grove of oak trees. 我们穿过一片阴凉的橡树林。 |例句：The lemon grove was filled with the scent of blossoms. 柠檬树林里充满了花香。|近义词：woodland, copse, thicket |反义词：forest (in terms of size)
