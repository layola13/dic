grievable:/ˈɡriːvəbəl/| adj.| 可悲的，令人悲伤的 | Capable of causing grief; deserving of grief or sorrow.  Something grievable is something that causes or deserves sadness and sorrow.  | Example: The loss of a loved one is a grievable event.  失去亲人是件令人悲伤的事。  The injustice was a grievable situation for the victims.  对受害者来说，这种不公正是一种令人悲伤的境况。 |近义词：mournful, lamentable, regrettable, sorrowful | 反义词：joyful, delightful, joyous
