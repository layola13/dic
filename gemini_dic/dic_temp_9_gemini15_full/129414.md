nominated:/ˈnɑːmɪneɪtɪd/ | v. | 提名，推荐 | To officially suggest someone for a position or award.  |  She was nominated for an Oscar. 她被提名奥斯卡奖。  He was nominated to the committee. 他被提名为委员会成员。 | appointed, proposed |  rejected, overlooked | 动词
