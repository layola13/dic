coadventure:/koʊˈædventʃər/| n. |共同冒险；合作探险|A co-adventure is a joint adventure, an undertaking shared by two or more people.  It emphasizes the collaborative and shared nature of the experience, often implying a sense of risk, excitement, or exploration.|例句：Their coadventure to climb Mount Everest was fraught with peril but ultimately successful.  他们的珠穆朗玛峰联合攀登冒险充满危险，但最终成功了。|近义词：joint venture, collaboration, partnership|反义词：solo adventure, individual undertaking|名词
