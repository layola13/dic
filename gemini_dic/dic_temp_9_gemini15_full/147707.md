overpreoccupy: /ˌoʊvərˈpriːɒkjʊpaɪ/ | v. |过度担忧，过分牵挂 | To worry or think about something too much; to be excessively preoccupied. |例句：He overpreoccupies himself with minor details, neglecting the bigger picture. 他过度担忧细枝末节，忽略了大局。| 近义词：obsess, dwell on, fret over | 反义词：neglect, disregard
