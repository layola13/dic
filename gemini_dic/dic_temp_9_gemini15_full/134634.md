nonmajors:/ˈnɑːnˌmeɪdʒərz/| n.|非专业人士，非主修学生|Students who are not majoring in a particular subject.  This term is often used in the context of college or university courses designed for students outside a specific academic field.|例句：The introductory biology course is designed for nonmajors. 这门生物入门课程是为非专业人士设计的。|例句：This physics lecture is open to nonmajors as well. 这场物理讲座也对非专业人士开放。|近义词：undergraduates (in a broader context), general students|反义词：majors
