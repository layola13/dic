repracticing: /ˌriːˈpræktɪsɪŋ/ | v. |反复练习，再练习 |The act of practicing something again, often to improve skill or mastery.  It implies a previous practice session and a renewed effort to refine the skill. |例句：She spent the weekend repracticing her piano sonata. = 她周末花时间反复练习她的钢琴奏鸣曲。

例句：He's repracticing his lines for the play. = 他正在反复练习他话剧的台词。


近义词：rehearsing, reviewing, drilling, refining


反义词： neglecting, abandoning


词性：动词
