laughter: /ˈlɑːftər/ /ˈlæf.tɚ/ | n. | 笑声，笑 | The sound of laughing. It can be a spontaneous expression of joy, amusement, or even nervousness.  Laughter is contagious and can be a powerful social bonding tool. | The children's laughter filled the room. 孩子们的笑声充满了房间。
Their laughter was infectious. 他们的笑声很有感染力。 | mirth, giggling, chuckling | silence, crying
