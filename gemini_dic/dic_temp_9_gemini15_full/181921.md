remands:/rɪˈmændz/ | vt. | 遣送， remanded | To send (a person) back into custody to await trial or further inquiry.  It typically refers to a judicial order sending someone back to jail or prison while awaiting trial, further legal proceedings, or sentencing. |

例句：

The judge remanded the defendant to custody until the next hearing. | 法官将被告还押候审，直到下次开庭。

The suspect was remanded in custody pending further investigation. | 嫌疑人被还押候审，等待进一步调查。


近义词：refer, commit, send back

反义词：release, discharge, free


词性：动词
