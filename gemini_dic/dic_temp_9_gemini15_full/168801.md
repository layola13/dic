produces:/prəˈduːsəs/ | vt. |生产；制造；创作；引起；导致 | to make or manufacture from components or raw materials; to bring forth or create; to cause or bring about a result |例句：The factory produces cars.  这家工厂生产汽车。

The author produces a new novel every year.  这位作者每年创作一部新小说。


近义词：creates, manufactures, generates, yields, brings forth | 反义词：consumes, destroys, abolishes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory produces high-quality products. 这家工厂生产高质量的产品。
She produces beautiful artwork. 她创作美丽的艺术品。

一般过去时:
The company produced a record profit last year.  该公司去年创下了创纪录的利润。
The play produced a strong emotional response from the audience. 这部戏剧引起了观众强烈的感情共鸣。

现在完成时:
They have produced several successful films.  他们已经制作了几部成功的电影。
The drought has produced a severe water shortage. 干旱导致了严重的水资源短缺。


过去完成时:
Before the presentation, she had produced all the necessary materials.  在演示文稿之前，她已经准备好了所有必要的材料。
By the end of the year, they had produced over 10,000 units. 到年底，他们已经生产了超过10000个单位。


将来时:
We will produce a detailed report next week. 我们下周将提交一份详细的报告。
The new technology will produce significant improvements in efficiency. 新技术将显著提高效率。


虚拟语气:
If the company produced more innovative products, it would be more successful. 如果该公司生产更多创新产品，它将会更成功。
I wish I had produced a better presentation. 我希望我当时能做一个更好的演示。


被动语态:
The goods are produced in China.  这些商品是在中国生产的。
A new report is being produced to address the issue. 正在编写一份新的报告来解决这个问题。


疑问句:
What does this machine produce? 这台机器生产什么？
Does the company produce environmentally friendly products? 这家公司生产环保产品吗？


否定句:
The factory doesn't produce that model anymore.  这家工厂不再生产那种型号了。
The writer didn't produce a single novel last year.  这位作家去年一部小说也没写。
