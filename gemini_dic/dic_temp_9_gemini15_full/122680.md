It's often used in programming and number theory. | 例句：17 modulo 5 is 2 (because 17 divided by 5 is 3 with a remainder of 2).  17 模 5 等于 2（因为 17 除以 5 商 3 余 2）。|近义词：remainder, residue |反义词：quotient, divisor | preposition, adverb
