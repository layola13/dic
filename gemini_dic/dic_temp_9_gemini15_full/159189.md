podophyllum:/ˌpɒdəˈfɪləm/|/ˌpɑːdəˈfɪləm/| n. |鬼臼属植物，鬼臼|Podophyllum is a genus of flowering plants in the family Berberidaceae, commonly known as mayapples.  The genus includes several species, some of which are used medicinally.  The rhizomes of certain species contain podophyllotoxin, a cytotoxic compound. |Example:  The extract from the podophyllum plant is used in some cancer treatments. 鬼臼植物的提取物用于某些癌症治疗。|近义词:mayapple (指特定物种)|反义词: N/A
