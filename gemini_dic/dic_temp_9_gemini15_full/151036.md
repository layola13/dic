parapet:/ˈpærəpɛt/| n.|矮墙，护墙，栏杆 |A low wall or railing at the edge of a roof, bridge, or balcony, designed to prevent falls.  |例句：The parapet around the roof offered a little protection from the wind. 屋顶周围的矮墙提供了一点防风的保护。|例句：We leaned against the parapet, enjoying the view. 我们靠着矮墙，欣赏着景色。|近义词：wall, railing, balustrade |反义词：opening, gap
