crabbiest:/ˈkræbiɪst/| adj.|最脾气坏的，最乖戾的，最暴躁的 |Most irritable; ill-tempered; grumpy.  The word describes someone who is in a persistently bad mood and is easily annoyed.|例句：He was in the crabbiest mood I'd ever seen him in. 他是我见过的脾气最坏的时候。  My grandpa is usually crabby in the mornings. 我爷爷通常早上脾气很坏。|近义词：grumpy, surly, irritable, crotchety, cantankerous |反义词：cheerful, pleasant, amiable, good-natured
