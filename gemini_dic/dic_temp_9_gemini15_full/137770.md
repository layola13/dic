nonvascular: /ˌnɒnˈvæskjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 无维管束的；非维管的 | Lacking vessels, especially xylem and phloem, for the conduction of water and nutrients.  This term is primarily used in botany to describe plants that don't have a specialized system for transporting water and nutrients.  | Example: Mosses are nonvascular plants.  苔藓是无维管束植物。|近义词：avascular |反义词：vascular

Nonvascular plants, such as liverworts, rely on diffusion for nutrient transport. =  像地钱这样的无维管束植物依靠扩散来运输养分。
