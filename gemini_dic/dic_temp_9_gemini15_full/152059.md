patenter:/ˈpætntər/| n. |专利权人，专利申请人| A person who holds a patent or has applied for one.  They own the exclusive rights to an invention for a set period. |  He is the patenter of a revolutionary new medical device. 他是这项革命性新型医疗设备的专利权人。| inventor, licensee | infringer | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The patenter owns the rights to the invention. = 专利权人拥有这项发明的权利。
He is the patenter of several successful products. = 他是几款成功产品的专利权人。


一般过去时:
The patenter successfully defended his patent. = 专利权人成功地捍卫了他的专利。
She was the patenter of a groundbreaking technology. = 她是一项突破性技术的专利权人。


现在完成时:
The patenter has already filed multiple patent applications. = 专利权人已经提交了多份专利申请。
He has been the patenter of this technology for five years. = 他已经拥有这项技术的专利权五年了。


过去完成时:
The patenter had already secured the patent before the competitor filed their application. = 在竞争对手提交申请之前，专利权人已经获得了专利。
By the time the lawsuit started, she had been the patenter for over a decade. = 在诉讼开始时，她已经拥有专利权超过十年了。


将来时:
The patenter will likely license the technology to other companies. = 专利权人可能会将这项技术授权给其他公司。
She will be the patenter of a new type of engine next year. = 明年她将成为一种新型发动机的专利权人。


虚拟语气:
If he were the patenter, he would have more control over the product's development. = 如果他是专利权人，他将对产品的开发有更多控制权。
I wish I were the patenter of that amazing invention. = 我真希望我是那项惊人发明的专利权人。


被动语态:
The patent was held by a large corporation. = 该专利由一家大公司持有。(注意：这里是被动语态，但主语不是patenter)
The rights were assigned to a new patenter. = 权利被分配给了一个新的专利权人。


疑问句:
Who is the patenter of this technology? = 这项技术的专利权人是谁？
Is he still the patenter of that device? = 他还是那个设备的专利权人吗？


否定句:
He is not the patenter of that software. = 他不是那个软件的专利权人。
She was not the original patenter of the design. = 她不是该设计的最初专利权人。
