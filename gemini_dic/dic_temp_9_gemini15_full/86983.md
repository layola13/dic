henhouse:/ˈhenhaʊs/| n. |鸡舍|A small structure or building where chickens are kept.  It typically provides shelter and protection from predators and the elements.  |例句：The farmer repaired the henhouse roof after the storm damaged it.  农夫在暴风雨损坏鸡舍屋顶后修理了它。|例句：The chickens were safely tucked away in the henhouse for the night. 鸡在夜间安全地藏在鸡舍里。|近义词：chicken coop, poultry house|反义词：无
