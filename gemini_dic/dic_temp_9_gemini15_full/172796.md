quadrilateral:/ˌkwɑːdrɪˈlætərəl/| n. |四边形 |A quadrilateral is a polygon with four sides and four angles.  It is a closed, two-dimensional figure.  Different types of quadrilaterals include squares, rectangles, parallelograms, rhombuses, trapezoids, and kites. |Example: A square is a type of quadrilateral.|近义词：four-sided figure|反义词：triangle, pentagon
