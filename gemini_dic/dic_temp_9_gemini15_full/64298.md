equivalve:/ˈiːkwɪvælv/| adj.|等瓣的；双壳类等瓣的|Having two valves or shells of approximately equal size and shape.  Equivalve shells are common in many bivalve mollusks. |例句：Many clams are equivalve, meaning their two shells are nearly identical. 许多蛤蜊是等瓣的，这意味着它们的两个壳几乎完全相同。
例句：The fossil shows an equivalve shell, indicating a symmetrical bivalve. 化石显示了一个等瓣壳，表明这是一个对称的双壳类动物。|近义词：symmetrical (in the context of shells)|反义词：inequivalve|词性：adj.
