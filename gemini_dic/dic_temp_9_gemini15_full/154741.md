persuader:/pərˈsweɪdər/| n. |劝说者，说服者 | A person who persuades others.  Someone skilled in influencing or convincing others to adopt a particular belief, opinion, or course of action. | Example: He's a skilled persuader, able to convince almost anyone. 他是一位熟练的说服者，几乎可以劝服任何人。|近义词：influencer, convincer, advocate |反义词：opponent, detractor
