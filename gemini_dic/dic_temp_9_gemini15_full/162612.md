preannouncing: /ˌpriːəˈnaʊnsɪŋ/ | v. | 预先宣布，提前公布 |  The act of announcing something before it officially happens.  It implies giving notice or information in advance. | 例句：The company is preannouncing its new product line next week. = 公司下周将预先公布其新的产品线。


The company preannounced its earnings, exceeding analysts' expectations. = 公司提前公布了盈利，超过了分析师的预期。


近义词：forewarning, advance notice, preliminary announcement | 反义词：postponing, delaying
