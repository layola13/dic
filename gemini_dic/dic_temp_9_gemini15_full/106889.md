labyrinthodonta:/ˌlæbɪrɪnˈθɒntə/| n. |迷齿类动物|Extinct group of amphibians characterized by a complex infolding of the tooth enamel, giving the teeth a labyrinthine structure.  They were large, mostly aquatic, and lived from the Carboniferous to the Triassic periods.|Example:  The discovery of a new labyrinthodont fossil shed light on the evolution of early amphibians. 迷齿类动物新化石的发现，为早期两栖动物的进化提供了新的线索。|近义词: temnospondyls (a suborder of labyrinthodonts)|反义词: N/A
