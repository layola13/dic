laborer:/ˈleɪbərər/ | n. |劳动者，工人 | A laborer is a person who does manual labor, especially unskilled work.  |例句：He worked as a laborer on a construction site. 他在建筑工地做了一个劳动者。 |例句：Many laborers were needed to build the pyramids.  许多劳动者被需要来建造金字塔。|近义词：worker, manual worker, toiler |反义词：employer, manager, executive
