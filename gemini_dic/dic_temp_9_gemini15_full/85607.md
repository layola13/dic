haugh:/hɔː/| n.|傲慢；自大；高傲|Haughty implies a disdainful pride, often accompanied by a condescending attitude towards those considered inferior.  It suggests a feeling of superiority and a lack of respect for others.|She treated the servants with a haughty disdain.|近义词：arrogant, supercilious, disdainful, imperious|反义词：humble, modest, meek
