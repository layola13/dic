glaciation:/ˌɡleɪʃiˈeɪʃən/| n. |冰川作用；冰川期 | The process by which glaciers form and move, causing significant changes to the landscape.  Glaciation includes the erosion, transportation, and deposition of rock and sediment by glaciers. | Example: The landscape of the Alps bears clear evidence of past glaciation.  阿尔卑斯山的地貌清楚地表明了以往的冰川作用。

The extensive glaciation of the northern hemisphere during the last ice age significantly altered coastlines.  上一个冰河时期北半球大范围的冰川作用显著地改变了海岸线。


近义词：ice age, glacial period | 反义词：deglaciation
