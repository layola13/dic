pail:/peɪl/| n.|桶，提桶|A pail is a bucket, typically made of metal or plastic, used for carrying liquids or other materials.|例句：She carried a pail of water to the plants. 她提着一桶水去浇花。

例句：He used a pail to collect rainwater. 他用桶收集雨水。


近义词：bucket, tub|反义词：无


词源：源自古英语单词 *pægl,  意为"桶"，与古诺尔斯语单词 *paggr 有关，也指"桶"。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pail holds five gallons of water. = 这个桶能装五加仑水。
He uses a pail to wash his car. = 他用桶洗车。


一般过去时:
She emptied the pail of garbage. = 她倒掉了桶里的垃圾。
They carried the pail of sand to the beach. = 他们把装满沙子的桶搬到海滩。


现在完成时:
I have filled the pail with soil. = 我已经用泥土装满了桶。
She has used that pail for years. = 她已经用了那个桶好多年了。


过去完成时:
He had already emptied the pail before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经把桶倒空了。
They had filled the pail with water before starting the experiment. = 他们在开始实验之前已经把桶装满了水。


将来时:
I will use a pail to collect the leaves. = 我将用桶收集落叶。
She will fill the pail with fresh milk. = 她将用鲜奶装满桶。


虚拟语气:
If I had a pail, I would collect the rainwater. = 如果我有一个桶，我会收集雨水。
I wish I had a bigger pail. = 我真希望我有一个更大的桶。


被动语态:
The pail was filled with water. = 桶被装满了水。
The pail is being cleaned. = 桶正在被清洗。


疑问句:
Is this pail big enough? = 这个桶够大吗？
Where is the pail? = 桶在哪里？


否定句:
The pail isn't full. = 桶没有装满。
He didn't use a pail. = 他没有用桶。
