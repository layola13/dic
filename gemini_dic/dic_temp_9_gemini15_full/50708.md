dermoptera:/dɜːrˈmɒptərə/| n. |皮翼目|Dermoptera is an order of mammals, sometimes called colugos or flying lemurs.  They are arboreal mammals characterized by a patagium, a membrane of skin stretching between their limbs and body, enabling them to glide between trees.  They are not true lemurs, nor do they truly fly.| Example:  The Sunda colugo is a fascinating example of a dermopteran mammal.  苏门答腊鼯猴是皮翼目哺乳动物的一个迷人例子。|近义词：colugo, flying lemur (these are common names, not synonyms in a strict biological sense)|反义词：无|
