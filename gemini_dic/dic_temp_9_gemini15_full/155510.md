philanthropies: /fɪˈlænθrəpiz/ | n. |慈善事业（复数）；慈善机构 |The generous activities or donations of money or goods to help people in need.  Philanthropies can encompass a wide range of activities, from funding research to providing direct aid to vulnerable populations. |例句：The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation is one of the world's largest philanthropies. 比尔及梅琳达·盖茨基金会是世界上最大的慈善机构之一。

The Rockefeller philanthropies have significantly impacted global health initiatives.洛克菲勒家族的慈善事业极大地影响了全球健康计划。|近义词：charities, benefactions, donations |反义词：selfishness, greed
