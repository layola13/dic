nonefficacy: /ˌnɒnˈɛfɪkəsi/ | n. |无效；无效性；缺乏功效|The lack of efficacy; ineffectiveness.  It refers to the failure of a treatment, medication, or intervention to produce the desired or expected effect.  This term emphasizes the absence of positive results.|

例句：

The study demonstrated the nonefficacy of the new drug in treating the disease.  = 这项研究表明这种新药治疗该疾病无效。

The nonefficacy of the policy led to its eventual repeal. = 这项政策的无效导致其最终被废除。


近义词：ineffectiveness, ineffectiveness, futility, impotence


反义词：efficacy, effectiveness


词性：名词
