quintet:/kwɪnˈtɛt/| n.|五重奏；五重奏乐队；五人一组|A quintet is a group of five people or things, often specifically a musical ensemble of five instrumentalists or vocalists.  It can also refer to a composition written for five instruments or voices.|例句：The string quintet played a beautiful piece of chamber music.  弦乐五重奏演奏了一首优美的室内乐。

The jazz quintet included a saxophone, trumpet, piano, bass, and drums.  爵士五重奏包括萨克斯管、小号、钢琴、贝斯和鼓。|近义词：group of five, ensemble, team|反义词：solo, duet, trio, quartet, sextet

词源:
The word "quintet" comes from the Late Latin word "quintetus," which is derived from the Latin word "quinque," meaning "five."  The suffix "-et" is a diminutive suffix, suggesting a group of five.
