returfing:/rɪˈtɜːrfɪŋ/ | n. | 重新铺设草皮；更换草皮 | The act of replacing old or damaged turf with new turf.  This often involves removing the existing turf, preparing the ground, and then laying down new turf. | The golf course is undergoing returfing this spring.  今年春天高尔夫球场正在进行重新铺设草皮的工作。 | resodding, relaying turf |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：leaving the turf as it is) | 名词
