cable:/ˈkeɪbl/| n. v.|缆线，电缆；电报；发送电报|A cable is a thick, strong rope, usually made of metal wires twisted together. It can be used for various purposes, such as supporting bridges, anchoring ships, or transmitting electricity or data.  The word can also refer to a telegram sent by cable.|例句：The submarine cable was damaged during the storm. 海底电缆在暴风雨中受损了。

The engineer checked the power cable before starting the machine.工程师在启动机器之前检查了电源线。|近义词：cord, wire, rope|反义词：wireless
