pole-dancing:/ˈpoʊlˌdænsɪŋ/| n. |钢管舞|A form of dance performed around a vertical pole, often incorporating acrobatic elements and sensual movements.  It can be performed as a form of fitness, artistic expression, or entertainment. |例句：She's been taking pole-dancing classes for the past year and has become quite skilled. 她过去一年一直在上钢管舞课，现在已经很熟练了。|例句：Many people view pole dancing as a challenging and rewarding form of exercise. 许多人认为钢管舞是一种具有挑战性和回报的锻炼方式。|近义词：pole fitness, acrobatic dance |反义词：None specifically, but might be contrasted with less physically demanding dance forms.
