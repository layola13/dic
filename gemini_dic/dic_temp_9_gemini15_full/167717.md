priesting:/ˈpriːstɪŋ/| n. |祭司职务；行使祭司职务|The act of performing the duties of a priest; the state of being a priest.  It can also refer to the act of imposing priestly authority or influence. |例句：The young man was ordained and began his priesting.  这个年轻人被任命为神父，并开始了他的祭司生涯。  The priesting of the temple was interrupted by the earthquake. 寺庙的祭祀活动被地震打断了。|近义词：ministry, officiation, service|反义词：layperson, secularization
