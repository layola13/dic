rhino:/ˈraɪnoʊ/ | n. |犀牛 |A large, thick-skinned, plant-eating mammal with one or two horns on its snout.  Rhinos are known for their powerful build and are found in Africa and Asia. |  The rhino charged at the jeep. 这只犀牛向吉普车冲去。  The black rhino is critically endangered. 黑犀牛濒临灭绝。 |  近义词: pachyderm | 反义词:  (无明确反义词)
