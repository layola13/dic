molestations:/ˌmɒlɛˈsteɪʃənz/| n. |性骚扰；猥亵；侵犯|The act of molesting, especially the sexual abuse of a child or other vulnerable person.  It often involves unwanted sexual advances, physical contact, or any behavior that violates the victim's personal boundaries in a sexual manner. |例句：The allegations of molestations against the teacher led to a police investigation.  老师性骚扰的指控导致了警方的调查。

The child suffered years of molestations at the hands of a family member.  这个孩子多年来遭受着家庭成员的性侵犯。


近义词：sexual abuse, assault, harassment, abuse, violation|反义词：protection, safety, care
