inkle:/ˈɪŋklɪŋ/| n. |隐约的预感，暗示，迹象 |a slight indication or suggestion of something; a vague feeling or notion|  She had an inkling that something was wrong. 她隐约觉得有什么不对劲。|近义词：hint, suspicion, suggestion |反义词：certainty, assurance


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I have an inkling of what he means. = 我隐约知道他的意思。
She gets an inkling of the truth. = 她对真相有了一点感觉。

一般过去时:
I had an inkling that it would rain. = 我预感到要下雨。
He gave me an inkling of his plan. = 他给了我一点关于他计划的暗示。

现在完成时:
I have had an inkling that something is amiss for days. = 我几天来一直隐隐约约觉得有什么不对劲。
She has had an inkling of the danger. = 她对危险有了些许预感。


过去完成时:
I had had an inkling that the project would fail. = 我之前就隐隐约约觉得这个项目会失败。
They had had an inkling of the upcoming storm. = 他们之前就对即将到来的风暴有所预感。


将来时:
I will have an inkling of the results soon. = 我很快就会对结果有所了解。
She will have an inkling of what happened. = 她将会对发生的事情有所了解。


虚拟语气:
If I had an inkling, I would have acted differently. = 如果我当时有所预感，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had had an inkling of the trouble ahead. = 我真希望我之前对将要面临的麻烦有所预感。


被动语态:
An inkling of the truth was given to her. = 真相的一丝线索被告诉了她。
Little inkling of the conspiracy was given to the public. = 公众几乎没有得到任何关于阴谋的暗示。


疑问句:
Do you have any inkling of the answer? = 你对答案有任何想法吗？
Has anyone any inkling of the perpetrator? = 有人对罪犯有任何线索吗？


否定句:
I have no inkling of what's going on. = 我完全不知道发生了什么。
He gave me no inkling of his intentions. = 他没有向我透露他的意图。


PHRASE->
have an inkling of = 对...略知一二
give an inkling of = 给出...的暗示
a slight inkling = 一点点预感
the first inkling = 最初的迹象
not the slightest inkling = 丝毫没有预感
