hatting:/ˈhætɪŋ/| v.|制帽；戴帽；(俚语)殴打|The action of making hats or the process of putting on a hat.  In slang, it can refer to hitting someone.|例句：He's good at hatting. 他擅长制帽。

He was hatting around in the cold weather. 他在寒冷的天气里戴着帽子四处走动。


近义词：capmaking, millinery (for making hats) , donning (for putting on a hat) ,  bashing (slang for hitting)

反义词：removing (for taking off a hat)
