romeos: /ˈroʊmiˌoʊz/ | n. | 罗密欧们（复数） |  The plural form of Romeo, referring to multiple men named Romeo or possessing qualities associated with Romeo, the character from Shakespeare's play.  This often implies romantic, passionate, or perhaps somewhat reckless or impulsive men. | Example:  The party was filled with romeos vying for the attention of the beautiful Juliet.  派对上挤满了争夺美丽朱丽叶注意力的罗密欧们。 |近义词: lovers, suitors, romantics | 反义词:  None directly, but could be contrasted with terms like "cynics," "misogynists," or "cowards". | 名词
