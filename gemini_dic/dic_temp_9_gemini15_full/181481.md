rejudged:/ˌriːˈdʒʌdʒd/ | v. | 再审理；重新评判 | To judge again, often with a different outcome.  This implies a previous judgment that is being reconsidered. |例句：The court rejudged the case after new evidence emerged. = 新证据出现后，法院重新审理了此案。
The painting was rejudged as more valuable after its restoration. = 这幅画修复后被重新评估为更有价值。|近义词：reassess, reconsider, re-evaluate |反义词：uphold (a previous judgment)
