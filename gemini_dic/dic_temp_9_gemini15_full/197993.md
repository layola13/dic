shoals:/ʃoʊlz/ | n. |浅滩；鱼群；大量；众多 |A shoal is a shallow area in the sea.  It can also refer to a large number of fish swimming together, or a large group or number of things. |例句：The ship ran aground on a shoal. 这艘船在浅滩搁浅了。|例句：A shoal of herring filled the net. 一大群鲱鱼填满了渔网。|近义词：shallow, reef, multitude, throng |反义词：deep, abyss
