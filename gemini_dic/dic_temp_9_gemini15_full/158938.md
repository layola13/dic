pluralizes: /ˈplʊərəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使复数化；变为复数 | To make something plural.  This typically refers to the grammatical process of changing a singular noun or pronoun into its plural form.  It can also refer to the act of making multiple copies of something. |

例句：

The program automatically pluralizes nouns in the text. = 该程序自动将文本中的名词复数化。

The printer pluralizes the image to create multiple copies. = 打印机将图像复数化以创建多个副本。


近义词：pluralize (同义词，更常用)

反义词：singularize (使单数化)

词性:动词
