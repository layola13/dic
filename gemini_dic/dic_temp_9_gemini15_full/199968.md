skanking:/ˈskæŋkɪŋ/| v.| 俚语：跳舞；(尤指)斯卡舞 |  To dance, especially in a style associated with ska music.  The term often implies a particular energetic, jerky, and sometimes aggressive style of movement. |例句：He was skanking away to the upbeat rhythm. 他随着欢快的节奏跳斯卡舞。

例句：The whole crowd was skanking furiously at the concert.  整个观众都在音乐会上疯狂地跳斯卡舞。


近义词：dancing, grooving, bouncing,  moving (to the music)

反义词：standing still, remaining immobile


词性: 动词
