sinnet:/ˈsɪnɪt/| n.|绳索；细绳；编织物|A sinnet is a type of cordage made by plaiting together strands of fiber, such as hemp, flax, or coconut husk.  It's often used in nautical contexts and for making various crafts.|The sailor used sinnet to secure the sail.|cordage, plaiting, twine| (no direct antonym, but could be considered opposite to loose, unbraided fibers)|名词
