USAGE->
一般现在时:
The careless driver fractures many cars every year. = 粗心的司机每年都会撞坏很多车。
Fracturers often cause significant damage to property. = 破坏者常常对财产造成重大损害。

The earthquake fractured the ancient building. = 地震使古老的建筑物破裂了。
