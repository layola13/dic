decomposing: /ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊzɪŋ/ | adj. | 腐烂的；分解的 |  Describing something in the process of breaking down into simpler substances, usually due to the action of bacteria or other microorganisms.  It often implies a process of decay or deterioration. |


例句：

The decomposing leaves released nutrients into the soil.  腐烂的树叶释放养分到土壤中。

The smell of decomposing garbage was unbearable.  腐烂垃圾的气味令人难以忍受。


近义词：rotting, decaying, putrefying


反义词：growing, forming, regenerating


词性:形容词
