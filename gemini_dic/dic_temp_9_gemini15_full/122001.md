misspending:/ˌmɪsˈspɛndɪŋ/ | n. | 滥用；挥霍；浪费 | The misuse of funds; the act of spending money unwisely or wastefully.  |例句：The misspending of public funds led to a scandal.  公共资金的滥用导致了一场丑闻。|例句：His misspending on luxury goods left him deeply in debt. 他在奢侈品上的挥霍让他负债累累。|近义词：waste, extravagance, profligacy, squandering |反义词：thrift, frugality, economy |名词
