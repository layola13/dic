Enunciators are crucial for effective communication, especially in situations requiring precise articulation. | Example: The news enunciators spoke clearly and with great precision. 新闻播音员清晰而精确地说话。 The professor was a skilled enunciator, making complex ideas accessible to students.  这位教授是一位熟练的发音者，使复杂的想法更容易被学生理解。 |近义词：speakers, articulators, announcers | 反义词：mumblers, whisperers | 名词
