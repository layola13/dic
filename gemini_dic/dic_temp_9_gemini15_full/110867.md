lionet:/ˈlaɪənɛt/| n. |小狮子|A young lion.  A lionet is a cub of a lion, typically less than a year old, characterized by its tawny coat and playful behavior.  It is dependent on its mother for protection and nourishment during its early life.  |例句：The lioness protectively watched over her lionet. 母狮保护性地守护着她的幼狮。

例句：The lionet playfully pounced on its mother's tail. 小狮子顽皮地扑向它母亲的尾巴。

近义词：cub (general term for a young animal)
反义词：None (no direct opposite)

词性:名词
