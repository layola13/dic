reassumed:/ˌriːəˈsjuːmd/| v.|重新承担；再次担任；重新开始|To assume again; to take up or begin again.|例句：After a brief hiatus, he reassumed his duties as CEO.  他短暂休整后，重新担任了首席执行官的职务。

例句：The company reassumed its expansion plans after the market stabilized.  市场稳定后，公司重新启动了扩张计划。

近义词：resume, retake, reinstate|反义词：abandon, relinquish, forsake

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reassumes his position every morning. = 他每天早上都重新担任他的职位。
She reassumes her role as project manager. = 她重新担任项目经理的职务。

一般过去时:
He reassumed his duties after his vacation. = 他休假后重新开始工作。
She reassumed control of the situation. = 她重新掌控了局面。

现在完成时:
He has reassumed his responsibilities. = 他已经重新承担了他的责任。
She has reassumed her leadership position. = 她已经重新担任领导职务。

过去完成时:
He had reassumed his post before the crisis deepened. = 在危机加剧之前，他已经重新上任了。
She had reassumed her studies after a long break. = 她在长时间休息后重新开始学习。

将来时:
He will reassume his role next week. = 他下周将重新担任他的角色。
She will reassume her duties after the investigation. = 调查结束后，她将重新履行职责。

否定句:
