replenisher:/rɪˈplɛnɪʃər/ | n. |补充者；补充物 |A person or thing that replenishes something.  Someone or something that restores something to a former state or level. |例句：The heavy rainfall acted as a natural replenisher for the depleted reservoir. 大雨充当了枯竭水库的天然补充物。

例句：The company invested in a new replenisher to keep its inventory levels high. 公司投资了一个新的补充系统以保持其库存水平。


近义词：refiller, restorer, supplier, replenishment system

反义词：depleter, consumer, exhaustor

词性：名词


USAGE->

The machine replenishes the water supply automatically. = 这台机器自动补充供水。
She replenishes her stock of stationery regularly. = 她定期补充文具库存。
