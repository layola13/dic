nonrecyclable: /ˌnɒnˌriˈsaɪkləbl/ | adj. | 不可回收的 |  Unable to be recycled.  This adjective describes materials or items that cannot be processed and reused through recycling programs.  | Example:  The plastic bag is nonrecyclable in our city.  这个塑料袋在我们城市是不可回收的。  The packaging was made from nonrecyclable materials. 包装是用不可回收的材料制成的。| 近义词:unrecyclable, unreclaimable | 反义词: recyclable
