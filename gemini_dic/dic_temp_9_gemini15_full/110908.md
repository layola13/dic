lipid:/ˈlɪpɪd/| n.|脂质，类脂|A lipid is any of a class of organic compounds that are fatty acids or their derivatives and are insoluble in water but soluble in nonpolar solvents. They include fats, waxes, oils, hormones, and certain components of membranes.|例句：Lipids are essential components of cell membranes. 脂质是细胞膜的重要组成部分。|例句：The study focused on the role of lipids in heart disease. 这项研究的重点是脂质在心脏病中的作用。|近义词：fat, oil, wax|反义词：N/A
