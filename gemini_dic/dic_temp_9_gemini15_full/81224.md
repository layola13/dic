grandparental:/ˌɡrændˈpɛərənṭəl/| adj.|祖父母的；外祖父母的| Relating to grandparents or grandparenting.  The adjective describes something connected to or characteristic of grandparents. |例句：Grandparental visits are always cherished by the children.  祖父母的探访总是受到孩子们珍惜。

例句：He received a grandparental legacy of antique furniture. 他继承了一套祖传的古董家具。


近义词：grandparent-related, ancestral (in some contexts)


反义词：parental (opposite generation)


词性：形容词
