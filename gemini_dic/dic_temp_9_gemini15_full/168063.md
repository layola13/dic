privatized:/ˈpraɪvətaɪzd/| adj. v.|私有化的；使私有化|Transferred to private ownership or control.  The process of transferring ownership of a business, industry, or service from a government to a private company or individual.|例句：Many state-owned industries have been privatized in recent years. 近年来，许多国有企业已被私有化。 The government plans to privatize the railway system. 政府计划将铁路系统私有化。|近义词：privatised, commercialized|反义词：nationalized, socialized


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government privatizes state-owned enterprises. = 政府将国有企业私有化。
They privatize the water supply system. = 他们将供水系统私有化。

一般过去时:
The company privatized its operations last year. = 该公司去年将其业务私有化。

现在完成时:
They have privatized several key industries. = 他们已经将几个关键产业私有化。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
