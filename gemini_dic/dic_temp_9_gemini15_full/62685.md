engenderers:/ɪnˈdʒɛndərz/| n.| 产生者；引起者 | Those who bring something into existence or cause something to happen.  | The report highlighted the key engenderers of social inequality. 这份报告强调了社会不平等的主要制造者。|近义词：creators, instigators, generators, authors |反义词：destroyers, terminators, annihilators |名词

一般现在时:
The artists engender new ideas through their creative process. = 艺术家们通过他们的创作过程产生新的想法。
These policies engender economic growth in developing countries. = 这些政策促进了发展中国家的经济增长。

虚拟语气:
