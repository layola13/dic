reseason:/ˌriːˈziːzn/| vt.|再次调味；重新给…调味|To add seasoning to something again; to season something a second time.|例句：I need to reseason the steak. 我需要再给牛排调味。|例句：She reseasoned the soup after tasting it. 她尝过汤之后又重新调味了。|近义词：season again, re-spice|反义词：unseason
