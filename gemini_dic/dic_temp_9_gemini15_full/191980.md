scoreboards:/ˈskɔːrˌbɔːrdz/ | n. |记分牌；记分板 |A scoreboard is a board or display that shows the score in a game or competition.  It can be electronic or manual, and is used to keep track of points, goals, or other metrics. |Example: The scoreboard showed the home team was winning by ten points. 记分牌显示主队领先十分。

The scoreboard at the stadium was huge. 体育场的记分牌很大。


近义词：score displays, score indicators, score sheets

反义词：None (no direct antonym)

词性：名词
