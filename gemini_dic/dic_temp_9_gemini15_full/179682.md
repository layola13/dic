reenlightenment:/ˌriːɪnˈlaɪtnmənt/ | n. | 再启蒙；新启蒙运动 | A renewed emphasis on reason and individualism, often seen as a continuation or revival of Enlightenment ideals in a new context.  It often focuses on addressing issues left unresolved or exacerbated by the original Enlightenment. | Example: The rise of the internet has spurred a reenlightenment of democratic ideals, allowing for a wider spread of information and debate.  再启蒙运动的兴起促进了民主理想的传播，使信息和辩论得到更广泛的传播。| 近义词：neo-Enlightenment, revival of Enlightenment ideals | 反义词：obscurantism, dark ages
