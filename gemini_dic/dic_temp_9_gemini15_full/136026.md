nonrealizable: /ˌnɒnˈriːəlaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 不可实现的，无法实现的 |  Impossible to achieve or realize; impractical.  The proposed plan, due to its high cost and complex logistics, was deemed nonrealizable.  | 近义词：unattainable, impracticable, infeasible, impossible | 反义词：realizable, achievable, feasible


例句：

The company's ambitious goal of conquering the global market within a year proved nonrealizable. 公司雄心勃勃的目标——在一年内征服全球市场——证明是无法实现的。


The architect's design, while aesthetically pleasing, was unfortunately nonrealizable due to building codes.  建筑师的设计虽然美观，但不幸的是由于建筑规范而无法实现。


词源：

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of"
* **realizable**:  derived from "realize," meaning "to achieve" or "to bring into existence."  "-able" is a suffix that forms adjectives indicating capability or possibility.

因此，"nonrealizable"字面意思是"不能实现的"。
