internationalized:/ˌɪntərˌnæʃənəlaɪzd/| adj. |国际化的；具有国际特色的|  Made suitable for or adapted to international use; designed to be used across multiple countries and cultures.  This includes considerations for language, currency, date/time formats, and cultural norms.|例句：The company's website is internationalized to cater to a global audience.  这家公司的网站经过国际化处理，以满足全球用户的需求。 The software has been internationalized, supporting multiple languages and currencies. 该软件已经过国际化处理，支持多种语言和货币。|近义词：globalized, worldwide, multinational|反义词：localized, domestic, national|
