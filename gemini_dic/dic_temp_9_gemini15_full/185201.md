retrace:/rɪˈtreɪs/ | vt. |追溯；重走；重温 |To go back over again, often following the same route.  It can refer to physical journeys or to events in the past. |例句：He retraced his steps to find his lost wallet. 他重走了一遍路，寻找他丢失的钱包。

例句：She retraced the events leading up to the accident. 她追溯了导致事故发生的一系列事件。


近义词：recapitulate, review, follow, re-examine | 反义词：advance, proceed, forge ahead | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retraces his steps every morning. = 他每天早上都重走一遍路。
She retraces the route on the map. = 她在地图上重走路线。


一般过去时:
I retraced my journey to find where I’d left my phone. = 我重走了一遍路，寻找我丢在哪里的手机。
The detective retraced the killer's steps. = 侦探重走了杀手的路线。


现在完成时:
He has retraced his family history back to the 17th century. = 他已经追溯了他的家族历史到17世纪。
She has retraced her childhood memories in her new novel. = 她在新小说中重温了她童年的回忆。


过去完成时:
After the storm, they retraced the path they had taken to the mountain. = 暴风雨过后，他们重走了他们去山的路。
Having lost his way, he retraced the steps he had taken earlier. = 迷路后，他重走了他之前走过的路。


将来时:
I will retrace my steps to the cafe. = 我将重回咖啡馆。
We will retrace the journey we took last year. = 我们将重走我们去年走过的路。


虚拟语气:
If I had a map, I would retrace my route more easily. = 如果我有一张地图，我就能更容易地重走路线。
If she had paid more attention, she could have retraced the events more accurately. = 如果她当时更注意一些，她就能更准确地追溯这些事件了。


被动语态:
The route was retraced by the search party. = 搜索队重走了这条路线。
The events of that day were retraced in the newspaper article. = 那天发生的事情在报纸文章中被重述。


疑问句:

Will they retrace their journey? = 他们会重走他们的旅程吗？


否定句:
He couldn't retrace his steps in the dark. = 他在黑暗中无法重走他的路。
She refused to retrace her past mistakes. = 她拒绝重温她过去的错误。
