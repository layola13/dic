predelay:/ˈpriːdɪleɪ/| n.|预延迟，预先延迟|A predelay is a delay effect in audio processing that adds a very short delay to the beginning of a sound. This short delay can subtly enhance the clarity and presence of the sound, particularly in the context of reverberation or other effects.  It is often used to create a more natural and spacious sound.|Example: The engineer added a subtle predelay to the vocals to give them more depth. 工程师在人声中添加了细微的预延迟，以使其更具深度。|近义词：initial delay, leading delay|反义词：postdelay
