richest: /ˈrɪtʃɪst/ | adj. | 最富有的；最丰富的 |

英文解析:The superlative form of "rich," indicating the highest degree of wealth, abundance, or intensity.  It describes something or someone possessing the greatest amount of money, resources, or a particular quality.

例句:
He is the richest man in the town. = 他是镇上最富有的人。

She owns the richest collection of stamps in the country. = 她拥有全国最丰富的邮票收藏。


近义词: wealthiest, most affluent, most opulent

反义词: poorest, most impoverished, most destitute


词源:  From Old English *rīċ, "powerful, mighty," from Proto-Germanic *rīkiz, related to Old High German rīhhi, "rich, powerful," and Old Norse ríkr, "powerful, rich." The modern sense of "wealthy" developed later.
