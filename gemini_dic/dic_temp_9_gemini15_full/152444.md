pawnors:/ˈpɔːnərz/| n. |典当人；抵押人 |A pawnbroker is someone who lends money in exchange for an item of value that is left as collateral, while a pawnor is the person who pledges that item.  Pawnors often need quick access to cash and use pawning as a short-term solution.  |例句：The pawnors received a loan and pledged their jewelry. 典当人收到贷款并抵押了他们的珠宝。 The pawnors were happy to retrieve their items after repaying the loan. 抵押人很高兴在偿还贷款后取回他们的物品。|近义词： pledger, mortgagor |反义词：pawnbroker, mortgagee
