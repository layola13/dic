rifles: /ˈraɪfəlz/ | n. |步枪；来复枪|A rifle is a long-barreled firearm designed to be held against the shoulder and fired with accuracy at relatively long range.  It uses a rifled barrel, which imparts spin to the projectile, improving accuracy and range. |例句：The soldier carried a high-powered rifle.  士兵携带一把高性能步枪。

He cleaned his rifle meticulously before each hunting trip. 他每次打猎前都会仔细清洁他的步枪。|近义词：gun, firearm, musket |反义词：bow and arrow, slingshot
