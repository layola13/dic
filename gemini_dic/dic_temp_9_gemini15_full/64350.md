erasing: /ɪˈreɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 擦除；抹去；消除 | The act of removing something, typically writing or markings, from a surface.  It can also refer to the removal of data or memories. | 例句：He was erasing the whiteboard. 他正在擦黑板。

例句：She spent hours erasing the mistakes in her essay. 她花了几个小时修改论文中的错误。


近义词：rubbing out, deleting, removing, obliterating | 反义词：writing, adding, creating, inscribing | 词性：动词


USAGE->

He erases the board every day. = 他每天擦黑板。
She erases her mistakes carefully. = 她仔细地擦掉她的错误。


一般过去时:
