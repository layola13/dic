loyalnesses:/ˈlɔɪəlniːsɪz/| n.|忠诚(复数)|The plural form of loyalty, referring to multiple instances or types of faithfulness, devotion, or allegiance.  It emphasizes the various expressions or aspects of being loyal.|例句：His loyalnesses to his family, friends, and country were unwavering.  他的对家庭、朋友和国家的忠诚是坚定不移的。


例句：The different loyalnesses within the organization led to internal conflict.  组织内部不同的忠诚导致了内部冲突。


近义词：allegiances, devotions, fidelities


反义词：treacheries, disloyalties, betrayals


词性:名词 (复数)
