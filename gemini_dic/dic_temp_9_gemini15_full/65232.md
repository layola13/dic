etosha:/eɪˈtɒʃə/| n. |埃托沙|Etosha is a name primarily associated with Etosha National Park in Namibia.  It's a large, flat, white pan (salt pan) that dominates the landscape of the park. The name itself is derived from the Ovambo language, spoken by indigenous people of the region.|The Etosha Pan is a vast, stunning landscape. |近义词：salt pan, salt flat |反义词：无
