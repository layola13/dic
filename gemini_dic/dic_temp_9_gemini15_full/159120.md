pockiness:/ˈpɒkɪnəs/| n. |麻点；痘痕；坑洼|The state of being covered in pocks or small pits; a pitted or scarred surface.  It often refers to skin blemishes, but can also describe the surface texture of other materials.|例句：The pockiness of the moon's surface is visible even through a small telescope. 月球表面的坑洼即使通过小型望远镜也能看到。


The pockiness of his skin was a result of the chickenpox he had as a child. 他皮肤上的麻点是他小时候患水痘留下的。|近义词：pittedness, roughness, scarredness |反义词：smoothness, evenness
