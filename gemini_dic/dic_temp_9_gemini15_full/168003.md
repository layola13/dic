prison:/ˈprɪzən/| n. |监狱，监禁 |A prison is a building in which people are legally held as a punishment for a crime they have committed, or while awaiting trial.  |He was sent to prison for five years. 他被判处五年监禁。|jail, gaol, penitentiary |freedom, liberty |名词
