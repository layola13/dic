duenna:/ˈduːɛnə/| n.| 女保姆；女监护人；老处女 | A duenna is an older woman employed to chaperone a young unmarried woman.  The term often carries a connotation of strictness and sometimes even a slightly comical or interfering nature.  |Example: The young heiress was constantly shadowed by her duenna, preventing her from socializing freely.  年轻的女继承人总是被她的女保姆寸步不离地跟着，这妨碍了她自由地社交。|近义词：chaperone, guardian, governess|反义词：None directly antonymous, but perhaps "companion" implies less restriction. |名词
