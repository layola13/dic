irwin:/ˈɜːrwɪn/ | n. | 欧文（男子名） | Irwin is a given name, primarily of English origin. It is a variant of Irwine, which itself is derived from the Old English elements "ir" (meaning "river") and "wine" (meaning "friend").  The name suggests someone who is a "friend of the river."|例句：My friend's name is Irwin. 我的朋友名叫欧文。  |近义词：Erwin (similar variant)|反义词：无 | 名词
