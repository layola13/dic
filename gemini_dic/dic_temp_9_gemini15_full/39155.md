confuter:/kənˈfjuːtər/| n.|驳斥者，反驳者|A person who refutes or disproves something.  Someone who argues against or contradicts another. |例句：He was a formidable confuter of his opponents' arguments. 他是一位令对手难以招架的反驳者。|例句：The professor, a skilled confuter, easily dismantled the student's weak thesis.  这位教授，一位技艺精湛的反驳者，轻易地驳斥了学生薄弱的论文。|近义词：opponent, refuter, contradictor, debater|反义词：supporter, advocate, confirmer|名词
