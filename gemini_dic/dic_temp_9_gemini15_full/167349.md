pretrim:/ˈpriːtrɪm/| vt.|预修整；预先整理|To trim or prepare something beforehand, often in preparation for a further process.  This often involves removing excess material or making minor adjustments.|例句：The editor pretrimmed the article before sending it to the printer. 编辑在送交印刷厂之前预先修整了这篇文章。


例句：The seamstress pretrimmed the fabric to ensure a clean finish.  裁缝预先修整了布料以确保干净的缝边。


近义词：prepare, adjust, refine, preprocess


反义词：leave as is, neglect


词性:动词
