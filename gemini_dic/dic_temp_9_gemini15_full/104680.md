karting:/ˈkɑːrtɪŋ/| n. |卡丁车运动 |The sport of racing small, open-wheeled cars called karts.  Karting involves driving karts on specially designed tracks.  It's a popular recreational activity and a stepping stone to higher levels of motorsport. |例句：He's been karting since he was ten. 他从十岁就开始玩卡丁车了。  My son is really into karting; he dreams of becoming a Formula 1 driver. 我的儿子非常喜欢卡丁车运动；他梦想成为一名一级方程式赛车手。|近义词：go-karting |反义词：无
