matter:/ˈmætər/ | n., v. | 事情，物质；要紧，有关系 |  to be important; to be of consequence;  substance; physical substance |例句：The matter is urgent. 这件事很紧急。 |近义词：issue, affair, substance, importance |反义词：unimportance, triviality
