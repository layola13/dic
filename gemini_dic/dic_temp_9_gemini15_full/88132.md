hiking:/ˈhaɪkɪŋ/| n.|徒步旅行；远足|The activity of going for long walks in the country for pleasure.|例句：We went hiking in the mountains last weekend.  我们上周末去山里远足了。
例句：Hiking is a great way to exercise and enjoy nature. 远足是锻炼身体和享受大自然的好方法。|近义词：trekking, rambling, backpacking|反义词:staying home, driving|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hike every weekend. = 我每个周末都远足。
He hikes in the national park. = 他在国家公园远足。

一般过去时:
We hiked to the summit yesterday. = 我们昨天徒步到达山顶。
They hiked through the forest. = 他们徒步穿过森林。

现在完成时:
I have hiked many trails in this area. = 我在这个地区徒步走了许多小路。
She has hiked Mount Fuji twice. = 她已经两次徒步登上富士山。

过去完成时:
We had hiked for hours before we reached the lake. = 我们徒步走了几个小时才到达湖边。
They had hiked all day before they found a campsite. = 他们徒步走了一整天后才找到一个营地。

将来时:
We will hike to the waterfall tomorrow. = 我们明天将徒步去瀑布。
They will be hiking in the Alps next month. = 他们下个月将在阿尔卑斯山徒步旅行。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would hike the Appalachian Trail. = 如果我有更多时间，我会徒步走阿巴拉契亚山道。
I wish I could hike to the top of that mountain. = 我希望我能徒步登上那座山顶。

被动语态:
The trail is rarely hiked in winter. = 这条小路很少在冬天被徒步。
The mountain was hiked by many people last year. = 这座山去年被很多人徒步攀登。

疑问句:
Do you like hiking? = 你喜欢徒步旅行吗？
Have you ever hiked in the rainforest? = 你曾经在热带雨林徒步旅行过吗？

否定句:
I don't hike very often. = 我不经常去远足。
They didn't hike yesterday because of the rain. = 因为下雨，他们昨天没有去远足。


PHRASE->
go hiking = 去远足
hiking trail = 徒步小径
hiking boots = 徒步靴
hiking backpack = 徒步背包
day hiking = 日间远足
