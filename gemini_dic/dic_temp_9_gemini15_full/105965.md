klutziest:/ˈklʌtziəst/| adj.| 最笨拙的，最笨手笨脚的 | Most awkward, clumsy, or inept.  It is the superlative form of "klutzy".|例句：He's the klutziest person I know; he trips over air. 他是我认识的最笨拙的人；他连空气都能绊倒。  That was the klutziest attempt at a backflip I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最笨拙的后空翻尝试。|近义词：awkward, clumsy, ungainly, inept, maladroit |反义词：graceful, deft, skillful, adroit
