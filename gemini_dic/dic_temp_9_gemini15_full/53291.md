dirtiest:/ˈdɜːrtiəst/ | adj. | 最脏的 | Most unclean; covered with dirt or filth.  The dirtiest part of the city is known for its high crime rate.  |近义词：filthiest, foulest, grimiest |反义词：cleanest, purest
