interlink:/ˈɪntərˌlɪŋk/| v. |相互关联；互相连接|To connect or link together.  Interlinking refers to the connection or relationship between two or more things, often in a complex or interwoven way.  It implies a reciprocal relationship, where each element influences and is influenced by the others.|例句：The success of the project depends on the interlinking of various departments.  这个项目的成功取决于各个部门之间的相互关联。|例句：The two companies are interlinked through a series of joint ventures. 这两家公司通过一系列合资企业相互关联。|近义词：connect, link, interconnect, interrelate |反义词：disconnect, separate, isolate |动词
