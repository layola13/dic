free-trade:/friːˈtreɪd/| n.|自由贸易|The policy of allowing goods and services to be bought and sold across international borders without tariffs or other restrictions.  It aims to increase economic efficiency and global wealth.|例句：Many countries advocate for free-trade agreements to stimulate economic growth. 许多国家倡导自由贸易协定以刺激经济增长。|例句：The elimination of trade barriers is a key component of free-trade policies. 消除贸易壁垒是自由贸易政策的关键组成部分。|近义词：free market, open trade, unrestricted trade|反义词：protectionism, trade barriers, trade restrictions|名词
