obituary:/əˈbɪtʃuˌɛri/| n. |讣告，死亡公告 |A notice of a death, especially one including a brief biography of the deceased person.  |例句：The newspaper published an obituary for the famous actor. 报纸刊登了这位著名演员的讣告。
例句：His obituary detailed his many accomplishments in the field of medicine. 他的讣告详细叙述了他医学领域里取得的诸多成就。|近义词：death notice, necrology |反义词：birth announcement
