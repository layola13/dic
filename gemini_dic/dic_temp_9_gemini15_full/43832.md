creosotes:/ˈkriːəˌsoʊts/| n. |木馏油；杂酚油|A dark, viscous liquid obtained from the distillation of wood tar, consisting mainly of phenolic compounds.  It's known for its preservative properties and pungent odor. |例句：Creosotes were historically used to preserve railway sleepers. 木馏油历史上被用来防腐铁路枕木。|例句：The pungent smell of creosotes permeated the air near the docks. 码头附近弥漫着刺鼻的木馏油气味。|近义词：coal tar, wood tar|反义词：N/A
