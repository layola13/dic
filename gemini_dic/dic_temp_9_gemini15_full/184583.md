restorations:/ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 修复；恢复；重建 | The act of restoring something to its former state or condition.  It often refers to the process of repairing damaged or deteriorated objects, buildings, or ecosystems. |例句：The restorations of the ancient temple took many years.  古代寺庙的修复工程耗时多年。

例句：The art conservator meticulously documented the restorations of the painting.  艺术保护者一丝不苟地记录了绘画的修复过程。


近义词：repairs, renovations, rebuildings, rehabilitations | 反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin
