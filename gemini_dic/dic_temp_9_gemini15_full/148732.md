/ˌoʊvərˈtriːtmənt/ | n. |过度治疗 |The provision of medical treatment that is excessive, unnecessary, or inappropriate for a patient's condition.  This can lead to adverse effects, increased costs, and reduced quality of life. | Example: The patient experienced overtreatment, leading to complications and prolonged recovery.  过度治疗导致患者出现并发症并延长了恢复期。|近义词：excessive treatment, unnecessary medical intervention |反义词：undertreatment, appropriate care
