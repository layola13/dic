hit-the-bottle:/hɪt ðə ˈbɑːtəl/| phrasal v.|酗酒，烂醉|To drink alcohol excessively.|He hit the bottle after losing his job. 他失业后开始酗酒。|近义词：drink heavily, get drunk, overindulge in alcohol|反义词：abstain from alcohol, be teetotal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hits the bottle every weekend. = 他每个周末都酗酒。
She hits the bottle to cope with stress. = 她酗酒来应对压力。


一般过去时:
He hit the bottle after the party. = 派对后他喝醉了。
She hit the bottle last night and missed work today. = 她昨晚喝醉了，今天没去上班。


现在完成时:
He has hit the bottle too many times recently. = 他最近酗酒太多次了。
She has hit the bottle since her breakup. = 自从分手后，她就开始酗酒了。


过去完成时:
He had hit the bottle before he drove home. = 他酒驾回家之前已经喝醉了。
She had hit the bottle the night before the important meeting. = 她在重要会议的前一天晚上喝醉了。


将来时:
He will hit the bottle again if he doesn't get help. = 如果他不寻求帮助，他还会再次酗酒。
She will probably hit the bottle to celebrate her success. = 她可能会喝酒庆祝她的成功。(注意：此例中“hit the bottle”不一定是负面含义)


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't hit the bottle, he wouldn't have lost his job. = 如果他没有酗酒，他就不会失去工作。
I wish he hadn't hit the bottle last night. = 我真希望他昨晚没有酗酒。


被动语态: (此短语通常不用于被动语态)


疑问句:
Does he hit the bottle often? = 他经常酗酒吗？
Did she hit the bottle at the party? = 她在派对上喝酒了吗？


否定句:
He doesn't hit the bottle anymore. = 他不再酗酒了。
She didn't hit the bottle at the wedding. = 她婚礼上没有酗酒。


PHRASE->
hit the bottle hard = 狂饮
hit the bottle regularly = 经常酗酒
hit the bottle to excess = 酗酒过度
hit the bottle again = 再次酗酒
stop hitting the bottle = 戒酒
