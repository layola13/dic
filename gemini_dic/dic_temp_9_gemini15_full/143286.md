outdrag:/ˌaʊtˈdræɡ/| vt.|超过，拖延超过|to last longer than something else; to exceed in duration|例句：The negotiations outdragged everyone's expectations.  谈判持续的时间超出了所有人的预期。


例句：The movie outdragged even the most patient viewers. 这部电影的时长甚至超过了最耐心的观众的忍受极限。

近义词：outlast, surpass, exceed

反义词：be shorter than, end before
