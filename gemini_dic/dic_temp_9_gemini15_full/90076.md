hostels:/ˈhɒstəlz/ /ˈhɑːstəlz/| n.|青年旅社，宿舍|A hostel is a type of inexpensive lodging, typically offering dormitory-style rooms and shared facilities.  Hostels are popular with budget travelers and backpackers.| 例句：I stayed in a hostel during my trip to Europe. 我在去欧洲旅行期间住过青年旅社。| 例句：The hostel provided clean sheets and towels. 青年旅社提供了干净的床单和毛巾。|近义词：dorm, inn, lodging |反义词：hotel, luxury accommodation|名词
