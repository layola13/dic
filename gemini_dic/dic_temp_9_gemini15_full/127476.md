necessitating:/nəˈsɛsɪteɪtɪŋ/ | v.ing | 使成为必要；需要 |  Making something necessary; requiring.  | The severity of the injuries necessitated immediate surgery.  严重受伤需要立即手术。| requiring, demanding, calling for |  unnecessary, optional | 动名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The current situation necessitates a change in strategy. = 目前的情况需要改变策略。
This project necessitates a significant investment of time and resources. = 这个项目需要大量的时间和资源投入。

一般过去时:
The unexpected storm necessitated the cancellation of the flight. = 突如其来的暴风雨导致航班取消。
His illness necessitated a long period of rest. = 他的病需要长时间的休息。

现在完成时:
Recent events have necessitated a review of our security procedures. = 最近发生的事件需要我们重新审查安全程序。
The increase in demand has necessitated the hiring of additional staff. = 需求的增加使得我们不得不雇佣更多员工。

过去完成时:
The previous year's drought had necessitated water rationing. = 前一年的干旱导致了限水。
The discovery of the flaw had necessitated a complete redesign of the product. = 发现缺陷后，产品不得不重新设计。

将来时:
Future economic challenges will necessitate adjustments to our budget. = 未来的经济挑战将需要我们调整预算。
Technological advancements will necessitate retraining for many workers. = 技术进步将需要许多工人进行再培训。

虚拟语气:
If the situation were different, it wouldn't necessitate such drastic measures. = 如果情况不同，就不需要采取如此严厉的措施。
It is imperative that we act decisively, as the situation necessitates. = 情况所迫，我们必须果断行动。

被动语态:
A change in policy was necessitated by the recent scandal. = 最近的丑闻迫使政策发生了变化。
Further investigation is necessitated by the conflicting evidence. = 互相矛盾的证据需要进一步调查。

疑问句:
Does the project necessitate additional funding? = 这个项目需要额外的资金吗？
What measures does this situation necessitate? = 这种情况需要采取什么措施？

否定句:
The current situation does not necessitate any immediate action. = 目前的情况不需要任何立即行动。
The task does not necessitate specialized skills. = 这项任务不需要专门技能。
