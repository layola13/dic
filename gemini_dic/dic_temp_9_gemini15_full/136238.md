nonrenewals:/ˌnɑːnriˈnuːəlz/| n. |未续签；未续约；终止续约|The plural form of "nonrenewal," referring to instances where something is not renewed or continued.  It often refers to contracts, subscriptions, or memberships that are not extended. | Example: The company announced a series of nonrenewals for underperforming employees.  该公司宣布了一系列针对表现不佳员工的合同终止续签。|近义词： cancellations, terminations, lapses |反义词：renewals, extensions
