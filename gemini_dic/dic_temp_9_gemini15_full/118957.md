meteorometer:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɒmɪtər/ | n. |测陨石仪，流星计|A meteorometer is a device used to detect and measure meteors.  It often involves sophisticated techniques to track the trajectory, speed, and brightness of meteors.  |例句：The new meteorometer detected a meteor shower unseen by previous instruments. 新的测陨石仪探测到以前仪器未观测到的流星雨。

|例句：Scientists used the meteorometer to analyze the composition of the meteor. 科学家们用测陨石仪分析了陨石的成分。|近义词：meteor detector, meteor monitor |反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "meteorometer" is a combination of "meteor" (from Greek *metéōros*, meaning "high in the air") and "-meter" (from Greek *metron*, meaning "measure").  It literally means "meteor measurer".
