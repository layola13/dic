decanters:/dɪˈkæntərz/| n. |酒瓶；斟酒器|A decanter is a glass container, usually with a stopper, used for holding and serving wine or other alcoholic beverages.  It is often used to allow sediment to settle out of the wine before serving.|The waiter carefully decanted the red wine before pouring it into our glasses.  侍者在将红酒倒入我们的酒杯之前小心地把它醒酒。|carafe, flask|bottle (general term)|名词

He decants the wine before every dinner. = 他每次晚餐前都醒酒。
She decants the expensive port. = 她醒那瓶昂贵的波特酒。
