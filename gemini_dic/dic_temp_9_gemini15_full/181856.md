relocks:/ˌriːˈlɒks/| v.|重新锁上，再锁上 |To lock something again; to fasten something securely once more.|例句：He relocked the door after checking the windows. 他检查完窗户后又重新锁上了门。  He relocked the safe after placing the documents inside. 他把文件放进去后又重新锁上了保险箱。|近义词：refasten, secure, lock again|反义词：unlock, open
