lavished:/ˈlævɪʃt/| v.| 慷慨给予；浪费；铺张 | To bestow something generously or extravagantly upon.  It implies abundance and often suggests excess. |例句：They lavished praise on the young artist.  他们对这位年轻艺术家赞不绝口。

例句：The king lavished gifts upon his courtiers. 国王慷慨地赏赐他的朝臣。


近义词：bestowed, showered, squandered, expended, wasted

反义词：stinted, deprived, scrimped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They lavish attention on their children. = 他们对孩子关爱有加。
He lavishes money on expensive cars. = 他把钱花在昂贵的汽车上。


一般过去时:
She lavished affection on her pet dog. = 她对她的宠物狗非常疼爱。
They lavished praise on the winning team. = 他们对获胜的队伍赞扬不已。


现在完成时:
They have lavished millions of dollars on the project. = 他们已经在这个项目上花费了数百万美元。
She has lavished care and attention on her garden. = 她精心照料着自己的花园。


过去完成时:
He had lavished his fortune on gambling before he realised his mistake. = 他在他意识到错误之前已经把他的财产都花在了赌博上。
They had lavished gifts on their friends before the party started. = 在派对开始之前，他们已经给朋友们送了很多礼物。


将来时:
They will lavish attention on the new baby. = 他们将对新生儿倍加呵护。
The company will lavish resources on its new product. = 公司将大力投资其新产品。


虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would lavish gifts on my family. = 如果我有钱，我会慷慨地给我的家人送礼物。
I wish I could lavish more time on my hobbies. = 我希望我能有更多时间来从事我的爱好。


被动语态:
The guests were lavished with food and drink. = 客人们受到了盛情款待。
The movie star was lavished with attention by the paparazzi. = 电影明星受到了狗仔队的热烈追捧。


疑问句:
Were they lavished with gifts at the wedding? = 婚礼上他们收到了很多礼物吗？
How much money will be lavished on the new stadium? = 新体育场将投资多少钱？


否定句:
He didn't lavish much attention on his studies. = 他并没有花很多时间学习。
They did not lavish praise on the mediocre performance. = 他们并没有对平庸的表演给予赞扬。
