hit the hay:/hɪt ðə heɪ/| phrasal verb|睡觉，上床睡觉|To "hit the hay" is an informal idiom meaning to go to bed.  It is believed to derive from the practice of sleeping on a bed filled with hay. |It's getting late; I think I'll hit the hay.|近义词：go to bed, turn in, retire|反义词：wake up, get up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hit the hay at 11 pm every night. = 我每天晚上11点睡觉。
She hits the hay early on weekdays. = 她工作日很早就睡觉了。


一般过去时:
I hit the hay early last night because I was tired. = 因为我累了，我昨晚很早就睡了。
They hit the hay after watching a movie. = 他们看完电影后就睡了。


现在完成时:
I have hit the hay early every night this week. = 这周我每天晚上都睡得很早。
She has hit the hay already, so don't disturb her. = 她已经睡了，别打扰她。


过去完成时:
I had hit the hay before the storm started. =暴风雨开始之前我就已经睡了。
He had hit the hay before the phone rang. = 电话响之前他已经睡着了。


将来时:
I will hit the hay as soon as I finish this work. = 我一完成这项工作就去睡觉。
We will hit the hay after the party. = 聚会结束后我们就要睡觉了。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would hit the hay now. = 如果我是你，我现在就睡觉。
I wish I had hit the hay earlier last night. = 我真希望昨晚早点睡觉。


被动语态: *(This idiom is rarely, if ever, used in the passive voice.)*


疑问句:
When do you usually hit the hay? = 你通常几点睡觉？
Will you hit the hay soon? = 你很快就要睡觉了吗？


否定句:
I didn't hit the hay until midnight. = 我直到午夜才睡觉。
She didn't hit the hay because she was too excited. = 她因为太兴奋而没有睡觉。
