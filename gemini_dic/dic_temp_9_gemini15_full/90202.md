hound:/haʊnd/ | n. /haʊnd/ | v.|猎犬；追踪；烦扰 | A hound is a type of dog, typically used for hunting.  It can also refer to the act of pursuing or harassing someone relentlessly. |例句：The hunter used his hounds to track the fox. 猎人用猎犬追踪狐狸。

The police hounded the suspect until he confessed. 警方穷追不舍地追捕嫌疑犯，直到他认罪。|近义词：dog, chase, pursue, harass |反义词：release, abandon


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hounds his employees relentlessly. 他无情地追赶他的员工。
The dogs hound the rabbit across the field. 狗追着兔子穿过田野。


一般过去时:
The detectives hounded the criminal for weeks. 探员们追捕罪犯数周。
She hounded him with questions all evening. 她整个晚上都用问题烦扰他。


现在完成时:
They have hounded the thief for months, but still haven't caught him. 他们已经追捕小偷几个月了，但仍然没有抓住他。
The media has hounded the celebrity since the scandal broke. 自丑闻爆发以来，媒体一直在骚扰这位名人。


过去完成时:
The police had hounded the suspect for years before finally apprehending him. 警方在最终逮捕嫌疑人之前已经追捕了他多年。
She had hounded him with phone calls before he finally agreed to meet. 她在他最终同意见面之前一直用电话骚扰他。


将来时:
The press will hound the politician until he resigns. 媒体将追着这位政客直到他辞职。
I will hound you until you give me back my money. 我会一直纠缠你，直到你把我的钱还给我。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't hound him like that. 如果我是你，我就不会那样纠缠他。
I wish they wouldn't hound me with emails. 我希望他们不要用邮件烦我。


被动语态:
He was hounded by the paparazzi. 他被狗仔队纠缠。
The suspect was hounded relentlessly by the police. 嫌疑人被警方无情地追捕。


疑问句:
Why are you hounding me? 你为什么纠缠我？

否定句:

The police didn't hound the suspect after he provided an alibi. 嫌疑人提供不在场证明后，警方没有继续追捕他。

词源:

The word "hound" comes from the Old English word "hund," meaning "dog."  This word is related to other Germanic words for dog, and ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *hundaz.  The specific meaning of "hunting dog" developed over time.
