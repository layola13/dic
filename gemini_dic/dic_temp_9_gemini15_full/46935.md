darnation:/dɑːrˈneɪʃən/| interj.|该死，讨厌；真倒霉|An exclamation of anger, frustration, or annoyance.  It is a milder form of damnation.|例句：Darnation! I missed the bus again! 该死！我又错过公交车了！|近义词：damn, blasted, heck, shoot|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I darn socks every Sunday. = 我每周日都缝补袜子。
She darns her husband's shirts. = 她缝补她丈夫的衬衫。


一般过去时:
He darned the hole in his jeans. = 他缝补了他牛仔裤上的洞。
They darned the ripped fabric carefully. = 他们仔细地缝补了撕裂的布料。


现在完成时:
I have darned all the holes in my sweaters. = 我已经缝补了我毛衣上所有的洞。
She has darned many pairs of socks in her life. = 她一生缝补过很多双袜子。


过去完成时:
She had darned the socks before she washed them. = 在她洗袜子之前，她已经缝补好了。
He had darned the tear before he put on the pants. = 他在穿上裤子之前已经缝补好了裂口。


将来时:
I will darn these socks tomorrow. = 我明天会缝补这些袜子。
She will darn the hole in her dress before the party. = 派对前她会缝补她裙子上的洞。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would darn the rip in my shirt. = 如果我有时间，我会缝补衬衫上的裂口。
I wish I had darned the hole before I wore the jeans. = 我真希望在我穿牛仔裤之前就缝补了那个洞。


被动语态:
The socks were darned by my grandmother. = 这些袜子是我祖母缝补的。
The hole in the sweater needs to be darned. = 毛衣上的洞需要缝补。


疑问句:
Have you ever darned anything? = 你曾经缝补过什么东西吗？
Will you darn the hole in my pants? = 你会缝补我裤子上的洞吗？


否定句:
I didn't darn the socks. = 我没有缝补袜子。
She will not darn the sweater today. = 她今天不会缝补毛衣。
