cullis:/ˈkʌlɪs/| n.|浓汁；调味汁|A cullis is a smooth, rich sauce or puree, typically made from strained vegetables or meat stock. It's often used as a base for other sauces or as a finishing touch to add richness and depth of flavor to a dish.|Example: The chef created a vibrant red pepper cullis to complement the roasted duck.  厨师制作了一种鲜艳的红辣椒浓汁来搭配烤鸭。|近义词：puree, sauce, velouté|反义词：N/A
