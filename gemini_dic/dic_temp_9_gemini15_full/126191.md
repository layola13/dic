muzzler:/ˈmʌzələr/| n. |口套；(对言论等的)压制者 |A muzzle is a covering placed over the mouth of an animal, typically a dog, to prevent it from biting or barking.  A muzzler can also refer to someone or something that suppresses free speech or expression. |例句：The police officer put a muzzler on the aggressive dog.  警察给那条凶猛的狗戴上了口套。

例句：The government's censorship policies made them muzzlers of free speech. 政府的审查制度使他们成为言论自由的压制者。

近义词：gag, suppressor, censor |反义词：advocate, liberator
