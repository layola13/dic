overflying: /ˌoʊvərˈflaɪɪŋ/ | v. |飞越；凌驾于 | The act of flying over something.  This word describes the action of an aircraft or bird passing over a particular area or object. |例句：The plane was overflying the city. 这架飞机正在飞越这座城市。

The birds were overflying the mountains.  这些鸟儿正在飞越群山。|近义词：flying over, passing over |反义词：underflying (less common)

The airplane is overflying the Pacific Ocean. = 飞机正在飞越太平洋。
He overflies the countryside every morning in his hot air balloon. = 他每天早上都乘坐热气球飞越乡村。

词源:
The word "overflying" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning above or across) and the verb "flying" (the act of moving through the air).  It's a relatively straightforward derivation reflecting the action of the verb.
