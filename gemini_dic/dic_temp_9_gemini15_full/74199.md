fourling:/ˈfɔːrlɪŋ/| n. |四胞胎之一|A fourling is one of four offspring born at the same birth.  It refers to a single member of a set of quadruplets.  The term is less common than "quadruplet," which refers to the group as a whole.|Example: One of the fourlings was born prematurely.|近义词:quadruplet (one of four)|反义词:singleton, twin, triplet
