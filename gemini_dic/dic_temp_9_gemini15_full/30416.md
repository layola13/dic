/sɛnˈtrɑːleɪ/ | n. |中央的；主要的；电力公司 |A centrale is a main power station, especially one that generates electricity.  It can also refer to something central or main in importance or location. |Example: The city's centrale provides electricity to the entire metropolitan area.  这个城市的中心电站为整个都会区提供电力。|近义词：central, main, principal |反义词：peripheral, outlying
