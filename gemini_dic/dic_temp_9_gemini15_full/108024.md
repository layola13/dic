lasting: /ˈlɑːstɪŋ/ | adj. | 持久的；永久的 |  lasting describes something that continues for a long time or is expected to continue for a long time. It emphasizes the duration and endurance of something.  |例句：The agreement ensures a lasting peace.  这项协议确保了持久的和平。

The movie had a lasting impact on me. 这部电影对我产生了持久的影响。


近义词：enduring, permanent, abiding, durable | 反义词：fleeting, temporary, transient, ephemeral | 词性: adjective

词源:
The word "lasting" is derived from the Old English word "læstan," meaning "to last," which in turn likely comes from a Proto-Germanic root.  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a present participle, which then functions as an adjective.
