reacknowledgements:/ˌriːækˈnɒlɪdʒmənts/ | n. |重新确认；再次感谢 |The reacknowledgements in the revised manuscript were appreciated.  |近义词: reaffirmation, renewed thanks |反义词: disregard, denial |名词


英文解析: Reacknowledgements refers to the act of acknowledging something again, often after a previous acknowledgement.  This is commonly seen in situations where a previous acknowledgement may have been overlooked or needs to be emphasized.  It's most frequently used in the context of thanking someone again or confirming something previously stated.

例句：

The company issued reacknowledgements to all employees for their hard work during the project. 公司再次感谢所有员工在项目期间的辛勤工作。

The professor sent reacknowledgements for the student's insightful comments on his latest paper. 教授再次感谢学生对他最新论文的深刻评论。
