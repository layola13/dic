platinotype:/ˈplætɪnəʊtaɪp/| n. |铂金印相法，铂金照片 |A platinotype is a photographic print made using a platinum or palladium salt.  The process is known for its rich tonal range and archival quality.  |Example: The museum displayed a collection of exquisite platinotypes from the early 20th century.  博物馆展出了20世纪早期一系列精美的铂金照片。|近义词：platinum print, palladium print |反义词：无
