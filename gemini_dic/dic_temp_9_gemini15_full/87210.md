heredities:/həˈrɛdətiz/| n.|遗传特性；遗传；遗传学|The characteristics or features of an organism that are passed down from parents to offspring through genes.  Heredity plays a significant role in determining an individual's traits, such as eye color and height, but environmental factors also contribute.|例句：The study of heredity is crucial to understanding the transmission of diseases across generations. 遗传学研究对于理解疾病在几代人之间的传播至关重要。|例句：His athletic abilities are largely due to heredity.他的运动能力很大程度上是遗传的。|近义词：inheritance, genetic inheritance, lineage|反义词：acquired characteristics
