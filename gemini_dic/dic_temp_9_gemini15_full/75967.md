funks:/fʌŋks/| n. v.|昏迷；功能失调；无能状态；(俚语)沮丧；颓废；|英文解析:A state of depression or listlessness; a temporary inability to function properly.  Also refers to a style of music. |例句：He fell into a funk after losing his job. 他失业后陷入低落状态。He's in a creative funk and can't seem to write anything. 他创作上陷入了困境，似乎写不出任何东西。|近义词：depression, slump, doldrums, lethargy |反义词：excitement, enthusiasm, energy
