non-soluble:/ˌnɒnˈsɒljʊbəl/ | adj. | 不溶的，难溶的 |  Insoluble; unable to be dissolved in a liquid.  This refers to a substance that does not dissolve or only dissolves to a very small extent in a given solvent.  | Example:  Salt is soluble in water, but sand is non-soluble.  盐溶于水，但沙子不溶于水。|近义词:insoluble, undissolvable |反义词:soluble, dissolvable
