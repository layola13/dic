caudicle:/ˈkɔːdɪkl/| n.|小柄；花柄；子房柄|A small stalk or stem, especially one attaching a structure to a larger body.  For example, in orchids, it refers to the stalk that connects the pollinia (pollen masses) to the viscidium (sticky gland).|The caudicle of the orchid was carefully examined under the microscope.|近义词：stalk, peduncle, stipe|反义词：无
