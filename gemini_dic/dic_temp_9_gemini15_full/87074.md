heptarch:/ˈheptɑːrk/| n. |七王统治；七国联盟|A heptarchy is a state or region governed by seven rulers or kingdoms.  The term is most famously associated with the Anglo-Saxon kingdoms of England before their unification. |Example: The Anglo-Saxon heptarchy was a period of significant political and social change.  = 盎格鲁-撒克逊七国时期是一个政治和社会变革显著的时期。|近义词：septemvirate (a government of seven men)|反义词：monarchy (rule by one)|名词
