mechanician:/məˈkænɪʃən/| n. |机械师，技工 | A person who is skilled in repairing and maintaining machinery.  | The mechanician quickly repaired the broken engine.  这位机械师迅速修理了损坏的引擎。| engineer, technician, mechanic |  (无明确反义词，可考虑 unskilled worker) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mechanician checks the engine regularly. = 机械师定期检查引擎。
He maintains all the factory machinery. = 他维护工厂里所有的机器。


一般过去时:
The mechanician repaired the car yesterday. = 机械师昨天修理了汽车。
She fixed the broken washing machine. = 她修理了坏掉的洗衣机。

The mechanician has already fixed the problem. = 机械师已经解决了问题。
