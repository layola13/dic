prying:/ˈpraɪɪŋ/| adj. v.|爱打探隐私的；窥探的；撬开的； | inquisitive, meddling, intrusive;  to force something open using a tool |例句：His prying questions made her uncomfortable. 他爱打探隐私的问题让她感到不舒服。  He was prying into other people's affairs. 他在窥探别人的私事。|近义词：intrusive, inquisitive, meddlesome |反义词：discreet, reserved, private


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is always prying into other people's business. 他总是打探别人的事。
She pries into my personal life too much. 她太探究我的私生活了。


一般过去时:
They pried open the locked box with a crowbar. 他们用撬棍撬开了锁着的箱子。
He pried the lid off the jar. 他撬开了罐子的盖子。


现在完成时:
She has been prying into my affairs for weeks. 她几周来一直在打探我的事情。
He has pried open several of the windows. 他已经撬开了好几个窗户。


过去完成时:

She had been prying into his secrets for months. 她已经探听他的秘密好几个月了。


将来时:
He will pry into the matter further. 他会进一步调查此事。
They will pry the window open to get some fresh air. 他们会撬开窗户透透气。


虚拟语气:
If he were not so prying, he would have more friends. 如果他不那么爱打探，就会有更多朋友。

被动语态:
