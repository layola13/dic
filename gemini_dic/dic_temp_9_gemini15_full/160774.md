posited:/ˈpɒzɪtɪd/ /ˈpɑːzɪtɪd/ | vt. | 假设；提出；断定 | To assume as a fact or to suggest (an idea, theory, etc.) as a basis for reasoning or further investigation.  It implies a tentative or provisional acceptance, not necessarily a proven fact. | The scientist posited a new theory to explain the phenomenon. 科学家提出了一种新的理论来解释这种现象。  They posited that the increase in temperature was due to climate change.  他们假设气温升高是由于气候变化造成的。 | proposed, suggested, assumed, hypothesized | refuted, denied, disproved | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He posits a new theory every year. = 他每年都提出一个新理论。
She posits that the earth is flat. = 她假设地球是平的。

一般过去时:
The philosopher posited the existence of God. = 这位哲学家假设上帝的存在。
They posited a link between smoking and cancer. = 他们假设吸烟与癌症之间存在联系。

现在完成时:
Scientists have posited many theories about the origin of the universe. = 科学家们已经提出了许多关于宇宙起源的理论。
Researchers have posited a correlation between diet and disease. = 研究人员已经假设饮食与疾病之间存在相关性。

过去完成时:
Before the experiment, they had posited that the results would be positive. = 在实验之前，他们已经假设结果会是积极的。
The author had posited a complex plot before writing the novel. = 作者在写小说之前已经设想了一个复杂的故事情节。


将来时:
The team will posit a new approach to the problem. = 该团队将提出解决这个问题的新方法。
We will posit a different hypothesis in our next study. = 我们将在我们的下一项研究中提出不同的假设。

虚拟语气:
If he posited a different scenario, the outcome might have been different. = 如果他假设不同的情况，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish they had posited a more realistic solution. = 我希望他们能提出一个更现实的解决方案。

被动语态:
A new theory was posited by the researchers. = 研究人员提出了一种新的理论。
The hypothesis was posited as a starting point for further investigation. = 假设被作为进一步调查的起点。

疑问句:
What did the article posit as the main cause? = 这篇文章假设的主要原因是什么？
What will the study posit as its central argument? = 这项研究将把什么作为其核心论点？

否定句:
The study did not posit a causal relationship. = 这项研究没有假设因果关系。
The author did not posit any specific solutions.  = 作者没有提出任何具体的解决方案。


PHRASE->

posit a question: 提出一个问题
