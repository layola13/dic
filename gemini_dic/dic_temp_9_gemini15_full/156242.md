phycoerythrine:/ˌfaɪkoʊˈɛrɪθrin/| n. |藻红蛋白|A red pigment found in certain red algae and cyanobacteria that absorbs light energy for photosynthesis.  It's a type of accessory pigment, meaning it helps chlorophyll capture a broader spectrum of light.|例句：Phycoerythrin is responsible for the reddish color of some marine algae.  某些海洋藻类的红色是由于藻红蛋白。|近义词：phycobilin (a broader category)|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
