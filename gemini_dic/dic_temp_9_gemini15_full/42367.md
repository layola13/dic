counterattack:/ˈkaʊntərəˌtæk/| n. v.|反攻，反击|A counterattack is an attack made in response to an attack.  It's a military tactic, but can also be used metaphorically.|例句：The army launched a fierce counterattack after being pushed back.  军队在被击退后发起了猛烈的反攻。

The company used a clever marketing campaign as a counterattack against its competitors. 公司用巧妙的营销活动来反击竞争对手。|近义词：retaliation, reprisal, counteroffensive|反义词：retreat, surrender


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The army counterattacks the enemy.  军队反击敌人。
She counterattacks with a witty remark. 她以一句机智的回应进行反击。


一般过去时:
The soldiers counterattacked at dawn. 士兵们在黎明时分发起了反击。
He counterattacked his opponent's argument effectively. 他有效地反驳了对手的论点。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
