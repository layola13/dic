oxidises:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzɪz/ | v. | 氧化；使氧化 | To combine or become combined chemically with oxygen.  The process can be slow (rusting) or rapid (combustion).|


例句：

Iron oxidises when exposed to air and moisture. = 铁暴露在空气和潮湿的环境中会氧化。

The fruit oxidises quickly once cut open. = 水果一旦切开就会很快氧化。


近义词：rust, corrode, tarnish


反义词：reduce


词性：动词
