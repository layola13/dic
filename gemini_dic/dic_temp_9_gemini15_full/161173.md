postholiday: /ˈpoʊstˈhɑːlədeɪ/ | adj. | 节假日的后；节后 |  Relating to or occurring after a holiday or holidays.  This term often describes the period immediately following a holiday season, such as Christmas or the New Year, and often carries connotations of a return to routine and the associated feelings (e.g., tiredness, let-down). |

例句：The postholiday slump in sales was significant.  节后销售额大幅下降。

例句：Many people experience postholiday blues after the festive season. 许多人在节日后感到情绪低落。


近义词：post-festive, after-holiday


反义词：preholiday, before-holiday


词性：形容词
