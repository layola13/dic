postauditory:/ˌpoʊstˈɔːdɪtɔːri/| adj. | 听觉后的；听觉之后的 | Relating to or occurring after the process of hearing.  This term often refers to cognitive processes that occur after auditory information has been received and processed by the brain.  | Example: Postauditory processing involves interpreting and understanding the meaning of sounds.  听觉后处理涉及解释和理解声音的含义。|近义词：postperceptual, secondary auditory |反义词：auditory, preauditory
