conmen:/ˈkɒnmən/| n.|骗子，江湖骗子|A conman is a person who cheats others out of money or property, often using clever tricks or deception.  They are skilled at manipulating people and exploiting their trust.|例句：The conman tricked the elderly woman out of her life savings. 这个骗子骗走了这位老妇人的毕生积蓄。|例句：Several conmen were arrested for running a sophisticated investment scam.  几个骗子因策划复杂的投资骗局而被捕。|近义词：swindler, scammer, fraudster, sharper|反义词：honest person, philanthropist
