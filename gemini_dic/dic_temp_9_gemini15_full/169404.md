pronominals:/prəˈnɑːmɪnəlz/| n. |代词性词语|Pronominals are words or phrases that function like pronouns, substituting for nouns or noun phrases.  They often have a referential function, pointing back to something already mentioned or implied in the context.  This can include true pronouns (he, she, it, they, etc.), but also other word classes used in a pronominal way. | Example: "The car is red. It is fast." (Here, "it" is a pronominal substituting for "the car"). |近义词:pronouns, pro-forms|反义词:nouns, noun phrases
