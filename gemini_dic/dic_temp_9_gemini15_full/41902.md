词源:
The word "cosmetics" comes from the Greek word "kosmetikos," meaning "skilled in adornment," which in turn is derived from "kosmos," meaning "order," "harmony," or "the world."  The original sense referred to the art of enhancing one's appearance, and later extended to the products themselves.
