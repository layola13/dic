misperceiving:/ˌmɪspərˈsiːvɪŋ/ | v. |误解，误认为 | The act of understanding or interpreting something incorrectly.  It implies a failure to grasp the true nature or meaning of something. | Example: He was misperceiving the situation, believing it to be far more dangerous than it actually was. 他误解了形势，认为形势比实际危险得多。  |近义词:misunderstanding, misinterpreting, mistaking |反义词:understanding, comprehending

词源:
mis- (错误地) + perceive (感知，理解)  The prefix "mis-" indicates a wrong or faulty action, while "perceive" means to become aware of something through the senses or mind.  Therefore, "misperceiving" means to wrongly understand or interpret something.
