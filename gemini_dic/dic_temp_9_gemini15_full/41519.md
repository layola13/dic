coronach:/ˈkɒrənæk/| n. |新冠疫情期间的哀歌；对新冠疫情的悲痛表达|A coronach is a lament, typically a song or wail, expressing grief or sorrow, especially associated with the COVID-19 pandemic.  It often reflects the collective mourning and anxieties surrounding the pandemic's impact on individuals and society.| Example: The artist's new work is a coronach, reflecting the widespread loss and suffering during the pandemic.  = 艺术家的新作是一首哀歌，反映了疫情期间普遍的损失和痛苦。 The coronach resonated deeply with those who had lost loved ones to the virus. = 这首哀歌深深地触动了那些失去亲人的人。|近义词：lament, dirge, elegy, threnody|反义词：celebration, rejoicing
