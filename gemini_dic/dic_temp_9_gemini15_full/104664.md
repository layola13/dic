karmas:/ˈkɑːrməz/|/ˈkɑːrmɑːz/| n. |业报；因果报应；命运|The consequences of a person's actions in this and previous states of existence, viewed as determining their fate in future existences.|例句：Many believe that good karmas lead to good fortune. 很多人相信善有善报。|例句：His bad karmas seem to be catching up with him. 他的恶报似乎正在追赶他。|近义词：consequences, retribution, fate, destiny|反义词：blessing, good fortune
