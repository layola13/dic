half-frowningly:/ˌhæfˈfraʊnɪŋli/| adv. |半皱眉地，带着一丝皱眉的表情|This word describes an action performed with a slight frown, suggesting displeasure, thoughtfulness, or uncertainty.  It combines the adverbial suffix "-ly" with "half-frowning," indicating a partial or incomplete frown.|He looked half-frowningly at the complicated instructions. 他半皱眉地看着复杂的说明。|She listened half-frowningly to the gossip. 她带着一丝皱眉的表情听着八卦。| frowningly, thoughtfully, dubiously |smilingly, happily

USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what" does.  The example sentences above demonstrate its usage.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed.


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "half-frowningly".  Its usage is primarily as a single descriptive adverb.
