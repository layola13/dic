harns:/hɑːrnz/| n.|  harnesses(复数)|  挽具；马具；利用；支配| Harnesses are used to control and guide horses.  挽具用来控制和引导马匹。| 近义词：gear, tackle, equipment | 反义词：freedom, liberty | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer harnesses his horse to the plow. = 农夫把他的马套在犁上。
She harnesses the power of the wind to generate electricity. = 她利用风力发电。


一般过去时:
They harnessed the river to provide water for the city. = 他们利用河流为城市提供水。
The knight harnessed his armor before battle. = 骑士在战斗前穿戴好盔甲。


现在完成时:
Scientists have harnessed the power of the atom. = 科学家们已经利用了原子的力量。
We have harnessed solar energy to heat our home. = 我们已经利用太阳能来加热我们的家。


过去完成时:
They had harnessed the horses before the sun rose. = 他们日出前就把马套好了。
The engineers had harnessed the waterfall to power the mill. = 工程师们已经利用瀑布为磨坊提供动力。


将来时:
We will harness the energy of the tides. = 我们将利用潮汐的能量。
They will harness the power of the sun to create electricity. = 他们将利用太阳能发电。


虚拟语气:
If we harnessed renewable resources, we would reduce pollution. = 如果我们利用可再生资源，我们将减少污染。
I wish we could harness the power of the mind. = 我希望我们能够利用心灵的力量。


被动语态:
The horses were harnessed to the cart. = 马被套在车上。
The energy of the wind is harnessed to generate electricity. = 风能被用来发电。


疑问句:
Is the horse properly harnessed? = 马套好了吗？
How is the power of the sun harnessed? = 太阳能是如何被利用的？


否定句:
The farmer didn't harness the oxen to the plow. = 农夫没有把牛套在犁上。
We haven't harnessed the full potential of this technology. = 我们还没有充分利用这项技术的潜力。
