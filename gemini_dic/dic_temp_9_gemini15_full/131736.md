nonconfirmation:/ˌnɒnkɒnfɜːˈmeɪʃən/| n. |未确认；未证实；否认|The lack of confirmation;  the act of not confirming something; a statement or action that denies or contradicts something.  It implies a previous expectation or claim that is now shown to be untrue or unsupported.|例句：The nonconfirmation of his alibi led to his arrest.  他的不在场证明未获证实导致了他的逮捕。  The nonconfirmation of the rumor quickly spread.  谣言未得到证实的消息迅速传播开来。|近义词：disconfirmation, denial, refutation, contradiction|反义词：confirmation, verification, corroboration|名词
