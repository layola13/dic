praises:/ˈpreɪzɪz/| n.|赞美，赞扬|Expressions of approval or admiration.|The teacher showered praises on her students for their excellent work. 老师对学生们优秀的作业赞赏有加。|近义词：commendations, accolades, compliments|反义词：criticisms, condemnations|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He praises his team's efforts. 他赞扬了他团队的努力。
She praises the beauty of nature. 她赞美大自然的美丽。


一般过去时:
They praised the singer's performance. 他们称赞了歌手的表演。
He praised her for her courage. 他称赞了她的勇气。


现在完成时:
She has praised him many times. 她已经多次赞扬他了。
They have praised the new policy. 他们已经赞扬了这项新政策。


过去完成时:
They had praised his work before he left. 他离开之前，他们已经称赞了他的工作。
She had praised the painting before the exhibition. 展览前她就赞扬过这幅画。


将来时:
He will praise her achievements. 他将赞扬她的成就。
They will praise the winner. 他们将赞扬获胜者。


虚拟语气:
I wish he would praise my efforts. 我希望他会赞扬我的努力。
If he praised my work, I would be very happy. 如果他赞扬我的工作，我会非常高兴。


被动语态:
His work was praised by many people. 他的作品受到许多人的赞扬。
The new product is praised for its innovation. 新产品因其创新而受到赞扬。


疑问句:
Will they praise my presentation? 他们会赞扬我的演讲吗？
Do you often praise your children? 你经常表扬你的孩子吗？


否定句:
They did not praise his behavior. 他们没有赞扬他的行为。
She doesn't praise people easily. 她不会轻易赞扬别人。
