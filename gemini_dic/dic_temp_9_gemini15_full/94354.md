impish:/ˈɪmpɪʃ/| adj.|淘气的，顽皮的；恶作剧的|Mischievous; playfully naughty.|例句：He has an impish grin. 他脸上带着淘气的笑容。

例句：The impish child pulled his sister's hair.  那个顽皮的孩子拽了他姐姐的头发。


近义词：mischievous, naughty, playful, roguish|反义词：obedient, well-behaved, serious


词源:  The word "impish" comes from the word "imp," a small mischievous demon or sprite in folklore.  The suffix "-ish" adds a quality or characteristic.  Therefore, "impish" literally means "like an imp."
