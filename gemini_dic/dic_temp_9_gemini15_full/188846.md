russeted:/ˈrʌsɪtɪd/| adj. | 锈红色的；粗糙的；(指苹果) 带有锈斑的 |  Describes a color resembling rust, or a rough, reddish-brown texture, often applied to apples with a characteristic rough, brownish skin. |The russet apple was slightly bruised but still tasted delicious. 这颗带锈斑的苹果有点瘀伤，但仍然味道鲜美。|近义词：reddish-brown, rough, coarse |反义词：smooth, polished
