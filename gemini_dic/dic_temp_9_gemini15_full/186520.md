rickstand:/ˈrɪkˌstænd/| n. |临时支架；活动支架|A rickstand is a temporary, often makeshift, structure used to support something, typically during construction or repair.  It's usually made of readily available materials and is not intended to be permanent. |例句：The workers used a rickstand to hold up the scaffolding while they repaired the roof. 工人们用一个临时支架支撑脚手架，以便他们修理屋顶。|近义词：scaffolding, support, framework, trestle|反义词：permanent structure, fixed support
