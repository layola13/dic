scrupulist:/ˈskruːpjʊlɪst/| n. |一丝不苟的人；非常谨慎小心的人 |A scrupulous person; someone who is very careful and precise in their actions and decisions, often to an extreme degree.  They may be overly concerned with details and moral considerations. |例句：He was such a scrupulist that he checked and rechecked his work multiple times before submitting it. 他是一个一丝不苟的人，在提交作业之前，他会反复检查好几次。


例句：Despite being a scrupulist, she sometimes missed minor details. 尽管她是一个一丝不苟的人，但她有时还是会错过一些小细节。


近义词：perfectionist, stickler, meticulist |反义词：slovenly, careless, negligent |词性:n.
