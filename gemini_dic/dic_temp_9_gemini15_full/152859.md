pedagogy:/ˈpɛdəɡɑːdʒi/|/ˈpɛdəɡɒdʒi/| n. |教学法；教育学|The art, science, or profession of teaching.  Pedagogy encompasses the strategies, methods, and approaches used to facilitate learning.  It considers the learner's needs, the subject matter, and the learning environment. |Example: The professor's pedagogy emphasized interactive learning and student collaboration.|近义词：teaching, education, didactics|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to concepts opposing effective teaching, such as neglect or disengagement.
