condignly:/kənˈdaɪnli/| adv.|应得地；适当地；恰如其分地 |  Deserving or receiving punishment that is considered to be fair and appropriate for the offense committed.  It emphasizes the deserved nature of the punishment or reward. |例句：He was condignly punished for his crimes. 他因罪行而受到应得的惩罚。

The criminal was condignly sentenced to life imprisonment for his heinous acts. 这个罪犯因其令人发指的行为而被判处了应得的无期徒刑。|近义词：deservedly, justly, rightfully, appropriately |反义词：unjustly, undeservedly, inappropriately |副词
