megabucks:/ˈmɛɡəbʌks/| n. |巨款，百万美元|A large sum of money, typically in the millions of dollars.  It's an informal term.|例句：He made megabucks from his latest invention. 他从他的最新发明中赚了巨款。

例句：Winning the lottery meant megabucks for the lucky winner.  赢得彩票意味着幸运的赢家获得了巨款。


近义词：millions, fortune, a lot of money, big money

反义词：pennies, small change, broke


词性：名词
