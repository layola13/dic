cirques:/ˈsɜːrkz/| n.|冰斗；圏谷|A cirque is a bowl-shaped, amphitheatre-like valley formed by glacial erosion.  It's characterized by steep, often almost vertical, headwalls and a relatively flat floor.  Cirques are typically found high in mountainous regions.| Example: The hikers admired the stunning view from the bottom of the cirque.|近义词：corrie, cwm|反义词：无
