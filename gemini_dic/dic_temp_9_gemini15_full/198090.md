shoo:/ʃuː/| int. v.|嘘；赶走；驱赶|To make a hissing sound to frighten or drive away someone or something.|例句：Shoo! Get out of here! 嘘！滚出去！|例句：The farmer shooed the chickens into the coop. 农夫把鸡赶进鸡舍。|近义词：chase, drive away, scare away|反义词：welcome, invite


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I shoo the birds away from my garden. = 我把鸟从我的花园里赶走。
He shoos the cat out of the house every morning. = 他每天早上把猫赶出房子。


一般过去时:
I shooed the flies away from the food. = 我把苍蝇从食物上赶走了。
She shooed the children away from the street. = 她把孩子们从街上赶走了。


现在完成时:
I have shooed away many birds from my garden this year. = 今年我已经从我的花园里赶走了许多鸟。
She has shooed the dog out of the room several times. = 她已经几次把狗赶出房间了。


过去完成时:
I had shooed the cat out before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，我已经把猫赶出去了。
She had shooed away all the insects before she started cooking. = 在她开始做饭之前，她已经把所有的昆虫都赶走了。


将来时:
I will shoo the pigeons away from the park bench. = 我会把鸽子从公园长椅上赶走。
He will shoo the stray dog away from his property. = 他会把流浪狗从他的财产赶走。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a mouse, I would shoo it away. = 如果我看到一只老鼠，我会把它赶走。
I wish I could shoo away all my worries. = 我希望我能赶走我所有的烦恼。


被动语态:
The birds were shooed away by the gardener. = 鸟儿被园丁赶走了。
The cat was shooed out of the house by the owner. = 猫被主人赶出了房子。


疑问句:
Will you shoo the dog away? = 你会把狗赶走吗？
Did you shoo the bees away from the picnic? = 你把蜜蜂从野餐上赶走了吗？


否定句:
I didn't shoo the cat away. = 我没有把猫赶走。
He doesn't shoo the birds away from his garden. = 他不把鸟从他的花园里赶走。


PHRASE->
shoo away = 赶走
shoo off = 赶走
shoo...out = 把...赶出去
give sb a shoo =  (非正式) 赶走某人
shooing away = 赶走
