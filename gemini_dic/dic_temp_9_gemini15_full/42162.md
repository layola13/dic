couchless: /ˈkaʊtʃləs/ | adj. | 无沙发的；没有沙发的 | Lacking a couch or sofa.  |The apartment was small and couchless, forcing us to sit on the floor.  这套公寓很小，而且没有沙发，迫使我们只能坐在地板上。|  unfurnished, without seating | furnished, with seating |
