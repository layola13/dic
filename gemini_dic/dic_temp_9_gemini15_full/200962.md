sliver:/ˈslɪvər/| n.|细条，薄片，碎片|A sliver is a small, thin piece of something, often broken off from a larger piece.  It suggests something long and narrow, like a splinter.|例句：A sliver of moon peeked through the clouds.  一弯新月透过云层露出。

例句：He got a sliver in his finger. 他手指里扎进了一根木刺。


近义词：fragment, shard, splinter, chip|反义词：chunk, mass, whole|名词
