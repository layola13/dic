mod:/mɒd/ /mɑːd/| n., v., adj.| 模块；修改；时尚的；适度的 |  A unit of a larger system; to modify or change something; stylish; moderate. |例句：The game has several downloadable mods.  这个游戏有几个可下载的模组。|近义词：unit, component, alter, adjust, trendy, fashionable |反义词：original, unmodified, unfashionable, extreme


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mods the game with new weapons. 他用新武器修改游戏。
This mod adds new features to the software. 这个模块为软件添加了新功能。


一般过去时:
She modded her car to increase its speed. 她改装了她的车以提高速度。
They modded the website to improve its design. 他们修改了网站以改进其设计。


现在完成时:
He has modded his computer to improve its performance. 他已经改装了他的电脑以提高其性能。
We have modded the game to remove some bugs. 我们已经修改了游戏以消除一些错误。


过去完成时:
They had modded the server before the tournament started. 比赛开始前他们已经修改了服务器。
She had modded her dress before the party. 派对前她已经修改了她的衣服。


将来时:
He will mod the engine to make it more powerful. 他将改装发动机使其更强大。
They will mod the script to fix the error. 他们将修改脚本以修复错误。


虚拟语气:
If I modded my car, it would be faster. 如果我改装我的车，它会更快。
I wish I had modded my computer earlier. 我真希望我早点改装我的电脑。


被动语态:
The game was modded by a talented player. 游戏由一名有天赋的玩家修改。
The software is being modded to improve its security. 软件正在被修改以提高其安全性。


疑问句:
Did you mod the game yourself? 你自己修改的游戏吗？
Has the website been modded recently? 网站最近被修改了吗？


否定句:
He didn't mod the car. 他没有改装那辆车。
She hasn't modded the software yet. 她还没有修改软件。
