orangerie:/ɔːrənˈdʒəri/| n. |柑橘园；温室；栽种柑橘类的温室|A building, usually a greenhouse, for growing orange and other citrus trees.  Orangeries are often elaborate structures, used as extensions of grand houses.|The orangerie was filled with the sweet scent of blossoming orange trees.  这座柑橘园里充满了盛开的橘树的甜香。|conservatory, greenhouse|—|
