settledly:/ˈsɛtəldli/| adv.| 安定地；平静地；坚决地 | In a settled or calm manner; resolutely.  The adverb "settledly" implies a state of being established, calm, and unwavering. It suggests a firm and resolute manner, free from doubt or uncertainty. |例句：He settledly refused the offer. 他坚决地拒绝了这个提议。

例句：She spoke settledly, her voice betraying no emotion. 她平静地说话，声音听不出任何情绪。


近义词：calmly, peacefully, resolutely, firmly, steadfastly

反义词：restlessly, nervously, hesitantly, uncertainly


词性: 副词
