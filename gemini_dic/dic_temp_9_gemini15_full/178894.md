redbelly:/ˈrɛdˌbɛli/| adj. n.|红腹的；红腹动物|Having a red belly.  This term is often used to describe animals, particularly fish and amphibians, with red coloration on their undersides. |例句：The redbelly dace is a small, colorful fish. 红腹笛鲷是一种小型彩色鱼类。  The frog's redbelly was a warning sign. 青蛙的红肚子是一个警告信号。|近义词：rufous-bellied, red-underbellied|反义词：pale-bellied, white-bellied
