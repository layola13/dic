limelights:/ˈlaɪmlaɪts/| n.|聚光灯；公众瞩目 |The intense focus of public attention; the state of being famous and in the public eye.|例句：She thrived in the limelight. 她喜欢成为众人瞩目的焦点。  |例句：The band enjoyed the limelight after their hit song.  乐队凭借他们的热门歌曲享受着聚光灯的照耀。|近义词：spotlight, public attention, fame, celebrity|反义词：obscurity, anonymity
