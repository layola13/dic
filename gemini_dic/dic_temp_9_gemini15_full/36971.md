colter:/ˈkɒltər/| n.|犁刀；割草机刀片|A colter is a knife-like blade attached to a plow or other agricultural implement, used to cut the sod before the plowshare.  It helps to cleanly slice through the soil, reducing friction and improving the plow's efficiency.|例句：The farmer sharpened the colter on his plow before starting the field work.  这位农民在开始田间工作之前磨快了犁上的犁刀。


The old tractor's colter was worn and needed replacing.  那台旧拖拉机的犁刀磨损了，需要更换。|近义词：cutter, blade|反义词:None.
