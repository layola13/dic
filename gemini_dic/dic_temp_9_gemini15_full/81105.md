grammarian:/ɡræˈmɛəriən/ | n. |语法学家，语法专家 | A grammarian is an expert in grammar, the study of the structure and use of language.  They often analyze and describe the rules of language, and sometimes prescribe how language should be used. | Example:  The grammarian meticulously corrected the errors in the manuscript. 语法学家一丝不苟地纠正了手稿中的错误。


The renowned grammarian published a new textbook on English syntax. 著名的语法学家出版了一本新的英语句法教科书。


近义词:linguist, philologist | 反义词:None that directly contradicts the meaning.  Perhaps "illiterate" could be considered an indirect antonym in the context of language expertise.


词性:名词
