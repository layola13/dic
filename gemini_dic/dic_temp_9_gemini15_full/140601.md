olives:/ˈɒlɪvz/ /ˈɑːlɪvz/| n.|橄榄|The fruit of the olive tree, typically oval and dark purple when ripe, used for oil and eaten as food.  Olives are a common ingredient in Mediterranean cuisine.|例句：We enjoyed a delicious Greek salad with feta cheese and olives. 我们享用了一份美味的希腊沙拉，里面有菲达奶酪和橄榄。|例句：She added some green olives to her pizza. 她往披萨上加了一些绿橄榄。|近义词：olives (different varieties)|反义词：无
