deportee:/ˌdɛpɔːrˈtiː/ | n. |被驱逐出境者 | A person who has been forced to leave a country, typically their own, against their will.  |Example: The deportee was sent back to his homeland with nothing but the clothes on his back.  被驱逐出境者身无分文，只穿着身上的衣服被送回祖国。|近义词：expellee, exile |反义词：immigrant, resident |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government deports illegal immigrants. = 政府驱逐非法移民。
He is a deportee from Syria. = 他是来自叙利亚的被驱逐出境者。


一般过去时:
They deported him last week. = 他们上周将他驱逐出境了。
The country deported thousands of refugees. = 该国驱逐了数千名难民。


现在完成时:
The authorities have deported many undocumented workers. = 当局已经驱逐了许多无证工人。
Several families have been deported since the new law was enacted. = 自从新法律颁布以来，已经有几户人家被驱逐出境。


过去完成时:
The court had already deported him before his appeal was heard. = 在他的上诉被审理之前，法院已经将他驱逐出境了。
They had been deported several times before they finally settled in a new country. = 他们在最终在一个新的国家定居之前已经被驱逐出境过几次了。


将来时:
They will deport him if he doesn't have the correct visa. = 如果他没有正确的签证，他们将驱逐他。
The government is expected to deport more people next year.  = 预计明年政府将驱逐更多的人。


虚拟语气:
If he were a citizen, he wouldn't be deported. = 如果他是公民，他就不会被驱逐出境。
I wish they hadn't deported my family. = 我希望他们没有驱逐我的家人。


被动语态:
He was deported for violating immigration laws. = 他因违反移民法而被驱逐出境。
Many refugees are being deported back to their war-torn homeland. = 许多难民正被遣返回他们饱受战争蹂躏的家园。


疑问句:
Has he been deported yet? = 他已经被驱逐出境了吗？
Will they be deported? = 他们会被驱逐出境吗？


否定句:
He was not deported. = 他没有被驱逐出境。
They did not deport him immediately. = 他们没有立即驱逐他。
