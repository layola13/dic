hornbill:/ˈhɔːrnˌbɪl/| n. |犀鸟|A hornbill is a bird with a large, often brightly colored bill, sometimes with a casque (a bony growth) on top.  They are found in tropical regions of Africa and Asia.  Many species are known for their elaborate courtship rituals and nesting behavior, often involving the female sealing herself into a tree hollow to incubate her eggs.| The hornbill's impressive beak is used for foraging and displays.|近义词：None directly comparable, but some might use "bird" as a general term|反义词：None|名词
