skied:/skiːd/| v.|滑雪；滑行|To move over snow or ice on skis.|例句：We skied down the mountain. 我们从山上滑雪下来了。 I skied for the first time last winter. 我去年冬天第一次滑雪。|近义词：sledded, slid|反义词：walked, climbed.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skis every weekend. = 他每个周末都滑雪。
They ski down the slopes with great speed. = 他们以很快的速度滑下斜坡。

一般过去时:
I skied in the Alps last year. = 我去年在阿尔卑斯山滑雪。
She skied all day long. = 她整天都在滑雪。

现在完成时:
He has skied since he was a child. = 他从小就滑雪。
They have skied many times in this resort. = 他们在该度假村滑雪过很多次。

过去完成时:
We had skied for hours before we stopped for lunch. = 我们滑雪几个小时后才停下来吃午饭。
She had skied several times before this trip. = 在这次旅行之前她已经滑雪好几次了。

将来时:
I will ski in Colorado next winter. = 我明年冬天将在科罗拉多州滑雪。
They will ski tomorrow if the weather is good. = 如果天气好，他们明天将去滑雪。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ski more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常滑雪。
I wish I could ski like a professional. = 我希望我能像职业选手一样滑雪。

被动语态:
The slope is skied by many people every day. = 这条斜坡每天都被很多人滑。 (This example is less common for this verb)
The race was skied very fast. = 这场比赛滑雪速度很快。(This example is less common for this verb)

疑问句:
Did you ski yesterday? = 你昨天滑雪了吗？
Have you ever skied before? = 你以前滑过雪吗？

否定句:
I didn't ski last winter. = 我去年冬天没滑雪。
They haven't skied this season yet. = 他们这个赛季还没滑雪。


PHRASE->
cross-country skiing = 越野滑雪
downhill skiing = 速降滑雪
water skiing = 滑水
ski jumping = 跳台滑雪
ski patrol = 滑雪巡逻队
