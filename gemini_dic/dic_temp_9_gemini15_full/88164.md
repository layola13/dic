hillock:/ˈhɪlək/| n. |小丘，土堆 | A hillock is a small hill or mound.  It is a gently rounded elevation of land, smaller than a mountain or hill. |例句：A small hillock rose gently from the plain.  一个小土堆从平原上轻轻升起。
例句： We rested on the top of the hillock, enjoying the view. 我们在小丘顶上休息，欣赏景色。|近义词：knoll, mound, hummock |反义词：plain, valley
