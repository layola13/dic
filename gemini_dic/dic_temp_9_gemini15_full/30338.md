censusing:/ˈsɛnsɪŋ/ | v. |人口普查；调查；统计 |The act of conducting a census; taking a count or survey of a population or group.  This involves systematically collecting and recording information about individuals or entities within a defined area or group. |例句：The government is censusing the population to determine the number of people in each region. 政府正在进行人口普查以确定每个地区的人口数量。

The wildlife organization is censusing the bird population in the national park.  野生动物组织正在对国家公园的鸟类数量进行调查。|近义词：counting, surveying, polling, enumerating |反义词：ignoring, neglecting
