glutton:/ˈɡlʌtn/| n.|贪吃的人，暴食者|A glutton is a person who eats and drinks excessively.  They are characterized by their excessive consumption of food and drink, often to the point of overindulgence. |He's a glutton for punishment, always volunteering for the hardest jobs.|近义词： gourmand, gormandizer, devourer|反义词：abstainer, ascetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a glutton for sweets. = 他是个嗜甜食的人。
She gluts herself on chocolates every weekend. = 她每个周末都吃很多巧克力。


一般过去时:
He was a glutton in his youth. = 他年轻时是个贪吃的人。
They glutted themselves on the delicious buffet. = 他们尽情享用美味的自助餐。


现在完成时:
He has been a glutton all his life. = 他一辈子都是个贪吃的人。
She has glutted herself on junk food recently. = 她最近吃很多垃圾食品。


过去完成时:
He had been a glutton before he started dieting. = 他在开始节食之前是个贪吃的人。
They had glutted themselves before the main course arrived. = 正餐上来之前，他们已经吃得很饱了。


将来时:
He will be a glutton until he gets sick. = 他会一直贪吃直到生病为止。
They will glut themselves at the upcoming feast. = 他们将在即将到来的盛宴上大吃一顿。


虚拟语气:
If he were not a glutton, he would be healthier. = 如果他不贪吃，他会更健康。
I wish I hadn't glutted myself last night. = 我真希望昨晚我没有吃那么多。


被动语态:
The buffet was glutted by the hungry guests. = 自助餐被饥饿的客人吃光了。
His appetite was glutted with rich desserts. = 他丰富的甜点满足了他的食欲。


疑问句:
Is he a glutton? = 他是个贪吃鬼吗？
Were they glutting themselves on pizza? = 他们当时正在猛吃披萨吗？


否定句:
He is not a glutton, he just enjoys food. = 他不是个贪吃鬼，他只是喜欢食物。
They did not glut themselves at the party. = 他们在派对上并没有吃很多。
