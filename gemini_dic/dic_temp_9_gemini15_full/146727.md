overhonor:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːnər/ | vt. |过分尊敬；过誉|To honor or praise excessively; to give more honor than is deserved.  |例句：The critics overhonored his early work, leading to unrealistic expectations for his later projects. 批评家们对他早期的作品给予了过高的评价，导致人们对他后来的作品抱有不切实际的期望。|例句：Don't overhonor him; his accomplishments are modest. 不要过分赞扬他；他的成就很普通。|近义词：exaggerate, overpraise, laud excessively |反义词：underestimate, belittle, slight |动词
