overmedication:/ˌoʊvərˌmɛdɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |药物过量，用药过度 |The act of taking or administering more medication than is medically necessary or advisable.  It can lead to serious health consequences. |例句：Overmedication is a serious concern for the elderly, often leading to adverse drug reactions. 药物过量是老年人的一个严重问题，常常会导致不良药物反应。

例句：His overmedication contributed to his weakened immune system. 他的药物过量导致了他的免疫系统减弱。|近义词：polypharmacy, drug overdose, excessive medication |反义词：undermedication, appropriate medication |词性:名词
