overproficiently: /ˌoʊvərprəˈfɪʃəntli/ | adv. | 过于熟练地；过于精通地 | Excessively skilled or proficient; to a degree exceeding what is necessary or desirable. | 例句：He overproficiently edited the manuscript, changing the author's intended style. 他过于熟练地编辑了手稿，改变了作者原本的风格。


She overproficiently demonstrated her piano skills, making the simple piece seem overly complex. 她过于熟练地展示了她的钢琴技巧，使得这首简单的乐曲显得过于复杂。


近义词：excessively skillfully, supremely adeptly, masterfully (to excess) | 反义词：clumsily, ineptly, awkwardly


词性: 副词
