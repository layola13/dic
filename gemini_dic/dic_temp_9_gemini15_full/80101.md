gobble-up:/ˈɡɑːbəl ʌp/| vt.|狼吞虎咽；迅速吃光；贪婪地吸收|To eat something very quickly and eagerly, often in large amounts. Also, to consume or absorb something quickly and completely. |例句：The children gobbled up the cookies in a matter of minutes.孩子们几分钟内就狼吞虎咽地吃掉了饼干。

例句：The company gobbled up its smaller competitor in a hostile takeover.这家公司恶意收购了其规模较小的竞争对手。|近义词：devour, gulp, consume, swallow|反义词：nibble, savor, slowly consume|动词


USAGE->

虚拟语气:
