forewarns:/fɔːrˈwɔːrnz/| vt.|预先警告，预先通知|To warn beforehand; to give prior notice of (something impending).|例句：The weather report forewarned us of the approaching storm. 天气预报预先警告我们即将到来的暴风雨。

例句：The teacher forewarned the students about the difficulty of the exam.老师预先警告学生考试的难度。


近义词：advise, alert, caution, notify


反义词：conceal, hide, keep secret


词性：动词
