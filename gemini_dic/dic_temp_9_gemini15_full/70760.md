fineries:/ˈfaɪnəriːz/| n. |精美物品；华丽服饰|Fine things, especially clothes, considered as a group.  The term often implies a sense of luxury and elegance.|例句：She displayed her fineries at the party. =她在派对上展示了她漂亮的衣服。  The museum showcased the fineries of the royal court. =博物馆展出了皇室的精美物品。|近义词：luxuries, adornments, embellishments|反义词：rags, tatters, shabbiness
