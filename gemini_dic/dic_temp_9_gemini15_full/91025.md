hushfully:/ˈhʌʃfli/| adv.|安静地，悄声地，低声地|In a quiet or hushed manner; softly.|例句：She spoke hushfully, barely above a whisper. 她悄声细语，几乎听不见。|例句：He moved hushfully through the darkened room. 他悄悄地穿过昏暗的房间。|近义词：quietly, softly, silently, stealthily|反义词：loudly, boisterously, noisily


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "is," "was," "will be," etc.  It modifies verbs describing actions performed quietly.

一般现在时:
He speaks hushfully. = 他轻声细语。
They walk hushfully down the hallway. = 他们悄悄地走下走廊。

一般过去时:
He spoke hushfully to her. = 他轻声对她说话。
They walked hushfully through the garden. = 他们悄悄地走过花园。

现在完成时:
She has always spoken hushfully in libraries. = 她一直以来在图书馆里都轻声细语。
They have hushfully approached the situation. = 他们已悄然处理这种情况。

过去完成时:
He had hushfully entered the room before anyone noticed. = 他在任何人注意到之前就已悄声进入房间。
They had hushfully prepared for the surprise party. = 他们已悄悄地为惊喜派对做好了准备。

将来时:
She will speak hushfully to avoid disturbing others. = 她将轻声细语以避免打扰他人。
They will hushfully enter the building. = 他们将悄悄进入大楼。

虚拟语气:
If she spoke hushfully, she wouldn't disturb anyone. = 如果她轻声细语，她就不会打扰任何人。
I wish he would hushfully close the door. = 我希望他能够轻轻地关上门。

被动语态:

疑问句:
Did he speak hushfully? = 他是不是轻声细语的？
Were they walking hushfully? = 他们是不是悄悄地走着的？


否定句:
She did not speak hushfully; she shouted. = 她没有轻声细语；她大喊大叫。
They did not walk hushfully; they stomped. = 他们没有悄悄地走；他们跺着脚走。


PHRASE->
There aren't common set phrases using "hushfully."  The word is used to describe the manner of an action.  Examples are shown in the USAGE section.
