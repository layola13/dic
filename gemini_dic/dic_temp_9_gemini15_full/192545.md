scuffed:/skʌft/| v. adj. n.|擦伤；磨损；拖着脚走|to scrape or rub the surface of something, causing minor damage or wear; also, to walk with a shuffling gait |例句：He scuffed his shoes against the wall. 他把鞋蹭在墙上。  The leather shoes were scuffed and worn. 这双皮鞋磨损了并有擦痕。|近义词：scrape, graze, abrade, mar|反义词：polish, shine


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He scuffs his shoes every day. = 他每天都磨损他的鞋子。
They scuff their feet on the carpet. = 他们用脚蹭地毯。

一般过去时:
I scuffed my knee on the pavement. = 我把膝盖擦伤在人行道上。
She scuffed her new shoes walking home. = 她走路回家时磨损了她的新鞋。

现在完成时:
He has scuffed the paintwork on the car. = 他把汽车的油漆蹭掉了。
We have scuffed our shoes on the gravel path. = 我们在碎石小路上磨损了鞋子。

过去完成时:
I had scuffed my favourite boots before the party. = 在派对之前我就把我的最喜欢的靴子磨损了。
They had scuffed the floor badly before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经把地板磨损得很厉害了。

将来时:
She will scuff her shoes if she walks on that rough ground. = 如果她在那种粗糙的地面上行走，她会磨损她的鞋子。
They will probably scuff the furniture if they are not careful. = 如果他们不小心，可能会把家具磨损。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't scuffed my shoes, they would look brand new. = 如果我没有磨损我的鞋子，它们看起来会崭新如初。
I wish I hadn't scuffed the table. = 我真希望我没有磨损桌子。

被动语态:

His shoes were scuffed during the hike. = 他的鞋子在徒步旅行中磨损了。

疑问句:

否定句:

He hasn't scuffed the furniture. = 他没有磨损家具。
