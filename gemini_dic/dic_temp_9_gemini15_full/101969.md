isobathytherm: /ˌaɪsoʊˈbæθəθɜːrm/ | n. | 等深线 (表示水温相同的线) | A line on a map connecting points of equal water temperature at a given depth.  Isobaths are lines connecting points of equal depth.  Isobaths and isobathytherms are used together to understand the three-dimensional structure of water bodies. | The isobathytherm map showed a significant temperature gradient at 100 meters depth. 等深线图显示在100米深处存在明显的温度梯度。

|近义词:| isotherm (for surface water temperatures) |反义词:|  (no direct antonym, but contrasting concepts could be lines of unequal temperature) |词性:|名词|
