plethysmography:/plɛθɪˈzɑːɡrəfi/ | n. |容积描记法，容积描记术 | The measurement of volume changes in an organ or body part.  Plethysmography is used to assess blood volume and circulation.  | Example:  Plethysmography is a valuable tool for diagnosing venous insufficiency.  容积描记法是诊断静脉功能不全的宝贵工具。  |近义词:volumetry,  |反义词: N/A
