pertinately:/ˈpɜːtɪnətli/| adv.| pertinently地；相关地；贴切地 |In a way that is relevant or appropriate to the matter at hand.  It refers to something that is directly related and significant to the topic being discussed.  |例句：He pertinently pointed out the flaws in the argument. 他恰当地指出了论证中的缺陷。

例句：The professor pertinently addressed the students' questions. 教授恰当地回答了学生的问题。


近义词：relevantly, appropriately, aptly, fittingly

反义词：irrelevantly, inappropriately, inappositely


词性：副词
