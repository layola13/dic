reaffirm:/ˌriːəˈfɜːrm/ | vt. | 重申；再次确认；坚定 | To state again or emphasize one's commitment to or belief in something.  |例句：The president reaffirmed his commitment to economic reform. 总统重申了他对经济改革的承诺。|例句：She reaffirmed her belief in the power of love. 她重申了她对爱的力量的信念。|近义词：reassert, reiterate, confirm, restate |反义词：deny, retract, repudiate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reaffirms his support for the project every week. 他每周都重申他对该项目的支持。
They reaffirm their commitment to the agreement annually. 他们每年都重申他们对协议的承诺。

一般过去时:
She reaffirmed her position on the issue during the debate.  她在辩论中重申了她对这个问题的立场。
The government reaffirmed its commitment to environmental protection after the disaster. 灾难过后，政府重申了其对环境保护的承诺。

现在完成时:
They have reaffirmed their intention to invest in the company.  他们已经重申了他们投资该公司的意图。
We have reaffirmed our dedication to providing quality service to our customers. 我们已经重申了我们致力于为客户提供优质服务的决心。

过去完成时:
He had reaffirmed his loyalty to the king before the rebellion.  叛乱前，他已经向国王重申了他的忠诚。
They had reaffirmed their friendship after years of separation.  多年分离后，他们重申了彼此的友谊。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he reaffirmed his position, the situation might improve. 如果他重申他的立场，情况可能会好转。
I wish he had reaffirmed his commitment earlier. 我真希望他早点重申他的承诺。

被动语态:
His commitment to the project was reaffirmed by the board.  他的对该项目的承诺得到了董事会的再次确认。

否定句:
