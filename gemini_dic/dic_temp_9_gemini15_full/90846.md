hung out:/hʌŋ aʊt/| v.|闲逛，消磨时间|To spend time in a relaxed way, often with friends.|例句：We hung out at the mall all afternoon. 我们整个下午都在商场闲逛。  例句：They hung out at the park, chatting and laughing. 他们在公园里闲逛，聊天和大笑。|近义词：relax, socialize, spend time, chill|反义词：work, be busy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
We hang out every weekend. = 我们每个周末都一起出去玩。
They hang out at the coffee shop. = 他们经常在咖啡店闲逛。

一般过去时:
I hung out with my friends last night. = 我昨晚和朋友们一起出去玩了。
We hung out by the beach. = 我们在海滩边闲逛。

现在完成时:
I haven't hung out with them in ages. = 我好久没和他们一起出去玩了。
They have hung out at that bar many times. = 他们在那家酒吧闲逛过很多次了。

过去完成时:
Before the movie, we had hung out at the arcade. = 在看电影之前，我们去游戏厅玩了一会。
They had hung out for hours before deciding what to do. = 他们在决定做什么之前已经闲逛了好几个小时了。

将来时:
We will hang out at the park tomorrow. = 我们明天将在公园里闲逛。
They are going to hang out after work. = 他们下班后打算一起出去玩。

虚拟语气:
If I had more free time, I would hang out more often. = 如果我有更多空闲时间，我会更经常地出去玩。
I wish I could hang out with them tonight. = 我真希望今晚能和他们一起出去玩。

被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Do you want to hang out later? = 你晚点想一起出去玩吗？
Where do they usually hang out? = 他们通常在哪里闲逛？


否定句:
I didn't hang out last night. = 我昨晚没有出去玩。
They don't hang out very often. = 他们不经常一起出去玩。


PHRASE->
hang out with = 和...一起闲逛
hang out at = 在...闲逛
hang out around = 在...附近闲逛
check out = 看看
hang loose = 放松
