cinque:/ˈsɪŋkweɪ/| n. |五；五重奏|Five; a group of five, especially in music a five-part composition or ensemble.  The word comes from the Italian word for "five".|Example: The composer wrote a beautiful cinque for strings.  作曲家为弦乐器写了一首优美的五重奏。|近义词：five, quintet|反义词：无
