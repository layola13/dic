reserves:/rɪˈzɜːrvz/| n. |储备；储量；保留地；预订；|The country has large oil reserves.  这个国家拥有大量的石油储备。|近义词：stock, supply, store |反义词：expenditure, consumption


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reserves the right to change its prices. = 公司保留更改价格的权利。
We reserve a table for dinner. = 我们预订了一张晚餐桌子。


一般过去时:
They reserved a room at the hotel. = 他们在酒店预订了一个房间。
The government reserved a large area for a national park. = 政府为国家公园保留了一大片区域。


现在完成时:
She has reserved a seat on the flight. = 她已经预订了航班上的一个座位。
We have reserved enough money for the trip. = 我们已经为这次旅行储备了足够的钱。


过去完成时:
He had reserved a ticket before the tickets sold out. = 在票卖完之前，他已经预订了一张票。
They had reserved a large amount of grain for the winter. = 他们已经为冬天储备了大量的粮食。


将来时:
They will reserve a suite for their honeymoon. = 他们将预订一个套房度蜜月。
The company will reserve funds for research and development. = 公司将为研发保留资金。


虚拟语气:
If we had reserved a table, we wouldn't be standing in line. = 如果我们预订了桌子，我们就不会排队了。
I wish I had reserved a ticket earlier. = 我真希望我早点订票。


被动语态:
A table was reserved for us. = 为我们预订了一张桌子。
Sufficient funds were reserved for the project. = 为该项目保留了足够的资金。


疑问句:
Have you reserved a hotel room? = 你预订了酒店房间了吗？
Were the seats reserved? = 座位预订了吗？


否定句:
They didn't reserve a car for the trip. = 他们没有为这次旅行预订汽车。
We haven't reserved enough time for the project. = 我们没有为这个项目预留足够的时间。


PHRASE->

natural reserves 自然保护区
