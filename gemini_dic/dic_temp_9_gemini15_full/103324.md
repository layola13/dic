jiggled:/ˈdʒɪɡld/| vt.|轻微摇晃；抖动|To move or shake something slightly and repeatedly.|The baby jiggled her rattle.  婴儿摇晃着她的拨浪鼓。|He jiggled the doorknob, trying to open the door. 他轻轻地摇动门把手，试图打开门。|shook, wobbled|still, stable

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jiggles the handle. = 他摇动把手。
She jiggles her foot nervously. = 她紧张地抖动着脚。

一般过去时:
I jiggled the car keys. = 我摇晃了车钥匙。
He jiggled the loose tooth. = 他晃动那颗松动的牙齿。

现在完成时:
She has jiggled the machine all day. = 她整天都在摇晃那台机器。
The baby has jiggled the rattle until it broke. = 婴儿摇晃拨浪鼓直到它坏了。


过去完成时:
I had jiggled the door handle before realizing it was locked. = 我在意识到门锁上了之前已经摇晃了门把手。
She had jiggled the mobile for hours before it finally fell. = 她摇晃了手机好几个小时，它才终于掉下来。


将来时:
I will jiggle the lever to start the machine. = 我将摇动杠杆启动机器。
He will jiggle the loose tile until it comes off. = 他会晃动那块松动的瓷砖直到它脱落。


虚拟语气:
If I jiggled it harder, it might open. = 如果我用力摇晃它，它可能会打开。
I wish I hadn't jiggled the table so much; now the vase has fallen. = 我真希望我没有那么用力地摇晃桌子；现在花瓶掉下来了。


被动语态:
The handle was jiggled repeatedly. = 门把手被反复摇晃。
The machine was jiggled until it broke. = 机器被摇晃直到它坏了。


疑问句:
Did you jiggle the door before you kicked it? = 你踢门之前有摇晃过门吗？
Has anyone jiggled the window lately? = 最近有人摇晃过窗户吗？


否定句:
I didn't jiggle the box; it fell on its own. = 我没有摇晃盒子；它是自己掉下来的。
He didn't jiggle the steering wheel; he just gripped it tightly. = 他没有摇动方向盘；他只是紧紧地握着它。
