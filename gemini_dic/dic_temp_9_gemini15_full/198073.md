shogunates:/ˈʃoʊɡənˌeɪts/| n.|幕府时代，将军统治时期|The system of government in Japan from 1192 to 1868, characterized by the rule of the shogun.|例句：The Edo shogunate lasted for over 250 years. 江户幕府持续了250多年。 The Kamakura shogunate marked a significant shift in Japanese political power.镰仓幕府标志着日本政治力量的重大转变。|近义词：military dictatorship, feudal system|反义词：democracy, republic|名词
