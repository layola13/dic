locate:/loʊˈkeɪt/| /ˈləʊkeɪt/ | vt.| 定位；找出；位于 | To find the exact place or position of something or someone.  |例句：Police were able to locate the missing child.  警方成功找到了失踪的孩子。|例句：The factory is located in the suburbs.  工厂位于郊区。|近义词：find, discover, pinpoint, situate |反义词：misplace, lose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They locate the problem quickly. = 他们很快找到了问题。
We locate the missing files in the cloud. = 我们在云端找到了丢失的文件。

一般过去时:
The police located the suspect yesterday. = 警方昨天找到了嫌疑人。
I located the book on the top shelf. = 我在顶层书架上找到了那本书。

现在完成时:
They have located the source of the leak. = 他们已经找到了泄漏的源头。
We have located several potential investors. = 我们已经找到了几个潜在的投资者。

过去完成时:
They had located the missing documents before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经找到了丢失的文件。
I had located the right address before I left home. = 我出门前已经找到了正确的地址。

将来时:
We will locate the missing piece soon. = 我们很快就会找到丢失的那块零件。
They will locate the best candidate for the job. = 他们将找到这个职位的最佳人选。

虚拟语气:
If we had a map, we could locate the treasure. = 如果我们有地图，就能找到宝藏。
I wish I could locate my lost keys. = 我真希望我能找到我丢失的钥匙。

被动语态:
The missing plane was finally located. = 失踪的飞机最终找到了。
The problem will be located and solved soon. = 问题很快就会被找到并解决。

疑问句:
Can you locate the information you need? = 你能找到你需要的信息吗？
Where is the library located? = 图书馆在哪里？

否定句:
I couldn't locate the file on my computer. = 我在我的电脑上找不到那个文件。
They failed to locate the witness. = 他们没能找到证人。


PHRASE->
locate something/someone = 找到某物/某人
locate oneself = 位于；安顿下来
be located in/at/on = 位于……
locate a problem = 找到问题
locate resources = 寻找资源
