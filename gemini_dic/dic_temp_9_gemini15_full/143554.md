outhearing: /ˌaʊtˈhɪərɪŋ/ | v. |无意中听到；偷听到 |  The act of hearing something unintentionally or secretly.  It implies a lack of intention to listen, often suggesting the information was meant to be private. | 例句：I outheard them arguing about the project. 我无意中听到他们在争论项目。  | 例句：She outheard a conversation about her in the hallway. 她无意中听到走廊里有人谈论她。| 近义词：overhearing, eavesdropping | 反义词：intentionally listening
