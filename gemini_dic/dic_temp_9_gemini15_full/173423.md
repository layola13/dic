quasi-envious:/ˈkwɑːziˈɛnviəs/ | adj. |略微嫉妒的，似嫉妒的 |  Feeling or showing a slight or qualified envy; almost envious but not quite.  It suggests a milder form of envy, perhaps tinged with admiration or other emotions. | Example:  He felt a quasi-envious admiration for her success. 他对她成功的成就感到一丝嫉妒的钦佩。  She felt a quasi-envious longing for their carefree lifestyle. 她对他们无忧无虑的生活方式感到一丝嫉妒的渴望。|近义词：slightly envious, mildly jealous |反义词：uninterested, indifferent
