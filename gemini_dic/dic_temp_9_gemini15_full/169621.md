propitiable:/ˈprɒpɪʃəbəl/| adj.| 可求得宽恕的；可抚慰的；易于劝说的 | Easily appeased or placated; capable of being propitiated.  It suggests a willingness to forgive or be reconciled. |例句：The angry god was, surprisingly, propitiable after a generous sacrifice.  愤怒的神，令人惊讶的是，在丰厚的祭祀之后变得容易宽恕了。

例句：After much pleading, the stern judge proved propitiable to the defendant's remorseful plea.  经过多次恳求，严厉的法官最终被被告悔恨的请求所打动。


近义词：appeasable, placable, amenable, conciliable

反义词：inexorable, implacable, unappeasable


词性：形容词
