filially: /ˈfɪliəli/ | adj. |孝顺的；子女应尽的 | Relating to or characteristic of a son or daughter; showing filial piety.  |例句：She showed filial piety by caring for her aging parents. 她通过照顾年迈的父母表现出孝顺。  He fulfilled his filial duties by supporting his family. 他通过赡养家人履行了他作为子女的责任。|近义词：dutiful, obedient, respectful |反义词：disobedient, disrespectful, undutiful |形容词

词源:
From Latin *filiālis, from fīlius "son".  The word reflects the historical and cultural emphasis on the relationship between children and parents, particularly the duties and respect owed by children to their parents.
