overcreep:/ˈoʊvərkriːp/| v.|过度蔓延，逐渐侵蚀|To spread or extend gradually beyond a limit or boundary.  The term often refers to geological processes, but can also be used metaphorically.|例句：The glacier slowly overcrept the valley floor. 冰川慢慢地蔓延到山谷底部。|例句：The influence of social media has overcrept into every aspect of our lives. 社交媒体的影响已经逐渐渗透到我们生活的方方面面。|近义词：encroach, creep, spread, extend|反义词：recede, contract, shrink|动词
