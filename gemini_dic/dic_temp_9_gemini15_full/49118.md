deinternationalizing: /ˌdiɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 去国际化；使脱离国际联系 | The process of reducing or reversing the integration of a country's economy or society with the global system.  This can involve policies aimed at protecting domestic industries, limiting foreign investment, or reducing participation in international organizations. |例句：The country is deinternationalizing its economy by imposing high tariffs on imported goods. = 该国通过对进口商品征收高额关税来使其经济去国际化。


例句：The government's new policies are seen by many as a step towards deinternationalizing the country's trade relations. = 政府的新政策被许多人视为该国贸易关系去国际化的一个步骤。


近义词：globalization, internationalization | 反义词:  globalizing, internationalizing


词性: 动词
