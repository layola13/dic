overtilt:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪlt/| vt.|倾斜过度；过度倾斜|To tilt or lean something beyond its normal or intended angle; to cause something to be excessively tilted.|例句：The strong wind overtilted the sailboat, causing it to capsize.  强风使帆船过度倾斜，导致其倾覆。  例句：He overtilted the picture frame, and it broke. 他把相框倾斜过度，结果把它弄坏了。|近义词：overturn, capsize, upset|反义词：right, straighten
