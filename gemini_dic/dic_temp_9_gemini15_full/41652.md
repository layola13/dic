corregidor:/ˌkɔːrɪˈdɔːr/|/ˈkɔːrɪdʒɔːr/| n.|科雷希多尔岛；纠察官|A corregidor was a high-ranking official in Spanish colonial government, typically in charge of a city or province.  The word also refers to a specific island in the Philippines.|例句：The corregidor of Manila held significant power. 马尼拉的科雷希多尔长官拥有重要的权力。
The island of Corregidor played a crucial role in World War II. 科雷希多尔岛在二战中扮演了至关重要的角色。|近义词：governor, magistrate, administrator|反义词：subject, subordinate
