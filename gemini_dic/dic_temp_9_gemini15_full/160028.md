polytechnical:/ˌpɒliˈtɛknɪkəl/ /ˌpɑːliˈtɛknɪkəl/| adj. |理工科的；多技术的；多专业的| Relating to or involving many different branches of technology.  The word combines "poly" (many) and "technical" (relating to technology or skills). It often describes institutions or education that encompass a broad range of technical subjects.|The polytechnical institute offered courses in engineering, computer science, and design.|近义词：multidisciplinary, technological, technical|反义词：specialized, single-discipline
