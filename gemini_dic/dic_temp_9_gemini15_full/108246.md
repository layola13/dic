laughworthy: /ˈlæfˌwɜːrði/ | adj. | 值得笑的，好笑的 |  Something that is laughworthy is amusing and likely to make people laugh. It suggests that the humor is obvious and readily apparent.  | Example:  His attempt to cook was quite laughworthy.  他的烹饪尝试相当好笑。  | 近义词: funny, hilarious, amusing, comical | 反义词: serious, dull, boring, uninteresting
