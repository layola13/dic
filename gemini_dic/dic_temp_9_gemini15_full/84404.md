halloing:/hɑːˈloʊɪŋ/| v. |大声叫喊；呼喊|The act of shouting or calling out loudly.  It often implies a repeated or prolonged shout, unlike a single, brief call.|例句：The children were halloing for their mother.孩子们大声呼喊着他们的母亲。
The sailors were halloing across the water. 水手们在水面上大声呼喊。|近义词：shouting, yelling, calling, bawling |反义词：whispering, murmuring
