pips:/pɪps/| n.|少量，一点儿；鸟喙；(股票或商品交易中的)最小价格变动单位；种子| a small seed; a small amount; the smallest price movement of a stock or commodity|例句：He only had a few pips left. 他只剩下几粒种子了。|近义词：seeds, bits, points|反义词：abundance, plenty
