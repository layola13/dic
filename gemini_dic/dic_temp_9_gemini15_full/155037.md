petition:/pəˈtɪʃən/| n. v.|请愿；请求；诉状|A formal written request, typically one signed by many people, appealing to authority with respect to a particular cause.|例句：The students submitted a petition to the principal, demanding better facilities. 学生们向校长提交了一份请愿书，要求改善设施。
例句：They are petitioning for the release of political prisoners. 他们正在请愿释放政治犯。|近义词：request, appeal, application|反义词：opposition, denial|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They petition the government for change. = 他们向政府请愿要求改变。
The citizens petition for better public transportation. = 市民们请愿要求改善公共交通。

一般过去时:
The workers petitioned the company for higher wages. = 工人们向公司请愿要求提高工资。
She petitioned the court for a divorce. = 她向法院请求离婚。

现在完成时:
They have petitioned the council several times. = 他们已经向议会请愿好几次了。
We have petitioned for a new school for years. = 我们多年来一直在请愿要求一所新学校。

过去完成时:
They had petitioned the authorities before the incident. = 他们在事件发生前已经向当局请愿。
She had petitioned for a pardon many times. = 她已经多次请求赦免。

将来时:
They will petition the mayor next week. = 他们下周将向市长请愿。
We will petition the government for environmental protection. = 我们将向政府请愿要求环境保护。

虚拟语气:
If they petitioned the court, they might win. = 如果他们向法院请愿，他们可能会胜诉。
I wish they had petitioned the government sooner. = 我希望他们早点向政府请愿。

被动语态:
The petition was rejected by the council. = 该请愿书被议会否决了。
The case was petitioned to the Supreme Court. = 该案件已上诉至最高法院。

疑问句:
Will they petition the government? = 他们会向政府请愿吗？
Have they petitioned the court yet? = 他们已经向法院请愿了吗？

否定句:
They did not petition the government. = 他们没有向政府请愿。
She will not petition the court again. = 她不会再次向法院请愿。


PHRASE->
file a petition = 提交请愿书
sign a petition = 签署请愿书
grant a petition = 批准请愿
deny a petition = 驳回请愿
a petition for = 关于…的请愿


词源:
源自古法语 *peticion，最终源自拉丁语 *petitio，意思是“请求”。
