peperino:/ˌpɛpəˈriːnoʊ/| n. |一种火山岩|A type of volcanic rock, typically a light-colored, porous, and easily worked stone found in central Italy.  It is often used in construction and decorative work.  The exact mineralogical composition can vary, but it generally includes minerals like feldspar, quartz, and volcanic glass. |The Colosseum in Rome is partially built from peperino. 罗马斗兽场部分由佩佩里诺石建造。|travertine, tuff|granite|名词
