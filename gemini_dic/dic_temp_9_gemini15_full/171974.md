purchasers:/ˈpɜːrtʃəsərz/ | n. |购买者，购货人 |People or organizations that buy something.  |Example: The purchasers of the property were a young couple.  购买这处房产的是一对年轻夫妇。 The company is looking for new purchasers for its products. 这家公司正在寻找其产品的新买家。|近义词：buyers, customers, clients |反义词：sellers, vendors
