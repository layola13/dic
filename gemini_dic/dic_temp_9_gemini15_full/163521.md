precorrections:/ˌpriːkəˈrekʃənz/| n.|预先校正，预校|Actions taken before something happens to prevent or reduce errors or problems.  Precorrections are preventative measures aimed at minimizing potential issues.|Example: The editor implemented several precorrections to the manuscript before sending it to the printer. 编辑在将稿件送交印刷厂之前对稿件进行了多次预校正。|近义词：preventive measures, proactive steps, preemptive actions|反义词：corrections, post-corrections
