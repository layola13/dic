counterattacker: /ˌkaʊntərəˈtækər/ | n. | 反击者，还击者 | A person who counterattacks. Someone who responds to an attack with a counterattack.  | Example: The counterattacker skillfully deflected the opponent's blow and launched a devastating counteroffensive.  反击者巧妙地化解了对手的攻击，并发动了毁灭性的反攻。 |近义词:  retaliator, responder, avenger | 反义词: victim, target
