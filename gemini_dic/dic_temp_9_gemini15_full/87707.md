hexapodous:/hɛkˈsɑːpədəs/| adj.|六足的，有六条腿的|Having six legs.  The term is primarily used in zoology and entomology to describe insects and other arthropods.|例句：Ants are hexapodous insects.  蚂蚁是六足昆虫。

例句：The hexapodous creature scuttled across the floor.  那个六条腿的生物在木地板上疾走。


近义词：six-legged


反义词：apodous (without legs), tetrapodous (four-legged)


词性:形容词
