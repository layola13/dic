pursed:/pɜːrst/| v.|噘嘴，紧闭（嘴唇）；追赶；追求|To press one's lips together, especially to express disapproval or displeasure; to pursue something diligently.|例句：She pursed her lips in disapproval. 她噘嘴表示不满。|She pursed her lips and said nothing. 她紧闭嘴唇一言不发。|近义词：compressed, pressed,  pucker|反义词：opened, relaxed
