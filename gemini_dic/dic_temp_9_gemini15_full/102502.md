The term often implies a strong loyalty, sometimes bordering on fanaticism, to the Stuart cause.|The Jacobitish uprising of 1745 was brutally suppressed.  雅各布派在1745年的起义被残酷镇压。|  Bonny, loyalist|Whig, Hanoverian|
