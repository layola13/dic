/priːdɪˈlaɪnieɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先描绘；预先勾勒；预先确定 |  The act of predelineating involves outlining or sketching something in advance, often to serve as a plan or guide for later work. It implies a preliminary definition or marking of boundaries or characteristics before the full development or execution of something.  | The architect spent weeks predelineating the building's structure before beginning construction. 建筑师在开始施工前花了数周时间预先勾勒出建筑物的结构。

The city council predelineated the zones for residential and commercial development.  市议会预先划定了住宅和商业开发区。| delineate, outline, sketch, prefigure |  | v.
