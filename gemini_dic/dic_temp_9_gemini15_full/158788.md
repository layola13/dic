plug-in:/ˈplʌɡɪn/| n.  |插件；插入物|A plug-in is a piece of software that adds a specific feature to an existing computer program or system.  It's designed to be easily added or removed without affecting the core functionality. |Example: I installed a new plug-in to enhance the photo editing capabilities of my software. 我安装了一个新的插件来增强我的软件的图片编辑功能。|近义词：add-on, extension, module |反义词：core component, integral part
