gunnysacks:/ˈɡʌniˌsæks/| n.|麻袋；粗布袋|A gunnysack is a large sack made of burlap or other coarse fabric, typically used for storing or transporting goods.  It's a strong, durable bag often used for agricultural products.|例句：The farmer filled the gunnysacks with potatoes.  农民用麻袋装满了土豆。

例句：They used gunnysacks to transport the harvested wheat. 他们用麻袋运输收割的小麦。


近义词：burlap sack, hessian sack, feed bag|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
