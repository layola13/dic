epispadias:/ˌɛpɪˈspeɪdiəs/ | n. |尿道上裂 | A congenital defect in males in which the urethra opens on the upper surface of the penis instead of at the tip.  In females, it involves an abnormal opening of the urethra on the clitoris or in the upper part of the vagina. | Example: The infant was diagnosed with epispadias at birth and scheduled for corrective surgery.  婴儿出生时被诊断患有尿道上裂，并安排了矫正手术。|近义词：urethral malformation |反义词：hypospadias
