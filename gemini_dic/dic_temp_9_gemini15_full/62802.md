engulfs:/ɪnˈɡʌlfs/ | vt. |吞没；淹没；吞噬| To overwhelm or swallow up completely.  It suggests a process of being surrounded and consumed, often by something overwhelming or destructive. |例句：The flames engulfed the building in minutes.  火焰几分钟内就吞没了整栋建筑物。

The rising tide engulfed the coastal village.  上涨的潮水淹没了沿海村庄。|近义词：swallow, overwhelm, submerge, inundate |反义词：emerge, rescue, save


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The river engulfs the small town during the rainy season. = 雨季时，河流吞没了这个小镇。
Fear engulfs her when she thinks about the future. = 当她想到未来时，恐惧吞噬了她。


一般过去时:
The tsunami engulfed the entire coastline. = 海啸吞噬了整个海岸线。
Doubt engulfed him after he received the bad news. = 在他收到坏消息后，怀疑吞噬了他。


现在完成时:
The forest fire has engulfed thousands of acres. = 森林大火已经吞噬了数千英亩土地。
Grief has engulfed her since her husband's death. = 自从丈夫去世以来，悲伤一直吞噬着她。


过去完成时:
The army had been engulfed by the enemy before reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，军队已经被敌人包围了。
She had already been engulfed in her work before he called. = 他打电话之前，她已经被工作完全占据了。


将来时:
The volcano will engulf the nearby villages if it erupts. = 如果火山爆发，它将吞没附近的村庄。
Anxiety will engulf him before the important presentation. = 在重要的演讲之前，焦虑将会吞噬他。


虚拟语气:
If the flood engulfed the city, many people would lose their homes. = 如果洪水淹没了城市，许多人将失去家园。
I wish the darkness hadn't engulfed my hope. = 我真希望黑暗没有吞噬我的希望。


被动语态:
The small boat was engulfed by the waves. = 小船被海浪吞噬了。
The town was engulfed by the rapidly spreading wildfire. = 城镇被迅速蔓延的野火吞噬了。


疑问句:

Is she engulfed by self-doubt? = 她被自我怀疑所吞噬了吗？


否定句:
The firefighters prevented the fire from engulfing the whole forest. = 消防员阻止了火势蔓延吞噬整个森林。
He tried to keep his emotions from engulfing him completely. = 他试图不让自己的情绪完全吞噬自己。
