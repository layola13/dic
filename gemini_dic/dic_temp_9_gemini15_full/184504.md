restation:/ˌriːˈsteɪʃən/| v.|重新安置，重新设置，恢复原状|To put something back in its original place or condition; to relocate something.|例句：The company decided to restation its employees to different branches. 公司决定将其员工重新安置到不同的分公司。|例句：The technician restationed the server after the power outage. 技术人员在停电后重新设置了服务器。|近义词：relocate, reposition, reinstate|反义词：remove, displace, dislocate


USAGE->

They restation the equipment regularly. = 他们定期重新安置设备。
The company restations its staff every few years. = 公司每隔几年就会重新安排员工。
