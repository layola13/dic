dinosaur: /ˈdaɪnəsɔːr/ | n. | 恐龙 | A reptile of the extinct order Dinosauria, typically very large, that lived during the Mesozoic Era.  Dinosaurs are characterized by their upright posture and three major groups:  saurischia, ornithischia, and theropoda. |例句：Many dinosaur fossils have been discovered in this area.  这个地区发现了许多恐龙化石。

Many different species of dinosaurs roamed the Earth millions of years ago.数百万年前，许多不同种类的恐龙在地球上漫游。


近义词：prehistoric reptile (史前爬行动物) | 反义词：None (没有合适的反义词)
