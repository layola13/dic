selvedged:/ˈsɛlvɪdʒd/| adj.|经纬线织边，织边缝的|Having a finished edge, typically on woven fabric, that prevents unraveling.  Selvedge is a tightly woven edge on a piece of fabric that prevents it from fraying.|例句：This shirt is made of high-quality selvedged denim. 这件衬衫是用高品质的经纬线织边牛仔布制成的。|近义词：finished edge, self-edged|反义词：raw edge, unfinished edge
