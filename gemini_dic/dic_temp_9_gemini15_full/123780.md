mooted:/ˈmuːtɪd/| adj.| 提出的；讨论过的；被争论的 |  A mooted point is one that has been discussed but not yet decided.  It suggests something is under consideration or has been suggested, often without a resolution. | 例句：The proposal to build a new stadium remains mooted.  新建体育馆的提案仍在讨论中。

例句：The idea of raising taxes was mooted at the meeting, but no decision was reached.  会议上提出了增税的建议，但没有达成任何决定。


近义词：debated, discussed, proposed, suggested, considered

反义词：decided, settled, resolved

词性:形容词
