envisaged:/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒd/| v.|预想，设想，想象|To have a mental picture of something that might happen in the future; to conceive of as a possibility.|The architect envisaged a building that would be both functional and aesthetically pleasing.  建筑师设想了一座既实用又美观的建筑。|They envisaged a future where technology would solve all our problems. 他们设想了一个未来，技术将解决我们所有的问题。|imagined, contemplated, foreseen|unforeseen, unplanned


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He envisages a bright future for his company. = 他设想公司有一个光明的未来。
She envisages a world without poverty. = 她设想一个没有贫困的世界。

一般过去时:
They envisaged a different outcome. = 他们预想了一个不同的结果。
We envisaged many challenges ahead. = 我们预想到了许多未来的挑战。

现在完成时:
I have envisaged many scenarios. = 我已经设想了许多情况。
She has envisaged a solution to the problem. = 她已经设想出了解决这个问题的方法。

过去完成时:
They had envisaged a smoother process. = 他们原本设想了一个更顺利的过程。
We had envisaged a much larger crowd. = 我们原本预计会有更多的人。

将来时:
They will envisage new possibilities. = 他们将设想新的可能性。
She will envisage a more sustainable future. = 她将设想一个更可持续的未来。

虚拟语气:
If I had envisaged this, I would have acted differently. = 如果我预想到了这一点，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had envisaged the consequences. = 我真希望我预想到了后果。

被动语态:
A brighter future was envisaged by the government. = 政府设想了一个更光明的未来。
A new strategy was envisaged to address the crisis. = 设想了一个新的策略来应对危机。

疑问句:
What did you envisage happening? = 你预想会发生什么？
What kind of future do you envisage? = 你设想的是什么样的未来？

否定句:
I didn't envisage such a negative reaction. = 我没有预料到如此负面的反应。
They didn't envisage any major problems. = 他们没有预料到任何重大问题。


PHRASE->
envisage a future = 设想未来
envisage a scenario = 设想一种情况
envisage the possibility = 设想可能性
envisage a solution = 设想一个解决方案
envisage the consequences = 预料后果
