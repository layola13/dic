cobbles:/ˈkɒblz/ | n. |鹅卵石路面；鹅卵石 | Small, rounded stones used to pave roads or paths.  Cobbles are typically larger than pebbles and smaller than boulders. They create a characteristic uneven surface. |例句：The old town square was charming with its cobbled streets.  古老的城镇广场，鹅卵石街道别具一番魅力。

例句：We walked along the cobbled pathway leading to the castle. 我们沿着通往城堡的鹅卵石小路走去。


近义词：paving stones, stones, setts | 反义词：smooth pavement, asphalt


词源：源自古英语 *cobble，意为“圆形石头”。
