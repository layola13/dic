multipart:/ˈmʌltiˌpɑːrt/| adj.|多部分的，多部件的|Consisting of or divided into multiple parts.  It often refers to something composed of several separate sections or components.|例句：The multipart form allowed users to upload multiple files simultaneously.  多部分表单允许用户同时上传多个文件。

例句：This multipart project requires collaboration from several teams. 这个多部分项目需要多个团队合作。


近义词：multi-part, composite, compound, many-part


反义词：single-part, unitary, whole


词性：形容词
