lawer:/ˈlɔːjər/| n.|律师|A lawyer is a person who is qualified to advise people on legal matters and represent them in legal proceedings.|例句：My lawyer advised me to plead not guilty. 我的律师建议我认罪不认罚。|近义词：attorney, solicitor, barrister|反义词：client, defendant
