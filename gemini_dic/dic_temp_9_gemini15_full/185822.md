revitalised:/ˌriːˈvaɪtəlaɪzd/| adj.| 恢复活力的；使复兴的；更新的 |  To give new life or energy to something; to renew or refresh.  It implies a restoration to a previous state of vigor or a transformation into a more lively or active condition. |The revitalised city center now boasts new shops and restaurants.  使复兴的城市中心现在拥有新的商店和餐馆。|  renewed, refreshed, rejuvenated | stagnant, lifeless, moribund | 形容词
