reassignations:/ˌriːəˈsaɪnments/ | n. |重新分配；任务重新分配 |The process of assigning something or someone to a different role, task, or position.  It often involves a change in responsibility or function.  |例句：The company announced a series of reassignments within the marketing department. 公司宣布市场部内部进行了一系列的重新分配。

例句：The project manager oversaw the reassignments of tasks to ensure timely completion. 项目经理监督任务的重新分配以确保按时完成。


近义词：redeployments, reallocations, reassignments of duties


反义词：assignments (initial assignment), allocations (initial allocation)


词性：名词
