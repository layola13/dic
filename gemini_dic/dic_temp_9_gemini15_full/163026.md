preclaiming:/prɪˈkleɪmɪŋ/| v. |预告；预先声明；提前宣布 |Announcing or declaring something beforehand.  It implies a formal or public announcement, often of something significant or noteworthy. |例句：The company was preclaiming its new product launch for months before the actual event. 公司在实际活动前几个月就预告了其新产品的发布。

例句：The government preclaimed a state of emergency before the hurricane hit.  政府在飓风来临之前宣布进入紧急状态。


近义词：foretelling, foreshadowing, announcing, proclaiming, heralding


反义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing, keeping secret


词性：动词
