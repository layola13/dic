diddly:/ˈdɪdli/| n.|一点儿也不，微不足道的东西|A very small amount or nothing at all; used to express disbelief or lack of importance.  It's often used informally and can be considered vulgar.|例句：I didn't get diddly for my birthday. = 我生日什么也没得到。  He got diddly-squat out of the deal. = 他从这笔交易中一无所获。|近义词：nothing, zilch, nada, squat|反义词：a lot, much, plenty
