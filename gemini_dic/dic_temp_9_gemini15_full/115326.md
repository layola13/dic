mannish: /ˈmænɪʃ/ | adj. | 男子气的；像男人的；粗鲁的 | Having characteristics traditionally associated with men;  masculine in appearance or behavior.  Often implies a lack of femininity or grace. |

例句：

Her mannish haircut made her look older.  她的男人婆似的发型让她看起来更老了。

She had a mannish way of speaking. 她说话的方式很男性化。


近义词：masculine, tomboyish, butch,  unfeminine

反义词：feminine, womanly, delicate, graceful


词性:形容词
