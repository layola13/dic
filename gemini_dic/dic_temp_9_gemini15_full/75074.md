frigidity:/frɪˈdʒɪdəti/| n.|冷漠；性冷淡；严寒|The state or quality of being frigid; coldness of manner or feeling; lack of warmth or enthusiasm.  It can refer to a lack of sexual desire or responsiveness, or to an extremely cold temperature.|例句：Her frigidity made him feel unwelcome.她的冷漠让他感到不受欢迎。

例句：The frigidity of the Arctic winter is well known.北极冬季的严寒是众所周知的。


近义词：coldness, coldness of manner, unresponsiveness, apathy, indifference|反义词：warmth, passion, ardor, enthusiasm
