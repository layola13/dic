shieldtail:/ˈʃiːldteɪl/| n. |盾尾（某些蜥蜴的名称）|A shieldtail is a type of lizard, typically referring to lizards in the genus *Cordylus*, characterized by their broad, flattened tails that resemble shields. These lizards are known for their defensive behavior, often using their tails to block attacks.  |The shieldtail lizard used its broad tail to protect itself from the predator.  盾尾蜥蜴用它宽大的尾巴保护自己免受捕食者的攻击。|  |  |名词
