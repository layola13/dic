mock:/mɒk/ /mɑːk/| v., n., adj.| 嘲笑；模仿；模拟；伪造的；模拟的 |To make fun of; to imitate, often in a derisive way; to mimic; something designed to imitate something else; a practice exercise or test; something that is not genuine;  |例句：They mocked his efforts.  他们嘲笑他的努力。

例句：The students took a mock exam before the real one. 学生们在真正的考试前进行了一次模拟考试。


近义词：deride, ridicule, imitate, simulate, fake, sham |反义词：praise, commend, genuine, real


词性：动词，名词，形容词
