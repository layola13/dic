hepatitis:/ˌhɛpəˈtaɪtɪs/| n.|肝炎|Inflammation of the liver, typically caused by a viral infection, but also possibly by other factors such as alcohol abuse or autoimmune diseases.|例句：He was diagnosed with hepatitis B. 他被诊断出患有乙型肝炎。|例句：Chronic hepatitis can lead to cirrhosis and liver failure. 慢性肝炎可导致肝硬化和肝功能衰竭。|近义词：liver inflammation, hepatic inflammation|反义词：liver health
