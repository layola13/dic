heart-to-heart:/ˌhɑːrt tə hɑːrt/| adj. |坦诚的；知心的| a frank and open discussion or conversation, especially one in which intimate feelings are expressed.|例句：We had a heart-to-heart talk about our future. 我们进行了一次坦诚的关于我们未来的谈话。|近义词：candid, intimate, frank, open|反义词:superficial, insincere
