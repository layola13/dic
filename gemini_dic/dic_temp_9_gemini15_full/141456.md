opined:/əˈpaɪnd/| vt.|表达意见；认为；主张|To express an opinion; to believe or hold as one's opinion.|例句：The critic opined that the play was a masterpiece. 批评家认为这出戏是一部杰作。|例句：He opined that the new policy would be beneficial. 他认为新政策将是有益的。|近义词：believed, thought, considered, suggested|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be related to the opposite opinion expressed.|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He opines that the economy will improve. = 他认为经济将会好转。
She opines frequently on matters of politics. = 她经常就政治问题发表意见。

一般过去时:
The professor opined that the student's work was exceptional. = 教授认为学生的作业非常出色。
He opined that the solution was impractical. = 他认为这个方案不切实际。

现在完成时:
They have opined on the matter several times. = 他们已经多次就此事发表意见。
She has opined that the project is doomed to fail. = 她认为该项目注定会失败。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, he had opined against the proposal. = 会议之前，他已经反对该提案。
They had opined that the evidence was insufficient. = 他们曾认为证据不足。

将来时:
He will opine on the subject at the conference. = 他将在会议上就此主题发表意见。
She will opine that the new law is unjust. = 她将认为新法律是不公正的。

虚拟语气:
If he had opined differently, the outcome might have been different. = 如果他当时表达不同的意见，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish he had opined more forcefully. = 我希望他当时能更强有力地表达自己的观点。

被动语态:
It was opined by many that the decision was correct. = 许多人认为这个决定是正确的。(较为少见)
The proposal was opined upon extensively before being approved. = 在被批准之前，对该提案进行了广泛的讨论和评价。(较为少见)


疑问句:
What did the expert opine about the situation? = 专家对这种情况有什么看法？
Did anyone opine against the plan? = 有人反对这项计划吗？

否定句:
He did not opine on the matter publicly. = 他没有公开对此事发表意见。
She did not opine that the evidence was conclusive. = 她不认为证据是确凿的。


PHRASE->

opine freely = 自由表达意见
opine cautiously = 谨慎地发表意见
opine with conviction = 坚定地表达意见
