exhibitionistically: /ˌɛksɪbɪˈʃənɪstɪkli/ | adv. |炫耀地，卖弄地 | Acting in a way designed to attract attention.  This adverb describes behavior intended to show off or display oneself in a noticeable and often ostentatious manner.  It implies a desire for admiration or attention, sometimes to an excessive degree. |例句：He dressed exhibitionistically, hoping to impress everyone at the party. 他穿着炫耀的衣服，希望能给派对上的人留下深刻印象。

He behaved exhibitionistically throughout the awards ceremony. 他在整个颁奖典礼上都表现得很炫耀。


近义词：ostentatiously, conspicuously, showily, flamboyantly |反义词：modestly, humbly, inconspicuously


词源：源自名词exhibitionist（展览主义者，爱炫耀的人），加上后缀-ically构成副词。exhibitionist本身来自exhibition（展览，展示）+ -ist (表示人)。
