mercurials:/mɜːˈkjʊəriəlz/| n.|升汞；水银药剂；（尤指）兴奋剂|Preparations containing mercury, formerly used medicinally but now largely obsolete due to their toxicity.  The term can also refer to stimulating or exciting substances, often in a sports context, implying an unfair advantage.|例句：The athlete was disqualified for using mercurials.  这位运动员因使用兴奋剂而被取消资格。|近义词：stimulants, performance enhancers|反义词：depressants
