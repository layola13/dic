rutting:/ˈrʌtɪŋ/| n. v.|发情期；车辙；刻板；陷入困境 |The act of animals, especially deer, during mating season;  the formation of ruts in a road; a fixed or habitual routine; being stuck in a difficult situation.|例句：The rutting season for deer is in the fall.  鹿的发情期在秋季。 The car got stuck in a rut. 车陷入了车辙。 He's fallen into a rut in his job. 他在工作中陷入了困境。|近义词：mating, rut, groove, track, groove,  monotony, stagnation|反义词：novelty, innovation, excitement
