grossification:/ˌɡrɒsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/|/n.|粗化；增大；变粗；(尤指)肥大化|The process of becoming gross or coarser;  the act of making something gross or coarser; especially the enlargement or thickening of something, particularly in the context of biological growth.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting an undesirable increase in size or coarseness.|例句：The grossification of the soil reduced its fertility. 土壤的粗化降低了它的肥力。The grossification of the grain after harvest was a major concern. 收获后谷物的增粗是一个主要问题。|近义词：coarsening, thickening, enlargement, augmentation|反义词：refinement, miniaturization, reduction
