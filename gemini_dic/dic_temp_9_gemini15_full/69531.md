felled:/fɛld/ | vt. | 砍倒；击倒；使…倒下 | To cut down (a tree) or cause (something) to fall to the ground.  | 例句：The lumberjacks felled the giant redwood trees.  伐木工人砍倒了巨大的红杉树。

The strong wind felled the old oak tree.  强风吹倒了那棵古老的橡树。

| 近义词：cut down, knock down, overthrow | 反义词：plant, erect, raise | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fells trees for a living. = 他靠砍树为生。
The boxer fells his opponent with a single punch. = 拳击手一拳击倒了他的对手。

一般过去时:
The storm felled many trees. = 暴风雨吹倒了许多树木。
The weight of the snow felled the power lines. =  雪的重量压断了电线。


现在完成时:
The company has felled thousands of trees this year. =  这家公司今年已经砍伐了成千上万棵树木。
The boxer has felled all of his opponents in the tournament. = 拳击手在锦标赛中已经击倒了所有对手。


过去完成时:
The tree had already been felled before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，那棵树已经被砍倒了。
The strong wind had felled the old house before the firefighters could arrive. = 在消防员到达之前，强风已经吹倒了那座老房子。


将来时:
They will fell the old building next week. = 他们下周将拆除那座旧建筑。
The workers will fell the trees to make way for the new road. = 工人们将砍伐树木为新路让路。


虚拟语气:
If I had a chainsaw, I could fell that tree easily. = 如果我有链锯，我就能轻松地砍倒那棵树。
If the wind had been stronger, it might have felled the tower. = 如果风更强的话，它可能就把塔吹倒了。


被动语态:
Many trees were felled by the storm. = 许多树木被暴风雨吹倒了。
The old building was felled to make way for a new development. =  为了建造新的建筑，旧建筑被拆除了。


疑问句:
Were the trees felled legally? =  这些树木是被合法砍伐的吗？
Has the champion been felled yet? =  冠军已经被击倒了吗？


否定句:
They did not fell the tree. = 他们没有砍倒那棵树。
The boxer did not fell his opponent in the first round. = 拳击手第一轮没有击倒他的对手。


PHRASE->
fell a tree = 砍倒一棵树
