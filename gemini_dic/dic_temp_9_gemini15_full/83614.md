haematoxylin:/ˌhiːmætˈɒksɪlɪn/| n. |苏木精|A natural dye extracted from the heartwood of the logwood tree (Haematoxylum campechianum), used as a stain in microscopy, especially for staining cell nuclei.|例句：Haematoxylin is a common stain used in histology to visualize cell nuclei. 苏木精是组织学中常用的染色剂，用于观察细胞核。

例句：The slide was stained with haematoxylin and eosin to enhance contrast.  载玻片用苏木精和伊红染色以增强对比度。
