词源:
The word "nonrestorative" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "restorative," which derives from the verb "restore," ultimately from the Latin word "restaurare" (to restore, rebuild).  Therefore, "nonrestorative" literally means "not restoring."
