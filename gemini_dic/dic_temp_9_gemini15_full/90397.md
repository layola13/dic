howler:/ˈhaʊlər/| n. |大声喊叫；(尤指)愚蠢的错误；吼叫声|a loud cry or shout, especially one expressing pain or anger; a howler is also a very obvious and silly mistake.|例句：The child let out a howler when he fell. 孩子摔倒时发出一声大叫。|例句：That's a real howler!  那是個明顯的錯誤！|近义词：shout, yell, scream, blunder, gaffe|反义词：whisper, murmur, correctness
