do-it-yourselfers:/ˌduːɪtjuːrˈsɛlfərz/| n. |自己动手做的人；DIY爱好者 |People who enjoy or are skilled at making or repairing things themselves, rather than paying someone else to do it.  They often take pride in their ability to handle home repairs, renovations, or crafting projects independently. |Many do-it-yourselfers find satisfaction in creating unique items and saving money. 许多自己动手做的人从创造独特物品和省钱中获得满足感。|  home improvers, handymen (but this typically refers to men), crafters | professional contractors, people who hire out work. |名词
