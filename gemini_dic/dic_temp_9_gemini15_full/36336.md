coiffuring:/kwɑːˈfjʊərɪŋ/| n. |发型设计；做头发|The art or process of styling hair.  It encompasses a range of techniques used to create different hairstyles, from simple everyday looks to elaborate updos. |例句：Her expertise in coiffuring was evident in the client's stunning new hairstyle.  她的发型设计专业知识在顾客惊艳的新发型中显而易见。|近义词：hairstyling, hairdressing|反义词：uncombing (反义词较为宽泛，指不梳理头发的状态)|名词
