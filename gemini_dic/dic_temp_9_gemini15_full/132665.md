nondismissal: /ˌnɒn.dɪsˈmɪs.əl/ | n. | 不解雇，不辞退 | The act or instance of not dismissing someone from a job or position.  It implies a conscious decision to retain someone despite potential reasons for dismissal. | Example: The nondismissal of the CEO surprised many shareholders.  首席执行官没有被解雇让许多股东感到惊讶。|近义词：retention, keeping,  reinstatement |反义词：dismissal, termination, firing | 名词
