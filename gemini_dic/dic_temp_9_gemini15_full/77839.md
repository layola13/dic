genericize:/ˈdʒɛnəraɪz/| vt. |使一般化；使大众化；泛化|To make something generic; to treat something as a general case rather than a specific instance.  This often involves losing some detail or specificity.|例句：The company decided to genericize its product line, offering fewer variations to reduce costs. 公司决定使其产品线大众化，减少产品种类以降低成本。

例句：The researchers genericized their findings, focusing on overall trends rather than individual cases. 研究人员将他们的研究结果泛化，关注整体趋势而非个别案例。

近义词：generalize, standardize, homogenize |反义词：specialize, particularize, individualize|动词
