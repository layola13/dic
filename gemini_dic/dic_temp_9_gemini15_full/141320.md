opencast: /ˈoʊpənˌkæst/ | adj. n. | 露天开采的；露天矿 | Opencast refers to a method of mining in which the overburden is removed to expose the mineral seam, which is then extracted.  It's characterized by large-scale earthmoving and open pits. | Example: The opencast mine scarred the landscape.  露天矿破坏了景观。 The company is planning to switch to opencast coal mining. 公司计划改用露天开采煤炭。|近义词：surface mining, strip mining |反义词：underground mining
