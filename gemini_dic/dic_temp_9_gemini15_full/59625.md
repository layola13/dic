ecclesiarch:/ɪˈkliːziɑːrk/| n. | 教会首脑，教长 | A church dignitary or ruler, especially a chief administrator or governor of a church or religious community.  The term often implies significant authority and responsibility within the ecclesiastical structure. | The ecclesiarch oversaw the daily operations of the cathedral and the welfare of its clergy. 教会首脑负责监督大教堂的日常运作以及神职人员的福利。|近义词:  archbishop, patriarch, prelate | 反义词: parishioner, layman
