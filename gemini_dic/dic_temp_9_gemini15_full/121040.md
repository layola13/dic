misconclusion:/ˌmɪskənˈkluʒən/| n. |错误的结论，误解|A misconclusion is a conclusion that is wrong because it is based on incorrect information or reasoning. It's a mistaken inference or judgment.|Example:  His analysis led to a serious misconclusion about the market's future.他的分析导致对市场未来作出了严重的错误结论。|近义词：mistake, error, fallacy, misjudgment |反义词：correct conclusion, accurate assessment
