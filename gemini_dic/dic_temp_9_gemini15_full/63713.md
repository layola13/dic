ephemerist:/ɪˈfɛmərɪst/| n. |天文历算家；编写天文历书的人|A person who computes and publishes ephemerides (tables of the positions of celestial bodies).  Ephemerists use astronomical data to predict the future positions of planets, stars, and other celestial objects.|The ephemerist meticulously calculated the comet's trajectory for the next decade.|近义词：astronomer, celestial calculator|反义词：无
