lookalike:/ˈlʊkəlaɪk/| n. adj.| 长相相似的人；酷似的人；非常相像的 |A person who looks very similar to another person.  Someone or something that closely resembles another.|The celebrity lookalike was astonishingly similar to the real star.|近义词：double, counterpart, similar, resemblance |反义词:dissimilar, unlike
