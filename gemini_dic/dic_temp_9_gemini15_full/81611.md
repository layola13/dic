greatnesses:/ˈɡreɪtnəsɪz/| n.|伟大之处；卓越之处；重要性 | The plural form of "greatness," referring to multiple instances or aspects of greatness.  It signifies outstanding achievements, qualities, or significance.  |例句：The greatnesses of the Roman Empire are still studied today. 罗马帝国的伟大之处至今仍在被研究。

例句：Her numerous achievements demonstrate the multiple greatnesses of her character. 她的众多成就展现了她性格的诸多伟大之处。


近义词：magnificences, splendors, excellences, achievements, merits


反义词： insignificances, trivialities, shortcomings, failures


词性: 名词
