optimizers:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzəz/ | n. |优化器，优化算法 |Algorithms or methods used to improve the performance of a system, typically by finding the best settings or parameters.  Optimizers are crucial in machine learning for finding the minimum of a loss function. |Example:  Gradient descent is a common optimizer used in deep learning.  梯度下降是深度学习中常用的优化器。|近义词: optimals, improvers |反义词: deteriorators, degraders
