personalised:/ˈpɜːsənəlaɪzd/ /ˈpɜrsənəlaɪzd/ | adj. |个性化的，个人定制的 |  Something that is personalised is made or done especially for a particular person. It is adapted to meet the individual needs or preferences of that person.  | Example:  She received a personalised birthday card from her grandfather. 她收到了爷爷送的个性化生日贺卡。


The company offers a personalised service to its clients. 这家公司为客户提供个性化服务。


近义词：customised, tailor-made, individualised


反义词：standardised, generic, mass-produced


词性：形容词
