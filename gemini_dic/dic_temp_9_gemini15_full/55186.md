dissenter:/dɪˈsɛntər/ | n. | 持异议者；反对者 | A person who opposes a belief, opinion, or practice that is widely accepted by others.  | Example:  He was a vocal dissenter against the war. 他是反对战争的直言不讳的异议者。


Example: The dissenter refused to sign the petition.  这位持异议者拒绝签署请愿书。

近义词：objector, opponent, protester, rebel

反义词：conformist, follower, supporter, adherent

词性: 名词
