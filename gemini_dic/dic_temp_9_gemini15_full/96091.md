indefensible:/ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsəbəl/| adj. |不可辩护的；无法为...辩解的 | incapable of being defended or justified.  The government's actions are indefensible. |近义词：unjustifiable, inexcusable, unpardonable |反义词：justifiable, defensible


例句：

His behavior was indefensible and he was rightly punished. 他的行为不可原谅，他理应受到惩罚。

The company's response to the crisis was completely indefensible. 公司对危机的回应完全无法辩解。


词源：

in- (否定前缀) + defensible (可辩护的)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His actions are indefensible. 他的行为是不可辩护的。
The policy is indefensible on moral grounds.  这项政策在道德上是站不住脚的。

一般过去时:
His excuse was indefensible.  他的借口是站不住脚的。
The judge ruled the argument indefensible.  法官裁定该论点站不住脚。

现在完成时:
Their behavior has been indefensible throughout this whole ordeal. 在整个磨难中，他们的行为一直是不可辩护的。
The company's actions have proven indefensible in the court of public opinion. 公司的行为在舆论法庭上已被证明是不可辩护的。

过去完成时:
The evidence presented had rendered their position indefensible.  提出的证据使得他们的立场无法自圆其说。
It had become indefensible to ignore the problem any longer.  继续忽视这个问题已经变得无法自圆其说。

将来时:
His actions will be indefensible in the eyes of the law.  他的行为在法律面前将是站不住脚的。
The company's plan will likely be indefensible once the full details emerge.  一旦全部细节浮出水面，公司的计划很可能就站不住脚了。

虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, their position would be less indefensible. 如果证据更充分，他们的立场就不会那么站不住脚。
I wish their actions weren't so indefensible.  我希望他们的行为没有那么不可辩护。

被动语态:
The decision was deemed indefensible by many critics.  许多批评家认为这一决定是站不住脚的。
His arguments were found to be completely indefensible.  他的论点被发现完全站不住脚。

疑问句:
Is their explanation of events truly indefensible?  他们对事件的解释真的不可辩护吗？
Isn't this policy indefensible given the current circumstances?  鉴于目前的形势，这项政策难道不是站不住脚的吗？


否定句:
Their actions are not indefensible; there are mitigating circumstances. 他们的行为并非不可辩护；存在一些减轻处罚的情况。
The argument, while flawed, isn't entirely indefensible.  虽然论点有缺陷，但并非完全站不住脚。
