ordinance:/ˈɔːrdənəns/| n.|法令；条例；条规|A law or regulation made by a city, town, or local government.  It is usually less significant than a federal or state law.|例句：The city council passed a new ordinance banning smoking in public places. 市议会通过了一项禁止在公共场所吸烟的新法令。
The building code is a strict ordinance that must be followed.|建筑规范是一项必须遵守的严格条例。|近义词：decree, regulation, rule, statute|反义词：deregulation, lawlessness


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The city council enforces many ordinances. = 市议会执行许多条例。
This ordinance regulates parking. = 这条条例规范了停车。


一般过去时:
They repealed the old ordinance last year. = 他们去年废除了旧条例。
The town council passed a new ordinance to control noise pollution. = 镇议会通过了一项新的条例来控制噪音污染。


现在完成时:
The city has implemented several new ordinances this year. = 今年市里实施了几项新条例。
They have followed all the relevant ordinances. = 他们遵守了所有相关的条例。


过去完成时:
Before the accident, they hadn't updated the safety ordinance. = 事故发生前，他们还没有更新安全条例。
The building had already violated several ordinances before the inspection. = 在检查之前，该建筑已经违反了几项条例。


将来时:
The council will introduce a new ordinance next month. = 议会下个月将引入一项新的条例。
They will likely amend the existing ordinance soon. = 他们可能会很快修改现有的条例。


虚拟语气:
If they had a better ordinance, the problem would be solved. = 如果他们有更好的条例，这个问题就能得到解决。
I wish the city had a stricter ordinance on littering. = 我希望城市对乱扔垃圾有更严格的条例。


被动语态:
The ordinance was passed unanimously. = 该条例获得一致通过。
Several ordinances are being reviewed by the committee. = 几个条例正在由委员会审查。


疑问句:
Is this ordinance still in effect? = 这条条例还在生效吗？
What does this ordinance prohibit? = 这条条例禁止什么？


否定句:
That ordinance is not applicable in this case. = 该条例在这种情况下不适用。
The company did not comply with the ordinance. = 公司没有遵守该条例。

词源:

The word "ordinance" comes from the Old French word "ordinance," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "ordinare," meaning "to order" or "to arrange."  This reflects the fundamental purpose of an ordinance: to establish order and regulate behavior within a specific jurisdiction.
