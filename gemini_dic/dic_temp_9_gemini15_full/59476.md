easements:/ˈiːzmənts/| n.|地役权；便利；轻松|A right to use another person's land for a specific purpose.|The easement allowed the utility company to access the pipeline across his property.  地役权允许公用事业公司穿过他的财产进入管道。|近义词：right-of-way, privilege|反义词：restriction, encumbrance


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The easement grants access to the beach. = 这份地役权赋予了通往海滩的权利。
This land has several easements in place. = 这块土地已经设置了几项地役权。


一般过去时:
The court upheld the easement. = 法院维持了该地役权。
They granted an easement for a power line. = 他们批准了一条输电线的通行权（地役权）。


现在完成时:
The city has acquired several easements for the new highway. = 该市已为新建高速公路获得了几项地役权。
We have successfully negotiated the easement terms. = 我们已成功协商了地役权条款。


过去完成时:
Before the construction, they had secured all necessary easements. = 在施工前，他们已经获得了所有必要的地役权。
The developer had already obtained the easements when the project began. = 项目开始时，开发商已经获得了地役权。


将来时:
The company will obtain the necessary easements before starting the project. = 公司将在项目开始前获得必要的地役权。
They will need to file for an easement to run the pipeline. = 他们需要申请地役权以铺设管道。


虚拟语气:
If we had an easement, we could access the lake easily. = 如果我们有地役权，我们就能轻松地到达湖边。
I wish we had secured the easement sooner. = 我希望我们早点获得地役权。


被动语态:
The easement was granted by the landowner. = 该地役权由土地所有者授予。
The easements are being reviewed by the legal department. = 地役权正在由法律部门审查。


疑问句:
Does this property have any easements? = 这处房产有任何地役权吗？
What kind of easements are listed in the deed? = 契约中列出了哪些类型的地役权？


否定句:
The developer did not secure all the necessary easements. = 开发商没有获得所有必要的地役权。
There are no easements affecting this property. = 没有影响这处房产的地役权。
