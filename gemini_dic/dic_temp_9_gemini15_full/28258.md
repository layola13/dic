caption:/ˈkæpʃən/ | n. |标题，字幕，说明 |A caption is a brief explanation or title accompanying an image, illustration, or other visual media.  It provides context or information about the visual. |Example: The photo caption explained the significance of the event.  照片说明解释了该事件的重要性。|近义词：title, heading, legend, subtitle |反义词：None
