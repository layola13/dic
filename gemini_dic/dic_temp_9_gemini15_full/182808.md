repeal:/rɪˈpiːl/| vt.|废除，撤销|To repeal a law or act is to officially end it.  This usually involves a legislative process, such as a vote by a parliament or congress.|例句：The government decided to repeal the unpopular tax law. 政府决定废除不受欢迎的税法。

例句：Congress voted to repeal the controversial immigration bill. 国会投票废除了有争议的移民法案。


近义词：rescind, revoke, annul, abrogate|反义词：enact, establish, institute|动词
