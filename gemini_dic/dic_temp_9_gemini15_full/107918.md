larve:/ˈlɑːrviː/| n. |幼虫|The immature, wingless form of an insect that undergoes metamorphosis.  Larvae typically differ significantly in appearance and habitat from the adult form. They are usually active feeding stages.  |例句：The butterfly larva is a caterpillar. 蝴蝶的幼虫是毛毛虫。

例句：Many insects spend most of their life as larvae.  许多昆虫一生中的大部分时间都以幼虫的形式存在。


近义词：grub, caterpillar, maggot


反义词：adult, imago


词源：源自拉丁语 *larva，意为“幽灵”或“面具”。  This reflects the often dramatic difference in appearance between the larva and the adult insect.
