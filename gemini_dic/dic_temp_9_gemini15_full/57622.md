draughtier:/ˈdrɑːftiər/| adj.| 通风较差的，透风的，漏风的 | More drafty; having more drafts.  This adjective describes something that allows air to pass through more easily, resulting in a chilly or uncomfortable environment.  It is the comparative form of "drafty". |例句：The old house was much draughtier than our new one. 这栋旧房子比我们的新房子通风更差。

例句：The window frames were draughtier after the recent storm. 最近的风暴过后，窗框更漏风了。

近义词：airier, breezier, drafty, windy

反义词：airtight, sealed, insulated


词性:形容词
