secreted:/sɪˈkriːtɪd/ | adj. v. |分泌的；隐藏的；秘密的 |  Hidden or concealed; produced and released by a gland or cell. |例句：The gland secreted a hormone.  腺体分泌了一种激素。|例句：The valuable painting was secreted in a hidden compartment.  这幅名贵的画藏在一个隐蔽的隔间里。|近义词：hidden, concealed, clandestine, secreted (as a verb)|反义词：revealed, exposed, open, public
