nonextendible: /ˌnɒnɪkˈstɛndəbl/ | adj. | 不可扩展的，不能延长的 |  Not capable of being extended or prolonged.  It describes something that cannot be made larger, longer, or more comprehensive. This often applies to things with fixed limits or inherent properties preventing expansion.


例句：

The company's current software is nonextendible; it cannot handle the increased workload.  公司的现有软件是不可扩展的；它无法处理增加的工作量。

The contract has a nonextendible deadline; the project must be completed by the specified date. 合同有一个不可延长的截止日期；项目必须在指定日期前完成。


近义词： inflexible, inextensible, unalterable, immutable

反义词： extensible, expandable, flexible


词性: adjective
