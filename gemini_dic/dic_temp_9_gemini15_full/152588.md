peaceful: /ˈpiːsfəl/ | adj. | 平静的；安宁的；和睦的 | Characterized by peace and quiet; tranquil; serene.  Free from disturbance; calm. |例句：The countryside was peaceful and serene.  乡村宁静祥和。

The peaceful atmosphere helped us relax. 平静的氛围帮助我们放松。

近义词：calm, tranquil, serene, quiet, restful, placid | 反义词：turbulent, chaotic, violent, stormy, restless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The atmosphere is peaceful today.  今天气氛很平静。
The lake remains peaceful even during the storm.  即使在暴风雨中，湖面依然平静。


一般过去时:
The village was peaceful before the war.  战争爆发前，村庄很平静。
Yesterday, the park was peaceful and quiet. 昨天，公园宁静祥和。


现在完成时:
The world has been peaceful for a long time. 世界和平已久。
The area has remained peaceful since the conflict ended.  冲突结束后，该地区一直保持和平。


过去完成时:
The country had been peaceful for decades before the revolution.  革命爆发前，这个国家已经和平了几十年。
The situation had been peaceful until the recent unrest.  直到最近的动乱之前，局势一直很平静。


将来时:
I hope the future will be peaceful. 我希望未来和平。
The negotiations promise a peaceful resolution.  谈判有望和平解决。


虚拟语气:
If only the world were more peaceful.  但愿世界更和平。
I wish things were peaceful again.  我希望事情能再次平静下来。


被动语态:
The region was kept peaceful by the strong police presence.  强大的警力维持了该地区的和平。
Peace is often achieved through peaceful means. 和平往往通过和平的手段实现。


疑问句:
Is the situation peaceful now?  现在情况平静吗？
Does the environment feel peaceful to you? 你觉得环境平静吗？


否定句:
The situation is not peaceful at the moment. 目前局势并不平静。
The meeting was not peaceful; there were many disagreements. 会议并不平静；有很多分歧。

词源:
From Old French *paisible, from pais "peace" (from Latin pax, pacis).  The suffix -able is of Old French origin.
