shuttled:/ˈʃʌt(ə)ld/ | v. |穿梭；（快速）运送； |to move or transport something or someone repeatedly back and forth between two places.  |例句：The children were shuttled between their parents' houses after the divorce. 离婚后，孩子们在父母家之间来回奔波。|例句：The company shuttled goods between the factory and the warehouse. 公司在工厂和仓库之间运输货物。|近义词：ferry, transport, convey |反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shuttles the children to school every morning. = 他每天早上都开车送孩子们上学。
The bus shuttles between the airport and the city center. = 这辆巴士往返于机场和市中心之间。

一般过去时:
She shuttled the documents between the two offices. = 她把文件在两个办公室之间来回传递。
They shuttled the injured passengers to the hospital. = 他们把受伤的乘客送到了医院。

现在完成时:
We have shuttled this package three times already. = 我们已经把这个包裹来回运送了三次。
She has shuttled her kids to various activities all week. = 她整个星期都在送孩子参加各种活动。

过去完成时:
He had shuttled the goods before the deadline. = 他在截止日期之前已经把货物运送完毕。
They had shuttled the refugees to safety before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经将难民送到了安全地带。

将来时:
I will shuttle between London and Paris for this project. = 为这个项目，我将往返于伦敦和巴黎之间。
The company will shuttle the materials to the construction site tomorrow. = 公司明天将把材料运送到施工现场。

虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would shuttle my children to their activities. = 如果我有车，我会送我的孩子参加他们的活动。
If she had known, she would have shuttled the documents more carefully. = 如果她知道的话，她就会更仔细地运送文件。

被动语态:
The passengers were shuttled to the hotel by bus. = 乘客们被巴士送到了酒店。
The goods are being shuttled to the distribution center. = 货物正在被运送到配送中心。

疑问句:
Does the hotel shuttle guests to the airport? = 酒店是否提供往返机场的班车服务？
How often are the goods shuttled between the warehouses? = 货物在仓库之间多久运输一次？

否定句:
He didn't shuttle the children to school today. = 他今天没送孩子们上学。
The company doesn't shuttle goods on weekends. = 公司周末不运输货物。
