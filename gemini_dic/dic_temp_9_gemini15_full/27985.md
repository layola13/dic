canvasing:/ˈkænˌvæsɪŋ/| v.|游说；拉票；挨家挨户宣传|The act of visiting people in their homes or other places to try to persuade them to support a particular cause or candidate, or to sell them something.  |例句：They are canvasing door-to-door for votes. 他们在挨家挨户地拉票。|例句：The company is canvasing the neighborhood to sell its new product. 这家公司正在挨家挨户地推销它的新产品。|近义词：soliciting, canvassing, lobbying|反义词：ignoring, neglecting|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They canvas the neighborhood for donations. = 他们在社区挨家挨户地募捐。
We canvas potential customers for our new product. = 我们正在向潜在客户推销我们的新产品。


一般过去时:
The campaign workers canvassed the entire city. = 竞选工作人员走遍了整个城市。
They canvassed the area for support. = 他们挨家挨户地争取支持。


现在完成时:
They have canvassed over 1000 households. = 他们已经走访了1000多户人家。
The party has canvassed extensively in the rural areas. = 该党在农村地区进行了广泛的拉票活动。


过去完成时:
They had canvassed the district before the election. = 他们在选举前已经走访了该地区。
The volunteers had canvassed all the streets by nightfall. = 志愿者们在天黑前已经走访了所有街道。


将来时:
We will canvas the community for volunteers. = 我们将走访社区寻找志愿者。
They will canvas the electorate before the upcoming election. = 他们将在即将到来的选举前游说选民。


虚拟语气:
If we canvassed more effectively, we would win the election. = 如果我们更有效地拉票，我们就能赢得选举。

否定句:
