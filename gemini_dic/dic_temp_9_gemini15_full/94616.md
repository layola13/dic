impregnation: /ˌɪmˈprɛɡˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 浸渍；受孕；充满；施肥 | The act of impregnating or the state of being impregnated; the process of making something permeate or saturate something else.  It can refer to the fertilization of an ovum, the saturation of a substance, or the permeation of a material. | 例句：The impregnation of the wood with preservative prevented rot.  木材浸渍防腐剂防止腐烂。

The impregnation of the queen bee resulted in a new generation of bees.  蜂王的受孕导致了新一代蜜蜂的诞生。


近义词：fertilization, saturation, permeation, impregnation


反义词：depletion, evacuation, drying
