dunnish:/ˈdʌnɪʃ/| adj.| 昏暗的；阴沉的；乏味的 | Dull, gloomy, or drab in appearance or character.  It suggests a lack of brightness, cheerfulness, or interest.  | 例句：The dunnish light of the late afternoon cast long shadows.  下午迟暮的昏暗灯光投下长长的影子。


The room felt dunnish and depressing. 房间感觉昏暗而压抑。| 近义词：dull, drab, gloomy, somber, dreary | 反义词：bright, cheerful, vibrant, lively
