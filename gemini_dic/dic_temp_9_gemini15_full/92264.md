hyperventilating:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɛntɪleɪtɪŋ/| v.|过度换气，呼吸急促|The act of breathing too rapidly and deeply, often resulting in dizziness, lightheadedness, or other symptoms.|例句：She was hyperventilating after the car accident. 她车祸后呼吸急促。|例句：The patient was hyperventilating and needed immediate medical attention. 病人呼吸急促，需要立即就医。|近义词：overbreathing, rapid breathing|反义词：hypoventilation
