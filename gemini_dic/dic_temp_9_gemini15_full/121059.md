misconnections:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛkʃənz/ | n. | 误连，错误连接，脱节 | Instances where things that should be connected are not, leading to problems or misunderstandings.  This can refer to physical connections (like wires) or conceptual connections (like ideas). | Example: The frequent misconnections in the train schedule caused significant delays.  频繁的火车时刻表误连导致了严重的延误。 The misconnections between the different departments hampered the project's progress.  各部门之间的脱节阻碍了项目的进展。|近义词：failures, malfunctions, disruptions, discrepancies |反义词：connections, integrations, coherences |名词
