publicised:/ˈpʌblɪsaɪzd/| v.|公开宣传，公布|To make something known publicly.  This often involves using various media channels to spread information about a product, event, or idea. |例句：The company publicised its new product through television commercials. 公司通过电视广告宣传其新产品。
例句：The museum publicised the new exhibition extensively. 博物馆广泛宣传了新的展览。|近义词：advertised, promoted, announced, broadcast|反义词：suppressed, concealed, hidden|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company publicizes its products through social media. = 公司通过社交媒体宣传其产品。
They publicize their events widely. = 他们广泛宣传他们的活动。


一般过去时:
The government publicized the new policy. = 政府公布了这项新政策。
The band publicized their new album on their website. = 乐队在他们的网站上宣传了他们的新专辑。


现在完成时:
They have publicized the concert extensively. = 他们已经广泛宣传了这场音乐会。
The company has publicized its commitment to sustainability. = 公司已经宣传了其对可持续发展的承诺。


过去完成时:

The artist had publicized her work through several galleries. = 这位艺术家已经通过几家画廊宣传了自己的作品。

虚拟语气:
