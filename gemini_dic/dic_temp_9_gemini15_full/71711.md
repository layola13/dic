flavoured: /ˈfleɪvərd/ | adj. | 调味的，有香味的 | Having a particular flavor added.  | 例句：I prefer flavoured yogurt to plain yogurt.  我喜欢调味酸奶胜过原味酸奶。

例句：The cake was flavoured with vanilla and chocolate. 这个蛋糕用香草和巧克力调味。


近义词：seasoned, infused, scented, aromatic | 反义词：plain, unflavored, tasteless


词源:  From "flavour" (itself from Old French *flavor, from Latin *flavorem, from *flavus "yellow, golden"). The "-ed" suffix indicates the past participle used adjectivally, meaning "having been given a flavor".


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ice cream is flavoured with strawberry. = 冰激凌是草莓味的。
This tea is flavoured with lemon. = 这茶是柠檬味的。


一般过去时:

She flavoured the chicken with garlic and pepper. = 她用大蒜和胡椒粉给鸡肉调味。


现在完成时:

They have flavoured the cookies with chocolate chips. = 他们已经用巧克力碎给饼干调味了。


过去完成时:
