convenient:/kənˈviːniənt/| adj.|方便的，便利的|Suitable or agreeable to one's needs or plans.  Something that is convenient is easy to use or do. |例句：The store is conveniently located near the bus station. 这家商店位置便利，靠近公交车站。

例句：This software provides a convenient way to manage your files. 这个软件提供了一种方便的文件管理方式。


近义词：handy, easy, accessible, suitable

反义词：inconvenient, difficult, troublesome


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bus stop is convenient for me. = 公交车站对我来说很方便。
This chair is convenient to sit in. = 这把椅子坐起来很舒服。


一般过去时:
The hotel was convenient for visiting the museum. = 这家酒店去博物馆很方便。
The timing of the meeting was convenient for everyone. = 会议时间对每个人都很方便。


现在完成时:
This new app has been convenient for managing my schedule. = 这个新应用程序一直以来都很方便我管理我的日程安排。
The online service has proved convenient for many customers. = 在线服务已被证明对许多客户来说很方便。


过去完成时:
The earlier train had been more convenient for catching my flight. = 更早的那趟火车本来更方便我赶飞机。
The previous arrangement had been convenient, but this one is better. = 之前的安排很方便，但这个更好。


将来时:
The new road will be convenient for commuters. = 新建道路将方便通勤者。
Working from home will be more convenient for me. = 在家工作对我来说会更方便。


虚拟语气:
It would be convenient if the meeting were held earlier. = 如果会议提前举行就好了。
If I had a car, it would be more convenient to get around. = 如果我有辆车，出行就会方便得多。


被动语态:
The location of the office is considered convenient by many. = 许多人认为办公室的位置很方便。
The new system is designed to be convenient for users. = 新系统的设计目的是方便用户使用。


疑问句:
Is this time convenient for you? = 这个时间对你方便吗？
Would it be convenient to meet tomorrow? = 明天见面方便吗？


否定句:
The location of the building is not convenient. = 这座建筑物的位置不方便。
Unfortunately, the train schedule is not convenient for me. = 不幸的是，火车时刻表对我来说不方便。
