satisfied:/ˈsætɪsfaɪd/| adj.|满足的，满意的|Feeling or expressing pleasure or contentment.|I am satisfied with the result. 我对结果很满意。|pleased, content, gratified|dissatisfied, unhappy, discontent|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is satisfied with his job. = 他对他的工作很满意。
She is satisfied with her new house. = 她对她的新房子很满意。

一般过去时:
I was satisfied with the dinner. = 我对晚餐很满意。
They were satisfied with the service. = 他们对服务很满意。

现在完成时:
We have been satisfied with their performance. = 我们一直对他们的表现很满意。
She has been satisfied with her progress. = 她一直对自己的进步很满意。

过去完成时:
He had been satisfied with his life before the accident. = 事故发生前，他对自己的生活很满意。
They had been satisfied with the outcome of the negotiations. = 他们对谈判的结果很满意。

将来时:
I will be satisfied if the problem is solved. = 如果问题解决了，我就满意了。
They will be satisfied with the final product. = 他们会对最终产品感到满意。

虚拟语气:
I would be satisfied if they finished the project on time. = 如果他们按时完成项目，我会很满意。
If I were you, I would be satisfied with the offer. = 如果我是你，我会对这个提议感到满意。

被动语态:
His needs were satisfied. = 他的需求得到了满足。
The customers were satisfied with the quick response. = 客户对快速的回应感到满意。

疑问句:
Are you satisfied with the explanation? = 你对这个解释满意吗？
Is he satisfied with his current situation? = 他对目前的情况满意吗？

否定句:
I am not satisfied with the quality of the work. = 我对这项工作的质量不满意。
He was not satisfied with the outcome of the game. = 他对比赛的结果不满意。
