skyjack:/ˈskaɪdʒæk/| vt.|劫持飞机|To seize control of an aircraft in flight, typically by force or threat of force.|例句：The terrorists skyjacked the plane and demanded a ransom.  恐怖分子劫持了飞机并索要赎金。

例句：The pilot managed to outsmart the skyjackers and land the plane safely.  飞行员设法智胜劫机者，安全地降落了飞机。


近义词：hijack, seize control of (an aircraft)
反义词：None specifically, but actions like safely landing a plane could be considered an antonym to the action of skyjacking.
词性:verb
