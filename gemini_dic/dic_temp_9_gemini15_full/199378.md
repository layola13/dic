simperingly:/ˈsɪmpərɪŋli/| adv.|傻笑地；谄媚地；虚伪地 |In a way that suggests excessive or affected sweetness or humility; with a simper.  It implies insincerity or a desire to please excessively.|例句：He simpered his way through the interview, trying to win over the panel. 他在面试中一路傻笑，试图讨好评委。

She answered simperingly, hoping to avoid further questioning. 她谄媚地回答，希望能避免进一步的提问。|近义词：fawningly, obsequiously, ingratiatingly |反义词：sternly, severely, honestly
