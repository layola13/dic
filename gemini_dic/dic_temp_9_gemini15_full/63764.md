epicoele:/ˈɛpɪkoʊˌiːl/| n. | 脐疝 | A type of hernia where the intestine protrudes through the umbilicus (navel).  It's a protrusion of the bowel through a weakness in the abdominal wall at the navel. | Example: The infant was diagnosed with an epicoele requiring surgical repair.  婴儿被诊断出患有需要手术修复的脐疝。 | 近义词：umbilical hernia, omphalocele (omphaloceles are more severe and involve the intestines protruding through the umbilical cord) | 反义词：None (antonyms describe the absence of a hernia, not a specific type) | 名词
