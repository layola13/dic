ferment:/fərˈment/| n., v.|发酵；酝酿；骚动|

英文解析:
As a noun, "ferment" refers to a state of agitation or excitement.  As a verb, it means to undergo fermentation, or to cause to ferment.  It can also describe a process of gradual development or change, often with the implication of potential for trouble or disruption.

例句:
The political ferment in the country led to widespread unrest.  =  该国的政治动荡导致了广泛的不满。

The grapes were left to ferment for several weeks.  =  葡萄被放置发酵数周。


近义词:
(noun) turmoil, agitation, unrest, brewing,  (verb) brew,  leaven


反义词:
(noun) calm, peace, tranquility, (verb)  stabilize


词性:
Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The yeast ferments the sugar. = 酵母使糖发酵。
The situation is fermenting. = 情况正在发酵。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The tension had fermented for years before the conflict erupted. = 在冲突爆发前的几年里，紧张局势一直在酝酿。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
