jamdani:/ˈdʒæmdɑːni/| n. | 贾姆丹尼纱丽；贾姆丹尼织物 | A fine muslin cloth woven with a supplementary weft, typically in intricate patterns.  It is a traditional textile from Bengal, now Bangladesh.  The patterns are created by using a supplementary weft, which is added to the main weft to create the design, without any additional design elements. | Example: The bride wore a beautiful jamdani saree on her wedding day.  新娘在婚礼当天穿了一件美丽的贾姆丹尼纱丽。|近义词：muslin, textile, fabric | 反义词：N/A
