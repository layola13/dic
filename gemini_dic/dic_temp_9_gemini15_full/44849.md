crustific:/krʌsˈtɪfɪk/| adj.|硬壳的，有硬壳的|Relating to or resembling a crust; having a hard, brittle shell or outer layer.  The word suggests a hard, often brittle, outer layer, similar to a crust.|The crustific texture of the ancient artifact made it difficult to handle without damaging it. 古代文物的硬壳状结构使得在不损坏它的情况下难以处理。|近义词：crustaceous, shell-like, hard-shelled|反义词：soft, pliable
