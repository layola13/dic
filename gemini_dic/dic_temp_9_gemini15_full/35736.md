cochairing:/ˌkoʊˈtʃɛərɪŋ/| v.|共同主持；共同担任主席|The act of two or more people jointly presiding over a meeting, event, or organization.  It implies shared responsibility and authority.|例句：They are cochairing the conference this year.  今年他们共同主持这次会议。
例句：The two professors are cochairing the department's new initiative.两位教授共同主持该系的新项目。|近义词：jointly presiding, co-presiding|反义词：solely presiding, single-handedly leading|动词
