drugs:/drʌɡz/| n.|毒品，药物|Substances that affect the mind and body, often used for medical purposes but also capable of causing addiction or harm.|例句：The doctor prescribed some drugs to relieve the pain. 医生开了些药来缓解疼痛。|例句：The illegal drug trade is a major problem. 非法毒品交易是一个主要问题。|近义词：medications, narcotics, pharmaceuticals|反义词：placebo, antidote


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He takes his medication regularly. = 他定期服药。
She uses prescription drugs for her condition. = 她用处方药来治疗她的病情。

一般过去时:
They took illegal drugs at the party. = 他们在派对上吸毒。
He used to abuse drugs but is now clean. = 他过去滥用药物，但现在已经戒掉了。

现在完成时:
She has been taking antidepressants for years. = 她多年来一直在服用抗抑郁药。
He has never tried illegal drugs. = 他从未尝试过毒品。

过去完成时:
He had taken a sleeping pill before he went to bed. = 他睡觉前已经服了一片安眠药。
She had abused drugs in her youth. = 她年轻时滥用过毒品。

将来时:
I will take the prescribed medicine as directed. = 我将按照医嘱服用处方药。
They will stop taking the drugs once they feel better. = 他们感觉好一些后就会停止服药。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't take those drugs without a doctor's prescription. = 如果我是你，我就不会在没有医生处方的情况下服用这些药物。
I wish I hadn't taken those drugs last night. = 我真希望昨晚我没有服用那些药物。

被动语态:
The drugs were administered by a nurse. = 药物是由护士注射的。
The new drug is being tested for safety. = 这种新药正在接受安全测试。

疑问句:
Are you taking any drugs? = 你正在服用任何药物吗？
What drugs are you prescribed? = 医生给你开了什么药？

否定句:
He doesn't take any recreational drugs. = 他不服用任何娱乐性药物。
She didn't take the medicine as prescribed. = 她没有按处方服用药物。
