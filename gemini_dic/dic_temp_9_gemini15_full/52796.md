diligent:/ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/ | adj. |勤奋的，努力的 |Diligent means showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties.  It implies a persistent effort and attention to detail. |例句：She is a diligent student and always gets high marks. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是取得高分。

例句：His diligent work ethic led to his promotion. 他勤奋的工作态度使他得到了晋升。


近义词：industrious, hardworking, assiduous, painstaking, meticulous |反义词：lazy, indolent, slothful, careless, negligent
