pure:/pjʊr/| adj.| 纯粹的，纯洁的 |  Free from any foreign or undesirable admixture; unmixed or undiluted.  Essentially without any flaws or defects; completely satisfactory. | 例句：The mountain air was pure and refreshing.  山上的空气清新纯净。  She had a pure heart and kind intentions. 她有一颗纯洁的心和善良的意图。|近义词：clean, unadulterated, immaculate, pristine |反义词：impure, contaminated, adulterated, tainted


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water is pure. = 水是纯净的。
This diamond is pure. = 这颗钻石是纯净的。

一般过去时:
The air was pure before the factory opened. = 工厂开业前空气是纯净的。
The snow was pure white. = 雪是纯白的。

现在完成时:
The river has become pure again after the cleanup. = 河流在清理后又变得纯净了。
They have kept their love pure. = 他们一直保持着纯洁的爱。

过去完成时:
The scientist had ensured the sample was pure before the experiment. = 科学家在实验前确保样品是纯净的。
The water had been pure until the spill occurred. = 直到泄漏发生之前，水都是纯净的。

将来时:
The air will be pure after the rain. = 雨后空气会很纯净。
We hope the technology will make the water pure. = 我们希望这项技术能使水变得纯净。

虚拟语气:
If the ingredients were pure, the result would be perfect. = 如果原料是纯净的，结果就会完美。
I wish the air were pure in this city. = 我希望这个城市的空气是纯净的。

被动语态:
The water is purified to be pure. = 水被净化成纯净的。
The materials are tested to ensure they are pure. = 对材料进行测试以确保它们是纯净的。

疑问句:
Is the gold pure? = 这黄金是纯的吗？
Is the sample pure enough for the experiment? = 样品足够纯净用于实验吗？

否定句:
The water is not pure. = 水不纯净。
The air is not pure enough to breathe. = 空气不够纯净，无法呼吸。

词源:

The word "pure" originates from the Old French word "pur," which in turn comes from the Latin word "purus."  The Latin "purus" had a wide range of meanings, including "clean," "clear," "bright," "unmixed," and "unadulterated," all contributing to the modern English sense.
