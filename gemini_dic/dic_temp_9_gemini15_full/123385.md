monomorphous:/mɒnəˈmɔːfəs/ | adj. | 单形的；单态的 |  Existing or occurring in only one form or type;  lacking polymorphism.  It refers to a substance or crystal that exists in only one crystalline form.  In biology, it can refer to an organism exhibiting only one form or type. | Example:  The mineral calcite is often monomorphous, but some varieties exhibit polymorphism.  这种矿物方解石通常是单形的，但有些变种表现出多晶型现象。| 近义词：uniformal, uniform | 反义词：polymorphous, polymorphic
