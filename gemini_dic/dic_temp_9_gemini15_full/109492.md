leptodactyl:/ˈlɛptoˌdæktɪl/| n. |细趾的；细趾蛙|A leptodactyl is a frog belonging to the family Leptodactylidae, characterized by having slender toes.  They are typically found in South America. |Example:  Many leptodactyls are terrestrial and burrow in the soil.  许多细趾蛙是陆生的，并在土壤中挖洞。|近义词：narrow-toed frog (仅指蛙类)|反义词：broad-toed frog (仅指蛙类)|名词
