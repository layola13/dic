rabble-rousing: /ˈræbəlˌraʊzɪŋ/ | n. adj. |煽动；煽动性言论；煽动行为 |The act of inciting a mob or crowd to violence or riotous behavior; inflammatory speech or writing intended to arouse discontent or anger. |例句：His rabble-rousing speeches led to widespread unrest.  他的煽动性言论导致了广泛的不满。

The politician's rabble-rousing tactics alienated many voters.  这位政治家的煽动策略疏远了许多选民。


近义词：incitement, agitation, provocation, inflammatory speech,  煽动，鼓动，挑衅，煽动性言论

反义词：pacification, appeasement, conciliation,  安抚，绥靖，调解


词源:
rabble (n.)  "a disorderly crowd; mob," from Old French rable "rabble, trash," of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old French raber "to scrape, rub."  + rousing (adj.)  "exciting; stirring," from rouse (v.) "to awaken; stir up," from Old English rūsian "to shake, stir."
