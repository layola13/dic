green-thumber:/ˈɡriːnθʌmər/ | n. | 园艺高手，擅长园艺的人 | A person with a remarkable ability to make plants grow well, often implying a seemingly magical touch.  |  He's a real green-thumber; his garden is always bursting with vibrant flowers and vegetables. 他真是个园艺高手；他的花园里总是盛开着鲜艳的花朵和蔬菜。 |  expert gardener, gardening enthusiast |  gardening novice, plant killer
