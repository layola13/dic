immobile:/ɪˈmoʊbaɪl/ | adj. | 不动的；静止的；不能移动的 | Unable to move; stationary.  | The car was immobile after the accident.  事故后汽车无法移动。| mobile, movable | mobile, movable |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The statue remains immobile. = 雕像保持不动。
The plant is immobile in the pot. = 植物在盆里一动不动。


一般过去时:
The car was immobile in the snow. = 汽车被困在雪地里无法动弹。
He was immobile with fear. = 他吓得一动不动。
