courgette:/kʊrˈʒɛt/| n. |西葫芦，小胡瓜|A courgette is a young marrow, typically eaten while still small and tender.  It is also known as a zucchini in American English.  |例句：I added some grated courgette to the cake to make it moist.  我往蛋糕里加了一些磨碎的西葫芦，让它更湿润。

例句：The recipe calls for two medium-sized courgettes. 这个食谱需要两个中等大小的西葫芦。

近义词：zucchini, marrow (when young)

反义词：无

词源:
The word "courgette" is derived from the French word "courgette," which in turn is a diminutive of "courge," meaning "gourd" or "pumpkin."  The French word ultimately derives from the Latin word "cucurbita," also meaning gourd or pumpkin.
