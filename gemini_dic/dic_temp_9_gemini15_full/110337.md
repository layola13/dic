lightfully:/ˈlaɪtfʊli/| adj.|令人愉快的，轻快地，轻松地|pleasantly and cheerfully; in a light and airy manner.  It suggests a feeling of lightness, brightness, and pleasantness.|例句：The room was lightfully decorated for the party.  房间里装饰得非常漂亮，适合派对。

例句：She laughed lightfully at his joke. 她对他的笑话轻松地笑了。


近义词：delightfully, pleasantly, cheerfully, merrily

反义词：dismal, gloomily, depressingly

词性:形容词
