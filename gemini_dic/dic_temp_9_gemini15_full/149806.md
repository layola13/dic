paleobotanist:/ˌpeɪliˌoʊˈbɒtənɪst/| n. |古植物学家|A scientist who studies fossil plants to learn about plant life in the geological past.  Paleobotanists analyze plant fossils to understand past climates, ecosystems, and the evolution of plants. |例句：The paleobotanist carefully examined the fossilized leaf, searching for clues about its age and origin.  古植物学家仔细检查了化石叶子，寻找有关其年龄和起源的线索。|例句：  Her work as a paleobotanist has contributed significantly to our understanding of the prehistoric flora of the region. 她作为一名古植物学家所做的工作极大地促进了我们对该地区史前植物群的了解。|近义词：paleontologist (if their work overlaps with plants)|反义词：None|词性: 名词
