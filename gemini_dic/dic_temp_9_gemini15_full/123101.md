mongoose:/ˈmʌŋɡuːs/ | n. | 獴 | A mongoose is a small, carnivorous mammal belonging to the family Herpestidae.  They are known for their agility, intelligence, and ability to kill venomous snakes.  | Example: The mongoose skillfully evaded the cobra's strike.  獴巧妙地躲避了眼镜蛇的攻击。 | 近义词： none (due to unique characteristics) | 反义词： none (due to unique characteristics)
