donjon:/ˈdʌndʒən/| n. |地牢；主堡|A donjon is the principal tower of a medieval castle, often the strongest part and sometimes containing the living quarters of the lord.  It is also used as a synonym for dungeon, referring to a dark, underground prison. |The donjon of the castle stood defiant against the siege.|近义词：keep, fortress, dungeon|反义词：open space, freedom
