kitchen-sink:/ˈkɪtʃənˌsɪŋk/| n. |厨房水槽；包罗万象的事物|The term "kitchen sink" refers to a kitchen sink, but it is also used figuratively to describe something that includes everything or a large variety of things. It implies a comprehensive or inclusive nature, often to the point of being overwhelming or excessive.|例句：The movie is a kitchen-sink drama, tackling poverty, love, and social injustice.|近义词：everything but the kitchen sink, potpourri, miscellany|反义词：nothing, minimalist
