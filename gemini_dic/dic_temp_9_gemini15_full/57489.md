drag-the-chain:/dræɡ ðə tʃeɪn/| v.| 拖延；磨蹭；消极怠工 |To delay or procrastinate, often intentionally.  It implies a lack of enthusiasm or effort and a deliberate slowing down of progress. The image is that of a heavy chain being dragged along slowly and laboriously.|例句：He's dragging the chain on that project; it's going to be late. 他那个项目拖拖拉拉的，要完工晚了。

例句：Stop dragging the chain and get the work done! 别磨蹭了，把活干完！


近义词：procrastinate, delay, dawdle, stall, shirk|反义词：expedite, accelerate, hurry
