protege:/ˈprɒtəʒeɪ/| n. |被保护者；门生；受庇护者 | A person who is guided and supported by an experienced or influential person.  |例句：She was a protege of the famous composer. 她是一位著名作曲家的门生。  |例句：He carefully nurtured his protege's career. 他精心培养了他的门生的职业生涯。|近义词：pupil, apprentice, trainee |反义词：mentor, sponsor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a protege of the CEO. = 他是CEO的被保护者。
She protects her protege from harsh criticism. = 她保护她的门生免受严厉批评。

一般过去时:
He was a protege of a renowned artist. = 他是一位著名艺术家的门生。
The company supported its protege's new venture. = 公司支持其被保护者的新的事业。


现在完成时:
She has been a protege of this company for five years. = 她已经成为这家公司的被保护者五年了。
He has carefully mentored his protege throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都仔细地指导了他的门生。


过去完成时:
Before the scandal, he had been a successful protege. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他曾是一位成功的被保护者。
She had already established herself as a successful protege before starting her own business. = 在自己创业之前，她已经成为一个成功的被保护者。


将来时:
He will be a successful protege in the future. = 他将来会成为一个成功的被保护者。
The company will continue to support its protege's growth. = 公司将继续支持其被保护者的成长。


虚拟语气:
If he were a protege of a famous director, he would have more opportunities. = 如果他是著名导演的被保护者，他会更有机会。
I wish I were a protege of a successful entrepreneur. = 我希望我是成功企业家的被保护者。


被动语态:
His career was greatly influenced by being a protege of a prominent figure. = 作为一位杰出人物的被保护者，他的职业生涯受到了很大的影响。
The protege was carefully guided and trained by his mentor. = 被保护者受到了导师的精心指导和训练。


疑问句:
Is he a protege of anyone famous? = 他是谁的被保护者？
Was she a protege of that famous artist? = 她曾经是那位著名艺术家的门生吗？


否定句:
He is not a protege of anyone in particular. = 他并非任何特定人物的被保护者。
She was not a protege of the company, but she did receive some support. = 她不是公司的被保护者，但她确实得到了一些支持。
