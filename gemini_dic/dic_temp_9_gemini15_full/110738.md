lingered:/ˈlɪŋɡərd/| v.|逗留；徘徊；持续|To remain or stay longer than expected or necessary; to continue to exist or persist. |例句：He lingered in the garden, enjoying the sunset. 他在花园里逗留，欣赏着日落。|例句：The smell of smoke lingered in the air. 烟味在空气中久久不散。|近义词：remain, stay, persist, tarry|反义词：depart, leave, vanish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lingers in the cafe every afternoon. = 他每天下午都在咖啡馆里逗留。
The sweet smell lingers in the air. = 甜甜的味道在空气中弥漫。

一般过去时:
She lingered at the party until everyone had left. = 她一直待在派对上直到所有人都离开了。
The pain lingered for several days. = 疼痛持续了好几天。

现在完成时:
The aroma of coffee has lingered all morning. = 咖啡的香味整个上午都挥之不去。
They have lingered over their decision for weeks. = 他们已经纠结这个决定好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
The scent of roses had lingered in the room long after the flowers had wilted. = 玫瑰的香味在花朵枯萎很久后还留在房间里。
He had lingered too long, missing the last bus. = 他逗留太久，错过了末班车。

将来时:
The effects of the medicine will linger for several hours. = 药物的效果会持续几个小时。
I think the memory of this day will linger for a long time. = 我想对这一天的记忆将会持续很久。

虚拟语气:
If he had lingered a little longer, he might have seen her. = 如果他多待一会儿，他可能会看到她。
I wish the pleasant feeling would linger a little longer. = 我希望这种愉悦的感觉能持续久一点。

被动语态:
The music was lingered over by the audience. = 音乐被观众们回味无穷。
The problem was lingered on, unresolved. = 问题被拖延了，没有解决。

疑问句:
Did the smell linger long after the cooking was done? = 做完饭后，味道还久久不散吗？
Why did he linger so long at the doorway? = 他为什么在门口徘徊了那么久？

否定句:
The unpleasant memory didn't linger. = 那段不愉快的记忆并没有持续很久。
He didn't linger, he left immediately. = 他没有逗留，立刻离开了。


PHRASE->
linger on:继续存在；持续
linger over:慢慢享用；细细品味
linger in the memory:留在记忆中
linger in the air:弥漫在空气中
linger about/around:徘徊；闲荡
