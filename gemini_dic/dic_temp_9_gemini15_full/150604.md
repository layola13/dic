paperlike:/ˈpeɪpərlaɪk/| adj.|像纸一样的，纸质的|Resembling paper in texture, appearance, or feel.  The adjective "paperlike" describes something that has qualities similar to paper, such as thinness, smoothness, or a slightly fragile quality.|例句：The thin, paperlike skin of the fruit easily tore.  这种水果薄如纸的果皮很容易撕破。

The delicate, paperlike wings of the butterfly fluttered gently. 蝴蝶轻薄如纸的翅膀轻轻地扇动着。|近义词：papery, thin, delicate, flimsy|反义词：thick, sturdy, robust, strong
