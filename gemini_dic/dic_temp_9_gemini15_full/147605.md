overplayed:/ˌoʊvərˈpleɪd/ | adj. |过度演绎的；被过度使用的；陈词滥调的 |  Something that is overplayed has been used or done too much, to the point of being boring or ineffective. It suggests that something has lost its impact or novelty due to excessive repetition or emphasis.  |  The joke was overplayed and fell flat.  这个笑话被过度演绎，结果很冷场。
The theme of revenge was overplayed in the movie.  这部电影中复仇的主题被过度渲染了。| 近义词：overused, hackneyed, clichéd,  banal | 反义词：underplayed, original, fresh, novel
