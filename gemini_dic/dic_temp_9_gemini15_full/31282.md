chapels:/ˈtʃæpəlz/| n.|小教堂；礼拜堂|A chapel is a small building or room used for Christian worship, often attached to a larger building such as a hospital, school, or castle.|例句：The university has several chapels where students can worship. 这所大学有几座学生可以做礼拜的小教堂。|例句：Many castles contained private chapels for the lord and his family. 许多城堡都设有供领主及其家人使用的私人小教堂。|近义词：church, oratory|反义词：cathedral (in terms of size and significance)
