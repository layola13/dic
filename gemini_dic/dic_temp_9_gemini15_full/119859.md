miles:/maɪlz/| n.|英里，许多|A mile is a unit of length equal to 5,280 feet (approximately 1.609 kilometers).  The word can also be used figuratively to mean a great distance or a long way. |例句：The town is miles away.  这个小镇远在几英里之外。

例句：It felt like miles to walk home in the rain.  在雨中走回家感觉走了好几英里路。|近义词：kilometers, leagues|反义词：inches, feet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He walks many miles every day. = 他每天走很多英里。
The distance is several miles. = 距离有好几英里。

一般过去时:
We walked five miles yesterday. = 昨天我们走了五英里。
She drove miles to get here. = 她开了好几英里才到这儿。

现在完成时:
They have walked miles and miles. = 他们已经走了好几英里。
I have driven many miles in my life. = 我一生中开了许多英里。

过去完成时:
He had walked miles before he found shelter. = 他走了好几英里才找到避难所。
They had driven many miles before the car broke down. = 他们开了许多英里后汽车才抛锚。

将来时:
We will walk several miles tomorrow. = 我们明天将走几英里。
She will drive many miles to reach her destination. = 她将开车很多英里才能到达目的地。

虚拟语气:
If I had walked a few more miles, I would have seen the lake. = 如果我再走几英里，我就能看到湖了。
If she had driven a few more miles, she would have reached the gas station. = 如果她再多开几英里，就能到达加油站了。

被动语态:
Many miles were covered on the journey. = 旅途中走了许多英里。
Many miles are driven daily on this highway. = 这条高速公路上每天行驶着许多英里。

疑问句:
How many miles is it to the city? = 到城市有多少英里？
How many miles did you drive today? = 你今天开了多少英里？

否定句:
