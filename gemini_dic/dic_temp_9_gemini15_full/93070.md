idolizer: /ˈaɪdɑːlaɪzər/ | n. | 偶像崇拜者；盲目崇拜者 | One who idolizes; a person who admires and reveres someone or something excessively.  | Example: He became an idolizers of the rock star, mimicking his style and mannerisms. 他成了这位摇滚巨星的狂热崇拜者，模仿他的风格和举止。 |近义词:worshipper, devotee, admirer | 反义词:critic, detractor

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The idolizers constantly send gifts to their favorite singer.  = 偶像崇拜者们不断地给他们的偶像歌手送礼物。
He idolizes his grandfather and follows his advice. = 他崇拜他的祖父并遵循他的建议。


一般过去时:
Many idolizers attended the concert last night. = 许多偶像崇拜者昨晚参加了音乐会。
She idolized that actress when she was a teenager. = 她十几岁的时候崇拜那位女演员。


现在完成时:

He has idolized her since he was a child. = 他从小就崇拜她。


过去完成时:
They had idolized the athlete before his scandal. = 在他丑闻爆发之前，他们一直崇拜这位运动员。
She had idolized him for years before realizing his flaws. = 在意识到他的缺点之前，她已经崇拜了他多年。


将来时:
Future generations will likely idolize this historical figure. = 后代很可能会崇拜这位历史人物。
I think people will continue to idolize this musician for years to come. = 我认为人们在未来几年还会继续崇拜这位音乐家。


虚拟语气:
If I were a young girl, I might idolize a famous actress. = 如果我是一个年轻女孩，我可能会崇拜一个著名女演员。

疑问句:

否定句:
