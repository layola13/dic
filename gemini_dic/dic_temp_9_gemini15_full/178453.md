reconstituting: /ˌriːkənˈstɪtjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 重建；复原；恢复原状 | To restore something to its former state or condition; to rebuild or reconstruct.  |  例句：The chef is reconstituting the dried soup mix by adding hot water.  厨师正在通过加热水来复原干汤料。

例句：The archaeologist carefully reconstituting the ancient pottery fragments. 考古学家小心翼翼地重建古代陶器碎片。


近义词：rebuilding, restoring, reconstructing, reviving

反义词：destroying, demolishing, ruining


USAGE->
