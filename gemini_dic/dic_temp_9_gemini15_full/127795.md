neighbouring:/ˈneɪbəriŋ/| adj.|邻近的，附近的|Adjacent or nearby.  It describes something that is close or next to something else.|例句：The neighbouring houses were all similar in style.  附近的房子风格都很相似。

例句：We visited several neighbouring villages during our holiday.  我们在假期期间参观了几个邻近的村庄。


近义词：adjacent, adjoining, nearby, close, proximate
反义词：distant, remote, far-off

词源:  The word "neighbouring" is derived from the Old English word "neahgebûr," which is a compound of "neah" (near) and "gebûr" (neighbor, dweller).  Over time, it evolved through Middle English to its modern form.
