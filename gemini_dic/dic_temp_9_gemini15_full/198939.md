sifted:/ˈsɪftɪd/| v. |筛；筛选；仔细检查|To separate finer particles from coarser ones by passing through a sieve or screen; to examine thoroughly. |例句：She sifted the flour before adding it to the batter. 她把面粉过筛后才加入面糊中。

例句：The police sifted through the evidence carefully. 警方仔细检查了证据。|近义词：screen, strain, sift through, examine, scrutinize |反义词：combine, mix, jumble
