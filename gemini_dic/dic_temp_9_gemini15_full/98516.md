instar:/ˈɪnstɑːr/ | n. |龄期，（昆虫的）幼虫期 | A stage in the development of an insect between two successive molts.  Instar refers to the specific developmental stage between molts, each instar representing a different size and sometimes form of the insect.  |Example: The caterpillar is in its fifth instar before pupation.  这只毛虫在化蛹前处于第五龄期。|近义词：stage, phase |反义词：adult stage
