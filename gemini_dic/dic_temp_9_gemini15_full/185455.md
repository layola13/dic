retwist:/riːˈtwɪst/| vt.|再次捻；重新扭转；重新编织|To twist again; to twist in a different way or direction.  It implies a prior twisting action that is being modified or repeated.|例句：She had to retwist her braids after they got wet. 她不得不把湿了的辫子重新编一下。

例句：The mechanic retwisted the wires to fix the faulty connection. 机械师重新拧了电线来修复故障的连接。


近义词：twist, rebraid, rework


反义词：untwist, straighten


词性:动词
