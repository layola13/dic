housemates:/ˈhaʊsmeɪts/ | n. |室友，同住者 | People who share a house.  |Example: I get along well with my housemates. 我和我的室友相处融洽。|近义词：flatmates, roommates |反义词:  N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My housemates are having a party tonight. = 我的室友今晚要开派对。
We often cook dinner together with our housemates. = 我们经常和室友一起做饭。

一般过去时:
My housemates went out last night. = 我的室友昨晚出去了。
Last year, my housemates and I shared a big apartment. = 去年，我和我的室友合租了一套大公寓。

现在完成时:
My housemates have cleaned the kitchen. = 我的室友已经打扫了厨房。
We have lived with our current housemates for two years. = 我们和现在的室友一起住了两年。

过去完成时:
I had already met my housemates before moving in.  = 我搬进去之前就已经认识我的室友了。
They had decided to find new housemates before the lease ended. = 在租约到期前，他们已经决定找新的室友。

将来时:
My housemates will be moving out next month. = 我的室友下个月要搬出去了。
We will invite our housemates to the barbecue. = 我们将邀请我们的室友来参加烧烤。

虚拟语气:
If I had better housemates, I'd be happier. = 如果我有更好的室友，我会更快乐。
I wish my housemates were more considerate. = 我希望我的室友更体贴一些。

被动语态:
The house was cleaned by my housemates. = 房子被我的室友打扫干净了。(很少用被动语态)
The rent was split evenly among the housemates. = 房租由室友平均分摊。(很少用被动语态)

疑问句:

否定句:
