reliquidating: /ˌriːˈlɪkwɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再清算；重新清算 |  The act of liquidating something again, typically assets or liabilities. This often involves a second round of selling off assets or settling debts after an initial liquidation process.  It implies a more thorough or complete liquidation than the first attempt. |例句：The company is reliquidating its remaining assets to pay off its remaining debts.  公司正在再次清算其剩余资产以偿还剩余债务。


The bank is reliquidating the failed business's holdings to ensure all creditors are satisfied. 银行正在再次清算这家倒闭企业的资产，以确保所有债权人都得到满足。| 近义词：reselling, revaluing, reassessing | 反义词：accumulating, consolidating
