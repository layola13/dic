roborate:/ˈrɒbəreɪt/|/ˈroʊbəreɪt/| vt.|证实；确证；加强|To strengthen or support with evidence or authority; to confirm. |例句：The witness's testimony corroborated the suspect's alibi. 目击证人的证词证实了嫌疑人的不在场证明。

例句：Further research corroborated the initial findings. 进一步的研究证实了最初的发现。


近义词：confirm, verify, substantiate, support |反义词：contradict, refute, deny |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence corroborates his story. = 证据证实了他的说法。
The witness corroborates the victim's account. = 证人证实了受害者的说法。


一般过去时:
The experiment corroborated the theory. = 实验证实了这个理论。
Historical records corroborated the legend. = 历史记录证实了这个传说。


现在完成时:
Recent studies have corroborated the link between smoking and lung cancer. = 最近的研究证实了吸烟和肺癌之间的联系。
Multiple sources have corroborated the information. = 多个来源证实了这一信息。


过去完成时:
By the time the trial ended, several witnesses had corroborated his testimony. = 到审判结束时，几位证人已经证实了他的证词。
Previous research had corroborated his findings. = 此前的研究已经证实了他的发现。


将来时:
Further investigation will corroborate these findings. = 进一步的调查将证实这些发现。
I believe future research will corroborate our hypothesis. = 我相信未来的研究将证实我们的假设。


虚拟语气:
If more evidence were to corroborate this claim, it would be revolutionary. = 如果有更多证据证实这一说法，那将是革命性的。
If the results had corroborated the hypothesis, we would have published the study. = 如果结果证实了假设，我们就发表了这项研究。


被动语态:
His alibi was corroborated by several witnesses. = 他的不在场证明得到了几名证人的证实。
The theory is corroborated by extensive research. = 这个理论得到了大量研究的证实。


疑问句:
Does this evidence corroborate the defendant's claim? = 证据是否证实了被告的说法？
Does the research corroborate the proposed theory? = 这项研究是否证实了所提出的理论？


否定句:
The new evidence did not corroborate the original findings. = 新证据并没有证实最初的发现。
The witness's testimony failed to corroborate the suspect's story. = 证人的证词未能证实嫌疑人的说法。


PHRASE->
corroborate evidence = 证实证据
corroborate a statement = 证实一份声明
corroborate a theory = 证实一个理论
corroborate a claim = 证实一个说法
corroborate findings = 证实研究结果
