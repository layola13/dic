These hormones are crucial for regulating metabolism, growth, and development. | The doctor prescribed medication containing iodothyrin to treat the patient's hypothyroidism.  医生开了含有碘甲状腺素的药物来治疗患者的甲状腺功能减退症。|近义词：thyroid hormone (甲状腺激素) |反义词：无
