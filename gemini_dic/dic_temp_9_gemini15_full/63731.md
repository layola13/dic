epiblast:/ˈɛpɪˌblæst/| n.|上胚层|The epiblast is the outermost layer of the bilaminar germ disc, the earliest stage of embryonic development in animals.  It's the precursor to the ectoderm and contributes to the mesoderm and endoderm.  It's characterized by its columnar epithelial cells and its role in gastrulation, a crucial process in establishing the three germ layers.|例句：The epiblast cells undergo dramatic movements during gastrulation. 上胚层细胞在原肠胚形成过程中经历了剧烈的运动。|例句：Differentiation of the epiblast gives rise to the three germ layers. 上胚层的分化产生了三个胚层。|近义词：primitive ectoderm|反义词：hypoblast
