pied-à-terre: /ˌpiːeɪdəˈtɛər/ | n. | 住所，临时住所 | A small apartment or house in a city, used occasionally by someone who lives somewhere else.  It is typically a convenient place to stay while visiting a city. | 例句：She keeps a pied-à-terre in Paris. = 她住在巴黎的一套小公寓里。(用于偶尔居住)  He uses his pied-à-terre for business trips. = 他把他的临时住所用于商务旅行。| 近义词：second home, city apartment, crash pad | 反义词：permanent residence, main home | 名词
