exploiter:/ɪkˈsplɔɪtər/| n.|剥削者，利用者| A person who uses someone or something unfairly for their own advantage.  |例句：The factory owner was an exploiter who paid his workers very low wages.  这家工厂老板是个剥削者，他给工人们的工资非常低。|例句：He was accused of being an exploiter of cheap labor. 他被指控是廉价劳力的剥削者。|近义词：oppressor, abuser, leech|反义词：benefactor, altruist|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The corporation exploits its workers. = 这家公司剥削它的工人。
He exploits every opportunity. = 他利用每一个机会。


一般过去时:
They exploited the workers' desperation. = 他们利用了工人们的绝望。
She exploited a loophole in the law. = 她利用了法律中的漏洞。


现在完成时:
They have exploited the natural resources of the region. = 他们已经利用了该地区的自然资源。
He has exploited his position for personal gain. = 他利用自己的职位谋取私利。


过去完成时:
The company had exploited the market before its competitors. = 这家公司在竞争对手之前就已利用了市场。
She had exploited her talents to achieve success. = 她已经充分发挥了自己的才能以取得成功。


将来时:
They will exploit the new technology to their advantage. = 他们将利用这项新技术来获得优势。
He will exploit the situation to his benefit. = 他将利用这种情况对自己有利。


虚拟语气:
If they exploited the resources responsibly, the environment wouldn't suffer. = 如果他们负责任地利用资源，环境就不会受到损害。
I wish they hadn't exploited the workers so ruthlessly. = 我真希望他们没有如此残酷地剥削工人。


被动语态:
The workers were exploited by the factory owner. = 工人们被工厂老板剥削了。
The situation was exploited by the politician for his own gain. = 政治家利用了这种情况来谋取私利。


疑问句:

否定句:
