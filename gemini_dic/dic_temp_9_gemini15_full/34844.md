clinics:/ˈklɪnɪks/ | n. |诊所，临床教学 | A clinic is a place where people can receive medical treatment, often specializing in a particular area.  Clinics can range from small, private practices to large, hospital-affiliated facilities. |例句：He went to the clinic for a check-up. 他去诊所做体检。|例句：Medical students attend clinics to gain practical experience. 医学院的学生参加临床教学以获得实践经验。|近义词：medical center, dispensary, surgery |反义词：hospital (in some contexts, clinics can be considered smaller-scale than hospitals) |名词
