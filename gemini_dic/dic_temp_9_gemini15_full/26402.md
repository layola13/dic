bycatches:/ˈbaɪˌkætʃɪz/| n. |兼捕，误捕|Unintentional catches of non-target species while fishing.  Bycatches are a significant problem in fisheries, often resulting in the death of many unwanted animals.|例句：The trawler's bycatches included several endangered sea turtles. 拖网渔船的兼捕物种包括几只濒危海龟。
The shrimp fishery has a high rate of bycatches, including various fish and marine mammals. 虾类渔业的兼捕率很高，包括各种鱼类和海洋哺乳动物。|近义词：incidental catch, unwanted catch|反义词：target catch
