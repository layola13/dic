moussakas:/muːsəˈkɑːs/| n. |穆萨卡|A Greek casserole of layered eggplant, potatoes, minced meat (usually lamb), and a creamy béchamel sauce, baked in the oven.|例句：My grandmother's moussakas is the best I've ever tasted.  我奶奶做的穆萨卡是我吃过最好吃的。|例句：We're having moussakas for dinner tonight. 今晚我们吃穆萨卡。|近义词：casserole, gratin|反义词：None|名词
