mis-analyzing:/ˌmɪsænəˈlaɪzɪŋ/| v.|误解，分析错误|The act of interpreting or understanding something incorrectly.  It implies a flawed or incomplete analysis leading to an inaccurate conclusion. |例句：He was mis-analyzing the data, leading to flawed conclusions. 他误解了数据，导致结论有误。

例句：Mis-analyzing the market trends cost the company millions.  误判市场趋势使公司损失数百万。


近义词：misinterpreting, misunderstanding, misreading, wrongly interpreting


反义词：correctly analyzing, accurately interpreting, understanding correctly


词性：动词

USAGE->

We mis-analyze the problem, and thus fail to find the right solution. = 我们误解了问题，因此无法找到正确的解决方案。
