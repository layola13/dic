locomotives:/ˈloʊkəmoʊtivz/| n.|机车，火车头|A locomotive is a powered rail vehicle used for hauling trains.  It is typically a powerful engine that pulls or pushes carriages carrying passengers or freight.|例句：The powerful locomotive pulled the long freight train across the plains. 这台强大的机车拉着长长的货运列车穿过平原。

例句：Several old locomotives are on display at the railway museum.  几台老式机车在铁路博物馆展出。|近义词：engine, train engine|反义词：carriage, wagon
