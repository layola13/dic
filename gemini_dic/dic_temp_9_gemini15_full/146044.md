英文解析:
As a noun, "overdrive" refers to the act of driving a vehicle faster than its recommended speed or the operating speed beyond the normal limits.  It can also refer to a gear in a vehicle that allows for higher speeds.  As a verb, "overdrive" means to drive (a vehicle) at a speed exceeding the recommended limit or to operate (a machine) at a speed or power beyond its normal capacity. It often implies pushing something beyond its intended limits, possibly resulting in damage or failure.
