reboots:/ˌriːˈbuːts/| vt. n.|重启，重新启动；恢复活力|To restart (a computer or other electronic device)；To revive or restore something.|例句：The computer rebooted after a power surge.  电脑在电压波动后重新启动了。|例句：The company is undergoing a reboot to improve its image. 这家公司正在进行一次大改革以改善其形象。|近义词：restart, relaunch, revive|反义词：shutdown, terminate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system reboots automatically after an error. = 系统在出现错误后会自动重启。
He reboots his computer every morning. = 他每天早上都会重启电脑。


一般过去时:
I rebooted my computer after it froze. = 电脑死机后我重启了它。
They rebooted the server to fix the problem. = 他们重启服务器来解决问题。


现在完成时:
We have rebooted the server several times today. = 我们今天已经重启了服务器好几次了。
She has rebooted her career with a new job. = 她凭借一份新工作重振了她的职业生涯。


过去完成时:
The technician had rebooted the system before I arrived. = 我到达之前技术人员已经重启了系统。
They had rebooted the website several times before it finally worked. = 网站在最终运行之前，他们已经重启了好几次。


将来时:
I will reboot the computer if it continues to crash. = 如果电脑继续崩溃，我将重启它。
We will reboot the project with a new team. = 我们将用一个新的团队重启这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reboot the router. = 如果我是你，我会重启路由器。
It would be better if we rebooted the entire system. = 如果我们重启整个系统会更好。


被动语态:
The computer was rebooted remotely. = 电脑被远程重启了。
The project is being rebooted after the initial failure. = 项目在最初失败后正在重新启动。


疑问句:
Have you rebooted your computer recently? = 你最近重启过电脑吗？
Should we reboot the server now? = 我们现在应该重启服务器吗？


否定句:
I didn't reboot my computer yesterday. = 我昨天没有重启我的电脑。
They haven't rebooted the system yet. = 他们还没有重启系统。


PHRASE->
