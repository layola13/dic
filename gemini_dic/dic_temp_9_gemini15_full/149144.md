oxidizing:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | /ˈɑːksɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 氧化的；助氧的 |  Causing or involving oxidation; relating to or characterized by oxidation.  | Example:  Oxidizing agents are commonly used in bleaching processes.  氧化剂常用于漂白过程。

The oxidizing atmosphere in the furnace helped to speed up the reaction.  炉中氧化气氛有助于加快反应速度。|近义词：oxidative, oxidizing, oxygenating | 反义词：reducing

The chemical is oxidizing rapidly. = 这种化学物质正在迅速氧化。
The process is oxidizing the metal. = 该过程正在使金属氧化。


一般过去时:
The fruit oxidized quickly after being cut. = 水果被切开后很快氧化了。
The iron oxidized over time, forming rust. = 铁随着时间的推移氧化了，形成了锈。

否定句:

词源:
From the verb "oxidize," which itself comes from "oxygen," ultimately from Greek *ὀξύς (oxús, "sharp, acid, keen") + -γενής (-genēs, "producing").  The suffix "-izing" indicates the process of or causing something.
