robotics:/roʊˈbɑːtɪks/| n. |机器人技术；机器人学 |The branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, operation, and application of robots.  Robotics involves the integration of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, and artificial intelligence. |Example:  The advancements in robotics have revolutionized many industries. 机器人技术的进步彻底改变了许多行业。

The field of robotics is rapidly expanding. 机器人技术领域正在迅速发展。


近义词：robot engineering, automation technology |反义词：无
