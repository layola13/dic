crawfish: /ˈkrɔːfɪʃ/ | n. |小龙虾，蝲蛄|A freshwater crustacean with a long body, a hard shell, and large claws.  They are popular as food. |例句：We had a delicious crawfish boil last night.  昨晚我们吃了一顿美味的小龙虾宴。

例句：The children enjoyed catching crawfish in the creek. 孩子们喜欢在小溪里抓小龙虾。


近义词：crayfish, crawdad, mudbug


反义词：无


词源:  From Old French *crevis, from Old Northern French crevice, crevis, diminutive of crevice "crevice, cleft," from Vulgar Latin *crepissa, from Latin crepa "crack."  The shift in meaning from "crevice" to "crayfish" is believed to be due to the creature's habit of dwelling in crevices.
