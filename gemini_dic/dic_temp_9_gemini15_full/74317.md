fracturing: /ˈfrækʃərɪŋ/ | v. |压裂；破裂；使破裂 | The process of breaking something into smaller pieces, especially rock or other hard materials, often through the application of pressure or force.  It can also refer to the act of causing something to break apart, figuratively or literally. |例句：Hydraulic fracturing is a common method used in the oil and gas industry to extract resources.  水力压裂是石油和天然气行业中常用的开采资源的方法。


The shale was fractured during the earthquake.  页岩在地震中破裂了。


近义词：splitting, cracking, shattering, breaking, rupturing | 反义词：uniting, joining, mending, repairing | 词性:动词
