endogamies:/ˌɛndoʊˈɡæmiːz/ | n. | 内婚制；近亲结婚 | The practice of marrying only within one's own group, tribe, or social class.  Endogamies often lead to a decrease in genetic diversity within the population. | Example:  The strict endogamies of the community prevented outsiders from marrying into their families.  内婚制的社区严格禁止外人与他们家族通婚。 |近义词：inbreeding, consanguinity |反义词：exogamy
