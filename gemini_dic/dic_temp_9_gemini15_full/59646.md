echeloned:/ˈeʃəlɒnd/| adj.|梯形的，分层的，逐级排列的| Arranged in a staggered or layered formation, like the steps of a ladder or the ranks of an army.  It implies a hierarchical structure or a gradual progression. | 例句：The troops were echeloned along the ridge, providing overlapping fields of fire.  部队沿着山脊呈梯队排列，提供了重叠的火力范围。


The company's management structure is highly echeloned, with multiple layers of authority. 公司的管理结构是高度分层的，有多个级别的管理层。|近义词：layered, tiered, staggered, graduated|反义词：uniform, unranked, flat
