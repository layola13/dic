non-self-governing:/ˌnɒnˈsɛlfˈɡʌvənɪŋ/|/ˌnɑːnˈsɛlfˈɡʌvərniŋ/| adj.|非自治的，自我管理不足的|Not having self-government; subject to the authority of another government or power.|The UN's Committee on Decolonization works to support non-self-governing territories in their paths to independence.  联合国非殖民化委员会致力于支持非自治地区走向独立的道路。

Many non-self-governing territories still face challenges in achieving full autonomy.  许多非自治地区在实现完全自治方面仍然面临挑战。


近义词：dependent, autonomous (反义词), self-governing (反义词)
反义词：self-governing, independent, autonomous
词性：形容词
