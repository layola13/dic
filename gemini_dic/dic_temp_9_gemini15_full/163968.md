predictates:/ˈprɛdɪkets/ | n. |谓词，述语 |The predicates in a sentence describe the action or state of being of the subject.  谓词在句子中描述主语的动作或状态。| The sentence "The dog barked loudly" has "barked loudly" as its predicate.  句子“狗大声吠叫”的谓词是“大声吠叫”。 |近义词：verb phrase, predication |反义词：subject
