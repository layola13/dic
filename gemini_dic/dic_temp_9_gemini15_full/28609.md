caressed:/kəˈrɛst/ | vt. |爱抚；抚摸；轻抚 |To gently touch or stroke someone or something with one's hands in a loving or tender way.  |例句：She caressed the soft fur of the kitten. 她轻轻抚摸着小猫柔软的毛皮。

例句：He caressed her cheek gently. 他轻轻地抚摸着她的脸颊。


近义词：stroked, fondled, patted | 反义词：struck, slapped, hit | 动词


USAGE->

He caresses the dog every evening. 他每天晚上都抚摸着那条狗。
She caresses her child's hair before bed.  她睡前总是抚摸孩子的头发。

词源:
From Old French *caresser, from Vulgar Latin *caressare, frequentative of *carēre "to be dear, to be loved".  Ultimately related to Latin carus "dear, beloved".
