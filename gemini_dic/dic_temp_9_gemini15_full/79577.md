glooming:/ˈɡluːmɪŋ/| adj.|阴沉的；忧郁的；令人沮丧的|Dark and gloomy; depressing and causing a feeling of sadness.|The glooming sky threatened rain.  阴沉的天空预示着要下雨。|The glooming atmosphere in the room was palpable. 房间里阴沉的气氛令人窒息。|dark, somber, depressing|bright, cheerful, sunny|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sky is glooming. 天色阴沉。
The future looks glooming. 未来看起来很黯淡。

一般过去时:
The weather was glooming yesterday. 昨天的天气阴沉。
His face looked glooming after he heard the news. 他听到这个消息后脸色阴沉。

现在完成时:
The news has been glooming all week.  这周的消息一直令人沮丧。
The situation has become increasingly glooming. 局势变得越来越令人沮丧。

过去完成时:
The atmosphere had been glooming all day.  一整天气氛都很压抑。
He had felt glooming about the project's failure. 他对项目的失败感到沮丧。

将来时:
The forecast predicts that it will be glooming tomorrow. 预报说明天天气会阴沉。
I fear the future will be glooming for many. 我担心未来对许多人来说都会黯淡无光。

虚拟语气:
If the weather were glooming, we wouldn't go hiking. 如果天气阴沉，我们就不会去远足。
I wish the situation weren't so glooming. 我希望情况不要那么令人沮丧。

被动语态:
The room was made glooming by the heavy curtains. 厚重的窗帘使房间显得阴沉。
His prospects were gloomed by the recent economic downturn. 他最近的前景因经济衰退而变得黯淡。


疑问句:
Is the outlook glooming? 前景黯淡吗？
Does the future seem glooming to you? 你觉得未来看起来黯淡吗？


否定句:
The day wasn't glooming; it was sunny.  那天不是阴沉的；而是阳光明媚的。
The report wasn't glooming; it was actually quite optimistic. 报告并非令人沮丧；实际上相当乐观。
