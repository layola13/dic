carob:/ˈkærəb/| n.|角豆树；角豆粉；角豆胶|The carob tree is a small evergreen tree native to the Mediterranean region. Its pods are used to make carob powder, a sweet and chocolate-like substance.  Carob is also used as a thickener in various food products.|例句：The carob pods are harvested and processed to create carob powder. 角豆荚被收割和加工以制成角豆粉。

例句：Many people use carob powder as a healthier alternative to chocolate. 许多人使用角豆粉作为巧克力更健康的替代品。|近义词：locust bean (referring to the tree)|反义词：无|名词
