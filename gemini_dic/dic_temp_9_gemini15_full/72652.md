flywheels:/ˈflaɪˌwiːlz/| n.|飞轮；惯性轮|A flywheel is a heavy wheel that is used to store energy and help to keep a machine running smoothly.  It works by storing kinetic energy when the machine is running fast and releasing it when the machine slows down.|例句：The engine uses a flywheel to smooth out the power delivery.  引擎使用飞轮来使动力输出更加平稳。

The flywheel on the potter's wheel helps to maintain a constant speed. 陶工轮上的飞轮有助于保持恒定的速度。|近义词：inertia wheel, momentum wheel|反义词：None
