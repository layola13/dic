half-theatrical: /ˌhæfθiˈætrɪkəl/ | adj. | 半戏剧性的；略带戏剧性的 |  Suggestive of or resembling theatrical performance; partly theatrical.  It implies an element of exaggeration, artificiality, or dramatic flair, but not to the full extent of a theatrical performance.  |


例句：

His half-theatrical apology felt insincere.  他的半真半假的道歉听起来不真诚。

Her half-theatrical outburst surprised everyone in the room. 她略带戏剧性的爆发让房间里的每个人都大吃一惊。


近义词：dramatic, melodramatic, histrionic, affected


反义词：genuine, sincere, authentic, understated


词性：形容词


USAGE->  (由于half-theatrical本身是形容词，没有动词用法，以下只列举形容词的用法)

比较级：more half-theatrical

最高级：most half-theatrical

例句：

His reaction was more half-theatrical than genuinely upset. 他的反应与其说是真生气，不如说是装腔作势。

That was the most half-theatrical performance I've ever witnessed. 那是我见过的最做作的表演。
