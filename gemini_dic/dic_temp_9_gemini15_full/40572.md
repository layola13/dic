contuses:/kənˈtuːzɪz/| n. |挫伤；瘀伤 | bruises caused by blunt force trauma, characterized by swelling, pain, and discoloration.  Contuses are injuries to soft tissues,  often caused by impacts or blows. They don't usually involve broken bones.  | Example: The football player suffered several contuses after a particularly rough game.  足球运动员在一次特别激烈的比赛后遭受了几处挫伤。  |近义词：bruises, contusions | 反义词：intact, uninjured | 名词
