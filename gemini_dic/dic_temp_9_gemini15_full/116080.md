martlet:/ˈmɑːrtlɪt/ | n. | 小燕子；家燕；（纹章学）燕子图案 | A small swallow; in heraldry, a small swallow depicted without legs.  The term is often used poetically or in a heraldic context. | Example:  The heraldic martlet on the shield represented his family's lineage.  这个盾牌上的纹章燕子图案代表着他家族的血统。
