ironstone:/ˈaɪərnstoʊn/ | n. |铁矿石，铁质岩石 | A sedimentary rock that is rich in iron.  It is often used in the production of iron and steel.  | 例句：The miners extracted ironstone from the deep mines. 矿工们从深矿中开采铁矿石。

| 例句：This area is known for its rich deposits of ironstone. 这个地区以其丰富的铁矿石矿藏而闻名。 | 近义词：iron ore, ferruginous rock | 反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与非铁质岩石对比，例如limestone (石灰石) |  词性: 名词
