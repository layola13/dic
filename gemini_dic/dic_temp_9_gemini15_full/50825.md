descriptive: /dɪˈskrɪptɪv/ | adj. | 描写的；叙述的；具有描写性的 | Giving a description; relating to or involving the description of something.  |例句：He gave a descriptive account of the accident. 他对事故做了详细的描述。

例句：The novel is filled with descriptive passages of the countryside. 这部小说充满了对乡村的生动描写。


近义词：detailed, graphic, vivid, pictorial | 反义词：vague, abstract, general, concise
