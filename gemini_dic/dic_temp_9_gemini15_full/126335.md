myositic:/ˌmaɪəˈsaɪtɪk/| adj.|肌炎的；肌炎性的|Pertaining to or characterized by myositis (inflammation of muscle tissue).  It describes a condition affecting the muscles.|例句：The patient presented with myositic symptoms, including muscle pain and weakness.  患者出现肌炎症状，包括肌肉疼痛和无力。|近义词：muscular, myopathic|反义词：healthy, uninflamed
