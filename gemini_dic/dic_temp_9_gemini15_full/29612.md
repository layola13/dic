categorization:/ˌkætəɡəraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |分类，归类 |The process of assigning something to a category or class.  This involves identifying shared characteristics and grouping items accordingly.  It's a fundamental process in organizing information and understanding the world. |例句：The categorization of these plants is based on their flowering patterns.  这些植物的分类是根据它们的开花模式进行的。

例句：The library's categorization system made it easy to find the books I needed. 图书馆的分类系统使我很容易找到我需要的书。


近义词：classification, grouping, sorting, organization

反义词：randomness, disorganization


词性：名词
