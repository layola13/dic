coal:/koʊl/| n. |煤|A black or dark brown combustible sedimentary rock formed from ancient plant matter.  It is a fossil fuel and an important source of energy.  |例句：The power plant burns coal to generate electricity. 这家发电厂燃烧煤炭发电。|例句：The miners extracted coal from the deep mine. 矿工们从深矿中开采煤炭。|近义词：fuel, coke|反义词：renewable energy

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:
They will continue to use coal for the foreseeable future. = 在可预见的未来，他们将继续使用煤炭。
The country will likely phase out coal power gradually. = 该国可能会逐步淘汰燃煤发电。


虚拟语气:

疑问句:
