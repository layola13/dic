disassociated: /ˌdɪsəˈsoʊʃieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 分离的；脱节的；互不相关的 |

英文解析:  Not connected or associated; separated or detached.  It implies a lack of connection or relationship between things that might otherwise be expected to be linked.

例句:

He felt disassociated from the group, as if he were an outsider. = 他感觉自己与群体脱节，仿佛是个局外人。

The two concepts are completely disassociated in my mind. = 在我的头脑中，这两个概念完全没有联系。


近义词: unconnected, separate, detached, unrelated, disjoined, independent

反义词: associated, connected, related, linked, joined


词源:  The word comes from the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of"), and the verb "associate" (meaning "to connect or relate").  "Associate" itself has roots in the Latin word "associare," meaning "to join together."
