inflammatory: /ɪnˈflæməˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 炎症的；煽动性的；激烈的 |

英文解析：Causing or characterized by inflammation; tending to arouse strong feelings, especially anger or hostility.  The word refers to something that causes inflammation (a localized physical condition in which part of the body becomes reddened, swollen, hot, and often painful, due to infection or injury) or something that excites strong emotions.


例句：

The doctor prescribed anti-inflammatory medication to reduce the swelling.  医生开了消炎药来消肿。

His inflammatory remarks sparked a heated debate. 他煽动性的言论引发了一场激烈的辩论。


近义词：irritating, provocative, incendiary, agitating


反义词：soothing, calming, pacific, conciliatory


词性：形容词
