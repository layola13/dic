ordinations:/ˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |圣职授任；任命；排序；规则；规定 |The act of ordaining someone, especially into the priesthood; the conferring of holy orders.  It can also refer to the act of arranging things in a particular order or sequence.|例句：The ordinations will take place at the cathedral next Sunday.  下周日，圣职授任仪式将在教堂举行。

The recent ordinations have brought new energy to the church. 最近的圣职任命给教堂带来了新的活力。|近义词：consecrations, installations, appointments |反义词：dismissals, removals
