pentathlon:/ˈpɛntæθlɒn/ | n. |五项全能；五项运动 |A pentathlon is an athletic contest consisting of five events.  It is often used to refer to the modern pentathlon, which consists of fencing, swimming, equestrian show jumping, pistol shooting, and cross-country running.  |Example: He competed in the modern pentathlon and won a bronze medal. 他参加了现代五项全能并获得铜牌。|近义词：multi-event competition, combined event |反义词：single event competition
