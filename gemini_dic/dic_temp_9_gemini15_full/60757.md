electroglow:/ɪˈlɛktrəʊɡloʊ/| adj.|电光, 电致发光|Describes something that emits light through electroluminescence, a phenomenon where a material emits light in response to an electric current.  It often refers to a type of paint or coating that glows when electricity is applied.|The electroglow paint made the car dashboard strikingly visible at night.|近义词：phosphorescent, luminescent|反义词：dark, dull
