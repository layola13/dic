polygamy:/pəˈlɪɡəmi/ | n. |一夫多妻制；一妻多夫制 |The practice or custom of having more than one spouse at the same time.  |例句：Polygamy is illegal in most Western countries.  一夫多妻制在大多数西方国家都是非法的。|例句：Some cultures traditionally practice polygamy, either polygyny or polyandry. 一些文化传统上实行一夫多妻制或一妻多夫制。|近义词：bigamy (if specifically referring to having two spouses), plural marriage |反义词：monogamy


词源:

From poly- + -gamy.  "poly-" meaning "many" and "-gamy" from the Greek γάμος (gamos) meaning "marriage".  Therefore, it literally means "many marriages".
