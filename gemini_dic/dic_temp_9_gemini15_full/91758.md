hyperemotionality:/ˌhaɪpərˌiːmoʊʃənˈæləti/ | n. |高度情绪化，过度情绪化 |The state of experiencing emotions with excessive intensity or frequency.  This can manifest in various ways, including extreme emotional lability (rapid shifts in mood), heightened sensitivity to emotional stimuli, and difficulty regulating emotional responses.  It's often associated with certain mental health conditions. | Example: Her hyperemotionality made it difficult for her to maintain stable relationships.  她的高度情绪化让她难以维持稳定的关系。  His hyperemotionality often led to impulsive behaviors.  他的过度情绪化经常导致冲动行为。|近义词：emotional lability, emotional dysregulation, affective instability |反义词：emotional stability, emotional calmness, emotional regulation |名词
