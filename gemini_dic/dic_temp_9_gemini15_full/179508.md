reef:/riːf/| n.|礁石，暗礁|A reef is a ridge of rock or sand, often near the surface of the water.  Reefs can be made of coral, rock, or other materials. They often provide habitats for a wide variety of marine life.|例句：The coral reef teemed with colorful fish.  珊瑚礁充满了色彩斑斓的鱼。|例句：The ship ran aground on a rocky reef. 船触礁搁浅了。|近义词：rock, shoal, bank|反义词：deep water, open sea
