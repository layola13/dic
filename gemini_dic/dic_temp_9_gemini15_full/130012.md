non-observance:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːvəns/| n. |不遵守；未遵守；忽略|The failure to comply with a rule, law, or custom.  It implies a conscious or unconscious disregard for something that should be observed.|例句：The non-observance of safety regulations led to the accident.  不遵守安全条例导致了事故。|例句：His non-observance of the deadline resulted in a penalty. 他没有遵守最后期限，导致被罚款。|近义词：disregard, neglect, violation, breach|反义词：observance, compliance, adherence|名词
