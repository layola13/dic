pehlevi:/pɛˈleɪvi/| n. |帕提亚语；佩尔塞波利斯语|Middle Persian, the language of the Sasanian Empire (224-651 CE).  It's written in a script derived from Aramaic.  The language is extinct but is important for understanding the history and culture of ancient Persia and its influence on later Persian languages.| The Pehlevi inscriptions on the rock carvings provide valuable insights into the Sasanian period.  帕提亚语的岩石雕刻铭文为萨珊王朝时期提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：Middle Persian, Sasanian Persian|反义词：无
