opportunely:/ˌɒpəˈtjuːnli/ /ˌɑːpərˈtuːnli/ | adv. |适时地；恰好地；及时地 |Happening at a suitable or advantageous time.  |例句：He opportunely arrived just as the meeting was about to begin. 他恰好在会议即将开始时到达。

例句：The rain opportunely stopped just as we reached the shelter. 雨水在我们到达避难所时恰好停了。

近义词：timely, seasonably, conveniently, aptly

反义词：inconveniently, untimely, inappropriately

词性：副词
