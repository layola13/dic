headhunted: /ˈhɛdˌhʌntɪd/ | adj. v. |猎头招募的；被猎头公司挖角的 |  actively recruited, especially by a specialized agency |

英文解析:Headhunting refers to the practice of actively recruiting individuals for senior positions within a company.  It often involves approaching candidates who are not actively seeking new employment, targeting them for their specific skills and experience.  The term implies a more aggressive and targeted approach than traditional recruitment methods.

例句:

He was headhunted by a major tech company. = 他被一家大型科技公司挖角了。

She was headhunted for the CEO position. = 她被猎头公司推荐担任CEO职位。


近义词: recruited, poached, sought after

反义词: ignored, overlooked


词性: Verb (past participle used as an adjective)
