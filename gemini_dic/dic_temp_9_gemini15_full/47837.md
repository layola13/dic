decayed:/dɪˈkeɪd/| adj.|腐烂的；衰败的；衰退的|Something that has decayed is rotted or decomposed, often due to natural processes. It can also refer to a decline in quality, strength, or power.|例句：The decayed fruit was covered in mold.  腐烂的水果上长满了霉菌。
The decayed building needed immediate repair.  这座破败的建筑需要立即维修。|近义词：rotten, decomposed, deteriorated, ruined|反义词：fresh, intact, flourishing, thriving


USAGE->

The wood decays slowly in dry weather. = 木头在干燥的天气下腐烂得很慢。
This system is decaying gradually. = 这个系统正在逐渐衰败。


一般过去时:
The apple decayed quickly in the heat. = 苹果在高温下很快腐烂了。
The empire decayed after the death of the emperor. = 皇帝死后，帝国开始衰败。

过去完成时:
