mommy:/ˈmɑːmi/| n. |妈妈|A child's word for mother.  It is a term of endearment and is often used informally.| Example: "Mommy, can I have a cookie?"  妈妈，我能吃块饼干吗？|近义词：mom, mother, mama|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Mommy makes me breakfast every morning. = 妈妈每天早上都给我做早餐。
Mommy loves me very much. = 妈妈非常爱我。

一般过去时:
Mommy took me to the park yesterday. = 妈妈昨天带我去公园了。
Mommy read me a story before bed last night. = 妈妈昨晚睡前给我读了一个故事。

现在完成时:
Mommy has made dinner. = 妈妈已经做好了晚饭。
Mommy has bought me a new toy. = 妈妈给我买了一个新玩具。

过去完成时:
Mommy had already left when I woke up. = 我醒来的时候妈妈已经离开了。
Mommy had prepared a surprise party before I arrived. = 我到的时候，妈妈已经准备好了一个惊喜派对。

将来时:
Mommy will pick me up from school. = 妈妈会去学校接我。
Mommy will cook my favorite dish tonight. = 妈妈今晚会做我最喜欢的菜。

虚拟语气:
If Mommy were here, she would help me. = 如果妈妈在这里，她会帮助我的。
I wish Mommy could come to my school play. = 我希望妈妈能来看我的学校演出。

被动语态:
Dinner is made by Mommy. = 晚饭是妈妈做的。
I was taken to the zoo by Mommy. = 妈妈带我去动物园了。

疑问句:
Where is Mommy? = 妈妈在哪里？
What is Mommy doing? = 妈妈在做什么？

否定句:
Mommy doesn't like loud noises. = 妈妈不喜欢吵闹的声音。
Mommy didn't go to work today. = 妈妈今天没去上班。
