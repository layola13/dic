ecclesiastic:/ɪˌkliːziˈæstɪk/ | n., adj. | 教会人员；宗教的；教会的 | A person who is a member of the clergy or a religious official.  The adjective describes things relating to the church or clergy. |例句：The ecclesiastic delivered a powerful sermon.  这位教士发表了一篇有力的布道。

The local ecclesiastic community is very active. 当地的教会团体非常活跃。 |近义词：clergyman, cleric, priest, minister, religious |反义词：lay, secular, worldly
