puffery:/ˈpʌfəri/| n.|夸张宣传，吹嘘；浮夸之辞 |Exaggerated or extravagant praise; the act of praising something in an excessive way.  It often involves making unsubstantiated claims or promises. |例句：The advertisement was full of puffery, promising unrealistic results. 这则广告充满了夸张宣传，承诺了不切实际的结果。
The company's puffery about its new product quickly faded when customers experienced its shortcomings.  这家公司对其新产品的吹嘘之词，在顾客体验到其缺点后很快就消失了。|近义词：hype, exaggeration, boasting, bombast |反义词：understatement, truth, modesty |名词
