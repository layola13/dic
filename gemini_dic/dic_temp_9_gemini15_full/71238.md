fittest:/ˈfɪtɪst/| adj.|最适合的；最健康的；最强的|Most suitable or capable;  possessing the greatest physical strength and endurance.  The word often appears in the context of natural selection and survival of the fittest.|例句：He's the fittest person I know. 他是我认识的最健康的人。
The fittest candidate will be selected for the job. 最合适的候选人将被选中担任这份工作。|近义词：most suitable, strongest, healthiest|反义词：weakest, least suitable, unhealthiest|词性:形容词
