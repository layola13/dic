optimizer:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzər/| n. |优化器，优化算法|A computer program or algorithm that adjusts parameters to minimize or maximize an objective function.  It's used in machine learning to improve the performance of models. |Example:  The Adam optimizer is commonly used in deep learning.  Adam优化器通常用于深度学习。|近义词：optimizing algorithm, tuning algorithm|反义词：None (Antonyms are not directly applicable, as it's a process, not a quality.)|名词
