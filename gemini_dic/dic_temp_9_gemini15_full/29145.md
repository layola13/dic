casement:/ˈkeɪsmənt/| n.|窗扇，窗框|A casement is a window that is hinged at the side and opens outwards.  It's often found in older buildings and is characterized by its outward-swinging design.|The old house had charming casement windows.|近义词：window, sash|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The casement window is open. = 窗扇是开着的。
He closes the casement every night. = 他每天晚上都关上窗扇。


一般过去时:
She opened the casement to let in the fresh air. = 她打开窗扇让新鲜空气进来。
The strong wind broke the casement. = 大风把窗扇吹坏了。


现在完成时:
They have replaced all the old casements. = 他们已经更换了所有旧的窗扇。
We have never cleaned these casements before. = 我们以前从未清洁过这些窗扇。


过去完成时:
The casement had already been broken before the storm arrived. =暴风雨来临之前，窗扇就已经坏了。
He had closed the casement before going to bed. = 他睡觉前已经关上了窗扇。


将来时:
They will install new casements next week. = 他们下周将安装新的窗扇。
I will open the casement when the weather gets warmer. = 天气暖和起来的时候我会打开窗扇。


虚拟语气:
If the casement were open, we would have fresh air. = 如果窗扇开着，我们就会有新鲜空气。
I wish the casement were easier to open. = 我希望窗扇更容易打开。


被动语态:
The casement was opened by the wind. = 窗扇被风吹开了。
The casements are being repaired now. = 窗扇正在修理中。


疑问句:
Is the casement locked? = 窗扇锁上了吗？
Are those casements made of wood? = 那些窗扇是木头做的吗？


否定句:

He didn't close the casement last night. = 他昨晚没有关窗扇。


PHRASE->
casement window = 窗扇
double-hung casement = 双悬窗（虽然“双悬”通常指上下滑动的窗户，但有时也用于描述带有两个可打开部分的铰链式窗户）
casement fastener = 窗扇扣
lead casement = 铅窗扇（一种历史建筑中常见的窗扇）
casement stay = 窗扇撑
