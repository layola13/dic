misdeliver:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvər/| vt.|错误递送，送错|To deliver something to the wrong place or person.  This verb implies an error in the delivery process, resulting in the item not reaching its intended recipient.|The package was misdelivered to a neighboring house. =包裹被错送到隔壁的房子。|The courier misdelivered the urgent documents. =快递员送错了紧急文件。|deliver, send|correctly deliver, deliver accurately|动词
