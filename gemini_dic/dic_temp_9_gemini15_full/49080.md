dehyphened:/diːˈhaɪfənd/| adj.|已去除连字符的|Removed the hyphens from (a word or phrase).  This usually refers to a compound word or phrase that has been written as a single word after previously being hyphenated or written as separate words.|例句：The company decided to dehyphenate its brand name, making it easier to remember. 公司决定去掉其品牌名称中的连字符，使其更容易记住。|近义词：unhyphenated, combined, merged |反义词：hyphenated
