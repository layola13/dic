overdoing:/ˌoʊvərˈduːɪŋ/ | v. |做得过分；过度；夸张 |Doing something to an excessive degree.  |例句：He's overdoing the exercise; he needs to rest. 他运动过度；他需要休息。

例句：She's overdoing the makeup; it looks unnatural. 她化妆太浓了；看起来不自然。


近义词：exaggerating, overemphasizing, overacting


反义词：underdoing, neglecting, minimizing


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overdoes his work every day. = 他每天都工作过度。
They overdo the seasoning in their cooking. = 他们在烹饪中放太多的调料。


一般过去时:
She overdosed on medication last night. = 她昨晚服药过量了。
He overdosed on sleeping pills. = 他安眠药服用过量。


现在完成时:
I've been overdoing it lately and feeling exhausted. = 我最近过度操劳，感觉筋疲力尽。
They've overdosed on compliments. = 他们被夸奖过头了。


过去完成时:
She realized she had overdosed on caffeine. = 她意识到她摄入了过量的咖啡因。
He had overdosed on painkillers and was hospitalized. = 他服用过量止痛药并住院了。


将来时:
I think I'll overdo it if I try to finish this project today. = 如果我今天试图完成这个项目，我想我会做得过分。
They will overdo the decorations for the party. = 他们将会为派对准备过多的装饰。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't overdo the flattery. = 如果我是你，我不会过分奉承。
I wish I hadn't overdosed on the cake. = 我真希望我没有吃太多的蛋糕。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Are you overdoing it with the workout? = 你运动过度了吗？
Is he overdoing the sarcasm? = 他讽刺过度了吗？


否定句:
I don't think I'm overdoing it with the training. = 我认为我的训练并没有过度。
She doesn't want to overdo it on her first day. = 她不想第一天就做得过分。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "overdoing" is formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") to the present participle "doing."  The root is the verb "to do,"  from Old English *dōn.
