nonsisters: /ˈnɑːnˌsɪstərz/ | n. |非姐妹，非亲姐妹| Women who are not sisters, either by blood or adoption.  The term often emphasizes a lack of familial relationship despite some shared experience or similarity.|


例句：

The two women, though close friends, were nonsisters. = 这两位女士尽管是亲密的朋友，但并非姐妹。

Despite their shared interests, they remained nonsisters. = 尽管她们有共同的兴趣爱好，她们仍然不是姐妹。


近义词： unrelated women, non-relatives, acquaintances


反义词： sisters


词性: 名词
