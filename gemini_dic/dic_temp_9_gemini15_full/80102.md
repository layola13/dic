gobbled:/ˈɡɑːbəld/ | vt. |狼吞虎咽地吃；迅速吞下| To eat something quickly and greedily. |例句：The children gobbled up their dinner. 孩子们狼吞虎咽地吃完了晚饭。

例句：He gobbled the sandwich in two bites. 他两口就吞掉了三明治。

近义词：devoured, bolted, inhaled, scarfed down

反义词：nibble, savor, relish
