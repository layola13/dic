decrustation:/ˌdiːkrʌˈsteɪʃən/| n. |去垢；脱壳；除垢|The removal of a crust or incrustation; the process of removing a hard layer from a surface.  Decrustation can refer to the geological process of removing weathered rock layers or the cleaning of a surface by removing a hardened deposit.|例句：The decrustation of the ancient statue revealed its original beauty.  古代雕像的去垢揭示了它最初的美丽。

The decrustation of the boiler pipes was necessary to prevent further damage. 锅炉管道的除垢是必要的，以防止进一步的损坏。|近义词：cleaning, scaling, descaling|反义词：incrustation, encrustation
