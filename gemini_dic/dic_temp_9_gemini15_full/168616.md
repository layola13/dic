proclaiming:/prəˈkleɪmɪŋ/| v. |宣布；声明；公布 |  The act of publicly and formally declaring something.  It emphasizes the authoritative and widespread nature of the declaration. |例句：The government is proclaiming a state of emergency. = 政府正在宣布紧急状态。

The president is proclaiming a new economic policy. = 总统正在宣布一项新的经济政策。


近义词：announcing, declaring, publicizing, proclaiming, advertising |反义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing, keeping secret |动词
