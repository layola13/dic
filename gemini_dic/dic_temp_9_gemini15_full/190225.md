sapling:/ˈsæplɪŋ/| n. |树苗，小树|A young tree, especially one that is recently planted.|例句：The sapling was carefully planted in the fertile soil. 这棵树苗被小心地种在肥沃的土壤里。

例句：Several saplings were planted along the riverbank to prevent erosion.  几棵树苗被种植在河岸边以防止侵蚀。


近义词：young tree, seedling|反义词：mature tree, old tree|名词
