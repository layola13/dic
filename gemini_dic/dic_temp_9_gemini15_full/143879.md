outpoliticking:/ˌaʊtˈpɒlətɪkɪŋ/| v.|胜过…的政治手腕|To surpass someone in political skill or strategy; to outmaneuver someone politically.|例句：The seasoned politician outpoliticked his younger opponent, securing the nomination.  资深政治家凭借高超的政治手腕胜过了年轻的对手，获得了提名。


例句：The incumbent mayor was outpoliticked by a newcomer who cleverly exploited a local issue.  现任市长被一位巧妙利用当地问题的新人以高超的政治手腕击败。


近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit,  outplay


反义词：be outmaneuvered, be outplayed, be outsmarted


词性:动词
