evidencing:/ˈɛvɪdən(t)sɪŋ/| v.|证明；证实；表明|The study evidences a strong correlation between smoking and lung cancer.  这项研究证明了吸烟和肺癌之间有很强的相关性。|近义词：proving, demonstrating, showing, indicating |反义词：refuting, disproving, contradicting |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The data evidences a clear trend. = 数据表明了一个清晰的趋势。
His actions evidence his guilt. = 他的行为证明了他的罪行。


一般过去时:
The witness evidenced the defendant's presence at the scene. = 目击证人证明被告在场。
The research evidenced a link between diet and disease. = 研究证明了饮食和疾病之间的联系。


现在完成时:
The investigation has evidenced widespread corruption. = 调查已证实了普遍存在的腐败现象。
Recent studies have evidenced the benefits of regular exercise. = 最近的研究已经证明了定期锻炼的好处。


过去完成时:
The lawyer argued that the evidence had evidenced the client's innocence. = 律师辩称证据已证明了当事人的清白。
Previous research had evidenced a similar pattern. = 先前的研究已经证明了类似的模式。


将来时:
Further testing will evidence the effectiveness of the new drug. = 进一步的测试将证明这种新药的有效性。
The upcoming report is expected to evidence a significant economic downturn. = 预计即将发布的报告将证明经济出现了严重的衰退。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence evidenced his claim, he would win the case. = 如果证据证实了他的说法，他就会赢得官司。
I wish the results evidenced a more positive outcome. = 我希望结果能显示出更积极的结果。


被动语态:
The defendant's guilt was evidenced by the testimony. = 被告的罪行由证词证明。
The link between stress and illness is evidenced by numerous studies. = 压力和疾病之间的联系已被大量研究证实。


疑问句:
Does the evidence evidence the theory? = 证据是否证明了这个理论？
What evidences the claim of climate change? = 什么证据证明了气候变化的说法？


否定句:
The study did not evidence any significant differences. = 这项研究没有证明任何显著的差异。
The data does not evidence a causal relationship. = 数据并未证明因果关系。


PHRASE->
evidence of sth: 某事的证据
evidence for sth: 支持某事的证据
evidence against sth: 反对某事的证据
clear evidence: 明确的证据
substantial evidence: 大量的证据
