dibble:/ˈdɪbl/| n. vt.|小穴，小坑；(用小棍)戳，挖(小洞)|a small tool for making holes in the ground, typically for planting seeds; to plant (seeds or plants) using a dibble; to make a small hole in or with a dibble.|例句：He used a dibble to plant the seeds. 他用小棍播种。 He dibbled the seedlings into the prepared soil. 他把幼苗栽种到准备好的土壤里。|近义词：plant, set, poke|反义词：extract, remove


USAGE->

He dibbles seeds into the soil. = 他用小棍把种子种进土里。
She dibbles holes for the young plants. = 她用小棍为幼苗挖洞。
