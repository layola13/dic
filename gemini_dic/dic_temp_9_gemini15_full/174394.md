quieten:/ˈkwaɪətən/ | vt. |使安静；使平静；减弱 | to make someone or something quieter or calmer, or to become quieter or calmer |  The teacher asked the children to quieten down.老师让孩子们安静下来。 |  She tried to quieten the crying baby. 她试图让哭闹的婴儿安静下来。| calm, hush, still | excite, agitate, arouse | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quietens the noisy dog. = 他让吵闹的狗安静下来。
She quietens her voice. = 她压低了声音。


一般过去时:
The music quietened as the concert ended. = 音乐在音乐会结束时安静下来。
He quietened the excited crowd. = 他使兴奋的人群平静下来。


现在完成时:
The storm has quietened down. = 暴风雨已经平息了。
She has quietened the children with a story. = 她用故事哄孩子们安静下来了。


过去完成时:
The audience had quietened before the speaker began. = 演讲者开始讲话之前，观众已经安静下来了。
The wind had quietened considerably by morning. = 到早上，风已经平静了很多。


将来时:

The doctor believes the medication will quieten his nerves. = 医生认为药物会使他的神经平静下来。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
