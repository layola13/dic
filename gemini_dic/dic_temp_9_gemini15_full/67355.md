extensible:/ɪkˈstɛnsəbəl/ | adj. | 可扩展的，可延伸的 | Capable of being extended or enlarged.  It refers to something that can be added to, modified, or expanded without significant difficulty or disruption.  This can apply to physical objects, software, systems, or even ideas. | Example:  The software has an extensible architecture, allowing developers to add new features easily.  这个软件具有可扩展的架构，允许开发人员轻松添加新功能。


The system's extensible design allows for future upgrades.  系统的可扩展设计允许未来的升级。


近义词：expandable, scalable, flexible, adaptable | 反义词：inflexible, rigid, unchangeable | 词性: adjective
