frowning:/ˈfraʊnɪŋ/| v. adj.|皱眉，愁眉苦脸的|The act of drawing one's eyebrows together, typically expressing displeasure, displeasure, or concentration.  It can be a verb describing the action or an adjective describing a facial expression.|例句：He was frowning at the difficult problem. 他正皱眉看着那个难题。

He sat there frowning, lost in thought. 他坐在那里皱眉沉思。


近义词：scowling, glowering, pouting|反义词：smiling, beaming, grinning|动词，形容词
