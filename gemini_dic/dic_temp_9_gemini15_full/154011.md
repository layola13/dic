perforce:/pərˈfɔːrs/| adv.|必然地；必定；勉强地|By necessity; inevitably; whether one wishes it or not.|The project, perforce, had to be delayed due to unforeseen circumstances.  项目由于不可预见的情况，不得不推迟。| inevitably, necessarily | optionally, voluntarily |副词

She perforce attends the meeting. = 她不得不参加会议。
