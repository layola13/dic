pulverize:/ˈpʌlvəraɪz/| vt.|使粉碎；彻底击败；摧毁|To reduce to powder or dust; to completely defeat or overwhelm; to destroy utterly.|例句：The earthquake pulverized the buildings. 地震将建筑物夷为平地。|例句：He pulverized his opponent in the boxing match. 他在拳击比赛中彻底击败了他的对手。|近义词：crush, grind, demolish, obliterate|反义词：build, construct, create, assemble|动词
