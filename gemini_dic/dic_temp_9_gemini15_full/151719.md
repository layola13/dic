party-walled: /ˈpɑːrti wɔːld/ | adj. | 共有墙的；隔墙的 |  Describing a building or property where two or more adjacent units share a common wall.  This wall is usually a structural element and belongs to both units. |


例句：

The apartments are party-walled, meaning we share a wall with our neighbors.  = 这些公寓是共用墙的，这意味着我们与邻居共用一堵墙。

The noise from the party next door was easily heard through the party-walled. = 隔墙的噪音很容易从隔壁的派对传过来。


近义词：adjoining, shared-wall

反义词：detached, freestanding


词性：形容词
