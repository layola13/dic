personifier:/pərˈsɒnɪfaɪər/ | n. |拟人者，赋予人格者 | A personifier is someone or something that gives human qualities or characteristics to something that is not human, such as an animal, object, or idea.  It can also refer to a literary device or technique. |Example: The author was a master personifier, breathing life into inanimate objects through vivid descriptions.  作者是一位精通拟人法的大家，通过生动的描写，赋予无生命物体以生命。|近义词：anthropomorphizer, embodiment, incarnation |反义词：dehumanizer, depersonalizer |名词
