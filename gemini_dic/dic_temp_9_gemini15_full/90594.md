hullo:/ˈhʌləʊ/| int.|你好|A casual and slightly old-fashioned greeting, similar to "hello".  It is less formal than "hello" and often conveys a more friendly and informal tone.| Hullo, darling! How are you today?  你好，亲爱的！你今天好吗？|hello, hi|goodbye, farewell|感叹词


USAGE->
(Hullo, being an interjection, doesn't have tenses like verbs do.  The following examples show how it's used in different sentence structures.)


Simple Sentence:
Hullo!  =  你好！
Hullo there! = 喂！


Exclamatory Sentence:
Hullo, what a lovely day! = 哇，多么美好的一天！
Hullo, you scared me! = 哎哟，你吓到我了！


Question:
Hullo, is that you, John? = 喂，是约翰吗？
Hullo, can I help you? = 喂，我能帮你吗？


PHRASE->
(Hullo doesn't typically form phrases in the same way verbs or nouns do.  Its usage is primarily as a stand-alone greeting.)
