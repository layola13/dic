outplaying:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪɪŋ/ | v. | 技高一筹，胜过 | To surpass or defeat someone in skill or performance.  | Example:  The young chess player outplayed his opponent with a surprising strategy.  年轻的棋手以出人意料的策略击败了他的对手。 |近义词: outperforming, surpassing, excelling, besting | 反义词: being outplayed, losing to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outplays his opponents every game. = 他每一局都技高一筹胜过对手。
The team consistently outplays its rivals. = 这支球队总是胜过对手。


一般过去时:
She outplayed her sister in the tennis match. = 她网球比赛中胜过了她的姐姐。
The champion outplayed all his challengers. = 冠军击败了所有挑战者。


现在完成时:
He has outplayed every opponent he has faced this season. = 这个赛季他击败了他遇到的每一个对手。
Our team has outplayed the opposition in every match. = 我们队在每一场比赛中都胜过对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the game, they had outplayed their opponents completely. = 到比赛结束时，他们已经完全击败了对手。
She had outplayed him in every previous encounter. = 在之前的每一次交锋中，她都技高一筹。


将来时:
I believe she will outplay her opponent tomorrow. = 我相信她明天会击败她的对手。
The analysts predict that they will outplay the other team. = 分析师预测他们将击败另一支球队。


虚拟语气:
If he played his best, he would outplay anyone. = 如果他尽力发挥，他就能胜过任何人。
I wish I could outplay him as easily as he did me. = 我希望我能像他击败我那样轻松地击败他。


被动语态:
He was outplayed by a younger, faster opponent. = 他被一个更年轻、更快的对手击败了。
The strategy was outplayed by a more innovative approach. = 这一策略被更具创新性的方法所击败。


疑问句:
Does he regularly outplay his competitors? = 他经常击败他的竞争对手吗？
Will they be able to outplay the reigning champions? = 他们能够击败卫冕冠军吗？


否定句:
He didn't outplay his opponent this time. = 这次他没有击败他的对手。
They were unable to outplay the team from another school. = 他们无法击败另一所学校的球队。


PHRASE->
outplay the competition = 胜过竞争对手
outplay the opposition = 击败对手
outplay someone hands down = 轻而易举地击败某人
outplay someone strategically = 通过策略击败某人
outplay someone in a game = 在游戏中击败某人
