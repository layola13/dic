commentating:/ˈkɒmənˌteɪtɪŋ/|/ˈkɑːmənˌteɪtɪŋ/| v.|评论；解说；做评论|The act of providing spoken commentary, typically on a live event.|例句：He's commentating on the football match. 他正在评论足球比赛。|例句：She is commentating live from the scene of the accident. 她正在事故现场进行现场直播评论。|近义词：narrating, describing, explaining|反义词：acting, performing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commentates on political events. = 他评论政治事件。
She commentates on sporting events for a living. = 她靠评论体育赛事为生。

一般过去时:
He commentated on the game last night. = 他昨晚评论了这场比赛。
They commentated on the election results. = 他们对选举结果进行了评论。

现在完成时:
She has commentated on many important events. = 她已经评论过许多重要的事件。
He has commentated for this station for ten years. = 他为这个电视台解说了十年。

过去完成时:
He had commentated on several matches before he became famous. = 他成名之前已经评论过几场比赛了。
She had commentated on the previous election before the current one. = 在这次选举之前，她已经对上一届选举做过评论。

将来时:
He will commentate on the Olympics next year. = 他明年将对奥运会进行评论。
She will commentate on the debate tonight. = 她今晚将对这场辩论进行评论。

虚拟语气:
If he were commentating, the audience would enjoy it. = 如果他来解说，观众会很享受。
I wish I were commentating on the World Cup final. = 我真希望我能解说世界杯决赛。

被动语态:
The game was commentated on by a well-known expert. = 这场比赛由一位著名的专家进行了解说。
The event was commentated on live on television. = 该活动在电视上进行了现场直播评论。

疑问句:
Who is commentating on the match? = 谁在评论这场比赛？
Is she commentating on the race? = 她正在评论这场比赛吗？


否定句:
He doesn't commentate on controversial topics. = 他不评论有争议的话题。
She didn't commentate on the incident. = 她没有评论这起事件。


PHRASE->
commentate on = 对…进行评论
