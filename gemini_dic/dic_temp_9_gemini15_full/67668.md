extruders:/ɪkˈstruːdərz/| n. |挤出机；挤压机|A machine that forces molten material through a die to create a continuous shape.  Extruders are used in various industries, such as plastics manufacturing, food processing, and metalworking.|例句：The factory uses extruders to produce plastic pipes.  这家工厂使用挤出机生产塑料管道。|例句：This new extruder is capable of higher production rates. 这台新的挤出机能够实现更高的生产率。|近义词：press, former|反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑与"casting"或"molding"形成对比)|名词
