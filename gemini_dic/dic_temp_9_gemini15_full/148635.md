overthought:/ˈoʊvərθɔːt/| v. |想得太多；过度思考|To think too much about something, often to the point of anxiety or causing unnecessary problems.  It implies dwelling on details or possibilities excessively. |例句：I overthought the presentation and ended up making a mess of it. 我把演示想得太复杂了，结果搞砸了。

例句：She overthought her response to his email and sent a confusing reply. 她对他的邮件回复想得太久，结果发了一封让人困惑的回复。


近义词：ruminate, ponder excessively, worry excessively, obsess over

反义词：act impulsively,  think simply,  consider briefly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I overthink everything. = 我总是想太多。
He overthinks small problems. = 他把小问题想得太复杂了。


一般过去时:
I overthought my decision yesterday. = 我昨天把我的决定想得太复杂了。
She overthought the situation and missed a great opportunity. = 她把情况想得太复杂了，错过了很好的机会。


现在完成时:
I have overthought this issue for days. = 我几天来一直在过度思考这个问题。
She has overthought her choices and now feels regretful. = 她对自己的选择想得太久，现在感到后悔。


过去完成时:
I had overthought the plan before presenting it. = 我在提交计划之前已经想得太复杂了。
They had overthought the problem and devised an overly complicated solution. = 他们在解决问题之前已经想得太复杂了，并设计了一个过于复杂的解决方案。


将来时:
I will probably overthink this again tomorrow. = 我明天可能会再次想太多。
He will overthink his speech before the big event. = 他会在大型活动之前过度思考他的演讲。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overthought it, I would have been more successful. = 如果我没有想太多，我会更成功。
I wish I hadn't overthought the interview. = 我希望我没有在面试中想太多。


被动语态:  (Overthought is rarely used passively.)  The problem was overanalyzed, not overthought. = 这个问题被过度分析了，而不是过度思考了。 The situation was unnecessarily complicated; it had been overthought by the committee. = 情况被不必要地复杂化了；委员会已经过度思考了。


疑问句:
Did you overthink your answer? = 你是否想得太多了？
Are you overthinking this situation? = 你是不是在过度思考这种情况？


否定句:
I didn't overthink my decision; I acted instinctively. = 我没有过度思考我的决定；我是凭直觉行动的。
She didn't overthink the matter; she acted decisively. = 她没有过度思考这件事；她果断地采取了行动。


PHRASE->
overthink a problem = 想太多问题
overthink a decision =  对决定想太多
overthink a situation =  对情况想太多
overthink things = 想太多事情
overthink every detail =  对每个细节都过度思考
