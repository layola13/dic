deflation:/dɪˈfleɪʃən/| n. |通货紧缩，紧缩政策 | A reduction in the general level of prices for goods and services in an economy.  This typically occurs when the supply of goods and services is greater than the demand for them.  It's the opposite of inflation. |Example:  The country experienced a period of deflation after the economic crisis.  这个国家在经济危机后经历了一段时间的通货紧缩。

The government implemented deflationary policies to curb inflation. 政府实施紧缩政策以抑制通货膨胀。|近义词：price reduction, contraction, disinflation |反义词：inflation
