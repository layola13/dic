independency: /ˌɪndɪˈpɛndənsi/ | n. | 独立性；自主性 | The state or quality of being independent; freedom from outside control or influence.  |  例句：The country declared its independence from colonial rule. 这个国家宣布脱离殖民统治，独立了。

例句： National independence is a cherished goal for many oppressed peoples.  许多受压迫人民都珍视民族独立的目标。


近义词：autonomy, self-reliance, self-governance, freedom, liberty

反义词：dependence, reliance, subjection, servitude


词性：名词
