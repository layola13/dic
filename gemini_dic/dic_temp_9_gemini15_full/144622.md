outwinged:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋd/| v.|超过（在翅膀范围或飞行能力方面）|To surpass in the extent of wings or in the power of flight.|The larger bird outwinged the smaller one.|近义词：outsoared, surpassed|反义词：underwinged (该词无实际使用)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The eagle outwings the sparrow in flight. = 老鹰在飞行中超过麻雀。
He consistently outwings his rivals in the air races. = 他在空中竞赛中总是胜过他的对手。


一般过去时:
The albatross outwinged the other birds during the storm. = 信天翁在暴风雨中超过了其他鸟类。
In the competition, she outwinged all her opponents. = 在比赛中，她超过了所有对手。


现在完成时:
The new aircraft has outwinged all previous models in terms of range. = 新型飞机在航程方面已经超过了所有以前的型号。
He has outwinged his competitors in this year's sales. = 他今年的销售额已经超过了他的竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the time the race ended, the champion had outwinged all the other racers. = 到比赛结束时，冠军已经超过了所有其他赛车手。
Before the final lap, he had outwinged his closest competitor. = 在最后一圈之前，他已经超过了他最接近的竞争对手。


将来时:
They predict that the new design will outwing existing models. = 他们预测新设计将超过现有型号。
I believe she will outwing the others in the upcoming tournament. = 我相信她将在即将到来的比赛中胜过其他人。


虚拟语气:
If only I had trained harder, I might have outwinged my opponent. = 如果我训练更刻苦的话，我或许就能超过我的对手了。
If the wind had been stronger, the kite would have outwinged the plane. = 如果风更强的话，风筝就能超过飞机了。


被动语态:
The smaller plane was outwinged by the larger one. = 更小的飞机被更大的飞机超过了。
His record was outwinged by a younger athlete. = 他的纪录被一位年轻的运动员打破了。


疑问句:
Did the falcon outwing the hawk? = 猎鹰是否超过了鹰？
Will the new jet outwing its predecessor? = 新型喷气式飞机能否超过其前身？


否定句:
The small bird did not outwing the eagle. = 小鸟没有超过老鹰。
The underdog did not outwing the favorite in the race. = 弱者在比赛中没有超过热门选手。
