nonreflected:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈflɛktɪd/| adj. |未反射的，未反映的 |Not reflected; not thrown back.  This term is primarily used in physics and related fields, referring to waves or light that do not bounce back from a surface. It can also be used figuratively to describe something that hasn't been considered or shown. |例句：The nonreflected light was absorbed by the black surface.  未反射的光被黑色表面吸收了。The survey showed a nonreflected bias in the results.  调查显示结果存在未反映出的偏差。|近义词：unreflected, unrefracted (in the context of light)|反义词：reflected
