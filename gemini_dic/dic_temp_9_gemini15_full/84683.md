handedly: /ˈhændɪli/ | adv. | 熟练地；单手地；独自地 |  Skillfully or expertly; using only one hand; alone or unaided. | 例句：She handled the situation handily. 她熟练地处理了这种情况。 | 近义词：skillfully, deftly, adroitly, single-handedly | 反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly

词源:
The word "handedly" is derived from the adjective "handy," meaning convenient or skillful.  The adverbial suffix "-ly" is added to create the adverbial form "handedly," indicating the manner in which something is done.  The root can be traced back to Old English "hand," meaning "hand."
