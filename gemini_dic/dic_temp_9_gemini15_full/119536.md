microscopy:/maɪˈkrɒskəpi/| n.|显微镜检查；显微镜术|The science or practice of using microscopes to view objects or structures that are too small to be seen by the naked eye.  It involves the use of various techniques and instruments to magnify and visualize microscopic specimens.|例句：Advanced microscopy techniques allow us to study cellular structures in detail.  先进的显微镜技术使我们能够详细研究细胞结构。

例句：The doctor ordered microscopy to examine the tissue sample.  医生下令进行显微镜检查以检查组织样本。


近义词：microscopic examination, micrography|反义词：macroscopy
