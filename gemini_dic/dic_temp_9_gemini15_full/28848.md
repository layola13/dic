/ˈkɑːrpər/| n. |抱怨者；挑剔者；地毯|A person who complains frequently or finds fault with everything.  A heavy woven fabric used as a floor covering.|例句：He's such a carper; he always finds something to complain about. 他是个爱抱怨的人；他总是能找到一些东西抱怨。


He spent hours choosing the perfect carper for his new living room. 他花了几个小时为他的新客厅挑选完美的毯子。|近义词：complainer, critic, fault-finder, grumbler |反义词：praiser, complimenter|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He carps about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
She constantly carps at her coworkers. = 她不断地抱怨她的同事。

一般过去时:
He carped about the service at the restaurant. = 他抱怨餐厅的服务。
She carped at her son for his messy room. = 她因为儿子的房间乱而责备他。

现在完成时:
He has carped about this issue for years. = 他多年来一直在抱怨这个问题。
She has carped at me since I arrived. = 从我到达以来，她一直在责备我。

过去完成时:
He had carped about the food before anyone else had a chance to try it. = 在其他人有机会尝试之前，他就抱怨食物了。
She had carped at him for weeks before he finally fixed the problem. = 她抱怨了他几周，他才最终解决了这个问题。

将来时:
He will carp about the new rules. = 他会抱怨新规定的。
She will carp at him again if he is late. = 如果他迟到，她会再次责备他。

虚拟语气:
If he carped less, people would like him more. = 如果他少抱怨一些，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she wouldn't carp at me all the time. = 我希望她不要总是责备我。

被动语态:
His complaints were often carped about by his colleagues. = 他的抱怨经常被他的同事们议论。
The new policy was carped about by many employees. = 许多员工都在抱怨这项新政策。

疑问句:
Does he carp about everything? = 他是不是对所有事情都抱怨？
Why is she carp at him so much? = 她为什么对他抱怨那么多？

否定句:
He doesn't carp about minor things. = 他不会抱怨小事。
She didn't carp about the food, surprisingly. = 出乎意料的是，她没有抱怨食物。
