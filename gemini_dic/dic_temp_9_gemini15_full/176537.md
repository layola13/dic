re-roll:/ˌriːˈroʊl/ | vt. |重新掷骰子；重新尝试 |To roll (dice, a ball, etc.) again.  This usually implies a previous attempt that was unsatisfactory. |例句：I didn't like my first character, so I decided to re-roll. 我不喜欢我的第一个角色，所以我决定重新掷骰子。

例句：The game allows you to re-roll once if you get a bad result.  如果结果不好，游戏允许你重新掷一次骰子。


近义词：retry, redo, try again |反义词：accept, keep, finalize
