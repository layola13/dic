deorum: /ˈdiːrɔːm/ | n. | 神明，诸神 |  The plural of the Latin word "deus," meaning "god."  It refers to gods collectively.  The word is often used in a formal or literary context. | Example: The ancient Romans offered sacrifices to the deorum. 古罗马人向众神献祭。 | 近义词: gods, deities, divinities | 反义词: mortals, humans | 名词
