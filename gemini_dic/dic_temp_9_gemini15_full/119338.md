microcalorimeter:/ˌmaɪkroʊkæˈlɒrɪmɪtər/| n. |微量热计|A microcalorimeter is a highly sensitive instrument used to measure extremely small amounts of heat produced or absorbed during a chemical, physical, or biological process.  It operates on the principle of detecting minute temperature changes caused by these processes.  These instruments are crucial in various fields for studying subtle energy changes. |The experiment utilized a microcalorimeter to precisely measure the heat of reaction.  实验利用微量热计精确测量反应热。|近义词：calorimeter (less sensitive)|反义词：无
