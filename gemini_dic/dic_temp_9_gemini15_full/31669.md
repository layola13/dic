chatting:/ˈtʃætɪŋ/| v.|聊天；闲谈|The act of engaging in informal conversation, typically online or in person.|例句：They were chatting away happily. 他们兴高采烈地聊天。|例句：I spent the evening chatting with friends online. 我整个晚上都在网上和朋友聊天。|近义词： conversing, talking, gossiping |反义词： arguing, fighting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am chatting with my friend right now. = 我现在正在和我的朋友聊天。
We chat online every day. = 我们每天都在网上聊天。

一般过去时:
They chatted for hours last night. = 他们昨晚聊了好几个小时。
I chatted with my colleague about the project. = 我和同事讨论了项目。

现在完成时:
I have been chatting with him for years. = 我和他已经聊了很多年了。
She has chatted with many people online. = 她已经和很多人在网上聊过天了。

过去完成时:
They had chatted for a while before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经聊了一会儿。
We had chatted about the plan before we started working. = 我们在开始工作之前已经讨论过计划了。

将来时:
I will chat with you later. = 我稍后会和你聊聊。
We will be chatting online tomorrow. = 我们明天会在网上聊天。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would chat with you. = 如果我有时间，我会和你聊聊。
I wish I could chat with her now. = 我真希望现在能和她聊聊天。

被动语态:
The topic was chatted about extensively. = 这个话题被广泛讨论。
The issue is being chatted about online. = 这个问题正在网上被讨论。

疑问句:
Are you chatting with someone? = 你在和别人聊天吗？
What are you chatting about? = 你在聊什么？

否定句:
I am not chatting with anyone at the moment. = 我现在没有和任何人聊天。
They didn't chat much during the dinner. = 他们在晚餐期间聊得不多。
