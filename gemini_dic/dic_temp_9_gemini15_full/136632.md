nonschematic:/ˌnɒnʃɪˈmætɪk/| adj. |非图解的，非示意图的 |Not represented by or using a diagram or scheme; not systematic or planned.  It describes something that lacks a diagram, plan, or systematic approach.  |例句：The explanation was nonschematic, relying instead on lengthy prose.  = 解释是非图解的，而是依靠冗长的散文。

例句：His approach to problem-solving was nonschematic, often relying on intuition rather than a step-by-step process. = 他解决问题的方法是非图解的，常常依靠直觉而不是循序渐进的过程。

近义词：unplanned, unsystematic, informal, descriptive, nondiagrammatic

反义词：schematic, systematic, planned, diagrammatic

词性: adjective
