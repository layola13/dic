filmmakers:/ˈfɪlmˌmeɪkərz/ | n. |电影制作人 |People who make films, including directors, producers, writers, and other creative and technical personnel.  Filmmakers are responsible for all aspects of film production, from conception to completion. | Example:  The filmmakers behind the movie spent years researching the historical setting.  电影制作人花了数年时间研究这部电影的历史背景。  The independent filmmakers struggled to secure funding for their project. 独立电影制作人努力为他们的项目争取资金。 |近义词：film producers, movie makers, directors (if focusing on the directing aspect) |反义词： film viewers, audience
