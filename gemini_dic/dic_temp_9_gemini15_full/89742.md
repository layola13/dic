hopperdozer:/ˈhɑːpərˌdoʊzər/ | n. |跳跃式推土机|A hopperdozer is a type of earthmoving equipment that combines the functions of a hopper and a bulldozer. It uses a large hopper to collect and transport material, typically in mining or construction.  The hopper is often equipped with a conveyor belt to load the material into trucks or other transport vehicles.  It is typically used for moving large quantities of loose material over short distances.| Example: The mining company used a hopperdozer to move the extracted ore to the processing plant.  这条矿业公司用跳跃式推土机将开采的矿石运送到加工厂。|近义词：bulldozer, scraper, loader|反义词：无
