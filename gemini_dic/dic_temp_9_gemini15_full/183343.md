reprocess:/ˌriːˈprɑːses/| vt.|再处理；重新加工|To process again; to subject to a second or further process.|例句：The company will reprocess the waste materials. 这家公司将对废料进行再处理。|例句：The recycled plastic was reprocessed into new bottles. 回收的塑料被重新加工成新的瓶子。|近义词：rework, recycle, refine|反义词：dispose of, discard
