narrowing: /ˈnærəʊɪŋ/ | v. | 缩小，变窄；限制 | To make or become narrower or more limited.  | The river is narrowing as it approaches the sea. 河流在靠近大海时变窄了。 The choices are narrowing down. 选择范围越来越小了。 | widening, broadening | widening, expanding | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The road is narrowing ahead. = 前方的道路正在变窄。
He is narrowing his focus on one project. = 他正在将注意力集中在一个项目上。

一般过去时:
The tunnel narrowed dramatically. = 隧道急剧变窄。
She narrowed her eyes in suspicion. = 她怀疑地眯起了眼睛。

现在完成时:
The gap between the two groups has narrowed considerably. = 两组之间的差距已经大大缩小。
The search has narrowed down to a few suspects. = 搜索范围已缩小到少数几个嫌疑人。

过去完成时:
The path had narrowed to a barely discernible track. = 小路已经缩小到几乎难以辨认的小径。
They had narrowed their options down before making a decision. = 他们在做出决定之前已经缩小了选择范围。

将来时:
The bridge will narrow further after the reconstruction. = 桥梁重建后将进一步变窄。
The company will narrow its product line to focus on core businesses. = 公司将缩减产品线，专注于核心业务。

虚拟语气:
If the river narrowed further, it would be impassable. = 如果河流进一步变窄，它将无法通行。
I wish the choices were narrowing down more quickly. = 我希望选择范围能更快地缩小。

被动语态:
The search area was narrowed down by the police. = 搜索区域被警方缩小了。
The options were narrowed to just two possibilities. = 选项缩小到了只有两种可能性。

疑问句:
Is the road narrowing in this section? = 这段路在变窄吗？
Are you narrowing your job search criteria? = 你正在缩小求职标准吗？

否定句:
The river doesn't narrow until it reaches the delta. = 河流直到到达三角洲才变窄。
They didn't narrow their focus enough on the key issues. = 他们没有足够地将注意力集中在关键问题上。
