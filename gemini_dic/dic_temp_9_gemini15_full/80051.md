goal-kicker:/ˈɡoʊlˌkɪkər/| n. |得分者；关键球员；临危救主者|A goal-kicker is a player who scores important goals, especially those that win a game or competition.  The term often implies a player who might not be consistently brilliant but delivers when it matters most. |例句：He's not the most skillful player, but he's a real goal-kicker – always there when the team needs a goal. 他不是最技术娴熟的球员，但他是一个真正的关键球员——球队需要进球的时候总能出现。

He's a reliable goal-kicker for the team. 他是球队可靠的得分者。|近义词：match-winner, clutch player, decisive player|反义词：underperformer, liability
