lunulet:/ˈluːnjʊlət/| n. |月牙形物，新月形装饰|A lunule is a crescent-shaped marking, often found in art and design, resembling a small crescent moon.  It can also refer to the half-moon-shaped area at the base of the fingernail.|例句：The artist incorporated several lunules into the design of the necklace.  艺术家在项链的设计中加入了几个月牙形图案。 The lunule at the base of my thumb is particularly prominent. 我拇指根部的月牙形区域特别明显。|近义词：crescent, half-moon|反义词：full moon, circle
