intermomentary:/ˌɪntərˈmoʊməntəri/| adj. |短暂的，间歇的|  Existing or occurring between moments; fleeting; momentary.  The word combines "inter-" (between) and "momentary" (lasting for a very short time).|例句：The intermomentary pauses in his speech added to the suspense. 他演讲中短暂的停顿增加了悬念。The intermomentary connection between the two computers was lost.两台电脑之间的短暂连接中断。|近义词：fleeting, transient, ephemeral, momentary |反义词：permanent, enduring, lasting, continuous
