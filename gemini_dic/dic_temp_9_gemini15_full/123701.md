mooner:/ˈmuːnər/| n. |痴呆者，傻瓜；沉思者，梦想家|A mooner is someone who is considered foolish, dreamy, or absent-minded.  They might be lost in their thoughts or act in a way that seems silly or impractical. |例句：He's such a mooner; he's always staring off into space. 他真是个傻瓜；他总是盯着太空发呆。

例句：She's a bit of a mooner, always lost in her own little world. 她有点傻乎乎的，总是沉浸在自己的小世界里。

近义词：dreamer, daydreamer, airhead, goofball

反义词：practical, realistic, grounded
