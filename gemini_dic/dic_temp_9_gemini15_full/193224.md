sedulously:/ˈsɛdʒʊləsli/| adv.|勤勉地；孜孜不倦地；刻苦地|diligently and carefully|She sedulously cultivated her garden. 她勤勉地耕耘着她的花园。|近义词：diligently, assiduously, painstakingly|反义词：lazily, carelessly, negligently|

She sedulously studies every day. = 她每天勤奋地学习。
