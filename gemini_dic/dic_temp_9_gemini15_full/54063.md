disdiapason:/ˌdɪsˈdaɪəpeɪzən/| n. |不和谐，不协调；极端的不和谐|A disdiapason is a musical term referring to an extreme dissonance or discord, a sound so jarring and unpleasant as to be almost unbearable.  It implies a degree of harshness and unpleasantness far beyond a simple dissonance.|Example: The composer intentionally used a disdiapason in the climax of the piece to create a sense of overwhelming chaos.  作曲家故意在乐曲的高潮部分使用了不和谐音，以营造一种压倒一切的混乱感。|近义词：cacophony, discord, dissonance|反义词：harmony, consonance|名词
