/ˈʃɔːrtɪst/ | adj. | 最短的 | Shortest describes something that is the least in length or duration compared to others in a group.  It is the superlative form of the adjective "short". | The shortest route is usually the most direct. = 最短的路线通常是最直接的。 The shortest day of the year is the winter solstice. = 一年中白昼最短的一天是冬至。| shorter, longer | longest
