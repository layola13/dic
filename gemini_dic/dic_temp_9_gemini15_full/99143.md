intercessionate: /ˌɪntərˈsɛʃəneɪt/ | vt. |代祷；祈求；说情 | To plead on behalf of another; to intercede.  |例句：The priest intercessionated for the condemned man.  牧师为那个被判死刑的人代祷。

例句：She intercessionated with the manager to get a raise for her employee. 她向经理求情，为她的员工争取加薪。


近义词：intercede, mediate, plead, advocate

反义词：oppose, obstruct, hinder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intercessionates for his friend daily. 他每天都为他的朋友祈祷。
She intercessionates with the authorities on behalf of the refugees. 她为难民向当局求情。


一般过去时:
The monk intercessionated for the lost souls.  僧侣为迷失的灵魂祈祷。
They intercessionated with the judge to lessen the sentence. 他们向法官求情以减轻判决。


现在完成时:
He has intercessionated with God for a miracle. 他一直向上帝祈祷奇迹发生。
She has intercessionated on behalf of the poor and needy for years. 她多年来一直为穷人和弱势群体求情。


过去完成时:
He had intercessionated for her release before the trial even began. 在审判开始之前，他就为她的释放求情了。
They had intercessionated with the king many times, but to no avail. 他们多次向国王求情，但都无济于事。


将来时:
I will intercessionate for you with the boss. 我会向老板为你求情的。
They will intercessionate with the court for a fair trial. 他们将向法院求情以争取公平审判。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the right person, I would intercessionate for him. 如果我知道合适的人，我会为他求情。
I wish I could intercessionate for her, but I have no influence. 我希望我能为她求情，但我没有影响力。


被动语态:
The case was intercessionated for by a powerful lawyer.  这个案子得到了一个有权势的律师的求情。
His release was intercessionated for by his family. 他的释放得到了家人的求情。


疑问句:

否定句:

intercessionate on behalf of someone: 代表某人求情
