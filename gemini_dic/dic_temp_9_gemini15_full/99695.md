interlocked:/ˌɪntərˈlɒkt/| adj.|互相锁住的；连锁的；紧密结合的|Joined or linked together firmly.  The parts are so closely connected that they cannot be separated easily. |例句：The two companies have interlocked boards of directors.  两家公司董事会互锁。

例句：The fingers of the two hands were interlocked.  两只手的指头互相交叉握在一起。

近义词：intertwined, interconnected, linked, joined

反义词：separated, disconnected, unlinked

词性:形容词
