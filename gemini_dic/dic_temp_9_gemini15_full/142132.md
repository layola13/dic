originally:/əˈrɪdʒənəli/ | adv. | 原先；最初；本来 |  Originally, the plan was to go to the beach, but the weather changed. | 近义词：primarily, initially, at first | 反义词：finally, ultimately, eventually


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He originally comes from China. = 他原本来自中国。
She originally planned to be a doctor. = 她最初计划成为一名医生。


一般过去时:
They originally lived in a small village. = 他们最初住在一个小村庄。
I originally intended to go to the concert, but I got sick. = 我原本打算去听音乐会，但我病了。


现在完成时:
We have originally hoped for better results. = 我们本来希望得到更好的结果。
She has originally wanted to travel the world. = 她本来想环游世界。


过去完成时:
They had originally planned to leave earlier, but they were delayed. = 他们本来计划早些离开，但他们被耽搁了。
I had originally thought it would be easy, but I was wrong. = 我原本以为这很容易，但我错了。


将来时:
We will originally set off tomorrow morning. = 我们原本计划明天早上出发。
They will originally finish the project by next week. = 他们原本计划下周完成项目。


虚拟语气:
If it hadn't rained, we would have originally gone hiking. = 如果没下雨，我们原本会去远足。
I wish we had originally chosen a different route. = 我真希望我们当初选择了不同的路线。


被动语态:

The painting was originally displayed in a small gallery. = 这幅画最初在一间小型画廊展出。


疑问句:
Was the car originally red? = 这辆车最初是红色的吗？
Were the plans originally approved? = 计划最初被批准了吗？


否定句:
The movie wasn't originally intended for children. = 这部电影最初并非为儿童设计的。
He didn't originally want to go, but he changed his mind. = 他最初不想去，但他改变了主意。
