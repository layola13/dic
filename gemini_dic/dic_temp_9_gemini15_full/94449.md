imply:/ɪmˈplaɪ/ | vt. |暗示；暗指；意味；表明 | To suggest (something) without stating it directly.  |例句：Her silence implied consent.  她的沉默暗示着同意。

例句：His words implied a threat.  他的话暗示着威胁。


近义词：suggest, indicate, insinuate, hint at | 反义词：state explicitly, declare openly | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He implies that he is unhappy with the situation. = 他暗示他对这种情况不满意。
The data implies a significant trend. = 数据暗示了一个显著的趋势。


一般过去时:
She implied that she knew more than she was letting on. = 她暗示她知道的事情比她透露的更多。
The article implied a connection between the two events. = 这篇文章暗示了这两个事件之间的联系。


现在完成时:
His actions have implied a lack of commitment to the project. = 他的行为暗示了他对这个项目缺乏投入。
The evidence has implied that he was involved in the crime. = 证据暗示他参与了犯罪。


过去完成时:
The letter implied that she had known about the problem for some time. = 这封信暗示她早就知道这个问题了。
His behavior had implied that he was not interested in the job. = 他的行为暗示了他对这份工作不感兴趣。


将来时:
His upcoming speech will imply a shift in government policy. = 他即将发表的演讲将暗示政府政策的转变。
The results of the study will imply a need for further research. = 这项研究的结果将暗示需要进一步研究。


虚拟语气:
If his words implied a threat, I would have called the police. = 如果他的话暗示了威胁，我会报警。
If the evidence implied guilt, the jury would have convicted him. = 如果证据暗示有罪，陪审团就会判他犯有罪。


被动语态:
It was implied that he was responsible for the accident. = 暗示他是事故的责任人。
A connection between the two events was implied by the report. = 报告暗示了这两个事件之间的联系。


疑问句:
What does this passage imply? = 这段文字暗示了什么？
Does his behavior imply anything significant? = 他的行为暗示了什么重要的信息？


否定句:
He did not imply any criticism of her work. = 他并没有暗示对她工作的任何批评。
The statement did not imply any wrongdoing on their part. = 这份声明并没有暗示他们有任何不当行为。


PHRASE->
imply something to somebody = 向某人暗示某事
imply that = 暗示……
imply by = 通过……暗示
to imply something = 暗示某事
strongly imply = 强烈暗示


词源:
The word "imply" comes from the Late Latin word "implicare," meaning "to enfold, involve, or entangle."  This reflects the sense of something being indirectly suggested, wrapped up in the meaning, rather than explicitly stated.
