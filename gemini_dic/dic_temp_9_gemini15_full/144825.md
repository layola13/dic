over-inflates:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪts/| vt.|过度膨胀；夸大；高估|To inflate excessively; to exaggerate the importance or significance of something.|例句：The media over-inflates the importance of minor political events.媒体夸大了次要政治事件的重要性。|例句：The company over-inflated its profits to attract investors.该公司夸大了其利润以吸引投资者。|近义词：exaggerate, inflate, magnify|反义词：underestimate, downplay


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The media over-inflates the importance of minor issues. = 媒体夸大了小问题的严重性。
He constantly over-inflates his accomplishments. = 他不断夸大自己的成就。


一般过去时:
The report over-inflated the number of casualties. = 报告夸大了伤亡人数。
They over-inflated the balloon until it burst. = 他们把气球吹爆了。


现在完成时:

She has over-inflated her self-importance. = 她过分夸大了自己的重要性。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
