play fast and loose:/pleɪ fɑːst ænd luːs/| v. |轻率行事；不负责任；玩弄；随意对待 |To behave recklessly or irresponsibly; to treat something or someone casually and without proper care or respect.  |例句：He played fast and loose with the company's money, leading to its bankruptcy. 他挥霍公司的钱财，导致公司破产。

例句：She played fast and loose with the truth, omitting crucial details. 她对真相轻描淡写，省略了关键细节。


近义词：be careless, be reckless, trifle with, take liberties with

反义词：be careful, be responsible, be meticulous, be scrupulous

词性：动词短语
