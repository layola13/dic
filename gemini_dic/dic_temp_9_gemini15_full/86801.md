hematachometer: /ˌhiːmætəˈkɒmɪtər/ | n. |血球计数器|A hematachometer is a device used to count blood cells. It typically uses an electrical or optical method to detect and count the cells as they pass through a narrow channel.  Modern hematachometers are sophisticated instruments that provide a wide range of blood cell counts and other hematological parameters. |The hematachometer provided a quick and accurate count of the white blood cells. 血球计数器快速准确地计算了白细胞数量。  The doctor used a hematachometer to analyze the patient's blood sample. 医生用血球计数器分析了病人的血液样本。|  blood cell counter, hemocytometer |  ---|
