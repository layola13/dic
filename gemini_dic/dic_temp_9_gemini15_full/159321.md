points:/pɔɪnts/| n.|要点，分数，点，尖端|Points are crucial aspects or details of a subject.  Points can also refer to scores in a game or test, or the sharp end of something.|例句：The main points of the lecture were clearly explained.  这堂课的主要要点解释得很清楚。|例句：He scored 10 points in the basketball game. 他在篮球比赛中得了10分。|近义词：aspects, details, scores, tips|反义词：bluntness, dullness
