eighteen-wheeler:/ˌeɪˈtiːnˈwiːlər/| n. |十八轮卡车，重型卡车|A large truck with eighteen wheels, typically used for long-haul freight transportation.  It's characterized by its multiple axles and large cargo capacity.|例句：The eighteen-wheeler rumbled down the highway. 这辆十八轮卡车沿着高速公路隆隆驶去。|例句：He drives an eighteen-wheeler for a living. 他开十八轮卡车为生。|近义词：big rig, semi-trailer truck, tractor-trailer|反义词：car, motorcycle|名词
