destroyer:/dɪˈstrɔɪər/ | n. |驱逐舰，破坏者 | A warship designed to escort larger vessels and engage in anti-submarine and anti-surface warfare.  It is typically smaller and faster than a battleship or cruiser. |例句：The destroyer escorted the aircraft carrier through the Strait. 这艘驱逐舰护送航空母舰穿过海峡。

例句：The storm acted as a destroyer of the coastal town.  暴风雨摧毁了沿海城镇。|近义词：warship,  dreadnought,  corvette(较小的军舰) |反义词：protector, preserver, constructor |词性: 名词
