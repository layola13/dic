inevitableness:/ˌɪnˈɛvɪtəbəlnəs/| n. |不可避免性，必然性 |The inevitableness of death is a universal truth.  死亡的不可避免性是一个普遍的真理。|近义词：certainty, unavoidability |反义词：avoidability, chance


英文解析：Inevitableness refers to the state or quality of being inevitable;  something that is certain to happen and cannot be avoided. It emphasizes the certainty and lack of control over an outcome.


例句：

The inevitableness of the project's failure was apparent from the start.  这个项目失败的不可避免性从一开始就显而易见。

He accepted the inevitableness of aging with grace. 他优雅地接受了衰老的必然性。


近义词：

certainty:  a fact that is known to be true.  确定性
unavoidability: the state of being unable to be avoided.  不可避免性
predestination: the belief that all events have been willed by God.  预定论


反义词：

avoidability: the state of being able to be avoided. 可避免性
chance: the possibility of something happening.  可能性
contingency: a possible future event that must be prepared for.  偶然事件


词性：名词
