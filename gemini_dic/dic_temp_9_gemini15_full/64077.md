epizootiology:/ˌɛpɪˌzoʊiˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |动物流行病学|The study of the distribution, transmission, and control of diseases in animal populations.  It examines factors influencing the occurrence of disease outbreaks in animals, including environmental, genetic, and social factors. |The epizootiology of avian influenza is complex, involving various wild bird species and domestic poultry.  禽流感的动物流行病学十分复杂，涉及各种野生鸟类和家禽。 |Epidemiology, veterinary epidemiology|  No direct antonym.
