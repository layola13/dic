counterreforms:/ˈkaʊntərˌriːfɔːrmz/ | n. | 反改革；对抗改革 |  Actions taken to reverse or counteract the effects of previous reforms.  These often involve a return to previous policies or systems, or the introduction of new measures designed to undermine the goals of earlier reforms.  | The government's counterreforms sparked widespread protests.  政府的反改革引发了大规模抗议。 |  reactionary measures, retrogressive steps | reforms, progressive measures | 名词
