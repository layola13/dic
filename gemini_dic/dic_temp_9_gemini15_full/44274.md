crone:/krəʊn/| n.|老太婆；巫婆|An old woman, especially one who is ugly or unpleasant.  The word often carries negative connotations of wickedness or ugliness.|例句：The old crone cackled wickedly. 这老太婆邪恶地咯咯笑。|例句：She looked like a wicked crone from a fairy tale. 她看起来像童话故事里邪恶的老太婆。|近义词：hag, witch, old woman|反义词：young woman, maiden
