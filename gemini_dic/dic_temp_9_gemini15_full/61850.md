employer:/ɪmˈplɔɪər/| n. |雇主，老板| A person or organization that employs people.  |Example: My employer provides excellent health insurance. 我的雇主提供优良的医疗保险。|近义词：boss, manager, company |反义词：employee


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My employer pays me bi-weekly. = 我的雇主每两周付一次工资。
The company employs over 500 people. = 这家公司雇佣了500多名员工。


一般过去时:
My former employer offered me a severance package. = 我以前的雇主给了我遣散费。
The factory employed hundreds of workers before it closed. = 这家工厂在关闭之前雇佣了数百名工人。


现在完成时:
I have worked for this employer for five years. = 我已经为这家雇主工作五年了。
The company has employed many talented individuals. = 这家公司已经雇佣了许多有才华的人。


过去完成时:
I had worked for my previous employer for ten years before I left. = 在我离开之前，我已经为我以前的雇主工作了十年。
The company had employed thousands of workers before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退之前，这家公司已经雇佣了数千名工人。


将来时:
My employer will likely increase our salaries next year. = 我的雇主很可能会在明年提高我们的工资。
The company will employ more engineers in the future. = 这家公司将来会雇佣更多的工程师。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better employer, I would be happier. = 如果我有一个更好的雇主，我会更快乐。
I wish my employer offered more vacation time. = 我希望我的雇主提供更多的假期。


被动语态:
Many people are employed by large corporations. = 许多人被大型公司雇佣。
The new employees were quickly integrated into the team by their employer. = 新员工很快就被他们的雇主融入团队。


疑问句:
Who is your employer? = 你的雇主是谁？
What kind of employer are you looking for? = 你在寻找什么样的雇主？


否定句:
This company is not a good employer. = 这家公司不是一个好雇主。
I don't believe my employer is being fair. = 我不相信我的雇主公平。

potential employer = 潜在雇主
