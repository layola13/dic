hangingly: /ˈhæŋɪŋli/ | adv. | 悬垂地，悬挂地 |  Describes something in a hanging or drooping manner.  It suggests a state of being suspended or dangling.  The word is rarely used and often sounds unnatural in modern English.  It's more common in older literature or very specific contexts. | The plants hung hangingly from the trellis.  = 植物悬垂地从棚架上垂下来。 The decorations were hangingly arranged. = 装饰品是悬挂式布置的。 |  droopingly, pendulously | uprightly, erectly | 副词
