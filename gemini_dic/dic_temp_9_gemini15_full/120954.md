mischanneled: /ˌmɪsˈtʃænəld/ | adj. | 被误导的；被错误引导的 |  Describes something that has been wrongly directed or guided;  it implies a failure in proper direction or management, leading to unintended or negative consequences.  |  例句：His anger was mischanneled into destructive behavior.  他的愤怒被误导，导致了破坏性行为。


The company's marketing efforts were mischanneled, resulting in low sales.  公司的营销工作被误导，导致销售额低迷。| 近义词：misdirected, misguided, diverted, wrongly channeled | 反义词：channeled properly, well-directed, effectively guided |  词性：形容词
