proclaimed:/prəˈkleɪmd/ | vt. |宣布；声明；宣告 | To announce officially or publicly.  |例句：The president proclaimed a state of emergency.总统宣布进入紧急状态。 |例句：The judge proclaimed the defendant guilty. 法官宣判被告有罪。|近义词：announced, declared, proclaimed, published |反义词：concealed, suppressed, withheld |动词

USAGE->
