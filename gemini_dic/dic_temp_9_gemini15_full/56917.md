dote:/doʊt/| v.|溺爱，过度宠爱|To love or be excessively fond of someone or something.  It often implies a fondness that may be excessive or blind.|例句：Grandparents often dote on their grandchildren.  祖父母经常溺爱他们的孙子孙女。|例句：She dotes on her pet cat. 她溺爱她的宠物猫。|近义词：adore, cherish, idolize|反义词：neglect, ignore, disdain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dotes on his daughter. = 他溺爱他的女儿。
They dote on their new puppy. = 他们溺爱他们的新小狗。

一般过去时:
My grandmother doted on me when I was a child. = 我小时候祖母很溺爱我。
He doted on her for many years. = 他多年来一直很爱她。

现在完成时:
She has doted on him since he was born. = 从他出生以来，她就一直溺爱他。
They have doted on their grandchildren for years. = 他们多年来一直溺爱他们的孙子孙女。

过去完成时:
She had doted on her son until his death. = 直到他去世，她都一直溺爱着她的儿子。
They had doted on their pet for over a decade. = 他们溺爱他们的宠物超过十年了。
