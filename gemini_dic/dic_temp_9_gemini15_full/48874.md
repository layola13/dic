defraud:/dɪˈfrɔːd/| vt.|欺诈；诈骗；骗取|To deprive (someone) of something valuable by deceit; cheat.|例句：He defrauded the company of thousands of dollars. 他诈骗公司数千美元。 He was defrauded of his inheritance. 他被骗走了他的遗产。|近义词：cheat, swindle, fleece|反义词：benefit, aid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They defraud investors regularly. = 他们经常欺诈投资者。
The company defrauds its customers. = 这家公司欺诈它的顾客。


一般过去时:
He defrauded the bank of a large sum of money. = 他诈骗银行一大笔钱。
The con artist defrauded many people. = 骗子骗取了许多人的钱财。


现在完成时:
They have defrauded numerous clients. = 他们已经欺诈了许多客户。
The government has been defrauded of millions of dollars in taxes. = 政府在税收方面被骗取了数百万美元。


过去完成时:
The company had defrauded its shareholders before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，该公司已经欺诈了其股东。
He had defrauded his business partner for years. = 他多年来一直在欺骗他的商业伙伴。


将来时:
They will defraud the public again if they are not stopped. = 如果不阻止他们，他们将再次欺诈公众。
He will defraud his creditors if he gets away with it. = 如果他侥幸逃脱，他会欺骗他的债权人。


虚拟语气:
If he had not defrauded the bank, he would not be in jail. = 如果他没有诈骗银行，他就不会在监狱里。
I wish I had not defrauded my friend. = 我真希望我没有欺骗我的朋友。


被动语态:
Many people were defrauded in the investment scheme. = 许多人在投资计划中被欺诈。
The company was defrauded of its valuable assets. = 这家公司被骗走了其宝贵的资产。


疑问句:
Were they defrauded by the company? = 他们被该公司欺诈了吗？
Has anyone been defrauded in this transaction? = 在这次交易中有人被欺诈了吗？


否定句:
The company did not defraud its customers. = 这家公司没有欺诈它的顾客。
He did not defraud anyone intentionally. = 他并没有故意欺骗任何人。


PHRASE->
defraud someone of something = 骗取某人某物

defraud by deception = 通过欺骗手段诈骗
