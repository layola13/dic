crocodiling:/ˌkrɒkəˈdaɪlɪŋ/ | v. |鳄鱼式哭泣；假哭；虚情假意地哭泣 |To feign crying, typically in an exaggerated or theatrical manner, often to manipulate or deceive.  |例句：He was crocodiling, trying to get sympathy from his boss. 他假哭，试图博得老板的同情。

例句：She crocodiled tears when she heard the news, but I knew she wasn't truly upset.  她听到这个消息后假哭，但我明白她并没有真正难过。


近义词：feigning tears, crocodile tears,  pretending to cry,  affecting grief

反义词：genuinely crying,  sincere grief,  honest sorrow


词性:动词
