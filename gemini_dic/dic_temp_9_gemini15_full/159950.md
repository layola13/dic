polypifera:/pɒlɪˈpɪfərə/ | n. | 多孔动物门 |  A phylum of aquatic invertebrate animals that includes corals, sea anemones, and jellyfish. They are characterized by their radial symmetry and the presence of nematocysts (stinging cells). | 例句：The polypifera are a diverse group of animals found in marine environments worldwide.  多孔动物门是一个种类繁多的动物群体，遍布世界各地的海洋环境。|近义词：cnidarians (although not a perfect synonym, as it's a broader term)|反义词：无 | 名词
