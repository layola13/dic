productible:/prəˈdjuːstəbl/ | adj. | 可生产的，可制造的 | Capable of being produced or manufactured.  This word implies the possibility of production, often suggesting feasibility or practicality. It's less common than "producible." | Example:  The new material is productible using existing technology. 新材料可以使用现有技术生产。  The plan is theoretically productible, but the cost is prohibitive. 该计划理论上是可以生产的，但成本过高。 | 近义词: producible, manufacturable, feasible | 反义词: unproducible, impossible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory produces many cars. = 工厂生产很多汽车。
This machine produces high-quality goods. = 这台机器生产高质量的产品。

一般过去时:
They produced a new type of smartphone last year. = 他们去年生产了一种新型智能手机。
The company produced a record number of cars this quarter. = 本季度该公司汽车产量创纪录。

现在完成时:
We have produced over 1000 units this month. = 我们这个月已经生产了1000多件产品。
They have produced significant results in their research. = 他们在研究中取得了显著成果。

过去完成时:
They had produced several prototypes before the final design was chosen. = 在最终设计方案确定之前，他们已经生产了几个原型。
The company had produced a similar product in the past. = 该公司过去生产过类似的产品。

将来时:
We will produce more sustainable energy in the future. = 未来我们将生产更多可持续能源。
The factory will produce twice as many cars next year. = 工厂明年将生产两倍数量的汽车。

虚拟语气:
If we had better equipment, we could produce more efficiently. = 如果我们有更好的设备，我们可以更高效地生产。
I wish we could produce a more environmentally friendly product. = 我希望我们能够生产一种更环保的产品。

被动语态:
The product is produced in China. = 该产品在中国生产。
Many cars are produced by this company every year. = 每一年这家公司生产很多汽车。

疑问句:
What is produced in this factory? = 这个工厂生产什么？
How many cars are produced daily? = 每天生产多少辆汽车？

否定句:
We don't produce that type of product anymore. = 我们不再生产那种产品了。
The factory didn't produce anything yesterday due to a power outage. = 由于停电，工厂昨天没有生产任何东西。
