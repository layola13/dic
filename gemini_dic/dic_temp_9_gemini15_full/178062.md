recleaned:/ˌriːˈkliːnd/| adj.|重新清洁的，再次清洁的|Cleaned again.  This word describes something that has been cleaned a second time, or after a previous cleaning was insufficient.|例句：The recleaned dishes sparkled.  重新清洁的餐具闪闪发光。 The recleaned room smelled fresh.  重新清洁的房间闻起来很新鲜。|近义词：re-cleaned, cleaned again, thoroughly cleaned|反义词：dirty, unclean
