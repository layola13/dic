nonunity: /ˌnɒnˈjuːnɪti/ | adj. | 非统一性，不统一 | Lack of unity; the state of not being unified or united.  It refers to a situation or condition where there is a lack of cohesion, consistency, or agreement among different parts or elements. | Example: The nonunity of the opposing factions prevented them from forming a united front.  非对立派系的非统一性阻止了他们形成统一战线。 | 近义词：disunity, fragmentation, discord, heterogeneity | 反义词：unity, coherence, harmony, uniformity
