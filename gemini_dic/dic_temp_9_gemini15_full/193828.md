self-destructions: /ˌsɛlf dəˈstrʌkʃənz/ | n. | 自毁；自我毁灭 | The act or process of destroying oneself or itself.  This often refers to a planned or programmed destruction, as opposed to accidental destruction. It can refer to physical objects, systems, or even individuals. | 例句：The missile was equipped with a self-destruction mechanism.  这枚导弹配备了自毁装置。

The building's self-destruction was a result of a massive fire. 建筑物的自毁是由于大火造成的。


近义词：self-annihilation, demolition, ruin, destruction | 反义词：self-preservation, survival, construction, creation | 词性：名词
