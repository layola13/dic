paleo:/ˈpeɪliˌoʊ/|/ˈpæliˌoʊ/| adj. n.|古生物的；旧石器时代的；古生物学|Relating to or denoting the Paleolithic period.  The term is also used more broadly to refer to things related to ancient humans and their cultures.|例句：Paleoanthropologists study the fossils of early humans. 古人类学家研究早期人类的化石。
例句：The museum has a fascinating exhibit on paleo-art.  博物馆有一个关于旧石器时代艺术的迷人展览。|近义词：prehistoric, ancient|反义词：modern, contemporary
