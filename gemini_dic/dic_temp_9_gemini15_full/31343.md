characteristic:/ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪk/| n. adj. |特性；特征；特色；典型的|a feature or quality belonging typically to a person, place, or thing and serving to identify it.|The characteristic feature of this bird is its long beak.  这只鸟的显著特征是它的长喙。|This painting is characteristic of his early work. 这幅画是他早期作品的典型风格。|近义词：trait, feature, attribute, quality |反义词：uncharacteristic, atypical


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The characteristic of this material is its durability. = 这种材料的特性是耐用性。
What are the characteristics of a good leader? = 一个好领导的特征是什么？


一般过去时:
The characteristics of the product were well-received by customers. = 产品的特性受到了顾客的好评。
His characteristic shyness made it difficult to make friends.  = 他内向的性格让他很难交朋友。


现在完成时:
Scientists have identified several key characteristics of the disease. = 科学家们已经确定了这种疾病的几个关键特征。
The company has maintained its characteristic high standards. = 这家公司一直保持着其一贯的高标准。


过去完成时:
Before the discovery, the characteristics of the element were unknown. = 在发现之前，这种元素的特性是未知的。
We had already observed many characteristic behaviours of the animal. 我们已经观察到很多这种动物的典型行为。


将来时:
Researchers will investigate the characteristics of the new material. = 研究人员将研究这种新材料的特性。
The project's characteristics will be clearly defined in the proposal.  项目的特点将在提案中明确定义。


虚拟语气:
If the product had different characteristics, it would sell better. = 如果产品有不同的特性，它就会卖得更好。
I wish the car had more characteristic features. 我希望这辆车有更多特有的功能。


被动语态:
The characteristics of the soil were analyzed in the laboratory. = 土壤的特性在实验室进行了分析。
The car's unique characteristics are being highlighted in the advertisement.  汽车独特的特性在广告中被突出强调。


疑问句:
What are the defining characteristics of this culture? = 这种文化的决定性特征是什么？
What characteristics do you value most in a friend? 你最看重朋友的哪些特质？


否定句:
The painting does not exhibit the characteristics of his later works. = 这幅画没有表现出他后期作品的特点。
His behaviour was completely uncharacteristic.他的行为完全不像他。


PHRASE->

characteristic of =  …的特性
characteristic features = 特征
