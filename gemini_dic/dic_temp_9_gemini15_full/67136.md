exporting:/ɪkˈspɔːrtɪŋ/| v.| 出口，输出 | The act of sending goods or services to another country for sale.  |例句：China is a major player in exporting electronics. 中国是电子产品出口的主要参与者。 |例句：The company is focusing on increasing its exporting capacity. 这家公司正致力于提高其出口能力。|近义词：shipping, selling abroad, trading |反义词：importing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company exports textiles to many countries. = 这家公司向许多国家出口纺织品。
They are exporting a large quantity of rice this year. = 今年他们出口了大量的稻米。


一般过去时:
We exported a significant amount of wheat last year. = 我们去年出口了大量的麦子。
The country exported less oil than expected. = 该国石油出口量低于预期。


现在完成时:
The government has exported more goods than ever before. = 政府比以往任何时候都出口了更多的商品。
They have been exporting coffee beans for decades. = 几十年来，他们一直在出口咖啡豆。


过去完成时:
The company had already exported the goods before the payment arrived. = 款项到达之前，该公司已经出口了货物。
By the end of the year, they had exported over 10,000 units. = 到年底，他们已经出口了超过10,000个单位。


将来时:
We will export more products to the European market next year. = 我们明年将向欧洲市场出口更多产品。
The company expects to export even more goods in the future. = 这家公司预计未来将出口更多商品。


虚拟语气:
If we exported more, we would make more profit. = 如果我们出口更多，我们将获得更多利润。
It would be beneficial if we could export more high-tech products. = 如果我们能够出口更多高科技产品，那将是有益的。


被动语态:
Thousands of tons of grain are exported annually. = 数千吨粮食每年出口。
Many products are exported from this region. = 许多产品从这个地区出口。


疑问句:
What goods are they exporting? = 他们在出口什么商品？
Where are the products being exported to? = 产品出口到哪里？


否定句:
We do not export weapons. = 我们不出口武器。
The company was not exporting to that country at the time. = 那时该公司没有向那个国家出口。


PHRASE->
exporting business = 出口业务

exporting industry = 出口产业

exporting regulations = 出口法规
