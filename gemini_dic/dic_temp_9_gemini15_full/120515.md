minutes:/ˈmɪnɪts/| n.|分钟；会议记录|The word "minutes" refers to a unit of time equal to 60 seconds, or to the written record of a meeting.  It can also refer to a very short period of time.|例句：The meeting lasted for 60 minutes.  会议持续了60分钟。|例句：The minutes of the last meeting were distributed. 上次会议的记录已经分发了。|近义词：second (in the context of a very short time), record, proceedings|反义词：hours, days (in the context of time)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
How many minutes are there in an hour? = 一小时有多少分钟？
The meeting usually takes 30 minutes. = 会议通常需要30分钟。


一般过去时:
The lecture lasted 45 minutes. = 讲座持续了45分钟。
It took me five minutes to get there. = 我花了五分钟才到那里。


现在完成时:
We have waited for 10 minutes. = 我们已经等了10分钟。
The train has been delayed for minutes. = 火车已经延误了几分钟。


过去完成时:
The meeting had already started five minutes before I arrived. = 我到达之前，会议已经开始了五分钟。
They had been waiting for minutes before the bus came. = 公共汽车来之前，他们已经等了好几分钟。


将来时:
The film will last 90 minutes. = 电影将持续90分钟。
I will spend 15 minutes on this task. = 我将花15分钟在这个任务上。


虚拟语气:
If I had more minutes, I would finish my work. = 如果我有更多的时间，我就能完成我的工作。
I wish I had more minutes to relax. = 我希望我有更多的时间来放松。


被动语态:
The minutes of the meeting were recorded accurately. = 会议记录被准确地记录下来了。
The time was measured in minutes. = 时间是用分钟来衡量的。


疑问句:
How many minutes are left? = 还剩下多少分钟？
How long, in minutes, will the journey take? = 这段旅程需要多少分钟？


否定句:
It didn't take many minutes to complete. = 完成它没花多少时间。
The meeting did not last more than 10 minutes. = 会议没有超过10分钟。


PHRASE->
a few minutes = 几分钟
in minutes = 立刻；马上
minutes to spare = 剩余的时间
take minutes = 做记录
spend minutes doing sth. = 花时间做某事


词源:
The word "minutes" comes from the Old French word "minue," meaning "small," which itself derives from the Latin word "minutus," also meaning "small."  This reflects its derivation as a small fraction of an hour.
