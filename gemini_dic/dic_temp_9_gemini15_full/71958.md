flightily:/ˈflaɪtɪli/| adj.|轻率地；浮躁地；反复无常地|Acting in a frivolous, irresponsible, or capricious manner; characterized by lightness and lack of seriousness.|例句：She behaved flightily, flitting from one interest to another. 她行为轻率，三心二意地从一个兴趣转向另一个兴趣。

例句：He made a flighty decision without considering the consequences. 他做了一个轻率的决定，没有考虑后果。


近义词：frivolously, capriciously, recklessly, impulsively, erratically


反义词：carefully, cautiously, thoughtfully, deliberately, responsibly


词性：形容词
