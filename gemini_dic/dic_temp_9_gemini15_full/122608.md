modernizes:/ˈmɒdənaɪzɪz/| /ˈmɑːdərnˌaɪzɪz/| v. |使现代化；现代化改造|To make something more modern.  This involves updating or improving something to meet current standards or expectations, often by incorporating new technologies or methods. |例句：The company is modernizing its production facilities to increase efficiency. 公司正在对其生产设施进行现代化改造以提高效率。|例句：They modernized the old building by adding new windows and a modern heating system. 他们通过增加新的窗户和现代化的供暖系统来使这座旧建筑现代化。|近义词：update, renovate, revamp, upgrade|反义词：antiquate, obsolete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory modernizes its equipment regularly. = 工厂定期对其设备进行现代化改造。
She modernizes her teaching methods every year. = 她每年都更新她的教学方法。

一般过去时:
They modernized the railway system last year. = 他们去年对铁路系统进行了现代化改造。

现在完成时:

We have modernized our communication systems. = 我们已经将我们的通信系统现代化了。

过去完成时:
