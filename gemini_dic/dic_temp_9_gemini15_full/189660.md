sally:/ˈsæli/| n. v.|萨莉（女子名）；突袭，出击|A sally is a sudden attack or sortie, especially from a besieged place.  It can also be a woman's name.|例句：The soldiers made a sally from the fort. 士兵们从要塞里发起了突袭。|近义词：raid, sortie, foray|反义词：retreat, withdrawal

He often sallies into the unknown. = 他经常冒险进入未知领域。
