exasperate:/ɪɡˈzæs.pə.reɪt/| vt.|使恼怒；使生气；使加剧|to make someone very annoyed, usually when they are already feeling angry or impatient |His constant complaining is beginning to exasperate me. 他不停的抱怨开始让我恼火了。  The delay only exasperated the already difficult situation.  这次延误只会使本来就困难的局面雪上加霜。|annoy, irritate, infuriate, provoke|calm, soothe, appease|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exasperates his colleagues with his constant negativity. = 他总是消极悲观，这让他同事很恼火。
The constant noise exasperates the residents. = 持续的噪音让居民们很恼火。

一般过去时:
The traffic jam exasperated me yesterday. = 昨天的交通堵塞让我很生气。
Her stubbornness exasperated her parents. = 她的固执让她的父母很生气。

现在完成时:
His behavior has exasperated everyone in the office. = 他的行为已经激怒了办公室里的每个人。
The endless delays have exasperated the project team. = 无休止的延误已经激怒了项目组。

过去完成时:
I had already been exasperated by his behavior before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始，我就已经被他的行为激怒了。
The repeated failures had exasperated the researchers. = 反复的失败已经让研究人员们很沮丧。


将来时:
This situation will only exasperate the problem further. = 这种情况只会进一步加剧问题。
His actions will undoubtedly exasperate his boss. = 他的行为无疑会激怒他的老板。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to act like that, he would exasperate everyone. = 如果他继续那样做，他会激怒所有的人。
I wish he wouldn't exasperate me so much. = 我希望他不要再让我这么生气了。


被动语态:
The team was exasperated by the lack of support. = 团队因缺乏支持而感到沮丧。
He was exasperated by the constant interruptions. = 他被不断的打断弄得很恼火。


疑问句:
Does his attitude exasperate you? = 他的态度让你生气吗？
What exasperates you the most about this situation? = 在这种情况下，什么最让你生气？


否定句:
The news didn't exasperate her as much as I thought. = 这个消息并没有像我想象的那样让她生气。
His explanation didn't exasperate the situation. = 他的解释并没有使情况恶化。


PHRASE->
