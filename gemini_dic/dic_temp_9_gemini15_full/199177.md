silicone:/ˈsɪlɪkəʊn/|/ˈsɪlɪkɔːn/| n. |硅酮，硅胶|A synthetic polymer, typically a siloxane, containing silicon, oxygen, carbon, and hydrogen.  It is used in a wide variety of applications, from caulking to medical implants.|例句：The sealant is made of silicone.  密封胶是由硅酮制成的。

例句：This baking mold is made from silicone, making it easy to remove baked goods. 这个烘焙模具由硅胶制成，方便脱模。


近义词：siloxane (化学术语), elastomer (某些类型的硅酮)


反义词：无直接反义词，但可以根据语境用其他材料代替


词性:名词
