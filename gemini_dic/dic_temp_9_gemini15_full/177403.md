reawaking:/ˌriːəˈweɪkɪŋ/ | v. | 重新唤醒；重新激发；复苏 | The reawaking of interest in classical music is encouraging.  重新燃起对古典音乐的兴趣令人鼓舞。|近义词：reviving, awakening, rousing | 反义词：extinguishing, suppressing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun reawakens the sleeping city. = 太阳唤醒了沉睡的城市。
The music reawakens his memories. = 音乐唤醒了他对往事的回忆。

一般过去时:
The warm spring breeze reawakened the dormant plants. = 温暖的春风唤醒了休眠的植物。
The news reawakened her fears. = 这个消息重新唤起了她的恐惧。

现在完成时:
The project has reawakened public interest in environmental protection. = 这个项目重新唤起了公众对环境保护的兴趣。
His passion for painting has reawakened after many years. = 多年以后，他对绘画的热情重新被点燃了。

过去完成时:
The rain had reawakened the seeds, and they began to sprout. = 雨水已经唤醒了种子，它们开始发芽了。
She had reawakened her dream of becoming a writer. = 她已经重新燃起了成为作家的梦想。

将来时:
The coming summer will reawaken the sleeping insects. = 即将来临的夏天将唤醒沉睡的昆虫。
The new campaign will reawaken the public's awareness of the issue. = 新的运动将重新唤起公众对这个问题的认识。

虚拟语气:
If the music were louder, it might reawaken him. = 如果音乐再响亮一点，可能会把他唤醒。
If only the old magic could reawaken! = 但愿古老的魔法能够重新苏醒！

被动语态:
His interest in photography was reawakened by a recent exhibition. = 他对摄影的兴趣被最近的一次展览重新唤起。
The sleeping giant was reawakened by the earthquake. = 沉睡的巨人被地震惊醒了。

疑问句:

What experience reawakened your interest in art? = 什么样的经历重新唤起了你对艺术的兴趣？

否定句:
Nothing could reawaken his interest in politics. = 什么也无法重新唤起他对政治的兴趣。
The attempt to reawaken the dormant volcano failed. = 试图唤醒休眠的火山失败了。
