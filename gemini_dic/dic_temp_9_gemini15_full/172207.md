push-button:/ˈpʊʃˌbʌtən/| adj. n.|按钮的；按钮|A push-button device is operated by pressing a button.  A push-button phone is a telephone that uses buttons to dial numbers.|例句：The washing machine has a push-button start. 这台洗衣机有一个按钮启动装置。

The remote control is push-button operated. 遥控器是按钮操作的。|近义词：button-operated, key-operated|反义词：manual, lever-operated
