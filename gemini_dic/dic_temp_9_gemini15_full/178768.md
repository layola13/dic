recubation:/ˌriːkjuˈbeɪʃən/| n. |再孵化；重新培养|The process of incubating something again, especially eggs or bacteria.  It refers to the act of restarting or repeating an incubation process. This could involve re-incubating eggs that have been removed from an incubator, or restarting a bacterial culture after it has been stopped.|Example: After the power outage, the eggs required recubation to ensure proper development.  再停电后，鸡蛋需要重新孵化才能确保正常发育。|近义词：reincubation, reinoculation|反义词：incubation termination, destruction
