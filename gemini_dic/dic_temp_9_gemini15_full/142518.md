osseter:/ˈɒsɪtər/ | n. | 鲟鱼 | A large fish with bony plates instead of scales, belonging to the family Acipenseridae.  Osseters are known for their caviar.  | Example: The sturgeon, also known as osseter, is a valuable fish.  鲟鱼，也称为osseter，是一种珍贵的鱼类。 | 近义词: sturgeon,  | 反义词:  (no direct antonym) | 名词
