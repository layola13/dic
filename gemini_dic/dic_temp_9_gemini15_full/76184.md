fussily:/ˈfʌsəli/| adj.| 琐碎地；过于讲究地；挑剔地 | Acting in a fussy manner; showing excessive concern for detail; overly meticulous.  |She fussily arranged the flowers in the vase. 她小心翼翼地把花插进花瓶里。| meticulously, meticulously, painstakingly | carelessly, casually, roughly |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fussily arranges his socks by color. 他一丝不苟地按颜色整理袜子。
She fussily checks every detail of her work. 她仔细检查工作的每一个细节。


一般过去时:
He fussily ironed his shirt. 他仔细地熨烫了他的衬衫。

虚拟语气:
If she were fussily organized, she wouldn't be so late. 如果她做事很细致，她就不会这么晚了。
