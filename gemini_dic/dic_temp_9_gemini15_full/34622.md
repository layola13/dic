cleft's:/klɛfts/| n.|裂缝，分裂；（句法）分裂句|a split, crack, or fissure; (syntax) a cleft sentence|The earthquake caused many clefts in the earth. 地震在地面上造成了许多裂缝。  The baby was born with a cleft palate.  婴儿生下来就患有唇裂。|近义词：fissure, crack, split, rift|反义词：union, whole, intact
