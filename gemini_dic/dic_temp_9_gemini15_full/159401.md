polarimetry:/ˌpoʊləˈrɪmətri/| n. |旋光测定法；旋光度测量|The measurement of the rotation of the plane of polarization of light as it passes through an optically active substance.  This technique is used to identify and quantify chiral molecules. |Example: Polarimetry is a crucial technique in the pharmaceutical industry for quality control of chiral drugs.  旋光测定法是制药行业中用于控制手性药物质量的关键技术。|近义词：optical rotation measurement|反义词：无|名词
