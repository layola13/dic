quite:/kwaɪt/ | adv. |相当，完全，的确 | Quite means to a significant degree, but not extremely.  It can also mean completely or entirely. |例句：The movie was quite good.  这部电影相当不错。|例句： I'm quite sure he'll come. 我很确定他会来。|近义词：fairly, rather, pretty, very |反义词：slightly, hardly, scarcely |无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is quite happy. = 他相当开心。
She is quite tired. = 她相当疲惫。


一般过去时:
The food was quite delicious. = 这食物相当美味。
The journey was quite long. = 这旅程相当漫长。


现在完成时:
I have been quite busy lately. = 我最近相当忙碌。
She has been quite ill. = 她病得相当严重。


过去完成时:
He had been quite nervous before the presentation. = 演讲前他相当紧张。
They had been quite surprised by the news. = 他们对这个消息相当惊讶。


将来时:
It will be quite cold tomorrow. = 明天会相当冷。
The project will be quite challenging. = 这个项目将会相当具有挑战性。


虚拟语气:
If it were quite warm, we would go for a walk. = 如果天气相当暖和，我们会去散步。
I wish I were quite sure about that. = 我希望我对那件事相当有把握。


被动语态:
The work was quite well done. = 这工作做得相当好。
The problem was quite easily solved. = 这个问题相当容易解决。


疑问句:
Are you quite sure? = 你很确定吗？
Is he quite ready? = 他准备好了吗？


否定句:
It is not quite finished yet. = 它还没完全完成。
He is not quite himself today. = 他今天不太像他自己。


PHRASE->
quite a few = 相当多
quite a lot = 相当多
quite the opposite = 完全相反
quite simply = 简而言之
quite a while = 很长一段时间
