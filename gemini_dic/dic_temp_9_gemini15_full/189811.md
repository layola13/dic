salvationer:/ˌsælˈveɪʃənər/ | n. | 救世主，救星 | A person who brings about salvation or deliverance.  This word is rarely used and often considered archaic or literary. It suggests a more active role in salvation than "savior." | Example: The general was hailed as a salvationer by the grateful citizens after liberating their city.  将军在解放他们的城市后被感激的市民誉为救世主。 | 近义词：savior, redeemer, deliverer | 反义词：destroyer, ruiner, destroyer |  名词
