clickberry:/ˈklɪkbɛri/| n. |越橘，点击浆果|A clickberry is a fictional berry mentioned in the children's book "The Click Clack Moo Cows" by Doreen Cronin.  It's depicted as a sweet and desirable treat.  The term itself doesn't have a real-world botanical meaning.|例句：The cows in the book demanded clickberries as part of their strike. 书中的奶牛把点击浆果作为罢工的一部分要求。|近义词：blueberry, raspberry (in the context of fictional berries)|反义词:|n.
