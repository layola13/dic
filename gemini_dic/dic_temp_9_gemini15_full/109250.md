lekgotla:/lɛkˈɡɔtlɑː/| n. |会议；集会；聚会|A lekgotla is a meeting, assembly, or gathering, particularly one of a formal or official nature, often used in Setswana culture.  It implies a coming together for discussion and decision-making.|例句：The village elders held a lekgotla to discuss the drought.  村里的长老们举行了一次会议来讨论干旱问题。


The community organized a lekgotla to plan the new school. 社区组织了一次会议来规划新学校。|近义词：meeting, assembly, conference, gathering|反义词：dispersion, separation, scattering|名词
