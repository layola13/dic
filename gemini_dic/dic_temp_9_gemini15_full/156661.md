picturesque:/ˌpɪktʃəˈrɛsk/| adj. |风景如画的，美丽的|  pleasing or charmingly quaint in appearance; picturesque scenery or a picturesque village.  The word evokes a sense of beauty that is both pleasing and charming, often associated with a quaint or idyllic setting.  It suggests a scene that is visually appealing and evokes a sense of nostalgia or romanticism. |例句：The village nestled in the valley was picturesque.  山谷里依偎的小村庄风景如画。 The picturesque landscape inspired the artist to paint.  风景如画的景色激发了艺术家作画的灵感。|近义词：scenic, beautiful, charming, idyllic, quaint|反义词：ugly, unattractive, bleak, dreary
