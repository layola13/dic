namer:/ˈneɪmər/ | n. |命名者，取名者 | A person who gives names, especially to things or places.  |  The namer of this new species of bird is a renowned ornithologist.  这位新鸟类的命名者是一位著名的鸟类学家。|  titler, christener |  unnamed | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The namer carefully considers each option. = 命名者仔细考虑每一个选择。
She names her daughter Lily. = 她给她女儿取名莉莉。


一般过去时:
The artist namer his painting "Sunrise". = 艺术家给他的画命名为“日出”。
They named their son after his grandfather. = 他们以他祖父的名字给他儿子命名。


现在完成时:
The company has named its new product "Nova". = 公司已经将其新产品命名为“Nova”。
Scientists have named several new species this year. = 科学家们今年已经命名了几个新物种。


过去完成时:
They had already named the baby before the grandparents arrived. = 外祖父母到达之前，他们已经给婴儿取好名字了。
The committee had named the winner before the announcement. = 委员会在宣布之前就已经确定了获胜者。
