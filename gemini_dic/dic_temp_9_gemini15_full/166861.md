pressure: /ˈprɛʃər/ | n. |压力，压强，强制 | The force exerted on a surface by something in contact with it.  It can refer to physical force, social influence, or mental strain. |例句：The pressure of the water damaged the pipes. 水压损坏管道了。|例句：She felt immense pressure to succeed. 她感到巨大的成功压力。|近义词：stress, tension, strain, compulsion |反义词：relief, ease
