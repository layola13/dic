eulogic:/juːˈlɒdʒɪk/| n.|赞美，颂词|A eulogy is a speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, especially a tribute to someone who has recently died.  It often focuses on the positive aspects of the person's life and achievements.| Example: The eulogy at his funeral was both moving and inspiring.  他的葬礼上的悼词既感人又鼓舞人心。|近义词：encomium, panegyric, tribute, praise|反义词：condemnation, criticism, denunciation|名词
