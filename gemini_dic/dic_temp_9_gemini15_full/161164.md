postgrad:/ˈpəʊstɡræd/| n.|研究生；研究生课程|A postgraduate is a student who is studying for a degree after having already received a first degree.  It can also refer to the studies themselves.|She is a postgrad studying astrophysics. 她是一位学习天体物理学的研究生。 He's currently enrolled in a postgrad program. 他目前正在攻读研究生课程。|graduate student, postgraduate student| undergraduate student|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is a postgrad. = 他是研究生。
She is studying for a postgrad degree. = 她在攻读研究生学位。

一般过去时:
He was a postgrad at Oxford. = 他以前是牛津大学的研究生。
She completed her postgrad studies last year. = 她去年完成了研究生学业。

现在完成时:
I have completed my postgrad program. = 我已经完成了我的研究生课程。
She has been a postgrad for two years. = 她已经当了两年研究生了。

过去完成时:
He had finished his undergraduate degree before starting his postgrad studies. = 他在开始研究生学习之前已经完成了本科学习。
She had already applied to several postgrad programs before receiving an acceptance. = 在收到录取通知书之前，她已经申请了几所研究生院。

将来时:
He will be a postgrad next year. = 他明年将成为研究生。
She will start her postgrad studies in the fall. = 她将在秋季开始研究生学习。

虚拟语气:
If I were a postgrad, I would have more time for research. = 如果我是研究生，我会有更多时间做研究。
I wish I had applied to more postgrad programs. = 我希望我申请了更多研究生项目。

被动语态:
The application was reviewed by the postgrad admissions committee. = 申请书由研究生招生委员会审核。
Many postgrad courses are taught online. = 许多研究生课程都是在线授课的。

疑问句:
Are you a postgrad? = 你是研究生吗？
What postgrad program are you enrolled in? = 你报读了什么研究生课程？

否定句:
He is not a postgrad yet. = 他还不是研究生。
She did not apply for any postgrad programs this year. = 她今年没有申请任何研究生项目。
