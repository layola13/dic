cheapo:/ˈtʃiːpoʊ/ | adj. |便宜货；吝啬的；劣质的 |A cheapo is something that is of low quality or very inexpensive, often implying a lack of value for the money. It can also refer to a person who is stingy or unwilling to spend money. |例句：That watch is a real cheapo; it broke after a week. 那块手表真是个便宜货，一周后就坏了。

He's such a cheapo; he never tips. 他真是个吝啬鬼，从不给小费。|近义词：cheap, inexpensive, stingy, miserly |反义词：expensive, costly, generous
