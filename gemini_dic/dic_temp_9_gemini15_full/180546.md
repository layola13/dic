regilded:/rɪˈɡɪldɪd/ | v. | 再镀金；重新镀金 | To gild again; to cover with gold leaf or paint a second time.  The term implies a previous gilding that has worn off or been removed. | Example: The old frame was regilded to restore its former glory.  旧相框重新镀金以恢复其昔日的辉煌。 The statue, once regilded, shone brilliantly in the sun.  这座雕像重新镀金后，在阳光下闪闪发光。 | 近义词：re-plate, redecorate | 反义词：strip, remove gold
