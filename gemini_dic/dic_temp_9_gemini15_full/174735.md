rabate:/ˈreɪbeɪt/| n. |回扣；折扣；减免|A rebate is a partial refund to someone who has paid too much for something.  It can also refer to a discount or reduction in price.|Example:  The company offered a rebate on its new washing machine.  这家公司对其新款洗衣机提供折扣。

The store gave me a rebate on my purchase. 商店给了我购买商品的折扣。|近义词：discount, refund, reduction |反义词：surcharge, markup


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company offers a rebate on its products. = 公司对其产品提供折扣。
Customers receive a rebate after mail-in registration. = 顾客邮寄注册后会收到折扣。

一般过去时:
They gave me a rebate for the damaged goods. = 他们因商品损坏给了我折扣。
I received a rebate on my car insurance. = 我收到了汽车保险的折扣。

现在完成时:
We have received our rebate check. = 我们已经收到了折扣支票。
The government has offered rebates on energy-efficient appliances. = 政府已对节能家电提供折扣。

过去完成时:
They had already claimed their rebate before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经申请了折扣。
She had not received her rebate yet. = 她还没有收到她的折扣。

将来时:
They will offer a rebate on their new model. = 他们将对他们的新车型提供折扣。
We will apply for the rebate next week. = 我们下周将申请折扣。


虚拟语气:
If I bought that car, I would get a rebate. = 如果我买了那辆车，我会得到折扣。
I wish I had known about the rebate earlier. = 我真希望早点知道这个折扣。


被动语态:
Rebates are offered on select items. = 部分商品提供折扣。
The rebate was processed quickly. = 折扣很快就被处理了。


疑问句:
Is there a rebate available for this product? = 此产品是否有折扣？
What is the amount of the rebate? = 折扣金额是多少？


否定句:
There was no rebate available. = 没有可用的折扣。
They didn't offer a rebate on that item. = 他们没有对该商品提供折扣。


PHRASE->

cash rebate = 现金回扣
mail-in rebate = 邮寄回扣
rebate coupon = 折扣券
rebate program = 折扣计划
energy rebate = 能源补贴


词源:

The word "rebate" ultimately comes from the Old French word "rebattre," meaning "to beat back" or "to deduct."  This reflects the action of reducing a price or refunding a portion of a payment.
