grottos:/ˈɡrɒtoʊz/ | n. |岩洞，洞穴 |A grotto is a small cave, especially one that is attractive or interesting.  It often implies a picturesque or romanticized cave, perhaps with interesting formations or decorations.  |例句：The children loved exploring the grottos along the coast. 孩子们喜欢探索沿海的岩洞。

例句：We discovered a hidden grotto behind the waterfall. 我们在瀑布后面发现了一个隐藏的岩洞。


近义词：cave, cavern


反义词：open space, plain


词性：名词
