misremember:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbər/ | vt. | 误记，记错 | To remember something incorrectly; to have a false memory of something.  |例句：I misremembered the date of the meeting. 我记错了会议日期。

例句：He misremembered the details of the accident. 他记错了事故的细节。


近义词：forget, recall incorrectly, misrecollect | 反义词：remember correctly, recall accurately | 词性:动词
