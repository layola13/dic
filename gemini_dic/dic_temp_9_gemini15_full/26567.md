cabless:/ˈkæbləs/| adj.| 无线的，不使用电缆的 | Without cables; wireless.  The term describes something that operates without the need for physical cables or wires to transmit data or power.  | Example:  The new headphones are cabless, offering greater freedom of movement. 新耳机是无线的，提供了更大的活动自由。 The cabless network allowed for greater flexibility in device placement. 无线网络允许设备放置更加灵活。 |近义词：wireless, cordless |反义词：wired, cabled
