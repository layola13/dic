nattering:/ˈnætərɪŋ/| adj. |喋喋不休的；唠叨的 |Continuously talking in a rapid, inconsequential way; chattering.  It often implies annoyance or irritation at the incessant talk.|例句：I couldn't concentrate on my work with the children's constant nattering.  孩子们不停地唠叨，我无法集中精力工作。|例句：The nattering of the politicians on television was driving me crazy. 电视上政客们的喋喋不休快把我逼疯了。|近义词：chattering, gossiping, babbling, prattling |反义词：silent, quiet, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The children are nattering incessantly. = 孩子们不停地喋喋不休。
He is always nattering about his problems. = 他总是喋喋不休地抱怨他的问题。

一般过去时:
They nattered away for hours. = 他们喋喋不休地聊了好几个小时。
She nattered about her trip to Europe. = 她喋喋不休地谈论她去欧洲的旅行。

现在完成时:
They have been nattering since morning. = 他们从早上就开始喋喋不休了。
She has nattered on about the same topic all day. = 她一整天都在喋喋不休地谈论同一个话题。

过去完成时:
They had nattered for hours before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经喋喋不休地聊了好几个小时。
She had nattered about her work before she finally fell asleep. = 她喋喋不休地谈论她的工作，直到最后才睡着。

将来时:
They will natter all through the meeting. = 他们将在整个会议期间喋喋不休。
She will probably natter about her new car all evening. = 她今晚可能会喋喋不休地谈论她的新车。

虚拟语气:
If they nattered less, we could get some work done. = 如果他们少说些话，我们就能完成一些工作。
I wish they wouldn't natter so much. = 我希望他们不要说得那么多。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The problem was nattered over endlessly. = 这个问题被没完没了地喋喋不休地讨论着。(This is an unusual usage)
The topic was nattered to death. = 这个话题被喋喋不休地讨论到死。(This is an idiomatic usage)

疑问句:
Are they still nattering? = 他们还在喋喋不休吗？
Was she nattering about her neighbors again? = 她又在喋喋不休地谈论她的邻居了吗？

否定句:
They didn't natter during the presentation. = 他们在演示期间没有喋喋不休。
She didn't natter once during the quiet time. = 在安静的时间里，她一次也没有唠叨。
