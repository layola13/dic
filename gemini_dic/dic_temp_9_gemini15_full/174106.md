quagmire:/ˈkwæɡmaɪər/ | n. | 困境；沼泽；泥潭 | A difficult, precarious, or troublesome situation.  |例句：The project quickly became a quagmire of delays and cost overruns.  项目很快陷入了延误和成本超支的困境。
例句：He got stuck in a quagmire and had to be rescued. 他陷入了泥潭，不得不被救出来。| 近义词：morass, mire, predicament, impasse | 反义词：smooth sailing, easy path | 名词
