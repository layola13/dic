quotation:/kwoʊˈteɪʃən/| n. |引语，报价，引文 |A quotation is a group of words taken from a text or speech and repeated by someone else.  It can refer to a passage from a book, a speech, or any other source.  |例句：The essay included a quotation from Shakespeare. 这篇文章包含莎士比亚的引语。|例句：The company gave me a quotation for the repair work. 公司给了我一份维修报价。|近义词：citation, excerpt, quote |反义词：paraphrase
