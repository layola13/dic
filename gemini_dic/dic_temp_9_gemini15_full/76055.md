furnaced: /ˈfɜːrnəst/ | adj. | 经过炉火烧制的；用炉火烧制的 | Heated or treated in a furnace.  | The furnaced bricks were exceptionally strong and durable.  经过炉火烧制的砖块非常坚固耐用。| heated, baked, fired | unheated, raw |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The potter furnaces the clay to harden it. = 陶工将黏土放入炉中烧制以使其变硬。
He furnaces the metal to shape it. = 他将金属放入炉中加热以塑形。


一般过去时:
They furnaced the bricks before building the house. = 他们在建造房子之前烧制了砖块。
The blacksmith furnaced the iron to make a horseshoe. = 铁匠将铁放入炉中加热来制作马蹄铁。


现在完成时:
The kiln has furnaced thousands of pottery pieces. = 窑炉已经烧制了数千件陶器。
She has furnaced the glass to create unique shapes. = 她已经将玻璃放入炉中烧制，创造出独特的形状。


过去完成时:
The bricks had been furnaced before the mortar was applied. = 在涂抹砂浆之前，砖块已经被烧制过了。
The metal had been furnaced to a high temperature before being shaped. = 金属在成型前已经被加热到高温。


将来时:
They will furnace the metal tomorrow. = 他们明天将对金属进行炉火烧制。
The company will furnace the components before assembly. = 公司将在组装前对组件进行炉火烧制。


虚拟语气:
If they had furnaced the clay properly, it wouldn't have cracked. = 如果他们正确地烧制了黏土，它就不会开裂了。
I wish I had furnaced the glass at a lower temperature. = 我真希望我以较低的温度烧制了玻璃。


被动语态:

疑问句:
