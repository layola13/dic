nonstriated: /ˌnɒnˈstraɪeɪtɪd/ | adj. |平滑肌的；无条纹的 | Not having visible transverse striations, as certain muscle fibers.  This term typically refers to smooth muscle tissue, which lacks the striped appearance of skeletal and cardiac muscle.  The cells are spindle-shaped and involuntary, meaning they are not under conscious control. | Example: Smooth muscle, also known as nonstriated muscle, is found in the walls of internal organs.  平滑肌，也称为非纹状肌，存在于内脏器官的壁中。|近义词: smooth (in reference to muscle)|反义词: striated
