growlingly:/ˈɡraʊlɪŋli/| adv.|咆哮地，低沉地，怒气冲冲地|In a growling manner; with a growl.  It describes an action or utterance made with a low, guttural sound, often expressing anger, displeasure, or threat.|例句：He spoke growlingly, his voice a low rumble. 他咆哮着说话，声音低沉而模糊。

例句：The dog growlingly guarded its bone.  这条狗咆哮着守护着它的骨头。


近义词：grumblingly, angrily, sullenly, menacingly

反义词：sweetly, gently, calmly, softly

词性：副词
