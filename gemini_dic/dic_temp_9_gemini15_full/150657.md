pappus:/ˈpæpəs/| n.|冠毛，绒毛|The pappus is a modified calyx, typically composed of hairs or bristles, found in the seeds of many plants in the Asteraceae family (compositae), such as dandelions. It aids in seed dispersal by wind.  |Example: The dandelion's pappus allows its seeds to be carried far by the wind.  蒲公英的冠毛使它的种子能够被风吹得很远。|近义词：coma, down, fluff|反义词：无
