redeposit:/ˌriːdɪˈpɒzɪt/ | /ˌriːdɪˈpɑːzɪt/| vt. | 再存款；重新存放；再储蓄 | To deposit something again, especially money into a bank account.  |例句：I need to redeposit the check because it was rejected. 我需要重新存入支票，因为它被拒收了。

例句：She redeposited her savings after withdrawing them for a short time. 她提取存款一段时间后又重新存了进去。


近义词：reinvest, re-enter, replace


反义词：withdraw, remove


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redeposits his salary every month. = 他每月都把工资重新存入。
The bank redeposits the funds automatically. = 银行自动将资金重新存入。


一般过去时:
They redeposited the money into a different account. = 他们把钱存入了不同的账户。
She redeposited the check after correcting the error. = 她纠正错误后重新存入支票。


现在完成时:
I have redeposited the funds into my savings account. = 我已经将资金重新存入我的储蓄账户。
She has redeposited the money several times this week. = 本周她已经多次重新存入资金。


过去完成时:
He had redeposited the money before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已重新存入款项。
They had redeposited the check before the bank closed. = 他们在银行关门前已重新存入支票。


将来时:
I will redeposit the money tomorrow. = 我明天将重新存入这笔钱。
We will redeposit the funds once we receive confirmation. = 我们收到确认后将重新存入资金。


虚拟语气:
If I had remembered, I would have redeposited the check. = 如果我记得的话，我就已经重新存入支票了。
I wish I had redeposited the money earlier. = 我希望我早点把钱重新存进去。


被动语态:
