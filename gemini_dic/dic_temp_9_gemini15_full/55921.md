divvies:/ˈdɪviz/| vt.|分配，分摊|To divide something, especially money or property, among several people.|例句：The inheritance was divvied up among the three siblings.  遗产被三个兄弟姐妹分掉了。|例句：They divvied the profits fairly among the team members. 他们公平地将利润分配给团队成员。|近义词：divide, share, distribute, apportion|反义词：unite, combine, consolidate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They divvy up the work every morning. = 他们每天早上分工。
She divvies the candy among the children. = 她把糖果分给孩子们。


一般过去时:
He divvied the spoils among his crew. = 他把战利品分给了他的船员。
We divvied the cake into six equal pieces. = 我们把蛋糕分成六等份。


现在完成时:
They have divvied up the responsibilities for the project. = 他们已经分担了项目的责任。
She has divvied the inheritance fairly. = 她已经公平地分配了遗产。


过去完成时:
They had divvied the land before the dispute began. = 在争端开始之前，他们已经分了地。
She had divvied the funds according to the agreement. = 她已经根据协议分配了资金。


将来时:
They will divvy the profits at the end of the year. = 他们将在年底分配利润。
We will divvy the tasks among ourselves. = 我们将把任务分配给我们自己。


虚拟语气:
If they divvied the resources fairly, there would be less conflict. = 如果他们公平地分配资源，冲突就会减少。
I wish they had divvied the workload more evenly. = 我希望他们更平均地分配工作量。


被动语态:
The profits were divvied up equally. = 利润被平均分配了。
The land will be divvied among the heirs. = 土地将被分配给继承人。


疑问句:
How will they divvy up the costs? = 他们将如何分摊成本？
Have they divvied the responsibilities yet? = 他们已经分配责任了吗？


否定句:
They didn't divvy up the resources effectively. = 他们没有有效地分配资源。
She wouldn't divvy her fortune to strangers. = 她不会把她的财产分给陌生人。
