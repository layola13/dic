eddying:/ˈɛdiɪŋ/| v.|旋涡状流动，回旋|The movement of water in a circular motion, often caused by an obstacle or current.  It can also refer to a similar swirling motion in other mediums like air or smoke.|例句：The river eddied around the bend.  河水在河弯处打着旋儿流动。

The leaves eddied in the autumn wind. 秋风中落叶打着旋儿飘落。|近义词： swirling, whirling, circling, rotating|反义词：flowing straight, moving linearly|


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water eddies around the rocks. = 水在岩石周围打漩。
The smoke eddies upwards. = 烟向上打旋。

一般过去时:
The wind eddied the dust into a swirling cloud. = 风把尘土卷成一团旋风。
The boat eddied in the strong current. = 小船在急流中打转。


现在完成时:
The river has eddied away much of the sediment. = 河流已经冲走了大部分沉淀物。
The wind has eddied the leaves into a pile. = 风把树叶卷成一堆。


过去完成时:
The current had eddied the debris against the bridge supports. = 水流已将碎屑冲到桥墩旁。
The smoke had eddied and settled before the firefighters arrived. = 在消防员到达之前，烟雾已经打旋并沉降下来。


将来时:
The wind will eddy the snow into drifts. = 风将把雪吹成雪堆。
The river will eddy around the newly placed rocks. = 河水将绕着新放置的岩石打旋。


虚拟语气:
If the wind were stronger, the leaves would eddy more violently. = 如果风更大，树叶就会更剧烈地旋转。
I wish the smoke would eddy away more quickly. = 我希望烟能更快地散去。


被动语态:
The dust was eddied by the strong wind. = 强风卷起了尘土。
The leaves were eddied into a pile by the gust of wind. = 一阵风把树叶卷成一堆。


疑问句:
Does the current eddy around that pier? = 水流是否在那座码头周围打旋？
Will the smoke eddy towards the building? = 烟会飘向建筑物吗？


否定句:
The water didn't eddy much in the calm lake. = 在平静的湖面上，水几乎没有打旋。
The air didn't eddy after the explosion. = 爆炸后空气没有打旋。
