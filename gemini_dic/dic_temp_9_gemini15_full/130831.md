nonapplicability:/ˌnɒnˌæplɪkəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不适用性，无效性 | The state or quality of not being applicable; irrelevance.  It refers to something that is not relevant or appropriate in a particular situation or context.  | Example: The rules regarding overtime pay have nonapplicability in this specific case due to the employee's unique contract.  这个加班工资的规定在这个特定案例中不适用，因为员工的合同独一无二。  |近义词：inapplicability, irrelevance, inappropriateness | 反义词：applicability, relevance
