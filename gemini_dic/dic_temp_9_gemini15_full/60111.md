efface:/ɪˈfeɪs/| vt.|抹去；消除；使…显得不重要 |to remove or obliterate something, especially a mark or inscription; to make something less noticeable or important.|The rain had effaced the footprints in the sand. 雨水冲刷掉了沙子上的脚印。|The government tried to efface the memory of the past regime. 政府试图抹去过去政权的记忆。|obliterate, erase, remove, delete |emphasize, highlight, accentuate|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He effaces his mistakes. 他抹去他的错误。
She effaces the graffiti from the wall. 她擦掉了墙上的涂鸦。

一般过去时:
The inscription was effaced by time.  铭文被时间磨灭了。
They effaced all evidence of their crime. 他们抹去了他们犯罪的所有证据。

现在完成时:
The years have effaced many details from her memory. 岁月已从她的记忆中抹去了许多细节。
Time has effaced the inscription on the tombstone. 时间已经磨去了墓碑上的铭文。

过去完成时:
The wind had effaced the tracks in the snow. 风已将雪地上的痕迹抹去了。
They had effaced all traces of their presence before the police arrived. 警察到来之前，他们已经抹去了所有他们存在过的痕迹。

将来时:
The rain will efface the writing on the sidewalk. 雨水会冲掉人行道上的字迹。
The new paint will efface the old scratches on the wall. 新涂料会消除墙上的旧划痕。

虚拟语气:
If I could efface my past mistakes, I would. 如果我能抹去我过去的错误，我会这么做的。
I wish I could efface the memory of that day. 我希望我能忘记那天的事情。

被动语态:
The inscription was effaced by the weather. 铭文被风雨侵蚀掉了。
His accomplishments were almost effaced by his later failures. 他的成就几乎被他后来的失败所掩盖。

疑问句:
Can we efface this stain? 我们能去除这个污渍吗？
Will the cleaning solution efface the marks? 清洁剂能去除这些痕迹吗？

否定句:
The evidence could not be effaced. 证据无法抹去。
He refused to efface his name from the list. 他拒绝从名单上删除他的名字。


PHRASE->

efface oneself:  to make oneself inconspicuous or unimportant  让自己不引人注目或显得不重要
efface the past: 抹去过去
efface a memory: 抹去一段记忆
efface one's identity: 隐藏身份
efface the differences: 消除差异
