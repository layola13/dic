charitableness:/ˈtʃærɪtəblənəs/| n. |慈善；仁慈；慷慨|The quality of being charitable; generosity and kindness.  It refers to a willingness to give freely to those in need and to show compassion and understanding towards others. |例句：Her charitableness was evident in her numerous donations to local charities.  她的慈善行为体现在她对当地慈善机构的大量捐款中。

例句：The organization praised the charitableness of its volunteers.  该组织赞扬了志愿者的慈善精神。


近义词：benevolence, generosity, kindness, compassion, philanthropy|反义词：selfishness, greed, cruelty, meanness, stinginess|词性：名词
