silicates:/ˈsɪlɪkeɪts/| n.|硅酸盐|Silicates are minerals containing silicon and oxygen, usually with one or more metals. They are the most abundant minerals in the Earth's crust.  |例句：Feldspar and quartz are common examples of silicates.  长石和石英是常见的硅酸盐例子。|近义词：siliceous minerals|反义词：无
