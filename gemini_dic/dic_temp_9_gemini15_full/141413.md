ophiophagous:/ˌɒfiˈɒfəɡəs/ | adj. | 食蛇的；以蛇为食的 |  Eating or feeding on snakes.  The term refers to animals that have snakes as a significant part of their diet. | Example:  The eagle is an ophiophagous bird.  这只鹰是食蛇的鸟类。  | 近义词：serpentivorous | 反义词：无  |  词性：形容词
