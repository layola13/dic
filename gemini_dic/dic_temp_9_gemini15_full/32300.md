chieve:/tʃiːv/| v.|完成，实现，达到|To accomplish or succeed in doing something.|He finally achieved his goal of becoming a doctor. 他最终实现了成为医生的目标。| accomplish, attain, fulfill | fail, miss |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They achieve great things. = 他们取得了巨大的成就。
She achieves her goals through hard work. = 她通过努力工作实现了自己的目标。


一般过去时:
He achieved a lot in his career. = 他在事业上取得了很多成就。
We achieved our target ahead of schedule. = 我们提前完成了目标。


现在完成时:
