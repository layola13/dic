manganic:/mæŋˈɡeɪnɪk/ | adj. | 锰的（三价的）; 锰酸的 | Relating to or denoting manganese in the trivalent state.  Manganic compounds are those in which manganese has an oxidation state of +3. | Example: Manganic oxide is a compound containing manganese in the +3 oxidation state. 锰酸氧化物是一种含有+3价锰的化合物。|近义词：manganous (if referring to the lower oxidation state)|反义词：manganous (if referring to the lower oxidation state)
