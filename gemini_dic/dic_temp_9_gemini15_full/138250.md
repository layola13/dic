nosy-parkers:/ˈnoʊzi ˈpɑːrkərz/| n. |爱打听别人隐私的人；爱管闲事的人 |Nosy-parkers are people who are excessively curious about the private affairs of others and often interfere in matters that do not concern them. They enjoy gossiping and meddling in other people's business.  |例句：Mind your own business, you nosy-parker!  别管闲事，你这爱管闲事的人！

例句： Some nosy-parkers always try to eavesdrop on other people's conversations.  一些爱打听别人隐私的人总是试图偷听别人的谈话。


近义词：busybody, meddler, gossip, snoop, eavesdropper

反义词：mind-your-own-business person, private person


词性:名词 (复数形式)
