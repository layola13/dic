sequoia:/sɪˈkwɔɪə/| n. |红杉；巨杉|A sequoia is a type of redwood tree known for its immense size and longevity.  Sequoias are among the tallest and oldest living organisms on Earth. They are found primarily in California. |例句：The giant sequoia trees in Sequoia National Park are awe-inspiring.  加州红杉国家公园里巨大的红杉树令人敬畏。|近义词：redwood, giant redwood |反义词:None.
