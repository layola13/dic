lashon:/ˈlæʃən/| n.|鞭打；抽打；（尤指）睫毛；（非正式）严厉批评；|A lashon is a blow or stroke with a whip or something similar. It can also refer to an eyelash or, informally, a harsh criticism.|例句：The horse received a lashon from its owner. 这匹马受到了主人的鞭打。|近义词：whip, stroke, lash, criticism|反义词：praise, compliment|名词
