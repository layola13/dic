occurred:/əˈkɜːrd/| /əˈkɜːrd/ | v. |发生；出现 |To happen; to take place.  It suggests something unexpected or noteworthy happened. |The accident occurred at 3 pm. 事故发生在下午3点。|The idea occurred to me suddenly.  这个主意突然出现在我的脑海里。|happened, took place, transpired | ceased, ended |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It occurs to me that we need more help. = 我想到我们需要更多帮助。
Something interesting usually occurs in this area. = 这个区域通常会发生一些有趣的事情。


一般过去时:
The earthquake occurred last night. = 地震发生在昨晚。
The meeting occurred as planned. = 会议按计划进行。


现在完成时:
Many changes have occurred since I last visited. = 自从我上次来访以来，发生了很多变化。
A lot has occurred in the past few years. = 在过去的几年里发生了很多事情。


过去完成时:
The accident had occurred before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前事故已经发生了。
He realized that a mistake had occurred. = 他意识到发生了错误。


将来时:
The meeting is scheduled to occur next week. = 会议定于下周举行。
The eclipse will occur at dawn. = 日食将在黎明时分发生。


虚拟语气:
If it had occurred to me earlier, I would have acted differently. = 如果我早点想到，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish the accident had not occurred. = 我希望事故没有发生。


被动语态:
The incident was reported to have occurred on Friday. = 据报道，事件发生在星期五。
It was said that the accident occurred due to driver negligence. = 据说事故是由于司机疏忽造成的。


疑问句:
When did the problem occur? = 问题是什么时候发生的？
Where did the incident occur? = 事件发生在哪里？


否定句:
The meeting did not occur as scheduled. = 会议没有按计划进行。
Nothing unusual occurred during the night. = 夜间没有任何异常情况发生。



PHRASE->
occur to sb. = 想起；突然想到
It occurred to me that... = 我突然想到...
occur frequently = 经常发生
occur accidentally = 偶然发生
occur simultaneously = 同时发生
