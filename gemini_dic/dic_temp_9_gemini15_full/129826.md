non-hedonistic: /ˌnɒnˌhiːdəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非享乐主义的 | Not seeking pleasure or happiness as a primary goal; characterized by the rejection of hedonism.  It emphasizes other values or goals over the pursuit of pleasure. |


例句：

His non-hedonistic lifestyle focused on intellectual pursuits and spiritual growth, rather than material possessions or sensual gratification.  他的非享乐主义生活方式专注于知识追求和精神成长，而不是物质财富或感官享乐。


The philosopher presented a non-hedonistic argument for ethical behavior, suggesting that actions should be judged by their intrinsic value, not their capacity to produce pleasure.  这位哲学家提出了一个非享乐主义的伦理行为论证，认为应该根据行为的内在价值来判断行为，而不是其产生快乐的能力。


近义词：ascetic, austere, puritanical, self-denying


反义词：hedonistic, pleasure-seeking, sensual, self-indulgent


词性：形容词
