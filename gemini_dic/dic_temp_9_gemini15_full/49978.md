denim:/ˈdɛnɪm/| n. adj.|牛仔布，牛仔的|A sturdy cotton twill fabric, typically blue, used for jeans and other clothing.|例句：These jeans are made of 100% denim. 这条牛仔裤是100%的牛仔布做的。|例句：She wore a denim jacket. 她穿着一件牛仔外套。|近义词：dungaree, jeans(material)|反义词:silk, linen|名词，形容词
