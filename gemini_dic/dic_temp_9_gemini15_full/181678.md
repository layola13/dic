relegitimizing: /ˌriːləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使再次合法化；重新赋予合法性 |  The act of restoring legitimacy to something that has lost it.  This involves justifying or validating something previously considered illegitimate.  It often implies a process of regaining acceptance or authority. |  例句：The government is attempting to relegitimize its policies after the recent scandal. 政府正在试图在最近的丑闻后使它的政策重新合法化。


The ongoing efforts to relegitimize the monarchy are proving challenging.  持续努力使君主制重新合法化正证明具有挑战性。|近义词：rehabilitate,revalidate,reinstate,reauthorize |反义词：delegitimize,invalidate,discredit
