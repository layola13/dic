mund:/mʌnd/| n.|世界，宇宙；尘世；人群|The word "mund" refers to the world, the universe, or earthly existence, often implying a sense of ordinariness or triviality compared to spiritual or transcendent realms.  It can also refer to the worldly population or society. |例句：The mundane tasks of daily life can be overwhelming. 日常生活的琐事可能会让人不堪重负。|例句：She longed to escape the mundanity of her existence. 她渴望逃离她那平淡无奇的生活。|近义词：world, universe, cosmos, earth|反义词：heaven, spirit, otherworld|名词
