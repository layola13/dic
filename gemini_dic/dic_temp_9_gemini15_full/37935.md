completes:/kəmˈpliːts/| v. |完成，结束，使完整|To bring to an end; to finish; to make whole or entire.|例句：He completes his work before 5 pm. 他在下午5点前完成了他的工作。|例句：The team completed the project successfully.  团队成功完成了项目。|近义词：finishes, concludes, perfects|反义词：begins, starts, initiates|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He completes his homework every night. = 他每天晚上都完成作业。
She completes her tasks efficiently. = 她有效率地完成任务。

一般过去时:
They completed the marathon yesterday. = 他们昨天完成了马拉松。
The construction crew completed the building last month. = 建筑队上个月完成了大楼的建造。

现在完成时:
She has completed her degree. = 她已经完成了她的学位。
He has completed all the required training. = 他已经完成了所有必要的培训。

过去完成时:
They had completed the report before the meeting. = 他们会议前已经完成了报告。
She had completed her studies before she got the job. = 她获得工作之前已经完成了学业。

将来时:
He will complete the project by next week. = 他将在下周完成项目。
We will complete the assignment tomorrow. = 我们明天将完成作业。

虚拟语气:
If he had completed the task, he would have been promoted. = 如果他完成了任务，他就会被提升。
I wish I had completed my studies earlier. = 我希望我早点完成学业。

被动语态:
The project was completed on time. = 项目按时完成了。
The work will be completed next week. = 工作将在下周完成。

疑问句:
Have you completed the application? = 你完成了申请吗？
Did they complete the assignment? = 他们完成了作业吗？

否定句:
He did not complete the assignment. = 他没有完成作业。
She hasn't completed her report yet. = 她还没有完成她的报告。
