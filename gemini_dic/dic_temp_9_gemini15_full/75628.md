fugitively:/ˈfjuːdʒətɪvli/ | adv. | 逃亡地；短暂地；临时地 | In a fugitive manner; fleetingly; temporarily.  |例句：He lived fugitively, always looking over his shoulder. 他过着逃亡的生活，总是四处张望。

例句：The sun shone fugitively through the clouds.  阳光短暂地穿过云层。


近义词：fleetingly, transiently, temporarily, briefly


反义词：permanently, constantly, steadily


词性:副词
