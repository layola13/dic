misdirection:/ˌmɪs.dɪˈrɛk.ʃən/| n.|误导；注意力转移；障眼法|The act of misleading or distracting someone's attention; a deceptive maneuver.|例句：The magician's misdirection was so skillful that no one saw how he did the trick. 魔术师的障眼法非常巧妙，没有人看到他是如何变戏法的。

例句：The politician used misdirection to avoid answering the difficult question. 这位政治家用转移注意力的方式来避免回答那个棘手的问题。


近义词：deception, distraction, illusion, trickery|反义词：clarification, truth, honesty|名词
