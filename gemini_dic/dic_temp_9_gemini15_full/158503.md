plenitudes:/ˈplɛnɪˌtuːdz/| n. |丰富；充裕；充足 |Abundant amounts; a great quantity or supply.  The word suggests not just a large quantity, but also a feeling of overflowing abundance and richness. |例句：The garden was bursting with a plenitude of flowers. 花园里盛开着大量的花朵。

例句：The country enjoys a plenitude of natural resources. 这个国家拥有丰富的自然资源。


近义词：abundance, profusion, wealth, richness, affluence

反义词：scarcity, dearth, paucity, lack


词源:  From Middle English *plente, from Old French *plenté, from Latin *plēnitūdō, from plēnus "full".  The suffix "-tude" indicates a state or quality.
