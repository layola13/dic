polyclinic:/ˈpɒliˌklɪnɪk/ /ˌpɑːliˈklɪnɪk/| n. |多科诊所，综合门诊部 |A polyclinic is a medical facility that provides a wide range of medical services under one roof, often including outpatient services, diagnostic testing, and sometimes minor surgery.  It differs from a hospital in that it typically does not provide inpatient care. |例句：The new polyclinic offers a comprehensive range of services, from general practice to specialist consultations. 新的多科诊所提供全面的服务，从全科医生到专家咨询。

例句：Many people prefer the convenience of a polyclinic for routine checkups and minor ailments. 许多人更喜欢多科诊所的便利性来进行常规检查和治疗轻微疾病。


近义词：medical center, health center, clinic complex

反义词：specialized clinic (in the sense of providing only one type of care)


词源:  The word "polyclinic" is derived from the Greek words "poly" (meaning "many") and "klinikos" (meaning "relating to a clinic").  Therefore, it literally means "many clinics" or "a clinic with many specialties".
