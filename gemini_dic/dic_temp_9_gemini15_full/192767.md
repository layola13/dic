sealable:/ˈsiːləbl/ | adj. | 可密封的，密封的 | Capable of being sealed; able to be made airtight or watertight.  | Example:  This container is sealable to keep the contents fresh.  这个容器是可密封的，可以保持内容物的新鲜。 The bag is sealable with a zip lock. 这个袋子可以用拉链密封。|近义词：airtight, watertight, closable |反义词：unsealable, leaky
