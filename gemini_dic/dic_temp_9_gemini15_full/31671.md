chattel: /ˈtʃætəl/ | n. | 动产，财产 | Movable property; personal property as opposed to real estate.  Chattels are items of personal property that can be moved from one place to another.  | Example: The farmer's chattels included his livestock and farm equipment.  这个农民的动产包括他的牲畜和农场设备。|近义词：goods, effects, property | 反义词：real estate, land
