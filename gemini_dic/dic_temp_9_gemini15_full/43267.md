crafters:/ˈkræftərz/| n.|工匠，手工艺人|People who are skilled in making things by hand.  They often work with various materials and techniques to create unique and often functional items.|例句：The crafters at the market displayed their beautiful handmade jewelry.集市上的手工艺人展示了他们美丽的纯手工珠宝。
例句：Many skilled crafters participate in the annual craft fair.许多熟练的工匠参加了年度工艺品博览会。|近义词：artisans, craftspeople, makers|反义词：mass producers, factory workers|名词
