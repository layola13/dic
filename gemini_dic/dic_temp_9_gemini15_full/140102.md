off-limits: /ˌɒfˈlɪmɪts/ |adj.| 禁止进入的；禁止使用的； |Something that is off-limits is forbidden or inaccessible.  It is a place, item, or topic that one is not allowed to enter, use, or discuss. |例句：The top floor of the building is off-limits to unauthorized personnel.  大楼顶层禁止非授权人员进入。

The military base was declared off-limits to civilians.  军事基地被宣布禁止平民进入。


近义词：forbidden, prohibited, restricted, taboo, out of bounds |反义词：accessible, permissible, allowed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
That area is off-limits. = 那个区域禁止进入。
This topic is off-limits for discussion. = 这个话题禁止讨论。

一般过去时:
The beach was off-limits during the storm. = 暴风雨期间海滩禁止进入。
The files were off-limits to everyone except the manager. = 除经理外，所有文件都禁止查看。

现在完成时:
The server has been off-limits since the security breach. = 自安全漏洞以来，服务器一直禁止访问。
The project has been off-limits to new team members until now. = 直到现在，新团队成员都无法接触这个项目。

过去完成时:
The room had been off-limits for weeks before the renovation. = 在装修之前，这个房间已经禁止进入几周了。
The information had been off-limits until the official announcement. = 在官方宣布之前，该信息一直保密。

将来时:
That section of the forest will be off-limits during hunting season. = 打猎季节期间，森林的这一区域将禁止进入。
The software will be off-limits to beta testers until next week. = 该软件在下周之前将禁止测试人员使用。


虚拟语气:
If the area were off-limits, we wouldn't have gone there. = 如果该区域禁止进入，我们就不去了。
I wish that information wasn't off-limits. = 我希望那些信息不是禁止访问的。

被动语态:
The area was declared off-limits by the authorities. = 该区域已被当局宣布禁止进入。
The documents were made off-limits to the public. = 文件对公众而言被禁止访问。

疑问句:
Is this area off-limits? = 这个区域禁止进入吗？
Are those files off-limits to me? = 那些文件我不能访问吗？

否定句:
The park is not off-limits today. = 公园今天没有禁止进入。
That information isn't off-limits to senior staff. = 那些信息对高级员工并不禁止访问。


PHRASE->
completely off-limits = 完全禁止
strictly off-limits = 严格禁止
declared off-limits = 宣布禁止
remain off-limits = 仍然禁止
off-limits to the public = 对公众禁止
