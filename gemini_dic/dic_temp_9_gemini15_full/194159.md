self-interest:/ˈsɛlf ˈɪntrəst/| n.| 自私自利；私利 |The pursuit of one's own advantage, often regardless of the consequences for others.  It can refer to actions motivated by personal gain or benefit.|例句：He acted purely out of self-interest, ignoring the needs of his colleagues. 他完全出于私利行事，无视同事的需求。|例句：Her decision was driven by self-interest, not altruism. 她的决定是由私利而非利他主义驱动的。|近义词：egoism, selfishness, self-seeking |反义词：altruism, selflessness
