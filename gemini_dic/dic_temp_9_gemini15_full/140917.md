oncologists:/ˌɒŋˈkɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |肿瘤学家|A medical doctor specializing in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of cancer.  Oncologists are trained to understand the complex biology of cancer and to develop personalized treatment plans for their patients. They often work in collaboration with other healthcare professionals, such as surgeons, radiation oncologists, and nurses.|例句：The oncologist explained the treatment options for my mother's breast cancer. 肿瘤科医生解释了我母亲乳腺癌的治疗方案。

例句：My father is seeing an oncologist for his lung cancer. 我父亲正在看肿瘤科医生治疗他的肺癌。


近义词：cancer specialist, cancer doctor|反义词：无
