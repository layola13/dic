quick:/kwɪk/ | adj. | 快速的，敏捷的 |  Quick means done or happening with great speed. It can also describe someone who is mentally alert or someone who is clever. | 例句：He gave a quick answer. 他迅速地回答了。

He is a quick learner. 他是一个学习能力强的人。


近义词：fast, rapid, speedy, swift, brisk | 反义词：slow, sluggish, leisurely
