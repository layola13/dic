crystalling:/ˈkrɪstəliŋ/| v.|结晶；使结晶；变得清晰|The process of forming crystals; becoming clear and distinct.  |例句：The solution is slowly crystallising.  溶液正在缓慢结晶。

The sugar is crystallising in the jar. 糖在罐子里结晶了。|近义词：crystallization, solidifying|反义词：liquefying, melting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water is crystallising slowly. = 水正在缓慢结晶。
The solution is crystallising in the beaker. = 溶液正在烧杯中结晶。

一般过去时:
The mixture crystallised overnight. = 混合物一夜之间结晶了。
The scientist crystallised the protein sample. = 科学家使蛋白质样品结晶。

现在完成时:
The chemist has crystallised several new compounds. = 化学家已经结晶了几种新的化合物。
The sugar has crystallised at the bottom of the jar. = 糖已经沉淀在罐子底部结晶了。


过去完成时:
The experiment had crystallised before the observation. = 实验在观察之前就已经结晶了。
The solution had crystallised completely by morning. = 溶液到早上已经完全结晶了。


将来时:
The scientist will crystallise the sample tomorrow. = 科学家明天将使样品结晶。
The mixture will crystallise if left undisturbed. = 如果静置不动，混合物将会结晶。

虚拟语气:
If the conditions were right, the solution would crystallise. = 如果条件合适，溶液就会结晶。
I wish the protein would crystallise more easily. = 我希望蛋白质更容易结晶。


被动语态:
The crystals were crystallised by the researcher. = 晶体是由研究人员结晶的。(被动语态略显冗余，更常见的表达是：The researcher crystallised the crystals.)
The sample is being crystallised in the laboratory. = 样品正在实验室中结晶。


疑问句:
Is the solution crystallising properly? = 溶液结晶是否正常？
How long will it take for the substance to crystallise? = 该物质结晶需要多长时间？


否定句:
The solution is not crystallising quickly enough. = 溶液结晶速度不够快。
The experiment did not crystallise the protein. = 实验没有使蛋白质结晶。
