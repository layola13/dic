dialled:/daɪld/ | v. |拨打（电话）|To use a telephone to call someone.| I dialled his number but he didn't answer. 我拨了他的号码，但他没有接。|ring, call, phone|disconnect, hang up|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I dial my mother every Sunday. = 我每周日都给我母亲打电话。
She dials the number carefully. = 她小心翼翼地拨号。


一般过去时:
He dialled the emergency services. = 他拨打了紧急服务电话。
I dialled the wrong number. = 我拨错了号码。


现在完成时:
She has dialled that number several times. = 她已经拨打过那个号码好几次了。
They have dialled 911. = 他们已经拨打了911。


过去完成时:
I had dialled the number before he called me. = 在他给我打电话之前，我已经拨过那个号码了。
She had dialled the wrong number three times. = 她已经三次拨错号码了。


将来时:
I will dial your number as soon as I arrive. = 我一到就拨你的号码。
He will dial the number again tomorrow. = 他明天会再拨打这个号码。


虚拟语气:
If I had dialled sooner, I might have reached him. = 如果我早点拨打，也许就能联系上他了。
I wish I had dialled the right number. = 我真希望我拨对了号码。


被动语态:
The number was dialled incorrectly. = 号码拨错了。
The emergency services were dialled immediately. = 紧急服务部门立即被拨打了。


疑问句:
Have you dialled the correct number? = 你拨对了号码吗？
Did he dial the number himself? = 他自己拨的号码吗？


否定句:
I didn't dial the number. = 我没有拨打那个号码。
He hasn't dialled that number yet. = 他还没拨打那个号码。


PHRASE->
dial a number = 拨打一个号码
dial in = 拨入
dial up =拨号接入
speed dial =快速拨号
rotary dial =旋转拨号盘
