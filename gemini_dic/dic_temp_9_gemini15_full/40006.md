consulter:/kənˈsʌltər/| v. |咨询；请教；查阅|To seek information or advice from (someone), especially an expert.|例句：I consulted a doctor about my cough. 我因为咳嗽去看医生了。|例句：She consulted the dictionary for the meaning of the word. 她查字典查找这个词的意思。|近义词：ask, advise with, seek advice from|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consults his doctor regularly. = 他定期咨询他的医生。
She consults a lawyer before making important decisions. = 她做重要决定前会咨询律师。


一般过去时:
I consulted a map before starting my journey. = 我在开始旅程之前查阅了地图。
They consulted their parents before getting married. = 他们结婚前咨询了他们的父母。


现在完成时:
She has consulted several experts on this matter. = 她已经就此事咨询了几位专家。
He has consulted the historical records for evidence. = 他已经查阅历史记录寻找证据。


过去完成时:
I had consulted a specialist before visiting the hospital. = 我在去医院之前已经咨询过专家。
They had consulted the manual before operating the machine. = 他们操作机器之前已经查阅了说明书。


将来时:
We will consult our accountant about our taxes. = 我们将就税务问题咨询我们的会计师。
She will consult a travel agent to plan her trip. = 她将咨询旅行社来计划她的旅行。


虚拟语气:
If I had a problem, I would consult a professional. = 如果我有问题，我会咨询专业人士。
I wish I had consulted a lawyer earlier. = 我希望我早点咨询律师。


被动语态:
The doctor was consulted about the patient's condition. = 医生被咨询了病人的病情。
The lawyer was consulted regarding the contract. = 关于合同，律师被咨询了。


疑问句:
Will you consult a doctor about your headache? = 你会因为头痛去看医生吗？
Did you consult your parents before making the decision? = 你在做决定之前咨询过你的父母吗？


否定句:
He didn't consult anyone before making the investment. = 他在投资前没有咨询任何人。
She didn't consult the manual, so she made a mistake. = 她没有查阅说明书，所以犯了错误。
