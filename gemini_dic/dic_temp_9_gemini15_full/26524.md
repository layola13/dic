cabbing:/ˈkæbɪŋ/| n. |乘出租车；出租车行业|The act of traveling by taxi or the business of operating taxis.|例句：Cabbing is a convenient way to get around the city.  乘出租车是游览城市的一种便捷方式。|例句：The cabbing industry is facing challenges from ride-sharing apps. 出租车行业正面临着来自拼车应用的挑战。|近义词：taxiing, taking a cab |反义词：walking, cycling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is cabbing to the airport. = 他正在打车去机场。
They often cab to work. = 他们经常打车去上班。

一般过去时:
I cabbed home after the party. = 聚会后我打车回家了。
She cabbed to the hospital in an emergency. = 紧急情况下她打车去了医院。

现在完成时:
We have cabbed several times this week. = 我们本周已经打过几次车了。
He has never cabbed before. = 他以前从未打过车。

过去完成时:
They had cabbed to the station before the train left. = 火车离开前他们已经打车到了车站。
I had cabbed all day before I finally got home. = 我一整天都在打车，最后才到家。

将来时:
I will cab to the meeting tomorrow. = 我明天将打车去开会。
They will cab to the concert later. = 他们稍后将打车去音乐会。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would cab everywhere. = 如果我有更多钱，我就会到处打车。
I wish I could cab home right now. = 我真希望我现在就能打车回家。

被动语态:
The passengers were cabbed to their hotel. = 乘客们被送到了他们的酒店（被出租车送）。
The luggage was cabbed to the airport. = 行李被送到了机场（被出租车送）。

疑问句:

否定句:
