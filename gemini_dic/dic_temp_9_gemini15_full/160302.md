pool:/pul/| n. |水池；游泳池；（共用的一）资金；（人或物的）集合；|A pool of water; a swimming pool; a common fund; a group of people or things. |例句：The children are playing in the swimming pool.孩子们在游泳池里玩耍。

例句：We're pooling our resources to buy a new car. 我们正在合资买一辆新车。


近义词：pond, lake, collection, accumulation |反义词：N/A


词源：源自古英语 *pul,  意为“水池”。
