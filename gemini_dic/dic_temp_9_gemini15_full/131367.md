nonchurchgoing:/ˌnɒnˈtʃɜːrtʃɡəʊɪŋ/| adj.|不常去教堂的；非教徒的|Not regularly attending church services; not associated with a church.  This adjective describes individuals or groups who do not habitually participate in church activities. It doesn't necessarily imply atheism or a rejection of religion, but simply a lack of regular church attendance.|The survey revealed a surprisingly high percentage of nonchurchgoing individuals in the community.|近义词：unchurchly, secular, irreligious|反义词：churchgoing, devout, religious
