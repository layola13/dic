lifespan:/ˈlaɪfˌspæn/| n. |寿命，使用寿命，生命期 |The length of time for which something is expected to function or last.  It refers to the period of time from birth to death of a living organism, or the duration of something's existence or usefulness.|例句：The lifespan of a domestic cat is typically 15 years. 家猫的寿命通常是15年。|例句：The lifespan of this light bulb is about 10,000 hours. 这个灯泡的使用寿命大约是10000小时。|近义词：duration, lifetime, life expectancy|反义词：death, demise
