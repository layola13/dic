quantifiers:/ˈkwɒntɪfaɪərz/ /ˈkwɑːntɪfaɪərz/ | n. | 量词，限定词 | Words that express quantity or amount.  They specify how much or how many of something there is. Quantifiers can be used with both countable and uncountable nouns. | Examples:  Many students passed the exam. (countable)  A lot of water was wasted. (uncountable) |近义词：determiners, specifiers |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are many apples in the basket. = 篮子里有很多苹果。
Several people are waiting outside. = 好几个人在外面等着。


一般过去时:
A few birds sang sweetly in the morning. = 几只鸟在早晨婉转地歌唱。
Many people attended the concert. = 许多人参加了音乐会。


现在完成时:
I have eaten a lot of cake. = 我已经吃了很多蛋糕。
We have seen several interesting places. = 我们已经看过好几个有趣的地方。


过去完成时:
They had already drunk too much wine. = 他们已经喝了太多的酒。
She had read countless books. = 她读过无数本书。


将来时:
I will need a few more days to finish the project. = 我还需要几天才能完成这个项目。
There will be many opportunities in the future. = 未来会有很多机会。


虚拟语气:
If I had a little more time, I would have finished the work. = 如果我多一点时间，我就能完成工作了。
I wish I had more money. = 我希望我有更多的钱。


被动语态:
Many mistakes were made. = 犯了很多错误。
A significant amount of money was lost. = 损失了一大笔钱。


疑问句:
How many cars do you own? = 你拥有多少辆车？
How much milk do we have left? = 我们还剩多少牛奶？


否定句:
There aren't many opportunities available. = 没有多少机会可用。
I don't have much time. = 我没有多少时间。


虚拟语气:
If only I had fewer responsibilities. = 要是我责任少一点就好了。
I wish I didn't have so much work. = 我真希望我没有这么多工作。


被动语态:
Few changes were made to the plan. = 对计划只做了一些小的改动。
A small amount of damage was reported. = 报道说损失很小。


疑问句:
How much sugar do you want in your coffee? = 你咖啡里要加多少糖？
How many people are coming to the party? = 有多少人来参加聚会？


否定句:

I have few regrets. = 我几乎没有什么遗憾。
