justicehood:/ˈdʒʌstɪsˌhʊd/| n. |司法权；正义性；法官职位 |The state or quality of being just; the administration of justice; the office or dignity of a judge.  This word is rarely used and considered informal or non-standard. It is essentially a blend of "justice" and "hood," attempting to create a noun signifying the essence or domain of justice. |The justicehood of the court was questioned after the controversial ruling.  法院在做出有争议的裁决后，其公正性受到了质疑。|  fairness, righteousness |injustice, unfairness |名词
