modernization:/ˌmɒdənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌmɑːdərnəˈzeɪʃən/| n. |现代化；现代化过程 |The process of making something more modern.  This can refer to technologies, processes, systems, or even societies.  It often involves adopting new methods, ideas, or technologies to improve efficiency, effectiveness, or relevance. |例句：The modernization of the factory increased productivity. 工厂的现代化提高了生产力。


The modernization of the city involved significant infrastructure improvements. 城市的现代化涉及重大的基础设施改进。|近义词：modernization, updating, renovation, improvement|反义词：antiquation, obsolescence
