roebuck:/ˈroʊbʌk/ | n. |雄獐；(尤指)欧洲獐 | A male roe deer, especially the European roe deer (Capreolus capreolus).  | Example: The hunter spotted a magnificent roebuck in the early morning mist.  猎人清晨在薄雾中发现了一只雄伟的雄獐。 |近义词: roe deer, buck (in the context of deer)|反义词: doe (female roe deer) |名词
