didgeridoo:/ˌdɪdʒərɪˈduː/| n. |迪吉里杜管（澳大利亚原住民乐器）|A long, cylindrical Aboriginal Australian wind instrument, typically made from a hollowed-out eucalyptus branch, played by buzzing the lips into the mouthpiece.|例句：The didgeridoo's deep, resonant sound filled the amphitheater. 迪吉里杜管低沉、浑厚的声响充满了露天剧场。

例句：He learned to play the didgeridoo from an Aboriginal elder. 他向一位土著长者学习演奏迪吉里杜管。


近义词：None (unique instrument) |反义词：None (no direct opposite)|词性:noun
