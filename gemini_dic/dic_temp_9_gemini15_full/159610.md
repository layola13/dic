polliniferous:/ˌpɒlɪˈnɪfərəs/ | adj. | 传粉的；具有传粉能力的 |  Producing or carrying pollen.  The term refers to plants or parts of plants that are capable of producing or carrying pollen, essential for plant reproduction. | Example: The polliniferous flowers attracted many bees.  这些传粉的花朵吸引了许多蜜蜂。 The polliniferous stamens of the flower were heavy with pollen.  花朵上充满花粉的雄蕊沉甸甸的。 | 近义词： pollen-bearing, pollenate | 反义词： sterile, infertile
