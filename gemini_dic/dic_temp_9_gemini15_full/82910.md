gumless:/ˈɡʌmlɪs/| adj. | 无树胶的；无口香糖的 | Lacking gum or gumminess.  The term often refers to a lack of gum in the mouth or on a surface,  or to something lacking adhesive properties. | Example: The gumless candy was easy to chew.  无胶糖很容易咀嚼。 The gumless tire needed immediate replacement.  无胶轮胎需要立即更换。 | 近义词：adhesive-free, ungummed | 反义词：gummed, sticky
