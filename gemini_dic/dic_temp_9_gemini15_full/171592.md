pullulation:/ˌpʊljuˈleɪʃən/ | n. |大量繁殖；涌现；蜂拥 | The rapid increase in numbers, especially of living organisms; a swarming or teeming mass.  |例句：The pullulation of insects after the rain was astonishing. 雨后昆虫的大量繁殖令人吃惊。

例句：The pullulation of tourists in the summer months overwhelmed the small town. 夏季几个月游客的大量涌入使这个小镇不堪重负。


近义词：proliferation, swarming, teeming, burgeoning, multiplication |反义词：decline, decrease, diminution, scarcity


词源:  From Latin *pullulare "to sprout, to bud," from pullus "chick, young animal."  The sense evolved from the sprouting of plants to the rapid growth and multiplication of any living organism.
