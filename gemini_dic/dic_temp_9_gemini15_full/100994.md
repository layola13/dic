invalided:/ɪnˈvælɪdeɪt/ | vt. |使无效；使作废；推翻；使…丧失效力 | To make something invalid or ineffective; to discredit or refute something. |例句：The new evidence invalided the original theory. 新证据推翻了原来的理论。
The court ruling invalided the contract. 法院的裁决使合同无效。|近义词：invalidate, nullify, annul, negate |反义词：validate, confirm, ratify |动词
