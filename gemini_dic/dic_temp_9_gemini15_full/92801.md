icon's:/ˈaɪkɒnz/| n.|图标；偶像；标志|The word "icon's" is the possessive form of the noun "icon," indicating that something belongs to or is associated with an icon.  An icon is a small image or symbol used to represent a program, file, or function in a computer interface, or a person or thing regarded as a representative symbol of something.  |例句：The app's icon's design is very user-friendly.  这个应用程序的图标设计非常友好。

The program's icon's color changed after the update. 更新后程序图标的颜色变了。|近义词：symbol, emblem, image, pictogram|反义词：None specifically; the antonym depends on the context of "icon" (e.g., if referring to a person, "anti-hero" might be considered an antonym).|名词
