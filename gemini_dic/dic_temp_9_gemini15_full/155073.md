petrifies:/ˈpɛtrɪfaɪz/| v.|使石化；使惊呆；使僵硬|To change into stone or a stone-like substance; to paralyze with terror, astonishment, or fear; to make rigid or inflexible.|The sight of the accident petrified her.|近义词：terrify, horrify, paralyze|反义词：calm, soothe, relax|动词


USAGE->

The news petrifies him. = 这个消息让他惊呆了。
The intense heat petrifies the wood. = 强烈的热量使木材石化。

一般过去时:
