regluing: /ˌriːˈɡluːɪŋ/ | v. | 再粘合；重新粘贴 | The act of gluing something again.  It implies a previous gluing attempt that may have failed or needs reinforcement. | 例句：The broken vase required regluing. = 破碎的花瓶需要重新粘合。


The regluing of the antique chair took several hours. = 古董椅子的重新粘合花了几个小时。 | 近义词：re-adhering, reattaching | 反义词：ungluing, separating
