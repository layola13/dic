dozily:/ˈdɒzɪli/| adj. |昏昏欲睡地，懒洋洋地 |Drowsy and lethargic; sleepy.  It describes a state of being sleepy or half-asleep, often accompanied by a lack of energy and sluggishness.|例句：He dozily reached for his cup of tea. 他昏昏欲睡地去拿他的茶杯。 He spoke dozily, barely able to keep his eyes open. 他昏昏欲睡地说，几乎睁不开眼睛。|近义词：sleepily, drowsily, sluggishly |反义词：alertly, briskly, energetically
