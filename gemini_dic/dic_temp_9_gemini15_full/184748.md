resume:/rɪˈzuːm/| n. |简历，摘要，重新开始|A brief account of a person's education, qualifications, and previous experience, typically sent with a job application.|例句：I sent my resume to several companies. 我把我的简历寄给了几家公司。|近义词：curriculum vitae (CV), summary, synopsis |反义词：N/A


resume:/rɪˈzuːm/| v. |恢复，重新开始|To begin something again after it has been interrupted.|例句：He resumed his work after a long break.  他在长时间休息后恢复了工作。|近义词：restart, recommence, continue |反义词：stop, cease, discontinue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resumes his work every morning at 8 am. = 他每天早上8点恢复工作。
She resumes her studies after a long vacation. = 她暑假后重新开始学习。

一般过去时:
The meeting resumed after a short break. = 会议在短暂休息后恢复了。
They resumed their journey after the storm subsided. = 暴风雨过后，他们继续他们的旅程。

现在完成时:
She has resumed her training after a knee injury. = 她膝盖受伤后已经恢复了训练。
He has resumed his position as CEO. = 他已经恢复了首席执行官的职位。

过去完成时:
The game had resumed before I arrived. = 我到达之前比赛已经重新开始了。
They had resumed their conversation after the interruption. = 他们被打断后已经恢复了谈话。

将来时:
The project will resume next week. = 项目将在下周恢复。
He will resume his duties on Monday. = 他将在星期一恢复职务。

虚拟语气:
If he were healthy, he would resume his work. = 如果他健康的话，他会恢复工作。
I wish I could resume my travels soon. = 我希望我很快能恢复旅行。

被动语态:
The negotiations were resumed after a deadlock. = 谈判在僵局之后恢复了。
The game will be resumed tomorrow. = 比赛将于明天恢复。

疑问句:
When will the concert resume? = 音乐会什么时候恢复？
Has the project resumed yet? = 项目已经恢复了吗？

否定句:
He did not resume his work after the accident. = 事故后他没有恢复工作。
The meeting did not resume until 3 pm. = 会议直到下午3点才恢复。

词源:
The word "resume" comes from the Old French word "resumer," meaning "to take up again," or "to summarize."  It is ultimately derived from the Latin prefix "re-" (again) and the verb "sumere" (to take).  The noun and verb senses developed from this common root.
