flashness:/ˈflæʃnəs/| n.|炫耀，炫丽，俗丽，短暂的辉煌|The quality of being flashy; ostentatious display or behavior.  It often implies a lack of substance or taste. |例句：The flashiness of his new car didn't impress anyone.  他的新车虽然炫丽，却并没有给任何人留下深刻印象。|近义词：ostentation, showiness, gaudiness, flamboyance|反义词：subduedness, simplicity, modesty, plainness
