revoltingly:/rɪˈvɒltɪŋli/ | adv. |令人作呕地；令人厌恶地 | Extremely disgusting or offensive.  |例句：The smell in the kitchen was revoltingly sweet. 厨房里的气味甜得令人作呕。

例句：The way he treated his employees was revoltingly unfair. 他对待员工的方式令人厌恶地不公平。

近义词：disgustingly, sickeningly, nauseatingly, abominably

反义词：pleasantly, delightfully, agreeably
