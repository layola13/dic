capricioso: /ˌkæpriˈtʃoʊsoʊ/ | adj. | 变化无常的；反复无常的；任性的；异想天开的 |


英文解析:  Characterized by or full of caprice; impulsive and unpredictable.  Showing a tendency to act on sudden, unpredictable impulses.  Often used to describe whimsical or fanciful behavior, art, or music.


例句:

The capricious weather made planning the outdoor event difficult.  =  变化无常的天气使得户外活动的计划变得很困难。

Her capricious spending habits led to financial problems. = 她反复无常的消费习惯导致了财务问题。


近义词: whimsical, erratic, fickle, temperamental, volatile, impulsive


反义词: consistent, predictable, steady, reliable, dependable


词性: adjective
