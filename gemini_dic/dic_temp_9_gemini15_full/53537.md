disavowals: /ˌdɪsəˈvaʊəlz/ | n. |否认；否认声明；拒绝承认|The act of denying or repudiating something; a statement denying something.  Disavowals are often made in response to accusations or claims. They can be formal or informal, depending on the context.|


例句：

His disavowal of the allegations was unconvincing. = 他对指控的否认令人难以置信。

The company issued a formal disavowal of its previous statements. = 公司正式否认了之前的声明。


近义词：denials, repudiations, rejections, refutations


反义词：admissions, confirmations, endorsements, avowals


词性：名词
