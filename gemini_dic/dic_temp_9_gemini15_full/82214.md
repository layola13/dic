grotesques:/ɡroʊˈtɛsks/| n.|怪诞作品；怪诞风格；畸形的人或物|Things that are grotesques are extremely ugly, strange, or unnatural in a way that is often funny. They are often exaggerated or distorted versions of something normal.  The term often refers to artistic representations of such things.|The museum displayed a collection of grotesques, including gargoyles and distorted masks.  博物馆展出了一系列怪诞作品，包括石像鬼和扭曲的面具。

The carnival featured several grotesques performing bizarre acts.  嘉年华上有几个怪诞的人表演奇特的节目。|畸形；怪诞；怪异|beautiful, normal, natural|名词
