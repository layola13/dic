messenger: /ˈmɛsɪndʒər/ | n. |信使，使者，信差 | A person who carries messages or delivers goods.  |例句：The messenger delivered the urgent package on time. 信使准时送来了紧急包裹。

例句：He was a trusted messenger for the king. 他是国王的信使。


近义词：courier, envoy, emissary | 反义词：recipient (in the context of receiving a message)

词性：名词

词源:
The word "messenger" originates from the Old French word "messager," which itself comes from the Old French verb "messager" meaning "to carry a message."  The ultimate origin traces back to the Latin word "mittere," meaning "to send."
