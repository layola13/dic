lithologist:/lɪˈθɒlədʒɪst/| n. |岩石学家|A lithologist is a geologist who specializes in the study of rocks, their origin, composition, structure, and classification.  They analyze rock samples to understand geological processes and the history of the Earth. |The lithologist meticulously examined the rock samples, noting their texture and mineral content.|petrologist, geologist| (无明确反义词)|名词
