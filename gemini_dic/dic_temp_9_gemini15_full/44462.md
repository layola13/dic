cross-wind:/ˈkrɒsˌwɪnd/| n.|侧风，横风|A wind blowing across the direction of travel.|例句：The strong cross-wind made it difficult to land the plane. 强烈的侧风使飞机难以降落。
例句：The cyclists had to contend with a persistent cross-wind throughout the race.  整个比赛过程中，自行车手不得不应对持续的侧风。|近义词：side wind, lateral wind|反义词：headwind, tailwind|名词
