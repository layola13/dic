nourishable: /ˈnɜːrɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 可滋养的，有营养的 | Capable of being nourished; providing nourishment.  | Example:  The soil is rich and nourishable, ideal for growing vegetables. 这片土壤肥沃且富有营养，非常适合种植蔬菜。


The soil is nourishable and produces abundant crops. 这土壤富有营养，能产出丰收的庄稼。|近义词：nutritious, nutritious, nourishing, edible | 反义词：inedible, unnutritious


词源:  The word "nourishable" is derived from the verb "nourish," which comes from the Old French *nurrir (to nourish, feed), and ultimately from the Latin *nutrire (to nourish, feed, suckle). The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability.
