outkicked:/ˌaʊtˈkɪkt/| v. |胜过，技高一筹，力压|To defeat or surpass someone, especially in a contest or competition, by a significant margin.  It often implies a surprising or unexpected victory.|例句：The underdog team outkicked the favorites, winning the championship.  小球队力压热门球队，赢得了冠军。

The smaller company outkicked its larger rivals in the innovation race. 小公司在创新竞赛中胜过了更大的竞争对手。|近义词：outsmart, outmaneuver, outdo, surpass, defeat|反义词：be defeated by, be outdone by, be surpassed by|动词
