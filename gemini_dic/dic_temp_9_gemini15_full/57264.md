downpipe: /ˈdaʊnpaɪp/ | n. |落水管，排水管 | A pipe that carries rainwater from a roof gutter to a drain or other disposal point.  |Example:  The downpipe was clogged with leaves, causing water to overflow.  落水管被树叶堵住了，导致水溢出。

The downpipe on the west side of the house needs replacing. 房子的西侧落水管需要更换。|近义词：drainpipe, rainwater pipe |反义词：None  |词性:名词

词源:
The word "downpipe" is a compound word.  "Down" indicates the direction of water flow, and "pipe" refers to the tubular structure.  The word emerged in the 19th century as a descriptive term for rainwater pipes.
