lookalikes:/ˈlʊkəlaɪks/ | n. | 看起来相似的人或物 | People or things that look very similar.  They are often mistaken for each other because of their similar appearance. | Example: The twins were such lookalikes that even their parents sometimes confused them. 这对双胞胎长得太像了，甚至他们的父母有时也会弄混他们。 |近义词：doubles, counterparts, near-identical, similar |反义词： opposites, dissimilars
