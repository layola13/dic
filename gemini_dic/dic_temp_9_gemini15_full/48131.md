decoding:/dɪˈkoʊdɪŋ/| n. |解码；译码；破译|The process of converting encoded data into a human-readable or machine-processable format.  This involves transforming encrypted or compressed information into its original form.  Decoding can apply to various contexts, such as cryptography, data compression, and signal processing.|例句：The team worked tirelessly on decoding the encrypted message.  团队夜以继日地努力破译加密信息。

The decoding of the ancient hieroglyphs revealed fascinating insights into Egyptian civilization. 古代象形文字的破译揭示了关于埃及文明的迷人见解。|近义词：decryption, decipherment |反义词：encoding, encryption |名词
