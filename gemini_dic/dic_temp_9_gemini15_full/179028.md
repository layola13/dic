redelivering: /ˌriːdɪˈlɪvərɪŋ/ | v. |重新递送，再次发送 |The act of delivering something again.  This could refer to physical items, messages, or information. It implies a previous delivery that was unsuccessful, incomplete, or required a second attempt. |例句：The package is being redelivered tomorrow. =包裹明天将被重新递送。

The courier is redelivering the documents after the initial attempt failed. =快递员在第一次尝试失败后正在重新递送文件。


近义词：resending, retransmitting, redistributing | 反义词：withdrawing, recalling
