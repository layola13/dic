determiner:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnər/ | n. |限定词 |A word that introduces a noun phrase, specifying a noun or noun phrase (such as a, an, the, this, that, these, those, my, your, his, her, its, our, their, some, any, no, each, every, either, neither, much, many, few, little, several, enough).  Determiners modify nouns by limiting or specifying their meaning. They show whether the noun is specific or general, singular or plural, and so on. |例句：The dog barked loudly.  那条狗大声吠叫。  Some students are absent today.  一些学生今天缺席。|近义词：modifier, specifier |反义词：N/A
