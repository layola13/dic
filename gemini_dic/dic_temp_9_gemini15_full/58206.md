drudges:/drʌdʒ/| n.|苦工；苦差事；劳碌的人|A drudge is a person who does dull, uninteresting work, often of a menial nature.  It can also refer to the work itself.|例句：He's been a drudge in the factory for twenty years. 他在工厂里做了二十年的苦工。|例句：Cleaning the stables was a real drudge. 清洁马厩是一件苦差事。|近义词：toiler, laborer, slave, hack|反义词：leisure, relaxation, pleasure|名词
