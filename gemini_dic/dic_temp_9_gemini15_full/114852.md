mammillary:/ˈmæmɪləri/| adj.| 乳头的；乳头状的 | Relating to or resembling a nipple or papilla. The term is often used in anatomy to describe structures that are small, rounded, and nipple-like.  | Example: The mammillary bodies are small, rounded structures located at the base of the brain.  乳头体是位于大脑底部的小而圆的结构。| 近义词：papillary, nipple-shaped | 反义词：无  | 词性：形容词
