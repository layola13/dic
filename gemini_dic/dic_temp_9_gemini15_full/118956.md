meteoromancy:/ˌmiːtiərˈɒmənsi/ | n. |气象占卜|The practice of divination by means of observing meteorological phenomena, such as the weather.  It is an ancient form of divination that sought to predict the future based on atmospheric conditions.|例句：Ancient civilizations practiced meteoromancy to predict harvests and military victories. 古代文明曾利用气象占卜来预测收成和军事胜利。  The farmer relied on meteoromancy to determine the best time to plant his crops.  这位农民依靠气象占卜来决定播种的最佳时间。|近义词：divination, weather forecasting (in a modern context)|反义词：scientific weather prediction|名词
