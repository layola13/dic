The word "ignore" does not exist in standard English.  There is, however, the word "ignore".  I will provide the analysis for "ignore".


ignore: /ɪɡˈnɔːr/ | vt. | 忽视；不理；不顾 | To refuse to take notice of or acknowledge; disregard. | 例句：He ignored my warnings. 他无视我的警告。|  例句：She tried to ignore the pain. 她试图忽视疼痛。| 近义词：disregard, neglect, overlook, disregard | 反义词：heed, attend to, consider


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They ignore my advice. = 他们忽视我的建议。
He ignores the traffic rules. = 他无视交通规则。


一般过去时:
I ignored his phone call. = 我没接他的电话。
She ignored the problem. = 她忽视了这个问题。


现在完成时:
We have ignored the signs. = 我们一直忽视这些迹象。
He has ignored his responsibilities. = 他一直不履行他的职责。


过去完成时:
They had ignored the danger signs. = 他们之前忽视了危险信号。
She had ignored his previous warnings. = 她之前忽视了他的警告。


将来时:
I will ignore his comments. = 我将会忽略他的评论。
They will ignore the evidence. = 他们将会忽视证据。


虚拟语气:
If I ignored the problem, it would get worse. = 如果我忽视这个问题，情况会变得更糟。
I wish I hadn't ignored her advice. = 我真希望我没有忽视她的建议。


被动语态:
My advice was ignored. = 我的建议被忽视了。
The problem was ignored for too long. = 这个问题被忽视了太久。


疑问句:
Why did you ignore me? = 你为什么不理我？
Are they ignoring the facts? = 他们是否在忽视事实？


否定句:
I didn't ignore his plea. = 我没有忽视他的恳求。
She didn't ignore the evidence. = 她没有忽视证据。



PHRASE->

词源:
The word "ignore" comes from the Old French word "ignorer," meaning "to be unaware of" or "not to know."  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "ignorare,"  composed of "in-" (not) + "ignorare" (to know).
