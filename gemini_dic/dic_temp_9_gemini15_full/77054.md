garnering:/ˈɡɑːrnərɪŋ/| v.|收集，积累，获得|The act of gathering and storing something, especially grain or other agricultural produce.  It can also refer to gathering or accumulating something more generally, such as support, information, or praise.|例句：The farmer spent the autumn garnering his harvest.  农民花了整个秋天收割庄稼。

例句：Her speech garnered widespread support for her campaign.  她的演讲为她的竞选赢得了广泛的支持。


近义词：collecting, accumulating, gathering, assembling, amassing|反义词：dispersing, scattering, wasting, squandering|动词
