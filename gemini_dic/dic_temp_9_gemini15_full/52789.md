dilettanti:/ˌdɪləˈtæntiː/| n. |业余爱好者；浅尝辄止者 |A dilettante is someone who cultivates an area of interest, such as the arts, without real commitment or knowledge. They dabble in something superficially rather than engaging deeply.  |He's a dilettante in art, buying expensive paintings but showing little understanding of them. 他是个艺术方面的业余爱好者，买昂贵的画却不懂欣赏。|amateur, dabbler |expert, professional |名词
