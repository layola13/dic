fitted:/ˈfɪtɪd/ | adj. v. | 合身的；安装的；适合的 |  Fitted describes something that is adjusted or made to fit perfectly. As a verb, it means to install or adjust something to fit. | 例句：Her dress was perfectly fitted. 她的裙子非常合身。 The mechanic fitted new tires to the car. 技工给汽车装上了新轮胎。 | 近义词：adjusted, tailored, installed, suited | 反义词：loose, ill-fitting, unsuitable
