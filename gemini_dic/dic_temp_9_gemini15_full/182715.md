repaints:/ˌriːˈpeɪnts/| v.|重新粉刷，重新绘画|To paint again or anew.  The act of painting something again.|例句：He repainted the kitchen walls a cheerful yellow. 他把厨房的墙壁重新粉刷成了明快的黄色。
例句：The artist repainted the portrait to correct a flaw.艺术家重新绘制了肖像以纠正一个瑕疵。|近义词：repaint, redo, refresh|反义词：destroy, ruin

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repaints his house every five years. = 他每五年重新粉刷一次房子。
She repaints old furniture to give it a new look. = 她重新粉刷旧家具以赋予其新的外观。

一般过去时:
They repainted the car after the accident. = 事故后他们重新喷漆了汽车。
She repainted the mural last week. = 她上周重新粉刷了壁画。

现在完成时:
He has repainted the entire building. = 他已经重新粉刷了整栋建筑。
They have repainted the fence three times this year. = 他们今年已经三次重新粉刷了栅栏。

过去完成时:
She had repainted the room before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经重新粉刷了房间。
They had repainted the boat before the sailing competition. = 帆船比赛前，他们已经重新粉刷了船。

将来时:
We will repaint the kitchen next month. = 我们下个月将重新粉刷厨房。
He will repaint the damaged section of the wall. = 他将重新粉刷墙壁受损的部分。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would repaint the entire house. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新粉刷整栋房子。
I wish I had repainted the bathroom sooner. = 我真希望我早点重新粉刷浴室。

被动语态:
The house was repainted last year. = 这所房子去年重新粉刷了。
The mural is being repainted by a renowned artist. = 壁画正在由一位著名的艺术家重新绘制。

疑问句:
Has he repainted the shed yet? = 他已经重新粉刷了棚屋了吗？
Will they repaint the doors this weekend? = 他们这个周末会重新粉刷门吗？

否定句:
They didn't repaint the fence. = 他们没有重新粉刷栅栏。
She hasn't repainted the living room yet. = 她还没有重新粉刷客厅。
