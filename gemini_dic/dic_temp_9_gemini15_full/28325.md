carafes: /kəˈræfs/ | n. | 酒瓶；水瓶 | A carafe is a glass container, usually with a narrow neck and a wide body, used for serving beverages, especially wine or water.  | Example: The waiter poured the wine from the carafe into our glasses.  侍者将酒从酒瓶里倒进我们的酒杯。 | 近义词：decanter, bottle, jug | 反义词：None specifically.
