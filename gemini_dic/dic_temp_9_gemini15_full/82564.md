guaranteer:/ˌɡærənˈtiːr/|/ɡærənˈtiːr/| n., vt.|保证人；担保人；保证；担保；保证给予|To promise or undertake to do something; to secure something against loss or damage.|例句：The shop guarantees all its products for a year. 这家商店保证所有产品一年保修。|例句：He guaranteed me that the car was in perfect condition. 他向我保证这辆车状况良好。|近义词：assure, ensure, warrant|反义词：disavow, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company guarantees customer satisfaction. = 公司保证客户满意。
He guarantees the quality of his work. = 他保证他的工作质量。


一般过去时:
They guaranteed the loan. = 他们担保了贷款。
She guaranteed her friend's honesty. = 她保证她朋友的诚实。


现在完成时:
The bank has guaranteed the investment. = 银行已经担保了这项投资。
We have guaranteed the safety of the children. = 我们已经保证了孩子们的安全。


过去完成时:
He had guaranteed the payment before the project started. = 项目开始前他已经保证付款了。
They had guaranteed the success of the plan. = 他们已经保证了计划的成功。


将来时:
We will guarantee the delivery by next week. = 我们将在下周保证送货。
The manufacturer will guarantee the product for five years. = 制造商将对产品保修五年。


虚拟语气:
If they guaranteed the loan, we would invest. = 如果他们担保贷款，我们就投资。
I wish the shop guaranteed the repair. = 我希望商店保证维修。


被动语态:
The product is guaranteed for one year. = 产品保修一年。

疑问句:

否定句:

He didn't guarantee the success of the project. = 他没有保证项目的成功。
