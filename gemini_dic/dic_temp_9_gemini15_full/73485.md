forerider:/ˈfɔːraɪdər/|/ˈfɔːrˌraɪdər/| n. |先驱；先锋；先例| A person or thing that precedes others, especially in exploring or developing something new.  Foreriders often face challenges and risks in order to pave the way for those who follow. |Example:  She was a forerider in the field of artificial intelligence, developing groundbreaking algorithms years ahead of her peers. 她是在人工智能领域的一位先驱，比她的同龄人早几年就开发出了突破性的算法。


Example: The Wright brothers were foreriders in aviation, paving the way for the modern air travel industry.  莱特兄弟是航空业的先驱，为现代航空业铺平了道路。|近义词：pioneer, precursor, vanguard, trailblazer |反义词：follower, imitator
