sanitizing: /ˈsænɪtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 消毒；净化 | To make something clean and free from bacteria or other germs.  | Example:  We need to sanitize the kitchen surfaces before we start cooking. 我们需要在开始做饭之前对厨房表面进行消毒。  | 近义词：sterilize, disinfect, clean | 反义词：contaminate, pollute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She sanitizes the cutting board after each use. = 她每次使用后都会对案板进行消毒。
The restaurant sanitizes all its utensils regularly. = 这家餐馆定期对所有餐具进行消毒。

一般过去时:
He sanitized his hands before performing surgery. = 他在进行手术之前对双手进行了消毒。
They sanitized the entire room after the outbreak. = 爆发后，他们对整个房间进行了消毒。

现在完成时:
She has sanitized the bathroom thoroughly. = 她已经彻底清洁了浴室。
They have sanitized all the equipment for the experiment. = 他们已经为实验对所有设备进行了消毒。

过去完成时:
The nurse had sanitized the instruments before the operation began. = 护士在手术开始前已经对器械进行了消毒。
The chef had sanitized the countertops before preparing the food. = 厨师在准备食物之前已经对台面进行了消毒。

将来时:
We will sanitize the pool before opening it to the public. = 我们将在向公众开放之前对游泳池进行消毒。
The staff will sanitize the entire building tonight. = 工作人员今晚将对整栋建筑物进行消毒。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would sanitize the wound immediately. = 如果我是你，我会立即对伤口进行消毒。
It's crucial that we sanitize the equipment before use. = 在使用之前对设备进行消毒至关重要。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
