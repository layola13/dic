roux:/ruː/| n.|浓稠酱汁|A roux is a cooked mixture of fat (usually butter) and flour used as a base for sauces, soups, and stews.  It's cooked until it reaches the desired level of color and flavor, which affects the final taste and texture of the dish. |Example:  She started the sauce by making a light brown roux. 她先做了一个浅棕色的 roux 来做酱汁。|近义词：base, foundation|反义词：N/A
