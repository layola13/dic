precovered:/ˌpriːˈkʌvərd/| adj.| 预先覆盖的；预先恢复的 |  Precovered describes something that has been covered or restored beforehand. It implies a proactive measure taken in anticipation of something else.  | Example: The furniture was precovered to protect it during the move.  家具事先被覆盖起来以保护它在搬运过程中不受损坏。|近义词：pre-wrapped, pre-protected | 反义词：uncovered, damaged
