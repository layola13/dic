pre-arrest:/ˌpriː əˈrɛst/| adj. |逮捕前的；拘捕前的|  Relating to the period of time before someone is arrested.  It often refers to the investigation and evidence gathering that occurs before an arrest is made.|例句：Pre-arrest investigations are crucial for building a strong case. 逮捕前的调查对于建立一个强有力的案情至关重要。
The police conducted extensive pre-arrest surveillance.警方进行了广泛的逮捕前监视。|近义词：antecedent, prior to arrest |反义词：post-arrest
