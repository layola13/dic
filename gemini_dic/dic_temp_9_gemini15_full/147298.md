overmodifications:/ˌoʊvərˌmɑːdɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |过度修改；过度修饰 | Excessive alterations or embellishments, often resulting in a negative outcome.  It suggests that something has been changed or decorated too much, to the point of being detrimental.  This term is often used in the context of genetics, software, or writing. |

例句：

The overmodifications to the original design resulted in a less functional product.  过度修改原始设计导致产品功能降低。

The excessive overmodifications to the genetic code led to unforeseen consequences.  对基因代码的过度修改导致了不可预见的后果。


近义词：over-editing, over-embellishment, excessive alteration, over-processing


反义词：simplification, streamlining, minimalism


词性：名词
