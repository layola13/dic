cereals:/ˈsɪəriəlz/ | n. |谷物；谷类食物；早餐麦片 | Grains, especially the edible seeds of cultivated grasses, such as wheat, oats, barley, rye, etc.  Cereal also commonly refers to breakfast foods made from these grains. | 例句：We had cereals for breakfast. 我们早餐吃了谷物。

例句：The farmer grows various cereals on his land.  这位农民在他的土地上种植各种谷物。


近义词：grains, grains, corn (in some contexts) | 反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be meats, vegetables, or fruits depending on the context. | 词性：名词

词源:
The word "cereal" ultimately derives from  "Ceres," the Roman goddess of agriculture.  The term initially referred to grains in general, and only later became associated specifically with breakfast foods.
