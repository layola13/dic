siaga:/ˈsiɑːɡə/| n.| 犀牛；（印尼语）警戒状态 |  A large rhinoceros, particularly the Javan rhinoceros (Rhinoceros sondaicus).  In Indonesian, it also refers to a state of alert or readiness, often used in military contexts. | 例句：The Javan siaga is critically endangered. 爪哇犀牛极度濒危。  |The army was put on siaga.  军队进入戒备状态。| 近义词：rhinoceros, alert, readiness | 反义词：calm, peace, complacency |  名词
