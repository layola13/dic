contemporariness:/ˌkɒntɛmpəˈrerɪnəs/|/ˌkɑːntɛmpəˈrerɪnəs/| n. |当代性；同时代；同时期性 |The state or quality of being contemporary; the characteristic features of the present time.  It often refers to the feeling or atmosphere of the present, reflecting current trends, ideas, and styles. |例句：The museum's exhibition showcases the contemporariness of modern art.  博物馆的展览展示了现代艺术的当代性。

The novel's exploration of social media highlights its contemporariness. 这部小说对社交媒体的探讨突出了它的当代性。|近义词：modernity, currentness, timeliness |反义词：antiquity, obsolescence, outdatedness |名词
