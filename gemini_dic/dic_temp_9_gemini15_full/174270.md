questionably:/ˈkwɛʃənəbli/ | adv. | 值得怀疑地；可能地 |  Doubtfully; possibly.  It suggests uncertainty or doubt about the truth or validity of something. | 例句：He was questionably involved in the scandal. 他可能卷入了丑闻。 | 例句：The data is questionably accurate. 数据的准确性值得怀疑。| 近义词：doubtfully, uncertainly, arguably, perhaps | 反义词：certainly, definitely, undoubtedly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't conjugate like a verb.  It doesn't have tenses.

一般现在时:
He acts questionably sometimes. = 他有时行为可疑。
She speaks questionably about her past. = 她对自己的过去说法含糊其辞。

一般过去时:
He behaved questionably during the investigation. = 他在调查期间行为可疑。
She answered questionably to the police. = 她对警方的回答模棱两可。

现在完成时:
They have acted questionably in the past. = 他们过去的行为值得怀疑。
She has questionably handled the situation. = 她对局势的处理值得怀疑。

过去完成时:
He had questionably planned his escape. = 他对逃跑计划的安排值得怀疑。
They had questionably presented their evidence. = 他们提供的证据值得怀疑。

将来时:
He will questionably participate in the race. = 他是否参加比赛还很难说。
She will questionably complete the project on time. = 她能否按时完成项目还很难说。

虚拟语气:
If he acted questionably, he would be punished. = 如果他行为可疑，他将会受到惩罚。
If she had answered questionably, the outcome could have been different. = 如果她的回答模棱两可，结果可能会不同。

被动语态:
His actions were questionably interpreted. = 他的行为被解读得值得怀疑。
Her motives were questionably understood. = 她的动机理解起来值得怀疑。


PHRASE->
There are not many common phrases specifically with "questionably."  Its usage is typically standalone.  However, it can modify other words or phrases:

questionably true = 可能是真的
questionably ethical = 道德上值得怀疑
questionably legal = 法律上值得怀疑
questionably safe = 安全性值得怀疑
questionably effective = 效果值得怀疑
