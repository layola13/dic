nonmythologically:/ˌnɒnˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. |非神话般地，非神话地 |  Not in a mythological or legendary manner; in a literal or factual way.  The account of the event was presented nonmythologically, focusing on verifiable evidence. | 例句：The historian presented the events nonmythologically, avoiding fanciful interpretations. = 历史学家以非神话的方式呈现了这些事件，避免了奇特的解释。


The discovery was explained nonmythologically, with scientific evidence. =  这一发现是用科学证据非神话般地解释的。


近义词：factually, realistically, literally, prosaically


反义词：mythologically, legendarily, fantastically

词性：副词
