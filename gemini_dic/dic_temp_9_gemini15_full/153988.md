perfidious:/pərˈfɪdiəs/| adj.|背信弃义的；不忠实的；奸诈的 |Treacherous; faithless; deceitful.  It implies a deliberate betrayal of trust or confidence, often involving a violation of an oath or promise.  The term often suggests a more calculated and premeditated act of treachery than simply being unreliable. |例句：The perfidious general betrayed his country to the enemy.  背信弃义的将军把自己的国家出卖给了敌人。

例句：His perfidious actions shattered the trust his colleagues had placed in him. 他背信弃义的行为粉碎了他同事对他的信任。


近义词：treacherous, traitorous, deceitful, faithless, disloyal |反义词：loyal, faithful, trustworthy, honest
