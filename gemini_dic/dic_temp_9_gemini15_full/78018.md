gentling:/ˈdʒɛntliŋ/| n. |轻柔地对待；温柔地抚摸|The act of making something gentle or milder;  the process of becoming gentle or milder.  It often refers to the gentle handling or calming of an animal or person.|例句：The gentling of the wild horse took months of patience and care. 驯服这匹野马花了几个月的时间和耐心。The gentling process helped the child overcome their fear.  温柔的引导帮助孩子克服了恐惧。|近义词：soothing, calming, softening, taming|反义词：roughening, harshing, agitating
