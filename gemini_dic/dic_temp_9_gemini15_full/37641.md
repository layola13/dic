commutator:/ˈkɒmjʊteɪtə(r)/, /ˈkɑːmjuˌteɪtər/| n.|换向器；[电] 整流子；交换器|A commutator is a rotating electrical switch in a DC motor or generator that reverses the current direction in the armature coils.  This switching action converts the alternating current produced in the armature to direct current.  In some contexts, it can also refer to a device or system that performs a switching or exchanging function.|The commutator in the electric motor sparked and failed.|近义词：switch, rotary switch|反义词:None.
