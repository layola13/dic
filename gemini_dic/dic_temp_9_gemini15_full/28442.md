carboys:/ˈkɑːrbɔɪz/ | n. |大玻璃瓶；大瓶子 | A carboy is a large bottle, typically made of glass, used for storing and transporting liquids.  Carboys are often encased in a protective outer container, such as a wicker basket or wooden crate, to prevent breakage. |  The laboratory stored the chemicals in several carboys. 实验室将化学品储存在几个大玻璃瓶中。 We transported the wine in a sturdy carboy. 我们用一个结实的玻璃瓶运输葡萄酒。|  large bottle, demijohn |  small container, vial
