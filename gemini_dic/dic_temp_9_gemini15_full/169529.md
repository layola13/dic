propels:/prəˈpɛlz/ | vt. | 推进；驱动；激励 | To drive or push something forward.  |例句：The engine propels the car forward.  发动机推动汽车前进。

例句：His ambition propelled him to success. 他的雄心壮志驱使他走向成功。


近义词：drive, push, propel, impel, urge

反义词：hinder, impede, restrain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind propels the sailboat across the lake. = 风推动帆船穿过湖面。
The motor propels the small boat. = 马达推动小船。


一般过去时:
The rocket propelled the satellite into orbit. = 火箭把卫星送入轨道。
The strong current propelled the raft downstream. = 强劲的洋流把木筏冲向下游。


现在完成时:
The discovery has propelled her to international fame. = 这一发现使她获得了国际声誉。
His hard work has propelled him to the top of his field. = 他努力的工作使他成为该领域的佼佼者。


过去完成时:
The accumulated pressure had propelled the dam to burst. = 累积的压力导致大坝决堤。
The years of training had propelled her to victory. = 多年的训练使她获得了胜利。


将来时:
The new technology will propel the company to new heights. = 新技术将推动公司发展到新的高度。
The new policy will propel economic growth. = 新政策将推动经济增长。


虚拟语气:
If the wind propelled the ship, we would have arrived earlier. = 如果风推动船只，我们会早些到达。
If the engine propelled the train effectively, the journey would be much faster. = 如果火车引擎有效地推进，旅程将会快得多。


被动语态:
The boat was propelled by the strong wind. = 小船被强风推动。
The satellite was propelled into space by a powerful rocket. = 卫星被一枚强大的火箭送入太空。


疑问句:
What propels this amazing machine? = 什么推动了这台神奇的机器？
What force propels the planets around the sun? = 什么力推动行星绕太阳运行？


否定句:
The small motor couldn't propel the heavy boat. = 小马达无法推动沉重的船只。
The weak current didn't propel the boat forward. = 微弱的洋流没有推动船只前进。


PHRASE->
propel forward 推进
propel oneself 自我驱动
propel growth 推动增长
propel change 推动变革
propel innovation 推动创新

词源:
From Old French *propeler, from Latin propellere "to drive forward," from pro- "forward" + pellere "to drive, push."
