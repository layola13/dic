deductive:/dɪˈdʌktɪv/| adj.|演绎的；推论的|Relating to or using deduction or logical reasoning.  Deductive reasoning starts with a general statement, or hypothesis, and examines the possibilities to reach a specific, logical conclusion.|Example:  The detective used deductive reasoning to solve the crime.  这位侦探用演绎推理破案了。|近义词：inferential, logical, reasoned|反义词：inductive
