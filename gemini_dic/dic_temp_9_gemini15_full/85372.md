harrumphing:/ˈhærʌmfɪŋ/| n.|哼哼声，不满的嘟囔|The act of making a sound expressing disapproval or annoyance.  It often involves a forceful expulsion of air through the nose and mouth.|例句：Her harrumphing suggested she didn't approve of my suggestion.  她的哼哼声表明她不同意我的建议。
例句：The old man responded with a loud harrumphing sound. 老人用一声响亮的哼哼声回应。|近义词：grumbling, snorting, muttering,  |反义词：approving, agreeing
