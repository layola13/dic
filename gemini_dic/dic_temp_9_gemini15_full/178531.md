reconverted:/ˌriːkənˈvɜːtɪd/| adj. | 再转换的；重新改行的 |  Having been converted again; changed back to a previous state or form.  | Example: The old factory building was reconverted into apartments.  旧工厂建筑被改建成了公寓。 The reconverted farmland is now producing crops again. 重新开垦的农田现在又开始生产农作物了。|近义词：reverted, transformed, remodeled |反义词： unconverted, original

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory reconverts waste materials into usable products. = 工厂将废料重新转换为可用产品。
She reconverts her old clothes into new designs. = 她将旧衣服改造成新的设计。


一般过去时:
They reconverted the old church into a community center. = 他们把旧教堂改建成了社区中心。
The government reconverted the military base into a national park. = 政府将军事基地改建成了国家公园。


现在完成时:
The company has reconverted its manufacturing process to be more sustainable. = 公司已经将其制造流程重新转换为更可持续的流程。
They have reconverted the attic into a comfortable guest room. = 他们已将阁楼改造成舒适的客房。


过去完成时:
Before the war, the area had been reconverted to farmland. = 战争前，该地区已被改造成农田。
After the renovations, they had reconverted the space into a modern office. = 装修后，他们已将空间改造成现代化的办公室。


将来时:
They will reconvert the old warehouse into a trendy restaurant. = 他们将把旧仓库改造成一家时尚的餐厅。
The city plans to reconvert the abandoned railway line into a bike path. = 该城市计划将废弃的铁路改造成自行车道。


虚拟语气:
If they had reconverted the building sooner, they could have avoided many problems. = 如果他们早点改造那座建筑，就能避免很多问题。
I wish they had reconverted the outdated system. = 我希望他们已经更新了过时的系统。


被动语态:

疑问句:
