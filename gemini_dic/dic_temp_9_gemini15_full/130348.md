non-tropical: /ˌnɒnˈtrɒpɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈtrɑːpɪkəl/ | adj. | 非热带的 | Not situated in or characteristic of the tropics.  |例句：The plant is non-tropical and needs a cool climate to thrive. 这植物是非热带植物，需要凉爽的气候才能茁壮成长。
例句：Many non-tropical regions experience harsh winters. 许多非热带地区经历严酷的冬季。|近义词：extratroprical, temperate |反义词：tropical
