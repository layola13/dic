词源:  The word is a combination of "non-" (meaning "not") and "obstructive" (from "obstruct," meaning "to block or impede").  "Obstruct" itself comes from the Latin "obstruere," which combines "ob-" (meaning "against" or "in the way of") and "struere" (meaning "to build" or "to pile up").
