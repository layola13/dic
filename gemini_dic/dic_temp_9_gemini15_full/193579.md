self-appointed:/ˌsɛlf əˈpɔɪntɪd/ | adj. | 自封的，自行任命的 |  Describes someone who has given themselves a position or title without any official authorization.  This implies a lack of legitimacy or authority. | 例句：He was a self-appointed leader of the protest movement. 他是这场抗议运动的自封领袖。


例句：The self-appointed expert offered unsolicited advice.  这位自封的专家提供了不受欢迎的建议。

近义词：unofficial, unauthorized, self-proclaimed, presumptuous

反义词：official, appointed, authorized, legitimate


词性:形容词
