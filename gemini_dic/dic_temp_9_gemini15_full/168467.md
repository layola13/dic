probationary:/prəˈbeɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 试用的；缓刑期的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a probationary period.  It describes a period of time during which someone's suitability for a job or position is assessed before they are given permanent status.  It can also refer to a period of probation imposed as a sentence by a court. | 例句：She's currently on a probationary period at her new job. = 她目前正在新工作中进行试用期。


He was sentenced to three years' probation. = 他被判三年缓刑。


近义词：trial, temporary, provisional | 反义词：permanent, confirmed
