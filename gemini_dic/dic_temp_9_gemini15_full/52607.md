digesters:/daɪˈdʒɛstərz/| n.|消化器；消化者|A digester is a device or system used for digestion, often in an industrial or commercial context.  It can also refer to an organism that digests something.|例句：The factory uses large digesters to break down waste materials. 工厂使用大型消化器来分解废料。
例句：The soil acts as a natural digester, breaking down organic matter. 土壤充当天然消化器，分解有机物。|近义词：reactor,  processor|反义词：none
