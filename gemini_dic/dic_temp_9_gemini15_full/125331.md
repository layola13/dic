multilateral:/ˌmʌltiˈlætərəl/ | adj. | 多边的，多方面的 | Relating to or involving more than two parties or countries.  |例句：The treaty is a multilateral agreement signed by five nations. 这项条约是由五个国家签署的多边协议。

例句：Multilateral cooperation is essential to address global challenges. 多边合作对于应对全球挑战至关重要。


近义词：multinational, many-sided, polycentric

反义词：bilateral, unilateral


词性:形容词
