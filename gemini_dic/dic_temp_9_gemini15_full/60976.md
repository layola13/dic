elicitate:/ɪˈlɪsɪteɪt/ | vt. |引出，诱出，探出 | To evoke or draw out (a response, answer, or fact) from someone.  | The interviewer tried to elicitate information about the suspect's whereabouts.  采访者试图诱导嫌疑人的下落信息。 |  evoke, extract, draw out | suppress, conceal | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher elicits answers from the students. =老师向学生们引出答案。
The survey elicits opinions on the new policy. = 调查引出了对新政策的意见。


一般过去时:
The detective elicited a confession from the suspect. = 侦探诱使嫌疑人认罪。
The psychologist elicited childhood memories from the patient. = 心理学家从病人那里引出了童年记忆。


现在完成时:
The researcher has elicited valuable data from the experiment. = 研究人员已经从实验中得出了有价值的数据。
The interview has elicited strong reactions from the public. =  采访引发了公众的强烈反响。


过去完成时:
The lawyer had elicited all the necessary evidence before the trial. = 律师在审判前已经引出了所有必要的证据。
She had elicited a promise from him before she left. = 她离开前已从他那里得到了承诺。


将来时:
The experiment will elicit more data in the coming weeks. =  实验将在接下来的几周内得出更多数据。
We hope the new marketing campaign will elicit a positive response from customers. = 我们希望新的营销活动能引起客户的积极回应。


虚拟语气:
If I could elicit a response from him, I would. = 如果我能引出他的回应，我会的。
I wish I could elicit more information from the witness. = 我希望我能从证人那里获得更多信息。


被动语态:
Information was elicited from various sources. = 信息是从各种来源获取的。
A confession was elicited under duress. = 认罪是在胁迫下获得的。


疑问句:
How can we elicit cooperation from them? = 我们如何才能获得他们的合作？
What methods will be used to elicit responses from the participants? = 将使用什么方法来引出参与者的回应？


否定句:
The police failed to elicit any useful information from the witness. = 警察未能从证人那里获得任何有用的信息。
He couldn't elicit a single word from her. = 他无法从她那里得到一个字。


PHRASE->
elicit a response = 引出回应
elicit information = 诱导信息
elicit answers = 引出答案
elicit feedback = 获取反馈
elicit support = 获得支持
