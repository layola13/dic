shoveling: /ˈʃʌvəlɪŋ/ | v. | 铲，铲运 | The act of using a shovel to move something.  | 例句：He spent the afternoon shoveling snow. 他花了一下午铲雪。  | 例句：Shoveling the driveway was hard work. 铲车道很费力。 | 近义词：scooping, digging | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shovels snow every winter. = 他每年冬天都铲雪。
She shovels the dirt into the wheelbarrow. = 她把土铲进独轮车里。

一般过去时:
I shoveled the walk yesterday. = 我昨天铲了人行道。
They shoveled the grain into the sacks. = 他们把谷物铲进麻袋里。

现在完成时:
We have shoveled all the snow. = 我们已经把所有的雪都铲完了。
She has shoveled the garden for hours. = 她已经为花园铲土好几个小时了。

过去完成时:
He had shoveled the path before I arrived. = 我到达之前，他已经铲好了路。
They had shoveled all the snow before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经铲完了所有的雪。

将来时:
I will shovel the driveway tomorrow. = 我明天要铲车道。
They will shovel the sand onto the truck. = 他们要把沙子铲到卡车上。

虚拟语气:
If I had a shovel, I would shovel the snow. = 如果我有一把铲子，我就铲雪。
I wish I had shoveled the snow earlier. = 我真希望我早点铲雪。

被动语态:
The snow was shoveled by the workers. = 雪是被工人铲走的。
The sand is being shoveled into the hole. = 沙子正在被铲进洞里。

疑问句:
Are you shoveling the snow? = 你在铲雪吗？
Have they shoveled the driveway yet? = 他们铲车道了吗？

否定句:
He didn't shovel the snow. = 他没有铲雪。
They haven't shoveled the path yet. = 他们还没铲路。
