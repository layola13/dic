noncorrespondingly: /ˌnɒnkɒrəˈspɒndɪŋli/ | adv. | 不对应地；不相称地 |  This adverb describes a situation where there's a lack of correspondence or agreement between two or more things. It suggests a mismatch, inconsistency, or disparity.  | Example: The experimental results were noncorrespondingly low compared to the theoretical predictions.  实验结果与理论预测不相符，出奇的低。|近义词:inconsistently, disproportionately, dissimilarly|反义词:correspondingly, consistently, proportionally


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and thus doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  The usage is straightforward: it modifies a verb, adjective, or another adverb to indicate a lack of correspondence.


Example Sentences:

The two datasets were analyzed, and their results were noncorrespondingly different.  两个数据集经过分析，结果大相径庭。

His claims were noncorrespondingly grand considering the evidence presented. 考虑到提供的证据，他的说法夸大其词。


PHRASE->

There are no common phrases specifically using "noncorrespondingly".  Its use is generally within a sentence structure to describe a specific relationship between elements.
