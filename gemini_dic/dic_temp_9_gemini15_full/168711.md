It can also refer to someone who acts as an agent or representative for another.|例句：The proctor carefully watched the students during the exam. 监考员在考试期间仔细观察学生。|例句：He proctored the exam to ensure fairness. 他监考考试以确保公平。|近义词：supervisor, invigilator, monitor, representative|反义词：examinee, student (in the context of an exam)|词性：n. v.
