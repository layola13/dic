shoar:/ʃɔːr/| n.|海岸，滨；支柱|The shore is where the land meets the sea. 海岸是陆地与海洋交汇的地方。|近义词：coast, beach, strand|反义词：sea, ocean


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shore is beautiful today. = 今天的海岸很漂亮。
We often walk along the shore. = 我们经常沿着海岸散步。

一般过去时:
The ship ran aground on the shore. = 船搁浅在岸边。
We sat on the shore and watched the sunset. = 我们坐在岸边看日落。

现在完成时:
The storm has eroded the shore. = 暴风雨侵蚀了海岸。
They have built a seawall to protect the shore. = 他们建造了海堤来保护海岸。

过去完成时:
The tide had gone out before we reached the shore. = 我们到达海岸之前，潮水已经退了。
The storm had damaged the shore before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，暴风雨已经破坏了海岸。

将来时:
We will visit the shore tomorrow. = 我们明天将去海边。
The rising sea level will threaten the shore. = 海平面上升将威胁到海岸。

虚拟语气:
If I lived near the shore, I would go swimming every day. = 如果我住在海边，我每天都会去游泳。
I wish I were on the shore right now. = 我真希望我现在在海边。

被动语态:
The shore is often crowded in summer. = 夏天海边经常很拥挤。
The shore was damaged by the hurricane. = 海岸被飓风破坏了。

疑问句:
Is the shore safe for swimming? = 海边适合游泳吗？
Where is the nearest shore? = 最近的海岸在哪里？

否定句:
The shore was not suitable for landing. = 海滩不适合登陆。
We didn't go to the shore yesterday. = 我们昨天没有去海边。
