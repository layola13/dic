descend: /dɪˈsɛnd/ | v. | 下降；遗传；下落；起源于 | To move or fall from a higher to a lower level; to come down; to be inherited; to be derived from. |例句：The plane descended smoothly.  飞机平稳下降。

The family descended from a long line of kings. 这个家族起源于一个长长的国王世系。


近义词：go down, decline, drop, fall, derive, inherit | 反义词：ascend, climb, rise, mount


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds descend from the sky.  鸟儿们从空中下降。
He descends from a wealthy family. 他出身于一个富裕的家庭。

一般过去时:
The temperature descended rapidly.气温迅速下降。
The soldiers descended the hill.士兵们下山了。

现在完成时:
They have descended from the mountain. 他们已经下山了。
The tradition has descended through generations. 这个传统代代相传。

过去完成时:
The climbers had descended before nightfall. 登山者在夜幕降临前已下山。
The illness had descended upon the entire family.  疾病已经侵袭了整个家庭。

将来时:
The sun will descend below the horizon. 太阳将落到地平线以下。
The helicopter will descend to the rescue site. 直升机将下降到救援现场。

虚拟语气:
If the plane descended too quickly, there could be an accident. 如果飞机下降太快，可能会发生事故。
If he descended from such a noble family, he would be proud. 如果他出身如此高贵的家庭，他会感到自豪。

被动语态:
The city was descended upon by a swarm of locusts.  蝗虫群袭击了这座城市。(被下降)
The throne was descended by the eldest son. 王位由长子继承。(被遗传)

疑问句:
Does the path descend steeply? 这条路下降陡峭吗？
From whom does he descend? 他是哪位后裔？

否定句:
The plane did not descend smoothly. 飞机下降并不平稳。
He does not descend from a royal family. 他并非出身皇室。


PHRASE->
descend upon/on:  突然袭击；降临
descend from:  起源于；出自
descend into:  陷入；堕落
descend to:  屈尊；降低身份
descend the stairs: 下楼
