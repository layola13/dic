outgushes:/ˈaʊtˌɡʌʃɪz/| v.|涌出；奔涌而出；倾泻而出 |To flow or pour out suddenly and abundantly.  |例句：Joy outgushed from her heart as she received the good news.  喜悦从她心中涌出，当她收到好消息时。|例句：Lava outgushed from the volcano, destroying everything in its path.  熔岩从火山中喷涌而出，摧毁了沿途的一切。|近义词：gush, pour, erupt, overflow|反义词：trickle, seep
