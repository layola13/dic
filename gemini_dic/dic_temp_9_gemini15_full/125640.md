mumm:/mʌm/| n. |木乃伊；干尸|A mummy is a dead body that has been preserved, usually by embalming, especially in ancient Egypt.|例句：The museum displayed ancient Egyptian mummies.  博物馆展出了古埃及木乃伊。|例句：The archaeologists unearthed a well-preserved mummy. 考古学家挖掘出一具保存完好的木乃伊。|近义词：corpse, cadaver|反义词：living being
