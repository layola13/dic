gryphite:/ˈɡraɪfaɪt/| n.|灰岩，层状灰岩|A type of sedimentary rock composed primarily of calcite, often appearing in layered or laminated forms.  It's frequently found in marine environments and can contain fossils.|The gryphite deposit near the river provided valuable insights into the area's geological history.  这条河附近的灰岩矿床为了解该地区的地质历史提供了宝贵的资料。|limestone, stratified rock|—|名词
