racked:/rækt/ | v. | 折磨；使痛苦；储存；堆积 | to cause someone great mental or physical suffering; to store or arrange something systematically; to strain or overexert |例句：He was racked with guilt after the accident.  事故后他被罪恶感折磨着。 |近义词：torment, agonize, store, pile | 反义词：soothe, comfort, relieve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He racks his brain trying to solve the problem. = 他绞尽脑汁试图解决这个问题。
She racks up frequent flyer miles on business trips. = 她出差时积累了大量的飞行里程。


一般过去时:
The earthquake racked the city, causing widespread damage. = 地震摧毁了这座城市，造成了广泛的破坏。
They racked the books neatly on the shelves. = 他们整齐地把书放在书架上。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
