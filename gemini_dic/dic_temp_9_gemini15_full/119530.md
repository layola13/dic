microscopic:/ˌmaɪkrəˈskɑːpɪk/| adj.|显微镜的；微观的；极小的|extremely small; only visible with a microscope.  |例句：The bacteria are microscopic and cannot be seen with the naked eye.  细菌是肉眼看不见的微生物。|例句：He examined the sample under a microscopic lens. 他用显微镜观察样本。|近义词：minute, tiny, infinitesimal, submicroscopic|反义词：macroscopic, visible, large
