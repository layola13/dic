come-to-oneself: /ˌkʌm tuː wʌnˈsɛlf/ | v. |苏醒；恢复知觉；恢复常态 | To regain consciousness; to recover one's composure or senses after a shock, faint, or period of unconsciousness.  | 例句：She fainted but soon came to herself. 她晕倒了，但很快就苏醒过来了。  | 例句：After the initial shock, she slowly came to herself and began to assess the situation.  最初的震惊过后，她慢慢地恢复了镇定，开始评估局势。| 近义词：recover consciousness, revive, regain senses | 反义词：faint, lose consciousness | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He comes to himself after a long sleep. = 他睡了一觉后苏醒过来。
She usually comes to herself quickly after fainting. = 她晕倒后通常很快就会恢复知觉。


一般过去时:
The patient came to himself after the surgery. = 病人在手术后苏醒过来。
He came to himself and realized he was in a strange place. = 他恢复意识后，意识到自己在一个陌生的地方。


现在完成时:
She has come to herself and is now talking to the doctor. = 她已经苏醒过来，现在正在和医生说话。
He has finally come to himself after the accident. = 他在事故后终于恢复了知觉。


过去完成时:
By the time the ambulance arrived, he had come to himself. = 救护车到达时，他已经苏醒过来了。
She had come to herself before the police arrived. = 在警察到来之前，她已经恢复了意识。


将来时:
He will come to himself soon after the medication takes effect. = 药物生效后，他很快就会苏醒过来。
I hope she will come to herself before morning. = 我希望她能在早上之前苏醒过来。


虚拟语气:
If he came to himself, he would be able to tell us what happened. = 如果他苏醒过来，他就能告诉我们发生了什么。
I wish he had come to himself sooner. = 我希望他早点苏醒过来。


被动语态: (此短语通常不用于被动语态)


疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't come to himself for hours. = 他几个小时都没苏醒过来。
She hadn't come to herself when the doctor arrived. = 医生到达时，她还没苏醒过来。
