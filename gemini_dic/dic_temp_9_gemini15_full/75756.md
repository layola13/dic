fulminates:/ˈfʊlmɪneɪts/| v.|大声斥责；猛烈抨击；爆炸；(化学物质)剧烈反应|To utter or pronounce with vehement denunciation; to explode or detonate violently; to react violently (as a chemical substance).|例句：The critic fulminated against the film's poor acting. 批评家猛烈抨击这部电影糟糕的演技。

例句：The volcano fulminated, spewing ash and lava. 火山爆发，喷出火山灰和熔岩。

近义词：denounce, condemn, explode, detonate

反义词：praise, commend, approve
