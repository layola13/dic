chronologic:/ˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪk/|/ˌkroʊnəˈlɑːdʒɪk/| adj. |按时间顺序的，年代学的 | Arranged in or relating to chronological order.  Chronological order refers to the arrangement of events in the order in which they occurred in time.  | Example: The museum displays its artifacts in chronological order, starting with the earliest examples and progressing to the most recent.  博物馆按时间顺序陈列文物，从最早的例子开始，一直到最新的例子。|近义词：temporal, sequential |反义词：a-chronological, non-sequential |形容词
