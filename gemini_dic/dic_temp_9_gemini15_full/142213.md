ornithologies:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 鸟类学；鸟类学著作 | The scientific study of birds, including their classification, behavior, physiology, and distribution.  It encompasses various aspects of avian biology, ecology, and conservation. | Example:  The ornithologies of the Amazon basin are particularly rich and diverse.  亚马逊盆地的鸟类学研究特别丰富多样。  |近义词: avian studies, bird studies | 反义词:  (无直接反义词)
