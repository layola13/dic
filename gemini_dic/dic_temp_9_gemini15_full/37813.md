compensated:/ˈkɒmpenseɪtɪd/ /ˈkɑːmpənseɪtɪd/ | adj. | 补偿的；得到补偿的 |  Having received something, typically money, to recompense for loss, injury, or suffering.  |例句：The victims of the flood were compensated for their losses. 洪水受害者得到了损失赔偿。

例句：He was compensated handsomely for his years of service. 他因为多年的服务而得到了丰厚的报酬。


近义词：reimbursed, repaid, indemnified | 反义词：uncompensated, unrewarded | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company compensates its employees well. = 公司给员工很好的薪酬。
They compensate for the lack of experience with hard work. = 他们用努力工作来弥补经验不足。


一般过去时:
The insurance company compensated him for the damage to his car. = 保险公司赔偿了他汽车的损失。
She compensated her friend for the lost book. = 她赔偿了朋友丢失的书。


现在完成时:
The government has compensated the victims of the accident. = 政府已经赔偿了事故受害者。
He has been compensated fairly for his work. = 他因为他的工作得到了公平的报酬。


过去完成时:
They had already compensated the workers before the strike began. = 罢工开始前他们已经赔偿了工人们。
The company had compensated her for her injuries before the lawsuit. = 在诉讼之前，公司已经赔偿了她的伤势。


将来时:
The company will compensate the employees for their overtime. = 公司将赔偿员工的加班费。
We will be compensated for our travel expenses. = 我们将获得差旅费用的补偿。


虚拟语气:
If the company compensated its employees fairly, there would be less turnover. = 如果公司公平地补偿员工，员工流动率就会降低。
I wish they had compensated me for the extra hours I worked. = 我希望他们能补偿我加班的额外时间。


被动语态:
The employees were compensated for their lost wages. = 员工获得了损失工资的补偿。
The damages were fully compensated by the insurance company. = 保险公司全额赔偿了损失。


疑问句:
Were the victims compensated for their suffering? = 受害者是否得到了痛苦的补偿？
Will the company compensate the employees for their travel expenses? = 公司会补偿员工的差旅费吗？


否定句:
The company did not compensate the employees for their overtime. = 公司没有补偿员工的加班费。
He was not compensated for his injuries. = 他的伤势没有得到赔偿。

词源:
来自拉丁语 *compensatus, 过去分词compensare "to compensate," 来自com- "together" + pensare "to weigh,"  字面意思是“一起衡量”，引申为“补偿”。
