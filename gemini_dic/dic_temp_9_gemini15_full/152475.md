payableness: /ˌpeɪəˈbɪlnəs/ | n. | 可支付性，应付性 | The state or quality of being payable; the ability to be paid.  It often refers to a debt or obligation that is due and can be paid. | Example: The payableness of the invoice was questioned due to a discrepancy in the amount.  发票的可支付性由于金额差异而受到质疑。  |近义词：solvency,liquidity|反义词：insolvency,illiquidity
