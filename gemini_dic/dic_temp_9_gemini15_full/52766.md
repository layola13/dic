dilation:/daɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. |扩张；扩大；膨胀 | The widening, stretching, or enlarging of an opening, vessel, or structure.  It often refers to the expansion of a blood vessel or pupil of the eye. |例句：The dilation of the pupils indicated that she was under stress. 瞳孔扩张表明她处于压力之下。 |例句：The doctor observed the dilation of the blood vessels during the examination. 医生在检查过程中观察到血管扩张。 |近义词：expansion, enlargement, widening |反义词：constriction, contraction
