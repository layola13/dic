opulency:/ˈɒpjʊlənsɪ/ /ˈoʊpjələnsi/ | n. |富裕；奢华；繁荣 |Great wealth or luxuriousness.  The opulence of the palace was breathtaking.  |近义词：affluence, luxury, richness, splendor |反义词：poverty, destitution, scarcity


英文解析：Opulency refers to a state of great wealth, abundance, or luxuriousness. It suggests an extravagant display of riches and comfort.  It often describes a lifestyle characterized by lavish spending and possessions.

例句：

The opulence of the royal wedding was evident in the elaborate decorations and the expensive attire of the guests. 皇家婚礼的奢华程度从精心制作的装饰和宾客昂贵的服装中可见一斑。

The hotel's opulence attracted wealthy tourists from all over the world.  酒店的奢华吸引了来自世界各地的富裕游客。


词源：

The word "opulency" comes from the Latin word "opulentus," meaning "wealthy" or "rich."  "Opulentus" is derived from "ops," meaning "wealth" or "power."
