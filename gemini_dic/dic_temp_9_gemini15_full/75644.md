The term carries extremely negative connotations due to its association with Hitler and the Holocaust. | Example: Hitler's regime as Führer of Germany led to World War II and unimaginable atrocities. 希特勒作为德国元首的统治导致了第二次世界大战和难以想象的暴行。|近义词：leader, chief, dictator |反义词：follower, subordinate |名词
