respectably:/rɪˈspektəbli/ | adj. | 可敬地；体面地；相当好地 |  In a way that deserves respect; in a way that is considered proper or socially acceptable; to a reasonably good degree. | 例句：He behaved respectably at the party. 他在派对上的表现很得体。

He earned a respectably high salary. 他挣了一笔相当高的薪水。


近义词：decently, properly, honorably, commendably | 反义词：disrespectfully, improperly, disgracefully | 词性：形容词

词源:
