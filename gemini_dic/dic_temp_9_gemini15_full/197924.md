/ʃɪst/| n. |片岩|A metamorphic rock formed from shale or mudstone by low-grade regional metamorphism.  It is characterized by a layered structure and often contains mica minerals.|Example: The geologist identified the sample as a type of schist.|近义词：slate, phyllite|反义词：granite, gneiss|名词
