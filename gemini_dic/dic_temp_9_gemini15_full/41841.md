corvorant:/ˈkɔːrvɒrənt/| n. |鸬鹚|A large, dark-colored bird with a long neck and a hooked beak, known for its diving skills to catch fish.  They are often seen perched on rocks or pilings near water.|Example:  The cormorant dived into the water and emerged moments later with a fish in its beak.  这只鸬鹚潜入水中，片刻后叼着一条鱼浮出水面。|近义词：shag, darter|反义词：None specifically
