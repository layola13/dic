disencumber:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkʌmbər/| vt.|解除负担；使摆脱；使自由|To free from encumbrance or burden; to relieve of something burdensome.|例句：He disencumbered himself of his heavy coat. 他脱掉了沉重的外套。|例句：The company is trying to disencumber itself from unnecessary debt. 公司正试图摆脱不必要的债务。|近义词：unburden, relieve, liberate|反义词：encumber, burden, weigh down|动词
