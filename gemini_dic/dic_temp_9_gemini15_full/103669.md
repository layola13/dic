jollying:/ˈdʒɒlɪŋ/| v.|使高兴；使兴奋；戏弄；取笑 |To make someone feel happy and cheerful; to playfully tease or banter with someone. |例句：He was jollying his friends along. 他在逗他的朋友开心。|例句：She spent the evening jollying her friends along. 她整个晚上都在逗她的朋友开心。|近义词：cheer up, encourage, tease, kid |反义词：depress, discourage, insult
