metallotherapy:/ˌmɛtælˈoʊθɛrəpi/| n. |金属疗法|The use of metals in the treatment of disease.  This can encompass a range of practices, from applying metallic substances directly to the body to ingesting metallic compounds or utilizing metal-based devices. The efficacy of many metallotherapeutic approaches remains largely unproven and some may even be harmful. |Example:  Some historical accounts describe metallotherapy using silver to treat infections.  一些历史记载描述了使用银进行金属疗法的例子，以治疗感染。|近义词：metal medication, metallic treatment (less precise)|反义词：None directly, but  'placebo therapy' could be considered a contrasting approach if the metal itself is not the active agent.|名词
