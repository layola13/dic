resoled:/riːˈsoʊld/ | vt. |重底(鞋); 修补鞋底 | To put new soles on (shoes or boots).  This verb describes the action of replacing the worn-out soles of footwear with new ones.  |例句：I need to get my hiking boots resoled; the soles are completely worn out. = 我需要把我的登山靴重底；鞋底完全磨损了。

例句：The cobbler expertly resoled my favorite pair of shoes, extending their lifespan. = 鞋匠熟练地为我最喜欢的鞋子重底，延长了它们的寿命。


近义词：repair, mend, resole (itself is a synonym for re-soling) | 反义词：None directly opposite, but  "to ruin" or "to destroy" could be considered antonyms in the context of shoe repair. | 词性: Verb (transitive)


词源:  The word "resoled" is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again") to the past participle of "sole" (meaning "to provide with a sole"). "Sole" itself comes from the Old French word "sole," meaning the bottom part of the foot or shoe.
