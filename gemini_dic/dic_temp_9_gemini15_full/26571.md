cablings:/ˈkeɪblɪŋz/ | n. | 电缆敷设；电缆系统 | The process of installing or laying cables, or the system of cables itself.  It can refer to the physical act of running cables, the design and planning of a cable network, or the network itself. | Example: The cabling in this building is outdated and needs replacing.  这个建筑物的电缆系统已经过时，需要更换。  The cabling project took longer than expected. 电缆敷设工程比预期时间长。|近义词：wiring, cabling system, cable installation | 反义词：disconnection, removal
