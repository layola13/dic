restruct:/ˌriːˈstrʌktʃər/| vt.| 重组；改组；重建 | To reorganize the structure or internal arrangement of something, typically a business or organization, in order to improve efficiency or effectiveness.  |例句：The company restructured its operations to reduce costs. 公司为了降低成本而重组了业务。

例句：The government is planning to restructure the healthcare system. 政府正计划重组医疗体系。


近义词：reorganize, revamp, reform, reorganise (BrE) | 反义词：disorganize, disintegrate


词性：动词
