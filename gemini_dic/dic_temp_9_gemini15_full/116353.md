mastheading:/ˈmæstˌhedɪŋ/| n.| 标题栏；报头；（船的）桅杆标题 |The masthead is the top section of a newspaper, containing the title, publication information, and sometimes a tagline.  It's also used in a figurative sense to refer to the prominent or leading figures in an organization or field.| 例句：The newspaper's masthead proudly displayed its founding date. 这家报纸的报头自豪地展示了它的创刊日期。

The ship's masthead light guided the sailors through the storm. 船的桅杆灯引导水手们度过暴风雨。|近义词：headline, title, heading, banner |反义词：body (of text), content
