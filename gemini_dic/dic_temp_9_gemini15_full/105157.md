kettle:/ˈketl/| n.|水壶|A kettle is a container, typically metallic, with a spout, handle, and lid, used for boiling water.  It is often used to make hot drinks such as tea and coffee.|例句：I put the kettle on to make some tea. 我烧开水泡茶。|例句：The electric kettle boiled the water quickly. 电热水壶很快就把水烧开了。|近义词：boiler, teakettle|反义词:None


USAGE->

The kettle whistles when the water boils. = 水烧开时，水壶会发出汽笛声。
She uses a kettle to make coffee every morning. = 她每天早上用烧水壶煮咖啡。
