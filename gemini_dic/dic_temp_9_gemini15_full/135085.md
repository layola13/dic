nonobjectifications: /ˌnɒnˌɒbdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |非客体化；非物化；不被当作客体对待 | The act or process of not treating someone as an object; the avoidance of objectification.  It emphasizes the inherent value and agency of individuals, respecting their thoughts, feelings, and autonomy.  This concept is particularly relevant in discussions of feminism, ethics, and social justice. |例句：The artist's work promotes nonobjectifications of women, presenting them as complex individuals rather than mere sexual objects.  艺术家作品提倡对女性的非物化，将她们呈现为复杂的个体，而非仅仅是性对象。
