outexecuted:/ˌaʊtɪˈɡzɪkjuːtɪd/| adj.|被超越的，被胜过的|Out-executed describes a situation where someone or something has been surpassed in terms of skill, performance, or execution.  It implies a clear superiority in the execution of a task or plan.|例句：The opposing team completely outexecuted us in the second half, leading to our defeat.  对手在下半场完全胜过我们，导致我们失败。|近义词：outsmarted, outperformed, outmaneuvered, surpassed|反义词：outperformed (in the context of being the one who outperforms), excelled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Our team is outexecuted in every aspect of the game. = 我们队在比赛的各个方面都被超越了。
Their strategy consistently outexecutes ours. = 他们的策略始终胜过我们的策略。


一般过去时:
The project was outexecuted by the rival company. = 这个项目被竞争对手公司超越了。
He was clearly outexecuted by his opponent in the debate. = 在辩论中，他显然被对手超越了。

虚拟语气:
