hemipteran:/ˈhɛmɪptərən/| n. |半翅目昆虫|A hemipteran is an insect belonging to the order Hemiptera, characterized by piercing-sucking mouthparts and often having forewings that are partly hardened and partly membranous.  Hemipterans include true bugs, cicadas, aphids, and leafhoppers. |Example:  The researcher observed several hemipterans feeding on the plant.  研究人员观察到几种半翅目昆虫正在以植物为食。
