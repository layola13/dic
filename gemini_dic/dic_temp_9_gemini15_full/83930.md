half-chanted:/ˈhæfˌtʃæntɪd/| adj. |半吟诵的，含糊不清地吟诵的 |Partially or imperfectly chanted; mumbled or indistinctly recited.  It suggests a chant that is incomplete, unclear, or lacking the full force of a proper chant. |例句：The half-chanted incantation barely reached the ears of the onlookers. 半吟诵的咒语几乎没有传到旁观者耳中。
The priest's half-chanted prayer was lost in the noise of the storm.  牧师含糊不清的祈祷声被暴风雨的噪音淹没了。|近义词：murmured, mumbled, whispered, indistinct |反义词：clear, distinct, loud, resonant
