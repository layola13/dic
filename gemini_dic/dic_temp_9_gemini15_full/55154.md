disseizin: /ˌdɪsˈaɪzɪn/ | n. | 剥夺土地所有权；非法侵占；驱逐 | The wrongful dispossession of a person from his or her land.  It involves the unlawful taking of possession of land and the ousting of the rightful owner. | Example: The disseizin of the farmer led to a lengthy and costly legal battle.  = 农民被非法剥夺土地所有权，导致旷日持久的昂贵诉讼。| 近义词：ousted, dispossession, eviction | 反义词：seisin, possession
