cavernously:/ˈkævərnəsli/| adj.|  像洞穴似的；空旷而回响的；低沉而空洞的 |  Resembling a cavern; large, hollow, and echoing.  It suggests a vastness and emptiness, often with a sense of gloom or mystery. | 例句：The sound echoed cavernously across the vast hall.  声音在巨大的大厅里空旷地回响。

例句：His voice boomed cavernously from the depths of the cave. 他的声音从洞穴深处低沉地回响。


近义词：hollowly, resonantly, deeply, vast


反义词：closely, compactly, tightly, small


词性：形容词
