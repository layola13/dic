go-on-lines:/ɡoʊ ɑːn laɪnz/| phr. v.|上线，开始在线活动| This phrase describes the action of starting to use online services or platforms. It implies a transition from offline to online activity.| 例句：I'll go on lines after dinner. = 晚饭后我会上线。  I need to go online to check my email. = 我需要上线查看我的邮件。|近义词：go online, get online, come online |反义词：go offline, log off, disconnect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They go online every day. = 他们每天都上线。
We go online to research information. = 我们上线来查找信息。


一般过去时:
She went online last night. = 她昨晚上线了。
He went online to buy a book. = 他上线买了一本书。


现在完成时:
I have gone online several times today. = 我今天已经上线好几次了。
They have gone online to collaborate on a project. = 他们已经上线合作一个项目了。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had gone online to share documents. = 会议之前，他们已经上线分享文件了。
He had gone online many times before he found the solution. = 他在找到解决方案之前已经上线很多次了。


将来时:
I will go online later to check my messages. = 我稍后会上线查看我的消息。
We will go online to book our flights. = 我们会上线预订我们的航班。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would go online to watch that video. = 如果我有更多时间，我会上线观看那个视频。
I wish I could go online right now. = 我希望我现在能上线。


被动语态:  *(This phrase is rarely used in passive voice)*


疑问句:
Do you go online often? = 你经常上线吗？
When will they go online? = 他们什么时候上线？


否定句:
I don't go online very often. = 我不经常上线。
He didn't go online yesterday. = 他昨天没有上线。
