dukedom:/ˈdjuːkdəm/ | n. | 公国；公爵领地 | The territory ruled by a duke; the rank or dignity of a duke.  | Example:  The dukedom of Lancaster is a significant part of British history. 兰开斯特公国是英国历史上重要的一部分。  He inherited his father's dukedom and vast estates. 他继承了他父亲的公国和广阔的产业。|近义词：principality, domain | 反义词：kingdom (in terms of size and power), republic
