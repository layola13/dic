glassfuls:/ˈɡlæsˌfʊlz/| n.|满杯的量；一杯一杯的量|The plural of glassful.  It refers to multiple instances of a glass being completely filled.  This word is less common than simply using "glasses" to refer to multiple glasses.|例句：She drank several glassfuls of water after the run. 她跑步后喝了几大杯水。


例句：The recipe called for three glassfuls of milk. 这个食谱需要三杯牛奶。


近义词：glasses (in the context of full glasses)


反义词：None (It's not a word that directly implies an antonym)


词性:名词
