niches:/ˈnɪtʃɪz/ | n. |生态位；合适的职位；专门市场；壁龛 | A niche is a specialized segment of the market for a particular kind of product or service.  It can also refer to a comfortable or suitable position in life or society, or a recess in a wall. | 例句：The company found a niche market for its innovative product. 这家公司为其创新产品找到了一个利基市场。

The artist carved a niche in the stone wall to display her sculpture.艺术家在石墙上雕刻了一个壁龛来展示她的雕塑。


近义词：specialty, recess, alcove,  slot

反义词：mainstream, commonplace, general market
