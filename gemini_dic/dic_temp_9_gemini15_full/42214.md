counsel:/ˈkaʊnsəl/ | n., v. | 劝告；建议；律师；法律顾问 |  advice, guidance, or consultation given by a professional; a lawyer | 近义词：advice, guidance, recommendation | 反义词：misguidance


英文解析：As a noun, "counsel" refers to advice, guidance, or consultation, especially given by a professional such as a lawyer or therapist. As a verb, it means to advise or give guidance to someone.


例句 (Noun):

The doctor offered her some sound counsel on how to manage her stress.  医生给她提供了一些关于如何控制压力的合理建议。

He sought the counsel of his lawyer before making a decision. 他在做决定之前寻求了他律师的建议。


例句 (Verb):

I would counsel you to think carefully before acting. 我劝你三思而后行。

She counseled him to seek professional help. 她劝他寻求专业帮助。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She counsels clients on financial matters. = 她为客户提供财务方面的咨询。
He counsels people dealing with addiction. = 他为有瘾的人提供咨询。


一般过去时:
The therapist counseled her through her grief. = 治疗师帮助她走出悲伤。
My lawyer counseled me to plead not guilty. = 我的律师建议我认罪。


现在完成时:
She has counseled many people facing difficult choices. = 她已经为许多面临艰难抉择的人提供过咨询。
He has counseled countless couples over the years. = 多年来，他为无数对夫妇提供过咨询。


过去完成时:
I had counseled him against investing in that company. = 我曾劝告他不要投资那家公司。
She had counseled him many times before. = 她以前多次劝过他。


将来时:
He will counsel the defendant tomorrow. = 他明天将为被告提供咨询。
She will counsel her son on his future career. = 她将就儿子的未来职业为其提供咨询。


虚拟语气:
If I had counseled him differently, things might have turned out better. = 如果我当时给他不同的建议，结果可能会更好。
I wish I had counseled her to take a break. = 我真希望我当时劝她休息一下。


被动语态:
The client was counseled by the therapist. = 这位客户得到了治疗师的咨询。
The defendant was well-counseled by his lawyer. = 被告得到了他律师很好的咨询。


疑问句:
Did the lawyer counsel you on this matter? = 律师就此事给你提供咨询了吗？
Will she be counseled before the trial? = 在审判之前她会接受咨询吗？


否定句:
He did not counsel his friend against taking the risk. = 他没有劝他的朋友不要冒险。
She didn't counsel him to file a lawsuit. = 她没有劝他提起诉讼。
