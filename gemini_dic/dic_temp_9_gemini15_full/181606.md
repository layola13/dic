relatively:/ˈrɛlətɪvli/| adv.|相对地，比较地|In a relative manner; to a certain extent.|The task was relatively easy. 这项任务相对容易。|近义词：comparatively, comparatively speaking, somewhat|反义词：absolutely, entirely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather is relatively warm today. = 今天天气相对温暖。
This task is relatively simple. = 这项任务相对简单。


一般过去时:
The journey was relatively quick. = 这趟旅程相对很快。
The exam was relatively easy last year. = 去年的考试相对容易。


现在完成时:
The project has been relatively successful. = 这个项目相对成功。
She has felt relatively better since taking the medicine. = 她自从服药后感觉好多了。


过去完成时:
The situation had been relatively stable before the incident. = 事发前情况相对稳定。
They had been relatively happy in their marriage. = 他们结婚后一直比较幸福。


将来时:
The weather will be relatively mild next week. = 下周天气将会相对温和。
The traffic will be relatively light this time of day. = 在一天的这个时候，交通会相对比较畅通。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were relatively warm, we would go for a walk. = 如果天气比较暖和，我们就去散步。
I wish the workload were relatively lighter. = 我希望工作量能相对轻一些。


被动语态:
The problem was relatively easily solved. = 这个问题相对容易解决。
The situation is being relatively well managed. = 局势正在得到相对良好的控制。


疑问句:
Is the price relatively high? = 价格相对高吗？
Is the distance relatively short? = 距离相对短吗？


否定句:
The result was not relatively satisfactory. = 结果并非相对令人满意。
The response wasn't relatively positive. = 反响并非相对积极。
