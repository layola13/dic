not-so-bad: /ˌnɑːt soʊ bæd/ | adj. | 还不错，不算太坏 | This phrase is an informal way of saying something is acceptable or not very bad. It softens the negativity of "bad" by adding "not so."  The phrase emphasizes that while not excellent, the situation or thing is tolerable. | Example: "The movie wasn't great, but it was not-so-bad."  这部电影不算精彩，但还算不错。|近义词：tolerable, acceptable, decent, alright, passable |反义词：terrible, awful, horrible, dreadful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather is not-so-bad today. = 今天天气还不错。
This cake is not-so-bad. = 这个蛋糕还不错。


一般过去时:
The exam was not-so-bad. = 考试不算太难。
The dinner party wasn't not-so-bad. = 晚宴还算不错。


现在完成时:
The project has been not-so-bad. = 项目进展还算不错。
Things have been not-so-bad lately. = 最近情况还不错。


过去完成时:
The situation hadn't been not-so-bad before the intervention. = 干预之前情况还不算太糟。
I hadn't thought it was not-so-bad until I compared it. = 直到我进行比较后才觉得它还算不错。


将来时:
The weather will be not-so-bad tomorrow. = 明天天气会还不错。
I think the results will be not-so-bad. = 我认为结果应该还不错。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were not-so-bad, we would go hiking. = 如果天气还不错的话，我们就去远足。
It would be not-so-bad if we had more time. = 如果我们有更多时间的话，情况会好一些。


被动语态:
The problem wasn't considered not-so-bad initially. = 起初这个问题并没有被认为不太严重。
The damage is not considered not-so-bad. = 损害程度不算太严重。


疑问句:
Is the situation not-so-bad? = 情况不算太糟吗？
Wasn't the movie not-so-bad? = 电影还不算太差吧？


否定句:
The situation is not not-so-bad, it's terrible. = 情况不是还不错，而是糟糕透顶。
The food wasn't not-so-bad, it was delicious. = 食物并非还不错，而是美味可口。
