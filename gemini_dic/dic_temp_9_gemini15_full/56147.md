documenter:/ˌdɒkjuˈmentər/|/ˈdɑːkjumɛntər/| n. |记录者；文献编纂者；纪录片制作人 |A person who documents something, such as a filmmaker who creates documentaries.  |The documenter meticulously recorded every detail of the event.  记录者一丝不苟地记录了事件的每一个细节。| chronicler, recorder, archivist |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境使用例如:forgetter, destroyer) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The documenter records the meeting. = 记录者记录会议。
He documents the historical events. = 他记录历史事件。


一般过去时:
The documenter recorded the interview. = 记录者记录了采访。
She documented the trip with photos. = 她用照片记录了这次旅行。


现在完成时:
The documenter has recorded all the evidence. = 记录者已经记录了所有证据。
They have documented the progress of the project. = 他们已经记录了项目的进展。


过去完成时:
The documenter had already recorded the testimony before the trial. = 在审判之前，记录者已经记录了证词。
She had documented the changes in the ecosystem for years. = 她多年来一直在记录生态系统的变化。


将来时:
