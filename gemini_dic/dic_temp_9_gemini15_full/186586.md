ridicules:/rɪˈdɪkjulz/| vt.| 嘲笑；戏弄；使…显得荒谬 | To make fun of someone or something; to treat someone or something as absurd or ridiculous. |例句：He ridiculed her efforts to learn to dance. 他嘲笑她学习跳舞的努力。

例句：Don't ridicule his attempts; he's trying his best. 不要嘲笑他的尝试；他正在尽力而为。


近义词：mock, scorn, deride, jeer, sneer

反义词：praise, commend, applaud


词性：动词
