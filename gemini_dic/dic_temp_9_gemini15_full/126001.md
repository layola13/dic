muslin:/ˈmʌzlɪn/| n. |细棉布；薄棉布 |A lightweight cotton fabric, originally from Mosul.  It's known for its softness and breathability.  It's often used for clothing, bedding, and other textiles. |例句：The dress was made of fine muslin. 这件衣服是用细棉布做的。

例句：She draped the muslin over the furniture to protect it from dust. 她用细棉布盖住家具以防尘。


近义词：cotton, gauze, voile|反义词：None specifically, but heavier fabrics like denim or canvas could be considered opposites.  |词性:名词
