lavish:/ˈlævɪʃ/| adj. v.| 丰富的；浪费的；慷慨的 |  giving or spending freely and abundantly; profuse; extravagant. |例句：They lived a lavish lifestyle. 他们过着奢侈的生活。


They lavishly decorated the room for the party.  他们为派对奢华地装饰了房间。| 近义词：extravagant, profuse, abundant, generous | 反义词：frugal, sparing, meager, stingy | 词性:形容词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They lavish praise on their children. = 他们对孩子们赞不绝口。
He lavishes attention on his garden. = 他非常用心打理他的花园。


一般过去时:
She lavished gifts on her friends. = 她慷慨地赠送礼物给她的朋友们。
They lavished money on their new house. = 他们在新房子上挥霍了金钱。


现在完成时:
They have lavished care on the project. = 他们对这个项目非常用心。
She has lavished affection on her pet dog. = 她对她的宠物狗非常疼爱。


过去完成时:
They had lavished attention on the details before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前非常注重细节。
She had lavished praise on his work in the past. = 她过去曾对他的作品赞赏有加。


将来时:
They will lavish resources on the new initiative. = 他们将大力投资这个新的计划。
She will lavish compliments on the chef. = 她将对厨师赞不绝口。


虚拟语气:
If they lavished more time on it, the project would be better. = 如果他们花更多时间在这上面，这个项目会更好。
I wish they lavished more attention to detail. = 我希望他们能更注重细节。


被动语态:
The team was lavished with praise for their achievements. = 该团队因其成就而受到高度赞扬。
The garden was lavished with water. = 花园浇灌了大量的水。


疑问句:
Do they lavish enough attention on their employees? = 他们对员工是否足够关注？
Will they lavish money on the renovation? = 他们会在装修上花钱吗？


否定句:
They did not lavish attention on the minor details. = 他们没有关注那些细枝末节。
He doesn't lavish money on unnecessary things. = 他不会把钱花在不必要的东西上。



PHRASE->
lavish praise on = 赞扬
lavish attention on = 关注
lavish money on = 挥霍金钱
lavish gifts on = 赠送礼物
lavish compliments on = 赞美
