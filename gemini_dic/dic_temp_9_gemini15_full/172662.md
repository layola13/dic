pythogenesis:/ˌpaɪθəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. | 蛇的起源；蛇的发生；蛇类发生学 | The study of the origin and development of snakes.  This field encompasses various aspects, from their evolutionary history to their embryological development. | The pythogenesis of boas is a complex topic requiring interdisciplinary research.  =蟒蛇的起源是一个需要跨学科研究的复杂课题。|  serpentinogenesis, ophiogenesis |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
