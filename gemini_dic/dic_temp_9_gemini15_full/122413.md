mning:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ/| n. |早晨，上午|The morning is the period of time between midnight and noon.  It is typically characterized by the rising of the sun.| 例句：Good morning! 早上好！
The meeting is scheduled for tomorrow morning. 明天的上午安排了会议。|近义词：forenoon, daybreak |反义词：evening, afternoon, night


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun rises in the morning. 太阳在早晨升起。
We usually have breakfast in the morning. 我们通常在早上吃早餐。

一般过去时:
I woke up early this morning. 我今天早上起得很早。
She went for a walk in the morning yesterday. 她昨天早上出去散步了。

现在完成时:
I have already had my coffee this morning. 我今天早上已经喝过咖啡了。
It has been raining since morning. 从早上就开始下雨了。

过去完成时:
I had already left before the morning meeting. 早上会议开始之前我已经离开了。
They had finished their work before morning. 他们在早晨之前已经完成了工作。


将来时:
I will meet you tomorrow morning. 我明天早上会和你见面。
We will have a test next morning. 我们明天早上有考试。

虚拟语气:
If it were morning, the birds would be singing. 如果是早上，鸟儿就会唱歌。
I wish it were morning already. 我真希望现在已经是早上了。

被动语态:
The streets were cleaned before morning. 街道在早晨之前被打扫干净了。
Breakfast is usually served in the morning. 早餐通常在早晨供应。

疑问句:
What time is it in the morning? 现在早上几点？
What are you doing this morning? 你今天早上在做什么？

否定句:
It wasn't morning yet.  现在还不是早上。
I didn't go to work this morning. 我今天早上没去上班。
