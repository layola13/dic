overproduces:/ˌoʊvərprəˈduːsɪz/ | v. | 过度生产；产量过多 | To produce more of something than is needed or wanted.  | The factory overproduces widgets, leading to a surplus in the market. 工厂生产的小部件过多，导致市场过剩。 |  overproduce, exceed, surpass | underproduce, underproduce  | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company overproduces every year. = 公司每年都过度生产。
They overproduce clothing for the upcoming season. = 他们为即将到来的季节过度生产服装。


一般过去时:
The farm overproduced corn last year. = 农场去年玉米产量过剩。
The factory overproduced toys during the holiday season. = 节日期间工厂玩具生产过剩。


现在完成时:
The government has overproduced food and now faces a storage problem. = 政府粮食生产过剩，现在面临储存问题。
The artist has overproduced paintings, resulting in lower prices. = 艺术家绘画作品产量过多，导致价格下降。


过去完成时:
They had overproduced before the market demand decreased. = 在市场需求下降之前，他们就已经生产过剩了。
The company had overproduced the product before realizing the poor sales projection. = 公司在意识到销售预测不佳之前就已经生产过剩了。


将来时:
The factory will overproduce if they don't adjust their production line. = 如果他们不调整生产线，工厂将会生产过剩。
We will overproduce next year if the demand stays high. = 如果需求保持高位，我们明年将会生产过剩。


虚拟语气:
If they had listened to the market research, they wouldn't have overproduced. = 如果他们听了市场调查，就不会生产过剩了。
I wish they hadn't overproduced so much. = 我真希望他们不要生产这么多。


被动语态:
Too much grain was overproduced last harvest. = 上一次收成粮食生产过多。
The products were overproduced, leading to a loss. = 产品生产过剩，导致亏损。


疑问句:
Did the factory overproduce this month? = 这个月工厂生产过剩了吗？
Is the company overproducing again this year? = 公司今年又生产过剩了吗？


否定句:
The company didn't overproduce this quarter. = 本季度公司没有生产过剩。
We did not overproduce despite the high demand. = 尽管需求量很大，我们也没有生产过剩。
