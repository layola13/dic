creativity:/ˌkriːeɪˈtɪvɪti/| n. |创造力，创造性|The ability to generate, develop, and communicate new ideas, approaches, and possibilities.  It involves imagination, originality, and the capacity to put ideas into action.|例句：Her creativity shines through in her artwork.  她的创造力在她的艺术作品中闪耀。|例句：The company encourages creativity among its employees. 公司鼓励员工发挥创造力。|近义词：inventiveness, originality, imagination|反义词：unoriginality, unimaginativeness
