lateritic:/ˌlætəˈrɪtɪk/ | adj. | 砖红色的；红土的； | Relating to or denoting laterite.  Laterite is a reddish-brown soil, rich in iron and aluminum oxides, formed by the weathering of certain rocks in hot, humid climates.  | 例句：The soil in this region is lateritic, making it difficult to cultivate.  这个地区的土壤是红土的，因此很难耕种。  The lateritic crust is very hard and difficult to penetrate. 这层红土很坚硬，很难穿透。 | 近义词：ferruginous, iron-rich | 反义词：暂无明确反义词
