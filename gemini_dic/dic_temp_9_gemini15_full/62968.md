ennuis:/ˈɑːnwiː/| n.|倦怠；厌倦；无聊|A feeling of listlessness and dissatisfaction arising from a lack of occupation or excitement.|例句：The ennui of the long summer holidays set in. 漫长的暑假带来了倦怠。
例句：He felt a deep sense of ennui after losing his job. 丢了工作后，他感到深深的倦怠。|近义词：boredom, tedium, weariness|反义词：excitement, enthusiasm, animation|名词
