generational: /ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 世世代代的；一代人的；与几代人相关的 | Relating to or characteristic of a generation or generations.  |The generational shift in attitudes towards technology is remarkable.  一代人对技术的态度转变令人瞩目。 |  intergenerational, age-related | ahistorical, timeless
