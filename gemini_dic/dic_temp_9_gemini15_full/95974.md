incumbency:/ɪnˈkʌmbənsi/| n. |在职；现任；保有职位|The period during which someone holds an office or position.  It also refers to the fact of holding an office or position.|Example:  The incumbent's long incumbency has led to complacency.  现任者的长期在职导致了自满。

The senator benefited from the advantages of incumbency during the reelection campaign. 参议员在竞选连任期间受益于在职优势。|近义词：tenure, occupancy, holding office|反义词：vacancy, absence from office
