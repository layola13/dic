cheerfullest:/ˈtʃɪərfʊləst/| adj.|最快乐的，最愉快的 |The superlative form of cheerful, indicating the highest degree of cheerfulness.  It describes someone or something exhibiting the utmost happiness and good spirits.|例句：Her cheerfullest smile lit up the room. 她最快乐的笑容照亮了整个房间。  The cheerfullest time of year is summer. 一年中最快乐的季节是夏天。|近义词：merriest, happiest, most joyful|反义词：saddest, most miserable,gloomiest|形容词
