raining: /ˈreɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 下雨 |  The present participle of "rain." It describes the action of rain falling.  | Example: It's raining cats and dogs.  =  下着倾盆大雨。 |近义词：showering, pouring, downpouring |反义词：sunny, dry


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is raining heavily. =  雨下得很大。
It rains a lot in this region. =  这个地区雨水很多。


一般过去时:
It rained all day yesterday. =  昨天一整天都在下雨。
It rained cats and dogs last night. =  昨晚下着倾盆大雨。

将来时:

被动语态:
