internationalising: /ˌɪntərˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 国际化；使国际化 | The act of making something international in scope or character.  It involves adapting products, services, and practices to meet the needs and preferences of different countries and cultures. | 例句：The company is internationalising its marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. 公司正在将其营销策略国际化，以吸引更广泛的受众。


The company is internationalising its operations by establishing offices in several countries. 公司正在通过在多个国家建立办事处来使其业务国际化。


近义词：globalizing, globalising


反义词：localization, localising


词性：动词
