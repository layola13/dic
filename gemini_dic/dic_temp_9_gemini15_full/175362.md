raking:/ˈreɪkɪŋ/| n. v.|耙；耙的动作；批判；严厉批评|The act of using a rake;  a critical examination or assessment.|例句：The gardener is raking leaves. 园丁正在耙树叶。  The critic's raking review of the play was brutal. 批评家对该剧的严厉批评非常残酷。|近义词：gathering, collecting, combing, criticism, censure|反义词：praise, commendation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rakes the leaves every autumn. = 他每年秋天都耙树叶。
She rakes the sand from the beach. = 她把沙子从沙滩上耙走。

一般过去时:
I raked the yard yesterday. = 我昨天耙了院子。
They raked the gravel off the driveway. = 他们把砾石从车道上耙掉。


现在完成时:
He has raked all the leaves into a pile. = 他已经把所有的树叶耙成一堆了。
She has raked the beach clean. = 她已经把沙滩耙干净了。
