congratulations:/kənˌɡrædʒʊˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |祝贺；恭喜 | Expressions of joy or approval at someone's achievement or good fortune.  |例句：Congratulations on your graduation! 恭喜你毕业！
                                                                                                    Congratulations on your new job! 恭喜你找到新工作！|近义词：felicitations, felicitations, commendation |反义词：condolences

词源:
The word "congratulations" originates from the Late Latin word "congratulatio," which is a combination of "con-" (meaning "together") and "gratulari" (meaning "to rejoice" or "to express joy").  Therefore, the word literally means "rejoicing together."
