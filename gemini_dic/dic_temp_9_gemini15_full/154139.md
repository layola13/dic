perilymph:/ˈpɛrɪlɪmf/ | n. |外淋巴|A clear, watery fluid that fills the space between the membranous labyrinth and the bony labyrinth of the inner ear.  It plays a crucial role in transmitting sound vibrations and maintaining the pressure balance within the inner ear. |The perilymph helps to transmit sound vibrations from the oval window to the cochlea.|近义词：endolymph (but with opposite location)|反义词：endolymph
