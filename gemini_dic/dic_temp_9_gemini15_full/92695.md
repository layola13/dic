iced:/aɪst/| adj.|冰冷的，加冰的|covered with or containing ice; made cold with ice. |例句：I'd like an iced coffee, please.  请给我一杯冰咖啡。|例句：The iced cake was delicious. 冰镇蛋糕很好吃。|近义词：chilled, frozen, frosty|反义词：hot, warm
