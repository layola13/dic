overpopulously:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːpjʊləsli/| adv.|过度拥挤地，人口过剩地|The city is overpopulously developed, leading to traffic congestion and housing shortages.|近义词：overcrowdedly, densely populated|反义词：sparsely populated


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and thus doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Its usage is always modifying a verb, adjective, or another adverb to describe the manner in which an action or state is characterized by being excessively populated.


Example Sentences:


The island was overpopulously settled, straining its resources. = 岛屿人口过度密集，资源不堪重负。

They lived overpopulously in a small apartment. = 他们一家挤在一个小公寓里，过度拥挤。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "overpopulously".  The word itself is relatively uncommon and its meaning is usually conveyed through alternative phrasing.  Instead of using phrases, you would typically use phrases describing the consequences of overpopulation, such as: "suffering from overpopulation",  "a densely populated area", or "facing population explosion".
