mesocarp:/ˈmɛsoʊkɑːrp/ | n. | 中果皮 | The mesocarp is the middle layer of the pericarp of a fruit, located between the exocarp (outer layer) and the endocarp (inner layer).  It can be fleshy, fibrous, or hard, depending on the type of fruit.  For example, in a peach, the mesocarp is the edible fleshy part. | The juicy mesocarp of the peach is its most appealing part.  桃子的多汁中果皮是其最吸引人的部分。 The mesocarp of a coconut is fibrous. 椰子的中果皮是纤维状的。 | 近义词：middle layer (of pericarp) | 反义词：exocarp, endocarp
