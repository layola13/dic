minter:/ˈmɪntər/ | n. |造币者；创造者；发明者 |A minter is someone who makes coins, or, more broadly, someone who creates or invents something new.  |例句：The royal minter struck a new coin to commemorate the king's jubilee. 皇家造币厂铸造了一种新的硬币以纪念国王的禧年。|例句：He's a minter of new ideas, always coming up with innovative solutions. 他是一个新的想法的创造者，总是想出创新的解决方案。|近义词：coin maker, creator, innovator, inventor |反义词：destroyer, obliterator
