retransplants:/ˌriːˈtrænzˌplænts/| vt.| 再移植；重新移植 | To transplant again; to transplant something that has already been transplanted.  The act of replanting something that was previously planted. |例句：The surgeon had to retransplant the kidney after it failed to function properly.  外科医生不得不再次移植肾脏，因为它功能失常。


The gardener retransplanted the seedlings after the initial planting site proved unsuitable. 园丁在最初的种植地点被证明不合适后，对幼苗进行了重新种植。|近义词：replant, transplant anew, re-establish|反义词：remove, extract, uproot|动词
