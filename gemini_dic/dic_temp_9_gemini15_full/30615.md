cerebroid:/ˈsɛrɪbrɔɪd/| adj.|大脑的；脑状的；似脑的|Resembling the cerebrum in structure or function; relating to the cerebrum or its functions.  The term often implies a resemblance to brain tissue in appearance or texture, but not necessarily in function.|例句：The tumor had a cerebroid appearance under the microscope.  显微镜下，肿瘤呈现脑状外观。

例句：The doctor noted the patient's cerebroid symptoms, such as confusion and difficulty speaking. 医生注意到患者的大脑症状，例如混乱和说话困难。


近义词：encephalic, cerebral|反义词：peripheral
