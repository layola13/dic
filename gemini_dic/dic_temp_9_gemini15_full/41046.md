copier:/ˈkɑːpiər/| n.|复印机；复制者|A machine that makes copies of documents or images.|例句：The copier is jammed again.  复印机又卡纸了。|例句：He's a skilled copier of old masters' paintings. 他是一位技巧娴熟的古代大师绘画复制者。|近义词：duplicator, photocopier|反义词：original (in the sense of the original document/artwork)
