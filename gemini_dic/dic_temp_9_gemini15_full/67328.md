extemporize:/ɪkˈstɛmpəraɪz/| vt.|即兴创作；临时发挥；无准备地演讲|To speak or perform without preparation; to improvise.|例句：She had to extemporize a speech when the scheduled speaker fell ill. 她不得不临时准备演讲，因为原定的演讲者病倒了。|例句：The musician extemporized a beautiful melody on the piano.  这位音乐家即兴演奏了一段优美的钢琴曲。|近义词：improvise, ad-lib, wing it|反义词：rehearse, prepare, script
