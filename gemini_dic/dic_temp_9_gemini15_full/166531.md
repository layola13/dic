presbyteral:/ˌprɛzbɪˈtɪəriəl/| adj. |长老制的；长老会教派的；长老的；|Relating to or characteristic of presbyters or elders, especially in a church government.  The term often refers to the Presbyterian system of church governance, where a body of elders governs the church.|The presbyteral system of church government is based on the principle of shared leadership.  |近义词：ecclesiastical, clerical |反义词：lay, secular
