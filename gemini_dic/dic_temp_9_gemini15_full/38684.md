condensation:/ˌkɒndənˈseɪʃən/| n. |凝结，冷凝；浓缩 |The process of changing from a gas to a liquid.  This typically happens when a gas cools down.  It's also used to describe the act of making something more concise or concentrated.|

例句：
The condensation on the window made it difficult to see.  窗户上的凝结水使得难以看清。

The condensation of the long report into a summary was helpful.  将冗长的报告浓缩成摘要很有帮助。


近义词：liquefaction, concentration

反义词：evaporation, vaporization


词源：
From the Latin word "condensare," meaning "to make thick or dense,"  derived from "con-" (together) and "densare" (to thicken).
