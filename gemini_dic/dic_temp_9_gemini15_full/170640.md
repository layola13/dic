pseudo-Mexican:/ˈsuːdoʊˈmɛksɪkən/| adj.|伪墨西哥的，貌似墨西哥的|Appearing to be Mexican but not genuinely so; often used to describe food, culture, or products that imitate Mexican style but lack authenticity.|例句：The restaurant served pseudo-Mexican food that lacked the genuine flavors of Mexico. 这家餐厅供应的伪墨西哥菜缺乏墨西哥的真正风味。
例句：That band's music is pseudo-Mexican; it appropriates some elements but misses the cultural context.  那个乐队的音乐是伪墨西哥的；它挪用了某些元素，但忽略了文化背景。|近义词：faux-Mexican, imitation Mexican|反义词：authentic Mexican, genuine Mexican
