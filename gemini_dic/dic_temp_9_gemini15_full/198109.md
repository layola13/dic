shooters:/ˈʃuːtərz/| n.|射手；枪手；射击运动员；（指电影）枪战片|People or things that shoot.  This word can refer to individuals skilled in shooting,  weapons themselves, or films featuring gun battles.|例句：The Olympic shooters demonstrated amazing precision. 奥运会射手们展现了惊人的精准度。

例句：The new action movie is full of exciting shooters. 这部新的动作电影充满了刺激的枪战场景。|近义词：marksmen, gunners, sharpshooters|反义词：targets (in the context of shooting)|词性:名词
