sarnie:/ˈsɑːni/| n. |三明治|A sarnie is an informal British English term for a sandwich.  It's a shortened and often playfully used version of "sandwich".|例句：Fancy a sarnie for lunch?  午餐想吃个三明治吗？

例句：I packed a sarnie and some crisps for my trip. 我带了一个三明治和一些薯片去旅行。|近义词：sandwich, butty (BrE)|反义词:  None (it's a noun referring to food)|词性:noun
