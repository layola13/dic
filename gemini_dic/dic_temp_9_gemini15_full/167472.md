preventingly: /prɪˈvɛntɪŋli/ | adv. | 预防地；防患于未然地 |  Acting in a way that prevents something from happening; proactively avoiding something undesirable.  This word is rarely used and often considered informal or archaic. | 例句：He acted preventingly, securing the building before the storm hit. = 他未雨绸缪，暴风雨来临前就保护好了建筑物。


例句：The doctor administered the vaccine preventingly. = 医生预防性地注射了疫苗。


近义词：prophylactically, preemptively, proactively


反义词：reactively, belatedly, after the fact


词性：副词
