popstar:/ˈpɒpˌstɑːr/| n. |流行歌星|A pop star is a singer or musician who is very popular and successful in the pop music genre.  They often have a large fan base and achieve significant commercial success. |Example:  Taylor Swift is a hugely successful pop star.  泰勒·斯威夫特是一位非常成功的流行歌星。

Many young people dream of becoming pop stars. 许多年轻人梦想成为流行歌星。


近义词：pop singer, music star, celebrity
反义词：unknown musician, nobody
