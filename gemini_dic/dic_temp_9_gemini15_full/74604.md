freaks:/friːks/| n., v.|怪人；怪癖；反常现象；使震惊；使狂怒|

英文解析：
As a noun, "freak" refers to a person who is considered unusual or abnormal, often in a way that is considered strange or unsettling.  It can also refer to something unusual or abnormal in general, or a sudden, unexpected, and often violent event. As a verb, it means to become extremely excited or enthusiastic, or to cause someone to feel this way.  The word carries a strong connotation, often negative, implying something is outside the norm and potentially disturbing.

例句：
He's a real freak; he collects antique toilet brushes.  = 他是个怪人；他收集古董马桶刷。

The storm was a freak of nature, causing widespread devastation. = 那场风暴是自然界的反常现象，造成了大范围的破坏。


近义词：
monster, oddball, weirdo, eccentric, anomaly, phenomenon

反义词：
normal, typical, ordinary, regular, conventional


词性：
Noun, Verb
