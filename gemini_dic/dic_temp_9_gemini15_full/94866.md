in all:/ɪn ɔːl/ | adv. |总共，总计，总而言之 |In all, there were fifty people at the party. 总共，聚会上来了五十个人。|近义词：altogether, in total, overall |反义词：individually, separately


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The total cost is $100 in all. = 总成本是100美元。
There are ten apples in all. = 总共有十个苹果。

一般过去时:
We spent $500 in all last month. = 上个月我们总共花了500美元。
They collected 100 signatures in all. = 他们总共收集了100个签名。

现在完成时:
We have earned $1000 in all this year. = 今年我们总共赚了1000美元。
She has read five books in all. = 她总共读了五本书。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had sold 1000 cars in all. = 到年底，他们总共卖出了1000辆汽车。
Before the project finished, they had spent $10000 in all. = 项目结束前，他们总共花了1万美元。

将来时:
We will need 1000 bricks in all for the house. = 盖房子总共需要1000块砖。
They will have traveled 10,000 miles in all by the end of their trip. = 到旅行结束时，他们总共将旅行10000英里。


虚拟语气:
If we had had more money, we could have bought everything we needed in all. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们就可以买到所有我们需要的东西。
I wish I had bought all the supplies in all before the storm. = 我真希望我在暴风雨来临之前把所有物资都买齐了。

被动语态:
A total of 1000 votes were cast in all. = 总共投出了1000张选票。
Five different colors were used in all on the painting. = 这幅画总共使用了五种不同的颜色。

疑问句:
How many people attended the conference in all? = 总共有多少人参加了会议？
How much money was spent in all on the project? = 这个项目总共花了多少钱？

否定句:
Not all the students passed the exam. = 不是所有学生都通过了考试。(This example doesn't directly use "in all", but shows a similar concept of totality.)
The plan did not include all the necessary steps in all. = 该计划并非包含所有必要的步骤。
