buyin's:/ˈbaɪɪnz/| n.|买入；认购；认同；接受|The plural form of "buy-in," referring to instances of purchase, agreement, or acceptance.|例句：The company's buyins of raw materials increased last quarter.  公司上季度原材料的采购量增加了。|近义词：purchases, acquisitions, acceptances|反义词：sales, rejections, denials
