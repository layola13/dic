lea:/liː/| n.|草地，牧场，田地|A lea is a field, especially one of grass or pasture.  It often evokes a sense of rural tranquility and open space. |例句：The sheep grazed peacefully in the green lea.  羊儿在青翠的草地上安静地吃草。|近义词：meadow, pasture, field|反义词：city, urban area


lea:/liː/| n.|许可，允许，特许|In some older or regional dialects, lea can mean permission or leave. This usage is less common now.|例句：He asked for lea to leave early. 他请求提早离开的许可。|近义词：permission, leave, license|反义词：prohibition, denial
