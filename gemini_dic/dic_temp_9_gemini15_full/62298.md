endangerment:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒərmənt/ | n. | 危害；威胁；濒危 | The endangerment of the species is a serious concern.  物种的濒危是一个严重的问题。| 近义词：threat, peril, jeopardy, risk | 反义词：safety, security, protection |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The deforestation endangers the local wildlife.  滥砍滥伐危及当地野生动物。
Endangerment of the environment is a global issue. 环境的破坏是一个全球性问题。


一般过去时:
The factory's pollution caused significant endangerment to the river ecosystem. 工厂的污染对河流生态系统造成了严重的危害。
The reckless driving resulted in the endangerment of several pedestrians.  鲁莽驾驶导致了几名行人的危险。


现在完成时:
Human activity has caused the endangerment of many plant and animal species. 人类活动导致了许多动植物物种的濒危。
The government has taken steps to prevent further endangerment of the endangered species. 政府已采取措施防止濒危物种进一步受到威胁。


过去完成时:
By the time the authorities intervened, the endangerment of the coral reef had already reached a critical point. 当当局介入时，珊瑚礁的破坏已经达到了临界点。
They realized that the construction project had caused significant endangerment to the local bird population before it was finished. 他们在项目完工前才意识到该建筑项目对当地鸟类数量造成了严重的威胁。


将来时:
The proposed dam project will cause the endangerment of several fish species.  拟建的大坝项目将导致几种鱼类的灭绝。
Continued pollution will lead to the endangerment of the entire ecosystem.  持续的污染将导致整个生态系统的危害。


虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, we risk further endangerment of the rainforest. 如果我们现在不采取行动，我们就有可能进一步破坏雨林。
I wish the endangerment of the whales could be stopped. 我希望鲸鱼的灭绝能够停止。


被动语态:

Many ecosystems are currently experiencing endangerment from climate change.  许多生态系统目前正遭受气候变化的威胁。


疑问句:

否定句:
