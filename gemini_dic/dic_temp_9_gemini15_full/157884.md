plainsman:/ˈpleɪnzˌmæn/| n. |平原居民，大草原人 |A plainsman is a person who lives on or is familiar with the plains, especially in North America.  They are often associated with a particular lifestyle and skills related to this environment.  |Example:  The old plainsman told tales of his adventures on the open range.  这位老平原居民讲述了他在大草原上的冒险故事。  The plainsman skillfully rode his horse across the vast expanse.这位平原居民熟练地骑着马穿越广阔的平原。|近义词：prairie dweller, plains dweller, frontiersman (in a broader context)|反义词：city dweller, urbanite
