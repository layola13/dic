corkscrews:/ˈkɔːrkskruːz/ | n. | 开瓶器；螺旋状物 | A corkscrew is a tool used to remove corks from bottles of wine or other drinks. It typically consists of a helical screw and a handle.  | 例句：He used a corkscrew to open the bottle of wine. 他用开瓶器打开了这瓶葡萄酒。

例句：The staircase spiraled upwards like a giant corkscrew.  楼梯像巨大的螺旋桨一样向上盘旋。


近义词：bottle opener, screw puller | 反义词：None
