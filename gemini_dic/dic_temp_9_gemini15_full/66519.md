exhaling: /ɛkˈseɪlɪŋ/ | v. |呼气，吐气 | The act of breathing out.  The process of releasing air from the lungs. |例句：She exhaled slowly, trying to calm herself. 她慢慢地呼气，试图让自己平静下来。

例句：After a long run, he exhaled deeply, feeling relief. 长跑之后，他深深地呼了一口气，感到轻松。


近义词：breathing out, expelling air | 反义词：inhaling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exhales deeply before each dive. 他每次潜水前都深呼吸。
She exhales slowly and steadily during yoga. 她在做瑜伽时缓慢而稳定地呼气。


一般过去时:
The patient exhaled his last breath. 病人呼出了最后一口气。
He exhaled a long sigh of relief. 他长长地舒了一口气。


现在完成时:
She has exhaled a lot of stress today. 她今天释放了很多压力。
He has exhaled smoke from his cigarette. 他已经从香烟中呼出了烟雾。


过去完成时:
Before fainting, she had exhaled a sharp gasp. 晕倒之前，她猛地吸了一口气。
After finishing the race, she had exhaled a wave of exhaustion. 比赛结束后，她感到一阵疲惫。


将来时:
He will exhale deeply to relax before the speech. 他将在演讲前深呼吸以放松。
She will exhale the last bit of air from her lungs. 她将呼出肺部最后一点空气。


虚拟语气:
If I were calm, I would exhale more slowly. 如果我平静下来，我会更慢地呼气。
I wish I could exhale all my worries. 我希望我能吐出所有的忧虑。


被动语态: (被动语态较少用于此词)
Air is exhaled from the lungs. 空气从肺部呼出。(较为正式，通常不用被动)


疑问句:
Does he exhale smoke while he's working? 他工作时会吸烟吗？(此处是“呼出烟雾”，可以理解为被动)
How does she exhale during her meditation? 她冥想时是如何呼气的？


否定句:
He didn't exhale until he was sure it was safe. 直到他确定安全后才呼气。
She couldn't exhale properly due to the pain. 由于疼痛，她无法正常呼气。


PHRASE->

exhale slowly 慢慢呼气
exhale completely 完全呼气
exhale quietly 安静地呼气
exhale forcefully 用力呼气
