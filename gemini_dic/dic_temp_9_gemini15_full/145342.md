overbuilt:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪlt/ | adj. | 过度建设的；过度发展的 | Excessively built or developed; having more buildings or infrastructure than is necessary or desirable.  | The city is overbuilt, leading to traffic congestion and a lack of green spaces.  这个城市过度建设，导致交通拥堵和缺乏绿地。| overdeveloped, congested | underdeveloped, sparsely populated | 形容词
