phalanstere:/fəˈlænstər/| n. |乌托邦式公社；集体住宅；方阵|A phalanstere is a type of utopian community, typically characterized by communal living and shared resources, often in a large, planned building or complex.  The concept was popularized by Charles Fourier, who envisioned them as self-sufficient and harmonious societies.  |Example: Fourier's phalanstere was designed to maximize efficiency and social harmony.  傅立叶的乌托邦式公社旨在最大限度地提高效率和社会和谐。|近义词：commune, collective, kibbutz |反义词：individualistic society, capitalist society|名词
