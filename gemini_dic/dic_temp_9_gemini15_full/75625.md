fughetta:/fʊˈɡɛtə/| n. |赋格小曲|A short, relatively simple fugue.  It often serves as a compositional exercise or a prelude to a larger work.  Fughettas are characterized by their concise structure and less complex contrapuntal interplay compared to full-fledged fugues.|Example: The composer included a delightful fughetta as an introduction to the symphony.|近义词：short fugue, prelude|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a full-scale fugue.
