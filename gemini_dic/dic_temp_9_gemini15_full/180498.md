regenerateness:/rɪˌdʒɛnərətnəs/| n. |再生能力；新生；复兴|The capacity for renewal or rebirth; the state of being regenerated.  It often refers to spiritual renewal or the revival of something.  The term can also describe the biological process of regeneration.|The regenerateness of the forest after the fire was remarkable.  森林大火后的再生能力令人惊叹。|The city experienced a period of economic regenerateness after the implementation of new policies.  在实施新政策后，这座城市经历了一段经济复兴时期。|renewal, revival, rebirth|decay, decline, deterioration|名词
