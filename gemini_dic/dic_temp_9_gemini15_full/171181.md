psychologists:/saɪˈkɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |心理学家 |People who study the human mind and behavior. They apply psychological principles to understand and treat mental health issues, improve human performance, or conduct research on various aspects of human cognition, emotion, and behavior. | Many psychologists work in clinical settings, providing therapy and counseling.  |Psychiatrists, therapists, counselors |sociologists, anthropologists
