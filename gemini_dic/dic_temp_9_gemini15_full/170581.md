pseudo-Catholics:/ˈsuːdoʊ ˈkæθəlɪk/| n. |假天主教徒；名义上的天主教徒|People who outwardly profess Catholicism but do not adhere to its doctrines or practices.  They may participate in Catholic rituals but lack genuine faith or understanding of Catholic beliefs.|Example: Some historians argue that a significant portion of the population in that era were pseudo-Catholics, going through the motions without true religious conviction.|近义词: nominal Catholic, superficial Catholic |反义词: devout Catholic, true believer |名词
