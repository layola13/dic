re-dispatch:/ˌriːˈdɪspætʃ/| vt.|重新调度；再次派遣|To send something or someone again or to a different place.|The package was re-dispatched after the initial delivery failed.  包裹在第一次投递失败后被重新投递。|The injured hiker was re-dispatched to a better-equipped hospital. 受伤的徒步旅行者被重新送往设备更好的医院。|dispatch, resend, reroute|—|verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-dispatch the goods daily. = 他们每天重新调度货物。
The company re-dispatches packages to correct delivery errors. = 公司重新分发包裹以纠正投递错误。


一般过去时:
They re-dispatched the shipment yesterday. = 他们昨天重新调度了货物。
The manager re-dispatched the team to a new location. = 经理将团队重新派往新的地点。


现在完成时:
The company has re-dispatched all the faulty products. = 公司已经重新调度了所有有缺陷的产品。
We have re-dispatched the email with the correct attachments. = 我们已经重新发送了包含正确附件的电子邮件。


过去完成时:
They had re-dispatched the order before the customer complained. = 在客户投诉之前，他们已经重新调度了订单。
The authorities had re-dispatched the rescue team to a different area. = 当局已将救援队重新派往其他地区。


将来时:
We will re-dispatch the package tomorrow. = 我们明天将重新投递包裹。
The government will re-dispatch aid to the affected region. = 政府将向受灾地区重新派遣援助。


虚拟语气:
If the delivery failed, they would re-dispatch the item immediately. = 如果送货失败，他们会立即重新投递物品。
If I were the manager, I would re-dispatch the staff to support the new project. = 如果我是经理，我会重新安排员工支持新项目。


被动语态:
The package was re-dispatched after the address was corrected. = 地址更正后，包裹被重新投递。

疑问句:

否定句:
They did not re-dispatch the order. = 他们没有重新调度订单。
