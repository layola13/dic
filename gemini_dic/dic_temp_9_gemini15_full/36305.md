cohesionless:/ˌkoʊˈhiʒən.ləs/| adj.|无凝聚力的，不粘结的|Lacking cohesion; not sticking together.  The soil is cohesionless, making it prone to erosion.|例句：The sandcastle collapsed because the sand was cohesionless. 沙堡倒塌了，因为沙子没有黏性。|The cohesionless soil easily washed away in the heavy rain.  在暴雨中，疏松的土壤很容易被冲走。|近义词：unbound, loose, friable|反义词：cohesive, compact, consolidated
