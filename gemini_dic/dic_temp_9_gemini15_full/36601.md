colleagueship:/ˈkɒliːɡʃɪp/| n. |同事关系，同僚情谊 |The state or quality of being colleagues; the relationship between colleagues.|例句：The colleagueship between the two researchers was essential to the success of their project.  两位研究人员之间的同事关系对他们项目的成功至关重要。


例句：Good colleagueship fosters a positive and productive work environment. 良好的同事关系能营造积极高效的工作环境。


近义词：comradeship, fellowship, companionship


反义词：antagonism, hostility, conflict
