gangway:/ˈɡæŋweɪ/ | n. |通道，舷梯，走道 | A narrow passageway, especially one providing access to a ship or aircraft.  |例句：The passengers boarded the ship via the gangway. 乘客通过舷梯登上船。
例句：The gangway was narrow and crowded. 走道又窄又挤。|近义词：passage, walkway, corridor |反义词：N/A
