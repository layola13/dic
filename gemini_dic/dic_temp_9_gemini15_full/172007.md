purgatorio:/ˌpɜːrɡəˈtɔːri.oʊ/|/ˌpɜːrɡəˈtɔːri.əʊ/| n. |炼狱；净化过程|The second part of Dante Alighieri's epic poem *The Divine Comedy*, describing the souls in Purgatory undergoing purification before entering Paradise.  It depicts a mountain rising from the ocean, where souls atone for their sins through various trials.|Example:  Dante's *Purgatorio* vividly portrays the process of spiritual cleansing.|近义词：purgatory, intermediate state|反义词：heaven, hell
