oilcloths: /ˈɔɪlklɒθs/ | n. |油布，油画布 | A heavy cloth treated with oil or other substances to make it waterproof.  It is often used to cover tables or protect surfaces. |例句：We covered the picnic table with an oilcloth to protect it from the rain. 我们用油布盖住了野餐桌以防下雨。

例句：The old oilcloth on the kitchen table was faded and stained. 厨房桌子上那块旧油布已经褪色并沾上了污渍。


近义词：waterproof cloth, plastic sheeting


反义词：None (No direct antonym, as it describes a specific type of material)
