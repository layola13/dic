clingier:/ˈklɪŋiər/| adj.|粘人的，依恋的 | More clingy; showing excessive dependence or attachment.  It is the comparative form of "clingy."| 例句：He's become much clingier since his girlfriend left. 他自从女朋友离开后变得更粘人了。

例句：The puppy was clingier than usual after its mother died. 小狗在妈妈死后比平时更粘人。


近义词：dependent, attached, possessive, needy | 反义词：independent, detached, self-reliant


词源:  Derived from the adjective "clingy," which in turn comes from the verb "cling," meaning to hold on tightly or adhere closely. The "-ier" suffix indicates a comparative degree, suggesting a greater degree of clinginess.  The root of "cling" can be traced back to Old English "clyngan."
