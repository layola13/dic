polydentate:/ˌpɒliˈdɛnteɪt/ /ˌpɑːliˈdɛnteɪt/| adj.|多齿的；多配位的|  Relating to or denoting a ligand that can form two or more coordinate bonds with a single central atom. A polydentate ligand is also called a chelating agent because it forms a ring structure with the central atom.  | Example: EDTA is a polydentate ligand that is commonly used in chelation therapy. EDTA是一种常用的螯合疗法中的多齿配体。|近义词：chelating, multidentate |反义词：monodentate
