pro-choice: /ˈproʊ ˈtʃɔɪs/ | adj. n. | 支持堕胎选择权的；选择权支持者 |  Supporting a woman's right to choose whether or not to have an abortion.  The term encompasses a broader belief in bodily autonomy and reproductive freedom. |  例句：The pro-choice movement advocates for access to safe and legal abortion. 支持堕胎选择权的运动倡导获得安全和合法的堕胎服务。


例句：Many people identify as pro-choice, believing that the decision about abortion should rest solely with the pregnant person. 许多人认为自己是支持堕胎选择权的，认为堕胎的决定应该完全取决于孕妇本人。


近义词：pro-abortion rights (in some contexts),  supporter of reproductive rights


反义词：pro-life, anti-abortion


词性：形容词，名词
