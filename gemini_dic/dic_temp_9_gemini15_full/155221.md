phaenogamic:/ˌfiːnəˈɡæmɪk/| adj.| 显花植物的；有花的 | Relating to or denoting plants that produce flowers and seeds.  The term is often used in contrast to "cryptogamic," which refers to plants that reproduce by spores rather than seeds. | Example:  The study focused on the reproductive strategies of phaenogamic plants in diverse ecosystems.  显花植物在不同生态系统中的繁殖策略研究是本研究的重点。|近义词：flowering, seed-bearing | 反义词：cryptogamic
