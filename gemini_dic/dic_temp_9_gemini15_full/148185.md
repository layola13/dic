overshade:/ˌoʊvərˈʃeɪd/ | vt. | 遮蔽过多；使过于阴暗 | To shade or darken excessively; to overshadow.  | The tall building overshaded the small house next door.  高楼遮蔽了隔壁的小房子。The dense forest overshaded the path, making it difficult to see.  茂密的森林遮蔽了小路，使人难以看清。 | overshadow, obscure | illuminate, brighten | 动词
