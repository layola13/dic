pyrotechnian:/ˌpaɪrəʊˈtɛkniən/| n. |烟火技师，烟火专家|A pyrotechnician is a person who designs, manufactures, or sets off fireworks.  They are skilled in the handling of explosive materials and the creation of pyrotechnic displays.|The pyrotechnician carefully prepared the fireworks for the Fourth of July celebration. 烟火技师仔细地准备了七月四日的烟火庆祝活动。|fireworks expert, pyrotechnist| (no direct antonym, but perhaps someone who actively opposes fireworks displays)|名词
