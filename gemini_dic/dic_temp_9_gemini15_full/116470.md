material:/məˈtɪəriəl/| n. adj.|材料；物质；素材；布料|The word "material" refers to the physical substance or matter of which something is made. It can also refer to the substance from which something is composed, or the elements or components of something.  In a broader sense, it can also mean the subject matter or data used in a creative work.|例句：The building is made of strong material. 这座建筑是用坚固的材料建造的。|例句：She used recycled material in her artwork. 她在艺术作品中使用了回收材料。|近义词：substance, stuff, matter, fabric|反义词：immaterial, spiritual, intangible|名词
