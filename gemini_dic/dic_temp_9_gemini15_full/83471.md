hacienda:/ɑːˈsiɛndɑː/| n.|大庄园；种植园|A large estate or plantation, especially one in Spanish-speaking countries, often including a large house and outbuildings.  It typically encompasses extensive agricultural lands and may include residential quarters for workers.|例句：The hacienda produced coffee, sugar, and other crops. 这座大庄园种植咖啡、糖和其他农作物。|例句：They visited a beautiful old hacienda in the countryside. 他们在乡下参观了一座美丽的古老庄园。|近义词：estate, plantation, ranch, farm|反义词：smallholding, cottage
