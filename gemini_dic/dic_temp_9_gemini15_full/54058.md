disdainfully: /dɪsˈdeɪnfəli/ | adv. | 轻蔑地，不屑地 | Showing or expressing contempt or lack of respect.  |  She looked at him disdainfully. 她轻蔑地看他。 He spoke disdainfully of his rivals. 他轻蔑地谈论他的竞争对手。| contemptuously, scornfully, sneeringly | respectfully, admiringly | 副词
