dandelion: /ˈdændɪlaɪən/ | n. |蒲公英|A common wild plant with bright yellow flowers that turn into fluffy white seed heads.  It is known for its ability to spread its seeds widely via the wind. |例句：The children blew the dandelion seeds into the wind.孩子们把蒲公英的种子吹向风中。

例句：A field of dandelions looked like a golden carpet.一片蒲公英田野看起来像金色的地毯。


近义词：dandelion clock (指蒲公英的种子球), blowball


反义词：无


词源:The word "dandelion" comes from the French "dent-de-lion," literally meaning "lion's tooth," referring to the jagged shape of its leaves.  This reflects the plant's appearance in several languages.
