self-exhibitions:/ˌsɛlfˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənz/| n. |自我展示，自我表现 |The act of displaying oneself or one's work to others, often with a sense of pride or seeking attention.  It can refer to boasting, showing off, or publicly sharing one's accomplishments, creations, or personality. |例句：Her constant self-exhibitions on social media became tiresome to her friends.  她不断在社交媒体上自我炫耀，让朋友们感到厌烦。

例句：His self-exhibitions at the art show were met with mixed reactions; some found them impressive, others arrogant. 他在艺术展上的自我表现引起了褒贬不一的反应；一些人觉得他令人印象深刻，另一些人则觉得他傲慢。


近义词：showboating, boasting, self-promotion, grandstanding, ostentation

反义词：humility, modesty, self-effacement


词性:名词
