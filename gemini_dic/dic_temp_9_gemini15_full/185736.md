revibrate:/ˌriːˈvaɪbreɪt/ | v. | 再震动；重新振动 | To vibrate again; to begin to vibrate after a pause or cessation.  The word implies a renewed or repeated vibration, not necessarily a stronger one. | 例句：The building revived after the initial tremor subsided.  建筑物在初始震动平息后再次震动。

The earthquake caused the ground to revibrate several times. 地震导致地面多次再次震动。

| 近义词：re-vibrate, resonate (in some contexts) | 反义词：cease vibrating, become still | 动词
