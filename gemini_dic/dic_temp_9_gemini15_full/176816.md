readoption:/riːˈdɒpʃən/ | n. | 再收养；重新选择 | The act of adopting again; the selection or adoption of something previously rejected or discarded.  | Example: The couple decided on a readoption after their first adoption failed. 这对夫妇在第一次收养失败后决定再次收养。 |近义词：re-adoption, readmission |反义词：abandonment, rejection |名词
