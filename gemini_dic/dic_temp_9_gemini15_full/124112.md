mortally:/ˈmɔːrtəli/| adj.|致命的；非常地；极度地|  extremely; to a very great degree; fatally.|例句：He was mortally wounded in the battle. 他在战斗中受了致命的伤。

例句：She was mortally offended by his remarks. 她对他说的那些话感到非常生气。


近义词：fatally, deadly, extremely, intensely|反义词：slightly, mildly, insignificantly|词性：形容词


词源：源自于古法语“mortalment”，意为“致死地”，进一步源于拉丁语“mortalis”，意为“凡人，必死的”。
