panoplies:/ˈpænəpliːz/| n. |全副武装；一系列；盛装|A complete or impressive collection of things.|The museum boasts a panoply of dinosaur fossils.  博物馆拥有令人印象深刻的恐龙化石收藏。|近义词：array, collection, display|反义词：lack, scarcity

A full panoply of skills is required for the job. = 这份工作需要全套技能。
