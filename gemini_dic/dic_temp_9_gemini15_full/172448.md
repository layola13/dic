pygostyle:/ˈpaɪɡoʊstaɪl/| n. |尾综骨，尾骨|The pygostyle is a bony structure formed by the fusion of the last few caudal vertebrae in birds. It provides attachment points for flight muscles and supports the tail feathers.|例句：The pygostyle is a key anatomical feature distinguishing birds from other vertebrates. 尾综骨是区分鸟类和其他脊椎动物的关键解剖特征。|例句：The size and shape of the pygostyle can vary greatly among different bird species.不同鸟类的尾综骨的大小和形状差异很大。|近义词：tail bone (informal)|反义词：无
