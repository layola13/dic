defuelling: /ˌdiːˈfjuːlɪŋ/ | v. | 去燃料；卸油；排油 | The process of removing fuel from something, typically an aircraft, vehicle, or storage tank.  It often involves safety procedures and specialized equipment. |

例句：

The defuelling process took longer than expected due to the unexpected equipment malfunction.  = 由于设备意外故障，卸油过程比预期时间长。

The aircraft underwent a complete defuelling before undergoing maintenance. =  飞机在进行维护之前进行了彻底的卸油。


近义词：fuel removal, discharging, draining


反义词：refuelling, fueling


词性:动词
