grimaces:/ɡrɪˈmeɪs/| v.|做鬼脸，皱眉表示厌恶|To make a grimace is to distort one's face, typically to express disgust, disapproval, or pain.  It involves contorting the facial muscles, often involving the mouth, nose, and eyebrows. |He grimaced at the bitter taste of the medicine. 他对药的苦味做了个鬼脸。|She grimaced in pain after she stubbed her toe. 她脚趾撞到后痛苦地皱起了眉头。|近义词：wince, frown|反义词：smile
