journalish: /ˈdʒɜːrnəlɪʃ/ | adj. |新闻记者式的，新闻风格的 |  Characterized by the style or manner of journalistic writing; often implying sensationalism or a lack of depth.  It suggests writing that prioritizes brevity and impact over nuanced analysis. | The article felt a bit journalish, focusing more on grabbing headlines than on thorough investigation.  这篇文章感觉有点新闻记者式的，更注重吸引眼球而不是彻底调查。 |近义词：sensationalist, tabloid, newsy |反义词：scholarly, academic, in-depth
