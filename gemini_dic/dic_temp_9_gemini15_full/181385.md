reinvolved:/ˌriːɪnˈvɒlvd/ | v. |重新卷入；再次参与|To become involved again in something; to involve someone or something again.  |例句：After a period of inactivity, he became reinvolved in the project.  = 在一段时间的休整后，他又重新参与了这个项目。

例句：The company was reinvolved in the scandal after new evidence emerged. =  随着新证据的出现，该公司再次卷入了丑闻。


近义词：re-engage, re-enter, participate again, become involved again
反义词：withdraw, disengage, remove, exclude

词性:动词
