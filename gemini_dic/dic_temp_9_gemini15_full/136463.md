nonreviewable: /ˌnɒn.rɪˈvjuː.ə.bl/ | adj. | 不可审查的，不可复查的 | Not subject to review or reconsideration.  This typically applies to decisions or actions that are final and cannot be appealed or challenged. |  The judge's ruling was nonreviewable, meaning there was no higher court to appeal to.  法官的裁决是不可审查的，这意味着没有更高的法院可以上诉。 |  final, unappealable, irrevocable | reviewable, appealable |
