photochromatic:/ˌfoʊtoʊkroʊˈmætɪk/|/ˌfəʊtəʊkrəʊˈmætɪk/| adj.|变色的；感光变色的|Changing color in response to light; relating to photochromism.  Photochromatic lenses darken in bright sunlight and lighten in dim light.|Example:  The photochromatic lenses in my sunglasses automatically adjust to changing light conditions. 我的太阳镜的感光变色镜片会自动适应变化的光照条件。

The photochromatic paint changed color when exposed to ultraviolet light.  这种感光变色涂料在紫外线下会改变颜色。|近义词：photosensitive, light-sensitive|反义词：colorfast, non-photosensitive|
