corbelled:/ˈkɔːrbəld/| adj.| 拱形砌筑的；有拱形支撑的 |  Having a corbel; constructed with corbels.  Corbelled construction is a method of building where stones or bricks are progressively projected outward to form a support for a structure above, often used in arches or vaults. | 例句：The ancient bridge was corbelled, showcasing impressive engineering skills.  这座古桥是拱形砌筑的，展现了令人印象深刻的工程技术。


The Mayan temple featured a striking corbelled vault. 玛雅神庙有一个引人注目的拱形穹顶。| 近义词：arched, vaulted | 反义词：straight, unsupported
