cyanotype:/ˈsaɪənəʊtaɪp/ | n. | 青图，蓝晒图 | A photographic printing process using light-sensitive iron salts that produces a cyan-blue print.  The process is known for its unique blue and white aesthetic and is often used in art and architectural blueprints. | Example:  She created a beautiful cyanotype of the wildflowers. 她创作了一幅美丽的野花青图。  The architect showed us the cyanotype plans for the new building. 建筑师向我们展示了新建筑的青图。 | 近义词：blueprint (in the context of architectural plans) | 反义词：None directly applicable.
