describing:/dɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/ | v. | 描绘；描述 |  The act of giving a detailed account of something; representing something in words or images. | Example: She was describing the beautiful scenery she saw during her trip. 她正在描述她在旅行中看到的美丽风景。 |近义词：portraying, depicting, narrating | 反义词：obscuring, concealing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is describing the painting to the class. 他正在向全班同学描述这幅画。
She describes her childhood as happy. 她把她的童年描述成快乐的。


一般过去时:
The witness described the suspect accurately. 目击者准确地描述了嫌疑人。
He described the accident in detail. 他详细描述了事故经过。


现在完成时:
She has described the process several times. 她已经多次描述了这个过程。
They have described the new policy in the report. 他们在报告中描述了这项新政策。


过去完成时:
He had described the situation before the meeting. 他在会议之前已经描述了这种情况。
She had described her feelings to her therapist. 她已经向她的治疗师描述了她的感受。


将来时:
He will describe his research findings at the conference. 他将在会议上描述他的研究结果。
She will describe the new product to the potential clients. 她将向潜在客户描述新产品。


虚拟语气:
If he described the problem clearly, they would understand. 如果他清楚地描述了问题，他们就会理解。
I wish he had described the instructions more carefully. 我希望他当时能更仔细地描述说明。


被动语态:
The scene was described vividly in the novel. 小说中对场景的描写非常生动。
The experiment was described in the scientific paper. 实验过程在科学论文中有所描述。


疑问句:
How would you describe your work style? 你会如何描述你的工作风格？
Can you describe what happened? 你能描述一下发生了什么吗？


否定句:
He didn't describe the incident properly. 他没有恰当地描述这一事件。
She couldn't describe the attacker's face. 她无法描述袭击者的脸。


PHRASE->
self-describing: 自描述的
vividly describing: 生动地描述
accurately describing: 准确地描述
briefly describing: 简要地描述
fully describing: 全面地描述
