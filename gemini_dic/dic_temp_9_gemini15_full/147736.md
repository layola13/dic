overprocesses:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːsɛsɪz/ | v. |过度处理；流程过多 |To subject to excessive or unnecessary processing.  This often refers to data, information, or tasks, where more steps are taken than are necessary or efficient.  The term can also imply a bureaucratic or inefficient approach. | Example:  The company overprocesses its purchase orders, leading to delays and increased costs.  公司过度处理采购订单，导致延误和成本增加。The system overprocesses the data, making the analysis unnecessarily complex. 系统过度处理数据，使分析变得不必要地复杂。|近义词：overwork, overcomplicate, overanalyze |反义词：streamline, simplify, optimize |动词
