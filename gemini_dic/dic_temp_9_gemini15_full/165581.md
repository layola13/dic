premeditately:/ˌpriːˈmɛdɪteɪtli/| adv. |预谋地，蓄意地 | done in a deliberate and intentional way, often with malice aforethought.  It implies planning and consideration before the action. |例句：He premeditately destroyed the evidence. 他蓄意销毁了证据。

例句：She premeditately lied to the police. 她蓄意向警方撒谎。


近义词：intentionally, deliberately, purposely, designedly, aforethought

反义词：accidentally, unintentionally, inadvertently, spontaneously


词性：副词
