labeler:/ˈleɪbələr/ | n. |标签员，贴标签者 | A person who labels things, especially in a factory or warehouse.  | Example: The labeler carefully affixed the price tags to each item. 标签员仔细地将价格标签贴到每件商品上。  |近义词：tagger, marker | 反义词：None


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The labeler labels hundreds of boxes every day.  = 标签员每天贴标签数百个箱子。
She labels each product with a unique code. = 她用独特的代码标记每个产品。


一般过去时:
The labeler labeled all the jars yesterday. = 标签员昨天贴好了所有罐子的标签。
He labeled the boxes incorrectly. = 他把箱子贴错了标签。


现在完成时:
The labeler has labeled all the shipments this week. = 标签员本周已经贴好了所有货物的标签。
She has labeled over a thousand items. = 她已经贴了超过一千件商品的标签。


过去完成时:
The labeler had labeled all the products before the deadline. = 标签员在截止日期前已贴好所有产品的标签。
They had labeled the boxes before shipping them. = 他们在发货前已经贴好了箱子的标签。


将来时:
The labeler will label the new products tomorrow. = 标签员明天将贴上新产品的标签。
He will have labeled all the boxes by the end of the day. = 他将在今天结束前贴好所有箱子的标签。


虚拟语气:
If the labeler were faster, we would finish earlier. = 如果标签员更快，我们会早点完成。
I wish the labeler had been more careful. = 我希望标签员更仔细一些。


被动语态:
The boxes are labeled by the labeler. = 箱子由标签员贴标签。
The products are being labeled efficiently. = 产品正在高效地贴标签。


疑问句:
Is the labeler working today? = 标签员今天上班吗？
Has the labeler finished labeling the items? = 标签员贴完标签了吗？


否定句:
The labeler didn't label the boxes correctly. = 标签员没有正确地贴箱子标签。
She doesn't label the bottles by hand anymore. = 她不再手工贴瓶子标签了。
