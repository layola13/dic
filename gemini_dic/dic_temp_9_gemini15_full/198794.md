sicks:/sɪks/| v.|使恶心；使厌恶；使讨厌|to make someone feel sick or disgusted.  It can also refer to making something unpleasant or annoying.|例句：The smell of the rotting fish sickened me.  腐烂鱼的臭味使我作呕。|例句：The constant complaining sickens me.  没完没了的抱怨让我厌烦。|近义词：disgust, nauseate, repulse|反义词：delight, please


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The smell of garbage sickens me. = 垃圾的气味让我恶心。
That noise sickens her. = 那个噪音让她厌烦。


一般过去时:
The movie sickened the audience. = 这部电影让观众感到恶心。
The news sickened him. = 这个消息让他感到厌恶。


现在完成时:
The constant negativity has sickened me. = 持续的消极情绪让我厌烦了。
This situation has sickened many people. = 这种情况让很多人感到厌恶。


过去完成时:
He had sickened of the endless meetings. = 他对没完没了的会议感到厌倦了。
She had sickened of his constant lies. = 她对他不断的谎言感到厌恶了。


将来时:

否定句:
