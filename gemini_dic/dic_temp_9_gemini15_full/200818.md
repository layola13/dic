sliding:/ˈslaɪdɪŋ/ | adj. v. | 滑动的；滑动；|  Moving smoothly along a surface.  | 例句：The children were sliding down the grassy hill. 孩子们正从草坡上滑下来。| 例句：The drawer slides open easily. 抽屉很容易滑开。|近义词：gliding, slipping, sliding | 反义词：static, stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The box slides smoothly. = 箱子滑得很顺畅。
He slides the window open. = 他把窗户滑开了。


一般过去时:
The car slid on the ice. = 汽车在冰上打滑了。
She slid into the seat. = 她滑进了座位。


现在完成时:
The price has slid downwards. = 价格一直在下跌。
He has slid into bad habits. = 他养成了坏习惯。


过去完成时:
The boat had slid silently into the harbor. = 船悄无声息地滑进了港口。
They had slid into a comfortable routine. = 他们已经陷入了一种舒适的常规。


将来时:
The drawer will slide open easily. = 抽屉将会很容易滑开。
The snow will slide off the roof. = 雪会从屋顶滑落。


虚拟语气:
If the ice were thicker, it wouldn't slide so easily. = 如果冰更厚一些，它就不会这么容易滑了。
I wish the door would slide open more smoothly. = 我希望门能更顺畅地滑开。


被动语态:
The boat was slid into the water. = 船被滑入水中。
The window is slid open. = 窗户被滑开了。


疑问句:
Does the door slide open? = 门是滑开的吗？
How does this mechanism slide? = 这个机制是如何滑动的？


否定句:
The drawer doesn't slide easily. = 抽屉不容易滑开。
The plan didn't slide into place. = 计划没有顺利进行。


PHRASE->
sliding scale: 滑动标尺

sliding window: 滑动窗口
sliding friction: 滑动摩擦
