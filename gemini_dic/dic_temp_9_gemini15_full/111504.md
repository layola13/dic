loaminess:/ˈloʊminəs/| n. |壤土性，肥沃性 |The quality or state of being loamy;  a soil rich in humus and having a good balance of sand, silt, and clay.  It is ideal for plant growth because of its good drainage and water retention. |The loaminess of the soil contributed to the abundance of the harvest. 这块土壤的壤土性使得收成十分丰富。 The garden soil lacked loaminess, requiring amendment with compost. 花园土壤缺乏壤土性，需要用堆肥改良。|richness, fertility|　sandy, clayey|名词
