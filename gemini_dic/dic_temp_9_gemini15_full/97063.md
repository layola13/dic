inferior:/ɪnˈfɪəriər/| adj.|劣等的；下级的；次等的|Of lower rank, status, or quality.  It implies a comparison and suggests something is not as good as something else.|The inferior quality of the materials led to the product's failure.  劣质材料导致产品失败。The inferior officer obeyed his superior's orders.  下级军官服从了上级军官的命令。|superior, lesser, subordinate|superior, better, excellent


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The inferior product is less expensive. = 劣质产品比较便宜。
This model is inferior to the other. = 这种型号不如另一种。


一般过去时:
The old system was inferior to the new one. = 旧系统不如新系统。
His performance was inferior to his previous attempts. = 他的表现不如他之前的尝试。


现在完成时:
The team has consistently performed inferior to expectations. = 这支球队一直表现不如预期。
The results have proven inferior to the projections. = 结果证明不如预期。


过去完成时:
The previous design had been inferior to the current one. = 之前的设计不如现在的设计。
They had realized their strategy was inferior to their opponent's. = 他们意识到他们的策略不如对手的策略。


将来时:
Future models will likely be superior to this inferior version. = 未来型号可能会比这个劣质版本更好。
The new technology will render this system inferior. = 新技术将使这个系统变得落后。


虚拟语气:
If the materials were not inferior, the product would be better. = 如果材料不是劣质的，产品就会更好。
I wish the quality was not inferior. = 我希望质量不是劣等的。


被动语态:
The inferior product was quickly removed from the shelves. = 劣质产品很快就被下架了。
This design was deemed inferior to the competitor's. = 这个设计被认为不如竞争对手的设计。


疑问句:
Is this material inferior to the other? = 这种材料不如另一种吗？
Are the results inferior due to poor planning? = 结果之所以较差是因为计划不周吗？


否定句:
The quality is not inferior; it's just different. = 质量并非劣等；只是有所不同。
The performance was not inferior, but rather average. = 表现并非劣等，而是中等水平。
