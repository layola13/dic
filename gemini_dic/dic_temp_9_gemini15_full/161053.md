postconnubial: /ˌpoʊstˈkɑːnjuːbiəl/ | adj. |婚后的，结婚后的| Relating to the period after marriage.  It describes the state, events, or experiences that occur after a marriage has taken place.|

例句：
Postconnubial bliss is often short-lived for some couples. = 一些夫妇的婚后幸福往往短暂。

The study examined postconnubial adjustment among newlyweds. = 该研究考察了新婚夫妇的婚后适应情况。


近义词：marital (referring to marriage in general), postnuptial


反义词：prenuptial (before marriage)


词性:形容词
