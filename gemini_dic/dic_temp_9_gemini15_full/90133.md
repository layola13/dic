hotbedded:/ˈhɑːtˌbɛdɪd/ | adj. |  热点，温床，滋生地 |  A hotbed is a place where something, especially something undesirable, develops rapidly.  It implies a concentrated area of activity or growth, often negative. |  The city became a hotbed of political unrest. 这个城市变成了政治动乱的温床。| The swamp was a hotbed of disease. 这片沼泽是疾病的温床。 |fertile ground, breeding ground, incubator | sterile, barren, unproductive | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The city is a hotbed of crime. = 这个城市是犯罪的温床。
This region is a hotbed of innovation. = 这个地区是创新的温床。


一般过去时:
The school was a hotbed of gossip. = 这所学校是流言蜚语的温床。
That area was a hotbed of revolutionary activity. = 那个地区是革命活动的温床。


现在完成时:
The internet has become a hotbed of misinformation. = 互联网已经成为错误信息的温床。
This organization has been a hotbed of corruption for years. = 这个组织多年来一直是腐败的温床。


过去完成时:
The factory had been a hotbed of industrial accidents before safety regulations were implemented. = 在实施安全条例之前，这家工厂一直是工业事故的温床。
The village had been a hotbed of religious fervor for centuries. = 这个村庄几个世纪以来一直是宗教狂热的温床。


将来时:
This new policy will likely create a hotbed of controversy. =这项新政策可能会引发一场激烈的争议。
That area could become a hotbed of development in the near future. = 那个地区在不久的将来可能会成为发展的温床。


虚拟语气:
If the government didn't intervene, the country would become a hotbed of violence. = 如果政府不干预，这个国家将会成为暴力的温床。
If only we had realized earlier, we could have prevented this from becoming a hotbed of discontent. = 如果我们早点意识到，我们就能阻止这种情况成为不满的温床。


被动语态:
The region was considered a hotbed of rebellion. = 该地区被认为是叛乱的温床。
The conference was described as a hotbed of new ideas. = 会议被描述为新思想的温床。


疑问句:
Is this neighborhood a hotbed of crime? = 这个街区是犯罪的温床吗？
Could this company be a hotbed of unethical practices? = 这家公司可能是违反道德行为的温床吗？


否定句:
This area is not a hotbed of activity. = 这个地区不是一个活跃的地区。
The project was not a hotbed of innovation as initially hoped. = 该项目不像最初希望的那样是创新的温床。
