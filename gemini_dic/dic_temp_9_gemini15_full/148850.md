overwatch:/ˈoʊvərwɑːtʃ/ | n. |监视，监督；警戒；瞭望；（游戏名）守望先锋 |  The act of watching over something or someone; surveillance;  a video game. |例句：The police are conducting an overwatch on the suspect’s house.  警方正在对嫌疑人的住所进行监视。 |例句：Overwatch is a popular team-based first-person shooter game.  守望先锋是一款流行的团队合作第一人称射击游戏。|近义词：supervision, surveillance, monitoring, observation |反义词：neglect, disregard |名词
