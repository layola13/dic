dibbler:/ˈdɪblər/| n. |小动物；种植工具|A small animal, especially a small insectivorous mammal, or a tool used for planting.  |例句：The dibbler is a small, nocturnal marsupial found in Western Australia.  = 袋狸是一种生活在西澳大利亚的小型夜行动物。  |例句：He used a dibbler to plant the seedlings. = 他用种植工具种植幼苗。|近义词：planter (for the tool), shrew, bandicoot (for the animal)|反义词：None directly applicable.|名词
