词源:
From "dermat-" (skin) + "-ologist" (specialist).  "Dermat-" comes from the Greek word "δέρμα" (derma), meaning "skin," and "-ologist" is from the Greek "-λογος" (-logos), meaning "study of" or "specialist in."
