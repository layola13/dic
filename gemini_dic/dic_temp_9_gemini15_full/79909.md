gnaws:/nɔːz/| vt.| 啃，咬，折磨，使苦恼 |To bite or chew on something persistently.|The dog gnawed on the bone.  这只狗啃着骨头。|The problem gnawed at his conscience.  这个问题折磨着他的良心。|近义词：chew, bite, nibble, worry, torment |反义词：soothe, comfort


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rat gnaws on the wood. = 老鼠啃木头。
He gnaws his nails nervously. = 他紧张地啃指甲。

一般过去时:
The dog gnawed the rope. = 狗咬断了绳子。
She gnawed on her lip thoughtfully. = 她若有所思地咬着嘴唇。

现在完成时:
The mice have gnawed through the wires. = 老鼠已经咬断了电线。
She has gnawed at the problem for weeks. = 她已经苦思这个问题好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
The termites had gnawed away the support beams. = 白蚁已经蛀空了支撑梁。
He had gnawed at his guilt for years. = 他多年来一直被内疚折磨着。

将来时:
The beaver will gnaw down that tree. = 海狸会咬倒那棵树。
I think this worry will gnaw at me for a long time. = 我想这个担忧会困扰我很久。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the truth, I wouldn't have gnawed at my uncertainties. = 如果我知道真相，我就不会一直焦虑不安了。
If only I hadn't gnawed on that issue, I could have slept better last night. = 要是我没一直纠结这个问题就好了，昨晚我就能睡得更好。


被动语态:
The wood was gnawed by the mice. = 木头被老鼠啃了。
His conscience was gnawed by guilt. = 他的良心被内疚折磨着。


疑问句:
Is the dog gnawing on something again? = 狗又在啃什么东西吗？
What is gnawing at you? = 你有什么烦心事？


否定句:
The dog didn't gnaw on the furniture. = 狗没有啃家具。
The problem didn't gnaw at him as much as he thought it would. = 这个问题并没有像他想象的那样困扰他。


PHRASE->
gnaw at:  折磨，困扰
gnaw on: 啃咬
gnaw through: 咬穿
gnaw away at: 逐渐破坏
gnawing doubt:  挥之不去的怀疑
