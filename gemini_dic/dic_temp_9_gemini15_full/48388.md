dedecorate:/ˌdiːˈdɛkəreɪt/| vt.| 去除装饰；除去…的装饰 | To remove the decorations from something.  |例句：They dedecorated the Christmas tree after the holidays.  他们假期结束后拆掉了圣诞树上的装饰。|近义词：strip, undecorate, unadorn |反义词：decorate, adorn
