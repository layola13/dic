doubted:/ˈdaʊtɪd/| v.|怀疑，不相信|To have or express doubt about (something).  To feel uncertain about.|例句：I doubted his story. 我怀疑他的话。

例句：She doubted whether he would come. 她怀疑他是否会来。


近义词：questioned, suspected, mistrusted, wondered

反义词：believed, trusted, accepted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I doubt his honesty. = 我怀疑他的诚实。
They doubt the accuracy of the report. = 他们怀疑报告的准确性。

一般过去时:
I doubted his ability to finish the project on time. = 我怀疑他能否按时完成项目。
She doubted the wisdom of her decision. = 她怀疑自己决定的明智性。


现在完成时:
I have doubted his intentions since the beginning. = 从一开始我就怀疑他的意图。
They have doubted the effectiveness of the new policy. = 他们一直怀疑新政策的有效性。

过去完成时:
I had doubted his sincerity before he confessed. = 在他坦白之前，我就怀疑他的真诚。
We had doubted the outcome of the election until the final results were announced. = 直到最终结果公布，我们才对选举结果产生怀疑。


将来时:
I will doubt his claims until I see proof. = 在看到证据之前，我会怀疑他的说法。
They will doubt the feasibility of the plan without further investigation. = 没有进一步调查，他们就会怀疑这项计划的可行性。


虚拟语气:
I would doubt his word if I were you. = 如果我是你，我会怀疑他的话。
If I had doubted his motives earlier, I would have acted differently. = 如果我早点怀疑他的动机，我会采取不同的行动。


被动语态:
His honesty was doubted by many. = 许多人怀疑他的诚实。
The validity of the study was doubted by some experts. = 一些专家对这项研究的有效性表示怀疑。


疑问句:
Do you doubt my ability? = 你怀疑我的能力吗？
Does she doubt the truth of the rumor? = 她怀疑谣言的真实性吗？


否定句:
I do not doubt his loyalty. = 我不怀疑他的忠诚。
She did not doubt his innocence. = 她不怀疑他的清白。


PHRASE->
doubt about = 对…的怀疑
cast doubt on = 对…表示怀疑
no doubt = 毫无疑问
beyond doubt = 毫无疑问地
in doubt = 令人怀疑
