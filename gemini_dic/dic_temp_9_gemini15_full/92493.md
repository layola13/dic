hypothenar:/ˌhaɪpəˈθiːnɑːr/| n. |小鱼际肌|The hypothenar refers to the fleshy mound on the ulnar side of the palm of the hand, formed by the hypothenar muscles.  It is the region opposite the thenar eminence (the thumb side).|The hypothenar muscles help with hand movements and gripping.  The hypothenar eminence is often used as a reference point in anatomical studies.|近义词:ulnar eminence|反义词:thenar
