/ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪks/ | n. |形而上学 |The branch of philosophy that deals with the fundamental nature of reality and existence, including concepts such as being, knowing, identity, time, and space.  It explores questions beyond the physical world, often concerning the relationship between mind and matter, and the nature of consciousness. |Metaphysics explores the fundamental nature of reality. 形而上学探讨现实的基本本质。|Cosmology, ontology, theology |Physics, empiricism |名词
