hypothecation: /ˌhaɪpəθɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |抵押；质押；设定抵押 | The act of pledging something as security for a loan or debt, without giving up possession of it.  This involves using an asset as collateral without transferring ownership.  | Example: The company used its inventory as collateral through hypothecation to secure a loan.  这家公司通过抵押其库存商品来获得贷款。

Example: The bank accepted the hypothecation of the ship as security for the loan.  银行接受将船只抵押作为贷款担保。 | 近义词：pledge, security, collateralization | 反义词：release, discharge
