commemorate:/kəˈmɛməreɪt/ | vt. | 纪念；庆祝；缅怀 | To remember and celebrate an important event or person, especially with a ceremony.  | 例句：We will commemorate the 50th anniversary of the company's founding. 我们将纪念公司成立50周年。

例句：The city held a ceremony to commemorate the victims of the earthquake.  城市举行仪式纪念地震中的遇难者。


近义词：celebrate, remember, honor, observe | 反义词：forget, ignore, neglect | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They commemorate the fallen soldiers every year. = 他们每年都纪念阵亡的士兵。
We commemorate the signing of the peace treaty annually. = 我们每年都纪念和平条约的签署。


一般过去时:
The nation commemorated the 100th anniversary of its independence. = 这个国家纪念了独立100周年。
They commemorated the event with a memorial service. = 他们用追悼会来纪念这一事件。


现在完成时:
The museum has commemorated the artist's life and work through a special exhibition. = 博物馆通过一个特别展览纪念了这位艺术家的生平和作品。
We have commemorated the victims of the war with a monument. = 我们用一座纪念碑来纪念战争的受害者。


过去完成时:
They had commemorated the battle before the new museum opened. = 新博物馆开放之前，他们已经纪念了那场战役。
The town had commemorated the anniversary quietly before the public ceremony. = 在公众仪式之前，小镇已经默默地纪念了周年纪念日。


将来时:
We will commemorate the event with a special dinner. = 我们将以一顿特别的晚餐来纪念这个事件。
The school will commemorate the graduation of the class of 2024. = 学校将纪念2024届毕业生。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would commemorate the occasion properly. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们将妥善纪念这个场合。
I wish we could commemorate our history more effectively. = 我希望我们能够更有效地纪念我们的历史。


被动语态:
The anniversary was commemorated with a grand parade. = 周年纪念日以盛大的游行庆祝。
The fallen soldiers are commemorated every year with a wreath-laying ceremony. = 阵亡的士兵每年都以献花圈仪式来纪念。


疑问句:
How will the event be commemorated? = 这个事件将如何纪念？
How did they commemorate the victory? = 他们是如何纪念胜利的？


否定句:
They did not commemorate the event properly. = 他们没有妥善纪念这个事件。
The government failed to commemorate the victims' sacrifices. = 政府未能纪念受害者的牺牲。

词源:
来自拉丁语 *commemorare，由com-（一起）和memorare（记住）构成。
