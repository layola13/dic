dashikis:/ˈdæʃɪkiːz/| n. |达希基（非洲长袍）|A dashiki is a loose-fitting, often brightly colored, tunic-like garment traditionally worn by men in West Africa.  It typically features embroidery or other decorative details.  The term is now also used more broadly to refer to similar garments worn by people of African descent worldwide. |He wore a beautiful dashiki to the cultural festival. 他在文化节上穿了一件美丽的达希基。  She gifted her grandfather a hand-embroidered dashiki for his birthday. 她送给她祖父一件手工刺绣的达希基作为生日礼物。|  shirt, tunic, robe |  (no direct antonyms, as it describes a specific type of garment)
