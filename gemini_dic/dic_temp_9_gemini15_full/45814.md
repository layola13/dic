customable: /ˈkʌstəməbl/ | adj. | 可定制的，可订做的 | Capable of being customized or made to order; able to be adapted to individual needs or preferences.  |  例句：The software is customable to fit the needs of different users.  这个软件可以定制以满足不同用户的需求。

例句：This furniture is customable; you can choose the fabric and the legs. 这款家具是可定制的；您可以选择面料和腿部样式。


近义词：adaptable, customizable, modifiable, adjustable


反义词：standard, fixed, inflexible


词性：形容词
