improbation:/ˌɪm.prəˈbeɪ.ʃən/| n. |不赞成；反对；非难|The expression of disapproval or condemnation; the act of disapproving.  It often implies a strong feeling of dislike or opposition. |例句：His imprecation was met with the improbation of the entire assembly. 他的咒骂遭到了全体与会人员的反对。  The improbation of his peers led him to reconsider his actions. 同行的反对使他重新考虑了自己的行为。|近义词：disapproval, condemnation, censure, reprobation|反义词：approval, acceptance, commendation
