non-prescription: /ˌnɒnprɪˈskrɪpʃən/| adj. |非处方药的；无需处方|Not requiring a prescription from a doctor or other licensed medical professional.  It refers to medications, drugs, or other medical treatments that can be bought without a prescription. |  Example:  Many painkillers are available over-the-counter as non-prescription medications. 许多止痛药作为非处方药可以买到。


Example:  You can buy non-prescription cold medicine at any pharmacy. 你可以在任何药店买到非处方感冒药。|近义词：over-the-counter (OTC),  without prescription |反义词：prescription, requiring a prescription |词性：形容词
