/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɒlvz/ | v. |过度参与；卷入过多 |To become excessively involved in something; to involve oneself too much in something.  The term suggests a negative connotation, implying an unnecessary or detrimental level of involvement. | Example: He overinvolves himself in his children's lives, hindering their independence. 他过度参与孩子的生活，妨碍了他们的独立性。  She overinvolved herself in the office politics, causing unnecessary stress. 她过度卷入办公室政治，造成了不必要的压力。|近义词：interfere, meddle, intrude |反义词：detach, disengage, ignore
