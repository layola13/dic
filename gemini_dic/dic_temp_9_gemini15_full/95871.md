incrassation:/ˌɪnkræˈseɪʃən/| n. |增厚；肥大；加粗|The thickening or enlargement of a part or organ, often due to an increase in the size of its cells.  It often refers to the thickening of a plant stem or organ, but can also be used in other contexts such as the thickening of blood vessels. | Example: The incrassation of the plant stem provided additional support. 植物茎杆的增厚提供了额外的支撑。The incrassation of the artery wall contributed to the heart attack. 动脉壁的增厚导致了心脏病发作。|近义词：thickening, enlargement, hypertrophy |反义词：thinning, atrophy, reduction | 名词
