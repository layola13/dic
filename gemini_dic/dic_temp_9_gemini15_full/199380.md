simple:/ˈsɪmpəl/ | adj. |简单的，简易的，朴素的 |easy to understand or do; not complicated; plain in style or appearance.  |例句：This is a simple problem.  这个题目很简单。|例句：She wore a simple dress. 她穿着一件朴素的衣服。|近义词：easy, uncomplicated, straightforward, plain |反义词：complex, complicated, intricate, elaborate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The instructions are simple. = 说明很简单。
This task is simple to complete. = 这个任务很容易完成。

一般过去时:

词源:
The word "simple" comes from the Old French word "simple," meaning "plain, unadorned," which itself derives from the Latin word "simplex," meaning "single, onefold, unmixed, plain, artless."  The Latin word is composed of "sim," meaning "one," and "plex," meaning "fold."
