double-chinned:/ˈdʌbəl tʃɪnd/| adj.| 双下巴的 | Having a double chin.  A double chin is an excess of fat under the chin that creates a second chin.  This term is typically used to describe a person's physical appearance. |例句：The woman in the picture is double-chinned. 图片中的那位女士有双下巴。

The overweight man was double-chinned.  那个超重的男人有双下巴。|近义词：chubby, jowly |反义词：slim-faced,  defined jawline
