have-no-words-for: /ˌhæv ˈnoʊ wɜːrdz fɔːr/ | adj. phrase |  难以言表，无法形容 |  Expressing a feeling or experience so intense that words are inadequate to describe it. It's used to convey overwhelming emotion, typically positive or negative.  | 例句：I have no words for the beauty of the sunset.  我无法用语言形容日落的美丽。


I have no words for how grateful I am. 我无法表达我有多感激。


近义词：speechless, beyond description,indescribable, overwhelmed, awestruck


反义词：articulate, easily described, expressible

词性：形容词短语 (adjective phrase)
