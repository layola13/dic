retaliations:/ˌrɪtæliˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 报复；反击 | Acts of retaliation are actions taken in response to a previous harmful action, often with the intention of inflicting similar harm.  Retaliations can range from individual acts of revenge to large-scale military conflicts. | Example: The country launched retaliations against its neighbor after a border incursion. 这个国家在边界入侵后对邻国进行了报复。The company faced several retaliations after firing employees for union organizing. 公司在解雇参与工会组织的员工后面临多次报复。|近义词：revenge, reprisal, counterattack |反义词：forgiveness, reconciliation |名词
