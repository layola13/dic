reformulate:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmjuleɪt/ | vt. |重新表述；改写；重新制定 |To express something in a new or different way; to restructure or reorganize something.  |例句：The scientist reformulated his hypothesis after the new data emerged. = 科学家在新的数据出现后重新表述了他的假设。

例句：The company reformulated its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. = 公司重新制定了其营销策略以瞄准更年轻的人群。


近义词：rephrase, restate, revise, restructure, redefine | 反义词：maintain, keep, preserve | 词性：动词
