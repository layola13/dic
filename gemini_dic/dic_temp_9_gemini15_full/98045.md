词源:

* **in-**:  prefix indicating negation or reversal (不，无)
* **oxid-**:  from "oxygen," referring to oxidation (氧化)
* **-izable**: suffix meaning "capable of being" (可…的)

Therefore, "inoxidizable" literally means "not capable of being oxidized."
