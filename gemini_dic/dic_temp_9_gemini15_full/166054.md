preply:/ˈpriːpliː/ | n. |预先回复；提前回复| A preply is a reply sent before the expected time of response, often in anticipation of a question or request.  It's common in online communication and customer service. |Example:  I sent a preply to the customer's inquiry to assure them I'd get back to them fully soon. 我提前回复了客户的询问，以确保我很快会完整地回复他们。|近义词：advance reply, anticipatory response|反义词：delayed reply, late response|名词
