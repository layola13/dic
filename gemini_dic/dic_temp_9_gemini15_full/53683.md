disciplinarily: /ˌdɪsɪˈplɪnərili/ | adv. | 纪律上地；根据纪律地 | In a manner relating to discipline; according to the rules of discipline.  | 例句：He was dealt with disciplinarily for his misconduct. 他因行为不端而受到纪律处分。

The employee was reprimanded disciplinarily for violating company policy.  这位员工因违反公司政策而受到纪律上的谴责。


近义词：officially, formally, by way of discipline


反义词：informally, casually, unofficially


词性：副词
