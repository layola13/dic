satirise:/ˈsætəraɪz/| vt.|讽刺，挖苦|To ridicule or make fun of (someone or something) in a humorous or satirical way, often to expose or criticize their flaws.|例句：The cartoon satirised the politician's recent gaffe. 这幅漫画讽刺了这位政治家最近的失误。|例句：The writer satirised the hypocrisy of the ruling class.  这位作家讽刺了统治阶级的虚伪。|近义词：mock, lampoon, parody, caricature|反义词：praise, commend, flatter|动词
