guildhall:/ˈɡɪldhɔːl/| n.|行会大厅；市政厅|A guildhall is a large building, usually in a town or city, that was originally used for meetings of a guild (a medieval association of craftsmen or merchants).  Today, it often serves as a town hall or civic center.  |Example: The city's guildhall hosted a grand celebration for the anniversary.  这座城市的公会大厅为周年庆典举办了一场盛大的庆祝活动。|近义词：town hall, municipal building, civic center|反义词：private residence, small building|名词
