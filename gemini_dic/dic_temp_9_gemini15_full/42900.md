cousinhood:/ˈkʌzɪnhʊd/| n. |表亲关系；族亲关系；家族关系|The state or quality of being cousins; the collective body of one's cousins.  It refers to the kinship network formed by cousins and their interactions. |例句：The strong cousinhood within the family ensured close ties across generations.  家族内部强大的表亲关系确保了世代之间的紧密联系。
例句：He valued the sense of community fostered by the close cousinhood of his extended family. 他珍视大家庭中亲密的表亲关系所培养的社区意识。|近义词：kinship, family ties, clan, relatives|反义词：estrangement, isolation
