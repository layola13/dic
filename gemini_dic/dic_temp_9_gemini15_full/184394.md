responder:/rɪˈspɑːndər/| n. |回应者，响应者，应答者 | A person or thing that responds.  Someone or something that reacts to a stimulus or event. |Example: The first responders arrived at the scene of the accident within minutes.  第一批救援人员在几分钟内到达了事故现场。 |近义词：reactor, answerer, respondent |反义词：initiator, instigator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fire alarm responder checks the system regularly. = 消防报警器响应器定期检查系统。
She responds to emails promptly. = 她及时回复邮件。


一般过去时:
He responded to the question with a smile. = 他笑着回答了这个问题。
The sensor responded to the change in temperature. = 传感器对温度变化做出了反应。


现在完成时:
The company has responded to customer complaints effectively. = 公司有效地回应了客户投诉。
They have responded positively to the new policy. = 他们对新政策做出了积极的回应。


过去完成时:
She had responded to the email before leaving the office. = 她在离开办公室之前已经回复了邮件。
The government had responded to the crisis with a new plan. = 政府已经用一个新的计划来应对危机。


将来时:
He will respond to the survey by the end of the week. = 他将在本周末之前回复调查问卷。
The system will respond automatically to any threat. = 系统将自动应对任何威胁。


虚拟语气:
If he responded honestly, he would have avoided the problem. = 如果他诚实地回答，他就能避免这个问题。
I wish she had responded more quickly to my message. = 我希望她能更快地回复我的信息。


被动语态:
The email was responded to promptly. = 邮件得到了及时的回复。
The request was responded to within 24 hours. = 请求在24小时内得到了回复。


疑问句:
How did he respond to your request? = 他如何回应你的请求？
Will the system respond to the input correctly? = 系统会正确响应输入吗？


否定句:
He did not respond to my calls. = 他没有回复我的电话。
The device didn't respond to the command. = 设备没有响应命令。


PHRASE->
first responder = 急救人员
emergency responder = 紧急救援人员
responder system = 响应系统
customer responder = 客户回应
responder chain = 响应链
