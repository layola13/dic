nonelectronics: /ˌnɑːnɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪks/ | n. |非电子产品，非电子设备|Things that are not electronic in nature;  the opposite of electronics.  It refers to products, devices, or systems that do not use electricity or electronic components for their primary function. |例句：The museum had a fascinating exhibit on nonelectronics, showcasing antique mechanical clocks and toys.  博物馆有一个关于非电子产品的迷人展览，展示了古老的机械钟表和玩具。


例句：Many people prefer the simplicity and reliability of nonelectronics compared to modern electronics. 许多人更喜欢非电子产品的简单性和可靠性，而不是现代电子产品。


近义词：mechanical devices, non-electronic equipment, analog devices


反义词：electronics, electric devices
