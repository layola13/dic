英文解析:Indole is a bicyclic, aromatic heterocyclic organic compound. It consists of a benzene ring fused to a pyrrole ring.  It's a colorless solid at room temperature, but it can become slightly yellow upon exposure to air and light. It has a strong, fecal-like odor at high concentrations, but at low concentrations it has a sweet, floral fragrance, contributing to the scent of jasmine and other flowers.
