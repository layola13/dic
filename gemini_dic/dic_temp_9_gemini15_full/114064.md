magnetized:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪzd/| adj.|磁化的；有磁性的|Having the properties of a magnet; exhibiting magnetism.|The magnetized needle pointed north. 磁化的指针指向北方。 The iron bar became magnetized after being exposed to a strong magnetic field.  铁棒在暴露于强磁场后被磁化了。|近义词：magnetic, attracted|反义词：demagnetized
