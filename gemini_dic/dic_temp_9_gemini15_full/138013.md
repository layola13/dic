noology:/nuˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 新知识学；新学科 | The study of new knowledge and emerging fields of study.  Noology often focuses on areas that are rapidly evolving or interdisciplinary, bridging gaps between established disciplines.  It emphasizes the process of knowledge creation and its societal impact. |  例句：The professor's research was considered cutting-edge noology, exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and neuroscience.  教授的研究被认为是尖端的新知识学，探索了人工智能和神经科学的交叉点。| 近义词：neology, emerging field studies | 反义词：traditional scholarship
