hookas:/ˈhʊkəz/ | n. | 水烟筒；水烟 | A hookah, also known as a shisha, is a water pipe that is used to smoke flavored tobacco.  It typically consists of a bowl, a shaft, a base filled with water, and a hose. | 例句：He enjoyed smoking a hookah with his friends in the cafe. 他喜欢在咖啡馆和朋友一起抽水烟。

例句：The hookah smoke filled the room with a sweet aroma. 水烟的烟雾充满了房间，散发出甜美的香气。


近义词：shisha, narghile, hubble-bubble | 反义词：无


词源：The word "hookah" comes from the Hindi word "huqqa," which is derived from the Persian word "ḥuqqah."  These words ultimately trace back to the Arabic word "ḥuqqah," which originally referred to a container or vessel. The adoption of the word into English reflects the widespread use and cultural significance of the hookah across various regions.
