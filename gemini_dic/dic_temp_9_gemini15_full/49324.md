delightful: /dɪˈlaɪtfəl/ | adj. |令人愉快的，快乐的，可爱的 |  Giving great pleasure; delightful.  |例句：The weather was delightful; it was sunny and warm. 天气令人愉快；阳光明媚，温暖宜人。

例句：The children were delightful, full of energy and laughter. 孩子们很可爱，充满活力和笑声。


近义词：pleasant, enjoyable, charming, lovely, pleasing | 反义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, awful, terrible


词性：形容词
