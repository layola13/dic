incharity:/ɪnˈtʃærəti/| adj.|慈善的；仁慈的；施舍的 |In charity means showing kindness and compassion, often involving giving to those in need.  It can also refer to an act of charity or a charitable donation.|例句：He gave the money in charity. 他出于慈善捐了钱。|例句：She acted in charity toward the homeless. 她对无家可归的人施以仁慈。|近义词：benevolence, compassion, philanthropy|反义词：cruelty, selfishness, avarice


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They give to charity regularly. = 他们定期捐款行善。
She volunteers at the local charity shop. = 她自愿在当地的慈善商店做志愿者。


一般过去时:
He donated a large sum of money to charity last year. = 他去年向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱。
The company held a fundraising event for charity. = 公司为慈善事业举办了一场筹款活动。


现在完成时:
We have donated many times to this charity. = 我们已经多次向这个慈善机构捐款了。
She has volunteered at the homeless shelter for years. = 她多年来一直在无家可归者收容所做志愿者。


过去完成时:
They had already given to the charity before the appeal was launched. = 在发起募捐活动之前，他们就已经向慈善机构捐款了。
She had volunteered at several charities before settling on this one. = 在选择这家慈善机构之前，她曾在几家慈善机构做过志愿者。


将来时:
We will continue to support this charity in the future. = 我们将来会继续支持这家慈善机构。
They will organize a charity marathon next month. = 他们下个月将组织一场慈善马拉松。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would donate more to charity. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会向慈善机构捐更多款。
I wish I could do more to help charities. = 我希望我能为慈善事业做更多的事情。


被动语态:
Donations to charity are tax deductible. = 向慈善机构的捐款可以抵税。
The event was organized by a local charity. = 此活动由当地一家慈善机构组织。


疑问句:
Do you donate to charity? = 你捐款行善吗？
Which charities do you support? = 你支持哪些慈善机构？


否定句:
He didn't give money to charity. = 他没有向慈善机构捐款。
They don't support that particular charity. = 他们不支持那个特定的慈善机构。
