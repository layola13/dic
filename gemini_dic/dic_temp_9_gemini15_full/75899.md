fundholding: /ˈfʌndˌhoʊldɪŋ/ | n. | 基金持有；资金持有 | The act of holding or owning funds, often referring to the ownership of shares or other financial assets within a fund.  It can also refer to the total amount of funds held by an entity. | Example: The fundholding company manages billions of dollars in assets.  这家基金持有公司管理着数十亿美元的资产。 |近义词：investment, ownership, possession |反义词：fund disposal, liquidation
