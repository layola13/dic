geotracking: /ˈdʒioʊˌtrækiŋ/ | n. | 地理追踪，地理定位追踪 | The process of tracking the location of a person, object, or vehicle using geographic coordinates.  This often involves using GPS, RFID, or other technologies to record and monitor movement. | Example: Geotracking allows delivery companies to provide real-time updates on the location of packages.  地理追踪允许快递公司提供包裹位置的实时更新。 | 近义词：location tracking, GPS tracking, position tracking | 反义词：N/A
