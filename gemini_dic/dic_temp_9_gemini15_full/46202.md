cyrenaic:/sɪˈriːneɪɪk/| adj.|昔兰尼的；享乐主义的；|Of or relating to Cyrene, a Greek colony in North Africa, or its inhabitants; characterized by the philosophy of the Cyrenaics, emphasizing pleasure as the highest good.|The Cyrenaic school of philosophy was known for its hedonistic views.  昔兰尼学派以其享乐主义观点而闻名。|近义词：hedonistic, pleasure-seeking|反义词：ascetic, puritanical|形容词
