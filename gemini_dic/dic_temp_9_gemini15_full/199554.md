single-mindedness:/ˈsɪŋɡəlˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ | n. | 专注；一意孤行 | The unwavering focus on a single goal or purpose.  It can be positive, describing determination and dedication, or negative, suggesting inflexibility and an inability to consider alternative perspectives. | Example:  Her single-mindedness allowed her to overcome many obstacles and achieve her ambitious goal.  她的专注使她克服了许多障碍，并实现了她的宏伟目标。  His single-mindedness prevented him from seeing the flaws in his plan. 他的固执己见使他看不到计划中的缺陷。 | 近义词：determination, focus, concentration, dedication, obsession | 反义词：indecisiveness, vacillation, flexibility, open-mindedness |  词性：名词
