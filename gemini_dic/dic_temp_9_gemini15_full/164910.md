pregeneration: /ˌpriːdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 预先生成，预先产生 | The act or process of generating something beforehand;  creating something in advance.  This often refers to data, resources, or components that are prepared before they are needed, improving efficiency or performance. | 例句：The pregeneration of thumbnails significantly speeds up website loading times. = 预先生成缩略图显著加快了网站加载速度。


The system uses pregeneration of reports to minimize wait times for users. = 系统使用报表预先生成来最大限度地减少用户的等待时间。


近义词：precreation, advance preparation, pre-processing | 反义词：postgeneration, on-demand generation | 词性: 名词
