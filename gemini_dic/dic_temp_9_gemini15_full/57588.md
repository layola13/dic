dramedy:/ˈdræmədi/| n.|喜剧悲剧|A genre of film or television that blends elements of comedy and drama.|例句：The show is a dramedy that explores the complexities of family life. 这部剧是一部喜剧悲剧，探讨了家庭生活的复杂性。  The movie was a dramedy, full of both laughter and tears.这部电影是一部喜剧悲剧，充满了欢笑和泪水。|近义词：comedy-drama, tragicomedy |反义词：pure comedy, pure tragedy
