flotations:/floʊˈteɪʃənz/| n. |浮选；漂浮；发行；上市|The process of separating materials based on their density, often used in mining to separate valuable minerals from waste.  It can also refer to the act of a company's stock becoming publicly traded. |例句：The gold was extracted through a process of flotation.  黄金是通过浮选法提取的。

例句：The company's flotation on the stock market was a huge success. 这家公司在股市的上市非常成功。|近义词：separation, issuance, listing |反义词：sinking, submersion
