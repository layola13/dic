meltabilities:/ˌmɛltəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |熔化能力；可熔性|The ability of a substance to melt.  This word is not commonly used and is often considered a neologism (newly coined word). It implies the capacity or potential for something to melt, perhaps encompassing various factors influencing the melting process.  It might be used in a scientific context or in a creative writing setting. |例句：The meltabilities of different types of cheese were compared in the study.  这项研究比较了不同类型奶酪的熔化能力。 The sculptor explored the surprising meltabilities of the new wax he had discovered. 雕塑家探索了他发现的新蜡的令人惊讶的可熔性。|近义词：fusibility, liquefiability|反义词：infusibility, unmeltability
