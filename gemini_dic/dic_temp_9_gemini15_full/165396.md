prelabel:/ˈpriːleɪbəl/ | adj. | 预先标记的，预先贴标签的 |  Describes something that has been labeled or marked beforehand.  | Example: The prelabeled boxes made packing much faster. 预先贴好标签的箱子使打包快多了。 | 近义词：premarked, pre-tagged | 反义词：unlabeled, unmarked | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The items are prelabeled for easy identification. = 这些物品预先贴好标签，便于识别。
This company prelabels all its products before shipping. = 这家公司在发货前预先贴好所有产品的标签。

一般过去时:
The scientist prelabeled the cells before the experiment. = 科学家在实验前预先标记了细胞。
We prelabeled all the jars with the contents. = 我们预先在所有罐子上贴上了标签，标明了里面的内容。

现在完成时:
They have prelabeled all the packages for delivery. = 他们已经为送货预先贴好了所有包裹的标签。
She has prelabeled the samples to avoid confusion. = 她已经预先贴好样品的标签，以避免混淆。

过去完成时:
He had prelabeled the specimens before the analysis began. = 在分析开始之前，他已经预先标记了样本。
We had prelabeled the boxes so that the sorting process went smoothly. = 我们预先贴好箱子的标签，以便顺利进行分类。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If we had prelabeled the samples, we would have saved time. = 如果我们预先贴好样品的标签，就能节省时间。
