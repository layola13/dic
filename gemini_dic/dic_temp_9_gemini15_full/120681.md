misadjusted:/ˌmɪs əˈdʒʌstɪd/| adj.|调整不当的；失调的；适应不良的|Not properly adjusted; out of adjustment; poorly adapted or integrated.  The term often implies a psychological or emotional imbalance, rather than simply a mechanical malfunction.|例句：His misadjusted childhood led to emotional problems later in life. 他童年时期调整不当，导致后来出现情绪问题。

His misadjusted bicycle brakes caused a near-accident. 他的自行车刹车调整不当，险些造成事故。|近义词：maladjusted, unbalanced, dysfunctional, out of kilter|反义词：adjusted, balanced, well-adjusted, functional
