ransack:/ˈrænsæk/| vt.|洗劫；彻底搜查|To thoroughly search a place, typically in order to steal something. It implies a violent or destructive search.|例句：The thieves ransacked the house, leaving nothing untouched.  盗贼洗劫了房子，什么都没留下。|例句：Police ransacked the suspect's apartment looking for evidence. 警察搜查了嫌疑人的公寓寻找证据。|近义词：pillage, plunder, loot, search thoroughly|反义词：tidy, organize


USAGE->

They ransack the house every day. = 他们每天都翻箱倒柜。
