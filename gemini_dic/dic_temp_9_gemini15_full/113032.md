luminary:/ˈluːmɪneri/| n.|杰出人物；才智之士；灯具|A person who is an expert in a particular field.  A luminary in the field of astrophysics, she published groundbreaking research. 天体物理学领域的杰出人物，她发表了具有开创性的研究成果。|近义词：expert, authority, celebrity, star|反义词：nobody, unknown, insignificant|名词

词源:
