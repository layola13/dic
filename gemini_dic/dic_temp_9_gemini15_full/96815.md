inert: /ɪˈnɜːrt/ | adj. |惰性的；迟钝的；无效的 | Not tending to react readily; sluggish.  Lacking the power to move or act. |例句：The inert gas filled the balloon. 惰性气体充满了气球。
The inert atmosphere prevented oxidation. 惰性气氛防止了氧化。


近义词：passive, inactive, sluggish, dormant, lifeless | 反义词：active, reactive, energetic, dynamic


词源:  From Middle French *inert, from Latin inert-, inertis, present participle of iners "unskillful, weak, inactive," from in- "not" + ars, artis "skill, art."


用法说明:

一般现在时:
The gas remains inert. = 气体保持惰性。
He is inert and unresponsive. = 他迟钝且反应迟缓。

一般过去时:
The mixture remained inert. = 混合物保持惰性。
She was inert after the accident. = 事故后她一动不动。

现在完成时:
The chemical has remained inert. = 化学物质一直保持惰性。
He has been inert throughout the meeting. = 他在整个会议期间都一动不动。

过去完成时:
The reaction had been inert until the catalyst was added. = 直到添加催化剂之前，反应一直处于惰性状态。
The engine had been inert for hours. = 引擎已经停止运转数小时了。


将来时:
The material will remain inert under normal conditions. = 在正常条件下，该材料将保持惰性。
He will likely remain inert in the face of such criticism. = 面对这样的批评，他很可能仍然无动于衷。

虚拟语气:
If the gas were inert, it would not react. = 如果气体是惰性的，它就不会发生反应。
If he had been more inert, he would have avoided danger. = 如果他更迟钝一些，他就能避免危险。


被动语态:
The reaction was considered inert. = 该反应被认为是惰性的。
The process is kept inert by a protective atmosphere. = 通过保护性气氛使过程保持惰性。

疑问句:

Are the employees inert to the changes? = 员工对这些变化无动于衷吗？


否定句:

The students were not inert during the lecture. = 学生们在讲座期间并非无动于衷。
