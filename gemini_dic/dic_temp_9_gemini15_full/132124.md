noncrystalline: /ˌnɒnˈkrɪstəlɪn/ | adj. | 非晶体的；无定形的 | Not having a crystalline structure; amorphous.  Noncrystalline materials lack the long-range order characteristic of crystals. They are often characterized by a disordered arrangement of atoms or molecules. Examples include glass, rubber, and many polymers. | The glass is a noncrystalline solid.  玻璃是一种非晶体固体。| amorphous, glassy | crystalline
