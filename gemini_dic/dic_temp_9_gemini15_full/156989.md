pillboxes:/ˈpɪlbɒksɪz/ | n. |药盒；碉堡；小盒子 |Small containers for holding pills;  also, small fortified structures, typically for defensive purposes.  |例句：She keeps her pills in a small pillbox. 她把药放在一个小药盒里。

例句：The soldiers took shelter in the pillbox during the enemy attack. 士兵们在敌人的袭击中躲进了碉堡。


近义词：medicine case, dispenser, bunker, strongpoint, redoubt |反义词：None specifically.  The antonyms would depend on the context (e.g., open field if referring to a pillbox as a defensive structure).
