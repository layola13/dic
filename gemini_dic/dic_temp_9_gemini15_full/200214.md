skinny-dipping:/ˈskɪniˌdɪpɪŋ/| n. |裸泳|The act of swimming in the nude.  It often implies a playful or illicit activity, often done in a secluded location.|例句：They went skinny-dipping in the lake after dark.  他们在天黑后去湖里裸泳了。|近义词：nude swimming, swimming in the buff|反义词：fully clothed swimming
