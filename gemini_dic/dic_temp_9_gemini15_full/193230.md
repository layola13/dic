see-sawed:/siːsɔːd/ | v. | 摇摆不定；反复变化；波动 |  To move up and down repeatedly like a seesaw; to fluctuate or alternate between two states or conditions. | The stock market see-sawed throughout the day.  股市全天都在波动。| The negotiations see-sawed between hope and despair. 谈判在希望和绝望之间摇摆不定。 | fluctuated, oscillated, swung | remained stable, stayed constant |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pendulum see-saws constantly.  钟摆不断地摇摆。
The political climate see-saws between optimism and pessimism.  政治气候在乐观和悲观之间摇摆不定。


一般过去时:
The children see-sawed on the playground. 孩子们在操场上玩跷跷板。
His emotions see-sawed between joy and sorrow. 他的情绪在喜悦和悲伤之间摇摆不定。


现在完成时:
The price of oil has see-sawed for months.  油价已经波动了好几个月了。
The team's performance has see-sawed all season.  该队的表现整个赛季都起伏不定。


过去完成时:
By the end of the debate, the public opinion had see-sawed several times.  辩论结束时，公众舆论已经反复变化了好几次。
Before the final decision, the committee's vote had see-sawed back and forth. 在最终决定之前，委员会的投票来回摇摆不定。


将来时:
The negotiations will likely see-saw for some time.  谈判可能会持续一段时间。
The weather forecast predicts that the temperature will see-saw over the next few days.  天气预报预测未来几天气温将波动。


虚拟语气:
If the negotiations had not see-sawed so much, we would have reached an agreement sooner. 如果谈判没有如此反复，我们本可以更快达成协议。
I wish the stock market hadn't see-sawed so dramatically last week. 我希望股市上周没有如此剧烈波动。


被动语态:
The result of the election was seen to have been see-sawed until the very last minute.  选举结果直到最后一刻才尘埃落定。 (Note:  Passive voice is less common with this verb.)


疑问句:
Did the prices see-saw throughout the year?  价格全年都在波动吗？
Will the political situation see-saw before the election?  选举前政治局势会摇摆不定吗？


否定句:
The situation did not see-saw; it remained relatively stable.  情况没有波动；它保持相对稳定。
The company's profits did not see-saw; they increased steadily. 该公司的利润没有波动；它们稳步增长。
