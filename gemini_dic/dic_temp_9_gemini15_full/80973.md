graciosities:/ɡreɪʃiˈɒsɪtiz/| n. |优雅的行为；优雅的举止；恩惠；好意 | Acts of grace, courtesy, or kindness; elegant or refined manners.  |例句：His graciosities toward the staff were appreciated. 他对员工的优雅举止受到了好评。
例句：The king showed many graciosities to his subjects. 国王对他的臣民表现出许多恩惠。


近义词：courtesies, kindnesses, favors, amenities


反义词：rude acts, discourtesies, unkindnesses, insults


词性：名词
