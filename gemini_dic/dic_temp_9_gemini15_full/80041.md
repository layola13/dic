go wrong:/ɡoʊ ˈrɒŋ/ |/ɡəʊ ˈrɒŋ/ | v. | 出错，出问题，发生故障 | To malfunction; to develop problems; to become unsuccessful.  |  The engine went wrong halfway through the journey.  引擎在旅途中途出了故障。 | The plan went wrong because of unforeseen circumstances. 计划由于不可预见的情况而失败了。 | succeed, work, function | work correctly, succeed, function properly |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Something always goes wrong when I try to bake a cake. = 当我尝试烘焙蛋糕时，总会出现一些问题。
The printer goes wrong frequently. = 这台打印机经常出故障。

一般过去时:
The experiment went wrong because of a faulty instrument. = 实验由于仪器故障而失败了。
Everything went wrong on our vacation. = 我们假期里一切都出错了。

现在完成时:
What has gone wrong with the computer? = 电脑出了什么问题？
My attempts to fix the car have gone wrong. = 我修理汽车的尝试失败了。

过去完成时:
The project had gone wrong before they even realised it. = 项目在他们意识到之前就已经失败了。
The whole plan had gone wrong because of a simple mistake. = 整个计划都因为一个简单的错误而失败了。

将来时:
If you don't follow the instructions, something could go wrong. = 如果你不按照说明操作，可能会出现问题。
I'm worried that something might go wrong during the operation. = 我担心手术过程中可能会出现问题。

虚拟语气:
If only things hadn't gone wrong, we would have won. = 如果事情没有出错，我们就赢了。
I wish the interview hadn't gone wrong. = 我希望面试没有失败。


被动语态:
The plan was going wrong and nobody knew why. = 计划正在出错，没有人知道为什么。
The deal went wrong, and the company was badly affected. = 这笔交易失败了，公司受到了严重影响。


疑问句:
What went wrong with the car? = 汽车出了什么问题？
Why did the plan go wrong? = 计划为什么失败了？


否定句:
Nothing went wrong during the performance. = 演出过程中没有任何问题。
The mission didn't go wrong, despite the difficult conditions. = 尽管条件艰苦，但任务并没有失败。


PHRASE->
go wrong with = 出问题
go badly wrong = 严重出错
go horribly wrong = 极其糟糕地出错
go spectacularly wrong = 出了大问题
go pear-shaped = 出了岔子 (informal)
