hyperparameters:/ˌhaɪpərˈpærəmiːtərz/ | n. |超参数 |  Parameters that are set before the learning process begins.  They control the learning process itself, rather than being learned from the data.  Choosing appropriate hyperparameters is crucial for the success of machine learning models. | Example:  The learning rate and the number of hidden layers are hyperparameters in a neural network.  超参数例如神经网络中的学习率和隐藏层的数量。|近义词:  tuning parameters, meta-parameters |反义词: model parameters


USAGE->
This word is primarily used in the context of machine learning and does not have verb conjugations or typical grammatical usage like "what".  Therefore, tense and voice conjugations are not applicable.

PHRASE->
hyperparameter optimization: 超参数优化
hyperparameter tuning: 超参数调整
grid search for hyperparameters: 网格搜索超参数
random search for hyperparameters: 随机搜索超参数
Bayesian optimization of hyperparameters: 贝叶斯优化超参数
