half-normally:/ˌhæfˈnɔːrməli/| adj.| 半正态分布的 | Half-normally distributed data follows a probability distribution where the data is only positive and mirrors a normal distribution's positive half. It's often used in statistical modeling where negative values are impossible or nonsensical.  | Example:  The response time data was modeled using a half-normal distribution because negative times are not physically possible. 半正态分布被用来模拟响应时间数据，因为负时间在物理上是不可能的。|近义词：semi-normally (less common)|反义词：normally distributed|形容词
