dying: /ˈdaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 垂死的；即将消亡的 |  Approaching death; nearing extinction.  It describes something or someone in the process of ceasing to exist or live. |例句：The dying man gasped for breath.  垂死的人喘着粗气。

The dying embers glowed faintly in the fireplace.  壁炉里快要熄灭的余烬发出微弱的光。

近义词：perishing, expiring, fading, weakening | 反义词：thriving, flourishing, blooming, prospering | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant is dying.  这株植物正在枯萎。
He is dying of cancer. 他正死于癌症。

一般过去时:
The old tree was dying. 那棵老树快死了。
The company was dying a slow death. 那家公司正在慢慢走向衰亡。

现在完成时:
The flowers have been dying since the frost. 霜冻后，花儿一直在枯萎。
My hopes have been dying slowly. 我的希望正在慢慢破灭。

过去完成时:
The patient had been dying for days before the doctor arrived.  医生到达之前，病人已经病危好几天了。
The business had been dying for years before it finally closed.  那家公司在最终倒闭前已经经营不善多年了。

将来时:
The old man will be dying soon.  这位老人快要死了。
If we don't act now, the project will be dying. 如果我们现在不采取行动，这个项目将会失败。

虚拟语气:
If he were dying, he would call his family. 如果他快死了，他会打电话给他的家人。
I wish I weren't dying. 我真希望我没有快死了。

被动语态:
The plant is being allowed to die. 这株植物被任其枯萎。
The old traditions are slowly being allowed to die. 那些古老的传统正在慢慢被遗忘。

疑问句:
Is the tree dying? 这棵树快要死了吗？
Is he dying? 他快死了吗？

否定句:
The plant is not dying. 这株植物没有枯萎。
The rumor is not dying down. 这谣言并没有平息。
