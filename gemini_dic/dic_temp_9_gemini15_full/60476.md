ejectable:/ɪˈdʒɛktəbəl/| adj.| 可弹出式；可喷射的；可驱逐的 | Capable of being ejected or expelled.  Something that is ejectable can be forced out or removed from something else. |例句：The aircraft's ejection seat is ejectable in case of emergency.  飞机的弹射座椅在紧急情况下是可以弹出的。

The computer's hard drive is ejectable; you can easily remove it. 这台电脑的硬盘是可以弹出的；你可以很容易地把它取出来。|近义词：removable, extractable, dischargeable |反义词：fixed, permanent, irremovable
