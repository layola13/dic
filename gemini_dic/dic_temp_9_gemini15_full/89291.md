homoeomerical:/ˌhoʊmioʊˈmɛrɪkəl/| adj.|同质的，均匀的|Consisting of parts that are similar or identical in structure and composition.  The term is often used in biology and philosophy to describe systems or organisms made up of essentially similar parts.|The ancient Greek philosopher Anaximander believed the universe was homoeomerical.|近义词：homogeneous, uniform, similar|反义词：heterogeneous, diverse, dissimilar
