demonstrates: /ˈdɛmənstreɪts/ | vt. |证明；展示；示范；说明|To clearly show the existence or truth of something by giving proof or evidence; to show or explain how something works or is done.|例句：The experiment demonstrates the power of gravity.  实验证明了万有引力的力量。

例句：The teacher demonstrates how to solve the equation. 老师演示了如何解这个方程。

近义词：shows, proves, illustrates, exhibits, exemplifies

反义词：conceals, hides, obscures


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demonstrates his skills daily. = 他每天都展示他的技能。
The data demonstrates a clear trend. = 数据显示了一个明显的趋势。

一般过去时:
She demonstrated her commitment to the cause. = 她证明了她对事业的承诺。
The scientist demonstrated the new theory. = 科学家论证了新的理论。

现在完成时:
They have demonstrated their loyalty time and again. = 他们一次又一次地证明了他们的忠诚。
Researchers have demonstrated the effectiveness of the new drug. = 研究人员已经证明了这种新药的有效性。

过去完成时:
He had demonstrated his abilities before the interview. = 他在面试前已经展示了他的能力。
The evidence had demonstrated his guilt. = 证据已经证明了他的罪行。

将来时:
She will demonstrate the new software next week. = 她下周将演示新的软件。
The professor will demonstrate the principles of physics. = 教授将演示物理学的原理。

虚拟语气:
If he demonstrated more effort, he would succeed. = 如果他付出更多努力，他就会成功。
I wish he had demonstrated more respect. = 我希望他表现出更多的尊重。

被动语态:
The theory was demonstrated to be incorrect. = 该理论被证明是错误的。
The experiment's results were clearly demonstrated. = 实验的结果被清楚地展示出来。

疑问句:
Does this example demonstrate the concept clearly? = 这个例子能清楚地说明这个概念吗？
How will you demonstrate your understanding? = 你将如何证明你的理解？

否定句:
The study did not demonstrate any significant results. = 这项研究没有显示任何显著的结果。
He failed to demonstrate his competence. = 他未能证明他的能力。
