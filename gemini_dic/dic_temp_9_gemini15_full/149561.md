paginating:/ˈpædʒɪneɪtɪŋ/| v.|分页，编号|The act of adding page numbers to a document or assigning page numbers to a document.  It can also refer to the process of dividing a document into pages.|例句：The software automatically paginates the document.  软件会自动为文档分页。

The printer is paginating the report. 打印机正在为报告分页。|近义词：numbering, paging|反义词：None.
