nonmigratory: /ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡrəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 不迁徙的，定居的 |  Not migrating; remaining in one place throughout the year.  This adjective describes animals, plants, or even people who do not migrate. | Example:  Many species of birds are migratory, while others are nonmigratory. 许多鸟类是候鸟，而另一些则是留鸟。


Example: The nonmigratory population of geese stayed near the lake all year.  这些不迁徙的鹅群全年都待在湖附近。


近义词: sedentary, resident, stationary


反义词: migratory, nomadic


词性: adjective
