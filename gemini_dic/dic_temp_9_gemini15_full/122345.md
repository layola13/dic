mixes:/mɪksɪz/ | v. |混合；调配；混杂|To combine or blend into one mass or mixture.  It often implies a thorough intermingling of different elements.|例句：The baker mixes flour, sugar, and eggs to make a cake. = 面包师混合面粉、糖和鸡蛋来做蛋糕。
The artist mixes colors to create a unique shade. = 艺术家混合颜色以创造独特的色调。|近义词：blend, combine, mingle, amalgamate |反义词：separate, segregate

词源:
The word "mixes" is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "mix," which ultimately derives from Old English *micsian, meaning "to mingle, mix."  The Old English word itself likely has Germanic roots.
