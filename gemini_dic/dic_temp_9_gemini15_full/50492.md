depressurising: /ˌdiːˈprɛʃəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 减压；降压 | The process of reducing pressure, typically in a container or system.  This often involves releasing gas or liquid to lower the internal pressure. |例句：The aircraft is depressurising rapidly.  飞机正在迅速减压。

例句：The engineers are depressurising the pipeline before maintenance. 工程师们在维护之前正在对管道进行减压。


近义词：decompressing, venting, releasing pressure

反义词：pressurising, increasing pressure

词性：动词
