differentiated: /ˌdɪfəˈrenʃeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 区分的；有差别的；分化的 |  Distinguished, set apart, or different from others.  It implies a process of making distinctions or identifying differences. |

例句：
The teacher differentiated the curriculum to meet the needs of students at different learning levels. = 老师对课程进行了分层教学，以满足不同学习水平的学生的需求。

The differentiated marketing strategy targeted specific customer segments. = 有差异化的营销策略针对特定的客户群体。


近义词：distinguished, varied, diverse, distinct, separate

反义词：homogeneous, uniform, undifferentiated, alike


词性：形容词
