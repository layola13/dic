self-punitive: /ˌsɛlfˈpjuːnətɪv/ | adj. | 自我惩罚的，自我苛责的 |  Characterized by or inflicting self-punishment.  It describes actions or tendencies towards harming oneself as a form of punishment or self-discipline. | 例句：Her self-punitive behavior stemmed from a deep-seated guilt.  她的自我惩罚行为源于深深的内疚感。


Her constant self-criticism was a form of self-punitive behavior. 她持续不断的自我批评是一种自我惩罚的行为。


近义词：self-destructive, masochistic, self-flagellating | 反义词：self-indulgent, self-compassionate
