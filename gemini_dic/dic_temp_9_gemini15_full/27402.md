cameos:/ˈkæmioʊz/| n.|客串演出；浮雕宝石；凸雕画像|A cameo is a small role in a film or play, often played by a famous actor.  It can also refer to a piece of jewelry with a raised design, typically a portrait. |例句：The film featured a cameo by the former president. 这部电影有前总统的客串演出。

例句：She wore a beautiful cameo brooch. 她戴着一个美丽的浮雕胸针。


近义词：brief appearance, small part, cameo appearance


反义词：leading role, starring role


词性:名词
