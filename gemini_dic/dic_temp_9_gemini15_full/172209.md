push-on: /pʊʃ ˈɒn/ | v. |继续努力；坚持；推动；前进| To continue doing something despite difficulties; to press forward; to urge or drive forward.|例句：Despite the setbacks, they pushed on with the project. 尽管遭遇挫折，他们还是坚持完成了项目。|近义词：continue, persevere, press on, proceed|反义词：give up, stop, quit|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They push on with their work every day. = 他们每天都坚持工作。
We push on towards our goal. = 我们朝着目标前进。


一般过去时:
She pushed on despite the rain. = 尽管下雨，她还是坚持前行。
The soldiers pushed on through the night. = 士兵们整夜都在坚持行军。


现在完成时:
They have pushed on for many years. = 他们已经坚持了很多年。
We have pushed on despite numerous challenges. = 尽管面临诸多挑战，我们还是坚持了下来。


过去完成时:
They had pushed on for hours before they rested. = 他们在休息之前已经坚持了几个小时。
She had pushed on relentlessly before finally achieving her goal. = 她坚持不懈地努力，最终实现了目标。


将来时:
We will push on until we succeed. = 我们会坚持到成功为止。
They will push on regardless of the difficulties. = 不管遇到什么困难，他们都会继续努力。


虚拟语气:
If we pushed on, we might succeed. = 如果我们坚持下去，也许会成功。
If they had pushed on harder, they might have won. = 如果他们更加努力，也许就能赢了。


被动语态:
The project was pushed on by the determined team. = 这个项目在决心满满的团队的推动下继续进行。
The reforms were pushed on by the government. = 改革在政府的推动下继续进行。


疑问句:
Will they push on even if it rains? = 即使下雨他们还会继续吗？
Should we push on or take a break? = 我们应该继续还是休息一下？


否定句:
They didn't push on after the accident. = 事故发生后，他们没有继续前进。
We won't push on if the weather gets worse. = 如果天气变坏，我们就不继续了。


PHRASE->
push on with = 继续进行...
push on through = 坚持克服...
push on regardless = 不顾一切地坚持
push on to = 向...前进
push on towards = 朝着...前进
