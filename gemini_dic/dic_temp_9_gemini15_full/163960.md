predicrotic:/ˌpriːdɪˈkrɒtɪk/| adj.| 预测的，先兆的 | Relating to or denoting the small, usually insignificant, wave in the arterial pulse immediately preceding the dicrotic wave.  It reflects the closure of the aortic valve. | Example: The predicrotic wave was barely perceptible in the patient's weak pulse.  患者微弱的脉搏中几乎无法察觉到预测波。|近义词：premonitory, precursory |反义词：postdicrotic
