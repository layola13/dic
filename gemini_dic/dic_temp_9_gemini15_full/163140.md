precombustion:/ˌpriːkəmˈbʌʃən/| n. |预燃，预燃烧|The process of converting fuel into a gaseous form before combustion, typically to improve efficiency and reduce emissions.|例句：Precombustion gasification is a promising technology for clean energy production. 预燃气化是清洁能源生产中一项很有前景的技术。
例句：The plant uses precombustion carbon capture to minimize its environmental impact. 该工厂采用预燃碳捕获技术，以最大限度地减少其对环境的影响。|近义词：pre-burning, prior combustion|反义词：postcombustion
