overassert:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrt/ | vt. | 过分自信地宣称；武断地断言 | To state or claim something too strongly or forcefully, often without sufficient evidence.  | 例句：He overasserted his own importance in the project. 他过分夸大了自己在项目中的重要性。

He overasserted his opinions during the meeting, silencing other participants. 他在会议上武断地发表意见，使其他与会者噤声。| 近义词：dogmatize, insist,  assert strongly,  | 反义词：hesitate, doubt, be tentative, be modest | 动词
