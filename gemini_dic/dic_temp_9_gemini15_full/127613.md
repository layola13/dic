needing:/ˈniːdɪŋ/| adj.|需要的，必要的 | needing 表示某种东西或某种情况是需要的或必要的。它通常用作形容词，修饰名词。  |例句：The plant is needing water. 这植物需要浇水。

The project is needing more funding.  这个项目需要更多资金。


近义词：required, necessary, essential | 反义词：unnecessary, superfluous | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car is needing a new tire. 这辆车需要一个新轮胎。
The plants are needing more sunlight. 植物需要更多阳光。


一般过去时:
The house was needing repairs. 这房子需要修理。
The computer was needing an upgrade. 这台电脑需要升级。


现在完成时:
The project has been needing attention for weeks. 这个项目几周来都需要关注。
The garden has been needing weeding for a long time. 花园很久都没除草了。


过去完成时:
The patient had been needing medication for months. 病人几个月来都需要服药。
The bridge had been needing reinforcement for years. 这座桥多年来都需要加固。


将来时:
The building will be needing a new roof soon. 这栋楼很快就会需要一个新屋顶。
The children will be needing new shoes for school. 孩子们上学需要新鞋子。


虚拟语气:
If the project were needing more funds, we would apply for a grant. 如果项目需要更多资金，我们会申请拨款。
If she were needing help, she would ask. 如果她需要帮助，她会开口的。


被动语态:
More funding is being needed for the research. 这项研究需要更多资金。
A new strategy is being needed to address the issue. 需要一个新的策略来解决这个问题。


疑问句:
Is the car needing a service? 这车需要保养吗？
Is the plant needing water again? 这植物又要浇水了吗？


否定句:
The car isn't needing any repairs at the moment. 目前这车不需要修理。
The project isn't needing any further assistance.  这个项目不需要任何进一步的帮助。
