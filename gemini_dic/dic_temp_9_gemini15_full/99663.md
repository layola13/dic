interlibeling:/ˌɪntərˈlaɪbəliŋ/| n.|相互诽谤；相互中伤|The act of two or more parties engaging in reciprocal defamation or slander.  It often involves accusations and counter-accusations of wrongdoing, typically in a public forum.|例句：The ongoing interlibeling between the two political candidates is damaging their reputations.  两个政治候选人之间的持续相互诽谤正在损害他们的声誉。|近义词：mutual defamation, reciprocal slander, cross-accusation|反义词：mutual praise, mutual support|名词
