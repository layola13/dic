foundation: /faʊnˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 基础；地基；基金会；创立 |

英文解析:  A foundation can refer to the base or groundwork upon which something is built, both literally and figuratively.  It can be the physical base of a building, a principle or idea upon which a system or theory is based, or an organization established to support a particular cause.

例句:

The foundation of the house is made of concrete. = 房子的地基是用混凝土做的。

The company's success is built on a strong foundation of innovation. = 这家公司的成功是建立在强大的创新基础之上的。


近义词: base, groundwork, basis, underpinning, establishment

反义词:  superstructure, top, culmination


USAGE->

一般现在时:

The foundation supports many charitable causes. = 这个基金会支持许多慈善事业。
This building has a weak foundation. = 这栋建筑的地基很薄弱。


一般过去时:

They laid the foundation for the new school last year. = 他们去年为新学校奠定了基础。
The organization’s foundation was established in 1950. = 该组织的基金会成立于1950年。


现在完成时:

They have built a solid foundation for their future. = 他们为他们的未来打下了坚实的基础。
The charity has raised significant funds since its foundation. = 自成立以来，该慈善机构筹集了大量资金。


过去完成时:

Before the construction began, they had already checked the foundation's stability. = 在施工开始之前，他们已经检查了地基的稳定性。
The artist had laid the foundation for his masterpiece years before its completion. = 这名艺术家在完成其杰作的多年之前就为其奠定了基础。


将来时:

They will lay the foundation for the new bridge next month. = 他们下个月将为新桥奠基。
We will establish a foundation to support research in this field. = 我们将成立一个基金会来支持该领域的科研。


虚拟语气:

If the foundation had been stronger, the building wouldn't have collapsed. = 如果地基更坚固，建筑物就不会倒塌了。
If I had more funds, I would establish a foundation to help the homeless. = 如果我有更多的资金，我会成立一个基金会来帮助无家可归的人。


被动语态:

The foundation was inspected thoroughly before construction. = 在施工前对地基进行了彻底检查。
The new building's foundation is being laid now. = 新建筑的地基正在奠定中。


疑问句:

What is the foundation of your argument? = 你论证的基础是什么？
Is the foundation strong enough to support the weight of the building? = 地基是否足够坚固以支撑建筑物的重量？


否定句:

The foundation of this theory is not sound. = 这个理论的基础并不牢固。
The building lacks a solid foundation. = 这栋建筑物缺乏坚实的基础。
