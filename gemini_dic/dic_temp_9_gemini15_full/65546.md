euthanise:/ˈjuːθənaɪz/ | vt. | 安乐死 | To kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to end suffering.  | The vet had to euthanise the injured dog. 兽医不得不对受伤的狗实施安乐死。 |  put to sleep, dispatch, destroy |  —  | verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The vet euthanises many animals each year. = 兽医每年都会对许多动物实施安乐死。
They euthanise terminally ill patients in some countries. = 在一些国家，他们会对患有绝症的病人实施安乐死。


一般过去时:
The farmer euthanised the sick cow. = 农民对那头生病的奶牛实施了安乐死。
