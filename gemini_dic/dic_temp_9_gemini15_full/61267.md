emargination:/ɪˌmɑːrʒɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |边缘化；社会排斥 |The process of relegating someone or something to the periphery or margin of society, rendering them powerless or insignificant.  It often involves social, economic, or political exclusion. |Example: The emargination of minority groups is a serious social problem.  少数群体的边缘化是一个严重的社会问题。|近义词：marginalization, exclusion, ostracism |反义词：inclusion, integration, empowerment |名词
