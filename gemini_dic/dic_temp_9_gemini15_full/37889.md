complains:/kəmˈpleɪnz/| v.|抱怨，投诉|To express dissatisfaction or annoyance about something.|例句：He constantly complains about the weather. 他不停地抱怨天气。|例句：She complained to the manager about the poor service. 她向经理投诉糟糕的服务。|近义词：grumble, protest, object|反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He complains about the noise every day. = 他每天都抱怨噪音。
She complains that the food is too spicy. = 她抱怨食物太辣了。


一般过去时:
He complained about his headache yesterday. = 他昨天抱怨头痛。
She complained to the teacher about the unfair test. = 她向老师抱怨考试不公平。


现在完成时:
He has complained several times about the slow internet speed. = 他已经多次抱怨网速慢了。
She has complained that she hasn't received her salary yet. = 她抱怨说她还没收到工资。


过去完成时:
He had complained about the problem before, but nothing was done. = 他以前就抱怨过这个问题，但是什么都没做。
She had complained many times, but no one listened. = 她抱怨过很多次，但没有人听。


将来时:
He will complain if the project is delayed again. = 如果项目再次延误，他会抱怨。
She will complain to the authorities if the issue isn't resolved. = 如果问题得不到解决，她会向当局投诉。


虚拟语气:
I wish he wouldn't complain so much. = 我希望他不抱怨那么多。
If he complained, I would try to help. = 如果他抱怨，我会尽力帮忙。


被动语态:
His complaints were ignored. = 他的抱怨被忽视了。
The matter was complained about by many customers. = 这件事被许多顾客投诉了。


疑问句:
What are you complaining about? = 你在抱怨什么？
Does he often complain? = 他经常抱怨吗？


否定句:
He didn't complain about the long hours. = 他没有抱怨长时间工作。
She doesn't complain easily. = 她不容易抱怨。


PHRASE->
complain about = 抱怨…
complain to = 向…抱怨
lodge a complaint = 正式投诉
file a complaint = 提交投诉
have a complaint = 有意见/投诉
