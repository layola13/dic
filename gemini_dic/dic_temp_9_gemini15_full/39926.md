例句：His constricted breathing indicated a serious medical problem. 他呼吸急促，表明存在严重的医疗问题。


近义词：narrowed, squeezed, cramped, restricted, confined, compressed

反义词：expanded, widened, enlarged, open, unrestricted


词性：形容词
