contestably:/kənˈtɛstəbli/ | adv. | 可争辩地；有争议地 |  Arguably; in a way that can be challenged or disputed.  | The election results were contestably close, leading to calls for a recount.  选举结果非常接近，存在争议，导致人们呼吁重新计票。 | arguably, disputably | uncontestably, undeniably | 副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contestably claims the land is his. = 他声称这块地是他的，但这说法是有争议的。
The evidence contestably supports the defendant's innocence. = 证据可以说是支持被告无罪的，但也有争议。


一般过去时:
She contestably won the debate. = 她赢得辩论是有争议的。
They contestably argued over the inheritance. = 他们就遗产问题进行了有争议的争论。


现在完成时:
The scientists have contestably proven their theory. = 科学家们已经证明了他们的理论，但这说法是有争议的。
He has contestably changed his mind several times. = 他已经几次改变主意了，但这说法是有争议的。


过去完成时:
They had contestably finished the project before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了项目，但这说法是有争议的。
She had contestably lied about her qualifications. = 她对自己的资格撒了谎，但这说法是有争议的。


将来时:
He will contestably lose the election. = 他可能会输掉选举，但这说法是有争议的。
They will contestably face opposition to their plans. = 他们将面临对其计划的反对，但这说法是有争议的。


虚拟语气:
If he had contestably won, he would have celebrated. = 如果他赢了（但结果存疑），他会庆祝的。
If she had contestably provided evidence, the case would have been different. = 如果她提供了证据（但证据存疑），案情就会不同。


被动语态:
The claim was contestably refuted by new evidence. = 这一说法被新的证据驳斥了，但这说法是有争议的。
His argument was contestably dismissed by the judge. = 他的论点被法官驳回了，但这说法是有争议的。


疑问句:
Is his claim contestably true? = 他的说法有争议吗？是真的吗？
Was the decision contestably fair? = 这个决定有争议吗？公平吗？


否定句:
The results were not contestably flawed. = 结果并非存在明显的缺陷（没有争议）。
His actions were not contestably illegal. = 他的行为并非明显违法（没有争议）。


PHRASE->
contestably true  可争辩的真相
contestably false  可争辩的谬误
contestably superior  可争辩的优越性
contestably better 可争辩的更好
contestably different  可争辩的不同


词源:
From "contest" + "-able" + "-ly".  "Contest" comes from Old French "contester," ultimately from Latin "contestari" ("to call to witness").
