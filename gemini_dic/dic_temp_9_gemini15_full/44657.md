crucial:/ˈkruːʃəl/| adj.| 至关重要的，关键的 | extremely important or necessary |  The crucial point is that we need more time.  至关重要的一点是我们需要更多时间。  His contribution was crucial to the project's success. 他的贡献对项目的成功至关重要。 | important, vital, essential, critical | unimportant, insignificant, trivial | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This step is crucial to the experiment. = 这个步骤对实验至关重要。
The details are crucial to understanding the situation. = 细节对于理解情况至关重要。


一般过去时:
His decision was crucial in changing the company's direction. = 他的决定对改变公司的方向至关重要。
Her testimony was crucial to the case. = 她的证词对案件至关重要。


现在完成时:
His work has been crucial to the team's achievements. = 他的工作对团队的成就至关重要。
The evidence has proven crucial to solving the mystery. = 证据已被证明对解决这个谜团至关重要。


过去完成时:
The earlier agreement had been crucial to avoiding conflict. = 早期协议对避免冲突至关重要。
His prior experience had been crucial to his success in the new role. = 他之前的经验对他新角色的成功至关重要。


将来时:
Their support will be crucial to the project's completion. = 他们的支持对项目的完成至关重要。
Her participation will be crucial to the success of the conference. = 她的参与对会议的成功至关重要。


虚拟语气:
If funding were crucial, we would have sought more investment. = 如果资金至关重要，我们会寻求更多投资。
It would be crucial if the leader showed more commitment. = 如果领导能展现更多承诺，那将至关重要。


被动语态:
The timing of the announcement was considered crucial. = 公告的时间被认为至关重要。
Sufficient resources were deemed crucial for the project. = 足够的资源被认为对该项目至关重要。


疑问句:
Is his role crucial to the success of this project? = 他的角色对这个项目的成功至关重要吗？
Are these details crucial to the understanding of the problem? = 这些细节对理解问题至关重要吗？


否定句:
The color of the car wasn't crucial to the investigation. = 车的颜色对调查并不重要。
His opinion wasn't crucial to the decision-making process. = 他的意见对决策过程并不重要。


PHRASE->
a crucial factor = 一个关键因素
a crucial moment = 一个关键时刻
crucial to success = 对成功至关重要
of crucial importance = 至关重要
play a crucial role = 发挥关键作用


词源:
From Old French *crucial, from Latin crux, crucis "cross".  Originally referring to a crucial experiment or test that would determine the truth of something, hence the importance.  The image of a cross is that of something central and important.
