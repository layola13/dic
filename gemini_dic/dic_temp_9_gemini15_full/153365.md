penetration:/ˌpɛnəˈtreɪʃən/ | n. |渗透；穿透；进入；理解；影响力 | The ability to enter, permeate, or pass through something.  It can also refer to the act of understanding something deeply or achieving influence. |例句：The penetration of the market by the new product was surprisingly rapid. 新产品迅速渗透市场。  The penetration of sunlight through the clouds was weak.阳光透过云层的穿透力很弱。|近义词：infiltration, intrusion, ingress, insight, impact |反义词：superficiality, exclusion, resistance
