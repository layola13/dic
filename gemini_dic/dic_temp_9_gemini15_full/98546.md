instilled:/ɪnˈstɪld/| vt.|灌输，注入，逐渐养成|To gradually establish (an idea or attitude, especially a desirable one) in a person's mind.|例句：He tried to instill in his children a love of nature. 他试图在他的孩子们心中灌输对自然的热爱。|例句：Years of training had instilled discipline in the soldiers. 多年的训练使士兵们养成了纪律。|近义词：implant, inculcate, infuse|反义词：eradicate, remove, uproot|动词
