heil:/haɪl/|v.|向…欢呼；向…致敬；赞扬|To acclaim or salute enthusiastically; to hail someone or something is to greet them with cheers and approval.  |例句：The crowd hailed the victorious team.  人群欢呼胜利的队伍。
|例句：They hailed the new law as a major step forward. 他们将这项新法视为向前迈出的重要一步。|近义词：acclaim, salute, greet, welcome|反义词：condemn, denounce, scorn, reject|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hail the new leader. = 他们欢呼新领导。
We hail the arrival of spring. = 我们庆祝春天的到来。


一般过去时:
The crowd hailed the winning athlete. = 人群向获胜的运动员欢呼。
They hailed the taxi. = 他们拦了一辆出租车。
