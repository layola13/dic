regress:/rɪˈɡres/| v. |倒退；退化；回归；(统计)回归|To return to a former or less developed state; to go back to an earlier time or condition; in statistics, to analyze the relationship between variables.|例句：The country has regressed into a state of civil war.  这个国家已经倒退到内战状态。|例句：His behaviour regressed after his mother's death. 他母亲去世后，他的行为退化了。|近义词：revert, decline, retrogress|反义词：progress, advance, develop
