ostracizations:/ˈɒstrəsaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 排斥；放逐；冷落 | The act of ostracizing someone or a group of people; the state of being ostracized.  It often involves exclusion from a group or society. | 例句：The ostracizations he suffered as a child profoundly affected his adult life. 他童年时遭受的排斥深刻地影响了他的成年生活。| 例句：His outspoken views led to his ostracizations from the community. 他直言不讳的观点导致他被社区排斥。 | 近义词：exclusion, shunning, isolation, banishment, rejection | 反义词：inclusion, acceptance, welcome
