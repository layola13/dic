helpmates:/ˈhɛlpmɛɪts/ | n. |帮手，助手，配偶 | A helpmate is a person who helps another person, especially a spouse.  The term often implies a supportive and collaborative relationship, particularly in a marriage.  It can be used for both men and women, though traditionally it carries a slightly more feminine connotation. | Example:  She was a wonderful helpmate to him in his business ventures. 她在他经商的过程中是一位很棒的助手。  He found a true helpmate in his wife. 他在他妻子身上找到了真正的伴侣。 |近义词：assistant, partner, spouse, companion |反义词：burden, hindrance, obstacle |名词
