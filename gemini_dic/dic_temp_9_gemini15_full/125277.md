multifeatured:/ˌmʌltiˈfiːtʃərd/ | adj. | 多功能的，多特性的 | Having many features or functions.  This refers to something possessing a variety of characteristics or capabilities. |  例句：This multifeatured software offers editing, design, and animation capabilities.  这个多功能软件提供编辑、设计和动画功能。

例句：The multifeatured smartphone has a camera, GPS, and internet access. 这款多功能智能手机具有相机、GPS和互联网访问功能。


近义词：versatile, multifunctional, multifaceted, comprehensive

反义词：single-featured, limited-function, basic


词源:  The word is a combination of "multi-" (meaning many) and "featured" (having features).  "Feature" itself comes from the Old French *feature*,  meaning "form, shape, appearance".
