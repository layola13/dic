insemination:/ˌɪnsɛmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |人工授精；播种|The process of introducing semen into the vagina or uterus other than by sexual intercourse.  This is often done for reproductive purposes in humans and animals. |例句：Artificial insemination is a common method of assisted reproductive technology. 人工授精是辅助生殖技术的一种常用方法。

例句：The farmer practiced insemination to improve the breed of his cattle.  这位农民采用人工授精来改良他的牛群品种。


近义词：artificial fertilization, impregnation


反义词：None directly opposite, but "natural conception" could be considered a contrasting term.


词性：名词
