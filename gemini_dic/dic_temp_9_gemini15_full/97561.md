例句：The ingression of invasive species into the ecosystem is a serious concern. 外来物种入侵生态系统是一个严重的问题。


近义词：entry, incursion, invasion, penetration|反义词：egress, exit, departure|词性:名词


词源:  From Middle English *ingression, from Old French ingressioun, from Latin ingressio, from ingredi "to enter," from in- "in" + gradi "to step."
