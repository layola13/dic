rehospitalized:/ˌriːˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 再住院的；二次住院的 |  Being admitted to a hospital again after a previous discharge.  This term describes a patient who has been discharged from a hospital and subsequently readmitted.  | Example: The patient was rehospitalized due to complications from the surgery.  病人因手术并发症而再次住院。  | 近义词: readmitted, readmission (n.) | 反义词: discharged, released from hospital


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is rehospitalized because of his heart condition. = 他因为心脏病而再次住院。
The hospital frequently rehospitalizes patients with chronic illnesses. = 这家医院经常让患有慢性病的病人再次住院。


一般过去时:
She was rehospitalized after experiencing severe chest pains. = 她经历了剧烈的胸痛后再次住院。
The doctor decided that he needed to be rehospitalized immediately. = 医生决定他需要立即再次住院。


现在完成时:
He has been rehospitalized three times this year. = 他今年已经三次再次住院了。
She has rehospitalized herself after a minor setback in her recovery.  = 她在康复过程中遭遇了轻微的挫折后再次住院了。


过去完成时:
By the time the ambulance arrived, he had already been rehospitalized by his family. = 救护车到达时，他已经由家人送去再次住院了。
The patient had been rehospitalized several times before the doctors found the underlying cause. = 在医生找到根本原因之前，病人已经多次再次住院了。


将来时:
They will likely be rehospitalized if their condition doesn't improve. = 如果他们的病情没有好转，他们很可能会再次住院。
The surgeon believes the patient will be rehospitalized for post-operative care. = 外科医生认为病人将会再次住院进行术后护理。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't ignored his doctor's advice, he wouldn't have been rehospitalized. = 如果他没有忽视医生的建议，他就不会再次住院了。
I wish she hadn't been rehospitalized so soon after her initial discharge. = 我真希望她第一次出院后没有这么快再次住院。


被动语态:
He was rehospitalized against his will. = 他被迫再次住院。
Many patients are rehospitalized due to a lack of adequate post-discharge care. = 许多病人因为缺乏足够的出院后护理而再次住院。


疑问句:
Was he rehospitalized after the accident? = 他在事故后再次住院了吗？
Why was she rehospitalized? = 她为什么再次住院？


否定句:
He was not rehospitalized after his initial treatment. = 他在初次治疗后没有再次住院。
She hasn't been rehospitalized since her last surgery. = 自从她上次手术后，她就再也没有再次住院了。
