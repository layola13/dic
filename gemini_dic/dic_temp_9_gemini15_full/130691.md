nonage:/ˈnɒnɪdʒ/ | n. |未成年；青春期 |The state of being under the legal age of majority; minority.  It typically refers to the period before a person reaches adulthood, often legally defined as 18 or 21 years old.  |Example:  He committed the crime during his nonage. 他在未成年时犯了罪。|近义词：minority, youth, adolescence |反义词：adulthood, majority
