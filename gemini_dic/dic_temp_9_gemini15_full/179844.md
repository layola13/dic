reexperiment:/ˌriːɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/| v.|再实验，重复实验|To perform an experiment again, often with modifications or under different conditions.|The scientists decided to reexperiment using a different methodology. 科学家们决定采用不同的方法重新进行实验。|repeat, replicate|—|v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reexperiment with the new formula every week. = 他们每周都用新配方进行再实验。
We reexperiment to ensure the accuracy of the results. = 我们重复实验以确保结果的准确性。


一般过去时:
The researchers reexperimented with the modified design. = 研究人员用改进的设计进行了再实验。
She reexperimented, this time paying closer attention to detail. = 她又做了一次实验，这次更仔细地注意细节。


现在完成时:
They have reexperimented several times, but still haven't found a solution. = 他们已经重复实验了好几次，但仍然没有找到解决方案。
Scientists have reexperimented to confirm the initial findings. = 科学家们已经重复实验以证实最初的发现。


过去完成时:
They had reexperimented before publishing their findings. = 他们在发表研究结果之前已经重复实验过了。
The team had reexperimented using a different approach. = 团队已经尝试了不同的方法重新进行了实验。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
