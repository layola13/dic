law-enforcer:/lɔːˈɛnfɔːrsər/| n. |执法者，警务人员|A law-enforcer is a person or organization responsible for enforcing laws and regulations.  They may be police officers, judges, or other officials with the authority to ensure compliance with the law.|例句：The law-enforcers arrived quickly after the call.|近义词：police officer, law enforcement officer, peace officer|反义词：lawbreaker, criminal
