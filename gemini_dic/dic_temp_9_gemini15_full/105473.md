killers:/ˈkɪlərz/| n.|杀手，杀人犯；极具破坏力的事物|People or things that kill.  The word can refer to individuals who commit murder, or to something that causes widespread destruction or harm. |例句：The police are searching for the killers. 警方正在搜寻凶手。


例句：The killer heatwave caused widespread power outages. 致命的热浪导致大范围停电。


近义词：murderers, assassins, slayers, destroyers|反义词：saviors, rescuers
