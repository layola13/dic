flushers:/ˈflʌʃərz/| n.|冲洗器；(厕所)冲水装置；|A flusher is a device that uses water to clean or rinse something.  It is most commonly used in the context of toilets, where it is the mechanism that flushes waste away.|Example: The toilet flusher was broken, so we had to use a bucket of water.  厕所冲水装置坏了，所以我们不得不使用一桶水。|近义词：toilet flush, rinsing device|反义词：无
