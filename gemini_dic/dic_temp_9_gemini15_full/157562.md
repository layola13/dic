pitch-in:/pɪtʃ ɪn/| v.| 参与；帮忙；贡献；投入 | To join in and help with a task or project; to contribute (money, time, effort, etc.).|例句：Let's all pitch in and clean up the park. = 我们大家一起动手清理公园吧。

例句：Everyone pitched in to help the family after the fire. = 大火之后，大家纷纷伸出援手帮助这个家庭。


近义词：contribute, help, assist, participate, cooperate | 反义词：refuse, neglect, hinder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pitch in to help their neighbours regularly. = 他们定期帮助邻居们。
We pitch in whenever there's a community event. = 每当有社区活动，我们都会参与其中。


一般过去时:
She pitched in with the cooking for the party. = 她帮忙做了派对的菜。
He pitched in and fixed the broken fence. = 他帮忙修理了坏掉的围栏。


现在完成时:
They have pitched in to raise enough money for the charity. = 他们已经一起筹集到了足够的善款。
We have pitched in on several projects this year. = 今年我们已经参与了好几个项目。


过去完成时:
They had pitched in before the deadline, so the project was completed on time. = 他们在截止日期前就已参与进来，所以项目按时完成了。
She had pitched in earlier, which helped finish the work quickly. = 她之前就参与进来了，这有助于快速完成工作。


将来时:
We will pitch in to help with the move next weekend. = 我们下周末将帮忙搬家。
They will pitch in to organize the fundraiser. = 他们将参与筹款活动的组织工作。


虚拟语气:
If everyone pitched in, we could finish the job much faster. = 如果每个人都参与进来，我们就能更快地完成工作。
I wish they had pitched in earlier; it would have saved a lot of time. = 我真希望他们早点参与进来，那样就能节省很多时间。


被动语态:
The work was completed quickly because everyone pitched in.  = 工作很快完成是因为每个人都参与了。
The extra funds were needed because no one pitched in enough time. = 需要额外资金是因为没有人投入足够的时间。


疑问句:
Will you pitch in and help with the dishes? = 你会帮忙洗碗吗？
Did everyone pitch in during the emergency? = 紧急情况下每个人都参与了吗？


否定句:
He didn't pitch in at all, leaving all the work to others. = 他根本没有帮忙，把所有工作都留给了别人。
She refused to pitch in, which delayed the project. = 她拒绝帮忙，这导致项目延误。


PHRASE->
pitch in and help = 积极帮忙

pitch in to do sth = 帮忙做某事

pitch in their share = 贡献自己的一份力量
