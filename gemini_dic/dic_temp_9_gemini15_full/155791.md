phonomotor:/ˌfoʊnoʊˈmoʊtər/ | n. |语音运动器，发音运动器 | A phonomotor is a device or system that produces or controls speech sounds.  It could refer to a mechanical device, a software program simulating speech production, or even a theoretical model of the human vocal tract.  The term emphasizes the motor aspects of speech production. |Example:  The researchers used a sophisticated phonomotor to synthesize various speech sounds.  研究人员使用复杂的语音运动器合成各种语音。


近义词：speech synthesizer, voice generator, articulatory model

反义词：无


词性:名词
