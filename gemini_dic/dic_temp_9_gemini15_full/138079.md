normalizing:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |标准化；使正常化；使标准化|To make something normal or standard.  This involves bringing something into conformity with a standard or norm. It can refer to processes, data, behavior, or other things. |例句：The company is normalizing its production processes to improve efficiency. 公司正在使其生产流程标准化以提高效率。

例句：We need to normalize the data before running the analysis. 我们需要在运行分析之前标准化数据。

近义词：standardizing, regularizing, unifying |反义词：disorganizing, disrupting, deranging
