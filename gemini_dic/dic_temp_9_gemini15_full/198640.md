shut-in:/ʃʌt ɪn/| adj. n.|隐居的；与世隔绝的人；不出门的人|A shut-in is a person who stays indoors most of the time, often due to illness, disability, or social anxiety.  They are often isolated from the outside world.|例句：He became a shut-in after his wife passed away. 他妻子去世后就成了隐居的人。

例句：Many elderly people become shut-ins due to limited mobility. 许多老年人由于行动不便而成为不出门的人。|近义词：recluse, hermit, homebody|反义词：extrovert, social butterfly
