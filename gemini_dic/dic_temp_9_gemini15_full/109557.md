let:/lɛt/ | v. |让，允许，假设，出租 | To permit or allow (someone) to do something.  |例句：Let me help you with that.  让我帮你做那件事。|例句：Let's go to the park.咱们去公园吧。|近义词：allow, permit, enable |反义词：forbid, prevent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Let me go. = 让我走。
Let them play. = 让他们玩。

一般过去时:
He let me borrow his car. = 他让我借了他的车。
She let the cat out. = 她把猫放出去了。

现在完成时:
I have let you down. = 我让你失望了。
They have let the opportunity slip away. = 他们错失了机会。

过去完成时:
He had let his anger get the better of him. = 他让怒火控制了他。
She had let the secret slip. = 她泄露了秘密。

将来时:
I will let you know when I arrive. = 我到了会让你知道。
They will let us know the results later. = 他们稍后会让我们知道结果。

虚拟语气:
I wish you would let me explain. = 我希望你能让我解释一下。
If only he would let me help. = 但愿他能让我帮忙。

被动语态:
The cat was let out. = 猫被放出来了。
The children were let out early from school. = 孩子们被提前放学了。

疑问句:
Will you let me use your phone? = 你能让我用一下你的手机吗？
Can I let my dog run free in this park? = 我可以在这个公园里让我的狗自由奔跑吗？

否定句:
Don't let him fool you. = 不要让他愚弄你。
She wouldn't let me go. = 她不肯让我走。


PHRASE->
let alone = 更不用说
let go = 放手
let down = 失望
let's = 让我们
let me see = 让我想想
