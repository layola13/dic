secretly:/ˈsiːkrɪtli/ | adv. | 秘密地，偷偷地 | done in a way that is not seen or known by others; privately.  |例句：She secretly admired him from afar. 她暗中从远处钦佩他。|例句：They secretly met at night. 他们晚上秘密会面。|近义词：privately, clandestinely, stealthily |反义词：openly, publicly
