purparty:/ˈpɜːrpɑːrti/| n.|党派；集团；一伙人|A purparty is a group of people united for a common purpose, often with political or social implications.  It often implies a less formal or less legitimate group than a political party.|例句：A purparty of rebels seized control of the city. 一伙叛乱分子夺取了该市的控制权。|近义词：faction, clique, cabal, group, party|反义词：individual, loner
