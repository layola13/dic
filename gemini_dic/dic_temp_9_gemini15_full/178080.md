reclined:/rɪˈklaɪnd/| v.|斜倚；躺下；放松|To lean or lie back in a relaxed position.|例句：She reclined on the sofa, reading a book. 她斜倚在沙发上看书。|例句：He reclined in his chair, exhausted after a long day. 他疲惫地躺在椅子上，长长的一天之后。|近义词：lean, lie back, lounge|反义词：sit upright, stand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reclines on the couch every evening. = 他每天晚上都躺在沙发上。
She reclines gracefully in her armchair. = 她优雅地斜倚在扶手椅上。

一般过去时:
I reclined on the beach and enjoyed the sunset. = 我躺在沙滩上欣赏日落。
He reclined his head against the pillow. = 他把头靠在枕头上。

现在完成时:
She has reclined by the pool all afternoon. = 她整个下午都躺在游泳池边。
He has reclined in that chair for hours. = 他已经在那个椅子上躺了几个小时了。

过去完成时:
I had reclined for a while before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，我已经躺了一会儿了。
They had reclined in the shade all morning. = 他们整个上午都躺在阴凉处。

将来时:
I will recline by the fire after dinner. = 晚饭后我将在火炉旁躺下。
She will recline on the deck and read a novel. = 她将躺在甲板上读一本小说。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would recline by the pool all day. = 如果我有时间，我会整天躺在游泳池边。
I wish I could recline in a hammock right now. = 我真希望现在能躺在吊床上。

被动语态:  (Recline is rarely used passively)
The chair was designed to be reclined in comfortably. = 这把椅子设计得很舒适，可以让人舒服地躺着。
The body was found reclined against the wall. = 尸体被发现斜倚在墙边。(This is a less common passive usage)


疑问句:
Does she often recline while watching TV? = 她看电视时经常躺着吗？
Will he recline in the sun all day? = 他会整天躺在太阳底下晒太阳吗？

否定句:
He didn't recline, he sat upright. = 他没有躺下，而是坐直了。
She doesn't recline often; she prefers to sit up straight. = 她不常躺着；她更喜欢坐直。


PHRASE->

recline back = 向后躺
recline your head = 躺下你的头
fully reclined = 完全躺下
