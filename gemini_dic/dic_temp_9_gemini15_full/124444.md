motormouthed: /ˈmoʊtərmaʊθt/ | adj. |喋喋不休的，夸夸其谈的 |  Talkative and often boastful or aggressive in speech.  It suggests a rapid, unstoppable flow of words, often without much substance. |


例句：

The motormouthed salesman wouldn't let me get a word in edgewise. = 那个喋喋不休的推销员不让我插一句话。

He was a motormouthed politician known for his inflammatory speeches. = 他是一个以煽动性演讲而闻名的夸夸其谈的政客。


近义词：

garrulous, loquacious, voluble, verbose, talkative, long-winded


反义词：

taciturn, reticent, reserved, silent, laconic


词源:

The word is a compound of "motor" (suggesting continuous motion or activity) and "mouthed" (referring to speech).  The combination creates an image of someone whose mouth is constantly in motion, speaking rapidly and incessantly.
