proudly:/ˈpraʊdli/| adv. |自豪地，骄傲地 |  Expressing pride or satisfaction.  It is an adverb that modifies a verb, adjective, or other adverb, describing how an action is performed or a quality is exhibited. |例句：He proudly showed us his award. 他自豪地向我们展示了他的奖项。

She proudly announced her engagement. 她自豪地宣布了她的订婚。


近义词：haughtily, triumphantly, boastfully |反义词：humbly, sheepishly, modestly


词性：副词
