fattest:/ˈfæɾɪst/| adj. |最肥胖的，最胖的 |The adjective "fattest" is the superlative form of the adjective "fat," indicating the highest degree of fatness or obesity.  It describes the person or thing that is the most overweight among a group. |例句：He is the fattest person in the class. 他是班上最胖的人。

例句：That's the fattest cat I've ever seen! 那是我见过的最胖的猫！


近义词：most obese, chubbiest, plumpest |反义词：thinnest, slimmest, leanest
