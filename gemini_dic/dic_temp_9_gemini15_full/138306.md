notebook:/ˈnoʊtbʊk/| n.|笔记本，记事本|A notebook is a small, bound book with blank pages for writing or drawing.  It's often used for taking notes, making sketches, or keeping a diary.|Example: I always carry a notebook and pen to jot down ideas.|近义词：pad, journal, diary|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I use a notebook to take notes in class. = 我在课堂上用笔记本记笔记。
She keeps a notebook of her daily activities. = 她用笔记本记录她每天的活动。

一般过去时:
