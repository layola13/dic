eugenic:/juːˈdʒenɪk/| n. adj.|优生学的；优生论的|The science of improving the qualities of the human population by such means as discouraging people with genetic defects from reproducing, or encouraging those with desirable hereditary traits to have more children.|例句：Eugenics is a controversial field with a dark history. 优生学是一个饱受争议且有着黑暗历史的领域。|近义词：selective breeding, hereditary improvement|反义词：dysgenics
