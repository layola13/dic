词源:

The word "overstrengthened" is formed by combining the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") with the past participle "strengthened" of the verb "strengthen."  "Strengthen" itself has Old English roots, ultimately deriving from the Proto-Germanic *strankjanan.
