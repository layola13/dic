lionized:/ˈlaɪənaɪzd/| vt.| 崇拜；赞扬；追捧 | To treat (someone) as a celebrity; to give someone a lot of public attention and admiration. |例句：The author was lionized by critics and readers alike after the release of her latest novel.  她的最新小说发行后，这位作者受到了评论家和读者的一致好评。

例句：After winning the Nobel Prize, the scientist was lionized by the media.  获得诺贝尔奖后，这位科学家受到了媒体的追捧。


近义词：celebrate, praise, glorify, idolize, extol

反义词：ignore, neglect, denigrate, criticize

词性：动词
