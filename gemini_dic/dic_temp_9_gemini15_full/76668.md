galvanizations:/ˌɡælvnɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |镀锌；激励；兴奋|The process of coating iron or steel with zinc to protect it from rust.  It can also refer to the act of stimulating or exciting someone. |例句：The galvanization of the iron fence prevented rust. 镀锌铁栅栏防止了生锈。

The speech aimed at the galvanization of the troops before the battle.  演讲旨在鼓舞军队士气。|近义词：zincing, stimulation, excitation |反义词：corrosion, depression, apathy
