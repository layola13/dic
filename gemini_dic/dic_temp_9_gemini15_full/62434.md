endoplasms:/ˈɛndoʊˌplæzəm/| n. |内质,细胞质|The inner substance of a cell, excluding the nucleus and cell membrane;  the cytoplasm.  Endoplasm is often granular and contains various organelles and inclusions.  |例句：The amoeba's endoplasm streamed forward, engulfing the food particle.  变形虫的内质向前涌动，吞噬了食物颗粒。|例句：Scientists studied the endoplasm of the cell to understand its metabolic processes.科学家研究了细胞的内质以了解其代谢过程。|近义词：cytoplasm, protoplasm |反义词：ectoplasm
