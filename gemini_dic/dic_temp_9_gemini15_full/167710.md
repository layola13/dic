priestdom:/ˈpriːstdəm/| n.|僧侣身份；僧侣制度；祭司职位|The state or condition of being a priest; the office or dignity of a priest; the body of priests of a particular church or religion.  It often refers to the collective body of priests or the priestly power and influence within a religious system.|The priestdom of the ancient temple was powerful and influential.  古代神庙的僧侣阶层势力强大且有影响力。|The decline of the priestdom led to social unrest. 僧侣阶层的衰落导致了社会动荡。|priesthood, clergy|laypeople
