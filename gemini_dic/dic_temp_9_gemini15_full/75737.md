fullest:/ˈfʊlɪst/ | adj. | 最满的；最完全的；最彻底的 |  The superlative form of "full," indicating the highest degree of fullness, completeness, or intensity.  It describes something that is completely filled, saturated, or developed to the greatest extent possible. | Examples:  The fullest expression of his anger; The fullest possible explanation. | 近义词：most complete, most comprehensive, uttermost | 反义词：empty, bare, incomplete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cup is fullest when it overflows. = 杯子溢出来的时候是最满的。
This explanation is the fullest I can offer. = 这是我能提供的最完整的解释。


一般过去时:
The tank was fullest just before the leak. = 水箱在漏水之前是最满的。
Her explanation was the fullest she could provide at that time. = 她当时所能提供的解释是最完整的。


现在完成时:
The reservoir has been fullest this spring. = 水库今年春天水位最高。
We have achieved the fullest understanding of the problem. = 我们已经对这个问题有了最全面的了解。


过去完成时:
The glass had been fullest before I accidentally knocked it over. = 我不小心打翻之前，杯子是最满的。
They had already reached the fullest extent of their agreement. = 他们已经达成了最全面的协议。


将来时:
The moon will be fullest on Friday. = 月亮将在星期五达到满月。
We will have the fullest report available by next week. = 我们将在下周提供最完整的报告。


虚拟语气:
If the tank were fullest, it would overflow. = 如果水箱是最满的，它就会溢出。
If I had the fullest information, I would make a better decision. = 如果我有最全面的信息，我会做出更好的决定。


被动语态:
The stadium was fullest during the concert. = 音乐会期间体育场人最多。
The details were presented in the fullest way possible in the report. = 报告以尽可能完整的方式呈现了细节。


疑问句:
When will the moon be fullest? = 月亮什么时候最圆？
When was the river fullest this year? = 今年这条河什么时候水位最高？


否定句:
The explanation wasn't fullest; some details were missing. = 解释并不完整；一些细节缺失。
The tank wasn't fullest; there was still room for more water. = 水箱并不满；还有空间可以装更多水。
