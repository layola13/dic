cestui:/ˈsɛstuˌiː/| n. | 受益人，受托人 |  A person who is entitled to something, especially a beneficiary under a trust or will.  The term is archaic and often used in legal contexts. | Example: The cestui que trust was finally able to receive their inheritance.  受益人最终能够收到他们的遗产。 |近义词：beneficiary, grantee | 反义词：trustor, grantor


USAGE->
(由于cestui本身是名词，没有动词变化，以下语法例句不适用)


PHRASE->
cestui que trust: 受益人 (This is the most common phrase using "cestui")
cestui que use: 使用人
cestui que vie: 活命人 (refers to a person whose life is measured in a legal context)
cestui que trust's interest: 受益人的权益
cestui que trust's rights: 受益人的权利
