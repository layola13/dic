hornbeams: /ˈhɔːrnbiːmz/ | n. |角树属植物；角树| Hornbeams are a genus of trees in the family Betulaceae, known for their smooth, gray bark and strong, tough wood. They are often used as ornamental trees and for hedging. |例句：The hornbeams in the park provided a lovely shade on a hot summer day. 公园里的角树在炎热的夏日提供了宜人的树荫。


The hornbeam hedge was meticulously trimmed. 角树树篱修剪得一丝不苟。|近义词：Carpinus (genus name),  |反义词：无|
