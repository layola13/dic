predata: /ˌpriːˈdeɪtə/ | n. |预先数据，预处理数据 |  Data that is processed or prepared before being used in a main process or analysis.  It often involves cleaning, transforming, or enriching raw data to make it suitable for a specific purpose.  | Example: The predata cleaning step removed inconsistencies and errors from the dataset.  预处理数据清洗步骤消除了数据集中的不一致性和错误。|近义词：preprocessed data, preliminary data |反义词：postdata (less common and less formally defined)|名词
