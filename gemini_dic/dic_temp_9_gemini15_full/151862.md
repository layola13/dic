passionless:/ˈpæʃən ləs/| adj.|缺乏热情的；冷漠的；无动于衷的|Lacking passion or strong emotion; indifferent.|例句：Her passionless delivery of the speech bored the audience.  她的演讲缺乏激情，让听众感到厌倦。

例句：He reacted to the good news with a passionless shrug. 他对这个好消息只是无动于衷地耸了耸肩。


近义词：apathetic, indifferent, unemotional, cold, detached|反义词：passionate, enthusiastic, fervent, zealous
