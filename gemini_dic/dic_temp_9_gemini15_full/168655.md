proconscription:/ˌproʊkənˈskrɪpʃən/| n. |预先禁止；(尤指对某些书籍或言论的)查禁；|The act of prohibiting something beforehand, especially the suppression of books or speech. It often implies a preemptive measure to prevent the spread of potentially harmful or subversive ideas.  |Example: The government implemented a proconscription against all pamphlets criticizing the monarchy.  政府对所有批评君主制的传单进行了预先查禁。|近义词：preemption, suppression, censorship |反义词：permission, allowance, freedom of speech |名词
