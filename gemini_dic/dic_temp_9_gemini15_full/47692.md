debunked:/dɪˈbʌŋkt/ | v. | 揭穿；驳斥；证明…是错误的 | To expose the falseness or hollowness of (a claim, belief, etc.).  |  The scientist debunked the theory with new evidence. 科学家用新的证据驳斥了这个理论。  | refuted, disproved, exposed | accepted, confirmed, validated | 动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They debunk conspiracy theories on their podcast.  他们在播客上揭穿阴谋论。
Scientists debunk myths about healthy eating. 科学家们揭穿了关于健康饮食的神话。

一般过去时:
The journalist debunked the politician's claims.  记者揭穿了这位政治家的说法。
Historians debunked the legend of King Arthur.  历史学家们揭穿了亚瑟王的故事。

现在完成时:
Researchers have debunked the idea that coffee is harmful. 研究人员已经揭穿了咖啡有害的观念。
She has debunked many popular misconceptions. 她已经揭穿了许多流行的误解。

过去完成时:
Before the new study, scientists had debunked similar theories. 在新的研究之前，科学家们已经驳斥了类似的理论。
They had debunked the fraud before the trial started. 他们在审判开始之前就揭穿了这场骗局。

将来时:
Future research will debunk more medical myths. 未来的研究将揭穿更多医学神话。
He will debunk the rumors spreading online. 他将揭穿在网上散布的谣言。


虚拟语气:
If we had more evidence, we could debunk this claim. 如果我们有更多证据，我们就能驳斥这一说法。
I wish someone would debunk this persistent myth. 我希望有人能揭穿这个持续存在的谬论。


被动语态:
The theory was debunked by recent studies.  这个理论被最近的研究驳斥了。
Many false claims have been debunked over the years. 多年来，许多虚假说法都被揭穿了。

疑问句:
Has the claim been debunked yet?  这个说法已经被揭穿了吗？
Can this theory be debunked with further research?  这个理论可以通过进一步的研究来驳斥吗？


否定句:
The study did not debunk the original hypothesis.  这项研究并没有驳斥最初的假设。
They failed to debunk the conspiracy theory. 他们未能揭穿阴谋论。


PHRASE->
Debunk a myth  揭穿一个神话
Debunk a theory 驳斥一个理论
Debunk a claim 驳斥一个说法
Debunk a rumor 揭穿一个谣言
Debunk misinformation 揭穿错误信息
