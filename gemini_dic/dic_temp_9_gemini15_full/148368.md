overspend:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛnd/ | vt. |超支；过度消费 | To spend more money than one has budgeted or intended.  |例句：We overspent on our holiday. 我们在假期超支了。 |例句：They overspent their monthly budget by $500. 他们每月预算超支了500美元。|近义词：exceed, surpass, outspend |反义词：underspend, save, economize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overspend every month. = 他们每个月都超支。
We overspend on eating out. = 我们在外出就餐上花费太多。

一般过去时:
We overspent on our last vacation. = 我们上次度假超支了。
She overspent her allowance. = 她超支了零花钱。

现在完成时:
They have overspent again this month. = 他们这个月又超支了。
We have overspent significantly this year. = 今年我们超支得相当厉害。

过去完成时:
They had overspent before they realized it. = 他们在意识到之前就已经超支了。
We had overspent by the end of the week. = 到周末我们就已经超支了。

将来时:
I think we will overspend if we go on that trip. = 我想如果我们去那趟旅行就会超支。
They will probably overspend if they don't budget carefully. = 如果他们不仔细预算，他们可能会超支。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overspent, I would have more money now. = 如果我没有超支，我现在就会有更多钱。
I wish I hadn't overspent on that dress. = 我真希望我没有在那件衣服上花那么多钱。

被动语态:
The budget was overspent by the marketing department. = 预算被市场部超支了。
The funds were overspent on unnecessary projects. = 资金被花在了不必要的项目上。

疑问句:
Did you overspend this month? = 这个月你超支了吗？
Have they ever overspent on a holiday? = 他们曾经在假期超支过吗？

否定句:
We didn't overspend this month. = 我们这个月没有超支。
They didn't overspend on their wedding. = 他们在婚礼上没有超支。


PHRASE->
