intemperately:/ɪnˈtɛmpərɪtli/| adv.|不节制地；过度地；放肆地 |Acting or done without restraint; excessively.  It implies a lack of moderation or self-control, often leading to negative consequences. |例句：He drank intemperately and frequently got into fights. 他酗酒，经常打架。

例句：She spent money intemperately, quickly exhausting her savings. 她挥霍无度，很快就花光了积蓄。


近义词：excessively, immoderately, unrestrainedly, wildly


反义词：moderately, temperately, restrainedly, sparingly


词性：副词
