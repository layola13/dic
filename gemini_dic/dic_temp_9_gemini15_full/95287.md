incarcerating: /ɪnˈkɑːrsəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |监禁；关押 | The act of putting someone in prison or jail.  It implies confinement and restriction of freedom. |例句：The government is being criticized for incarcerating political opponents.  政府因监禁政治对手而受到批评。


例句：The judge decided to incarcerate the criminal for ten years.  法官决定将罪犯监禁十年。


近义词：imprisoning, jailing, confining, imprisoning, detaining


反义词：releasing, freeing, liberating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are incarcerating protesters without trial. = 他们正在未经审判就监禁抗议者。
The prison system incarcerates thousands of people each year. = 监狱系统每年监禁数千人。

一般过去时:
The police incarcerated the suspect immediately after the arrest. = 警方在逮捕后立即将嫌疑人监禁。
They incarcerated him for his involvement in the robbery. = 他们因他参与抢劫而将他监禁。

现在完成时:
The government has incarcerated many activists in recent years. = 近年来，政府已监禁了许多活动家。
They have incarcerated him without a proper hearing. = 他们未经适当听证就将他监禁。

过去完成时:
He had been incarcerated for several years before his release. = 他在获释前已被监禁数年。
The authorities had incarcerated him on suspicion of murder. = 当局以谋杀嫌疑将其监禁。

将来时:
They will incarcerate him if he is found guilty. = 如果他被判有罪，他们将监禁他。
The court will likely incarcerate the defendant for a lengthy period. = 法院很可能会将被告监禁很长时间。

虚拟语气:
If they found sufficient evidence, they would incarcerate him. = 如果他们找到足够的证据，他们就会将他监禁。
I wish they hadn't incarcerated him without a trial. = 我真希望他们没有未经审判就将他监禁。

被动语态:
He was incarcerated for his crimes. = 他因犯罪而被监禁。
Many innocent people have been incarcerated unjustly throughout history. = 历史上许多无辜的人都被不公正地监禁了。

疑问句:
Why are they incarcerating him? = 他们为什么要监禁他？
Will the judge incarcerate the accused? = 法官会监禁被告吗？

否定句:
They did not incarcerate him despite the evidence. = 尽管有证据，他们也没有监禁他。
The judge decided not to incarcerate the defendant. = 法官决定不监禁被告。


PHRASE->
incarcerate someone for a crime = 因犯罪而监禁某人
incarcerate someone without trial = 未经审判就监禁某人
be incarcerated in prison = 被监禁在监狱里
lengthy incarceration = 长期监禁
incarceration rate = 监禁率

词源:
From Late Latin *incarcerare, from Latin carcer "prison".  The prefix "in-" adds the sense of "into".
