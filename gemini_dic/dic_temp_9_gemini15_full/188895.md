rusting: /ˈrʌstɪŋ/ | adj. | 生锈的，腐蚀的 |  Describes the process or state of something becoming covered with rust.  | Example: The old car was rusting badly. 这辆旧车严重生锈了。 The gate was rusting in the damp climate.  潮湿的气候下，大门已经生锈了。|近义词：corroding, decaying, oxidizing | 反义词：shiny, new, uncorroded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The iron is rusting.  铁正在生锈。
The car is constantly rusting in this salty air. 这辆车在这种咸湿的空气中不断生锈。


一般过去时:
The tools rusted overnight.  工具一夜之间生锈了。
The ship's hull rusted during its long voyage.  船体在漫长的航行中生锈了。


现在完成时:
The pipes have rusted through. 管子已经锈穿了。
The fence has rusted considerably over the years.  多年来，栅栏已经严重生锈了。


过去完成时:
The car had rusted significantly before we bought it. 我们买这辆车之前，它已经严重生锈了。
The metal had rusted completely before we could repair it.  在我们能修理它之前，金属已经完全生锈了。


将来时:
The metal will rust if it's left exposed to the elements. 如果金属暴露在自然环境中，它将会生锈。
The car will likely rust quickly in this climate. 这辆车在这种气候下很可能会很快生锈。


虚拟语气:
If the car hadn't been painted, it would have rusted faster. 如果汽车没有被涂漆，它就会生锈得更快。
I wish that old bike hadn't rusted so badly. 我真希望那辆旧自行车没有那么严重地生锈。


被动语态:
The tools were rusted by the rain. 工具被雨水锈蚀了。
The metal parts are being rusted by exposure to moisture. 金属部件正在因受潮而生锈。


疑问句:
Is the metal railing rusting? 金属栏杆生锈了吗？
Are the tools rusting in the shed? 工具在棚子里生锈了吗？


否定句:
The car isn't rusting yet. 这辆车还没生锈。
The new tools haven't rusted at all. 新工具一点也没生锈。
