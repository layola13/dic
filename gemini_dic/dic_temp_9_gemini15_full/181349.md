reinvent:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛnt/| vt.|重新发明；彻底改造；改头换面|To create or develop something again in a new or different form.|The company is reinventing itself as a technology firm. 这家公司正在将自己改造成一家科技公司。|reimagine, reformulate, revamp|destroy, ruin, obliterate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinvent their marketing strategy every year.  = 他们每年都会重新设计他们的营销策略。
We reinvent the wheel too often in this industry. = 在这个行业里，我们经常重复发明轮子（做无用功）。


一般过去时:
The artist reinvented himself as a sculptor. =这位艺术家把自己重新塑造成了雕塑家。
Ford reinvented the automobile with the Model T. =福特用T型车彻底改变了汽车。


现在完成时:
They have reinvented their image since the scandal. = 自丑闻发生以来，他们已经彻底改变了自己的形象。
The company has reinvented itself as a leader in sustainable energy. = 这家公司已经将自己重新定位为可持续能源领域的领导者。


过去完成时:
They had reinvented the product before launching it. = 他们在推出产品之前已经对产品进行了重新设计。
She had reinvented her life after the divorce. = 离婚后，她彻底改变了自己的生活。


将来时:
They will reinvent the way we communicate. = 他们将彻底改变我们的沟通方式。
The company will reinvent its product line next year. = 该公司明年将对其产品线进行重新设计。


虚拟语气:
If they reinvented the process, it would be more efficient. = 如果他们重新设计这个流程，效率会更高。
I wish they had reinvented the software before the launch. = 我希望他们在发布之前已经对软件进行了重新设计。


被动语态:
The product was reinvented to appeal to a younger audience. = 该产品经过重新设计，以吸引更年轻的受众。
The company's image is being reinvented through a new marketing campaign. = 公司的形象正在通过新的营销活动而得到重新塑造。


疑问句:
Will they reinvent the classic novel for a modern audience? = 他们会为现代观众重新演绎这部经典小说吗？
How can we reinvent our approach to problem-solving? = 我们如何才能重新思考我们解决问题的方法？


否定句:
They did not reinvent the wheel; they simply improved it. = 他们并没有重新发明轮子；他们只是改进它。
The company has not yet reinvented its outdated business model. = 这家公司尚未对其过时的商业模式进行改革。
