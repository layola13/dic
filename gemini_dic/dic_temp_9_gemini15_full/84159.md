half-playful: /ˌhɑːfˈpleɪfəl/ | adj. | 半开玩笑的，略带玩笑意味的 |  Describes something that is partly playful and partly serious, showing a mixture of amusement and seriousness. It suggests a lightheartedness that doesn't entirely mask an underlying seriousness or intent. | 例句：He gave her a half-playful shove. 他半开玩笑地推了她一下。


He delivered the news with a half-playful, half-serious tone. 他以半开玩笑半认真的语气传达了这个消息。


近义词：teasing, jocular, slightly playful, mildly joking | 反义词：serious, solemn, grave, earnest
