nonpervasively:/ˌnɒnpəˈveɪsɪvli/ | adv. | 不具侵略性地，不显著地 |  Not in a pervasive or intrusive manner; subtly.  It describes an action or process that happens without being overly noticeable or disruptive. | Example: The technology was implemented nonpervasively, minimizing disruption to daily routines.  = 这项技术以不具侵略性的方式实施，最大限度地减少了对日常生活的干扰。 |近义词：subtly, discreetly, unobtrusively, gently | 反义词：pervasively, intrusively, obtrusively
