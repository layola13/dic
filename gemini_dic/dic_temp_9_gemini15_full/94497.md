importing:/ɪmˈpɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 进口；引进；输入；包含；具有重要意义 | The act of bringing goods or services into a country from abroad for sale.  It can also refer to the process of including something into a larger system or context. | 例句：The company is importing high-quality coffee beans from Colombia. 这家公司正在从哥伦比亚进口高质量的咖啡豆。

The software is importing data from the spreadsheet.  这个软件正在从电子表格中导入数据。


近义词：introducing, bringing in, including, incorporating | 反义词：exporting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are importing textiles from India. = 他们正在从印度进口纺织品。
We import a lot of technology from Japan. = 我们从日本进口很多技术。


一般过去时:
The government imported a large quantity of wheat last year. = 政府去年进口了大量的麦子。
They imported the necessary equipment from Germany. = 他们从德国进口了必要的设备。


现在完成时:
The company has imported over 1000 tons of steel this year. = 公司今年已经进口了超过1000吨的钢铁。
We have imported many new ideas into our business model. = 我们已经将许多新理念引入了我们的商业模式。


过去完成时:
Before the crisis, they had imported millions of dollars worth of goods. = 在危机之前，他们已经进口了价值数百万美元的商品。
The factory had imported all the necessary parts before starting production. = 工厂在开始生产之前已经进口了所有必要的零件。


将来时:
We will import more sustainable products next year. = 我们明年将进口更多可持续产品。
The company will be importing new machinery next quarter. = 公司下个季度将进口新机器。


虚拟语气:
If we imported more ethically sourced materials, our customers would be happier. = 如果我们进口更多符合道德采购的材料，我们的顾客会更开心。
I wish we had imported more components before the supply chain disruption. = 我希望我们在供应链中断之前已经进口了更多组件。


被动语态:
A large amount of rice is imported annually. = 大量的大米每年都被进口。
The new software was imported from a different department. = 新软件是从不同的部门导入的。


疑问句:
Are they importing anything from China? = 他们从中国进口任何东西吗？
What products are being imported from Brazil? = 从巴西进口哪些产品？


否定句:
We are not importing any products from that supplier. = 我们没有从那个供应商进口任何产品。
The company did not import enough raw materials. = 公司没有进口足够的原材料。
