rappelling:/ræˈpɛlɪŋ/| n. |速降；绳降；垂降|The controlled descent down a steep slope using ropes and specialized equipment.  It involves using ropes and specialized equipment to descend a steep cliff, rock face, or other vertical surface.|例句：We went rappelling down the cliff face. 我们沿着悬崖峭壁进行了速降。

例句：The experienced climbers demonstrated proper rappelling techniques.  经验丰富的登山者展示了正确的速降技巧。


近义词：abseiling|反义词：ascending
