czarism:/ˈzɑːrɪzəm/ | n. |沙皇制度，沙皇专制 | The system of government in which a country is ruled by a czar (or tsar).  It is characterized by absolute autocratic rule. | Example: The fall of czarism in Russia marked the beginning of a new era.  沙皇制度在俄国的垮台标志着一个新时代的开始。| 近义词：autocracy, despotism, tyranny | 反义词：democracy, republic
