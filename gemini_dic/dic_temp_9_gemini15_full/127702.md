neglected:/nɪˈɡlɛktɪd/| adj.|被忽视的；疏忽的；被忽略的|Something that is neglected has not been given enough care or attention.  It implies a failure to do something that should have been done.|The garden had been sadly neglected.  花园被 sadly neglected. |近义词：ignored, overlooked, disregarded |反义词：cared for, attended to, cherished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The child is neglected by his parents. = 这个孩子被他的父母忽视了。
His needs are neglected. = 他的需求被忽视了。


一般过去时:
She neglected her studies. = 她忽视了她的学业。
He neglected to mention the important detail. = 他忽略了提到那个重要的细节。


现在完成时:
They have neglected their responsibilities. = 他们已经忽视了他们的责任。
The house has been neglected for years. = 这房子多年来一直无人照料。


过去完成时:
He had neglected to tell her the truth. = 他之前没有告诉她真相。
She realized she had neglected her friends. = 她意识到她疏忽了她的朋友们。


将来时:
They will neglect their duties if they are not careful. = 如果他们不小心，他们将会忽略他们的职责。
He will likely neglect his health if he continues this lifestyle. = 如果他继续这种生活方式，他很可能会忽视他的健康。


虚拟语气:
If only I hadn't neglected my health. = 要是我没有忽视我的健康就好了。
I wish I hadn't neglected my studies. = 我真希望我没有忽视我的学业。


被动语态:
The problem was neglected for too long. = 这个问题被忽视了太久。
The building has been neglected and is falling apart. = 这座建筑物被忽视了，正在倒塌。


疑问句:
Were the safety regulations neglected? = 安全规章是否被忽视了？
Has the garden been neglected? = 花园是否被忽视了？


否定句:
They didn't neglect their children. = 他们没有忽视他们的孩子。
He didn't neglect his responsibilities. = 他没有忽略他的责任。
