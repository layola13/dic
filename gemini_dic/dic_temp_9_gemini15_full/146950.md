overinvested: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛstɪd/ | adj. | 投资过度的；过度投入的 | Excessively invested in something, often to the point of being detrimental.  This term usually implies a disproportionate amount of time, energy, emotion, or resources have been dedicated to a particular pursuit, relationship, or endeavor.  |  He was overinvested in his failing business, refusing to accept defeat even when it was financially crippling. 他过度投入了他即将倒闭的生意，即使在经济上已经让他崩溃，也拒绝接受失败。 |  heavily invested, deeply committed | underinvested, disengaged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are overinvested in their children's education. = 他们过度投入到孩子的教育中。
She is overinvested in her work, neglecting her personal life. = 她过度投入工作，忽略了个人生活。


一般过去时:
He was overinvested in that stock, losing a significant portion of his savings. = 他过度投资于那支股票，损失了他大部分的积蓄。
They were overinvested in the project, causing delays and budget overruns. = 他们过度投入到这个项目中，导致了延误和预算超支。


现在完成时:
I have been overinvested in this relationship for too long. = 我在这个关系中投入过多时间了。
She has overinvested in her career, sacrificing her health and family. = 她过度投入她的事业，牺牲了健康和家庭。


过去完成时:
He had been overinvested in the company before the merger, leading to significant losses. = 在合并之前，他已经过度投资于该公司，导致了巨大的损失。
They had been overinvested in the failing project before they finally decided to pull out. = 在他们最终决定退出之前，他们已经过度投入到失败的项目中了。


将来时:
I will be overinvested in my new business if I'm not careful. = 如果我不小心的话，我会过度投入我的新事业。
They will be overinvested in the research if they don't set a deadline. = 如果他们不设定截止日期，他们将会过度投入到研究中。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been overinvested in that project, I wouldn't have lost so much money. = 如果我没有过度投入到那个项目中，我就不会损失这么多钱。
I wish I hadn't been so overinvested in her promises. = 我真希望我没有对她那些承诺如此投入。


被动语态:
The project was overinvested in by the management team. = 该项目受到了管理团队的过度投资。
Resources were overinvested in the marketing campaign, yielding diminishing returns. = 资源过度投入到营销活动中，收益递减。


疑问句:
Are you overinvested in this relationship? = 你在这个关系中投入过度了吗？

否定句:
He wasn't overinvested in the venture; he simply miscalculated the risks. = 他并没有过度投资于这个风险投资；他只是误判了风险。
She wasn't overinvested in the outcome; she just wanted to do her best. = 她并没有过度关注结果；她只是想尽力而为。
