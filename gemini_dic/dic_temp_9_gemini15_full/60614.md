elderhood:/ˈɛldərhʊd/| n. |老年；老年期|The state or period of being an elder; old age.  Elderhood is a time of reflection and often involves a shift in priorities. |例句：Many people find joy and fulfillment in elderhood.  许多人发现老年生活充满快乐和满足。

例句：She embraced the challenges and rewards of elderhood with grace. 她优雅地接受了老年生活的挑战和回报。


近义词：old age, senescence, senior years |反义词：youth, adolescence, childhood |词性:名词
