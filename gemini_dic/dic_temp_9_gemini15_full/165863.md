preoffers:/ˈpriːˌɒfərz/ | n. |预先报价，初步报价|Preliminary offers made before formal negotiations begin.  These are often non-binding and serve as a starting point for discussions.|例句：The company sent out preoffers to several potential suppliers. 公司向几家潜在供应商发出了初步报价。|例句：We received several preoffers for the project, but none met our budget. 我们收到了几个项目的初步报价，但没有一个符合我们的预算。|近义词：initial offers, tentative offers, preliminary bids |反义词：final offers, binding offers
