restores:/rɪˈstɔːrz/| vt.|恢复；修复；重建；归还|To bring back to a former condition; to repair or renovate; to return something to its rightful owner.|例句：The antique furniture was carefully restored to its original beauty.  这件古董家具被仔细修复，恢复了原来的美丽。

例句：The government is working to restore the damaged infrastructure after the hurricane.  政府正在努力修复飓风过后受损的基础设施。


近义词：repair, renovate, rehabilitate, recover, reinstate|反义词：destroy, damage, ruin, impair


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He restores antique cars. = 他修复古董车。
She restores old paintings. = 她修复旧画。

一般过去时:
They restored the building to its former glory. = 他们把建筑物修复到了它昔日的辉煌。
He restored the stolen painting to its owner. = 他把被盗的画还给了它的主人。

现在完成时:
The museum has restored many valuable artifacts. = 博物馆已经修复了许多珍贵的文物。
She has restored her health after a long illness. = 她经过长期疾病后恢复了健康。

过去完成时:
They had restored the church before the anniversary. = 他们在周年纪念日之前修复了教堂。
The artist had restored the damaged sculpture before the exhibition. = 艺术家在展览前修复了受损的雕塑。

将来时:
The company will restore the damaged bridge next year. = 公司明年将修复受损的桥梁。
They will restore the old house to its original state. = 他们将把旧房子恢复到原来的状态。

虚拟语气:
If they had restored the data, we wouldn't have lost it. = 如果他们恢复了数据，我们就不会丢失它了。
I wish they would restore my faith in humanity. = 我希望他们能恢复我对人类的信心。

被动语态:
The damaged paintings were restored by a skilled conservator. = 受损的绘画由一位熟练的保护者修复。
The old house is being restored by a team of builders. = 一队建筑工人正在修复旧房子。

疑问句:
Can the old photograph be restored? = 这张旧照片可以修复吗？
Will the government restore the historical landmark? = 政府会修复这个历史地标吗？

否定句:
They couldn't restore the damaged files. = 他们无法恢复损坏的文件。
He didn't restore the book to the library. = 他没有把书还给图书馆。
