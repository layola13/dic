causatives:/ˈkɔːzətɪvz/ | n. | 使役动词；使役结构 |  Causatives are verbs that cause someone or something else to do something.  They often involve a causative verb followed by an object and a bare infinitive (without "to").  They can also be expressed with "get" or "have" constructions. |

例句：
The teacher made the students clean the classroom. = 老师让学生打扫教室。

She had her car repaired. = 她让人修理了她的车。


近义词：effectors, activators | 反义词：inhibitors, deactivators | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes his children study hard. = 他让他的孩子们努力学习。
She lets her dog sleep in her bed. = 她允许她的狗睡在她的床上。


一般过去时:
They made me laugh. = 他们让我笑了。
He had his assistant type the letter. = 他让他的助手打这封信。


现在完成时:
I have had my hair cut. = 我已经理发了。
She has gotten her car fixed. = 她已经让人修好了她的车。


过去完成时:
He had had his house painted before he moved in. = 他搬进去之前已经让人粉刷了他的房子。
They had gotten their work done before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了他们的工作。


将来时:
I will have the mechanic fix my car. = 我会让技工修理我的车。
She will get her daughter to practice the piano. = 她会让她女儿练习钢琴。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could make him understand. = 我希望我能让他明白。
If I had the money, I would have my house renovated. = 如果我有钱，我会让人翻新我的房子。


被动语态:
The students were made to clean the classroom. = 学生们被迫打扫教室。
The car was gotten repaired. = 车被修好了。(比较口语化)


疑问句:
Did you get your car washed? = 你洗车了吗？
What made you change your mind? = 什么让你改变主意了？


否定句:
I couldn't make the machine work. = 我无法让机器工作。
She didn't have her dress cleaned. = 她没有让人清洗她的裙子。


PHRASE->
make someone do something = 让某人做某事
let someone do something = 让某人做某事
have someone do something = 让某人做某事
get someone to do something = 让某人做某事
cause someone to do something = 导致某人做某事
