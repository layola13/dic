poursuivant:/ˌpʊrsuˈɪvɒnt/| n.|追随者；检察官；(旧)侍从|An officer of a court of law, especially one who served process.  Historically, a pursuivant was a  herald's assistant, ranking below a herald and a pursuivant.|例句：In medieval times, pursuivants were important messengers and officers of the court.|近义词：follower, attendant, messenger|反义词：leader, superior
