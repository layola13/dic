osteologist:/ˌɒstiˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |骨骼学家，骨学专家 |A scientist who studies bones.  Osteologists specialize in the structure, function, and development of bones, often working in fields like paleontology, archaeology, or forensic science. |例句：The osteologist carefully examined the ancient skeleton to determine its age and cause of death. 骨骼学家仔细检查了古代骨骼以确定其年龄和死因。

例句：The osteologist's report revealed that the bones belonged to a previously unknown species of dinosaur. 骨骼学家的报告显示，这些骨头属于一种以前未知的恐龙物种。


近义词：bone specialist, skeletal biologist

反义词：无


词性：名词
