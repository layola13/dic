cosmetically:/kɒzˈmɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 外观上；化妆品地 | Relating to or involving cosmetics; superficially.  It refers to changes or improvements that affect only the appearance, not the underlying substance or function. | 例句：The car was cosmetically repaired, but the engine still needed work.  这辆车只是外观上修理了一下，但发动机仍然需要修理。  The building was cosmetically improved with a new paint job. 这栋建筑物通过粉刷焕然一新。 | 近义词：superficially, outwardly, superficially, aesthetically | 反义词：fundamentally, intrinsically, substantially
