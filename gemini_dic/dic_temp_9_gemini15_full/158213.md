platycoelian:/ˌplætɪˈsiːliən/| adj. |扁平的；扁平腹部的| Relating to or characterized by a flattened body, especially in reference to certain flatworms.  The term often describes animals with a dorsoventrally flattened body shape. | Example:  Many platycoelian turbellarians inhabit marine environments.  许多扁平涡虫生活在海洋环境中。|近义词:flattened, depressed|反义词:convex, bulging
