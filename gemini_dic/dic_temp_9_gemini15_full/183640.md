rereel:/ˌriːˈriːl/|v.|重新卷绕；重播|To wind (film, tape, etc.) onto a reel again; to replay (a recording).|例句：The technician had to rereel the film before showing it again. 技术人员必须先重新卷绕胶片才能再次播放。|例句：Let's rereel that amazing scene from the movie. 让我们重播电影中那个精彩的场景。|近义词：rewind, replay|反义词：unreel
