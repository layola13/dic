sjambokker:/ˈʃæmbɒkə(r)/| n. | 鞭打者；使用sjambok的人 |A sjambokker is a person who uses a sjambok (a type of whip).  The word carries a strong connotation of brutality and often implies the abuse of power.| The farmer was known as a cruel sjambokker, often whipping his horses mercilessly.  那个农场主以残忍的鞭打者而闻名，经常无情地鞭打他的马。|  whipper, flogger |  (no direct antonym, but implies the opposite of someone who treats others kindly)| 名词
