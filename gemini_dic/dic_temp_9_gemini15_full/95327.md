incendies:/ɪnˈsɛndiːz/| n. |纵火案；大火；骚乱|The plural of "incendie," meaning a fire or conflagration, often used to describe a large-scale or destructive fire, or figuratively, a situation of great turmoil or chaos.  It can also refer to the act of setting something on fire.|例句：The wildfires caused widespread incendies across the region.  这场野火在该地区引发了大范围的火灾。|例句：The political situation in the country was a scene of incendies. 该国的政治局势一片混乱。|近义词：conflagrations, fires, upheavals|反义词：peace, calm, tranquility|名词
