semidivisions:/ˌsɛmiˈdɪvɪʒənz/| n. |半区，半部门|A semidivision is a subdivision of a larger division, often used in organizational structures or geographical contexts.  It represents a less significant division than a full division but more significant than a smaller unit.  The term emphasizes the incomplete or partial nature of the division.|Example: The company restructured, creating several semidivisions within each major department. 公司重组，在每个主要部门内创建了几个半部门。|近义词：subdivision, subunit, section|反义词：division, whole
