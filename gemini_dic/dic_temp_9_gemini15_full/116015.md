marshlike:/ˈmɑːʃlaɪk/| adj. |沼泽似的，像沼泽的|Resembling a marsh; swampy or boggy.  The adjective describes something that shares the characteristics of a marsh, such as being wet, muddy, soft, and overgrown with vegetation.|例句：The ground was marshlike and treacherous underfoot.  地面的沼泽地使得行走十分危险。The marshlike landscape was teeming with wildlife.  沼泽地带充满了野生动物。|近义词：swampy, boggy, miry, fenny|反义词：dry, arid, firm
