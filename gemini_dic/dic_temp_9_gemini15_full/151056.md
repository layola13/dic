paraphrases:/ˈpærəfreɪzɪz/ | n. |释义，改述，意译 |  Paraphrases are rewordings of a text that retain the original meaning.  They are used to clarify, simplify, or explain a complex idea. | 例句：The teacher asked us to write paraphrases of the difficult passages.  老师让我们为那些难懂的段落写出释义。

例句：He gave a paraphrase of the poem, making it easier to understand. 他对这首诗作了改述，使其更容易理解。


近义词：rewording, restatement, summary, interpretation | 反义词：verbatim, quotation
