divisionalization:/ˌdɪvɪʒənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |部门化；分部门管理 | The process of organizing a business into separate divisions, each with its own management and responsibilities.  This structure aims to improve efficiency and accountability by delegating authority and focusing on specific areas of expertise. | Example: The company's divisionalization allowed for greater specialization and responsiveness to market demands. 公司的分部门管理使得公司能够更好地专注于特定领域并对市场需求做出更快的反应。|近义词：decentralization, departmentalization |反义词：centralization
