practicalest: /ˈpræktɪkəlist/ | adj. | 最实用的，最实际的 | Most practical; to the highest degree practical.  This word is considered somewhat archaic and less frequently used than "most practical". It's a superlative form, implying a comparison among several practical options. |  This is the practicalest solution to the problem.  = 这是解决这个问题最实际的方案。  The practicalest approach is often the best. = 最实用的方法通常是最好的。|  practical, useful, efficient | impractical, theoretical, idealistic |  形容词
