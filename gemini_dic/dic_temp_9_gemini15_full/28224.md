capsicums:/ˈkæpsɪkəmz/| n.|辣椒(复数)|Capsicums are the fruits of plants belonging to the genus Capsicum, and are commonly known as peppers or chillies. They vary widely in shape, size, colour, and pungency.  |例句：We used a variety of capsicums in the stir-fry.  我们用各种辣椒来炒菜。|例句：The capsicums were roasted until they were slightly charred.  辣椒烤到略微焦为止。|近义词：peppers, chillies|反义词：无|
