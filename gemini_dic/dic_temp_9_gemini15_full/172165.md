pursuant:/pərˈsuːənt/ | adj. |依照；根据；遵照 |  Following; in accordance with.  It describes an action or decision that is done according to a particular rule, instruction, or agreement. | 例句：Pursuant to your request, I have attached the document. = 根据你的请求，我已经附加了文件。

The contract was terminated pursuant to clause 12. = 根据第12款，合同终止了。


近义词：according to, in accordance with, under, following | 反义词：contrary to, against, disregarding | 词性：形容词
