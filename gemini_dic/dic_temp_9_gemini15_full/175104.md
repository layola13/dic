raduliform:/ˈrædʒʊlɪfɔːrm/| adj.| 耙状的；齿状的 | Resembling a rake or having teeth or projections like a rake.  The term is used primarily in zoology and botany to describe structures with multiple, closely spaced, pointed projections. | Example: The insect's raduliform mouthparts were well-suited for scraping algae from rocks.  昆虫的耙状口器非常适合从岩石上刮藻类。|近义词：rake-like, pectinate, dentate |反义词：smooth, entire
