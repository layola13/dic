contraindication: /ˌkɒntrəɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/ | /ˌkɑːntrəɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |禁忌症，禁忌；反证 | A contraindication is a specific situation, symptom, or condition that makes a particular treatment or procedure potentially harmful or inappropriate.  It's a reason why a particular treatment shouldn't be used. |


例句：
The patient's allergy to penicillin is a contraindication to using that antibiotic.  = 患者对青霉素过敏，这是使用该抗生素的禁忌症。

Her recent heart attack is a major contraindication for strenuous exercise. = 她最近的心脏病发作是剧烈运动的主要禁忌症。


近义词：
prohibition, warning, precaution


反义词：
indication, recommendation


词源:
From Latin *contra- ("against") + indicatio ("indication").  The word developed its current medical meaning over time, reflecting the idea of something pointing against a particular course of action.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A known allergy is a contraindication for many medications. = 已知的过敏是对许多药物的禁忌症。
This condition presents a strong contraindication to surgery. = 这种状况对手术来说是一个强烈的禁忌症。


一般过去时:
The doctor found a contraindication in the patient's medical history. = 医生在病人的病历中发现了一个禁忌症。
Previous heart problems were a contraindication for the planned surgery. = 之前的<u>心脏</u>问题是计划手术的禁忌症。


现在完成时:
Several studies have highlighted important contraindications to this treatment. = 几项研究已经突出了对这种治疗的重要禁忌症。
The research has revealed several new contraindications for this drug. = 研究揭示了该药物的几个新的禁忌症。


过去完成时:
The surgeon had already identified a contraindication before the procedure began. = 手术医生在手术开始之前就已经发现了禁忌症。
They had overlooked a critical contraindication in the initial assessment. = 他们在初步评估中忽略了一个关键的禁忌症。


将来时:
Further tests will determine if there are any contraindications to the operation. = 进一步的检查将确定手术是否有任何禁忌症。
This condition may present contraindications to future treatment. = 这种情况可能会对未来的治疗产生禁忌症。


虚拟语气:
If there were any contraindications, the treatment would be postponed. = 如果有任何禁忌症，治疗将会被推迟。
It would be unwise to proceed with the treatment if significant contraindications existed. = 如果存在严重的禁忌症，继续治疗是不明智的。


被动语态:
This medication is contraindicated in patients with liver disease. = 这种药物对患有肝病的患者是禁忌的。
The procedure was contraindicated due to the patient's unstable condition. = 由于患者病情不稳定，该手术是禁忌的。


疑问句:
Are there any contraindications to this course of treatment? = 这种疗程是否有任何禁忌症？
What are the contraindications for using this new drug? = 使用这种新药的禁忌症是什么？


否定句:
There are no known contraindications to this procedure. = 这个程序没有已知的禁忌症。
The patient had no contraindications to receiving the vaccine. = 病人接种疫苗没有任何禁忌症。
