reintroduce:/ˌriːɪntrəˈduːs/ | vt. |重新介绍；重新引进；再提出| To introduce again; to bring something back into use or existence.  |例句：The company decided to reintroduce the popular product after years of absence. 公司决定在销声匿迹多年后重新推出这款畅销产品。

例句：The professor reintroduced the concept of gravity in a new and engaging way. 教授以一种新颖而引人入胜的方式重新介绍了重力概念。


近义词：reinstate, revive, reestablish | 反义词：remove, abolish, eliminate | 词性：verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reintroduce the bill every year. = 他们每年都重新提出这项法案。
We reintroduce new plants to the garden each spring. = 我们每年春天都在花园里重新种植新的植物。


一般过去时:
The government reintroduced the tax last month. = 政府上个月重新开征了这项税。
She reintroduced herself to her old friends after many years. = 多年后，她再次向老朋友们介绍自己。


现在完成时:
They have reintroduced the classic car model. = 他们已经重新推出了这款经典车型。
The company has reintroduced its original logo. = 公司已经重新使用了它的原始标识。


过去完成时:
They had reintroduced the product before the market crash. = 他们在市场崩盘前已经重新推出了这款产品。
She had reintroduced herself before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，她已经再次做了自我介绍。


将来时:
They will reintroduce the product next year. = 他们明年将重新推出这款产品。
The school will reintroduce the arts program. = 学校将重新开设艺术课程。


虚拟语气:
If they reintroduced the policy, it would be beneficial. = 如果他们重新实施这项政策，将是有益的。
I wish they would reintroduce the old system. = 我希望他们能重新启用旧系统。


被动语态:

The program is being reintroduced in schools across the country. = 该项目正在全国各地的学校重新引入。


疑问句:

Have they reintroduced the product yet? = 他们已经重新推出产品了吗？


否定句:
They did not reintroduce the policy. = 他们没有重新实施这项政策。
The school will not reintroduce the unpopular program. = 学校不会重新引入不受欢迎的项目。
