obligated:/ˈɑːblɪɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有义务的；被迫的 | bound by moral or legal duty; compelled |  The company is obligated to provide a safe working environment for its employees. 公司有义务为其员工提供安全的工作环境。 I feel obligated to help him, even though I don't want to.  我觉得有义务帮助他，即使我不想。|近义词：bound, compelled, required |反义词：free, unobligated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is obligated to pay his taxes. = 他有义务纳税。
We are obligated to follow the rules. = 我们有义务遵守规章制度。


一般过去时:
She was obligated to attend the meeting. = 她被迫参加会议。
They were obligated to repay the loan. = 他们有义务偿还贷款。


现在完成时:
I have been obligated to work overtime for the past week. = 我过去一周一直被迫加班。
He has obligated himself to donate to charity. = 他已承诺向慈善机构捐款。


过去完成时:
She had been obligated to care for her ailing parents. = 她一直有义务照顾她生病的父母。
They had obligated themselves to finish the project by the deadline. = 他们已承诺在截止日期前完成项目。


将来时:
I will be obligated to explain my actions. = 我将有义务解释我的行为。
They will be obligated to comply with the new regulations. = 他们将有义务遵守新的规章制度。


虚拟语气:
If I were obligated to choose, I would pick the blue one. = 如果我被迫选择，我会选蓝色的。
I wish I were not obligated to go to that party. = 我真希望我不必去参加那个聚会。


被动语态:
He was obligated to testify in court. = 他被迫在法庭上作证。
The company was obligated to compensate the victims. = 公司被迫赔偿受害者。


疑问句:
Are you obligated to disclose that information? = 你有义务披露那些信息吗？
Is she obligated to attend the ceremony? = 她有义务参加典礼吗？


否定句:
I am not obligated to help you. = 我没有义务帮助你。
They are not obligated to answer your questions. = 他们没有义务回答你的问题。


PHRASE->
feel obligated to = 感到有义务去做…
be morally obligated to = 道德上有义务…
legally obligated to = 法律上有义务…
obligated to comply with = 有义务遵守…
be obligated under contract to = 根据合同有义务…
