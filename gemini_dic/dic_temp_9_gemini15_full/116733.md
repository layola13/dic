mauled:/mɔːld/| vt.| 粗暴对待；严重损坏；猛烈攻击 |To handle roughly or violently; to inflict serious damage on; to attack fiercely.|例句：The dog mauled the intruder.  这条狗袭击了入侵者。|例句：The storm mauled the coastal towns.  暴风雨重创了沿海城镇。|近义词：attacked, injured, damaged, battered |反义词：caressed, healed, repaired


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mauls his food. = 他狼吞虎咽地吃东西。
The bear mauls its prey. = 熊撕咬它的猎物。

一般过去时:
The bear mauled the camper. = 熊袭击了露营者。
The car was mauled in the accident. = 汽车在事故中严重受损。

现在完成时:
The dog has mauled several people. = 这条狗已经袭击了好几个人。
The economy has been mauled by the recession. = 经济已经被经济衰退严重打击。

过去完成时:

The building had been mauled by the earthquake before rescuers arrived. = 在救援人员到达之前，建筑物已经被地震严重破坏。

将来时:
The wild animal will maul anyone who gets too close. = 任何靠得太近的野生动物都会被袭击。
The company's reputation will be mauled if the scandal is revealed. = 如果丑闻被揭露，公司的声誉将受到严重损害。

虚拟语气:
If the dog had been properly trained, it would not have mauled the child. = 如果这条狗经过适当的训练，它就不会袭击孩子了。
I wish the storm hadn't mauled the crops so badly. = 我真希望暴风雨没有这么严重地破坏庄稼。

被动语态:
