predetachments:/ˌpriːdɪˈtætʃmənts/| n.|预先分离，预先拆卸|The process of separating or detaching something before the main action or event.  It often refers to military units or equipment being prepared for deployment or removal in advance.|例句：The predetachments of the engineering units ensured the bridge was ready for the main force's crossing.  预先派遣的工程部队确保了桥梁能够让主力部队通行。|近义词：advance preparations, preliminary detachments|反义词：post-detachments, late detachments|名词
