haematogenesis:/ˌhiːmætəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |造血作用，血细胞生成|The formation of blood cells in the body.  This process involves the differentiation of hematopoietic stem cells into various blood cell lineages. |Example:  Disorders affecting haematogenesis can lead to anemia or leukemia.  造血障碍可导致贫血或白血病。|近义词：hemopoiesis, hematopoiesis |反义词：无
