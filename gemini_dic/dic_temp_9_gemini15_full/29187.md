cashierer:/kæʃɪr/| n. |收银员|A cashier is a person who handles financial transactions in a retail store, restaurant, or other business.  They typically receive payments from customers, operate cash registers, and balance their cash drawers. |The cashier gave me my change after I paid for my groceries.  收银员在我付完杂货款后给了我找零。|clerk, teller|thief, robber|名词
