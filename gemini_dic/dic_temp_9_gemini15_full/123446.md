monopoly:/məˈnɒpəli/|/ˈmɑːnəpəli/| n. |垄断；专卖；独占；专利权 |A monopoly exists when a single seller controls the supply of a particular good or service.  This lack of competition often leads to higher prices and lower quality.  It can also refer to the exclusive possession or control of something.|

例句：

The company has a monopoly on the production of this software. 这家公司垄断了这种软件的生产。

Standard Oil once held a monopoly over the oil industry in the United States. 标准石油公司曾经垄断了美国的石油产业。


近义词：control, domination, exclusive right, cartel

反义词：competition, free market

过去完成时:
