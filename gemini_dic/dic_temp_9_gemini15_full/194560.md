self-speeches:/selfˈspiːtʃɪz/| n.| 自我演讲；自言自语 | Self-speeches are private conversations one has with oneself, often involving internal dialogue and reflection.  They are not typically intended for an audience. | 例句：Her self-speeches often revealed her anxieties and insecurities.  她的自言自语经常暴露她的焦虑和不安全感。

例句：The athlete engaged in positive self-speeches before the competition to boost confidence.  这位运动员在比赛前进行了积极的自我鼓励，以增强信心。


近义词：self-talk, internal dialogue, soliloquy


反义词：public speaking, external communication


词性:名词
