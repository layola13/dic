nonascendancies: /ˌnɒnəˈsɛndənsiz/ | n. | 非继承 | The lack of inheritance or succession; instances where something is not inherited or passed down.  This word is rarely used and is essentially a combination of "non-" and "ascendancies". It describes situations where there's a break in the usual line of succession or inheritance. | Example: The company's nonascendancies in leadership led to instability. 公司领导层的非继承性导致了不稳定。 | 近义词: lack of succession, absence of inheritance, discontinuity | 反义词: succession, inheritance, continuity | 名词
