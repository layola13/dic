impatientness:/ɪmˈpeɪʃənts/| n. | 不耐烦；急躁 | The state of being impatient; a lack of patience.  It often manifests as restlessness, irritability, or a tendency to interrupt. | Example: Her impatientness was evident in the way she tapped her foot.  她焦躁不安，从她不停地跺脚就能看出来。  His impatientness led him to make rash decisions. 他的急躁导致他做出草率的决定。|近义词：impatience, restlessness, irritability, anxiety|反义词：patience, calmness, composure|名词
