hitches:/ˈhɪtʃɪz/| n. v.| 障碍；困难；系；拴住；|A hitch is a problem or difficulty that causes a temporary delay or interruption.  It can also refer to the act of fastening something, typically with a hook or knot.|例句：We encountered a few hitches during the move. 我们搬家时遇到了一些麻烦。|The horse was hitched to the fence. 马被拴在栅栏上。|近义词：snag, problem, difficulty, obstacle, impediment |反义词：smoothness, ease, solution
