divid:/dɪˈvaɪd/| vt. |分开；划分；分割；使分裂|To separate or be separated into parts.  The verb "divide" implies separating something into two or more parts. It can refer to physical objects, groups of people, or abstract concepts.|例句：We divided the cake into eight slices. 我们把蛋糕切成八块。|例句：The river divides the city into two parts. 这条河把城市分成两部分。|近义词：separate, split, partition, share|反义词：unite, combine, merge, join|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher divides the class into four groups. = 老师把班级分成四个小组。
She divides her time between work and family. = 她把时间分配在工作和家庭之间。


一般过去时:
They divided the land among themselves. = 他们把土地分给了自己。
The scientists divided the sample into two parts. = 科学家们把样本分成两部分。


现在完成时:
We have divided the tasks equally. = 我们已经平均分配了任务。
She has divided her inheritance among her children. = 她已经把遗产分给了她的孩子们。


过去完成时:
They had divided the spoils before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们就已经瓜分了战利品。
The committee had divided on the issue before the vote. = 在投票之前，委员会就已经就这个问题分歧了。


将来时:
We will divide the work tomorrow. = 我们明天将分配工作。
The company will divide its profits among its shareholders. = 公司将把利润分给股东们。


虚拟语气:
If we divided the resources fairly, everyone would be satisfied. = 如果我们公平地分配资源，每个人都会满意。
I wish we had divided the workload more evenly. = 我希望我们更平均地分配工作量。


被动语态:
The cake was divided into six pieces. = 蛋糕被切成了六块。
The class was divided into smaller groups for the activity. = 为了这次活动，班级被分成更小的组。


疑问句:
How will you divide the cost? = 你将如何分摊费用？
How is the land divided? = 土地是如何划分的？


否定句:
They did not divide the profits fairly. = 他们没有公平地分配利润。
The problem cannot be easily divided into smaller parts. = 这个问题不能轻易地分成更小的部分。
