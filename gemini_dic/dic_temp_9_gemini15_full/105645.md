kingdoms:/ˈkɪŋdəmz/| n. |王国；领域；界 |The word "kingdoms" refers to plural of kingdom, signifying multiple sovereign states ruled by a king or queen, or more figuratively,  distinct realms or areas of activity. |例句：Many ancient civilizations were divided into several kingdoms.  许多古代文明被分成几个王国。

例句：The animal kingdom is incredibly diverse. 动物王国极其多样化。


近义词：realms, empires, domains, spheres


反义词：无


词性:名词
