impregnator:/ɪmˈprɛɡneɪtər/| n.| 浸渍剂；施肥者；使怀孕者 | A substance used to saturate or treat a material, often to improve its properties (e.g., waterproofing, fire resistance).  It can also refer to someone or something that impregnates. | Example: The wood was treated with a deep impregnator to protect it from rot.  木材用深层浸渍剂处理以防止腐烂。The farmer used an impregnator to fertilize his fields. 农民用施肥机给田地施肥。|近义词：saturant, filler, inseminator |反义词：dehydrator (in the context of saturating a material)
