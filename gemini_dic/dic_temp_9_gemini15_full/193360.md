segregating:/ˈsɛɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |隔离；种族隔离；分开 |The act of separating or setting apart from others.  This often carries a negative connotation, especially in the context of racial or social segregation. |例句：The school was segregating students based on race. 学校根据种族隔离学生。  The factory is segregating the defective products from the good ones. 工厂正在将次品与合格品分开。|近义词：separating, isolating, partitioning |反义词：integrating, unifying, mixing |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is segregating its waste products. = 公司正在隔离其废物。
They are segregating the recyclables from the trash. = 他们正在将可回收物与垃圾分开。

一般过去时:
The government segregated neighborhoods by race. = 政府按种族隔离街区。
They segregated the sick patients from the healthy ones. = 他们将病人与健康人隔离开来。

现在完成时:
They have segregated the data for analysis. = 他们已经将数据隔离以进行分析。
The city has long segregated its public housing projects. = 这个城市长期以来一直对它的公共住房项目进行隔离。

过去完成时:
Before the war, they had segregated the communities. = 在战争爆发前，他们已经将社区隔离了。
The school had segregated students by ability level for many years. = 多年来，学校一直根据学生的水平将学生分开。

将来时:
The company will segregate the hazardous materials. = 公司将隔离危险材料。
They will segregate the different types of waste for proper disposal. = 他们将对不同类型的废物进行分类处理。

虚拟语气:
If they segregated the data properly, the analysis would be more accurate. = 如果他们正确地隔离数据，分析将更准确。
I wish they hadn't segregated the students based on their backgrounds. = 我希望他们不要根据学生的背景将学生分开。

被动语态:
The students were segregated by their teachers. = 学生们被老师分开了。
The waste materials are being segregated for recycling. = 废料正在被分类回收。

疑问句:
Are they segregating the children by age? = 他们是不是按年龄把孩子们分开？
Is the company segregating its operations in different locations? = 公司是否在不同地点隔离其业务？


否定句:
The school doesn't segregate its students. = 学校不隔离它的学生。
The factory doesn't segregate its products. = 工厂不隔离它的产品。


PHRASE->
segregate by = 按…分类
segregate from = 从…中分离
segregation of = …的分离
racial segregation = 种族隔离
segregation laws = 种族隔离法
