deconsecrates:/ˌdiːˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/| vt.|  使……不再神圣；取消……的圣职；亵渎|To remove the sacred character from (something consecrated).  To divest of priestly orders. |例句：The church was deconsecrated and turned into a museum. 这座教堂被取消了圣职，变成了博物馆。

The old temple was deconsecrated after years of neglect.  这座古老的寺庙由于多年的忽视而被取消了神圣的地位。


近义词：desecrate, profane, violate, defile|反义词：consecrate, sanctify|动词
