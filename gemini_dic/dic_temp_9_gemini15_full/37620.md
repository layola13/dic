communique:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪ/| n. |公报；正式公告；官方声明 |An official announcement, especially one made to the public or media.  |The government issued a communique denying the allegations.  政府发布公报否认这些指控。 |statement, announcement, bulletin |(无明显反义词) |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government issues communiques regularly. = 政府定期发布公报。
This company sends out a communique every quarter. = 这家公司每个季度都会发布一份公报。


一般过去时:
The president released a communique last night. = 总统昨晚发布了一份公报。
They issued a communique after the emergency meeting. = 紧急会议结束后，他们发布了一份公报。


现在完成时:
The authorities have issued several communiques on the matter. = 当局已就此事发布了几份公报。
The organization has not yet released a formal communique. = 该组织尚未发布正式公报。


过去完成时:
Before the announcement, they had prepared a draft communique. = 在宣布之前，他们已经准备了一份公报草案。
The media had already reported on the communique before the official release. = 在正式发布之前，媒体已经报道了这份公报。


将来时:
The spokesperson will release a communique later today. = 发言人今天晚些时候将发布一份公报。
The committee will issue a communique following their deliberations. = 委员会将在讨论之后发布一份公报。


虚拟语气:
If they had released a communique sooner, the confusion might have been avoided. = 如果他们早点发布公报，混乱可能就会避免。
I wish the company had issued a communique to address the concerns. = 我希望公司能发布一份公报来解决这些担忧。


被动语态:
A communique was released to the press. = 一份公报发布给了新闻界。
The communique is being translated into several languages. = 公报正在被翻译成多种语言。


疑问句:
Has a communique been issued yet? = 公报发布了吗？
When will the communique be released? = 公报何时发布？


否定句:
No communique was issued regarding the incident. = 关于此事没有发布任何公报。
The government did not issue a communique denying the rumors. = 政府没有发布公报否认这些谣言。
