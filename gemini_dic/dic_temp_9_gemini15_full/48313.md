decrescendo:/deɪkrəˈʃɛndoʊ/| n. v.|渐弱；减弱|A gradual decrease in loudness or intensity.  It can be used as a noun to describe the act of gradually decreasing in volume, or as a verb to indicate the process of something becoming less intense.|The orchestra played a beautiful decrescendo at the end of the movement. 乐队在乐章结尾演奏了一个优美的渐弱。 The storm gradually decrescendoed as the night wore on. 随着夜晚的推移，风暴逐渐减弱了。| diminuendo, tapering off | crescendo, fortissimo |n.v.
