scandalmongers:/ˈskændəlˌmʌŋɡərz/| n.|造谣者；散布丑闻者|People who spread scandalous rumors or gossip.  They delight in spreading damaging or embarrassing information about others, often without regard for the truth.|例句：The scandalmongers had a field day with the politician's indiscretion. 造谣者对这位政治家的不检点行为大肆渲染。
The scandalmongers' lies nearly ruined his reputation. 散布谣言的人的谎言几乎毁了他的名声。|近义词：gossip-mongers, rumour-mongers, talebearers, backbiters|反义词：truth-tellers, peacemakers
