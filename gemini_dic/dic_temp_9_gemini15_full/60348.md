egotist:/ˈiːɡəʊtɪst/| n. |自大者，自我中心的人 |A person who is excessively conceited or absorbed in themselves; someone who talks about themselves too much.  |He's such an egotist; the conversation always revolves around him. 他是个十足的自大狂，谈话总是围绕着他转。|近义词：narcissist, self-centered person, braggart |反义词：humble person, altruist |名词
