disciple:/dɪˈsaɪpəl/ | n. | 信徒；弟子；门徒 | A follower or student of a teacher, leader, or philosophy.  A disciple usually implies a strong commitment to the teachings of their leader. | Example:  Many disciples followed the Buddha's teachings.  许多信徒追随佛陀的教诲。

The early disciples of Jesus spread his message throughout the Roman Empire.  耶稣早期的门徒将他的信息传播到整个罗马帝国。


近义词: follower, student, adherent, devotee | 反义词: teacher, master, leader


词源:  From Old French *disciple, from Latin discipulus, from discere "to learn".  The word's root reflects the idea of learning from a teacher or mentor.
