lobbyists:/ˈlɒbiɪsts/ | n. |游说者，说客 |People who try to influence legislators or other decision-makers on behalf of a particular group or organization. They often work for corporations, interest groups, or non-profit organizations.  | Example:  The lobbyists spent months working to pass the new legislation. 游说者花了几个月的时间致力于通过新的立法。  |近义词：advocates, pressure groups, influencers |反义词：opponents, adversaries


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Lobbyists often meet with lawmakers. = 游说者经常会见立法者。
These lobbyists represent powerful corporations. = 这些游说者代表强大的公司。


一般过去时:
The lobbyists argued their case effectively last year. = 去年游说者有效地论证了他们的理由。
Several lobbyists contacted the senator. = 好几个游说者联系了参议员。


现在完成时:
Lobbyists have successfully influenced many laws. = 游说者已经成功地影响了许多法律。
They have lobbied for years on this issue. = 他们在这个问题上游说了好几年。


过去完成时:
The bill had been heavily lobbied against before the vote. = 在投票之前，该法案受到了大量的游说反对。
The committee had already met with the lobbyists. = 委员会已经会见了游说者。


将来时:
The corporation will hire more lobbyists next year. = 公司明年将雇佣更多游说者。
Lobbyists will continue to push for change. = 游说者将继续推动变革。


虚拟语气:
If the lobbyists had been more persuasive, the bill would have passed. = 如果游说者更有说服力，法案就会通过。
I wish the lobbyists would stop harassing my representatives. = 我希望游说者停止骚扰我的代表。


被动语态:
The legislation was heavily lobbied for by environmental groups. = 这项立法受到了环保团体的强烈游说。
The issue is being lobbied on by both sides. = 双方都在游说这个问题。


疑问句:
Who are the lobbyists representing this corporation? = 代表这家公司的游说者是谁？
How many lobbyists are involved in this campaign? = 有多少游说者参与了这场运动？


否定句:
The company did not hire any lobbyists. = 公司没有雇佣任何游说者。
The government isn't influenced by all lobbyists. = 政府并非受到所有游说者的影响。
