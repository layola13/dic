deactivates:/ˈdiːæktɪveɪts/| vt.|使失效；使停止活动；使钝化|To make something inactive or ineffective; to stop something from working. |例句：The technician deactivated the alarm system. 技术员使报警系统失效了。|例句：The cold weather deactivated the bacteria.  寒冷的天气使细菌钝化了。|近义词：disable, deactivate, inactivate, switch off|反义词：activate, energize, enable
