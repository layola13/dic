performances:/pərˈfɔːrmənsɪz/ | n. | 表演；演出；表现；工作效率 |  The plural form of "performance," referring to multiple instances of acting, playing music, or other displays of skill or talent, or to multiple instances of accomplishing a task or fulfilling a duty. It can also refer to the quality of an action or task. | 例句：The actor's performances were captivating.  演员的表演引人入胜。


The band's performances throughout the tour were consistently excellent. 乐队在巡演中的演出始终很棒。


近义词：shows, presentations, displays, achievements, accomplishments | 反义词：failures, inadequacies, shortfalls | 词性：名词
