offprints:/ˈɒfˌprɪnts/ | n. |抽印本，作者单行本 | Separate copies of an article or chapter, printed in advance of or in addition to a larger publication, and typically given to the author for distribution.  |The author received 50 offprints of his article. 作者收到了50份他文章的抽印本。| reprints, separates |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
