gloss:/ɡlɒs/ /ɡlɑːs/| n. v.|注释；光泽；解释；掩盖|A brief explanation of a word or phrase; a superficial or shiny appearance; to explain or interpret; to cover or gloss over something.|The glossary at the back of the book provided helpful glosses for difficult terms.  书后面的词汇表为难懂的术语提供了有用的解释。|The painting's gloss was remarkable, reflecting light beautifully. 这幅画的光泽非常出色，能很好地反射光线。|近义词：explanation, commentary, sheen, polish|反义词：dullness, obscurity
