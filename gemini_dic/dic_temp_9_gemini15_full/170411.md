prowl:/praʊl/| v. |潜行；徘徊；悄悄走动 |To move about stealthily, especially in search of prey or something to steal.  |例句：The tiger prowled through the jungle.  老虎在丛林中潜行。|例句：The burglar prowled the streets looking for an unlocked house.  窃贼在街上徘徊寻找没锁的门。|近义词：stalk, creep, slink, roam |反义词：stand still, remain stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat prowls the garden at night. = 猫晚上在花园里潜行。
He prowls the streets looking for trouble. = 他在街上游荡寻找麻烦。

一般过去时:
The lion prowled the savanna. = 狮子在稀树草原上潜行。
She prowled the shops looking for a bargain. = 她逛商店寻找便宜货。

现在完成时:
The police have prowled the area for days, but haven't found any clues. = 警方搜查了该地区几天，但没有发现任何线索。
He has prowled the streets for years, a shadow in the night. = 他多年来一直潜伏在街头，像夜晚的影子。

过去完成时:
The thief had prowled the neighborhood before committing the robbery. = 窃贼在实施抢劫之前已经潜伏在附近。
She had prowled the museum for hours before she found the painting. = 在找到那幅画之前，她已经在博物馆里徘徊了几个小时。

将来时:
The panther will prowl the forest tonight. = 黑豹今晚将在森林中潜行。
They will prowl the city for information. = 他们将在城市中寻找信息。

虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would prowl the rooftops. = 如果我是一只猫，我会在屋顶上潜行。
I wish I could prowl the streets unseen. = 我希望我能不被察觉地在街上游荡。

被动语态:
The streets were prowled by the police all night. = 警察整夜都在街上巡逻。
The area has been prowled by suspicious characters recently. = 最近可疑人物一直在该地区徘徊。

疑问句:
Does the dog prowl at night? = 狗晚上会潜行吗？
Will they prowl the dark alleys? = 他们会在黑暗的小巷里徘徊吗？

否定句:
The bear did not prowl near the village. = 熊没有在村庄附近徘徊。
The cat doesn't prowl during the day. = 猫白天不潜行。


PHRASE->
prowl around: 在...周围徘徊
prowl the streets: 在街上徘徊
prowl for: 寻找（猎物等）
prowl the night: 夜间潜行
on the prowl: 潜行；寻找机会
