parachutage:/ˌpærəˈʃuːtɑːʒ/ | n. |空投；空降；伞降 | The dropping of supplies or troops by parachute.  It involves the use of parachutes to deliver goods or personnel to a designated location. |Example: The army conducted a large-scale parachutages of supplies to the besieged city.  军队对被围困的城市进行了大规模的空投物资。 |近义词：airdrop, airlift |反义词：None specifically, but "ground transport" could be considered a contrasting term.
