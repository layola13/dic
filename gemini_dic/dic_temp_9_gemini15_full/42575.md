counterpicketing:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɪkɪtɪŋ/| n. |对位抗议，反示威 |The act of picketing in opposition to another picket line; a protest against a protest.  It's a form of counter-demonstration, often used by opposing labor unions, political groups, or other organizations. |Example: The workers' union organized counterpicketing to protest the company's unfair labor practices. 工人们的工会组织了反示威活动以抗议公司的不公平劳动行为。|近义词：counter-demonstration, protest against a protest |反义词：supporting picket, agreeing demonstration |名词
