engloom:/ɪnˈɡluːm/| vt.|笼罩，使阴暗；使忧郁|To cover or envelop in darkness or gloom; to overshadow; to depress.|例句：The storm engloomed the valley.  暴风雨笼罩着山谷。|例句：The news engloomed her spirits. 这个消息使她情绪低落。|近义词：obscure, overshadow, darken, depress|反义词：illuminate, brighten, cheer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm englooms the valley. = 暴风雨笼罩着山谷。
Fear englooms his heart. = 恐惧笼罩着他的心。

一般过去时:
The darkness engloomed the city. = 黑暗笼罩着城市。
Sadness engloomed her after the loss. = 失去后悲伤笼罩着她。

现在完成时:
The fog has engloomed the mountain. = 雾已笼罩了山峰。
Doubt has engloomed their plans. = 怀疑已笼罩了他们的计划。

过去完成时:
The clouds had engloomed the sky before the rain started. = 下雨前乌云已笼罩天空。
Despair had engloomed her before she found help. = 在她找到帮助之前，绝望已经笼罩了她。

将来时:
The approaching storm will engloom the coastline. = 即将到来的风暴将笼罩海岸线。
Anxiety will engloom him before the exam. = 考试前焦虑将会笼罩着他。

虚拟语气:
If the sun didn't appear, darkness would engloom the land. = 如果太阳没有出现，黑暗将会笼罩大地。
If only joy could engloom her heart instead. = 但愿快乐而不是悲伤能占据她的心房。

被动语态:
The village was engloomed by a thick fog. = 村庄被浓雾笼罩。
His spirit was engloomed by grief. = 他的精神被悲伤笼罩。

疑问句:
Will the approaching darkness engloom the path? = 即将到来的黑暗会笼罩这条路吗？
Does the coming winter engloom your outlook? = 即将来临的冬天是否让你感到悲观？

否定句:
The sun did not engloom the city. = 太阳没有笼罩城市。(This sentence is grammatically correct but semantically unusual, as the sun illuminates, not englooms.)
The light did not engloom the room; it brightened it. = 光线没有使房间阴暗；它使房间明亮。
