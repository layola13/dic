flamer:/ˈfleɪmər/| n.|煽动者；恶意攻击者；（网络）恶意攻击者|A person who deliberately provokes arguments or makes inflammatory remarks, especially online.|例句：He was banned from the forum for being a flamer. 他因为是煽动者而被禁止进入论坛。  例句：The online flamer targeted users with hateful messages.  网络上的恶意攻击者用仇恨信息攻击用户。|近义词：agitator, provocateur, troll|反义词：peacemaker, conciliator


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He flames other users online. 他在网上攻击其他用户。
The flamer posts inflammatory comments.  煽动者发布煽动性评论。

一般过去时:
He flamed me in the chat room. 他在聊天室里攻击我。
The troll flamed everyone in the thread.  这个巨魔攻击了帖子里的所有人。

现在完成时:
He has flamed many people on this forum. 他在这个论坛上攻击过很多人。
She has been a flamer for years. 她当煽动者已经很多年了。

过去完成时:
He had flamed her before, so she ignored him. 他以前就攻击过她，所以她无视了他。
They had flamed each other relentlessly before the moderator stepped in. 在版主介入之前，他们一直无情地互相攻击。

将来时:
He will flame you if you disagree with him. 如果你不同意他，他会攻击你。
They will likely flame anyone who challenges their views. 他们可能会攻击任何挑战他们观点的人。

虚拟语气:
If he flamed me again, I would report him. 如果他再次攻击我，我会举报他。
I wish he wouldn't flame people so much.  我希望他不要那么频繁地攻击别人。

被动语态:
He was flamed for his controversial opinion. 他因为有争议的观点而受到攻击。
Many users were flamed for expressing their dissent.  许多用户因为表达异见而受到攻击。

疑问句:
Is he a flamer? 他是煽动者吗？
Why is he flaming everyone? 他为什么攻击所有人？

否定句:
He didn't flame anyone in that post. 他在那篇文章里没有攻击任何人。
She doesn't usually flame people, but she was provoked. 她通常不会攻击人，但她被激怒了。
