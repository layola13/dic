carousels: /kəˈrʊzəlz/ | n. |旋转木马；旋转的装置 | A carousel is a type of amusement ride consisting of a rotating circular platform with seats for riders.  It can also refer to a rotating display of items, like images or products. | The children loved riding the carousel at the fair. 孩子们喜欢在游乐会上骑旋转木马。  The carousel of images on the website was mesmerizing. 网站上旋转的图片令人着迷。 | merry-go-round, roundabout |  static display | n.
