flattened:/ˈflætənd/ | adj. v. |压平的；变平的；使扁平；使衰弱|  To make something flat or level; to become flat or level.  The process of reducing something to a flat state, either physically or figuratively. |


例句：
The heavy rain flattened the crops.  | 大雨把庄稼压倒了。

The accident flattened the car. | 事故把汽车压扁了。


近义词：compressed, crushed, squashed, leveled, depressed


反义词：raised, elevated, inflated, expanded


词性: Verb (transitive and intransitive), Adjective
