exocrines:/ˈɛksoʊkraɪnz/ | n. | 外分泌腺；外分泌的 | Relating to or denoting secretion external to an organ or part, as opposed to internal secretion. Exocrine glands secrete their products into ducts that lead to the surface of an organ or body.  | Example: The pancreas has both endocrine and exocrine functions; its exocrine secretions aid in digestion.  胰腺既有内分泌功能，也有外分泌功能；其外分泌物有助于消化。 | 近义词：glandular secretion | 反义词：endocrine
