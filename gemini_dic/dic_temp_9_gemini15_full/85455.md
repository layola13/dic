/hæʃt/| n.|井号，散列，哈希|A symbol (#) used to indicate a number or topic on social media or in computer programming.  It is also used as a verb meaning to convert data into a hash.|例句：The hashtag #travelphotography is very popular on Instagram.  这个旅行摄影的标签#travelphotography在Instagram上很受欢迎。|近义词：pound sign, number sign|反义词：无
