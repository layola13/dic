gambler:/ˈɡæmblər/| n. |赌徒，投机者 | A person who gambles, especially one who does so habitually or excessively.  |He was a high-roller gambler, known for his extravagant bets. 他是一个高额赌注的赌徒，以其奢侈的赌注而闻名。| speculator, player | non-gambler, investor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gambler plays poker every night. = 这位赌徒每晚都玩扑克。
He gambles on horse races. = 他赌赛马。


一般过去时:
The gambler lost a fortune last night. = 这位赌徒昨晚输了一大笔钱。
She gambled away her savings. = 她把积蓄都赌掉了。


现在完成时:
He has gambled away all his money. = 他已经把所有的钱都赌光了。
She has been a gambler for many years. = 她当赌徒已经很多年了。


过去完成时:
He had gambled away his inheritance before he turned 30. = 他在30岁之前就把他继承的财产都赌掉了。
They had gambled all their money on one hand. = 他们把所有的钱都押在了一次牌局上。


将来时:
He will gamble again tomorrow. = 他明天还会赌博。
She will not gamble anymore. = 她不会再赌博了。


虚拟语气:

I wish I hadn't gambled away my money. = 我真希望我没有把我的钱都赌掉。

否定句:
