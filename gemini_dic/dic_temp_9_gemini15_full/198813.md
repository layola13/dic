side-swipe:/ˈsaɪdˌswaɪp/| n. vt.|侧面碰撞；轻蔑的攻击；轻微的批评|A side-swipe is a glancing blow or a brief, indirect attack.  It can refer to a physical collision or a verbal criticism. |例句：The car suffered a side-swipe during the accident. 这辆车在事故中受到了侧面碰撞。

The politician launched a side-swipe at his opponent during the debate.  这位政客在辩论中对他的对手进行了轻蔑的攻击。|近义词：glancing blow, brush, oblique attack, slight, criticism|反义词：direct attack, frontal assault, full-on criticism
