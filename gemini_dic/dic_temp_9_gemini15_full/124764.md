/ˌɛm ɑːr ˈtiː/ |abbr.|地铁，大众捷运系统|Mass Rapid Transit, a system of public transportation using trains.|例句：I take the MRT to work every day. 我每天乘地铁上班。|近义词：subway, underground, metro|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The MRT runs frequently during peak hours. = 高峰时段地铁运行频繁。
Many people use the MRT to commute. = 许多人使用地铁通勤。


一般过去时:
I took the MRT to the airport yesterday. = 我昨天乘地铁去机场。
The MRT was delayed due to a signal failure. = 由于信号故障，地铁延误了。


现在完成时:
I have used the MRT many times. = 我已经多次使用地铁了。
Have you ever ridden the MRT in Singapore? = 你曾在新加坡乘坐过地铁吗？


过去完成时:
I had already taken the MRT before the rain started. = 下雨之前我已经乘坐地铁了。
They had missed the MRT when they arrived at the station. = 他们到达车站时已经错过了地铁。


将来时:
I will take the MRT to the concert tomorrow. = 我明天将乘坐地铁去音乐会。
The new MRT line will open next year. = 新的地铁线路将于明年开通。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had taken the MRT instead of the bus. = 我真希望我乘坐地铁而不是公共汽车。
