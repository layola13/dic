prerestrains:/ˌpriːrɪˈstreɪnz/ | vt. | 预先约束；预先限制 | To restrain or restrict beforehand; to prevent something from happening in advance.  |例句：The strict regulations prerestrained any potential misuse of funds. 严格的规章预先约束了任何可能的资金滥用。

例句：The company prerestrained its employees from accessing certain websites. 公司预先限制了员工访问某些网站。


近义词：preclude, prevent, inhibit, restrict


反义词：permit, allow, enable


词性：动词
