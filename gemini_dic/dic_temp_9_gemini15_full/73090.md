footwear:/ˈfʊtˌwɛr/| n.|鞋类，鞋袜|Protective coverings for the feet, such as shoes, boots, sandals, etc.|例句：She bought a new pair of footwear for the hiking trip. 她为徒步旅行买了一双新鞋。|例句：The type of footwear you choose depends on the activity. 你选择的鞋类取决于活动类型。|近义词：shoes, boots, sandals|反义词：bare feet
