interpermeate: /ˌɪntərˈpɜːrmieɪt/ | v. | 渗透，弥漫，互相渗透 | To penetrate mutually or diffusely; to spread throughout | The two cultures interpermeated, influencing each other's customs and beliefs.  两种文化相互渗透，互相影响彼此的风俗和信仰。 | permeate, infuse, imbue | separate, segregate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The aroma of coffee interpermeates the air. = 咖啡的香味弥漫在空气中。
His ideas interpermeate his writing. = 他的思想渗透在他的写作中。


一般过去时:
The colors interpermeated to create a beautiful blend. = 颜色互相渗透，创造出美丽的混合。
The rain interpermeated the soil. = 雨水渗透了土壤。


现在完成时:
The two cultures have interpermeated over centuries. = 两种文化已经互相渗透了几个世纪。
His influence has interpermeated every aspect of the company. = 他的影响已经渗透到公司的各个方面。


过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the scent of baking bread had interpermeated the entire house. = 当我到达时，烘焙面包的香味已经弥漫了整个房子。
The old and new styles had interpermeated, creating a unique aesthetic. = 旧式和新式风格已经互相渗透，创造了一种独特的审美。


将来时:
The new technology will interpermeate all aspects of our lives. = 新技术将渗透到我们生活的方方面面。
The two groups will interpermeate as they work together. = 这两个群体在合作时将会互相融合。


虚拟语气:
If the two liquids were allowed to mix, they would interpermeate. = 如果允许两种液体混合，它们就会互相渗透。
I wish my ideas could interpermeate the minds of the decision-makers. = 我希望我的想法能够渗透到决策者的脑海中。


被动语态:
The fabric was interpermeated with silver threads. = 面料中渗透着银线。
The community was interpermeated by a sense of unity. = 社区充满了团结感。


疑问句:
Does the aroma of spices interpermeate the whole market? = 香料的香味是否弥漫整个市场？
How deeply did the foreign culture interpermeate the local traditions? = 外来文化在多大程度上渗透了当地传统？


否定句:
The two groups did not interpermeate; they remained distinct. = 这两个群体没有互相融合；它们仍然保持着各自的特色。
The new ideas did not interpermeate the old system easily. = 新思想并没有轻易地渗透到旧体系中。


PHRASE->
interpermeate with:  渗透于...之中
interpermeate into: 渗透进...
interpermeate throughout: 遍布于...
fully interpermeate: 完全渗透
mutually interpermeate: 互相渗透


词源:
inter- (between, among) + permeate (to spread throughout)  来自拉丁语 *per meāre (to go through)
