inofficially:/ˌɪnəˈfɪʃəli/ | adv. |非正式地，私下地 |  Not officially; in a way that is not officially recognized or sanctioned.  | The committee inofficially decided to postpone the vote. 委员会非正式地决定推迟投票。 | officially | formally


例句：

The news was inofficially confirmed by a source close to the government.  这条消息已被接近政府的消息来源非正式确认。


The company inofficially announced its new product line.  该公司非正式地宣布了其新的产品线。


近义词：unofficially, informally, privately, secretly

反义词：officially, formally, publicly
