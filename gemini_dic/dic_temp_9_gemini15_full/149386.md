pacifyingly:/ˈpæsɪfaɪɪŋli/| adv.| 安抚地，平静地 | In a way that soothes or calms.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed. |例句：She spoke pacifyingly to the crying child. 她安抚地对哭泣的孩子说话。

例句：He touched her arm pacifyingly. 他安抚地摸了摸她的胳膊。


近义词：soothingly, calmingly, reassuringly, tranquilly

反义词：aggressively, irritably, antagonistically


词性:副词
