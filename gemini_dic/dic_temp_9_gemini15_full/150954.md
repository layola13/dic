paralyzes:/ˈpærəlaɪzɪz/| v.|使瘫痪；使麻痹；使无力；使停止运转|To cause (a person, animal, or part of the body) to become partly or wholly incapable of movement. To bring to a standstill; stop completely.|例句：The stroke paralyzed his left side. 中风使他的左侧瘫痪。

The sudden news paralyzed her with fear.  突如其来的消息使她吓得僵住了。|近义词：cripple, disable, incapacitate|反义词：activate, energize, invigorate


USAGE->

The heat paralyzes the engine. = 炎热使发动机停止运转。
