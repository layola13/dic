criticisms:/ˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/| n.|批评；批判；指责|The act of criticizing or the expression of disapproval.  It can refer to a single instance of criticism or a collection of criticisms.  The plural form indicates multiple criticisms.|例句：His criticisms were harsh but fair. 他的批评很严厉，但很公正。


例句：The film received a barrage of criticisms after its release. 这部电影上映后受到了大量的批评。


近义词：condemnation, censure, rebuke, objections, complaints


反义词：praise, commendation, approval


词性:名词
