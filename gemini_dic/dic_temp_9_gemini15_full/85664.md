hauteurs:/oʊˈtɜːrz/| n.|傲慢；自大；高傲；高处|The word "hauteurs" refers to a proud or arrogant disdain for those considered inferior; it can also refer to great heights.  It suggests a condescending attitude and a sense of superiority. |例句：She treated the servants with hauteur. 她对仆人傲慢无礼。

例句：From the mountain's hauteurs, the valley looked tiny. 从山顶上看，山谷显得非常渺小。|近义词：arrogance, pride, superciliousness, disdain, loftiness |反义词：humility, modesty, deference
