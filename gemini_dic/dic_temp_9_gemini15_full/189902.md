sample:/ˈsæmpəl/| n. |样本，示例，样品|A representative part or a single item from a larger whole, intended to show the quality or nature of the whole.  |例句：The lab technician analyzed a sample of the patient's blood. 实验室技术人员分析了患者的血样。|例句：This painting is a sample of his early work. 这幅画是他早期作品的范例。|近义词：specimen, example, illustration|反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This sample represents the quality of our product. = 这个样品代表了我们产品的质量。
The doctor took a blood sample for testing. = 医生取了血样进行化验。

一般过去时:
They showed us a sample of the new fabric. = 他们给我们展示了新面料的样品。

We have already received the sample we ordered. = 我们已经收到了我们订购的样品。
Scientists have analyzed numerous samples from the site. = 科学家们已经分析了该地点的许多样本。

过去完成时:
