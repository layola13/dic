deadener:/ˈdedənər/| n.|减震器；减音器；麻醉剂；使人厌倦的事物|Something that reduces or dampens the effect of something else, such as noise, vibration, or pain.|The thick carpet acted as a deadener for the noise from the street. 厚厚的地毯起到了降低街道噪音的作用。|近义词：dampener, muffler, analgesic, bore|反义词：stimulant, amplifier
