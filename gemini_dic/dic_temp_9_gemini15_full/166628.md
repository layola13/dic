presented:/prɪˈzɛntɪd/| v.| 呈现；介绍；提交；赠送 | To show, offer, or give something to someone.  It implies a formal or deliberate act of showing or giving. |例句：The scientist presented his findings to the committee.  科学家向委员会提交了他的研究结果。

例句：He presented her with a bouquet of flowers. 他送给她一束鲜花。


近义词：showed, offered, submitted, gave, displayed

反义词：withheld, concealed, kept secret

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He presents his ideas clearly. 他清晰地表达了他的想法。
She presents a compelling argument. 她提出了一个令人信服的论点。


一般过去时:
The professor presented the lecture yesterday. 教授昨天做了演讲。
They presented their project to the board. 他们向董事会提交了他们的项目。


现在完成时:
She has presented her work at many conferences. 她已在许多会议上展示了她的作品。
He has presented a convincing case. 他已经提出了一个令人信服的案例。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had presented their proposals. 会议之前，他们已经提交了他们的提案。
After she had presented her evidence, the jury reached a verdict.  在她提交证据后，陪审团做出了裁决。


将来时:
He will present his research at the upcoming symposium. 他将在即将到来的研讨会上发表他的研究成果。
They will present awards to the winners. 他们将向获奖者颁奖。


虚拟语气:
If he presented more evidence, the judge might reconsider. 如果他提交更多证据，法官可能会重新考虑。
I wish I had presented a better argument. 我希望我当时能提出更好的论点。


被动语态:
The report was presented to the government. 报告提交给了政府。
The award was presented by the mayor. 奖项由市长颁发。


疑问句:
When will the results be presented? 结果什么时候公布？
Who presented the keynote speech? 谁做了主题演讲？


否定句:
He didn't present any evidence. 他没有提供任何证据。
She didn't present her case effectively. 她没有有效地陈述她的案情。


PHRASE->
present oneself:  展现自己
present a case: 提出一个案例
present an argument: 提出论点
present a challenge: 提出挑战
present findings: 提交研究结果
