popinjay:/ˈpɒpɪnˌdʒeɪ/| n. | 花花公子；爱炫耀的人；傻瓜 | A popinjay is a vain and foppish young man, often one who is also boastful and shallow.  They are characterized by their showy clothes and superficial charm.  The term carries a strong connotation of disdain and mockery. | Example: He strutted around like a popinjay, showing off his new clothes. 他像个花花公子一样趾高气扬地四处炫耀他的新衣服。|近义词：fop, dandy, coxcomb, peacock |反义词：humble, modest, unassuming |名词
