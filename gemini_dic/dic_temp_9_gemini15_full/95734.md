inconsiderately:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪdərətli/| adv. | 不体谅地；粗心地；轻率地 | Acting without consideration for the feelings or needs of others;thoughtlessly. |例句：He inconsiderately interrupted the speaker. 他不顾场合地打断了发言者。|例句：She inconsiderately left her dirty dishes in the sink. 她粗心地把脏盘子留在水槽里。|近义词：thoughtlessly, carelessly, heedlessly, recklessly |反义词：considerately, thoughtfully, carefully


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inconsiderately interrupts others. = 他不体谅地打断别人。
She inconsiderately leaves her belongings everywhere. = 她粗心地把东西乱扔。


一般过去时:
He inconsiderately interrupted the meeting. = 他不顾场合地打断了会议。
She inconsiderately left her child alone. = 她粗心地把孩子独自留下。


现在完成时:
He has inconsiderately ignored my emails. = 他一直不体谅地忽略我的邮件。
She has inconsiderately used all the milk. = 她粗心地用完了所有的牛奶。


过去完成时:
He had inconsiderately forgotten his promise. = 他之前不体谅地忘记了他的承诺。
She had inconsiderately spilled coffee all over the table. = 她之前粗心地把咖啡洒了一桌子。


将来时:
He will inconsiderately leave the lights on. = 他将会粗心地把灯开着。
She will inconsiderately overspend her money again. = 她将会再次粗心地超支。


虚拟语气:
If he had acted considerately, things wouldn't have turned out so badly. = 如果他当时考虑周全一些，事情就不会变得这么糟糕。
I wish she hadn't acted so inconsiderately. = 我真希望她当时不要那么不体谅。


被动语态:
His actions were inconsiderately planned.  = 他的行动计划考虑不周。
The problem was inconsiderately handled. = 这个问题处理得很粗心。


疑问句:
Was he being inconsiderate when he said that? = 他这么说的时候是不是很粗鲁？
Did she act inconsiderately towards her colleagues? = 她对同事的行为是否不体谅？


否定句:
He did not act inconsiderately; he simply didn't know.  = 他并非故意不体谅；他只是不知道。
She didn’t inconsiderately leave the door open; she closed it carefully. = 她并没有粗心地把门留着开着；她小心地关上了门。


PHRASE->
inconsiderately act 行为不体贴
inconsiderately spoken  说话不考虑后果
inconsiderately planned 计划不周全
inconsiderately handled 处理粗心
inconsiderately behaved 行为粗鲁


词源:
in- (否定前缀) + considerately (体贴地，考虑周到地)  considerate 的副词形式，considerate 源于consider (考虑)。
