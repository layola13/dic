misbehave:/ˌmɪs bɪˈheɪv/| v.|行为不端，举止不良|To behave badly or in a way that is not acceptable.  |例句：The children misbehaved during the school trip. 孩子们在学校旅行期间行为不端。
例句：He was punished for misbehaving in class. 他因为在课堂上行为不端而受到惩罚。|近义词：act up, be naughty, play up|反义词：behave well, obey, be well-behaved|动词


USAGE->

The children misbehave frequently. = 孩子们经常行为不端。
He usually misbehaves when he's tired. = 他累了的时候通常会行为不端。


一般过去时:
The dog misbehaved and chewed up my shoes. = 狗行为不端，把我的鞋子嚼烂了。
