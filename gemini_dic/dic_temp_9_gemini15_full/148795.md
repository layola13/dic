overvaluation:/ˌoʊvərˌvæljuˈeɪʃən/| n. |高估；估价过高|The act of assigning a value to something that is higher than its actual worth.  It can refer to assets, currencies, or even people.  Overvaluation often leads to inflated prices and potential losses when the true value is eventually revealed.|The stock market experienced a period of overvaluation before the crash.  股票市场在崩盘前经历了一段估值过高的时期。| undervaluation |fair valuation| 名词
