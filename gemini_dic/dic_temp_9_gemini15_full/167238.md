preterm:/ˈpriːtɜːrm/| adj.|早产的，过早的|Born or occurring before the expected time, especially of a baby born before the 28th week of pregnancy.|例句：The baby was preterm and needed special care. 这个婴儿早产，需要特别护理。  例句：The preterm election was called due to the political crisis. 由于政治危机，提前举行了选举。|近义词： premature, untimely |反义词：full-term, late-term
