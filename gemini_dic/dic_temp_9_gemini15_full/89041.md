holoblastic: /ˌhɒləˈblæstɪk/ | adj. | 完全卵裂的；全裂的 | Relating to or characterized by holoblastic cleavage, a type of cell division in which the entire egg is divided into blastomeres.  This is typical of eggs with relatively little yolk, such as those of many marine invertebrates and mammals. | Example: "Sea urchin eggs undergo holoblastic cleavage." = 海胆卵进行完全卵裂。 | 近义词：complete cleavage | 反义词：meroblastic
