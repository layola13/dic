outglare:/ˌaʊtˈɡleər/| vt.| 怒目而视，超过…的强光|To stare angrily or fiercely at someone; to surpass in brightness.|例句：The sun outglared the city lights.  太阳的光芒盖过了城市灯光。

例句：He outglared his opponent, trying to intimidate him. 他怒视着对手，试图恐吓他。


近义词：outshine, overshadow, glare at


反义词：look kindly at, ignore


词性:动词
