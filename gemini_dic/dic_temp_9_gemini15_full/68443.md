falx:/fælks/| n.|镰刀；大脑镰|A falx is a sickle-shaped structure, particularly referring to anatomical structures resembling a sickle.  In anatomy, it most commonly refers to the falx cerebri, a fold of dura mater separating the two cerebral hemispheres of the brain.  |例句：The falx cerebri is a crucial part of the brain's protective structure. 大脑镰是大脑保护结构的关键部分。|例句：The farmer used a falx to harvest the wheat. 农民用镰刀收割小麦。|近义词：sickle, scythe|反义词：N/A
