free-float:/friːˈfloʊt/ | adj. | 自由浮动；自由交易 |  Refers to a situation where something, typically a currency or a company's shares, is not subject to artificial controls or restrictions and is allowed to fluctuate freely in the market.  | Example: The company's shares are free-floating on the stock exchange. 这家公司的股票在证券交易所自由交易。 | 近义词：unrestricted, unregulated, independent | 反义词：fixed, pegged, controlled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The exchange rate free-floats. = 汇率自由浮动。
The stock price free-floats. = 股价自由浮动。


一般过去时:
The currency free-floated until the crisis. = 在危机之前，货币自由浮动。
The shares free-floated for many years. = 这些股票自由交易了很多年。


现在完成时:
The market has free-floated since deregulation. = 自从放松管制以来，市场一直自由浮动。
The company's stock has free-floated successfully. = 这家公司的股票成功地自由交易了。


过去完成时:
The central bank had free-floated the currency before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，中央银行已经让货币自由浮动。
