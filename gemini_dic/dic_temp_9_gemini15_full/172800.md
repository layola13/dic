quadrillion:/kwɑːˈdrɪljən/ | n. |千万亿；百万的百万的百万|A quadrillion is a number equal to 10<sup>15</sup> (one million billion, or a million million million) in the short scale, and 10<sup>24</sup> (one million sextillion) in the long scale.  It's a very large number used to express extremely vast quantities.  The use of "quadrillion" varies slightly depending on the numbering system used (short scale or long scale).  In the short scale (used in the US, UK, and most of the world), a quadrillion is 1,000,000,000,000,000.  In the long scale (used in some European countries), it is 10<sup>24</sup>.|The national debt is in the quadrillions of dollars.|近义词：quintillion, sextillion |反义词：fraction, infinitesimal
