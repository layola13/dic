dichotomies:/daɪˈkɒtəmiːz/ | n. | 二分法；对立；矛盾 |  The term refers to a division into two mutually exclusive, often contradictory, categories.  It often implies a simplification of a complex issue.  Dichotomies are used in various fields to simplify analysis or highlight contrasting concepts. |  The dichotomy between good and evil is a recurring theme in literature.  The false dichotomy between reason and emotion often hinders understanding. |  binary, duality, antithesis |  synthesis, unity, continuity | 名词
