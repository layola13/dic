multisport:/ˈmʌltiˌspɔːrt/| n. |多项目运动；多项运动 |A sport involving multiple disciplines or activities.  For example, a multisport event might include swimming, cycling, and running.  |例句：The triathlon is a popular multisport event.  铁人三项是一项流行的多项目运动。

例句：She trains for various multisport competitions throughout the year. 她全年都在为各种多项目运动比赛训练。


近义词：multi-event, combined sports


反义词：single sport, monosport


词性:名词
