This can manifest in various ways, from a preference for French fashion and cuisine to a belief in the inherent superiority of French culture and values.|例句：Her gallomania was evident in her exclusive preference for French films and literature.  她的狂法症在她只喜欢法国电影和文学这一点上表现得很明显。|近义词：francophilia,  France-mania|反义词：francophobia
