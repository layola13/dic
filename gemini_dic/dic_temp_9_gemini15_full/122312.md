mitts:/mɪts/| n.|手套；拳击手套|A mitt is a type of glove, typically without fingers, worn to protect the hands.  They can be used for various purposes, including playing sports like baseball or softball, or simply for warmth.|例句：The catcher wore a large mitt to protect his hands from the fastball.  捕手戴着一个大手套来保护他的手免受快速球的伤害。


例句：I need a pair of warm mitts for winter. 我需要一双暖和的手套过冬。|近义词：gloves, mittens|反义词：bare hands
