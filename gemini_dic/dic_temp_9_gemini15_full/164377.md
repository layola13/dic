preemployee: /ˌpriːɪmˈplɔɪiː/ | n. | 预备员工，候选员工 | A person who is being considered for a job but has not yet been hired.  This term often refers to someone who is undergoing background checks, interviews, or other pre-employment processes. |  The company conducted thorough background checks on all preemployees. 这家公司对所有候选员工进行了彻底的背景调查。  The preemployee training program prepared candidates for their roles. 预备员工培训项目为候选人做好角色准备。 |  candidate, applicant | employee |  n.
