chumps:/tʃʌmps/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋，蠢货 |A chump is a foolish or gullible person.  They are easily tricked or deceived.  The word often carries a connotation of contempt or ridicule. |例句：Don't be such a chump and fall for that scam. 不要像个傻瓜一样上当受骗。
He was a chump to believe her lies. 他真傻，居然相信她的谎话。|近义词：fool, idiot, dupe, sucker |反义词：genius, mastermind, clever person
