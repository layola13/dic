remodel:/ˌriːˈmɒdəl/ /ˌriːˈmɑːdl/| vt.|改建；改造；重塑|To change the appearance, structure, or system of something, typically to improve it.|例句：We're going to remodel the kitchen. 我们打算改建厨房。|例句：The company remodeled its marketing strategy. 公司重塑了其营销策略。|近义词：renovate, refurbish, reconstruct|反义词：destroy, demolish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They remodel their house every year. = 他们每年都翻新他们的房子。
He remodels old cars and sells them. = 他改装旧车然后卖掉。

一般过去时:
She remodeled the bathroom last month. = 她上个月重新装修了浴室。
We remodeled the garden to make it more inviting. = 我们重新设计了花园，使其更具吸引力。

现在完成时:
They have remodeled their entire apartment. = 他们已经重新装修了他们的整个公寓。
She has remodeled her business plan to attract more investors. = 她已经修改了她的商业计划以吸引更多投资者。

过去完成时:
He had remodeled the car before he sold it. = 他在卖车之前已经改装了那辆车。
They had remodeled the kitchen twice before they were satisfied. = 他们对厨房进行了两次改造才满意。

将来时:
We will remodel the living room next spring. = 我们明年春天将重新装修客厅。
They will remodel their website to improve user experience. = 他们将重新设计他们的网站以改善用户体验。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would remodel the entire house. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会重新装修整栋房子。
I wish I could remodel my life and start over. = 我希望我能重塑我的生活并重新开始。

被动语态:
The kitchen is being remodeled at the moment. = 厨房目前正在装修中。
The old building will be remodeled into a modern hotel. = 这座旧建筑将被改建成一家现代化酒店。

疑问句:
Are you going to remodel the bedroom? = 你打算重新装修卧室吗？
Will they remodel the office this year? = 他们今年会重新装修办公室吗？

否定句:
We didn't remodel the house last year. = 我们去年没有装修房子。
They are not going to remodel the bathroom this time. = 他们这次不会装修浴室。
