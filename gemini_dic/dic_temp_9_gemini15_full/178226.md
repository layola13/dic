recompare:/ˌriːkəmˈpeər/| vt.|重新比较，再比较|To compare again; to make a second comparison.|例句：Let's recompare the two proposals and see which one is better.  让我们重新比较这两个提案，看看哪个更好。  例句：After reviewing the data, we decided to recompare the results with the previous study. 在审查数据后，我们决定将结果与之前的研究进行重新比较。|近义词：reexamine, review, reconsider|反义词：accept without question, disregard
