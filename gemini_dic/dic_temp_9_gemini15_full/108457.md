lay aside:/leɪ əˈsaɪd/ | vt. |搁置；把…放在一边；储蓄；放弃 |To put something to one side, especially for a later time; to save or store something; to give up or abandon something.  |例句：Let's lay aside our differences and work together.  让我们搁置分歧，一起努力。
He laid aside a portion of his earnings each month. 他每个月都存一部分收入。|近义词：set aside, put aside, reserve, postpone, abandon |反义词：retain, keep, continue, pursue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lays aside his work to answer the phone. 他放下工作接电话。
They lay aside some money for their vacation. 他们存了一些钱用于度假。

一般过去时:
She laid aside her knitting to greet the visitor. 她放下针线活去迎接客人。
He laid aside his ambition to start a family. 他放弃了他的雄心壮志去组建家庭。

现在完成时:
We have laid aside our differences and become friends. 我们已经放下分歧成为朋友了。
She has laid aside a considerable sum of money for her retirement. 她为退休存了一大笔钱。

过去完成时:
They had laid aside their plans for a trip when the crisis hit.  危机来临时，他们已经搁置了旅行计划。
He had laid aside his concerns about his job when he received the good news. 他在收到好消息后，已经不再担心他的工作了。

将来时:
I will lay aside my concerns until after the meeting. 我将把我的担忧留到会议之后。
They will lay aside their differences and find a solution.  他们将搁置分歧，找到解决办法。

虚拟语气:
If I laid aside my fears, I could achieve my goals. 如果我不害怕，我就能实现我的目标。
I wish I had laid aside more money for a rainy day. 我真希望我为以防万一存了更多的钱。

被动语态:
The project was laid aside due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目被搁置了。
His concerns were laid aside after he received reassurance.  在他得到保证后，他的担忧被消除了。

疑问句:
Have you laid aside enough money for the down payment? 你已经为首付存够钱了吗？
Did she lay aside her personal feelings to make a fair judgment? 她是否摒弃个人感情做出了公正的判断？

否定句:
He didn't lay aside his prejudices despite the evidence.  尽管有证据，他也没有放下他的偏见。
They haven't laid aside their disagreement about the budget. 他们还没有就预算问题达成一致。
