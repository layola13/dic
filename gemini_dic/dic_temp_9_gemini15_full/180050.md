reflectingly: /rɪˈflɛktɪŋli/ | adv. | 反思地；沉思地 |  In a thoughtful or reflective manner; showing contemplation.  |例句：He looked reflectingly at the photo, lost in memories. 他若有所思地望着照片，沉浸在回忆中。


She sat reflectingly by the fire, pondering her future. 她坐在火炉旁沉思，思考着她的未来。


近义词：thoughtfully, contemplatively, pensively, musingly | 反义词：carelessly, rashly, impulsively

词性: 副词
