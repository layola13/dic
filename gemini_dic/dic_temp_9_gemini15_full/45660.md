curtailment:/kərˈteɪlmənt/| n. |缩减；减少；限制 |The curtailment of the project was due to budget cuts.  项目的缩减是由于预算削减。|近义词：reduction, decrease, limitation |反义词：expansion, increase, extension


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government plans a curtailment of spending. = 政府计划削减开支。
This policy leads to a curtailment of freedom. = 这项政策导致自由受到限制。


一般过去时:
The company announced a curtailment of its operations last year. = 公司去年宣布缩减业务。
There was a significant curtailment of services during the storm. = 暴风雨期间服务受到了大幅度缩减。


现在完成时:
The recent economic downturn has resulted in a curtailment of many projects. = 最近的经济衰退导致许多项目被缩减。
We have seen a curtailment of press freedom in recent years. = 近年来，我们看到新闻自由受到了限制。


过去完成时:
The project had already seen a curtailment before the funding was completely withdrawn. = 在资金完全撤回之前，该项目就已经被缩减了。
They had implemented a curtailment of production due to low demand. = 由于需求低迷，他们已经实施了减产措施。


将来时:
The company will announce a curtailment of its workforce next month. = 公司将于下个月宣布裁员。
They predict a curtailment of services due to the upcoming strike. = 他们预测由于即将到来的罢工，服务将受到限制。


虚拟语气:
If there were a curtailment of military spending, we could invest more in education. = 如果削减军费开支，我们可以更多地投资于教育。
It is preferable that there is no curtailment of basic rights. = 最好不要限制基本权利。


被动语态:
Production was curtailed due to the shortage of raw materials. = 由于原材料短缺，生产被缩减了。
Further curtailment of the program is being considered. = 正在考虑进一步缩减该项目。


疑问句:
Is there any curtailment of the proposed plans? = 拟议的计划有缩减吗？
Will there be a curtailment of benefits for employees? = 员工的福利会有所削减吗？


否定句:
There was no curtailment of the festival despite the bad weather. = 尽管天气恶劣，节日并没有缩减。
The government refused to implement any curtailment of its policies. = 政府拒绝对其实施任何政策限制。


PHRASE->
curtailment of rights = 权利的限制
curtailment of freedom = 自由的限制
curtailment of spending = 支出的削减
curtailment of production = 生产的缩减
curtailment of services = 服务的缩减


词源:
From Middle English *curtaillen, from Anglo-French curteiller, from Old French cortailer, from curt “short” + tailler “to cut”.  Literally "to cut short".
