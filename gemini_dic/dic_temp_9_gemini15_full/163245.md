preconcentration: /ˌpriːkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən/ | n. |预富集；预浓缩|The process of increasing the concentration of a substance before analysis or further processing.  It is often used to improve the detection limit or sensitivity of an analytical method. |例句：Preconcentration techniques are crucial for trace element analysis in environmental samples. 预富集技术对于环境样品中痕量元素的分析至关重要。


例句：The sample underwent preconcentration before being analyzed by atomic absorption spectroscopy. 样品在用原子吸收光谱法分析之前进行了预富集。


近义词：concentration enrichment, pre-concentration


反义词：dilution


词性: 名词
