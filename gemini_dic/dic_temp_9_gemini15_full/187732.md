roote:/ruːt/| n.|根；根源；起因|The root of the problem is a lack of communication. 问题的根源在于沟通不足。|近义词：source, origin, base|反义词：branch, offshoot, result


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tree has strong roots. = 这棵树根系发达。
The problem's roots lie in poor planning. = 问题的根源在于糟糕的计划。

一般过去时:
The plant's roots reached deep into the soil. = 植物的根扎得很深。
They traced the rumor back to its roots. = 他们追溯谣言的源头。


现在完成时:
The roots have grown significantly this year. = 根系今年长得很大。
We've finally gotten to the root of the matter. = 我们终于找到了问题的症结所在。


过去完成时:
The roots had already penetrated the clay before the drought hit. = 干旱来临之前，根系已经穿透了粘土。
They had traced the family tree back to its roots centuries ago.  = 他们几个世纪前就追溯了家谱的起源。


将来时:
The roots will grow deeper with time. = 根系会随着时间的推移而长得更深。
We will get to the root of this mystery. = 我们将找到这个谜团的真相。


虚拟语气:
If the roots were stronger, the tree would survive. = 如果根系更强壮，这棵树就能存活。
I wish I knew the root cause of his anger. = 我真希望知道他生气的原因。


被动语态:
The roots were damaged by the storm. = 根系被暴风雨破坏了。
The problem's roots are being investigated by experts. = 问题的根源正在由专家调查。


疑问句:
What are the roots of this conflict? = 这次冲突的根源是什么？

否定句:

His explanation didn't get to the root of the problem. = 他的解释没有找到问题的症结所在。
