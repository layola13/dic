corolline:/ˈkɒrəlaɪn/| adj.|花冠状的；似花冠的|Resembling a corolla; having the form or arrangement of a corolla.  The term often refers to structures in marine invertebrates that are reminiscent of the petals of a flower. |Example: The coralline algae created a beautiful, flower-like structure on the reef.  =  珊瑚藻在礁石上创造了一个美丽的花状结构。|近义词：floral, flower-like|反义词：non-floral
