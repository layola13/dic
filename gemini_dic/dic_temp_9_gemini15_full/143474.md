outgalloping: /ˌaʊtˈɡæləpɪŋ/ | v. |超越…的速度；比…跑得快 |  To surpass in speed; to outrun.  This word describes the act of running or moving faster than someone or something else. It implies a significant difference in speed. |

例句：

The greyhound outgalloped all the other dogs in the race. = 灰狗在比赛中超过了所有其他狗。

The young horse outgalloped its mother across the field. = 小马在田野里跑得比它母亲快。


近义词：outrun, outpace, exceed, surpass

反义词：be outpaced by, be overtaken by


词性：动词
