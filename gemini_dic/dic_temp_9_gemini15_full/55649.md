dithering:/ˈdɪðəriŋ/| n. v.|犹豫不决；畏缩；闪烁；(图像)抖动|The act of being indecisive or hesitant; a wavering or trembling effect, especially in an image.|例句：He was dithering over which dress to wear. 他犹豫不决，不知道穿哪件衣服。  The image was slightly dithered to reduce the appearance of banding. 图片略微抖动以减少条带状外观。|近义词：hesitation, vacillation, wavering, faltering|反义词： decisiveness, resoluteness
