dissuade:/dɪˈsweɪd/| vt.|劝阻，劝退|To persuade (someone) not to take a particular course of action.|例句：I tried to dissuade him from going, but he wouldn't listen. 我试图劝他不要去，但他不肯听。|例句：Nothing could dissuade her from her purpose. 什么也无法劝退她。|近义词：deter, discourage, prevent|反义词：encourage, persuade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dissuades his friend from smoking. = 他劝他的朋友不要吸烟。
She dissuades her daughter from marrying that man. = 她劝女儿不要嫁给那个人。


一般过去时:
They dissuaded me from investing in that company. = 他们劝我不要投资那家公司。
The police dissuaded the protestors from marching on the government building. = 警察劝阻抗议者不要向政府大楼进军。


现在完成时:
We have dissuaded him from making that risky decision. = 我们已经劝他不要做那个冒险的决定。
She has dissuaded many students from dropping out of school. = 她已经劝退了许多学生不要辍学。


过去完成时:
They had dissuaded him from going to the party, but he went anyway. = 他们曾劝他不要去参加派对，但他还是去了。
I had dissuaded her from quitting her job, but she did it anyway. = 我曾劝她不要辞职，但她还是辞职了。


将来时:
I will dissuade him from taking that job. = 我将劝他不要接受那份工作。
They will dissuade the children from playing near the road. = 他们将劝孩子们不要在路边玩耍。


虚拟语气:
I would dissuade him, if I could. = 如果可以，我会劝退他。
If I had been there, I would have dissuaded her from doing that. = 如果我在那里，我会劝她不要那样做。


被动语态:
He was dissuaded from entering the competition. = 他被劝退，没有参加比赛。
She was dissuaded by her parents from travelling alone. = 她被父母劝阻，没有独自旅行。


疑问句:
Can you dissuade him from this foolish plan? = 你能劝他放弃这个愚蠢的计划吗？
Will anything dissuade him from his goal? = 有什么能劝阻他放弃目标吗？


否定句:
I couldn't dissuade him from his course of action. = 我无法劝他改变主意。
They failed to dissuade her from going. = 他们没能劝阻她离开。


PHRASE->
dissuade someone from doing something = 劝某人不要做某事
dissuade someone against something = 劝某人反对某事
be dissuaded from = 被劝阻不做…
try to dissuade someone = 试图劝阻某人
strongly dissuade = 强烈劝阻


词源:
dissuade comes from the Old French word *dissuader*, which is composed of the prefix *dis-* (meaning "away, apart") and *suader* (meaning "to advise, persuade").  Therefore, dissuade literally means "to advise away from" or "to persuade against".
