glissando:/ɡlɪˈsændoʊ/| n.|滑音|A gliding musical effect produced by rapidly sliding the finger(s) up or down a string or keyboard.|例句：The pianist executed a beautiful glissando across the keyboard.  钢琴家在键盘上演奏了一个优美的滑音。

The violinist's glissando added a dramatic flair to the performance. 小提琴家的滑音为演奏增添了戏剧性的韵味。|近义词：slide, sweep|反义词：staccato
