jujitsu:/dʒuːˈdʒɪtsuː/ | n. |柔术，柔道 |A Japanese martial art emphasizing grappling and throws, rather than striking. It is characterized by its use of leverage, joint locks, pins, and throws to subdue an opponent.  |例句：He learned jujitsu to improve his self-defense skills. 他学习柔术来提高他的自卫能力。
He practiced jujitsu for years before competing. 他在比赛前练习了多年的柔术。|近义词：judo, grappling, martial art |反义词：boxing, karate (in terms of emphasis on striking)
