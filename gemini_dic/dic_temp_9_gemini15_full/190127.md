sanhita:/sənˈhiːtə/| n. |圣典；吠陀经的章节|A samhita is a collection of Vedic hymns, mantras, and prose texts, forming a foundational scripture in Hinduism.  It's often organized systematically, unlike the later Brahmanas.  Different samhitas exist, each associated with a specific Veda.|The Rigveda Samhita is the oldest and most important of the four Vedas. |近义词：collection, compilation, scripture|反义词：None specifically.
