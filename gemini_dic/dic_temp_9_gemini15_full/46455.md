daguerreotypists:/ˌdæɡəˈraɪtəpɪsts/| n. |达盖尔摄影师|A daguerreotypist is a person who practices or is skilled in the art of daguerreotypy, an early photographic process.  They create images using a daguerreotype camera and the chemical processes involved in developing the resulting plates.|The renowned daguerreotypist captured stunning portraits of the era's leading figures.  著名的达盖尔摄影师拍摄了那个时代主要人物的令人惊叹的肖像。| photographer, artist |  (no direct antonym, as it refers to a specific photographic technique)
