nonpurposively:/ˌnɒn pɜːrpəsɪvli/| adj.|非故意地，无意地| done without intention or purpose; accidentally.  The word is formed from "non-" (not) + "purposive" (having a purpose).|例句：He nonpurposively stumbled upon the solution. 他无意中发现了这个解决方法。

The data was collected nonpurposively, meaning it wasn't gathered with a specific research question in mind. 数据收集是非目的性的，这意味着收集数据时没有特定的研究问题。|近义词：accidentally, unintentionally, inadvertently, casually|反义词：intentionally, deliberately, purposefully, designedly
