dialers:/ˈdaɪlərz/ | n. |拨号器；呼叫器 | Devices used to make phone calls, often referring to older technologies or specialized equipment for making large numbers of calls.  This can include modem dialers,  pulse dialers, or automated dialing systems. | Example:  Many telemarketers use automated dialers to reach potential customers.  许多电话推销员使用自动拨号器来联系潜在客户。 |近义词：auto-dialers, calling systems |反义词：manual callers (less common and less direct antonym)
