continued:/kənˈtɪnjuːd/| adj. |继续的，延续的 |Continuing without interruption; prolonged.|The continued rain caused flooding. 持续的降雨导致了洪水。| ongoing, persistent, uninterrupted | interrupted, ceased, stopped |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project continues to develop. = 项目持续发展。
He continues to work hard. = 他继续努力工作。


一般过去时:
The meeting continued for two hours. = 会议持续了两个小时。
She continued to read after dinner. = 她晚饭后继续看书。


现在完成时:
The rain has continued all day. = 雨下了一整天。
He has continued his studies despite the difficulties. = 尽管困难重重，他仍然继续学习。


过去完成时:
The game had continued even after it got dark. = 即使天黑了，比赛也还在继续。
They had continued their journey despite the storm. = 尽管暴风雨来临，他们还是继续他们的旅程。


将来时:
The concert will continue until midnight. = 音乐会将持续到午夜。
We will continue our discussion tomorrow. = 我们明天将继续讨论。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to work hard, he would succeed. = 如果他继续努力工作，他就会成功。
I wish he continued his studies. = 我希望他继续他的学业。


被动语态:
The work was continued the next day. = 工作在第二天继续进行。
The meeting was continued after a short break. = 短暂休息后，会议继续进行。


疑问句:
Does the meeting continue tomorrow? = 会议明天继续吗？
Will the work continue after the holiday? = 假期后工作还会继续吗？


否定句:
The rain did not continue long. = 雨没有持续很久。
He did not continue his education. = 他没有继续他的教育。


PHRASE->
continued success = 持续的成功

continued growth = 持续的增长
continued support = 持续的支持
