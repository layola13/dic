preactivities: /ˌpriːækˈtɪvɪtiz/ | n. |预备活动，准备活动 | Activities performed in preparation for a main event or task.  These activities are done before the main activity begins and often serve to prepare participants or set the stage. | Example: The preactivities for the conference included setting up the registration desk and preparing the presentation materials.  预备会议的活动包括设置登记处和准备演示材料。|近义词：preparations, preliminary activities, groundwork |反义词：postactivities, follow-up activities
