monitoring: /ˈmɒnɪtərɪŋ/ /ˈmɑːnɪtərɪŋ/ | n. | 监控，监测，监督 | The act of regularly checking something or someone to see how they are doing or performing.  It involves observing, measuring, and evaluating a process, system, or individual over time. | 例句：The company implemented a new system for monitoring employee performance. 公司实施了一个新的员工绩效监控系统。

例句：Regular monitoring of blood pressure is crucial for heart health. 定期监测血压对心脏健康至关重要。


近义词：supervision, surveillance, observation, tracking, overseeing


反义词：neglect, disregard, ignoring


词性: 名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
We monitor the server's performance daily. = 我们每天监控服务器的性能。
They monitor the progress of the project closely. = 他们密切监控项目的进展。

一般过去时:
The engineers monitored the equipment throughout the night. = 工程师整夜监控设备。
The doctor monitored the patient's vital signs. = 医生监测病人的生命体征。

现在完成时:
We have monitored the situation for several weeks. = 我们已经监控了这个情况好几个星期了。
They have monitored the water quality for years. = 他们多年来一直监测水质。

过去完成时:
The team had monitored the data before presenting their findings. = 团队在提交调查结果之前已经监控了数据。
She had monitored the patient's condition for hours before calling the doctor. = 在打电话给医生之前，她已经监控了病人的病情数小时。

将来时:
We will monitor the weather conditions carefully. = 我们将仔细监测天气状况。
They will monitor the experiment for any unexpected results. = 他们将监测实验是否有任何意外结果。

虚拟语气:
If we monitored the system more closely, we might have prevented the error. = 如果我们更密切地监控系统，我们也许可以防止错误。
It is essential that we monitor the patient's progress regularly. = 我们必须定期监测病人的进展。

被动语态:
The system is monitored 24/7. = 系统全天候监控。

疑问句:

否定句:
