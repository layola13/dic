reductiveness:/rɪˈdʌktɪvnəs/| n. |简化论；还原论|The tendency to reduce complex phenomena to simpler explanations, often overlooking crucial details or nuances.  It often implies an oversimplification that ignores complexity and context.  |The reductiveness of his analysis failed to account for the social and economic factors influencing the situation.  他的分析过于简化，未能考虑到影响局势的社会和经济因素。|Oversimplification, simplification|Holism, complexity, nuance|名词
