impendent:/ɪmˈpɛndɪnt/| adj.|迫在眉睫的，即将发生的| imminent, impending, threatening, about to happen;  close at hand.  Describes something that is likely to occur very soon, often with a sense of urgency or threat. |例句：The storm is impendent; we need to take shelter immediately.  暴风雨迫在眉睫；我们需要立即躲避。

The impendent deadline caused him to work late into the night.  即将到来的截止日期导致他工作到深夜。


近义词：imminent, forthcoming, looming, threatening


反义词：distant, remote, far-off


词性：形容词
