curled:/kɜːld/ | /ˈkɜːrld/ | adj., v. | 卷曲的；卷起 |  To form or become curled; having a curved or spiral shape. |


例句：

Her hair was beautifully curled.  = 她的头发卷得很漂亮。

The leaves curled up in the heat. = 叶子在高温下卷曲了。


近义词：coiled, twisted, crimped


反义词：straight, uncurled


词性：形容词，动词

词源:

The word "curled" originates from the Old English word *curlian, meaning "to twist, curl."  This is related to the Proto-Germanic *kurzljan, and ultimately to a Proto-Indo-European root with similar meaning, suggesting a long history of the word's association with curved shapes.
