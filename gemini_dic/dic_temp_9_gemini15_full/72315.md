flourish: /ˈflʌrɪʃ/ | v. n. adj. |繁荣；兴旺；挥舞；装饰；茂盛；装饰性笔画 | to grow or develop successfully; to wave something around; a showy or exaggerated gesture; an ornamental flourish |近义词：thrive, prosper, flourish, wave, adorn |反义词：decline, wither, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The business is flourishing. = 这家公司生意兴隆。
The flowers flourish in the spring. = 花儿在春天盛开。

一般过去时:
The company flourished under his leadership. = 在他的领导下，公司蓬勃发展。
She flourished her sword.  = 她挥舞着她的剑。

现在完成时:
The city has flourished since the new infrastructure was built. = 自从新的基础设施建成以来，这座城市蓬勃发展。
He has flourished his pen in the air. = 他在空中挥舞着他的笔。

过去完成时:
The empire had flourished for centuries before its decline. = 这个帝国在衰落之前已经繁荣了几个世纪。
She had flourished the flag before the crowd. = 她在人群面前挥舞着旗帜。

将来时:
The economy will flourish if the government implements the right policies. = 如果政府实施正确的政策，经济将会繁荣。
She will flourish the bouquet during the ceremony. = 她将在仪式上挥舞花束。

虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, our business would flourish. = 如果我们更努力工作，我们的生意就会兴隆。
If she had flourished her weapon earlier, the outcome might have been different. = 如果她早点挥舞武器，结果可能会有所不同。

被动语态:
The garden was flourished with colorful flowers. = 花园里盛开着五彩缤纷的花朵。(此处flourished作被动语态略显不自然，更常用的表达是"The garden was filled with colorful flowers" 或 "Colorful flowers flourished in the garden.")
The flag was flourished triumphantly. = 旗帜被胜利地挥舞着。

疑问句:
Does your business flourish? = 你的生意兴隆吗？
Did the plants flourish last year? = 这些植物去年长得茂盛吗？

否定句:
The business didn't flourish after the economic downturn. = 经济衰退后，生意没有兴隆。
He didn't flourish the document in the air. = 他没有在空中挥舞文件。


PHRASE->
flourish and thrive: 茁壮成长
flourish with:  以...而繁荣
flourish in: 在...中繁荣
a flourish of trumpets: 一阵嘹亮的号声
with a flourish:  带有夸张的动作


词源:
源自古法语 *florir,  源于拉丁语 *florēre,  意思是“开花”。  因此，其核心含义与植物的生长和繁荣相关，后来引申出其他含义。
