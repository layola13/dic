cattles:/ˈkætəl/| n.|牛群；牲口|Cattle are a collective noun referring to a group of cows, bulls, and oxen.  It is always used in the plural form and does not take a singular form. |例句：The farmer is taking his cattle to the pasture. 那个农民正把他的牛群赶到牧场。|例句：We saw a herd of cattle grazing in the field. 我们看见一群牛在田里吃草。|近义词：livestock, herd, oxen|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cattle graze in the field every day. = 牛群每天都在田里吃草。
The cattle are being herded into the barn. = 牛群正被赶进谷仓。


一般过去时:
The cattle grazed peacefully in the meadow. = 牛群平静地吃着草。
The cattle were driven to a new pasture last week. = 上周牛群被赶到了一个新的牧场。


现在完成时:
The farmer has moved the cattle to higher ground. = 农民已经把牛群迁到了高处。
The cattle have been vaccinated against disease. = 牛群已经接种了疫苗来预防疾病。


过去完成时:
The cattle had already grazed before the storm broke. = 暴风雨来临之前，牛群已经吃过草了。
The cattle had been sold before the drought hit. = 干旱来临之前，牛群已经被卖掉了。


将来时:
The cattle will be moved to a new pasture next month. = 牛群下个月将被转移到新的牧场。
The farmer will sell the cattle in the autumn. = 农民将在秋季出售牛群。


虚拟语气:
If the cattle were healthier, they would produce more milk. = 如果牛群更健康，它们就会产更多的奶。
I wish the cattle weren't so susceptible to disease. = 我希望牛群不会那么容易生病。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
