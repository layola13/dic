cheer:/tʃɪr/ | n., v. |欢呼；鼓励；高兴；振奋 |to shout loudly in order to express happiness or encouragement; to become more cheerful or optimistic;  a shout of joy or encouragement |例句：The crowd cheered wildly when the home team scored.  观众在主队得分时疯狂欢呼。

The news cheered her up considerably.  这个消息让她高兴了不少。


近义词：applaud, acclaim,  exult,  encourage,  raise,  hearten |反义词：depress, discourage, deject, dampen
