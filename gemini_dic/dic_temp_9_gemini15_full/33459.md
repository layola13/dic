chutes:/tʃuːts/| n.|滑槽；滑道；降落伞；(用于运输货物的)倾斜槽道|A chute is a sloping channel or trough, typically made of wood or metal, down which things can slide.  It can refer to a water slide, a delivery chute for packages, or the part of a parachute that allows a person or object to descend safely.|例句：The children loved the water chutes at the amusement park. 孩子们喜欢游乐园里的滑水道。

The workers used a chute to move the grain into the silo. 工人们用滑槽把谷物运进筒仓。|近义词：slide, trough, ramp, conveyor|反义词：lift, elevator
