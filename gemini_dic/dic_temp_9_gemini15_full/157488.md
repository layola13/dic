piss-away: /pɪs əˈweɪ/ | v. | 浪费；挥霍；糟蹋 | To waste or squander something, typically time or money.  It's often used informally and can be considered vulgar. | 例句：He pissed away his inheritance on gambling. 他把继承的财产都挥霍在赌博上了。  They pissed away a whole afternoon arguing. 他们浪费了一个下午的时间争吵。 | 近义词：squander, waste, fritter away, throw away | 反义词：save, conserve, hoard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pisses away his money on useless things. = 他把钱浪费在无用的东西上。
They piss away their time watching TV all day. = 他们整天看电视浪费时间。

一般过去时:
She pissed away her chance to win the competition. = 她浪费了赢得比赛的机会。
He pissed away a fortune on a failed business venture. = 他在一个失败的商业冒险中挥霍了一大笔钱。

现在完成时:
I have pissed away too much time on social media. = 我在社交媒体上浪费了太多时间。
They have pissed away their savings on frivolous purchases. = 他们把积蓄都花在了毫无意义的购买上。

过去完成时:
He realised he had pissed away years of his life on unproductive pursuits. = 他意识到他已经把几年的时间浪费在了无益的追求上。
She regretted that she had pissed away the opportunity to study abroad. = 她后悔错过了出国留学的机会。

将来时:
If he continues like this, he will piss away all his money. = 如果他继续这样下去，他会把所有的钱都挥霍掉。
They will piss away their summer holiday if they don't plan properly. = 如果他们不做好计划，他们会浪费掉他们的暑假。

虚拟语气:
If only he hadn't pissed away his savings! = 要是他没有挥霍掉积蓄就好了！
I wish I hadn't pissed away so much time on that project. = 我真希望我没有在这个项目上浪费那么多时间。

被动语态:  (The passive voice is less common with this verb due to its informal nature.)
His inheritance was pissed away on foolish investments. = 他的遗产被挥霍在了愚蠢的投资上。
(Note: This sounds unnatural; active voice is preferred.)

疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't piss away his time; he used it wisely. = 他没有浪费时间；他明智地利用了时间。
They vowed not to piss away another opportunity. = 他们发誓不再浪费机会。
