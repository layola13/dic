moose:/muːs/| n. |驼鹿|A large North American deer with large antlers.  It is the largest member of the deer family.|例句：We saw a moose while hiking in the woods.我们在森林徒步旅行时看到了一只驼鹿。|例句：The moose stood silently by the lake. 这只驼鹿静静地站在湖边。|近义词：elk (though technically different)|反义词：None directly applicable.
