numbingly:/ˈnʌmbɪŋli/| adv. |令人麻木地，令人感觉迟钝地 |In a way that causes numbness or a loss of feeling;  to a degree that causes numbness.  It describes something that causes a dulling or numbing effect, often in a negative or overwhelming way.|例句：The pain was numbingly intense.  疼痛剧烈得令人麻木。

The lecture was numbingly boring.  讲座枯燥乏味得令人昏昏欲睡。|近义词：dullingly, deadeningly, benumbingly |反义词：stimulatingly, excitingly, thrillingly
