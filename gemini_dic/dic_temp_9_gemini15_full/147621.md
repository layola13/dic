词源:

The word "overplentifulness" is formed by combining:

* **over-**: a prefix indicating excess or too much.
* **plentiful**:  meaning abundant or more than enough.
* **-ness**: a suffix forming nouns of state or quality.

Therefore, "overplentifulness" literally means "the state of being excessively plentiful."  The root of "plentiful" goes back to Old French *plein* (full) and ultimately to Latin *plenus* (full).
