plow-under: /ˈplaʊ ˈʌndər/ | vt. | 犁地掩埋；掩盖；压制；使…失败 | To bury (something, such as a crop) by plowing it under the ground; to suppress or disregard something.  |  例句：The farmer plowed under the failed crop.  农民把歉收的庄稼犁埋了。

The government plowed under the dissenting voices. 政府压制了异议的声音。| 近义词：bury, suppress, cover up, obliterate | 反义词：reveal, expose, cultivate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They plow under the weeds every spring. = 他们每年春天都把杂草犁埋掉。
The company plows under any negative publicity. = 这家公司掩盖任何负面宣传。


一般过去时:
The farmer plowed under the corn after the frost. = 霜冻过后，农民把玉米犁埋了。
They plowed under the evidence to protect themselves. = 他们掩盖证据以保护自己。


现在完成时:
They have plowed under several acres of land this year. = 今年他们已经犁埋了几英亩的土地。
The administration has plowed under many important reforms. = 政府已经压制了许多重要的改革。


过去完成时:
The evidence had been plowed under before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，证据已经被掩盖了。
They had plowed under the truth for years. = 他们多年来一直掩盖真相。


将来时:
They will plow under the remaining crops next week. = 他们下周将把剩余的庄稼犁埋掉。
The government will plow under any opposition to the new law. = 政府将压制任何反对新法的势力。


虚拟语气:
If they plowed under the evidence, they would be in trouble. = 如果他们掩盖证据，他们将会遇到麻烦。
I wish they hadn't plowed under those important documents. = 我真希望他们没有掩盖那些重要的文件。


被动语态:
The old building was plowed under to make way for a new highway. = 旧建筑被拆除以修建新的高速公路。(Note:  While "plow under" is usually active,  passive constructions are possible in certain contexts where the focus is on what happened to the object)
The dissenting opinions were plowed under by the majority vote. = 少数意见被多数票压制了。


疑问句:
Will they plow under the entire field? = 他们会把整个田地都犁埋掉吗？
Did they plow under the truth? = 他们掩盖真相了吗？


否定句:
They did not plow under the evidence. = 他们没有掩盖证据。
The farmer did not plow under the healthy crops. = 农民没有犁埋健康的庄稼。
