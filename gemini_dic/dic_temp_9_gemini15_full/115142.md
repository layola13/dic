mangrove:/ˈmæŋɡroʊv/| n. |红树林；红树植物|Mangroves are salt-tolerant trees and shrubs that grow in tropical and subtropical coastal areas.|例句：The mangrove forest provides a vital habitat for many species. 红树林为许多物种提供了重要的栖息地。|近义词：coastal forest, salt marsh|反义词:无
