giantess:/ˈdʒaɪəntɛs/| n.|女巨人，巨大的女人|A female giant; a woman of exceptional size or strength.|例句：In the fairy tale, the princess battled a fearsome giantess.  在童话故事中，公主与一个可怕的女巨人搏斗。|例句：She was a giantess in the world of finance, commanding respect and success. 她在金融界是一位女强人，备受尊重并取得了成功。|近义词：giant woman, colossal woman|反义词：dwarf woman, petite woman
