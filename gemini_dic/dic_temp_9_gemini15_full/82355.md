growl:/ɡraʊl/| v. n.|咆哮，低吼|To make a low, guttural sound, typically in anger or aggression.  A low, guttural sound, typically expressing anger or aggression.|例句：The dog growled at the stranger.  那条狗对着陌生人咆哮。
The lion let out a fearsome growl.  狮子发出可怕的咆哮声。|近义词：snarl, rumble, grumble |反义词：purr, meow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog growls at the mailman every day. = 这条狗每天都对邮递员咆哮。
He growls when he's angry. = 他生气时会咆哮。


一般过去时:
The bear growled fiercely before attacking. = 熊在攻击前凶猛地咆哮。
She growled a warning at the intruder. = 她对着闯入者低吼警告。


现在完成时:
The dog has growled at me several times today. = 这条狗今天已经对我咆哮了好几次。
He has never growled at anyone before. = 他以前从未对任何人咆哮过。


过去完成时:
The tiger had growled before it pounced. = 老虎在猛扑之前已经咆哮了。
She had growled at him earlier that day. = 她那天早些时候已经对他咆哮了。


将来时:
The dog will growl if you approach it. = 如果你靠近它，那条狗会咆哮。
I think he will growl at me again. = 我想他会再次对我咆哮。


虚拟语气:
If the dog growled, I would run away. = 如果狗咆哮，我会跑开。
I wish the dog wouldn't growl so much. = 我希望狗不要咆哮那么多。


被动语态:
A growl was heard from the bushes. = 从灌木丛中传来一声咆哮。
His anger was expressed in a low growl. = 他的愤怒以低沉的咆哮表达出来。


疑问句:
Did the dog growl at you? = 那条狗对你咆哮了吗？
Why did the lion growl? = 为什么狮子咆哮？


否定句:
The dog didn't growl. = 狗没有咆哮。
The cat didn't growl; it just hissed. = 猫没有咆哮；它只是嘶嘶作响。

a deep growl = 深沉的咆哮
