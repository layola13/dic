cassock:/ˈkæsək/| n. |长袍；法衣|A long, usually black, garment worn by clergy.  It is typically ankle-length and has long sleeves.|例句：The priest wore a black cassock during the service.  牧师在仪式上穿着一件黑色法衣。

例句：The choirboys wore short cassocks for the performance. 合唱团的男孩们在演出时穿着短法衣。


近义词：robe, vestment|反义词：None specifically.|词性：Noun
