self-understood:/ˌsɛlf ˌʌndərˈstʊd/ | adj. | 不言而喻的；不需解释的 | Implicitly understood; requiring no explanation.  |The rules were self-understood by everyone.  规则对每个人来说都是不言而喻的。| obvious, clear, evident | unclear, obscure, ambiguous | 形容词


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  The usage is described below.

Example 1: The meeting's agenda was self-understood. 会议的议程是不言而喻的。
Example 2:  It was a self-understood agreement between them. 这对他们来说是一个不言而喻的协议。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "self-understood".  However, phrases conveying similar meaning include:


goes without saying = 不言而喻
it's understood = 彼此心照不宣
taken for granted = 想当然
a matter of course = 理所当然
implicit agreement = 默契


词源:
The word is a compound of "self-" (meaning "of or by oneself") and "understood" (the past participle of the verb "understand").  The meaning arises from the idea that the understanding is inherent or implied, requiring no external explanation.
