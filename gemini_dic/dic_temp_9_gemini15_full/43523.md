craw:/krɔː/| v.|爬行，缓慢移动|To move slowly and clumsily, often on the hands and knees or with the body close to the ground.|例句：The baby crawled across the floor.  婴儿在地上爬行。|例句：The traffic crawled along at a snail's pace. 车辆缓慢地蠕动前进。|近义词：creep, inch, slither|反义词：run, sprint


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The snail crawls slowly. = 蜗牛缓慢地爬行。
The injured man crawls towards safety. = 受伤的人向安全的地方爬去。

一般过去时:
The child crawled to his mother. = 孩子爬向他的母亲。
The snake crawled into the hole. = 蛇爬进了洞里。

现在完成时:
The baby has crawled across the room. = 婴儿已经爬过了房间。
The plant has crawled up the wall. = 植物已经爬上了墙。

过去完成时:
He had crawled under the bed before I entered the room. = 我进房间之前，他已经爬到床底下去了。
The thief had crawled away before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经爬走了。

将来时:
The insect will crawl into the crevice. = 昆虫将会爬进裂缝。
She will crawl across the finish line, exhausted but victorious. = 她将筋疲力尽却胜利地爬过终点线。


虚拟语气:
If I were a snake, I would crawl silently. = 如果我是一条蛇，我会悄悄地爬行。
I wish I could crawl through that small hole. = 我真希望我能穿过那个小洞。

被动语态:
The floor was crawled over by the child. = 地板被孩子爬过了。(比较少用)
The wall was slowly crawled upon by the ivy. = 常春藤慢慢地爬上了墙。(比较少用)


疑问句:
Does the caterpillar crawl? = 毛毛虫会爬吗？
Will the lizard crawl away? = 蜥蜴会爬走吗？

否定句:
The injured man couldn't crawl. = 受伤的人无法爬行。
The robot doesn't crawl; it walks. = 机器人不会爬行；它走路。
