demoniac: /dɪˈmoʊniæk/ | adj. |魔鬼般的；狂热的；癫狂的 |  relating to or characteristic of a demon or demons; fiendish; wildly enthusiastic or energetic.  |例句：His eyes held a demonic gleam.  他的眼睛闪着魔鬼般的光芒。

The demonic laughter echoed through the hall.  恶魔般的笑声回荡在大厅里。


近义词：devilish, fiendish, diabolic, infernal, satanic | 反义词：angelic, heavenly, saintly, benign


词源:  From Middle English *demoniak, from Old French demoniaque, from Late Latin daemonicus, from Greek daimōnikos, from daimon "spirit, demon."
