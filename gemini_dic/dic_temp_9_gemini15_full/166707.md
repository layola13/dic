preshipped:/ˌpriːˈʃɪpt/| adj.|预先发货的，预先运送的|Already shipped before a certain time or event.  This term is typically used in logistics and e-commerce to indicate that an item has already been sent out before a specific date or time.|例句：The preshipped items arrived a week before the official launch date. 预先发货的商品在正式发布日期前一周到达了。|  The customer service representative confirmed that the order was preshipped. 客服代表确认订单已预先发货。|近义词：pre-sent, advanced, beforehand|反义词：late, delayed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The product is preshipped to the warehouse. = 产品预先运送到仓库。
These items are preshipped to ensure timely delivery. = 这些商品预先发货以确保及时送达。


一般过去时:
The goods were preshipped last week. = 货物上周已预先发货。
The order was preshipped to meet the deadline. = 订单已预先发货以满足截止日期。


现在完成时:
The company has preshipped all the orders. = 公司已预先发货所有订单。
They have preshipped the components for the assembly line. = 他们已预先运送组装线的组件。


过去完成时:
The supplier had preshipped the materials before the project started. = 项目开始前，供应商已预先运送了材料。
We had preshipped the samples to the client before the meeting. = 会议前，我们已将样品预先发送给客户。


将来时:
The vendor will preship the goods next Monday. = 供应商将于下周一预先发货。
We will preship the parts to the factory next week. = 我们下周将把零件预先运送到工厂。


虚拟语气:
If they had preshipped the equipment, the project would have been completed on time. = 如果他们预先运送了设备，项目就能按时完成。
I wish we had preshipped those items; now we're behind schedule. = 我真希望我们当时预先发货了那些商品；现在我们进度落后了。


被动语态:
The packages were preshipped by air freight. = 包裹是通过空运预先发货的。
All the necessary parts are being preshipped to the destination. = 所有必要的零件正在被预先运送到目的地。


疑问句:
Were the components preshipped? = 零件预先发货了吗？
Have the products been preshipped yet? = 产品已经预先发货了吗？


否定句:
The supplier did not preship the parts. = 供应商没有预先发货零件。
The items were not preshipped; they arrived late. = 商品没有预先发货；它们到达晚了。
