playmates:/ˈpleɪmeɪts/| n.|玩伴，伙伴|A playmate is a child who plays with another child.|例句：The children were playing happily with their playmates in the park.孩子们在公园里和他们的玩伴快乐地玩耍。|近义词：companions, friends, buddies|反义词:enemies, rivals|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My daughter has many playmates at school. = 我女儿在学校有很多玩伴。
The children are playing with their playmates in the garden. = 孩子们在花园里和他们的玩伴玩耍。


一般过去时:
She had several playmates when she was young. = 她小时候有几个玩伴。
They were good playmates throughout their childhood. = 他们童年时期是很好的玩伴。


现在完成时:
He has made many playmates since he moved to the neighborhood. = 自从他搬到这个社区以来，他已经交了很多玩伴。
The twins have had the same playmates for years. = 这对双胞胎多年来一直有相同的玩伴。


过去完成时:
She had already made several playmates before the school year began. = 在学年开始之前，她已经交了几个玩伴。
They had been best playmates since kindergarten. = 从幼儿园开始，他们就是最好的玩伴。


将来时:
I hope my son will have many playmates at his new school. = 我希望我的儿子在新学校里会有很多玩伴。
She will likely make new playmates at summer camp. = 她很可能会在夏令营结识新的玩伴。


虚拟语气:
If only I had more playmates when I was a child! = 要是我小时候有更多玩伴就好了！
I wish I had known those children as playmates when I was younger. = 我真希望我年轻时能认识那些孩子作为玩伴。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The children were often chosen as playmates by their parents.  = 孩子们经常被他们的父母选作玩伴。(This sounds unnatural)
Playmates were not easy to find in the remote village. = 在偏远的村庄里，很难找到玩伴。(This is more natural)


疑问句:
Do you have many playmates? = 你有很多玩伴吗？
Does he have any playmates at his new school? = 他在新学校有玩伴吗？


否定句:
He didn't have many playmates in his previous school. = 他以前学校里没有很多玩伴。
The shy child had no playmates for a long time. = 这个害羞的孩子很长时间都没有玩伴。


PHRASE->
