lows:/loʊz/| n.|低点，低谷，低音，低价|The lows refer to the lowest points or values of something, such as the lowest temperature, the lowest price, or the lowest point in a graph.  It can also refer to the low notes in music.|例句：The lows of winter are finally behind us. 冬天的低温终于过去了。|例句：Stock prices hit new lows this week. 股价本周跌至新低。|近义词：depths, bottoms, troughs|反义词：highs, peaks, summits
