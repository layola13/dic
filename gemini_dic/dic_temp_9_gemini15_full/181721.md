reliably:/rɪˈlaɪəbli/| adv. |可靠地，可信赖地 |Dependably; in a way that can be relied upon.  |例句：The car runs reliably. 这辆车运行可靠。|例句：He reliably delivers on his promises. 他总是信守承诺。|近义词：dependably, consistently, surely, steadfastly |反义词：unreliably, inconsistently, erratically


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reliably completes his tasks. = 他可靠地完成他的任务。
The system reliably processes data. = 系统可靠地处理数据。


一般过去时:
The engine reliably performed throughout the journey. = 引擎在整个旅程中运行可靠。
She reliably followed the instructions. = 她可靠地遵循了指示。


现在完成时:
The software has reliably functioned for years. = 该软件多年来一直运行可靠。
He has reliably supported his family. = 他一直可靠地支持他的家人。


过去完成时:
The machine had reliably operated for decades before failing. = 这台机器在故障前可靠地运行了几十年。
She had reliably predicted the outcome. = 她先前可靠地预测了结果。


将来时:
The new system will reliably handle increased traffic. = 新系统将可靠地处理增加的流量。
He will reliably meet the deadline. = 他将可靠地按时完成。


虚拟语气:
If the system were more reliable, we would use it more often. = 如果系统更可靠，我们会更频繁地使用它。
I wish the results were more reliably obtained. = 我希望结果能更可靠地获得。


被动语态:
The information is reliably sourced. = 信息来源可靠。
The data was reliably collected and analyzed. = 数据经过可靠地收集和分析。


疑问句:
Does the device reliably perform its function? = 该设备是否可靠地执行其功能？
Can we reliably predict the weather? = 我们能可靠地预测天气吗？


否定句:
The machine did not reliably function. = 机器运行不可靠。
The witness did not reliably recount the events. = 目击者没有可靠地叙述事件。
