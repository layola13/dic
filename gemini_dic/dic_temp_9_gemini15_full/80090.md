goatskins:/ˈɡoʊtskins/| n. |山羊皮；山羊皮制品|The plural of "goatskin," referring to the skins of goats, often used to make leather goods.  Goatskins are durable and have been used for centuries for various purposes. |例句：The nomadic tribe used goatskins to make tents and clothing.  游牧部落用山羊皮制作帐篷和衣服。

例句：Many traditional drums are made from stretched goatskins. 许多传统的鼓是用拉紧的山羊皮制成的。|近义词：leather, hides, skins|反义词：无|词性:名词
