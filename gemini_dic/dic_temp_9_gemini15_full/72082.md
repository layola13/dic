floatative:/ˈfloʊteɪtɪv/| adj.|能漂浮的；漂浮的；轻浮的|Able to float; characterized by or tending to float.  The word suggests a lightness and possibly a lack of substance or seriousness.|The cork is floatative.  软木塞能漂浮。|The floatative properties of the new material are being tested. 正在测试这种新型材料的漂浮特性。|buoyant, buoyant, floating|grounded, submerged, weighty
