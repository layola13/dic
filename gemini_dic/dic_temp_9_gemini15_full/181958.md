remarrying:/ˌriːˈmæriɪŋ/| v.|再婚|The act of marrying again after a previous marriage has ended.  It implies a previous marriage that ended through divorce, annulment, or death. |例句：She's remarrying next spring. 她明年春天要再婚。

例句：He remarried just six months after his first wife passed away. 他第一任妻子去世后仅仅六个月就再婚了。


近义词： marrying again, getting remarried

反义词：remaining single, staying single


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are remarrying soon. = 他们很快就要再婚了。
He is remarrying his college sweetheart. = 他要和他的大学恋人再婚了。


一般过去时:
They remarried last year. = 他们去年再婚了。
She remarried after a long period of singlehood. = 她单身很久之后再婚了。


现在完成时:
They have remarried and are very happy. = 他们已经再婚了，并且非常幸福。
He has remarried twice since his first divorce. = 自从第一次离婚后，他已经再婚两次了。


过去完成时:
They had remarried before I even knew about their divorce. = 在我甚至知道他们离婚之前，他们就已经再婚了。
She had remarried quietly, without telling her children. = 她悄悄地再婚了，没有告诉她的孩子们。


将来时:
They will remarry in the spring. = 他们将在春天再婚。
He will remarry once he feels emotionally ready. = 他会在感觉情感上准备好后才会再婚。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't met her, he wouldn't be remarrying now. = 如果他没有遇到她，他现在就不会再婚了。
I wish I hadn't remarried so quickly. = 我希望我没有那么快就再婚。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be used.)


疑问句:
Are they remarrying? = 他们要再婚了吗？
Is he seriously considering remarrying? = 他在认真考虑再婚吗？


否定句:
They are not remarrying. = 他们不会再婚。
He decided against remarrying. = 他决定不再婚了。
