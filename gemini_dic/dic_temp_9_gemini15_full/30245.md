cellulitis:/ˌsɛljuˈlaɪtɪs/| n. |蜂窝织炎|A common and potentially serious bacterial skin infection. It occurs in the deeper layers of the skin and causes redness, swelling, and pain.  It often occurs on the legs but can appear anywhere on the body.  Prompt treatment with antibiotics is crucial to prevent serious complications.|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with cellulitis after examining the swollen and reddened area on her leg.医生检查了她腿上肿胀发红的区域后，诊断她患有蜂窝织炎。

例句：He developed cellulitis in his arm after a minor cut became infected.他手臂上一个小伤口感染后，患上了蜂窝织炎。


近义词：erysipelas (but erysipelas is a superficial infection, while cellulitis is deeper)|反义词：None|词性:名词
