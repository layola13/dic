nonmaternal: /ˌnɒn məˈtɜːnəl/ | adj. | 非母系的；非母性的 | Not relating to or derived from a mother.  This term is often used in biological or social contexts to describe things that are not related to or associated with a mother or motherhood. For example, nonmaternal care refers to childcare provided by someone other than the biological mother. |

例句：

The study examined the effects of nonmaternal care on infant development. 这项研究考察了非母系照料对婴儿发育的影响。

The child received nonmaternal affection from her aunt.  这孩子从她阿姨那里获得了非母性的关爱。


近义词：non-parental,  unrelated (in some contexts)

反义词：maternal

词源:

The word "nonmaternal" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "maternal" (relating to a mother).  "Maternal" itself comes from the Latin word "mater," meaning "mother."
