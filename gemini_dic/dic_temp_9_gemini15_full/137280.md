nonsynchronical:/ˌnɒnˈsɪŋkrənɪkəl/ | adj. |不同步的；不同期的；不同时的| Not occurring or existing at the same time; out of synchronization.  This adjective describes events, processes, or systems that lack simultaneous timing or coordination. |Example: The two computers operated nonsynchronously, causing data loss. 这两台电脑不同步运行，导致数据丢失。The nonsynchronical nature of the two events made it difficult to establish a causal relationship. 两个事件不同步的性质使得难以建立因果关系。|近义词：asynchronous, uncoordinated, discordant |反义词：synchronous, simultaneous, coordinated
