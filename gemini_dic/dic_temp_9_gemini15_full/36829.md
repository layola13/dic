colonise:/ˈkɒlənaɪz/ /ˈkɑːlənaɪz/ | vt. |殖民；开拓；移居；（动植物）大量繁殖 | To establish control over (a place or people) and settle there, typically by establishing a colony.  It can also refer to the spreading of a plant or animal species into a new area. |例句：The Romans colonised much of Europe. 罗马人殖民了欧洲大部分地区。

The explorers colonised the new land, establishing settlements and claiming it for their country. 探险家们殖民了这片新土地，建立了定居点并将其据为己有。


近义词：settle, occupy, inhabit, populate | 反义词：decolonize, abandon
