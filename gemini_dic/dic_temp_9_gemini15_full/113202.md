lurking:/ˈlɜːrkɪŋ/|/ˈlɝːkɪŋ/ | v. |潜伏；潜藏；暗中行动 | to be present or exist in a hidden or concealed way |例句：A danger was lurking just around the corner.  危险潜伏在附近。

There's a lurking feeling of unease in the town. 镇上有一种潜藏的不安感。|  近义词：hiding, concealed,  creeping,  | 反义词：obvious, open, visible | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is lurking in the shadows. = 他潜伏在阴影中。
Something is lurking beneath the surface. = 表面下潜藏着一些东西。

一般过去时:
The danger lurked in the darkness. = 危险潜伏在黑暗中。
A killer lurked in the woods. = 一个杀手潜伏在树林里。

现在完成时:
The threat has been lurking for months. = 威胁已经潜伏了几个月。
Danger has been lurking just around the corner. = 危险一直在潜伏着。

过去完成时:
The danger had lurked unnoticed for years. = 危险多年来一直潜伏未被察觉。
He had been lurking in the shadows for hours. = 他在阴影中潜伏了几个小时。

将来时:
I fear what may be lurking around the bend. = 我担心前方可能会潜伏着什么。
Trouble will be lurking if we don't act carefully. = 如果我们不小心，麻烦就会潜伏着。


虚拟语气:
If a predator were lurking nearby, we would be in danger. = 如果附近潜伏着捕食者，我们就危险了。
I wish I didn't feel that something sinister was lurking. = 我希望我不觉得有什么不祥的东西潜伏着。

被动语态:
The threat was being lurked by the enemy. = 威胁正被敌人潜伏着。(这个被动语态用法较少见)
Nothing was lurking in the closet, it turned out. = 事实证明，壁橱里什么也没有潜伏着。

疑问句:
Is there anything lurking in the dark? = 黑暗中有什么潜伏的东西吗？
What dangers are lurking in this area? = 这个地区潜伏着什么危险？

否定句:
Nothing was lurking in the shadows. = 阴影中什么也没有潜伏着。
There is nothing lurking beneath the surface. = 表面下没有任何东西潜伏着。


PHRASE->
lurking danger = 潜伏的危险
lurking fear = 潜伏的恐惧
lurking suspicion = 潜伏的怀疑
lurking threat = 潜伏的威胁
lurking in the shadows = 潜伏在阴影中
