reunite:/ˌriːjuˈnaɪt/| vt.|使重聚；使重合|To bring people or things together again after a period of separation.  |例句：The family were reunited after many years apart.  多年分离后，这家人终于团聚了。|例句：The separated parts of the machine were carefully reunited. 机器分离的部件被小心地重新组合在一起。|近义词：recombine, rejoin, reunite|反义词：separate, divide, part


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We reunite every year for a family gathering. = 我们每年都团聚一次。
The separated pieces reunite to form a whole. = 分离的碎片重新组合成一个整体。

一般过去时:
The lost dog was reunited with its owner. = 走失的狗与主人团聚了。
The two countries reunited after the war. = 战争结束后，这两个国家重新统一了。

现在完成时:
They have reunited after a decade of separation. = 分离十年后，他们重逢了。
The broken vase has been carefully reunited. = 破碎的花瓶已经被小心地粘合起来了。

过去完成时:
Before the reunion, they had not seen each other for years. = 在重聚之前，他们多年未见了。
The puzzle pieces had been reunited before she realized it. = 拼图碎片在她意识到之前就已经拼合在一起了。


将来时:
We will reunite next Christmas. = 我们将在下一个圣诞节团聚。
The broken parts will be reunited using a special glue. = 破损的部件将用特殊的胶水粘合在一起。

虚拟语气:
If only we could reunite our family. = 但愿我们能使家人团聚。
I wish I could reunite with my childhood friends. = 我希望我能与我的童年朋友重逢。

被动语态:
The family was reunited by the Red Cross. = 这个家庭由红十字会帮助团聚。
The separated fragments were carefully reunited by the scientist. = 分离的碎片被科学家小心地重新组合在一起。

疑问句:
Will they ever reunite? = 他们会重聚吗？
Can the broken pieces be reunited? = 破损的碎片可以重新组合吗？

否定句:
They did not reunite after the argument. = 争吵后他们没有重归于好。
The broken pottery could not be reunited. = 破碎的陶器无法修复。


PHRASE->
reunite with = 与…重聚
reunite after = 分离后重聚
reunite families = 使家庭团聚
reunite the country = 使国家统一
reunite the fragments = 使碎片重合


词源:
re- (prefix indicating again) + unite (from Latin *ūnīre "to make one, unite")
