curry:/ˈkʌri/| n. v.|咖喱；咖喱菜；用咖喱调味|A curry is a dish of meat, vegetables, or other ingredients cooked in a spicy sauce containing curry powder.  The word can also refer to the sauce itself or the act of cooking with this sauce. |例句：I made a delicious chicken curry last night.  昨晚我做了一道美味的鸡肉咖喱。

例句：She curried favor with her boss by working late. 她通过加班讨好老板。|近义词：stew,  sauce|反义词：N/A|名词，动词
