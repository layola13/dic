denned:/dɛnd/| adj.|  穴居的；隐蔽的；秘密的 |  Defined as living in a den or cave; secluded or hidden; secret or clandestine.  | 例句：The bears lived in a denned area of the forest.  = 熊住在森林里一个隐蔽的区域。  The denned operation was carefully planned. =  这个秘密行动是精心策划的。 | 近义词：hidden, secluded, secret, covert | 反义词：open, exposed, public
