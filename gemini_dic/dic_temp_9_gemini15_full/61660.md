emits:/ɪˈmɪts/| vt.|发射；散发；发出|To send out (something, especially gas or radiation)|The sun emits light and heat. 太阳发出光和热。 The factory emits harmful pollutants into the atmosphere.  工厂向大气中排放有害污染物。|近义词：radiates, releases, gives off|反义词：absorbs


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun emits light and heat. = 太阳发出光和热。
The factory emits pollutants into the air. = 工厂向空气中排放污染物。

一般过去时:
The volcano emitted a large cloud of ash. = 火山喷发出大量的火山灰。
The car emitted a strange noise. = 汽车发出奇怪的声音。

现在完成时:
The factory has emitted tons of pollutants this year. = 今年这家工厂已经排放了数吨污染物。
The device has emitted a warning signal. = 该设备已经发出警告信号。

过去完成时:
The reactor had emitted radiation before the accident was discovered. = 事故被发现之前，反应堆就已经释放出辐射。
The car had emitted smoke before it stopped working. = 汽车停止工作之前就已经冒烟了。

将来时:
The rocket will emit a bright flash of light. = 火箭将发出耀眼的光芒。
The machine will emit a high-pitched sound when it is malfunctioning. = 机器发生故障时会发出高亢的声音。

虚拟语气:
If the device emitted a higher frequency, we could detect it more easily. = 如果该设备发出更高的频率，我们可以更容易地检测到它。
I wish the factory didn't emit so much pollution. = 我希望工厂不要排放那么多污染物。

被动语态:
Harmful gases were emitted by the power plant. = 发电厂排放了有害气体。
A warning signal is being emitted by the system. = 系统正在发出警告信号。

疑问句:
What does this device emit? = 这个设备发射什么？
Does the plant emit any harmful substances? = 这个工厂排放任何有害物质吗？

否定句:
The device does not emit any radiation. = 该设备不发射任何辐射。
The star doesn't emit visible light. = 这颗恒星不发出可见光。
