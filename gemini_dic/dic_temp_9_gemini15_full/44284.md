cronyism:/ˈkroʊniˌɪzəm/| n. |任人唯亲；裙带关系 |The practice of favoring one's friends and associates, especially by giving them jobs or other advantages.  It often involves ignoring merit or qualifications in favor of personal connections.  This can lead to unfairness, inefficiency, and corruption. |Example: The company's promotion policy was criticized for its blatant cronyism, as many deserving employees were overlooked in favor of the CEO's golfing buddies. 公司的晋升政策因其公然的任人唯亲而受到批评，因为许多有能力的员工都被忽视了，而老板的高尔夫球友却得到了晋升。|近义词：favoritism, nepotism, patronage |反义词：meritocracy, impartiality
