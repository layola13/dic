gamble:/ˈɡæmbəl/| v. n.| 赌博；冒险；孤注一掷 | To bet on an uncertain outcome; to risk something valuable on a chance.  |例句：He gambled away all his savings. 他把所有的积蓄都赌掉了。|例句：She gambled on a new business venture. 她在一个新的商业冒险中孤注一掷。|近义词： wager, risk, speculate | 反义词：certain, sure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gambles every weekend. = 他每个周末都赌博。
They gamble on the stock market. = 他们在股市上投机。

一般过去时:
I gambled on the horse race yesterday. = 我昨天赌马了。
She gambled her inheritance away. = 她把她的遗产都赌掉了。

现在完成时:
He has gambled away his fortune. = 他已经把他的财产都赌掉了。
They have gambled their future on this project. = 他们把未来押在这个项目上了。

过去完成时:
I had gambled all my money before I realised my mistake. = 我在意识到我的错误之前已经把所有的钱都赌掉了。
She had gambled on the wrong horse and lost. = 她赌错了马，输了。

将来时:
I will gamble on the next race. = 我将在下一场比赛中赌博。
They will gamble their savings on this investment. = 他们将把积蓄押在这个投资上。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't gamble my money away. = 如果我是你，我不会把我的钱都赌掉。
I wish I hadn't gambled so much last night. = 我真希望昨晚不要赌那么多。

被动语态:
The money was gambled away quickly. = 钱很快就被赌掉了。
The entire business was gambled on one risky decision. = 整个生意都押在一个冒险的决定上。

疑问句:
Do you gamble often? = 你经常赌博吗？
Did he gamble his savings? = 他把积蓄赌了吗？

否定句:
I don't gamble. = 我不赌博。
He didn't gamble on the election. = 他没有在选举中赌博。


PHRASE->
gamble on:  押宝于…
gamble away: 赌掉
high-stakes gamble: 高风险赌博
a calculated gamble: 经过深思熟虑的冒险
take a gamble: 冒险一试
