dark:/dɑːrk/| adj. |黑暗的，深色的，阴暗的 |  Dark refers to the absence of light or to a deep, rich color.  It can also describe something gloomy or sinister. |例句：The night was dark and stormy.  夜晚又黑又暴风雨。|例句：She wore a dark blue dress. 她穿着一件深蓝色的裙子。|近义词：obscure, shadowy, gloomy, dim |反义词：light, bright, sunny, luminous

一般现在时:
The room is dark. = 房间很暗。
The sky is dark with clouds. = 天空乌云密布。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

I wish it weren't so dark. = 我希望不要这么黑。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
