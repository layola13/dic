resentment:/rɪˈzɛntmənt/| n. |怨恨；愤恨；不满 | A feeling of displeasure and ill will caused by a wrong or insult.  It often involves a sense of being treated unfairly. |例句：Her resentment towards her boss stemmed from years of unfair treatment.  她的怨恨源于多年来受到的不公平待遇。

例句：He felt a deep resentment at being overlooked for promotion. 他对被忽视晋升感到深深的怨恨。


近义词：anger, indignation, bitterness, animosity, displeasure | 反义词：acceptance, forgiveness, gratitude, appreciation | 词性:名词
