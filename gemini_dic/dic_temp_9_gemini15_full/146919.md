overintellectualize: /ˌoʊvərɪnˌtɛləkˈtʃuəlˌaɪz/ | vt. | 过度思考；过度解读；赋予过多的智力含义 | To analyze or interpret something in an excessively intellectual or complicated way, often to the point of obscuring its simpler meaning or significance.  | 例句：He overintellectualized the simple act of sharing a meal, analyzing its social and symbolic implications to an exhausting degree. 他过度解读了简单的共享膳食的行为，分析其社会和象征意义，令人筋疲力尽。


例句：She tends to overintellectualize everyday situations, making them seem far more complex than they actually are. 她倾向于过度思考日常情境，使它们看起来比实际情况复杂得多。


近义词：overanalyze, overthink, overcomplicate, intellectualize excessively


反义词：simplify,  underestimate,  take at face value


词性:动词
