justest:/ˈdʒʌstɪst/| adj.|最公正的，最公平的|Most righteous or fair; exhibiting the highest degree of justice.  The judge's decision was considered the justest possible outcome given the circumstances.|例句：His judgment, though controversial, was considered the justest solution to the complex dispute.  他的判决虽然有争议，但在这种复杂纠纷中被认为是最公正的解决方案。
