nonphotosynthetic: /ˌnɒnˌfoʊtoʊsɪnˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 不进行光合作用的 | Not capable of performing photosynthesis.  Organisms that are nonphotosynthetic cannot produce their own food using sunlight.  They rely on other sources of energy, such as consuming other organisms. |

例句：

Many fungi are nonphotosynthetic organisms. = 许多真菌是不进行光合作用的生物。

Deep-sea hydrothermal vent communities often contain nonphotosynthetic bacteria. = 深海热液喷口群落通常含有不进行光合作用的细菌。


近义词：heterotrophic

反义词：photosynthetic


词性:形容词
