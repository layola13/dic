rousing: /ˈraʊzɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人振奋的；热烈的；激动人心的 |  Arousing or exciting; stimulating or invigorating.  It suggests something that stirs strong positive feelings or emotions. | 例句：The speaker gave a rousing speech that inspired the audience.  演讲者发表了一篇激动人心的演讲，鼓舞了听众。

The band played a rousing rendition of the national anthem. 乐队演奏了令人振奋的国歌。


近义词：inspiring, exhilarating, stimulating, exciting, thrilling | 反义词：dull, boring, tedious, depressing, unexciting


词源:  Comes from the verb "rouse," meaning to wake someone up or to stir something up.  The suffix "-ing" transforms it into an adjective describing something that has the effect of rousing.  Ultimately derived from Old English *hrūsian, meaning to shake or stir.
