culminated:/ˈkʌlmɪneɪtɪd/ | v. |达到顶点；以…告终|To reach the highest point or degree; to end or finish in a particular way.|例句：Years of research culminated in a successful new treatment.  多年的研究最终导致了一种成功的治疗方法。 |例句：The events of the past few weeks culminated in the resignation of the CEO.  过去几周的事件最终导致了首席执行官的辞职。|近义词：peaked, climaxed, concluded|反义词：began, started, commenced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project culminates in a final presentation. = 项目最终以最终演示告终。
His efforts culminate in a successful business. = 他的努力最终成就了一家成功的企业。


一般过去时:
The festival culminated in a spectacular fireworks display. = 节日以壮观的烟火表演告终。
Their long journey culminated in reaching the summit. = 他们的漫长旅程最终到达了山顶。


现在完成时:
Years of hard work have culminated in this achievement. = 多年的辛勤工作最终成就了这一成就。
Our efforts have culminated in a successful product launch. = 我们的努力最终导致了产品的成功发布。


过去完成时:
By the time he retired, his career had culminated in a prestigious award. = 等他退休时，他的职业生涯最终获得了享有盛誉的奖项。
The investigation had culminated in the arrest of the suspect. = 调查最终导致了嫌疑人的逮捕。


将来时:
The conference will culminate in a panel discussion. = 会议将以小组讨论结束。
His training will culminate in a marathon run. = 他的训练将以马拉松比赛结束。


虚拟语气:
If the plan had worked, everything would have culminated in success. = 如果计划成功了，一切都会以成功告终。
I wish my efforts had culminated in a better result. = 我希望我的努力能有更好的结果。


被动语态:
The project was culminated in a grand opening ceremony. = 项目以盛大的开幕式结束。
The meeting was culminated by a vote on the proposal. = 会议以对提案的投票结束。（注意：被动语态用在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用）


疑问句:
How will the concert culminate? = 音乐会将如何结束？
Does the story culminate in a happy ending? = 故事是以快乐结局结束的吗？


否定句:
The negotiations did not culminate in an agreement. = 谈判没有达成协议。
His efforts did not culminate in the desired outcome. = 他的努力没有达到预期的结果。


PHRASE->
culminate in:  以…告终
culminate with:  以…结束
culminate at:  在…达到顶点
culminate in a:  以…告终
culminate the:  使…达到顶点
