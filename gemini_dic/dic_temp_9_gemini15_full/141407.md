ophiologist:/ˌoʊfiˈɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |蛇类学家|A person who studies snakes.  Ophiologists are experts in herpetology, specializing in the taxonomy, biology, behavior, and ecology of snakes.|例句：The ophiologist carefully examined the venomous snake, noting its unique markings.  这位蛇类学家仔细检查了这条毒蛇，注意到了它独特的 markings。|近义词：herpetologist (more general)|反义词：无
