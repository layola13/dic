practice:/ˈpræktɪs/| n./v.|练习，实践；实习；业务；惯例|The act of doing something repeatedly to improve one's skill or ability;  the customary or usual way of doing something.|例句：She needs more practice to perfect her technique. 她需要更多练习来完善她的技巧。
例句：The practice of medicine has changed dramatically in recent years. 医学实践近年来发生了巨大变化。|近义词：exercise, training, drill, custom, habit|反义词：theory, neglect, idleness|名词/动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I practice the piano every day. = 我每天练习钢琴。
He practices law in New York. = 他在纽约执业律师。


一般过去时:
She practiced yoga yesterday. = 她昨天练习瑜伽。
They practiced for the concert all week. = 他们整个星期都在为音乐会排练。


现在完成时:
I have practiced medicine for ten years. = 我行医十年了。
She has practiced the violin since she was a child. = 她从小就开始练习小提琴。


过去完成时:
He had practiced the speech many times before the presentation. = 他在演讲之前已经练习了很多遍。
They had practiced hard, so they felt confident. = 他们刻苦练习，所以感觉很自信。


将来时:
I will practice more next week. = 我下周会多练习。
They will practice their lines before the play. = 他们在演出前会练习他们的台词。


虚拟语气:
If I practiced more, I would be a better musician. = 如果我练习更多，我会成为一个更好的音乐家。
I wish I had practiced harder for the exam. = 我真希望我为考试更努力地练习过。


被动语态:
The technique is practiced by many athletes. = 许多运动员都在练习这项技术。
The law is practiced differently in various countries. = 在不同的国家，法律的实践方式也不同。


疑问句:
Do you practice any sports? = 你练习任何运动吗？
How often do they practice? = 他们多久练习一次？


否定句:
I don't practice enough. = 我练习不够。
He didn't practice for the test, so he failed. = 他没有为考试练习，所以考试不及格。
