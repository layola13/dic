minefields:/ˈmaɪnfiːldz/| n.|雷区，险境，充满危险的事物|Areas of land that have been deliberately planted with mines.  The term can also be used metaphorically to refer to situations that are full of hidden dangers or difficulties.|例句：The soldiers carefully navigated the minefields.士兵们小心翼翼地穿越雷区。

例句：The political situation is a minefield of potential conflicts.  政治局势充满了潜在的冲突，如同雷区。|近义词：danger zones, hazardous areas, treacherous terrain, perilous situations|反义词：safe areas, secure zones, clear paths
