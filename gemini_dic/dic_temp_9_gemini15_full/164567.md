preexaminer:/ˌpriːɪɡˈzæmɪnər/| n. |预审员，预先审查员|A preexaminer is a person who conducts a preliminary examination of something, such as a patent application, before it is fully examined.  They identify potential issues and help streamline the examination process. |The preexaminer flagged several inconsistencies in the patent application.|近义词： preliminary examiner, reviewer |反义词：final examiner
