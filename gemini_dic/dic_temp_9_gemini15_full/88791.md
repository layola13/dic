hoecake:/ˈhoʊkeɪk/| n. |玉米烙饼|A hoecake is a type of cornbread, traditionally cooked outdoors on a flat griddle or directly on hot coals or ashes.  It's often thin and round, similar to a pancake, but made with cornmeal.  The name likely derives from its cooking method, "hoe" referring to the tool used to prepare the cooking surface.|  She made a delicious hoecake over the campfire. 她用篝火烤制了一个美味的玉米烙饼。  He cooked the hoecake on a hot griddle. 他在热的烤盘上烤玉米烙饼。|cornbread, johnnycake|  (no direct antonyms)|名词
