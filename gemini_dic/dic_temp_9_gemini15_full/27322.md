calyptriform:/kælɪpˈtraɪfɔːrm/| adj.| 罩状的；帽状的 | Resembling a calyptra; hood-shaped.  The term refers to a structure that is cup-shaped or hood-shaped, often covering or protecting another structure.  | Example: The calyptriform covering protected the delicate new growth.  罩状的覆盖物保护着娇嫩的新芽。 |近义词：cup-shaped, hood-shaped,  calyptrate |反义词：open, exposed
