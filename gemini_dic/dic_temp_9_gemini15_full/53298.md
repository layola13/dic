dirtying: /ˈdɜːrtiɪŋ/ | v. |弄脏；使污秽 |The act of making something dirty.  This word is the present participle of the verb "to dirty". It describes the process of something becoming dirty, or the act of making something dirty. |例句：The children were dirtying their clothes playing in the mud.孩子们在泥地里玩耍弄脏了衣服。

The constant rain was dirtying the newly painted walls.  持续的雨水弄脏了新粉刷的墙壁。


近义词：soiling, fouling, staining, polluting | 反义词：cleaning, purifying


USAGE->

The dog is constantly dirtying the carpet. = 狗一直在弄脏地毯。
She is dirtying her hands with the project. = 她因为这个项目弄脏了双手。

一般过去时:
He dirtied his shirt playing football. = 他踢足球时弄脏了衬衫。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The children had dirtied all their clothes playing outside. = 孩子们在外面玩耍弄脏了所有的衣服。

将来时:

疑问句:

否定句:
