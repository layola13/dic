polyclimax:/ˌpɒliˈklaɪmæks/ /ˌpɑːliˈklaɪmæks/| n. |多顶极，多高潮|A polyclimax is a vegetation community that is not determined solely by climate, but also by other factors such as soil type, topography, and fire.  It suggests that multiple climax communities can exist in a given area, each reaching a stable state under different environmental conditions. |The study demonstrated a polyclimax pattern in the alpine meadow, with different plant communities thriving on various slopes and soil types.|近义词：multiple climax, complex climax|反义词：monoclimax
