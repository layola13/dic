misworshipped:/ˌmɪsˈwɜːrʃɪpt/| v.| 误拜；错误地崇拜 | To worship incorrectly or inappropriately; to give religious reverence to something undeserving. | Example: The ancient tribe misworshipped a stone as their god.  古代部落错误地崇拜一块石头为他们的神。| 近义词：misinterpret, misunderstand, idolize wrongly | 反义词：properly worship, truly venerate
