knowledge:/ˈnɒlɪdʒ/ /ˈnɑːlɪdʒ/| n. |知识，学识，了解|Understanding or awareness gained through experience or education.  It encompasses facts, information, descriptions, or skills acquired through experience or education.  |例句：He has a vast knowledge of history. 他有渊博的历史知识。

例句：Her knowledge of French is excellent. 她的法语知识很棒。


近义词：information, understanding, learning, expertise, wisdom |反义词：ignorance, unawareness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He possesses extensive knowledge of physics. = 他拥有广泛的物理知识。
She demonstrates a good knowledge of grammar. = 她展现了良好的语法知识。

一般过去时:
They gained valuable knowledge from their travels. = 他们从旅行中获得了宝贵的知识。
He acquired a deep knowledge of mathematics in college. = 他在大学里获得了深厚的数学知识。

现在完成时:
I have acquired a lot of knowledge in this field. = 我在这个领域已经积累了很多知识。
She has expanded her knowledge of art through museum visits. = 她通过参观博物馆扩大了她对艺术的了解。

过去完成时:
By the time he graduated, he had acquired a comprehensive knowledge of literature. = 到他毕业时，他已经掌握了全面的文学知识。
They had already amassed considerable knowledge before starting the project. = 在开始项目之前，他们已经积累了相当多的知识。

将来时:
He will gain more knowledge through further study. = 他将通过进一步学习获得更多知识。
She will have a better knowledge of the subject after the course. = 课程结束后，她将对该主题有更好的了解。

虚拟语气:
If I had more knowledge, I could solve this problem. = 如果我有更多知识，我就能解决这个问题。
I wish I had the knowledge to fix this machine. = 我希望我有修理这台机器的知识。

被动语态:
Much knowledge is gained through observation. = 许多知识是通过观察获得的。
This knowledge was passed down through generations. = 这项知识代代相传。

疑问句:
What knowledge do you need to complete this task? = 完成这项任务你需要什么知识？
How much knowledge do they possess in this area? = 他们在这一领域拥有多少知识？

否定句:
He lacks the knowledge to operate this machine. = 他缺乏操作这台机器的知识。
She has no knowledge of this event. = 她对这件事一无所知。
