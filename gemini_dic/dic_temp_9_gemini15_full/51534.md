detumescence: /ˌdiːtjuˈmɛsns/ | n. |消肿；消退| The gradual reduction in swelling.  It refers to the process by which a swollen area returns to its normal size and shape. This process is often a natural part of the body's healing response to injury or inflammation. | Example:  The detumescence of the ankle took several days after the sprain.  踝关节扭伤后的消肿用了好几天。|近义词：resolution, subsidence |反义词：tumescence, swelling |名词
