compliance:/kəmˈplaɪəns/ | n. |遵从，服从，遵守 | The act of conforming to a rule, standard, or law; the state of being compliant.  It often refers to adhering to regulations or requirements. |例句：The company is under investigation for non-compliance with safety regulations. 这家公司因未遵守安全条例而正在接受调查。

例句：Their compliance with the new rules was immediate and complete. 他们立即且完全遵守了新规。


近义词：obedience, conformity, adherence, observance | 反义词：disobedience, defiance, noncompliance, violation | 词性：名词

词源:
The word "compliance" comes from the Old French word "complience," meaning "fulfillment," "completion," or "agreement."  This, in turn, is derived from the Latin word "complere," meaning "to fill up," "to complete," or "to fulfill."  The sense of "obedience" or "conformity" developed later.
