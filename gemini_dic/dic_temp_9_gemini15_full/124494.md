moulds:/moʊldz/| n. v.| 模具；霉菌；塑造；使成形 |  Moulds refer to either a hollow container used to give a specific shape to molten material or a type of fungus that grows on decaying organic matter.  As a verb, it means to shape or form something. |  例句：The baker used moulds to make beautiful bread. 面包师用模具做出了漂亮的麵包。  The cheese was covered in mould. 乳酪上长满了霉菌。|近义词：molds, shapes, forms, casts |反义词：destroy, ruin
