innerness:/ˈɪnənəs/| n. |内在性；内在；本质|The quality or state of being inner; the innermost part of something; the essential nature of something.  It often refers to a person's inner life, thoughts, and feelings, or the core essence of something.|例句：He sought to understand the innerness of the painting. 他试图理解这幅画的内在含义。


例句：The artist captured the innerness of the subject's soul.  这位艺术家捕捉到了模特灵魂的内在。


近义词：inwardness, essence, core, heart, depth|反义词：exteriority, superficiality, surface


词性：名词
