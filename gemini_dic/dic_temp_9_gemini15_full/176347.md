re-excite:/ˌriːɪkˈsaɪt/| vt.|再次激发；重新点燃热情|To excite again; to rekindle enthusiasm or interest.|例句：The announcement re-excited the already enthusiastic crowd.  公告再次激发了已经热情高涨的人群。|例句：The unexpected victory re-excited the team's hopes for the championship.  意外的胜利重新点燃了球队夺冠的希望。|近义词：reinvigorate, rekindle, stimulate|反义词：dampen, quell, discourage|动词


USAGE->

The news re-excites the market. = 这则新闻再次刺激了市场。
They re-excite the audience with their performance. = 他们的表演再次点燃了观众的热情。
