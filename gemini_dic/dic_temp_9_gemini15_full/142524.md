ossiferous:/ˈɒsɪfərəs/ | adj. |含骨的；成骨的|Containing or composed of bone; relating to bone formation.|The paleontologist carefully examined the ossiferous rock formation, hoping to find fossilized remains.|近义词：bony, osteal |反义词：boneless, cartilaginous


USAGE->

The bone marrow is ossiferous. = 骨髓是含骨的。（This example is slightly unnatural, as "ossiferous" usually describes rock or soil.  A better example would be needed for natural usage in present simple.)
This area is known to be ossiferous. = 这个地区以含骨而闻名。
