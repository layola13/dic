cochairwoman:/ˈkoʊtʃɛrwʊmən/| n. |女共同主席 |A woman who shares the role of chairperson with another person.  This term emphasizes the shared responsibility and equal standing of the two individuals leading a meeting, organization, or committee.|例句：The conference will be co-chaired by two prominent academics.  这次会议将由两位著名的学者共同主持。|近义词：co-president, joint chairwoman, copresident|反义词：sole chairwoman, single chair, chairperson
