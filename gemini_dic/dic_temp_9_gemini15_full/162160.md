pre-induction: /ˌpriːɪnˈdʌkʃən/ | adj. | 征兵前的；入伍前的 |  Relating to the period of time before someone is officially inducted into military service or a similar organization. This period often involves medical examinations, training, and administrative processing. | 例句：The pre-induction physical examination is mandatory for all recruits. = 所有新兵都必须进行入伍前的体检。

He underwent extensive pre-induction training. = 他接受了广泛的入伍前训练。


近义词：antecedent, preliminary, preparatory | 反义词：post-induction, subsequent


词性:形容词
