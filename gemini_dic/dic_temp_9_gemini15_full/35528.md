coagulating:/koʊˈæɡjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 凝结；凝固；使凝结 | The process of changing from a liquid to a solid or semi-solid state.  This often involves the clumping together of particles. | Example: The blood was coagulating rapidly.  血液正在迅速凝固。 The milk is coagulating in the heat. 牛奶在高温下凝结了。|近义词：clotting, thickening, solidifying | 反义词：liquefying, thinning, dissolving | 动词
