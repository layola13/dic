hardenabilities:/ˌhɑːrdənəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 硬化能力；淬透性 | The ability of a metal to be hardened by heat treatment.  The hardenabilities of different steels vary greatly.  | 例句：The hardenabilities of these alloys are being tested.  这些合金的淬透性正在测试中。

The steel's high hardenability allowed for deep hardening.  这种钢材的高淬透性使得深层硬化成为可能。


近义词：hardening capacity, hardenability  | 反义词：no specific antonym, but the opposite would be the inability to harden. | 词性: noun
