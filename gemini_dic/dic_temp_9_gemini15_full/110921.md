lipoma:/laɪˈpoʊmə/| n. |脂肪瘤|A lipoma is a benign (noncancerous) tumor made up of fat cells.  Lipomas are usually found just beneath the skin, and they feel soft and rubbery to the touch. They are typically painless and slow-growing. |Example: The doctor confirmed that the lump on my arm was a harmless lipoma.  医生确认我胳膊上的肿块是良性的脂肪瘤。|近义词：fatty tumor, adipose tumor |反义词：liposarcoma (恶性脂肪肉瘤)
