rustication:/rʌstɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|乡村生活；放逐；休学；粗糙的表面处理|The act of sending someone to live in the country, especially as a punishment;  the state of living in the country; a rough, unfinished surface treatment.  It can also refer to the suspension of a student from a college or university.|例句：His rustication to the countryside proved surprisingly restorative.  他的乡村放逐生活出人意料地恢复了他的元气。

例句：The rustication of the stone walls gave the building a charming, old-world feel. 石墙粗糙的表面处理使建筑物呈现出迷人的古老风格。


近义词：exile, banishment, suspension, expulsion,  roughening

反义词：urbanization, sophistication, refinement, polish
