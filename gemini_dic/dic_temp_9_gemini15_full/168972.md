profiter:/ˈprɒfɪtə(r)/|/ˈproʊfɪtər/| n. |获利者，受益者 |A profiter is someone who benefits from a situation, often unfairly or at someone else's expense.  They gain an advantage or profit from a situation, sometimes without contributing much. |Example:  He was a profiter from the war, making a fortune selling supplies to the army. 他是战争的获利者，通过向军队出售物资赚了一大笔钱。|近义词: beneficiary, gainer, exploiter |反义词: loser, victim


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company profits from its innovative products. = 这家公司从其创新产品中获利。
She profits greatly from her investments. = 她从投资中获益良多。


一般过去时:
They profited handsomely from the deal. = 他们从这笔交易中获得了丰厚的利润。
He profited little from his hard work. = 他从辛勤工作中获益甚少。


现在完成时:
We have profited immensely from this partnership. = 我们从这次合作中受益匪浅。
She has profited significantly from her education. = 她从教育中获得了巨大的收益。


过去完成时:
They had profited considerably before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经获得了相当可观的利润。
The company had profited from the previous year's sales. = 公司从上一年的销售中获利。


将来时:
We will profit from this new technology. = 我们将从这项新技术中获益。
The investors expect to profit greatly from the venture. = 投资者期望从这项投资中获得巨大利润。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested earlier, we would have profited more. = 如果我们早点投资，我们会获得更多利润。
I wish I had profited from his advice. = 我真希望我听了他的建议。


被动语态:
The shareholders were profited by the company's success. = 公司的成功使股东们获益。
The community will be profited by the new project. = 新项目将使社区受益。


疑问句:
How much did they profit from the sale? = 他们从这次销售中获利多少？
Will we profit from this investment? = 我们会从这项投资中获利吗？


否定句:
They didn't profit from the venture. = 他们没有从这项投资中获利。
The company did not profit as much as expected. = 公司的利润没有达到预期。
