mixability:/ˌmɪksəˈbɪləti/| n. |混合性，可混合性 |The ability of substances or things to be mixed together without undesirable effects.  This refers to the ease and compatibility of mixing different components, often in a specific context like in a chemical reaction, food preparation or materials science.  It can also refer to the ability of different groups of people to interact harmoniously. |例句：The mixability of these paints is excellent, producing a smooth, even finish.  这些油漆的混合性极佳，能产生光滑均匀的表面。

The high mixability of the ingredients makes it easy to prepare the dish. 配料的高混合性使得菜肴很容易制作。|近义词：compatibility, blendability, miscibility |反义词：incompatibility, immiscibility
