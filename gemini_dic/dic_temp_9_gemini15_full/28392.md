carbolize:/ˈkɑːrbəlaɪz/| vt.|用碳酸处理；用石炭酸消毒|To treat or disinfect with carbolic acid (phenol).  This process was commonly used in the past for disinfecting wounds and preventing infection.  The effectiveness of carbolic acid as an antiseptic is now largely superseded by more modern methods.|例句：The surgeon carbolized the wound before stitching it. 外科医生在缝合伤口前用石炭酸消毒了伤口。 The laboratory technician carbolized the equipment to ensure sterility. 实验室技术员用石炭酸消毒设备以确保无菌。|近义词：disinfect, sterilize|反义词：contaminate
