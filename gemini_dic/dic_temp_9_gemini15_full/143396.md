outfit:/ˈaʊtˌfɪt/| n. |一套衣服，服装，装备 |A complete set of clothing, equipment, or furnishing.  |Example:  She wore a stylish outfit to the party. 她穿着一套时尚的衣服去参加派对。|近义词：ensemble, attire, costume |反义词: none


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My outfit is comfortable. = 我的衣服很舒服。
This outfit looks great on you. = 这套衣服你穿起来很好看。


一般过去时:
I wore a different outfit yesterday. = 昨天我穿了不同的衣服。
The outfit was too formal for the occasion. = 这套衣服对于这个场合来说太正式了。


现在完成时:
I have worn this outfit several times. = 我已经穿过这套衣服好几次了。
She has bought a new outfit for the wedding. = 她为婚礼买了一套新衣服。


过去完成时:
I had already changed my outfit before they arrived. = 在他们到达之前我已经换好衣服了。
They had prepared the outfits for the play earlier that week. = 他们在那周早些时候就为戏剧准备好了服装。


将来时:
I will wear a new outfit to the interview. = 我将穿一套新衣服去面试。
What outfit will you wear to the concert? = 你要去音乐会穿什么衣服？


虚拟语气:
If I had a nicer outfit, I would go to the party. = 如果我有一套更好的衣服，我会去参加派对。
I wish I had a more suitable outfit for the meeting. = 我希望我有一套更适合会议的衣服。
