reentering:/ˌriːˈɛntəriŋ/| v.|重新进入，再进入|The act of entering again; returning to a place or situation.  It often implies a previous entry or a return after an absence or interruption.|例句：The spacecraft is currently reentering the Earth's atmosphere.  宇宙飞船目前正在重返地球大气层。

例句：After a brief intermission, the actors reentered the stage.  短暂的中场休息后，演员们重新登台。


近义词：re-entering, returning, coming back|反义词：exiting, leaving, departing|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reenters the room every five minutes. 他每五分钟就重新进入房间。
She reenters the conversation after a long pause. 她长时间停顿后重新加入谈话。


一般过去时:
The astronaut reentered the spaceship safely. 宇航员安全地重新进入宇宙飞船。
He reentered the competition after a year's absence.  他休战一年后重新参加了比赛。


现在完成时:
The satellite has reentered the Earth's atmosphere and burned up.  卫星已经重返地球大气层并燃烧殆尽。
She has reentered the workforce after raising her children. 她抚养孩子后重新回到了职场。


过去完成时:
They had reentered the building before the fire alarm sounded.  火警响起之前他们已经重新进入大楼。
Before the meeting resumed, he had reentered the conference room.  会议恢复之前，他已经重新进入会议室。


将来时:
The spacecraft will reenter the atmosphere tomorrow morning.  宇宙飞船将于明天早上重返大气层。
He will reenter the race after recovering from his injury. 他伤愈后将重新参加比赛。


虚拟语气:
If he reentered the contest, he might win. 如果他重新参加比赛，他可能会赢。
I wish he had reentered the university. 我希望他重新回到大学。


被动语态:
The building was reentered by the police.  这栋建筑物被警方重新进入。
The data was reentered into the system after the error was corrected.  在错误被纠正后，数据被重新输入系统。


疑问句:
Will the pilot reenter the cockpit?  飞行员会重新进入驾驶舱吗？
Did the thief reenter the house after the police left?  警察离开后，小偷是否又重新进入房子？


否定句:
He did not reenter the competition. 他没有重新参加比赛。
She will not reenter the building without permission. 她未经许可不会重新进入大楼。
