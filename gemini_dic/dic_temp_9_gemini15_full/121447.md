misinterpreted:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrpətɪd/| v. |误解，曲解 |To understand or explain something incorrectly. |例句：The instructions were misinterpreted, leading to a delay in the project.  指令被误解了，导致项目延期。
The speaker's words were misinterpreted by the audience. 演讲者的言辞被听众误解了。|近义词：misunderstood, misconstrued, mistook |反义词：understood, correctly interpreted
