nominalising:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 名词化；使…成为名词 | The process of converting a verb, adjective, or other part of speech into a noun. This often involves adding suffixes like "-tion," "-ment," "-ance," or "-ing."  Nominalisation can make writing more concise but can also make it less clear and more abstract. | The *publication* of the findings was delayed. (from "publish")  His *appointment* to the position was unexpected. (from "appoint") |  verbification, substantiation |  (No direct antonym, but the opposite process could be described as "de-nominalisation" or "verbifying" etc.) | 动词
