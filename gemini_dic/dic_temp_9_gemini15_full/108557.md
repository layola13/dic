lazying:/ˈleɪziŋ/ | v. | 懒洋洋地躺着；懒散地度过 |  The verb "lazying" describes the act of relaxing and doing very little, often in a leisurely or indolent manner. It implies a state of relaxation and inactivity.  |例句：He spent the afternoon lazying by the pool. 他在泳池边懒洋洋地度过了一下午。

例句：She was lazying on the sofa, watching television. 她懒洋洋地躺在沙发上看电视。


近义词：loafing, idling, lounging, relaxing, dozing | 反义词：hustling, bustling, working, toiling | 词性：动词
