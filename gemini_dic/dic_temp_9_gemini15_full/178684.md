recriminative:/rɪˈkrɪmɪneɪtɪv/| adj.|相互指责的；反驳的|Accusatory and bitter; characterized by mutual accusations.  It implies a blaming atmosphere where each party accuses the other. |Example: The meeting descended into a recriminative shouting match, with each side blaming the other for the project's failure.  这次会议演变成一场相互指责的喊叫比赛，双方互相指责对方导致项目失败。|近义词：accusatory, reproachful, blaming, censorious|反义词：forgiving, conciliatory, apologetic
