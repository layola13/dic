rocambole:/ˌroʊkæmˈboʊl/| n. |卷心菜, 蒜苗|A type of garlic, Allium scorodoprasum, also known as wild garlic or sand leek. It is a perennial herb with a mild garlic flavor. The leaves are edible and can be used in salads or cooked dishes.
