kinkajou: /ˈkɪŋkədʒuː/ | n. |蜜熊|A nocturnal, arboreal mammal of Central and South America, belonging to the family Procyonidae (raccoons, coatis, etc.). It is characterized by its prehensile tail, small size, and omnivorous diet.  Kinkajous are known for their playful and curious nature. |例句：The kinkajou deftly swung from branch to branch using its prehensile tail.  蜜熊灵巧地用它那条缠绕的尾巴在树枝间荡来荡去。

The kinkajou's diet consists of fruits, nectar, insects, and small animals. 蜜熊的食物包括水果、花蜜、昆虫和小动物。
