re-christianizes: /ˌriːˈkrɪʃtʃənʌɪz/ | vt. |重新基督教化；使再次皈依基督教 | To convert (a person or place) back to Christianity or to impose Christian beliefs and practices on (a person or place) again.  | 例句：The government attempted to re-christianize the country after decades of secular rule.  政府试图在几十年的世俗统治后重新使国家基督教化。


The movement aimed to re-christianize the region by establishing new churches and schools.  该运动旨在通过建立新的教堂和学校来使该地区重新基督教化。

近义词：reconvert, re-evangelize | 反义词：secularize, dechristianize | 动词
