confided:/kənˈfaɪdɪd/| v.| 吐露，倾诉，信任 | To tell someone something secret or private, or to trust someone with a secret. |例句：She confided her deepest fears to her diary. 她把她最深的恐惧倾诉给了日记。

例句：He confided in his best friend about his problems. 他向他最好的朋友倾诉了他的烦恼。


近义词：entrusted, revealed, disclosed | 反义词：concealed, hid, kept secret | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confides in his therapist every week. 他每周都向他的治疗师倾诉。
She confides her secrets to her sister. 她向她姐姐吐露她的秘密。


一般过去时:
I confided my worries to my mother. 我向母亲倾诉了我的担忧。
She confided in her friend about the breakup. 她向朋友倾诉了分手的事情。


现在完成时:
He has confided in me about his financial difficulties. 他向我吐露了他经济上的困难。
She has confided all her secrets to him. 她已经把所有秘密都告诉他了。


过去完成时:
I had confided in him before, so he knew what to expect. 我以前就向他倾诉过，所以他知道该期待什么。
She had confided her plans to her family before announcing them publicly. 在公开宣布之前，她已经向家人透露了她的计划。


将来时:
I will confide in you when I'm ready. 我准备好后会向你倾诉。
She will confide her feelings to her journal. 她将把她的感受倾诉给日记。


虚拟语气:
If I had confided in someone sooner, things might have been different. 如果我早点向别人倾诉，情况可能会有所不同。
I wish I had confided in my parents when I was struggling. 我真希望在我挣扎的时候能向父母倾诉。


被动语态:
His secrets were confided to his closest friend. 他的秘密被告诉了他最亲密的朋友。
The information was confided to only a select few. 信息只被告诉了少数几个人。


疑问句:
Did you confide in anyone about your problems? 你有没有向任何人倾诉你的烦恼？
To whom did she confide her deepest fears? 她向谁倾诉了她最深的恐惧？


否定句:
He did not confide in anyone about his illness. 他没有向任何人倾诉他的病情。
She did not confide her anxieties to her family. 她没有向家人倾诉她的焦虑。
