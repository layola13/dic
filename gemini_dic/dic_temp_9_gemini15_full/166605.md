preselects:/ˌpriːˈsɛləkts/| vt.|预选，预先选择|To choose or select something in advance.|The committee preselects candidates before the final interview. 委员会在最终面试前预先挑选候选人。|近义词：choose beforehand, select in advance|反义词：postselect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company preselects candidates based on their resumes. = 公司根据简历预先筛选候选人。
He preselects his favorite songs for the playlist. = 他预先选择他最喜欢的歌曲添加到播放列表。

一般过去时:
They preselected the winning team before the competition. = 他们在比赛前预先选定了获胜队伍。
She preselected the fabrics for the new dress. = 她预先挑选了新裙子的面料。

现在完成时:
The organizers have preselected the speakers for the conference. = 组织者已经为会议预先挑选了演讲者。
We have preselected the locations for our vacation. = 我们已经预先选好了度假地点。

过去完成时:
They had preselected the finalists before the judges arrived. = 在评委到达之前，他们已经预先选定了决赛选手。
The director had preselected the actors before the auditions. = 导演在试镜前就已经预先选好了演员。

将来时:
The school will preselect students for the scholarship program. = 学校将为奖学金项目预先挑选学生。
We will preselect the menu items for the party. = 我们将预先挑选派对的菜单。

虚拟语气:
If they preselected the right candidate, the project would succeed. = 如果他们预先选对了候选人，项目就会成功。
I wish they had preselected a better location for the event. = 我希望他们为活动预先选择一个更好的地点。

被动语态:
The candidates were preselected by a panel of experts. = 候选人是由专家小组预先选定的。
The songs were preselected for the radio program. = 歌曲是为广播节目预先选定的。

疑问句:
Were the participants preselected for the study? = 研究参与者是预先选定的吗？
Have the finalists been preselected yet? = 决赛选手已经被预先选定了吗？

否定句:
The committee did not preselect any candidates for the position. = 委员会没有为该职位预先挑选任何候选人。
They didn't preselect the music for the wedding. = 他们没有为婚礼预先挑选音乐。
