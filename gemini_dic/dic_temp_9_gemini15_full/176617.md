reacclimated:/ˌriːəˈklaɪmətɪd/ | v. |重新适应；使重新适应 | To become accustomed or adjusted to a new climate or environment again after a period of change.  |例句：After a week in the mountains, we reacclimated to the city's heat and noise.  在一周的山区旅行后，我们重新适应了城市里喧嚣的热浪。

例句：The astronauts reacclimated to Earth's gravity after their space mission. 宇航员在太空任务后重新适应了地球的重力。


近义词：readapt, readjust, re-accustom | 反义词：maladapt
