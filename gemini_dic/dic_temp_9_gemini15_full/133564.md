nonfixation:/ˌnɒnˌfɪkˈseɪʃən/ | n. |非注视；非注视点 |The lack of fixation or a tendency to avoid fixation.  It refers to the absence of a stable point of visual focus, often associated with eye movement disorders or certain neurological conditions.  This term is primarily used in the fields of ophthalmology, optometry, and neurology. |例句：The patient exhibited symptoms of nonfixation, making it difficult to assess their visual acuity.  患者表现出非注视症状，这使得难以评估他们的视力。


The study revealed a significant correlation between nonfixation and reading difficulties. 这项研究揭示了非注视与阅读困难之间存在显著的相关性。|近义词：lack of fixation, aversion to fixation |反义词：fixation, stable gaze
