escap:/ɪˈskeɪp/ | v. |逃脱，逃避 | to get away from a place or situation, especially one that is dangerous or unpleasant. | 例句：He escaped from prison. 他越狱了。| 例句：She narrowly escaped being injured in the accident. 她险些在事故中受伤。|近义词： flee, evade, avoid | 反义词：capture, arrest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He escapes his responsibilities. = 他逃避责任。
They escape the city for the weekend. = 他们周末逃离城市。

一般过去时:
The prisoner escaped from jail. = 囚犯越狱了。
She escaped the burning building. = 她逃离了燃烧的建筑物。

现在完成时:
He has escaped capture for months. = 他已经逃亡数月了。
They have escaped the worst of the storm. = 他们躲过了风暴最严重的部分。

过去完成时:
She had escaped serious injury. = 她逃过了一场重伤。
The thieves had escaped before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经逃跑了。

将来时:
He will escape the consequences of his actions. = 他将会逃避他行为的后果。
They will escape to the mountains for a holiday. = 他们将去山里度假。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't run, he wouldn't have escaped. = 如果他不跑，他就不会逃脱。
I wish I could escape this feeling of dread. = 我希望我能摆脱这种恐惧感。

被动语态:
The prisoner was escaped. = 囚犯逃跑了。(注意此句被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The city was escaped by many people during the war. = 战争期间许多人逃离了这座城市。

疑问句:
Did he escape? = 他逃脱了吗？
How did she escape? = 她是怎么逃脱的？

否定句:
He didn't escape the consequences. = 他没有逃避后果。
They couldn't escape their fate. = 他们无法逃避他们的命运。
