regret:/rɪˈɡrɛt/| vt. n.|后悔，遗憾；抱歉|to feel sorry about something you have done or about something that has happened| I regret telling him the truth. 我后悔告诉他真相了。|近义词：rue, repent|反义词：rejoice, celebrate|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I regret my decision. = 我后悔我的决定。
They regret missing the concert. = 他们后悔错过了音乐会。

一般过去时:
He regretted his actions. = 他后悔他的行为。
She regretted not studying harder. = 她后悔没有更努力学习。

现在完成时:
I have regretted this ever since. = 从那以后我一直后悔这件事。
We have regretted our hasty words. = 我们一直后悔我们说过的话。

过去完成时:
I had regretted my decision before I could change it. = 我还没来得及改变决定就后悔了。
She had regretted her choice long before the consequences became apparent. = 早在她意识到后果之前，她就后悔她的选择了。

将来时:
I will regret this someday. = 我总有一天会后悔这件事。
You will regret not taking this opportunity. = 你会后悔没有抓住这次机会的。

虚拟语气:
I wish I hadn't said that; I regret saying it. = 我真希望我没说过那些话；我后悔说了那些话。
If I had studied harder, I wouldn't regret it now. = 如果我当时学习更努力，现在就不会后悔了。

被动语态:
His actions are regretted by many. = 他的行为被许多人所后悔。
The decision was deeply regretted by everyone involved. = 这个决定让所有参与者都深感遗憾。

疑问句:
Do you regret your choice? = 你后悔你的选择吗？
Does she regret her actions? = 她后悔她的行为吗？

否定句:
I don't regret anything I did. = 我不后悔我所做的一切。
He didn't regret his decision at all. = 他一点也不后悔他的决定。


PHRASE->
regret to inform you = 遗憾地通知你
regrettably = 遗憾的是
have no regrets = 毫无遗憾
express regret = 表示遗憾
deep regret = 深切的遗憾
