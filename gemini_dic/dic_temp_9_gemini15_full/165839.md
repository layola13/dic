preoccupy:/ˌpriːˈɒkjʊpaɪ/ | /ˌpriːˈɑːkjupaɪ/| vt. |使全神贯注；占据（某人的思想）；抢先占用 |To dominate or engross the mind of (someone); to fill the mind of (someone) to the exclusion of other thoughts.  |例句：The problem of global warming preoccupies many scientists. 全球变暖问题让许多科学家忧心忡忡。

例句：Her worries about her son preoccupied her thoughts all day.  她整天都被对儿子的担忧所占据。


近义词：absorb, engross, occupy


反义词：distract, divert, free


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preoccupies himself with his work. = 他专心致志于他的工作。
My worries preoccupy me. = 我的担忧占据着我的思绪。


一般过去时:
The news preoccupied her for days. = 这个消息让她忧心忡忡了好几天。
The meeting preoccupied the manager's attention. = 会议占据了经理的注意力。


现在完成时:
The project has preoccupied her for months. = 这个项目已经让她忙了好几个月了。
This problem has preoccupied my mind for a week. = 这问题困扰了我一个星期。


过去完成时:
She had been preoccupied with her studies all week, so she missed the party. = 她整个星期都忙于学习，所以错过了派对。
He had preoccupied himself with the task, ignoring all other requests. 他一直忙于这项任务，忽略了所有其他的请求。


将来时:
I know that this matter will preoccupy us for quite some time. = 我知道这件事会让我们忙很长一段时间。
The upcoming exam will preoccupy him next week. = 下周即将到来的考试会让他分心。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't preoccupied with work, I would have gone to the beach. = 如果我不忙于工作，我就去海滩了。
I wish I weren't so preoccupied with my problems. = 我希望自己不要被问题困扰。


被动语态:
His mind was preoccupied with anxieties. = 他的思绪被焦虑所占据。
Their attention was preoccupied by the loud music. = 他们的注意力被吵闹的音乐所吸引。


疑问句:
Is anything preoccupying you at the moment? = 目前有什么事情让你担忧吗？
What preoccupies you most these days? = 这些天来最让你担忧的是什么？


否定句:
She wasn't preoccupied with her work, she was simply tired. = 她不是忙于工作，她只是累了。
He wasn't preoccupied; he was just daydreaming. = 他并没有专心致志；他只是在做白日梦。



PHRASE->
be preoccupied with = 忙于……；为……所占据
become preoccupied = 变得心事重重
preoccupied expression = 心事重重的表情
deeply preoccupied = 非常专注
preoccupied silence = 沉默不语
