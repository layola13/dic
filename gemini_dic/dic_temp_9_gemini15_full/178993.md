redefector:/ˌriːˈdɛfɛktər/| n. |重新叛逃者，二次叛逃者 | A person who defects from one side to another and then defects back again.  This term is less common and often implies a cyclical pattern of defection. | Example: The redefector's actions caused significant mistrust among both sides.  重新叛逃者的行为导致双方都极度不信任。 |近义词:double defector, turncoat (in a less specific sense) |反义词:loyalist, patriot |名词
