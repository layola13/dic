logs:/lɒɡz/ /lɑːɡz/| n.|原木；日志；记录|Wooden stems of trees, especially those felled for timber;  a chronological record of events or activities.|例句：The logs were stacked neatly by the fireplace.  原木整齐地堆放在壁炉旁。
The system logs all user activity. 系统记录所有用户活动。|近义词：records, entries, chronicles|反义词：N/A
