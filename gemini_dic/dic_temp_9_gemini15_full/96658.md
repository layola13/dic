industrialize: /ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使工业化；使产业化 | To develop industry in (a country or region) on a large scale.  |例句：The government is committed to industrializing the rural areas. 政府致力于将农村地区工业化。|例句：Many developing countries are striving to industrialize rapidly. 许多发展中国家都在努力快速工业化。|近义词：modernize, mechanize |反义词：deindustrialize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The country industrializes its agricultural sector. = 这个国家正在将其农业部门工业化。
They industrialize the production process. = 他们使生产过程工业化。


一般过去时:
The region industrialized rapidly after the discovery of oil. = 在发现石油后，该地区迅速实现了工业化。
Britain industrialized during the 18th and 19th centuries. = 英国在18和19世纪经历了工业化。


现在完成时:
Many nations have industrialized in the past century. = 许多国家在上个世纪已经实现了工业化。
China has rapidly industrialized its economy. = 中国已经迅速使其经济工业化。


过去完成时:
Before the war, the country had not fully industrialized. = 在战争爆发前，这个国家尚未完全工业化。
By the time the war ended, the nation had largely industrialized. = 到战争结束时，这个国家已经很大程度上实现了工业化。


将来时:
The government plans to industrialize the underdeveloped regions. = 政府计划将欠发达地区工业化。
This technology will help us industrialize the manufacturing process. = 这项技术将帮助我们使制造过程工业化。


虚拟语气:
If we industrialized quickly, we would become a major economic power. = 如果我们快速工业化，我们将成为一个主要的经济强国。
I wish the country had industrialized more sustainably. = 我希望这个国家能更可持续地发展工业化。


被动语态:
The country's economy was industrialized by foreign investment. = 该国的经济是通过外国投资实现工业化的。
The process is being industrialized to increase efficiency. = 为了提高效率，这个过程正在被工业化。


疑问句:
Has the region successfully industrialized? = 这个地区是否成功地实现了工业化？
How will we industrialize sustainably? = 我们将如何可持续地发展工业化？


否定句:
The government did not industrialize the economy effectively. = 政府没有有效地使经济工业化。
They haven't yet industrialized that part of the country. = 他们还没有将该国的那一部分工业化。
