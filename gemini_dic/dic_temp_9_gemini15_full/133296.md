nonexperientially:/ˌnɒnˌɛkspɪəriˈɛnʃəli/ | adv. | 非经验地，非经验性地 |  Not based on or derived from experience; theoretically or conceptually.  It describes something that is understood or discussed without relying on personal experience or observation. |  The philosopher argued for a nonexperientially based understanding of morality.  哲学家论证了一种基于非经验的道德理解。


The theory was developed nonexperientially, relying solely on logical deduction. 这一理论的形成并非基于经验，而是完全依靠逻辑推演。| 近义词：theoretically, conceptually, abstractly | 反义词：empirically, experimentally
