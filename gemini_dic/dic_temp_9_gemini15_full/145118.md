overargue:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrɡjuː/ | vt. | 过度争论；争论不休 | To argue excessively or to a point of exhaustion or futility.  It implies a prolonged and often unproductive argument. | 例句：They overargued the point until everyone was exhausted.  他们对这一点争论不休，直到每个人都筋疲力尽。

They overargued the details of the contract, delaying the signing. 他们对合同的细节争论不休，延误了签字。| 近义词：quarrel excessively, bicker endlessly, debate tediously | 反义词：agree readily, concede easily, compromise willingly | 动词
