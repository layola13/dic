multilateralism:/ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlɪzəm/ | n. | 多边主义 | The policy or practice of involving multiple countries in an activity or agreement.  It emphasizes cooperation and collaboration among multiple nations to achieve shared goals. |  Example: The success of the climate change agreement depends heavily on multilateralism.  气候变化协议的成功很大程度上取决于多边主义。


The United Nations is a prime example of multilateralism in action. 联合国是多边主义行动的一个主要例子。| 近义词：international cooperation, global collaboration | 反义词：unilateralism, bilateralism, isolationism
