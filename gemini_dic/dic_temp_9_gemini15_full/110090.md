lichgate:/ˈlɪtʃˌɡeɪt/| n. |灵柩门，墓地入口处的遮盖物|A covered shelter, usually with a roof and sometimes walls, placed over the entrance to a churchyard, providing shelter for mourners and pallbearers during funeral processions.  It often serves as a symbolic transition between the living world and the world of the dead. | Example: The ancient lichgate stood sentinel over the graveyard, its weathered wood a testament to time.  古老的灵柩门守护着墓地，其风化的木头诉说着时间的痕迹。 The mourners paused beneath the lichgate before entering the churchyard. 哀悼者在进入墓地之前停留在灵柩门下。|近义词：churchyard gate, covered gateway|反义词：None directly applicable.
