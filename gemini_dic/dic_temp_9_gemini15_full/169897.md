prospecting:/ˈprɒspektɪŋ/| /ˈprɑːspektɪŋ/| n. v.|勘探；寻找；寻找潜在客户|The act of searching for something, typically valuable resources or potential customers.|例句：The company is prospecting for oil in the North Sea.  该公司正在北海勘探石油。

例句：The sales team is prospecting for new clients. 销售团队正在寻找新的客户。


近义词：exploration, searching, scouting, prospecting for clients


反义词：exploitation (in the sense of already having found and utilizing a resource)


词性：Noun & Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They prospect for gold in the mountains. = 他们在山上寻找黄金。
She prospects for new business opportunities. = 她正在寻找新的商机。


一般过去时:
We prospected the area for minerals. = 我们勘探了该地区的矿产。
He prospected for oil in Texas. = 他在德克萨斯州勘探石油。


现在完成时:
They have prospected this region for years. = 他们多年来一直在勘探这个地区。
She has prospected many potential clients this month. = 这个月她已经寻找了许多潜在客户。


过去完成时:
They had prospected several sites before finding gold. = 他们在找到金子之前已经勘探过几个地点。
The company had prospected that area for years without success. = 那家公司多年来一直在勘探那个地区，但没有成功。


将来时:
We will prospect for oil in the Amazon rainforest. = 我们将在亚马逊雨林勘探石油。
They will prospect for new markets in Asia. = 他们将在亚洲寻找新的市场。


虚拟语气:
If we had better equipment, we would prospect more efficiently. = 如果我们有更好的设备，我们将更有效地进行勘探。
If I were you, I would prospect more carefully before making a decision. = 如果我是你，我会在做决定之前更仔细地进行勘探。


被动语态:
The area was prospected for diamonds. = 该地区被勘探钻石。
New clients are being prospected by the sales team. = 销售团队正在寻找新的客户。


疑问句:
Have you prospected that area before? = 你以前勘探过那个地区吗？
Are they currently prospecting for new investors? = 他们目前正在寻找新的投资者吗？


否定句:
We did not prospect that area due to environmental concerns. = 由于环境问题，我们没有勘探那个地区。
They haven't prospected the northern region yet. = 他们还没有勘探北部地区。
