iterate:/ˈɪtəreɪt/ | vt. vi. n.|重复；迭代；反复；重申|To say or do something again several times.  To perform or repeat a process repeatedly.  As a noun, it refers to a single repetition in a process.|例句：The teacher iterated the instructions several times to ensure everyone understood.  老师反复重复说明以确保每个人都理解。

The program iterates through the list, processing each item. 程序迭代遍历列表，处理每个项目。


近义词：repeat, reiterate, replay,  rehash|反义词：terminate, cease, stop|动词

词源:
The word "iterate" comes from the Latin word "iterare," meaning "to repeat."  The Latin word itself is derived from "iter," meaning "a journey" or "course." The sense of repetition comes from the idea of retracing a path or repeating a course of action.
