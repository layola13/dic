rammer:/ˈræmər/| n. v.|夯锤；夯实机；捣实器；猛击者；|A rammer is a tool used for compacting or consolidating materials, typically by repeatedly striking them.|例句：The construction worker used a rammer to compact the gravel. 建筑工人用夯锤夯实碎石。
例句：The road builder used a powerful rammer to prepare the base for the asphalt.  筑路工人用强大的夯实机为沥青铺设准备路基。|近义词：compactor, tamper, pounder |反义词：loosener, disperser
