overconsume:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːm/| vt.|过度消费，消费过度|To consume something in excess; to use or spend more of something than is necessary or desirable.|例句：The holiday season often leads people to overconsume on food and gifts. 节假日常常导致人们在食物和礼物上过度消费。|例句：Overconsuming resources is damaging the environment. 过度消耗资源正在破坏环境。|近义词：exceed, squander, waste|反义词：conserve, save, economize|动词
