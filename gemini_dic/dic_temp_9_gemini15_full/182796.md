repaves:/ˌriːˈpeɪvz/| vt.|重新铺设；修复路面|To cover (a surface, especially a road) again with paving material.|The city repaved Main Street last year.  去年市里重新铺设了主街的路面。|They repaved the driveway after the winter damage.  他们在冬季损坏后重新铺设了车道。|pave, resurface|destroy, ruin|动词
