miniaturizations:/ˌmɪnɪətʃəraɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |小型化；微型化 |The process of making something smaller.  It refers to the reduction in size of a device, system, or object while retaining its functionality.  This often involves advanced technologies and techniques. |例句：The miniaturizations of electronic components have revolutionized the mobile phone industry. 电子元件的小型化彻底改变了手机行业。
例句：Recent advances in miniaturizations have led to smaller and more powerful computers. 小型化技术的最新进展导致了更小巧、更强大的计算机。|近义词：reduction in size, downsizing, scaling down |反义词：enlargement, magnification, scaling up |名词
