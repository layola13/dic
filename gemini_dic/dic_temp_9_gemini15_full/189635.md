salivation:/ˌsælɪˈveɪʃən/| n. |唾液分泌；流口水|The secretion of saliva by the salivary glands.  Salivation is a crucial part of digestion, helping to break down food and lubricate the mouth.  |例句：The smell of food induced salivation.  闻到食物的味道引起唾液分泌。

The sight of the delicious cake caused excessive salivation in the children.  看到美味的蛋糕使孩子们流口水。|近义词：ptyalism|反义词：dry mouth|名词
