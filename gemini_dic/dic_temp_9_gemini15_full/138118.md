north-country:/ˈnɔːrθ ˌkʌntri/| adj.|北方地区的；北部乡村的|Relating to or characteristic of the northern parts of a country, especially England.  The term often evokes images of rugged landscapes, harsh weather, and a distinct dialect and culture.|例句：The north-country dialect is quite different from standard English. 北方地区的方言与标准英语大相径庭。

例句：She grew up in the bleak beauty of the north-country moors.她在北部荒原的凄美景色中长大。

近义词：northern, rural


反义词：southern, urban

词性：形容词
