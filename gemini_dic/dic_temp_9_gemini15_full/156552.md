picked:/pɪkt/| v.|挑选，采摘，拾起|to choose or select something or someone from a group; to gather or collect something, especially fruit or flowers; to lift something up.|例句：She picked the red apple from the tree. 她从树上摘下了那个红苹果。
She picked a beautiful bouquet of flowers. 她采了一束漂亮的花。|近义词：selected, chose, gathered, collected|反义词：left, discarded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He picks apples every autumn. 他每年秋天都摘苹果。
I pick my children up from school. 我去学校接我的孩子们。


一般过去时:
She picked a winning lottery ticket. 她买到了一张中奖彩票。
They picked up the trash along the beach. 他们捡起了海滩上的垃圾。


现在完成时:
I have picked all the ripe tomatoes. 我已经把所有成熟的西红柿都摘了。
She has picked up a new hobby. 她培养了一个新的爱好。


过去完成时:
He had picked the flowers before the rain started. 下雨之前他已经采好了花。
They had picked up their luggage before boarding the plane. 他们登机前已经取了行李。


将来时:
I will pick you up at the airport. 我会在机场接你。
They will pick the best candidates for the job. 他们将挑选最适合这份工作的候选人。


虚拟语气:
If I had picked a different number, I would have won. 如果我选了不同的号码，我就赢了。
I wish I had picked a better time to call. 我真希望我选了个更好的时间打电话。


被动语态:
The apples were picked by the farmer. 苹果是由农民采摘的。
The winner will be picked randomly. 获胜者将被随机挑选。


疑问句:
Have you picked a date for the wedding yet? 你选定婚礼日期了吗？
Did he pick up the phone? 他接电话了吗？


否定句:
I didn't pick up the package. 我没有取包裹。
She hasn't picked a dress yet. 她还没选好衣服。


PHRASE->
pick up: 捡起，学会，搭载
pick out: 挑选，辨认出
pick on: 欺负，挑剔
pick at: 挑剔，少量食用
hand-picked: 精挑细选的
