germ:/dʒɜːrm/| n. |细菌；病菌；微生物；萌芽；起源 |A germ is a microorganism, especially one that causes disease.  Germs can be bacteria, viruses, or fungi.  The word also refers to the beginning or origin of something. |例句：Wash your hands to avoid spreading germs. = 洗手以避免传播细菌。

The germ of an idea started to form in his mind. = 一个想法的萌芽开始在他的脑海中形成。|近义词：microbe, bacterium, virus, pathogen |反义词：sterility, cleanliness
