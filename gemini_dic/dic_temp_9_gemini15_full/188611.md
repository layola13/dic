rumblings:/ˈrʌmblɪŋz/| n.| 隆隆声；低沉的抱怨声；骚动；预兆|The low, continuous sound of something vibrating or moving.  It can also refer to low, grumbling sounds of discontent or protest, or to a sense of unease or impending trouble.|例句：We could hear the distant rumblings of thunder. 我们能听到远处雷声隆隆。

例句：There were rumblings of discontent among the workers. 工人们中出现了不满的低沉抱怨声。|近义词：murmurs, grumbles, mutterings, tremors, premonitions|反义词：silence, calm, peace, stillness


USAGE->

The volcano is rumbling. = 火山正在隆隆作响。

虚拟语气:
