greeters:/ˈɡriːtərz/| n. |迎接者，问候者 |People whose job is to welcome and assist visitors or guests.  They often provide information and guidance.|Example: The event was well-organized, with friendly greeters at every entrance.  这次活动组织得很好，每个入口处都有友好的迎宾员。|近义词：ushers, receptionists, hosts|反义词：leavers, senders


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The greeters welcome visitors warmly. = 迎宾员热情地欢迎游客。
Our greeters assist guests with their luggage. = 我们的迎宾员帮助客人搬运行李。


一般过去时:
The greeters greeted all the attendees. = 迎宾员问候了所有与会者。
The greeters helped the guests find their seats. = 迎宾员帮助客人找到座位。


现在完成时:
The greeters have welcomed hundreds of guests. = 迎宾员已经接待了数百位客人。
The greeters have provided excellent service. = 迎宾员提供了优质的服务。


过去完成时:
The greeters had already prepared the welcome packets. = 迎宾员已经准备好欢迎礼包了。
The greeters had arranged the seating before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，迎宾员已经安排好座位了。


将来时:
The greeters will be at the entrance to greet guests. = 迎宾员将在入口处迎接客人。
The greeters will guide the visitors to the registration desk. = 迎宾员将引导游客到登记处。


虚拟语气:
If we had more greeters, the event would run more smoothly. = 如果我们有更多迎宾员，活动将会进行得更顺利。
I wish we had hired more greeters for the conference. = 我希望我们为这次会议雇佣了更多迎宾员。


被动语态:
The guests were greeted by friendly greeters. = 客人们受到了友好的迎宾员的迎接。
The information was provided by the greeters at the entrance. = 入口处的迎宾员提供了信息。


疑问句:
Are the greeters ready for the event? = 迎宾员准备好迎接活动了吗？
How many greeters do we need for this event? = 这次活动我们需要多少迎宾员？


否定句:
The greeters weren't available at the time. = 当时迎宾员不在。
The greeters didn't provide enough assistance. = 迎宾员没有提供足够的帮助。
