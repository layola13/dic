obdurate:/ˈɒbdʒʊreɪt/|adj.|固执的，顽固的|stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action.|The obdurate defendant refused to plead guilty, even in the face of overwhelming evidence.  顽固的被告拒绝认罪，即使面对压倒性的证据。|近义词：stubborn, obstinate, inflexible, intractable|反义词：yielding, compliant, flexible

The child remains obdurate about eating his vegetables.  这个孩子仍然顽固地不吃蔬菜。
