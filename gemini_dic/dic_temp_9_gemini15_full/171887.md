puns:/pʌnz/| n. |双关语；俏皮话 |a joke exploiting the different possible meanings of a word or the similar sense or sound of different words.| 例句：He's a master of puns, always slipping them into conversations. 他是双关语大师，总是在谈话中穿插双关语。

例句：His jokes were full of puns, some clever, some groan-worthy. 他的笑话充满了双关语，有些很巧妙，有些则让人尴尬。


近义词：wordplay, witticism, joke


反义词：serious statement, straightforward remark


词源：源自中古英语 punne，意为“微不足道的事情”，可能与古法语 poin（点）有关，暗示着双关语依赖于词语的细微差别。
