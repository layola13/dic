nontropic:/nɑːnˈtrɒpɪk/| adj. |非热带的；温带的|Not situated in or characteristic of the tropics.  The word is formed by the prefix "non-" meaning "not" and the word "tropic," referring to the tropical regions.|Example: The nontropic climate of the region makes it unsuitable for growing certain crops.  这个地区的非热带气候使其不适合种植某些作物。|近义词：temperate, extratropical|反义词：tropical
