retractations:/ˌrɛtrækˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 撤回；收回声明；否认 | The act of retracting or withdrawing a statement, claim, or belief.  It often implies a previous assertion that is now being reversed or disavowed.  The plural form indicates multiple instances of retraction. | Example: The newspaper published several retractions after being found to have printed false information.  报纸在发现刊登了虚假信息后发表了几则更正声明。


Example:  His numerous retractions damaged his credibility. 他多次收回声明损害了他的信誉。

近义词：withdrawals, recantations, denials

反义词：affirmations, confirmations, endorsements

词性：名词
