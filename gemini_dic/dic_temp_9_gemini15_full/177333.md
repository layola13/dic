reattack:/ˌriːəˈtæk/| v.|重新攻击，再次攻击|To attack again; to make a renewed attack.|例句：The enemy reattacked at dawn.  敌人黎明时分再次发起进攻。
例句：The army decided to reattack after regrouping.  军队决定重整旗鼓后再次发起攻击。|近义词：attack again, renew the attack|反义词：retreat, surrender
