lability:/læˈbɪləti/| n. |不稳定性；易变性；变化性 |The tendency or capacity to change; instability.  It often refers to emotional instability or the instability of a physical or chemical system.  |例句：His emotional lability made it difficult to maintain a stable relationship.他的情绪不稳定使得维持稳定的关系变得困难。

例句：The lability of the chemical compound made it difficult to store.这种化合物的易变性使得储存变得困难。

近义词：instability, volatility, changeability, fickleness

反义词：stability, constancy, firmness, steadfastness

词性:名词
