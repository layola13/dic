mudskippers:/ˈmʌdˌskɪpərz/| n.|弹涂鱼|Mudskippers are amphibious fish that live in intertidal zones and are known for their ability to "skip" across mudflats using their pectoral fins. They are fascinating creatures that have adapted to survive in harsh environments.|例句：The mudskippers hopped across the mudflats in search of food.  弹涂鱼在泥滩上跳跃着寻找食物。


例句：We observed several mudskippers building burrows in the mangrove roots. 我们观察到几条弹涂鱼在红树根部筑巢。


近义词：amphibious fish, goby (部分种类)|反义词:|无
