drop-behind:/drɑːp bɪˈhaɪnd/ | adj. |落后的，滞后的 |Something that is left behind or lags behind something else in progress or development.  It often describes a situation where something or someone is falling behind schedule, progress, or a group. |例句：The project is dropping behind schedule.  项目进度落后了。|例句：He's dropping behind in his studies. 他学习落后了。|近义词：lagging, delayed, retarded |反义词：ahead, leading, advanced
