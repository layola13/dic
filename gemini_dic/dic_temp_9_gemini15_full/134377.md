noninterpretable: /ˌnɒnɪnˈtɜːprətəbl/ | adj. | 不可解释的，难以理解的 |  incapable of being interpreted or understood.  This refers to something that lacks a clear meaning or explanation, often due to complexity, ambiguity, or lack of sufficient information. | 例句：The results of the experiment were noninterpretable due to faulty equipment.  实验结果由于设备故障而无法解释。

The patient's symptoms were noninterpretable, requiring further medical investigation. 病人的症状难以解释，需要进一步的医学检查。

近义词：unintelligible, incomprehensible, inexplicable, inscrutable, enigmatic

反义词：interpretable, understandable, clear, lucid, explicit


词源:  The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "interpretable," which derives from the verb "interpret" (from Latin "interpretari," meaning "to explain," "to translate").  Therefore, "noninterpretable" literally means "not capable of being interpreted."
