madness:/ˈmædnɪs/ | n. |疯狂；疯狂的行为；愚蠢；荒谬 |The state or quality of being mad; extreme folly or unreasonableness.  It can refer to a mental illness, or to behavior that is irrational, reckless, or foolish. |例句：His actions bordered on madness. 他的行为近乎疯狂。

His relentless pursuit of power demonstrated a chilling madness. 他对权力的无情追求展现出令人不寒而栗的疯狂。


近义词：insanity, craziness, lunacy, folly, frenzy, mania |反义词：sanity, reason, sense, rationality
