demisability: /ˌdɛmɪˈzəbɪləti/ | n. | 半残疾；部分丧失能力 | The state of being partially disabled;  inability to perform some tasks or functions.  This term is not commonly used and might be considered informal or even imprecise. It suggests a condition less severe than total disability but more significant than a minor impairment. |  His demisability after the accident limited his ability to work full-time.  他事故后的部分丧失能力限制了他全职工作的能力。|  partial disability, impairment, handicap | full ability, complete functionality | 名词
