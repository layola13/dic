hotter:/ˈhɑːtər/| adj. |更热的，更辣的 |  A comparative adjective, meaning "more hot" than something else. It describes a higher degree of heat or spiciness. |例句：The sun is hotter today than yesterday. 今天太阳比昨天更热。

例句：This chili is much hotter than I expected.  这道辣椒比我预期的辣得多。


近义词：warmer, more intense, spicier |反义词：cooler, colder, milder

词性：形容词
