postisolation:/ˌpoʊstˈaɪsəleɪʃən/| n. |隔离后，隔离期之后|The period of time following a period of isolation; the state or condition of being after isolation.  It often refers to the challenges and adjustments individuals face after being isolated, such as reintegrating into society or dealing with the psychological effects of isolation.|例句：Postisolation support groups help individuals cope with the challenges of returning to normal life after quarantine. 隔离后的支持小组帮助个人应对在隔离后重返正常生活的挑战。

例句：The study examined the long-term effects of postisolation on mental health. 这项研究调查了隔离后对心理健康的长期影响。


近义词：post-quarantine, post-confinement, after isolation |反义词：isolation, quarantine, confinement|名词
