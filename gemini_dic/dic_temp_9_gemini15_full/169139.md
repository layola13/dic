projects:/ˈprɑːdʒɛkts/ /ˈprɒdʒɛkts/ | n. |计划；项目；工程；事业 |A project is a planned undertaking or endeavor with a specific goal, often requiring a series of actions and resources.  It can range from a small personal task to a large-scale enterprise.|例句：She's working on a research project. = 她正在做一个研究项目。

例句：The construction project is behind schedule. = 建设项目进度落后。


近义词：plans, schemes, undertakings, enterprises | 反义词：None,  (antonyms depend on context, e.g., if "project" means a plan, the opposite might be "lack of planning") | 词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He projects an image of confidence. = 他展现出自信的形象。
They project their feelings onto others. = 他们将自己的情感投射到他人身上。


一般过去时:
The company projected a loss for the quarter. = 公司预测本季度亏损。
He projected his voice to be heard over the noise. = 他提高嗓音以便让人听到。


现在完成时:

She has projected her ideas effectively in the presentation. = 她在演示文稿中有效地表达了自己的想法。


过去完成时:

He had projected a sense of calm despite the chaos. = 尽管一片混乱，他还是表现得很平静。


将来时:
We will project the data onto the chart. = 我们将把数据投射到图表上。
The company projects significant growth next year. = 公司预计明年将大幅增长。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
