foregoer:/fɔːrˈɡoʊər/| n. |先行者，前辈，前驱|A foregoer is a person who precedes or goes before others, often in a particular field or endeavor.  They are pioneers or forerunners, setting the stage for those who follow.  They may be influential figures who establish trends or techniques.| The foregoers of modern computing were brilliant mathematicians and engineers who laid the groundwork for today's technology.  先行者们为现代计算技术奠定了基础。|predecessor, pioneer, forerunner|successor, follower
