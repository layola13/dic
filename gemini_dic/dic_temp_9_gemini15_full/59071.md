dwindled: /ˈdwɪndld/ | v. | 减少；逐渐变小；衰落 | To gradually decrease in size, amount, or strength.  |例句：His savings dwindled to nothing. 他的积蓄逐渐减少到零。

The population of the town has dwindled over the years.  这个小镇的人口多年来一直在减少。|近义词：decrease, diminish, shrink, lessen |反义词：increase, grow, expand, augment |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His savings dwindle every month. =他的积蓄每个月都在减少。
The number of students in the class dwindles. =班上的学生人数正在减少。

一般过去时:
The crowd dwindled as the concert ended. =音乐会结束后，人群散去了。
Her energy dwindled throughout the long day. =她一整天的精力都在逐渐减少。

现在完成时:
The company's profits have dwindled significantly this year. =公司今年的利润大幅减少。
Our food supplies have dwindled since we left base camp. =自从我们离开大本营以来，我们的食物供应已经减少了。

过去完成时:
By the time we reached the summit, our water supply had dwindled to almost nothing. =当我们到达山顶时，我们的水供应几乎已经用完了。
The team's morale had dwindled after a series of defeats. =在一系列失败之后，团队的士气下降了。

将来时:
If we don't conserve water, our resources will dwindle rapidly. =如果我们不节约用水，我们的资源将会迅速减少。
The number of endangered species will dwindle further without conservation efforts. =如果没有保护措施，濒危物种的数量将会进一步减少。

虚拟语气:
If the funding had not dwindled, the project would have been completed on time. =如果资金没有减少，该项目本来可以按时完成。
I wish my patience hadn't dwindled so quickly. =我希望我的耐心不会这么快就消失。

被动语态:
The army was dwindled by disease and desertion. =军队因疾病和逃亡而人数减少。
The forest has been dwindled by illegal logging. =森林因非法伐木而减少。

疑问句:
Has the number of participants dwindled? =参与者的数量减少了吗？
Did the supply of oil dwindle during the war? =战争期间石油供应减少了吗？

否定句:
The problem didn't dwindle despite our best efforts. =尽管我们尽了最大努力，问题并没有减少。
The support for the candidate didn't dwindle; in fact, it grew. =对候选人的支持并没有减少，事实上，它反而增加了。
