prediplomacy:/ˌpriːdɪˈplɒməsi/ | n. | 预外交；外交前期活动 |  The activities and interactions that occur before formal diplomatic relations are established.  It often involves informal contacts and negotiations to lay the groundwork for future diplomatic engagement.  | Example: The prediplomacy phase involved several secret meetings between representatives of both countries.  预外交阶段包括双方代表之间多次秘密会晤。| 近义词:  protodiplomacy, preliminary negotiations | 反义词:  formal diplomacy
