self-guarded:/ˌsɛlfˈɡɑːrdɪd/ | adj. | 自我保护的，自行防护的 |  Self-guarded refers to something or someone that protects itself or is protected by its own means, without external assistance.  It implies a degree of independence and self-reliance in terms of security or defense. | Example: The self-guarded system requires minimal human intervention.  这个自我保护的系统需要最少的人工干预。|近义词：self-protected, autonomous, independent |反义词：vulnerable, unprotected, dependent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system is self-guarded against unauthorized access. = 该系统自我保护，防止未经授权的访问。
The fortress is self-guarded by its high walls and automated defenses. = 要塞依靠其高墙和自动化防御系统进行自我保护。


一般过去时:
The data was self-guarded by robust encryption. = 数据通过强大的加密技术自我保护。
The ancient city was self-guarded by its strategic location and strong fortifications. = 古城凭借其战略位置和坚固的防御工事进行自我保护。


现在完成时:
The network has been self-guarded against cyberattacks for years. = 多年来，该网络一直通过自我保护措施抵御网络攻击。
The company has self-guarded its intellectual property through stringent security protocols. = 公司通过严格的安全协议来保护其知识产权。


过去完成时:
The vault had been self-guarded by a complex series of locks and alarms. = 金库通过一系列复杂的锁和警报系统进行自我保护。
The kingdom had been self-guarded for centuries, relying on its powerful army. = 这个王国依靠其强大的军队自我保护了几个世纪。


将来时:
The new software will be self-guarded against malware. = 新软件将能够自我保护以抵御恶意软件。
The facility will be self-guarded by advanced surveillance technology. = 该设施将通过先进的监控技术进行自我保护。


虚拟语气:
If the database were self-guarded, we wouldn't need so many security personnel. = 如果数据库能够自我保护，我们就无需那么多安保人员。
It's essential that the information be self-guarded to maintain confidentiality. = 为了维护机密性，信息必须得到自我保护至关重要。


被动语态:
The building is self-guarded by motion sensors and security cameras. = 这座建筑物由运动传感器和安全摄像头进行自我保护。
The process is self-guarded against errors by a series of checks and balances. = 通过一系列的检查和制衡，该过程可以自我保护以防止错误。


疑问句:
Is the server self-guarded against hacking attempts? = 服务器能够自我保护抵御黑客攻击吗？
Are the files self-guarded using encryption? = 文件是否使用加密技术进行自我保护？


否定句:
The system is not self-guarded; it requires regular maintenance. = 该系统不能自我保护；它需要定期维护。
The old system was not self-guarded and suffered numerous security breaches. = 旧系统不能自我保护，遭受了多次安全漏洞。
