carcinological:/ˌkɑːrsɪnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/|/ˌkɑːrsɪnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.| 癌的；与癌症相关的 | Relating to the study of crustaceans (crustacean biology).  The term is sometimes mistakenly used to refer to things related to cancer (carcinogenesis), due to the similar-sounding root "carcin". However, the true etymology is distinct.  | Example:  His research focused on the carcinological aspects of the deep-sea crab population.  他的研究集中在深海螃蟹种群的甲壳动物学方面。
