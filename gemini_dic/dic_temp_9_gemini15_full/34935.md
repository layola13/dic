clobber:/ˈklɒbər/| /ˈklɑːbər/ | vt. n. |痛打；猛击；损坏；(非正式)衣服；|To hit someone or something hard; to damage or destroy something; (informal) clothes |例句：He clobbered the ball out of the park. 他把球猛击出了球场。
The storm clobbered the town. 暴风雨摧毁了小镇。


例句：He was clobbered by the other team. 他被对方队痛打了一顿。

例句：The car was badly clobbered in the accident. 汽车在事故中严重受损。


近义词：beat, thrash, batter, smash, wreck |  衣服: clothes, garments, attire


反义词：repair, mend, fix (针对损坏)


词性：动词，名词
