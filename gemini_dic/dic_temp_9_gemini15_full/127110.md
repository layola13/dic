nationalizes:/ˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/| vt.|国有化；使成为国有|To bring under state control; to transfer ownership of (a company or industry) from private to public ownership.|The government nationalized the railway system. 政府将铁路系统国有化了。|The government plans to nationalize the oil industry. 政府计划将石油工业国有化。|privatize|privatize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government nationalizes key industries. = 政府将关键产业国有化。
He nationalizes his business. = 他将他的生意国有化。

一般过去时:
The country nationalized its banks in the 1970s. = 这个国家在20世纪70年代将银行国有化。
They nationalized the airline last year. = 他们去年将航空公司国有化了。

现在完成时:
Many countries have nationalized their utilities. = 许多国家已经将它们的公用事业国有化了。
The government has recently nationalized the telecommunications company. = 政府最近将电信公司国有化了。

过去完成时:
Before the war, they had nationalized all major industries. = 在战争爆发前，他们已经将所有主要产业国有化了。
The party had nationalized the railroads long before the revolution. = 在革命之前很久，这个政党就已经将铁路国有化了。

将来时:
The new government will nationalize the mining sector. = 新政府将把采矿业国有化。
They will probably nationalize the energy companies next year. = 他们很可能明年将能源公司国有化。

虚拟语气:
If the government nationalized the banks, it might stabilize the economy. = 如果政府将银行国有化，它可能会稳定经济。
I wish the government would nationalize the healthcare system. = 我希望政府能够将医疗体系国有化。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
