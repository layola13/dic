mastodynia:/ˌmæstoʊˈdɪniə/| n. |乳痛；乳房痛|Pain in the breast.  The pain can range from mild discomfort to severe, sharp pain. It can be caused by various factors, including hormonal changes, infections, injuries, and underlying medical conditions.  |Example:  She experienced severe mastodynia during her menstrual cycle. 她月经期间经历了严重的乳痛。

Example: The doctor diagnosed her mastodynia as being caused by a benign cyst. 医生诊断她的乳房痛是由良性囊肿引起的。|近义词：breast pain, mammodynia |反义词：无
