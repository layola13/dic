dominantly:/ˈdɒmɪnəntli/ /ˈdɑːmɪnəntli/| adv. |主要地；支配地；显著地 |In a way that is dominant; primarily or chiefly.  The adverb form of "dominant." It describes something that is most important or influential, or that stands out prominently. |例句：He dominantly influenced the design of the building. 他主要影响了建筑的设计。

例句：Her personality dominantly shaped the group's dynamics. 她的个性主要塑造了团队的动态。


近义词：primarily, chiefly, mainly, principally, predominantly


反义词：subordinately, minimally, insignificantly


词性: 副词
