mousquetaire:/ˌmuskəˈtɛər/| n.|火枪手，（尤指）法国国王路易十三时期国王卫队队员|A musketeer is a soldier armed with a musket, especially a member of the King's Guard in the reign of Louis XIII of France.  The term evokes a romantic image of bravery, chivalry, and adventure, often associated with historical fiction.|例句：D'Artagnan was a brave and dashing musketeer. 达达尼昂是一位勇敢而潇洒的火枪手。|例句：The novel depicts the lives of four musketeers serving the king. 这部小说描述了四个为国王效力的火枪手的生命。|近义词：soldier, guardsman|反义词：civilian
