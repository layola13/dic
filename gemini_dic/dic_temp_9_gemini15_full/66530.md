exhaustingly: /ɪɡˈzɔːstɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人筋疲力尽地；极其费力地 |  In a way that causes extreme tiredness; extremely laboriously. |

例句：

They worked exhaustingly to finish the project on time.  = 他们为了按时完成项目而极其努力地工作。

She described the journey as exhaustingly long and arduous. = 她形容这次旅程极其漫长而艰辛。


近义词：tiringly, wearisomely, laboriously, strenuously

反义词：easily, effortlessly, readily


词源:

exhaust (v.) + -ingly (adv. suffix)

exhaust (v.) 源自于古法语 *exhauster,  来自拉丁语 exhaurire "to draw out, drain, empty,"  来自 ex- "out" + haurire "to draw."
