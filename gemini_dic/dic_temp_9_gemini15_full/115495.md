manuring:/məˈnʊərɪŋ/| n. |施肥；施用肥料 |The act of applying manure to land as fertilizer.  Manuring is crucial for maintaining soil fertility. |近义词：fertilizing, dunging |反义词：depleting (soil nutrients)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer is manuring the fields. = 农民正在给田地施肥。
They manure the garden regularly. = 他们定期给花园施肥。


一般过去时:
He manured the crops last week. = 他上周给庄稼施了肥。
We manured the vegetable patch yesterday. = 我们昨天给菜地施了肥。


现在完成时:
They have manured the orchard this year. = 他们今年已经给果园施了肥。
The gardener has manured all the flower beds. = 园丁已经给所有的花坛施了肥。


过去完成时:
They had manured the land before planting the seeds. = 他们在播种前已经给土地施了肥。
The farmer had manured the field before the rain came. = 暴雨来临之前，农民已经给田地施了肥。


将来时:
We will manure the lawn next spring. = 我们明年春天将给草坪施肥。
They will manure the fields after the harvest. = 收获后他们将给田地施肥。


虚拟语气:
If we manured the soil properly, we would have a better harvest. = 如果我们正确地给土壤施肥，我们将会有更好的收成。
I wish I had manured the garden earlier. = 我真希望我早点给花园施肥。


被动语态:
The fields are manured twice a year. = 田地一年施肥两次。
The soil was manured before planting. = 种植前土壤已施肥。


疑问句:
Are they manuring the fields today? = 他们今天在给田地施肥吗？
Have you manured the garden yet? = 你已经给花园施肥了吗？


否定句:
We didn't manure the garden this year. = 我们今年没有给花园施肥。
They haven't manured the fields yet. = 他们还没有给田地施肥。
