preenumerate: /ˌpriːɪˈnjuːməreɪt/ | vt. | 预先列举；预先枚举 | To list or mention (something) beforehand.  This is a relatively rare word, formed by combining "pre-" (before) and "enumerate" (to list). It implies a listing or counting that happens before something else. | The professor preenumerated the key topics for the upcoming lecture. 教授预先列举了即将进行的讲座中的关键主题。 | 近义词：list beforehand, specify in advance, detail previously | 反义词：omit, overlook | 动词
