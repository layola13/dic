hot-doggers:/ˈhɑːtˌdɒɡərz/ | n. | 热狗贩卖者；（尤指）炫技者，吹牛者 | A person who sells hot dogs; someone who shows off their skills (often boastfully).  | Example:  He's a real hot-dogger on the skateboard, showing off all his tricks. 他在滑板上真是个炫技者，炫耀着他所有的技巧。|近义词：show-off, braggart, boaster | 反义词：humble, modest

USAGE->

He sells hot dogs at the park every day. = 他每天都在公园卖热狗。
Those hot-doggers always show off their skills. = 那些炫技者总是炫耀他们的技能。
