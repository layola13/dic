coteau:/koʊˈtoʊ/| n. |小山，缓坡|A coteau is a gently sloping hill or ridge, especially one found in the prairies of North America.  It's often characterized by its relatively smooth, gradual incline. |Example: The prairie stretched out before us, punctuated by numerous low coteaus.  大草原展现在我们面前，点缀着许多低矮的小山。|近义词：hill, rise, elevation, ridge |反义词：valley, depression, hollow
