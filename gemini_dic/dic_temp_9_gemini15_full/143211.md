outcropped:/ˈaʊtkrɒpt/| v.|露头；出露；显露|To appear on the surface of the earth, as a rock formation.  The verb implies a previously hidden or buried element now becoming visible.|The ancient bedrock outcropped along the riverbank.  古老的基岩在河岸边露头。|The newly paved road outcropped from beneath the snow. 新铺好的路从雪下露了出来。|appear, emerge, surface|submerged, hidden, concealed|动词
