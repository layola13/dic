rubian:/ˈruːbiən/| n.|茜素，茜草根|A red dye obtained from the madder plant, historically important for dyeing textiles.  It is also sometimes used as a synonym for madder itself.|例句：The ancient Egyptians used rubian to dye their fabrics a vibrant red.  古代埃及人用茜素染他们的织物成鲜艳的红色。|近义词：madder, alizarin|反义词:None.
