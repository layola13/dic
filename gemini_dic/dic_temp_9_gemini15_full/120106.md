mimetically:/ˌmɪˈmɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 模仿地，模拟地 |  In a mimetic manner; by imitation.  It describes actions or processes that are characterized by imitation or mimicry. | Example: The child learned to speak mimetically, repeating the words of her parents.  孩子通过模仿父母说话的方式学习说话。 | 近义词：imitative, simulatively | 反义词：originally, innovatively
