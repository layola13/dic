cross-bearer:/ˈkrɒsˌbɛərər/|/ˈkrɔːsˌbɛrər/| n. |背十字架的人；承担重任的人|A cross-bearer is a person who carries a cross, especially in a religious procession.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe someone who bears a heavy burden or responsibility.|例句：In the Easter procession, the cross-bearer led the way.  复活节游行中，背十字架的人走在前面。

The young entrepreneur is a cross-bearer for his family's financial future.  这位年轻的企业家承担着家族未来经济的重任。|近义词：burden-bearer, standard-bearer (in a figurative sense)|反义词：idler, shirker
