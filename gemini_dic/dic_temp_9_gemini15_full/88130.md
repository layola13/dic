hikers:/ˈhaɪkərz/| n. |徒步旅行者，远足者 |People who go hiking.  Hikers often enjoy the outdoors and challenging themselves physically. |例句：The hikers reached the summit at sunset.  徒步旅行者在日落时到达山顶。
例句：Experienced hikers know how to prepare for different weather conditions.  经验丰富的徒步旅行者知道如何为不同的天气状况做好准备。|近义词：walkers, trekkers, ramblers |反义词：sedentary people (久坐不动的人)
