healingly:/ˈhiːlɪŋli/| adv.| 治愈地，有益健康地 |  In a way that heals or promotes healing; in a soothing or restorative manner.  It describes something that has a healing effect or quality. |例句：The warm sun shone healingly on her sunburnt skin.  温暖的阳光治愈性地照在她晒伤的皮肤上。
The music played healingly, calming her troubled mind. 音乐治愈般地演奏着，使她平静不安的心绪。|近义词：soothingly, therapeutically, restoratively |反义词：harmfully, destructively, damagingly
