epistlers:/ɪˈpɪslərz/| n.|书信作者；使徒；书信|Writers of letters, especially the authors of the epistles in the New Testament.  The term often refers to the apostles Paul, Peter, James, and Jude, whose letters are included in the Bible.|例句：The epistles of Paul are studied by theologians worldwide. 保罗的书信在全世界都被神学家们研究。|例句：The epistles offer valuable insight into early Christianity.  这些书信为早期基督教提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：letter writers, authors, correspondents|反义词：recipients, addressees
