/ˈsaɪmə/ | n. |装饰线条；波浪线|A cyma is an architectural molding with a double curve, concave at one end and convex at the other.  It can be either cyma recta (convex then concave) or cyma reversa (concave then convex).  Cymas are often used as decorative elements in classical architecture and design.|The building's cornice featured an elegant cyma recta molding.|近义词：molding, cornice|反义词：straight line|名词
