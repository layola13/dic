leggins:/ˈlɛɡɪŋz/| n.|紧身裤，骑马裤|Tights that fit closely to the legs, extending from the waist to the ankles. They are often made of stretchy material like Lycra or spandex.|例句：She wore black leggings and a long tunic. 她穿着黑色紧身裤和一件长衫。|例句：Leggings are a popular choice of clothing for exercising. 紧身裤是运动服装的流行选择。|近义词：tights, yoga pants|反义词：wide-leg pants
