/ˌaʊtˈpiːpəl/| v. |超过人口数量；人数超过；超过…的人口|To surpass in population; to exceed in number of people.  The city's population outpeoples the surrounding countryside.  这个城市的人口超过了周围农村地区的人口。|近义词：outnumber, surpass, exceed |反义词：be outnumbered by, be less than


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city outpeoples the surrounding towns. = 这个城市的人口超过了周围城镇。
This country outpeoples its neighbor to the west. = 这个国家的人口比它西边的邻国多。


一般过去时:
The small village was outpeopled by the growing city. = 发展中的城市人口超过了这个小村庄。
Last year, this region outpeopled the previous record. = 去年，这个地区的人口超过了之前的记录。


现在完成时:
The coastal region has outpeopled the inland areas. = 沿海地区的人口已经超过了内陆地区。
This province has outpeopled its neighbor for many years. = 这个省份多年来人口一直超过它的邻省。


过去完成时:
By 1950, the capital had outpeopled all other cities in the country. = 到1950年，首都的人口已经超过了全国其他所有城市。
Before the industrial revolution, the countryside had outpeopled the cities. = 在工业革命之前，农村人口超过城市人口。


将来时:
Experts predict that this city will outpeople the current largest city in the nation. = 专家预测，这个城市的人口将超过目前全国最大的城市。
Demographers believe that this region will outpeople its neighboring regions by 2050. = 人口统计学家认为，到2050年，该地区的人口将超过其周边地区。


虚拟语气:
If the birth rate continued to increase, the city would outpeople the entire state. = 如果出生率继续增加，这个城市的人口将超过整个州。
If only the government had invested more in rural areas, the situation might not have outpeopled the cities. = 要是政府在农村地区多投资一些，情况可能就不会出现城市人口超过农村人口的局面。


被动语态:
The smaller town was outpeopled by the larger metropolis. = 小镇的人口被大都市超过了。
The rural areas were outpeopled by the urban centers for decades. = 几十年来，农村地区的人口一直被城市中心超过。


疑问句:
Does this state outpeople its neighboring states? = 这个州的人口是否超过其邻州？
Will this city outpeople its rival city in the future? = 未来这个城市的人口会超过其竞争对手城市吗？


否定句:
The village did not outpeople the nearby town. = 这个村庄的人口没有超过附近的城镇。
This region has not outpeopled its neighboring region in recent years. = 近年来，这个地区的人口没有超过其邻近地区。
