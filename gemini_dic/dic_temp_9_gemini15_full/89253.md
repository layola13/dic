hominid:/ˈhɒmɪnɪd/ /ˈhɑːmənɪd/| n. |人科动物；人类（及其祖先）|A hominid is a member of the family Hominidae, which includes humans and their extinct bipedal ancestors.  Hominids are characterized by their upright posture and large brains relative to body size.|  The discovery of this fossilized skull provides valuable insights into early hominid evolution.  这个化石头盖骨的发现为了解早期人科动物的进化提供了宝贵的资料。| primate, ape |  none |  名词
