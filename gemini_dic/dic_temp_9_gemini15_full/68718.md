farina:/fəˈriːnə/| n. |细面粉，粗粒小麦粉|Fine flour or meal, especially that made from wheat.  It is often used in baking and cooking. |例句：The recipe calls for two cups of farina. 这道菜谱需要两杯细面粉。

例句：He made a porridge using farina and milk. 他用细面粉和牛奶做了粥。


近义词：flour, meal|反义词：None specifically, but  "coarse grain" could be considered an antonym in a comparative sense.


词源: From Latin *farina, meaning "flour".  This ultimately derives from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂- "to grind".
