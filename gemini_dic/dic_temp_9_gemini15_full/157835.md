plagiarizing: /ˈpleɪdʒəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |剽窃；抄袭 | The act of using another person's words or ideas without giving them credit.  It is a serious academic offense and can have significant consequences. | He was expelled from school for plagiarizing his essay. 他因剽窃论文而被学校开除。 | copying, appropriating, stealing |  originality, honesty | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plagiarizes frequently. = 他经常剽窃。
She plagiarizes from online sources. = 她从网上抄袭资料。

一般过去时:
He plagiarized his term paper. = 他剽窃了他的学期论文。
She plagiarized a significant portion of her thesis. = 她剽窃了她论文中很大一部分内容。

现在完成时:
He has plagiarized several times this semester. = 本学期他已经剽窃过几次了。
She has never plagiarized before. = 她以前从未剽窃过。

过去完成时:
He had plagiarized the poem before submitting it. = 他在提交诗歌之前就已经剽窃了它。
She had plagiarized parts of her report, leading to its rejection. = 她剽窃了她报告的部分内容，导致报告被拒绝。

将来时:
He will plagiarize again if he doesn't change his habits. = 如果他不改变习惯，他会再次剽窃。
She will likely plagiarize if she doesn't have enough time to complete the assignment. = 如果她没有足够的时间完成作业，她可能会剽窃。

虚拟语气:
If he had not plagiarized, he would have passed the course. = 如果他没有剽窃，他就会通过这门课程。
I wish I hadn't plagiarized that essay. = 我真希望我没有剽窃那篇论文。

被动语态:
His essay was plagiarized from a well-known website. = 他的论文是从一个知名网站上剽窃的。
The student's work was found to have been plagiarized. = 发现学生的作业是剽窃的。

疑问句:

Has she ever been caught plagiarizing? = 她曾经被抓到剽窃过吗？

否定句:
He did not plagiarize the article. = 他没有剽窃那篇文章。
She denies plagiarizing the content. = 她否认剽窃了内容。
