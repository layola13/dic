shortlisting: /ˈʃɔːrtlɪstɪŋ/ | n. | 筛选；入围名单 | The process of selecting a smaller number of candidates or applicants from a larger group for further consideration.  It often involves reviewing applications, resumes, or other materials to identify the most promising individuals. |  The company is currently shortlisting candidates for the open position. 公司目前正在筛选空缺职位的候选人。

The shortlisting process was rigorous and involved several rounds of interviews. 筛选过程非常严格，包括几轮面试。|  selection, narrowing down |  rejection, elimination |  名词
