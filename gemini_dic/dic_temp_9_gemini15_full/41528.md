coronate:/ˈkɒrəneɪt/| /ˈkɔːrəneɪt/ | vt. |加冕；戴冠于；使加冕|To place a crown on someone's head, thereby making them a monarch or other person of high rank.  The act of crowning someone. |例句：The prince was coronated king after his father's death.  王子在他父亲去世后加冕为王。

例句：They coronated her queen in a grand ceremony. 他们在盛大的仪式上为她加冕为王后。


近义词：crown, invest


反义词：depose, dethrone
