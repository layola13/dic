myob:/ˈmaɪɒb/|/ˈmaɪɑːb/| n.|少管闲事；自己的事；（非正式）|Mind your own business.  It's an informal expression urging someone to not interfere in other people's affairs.  It's often used as a response to unwanted or intrusive questions or comments.|例句：Just MYOB! It's none of your business. = 少管闲事！这不关你的事。

例句：Mind your own business!  This is MYOB. = 少管闲事！这是你的事。|近义词：none of your business, butt out, stay out of it|反义词：intrude, meddle
