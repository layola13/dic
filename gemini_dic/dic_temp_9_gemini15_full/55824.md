divide:/dɪˈvaɪd/ | vt. n. | 分配，分开，划分 | To separate or be separated into parts.  | The cake was divided equally among the children. 蛋糕被平均分给孩子们。  The river divides the city into two parts. 这条河将城市分成两部分。 |  separate, split, share, apportion | unite, combine, merge | v. n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher divides the class into four groups. = 老师把班级分成四个小组。
She divides her time between work and family. = 她把时间分配在工作和家庭之间。

一般过去时:
They divided the land among their sons. = 他们把土地分给了儿子们。
We divided the task into smaller parts. = 我们把任务分成更小的部分。

现在完成时:
The scientists have divided the research project into several phases. = 科学家们已将研究项目分成了几个阶段。
She has divided her inheritance among her children. = 她已将遗产分给了她的孩子们。

过去完成时:
They had already divided the spoils before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经分好了战利品。
The committee had divided on the issue before the vote. = 委员会在投票前就已经就这个问题产生了分歧。

将来时:
We will divide the profits equally. = 我们将平均分配利润。
The company will divide into two separate entities. = 公司将分裂成两个独立的实体。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would divide the work more effectively. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更有效地分配工作。
I wish they had divided the resources more fairly. = 我希望他们能更公平地分配资源。

被动语态:
The cake was divided into six slices. = 蛋糕被切成了六块。
The work will be divided among the team members. = 工作将分配给团队成员。

疑问句:
How will you divide the responsibilities? = 你将如何分配责任？
How many parts is the project divided into? = 项目分成几部分？

否定句:
They did not divide the work evenly. = 他们没有平均分配工作。
The country could not be divided without bloodshed. = 国家不能不流血就分裂。
