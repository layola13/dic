looked-over:/ˌlʊktˈoʊvər/ | adj. | 被仔细检查过的；被忽略的；粗略检查过的 |  examined casually or superficially; neglected or overlooked. |


例句：

The proposal was looked-over by the committee, and they found several significant flaws.  =  委员会粗略检查了该提案，并发现了一些重大缺陷。


The project was looked-over due to lack of funding. =  由于缺乏资金，该项目被搁置了。


近义词：inspected, reviewed, surveyed, neglected, ignored


反义词：scrutinized, thoroughly examined


词性：形容词
