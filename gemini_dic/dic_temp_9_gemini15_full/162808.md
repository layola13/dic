prebuying:/ˌpriːˈbaɪɪŋ/| n.|预购，提前购买|The act of purchasing something before it is officially released or available.  It often involves making a payment in advance to secure the item.|例句：Prebuying concert tickets is a good way to guarantee your spot. 预购演唱会门票是保证你座位的好方法。

例句：Many fans engaged in prebuying the limited edition sneakers. 许多粉丝参与了限量版运动鞋的预购。


近义词：advance purchase, advance booking, pre-order


反义词：post-purchase, late purchase


词性:名词
