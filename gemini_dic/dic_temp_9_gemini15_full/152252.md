patronised: /ˈpætrənaɪzd/ | vt. | 惠顾；赞助；以高人一等的态度对待 |

英文解析：To patronize can mean to be a regular customer of a business, to support financially, or to treat someone in a condescending manner, implying superiority.  The meaning depends heavily on context.

例句：

He patronised the local bakery every Saturday. = 他每个星期六都惠顾当地的面包店。

The arts council patronised his latest project. = 艺术委员会赞助了他的最新项目。

She patronised him, speaking slowly and clearly as if he were a child. = 她以高人一等的态度对待他，说话缓慢清晰，好像他是个孩子一样。


近义词：support, sponsor, condescend, talk down to

反义词：ignore, neglect, respect, treat equally


词性:动词
