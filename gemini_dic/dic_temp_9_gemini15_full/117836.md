melliloquent:/mɛlˈɪləkwənt/ | adj. | 蜂蜜般甜美流畅的；甜言蜜语的 |  Characterized by a honeyed, smooth, and often insincere fluency of speech; honey-tongued.  It suggests a sweetness that might be deceptive or manipulative. | Example: Her melliloquent promises proved to be empty words. 她甜言蜜语的承诺证明是空话。 | 近义词：honeyed, smooth-talking, silver-tongued | 反义词：abrasive, blunt, harsh
