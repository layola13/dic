overindex:/ˌoʊvərˈɪndɛks/ | v. | 超比例代表；过度集中 | To be represented or present at a rate higher than expected or average.  This often refers to a demographic group's disproportionate presence in a particular activity, market, or characteristic. | 例句：Young adults overindex in social media usage. 年轻人在社交媒体的使用比例高于平均水平。


例句：The company's marketing campaign overindexed among women aged 25-34. 公司的营销活动在25-34岁的女性群体中取得了超预期的效果。


近义词：disproportionately represent, exceed expectations, be overrepresented


反义词：underindex, underrepresent
