octettes:/ɒkˈtɛts/| n. |八重奏；八行诗节|A group of eight lines of verse, especially one forming a stanza.  Also refers to a musical composition for eight voices or instruments.|Example: The composer wrote a beautiful octet for strings.  作曲家为弦乐器写了一首优美的八重奏。 The poem's final octet resolved the narrative beautifully. 这首诗的最后八行诗节完美地解决了叙述问题。|近义词：octet (近义词，但octet更常用)|反义词：N/A|名词
