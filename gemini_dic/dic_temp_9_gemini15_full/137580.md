nontransposing: /ˌnɒntrænzˈpoʊzɪŋ/ | adj. |不移调的，不改变音高的| Not involving or causing transposition;  maintaining the original pitch or key.  This term is primarily used in music theory and related fields.


例句：

The nontransposing instrument played the melody in its original key. = 不移调乐器以其原调演奏旋律。

The piece was written for a nontransposing instrument, so the score accurately reflects the pitch. = 这首曲子是为不移调乐器而作的，因此乐谱准确地反映了音高。


近义词：untransposed, original-pitch

反义词：transposing


词性：形容词
