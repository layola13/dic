omnipresent:/ˌɒmnɪˈprezənt/ /ˌɑːmnɪˈprɛzənt/ | adj. |无所不在的，普遍存在的 | Existing or being everywhere at the same time; ubiquitous.  |例句：The omnipresent threat of war hung over the nation.  战争的无处不在的威胁笼罩着全国。
例句：The internet has become an omnipresent force in modern life. 互联网已成为现代生活中无处不在的力量。|近义词：ubiquitous, pervasive, universal |反义词：absent, nonexistent, rare


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The influence of social media is omnipresent in our daily lives. = 社交媒体的影响在我们日常生活中无处不在。
God is omnipresent, according to many religions. = 许多宗教认为上帝无所不在。


一般过去时:
The fear of the unknown was omnipresent during the dark ages. = 在黑暗时代，对未知的恐惧无处不在。
The feeling of anxiety was omnipresent before the exam. = 考试前焦虑感无处不在。


现在完成时:
Technology has become omnipresent in the modern world. = 技术已在现代世界中变得无处不在。
The effects of climate change have become increasingly omnipresent. = 气候变化的影响越来越普遍。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of the internet, information wasn't as omnipresent. = 在互联网发明之前，信息并非如此普遍。
The feeling of oppression had been omnipresent throughout their childhood. = 压抑感贯穿了他们的童年。


将来时:
Artificial intelligence will likely become increasingly omnipresent in the future. = 人工智能未来很可能会越来越普遍。
Automation will become omnipresent in many industries. = 自动化将在许多行业中变得无处不在。


虚拟语气:
If technology were omnipresent, life would be very different. = 如果技术无处不在，生活将会大相径庭。
If love were omnipresent, there would be no suffering. = 如果爱无处不在，就不会有痛苦。


被动语态:
The problem of pollution is considered omnipresent. = 污染问题被认为是无处不在的。
The feeling of being watched was omnipresently felt. = 被监视的感觉无处不在。


疑问句:
Is the influence of technology truly omnipresent? = 技术的影响真的无处不在吗？
Is happiness omnipresent, or is it elusive? = 幸福是无处不在的，还是难以捉摸的？


否定句:
The feeling of peace was not omnipresent in that chaotic environment. = 在那个混乱的环境中，平静的感觉并非无处不在。
Their happiness was not omnipresent, but sporadic. = 他们的幸福并非无处不在，而是零星的。
