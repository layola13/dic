returning:/rɪˈtɜːrnɪŋ/ | v. | 返回；归还；回复 |  Returning to the topic, let's discuss the budget. 回到主题，让我们讨论一下预算。  The book is overdue; please return it to the library. 这本书过期了；请把它还给图书馆。 | coming back, reverting | leaving, departing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is returning home. = 他正在回家。
She returns the calls promptly. = 她迅速地回电话。


一般过去时:
They returned from their vacation last week. = 他们上周从度假回来了。
He returned the borrowed money. = 他还了借来的钱。


现在完成时:
I have returned the book to the library. = 我已经把书还给图书馆了。
She has returned to her hometown after many years. = 许多年后，她回到了家乡。


过去完成时:
He had returned the car before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经把车还了。
They had returned home before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经回家了。


将来时:
We will return to this issue later. = 我们稍后再讨论这个问题。
She will be returning from Paris next week. = 她下周将从巴黎回来。


虚拟语气:
If I were returning to my hometown, I would visit my old friends. = 如果我回到家乡，我会去看望我的老朋友。
I wish I were returning to school. = 我希望我能够回到学校。


被动语态:
The borrowed items must be returned. = 借来的东西必须归还。
The call was returned immediately. = 电话立即回了过去。


疑问句:
Are you returning to work tomorrow? = 你明天要上班吗？
When is he returning? = 他什么时候回来？


否定句:
He is not returning my calls. = 他没有回我的电话。
She did not return the book on time. = 她没有按时还书。


PHRASE->
