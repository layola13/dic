clingy:/ˈklɪŋi/| adj.|黏人的，依恋的，难以摆脱的|  Describes a person who is excessively dependent on the attention, affection, or approval of others.  It often implies a need for constant reassurance and close physical contact.  The term can be used to describe both children and adults. |例句：The child was clingy and wouldn't let go of her mother's hand.  这个孩子很黏人，不肯放开她母亲的手。

The puppy was so clingy that it followed me everywhere. 这只小狗非常黏人，走到哪里都跟着我。|近义词：dependent, possessive, attached, needy |反义词：independent, detached, self-reliant
