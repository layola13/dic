nonthermally:/ˌnɑːnˈθɜːrməli/| adj. |非热力地，非热地|Not involving or produced by heat.  This adjective describes processes or effects that occur without the involvement of heat.  For instance, nonthermal plasma processing uses plasma at low temperatures to modify materials.|Example:  Nonthermal plasma etching is a promising technique in semiconductor manufacturing. 非热等离子体刻蚀是半导体制造中一种很有前景的技术。  The experiment demonstrated a nonthermal method of generating electricity.  实验演示了一种非热力发电方法。|近义词：athermal, non-heat-producing|反义词：thermally
