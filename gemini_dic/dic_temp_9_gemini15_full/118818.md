metamorphosing:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəʊzɪŋ/| v.|变形；蜕变；转化|The process of undergoing a metamorphosis; changing in form or nature.|例句：The caterpillar is metamorphosing into a butterfly. 毛毛虫正在变成蝴蝶。 The company is metamorphosing into a tech giant. 这家公司正在蜕变成科技巨头。|近义词：transforming, changing, evolving|反义词：remaining the same, stagnating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The caterpillar is metamorphosing. = 毛毛虫正在蜕变。
The city is metamorphosing rapidly. = 城市正在迅速变化。

一般过去时:
The tadpole metamorphosed into a frog. = 蝌蚪变成了青蛙。
The landscape metamorphosed after the wildfire. = 野火过后，地貌发生了变化。

现在完成时:
The artist has metamorphosed his style. = 艺术家已经改变了他的风格。
The country has metamorphosed since the revolution. = 自从革命以来，这个国家已经发生了翻天覆地的变化。

过去完成时:
The larva had metamorphosed before I observed it. = 我观察到它之前，幼虫已经完成了变态。
The society had metamorphosed significantly by the 20th century. = 到20世纪，社会已经发生了巨大的变化。

将来时:
The pupa will metamorphose into a moth. = 蛹将变成飞蛾。
The town will metamorphose into a bustling metropolis. = 这个小镇将变成一个繁华的大都市。

虚拟语气:
If the frog had not metamorphosed, it would still be a tadpole. = 如果青蛙没有变态，它仍然会是蝌蚪。
I wish my understanding of this topic had metamorphosed sooner. = 我希望我对这个主题的理解能早点改变。

被动语态:
The rock was metamorphosed by intense heat and pressure. = 岩石在高温高压下发生了变质。
His ideas were metamorphosed by his experiences. = 他的思想因他的经历而改变。

疑问句:
Is the insect metamorphosing? = 昆虫正在蜕变吗？
How is the company metamorphosing its business model? = 公司如何改变其商业模式？

否定句:
The material did not metamorphose under normal conditions. = 在正常条件下，这种材料不会发生变质。
The plan did not metamorphose despite numerous revisions. = 尽管经过多次修改，计划并没有改变。
