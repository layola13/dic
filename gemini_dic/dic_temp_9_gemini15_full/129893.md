non-ionic: /ˌnɒn aɪˈɒnɪk/ | adj. |非离子性的|Not carrying an electric charge; not forming ions in solution.  This term describes substances that do not dissociate into ions when dissolved in water or other solvents.  They remain as neutral molecules.  |例句：Many detergents are non-ionic, making them suitable for sensitive skin.  许多清洁剂是非离子性的，这使得它们适合敏感肌肤。|例句：Non-ionic surfactants are commonly used in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. 非离子表面活性剂通常用于化妆品和药品。|近义词：uncharged, neutral |反义词：ionic
