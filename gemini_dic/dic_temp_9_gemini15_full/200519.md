slap-dash: /ˈslæpˌdæʃ/ | adj. | 粗心大意的；草率的；马虎的 |  characterized by a lack of care or attention to detail; hasty and careless.  | 例句：The slap-dash renovation left the house looking worse than before.  粗心的装修使房子看起来比以前更糟了。


例句：He completed the assignment in a slap-dash manner, resulting in numerous errors. 他草率地完成了作业，导致许多错误。


近义词：hasty, careless, cursory, perfunctory, slipshod


反义词：meticulous, careful, thorough, painstaking, diligent


词性：形容词
