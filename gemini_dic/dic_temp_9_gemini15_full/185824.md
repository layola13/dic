revitalising: /ˌriːˈvaɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 使恢复活力的；使新生；复兴的 |  Giving new life, energy, or vigor to something.  The process of revitalizing something often involves renewing or restoring it to a former state of health or activity. |

例句：
The government's new policies are aimed at revitalising the economy. = 政府的新政策旨在振兴经济。

The revitalising effects of the spa treatment were immediate. = 水疗的恢复活力效果是立竿见影的。


近义词：rejuvenating, renewing, refreshing, invigorating, regenerating

反义词：debilitating, weakening, draining, exhausting
