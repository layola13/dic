defraud:/dɪˈfrɔːd/| vt.|欺诈；诈骗|To deprive someone of something by deceit, trickery, or other dishonest means.  This typically involves illegally obtaining money or property.|例句：He defrauded the company of thousands of dollars. 他欺诈公司数千美元。|例句：The accountant was found guilty of defrauding his clients. 会计师被发现犯有欺诈客户的罪行。|近义词：cheat, swindle, fleece, trick|反义词：benefit, help


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defrauds his clients regularly. = 他经常欺诈他的客户。
The company defrauds customers through misleading advertising. = 公司通过误导性广告欺骗顾客。

一般过去时:
They defrauded the bank of a large sum of money. = 他们诈骗银行一大笔钱。
The businessman was jailed for defrauding investors. = 那位商人因诈骗投资者而入狱。

现在完成时:
He has defrauded numerous people over the years. = 多年来，他已经欺诈了无数人。
The organization has been defrauded of millions of dollars. = 该组织已被诈骗数百万美元。

过去完成时:
They had defrauded the insurance company before they were caught. = 他们在被抓之前就已诈骗了保险公司。
The investors realized they had been defrauded after the company went bankrupt. = 公司破产后，投资者才意识到自己被骗了。

将来时:
They will defraud more people if they are not stopped. = 如果不阻止他们，他们将欺骗更多的人。
The con artist plans to defraud the wealthy businessman. = 骗子计划诈骗那位富有的商人。

虚拟语气:
If he had defrauded the bank, he would be in jail now. = 如果他诈骗了银行，他现在就会在监狱里。
I wish I hadn't been defrauded by that investment scheme. = 我真希望我没有被那个投资计划骗了。

被动语态:
The company was defrauded by a sophisticated scam. = 公司被一个复杂的骗局欺诈了。
Many people were defrauded in the Ponzi scheme. = 许多人在庞氏骗局中被欺诈。

疑问句:
Was the company defrauded of its assets? = 公司的资产是否被诈骗了？
How were the investors defrauded? = 投资者是如何被欺诈的？

否定句:
He did not defraud the government. = 他没有诈骗政府。
She was not defrauded in the transaction. = 她在这笔交易中没有被欺诈。
