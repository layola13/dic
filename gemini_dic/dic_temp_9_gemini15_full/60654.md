elections:/ɪˈlɛkʃənz/ | n. |选举，投票 |The process of selecting a person or group of people for a position, usually by voting. Elections are fundamental to democratic systems, allowing citizens to choose their leaders and representatives.  |Example: The upcoming elections are generating a lot of excitement.  即将到来的选举激起了很多兴奋。
The general elections were held peacefully. 大选和平举行。|近义词：ballots, votes, polls |反义词：appointments (in non-democratic contexts)
