hence:/hens/| adv.|因此，所以|Therefore; consequently; from this; for this reason.|The evidence is strong; hence, the jury's decision.|近义词：therefore, thus, consequently, accordingly|反义词：nevertheless, however


USAGE->

一般现在时:
It is raining; hence, I will stay home. = 正在下雨；因此，我将待在家里。
The sun is shining; hence, it is a beautiful day. = 太阳正在照耀；因此，这是一个美丽的日子。


一般过去时:
He was tired; hence, he went to bed early. = 他很累；因此，他早早睡了。
The road was blocked; hence, we took a detour. = 道路被封锁了；因此，我们绕道而行。


现在完成时:
She has finished her work; hence, she can relax. = 她已经完成了她的工作；因此，她可以放松一下了。
They have won the game; hence, they are celebrating. = 他们赢得了比赛；因此，他们正在庆祝。


过去完成时:
He had missed the train; hence, he was late for the meeting. = 他错过了火车；因此，他开会迟到了。
The storm had passed; hence, the sun came out. = 暴风雨过去了；因此，太阳出来了。


将来时:
It will rain tomorrow; hence, we will stay indoors. = 明天下雨；因此，我们将待在室内。
The project will be delayed; hence, the deadline will be extended. = 项目将被推迟；因此，截止日期将被延长。


虚拟语气:
If it were to rain, hence we would stay inside. = 如果下雨的话，因此我们将待在里面。
If I had known, hence I would have acted differently. = 如果我知道的话，因此我会采取不同的行动。


被动语态:
The meeting was cancelled; hence, no decisions were made. = 会议被取消了；因此，没有做出任何决定。
The project was delayed; hence, the budget was revised. = 项目被推迟了；因此，预算被修改了。


疑问句:
Is it raining? Hence, are we going to the park? = 下雨了吗？因此，我们要到公园去吗？
Is the project delayed? Hence, what is the new deadline? = 项目延期了吗？因此，新的截止日期是什么？


否定句:
It is not raining; hence, we can go out. = 天没下雨；因此，我们可以出去了。
The project is not delayed; hence, the deadline remains unchanged. = 项目没有延期；因此，截止日期保持不变。


PHRASE->

henceforth = 从今以后
from hence = 从这里
henceforward = 今后
thence = 从那里
and hence = 因此
