modernities:/məˈdɜːrnɪtiz/ | n. |现代性；现代事物 |The characteristics of modern times or modern life;  modern things.  This encompasses the new ideas, practices, and technologies that define a particular era.  |例句：The museum showcases the modernities of the early 20th century.  这个博物馆展示了20世纪初的现代事物。

例句：The rapid advance in technology has brought about many new modernities. 科技的快速发展带来了许多新的现代事物。


近义词：modernity, contemporaneity, currentness

反义词：antiquity, archaism, old-fashionedness

词性：名词
