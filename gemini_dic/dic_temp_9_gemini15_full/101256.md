invoicing:/ˈɪnˌvɔɪsɪŋ/| n.|开具发票；记账；开票|The process of creating and sending invoices to customers for goods or services provided.  It involves detailing the items sold, their prices, taxes, and any applicable discounts.|例句：Our invoicing system is automated, saving us significant time and effort. = 我们的开票系统是自动化的，这为我们节省了大量的时间和精力。|例句：Accurate invoicing is crucial for maintaining good financial records. = 准确的开票对于维护良好的财务记录至关重要。|近义词：billing, accounting, billing process|反义词：payment, receipt
