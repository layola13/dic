equality:/ɪˈkwɒləti/| n. |平等；相等|The state of being equal, especially in status, rights, and opportunities.  It refers to a condition where all individuals or groups are treated with the same respect and have the same opportunities. |Example:  The company strives for equality in its hiring practices. 这家公司努力在其招聘实践中实现平等。|近义词：evenness, equivalence, uniformity|反义词：inequality, disparity
