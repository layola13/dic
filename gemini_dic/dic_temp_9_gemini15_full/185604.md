revengeable:/rɪˈvendʒəbl/ | adj. | 可报复的，可复仇的 | Capable of being avenged;  deserving of revenge.  This word suggests that a wrong has been committed and that revenge is a justified or possible response. | The insult was so grievous that it felt revengeable.  这侮辱如此严重，感觉是可报复的。 |  avengeable,  punishable | unforgivable, irredeemable | 形容词
