reassessed:/ˌriːəˈsɛst/| v.|重新评估，重新考虑|To assess something again, often with more information or a different perspective.|The company reassessed its marketing strategy after poor sales figures. 公司在销售业绩不佳后重新评估了其营销策略。| reassess, re-evaluate, reconsider | accept, confirm |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reassess the project's feasibility every quarter. = 他们每个季度都会重新评估项目的可行性。
We reassess our investment portfolio annually. = 我们每年都会重新评估我们的投资组合。


一般过去时:
The government reassessed the environmental impact of the dam project. = 政府重新评估了水坝项目对环境的影响。
The teacher reassessed the student's work after finding an error in the grading. = 老师在发现评分错误后重新评估了学生的作业。


现在完成时:
The insurance company has reassessed the claim and increased the payout. = 保险公司已重新评估索赔并增加了赔付金额。
They have reassessed their budget and found they can afford the new equipment. = 他们重新评估了预算，发现他们买得起新设备。


过去完成时:
After the accident, the engineers had reassessed the bridge's structural integrity. = 事故发生后，工程师们重新评估了桥梁的结构完整性。
The committee had reassessed the proposal before submitting their final recommendation. = 委员会在提交最终建议之前重新评估了该提案。


将来时:
The council will reassess the property taxes next year. = 理事会明年将重新评估房产税。
The doctor will reassess the patient's condition after the surgery. = 医生将在手术后重新评估病人的病情。


虚拟语气:
If they had reassessed the risks earlier, they might have avoided the crisis. = 如果他们早些重新评估风险，他们本可以避免这场危机。
It would be wise to reassess the situation before making a decision. = 在做决定之前重新评估一下情况是明智的。


被动语态:
The risks were reassessed by the project manager. = 风险由项目经理重新评估。
The budget is being reassessed by the finance department. = 预算正在由财务部门重新评估。


疑问句:
Will the committee reassess the proposal? = 委员会会重新评估该提案吗？
Has the company reassessed its marketing strategy yet? = 公司是否已经重新评估了其营销策略？


否定句:
The board did not reassess the company's strategic direction. = 董事会没有重新评估公司的战略方向。
They refused to reassess their position despite the new evidence. = 尽管有新的证据，他们还是拒绝重新评估自己的立场。

reassess the value = 重新评估价值
reassess the impact = 重新评估影响
