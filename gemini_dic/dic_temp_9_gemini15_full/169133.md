projectionists:/prəˈdʒɛkʃənɪsts/ | n. |放映员 | A projectionist is a person who operates a film projector in a cinema or other venue. They are responsible for setting up the projector, threading the film, and ensuring a smooth and uninterrupted screening.  Their role was especially crucial in the pre-digital era of cinema. | The old movie theater hired a projectionist to run the equipment.  老电影院雇了一个放映员来操作设备。  The projectionist expertly threaded the film reel. 放映员熟练地穿好了胶片卷轴。 |  cinema operator, film operator |  (no direct antonym,  perhaps "audience member" in a contrasting sense) | 名词
