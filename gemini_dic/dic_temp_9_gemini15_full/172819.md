quadriplegia:/kwɑːdrɪˈpliːdʒə/|/ˈkwɒdrɪplɪːdʒə/| n. |四肢瘫痪|Paralysis affecting all four limbs.  It results from injury to the spinal cord, often in the neck region.  The degree of paralysis can vary.|例句：The accident left him with quadriplegia, requiring constant care.  这场事故导致他四肢瘫痪，需要持续的护理。

例句：She was diagnosed with quadriplegia after a severe car crash.  她在一次严重的交通事故后被诊断为四肢瘫痪。


近义词：tetraplegia, complete paralysis|反义词：full mobility, unimpaired movement|词性：名词
