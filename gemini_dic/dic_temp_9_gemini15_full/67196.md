expressionism:/ɪkˈspreʃənɪzəm/| n.| 表现主义 |A style of art, literature, or music characterized by a subjective and emotional approach, often employing distortion, exaggeration, and non-representational techniques to express inner feelings or subjective experience.  表现主义是一种艺术、文学或音乐风格，其特点是采用主观和情感化的方法，常常运用扭曲、夸张和非具象的技巧来表达内心的感受或主观体验。| The paintings of Edvard Munch are prime examples of expressionism.  爱德华·蒙克的绘画是表现主义的典型例子。| surrealism, abstract expressionism | realism, naturalism | 名词
