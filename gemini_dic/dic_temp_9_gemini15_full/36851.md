colons:/ˈkoʊlɒnz/|/ˈkəʊlənz/| n. |冒号|A colon is a punctuation mark (:) used to introduce a list, an explanation, or a quotation.|The recipe called for: flour, sugar, and eggs.  这个食谱需要：面粉、糖和鸡蛋。|semicolon, dash|period|名词
