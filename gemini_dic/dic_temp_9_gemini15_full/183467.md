repudiate:/rɪˈpjuːdieɪt/| vt.|拒绝；否认；抛弃；与…断绝关系|To refuse to accept, acknowledge, or be associated with.  To reject a person, idea, or claim. |例句：He repudiated the accusations against him. 他否认了对他的指控。

例句：The government repudiated the treaty. 政府否决了该条约。


近义词：reject, deny, renounce, disown, disavow |反义词：accept, acknowledge, endorse, approve |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repudiates his past actions. 他否认他过去的行动。
She repudiates the claim made by her ex-husband. 她否认了她前夫提出的说法。


一般过去时:
They repudiated the agreement. 他们否决了协议。
The company repudiated its debt. 公司拒不偿还债务。


现在完成时:
He has repudiated all responsibility for the accident. 他已否认对事故负有任何责任。
She has repudiated her family's beliefs. 她已经放弃了她家族的信仰。


过去完成时:
He had repudiated the offer before I could even speak. 我还没来得及说话，他就已经拒绝了提议。
They had repudiated the contract before the deadline.  他们在截止日期前就已经否决了合同。


将来时:
The court will repudiate the contract if fraud is proven. 如果能证明存在欺诈行为，法院将否决该合同。
She will repudiate her inheritance. 她将放弃她的遗产。


虚拟语气:
If he repudiated the deal, what would happen? 如果他拒绝这笔交易，会发生什么？
I wish he would repudiate his false claims. 我希望他会否认他虚假的声明。


被动语态:
The treaty was repudiated by the government. 该条约被政府否决了。
His claims were repudiated by several witnesses. 他的说法被几位证人否认了。


疑问句:
Will he repudiate the accusations? 他会否认这些指控吗？
Does she repudiate her past? 她否认她的过去吗？


否定句:
He did not repudiate his promise. 他没有食言。
She did not repudiate the evidence. 她没有否认证据。


PHRASE->
repudiate a claim 否认一项说法
repudiate a debt 拒不偿还债务
repudiate an agreement 否决一项协议
repudiate a relationship 与…断绝关系
repudiate one's heritage 抛弃自己的传统


词源:
From Latin *repudiāre, from re- (“again”) + pudiāre (“to feel shame”).  The original meaning implied a rejection with shame or disgrace.
