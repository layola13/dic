quadriennial:/kwɒdriˈɛniəl/| adj.|四年一次的；每四年发生的|Occurring every four years.  The word is derived from the Latin "quadri-" (four) and "annus" (year).|例句：The Olympic Games are a quadriennial event. 奥运会是四年一度的盛事。

The presidential election is a quadriennial event in the United States. 美国总统选举是四年一次的活动。|近义词：four-yearly|反义词：annual, biennial
