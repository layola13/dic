sayest:/ˈseɪɪst/| v.|你说(古英语第二人称单数现在时)|The second-person singular present tense form of the verb "to say" in older English, now largely archaic.  It indicates that the speaker is addressing a single person and describing an action happening in the present. |Example: Thou sayest well. = 你说得对。|近义词：sayst (archaic), assert, declare, affirm|反义词:deny, contradict.
