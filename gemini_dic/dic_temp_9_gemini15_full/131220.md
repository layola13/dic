noncalcified:/ˌnɒnˈkælsɪfaɪd/| adj. |未钙化的，非钙化的 |Not hardened or solidified by the deposition of calcium salts.  This term is primarily used in medical contexts, often referring to tissues or structures that lack the normal amount of calcium. |Example: The noncalcified nodule was discovered during the ultrasound.  未钙化的结节在超声检查中被发现。|近义词：uncalcified, unmineralized |反义词：calcified, mineralized
