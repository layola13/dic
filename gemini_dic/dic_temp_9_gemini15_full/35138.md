cloudy:/ˈklaʊdi/| adj.|阴天的；多云的；模糊的；不清晰的 |Cloudy describes the sky when it is covered with clouds.  It can also describe something that is unclear or indistinct. |例句：The sky is cloudy today. 今天天空多云。|例句：His explanation was rather cloudy. 他的解释相当模糊。|近义词：overcast, hazy, murky, unclear |反义词：sunny, clear, bright


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sky is cloudy today. = 今天的天空多云。
It is usually cloudy in this region. = 这个地区通常是多云的。


一般过去时:
It was cloudy yesterday. = 昨天是多云的。
The weather was cloudy and rainy. = 天气阴雨。


现在完成时:
It has been cloudy for three days. = 已经连续三天多云了。
It has been cloudy all week. = 整周都是阴天。


过去完成时:
It had been cloudy all morning. = 一上午都是阴天。
Before the storm, it had been cloudy. = 暴风雨来临前，天气一直阴沉。


将来时:
It will be cloudy tomorrow. = 明天将是多云的。
The forecast says it will be cloudy all week. = 预报说本周都将是多云天气。


虚拟语气:
If it were cloudy, we wouldn't go to the beach. = 如果是阴天，我们就不去海滩了。
I wish it weren't so cloudy today. = 我希望今天不要这么阴天。


被动语态:
(Cloudy is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Is it cloudy outside? = 外面是多云的天气吗？
Will it be cloudy this weekend? = 这个周末会是阴天吗？


否定句:
It isn't cloudy today; it's sunny. = 今天不是阴天，是晴天。
The sky wasn't cloudy yesterday; it was clear. = 昨天天空不是阴天，而是晴朗的。
