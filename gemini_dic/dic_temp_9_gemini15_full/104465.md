kaiserdom:/ˈkaɪzərdɒm/| n. |德意志帝国；皇帝统治；帝王统治|The territory or dominion ruled by a Kaiser (German Emperor).  The term often evokes the German Empire (1871-1918).|Example: The Kaiserdom was a powerful force in European politics during the late 19th and early 20th centuries.  德意志帝国在19世纪末和20世纪初是欧洲政治中一股强大的力量。|近义词：empire, kingdom, realm|反义词：republic, democracy|名词
