grazingly:/ˈɡreɪzɪŋli/ | adv. |漫不经心地；漫游地；随意地 |In a grazing manner; casually or idly.  The word suggests a slow, meandering movement, like that of an animal grazing.  |例句：He looked grazingly at the scenery as he drove. 他开车时漫不经心地看着风景。

例句：She grazingly flipped through the magazine, not really paying attention. 她漫不经心地翻阅杂志，并没有真正注意。


近义词：casually, idly, listlessly, indifferently, absently


反义词：attentively, carefully, intently, diligently


词性: 副词
