remaking:/ˌriːˈmeɪkɪŋ/| v. |重拍；重制；改造；重建 |The act of making something again, often with improvements or changes.  It can refer to films, products, or even situations. |例句：The studio is remaking the classic film. 制片厂正在重拍这部经典影片。

例句：They are remaking the old house. 他们在重建这座旧房子。


近义词：reconstructing, refashioning, renovating, revamping

反义词：destroying, demolishing, ruining


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are remaking the old building. = 他们在重建那座旧建筑。
The company is remaking its marketing strategy. = 公司正在重新制定其营销策略。

一般过去时:
The director remade the film with a new cast. = 导演用新的演员阵容重拍了这部电影。
She remade the dress from old fabric. = 她用旧布料重新制作了这件衣服。

现在完成时:
They have remade the entire kitchen. = 他们已经彻底改造了整个厨房。
The band has remade several of their early songs. = 乐队已经重新演绎了他们早期的一些歌曲。

过去完成时:
He had remade his life after the accident. = 事故后，他已经重新开始了生活。
She had remade the cake after it burned. = 蛋糕烧焦后，她已经重新做了一个。

将来时:
They will remake the movie next year. = 他们明年将重拍这部电影。
We will remake the garden in the spring. = 我们将在春天重新设计花园。

虚拟语气:
If they remade the film, it would be better. = 如果他们重拍这部电影，它会更好。
I wish they had remade the ending. = 我希望他们重拍结局。

被动语态:
The old house is being remade. = 这座旧房子正在被重建。
The software is being remade to fix the bugs. = 该软件正在被重新制作以修复错误。

疑问句:
Are they remaking that popular TV show? = 他们正在重拍那部受欢迎的电视剧吗？
Is the company remaking its product line? = 公司正在改造其产品线吗？

否定句:
They are not remaking the movie. = 他们没有重拍这部电影。
The chef did not remake the dish. = 厨师没有重新制作这道菜。
