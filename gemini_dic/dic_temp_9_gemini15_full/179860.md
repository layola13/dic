reexplores:/ˌriːɪkˈsplɔːrz/| v.|重新探索，再次探究|To explore again or anew; to investigate something previously examined.|例句：The team reexplores the cave system, hoping to find new passages.  团队重新探索了洞穴系统，希望能找到新的通道。

例句：The historian reexplores the events leading up to the war, uncovering new evidence. 历史学家重新探究了导致战争的事件，发现了新的证据。


近义词：reinvestigate, reexamine, revisit


反义词：ignore, neglect


词性:动词
