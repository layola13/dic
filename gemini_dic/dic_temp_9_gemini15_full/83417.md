habituate:/həˈbɪtʃueɪt/ | vt. | 使习惯，使适应 | To accustom or become accustomed to something.  |  He habituated himself to the cold climate. 他使自己习惯了寒冷的气候。| accustom, adapt, adjust |  disaccustom, unaccustom | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher habituates her students to early rising. = 老师使她的学生习惯早起。
They habituate themselves to a vegetarian diet. = 他们使自己习惯了素食。


一般过去时:
He habituated himself to the noise of the city. = 他使自己习惯了城市的喧嚣。
She quickly habituated to the new job. = 她很快适应了新的工作。


现在完成时:
I have habituated myself to working long hours. = 我已经习惯了长时间工作。
They have habituated to the heat and humidity. = 他们已经习惯了炎热潮湿的天气。


过去完成时:
She had habituated herself to a life of luxury before the accident. = 事故发生前，她已经习惯了奢侈的生活。
He had habituated to the pressure of his job. = 他已经习惯了他工作的压力。


将来时:
I will habituate myself to the new schedule. = 我将使自己适应新的时间安排。
We will habituate our pets to their new home. = 我们将会使我们的宠物适应它们的新家。


虚拟语气:
If I habituated myself to regular exercise, I would feel much better. = 如果我养成定期锻炼的习惯，我会感觉好得多。
I wish I had habituated myself to saving money earlier. = 我真希望我早点养成储蓄的习惯。


被动语态:
The students were habituated to strict discipline. = 学生们被训练得服从严格的纪律。
The animals are being habituated to their new environment. = 动物们正在适应它们的新环境。


疑问句:
How long will it take to habituate to this medication? = 需要多长时间才能适应这种药物？
Have you habituated to the new working hours yet? = 你已经适应新的工作时间了吗？


否定句:
He couldn't habituate himself to the early morning classes. = 他无法适应早上的课程。
She failed to habituate to the demanding lifestyle. = 她没能适应要求很高的生活方式。


PHRASE->
habituate oneself to = 使自己习惯于...
habituate to = 适应...
become habituated to = 变得习惯于...
gradually habituate = 逐渐适应
habituate by repetition = 通过重复来适应
