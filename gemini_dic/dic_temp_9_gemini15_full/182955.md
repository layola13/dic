replaced:/rɪˈpleɪst/| v. |替换，取代|To take the place of (something or someone).|例句：The old equipment has been replaced with new, more efficient models. 旧设备已被更新、更高效的型号取代。|近义词：substitute, exchange, supplant|反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They replace the broken window pane. = 他们更换了破损的窗格。
The company replaces its outdated software every year. = 公司每年都会更换过时的软件。


一般过去时:
He replaced the flat tire with a spare. = 他用备胎换掉了瘪胎。
She replaced the missing files. = 她找回了丢失的文件。


现在完成时:
They have replaced all the light bulbs. = 他们已经更换了所有的灯泡。
We have replaced the old system with a new one. = 我们已经用新的系统取代了旧系统。


过去完成时:
He had replaced the engine before the race. = 他在比赛前已经更换了发动机。
She had replaced the damaged parts before the inspection. = 她在检查前已经更换了损坏的部件。


将来时:
They will replace the roof next week. = 他们下周将更换屋顶。
We will replace the broken chair tomorrow. = 我们明天将更换坏掉的椅子。


虚拟语气:
If they had replaced the battery, the car would have started. = 如果他们更换了电池，汽车就会启动了。
I wish they had replaced the faulty equipment earlier. = 我希望他们早点更换有故障的设备。


被动语态:
The old machine was replaced by a new one. = 旧机器被一台新机器取代了。
The broken bridge is being replaced. = 那座坏掉的桥正在被修复。


疑问句:
Will the damaged parts be replaced? = 损坏的部件会被更换吗？
What is replacing the old model? = 什么东西正在取代旧型号？


否定句:
They didn't replace the broken fence. = 他们没有更换坏掉的栅栏。
We haven't replaced the worn-out tires yet. = 我们还没更换磨损的轮胎。


PHRASE->
replace with = 用...替换
replace A with B = 用B替换A
in place of = 代替
instead of = 代替
to replace = 为了取代
