euphorbiaceous:/ˌjuːfɔːrˈbeɪʃəs/| adj. |大戟科的；大戟属的|Belonging to or characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae, a large family of flowering plants that includes many succulents and poisonous species.  The term often describes plants with milky sap.|例句：Many euphorbiaceous plants are known for their distinctive milky sap. 许多大戟科植物以其独特的乳状汁液而闻名。


例句：The euphorbiaceous plant thrived in the arid climate.这种大戟科植物在干旱气候中茁壮成长。


近义词：spurge-related (less formal)


反义词：无


词性：形容词
