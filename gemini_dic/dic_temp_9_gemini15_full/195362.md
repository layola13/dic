semimanufacturing: /ˌsɛmiˈmænjuˌfæktʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 半成品制造；半成品加工 | The process of partially manufacturing a product, leaving some stages of production incomplete.  This often involves assembling components or performing some initial processing steps before the product is fully completed.  It's a common practice in many industries to improve efficiency and reduce costs. | Example: The company specializes in semimanufacturing of electronic components, supplying partially assembled boards to other manufacturers. 这家公司专门从事电子元件的半成品制造，向其他制造商供应部分组装的电路板。|近义词: partial manufacturing, prefabrication |反义词: full manufacturing, complete fabrication
