re-perform:/ˌriːpərˈfɔːrm/| vt.|再次表演；重新执行|To perform again; to perform something again, often with improvements or corrections.|例句：The orchestra will re-perform Beethoven's Fifth Symphony next week.  乐队下周将再次演奏贝多芬的第五交响曲。|例句：The experiment was re-performed under stricter conditions. 实验在更严格的条件下重新进行了。|近义词：repeat, replay, redo|反义词：cancel, abandon

The company re-performs its safety checks regularly. = 公司定期重新进行安全检查。
