recognitions:/ˌrekəɡˈnɪʃənz/| n. |认可；承认；表彰；认知|The act of recognizing or being recognized; acknowledgement; appreciation; awards.  It can also refer to the process of identifying something or someone. |例句：His years of service earned him many recognitions.  他多年的服务为他赢得了许多表彰。

例句：The company gave recognitions to its top performers. 公司对表现最佳的员工给予了表彰。


近义词：acknowledgments, accolades, awards, appreciations, identifications

反义词：ignorance, disregard, denial


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company gives recognitions to its employees annually. = 公司每年都会给员工表彰。
Many people recognize his talent. = 许多人认可他的才能。


一般过去时:
The professor received many recognitions for his research. = 教授因其研究获得了许多表彰。
She didn't recognize her childhood friend at first. = 她一开始没有认出她儿时的朋友。


现在完成时:
The organization has given numerous recognitions to volunteers. = 该组织已经给予志愿者无数的表彰。
Have you recognized the pattern yet? = 你已经认出模式了吗？


过去完成时:
By the time he retired, he had received numerous recognitions for his work. = 到他退休的时候，他已经因为他的工作获得了许多表彰。
I had recognized him from a previous meeting. = 我从之前的会议上就认出了他。


将来时:
The artist will receive many recognitions for his groundbreaking work. = 这位艺术家将因其开创性的作品而获得许多表彰。
I think you will recognize her when you see her. = 我想你会认出她来的。


虚拟语气:
If he had received more recognitions, he might have continued his research. = 如果他得到更多表彰，他可能会继续他的研究。
I wish I had recognized the danger sooner. = 我希望我早点意识到危险。


被动语态:
His contributions were widely recognized. = 他的贡献得到了广泛的认可。
The awards ceremony will be recognized as a great success. = 颁奖典礼将被认为是一次巨大的成功。


疑问句:
Do you recognize that man? = 你认识那个人吗？
Were his achievements properly recognized? = 他的成就得到适当的认可了吗？


否定句:

I didn't recognize the voice on the phone. = 我没认出电话里的声音。
