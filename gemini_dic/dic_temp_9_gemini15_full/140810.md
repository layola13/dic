on-ground:/ˌɒnˈɡraʊnd/ | adj. | 实地考察的；现场的；地面的 |  Relating to or occurring on the ground or at a location, as opposed to remotely or theoretically.  It often implies direct, physical presence and involvement. |例句：The on-ground team reported significant damage. 实地考察小组报告了严重的损失。
例句：The on-ground situation is more complicated than we anticipated.  现场情况比我们预期的要复杂。|近义词：field, in-situ, onsite, local |反义词：remote, virtual, theoretical, offsite |词性：形容词
