hyperdeliciously: /ˌhaɪpər dɪˈlɪʃəsli/ | adv. |极其美味地，非常美味地 |  This word is not found in standard dictionaries. It's a playful, informal creation formed by combining "hyper-" (meaning excessively or extremely) with "deliciously" (meaning in a delicious manner).  It emphasizes the extreme deliciousness of something. |例句：The cake was hyperdeliciously moist and chocolatey. 这蛋糕湿润又巧克力味十足，极其美味。 The chef prepared the dish hyperdeliciously, exceeding all expectations.  厨师烹制这道菜非常美味，超出了所有人的预期。 | 近义词：exquisitely, supremely, incredibly deliciously | 反义词：disgustingly, terribly, awfully
