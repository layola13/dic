manna:/ˈmænə/| n. |吗哪，甘露；恩赐 |Sweet, edible substance said to have fallen from heaven as food for the Israelites in the desert.  The word is often used figuratively to refer to something that unexpectedly provides sustenance or relief. |例句：The unexpected inheritance felt like manna from heaven. 这笔意外的遗产像是天降甘露。

例句：The timely rain was manna to the parched farmers. 及时的雨水对干旱的农民来说是天赐之物。


近义词：blessing, boon, gift, provision |反义词：curse, misfortune, hardship |词性：名词
