opisometer:/oʊˈpɪsəmɪtər/ | n. |测距仪，里程计 | An instrument for measuring the length of a curve or irregular line.  It typically consists of a wheel that rolls along the line, registering the distance on a counter. | Example: The cartographer used an opisometer to measure the length of the winding river on the map.  制图师用测距仪测量地图上蜿蜒河流的长度。|近义词：curvimeter, planimeter | 反义词：None
