crassest:/ˈkræsɪst/| adj.| 最粗俗的，最粗鲁的 | Extremely insensitive, vulgar, or unrefined.  It is the superlative form of "crass".|例句：His crassest remarks offended everyone at the meeting. 他最粗俗的言论冒犯了会议上的每个人。

例句：The crassest display of greed I've ever witnessed was the CEO's bonus. 我见过的最贪婪的表现是CEO的奖金。


近义词：vulgar, rude, insensitive, boorish, uncouth, gauche

反义词：refined, polite, sensitive, cultured, sophisticated

词性:形容词
