dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/ | vt. vi. |扩张，扩大，膨胀 |To make or become wider, larger, or more open. |例句：The pupils of his eyes dilated in the darkness.  他的瞳孔在黑暗中扩张了。|例句：The blood vessels dilate to improve blood flow. 血管扩张以改善血液流动。|近义词：expand, widen, enlarge |反义词：contract, constrict


USAGE->

The pupils dilate in dim light. = 瞳孔在昏暗的光线下扩张。
The doctor dilates the patient's pupils before the examination. = 医生在检查前扩张病人的瞳孔。
