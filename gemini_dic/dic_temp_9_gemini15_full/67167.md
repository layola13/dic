expostulates:/ɪkˈspɒstʃəleɪt/| vt.| 规劝；争论；抗议 | To reason earnestly with someone in an attempt to dissuade them from some course or action.  |例句：The teacher expostulated with the student about the importance of attending class.  老师规劝学生要重视上课的重要性。

例句：He expostulated with his friend about the dangers of smoking. 他苦口婆心地劝说朋友戒烟的危害。


近义词：remonstrate, protest, argue, reason with | 反义词：acquiesce, consent, agree


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expostulates with his colleagues about the new policy. 他就新政策和同事们据理力争。
She expostulates daily with her children about their messy rooms. 她每天都苦口婆心地劝说孩子们打扫他们凌乱的房间。


一般过去时:
The manager expostulated with the employee about their tardiness. 经理就员工迟到的事情进行了规劝。
My father expostulated with me for hours about my career choice. 我父亲就我的职业选择和我争论了几个小时。


现在完成时:
She has expostulated with him repeatedly about his reckless driving. 她已经反复劝诫他不要鲁莽驾驶。
They have expostulated with the government about the new tax law. 他们就新的税法向政府提出了抗议。


过去完成时:
He had expostulated with her many times before she finally listened. 在她最终听从劝告之前，他已经多次劝诫她了。
The union had expostulated with management for weeks before the strike. 在罢工之前，工会已经和管理层争论了几个星期。


将来时:
I will expostulate with him about his behavior. 我会就他的行为规劝他。
She will expostulate with the board about the proposed budget cuts. 她将就拟议的预算削减向董事会提出抗议。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would expostulate with them about the injustice. 如果我是你，我会就这种不公正的事情和他们理论。
I wish I had expostulated with him sooner about his drinking problem. 我真希望我早点劝他戒酒。


被动语态:
He was expostulated with for his inappropriate remarks. 他因不当言论受到了规劝。
The proposal was expostulated against by several members of the committee.  委员会的几位成员对该提案提出了异议。


疑问句:
Why did he expostulate with her? 他为什么要规劝她？
Does she often expostulate with her employees? 她经常规劝她的员工吗？


否定句:
He did not expostulate with them about the decision. 他没有就这个决定劝说他们。
She will not expostulate with him again about this matter. 她不会再就此事劝说他了。


PHRASE->
expostulate with someone about something  就某事规劝某人
expostulate against something  反对某事
expostulate vehemently  强烈抗议
expostulate in vain  徒劳地规劝
expostulate earnestly  认真地规劝


词源:
From Late Latin *expostulare, from Latin ex- "out" + postulare "to demand, request."  The original meaning implied a strong demand or request, which evolved into the sense of earnest reasoning or remonstrance.
