related:/rɪˈleɪtɪd/ | adj. |相关的，关联的 | Having a connection or relationship.  |例句：The two incidents are closely related. 这两起事件密切相关。|例句：His illness is related to stress. 他的病与压力有关。|近义词：connected, associated, linked |反义词：unrelated, unconnected, separate |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The events are related. = 这些事件是相关的。
This topic is related to our discussion. = 这个话题与我们的讨论相关。

一般过去时:
The two cases were related. = 这两个案例是相关的。
The problem was related to the software. = 这个问题与软件相关。

现在完成时:
Many studies have related smoking to lung cancer. = 许多研究已经将吸烟与肺癌联系起来。
The research has related the two phenomena. = 这项研究将这两种现象联系了起来。

过去完成时:
It had been related that the king was ill. = 据悉国王生病了。(注意此处related作过去分词，并非一般过去时)
It had already been related that the project was delayed. = 项目延误的消息已经传开了。(注意此处related作过去分词，并非一般过去时)

将来时:
The report will relate the incident to a recent crime. = 报告将把这起事件与最近的一起犯罪联系起来。
They will relate their experiences to the audience. = 他们将向观众讲述他们的经历。(注意此处relate作动词，并非形容词related)


虚拟语气:
If the two events were related, we'd see a pattern. = 如果这两件事有关联，我们会看到一个模式。
If it were related to the project, I would have known. = 如果它与项目有关，我会知道的。

被动语态:
The findings were related to the previous research. = 这些发现与之前的研究有关。
The information is related to the ongoing investigation. = 此信息与正在进行的调查有关。

疑问句:
Are these two ideas related? = 这两个想法有关联吗？
Is this problem related to the hardware? = 这个问题与硬件有关吗？

否定句:
The incidents are not related. = 这些事件没有关联。
The two events were unrelated. = 这两件事没有关系。
