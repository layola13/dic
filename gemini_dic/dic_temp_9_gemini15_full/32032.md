cherished: /ˈtʃerɪʃt/ | v. adj. | 珍爱的；珍惜的 | To hold dear; to keep carefully and protect.  |例句：She cherished the memory of her childhood. 她珍藏着她童年时的记忆。  |近义词：treasure, prize, value, adore |反义词：despise, neglect, disregard |动词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cherishes his family. = 他珍爱他的家人。
They cherish every moment together. = 他们珍惜在一起的每一刻。

一般过去时:
She cherished her grandmother's locket. = 她珍藏着她祖母的锁匙。
We cherished the opportunity to travel. = 我们珍惜了这次旅行的机会。

现在完成时:
I have cherished this friendship for many years. = 我珍视这份友谊多年了。
They have cherished their memories of the trip. = 他们一直珍藏着旅行的回忆。

过去完成时:
She had cherished the hope of seeing him again. = 她一直怀抱着再次见到他的希望。
He had cherished the dream of becoming a doctor. = 他一直珍藏着成为一名医生的梦想。

将来时:
I will always cherish this day. = 我将永远珍视这一天。
We will cherish the memories we made together. = 我们将珍藏我们一起创造的回忆。

虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have cherished those moments more. = 如果我知道的话，我会更加珍惜那些时刻。
I wish I had cherished my time with my family more. = 我真希望我当时更珍惜与家人在一起的时间。

被动语态:
The old photograph was cherished by the family. = 这张旧照片被家人珍藏着。
These memories should be cherished. = 这些回忆应该被珍藏。

疑问句:
Do you cherish your childhood memories? = 你珍藏着你的童年回忆吗？
Does he cherish his freedom? = 他珍惜他的自由吗？


否定句:
He didn't cherish the opportunity. = 他没有珍惜这次机会。
They didn't cherish their time together. = 他们没有珍惜在一起的时间。


PHRASE->
cherished memories = 珍贵的回忆
cherished possession = 珍爱的物品
cherished hope = 珍藏的希望
cherished dream = 珍藏的梦想
cherished friend = 珍爱的朋友


词源:
源自古法语 *cherir，意思是“爱抚，抚慰”，最终来自拉丁语 *carus，“亲爱的”。
