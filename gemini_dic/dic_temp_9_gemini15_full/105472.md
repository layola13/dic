killer:/ˈkɪlər/| n. adj. |杀手；极好的；极棒的|

英文解析:A killer is a person who kills another person.  The word can also be used informally as an adjective to describe something exceptionally good or impressive.

例句:
The police arrested the killer after a long investigation. = 警方经过长时间的调查后逮捕了凶手。

The new restaurant's burger is a killer! = 这家新餐馆的汉堡太棒了！


近义词: murderer, assassin, awesome, fantastic

反义词: savior, lifesaver (as a noun); terrible, awful (as an adjective)


词性:noun, adjective
