bustle:/ˈbʌsəl/| n. v.|喧嚣；忙碌；裙撑；(使)忙碌 |A state of great activity and energy; to move about in a busy way.|例句：The market was a bustle of activity. 市场一片繁忙景象。|近义词：hustle, flurry, rush|反义词：calm, quiet, stillness|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city bustles with activity. = 城市充满活力。
She bustles around the kitchen. = 她忙忙碌碌地在厨房里走动。

一般过去时:
The streets bustled with people. = 街道上人来人往，热闹非凡。
She bustled about, preparing for the party. = 她忙忙碌碌地准备着派对。

现在完成时:
The town has bustled with tourists all summer. = 整个夏天，这个小镇都挤满了游客。
She has bustled through her work all day. = 她一整天都在忙碌地工作。

过去完成时:
The market had bustled with shoppers before the rain started. = 下雨之前，市场上挤满了购物者。
She had bustled through her chores before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经完成了家务。

将来时:
The city will bustle with excitement during the festival. = 节日期间，城市将充满兴奋。
She will bustle around getting everything ready. = 她将忙忙碌碌地准备一切。

虚拟语气:
If the city bustled more, it would be more lively. = 如果城市更繁华，它就会更有活力。
I wish she wouldn't bustle so much. = 我希望她不要这么忙乱。

被动语态:
The streets were bustled with life. = 街道上充满了生机。（较为少见，通常用主动语态）
The kitchen was bustled in preparation for dinner. = 厨房里一片为晚餐做准备的忙碌景象。（较为少见，通常用主动语态）

疑问句:
Does the town bustle during the summer? = 夏天小镇热闹吗？
Does she often bustle around like that? = 她经常那样忙忙碌碌吗？

否定句:
The town didn't bustle much during the off-season. = 淡季小镇并不热闹。
She didn't bustle around; she worked calmly and efficiently. = 她没有忙乱，而是冷静而高效地工作。
