handsaw:/ˈhændsɔː/| n. |手锯|A handsaw is a saw with a relatively short blade, designed to be used with one hand.  It is typically used for cutting wood, but can also be used for other materials depending on the type of blade.|例句：He used a handsaw to cut the wood for the birdhouse. 他用一把手锯锯木头做鸟舍。

例句：The carpenter carefully measured and marked the wood before using the handsaw. 木匠在使用手锯之前仔细测量并标记了木材。


近义词：saw (general term)|反义词：None (antonyms are not applicable for tools)|词性:noun
