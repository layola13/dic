reforged:/ˌriːˈfɔːrdʒd/| v. |重铸，重新锻造；彻底改革|To shape or create something again, typically improving it in the process.  It implies a transformation from a previous state, often involving significant change and improvement.|例句：The company was reforged in the image of its new CEO.  这家公司在新的首席执行官的领导下焕然一新。

The ancient sword was reforged, stronger than ever before. 这把古剑经过重铸，比以往任何时候都更加坚固。|近义词：reshape, remake, reform, reconstruct|反义词：destroy, ruin, dismantle

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They forge new alliances every year. 他们每年都会缔结新的联盟。
He reforges his beliefs after a life changing experience. 他在经历了一次改变人生的经历后重塑了自己的信仰。


一般过去时:
The blacksmith reforged the broken sword.  铁匠重铸了那把断剑。
The government reforged its economic policy after the crisis. 政府在危机后重新制定了经济政策。


现在完成时:
The artist has reforged his style several times throughout his career.这位艺术家在其职业生涯中多次重塑了自己的风格。
We have reforged our commitment to sustainability. 我们已经重申了我们对可持续发展的承诺。


过去完成时:
They had reforged their relationship before the wedding. 他们在婚礼前已经修复了彼此的关系。
She had reforged her identity after years of hardship.  她经过多年的磨难后重塑了自己的身份。


将来时:
The company will reforge its image through a new marketing campaign. 公司将通过新的营销活动重塑其形象。
He will reforge his life after his recovery. 他康复后将重新开始生活。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
