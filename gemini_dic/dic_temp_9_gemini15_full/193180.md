sedges:/ˈsɛdʒɪz/ | n. |莎草；莎草科植物 | Sedges are grass-like plants belonging to the family Cyperaceae. They are often found in wet or marshy areas.  They are characterized by their triangular stems and their inconspicuous flowers. | Example:  The riverbank was overgrown with sedges and reeds. 河岸上长满了莎草和芦苇。  The botanist identified several species of sedges in the wetland. 植物学家在湿地中识别出几种莎草。|近义词：reeds, rushes |反义词：N/A
