devolute:/dɪˈvoːluːt/| vt.|转移；委任；衰退；堕落|To transfer (power, rights, or responsibilities) to another; to delegate; to deteriorate or decline in quality or value; to sink to a lower level. |例句：The company devolved responsibility for the project to a smaller team. 公司将项目的责任下放给了一个较小的团队。  The empire gradually devolved into a collection of squabbling states.  帝国逐渐衰落，变成了一堆互相争吵的国家。|近义词：delegate, transfer, assign, deteriorate, decline|反义词：centralize, ascend, improve, enhance|动词
