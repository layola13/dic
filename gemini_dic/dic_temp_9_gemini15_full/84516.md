hamate:/ˈheɪmeɪt/| n.|钩状的；钩状物；腕骨|A hook-shaped structure, especially the hamate bone in the wrist.  The term can also refer to anything hook-shaped.|例句：The hamate bone is one of the carpal bones in the wrist.  = 钩状骨是腕骨之一。

The hamate process of the ulna is an important anatomical feature. = 尺骨的钩状突是一个重要的解剖结构。|近义词：hooked, uncinate|反义词：straight, unhooked
