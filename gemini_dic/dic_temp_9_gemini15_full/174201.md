quelled:/kweld/| vt.|压制；平息；抑制；使平静|to suppress or extinguish, especially a feeling or uprising|The police quickly quelled the riot.  警察迅速平息了暴乱。|近义词：suppress, subdue, quell, crush, stifle|反义词：incite, encourage, agitate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government quells dissent. = 政府压制异议。
He quells his anger. = 他压制住自己的怒火。


一般过去时:
The firefighters quelled the fire. = 消防队员扑灭了大火。
She quelled her anxiety before the presentation. = 她在演讲前平息了焦虑。


现在完成时:
The army has quelled the rebellion. = 军队已经平息了叛乱。
She has quelled her fear of public speaking. = 她已经克服了她对公开演讲的恐惧。


过去完成时:
They had quelled the rumors before they spread widely. = 他们在谣言广泛传播之前就将其压制住了。
He had quelled his initial panic and began to think rationally. = 他已经平息了最初的恐慌，开始理性思考。


将来时:
The police will quell any further disturbances. = 警方将平息任何进一步的骚乱。
We hope to quell the spread of the disease. = 我们希望能够遏制疾病的传播。


虚拟语气:
If only they had quelled the conflict earlier. = 如果他们早点平息冲突就好了。
I wish I could quell my doubts. = 我希望我能消除我的疑虑。


被动语态:
The riot was quelled by the police. = 暴乱被警察平息了。
The fire was quickly quelled. = 大火很快就被扑灭了。


疑问句:
Can we quell this argument? = 我们能平息这场争论吗？
How did they quell the rebellion? = 他们是如何平息叛乱的？


否定句:
They failed to quell the unrest. = 他们没能平息骚乱。
He couldn't quell his feelings of frustration. = 他无法压制住他的挫败感。


PHRASE->
quell a rebellion = 平息叛乱
quell a riot = 平息暴乱
quell dissent = 压制异议
quell an uprising = 平息起义
quell one's fears = 克服恐惧


词源:
源自古法语 *queller，意为“to kill, destroy”。  最终源自拉丁语 *coaculare，意为“to press together”。
