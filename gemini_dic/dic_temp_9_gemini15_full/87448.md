hesitators:/ˈhɛzɪteɪtərz/ | n. |犹豫者，迟疑者 |People who hesitate or are slow to act or decide.  Those who frequently pause before speaking or acting, often due to uncertainty or doubt. |例句：The hesitators in the room were outnumbered by those who voted immediately.  房间里犹豫不决的人比那些立即投票的人少。

The hesitators at the conference finally agreed on a compromise.  会议上的犹豫者最终达成妥协。|近义词：procrastinators, waverers, doubters |反义词：decisiveness,  leaders
