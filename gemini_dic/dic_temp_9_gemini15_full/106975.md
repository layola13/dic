lacking-in: /ˈlækɪŋ ɪn/ | adj. | 缺乏…的；不足…的 |  Describes something or someone that is deficient or insufficient in a particular quality or attribute.  It emphasizes the absence of something considered desirable or necessary. | 例句：His presentation was lacking in originality.  他的演讲缺乏原创性。


例句：The plan was lacking in detail.  这个计划缺乏细节。


近义词：deficient in, short of, wanting in, devoid of | 反义词：abundant in, replete with, rich in | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cake is lacking in sweetness. = 蛋糕不够甜。
His argument is lacking in logic. = 他的论点缺乏逻辑。


一般过去时:
The report was lacking in crucial information. = 报告缺少关键信息。
Her performance was lacking in energy. = 她的表演缺乏活力。


现在完成时:
The project has been lacking in funding. = 这个项目一直缺乏资金。
The team has been lacking in motivation.  = 团队一直缺乏动力。


过去完成时:
The evidence had been lacking in the initial investigation. = 初步调查中证据不足。
The previous attempt had been lacking in planning. = 之前的尝试缺乏规划。


将来时:
The next proposal will be lacking in feasibility. = 下一个提案将会缺乏可行性。
Their efforts will be lacking in support. = 他们的努力将会缺乏支持。


虚拟语气:
If the proposal were lacking in detail, it would be rejected. = 如果提案缺乏细节，它将被拒绝。
I wish the presentation wasn't lacking in impact. = 我希望演示文稿更有影响力。


被动语态:
The proposal was found to be lacking in substance. = 提案被发现缺乏实质内容。
The team's efforts are being seen as lacking in commitment. = 团队的努力被认为缺乏承诺。


疑问句:
Is the design lacking in creativity? = 设计缺乏创意吗？
Are the instructions lacking in clarity? = 说明不够清晰吗？


否定句:
The product is not lacking in quality. = 产品质量并不差。
The speech was not lacking in passion. = 演讲不缺乏激情。
