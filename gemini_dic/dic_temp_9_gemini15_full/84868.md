hanging-about:/ˈhæŋɪŋ əˈbaʊt/| v.|闲逛，游荡，无所事事 |To spend time idly or aimlessly; to loiter.|例句：He spends his days hanging about the streets. 他整天在街上闲逛。|例句：Stop hanging about and get some work done! 别闲逛了，干点活！|近义词：loafing, loitering, idling, hanging around|反义词：working, toiling, bustling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are hanging about in the park. = 他们在公园里闲逛。
We hang about the cafe after school. = 我们放学后在咖啡馆闲逛。

一般过去时:
I hung about the town centre all afternoon. = 我整个下午都在镇中心闲逛。
They hung about waiting for the bus. = 他们闲逛着等公共汽车。

现在完成时:
We've been hanging about for hours. = 我们已经闲逛了好几个小时了。
He has hung about this area for years. = 他在这个地区闲逛了很多年了。

过去完成时:
They had hung about until the shops closed. = 他们一直闲逛到商店关门。
She had hung about waiting for her friend. = 她一直闲逛着等她的朋友。

将来时:
I will hang about until you come back. = 我会一直闲逛到你回来。
We will be hanging about the beach all day. = 我们整天都会在海滩上闲逛。

虚拟语气:
If I had nothing to do, I would hang about all day. = 如果我没事可做，我就会整天闲逛。
I wish I wasn't hanging about doing nothing. = 我希望我没有在闲逛无所事事。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an action done by the subject.)


疑问句:
What are you hanging about for? = 你在闲逛什么？
Are they hanging about near the school? = 他们是不是在学校附近闲逛？


否定句:
I didn't hang about after work. = 我下班后没有闲逛。
They won't be hanging about tomorrow. = 他们明天不会闲逛。
