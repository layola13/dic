coward:/ˈkaʊərd/| n. |懦夫，胆小鬼 |A coward is someone who lacks courage and is easily frightened.  Cowards often avoid difficult or dangerous situations. |例句：He acted like a coward when faced with danger. 他面对危险时表现得像个懦夫。

例句：The bully turned out to be a coward when he was confronted.  那个恶霸在被质问时露出了懦夫的本性。


近义词：poltroon, craven, dastard |反义词：hero, brave, courageous |词性：名词
