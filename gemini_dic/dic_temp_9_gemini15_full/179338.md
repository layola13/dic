redoubt's:/rɪˈdaʊts/| n.|堡垒的；防御工事的|A redoubt is a small fort, typically one that is temporary or used as part of a larger defensive system.  The word often implies a strong defensive position, even if not a fully fortified structure.|例句：The soldiers took refuge in the redoubt during the enemy attack. 士兵们在敌人的攻击中躲进了堡垒。 The redoubt's position on the hill gave them a commanding view of the battlefield.  堡垒在山上的位置使他们能够俯瞰战场。|近义词：fort, stronghold, fortification, rampart|反义词：open field, undefended area|名词
