USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have tenses like verbs.  Its usage focuses on describing how a process unfolds, specifically in a manner that is not ascending.

Example 1:
The stock prices moved nonascendently throughout the day. = 股票价格在当天非递增地波动。

Example 2:
The temperature changed nonascendently, sometimes dropping, sometimes remaining stable. = 温度变化非递增，有时下降，有时保持稳定。

词源:

The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not"), "ascend" (meaning "to rise" or "to climb"), and the adverbial suffix "-ently" (indicating manner or state).  Therefore, "nonascendently" means "not in an ascending manner".
