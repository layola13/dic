overcomplex:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːmplɛks/ | adj. |过于复杂的；过度复杂的 | excessively complex; more complicated than necessary.  The instructions were overcomplex and difficult to follow.  | 近义词：complicated, intricate, convoluted, involved | 反义词：simple, straightforward, uncomplicated


例句：

The software's design is overcomplex, leading to frequent crashes.  软件设计过于复杂，导致频繁崩溃。

His explanation of the problem was overcomplex; a simpler approach would have been better.  他对问题的解释过于复杂，更简单的办法会更好。
