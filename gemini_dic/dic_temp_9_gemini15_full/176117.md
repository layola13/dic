raze:/reɪz/| vt.|夷为平地；摧毁；拆除|To completely destroy a building, town, etc., so that nothing is left:|The town was razed to the ground during the bombing.  在轰炸中，该镇被夷为平地。|近义词：destroy, demolish, obliterate|反义词：build, construct, erect|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They raze old buildings to make way for new construction. = 他们夷平旧建筑为新的建筑腾出空间。
The company razes outdated factories every year. = 这家公司每年都拆除过时的工厂。


一般过去时:
The army razed the enemy's fortifications. = 军队夷平了敌人的防御工事。
The old house was razed last month. = 老房子上个月被拆除了。


现在完成时:
They have razed several blocks to build a new shopping mall. = 他们已经夷平了好几个街区来建造一个新的购物中心。
The developers have razed the entire neighborhood. = 开发商已经夷平了整个街区。


过去完成时:
The city had already been razed before the relief efforts began. = 在救援工作开始之前，这座城市已经被夷为平地了。
They had razed the old stadium before building the new one. = 他们在建造新的体育场之前已经拆除了旧体育场。


将来时:
They will raze the derelict buildings next year. = 他们明年将拆除这些废弃的建筑物。
The city council will raze the condemned houses soon. = 市议会很快将拆除那些被判决要拆除的房屋。


虚拟语气:
If they razed the building, they would have more space. = 如果他们拆除了那栋建筑，他们就会有更多空间。
I wish they had not razed that historical building. = 我真希望他们没有拆除那座历史建筑。


被动语态:
The old factory was razed last week. = 老工厂上周被拆除了。
Many buildings were razed during the war. = 战争期间许多建筑物被夷为平地。


疑问句:
Will they raze the park to build a highway? = 他们会为了修建高速公路而夷平公园吗？
Have they razed the old hospital yet? = 他们已经拆除旧医院了吗？


否定句:
They did not raze the building. = 他们没有拆除那栋建筑。
The city will not raze the historic district. = 这个城市不会夷平历史街区。


PHRASE->

raze objections = 消除异议
raze hopes = 打碎希望
