openabilities:/ˌoʊpənəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |开放能力；开放性；可开放性 |Openabilities refer to the capacity or potential for openness, adaptability, and flexibility.  It encompasses various aspects, including the ability to embrace new ideas, perspectives, and experiences; willingness to collaborate and communicate openly; and the capacity to adapt to change and uncertainty.  The term suggests a proactive and positive approach to openness, rather than simply being open to things passively. |The company's success is largely due to its openabilities and willingness to innovate.  公司成功很大程度上是由于其开放性和创新意愿。
