ruined:/ˈruːɪnd/| adj. v. |毁灭的；破败的；(使)破产|destroyed or damaged to such an extent that it is no longer usable or valuable;  to bring about the ruin of; to cause the failure of.|例句：The earthquake ruined the city. 地震摧毁了这座城市。The company was ruined by bad management. 公司因管理不善而破产。|近义词：destroyed, devastated, wrecked, bankrupt|反义词：saved, restored, improved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain ruins the picnic. = 雨毁了野餐。
Bad management ruins the company. = 糟糕的管理毁了公司。

一般过去时:
The storm ruined the crops. = 暴风雨毁了庄稼。
The fire ruined the house. = 大火烧毁了房子。

现在完成时:
The drought has ruined the harvest. = 干旱毁了收成。
Years of neglect have ruined the building. = 多年的忽视毁坏了这座建筑。

过去完成时:
The flood had ruined the bridge before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，洪水已经毁坏了桥梁。
The war had ruined the country before peace talks began. = 在和平谈判开始之前，战争已经摧毁了这个国家。

将来时:
The coming storm will ruin the party. = 即将来临的风暴将毁掉聚会。
The new policy will ruin the small businesses. = 新政策将毁掉小型企业。

虚拟语气:
If it rained, it would ruin the outdoor concert. = 如果下雨，它将毁掉户外音乐会。
I wish the bad weather hadn't ruined our vacation. = 我真希望糟糕的天气没有毁掉我们的假期。

被动语态:
The city was ruined by the war. = 这个城市被战争摧毁了。
The crops were ruined by the frost. = 庄稼被霜冻毁了。

疑问句:
Was the project ruined by the delay? = 项目是否因为延误而毁了？
Has the reputation of the company been ruined? = 公司的声誉是否被毁了？

否定句:
The rain didn't ruin the party. = 雨没有毁掉聚会。
The bad news didn't ruin her day. = 这个坏消息并没有毁掉她的一天。


PHRASE->
ruined city  = 废墟城市

ruined life = 毁掉的生活
ruined reputation = 毁坏的名声
ruined economy = 破产的经济
