quagmiring:/ˈkwæɡmɪrɪŋ/| v.|使陷入困境；使深陷泥沼|To be or become stuck or entangled in a difficult or precarious situation; to sink into a bog or mire.|例句：The project is quagmiring in endless bureaucratic delays.  这个项目因为无尽的官僚程序延误而陷入困境。

例句：The army was quagmiring in the mud of the battlefield.  军队陷入了战场上的泥潭。


近义词：bog down, mire, embroil, ensnare|反义词：extricate, rescue, liberate|词性：动词
