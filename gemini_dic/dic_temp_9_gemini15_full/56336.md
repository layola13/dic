dogsbodies:/ˈdɒɡzbɒdiz/| n.|下等人；卑鄙的人；受气包|A derogatory term for someone of low social status or someone who is treated badly and with contempt.  It implies being subservient and lacking respect.|例句：He was treated like a dogsbody by his boss. 他被老板当作下等人对待。|例句：They made him do all the dirty work – a real dogsbody.  他们让他做所有脏活累活——一个真正的受气包。|近义词：underling, lackey, flunky, minion|反义词：boss, superior, leader
