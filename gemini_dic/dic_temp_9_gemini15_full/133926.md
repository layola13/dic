nonhallucinatory: /ˌnɒnˌhəˌluːsɪnəˌtɔːri/ | adj. |非幻觉的，真实的| Not involving or relating to hallucinations; real or actual.  The experience was nonhallucinatory; it was a real event. |近义词：real, actual, genuine, factual |反义词：hallucinatory, illusory, unreal


例句：

The patient's description of the event was entirely nonhallucinatory, confirmed by independent witnesses. 患者对事件的描述完全是非幻觉的，得到了独立证人的证实。

His seemingly supernatural abilities were later explained as nonhallucinatory phenomena, resulting from clever stagecraft. 他看似超自然的能力后来被解释为非幻觉现象，是巧妙的舞台技巧造成的。


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **hallucin-**: from "hallucination," ultimately from Latin *hallucinari* meaning "to wander in mind," "to rave," "to be delirious."
* **-atory**: suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to," "characterized by," or "tending to."
