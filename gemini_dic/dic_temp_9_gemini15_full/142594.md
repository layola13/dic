osteoid:/ˈɒstiɔɪd/ /ˈɑːstiɔɪd/| n. |骨样组织|Unmineralized bone matrix secreted by osteoblasts, consisting primarily of collagen and ground substance. It is the organic precursor to bone. Once mineralized, it becomes bone.  |例句：Osteoid is the unmineralized organic component of bone. 骨样组织是骨骼中未矿化的有机成分。

例句：The osteoid seam is the site of new bone formation. 骨样层是新骨形成的部位。


近义词：bone matrix (骨基质), prebone (前骨)


反义词：mineralized bone (矿化骨)


词性：名词
