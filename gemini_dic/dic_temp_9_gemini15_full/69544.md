fellow:/ˈfɛloʊ/| n. adj.|伙伴；家伙；同事；同行；家伙；同辈；|A fellow is a man.  A fellow is also a member of a learned society or college.|例句：He's a good fellow. 他是个好人。 He is a fellow of the Royal Society. 他是皇家学会的会员。|近义词：companion, colleague, associate, comrade|反义词：enemy, opponent


USAGE->

一般现在时:
My fellow workers are very friendly. = 我的同事们都很友好。
That fellow always makes trouble. = 那家伙总是惹麻烦。

一般过去时:
My fellow student helped me with my homework. = 我的同学帮我做了作业。
The fellow who stole the car was caught. = 偷车的那家伙被抓住了。

现在完成时:
I have known this fellow for years. = 我认识这个家伙很多年了。
My fellows have completed the project. = 我的同事们完成了项目。

过去完成时:
I had already met my fellow traveler before the trip. = 我在旅行之前就已经见过我的同伴了。
They had never seen such a fellow before. = 他们以前从未见过这样的人。


将来时:
I will meet my fellow students tomorrow. = 我明天将与我的同学们见面。
We will invite our fellows to the party. = 我们将邀请我们的同事参加聚会。


虚拟语气:
If I were a fellow of that club, I would have access to the library. = 如果我是那个俱乐部的会员，我就可以进入图书馆。
I wish I had a fellow to share this experience with. = 我希望我有一个伙伴一起分享这段经历。


被动语态:
He was chosen as a fellow of the academy. = 他被选为学院的会员。
The fellow was severely reprimanded for his behavior. = 那家伙因为他的行为受到了严厉的训斥。


疑问句:

否定句:
