faith:/feɪθ/| n. |信任；信心；信仰；忠诚| strong belief in God or in the doctrines of a religion, strong trust or confidence in someone or something.  |例句：He had faith in his ability to succeed. 他相信自己能够成功。|例句：She has unwavering faith in her religion. 她对她的宗教有着坚定的信仰。|近义词：belief, trust, confidence, conviction|反义词：doubt, disbelief, skepticism


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She has faith in her doctor. = 她相信她的医生。
I have faith in my team. = 我相信我的团队。


一般过去时:
He had faith in his friend's honesty. = 他相信他朋友的诚实。
They had faith that their plan would work. = 他们相信他们的计划会奏效。


现在完成时:
We have had faith in this project from the start. = 从一开始我们就相信这个项目。
She has always had faith in her own abilities. = 她一直都相信自己的能力。


过去完成时:
He had had faith in the system until it failed him. = 直到系统让他失望，他才不再相信这个系统。
They had had faith in their leader, even during difficult times. = 即使在困难时期，他们也一直相信他们的领导。


将来时:
I will have faith in you, no matter what happens. = 无论发生什么，我都会相信你。
We will continue to have faith in the power of prayer. = 我们将继续相信祈祷的力量。


虚拟语气:
If I had faith, I would try harder. = 如果我有信心，我会更加努力。
I wish I had more faith in myself. = 我希望我对自己的信心更强。


被动语态:
Faith in the government is slowly being eroded. = 对政府的信任正在慢慢被侵蚀。
His faith in humanity was never betrayed. = 他对人类的信仰从未被辜负。


疑问句:
Do you have faith in the future? = 你相信未来吗？
Does he have any faith left in his friend? = 他对他朋友还有信心吗？


否定句:
I don't have faith in his promises. = 我不相信他的承诺。
She lost faith in her own abilities. = 她失去了对自身能力的信心。



PHRASE->

have faith in = 相信…
lose faith in = 对…失去信心
in good faith = 善意地
act in faith =  凭着信念行事
keep the faith = 保持信念
