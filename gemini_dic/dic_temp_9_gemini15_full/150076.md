palterer:/ˈpæltrər/| n. v.|支吾其辞者；含糊其辞者；讨价还价者 |A person who talks vaguely or evasively; one who trifles or deals in trifles;  one who haggles over prices. |例句：He was a palterer, always avoiding direct answers. 他是个支吾其辞的人，总是回避直接回答。  The shopkeeper was a notorious palterer, always trying to get more money from customers.  那个店主是个臭名昭著的讨价还价者，总是试图从顾客那里得到更多钱。|近义词：equivocator, prevaricator, shuffler, haggler|反义词：truth-teller, straightforward person
