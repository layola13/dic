dings:/dɪŋz/| n.|叮当声，敲击声|The sound of a bell or other metallic object ringing.  It can also refer to a series of short, sharp sounds.|例句：The wind chimes made soft dings in the breeze. 微风中风铃发出轻柔的叮当声。

例句：I heard a series of sharp dings coming from the kitchen. 我听到厨房传来一阵急促的叮当声。


近义词：clangs, chimes, tinkles, pings


反义词：无


词性: 名词
