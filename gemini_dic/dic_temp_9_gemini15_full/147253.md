/ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪt/ | v. |过分称赞；过高评价 | To praise or value (someone or something) too highly.  Overmerit suggests an excessive or unwarranted level of praise, often implying a lack of objective assessment. | The critic overmerited the film, ignoring its flaws.  评论家对这部电影评价过高，忽略了它的缺陷。 | overpraise, overrate, exaggerate | undervalue, underrate, downplay | 动词
