immigrants:/ˈɪmɪɡrənts/| n. |移民 |People who have come to live permanently in a foreign country.  Immigrants often bring diverse cultures, skills, and perspectives to their new homes, contributing to the economic and social fabric of their adopted country. However, the integration process can be challenging, and immigrants may face obstacles such as language barriers, cultural adjustment difficulties, and discrimination. |Example:  Many immigrants have enriched the cultural landscape of the United States. 许多移民丰富了美国的文化景象。

The influx of immigrants has boosted the local economy. 移民的涌入推动了当地经济发展。|近义词：migrants, newcomers |反义词：emigrants (those leaving a country)
