fatalism: /ˈfeɪtəlɪzəm/ | n. | 宿命论；听天由命 | The belief that all events are predetermined and inevitable; the acceptance of all things as inevitable.  It suggests a lack of control over one's destiny and a passive resignation to whatever happens. |  例句：His fatalism prevented him from even trying to improve his situation.  他的宿命论使他甚至不去尝试改善自己的处境。

例句：She accepted her illness with a weary fatalism. 她以疲惫的宿命论接受了她的疾病。


近义词：determinism, predestination, resignation, acceptance

反义词：free will, optimism, self-determination
