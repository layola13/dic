puff-ball:/ˈpʌfˌbɔːl/| n. |马勃菌；灰包菌； puffball |A puffball is a type of fungus with a round, usually white, fruiting body that releases spores when mature.  They are often found in grasslands and forests.  Some species are edible when young. |例句：I found a giant puffball mushroom in the woods. 我在树林里发现了一个巨大的马勃菌。

例句：We carefully sliced the puffball and sautéed it with garlic. 我们小心地切开马勃菌，然后用蒜炒。


近义词：fungus, mushroom (in some contexts) |反义词：N/A


词源:  The word "puffball" is a descriptive term, combining "puff," referring to the way the fungus releases its spores (like a puff of smoke), and "ball," describing its spherical shape.  The etymology is purely descriptive and doesn't trace back to a single root word in a specific language.
