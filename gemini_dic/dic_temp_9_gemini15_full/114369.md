majorly:/ˈmeɪdʒərli/| adv.|主要地，极大地，非常|The project was majorly impacted by the unexpected rain.  项目主要受突降大雨的影响。|近义词：mostly, significantly, greatly, considerably|反义词：slightly, minimally, insignificantly


USAGE->

He majorly contributes to the team's success. 他对团队的成功做出了重大贡献。
They majorly influence the market trend. 他们极大地影响着市场趋势。

The decision majorly impacted his career. 这个决定极大地影响了他的职业生涯。


现在完成时:

The team has majorly exceeded expectations. 团队已经大大超出了预期。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
