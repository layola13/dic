last-ditch:/ˈlɑːst dɪtʃ/| adj.|孤注一掷的，最后的努力|  Describes a final attempt to achieve something when all other possibilities have failed. It implies desperation and a high risk of failure. |例句：He made a last-ditch attempt to save the company from bankruptcy. 他作了最后一次努力以拯救公司免于破产。

例句：As a last-ditch effort, they decided to appeal to the Supreme Court. 作为最后一次努力，他们决定向最高法院上诉。


近义词：desperate, final, ultimate, ultimate resort

反义词：first attempt, initial effort


词性：形容词
