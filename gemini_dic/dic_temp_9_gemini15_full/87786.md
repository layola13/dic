hibernating:/ˈhaɪbərˌneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 冬眠的；蛰伏的 |  Describes an animal or plant that is in a state of dormancy during winter, characterized by reduced metabolic rate and inactivity.  | Example: The bear is hibernating in its cave. 这只熊正在洞穴里冬眠。The seeds were hibernating until the spring rains arrived. 种子一直冬眠到春雨到来。 | 近义词：dormant, inactive, sleeping | 反义词：active, awake


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bear is hibernating in its cave. = 熊正在洞穴里冬眠。
Many animals hibernate during the winter. = 许多动物在冬天冬眠。

一般过去时:
The groundhog hibernated all winter. = 土拨鼠整个冬天都在冬眠。
