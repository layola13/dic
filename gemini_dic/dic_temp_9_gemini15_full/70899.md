fire-eater: /ˈfaɪərˌiːtər/ | n. | 食火者，喷火表演者 | A person who performs the act of appearing to eat fire. This usually involves manipulating flames in the mouth and sometimes involves swallowing burning substances.  | Example: The circus featured a daring fire-eater who wowed the audience with his spectacular performance.  马戏团里有一个胆大的食火者，他精彩的表演让观众叹为观止。|近义词：fire performer, fire breather|反义词：N/A
