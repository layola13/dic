deducer:/dɪˈduːsər/| n. |推断者，推理者 |A person who deduces; one who infers conclusions from facts or premises.  |Example: The detective was a skilled deducer, able to piece together clues to solve the mystery.  这位侦探是一位熟练的推理者，能够将线索拼凑起来解开谜团。|近义词：reasoner, inferrer, analyst |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to words implying illogical thinking or guessing. |名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The detective deduces the culprit from the evidence. = 侦探从证据中推断出罪犯。
He deduces the answer from the clues provided. = 他从提供的线索中推断出答案。


一般过去时:
Sherlock Holmes deduced the thief's identity from a single footprint. = 夏洛克·福尔摩斯从一个脚印中推断出小偷的身份。
She deduced that he was lying from his nervous demeanor. = 她从他紧张的神态中推断出他在撒谎。


现在完成时:
The scientist has deduced the cause of the disease. = 科学家已经推断出这种疾病的原因。
They have deduced the solution to the problem. = 他们已经推断出问题的解决方案。


过去完成时:
Having carefully reviewed the evidence, the jury had deduced his innocence. = 仔细审查了证据后，陪审团推断出他是无辜的。
She had deduced the meaning of the cryptic message. = 她已经推断出神秘信息的含义。


将来时:
The professor will deduce the next step in the experiment. = 教授将推断出实验的下一步。
We will deduce the best course of action from the available information. = 我们将从现有信息中推断出最佳行动方案。


虚拟语气:
If I had more information, I would deduce the answer more easily. = 如果我有更多信息，我就能更容易地推断出答案。
If she were a better deducer, she would have solved the puzzle. = 如果她是一位更好的推理者，她就能解开这个谜题。


被动语态:
The solution was deduced by the team after hours of work. = 经过几个小时的工作后，团队推断出了解决方案。
The criminal's motive was deduced from his actions. = 罪犯的动机是从他的行为中推断出来的。


疑问句:
Can you deduce the meaning of this symbol? = 你能推断出这个符号的含义吗？
How did you deduce the correct answer? = 你是如何推断出正确答案的？


否定句:
I cannot deduce the answer from the given clues. = 我无法从给定的线索中推断出答案。
He failed to deduce the true nature of the situation. = 他未能推断出情况的真相。
