invests:/ɪnˈvɛsts/| vt.|投资；投入；赋予…权力|To put money into something with the expectation of achieving a profit or gain.|例句：He invests heavily in the stock market. 他大量投资股市。|例句：The company invests in its employees' training. 公司投资员工培训。|近义词：put in, commit, place|反义词：withdraw, divest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He invests in real estate. = 他投资房地产。
She invests her time in her children. = 她把时间花在她孩子身上。

一般过去时:
They invested in a new business last year. = 他们去年投资了一家新公司。
He invested all his savings in the project. = 他把所有积蓄都投资到这个项目中了。

现在完成时:
She has invested wisely over the years. = 这些年来她投资得很明智。
They have invested a significant amount of money in research and development. = 他们已经投入了大量的资金用于研发。

过去完成时:
He had invested heavily in the company before it went bankrupt. = 在公司破产之前，他已大量投资于该公司。
They had invested all their money before the market crashed. = 市场崩盘之前，他们已经把所有的钱都投资出去了。

将来时:
We will invest in renewable energy sources. = 我们将投资可再生能源。
She will invest more in her education. = 她将更多地投资于她的教育。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would invest in a new car. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会投资买一辆新车。
I wish I had invested in that company. = 我真希望我当时投资了那家公司。

被动语态:
A large sum of money was invested in the project. = 大笔资金投资于该项目。
Millions of dollars are being invested in infrastructure development. = 数百万美元正被投资于基础设施建设。

疑问句:
Where do you invest your money? = 你把钱投资在哪里？
What are they investing in? = 他们在投资什么？

否定句:
He doesn't invest in risky ventures. = 他不投资高风险的企业。
She didn't invest in the stock market. = 她没有投资股市。


PHRASE->
invest in = 投资于
invest time/effort = 投入时间/精力
invest money = 投资资金
invest heavily = 大量投资
invest wisely = 投资明智
