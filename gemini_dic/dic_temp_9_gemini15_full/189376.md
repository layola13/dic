This process is often used to improve the quality and quantity of meat produced.|例句：The farmer engaged in intensive sagination to prepare his pigs for market.  这个农民进行集约化肥育，为将他的猪送去市场做准备。|近义词：fattening, feeding, fleshing|反义词：starvation, emaciation|名词
