revile:/rɪˈvaɪl/| vt.|辱骂；痛斥；斥责|To criticize or speak abusively to someone.  To assail with abusive language; berate.|例句：The angry mob reviled the politician for his unpopular policies.  愤怒的暴民因其不得人心的政策而痛斥这位政治家。

例句：He was reviled by the press for his scandalous behavior. 他因其丑闻行为而受到媒体的痛斥。


近义词：abuse, berate, condemn, denounce, lambaste, reproach|反义词：praise, commend, applaud|动词
