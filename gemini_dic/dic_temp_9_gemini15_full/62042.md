encamping:/ɛnˈkæmpɪŋ/| v. |宿营；扎营|The act of setting up a camp, typically outdoors for a temporary period. It involves pitching tents, preparing a sleeping area, and setting up other necessary facilities.  |例句：The scouts were encamping by the lake.  童子军在湖边扎营。

例句：After a long day's hike, we encamping in a clearing in the woods.  长途跋涉一天后，我们在树林里的空地上扎营。


近义词：camping, pitching camp, setting up camp |反义词：departing, leaving camp, moving on

词性：动词
