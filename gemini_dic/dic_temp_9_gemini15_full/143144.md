outcavilling: /ˌaʊtˈkævɪlɪŋ/ | v. |  大声争论；超过…的争论；驳倒 |  To surpass in argument; to overpower in a verbal contest.  It implies a noisy and forceful refutation. |  例句：He outcavilled his opponent with a torrent of well-researched facts. 他以大量翔实的证据驳倒了他的对手。

例句：The lawyer attempted to outcavil the witness's testimony, but ultimately failed. 律师试图驳斥证人的证词，但最终失败了。


近义词：outargue,  refute,  contradict,  gainsay

反义词：concede, acquiesce, agree


词性：动词
