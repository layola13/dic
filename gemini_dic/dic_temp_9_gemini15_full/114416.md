make-over:/ˈmeɪkˌoʊvər/ | n. |改头换面；彻底改造；翻新 | A makeover is a significant change or improvement to the appearance or condition of something, typically involving a transformation or renovation.  It often refers to changes in a person's appearance, such as a new hairstyle, makeup, or clothing, but can also apply to objects, places, or even systems. |例句：She got a complete makeover before the wedding. 她婚前彻底改头换面了一番。

例句：The old house underwent a major makeover. 这栋旧房子进行了大规模的翻新。


近义词：transformation, renovation, revamp, refurbishment, redecoration | 反义词：degradation, deterioration, decline
