de-stressing:/ˌdiːˈstrɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 解压的；减压的 |  Reducing or relieving stress.  The process or activity of de-stressing involves techniques and activities aimed at lowering stress levels and promoting relaxation. | Example:  She finds yoga a very de-stressing activity. 她发现瑜伽是一种非常减压的活动。 |近义词：relaxing, calming, unwinding |反义词：stressing, taxing, demanding
