reactional: /rɪˈækʃənəl/ | adj. | 反动的；保守的；过敏的 | Relating to or characterized by reaction; tending to oppose change or progress.  |  例句：His reactional views on social issues alienated many of his colleagues.  他的社会问题上的保守观点疏远了他的许多同事。

例句：She suffered a reactional skin rash after using the new cream. 使用新面霜后，她出现了过敏性皮疹。


近义词：conservative, retrograde, resistant, allergic


反义词：progressive, liberal, forward-thinking, tolerant


词源:  Derived from "reaction,"  itself from the Latin "reactio," meaning "an acting back, recoil." The "-al" suffix denotes an adjective.  The word gained its political connotation in the 19th and 20th centuries, associated with opposition to revolutionary or progressive movements.
