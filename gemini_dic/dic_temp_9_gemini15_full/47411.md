deadline:/ˈdedlaɪn/| n. |最后期限，截止日期 | a date or time by which something must be completed.  |The deadline for the project is Friday.  这个项目的截止日期是星期五。|  cutoff, limit, expiry date | extension, postponement |名词


USAGE->

The deadline is next week. = 最后期限是下周。
What is the deadline for this assignment? = 这项作业的截止日期是什么时候？


一般过去时:
The deadline was yesterday. = 截止日期是昨天。
