renouncements:/rɪˈnaʊnsmənts/ | n. | 弃权；放弃；声明放弃 | The act of renouncing something; a formal statement of giving something up.  | Example: The company's renouncements of its environmentally damaging practices were met with skepticism. 公司放弃其破坏环境的做法的声明受到了怀疑。  | 近义词：abandonment, relinquishment, repudiation | 反义词：acceptance, retention | 名词
