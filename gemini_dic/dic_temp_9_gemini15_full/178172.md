recollet:/ˌrekəˈlekt/ | v. | 回忆，想起 | To remember something from the past.  |例句：I can't recollect his name. 我记不起他的名字了。|例句：She tried to recollect the details of the accident. 她试图回忆起事故的细节。|近义词：remember, recall, remind |反义词：forget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I recollect my childhood often. = 我经常回忆我的童年。
Do you recollect the address? = 你还记得地址吗？


一般过去时:
He recollected the story vividly. = 他生动地回忆起了那个故事。
She recollected having seen him before. = 她回忆起以前见过他。


现在完成时:
I have recollected many happy memories. = 我已经回忆起许多快乐的回忆。
Have you recollected where you left your keys? = 你想起来把钥匙放在哪里了吗？


过去完成时:
He recollected that he had already told me. = 他回忆起他已经告诉过我了。
She recollected that she had promised to call. = 她想起来她答应过要打电话。


将来时:
I will recollect this day for years to come. = 未来几年我都会想起这一天。
Will you be able to recollect the information by tomorrow? = 你明天能想起这些信息吗？


虚拟语气:
If I could recollect the details, I would tell you. = 如果我能想起细节，我会告诉你的。
I wish I could recollect his name. = 我真希望我能想起他的名字。


被动语态:
The details of the meeting were recollected by the secretary. = 会议的细节由秘书回忆起来了。
His childhood memories were often recollected by him. = 他经常回忆起他的童年记忆。


疑问句:
Do you recollect what happened? = 你还记得发生了什么事吗？
Can you recollect the password? = 你能想起密码吗？


否定句:
I can't recollect what he said. = 我记不起他说过什么。
She didn't recollect seeing him that day. = 她不记得那天见过他。


PHRASE->
