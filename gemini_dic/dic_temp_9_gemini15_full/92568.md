hysterotomy:/ˌhɪstəˈrɒtəmi/ | n. | 子宫切开术 | A surgical incision into the uterus.  This procedure is typically performed for various reasons, including the removal of a fetus (in cases of a difficult or dangerous pregnancy) or the treatment of certain uterine conditions. | Example: The hysterotomy allowed for the safe removal of the fetus.  子宫切开术使胎儿能够安全取出。  The doctor recommended a hysterotomy to address the uterine fibroids. 医生建议进行子宫切开术来治疗子宫肌瘤。 | 近义词: Cesarean section (in the context of fetal removal), uterine incision | 反义词:  hysterectomy (removal of the uterus) | 名词
