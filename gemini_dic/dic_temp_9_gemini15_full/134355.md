noninterchangeability: /ˌnɒnɪntəˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可互换性 | The inability of two or more things to be exchanged or substituted for one another without affecting the outcome.  This refers to a situation where different elements cannot be used interchangeably because they have unique properties or functions that cannot be replicated.  | Example: The noninterchangeability of the parts in this engine means that only the correct part can be used for repair.  这个引擎零件的不可互换性意味着只能使用正确的零件进行修理。 | 近义词：indispensability, uniqueness, irreplaceability | 反义词：interchangeability, substitutability |  名词
