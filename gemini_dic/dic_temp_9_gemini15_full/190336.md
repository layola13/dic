sarcophagus:/sɑːrˈkɒfəɡəs/| n. |石棺；(比喻)坟墓|A sarcophagus is a stone coffin, often ornately decorated, typically used for important people.  It is sometimes used metaphorically to refer to something that entombs or contains something else.|Example: The ancient Egyptian sarcophagus was beautifully carved with hieroglyphs.  这个古埃及石棺上精美的雕刻着象形文字。The old house felt like a sarcophagus, trapping her in its gloom. 这栋老房子感觉像个石棺，把她困在阴暗中。|近义词：coffin, tomb|反义词：N/A
