camper:/ˈkæmpər/ | n. |露营者；露营车 | A person who camps, especially as a hobby.  A motor vehicle equipped for camping. |例句：The campers built a fire to keep warm. 露营者们生火取暖。  We rented a camper van for our road trip. 我们租了一辆露营车进行公路旅行。|近义词：campist, tent dweller (for person), RV, caravan (for vehicle) |反义词：homebody, indoor person (for person),  hotel, motel (for vehicle)
