re-bating:/ˌriːˈbeɪtɪŋ/| v. |回扣；返还；折扣；减免|The act of rebating, or giving back a portion of a payment.  It often refers to a refund or discount given after a purchase or transaction. This can apply to various contexts, such as insurance premiums, taxes, or sales.|例句：The company offered a rebating of 10% on bulk orders. 公司对批量订单提供10%的回扣。|例句：After a successful negotiation, they secured a rebating on the late delivery fee.  经过成功的谈判，他们获得了延迟送货费用的退款。|近义词：refund, discount, rebate, repayment|反义词：surcharge, penalty, upcharge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company rebates customers for early payment. = 公司对提前付款的客户给予回扣。
He rebates a portion of his earnings to his parents. = 他将部分收入返还给父母。


一般过去时:
They rebated the extra charges after the complaint. = 投诉后，他们退还了额外费用。
The store rebated the faulty product's price. = 商店退还了有缺陷产品的价格。


现在完成时:
We have rebated all the overpaid taxes. = 我们已经退还了所有多交的税款。
She has rebated the interest on the loan. = 她已经退还了贷款利息。


过去完成时:
They had rebated the money before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经退还了钱。
The government had rebated the fuel tax earlier this year. = 政府在今年早些时候已经退还了燃油税。


将来时:

The company will rebate the customers for any damage. = 公司将对客户的任何损失进行赔偿。


虚拟语气:
If the customer was unhappy, they would rebate the full amount. = 如果顾客不满意，他们会全额退款。
I wish they would rebate the service charge. = 我希望他们能退还服务费。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
