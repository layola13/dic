invocation's:/ˌɪnvəˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |祈求；祈祷；调用；恳求 |The plural form of "invocation," referring to an act of invoking or calling upon something or someone, often a deity or a spirit, for help or guidance.  It can also refer to the act of calling upon a function or subroutine in a computer program. |例句：The ceremony began with invocations to the gods for a bountiful harvest.  仪式以祈求丰收的祭祀开始。

The programmer used invocations to access the database. 程序员使用调用来访问数据库。|近义词：prayer, supplication, appeal, call, summoning |反义词：rejection, denial, ignoring
