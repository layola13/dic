outgun:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌn/| vt.|火力压倒，火力胜过|To surpass in firepower or weaponry.|The rebels were outgunned by the government forces. 反政府武装被政府军火力压制。|近义词：outmatch, overpower, overwhelm|反义词：be outmatched by, be overpowered by


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The navy outguns the air force in this region. = 在这个地区，海军火力胜过空军。
Our team consistently outguns its opponents. = 我们的队伍总是火力压倒对手。


一般过去时:
The army outgunned the insurgents in the battle. = 在战斗中，军队火力压制了叛乱分子。
The pirates were outgunned by the naval vessel. = 海盗被军舰火力压制。


现在完成时:
The terrorists have been outgunned by the police in recent operations. = 在最近的行动中，恐怖分子已被警方火力压制。
Our artillery has outgunned the enemy throughout the campaign. = 在整个战役中，我们的炮兵火力一直压制着敌人。


过去完成时:
The rebels had been outgunned by the time reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达时，叛军已经被火力压制了。
The defenders had been thoroughly outgunned before the siege ended. = 在围城结束之前，守军已经被彻底火力压制了。


将来时:
We will outgun the enemy with our new weapons systems. = 我们将用我们的新型武器系统火力压制敌人。
They expect to be outgunned in the upcoming conflict. = 他们预计在即将到来的冲突中会被火力压制。


虚拟语气:
If we had more advanced weaponry, we would outgun the opposition. = 如果我们有更先进的武器，我们将火力压制对手。
I wish our forces could outgun the enemy so decisively. = 我希望我们的部队能够如此果断地火力压制敌人。


被动语态:
The insurgents were outgunned in the clash. = 叛乱分子在冲突中被火力压制。
The smaller nation was easily outgunned by its larger neighbor. = 小国很容易被其较大的邻国火力压制。


疑问句:
Were the rebels outgunned in the conflict? = 叛乱分子在冲突中是否被火力压制？
Will our forces be outgunned in the upcoming battle? = 我们的部队在即将到来的战斗中会被火力压制吗？


否定句:
The rebels were not outgunned; they simply lacked strategy. = 叛乱分子并没有被火力压制；他们只是缺乏策略。
The militia was not outgunned, but they were outnumbered. = 民兵没有被火力压制，但他们人数少。
