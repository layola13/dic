dwarfness:/ˈdwɔːrfnəs/ | n. | 矮小；侏儒症 | The condition of being a dwarf; abnormally small stature.  This can be caused by genetic disorders or hormonal imbalances. | Example:  His dwarfness was a result of a rare genetic condition. 他的矮小是由于一种罕见的基因疾病造成的。|近义词：shortness, smallness, littleness |反义词：tallness, giantism
