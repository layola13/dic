refits:/ˌriːˈfɪts/| vt.|改装，修理；重新装备|To repair and renovate (something), especially a ship or aircraft, to make it fit for use again.  It also implies significant upgrades or improvements beyond simple repair.|例句：The company refitted the old factory to meet modern safety standards. 公司改装了旧工厂以符合现代安全标准。

The ship underwent a major refit before its next voyage. 这艘船在下次航行前进行了大规模的改装。


近义词：renovate, overhaul, refurbish, revamp|反义词：demolish, destroy, wreck|动词
