mizens:/ˈmɪzən/ | n. |mizzen帆；后帆| The mizzen is a sail on the mizzen mast, a mast located aft (towards the stern) of the main mast on a sailing ship.  It's typically smaller than the mainsail and helps steer the ship and control its speed, particularly when sailing close-hauled.  |The captain ordered the mizzen to be furled before the storm hit.|近义词：aftersail, spanker|反义词：foresails
