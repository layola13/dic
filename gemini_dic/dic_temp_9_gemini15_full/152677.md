peal:/piːl/| n. v.|响亮的铃声；大声疾呼；剥皮；|a loud ringing sound, especially of bells; a loud cry; to ring loudly; to remove the skin of fruit or vegetables|The church bells pealed merrily. 教堂的钟声愉快地鸣响。  The crowd pealed with laughter. 人群爆发出笑声。|近义词：ring, clang, peel (v.)|反义词：silence, whisper
