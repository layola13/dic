phthalimide:/ˈθælɪmaɪd/| n. |邻苯二甲酰亚胺|A cyclic imide derived from phthalic acid. It is a white crystalline powder used in the synthesis of various organic compounds, including pharmaceuticals and dyes.  Phthalimide is also known for its use in the Gabriel synthesis, a method for synthesizing primary amines.|Phthalimide is used as a precursor in the synthesis of many important compounds.|近义词：N-phthalimide (a more specific term)|反义词：无
