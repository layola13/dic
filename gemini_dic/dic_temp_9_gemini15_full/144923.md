over-there:/ˌoʊvərˈðɛr/ | adv. | 在那边，那里 |  An adverb indicating a location that is visible but at a distance from the speaker. It is often used in informal conversation. | Example: The books are over there on the shelf. 书在那边书架上。 | 近义词: there, yonder | 反义词: here, over here


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is sleeping over there. = 猫在那里睡觉。
The party is over there in the garden. = 派对在花园那边。


一般过去时:
The car was parked over there. = 车停在那儿。
He saw her standing over there. = 他看见她站在那边。


现在完成时:
They have put the chairs over there. = 他们把椅子放在那边了。
The children have been playing over there all afternoon. = 孩子们整个下午都在那边玩。


过去完成时:
The dog had already gone over there before I called him. = 我叫它之前，狗已经跑到那边去了。
She had left her bag over there. = 她把包落在那边了。


将来时:
I will put the flowers over there. = 我要把花放在那边。
We will meet over there tomorrow.  = 我们明天在那儿见面。


虚拟语气:
If the shop were over there, it would be more convenient. = 如果商店在那儿，就会更方便。
I wish the restaurant were over there. = 我希望餐馆在那儿。


被动语态:
The boxes were left over there. = 箱子被留在那边了。
The toys are being stored over there. = 玩具正在那边被储存。


疑问句:
Is the restroom over there? = 洗手间在那儿吗？
Are the tools over there? = 工具在那儿吗？


否定句:
The dog isn't over there; it's in the house. = 狗不在那边；它在房子里。
The meeting isn't over there, it's in the conference room. = 会议不在那边，在会议室。



PHRASE->
look over there = 看那边
go over there = 去那边
walk over there = 走过去那边
sit over there = 坐在那边
come over there = 来这边 (although grammatically 'there' implies a distance from the speaker)
