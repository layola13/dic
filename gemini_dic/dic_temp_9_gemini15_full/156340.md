physics:/ˈfɪzɪks/| n. |物理学|The science that deals with the structure of matter and how it behaves under various conditions, including its motion, energy, and forces.  It encompasses various branches such as mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, and quantum mechanics. |例句：He is majoring in physics at university. 他在大学主修物理学。|例句：The laws of physics govern the universe. 物理定律支配着宇宙。|近义词：physical science|反义词：None
