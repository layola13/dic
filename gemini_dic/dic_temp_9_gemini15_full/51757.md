devoted:/dɪˈvoʊtɪd/ | adj. | 献身的；忠诚的；热爱的 |  Devoted means showing great love, affection, or loyalty. It implies a strong commitment and dedication to a person, cause, or activity. |例句：She is a devoted mother who always puts her children first. 她是一位慈爱的母亲，总是把孩子放在第一位。

She's devoted to her work and puts in long hours. 她全身心地投入工作，经常加班。

近义词：dedicated, committed, loyal, faithful | 反义词：uncaring, indifferent, apathetic
