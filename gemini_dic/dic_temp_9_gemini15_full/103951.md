judicialness: /ˌdʒuːdɪʃəlˈnəs/ | n. | 公正性；司法性 | The quality of being judicial; fairness; impartiality in judgment.  It suggests a careful and considered approach to decision-making, based on established principles and laws, rather than personal biases or emotions. | The judge's judicialness was evident in his careful consideration of all evidence presented.  法官的公正性体现在他对所有呈堂证供的仔细审理中。| fairness, impartiality, equity | bias, prejudice, partiality | 名词
