motherboards:/ˈmʌðərbɔːrdz/| n. |主板|A motherboard is the main printed circuit board (PCB) in a computer. It holds and allows communication between many crucial electronic components of a system, such as the central processing unit (CPU), memory, and storage. |例句：My computer's motherboard needs replacing. 我的电脑主板需要更换。

例句：The new motherboard significantly improved the computer's performance. 新的主板显著提高了电脑的性能。


近义词：mainboard, system board |反义词: N/A

词源:
The word "motherboard" is a compound word.  "Mother" refers to its central and primary role in the computer system, similar to a mother's role in a family. "Board" refers to the flat printed circuit board itself.  The term evolved naturally from the technology's development, reflecting its central function.
