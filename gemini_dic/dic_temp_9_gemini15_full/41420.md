cornercap: /ˈkɔːrnərˌkæp/ | n. | 角帽；拐角盖板 | A cornercap is a decorative or protective covering placed on the corner of an object, often furniture or architectural features. It can be made of various materials like wood, metal, or plastic.  | Example: The antique chair had beautifully carved cornercaps. 这把古董椅子有精美的雕花角帽。 | 近义词：corner piece, angle cap | 反义词：无
