fabricator:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtər/| n. |制造商；伪造者；捏造者|A fabricator is a person or company that manufactures goods, often from raw materials, or constructs something.  It can also refer to someone who creates something false or untrue.  |例句：The company is a leading fabricator of steel structures. 这家公司是领先的钢结构制造商。

例句：He was accused of fabricating evidence to win the case. 他被指控伪造证据以赢得官司。


近义词：manufacturer, producer, constructor, falsifier, forger

反义词：deconstructor, destroyer (in the context of manufacturing), truth-teller (in the context of fabrication)

词性:名词
