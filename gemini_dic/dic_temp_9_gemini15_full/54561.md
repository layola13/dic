dislodge:/dɪsˈlɒdʒ/ | /dɪsˈlɑːdʒ/| vt. |驱逐；移位；使脱落| To force or knock (something) out of its position.  |例句：The earthquake dislodged rocks from the mountainside. 地震使山坡上的岩石松动脱落。

例句：He used a crowbar to dislodge the stubborn stone. 他用撬棍撬动那块顽固的石头。


近义词：remove, displace, dislocate, oust


反义词：lodge, secure, fasten, implant


USAGE->

The wind dislodges leaves from the trees. = 风把树叶吹落。
