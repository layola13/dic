词源:
The word "pyrotechnical" is derived from the Greek words "pyr" (πῦρ), meaning "fire," and "techne" (τέχνη), meaning "art" or "skill."  The suffix "-ical" indicates an adjective form.  Therefore, "pyrotechnical" literally means "relating to the art or skill of fire."
