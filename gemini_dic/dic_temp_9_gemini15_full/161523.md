pother:/ˈpɒðə(r)/| /ˈpɑːðər/| n. v.|喧嚣；忙乱；扰乱；炖；煨|

英文解析：A pother is a fuss, commotion, or bustle. It can also refer to the act of making a fuss or commotion, or to a stew or a dish cooked by stewing.  As a verb, it means to fuss or bustle about.

例句：
The children made a terrible pother getting ready for the school trip. = 孩子们为学校旅行做准备时大吵大闹。

There was a great pother in the kitchen as everyone prepared for the feast. = 大家为宴会做准备时，厨房里一片混乱。


近义词：fuss, commotion, bustle, ado, stir, turmoil, stew (as a noun)

反义词：calm, peace, quiet, tranquility


词性：Noun, Verb
