disturbs:/dɪˈstɜːrbz/ | vt. |打扰；扰乱；使不安 | To interrupt the peace, quiet, or order of; to cause anxiety or concern to. |例句：The noise disturbed my sleep.  噪音扰乱了我的睡眠。|例句：The news disturbed her deeply.  这个消息使她深感不安。|近义词：interrupt, bother, unsettle, trouble |反义词：calm, soothe, pacify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog disturbs the neighbors every night. = 这条狗每晚都打扰邻居。
Loud music disturbs my concentration. = 吵闹的音乐扰乱了我的注意力。


一般过去时:
The storm disturbed the peace of the countryside. = 暴风雨扰乱了乡村的宁静。
The unexpected news disturbed him greatly. = 意外的消息使他非常不安。


现在完成时:
The construction work has disturbed the local residents. = 建设工程已经扰乱了当地居民的生活。
Nothing has disturbed me all day. = 一整天都没有什么事打搅我。


过去完成时:
The loud noise had disturbed their sleep. = 吵闹声已经扰乱了他们的睡眠。
I had already been disturbed by several phone calls before the meeting. = 会议前我已经接了好几个电话，被打扰了好几次。


将来时:
The new highway will disturb the natural habitat. = 新高速公路将扰乱自然栖息地。
I think the bad weather will disturb our plans. = 我想坏天气会打乱我们的计划。


虚拟语气:
If the noise didn't disturb me, I would have slept soundly. = 如果噪音没有打扰我，我就睡得很香了。
I wish the traffic wouldn't disturb my work. = 我希望交通不会影响我的工作。


被动语态:
His sleep was disturbed by the loud music. = 他的睡眠被吵闹的音乐扰乱了。
The meeting was disturbed by an unexpected visitor. = 会议被一位不速之客打断了。


疑问句:
Does the noise disturb you? = 噪音打扰你了吗？
What disturbed you so much? = 什么事让你这么不安？


否定句:
The quiet atmosphere didn't disturb him. = 安静的气氛没有打扰他。
The news didn't disturb her as much as she had expected. = 消息并没有像她预期的那样让她不安。
