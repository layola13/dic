coot:/kuːt/| n.| 潜鸟；笨蛋；傻瓜 |A coot is a type of waterbird with lobed toes.  The term can also be used as a derogatory term for a foolish or stupid person. |例句：We saw a coot swimming on the lake. 我们看到一只潜鸟在湖里游泳。|例句：Don't be such a coot! 别那么傻！|近义词：fool, idiot, dunce |反义词：genius, intelligent
