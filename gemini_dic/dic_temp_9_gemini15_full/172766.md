quadri: /ˈkwɑːdraɪ/ |prefix| 四；四个；四倍的 |A combining form meaning "four," used in the formation of compound words.  It is derived from Latin "quattuor" meaning "four".|  The word "quadriplegic" combines "quadri" with "plegic" (paralyzed) to describe someone paralyzed in all four limbs. |近义词:tetra-,quart-|反义词:uni-,bi-,tri-
