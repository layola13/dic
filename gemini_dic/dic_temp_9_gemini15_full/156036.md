photomicrograph:/ˈfoʊtoʊˌmaɪkrəˌɡræf/ | n. |显微照片，显微摄影 | A photograph of something seen through a microscope.  It is a visual record of a microscopic specimen, magnified to show details invisible to the naked eye. | Example: The photomicrograph clearly showed the bacteria's structure. 这张显微照片清楚地显示了细菌的结构。


The photomicrograph revealed intricate details of the cell's organelles. 这张显微照片揭示了细胞器复杂的细节。|近义词：microphotograph |反义词：macrophotograph
