skimmed:/skɪmd/ | v. |略读；撇去（液体表面的）;去除…的表面 | To read something quickly in order to get a general understanding, without reading every word.  Also to remove something from the surface of a liquid. | 例句：I skimmed the article before deciding to read it more carefully. 我快速浏览了这篇文章，然后再决定是否仔细阅读。


He skimmed the cream off the top of the milk. 他撇去了牛奶表面的奶油。 | 近义词：scan, glance, peruse, skim over | 反义词：read thoroughly, pore over


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He skims the newspaper every morning. = 他每天早上浏览报纸。
She skims the surface of the soup before serving. = 上菜前她撇去汤的表面浮沫。


一般过去时:
