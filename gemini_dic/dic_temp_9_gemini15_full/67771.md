eyelash:/ˈaɪlæʃ/| n. |睫毛|A short hair growing on the edge of the eyelid.  Eyelashes protect the eyes from dust and other small particles.|例句：She has long, dark eyelashes. 她有又长又黑的睫毛。|例句：He carefully applied mascara to his eyelashes. 他仔细地在他的睫毛上涂抹睫毛膏。|近义词：eyelash hair|反义词:None
