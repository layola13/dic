sedimented:/ˈsɛdəmɛntɪd/| adj.|沉淀的；沉积的|Having been deposited as sediment.|The sedimented layer contained fossils.  沉积层含有化石。|  deposited, precipitated | floating, suspended |形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river sediments constantly. 这条河一直在沉积泥沙。
Sediments accumulate on the ocean floor. 沉积物在海底堆积。


一般过去时:
The particles sedimented quickly. 颗粒迅速沉淀。
The lake sedimented over time. 湖泊随着时间的推移而沉积。


现在完成时:
Fine sediments have accumulated over millennia.  细小的沉积物已经积累了数千年。
Much sediment has been deposited in the delta. 三角洲沉积了大量的泥沙。


过去完成时:
The water had already sedimented before the test. 在测试之前，水已经沉淀了。
The sediment had settled at the bottom of the container. 沉淀物已经沉淀在容器底部。


将来时:
The particles will sediment out of the solution. 颗粒将从溶液中沉淀出来。
More sediment will accumulate in the reservoir. 水库将积累更多的沉积物。


虚拟语气:
If the solution were left undisturbed, it would sediment. 如果溶液保持不动，它就会沉淀。
If the particles had been larger, they would have sedimented faster. 如果颗粒更大，它们沉淀速度会更快。


被动语态:
The sediment was carefully collected. 沉淀物被小心地收集起来。
The sample was sedimented by centrifugation. 样品通过离心沉淀。


疑问句:
Has the sediment settled completely? 沉淀物完全沉淀了吗？
Were the samples adequately sedimented? 样品是否充分沉淀？


否定句:
The particles did not sediment properly. 颗粒没有正确沉淀。
The solution did not sediment completely. 溶液没有完全沉淀。
