pre-compresses:/ˌpriːkəmˈprɛsɪz/| v.|预压缩|To compress beforehand; to compress in advance.  This term is often used in the context of data compression, image processing, or other scenarios where data is compressed before further processing or storage. |例句：The software pre-compresses the video files before uploading them to the cloud.  软件在将视频文件上传到云端之前会先对其进行压缩。

The image was pre-compressed to reduce its file size before emailing. 这张图片在发邮件之前进行了预压缩以减小文件大小。|近义词：pre-process, compress beforehand, pre-encode |反义词：decompress, expand
