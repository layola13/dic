polysemies:/pɒliˈsiːmiːz/| n. |多义性；一词多义|The existence of multiple meanings for a single word or phrase.  This is a common linguistic phenomenon, and understanding the context is crucial for disambiguating the intended meaning. For example, the word "bank" can refer to a financial institution or the side of a river.  The specific meaning is determined by the surrounding words and the overall situation.|例句：The polysemies of the word "bright" can lead to confusion if not considered carefully.  多义词“bright”的多种含义如果不仔细考虑可能会导致混淆。|近义词：ambiguity, multiple meanings, semantic ambiguity|反义词：unambiguity, monosémie|名词
