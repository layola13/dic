opercula: /ɒˈpɜːrkjʊlə/ | n. | 鳃盖；帽盖；盖子 |  A operculum is a structure that closes an opening, particularly the bony flap covering the gills of a fish.  It can also refer to other similar structures in various organisms.  | Example: The fish closed its opercula to protect its gills.  这条鱼闭上了鳃盖来保护它的鳃。 |近义词：lid, cover, flap | 反义词：opening
