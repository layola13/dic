corers:/ˈkɔːrərz/| n.|去核器；采芯器|A corer is a tool used to remove the core or center part of something, often fruits or vegetables.|例句：We used a corer to remove the cores from the apples before making applesauce. 我们在做苹果酱之前用去核器去掉了苹果核。|近义词：corer, pitter|反义词：无

She cores the fruit before making pies.  她在做馅饼之前去果核。
