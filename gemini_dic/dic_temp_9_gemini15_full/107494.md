lances:/ˈlænsɪz/| n.|长矛；枪；攻击|Long, pointed weapons used for thrusting, often used in warfare or hunting.  They can also refer to a forceful attack or criticism.|例句：The knights charged into battle, their lances lowered.  骑士们冲入战斗，长矛低垂。|例句：His words were like lances, piercing her heart.  他的话如同长矛，刺痛了她的心。|近义词：spears, javelins, darts|反义词：shields, defenses
