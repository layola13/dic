classicism:/ˈklæsɪsɪzəm/| n. |古典主义；古典风格|The adherence to the principles and styles of classical antiquity, especially in literature, art, or architecture.  It emphasizes order, balance, harmony, and restraint.  Classicism often contrasts with romanticism.|Example: The building's classicism is evident in its symmetrical facade and use of columns. 这座建筑的古典主义风格在其对称的正面和柱子的使用中显而易见。|近义词：classic style, traditionalism|反义词：romanticism, modernism
