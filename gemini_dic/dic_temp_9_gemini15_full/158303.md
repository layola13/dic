playgoer:/ˈpleɪɡəʊər/| n. |戏迷；常看戏的人|A playgoer is a person who frequently attends theatrical performances.  They are often knowledgeable about theatre and enjoy various aspects of the performing arts. |She is a dedicated playgoer, attending at least one show a week. 她是一位忠实的戏迷，每周至少看一场演出。 He's a discerning playgoer with a keen eye for talent. 他是一位眼光独到的戏迷，对人才有敏锐的洞察力。| theatregoer, audience member | non-playgoer
