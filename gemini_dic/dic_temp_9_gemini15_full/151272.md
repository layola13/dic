paresis:/pəˈriːsɪs/| n.|轻瘫, 瘫痪|Partial or incomplete paralysis.  It often results from damage to the nervous system.  The term can refer to various conditions affecting different parts of the body. |例句：The stroke left him with paresis in his left arm. 中风导致他的左臂轻瘫。

例句：She experienced facial paresis after the Bell's palsy diagnosis.  贝尔氏麻痹诊断后，她出现了面瘫。


近义词：paralysis, palsy, weakness|反义词：full function, strength|词性:名词
