romanticisms:/rəʊˈmænɪsɪzəm/| n. |浪漫主义；浪漫主义思潮 |The body of ideas characteristic of the Romantic movement.  Romanticisms encompass various aspects of art, literature, music, and philosophy emphasizing emotion, imagination, individualism, and nature.  |例句：The paintings are a good example of the romanticisms of the era.  这些画作是那个时代浪漫主义的良好范例。 |近义词：romanticism, idealism, sentimentality |反义词：classicism, realism
