overspill:/ˈoʊvərspɪl/| n. |溢出；外溢；过剩；外流人口|The overflow of something, especially liquid, over the edge of its container; something that overflows or extends beyond its normal limits.  It can refer to a surplus, excess, or the migration of people from a congested area to surrounding areas.|例句：The river overspill flooded the nearby fields.  河水泛滥淹没了附近的田地。

例句：The success of the event caused an overspill of people into the adjacent streets. 活动的成功导致人群溢出到邻近的街道。


近义词：overflow, surplus, excess, spillover

反义词：deficit, shortage, undersupply


词性：名词
