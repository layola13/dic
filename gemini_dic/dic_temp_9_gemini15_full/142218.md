ornithomancy:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒmənsi/ | n. |鸟占卜；观鸟占卜 |The practice of divination by observing the flight, songs, or other behaviors of birds.  It is an ancient form of divination believed to provide insights into the future or divine will. | Example:  Ancient cultures practiced ornithomancy to predict the outcome of battles. 古代文化通过鸟占卜来预测战争的结果。|近义词：augury, divination, bird lore |反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would be a method of fortune-telling that *doesn't* involve birds.
