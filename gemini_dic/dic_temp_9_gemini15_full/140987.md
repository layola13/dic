loneliness:/ˈloʊnlinəs/ | n. |孤独；寂寞 |The feeling of being lonely; a state of being alone and often unhappy.  Loneliness is a common human experience, but it can be particularly challenging for some individuals.  |例句：The loneliness of the long winter nights was almost unbearable. 冬天漫漫长夜的孤独几乎让人难以忍受。

例句：He felt a deep sense of loneliness after his wife passed away.  妻子去世后，他感到深深的孤独。


近义词：isolation, solitude, desolation, forsakenness |反义词：companionship, togetherness, sociability
