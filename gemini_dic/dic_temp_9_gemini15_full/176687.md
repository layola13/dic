reacquisition:/ˌriːækwɪˈzɪʃən/| n. |重新获得；收回；再取得 |The act of acquiring something again, especially something that was lost or given up.  It implies a previous possession or ownership.  |例句：The company announced the reacquisition of its former subsidiary. 公司宣布重新收购其以前的子公司。

例句：The museum's reacquisition of the stolen painting was celebrated by art lovers worldwide.  博物馆重新获得被盗画作的消息受到了世界各地艺术爱好者的庆祝。


近义词：recovery, regaining, repossession, retrieval

反义词：loss, relinquishment, surrender


词性: 名词
