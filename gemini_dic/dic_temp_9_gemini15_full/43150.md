cozily:/ˈkoʊzɪli/| adj.|舒适地；惬意地；温暖舒适地 |Adjective describing a state of comfortable and pleasant warmth and relaxation. It suggests a feeling of snugness and contentment.  |例句：We sat cozily by the fire, reading books. 我们舒适地坐在火炉旁看书。

例句：The cat curled up cozily on the sofa. 猫舒服地蜷缩在沙发上。

近义词：comfortably, snugly, comfortably, pleasantly

反义词：uncomfortably, discomfortably, bleakly
