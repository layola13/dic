jerrymanders:/ˈdʒɛriˌmændər/| v.|  不公平地划分选区| To manipulate the boundaries of (an electoral constituency) so as to favor one party or class.|The state legislature jerrymandered the voting districts to ensure their party's victory.  该州立法机关对投票区进行了人为操纵，以确保其党的胜利。| gerrymander, rig | fair apportionment | 动词

一般现在时:
They jerrymander the districts every ten years. = 他们每十年对选区进行一次人为操纵。
The politicians often jerrymander the electoral map. = 政客们经常操纵选举地图。


一般过去时:
The ruling party jerrymandered the election districts last year. = 执政党去年操纵了选举区。
They jerrymandered the boundaries to favor the incumbent. = 他们操纵边界以利于现任者。


现在完成时:
They have jerrymandered the map to maintain power. = 他们已经操纵了地图以维持权力。
The opposition party claims the government has jerrymandered the election. = 反对党声称政府操纵了选举。


过去完成时:

将来时:

The party plans to jerrymander the voting areas to increase their chances of winning. = 该党计划操纵投票区域以增加其获胜的机会。


虚拟语气:
