justifyingly:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪɪŋli/| adv.|  公正地；有理由地；正当性地 | In a way that justifies or provides a valid reason.  It suggests the action or statement is defensible or reasonable. |例句：He justifyingly explained his actions to the police. 他向警方公正地解释了他的行为。

例句：She justifyingly refused to participate in the unethical project. 她有理由拒绝参与那个不道德的项目。


近义词：rightfully, reasonably, legitimately, justifiably

反义词：unjustifiably, wrongly, improperly, unfairly


词源：源于动词justify（使正当；证明…是正当的），加上副词后缀-ly构成。justify的词源可追溯到拉丁语“justificare”，由“justus”（公正的）和“facere”（做）构成。
