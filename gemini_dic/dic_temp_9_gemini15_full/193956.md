self-evaluated:/ˌsɛlfɪˈvæljueɪtɪd/ | adj. | 自我评价的，自我评估的 |  Describes something that has been assessed or judged by the person or thing itself.  It implies a process of self-reflection and judgment. | Example:  The self-evaluated report showed a significant improvement in his work performance.  自我评价报告显示他的工作表现有了显著提高。 | 近义词: self-assessed, self-appraised | 反义词: externally evaluated, objectively assessed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The employee self-evaluates their performance annually. = 员工每年都会进行自我业绩评估。
He self-evaluates his strengths and weaknesses regularly. = 他定期评估自己的优势和劣势。


一般过去时:
She self-evaluated her presentation skills after the conference. = 会议后，她对自己演讲技巧进行了自我评价。
The team self-evaluated their project's success. = 团队对项目的成功进行了自我评估。


现在完成时:
He has self-evaluated his progress and feels confident about the future. = 他已经对自己取得的进步进行了自我评价，并对未来充满信心。
They have self-evaluated their contributions to the team project. = 他们已经对自己对团队项目的贡献进行了自我评估。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, she had self-evaluated her performance for the past quarter. = 会议之前，她已经对自己过去一个季度的表现进行了自我评价。
The students had self-evaluated their essays before submitting them. = 学生们在提交论文之前已经对自己的论文进行了自我评价。


将来时:
I will self-evaluate my work after completing this project. = 完成这个项目后，我将对自己的工作进行自我评价。
The company will self-evaluate its environmental impact next year. = 公司明年将对其环境影响进行自我评估。


虚拟语气:
If I were to self-evaluate honestly, I would admit to some shortcomings. = 如果我要诚实地进行自我评价，我会承认自己有一些缺点。
I wish I had self-evaluated my options more carefully before making the decision. = 我希望在做决定之前，我已经更仔细地对自己的选择进行了自我评估。


被动语态:  (This word is rarely used in the passive voice because it inherently implies self-action.)  The self-evaluation was required for promotion. 自我评估是晋升的必要条件。
The self-evaluation process was streamlined this year. 今年简化了自我评估流程。


疑问句:
Have you self-evaluated your performance this year? = 你今年对自己的表现进行了自我评价了吗？
Will the employees self-evaluate their work before the deadline? = 员工会在截止日期前对自己的工作进行自我评价吗？


否定句:
He did not self-evaluate his work thoroughly enough. = 他对自己的工作评价不够彻底。
The students did not self-evaluate their essays before submitting them. = 学生们在提交论文之前没有对自己的论文进行自我评价。
