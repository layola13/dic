outrivals:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvəlz/| vt.|胜过；超过|To surpass or exceed in excellence, achievement, or ability.|例句：His latest novel outrivals all his previous works. 他的最新小说超过了他以前的所有作品。|例句：Her performance outrivaled that of her competitors. 她的表现胜过了她的竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, excel, exceed, outdo|反义词：underperform, be inferior to|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new restaurant outrivals all others in the area. = 这家新餐厅胜过该地区所有其他餐厅。
His skills outrival those of his colleagues. = 他的技能超过了他的同事。


一般过去时:
Her painting outrivaled all others in the competition. = 她的画在比赛中胜过了所有其他的画。
The team outrivaled their opponents in the final match. = 该队在决赛中胜过了他们的对手。


现在完成时:
This technology has outrivaled all previous attempts. = 这项技术已经超越了以往所有尝试。
She has outrivaled her competitors in the market. = 她已经在市场上胜过了她的竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the game, they had outrivaled the other team by a large margin. = 到比赛结束时，他们已经以很大的优势胜过了另一支球队。
He had outrivaled his expectations for his business. = 他已经超过了他对业务的预期。


将来时:
The new model will outrival its predecessor in terms of efficiency. = 新型号在效率方面将胜过其前代产品。
Their new product will outrival the competition. = 他们的新产品将胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he worked harder, he might outrival his rivals. = 如果他更努力工作，他可能会胜过他的竞争对手。
If she had trained more, she would have outrivaled her opponent. = 如果她训练更多，她就会胜过她的对手。


被动语态:
His work was outrivaled by his younger brother's. = 他的作品被弟弟的作品所超越。
The company's strategy was outrivaled by its competitors. = 该公司的战略被竞争对手超越。


疑问句:
Does this product outrival its competitors? = 这款产品是否胜过其竞争对手？
Will his new book outrival his previous best seller? = 他的新书会超过他之前的畅销书吗？


否定句:
His attempt did not outrival the existing solution. = 他的尝试没有超越现有的解决方案。
Their performance did not outrival the other team’s. = 他们的表现没有胜过另一支球队。


PHRASE->

outrival in sales = 在销量上胜过
outrival in popularity = 在受欢迎程度上胜过
outrival in innovation = 在创新方面胜过
