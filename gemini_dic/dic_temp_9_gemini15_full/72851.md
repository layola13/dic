folkething:/ˈfɒlkeθɪŋ/| n. |国民议会；人民大会|The folkething is the Danish parliament, a unicameral legislature.  The term originates from Old Norse, reflecting the historical assembly of free men.  It's a historically significant institution representing popular sovereignty. |Example: The Danish Folkething passed a new law regarding climate change. 丹麦国民议会通过了一项关于气候变化的新法律。|近义词：parliament, assembly, congress |反义词：monarchy (as a contrasting system)
