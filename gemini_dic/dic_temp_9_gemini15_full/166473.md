prerinses:/ˈpriːrɪnsɪz/| v.|预洗|To rinse something before washing it thoroughly.  This is typically done to remove loose dirt or debris before the main washing process.|例句：I always prerinse my dishes before putting them in the dishwasher. = 我总是先冲洗我的餐具，然后再把它们放进洗碗机。

例句：She prerinses her laundry to remove excess soil before washing. = 她先预洗衣服以去除污垢再正式清洗。


近义词：prewash, rinse beforehand


反义词：None (It's an action, not easily described with a direct opposite)


词性：动词
