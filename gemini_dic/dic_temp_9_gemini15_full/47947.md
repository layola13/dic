decide:/dɪˈsaɪd/ | vt. |决定，判决，裁决 | To make a choice or judgment about something; to settle a question or dispute. |例句：I have decided to go to college. 我决定去上大学。|例句：The judge decided the case in favor of the plaintiff. 法官判决原告胜诉。|近义词：determine, resolve, conclude |反义词：hesitate, waver


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She decides what to wear every morning. = 她每天早上都决定穿什么。
He decides the company's strategy. = 他决定公司的战略。

一般过去时:
They decided to postpone the meeting. = 他们决定推迟会议。
We decided against going to the beach. = 我们决定不去海滩。

现在完成时:
I have decided to quit my job. = 我已经决定辞职了。
She has decided on her major. = 她已经决定了她的专业。

过去完成时:
He had decided to leave before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他已经决定离开了。
They had decided on a course of action. = 他们已经决定采取行动方案了。

将来时:
We will decide later. = 我们稍后再决定。
She will decide what to do tomorrow. = 她明天会决定做什么。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would decide quickly. = 如果我是你，我会迅速决定。
I wish I had decided differently. = 我真希望我当初做了不同的决定。

被动语态:
The matter was decided by a vote. = 这件事由投票决定。
The date of the wedding has been decided. = 婚礼日期已经确定了。

疑问句:
Have you decided yet? = 你决定了吗？
What will we decide to do? = 我们将决定做什么？

否定句:
I haven't decided what to do for my birthday. = 我还没决定生日做什么。
They didn't decide to go. = 他们决定不去。


PHRASE->
decide on = 决定…
decide against = 决定反对…
decide to = 决定…
decide upon = 决定…
make a decision = 做决定
