nonincumbent:/ˌnɑːnˈɪŋkʌmbənt/ | adj. | 非在职的；未连任的 | Not currently holding an office or position.  This term is most commonly used in political contexts to refer to a candidate who is not the current officeholder. | Example:  The nonincumbent challenger surprised everyone by winning the election.  非在职的挑战者以获胜震惊了所有人。 | 近义词: challenger, outsider | 反义词: incumbent
