rematriculates: /ˌriːˈmætrɪkjuleɪts/ | v. |重新注册入学；重新注册|To re-enroll as a student at a school or college, especially after a period of absence.|

例句：

After a year off, she rematriculated at the university.  =  休学一年后，她重新注册了大学。

He rematriculated at the law school to complete his degree. = 他重新注册了法学院以完成他的学位。


近义词：reenroll, re-register


反义词：withdraw, drop out


词性：动词
