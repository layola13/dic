resistful: /rɪˈzɪstfəl/ | adj. | 反抗的；顽抗的；抵抗的 | Showing resistance; unwilling to comply.  |The resistful child refused to eat his vegetables.  那个反抗的孩子拒绝吃蔬菜。|  unyielding, defiant, recalcitrant | compliant, submissive, yielding |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is resistful of authority. 他反抗权威。
The plant is resistful to disease. 这植物抗病。

一般过去时:
She was resistful to any suggestion of compromise. 她对任何妥协的建议都持抵制态度。
The prisoners were resistful of their captors. 囚犯们反抗他们的看守。

现在完成时:
They have been resistful of change for years. 他们多年来一直抵制变革。
The community has been resistful to the new development plan. 社区一直抵制新的发展计划。

过去完成时:
He had been resistful to his parents' wishes his whole life. 他一生都反抗父母的意愿。
The nation had been resistful of foreign influence for decades. 这个国家几十年来一直抵制外国影响。

将来时:
They will be resistful to any attempt to control them. 他们将抵制任何控制他们的企图。
The rebels will be resistful of any peace talks. 反叛者将抵制任何和平谈判。

虚拟语气:
If he were less resistful, he would have more friends. 如果他不那么抗拒，他会拥有更多朋友。
If the people were not so resistful, the reform would succeed. 如果人民不那么抗拒，改革就会成功。

被动语态:
His ideas were resistfully met by the board. 他的想法受到了董事会的抵制。
The new policy was resistfully received by the public. 新政策受到了公众的抵制。

疑问句:
Is he resistful of your instructions? 他对你的指示有抵触吗？
Are the workers resistful of the new rules? 工人们抵制新规吗？

否定句:
He is not resistful to reason. 他不是不讲道理的。
The soldiers were not resistful to the general's orders. 士兵们没有反抗将军的命令。
