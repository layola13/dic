marsh:/mɑːʃ/|n.|沼泽，湿地|A low-lying wetland, typically characterized by soft, wet, spongy ground, and vegetation adapted to saturated soil conditions. Marshes are often found near rivers, lakes, or coastlines.|Example: The birds nested in the marsh.  鸟儿在沼泽地筑巢。|近义词：swamp, bog, fen, mire|反义词：dry land, upland
