misreporting:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrtɪŋ/ | n. | 歪曲报道；错误报道 | The act of giving a false or inaccurate account of something.  It often involves intentionally distorting facts or omitting crucial information to create a misleading narrative. |例句：The newspaper was accused of misreporting the facts of the accident. 报纸被指控歪曲了事故的事实。  The misreporting of the study's findings led to widespread confusion.  对研究结果的错误报道导致了广泛的混乱。|近义词：distortion, falsification, misrepresentation, inaccurate reporting | 反义词：accurate reporting, truthful account | 名词
