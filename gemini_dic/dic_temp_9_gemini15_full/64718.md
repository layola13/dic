eschatology:/ˌɛskəˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. |末世论，世界末日学 |The study of the end times, especially in a religious context. It deals with beliefs about death, judgment, heaven, hell, and the ultimate destiny of humanity.  | Example:  Many religions have elaborate eschatologies describing the end of the world and the afterlife.  许多宗教都有关于世界末日和来世的详细末世论。|近义词：apocalypticism, millennialism |反义词： (No direct antonym, but concepts like presentism or focusing solely on the present could be considered contrasting.)
