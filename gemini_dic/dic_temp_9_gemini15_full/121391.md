mishit:/ˈmɪʃɪt/| v.|击偏，击不准；打歪|To hit something inaccurately or badly.|例句：He mishit the ball and it went into the rough. 他击球不准，球进了粗糙地带。 He mishit the shot completely, sending the ball flying into the stands. 他完全打歪了，球飞进了看台。|近义词：misplay, misstrike, botch|反义词：hit accurately, strike perfectly
