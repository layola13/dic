novator:/ˈnoʊveɪtər/| n. |创新者，改革者 |A novator is a person who introduces something new, especially a new method, idea, or invention.  They are pioneers and leaders in their fields, often challenging established norms and practices. |Example:  Steve Jobs was a novator in the technology industry. 史蒂夫·乔布斯是科技行业的创新者。

Example:  The company is looking for novators to develop new products. 公司正在寻找创新者来开发新产品。

近义词: innovator, pioneer, reformer, trailblazer

反义词: traditionalist, conservative, reactionary


词性:名词
