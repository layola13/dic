chafes:/tʃeɪfs/ | v. |擦伤，磨损；使烦恼，使恼怒 | to make (skin) sore by rubbing; to annoy or irritate someone |例句：The new shoes chafed my heels. 这双新鞋磨破了我的脚后跟。|例句：His constant criticism chafed her. 他不断的批评让她恼火。 |近义词：irritate, annoy, gall, abrade |反义词：soothe, comfort


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The collar chafes his neck. = 衣领磨擦他的脖子。
His constant complaining chafes me. = 他不断的抱怨让我心烦。
