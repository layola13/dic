craftsperson:/ˈkræftsˌpɜːrsən/ | n. |工匠，技工 | A skilled worker who makes things by hand.  A craftsperson is someone who is highly skilled in a particular craft, such as pottery, woodworking, or metalworking. They often create unique and handcrafted items. | Example:  The craftsperson meticulously carved the wooden bird.  这位工匠细致地雕刻着木鸟。  The craftsperson skillfully wove the intricate tapestry. 这位工匠熟练地编织着复杂的挂毯。 | 近义词：artisan, artisan, craftswoman, craftsman | 反义词：amateur, unskilled worker
