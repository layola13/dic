nonidentities:/ˌnɒnˌaɪˈdentɪtiz/ | n. | 非同一性；非同一认定；非身份 |  The term refers to situations or entities that lack a unique identifying characteristic or are deliberately kept anonymous.  It often arises in contexts of data privacy, security, and anonymity.  For example, in statistical analysis, nonidentities are used to protect individual privacy while still allowing for data analysis. | Example:  The study used anonymized data, ensuring the nonidentities of participants.  这项研究使用了匿名数据，确保了参与者的身份不被识别。 | 近义词：anonymity, unidentifiability | 反义词：identities, identification
