eat-in: /ˈiːtɪn/ | adj. | 可在内用餐的；带餐厅的 |  Describes a space, typically an apartment or house, that has a dining area suitable for eating meals within the unit, as opposed to needing to eat elsewhere.  It often refers to a kitchen area designed to accommodate eating. |


例句：

They bought an eat-in kitchen apartment. = 他们买了一套带餐厅的公寓。

The house features an eat-in kitchen and a separate dining room. = 这栋房子有一个带餐厅的厨房和一个单独的餐厅。



近义词：with dining area,  having a dining space


反义词：take-out, eat-out


词性：形容词
