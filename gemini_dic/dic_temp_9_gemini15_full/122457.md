mobilises: /ˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ | /ˈməʊbəlaɪz/ | vt. | 动员；调动；促使…活动 | To put into action or use; to assemble and prepare for action; to rouse to action. |

例句：

The government mobilised the army to quell the rebellion. = 政府动员军队平息叛乱。

The news mobilised public opinion against the proposed tax increase. = 这则新闻调动了公众舆论反对提议的增税。


近义词：muster, rally, marshal, activate, rouse

反义词：demobilize, deactivate, quell
