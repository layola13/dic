revolving:/rɪˈvɒlviŋ/ | /rɪˈvɑːlviŋ/ | adj. |旋转的，循环的 | Turning around a central point; continuously recurring.  |例句：The revolving door allowed people to enter and exit the building easily.旋转门使人们可以轻松地进出大楼。

The revolving credit allows for flexible borrowing.循环信贷允许灵活借款。


近义词：rotating, spinning, cyclical, recurring | 反义词：stationary, fixed, static | 词性：形容词
