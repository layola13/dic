rebuilders:/riˈbɪldərz/| n. |重建者，修复者|People or companies that rebuild or repair things, often after damage or destruction.  They are involved in the process of restoring something to its former state or improving it. |例句：The rebuilders worked tirelessly to restore the damaged buildings after the hurricane.  重建者们在飓风过后不懈地努力修复受损的建筑物。

The city hired a team of expert rebuilders to renovate the old town square.  城市雇佣了一支专家重建者团队来翻新旧城广场。

|近义词：repairers, restorers, renovators |反义词：destroyers, demolishers |名词
