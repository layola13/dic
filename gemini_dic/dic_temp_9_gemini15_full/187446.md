rokambole:/ˈroʊkæmboʊl/| n.| 蒜香葱, 堪比葱, 宽叶韭 | A type of broad-leaved garlic with a mild flavor, sometimes called elephant garlic or great-headed garlic. It is larger than common garlic but milder in flavor.  | Example:  The chef used rokambole to add a subtle garlic flavor to the soup.  厨师用蒜香葱为汤增添了微妙的大蒜味。 |近义词：elephant garlic, great-headed garlic |反义词：None  |名词
