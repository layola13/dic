noncorrectives: /ˌnɒn.kəˈrɛktɪvz/ | adj. | 非矫正的；非纠正性的 |  Describing actions, measures, or treatments that do not aim to correct or fix a problem or condition, but rather to manage or alleviate its symptoms.  They often deal with the effects of a problem rather than its root cause. | Example:  The medication provided only noncorrective relief from the pain; it didn't address the underlying inflammation.  |近义词: palliative, symptomatic, alleviative | 反义词: curative, corrective, remedial |
