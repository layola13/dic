proven:/ˈpruːvən/| adj.|被证明的；已被证实的|Having been shown to be true or valid.|The theory has been proven correct.  这个理论已被证明是正确的。|His innocence has been proven.  他的清白已被证明。|近义词：verified, established, confirmed|反义词：disproven, refuted, unproven


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence proves his guilt. = 证据证明了他的罪行。
This method has proven effective. = 这种方法已被证明有效。


一般过去时:
Scientists proved the theory wrong. = 科学家证明了这个理论是错误的。
The experiment proved successful. = 实验成功了。


现在完成时:
They have proven their loyalty. = 他们已经证明了他们的忠诚。
Numerous studies have proven the benefits of exercise. = 许多研究都证明了锻炼的好处。


过去完成时:
It had been proven that the drug was harmful. = 已经证明这种药物是有害的。
They had proven their capabilities before the project began. = 在项目开始之前，他们已经证明了自己的能力。


将来时:
Further research will prove the hypothesis. = 进一步的研究将证明这个假设。
The results of the test will prove conclusive. = 测试结果将证明是确凿的。


虚拟语气:
If it were proven true, it would change everything. = 如果它被证明是真的，它将改变一切。
I wish it could be proven that he is innocent. = 我希望能够证明他是无辜的。


被动语态:
His innocence was proven in court. = 他的清白在法庭上得到了证明。
The effectiveness of the treatment has been proven. = 这种治疗方法的有效性已被证明。


疑问句:
Has it been proven that this is safe? = 这是否已被证明是安全的？
Can this claim be proven? = 这个说法能够被证明吗？


否定句:
The theory has not been proven. = 这个理论尚未得到证明。
His alibi couldn't be proven. = 他的不在场证明无法被证实。


PHRASE->
proven track record: 业绩记录
proven technology: 经过验证的技术
proven method: 行之有效的方法
proven results: 显著成果
proven success: 成功的案例
