glamourously:/ˈɡlæmərəsli/| adj.|迷人地，魅力四射地|In a glamorous manner; attractively and excitingly.  The word suggests a captivating allure, often associated with elegance, sophistication, and a touch of extravagance.|例句：She dressed glamourously for the party. 她盛装出席派对。

例句：The actress walked glamourously down the red carpet. 女演员迷人地走过红毯。


近义词：alluringly, captivatingly, dazzlingly, enchantingly, fascinatingly

反义词：plainly, dowdily, unattractively, shabbily


词源：源自法语“glamour”，最终源自苏格兰盖尔语“glamour”，意为“魔法，魅惑”。  The modern sense evolved from the association of magic and enchantment with a captivating and alluring appearance.
