laster:/ˈlæstər/| n. v.|持续者；持久者；持续时间；使持续| A person or thing that lasts a long time; something that endures.  To cause something to last longer.|The new paint is a laster.这新油漆很耐用。The company is trying to laster its products.公司正试图延长其产品的使用寿命。|近义词：endurer, survivor, durable|反义词: ephemeral, fleeting, transient
