hydrofoils:/ˈhaɪdroʊfɔɪlz/| n. |水翼船（复数）|A boat or ship that uses hydrofoils to lift its hull out of the water at high speeds.  Hydrofoils are underwater wings that generate lift as they move through the water, similar to airplane wings in air. This allows the vessel to reduce friction and increase speed.|The hydrofoils skimmed across the water, leaving a white wake.|近义词：high-speed craft, hydrofoil boat|反义词：conventional boat
