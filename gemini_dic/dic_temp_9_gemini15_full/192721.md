sea-bathing: /ˈsiːbeɪðɪŋ/ | n. | 海水浴；海浴 | The act of bathing in the sea.  It involves swimming, wading, or simply immersing oneself in the ocean for recreational purposes. | Example:  Sea-bathing is a popular summer activity. 海水浴是夏季一项很受欢迎的活动。  She enjoys sea-bathing every weekend during the summer. 她夏天每个周末都喜欢去海边游泳。| 近义词：ocean bathing, sea swimming | 反义词：N/A
