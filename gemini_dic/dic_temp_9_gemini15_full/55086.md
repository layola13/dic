disrespect:/dɪsrɪˈspɛkt/| n. v. |不尊重；无礼；轻视 |The lack of proper respect; disregard.  Showing a lack of respect. |例句：His disrespect for authority led to his dismissal. 他的目无尊长导致了他的解雇。

例句：The students showed blatant disrespect towards their teacher. 学生们对老师表现出公然的无礼。


近义词：contempt, scorn, disdain, slight, insult |反义词：respect, reverence, esteem, honor


词源:  From dis- + respect.  The prefix "dis-" indicates negation or reversal, modifying the meaning of "respect".
