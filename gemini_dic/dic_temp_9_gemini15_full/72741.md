fog: /fɒɡ/ /fɔːɡ/ | n. | 雾 | A visible aggregate of minute water droplets or ice crystals suspended in the atmosphere at or near the earth's surface.  It reduces visibility. | The fog was so thick we couldn't see the road.  浓雾弥漫，我们看不见路了。| mist, haze | clear weather, sunshine | noun
