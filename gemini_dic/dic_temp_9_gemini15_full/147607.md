overplays:/ˈoʊvərpleɪz/| vt.|过分强调；夸大；演得过火|To emphasize or exaggerate something excessively; to act a role with excessive force or emotion.|例句：The media overplayed the significance of the incident.媒体过分强调了事件的重要性。

例句：He overplayed his part in the play, making it seem melodramatic.他在剧中演得过火，显得矫揉造作。


近义词：exaggerate, overemphasize, overact

反义词：underplay, downplay, understate


词性:动词


USAGE->
