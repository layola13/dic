outworth:/ˈaʊtwɜːrθ/| adj.|胜过；价值高于| surpassing in worth or value; exceeding in value or merit.|The antique vase outworths all the other items in the collection. 这古董花瓶价值胜过收藏中的其他所有物品。|近义词：surpass, excel, exceed|反义词：underworth, undervalue|形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painting outworths all his other possessions. 这幅画的价值超过了他所有的其他财产。
This strategy outworths the previous one in terms of efficiency. 就效率而言，这个策略优于之前的策略。


一般过去时:
The old house outworth the new apartment building in terms of historical significance. 那栋旧房子在历史意义上胜过那栋新建的公寓楼。
Her contributions to the project outworth those of her colleagues. 她对项目的贡献胜过她的同事们。


现在完成时:
The investment has outworth the initial cost many times over. 这项投资的价值已远远超过了初始成本。
His dedication to the cause has outworth any difficulties he faced. 他对事业的奉献精神已经超越了他所面临的任何困难。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the company’s assets had outworth its liabilities. 到年底，公司的资产已经超过了负债。
It had outworth all our expectations. 它已经超出了我们所有的预期。


将来时:
This technology will outworth all the existing solutions in the future. 这项技术将来会优于所有现有的解决方案。
With this new approach, our results will outworth previous ones. 采用这种新方法，我们的成果将优于以往。


虚拟语气:
If only I had invested earlier, my returns would have outworth my initial investment.  如果我早点投资，我的回报就会超过我的初始投资。
If this plan were implemented, the outcome would outworth the effort. 如果实施这个计划，结果将超过付出的努力。


被动语态:
The benefits of this program are outworth by its cost. 这个项目的益处被认为不值其成本。（此句被动语态略显不自然，outworth 通常不用于被动语态）
The value of the land was outworth by the developer. 这块土地的价值被开发商高估了。（此句被动语态略显不自然，outworth 通常不用于被动语态）


疑问句:
Does this diamond outworth that one? 这颗钻石的价值高于那颗吗？
Does the potential benefit outworth the risk? 潜在的好处是否胜过风险？


否定句:
The risk doesn't outworth the potential reward. 风险不值得潜在的回报。
The cost of the project does not outworth the benefits.  项目的成本不值得其收益。
