curetting:/kʊˈrɛtɪŋ/| n.|刮除术；清创术|The process of scraping or cleaning a surface, especially a wound or cavity, using a curette.  It's a surgical procedure used to remove tissue or material from a body cavity or wound.|例句：The doctor performed a curetting to remove the abnormal tissue from the uterus.医生进行了刮除术以去除子宫内的异常组织。

The curetting of the wound was necessary to prevent infection. 清创术对于预防感染是必要的。|近义词：scraping, cleaning, debridement|反义词：None specifically, but words implying leaving something in place could be considered antonyms.|名词
