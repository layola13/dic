overcount:/ˈoʊvərkaʊnt/| vt.| 错算，多算|To count something more than the actual number.  It often implies a mistake or inaccuracy in counting.|例句：The cashier overcounted the items, giving me too much change. 收银员多算了商品数量，给了我太多的零钱。

例句：They overcounted the votes in the election, leading to a recount. 他们在选举中多算了选票，导致了重新计票。


近义词：miscount, overestimate


反义词：undercount


词性:动词
