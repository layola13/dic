peninsular:/pəˈnɪnsjʊlə(r)/| adj. n.| 半岛的；半岛 | Relating to or characteristic of a peninsula.  A piece of land almost entirely surrounded by water but connected to a larger landmass. |例句：Florida is a peninsular state. 佛罗里达州是一个半岛州。

The peninsular climate is mild. 半岛气候温和。|近义词：peninsula (n.) |反义词：mainland (n.)
