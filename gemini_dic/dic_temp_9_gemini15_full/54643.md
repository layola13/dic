dismounting: /dɪsˈmaʊntɪŋ/ | v. | 下马；卸下；拆卸 | The act of getting off a horse or other mount; the act of removing something from its mounting or support. | 例句：He dismounted gracefully from his horse. 他优雅地下马了。
