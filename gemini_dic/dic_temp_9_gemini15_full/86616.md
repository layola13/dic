heliotype:/ˈhiːliəʊtaɪp/| n. |日光照相术；日光印版|A heliotype is a photographic printing process that uses a bichromated gelatin plate to create a positive image.  The process is known for its fine detail and tonal range.|Example: The museum displayed several prints made using the heliotype process.  博物馆展出了几张采用日光照相术制作的版画。|近义词：photogravure, photolithograph|反义词：hand-drawn, painted
