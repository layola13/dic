hamburgers:/ˈhæmbɜːrɡər z/| n.|汉堡包|A hamburger is a food consisting of a patty of ground beef served inside a sliced bun.  It is often accompanied by various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, pickles, and condiments like ketchup and mustard.|Example: I ordered a cheeseburger with fries. 我点了一个芝士汉堡配薯条。

I ate two hamburgers for lunch. 我午餐吃了两个汉堡包。|近义词：burger, patty melt|反义词：无
