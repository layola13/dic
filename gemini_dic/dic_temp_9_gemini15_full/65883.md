evolutionists:/ˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪsts/| n. |进化论者|Those who believe in and advocate for the theory of evolution.  They study and support the scientific explanation of how life on Earth has changed over time through processes like natural selection.|Example: Many evolutionists point to the fossil record as evidence of gradual change in species.  许多进化论者将化石记录作为物种逐渐变化的证据。|近义词：evolutionary biologists, Darwinists|反义词：creationists
