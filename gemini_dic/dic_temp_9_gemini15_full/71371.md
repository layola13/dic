flagellators: /ˈflædʒəleɪtərz/ | n. |鞭毛虫；自虐者 | Those who whip themselves as a religious practice, or organisms with flagella.  |例句：Some religious flagellators inflict severe wounds upon themselves during processions.  一些宗教自虐者在游行期间会给自己造成严重的伤口。


The term refers to both religious practitioners of self-flagellation and organisms possessing flagella.  In the biological context, it refers to a diverse group of organisms including bacteria, protists, and some eukaryotic cells, characterized by the presence of flagella—whip-like appendages used for locomotion.

近义词： (for religious context) penitents, ascetics; (for biological context) ciliates (though cilia are different from flagella)
反义词： N/A  (no direct antonyms exist for either meaning)
