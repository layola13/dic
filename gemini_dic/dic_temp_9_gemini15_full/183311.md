reproaches:/rɪˈproʊtʃɪz/| n. |责备；谴责；批评 |The act of expressing disapproval or criticism; blame.  |例句：Her reproaches stung him deeply.  她的责备深深地刺痛了他。

例句：His silence was a reproach to his actions. 他的沉默是对他的行为的一种谴责。


近义词：rebukes, criticisms, reprimands, censures |反义词：praises, compliments, approvals |词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reproaches his son for his bad grades. 他责备他儿子学习成绩差。
She often reproaches her husband for his laziness. 她经常责备她丈夫懒惰。


一般过去时:
The teacher reproached the student for cheating. 老师因作弊而责备了学生。
My mother reproached me for my bad behavior. 我妈妈因我的不良行为而责备了我。


现在完成时:
He has reproached me several times for being late. 他因为我迟到已经责备过我几次了。
She has reproached him for his dishonesty. 她已经因为他的不诚实而责备他了。


过去完成时:
He had reproached her many times before she finally understood. 在她最终明白之前，他已经责备过她很多次了。
They had reproached him for his actions before he apologized. 在他道歉之前，他们已经因为他的行为责备过他了。


将来时:
I will reproach him for his carelessness tomorrow. 我明天将因他的粗心大意而责备他。
She will reproach her daughter for her bad choices. 她将因女儿的不良选择而责备她女儿。


虚拟语气:
I wish I hadn't reproached him so harshly. 我真希望我没有那样严厉地责备他。
If I had known better, I wouldn't have reproached her. 如果我早知道，我就不会责备她了。


被动语态:
He was reproached for his mistakes. 他因他的错误而受到责备。
She was reproached by her boss for her inefficiency. 她因效率低下而受到老板的责备。


疑问句:
Why are you reproaching me? 你为什么责备我？
Does he often reproach his employees? 他经常责备他的员工吗？


否定句:
He didn't reproach her for her failure. 他没有因为她的失败而责备她。
I would not reproach her for her honesty. 我不会因为她的诚实而责备她。


PHRASE->
cast reproach upon  对…表示谴责
a reproach to 对…的耻辱
level a reproach at  向…提出指责
under reproach 受谴责
subject to reproach 易受责备


词源:
From Old French *reprochier, from reprocher "to bring near, approach," from Latin repropinquare "to approach closely," from re- "back, again" + prope "near."  The sense evolved from "bringing something close for consideration" to "bringing a fault or blame to someone's attention."
