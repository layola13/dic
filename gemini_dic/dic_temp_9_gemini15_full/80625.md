gooseberry:/ˈɡuzˌbɛri/| n. |醋栗，醋栗果|A gooseberry is a small, tart berry that grows on a thorny bush.  It's often used in jams, pies, and other desserts, though its tartness requires balancing with sweeteners. |例句：We picked a bucketful of gooseberries from the bushes at the edge of the wood. 我们从树林边的灌木丛中摘了一桶醋栗。|例句：The gooseberry fool was a surprisingly refreshing dessert. 醋栗奶冻是一种令人耳目一新的甜点。|近义词：currant (especially red currant)|反义词：N/A
