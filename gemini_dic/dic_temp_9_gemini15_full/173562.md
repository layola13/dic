quasi-honestly:/ˈkwɑːzaɪˈɑːnɪstli/| adj.|似是而非地诚实地;表面上诚实地|Almost but not completely honest; seemingly honest but with reservations or hidden motives.  It suggests a pretense of honesty, a lack of complete sincerity, or an attempt to appear honest while potentially concealing something.|例句：He quasi-honestly admitted to being slightly involved in the incident. 他似是而非地承认自己稍微参与了这起事件。

例句：She answered the question quasi-honestly, omitting some crucial details. 她似是而非地回答了这个问题，省略了一些关键细节。


近义词：apparently honestly, seemingly honestly, superficially honestly|反义词：completely honestly, truthfully, frankly|词性:形容词
