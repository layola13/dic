eunuch: /ˈjuːnək/ | n. |太监，阉人 | A castrated man, especially one formerly employed in a palace or harem.  | Example:  The eunuch served the emperor faithfully for many years.  这位太监多年来忠实地侍奉皇帝。 |近义词：castrate, gelding (for animals) | 反义词：N/A
