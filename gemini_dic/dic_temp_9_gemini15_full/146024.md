overdramatized:/ˌoʊvərˈdræməˌtaɪzd/| adj.|夸大其词的，戏剧化的|excessively dramatic or emotional; exaggerated.|例句：The movie was overdramatized and unrealistic. 这部电影过于戏剧化且不现实。 The account of the incident was clearly overdramatized.  对事件的描述显然夸大了。|近义词：exaggerated, melodramatic, sensationalized|反义词：understated, realistic, muted|词性:形容词
