centime:/sɑ̃ˈtiːm/| n. |分；生丁|A centime is a unit of currency, historically used in several countries, equal to one hundredth of a franc or other similar unit.  It's a very small monetary unit. |例句：The old postcard cost only a few centimes. 这张旧明信片只值几个生丁。  |例句：In the past, children would collect centimes to buy sweets. 过去，孩子们会收集生丁来买糖果。|近义词：cent (in some contexts)|反义词：franc (as it's a subunit)|名词
