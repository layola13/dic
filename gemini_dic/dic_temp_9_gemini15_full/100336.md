intersprinkle:/ˌɪntərˈsprɪŋkəl/ | vt. | 散布；点缀；插播 | To scatter or distribute something among other things.  | 例句：The teacher interspersed jokes throughout her lecture. 老师在讲座中穿插了一些笑话。

例句：Flowers were interspersed among the vegetables in the garden. 花园里蔬菜之间点缀着鲜花。


近义词：scatter, strew, sprinkle, dot, diversify | 反义词：concentrate, mass, aggregate | 词性：动词
