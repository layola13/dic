hayloft:/ˈheɪlɔːft/| n. | 干草棚；阁楼 | A hayloft is a loft or upper floor of a barn or other building used for storing hay.  |例句：The farmer stored his hay in the hayloft.  农夫把干草储存在干草棚里。

例句：We found a hidden stash of old toys in the dusty hayloft. 我们在尘土飞扬的干草棚里发现了一堆旧玩具。


近义词：loft, attic (部分情况)


反义词：ground floor, basement


词性：名词
