redirector's: /ˌriːˈdaɪrɛktərz/ | n. | 重定向器（的） |  The word "redirector's" is the possessive form of "redirector,"  referring to something belonging to or associated with a redirector. A redirector is a device or program that alters the path of data flow, typically redirecting network traffic or input/output streams.  | Example: The redirector's configuration file was corrupted.  重定向器的配置文件已损坏。 | 近义词: router's (in some contexts), switch's (in some contexts) | 反义词:  N/A (possessive forms don't have direct antonyms) | 名词
