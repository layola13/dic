reaggravating: /ˌriːˈæɡrəveɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 使恶化；加剧的 |  Making a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling worse.  |例句：His constant complaining was reaggravating the already tense situation.  他的不断抱怨使本已紧张的局势更加恶化。
例句：The rainy weather is reaggravating the flooding in the low-lying areas. 雨天加剧了低洼地区的洪涝灾害。| 近义词：exacerbating, worsening, intensifying | 反义词：alleviating, improving, mitigating | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
