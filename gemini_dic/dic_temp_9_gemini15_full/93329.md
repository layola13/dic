ill-treating: /ˌɪlˈtriːtɪŋ/ | v. |虐待；粗暴对待 |  The act of treating someone badly or cruelly; to mistreat.  |例句：He was accused of ill-treating his animals. 他被指控虐待动物。
例句：The report detailed the horrific ways in which the prisoners were ill-treated. 报告详细描述了囚犯遭受残酷虐待的方式。|近义词：abuse, mistreat, maltreat, persecute |反义词：cherish, care for, nurture
