inferences:/ˈɪnfərənsɪz/| n.|推论，推断，结论|The process of drawing conclusions based on evidence and reasoning.  It involves making logical leaps from known facts to unknown ones.|The detective made several inferences based on the evidence found at the crime scene.  侦探根据在犯罪现场发现的证据作出了几项推断。| deductions, conclusions, implications | assumptions, guesses | noun

一般现在时:
Scientists make inferences based on data. = 科学家根据数据进行推断。
Her inferences are often insightful. = 她的推论往往很有见地。


一般过去时:
The jury made inferences from the witness testimony. = 陪审团根据证人证词进行了推断。
He drew incorrect inferences from the limited information. = 他根据有限的信息作出了错误的推断。


现在完成时:
Researchers have made significant inferences about climate change. = 研究人员已经对气候变化作出了重大推断。
We have drawn several inferences from the recent survey results. = 我们从最近的调查结果中得出了一些推论。


过去完成时:
Before concluding, she had carefully considered all possible inferences. = 在得出结论之前，她仔细考虑了所有可能的推断。
They had made inaccurate inferences based on outdated data. = 他们根据过时的数据作出了不准确的推断。


将来时:
Future studies will make further inferences about the effects of pollution. = 未来的研究将对污染的影响作出进一步推断。
Scientists predict that they will be able to make more precise inferences with new technologies. = 科学家预测，随着新技术的出现，他们将能够进行更精确的推断。


虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we could make more reliable inferences. = 如果我们有更多的数据，我们就能做出更可靠的推断。
If I were you, I would reconsider my inferences. = 如果我是你，我会重新考虑我的推断。


被动语态:
Many inferences were drawn from the archaeological findings. = 从考古发现中得出了许多推论。
Important inferences are being made about the origins of the universe. = 正在对宇宙的起源作出重要的推断。


疑问句:
What inferences can be drawn from this study? = 从这项研究中可以得出什么推论？
What are the possible inferences based on these facts? = 基于这些事实，可能的推论是什么？


否定句:
No inferences can be made without further investigation. = 没有进一步调查，就无法做出任何推论。
His conclusions were not supported by the inferences presented. = 他的结论并没有得到所提出的推论的支持。
