neurosecretory:/ˌnʊroʊˈsiːkrɪtɔːri/|/ˌnjʊərəʊˈsiːkrɪtəri/| adj. |神经分泌的；神经内分泌的 | Relating to or denoting cells or tissues that secrete hormones or other substances into the bloodstream.  Neurosecretory cells are specialized nerve cells that produce and release hormones. | Example: The hypothalamus contains numerous neurosecretory cells that regulate various bodily functions.  下丘脑包含许多调节各种身体功能的神经分泌细胞。The neurosecretory system plays a vital role in maintaining homeostasis. 神经分泌系统在维持体内平衡方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：neuroendocrine |反义词：somatic (referring to non-hormone producing cells)
