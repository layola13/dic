derailleur:/ˌdeɪrˈeɪlər/| n. |变速器，拨链器 |A derailleur is a mechanism in a bicycle that shifts the chain between different gears. It allows the rider to change gears easily, making it possible to ride at different speeds and on different terrains.  |Example: My bike's derailleur is broken, so I can't change gears. 我的自行车变速器坏了，所以我无法换挡。|近义词：gear shifter, gear changer |反义词：None
