seeking:/ˈsiːkɪŋ/| v. |寻找；追求；试图 |The act of looking for something or someone, or trying to achieve something.  It implies a persistent effort to find or obtain something.|例句：She is seeking a new job. 她正在寻找一份新工作。
例句：They are seeking justice for the victims. 他们正在为受害者寻求正义。|近义词：searching, pursuing, striving, requesting |反义词：finding, achieving, abandoning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are seeking a solution to the problem. = 他们在寻求解决这个问题的方法。
He is seeking advice from his mentor. = 他正在向他的导师寻求建议。


一般过去时:
She sought help from her friends. = 她向朋友寻求帮助。
They sought refuge in the mountains. = 他们在山里寻求庇护。


现在完成时:
I have been seeking employment for months. = 我已经找工作好几个月了。
We have sought answers to this question for years. = 我们多年来一直在寻找这个问题的答案。


过去完成时:
He had sought her advice before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经寻求过她的建议。
They had sought funding for their project unsuccessfully. = 他们之前曾尝试为项目寻求资金，但没有成功。


将来时:
I will be seeking a promotion next year. = 我明年将寻求晋升。
They will seek further information before proceeding. = 他们将在继续之前寻求更多信息。


虚拟语气:
If I were seeking a job, I would update my resume. = 如果我正在找工作，我会更新我的简历。
I wish I were seeking a more challenging role. = 我希望我正在寻求一个更有挑战性的角色。


被动语态:
A solution is being sought to the crisis. = 正在寻求解决危机的方案。
Justice is being sought for the victims. = 正在为受害者寻求正义。


疑问句:
What are you seeking in a partner? = 你在伴侣身上寻找什么？
What are they seeking to achieve? = 他们试图取得什么成就？


否定句:
He is not seeking attention. = 他并没有寻求关注。
They were not seeking trouble. = 他们并非在找麻烦。


PHRASE->
seek out = 找出；搜寻
seek after = 追求；追寻
seek to = 试图；力求
seek refuge = 寻求庇护
in seeking = 在寻找的过程中
