self-inconsistency: /ˌsɛlf ɪn.kənˈsɪstənsi/ | n. | 自相矛盾；前后不一致 |

英文解析:  The state or quality of being self-contradictory; a lack of consistency or harmony within oneself or one's statements, actions, or beliefs.  It refers to a situation where someone's words, actions, or beliefs contradict each other.

例句:

His argument suffered from a glaring self-inconsistency.  他的论点存在明显的自相矛盾之处。

The politician's recent statements reveal a troubling degree of self-inconsistency.  这位政治家最近的声明暴露出令人不安的程度的自相矛盾。


近义词: contradiction, inconsistency, paradox, self-contradiction, discrepancy

反义词: consistency, coherence, harmony


词性: noun (名词)
