pseudostudious:/ˌsuːdəʊˈstuːdiəs/| adj. |假装用功的；好学样的（但实际上并非如此） | Affecting or suggesting scholarship; studious in appearance only.  It implies a superficial display of learning or intellectual effort, often to impress others. |例句：He adopted a pseudostudious air, hoping to impress his professors. 他装出一副好学的样子，希望能给教授们留下好印象。

例句：Her pseudostudious demeanor concealed a surprising lack of knowledge. 她假装用功的样子掩盖了她惊人的无知。


近义词：pretentious, affected, bookish (in a negative sense), ostentatious


反义词：genuine, sincere, unpretentious, diligent (truly)


词性：形容词
