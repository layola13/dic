/hɑːfˈfɜːrtaɪlli/ | adj. | 半育的，半肥沃的 |  Describes something that is only partially fertile or productive.  It combines "half" indicating incompleteness and "fertilely" (an adverb derived from "fertile") indicating the degree of fertility.  The word suggests a state of limited or incomplete productivity or fecundity. | The farmer found that only half-fertilely the land yielded a poor harvest.  = 农民发现只有半肥沃的土地收成很差。 The half-fertilely soil produced fewer crops than expected. = 半肥沃的土壤比预期的产量少。 | partially fertile, somewhat productive | infertile, barren, unproductive | adj.
