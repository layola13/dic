outplans:/ˌaʊtˈplænz/| v.|预先计划，事先计划 |To plan or scheme in advance, often secretly or cunningly.  |例句：They outplanned their opponents, securing a decisive victory. 他们事先计划周密，取得了决定性胜利。|近义词：preplan, strategize, scheme, plot|反义词：improvise, wing it


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outplans his competitors every year. = 他每年都精心计划以胜过竞争对手。
They outplan their marketing strategies meticulously. = 他们一丝不苟地预先计划他们的营销策略。

一般过去时:
The general outplanned the enemy, leading to their defeat. = 将军巧妙地预先计划，导致了敌人的失败。
She outplanned her rivals by securing the key contract. = 她通过获得关键合同而胜过对手。


现在完成时:
He has outplanned his rivals in several key projects. = 他在几个关键项目中都胜过了竞争对手。
The team has outplanned its opposition consistently this season. = 这个赛季球队一直精心计划，胜过对手。


过去完成时:
The detective realized the criminal had outplanned him. = 警探意识到罪犯已经事先计划好了，胜过了他。
They discovered that the company had outplanned its investors. = 他们发现公司已经事先计划好，胜过了投资者。


将来时:
She will outplan her opponents with a new strategy. = 她将用新战略胜过对手。
We will outplan the challenges ahead with careful preparation. = 我们将通过细致的准备来克服未来的挑战。


虚拟语气:
If they had outplanned their opponents better, they would have won. = 如果他们能更好地事先计划，他们就会赢了。
I wish we had outplanned the unexpected delays. = 我希望我们能预先计划好以应对意外的延误。


被动语态:
The campaign was outplanned by the opposition party. = 该活动被反对党巧妙地预先计划好了并压制。
The surprise attack was outplanned by the intelligence agency. = 这次突然袭击被情报机构事先计划好了。


疑问句:
Did they outplan their rivals in the negotiations? = 他们在谈判中胜过对手了吗？

否定句:
The team did not outplan their opponents; they were defeated. = 球队没有胜过对手，他们被击败了。
We failed to outplan the unforeseen circumstances. = 我们未能预先计划好应对不可预见的情况。
