pinnipedes:/ˈpɪnɪpiːdz/| n. |鳍足类动物|  Animals belonging to the order Pinnipedia, characterized by their flipper-like limbs adapted for swimming.  They include seals, sea lions, and walruses.  | Example: Pinnipeds are highly adapted to aquatic life. 鳍足类动物高度适应水生生活。 |近义词：seals, sea lions, walruses (depending on context)|反义词：None specifically, but could contrast with terrestrial mammals.
