reminisce:/ˌrɛmɪˈnɪs/| v. |回忆，缅怀|To recall and talk about past experiences or events.|例句：We reminisced about our college days. 我们回忆了大学时光。
例句：The old friends spent hours reminiscing about their youth. 老朋友们花了好几个小时回忆他们的青春。|近义词：recall, remember, recollect|反义词：forget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I often reminisce about my childhood. = 我经常回忆我的童年。
They reminisce about their travels every weekend. = 他们每个周末都回忆他们的旅行。


一般过去时:
We reminisced about old times last night. = 我们昨晚回忆了往事。
She reminisced about her first job interview. = 她回忆了她第一次求职面试。


现在完成时:
I have reminisced about this event many times. = 我已经多次回忆起这件事了。
They have reminisced about their school days countless times. = 他们无数次回忆起他们的学校时光。


过去完成时:
We had reminisced about our trip before we fell asleep. = 我们在睡着之前回忆了我们的旅行。
She had reminisced about her grandmother before she started crying. = 她开始哭泣之前回忆了她的祖母。


将来时:
We will reminisce about this day in years to come. = 我们将在未来的几年里回忆这一天。
They will reminisce about this special occasion for a long time. = 他们将会很久回忆这个特殊的场合。


虚拟语气:
If I could reminisce about my past, I would choose to relive my happy memories. = 如果我能回忆我的过去，我会选择重温我的快乐回忆。
I wish I could reminisce about my grandfather more often. = 我希望我能更频繁地回忆起我的祖父。


被动语态:
The past is often reminisced about with fondness. = 过去常常被怀旧地回忆。
Their adventures were reminisced about by everyone at the party. = 他们的冒险经历被聚会上的每个人都回忆起来。


疑问句:
Do you often reminisce about your childhood? = 你经常回忆你的童年吗？
What do you usually reminisce about with your friends? = 你通常和朋友们回忆些什么？


否定句:
I don't often reminisce about unpleasant experiences. = 我不经常回忆不愉快的经历。
They didn't reminisce about the difficult times they had faced. = 他们没有回忆他们所面临的困难时期。


PHRASE->
reminisce about = 回忆...
reminisce fondly =  fondly 回忆
reminisce with = 与...回忆
fondly reminisce = 深情地回忆
happily reminisce = 开心地回忆
