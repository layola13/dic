e-friend:/ˈiː.frɛnd/ | n. |电子朋友，网络朋友 | An e-friend is a friend you have met and communicate with primarily online, through social media, email, or other digital platforms.  They may or may not be people you have met in person. |例句：I've made several e-friends through online gaming. 我通过网络游戏交了好几个电子朋友。

例句：She maintains a close relationship with her e-friends despite living far apart. 尽管相隔遥远，她仍与她的电子朋友保持着密切的关系。


近义词：online friend, internet friend, virtual friend | 反义词：real-life friend, in-person friend | 词性: noun
