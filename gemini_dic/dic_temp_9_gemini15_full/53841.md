disconsolations:/ˌdɪskənˈsəˈleɪʃənz/| n.|令人沮丧的事物；悲伤；失望|The feeling of being disconsolate; a source of unhappiness or disappointment.  Disconsolations are things that cause sadness, dejection, or lack of hope. They are the opposite of consolations. |例句：The constant rain added to her disconsolations.  持续的雨雪加剧了她的沮丧。

例句：His financial troubles were a source of great disconsolation to him. 他的经济困难给他带来了极大的沮丧。


近义词：disappointments, sorrows, griefs, woes, troubles

反义词：consolations, joys, delights, pleasures

词性：名词
