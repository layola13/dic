scorpion-like:/ˈskɔːrpɪənlaɪk/| adj.|像蝎子的；蝎子似的|Resembling a scorpion in appearance or behavior, especially in being venomous or menacing.  The word combines "scorpion" (a venomous arachnid) with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The plant had scorpion-like thorns. 这植物长着像蝎子一样的刺。

The creature moved with scorpion-like stealth.  那生物以蝎子般偷偷摸摸的方式移动。|近义词：venomous, menacing, threatening, dangerous|反义词：harmless, benign, innocuous
