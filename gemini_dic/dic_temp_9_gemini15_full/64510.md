erosions:/ɪˈroʊʒənz/| n.|侵蚀，腐蚀|The gradual destruction or wearing away of something.|例句：The coastal erosions were caused by the strong waves.  海岸侵蚀是由强浪造成的。|例句：Years of wind and rain had caused significant erosions to the stonework.  多年的风吹雨打对石材造成了严重的侵蚀。|近义词：corrosion, deterioration, wearing away|反义词：construction, accretion, building up|名词
