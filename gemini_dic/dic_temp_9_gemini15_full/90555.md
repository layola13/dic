hugger:/ˈhʌɡər/| n. |拥抱者，紧抱者，(尤指)紧紧拥抱的人|A person who hugs a lot, often tightly.  Someone who is very affectionate and physically demonstrative. |例句：He's such a hugger; he greets everyone with a big hug. 他是个很爱拥抱的人；他见到每个人都热情拥抱。|例句：She's a bit of a hugger, always wanting to cuddle up. 她有点爱拥抱，总是想依偎在一起。|近义词：cuddler, snuggler|反义词：distant, aloof


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hugs his children every night. = 他每天晚上都拥抱他的孩子们。
She hugs her friends whenever they meet. = 她每次见到朋友都会拥抱他们。

一般过去时:
He hugged his wife goodbye. = 他拥抱了他的妻子道别。
They hugged each other tightly after the reunion. = 他们重逢后紧紧拥抱对方。

现在完成时:
I have hugged my cat many times today. = 我今天已经抱过我的猫很多次了。
She has hugged all her relatives. = 她已经拥抱了她所有的亲戚。

过去完成时:
He had hugged her before he left for the airport. = 他在去机场之前已经拥抱了她。
They had hugged each other before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始之前他们已经拥抱了彼此。

将来时:
I will hug you when I see you. = 我见到你时会拥抱你。
She will hug her son when he arrives. = 她儿子到达时会拥抱他。

虚拟语气:
If I saw her, I would hug her. = 如果我看到她，我会拥抱她。
I wish I could hug my grandmother one last time. = 我希望我能最后一次拥抱我的祖母。

被动语态:
The child was hugged by his mother. = 这个孩子被他的母亲抱住了。

疑问句:

否定句:
