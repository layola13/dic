cudgeling:/ˈkʌdʒəlɪŋ/| v.|  连续猛击；苦思冥想 |  Repeatedly striking with a cudgel; to think hard and long. |例句：He was cudgeling his brains trying to solve the problem. 他绞尽脑汁试图解决这个问题。

例句：The prisoners were cudgeling each other in a desperate fight for survival. 囚犯们为了生存而互相猛击。


近义词：beating, striking, pondering, racking one's brains

反义词：resting, relaxing, ignoring


词源:  From "cudgel," a short, heavy stick used as a weapon, ultimately from Old French *cognel.  The "-ing" suffix indicates continuous action.
