show-offs:/ʃoʊˈɔːfs/| n.|炫耀者，爱卖弄的人|People who like to show off their abilities or possessions.  They often do this to impress others, sometimes excessively or boastfully.|例句：He's such a show-off; he always talks about his achievements. 他是个十足的炫耀者；他总是夸夸其谈自己的成就。|例句：The show-offs at the party annoyed many guests. 派对上的那些爱炫耀的人惹恼了许多客人。|近义词：boasters, braggarts, egotists|反义词：humble, modest|名词
