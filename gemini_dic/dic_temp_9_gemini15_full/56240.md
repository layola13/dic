dog-end:/ˈdɒɡ.ɛnd/|/ˈdɑːɡ.ɛnd/| n. |烟蒂，香烟屁股|The last part of a cigarette, cigar, or similar item after it has been smoked.  It is often discarded. |例句：He flicked the dog-end into the ashtray. 他把烟蒂弹进烟灰缸。

例句：I found a dog-end on the pavement. 我在人行道上发现了一个烟蒂。


近义词：butt, cigarette end, cigar end|反义词：None

dog-end of something:  the remaining, unwanted, or least valuable part of something.  
dog-end of a project: 项目的剩余部分（通常指不重要的部分）
dog-end of the day: 一天的最后时刻
dog-end of a meeting: 会议的尾声
dog-end of a career: 事业的晚期
