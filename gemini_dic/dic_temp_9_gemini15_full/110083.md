lichenoid:/ˈlaɪkənɔɪd/ | adj. |地衣状的；似地衣的 | Resembling a lichen in appearance or structure.  The term often describes skin lesions that have a flat, slightly raised, and often scaly appearance similar to lichens. | Example: The patient presented with lichenoid lesions on their arms.  患者手臂上出现了地衣状皮损。 The biopsy revealed lichenoid changes in the skin. 活检显示皮肤有地衣样改变。|近义词：lichenous|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be terms describing contrasting skin lesions (e.g., smooth, vesicular).|


USAGE->
