distempering: /dɪsˈtɛmpərɪŋ/ | v. | 使坏脾气；使生气；使（绘画颜料）调和；破坏；使…变坏 | To put someone in a bad mood; to mix paints; to spoil or damage something. |  例句：The children's constant quarreling was distempering their parents. 孩子们不断的争吵使他们的父母很生气。 The artist carefully distempered the paints before applying them to the canvas.  艺术家在将颜料涂到画布上之前仔细地调和了它们。 | 近义词：irritating, infuriating, vexing, spoiling, damaging | 反义词：soothing, calming, pleasing, improving
