contestation:/ˌkɒntɛˈsteɪʃən/|/kɑːntɛˈsteɪʃən/ | n. |竞争；争论；质疑 |The act of contesting; a dispute or struggle;  a challenge to something.  It often implies a strong disagreement or opposition. |例句：The election was marked by fierce contestation between the two main parties.  这次选举以两大政党之间的激烈竞争为标志。

The ongoing contestation over the proposed new law has delayed its implementation.  关于拟议中的新法律的持续争论已经延缓了它的实施。


近义词：dispute, contention, debate, challenge, opposition |反义词：agreement, accord, consensus, harmony

词源:
Comes from the verb "conteste," meaning "to dispute" or "to contend," ultimately deriving from the Latin "contestari," meaning "to bear witness together," from "con-" (together) and "testari" (to bear witness).  The suffix "-ation" forms a noun denoting an action or process.
