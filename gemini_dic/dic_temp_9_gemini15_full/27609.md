cancellations:/ˌkænsəˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |取消；作废 |The plural form of "cancellation," referring to multiple instances of something being canceled.  It signifies the act of stopping or ending something that was planned or scheduled. |例句：The airline announced a series of cancellations due to the storm. 航空公司宣布由于暴风雨而取消一系列航班。|例句：Numerous cancellations were reported following the announcement of the strike.  罢工宣布后，据报道有多起取消事件。|近义词：withdrawals, annulments |反义词：confirmations, approvals |名词
