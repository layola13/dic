half-rotten:/ˌhæfˈrɑːtn/| adj. |半腐烂的；部分腐败的 |Partially decayed or decomposed.  The adjective describes something that is only partly rotten, not completely spoiled. |例句：The half-rotten apple was still usable for applesauce.  半腐烂的苹果仍然可以用来做苹果酱。

The fruit was half-rotten and covered in mold.  水果半腐烂了，上面长满了霉菌。|近义词：partially decayed, partially spoiled, decaying, rotting |反义词：fresh, unspoiled, sound
