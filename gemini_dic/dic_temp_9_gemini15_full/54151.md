disenroll:/ˌdɪsɪnˈroʊl/ | vt. | 退学；注销；取消注册 | To officially remove someone from a course, school, or program.  | Example:  She decided to disenroll from the advanced math class because it was too difficult. 她决定从高等数学课退学，因为太难了。  He was disenrolled from the university for failing to meet the academic requirements. 他因未达到学术要求而被大学除名。 | 近义词：withdraw,unregister,deregister | 反义词：enroll,register,enlist
