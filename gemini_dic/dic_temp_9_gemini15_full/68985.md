词源:
The word "fatiguesome" is derived from the word "fatigue," which comes from the Middle French word "fatiguer" (to tire), ultimately deriving from the Latin word "fatigare" (to weary, tire).  The suffix "-some" is added to create the adjective, meaning "causing or full of."
