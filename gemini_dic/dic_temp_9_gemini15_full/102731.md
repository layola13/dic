janitor:/ˈdʒænɪtər/ | n. |看门人，清洁工 | A janitor is a person employed to clean and maintain a building.  They are responsible for tasks such as cleaning floors, emptying trash cans, and making minor repairs. |The janitor cleaned the spills in the hallway.  看门人清扫了走廊里的污渍。| custodian, caretaker, cleaner |  (无明确反义词，可考虑"building owner"或"manager")| 名词
