proposals:/prəˈpoʊzəlz/ | n. | 建议；提议；求婚 | Suggestions or plans of action, often formally presented.  It can also refer to an offer of marriage. |

例句：

The committee considered several proposals for improving the city's transportation system.  = 委员会考虑了几个改进城市交通系统的建议。

He nervously presented his marriage proposal to his girlfriend. = 他紧张地向女朋友提出了求婚。


近义词：suggestions, offers, plans, schemes, bids, overtures

反义词：rejections, refusals


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company regularly reviews proposals from its employees. = 公司定期审查员工的建议。
She often makes proposals to improve efficiency. = 她经常提出提高效率的建议。

一般过去时:
He submitted his proposals last week. = 他上周提交了他的建议。
They discussed several proposals at the meeting. = 他们在会议上讨论了几个建议。

现在完成时:
We have received many proposals for the new project. = 我们收到了很多关于新项目 的建议。
She has already presented her proposals to the board. = 她已经向董事会提交了她的建议。

过去完成时:
They had already considered similar proposals before. = 他们之前已经考虑过类似的建议了。
He had submitted his proposals before the deadline. = 他在截止日期之前提交了他的建议。

将来时:
We will review all proposals next month. = 我们下个月将审查所有建议。
They will present their proposals at the conference. = 他们将在会议上提出他们的建议。

虚拟语气:
If I had a better proposal, I would submit it. = 如果我有一个更好的建议，我会提交它。
I wish I had presented a more convincing proposal. = 我希望我当时提出了一个更有说服力的建议。

被动语态:
The proposals were carefully reviewed by the committee. = 这些建议受到了委员会的仔细审查。
His proposal was rejected by the board. = 他的建议被董事会否决了。

疑问句:
What proposals do you have for the project? = 你对这个项目有什么建议？
Have you seen the proposals yet? = 你看过这些建议了吗？

否定句:
They didn't accept any of the proposals. = 他们没有接受任何建议。
We haven't finalized the proposals yet. = 我们还没有最终确定这些建议。


PHRASE->
