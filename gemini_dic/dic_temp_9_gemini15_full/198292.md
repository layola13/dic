shouldered:/ˈʃoʊldərd/ | v. | 扛，肩负，承担 | To carry or support something on one's shoulders; to assume responsibility for something. |例句：He shouldered the heavy bag. 他扛着沉重的包。|例句：She shouldered the responsibility for the project's failure. 她承担了项目失败的责任。|近义词：carried, bore, assumed, accepted |反义词：abandoned, relinquished, shirked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shoulders the burden of his family. = 他承担着家庭的重担。
They shoulder the responsibility for the team's success. = 他们承担着团队成功的责任。

一般过去时:
She shouldered her way through the crowd. = 她挤过人群。
He shouldered the blame for the accident. = 他承担了事故的责任。

现在完成时:
They have shouldered many hardships in their lives. = 他们一生中经历了许多苦难。
She has shouldered the responsibility of raising her children alone. = 她独自承担了抚养孩子的责任。

过去完成时:
He had shouldered the weight of the world on his shoulders. = 他曾肩负着沉重的世界。
They had shouldered the task before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了任务。

将来时:
He will shoulder the responsibility for the project. = 他将承担该项目的责任。
She will shoulder the burden of her debt. = 她将承担她的债务负担。

虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I could shoulder this heavy load. = 如果我更强壮，我就能扛起这个重担。
I wish I could shoulder the burden of their problems. = 我希望我能承担他们的问题。

被动语态:
The responsibility was shouldered by the team leader. = 责任由团队领导承担。
The burden should not be shouldered by one person alone. = 负担不应该由一个人独自承担。

疑问句:
Who shouldered the responsibility for the mistake? = 谁承担了这个错误的责任？
Can he shoulder the weight of the project? = 他能承担这个项目的重量吗？

否定句:
He did not shoulder the responsibility. = 他没有承担责任。
She couldn't shoulder the burden alone. = 她不能独自承担重担。


PHRASE->
shoulder to shoulder = 肩并肩
shoulder the burden = 承担重担
shoulder a responsibility = 承担责任
shoulder the blame = 承担责任
shoulder to shoulder with = 与...并肩作战
