imitative:/ɪˈmɪtətɪv/| adj.| 模仿的；模拟的；学习的 |  Imitative describes something that copies or imitates something else.  It suggests a deliberate attempt to reproduce something, often in an artistic or creative context.  |例句：Her painting style is highly imitative of the Impressionists.  她的绘画风格高度模仿印象派。

例句：The child's speech is imitative; he repeats everything he hears.  孩子的语言是模仿性的；他重复听到的一切。


近义词：copycat, mimicking, simulative, derivative | 反义词：original, innovative, inventive | 词性: adjective


词源:  From Latin *imitātivus, from imitārī "to imitate."  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *mey- "to exchange, change."
