carbonizing:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |使碳化；使成焦炭|To convert (a substance) into carbon or a carbon-like material.  This process often involves heating organic matter in the absence of oxygen.|例句：The wood was carbonizing in the intense heat.  木材在高温下正在碳化。

The process of carbonizing the coal increased its efficiency as fuel.  对煤进行碳化处理提高了其作为燃料的效率。


近义词：charring, coking |反义词：decarbonizing
