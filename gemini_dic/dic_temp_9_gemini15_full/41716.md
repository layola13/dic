corroboratory:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtəri/ | adj. | 证实的；确证的 | Providing evidence or support for a statement, theory, or finding.  | The corroboratory evidence strengthened the prosecutor's case.  证实的证据加强了检察官的案情。 | confirmatory, supportive, validating | contradictory, refuting | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence is corroboratory. = 证据是确凿的。
This statement is corroboratory of his claim. = 这份声明证实了他的说法。


一般过去时:
The witness testimony was corroboratory. = 证人的证词是证实的。
The new findings were corroboratory of the old theory. = 新的发现证实了旧的理论。


现在完成时:
The investigation has yielded corroboratory evidence. = 调查已经产生了证实的证据。
Several studies have provided corroboratory data. = 几项研究提供了佐证数据。


过去完成时:
The police had already obtained corroboratory evidence before the arrest. = 警方在逮捕之前就已经获得了证实的证据。
The researchers had found corroboratory results in their previous experiments. = 研究人员在之前的实验中已经发现了佐证结果。


将来时:
Further investigation will likely produce corroboratory findings. = 进一步调查可能会产生证实的调查结果。
We expect the upcoming tests to provide corroboratory data. = 我们预计即将进行的测试将提供佐证数据。


虚拟语气:
If we had more corroboratory evidence, the case would be stronger. = 如果我们有更多证实的证据，这个案子就会更强有力。
I wish we had some corroboratory evidence for this hypothesis. = 我希望我们对这个假设有一些佐证证据。


被动语态:
The theory was corroborated by recent studies. = 最近的研究证实了这一理论。
His alibi was corroborated by independent witnesses. = 他的不在场证明得到了独立证人的证实。


疑问句:
Is there any corroboratory evidence to support his statement? = 有什么证据支持他的说法吗？
Is this finding corroboratory of the earlier research? = 这一发现是否证实了早期的研究？


否定句:
There was no corroboratory evidence to support the defendant's claim. = 没有证据支持被告的主张。
The study did not provide any corroboratory results. = 这项研究没有提供任何佐证结果。
