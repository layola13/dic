silentness: /ˈsaɪlənts/ | n. |寂静，沉默 |The state or quality of being silent; absence of noise or sound.  It often implies a sense of peace or stillness, but can also suggest unease or tension depending on the context. |例句：The silentness of the forest was broken only by the chirping of crickets. 森林的寂静只被蟋蟀的鸣叫打破了。

The silentness in the room was unnerving. 房间里的寂静令人不安。|近义词：quiet, stillness, hush, calm, peace |反义词：noise, clamor, uproar, din, sound
