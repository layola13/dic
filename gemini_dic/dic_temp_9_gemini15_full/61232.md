email:/ˈiːmeɪl/ | n. |电子邮件，电邮 |A message sent electronically from one person to another.  Emails are commonly used for communication, both personal and professional. |例句：I sent an email to my boss. 我给老板发了一封邮件。|例句： I received an email from a friend. 我收到朋友的一封邮件。|近义词：e-mail, electronic mail |反义词:letter (traditional mail)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I send emails every day. = 我每天都发邮件。
He emails his clients regularly. = 他定期给客户发邮件。


一般过去时:
She emailed me yesterday. = 她昨天给我发了邮件。
They emailed the report last night. = 他们昨晚发了报告。


现在完成时:
I have emailed him twice this week. = 我这周已经给他发了邮件两次。
She has not emailed back yet. = 她还没有回复邮件。


过去完成时:
I had emailed the documents before the meeting. = 我在会议前已经发了文件。
They had emailed each other several times before they met in person. = 他们在见面之前已经通过邮件联系过好几次了。


将来时:
I will email you the details tomorrow. = 我明天会把细节发邮件给你。
He will email the contract to you soon. = 他很快会把合同发邮件给你。


虚拟语气:
If I had emailed him sooner, he would have replied. = 如果我早点给他发邮件，他会回复的。
I wish I had emailed her before she left. = 我真希望在她离开之前给她发邮件。


被动语态:
The email was sent yesterday. = 邮件是昨天发的。
The report is being emailed to all staff. = 报告正在发邮件给所有员工。


疑问句:
Have you emailed him yet? = 你给他发邮件了吗？
Did she email you back? = 她回复你的邮件了吗？


否定句:
I didn't email him. = 我没有给他发邮件。
She hasn't emailed me yet. = 她还没给我发邮件。


PHRASE->
email address: 电子邮件地址
email client: 邮件客户端
email server: 邮件服务器
email attachment: 邮件附件
reply to email: 回复邮件
