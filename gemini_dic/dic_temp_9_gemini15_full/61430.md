embolismic:/ɛmˈbɒlɪzmɪk/ | adj. |闰年的；插入的；额外添加的 | Relating to or characteristic of an embolism or intercalary period; inserted or added.  The word often refers to an intercalary month or year added to a calendar to keep it synchronized with the solar year.  | Example:  The embolismic month was added to the lunar calendar to correct for the discrepancy between the lunar and solar cycles.  闰月被添加到阴历中以纠正阴历和阳历之间的差异。|近义词：intercalary, supplementary |反义词：ordinary, regular
