resuscitation:/ˌrɛsʌsɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |复苏；复活；抢救 |The act of reviving someone from unconsciousness or apparent death.  It involves measures to restore breathing and circulation. |例句：The paramedics performed resuscitation on the accident victim.  急救人员对事故受害者进行了抢救。

例句：After several minutes of CPR, resuscitation was successful.  经过几分钟的心肺复苏术后，抢救成功了。


近义词：revival, recovery, revitalization |反义词：death, demise
