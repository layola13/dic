/ˈɛɡzɑː/| n. |例子，示例|An example is a thing characteristic of its kind or illustrating a general principle.|例句：He gave me an example of how to do it. 他给了我一个怎么做它的例子。|近义词：illustration, instance, specimen |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is an example of good writing. = 这是优秀写作的一个例子。
That serves as an example of his kindness. = 那可以作为他善良的一个例子。


一般过去时:
The teacher gave an example of a complex sentence. = 老师举了一个复杂句子的例子。
She showed us an example of how to solve the equation. = 她给我们展示了解决这个方程式的例子。


现在完成时:
I have given many examples in my presentation. = 我在我的演示中已经举了很多例子。
They have seen many examples of this phenomenon. = 他们已经见过很多这种现象的例子。


过去完成时:
He had already given several examples before the question was asked. = 在问题被问到之前，他已经举了好几个例子了。
We had never seen such a clear example before. = 我们以前从未见过如此清晰的例子。


将来时:
I will give you more examples tomorrow. = 我明天会给你更多例子。
She will provide examples to support her argument. = 她将提供例子来支持她的论点。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would give you more examples. = 如果我有更多时间，我会给你更多例子。
I wish I had a better example to illustrate my point. = 我希望我有一个更好的例子来阐明我的观点。


被动语态:
Many examples were given during the lecture. = 讲座中举了很多例子。
The example was carefully chosen to illustrate the point. = 这个例子是精心挑选的，以说明这一点。


疑问句:
Can you give me an example? = 你能给我举个例子吗？
What is an example of a metaphor? = 比喻的例子是什么？


否定句:
There were no examples of that behavior. = 没有那种行为的例子。
I cannot provide a relevant example at this moment. = 我现在无法提供相关的例子。


PHRASE->
for example = 例如
by way of example = 以...为例
a case in point = 一个恰当的例子
to illustrate = 为了说明
take for example = 以...为例
