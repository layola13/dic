geologist:/dʒiˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |地质学家|A geologist is a scientist who studies the Earth, its structure, substance, history, and the processes that act upon it.  Geologists work in a variety of settings, from universities and research institutions to mining companies and government agencies.|例句：The geologist examined the rock samples carefully.  这位地质学家仔细检查了岩石样本。|例句：Many geologists believe that the earthquake was caused by tectonic plate movement.许多地质学家认为地震是由板块运动引起的。|近义词：geophysicist, earth scientist|反义词：N/A
