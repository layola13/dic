hoarfrost:/ˈhɔːfrɒst/| n.|白霜，霜|A deposit of white frost formed on cold surfaces by the freezing of water vapor in the air.|例句：The trees were covered in a thick layer of hoarfrost. 树木上覆盖着一层厚厚的霜。|近义词：frost, rime|反义词：None.


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->
a layer of hoarfrost = 一层白霜
hoarfrost on the ground = 地上的白霜
heavy hoarfrost = 厚厚的霜
beautiful hoarfrost = 美丽的白霜
patterns of hoarfrost = 白霜的图案
