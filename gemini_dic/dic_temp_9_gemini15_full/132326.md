nondeflationary: /ˌnɒnˌdiːˈfleɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 非通货紧缩的 | Not characterized by deflation; showing no tendency toward a decrease in the general price level.  It describes an economic situation where prices are not falling, either remaining stable or rising. |


例句：

The government implemented nondeflationary policies to stimulate economic growth.  政府实施了非通货紧缩政策以刺激经济增长。

The central bank's actions prevented a nondeflationary environment from turning into a deflationary spiral.  央行的行动阻止了非通货紧缩的环境演变成通货紧缩的恶性循环。


近义词：inflationary (if prices are rising), stable (if prices are remaining constant)


反义词：deflationary


词性：形容词
