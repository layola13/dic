montezuma:/ˌmɒntɪˈzuːmə/ /ˌmɑːntɪˈzuːmə/| n. |蒙特祖马（墨西哥阿兹特克帝国皇帝）|Montezuma refers to any of the Aztec emperors, most commonly Montezuma II, the last Aztec emperor before the Spanish conquest.  The name is often used to evoke a sense of exoticism, ancient power, and ultimately, downfall.  |The story of Montezuma and Cortés is a classic tale of conquest and cultural clash. |近义词：Aztec emperor |反义词：无
