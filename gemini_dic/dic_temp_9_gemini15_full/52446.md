diecious:/daɪˈiːʃəs/| adj.|雌雄异株的；雌雄异体的|Having the male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.  This is in contrast to monoecious plants, which have both male and female reproductive organs on the same plant.| Example: Holly is a diecious plant, meaning some trees bear only male flowers, and others only female flowers.  冬青是雌雄异株的植物，这意味着有些树只开雄花，有些树只开雌花。|近义词：gonochoristic|反义词：monoecious
