reciting:/rɪˈsaɪtɪŋ/| v.|背诵；朗诵；列举 |The act of repeating something from memory, often aloud.  It can also refer to the act of listing or enumerating items. |例句：She was reciting poetry. 她正在背诵诗歌。

例句：He recited the names of all fifty states. 他背诵出了所有五十个州的名字。


近义词：repeating, rehearsing, enumerating, recounting |反义词：forgetting, omitting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recites the poem every day. 他每天都背诵这首诗。
She recites the alphabet to her students. 她给学生们朗诵字母表。

一般过去时:
He recited a Shakespearean sonnet last night. 他昨晚背诵了一首莎士比亚十四行诗。
She recited the pledge of allegiance at the ceremony. 她在仪式上宣读了效忠誓言。

现在完成时:
He has recited this poem many times. 他已经背诵过这首诗很多次了。
She has recited the entire play from memory. 她已经完整地从记忆中背诵了整部戏。

过去完成时:
He had recited the poem before the audience arrived.  在他到达之前他已经背诵过这首诗了。
She had recited the script several times before the performance.  演出前她已经排练过几次剧本了。

将来时:
He will recite a new poem at the school event. 他将在学校活动上朗诵一首新诗。
She will recite the names of the winners. 她将宣读获奖者的名字。


虚拟语气:
If he could recite the whole play, he would be famous.如果他能背诵整部戏，他会出名。
I wish I could recite poetry as well as she can. 我希望我能像她一样背诵诗歌。


被动语态:
The poem was recited by many students. 这首诗被许多学生背诵。
The list of names was recited by the announcer.  名单由播音员宣读。


疑问句:
Will you recite the poem for us? 你会为我们朗诵这首诗吗？
Can she recite the multiplication tables? 她能背诵乘法表吗？


否定句:
He did not recite the poem correctly. 他没有正确地背诵这首诗。
She couldn't recite the poem from memory. 她无法从记忆中背诵这首诗。
