saddled:/ˈsædəld/| vt. adj.| 1. 给…装鞍；2. 使承担；3.  burdened with (a problem, task, etc.) | to put a saddle on (a horse, etc.) | The cowboy saddled his horse before the long ride. 牛仔在长途骑行前给他的马装上了鞍子。|近义词：burden, load, encumber | 反义词：unburden, unload

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saddles his horse every morning. = 他每天早上都给他的马装鞍。
She saddles her responsibilities with grace. = 她优雅地承担起她的责任。


一般过去时:
They saddled their mules before the trek. = 他们在远足前给他们的骡子装上了鞍子。
The unexpected problem saddled the team with extra work. = 这个意外的问题给团队带来了额外的工作。


现在完成时:
He has saddled many horses in his life. = 他一生中给许多马装过鞍。
The company has been saddled with debt for years. = 这家公司多年来一直背负着债务。


过去完成时:
They had saddled their horses before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前，他们已经给马装上了鞍子。
The project had already saddled us with considerable costs before we realized the flaws. = 在我们意识到缺陷之前，这个项目已经给我们带来了相当大的成本。


将来时:
She will saddle her horse and ride to the town. = 她将给她的马装鞍并骑到镇上。
The new regulation will saddle small businesses with additional paperwork. = 新规定将使小型企业承担额外的文书工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would saddle it and ride. = 如果我有一匹马，我会给它装鞍并骑上它。
If the company hadn't taken on that project, it wouldn't have been saddled with so much debt. = 如果公司没有承担那个项目，它就不会背负如此巨额的债务。


被动语态:
The horse was saddled by the groom. = 马由马夫装上了鞍子。
The team was saddled with an impossible task. = 团队承担了一项不可能完成的任务。


疑问句:
Has the horse been saddled yet? = 马已经装鞍了吗？
Was the project saddled with unnecessary delays? = 这个项目是否因为不必要的延误而增加了负担？


否定句:
He didn't saddle his horse before riding. = 他骑马前没有给马装鞍。
The government didn't want to saddle taxpayers with higher taxes. = 政府不想让纳税人承担更高的税收。


PHRASE->
be saddled with = 承担…；背负…
saddle up = 给…装鞍；准备出发
saddle sore = 骑马引起的臀部疼痛
saddle bag = 马鞍袋
saddle blanket = 马鞍毯
