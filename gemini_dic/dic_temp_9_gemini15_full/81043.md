graffito:/ɡrəˈfiːtoʊ/|/ˈɡræfɪtoʊ/| n. |涂鸦；壁画 |A graffito is a drawing or writing scratched or scribbled on a wall or other surface.  It is often informal and can range from simple tags to elaborate artwork. |Example: The ancient Roman graffito depicted a gladiatorial contest.  这幅古罗马壁画描绘了一场角斗比赛。 The building was covered in graffito.  这栋建筑物上布满了涂鸦。|近义词：inscription, mural, doodle, tagging |反义词：clean surface, blank wall
