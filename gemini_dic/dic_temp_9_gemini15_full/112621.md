low-end:/ˈloʊ.ɛnd/ | adj. |低端的，低价位的|  Relating to or denoting products or services of inferior quality or at a low price.  It often implies a lack of sophistication or advanced features compared to higher-priced alternatives. |例句：The store sells low-end electronics. 这家商店销售低端电子产品。

例句：He bought a low-end laptop because he didn't have much money. 他买了一台低端笔记本电脑，因为他钱不多。


近义词：cheap, inexpensive, budget, inferior, basic

反义词：high-end, premium, luxury, superior, advanced

词性：形容词
