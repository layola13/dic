reiterates:/ˈriːɪtəreɪts/| vt.|重复；重申；再讲一遍|To say something again or repeatedly.|The president reiterated his commitment to economic reform. 总统重申了他对经济改革的承诺。|The teacher reiterated the instructions several times.老师反复重申了指示。|repeat, restate, reaffirm|deny, contradict|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reiterates his point every meeting. 他每次会议都重复他的观点。
She reiterates the importance of safety regularly. 她定期重申安全的重要性。

一般过去时:
The speaker reiterated his key arguments. 演讲者重申了他的主要论点。
He reiterated his apology to the victims. 他向受害者再次道歉。


现在完成时:
She has reiterated her support for the project. 她已经重申了她对该项目的支持。
The government has reiterated its commitment to environmental protection. 政府已经重申了其对环境保护的承诺。


过去完成时:
He had reiterated his concerns before the meeting. 他在会议前已经重申了他的担忧。
They had reiterated their position many times. 他们已经多次重申了他们的立场。


将来时:
He will reiterate his position in the upcoming debate. 他将在即将到来的辩论中重申他的立场。
She will reiterate the instructions before the test. 她将在考试前重申指示。


虚拟语气:
If he reiterated his request, they might comply. 如果他再次提出请求，他们可能会同意。
I wish he had reiterated his offer. 我希望他当时再次提出他的提议。


被动语态:
His concerns were reiterated by several members of the committee. 他的担忧被委员会的几位成员重申了。
The company's commitment to quality was reiterated in the annual report. 公司对质量的承诺在年度报告中得到了重申。


疑问句:
Will he reiterate his statement tomorrow? 他明天会重申他的声明吗？
Did she reiterate her objections? 她重申了她的反对意见吗？


否定句:
He did not reiterate his earlier statement. 他没有重申他之前的声明。
She did not reiterate the instructions. 她没有重复说明。


PHRASE->
