retractation: /ˌrɛtrækˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 撤回；收回；否认 | The act of withdrawing or taking back something previously said, done, or offered.  It often refers to a statement, claim, or accusation. | Example: The politician issued a retraction of his earlier statement after it was proven false.  政治家在之前的声明被证明是错误之后发表了撤回声明。|近义词：withdrawal, revocation, recantation |反义词：confirmation, reaffirmation | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The newspaper retracts its false report. = 报纸撤回了其不实的报道。
This company retracts its offer. = 这家公司撤回了它的报价。


一般过去时:
He retracted his confession. = 他撤回了他的供词。
She retracted her earlier statement. = 她撤回了她之前的声明。


现在完成时:
The scientist has retracted his controversial theory. = 科学家已经撤回了他的有争议的理论。
The company has retracted its product from the market. = 公司已将产品从市场上撤回。


过去完成时:
They had retracted their support before the election. = 他们在选举前就撤回了他们的支持。
He had retracted his accusations after discovering the truth. = 他在发现真相后撤回了他的指控。


将来时:
The magazine will retract the article next week. = 该杂志下周将撤回这篇文章。
She will likely retract her statement under pressure.  = 在压力下，她很可能会收回她的声明。


虚拟语气:
If he were honest, he would retract his lies. = 如果他诚实，他会收回他的谎言。
I wish they had retracted their hurtful words. = 我希望他们能收回那些伤人的话。


被动语态:
The article was retracted due to inaccuracies. = 这篇文章由于不准确而被撤回。
His statement was retracted after public outcry. = 他的声明在公众抗议后被撤回。


疑问句:
Will the company retract its faulty product? = 公司会召回其有缺陷的产品吗？
Has the newspaper retracted the libelous article? = 报纸撤回了诽谤性文章了吗？


否定句:
He refused to retract his statement. = 他拒绝收回他的声明。
The government did not retract its controversial policy. = 政府没有收回其有争议的政策。


PHRASE->
retraction of a statement = 声明的撤回
retraction of an offer = 报价的撤回
retraction of a claim = 索赔的撤回
retraction of support = 支持的撤回
full retraction = 完全撤回
