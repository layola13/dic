capitalization:/ˌkæpɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 大写；资本化；资本积累 | The act of writing or printing the first letter of a word in uppercase.  It also refers to the process of converting something into capital or accumulating capital. | Example: The capitalization of the company's name is important for branding.  这家公司名称的大写对于品牌推广很重要。  The capitalization of the new technology led to significant profits. 新技术的资本化带来了可观的利润。 |近义词：uppercasing, capitalisation (BrE) | 反义词：lowercase, small letters
