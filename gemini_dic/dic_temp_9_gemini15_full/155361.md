pharmacopoeic: /ˌfɑːrməkəˈpiːɪk/ | adj. | 药典的；药剂学的 | Relating to or contained in a pharmacopoeia (an official publication containing a list of medicinal drugs with their effects and methods of preparation).  | The pharmacopoeic standards ensure the quality and safety of the medicine.  药典标准确保药品的质量和安全。 | pharmaceutical, medicinal | non-pharmacopoeic |  形容词

词源:
From pharmacopoeia + -ic.  Pharmacopoeia itself comes from  Greek *pharmakon* ("drug, medicine") + *poiein* ("to make").  Therefore, the word literally means "relating to the making of medicines according to an official list".
