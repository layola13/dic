drudge:/drʌdʒ/| n., v.|苦工；苦差事；辛劳工作；(使)辛劳工作|A drudge is a person who does dull, hard, and tiring work.  It can also refer to the work itself.  As a verb, it means to work hard and tirelessly at a menial task.|例句：He was a drudge in the factory, working long hours for little pay. 他在工厂里是个苦力，长时间工作却薪水微薄。

例句：She drudged away at the report, barely pausing for lunch. 她辛勤地撰写报告，几乎没有停下来吃午饭。|近义词：toil, slave, slog, grind,  (n.) hack,  (v.)  plod|反义词：relax, rest, leisure, recreation|名词，动词
