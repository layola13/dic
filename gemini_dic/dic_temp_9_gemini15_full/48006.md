decipherable: /dɪˈsaɪfərəbl/ | adj. | 可解读的，可破译的 |  Able to be deciphered; understandable.  | The message, though coded, was decipherable. 这条信息虽然经过编码，但仍然可以解读。  The handwriting on the ancient scroll was barely decipherable. 古卷上的字迹几乎无法辨认。 | understandable, intelligible, readable | indecipherable, unreadable, unintelligible


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The code is decipherable. = 代码是可以破译的。
This text is decipherable with a little effort. = 这段文字只要稍微努力就能解读。


一般过去时:
The message was decipherable. = 这条信息是可以解读的。
The inscription was decipherable only after years of study. = 只有经过多年的研究后，才能解读这块铭文。


现在完成时:
The ancient script has been decipherable since the discovery of the Rosetta Stone. = 自从罗塞塔石碑被发现以来，古代文字就已被破译。
The puzzle has finally become decipherable. = 这个谜题终于可以解开了。


过去完成时:
