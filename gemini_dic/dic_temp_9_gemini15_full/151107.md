parasitic:/ˌpærəˈsɪtɪk/ | adj. |寄生的；寄生虫的；依赖别人的| Relating to or denoting a parasite or parasitism.  A parasitic organism lives on or in a host and gets its food from or at the expense of its host, typically without killing it.  This word can also describe something that lives off or exploits something else. |例句：Many plants have parasitic relationships with other species. 许多植物与其他物种存在寄生关系。


例句：The parasitic fungus infected the roots of the tree.  这种寄生真菌感染了树根。


近义词：dependent, exploitative


反义词：self-sufficient, independent


词性：形容词
