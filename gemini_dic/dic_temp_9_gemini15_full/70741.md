fine-tune:/faɪnˈtjuːn/| vt.|微调，精细调整|To make small adjustments to something in order to improve it or make it more effective.  It often implies a process of iterative refinement.|例句：The engineers fine-tuned the engine to improve its fuel efficiency. 工程师们微调了发动机以提高燃油效率。|例句：She fine-tuned her presentation after receiving feedback from her colleagues.  她在收到同事的反馈后微调了她的演示文稿。|近义词：adjust, refine, optimize, perfect|反义词：roughen, coarsen|动词
