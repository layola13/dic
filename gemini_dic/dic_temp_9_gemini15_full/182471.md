renotify:/ˌriːˈnoʊtɪfaɪ/| vt.|重新通知，再次通知|To notify again; to give notice a second time.|例句：The supplier will renotify us if the goods are delayed. 供应商如果货物延误会再次通知我们。  The bank will renotify the customer of any changes to their account. 银行会将任何账户变更重新通知客户。|近义词：reinform, remind, advise|反义词：ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They renotify customers of any changes. = 他们会重新通知客户任何更改。
We renotify the client regularly. = 我们定期重新通知客户。


一般过去时:
The company re-notified all its clients. = 公司重新通知了所有客户。
They re-notified me about the meeting. = 他们再次通知我会议的事情。


现在完成时:
We have re-notified him several times. = 我们已经几次重新通知他了。
They have re-notified the participants. = 他们已经重新通知了参与者。


过去完成时:
They had re-notified the participants before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经重新通知了参与者。
The bank had re-notified me of the change before I noticed it. = 我注意到变化之前银行已经重新通知我了。


将来时:
We will renotify you if there are any problems. = 如果有任何问题，我们会重新通知您。
They will renotify the customers tomorrow. = 他们明天将重新通知客户。


虚拟语气:
If they re-notified me earlier, I wouldn't have missed the deadline. = 如果他们早点重新通知我，我就不会错过截止日期了。
I wish they had re-notified me sooner. = 我希望他们早点重新通知我。


被动语态:
Customers are re-notified of any changes. = 客户会收到任何更改的重新通知。
The clients were re-notified about the delay. = 客户收到了关于延误的重新通知。


疑问句:
Will they renotify us? = 他们会重新通知我们吗？
Have you been re-notified about the meeting? = 你已经收到关于会议的重新通知了吗？


否定句:
They did not renotify us of the change. = 他们没有将更改重新通知我们。
We haven't been re-notified yet. = 我们还没有收到重新通知。
