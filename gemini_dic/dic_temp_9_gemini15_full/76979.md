gard:/ɡɑːrd/| n. |花园，园子|A piece of land, often near a house, that is used for growing flowers, fruit, or vegetables.|例句：We have a beautiful garden full of roses. 我们有一个种满玫瑰的漂亮花园。|近义词：yard, orchard, plot|反义词：wilderness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gardener tends the garden every day. = 园丁每天都打理花园。
We grow tomatoes in our garden. = 我们在花园里种植西红柿。


一般过去时:
They planted flowers in the garden last spring. = 他们去年春天在花园里种了花。
He watered the garden yesterday afternoon. = 他昨天下午浇了花园。


现在完成时:
We have harvested all the vegetables from the garden. = 我们已经收割了花园里所有的蔬菜。
They have been working in the garden all morning. = 他们整个上午都在花园里工作。


过去完成时:
They had already finished weeding the garden before it rained. = 下雨之前他们已经除完了花园里的杂草。
She had planted the seeds a week before the first sprouts appeared. =  第一批幼苗出现之前的一周，她已经播种了。


将来时:
We will plant new flowers in the garden next month. = 我们下个月将在花园里种新的花。
They will be building a new fence around the garden next week. = 他们下周将建造一个新的围栏围住花园。


虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger garden, I would grow more vegetables. = 如果我有一个更大的花园，我会种植更多的蔬菜。
I wish I had time to work in the garden more often. = 我希望我有更多的时间经常在花园里工作。


被动语态:
The garden is watered every morning. = 花园每天早上都被浇水。
The roses were planted by my grandmother. = 玫瑰花是我祖母种的。


疑问句:
Do you have a garden? = 你有花园吗？
What kind of flowers are growing in your garden? = 你的花园里种着什么花？


否定句:
We don't have much space for a garden. = 我们没有太多空间来弄个花园。
He didn't water the garden yesterday. = 他昨天没有浇花园。
