repurification: /ˌriːˌpjʊərɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 再净化；再次纯化 | The process of purifying something again, typically after it has become impure or contaminated.  It implies a previous purification process has already taken place. | Example: The repurification of the water supply was necessary after the chemical spill.  再次净化供水系统在化学品泄漏后是必要的。 |近义词：re-purification,  further purification, secondary purification | 反义词：contamination, pollution
