fracking:/ˈfrækɪŋ/| n. v.| 水力压裂法；水力裂缝法 |The process of injecting liquid at high pressure into subterranean rocks, boreholes, etc., so as to force open existing fissures and extract oil or gas.  |例句：Fracking is a controversial method of natural gas extraction. 水力压裂是一种有争议的天然气开采方法。|例句：The environmental impact of fracking is a major concern. 水力压裂的环境影响是一个主要问题。|近义词：hydraulic fracturing, hydrofracturing |反义词：conventional drilling (传统钻井) |名词，动词
