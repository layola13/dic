eye-rolling:/ˈaɪˌroʊlɪŋ/| n.|翻白眼，白眼|The act of rolling one's eyes, typically to express boredom, disapproval, or annoyance.  It is a nonverbal communication that conveys negative feelings.|例句：Her constant eye-rolling during the lecture showed her disinterest.  她上课时不断翻白眼，显示出她的不感兴趣。

例句：His eye-rolling was a clear sign that he wasn't impressed. 他翻白眼很明显地表示他不以为然。|近义词：scoffing, sneering, disdain|反义词：approval, admiration
