intersegments: /ˌɪntərˈsɛɡmənts/ | n. | 节间，节与节之间 |  Refers to the area or region between segments, particularly in segmented structures like the body of an arthropod (insect, crustacean etc.) or in geology referring to spaces between rock strata.  | Example: The intersegments of the caterpillar were clearly visible.  毛虫的体节间清晰可见。|近义词：internode, space between|反义词：segment, node
