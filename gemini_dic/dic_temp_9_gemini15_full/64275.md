equisetum:/ˌɛkwɪˈsiːtəm/ | n. |木贼属植物|A genus of about 15 species of mostly herbaceous perennial plants in the family Equisetaceae, characterized by their jointed, ribbed stems and whorls of scale-like leaves.  They are also known as horsetails or scouring rushes. |Example:  The fossilized remains of *Equisetum* provide valuable insights into ancient ecosystems.  木贼属植物的化石遗迹为了解古代生态系统提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词: horsetail, scouring rush |反义词:  (无直接反义词, 可考虑与开花植物等对比)|名词
