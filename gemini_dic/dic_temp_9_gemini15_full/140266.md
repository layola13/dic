offsite:/ˈɔːfsaɪt/ /ˈɒfsaɪt/| adj. adv. n.|场外的；异地的；不在现场的；|Located or occurring away from the usual or main place of work or activity.  An offsite meeting is held outside the usual office location.|例句：The team held an offsite meeting at a resort.  团队在一个度假村举行了异地会议。
The project's offsite data storage is secure. 项目的异地数据存储是安全的。|近义词：remote, external, away from the office|反义词：onsite, in-house, local|形容词，副词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting is offsite this week. = 本周会议在异地举行。
The data is stored offsite for security. = 数据为安全起见存储在异地。


一般过去时:
The training was offsite last month. = 上个月培训是在异地进行的。

We have had several successful offsite events. = 我们已经举办了几次成功的异地活动。
