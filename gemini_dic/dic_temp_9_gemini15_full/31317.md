chaps:/tʃæps/| n.|家伙们，伙计们，章节|The word "chaps" is a shortened form of "chap," which is an informal term for a man or boy.  It's often used in plural form ("chaps") to refer to a group of men.|例句：The chaps in the pub were having a lively discussion. 酒吧里的那些家伙们正在热烈讨论。|例句：The book is divided into several chapters. 这本书分为几个章节。|近义词：guys, fellows, blokes, men|反义词：无
