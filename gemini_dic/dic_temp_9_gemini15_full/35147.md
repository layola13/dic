clove:/kloʊv/| n.|丁香；丁香花蕾；丁香色|A clove is the dried flower bud of a tropical evergreen tree (Syzygium aromaticum). It is used as a spice and has a strong, pungent aroma and flavor.|The recipe calls for two cloves of garlic.  这个食谱需要两瓣大蒜。|The soup had a distinct clove flavor. 这汤有明显的丁香味。|spice, garlic|—|
