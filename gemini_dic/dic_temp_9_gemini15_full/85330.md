harpooner:/hɑːˈpuːnər/| n. |捕鲸手；用鱼叉捕鱼的人|A harpooner is a person who uses a harpoon to catch whales or other large sea creatures.  They are skilled in handling the harpoon and the boat, often working as part of a whaling crew.|The experienced harpooner expertly flung the harpoon, striking the whale.|whaler, fisherman| (no direct antonym)|名词
