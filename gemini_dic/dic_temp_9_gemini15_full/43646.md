creator: /kriˈeɪtər/ | n. |创造者，创作者 | A person or thing that brings something into existence.  Someone or something that creates something new. |例句：God is the creator of the universe.  上帝是宇宙的创造者。|例句：She is a talented creator of beautiful jewelry. 她是一位才华横溢的珠宝设计师。|近义词：maker, originator, inventor, designer |反义词：destroyer, annihilator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The creator designs new games every year. = 这位创造者每年都设计新的游戏。
The creators are working hard on the project. = 这些创作者正在努力进行这个项目。


一般过去时:
The creator built a magnificent castle. = 这位创造者建造了一座宏伟的城堡。
The creators finished their masterpiece. = 这些创作者完成了他们的杰作。


现在完成时:

The creators have collaborated on many successful projects. = 这些创作者在许多成功的项目中进行了合作。

将来时:

The creators will present their findings at the conference. = 这些创作者将在会议上展示他们的研究成果。


虚拟语气:
If he were the creator, he would have done things differently. = 如果他是创造者，他会采取不同的方式。
I wish I were the creator of that amazing technology. = 我希望我是那项惊人技术的创造者。


被动语态:
The game was created by a talented team. = 这个游戏是由一个才华横溢的团队创造的。
The sculpture was created using ancient techniques. = 这尊雕塑是使用古代技术创造的。


疑问句:

Are they the creators of the famous software? = 他们是不是那个著名软件的创作者？


否定句:
He is not the creator of the universe. = 他不是宇宙的创造者。
They were not the creators of that particular style of painting. = 他们不是那种特定绘画风格的创作者。
