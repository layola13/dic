kupfernickel:/ˈkʊpfərˌnɪkəl/| n. |镍铜合金；假银币|A nickel-copper alloy, historically used as a base for imitation silver coins.  The name is a German compound word, literally meaning "copper nickel".  It refers to a naturally occurring alloy of nickel and copper, often used in the past to create coins that resembled silver but were less valuable.|The old coins were made of kupfernickel.|近义词：nickel silver, German silver|反义词：pure silver
