mystifier:/mɪsˈtaɪfər/| n. |使迷惑的人或事物；令人费解的事物|Something or someone that mystifies; something puzzling or confusing.|The magician was a master mystifier, baffling the audience with his illusions.  魔术师是一位精通迷惑术的大师，他的幻象使观众困惑不已。|The origin of the ancient symbols remained a mystifier to scholars.  古代符号的起源仍然是学者们感到困惑的事情。|近义词：enigma, puzzle, riddle|反义词：clarifier, explanation

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The puzzle mystifies me. = 这个谜题让我迷惑不解。
This magic trick mystifies the audience. = 这个魔术让观众迷惑不解。


一般过去时:
The strange event mystified the police. = 那件奇怪的事件让警方感到困惑。
His behavior mystified everyone. = 他的行为让每个人都感到困惑。


现在完成时:
The disappearance of the painting has mystified investigators for years. = 这幅画的消失多年来一直让调查人员感到困惑。
This complex equation has mystified mathematicians for decades. = 这个复杂的方程式几十年来一直让数学家们感到困惑。


过去完成时:
The ancient writings had mystified scholars for centuries. = 古代文字几个世纪以来一直让学者们感到困惑。
The strange noises had mystified the residents of the village. = 奇怪的声音让村里的居民感到困惑。


将来时:
The new technology will mystify many people. = 新技术会让很多人感到困惑。
His future plans will mystify his friends. = 他未来的计划会让他的朋友们感到困惑。


虚拟语气:
If only I understood, this wouldn't mystify me. = 如果我理解的话，这不会让我迷惑不解。
I wish this complex situation didn't mystify me so much. = 我希望这种情况不会让我如此困惑。


被动语态:
The audience was mystified by the magician's trick. = 观众被魔术师的戏法迷惑了。
Many people are mystified by the workings of the universe. = 许多人对宇宙的运行感到困惑。


疑问句:
What mystifies you about this problem? = 关于这个问题，什么让你困惑？
What aspects of quantum physics mystify you the most? = 量子物理学的哪些方面最让你困惑？


否定句:
The explanation didn't mystify me at all. = 这个解释一点也没有让我困惑。
The instructions were clear and didn't mystify her. = 说明很清楚，没有让她困惑。
