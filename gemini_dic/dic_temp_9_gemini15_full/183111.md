reports:/rɪˈpɔːrts/| n. v.|报告；报道；汇报|A statement or account giving information about something.|例句：The police are preparing a report on the accident.  警察正在准备一份关于事故的报告。|近义词：account, statement, summary|反义词：concealment, suppression|noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reports the news every day. = 他每天报道新闻。
She reports to the manager. = 她向经理汇报工作。


一般过去时:
They reported the crime to the police. = 他们向警方报案。
He reported his findings to the committee. = 他向委员会汇报了他的调查结果。


现在完成时:
She has reported the incident to her supervisor. = 她已经向她的主管报告了此事。
They have reported a significant increase in sales. = 他们报告说销售额显著增长。


过去完成时:
The witness had reported seeing the suspect earlier. = 目击者曾报告说早些时候见过嫌疑人。
They had reported the problem before, but nothing was done. = 他们之前就报告过这个问题，但什么也没做。


将来时:
He will report the results tomorrow. = 他明天将汇报结果。
She will report to the headquarters next week. = 她下周将向总部汇报。


虚拟语气:
If he reported the truth, he would be in trouble. = 如果他说实话，他会有麻烦。
I wish she had reported the problem sooner. = 我希望她早点报告这个问题。


被动语态:
The accident was reported to the authorities. = 事故已向当局报告。
The findings were reported in detail in the journal. = 这些研究结果在期刊上进行了详细报道。


疑问句:
Did he report the incident? = 他报告了这件事吗？
What did the report say? = 报告说了什么？


否定句:
He didn't report the accident. = 他没有报告事故。
She didn't report for duty this morning. = 她今天早上没有上班。



PHRASE->
report on = 报道…
report to = 向…汇报
annual report = 年度报告
police report = 警察报告
financial report = 财务报告
