endower:/ɪnˈdaʊər/| n. |捐赠者；赋予者；资助者 | A person or thing that endows something or someone with a particular quality, ability, or right.  Someone who gives a large sum of money to a school, hospital, etc. |例句：The university's generous endower funded the new science building. 这所大学的慷慨捐赠者资助了新的科学大楼。

例句：She is a generous endower to many charitable causes. 她是一位慷慨地捐助许多慈善事业的人。


近义词：donor, benefactor, grantor, patron |反义词：recipient, receiver


词性:名词


USAGE->

The endower supports many charities. = 这位捐赠者支持许多慈善机构。
He endows the university annually. = 他每年捐赠给大学。


一般过去时:
The endower funded the research project. = 这位捐赠者资助了该研究项目。
