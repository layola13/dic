impetuously:/ɪmˈpɛtʃuəsli/| adv.|冲动地；鲁莽地|Acting or done in a hasty or rash manner, without forethought.  It suggests a lack of control over one's emotions or impulses.|He impetuously threw the letter into the fire.|近义词：rashly, recklessly, hastily, impulsively|反义词：cautiously, deliberately, thoughtfully


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He impetuously interrupts the meeting. = 他冲动地打断了会议。
She impetuously buys whatever she wants. = 她冲动地买下她想要的任何东西。


一般过去时:
He impetuously quit his job. = 他冲动地辞掉了工作。
She impetuously married him. = 她冲动地嫁给了他。


现在完成时:
He has impetuously spent all his savings. = 他冲动地花光了所有的积蓄。
She has impetuously changed her mind several times. = 她冲动地改变主意好几次了。


过去完成时:
He had impetuously promised more than he could deliver. = 他冲动地许诺了他无法兑现的东西。
She had impetuously signed the contract without reading it. = 她冲动地在没有阅读的情况下签署了合同。


将来时:
He will impetuously react to any criticism. = 他会冲动地回应任何批评。
She will impetuously buy that expensive car. = 她会冲动地买下那辆昂贵的车。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't acted so impetuously, the situation wouldn't be so bad. = 如果他不那么冲动行事，情况就不会这么糟糕了。
I wish I hadn't acted so impetuously yesterday. = 我真希望昨天我没有那么冲动。


被动语态:
(Impetuously is rarely used in the passive voice, as it describes the manner of an action, not the action itself.  A sentence like "The decision was made impetuously" is possible but less common.)  The decision was made rashly. = 这个决定草率地做出了。
The project was launched hastily. = 这个项目仓促地启动了。


疑问句:
Did he act impetuously? = 他是不是冲动地行事了？
Why did she react so impetuously? = 她为什么反应如此冲动？


否定句:
He did not act impetuously; he thought carefully before he spoke. = 他没有冲动行事；他说话前仔细考虑过了。
She did not impetuously spend her money; she saved it carefully. = 她没有冲动地花钱；她小心地存了起来。
