engineer:/ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪr/| n. |工程师，技师|A person who designs, builds, or maintains engines, machines, or public works.  Engineers apply scientific and mathematical principles to solve practical problems.|例句：He's a civil engineer who works on bridges. 他是一名土木工程师，从事桥梁建设。|例句：The chief engineer oversaw the entire construction project.  首席工程师负责整个建筑项目的监督。|近义词：technician, architect (in certain contexts)|反义词：N/A


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He engineers innovative solutions. = 他设计创新的解决方案。
She engineers software for large corporations. = 她为大型公司设计软件。

一般过去时:
They engineered a new type of engine. = 他们设计了一种新型发动机。
The team engineered a successful marketing campaign. = 该团队策划了一个成功的营销活动。

现在完成时:
We have engineered a breakthrough in renewable energy. = 我们在可再生能源方面取得了突破。
She has engineered several successful product launches. = 她成功策划了几次产品发布。

过去完成时:
They had engineered the system before the launch date. = 他们在发布日期之前就设计好了系统。
The company had engineered a solution to the problem long before it occurred. = 公司早在问题出现之前就设计出了解决方案。

将来时:
We will engineer a more efficient process. = 我们将设计一个更高效的流程。
They will engineer a new building in the city center. = 他们将在市中心设计一座新建筑。

虚拟语气:
If we engineered a better system, we would have fewer problems. = 如果我们设计一个更好的系统，我们将会有更少的问题。
I wish I had engineered a different approach. = 我希望我采用了不同的方法。

被动语态:
The bridge was engineered by a renowned firm. = 这座桥是由一家著名的公司设计的。
The software is being engineered by a team of experts. = 该软件正在由一个专家团队设计。

疑问句:
Who engineered this magnificent structure? = 谁设计了这座宏伟的建筑？
What principles were used to engineer this solution? = 使用什么原理来设计这个解决方案？

否定句:
They did not engineer the project successfully. = 他们没有成功地完成该项目的设计。
The company failed to engineer a solution to the problem. = 公司未能设计出解决这个问题的方案。
