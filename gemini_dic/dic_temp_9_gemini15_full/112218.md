loops:/luːps/| n. v.|循环；圈；环；反复|A loop is a curved or circular line, or a series of connected lines, forming a closed figure. It can also refer to a repeated sequence of actions or instructions.|例句：The bird flew in loops.  鸟儿盘旋飞行。|近义词：cycle, circle, repetition |反义词：straight line, single action
