monarch:/ˈmɒnək/ /ˈmɑːnɑːrk/| n.|君主，帝王，统治者|A monarch is a sovereign ruler, such as a king or queen, who reigns over a kingdom or empire.  The term often implies hereditary succession.|例句：King Charles III is the current monarch of the United Kingdom.  查尔斯三世国王是英国现任君主。

例句：The monarch ruled with an iron fist, suppressing any dissent.  这位君主铁腕统治，压制任何异议。


近义词：sovereign, ruler, king, queen, emperor, empress|反义词：subject, commoner|词性：名词
