exacts:/ɪɡˈzækts/| v. n.|精确地做；索取；强求；(税款等的)确切数额|to demand and obtain something, especially by force or authority.  It can also refer to the exact amount of something, such as taxes.|The government exacts a heavy tax on imported goods. = 政府对进口商品征收重税。|He exacted a promise from her. = 他向她索取了一个承诺。|近义词：demand, require, claim|反义词：give, offer, relinquish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exacts obedience from his employees. = 他强迫他的员工服从。
The landlord exacts high rent from his tenants. = 房东向他的房客收取高额租金。

一般过去时:
The tax collector exacted the full amount due. = 税务员收取了所有应缴税款。
She exacted revenge on her enemies. = 她向敌人报仇了。

现在完成时:
The war has exacted a heavy toll on the country. = 这场战争给国家带来了巨大的损失。
They have exacted a high price for their victory. = 他们为他们的胜利付出了巨大的代价。

过去完成时:
He had exacted a promise from her before he left. = 他在他离开之前已向她索取了一个承诺。
The king had exacted tribute from the conquered provinces. = 国王已向被征服的省份征收贡赋。

将来时:
The new law will exact a higher tax from the wealthy. = 新法律将向富人征收更高的税款。
They will exact their revenge in due time. = 他们将在适当的时候报仇。

虚拟语气:
If he exacted more effort from his team, they would achieve more. = 如果他向他的团队要求更多努力，他们将会取得更大的成就。
I wish he hadn't exacted such a high price. = 我希望他没有索取如此高的代价。

被动语态:
A heavy tax was exacted from the citizens. = 向公民征收了重税。
Full payment will be exacted from all debtors. = 所有债务人都将被要求全额支付。

疑问句:
What price did they exact for their services? = 他们为他们的服务收取了什么价格？
How much tax will be exacted this year? = 今年将征收多少税？

否定句:
The government did not exact any further payments. = 政府没有再要求任何付款。
He did not exact revenge, opting for forgiveness instead. = 他没有报仇，而是选择了原谅。
