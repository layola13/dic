joyed:/dʒɔɪd/| adj.|快乐的；欣喜的|Feeling or showing great pleasure and happiness.|例句：She was joyed by the news of her son's success.  她听到儿子成功的消息后非常高兴。|例句：The children were joyed to receive such wonderful gifts.孩子们收到如此美好的礼物感到非常高兴。|近义词：delighted, thrilled, elated, glad|反义词：sad, unhappy, miserable, dejected

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is joyed by the good news. = 他对这个好消息感到高兴。
She is always joyed to see her family. = 她总是很高兴见到家人。

一般过去时:
I was joyed to hear from you. = 收到你的来信我很高兴。
They were joyed at the outcome of the game. = 他们对比赛的结果感到高兴。

现在完成时:
We have been joyed by the recent developments. = 我们对最近的发展感到高兴。
She has been joyed since she got the promotion. = 自从她升职以来，她就一直很高兴。


过去完成时:
He had been joyed to learn that he had passed the exam. = 他得知自己考试通过后非常高兴。
They had been joyed to finally reach their destination. = 他们终于到达目的地后感到非常高兴。


将来时:
I will be joyed to see you again soon. = 我很快就能再次见到你，我会很高兴。
We will be joyed to celebrate with you. = 我们很高兴能和你一起庆祝。


虚拟语气:

She would have been joyed if she had known earlier. = 如果她早知道的话，她会很高兴。


被动语态:
(Joyed is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be used, such as "She was filled with joy.")


疑问句:

否定句:

She wasn't joyed by the criticism she received. = 她对收到的批评并不高兴。
