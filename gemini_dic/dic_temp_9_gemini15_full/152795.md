pectinal:/ˈpektɪnəl/| adj.| 梳状的；栉状的；| Relating to or resembling a pecten (a comb-like structure).  The term often refers to anatomical structures that resemble a comb in shape or function. |The pectinal line of the pubis is an important anatomical landmark.  耻骨的梳状线是一个重要的解剖学标志。

The pectinal muscles in the bird's wing help it to control its flight. 鸟翅膀上的梳状肌肉有助于它控制飞行。|近义词：comb-like, pectinate|反义词：无
