previolating: /ˌpriːvaɪəˈleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 侵犯；违反；先于…而违反 |  The act of violating something beforehand or in advance of something else.  It implies a preemptive or anticipatory violation. This word is not commonly used and might be considered a neologism or a less common variant.  It suggests a violation that occurs before some other event or action. | 例句：The company was accused of previolating the contract by secretly negotiating with a competitor before the official termination date. 公司被指控在合同正式终止日期之前秘密与竞争对手谈判，从而侵犯了合同。


例句：The government's previolating the treaty was a major cause of the international crisis. 政府先于条约而违反条约是国际危机的主要原因。


近义词：violating, infringing, contravening, transgressing


反义词：obeying, complying, upholding


词性：动词
