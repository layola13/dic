on-the-jump:/ˌɒnðəˈdʒʌmp/| adj.| 随时准备行动的；立刻行动的 | Ready to act immediately; in a state of readiness.  |例句：The soldiers were on the jump, ready for any emergency.士兵们随时准备行动，以应对任何紧急情况。
The team was on the jump, prepared to solve the problem. 团队随时待命，准备解决问题。|近义词：ready, alert, prepared |反义词：unprepared, idle, inactive
