putty:/ˈpʌti/ | n. |油灰，腻子 |A soft, pliable material used for filling holes or cracks, typically made of a mixture of whiting and linseed oil.  |Example: He used putty to fill the cracks in the window frame. 他用油灰填补了窗框的裂缝。|近义词：filler, sealant |反义词：None specifically.
