nominals:/ˈnɑːmɪnəlz/ | n. |名词性词语；名词性成分|Words or phrases that function as nouns.  They can include nouns, pronouns, noun phrases, and clauses that function nominally.|The sentence "The dog barked loudly" contains two nominals: "the dog" and "loudly" (functioning as an adverbial nominal).|近义词：noun phrase, nominal phrase|反义词：verb, adjective, adverb
