/kənˈstrʌktər/ | n. | 构造者；建筑者；创建者 | A person or thing that constructs something.  It can also refer to a constructor in programming. |例句：The master constructer oversaw the building of the cathedral.  这位总工程师负责监督大教堂的建造。

例句：The program uses a constructor to initialize new objects.  该程序使用构造函数来初始化新对象。| 近义词：builder, creator, developer, architect (in specific contexts) | 反义词：deconstructor (in programming context), destroyer |  词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constructor builds houses. = 建筑工人建造房屋。
He is a skilled constructor. = 他是一位熟练的建筑工人。
