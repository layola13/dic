preallude:/ˈpriːæljuːd/| n.|前奏；序幕；先兆|An introductory or preliminary event, action, or remark; something that precedes and prepares for something else.|The preallude to the concert was a short piece of classical music.|近义词：prelude, preface, introduction, precursor|反义词：coda, conclusion
