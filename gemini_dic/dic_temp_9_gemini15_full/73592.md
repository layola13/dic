foretellers: /fɔːrˈtɛlərz/ | n. | 预言者，预兆 | Those who predict future events;  things that foreshadow future events. |例句：The ancient foretellers predicted a great flood.  古代的预言者预言了一场大洪水。
The dark clouds were foretellers of the approaching storm.  乌云是即将到来的暴风雨的预兆。|近义词：prophets, seers, predictors, harbingers |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be related to those who deny or dismiss predictions.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The foretellers predict a prosperous year. = 预言者预言来年将会兴旺发达。
These signs foretell a change in the weather. = 这些迹象预示着天气将要变化。


一般过去时:
The ancient foretellers accurately predicted the eclipse. = 古代预言者准确地预测了日食。
The old woman's words foretold disaster. = 老妇人的话预示着灾难。


现在完成时:
The soothsayers have foretold many events that have since come to pass. = 预言家们已经预言了许多后来发生的事件。
Recent events have foretold the upcoming economic downturn. = 最近发生的事件预示着即将到来的经济衰退。


过去完成时:
The oracles had foretold the king's demise before he even ascended the throne. = 预言家们在他登基之前就预言了他的死亡。
They had foretold a harsh winter, and they were right. = 他们曾预言过一个严冬，而且他们是正确的。


将来时:
Some believe that the stars will foretell the future. = 有些人相信星星会预言未来。
Experts predict that the market will recover next year. = 专家预测市场明年将复苏。


虚拟语气:
If the foretellers had been correct, we would have been prepared. = 如果预言者是正确的，我们就已经做好了准备。
I wish the foretellers had been more specific about the details. = 我希望预言者对细节更具体一些。


被动语态:
The future was foretold by the mystic. = 未来被神秘主义者预言了。
The coming storm is being foretold by the falling barometer. = 即将到来的风暴正被下降的气压预示着。


疑问句:
What do the foretellers say about the future? = 预言者对未来说了些什么？
What events foretold the revolution? = 哪些事件预示着革命？


否定句:
The fortune teller did not foretell any major changes. = 算命先生没有预言任何重大变化。
The signs did not foretell the sudden catastrophe. = 这些迹象并没有预示着突然的灾难。
