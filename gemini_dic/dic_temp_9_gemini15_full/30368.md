centered:/ˈsɛntərd/| adj.|居中的，以…为中心的；集中的|Having a central point or position;situated in or forming the center.|例句：The table is centered in the room.  桌子位于房间中央。|例句：His whole life centered around his family. 他的整个生活都围绕着他的家人。|近义词：central, focused, concentrated|反义词：peripheral, off-center, decentralized


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The design is centered on the page. = 设计居中于页面。
The focus is centered on customer satisfaction. = 重点放在客户满意度上。

一般过去时:
The image was centered perfectly. = 图片完美居中。
The discussion centered on the budget. = 讨论集中在预算上。

现在完成时:
The artist has centered the main figure in the painting. = 艺术家已将主要人物置于绘画的中心。
Everything has centered around this one problem. = 一切都围绕着这一个问题展开。

过去完成时:
The photographer had centered the subject before taking the picture. = 摄影师在拍照前已经将拍摄对象居中。
The debate had centered on the issue of immigration. = 辩论的中心议题是移民问题。

将来时:
The website will be centered around user experience. = 网站将以用户体验为中心。
The meeting will center on the new project proposal. = 会议将集中讨论新的项目提案。

虚拟语气:
If the text were centered, it would look better. = 如果文字居中，看起来会更好。
I wish the image was centered more precisely. = 我希望图像能更精确地居中。

被动语态:
The text is centered automatically. = 文字自动居中。
The argument was centered on the question of fairness. = 争论的中心是公平的问题。

疑问句:
Is the logo centered correctly? = 标志是否正确居中？
Is the discussion centered on the right topic? = 讨论是否集中在正确的主题上？

否定句:
The picture is not centered. = 图片没有居中。
The project is not centered around sustainability. = 该项目并非以可持续性为中心。


PHRASE->

be centered on/around:  以…为中心
centered approach: 集中方法
centered system: 集中系统
perfectly centered: 完美居中
off-center:  偏离中心
