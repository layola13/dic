non-linearity: /ˌnɒnˌlɪniˈærəti/ | /ˌnɑːnˌlɪniˈærəti/ | n. |非线性|The quality or state of not being linear;  a relationship or process where the output is not directly proportional to the input.  It often involves complex or unpredictable behavior.  The term is used across various fields including mathematics, physics, and computer science. |


例句：

The chaotic behavior of the system is due to its inherent non-linearity.  系统的混沌行为是由于其固有的非线性造成的。

The model incorporates non-linearity to account for the complex interactions between variables.  该模型包含非线性以解释变量之间的复杂相互作用。


近义词：curvilinearity, nonproportionality


反义词：linearity


词性：名词
