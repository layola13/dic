non-Italians:/ˌnɑːnɪˈtæliənz/ | n. | 非意大利人 |  People who are not Italian. This term refers to individuals of any nationality or ethnicity other than Italian.  It can be used in a neutral or sometimes slightly negative context depending on the situation. | Example:  The survey included responses from both Italians and non-Italians.  这项调查包括了意大利人和非意大利人的回应。 | 近义词：foreigners (更广泛), non-citizens (如果指国籍) | 反义词：Italians | 名词
