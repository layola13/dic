self-resentments:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈzɛntmənts/| n. |自我怨恨，自责|Feelings of anger or bitterness towards oneself.  This often involves feelings of guilt, shame, or inadequacy.  It's a negative emotional state where one directs negative feelings inward. |例句：Her self-resentments stemmed from her inability to achieve her childhood dreams.  她的自我怨恨源于她无法实现儿时梦想。

Her self-resentments manifested as procrastination and self-sabotage. 她的自我怨恨表现为拖延和自我破坏。|近义词：self-condemnation, self-loathing, self-reproach |反义词：self-acceptance, self-love, self-compassion |名词
