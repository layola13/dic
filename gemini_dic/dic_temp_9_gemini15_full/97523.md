ingot:/ˈɪŋɡət/| n. |金条；锭；铸块 |A mass of metal, typically a precious metal, cast into a bar.  Ingots are used for storage, transportation, and further processing of metals. |例句：The gold ingots were carefully stacked in the vault. 金条被小心地堆放在金库里。|例句：The smelter produced ingots of various sizes. 冶炼厂生产各种尺寸的金属锭。|近义词：bar, block, lump |反义词：powder, dust
