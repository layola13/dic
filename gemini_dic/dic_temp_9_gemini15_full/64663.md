escalating: /ˈɛskəleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 逐步升级；恶化；加剧 |  Increasing in intensity, seriousness, or extent.  | 例句：The conflict is escalating rapidly. 冲突正在迅速升级。 | 例句：Tensions are escalating between the two countries.  两国之间的紧张关系正在升级。 | 近义词：intensifying, worsening, heightening | 反义词：de-escalating, diminishing, lessening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is escalating. = 情况正在恶化。
He is escalating the conflict. = 他正在加剧冲突。

一般过去时:
The violence escalated quickly. = 暴力迅速升级。
The argument escalated into a fight. = 争吵升级为打架。

现在完成时:
Tensions have escalated significantly. = 紧张局势已显著升级。
The problem has escalated beyond our control. = 问题已升级到我们无法控制的地步。

过去完成时:
The situation had escalated before we intervened. = 我们干预之前，情况就已经恶化了。
The company's debt had escalated dramatically. = 公司的债务已经急剧增加。

将来时:
The crisis will escalate if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，危机将会升级。
The costs are likely to escalate in the coming years. = 未来几年成本可能会上升。

虚拟语气:
If we hadn't intervened, the violence would have escalated. = 如果我们没有干预，暴力就会升级。
I wish the situation hadn't escalated so quickly. = 我希望情况没有这么快恶化。

被动语态:
The conflict was escalated by both sides. = 冲突由双方升级。
The situation is being escalated by their actions. = 他们的行为正在加剧事态。

疑问句:
Is the situation escalating? = 情况正在恶化吗？
Is he escalating the conflict intentionally? = 他是在故意加剧冲突吗？

否定句:
The situation is not escalating. = 情况没有恶化。
He is not escalating the conflict. = 他没有加剧冲突。


PHRASE->
escalating conflict = 升级的冲突
escalating tensions = 升级的紧张局势
rapidly escalating = 快速升级
escalating costs = 升级的成本
escalating debt = 升级的债务
