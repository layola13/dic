hullabaloo:/ˌhʌləˈbəluː/| n. |喧嚣；吵闹；混乱 |A hullabaloo is a commotion; a fuss; a noisy and excited disturbance. It often implies a lot of noise and excitement, but not necessarily anything serious.  |例句：There was a terrible hullabaloo when the celebrity arrived.  名流到来时引起了一阵可怕的喧嚣。

例句：The announcement caused quite a hullabaloo in the media.  这个公告在媒体上引起了相当大的喧嚣。


近义词：commotion, uproar, turmoil, fuss, ado, racket, brouhaha


反义词：quiet, peace, calm, silence


词性：名词
