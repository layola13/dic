paris:/ˈpærɪs/ | n. | 巴黎 | The capital and most populous city of France.  A city known for its art, fashion, and history. | Example: I spent a week in Paris last summer. 我去年夏天在巴黎待了一周。|近义词: capital (of France) |反义词: (None directly,  but could use antonyms related to aspects of Paris, like "rural" for its urban nature.)
