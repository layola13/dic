reconsecrate:/ˌriːˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/| vt.|重新奉献；重新祝圣|To consecrate again; to dedicate anew to sacred use.  The church was reconsecrated after the renovations.|近义词：rededicate, rehallow |反义词：desecrate, profane


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reconsecrate the temple every year. = 他们每年都重新奉献这座寺庙。
The priest reconsecrates the chalice before each mass. = 牧师在每次弥撒前都重新祝圣圣杯。


一般过去时:
The building was reconsecrated after extensive repairs. = 这座建筑经过大规模维修后被重新奉献。
She reconsecrated her life to helping the poor. = 她重新将自己的一生奉献给帮助穷人。


现在完成时:
They have reconsecrated the altar. = 他们已经重新奉献了祭坛。
The community has reconsecrated its commitment to environmental protection. = 社区已经重新承诺致力于环境保护。


过去完成时:
The priest had reconsecrated the land before the ceremony began. = 祭司在仪式开始之前已经重新奉献了这块土地。
They had reconsecrated the statue before placing it back in the temple. = 他们在把雕像放回寺庙之前已经重新奉献了它。


将来时:
The bishop will reconsecrate the cathedral next month. = 主教下个月将重新祝圣大教堂。
They will reconsecrate the park to the memory of the fallen soldiers. = 他们将把公园重新奉献给阵亡士兵的纪念。


虚拟语气:
If they had reconsecrated the shrine, the villagers would have felt more secure. = 如果他们重新奉献了神社，村民们就会感到更有安全感。
I wish they would reconsecrate the old chapel. = 我希望他们能重新奉献那座旧教堂。


被动语态:
The church was reconsecrated by the archbishop. = 这座教堂由大主教重新祝圣。
The land will be reconsecrated next spring. = 这块土地将在明年春天重新奉献。


疑问句:
Will the temple be reconsecrated soon? = 寺庙很快就会重新奉献吗？
Has the statue been reconsecrated yet? = 雕像已经被重新奉献了吗？


否定句:
They did not reconsecrate the building. = 他们没有重新奉献那座建筑。
The priest refused to reconsecrate the defiled altar. = 牧师拒绝重新奉献被亵渎的祭坛。


PHRASE->
reconsecrate a building = 重新奉献建筑物
reconsecrate one's life = 重新奉献一生
reconsecrate oneself to a cause = 将自己重新奉献给一个事业
reconsecrate a sacred space = 重新奉献圣地
reconsecrate a vow = 重新立誓


词源:
re- (again) + consecrate (to make or declare sacred)  The word "consecrate" itself comes from the Latin "consecrare,"  from "con-" (together) + "sacrare" (to make sacred).
