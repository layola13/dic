scrawnier:/ˈskrɔːniər/| adj. |更瘦弱的，更骨瘦如柴的 | More slender or thin; less fleshy.  It suggests a negative connotation, implying that the thinness is unhealthy or undesirable. |The scrawnier dog looked weak and needed more food. 那只更瘦弱的狗看起来很虚弱，需要更多食物。| thinner, leaner, skinnier | obese, overweight, plump
