rotascope:/ˈroʊtəskoʊp/| n. |描图动画|A technique in animation where animators trace over live-action footage, frame by frame, to create a realistic-looking animation.|Example: The animators used rotoscoping to create the incredibly lifelike movements of the characters in the film.|近义词: animation tracing, live-action tracing|反义词:  (no direct antonym, but opposite in effect could be cel animation)|
