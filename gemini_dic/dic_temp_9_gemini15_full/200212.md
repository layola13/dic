skinning:/ˈskɪnɪŋ/| v.|剥皮，去皮；欺骗，诈骗 |The act of removing the skin from something, or the process of being cheated or defrauded. |例句：He's skinning the rabbit. 他正在剥兔皮。  He accused the company of skinning its customers. 他指责该公司欺骗顾客。|近义词：flaying, fleecing,  cheating, defrauding|反义词：covering, protecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skins the potatoes before cooking. = 他在烹饪之前削土豆皮。
She skins the apples for the pie. = 她为馅饼削苹果皮。

一般过去时:
The hunter skinned the deer. = 猎人剥了鹿皮。
They skinned the oranges for juice. = 他们剥了橘子榨汁。

现在完成时:
I have skinned several chickens today. = 我今天已经剥了几只鸡皮。
She has skinned all the onions. = 她已经把所有洋葱都剥了皮。

过去完成时:
He had skinned the animal before nightfall. = 他在夜幕降临前已经剥了动物的皮。
They had skinned the fruit before making the jam. = 他们在制作果酱之前已经剥好了水果皮。

将来时:
I will skin the fish for dinner. = 我将为晚餐处理鱼皮。
She will skin the tomatoes for the salad. = 她会为沙拉处理西红柿皮。

虚拟语气:
If I skinned the potatoes, the soup would taste better. = 如果我把土豆削皮，汤会更好喝。
I wish I had skinned the fruit earlier. = 我真希望我早点把水果削皮。

被动语态:
The oranges were skinned and juiced. = 橘子被剥皮并榨汁了。
The rabbit was skinned by the hunter. = 兔子被猎人剥了皮。

疑问句:
Have you skinned the chicken yet? = 你已经剥鸡皮了吗？
Will he skin the potatoes for the stew? = 他会为炖菜削土豆皮吗？

否定句:
He didn't skin the fish properly. = 他没有把鱼皮处理好。
They didn't skin the grapes before eating them. = 他们吃葡萄前没有剥皮。
