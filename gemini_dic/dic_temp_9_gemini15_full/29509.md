catasterism:/kəˈtæstəˌrɪzəm/| n. |星宿学；灾星说|The assigning of stars to constellations and the study of their influence.  The belief that certain stars or constellations influence human affairs, often negatively.|例句：The ancient Greeks practiced catasterism, believing the stars influenced human destiny. 古希腊人信奉星宿学，认为星辰影响人类命运。

例句：Some historians attribute the fall of the Roman Empire to catasterism, linking it to unfavorable celestial alignments.  一些历史学家将罗马帝国的衰落归因于灾星说，将其与不利的星象联系起来。


近义词：astrology, celestial divination | 反义词：none (no direct opposite)


词性:名词
