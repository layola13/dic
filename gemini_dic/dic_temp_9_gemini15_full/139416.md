obsess:/əbˈsɛs/ | vt. | 使痴迷；使困扰 | To fill one's mind with or be engrossed by a particular idea, image, feeling, etc., to the exclusion of others. | 例句：He's obsessed with his work. 他迷恋他的工作。

例句：She is obsessed with the idea of becoming a doctor. 她一心想成为一名医生。


近义词：preoccupy, haunt, enthrall | 反义词：ignore, neglect | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He obsesses over every detail. 他对每一个细节都念念不忘。
They obsess about their weight. 他们总是担心自己的体重。

一般过去时:
She obsessed over her appearance. 她曾经非常在意自己的外貌。
He obsessed about the possibility of failure. 他曾经一直担心失败的可能性。

现在完成时:
I have obsessed over this problem for days. 我已经纠结这个问题好几天了。
She has obsessed over her past mistakes. 她一直耿耿于怀过去的错误。

过去完成时:
He had obsessed over the project for months before it finally launched.  他在项目最终启动前已经痴迷于这个项目数月了。
They had obsessed over the details for weeks, leading to burnout. 他们纠结细节达数周，最终导致筋疲力尽。

将来时:
I know I will obsess over the results of the test. 我知道我会一直担心考试的结果。
She fears she will obsess over her children's future. 她担心她会一直担心孩子们的未来。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't have so much free time, I wouldn't obsess over these trivial matters. 如果我没有那么多空闲时间，我就不会纠结这些琐事了。
I wish I didn't obsess over every little thing. 我希望我不必事事都过于在意。

被动语态:
He is obsessed by his fear of spiders. 他被对蜘蛛的恐惧所困扰。
She felt obsessed by the media's attention. 她觉得被媒体的关注所困扰。

疑问句:
Does he obsess over his health? 他是否过于担心自己的健康？
Do you often obsess about things you can't control? 你是否经常为无法控制的事情而烦恼？

否定句:
He doesn't obsess about his appearance. 他并不在意自己的外表。
She tried not to obsess over the negative comments. 她试图不去在意那些负面评论。


PHRASE->
be obsessed with: 对…痴迷
obsessed fan: 狂热粉丝
become obsessed: 变得痴迷
obsessive-compulsive: 强迫症的
obsessive behavior: 强迫行为
