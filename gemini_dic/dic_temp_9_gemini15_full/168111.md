pro-Algerians: /ˌproʊælˈdʒɪəriənz/ | adj. | 支持阿尔及利亚人的；亲阿尔及利亚的 |  Supporting or favoring the people of Algeria.  This term describes individuals or groups who advocate for Algerian interests, rights, or causes. It can encompass a wide range of positions, from simple sympathy to active political support. |

例句：

The pro-Algerians demonstrated in front of the embassy.  支持阿尔及利亚人在大使馆前示威。

Many pro-Algerians groups voiced their concerns about the political situation. 许多亲阿尔及利亚团体表达了他们对政治局势的担忧。


近义词：Algerian-supporting, pro-Algeria, Algeria-friendly

反义词：anti-Algerian


词性：形容词
