intricable:/ˈɪntrɪkəbəl/ | adj. | 复杂的；难以理解的；解不开的 | extremely complicated; difficult or impossible to understand or solve | The plot of the novel was so intricate and tangled that it was nearly impossible to follow. 这部小说的情节如此复杂和错综复杂，几乎无法理解。  |近义词：complex, complicated, convoluted, tangled | 反义词：simple, straightforward, easy

词源:
From Middle French *intriqué (past participle of *intriquer), from Latin *intricare ("to entangle").  The Latin root *trica derives from *trix, meaning "hair," referring to the tangled nature of hair.
