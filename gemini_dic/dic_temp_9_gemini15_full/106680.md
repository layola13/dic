kvetching:/ˈkvɛtʃɪŋ/| v.|抱怨，牢骚，诉苦 |持续不断地抱怨；表达不满；唠叨 |例句：He spent the whole evening kvetching about his job. 他整个晚上都在抱怨他的工作。|例句：Stop kvetching and do something about it! 别抱怨了，想想办法！|近义词：complaining, grumbling, whining, griping|反义词：praising, complimenting, celebrating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kvetching about the weather every day. 他每天都在抱怨天气。
They are always kvetching about their boss. 他们总是抱怨他们的老板。

一般过去时:
She kvetched about the long lines at the store. 她抱怨商店的长队。
He kvetched to his wife about his work. 他向妻子抱怨他的工作。

现在完成时:
I have been kvetching about this problem for weeks. 我已经抱怨这个问题几周了。
They have kvetched incessantly since the meeting. 自从会议以来，他们一直在不停地抱怨。


过去完成时:
She had kvetched to her friends before she told her boss. 在告诉老板之前，她已经向朋友们抱怨过了。
He had kvetched all day, so he was exhausted. 他抱怨了一整天，所以筋疲力尽了。

将来时:
I will kvetch to him about the unfair treatment. 我会向他抱怨这种不公平的待遇。
They will kvetch loudly if the service is poor. 如果服务不好，他们会大声抱怨。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't kvetch so much. 如果我是你，我就不会抱怨这么多。
I wish he wouldn't kvetch all the time. 我希望他不要总是抱怨。

被动语态:
His complaints were often kvetched about by his colleagues. 他的抱怨经常被同事们议论。
The issue was constantly kvetched about in the meeting. 会议上不断有人抱怨这个问题。

疑问句:
Are you kvetching again? 你又在抱怨了吗？
Why is she always kvetching? 她为什么总是抱怨？

否定句:
He didn't kvetch about the food. 他没有抱怨食物。
She doesn't kvetch often. 她不经常抱怨。
