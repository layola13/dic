例句：The overhastiness of the decision resulted in significant financial losses.  这个仓促的决定导致了巨大的经济损失。|近义词：rashness, precipitateness, impulsiveness, recklessness|反义词：deliberation, carefulness, patience, thoughtfulness|词性：名词
