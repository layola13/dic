perceived:/pərˈsiːvd/| v.|感知的；被理解的| to become aware of something through the senses or the mind; to understand or interpret something in a particular way |例句：The audience perceived the play as a satire.  观众认为这出戏是一部讽刺剧。
The problem is often perceived as insurmountable.  这个问题往往被认为是无法克服的。|近义词：understood, grasped, recognized, sensed|反义词：misunderstood, ignored, overlooked|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perceives the world differently. = 他对世界的看法不同。
She perceives a pattern in the data. = 她觉察到数据中的一种模式。

一般过去时:
I perceived a change in his mood. = 我觉察到他情绪的变化。
They perceived the danger immediately. = 他们立刻察觉到了危险。

现在完成时:
She has perceived the truth behind the lie. = 她已经看穿了谎言背后的真相。
We have perceived a shift in public opinion. = 我们已经觉察到民意的转变。

过去完成时:
He had perceived the flaw in the argument before anyone else. = 他比其他人更早地觉察到论证中的缺陷。
They had perceived the signs of trouble earlier that day. = 他们当天早些时候就已经察觉到麻烦的迹象。

将来时:
I will perceive the difference when I see it. = 当我看到的时候，我会察觉到不同之处。
They will perceive the benefits of this program soon. = 他们很快就会意识到这个项目的益处。

虚拟语气:
If I perceived the risk, I would have acted differently. = 如果我察觉到风险，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had perceived the warning signs sooner. = 我希望我早点察觉到警告信号。

被动语态:
The painting was perceived as a masterpiece. = 这幅画被认为是杰作。
The situation is often perceived as hopeless. = 这种情况常常被认为是绝望的。

疑问句:
How did you perceive the situation? = 你是如何看待这种情况的？
Did they perceive any threat? = 他们察觉到任何威胁了吗？

否定句:
I didn't perceive any hostility in his tone. = 我没有觉察到他语气中的敌意。
They didn't perceive the significance of the event. = 他们没有意识到这件事的重要性。
