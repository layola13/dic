loaned:/loʊnd/| vt.|借出；贷款；给予|To give something to someone for a period of time with the understanding that it will be returned.  It implies a temporary transfer of possession or use. |He loaned me his car for the weekend. 他把他的车借给我周末用。|  She loaned the library a rare book. 她向图书馆捐赠了一本珍稀书籍。| lent, borrowed |  received, kept | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He loans books to his friends regularly. = 他定期借书给他的朋友。
The bank loans money to small businesses. = 银行向小型企业贷款。


一般过去时:
I loaned him my bicycle last week. = 我上周把我的自行车借给他了。
They loaned their cottage to their neighbors for the summer. = 他们把他们的别墅借给邻居们整个夏天用。


现在完成时:
She has loaned her car to several people this year. = 今年她已经把她的车借给好几个人了。
The company has loaned millions of dollars to various projects. = 公司已经向多个项目贷款数百万美元。


过去完成时:
He had loaned his tools to his brother before he needed them himself. = 他在他自己需要之前已经把工具借给了他兄弟。
The museum had loaned the painting to another gallery for an exhibition. = 博物馆之前已经把这幅画借给另一家画廊举办展览了。


将来时:
I will loan you my camera if you promise to take care of it. = 如果你答应好好保管，我会把我的相机借给你。
The university will loan more money to research projects next year. = 大学明年将向研究项目提供更多资金。


虚拟语气:
If I loaned him the money, he would be able to start his business. = 如果我借钱给他，他就能开始他的事业了。
I wish I had loaned him my umbrella; he got soaked in the rain. = 我真希望我把雨伞借给他；他淋雨淋湿了。


被动语态:
Money was loaned to the farmers at a low interest rate. = 以低利率向农民提供贷款。
The equipment was loaned to the school for a year. = 设备借给了学校一年。


疑问句:
Did you loan your car to John? = 你把你的车借给约翰了吗？
Will the bank loan me the money I need? = 银行会借给我我需要的钱吗？


否定句:
I didn't loan him any money. = 我没有借给他任何钱。
They wouldn't loan us their car. = 他们不肯把车借给我们。
