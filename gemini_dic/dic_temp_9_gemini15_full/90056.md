hospitalizing: /ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 将…送入医院；使住院 |  The act of placing someone in a hospital for treatment or observation.  It implies a need for medical care beyond what can be provided at home. |

例句：

The doctor decided to hospitalize the patient after the accident. = 医生在事故发生后决定将病人送入医院。

They were hospitalizing children with severe burns. = 他们正在将严重烧伤的儿童送入医院。


近义词：admitting (to hospital), confining (to hospital)

反义词：discharging (from hospital), releasing (from hospital)


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hospital is hospitalizing many patients with the flu. = 医院正在收治许多患流感的病人。
He usually hospitalizes patients who need immediate care. = 他通常会收治需要立即护理的病人。


一般过去时:
The surgeon hospitalized the patient immediately after the operation. = 手术后，外科医生立即将病人送入医院。
They hospitalized her because of her severe injuries.  = 因为她伤势严重，他们把她送进了医院。


现在完成时:
The hospital has hospitalized over 100 patients this week. = 这家医院本周已收治了100多名病人。
They have hospitalized him twice this year for the same condition. = 今年因为同样的病情，他们已经将他送医两次了。


过去完成时:
The doctor had hospitalized the patient before the emergency surgery. = 在紧急手术之前，医生已经将病人送入了医院。
Before the accident, they had never hospitalized him. = 事故发生前，他们从未将他送入医院。


将来时:
They will hospitalize her if her condition worsens. = 如果她的病情恶化，他们将会把她送进医院。
The doctors think they will hospitalize him for observation.  = 医生认为他们会将他送院观察。


虚拟语气:
If his condition were worse, they would hospitalize him immediately. = 如果他的病情更糟，他们会立即将他送入医院。
I wish they had hospitalized him sooner. = 我希望他们早点把他送进医院。


被动语态:
The patient was hospitalized for three days. = 病人住院三天。
Many patients are being hospitalized due to the outbreak. = 由于疫情爆发，许多病人正在住院。


疑问句:
Was the child hospitalized after the fall? = 孩子摔倒后住院了吗？
Will he be hospitalized for his heart condition? = 他会因为心脏病而住院吗？


否定句:
They did not hospitalize the patient because his injuries were minor. = 因为他的伤势轻微，他们没有将他送入医院。
The doctor decided not to hospitalize her. = 医生决定不让她住院。


PHRASE->

planned hospitalization = 计划住院
