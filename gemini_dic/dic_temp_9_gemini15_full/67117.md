expone:/ɪkˈspoʊn/ | vt. |阐述；解释；说明|To explain or set forth systematically and in detail.  It often involves clarifying a complex topic or idea.|例句：The professor expounded on the theory of relativity. 教授详细阐述了相对论。|例句：He expounded his views on the matter at length. 他详细阐述了他对这件事的看法。|近义词：explain, elucidate, clarify, illustrate |反义词：obscure, conceal, mystify |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expounds his ideas clearly. = 他清晰地阐述了他的想法。
The teacher expounds the grammar rules patiently. = 老师耐心地讲解语法规则。


一般过去时:
She expounded the poem's meaning beautifully. = 她优美地阐述了这首诗的含义。
The speaker expounded the benefits of the new policy. = 演讲者阐述了新政策的好处。


现在完成时:
He has expounded on this topic in several articles. = 他已经在几篇文章中阐述了这个话题。
The scientist has expounded the latest research findings. = 科学家已经阐述了最新的研究成果。


过去完成时:
She had expounded the theory before the audience arrived. = 观众到来之前，她已经阐述了这个理论。
They had expounded their concerns to the committee. = 他们已经向委员会阐述了他们的担忧。


将来时:
He will expound on the subject in his upcoming lecture. = 他将在即将到来的讲座中阐述这个主题。
The expert will expound the technical details of the project. = 专家将阐述项目的技术细节。


虚拟语气:
If he expounded his ideas more clearly, people would understand better. = 如果他更清晰地阐述他的想法，人们就会更好地理解。
I wish he had expounded on the implications of his research. = 我希望他阐述一下他的研究的意义。


被动语态:
The theory was expounded in detail in the textbook. = 理论在教科书中得到了详细的阐述。
The new findings are being expounded at the conference. = 新发现正在会议上被阐述。


疑问句:
How will he expound this complex issue? = 他将如何阐述这个复杂的问题？
Will the speaker expound on the recent developments? = 演讲者会阐述最近的发展吗？


否定句:
He didn't expound his ideas effectively. = 他没有有效地阐述他的想法。
The article didn't expound the topic adequately. = 这篇文章没有充分阐述这个主题。


PHRASE->
expound upon = 详细解释
expound at length = 详细阐述
expound a theory = 阐述一个理论
expound the facts = 阐述事实
expound one's views = 表达自己的观点


词源:
From the Latin word "expōnere," meaning "to set forth," "to explain," or "to expose."  It combines the prefix "ex-" (out) and "pōnere" (to put, to place).
