galaxy:/ˈɡæləksi/| n. |星系，银河系 | A galaxy is a system of millions or billions of stars, together with gas and dust, held together by gravitational attraction.  Galaxies can be spiral, elliptical, or irregular in shape. | Example:  Our solar system is located in the Milky Way galaxy. 我们的太阳系位于银河系。

The Andromeda galaxy is the closest large galaxy to our own. 仙女座星系是我们自己星系最近的大星系。|近义词：nebula (sometimes), cosmos (in a broader sense) |反义词：None (it's a vast astronomical object; no direct opposite exists). |名词
