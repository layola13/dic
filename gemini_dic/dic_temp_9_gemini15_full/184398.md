respond:/rɪˈspɒnd/ /rɪˈspɑːnd/| v.|回应，回答，反应|To answer or react to something or someone.|The company responded quickly to customer complaints. 公司迅速回应了顾客的投诉。 He responded with a smile. 他报以微笑。|answer, reply, react|ignore, neglect|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He responds to emails promptly. 他及时回复邮件。
She responds well to treatment. 她对治疗反应良好。


一般过去时:
I responded to his question immediately. 我立即回答了他的问题。
The government responded slowly to the crisis. 政府对危机反应迟缓。


现在完成时:
She has responded positively to the news. 她对这个消息反应积极。
He has responded to all my emails. 他已经回复了我所有的邮件。


过去完成时:
They had responded to the alarm before the fire spread.  他们在火势蔓延之前已经做出了反应。
She had responded angrily to his accusation. 她对他指控的回应很生气。


将来时:
I will respond to your letter as soon as possible. 我会尽快回复你的信。
How will the market respond to the new product? 市场会如何回应新产品？


虚拟语气:
If he responded differently, the situation would have been better. 如果他的回应不同，情况就会好得多。
I wish he had responded more thoughtfully. 我希望他当时考虑得周到一些。


被动语态:
His proposal was responded to favorably. 他的提案受到了好评。
The incident was responded to quickly by the police. 警方迅速回应了这一事件。


疑问句:
How did she respond to your request? 她如何回应你的请求？
Will the company respond to our concerns? 公司会回应我们的担忧吗？


否定句:
He didn't respond to my email. 他没有回复我的邮件。
She didn't respond well to criticism. 她对批评反应不好。


PHRASE->
respond to: 回应
respond in kind: 以牙还牙
respond positively: 积极回应
respond negatively: 消极回应
respond appropriately: 适当地回应
