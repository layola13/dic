seen:/siːn/| v. |看见，看到；经历；理解|The past participle of "see". It can be used as an adjective or part of a verb phrase.|例句：I have seen that movie before. 我以前看过那部电影。  I've seen better days. 我经历过更好的日子。|近义词：witnessed, observed, perceived |反义词：unseen, overlooked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I see the bird. = 我看见那只鸟。
They see the problem. = 他们看到了问题。

一般过去时:
I saw the bird. = 我看见了那只鸟。
They saw the problem. = 他们看到了问题。

现在完成时:
I have seen the bird. = 我看见过那只鸟。
They have seen the problem. = 他们已经看到了问题。

过去完成时:
I had seen the bird before. = 我以前见过那只鸟。
They had seen the problem earlier. = 他们早些时候就看到了问题。

将来时:
I will see the bird tomorrow. = 我明天会看见那只鸟。
They will see the problem soon. = 他们很快就会看到问题。


被动语态:
The bird was seen by me. = 那只鸟被我看见了。
The problem was seen by them. = 那个问题被他们看到了。

疑问句:
Have you seen my keys? = 你看见我的钥匙了吗？
Did you see the accident? = 你看见事故了吗？

否定句:
I haven't seen him today. = 我今天没看见他。
They didn't see the sign. = 他们没有看见那个标志。


PHRASE->
It has been seen that... = 人们已经看到...
As can be seen... = 正如所见...
It can be seen from... = 从...可以看出...
Seen from this perspective... = 从这个角度来看...
To be seen = 有待观察
