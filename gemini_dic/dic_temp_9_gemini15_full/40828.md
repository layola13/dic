convulses:/kənˈvʌlsɪz/ | v. | 使抽搐；使震动；使剧烈摇动 | To shake violently and uncontrollably; to cause to shake violently.  |The earthquake convulsed the city. 地震使这座城市剧烈震动。The news convulsed her with laughter.  这个消息让她笑得前仰后合。 | shake, agitate, jolt | soothe, calm | 动词
