sight-reads:/ˈsaɪt riːdz/| v.|视奏，看谱演奏|To play a piece of music from the written score without prior practice or rehearsal.|例句：She sight-reads incredibly well. 她视奏能力极强。|例句：The pianist effortlessly sight-read the complex sonata.  这位钢琴家轻松地视奏了这首复杂的奏鸣曲。|近义词：plays at sight, reads at sight|反义词：memorizes, rehearses


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sight-reads new music every day. = 他每天都视奏新的乐曲。
She sight-reads with amazing fluency. = 她视奏非常流畅。

一般过去时:
The orchestra sight-read the difficult piece flawlessly. = 乐队完美地视奏了这首难度高的乐曲。
