late-night: /ˌleɪtˈnaɪt/ | adj. |深夜的，夜深了的 |  Relating to or occurring late at night.  This adjective describes something that happens or is done during the late hours of the night, typically after midnight or past a conventional bedtime. |

近义词：midnight, nocturnal


反义词：early-morning, daytime


词性：形容词
