lamblike:/ˈlæmlaɪk/| adj.|像羔羊的；温顺的；驯服的|Resembling a lamb in gentleness, meekness, or innocence.  It often implies a lack of assertiveness or aggression.|The lamblike child readily obeyed her parents.  这个像羔羊一样温顺的孩子很容易听从父母的话。|近义词：meek, gentle, docile, submissive|反义词：aggressive, assertive, defiant, unruly
