gamekeeping: /ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpɪŋ/ | n. |狩猎管理；猎场管理| The practice of managing wildlife, especially game animals, for hunting.  It involves habitat management, population control, and the enforcement of hunting regulations. | 例句：Good gamekeeping practices ensure a sustainable population of deer.  良好的狩猎管理措施确保了鹿群的可持续发展。


例句：The estate relies heavily on its income from gamekeeping.  这片庄园很大程度上依赖于狩猎管理带来的收入。


近义词：wildlife management, hunting management, conservation


反义词：neglect, mismanagement


词性：名词
