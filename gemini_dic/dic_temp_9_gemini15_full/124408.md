motorbiked:/ˈmoʊtərbɑɪkt/| v. |骑摩托车|To travel or ride on a motorbike.|例句：He motorbiked across the country. 他骑摩托车穿越了全国。|近义词：rode a motorbike, motored|反义词：walked, cycled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He motorbikes to work every day. = 他每天骑摩托车上班。
They motorbike around the lake on weekends. = 他们周末在湖边骑摩托车兜风。


一般过去时:
She motorbiked to the beach yesterday. = 她昨天骑摩托车去了海滩。
We motorbiked through the mountains last summer. = 我们去年夏天骑摩托车穿越了山区。


现在完成时:
He has motorbiked across three continents. = 他已经骑摩托车穿越了三个洲。
They have motorbiked extensively throughout Southeast Asia. = 他们已经在东南亚广泛地骑摩托车旅行。


过去完成时:
She had motorbiked for hours before she stopped for a rest. = 她骑了几个小时的摩托车才停下来休息。
They had motorbiked all day before reaching their destination. = 他们在到达目的地之前骑了一整天的摩托车。


将来时:
I will motorbike to the store tomorrow. = 我明天将骑摩托车去商店。
We will motorbike along the coast next week. = 我们下周将沿着海岸骑摩托车。


虚拟语气:
If I had a motorbike, I would motorbike to work. = 如果我有一辆摩托车，我会骑摩托车上班。
I wish I could motorbike across the Sahara Desert. = 我真希望我能骑摩托车穿越撒哈拉沙漠。


被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used passively)  The motorbike was motorbiked (rare and unnatural). = 摩托车被...骑行（不自然）


疑问句:
Do you motorbike often? = 你经常骑摩托车吗？
Did he motorbike to school this morning? = 他今天早上骑摩托车去学校了吗？


否定句:
I don't motorbike very often. = 我不经常骑摩托车。
She didn't motorbike to the party last night. = 她昨晚没有骑摩托车去派对。
