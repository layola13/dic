mirages:/mɪˈrɑːʒ/| n.|海市蜃楼，幻想|An optical phenomenon that creates the illusion of water or other objects where none exist, typically in deserts or on hot roads.  It's caused by the refraction of light through layers of air with different temperatures. |例句：The desert travelers were fooled by the shimmering mirages.  沙漠旅行者被闪烁的海市蜃楼欺骗了。

例句：He chased after his career mirages, only to find disillusionment. 他追逐事业的幻想，最终却发现只是一场空。


近义词：illusion, hallucination, fantasy


反义词：reality, truth


词性:名词
