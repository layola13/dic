dialect:/ˈdaɪəlekt/| n. |方言，土语|A variety of a language that is spoken in a particular region or by a particular social group and that often has features of pronunciation, grammar, and vocabulary that are different from other varieties of the same language.  |Example: The villagers spoke a dialect of English that was difficult for outsiders to understand.  村民们说的是一种英语方言，外人很难理解。|近义词：regionalism, vernacular, idiom|反义词：standard language, formal language
