idolizers:/ˈaɪdɑːlaɪzərz/| n. |崇拜者，偶像迷|Those who admire and revere someone or something to an excessive degree.  They often emulate their idol's actions and beliefs. |例句：The idolizers camped outside the stadium for days to catch a glimpse of their favorite band. 崇拜者们在体育场外露营数天，只为一睹他们最喜欢的乐队的风采。

例句：Many young idolizers  try to imitate the fashion sense of their favorite celebrities.  许多年轻的崇拜者试图模仿他们最喜欢的名人的时尚感。


近义词：admirers, devotees, fans, worshippers|反义词：critics, detractors, opponents|词性:名词
