caravaned:/ˈkærəvænd/| v.| 旅行；以篷车旅行 | To travel in a caravan; to journey in a group, often with covered wagons or vehicles. |例句：They caravaned across the desert for weeks. 他们穿越沙漠旅行了好几个星期。  They caravaned through the mountains, enjoying the scenery.  他们穿过山脉旅行，欣赏着风景。|近义词：travelled, journeyed, trekked |反义词：stayed, remained


USAGE->

They caravan regularly across the country. = 他们定期在全国各地旅行。
We caravan to different campsites every summer. = 我们每年夏天都会去不同的营地旅行。
