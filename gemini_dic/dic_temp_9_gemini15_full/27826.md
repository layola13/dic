canoeing:/kəˈnuɪŋ/| n. |独木舟运动；划独木舟|The activity of travelling in a canoe.  Canoeing is a popular recreational activity enjoyed by many.|例句：We went canoeing on the lake yesterday. = 我们昨天在湖上划独木舟。

例句：They are planning a canoeing trip down the river. = 他们正计划沿着河进行一次独木舟旅行。


近义词：kayaking (皮划艇运动)


反义词：None (没有直接的反义词)


词性：名词


词源:源自canoe (独木舟) + -ing (动名词后缀)，表示“划独木舟”的动作或活动。  The word "canoe" itself originates from the Caribbean Taíno language.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He canoes every weekend. = 他每个周末都划独木舟。
She canoes on the calm river. = 她在平静的河上划独木舟。


一般过去时:
We canoed down the river last summer. = 我们去年夏天沿着河划独木舟。
They canoed across the lake in two hours. = 他们两个小时划船横渡了湖泊。


现在完成时:
I have canoed many times in my life. = 我一生中划过很多次独木舟。
She has never canoed before. = 她以前从未划过独木舟。


过去完成时:
They had canoed for hours before they reached the island. = 他们在到达岛屿之前已经划了几个小时的独木舟。
We had canoed five miles before the storm hit. =暴风雨来临之前，我们已经划了五英里。


将来时:
We will canoe in the sea next week. = 我们下周将在海里划独木舟。
They will canoe to the hidden waterfall. = 他们将划独木舟到隐藏的瀑布。


虚拟语气:
If I had a canoe, I would canoe on the lake. = 如果我有一艘独木舟，我会在湖上划独木舟。
I wish I were canoeing right now. = 我真希望我现在正在划独木舟。


被动语态:  (Canoeing itself cannot be easily put into the passive voice.  The action is inherently active.)  Instead, we would use a related verb:
The canoe was paddled skillfully. = 这艘独木舟被熟练地划动。
The race was won by the experienced canoeist. = 比赛由经验丰富的独木舟运动员赢得。


疑问句:
Do you like canoeing? = 你喜欢划独木舟吗？
Will they be canoeing tomorrow? = 他们明天会划独木舟吗？


否定句:
He doesn't enjoy canoeing. = 他不喜欢划独木舟。
They didn't canoe yesterday. = 他们昨天没有划独木舟。
