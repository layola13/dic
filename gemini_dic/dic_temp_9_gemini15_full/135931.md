nonpungently:/ˌnɒnˈpʌndʒəntli/ | adv. | 不辛辣地，不刺激地 | Not in a pungent manner; mildly.  Describes something lacking pungency, a strong, sharp taste or smell. | The chef prepared the dish nonpungently, avoiding strong spices.  厨师不辛辣地烹制这道菜，避免使用辛辣的香料。| mildly, gently, subtly | pungently, sharply, acridly |


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.  Therefore, standard verb tense examples are not applicable.

Example sentences illustrating different sentence structures:

Declarative Sentence:
The sauce was prepared nonpungently to appeal to a wider audience. = 酱汁调制得比较清淡，以吸引更广泛的受众。


Interrogative Sentence:
Was the curry prepared nonpungently, or was it quite spicy? = 咖喱是清淡的还是相当辣的？


Exclamatory Sentence:
How nonpungently the aroma filled the room! = 香气多么温和地充满了整个房间！


Negative Sentence:
The food was not prepared nonpungently; it was intensely flavorful. = 这食物并没有清淡的烹制；它味道非常浓郁。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "nonpungently".  The adverb is used to describe a single action or quality.
