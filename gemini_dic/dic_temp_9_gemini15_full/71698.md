flavol:/ˈfleɪvər/| n. |风味，滋味|Flavor refers to the taste and smell of food or drink, often including other sensory perceptions such as texture and temperature. It's a complex sensory experience.  |The cake has a delicious chocolate flavor.|近义词：taste, savor, aroma |反义词：tastelessness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wine possesses a fruity flavor. = 这葡萄酒带有果香味。
This cheese has a strong flavor. = 这奶酪味道很浓。


一般过去时:
The soup lacked flavor yesterday. = 昨天的汤没味道。
The pie had a surprisingly good flavor. = 这馅饼的味道出奇的好。


现在完成时:
They have added artificial flavor to the drink. = 他们在这饮料里添加了人工香料。
This dish has lost its original flavor. = 这道菜失去了原本的味道。


过去完成时:
The cook had enhanced the flavor with herbs before serving. = 厨师在端上菜之前用香草增进了菜肴的风味。
I had never tasted such a unique flavor before. = 我以前从未尝过如此独特的味道。


将来时:
The chef will add more flavor to the sauce. = 厨师将会在酱汁里添加更多调味料。
This new recipe will have a spicy flavor. = 这新的食谱会有辣味。


虚拟语气:
If it had more flavor, I would eat it. = 如果它更有味道，我会吃的。
I wish this coffee had a stronger flavor. = 我希望这咖啡味道更浓一些。


被动语态:
The food is enhanced with natural flavor. = 食物是用天然香料增味的。
The product's flavor is often described as sweet and tangy. = 该产品的味道通常被描述为又甜又酸。


疑问句:
What is the flavor of this ice cream? = 这个冰淇淋是什么味道的？
Does this tea have a strong flavor? = 这茶味道浓吗？


否定句:
The dish doesn't have much flavor. = 这道菜没什么味道。
The food lacked any discernible flavor. = 这食物没有任何可辨别的味道。


PHRASE->
flavor enhancer = 增味剂
flavor profile = 风味特征
artificial flavor = 人工香料
natural flavor = 天然香料
full-bodied flavor = 浓郁的味道
