e-book:/ˈiːbʊk/| n. |电子书|An electronic version of a book, readable on computers or other electronic devices.  E-books often have features such as search functions, adjustable font sizes, and interactive content not found in print books.|例句：I prefer reading e-books on my tablet because they are lightweight and convenient. 我更喜欢在我的平板电脑上阅读电子书，因为它们轻便又方便。|例句： Many libraries now offer a large selection of e-books for their patrons to borrow. 许多图书馆现在为其读者提供大量电子书供其借阅。|近义词：digital book, online book|反义词：paperback, hardcover
