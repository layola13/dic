fathered:/ˈfɑːðərd/|/ˈfɑːðɚd/| v. |当父亲；创造；促成|To be the father of; to have fathered a child.  Also, to create or bring about something. |例句：He fathered ten children. 他养育了十个孩子。

例句：The new policy fathered significant economic growth.  这项新政策促成了显著的经济增长。


近义词：sired, begat, engendered, produced, created

反义词：abandoned, neglected (in the sense of raising a child)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fathers many children. = 他养育许多孩子。
She fathers innovative ideas. = 她孕育创新理念。


一般过去时:
He fathered a child last year. = 他去年生了一个孩子。
The scientist fathered a groundbreaking theory. = 科学家提出了一项具有开创性的理论。


现在完成时:
He has fathered three children in his life. = 他一生养育了三个孩子。
The company has fathered many successful products. = 这家公司已经创造了许多成功的产品。


过去完成时:
He had fathered two children before he married. = 他结婚前已经养育了两个孩子。
The artist had fathered many masterpieces before his untimely death. =这位艺术家在英年早逝之前创作了许多杰作。


将来时:
He will father more children in the future. = 他将来会生育更多孩子。
The project will father new opportunities for collaboration. = 该项目将为合作创造新的机遇。


虚拟语气:
If he fathered a child, he would be a great dad. = 如果他生了孩子，他会成为一个好爸爸。
If the experiment had fathered positive results, they would have published it. = 如果实验产生了积极的结果，他们就会发表它。


被动语态:
Many children were fathered by him. = 许多孩子都是他抚养的。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常不使用)
The invention was fathered by a team of brilliant minds. = 这个发明是由一群杰出的思想家创造的。


疑问句:
Did he father that child? = 他是那个孩子的父亲吗？
What innovative ideas did this company father this year? = 今年这家公司孕育了什么创新理念？


否定句:
He did not father that child. = 他不是那个孩子的父亲。
The project failed to father any significant results. = 该项目未能产生任何重大成果。
