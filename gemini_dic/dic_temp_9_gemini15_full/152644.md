peacockly:/ˈpiːkɒkli/ | adj. |孔雀般地，华丽地，炫耀地 |  Resembling a peacock in appearance or behavior; ostentatiously showy.  The word is not widely used and is considered informal.  It implies excessive display or vanity. | 例句：He strutted peacockly across the room, showing off his new suit. 他在房间里昂首阔步，炫耀他的新西装。


The woman dressed peacockly for the party, attracting a lot of attention.  这位女士穿着华丽的衣服参加派对，吸引了很多人的注意。| 近义词：showily, ostentatiously, flamboyantly | 反义词：plainly, modestly, simply |  词性：形容词
