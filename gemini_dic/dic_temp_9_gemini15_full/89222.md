homeworks:/ˈhoʊmwɜːrks/| n.|家庭作业|Homework refers to tasks assigned to students by their teachers to be completed outside of class time.  It is usually intended to reinforce learning, practice skills, or extend understanding of concepts taught in the classroom. |Example: I have a lot of homeworks to do this weekend.  这个周末我有很多家庭作业要做。|近义词：assignments, exercises|反义词：N/A

My homework is due tomorrow. = 我的作业明天到期。
I do my homework every day. = 我每天都做作业。

一般过去时:
I finished my homework last night. = 我昨晚完成了作业。
She did her homework before dinner. = 她晚饭前做了作业。

She has already done her homework. = 她已经完成了作业。

过去完成时:
