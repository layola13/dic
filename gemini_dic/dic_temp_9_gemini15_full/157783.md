placentation:/ˌplæsənˈteɪʃən/| n. |胎盘形成；胎座；胎盘着生方式 |The arrangement of placentae within the ovary of a plant, or the way in which the placenta is attached to the uterine wall in mammals.  This refers to the location and arrangement of the placenta within the ovary of a plant or the uterus of a mammal. In plants, it describes how ovules are attached to the ovary wall; in mammals, it describes how the placenta is attached to the uterine wall. |Example:  The study focused on the placentation of different plant species.|近义词：implantation (in mammals), ovule attachment (in plants) |反义词：无
