nondrivable: /ˌnɒnˈdraɪvəbəl/ | adj. | 不可驾驶的，无法通行的 | Not able to be driven on.  This term usually refers to roads or paths that are impassable by vehicles due to their condition or restrictions. | 例句：The mountain road became nondrivable after the heavy snowfall.  大雪过后，山路变得无法通行。


The muddy track was nondrivable after the rain. 雨后泥泞的小路无法通行。


近义词：impassable, inaccessible, unpassable, blocked | 反义词：drivable, passable, accessible
