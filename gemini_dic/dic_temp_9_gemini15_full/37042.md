comas: /ˈkɒməz/ | n. | 昏迷；失去知觉 | A state of prolonged unconsciousness; a coma is a deep state of unconsciousness from which a person cannot be easily awakened.  It's a serious medical condition often caused by trauma, stroke, or illness. | 例句：The accident left him in a coma for several weeks.  事故使他昏迷了几周。


The patient was diagnosed with a diabetic coma after experiencing a sudden drop in blood sugar. 患者因血糖突然下降而被诊断为糖尿病昏迷。 | 近义词：unconsciousness, stupor | 反义词：consciousness, wakefulness |  名词
