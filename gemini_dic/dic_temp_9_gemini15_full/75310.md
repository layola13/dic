frontlet:/ˈfrʌntlət/| n. |额饰，额带|A narrow band worn across the forehead, often as an ornament or to keep hair in place.  It can also refer to a small frontal area or part.|例句：She wore a jeweled frontlet to the ball. 她戴着镶宝石的额饰去参加舞会。

例句：The baby's frontlet was damp with sweat. 婴儿的额头湿漉漉的。|近义词：headband, diadem (for more formal/regal versions), fillet|反义词：None directly opposite in meaning.
