phosphate:/ˈfɒsfeɪt/| n. |磷酸盐，磷酸酯|A salt or ester of phosphoric acid. Phosphates are essential for many biological processes, including energy transfer and the building of bones and teeth.|例句：Calcium phosphate is a major component of bones and teeth. 磷酸钙是骨骼和牙齿的主要成分。|例句：Many fertilizers contain phosphates to boost plant growth.  许多肥料含有磷酸盐以促进植物生长。|近义词：phosphoric acid salt|反义词：无
