gorillas:/ɡəˈrɪləz/| n. |大猩猩 |Large, powerful apes with dark fur, inhabiting forests of central Africa. They are known for their intelligence and social behavior.  | Gorillas are the largest living primates. |近义词：apes, primates |反义词:None

Gorillas live in the forests of Central Africa. = 大猩猩生活在非洲中部森林里。
They eat mostly plants. = 它们主要吃植物。
