cage:/keɪdʒ/| n. |笼子，鸟笼 |A cage is a structure made of bars or wires, used to confine animals or birds.  |例句：The bird sings happily in its cage.  这只鸟在笼子里快乐地唱歌。

例句：The lion was kept in a large cage at the zoo.  这只狮子被关在动物园里一个大笼子里。


近义词：enclosure, coop, pen |反义词：freedom, liberty |词性：名词

词源:
The word "cage" ultimately derives from the Old French word "cage," meaning "cage," which itself comes from the Latin word "cavea," meaning "a hollow place, den, enclosure."  The evolution reflects the basic function of a cage: to provide a confined space.
