hospodar:/ˈhɒspədɑːr/| n. |领主，统治者 |A hospodar was a ruler, especially in the Balkans, often with considerable autonomy but ultimately subject to a higher power.  The term carries historical connotations of a powerful landowner or regional governor. |Example: The hospodar ruled his province with an iron fist, but was himself answerable to the Sultan.  =这位领主铁腕统治他的省份，但他本身要对苏丹负责。  The former hospodar was exiled after a rebellion. = 一场叛乱后，前任领主被流放。|近义词：lord, prince, governor, potentate |反义词：subject, vassal, peasant |名词
