reapproach:/ˌriːəˈproʊtʃ/| vt.|重新接近；再次尝试；重新考虑|To approach again; to make a new attempt to deal with something; to reconsider.|例句：After the initial rejection, he decided to reapproach the investor with a revised proposal.  在最初被拒绝后，他决定带着修改后的提案再次尝试说服投资者。

例句：The committee decided to reapproach the problem from a different angle.  委员会决定从不同的角度重新考虑这个问题。


近义词：revisit, reconsider, retry, reassess|反义词：abandon, forsake, reject, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reapproaches the problem every day. = 他每天都重新考虑这个问题。
They reapproach the negotiation frequently. = 他们经常重新开始谈判。


一般过去时:
She reapproached her boss about the raise. = 她再次向老板提及加薪的事。
He reapproached the task with renewed determination. = 他以新的决心重新开始这项任务。


现在完成时:
We have reapproached the issue several times. = 我们已经多次重新审视了这个问题。
They have reapproached the customer with a better offer. = 他们已经向客户提出了一个更好的报价。


过去完成时:
I had reapproached the subject before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经再次谈及了这个话题。
They had reapproached the design many times before it was finalized. = 在最终定稿之前，他们已经多次修改了设计。


将来时:
She will reapproach him after she has more evidence. = 她收集到更多证据后会再次与他接触。
We will reapproach the project after the holidays. = 假期结束后，我们将重新开始这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If I were to reapproach the situation, I would do it differently. = 如果我要重新处理这种情况，我会采取不同的方法。
I wish I had reapproached the client sooner. = 我真希望我早点再次联系客户。


被动语态:
The problem was reapproached by the team. = 这个问题被团队重新审视了。
The issue will be reapproached in the next meeting. = 这个问题将在下一次会议上再次讨论。


疑问句:
Will you reapproach the lender for a loan extension? = 你会再次向贷款机构申请延期吗？
Have they reapproached the matter since the last discussion? = 自上次讨论以来，他们是否再次考虑过这个问题？


否定句:
He did not reapproach the topic after the initial discussion. = 初次讨论后，他没有再次谈及这个话题。
They will not reapproach the negotiations until next week. = 在下周之前，他们不会重新开始谈判。
