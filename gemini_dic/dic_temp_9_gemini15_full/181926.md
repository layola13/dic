remanifest:/ˌriːˈmænɪfɛst/ | vt. |重新显示；再次证明；重新体现 | To show or prove something again; to make something apparent again.  |例句：The recent events remanifest the importance of international cooperation. 近期事件再次证明了国际合作的重要性。

The newly discovered documents remanifest the artist's early influences.  新发现的文件再次体现了这位艺术家早期的影响。| 近义词：rediscover, reaffirm, demonstrate, reveal | 反义词：obscure, conceal, hide |动词
