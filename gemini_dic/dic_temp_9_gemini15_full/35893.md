cocoon:/kəˈkuːn/| n. |茧；保护层；庇护所 |A cocoon is a protective covering, usually of silk, spun by a larva (such as a moth or butterfly) to protect itself during its pupal stage. It can also refer to a situation or environment that provides protection or shelter. |例句：The caterpillar spun a silken cocoon around itself. 毛毛虫把自己裹在丝茧里。

The shy girl built a cocoon of silence around herself.  害羞的女孩在自己周围筑起沉默的屏障。|近义词：casing, shell, shroud, shelter |反义词：exposure, openness
