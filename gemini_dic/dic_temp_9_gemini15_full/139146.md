obeyer:/oʊˈbeɪər/ | n. |服从者，遵守者 | A person who obeys; someone who follows instructions or rules.  |Example: He was a loyal obeyer of the law. 他是忠实的守法者。|近义词: follower, adherent, conformist |反义词: rebel, defiant, disobedient |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The obeyer follows the instructions carefully.  = 服从者仔细地遵循指示。
He is a strict obeyer of the rules. = 他是严格遵守规则的人。


一般过去时:
The obeyer obeyed the orders without question. = 服从者毫无疑问地服从了命令。
She was a willing obeyer in her youth. = 她年轻时是一个乐意的服从者。


现在完成时:
The obeyer has always followed the leader's commands. = 服从者一直遵循领导的命令。
He has been a faithful obeyer throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中一直都是忠实的服从者。


过去完成时:
The obeyer had already obeyed before the order was repeated. = 在命令重复之前，服从者已经服从了。
She had always been an obeyer of her parents' wishes. = 她一直都是服从父母意愿的人。


将来时:
The obeyer will follow all the new regulations. = 服从者将遵守所有新规章。
He will be a good obeyer of the law in the future. = 他将来会成为一个遵守法律的好公民。


虚拟语气:
If he were an obeyer, he would have followed the rules. = 如果他是服从者，他会遵守规则。
I wish he were a more willing obeyer. = 我希望他更乐意服从。


被动语态:
The rules were obeyed by the obeyer. = 规则被服从者遵守了。
The orders were promptly obeyed by the obeyer. = 命令被服从者迅速执行了。


疑问句:
