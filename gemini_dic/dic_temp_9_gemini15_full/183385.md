reproject:/ˌriːˈprɒdʒekt/| vt.|重新投影；再投射；重新规划|To project again or anew; to project onto a different surface or coordinate system.  This often involves changing the projection parameters (e.g., changing the map projection) or transforming the data to a new coordinate reference system. |例句：We need to reproject the satellite imagery to a UTM coordinate system. 我们需要将卫星图像重新投影到UTM坐标系。

例句：The architect decided to reproject the building's design to better suit the site. 建筑师决定重新设计建筑物，以更好地适应场地。


近义词：remap, transform, convert|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action could be described as "leaving the projection unchanged" or "using the original projection".|动词
