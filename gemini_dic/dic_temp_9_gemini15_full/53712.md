discloser:/dɪˈskloʊzər/| n.|披露者；揭露者；公开声明|The act of disclosing something; the person or thing that discloses something.  A person or thing that makes something known. |例句：The discloser of the information remained anonymous.  信息披露者仍然匿名。

例句：The company's press release was a carefully worded discloser of their financial difficulties. 公司的新闻稿是精心措辞的，披露了他们的财务困境。


近义词：revealer, informant, exposer, declarer

反义词：concealer, hider, suppressor


词性:名词
