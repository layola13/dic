co-instantaneously: /ˌkoʊɪnˌstænˈteɪniəsli/ | adv. |同时地，同时发生地 | happening at the same instant; simultaneously.  | The two events happened co-instantaneously. 这两个事件同时发生。 | simultaneously, concurrently | sequentially, successively | 无


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't conjugate like verbs or nouns.  Therefore, grammatical tenses don't apply.  Its use is always in the same form.

例句1: The explosion and the earthquake occurred co-instantaneously. 爆炸和地震同时发生。
例句2: The two signals arrived co-instantaneously at the receiver. 两个信号同时到达接收器。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "co-instantaneously."  The word itself functions as a standalone adverbial modifier.
