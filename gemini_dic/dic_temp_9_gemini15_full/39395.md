conjurer:/ˈkʌndʒərər/ | n. |变戏法的人；魔法师；骗子 | A conjurer is a person who performs magic tricks, illusions, or sleight of hand.  They often use misdirection and skillful manipulation to create the illusion of supernatural powers.  | Example: The conjurer amazed the audience with his incredible card tricks.  变戏法的人用他不可思议的纸牌戏法让观众惊叹不已。 |近义词：magician, illusionist, sorcerer | 反义词：realist
