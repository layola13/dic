magniloquent:/mæɡˈnɪləkwənt/| adj.|夸夸其谈的；雄辩的；华而不实的| characterized by lofty and pompous language; bombastic.  It implies excessive use of impressive-sounding language, often to the point of being insincere or affected. |例句：His magniloquent speech failed to impress the audience, who found it overly verbose and lacking in substance.  他的夸夸其谈的演讲未能打动听众，他们觉得它过于冗长且缺乏实质内容。

例句：The general delivered a magniloquent address to his troops, full of patriotic fervor and promises of victory.  将军向他的部队发表了一篇雄辩的讲话，充满了爱国热情和胜利的承诺。


近义词：bombastic, pompous, grandiose, high-flown, oratorical

反义词：simple, modest, unassuming, concise, terse
