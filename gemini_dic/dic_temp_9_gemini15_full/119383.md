microdegree:/ˈmaɪkroʊdɪɡriː/| n. |微学位，微型学位 |A short, focused online course that provides professional skills in a specific area, typically shorter and less expensive than a full degree.  It often focuses on a single skill or competency and leads to a certificate or badge upon completion. | Example:  She completed a microdegree in digital marketing to boost her career prospects. 她完成了一个数字营销微学位课程来提升她的职业前景。|近义词：nanodegree, online course, professional certificate |反义词：full degree, master's degree

词源:

The word "microdegree" is a blend of "micro" (meaning small) and "degree" (meaning a qualification awarded by an educational institution).  It reflects the shorter duration and more focused nature of these online learning programs compared to traditional degrees.
