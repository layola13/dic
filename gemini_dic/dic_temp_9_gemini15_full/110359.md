lightship:/ˈlaɪtʃɪp/| n. |灯船；灯塔船|A lightship is a ship that is anchored in a specific location and equipped with lights and other navigational aids to warn ships of dangers or guide them through hazardous waters.  It serves as a floating lighthouse.|The lightship warned approaching vessels of the treacherous reef. 这艘灯船警告驶近的船只注意危险的暗礁。|近义词：lighthouse (but fixed, not mobile), buoy|反义词：无
