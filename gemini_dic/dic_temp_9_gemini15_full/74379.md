framboesia:/ˌfræmˈbeɪziə/| n. |雅司，弗朗博阿西病（一种热带皮肤病）|Framboesia, also known as yaws, is a chronic, infectious disease caused by the bacterium Treponema pallidum pertenue. It is characterized by granulomatous lesions on the skin, bones, and joints.  The disease is primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions.|The child was diagnosed with framboesia after showing characteristic skin lesions.|近义词：yaws|反义词：无
