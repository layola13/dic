repress:/rɪˈprɛs/| vt.|压制；抑制；克制；阻止…的表达|to hold back or subdue (a feeling, impulse, etc.); to prevent the expression of (a feeling, impulse, etc.)|例句：She repressed a sigh of relief. 她强忍住一声轻松的叹息。

例句：The government tried to repress the rebellion. 政府试图压制叛乱。


近义词：suppress, subdue, restrain, inhibit|反义词：express, unleash, release, encourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He represses his anger. 他压制他的怒火。
She represses her feelings. 她压抑她的感情。


一般过去时:
They repressed the uprising. 他们镇压了起义。
He repressed a scream. 他忍住一声尖叫。


现在完成时:
She has repressed her emotions for years. 她多年来一直压抑着自己的情绪。
He has repressed the truth. 他隐瞒了真相。


过去完成时:
They had repressed any mention of the incident. 他们此前已经压制了对该事件的任何提及。
She had repressed her memories of childhood trauma. 她已经压抑了她对童年创伤的记忆。


将来时:
The government will repress any dissent. 政府将压制任何异见。
He will repress his emotions no longer. 他将不再压抑自己的情绪。


虚拟语气:
If he repressed his feelings, he would be happier. 如果他压抑自己的感情，他会更快乐。
I wish I could repress my anxiety. 我希望我能压制我的焦虑。


被动语态:
The rebellion was repressed swiftly. 叛乱被迅速镇压。
His emotions were repressed for too long. 他的情绪被压抑了太久。


疑问句:
Does he repress his feelings? 他压抑自己的感情吗？
How does she repress her anger? 她如何压制她的怒火？


否定句:
He doesn't repress his opinions. 他不压抑自己的意见。
She didn't repress her laughter. 她没有忍住笑。


PHRASE->
repress one's feelings 压抑感情

词源:
re- (again, back) + press (to press, to push)  字面意思是“再次按下，压下去”。  它反映了其含义：压制，阻止某事物显露。
