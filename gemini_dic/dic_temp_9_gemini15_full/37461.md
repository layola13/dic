commissionaire:/ˌkɒmɪʃəˈneə(r)/| n. |委任管理人；管理员；（尤指大楼的）门卫|A commissionaire is a person employed to perform various duties, often involving security and reception, especially in large buildings or organizations.  They are often uniformed and responsible for greeting visitors, managing access, and providing general assistance. |The commissionaire politely directed me to the correct office.  这位管理员礼貌地把我引到了正确的办公室。|receptionist, attendant, concierge|criminal, intruder|名词
