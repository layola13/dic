combatted:/kəmˈbætɪd/| v.|战斗，对抗，搏斗|英文解析：Past tense of combat. To fight against; oppose. |例句：The soldiers bravely combatted the enemy. 士兵们勇敢地与敌人作战。  The government combatted inflation with new economic policies. 政府用新的经济政策来对抗通货膨胀。|近义词：fought, battled, struggled, opposed|反义词：cooperated, aided, assisted|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They combat poverty through education. = 他们通过教育来对抗贫困。
We combat climate change by reducing emissions. = 我们通过减少排放来对抗气候变化。

一般过去时:
The firefighters combatted the blaze for hours. = 消防员们与大火搏斗了好几个小时。
She combatted her illness with determination and courage. = 她以决心和勇气与疾病作斗争。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
