shamois:/ʃæˈmɔɪ/| n. |沙莫皮革；羚羊皮；擦拭皮|A chamois is a type of soft leather traditionally made from the skin of a chamois (a type of antelope), now often made from synthetic materials. It's used for cleaning and polishing, especially glass.  |Example: He used a chamois to clean the car windows. 他用擦拭皮擦干净了车窗。|近义词：leather cleaner, polishing cloth|反义词：无|
