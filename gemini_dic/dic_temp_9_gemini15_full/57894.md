drifters:/ˈdrɪftərz/| n.|漂泊者，流浪者，游民|People who have no fixed home and travel from place to place, often in search of work or adventure.  They are characterized by a lack of stability and a transient lifestyle.|例句：The drifters gathered around the campfire, sharing stories of their journeys. 漂泊者们围坐在篝火旁，分享着他们旅程中的故事。

例句：Many drifters found themselves drawn to the promise of gold during the California Gold Rush.  许多淘金热期间的漂泊者被加利福尼亚淘金的希望吸引。


近义词：wanderers, nomads, vagrants, itinerants, transients|反义词：settlers, residents, homebodies
