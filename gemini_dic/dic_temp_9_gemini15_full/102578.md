jaguars:/ˈdʒæɡwɑːrz/|/ˈdʒæɡjuːrz/| n. |美洲豹|A large, powerful cat found in the Americas, known for its spotted coat and powerful jaws. Jaguars are apex predators, playing a vital role in their ecosystems.|  Example:  The jaguar silently stalked its prey through the dense jungle.  美洲豹悄悄地穿过茂密的丛林跟踪猎物。


Example: We saw a jaguar on our trip to the Amazon rainforest.  我们在亚马逊雨林旅行时看到了一只美洲豹。


近义词: panther (in some contexts), big cat


反义词:  None directly, but words describing small or weak animals could be considered antonyms in a comparative sense.


词性: 名词
