daydreamer: /ˈdeɪdriːmər/ | n. | 白日梦者，幻想家 | A person who spends a lot of time daydreaming; someone who is impractical and not focused on reality.  |例句：He's such a daydreamer; he's always lost in his own world. 他是个十足的白日梦者，总是沉浸在自己的世界里。


例句：She's a talented artist, but her daydreaming nature sometimes prevents her from finishing her projects. 她是一位才华横溢的艺术家，但她爱做白日梦的天性有时会妨碍她完成项目。


近义词：fantasizer, dreamer, idealist, visionary


反义词：realist, pragmatist


词源：The word "daydreamer" is a compound word formed from "daydream" and the suffix "-er," which denotes a person who performs the action.  "Daydream" itself combines "day" (referring to the daytime) and "dream" (a sequence of images, thoughts, and emotions occurring during sleep or daydreaming).
