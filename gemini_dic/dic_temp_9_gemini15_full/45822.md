custombuilt:/ˈkʌstəm bɪlt/| adj.|定制的，量身定制的|Built or made to order according to a customer's specifications.  It implies a high degree of personalization and often a higher price than mass-produced items.|例句：The custom-built car had all the features he desired. 这辆定制汽车拥有他想要的所有功能。
例句：She ordered a custom-built wardrobe to fit the alcove. 她订购了一个定制衣柜以适合壁龛。|近义词：made-to-order, bespoke, tailored, personalized|反义词：mass-produced, standard, off-the-shelf|形容词
