podophyllin:/ˌpɒdəˈfɪlɪn/| n. |鬼臼毒素|A resin obtained from the rhizomes and roots of plants of the genus Podophyllum, used medicinally as a caustic and purgative.  It's a complex mixture of lignans, primarily podophyllotoxin.  |例句：Podophyllin is used in the treatment of certain types of warts and genital warts.|近义词：podophyllotoxin|反义词：无
