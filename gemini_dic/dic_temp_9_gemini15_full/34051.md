citystate:/ˈsɪtiˌsteɪt/ | n. |城邦；城邦国家 | A city that with its surrounding territory forms an independent state.  A city state is characterized by its urban center and its surrounding agricultural lands that are politically unified under a single government.  | Example: Ancient Athens was a powerful city-state.  古代雅典是一个强大的城邦。  Singapore is a modern example of a successful city-state. 新加坡是现代成功的城邦国家的例子。 | 近义词: independent city, polis (古希腊语) | 反义词: empire, province
