homes:/hoʊmz/| n.|家园；住宅；家乡|Plural of "home," referring to houses or places where people live, or to one's native place or country.  It can also refer to a place of comfort, security, and belonging. |例句：Many homes were destroyed by the hurricane.  许多房屋被飓风摧毁了。|例句：She longed for the comforts of her childhood homes. 她渴望童年家园的舒适。|近义词：houses, residences, dwellings, abodes|反义词： homelessness, estrangement
