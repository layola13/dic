incorrigibles:/ɪnˈkɒrɪdʒəbəlz/| n.|无可救药的人，积习难改的人|People who cannot be reformed or corrected.  They are often characterized by persistent bad behavior or criminal tendencies.|例句：The incorrigibles were sent to a reform school.  无可救药的人被送去感化院。
The incorrigibles continued their disruptive behavior despite repeated warnings.  尽管反复警告，那些积习难改的人仍然继续他们的破坏性行为。|近义词：irredeemable, irreformable, incorrigible, unrepentant|反义词：reformable, correctable, amendable
