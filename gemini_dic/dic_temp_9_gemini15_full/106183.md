knocker:/ˈnɒkər/ | n. |敲门者；门环 | A knocker is a device attached to a door for knocking to announce one's arrival, or a person who knocks. |例句：He gave the heavy knocker a resounding rap. 他用力敲响了沉重的门环。

例句：The knocker was made of brass and shaped like a lion's head.  门环是黄铜做的，形状像狮子的头。


近义词：door knocker, rapper | 反义词：None.


词源：From Middle English *knokkere, from Old English *cnoc, "a knock."
