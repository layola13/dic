masterdom:/ˈmɑːstədəm/| n. |统治；支配；专制；精通的技能|The absolute rule or authority of a master; mastery; dominion.|例句：His masterdom over the workforce was absolute.  他的对劳动力的统治是绝对的。|例句：Her masterdom of the violin was evident in her performance.  她在小提琴演奏中展现了她精湛的技艺。|近义词：domination, control, mastery, authority|反义词：subjection, servitude, incompetence
