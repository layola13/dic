parquetry:/pɑːˈkeɪtri/ | n. |镶木地板；木镶嵌工艺 | Decorative work made from small pieces of wood inlaid to form patterns.  Parquetry is often used for flooring, but can also be used for wall panels and furniture. | The dining room floor was adorned with intricate parquetry. 餐厅的地板装饰着精美的镶木地板。 The antique chest featured beautiful parquetry designs. 那只古董箱子上装饰着美丽的镶木嵌花图案。|inlay, marquetry|plain flooring|名词
