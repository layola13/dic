guacamoles:/ˌɡwɑːkəˈmoʊliːz/| n. |鳄梨酱(复数)| Guacamoles is the plural form of guacamole, a dish made from mashed avocados.  It typically includes other ingredients such as onions, tomatoes, cilantro, lime juice, and chili peppers. | Example:  The party had several different types of guacamoles, each with a unique flavor profile.  派对上有几种不同类型的鳄梨酱，每种都有独特的风味。|近义词：avocado dips, mashed avocado spread|反义词：无
