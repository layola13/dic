guacamole:/ˌɡwɑːkəˈmoʊleɪ/|/ˈɡwækəmoʊli/ | n. |鳄梨酱|A creamy dip or spread made from mashed avocados, typically seasoned with lime juice, onions, cilantro, and chili peppers.|例句：I love guacamole with tortilla chips. 我喜欢用玉米片蘸鳄梨酱。|例句：The restaurant is famous for its delicious guacamole. 这家餐馆以其美味的鳄梨酱而闻名。|近义词：avocado dip, avocado spread|反义词：无
