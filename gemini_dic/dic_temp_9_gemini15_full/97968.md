innyard:/ˈɪnjɑːrd/| n. |内院，庭院|An innyard is a courtyard or yard inside a building or a group of buildings, often enclosed by walls or buildings.  It's a less common term than "courtyard" or "yard".|例句：The old farmhouse had a charming innyard where chickens pecked around.|近义词：courtyard, yard, inner court|反义词：exterior, outside
