shippo:/ˈʃɪpoʊ/| n.|邮递，快递|Shipping refers to the process of transporting goods or merchandise from one location to another, typically using a commercial carrier. It encompasses various aspects, from packaging and labeling to the actual transportation and delivery.|例句：The shippo company delivered the package on time.  这家快递公司准时送达了包裹。|近义词：delivery, mailing, conveyance |反义词：receipt
