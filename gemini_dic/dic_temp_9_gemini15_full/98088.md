inquisitively:/ɪnˈkwɪzɪtɪvli/| adv.|好奇地，探究地|Showing curiosity and a desire to learn or investigate.|The child inquisitively examined the strange object.  孩子好奇地检查着那个奇怪的物体。|She looked at him inquisitively. 她好奇地望着他。|curiously, inquiringly|uninterestedly, indifferently|副词

USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't have tenses like verbs do.  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.

Example sentences illustrating different contexts:

She listened inquisitively to the whispered conversation. 她好奇地听着窃窃私语。

He inquisitively poked at the smoldering embers. 他好奇地戳着余烬。

The detective inquisitively examined the crime scene.  侦探好奇地检查了犯罪现场。


The children inquisitively peered into the box. 孩子们好奇地往箱子里看。


He asked inquisitively about her family. 他好奇地询问了她的家人。
