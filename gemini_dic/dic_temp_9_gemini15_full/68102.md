faeces:/ˈfiːsiːz/| n.|粪便，排泄物|The word "faeces" refers to the waste matter discharged from the bowels; excrement.|例句：The farmer used the faeces as fertilizer.  农民用粪便作肥料。
例句：The dog left its faeces on the lawn. 狗在草坪上拉了屎。|近义词：excrement, dung, stool, manure|反义词：无|词性：名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense, voice, etc., are not applicable.  Its usage is straightforward as a singular or plural noun.


PHRASE->
animal faeces: 动物粪便
human faeces: 人类粪便
faeces analysis: 粪便分析
faecal matter: 粪便物质
removal of faeces: 排便


词源:

The word "faeces" originates from the Latin word "faex," meaning "dregs" or "sediment."  This reflects the nature of faeces as the residue remaining after digestion.  The plural form "faeces" is directly derived from the Latin plural.
