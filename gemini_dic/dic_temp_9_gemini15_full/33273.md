chrysogen:/kraɪˈsɒdʒən/| n. |产金菌；产金霉素菌|A bacterium, *Streptomyces chrysogenes*, that produces the antibiotic aureomycin (chlortetracycline).  It is a soil bacterium known for its ability to synthesize various antibiotics.|The chrysogen strain is crucial in the mass production of chlortetracycline.|近义词：aureomycin-producing bacterium|反义词：无
