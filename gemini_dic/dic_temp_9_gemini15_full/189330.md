safflower:/ˈsæfloʊər/| n.|红花，红花属植物|A safflower is an annual plant with orange-red flowers, cultivated for its oil and dye.  The oil is used in cooking and cosmetics, while the dye is used to color fabrics.|例句：Safflower oil is a healthy alternative to other cooking oils. 红花油是一种健康的食用油替代品。|例句：The vibrant colors of the safflower fields were breathtaking. 红花田里鲜艳的颜色令人叹为观止。|近义词：dyeplant, oil plant|反义词：无
