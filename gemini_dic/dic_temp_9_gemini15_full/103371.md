jingler:/ˈdʒɪŋɡlər/| n. |叮当声制造者；(广告)歌曲创作人；|A jingler is someone who creates short, catchy tunes, often for advertisements.  They might also be the person who makes something jingle, like small bells. |例句：The jingle writer created a catchy tune for the new soda commercial.  这首广告歌曲的创作人为新的苏打水广告创作了一首朗朗上口的曲子。|近义词：songwriter, composer, jingle writer |反义词: N/A
