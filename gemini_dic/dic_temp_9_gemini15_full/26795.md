caffeine:/ˈkæfiːn/ | n. | 咖啡因 | A bitter crystalline compound that is a central nervous system stimulant, found especially in coffee and tea plants. It is also added to some medications and soft drinks. | Example:  Coffee contains caffeine, which can keep you awake. 咖啡含有咖啡因，可以让你保持清醒。  Many energy drinks are loaded with caffeine. 许多能量饮料都含有大量的咖啡因。|近义词： stimulant |反义词：depressant |名词
