recordings:/rɪˈkɔːrdɪŋz/| n.|录音；记录|The act or process of recording sound or images; also, the resulting sound or images.  The plural form of "recording".|例句：The recordings of the concert were excellent.  音乐会的录音非常好。

例句：She made several recordings of her grandmother's stories. 她录制了几段祖母讲述故事的录音。


近义词：recordings, tapes, tracks, albums


反义词：live performance (反义词较宽泛，指现场演出而非录音)


词性:名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The band makes recordings regularly. = 这个乐队定期进行录音。
He often listens to recordings of classical music. = 他经常听古典音乐的录音。


一般过去时:
They made recordings of the meeting. = 他们录制了会议的录音。
She recorded her lectures and made recordings available online. = 她录下了她的讲座并把录音放在网上。


现在完成时:
The singer has made many recordings throughout her career. =这位歌手在她整个职业生涯中录制了许多唱片。
We have listened to all the recordings from the conference. = 我们已经听完了会议的所有录音。


过去完成时:
They had already made the recordings before the deadline. =他们在截止日期之前已经完成了录音。
She had finished all the recordings before the concert. =她在音乐会之前已经完成了所有的录音。


将来时:
The artist will make recordings of his new album next month. =这位艺术家下个月将录制他的新专辑。
We will make recordings of the interviews for the documentary. =我们将为纪录片录制采访录音。


虚拟语气:
If I had better equipment, I would make better recordings. =如果我有更好的设备，我会制作更好的录音。
I wish I had made recordings of my grandfather's stories. =我希望我曾经录下我祖父的故事。


被动语态:
The recordings were made in a professional studio. =这些录音是在专业的录音棚里制作的。
The recordings are being edited now. =录音正在编辑中。


疑问句:
Are there any recordings of the speech? = 有演讲的录音吗？
How many recordings did they produce last year? = 他们去年制作了多少录音？


否定句:
They didn't make any recordings of the performance. =他们没有录制这场演出的任何录音。
There were no recordings left of the old radio show. = 没有留下那个旧广播节目的任何录音。
