narrow-gage:/ˈnærəʊɡeɪdʒ/| adj.|窄轨的，狭轨的|Narrow-gage refers to a railway system using a track gauge narrower than the standard gauge (typically 1,435 mm or 4 ft 8 1⁄2 in). This narrower gauge is often used in mountainous or geographically challenging areas where construction costs and space are limited.|Example: The mountain railway used a narrow-gage track to navigate the steep inclines.  山区铁路采用窄轨轨道来应对陡峭的斜坡。|近义词：narrow-gauge, light railway|反义词：standard-gauge, broad-gauge
