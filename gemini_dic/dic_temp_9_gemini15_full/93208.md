ignoring:/ɪɡˈnɔːrɪŋ/ | v. |忽略；不理；忽视 |  Ignoring something means choosing not to pay attention to it or deal with it.  It implies a deliberate act of overlooking or disregarding. |例句：He was ignoring the warning signs. 他无视警告标志。

例句：She's ignoring his calls.  她在不接他的电话。


近义词：neglecting, disregarding, overlooking, disregarding, overlooking, dismissing


反义词：heeding, attending to, considering


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is ignoring my emails. 他正在忽略我的邮件。
They ignore the traffic rules. 他们无视交通规则。


一般过去时:
I ignored his advice. 我忽略了他的建议。
She ignored the phone ringing. 她无视电话铃声。


现在完成时:
He has been ignoring me for weeks. 他已经几周不理我了。
They have ignored all our requests. 他们无视了我们所有的请求。


过去完成时:
I had ignored the problem for too long. 我已经忽略这个问题太久了。
She had ignored the warning signs before the accident. 事故发生前，她一直无视警告标志。


将来时:
I will ignore him next time. 下次我将无视他。
They will ignore your complaints. 他们会无视你的抱怨。


虚拟语气:
If I ignored the problem, it would get worse. 如果我忽略这个问题，情况会更糟。
I wish I hadn't ignored her advice. 我真希望我没有忽略她的建议。


被动语态:
His warnings were ignored. 他的警告被忽略了。
The problem is being ignored. 这个问题正在被忽视。


疑问句:
Are you ignoring me? 你是不是在不理我？
Why are they ignoring the evidence? 他们为什么忽略证据？


否定句:
I didn't ignore her. 我没有忽略她。
He wasn't ignoring the facts. 他并没有忽视事实。


PHRASE->
ignoring the fact that... 忽略...这一事实

ignoring the warnings 忽略警告
