overinsuring:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈʃʊərɪŋ/| v.|过度保险|Purchasing insurance coverage that exceeds the actual value of an asset or the potential for loss.  This can be financially wasteful.|Example:  He overinsured his car, paying for coverage far exceeding its market value. 他为他的车投保了过高的保险，支付的保费远远超过了它的市场价值。|近义词：over-covering (in insurance context)|反义词：underinsuring


USAGE->

They overinsure their property every year. = 他们每年都为他们的房产购买过高的保险。
He overinsures his classic car. = 他为他的老爷车投保过高。
