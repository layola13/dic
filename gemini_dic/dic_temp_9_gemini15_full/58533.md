duffers:/ˈdʌfərz/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A duffer is a clumsy or incompetent person.  The word often implies a somewhat endearing clumsiness rather than outright malice or stupidity.|例句：He's a bit of a duffer when it comes to technology. 他在技术方面有点笨拙。|例句：Don't rely on him; he's a hopeless duffer.  别指望他；他是个无可救药的笨蛋。|近义词：fool, idiot, klutz, bungler|反义词：expert, professional, skilled, adept|名词
