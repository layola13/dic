semifloscule:/ˈsemiˌflɒskjuːl/ | n. |小花，半花 | A small or imperfect flower, especially one of the florets composing a composite flower head.  It typically lacks a fully developed corolla.  | Example: The daisy's head is composed of many tiny semifloscules.  雏菊的花头由许多细小的半花组成。 | 近义词：floret, small flower | 反义词：large flower, complete flower
