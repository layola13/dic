overmatch:/ˈoʊvərmætʃ/ | v. |技压；胜过；|To defeat or surpass someone or something completely.|The champion easily overmatched his opponent. 冠军轻松击败了他的对手。|近义词：outmatch, defeat, surpass |反义词：undermatch, be defeated by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overmatches his opponents in every game. = 他在每一场比赛中都技压对手。
The new software overmatches the old version in terms of functionality. = 新软件在功能方面胜过旧版本。


一般过去时:
She overmatched her rival in the debate. = 她在辩论中胜过她的对手。
The army overmatched the rebels in terms of firepower. = 在火力方面，军队胜过了叛军。


现在完成时:
He has overmatched every challenger he's faced. = 他已经击败了他遇到的每一个挑战者。
Technology has overmatched human capabilities in many areas. = 在许多领域，技术已经超越了人类的能力。


过去完成时:
They had overmatched their opponents before the final round. = 在决赛之前，他们就已经击败了对手。
The company had overmatched its competitors in the market before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退之前，该公司已经在市场上胜过了竞争对手。


将来时:
She will overmatch her opponent with her superior skills. = 她将凭借高超的技能击败对手。
The new strategy will overmatch the current market limitations. = 新战略将克服当前市场的局限性。


虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he might overmatch his rival. = 如果他训练更努力，他可能会击败他的对手。
If we had better resources, we could overmatch our competitors. = 如果我们有更好的资源，我们就能胜过竞争对手。


被动语态:
His opponent was easily overmatched. = 他的对手很容易就被击败了。
The old system was overmatched by the new technology. = 旧系统被新技术所超越。


疑问句:
Can he overmatch such a strong opponent? = 他能击败如此强大的对手吗？
Will the new product overmatch existing ones on the market? = 新产品会在市场上胜过现有产品吗？


否定句:
He didn't overmatch his opponent in the final match. = 他在决赛中没有击败对手。
The company failed to overmatch its competitors in the new market. = 该公司未能在新市场上胜过竞争对手。
