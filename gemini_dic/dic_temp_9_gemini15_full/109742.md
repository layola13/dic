leverlike: /ˈliːvərlaɪk/ | adj. | 杠杆似的；像杠杆的 | Resembling a lever in appearance, function, or action.  Something leverlike operates on the principle of a lever, using a small force to move a larger object. |

例句：

The mechanism had a leverlike action, multiplying the force applied to it.  该机构具有杠杆作用，放大了施加在其上的力。

The long, thin handle of the tool gave it a leverlike quality, making it easier to pry open the lid.  工具细长的把手使其具有杠杆般的特性，更容易撬开盖子。


近义词：lever-shaped,  fulcrum-like


反义词：Non-lever-like (this is a descriptive phrase rather than a single word)


词性:  形容词
