searched:/sɜːtʃt/| /sɝtʃt/| v. |搜索过；查找过|To have looked for something or someone.|The police searched the house for clues.  警察搜查了房子寻找线索。|They searched the database for information. 他们在数据库中搜索信息。|investigated, explored, examined|unsearched, unexplored|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I search the internet every day. = 我每天都搜索互联网。
He searches for new recipes online. = 他在网上搜索新的食谱。

一般过去时:
I searched for my keys this morning. = 我今天早上找我的钥匙。
She searched the room carefully. = 她仔细地搜查了房间。

现在完成时:
I have searched everywhere for my glasses. = 我到处都找过我的眼镜了。
They have searched the area thoroughly. = 他们已经彻底搜查了该地区。

过去完成时:
I had searched for hours before I found it. = 我找了几个小时才找到它。
She had searched the entire house before giving up. = 她搜查了整栋房子后才放弃。

将来时:
I will search for a new job next year. = 我明年将寻找一份新工作。
They will search the archives for the document. = 他们将搜索档案寻找该文件。

虚拟语气:
If I had searched harder, I would have found it sooner. = 如果我更努力地寻找，我就会早点找到它。
I wish I had searched the attic. = 我真希望我搜查过阁楼。

被动语态:
The house was searched by the police. = 这房子被警察搜查了。
The database is being searched for relevant information. = 正在搜索数据库以查找相关信息。

疑问句:
Have you searched your bag? = 你搜查过你的包了吗？
Did they search the car? = 他们搜查了汽车吗？

否定句:
I didn't search the internet today. = 我今天没有搜索互联网。
They haven't searched the entire building yet. = 他们还没有搜查整个建筑物。
