fishtailed:/ˈfɪʃteɪld/| v.| 鱼尾摆动；(车)甩尾 | To move or cause to move with a sideways swaying motion, like the tail of a fish.  This is often used to describe the movement of a vehicle losing traction. |例句：The car fishtailed on the icy road.  这辆车在结冰的路上甩尾了。 The dancer fishtailed across the stage. 舞者在舞台上鱼尾般地摆动。|近义词：skid, swerve, swing |反义词：drive straight, proceed steadily


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The car fishtails on every turn. = 这辆车每次转弯都甩尾。
He fishtails his way through the crowded street. = 他在拥挤的街道上灵活地穿梭。


一般过去时:
The bus fishtailed violently and almost overturned. = 公共汽车剧烈甩尾，差点翻车。
She fishtailed gracefully across the dance floor. = 她优雅地在舞池中鱼尾般地摆动。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I hadn't fishtailed; I almost hit that pedestrian. = 我真希望我没有甩尾；我差点撞到那个行人。


被动语态:
The car was fishtailed by the sudden gust of wind. = 汽车被突如其来的阵风吹得甩尾了。
The boat was skillfully fishtailed into the harbor. = 船被熟练地操纵着，鱼尾般地驶入港口。


疑问句:
Did the car fishtail before the accident? = 事故发生前汽车是否甩尾了？
Will the dancer fishtail during her performance? = 舞者在表演中会鱼尾般地摆动吗？


否定句:
The driver didn't fishtail, he just braked hard. = 司机没有甩尾，只是紧急刹车。
The skilled driver didn't fishtail even on the icy road. = 即使在结冰的路面上，经验丰富的司机也没有甩尾。

词源:

The word "fishtailed" comes from the resemblance of the sideways swaying motion to the movement of a fish's tail.  "Fishtail" as a noun describing this tail shape is older, and the verb form followed naturally.
