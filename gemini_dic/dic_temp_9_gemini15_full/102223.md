itching:/ˈɪtʃɪŋ/ | n. | 瘙痒；渴望 | A feeling of discomfort on the skin that causes a desire to scratch.  It can be caused by various factors, including allergies, insect bites, or dry skin.  It can also refer to a strong desire or yearning for something. |例句：The mosquito bite caused intense itching.  蚊子叮咬引起剧烈瘙痒。

The itching to travel was almost unbearable.  渴望旅行的欲望几乎难以忍受。|近义词：irritation, tingling, longing |反义词：numbness, satisfaction


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The rash is itching constantly. = 疹子一直在痒。
My skin itches all over. = 我的皮肤到处都痒。


一般过去时:
The wool sweater made my neck itch last night. = 昨晚羊毛衫让我的脖子痒。
My skin itched terribly after the hike. = 徒步旅行后我的皮肤奇痒无比。


现在完成时:
I have been itching to read that book for weeks. = 我几周来一直渴望阅读那本书。
She has been itching to tell him the news. = 她一直渴望告诉他这个消息。


过去完成时:
I had been itching to see the movie before it left theaters. = 在电影下映之前，我一直渴望去看。
He had been itching to quit his job for months. = 他几个月来一直渴望辞职。


将来时:
I will be itching to get back to work after my vacation. = 假期结束后，我会渴望重返工作岗位。
We will be itching to know the results of the test. = 我们将渴望知道测试的结果。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have this rash, I wouldn't be itching so much. = 如果我没有这种皮疹，我就不会这么痒了。
If I were you, I would scratch that itch. = 如果我是你，我会抓抓那个痒。


被动语态:
The skin is often itched by insect bites. = 皮肤经常被昆虫叮咬而发痒。
His skin was itched by the rough fabric. = 粗糙的布料使他的皮肤发痒。


疑问句:
Is your skin itching? = 你的皮肤痒吗？
Does that mosquito bite still itch? = 那个蚊子叮咬的地方还痒吗？


否定句:
My skin isn't itching anymore. = 我的皮肤不再痒了。
The cream stopped my skin from itching. = 这种药膏阻止了我的皮肤发痒。
