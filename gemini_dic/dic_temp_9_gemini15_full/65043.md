etched:/ɛtʃt/ | v. |蚀刻；铭刻；深深地印在脑海里 |To cut or carve a design, pattern, or words into a surface.  It can also mean to be deeply impressed or remembered.|例句：The artist etched a beautiful design into the glass.  艺术家在玻璃上蚀刻了一个美丽的图案。

The memory of that day is etched in my mind. 那一天的记忆深深地印在我的脑海里。|近义词：carve, engrave, inscribe |反义词：erase, obliterate |动词
