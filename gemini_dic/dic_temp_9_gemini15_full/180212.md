refounded:/ˌriːˈfaʊndɪd/ | vt. |重建；重新创立；重新奠定基础 | To establish or create (something) again after it has been destroyed, abolished, or ended.  It implies a significant restructuring or rebuilding, going beyond simple repair or restoration. |例句：The company was refounded on new principles after the scandal.  公司在丑闻后基于新的原则重新建立。

The university was refounded in 1990 with a new focus on technology. 这所大学在1990年以新的科技重点而重建。|近义词：re-establish, rebuild, reorganize, reinstate |反义词：destroy, abolish, dismantle |动词
