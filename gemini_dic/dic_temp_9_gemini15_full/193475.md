selectively:/səˈlɛktɪvli/ | adv. | 选择地；选择性地 |  Choosing or done by choosing carefully from a number of possibilities.  It implies a careful and deliberate process of selection, not random or haphazard. |例句：The company selectively hired only the most qualified candidates.  这家公司选择性地只招聘最合格的候选人。

例句：The government selectively targeted its aid to the most vulnerable communities. 政府有选择性地将其援助目标定为最弱势群体。


近义词：discriminately, discerningly, particularly, specifically


反义词：indiscriminately, randomly, haphazardly


词性:副词
