mutated:/ˈmjuːteɪtɪd/ | adj. | 突变的；变异的 |  Having undergone mutation; changed in form or nature.  | Example: The mutated gene caused the disease.  突变的基因导致了这种疾病。  The mutated virus was highly contagious.  突变的病毒具有高度传染性。|近义词：altered, changed, modified, transformed | 反义词：unchanged, original, normal | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The virus is mutated. = 病毒发生了突变。
The bacteria are mutated. = 细菌发生了突变。

一般过去时:
The gene mutated. = 基因发生了突变。
The plant mutated after exposure to radiation. = 植物在接触辐射后发生了突变。

现在完成时:
The virus has mutated significantly. = 病毒已经发生了显著的突变。
The species has mutated over millions of years. = 这种物种经过数百万年的进化发生了突变。

过去完成时:
The scientist found that the gene had mutated before the experiment. = 科学家发现基因在实验前就已经发生了突变。
The plant had mutated due to the long-term exposure to chemicals. = 由于长期接触化学物质，植物已经发生了突变。

将来时:
The virus will likely mutate further. = 病毒很可能会进一步突变。
Scientists predict that the bacteria will mutate in response to the new antibiotic. = 科学家预测细菌会对新的抗生素产生反应而发生突变。


虚拟语气:
If the gene had mutated differently, the result would have been different. = 如果基因的突变方式不同，结果也会不同。
It is important that the virus not mutate further. = 重要的是病毒不要进一步突变。


被动语态:
The bacteria were mutated by the researcher. = 细菌被研究人员进行了基因突变。
The gene was found to be mutated. = 基因被发现发生了突变。


疑问句:
Has the virus mutated? = 病毒发生了突变吗？
Have the bacteria mutated significantly? = 细菌发生了显著的突变吗？


否定句:
The gene has not mutated. = 基因没有发生突变。
The virus did not mutate in the experiment. = 病毒在实验中没有发生突变。


PHRASE->
mutated gene  突变基因
mutated protein 突变蛋白
mutated strain 突变菌株
highly mutated 高度突变的
genetically mutated 基因突变的

词源:
From the verb "mutate," ultimately from Latin *mutare "to change."  The suffix "-ed" indicates the past participle, describing something that has undergone the process of mutation.
