ensnares:/ɪnˈsneərz/| vt.|诱捕；使...落入圈套|To trap or catch someone in a snare or in a difficult situation.|The spider ensnared the fly in its web. 蜘蛛用网诱捕了苍蝇。
The clever marketing campaign ensnared many unsuspecting consumers.  巧妙的营销活动诱捕了许多毫无戒心的消费者。|entrap, trap, snare|free, liberate|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The hunter ensnares rabbits in the forest. = 猎人诱捕森林里的兔子。
He often ensnares his friends with his witty jokes. = 他经常用他风趣的玩笑诱捕他的朋友。

一般过去时:
The police ensnared the thief. = 警察诱捕了小偷。
She was ensnared by his charm. = 她被他迷人的魅力所吸引。

现在完成时:
The company has ensnared a large market share. = 这家公司已经占据了很大的市场份额。
They have ensnared themselves in a complex legal battle. = 他们陷入了一场复杂的法律纠纷。

过去完成时:
He had ensnared the bird before it could escape. = 他在鸟儿逃脱之前已经诱捕到了它。
The criminal had ensnared many victims before his arrest. = 罪犯在被捕之前已经诱捕了许多受害者。

将来时:

I believe this plan will ensnare them into our agreement. = 我相信这个计划会让他们接受我们的协议。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

The criminal was finally ensnared by the police. = 罪犯最终被警方逮捕。
