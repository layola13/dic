/ˈpɒtɪnɪs/ | n. |傻气，愚蠢；笨拙；醉酒；陶器；(尤指)劣质陶器 |  A state of being silly, foolish, or clumsy;  intoxication; pottery, especially of poor quality. |例句：His pottiness was evident in his clumsy attempts to fix the vase.他的笨手笨脚在试图修理花瓶时显而易见。

例句：The pottiness of the cheap pottery was apparent in its uneven glaze.廉价陶器的粗糙在它不均匀的釉面上显而易见。


近义词：silliness, foolishness, clumsiness, inebriation, drunkenness

反义词：cleverness, intelligence, skill, sobriety


词源:  Derived from "pot," referring to a drinking vessel, implying intoxication. The sense of silliness likely developed from the association of drunkenness with foolish behavior.  The pottery meaning is a direct extension of the original meaning of "pot".
