reviving:/rɪˈvaɪvɪŋ/| v.|使复苏；使复活；使重新流行；复兴|The act of bringing something back to life, consciousness, or activity;  to restore to life or consciousness; to renew or refresh; to bring back into use or fashion.|例句：The doctor is reviving the patient. 医生正在抢救病人。

例句：The old tradition is being revived.  古老的传统正在复兴。


近义词：reanimate, resuscitate, revitalize, regenerate, resurrect|反义词：kill, destroy, extinguish, terminate|动词
