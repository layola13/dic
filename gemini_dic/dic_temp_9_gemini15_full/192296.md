screwed:/skruːd/| adj. v. n.| 拧紧的；被欺骗的；喝醉的；损坏的；|

英文解析:As an adjective, "screwed" means fastened or tightened with a screw. As a verb, it means to fasten or tighten with a screw, or to cheat or exploit someone. It can also be used informally to mean drunk or in a difficult situation. As a noun, it's a less common usage, usually referring to a screw itself or a difficult situation.

例句:
He screwed the lid onto the jar. 他拧紧了罐子的盖子。

The company screwed its employees out of their bonuses. 公司欺骗员工，克扣了他们的奖金。


近义词: (depending on context) fastened, cheated, drunk, ruined, damaged, in trouble, screwed up

反义词: (depending on context) loosened, honest, sober, undamaged, repaired, fortunate, fine


词性: adjective, verb, noun
