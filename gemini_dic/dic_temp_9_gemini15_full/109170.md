legitimatize:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪz/ | vt. | 使合法化；使正当化 | To make something legitimate or acceptable.  To give something a legal basis or justification. | 例句：The government tried to legitimize its actions by claiming they were necessary for national security. 政府试图通过声称其行动对国家安全必要来使其合法化。 | 例句：The new law legitimized same-sex marriage. 新法律使同性婚姻合法化。 | 近义词：authorize, validate, justify, sanction | 反义词：invalidate, discredit, condemn, outlaw | 动词
