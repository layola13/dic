outriggers:/ˈaʊtˌrɪɡərz/| n.|平衡木，舷外支架|Extensions that project outward from a vessel's hull to provide stability, often used in canoes and boats.|例句：The outriggers kept the canoe stable in the rough water.  舷外支架使独木舟在汹涌的水中保持稳定。The outriggers on the sailing boat provided extra support. 帆船上的舷外支架提供了额外的支撑。|近义词：stabilizers, braces|反义词：none
