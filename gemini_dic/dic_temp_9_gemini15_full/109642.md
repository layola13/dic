lettuce-like: /ˈlɛtɪsˌlaɪk/ | adj. |像莴苣的，莴苣似的| Resembling lettuce in appearance or texture.  The term usually refers to the leaf structure, being loose, leafy, and somewhat fragile.  It can also imply a light green color. |例句：The plant had lettuce-like leaves, soft and easily bruised. 这株植物的叶子像莴苣一样，柔软易碎。

The delicate, lettuce-like leaves of the baby spinach made a beautiful garnish.  嫩嫩的，像莴苣一样的嫩菠菜叶做成美丽的装饰。|近义词：leafy, verdant (referring to color), delicate|反义词：coarse, sturdy, robust
