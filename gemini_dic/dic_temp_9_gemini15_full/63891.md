epimeron:/ɛpɪˈmɪrɒn/| n. |侧板；(昆虫的)侧胸片|A lateral part of the insect thorax, lying between the notum and the pleuron.  It is often small and inconspicuous, and its function is not fully understood.  It is believed to play a role in muscle attachment and wing articulation.|The epimeron is a small sclerite on the insect thorax.|近义词:pleuron (though distinct)|反义词:notum (anatomically opposite)|名词
