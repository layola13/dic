chelone:/ˈkɛləʊn/ | n. | 龟；箱龟属；（希腊神话中的）龟神 | A chelone is a type of turtle, specifically referring to the genus Chelone and sometimes used more broadly to refer to other turtles. In Greek mythology, Chelone was a sea nymph transformed into a turtle. | Example:  The Galapagos tortoise is a large species of chelone. 加拉帕戈斯象龟是一种大型的龟。  |近义词：turtle, tortoise | 反义词：无
