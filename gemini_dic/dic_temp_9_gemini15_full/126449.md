mystify:/ˈmɪstɪfaɪ/ | vt. |使迷惑；使困惑；使神秘化 | to confuse or bewilder someone; to make something seem mysterious |例句：The magician's tricks mystified the audience. 魔术师的戏法迷惑了观众。

例句：The complexity of the instructions mystified me.  这些复杂的指令让我困惑不解。


近义词：confuse, bewilder, puzzle, perplex, baffle |反义词：clarify, enlighten, explain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The magician mystifies the audience with his tricks. = 魔术师用他的戏法迷惑观众。
His strange behavior mystifies everyone. = 他古怪的行为使每个人都感到困惑。


一般过去时:
The riddle mystified the children. = 这个谜语难倒了孩子们。
The sudden disappearance mystified the police. = 突然的消失让警方感到困惑。


现在完成时:
The scientists have mystified the public with their latest findings. = 科学家们用他们最新的发现迷惑了公众。
His cryptic message has mystified everyone who received it. = 他神秘的信息迷惑了所有收到它的人。


过去完成时:
The ancient writings had mystified scholars for centuries. = 古代文献几个世纪以来一直让学者们困惑不解。
The clues had mystified the detective until he found the hidden message. = 线索让侦探困惑不解，直到他找到隐藏的信息。


将来时:
The new technology will mystify many people at first. = 新技术起初会让许多人感到困惑。
The reveal of the plot twist will mystify the audience. = 情节的转折将会迷惑观众。


虚拟语气:
If the instructions were clearer, they wouldn't mystify me. = 如果说明更清晰，它们就不会让我困惑。
I wish the instructions didn't mystify me so much. = 我真希望这些说明不会让我这么困惑。


被动语态:
The audience was mystified by the magician's performance. = 观众被魔术师的表演迷惑了。
The public is mystified by the government's actions. = 公众对政府的行动感到困惑。


疑问句:
What mystifies you about this problem? = 你对这个问题什么感到困惑？
Does the ending of the movie mystify you? = 电影的结局让你困惑吗？


否定句:
The explanation didn't mystify me at all. = 这个解释一点也没有让我困惑。
The instructions didn't mystify her; she understood them perfectly. = 说明没有让她困惑；她完全理解了。
