couveuse:/kuˈvəz/| n. |孵卵器；保育箱|A couveuse is a device used to incubate eggs or to provide a controlled environment for premature or sick infants.  It maintains a stable temperature, humidity, and sometimes oxygen levels.|例句：The premature baby was placed in a couveuse to help it survive.|近义词：incubator|反义词:无
