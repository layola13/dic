musky:/ˈmʌski/ | adj. |麝香的；有麝香味的；强烈的；令人作呕的 | Having a strong, often unpleasant smell, similar to that of musk.  The word evokes the scent of musk, a substance with a powerful and distinctive odor obtained from the musk deer.  It can be used to describe both pleasant and unpleasant scents, depending on context.  |例句：The room had a musky odor, a blend of old books and damp earth.  房间里有一种麝香味，混合着旧书和潮湿泥土的气味。  The perfume had a musky undertone. 这款香水有一种麝香的底香。|近义词：pungent, fragrant (if pleasant), fetid (if unpleasant), cloying (if overly sweet and unpleasant) |反义词：fragrant (if used to describe a pleasant scent), sweet, delicate |词性：形容词
