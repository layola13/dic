integer:/ˈɪntɪdʒər/| n. |整数|A whole number; a number that is not a fraction.| 例句：The integers from 1 to 10 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10.  从1到10的整数是1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9和10。


例句：Negative integers are less than zero. 负整数小于零。

近义词：whole number, whole, entire

反义词：fraction, decimal


词源:  From Latin *integer, literally "untouched," "whole," from in- "not" + tangere "to touch".  The mathematical sense developed in the 16th century.
