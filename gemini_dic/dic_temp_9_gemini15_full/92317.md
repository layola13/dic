hypnotised: /ˈhɪpnətaɪzd/ | v. |催眠的；着迷的 |  To put (someone) into a hypnotic state; to fascinate or enthrall someone completely. |例句：The magician hypnotised the volunteer on stage.  魔术师把舞台上的志愿者催眠了。


The audience was hypnotised by her performance. 观众被她的表演迷住了。|近义词：mesmerized, captivated, enthralled |反义词：awakened, bored, uninterested |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hypnotises patients to help them relax. 他用催眠疗法帮助病人放松。
She hypnotises birds with her flute playing. 她用长笛演奏催眠鸟类。


一般过去时:
The therapist hypnotised the patient before the session. 治疗师在治疗前催眠了病人。
The magician hypnotised his audience with a simple trick. 魔术师用一个简单的戏法催眠了他的观众。


现在完成时:
I have hypnotised many people successfully. 我已经成功地催眠了许多人。
She has never been hypnotised before. 她以前从未被催眠过。


过去完成时:
He had hypnotised the subject before conducting the experiment. 他在进行实验之前已经催眠了受试者。
They had hypnotised her several times in the past. 他们过去曾多次催眠她。


将来时:
The doctor will hypnotise the patient to alleviate pain. 医生将催眠病人以减轻疼痛。
I will try to hypnotise myself to sleep tonight. 我今晚将尝试自我催眠入睡。


虚拟语气:
If I could hypnotise people, I would help them overcome their fears. 如果我能催眠人，我会帮助他们克服恐惧。
I wish I could hypnotise myself to stop smoking. 我希望我能自我催眠戒烟。


被动语态:
The patient was hypnotised by the therapist. 病人被治疗师催眠了。
The volunteer was hypnotised and then asked questions. 志愿者被催眠后被问了一些问题。


疑问句:
Was she hypnotised during the performance? 她在表演中被催眠了吗？
Have you ever been hypnotised? 你曾经被催眠过吗？


否定句:
He could not hypnotise the subject. 他无法催眠受试者。
I did not hypnotise him; he fell asleep naturally. 我没有催眠他；他自然睡着了。


PHRASE->
hypnotised state 催眠状态
deeply hypnotised 深度催眠
easily hypnotised 易受催眠
hypnotised by  被…催眠
under hypnosis  在催眠状态下

词源:
From  "hypnosis" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Hypnosis" itself comes from the Greek word "hypnos" (ὕπνος), meaning "sleep".
