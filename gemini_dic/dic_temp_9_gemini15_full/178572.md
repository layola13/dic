recordkeeping:/ˈrɛkərdˌkiːpɪŋ/| n. |记录保存；簿记|The systematic process of recording and maintaining information.  This includes creating, storing, retrieving, and disposing of records in a way that ensures their integrity, authenticity, and usability.  It's crucial for compliance, accountability, and decision-making.|例句：Good recordkeeping is essential for any successful business. 良好的记录保存对于任何成功的企业都至关重要。

例句：The company's poor recordkeeping led to significant financial losses. 该公司的糟糕记录保存导致了巨大的经济损失。


近义词：record management, documentation, archiving|反义词：negligence, carelessness|词性：名词
