riddlers: /ˈrɪd.lərz/ | n. |谜语者，喜欢出谜语的人|  A person who enjoys making or posing riddles. They are often clever and enjoy testing others' intelligence.  |Example: The riddlers gathered around the fire, each eager to share their latest brain teaser.|近义词:enigmatists, puzzle-makers|反义词:answerers (in the context of riddles)
