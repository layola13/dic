moderately:/ˈmɒdərətli/ /ˈmɑːdərətli/| adj. |适度地；中等地；相当地| Moderately means to a moderate degree; not excessively or insufficiently.  It indicates a level that is neither extreme nor minimal. |例句：He was moderately successful in his business. 他在生意上取得了中等程度的成功。

例句：The weather was moderately warm. 天气相当温暖。


近义词：reasonably, fairly, tolerably, somewhat, passably |反义词：extremely, excessively, intensely, minimally, slightly


词性：形容词
