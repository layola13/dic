laid-over: /ˌleɪdˈoʊvər/ | adj. | 飞机中途停留的；（指人）在中途停留的 |  Describes a flight with a stop between the origin and destination, or a person who is making such a stop.  | 例句：We had a three-hour laid-over in Chicago. = 我们在芝加哥有一个三个小时的飞机中途停留。  I spent the laid-over reading a book. = 我在中途停留期间读了一本书。| 近义词：connecting, stopover | 反义词：direct, nonstop |  形容词
