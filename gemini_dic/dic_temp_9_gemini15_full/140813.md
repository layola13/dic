on his part:/ˌɒn hɪz pɑːt/ | /ɑːn hɪz pɑːrt/ | adv. |就他而言；对他来说；就他那一方面而言 | This phrase indicates that the following action, statement, or quality is attributable to a specific person.  It emphasizes the perspective or contribution of that individual. | Example: On his part, he offered a sincere apology. 就他而言，他道了真诚的歉意。  On his part, the project was a complete success.  就他而言，这个项目非常成功。 |近义词：as for him, from his perspective, in his view | 反义词：N/A | 副词
