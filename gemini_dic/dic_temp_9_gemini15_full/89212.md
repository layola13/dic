homesteaders:/ˈhoʊmstɛdərz/ | n. | 拓荒者；自耕农 | People who settle on and cultivate previously unoccupied land, often with the intention of claiming ownership.  |The homesteaders built their cabin on the prairie. 拓荒者在草原上建起了他们的木屋。  Many homesteaders struggled with harsh conditions. 许多拓荒者都在与恶劣的环境作斗争。|pioneers, settlers | city dwellers, urbanites | 名词
