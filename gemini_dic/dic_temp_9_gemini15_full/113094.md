lunched:/lʌntʃt/ | v. | 用午餐；吃午饭 | To eat lunch.  |例句：We lunched at a small cafe near the park. 我们在公园附近的一家小咖啡馆吃了午饭。|近义词：had lunch, ate lunch |反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They lunch at noon every day. = 他们每天中午吃午饭。
We usually lunch together on Fridays. = 我们通常星期五一起吃午饭。

一般过去时:
I lunched with my client yesterday. = 我昨天和我的客户一起吃了午饭。
She lunched quickly before her meeting. = 她会议前快速地吃了午饭。

现在完成时:
He has lunched already. = 他已经吃过午饭了。
They haven't lunched yet. = 他们还没吃午饭。

过去完成时:
We had lunched before the movie started. = 电影开始前我们已经吃过午饭了。
She had lunched early that day. = 那天她吃午饭吃得很早。

将来时:
I will lunch with my family tomorrow. = 我明天将和家人一起吃午饭。
We will be lunching at a fancy restaurant next week. = 我们下周将在一家高档餐厅吃午饭。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would lunch with you. = 如果我有时间，我会和你一起吃午饭。
I wish I had lunched earlier. = 我真希望我早点吃午饭。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice)
Lunch was being prepared when they arrived. = 他们到达时午餐正在准备中。(强调午餐的准备过程，而非“吃”的动作)
The meeting was lunched by the committee. (This is unnatural and grammatically incorrect)


疑问句:
Did you lunch at the office today? = 你今天在办公室吃午饭了吗？
Where will you be lunching tomorrow? = 你明天将在哪里吃午饭？

否定句:
I didn't lunch today. = 我今天没吃午饭。
They won't be lunching with us. = 他们不会和我们一起吃午饭。
