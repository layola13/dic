noninsured:/ˌnɒnɪnˈʃʊəd/ | adj. | 未投保的；无保险的 | Not covered by insurance.  This term refers to individuals or entities lacking insurance coverage for a specific risk or generally. | Example:  The noninsured driver was responsible for the accident costs.  未投保的司机要对事故造成的损失负责。  The noninsured patient had to pay the full medical bill. 未投保的病人必须支付全部医疗费用。|近义词：uninsured, uncovered | 反义词：insured, covered
