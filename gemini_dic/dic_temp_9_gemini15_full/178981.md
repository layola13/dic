redeemed:/rɪˈdiːmd/ | vt. |赎回；救赎；兑换；履行；挽回 | to buy back something that you have previously sold; to save someone or something from a bad situation; to exchange something such as a voucher for goods or services; to fulfill a promise or expectation; to improve a bad situation | 例句：He redeemed his promise by paying back the money. 他通过还钱履行了诺言。


例句：The company redeemed all the outstanding shares. 公司赎回了所有未偿还的股份。


近义词：ransom, atone, recover, reclaim, fulfill, salvage


反义词： forfeit, lose, condemn, damn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redeems his mistakes by working harder. = 他通过更努力的工作来弥补自己的错误。
She redeems her coupons for groceries every week. = 她每周都用优惠券兑换杂货。

一般过去时:
He redeemed his lost reputation through hard work. = 他通过努力工作挽回了他失去的名声。
They redeemed their pledge to donate to charity. = 他们兑现了向慈善机构捐款的承诺。

现在完成时:
She has redeemed herself from past failures. = 她已经从过去的失败中重新振作起来。
He has redeemed his soul through acts of kindness. = 他通过善举救赎了自己的灵魂。

过去完成时:
He had redeemed his debt before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前偿还了债务。
They had redeemed their promise to help us. = 他们已经兑现了帮助我们的承诺。

将来时:
She will redeem the gift certificate at the store. = 她将在商店兑换礼品券。
He will redeem his losses by investing wisely. = 他将通过明智的投资来弥补损失。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would redeem my shares. = 如果我有更多钱，我会赎回我的股票。
If he had apologized, he might have redeemed his image. = 如果他道歉了，他也许就能挽回自己的形象。

被动语态:
The shares were redeemed by the company last year. = 这些股份去年被公司赎回了。
His promise was redeemed after many years. = 他的诺言在许多年后才得以实现。

疑问句:
Can I redeem these points for a free flight? = 我可以用这些积分兑换免费机票吗？
Will the company redeem the bonds early? = 公司会提前赎回债券吗？

否定句:
He failed to redeem himself from the scandal. = 他未能从丑闻中挽回自己的名誉。
The company did not redeem the bonds as promised. = 公司没有按照承诺赎回债券。
