redemption:/rɪˈdɛmpʃən/| n. |救赎；赎回；偿还；履行；兑现|The act of saving or being saved from sin, error, or evil.  The act of redeeming or being redeemed. |例句：He found redemption in helping others. 他在帮助他人中找到了救赎。

例句：The company offered redemption of its bonds. 公司提供了债券的赎回。


近义词：salvation, deliverance, release, recovery, atonement |反义词：damnation, condemnation, ruin, destruction |词性: 名词
