parboil:/ˈpɑːrbɔɪl/| vt.|  半煮；烫；略煮|To boil (food) partially or briefly, typically as a preliminary step before further cooking.|The potatoes were parboiled before being roasted.|近义词：simmer, blanch|反义词：roast, fry


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She parboils the vegetables before adding them to the soup. = 她先将蔬菜略煮一下，然后再放入汤中。
The chef parboils the pasta to ensure it cooks evenly. = 厨师将面食略煮一下，以确保其均匀煮熟。

一般过去时:
I parboiled the carrots until they were slightly tender. = 我把胡萝卜略煮至略微变软。
He parboiled the rice before frying it with the vegetables. = 他先把米饭略煮一下，然后再与蔬菜一起翻炒。

现在完成时:
She has parboiled the potatoes for the salad. = 她已经把土豆略煮了，准备做沙拉。
They have parboiled the green beans, preparing them for the main course. = 他们已经把青豆略煮了，为正餐做准备。

过去完成时:
The cook had parboiled the fish before grilling it. = 厨师在烤鱼之前已经把鱼略煮过了。
Before adding the pasta, she had parboiled it until al dente. = 在加入意面之前，她已经将其略煮至 al dente 状态。

将来时:
I will parboil the broccoli before adding it to the stir-fry. = 我将先把西兰花略煮一下，然后再加入炒菜中。
They will parboil the corn on the cob before grilling it. = 他们会在烤玉米棒之前先将其略煮一下。


被动语态:
The vegetables were parboiled for the soup. = 蔬菜被略煮过，准备做汤。
The pasta was parboiled before being added to the sauce. = 面食被略煮过，然后再加入酱汁中。


疑问句:
Were the potatoes parboiled before they were mashed? = 土豆在捣碎之前是否被略煮过？
Have you parboiled the rice yet? = 你已经把米饭略煮了吗？


否定句:
I didn't parboil the asparagus; I just steamed it. = 我没有把芦笋略煮，我只是蒸了一下。
She hadn't parboiled the chicken before baking it. = 她并没有在烤鸡肉之前先略煮一下。
