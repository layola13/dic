misjudged:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒd/| v.|误判，错估，曲解 | To make a wrong judgment about someone or something; to assess incorrectly.  |例句：I completely misjudged the situation. 我完全误判了形势。

例句：He misjudged the distance and crashed into the wall. 他判断距离失误，撞到了墙上。


近义词：miscalculate, misinterpret, underestimate, overestimate

反义词：judge correctly, assess accurately


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misjudges people easily. = 他很容易误判别人。
They misjudge the market trends regularly. = 他们经常误判市场趋势。


一般过去时:
I misjudged his intentions. = 我误判了他的意图。
The jury misjudged the evidence. = 陪审团误判了证据。


现在完成时:
She has misjudged the situation several times. = 她已经好几次误判了局势。
We have misjudged the impact of our actions. = 我们误判了我们行为的影响。


过去完成时:
They had misjudged the strength of the opposition. = 他们之前就低估了对手的实力。
I realized I had misjudged her character. = 我意识到我误判了她的性格。


将来时:
He will misjudge the risks involved. = 他将会误判所涉及的风险。
They will likely misjudge the outcome of the election. = 他们很可能会误判选举的结果。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't misjudged him, things would have been different. = 如果我没有误判他，情况就会不一样了。
I wish I hadn't misjudged her capabilities. = 我真希望我没有误判她的能力。


被动语态:
The situation was misjudged by the authorities. = 当局误判了形势。
His abilities were misjudged by his employer. = 他的能力被他的老板误判了。


疑问句:
Did I misjudge his reaction? = 我是不是误判了他的反应？
Could we have misjudged the potential profits? = 我们会不会误判了潜在的利润？


否定句:
I didn't misjudge her at all. = 我根本没有误判她。
They didn't misjudge the severity of the problem. = 他们没有误判问题的严重性。


PHRASE->

词源:
mis- (错误地) + judge (判断)
