recradle:/riːˈkreɪdl/ | vt. |重新放入摇篮；重新安置；重新开始 |To put (something) back into a cradle or a similar supportive structure; to restart or reinstate something.|The baby fussed, so the mother gently recradled her.  婴儿烦躁不安，于是母亲轻轻地把她重新放回摇篮。|He recradled the project after the initial setback.  在最初的挫折后，他重新启动了这个项目。|restart, reinstate, resume|end, finish, abandon|动词
