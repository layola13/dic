peace-maker:/ˈpiːsˌmeɪkər/| n. | 和事佬；调解人 | A peacemaker is a person who tries to stop conflicts and bring about peace between people or groups.  | Example:  The mayor acted as a peacemaker in the dispute between the two neighborhood groups. 市长在两个邻里群体之间的争端中充当了和事佬的角色。 | 近义词：mediator, arbitrator, conciliator | 反义词：troublemaker, agitator
