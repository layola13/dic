shorthanded:/ˈʃɔːrtˌhændɪd/ | adj. |人手不足的，缺少员工的 |Having too few people to do the work.  It implies a temporary lack of staff, often due to illness, leave, or unexpected workload. |例句：The office is shorthanded this week because several employees are on vacation. 这周办公室人手不足，因为好几个员工在休假。

例句：We're shorthanded in the kitchen tonight, so service might be a little slower. 今晚厨房人手不足，所以服务可能会慢一点。


近义词：understaffed, short-staffed, undermanned


反义词：overstaffed, fully staffed


词性:形容词
