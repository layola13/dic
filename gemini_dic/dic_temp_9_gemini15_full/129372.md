nomadics:/nəˈmædɪks/| adj.|游牧的；流浪的|Relating to or characteristic of nomads; wandering from place to place.  The word describes a lifestyle characterized by frequent movement and a lack of permanent settlement, often associated with pastoralism (raising livestock).|例句：The nomadic tribes migrated seasonally following the available pasture. 游牧部落随着可用的牧场季节性迁移。

例句：Their nomadic lifestyle allowed them to experience diverse cultures and environments.  他们的游牧生活方式使他们能够体验不同的文化和环境。


近义词：wandering, itinerant, migratory, roving

反义词：settled, sedentary, stationary


词性:形容词
