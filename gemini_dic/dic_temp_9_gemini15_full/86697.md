hells:/hɛlz/ | interj. |见鬼，该死|An exclamation expressing anger, frustration, or disbelief.  It is a more intense and vulgar version of "hell".|例句：Hells bells, I've lost my keys!  该死，我丢了钥匙！
例句：Hells, what a mess!  该死，真是一团糟！|近义词：damn, darn, shit|反义词:  None (it's an exclamation, not a descriptive word)|词性:感叹词


USAGE->
(Hells is an interjection and doesn't conjugate like a verb.)  The grammatical structures shown below for "what" are not applicable to "hells."


PHRASE->
hells bells = 该死，天哪
what the hells = 到底是怎么回事
like hell = 绝对不
hell of a = 非常，极度的
hellbent = 坚决的，一心一意的
