gradino:/ɡrəˈdiːnoʊ/| n. |台阶；梯级|A gradino is a single step or stair.  It is often used in the context of architecture or landscaping, referring to a single step in a series of steps forming a staircase or a terraced slope. |例句：The sculptor carefully carved each gradino of the monumental staircase.  雕塑家仔细地雕刻着纪念性楼梯的每一个台阶。

The ancient Roman amphitheater was built with many gradini. 古罗马圆形剧场是用许多台阶建造的。|近义词：step, stair, riser|反义词：level, flat
