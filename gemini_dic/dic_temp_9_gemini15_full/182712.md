repainter:/ˌriːˈpeɪntər/ | n. | 重漆工，重新油漆的人 | A person whose job is to repaint things.  | Example: The repainter arrived promptly and began work on the house.  重漆工准时到达并开始粉刷房子。 |近义词: painter, decorator | 反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑 "person who doesn't paint") | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The repainter paints the walls every day. = 重漆工每天粉刷墙壁。
He repaints houses for a living. = 他靠粉刷房屋为生。

一般过去时:
The repainter painted the entire house last week. = 重漆工上周粉刷了整栋房子。
She repainted her bedroom a bright yellow. = 她把她的卧室重新粉刷成了亮黄色。

现在完成时:
The repainter has repainted several houses this month. = 这个月重漆工已经粉刷了几栋房子。

过去完成时:

将来时:
The repainter will repaint the trim tomorrow. = 重漆工明天将重新粉刷装饰条。
