quencher:/ˈkwɛntʃər/ | n. | 抑制剂；解渴剂；消音器 | A quencher is something that reduces or stops the effect of something else.  This can refer to a substance that reduces or eliminates fluorescence or phosphorescence, a drink that quenches thirst, or a device that reduces noise. | Example:  The scientist used a fluorescence quencher to study protein interactions.  科学家使用荧光猝灭剂来研究蛋白质相互作用。  |近义词：suppressor, inhibitor, damper |反义词：stimulant, enhancer
