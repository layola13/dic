fast-forward:/ˈfæstˈfɔːrwərd/ | vt. |快进；快速推进；提前 | To move quickly to a later point in time or a later stage in a process.  |例句：Let's fast-forward to the end of the movie.让我们快进到电影结尾。
例句：The company fast-forwarded its expansion plans after a successful first quarter. 公司在第一季度成功后加快了扩张计划。|近义词：advance, accelerate, expedite |反义词：rewind, regress, delay |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fast-forwards through boring parts of the movie. = 他快进电影中无聊的部分。
They fast-forward their careers by taking on challenging projects. = 他们通过承担具有挑战性的项目来快速发展自己的职业生涯。


一般过去时:
I fast-forwarded through the commercials. = 我快进了广告。
She fast-forwarded the conversation to the important details. = 她快进到谈话的重要细节部分。


现在完成时:
We have fast-forwarded the project timeline significantly. = 我们已经大大提前了项目时间表。
They have fast-forwarded their research by using new technologies. = 他们通过使用新技术加快了研究进度。


过去完成时:
He had fast-forwarded the video to the climax before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经把视频快进到了高潮部分。
They had fast-forwarded their negotiations to avoid delays. = 为了避免延误，他们加快了谈判进度。


将来时:
We will fast-forward the process to meet the deadline. = 我们将加快流程以满足截止日期。
She will fast-forward her studies to graduate early. = 她将加快学习进度以便提前毕业。


虚拟语气:
If we fast-forwarded the schedule, we could finish sooner. = 如果我们加快进度，就能更快完成。
I wish I had fast-forwarded that meeting; it was so unproductive. = 我真希望我当时快进了那个会议；它太没有效率了。


被动语态:
The film was fast-forwarded by the audience several times. = 电影被观众快进了好几次。
The project timeline was fast-forwarded by the management team. = 项目时间表被管理团队提前了。


疑问句:
Can we fast-forward this discussion? = 我们可以加快讨论进度吗？
Should we fast-forward to the next chapter? = 我们应该快进到下一章吗？


否定句:
I didn't fast-forward the recording; I listened to it all. = 我没有快进录音；我全部听完了。
They didn't fast-forward their plans; they proceeded cautiously. = 他们没有加快计划；他们谨慎行事。
