forefoot:/ˈfɔːrfʊt/| n. |前脚掌，前足|The part of the foot in front of the ankle.  It includes the metatarsals and phalanges. |例句：He injured his forefoot during the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛中伤了前脚掌。

例句：The dancer's forefoot was particularly flexible.  这位舞者的前脚掌特别灵活。


近义词：front foot, metatarsus (partially)

反义词：hind foot, rear foot


词性：名词
