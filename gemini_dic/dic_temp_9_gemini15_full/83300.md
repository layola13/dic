gyneceum:/ˌdʒaɪniˈsiːəm/| n. |雌蕊群,雌蕊|The gyneceum is the female reproductive part of a flower, consisting of one or more carpels.  It is often referred to as the pistil.  The gyneceum may be composed of a single carpel (monocarpous) or multiple carpels (polycarpous) that may be fused or free.  The structure and arrangement of the carpels are important taxonomic characters in plant classification. | The flower's gyneceum was clearly visible, showcasing its three distinct carpels.  这朵花的雌蕊群清晰可见，展示了其三个不同的心皮。|pistil, carpel|stamen|名词
