distant:/ˈdɪstənt/ | adj. |遥远的，疏远的，冷淡的 |  Relating to something far away in space or time; not close or intimate. |例句：The distant mountains were shrouded in mist. 远山笼罩在薄雾中。

例句：He maintained a distant relationship with his family. 他与家人保持着疏远的關係。


近义词：remote, faraway, aloof, detached | 反义词：near, close, intimate, warm
