laughable:/ˈlɑːfəbl/ /ˈlæfəbəl/| adj.|可笑的，荒谬的|Something laughable is so silly or unreasonable that it makes you want to laugh.  It suggests that the thing is not just funny, but also deserving of ridicule or scorn. |例句：His excuse was utterly laughable.  他的借口完全是可笑的。

His attempt to cook was laughable.  他做饭的尝试很可笑。


近义词：ridiculous, absurd, funny, comical, amusing|反义词：serious, credible, believable, sensible

词源:
The word "laughable" is derived from the verb "laugh," ultimately from Old English *hlæhhan.  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or worthiness, resulting in "worthy of laughter."
