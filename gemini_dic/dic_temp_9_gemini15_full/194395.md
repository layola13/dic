self-protectiveness: /ˌsɛlf prəˈtɛktɪvnəs/ | n. |自我保护性，自我防卫 |The tendency or behavior of protecting oneself from harm or danger.  It often involves actions taken to safeguard one's physical, emotional, or psychological well-being.  This can manifest in various ways, from avoiding risky situations to setting strong boundaries. |例句：Her self-protectiveness stemmed from a difficult childhood.  她的自我保护源于痛苦的童年。

Her self-protectiveness manifested as a reluctance to trust others.  她的自我保护表现为不愿相信他人。


近义词：self-preservation, defensiveness, guardedness, caution |反义词：vulnerability, openness, naiveté |名词
