parceled:/ˈpɑːrsld/| v.|打包，分发|To divide into parcels; to distribute in parcels.|例句：She parceled out the candy to the children. 她把糖果分给孩子们。|例句：The land was parceled into smaller lots. 这块地被分成较小的地块。|近义词：divided, distributed, portioned|反义词：combined, unified


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He parcels out his time carefully. = 他仔细地分配他的时间。
She parcels the goods for shipment. = 她打包货物以便装运。


一般过去时:
They parceled the land among their heirs. = 他们把土地分给他们的继承人。
The teacher parceled out the worksheets to the students. = 老师把工作表分发给学生们。


现在完成时:
He has parceled out all his possessions. = 他已经分掉了所有的财产。
She has parceled the letters ready for the post. = 她已经把信打包好准备邮寄。


过去完成时:

将来时:
