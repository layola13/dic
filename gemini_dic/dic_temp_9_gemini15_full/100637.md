intolerably:/ɪnˈtɑːləreɪbli/ | adj. |无法忍受地；极其；非常 | Extremely unpleasant, difficult, or painful to bear.  |例句：The heat was intolerably high.  = 炎热得令人难以忍受。|例句：The pain was intolerably sharp. = 疼痛剧烈无比。|近义词：unbearably, excessively, extremely |反义词：tolerably, bearably
