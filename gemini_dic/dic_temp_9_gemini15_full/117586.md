megalomanic:/ˌmɛɡələˈmeɪnɪk/| adj.|患有夸大狂的；妄想狂的|A megalomanic person has an inflated sense of their own importance and power, often accompanied by delusions of grandeur.  They crave power and control, often at the expense of others.  The term often implies a pathological condition. |例句：His megalomaniac tendencies led him to believe he could single-handedly solve the world's problems. 他患有夸大狂倾向，这使他相信自己可以独自解决世界上的问题。


例句：The dictator's megalomaniac speeches were filled with boasts of his unmatched abilities. 独裁者的夸大狂言论充满了对他无与伦比能力的吹嘘。


近义词：narcissistic, grandiose, egotistical, self-important, power-hungry


反义词：humble, modest, self-effacing, unassuming


词性:形容词
