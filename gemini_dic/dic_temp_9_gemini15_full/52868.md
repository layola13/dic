diminished:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃt/ | adj. v. | 减少的；减弱的；贬低的 | Reduced in size, extent, or importance; lessened.  |例句：His influence has diminished considerably in recent years. 近年来他的影响力大大减弱了。

The value of the property has diminished since the factory closed. 工厂关闭后，房产价值下降了。|近义词：reduced, lessened, decreased, weakened, impaired |反义词：increased, enlarged, enhanced, amplified


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun diminishes in size as it sets. 太阳落山时体积变小。
His power diminishes with each passing day. 他的权力一天天减弱。


一般过去时:
The storm diminished in intensity as night fell. 夜幕降临，暴风雨的强度减弱了。
Her confidence diminished after the failure. 她失败后信心减弱了。


现在完成时:
The population has diminished significantly over the past decade.  过去十年人口显著减少。
His reputation has diminished due to the scandal. 由于丑闻，他的名誉受损。


过去完成时:
His influence had diminished considerably before he resigned. 他辞职前，他的影响力已经大大减弱了。
The number of students had diminished before the new curriculum was introduced. 新课程引入之前，学生人数已经减少了。


将来时:
The effect of the medicine will diminish over time. 药物的效果会随着时间的推移而减弱。
Their enthusiasm will diminish if they don't see results soon. 如果他们很快看不到结果，他们的热情就会减弱。


虚拟语气:
If the noise diminished, we could concentrate better. 如果噪音减小，我们就能更好地集中注意力。
I wish my anxiety diminished more quickly. 我希望我的焦虑能更快地减轻。


被动语态:
His authority was diminished by the scandal. 他的权威因丑闻而受损。
The value of the artwork was diminished by the damage. 艺术品的价值因损坏而降低。


疑问句:
Has the problem diminished at all? 问题有任何减轻吗？
Will the pain diminish soon? 疼痛很快会减轻吗？


否定句:
The problem hasn't diminished despite our efforts. 尽管我们努力了，问题并没有减少。
His skills haven't diminished with age. 他的技能并没有随着年龄的增长而下降。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "diminished" comes from the Old French word "diminuer," meaning "to lessen, diminish."  This, in turn, derives from the Latin word "minuere," which has the same meaning.  The "-ed" suffix indicates the past participle form, making it an adjective in this context.
