frescade:/freˈskɑːdeɪ/| n.|凉爽；清凉的微风；凉快的地方|A frescade is a refreshing coolness, especially a cool breeze. It can also refer to a cool, shady place.  The word is primarily used in Spanish and Portuguese.|例句：The frescade after the midday sun was most welcome.  正午烈日后的凉爽令人非常惬意。


The afternoon frescade offered respite from the heat. 下午的凉风为酷暑带来一丝喘息。|近义词：coolness, breeze, refreshment|反义词：heat, swelter, scorch|名词
