periproct:/ˈpɛriprɑːkt/ | n. | 肛门周围 | The periproct is the region surrounding the anus, particularly in echinoderms.  It often includes specialized structures for respiration, excretion, or sensory perception. | Example: The periproct of the sea urchin is easily visible.  = 海胆的肛门周围区域很容易看到。 | 近义词: anal region, anus environs (informal) | 反义词:  None directly opposite, but terms referring to opposite ends of the body could be considered contrasting. | 名词
