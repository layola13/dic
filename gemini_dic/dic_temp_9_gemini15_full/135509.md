nonplanetary: /ˌnɑːnˈplænɪtəri/ | adj. |非行星的；与行星无关的 | Not relating to or characteristic of a planet.  The term describes objects or phenomena that are not planets or are not associated with planetary systems.


英文解析:The word "nonplanetary" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "planetary" (relating to planets). It signifies something that is not a planet or does not exhibit the characteristics typically associated with planets.


例句:

The study focused on nonplanetary objects in the Kuiper Belt. 这项研究集中在柯伊伯带的非行星天体上。

Scientists are exploring the possibility of life on nonplanetary bodies. 科学家们正在探索非行星天体上存在生命的可能性。


近义词: extraplanetary (sometimes, depending on context), non-stellar


反义词: planetary


词性: adjective
