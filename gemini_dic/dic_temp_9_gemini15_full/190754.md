saveall:/ˈseɪvɔːl/| n.|保存所有；（尤指电脑文件）批量保存|A term often used in computer software to refer to a function that saves all open files or documents simultaneously.  It's a compound word formed from "save" and "all".|例句：I clicked "Save All" to avoid losing my work. = 我点击了“全部保存”以避免丢失我的工作。

例句：The "Save All" function is incredibly useful when working on multiple documents. = 当处理多个文档时，“全部保存”功能非常有用。

近义词：save everything, batch save |反义词：N/A |词性:n.
