perish:/ˈpɛrɪʃ/| v.| 死亡；毁灭；腐烂；枯萎| to die, especially in a violent or sudden way; to be destroyed or ruined; to decay or wither.|例句：Many people perished in the earthquake.  许多人在地震中丧生。|例句：His hopes perished with his dreams. 他的希望随着梦想破灭了。|近义词：die, expire, decay, rot|反义词：survive, thrive, flourish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flowers perish in the frost. = 花儿在霜冻中枯萎。
The old building is perishing slowly. = 这座老建筑正在慢慢地毁坏。

一般过去时:
Many soldiers perished in the battle. = 许多士兵在那场战斗中丧生。
The ancient civilization perished centuries ago. = 古代文明在几个世纪前消失了。

现在完成时:
Many species have perished due to climate change. = 许多物种由于气候变化而灭绝。
The evidence has perished over time. = 证据随着时间的推移而消失了。

过去完成时:
The plants had perished before the rain came. = 雨来之前，植物已经枯萎了。
The kingdom had perished long before the explorers arrived. = 探险家到达之前，王国早已灭亡了。

将来时:
If we don't act, the ecosystem will perish. = 如果我们不采取行动，生态系统将会崩溃。
The crops will perish without sufficient water. = 没有足够的水，庄稼将会枯萎。

虚拟语气:
If only he hadn't taken that risk, he wouldn't have perished. = 要是他没有冒险，他就不会死了。
I wish the ancient manuscripts hadn't perished in the fire. = 我真希望那些古代手稿没有在火灾中烧毁。

被动语态:
The entire fleet was perished in a storm. = 整个舰队都在暴风雨中沉没了。
The old house was slowly perishing from neglect. = 老房子由于疏于管理而慢慢地毁坏。


疑问句:
Will the project perish due to lack of funding? = 项目会因为缺乏资金而失败吗？
Did the ancient civilization perish due to war or disease? = 古代文明是因战争还是疾病而灭亡的？


否定句:
The plants did not perish despite the drought. = 尽管干旱，植物却没有枯死。
The company did not perish; it merely changed its strategies. = 公司并没有倒闭，它只是改变了策略。
