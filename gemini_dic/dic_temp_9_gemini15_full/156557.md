picker:/ˈpɪkər/ | n. |采摘者；选择器；（尤指）水果采摘机 | A person or device that picks something, especially fruit.  |The apple picker worked tirelessly all day. 苹果采摘者整天辛勤工作。  The software has a convenient date picker. 这个软件有一个方便的日期选择器。 |selector, chooser, gatherer |  (no direct antonym, depends on context)
