choke back:/tʃoʊk bæk/| v.|强忍（眼泪、情绪等）|To suppress or restrain (a feeling or emotion), especially with difficulty.|例句：She choked back a sob. 她强忍住哭泣。|例句：He choked back his anger. 他强忍住怒火。|近义词：suppress, restrain, stifle, repress|反义词：express, unleash, release


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chokes back his tears every day. 他每天都强忍泪水。
She chokes back her laughter. 她强忍住笑声。

一般过去时:
He choked back a scream when he saw the accident. 他看到事故时强忍住一声尖叫。
She choked back her anger, but it was visible in her eyes. 她强忍住怒火，但这从她的眼睛里看得出来。

现在完成时:
He has choked back his emotions for far too long. 他压抑情绪太久了。
She has choked back her disappointment for weeks. 她已经压抑失望情绪好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
He had choked back his feelings until he could no longer bear it. 他一直强忍着感情，直到再也无法忍受。
She had choked back her tears throughout the entire ceremony. 在整个仪式中，她一直强忍着泪水。

将来时:
He will choke back his anger when he sees the results. 他看到结果时会强忍住怒火。
She will choke back a scream if she sees a spider. 如果她看到蜘蛛，她会强忍住尖叫。

虚拟语气:
If he saw her, he would choke back his feelings. 如果他看到她，他会强忍住自己的感情。
If she knew the truth, she would choke back her laughter. 如果她知道真相，她会强忍住笑声。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used passively)
His anger was barely choked back. 他的怒火几乎无法抑制。
Her tears were almost choked back. 她的眼泪几乎要夺眶而出。

疑问句:
Can he choke back his tears? 他能强忍住泪水吗？
Will she be able to choke back her laughter? 她能强忍住笑声吗？

否定句:
He couldn't choke back his tears any longer. 他再也无法强忍泪水了。
She didn't choke back her feelings; she let them all out. 她没有强忍住自己的感情；她把所有的感情都发泄了出来。
