salvoing:/ˈsælvɔːɪŋ/| v.|抢救；打捞；营救|The act of salvaging something; rescuing something from loss or destruction.  It often involves recovering something from a shipwreck, fire, or other disaster. |例句：The crew were engaged in salvoing the wrecked ship. 船员们正忙于打捞失事船只。

例句：The firefighters successfully salvoed valuable documents from the burning building. 消防员成功地从燃烧的建筑物中抢救出珍贵的文件。


近义词：rescuing, recovering, retrieving, saving

反义词：abandoning, destroying, losing
