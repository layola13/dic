cephalopodan:/ˌsefəˈlɒpədən/ | n. | 头足类动物 | A marine mollusk of the class Cephalopoda, characterized by a prominent head and tentacles.  Examples include octopuses, squid, and cuttlefish. | Example: The cephalopodan's camouflage abilities are remarkable.  头足类动物的伪装能力令人惊叹。  The scientist studied the unique nervous system of the cephalopodan.  科学家研究了头足类动物独特的神经系统。|近义词：cephalopod | 反义词：无
