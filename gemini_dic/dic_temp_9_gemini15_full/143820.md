outpath:/ˈaʊtˌpæθ/| n. |输出路径|The outpath specifies the directory where the output files will be saved.  输出路径指定保存输出文件的位置。|近义词：output directory, destination path|反义词：input path


USAGE->
This word is a noun,  and therefore does not conjugate through verb tenses.  The usage is primarily descriptive, specifying a location.  There is no tense to describe.
