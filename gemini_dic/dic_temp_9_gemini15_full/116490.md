materializing:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 实现；具体化；显现 | To become real or visible, especially suddenly; to give a physical form to something.  |  例句：His dreams are finally materializing. 他的梦想终于实现了。
例句：The ghost materialized before our eyes.  鬼魂在我们眼前显现。|近义词：realizing, manifesting, appearing |反义词：vanishing, disappearing, abstracting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The idea is materializing slowly. = 这个想法正在慢慢实现。
His plan is materializing before our eyes. = 他的计划在我们眼前实现。


一般过去时:
The vision materialized into a tangible reality. = 愿景变成了切实的现实。
