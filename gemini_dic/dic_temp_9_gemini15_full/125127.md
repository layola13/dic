/ˈmʌltiɡreɪnz/ | n. | 多谷物（食品） |  Multi-grain refers to food products made from more than one type of grain.  These grains can include wheat, oats, rye, barley, corn, and others.  Multi-grain foods are often marketed as being more nutritious than foods made from a single grain due to the wider range of nutrients provided. | Example: I prefer multi-grain bread to white bread because it's healthier. 我更喜欢多谷物面包而不是白面包，因为它更健康。 | 近义词: whole grain, mixed grain | 反义词: single-grain, refined grain | 名词
