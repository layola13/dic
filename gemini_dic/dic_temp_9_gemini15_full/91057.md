hustle:/ˈhʌsəl/| n., v.|奔忙；努力工作；推销；欺骗|

英文解析:
As a noun, "hustle" refers to energetic activity; vigorous action; a way of doing things that is fast and energetic.  It can also refer to a way of life characterized by energetic pursuit of success, often involving a degree of competitiveness and shrewdness. As a verb, "hustle" means to work energetically and persistently; to push or shove someone roughly; to swindle or cheat someone.

例句：
He got the job through sheer hustle and determination. 他凭借纯粹的努力和决心得到了这份工作。

She hustled through her work to finish before the deadline. 她努力工作，以便在截止日期前完成。


近义词：
rush, bustle, scramble, strive, push, shove, cheat, swindle

反义词：
loaf, idle, dawdle, relax, shirk


词性:
Noun and Verb
