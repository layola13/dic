/ˌoʊvərɪˈflɛkʃən/ | n. |过度思考；反复思考；反省过度 | Overreflection refers to excessive or prolonged contemplation or analysis of something, often to the point of hindering progress or causing distress. It involves dwelling on thoughts, memories, or events to an unhealthy degree. | Example:  Her overreflection on past mistakes prevented her from moving forward.  她的过度反思过去犯的错误阻碍了她前进。  |近义词：rumination, brooding, introspection (when excessive) |反义词：lightheartedness, unconcern,  action
