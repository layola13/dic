dissonance:/ˈdɪsənəns/| n. |不和谐，不一致；不协调；冲突 |A lack of harmony or agreement; incongruity.  Dissonance can refer to a clash of sounds, ideas, or elements. It often creates a feeling of tension or unease. |例句：The harsh dissonance of the trumpets jarred the listeners.  刺耳的小号声刺痛了听众的耳朵。|例句：There was a dissonance between her words and her actions.  她的言行不一。|近义词：discord, clash, conflict, incongruity |反义词：harmony, concord, agreement, consistency
