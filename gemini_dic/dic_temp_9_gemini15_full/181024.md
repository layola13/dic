reinaugurate:/ˌriːɪˈnɔːɡjʊreɪt/| vt.|重新启用；再次就职|To inaugurate again; to install again in office.|例句：The president was reinaugurated for a second term.  总统再次就职，开始了他的第二个任期。

例句：The museum was reinaugurated after extensive renovations.  博物馆在经过大规模翻修后重新开放。


近义词：inaugurate again, reinstall, recommence


反义词：terminate, close down, decommission


词性:动词
