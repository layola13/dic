liturgy:/ˈlɪtɜːrʒi/| n. |礼拜仪式；礼仪；宗教仪式 | A prescribed form of public worship.  It typically involves a set order of service, prayers, hymns, and readings. |The church follows a traditional liturgy. 这座教堂遵循传统的礼拜仪式。  The liturgy included prayers, readings, and hymns.  礼拜仪式包括祈祷、诵读和圣歌。 | service, rite, ritual | chaos, disorder | 名词
