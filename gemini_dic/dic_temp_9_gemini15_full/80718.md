gormander:/ˈɡɔːrmændər/| n.|贪吃的人，暴食者|A gormander is a person who eats greedily or excessively.  The word evokes a sense of gluttony and often implies a lack of refinement in eating habits.|Example: He was a notorious gormander, known for clearing entire platters at every meal. 他是个臭名昭著的贪吃鬼，以每顿饭都吃光整盘菜而闻名。|近义词：glutton, gourmand, voracious eater|反义词：abstemious eater, picky eater|名词
