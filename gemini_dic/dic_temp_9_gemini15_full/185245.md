retranscribed:/ˌriːˈtrænskraɪbd/ | v. |重新抄写；重新转录 | To transcribe something again, often correcting errors or improving the quality of the original transcription.  | Example: The audio was difficult to understand, so it had to be retranscribed.  音频难以理解，因此必须重新转录。 |近义词：rewritten, recopied |反义词：originally transcribed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They retranscribe the interviews every week. = 他们每周都重新转录采访。
He retranscribes the notes for better clarity. = 他为了更好的清晰度而重新抄写笔记。

一般过去时:
She retranscribed the document after finding several mistakes. = 她发现几个错误后重新抄写了文件。
They retranscribed the old tapes to preserve the history. = 他们重新转录了旧磁带以保存历史。

现在完成时:
He has retranscribed all the audio files. = 他已经重新转录了所有音频文件。
We have retranscribed the minutes of the meeting for accuracy. = 我们已经为了准确性而重新转录了会议记录。

They had retranscribed the data before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经重新转录了数据。
