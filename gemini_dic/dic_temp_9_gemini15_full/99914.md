internationalizing: /ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使国际化；使具有国际性 | The act of making something international in scope or character.  This often involves adapting products, services, or content to be suitable for different cultures and markets. | 例句：The company is internationalizing its marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. 公司正在将其营销策略国际化，以吸引更广泛的受众。

The process of internationalizing our software requires careful consideration of different languages and cultural norms.  将我们的软件国际化的过程需要仔细考虑不同的语言和文化规范。| 近义词：globalizing, universalizing | 反义词：localizing, regionalizing | 动词

词源:

The word "internationalizing" is derived from "international," which itself comes from "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among") and "national" (relating to a nation).  The "-izing" suffix indicates the process of making something international.
