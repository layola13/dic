invokable:/ɪnˈvoʊkəbəl/| adj.| 可调用的，可引用的 | Capable of being invoked or called upon; readily accessible or usable.  This typically refers to functions, methods, or subroutines in programming or other contexts where a routine can be called upon to perform a specific task.  | Example: The library provides several invokable functions for image processing.  该库提供了几个可用于图像处理的函数。

The function is invokable from any part of the program. 该函数可在程序的任何部分调用。|近义词：callable, accessible, usable |反义词： inaccessible, unusable, unavailable

词源:
The word "invokable" is derived from the verb "invoke," meaning "to call upon,"  combined with the suffix "-able," indicating capability or possibility.  The root of "invoke" traces back to Latin *invocare, from *in- ("in," "upon") + vocare ("to call").
