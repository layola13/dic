reattributed:/ˌriːˈætrɪbjuːtɪd/| v. |重新归因；重新认定归属 | To attribute (something) again or differently.  To ascribe (something) to a different cause, source, or author. |例句：The painting was reattributed to a lesser-known artist after further research.  进一步研究后，这幅画被重新归于一位鲜为人知的艺术家。

The discovery of new evidence led to the reattribution of the authorship of the play. 新证据的发现导致了该剧作作者归属的重新认定。

| 近义词：reassign, reassign authorship, re-credit | 反义词：attribute (in its original sense) | 动词
