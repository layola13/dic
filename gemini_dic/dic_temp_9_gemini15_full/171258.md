pterobranchia:/ˈtɛrəʊbræŋkiə/| n. |腕足动物亚纲|A class of small, sessile, marine invertebrates belonging to the phylum Hemichordata.  They are characterized by a worm-like body with a proboscis, collar, and trunk, and often build tubes or houses.  They are considered to be related to echinoderms and chordates.|例句：The discovery of new pterobranch species in deep-sea vents expands our understanding of Hemichordata evolution. 深海热液喷口处发现的新的腕足动物物种扩展了我们对半索动物进化史的理解。|近义词：hemichordate (broader term)|反义词:|词性|名词
