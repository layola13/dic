reemergence:/ˌriːɪˈmɜːrdʒəns/| n. |重新出现；复出；再次兴起 |The reappearance of something that was previously lost, hidden, or inactive.  It suggests a return after a period of absence or dormancy. |例句：The reemergence of the disease caused widespread panic.  疾病的重新出现引起了广泛的恐慌。

例句：After years of obscurity, the band's reemergence into the music scene was met with great enthusiasm.  乐队沉寂多年后重新出现在乐坛，受到了极大的热情欢迎。


近义词：resurgence, reappearance, revival, comeback, return


反义词：disappearance, extinction, demise, vanishing


词性: 名词
