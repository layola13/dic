gossoon:/ɡɒˈsuːn/| n. |乡下青年；小伙子；年轻人|A young man, especially a country lad; a youth.  The word often carries a connotation of boisterousness or mischievousness. |例句：He was a strapping gossoon, always up to some mischief. 他是个身材魁梧的小伙子，总是捣蛋。

例句：The gossoon charmed everyone with his quick wit and infectious laughter.  这个小伙子以他敏捷的才智和富有感染力的笑声赢得了所有人的喜爱。
