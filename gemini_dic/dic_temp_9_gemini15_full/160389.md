poplar:/ˈpɒplər/ /ˈpɑːplər/| n. |白杨树，杨树|A poplar is a tall, fast-growing tree with leaves that tremble easily in the wind.  They are known for their smooth, often grayish bark and their relatively quick growth rate.  Poplars are widely cultivated for timber, pulpwood, and ornamental purposes. |Example: The wind rustled through the leaves of the poplar trees. 风吹过白杨树的树叶沙沙作响。

The poplar trees lined the riverbank. 白杨树沿着河岸排列。|近义词：aspen, cottonwood |反义词：无
