melter:/ˈmɛltər/| n. |熔化者；融化器；使熔化之物|A melter is a person or thing that melts something.  It can refer to a machine used for melting, a person who works with melting processes, or something that causes melting. |The snow melter worked overtime during the blizzard.  融雪机在暴风雪期间超负荷运转。 The old woman's heart was a melter of ice.  老妇人的心能融化冰雪。|近义词：furnace, liquefier |反义词：freezer, solidifier
