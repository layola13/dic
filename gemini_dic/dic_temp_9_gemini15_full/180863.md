rehinges:/ˌriːˈhɪndʒɪz/ | v. |重新安装铰链；改变…的中心或重点 |To attach hinges to something again; to change the central point or emphasis of something.  |例句：The cabinet door needed rehinging after it was damaged.  =  柜门损坏后需要重新安装铰链。

The campaign rehinges on the candidate's new economic plan. = 这场竞选活动的关键在于候选人的新经济计划。 |近义词：reinstall, refit, pivot, refocus |反义词：remove, detach
