preserving: /prɪˈzɜːrvɪŋ/ | v. | 保护；保存；腌制；维护 |  To keep something in its original state, to protect it from damage or decay.  |


例句：
We need to find a way of preserving our natural resources. = 我们需要找到一种保护自然资源的方法。

They're preserving the old building by restoring it. = 他们正在通过修复来保护这座古老的建筑。


近义词：conserving, protecting, maintaining, safeguarding


反义词：destroying, damaging, ruining, wasting


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We preserve our cultural heritage. = 我们保护我们的文化遗产。
They preserve food by canning or freezing. = 他们通过罐装或冷冻来保存食物。


一般过去时:
The museum preserved the ancient artifacts. = 博物馆保存了古代文物。
She preserved her grandmother's recipes. = 她保存了她祖母的食谱。


现在完成时:
They have preserved the historical building for future generations. = 他们已经为子孙后代保存了这座历史建筑。
We have preserved many of the old traditions. = 我们已经保存了许多古老的传统。


过去完成时:
They had preserved the documents for many years. = 他们已经保存了这些文件很多年了。
She had preserved the fruit by drying it in the sun. = 她通过在阳光下晒干来保存水果。


将来时:
We will preserve the environment for our children. = 我们将为我们的孩子保护环境。
The government will preserve the national park. = 政府将保护国家公园。


虚拟语气:
If we acted now, we could preserve the rainforest. = 如果我们现在采取行动，我们就能保护雨林。
I wish we could preserve all the endangered species. = 我希望我们能够保护所有濒危物种。


被动语态:
The historical site is being preserved by the local government. = 当地政府正在保护这个历史遗址。
The old photos were preserved by careful storage. = 这些旧照片通过仔细存放而被保存下来。


疑问句:
How do they preserve the ancient paintings? = 他们如何保存古代绘画？
What methods are used to preserve food? = 使用什么方法来保存食物？


否定句:
They did not preserve the evidence properly. = 他们没有妥善保存证据。
We cannot preserve everything. = 我们不可能保存一切。


PHRASE->
preserve the peace = 维持和平
preserve one's health = 保持健康
preserve one's dignity = 保持尊严
preserve the environment = 保护环境
preserve wildlife = 保护野生动物


词源:
源自古法语 *preserver，来自拉丁语 *preservare，由 *prae- (before) 和 *servare (to keep, save) 组成。
