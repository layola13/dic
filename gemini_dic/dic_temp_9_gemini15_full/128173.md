networkers:/ˈnɛtwɜːrkərz/ | n. | 网络工作者；人脉高手 | People who are skilled at building and maintaining relationships, often for professional purposes. They actively seek out and connect with others to expand their professional circle and opportunities.  |  He's a great networker; he always seems to know the right people. 他是个社交高手；他似乎总是认识合适的人。  | connectors, socializers, relationship builders | loners, isolates  | 名词
