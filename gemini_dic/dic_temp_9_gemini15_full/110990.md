liquidizes: /ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 液化；使成液体 | To change or become a liquid.  The process of turning something solid into a liquid. | 例句：Heat liquidizes many solids.  加热可以使许多固体液化。

The enzyme liquidizes the starch. 这种酶使淀粉液化。


近义词：liquefies, melts, dissolves | 反义词：solidifies, freezes

词源:
liquidize comes from the word "liquid," + "-ize," a suffix meaning "to make or become."  The root ultimately traces back to the Latin word "liquefacere," meaning "to make liquid."
