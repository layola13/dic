defibrinize:/ˌdiːˈfaɪbrɪnaɪz/| vt.|去除纤维蛋白|To remove fibrin from (a substance, such as blood).  The process typically involves the use of anticoagulants or physical methods to prevent blood clotting.|例句：The blood sample was defibrinized before analysis.  血液样本在分析前被除去了纤维蛋白。 The researchers defibrinized the plasma to study its properties. 研究人员去除血浆中的纤维蛋白以研究其特性。|近义词：defibrinate|反义词：fibrinate
