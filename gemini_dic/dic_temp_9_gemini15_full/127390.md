near-by:/ˌnɪrˈbaɪ/| adj.|附近的，邻近的|Near-by means close in proximity or distance. It describes something that is not far away.|例句：There's a near-by park where we can have a picnic.  附近有个公园，我们可以在那里野餐。|例句：The near-by shops offer a wide variety of goods. 附近的商店提供各种各样的商品。|近义词：nearby, close, adjacent, neighboring|反义词：distant, faraway, remote|形容词
