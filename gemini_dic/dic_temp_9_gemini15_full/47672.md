debt:/dɛt/| n. |债务，欠款 |A debt is a sum of money that is owed to someone.  It can be a result of borrowing money, goods, or services.  Debts can be personal, such as credit card debt, or business-related, such as loans from banks.|Example:  He is burdened by a large debt. 他背负着巨额债务。

Example: The company is struggling to pay off its debts. 这家公司正在努力偿还债务。


近义词：liability, obligation, arrears |反义词：credit, asset


PHRASE->
in debt: 负债
get into debt: 负债
pay off debt: 还清债务
clear a debt: 还清债务
debt burden: 债务负担


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He owes a debt to his parents. 他欠他父母的债。
She has a large debt to repay. 她有一笔巨额债务需要偿还。


一般过去时:
He paid off his debt last year. 他去年还清了他的债务。
They incurred a significant debt to start the business. 他们为了创业而背负了巨额债务。


现在完成时:
I have accumulated a considerable debt. 我已经积累了一笔相当大的债务。
She has paid off most of her student loan debt. 她已经还清了大部分学生贷款。


过去完成时:
By the time he retired, he had paid off all his debts. 等他退休时，他已经还清了所有债务。
Before starting the business, they had already accumulated some debt. 在创业之前，他们已经积累了一些债务。


将来时:
He will pay off his debt within five years. 他将在五年内还清债务。
She will likely incur more debt to fund her education. 她可能会为了资助她的教育而承担更多的债务。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't taken out that loan, he wouldn't be in so much debt. 如果他没有贷款，他就不会欠这么多债。
I wish I hadn't gotten into so much debt. 我真希望我没有欠这么多债。


被动语态:
The debt was repaid in full. 债务已全部偿还。
The debt is being gradually reduced. 债务正在逐渐减少。


疑问句:
How much debt do you have? 你欠多少钱？
What kind of debt is this? 这是什么类型的债务？


否定句:
He doesn't have any debt. 他没有任何债务。
She doesn't want to get into debt. 她不想负债。


词源:
The word "debt" comes from the Old French word "dette," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "debitum," the past participle of "debere," meaning "to owe."  The sense of owing something, whether money or something else, is at the core of the word's meaning.
