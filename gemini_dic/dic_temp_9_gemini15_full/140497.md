oldie:/ˈoʊldi/| n.|老歌；老东西；老年人|An oldie is something old, especially a song or film that is still popular.  It can also refer to an elderly person, though this usage is less common and sometimes considered slightly disrespectful.|例句：That song is a real oldie but a goodie. 那首歌是首真正的老歌，但很好听。

例句：He's an oldie but he still has a lot of energy. 他年纪大了，但他仍然精力充沛。(This usage is less common and could be considered slightly informal or even disrespectful depending on context)


近义词：classic, veteran (for people), antique


反义词：newbie, newcomer (for people), novelty


词性：名词
