nonpersistence: /ˌnɒn.pərˈsɪs.təns/ | n. |非持久性，短暂性 |The lack of persistence; the tendency not to continue or remain.  It refers to something that does not last or endure.  |例句：The nonpersistence of the effect surprised the researchers.  非持久性的效果让研究人员感到惊讶。

例句：The nonpersistence of the memory trace made it difficult to recall the details. 记忆痕迹的短暂性使得难以回忆起细节。


近义词：transience, ephemerality, impermanence, fleetingness

反义词：persistence, permanence, durability, longevity

词性: noun
