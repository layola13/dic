forwards:/ˈfɔːrwərdz/ | adv. n. | 向前；前锋；将来 |  Moving or directed toward the front.  A player in certain team sports who plays in an attacking position. |例句：He moved forwards. 他向前移动了。  She's a talented forwards on the soccer team. 她是足球队里一名有天赋的前锋。|近义词：ahead, onward, forward, striker |反义词：backwards, behind, rearward

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team moves forwards aggressively. = 球队积极向前推进。
He always looks forwards to the weekend. = 他总是期待着周末。

一般过去时:
The soldiers marched forwards. = 士兵们向前行进。
