fleshen:/ˈflɛʃən/| vt.|使…长肉；使…丰满；具体化|To make something more full, detailed, or realistic; to add flesh to something.  |例句：He fleshed out the story with vivid details. 他用生动的细节充实了这个故事。 |例句：The artist fleshed out the sketch with vibrant colors. 艺术家用鲜艳的色彩使素描更加丰满。|近义词：embody, elaborate, detail, expand|反义词：simplify, abstract, generalize|动词
