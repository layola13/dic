gifts:/ɡɪfts/ | n. | 礼物，天赋 | Presents given voluntarily to someone; a natural talent or aptitude.  |  例句：She received many gifts on her birthday. 她生日收到很多礼物。 | 例句：His musical gifts were evident from a young age. 他从很小的时候就展现出音乐天赋。 | 近义词：presents, talents, endowments | 反义词：burden, curse


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He gives gifts generously. = 他慷慨地赠送礼物。
She often receives gifts from her friends. = 她经常收到朋友的礼物。


一般过去时:
They gave me a wonderful gift. = 他们给了我一件很棒的礼物。
He presented gifts to the guests. = 他向宾客赠送礼物。


现在完成时:
I have received many gifts this year. = 今年我收到了很多礼物。
She has given gifts to charity for years. = 她多年来一直向慈善机构捐赠礼物。


过去完成时:
They had already given all the gifts before I arrived. = 我到之前他们已经把所有的礼物都送出去了。
She had carefully wrapped the gifts before the party. = 派对前她已经仔细地包装好了礼物。


将来时:
I will give you a gift for your graduation. = 我会送你一份毕业礼物。
They will be giving gifts to the children at Christmas. = 圣诞节他们会给孩子们送礼物。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would give more generous gifts. = 如果我有更多钱，我会送更慷慨的礼物。
I wish I could give you the gift of time. = 我希望我能送你时间作为礼物。


被动语态:
The gifts were distributed equally. = 礼物被平均分配了。
The gifts are being wrapped now. = 礼物现在正在包装。


疑问句:
What gifts did you receive? = 你收到了什么礼物？
What kind of gifts do you like? = 你喜欢什么类型的礼物？


否定句:
He didn't give any gifts. = 他没有送任何礼物。
They haven't received the gifts yet. = 他们还没有收到礼物。
