huddles:/ˈhʌdəlz/ | v. n. | 挤作一团；聚集；低声交谈 | To crowd together; to gather closely; to whisper together. | 例句：The sheep huddled together for warmth. 羊群挤在一起取暖。

The team huddled before the big game to discuss strategy.  球队在比赛前聚在一起讨论策略。


近义词：cluster, gather, congregate, bunch
反义词：scatter, disperse, spread out

词性：动词(v.)和名词(n.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They huddle together for warmth. = 他们挤在一起取暖。
The players huddle before the game. = 球员们在比赛前聚集在一起。


一般过去时:
The crowd huddled in the doorway during the storm. = 暴风雨期间人群挤在门口。
The children huddled together, whispering secrets. = 孩子们挤在一起，窃窃私语。

The sheep have huddled together all night. = 羊群整夜都挤在一起。
They have huddled in the corner since the argument began. = 从争吵开始，他们就一直挤在角落里。


过去完成时:
The birds had huddled together before the blizzard hit. = 在暴风雪来临之前，鸟儿们已经挤在一起了。
The refugees had huddled in the camp for days. = 难民们已经在营地里挤了好几天了。
