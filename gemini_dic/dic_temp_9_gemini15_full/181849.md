relocates:/ˌriːˈloʊkeɪts/ | v. | 搬迁；重新安置 | To move to a new place and establish oneself there.  The verb implies a more permanent move than simply moving temporarily. | 例句：The company relocated its headquarters to a larger building. 公司将其总部搬迁到了一栋更大的建筑物。

例句：The family relocated to the countryside for a quieter life.  这个家庭搬到了乡下过一种更安静的生活。


近义词：move, transfer, transplant, remove

反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relocates every few years. = 他每隔几年就搬家一次。
The company relocates its employees frequently. = 公司经常调动员工。


一般过去时:
They relocated to California last year. = 他们去年搬到了加利福尼亚州。
She relocated her business to a more profitable area. = 她把她的生意搬到了一个更赚钱的地区。


现在完成时:
We have relocated three times since we got married. = 自从我们结婚以来，我们已经搬过三次家了。
The government has relocated many families to safer areas. = 政府已将许多家庭迁往更安全的地区。


过去完成时:
They had relocated before the earthquake struck. = 地震发生之前，他们已经搬家了。
The company had relocated its server before the cyberattack. = 网络攻击之前，公司已经迁移了服务器。


将来时:
We will relocate to a new city next spring. = 我们明年春天将搬到一个新的城市。
The factory will relocate to a more cost-effective location. = 工厂将搬迁到一个更经济高效的地点。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would relocate to a warmer climate. = 如果我有钱，我会搬到气候温暖的地方。
I wish I could relocate to the coast. = 我希望我能搬到海边。


被动语态:
The employees were relocated to a different office. = 员工被调到了不同的办公室。
The refugees were relocated to temporary shelters. = 难民被安置在临时住所。


疑问句:
When are you relocating? = 你什么时候搬家？
Will the company be relocating soon? = 公司很快就要搬迁了吗？


否定句:
He didn't relocate despite the high cost of living in the city. = 尽管城市生活成本高昂，但他并没有搬家。
The project was not relocated due to funding issues. = 由于资金问题，项目没有搬迁。


PHRASE->
relocate to = 搬迁到...
relocate oneself = 自己搬迁
relocate a business = 搬迁公司
relocate employees = 调动员工
relocate headquarters = 搬迁总部
