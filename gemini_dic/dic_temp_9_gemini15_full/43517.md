craving:/ˈkreɪvɪŋ/| n. v.|渴望，热切的欲望| a powerful desire or need for something|例句：I have a craving for chocolate. 我非常想吃巧克力。  He had a powerful craving for success. 他渴望成功。|近义词： yearning, longing, desire |反义词：disgust, aversion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I crave chocolate every day. = 我每天都渴望吃巧克力。
She craves attention. = 她渴望得到关注。


一般过去时:
I craved pizza after the workout. = 锻炼后我渴望吃披萨。
He craved freedom from his demanding job. = 他渴望从他要求苛刻的工作中解脱出来。


现在完成时:
I have craved this dish for weeks. = 我几周以来一直渴望这道菜。
She has craved independence since she was a teenager. = 自从十几岁以来，她就渴望独立。


过去完成时:
I had craved a vacation before the project started. = 在项目开始之前我就渴望休假。
They had craved peace and quiet after the noisy party. = 吵闹的派对后，他们渴望平静。


将来时:
I will crave something sweet after dinner. = 晚饭后我会渴望吃点甜食。
She will crave a more challenging role in the future. = 她将来会渴望一个更有挑战性的角色。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have a craving for coffee, I wouldn't drink it. = 如果我不渴望咖啡，我就不会喝它。
I wish I didn't have such a strong craving for junk food. = 我真希望我不那么渴望垃圾食品。


被动语态:
The sweet treat was craved by many. = 许多人都渴望这种甜食。
A quiet life was craved by the exhausted worker. = 精疲力尽的工人渴望平静的生活。


疑问句:
What are you craving right now? = 你现在渴望什么？
What did she crave most in her life? = 她一生中最渴望的是什么？


否定句:
I don't crave spicy food. = 我不渴望吃辛辣食物。
He didn't crave fame or fortune. = 他不渴望名利。


PHRASE->
sugar craving = 糖分渴望
intense craving = 强烈的渴望
chocolate craving = 巧克力渴望
drug craving = 毒瘾
food craving = 对食物的渴望
