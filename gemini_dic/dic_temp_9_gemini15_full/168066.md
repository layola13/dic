privatizing: /ˈpraɪvətaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 私有化 | The act of transferring ownership of a business, industry, or service from the public sector (government) to the private sector (individuals or corporations).  It involves the sale or lease of state-owned assets to private entities. | 例句：The government is privatizing the railway system. = 政府正在对铁路系统进行私有化。

例句：The city is considering privatizing its water utility. = 该市正在考虑将其自来水公司私有化。


近义词：denationalizing, commercializing

反义词：nationalizing, socializing
