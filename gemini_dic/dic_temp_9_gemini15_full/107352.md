lamellar:/læˈmɛlə(r)/| adj.|层状的；片状的|consisting of or arranged in thin layers or plates.  The term refers to structures that are composed of many thin, flat layers stacked upon each other, resembling the layers of a cake or the leaves of a book.|例句：The mineral exhibited a distinct lamellar structure under the microscope.  显微镜下，这种矿物呈现出明显的层状结构。

例句：The lamellar arrangement of the cells contributed to the tissue's strength. 细胞的层状排列增强了组织的强度。


近义词：layered, stratified, foliated, platy|反义词：amorphous, massive, homogeneous


词性:形容词
