monostrophic:/ˌmɒnəˈstrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 单节的；单段的 | Consisting of only one strophe or stanza; having only one verse.  A monostrophic poem has only one stanza. | Example: The poem, though short, was surprisingly effective in its monostrophic form.  这首诗虽然短小，但其单节的形式却出奇地有效。 | 近义词：unistrophe, single-stanzaed | 反义词：polystrophic, multistrophic
