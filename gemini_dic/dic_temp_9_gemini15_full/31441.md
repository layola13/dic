charity:/ˈtʃærəti/| n. |慈善；慈善机构；施舍；仁慈|The act of giving money, goods, or help to those in need;  a charitable organization; kindness or generosity.|例句：Many charities rely on donations from the public. 许多慈善机构依靠公众的捐款。|例句：She showed great charity in forgiving her enemies. 她宽恕敌人，表现出极大的仁慈。|近义词：benevolence, philanthropy, altruism|反义词：selfishness, greed, cruelty


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The charity organizes many fundraising events. = 这家慈善机构组织许多筹款活动。
He regularly donates to charity. = 他定期向慈善机构捐款。

一般过去时:
The company donated a large sum to charity last year. = 这家公司去年向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔款项。
She volunteered at a charity shop during her holidays. = 她假期期间在慈善商店做义工。

现在完成时:
We have raised a lot of money for charity this year. = 我们今年为慈善事业筹集了很多钱。
They have supported this charity for many years. = 他们多年来一直支持这家慈善机构。

过去完成时:
Before the disaster, the charity had already distributed aid supplies. = 在灾难发生之前，慈善机构已经分发了救济物资。
He had never volunteered for charity before that experience. = 在那次经历之前，他从未做过慈善义工。

将来时:
The charity will launch a new campaign next month. = 该慈善机构下个月将启动一项新活动。
She will donate her earnings to charity. = 她将把她的收入捐献给慈善机构。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would donate more to charity. = 如果我有更多钱，我会向慈善机构捐更多款。
I wish I could do more for charity. = 我希望我能为慈善事业做更多的事情。

被动语态:

The charity event is being sponsored by a local business. = 慈善活动由当地一家企业赞助。

疑问句:
Which charity do you support? = 你支持哪个慈善机构？
What kind of charity work are they doing? = 他们在做哪种慈善工作？

否定句:
He doesn't believe in giving money to charity. = 他不相信向慈善机构捐钱。
She didn't want to disclose which charity she donated to. = 她不想透露她向哪个慈善机构捐款。
