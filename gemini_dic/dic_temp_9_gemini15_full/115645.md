marconigraph:/ˈmɑːrkənəɡræf/| n. |马可尼电报；无线电报|A message sent by wireless telegraphy (Marconi's system).  The term is largely obsolete, superseded by more general terms like "radio message" or "wireless telegram".|Example:  The ship sent a marconigraph reporting its arrival.|近义词：radio message, wireless telegram|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a hand-delivered message.|名词
