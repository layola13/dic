colleterial: /kəˈlætərəl/ | n. | 担保品；抵押品 | Collateral refers to something pledged as security for a loan or other obligation, to be forfeited in case of default.  It's an asset that a borrower offers to a lender to secure a loan. If the borrower fails to repay the loan, the lender can seize and sell the collateral to recover their losses. | Example: The bank required collateral before approving the business loan.  银行在批准商业贷款之前要求提供抵押品。

The house served as collateral for the mortgage.  房子作为抵押贷款的抵押品。|近义词：security, guarantee, pledge |反义词：None (no direct antonym, but the opposite concept would be unsecured loan)|
