interchromosomally:/ˌɪntərkroʊməˈsoʊməli/| adj.|染色体间的|Occurring or acting between chromosomes.  This term describes events or processes that take place between non-homologous chromosomes. For example, interchromosomal recombination refers to the exchange of genetic material between non-homologous chromosomes. |Example: Interchromosomal recombination events are relatively rare compared to intrachromosomal events.|近义词:between chromosomes|反义词:intrachromosomally
