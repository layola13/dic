grievous:/ˈɡriːvəs/| adj.|严重的，悲伤的，令人痛苦的|extremely serious and causing great suffering.|The grievous loss of life in the accident shocked the community.  这场事故中严重的伤亡震惊了整个社区。


The grievous injury to his leg required surgery. 他腿部严重的伤势需要手术。

近义词：serious, severe, painful, distressing, agonizing

反义词：minor, insignificant, trivial, slight, unimportant

词性：形容词
