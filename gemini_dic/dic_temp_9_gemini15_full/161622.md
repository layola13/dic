poultice-wise:/ˈpʊltɪsˌwaɪz/| adj.| 关于药膏的；就药膏而言 |  Adjective formed by adding the suffix "-wise" to "poultice," indicating "in respect of" or "as regards" poultices. It signifies a perspective or consideration specifically related to poultices.  | Example: Poultice-wise, the treatment seems effective, but we need to monitor other symptoms. 就药膏而言，治疗似乎有效，但我们需要监测其他症状。| 近义词:regarding poultices, in terms of poultices | 反义词: N/A
