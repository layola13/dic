destrer:/dɛˈstreɪər/| n.|破坏者，毁灭者|A destroyer is someone or something that destroys or ruins something.|例句：The storm was a destroyer of homes and lives.  暴风雨摧毁了房屋和生命。|近义词：ruiner, wrecker, vandal|反义词：creator, builder, preserver|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm destroys everything in its path. = 暴风雨摧毁了它路径上的所有东西。
He is a destroyer of hope. = 他是希望的破坏者。


一般过去时:
The earthquake destroyed the city. = 地震摧毁了这座城市。
The fire destroyed the old building. = 大火烧毁了那座旧建筑。


现在完成时:
The war has destroyed many lives. = 战争已经夺去了许多人的生命。
The disease has destroyed many crops. = 这种疾病已经摧毁了许多庄稼。


过去完成时:
The army had destroyed the bridge before the enemy arrived. = 敌人到达之前，军队已经摧毁了桥梁。
They had destroyed all the evidence. = 他们已经销毁了所有证据。


将来时:
The flood will destroy the village. = 洪水将摧毁这个村庄。
The new technology will destroy traditional industries. = 新技术将摧毁传统产业。


虚拟语气:
If he had not been a destroyer, the world would be a better place. = 如果他不是一个破坏者，世界将会更好。
I wish he hadn't destroyed my trust in him. = 我真希望他没有破坏我对他的信任。


被动语态:
The city was destroyed by the earthquake. = 这座城市被地震摧毁了。
The forest was destroyed by the fire. = 森林被大火烧毁了。


疑问句:
What destroyed the ancient civilization? = 什么摧毁了古代文明？
Who is the destroyer of this beautiful painting? = 谁毁了这幅美丽的画？


否定句:
The rain did not destroy the crops. = 雨水没有毁坏庄稼。
He did not intend to be a destroyer. = 他并不想成为一个破坏者。
