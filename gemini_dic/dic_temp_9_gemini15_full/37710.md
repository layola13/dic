comparableness:/kəmˈpɛərəbəlnəs/| n. |可比性，可比较性 |The degree to which things can be compared or are similar.  It refers to the extent to which two or more things can be meaningfully compared based on shared characteristics or attributes.  |例句：The comparableness of the two studies was questionable due to their different methodologies.  两项研究的可比性由于其不同的方法而令人质疑。
The comparableness of the data sets is crucial for a valid analysis. 数据集的可比性对于有效的分析至关重要。|近义词：similarity, comparability, resemblance |反义词：incomparability, dissimilarity, difference|名词
