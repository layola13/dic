conditionalist: /kənˈdɪʃənəlɪst/ | n. | 条件论者，条件主义者 | A person who believes in conditionalism, the theological doctrine that salvation is conditional upon the believer's continued faith and obedience.  |  He is a staunch conditionalist, believing that salvation is not guaranteed but dependent on persistent faith. 他是一个坚定的条件论者，相信救赎不是有保证的，而是取决于持久的信仰。 | 近义词：conditionalist believer | 反义词：unconditionalist
