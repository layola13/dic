liquidizers: /ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzərz/ | n. | 液化器；果汁机 | A device used to liquefy or blend substances, especially food.  It typically involves high-speed blades to chop and mix ingredients until smooth.  | Example:  She used a liquidizer to make a smoothie for breakfast. 她用果汁机做了一杯早餐奶昔。  | 近义词：blender, mixer | 反义词：solidifier (a device that solidifies substances)
