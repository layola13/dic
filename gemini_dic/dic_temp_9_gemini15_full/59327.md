earldoms:/ˈɜːldəm/| n.|伯爵领地；伯爵爵位|The territories and privileges associated with an earl's title.  Earldoms are hereditary titles of nobility. |例句：Many earldoms were granted by the monarch during the medieval period.  许多伯爵领地在中世纪时期由君主授予。|例句：The earldom of Northumberland has a long and distinguished history.  诺森伯兰伯爵领地拥有悠久而显赫的历史。|近义词：county, dukedom, marquisate|反义词：无
