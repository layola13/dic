orthogonally:/ɔːrˈθɑːɡənəli/ | adv. | 正交地；垂直地；互相独立地 | In mathematics and geometry, orthogonally describes lines or vectors that are perpendicular to each other, forming a right angle.  In a broader sense, it can refer to things that are independent or unrelated. | Example: The two axes are orthogonally arranged.  两个轴线是正交排列的。 The research projects were conducted orthogonally, ensuring no overlap.  研究项目是正交进行的，确保没有重叠。 |近义词：perpendicularly, independently |反义词：parallel, dependently
