/ˌɪntərˌsɛləˈbreɪʃənz/| n. |相互庆祝；共同庆祝|The word "intercelebrations" is not a standard English word found in common dictionaries. It's a relatively rare neologism, likely formed by combining "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among") and "celebrations" (the act of celebrating).  It suggests multiple entities celebrating together or celebrating each other.  The meaning depends heavily on context.|例句：There were joyous intercelebrations between the rival teams after the unexpectedly friendly competition.  (在意外的友好竞争之后，敌对球队之间进行了愉快的相互庆祝。)  The festival featured intercelebrations of diverse cultures. (节日以多种文化的共同庆祝为特色。) |近义词: joint celebrations, mutual celebrations, shared celebrations |反义词: isolation, separation
