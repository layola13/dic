disbody:/dɪsˈbɒdi/| vt.|使…失去形体；使…脱离肉体；肢解|To deprive of a body; to separate the body from the soul or spirit; to dismember.|例句：The magician seemingly disbodied the assistant, making her disappear from the box. 魔术师似乎让助手失去了形体，让她从箱子里消失了。

例句：The gruesome accident disbodied the victim, leaving only scattered remains.  这场可怕的事故肢解了受害者，只留下散落的残骸。


近义词： disembody, dismember, separate


反义词： embody, unite, integrate


词性:动词
