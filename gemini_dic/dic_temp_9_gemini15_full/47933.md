decertation: /ˌdɪsɜːrˈteɪʃən/| n.|学位论文；长篇论文|A long essay or dissertation, especially one written for a university degree.|例句：She completed her dissertation on the effects of climate change. 她完成了关于气候变化影响的学位论文。|近义词：thesis, treatise, essay|反义词：brief, short note


USAGE->

His dissertation explores the impact of social media. = 他的学位论文探讨了社交媒体的影响。

一般过去时:
