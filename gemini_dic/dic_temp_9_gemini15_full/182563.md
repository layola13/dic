reoblige:/ˌriːˈɒblaɪdʒ/| vt.|重新施恩惠；再次帮忙|To oblige someone again; to do a favor for someone a second time.|例句：I'd be happy to reoblige if you need further assistance.  如果你需要更多帮助，我很乐意再次帮忙。

例句：He reobliged me by lending me his car after I returned his book. 他把书还给他之后，又把车借给了他，再次帮了他一个忙。

近义词：help again, assist again, aid again

反义词：refuse, deny, neglect
