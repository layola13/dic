pound-out: /paʊnd aʊt/ | vt. | 敲打出来；费力地完成；大声朗读 | To strike repeatedly with a heavy instrument; to produce something with great effort; to read aloud forcefully and clearly. |


例句：

He pounded out a rhythm on the table. = 他在桌子上敲打出一个节奏。

She pounded out the report in a single night. = 她一夜之间费力地完成了报告。


近义词：hammer out, knock out, bash out


反义词：refine, polish


词性：动词

词源:

The verb "pound" meaning "to strike repeatedly" is of Old English origin, stemming from the word "pundian," which also meant to strike or beat.  The "-out" suffix indicates completion or thoroughness of the action.
