excursioning:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒənɪŋ/| v.|远足；短途旅行；游览|The act of going on excursions; making short journeys or trips for pleasure or recreation.  It implies a sense of exploration and enjoyment of the journey itself, rather than just reaching a destination.|例句：We spent the weekend excursioning in the countryside. = 我们周末在乡下远足。

例句：The family enjoyed excursioning to the nearby national park. = 这家人享受了去附近的国家公园游览的乐趣。


近义词：touring, rambling, traveling, sightseeing|反义词：staying home, remaining stationary|词性：动词
