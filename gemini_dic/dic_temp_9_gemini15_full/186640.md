rift:/rɪft/| n. v.|裂缝；裂痕；不和；分歧；|A rift is a crack, split, or break in something.  It can also refer to a serious disagreement or breach between people or groups.|例句：A deep rift had opened up in the earth after the earthquake. 地震后，地表出现了一道深深的裂缝。

例句：The rift between the two brothers widened over the years. 兄弟俩之间的裂痕多年来越来越大。


近义词：crack, fissure, breach, split, discord, disagreement|反义词：harmony, accord, unity, agreement|名词，动词
