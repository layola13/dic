punch-ups:/ˈpʌntʃʌps/| n.|打架，冲突|A punch-up is a fight, typically a short and unplanned one involving several people.  It often implies a noisy and somewhat chaotic brawl.|例句：There was a punch-up outside the pub last night. 昨晚酒吧外发生了一场打架。|例句：The two teams got into a punch-up after the game.  比赛结束后两队发生冲突。|近义词：brawl, fight, scuffle, fracas|反义词：peace, harmony
