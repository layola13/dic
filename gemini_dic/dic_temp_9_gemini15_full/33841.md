circumnavigate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪt/| vt.|环航；绕…航行|To sail or travel all the way around (something, especially a large body of water).  This verb implies a complete circuit.|例句：Magellan was the first person to circumnavigate the globe. 麦哲伦是第一个环球航行的人。

例句：The yacht will circumnavigate the island before returning to port.  这艘游艇将在返回港口之前绕岛航行。


近义词：sail around, go around, encircle


反义词：traverse directly, cross directly


词性：动词
