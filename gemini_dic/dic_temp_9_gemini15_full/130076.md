non-premium: /ˌnɒnˈpriːmiəm/ | adj. |非高级的；非付费的；标准的| Not offering or relating to premium services or features.  It implies a basic or standard level of service, lacking the enhanced benefits or features found in premium options. |例句：The non-premium version of the software lacks several key features.  非高级版本的软件缺少几个关键功能。

The airline offers both premium and non-premium seating options. 这家航空公司提供高级和非高级座位选择。|近义词：standard, basic, ordinary, regular |反义词：premium, deluxe, superior, high-end

词源:
The word is a compound of "non-" (meaning "not") and "premium" (meaning "of superior quality; higher price").  Therefore, non-premium literally means "not of superior quality; not higher price".
