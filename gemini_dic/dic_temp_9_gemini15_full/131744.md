nonconformist:/ˌnɒnˈkɒnfɔːmɪst/| n. adj.|不墨守成规者；特立独行者；非国教信徒|A person who does not conform to generally accepted beliefs, customs, or practices.|The artist was a nonconformist, always pushing boundaries and challenging conventions.  这位艺术家是一位特立独行者，总是突破界限，挑战传统。|He embraced his nonconformist lifestyle, despite societal pressure. 他接受了他不墨守成规的生活方式，尽管受到了社会压力。|conformist, maverick|conformist, traditionalist
