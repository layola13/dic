injure:/ˈɪndʒər/ | vt. | 伤害，损害 | To do physical harm or damage to someone or something. | 例句：He injured his leg in the accident. 他在事故中伤了腿。 | 例句：The storm injured many buildings. 暴风雨损坏了许多建筑物。 | 近义词：harm, hurt, wound, impair | 反义词：heal, cure, mend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He injures his knee playing basketball. = 他打篮球时伤了膝盖。
The policy injures small businesses. = 这项政策损害了小型企业。

一般过去时:
She injured her ankle during the hike. = 她在远足时扭伤了脚踝。
The company injured its reputation with the scandal. = 这家公司因丑闻而损害了其声誉。

现在完成时:
He has injured his back lifting weights. = 他举重时伤了背。
The scandal has injured the reputation of the company. = 这场丑闻损害了公司的声誉。

过去完成时:
I had injured my hand before the competition. = 我在比赛前已经伤了手。
The company had injured its credibility long before the crisis hit. = 公司在危机爆发前很久就已损害了其信誉。

将来时:
He will injure himself if he isn't careful. = 如果他不小心，他会伤到自己。
The new regulations may injure the competitiveness of local businesses. = 新规可能会损害当地企业的竞争力。

虚拟语气:
If I had been more careful, I wouldn't have injured my knee. = 如果我更小心，就不会伤到膝盖了。
If they hadn't acted so recklessly, they wouldn't have injured their reputation. = 如果他们没有如此鲁莽行事，就不会损害他们的声誉了。

被动语态:
His leg was injured in the car crash. = 他的腿在车祸中受了伤。
The company's image was injured by the negative publicity. = 该公司的形象因负面宣传而受损。

疑问句:
Was anyone injured in the fire? = 火灾中有人受伤吗？
Were any buildings injured by the earthquake? = 地震损坏了哪些建筑物？

否定句:
He didn't injure himself badly. = 他没有伤得很重。
The accident didn't injure any of the passengers. = 事故没有造成任何乘客受伤。


PHRASE->
seriously injure = 严重受伤
slightly injure = 轻微受伤
injure oneself = 自残，受伤
injure one's reputation = 损害名誉
injure one's pride = 伤害自尊心


词源:
The word "injure" comes from the Old French word "injurier," meaning "to insult, to abuse."  Over time, the meaning shifted to focus on physical harm.  The Old French word itself has roots in the Latin word "injuria," meaning "injury, wrong, injustice."
