forsaking:/fərˈseɪkɪŋ/| v.|抛弃；放弃；遗弃 | Abandoning or giving up something, especially something valuable or important.  The act of forsaking something. |例句：He forsook his family and went to live abroad. 他抛弃了他的家人，去国外生活了。


例句：She forsook her career to raise her children. 她放弃了自己的事业去抚养孩子。


近义词：abandoning, relinquishing, deserting, giving up, leaving


反义词：keeping, cherishing, retaining, embracing


词性：动词(现在分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is forsaking his old habits. 他正在放弃他的旧习惯。
They are forsaking their principles. 他们在放弃他们的原则。


一般过去时:
She forsook her comfortable life. 她放弃了她舒适的生活。
He forsook his dreams of glory. 他放弃了他光荣的梦想。


现在完成时:
He has forsaken his friends. 他已经抛弃了他的朋友们。
She has forsaken her faith. 她已经放弃了她的信仰。


过去完成时:
They had forsaken their homeland. 他们已经抛弃了他们的祖国。
He had forsaken all hope. 他已经放弃了一切希望。


将来时:
She will forsake her family. 她将会抛弃她的家人。
They will forsake their studies. 他们将会放弃他们的学业。


虚拟语气:
If he forsook his ambition, he would be happier. 如果他放弃了他的野心，他会更快乐。
I wish she hadn't forsaken her talent. 我真希望她没有放弃她的天赋。


被动语态:
His principles were forsaken. 他的原则被放弃了。
His dreams were forsaken. 他的梦想被放弃了。


疑问句:
Is he forsaking his responsibilities? 他是否正在放弃他的责任？
Was she forsaking her beliefs? 她是否正在放弃她的信仰？


否定句:
He did not forsake his duty. 他没有放弃他的职责。
She did not forsake her children. 她没有抛弃她的孩子。



PHRASE->
forsake all hope: 放弃所有希望
forsake one's faith: 放弃信仰

forsake one's family: 抛弃家人
forsake one's principles: 放弃原则

词源:
Old English *forsacan, from for- ("completely") + sacan ("to strive against, oppose").  The meaning evolved from "to renounce" or "to reject" to its modern sense of abandoning or deserting.
