crinoidea:/kraɪˈnɔɪdiə/| n. |海百合类，海百合|Sea lilies and feather stars are examples of crinoids.  They are marine animals belonging to the class Crinoidea within the phylum Echinodermata.  Crinoidea are characterized by their feathery or branching arms, which they use to filter feed. They are found in various marine habitats, from shallow waters to deep seas. |The fossil record of crinoids is extensive, providing valuable insights into past marine ecosystems.|近义词：echinoderm (更广义的分类)|反义词：无
