party-pooping:/ˈpɑːrtiˌpuːpɪŋ/| adj. |扫兴的，破坏乐趣的|  Describes someone or something that spoils the fun or enjoyment of a party or social gathering.  It often implies a deliberate or unintentional act that dampens the festive atmosphere. | Example:  He's such a party-pooper; he always complains about the music and the food. 他真是个扫兴的人；他总是抱怨音乐和食物。|近义词: killjoy, spoilsport, wet blanket|反义词:life of the party, fun-loving
