criminally:/ˈkrɪmɪnəli/| adv.| 犯罪地；极度地；非常地 |  In a criminal manner;  in a way that is extremely bad or shocking.  It describes actions or situations characterized by crime or extreme negativity. |例句：He was criminally negligent in his duties. 他在履行职责方面严重失职。  The project was criminally underfunded. 这个项目严重缺乏资金。|近义词：wickedly, outrageously, excessively, severely|反义词：innocently, rightly, justly
