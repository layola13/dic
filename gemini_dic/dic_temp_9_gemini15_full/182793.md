repatterning:/ˌriːˈpætərnɪŋ/ | n. | 重塑模式；重新规划；改变模式 | The process of changing or reorganizing a pattern, system, or structure.  This often involves breaking down existing structures and creating new ones. It's used in various fields like therapy, business, and technology to describe the restructuring of something to achieve a desired outcome. | Example:  The therapist guided the patient through a process of repatterning negative thought processes.  治疗师引导病人经历了一个重塑消极思维模式的过程。|近义词：restructuring, reorganization, reforming |反义词：maintaining, preserving, perpetuating | 名词
