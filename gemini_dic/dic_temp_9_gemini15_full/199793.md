sismometer:/ˈsaɪzmɒmɪtər/| n. |地震仪|An instrument that measures and records ground motion, typically during an earthquake.|例句：Scientists use sismometers to detect and study earthquakes.  科学家们使用地震仪来探测和研究地震。|近义词：seismograph|反义词:None
