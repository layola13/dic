hydras:/ˈhaɪdrəz/ | n. |九头蛇；水螅 | A hydra is a small, freshwater animal with a simple body plan and remarkable regenerative abilities.  It is often used as a model organism in biological research due to its ability to reproduce asexually by budding and its capacity for regeneration.  The mythical Hydra was a multi-headed serpent slain by Heracles in Greek mythology. |例句：Scientists study hydras to understand cell regeneration. 科学家研究水螅以了解细胞再生。

例句：The mythical Hydra was a fearsome beast.  神话中的九头蛇是一个可怕的野兽。
