deterrer:/dɪˈtɜːrər/| n.|威慑物；劝阻者|Something or someone that deters.|The threat of severe punishment acts as a deterrer to crime.  严厉的惩罚威胁可以作为犯罪的威慑因素。|近义词：discouragement, preventative, restraint|反义词：encouragement, incentive|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The new security system acts as a deterrer. = 新的安全系统起到了威慑作用。
This policy is a strong deterrer to illegal activities. = 这一政策是对非法活动的强烈威慑。


一般过去时:
The high prison sentence served as a deterrer to potential offenders. = 严厉的监禁判决对潜在的罪犯起到了威慑作用。
The public awareness campaign was a successful deterrer. = 公众意识宣传活动取得了成功，起到了威慑作用。


现在完成时:
Increased police presence has been a deterrer in this neighborhood. = 增强的警力在这个社区已经起到了威慑作用。
The new law has acted as a deterrer against tax evasion. = 新法律已经对逃税行为起到了威慑作用。


过去完成时:
The previous government's tough stance had been a deterrer against corruption. = 前政府强硬的态度已经对腐败起到了威慑作用。
The threat of war had acted as a deterrer to further conflict. = 战争的威胁已经对进一步的冲突起到了威慑作用。


将来时:
The new security measures will be a deterrer to future attacks. = 新的安全措施将对未来的袭击起到威慑作用。
This policy will act as a strong deterrer against fraud. = 这一政策将对欺诈行为起到强有力的威慑作用。


虚拟语气:
If we had a stronger deterrer, crime rates would be lower. = 如果我们有更强的威慑力，犯罪率就会更低。
I wish there were a more effective deterrer against cybercrime. = 我希望有一种更有效的措施来阻止网络犯罪。


被动语态:
The potential penalties are designed to be a deterrer. = 潜在的惩罚旨在起到威慑作用。
Effective deterrents are needed to discourage violence.  = 需要有效的威慑措施来制止暴力行为。


疑问句:
What is the best deterrer against shoplifting? = 制止商店扒窃的最佳威慑措施是什么？
Is the fear of punishment a sufficient deterrer? = 对惩罚的恐惧是否足够起到威慑作用？


否定句:
The current penalties are not a sufficient deterrer. = 目前的惩罚不足以起到威慑作用。
The lack of consequences was not a deterrer to the criminals. = 后果的缺乏并没有对罪犯起到威慑作用。


PHRASE->
a strong deterrer = 强有力的威慑
an effective deterrer = 有效的威慑
a potential deterrer = 潜在的威慑
a credible deterrer = 可信的威慑
a primary deterrer = 主要威慑


词源:
源自动词deter，意为“阻止，劝退”，加上-er构成名词，表示“阻止者，威慑物”。  deter本身源自古法语detourner，意为“转移，使偏离”。
