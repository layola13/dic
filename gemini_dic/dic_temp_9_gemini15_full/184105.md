resinaceous:/ˌrɛzɪˈneɪʃəs/| adj.| 树脂的；含树脂的 | Resinous; containing or resembling resin.  The word describes substances or materials that are composed of, or characterized by, the presence of resin.  | Example: The resinaceous fragrance of the pine forest filled the air. 松树林里树脂的香味充满了空气。 |近义词：resinous, gummy, pitchy |反义词：resin-free, non-resinous
