redeclares:/ˌriːdɪˈklɛərz/| vt.|重新声明；再次宣布|To state or declare something again, often with emphasis or after a previous statement has been challenged or questioned.  It implies a formal or official act of restating something.|例句：The government redeclared its commitment to reducing poverty. 政府再次声明其致力于减贫。|例句：After the initial announcement was met with skepticism, the company redeclared its intentions. 在最初的声明受到怀疑后，该公司再次声明其意图。|近义词：reassert, reaffirm, reiterate|反义词：retract, deny, withdraw|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redeclares his innocence every chance he gets. = 他抓住每一个机会重新声明自己的清白。
The company redeclares its commitment to sustainability annually. = 公司每年都会重新声明其对可持续发展的承诺。

一般过去时:
She redeclared her support for the candidate after the debate. = 辩论之后，她再次表示支持该候选人。
The witness redeclared his testimony under oath. = 证人在宣誓下重新作证。

现在完成时:
The president has redeclared a state of emergency. = 总统已再次宣布进入紧急状态。
They have redeclared their allegiance to the crown. = 他们已经再次宣誓效忠王室。

过去完成时:
He had redeclared his intentions before the meeting concluded. = 会议结束前，他已经再次声明了他的意图。
She had redeclared her love for him many times. = 她已经多次重新表白对他的爱。

将来时:
The court will redeclare the verdict after reviewing the evidence. = 法院将在审查证据后重新宣判。
He will redeclare his candidacy next week. = 他下周将再次宣布参选。

虚拟语气:
If he redeclared his innocence, would anyone believe him? = 如果他再次声明自己的清白，有人会相信他吗？
I wish he had redeclared his support for the project. = 我希望他曾再次表示支持该项目。

被动语态:

His intentions were redeclared in a formal statement. = 他的意图在一份正式声明中再次被阐明。

否定句:
