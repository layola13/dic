greyscale:/ˈɡreɪskeɪl/| n. adj.|灰度；灰阶；黑白的|A greyscale image uses shades of grey, ranging from black to white, to represent the image.  It does not use color.|例句：The photograph was in greyscale. 这张照片是灰度的。|例句：The monitor supports greyscale and color displays. 监视器支持灰度和彩色显示。|近义词：monochrome, black and white|反义词：color, full-color|名词，形容词
