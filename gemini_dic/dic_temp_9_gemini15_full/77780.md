generalised:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzd/|/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzd/| adj.|普遍的；一般的；概括的|  Having been made general; common or widespread.  It implies a lack of specific detail or a focus on common characteristics rather than unique ones. |The study showed a generalised increase in anxiety levels among teenagers.|近义词：general, common, widespread, universal|反义词：specific, particular, detailed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher generalised the lesson to apply to all students. = 老师将这节课的内容泛化，使其适用于所有学生。
This approach generalises well to other problems. = 这种方法很好地推广到其他问题。


一般过去时:
He generalised his findings to the entire population. = 他将他的研究结果推广到整个人群。
She generalised about the behaviour of men. = 她对男人的行为作了概括性的描述。


现在完成时:
Scientists have generalised the theory to explain various phenomena. = 科学家们已经将这一理论泛化，以解释各种现象。
Researchers have generalised the results of the study. = 研究人员已经对研究结果进行了概括。


过去完成时:
They had generalised the problem before they started working on a solution. = 在他们开始着手解决问题之前，他们已经对问题进行了概括。
The author had generalised the characters to represent different archetypes.  = 作者将人物角色进行了概括，以代表不同的原型。


将来时:
We will generalise our marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. = 我们将推广我们的营销策略，以接触更广泛的受众。
The company plans to generalise the use of this new technology. = 公司计划推广使用这项新技术。


虚拟语气:
If he generalised his argument further, it would be more convincing. = 如果他进一步概括他的论点，它会更有说服力。
I wish he hadn't generalised so much about the situation. = 我希望他没有对这种情况进行如此多的概括。


被动语态:
The concept was generalised to encompass a wider range of applications. = 这个概念被推广以涵盖更广泛的应用范围。
The results have been generalised to the whole sample. = 结果已被推广到整个样本。


疑问句:
Can we generalise these findings to other contexts? = 我们能否将这些发现推广到其他语境？
Does this theory generalise to all cases? = 这一理论适用于所有情况吗？


否定句:
We cannot generalise about the causes of this phenomenon. = 我们不能对这种现象的原因进行概括。
The study did not generalise the findings to the entire population. = 这项研究没有将研究结果推广到整个人群。
