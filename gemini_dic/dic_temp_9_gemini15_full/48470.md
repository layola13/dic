deep:/diːp/| adj.|深的；深奥的；强烈的；深沉的|  Deep refers to something that extends far down or inward from the surface. It can also describe something intense or profound in feeling or meaning.  | 例句：The pool is very deep.  这个游泳池很深。| 例句：He has a deep understanding of philosophy. 他对哲学有深刻的理解。|近义词：profound, intense, thorough, abysmal |反义词：shallow, superficial, slight
