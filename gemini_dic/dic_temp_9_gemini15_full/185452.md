retuse:/rɪˈfjuːz/ | v. | 拒绝；回绝 | To refuse something means to say no to it, or to not accept it.  | He refused the offer of a job. 他拒绝了这份工作。She refused to answer his questions. 她拒绝回答他的问题。| accept, decline | agree, consent | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refuses to cooperate. = 他拒绝合作。
They refuse all offers of help. = 他们拒绝一切帮助。


一般过去时:
She refused my request. = 她拒绝了我的请求。
He refused to believe it. = 他拒绝相信它。


现在完成时:
I have refused several invitations this week. = 这周我已经拒绝了好几个邀请。
She has refused to comment on the situation. = 她拒绝就此事发表评论。


过去完成时:
He had refused to see the doctor until it was too late. = 他直到为时已晚才拒绝去看医生。
They had refused to admit their mistake. = 他们拒绝承认自己的错误。


将来时:
I will refuse any further requests. = 我将拒绝任何进一步的要求。
She will refuse to sign the contract. = 她将拒绝签署合同。


虚拟语气:
I would refuse even if they begged me. = 即使他们求我，我也会拒绝。
If I were you, I would refuse the offer. = 如果我是你，我会拒绝这个提议。


被动语态:
His request was refused. = 他的请求被拒绝了。
My offer was refused by them. = 我的提议被他们拒绝了。


疑问句:
Will you refuse to help me? = 你会拒绝帮助我吗？
Did he refuse the job offer? = 他拒绝了工作邀请吗？


否定句:
He didn't refuse to help. = 他没有拒绝帮忙。
She didn't refuse the gift. = 她没有拒绝礼物。


PHRASE->
refuse to comply = 拒绝遵守
refuse entry = 拒绝入境
refuse point-blank = 断然拒绝
refuse treatment = 拒绝治疗
refuse an offer = 拒绝一个提议
