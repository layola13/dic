gauging:/ˈɡeɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|测量，估计，判断|The act of measuring or estimating something.|例句：He was gauging the depth of the water before he jumped in. 他在跳入水中之前测量了水的深度。
例句：I'm trying to gauge her reaction to my proposal. 我正在试着判断她对我提议的反应。|近义词：measuring, estimating, assessing, judging|反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are gauging public opinion on the new policy. = 他们在评估公众对这项新政策的意见。
We gauge the distance between two objects. = 我们测量两个物体之间的距离。


一般过去时:
The engineer gauged the pressure in the pipe. = 工程师测量了管道内的压力。
She gauged the size of the room before deciding to buy the furniture. = 她在决定购买家具之前估算了房间的大小。


现在完成时:
They have gauged the impact of the advertising campaign. = 他们已经评估了广告宣传的影响。
We have gauged the public's mood towards the election. = 我们已经探测了公众对选举的情绪。


过去完成时:
He had gauged the situation before he made his move. = 他在采取行动之前已经评估了形势。
They had gauged the depth of the river before attempting to cross it. = 他们在尝试过河之前已经测量了河的深度。


将来时:
We will gauge the effectiveness of the treatment next week. = 我们下周将评估治疗的有效性。
They will gauge the market demand before launching the product. = 他们将在推出产品之前评估市场需求。


虚拟语气:
If I could gauge the future, I would be a rich man. = 如果我能预测未来，我就会成为富翁。
I wish I could gauge his true intentions. = 我希望我能判断出他的真实意图。


被动语态:
The water level was gauged regularly. = 水位定期测量。
Public opinion is being gauged through surveys. = 通过调查正在评估公众舆论。


疑问句:
How do you gauge the success of a project? = 你如何衡量一个项目的成功？
How are they gauging the risk involved? = 他们如何评估所涉及的风险？


否定句:
He couldn't gauge the distance accurately. = 他无法准确地测量距离。
We failed to gauge the potential impact of the decision. = 我们未能评估该决定的潜在影响。
