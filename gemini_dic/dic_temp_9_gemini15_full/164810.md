prefilled:/ˌpriːˈfɪld/| adj.|预先填写的，预先填充的|Already filled or completed before being used or presented.  It implies that something has been filled in advance, often to save time or effort.|例句：The online form was prefilled with my personal information.  在线表格已预先填写了我的个人信息。

例句：The survey was prefilled with the respondent's name and address.  调查问卷已预先填写了受访者的姓名和地址。


近义词：pre-completed, prepared, filled in advance|反义词：blank, empty, unfilled|词性：形容词
