hardwire:/ˈhɑːrdwaɪər/| vt. n. adj.|硬接线，预设；固化；|to connect (something, especially electronic equipment) directly by wires, rather than using a wireless connection or software.|例句：The system is hardwired to the alarm system.  这个系统与报警系统硬连线。
The company's culture is hardwired into its operations. 公司文化已融入其运营中。|近义词：wire, connect, embed|反义词：software, wireless
