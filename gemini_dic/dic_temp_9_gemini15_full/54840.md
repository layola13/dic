displaceable: /dɪsˈpleɪsəbl/ | adj. | 可取代的，可移动的，可置换的 | Capable of being displaced or replaced. Something that is displaceable can be moved from its position or replaced by something else.  | The old factory equipment is displaceable with more modern machinery. 旧工厂设备可以用更现代化的机器取代。  The water in the glass is displaceable by the ice cubes. 杯子里的水可以被冰块取代。| replaceable, removable, substitutable | irreplaceable, fixed, immovable |  形容词
