klutzes:/ˈklʌtsɪz/| n. |笨手笨脚的人，笨拙的人 |A klutz is a clumsy or awkward person.  They often trip, drop things, or generally lack coordination. |例句：He's such a klutz; he tripped over the rug again. 他真是个笨手笨脚的人，他又绊倒在地毯上了。  She's a bit of a klutz in the kitchen.  她在厨房里有点笨手笨脚的。|近义词：clumsy person, butterfingers, oaf |反义词：graceful person, dexterous person
