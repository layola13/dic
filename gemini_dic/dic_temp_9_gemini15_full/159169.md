podiatrists:/poʊˈdaɪ.ə.trɪsts/| n. |足病医生，足科医生 |Professionals who diagnose and treat conditions affecting the feet and ankles.  They often specialize in areas such as biomechanics, sports medicine, and diabetic foot care. |例句：My podiatrist recommended custom orthotics to alleviate my heel pain. 我的足病医生建议我定制矫形器来缓解我的脚跟疼痛。

例句：Many athletes visit podiatrists regularly to prevent injuries. 许多运动员定期去看足病医生以预防受伤。

近义词：foot doctor, chiropodist (less common in the US) |反义词：N/A

词性：名词
