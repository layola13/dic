pairings:/ˈpeərɪŋz/| n. |配对；组合；搭配 |A pairing is a set of two things that are connected or associated in some way.  It can refer to two items used together, two people in a relationship, or combinations of things in general.  The word often implies a deliberate or intentional connection. |例句：The wine pairings with the meal were excellent.  这顿饭的葡萄酒搭配非常棒。

The study examined the pairings of different bird species. 这项研究考察了不同鸟类的配对情况。


近义词：combinations, matches, couplings, duos, sets |反义词：separations, divisions, uncoupling
