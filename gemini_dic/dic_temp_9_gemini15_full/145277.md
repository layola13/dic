overborrow:/ˌoʊvərˈbɒroʊ/| vt.|过度借贷，借贷过多|To borrow more money than is prudent or necessary, often leading to financial difficulties.|例句：The company overborrowed to finance its expansion, leading to bankruptcy. 公司过度借贷以资助其扩张，最终导致破产。

例句：He overborrowed on his credit cards and now faces mounting debt. 他信用卡透支过多，现在面临越来越多的债务。


近义词：excessively borrow, overextend oneself financially|反义词：underborrow, save, conserve


词性：动词
