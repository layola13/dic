goddamn:/ˈɡɑːdˌdæm/| adj., adv., interj.|该死的，可恶的；非常；该死|An intensifying adjective or adverb used to express anger, frustration, or emphasis.  It is a vulgar term and should be used cautiously. |例句：That goddamn dog keeps barking!  = 那条该死的狗不停地叫！
That was a goddamn masterpiece! = 那真是个了不起的杰作！|近义词：damn, blasted, cursed, infernal|反义词:blessed, wonderful, amazing
