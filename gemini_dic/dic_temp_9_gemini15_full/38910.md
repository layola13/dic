confessing:/kənˈfɛsɪŋ/| v.|承认，坦白|The act of admitting to a fault, crime, or wrongdoing.  It often implies guilt or shame.|例句：He confessed to stealing the money. 他承认偷了钱。

例句：She confessed her love for him. 她承认爱他。


近义词：admitting, acknowledging, revealing, divulging|反义词：denying, concealing, hiding|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confesses his mistakes regularly. = 他定期承认自己的错误。
She confesses her feelings to her friend. = 她向朋友倾诉她的感情。


一般过去时:
He confessed his guilt to the police. = 他向警方承认了自己的罪行。
She confessed her sins to the priest. = 她向牧师忏悔了她的罪过。


现在完成时:
He has confessed to the crime. = 他已经承认了罪行。
She has confessed all her secrets. = 她已经坦白了她所有的秘密。


过去完成时:
He had confessed his involvement before the trial. = 他在审判前已经承认了他的参与。
She had confessed her fears to her therapist. = 她已经向她的治疗师倾诉了她的恐惧。


将来时:
He will confess his wrongdoing soon. = 他很快就会承认自己的错误。
She will confess everything to her parents. = 她会把一切都告诉她的父母。


虚拟语气:
If he confessed, he would feel better. = 如果他坦白了，他会感觉好一些。
I wish she had confessed earlier. = 我希望她早点坦白。


被动语态:
His guilt was confessed to the court. = 他的罪行在法庭上被承认了。
The truth was confessed by the suspect. = 真相被嫌疑人承认了。


疑问句:
Will he confess his crime? = 他会承认他的罪行吗？
Has she confessed her feelings yet? = 她已经坦白她的感情了吗？


否定句:
He did not confess to the murder. = 他没有承认谋杀。
She refused to confess her involvement. = 她拒绝承认她的参与。


PHRASE->
confess to sth = 承认某事
confess one's sins = 忏悔
confess to doing sth = 承认做了某事
make a confession = 坦白
public confession = 公开承认
