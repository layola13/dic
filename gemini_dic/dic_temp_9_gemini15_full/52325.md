diclinic: /daɪˈklɪnɪk/ | adj. |  具有两性花的；雌雄异花的 |  Relating to or characterized by having flowers with both male and female reproductive organs (hermaphrodite) or separate male and female flowers on the same plant (monoecious).  The term often implies a distinction from the more general term "hermaphrodite," suggesting a specific arrangement or structure within the plant. |  The diclinic flowers of the oak tree are a key feature of its reproductive strategy.  | 近义词: monoecious, unisexual | 反义词: hermaphroditic, monoclinous
