carpenters:/ˈkɑːrpəntərz/| n.|木匠，木工|A carpenter is a skilled craftsperson who works with wood to construct or repair buildings, furniture, and other items.  They use a variety of tools and techniques to shape, cut, and join wood.|例句：My uncle is a carpenter and built our kitchen cabinets.  我的叔叔是个木匠，他做了我们厨房的橱柜。
The carpenter skillfully repaired the damaged window frame. 木匠熟练地修理了损坏的窗框。|近义词：joiner, woodworker|反义词：architect (in some contexts)
