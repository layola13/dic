prereversal: /ˌpriːrɪˈvɜːrsəl/ | adj. | 反转前的，逆转之前的 |  Describing something that occurs or exists before a reversal or turning point.  It often refers to a state, condition, or action that precedes a change in direction, status, or trend. |


例句：

The prereversal market conditions were characterized by high volatility. = 反转前的市场状况以高波动性为特征。

Prereversal symptoms of the disease are often subtle and easily overlooked. = 该病的反转前症状通常很细微，很容易被忽视。


近义词：preceding, antecedent, prior, precursory

反义词：postreversal, subsequent


词性:形容词
