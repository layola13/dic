prehensor:/prɪˈhensər/| n. |抓握器；具有抓握能力的器官|A prehensor is an organ or appendage used for grasping or seizing. It typically refers to the hands and feet of primates, but can also describe similar structures in other animals.  | Example:  The monkey used its prehensile tail as a prehensor to swing through the trees.  这只猴子用它具有抓握能力的尾巴在树林中荡秋千。 |近义词：grasping organ, gripping appendage |反义词：None directly applicable, but the opposite would be a limb lacking grasping capabilities. |名词
