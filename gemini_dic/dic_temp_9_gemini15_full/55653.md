dithyramb:/ˈdɪθɪræm/| n. |狄俄尼索斯颂歌；狂想曲；热情洋溢的颂词 |A dithyramb is an ode or a lyric poem, especially one celebrating Dionysus, characterized by wild, passionate, and unrestrained expression.| The poet penned a dithyramb celebrating the beauty of the natural world.  这位诗人写了一首颂扬自然界美丽的狄俄尼索斯颂歌。| ode, paean, rhapsody |  prose, calm description | 名词


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "hello"  The usage focuses on its application in different sentence structures.


Simple Sentence:
The poem was a vibrant dithyramb. = 这首诗是一首充满活力的狄俄尼索斯颂歌。

Complex Sentence:
His speech, a dithyramb of praise for the new leader, was met with enthusiastic applause. = 他那篇对新领导人赞不绝口的热情洋溢的颂词赢得了热烈的掌声。


Compound Sentence:
The play was a dithyramb; it was also a commentary on society. = 这部戏剧是一首狂想曲；它也是对社会的评论。

Sentence with Adjective:
A powerful dithyramb filled the concert hall. = 一首强有力的狄俄尼索斯颂歌充满了音乐厅。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "dithyramb".  Its use is largely standalone as a descriptive noun.
