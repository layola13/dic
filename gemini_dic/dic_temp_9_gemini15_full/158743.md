plowed:/plaʊd/| vt.|耕地；犁地；费力地前进；|to turn over and break up the soil of (land) with a plow; to move through or over (something) with difficulty or effort.|例句：The farmer plowed the field before planting the seeds.  农民在播种前耕了地。

例句：The ship plowed through the heavy seas. 船费力地航行在汹涌的大海中。|近义词：till, furrow, plough (BrE)|反义词：harvest
