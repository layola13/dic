lemmata:/ˈlɛmətɑː/| n. |词条；词目；引文；主题句|A lemmata is a headword in a dictionary or lexicon, typically accompanied by its definition, pronunciation, and other relevant information.  It's the basic unit of a lexicon.  The plural form is lemmata or lemmas. |Example:  The dictionary entry for "run" is a lemmata.|近义词：headword, entry, citation |反义词：无
