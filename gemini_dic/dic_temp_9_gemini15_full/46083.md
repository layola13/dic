cyclops:/ˈsaɪklɒps/| n. |独眼巨人|A mythical creature with a single eye in the middle of its forehead.  Cyclopes are often depicted as giants with immense strength.  They are found in Greek mythology and other cultures.|例句：The Cyclops Polyphemus was famously imprisoned by Odysseus.  独眼巨人波吕斐摩斯被奥德修斯囚禁的故事广为人知。|近义词：giant, monster|反义词:N/A
