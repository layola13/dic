hellenian:/hɛˈliːniən/| adj. n.| 希腊的；希腊人；|Of or relating to Greece or its people, language, or culture.  The term often refers to ancient Greece, but can also be used to refer to modern Greece and its culture.|The Hellenian civilization was renowned for its philosophy and art. 希腊文明以其哲学和艺术而闻名。|Greek, Grecian|barbarian, non-Greek|adjective, noun
