hazardable:/ˈhæzədəbl/| adj.|可冒险的；有危险的；可能危险的 |Capable of being risked or endangered; involving risk or danger.  The term often implies a degree of uncertainty or unpredictability regarding the outcome.  It suggests that a situation or action could potentially lead to negative consequences, but doesn't necessarily guarantee them. |例句：The investment is hazardable, but the potential returns are high. 这项投资有风险，但潜在回报也很高。

The expedition into the uncharted territory was hazardable, but the scientists were determined to proceed.  对未知领地的探险是冒险的，但科学家们决心继续前进。|近义词：risky, dangerous, perilous, precarious |反义词：safe, secure, harmless
