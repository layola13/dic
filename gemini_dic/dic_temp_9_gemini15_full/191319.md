scenic:/ˈsiːnɪk/| adj.|风景优美的，景色宜人的|relating to or involving visually attractive scenery.|The scenic route took us through rolling hills and charming villages. 这条风景优美的路线带我们穿过连绵起伏的丘陵和迷人的村庄。|picturesque, beautiful, breathtaking|ugly, unpleasant, unattractive|形容词
