genealogist:/ˌdʒiːniˈælədʒɪst/| n. |系谱学家；家谱学家 |A genealogist is a person who studies family history and pedigrees.  They trace lineages and create family trees. |例句：The genealogist traced my family back to the 17th century.  这位家谱学家将我的家族追溯到17世纪。

例句：A professional genealogist can help you find lost relatives. 一位专业的家谱学家可以帮助你找到失散的亲属。


近义词：family historian, lineage tracer| 反义词：无
