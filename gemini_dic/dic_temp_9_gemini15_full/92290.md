hyphenations:/ˌhaɪfəˈneɪʃənz/| n. |分词法，连字号用法|The process or practice of dividing words into syllables with hyphens, especially in dictionaries or to aid pronunciation or readability.  It also refers to the instances of hyphenation in a text.|Example: The dictionary provides detailed hyphenations for difficult words.  这本词典为难词提供了详细的分词法。|近义词：syllabification, word division|反义词：N/A
