inestimable: /ɪˈnɛstɪməbl/ | adj. | 无法估量的；极其宝贵的 |  incapable of being estimated or valued; priceless.  | The experience was of inestimable value to her future career.  这次经历对她未来的职业生涯具有无法估量的价值。 | invaluable, priceless, immeasurable, beyond price | worthless, valueless


USAGE->

The artwork is inestimable. = 这件艺术品是无法估量的。
Her contribution to the project is inestimable. = 她对项目的贡献是无法估量的。
