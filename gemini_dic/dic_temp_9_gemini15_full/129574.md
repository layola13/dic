non-chronic: /ˌnɒnˈkrɒnɪk/ | adj. |非慢性的，非持续性的| Not chronic; not of long duration or frequent recurrence.  It refers to conditions or illnesses that are not long-lasting or persistent.  The opposite of chronic. |例句：The doctor determined that her pain was non-chronic and likely caused by a temporary inflammation.  医生判断她的疼痛是非慢性的，可能是由暂时性炎症引起的。


The symptoms were non-chronic, resolving within a week. 症状是非慢性的，在一周内就消失了。


近义词：acute, short-term, transient, temporary |反义词：chronic, persistent, long-term, recurring
