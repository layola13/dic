/siːˈtiː/ | n. | 城市；都市 | A city is a large and densely populated urban area.  | 例句：London is a vibrant city.  伦敦是一个充满活力的城市。 | 例句：Many people move from rural areas to cities for job opportunities. 许多人从农村地区搬到城市寻找工作机会。 | 近义词：town, metropolis, urban area | 反义词：countryside, rural area
