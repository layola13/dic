refrigerators:/rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪtərz/ | n. |冰箱 |A kitchen appliance that uses refrigeration to keep food and drinks cold and prevent spoilage.  Refrigerators work by using a refrigerant to absorb heat from inside the unit and release it outside. |例句：We need a new refrigerator; this one is too old and inefficient. 我们需要一台新冰箱；这台太旧而且效率低下了。

例句：The refrigerator is humming quietly in the kitchen. 冰箱在厨房里轻轻地嗡嗡作响。


近义词：icebox, cooler, fridge


反义词：heater, oven


词性: 名词


词源:  The word "refrigerator" comes from the verb "refrigerate," which itself derives from the Latin word "refrigerare," meaning "to cool."  The suffix "-ator" denotes an agent or instrument, thus creating "refrigerator" to mean "something that cools."
