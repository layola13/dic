reimpregnate:/ˌriːɪmˈprɛɡneɪt/| vt.|再次使怀孕；再次浸渍|To impregnate again; to saturate thoroughly again.|例句：The artist reimpregnated the canvas with more paint.  艺术家再次用颜料浸渍画布。

例句：The farmer reimpregnated the soil with fertilizer after the harvest.  收获后，农民再次用肥料浸渍土壤。


近义词：resaturate, resoften, reinvigorate


反义词：dehydrate, dry out


词性:动词
