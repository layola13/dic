chomped:/tʃɑːmpt/| vt.|用力咀嚼；咬断|To bite or chew forcefully and noisily.|例句：The dog chomped on the bone.  那条狗用力啃着骨头。|例句：He chomped through the apple in a few bites. 他几口就用力咬掉了苹果。|近义词：chew, gnaw, munch|反义词：sip, nibble


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chomps on his food. = 他用力咀嚼他的食物。
She chomps her gum loudly. = 她大声地嚼着口香糖。

一般过去时:
The bear chomped the fish. = 熊用力咬了那条鱼。
He chomped down on the sandwich. = 他用力咬着三明治。

现在完成时:
The dog has chomped through the leash. = 狗已经咬断了绳子。
She has chomped her way through half the cake. = 她已经吃掉了半块蛋糕。

过去完成时:
The child had chomped all the cookies before his parents came. = 孩子在他父母来之前已经吃掉了所有的饼干。
They had chomped their way through a whole pizza. = 他们已经吃掉了一整个披萨。

将来时:
He will chomp on the carrot. = 他将啃胡萝卜。
The horse will chomp at the bit. = 马会咬住马嚼子。

虚拟语气:
If he were hungry, he would chomp on that apple. = 如果他饿了，他会啃那个苹果。
I wish I hadn't chomped on that spicy pepper. = 我真希望我没有吃那个辣辣椒。

被动语态:
The apple was chomped by the rabbit. = 苹果被兔子咬了。
The rope was chomped in two. = 绳子被咬断了。

疑问句:
Did the child chomp the toy? = 孩子咬了玩具吗？
Is the dog chomping on something? = 狗在啃什么东西吗？

否定句:
The baby didn't chomp the food. = 宝宝没有嚼食物。
He didn't chomp on the hard candy. = 他没有嚼硬糖。
