perverse:/pərˈvɜːrs/| adj.| 乖张的；反常的；故意作对的 | Contrary to what is expected, desired, or considered to be right or proper; stubbornly refusing to obey or comply.  It suggests a deliberate defiance or opposition to what is considered normal or acceptable. | 例句：He took a perverse pleasure in upsetting people. 他以扰乱别人为乐。(故意为之)

He showed a perverse disregard for safety regulations. 他表现出对安全规章的故意漠视。(故意违反)


近义词：contrary, wayward, obstinate, intractable, willful, deviant

反义词：obedient, compliant, docile, amenable, conventional
