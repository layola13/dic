miserableness:/ˈmɪzərəblnis/| n.|悲惨；痛苦；可怜|The state or quality of being miserable; wretchedness; unhappiness.  It describes a state of extreme unhappiness, discomfort, or destitution.|例句：The miserableness of his life was evident in his gaunt face.  他脸上憔悴的神情显露出了他悲惨的生活。


例句：The miserableness of the situation weighed heavily on her.  这种情况的悲惨让她感到沉重。|近义词：wretchedness, unhappiness, misery, distress|反义词：happiness, joy, bliss, contentment|词性: 名词
