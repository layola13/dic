climatologically: /ˌklaɪmətəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adj. | 气候学上地，从气候学的角度 | Relating to or in accordance with climatology.  The study of climates and their phenomena. |


例句：

Climatologically, this region is characterized by a dry season and a wet season.  气候学上，这个地区的特点是旱季和雨季。

The data was analyzed climatologically to determine long-term trends. 数据经过气候学分析以确定长期趋势。


近义词：meteorologically (in terms of meteorology)

反义词：None (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with terms like geographically or biologically)


词性：形容词
