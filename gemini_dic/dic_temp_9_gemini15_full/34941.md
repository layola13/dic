cloches:/ˈklɒʃɪz/| n.|钟罩；玻璃罩|A cloche is a bell-shaped cover, typically made of glass or similar material, used to protect plants from frost or harsh weather.  It can also refer to a woman's close-fitting hat. |例句：The gardener used cloches to protect the young seedlings from the frost. 园丁用钟罩保护幼苗免受霜冻。

The chef covered the dish with a cloche to keep it warm. 厨师用钟罩盖住菜肴以保持其温暖。|近义词：bell jar, cover, glass cover|反义词：open air
