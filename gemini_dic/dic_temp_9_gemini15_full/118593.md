mesozoic:/ˌmɛzoʊˈzoʊɪk/| adj. n.|中生代的；中生代 |The Mesozoic Era, also known as the Age of Reptiles, is a significant period in Earth's history spanning from approximately 252 to 66 million years ago. It is characterized by the dominance of dinosaurs and other reptiles, the breakup of the supercontinent Pangaea, and significant changes in plant and animal life.|The Mesozoic Era saw the rise and fall of the dinosaurs. = 中生代见证了恐龙的兴衰。|Paleozoic, Cenozoic|Precambrian|adjective
