The term often carries a connotation of artificiality or an idealized beauty. |例句：The little girl had doll-like features, with big eyes and rosy cheeks.  小女孩有着像洋娃娃一样的精致五官，大大的眼睛和红扑扑的脸颊。


例句：Her doll-like porcelain skin was almost translucent. 她像瓷娃娃一样白皙的皮肤几乎是透明的。
