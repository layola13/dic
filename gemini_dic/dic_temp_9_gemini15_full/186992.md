risks:/rɪsks/| n.|风险，危险|The potential for loss or damage.|例句：We need to assess the risks before investing. 我们需要在投资前评估风险。|近义词：hazards, dangers, perils|反义词：safeties, securities|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He takes many risks in his job. = 他在工作中承担很多风险。
The project involves significant risks. = 该项目涉及重大风险。


一般过去时:
They underestimated the risks involved. = 他们低估了所涉及的风险。
She took a big risk by quitting her job. = 她辞职冒险很大。


现在完成时:
We have assessed all the risks. = 我们已经评估了所有风险。
They have avoided many risks this year. = 他们今年避免了许多风险。


过去完成时:
I had already considered the risks before making the decision. = 我在做决定之前就已经考虑过风险了。
She had overlooked some crucial risks in her plan. = 她在她计划中忽略了一些关键风险。


将来时:
We will mitigate the risks as much as possible. = 我们将尽量减轻风险。
The company will face many risks in the future. = 公司将来会面临许多风险。


虚拟语气:
If we had known the risks, we wouldn't have invested. = 如果我们知道风险，我们就不会投资了。
I wish I had considered the risks more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地考虑过风险。


被动语态:
The risks were carefully evaluated. = 风险得到了仔细评估。
All potential risks are being assessed. = 所有潜在风险正在评估中。


疑问句:
What are the risks associated with this project? = 与这个项目相关的风险是什么？
Are there any significant risks involved? = 有任何重大风险吗？


否定句:
There are no significant risks associated with this investment. = 这项投资没有重大风险。
He doesn't take unnecessary risks. = 他不承担不必要的风险。


PHRASE->
