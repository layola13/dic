outpopulates:/ˌaʊtˈpɑːpjuleɪts/ | vt. |超过…的数量；超过…的繁殖速度 | To exceed in number or rate of reproduction.  |例句：The rabbits outpopulated the local predators. 兔子数量超过了当地的捕食者。

The invasive species outpopulated the native plants. 这种入侵物种的数量超过了本地植物。

近义词：outnumber, surpass, exceed | 反义词：be outnumbered by, be surpassed by
