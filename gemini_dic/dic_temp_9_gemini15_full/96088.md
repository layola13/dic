indefeisible:/ɪnˈdɪfɪzəbl/| adj.|不可剥夺的；不可废除的；不容争辩的 | incapable of being annulled, refuted, or set aside.  It implies a right or claim that is so strong and certain that it cannot be challenged or overturned.  |例句：The court ruled that the contract was indefeisible. 法院裁定该合同不可废除。

例句：His claim to the throne was indefeisible. 他对王位的继承权不容置疑。


近义词：unassailable, incontrovertible, irrefutable, undeniable, absolute

反义词：revocable, assailable, disputable, questionable, refutable


词源：来自古法语 *indefeasible，来自拉丁语 *indefensablis，由 *in- (否定前缀) + *defensablis (可辩护的) 构成。
