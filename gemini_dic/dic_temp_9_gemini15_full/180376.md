refused:/rɪˈfjuːzd/ | vt. |拒绝 |To decline or reject (something offered, proposed, or requested).  |例句：He refused the offer of a job. 他拒绝了这份工作。|例句：She refused to answer my questions. 她拒绝回答我的问题。|近义词：rejected, declined, denied |反义词：accepted, agreed to |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refuses to cooperate. = 他拒绝合作。
They refuse my request. = 他们拒绝了我的请求。


一般过去时:
She refused his invitation. = 她拒绝了他的邀请。
He refused to believe me. = 他拒绝相信我。


现在完成时:
They have refused all offers. = 他们拒绝了一切提议。
She has refused to comment. = 她拒绝置评。


过去完成时:
He had refused to help before. = 他以前就拒绝过帮忙。
They had refused the deal earlier. = 他们之前已经拒绝了这笔交易。


将来时:
I think he will refuse the help. = 我认为他会拒绝帮助。
She will likely refuse to participate. = 她很可能会拒绝参加。


虚拟语气:
If he refused, what would you do? = 如果他拒绝了，你会怎么做？
I wish he hadn't refused my offer. = 我真希望他没有拒绝我的提议。


被动语态:
The offer was refused. = 提议被拒绝了。
His request was refused by the committee. = 他的请求被委员会拒绝了。


疑问句:
Did he refuse to go? = 他拒绝去了吗？
Will they refuse our application? = 他们会拒绝我们的申请吗？


否定句:
He didn't refuse my help. = 他没有拒绝我的帮助。
She didn't refuse to listen. = 她没有拒绝倾听。


PHRASE->
refuse to  = 拒绝…
refuse entry = 拒绝入境
refuse treatment = 拒绝治疗
refuse point-blank = 断然拒绝
refuse categorically = 坚决拒绝
