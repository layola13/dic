ricochets: /ˈrɪkəʃeɪz/ | v. | 反弹，跳跃 | To rebound or glance off a surface at an angle.  | The bullet ricocheted off the wall. 子弹击中墙壁后反弹。 The ball ricocheted across the court. 球在球场上反弹。 | bounce, rebound, skip |  (无明确反义词，取决于语境，例如：direct hit) | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ball ricochets off the wall. = 球从墙上反弹。
The bullet ricochets wildly. = 子弹四处乱飞。


一般过去时:
The stone ricocheted off the water. = 石头在水上反弹。
The sound ricocheted through the canyon. = 声音在峡谷中回荡。


现在完成时:
The bullet has ricocheted several times. = 子弹已经反弹了好几次。
The rumor has ricocheted around the office. = 谣言在办公室里四处传播。


过去完成时:
The ball had ricocheted before it landed. = 球在落地前已经反弹过了。
The news had ricocheted across the globe. = 这个消息已经在全球传播开来。


将来时:
The projectile will ricochet off the shield. = 抛射物将从护盾上反弹。
The light will ricochet around the room. = 光线将在房间内四处反射。


虚拟语气:
If the bullet ricocheted, it could have hit someone. = 如果子弹反弹，它可能会击中某人。
I wish the sound hadn't ricocheted so loudly. = 我希望声音没有那么大声地回荡。


被动语态:
The bullet was ricocheted off the target. = 子弹从目标上反弹了。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The sound was ricocheted through the mountains. = 声音在山间回荡。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Did the ball ricochet? = 球反弹了吗？
Will the shot ricochet? = 子弹会反弹吗？


否定句:
The bullet did not ricochet. = 子弹没有反弹。
The sound didn't ricochet off the walls. = 声音没有从墙上反弹。


PHRASE->
ricochet effect  反弹效应
ricochet shot  跳弹射击
ricochet off  从...反弹
ricocheting bullets  跳弹
a ricocheting conversation  跳跃式对话
