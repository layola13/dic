lie-about:/ˈlaɪ əˈbaʊt/ | v. | 闲逛；四处走动；说谎；散布谣言 | To move around aimlessly; to tell lies; to spread rumors.  | 例句：He spent the afternoon lying about the house. 他整个下午都在家里闲逛。  | 例句： Don't lie about your age. 不要谎报年龄。 | 近义词：wander, roam, stroll, fib, deceive | 反义词：stay put, be truthful, be honest | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lies about his accomplishments. = 他夸大自己的成就。
They lie about their whereabouts. = 他们对自己的行踪撒谎。


一般过去时:
She lied about seeing the accident. = 她撒谎说没看到事故。
He lied about his age to join the army. = 他为了参军谎报年龄。


现在完成时:
He has lied about his qualifications for years. = 他多年来一直在谎报自己的资历。
She has lied about her past relationship. = 她对过去的恋情撒谎。


过去完成时:
They had lied about their involvement before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，他们就对自己的参与撒谎了。
He had lied about his identity to avoid capture. = 他为了避免被捕而谎报了自己的身份。


将来时:
I will not lie about the incident. = 我不会对这件事撒谎。
They will lie about their plans to surprise her. = 他们会对他们的计划撒谎以给她一个惊喜。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't lie about it. = 如果我是你，我不会撒谎。
I wish I hadn't lied about my whereabouts. = 我真希望我没有对我的行踪撒谎。


被动语态:
His lies about his income were exposed. = 他关于收入的谎言被揭露了。
The truth about the accident was lied about by many witnesses. = 事故的真相被许多证人隐瞒了。


疑问句:
Were you lying about what happened? = 你在说谎吗？
Is he lying about his intentions? = 他在隐瞒他的意图吗？


否定句:
