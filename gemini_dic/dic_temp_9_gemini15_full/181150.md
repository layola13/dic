reinforced:/ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrst/| adj. v. | 加强的；加固的；强化了的 | Strengthened or made stronger.  Reinforced concrete is stronger than regular concrete because steel bars are added to it. |The bridge was reinforced with steel beams. 这座桥用钢梁加固了。
The teacher reinforced the lesson with extra examples. 老师用额外的例子强化了课程。 | strengthened, strengthened, bolstered | weakened, undermined |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They reinforce the walls with steel beams. = 他们用钢梁加固墙壁。
The company reinforces its commitment to sustainability. = 公司强化了其对可持续发展的承诺。

一般过去时:
The engineer reinforced the structure to withstand the earthquake. = 工程师加固了结构以承受地震。
She reinforced her argument with compelling evidence. = 她用令人信服的证据强化了她的论点。

现在完成时:
They have reinforced the security measures after the incident. = 发生事故后，他们加强了安全措施。
The government has reinforced its border controls. = 政府加强了边境管制。

过去完成时:
He had reinforced the fence before the storm arrived. = 他在暴风雨来临之前加固了栅栏。
They had reinforced their defenses before the enemy attacked. = 敌人进攻前，他们已加强了防御。

将来时:
We will reinforce the team with additional staff. = 我们将增加人员以加强团队。
The army will reinforce its position on the hill. = 军队将在山顶加强其阵地。

虚拟语气:
If they reinforced the bridge, it would be safer. = 如果他们加固桥梁，它会更安全。
I wish they had reinforced the roof before the snowstorm. = 我真希望他们在暴雪前加固了屋顶。

被动语态:
The building was reinforced with concrete pillars. = 这栋建筑物用混凝土柱加固。
The policy is being reinforced through stricter enforcement. = 该政策正在通过更严格的执行而得到强化。

疑问句:
Will the company reinforce its marketing strategy? = 公司会加强其营销策略吗？
Were the foundations reinforced before construction began? = 施工前地基是否加固了？

否定句:
They did not reinforce the dam in time. = 他们没有及时加固大坝。
The company has not reinforced its safety protocols. = 公司没有加强其安全规程。
