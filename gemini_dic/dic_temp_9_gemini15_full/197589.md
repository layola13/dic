shenanigans:/ʃəˈnænɪɡənz/| n. |恶作剧；胡闹；诡计| mischievous or playful tricks or activities;  underhand or dishonest activities.|例句：The kids were up to their usual shenanigans. 孩子们又在搞他们惯常的恶作剧了。

例句：The police suspected shenanigans in the election. 警方怀疑选举中存在舞弊行为。


近义词：pranks, tricks, mischief, antics, shenanigans


反义词：seriousness, propriety, honesty
