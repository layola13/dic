moither:/ˈmɔɪðə(r)/| v.|絮絮叨叨地唠叨；喋喋不休地抱怨|To talk or complain incessantly and tiresomely; to fuss or bother excessively. |例句：She moithered him about his messy room. 她唠叨他房间乱糟糟的。|例句：He moithered over the small details of the project. 他对项目的细枝末节喋喋不休。|近义词：nag,  pester,  fuss,  harangue |反义词：be quiet,  be silent
