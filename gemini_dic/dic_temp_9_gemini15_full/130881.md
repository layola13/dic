nonarmament: /ˌnɒnˈɑːrməmənt/ | n. |裁军；不武装；非军备 |The absence of weaponry or military preparedness.  It refers to a state or policy of not having or using weapons, or a movement advocating for such a state.|例句：The country's policy of nonarmament was praised by many international organizations. 这个国家的裁军政策受到了许多国际组织的赞扬。

例句：Their commitment to nonarmament is a testament to their peaceful intentions. 他们致力于裁军，这证明了他们的和平意图。


近义词：disarmament, demilitarization, pacifism

反义词：armament, militarism, war


词性：名词
