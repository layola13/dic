gnarl:/nɑːrl/| n., v.| 瘤节；扭曲；使粗糙 | A knot in wood; a gnarled tree has many knots and twists in its branches.  The verb means to make something twisted or rough. | 例句：The old tree had gnarled branches reaching towards the sky. 这棵老树有着伸向天空的扭曲的树枝。

The wind gnarled the young sapling, bending it out of shape. 风使这棵幼苗扭曲变形。 | 近义词：knot, twist, deform | 反义词：smooth, straight
