embroiled:/ɪmˈbrɔɪld/ | vt. |卷入；使陷入；使纠缠 | to involve someone or something in a conflict or difficult situation | The family was embroiled in a bitter inheritance dispute.  这个家庭卷入了一场激烈的遗产纠纷。|近义词：involved, entangled, implicated |反义词：uninvolved, disentangled

一般现在时:
The scandal embroils several prominent politicians. 这场丑闻牵涉到几位政界要员。
He is embroiled in a complex legal battle. 他卷入了一场复杂的法律诉讼。


一般过去时:
The two countries were embroiled in a long and bloody war. 这两个国家卷入了一场漫长而血腥的战争。
The company was embroiled in a controversy over its environmental practices. 这家公司因其环保行为而卷入了一场争议。


现在完成时:
The government has been embroiled in this corruption scandal for months. 政府已经卷入这场腐败丑闻数月了。
She has been embroiled in a series of unfortunate events. 她一直卷入一系列不幸的事件。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, the lawyer had already been embroiled in a similar case. 在审判之前，这位律师已经卷入过类似的案件。
It turned out that he had been embroiled in illegal activities for years. 结果发现，他多年来一直参与非法活动。


将来时:
This new evidence suggests that the mayor will soon be embroiled in a scandal. 这项新证据表明市长很快就会卷入丑闻。
I fear that the upcoming election will embroil the country in further political turmoil. 我担心即将到来的选举会使国家陷入进一步的政治动荡。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't interfered, he wouldn't have been embroiled in this mess. 如果他不干涉，他就不会卷入这场混乱。
I wish I hadn't been embroiled in that argument. 我真希望我没有卷入那场争论。


被动语态:
The company was embroiled in a public relations crisis.  该公司陷入了一场公关危机。
He was embroiled in a dispute with his neighbor. 他与邻居发生了争执。


疑问句:
Is the company embroiled in any legal issues? 这家公司是否卷入了任何法律问题？
Were they embroiled in the conflict? 他们卷入了冲突吗？


否定句:
The investigation revealed that he was not embroiled in the crime. 调查显示他没有卷入犯罪。
She remained uninvolved and was not embroiled in the gossip. 她没有参与其中，也没有卷入流言蜚语。
