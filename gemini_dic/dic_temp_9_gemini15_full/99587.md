interinstitutional: /ˌɪntərˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənəl/ | adj. |机构间的；跨机构的| Relating to or involving more than one institution.  The term typically refers to relationships, agreements, or actions between different organizations, often governmental or educational. |例句：The project requires significant interinstitutional cooperation.  这个项目需要大量的机构间合作。|例句： Interinstitutional agreements are crucial for the smooth implementation of the policy. 机构间的协议对于政策的顺利实施至关重要。|近义词：interagency, cross-institutional|反义词：intra-institutional
