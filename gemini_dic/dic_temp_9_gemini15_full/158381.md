pleasant:/ˈplɛznt/| adj. |令人愉快的，舒适的 |Agreeable, enjoyable, or pleasing.  It suggests a feeling of comfort and satisfaction. |例句：The weather was pleasant and sunny. 天气晴朗宜人。

例句：We had a pleasant evening together. 我们一起度过了一个愉快的夜晚。


近义词：agreeable, delightful, enjoyable, nice, satisfying |反义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, awful, nasty


词源:  The word "pleasant" comes from the Old French word "plesant," which means "pleasing."  This in turn derives from the Latin word "placens," the present participle of "placere," meaning "to please" or "to be pleasing."


用法说明:

一般现在时:
The music is pleasant to listen to. = 这音乐听起来很悦耳。
The atmosphere in the room is pleasant. = 房间里的气氛很舒适。


一般过去时:
The trip was very pleasant. = 这趟旅行非常愉快。

被动语态:
