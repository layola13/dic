noncorruptibilities:/ˌnɒnkəˌrʌptɪˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |不可腐败性；清廉；正直 | The inherent qualities that resist corruption or decay.  The word refers to the state or quality of being incorruptible, the inability to be corrupted or influenced by bribery or dishonesty. It often refers to moral integrity and resistance to temptation. | Example: The investigation highlighted the noncorruptibilities of the judge, who remained steadfast in the face of immense pressure.  调查突显了法官的清廉正直，即使面临巨大的压力，他也始终坚定不移。|近义词: integrity, incorruptibility, honesty, uprightness |反义词: corruptibility, dishonesty, venality, depravity | 名词
