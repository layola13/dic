protrude:/prəˈtruːd/| v.|突出，伸出|To stick out or project from something.|The nail protruded from the wood.  钉子从木头里伸了出来。|The branches protruded from the dense foliage. 树枝从茂密的树叶中伸出来。|extend, project, jut|recess, indent


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The shelf protrudes from the wall. 架子从墙上突出。
His teeth protrude slightly. 他的牙齿略微突出。


一般过去时:
A rock protruded from the ground. 一块石头从地面突出。
The handle protruded from the bag.  把手从包里伸出来。


现在完成时:
Several nails have protruded from the board.  几颗钉子从木板上突出。
The truth has finally protruded. 真相最终大白。


过去完成时:
The branch had protruded into the road. 树枝已经伸到路上了。
The wire had protruded from the wall before the repair.  修理之前，电线已经从墙里伸出来了。


将来时:
The new building will protrude above the skyline. 新建筑将高耸于天际线之上。
The sun will protrude from behind the clouds soon. 太阳很快就会从云层后面露出来。


虚拟语气:
If the beam protruded further, it would be dangerous. 如果梁再伸出来一些，就会很危险。
I wish the handle didn't protrude so much. 我希望把手不要突出那么多。


被动语态:
The wires were protruded through the ceiling. 电线被穿过天花板。
The nail was protruded from the box. 钉子从箱子里伸了出来。


疑问句:

否定句:
