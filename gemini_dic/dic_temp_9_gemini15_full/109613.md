letter-size:/ˈlɛtər saɪz/| adj. |信纸大小的，标准信纸尺寸的|  Referring to the standard size of paper used for letters, typically 8.5 inches by 11 inches (216 mm × 279 mm) in the United States and other countries.  This size is commonly used for business correspondence, documents, and printing.|例句：The printer only accepts letter-size paper. 这台打印机只接受信纸大小的纸张。

例句：I need to print this document in letter-size. 我需要把这个文件打印成信纸大小。


近义词：standard size, 8.5x11


反义词：legal size, A4 size


词性:形容词
