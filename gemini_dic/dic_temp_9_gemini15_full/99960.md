interoperate:/ˌɪntərˈɒpəreɪt/| v.|互操作，交互操作|To work together or be compatible with other systems or components.  It implies a seamless exchange of information and functionality between different systems.|例句：The two software programs interoperate seamlessly, allowing for easy data transfer. 这两个软件程序可以无缝地互操作，从而可以轻松地进行数据传输。


The database system needs to interoperate with our existing infrastructure.  数据库系统需要与我们现有的基础设施互操作。|近义词：integrate, cooperate, interact|反义词：conflict, clash
