hamster:/ˈhæmstər/ | n. |仓鼠 | A small rodent with a short tail and fur, often kept as a pet.  Hamsters are known for their cheek pouches, which they use to store food. |例句：My daughter loves her pet hamster.  我的女儿非常喜欢她的宠物仓鼠。

例句：We bought a hamster cage and some sunflower seeds for the new hamster. 我们为新买的仓鼠买了一个笼子和一些葵花籽。


近义词：rodent, small pet | 反义词：None.
