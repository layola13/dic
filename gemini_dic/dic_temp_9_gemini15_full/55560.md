distrusting: /dɪsˈtrʌstɪŋ/ | adj. | 不信任的，怀疑的 | Showing or feeling a lack of trust; suspicious.  | 例句：He gave her a distrusting glance. 他给了她一个怀疑的眼神。

例句：Her distrusting nature made it difficult to form close relationships. 她不信任的性格使得她很难建立亲密的关系。


近义词：suspicious, skeptical, doubtful, wary, incredulous

反义词：trusting, believing, confident


词性：形容词
