feudatory:/ˈfjuːdəˌtɔːri/| n. adj.|封臣；受封者；封建的；|A person who holds land in fief or under feudal tenure; one who owes allegiance to a feudal lord.  A feudal system or state.|例句：Many feudal lords demanded absolute obedience from their feudatories. 许多封建领主要求他们的封臣绝对服从。

The king's feudatories were obligated to provide military service in times of war. 国王的封臣有义务在战争时期提供军事服务。


近义词：vassal, retainer, subject|反义词：sovereign, ruler, overlord|词性: noun, adjective
