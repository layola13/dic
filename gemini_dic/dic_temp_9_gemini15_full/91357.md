{hydros}: /ˈhaɪdroʊs/ | combining form | 水的；含水的；水生的 |  Combining form meaning "water," "hydrogen," or "hydroxide." It's used to form compound words related to water or its properties. |  Hydroelectric power uses the energy of moving water to generate electricity.  |  aqueous, aquatic | anhydrous | combining form


USAGE->
This is a combining form, not a standalone word, so it doesn't have verb conjugations or tenses.  It's used as a prefix in various words.  Examples are shown below in word formation.


PHRASE->
hydroelectric (水电的): Hydroelectric power plants are a clean source of energy. 水电站是清洁能源的来源。

hydrocarbon (碳氢化合物):  Fossil fuels are primarily composed of hydrocarbons.  化石燃料主要由碳氢化合物组成。

hydrophobic (疏水的):  Oil is a hydrophobic substance. 油是一种疏水性物质。

hydrotherapy (水疗):  She underwent hydrotherapy to relieve her muscle pain. 她接受了水疗来缓解肌肉疼痛。

hydroponics (水培法):  He grows tomatoes using hydroponics. 他用无土栽培法种植西红柿。
