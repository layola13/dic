drop:/drɒp/ /drɑːp/| n., v.|滴，落下；下降；放弃；投递|to fall or to allow something to fall by gravity; to let something fall; to decrease; to give up; to deliver something|The raindrop fell onto the leaf. 雨滴落到了叶子上。|The price of oil dropped sharply. 油价骤降。|近义词：fall, descend, relinquish, deliver|反义词：rise, ascend, keep, retain|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drops his keys every day. 他每天都掉钥匙。
The temperature drops in the evening. 晚上温度下降。


一般过去时:
She dropped the plate and it broke. 她把盘子掉了，盘子碎了。
The company dropped its advertising campaign. 公司放弃了它的广告宣传活动。


现在完成时:
The price of gas has dropped significantly.  汽油价格已经大幅下降。
He has dropped out of school. 他已经辍学了。


过去完成时:
They had dropped the subject before I arrived. 我到达之前他们已经不再谈论那个话题了。
The value of the stock had dropped before the news broke. 消息传出之前，股票价值已经下跌了。


将来时:
The government will drop the controversial bill. 政府将撤回有争议的法案。
The temperature will drop below freezing tonight. 今晚气温将降至冰点以下。


虚拟语气:
If I dropped the class, I would have more free time. 如果我退了这门课，我会更有空闲时间。
I wish I hadn't dropped the ball. 我真希望我没有犯这个错误。


被动语态:
The package was dropped off at the post office. 包裹已在邮局寄出。
The bomb was dropped on the city. 炸弹被投掷到城市。


疑问句:
Did he drop the ball? 他把球弄丢了吗？
Will the price drop further? 价格还会进一步下跌吗？


否定句:
He didn't drop the ball. 他没有把球弄丢。
The company did not drop its plans. 公司没有放弃它的计划。


PHRASE->
drop by: 顺便拜访
drop out: 退学；退出
drop off: 下降；送；让...下车
drop in: 顺便拜访
drop a hint: 暗示
