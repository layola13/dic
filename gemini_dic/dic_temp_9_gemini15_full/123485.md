monosyllable:/ˈmɒnəsɪləbəl/ /ˈmɑːnəˌsɪləbəl/ | n. |单音节词|A word having only one syllable.  A monosyllable is a word consisting of a single syllable. This means it is pronounced with a single uninterrupted sound.| Example:  The word "cat" is a monosyllable. 猫这个词是一个单音节词。|近义词：one-syllable word|反义词：polysyllable
