recumbents:/rɪˈkʌmbənts/| n.| 卧者；躺卧者；（尤指）自行车 | People or things lying down or reclining.  The term often refers to recumbent bicycles, which have a seat and pedals designed for a laid-back riding position. | Example: The recumbents on the beach were enjoying the sunshine. 海滩上的躺卧者们享受着阳光。 The new recumbent bicycle allows for a more comfortable ride.新型的卧式自行车骑起来更舒适。|近义词：prostrate, supine, prone|反义词：upright, erect|名词
