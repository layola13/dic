chamomile:/ˈkæməmaɪl/| n. |洋甘菊|A flowering plant of the daisy family, used to make a herbal tea known for its calming properties.  Chamomile tea is often drunk to promote relaxation and sleep.|Example: I drink chamomile tea every night to help me relax before bed.  我每天晚上喝洋甘菊茶来帮助我在睡觉前放松。|近义词：camomile|反义词:无
