quadruples:/ˈkwɑːdruːpəlz/| v.|使四倍；增至四倍|To multiply or increase by four times.|The company's profits quadrupled last year.  该公司去年的利润增长了四倍。|The population of the city quadrupled in just ten years.  这个城市的人口在短短十年内增长了四倍。|increase, multiply|decrease, halve

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price quadruples every year. = 价格每年翻两番。
He quadruples his efforts to finish the project. = 他加倍努力完成项目。

一般过去时:
The value quadrupled after the announcement. = 公告发布后，价值增长了四倍。
The company's stock quadrupled overnight. = 公司的股票一夜之间增长了四倍。

现在完成时:
The investment has quadrupled since we started. = 自从我们开始以来，投资已经增长了四倍。
My savings have quadrupled in the last decade. = 我的储蓄在过去十年中增长了四倍。

过去完成时:
The number of users had quadrupled by the end of the year. = 到年底，用户数量已增长了四倍。
The company's market share had quadrupled before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司的市场份额已经增长了四倍。

将来时:
The cost will quadruple by next year. = 到明年，成本将增长四倍。
They predict that the demand will quadruple in the coming years. = 他们预测未来几年需求将增长四倍。

虚拟语气:
If the investment quadrupled, we would be rich. = 如果投资增长四倍，我们就富有了。

被动语态:

The production capacity was quadrupled through automation. = 通过自动化，生产能力增长了四倍。

疑问句:
