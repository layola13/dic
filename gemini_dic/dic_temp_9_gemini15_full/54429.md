disinfective:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktɪv/| adj. n.|消毒剂；杀菌剂|An agent that destroys or inhibits the growth of microorganisms, especially harmful bacteria.  A disinfective is used to clean and sterilize surfaces and objects to prevent the spread of infection.|Example: The nurse used a strong disinfective to clean the wound. 护士用强效消毒剂清洗伤口。|近义词：antiseptic, germicide, sterilizer|反义词：contaminant
