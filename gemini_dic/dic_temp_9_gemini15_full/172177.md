pursuivant:/ˈpɜːsjuːvənt/ | n. |扈从；追随者；（纹章）王家侍从官 | A pursuivant is a follower or attendant, especially one in a position of authority, often historically associated with heraldry.  It also refers to someone who is diligently pursuing something. | Example: He was a loyal pursuivant of the king. 他是国王忠诚的扈从。  The detective was a pursuivant of justice.  这位侦探是正义的追随者。 |近义词：follower, attendant, follower,  |反义词：leader, opponent.
