slandered:/ˈslændərd/| vt.|诽谤，中伤|To make false and damaging statements about someone.|例句：He was slandered by his rivals in the election.  他在选举中被他的对手诽谤了。|例句：The newspaper article slandered the mayor's reputation. 这篇报纸文章诽谤了市长的名誉。|近义词：defame, libel, smear, calumniate|反义词：praise, commend, extol


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slanders his opponents frequently. = 他经常诽谤他的对手。
They slander the company's products. = 他们诽谤公司的产品。


一般过去时:
She slandered him in the newspaper. = 她在那家报纸上诽谤他。
The politician slandered his opponent during the debate. = 这位政治家在辩论中诽谤了他的对手。


现在完成时:
They have slandered my reputation for years. = 他们多年来一直在诽谤我的名誉。
He has slandered her to everyone he knows. = 他对他认识的每个人都诽谤了她。


过去完成时:
The rumours had slandered her good name before she could defend herself. = 在她能够为自己辩护之前，谣言就已经诽谤了她的好名声。
They had slandered the company's image for months. = 他们几个月来一直在诽谤公司的形象。


将来时:
They will slander him in the upcoming election. = 他们将在即将到来的选举中诽谤他。
She will slander her competitor on social media. = 她将在社交媒体上诽谤她的竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he slandered me, I would sue him. = 如果他诽谤我，我会起诉他。
I wish they hadn't slandered her. = 我希望他们没有诽谤她。


被动语态:
He was slandered by the media. = 他被媒体诽谤了。
Her reputation was slandered by false accusations. = 她的名声被不实的指控所诽谤。


疑问句:
Who slandered him? = 谁诽谤了他？
Was she slandered in the press? = 她是否在媒体上被诽谤了？


否定句:
He did not slander her. = 他没有诽谤她。
They didn't slander his character. = 他们没有诽谤他的品格。
