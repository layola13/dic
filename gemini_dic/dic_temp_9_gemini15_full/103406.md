jippo:/ˈdʒɪpoʊ/| n. |吉普车；小型聚会；临时活动|A jippo is a slang term for a small, informal gathering or a makeshift event, often impromptu and of short duration. It can also refer to a jeep.  |例句：We had a little jippo at the beach last weekend.  我们上周末在海滩上举行了一个小型聚会。|近义词：gathering, party, event, get-together, jeep|反义词：large formal event, planned event |名词
