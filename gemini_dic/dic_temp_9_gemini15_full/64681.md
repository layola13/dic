escapades:/ˌɛskəˈpeɪdz/ | n. | 冒险；恶作剧；胡闹 | exciting or adventurous exploits, often involving a degree of risk or recklessness.  It often implies a playful or mischievous element, but can also refer to more serious misadventures. |  例句：The teenagers' escapades kept the police busy all summer. 青少年们的恶作剧让警察整个夏天都很忙。

例句：His youthful escapades eventually landed him in serious trouble. 他年轻时的冒险最终使他陷入严重的麻烦。


近义词：adventures, pranks, exploits, shenanigans, antics

反义词：sedate behavior, responsible conduct, predictable actions
