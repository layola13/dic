outstridden:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪdn/| v. |超过，胜过|To surpass or exceed; to go beyond in achievement or performance.  It implies a significant degree of surpassing, often in a competitive context.|The company's innovative technology outstripped its competitors.  这家公司的创新技术超越了其竞争对手。|The runner outstripped all the others in the final sprint.  跑步者在最后的冲刺中超过了所有其他人。|surpass, exceed, outdo, eclipse|be surpassed, be overtaken


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outstrips his rivals in sales every year. 他每年在销售额上都超过他的竞争对手。
The new model outstrips the old one in performance. 新型号的性能优于旧型号。


一般过去时:
She outstripped her previous best time in the race. 她在比赛中超过了她之前的最好成绩。
The team outstripped expectations and won the championship.  该团队超越了预期并赢得了冠军。


现在完成时:
They have outstripped their sales targets for the quarter. 他们已经超过了本季度的销售目标。
Our company has outstripped all its competitors in market share.  我们的公司在市场份额上已经超越了所有竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outstripped all the other runners.  比赛结束时，他已经超过了所有其他跑步者。
The company had outstripped its competitors in innovation before the merger.  合并之前，该公司在创新方面已经超越了竞争对手。


将来时:
We expect to outstrip our rivals in the upcoming year. 我们预计在来年超越我们的竞争对手。
She believes she will outstrip the current world record. 她相信她会打破目前的 世界纪录。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we might outstrip our competitors. 如果我们更努力工作，我们可能会超越我们的竞争对手。
If only I had trained harder, I could have outstripped my rival. 如果我训练更努力，我本可以超过我的对手。


被动语态:
Their achievements were outstripped by the next generation of athletes. 他们的成就被下一代运动员超越了。
The company's growth was outstripped by the rapid expansion of the market.  该公司的增长被市场的快速扩张所超过。


疑问句:
Will they outstrip their competitors in the market? 他们会在市场上超越竞争对手吗？
Did he outstrip the previous record holder? 他是否超越了之前的纪录保持者？


否定句:
We did not outstrip our sales targets this month. 我们这个月没有达到销售目标。
He failed to outstrip his opponent in the final round. 他未能最终回合中超过对手。
