It is essentially a portable megaphone.|例句：The police officer used a loud-hailer to warn the protesters to disperse.警察用扩音器警告抗议者散去。

The construction workers used a loud-hailer to announce the lunch break. 建筑工人用扩音器宣布午休时间。|近义词：megaphone, bullhorn|反义词：whisperer, earphone
