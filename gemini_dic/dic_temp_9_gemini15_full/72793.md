foisted:/ˈfɔɪstɪd/| v.|强加；硬塞；欺骗性地推销 | To impose (something unwanted or unacceptable) on someone.  |例句：They foisted a worthless painting on him. 他们把一幅毫无价值的画硬塞给他。
例句：He foisted himself upon her as a friend. 他把自己硬塞给她当朋友。|近义词：impose, thrust, palm off |反义词：offer willingly, reject


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He foists his opinions on everyone. = 他把他的意见强加于所有人。
They foist substandard products on unsuspecting customers. = 他们把不合格的产品推销给毫无戒心的顾客。


一般过去时:
The salesman foisted a faulty appliance on the elderly woman. = 销售员把一台有故障的电器卖给了这位上了年纪的妇女。
They foisted the blame onto their colleagues. = 他们把责任推卸给他们的同事。


现在完成时:
The company has foisted several failed projects on the public. = 这家公司已经向公众强推了好几个失败的项目。
She has foisted herself into a position of power. = 她不择手段地获得了权力职位。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had foisted a false narrative on the media. = 会议之前，他们向媒体灌输了一个虚假的叙述。
He had foisted a fake antique on the collector. = 他以前曾把一件假古董卖给收藏家。


将来时:
They will foist more regulations on small businesses. = 他们将对小型企业施加更多规章制度。
She will try to foist her ideas on the team. = 她将会试图把她的想法强加于团队。


虚拟语气:
If they foisted that plan on us, we would strongly object. = 如果他们把那个计划强加于我们，我们会强烈反对。
I wish they hadn't foisted that responsibility on me. = 我真希望他们没有把那项责任强加于我。


被动语态:
A flawed product was foisted on the consumer. = 一个有缺陷的产品被强加给了消费者。
The blame was foisted onto the junior staff. = 责任被推卸给了初级员工。


疑问句:
Was the responsibility foisted on him unfairly? = 责任是否被不公平地强加于他？
Are they trying to foist their agenda on us? = 他们是否试图把他们的计划强加于我们？


否定句:
They did not foist their beliefs on others. = 他们没有把他们的信仰强加于他人。
The company wouldn't foist a substandard product on its customers. = 公司不会把不合格的产品推销给它的顾客。


PHRASE->
foist something on someone = 把某物强加于某人
foist oneself upon someone = 硬把自己塞给某人
foist a lie on someone = 向某人撒谎
foist a fake on someone = 向某人卖假货
foist a problem onto someone = 把问题推给某人
