/ˌɛdəˈmeɪtəz/| n. |水肿（复数）|Edemata is the plural form of edema, referring to the abnormal accumulation of fluid in interstitial spaces of tissues.  It causes swelling.  The plural form is less commonly used than "edemas".|The patient presented with edemata in both legs.|swellings, puffiness|no direct antonym, but the opposite condition would be a lack of fluid retention.|名词
