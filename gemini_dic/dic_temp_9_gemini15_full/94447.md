implunge:/ɪmˈplʌndʒ/| vt.| 投入；陷入；使陷入；猛冲| to plunge or thrust into something; to become deeply involved in something.|例句：He implunged his hands into the icy water. 他把手伸进冰冷的水中。

例句：The country implunged itself into a costly war.  国家陷入了一场代价高昂的战争。

近义词： plunge, thrust, immerse, submerge|反义词：extract, remove, withdraw|动词
