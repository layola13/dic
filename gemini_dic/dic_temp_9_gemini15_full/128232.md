词源:
The word "neuroelectric" is a combination of "neuro-" (from Greek νεῦρον, neuron, meaning "nerve") and "electric" (from Greek ήλεκτρον, elektron, meaning "amber," which was known for its static electricity).  Therefore, it literally means "relating to the electricity of nerves."
