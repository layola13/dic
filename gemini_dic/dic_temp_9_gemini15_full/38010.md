complimenter:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmɛntər/ | n. | 称赞者，恭维者 | A person who gives compliments.  | Example: He's a charming complimenter, always finding something nice to say. 他是一位迷人的称赞者，总是能找到一些好话来说。 |近义词：flatterer, praiser | 反义词：critic, detractor |


USAGE->

The complimenter often praises her work. = 称赞者经常称赞她的作品。
He compliments his colleagues regularly. = 他经常称赞他的同事。
