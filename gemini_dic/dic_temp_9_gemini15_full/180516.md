regents:/ˈriːdʒənts/ | n. | 理事会；摄政；管理者 |  A body of persons who govern or supervise an institution, such as a university or school system.  Specifically, it often refers to a governing board of a state university system. | Example: The University of California Regents approved the new budget. 加州大学理事会批准了新的预算。  The board of regents oversees the college's finances. 理事会负责监督学院的财务。 | 近义词：board, governors, trustees, supervisors | 反义词：N/A
