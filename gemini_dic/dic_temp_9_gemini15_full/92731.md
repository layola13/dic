ichnoscopy:/ɪkˈnɒskəpi/| n. |痕迹学；遗迹学|The study of traces and marks left by organisms, particularly fossils, to infer their behavior and environment.  It involves the detailed examination and interpretation of ichnofossils (trace fossils).|Example: Ichnoscopy is a valuable tool in paleontology for understanding the behavior of extinct animals.|近义词:paleontology, taphonomy|反义词:N/A
