preventively:/prɪˈvɛntɪvli/ | adv. | 预防性地，预防地 |  In a way that prevents something from happening.  It emphasizes action taken *before* a problem occurs to avoid it. | 例句：The doctor recommended taking preventative measures against the flu.  医生建议采取预防流感的措施。

The factory implemented preventative maintenance to reduce equipment downtime. 工厂实施了预防性维护以减少设备停机时间。


近义词：prophylactically, anticipatorily, preemptively | 反义词：reactively, curatively
