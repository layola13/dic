sequester:/sɪˈkwɛstər/| vt. |隔离；扣押；没收；使隔离； |to isolate or hide something away; to take legal possession of assets |The government sequestered the funds. 政府没收了这些资金。 |The jury was sequestered during the trial. 陪审团在审判期间被隔离。| confiscate, isolate, impound | release, free

一般现在时:
The government sequesters the funds for emergency use. = 政府扣押资金以备不时之需。
He sequesters himself in his study every evening. = 他每天晚上把自己关在书房里。


一般过去时:
The court sequestered the suspect's assets. = 法院扣押了嫌疑人的资产。
They sequestered the witnesses to prevent them from communicating. = 他们将证人隔离以防止他们沟通。


现在完成时:
The company has sequestered a significant portion of its profits. = 公司已经扣留了很大一部分利润。
