sedentary:/ˈsɛdntəri/| adj.|久坐的；定居的；不活动的|Characterized by or requiring a lot of sitting; doing or requiring much sitting; not physically active.|He leads a sedentary lifestyle, spending most of his day at a computer.|近义词：inactive, stationary, settled|反义词：active, mobile, nomadic|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leads a sedentary life. = 他过着久坐的生活。
She maintains a sedentary job. = 她从事一份久坐的工作。


一般过去时:
He lived a sedentary life in his retirement. = 他退休后过着久坐的生活。
They had a sedentary lifestyle before moving to the countryside. = 他们搬到乡下之前过着久坐的生活。


现在完成时:
She has maintained a sedentary lifestyle for years. = 她多年来一直保持着久坐的生活方式。
He has adopted a more sedentary routine since his injury. = 自从受伤以来，他过上了更加久坐的生活。


过去完成时:
He had already adopted a sedentary lifestyle before his health problems started. = 在他的健康问题开始之前，他已经过上了久坐的生活。
She had led a sedentary life for decades before she decided to take up hiking. = 在她决定开始远足之前，她已经过着久坐的生活几十年了。


将来时:
He will continue to lead a sedentary life. = 他将继续过着久坐的生活。
She will likely adopt a more active lifestyle to counter her sedentary job. = 她可能会采取更积极的生活方式来对抗她的久坐工作。


虚拟语气:
If he were not so sedentary, he would be healthier. = 如果他不那么久坐，他会更健康。
I wish I hadn't been so sedentary in my younger years. = 我真希望我年轻的时候不要那么久坐。


被动语态:
A sedentary lifestyle is often associated with health problems. = 久坐的生活方式通常与健康问题有关。
His sedentary job was considered a risk factor for heart disease. = 他的久坐工作被认为是心脏病的危险因素。


疑问句:
Is his lifestyle sedentary? = 他的生活方式是久坐的吗？
Are you worried about the effects of a sedentary job? = 你担心久坐工作的后果吗？


否定句:
His lifestyle is not sedentary; he's quite active. = 他的生活方式并非久坐；他相当活跃。
That job is not sedentary; it requires a lot of physical work. = 那份工作并非久坐；它需要大量的体力劳动。
