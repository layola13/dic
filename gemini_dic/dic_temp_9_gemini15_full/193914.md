self-education:/ˌsɛlfˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃən/| n. | 自学，自我教育 | The process of educating oneself, often independently of formal institutions.  It involves actively seeking knowledge and skills through various means. |  例句：He achieved a high level of self-education through extensive reading and online courses. 他通过广泛阅读和在线课程达到了很高的自学水平。


例句：Her self-education in art history was impressive, considering she never attended university.  考虑到她从未上过大学，她在艺术史方面的自学令人印象深刻。


近义词：independent study, self-learning, autodidacticism


反义词：formal education, institutional learning


词性：名词
