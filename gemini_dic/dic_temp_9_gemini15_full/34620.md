clefs:/klefs/| n.|谱号|A clef is a symbol placed at the beginning of a musical staff that indicates the pitch of the notes.  It is essential for reading and understanding musical notation.|Example: The treble clef is used for higher-pitched notes, while the bass clef is used for lower-pitched notes.  谱号用于指示音符的音高，高音谱号用于较高音高的音符，而低音谱号用于较低音高的音符。|近义词：musical symbol, notation mark|反义词：None|名词
