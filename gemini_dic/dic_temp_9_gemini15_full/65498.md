europhile:/ˈjʊərəʊfaɪl/| n. |亲欧洲者，欧洲爱好者 |A person who supports European integration and is generally pro-European.  They often favor closer ties between European nations and the expansion of the European Union. | Many europhile politicians advocated for deeper European integration. 许多亲欧洲的政治家倡导更深入的欧洲一体化。| pro-European, pro-EU | Eurosceptic, anti-European
