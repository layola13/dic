landscaping:/ˈlændˌskeɪpɪŋ/ | n. |景观设计；园林绿化 |The art or process of designing and creating outdoor spaces, including the placement of plants, hardscaping features (such as paths, patios, and walls), and other elements to enhance the aesthetic appeal and functionality of a property.  |例句：The landscaping around the house significantly increased its value.  房屋周围的景观设计大大提高了它的价值。
例句：He hired a professional for the landscaping of his new garden. 他雇了一位专业人士来设计他的新花园。|近义词：gardening, groundskeeping |反义词：destruction, demolition
