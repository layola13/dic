captured:/ˈkæptʃərd/ | v. adj. | 捕获的；被俘获的；被迷住的 | To take someone or something prisoner or into one's possession, especially by force.  To fascinate or strongly attract someone's attention or interest. |例句：The police captured the thief. 警察抓住了小偷。|例句：Her beauty captured his heart. 她的美貌吸引了他的心。|近义词：seized, apprehended, captivated, enthralled | 反义词：released, freed, liberated | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police capture criminals every day. = 警察每天都抓捕罪犯。
He captures the essence of the story perfectly. = 他完美地抓住了故事的精髓。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
