nullifier:/ˈnʌlɪfaɪər/| n. |使无效者；取消者；废止者 |A nullifier is a person or thing that makes something else ineffective or invalid.  It can refer to a person who opposes or rejects something, or a mechanism that cancels out an effect. |The Supreme Court acted as a nullifier of the state law. 最高法院充当了州法的废止者。 The new policy is a nullifier of previous agreements. 新政策使之前的协议无效。|invalidator, canceler, negator |enforcer, validator |名词
