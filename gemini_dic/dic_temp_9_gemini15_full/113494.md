lysosomes:/ˈlaɪsəˌsoʊmz/| n. |溶酶体|Membrane-bound organelles found in most animal cells. They contain hydrolytic enzymes that break down waste materials and cellular debris.  Lysosomes are crucial for maintaining cellular health and function by recycling cellular components and eliminating pathogens. |例句：Lysosomes are essential for the degradation of cellular waste. 溶酶体对于细胞废物的降解至关重要。|例句：The malfunction of lysosomes can lead to various diseases. 溶酶体的功能障碍会导致各种疾病。|近义词：organelle (细胞器)|反义词：N/A|名词
