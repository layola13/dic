decylic:/ˈdɛsɪlɪk/| adj.|癸基的；癸酸的；含十碳链的 |Decylic refers to a chemical compound containing a ten-carbon chain, specifically related to decanoic acid (also known as capric acid) and its derivatives.  It describes molecules with a ten-carbon straight chain. |Example: Decylic acid is a saturated fatty acid found in coconut oil. 癸酸是一种存在于椰子油中的饱和脂肪酸。|近义词:  deca- (prefix indicating ten carbon atoms) |反义词: N/A
