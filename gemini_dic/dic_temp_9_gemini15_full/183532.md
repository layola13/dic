repurposes:/ˌriːˈpɜːrpəs/ | vt. |重新利用；改变用途 | To find a new purpose or use for something.  |例句：The old factory building was repurposed as a modern art gallery.  旧厂房被改造成现代艺术馆。

例句：We repurposed the old shipping containers into affordable housing. 我们将旧集装箱改造成经济适用房。


近义词：reuse, recycle, refashion, convert, adapt


反义词：discard, dispose of, destroy


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They repurpose old tires into playground equipment. = 他们将旧轮胎重新利用，做成操场设备。
She repurposes vintage clothing into unique fashion pieces. = 她将旧服装改造成独特的时尚单品。


一般过去时:
The company repurposed its outdated technology for a new project. = 公司将过时的技术重新用于一个新项目。
He repurposed the old wood from his barn for a new bookshelf. = 他将谷仓里的旧木头重新做成一个新的书架。


现在完成时:
We have repurposed many items in our home to reduce waste. = 我们已经将家里的许多物品重新利用以减少浪费。
She has repurposed countless items since she started her crafting business. = 自从她开始她的工艺品生意以来，她已经重新利用了无数的物品。


过去完成时:
They had repurposed the materials before the construction began. = 在施工开始之前，他们已经对材料进行了重新利用。
The artist had repurposed many discarded objects in her latest installation. = 艺术家在她最新的装置艺术中重新利用了许多被丢弃的物品。


将来时:
We will repurpose the old furniture for the new office space. = 我们将把旧家具重新用于新的办公室空间。
The city plans to repurpose the abandoned railway line into a walking trail. = 城市计划将废弃的铁路改造成步行道。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would repurpose the old pallets into garden furniture. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会把旧托盘改造成花园家具。
I wish I could repurpose this broken vase into something beautiful. = 我真希望我能把这个破花瓶改造成一些漂亮的东西。


被动语态:
The old building is being repurposed into a community center. = 这座旧建筑正在被改造成一个社区中心。
Many items are repurposed by creative individuals to reduce waste and save money. = 许多物品都被有创意的人们重新利用，以减少浪费和节省开支。


疑问句:
How can we repurpose these old jars? = 我们如何重新利用这些旧罐子？
What can be repurposed from this old computer? = 这台旧电脑能重新利用什么？


否定句:
They did not repurpose the old equipment. = 他们没有重新利用旧设备。
We cannot repurpose these materials due to safety concerns. = 由于安全问题，我们不能重新利用这些材料。


PHRASE->
repurpose for = 为了…而重新利用
repurpose as = 作为…而重新利用
repurpose into = 改造成…
repurpose waste = 再利用废物
repurpose materials = 再利用材料
