carder:/ˈkɑːrdər/ | n. |梳棉工；整理工；诈骗犯 | A carder is someone who works in a textile mill, preparing wool or cotton for spinning, or someone who uses deceptive methods to obtain money or goods.  The word can also refer to a person who combs wool or cotton. |Example: The carder expertly separated the fibers of the wool.  梳棉工熟练地分离羊毛纤维。|Example: He was arrested for being a carder, defrauding numerous elderly people. 他因诈骗多名老年人而被捕。|近义词：woolcomber, swindler, cheat |反义词：honest person, benefactor
