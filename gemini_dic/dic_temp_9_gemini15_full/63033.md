enrapture:/ɪnˈræptʃər/| vt.|使狂喜；使着迷；使陶醉|To fill (someone) with great delight; to enchant or captivate.|例句：The beauty of the sunset enraptured her. 夕阳的美丽使她着迷。

例句：The breathtaking performance enraptured the audience. 令人惊叹的表演使观众着迷。


近义词：delight, enchant, captivate, fascinate, enthrall|反义词：bore, disenchant, disappoint


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music enraptures the listener.  音乐使听者着迷。
The scenery enraptures travelers.  风景使旅行者着迷。


一般过去时:
The story enraptured me. 故事使我着迷。
The magician's tricks enraptured the children. 魔术师的戏法使孩子们着迷。


现在完成时:
The novel has enraptured readers for decades. 这部小说几十年来一直使读者着迷。
Her singing has enraptured many audiences. 她的歌声已使许多观众着迷。


过去完成时:
