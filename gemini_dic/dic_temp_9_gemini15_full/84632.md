hand-in:/ˈhændɪn/| vt. n.|提交，交上；(尤指作业)上交|To give something to a person in authority, especially school work.|例句：Please hand in your assignments by Friday.  请在周五之前提交你们的作业。
例句：The hand-in date for the project is next week.  这个项目的提交截止日期是下周。|近义词：submit, deliver, turn in|反义词：receive, take back


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hands in his homework every week. = 他每周都交作业。
She hands in her report to the manager. = 她把报告交给经理。

一般过去时:
I handed in my application last week. = 我上周提交了我的申请。
They handed in their exams on time. = 他们按时交了考试卷。

现在完成时:
She has handed in all her assignments. = 她已经提交了所有的作业。
He has handed in his resignation. = 他已经递交了辞呈。

过去完成时:
I had handed in my essay before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经提交了我的论文。
They had handed in their projects before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经提交了他们的项目。

将来时:
I will hand in my report tomorrow. = 我明天将提交我的报告。
She will hand in her application next week. = 她下周将提交她的申请。

虚拟语气:
If I had handed in my assignment earlier, I wouldn't be in trouble. = 如果我早点提交作业，我就不会有麻烦了。
I wish I had handed in my work on time. = 我真希望我按时提交了我的工作。

被动语态:
The assignments were handed in on time. = 作业按时提交了。
The report will be handed in by Friday. = 报告将在周五之前提交。

疑问句:
Have you handed in your homework? = 你提交作业了吗？
When will the report be handed in? = 报告什么时候提交？

否定句:
He didn't hand in his assignment. = 他没有提交作业。
She hasn't handed in her application yet. = 她还没有提交申请。


PHRASE->
hand in one's notice = 递交辞呈
hand in hand = 手牵着手；密切相关
hand in glove = 密切合作；狼狈为奸
hand in with = 与...合作
to hand in = 提交
