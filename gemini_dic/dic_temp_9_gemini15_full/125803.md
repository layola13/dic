murkiness:/ˈmɜːrkɪnəs/ | n. |阴暗；模糊；浑浊 | The murkiness of the fog made it difficult to see.  浓雾的阴暗使能见度很低。|The murkiness of the political situation made it hard to predict the outcome.  政治局势的模糊不清使得难以预测结果。 |近义词：darkness, obscurity, gloom |反义词：clarity, brightness, transparency
