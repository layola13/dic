pandemia:/pænˈdeːmiə/| n.|大流行病，世界性流行病|A pandemic is an epidemic that has spread over a large region, for instance multiple continents, or even worldwide.|例句：The COVID-19 outbreak quickly escalated into a global pandemia.  新冠肺炎疫情迅速升级为全球性大流行病。

例句：The influenza pandemic of 1918 killed millions of people worldwide. 1918年的流感大流行导致全球数百万人死亡。


近义词：epidemic, plague |反义词：endemic (地方性疾病)


词性：名词
