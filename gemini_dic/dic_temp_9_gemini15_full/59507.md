eastmost: /ˈiːstmoʊst/ | adj. | 最东的，最东端的 |  Furthest east; situated or occurring furthest to the east.  This adjective is superlative form of "east". It describes the location of something as being the easternmost point or position relative to other locations. |


例句：
The eastmost point of the island is a dramatic cliff.  = 岛屿最东端是一处壮观的悬崖。

The eastmost building in the city is a modern skyscraper. = 城里最东边的建筑是一座现代化的摩天大楼。


近义词：easternmost, furthest east


反义词：westmost, westernmost


词性：形容词
