diatryma:/daɪˈætrɪmə/| n. |恐鹤|A large, flightless, predatory bird that lived during the Eocene Epoch.  Diatrymas were among the largest known terrestrial birds, characterized by their powerful legs and beaks. They were apex predators in their ecosystems. |例句：The diatryma, a fearsome predator of the Eocene, was a giant among birds.  恐鹤，始新世可怕的掠食者，是鸟类中的巨人。
The fossil evidence suggests that diatrymas were well-adapted to hunting large prey.化石证据表明，恐鹤非常适应捕猎大型猎物。|近义词：Gastornis (although the relationship is debated)|反义词：None|词性:noun
