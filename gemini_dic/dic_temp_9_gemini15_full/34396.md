classless:/ˈklæs.ləs/| adj.| 无阶级的；不分等级的；粗俗的；低俗的 | Lacking class distinctions; also, lacking refinement or good taste. |例句：The revolution aimed to create a classless society.  革命的目标是建立一个无阶级的社会。

例句：Her classless behavior embarrassed her parents. 她的粗俗行为让她父母难堪。

近义词：unclassed, egalitarian (for "无阶级的"), vulgar, tasteless (for "粗俗的")

反义词：class-conscious, stratified (for "无阶级的"), refined, elegant (for "粗俗的")

词性：形容词
