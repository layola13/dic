over-beat:/ˌoʊvərˈbiːt/ | v. |过度击打；节奏过快 | To strike or hit something too hard or too frequently; to play a musical rhythm too quickly.  |例句：He over-beat the eggs, making the cake too runny. 他把鸡蛋打过了，导致蛋糕太稀了。 |例句：The drummer over-beat the tempo, making the song sound rushed.  鼓手节奏过快，使得歌曲听起来很仓促。 |近义词：exceed, surpass, overdo |反义词：under-beat
