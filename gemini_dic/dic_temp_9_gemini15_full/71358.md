flacks:/flæks/| n.|批评者；新闻发言人；宣传人员|People who criticize, especially those who do so publicly and aggressively;  also refers to public relations professionals who handle media relations.|The politician's flacks worked overtime to counter the negative press.  这位政治家的新闻发言人加班加点地应对负面新闻。|Critics, publicists, spin doctors|Supporters, admirers|名词
