self-maimed:/ˌsɛlfˈmeɪmd/| adj.| 自残的；自我伤害的 |Self-maimed refers to someone who has intentionally inflicted injury upon themselves.  It implies a deliberate act of self-harm, often related to mental health issues or extreme distress. |例句：The police found evidence suggesting the victim was self-maimed.  警方发现证据表明受害者是自残的。

The suspect exhibited self-maimed wounds consistent with a suicide attempt. 嫌疑人身上有自残伤口，与自杀企图相符。|近义词：self-inflicted, self-harmed, self-mutilated |反义词：unharmed, healthy, well
