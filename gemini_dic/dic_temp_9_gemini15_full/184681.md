restuffed:/ˌriːˈstʌft/| v. |重新填塞，重新塞满 |To fill something again after it has been emptied or partially emptied.  This typically involves stuffing with the same or similar material.|例句：The old sofa was restuffed with new foam.  旧沙发重新用新泡沫填塞了。
The Thanksgiving turkey was restuffed with leftover dressing. 感恩节火鸡用剩余的调味料重新填塞了。|近义词：re-fill, repack|反义词：empty, unstuff

She restuffs her favorite teddy bear. 她重新填塞她最喜欢的泰迪熊。
