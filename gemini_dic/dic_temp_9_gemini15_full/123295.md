monogenism:/ˈmɒnəʊdʒənɪzəm/|/ˈmɑːnoʊdʒənɪzəm/| n. | 单源论；单一起源说 | The theory that all human races originated from a single pair of ancestors.  This theory contrasts with polygenism, which posits multiple origins for different races.  | Example:  The debate between monogenism and polygenism dominated anthropological discussions for centuries. = 单源论和多元论的争论持续了几世纪主导着人类学讨论。

The theory of monogenism posits a single origin for humanity, suggesting that all human races share a common ancestor. = 单源论认为人类起源于单一起源，认为所有种族都拥有共同的祖先。


近义词：unitarianism (in the context of human origins)
反义词：polygenism


词性：名词
