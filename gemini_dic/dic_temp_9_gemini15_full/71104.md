fished:/fɪʃt/ | v. | 钓鱼；捕鱼；捞出；找出 | To catch fish, typically with a rod, line, and hook; to search for and find something. | 例句：He fished in the lake all afternoon. 他整个下午都在湖里钓鱼。

例句：The police fished out the evidence from the river. 警察从河里捞出了证据。


近义词：angled, netted, caught | 反义词：N/A
