fakie:/ˈfeɪki/| adj.|反向的，反脚的（特指滑板或滑雪）|  Describing a skateboarding or snowboarding trick performed in the opposite direction to the usual stance.  For example, a fakie ollie is an ollie performed while riding backward. |例句：He landed a clean fakie heel flip. 他完成了一个干净利落的反脚后跟翻转。

例句：She was going fakie down the hill. 她正在反向滑下坡。

近义词：reverse (in this context)

反义词：regular (in this context)

词性：形容词
