modest: /ˈmɑːdɪst/ | /ˈmɒdɪst/ | adj. | 谦虚的；适度的；不大的 |

英文解析:  Describing someone who is humble and doesn't boast about their achievements or possessions.  It can also describe something that is small, simple, or inexpensive.


例句:
She's very modest about her success. = 她对自己的成功非常谦虚。

He lives in a modest house. = 他住在一间简朴的房子里。


近义词: humble, unassuming, unpretentious, moderate, restrained


反义词: boastful, arrogant, ostentatious, extravagant, immoderate


词源:  From Old French *modeste, from Latin modestus "moderate, temperate," from modus "measure, manner, limit."
