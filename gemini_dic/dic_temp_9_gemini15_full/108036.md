latah:/ˈlɑːtɑː/|/ˈlæteɪ/| n. |拉塔症；模仿症 |A rare neurological disorder, primarily found in Southeast Asia, characterized by involuntary imitation of others' actions or speech, often triggered by a sudden fright or surprise.  The affected person may also experience echolalia (repeating words or phrases) and echopraxia (imitating movements).|例句：The doctor diagnosed her condition as latah, a rare neurological disorder.  医生诊断她的病症为拉塔症，一种罕见的 neurological disorder。

例句：She suffers from latah and will sometimes repeat everything said to her. 她患有拉塔症，有时会重复别人对她所说的一切。


近义词：echopraxia, echolalia (these describe specific symptoms, not the whole disorder) | 反义词：None (it's a disorder, not something with a direct opposite) | 词性:Noun
