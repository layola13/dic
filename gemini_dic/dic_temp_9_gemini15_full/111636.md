localizes:/ˈloʊkəlaɪzɪz/| v.| 使本地化；使适应当地情况 |To adapt (something) to a particular place or region; to confine or restrict to a specific area. |例句：The software localizes well to different operating systems.  该软件能很好地适应不同的操作系统。|例句：The infection localized to the lungs. 感染局限于肺部。|近义词：adapt, adjust, regionalize |反义词：globalize, generalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company localizes its software for different markets. = 这家公司为不同的市场本地化其软件。
This app localizes perfectly to my phone. = 这个应用程序在我的手机上完美地本地化了。

一般过去时:
They localized the game for the Japanese market. = 他们为日本市场对游戏进行了本地化。
The company localized its marketing materials last year. = 这家公司去年对其营销材料进行了本地化。

现在完成时:
We have localized our website for several countries. = 我们已经为多个国家/地区本地化了我们的网站。
The developers have localized the game to several languages. = 开发人员已将游戏本地化到多种语言。

过去完成时:
They had localized the software before the launch date. = 他们早在发布日期之前就完成了软件的本地化工作。
The team had localized the documents prior to the meeting. = 团队在会议之前已经将文件本地化了。

将来时:
We will localize the application for the European market next year. = 我们明年将为欧洲市场本地化该应用程序。
The company will localize its website into Spanish next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度将其网站本地化成西班牙语。

虚拟语气:
If we localized the website, we would attract more customers. = 如果我们本地化网站，我们将吸引更多客户。
It would be beneficial if the company localized its products for the Asian market. = 如果公司将产品本地化到亚洲市场，将是有益的。

否定句:
