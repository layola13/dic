reconvicting:/ˌriːkənˈvɪktɪŋ/| v.|再次定罪；重新判刑|The act of convicting someone again for the same crime, often after an appeal or a retrial.  This usually implies that the original conviction was overturned or deemed insufficient.|例句：After the appeals court overturned his initial conviction, the prosecution decided to pursue reconvicting him. = 上诉法院推翻了他的初审判决后，检方决定再次对他提起诉讼。

例句：The defendant was reconvicting despite presenting new evidence. = 尽管被告提供了新的证据，但他还是被再次定罪。


近义词：re-sentencing, re-trying

反义词：acquitting
