hodgkin:/ˈhɒdʒkɪn/| n. |霍奇金（姓氏）；霍奇金氏病|Hodgkin is a surname, most famously associated with Thomas Hodgkin, a British physician who identified Hodgkin's lymphoma.  The term also refers to Hodgkin's lymphoma itself, a type of cancer of the lymphatic system.| Example:  The diagnosis was Hodgkin's lymphoma. = 诊断结果是霍奇金淋巴瘤。|近义词: (as a surname)  None specifically. (as a disease) lymphoma,  lymphoma cancer|反义词: (as a surname) None. (as a disease)  health, remission|名词
