re-presents:/ˌriːprɪˈzents/| vt.|再呈现；重新提出；代表|To present again; to represent anew; to act on behalf of someone or something.|例句：The museum re-presents the history of the region.  博物馆再次呈现了该地区的历史。|例句：The lawyer re-presented the case to the judge. 律师向法官再次陈述了案件。|近义词：represent, display, portray, embody|反义词：misrepresent, conceal, hide|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-presents the data every year. = 他每年都重新呈现数据。
The ambassador re-presents his country's interests. = 大使代表他国家的利益。

一般过去时:
She re-presented her findings at the conference. = 她在大会上再次陈述了她的发现。
The team re-presented their project proposal. = 团队重新提交了他们的项目提案。

现在完成时:
They have re-presented the evidence multiple times. = 他们已经多次重新提交证据。
The company has re-presented its new strategy. = 公司已经重新阐述了它的新战略。

过去完成时:
He had re-presented the argument before the judge intervened. = 在法官干预之前，他已经再次陈述了论点。
The witness had re-presented her testimony, clarifying some points. = 证人重新作证，澄清了一些要点。

将来时:
She will re-present the case next week. = 她下周将再次陈述此案。
The company will re-present its financial report. = 公司将重新提交其财务报告。

虚拟语气:
If he re-presented the evidence, the jury might change their verdict. = 如果他重新提交证据，陪审团可能会改变他们的判决。
I wish he had re-presented his argument more clearly. = 我希望他更清楚地重新陈述了他的论点。

被动语态:
The data was re-presented in a more visual format. = 数据以更直观的格式重新呈现。
The case will be re-presented to the appeals court. = 此案将提交上诉法院再次审理。

疑问句:
Will the company re-present its financial results? = 公司会重新提交财务业绩吗？
How often does he re-present his research findings? = 他多久重新提交一次他的研究结果？

否定句:
The defendant did not re-present any new evidence. = 被告没有提交任何新的证据。
She refused to re-present her argument. = 她拒绝再次陈述她的论点。
