noncaking: /ˌnɑːnˈkeɪkɪŋ/ | adj. | 不结块的，不结饼的 | Not forming lumps or cakes.  This adjective describes materials that remain free-flowing and do not clump together.  It's often used in the context of powders, granules, or other particulate materials. | Example: The noncaking formula ensures easy dispensing and prevents clogging.  不结块的配方确保易于分配并防止堵塞。| 近义词: free-flowing, lump-free | 反义词: caking, lumpy
