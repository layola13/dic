commercial:/kəˈmɜːrʃəl/| adj., n.|商业的；广告|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "commercial" means relating to or used in commerce or trade.  As a noun, it refers to an advertisement broadcast on television or radio.

例句:
The company's commercial success is undeniable.  这家公司的商业成功是不可否认的。

The television commercial featured a catchy jingle.  这个电视广告播放了一首朗朗上口的歌曲。


近义词:
(adj.) business, trading, mercantile, advertising

反义词:
(adj.) noncommercial, private, personal


词性:
adjective, noun
