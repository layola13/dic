fertilizing:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzɪŋ/| v. |施肥；使肥沃|The act of applying fertilizer to soil or other growing medium to promote plant growth.  It involves enriching the soil with essential nutrients to enhance plant health, yield, and overall quality.|例句：The farmer is fertilizing his fields with nitrogen-rich fertilizer.  农民正在用富含氮的肥料给他的田地施肥。
例句：Fertilizing the lawn regularly will keep it green and lush. 定期给草坪施肥会使它保持翠绿茂盛。|近义词：manuring, enriching, nourishing|反义词：depleting, impoverishing
