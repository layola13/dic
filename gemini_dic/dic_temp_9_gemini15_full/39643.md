conservable: /kənˈsɜːrvəbl/ | adj. | 可保存的；可持续的 |  Able to be conserved; capable of being preserved or protected from loss or damage.  This word often refers to resources or energy that can be used efficiently and sustainably. |


例句：

Renewable energy sources are conservable resources that can be replenished naturally.  可再生能源是可以自然补充的可保存资源。

The museum is dedicated to preserving conservable artifacts from the region's history. 该博物馆致力于保护该地区历史上可保存的文物。


近义词：sustainable, preservable, protectable

反义词：unconservable, wasteful, unsustainable

词性：形容词
