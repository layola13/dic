rehandling: /ˌriːˈhændlɪŋ/ | n. | 再处理；重新处理；再次搬运 | The act of handling something again, often after it has been processed or transported.  This can refer to physical objects or data. |例句：The rehandling of the damaged goods added to the shipping costs.  再次处理损坏的货物增加了运输成本。

The rehandling of customer complaints requires patience and understanding.  重新处理客户投诉需要耐心和理解。|近义词：reprocessing, reworking, re-examination |反义词：initial handling, first handling |名词
