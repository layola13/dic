luvvie:/ˈlʌvi/| n. |亲爱的，可爱的人，（尤指）社交媒体上的名人|A luvvie is a term, often used somewhat derisively, to refer to someone who is considered overly theatrical, pretentious, or self-important, especially an actor or other performer.  It is also used more affectionately to describe someone perceived as charming or endearing, particularly someone who is stylish or fashionable and enjoys the finer things in life.  The term is more common in British English.|例句：She's a bit of a luvvie, always talking about her latest theatre role. 她有点做作，总是谈论她最新的戏剧角色。

例句：He's a real luvvie, always dressed to the nines. 他真是个可爱的人，总是穿着光鲜亮丽。


近义词：darling, sweetie, sweetheart (affectionate uses);  thespian,  drama queen (derisive uses)


反义词：grump, curmudgeon (in the context of affectionate use)
