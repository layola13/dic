limbering:/ˈlɪmbərɪŋ/ | v. | 使柔软；使灵活；热身 | The act of making or becoming flexible; warming up muscles before exercise.  | 例句：He spent ten minutes limbering up before the race. 他在比赛前花了十分钟热身。 | 例句：The dancer limbered her body before the performance. 舞蹈演员在表演前活动了身体。 | 近义词：warming up, stretching, loosening | 反义词：stiffening, tightening
