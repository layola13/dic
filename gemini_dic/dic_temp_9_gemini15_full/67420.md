extension:/ɪkˈstɛnʃən/ | n. | 延伸；延长；扩展；分机；延期；扩建 |  An extension is the act of extending something, or the result of that act.  It can refer to a physical lengthening, an increase in duration, or an addition to something.  It also refers to a telephone extension. |例句：The extension of the deadline is greatly appreciated.  延期截止日期深受赞赏。

The building underwent a major extension last year.  这座建筑去年进行了大规模扩建。


近义词：prolongation, expansion, enlargement, addition | 反义词：reduction, shortening, contraction | 词性：名词
