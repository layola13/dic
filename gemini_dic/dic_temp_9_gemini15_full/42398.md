countercharge:/ˈkaʊntərtʃɑːrdʒ/ | n. |反诉，反控告 | A countercharge is a formal accusation made in response to an accusation made against oneself.  It's a claim made by the defendant against the plaintiff in a lawsuit.  | Example:  The defendant filed a countercharge of defamation against the plaintiff.  被告对原告提出诽谤的反诉。


Example: The accused man launched a countercharge of police brutality.  被告人提出警方暴行的反诉。


近义词：counterclaim, rebuttal, retort | 反义词：initial charge, original accusation | 词性: 名词
