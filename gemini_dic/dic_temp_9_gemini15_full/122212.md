misvaluing:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuɪŋ/| v.| 估价过低；低估；误解价值 | To undervalue something or someone; to fail to recognize the true worth of something or someone.  | 例句：The company was misvaluing its assets, leading to inaccurate financial reports.  公司低估了其资产，导致财务报告不准确。

例句：He was misvaluing her contributions to the project, failing to recognize her hard work. 他低估了她对项目的贡献，没有认识到她的辛勤工作。


近义词：undervaluing, underestimating, depreciating


反义词：overvaluing, appreciating


词性：动词
