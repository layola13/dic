excito:/ɪkˈsaɪtoʊ/| v.|使兴奋；刺激；激动|To excite or stimulate (a nerve cell or other biological structure).  The word is often used in the context of neuroscience and describes the action of excitatory neurotransmitters. |例句：Excessive alcohol can excito neurons in the brain, leading to damage. 过量的酒精会刺激大脑神经元，导致损伤。|例句：The music excited the audience and they started to dance. 音乐使观众兴奋起来，他们开始跳舞。|近义词：stimulate, excite, arouse|反义词：inhibit, depress, calm
