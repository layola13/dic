polymerize:/pəˈlɪməraɪz/| vt.|聚合，聚合反应|To combine (simple molecules) to form a polymer.|The monomers polymerize to form a long chain. 单体聚合形成长链。|近义词：polymerise|反义词：depolymerize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The monomers polymerize to form a long chain. = 单体聚合形成长链。
Scientists polymerize various materials in the lab. = 科学家在实验室里使各种材料聚合。

一般过去时:
The chemist polymerized the styrene to create polystyrene. = 化学家使苯乙烯聚合以制造聚苯乙烯。
They polymerized the solution under high pressure. = 他们在高压下使溶液聚合。
