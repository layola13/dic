prescription:/prɪˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. |处方，药方；规定，指示 |A prescription is a written order from a doctor for medicine.  It details the medication, dosage, and instructions for use.  A prescription can also refer to a set of instructions or rules. |例句：The doctor wrote me a prescription for antibiotics. 医生给我开了抗生素的处方。

例句：The company's success followed a strict prescription for growth.  公司的成功遵循了严格的增长规划。

近义词：formula, recipe, instruction | 反义词：prohibition, ban


词源:  From Middle English *prescripcioun, from Old French prescripcioun, from Latin præscriptio, from præscribere "to write before, prescribe," from prae- "before" + scribere "to write."
