phonography:/fəˈnɑːɡrəfi/| n. |速记法，声纹学|The system of writing by sounds; a method of writing shorthand.  It is also used in forensic science to analyze voice recordings.|例句：She learned phonography to take notes quickly during lectures. 她学习速记以便在课堂上快速记笔记。|例句：The police used phonography to identify the suspect from the phone call recording. 警方使用声纹学从电话录音中识别嫌疑人。|近义词：shorthand, stenography|反义词：longhand
