repeat:/rɪˈpiːt/ | v. n. |重复，反复 |To say or do something again.|例句：Please repeat the question. 请重复一下问题。  例句：The teacher asked us to repeat the words. 老师让我们重复这些单词。|近义词：reiterate, reiterate, duplicate |反义词：omit, skip


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repeats the same mistake every day. = 他每天都犯同样的错误。
They repeat the experiment several times. = 他们重复实验好几次。


一般过去时:
She repeated what she had heard. = 她重复了她听到的话。
He repeated the instructions clearly. = 他清晰地重复了指示。


现在完成时:
I have repeated myself many times. = 我已经重复了很多次了。
She has repeated this course twice. = 她已经重复这门课两次了。


过去完成时:
He had repeated the story before I could interrupt him. = 在我打断他之前，他已经重复了这个故事。
They had repeated the test, but still got the wrong answer. = 他们已经重复了测试，但仍然得到错误的答案。


将来时:
I will repeat the exercise tomorrow. = 我明天会重复练习。
We will repeat the process until we get the desired result. = 我们会重复这个过程，直到得到想要的结果。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would repeat the question. = 如果我是你，我会重复这个问题。
I wish I hadn't repeated that rumour. = 我真希望我没有重复那个谣言。


被动语态:
The experiment was repeated many times. = 这个实验被重复了很多次。
The instructions will be repeated before the test. = 在测试之前将重复说明。


疑问句:
Will you repeat that please? = 请你再说一遍好吗？
Can you repeat the last sentence? = 你能重复最后一句话吗？


否定句:
Don't repeat that again! = 不要再重复了！
He did not repeat the error. = 他没有重复错误。


PHRASE->
repeat oneself = 重复自己
repeat after me = 学我念
repeat offender = 惯犯
repeat business = 回头客
repeat order = 再订单


词源:
The word "repeat" comes from the Old French word "repeiter," which in turn comes from the Latin word "repetere," meaning "to seek again, attempt again."  The prefix "re-" indicates repetition, and the root "-petere" is related to words meaning "to seek" or "to strive."
