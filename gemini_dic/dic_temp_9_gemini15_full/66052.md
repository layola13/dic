/ɪɡˈzæs.pə.reɪ.tər/ | n. |使人恼怒的人或事|Something or someone that causes great irritation or annoyance.|The constant traffic jams were a real exasperater.  持续不断的交通堵塞真是让人恼火。|近义词：nuisance, annoyance, irritation |反义词：comfort, solace

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The paperwork exasperates me. = 这堆文书工作让我很恼火。
That noise exasperates the neighbors. = 那个噪音让邻居们很恼火。


一般过去时:
The delay exasperated the passengers. = 延误让乘客们非常恼火。
His stubbornness exasperated his wife. = 他的固执让他妻子很生气。


现在完成时:
The endless waiting has exasperated me. = 无休止的等待让我非常恼火。
Her constant complaints have exasperated everyone. = 她不断的抱怨已经惹恼了所有人。


过去完成时:
The children had exasperated their teacher with their misbehavior. = 孩子们的不良行为已经让老师忍无可忍了。
He had already been exasperated by the slow progress of the project. = 项目进展缓慢，他已经非常恼火了。


将来时:
The heat will exasperate the tourists. = 炎热的天气会让游客们感到烦躁。
This problem will exasperate you if you don't deal with it. = 如果你不处理这个问题，它会让你很烦。


虚拟语气:
If only the noise wouldn't exasperate me so much! = 但愿噪音不会让我这么烦！
I wish the constant interruptions wouldn't exasperate me. = 我希望这些不断的打扰不会让我感到恼火。


被动语态:
He was exasperated by the situation. = 他被这种情况弄得很恼火。
The audience was exasperated by the long delay. = 观众们因为长时间的延误而感到恼火。


疑问句:
Does this situation exasperate you? = 这种情况让你恼火吗？
What exasperates you the most about this job? = 关于这份工作，什么最让你恼火？


否定句:
The situation didn't exasperate him as much as I thought. = 情况并没有像我想象的那样让他恼火。
The news didn't exasperate her, surprisingly. = 令我惊讶的是，这个消息并没有让她恼火。
