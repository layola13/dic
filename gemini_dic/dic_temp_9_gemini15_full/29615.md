categorized:/ˈkætəɡəraɪzd/| v.|分类的，归类的|to arrange or classify something into categories|The books were categorized by author.  这些书按作者分类。|The students categorized the plants by their colors. 学生们按颜色对植物进行了分类。|classified, sorted, grouped|uncategorized


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He categorizes his emails by sender. = 他按发件人分类邮件。
She categorizes her expenses into different categories. = 她把她的开支分成不同的类别。

一般过去时:
The librarian categorized the new books. = 图书管理员对新书进行了分类。
They categorized the responses into positive and negative. = 他们将回复分为正面和负面。

现在完成时:
He has categorized all the data. = 他已经对所有数据进行了分类。
She has categorized her research papers by topic. = 她已按主题对她的研究论文进行了分类。

过去完成时:

They had categorized the information before presenting it. = 他们汇报前已将信息分类。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I had categorized my notes before the exam. = 我真希望我在考试前把笔记分类了。
