contraindicate:/ˌkɒntrəˈɪndɪkeɪt/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈɪndɪkeɪt/ | vt. |禁忌；表明…不宜；是…的禁忌症 | To suggest or indicate that a particular procedure or course of action should be avoided because it is harmful or inappropriate.  |Example:  His heart condition contraindicates strenuous exercise.  他的心脏状况表明剧烈运动是禁忌。


The doctor contraindicated surgery due to the patient's advanced age.  由于病人年龄较大，医生禁忌手术。


近义词：discourage, prohibit, warn against


反义词：indicate, recommend, suggest


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The patient's allergy contraindicates the use of penicillin. = 病人的过敏反应禁忌使用青霉素。
This medication contraindicates alcohol consumption. = 此药禁忌饮酒。


一般过去时:
The doctor contraindicated the surgery because of the patient's weakened condition. = 由于病人的身体虚弱，医生禁忌了手术。
High blood pressure contraindicated participation in the marathon. = 高血压禁忌参加马拉松。


现在完成时:
Recent studies have contraindicated the long-term use of this drug. = 最近的研究表明长期使用这种药物是禁忌。
The doctor has contraindicated any strenuous activity. = 医生禁忌任何剧烈活动。


过去完成时:
The previous test results had contraindicated the proposed treatment. = 先前的测试结果表明建议的治疗方法是禁忌。
His history of heart problems had contraindicated skydiving. = 他有心脏问题的病史，禁忌跳伞。


将来时:
The surgeon will contraindicate the operation if the patient's blood pressure remains high. = 如果病人的血压仍然很高，外科医生将禁忌手术。
The physician will contraindicate further treatment if the condition worsens. = 如果病情恶化，医生将禁忌进一步治疗。


虚拟语气:
If the test results were positive, the doctor would contraindicate the medication. = 如果测试结果呈阳性，医生将禁忌这种药物。
If the patient had a history of seizures, the procedure would contraindicate. = 如果病人有癫痫病史，该手术将是禁忌。


被动语态:
The treatment was contraindicated due to the patient's age. = 由于病人的年龄，该治疗方法是禁忌。
Surgery was contraindicated because of the risk of complications. = 由于并发症的风险，手术是禁忌。


疑问句:
Does the patient's history contraindicate this treatment? = 病人的病史是否禁忌这种治疗？
Does this medication contraindicate driving? = 这种药物是否禁忌驾驶？


否定句:
The test results did not contraindicate the proposed treatment. = 测试结果并没有禁忌建议的治疗方法。
The patient's age did not contraindicate the surgery. = 病人的年龄并没有禁忌手术。


PHRASE->
contraindicate surgery  禁忌手术

contraindicate medication 禁忌药物
contraindicate activity 禁忌活动
contraindicate driving 禁忌驾驶


词源:
The word "contraindicate" is derived from the Latin prefix "contra-" (against) and the verb "indicare" (to point out, to show).  It literally means "to point out against," thus indicating that something is inappropriate or harmful.
