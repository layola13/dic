pressor:/ˈprɛsər/| n. |压迫物；按压器；促进剂|A pressor is something that causes pressure or compression.  It can refer to a device that applies pressure, a substance that raises blood pressure, or a factor that promotes or accelerates a process.|例句：The surgeon used a pressor to stop the bleeding.  外科医生用压迫器止血。|例句：This medication acts as a pressor, increasing blood pressure. 这药作为升压剂，能提高血压。|近义词：compressor, pressurizer, stimulant|反义词：depressant
