inculpate:/ˈɪnkʌlpeɪt/| vt.|控告；归罪于|To accuse or blame someone for something.  The evidence inculpates the suspect in the robbery.|近义词：accuse, blame, incriminate|反义词：exonerate, absolve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prosecutor inculpates the defendant in the crime. = 检察官指控被告犯了罪。
They constantly inculpate others for their mistakes. = 他们总是把错误归咎于他人。


一般过去时:
The witness inculpated the driver in the accident. = 证人指控司机造成了事故。
The police inculpated him for the theft. = 警察指控他犯了盗窃罪。


现在完成时:
The investigation has inculpated several high-ranking officials. = 调查已经牵连了几名高级官员。
The evidence has inculpated the CEO in the scandal.  = 证据已经将CEO牵涉到丑闻中。


过去完成时:
They had inculpated him before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，他们就已经指控他了。
The court had already inculpated the defendant before the trial. = 在审判之前，法院就已经指控被告了。


将来时:
The police will inculpate the suspect with new evidence. = 警方将用新的证据指控嫌疑人。
The report will likely inculpate several individuals. = 这份报告可能会牵连到几个人。


虚拟语气:
If they found more evidence, they would inculpate the suspect. = 如果他们找到更多证据，他们就会指控嫌疑人。
I wish they hadn't inculpated him without sufficient proof. = 我希望他们没有在没有充分证据的情况下指控他。


被动语态:
The suspect was inculpated in the conspiracy. = 嫌疑人被指控参与了阴谋。
He was inculpated despite his pleas of innocence. = 尽管他声称无罪，但他还是被指控了。


疑问句:
Will they inculpate anyone else in this case? = 他们会在这个案件中指控其他人吗？
Who did the report inculpate? = 报告指控了谁？


否定句:
The police could not inculpate him without more evidence. = 警方如果没有更多证据，就无法指控他。
The investigation failed to inculpate the prime suspect. = 调查未能指控主要嫌疑人。



PHRASE->
inculpate someone with evidence = 用证据指控某人
inculpate someone in a crime = 指控某人犯了罪
be inculpated in a scandal = 被牵涉进丑闻
inculpate someone falsely = 冤枉某人
inculpate someone for a wrongdoing = 因不当行为而指控某人


词源:
From Latin *inculpare, from in- "in" + culpa "blame, fault."
