rambly:/ˈræmbli/ | adj. | 冗长的；漫无边际的；散乱的 |  Characterized by rambling or wandering; lacking a clear structure or direction.  It suggests a lack of focus or organization, often in speech or writing. | 例句：His speech was rambling and difficult to follow. 他的讲话冗长且难以理解。

His essay was rambling, jumping from one topic to another without a clear connection. 他的论文杂乱无章，从一个话题跳到另一个话题，没有清晰的联系。


近义词： rambling, discursive, meandering, diffuse, wandering, digressive | 反义词： concise, succinct, focused, organized, coherent


词性：形容词
