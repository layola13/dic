overteach:/ˌoʊvərˈtiːtʃ/| vt.|过度教授，教得过多|To teach something to someone excessively or to a point of diminishing returns.  It implies that the teaching is excessive, perhaps boring or overwhelming for the learner.|例句：The teacher overtaught the concept, causing the students to become confused.  老师对这个概念讲解过度，导致学生们感到困惑。|近义词：overexplain, inundate, overload|反义词：underteach, simplify
