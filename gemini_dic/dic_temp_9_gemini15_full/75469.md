frush:/frʌʃ/| adj.|  鲜嫩的；茂盛的；充满活力的 |  Describes something that is fresh and vigorous, often used to describe plants or vegetation.  It implies a healthy, vibrant state. |The frush green grass was a welcome sight after the long winter.  冬眠后，青翠欲滴的青草是一幅令人欣慰的景象。|  lush, verdant, thriving | withered, barren, decaying | 形容词
