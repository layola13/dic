hotchpotches:/ˈhɑːtʃpɑːtʃɪz/ | n. |大杂烩；混杂物 |A hotchpotch is a mixture of different things that are not well organized or do not go well together. It implies a lack of consistency or coherence.  | The garage was a hotchpotch of tools, old tires, and discarded furniture. 车库里堆满了工具、旧轮胎和废弃的家具，杂乱不堪。|hodgepodge, medley, mixture |order, organization|


USAGE->
This word is a noun and does not conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense changes are not applicable.  The word itself describes a state of being.
