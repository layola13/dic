refuel:/ˌriːˈfjuːəl/| vt.|加油；补充燃料|To supply (a vehicle, aircraft, etc.) with more fuel.  |例句：We need to refuel the car before we continue our journey.我们需要在继续旅程之前给汽车加油。|例句：The airplane refueled mid-flight.飞机在飞行途中进行了空中加油。|近义词：replenish, refill|反义词：deplete, exhaust|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plane refuels every few hours. = 飞机每隔几个小时加油一次。
He refuels his car weekly. = 他每周给他的车加油。

一般过去时:
They refueled the helicopter before the rescue mission. = 他们在救援任务之前为直升机加了油。
I refueled my car yesterday. = 我昨天给我的车加了油。

现在完成时:
We have refueled the generator twice today. = 我们今天已经给发电机加了两次油。
She has refueled her motorcycle several times this month. = 她这个月已经给她的摩托车加过几次油了。

过去完成时:
The pilot had refueled the plane before the emergency landing. = 飞行员在紧急着陆前已经为飞机加过油了。
They had refueled the car before they set off on their road trip. = 他们在开始公路旅行之前已经给车加过油了。

将来时:
We will refuel the ship tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将为船加油。
They will refuel the rocket before launch. = 他们将在发射前为火箭加油。

虚拟语气:
If we had refueled earlier, we wouldn't have run out of gas. = 如果我们早点加油，我们就不会没油了。
I wish I had refueled my car before leaving. = 我真希望我在离开前给我的车加了油。

被动语态:
The car was refueled by the mechanic. = 汽车由技工加油。
The aircraft is being refueled at the moment. = 飞机正在加油。

疑问句:
Have you refueled the tractor yet? = 你已经给拖拉机加油了吗？
When will the spaceship be refueled? = 宇宙飞船什么时候加油？

否定句:
They did not refuel the jet before takeoff. = 他们在起飞前没有给喷气式飞机加油。
We haven't refueled the boat since last week. = 自从上周以来我们还没有给船加油。


PHRASE->

refuel the tank = 给油箱加油
mid-air refueling = 空中加油
refuel the engine = 给发动机加油
