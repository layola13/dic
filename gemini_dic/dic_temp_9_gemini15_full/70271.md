fiddler:/ˈfɪdlər/| n. |小提琴手；骗子； fiddler crab(招潮蟹)| a person who plays the violin, especially one who plays for money or as a hobby;  someone who acts dishonestly or deceptively; a small crab with one claw much larger than the other. |例句：He's a talented fiddler. 他是一位才华横溢的小提琴手。|例句：That salesman is a fiddler; he cheated me out of my money.  那个推销员是个骗子；他骗了我的钱。|近义词：violinist, cheat, swindler |反义词：honest person
