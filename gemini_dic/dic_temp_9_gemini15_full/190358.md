sardines:/sɑːˈdiːnz/| n. |沙丁鱼|Small, oily fish, usually canned and eaten as food.  They are typically packed tightly in tins or cans. |Example:  We had sardines on toast for dinner last night.  昨晚我们晚餐吃了烤面包配沙丁鱼。|近义词：pilchard, sprat|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with larger fish like tuna or salmon.
