eurotrash: /ˈjʊərəʊtræʃ/ | n. | 粗俗的欧洲人；欧洲垃圾 |  A derogatory term for Europeans, especially those perceived as vulgar, tasteless, or of low social standing.  It often implies a lack of sophistication and cultural refinement, and is associated with stereotypical images of loud, brash behavior and materialistic values. The term carries strong negative connotations and is considered offensive. | Example:  He dismissed the tourists as "eurotrash." 他把那些游客斥为“欧洲垃圾”。  | 近义词: chavs, yobs, riff-raff | 反义词: refined, sophisticated, cultured | 名词
