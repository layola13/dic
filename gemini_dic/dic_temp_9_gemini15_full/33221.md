chronized:/ˈkrɒnɪsaɪzd/ | adj. |同步的，协调的 |Synchronized refers to two or more things happening at the same time or in a coordinated manner.  It implies a deliberate and planned arrangement to ensure simultaneous action or occurrence. | Example: The dancers' movements were perfectly synchronized. 舞者的动作完美同步。 The lights and music were synchronized for the show. 灯光和音乐为演出同步。|近义词：coordinated, simultaneous, concurrent |反义词：asynchronous, uncoordinated
