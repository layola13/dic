prereconcile: /ˌpriːˈrɛkənˌsaɪl/ | v. | 预先调节；预先协调 | To reconcile (accounts, etc.) before the regular reconciliation process.  This often involves identifying and correcting discrepancies before the formal reconciliation takes place. | 例句：The team prereconciled the bank statements with the accounting records to minimize errors during the month-end close. = 团队预先将银行对账单与会计记录进行核对，以最大限度地减少月末结账期间的错误。

The finance department prereconciled the data to ensure accuracy before submitting the report. = 财务部门预先协调数据，以确保在提交报告之前的准确性。| 近义词：pre-adjust, pre-balance | 反义词：post-reconcile


USAGE->
