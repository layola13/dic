nonreducibly:/ˌnɒn rɪˈdjuːsɪbli/ | adj. | 不可简化地；不能还原地 |  Incapable of being reduced or simplified further; irreducible.  It emphasizes the inherent complexity or indivisibility of something. | The system is nonreducibly complex, meaning its function cannot be understood by analyzing its parts in isolation.  这个系统不可简化地复杂，这意味着它的功能不能通过孤立地分析其各个部分来理解。 | irreducibly, intrinsically | reducibly, simply
