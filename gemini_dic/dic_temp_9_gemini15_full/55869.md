divis:/ˈdaɪvɪs/ | n. | 分配；除法；除数 | The act of dividing something into parts; the process of mathematical division; a number by which another number is divided. | Example: The divis of the inheritance was a contentious issue among the siblings.  继承遗产的分配在兄弟姐妹之间是一个有争议的问题。 The divis of 12 by 3 is 4. 12除以3等于4。| 近义词：distribution, apportionment, quotient | 反义词：combination, unification |  n.
