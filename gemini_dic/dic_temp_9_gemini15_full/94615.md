impregnating:/ɪmˈprɛɡneɪtɪŋ/| v.|使怀孕；使充满；使浸透|To make (a woman or female animal) pregnant; to fill or saturate something completely.|例句：The artist impregnated the canvas with vibrant colors.  艺术家用鲜艳的色彩使画布充满活力。

例句：The farmer impregnated his cows to increase the herd. 农民让他的奶牛怀孕以增加牛群数量。


近义词：fertilize, saturate, permeate, imbue|反义词：empty, deplete|词性:动词
