pleasanter:/ˈplɛzəntər/| adj.|更令人愉快的，更舒适的 |More pleasant; more agreeable.  It describes something that is to a greater degree pleasant.  This is the comparative form of the adjective "pleasant."|例句：The weather today is pleasanter than yesterday.  今天的天气比昨天更舒适。

The garden is pleasanter in the spring. 春天花园更令人愉快。|近义词：more agreeable, more delightful, more enjoyable |反义词：less pleasant, unpleasant, disagreeable
