catechuic: /ˌkætʃuːˈɪk/ | adj. | 儿茶酸的，含有儿茶酸的 | Relating to or derived from catechu, a brown extract from various plants, containing catechin and other tannins.  The term often refers to the acidic components of catechu. | The catechuic acid in the tea contributed to its astringent flavor.  儿茶酸在茶中使得其具有涩味。|近义词: tannic (referring to the tannin aspect) | 反义词:  None directly opposite, but perhaps "non-tannic" could be considered a contrasting term.
