multicar:/ˈmʌltiˌkɑːr/ | n. |多车，多车系统 |A system or arrangement involving multiple cars, often used in contexts like transportation, logistics, or racing.  For example, a fleet of cars used for a ride-sharing service could be described as a multicar system.  The term is not very common and often implies a specific technical or logistical setup. |例句：The race featured a multicar accident on the first lap.  |第一圈的比赛出现了一起多车相撞事故。|近义词：multi-vehicle, fleet |反义词：single-car
