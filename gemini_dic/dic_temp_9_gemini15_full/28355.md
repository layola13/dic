caravan:/ˈkærəvæn/ | n. | 旅行队，商队；大篷车；活动房屋；房车 | A group of people traveling together, especially in the past; a large covered vehicle used for travel, especially in deserts; a mobile home; a group of vehicles traveling together. |  例句：A caravan of camels crossed the desert. 一队骆驼穿过沙漠。  The family spent their summer vacation in a caravan.  这家人在房车里度过了他们的暑假。 | 近义词：convoy,  motor home,  trailer | 反义词：solitary traveler
