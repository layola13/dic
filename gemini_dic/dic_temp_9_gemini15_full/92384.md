hypocrite:/ˈhɪpəkrɪt/| n. |伪君子，伪善者 |A person who pretends to have virtues, moral or religious beliefs, principles, etc., that they do not actually possess.  A hypocrite is someone who says one thing but does another, or who behaves in a way that contradicts their stated beliefs. |He's a hypocrite; he preaches about honesty but cheats on his taxes. 他是个伪君子；他宣扬诚实，却逃税。|近义词：faker, deceiver, sham, fraud |反义词：sincere, genuine, honest
