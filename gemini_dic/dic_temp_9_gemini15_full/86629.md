helix:/ˈhiːlɪks/| n. |螺旋；螺旋线|A helix is a three-dimensional spiral shape, like a corkscrew or a spiral staircase.  It's a common shape in nature and science, appearing in DNA molecules, proteins, and some plants. |The DNA molecule is a double helix.  DNA分子是双螺旋结构。|spiral, coil, volute|straight line|名词
