carbonation:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 碳酸化；充气；苏打水 | The process of adding carbon dioxide to a liquid, typically water or another beverage, to create a fizzy effect.  Carbonation is responsible for the bubbles in soda and sparkling wine. |Example: The carbonation in this soda is quite strong. 这瓶苏打水的碳酸很浓。|Example:  The sparkling water gets its fizziness from carbonation.  苏打水的气泡来自碳酸化。 |近义词：effervescence, fizz |反义词：flatness
