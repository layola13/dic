primitivist:/ˈprɪmɪtɪvɪst/| adj. n.|原始主义者；原始主义的；崇尚原始的|Relating to or characteristic of primitivism, a style or movement in the arts that emphasizes simplicity, directness, and the expression of raw emotion.  It often involves a rejection of sophisticated techniques and a return to the perceived purity of earlier forms of art or life.|例句：The painting showed a primitivist style, with bold colors and simplified forms. 这幅画展现了原始主义风格，色彩大胆，形式简化。

例句：He is a primitivist who believes in a simpler way of life. 他是一个原始主义者，相信更简单的生活方式。


近义词：naturalistic, naive, unsophisticated|反义词：sophisticated, refined, complex|词性:adj. n.
