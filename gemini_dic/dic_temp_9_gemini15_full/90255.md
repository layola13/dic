housebreakings: /ˈhaʊsˌbreɪkɪŋz/ | n. | 入室盗窃(复数) | The act of illegally entering a house with the intent to commit a crime, typically theft.  It refers to multiple instances of such crimes. |  The police are investigating a series of housebreakings in the neighborhood.  警方正在调查该社区发生的一系列入室盗窃案。  Several housebreakings occurred over the weekend, leaving residents feeling vulnerable.  周末发生了几起入室盗窃案，让居民感到不安。 | burglaries, robberies, home invasions |  (no direct antonym, but the opposite action would be securing a home) | 名词
