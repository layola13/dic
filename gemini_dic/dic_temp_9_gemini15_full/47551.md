deauration:/ˌdiːɔːˈreɪʃən/| n. |镀金的去除；脱金|The removal of gold plating or gilding.  It involves the process of stripping away the gold layer from a metal surface. This can be done for various reasons, including recycling the gold, restoring an object to its original state, or preparing the surface for a different finish.|例句：The deauration of the antique frame revealed the underlying silver plating. 古董相框的脱金露出了下面的镀银层。
The deauration process was carefully controlled to avoid damaging the base metal. 脱金过程得到了仔细控制，以避免损坏底层金属。|近义词：degilding, stripping, removal|反义词：gilding, plating
