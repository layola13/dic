refinery:/rɪˈfaɪnəri/ | n. |炼油厂；精炼厂|A refinery is an industrial plant where crude oil is processed and refined into usable products such as gasoline, diesel fuel, and kerosene.  It's a complex facility involving numerous processes to separate and purify the different components of crude oil.|The oil refinery is located near the port for easy access to crude oil shipments.|近义词：processing plant, oil works |反义词：无

词源:
The word "refinery" comes from the verb "refine," meaning to purify or improve something.  "Refine" itself comes from the Old French word "raffiner," which ultimately derives from the Arabic word "raffaana," meaning "to purify."  The "-ery" suffix indicates a place where something is done.
