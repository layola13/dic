horses:/ˈhɔːrsɪz/ | n. | 马 |  The plural of "horse," referring to multiple equine animals.  Horses are known for their strength, speed, and intelligence. They have been domesticated for thousands of years and used for various purposes, including transportation, agriculture, and sport. |  例句：There were five horses in the field.  田野里有五匹马。

例句：We rode horses through the forest. 我们骑马穿过森林。


近义词：steeds (more poetic)

反义词：无
