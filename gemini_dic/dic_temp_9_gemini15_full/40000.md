consultation: /ˌkɒnsəlˈteɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːnsəlˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 咨询；会诊；商讨 | The act of consulting; seeking information or advice.  A meeting for consultation. |例句：The doctor recommended a consultation with a specialist. 医生建议去专科医生那里会诊。

例句：She booked a consultation with a financial advisor. 她预约了与理财顾问的咨询。


近义词：discussion, conference, meeting, advisement

反义词：decision (in the sense of acting without consulting)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We have regular consultations with our clients. = 我们定期与客户进行咨询。
The doctor holds consultations every Tuesday. = 医生每星期二进行会诊。


一般过去时:
I had a consultation with my lawyer yesterday. = 我昨天与我的律师进行了咨询。
They held a consultation to discuss the project. = 他们举行了一次会议来讨论这个项目。


现在完成时:
She has had several consultations about her health. = 她已经就她的健康问题进行了多次咨询。
We have completed the consultation process. = 我们已经完成了咨询流程。


过去完成时:
They had already had a consultation before making the decision. = 他们做出决定之前已经进行过一次咨询了。
The team had finished the consultation before the deadline. = 团队在截止日期之前完成了咨询。


将来时:
I will have a consultation with the professor next week. = 我下周将与教授进行咨询。
They will schedule a consultation to address the issue. = 他们将安排一次咨询来解决这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If I had had a consultation sooner, I might have avoided this problem. = 如果我早点咨询一下，我或许就能避免这个问题。
I wish I had had a consultation before investing. = 我真希望在投资前进行过咨询。


被动语态:
The matter was discussed in consultation with the board. = 这件事与董事会协商后进行了讨论。
The decision will be made after consultation with experts. = 决定将在与专家协商后做出。


疑问句:
Will there be a consultation before the final decision? = 最终决定之前会有咨询吗？
Did you have a consultation with the architect? = 你和建筑师进行过咨询了吗？


否定句:
They did not have a consultation before launching the product. = 他们没有在推出产品之前进行咨询。
The company refused further consultation on the matter. = 公司拒绝就此事进一步咨询。



PHRASE->
in consultation with = 与…协商
prior consultation = 事先协商
under consultation = 正在商讨中
seek consultation = 寻求咨询
a consultation fee = 咨询费
