reselection:/ˌriːsəˈlɛkʃən/| n. |重新选择，再选择 |The act of selecting again; a second or subsequent selection.  It often implies a previous selection that was unsatisfactory or needs revision. |例句：The reselection of the committee chair was met with some opposition.  重新选举委员会主席遭到了部分反对。

例句：After a flawed initial selection process, the company initiated a reselection of candidates. 在最初的选择流程出现缺陷后，公司启动了候选人的重新选择。


近义词：rechoice, second selection, renewed selection

反义词：first selection, initial selection
