credos:/ˈkriːdoʊz/| n. |信条；信经；圭臬|A creed is a statement of the beliefs or principles of a person or group.  It often refers to a formal statement of religious belief, but can also apply to any system of beliefs.|Example:  The company's credo emphasizes customer satisfaction and ethical practices. 这家公司的信条强调客户满意度和道德规范。|近义词：beliefs, principles, tenets, doctrines|反义词：heresies (反信条), disbeliefs (不信)|名词
