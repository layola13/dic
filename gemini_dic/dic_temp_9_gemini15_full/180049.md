reflecting: /rɪˈflɛktɪŋ/ | adj. | 反射的；反映的；沉思的 | Showing an image by reflection; representing or expressing something.  |例句：The lake was reflecting the sunset.  湖面映照着夕阳。

The mirror provides a reflecting surface. 这面镜子提供了一个反射面。


近义词：reflective, mirroring, showing

反义词：obscuring, hiding, concealing


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The still water is reflecting the clouds.  平静的水面反射着云朵。
The painting reflects the artist's emotions. 这幅画反映了艺术家的情感。


一般过去时:
The polished metal reflected the light brilliantly. 磨光的金属强烈地反射着光线。
Her actions reflected her upbringing. 她的行为反映了她的教养。


现在完成时:
The event has reflected poorly on the company's image. 这事件严重损害了公司的形象。
The changes have reflected a growing concern for the environment. 这些变化反映了人们日益增长的环保意识。


过去完成时:
The evidence reflected what had already been suspected. 证据反映了之前就已经怀疑的事情。
His words reflected what he had learned in his youth. 他的话语反映了他年少时学到的东西。


将来时:
The new policy will reflect the government's commitment to social justice. 新政策将反映政府对社会正义的承诺。
The results will reflect the hard work of the team. 结果将反映团队的辛勤工作。


虚拟语气:
If the surface were smoother, it would reflect light more efficiently. 如果表面更光滑，它会更有效地反射光线。
If the data reflected the reality, we would have acted differently. 如果数据反映了现实，我们会采取不同的行动。


被动语态:
The situation is reflected in the statistics. 情况在统计数据中有所反映。
The company's values are reflected in its mission statement. 公司的价值观在其使命宣言中有所体现。


疑问句:
Does the data accurately reflect the situation? 数据是否准确地反映了情况？
Does the poem reflect the author's personal experiences? 这首诗是否反映了作者的个人经历？


否定句:
The numbers do not reflect the true cost. 数字没有反映真实的成本。
His behavior did not reflect well on him. 他的行为没有给他带来好名声。



PHRASE->
reflect on:  反思, 沉思
reflect poorly on:  对...评价不好
be reflected in:  反映在...中
reflect light: 反射光线
deeply reflecting: 深思熟虑的
