ombudsmanship:/ˈɒmbʊdzmənʃɪp/| n. |监察员制度；申诉制度；调解；调停|The system of having an ombudsman to investigate complaints.  It is a process for resolving disputes and addressing grievances fairly and efficiently.  |Example: The company established a robust ombudsmanship program to handle customer complaints. 这家公司建立了一个健全的申诉制度来处理客户投诉。|近义词：mediation, arbitration, conciliation |反义词：injustice, oppression
