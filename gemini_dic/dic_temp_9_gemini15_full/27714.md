cane:/keɪn/| n. v.|手杖；藤条；鞭打|A cane is a stick used for support when walking, or a thin, flexible stem of a plant.  It can also be used as a verb, meaning to beat with a cane.|例句：He leaned heavily on his cane. 他重重地倚靠着他的手杖。  The farmer used a cane to beat the unruly horse. 农夫用藤条抽打那匹不听话的马。|近义词：stick, staff, rod|反义词：N/A
