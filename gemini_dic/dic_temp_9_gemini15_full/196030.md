sents:/sɛnts/| n.|句子|A sentence is a grammatical unit consisting of one or more words that express a complete thought.  Sentences typically contain a subject and a predicate, but there are exceptions.|例句：The cat sat on the mat.  猫坐在垫子上。|例句：He runs fast. 他跑得很快。|近义词：clause, phrase (in some contexts)|反义词：None|名词
