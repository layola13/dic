lacks:/læks/| v.|缺少，缺乏|to be without or deficient in something.|The plan lacks detail.  这个计划缺乏细节。|The project lacks funding. 这个项目缺乏资金。|possesses, has|abundant, plentiful|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lacks confidence. = 他缺乏自信。
The garden lacks sunlight. = 花园缺乏阳光。


一般过去时:
She lacked the necessary skills. = 她缺乏必要的技能。
The meeting lacked direction. = 会议缺乏方向。


现在完成时:
We have lacked support for years. = 多年来我们一直缺乏支持。
The company has lacked innovation recently. = 公司最近缺乏创新。


过去完成时:
They had lacked the resources to complete the project. = 他们缺乏完成项目的资源。
The country had lacked stability for decades. = 这个国家几十年来一直缺乏稳定。


将来时:
They will lack the manpower to finish the job. = 他们将缺乏完成工作的人力。
The team will likely lack motivation without proper incentives. = 没有适当的激励，团队可能会缺乏动力。


虚拟语气:
If he lacked the courage, he wouldn't have done it. = 如果他缺乏勇气，他就不会那样做了。
I wish I lacked nothing. = 我希望我什么都不缺。


被动语态:
The report is lacking in evidence. = 报告缺乏证据。
The presentation was lacking in clarity. = 演示文稿不够清晰。


疑问句:
Does the project lack funding? = 项目缺乏资金吗？
Does she lack the necessary experience? = 她缺乏必要的经验吗？


否定句:
He does not lack ambition. = 他并不缺乏雄心壮志。
The proposal does not lack merit. = 这份提案并非没有价值。


PHRASE->
lack of: 缺乏...

for lack of: 由于缺乏...
