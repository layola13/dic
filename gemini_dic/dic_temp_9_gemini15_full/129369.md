nomade:/ˈnoʊmeɪd/| n. |游牧民族；流浪者 |A member of a group of people who travel from place to place instead of living in one place all the time.  They typically move with their herds of animals, following the availability of grazing land and water. |Example: The nomadic tribes of the Sahara Desert have adapted to a harsh environment.  撒哈拉沙漠的游牧部落已经适应了恶劣的环境。  The artist led a nomadic life, traveling from city to city.这位艺术家过着游牧的生活，从一个城市旅行到另一个城市。|近义词：wanderer, rover, migrant, itinerant |反义词：settler, resident
