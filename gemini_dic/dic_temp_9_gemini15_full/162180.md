pre-Malaysian:/ˌpriːmæˈleɪʒən/| adj.| 马来西亚之前的；先于马来西亚的 |Describes something that existed or occurred before the formation of Malaysia as an independent nation.  The term often relates to historical periods, events, or entities. | Example: Pre-Malaysian political structures were vastly different from the modern system.  马来西亚之前的政治结构与现代体制大相径庭。| 近义词：antecedent, prior, preceding | 反义词：post-Malaysian
