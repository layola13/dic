plumbiferous:/ˌplʌmbɪfərəs/| adj. |含铅的，富含铅的|Containing or producing lead.  The term refers to substances or materials that contain a significant amount of lead, often as a constituent element or compound.|The ancient Roman water pipes were plumbiferous, leading to lead poisoning in the population.  古代罗马的输水管道含铅，导致人口铅中毒。|近义词：lead-bearing, containing lead|反义词：lead-free
