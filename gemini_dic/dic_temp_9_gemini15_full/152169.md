patriarch:/ˈpeɪtriɑːrk/| n.|族长；家长；（教会的）宗主教|The head of a family or tribe, especially the oldest male.  A high-ranking male in the Christian church.|例句：He was the patriarch of a large and influential family. 他是一个庞大而有影响力的家族的族长。

例句：The patriarch of the Eastern Orthodox Church resides in Constantinople. 东正教的宗主教居住在君士坦丁堡。


近义词：father, elder, chieftain, pope (in a religious context)

反义词：matriarch


词性:名词


词源：From Old French *patriarche, from Late Latin patriarcha, from Greek patriarkhēs, from patēr ("father") + arkhos ("ruler, chief, leader").
