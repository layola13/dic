indefatigable: /ɪnˈdɛfətɪɡəbəl/ | adj. |不知疲倦的；不屈不挠的 | Tireless and persistent in effort; incapable of being fatigued.  |例句：His indefatigable spirit enabled him to overcome many obstacles. 他不知疲倦的精神使他克服了许多障碍。

例句：She showed an indefatigable commitment to her work, working long hours without complaint. 她对工作表现出不屈不挠的奉献精神，长时间工作且从不抱怨。


近义词：tireless, tireless, persistent, persevering, assiduous, unflagging, untiring

反义词：lazy, indolent, lethargic, sluggish, weary, fatigued


词源:

* **in-:**  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **de-:** prefix often intensifying the meaning of the root word.  In this case, it contributes to the sense of completeness or thoroughness.
* **fatigable:**  derived from the verb "fatigue," meaning to tire out.  "Fatigable" itself means "capable of being fatigued."
