prepartitioning:/ˌpriːpɑːrˈtɪʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 预分区；预先分区 | The act of dividing a storage space into partitions before the main data is written.  This is often done to improve performance or manage data more efficiently.  | Example: Prepartitioning the hard drive improved the speed of the database application. 预先分区硬盘提高了数据库应用程序的速度。 | 近义词：preallocation, advance partitioning | 反义词：postpartitioning
