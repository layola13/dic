/ˌɪntərˈmjuːtʃuəl/| adj. |相互的，彼此的|  Relating to or involving each other reciprocally; mutual.  It emphasizes the reciprocal nature of the relationship. While often used interchangeably with "mutual," "intermutual" carries a stronger sense of two-way interaction and interdependence.
