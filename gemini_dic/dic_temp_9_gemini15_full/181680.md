relends:/riːˈlɛndz/| v.|再贷款，再借出 |To lend something again; to lend out something that has already been lent.|例句：The bank relends the money it receives from deposits.  银行将从存款中收到的钱再次贷出。|例句：The company relends the funds to smaller businesses. 这家公司将资金再次贷给小型企业。|近义词：reloan, lend again|反义词：repay, recall


USAGE->

The bank relends the money to its customers. = 银行把钱再次贷给客户。
She relends her books to her friends. = 她把书再次借给朋友。

He relended the equipment to the construction company. = 他把设备再次借给了建筑公司。
