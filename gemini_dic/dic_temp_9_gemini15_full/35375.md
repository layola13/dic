co-occurrences:/ˌkoʊəˈkʌrənsɪz/ | n. | 共同出现；共现 | The study analyzed the co-occurrences of words in a large corpus of text.  这项研究分析了大型文本语料库中单词的共同出现情况。|近义词：concurrent occurrences, simultaneous appearances |反义词：absence, non-occurrence


英文解析:Co-occurrences refer to instances where two or more events, items, or words appear together or within a specified proximity.  In linguistics, it often refers to the frequency with which words appear together in a text.  In statistics, it might describe the simultaneous occurrence of events.


例句:

The researchers found a high rate of co-occurrences between smoking and lung cancer. 研究人员发现吸烟和肺癌之间有很高的共同出现率。

The analysis of co-occurrences revealed a strong relationship between the two variables.  对共同出现情况的分析揭示了这两个变量之间的强烈关系。


词源:

The word is a combination of "co-" (meaning together or jointly) and "occurrences" (instances of something happening).  It's a relatively modern term reflecting the growing importance of analyzing patterns in data.
