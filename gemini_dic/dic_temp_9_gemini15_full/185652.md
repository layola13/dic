reverend:/ˈrevərənd/| adj.|尊敬的；牧师的|Reverend is a title of respect used before the name of a Christian clergyman.  It signifies respect and acknowledges their religious position.  It's typically used in formal settings.|The Reverend John Smith delivered a powerful sermon.  尊敬的约翰·史密斯牧师发表了一篇精彩的布道。|Honorable, respected|disrespectful, dishonorable|adj.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The reverend is leading the prayer. = 牧师正在带领祈祷。
He is known as a kind and reverend man. = 他以善良和受人尊敬而闻名。


一般过去时:
The reverend gave a blessing to the couple. = 牧师为这对夫妇祝福。
The congregation respected the reverend greatly. = 教区居民非常尊敬这位牧师。


现在完成时:
The reverend has served this church for thirty years. =  这位牧师已经为这个教堂服务了三十年。
The reverend has inspired many with his sermons. =  这位牧师的布道激励了许多人。


过去完成时:
The reverend had already finished his sermon before the choir began to sing. = 合唱团开始唱歌之前，牧师已经完成了布道。
The congregation had known the reverend for many years. = 教区居民已经认识这位牧师很多年了。


将来时:
The reverend will officiate at the wedding. = 牧师将主持婚礼。
The reverend will deliver a special message next Sunday. = 牧师将在下周日发表特别的信息。


虚拟语气:
If the reverend were here, he would know what to do. = 如果牧师在这里，他会知道该怎么做。
I wish the reverend had been able to attend the funeral. = 我希望牧师能够参加葬礼。


被动语态:
The reverend was respected by everyone in the town. = 这位牧师受到镇上所有人的尊敬。
The sermon was delivered by the reverend with great passion. = 布道是由牧师充满激情地发表的。


疑问句:
Is the reverend available to meet with us? = 牧师有空和我们见面吗？
Have you met the reverend yet? = 你见过这位牧师了吗？


否定句:
He was not a reverend, merely a respected teacher. = 他不是牧师，只是一个受人尊敬的老师。
The reverend did not attend the meeting. = 牧师没有参加会议。


PHRASE->
Reverend Father = 尊敬的父亲 (用于天主教神父)
Reverend Mother = 尊敬的母亲 (用于修女)
Reverend Doctor = 尊敬的博士 (用于拥有博士学位的牧师)
Reverend Sir = 尊敬的先生 (用于对牧师的尊称)
The Reverend Mr./Ms./Mrs. [Last Name] = 尊敬的先生/女士/夫人 [姓氏] (最正式的称呼方式)
