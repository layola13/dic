definitively:/dɪˈfɪnətɪvli/ | adv. | 确凿地；最终地；决定性地 |  Absolutely and without a doubt; conclusively.  It implies a finality or certainty that leaves no room for argument or further discussion. |例句：The evidence definitively proves his guilt.  证据确凿地证明了他的罪行。

例句：The study definitively showed that smoking causes cancer. 这项研究最终证明吸烟会导致癌症。


近义词：conclusively, decisively, certainly, undoubtedly, absolutely

反义词：tentatively, uncertainly, doubtfully, possibly

词性：副词
