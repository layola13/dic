compatible-with: /kəmˈpætəbl wɪð/ | adj. | 与…兼容的 |  Describes something that works well or is able to be used with something else without problems.  It indicates a harmonious or suitable relationship between two things. | 例句：This software is compatible with Windows 10.  这个软件与Windows 10兼容。  |近义词：consistent with, congruous with,  harmonious with | 反义词：incompatible with, conflicting with | 形容词


USAGE->

The new software is compatible with our existing system. = 新软件与我们现有的系统兼容。
