grunts:/ɡrʌnts/| n., v.|咕哝；哼哼声；猪叫声|A grunt is a short, low sound, often made by a pig or someone expressing displeasure or exertion.  It can be a noun referring to the sound itself, or a verb describing the act of making the sound.|例句：The pig let out a loud grunt. 这头猪发出一声响亮的哼哼声。

The weightlifter grunted with effort.举重运动员用力地哼了一声。|近义词：mumble, groan, snort, oink|反义词：shout, whisper
