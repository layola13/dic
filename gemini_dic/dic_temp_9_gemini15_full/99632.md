interlaces:/ˌɪntərˈleɪsɪz/ | v. | 交织；交错；互相缠绕 | To weave or twine together; to interweave.  It implies a complex and often intricate pattern of interwoven elements. |例句：The branches of the trees interlaced overhead, forming a leafy canopy. 树枝在头顶上交织在一起，形成一个绿叶遮盖物。

The threads interlaced to create a beautiful tapestry.  线交织在一起，创造出一幅美丽的挂毯。


近义词：intertwine, interweave, braid, entwine | 反义词：separate, disentangle
