decussated:/dɪˈkʌseɪtɪd/ | adj. | 交叉的；交互的 |  Crossing or intersecting in an X shape; arranged crosswise.  The term often refers to anatomical structures or pathways that cross over to the opposite side of the body.  | Example: The optic nerves decussate at the optic chiasm. (视神经在视交叉处交叉。) The fibers of the pyramidal tracts decussate at the medulla oblongata. (锥体束的纤维在延髓处交叉。) | 近义词：crossed, intersecting, interwoven | 反义词：parallel, uncrossed
