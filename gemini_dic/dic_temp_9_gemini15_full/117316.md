medals:/ˈmɛdəlz/| n.|奖章，勋章|A medal is an award given for an achievement, especially in sports or military service.  It's typically a small metal disc with an inscription or design.|例句：He received a gold medal for his outstanding performance in the Olympics. 他因在奥运会上表现出色而获得金牌。|例句：She was awarded a medal for bravery. 她因勇敢而被授予勋章。|近义词：award, decoration, badge|反义词：N/A
