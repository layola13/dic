deescalates: /ˌdiˈɛskəleɪts/ | v. |  使缓和；使降级；使平静 | To reduce the intensity or seriousness of (a situation).  | 例句：The police successfully de-escalated the tense situation.  警察成功地缓和了紧张的局势。

The mediator worked to de-escalate the conflict between the two parties. 调解员努力缓和双方之间的冲突。


近义词：reduce, lessen, mitigate, calm, soothe | 反义词：escalate, intensify, aggravate, inflame | 词性：动词

词源:  The word "deescalates" is formed by adding the prefix "de-" (meaning "to reverse" or "to remove") to the verb "escalate". "Escalate" ultimately comes from the Latin word "scala" meaning "ladder," reflecting the idea of something climbing or rising.
