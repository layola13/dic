flown-out:/floʊnˈaʊt/| adj. |飞出的；被驱逐出去的；筋疲力尽的 |  Describes something that has been flown out, expelled, or is extremely tired.  It's a past participle adjective formed from the phrasal verb "fly out." |例句：The bird flown-out of the cage was quickly caught.  飞出笼子的鸟很快就被抓住了。 The team felt completely flown-out after the long season.  这个队在漫长的赛季结束后感到筋疲力尽。|近义词：exhausted, expelled, evicted, depleted |反义词：refreshed, energized, invigorated
