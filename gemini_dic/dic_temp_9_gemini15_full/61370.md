embittered:/ɪmˈbɪtərd/ | adj. | 痛苦的；充满怨恨的 | Feeling or showing bitterness or resentment, especially as a result of past injustices.  | Example:  Years of hardship had left him embittered and cynical.  多年来的苦难使他变得痛苦和愤世嫉俗。

He felt embittered by his unfair dismissal. 他因不公平解雇而感到痛苦。|近义词： resentful, bitter, disgruntled, acrimonious | 反义词： content, happy, cheerful, satisfied |  adj.
