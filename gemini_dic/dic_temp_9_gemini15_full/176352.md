re-expressed:/ˌriːɪkˈsprest/| v. |重新表达；再次表达|To express something again, often in a different way or with more clarity.|The scientist re-expressed his findings in simpler terms for the public.  科学家用更简单的术语向公众重新表达了他的研究结果。|近义词：rephrase, restate, reformulate|反义词：obscure, obfuscate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-express their concerns regularly. = 他们定期重新表达他们的担忧。
The artist re-expresses her emotions through her paintings. = 艺术家通过绘画重新表达她的情感。

一般过去时:
He re-expressed his gratitude for their help. = 他再次表达了他对他们帮助的感激之情。
She re-expressed her disappointment with the outcome. = 她再次表达了她对结果的失望。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
