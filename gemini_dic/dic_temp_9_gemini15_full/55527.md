distributed:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪd/ | adj. | 分布的；散布的；分发的 |  Spread over a wide area; allocated or given out to several people or places. |例句：The company has a widely distributed network of offices. 这家公司拥有一个分布广泛的办事处网络。|例句：The teacher distributed the test papers to the students. 老师把试卷分发给学生们。|近义词：dispersed, scattered, spread, allocated |反义词：concentrated, centralized


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The workload is distributed evenly among the team members. = 工作量在团队成员中平均分配。
The company distributes its products globally. = 公司在全球分销其产品。


一般过去时:
The teacher distributed the handouts to the class. = 老师把讲义分发给全班同学。
The company distributed a press release yesterday. = 公司昨天发布了一份新闻稿。


现在完成时:
The company has distributed its profits to shareholders. = 公司已将其利润分发给股东。
The government has distributed aid to the affected areas. = 政府已向受灾地区分发了援助物资。


过去完成时:

词源:

The word "distributed" comes from the verb "distribute," which originates from the Latin word "distribuere."  "Distribuere" is composed of "dis-" (apart, away) and "tribuere" (to give, to assign).  Therefore, the core meaning reflects the action of separating and giving something out.
