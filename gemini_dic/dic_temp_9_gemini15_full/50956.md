designed:/dɪˈzaɪnd/ | adj. | 被设计的；设计的；有计划的 |  Something that is designed has been planned and made for a particular purpose.  |例句：This building was designed by a famous architect. 这座建筑是由一位著名建筑师设计的。|例句：The experiment was carefully designed to test the hypothesis. 实验经过仔细设计以检验假设。|近义词：planned, intended, created, engineered | 反义词：accidental, unplanned, haphazard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The software is designed for beginners. = 这款软件是为初学者设计的。
The system is designed to be user-friendly. = 这个系统的设计易于使用。


一般过去时:
The car was designed in Germany. = 这辆车是在德国设计的。
The bridge was designed to withstand strong winds. = 这座桥的设计能够承受强风。


现在完成时:
The product has been designed and is ready for launch. = 产品已经设计完成，准备上市。
The website has been designed to be mobile-friendly. = 网站已设计为移动友好型。


过去完成时:
The plans had been designed before the construction began. = 在施工开始之前，计划就已经设计好了。
The prototype had been designed and tested thoroughly. = 原型已经过设计和彻底测试。


将来时:
The new building will be designed by a renowned architect. = 新建筑将由一位知名建筑师设计。
The software will be designed to integrate with existing systems. = 该软件将被设计为与现有系统集成。


虚拟语气:
If the system were designed differently, it would be more efficient. = 如果系统设计不同，它会更高效。
I wish the website had been designed more intuitively. = 我希望网站设计得更直观一些。


被动语态:
The product is designed to meet customer needs. = 产品的设计是为了满足客户的需求。
The game was designed by a team of experienced developers. = 游戏是由一支经验丰富的开发团队设计的。


疑问句:
Is the car designed for safety? = 这辆车的设计安全吗？
Was the software designed to work on all platforms? = 这款软件的设计能否在所有平台上运行？


否定句:
The building was not designed for earthquake resistance.  = 这座建筑并非设计用于抗震。
The interface was not designed to be user-friendly. = 该界面并非设计为用户友好的。
