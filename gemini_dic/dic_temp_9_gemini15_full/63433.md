entreated:/ɪnˈtriːtɪd/| v.|恳求，哀求，乞求|to ask someone earnestly or beg someone for something|The child entreated his mother to buy him a candy.  孩子恳求他妈妈给他买糖果。
She entreated him to reconsider his decision. 她恳求他重新考虑他的决定。|beg, implore, beseech, supplicate|refuse, deny, reject|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He entreats his boss for a raise. 他恳求老板加薪。
She entreats her friends for help. 她恳求朋友帮忙。

一般过去时:
He entreated his father for forgiveness. 他恳求父亲原谅他。

现在完成时:
He has entreated his parents for years to let him go to college. 他多年来一直恳求父母让他上大学。
She has entreated the gods for help many times. 她多次恳求神灵帮助。

过去完成时:
He had entreated her to reconsider before she left.  在她离开之前，他曾恳求她重新考虑。
