reentry:/ˈriːentri/| n. |重新进入；重返；再入大气层|The act of entering or returning to a place or situation after having been away.  It often refers to spacecraft re-entering the Earth's atmosphere. |例句：The spacecraft's reentry into the Earth's atmosphere was successful.  宇宙飞船成功再入地球大气层。

The astronaut's reentry into the space program was met with great enthusiasm. 宇航员重返太空计划受到了极大的热情欢迎。|近义词：return, entry, re-entry|反义词：departure, exit
