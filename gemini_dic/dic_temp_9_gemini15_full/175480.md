rampant:/ˈræmpənt/| adj.|猖獗的；蔓生的；不受约束的|Unrestrained or excessive in growth or spread; widespread and unchecked.|例句：Crime is rampant in the city.  城市里犯罪猖獗。|例句：The rampant weeds choked the garden. 蔓生的杂草堵塞了花园。|近义词：uncontrolled, unchecked, widespread, prevalent|反义词：controlled, restrained, subdued, limited

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Rampant corruption threatens the stability of the government. = 猖獗的腐败威胁着政府的稳定。
The rampant vines cover the entire wall. = 蔓生的藤蔓覆盖了整面墙。


一般过去时:
Rumors ran rampant after the accident. = 事故发生后谣言四起。
The disease was rampant throughout the country last year. = 去年这种疾病在全国蔓延。


现在完成时:
Inflation has run rampant in the past decade. = 过去十年通货膨胀一直在肆虐。
The invasive species has become rampant in the region. = 这种入侵物种已在该地区泛滥成灾。


过去完成时:
Before the intervention, poverty had run rampant in the village. = 干预之前，贫困在村子里猖獗。
The rumors had become rampant by the time the truth emerged. = 真相大白时，谣言已满天飞。


将来时:
Without intervention, the disease will become rampant. = 如果不干预，这种疾病将会蔓延。
If unchecked, the weeds will run rampant across the field. = 如果不加以控制，杂草将遍布田野。


虚拟语气:
If the government had acted sooner, the problem wouldn't have become so rampant. = 如果政府早点采取行动，问题就不会如此猖獗。
I wish corruption wasn't so rampant in our society. = 我希望我们社会的腐败现象不要如此猖獗。


被动语态:
The spread of misinformation was allowed to become rampant. = 虚假信息的传播被允许泛滥。
The problem of homelessness is allowed to become rampant. = 无家可归的问题被允许泛滥。


疑问句:
Is crime rampant in your neighborhood? = 你家附近治安混乱吗？
Is the use of pesticides rampant in modern agriculture? = 现代农业中杀虫剂的使用是否猖獗？


否定句:
The government is trying to prevent crime from becoming rampant. = 政府正试图防止犯罪猖獗。
The spread of the disease was not allowed to become rampant. = 这种疾病的传播没有被允许泛滥。
