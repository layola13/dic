reprioritized:/ˌriːpraɪˈɔːrɪtaɪzd/ | v. |重新排序优先级，重新确定优先顺序 | To re-evaluate and change the order of priorities.  It implies a previous prioritization that is now being altered. | The project timeline was reprioritized after the new budget cuts were announced. 项目时间表在宣布新的预算削减后被重新确定了优先级。  | prioritize, rearrange, readjust |  deprioritize, ignore  | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We reprioritize tasks based on urgency. = 我们根据紧急程度重新安排任务优先级。
They reprioritize their goals every quarter. = 他们每个季度都会重新确定目标的优先级。

一般过去时:
The company reprioritized its marketing efforts. = 公司重新确定了其营销工作的优先级。
She reprioritized her to-do list after the emergency. = 紧急情况发生后，她重新安排了待办事项的优先级。

现在完成时:
We have reprioritized several projects this year. = 今年我们已经重新确定了几个项目的优先级。
They have reprioritized their investments in renewable energy. = 他们已经重新确定了对可再生能源投资的优先级。

过去完成时:
They had reprioritized their research before the funding was secured. = 在资金到位之前，他们已经重新确定了研究的优先级。
Management had reprioritized the product launch after the market analysis. = 在市场分析之后，管理层已经重新确定了产品发布的优先级。

将来时:
We will reprioritize the budget once the new data arrives. = 新数据到达后，我们将重新确定预算的优先级。
The team will reprioritize their tasks after the meeting. = 会议结束后，团队将重新安排任务的优先级。

虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would reprioritize the development of that feature. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们将重新确定该功能开发的优先级。
I wish we had reprioritized the customer support earlier. = 我希望我们早点重新确定客户支持的优先级。

被动语态:
The tasks were reprioritized by the project manager. = 任务的优先级由项目经理重新确定。
The budget was reprioritized to reflect the changing market conditions. = 预算被重新确定优先级以反映不断变化的市场状况。

疑问句:

Should we reprioritize our efforts? = 我们应该重新确定我们的工作重点吗？

否定句:
They did not reprioritize their commitments. = 他们没有重新确定其承诺的优先级。
We will not reprioritize the project until further notice. = 在另行通知之前，我们不会重新确定项目的优先级。
