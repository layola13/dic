slinks:/slɪŋks/| v.|偷偷摸摸地走，潜行 |To move smoothly and quietly, especially in a sneaky or furtive way.|The cat slinked through the tall grass.  猫偷偷地穿过高高的草丛。|He slinked away when he saw the police. 他看见警察就偷偷溜走了。|creeps, sneaks, slithers| marches, strides|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He slinks around the corner. 他偷偷摸摸地走到拐角处。
The shadow slinks across the wall. 影子悄悄地滑过墙壁。

一般过去时:
She slinked out of the room. 她偷偷地溜出了房间。
The thief slinked away into the night. 小偷偷偷地溜进了夜色中。

现在完成时:
He has slinked away before I could question him.  在我能问他之前，他已经偷偷溜走了。
The dog has slinked under the table to hide.  狗已经偷偷地躲到桌子底下去了。

过去完成时:
The cat had slinked away before we noticed it.在我们注意到之前，猫已经偷偷溜走了。
She had slinked into the room unnoticed. 她悄悄地溜进了房间，没有被人注意到。


将来时:
He will slink away when he hears the noise. 他听到声音就会偷偷溜走。
The mouse will slink into its hole. 老鼠会偷偷地溜进它的洞里。


虚拟语气:
If he were a thief, he would slink away. 如果他是个小偷，他会偷偷溜走。
I wish I could slink away unnoticed. 我希望我能悄无声息地溜走。


被动语态:
(Slinks is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an action done by the subject.)  (因为slinks描述的是主语主动进行的动作，所以很少用于被动语态。)


疑问句:
Does he slink away when he is in trouble? 他遇到麻烦时会偷偷溜走吗？
Why does the cat slink around the house? 为什么猫在房子周围偷偷摸摸地走动？


否定句:
He didn't slink away; he walked out boldly. 他没有偷偷溜走；他大胆地走了出去。
The dog didn't slink into the house; it ran in excitedly. 狗没有偷偷溜进屋子；它兴奋地跑进去了。
