overstocked:/ˌoʊvərˈstɑːkt/ | adj. | 库存过多的，积压的 | Having more stock than is needed or can be sold.  This usually refers to a situation where a business has too much inventory on hand, leading to potential losses due to storage costs, obsolescence, or markdowns. | 例句：The store is overstocked with winter coats because of the unusually warm weather. = 由于天气异常温暖，商店的冬衣库存过多。

The warehouse is overstocked with outdated electronics. = 仓库积压了过时的电子产品。


近义词：over supplied, surplus, overloaded, glutted | 反义词：understocked, undersupplied


词性:形容词
