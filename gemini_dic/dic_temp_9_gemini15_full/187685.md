rooks:/ruːks/| n.|  乌鸦；车（国际象棋）|A rook is a large, all-black bird belonging to the crow family, known for its intelligence and social behavior.  In chess, a rook is a piece that can move any number of squares horizontally or vertically.|例句：Several rooks were perched on the branches of the old oak tree. 几只乌鸦栖息在古老的橡树枝上。

The rook moved two squares to the left. 车向左移动了两格。|近义词：crow, raven|反义词：无|名词
