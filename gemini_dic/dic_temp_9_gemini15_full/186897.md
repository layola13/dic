rioting:/ˈraɪətiŋ/| n.|暴乱，骚乱|The violent act of a group of people, often involving disorderly behavior, destruction of property, and sometimes violence against people.|例句：The city experienced widespread rioting after the police shooting.  城市在警察枪击事件后发生了大规模暴乱。|例句：The rioting spread quickly through the downtown area. 暴乱迅速蔓延到市中心地区。|近义词：tumult, unrest, disorder, rebellion |反义词：peace, calm, order


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Rioting is happening in the streets. = 街头正在发生暴乱。
The crowd is rioting against the government. = 人群正在暴乱反抗政府。

一般过去时:
Rioting broke out after the election. = 选举后爆发了暴乱。
Serious rioting occurred in the capital city. = 首都发生了严重的暴乱。

现在完成时:
Rioting has erupted in several cities. = 几个城市都爆发了暴乱。
There has been extensive rioting following the announcement. = 声明之后发生了大规模暴乱。

过去完成时:
Rioting had already begun before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，暴乱就已经开始了。
The city had experienced similar rioting in the past. = 该城市过去曾发生过类似的暴乱。

将来时:
More rioting is expected if the demands are not met. = 如果不满足要求，预计还会有更多暴乱。
We fear further rioting will occur unless the situation improves. = 除非情况好转，否则我们担心还会发生进一步的暴乱。

虚拟语气:

I wish the rioting would stop immediately. = 我希望暴乱立即停止。

被动语态:
The rioting was quelled by the police. = 暴乱被警察平息了。
The damage caused by the rioting is being assessed. = 暴乱造成的损失正在评估中。

疑问句:

否定句:
