refractiveness:/rɪˈfræktɪvnəs/| n.| 折射率；抗拒性；难处理性 | The property of a substance that causes light to bend when it passes from one medium to another.  It also refers to the stubborn resistance to change or influence. | Example: The high refractiveness of the diamond made it sparkle brilliantly.  The refractiveness of the student made teaching him challenging. | 近义词：refraction, resistance, recalcitrance, obduracy | 反义词：compliance, docility, tractability | 名词
