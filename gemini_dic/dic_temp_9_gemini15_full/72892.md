follower:/ˈfɑːləʊər/ /ˈfɒləʊər/| n. |追随者，粉丝，信徒|A follower is a person who admires and supports someone or something, often a celebrity, political figure, or a particular ideology.  They may follow their activities closely, emulate their behavior, or advocate for their cause.|例句：She has millions of followers on Instagram. 她ins上拥有数百万粉丝。|例句：He was a devoted follower of Gandhi. 他是甘地的忠实信徒。|近义词：admirer, disciple, fan, subscriber|反义词：leader, pioneer, innovator


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He follows my blog regularly. = 他定期关注我的博客。
Many people follow his advice. = 很多人遵循他的建议。


一般过去时:
She followed him into the room. = 她跟着他进了房间。
Millions followed the royal wedding. = 数百万人观看了皇室婚礼。


现在完成时:
I have followed this band for years. = 我追随这个乐队多年了。
She has followed his career with great interest. = 她一直怀着极大的兴趣关注着他的事业。


过去完成时:
He had followed the instructions carefully. = 他已仔细按照指示做了。
They had followed the trail for hours.  = 他们沿着小路走了好几个小时。


将来时:
I will follow your instructions. = 我将遵循你的指示。
We will follow the map to find the treasure. = 我们将按照地图找到宝藏。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would follow his advice. = 如果我是你，我会听从他的建议。
I wish I had followed my doctor's orders. = 我真希望我听了医生的嘱咐。


被动语态:
His ideas were followed by many. = 他的想法被许多人所遵循。
The instructions were carefully followed. = 指示被仔细地执行了。


疑问句:

否定句:

They didn't follow the rules. = 他们没有遵守规则。
