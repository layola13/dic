dojos:/ˈdoʊdʒoʊz/| n. |道场；练武场；学习场所|A dojo is a place where martial arts are practiced, but it can also refer to a place where any skill or discipline is learned and practiced.  It emphasizes the importance of discipline, respect, and self-improvement.|Example: He trains in karate at a traditional dojo. 他在一个传统的道场学习空手道。|近义词：training center, school, studio|反义词：无
