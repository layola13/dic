shrewdly:/ʃruːdli/ | adv. | 精明地；狡猾地； shrewdly  |  In a shrewd manner; cleverly and skillfully, often in a way that is slightly dishonest or unfair. | 例句：He shrewdly avoided answering the question directly. 他精明地避开了直接回答这个问题。

She shrewdly negotiated a better deal for herself. 她精明地为自己争取到了更好的交易。


近义词：cleverly, cunningly, astutely, smartly, skillfully


反义词：foolishly, naively, stupidly, clumsily


词源：源自古英语 *scrūdian “to judge, discern”，与“shrewd” (精明的) 同源。
