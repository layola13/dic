pseudoexperimental: /ˌsuːdoʊˌɛkspɛrɪˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 准实验的；似实验的 |  Relating to or denoting research methods that resemble experimental methods but do not include all the features of a true experiment, typically lacking random assignment of participants to conditions.  Such methods are often used when true experimental designs are not feasible or ethical. |  The study employed a pseudoexperimental design, comparing outcomes in a treatment group and a control group without random assignment.  这个研究采用了准实验设计，比较了治疗组和对照组的结果，但没有进行随机分组。 | quasi-experimental, observational | true experimental |
