sheller:/ˈʃɛlər/| n. v. |脱壳者；去壳机；（使）脱壳|A person or machine that removes shells from nuts, seeds, etc.  A sheller can be a person or a machine that removes the shells from things like nuts, grains, or shellfish. |例句：The farmer used a sheller to remove the shells from the peanuts.  这个农民用脱壳机去除花生壳。


The automated sheller processed thousands of nuts per hour. 自动化脱壳机每小时处理数千个坚果。|近义词：huller, shucker|反义词：sheller (反义词取决于语境，例如，如果指“脱壳者”，反义词可以是“壳留者”等，但没有直接的反义词)|名词，动词
