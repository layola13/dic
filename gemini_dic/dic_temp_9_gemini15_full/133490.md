nonfermentability: /ˌnɒnfɜːmɛntəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可发酵性| The inability of a substance to be fermented.  This refers to the lack of ability of a substance to be broken down by microorganisms, such as bacteria or yeast, through fermentation. This often relates to the inability to be broken down into simpler substances with the release of energy. |The nonfermentability of this sugar makes it unsuitable for use in traditional bread-making.  这种糖的不可发酵性使其不适合用于传统的制面包。

The nonfermentability of the sample was confirmed by the lack of gas production during the test. 样品不可发酵性通过测试期间无气体产生得到证实。|fermentability|non-fertility, inertness|
