ootheca:/oʊˈθiːkə/| n. |卵鞘；卵囊|A protective case or capsule containing the eggs of some insects, arachnids, and other invertebrates.  It is often leathery or hardened.|例句：The praying mantis deposited its ootheca on a twig. 螳螂把它的卵鞘产在树枝上。

例句： Scientists studied the structure of the cockroach ootheca to understand its protective mechanisms. 科学家研究了蟑螂卵鞘的结构以了解其保护机制。


近义词：egg case, egg capsule, egg sac|反义词：无
