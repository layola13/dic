innovativeness:/ˌɪnəˈveɪtɪvnəs/| n. |创新性，革新性 |The ability to introduce new ideas, methods, or inventions.  It refers to the degree to which someone or something is innovative.  |Example: The company's success is largely due to its innovativeness in product design.  这家公司的成功很大程度上归功于其在产品设计上的创新性。|近义词：creativity, originality, inventiveness |反义词：conservatism, traditionalism
