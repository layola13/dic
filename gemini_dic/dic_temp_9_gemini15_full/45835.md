customizer:/ˈkʌstəmaɪzər/ | n. | 定制者，自定义工具 | A person or thing that customizes something.  A customizer can refer to a person who modifies or personalizes items, or a software tool that allows users to adjust settings and appearances. | Example:  The website offers a powerful customizer to create your own personalized theme.  这个网站提供了一个强大的自定义工具来创建你自己的个性化主题。 |近义词：modifier, personalizer, adjuster | 反义词：standardizer
