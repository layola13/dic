/ˈmoʊvə/ | n. | 移动者，搬运工 | A person or thing that moves.  | The movers carefully transported the antique furniture.  搬运工小心地搬运了古董家具。 | transporter, shifter | stationary, immobile | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mover carries the boxes. = 搬运工搬运箱子。
He moves furniture for a living. = 他靠搬家具为生。


一般过去时:
The mover carried the piano yesterday. = 搬运工昨天搬运了钢琴。
She moved to a new apartment last month. = 她上个月搬到了新公寓。


现在完成时:
The movers have finished moving all the boxes. = 搬运工已经搬完了所有的箱子。
They have moved three times this year. = 他们今年已经搬了三次家。


过去完成时:
The movers had already moved the furniture before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，搬运工已经搬走了家具。
She had moved out before I could say goodbye. = 我还没来得及说再见，她就搬走了。


将来时:
The movers will arrive tomorrow. = 搬运工明天会到达。
We will move to the countryside next year. = 我们明年将搬到乡下。


虚拟语气:
If I were a mover, I would be very strong. = 如果我是一个搬运工，我会非常强壮。
I wish I had moved sooner. = 我希望我早点搬家。


被动语态:
The boxes were moved carefully. = 箱子被小心地搬运了。
The furniture is being moved right now. = 家具正在被搬运。


疑问句:
Who is the mover? = 谁是搬运工？
When will they move the piano? = 他们什么时候搬钢琴？


否定句:
The mover did not move the heavy safe. = 搬运工没有搬动沉重的保险箱。
We won't move until next month. = 我们直到下个月才会搬家。
