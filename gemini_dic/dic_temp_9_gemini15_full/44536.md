crossway:/ˈkrɒsweɪ/| n.|十字路口，交叉路|A crossway is a place where two roads or paths cross each other.  It often implies a less formal or less major intersection than a junction.|例句：The accident happened at a busy crossway. 那次事故发生在一个繁忙的十字路口。|例句：We met at the crossway near the old church. 我们在老教堂附近的十字路口相遇。|近义词：intersection, crossing, junction|反义词：straight road, single track
