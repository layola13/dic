optical-character-reader: /ˈɒptɪkəl ˈkærəktər ˈriːdə(r)/ | n. | 光学字符识别器 | An optical character reader (OCR) is a device or software that converts scanned images of text into machine-readable text.  It uses optical recognition technology to identify characters and convert them into a digital format that can be edited and searched. | Example: The library uses an optical character reader to digitize old books. 图书馆使用光学字符识别器来数字化旧书。 | 近义词：OCR system, character reader | 反义词：None  |  词性: 名词
