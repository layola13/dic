mavens:/ˈmeɪvənz/| n.|专家，行家|An expert or connoisseur.  Often used informally to suggest someone with a deep and often enthusiastic knowledge of a particular subject.|例句：He's a maven of modern art. 他是现代艺术专家。|例句：She's a real maven when it comes to cooking.  说到烹饪，她可是个真正的行家。|近义词：expert, connoisseur, authority, specialist|反义词：novice, amateur, beginner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a maven of wine. = 他是葡萄酒专家。
They are mavens in their field. = 他们在各自的领域都是专家。


一般过去时:
He was a maven of 19th-century literature. = 他是19世纪文学专家。
She was a maven in the tech industry. = 她曾经是科技行业的专家。


现在完成时:
She has become a maven in the field of astrophysics. = 她已经成为天体物理学领域的专家。
He has been a maven of classic cars for decades. = 他几十年来一直是经典汽车专家。


过去完成时:
Before the conference, he had already established himself as a maven on the topic. = 会议之前，他已经确立了自己在这个话题上的专家地位。
She had been a maven of fashion design before she started her own company. = 在她创办公司之前，她就已经是时尚设计专家了。


将来时:
He will be a maven of artificial intelligence in the future. = 将来他会成为人工智能专家。
She will become a maven in her chosen field. = 她将在她选择的领域成为专家。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
