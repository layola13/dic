profilist:/ˈproʊfaɪlɪst/ | n. |个人资料创建者，侧写者 |A profilist is someone who creates profiles, often of individuals for investigative purposes.  This might involve gathering and analyzing information to build a comprehensive picture of a person's personality, behavior, and motivations.  They are often used in law enforcement, marketing, and other fields requiring detailed understanding of individuals. | Example: The police profilist built a detailed profile of the suspect based on witness testimonies and crime scene evidence.  警察侧写员根据证词和犯罪现场证据建立了嫌疑人的详细画像。 |近义词: profiler, investigator, analyst |反义词:  N/A
