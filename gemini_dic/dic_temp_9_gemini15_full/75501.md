fry-ups:/ˈfraɪʌps/| n. |油炸早餐，油煎食物|A fry-up is a full English breakfast, typically consisting of fried bacon, sausages, eggs, tomatoes, mushrooms, and fried bread, often with baked beans and black pudding.|Example: We had a hearty fry-up before our hike. 我们在远足前吃了一顿丰盛的油炸早餐。|近义词：full English breakfast, cooked breakfast |反义词：light breakfast, continental breakfast
