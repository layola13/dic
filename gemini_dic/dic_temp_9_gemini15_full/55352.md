distal:/ˈdɪstəl/| adj.|远端的，末端的| Situated farthest from the point of attachment or origin.  For example, the fingers are distal to the hand. |例句：The distal end of the bone is fractured.  = 骨骼的远端骨折了。

例句：The distal part of the colon is affected. = 结肠的远端受到影响。


近义词：terminal, farthest, peripheral|反义词：proximal


词源:  From Latin *distalis, from distare "to be distant," from dis- "apart" + stare "to stand."
