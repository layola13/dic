nonfortification: /ˌnɒnˌfɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 非强化；未强化 | The absence of fortification; the state of not being fortified.  This term is often used in the context of food, specifically referring to foods that do not contain added vitamins or minerals.  It can also refer to the lack of defensive structures in a place. | Example: The bread is characterized by its nonfortification, meaning it lacks added vitamins and minerals.  这个面包的特点是未强化，这意味着它缺乏添加的维生素和矿物质。| 近义词: unenrichment, absence of fortification | 反义词: fortification, enrichment |  名词
