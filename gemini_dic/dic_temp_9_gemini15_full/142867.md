out-measured:/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərd/| v.|超过…的测量结果；超出…的尺寸| to exceed in measurement or size; to surpass in quantity or extent.  |The bridge was out-measured by the increasing weight of traffic.  桥梁的承重能力被日益增长的交通负荷所超过。|近义词：surpassed, exceeded, outstripped|反义词：undermeasured, underestimated|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The architect out-measures the building's dimensions against the initial plan. = 建筑师将建筑物的尺寸与最初的计划进行比较。
This project out-measures all previous attempts in scale. = 这个项目的规模超过了之前所有的尝试。


一般过去时:
The final product out-measured the prototype in several key areas. = 最终产品在几个关键领域都超过了原型。
The team's performance out-measured expectations. = 团队的表现超过了预期。


现在完成时:
The construction has out-measured the original budget. = 建设费用已经超过了最初的预算。
This year's sales figures have out-measured last year's. = 今年的销售额超过了去年。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the company had out-measured its sales target. = 到年底，该公司已经超过了其销售目标。
The bridge had out-measured its design capacity due to the overload. = 由于超载，桥梁的承载能力已经超过了设计能力。


将来时:
We anticipate that the new model will out-measure its predecessor in efficiency. = 我们预计新车型在效率上将超过其前代产品。
The demand for this product will likely out-measure the supply. = 对该产品的需求可能会超过供应。


虚拟语气:
If the project had been better planned, it would not have out-measured the budget. = 如果项目规划得更好，就不会超出预算。
I wish the results had not out-measured our expectations; it makes things more complex. = 我希望结果没有超过我们的预期；这使得事情变得更加复杂。


被动语态:
The initial design was out-measured by the unforeseen challenges. = 初始设计被不可预见的问题所超越。
The project's cost was out-measured by inflation. = 项目的成本被通货膨胀所超过。


疑问句:
Does the current production out-measure the demand? = 当前产量是否超过了需求？
Will this year's harvest out-measure last year's? = 今年的收成会超过去年吗？


否定句:
The experiment did not out-measure the control group. = 实验结果没有超过对照组。
The company's profits did not out-measure their losses this quarter. = 本季度公司利润并没有超过其损失。
