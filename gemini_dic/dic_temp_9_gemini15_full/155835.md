phosphonic: /fɒsˈfɒnɪk/ | adj. | 膦酸的 | Relating to or denoting phosphonic acids or their derivatives.  Phosphonic acids are organic phosphorus acids containing a P=O bond and two P-OH bonds. They are used in various applications, including as flame retardants, pesticides, and in the synthesis of other compounds. | Example:  The research focused on the synthesis of novel phosphonic acid derivatives.  这项研究集中在新型膦酸衍生物的合成上。 | 近义词: organophosphorus (in some contexts) | 反义词:  None readily available.
