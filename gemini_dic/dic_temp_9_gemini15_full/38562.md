concocters:/kənˈkɑːktərz/ | n. |调制者；捏造者 |Those who concoct, especially in reference to creating something (often a plan or story) deceptively or imaginatively.  They might be chefs creating dishes, writers crafting narratives, or schemers devising plots. |The master chef and his team of concocters produced a culinary masterpiece.  顶级厨师和他的调制团队创作了一道烹饪杰作。| creators, inventors, devisers |  destroyers, dismantlers,  | 名词


USAGE->

The master chef and his team concoct delicious meals every day. = 顶级厨师和他的团队每天都烹制美味佳肴。
These writers concoct thrilling stories for their readers. = 这些作家为读者编造惊险的故事。
