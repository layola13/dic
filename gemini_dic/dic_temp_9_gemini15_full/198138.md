shopless:/ˈʃɒpləs/ | adj. | 无店的；没有商店的 |  Lacking shops; without shops.  This word describes a place or area devoid of retail establishments.  | Example: The remote island was shopless, requiring residents to rely on infrequent supply ships.  = 偏远岛屿上没有商店，居民不得不依靠不频繁的补给船。 | 近义词: storeless, shop-free | 反义词: well-stocked, full of shops
