picotee:/ˌpɪkəˈtiː/| n. |(尤指康乃馨的)细边花;|A picotee is a flower, especially a carnation, with a narrow edge of a contrasting colour.  It refers to the narrow border of a different color on the petal edge.|The picotee carnations were a vibrant display of colour.|近义词：edged flower|反义词：solid-coloured flower
