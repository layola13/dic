chemosis:/kɪˈmoʊsɪs/ | n. | 眼球结膜水肿 |  An abnormal condition in which the conjunctiva (the membrane that lines the eyelid and covers the sclera) of the eye is swollen and edematous, often appearing as a bulging, watery, or bloodshot appearance.  This condition is usually caused by inflammation or allergic reactions. | 例句：The patient presented with chemosis, suggesting an allergic reaction or infection.  患者出现眼球结膜水肿，提示过敏反应或感染。

The severe allergic reaction resulted in significant chemosis and blurry vision.  严重的过敏反应导致了明显的结膜水肿和视力模糊。| 近义词：conjunctival edema,  ocular edema | 反义词：None specifically, but  normal conjunctiva could be considered an antonym. | 名词
