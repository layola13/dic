clacker:/ˈklækər/| n.|咔哒声；拨浪鼓；闲聊的人|A clacker is a device that makes a clicking or clacking sound, often used as a toy or a noisemaker.  It can also refer to someone who talks excessively or idly. |例句：The children played with their clackers, making a rhythmic clicking sound.孩子们玩着他们的拨浪鼓，发出有节奏的咔哒声。

The old woman was a notorious clacker, spreading gossip all over town.  这位老妇人是个出了名的爱嚼舌根的人，在镇上到处传播八卦。|近义词：rattle, clapper, chatterbox|反义词：silent, quiet

词源:
