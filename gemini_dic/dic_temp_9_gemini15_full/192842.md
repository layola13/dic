searchings:/ˈsɜːrtʃɪŋz/ | n. |探索；追寻；搜寻 |The act of searching or seeking something;  a state of intense inquiry or investigation.  It can also refer to the results or expressions of such an inquiry.|例句：Her searchings for truth led her down many paths.  她的追寻真理之路让她走上了许多不同的道路。

例句：His paintings reveal deep searchings into the human condition. 他的绘画作品展现了对人类生存状态的深刻探索。


近义词：investigations, inquiries, explorations, quests


反义词：acceptance, complacency, resignation


词性: 名词
