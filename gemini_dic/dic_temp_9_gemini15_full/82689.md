guests:/ɡɛsts/ | n. | 客人，宾客 | People who are invited to a social event.  |例句：We had twenty guests at our wedding.  我们婚礼上有二十位宾客。
|例句：The hotel is full of guests. 这家酒店住满了客人。|近义词：visitors, invitees |反义词：host, hostess


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The guests are arriving now. = 客人们现在到了。
We have many guests this weekend. = 我们这个周末有很多客人。

一般过去时:
The guests left early. = 客人们早早离开了。
Many guests attended the party. = 许多客人参加了聚会。

现在完成时:
The guests have already eaten. = 客人们已经吃过了。
We have had several guests this week. = 我们这周已经接待了几位客人。

过去完成时:
The guests had already left when we arrived. = 我们到达时，客人们已经离开了。
All the guests had gone home before midnight. = 所有客人都已在午夜前回家了。

将来时:
The guests will arrive at 7 pm. = 客人们将在晚上七点到达。
We will have many guests for the holiday. = 假期我们将会有很多客人。

虚拟语气:
If we had more guests, we would need a bigger table. = 如果我们有更多客人，我们就需要一张更大的桌子。
I wish we had more guests at the party. = 我希望派对上有更多客人。

被动语态:
The guests were welcomed warmly. = 客人们受到了热烈的欢迎。
The food was prepared for the guests. = 食物是为客人们准备的。

疑问句:
How many guests are coming? = 有多少客人要来？
Are there any guests waiting? = 有客人正在等待吗？

否定句:
There weren't many guests at the party. = 派对上没有很多客人。
We don't expect many guests tomorrow. = 我们明天不期待很多客人。
