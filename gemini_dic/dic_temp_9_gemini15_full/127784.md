neighborly:/ˈneɪbərli/ | adj. | 友好的，邻居般的 | Friendly and helpful towards neighbors.  | The neighborly couple often helped each other with yard work.  邻居们经常互相帮忙做园艺工作。| friendly, amicable, neighborly | unfriendly, hostile, unneighborly | 形容词
