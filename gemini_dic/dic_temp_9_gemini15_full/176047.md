raveningly:/ˈrævənɪŋli/ | adv. | 贪婪地； voraciously |  Extremely hungrily; with intense eagerness.  |例句：He ravenously devoured the entire pizza in under five minutes. 他在不到五分钟内狼吞虎咽地吃掉了整个比萨。

例句：The dog ravenously lapped up the spilled milk.  那条狗贪婪地舔掉了洒在地上的牛奶。


近义词：voraciously, greedily, avidly, hungrily, fiercely


反义词：slowly, delicately, sparingly


词性：副词
