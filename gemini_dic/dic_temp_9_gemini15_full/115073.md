maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/ | n., v.|  策略；机动；调动；巧妙地操纵 |  A planned movement or action, especially one involving skillful changes of direction or position.  Also, to skillfully move or guide something. |

例句：
The general's maneuver secured victory. = 将军的策略确保了胜利。

The pilot skillfully maneuvered the plane through the storm. = 飞行员熟练地驾驶飞机穿过暴风雨。


近义词：
tactic, strategy, move, operation,  evolution

反义词：
direct, straightforward, simple, clumsy


词性：
Noun and Verb


USAGE->
