ebook:/ˈiːbʊk/ | n. |电子书 |An ebook is an electronic version of a book that can be read on a computer or other electronic device.  Ebooks are often cheaper than print books and can be easily stored and accessed. |I downloaded an ebook about ancient history. 我下载了一本关于古代历史的电子书。|digital book, e-publication |physical book, paperback |名词
