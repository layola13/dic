firstest:/ˈfɜːrstɪst/| adj.| 最快的，最先的 |The word "firstest" is a playful or informal superlative form of "first".  It's not considered standard English.  While grammatically understandable, it lacks the elegance and precision of standard English superlatives.  It's often used for humorous or emphatic effect. |例句：He wanted to be the firstest with the mostest. 他想要成为最先拥有最多的人。(幽默用法)  |近义词：fastest, quickest, earliest |反义词：slowest, latest
