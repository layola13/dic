hardships:/ˈhɑːrdʃɪps/| n.|艰难困苦，苦难|Difficulties and sufferings; severe or trying experiences.|例句：They endured great hardships during the war. 他们在战争期间忍受了巨大的苦难。|例句：The family faced many hardships after the father lost his job.  父亲失业后，这个家庭面临许多困难。|近义词：adversities, difficulties, trials, tribulations|反义词：ease, comfort, luxury
