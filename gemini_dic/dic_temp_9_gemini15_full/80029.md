go-to-pot:/ɡoʊ tuː pɑːt/| phrasal verb|每况愈下；衰败；毁坏|To deteriorate or decline in quality, condition, or value.  This idiom suggests a gradual and irreversible decline, often implying a loss of control or management.|例句：The garden has gone to pot since we moved.  自从我们搬家后，花园荒废了。

例句：The company's gone to pot since the new CEO took over.  自从新任CEO上任以来，公司每况愈下。


近义词：deteriorate, decline, decay, fall apart, crumble


反义词：improve, flourish, thrive, prosper


词性:phrasal verb
