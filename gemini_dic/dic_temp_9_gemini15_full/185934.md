revoyages:/ˌriːˈvɔɪədʒɪz/| n. |再航行，重游|A return journey; a revisit or repetition of a journey.|例句：After a long break, he embarked on a series of revoyages to his favorite destinations.  经过长时间的休息后，他开始了一系列重游他最喜欢的目的地的旅程。|近义词：return journey, revisit, return trip |反义词：first voyage, initial journey
