semi-desert:/ˈsɛmiˌdɛzərt/ | adj. | 半沙漠的；半荒漠的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a semi-desert; a region intermediate between a desert and a steppe or other more vegetated area, typically having sparse vegetation and low rainfall. |  The semi-desert landscape was dotted with hardy cacti and scrub brush.  半沙漠的景观点缀着耐旱的仙人掌和灌木丛。  The area is classified as a semi-desert due to its low precipitation and limited vegetation.  由于降水量低和植被有限，该地区被归类为半沙漠地区。 | arid, dry, sparsely vegetated | lush, fertile, well-watered |
