holidays:/ˈhɒlədeɪz/ /ˈhɑːlədeɪz/| n.|假期，节日|Periods of time when people do not work and often celebrate special events.|例句：We're going on holiday next week. 我们下周要去度假。|例句：Christmas is a major holiday in many countries. 圣诞节是许多国家的重大节日。|近义词：vacations, breaks, festivals|反义词：workdays


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Holidays are important for relaxation. = 假期对放松很重要。
My family always celebrates holidays together. = 我的家人总是聚在一起庆祝节日。

一般过去时:
We had a wonderful holiday last year. = 我们去年过了一个美好的假期。
They celebrated the holidays with a big feast. = 他们用盛大的宴会庆祝节日。

现在完成时:
I have taken many holidays this year. = 我今年休了很多假。
She has celebrated all the major holidays this year. = 她今年已经庆祝了所有主要的节日。

过去完成时:
We had already planned our holiday before the tickets sold out. = 门票售罄之前，我们已经计划好假期了。
They had finished all their work before the holidays began. = 节日前他们已经完成了所有工作。

将来时:
We will have a long holiday next month. = 我们下个月将有一个长假。
They will celebrate the holidays with their friends.  = 他们将和朋友们一起庆祝节日。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would go on a longer holiday. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会休更长的假。
I wish I were on holiday right now. = 我真希望我现在正在度假。


被动语态:
Holidays are often celebrated with family and friends. = 假期通常与家人和朋友一起庆祝。
The holiday was officially declared a public holiday. = 节日正式被宣布为公共假日。


疑问句:
Where are you going on holiday? = 你要去哪里度假？
What holidays do you celebrate? = 你庆祝哪些节日？


否定句:
We didn't have a holiday last year. = 我们去年没有休假。
They didn't celebrate the holidays this year. = 他们今年没有庆祝节日。
