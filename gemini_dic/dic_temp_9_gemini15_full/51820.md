dewfall:/ˈduːfɔːl/| n. |露水；夜露 |The condensation of atmospheric water vapor that settles on surfaces during the night, especially in cool or humid conditions.  It's a beautiful phenomenon often associated with early mornings. |例句：The dewfall was heavy last night, leaving the grass glistening. 昨晚露水很重，草地闪闪发光。

例句：We woke to a heavy dewfall on the fields. 我们醒来时发现田野上结了厚厚的露水。


近义词：morning dew, condensation

反义词：drought (lack of moisture)

词性：名词
