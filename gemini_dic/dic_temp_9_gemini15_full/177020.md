reanalyze:/ˌriːˈænəlaɪz/| vt.|重新分析，再分析|To analyze something again, often with a different approach or more information.|例句：We need to reanalyze the data to ensure accuracy. 我们需要重新分析数据以确保准确性。  例句：The professor decided to reanalyze the research findings in light of new evidence. 教授决定根据新的证据重新分析研究结果。|近义词：reexamine, review, reassess|反义词：accept, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reanalyze the market trends every quarter. = 他们每季度都会重新分析市场趋势。
We reanalyze the data to identify potential errors. = 我们重新分析数据以识别潜在错误。


一般过去时:
The scientists reanalyzed the samples after the initial results were questioned. = 科学家在最初的结果受到质疑后重新分析了样本。
She reanalyzed her approach to the problem after failing the first time. = 她第一次失败后重新分析了解决问题的方法。


现在完成时:
They have reanalyzed the data several times, but still haven't found a solution. = 他们已经多次重新分析了数据，但仍然没有找到解决方案。
The team has reanalyzed the budget and found some areas for improvement. = 团队已经重新分析了预算，并发现了一些改进之处。


过去完成时:
After the accident, investigators had reanalyzed the flight recorder data. = 事故发生后，调查人员重新分析了飞行记录仪数据。
They had reanalyzed the evidence before presenting their conclusions to the court. = 他们在向法庭陈述结论之前重新分析了证据。


将来时:
We will reanalyze the results once we have more information. = 我们一旦有了更多信息，就会重新分析结果。
The company will reanalyze its marketing strategy next year. = 公司明年将重新分析其营销策略。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would reanalyze the entire dataset. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新分析整个数据集。
It's essential that the data be reanalyzed before publication. = 在发表之前必须重新分析数据。


被动语态:
The data was reanalyzed by an independent team. = 数据由一个独立团队重新分析。
The report will be reanalyzed to address the concerns raised. = 报告将被重新分析以解决提出的担忧。


疑问句:
Will the committee reanalyze the proposal? = 委员会会重新分析该提案吗？
Should we reanalyze the initial findings? = 我们应该重新分析最初的发现吗？


否定句:
We did not reanalyze the data due to time constraints. = 由于时间限制，我们没有重新分析数据。
They will not reanalyze the results without additional evidence. = 没有更多证据，他们不会重新分析结果。



PHRASE->
reanalyze the data = 重新分析数据
reanalyze the results = 重新分析结果
reanalyze the situation = 重新分析形势
reanalyze the problem = 重新分析问题
reanalyze the evidence = 重新分析证据
