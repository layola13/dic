intra-urban:/ˌɪntrəˈɜːrbən/| adj.|城市内部的，市内的|Existing or occurring within a city or urban area.  It refers to activities, phenomena, or processes that take place within the boundaries of a city, as opposed to between cities (inter-urban) or outside of urban areas (rural).|例句：Intra-urban transportation is a major concern in many large cities.  许多大城市的市内交通是一个主要问题。
Intra-urban migration is a significant factor in urban growth. 市内迁移是城市发展的一个重要因素。|近义词：inner-city, municipal, metropolitan (in some contexts)|反义词：inter-urban, extra-urban, rural
