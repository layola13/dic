darnel:/ˈdɑːrnl/ | n. |毒麦，野麦 | A type of poisonous weed that resembles wheat and rye, often found growing amongst crops.  Its seeds can contaminate grain and cause illness if ingested. |Example: The farmer carefully removed the darnel from his wheat field to prevent contamination.  农民仔细地从他的麦田里清除毒麦以防止污染。|近义词：tares, cockle|反义词：wheat, rye (in the context of desirable crops)
