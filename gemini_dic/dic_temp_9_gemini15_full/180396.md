refuter:/rɪˈfjuːtər/ | n. |驳斥者；反驳者 | A person who refutes or denies something.  | The refuter presented compelling evidence against the defendant's claims. 驳斥者提出了令人信服的证据来反驳被告的主张。 | opponent, contradictor, denier | supporter, confirmer | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He refutes the accusations. = 他驳斥了这些指控。
She refutes the theory regularly. = 她经常驳斥这个理论。

一般过去时:
They refuted the claims in their report. = 他们在报告中驳斥了这些说法。
He refuted the evidence presented by the prosecution. = 他驳斥了检方提出的证据。

现在完成时:
She has refuted all the criticism leveled against her. = 她已经驳斥了所有针对她的批评。
Scientists have refuted the earlier hypothesis. = 科学家们已经驳斥了早先的假设。

过去完成时:
They had refuted similar arguments before. = 他们以前已经驳斥过类似的论点。
He had already refuted that point in his previous speech. = 他已经在之前的演讲中驳斥了那一点。

将来时:
She will refute the allegations in court. = 她将在法庭上驳斥这些指控。
They will refute the findings of the investigation. = 他们将驳斥调查的结果。

虚拟语气:
If he were to refute the evidence, the case would collapse. = 如果他能驳斥证据，案件就会崩溃。
I wish I could refute the rumors effectively. = 我希望我能有效地驳斥谣言。

被动语态:
The theory was refuted by later research. = 这一理论后来被研究驳斥了。
His arguments were refuted by the opposing counsel. = 他的论点被对方律师驳斥了。

疑问句:
Who refuted the initial hypothesis? = 谁驳斥了最初的假设？
Can you refute his claims with evidence? = 你能用证据驳斥他的说法吗？

否定句:
He did not refute the accusations directly. = 他没有直接驳斥这些指控。
She could not refute the evidence presented against her. = 她无法驳斥针对她的证据。


PHRASE->
