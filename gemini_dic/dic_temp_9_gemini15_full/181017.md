reimprisoning: /ˌriːɪmˈprɪzənɪŋ/ | v. | 再监禁，重新入狱 | The act of imprisoning someone again after they have been released.  It implies a renewed imprisonment, often for a related or subsequent offense. | Example:  The judge ordered the reimprisoning of the defendant after he violated his parole. =  法官下令再次监禁被告，因为他违反了假释规定。

The reimprisoning of political prisoners sparked widespread protests. =  再次监禁政治犯引发了广泛的抗议。


近义词：re-incarceration, re-confinement

反义词：release, liberation

词性：动词
