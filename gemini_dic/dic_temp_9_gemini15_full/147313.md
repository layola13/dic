/ˌoʊvər məˈnɑːpəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 垄断过度；过度控制 | To control or dominate something to an excessive degree, often to the detriment of others.  This word combines "over-" (excessively) with "monopolize" (to have exclusive control of something). |  The company overmonopolizes the market for software, leaving little room for competitors.  该公司过度垄断软件市场，几乎没有给竞争对手留下空间。

The government's policies overmonopolize the healthcare system, leading to high costs and limited access for many citizens. 政府的政策过度垄断医疗系统，导致许多公民医疗费用高昂且获取医疗资源有限。| 近义词：dominate, control excessively, corner the market | 反义词：share, distribute fairly, democratize
