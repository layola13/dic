/ˌɪntərˈlɛndz/ | v. | 互借；互相借贷 | To lend to and borrow from each other.  Interlending involves the reciprocal exchange of library materials between institutions.  This allows institutions to provide access to a wider range of resources than they could afford to own individually. | Libraries often interlend books and journals to meet the research needs of their patrons. 图书馆经常互借书籍和期刊以满足其用户的研究需求。 |  |  | verb
