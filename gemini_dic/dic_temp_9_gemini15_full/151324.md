parishioner:/pəˈrɪʃənər/ | n. |教区居民，教友 | A member of a parish, especially one who regularly attends a particular church.  | Example:  The parishioners were very welcoming to the new priest. 教区居民对新来的牧师非常欢迎。  The church relies heavily on the donations of its parishioners. 这座教堂很大程度上依赖于教区居民的捐款。 |近义词: member of a congregation, churchgoer |反义词: outsider, stranger
