reticularian:/rɪtɪˈkjʊləriən/| adj. n.| 网状的；网状生物| Relating to or characteristic of a reticulum; resembling a net or network in structure.  The term is often used in biology to describe organisms with a net-like structure, particularly certain protozoa. | Example:  The reticularian protist exhibited a complex, interconnected network of filaments.  网状原生生物表现出复杂互连的细丝网络。|近义词：netlike, reticulated|反义词：solid, compact|形容词，名词
