hurled:/hɜːld/| vt.| 投掷；猛掷；大声喊叫 | To throw something with great force.  |例句：He hurled a stone at the window. 他朝窗户扔了一块石头。

例句：The angry man hurled insults at his opponent.  那个愤怒的男人向他的对手大喊大叫。|近义词：throw, fling, cast, toss |反义词：receive, catch


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hurls the ball to his teammate. = 他把球扔给他的队友。
She hurls insults at anyone who disagrees with her. = 她对任何不同意她的人破口大骂。

一般过去时:
The pitcher hurled the ball with great speed. = 投手以极快的速度投球。
He hurled himself onto the couch in exhaustion. = 他筋疲力尽地把自己扔到沙发上。

现在完成时:
The volcano has hurled ash and rocks for miles. = 火山喷射出灰烬和岩石，绵延数英里。
She has hurled herself into her work since the promotion. = 自从升职以来，她全身心地投入到工作中。

过去完成时:

They had hurled accusations at each other for hours before reaching an agreement. = 在达成协议之前，他们互相指责了好几个小时。

虚拟语气:
