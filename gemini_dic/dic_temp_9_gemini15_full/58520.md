duets:/ˈduːɛts/ | n. |二重奏，对唱 | A duet is a musical composition for two performers, typically vocalists, but also instrumentalists.  It can be a song, a piece of instrumental music, or a section within a larger work. | Example: The opera featured a beautiful duet between the soprano and tenor.  这部歌剧以女高音和男高音之间的一段优美的二重奏为特色。  The piano duet was incredibly well-played. 这首钢琴二重奏演奏得非常好。|近义词：double act, ensemble (for a smaller group)|反义词：solo, solo performance
