earthlings:/ˈɜːrθlɪŋz/| n. |地球人，凡人|Inhabitants of the planet Earth; people.  The term often implies a slightly detached or even humorous perspective, contrasting humans with other beings (like aliens).|例句：Are we alone in the universe, or are there other earthlings out there?  宇宙中只有我们吗，还是有其他地球人存在？

例句：Some scientists believe that earthlings are not the only intelligent life in the galaxy. 一些科学家认为地球人并非银河系中唯一智慧生命。


近义词：humans, people, mortals|反义词：aliens, extraterrestrials|词性：名词
