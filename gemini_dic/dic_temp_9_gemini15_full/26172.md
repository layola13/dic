butcherers:/ˈbʊtʃərərz/| n. |屠夫们，肉贩们 |Those who work as butchers, collectively.  The word refers to a group of people involved in the trade of butchering meat. |The butcherers are preparing the meat for the upcoming festival. 屠夫们正在为即将到来的节日准备肉类。The local butcherers association held a meeting to discuss new regulations. 当地屠夫协会举行会议讨论新规章。| butchers, meat cutters |  farmers, bakers (antonyms are less direct,  referring to contrasting professions)
