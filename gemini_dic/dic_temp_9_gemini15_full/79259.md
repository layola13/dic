glassing:/ˈɡlæsiŋ/| v.|玻璃化；凝视；用玻璃覆盖|To make something glassy or smooth; to stare intently; to cover something with glass.|例句：The potter glazed the ceramic pieces, giving them a smooth, glassy finish. 陶工给陶瓷片上釉，使它们具有光滑的玻璃质感。

例句：He stood there, glassing at the distant mountains, lost in thought. 他站在那里，凝视着远处的山脉，陷入了沉思。


近义词：glazing, staring, covering|反义词：roughening, ignoring, uncovering|词性:动词
