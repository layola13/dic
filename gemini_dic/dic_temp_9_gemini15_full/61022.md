elision:/ɪˈlɪʒən/| n. |省略；脱落；(语音的)音节省略|The omission of a sound, syllable, or word.  It's a common feature in both spoken and written language, often used for euphony or to create a more concise expression. |Example:  The contraction "can't" is an elision of "cannot".  = “can’t”是“cannot”的省略。 The poet used elision to create a smoother rhythm. = 诗人运用省略来创造更流畅的节奏。|近义词：omission, deletion, contraction|反义词：inclusion, addition|无
