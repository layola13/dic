recalculates:/ˌriːˈkælkjuleɪts/| v.|重新计算，再计算|To calculate something again, often because of new information or a mistake in the original calculation.|The software automatically recalculates the total when you add or remove items.|近义词：recompute, recalculate, rethink|反义词：calculate (initially)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The computer recalculates the route every few seconds. = 电脑每隔几秒钟重新计算路线。
She recalculates her budget every month. = 她每月重新计算预算。


一般过去时:
He recalculated the figures after discovering an error. = 他发现错误后重新计算了数字。
The engineers recalculated the structural load after the earthquake. = 地震后工程师们重新计算了结构负荷。
