polyscope:/ˈpɑːliˌskoʊp/ | n. | 多视野镜；多功能显微镜 | A polyscope is a type of microscope that allows for viewing a sample from multiple perspectives simultaneously, often incorporating various imaging modalities.  It can provide a more comprehensive view of the sample than a traditional microscope. | Example: The researchers used a polyscope to study the three-dimensional structure of the cell.  研究人员使用多视野镜来研究细胞的三维结构。 | 近义词：microscope (with multiple functionalities), multi-view imaging system | 反义词：无
