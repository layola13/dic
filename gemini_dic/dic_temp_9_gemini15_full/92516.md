hypothetist: /haɪˈpɒθɪtɪst/ | n. | 假设者，假设论者 | A person who formulates or works with hypotheses.  Someone who proposes theories or explanations based on limited evidence, often as a starting point for further investigation. | Example: The hypothetist presented a compelling model, albeit based on preliminary data.  假设论者提出了一个令人信服的模型，尽管它基于初步数据。
