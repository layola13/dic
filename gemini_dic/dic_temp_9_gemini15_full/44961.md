crystal's:/ˈkrɪstəlz/| n. |水晶的；结晶的|The possessive form of "crystal," indicating belonging to or relating to a crystal.  It can refer to a single crystal or multiple crystals.  It is commonly used as an adjective or part of a noun phrase.|例句：The crystal's clarity was stunning.  (水晶的清澈令人惊叹。)

The crystal's facets shimmered in the sunlight. (水晶的刻面在阳光下闪闪发光。) |近义词: crystalline's|反义词: amorphous's
