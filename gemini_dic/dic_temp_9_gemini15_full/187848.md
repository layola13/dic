rosettes:/rəʊˈzɛts/ | n. |莲座；玫瑰花结；奖章；小玫瑰花|A rosette is a decorative ornament or design, often shaped like a rose or having radiating petals. It can be made of various materials and found in a variety of settings, from architectural details to culinary creations.|The cake was decorated with delicate sugar rosettes.  蛋糕上装饰着精致的糖玫瑰花结。| The gymnast received a gold rosette for her performance. 体操运动员因其表现获得金奖章。| medal, badge, decoration | (no direct antonym)| 名词
