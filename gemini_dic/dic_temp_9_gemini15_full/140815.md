on-ice:/ˌɒnˈaɪs/| adj.|待定的；搁置的；暂停的|On-ice refers to something that has been temporarily suspended or postponed, often indefinitely. It implies a state of inactivity or delay, but not necessarily cancellation.|例句：The project is on ice until further notice.  项目无限期搁置，直至另行通知。|例句：Their wedding plans are currently on ice. 他们的婚礼计划目前被搁置了。|近义词：postponed, delayed, suspended, tabled|反义词：active, proceeding, underway
