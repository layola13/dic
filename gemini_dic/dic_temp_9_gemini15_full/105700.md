kinking:/ˈkɪŋkɪŋ/| v.|弯曲；打结；(使)发生故障|The process of bending or twisting something out of shape, often creating a sharp bend or curve.  It can also refer to a malfunction or disruption in a system or process.|例句：The garden hose was kinking, restricting the water flow. 花园软管弯曲了，限制了水流。
The wire kinks easily. 这根电线很容易弯曲。|近义词：bending, twisting, buckling, wrinkling|反义词：straightening, unbending
