elicit:/ɪˈlɪsɪt/ | vt. | 引出，探出，诱出 | To evoke or draw out (a response, answer, or fact) from someone.  | The teacher tried to elicit a response from the students. 老师试图从学生那里引出回应。 | evoke, extract, draw out | suppress, conceal | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The interview aims to elicit key information from the candidate.  = 面试旨在从候选人那里引出关键信息。
Her questions elicited a surprising confession. = 她的问题诱使他做了一个令人惊讶的坦白。

一般过去时:
The detective elicited a confession from the suspect. = 侦探从嫌疑人那里套出了供词。
The experiment elicited no significant results. = 实验没有得出任何显著的结果。

现在完成时:
The survey has elicited a wide range of responses. = 调查已经引来了各种各样的回应。
They have elicited support from several organizations. = 他们已经从几个组织那里获得了支持。

过去完成时:
He had elicited the information before the meeting. = 他在会议前就已经获得了信息。
The police had elicited the truth through careful questioning. = 警方通过仔细的盘问套出了真相。

将来时:
The new marketing campaign will elicit strong customer interest. = 新的营销活动将引起客户强烈的兴趣。
We hope the discussion will elicit new ideas. = 我们希望讨论能够引出新的想法。

虚拟语气:
If only I could elicit a more detailed explanation. = 要是我能引出更详细的解释就好了。
I wish I could elicit a response from him. = 我希望我能得到他的回应。

被动语态:
Information was elicited from witnesses. = 信息是从证人那里获得的。
A detailed report was elicited from the committee. = 委员会提交了一份详细的报告。


疑问句:
Can we elicit more information from the data? = 我们能从数据中提取更多信息吗？
How can we elicit cooperation from our competitors? = 我们如何才能得到竞争对手的合作？

否定句:
The questions failed to elicit a response. = 这些问题未能引出回应。
He couldn't elicit any information from her. = 他无法从她那里得到任何信息。


PHRASE->
elicit a response = 引出回应
elicit information = 获取信息
elicit a confession = 诱使认罪
elicit support = 获得支持
elicit laughter = 引来笑声


词源:
From Latin *ēlicere "to entice out, lure out," from ē- "out" + licere "to allure, entice."
