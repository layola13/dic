funkiness:/ˈfʌŋkinɛs/ | n. | 特殊的风格；时髦；韵味；怪异；不寻常 |  A quality of being funky;  a distinctive style or character, often associated with something unconventional, exciting, or cool. It can also refer to a slightly unpleasant smell or a strange or unusual quality. | 例句：The band's funkiness captivated the audience. 乐队的独特风格迷住了观众。

The funkiness of the old house added to its charm.  这栋老房子的怪异增添了它的魅力。


近义词：stylishness, coolness, individuality, quirkiness, oddness | 反义词：ordinariness, dullness, banality, conventionality


词源：源于funk (n.)  “一种难闻的气味；低沉的忧郁；一种节奏蓝调音乐风格”，最终源于荷兰语或低地德语单词，意为“一种难闻的气味”。  Funk作为一种音乐风格的含义，赋予了funkiness积极的含义，代表着一种独特的，通常是令人兴奋的风格。
