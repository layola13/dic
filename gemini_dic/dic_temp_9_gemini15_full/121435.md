misinstructs:/ˌmɪsɪnˈstrʌkts/| vt.|错误指导，误导|To give someone the wrong instructions or information, leading them to do something incorrectly or make a mistake.|例句：The teacher misinstructed the students on the experiment, leading to inaccurate results.  老师在实验指导上误导了学生，导致结果不准确。

例句：He was misinstructed about the deadline and missed the submission. 他被错误告知了截止日期，错过了提交。


近义词：mislead, misguide, confuse, deceive

反义词：instruct correctly, guide properly, enlighten
