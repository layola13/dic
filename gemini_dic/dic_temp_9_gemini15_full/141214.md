oophyte:/ˈoʊəfaɪt/| n. |卵原细胞；卵母细胞|A female gametophyte, specifically the multicellular structure that produces the egg cell in plants.  It develops from a megaspore and contains the archegonium, which houses the egg.| Example: The oophyte develops within the ovule and is crucial for fertilization in flowering plants.|近义词：female gametophyte, megaspore (in a broader sense)|反义词：antheridium (male gametophyte)


USAGE->
This word is primarily used in botanical contexts and doesn't lend itself to typical tense variations like the example word "what."  The usage is largely descriptive and declarative.

Example 1 (Present Tense): The oophyte is a crucial component of plant reproduction. = 卵原细胞是植物繁殖的关键组成部分。

Example 2 (Past Tense): Scientists observed the oophyte's development under a microscope. = 科学家们在显微镜下观察了卵原细胞的发育。
