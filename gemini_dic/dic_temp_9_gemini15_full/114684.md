malignity:/məˈlɪɡnɪti/| n. |恶意；恶性；险恶|The inherent wickedness or malevolence of a person or thing.|例句：The malignancy of the tumor was confirmed by the biopsy.  肿瘤的恶性已被活检证实。

例句：Her actions were driven by pure malignancy. 她的行为是由纯粹的恶意驱使的。


近义词：malevolence, wickedness, spite, malice, venom|反义词：benevolence, kindness, goodwill


词源：源自于中古英语 *malignite，由malignant（恶性的）加上后缀-ity（性质）构成。malignant本身源自于拉丁语malignant-，是malignus的词干，意为“有害的，恶意的”。
