donorship:/ˈdoʊnərʃɪp/ | n. |捐赠行为；捐赠；捐赠者的身份 |The act of donating or giving something, especially money or goods to a cause.  It also refers to the status or role of a donor. |例句：Her donorship to the hospital was greatly appreciated.  她的捐赠受到了医院的高度赞扬。


The university benefited greatly from his generous donorship. 这所大学从他慷慨的捐赠中受益匪浅。|近义词：donation, contribution, benefaction |反义词：receiving, acceptance (as in the opposite of giving)|词性:名词
