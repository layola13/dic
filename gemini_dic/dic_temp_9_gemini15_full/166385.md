prerequisite:/ˌpriːˈrɛkwɪzɪt/| adj. n.|先决条件，必备条件|Something that is required as a prior condition for something else to happen or exist.  A prerequisite is something that must be done or in place before something else can happen.|例句：A degree in chemistry is a prerequisite for admission to the program. 化学学位是进入该课程的先决条件。


例句：Passing the entrance exam is a prerequisite for enrolling in the university. 通过入学考试是入学大学的先决条件。


近义词：requirement, necessity, precondition, essential


反义词：optional, unnecessary, nonessential


词性：adjective, noun
