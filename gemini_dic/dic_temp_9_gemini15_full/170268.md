pronunciation:/ˌproʊˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/ (美式)/prəˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/ (英式)| n. |发音，读音|The act or manner of pronouncing words or a language.  It refers to the way in which sounds are produced and combined to create words.  Pronunciation can vary significantly between dialects and individuals.|The pronunciation of "route" varies depending on the region.|近义词：articulation, enunciation|反义词：silence
