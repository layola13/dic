misconnects:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛkts/ | n. | 通信故障；脱节；误解 | Instances of a failure to connect properly, leading to communication breakdown or misunderstanding.  It can refer to technical issues in communication systems or to failures in understanding between people. |例句：The frequent misconnects disrupted our online meeting. 频繁的通信故障扰乱了我们的线上会议。

The misconnects between the two departments led to project delays.  两个部门之间的脱节导致了项目的延误。 |近义词：communication failures, breakdowns, misunderstandings, disconnections |反义词：connections, understandings, agreements |名词
