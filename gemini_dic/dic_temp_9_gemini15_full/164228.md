predominating:/prɪˈdɒmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 主要的；占优势的；卓越的 |  Describing something that is most noticeable or important; having the greatest influence or power.  | Example: The predominating color in the painting was blue.  这幅画中主要的颜色是蓝色。


Example:  Her predominating characteristic is her kindness.  她最突出的特点是她的善良。

近义词: dominant, prevalent, prevailing, chief, principal

反义词: subordinate, secondary, minor, insignificant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blue color is predominating in this picture. = 这幅画中蓝色是主要的。
His kindness is predominating in his personality. =  他的善良在他的个性中占主导地位。


一般过去时:
Red predominated in the artist's early works. = 红色在艺术家早期的作品中占主导地位。
Fear predominated his thoughts that night. = 那天晚上恐惧占据了他的思绪。


现在完成时:
Blue has predominated in fashion this year. = 今年蓝色在时尚界占据主导地位。
Her ambition has predominated her life choices. =  她的雄心壮志主导了她的人生选择。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, calm had predominated. = 暴风雨来临之前，平静占主导地位。
By the end of the war, despair had predominated in the city. =  战争结束时，绝望笼罩着这座城市。


将来时:
It is expected that electric vehicles will predominate in the future. = 预计未来电动汽车将占据主导地位。
We believe that environmentally friendly policies will predominate in the next decade. = 我们相信在未来十年中，环保政策将占据主导地位。


虚拟语气:
If only kindness predominated in the world! =  要是世界上只有善良就好了！
If fairness predominated, the outcome would be different. = 如果公平占主导地位，结果就会不同。


被动语态:
The color scheme was predominated by blues and greens.  =  配色方案以蓝色和绿色为主。
His decisions were predominated by his emotions. = 他的决定受情绪支配。


疑问句:
Does a particular color predominate in your design? = 你的设计中是否有一种颜色占主导地位？
Which factor predominates in your decision-making process? =  在你的决策过程中，哪个因素起主导作用？


否定句:
No single style didn't predominate in the exhibition. = 展览中没有一种风格占主导地位。
His logic did not predominate over his feelings. =  他的逻辑并没有压倒他的感情。
