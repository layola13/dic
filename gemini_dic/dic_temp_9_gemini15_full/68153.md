failingness: /ˈfeɪlɪŋnəs/ | n. | 失败的性质；缺点；不足 | The state or quality of being prone to failure; deficiency; imperfection.  It refers to the tendency or likelihood of something failing, or the inherent flaws that lead to failure. | Example: The failingness of the old bridge was evident in its cracked supports.  这座旧桥的缺陷在其开裂的桥墩上显而易见。The failingness of the project was attributed to poor planning. 项目的失败归因于计划不周。| 近义词：defect, flaw, imperfection, weakness | 反义词：strength, success, perfection | 名词
