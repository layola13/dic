readaptive: /ˌriːˈæptɪv/ | adj. | 再适应的，重新适应的 | Able to adapt again or anew; characterized by the ability to adapt after a period of maladaptation or disruption.  |  The readaptive strategies employed by the company allowed it to recover from the economic downturn.  这家公司采用的重新适应策略使其能够从经济衰退中恢复过来。 The readaptive capacity of human beings is remarkable. 人类的再适应能力非凡。|  adaptive, readjusting | maladaptive, unadaptable |  形容词
