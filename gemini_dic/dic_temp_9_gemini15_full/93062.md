idolises:/ˈaɪdəlaɪzɪz/| v.|崇拜；盲目追捧|To admire and idealize (someone or something) excessively or unrealistically; to regard as perfect or flawless.  |例句：Many teenagers idolize pop stars.  许多青少年崇拜流行歌星。

例句：She idolises her older sister and tries to emulate her in every way. 她崇拜她姐姐，并试图在各方面都模仿她。


近义词：adore, worship, revere, venerate

反义词：despise, scorn, disdain

词性：动词
