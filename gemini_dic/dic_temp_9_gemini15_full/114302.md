mainstream:/ˈmeɪnˌstriːm/| adj. n.|主流的；大众的；主流；大趋势|  The prevailing current or direction of activity or thought;  something that is widely accepted or popular.  |例句：His music is now firmly in the mainstream. 他的音乐现在已经完全进入主流。

例句：The mainstream media often ignores the views of minority groups.  主流媒体常常忽略少数群体的观点。


近义词：prevailing, dominant, popular, conventional |反义词：fringe, underground, avant-garde, unconventional


词性:形容词，名词
