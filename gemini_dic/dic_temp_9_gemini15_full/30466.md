centrifuge:/ˈsɛntrfjuːdʒ/| n. v.|离心机；离心分离|A centrifuge is a machine that spins very fast to separate substances of different densities.  It is commonly used in laboratories to separate blood components, for example.|The technician used a centrifuge to separate the blood plasma from the red blood cells.  = 技术员用离心机将血浆从红细胞中分离出来。|separator, spin dryer| -- |n.
The blood sample was centrifuged before analysis. = 血液样本在分析前进行了离心处理。 |v.
