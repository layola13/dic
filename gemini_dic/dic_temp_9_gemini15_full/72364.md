flowingness: /ˈfloʊɪŋnəs/ | n. | 流动性；流畅性；流畅度 | The quality or state of being fluent, smooth, and continuous in movement or form.  It suggests a graceful and effortless movement or a continuous, uninterrupted flow. |

The flowingness of the river was mesmerizing.  = 河水的流动令人着迷。

The flowingness of her prose was captivating. = 她散文流畅的风格引人入胜。


近义词：fluency, smoothness, continuity, fluidity

反义词：stagnation, stiffness, abruptness, discontinuity
