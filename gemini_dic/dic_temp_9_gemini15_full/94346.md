impinges:/ɪmˈpɪndʒɪz/ | v. | 侵犯；影响；撞击 | To have an effect or impact, often negative.  To encroach or infringe upon. To strike or collide with. | 例句：His loud music impinged on his neighbours' peace and quiet. = 他的大声音乐扰乱了他邻居的宁静。  The building's shadow impinges on the garden. = 建筑物的阴影遮挡了花园。| 近义词：affect, influence, encroach, infringe, collide, impinge upon | 反义词：enhance, improve, benefit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music impinges on their concentration. = 嘈杂的音乐影响着他们的注意力。
His opinions impinge on my freedom of thought. = 他的观点影响我的思想自由。


一般过去时:
The falling tree impinged on the power lines. = 倒下的树撞到了电线。
The crisis impinged heavily on the nation's economy. = 危机严重影响了国家的经济。


现在完成时:
The new regulations have impinged on small businesses. = 新的法规已经影响到小型企业。
His actions have impinged on the rights of others. = 他的行为侵犯了其他人的权利。


过去完成时:
The storm had impinged on the coastal areas before the warning was issued. = 在发布警告之前，暴风雨已经袭击了沿海地区。
The previous government's policies had impinged severely on social welfare. = 上届政府的政策严重影响了社会福利。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will impinge on coastal communities. = 海平面上升将影响沿海社区。
Further budget cuts will impinge on essential services. = 进一步的预算削减将影响基本服务。


虚拟语气:
If the project went ahead, it would impinge on the natural habitat. = 如果项目继续进行，它将影响自然栖息地。
I wish his actions hadn't impinged so negatively on our team's morale. = 我希望他的行为没有对我们团队的士气造成如此负面的影响。


被动语态:
Their privacy was impinged upon by the paparazzi. = 他们的隐私被狗仔队侵犯了。
The project was impinged upon by unexpected delays. = 该项目因意外延误而受阻。


疑问句:
Does the new law impinge on freedom of speech? = 新法律是否限制言论自由？
How does climate change impinge on our lives? = 气候变化如何影响我们的生活？


否定句:
The plan does not impinge on existing regulations. = 该计划不会影响现有法规。
The decision did not impinge on their personal lives. = 该决定没有影响他们的个人生活。


PHRASE->
impinge on/upon = 侵犯；影响
impinge against = 撞击；冲击
impinge with = 以...撞击
impinge significantly = 重大影响
impinge negatively = 负面影响
