lonesome:/ˈloʊnsəm/| adj.|孤独的，寂寞的，荒凉的 |Feeling sad because one is alone or without companions.  Characterized by a lack of people or activity; desolate. |例句：She felt lonesome after her friends moved away. 她朋友搬走后感到很孤独。|例句：The lonesome prairie stretched out before them.  广阔荒凉的大草原展现在他们面前。|近义词：lonely, solitary, desolate, isolated |反义词：companionable, sociable, crowded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog feels lonesome when left alone. = 狗独自留下时感到孤独。
He is lonesome because he has no friends. = 他很孤独因为他没有朋友。

一般过去时:
I felt lonesome during the long winter. = 在漫长的冬天我感到孤独。
She was lonesome after her husband passed away. = 她丈夫去世后感到孤独。

现在完成时:
I have been lonesome ever since she left. = 自从她离开后我就一直很孤独。
He has felt lonesome for many years. = 他多年来一直感到孤独。

过去完成时:
I had been lonesome for weeks before I met him. = 在遇到他之前我已经孤独了好几个星期了。
She had felt lonesome ever since her childhood. = 从小她就感到孤独。

将来时:
I will be lonesome without my family. = 没有家人我会很孤独。
They will feel lonesome if they move to a new city. = 如果他们搬到一个新的城市，他们会感到孤独。

虚拟语气:
If I were alone, I would feel lonesome. = 如果我独自一人，我会感到孤独。
I wish I weren't so lonesome. = 我希望我不那么孤独。

被动语态:
(Lonesome is rarely used in the passive voice.)  孤独很少用被动语态。
The house felt lonesome, neglected by its owners. 这房子感觉很孤独，被主人忽视了。(被动语态，但强调的是房子给人的感觉)


疑问句:
Are you feeling lonesome? = 你感到孤独吗？
Does he ever feel lonesome? = 他曾经感到孤独吗？

否定句:
I don't feel lonesome at all. = 我一点也不感到孤独。
She wasn't lonesome because she had her books. = 她不孤独，因为她有书陪伴。

look lonesome = 看起来孤独
a lonesome heart = 一颗孤独的心
