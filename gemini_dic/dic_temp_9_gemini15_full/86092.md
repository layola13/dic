heart-ache:/ˈhɑːrteɪk/| n.|心痛；悲伤；苦恼|A feeling of intense sadness or emotional pain.  It can refer to physical pain in the chest area, but more commonly refers to emotional distress.|例句：Her heart-ache was palpable after the loss of her pet.|近义词：anguish, sorrow, grief, distress|反义词：joy, happiness, elation
