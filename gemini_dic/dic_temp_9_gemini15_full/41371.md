corkscrewing: /ˈkɔːrkskruːɪŋ/ | v. |螺旋状旋转；盘旋前进 |  Moving or progressing in a spiral or twisting manner, like a corkscrew.  |例句：The plane corkscrewed downwards towards the earth.  飞机呈螺旋状向下俯冲。

The smoke corkscrewed lazily into the sky. 烟雾懒洋洋地螺旋上升到天空。| 近义词：spiraling, twisting, swirling | 反义词：straight, linear

词源:
The word "corkscrewing" derives from the noun "corkscrew," which is a tool used to remove corks from bottles.  The action of a corkscrew, twisting into a material, gives rise to the verb "corkscrewing" to describe a similar spiral motion.
