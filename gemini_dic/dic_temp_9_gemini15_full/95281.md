incapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsuleɪt/ | vt. |封装；把…装入胶囊；概括；总结 | To enclose in or as if in a capsule; to summarize or condense. |例句：The company encapsulated its new strategy in a single document. 公司在一份文件中概括了其新战略。

例句：The doctor encapsulated the medicine for easier swallowing. 医生把药封装成胶囊以便于吞服。


近义词：enclose, encapsulate, encase, contain, summarize, condense | 反义词：disperse, spread, expand, detail


词性：动词
