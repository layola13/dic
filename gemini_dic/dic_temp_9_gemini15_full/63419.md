entrants:/ˈɛntrənts/| n. |参赛者；进入者；新成员|People who enter a competition or a place.  The word often refers to those participating in a contest, competition, or event requiring entry. It can also refer to new members joining a group or organization.|

例句：

The entrants in the baking competition were all very talented. =  烘焙比赛的参赛者都很优秀。

Many entrants rushed to register for the marathon. = 许多参赛者争先恐后地报名参加马拉松比赛。


近义词：competitors, participants, applicants, newcomers

反义词：winners (in the context of a competition),  exitants (those leaving)


词性: 名词
