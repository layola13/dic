mibs:/mɪbz/| n.|管理信息库|Management Information Bases;  a collection of data about network devices and their performance.  MIBs are used by network management systems (NMS) to monitor and manage network devices. They provide a standardized way to access and interpret device-specific information.|Example: The network administrator used SNMP to query the MIBs on the routers to check their CPU utilization.|近义词：network data, system information|反义词：无
