flippy:/ˈflɪpi/| adj.|轻率的；反复无常的；易翻转的|easily flipped or turned over; fickle or capricious|例句：The flippy lid on the container made it difficult to keep the contents secure.  容器上易翻转的盖子使得内容物难以保持安全。


例句：He had a flippy attitude, changing his mind constantly. 他态度反复无常，不断改变主意。


近义词：fickle, capricious, volatile, erratic|反义词：steady, consistent, stable|词性:形容词
