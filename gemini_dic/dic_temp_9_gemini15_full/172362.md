putter:/ˈpʌtər/| n., v.| 轻轻敲打；拨弄；懒散地做事；高尔夫球推杆 |A putter is a small club used in golf to tap the ball into the hole. It can also refer to the act of doing something slowly and without much effort.  |例句：He spent the afternoon puttering around in the garden. 他在花园里懒洋洋地消磨了一下午。 He used a new putter to sink the winning shot. 他用一支新的推杆击中了制胜一球。|近义词：fuss, fiddle, trifle, dawdle |反义词：hustle, rush
