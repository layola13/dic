nonadoption: /ˌnɒnəˈdɒpʃən/ | n. | 未采用；未被采纳 | The failure to adopt or accept something, such as a policy, technology, or idea.  It often implies a conscious decision not to adopt, rather than simply a lack of adoption. |例句：The nonadoption of the new software led to continued use of the outdated system.  未采用新软件导致继续使用过时的系统。

The nonadoption of stricter regulations resulted in environmental damage.  未采用更严格的法规导致环境破坏。


近义词：rejection, refusal, avoidance, disregard


反义词：adoption, acceptance, embrace, implementation


词性: 名词
