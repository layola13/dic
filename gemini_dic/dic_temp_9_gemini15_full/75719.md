full-page: /ˌfʊlˈpeɪdʒ/ | adj. | 全页的；整页的 |  Describing something that occupies an entire page.  | Example:  The advertisement is a full-page spread in the magazine. 这则广告占据了杂志的一整页。 | 近义词: whole-page | 反义词: partial-page
