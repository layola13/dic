frizzling:/ˈfrɪzəlɪŋ/| adj.| 嘶嘶作响的；卷曲的；起泡的 |  Making a hissing or sizzling sound;  curling or crisping, as from heat; bubbling or frothing.  |例句：The bacon was frizzling in the pan.  培根在锅里发出嘶嘶声。

The water was frizzling as it boiled. 水沸腾时发出嘶嘶声。


近义词：sizzling, crackling, bubbling, hissing | 反义词：quiet, smooth, still | 词性：形容词
