cubhood:/ˈkʌbˌhʊd/| n. |幼兽期，幼年期|The period of time when an animal is a cub.  It refers to the stage of development between infancy and adulthood for young animals, particularly mammals.  This period is characterized by play, learning essential survival skills, and dependence on parents. |Example:  The lion cubs spent their cubhood learning to hunt from their mother.  狮子幼崽在幼年期向母亲学习狩猎。The playful energy of their cubhood was replaced by the serious responsibilities of adulthood. 他们幼年期的嬉戏活力被成年后的严肃责任所取代。|近义词：youth, infancy, childhood (for humans)|反义词：adulthood
