misrelate:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪt/| vt.|错误地叙述，曲解|To give an inaccurate or misleading account of something.  It implies a distortion of the truth, often unintentional.|例句：He misrelated the events of the meeting, leaving out key details. 他对会议的情况叙述错误，遗漏了关键细节。|例句：The witness misrelated the suspect's actions, leading to a wrongful conviction. 目击证人对嫌疑人的行为作了错误的叙述，导致了错误的定罪。|近义词：misrepresent, misinterpret, distort|反义词：accurately relate, correctly represent
