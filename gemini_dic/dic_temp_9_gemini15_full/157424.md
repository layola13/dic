parameter:/pəˈræmɪtər/ | n. | 参数，参量，变量 | A parameter is a measurable factor, feature, or characteristic that can be used to define a system or a process.  It is often used in mathematics, statistics, and computer science to represent a variable or constant that can be adjusted to affect the outcome. | The model's parameters were carefully calibrated to achieve optimal performance. 模型的参数经过仔细校准以达到最佳性能。 The experiment was designed to test the effect of several key parameters. 实验旨在测试几个关键参数的影响。 | variable, factor, argument, coefficient | constant, fixed value | 名词
