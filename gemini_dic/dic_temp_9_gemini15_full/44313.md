croons:/kruːnz/ | v. |低吟浅唱，哼唱 |To sing softly and smoothly, typically in a low voice. |例句：She crooned a sweet lullaby to her baby. 她轻轻地哼唱着摇篮曲哄她的宝宝睡觉。 |例句：The jazz singer crooned a sultry ballad. 爵士歌手低沉地哼唱了一首缠绵的歌谣。|近义词：hum, murmur, chant |反义词：shout, yell
