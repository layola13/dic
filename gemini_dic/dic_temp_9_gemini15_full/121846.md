misreported:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrtɪd/| adj.| 报道失实的；被误传的 |  Incorrectly or inaccurately reported.  The information was misreported by the media. | 近义词：inaccurately reported, falsely reported, distorted | 反义词：accurately reported, truthfully reported

例句：

The accident was misreported in the newspapers.  新闻报纸上对事故的报道失实。

The study's findings were misreported, leading to public confusion.  研究结果被误传，导致公众困惑。
