relieved:/rɪˈliːvd/| adj.| 解脱的；宽慰的；放心的 | Feeling or expressing relief.  |例句：I was relieved to hear that he was safe.  听到他平安无事，我如释重负。|例句：She felt relieved after completing the challenging project. 完成具有挑战性的项目后，她感到如释重负。|近义词：comforted, eased, soothed |反义词：stressed, anxious, worried


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is relieved to be home. 他很高兴回家了。
She is relieved that the test is over. 她很庆幸考试结束了。

一般过去时:
I was relieved when the doctor gave me good news. 医生告诉我好消息时，我如释重负。
They were relieved to find their lost dog. 他们找到丢失的狗后松了一口气。

现在完成时:
I have been relieved of my duties. 我已被免职。
She has been relieved to finally finish the marathon. 她终于完成了马拉松比赛，感到轻松多了。

过去完成时:
He had been relieved to hear that the storm had passed. 他听到暴风雨已经过去后感到轻松多了。
We had been relieved that the plane landed safely. 我们庆幸飞机安全着陆。

将来时:
I will be relieved when the exam is finished. 考试结束后我会松一口气。
They will be relieved to see their family after a long trip. 长途旅行后，他们将很高兴见到家人。

虚拟语气:
I would be relieved if I knew the truth. 如果我知道真相，我会轻松很多。
She would have been relieved if she had finished the work earlier. 如果她早点完成工作，她会轻松很多。

被动语态:
The pressure was relieved by the announcement.  公告缓解了压力。
His anxiety was relieved by a phone call. 一个电话让他安心了。

疑问句:
Are you relieved now? 你现在轻松了吗？
Is she relieved about the result? 她对结果满意吗？（指轻松）

否定句:
I am not relieved about the situation. 我对这种情况并不轻松。
He was not relieved until he saw the results. 直到他看到结果，他才放心。


PHRASE->

greatly relieved  非常宽慰
