frequenting:/frɪˈkwɛntɪŋ/| v. |常去，常到 |The act of visiting a place or doing something regularly.  It implies a habit or regular occurrence.|例句：He was frequenting the local pub every night. 他每晚都常去当地酒吧。|例句：She frequents the art gallery, often spending hours admiring the paintings. 她经常去美术馆，经常花几个小时欣赏绘画。|近义词：visiting, attending, patronizing |反义词：avoiding, shunning

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frequents that coffee shop. = 他经常去那家咖啡店。
They frequent the same restaurant. = 他们经常去同一家餐厅。

一般过去时:
She frequented the library during her college years. = 她大学期间经常去图书馆。
We frequented that beach every summer. = 我们每年夏天都经常去那个海滩。

现在完成时:
I have been frequenting this gym for six months. = 我已经在这家健身房锻炼六个月了。
They have frequented this bar since they moved to the city. = 他们自从搬到这个城市以来就经常去这家酒吧。

过去完成时:
He had frequented that bookstore before it closed down. = 那家书店倒闭之前，他经常去那里。
She had frequented that cafe for years before discovering a new one. = 在发现一家新的咖啡馆之前，她在那家咖啡馆光顾了好几年。

将来时:
I will be frequenting the yoga studio more often. = 我将更经常去瑜伽馆。
We will frequent that new restaurant next week. = 我们下周将经常去那家新餐厅。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would frequent the museum more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常去博物馆。
I wish I could frequent that concert hall more often. = 我希望我能更经常去那个音乐厅。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The cafe is frequented by many students. = 那家咖啡馆有很多学生常去。
The park is frequented by families on weekends. = 周末，公园里经常有家庭出游。

疑问句:
Do you frequent any particular restaurants? = 你经常去哪些特定的餐厅？
Does he frequent that bar often? = 他经常去那家酒吧吗？

否定句:
I don't frequent that shop anymore. = 我不再常去那家商店了。
She doesn't frequent that club very often. = 她不常去那个俱乐部。


PHRASE->
