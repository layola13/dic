damager:/ˈdæmɪdʒər/ | n. |损害者；破坏者 | A person or thing that causes damage.  Someone or something that causes harm or damage. |例句：The storm was a major damager to the crops.  暴风雨对庄稼造成了重大损害。|例句：The careless driver was a damager to the other cars.  粗心的司机损坏了其他车辆。|近义词： destroyer, ruiner, vandal, saboteur |反义词：protector, preserver,benefactor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The careless driver damages other cars frequently. = 粗心的司机经常损坏其他车辆。
The storm damages the crops every year. = 暴风雨每年都损害庄稼。

一般过去时:
The earthquake damaged many buildings. = 地震损坏了许多建筑物。
The vandals damaged the park last night. = 昨晚破坏分子破坏了公园。

现在完成时:
The heavy rain has damaged the road. = 大雨已经损坏了道路。
The company has damaged its reputation. = 公司损害了自己的声誉。

过去完成时:
The storm had damaged the bridge before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，暴风雨已经损坏了桥梁。
They had damaged the evidence before the police arrived. = 他们在警察到达之前已经毁坏了证据。

将来时:
The construction will damage the environment. = 建设将破坏环境。
The flood will damage many homes. = 洪水将损坏许多房屋。

虚拟语气:
If he had been more careful, he wouldn't have damaged the car. = 如果他更小心一点，就不会损坏汽车了。
I wish I hadn't damaged the painting. = 我真希望我没有损坏这幅画。

被动语态:
The building was damaged by the fire. = 建筑物被火灾损坏了。
The crops were damaged by the frost. = 庄稼被霜冻损坏了。

疑问句:
Was the car damaged in the accident? = 汽车在事故中受损了吗？
What was damaged in the storm? = 暴风雨中有什么东西被损坏了吗？

否定句:
The storm didn't damage my house. = 暴风雨没有损坏我的房子。
The accident didn't damage the car severely. = 事故没有严重损坏汽车。
