predecessor:/ˈpriːdɛsɛsər/| n. |前任，前辈；前身；先驱 |A predecessor is a person who held a job or position before someone else, or a thing that came before another thing in time.  |Example:  His predecessor was a much stricter manager.  他的前任是一位更加严格的经理。

Example: The new operating system is a significant improvement over its predecessor.  新的操作系统比其前身有了显著的改进。|近义词：forerunner, antecedent, precursor |反义词：successor
