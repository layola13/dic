reticulated:/rɪˈtɪkjuleɪtɪd/| adj.|网状的；有网状花纹的 |Having a network-like pattern or structure, resembling a net or mesh.  The word often describes textures or patterns, but can also refer to structures like a reticulated network. |例句：The skin of a giraffe is reticulated. 长颈鹿的皮肤是网状的。

The intricate, reticulated design of the stained-glass window was breathtaking.  彩色玻璃窗复杂的网状设计令人叹为观止。


近义词：netlike, latticed, mesh-like, networked

反义词：plain, smooth, unpatterned


词性:形容词
