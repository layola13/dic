hesitate: /ˈhɛzɪteɪt/ | v. |犹豫，迟疑 | To be slow or uncertain in acting or speaking.  |例句：He hesitated before answering the question. 他在回答问题之前犹豫了一下。|例句：Don't hesitate to ask if you need help.  如果你需要帮助，请尽管问。|近义词：waver, falter, pause, delay |反义词：decide, rush


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hesitate to ask for more money. = 我犹豫要不要多要些钱。
He hesitates before making important decisions. = 他在做重要决定之前会犹豫。

一般过去时:
She hesitated before accepting the offer. = 她犹豫了一下才接受了这个提议。
They hesitated to speak out against the injustice. = 他们犹豫着要不要公开反对这种不公正。

现在完成时:
I have hesitated for too long. = 我犹豫的时间太长了。
He has hesitated to commit to a long-term plan. = 他一直犹豫要不要制定一个长期计划。

过去完成时:
I had hesitated to tell him the truth. = 我曾犹豫要不要告诉他真相。
She had hesitated to apply for the job, fearing she wasn't qualified. = 她曾犹豫要不要申请这份工作，因为害怕自己不够资格。

将来时:
I will hesitate to take such a risk. = 我将犹豫是否要冒这样的风险。
They will hesitate before signing the contract. = 他们在签署合同之前会犹豫。

虚拟语气:
I would hesitate to judge him without knowing all the facts. = 在不知道所有事实的情况下，我会犹豫要不要评价他。
If I were you, I would hesitate to invest in that company. = 如果我是你，我会犹豫要不要投资那家公司。

被动语态:
The decision was hesitated over for weeks. = 这个决定被犹豫了几个星期。
His request was hesitated upon by the committee. = 他的请求受到了委员会的犹豫。


疑问句:
Do you hesitate to express your opinion? = 你是否犹豫要不要表达你的意见？
Would you hesitate to help someone in need? = 你是否会犹豫要不要帮助需要帮助的人？


否定句:
Don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions. = 如果你有任何问题，请随时联系我们。
She didn't hesitate to defend her beliefs. = 她毫不犹豫地捍卫自己的信仰。


PHRASE->
hesitate to do something = 犹豫做某事
hesitate over something = 对某事犹豫不决
hesitate in doing something = 在做某事上犹豫
without hesitation = 毫不犹豫地
don't hesitate = 请尽管
