rural:/ˈrʊərəl/ | adj. | 农村的；乡村的 | Relating to or characteristic of the countryside rather than the town.  |例句：The rural landscape was dotted with sheep and rolling hills.  乡村景色点缀着绵羊和连绵起伏的丘陵。|例句：They live a simple rural life, far removed from the hustle and bustle of the city.他们过着简朴的乡村生活，远离城市的喧嚣。|近义词：country, agricultural, rustic |反义词：urban, metropolitan, city


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lives in a rural area. = 他住在农村地区。
The farmer cultivates the rural land. = 农民耕种农村土地。


一般过去时:
She visited her grandparents in a rural village. = 她去农村的一个村庄看望她的祖父母。
They spent their childhood in the rural countryside. = 他们在农村度过了童年。


现在完成时:
We have seen many changes in this once-rural region. = 我们已经看到这个曾经是农村的地区发生了许多变化。
They have lived a rural life for many years. = 他们已经过着乡村生活多年了。


过去完成时:
Before the industrial revolution, most people lived rural lives. = 在工业革命之前，大多数人过着农村生活。
She had never experienced such peace before moving to the rural area. = 在搬到农村地区之前，她从未体验过如此平静的生活。


将来时:
They will move to a rural community next year. = 他们明年将搬到一个农村社区。
He will explore the rural parts of the country during his vacation. = 他将在假期探索该国的农村地区。


虚拟语气:
If I had the chance, I would live a rural life. = 如果我有机会，我会过乡村生活。
I wish I could escape to a quiet rural setting. = 我希望我能逃到一个安静的乡村环境中。


被动语态:
The land was transformed from rural to urban. = 这片土地从农村变成了城市。
The rural community is being supported by government grants. = 农村社区正在得到政府拨款的支持。


疑问句:
Is this area considered rural or urban? = 这个地区是农村还是城市？
What are the challenges faced by rural communities? = 农村社区面临哪些挑战？


否定句:
That house is not located in a rural area. = 那栋房子不在农村地区。
The government's policy does not adequately address rural poverty. = 政府的政策没有充分解决农村贫困问题。
