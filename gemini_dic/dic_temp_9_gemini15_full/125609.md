multiverses:/ˈmʌltɪˌvɜːrsɪz/| n.|多重宇宙，多元宇宙|A hypothetical collection of potentially diverse universes, including our own, considered as a whole.|例句：The theory of multiverses suggests that our universe is just one of many. 多元宇宙理论认为我们的宇宙只是众多宇宙中的一个。
例句：Scientists are exploring the possibility of other multiverses existing beyond our own. 科学家们正在探索我们宇宙之外可能存在其他多元宇宙的可能性。|近义词：multiple universes, parallel universes|反义词：universe (单一宇宙)
