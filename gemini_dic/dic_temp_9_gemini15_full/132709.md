nondistractedly:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈstræktɪdli/| adv.|专心致志地；全神贯注地|Without being distracted; with complete attention and focus.  It emphasizes the state of being free from distractions and the consequent undivided attention.|例句：He worked nondistractedly on the project, completing it ahead of schedule. 他专心致志地工作，提前完成了项目。

例句：She listened nondistractedly to the speaker, absorbing every word. 她专心致志地听着演讲者讲话，吸收了每一个字。|近义词：attentively, intently, focusedly, concentratedly|反义词：distractedly, carelessly, inattentively|词性：副词
