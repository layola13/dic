comrades:/ˈkɒmreɪdz/ /ˈkɑːmreɪdz/| n.|同志，战友|Fellow members of a group, especially those sharing the same political beliefs or involved in a struggle.  The word emphasizes a sense of shared purpose and solidarity.|例句：The comrades fought bravely side by side. 同志们并肩作战，英勇奋战。

例句：We are all comrades in this fight for justice. 在这场争取正义的斗争中，我们都是战友。


近义词：companions, allies, friends, associates

反义词：enemies, opponents, adversaries

词性:noun
