remote: /rɪˈmoʊt/ | adj., n., v. | 遥远的，偏远的；遥控器；远程操作 |  

英文解析:
As an adjective, "remote" means far away in distance or time; isolated. As a noun, it typically refers to a device used to control something from a distance, such as a television remote. As a verb, it means to operate something from a distance.


例句:
(Adjective)
The remote village was accessible only by boat. = 偏远的村庄只能乘船到达。

They live in a remote area with limited access to modern amenities. = 他们住在偏远地区，现代化设施有限。


(Noun)
I lost the remote; can you help me find it? = 我把遥控器弄丢了，你能帮我找找吗？

The presentation was controlled by a wireless remote. = 演示文稿由无线遥控器控制。


(Verb)
The pilot remotely controlled the drone. = 飞行员远程控制无人机。

The system allows you to remotely access your files. = 该系统允许您远程访问您的文件。


近义词:
(Adjective) distant, far-off, secluded, isolated, faraway

(Noun) controller, control


反义词:
(Adjective) close, nearby, proximate, accessible


词性:
adjective, noun, verb
