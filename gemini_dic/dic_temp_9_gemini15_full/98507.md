instantiate:/ˈɪnstænʃieɪt/| vt.|实例化，具体化，创建实例|To create an instance of something, especially a class in object-oriented programming.  It involves bringing an abstract concept or design into a concrete form.|例句：The programmer instantiated a new object of the "User" class.  程序员实例化了一个新的“用户”类对象。
例句：The architect instantiated his design by building a model. 建筑师通过建造模型来具体化他的设计。|近义词：create, generate, exemplify, embody|反义词：abstract, generalize
