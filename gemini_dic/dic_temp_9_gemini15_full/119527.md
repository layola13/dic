microscope:/ˈmaɪkrəˌskoʊp/| n. |显微镜 |An instrument used to see objects that are too small to be seen by the naked eye. It uses lenses to magnify the image of the object.|例句：Scientists use microscopes to study cells. 科学家用显微镜研究细胞。

例句：The electron microscope allows us to see even smaller structures. 电子显微镜让我们能够看到更小的结构。


近义词：magnifier, enlarger |反义词：telescope
