pecora:/ˈpɛkərə/| n.|羊群；绵羊(集合名词)|Sheep collectively; a flock of sheep.  The term often implies a large number of sheep.  |例句：The shepherd guided his pecora across the mountain. 牧羊人带领他的羊群越过山。|The farmer watched over his vast pecora grazing in the pasture. 农民看着他在牧场上吃草的大批羊群。|近义词：flock, herd|反义词：none (it's a collective noun, not easily paired with an antonym)|名词
