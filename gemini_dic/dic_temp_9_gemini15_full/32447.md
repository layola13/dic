chimney-breast:/ˈtʃɪmni brɛst/| n. |壁炉架；壁炉烟囱基座|The chimney-breast is a prominent architectural feature of the room, providing a focal point and adding warmth and character.|近义词：fireplace surround, hearth |反义词:None.


英文解析：A chimney-breast is the structure that surrounds a fireplace and chimney, projecting from the wall.  It typically includes the fireplace opening, the hearth, and the chimney flue.  It's a structural element built into the wall, often made of brick or stone.


例句：

The old house had a magnificent chimney-breast made of hand-carved stone.  = 这栋老房子有一个用手工雕刻的石头砌成的宏伟壁炉架。


The architect designed a recessed chimney-breast to save space in the small living room. = 建筑师设计了一个内嵌式壁炉架，以节省小客厅的空间。


词源：

* **Chimney:** From Middle English *chimene, *chymnee*, from Old French *cheminée* ("fireplace, chimney"), from *chemin* ("way, road").  The idea is that the chimney is the "way" for smoke to escape.

* **Breast:** From Old English *brēost*, meaning "chest, breast."  In this context, it refers to the projecting part of the wall that the chimney is built into, similar to the breast of a person.
