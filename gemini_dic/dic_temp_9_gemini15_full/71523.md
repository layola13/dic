flapjack:/ˈflæpˌdʒæk/| n. |薄煎饼，油煎薄饼 |A flapjack is a type of pancake, often made with oats, butter, and sugar, and sometimes other ingredients like fruit or chocolate chips.  It's typically thicker and chewier than a regular pancake. | Example:  I made a delicious batch of blueberry flapjacks for breakfast. 我做了一大批美味的蓝莓薄煎饼当早餐。

Example: He enjoyed a warm flapjack with syrup. 他享用了一块淋上糖浆的热薄煎饼。

近义词：pancake, griddle cake | 反义词：None (no direct antonym) | 词性：名词
