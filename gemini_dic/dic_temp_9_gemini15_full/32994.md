chores:/tʃɔːrz/ | n. |家务杂事 |Routine tasks, especially those around the house.  |例句：My chores include washing dishes and vacuuming. 我的家务包括洗碗和吸尘。|例句：I finished all my chores before going out. 我出门前完成了所有家务。|近义词：tasks, duties, errands |反义词：leisure, recreation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I do my chores every day. = 我每天做家务。
She does the laundry every week. = 她每周洗衣服。


一般过去时:
He did his chores before dinner. = 他晚饭前做了家务。
We did the dishes after the party. = 派对后我们洗了碗。


现在完成时:
I have already finished my chores. = 我已经完成了家务。
She has done all her homework and chores. = 她已经完成了所有的作业和家务。


过去完成时:
I had already done my chores when he arrived. = 他到达时我已经做完了家务。
They had finished their chores before the movie started. = 电影开始前他们已经完成了家务。


将来时:
I will do my chores tomorrow. = 我明天要做家务。
She will finish her chores before going to the party. = 她要去参加派对前会完成家务。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would do my chores more efficiently. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更高效地做家务。
I wish I hadn't neglected my chores for so long. = 我真希望我没有这么长时间忽视家务。


被动语态:
The chores were completed quickly. = 家务很快完成了。
The dishes are being done by the dishwasher. = 碗碟正在洗碗机里洗。


疑问句:
Have you done your chores yet? = 你做完家务了吗？
What chores need to be done today? = 今天需要做什么家务？


否定句:
I didn't do my chores yesterday. = 我昨天没有做家务。
She hasn't finished her chores yet. = 她还没有完成家务。
