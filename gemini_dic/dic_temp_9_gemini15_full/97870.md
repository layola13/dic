inmates:/ˈɪnmeɪts/ | n. |囚犯，住院病人 | People who are confined to an institution, such as a prison or hospital.  |例句：The inmates staged a riot. 囚犯们发动了一场暴动。
The hospital inmates were all recovering well. 医院里的病人都在康复中。|近义词：prisoners, patients, residents |反义词：free people, outside patients
