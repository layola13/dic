perpetuator: /pərˈpetʃuˌeɪtər/ | n. | 继续者；使持续者；永存者 | Someone or something that perpetuates something, especially something bad or undesirable.  A perpetuator keeps something going, often something negative like a harmful system or belief. |The perpetuator of the myth refused to acknowledge the evidence.  这个神话的传播者拒绝承认证据。

The perpetuator of violence must be brought to justice. 暴力行为的实施者必须绳之以法。


近义词:  maintainer, preserver, sustainer (if referring to something positive), propagator, promoter (if referring to something negative)

反义词:  terminator, extinguisher, abolisher


词性: 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perpetuates harmful stereotypes. 他延续着有害的刻板印象。
The system perpetuates inequality.  这个制度延续着不平等。


一般过去时:
She perpetuated the family business. 她延续了家族企业。
His actions perpetuated the cycle of poverty. 他的行为延续了贫困的循环。


现在完成时:
They have perpetuated a culture of silence. 他们延续了一种沉默的文化。
The government has perpetuated the myth for decades.  政府几十年来一直延续着这个神话。


过去完成时:
They had perpetuated the lie for years before the truth came out.  真相大白之前，他们多年来一直都在传播谎言。
The policy had perpetuated social injustice for generations.  这项政策延续了几代人的社会不公。


将来时:
He will perpetuate his father's legacy. 他将延续他父亲的遗产。
Their actions will perpetuate the conflict. 他们的行为将延续冲突。


虚拟语气:
If he continued this behavior, he would perpetuate the problem. 如果他继续这种行为，他将延续这个问题。
If she had not intervened, the injustice would have been perpetuated. 如果她没有介入，不公正将继续存在。


被动语态:
Harmful stereotypes are perpetuated by the media.  有害的刻板印象被媒体延续。
The tradition is perpetuated through storytelling.  这个传统通过讲故事的方式延续下来。


疑问句:
Who perpetuated this rumor? 谁散布了这个谣言？
What perpetuates this cycle of violence? 什么延续了这个暴力的循环？


否定句:
We will not perpetuate these outdated ideas. 我们不会延续这些过时的想法。
He refused to perpetuate the lie any longer. 他拒绝再继续说谎了。


PHRASE->
perpetuator of violence: 暴力行为的实施者
perpetuator of misinformation: 错误信息的传播者
perpetuator of injustice: 不公正的延续者
perpetuator of myths: 神话的传播者
perpetuator of inequality: 不平等的延续者


词源:
The word "perpetuator" comes from the Latin word "perpetuare," meaning "to make perpetual" or "to continue indefinitely."  The suffix "-ator" is a common agent suffix in English, indicating the person or thing that performs the action.
