disaffiliating: /ˌdɪsæˌfɪliˌeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 脱离；使脱离；取消隶属关系 | The act of ending an affiliation or association; withdrawing from a group or organization.  |例句：The university is disaffiliating from the athletic conference.  大学正在脱离该体育联盟。
The student group is disaffiliating itself from the larger organization. 学生团体正在脱离更大的组织。|近义词：severing, withdrawing, detaching, disassociating |反义词：affiliating, associating, joining, uniting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is disaffiliating from the political party. 他正在脱离该政党。
The company is disaffiliating its subsidiary.  公司正在剥离其子公司。

一般过去时:
She disaffiliated from the club last year. 她去年退出了俱乐部。
The school disaffiliated itself from the district. 学校脱离了学区。

现在完成时:
They have disaffiliated from the organization. 他们已经脱离了该组织。
The church has disaffiliated several congregations. 教堂已经取消了几个教区的隶属关系。

过去完成时:
He had disaffiliated from the union before the strike. 在罢工之前，他已经退出了工会。
The branch had disaffiliated itself from the main company long ago. 分公司很久以前就已经脱离了母公司。

将来时:
We will disaffiliate from the project next month. 我们下个月将退出该项目。
The hospital will disaffiliate from the healthcare system. 医院将脱离该医疗系统。

虚拟语气:
If I were them, I would disaffiliate immediately. 如果我是他们，我会立即脱离。
I wish the team had disaffiliated earlier. 我希望球队早点脱离。

被动语态:
The group was disaffiliated from the association. 该团体被取消了协会的隶属关系。
The member was disaffiliated for violating the rules.  该成员因违反规定而被取消了隶属关系。

疑问句:
Is the school disaffiliating from the board? 学校是否正在脱离董事会？
Are they disaffiliating from the program? 他们是否正在脱离该项目？

否定句:
They did not disaffiliate from the cause. 他们没有脱离事业。
The artist did not disaffiliate from the gallery.  这位艺术家没有脱离画廊。


PHRASE->
disaffiliating oneself: 脱离；与…脱离关系
disaffiliating from:  脱离…；与…脱离关系
disaffiliating with:  与…脱离关系；与…断绝关系
disaffiliating a member: 取消会员资格
disaffiliating a branch: 取消分公司的隶属关系
