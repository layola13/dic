repositor:/rɪˈpɒzɪtəri/| n. |知识库；存储库；仓库；容器|A repository is a place where things are stored.  It can refer to a physical location like a warehouse or a digital location like a database.  The term often implies organization and accessibility of the stored items.|例句：The museum's archives serve as a repository of historical documents.  博物馆的档案馆是历史文献的知识库。

例句：GitHub is a popular repository for software code. GitHub是一个流行的软件代码存储库。


近义词：storehouse, archive, database, depot, library |反义词：disposal, destruction
