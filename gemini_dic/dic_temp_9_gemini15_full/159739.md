polycross:/ˈpɒlikrɒs/| /ˈpɑːlikrɒs/| n.|多交配；杂交|The process of crossing a number of different plants or animals together to produce offspring with desirable characteristics.  It is a method used in plant and animal breeding to improve yields, disease resistance, or other traits.|例句：The farmer used polycross breeding to improve the yield of his corn crop.  这个农民用多交配育种来提高他的玉米产量。The experiment involved a polycross of several different strains of mice.  这个实验涉及几种不同品系小鼠的多交配。|近义词：crossbreeding, hybridization|反义词：inbreeding, pure breeding|名词
