denies:/dɪˈnaɪz/| vt.|否认，拒绝 |To state that something is not true; to refuse to give or grant something. |例句：He denies all knowledge of the crime. 他否认自己知道这桩罪行。|例句：The company denies responsibility for the accident. 公司否认对事故负责。|近义词：refute, contradict, reject |反义词：affirm, admit, concede |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He denies the allegations. = 他否认这些指控。
She denies knowing anything about it. = 她否认知道任何关于此事的事情。

一般过去时:
They denied the rumors. = 他们否认了那些谣言。
He denied having seen her. = 他否认见过她。

现在完成时:
She has denied any involvement in the scandal. = 她否认参与了这场丑闻。
He has consistently denied the accusations. = 他一直否认这些指控。

过去完成时:
He denied that he had stolen the money. = 他否认他偷了钱。
They denied that they had ever met. = 他们否认他们曾经见过面。

将来时:
He will deny the charges in court. = 他将在法庭上否认这些指控。
She will probably deny everything. = 她可能会否认一切。

虚拟语气:
If he denied the truth, no one would believe him. = 如果他否认真相，没有人会相信他。
I wish he would deny the rumors. = 我希望他会否认这些谣言。

被动语态:
The accusations were denied by the suspect. = 嫌疑人否认了这些指控。
His claims were vehemently denied. = 他的说法被强烈否认。

疑问句:
Does he deny his involvement? = 他否认他的参与吗？
Will she deny the evidence? = 她会否认证据吗？

否定句:
He doesn't deny that he made a mistake. = 他不否认他犯了一个错误。
She couldn't deny the obvious truth. = 她无法否认显而易见的真相。
