flogs:/flɒɡz/ | /flɑːɡz/ | vt. |鞭打；痛打；猛击；严厉批评|To beat (someone) with a whip or stick; to hit (something) repeatedly and forcefully; to criticize severely.|例句：The farmer flogged the mule to make it move faster. 农夫鞭打骡子让它跑得更快。|例句：The teacher flogged the students for their bad behavior.老师严厉批评学生们的恶劣行为。|近义词：whip, lash, thrash, beat, castigate |反义词：praise, commend, reward
