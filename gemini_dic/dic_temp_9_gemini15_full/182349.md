rend: /rend/ | vt. |撕裂；裂开；猛拉；强夺；使…痛苦；折磨 | To tear or split violently; to pull apart with force; to seize or snatch violently; to inflict pain or suffering on. |例句：The strong wind rended the sails. 狂风撕裂了船帆。
The earthquake rended the city apart. 地震将城市撕裂开来。


近义词：tear, rip, split, shatter | 反义词：mend, repair, heal

词源:  源自古英语 *rendan，意思是“撕裂，撕开”。与古高地德语 *rentan, 古诺尔斯语 *renda, 古弗里西亚语 *renda, 古萨克森语 *riandan 和荷兰语 *renden 有关。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog rends its toys. = 狗撕碎它的玩具。
The wind rends the fabric. = 风撕裂了织物。

一般过去时:
She rended the letter in anger. = 她愤怒地撕碎了信。
The storm rended the roof. = 暴风雨撕裂了屋顶。


现在完成时:
The storm has rended the coastline. = 暴风雨已经撕裂了海岸线。
The years have rended his health. = 岁月已经损害了他的健康。


过去完成时:
The wind had rended the tent before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，风已经撕裂了帐篷。
They had rended the agreement, causing great conflict. = 他们撕毁了协议，导致了巨大的冲突。


将来时:
The hurricane will rend the city. = 飓风将摧毁这座城市。
The weight will rend the fabric. = 重量会撕裂织物。


虚拟语气:
If I were to rend this contract, what would happen? = 如果我撕毁这份合同，会发生什么？
If the earthquake had rended the building, we would be in danger. = 如果地震摧毁了建筑物，我们将处于危险之中。


被动语态:
The fabric was rended by the sharp object. = 布料被锋利的物体撕裂了。
The old tree was rended by lightning. = 老树被闪电劈开了。


疑问句:
Was the fabric rended by the machine? = 布料是被机器撕裂的吗？
Has the storm rended the power lines? = 暴风雨是否撕裂了电线？


否定句:
The strong thread did not rend easily. = 结实的线不容易撕裂。
The fabric did not rend under the strain. = 布料在拉力下没有撕裂。
