ovality:/oʊˈvæləti/ | n. |椭圆度，卵形 |The degree to which something is oval or egg-shaped.  It often refers to a measurement of how much a shape deviates from a perfect circle or sphere.  For example, in engineering, it might describe the ellipticity of a pipe or a bearing. |例句：The ovality of the piston ring was measured using a micrometer.活塞环的椭圆度是用千分尺测量的。 The high ovality of the wheel caused vibration at high speed. 车轮的高椭圆度导致高速振动。|近义词：ellipticity, roundness (in a negative context)|反义词：circularity, sphericity
