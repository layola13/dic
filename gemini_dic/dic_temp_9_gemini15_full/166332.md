prerejoiced:/ˌpriːrɪˈdʒɔɪst/| v. |预先欣喜；提前感到高兴|To feel joy or happiness before something happens.  It implies an anticipation of pleasure or success.|例句：She prerejoiced at the prospect of her upcoming vacation. 她对即将到来的假期提前感到高兴。

例句：They prerejoiced over the likely success of their project. 他们对项目的可能成功提前感到高兴。


近义词：anticipate with pleasure, look forward to with joy

反义词：dread, anticipate with anxiety


词性：动词
