operates:/ˈɒpəreɪts/ /ˈɑːpəreɪts/| vt.|运转；操作；经营；起作用|To function, work, or be in effect.  It also means to control or manage a business or system.|例句：The machine operates smoothly. 这台机器运转顺畅。|例句：The company operates in five countries. 这家公司在五个国家开展业务。|近义词：functions, runs, works, manages|反义词：fails, malfunctions, ceases


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This machine operates efficiently. = 这台机器运转效率很高。
The restaurant operates 24 hours a day. = 这家餐厅24小时营业。


一般过去时:
The factory operated for 50 years. = 这家工厂运营了50年。
The system operated flawlessly yesterday. = 系统昨天运行完美无缺。


现在完成时:
The company has operated successfully for decades. = 这家公司几十年来一直经营成功。
The software has operated without problems since the update. = 软件自更新以来一直运行良好。


过去完成时:
The engine had operated for hours before it failed. = 引擎运行了几个小时才发生故障。
The business had operated at a loss for several months before closing. = 这家企业在倒闭前已经亏损了好几个月。


将来时:
The new system will operate automatically. = 新系统将自动运行。
The train will operate on a new schedule next week. = 火车下周将按照新的时刻表运行。


虚拟语气:
If the machine operated correctly, we wouldn't have this problem. = 如果机器运行正常，我们就不会有这个问题。
I wish this software operated more efficiently. = 我希望这个软件运行效率更高。


被动语态:
The machine is operated remotely. = 这台机器是远程操作的。
The business is operated by a family. = 这家企业由一个家族经营。


疑问句:
How does this device operate? = 这个设备如何操作？
Does the business operate profitably? = 这家企业盈利吗？


否定句:
The engine isn't operating correctly. = 引擎运转不正常。
The store doesn't operate on Sundays. = 这家商店星期天不营业。


PHRASE->
operate on (someone) = 为（某人）做手术
operate a business = 经营企业
operate a machine = 操作机器
operate under pressure = 在压力下工作
operate in the black = 盈利


词源:
From Latin *operārī "to work, labor", from opus "work".
