misfare:/mɪsˈfeər/| v.|  (古语)走错路；失败；不顺利 | To go wrong; to fail; to be unsuccessful.  The word is largely archaic and rarely used in modern English. It implies a journey or undertaking that has gone awry, not simply a minor mistake. |例句：His plans to conquer the kingdom misfared terribly.  他的征服王国的计划完全失败了。
例句：The expedition misfared due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，探险队失败了。|近义词：fail, miscarry, go wrong |反义词：succeed, prosper, thrive |动词
