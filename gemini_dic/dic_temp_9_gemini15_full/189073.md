sabres:/ˈseɪbərz/| n.|军刀，马刀|A sabre is a cavalry sword with a curved blade.  It is typically used by cavalry soldiers or in fencing.|例句：The cavalry charged, their sabres flashing in the sun.  骑兵冲锋，他们的军刀在阳光下闪闪发光。

The museum displayed a collection of antique sabres.  博物馆展出了一些古董军刀。|近义词：sword, cutlass|反义词：无
