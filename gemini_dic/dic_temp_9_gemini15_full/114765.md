malter:/ˈmɔːltər/| n. |麦芽工；搅拌者；捣碎者 |A malter is someone who works with malt, typically in a brewery or distillery.  They may be involved in the process of malting barley or other grains, or in the handling and processing of malt.  The term can also refer to someone who stirs or mixes something, especially a liquid. |例句：The experienced malter expertly judged the quality of the barley.  经验丰富的麦芽工熟练地判断了大麦的质量。


The old malter used a traditional wooden mash tun. 老麦芽工使用传统的木制糖化桶。|近义词：brewer, miller, mixer |反义词：None directly applicable.
