mousetrap: /ˈmaʊstræp/ | n. |老鼠夹；圈套；诡计 | A device for trapping and killing mice.  A seemingly attractive but ultimately harmful situation or plan. |例句：The old mousetrap was rusty and ineffective.  这只旧老鼠夹生锈了，而且无效。

The politician's promise turned out to be a mousetrap, designed to win votes but offering no real solutions.  这位政客的承诺结果是一个圈套，旨在赢得选票，但没有提供任何真正的解决方案。|近义词：trap, snare, trick |反义词：safety, security
