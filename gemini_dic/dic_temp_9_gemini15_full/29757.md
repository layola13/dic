catrina:/kəˈtriːnə/| n. |卡特里娜（女子名）；骷髅新娘；死亡的象征|A Catrina is a representation of death, typically depicted as a female skeleton elegantly dressed, often associated with the Mexican holiday, Día de Muertos (Day of the Dead).  It is a symbol of acceptance and celebration of death rather than fear. |例句：The artist painted a vibrant Catrina for the Day of the Dead altar.  艺术家为亡灵节祭坛画了一幅色彩鲜艳的卡特里娜画像。

The Catrina figure is a powerful symbol in Mexican culture.| 卡特里娜形象是墨西哥文化中一个强大的象征。|近义词：skeleton, death figure, memento mori|反义词：life, vitality
