moto:/ˈmoʊtoʊ/| n.|座右铭；格言；动力；目标|a short statement of beliefs or principles;  a personal guiding principle; the driving force behind something; an aim or goal.|例句：My moto in life is to always strive for excellence. 我的人生座右铭是永远追求卓越。|例句：The company's moto is "Quality first, customer second". 这家公司的座右铭是“质量第一，客户第二”。|近义词：motto, maxim, slogan, principle, creed|反义词：inaction, apathy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His moto is to help others. = 他的座右铭是帮助他人。
What is your moto in life? = 你的人生座右铭是什么？


一般过去时:
Her moto was simple: be kind. = 她的座右铭很简单：要善良。
What was their moto during the campaign? = 他们在竞选期间的座右铭是什么？


现在完成时:
My moto has always been to stay positive. = 我的座右铭一直都是保持积极。
What has been your company's moto throughout the years? = 多年来贵公司的座右铭是什么？


过去完成时:
He had forgotten his moto until he saw his grandfather's watch. = 直到他看到他祖父的手表，他才想起他的座右铭。
What had been their moto before the company's restructuring? = 在公司重组之前，他们的座右铭是什么？


将来时:
My moto will always be honesty and integrity. = 我的座右铭将永远是诚实和正直。
What will be the new moto of the organization? = 该组织的新座右铭将是什么？


虚拟语气:
If I had a moto, it would be "carpe diem". = 如果我有一个座右铭，那将是“抓住今天”。
I wish my moto were more clearly defined. = 我希望我的座右铭能更清晰地定义。


被动语态:
The moto was chosen by the team leader. = 座右铭是由团队领导选择的。
The new moto is being debated by the board. = 新的座右铭正在由董事会讨论。


疑问句:

否定句:
He doesn't have a clear moto. = 他没有明确的座右铭。
