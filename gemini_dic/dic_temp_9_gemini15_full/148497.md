overstrongly: /ˌoʊvərˈstrɒŋli/ | adv. | 过分强烈地；过于强烈地 |  The word "overstrongly" is an adverb formed by adding the prefix "over-" to the adverb "strongly." It indicates that something is done or felt with excessive force, intensity, or emphasis.  It is not a frequently used word and often sounds a bit unnatural.  Consider using more precise alternatives. | 例句：He overstrongly emphasized his point, losing his audience. 他过分强调了他的观点，失去了听众。

The argument was overstrongly presented, making it unconvincing. 论点表达得过于强烈，令人难以信服。


近义词：excessively, intensely, vehemently, unduly, too strongly | 反义词：weakly, mildly, gently, subtly
