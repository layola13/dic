sin-eating:/ˈsɪnˌiːtɪŋ/| n.|食罪，代人受过|The act of confessing one's sins to another person, who then symbolically "eats" those sins, thereby taking on the burden of guilt.|例句：In some cultures, sin-eating was a ritualistic practice to alleviate guilt and shame. 在某些文化中，食罪是一种减轻内疚和羞耻的仪式性行为。|近义词：atonement, expiation, confession|反义词：blame, accusation
