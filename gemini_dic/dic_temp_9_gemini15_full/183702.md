rerunning:/ˌriːˈrʌnɪŋ/| v. |重新运行；重播；重演|The act of running something again, such as a program, a film, or an event.  It implies a repetition of a previous action or process.|例句：They are rerunning the experiment to verify the results. = 他们正在重新运行实验以验证结果。

例句：The TV station is rerunning the popular drama series. = 电视台正在重播那部受欢迎的电视剧。


近义词：repeating, replaying, re-enacting


反义词：stopping, ending, concluding


词性：动词
