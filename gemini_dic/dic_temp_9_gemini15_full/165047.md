preignition:/ˌpriːɪɡˈnɪʃən/| n. |提前点火，早燃|The ignition of a fuel-air mixture before the intended time of ignition by the spark plug or other ignition source.  Preignition can cause engine damage and knocking.  It is often caused by hot spots in the combustion chamber, excessive compression, or incorrect ignition timing.|The engine experienced preignition, causing a loud knocking sound. 引擎发生提前点火，发出巨大的敲击声。


The car's preignition was due to a faulty spark plug.  汽车的提前点火是由于火花塞故障造成的。|knocking, detonation|normal ignition|名词
