congeals:/kənˈdʒiːlz/| v.|凝固；冻结；凝结|To change from a liquid to a solid state, especially as a result of cooling.|The jelly congealed in the refrigerator. 果冻在冰箱里凝固了。The blood congealed in the cold air. 血在寒冷的空气中凝固了。|solidifies, hardens, sets|liquefies, melts|动词
