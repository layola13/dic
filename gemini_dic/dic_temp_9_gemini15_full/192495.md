scrunchies:/ˈskrʌntʃiz/| n.|发圈，松紧发带|A scrunchie is a hair tie made from a fabric tube that is gathered at one end to create a soft, stretchy band.  They are often made from materials like fabric scraps, silk, or satin.|例句：She wore a bright pink scrunchie to match her outfit. 她戴着一个亮粉色发圈来搭配她的衣服。

例句： I bought a set of scrunchies in different colors. 我买了一套不同颜色的发圈。


近义词：hair tie, hair band|反义词:|无
