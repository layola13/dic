grocery:/ˈɡroʊsəri/ | n. | 食品杂货；食品店 | Goods sold at a grocery store, typically food and household supplies.  |  I need to go to the grocery store to buy milk and eggs. 我需要去杂货店买牛奶和鸡蛋。| provisions, supplies | luxury goods | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I buy groceries every week. = 我每周都买杂货。
The grocery store opens at 7 am. = 杂货店早上7点开门。


一般过去时:
We bought groceries yesterday. = 我们昨天买了杂货。
She went to the grocery store last night. = 她昨晚去了杂货店。


现在完成时:
I have already bought groceries for this week. = 我已经买了本周的杂货。
They haven't finished grocery shopping yet. = 他们还没完成购物。


过去完成时:
I had already bought groceries before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，我已经买好了杂货。
We had run out of groceries before the weekend. = 我们在周末之前就用完了杂货。


将来时:
I will go grocery shopping tomorrow. = 我明天要去买杂货。

虚拟语气:

I wish I had bought more groceries. = 我真希望我买了更多的杂货。


被动语态:

疑问句:
