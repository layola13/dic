recopied:/ˌriːˈkɒpid/| /ˌriːˈkɑːpid/ | v. |重新抄写；复印|To copy something again.  This implies a previous copying event. It suggests a more deliberate and careful process than a simple copy.|例句：He recopied the entire document to correct the errors. 他重新抄写了整份文件以纠正错误。

例句：She recopied the notes to ensure accuracy. 她重新抄写了笔记以确保准确性。


近义词：copy again, rewrite, reproduce


反义词：原创，编写original, compose


词性：动词
