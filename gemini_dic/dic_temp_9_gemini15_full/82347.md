growan:/ˈɡroʊæn/| n.|呻吟声，抱怨声|a low, prolonged sound expressing pain, unhappiness, or disapproval.|例句：A growan of pain escaped her lips.  一声痛苦的呻吟从她嘴唇发出。|例句：The audience let out a growan of displeasure at the announcement. 观众对这个消息发出了不满的呻吟声。|近义词：groan, moan, whine|反义词：cheer, laughter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The baby growans when it's hungry. = 宝宝饿了的时候会哼哼唧唧。
He growans every time he has to do the dishes. = 他每次不得不洗碗的时候都会抱怨。


一般过去时:
She growaned in her sleep last night. = 她昨晚睡觉时哼哼唧唧的。
The old man growaned with discomfort. = 老人因不舒服而呻吟。
