maturant:/ˈmætʃərənt/| n. |即将毕业的学生；成熟的人|A student who is about to graduate; someone who has reached maturity.|例句：He is a maturant at a prestigious high school. 他是一所著名高中的即将毕业的学生。

例句：She's a maturant, ready to face the challenges of adulthood. 她已经成熟，准备迎接成年后的挑战。

近义词：graduate, senior, adult

反义词：child, juvenile, immature

词性：名词
