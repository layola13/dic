distinctly:/dɪˈstɪŋktli/ | adv. | 清晰地；明显地；显著地 | Clearly and unmistakably; in a way that is easily perceived or understood.  | The speaker distinctly enunciated each word.  说话者清晰地吐字。 | clearly, plainly, evidently, noticeably | indistinctly, vaguely, unclearly, ambiguously
