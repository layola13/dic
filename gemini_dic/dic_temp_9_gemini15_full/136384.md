nonrestorative: /ˌnɒnˈrɛstərətɪv/ | adj. |非修复性的，非恢复性的 | Not restoring or capable of restoring something to its former state; not providing restoration or recuperation.  It implies a lack of replenishment or rejuvenation. | 例句：The nonrestorative sleep left her feeling exhausted.  (那种非修复性的睡眠让她感到筋疲力尽。)  The nonrestorative nature of the treatment meant the patient's condition did not improve. (治疗的非修复性意味着病人的病情没有好转。) | 近义词：unrestorative, unproductive, ineffective | 反义词：restorative, recuperative, refreshing
