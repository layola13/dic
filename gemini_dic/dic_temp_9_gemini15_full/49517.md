demanded:/dɪˈmændɪd/ | v. | 要求；需要；查问 | To ask for something forcefully, as a right or as a necessity; to require something. |例句：The workers demanded better pay and working conditions. 工人们要求提高工资和改善工作条件。
The situation demanded immediate action.  情况需要立即采取行动。|近义词：required, requested, insisted on, claimed |反义词：offered, provided, gave


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demands a lot from his employees. 他对他的员工要求很高。
The job demands long hours and hard work. 这份工作需要长时间工作和努力工作。


一般过去时:
She demanded an explanation for his behavior. 她要求对他行为的解释。
They demanded to see the manager. 他们要求见经理。


现在完成时:
He has demanded a refund for the faulty product. 他已经要求退回有缺陷的产品的退款。
The public has demanded greater transparency from the government. 公众已经要求政府提高透明度。


过去完成时:
She had demanded an apology before she would speak to him again. 在她再次和他说话之前，她已经要求他道歉。
They had demanded to know the truth for years. 他们多年来一直要求知道真相。


将来时:
He will demand an investigation into the matter. 他将要求对这件事进行调查。
The protesters will demand changes to the law. 抗议者将要求修改法律。


虚拟语气:
I demanded that he apologize. 我要求他道歉。（虚拟语气，从句谓语动词用过去时）
She demanded that the letter be sent immediately. 她要求立即寄出这封信。（虚拟语气，从句谓语动词用过去时）


被动语态:
Better working conditions were demanded by the workers. 工人们要求改善工作条件。（被动语态）
A full investigation was demanded by the police. 警方要求进行全面调查。（被动语态）


疑问句:
What did he demand? 他要求什么？
What are they demanding from the company? 他们向公司要求什么？


否定句:
He didn't demand anything from me. 他没有向我要求任何东西。
They didn't demand a raise. 他们没有要求加薪。


PHRASE->
in demand:  需求大的
demand for: 对…的需求
meet the demands of: 满足…的要求
on demand:  按要求；随叫随到
demanding job:  要求很高的工作
