dump's: /dʌmps/ | n. | 垃圾堆；倾倒；沮丧 |  This word is the possessive form of "dump," indicating something belonging to or associated with a dump.  It's less common than the base word. | Example: The dump's contents were overflowing. 垃圾堆里的东西都溢出来了。|近义词: landfill's, rubbish heap's |反义词:  N/A


USAGE->
This section is not applicable to "dump's" as it is a possessive noun and does not conjugate through verb tenses.  The usage examples would be for the word "dump."

一般现在时:
They dump the trash every Tuesday. = 他们每周二倾倒垃圾。
He dumps his old clothes. = 他丢弃旧衣服。

一般过去时:
They dumped the old furniture. = 他们丢弃了旧家具。
She dumped him. = 她甩了他（与人分手）。

现在完成时:
They have dumped all the waste. = 他们已经倾倒了所有废物。
He has dumped his girlfriend. = 他已经甩了他的女朋友。

过去完成时:
They had dumped the chemicals before the inspection. = 他们检查前已经倾倒了化学品。
She had dumped all her belongings before leaving. = 她离开前已经丢弃了所有财物。

将来时:
They will dump the excess water. = 他们将会倾倒多余的水。
He will dump the project. = 他将会放弃这个项目。

虚拟语气:
If they dumped the waste properly, the environment would be better. = 如果他们正确地倾倒废物，环境将会更好。
I wish they hadn't dumped the toxic chemicals. = 我真希望他们没有倾倒有毒化学品。

被动语态:
The trash was dumped illegally. = 垃圾被非法倾倒。
The old car was dumped by the roadside. = 旧车被丢弃在路边。

疑问句:
Where do they dump the garbage? = 他们把垃圾倒在哪里？
When will they dump the waste? = 他们什么时候倾倒废物？

否定句:
They don't dump trash here. = 他们不在这里倾倒垃圾。
He didn't dump the evidence. = 他没有丢弃证据。
