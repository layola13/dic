damage:/ˈdæmɪdʒ/ | n. vt. |损害；损失；赔偿 | to harm something, physically or emotionally; the harm done |近义词：harm, injury, impairment |反义词：repair, restoration, benefit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm damages the crops every year. = 暴风雨每年都会损害庄稼。
He damages his reputation with his careless words. = 他因轻率的言辞而损害了自己的名誉。


一般过去时:
The earthquake damaged many buildings. = 地震损坏了许多建筑物。
The accident damaged his car severely. = 事故严重损坏了他的汽车。


现在完成时:
The hurricane has damaged the coastal areas. = 飓风已经损坏了沿海地区。
He has damaged his health by smoking too much. = 他由于吸烟过多而损害了自己的健康。


过去完成时:
The heavy rain had damaged the bridge before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，大雨已经损坏了桥梁。
They had damaged the machine beyond repair before we noticed it. = 在我们注意到之前，他们已经把机器损坏到无法修理的地步了。


将来时:
The strong wind will damage the trees. = 强风会损坏树木。
The upcoming flood will damage the houses near the river. = 即将来临的洪水将损坏河边的房屋。


虚拟语气:
If I had been more careful, I wouldn't have damaged the vase. = 如果我更小心点，就不会损坏花瓶了。
It would be a great loss if the museum is damaged by fire. = 如果博物馆被烧毁，那将是巨大的损失。


被动语态:
The car was damaged in the accident. = 汽车在事故中受损了。
The painting was damaged by careless handling. = 这幅画因处理不当而受损。


疑问句:
Was the building damaged in the fire? = 建筑物在火灾中受损了吗？
How much damage did the storm cause? = 暴风雨造成了多少损失？


否定句:
The accident didn't damage the car too badly. = 事故并没有把车损坏得太严重。
The flood didn't damage my house. = 洪水没有损坏我的房子。


PHRASE->
do damage to 对…造成损害
cause damage 造成损害
repair the damage 修复损坏
assess the damage 评估损失
limit the damage 限制损失
