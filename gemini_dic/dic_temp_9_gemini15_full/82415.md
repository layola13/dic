grueling:/ˈɡruːəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 费力的；使人精疲力竭的；艰苦的 | extremely tiring and demanding | The grueling training schedule left the athletes exhausted.  艰苦的训练计划使运动员精疲力竭。  The marathon was a grueling experience. 马拉松是一次艰苦的经历。 | taxing, arduous, strenuous, exhausting | easy, relaxing, effortless | 形容词
