carp:/kɑːrp/| n. vi. |鲤鱼；抱怨；吹毛求疵| A carp is a type of fish.  To carp is to complain constantly, often about unimportant things. |例句：He caught a large carp in the lake. 他在湖里钓到一条大鲤鱼。

He constantly carps about the smallest things. 他总是为一些鸡毛蒜皮的小事抱怨。|近义词：complain, grumble, nag, kvetch |反义词：praise, compliment, commend |名词，不及物动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He carps about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
They carp about their jobs constantly. = 他们不断抱怨他们的工作。


一般过去时:
She carped about the service at the restaurant. = 她抱怨餐厅的服务。
He carped at his wife all evening. = 他整个晚上都在唠叨他的妻子。


现在完成时:
He has carped about this problem for weeks. = 他已经抱怨这个问题好几个星期了。
They have carped incessantly since the meeting. = 自从会议以来，他们一直在不停地抱怨。


过去完成时:
She had carped about the noise all day before she finally complained to management. = 在她最终向管理层投诉之前，她已经抱怨噪音一整天了。
They had carped for years before they decided to leave the company. = 在他们决定离开公司之前，他们已经抱怨了好几年了。


将来时:
He will carp about the food if it's not perfect. = 如果食物不完美，他会抱怨的。
They will carp endlessly unless we address their concerns. = 除非我们解决他们的担忧，否则他们会没完没了地抱怨。


虚拟语气:
If he carped less, people would like him more. = 如果他不那么爱抱怨，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish he wouldn't carp so much about trivial matters. = 我希望他不要对琐碎的事情抱怨那么多。


被动语态:
His complaints were carped about by his colleagues. = 他的抱怨受到了同事们的批评。(被动语态在此处用法略显牵强，更常用主动语态)
The decision was carped about extensively in the press. = 该决定在媒体上受到了广泛的批评。(被动语态在此处用法略显牵强，更常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Does he carp about everything? = 他是不是对所有事情都抱怨？
Why are they carping so much today? = 他们今天为什么抱怨这么多？


否定句:
He didn't carp about the delay. = 他没有抱怨延误。
They didn't carp at all during the presentation. = 他们在演示过程中根本没有抱怨。


PHRASE->
carp at someone = 对某人吹毛求疵
carp about something = 抱怨某事
constant carping = 不断的抱怨
idle carping = 无谓的抱怨
petty carping = 小题大做的抱怨
