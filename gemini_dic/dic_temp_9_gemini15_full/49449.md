delta:/ˈdɛltə/| n. |三角洲；增量；变化；德尔塔|A delta is a landform created by the deposition of sediment that is carried by a river as the flow leaves its mouth and enters slower-moving or stagnant water.  It is also used to represent a change or increment, particularly in mathematics and science.  The Greek uppercase letter Δ is often used as a symbol for delta.|例句：The Nile Delta is a fertile agricultural region. 尼罗河三角洲是一个肥沃的农业区。 The delta between the two measurements was significant. 两个测量值之间的差异很大。|近义词：alluvial fan, estuary, increment, variation|反义词：source (of a river)
