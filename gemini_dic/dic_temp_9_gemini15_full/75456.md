fruits:/fruːts/| n.|水果，果实|The word "fruits" refers to the sweet and fleshy product of a tree or other plant that contains seeds and can be eaten as food.  It is typically the mature ovary of a flowering plant, containing seeds.  The term can refer to a single piece of fruit or a collection of fruits.|例句：Many fruits are rich in vitamins. 许多水果富含维生素。

例句：The market is full of fresh fruits. 市场上有许多新鲜水果。


近义词： produce, berries,  (depending on context) vegetables


反义词：None (a direct antonym doesn't exist; you might use "vegetables" in contrast, but this isn't a true antonym)


词性:名词
