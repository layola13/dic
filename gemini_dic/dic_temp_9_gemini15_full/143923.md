outpromise:/ˌaʊtˈprɑːmɪs/ | vt. |超过…许诺；承诺过多 | To promise more than one can deliver; to exceed in promising.  |例句：He outpromised himself and couldn't fulfill all his pledges. 他承诺过多，无法兑现所有承诺。|例句：The company outpromised its capabilities in the marketing campaign.  这家公司在营销活动中夸大了自己的能力。|近义词：overpromise, exaggerate, oversell |反义词：underpromise


USAGE->

They outpromise what they can deliver. = 他们承诺的东西超出了他们的能力。
