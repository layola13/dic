hyposternum: /ˌhaɪpoʊˈstɜːrnəm/ | n. |胸骨下部|The hyposternum is the lower part of the sternum, also known as the xiphoid process.  It is a small, cartilaginous structure that ossifies (turns to bone) with age. It's an important anatomical landmark, and its location is relevant in various medical procedures and examinations.|The doctor palpated the hyposternum to locate the underlying organs.|近义词：xiphoid process|反义词：manubrium (of sternum)
