ecumenism:/ɪˈkjuːmənɪzəm/| n. |基督教合一运动；普世主义|The principle or aim of promoting unity among different Christian churches.  It emphasizes the commonalities between different denominations and encourages cooperation and understanding. |The World Council of Churches is a prominent example of ecumenism in action. 世界基督教协进会是普世主义行动的一个突出例子。|interfaith dialogue, religious unity|religious division, sectarianism|名词
