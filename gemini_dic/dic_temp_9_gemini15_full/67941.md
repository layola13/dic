facework:/ˈfeɪsˌwɜːrk/| n. |面子工程；人际交往策略；维护体面|The process of managing one's own and others' self-image during social interaction.  It involves strategies used to maintain a positive face (a desired self-image) and to avoid face-threatening acts (FTAs). |例句：She engaged in considerable facework to maintain a professional demeanor during the difficult negotiation. 她为了在艰难的谈判中保持专业的举止，进行了大量的面子工程。


例句：He used subtle facework to avoid embarrassing his colleague in front of the client. 他运用微妙的人际交往策略，避免在客户面前让同事难堪。


近义词：impression management, social skill, politeness strategy |反义词：insult, aggression, rudeness |名词
