caterwauls:/ˈkætərˌwoːlz/| v.|猫叫；发出尖厉刺耳的声音 |To make a harsh, unpleasant cry, like that of a cat.  The word often implies a prolonged and discordant sound.| 例句：The cats caterwauled all night long. = 猫整夜都在猫叫。 The children's caterwauling annoyed the neighbors. = 孩子们的尖声叫嚷惹恼了邻居们。|近义词：screech, yell, shriek, howl|反义词：whisper, murmur
