pumping:/ˈpʌmpɪŋ/| v. |抽送；泵送；激动；(心脏)搏动|The act of pumping something, typically a liquid or gas, using a pump.  It can also refer to the rhythmic action of a heart or the energetic activity of something.|例句：The farmer is pumping water from the well.  农民正在从井里抽水。

例句：My heart was pumping fast after the run.  跑步后我的心跳很快。|近义词：pushing, propelling, throbbing|反义词：stillness, inactivity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pumps water every day. = 他每天都抽水。
The heart pumps blood throughout the body. = 心脏将血液泵送到全身。

一般过去时:
They pumped the gas into the tank. = 他们把汽油泵进油箱。
The firefighters pumped water onto the burning building. = 消防员向燃烧的建筑物喷水。

现在完成时:
She has pumped up her bicycle tires. = 她已经给自行车轮胎充气了。
They have pumped millions of dollars into the project. = 他们已经向这个项目投入了数百万美元。

过去完成时:
He had pumped all the water out before the flood. = 在洪水到来之前，他已经把所有的水都抽出来了。
The machine had pumped out all the oil before it broke down. = 机器在故障前已经把所有的油都泵出来了。

将来时:
We will pump the water into the reservoir. = 我们将把水抽入水库。
They will pump the sewage into the treatment plant. = 他们将把污水泵入处理厂。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
