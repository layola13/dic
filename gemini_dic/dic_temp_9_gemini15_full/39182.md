congener:/ˈkɒndʒənər/| n. |同类；同种；同属物种 | A congener is a member of the same genus or species as another specified organism.  It refers to something of the same kind or class. | Example: The leopard is a congener of the lion.  豹是狮子的同类。|近义词：peer, fellow, counterpart |反义词：opposite, different species
