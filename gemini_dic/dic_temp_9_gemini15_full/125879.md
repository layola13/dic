muscles: /ˈmʌsəlz/ | n. |肌肉 |Muscles are the tissues in the body that cause movement.  They contract and relax to produce movement. |例句：He flexed his muscles to show his strength. 他弯曲他的肌肉来展示他的力量。

例句：Regular exercise helps build strong muscles. 定期锻炼有助于增强肌肉。


近义词：muscle tissue, musculature | 反义词：None (no direct antonym)


词源:  The word "muscle" comes from the Latin word "musculus," a diminutive of "mus," meaning "mouse."  The Latin term likely arose from the appearance of certain muscles, particularly the biceps, under the skin, which were thought to resemble mice.
