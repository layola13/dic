gladder:/ˈɡlædər/| adj.|更高兴的，更快乐的|More glad; happier.  It is the comparative form of the adjective "glad".|例句：I'm gladder to see you than I expected. 我比预期的更高兴见到你。

例句：She felt gladder after receiving the good news.  收到好消息后，她感觉更高兴了。
