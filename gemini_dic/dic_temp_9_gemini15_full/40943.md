coontie:/ˈkuːnti/| n. |泽米棕榈，西米棕榈|A cycad, Zamia integrifolia, native to the southeastern United States, with a starchy stem that was formerly used as a food source by Native Americans.  The plant is also known for its medicinal properties. |Example: The Seminole people traditionally used coontie starch as a food staple.  塞米诺尔人传统上使用泽米棕榈淀粉作为主食。|近义词：Zamia integrifolia (scientific name)|反义词：无
