irreduction:/ˌɪrɪˈdʌkʃən/ | n. |不可约化，不可简化 |The process or state of being irreducible;  inability to be reduced to simpler components or explained by simpler principles.  It often appears in contexts discussing complex systems, theories, or phenomena that cannot be simplified without losing essential information or meaning. | Example: The irreduction of the problem to a simpler form made it difficult to solve.  不可将问题简化为更简单的形式，这使得它难以解决。The irreduction of quantum mechanics to classical mechanics is a central tenet of quantum theory. 量子力学不可约化为经典力学是量子理论的核心信条。 |近义词：irreducibility, incompressibility, unsimplifiable |反义词：reduction, simplification |名词
