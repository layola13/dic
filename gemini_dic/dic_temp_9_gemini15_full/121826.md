misrelating: /ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 误解；曲解；错误地联系 |  To interpret or connect incorrectly; to misunderstand the relationship between things.  | 例句：He was misrelating the facts, leading to a flawed conclusion. 他曲解了事实，导致了错误的结论。

The constant misrelating of events led to widespread confusion.  不断地曲解事件导致了广泛的混乱。| 近义词：misinterpret, misconstrue, misrepresent | 反义词：understand, correctly interpret
