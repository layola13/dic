counterinflation: /ˌkaʊntərɪnˈfleɪʃən/ | n. | 反通货膨胀 | Measures taken to reduce inflation.  The process of reducing inflation. | Example: The government implemented a strict counterinflationary policy to curb rising prices.  政府实施了严格的反通货膨胀政策以抑制物价上涨。


The central bank's counterinflationary measures included raising interest rates.  中央银行的反通货膨胀措施包括提高利率。


近义词: anti-inflation, deflation (only if successful in reducing inflation to below 0%), inflation control


反义词: inflation, inflationary pressure


词性: noun
