diabetes insipidus: /ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz ɪnˈsɪpɪdəs/ | n. |尿崩症|A disorder characterized by the excretion of large amounts of dilute urine, resulting from a deficiency of vasopressin (antidiuretic hormone).  It is caused by problems with the kidneys or the pituitary gland, which produces vasopressin.  Symptoms include excessive thirst and frequent urination. It is different from diabetes mellitus, which involves problems with blood sugar.|The patient presented with polyuria and polydipsia, consistent with diabetes insipidus.|近义词：central diabetes insipidus, nephrogenic diabetes insipidus |反义词：diabetes mellitus
