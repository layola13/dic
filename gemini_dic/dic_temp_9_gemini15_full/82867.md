gullible:/ˈɡʌləbl/| adj.|轻信的，易受骗的|easily persuaded to believe something; credulous.  The word implies a lack of critical thinking and judgment.|例句：He's so gullible, he'll believe anything you tell him. 他太轻信了，你对他说什么他都相信。

例句：Don't be so gullible; that deal sounds too good to be true.  别那么轻信，那交易听起来好得不像真的。


近义词：credulous, naive, trusting, unsuspecting

反义词：skeptical, cynical, distrustful, discerning
