philosophising:/ˈfɪləsəfaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 哲学思考；思辨；空想 | The act of engaging in philosophical thought or speculation; reflecting on fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, values, reason, mind, and language. | Example: He spent hours philosophising about the meaning of life. 他花了数小时思考人生的意义。  |近义词：speculating, pondering, reflecting, contemplating | 反义词：acting, doing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He philosophises about ethics every evening. 他每天晚上都在思考伦理问题。
She philosophises on the nature of reality. 她沉思着现实的本质。


一般过去时:
They philosophised for hours about the existence of God. 他们花了几个小时讨论上帝的存在。
He philosophised deeply on the meaning of freedom. 他深入思考了自由的意义。


现在完成时:
I have philosophised on this topic for many years. 我思考这个话题很多年了。
She has philosophised extensively about the nature of consciousness. 她广泛地思考了意识的本质。


过去完成时:
We had philosophised about the problem for days before finding a solution. 在找到解决方案之前，我们已经思考这个问题好几天了。
They had philosophised extensively on the subject before writing their paper. 在写论文之前，他们已经对这个主题进行了广泛的思考。


将来时:
He will philosophise on the implications of artificial intelligence. 他将思考人工智能的含义。
We will philosophise together about the future of humanity. 我们将一起思考人类的未来。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would philosophise more often. 如果我有更多时间，我会更频繁地思考。
I wish I could philosophise more freely without fear of judgment. 我希望我能更自由地思考，而不必担心被评判。


被动语态:
The topic was philosophised about extensively by the scholars.  这个话题被学者们广泛地讨论过。
The problem has been philosophised upon for centuries.  这个问题已经被思考了几个世纪了。


疑问句:
What are you philosophising about? 你在思考什么？
What philosophical questions are you considering? 你正在考虑什么哲学问题？


否定句:
He doesn't philosophise much these days. 他最近很少思考了。
She didn't philosophise about the political situation. 她没有思考政治局势。


PHRASE->
philosophising about  关于…的哲学思考
philosophising on  对…的哲学思考

practical philosophising 实用的哲学思考
