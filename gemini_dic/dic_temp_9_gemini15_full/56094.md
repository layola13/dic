doctoral: /ˈdɒktərəl/| /ˈdɑːktərəl/ | adj. | 博士的；博士学位的 | Relating to a doctor or doctorate; characteristic of a doctorate.  |例句：He completed his doctoral studies in physics. 他完成了物理学博士学习。
例句：She is writing her doctoral dissertation on the history of art. 她正在撰写关于艺术史的博士论文。|近义词：doctoral degree, postgraduate, graduate |反义词： undergraduate
