ryders:/ˈraɪdərz/| n.|附加条款，补充协议|A rider is a supplementary provision or clause added to a document, contract, or agreement. It modifies or extends the original terms.  |例句：The contract included a rider specifying the delivery date.  合同中有一项附加条款，规定了交付日期。|例句：We added a rider to the lease agreement concerning pet ownership. 我们在租赁协议中增加了一条关于宠物饲养的附加条款。|近义词：addendum, amendment, supplement|反义词：main text, original agreement|名词
