hewn:/hjuːn/| adj. v. |砍成的；凿成的；雕刻的| Hewn refers to something that has been shaped or formed by cutting or chopping, typically with an axe or other sharp tool.  It implies a rough, often rustic, quality.|The hewn timber formed the sturdy frame of the cabin.  砍成的木材构成了小屋坚固的框架。The ancient stones were beautifully hewn.  古老的石头被精巧地凿成。|carved, shaped, fashioned|unshaped, rough, natural|adj. v.
