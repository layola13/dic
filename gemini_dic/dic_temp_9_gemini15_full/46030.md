cybersquatting: /ˈsaɪbə(r)ˌskwɑːtɪŋ/ | n. | 网络抢注域名 | The act of registering a domain name that is similar to, or identical with, a trademark or brand name of another entity with the intent to profit from it, often by selling the name to the trademark holder.  | 例句：The company accused its competitor of cybersquatting after they registered a nearly identical domain name.  该公司指控其竞争对手在注册了一个几乎相同的域名后进行网络域名抢注。

例句：He was found guilty of cybersquatting after registering numerous domain names that resembled popular brands. 他因注册许多类似知名品牌的域名而被判犯有网络域名抢注罪。


近义词：domain name grabbing, trademark infringement, online brandjacking | 反义词：None (the antonym would describe legitimate domain registration)
