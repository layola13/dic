pastoral:/ˈpæstərəl/| adj.|田园的；牧羊的；乡村的|Relating to shepherds or rural life; idyllically rustic.|例句：The painting depicts a pastoral scene of sheep grazing in a green meadow. 这幅画描绘了羊在绿草地上吃草的田园风光。例句：She longed for a pastoral life, far away from the city's noise and bustle. 她渴望过一种田园般的生活，远离城市的喧嚣。|近义词：rural, rustic, idyllic, bucolic|反义词：urban, metropolitan, industrial
