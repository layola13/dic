generators:/ˈdʒɛnəreɪtərz/ | n. | 发电机；发生器；生产者 | A generator is a machine that converts mechanical energy into electrical energy.  Generators are used in power plants to produce electricity for homes and businesses. |  例句：The power plant relies on several generators to maintain a steady supply of electricity.  这家电厂依靠几台发电机来维持稳定的电力供应。

例句：We need a backup generator in case of a power outage.  万一停电，我们需要一台备用发电机。


近义词：producer, creator, engine | 反义词：consumer


词源:  The word "generator" comes from the Latin word "generare," meaning "to produce" or "to beget."  The suffix "-tor" indicates an agent or instrument.  Thus, "generator" literally means "something that produces."
