re-revise:/ˌriːrɪˈvaɪz/ | v. | 再修改，反复修改 | To revise something again; to make further changes or improvements to something that has already been revised.  |  The professor asked me to re-revise my essay because of several grammatical errors. 教授让我再次修改我的论文，因为有一些语法错误。  | revise, amend, edit |  accept, finalize |  v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-revise the document regularly. = 他们定期地再次修改文件。
We re-revise the plan every week. = 我们每周都重新修订计划。


一般过去时:
She re-revised her thesis before submission. = 她提交论文前又修改了一遍。
He re-revised the contract after the meeting. = 会议后他又修改了合同。


现在完成时:
They have re-revised the budget several times. = 他们已经几次修改了预算。
We have re-revised the marketing strategy. = 我们已经重新修订了营销策略。


过去完成时:
She had re-revised the manuscript before sending it to the editor. = 她把稿件寄给编辑之前又修改了一遍。
They had re-revised their proposal before presenting it. = 他们在提交提案之前又修改了一遍。


将来时:
They will re-revise the report next week. = 他们下周将再次修改报告。
We will re-revise the presentation before the conference. = 我们将在会议前再次修改演示文稿。


虚拟语气:
If they had re-revised the code, the bug would have been found. = 如果他们重新修改了代码，错误就会被发现。
I wish I had re-revised my application before submitting it. = 我希望我在提交申请之前又修改了一遍。

否定句:
