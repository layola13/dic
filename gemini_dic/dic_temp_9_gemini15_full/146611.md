overgrow:/ˈoʊvərɡroʊ/ | vt. |过度生长；蔓延；覆盖 |To grow beyond the normal limits; to cover or spread over something excessively.  |例句：The weeds have overgrown the garden.  杂草已经蔓延到整个花园。
The ivy has overgrown the walls of the house.  常春藤爬满了房子的墙壁。|近义词：overrun, proliferate,  encroach |反义词：undergrow, diminish, shrink


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weeds overgrow the path every year.  杂草每年都蔓延到小路上。
The vines overgrow the fence. 藤蔓爬满了栅栏。

一般过去时:
The garden was overgrown after the long summer. 漫长的夏天过后，花园里杂草丛生。
The plants quickly overgrown the flower bed. 植物迅速蔓延到花坛里。

现在完成时:
The path has been overgrown since the last time I walked it. 自我上次走这条路以来，小路已经被杂草覆盖了。
The old ruins have been overgrown with ivy for centuries. 古老的废墟几个世纪以来都被常春藤覆盖了。

过去完成时:
The field had been overgrown before the farmer could harvest the crop. 在农民收割庄稼之前，田地已经被杂草覆盖了。
The house had been overgrown with vines for years before it was discovered. 那栋房子在被发现之前，多年来都被藤蔓覆盖了。

将来时:
The garden will be overgrown if we don't weed it regularly. 如果我们不定期除草，花园就会杂草丛生。
The plants will overgrow the containers if not pruned. 植物如果不修剪就会长出容器。


虚拟语气:
If we hadn't been so busy, the garden wouldn't have been overgrown. 如果我们不那么忙，花园就不会杂草丛生了。
If only the weeds hadn't overgrown the path, we could have taken a shortcut. 要是杂草没有蔓延到小路上，我们本来可以抄近路的。


被动语态:
The garden was overgrown by weeds. 花园被杂草覆盖了。
The path is being overgrown with vines. 小路正在被藤蔓覆盖。


疑问句:
Is the garden overgrown? 花园杂草丛生了吗？
Has the path been overgrown by bushes? 小路被灌木丛覆盖了吗？


否定句:
The garden isn't overgrown yet. 花园还没有杂草丛生。
The plants haven't overgrown the pots yet. 植物还没有长出花盆。
