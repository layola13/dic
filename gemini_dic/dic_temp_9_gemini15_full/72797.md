foisty:/ˈfɔɪsti/| adj.| 陈旧的；霉臭的；过时的 | Musty or stale, especially from dampness or age;  having a stale, moldy smell.  The word often implies a slightly unpleasant smell associated with age and dampness.|例句：The old book smelled foisty. 这本旧书闻起来很霉。
The foisty basement needed airing out.  潮湿的地下室需要通风。|近义词：musty, stale, fusty, moldy, damp |反义词：fresh, new, clean
