cronstedtite:/ˈkrɒnstɛdaɪt/| n. |绿帘石|A dark green to black mineral, a hydrated iron silicate, belonging to the chlorite group. It is commonly found in metamorphic rocks and hydrothermal veins.  | Example: Cronstedtite was identified in the sample from the mine.  绿帘石在矿山的样本中被鉴定出来。|近义词：chamosite (similar iron silicate)|反义词：无|名词
