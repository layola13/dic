ovulum: /ˈɒvjʊləm/ | n. | 卵细胞；胚珠 | A small ovule; the female reproductive cell in plants and animals.  It is the structure that develops into a seed after fertilization. In animals, it is equivalent to an egg cell. | Example: The ovulum is fertilized by the pollen grain.  胚珠被花粉粒授精。  The ovulum developed into a seed. 卵细胞发育成了种子。|近义词：egg cell (animal), ovule (plant) |反义词：sperm cell (animal), pollen grain (plant)
