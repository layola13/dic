naturalised: /ˈnætʃrəlaɪzd/ | v. | 使归化；使入籍；使自然化 | To grant someone the rights and privileges of a citizen of a country they were not born in.  It also refers to the process of adapting to a new environment and becoming accepted as a member of a community or society. | Example:  He naturalised in the United States after living there for ten years. 他在美国住了十年后入籍了。  The plant has become naturalised in this region.  这种植物在这个地区已经归化了。 | 近义词：denizen, acclimatize, assimilate | 反义词：expatriate, alienate
