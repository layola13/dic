flirtationless: /ˌflɜːrteɪʃənles/ | adj. | 无调情的；不轻浮的；不卖弄风情的 | Lacking flirtation or playful romantic behavior.  The adjective describes a relationship or interaction devoid of any romantic teasing or suggestive behavior. |

例句：

Their relationship was entirely flirtationless, a stark contrast to their previous interactions.  = 他们的关系完全没有调情，这与他们之前的互动形成鲜明对比。

The meeting was strictly business and entirely flirtationless. = 会议完全是公事公办，没有任何调情。


近义词：chaste, demure, serious, platonic, unromantic

反义词：flirtatious, romantic, amorous, playful
