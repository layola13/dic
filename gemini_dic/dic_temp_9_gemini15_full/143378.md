outfenced: /ˌaʊtˈfɛnst/ | v. |  用篱笆围起来，隔离 | To enclose or separate with a fence.  | The farmer outfenced his fields to keep the cattle in.  农夫用篱笆围住了他的田地以防止牛跑掉。 | enclose, surround, separate | open, expose

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outfence their garden every spring. = 他们每年春天都会用篱笆围住他们的花园。
The farmer outfences his property to protect his crops. = 农夫用篱笆围住他的财产以保护他的庄稼。


一般过去时:
He outfenced the entire property last year. = 他去年用篱笆围住了整块地。
The neighbors outfenced their yards to increase privacy. = 邻居们用篱笆围住了他们的院子以增加隐私。


现在完成时:
They have outfenced the entire area to prevent trespassing. = 他们已经用篱笆围起了整个区域以防止擅自进入。
The city has outfenced the park to improve safety. = 城市已经用篱笆围住了公园以提高安全性。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, they had outfenced the vulnerable areas. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经用篱笆围住了易受影响的地区。
They had outfenced the garden before the rabbits damaged the plants. = 兔子破坏植物之前，他们已经用篱笆围住了花园。


将来时:
We will outfence the new addition to the house next month. = 我们下个月将用篱笆围住房屋的新增部分。
The developers will outfence the construction site for safety reasons. = 开发商将出于安全原因用篱笆围住建筑工地。


虚拟语气:
If they had outfenced the property, the animals wouldn't have escaped. = 如果他们用篱笆围住了房产，动物就不会逃跑了。
I wish we had outfenced the yard earlier to prevent the dog from running away. = 我真希望我们早点用篱笆围住院子，以防止狗跑掉。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
