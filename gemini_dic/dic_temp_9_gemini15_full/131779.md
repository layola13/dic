nonconnectivity: /ˌnɒnkɒnɛkˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |非连通性，不连通|The absence or lack of connectivity; the state of not being connected.  This term is often used in the context of networks, graphs, or systems where elements are supposed to be interconnected but are not.|例句：The nonconnectivity of the network caused significant delays in data transmission. 网络的不连通性导致数据传输出现重大延迟。

The nonconnectivity of the road network made it difficult to reach the remote village.  道路网络的不连通性使得到达偏远村庄变得困难。


近义词：disconnection, discontinuity, isolation, separation|反义词：connectivity, connection, interconnectivity

词性: 名词
