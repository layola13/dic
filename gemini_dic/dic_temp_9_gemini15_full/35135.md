clouds:/klaʊdz/| n.|云彩，云朵|A visible mass of condensed water vapor floating in the atmosphere, typically high above the ground.|例句：Dark clouds gathered overhead.  乌云密布头顶。|例句：The clouds drifted lazily across the sky. 云朵懒洋洋地飘过天空。|近义词：vapor, mist, fog|反义词：sunshine, clear sky


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The clouds are white today. = 今天的云是白色的。
Dark clouds cover the sky. = 黑云遮蔽了天空。


一般过去时:
The clouds were dark and stormy. = 云是黑暗而暴风雨的。
Fluffy white clouds filled the sky yesterday. = 昨天天空充满了蓬松的白云。


现在完成时:
The clouds have gathered. = 云已经聚集起来了。
The clouds have dispersed. = 云已经散开了。


过去完成时:
The clouds had already disappeared before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前云就已经消失了。
The rain had stopped, and the clouds had cleared. = 雨停了，云也散开了。


将来时:
The clouds will likely bring rain. = 云可能会带来雨。
The clouds will probably clear up later. = 云可能会在晚些时候散开。


虚拟语气:
If the clouds were gone, we could see the stars. = 如果云消失了，我们就能看到星星了。
I wish the clouds would clear so we can have a picnic. = 我希望云能散开，这样我们就能野餐了。


被动语态:
The sky is covered by the clouds. = 天空被云覆盖着。
The mountain peaks were hidden by the clouds. = 山峰被云遮住了。


疑问句:
Are those clouds rain clouds? = 那些是雨云吗？
What kind of clouds are those? = 那些是什么样的云？


否定句:
There aren't any clouds in the sky. = 天空中没有云。
The clouds didn't bring rain. = 云没有带来雨。
