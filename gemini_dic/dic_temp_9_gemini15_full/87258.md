heritably:/ˈhɛrɪtəbli/| adj.| 可遗传地，遗传地 |  Capable of being inherited; transmissible from parent to offspring.  | Example:  The disease is heritably linked to a specific gene.  这种疾病与特定基因具有遗传联系。  |近义词：genetically, hereditarily | 反义词：non-heritable, acquired


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  It describes the characteristic of being heritable.  Therefore, tense conjugations are irrelevant.  Instead, we'll illustrate its use in different sentence structures.

肯定句:
The trait is heritably determined. =  这种性状是由遗传决定的。
Eye color is often heritably passed down from parents to children. = 眼睛颜色通常是遗传自父母给子女的。

否定句:
The characteristic is not heritably determined. = 这种特征不是由遗传决定的。
Acquired characteristics are not heritably transmitted. = 后天习得的特征不会遗传。

疑问句:
Is this condition heritably linked to a specific gene? = 这种情况与特定基因有遗传联系吗？
Are these traits heritably influenced by environmental factors? = 这些性状受环境因素的遗传影响吗？


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "heritably". The word is primarily used in scientific and medical contexts.  The following phrases illustrate its usage in context:

heritably determined traits = 遗传决定的性状
heritably predisposed to = 遗传易感于
heritably linked diseases = 遗传相关的疾病
heritably stable characteristics = 遗传稳定的特征
heritably low risk = 遗传低风险
