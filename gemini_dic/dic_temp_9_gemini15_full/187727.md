roosts:/ruːsts/| n. v.|栖息处；（鸟的）栖息；（人）睡觉；|栖息处；（鸟类的）栖息；（人）睡觉；The chickens are roosting in the henhouse. |近义词：perch, nest, sleep |反义词：take flight, awaken


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds roost in the trees every night. = 鸟儿每晚都栖息在树上。
He roosts on the sofa after work. = 他下班后在沙发上休息。


一般过去时:
The birds roosted in the trees last night. = 鸟儿昨晚栖息在树上。
He roosted on the couch after a long day. = 长途跋涉后，他在沙发上休息。

虚拟语气:

If I had a comfortable chair, I would roost in it all day. = 如果我有一把舒适的椅子，我就会整天坐在上面休息。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Where do the birds roost? = 鸟儿在哪里栖息？
Where does he usually roost? = 他通常在哪里休息？


否定句:
The birds did not roost in the trees last night. = 鸟儿昨晚没有栖息在树上。
He didn't roost in his bed last night. = 他昨晚没有睡在床上。
