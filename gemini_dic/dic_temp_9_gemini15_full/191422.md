scherzi:/ˈʃɛrtsi/| n.|诙谐曲；谐谑曲|A scherzo (plural scherzi) is a musical composition, typically part of a larger work such as a symphony or string quartet, that is characterized by its lively, playful, and often mischievous character. It is usually in a fast tempo and employs a light and sometimes capricious style.  The term scherzo is Italian for "joke" or "jest."| 例句：The third movement of Beethoven's Fifth Symphony is a famous scherzo. 贝多芬第五交响曲的第三乐章是一首著名的谐谑曲。

The composer included a playful scherzo between the more serious movements. 作曲家在较为严肃的乐章之间加入了一段轻松活泼的谐谑曲。|近义词：capriccio, divertimento|反义词：adagio, largo|名词
