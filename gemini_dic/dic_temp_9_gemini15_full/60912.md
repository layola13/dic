elephant:/ˈɛləfənt/ | n. |大象 |A large plant-eating mammal with a prehensile trunk, long ivory tusks, and large ears.  Elephants are known for their intelligence and social behavior. |例句：The elephant trumpeted loudly.  大象大声鸣叫。

例句：We saw a herd of elephants migrating across the savanna. 我们看到一群大象正在穿越草原迁徙。


近义词： pachyderm (厚皮动物)


反义词： None (没有合适的反义词)


词性: 名词
