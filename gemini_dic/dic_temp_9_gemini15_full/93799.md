immersing: /ɪˈmɜːrsɪŋ/ | v. | 使沉浸；使投入；使深深吸引 |  To involve oneself deeply in a particular activity or experience.  | 例句：He was completely immersing himself in his work. 他完全沉浸在工作中。  | 例句：The museum's interactive exhibits were immersing and engaging for visitors of all ages. 博物馆的互动展品让各个年龄段的游客都感到身临其境且引人入胜。| 近义词：engrossing, absorbing, enveloping | 反义词：detaching, withdrawing, disengaging | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am immersing myself in a new book. = 我正沉浸在一本新书中。
She immerses herself in her painting every weekend. = 她每个周末都沉浸在绘画中。

一般过去时:
He immersed himself in the project for months. = 他几个月都沉浸在那个项目中。
They immersed themselves in the local culture during their trip. = 他们在旅行期间沉浸在当地文化中。

现在完成时:
I have immersed myself in learning a new language. = 我一直沉浸在学习一门外语中。
She has immersed herself in the world of classical music. = 她一直沉浸在古典音乐的世界里。

过去完成时:
He had immersed himself in his studies before the exam. = 他在考试前一直沉浸在学习中。
They had immersed themselves in the research for years.  = 他们多年来一直沉浸在研究中。

将来时:
I will immerse myself in the challenge ahead. = 我将全身心地投入到未来的挑战中。
She will immerse herself in her new role at the company. = 她将全身心地投入到公司的新角色中。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would immerse myself in that hobby. = 如果我有更多时间，我会沉浸在那项爱好中。
I wish I could immerse myself in a good book right now. = 我真希望我现在能沉浸在一本好书中。

被动语态:
The students were immersed in the virtual reality experience. = 学生们沉浸在虚拟现实体验中。
The audience was completely immersed in the performance. = 观众完全沉浸在表演中。

疑问句:
Are you immersing yourself in your work too much? = 你是不是太专注于工作了？
Is she immersing herself in a new project? = 她是否正全身心地投入到一个新项目中？

否定句:
He didn't immerse himself in the details of the report. = 他没有深入研究报告的细节。
She didn't want to immerse herself in the drama. = 她不想卷入这场风波中。
