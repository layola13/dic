delitigation: /ˌdiːlɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 权力下放；责任分担；授权；委任 | The process of delegating tasks or responsibilities to others.  It involves entrusting authority and accountability to individuals or teams lower in the organizational hierarchy.  This can improve efficiency, boost morale, and develop employee skills. | Example: The manager's delitigation of tasks ensured the project was completed on time.  经理的权力下放确保项目按时完成。| 近义词：delegation, assignment, empowerment | 反义词：centralization, micromanagement | 名词
