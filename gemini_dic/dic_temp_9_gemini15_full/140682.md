ominous:/ˈɑːmɪnəs/ | adj. |不祥的；凶兆的；预示不幸的 | Suggesting that something bad is going to happen; threatening.  | The ominous clouds gathered overhead.  不祥的乌云聚集在头顶。  The silence was ominous.  这寂静不祥。| threatening, sinister, foreboding, portentous | auspicious, favorable, propitious | 形容词
