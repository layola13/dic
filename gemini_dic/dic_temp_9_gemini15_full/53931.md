discourteously:/dɪsˈkɜːtʃəsli/| adj.|不礼貌地，粗鲁地|Uncourteous; rude; impolite.  It implies a lack of consideration for others' feelings or social graces. |He spoke discourteously to the elderly woman. 他对那位老太太说话很不礼貌。|rudely, impolitely, offensively |politely, courteously, respectfully |形容词

词源:
dis- (否定前缀) + courteous (有礼貌的) + -ly (副词后缀)  The root "court" relates to the royal court and the expected politeness within its context.  Over time, "courteous" came to mean polite and respectful in general.
