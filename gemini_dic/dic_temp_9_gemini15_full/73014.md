footboy: /ˈfʊtˌbɔɪ/ | n. | 男仆，侍童 | A male servant, especially a young one in a wealthy household,  responsible for running errands and performing menial tasks.  | 例句：The footboy scurried to answer the doorbell. = 男仆急忙去开门。 | 例句：He started his career as a humble footboy. = 他一开始是做卑微的男仆。 | 近义词：valet, page, errand boy | 反义词：master, employer
