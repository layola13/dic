monolithic:/ˌmɒnəˈlɪθɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈlɪθɪk/| adj.|整体的；庞大的；单一的；巨大的|Consisting of a single, large, or unbroken mass or piece.|The company's monolithic structure hindered innovation. 这家公司单一的结构阻碍了创新。|近义词：unified, integral, massive|反义词：fragmented, diverse, disparate
