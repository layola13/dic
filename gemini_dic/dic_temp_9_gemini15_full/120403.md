ministry: /ˈmɪnɪstri/ | n. | 部；部委；政府部门；（尤指宗教的）服务；职责；工作；奉献；尽力 |

英文解析：A ministry is a government department responsible for a particular area of administration.  It can also refer to the act of serving or the work of a minister, especially in a religious context.

例句：

The Ministry of Education is responsible for the national curriculum.  教育部负责国家课程。

She devoted her life to the ministry of the church. 她将一生奉献给了教会的事工。


近义词：department, office, service, vocation

反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, but concepts like "neglect" or "abandonment" could be considered opposites in certain contexts)

词性：名词
