chomping:/tʃɑːmpɪŋ/| v.|用力咀嚼；大嚼；|The act of chewing vigorously and noisily.|例句：He was chomping on a large piece of steak. 他正在大嚼一块牛排。|例句：The horse was chomping at the bit, eager to run. 马儿用力嚼着马嚼子，渴望奔跑。|近义词：chewing, munching, gnawing|反义词：sipping, nibbling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chomps his food loudly. = 他大声咀嚼食物。
She chomps on a carrot stick. = 她啃着胡萝卜条。

一般过去时:
He chomped on an apple. = 他啃了一个苹果。
The dog chomped at the bone. = 狗啃着骨头。

现在完成时:
He has chomped through half the cake. = 他已经吃掉了半块蛋糕。
She has chomped her way through a whole bag of chips. = 她已经吃掉了一整袋薯片。

过去完成时:
He had chomped all the cookies before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经把所有的饼干都吃掉了。
She had chomped through a whole book in one sitting. = 她一口气读完了一整本书。

将来时:
He will chomp on popcorn during the movie. = 看电影时他将吃爆米花。
She will chomp her way to victory. = 她将一路奋勇前进走向胜利。

虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would chomp on that sandwich. = 如果我饿了，我会吃掉那个三明治。
I wish I could chomp on a juicy steak right now. = 我真希望现在能吃一块多汁的牛排。

被动语态:  (Chomp is rarely used in the passive voice.)
The apple was chomped by the child. = 苹果被孩子啃了。(很少用)
The bone was being chomped by the dog. = 骨头正在被狗啃。(很少用)

疑问句:
Is he chomping on something? = 他在吃东西吗？
Are they chomping at the bit? = 他们是否迫不及待？

否定句:
He doesn't chomp his food loudly. = 他不响亮地咀嚼食物。
She didn't chomp on anything all day. = 她一整天什么也没吃。


PHRASE->
chomp at the bit  = 迫不及待
chomp down =用力咬
chomp through =吃掉
chomp up =吃掉（通常指粗略地吃掉）
chomp one's way through = 吃光，啃光
