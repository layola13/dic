perches:/ˈpɜːtʃɪz/ | n. |栖息处；鲈鱼；高处 | A perch is a place where a bird or other animal sits.  It can also refer to a high position or a type of fish.  |例句：The bird perched on the branch.  那只鸟栖息在树枝上。The eagle perched high on the cliff.  鹰在悬崖上高高地栖息着。|近义词：roost, rest, seat |反义词：ground, bottom
