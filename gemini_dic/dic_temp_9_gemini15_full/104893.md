keepeth:/ˈkiːpɪθ/| v.| 保持；遵守；保存 |  Archaic third-person singular simple present form of keep.  It means to continue to have or possess something; to maintain a state or condition; to prevent from escaping or disappearing; to obey or follow. | 例句：He keepeth the commandments. = 他遵守诫命。  The old woman keepeth her secrets close. = 老妇人保守着她的秘密。| 近义词：holds, maintains, preserves, observes | 反义词：loses, abandons, neglects | 动词
