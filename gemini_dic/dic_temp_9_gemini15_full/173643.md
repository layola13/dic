quasi-introduced:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ɪntrəˈdjuːst/| adj.| 准引入的，近似引入的 |  Describes something that is almost or partially introduced, but not fully or officially. It suggests a preliminary or tentative introduction, or an introduction that lacks the formality or completeness of a full introduction. | Example:  The quasi-introduced species showed signs of adaptation to the new environment, but its long-term survival was uncertain.  这个准引入物种显示出对新环境的适应迹象，但其长期生存仍不确定。| 近义词：partially introduced, tentatively introduced, informally introduced | 反义词：fully introduced, officially introduced | 形容词
