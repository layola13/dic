gerontologists:/ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |老年病学家，老年学专家 | Experts who study aging and the problems of older people.  They research the biological, psychological, and social aspects of aging. |例句：Gerontologists are working to develop new treatments for age-related diseases. 老年病学家正在努力开发治疗与年龄相关的疾病的新疗法。  |例句：Many gerontologists believe that regular exercise can help to slow down the aging process. 许多老年病学家认为，定期锻炼有助于延缓衰老过程。|近义词：geriatricians, specialists in aging |反义词：pediatricians (specialists in children)
