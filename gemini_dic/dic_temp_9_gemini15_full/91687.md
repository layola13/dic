hyperconcentration:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən/ | n. | 高度集中；过度集中 | The extreme concentration of a substance, typically in a limited area or volume. This often leads to significant environmental or health impacts.  | Example: The hyperconcentration of pollutants in the river led to a fish kill.  河流中污染物的过度集中导致鱼类死亡。 | 近义词：high concentration, intense concentration | 反义词：dilution, dispersion |  词性：名词
