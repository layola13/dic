grata:/ˈɡrɑːtə/| adj.|受欢迎的；令人愉快的|Welcome; pleasing; agreeable.|例句：She is a grata guest at all parties. 她在所有的聚会上都是受欢迎的客人。|近义词：welcome, acceptable, pleasing |反义词：unwelcome, unwelcome, disagreeable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The guest is grata to the host. = 客人受到主人的欢迎。
This proposal is grata to the board. = 这个提议董事会很满意。

一般过去时:
The ambassador was grata to the government. = 大使受到政府的欢迎。
The idea was grata to everyone. = 大家都很喜欢这个主意。


现在完成时:
The new policy has been grata to the public. = 新政策受到公众的欢迎。
Her work has been grata to her colleagues. = 她的工作得到了同事们的认可。


过去完成时:
The decision had been grata to the shareholders before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，股东们就已经对这个决定表示满意。
The song had been grata to the audience before the encore. = 在返场之前，观众们就已经很喜欢这首歌了。


将来时:
The outcome will be grata to all parties involved. = 结果将会让所有相关方满意。
The new product will be grata to consumers. = 新产品将会受到消费者的欢迎。


虚拟语气:
If the plan were grata to the committee, it would be approved. = 如果委员会对这个计划满意，它就会被批准。
I wish the result were grata to everyone. = 我希望结果能让每个人都满意。


被动语态:
The proposal was found to be grata by the committee. = 委员会发现这个提议是令人满意的。
His efforts were grata to the company. = 他的努力受到了公司的认可。


疑问句:
Is the suggestion grata to you? = 你觉得这个建议怎么样？
Is the new design grata to the clients? = 客户是否喜欢新的设计？


否定句:
The suggestion was not grata to the manager. = 经理不喜欢这个建议。
Her behavior was not grata to her friends. = 她的行为让朋友们反感。
