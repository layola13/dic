invigorators:/ɪnˈvɪɡəreɪtərz/| n.|令人振奋的事物；使精力充沛的人或事物|Things or people that invigorate; something that gives strength and energy.|The morning sun and fresh air were invigorators.  早晨的阳光和新鲜空气令人精神振奋。|The new CEO's innovative ideas were real invigorators for the company. 新任CEO的创新理念极大地鼓舞了公司。|stimulants, energizers, boosters|depressants, sedatives|名词
