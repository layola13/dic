sailing-boat: /ˈseɪlɪŋ bəʊt/ | n. | 帆船 | A boat propelled by sails.  It is a vessel that uses sails to harness wind power for propulsion.  Sailing boats range in size and complexity from small dinghies to large yachts. |例句：We spent the afternoon sailing on a small sailing-boat. 我们在一条小帆船上度过了下午的时光。  The regatta featured many beautiful sailing-boats of various sizes.  帆船赛上有许多不同大小的漂亮帆船。|近义词：sailboat, yacht (for larger vessels) |反义词：motorboat, powerboat
