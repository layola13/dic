juncaceous:/dʒʌŋˈkeɪʃəs/| adj. | 类似灯芯草的；灯芯草科的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Juncaceae, a family of plants including rushes.  The term often describes plants with grass-like leaves and stems. | Example: The juncaceous plants thrived in the marshy area.  类似灯芯草的植物在沼泽地带茁壮成长。|近义词：rush-like, grassy | 反义词：woody, leafy (in the context of plant structure)
