congrue:/kənˈɡruː/| adj.|一致的，和谐的，符合的|  Congruent means agreeing or corresponding; being in harmony. It often describes geometrical figures that are identical in shape and size, or concepts that align perfectly.  |例句：The two triangles are congruent. 这两个三角形全等。|例句：His actions were congruent with his beliefs. 他的行为与他的信仰相符。|近义词：consistent, compatible, harmonious|反义词：inconsistent, incompatible, discordant
