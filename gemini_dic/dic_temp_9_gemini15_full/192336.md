scriggle:/ˈskrɪɡəl/| v.|涂鸦，乱写|To write or draw carelessly or hurriedly.|例句：The child scribbled all over the wall. 孩子在墙上乱涂乱画。|例句：He scribbled a quick note and rushed out. 他匆匆写了个便条就冲了出去。|近义词：doodle, scrawl, daub|反义词：draw meticulously, write neatly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scribbles in his notebook during the meeting. = 他在会议期间在笔记本上乱涂乱画。
She scribbles notes while listening to the lecture. = 她听课时匆匆记笔记。
