composts:/ˈkɒmpəʊsts/ /ˈkɑːmpoʊsts/| n.|堆肥；肥料；（使…）腐熟|The process of decomposing organic matter, such as food scraps and yard waste, to create a nutrient-rich soil amendment.  It is a natural way to recycle organic materials and improve soil health.|例句：We compost our food scraps and yard waste in a backyard bin. 我们把厨房垃圾和院子里的垃圾放在后院的垃圾箱里进行堆肥。

例句：The farmer uses compost to enrich the soil in his fields. 这位农民用堆肥来改良田里的土壤。


近义词：manure, humus, mulch|反义词：None directly applicable.
