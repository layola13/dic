This is often seen in homeopathy, where dilutions are believed to increase potency, potentially leading to overpotencies.  In other contexts, it could refer to excessive strength or effect of a drug or other substance.|例句：Some practitioners claim that homeopathic overpotencies can lead to enhanced therapeutic effects, while others argue that this is a result of placebo effect.|近义词：excess potency, hyperpotency|反义词：underpotency, insufficient potency
