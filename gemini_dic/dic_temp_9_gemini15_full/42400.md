countercharm:/ˈkaʊntərtʃɑːrm/ | n. |对抗符咒；反咒；解药 |A countercharm is a charm or spell intended to counteract or neutralize the effect of another charm or spell.  It's a magical means of defense or reversal. |Example: The witch cast a countercharm to break the evil spell.  女巫施了一个反咒来打破邪恶的咒语。|近义词：antidote, counter-spell |反义词：charm, spell
