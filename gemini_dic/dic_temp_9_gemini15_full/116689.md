maturement:/məˈtʃʊərmənt/ | n. | 成熟；到期；发育完全 | The process of becoming mature; the state of being mature; the time when something reaches full development or is due.  It can refer to the ripening of fruit, the development of a person, or the due date of a financial instrument. | Example: The maturement of the bonds is in five years.  五年后债券到期。| Example: The maturement of his understanding was remarkable. 他理解能力的成熟令人瞩目。|近义词：ripening, development, completion, due date | 反义词：immaturity, youth, infancy
