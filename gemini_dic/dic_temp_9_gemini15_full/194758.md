selkies:/ˈsɛlkiːz/| n.|海豹人，海豹妖精|Mythical creatures of Celtic folklore, typically depicted as seals that can transform into humans.  They often have a tragic love story associated with their transformations.  Selkies are shapeshifters, shedding their seal skin to become human and regaining it to return to the sea.|例句：The legend of the selkies is a powerful and enduring story in Scottish folklore.  海豹人的传说在苏格兰民间传说中是一个强大而持久的传说。

The selkies' mournful song echoed across the lonely shore. 海豹人悲伤的歌声在孤寂的海岸回荡。|近义词：seal-folk, merfolk|反义词：N/A
