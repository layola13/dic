erlking:/ˈɛrlkɪŋ/| n. |精灵王，死神（尤其指引诱儿童的）|A mythical being, often depicted as a malevolent spirit or demon, who lures children to their deaths.  The term is primarily associated with Goethe's ballad "Erlkönig."|Example: The Erlking's chilling laughter echoed through the forest. 精灵王的令人毛骨悚然的狂笑声回荡在森林中。|近义词：goblin, sprite, specter, demon|反义词：angel, guardian
