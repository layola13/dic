necrophagous:/nɛˈkrɒfəɡəs/| adj. |食腐的，吃腐肉的|Feeding on dead flesh; characteristic of animals that feed on carrion.|例句：Vultures are necrophagous birds.  秃鹫是食腐的鸟类。

例句：Many insects are necrophagous, playing a vital role in decomposition. 许多昆虫是食腐的，在分解过程中起着至关重要的作用。


近义词：carnivorous (if the flesh is from recently dead animals), saprophagous


反义词：herbivorous, frugivorous


词性：形容词
