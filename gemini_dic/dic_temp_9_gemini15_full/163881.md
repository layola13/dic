predestiny:/ˈpriːdɛstɪni/| n.| 预定命运，天命|The predetermined course of events; fate.  Predestiny suggests an inescapable fate, often divinely ordained. It implies that events are fixed and cannot be altered.|例句：Some believe in predestiny, that our lives are already written.|近义词：fate, destiny, doom|反义词：free will, chance, accident|名词
