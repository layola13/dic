crystallizability:/ˌkrɪstəlɪˈzeɪbɪləti/| n. |结晶能力；可结晶性 |The ability of a substance to form crystals.  This property is influenced by factors such as the purity of the substance, the temperature, and the solvent used.  |例句：The high crystallizability of this compound allows for easy purification.  这种化合物的结晶能力强，易于纯化。

The crystallizability of the solution was improved by adding a seed crystal. 通过加入晶种提高了溶液的结晶能力。|近义词：crystallization tendency, capacity to crystallize |反义词：amorphousness, non-crystallizability
