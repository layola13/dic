nonracially:/ˌnɒnˈreɪʃəli/ | adj. | 非种族地；不考虑种族地 | Not based on or related to race.  It implies a focus on factors other than race when making decisions or judgments. | Example: The company promotes nonracially based hiring practices.  这家公司提倡不基于种族的招聘制度。  |近义词:race-blind, colorblind | 反义词: racially, race-based


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, and therefore does not conjugate like a verb.  It does not have tense.  Its usage is primarily in modifying nouns.

Example 1:  They conducted a nonracially biased study.  他们进行了一项没有种族偏见的调查研究。
Example 2:  The judge ruled that the decision must be made nonracially.  法官裁定必须做出不考虑种族的决定。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "nonracially".  Its usage is usually incorporated into longer phrases describing policies or actions.  For example:

nonracially motivated actions  非种族动机行为
nonracially based assessment  非种族评估
nonracially equitable distribution  非种族公平分配
promoting nonracially inclusive environments  促进非种族包容的环境
nonracially diverse community  非种族多元化的社区
