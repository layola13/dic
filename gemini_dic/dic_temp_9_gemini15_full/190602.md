satsumas:/ˈsætʃʊməz/| n. |柑橘，萨摩柑 |A satsuma is a type of mandarin orange, known for its sweet, easy-to-peel skin and seedless fruit.  They are typically smaller and flatter than other oranges.|例句：These satsumas are incredibly juicy and sweet. 这些萨摩柑非常多汁甜美。|例句：I bought a bag of satsumas from the farmer's market. 我从农贸市场买了一袋萨摩柑。|近义词：mandarin orange, mandarin, clementine |反义词:无

词源:
The word "satsuma" comes from Satsuma Province (薩摩国, Satsuma-no kuni), a former province in southern Kyushu, Japan, where this type of mandarin orange originated.  The name reflects its geographic origin.
