polygastric:/ˌpɒliˈɡæstrɪk/| adj.|多胃的；复胃的|Having more than one stomach.  This term is primarily used in zoology to describe animals with multiple stomach compartments, each with a specific digestive function.  |Example:  Cows are polygastric animals, possessing a four-chambered stomach.  牛是多胃动物，拥有一个四室胃。|近义词：multi-stomached |反义词：monogastric
