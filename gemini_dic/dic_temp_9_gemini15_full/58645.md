dumbfounding: /ˌdʌmˈfaʊndɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人惊愕的；令人吃惊的；使人哑口无言的 | Extremely surprising or shocking; causing someone to be speechless with astonishment. |

例句：

The news of his sudden resignation was absolutely dumbfounding.  = 他突然辞职的消息绝对令人震惊。

The magician's trick was so dumbfounding that the audience gasped in amazement. = 魔术师的戏法令人难以置信，观众惊叹不已。


近义词：astonishing, astounding, bewildering, shocking, surprising, staggering

反义词： unsurprising, expected, predictable

词性：形容词
