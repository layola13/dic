butterbur: /ˈbʌtərˌbɜːr/ | n. |  款冬 | A large plant with large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, purplish-pink or yellowish flowers that appear before the leaves.  It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally to treat various ailments.  | The butterbur grows best in moist soil near streams and rivers.  款冬最适宜生长在溪流和河流附近的潮湿土壤中。|  coltsfoot,  |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
