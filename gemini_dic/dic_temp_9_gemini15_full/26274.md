buttoning:/ˈbʌtnɪŋ/ | n. | 扣纽扣；扣紧的动作 | The act of fastening something with buttons.  | Example: The buttoning of his shirt was a slow and deliberate process.  扣他的衬衫扣子是一个缓慢而谨慎的过程。  |近义词：fastening|反义词：unbuttoning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He buttoning his coat every morning. 他每天早上都扣他的外套。
She buttoning her dress before going out. 她出门前扣上她的连衣裙。


一般过去时:
He buttoned his coat before going outside. 他出门前扣上了外套。
She buttoned up her dress quickly. 她迅速地扣上了她的连衣裙。


现在完成时:
I have buttoned all the buttons on my shirt. 我已经扣好了衬衫上的所有扣子。
She has buttoned her coat against the cold. 她已经扣上外套御寒。


过去完成时:
He had buttoned his shirt before the meeting started. 会议开始前他已经扣好了衬衫。
She had buttoned up her jacket before she left the house. 她离开家之前已经扣好了夹克。


将来时:
I will button my coat when I go out. 我出门时会扣上外套。
She will button up her dress for the party. 她会为派对扣上她的连衣裙。


虚拟语气:
If I had buttoned my coat, I wouldn't have caught a cold. 如果我当时扣上了外套，我就不会感冒了。
I wish I had buttoned my shirt properly. 我真希望我当时把衬衫扣好。


被动语态:
His coat was buttoned neatly. 他的外套整齐地扣好了。
The buttons were buttoned securely. 扣子扣得很牢固。


疑问句:
Is your shirt buttoned? 你的衬衫扣好了吗？
Are all the buttons buttoned on your coat? 你外套上的所有扣子都扣好了吗？


否定句:
He didn't button his shirt. 他没有扣衬衫。
She didn't button up her jacket properly. 她没有把夹克扣好。
