fuchs:/fʊks/| n. |狐狸；狡猾的人|The word "fuchs" is a German word for "fox."  It can refer to the animal itself, or, figuratively, to a cunning or sly person.  The word's usage is similar to the English word "fox" in both its literal and figurative senses.|例句：Der Fuchs schlich sich an das Huhn heran. (The fox crept up to the hen.)  He's a clever Fuchs, always getting what he wants. (他是個狡猾的家伙，總是能得到他想要的東西。)|近义词：fox, sly person, cunning person |反义词：innocent, naive, gullible |名词
