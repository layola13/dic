pro-Indian: /ˌproʊ ˈɪndiən/ | adj. | 亲印的，支持印度的 | Favoring or supporting India or Indians.  This term can refer to policies, individuals, or groups that express positive sentiment towards India, its people, or its interests.  It can also describe something that benefits India.  The term can be used neutrally or, depending on context, might carry positive or negative connotations.

例句：

He holds pro-Indian views on many geopolitical issues. = 他在许多地缘政治问题上持有亲印的观点。

The government implemented several pro-Indian trade policies. = 政府实施了几项亲印的贸易政策。


近义词：India-supporting, pro-India, phil-Indian (less common)


反义词：anti-Indian, hostile to India


词性: 形容词
