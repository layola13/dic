oriole:/ˈɔːriəʊl/| n.|金莺（鸟）|A New World songbird, typically brightly colored, known for its melodious song and acrobatic feeding habits.  Many species build hanging nests.|例句：The Baltimore oriole is a striking bird with bright orange and black plumage.  巴尔的摩金莺是一种有着鲜艳橙黑羽毛的漂亮鸟类。

例句： We saw an oriole flitting through the branches of the oak tree. 我们看到一只金莺在橡树枝间飞舞。


近义词：暂无完全同义词，可根据语境选择其他鸣禽名称。|反义词：暂无|词性：名词
