dramatists:/ˈdræməˌtɪsts/ | n. | 剧作家们 |  People who write plays. Dramatists craft stories, characters, and dialogue to create theatrical experiences. They may work in various genres, from comedies to tragedies, and their plays can be performed on stage, screen, or radio.  | Example:  Shakespeare and Ibsen are considered two of the greatest dramatists in history.  莎士比亚和易卜生被认为是历史上最伟大的剧作家之一。  The dramatists of the golden age explored complex themes of love and loss. 黄金时代的剧作家们探索了爱与失的复杂主题。 |近义词：playwrights, scriptwriters | 反义词： (no direct antonym,  but could consider terms like "novelists" or "poets" as representing different literary forms) | 名词
