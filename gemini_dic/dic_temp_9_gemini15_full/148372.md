overspends:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛndz/| v.|超支；过度消费|To spend more money than one has budgeted or planned.  It implies exceeding a financial limit or exceeding a reasonable amount.|例句：He overspends every month on entertainment.|近义词：exceed, squander, waste|反义词：save, economize, budget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overspends on clothes every month. = 他每个月都在衣服上超支。
They overspend on their holidays. = 他们在假期里花销过度。


一般过去时:
We overspent on our vacation last year. = 我们去年度假时超支了。
She overspent on Christmas presents. = 她圣诞节礼物买多了，超支了。


现在完成时:
I have overspent this month already. = 我这个月已经超支了。
They have overspent on their new house. = 他们在新房子上超支了。


过去完成时:
They had overspent before they realized it. = 他们意识到之前就已经超支了。
We had overspent on the project by the end of the month. = 到月底为止，我们在项目上已经超支了。


将来时:
I think we will overspend if we go on that trip. = 我认为如果我们去那次旅行，我们会超支。
They fear they will overspend on their wedding. = 他们担心婚礼会超支。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overspent, I would have saved more money. = 如果我没有超支，我本可以存更多钱。
I wish I hadn't overspent on that concert ticket. = 我真希望我没有在那个演唱会门票上超支。


被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used passively)
The budget was overspent. = 预算超支了。(This is less common and sounds more formal)
The funds were overspent on the project. = 项目资金超支了。(This is less common and sounds more formal)


疑问句:
Did they overspend on their holiday? = 他们在假期里超支了吗？
Have you ever overspent on something? = 你曾经在某件事上超支过吗？


否定句:
We didn't overspend this month. = 我们这个月没有超支。
They tried not to overspend on their trip. = 他们尽量避免在旅行中超支。


PHRASE->
overspend on sth. 在...上超支
overspend regularly 定期超支
overspend consistently  持续超支
danger of overspending  超支的危险
avoid overspending 避免超支
