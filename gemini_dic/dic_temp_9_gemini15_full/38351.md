concentrated: /ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪtɪd/ /ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 集中的；浓缩的；专心的 |

英文解析:  Concentrated means having a high proportion of a particular substance in a liquid or other mixture, or involving a lot of effort and attention in a small space or period of time.  It can describe a substance (like concentrated juice) or a person's focus (like concentrated effort).


例句:

The solution is highly concentrated, handle with care. = 溶液浓度很高，小心处理。

He gave the problem his full concentrated attention. = 他全神贯注地解决了这个问题。


近义词: focused, intense, dense, strong, thick

反义词: dilute, weak, dispersed, scattered, unfocused


词性: adjective
