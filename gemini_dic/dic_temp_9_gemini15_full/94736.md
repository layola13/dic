improvement:/ɪmˈpruːvmənt/ | n. |改进，改善，提高 | The improvement in her health was remarkable.  她的健康状况有了显著的改善。 |  advancement, betterment, enhancement | deterioration, worsening | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is making improvements to its software. = 公司正在改进其软件。
This training program shows significant improvements in employee skills. = 这个培训项目显示出员工技能的显著提高。


一般过去时:
They made several improvements to the house. = 他们对房子进行了几处改进。
The improvements to the infrastructure boosted the local economy. = 基础设施的改善促进了当地经济的发展。


现在完成时:
We have seen significant improvements in our test scores. = 我们已经看到我们的考试成绩有了显著提高。
The team has made many improvements to the project since it started. = 自项目启动以来，团队对其进行了许多改进。


过去完成时:
They had made many improvements before the final presentation. = 他们在最终演示之前已经做了许多改进。
The city had made considerable improvements to the public transportation system. = 该市已经对公共交通系统进行了相当大的改进。


将来时:
We will make improvements to the website next month. = 我们下个月将改进网站。
The company will implement improvements to increase efficiency. = 公司将实施改进以提高效率。


虚拟语气:
It would be an improvement if we added more pictures. = 如果我们添加更多图片，将会更好。
I wish there were improvements in the quality of life in this area. = 我希望该地区的居民生活质量能够得到改善。


被动语态:
Improvements are being made to the building’s security system. = 正在改进大楼的安全系统。
Significant improvements were made to the process to reduce errors. = 对该流程进行了重大改进以减少错误。


疑问句:
What improvements can we make to this design? = 我们能对这个设计做哪些改进？
Are there any improvements you would suggest? = 你有什么改进建议吗？


否定句:
There were no improvements in his condition. = 他的病情没有好转。
They made no improvements to the outdated system. = 他们没有对过时的系统进行任何改进。


PHRASE->
make improvements = 做出改进
show improvement = 显示改进
significant improvement = 重大改进
marginal improvement = 微小改进
continuous improvement = 持续改进
