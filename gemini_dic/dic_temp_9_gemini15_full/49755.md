demolishable: /dɪˈmɒlɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 可拆除的，可摧毁的 | Capable of being demolished; able to be destroyed or pulled down.  | The old building is demolishable.  旧建筑物是可以拆除的。  The dam is considered demolishable due to safety concerns.  由于安全问题，该水坝被认为是可以拆除的。|  destructible, removable | indestructible, unbreakable | 形容词

The old house is demolishable. = 这栋旧房子是可以拆除的。
That structure is demolishable under current zoning laws. = 根据现行的分区条例，那座建筑是可以拆除的。

过去完成时:

The authorities had found the bridge demolishable long before the collapse. = 当局早在桥梁坍塌之前很久就发现它可以拆除了。

词源:
From *demolish* + *-able*.  "Demolish" itself comes from Old French *demolir*, from Latin *dē-*, intensive prefix + *molīre* "to grind, to crush."
