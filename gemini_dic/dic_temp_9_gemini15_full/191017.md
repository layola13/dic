scammers:/ˈskæmərz/| n.|骗子，诈骗犯|People who try to trick others out of their money or other valuables.  They use deceptive or fraudulent means to achieve their goals.|例句：The police arrested several scammers who had been operating a sophisticated online fraud.  警方逮捕了几名从事复杂网络诈骗的骗子。|例句：Beware of scammers who promise unrealistic returns on investments.  小心那些承诺投资有不切实际回报的骗子。|近义词：fraudsters, con artists, swindlers|反义词：honest people, philanthropists
