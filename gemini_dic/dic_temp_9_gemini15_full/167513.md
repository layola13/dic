previous:/ˈpriːviəs/| adj.|先前的，以前的；在前的| preceding in time or order; former.  The previous owner of the house made many improvements. 这栋房子的前任房主做了很多改进。|近义词：prior, former, earlier, preceding|反义词：subsequent, following, later


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The previous owner lived here for ten years. = 前任房主住在这里十年了。
This previous experience is valuable for my new job. = 这段之前的经历对我新工作很有价值。

一般过去时:
The previous meeting was very productive. = 上一次会议非常富有成效。

现在完成时:
I have read all his previous books. = 我读过他以前的所有书。
Have you considered your previous suggestions? = 你考虑过你之前的建议吗？
