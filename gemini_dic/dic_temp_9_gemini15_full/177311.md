reassure:/ˌriːəˈʃʊr/ | vt. |使安心；使放心；再保证 |To restore confidence or courage to someone; to make someone feel less worried.  |例句：He reassured her that everything would be alright. 他向她保证一切都会好起来的。 |例句：The doctor's words reassured the anxious patient. 医生的話讓焦慮的病人安心了。|近义词：comfort, console, encourage |反义词：alarm, frighten, unsettle |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reassures his clients daily. = 他每天都让他的客户安心。
She reassures me with her calm demeanor. = 她平静的举止让我安心。

一般过去时:
The teacher reassured the students after the test. = 考试后老师让学生们安心。
He reassured her that he would be there for her. = 他向她保证他会一直陪伴她。


现在完成时:
She has reassured me several times. = 她已经多次让我安心了。
The government has reassured the public about the safety of the food supply. = 政府已经向公众保证食品供应安全。


过去完成时:
He had reassured her before she left. = 她离开前他已经让她安心了。
They had reassured the investors before the market crash. = 市场崩盘前他们已经让投资者安心了。


将来时:
I will reassure you that everything is under control. = 我会让你放心，一切都在掌控之中。
She will reassure him once she arrives. = 她到达后会让他安心。


虚拟语气:
If I could reassure her, I would. = 如果我能让她安心，我会的。
I wish I could reassure him about his future. = 我希望我能让他对未来安心。


被动语态:
The public needs to be reassured about the safety of the new technology. = 公众需要对这项新技术的安全性放心。
The investors were reassured by the company's strong financial performance. = 公司强劲的财务业绩让投资者安心了。


疑问句:
Can you reassure me that this is safe? = 你能让我相信这是安全的嗎？
Will anything reassure him about his job security? = 有什么能让他对工作保障安心吗？


否定句:
Nothing could reassure her about the situation. = 没有任何东西能让她对这种情况安心。
He couldn't reassure her despite his best efforts. = 尽管他尽了最大努力，但他还是无法让她安心。



PHRASE->
reassure oneself = 自我安慰
reassure somebody about something = 就某事使某人安心
reassure somebody that... = 向某人保证…
reassure somebody of something = 使某人确信某事
feel reassured = 感到安心
