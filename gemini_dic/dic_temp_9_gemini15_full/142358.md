orthopaedics:/ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪks/| n. |骨科；矫形外科|The branch of surgery concerned with the correction of deformities and disorders of the musculoskeletal system.  It deals with injuries and diseases of the bones, joints, ligaments, muscles, and tendons.|例句：He's undergoing orthopaedic surgery to repair a torn ligament. 他正在进行骨科手术来修复撕裂的韧带。


例句：My daughter is under the care of an orthopaedic specialist for her scoliosis. 我的女儿正在接受骨科专家的治疗，治疗她的脊柱侧弯。
