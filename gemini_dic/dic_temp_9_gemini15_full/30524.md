cephalaspis:/sɛfˈæləspɪs/| n. |头甲鱼|An extinct genus of agnathous (jawless) fish from the Silurian and Devonian periods, characterized by a bony head shield.  Cephalaspis were bottom-dwelling, filter-feeding creatures.
