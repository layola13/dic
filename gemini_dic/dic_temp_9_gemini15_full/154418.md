permissively:/pərˈmɪsɪvli/| adv.| 宽容地；许可地；纵容地 | In a permissive manner; characterized by or given to leniency.  It describes actions or attitudes that allow or tolerate behavior that may be considered undesirable or inappropriate. |例句：The parents raised their children permissively, allowing them considerable freedom.  父母以宽容的方式抚养孩子，允许他们相当大的自由。

例句：The teacher permissively allowed students to use their phones during class.老师宽容地允许学生在课堂上使用手机。


近义词：tolerantly, indulgently, leniently

反义词：strictly, severely, rigorously

词性：副词
