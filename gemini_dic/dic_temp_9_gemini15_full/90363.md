hoveringly:/ˈhʌvərɪŋli/| adv.|盘旋地，犹豫地，徘徊地 |  In a hovering manner; hesitantly; uncertainly.  The meaning emphasizes a state of being suspended or uncertain.  |例句：The helicopter hoveredingly circled the burning building. 直升机盘旋在燃烧的建筑物上空。

The eagle hoveredingly searched for prey.  鹰盘旋着寻找猎物。


近义词：hesitantly, uncertainly, waveringly, tentatively, irresolutely | 反义词：decisively, resolutely, confidently


词源:  Derived from the verb "hover," which ultimately comes from the Old English word "hofian," meaning "to rise and fall," or "to remain suspended in the air." The suffix "-ingly" transforms the verb into an adverb, indicating the manner of hovering.
