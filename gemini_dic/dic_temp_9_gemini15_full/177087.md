reappears:/ˌriːəˈpɪrz/| v.|再次出现，重新出现|To appear again after a period of time.|The lost kitten reappeared after three days.  丢失的小猫三天后又出现了。|The sun reappeared after the storm.暴风雨过后，太阳又出现了。|appears again, reappears, shows up again|disappears, vanishes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reappears every year at this time. 他每年这个时候都会再次出现。
The problem reappears whenever it rains. 每当下雨的时候，这个问题就会再次出现。


一般过去时:
The actor reappeared on stage after a long absence.  演员在长时间缺席后再次登台。
The bird reappeared in the garden after the cat left. 猫走了之后，鸟又出现在花园里了。


现在完成时:
The symptoms have reappeared after a period of remission.  症状在缓解期后又复发了。
He has reappeared in the public eye after a long scandal.  在经历了一场长时间的丑闻后，他又重新出现在公众视野中。


过去完成时:
The pain had reappeared before he even reached the hospital.  在他到达医院之前，疼痛就又复发了。
She had reappeared in my life just when I thought I was over her.  就在我以为我已经忘记她的时候，她又重新出现在我的生活中。


将来时:
The comet will reappear in the night sky next year.  这颗彗星明年将会再次出现在夜空中。
The band will reappear on tour next spring. 乐队将在明年春天再次巡演。


虚拟语气:
If he reappeared, I don't know what I would do. 如果他再次出现，我不知道我会怎么做。
I wish the old feeling would reappear. 我希望旧日的感觉能够再次出现。


被动语态:
The lost dog was reappeared in the neighborhood. 走失的狗在附近再次出现。 (This is less common and may sound unnatural;  a more natural way to phrase this would be "The lost dog reappeared in the neighborhood.")
The missing painting was reappeared at an auction.  失窃的画作在拍卖会上再次出现。(This is also less common; a more natural way would be "The missing painting reappeared at an auction.")


疑问句:
Will the symptoms reappear? 症状会再次出现吗？
When will the comet reappear? 彗星什么时候会再次出现？


否定句:
The symptoms did not reappear. 症状没有再次出现。
He has not reappeared since the accident.  事故发生后，他再也没有出现过。
