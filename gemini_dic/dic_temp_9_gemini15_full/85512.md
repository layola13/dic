hastiness:/ˈheɪstinəs/| n. |轻率；匆忙；仓促 |The quality or state of being hasty; undue speed or hurry; rashness.  It often implies a lack of careful consideration or planning, potentially leading to mistakes or negative consequences. |例句：His hastiness in signing the contract led to unforeseen problems.  他的匆忙签署合同导致了无法预料的问题。

例句：The hastiness of his decision resulted in a significant loss. 他草率的决定导致了巨大的损失。


近义词：rashness, precipitateness, impulsiveness, hurry, speed


反义词：deliberation, carefulness, slowness, patience


词性：名词
