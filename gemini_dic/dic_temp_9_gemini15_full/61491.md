embroidered:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərd/| adj.| 刺绣的；绣花的 | Adorned with needlework.  The fabric is richly embroidered with gold thread. 这块布料用金线绣满了图案。|近义词：stitched, embellished, ornamented |反义词：plain, unembroidered


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The artist embroiders intricate patterns. = 艺术家绣着复杂的图案。
She embroiders a beautiful flower on the dress. = 她在一件连衣裙上绣了一朵美丽的鲜花。


一般过去时:
My grandmother embroidered this tablecloth years ago. = 我奶奶多年前绣了这块桌布。
He embroidered his name onto the shirt. = 他把他的名字绣在了衬衫上。


现在完成时:
She has embroidered several tapestries this year. = 她今年已经绣了几幅挂毯。
They have embroidered a message onto the quilt. = 他们已经把一条信息绣在了被子上。


过去完成时:
