kindness:/ˈkaɪndnəs/ | n. | 仁慈；善良；好意 | The act of being kind or showing consideration for others; benevolence; gentleness.  It encompasses actions, words, and thoughts that express care, compassion, and empathy. |例句：Her kindness towards the homeless man was evident in her actions.  她的善良在她的行动中显而易见。

例句：He showed great kindness by donating a large sum of money to charity. 他向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱，表现出极大的善意。


近义词：benevolence, compassion, empathy, generosity, gentleness, charity | 反义词：cruelty, harshness, meanness, unkindness, malice


词性：名词
