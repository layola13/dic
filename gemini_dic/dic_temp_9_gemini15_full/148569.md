overtakers:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪkərz/ | n. | 超车者，赶上者 | People who overtake others, usually in vehicles.  Those who surpass others in achievement or progress. |例句：The overtakers were driving recklessly.  超车者驾驶鲁莽。

The overtakers in the tech industry are constantly innovating. 科技行业的赶超者不断创新。 |近义词：passers, surpassers |反义词：overtaken, surpassed
