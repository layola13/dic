grassland:/ˈɡræsˌlænd/| n. |草原，牧草地 |Land covered with grass, especially in a temperate climate.  Grasslands are characterized by their herbaceous vegetation, which is dominated by grasses, sedges, and forbs. They support a diverse range of wildlife, including grazing animals, predators, and insects.  |Example: The African savanna is a vast grassland teeming with wildlife.  非洲大草原是一片充满野生动物的广阔草原。

The Mongolian steppe is a beautiful grassland.蒙古大草原是一片美丽的草原。|近义词：prairie, savanna, steppe, meadow |反义词：forest, jungle, desert |名词
