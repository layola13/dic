dry-eyed: /ˌdraɪˈaɪd/ | adj. | 干眼眶的；无泪的；冷静的 |  Without tears; unemotional.  Describes someone who shows no emotion, typically sadness or distress, in their eyes. |

例句：She listened to the sad news with a dry-eyed composure.  她平静地听着这个悲伤的消息，没有流泪。

例句：He remained dry-eyed throughout the funeral service.  在整个葬礼过程中，他始终没有流泪。


近义词：unmoved, stoic, impassive, emotionless, composed

反义词：tearful, weeping, crying, emotional


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is dry-eyed during the movie. 他看电影时没有流泪。
She remains dry-eyed despite the sad news. 尽管听到悲伤的消息，她仍然没有流泪。


一般过去时:
He was dry-eyed throughout the ceremony. 他在整个仪式上都没有流泪。
She stayed dry-eyed even when her friend cried. 即使她的朋友哭了，她仍然没有流泪。


现在完成时:
He has remained dry-eyed all day. 他整天都没掉眼泪。
She has been dry-eyed since hearing the news. 从听到这个消息以来，她就一直没有流泪。


过去完成时:
He had been dry-eyed until his mother walked in. 直到他母亲走进来，他才开始流泪。
She had remained dry-eyed until that moment. 直到那一刻，她才流泪。


将来时:
He will likely remain dry-eyed during the speech. 他很可能在演讲过程中不会流泪。
She will probably be dry-eyed at the wedding. 她婚礼上可能不会哭。


虚拟语气:
If he were to see that, he would not be dry-eyed. 如果他看到那样的事情，他不会无动于衷。
If she had known, she would not have remained dry-eyed. 如果她知道，她就不会保持无动于衷。


被动语态:
His composure was described as dry-eyed.  他的镇定被形容为无动于衷。 (This usage is less common)
His reaction was seen as dry-eyed by the observers. 观察者认为他的反应是冷漠的。(This usage is less common)


疑问句:
Was he dry-eyed during the entire performance? 他在整个演出过程中都没有流泪吗？
Were they dry-eyed when they heard the news? 他们听到这个消息时没有流泪吗？


否定句:
He was not dry-eyed; he wept openly. 他并没有无动于衷；他公开地哭泣了。
She was not dry-eyed; tears streamed down her face. 她并没有无动于衷；泪水顺着她的脸颊流淌。
