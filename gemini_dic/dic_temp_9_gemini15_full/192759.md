seagrass:/ˈsiːɡræs/| n. |海草|Submerged flowering plants that grow in marine environments, forming extensive underwater meadows.  They are vital to marine ecosystems, providing habitat and food for many species.|例句：The seagrass beds are a crucial habitat for many marine animals. 海草床是许多海洋动物的重要栖息地。|例句：Divers explored the vibrant underwater world, admiring the swaying seagrass meadows. 潜水员探索了充满活力的水下世界，欣赏着摇曳的海草草甸。|近义词：marine grass, eelgrass|反义词:None.
