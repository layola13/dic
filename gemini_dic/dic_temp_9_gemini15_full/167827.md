primitivize:/ˈprɪmɪtɪvaɪz/| vt.|使原始化；使粗糙化；使简化|To make something simpler, less developed, or more basic, often losing complexity or sophistication in the process.  It can also imply a regression to a less advanced state. |例句：The government's policies seem to primitivize the education system, focusing on rote learning rather than critical thinking. 政府的政策似乎使教育体系原始化，专注于死记硬背而不是批判性思维。

例句：The design of the new software primitivized the user interface, making it less intuitive and harder to use. 新软件的设计使用户界面原始化，使其不太直观且更难使用。


近义词：simplify, simplify,  reduce,  degrade,  coarsen

反义词：sophisticate, refine,  develop,  modernize
