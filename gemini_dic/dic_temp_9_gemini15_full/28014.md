capable-of: /ˈkeɪpəbəl ʌv/ | adj. | 有能力的，能够…的 |  Having the ability or power to do something.  It describes a subject's potential to perform an action or achieve a result. |


例句：
She is capable of great things. = 她有能力做大事。

He is capable of working long hours. = 他能够长时间工作。


近义词：able, competent, proficient, qualified


反义词：incapable, unable, incompetent


词性:形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is capable of playing the piano. = 她有能力弹钢琴。
He is capable of handling this project. = 他有能力处理这个项目。

一般过去时:
She was capable of running a marathon. = 她有能力跑马拉松。
He was capable of solving complex problems. = 他有能力解决复杂的问题。

现在完成时:
She has been capable of speaking three languages since childhood. = 她从小就有能力说三种语言。
He has always been capable of exceeding expectations. = 他总是能够超越预期。


过去完成时:
She had been capable of managing a team before her promotion. = 在晋升之前，她就有能力管理一个团队。
He had already been capable of designing software before graduating. = 他在毕业前就已经有能力设计软件了。

将来时:
She will be capable of driving a car next year. = 明年她将有能力开车。
He will be capable of leading the company in the future. = 他将来有能力领导公司。

虚拟语气:
If she were capable of flying, she would travel the world. = 如果她有能力飞行，她就会环游世界。
He wished he were capable of expressing his feelings better. = 他希望自己有能力更好地表达自己的感情。

被动语态:
This task is considered capable of being completed within a week. = 这个任务被认为能够在一周内完成。
The machine is capable of being used for multiple purposes. = 这台机器能够用于多种用途。

疑问句:
Is he capable of finishing the work on time? = 他能够按时完成工作吗？
Are they capable of understanding the instructions? = 他们能够理解这些指示吗？

否定句:
She is not capable of lifting that heavy box. = 她没有能力搬动那个沉重的箱子。
He was not capable of completing the assignment on his own. = 他独自一人无法完成这项任务。


PHRASE->
be capable of = 有能力…
more than capable of =绰绰有余
fully capable of = 完全有能力
highly capable of = 高度胜任
potentially capable of = 潜在地能够
