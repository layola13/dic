/ˈsɛntɪnəl/| n.|哨兵，警卫，守护者|A sentinel is a person or thing that stands guard or keeps watch.  Sentinels are typically soldiers or guards who protect a place or person.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe something that acts as a warning or protection.|例句：The soldier stood as a sentinel, guarding the bridge.  这位士兵像哨兵一样守护着桥梁。

The ancient oak tree served as a sentinel against the harsh winds. 这棵古老的橡树像哨兵一样抵御着强烈的风。|近义词：guard, watchman, sentry, protector|反义词：attacker, invader
