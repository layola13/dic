experimenter:/ɪkˈsperɪmɛntər/ | n. |实验者，试验者 | A person who conducts experiments.  |The experimenter carefully recorded the results. 实验者仔细记录了结果。| researcher, investigator |  observer, participant


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The experimenter observes the subjects. = 实验者观察受试者。
The experimenter designs new experiments. = 实验者设计新的实验。

一般过去时:
The experimenter conducted a series of tests. = 实验者进行了一系列测试。
The experimenter analyzed the data. = 实验者分析了数据。

现在完成时:
The experimenter has completed the study. = 实验者已经完成了研究。
The experimenter has published her findings. = 实验者已经发表了她的研究结果。

过去完成时:
The experimenter had prepared the equipment before the experiment began. = 实验开始前，实验者已经准备好了设备。
The experimenter had reviewed the literature extensively. = 实验者已经广泛地查阅了文献。

将来时:
The experimenter will present the results at the conference. = 实验者将在会议上展示结果。
The experimenter will conduct further research. = 实验者将进行进一步的研究。


虚拟语气:
If the experimenter had used a different method, the results might have been different. = 如果实验者使用了不同的方法，结果可能会有所不同。
It is essential that the experimenter remain unbiased. = 实验者必须保持公正。


被动语态:
The results were carefully analyzed by the experimenter. = 结果被实验者仔细分析了。
The experiment was designed by a team of experimenters. = 实验是由一个实验团队设计的。


疑问句:
Who is the lead experimenter on this project? = 这个项目的首席实验者是谁？
What methods did the experimenter employ? = 实验者采用了什么方法？


否定句:
The experimenter did not manipulate the independent variable. = 实验者没有操纵自变量。
The experimenter did not find any significant differences between the groups. = 实验者没有发现组间有任何显著差异。
