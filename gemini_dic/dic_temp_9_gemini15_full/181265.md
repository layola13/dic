reinstitutes:/ˌriːˈɪnstɪtjuːt/| vt.|重新设立；恢复；恢复职位|To restore or establish something again; to reinstate.|The company reinstituted its employee training program after a period of budget cuts. 公司在预算削减一段时间后恢复了员工培训项目。|近义词：restore, reinstate, re-establish|反义词：abolish, eliminate, discontinue|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinstitute the old rules every year. = 他们每年都重新设立旧规章。
The school reinstitutes the after-school program. = 学校恢复了课后活动项目。


一般过去时:
The government reinstituted the tax. = 政府恢复了这项税收。
The judge reinstituted the charges against the defendant. = 法官恢复了对被告的指控。


现在完成时:
They have reinstituted the scholarship program. = 他们已经恢复了奖学金项目。
The company has reinstituted its quality control measures. = 公司已经恢复了其质量控制措施。


过去完成时:
They had reinstituted the policy before the incident occurred. = 他们在那件事发生之前就恢复了这项政策。
The committee had reinstituted the regulations before the meeting. = 委员会在会议前已经恢复了规章。


将来时:
They will reinstitute the program next year. = 他们明年将恢复这个项目。
The council will reinstitute the ban on smoking. = 理事会将恢复禁烟令。


虚拟语气:
If they reinstituted the policy, the situation would improve. = 如果他们恢复这项政策，情况就会好转。
I wish they would reinstitute the old system. = 我希望他们能恢复旧制度。


被动语态:
The old rules were reinstituted last month. = 旧规章是上个月重新设立的。
The program is being reinstituted next semester. = 该项目将在下学期重新启动。


疑问句:
Will they reinstitute the program? = 他们会恢复这个项目吗？
When will the company reinstitute its policy? = 公司什么时候会恢复其政策？


否定句:
They did not reinstitute the program. = 他们没有恢复该项目。
The school will not reinstitute the policy. = 学校不会恢复这项政策。


PHRASE->
reinstitute a policy = 恢复一项政策
reinstitute a law = 恢复一项法律
reinstitute a system = 恢复一个系统
reinstitute an order = 恢复秩序
reinstitute a tradition = 恢复一项传统

词源:
re- (again) + institute (to establish)  重新建立
