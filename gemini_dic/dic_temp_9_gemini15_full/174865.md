I was so rack-out after the marathon that I slept for twelve hours.  = 马拉松之后我累坏了，睡了十二个小时。

After three attempts, their plan completely rack-out. = 三次尝试后，他们的计划彻底失败了。


近义词：pass out, collapse,conk out, crash,  (informal)  

反义词：stay awake, remain alert, be energized
