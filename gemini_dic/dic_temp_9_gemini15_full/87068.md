heptandria:/hepˈtændriə/| n. |七雄蕊植物|A term in botany referring to flowers with seven stamens (male reproductive organs).  This term is part of the Linnaean system of classification.  It is less commonly used in modern botany, which favors more detailed and descriptive systems. |例句：The botanist carefully examined the flower, noting its heptandria structure.  植物学家仔细检查了花朵，注意到了它的七雄蕊结构。
