hippo:/ˈhɪpəʊ/ | /ˈhɪpoʊ/| n. |河马|A large, semi-aquatic mammal of Africa, having thick grayish skin, a very large head, and small eyes, ears, and nostrils.| 例句：The hippopotamus opened its massive mouth, revealing rows of enormous teeth.  河马张开了巨大的嘴巴，露出了几排巨大的牙齿。


The hippos wallowed in the muddy river.  河马在泥泞的河里打滚。


近义词：river horse|反义词：无
