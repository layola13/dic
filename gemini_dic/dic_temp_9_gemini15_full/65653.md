evaporations:/ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |蒸发（过程）；挥发|The process of a substance in a liquid state changing to a gaseous state due to an increase in temperature and/or pressure.  It often refers to the change of water from liquid to gas. |例句：The evaporations from the lake increased in the hot summer. 夏季湖泊的蒸发量增加了。|例句：  The rapid evaporations of the solvent left behind a solid residue. 溶剂快速蒸发后留下固体残留物。|近义词：vaporization, volatilization|反义词：condensation, precipitation|名词
