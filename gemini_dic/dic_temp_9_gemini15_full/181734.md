relicensure:/ˌriːˈlaɪsənʃər/| n. |执照再注册，重新获得执照|The process of renewing or regaining a license after it has lapsed or been revoked.  It often involves meeting specific requirements or demonstrating continued competency.|例句：After a five-year break, she had to undergo relicensure to practice medicine again.  五年中断后，她必须重新获得执照才能继续行医。

例句：The nurse needed to complete continuing education courses for relicensure.  护士需要完成继续教育课程才能重新获得执照。


近义词：recertification, renewal, reinstatement|反义词：license revocation, decertification|词性:名词
