overappreciatively: /ˌoʊvərəˈpriːʃieɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 过分感激地，过度欣赏地 |  Expressing appreciation excessively or to an inappropriate degree.  It implies an over-the-top or excessive display of gratitude or admiration. |


例句：

He thanked her overappreciatively for the small favor, making her uncomfortable. = 他对她的小恩小惠表示了过分的感谢，让她感到不舒服。

She overappreciatively praised the painting, ignoring its flaws. = 她对这幅画赞不绝口，忽略了它的缺陷。


近义词：excessively gratefully, excessively admiringly, effusively


反义词：underappreciatively, indifferently, apathetically


词性：副词
