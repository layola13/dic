foreseeable:/fɔːˈsiːəbl/ | adj. | 可预见的，可预料的 |  capable of being foreseen or predicted; predictable.  | The foreseeable future looks bright for the company.  未来一段时间内，公司前景一片光明。| predictable, anticipated, expected | unpredictable, unexpected, unforeseen | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The risk is foreseeable. = 风险是可以预见的。
This outcome is foreseeable given the circumstances. = 考虑到这些情况，这个结果是可以预见的。


一般过去时:
The consequences were foreseeable. = 后果是可以预见的。
The problem was foreseeable, but we ignored it. = 这个问题是可以预见的，但我们忽略了它。


现在完成时:
The problems have been foreseeable for months. = 几个月来，这些问题一直是可以预见的。
Many experts have foreseen the crisis. = 许多专家都预见了这场危机。


过去完成时:
The danger had been foreseeable, but they took no precautions. = 危险是可以预见的，但他们没有采取任何预防措施。
It had been foreseeable that the project would be delayed. = 项目会被延误是可以预见的。


将来时:
The impact will be foreseeable. = 影响是可以预见的。
Any problems will be foreseeable in advance. = 任何问题都可以在提前预见。


虚拟语气:
If the risks were foreseeable, we would have taken action. = 如果风险是可以预见的，我们就会采取行动。
If the accident had been foreseeable, it could have been prevented. = 如果事故是可以预见的，它本来是可以避免的。


被动语态:
The situation was foreseen. = 局势是可以预见的。
The consequences were foreseen by many. = 后果已经被许多人预见到了。


疑问句:
Is the outcome foreseeable? = 结果是可以预见的么？
Are these problems foreseeable? = 这些问题是可以预见的么？


否定句:
The consequences were not foreseeable. = 后果是不可预见的。
The failure was not foreseeable. = 失败是不可预见的。


PHRASE->
in the foreseeable future = 在可预见的将来
for the foreseeable future = 在可预见的将来
as far as is foreseeable = 就目前所见
within the foreseeable future = 在可预见的将来
the foreseeable horizon = 可预见的未来


词源:

fore- (前缀，表示“在前，预先”) + see (动词，表示“看见，预见”) + -able (后缀，表示“可以…的”)  组合而成。  表示“可以预先看见的”。
