enjoys:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪz/ | v. |享受，喜爱 | To experience pleasure or satisfaction from something.  |例句：She enjoys reading novels. 她喜欢读小说。|例句：They enjoyed the beautiful scenery. 他们欣赏美丽的风景。|近义词：relishes, savors, appreciates |反义词：detests, abhors, loathes |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enjoys playing tennis. = 他喜欢打网球。
She enjoys her job. = 她喜欢她的工作。

一般过去时:
I enjoyed the movie last night. = 我昨晚很喜欢那部电影。
We enjoyed ourselves at the party. = 我们在派对上玩得很开心。

现在完成时:
They have enjoyed many happy years together. = 他们一起度过了许多快乐的时光。
She has enjoyed great success in her career. = 她在事业上取得了巨大的成功。

过去完成时:
He had enjoyed a long and fulfilling life. = 他过着漫长而充实的一生。
We had enjoyed a delicious meal before the concert. = 音乐会前我们享用了一顿美味的晚餐。

将来时:
I will enjoy my vacation next week. = 我下周会享受我的假期。
We will enjoy the fruits of our labor. = 我们将享受我们劳动成果。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would enjoy painting more. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更享受绘画。
I wish I enjoyed public speaking more. = 我希望我更喜欢公开演讲。

被动语态: (enjoy 很少用被动语态)
The concert was enjoyed by everyone. = 音乐会受到所有人的喜爱。(较为罕见)
The game was enjoyed by the players. =  球员们享受了这场比赛。(较为罕见)

疑问句:
Do you enjoy your work? = 你喜欢你的工作吗？
Does he enjoy spicy food? = 他喜欢吃辛辣食物吗？

否定句:
I don't enjoy cold weather. = 我不喜欢寒冷的天气。
She doesn't enjoy horror movies. = 她不喜欢恐怖电影。


PHRASE->
enjoy oneself = 玩得开心
enjoy doing sth = 喜欢做某事
enjoy the moment = 享受当下
enjoy a good reputation = 享有盛誉
enjoy life to the fullest =  充分享受生活
