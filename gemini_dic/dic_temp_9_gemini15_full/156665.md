picturesque: /ˌpɪktʃəˈrɛsk/ | adj. |风景如画的，美丽的；生动的，逼真的 |Descriptive of a place or scene that is visually attractive, especially in a quaint or charming way. It often suggests a scene that is pleasingly old-fashioned or idyllic.  |例句：The village was incredibly picturesque, with cobblestone streets and flower-filled window boxes.  这个村庄非常风景如画，鹅卵石街道和鲜花盛开的窗台花箱。

The rolling hills created a picturesque landscape.  连绵起伏的山峦构成了一幅美丽的风景画。|近义词：scenic, charming, beautiful, idyllic, quaint |反义词：ugly, unattractive, bleak, dreary
