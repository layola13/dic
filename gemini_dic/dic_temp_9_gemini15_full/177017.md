reanalysis:/ˌriːəˈnælɪsɪs/| n. |再分析，重新分析 |The process of reviewing and reinterpreting existing data or information.  It often involves applying new methods, perspectives, or data to gain a deeper understanding.  Reanalysis can be applied to various fields, including scientific research, economic modeling, and linguistic studies. |Example: The reanalysis of the geological data revealed a new fault line.  再分析的地质数据揭示了一条新的断层线。

The team conducted a thorough reanalysis of the patient's medical history.  团队对患者的病史进行了彻底的再分析。|近义词：re-examination, review, reconsideration |反义词：initial analysis, first analysis |名词
