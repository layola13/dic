flattens:/ˈflætənz/| vt.|压平；使…变平；使…衰弱|To make something flat or smoother; to reduce something in size, importance, or intensity.|例句：The steamroller flattens the road.  压路机压平了道路。|例句：The news flattened his spirits. 这个消息使他精神萎靡。|近义词：compresses, smooths, levels|反义词：raises, elevates, inflates|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flattens the clay with his hands. = 他用手把粘土压平。
The pressure flattens the cardboard box. = 压力压扁了纸板箱。

一般过去时:
She flattened the wrinkles in her shirt. = 她把衬衫上的褶皱熨平了。
The storm flattened the crops. = 暴风雨摧毁了庄稼。

将来时:
