reformers:/rɪˈfɔːrmərz/| n. |改革者，改良主义者 |People who advocate for or work towards significant changes in a system, society, or institution, usually to improve it. They often challenge the status quo and propose new methods or ideas.|Example:  The reformers fought for women's suffrage.  改革者为争取妇女选举权而斗争。|近义词：revolutionaries, innovators, progressives |反义词：reactionaries, conservatives, traditionalists
