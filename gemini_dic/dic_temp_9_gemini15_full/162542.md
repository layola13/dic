prealleged:/ˌpriːəˈlɛdʒd/| adj.|据称的，声称的，据报道的 |  Describes something that has been alleged or claimed, but not yet proven. It implies a degree of uncertainty or doubt about the truth of the allegation. |例句：The prealleged crime was a theft of company funds. 据称的犯罪行为是盗窃公司资金。 The prealleged victim identified the suspect in a police lineup. 据称的受害者在警方阵容中认出了嫌疑人。|近义词：alleged, purported, supposed |反义词：proven, confirmed, verified
