phthiriasis:/ˌθaɪriˈeɪsɪs/| n. |阴虱病|Infestation with lice, especially pubic lice.  Phthiriasis is a condition characterized by an infestation of lice, typically pubic lice (Pediculus pubis), but it can also refer to infestations with other types of lice.  Symptoms include intense itching, irritation, and the presence of lice or nits (lice eggs) on the body.  Treatment usually involves topical insecticides or other medications prescribed by a doctor.|The patient presented with phthiriasis, requiring immediate treatment for the pubic lice infestation.|近义词：lice infestation, pediculosis pubis|反义词：None specifically, but the absence of lice infestation could be considered an antonym.
