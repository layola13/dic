oversensitize: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/ | vt. | 使过度敏感；使过于敏感 | To make someone or something excessively sensitive.  |例句：The constant negative feedback oversensitized him to criticism.  他不断受到负面反馈，变得对批评过度敏感。 |近义词：hypersensitive, overreact |反义词：desensitize, toughen

词源:
The word "oversensitize" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the verb "sensitize," which means "to make sensitive."  "Sensitize" ultimately derives from the Latin word "sensus," meaning "sense" or "feeling."
