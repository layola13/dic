putrefy:/ˈpjuːtrɪfaɪ/| v.|腐烂；腐败|To decay and become foul-smelling; to cause to decay.|例句：The meat began to putrefy in the heat. 肉在高温下开始腐烂。|例句：The smell of putrefying fish filled the air. 腐烂的鱼腥味充满了空气。|近义词：rot, decay, decompose, fester|反义词：preserve, conserve
