epiphysial:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪziəl/| adj.|骨骺的；骺端的| Relating to the epiphysis, the end part of a long bone.  The epiphysial plate is the area of growing cartilage in a long bone.|例句：The epiphysial plate is responsible for the longitudinal growth of long bones. 骨骺板负责长骨的纵向生长。|例句：Fractures of the epiphysial plate can affect bone growth. 骨骺板骨折会影响骨骼生长。|近义词：epiphyseal|反义词：diaphyseal
