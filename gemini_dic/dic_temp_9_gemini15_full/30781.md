cestuy:/ˈsɛstuˌiː/| n. | 受益人；信托受益人 |  A cestuy que trust is the person for whose benefit a trust is created.  The term is archaic and largely replaced by "beneficiary".  | Example:  The cestuy que trust received the proceeds from the sale of the property.  受益人收到了房产出售的收益。 |近义词：beneficiary, trustee (in some contexts)|反义词：trustor, grantor
