nonchangeable: /ˌnɒnˈtʃeɪndʒəbəl/| adj. |不可改变的，不变的|  Immutable; incapable of being changed.  The laws of physics are considered nonchangeable.  | 近义词：unchangeable, immutable, invariable, permanent | 反义词：changeable, mutable, variable, alterable

例句：

The decision is nonchangeable; we must proceed as planned.  =  这个决定是不可改变的；我们必须按计划进行。

His commitment to the project is nonchangeable; he'll see it through. = 他对这个项目的承诺是不可改变的；他会坚持到底。
