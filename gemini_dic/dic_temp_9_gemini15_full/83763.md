hairbrained:/ˈhɛrˌbreɪnd/| adj.|异想天开的；愚蠢的；荒唐的|  The word describes a plan or idea that is foolish, impractical, and likely to fail due to a lack of thought or planning. It implies a reckless disregard for consequences.  |例句：He had a hairbrained scheme to make a million dollars by selling pet rocks.|近义词：impractical, foolish, absurd, crazy,  wild, reckless |反义词：sensible, practical, reasonable, well-thought-out
