ridings:/ˈraɪdɪŋz/| n.|选区；骑乘；（尤指英国的）郡；|English constituencies,  referring to geographical areas represented in parliament or other legislative bodies. Also refers to the act of riding a horse or other animal, or the journey undertaken on horseback.|例句：The election results showed a clear victory for the ruling party in most ridings.  选举结果显示执政党在大多数选区取得了明显的胜利。|近义词：constituencies, districts, electoral divisions|反义词：N/A
