hydrofracking: /ˈhaɪdroʊfrækɪŋ/ | n. | 水力压裂；水力压裂法 | The process of fracturing rock formations by injecting a high-pressure fluid, typically water, sand, and chemicals, to extract oil or natural gas.  | Example:  Hydrofracking is a controversial method of natural gas extraction. 水力压裂是一种备受争议的天然气开采方法。


The environmental impact of hydrofracking is a major concern. 水力压裂的环境影响是一个主要问题。| 近义词：hydraulic fracturing, fracking | 反义词：conventional drilling (传统钻井) | 名词
