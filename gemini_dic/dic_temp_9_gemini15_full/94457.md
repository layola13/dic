impolite:/ɪmˈpaɪlaɪt/| adj.|不礼貌的，粗鲁的|Lacking good manners; rude.|His impolite remarks offended many guests. 他不礼貌的言辞冒犯了许多客人。|rude, discourteous, uncivil, cheeky|polite, courteous, respectful, civil


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't conjugate like a verb.  It doesn't have tenses.  Instead, it modifies nouns.

Example Sentences:

He made an impolite comment about her dress. 他对她衣服做了不礼貌的评论。

Her impolite behavior was unacceptable. 她的不礼貌行为是不可接受的。
