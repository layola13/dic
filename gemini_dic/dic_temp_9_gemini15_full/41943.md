cosmopolitanly:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənli/ | adv. |世界性地；国际化地；四海为家般地 |In a cosmopolitan manner; with a worldly or sophisticated outlook.  It suggests a familiarity and comfort with diverse cultures and lifestyles. |例句：She lived cosmopolitanly, traveling the world and embracing different cultures. 她过着四海为家的生活，周游世界，拥抱不同的文化。

例句：The restaurant's menu was cosmopolitanly diverse, offering dishes from around the globe. 这家餐馆的菜单非常国际化，提供来自世界各地的菜肴。


近义词：internationally, globally, internationally minded, worldly


反义词：provincially, locally, insularly, narrowly
