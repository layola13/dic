refuges: /ˈrefjuːdʒɪz/ | n. | 避难所；庇护者；避难处 | Places offering safety or protection, especially for people or animals in danger.  |例句：Many refugees fled their homes seeking refuge in neighboring countries. 许多难民逃离家园，在邻国寻求庇护。

例句：The mountain cave provided refuge from the storm. 山洞为暴风雨提供了庇护。


近义词：sanctuary, shelter, asylum, haven | 反义词：danger, exposure, peril | 词性: noun
