herbivorous:/ˌɜːrˈbɪvərəs/ | adj. | 食草的；草食性的 |  Relating to or denoting animals that feed on plants.  | Example: Cows are herbivorous animals.  奶牛是食草动物。  Many herbivorous insects feed on leaves.许多食草昆虫以叶子为食。| 近义词：plant-eating, vegetarian (for animals) | 反义词：carnivorous, omnivorous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The deer are herbivorous. = 鹿是食草动物。
Rabbits are herbivorous mammals. = 兔子是食草哺乳动物。

一般过去时:
Dinosaurs were not all herbivorous. = 恐龙并非都是食草动物。
The ancient mammoths were largely herbivorous. = 古代猛犸象很大程度上是食草动物。


现在完成时:
Scientists have discovered many new herbivorous species. = 科学家们已经发现了许多新的食草物种。
Researchers have confirmed that this insect is herbivorous. = 研究人员已经证实这种昆虫是食草的。


过去完成时:
The paleontologists had determined that the fossil belonged to a herbivorous reptile. = 古生物学家已经确定该化石属于一种食草爬行动物。
Before the introduction of grazing animals, the area had been dominated by herbivorous insects. = 在引入食草动物之前，该地区一直被食草昆虫所主导。


将来时:
We will study the behavior of herbivorous animals in the Amazon rainforest. = 我们将研究亚马逊雨林中食草动物的行为。
Researchers predict that the population of herbivorous insects will increase next year. = 研究人员预测明年食草昆虫的数量将会增加。


虚拟语气:
If the animal were herbivorous, it would not have sharp teeth. = 如果这种动物是食草动物，它就不会有锋利的牙齿。
If the plants were poisonous, the herbivorous animals would likely avoid them. = 如果植物有毒，食草动物可能会避开它们。


被动语态:
Several plant species are consumed by herbivorous animals. = 几种植物物种被食草动物食用。
The impact of herbivorous insects on crops is being studied. = 食草昆虫对农作物的影响正在被研究。


疑问句:
Are all giraffes herbivorous? = 长颈鹿都是食草动物吗？
Is that a herbivorous or carnivorous animal? = 那是一种食草动物还是食肉动物？


否定句:
Not all animals are herbivorous; some are carnivorous. = 动物并非都是食草动物；有些是食肉动物。
That particular species is not herbivorous; it's omnivorous. = 那个特定物种不是食草的；它是杂食性的。
