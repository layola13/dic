nonacutely:/ˌnɒnəˈkjuːtli/| adv.|非急性地，非急剧地 |Not acutely; not sharply or intensely.  This adverb describes something that is not sudden, severe, or intense.  It implies a gradual or mild onset or progression.|例句：The illness progressed nonacutely, allowing for timely intervention.  疾病进展缓慢，这使得及时干预成为可能。

The symptoms manifested nonacutely, making diagnosis challenging.  症状出现得并不剧烈，这使得诊断变得困难。|近义词：gradually, mildly, subtly, insidiously|反义词：acutely, severely, intensely, rapidly

词性：副词
