non-productive: /ˌnɒn prəˈdʌktɪv/ | adj. | 不生产的；无效率的；无益的 | Not producing or achieving much.  Something that is non-productive does not result in a useful outcome or significant progress. It can refer to things, people, or processes. |

例句：

The meeting was completely non-productive; we didn't reach any decisions. = 会议完全没有成效；我们没有任何决定。

He spent a non-productive afternoon watching television instead of working. = 他下午无所事事地看电视，而不是工作。


近义词：unproductive, fruitless, inefficient, sterile, barren, idle, wasteful

反义词：productive, efficient, fruitful, effective, profitable

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
The project was deemed non-productive by the board of directors. = 该项目被董事会认为没有成效。
The old machinery was considered non-productive and was replaced. = 旧机器被认为效率低下，已被更换。


疑问句:
Is this approach truly non-productive, or are we just missing something? = 这种方法真的没有效率吗，还是我们只是遗漏了什么？

否定句:

The meeting wasn't entirely non-productive; we did make some progress on one issue. = 会议并非完全没有成效；我们在一个问题上取得了一些进展。
