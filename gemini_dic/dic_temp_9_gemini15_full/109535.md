lessees:/lɛˈsiːz/| n. |承租人，租户|A lessee is a person or entity that rents property from a lessor (landlord).  Lessees are bound by the terms of a lease agreement, which outlines their rights and responsibilities regarding the property.| 例句：The lessees were responsible for maintaining the property in good condition.  承租人有责任保持房产状况良好。|  The lessees failed to pay their rent on time.  承租人未能按时支付租金。|近义词：tenant, renter, occupant|反义词：lessor, landlord|名词
