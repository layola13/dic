reawakened: /ˌriːəˈweɪkənd/ | adj. | 重新唤醒的；重新被激发的 |  Awakened again; roused from inactivity or dormancy.  |例句：His reawakened interest in painting led him to enroll in art classes. 他重新燃起的绘画兴趣使他报了美术课。

例句：The reawakened city buzzed with activity after the long winter. 漫长的冬天过后，重新苏醒的城市充满了活力。


近义词：revived, renewed, reactivated, rekindled | 反义词：dormant, extinguished, suppressed | 词性：形容词
