faller:/ˈfɔːlər/| n. |摔倒者；倒塌物；伐木工人|A person or thing that falls; someone who cuts down trees.  It can also refer to something that is prone to falling or collapsing.|例句：The old building is a faller, and it's dangerous to be near it. 这座旧建筑物随时可能倒塌，靠近它很危险。

The experienced faller felled the giant redwood with ease.  经验丰富的伐木工人轻松地砍倒了巨大的红杉树。|近义词：logger, chopper, toppler|反义词：erector, builder
