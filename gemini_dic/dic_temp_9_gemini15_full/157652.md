pitting:/ˈpɪtɪŋ/| n. v.|麻点；点蚀；凹坑；（使）长麻点；（使）生凹坑|The formation of small pits or depressions on a surface.  It can refer to a defect in a material, a process of creating pits, or the pits themselves.|例句：The fruit showed signs of pitting.  这水果有麻点的迹象。

例句：The acid rain caused severe pitting on the car's paintwork. 酸雨导致汽车油漆严重点蚀。


近义词：pockmarking, pitting corrosion, erosion,  corrosion

反义词：smoothing, polishing


词性: noun, verb
