conga:/ˈkɒŋɡə/| /ˈkɑːŋɡə/ | n.  |康茄舞；康茄鼓 | A lively Cuban dance, usually performed in a line, with a rhythmic movement of the hips.  It can also refer to the drum used in this dance. | 例句：They danced the conga all night long.  他们整夜跳康茄舞。  | 例句：The band played a lively rhythm on the conga drum. 乐队用康茄鼓演奏了欢快的节奏。| 近义词：rumba, samba | 反义词：无
