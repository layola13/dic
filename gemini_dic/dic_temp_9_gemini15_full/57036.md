doubletree:/ˈdʌbltriː/| n. |双树酒店；双树(一种树)| A DoubleTree by Hilton is a brand of upscale hotels owned by Hilton Worldwide.  The name likely refers to the characteristic double-trunked trees often found in landscaping around the hotels, or to a double-crowned tree.  The word is a compound word combining "double" and "tree".| Example: We stayed at a DoubleTree hotel during our vacation. 我们在假期期间住在一间双树酒店。|近义词：Hilton hotel (a broader term)|反义词：None specifically, but a smaller, less upscale hotel could be considered an antonym in a relative sense.
