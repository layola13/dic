mound:/maʊnd/| n.|土堆；坟堆；少量；隆起|A mound is a raised mass of earth, typically artificial, such as a burial mound or an anthill. It can also refer to a small heap or pile of something.|例句：The ancient burial mound was carefully excavated. 古代的坟堆被仔细挖掘。

例句：There was a small mound of sand on the beach. 沙滩上有一小堆沙子。|近义词：hill, heap, pile|反义词：depression, hollow
