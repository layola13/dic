saddeningly:/ˈsædənɪŋli/| adv.|令人悲伤地，令人惋惜地|The news, sadly and unexpectedly, brought a wave of sadness.  The adjective "saddening" describes something that causes sadness.  The adverb "saddeningly" modifies a verb, adjective, or other adverb to indicate a manner of sadness. | Saddeningly, the team lost the championship game.令人悲伤的是，球队输掉了冠军赛。|Sadly, unfortunately, depressingly|Joyfully, happily, exhilaratingly|副词
