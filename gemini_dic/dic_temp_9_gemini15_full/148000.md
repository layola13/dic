词源:
The word "overrighteousness" is a combination of "over-" (meaning excessively or too much) and "righteousness" (the quality of being morally right or virtuous).  "Righteousness" itself has roots in Old English "rihtwisnes," from "riht" (right) + "wis" (wise).
