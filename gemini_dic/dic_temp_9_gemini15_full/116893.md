mayer:/ˈmeɪər/ | n. |市长 | A mayor is the head of a city or town's government.  Mayors are typically elected officials responsible for overseeing municipal services and policy. | Example: The mayor announced plans for a new park.  市长宣布了建造一个新公园的计划。 |近义词：burgomaster, city manager |反义词：citizen (in the sense of being governed rather than governing)
