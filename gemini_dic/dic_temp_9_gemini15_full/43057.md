cowlicks:/ˈkaʊlɪks/| n.|头发旋，乱发|A cowlick is a section of hair that stands up or lies in a different direction from the rest of the hair.  It's often caused by the direction of hair growth in the follicle.|例句：He has a stubborn cowlick that always sticks up. 他有一个顽固的头发旋总是竖起来。

例句：The little girl tried to tame her cowlicks with gel, but they remained unruly. 小女孩试图用发胶来梳理她的头发旋，但它们仍然很蓬乱。


近义词：whirl, tuft|反义词：None
