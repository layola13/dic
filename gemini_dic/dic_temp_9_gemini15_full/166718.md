/ˈpriːʃoʊɪŋ/ | n. | 预映，先行放映 | A preshowing is a showing of a film, play, or other performance before its official release or opening to the public, often for critics or a select audience.  | Example: The film received positive reviews after its preshowing to critics.  预映后，这部电影收到了评论家的好评。 | 近义词：preview, sneak peek | 反义词：final showing, official release | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The theatre offers a preshowing of the new play tonight. = 剧院今晚提供新剧的预映。
There is a preshowing of the movie for the press. = 媒体有场电影预映。


一般过去时:
We attended a preshowing of the musical last week. = 我们上周参加了这部音乐剧的预映。
The critics gave a positive response after the preshowing. = 预映后，评论家们给予了积极的回应。

过去完成时:

词源:

The word "preshowing" is a combination of the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") and the noun "showing" (meaning a presentation or exhibition).  It's a relatively modern word, formed through straightforward compounding.
