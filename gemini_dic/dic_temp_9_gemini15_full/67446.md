extirpating: /ˈɛkstɜːrpeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 根除；消灭；连根拔起 | The act of removing or destroying something completely, especially a plant or disease.  |例句：The government is committed to extirpating poverty in the region. 政府致力于根除该地区的贫困。

例句：Scientists are working to extirpate the invasive weed species. 科学家们正在努力根除这种入侵性杂草。


近义词：eradicate, eliminate, root out, destroy, abolish

反义词：cultivate, foster, encourage, promote

词性: 动词

词源:
The word "extirpate" comes from the Late Latin word "extirpare," which is a combination of the prefix "ex-" (out, from) and "stirps" (root, stock, stem).  Therefore, its literal meaning is "to root out" or "to pull up by the roots."
