cloistered: /ˈklɔɪstəd/ | adj. |与世隔绝的；隐居的；幽静的 | secluded and sheltered from the outside world; confined; monastic |

英文解析：The word "cloistered" describes a place or person that is secluded and protected from the outside world, often implying a sense of quiet and seclusion.  It frequently relates to monastic or religious settings, but can also be used more generally to describe any place or situation characterized by isolation.


例句：

The nuns lived a cloistered life, dedicated to prayer and contemplation. 修女们过着与世隔绝的生活，致力于祈祷和沉思。

He spent his days in a cloistered study, far removed from the hustle and bustle of city life. 他整天待在僻静的书房里，远离城市喧嚣。


近义词：secluded, isolated, secluded, sheltered, reclusive, monastic


反义词：exposed, open, accessible, public


词性：形容词
