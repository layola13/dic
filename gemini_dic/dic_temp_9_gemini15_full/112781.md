lubrication:/ˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |润滑；油润|The process of reducing friction between surfaces by introducing a lubricant.  Lubrication is essential for the smooth operation of machinery and reduces wear and tear.|例句：Regular lubrication of the engine is crucial for its longevity. 定期润滑发动机对其寿命至关重要。|例句：The technician ensured proper lubrication of all moving parts. 技术人员确保所有活动部件都得到适当的润滑。|近义词：oiling, greasing, lubrication|反义词：friction, dryness
