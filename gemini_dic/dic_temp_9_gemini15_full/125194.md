multichambered:/ˈmʌltiˌtʃeɪmbərd/ | adj. |多室的；多腔室的 | Having or consisting of many chambers or compartments.  This term is often used to describe anatomical structures or devices with multiple enclosed spaces.  | Example: The multichambered heart of a mammal is highly efficient.  哺乳动物的多腔室心脏非常高效。 The ancient building featured a multichambered tomb.  这座古老建筑有一个多室墓穴。 |近义词：multicompartmental, compartmentalized |反义词：single-chambered, unicameral
