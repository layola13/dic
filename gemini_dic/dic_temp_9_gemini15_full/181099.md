reinducted:/ˌriːɪnˈdʌktɪd/| v. |重新入会；重新任命；重新启用 |To induct someone again into a position or organization.  This implies a previous induction that was interrupted or ended, followed by a reinstatement.|

例句：

After a brief suspension, the professor was reinducted into the academy.  = 教授短暂停职后，被重新聘回学院。

The veteran was reinducted into the military after a period of civilian life. = 退伍军人在一段平民生活后重新入伍。


近义词：reinstate, reinstall, reappoint

反义词：remove, dismiss, discharge
