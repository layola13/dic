overrepressed:/ˌoʊvərɪˈprɛst/ | adj. |过度压抑的，抑制过度的 |  Describes a state or person where emotions, desires, or impulses are suppressed to an excessive degree, often leading to negative consequences.  This can manifest in various ways, including anxiety, depression, or psychosomatic illnesses.  It suggests a level of repression beyond what is considered healthy or adaptive. | Example:  His overrepressed anger finally erupted in a violent outburst. 他过度压抑的愤怒最终爆发成一次暴力事件。  The overrepressed emotions of her childhood contributed to her adult anxieties. 她童年时期过度压抑的情绪导致了她成年后的焦虑。 | 近义词: suppressed, inhibited, repressed, stifled | 反义词: uninhibited, unrestrained, expressive
