preparatively:/prɪˈpærətɪvli/| adv.|预备地，准备性地|In a way that shows preparation or readiness.  It emphasizes the act of preparing or the state of being prepared.|例句：He spoke preparatively, outlining his plan before beginning the task. 他预备性地发言，在开始任务之前概述了他的计划。

例句：The team worked preparatively, ensuring all equipment was in place before the experiment.  团队预先做了准备工作，确保所有设备在实验开始前都已到位。

近义词：preliminarily, in preparation, beforehand

反义词：improvisedly, spontaneously, haphazardly
