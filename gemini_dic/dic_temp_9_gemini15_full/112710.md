loxodromic:/ˌlɒksəˈdrɒmɪk/| adj.|罗德斯线的；斜航线的；|Of or pertaining to a loxodrome, or rhumb line;  following or traversing a rhumb line.  A loxodromic curve is a spiral that crosses all meridians of longitude at the same angle.  This is in contrast to a great circle, which follows the shortest distance between two points on a sphere.| The ship sailed a loxodromic course. 这艘船沿着罗德斯线航行。 The aircraft followed a loxodromic track across the ocean. 这架飞机沿着斜航线飞越海洋。|近义词：rhumb line,loxodromic curve|反义词：great circle
