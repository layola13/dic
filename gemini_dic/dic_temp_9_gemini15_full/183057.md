replunging: /rɪˈplʌndʒɪŋ/ | v. | 再投入；重新陷入 |  The act of plunging again; the state of being plunged again.  It implies a return to a previous state or situation, often one that is unpleasant or difficult. |

After a brief respite, the country replunged into civil war.  =  短暂的休战之后，这个国家再次陷入内战。

The economy, after a period of growth, is replunging into recession. = 经济在经历了一段时间的增长后，再次陷入衰退。


近义词：reimmerse, submerge again, relapse


反义词：emerge, extricate, rise


词性：动词
