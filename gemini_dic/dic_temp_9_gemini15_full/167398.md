preutilization:/ˌpriːjuːtɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |预先利用；预先使用|The act of using something before it is officially or normally available or needed.  It often implies a degree of unauthorized or premature use.|Example: The preutilization of company resources for personal gain is a serious offense.  公司资源的预先利用以谋取私利是一种严重的违规行为。|近义词:advance use, prior use, early use|反义词:postutilization, later use|名词
