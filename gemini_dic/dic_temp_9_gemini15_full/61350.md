embellishes:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪz/ | vt. | 装饰；润色；美化 | To make something more attractive by adding decorative details or features.  |  She embellished her story with exciting details. 她用激动人心的细节润色了她的故事。  | adorn, decorate, ornament | simplify, strip, plain | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She embellishes her cakes with intricate icing designs. = 她用复杂的糖霜图案装饰她的蛋糕。
He embellishes his speeches with witty anecdotes. = 他用诙谐的轶事来润色他的演讲。

一般过去时:
The artist embellished the painting with gold leaf. = 艺术家用金箔装饰了这幅画。
She embellished the truth to make her story more interesting. = 她为了使她的故事更有趣而美化了真相。

现在完成时:
They have embellished their home with antique furniture. = 他们已经用古董家具装饰了他们的家。
She has embellished her resume with skills she doesn't actually possess. = 她已经在简历上添油加醋地添加了她实际上并不具备的技能。

过去完成时:
He had embellished the report with false information before submitting it. = 他在提交报告之前已经用虚假信息润色了报告。
She had embellished her dress with sequins for the party. = 为了参加派对，她已经用亮片装饰了她的衣服。

将来时:
They will embellish the garden with colorful flowers. = 他们将用五颜六色的花朵来装饰花园。
She will embellish her presentation with relevant charts and graphs. = 她将用相关的图表来润色她的演示文稿。

虚拟语气:
If I were to embellish this cake, I would add some fresh berries. = 如果我要装饰这个蛋糕，我会添加一些新鲜浆果。
I wish I had embellished my essay with more compelling examples. = 我真希望我用更有说服力的例子来润色我的论文。

被动语态:
The story was embellished with fantastical elements. = 故事被添加了奇幻元素来润色。
The room was embellished with paintings and sculptures. = 房间被绘画和雕塑装饰得美轮美奂。

疑问句:

否定句:
He did not embellish his account of the events. = 他没有润色他对事件的描述。
She chose not to embellish the simple elegance of the dress. = 她选择不修饰这件衣服的简约优雅。
