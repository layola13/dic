privatize:/ˈpraɪvətaɪz/| vt.|私有化；将…私有化 | To transfer ownership of a company or industry from the government to private individuals or companies.  |例句：The government is planning to privatize the railway system. 政府计划将铁路系统私有化。|例句：Many state-owned enterprises have been privatized in recent years. 近年来，许多国有企业已被私有化。|近义词：denationalize, commercialize |反义词：nationalize, socialize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government privatizes many state-owned companies. = 政府将许多国有公司私有化。
They privatize their assets to increase efficiency. = 他们将资产私有化以提高效率。


一般过去时:
The company was privatized last year. = 这家公司去年被私有化了。
They privatized the water supply system. = 他们将供水系统私有化了。


现在完成时:
The government has privatized several key industries. = 政府已经将几个关键产业私有化了。
They have privatized their healthcare system.  = 他们已经将他们的医疗系统私有化了。


过去完成时:
The company had been privatized before the merger. = 这家公司在合并之前就已经被私有化了。
Before the reforms, they hadn't privatized any major industries. = 在改革之前，他们还没有私有化任何主要产业。


将来时:
The state will privatize the postal service next year. = 国家明年将把邮政服务私有化。
They will likely privatize the airline industry soon. = 他们很可能会很快将航空业私有化。


虚拟语气:
If the government privatized the energy sector, it would attract foreign investment. = 如果政府将能源部门私有化，它将吸引外国投资。
It would be better if they hadn't privatized the railways so quickly. = 如果他们没有那么快地将铁路私有化就好了。


被动语态:
The electricity grid is being privatized gradually. = 电力网正在逐步私有化。
The airport was privatized several years ago. = 机场几年前就被私有化了。


疑问句:
Will the government privatize the education system? = 政府会将教育系统私有化吗？
Has the water company been privatized yet? = 供水公司已经被私有化了吗？


否定句:
The government did not privatize the health service. = 政府没有将医疗服务私有化。
They won't privatize the national park. = 他们不会将国家公园私有化。



PHRASE->
privatize the industry = 将产业私有化
privatize the assets = 将资产私有化
privatize the services = 将服务私有化
rapid privatization = 快速私有化
partial privatization = 部分私有化


词源:
From private + -ize.  The suffix "-ize" indicates "to make or become." Thus, privatize means "to make private" or "to transfer to private ownership."
