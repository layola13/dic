grab:/ɡræb/| vt.  |抓住；夺取；攫取；赶紧抓住（机会）|to take hold of something or someone suddenly and roughly|He grabbed the phone and answered it. 他抓起电话接听了。She grabbed the opportunity to travel. 她抓住了旅行的机会。| snatch, seize, clutch | release, let go |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grabs the bag. = 他抓住那个包。
She grabs the chance. = 她抓住机会。

一般过去时:
I grabbed my keys and rushed out. = 我抓起钥匙冲了出去。
He grabbed the food before anyone else could. = 他在其他人之前抢走了食物。

现在完成时:
She has grabbed the attention of many people. = 她吸引了许多人的注意。
He has grabbed a lot of opportunities in his career. = 他在他职业生涯中抓住了许多机会。

过去完成时:
He had grabbed the winning ticket before the lottery ended. = 彩票结束前他已经抢到了中奖彩票。
They had grabbed their coats before the storm broke. = 暴风雨来临前他们已经抓起了外套。

将来时:
I will grab a bite to eat before the meeting. = 我将在会议前吃点东西。
She will grab her bag and leave. = 她将拿起她的包然后离开。

虚拟语气:
If I could grab a book, I would. = 如果我能拿到一本书，我会拿。
If I had grabbed the opportunity, I would be successful now. = 如果我当时抓住了机会，现在就会成功。

被动语态:
The bag was grabbed by the thief. = 包被小偷抢走了。
The opportunity was grabbed by her. = 机会被她抓住了。

疑问句:
Did he grab the money? = 他抢了钱吗？
Will she grab the chance? = 她会抓住机会吗？

否定句:
He didn't grab the opportunity. = 他没有抓住机会。
I didn't grab the book. = 我没有抓住那本书。


PHRASE->
grab a bite = 随便吃点东西
grab a drink = 随便喝点东西
grab some sleep = 随便睡一会儿
grab lunch = 匆忙吃午饭
grab attention = 吸引注意力
