reworked:/ˌriːˈwɜːrkt/| v. |重新制作；修改；再加工|To work on something again in order to improve it or correct errors.  It implies a significant alteration or refinement,  going beyond simple editing or correction.|例句：The architect reworked the blueprints after discovering a flaw. 建筑师在发现缺陷后重新修改了蓝图。

例句：The company reworked its marketing strategy after the product launch failed. 公司在产品发布失败后重新调整了营销策略。


近义词：revised, amended, revamped, refashioned,  remodelled


反义词：left untouched, unaltered, original

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rework the script every week. = 他们每周都修改剧本。
She reworks her designs until they are perfect. = 她不断修改设计图，直到完美为止。

一般过去时:
He reworked the paragraph to make it clearer. = 他改写了这一段话，使它更清晰。
They reworked the engine to improve its performance. = 他们重新改造了引擎以提高其性能。


现在完成时:
She has reworked the entire manuscript. = 她已经修改了整篇稿件。
They have reworked the budget several times. = 他们已经几次修改了预算。


过去完成时:
He had reworked the painting before it was displayed. = 在这幅画展出之前，他已经修改过它了。
She had reworked the presentation several times before the big meeting. = 在大型会议之前，她已经多次修改了演示文稿。


将来时:
We will rework the website next month. = 我们下个月将重新设计网站。
They will rework the contract before signing it. = 他们在签署合同之前将修改合同。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rework the report. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新修改报告。
It would be better if they reworked the plan. = 如果他们重新制定计划会更好。


被动语态:
