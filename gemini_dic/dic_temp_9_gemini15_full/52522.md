differer:/ˈdɪfərər/| n. |不同的人或事物；差异者|A person or thing that differs from others; one who differs in opinion.|例句：He is a differer from the mainstream. 他是一个与主流不同的人。|近义词：discrepancy, divergence, variant|反义词：similar, alike, identical|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The twins differ in their personalities. = 这对双胞胎性格不同。
Their opinions differ significantly. = 他们的意见大相径庭。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
His views have differed considerably over the years. = 他的观点多年来发生了很大的变化。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
