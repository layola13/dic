organoleptic:/ˌɔːrɡənəˈlɛptɪk/| adj.|感官的，感觉器官的 | Relating to or perceived by the sense organs.  The term typically refers to the five senses (taste, smell, sight, touch, and hearing) as they relate to the sensory qualities of food and drink. | Example: The organoleptic properties of the wine were exceptional; its aroma, taste, and appearance were all delightful.  葡萄酒的感官特性非常出色；它的香气、味道和外观都令人愉快。|近义词：sensory, perceptual|反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider "non-sensory" or "abstract"|
