rebuilder:/riˈbɪldər/| n. |重建者；修复者；翻新者 |A rebuilder is a person or company that rebuilds or restores something, often to a better or as-new condition.  They might rebuild engines, houses, or even relationships. |例句：The rebuilder meticulously restored the antique car to its former glory.  重建者一丝不苟地将古董车修复到以前的辉煌状态。
The skilled rebuilder managed to fix the damaged engine. 熟练的修理工设法修复了损坏的发动机。|近义词：restorer, renovator, repairer |反义词：destroyer, demolisher
