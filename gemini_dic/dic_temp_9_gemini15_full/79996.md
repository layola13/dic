go-in-the-tank:/ˌɡoʊ ɪn ðə tæŋk/| phrasal verb|彻底失败；垮掉；破产|To fail completely; to collapse; to go bankrupt.  The phrase originates from the image of a ship sinking and filling with water ("going in the tank").|例句：His business went in the tank after the recession.  经济衰退后，他的生意彻底垮了。|例句：The project went in the tank due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目彻底失败了。|近义词：fail completely, collapse, crash, bankrupt|反义词：succeed, thrive, prosper
