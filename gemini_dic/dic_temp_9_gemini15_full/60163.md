effemination:/ɪˌfɛməˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 柔弱化；女性化；娘娘腔 | The process of becoming effeminate; the adoption of feminine characteristics by a male.  This can refer to physical characteristics, behavior, or personality traits.  The term often carries negative connotations, implying weakness or lack of masculinity. | Example: The effeminization of men in some subcultures is a subject of ongoing debate.  男性在某些亚文化中女性化的现象是一个持续争论的话题。| 近义词: feminization, demasculinization | 反义词: masculinization, virility
