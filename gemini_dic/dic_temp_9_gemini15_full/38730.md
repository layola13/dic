conditioned:/kənˈdɪʃənd/ | adj. | 受条件限制的；有条件的；已训练的；习惯了的 |  Having been influenced or modified by a particular experience or set of circumstances; trained or habituated.  |例句：The experiment used conditioned rats.  这个实验使用了经过训练的老鼠。

例句：His conditioned response was to flinch at the sound. 他条件反射地听到声音就畏缩。


近义词：trained, habituated, accustomed, modified

反义词：unconditioned, natural, innate


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog is conditioned to sit on command. = 这只狗被训练成听到命令就坐下。
His behavior is conditioned by his upbringing. = 他的行为受其成长环境的影响。


一般过去时:
The athlete was conditioned for the marathon. = 这位运动员为马拉松比赛做了充分的准备。
The animal was conditioned to fear loud noises. = 这只动物被训练成害怕大声响。


现在完成时:
The child has been conditioned to obey his parents. = 这个孩子已经被训练成服从父母。
She has been conditioned to expect the worst. = 她已经被习惯于预期最坏的情况。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had been conditioned to withstand harsh conditions. = 士兵们已经习惯了忍受恶劣的环境。
He had been conditioned to believe that failure was unacceptable. = 他已经被训练成相信失败是不可接受的。


将来时:
The team will be conditioned to peak performance for the tournament. = 球队将为锦标赛做好充分的准备，达到最佳状态。
The students will be conditioned to think critically. = 学生们将被训练成批判性思维。


虚拟语气:
If the animals were conditioned properly, they would perform the task flawlessly. = 如果动物们被正确地训练，它们就能完美地完成任务。
I wish I had been conditioned to handle stress better. = 我希望我曾经被训练得更好以应对压力。


被动语态:
The students are conditioned to follow instructions. = 学生们被训练成服从指示。
The reflexes are conditioned by repeated stimuli. = 反射动作是由重复的刺激所形成的。


疑问句:
Is the equipment conditioned for use in extreme temperatures? = 设备是否适合在极端温度下使用？
Are the participants conditioned to the demands of the training program? = 参加者是否适应了训练项目的强度？


否定句:
The dog is not conditioned to strangers. = 这只狗没有被训练成适应陌生人。
He was not conditioned to handle such a large workload. = 他没有被训练成处理如此大的工作量。


PHRASE->
