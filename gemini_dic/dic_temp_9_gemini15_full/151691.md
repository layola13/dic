partizans:/ˈpɑːrtɪzənz/|/ˈpɑːrtɪzænz/| n. |党徒；游击队员；狂热支持者 |Supporters of a party, cause, or person, especially those who are enthusiastic and unwavering in their support.  Partisans can be involved in organized political movements or be fervent followers of a particular idea or individual.  In a military context, partisans refer to irregular soldiers who operate in occupied territory. |例句：The partisans fought bravely against the occupying forces.  游击队员英勇地对抗占领军。


The president's partisans rallied behind him despite the scandal. 总统的党徒们尽管发生了丑闻，仍然支持他。|近义词：supporters, adherents, advocates, guerrillas, fighters |反义词：opponents, adversaries, enemies
