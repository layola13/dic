semiconventional:/ˌsɛmɪkənˈvɛnʃənəl/| adj.| 半传统的；介于传统与现代之间的 | Partially conventional; blending traditional and modern elements.  It describes something that is neither entirely traditional nor entirely modern, but incorporates aspects of both.  |例句：The building's design is semiconventional, combining traditional materials with a modern, minimalist aesthetic. 这座建筑的设计是半传统的，将传统材料与现代极简美学相结合。

The restaurant offers a semiconventional menu, featuring classic dishes with innovative twists. 这家餐厅提供半传统的菜单，以经典菜肴为特色，并加入创新的变化。


近义词：moderately traditional, somewhat conventional, eclectic

反义词：completely traditional, entirely unconventional, radically modern

词性:形容词
