sigmodont:/ˈsɪɡmədɒnt/| n. |西格玛齿动物|A sigmodont is a rodent belonging to the subfamily Sigmodontinae, a large and diverse group of mainly South American mice and rats.  They are characterized by their distinctive molar teeth structure. |Example:  Many sigmodonts are important components of their ecosystems, serving as prey for larger animals. 许多西格玛齿动物是其生态系统的重要组成部分，作为大型动物的猎物。|近义词: cricetid (in a broader sense)|反义词: N/A
