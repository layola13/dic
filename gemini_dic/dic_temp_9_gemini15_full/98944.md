inter-chains:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪnz/| n. |互链；相互关联的链条|Inter-chains refer to the connections or linkages between different chains or sequences, often in a complex system. This term is commonly used in fields like chemistry (referring to polymer chains), computer science (referring to linked data structures), and biology (referring to protein chains).|Example: The strength of the material depends on the effective inter-chains between the polymer molecules.  这种材料的强度取决于聚合物分子之间有效的互链。|近义词：linkages, connections, interconnections|反义词：disconnections, separations|名词
