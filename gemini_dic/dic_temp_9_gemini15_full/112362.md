lory:/ˈlɔːri/ | n. |鹦鹉(一种)；彩鹦鹉|A lory is a type of parrot, typically brightly colored and found in the tropics.  They are known for their vibrant plumage and nectar-based diet.| Example: The lory perched on a branch, its feathers shimmering in the sunlight.  这只彩鹦鹉栖息在树枝上，羽毛在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：parrot, psittacine|反义词：无
