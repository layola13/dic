counteract:/ˈkaʊntərækt/| vt.|对抗，抵消，抵制|to act against something in order to reduce its force or effect |The medicine counteracts the poison. 这药能解毒。|近义词：neutralize, offset, counteract, oppose |反义词：reinforce, strengthen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They counteract the effects of the drug.  = 他们对抗药物的影响。
We counteract negative influences. = 我们对抗负面影响。


一般过去时:
The antidote counteracted the poison. = 解毒剂抵消了毒药的作用。
She counteracted his arguments effectively. = 她有效地反驳了他的论点。


现在完成时:
They have counteracted many threats to their security. = 他们已经抵消了许多对其安全的威胁。
Scientists have counteracted the spread of the disease. = 科学家们已经控制了疾病的传播。


过去完成时:
He had counteracted the initial impact of the crisis. = 他已经抵消了危机的最初影响。
She had counteracted his plans before he could act. = 在他采取行动之前，她已经挫败了他的计划。


将来时:
We will counteract any attempts to sabotage the project. = 我们将对抗任何破坏该项目的企图。
The government will counteract inflation with new policies. = 政府将用新的政策来对抗通货膨胀。


虚拟语气:
If we acted quickly, we could counteract the damage. = 如果我们迅速行动，我们就能减轻损失。
It would be difficult to counteract the effects of global warming. = 要对抗全球变暖的影响将是困难的。


被动语态:
The effects of the poison were counteracted by the antidote. = 解毒剂抵消了毒药的作用。
The negative trends were counteracted by government intervention. = 政府干预抵消了负面趋势。


疑问句:
How can we counteract the spread of misinformation? = 我们如何才能阻止虚假信息的传播？
What measures will be taken to counteract the economic downturn? = 将采取什么措施来对抗经济衰退？


否定句:
They did not counteract the effects of the storm. = 他们没有抵消风暴的影响。
The plan failed to counteract the problem. = 该计划未能解决问题。


PHRASE->
counteract the effects of = 抵消……的影响
counteract negative impacts = 抵消负面影响
counteract the spread of = 阻止……的蔓延
counteract each other = 相互抵消
counteract a trend = 逆转趋势
