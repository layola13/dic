commercialism:/ˈkəˈmɜːrʃəlɪzəm/| n. |商业主义；重商主义|The excessive emphasis on commercial values; the belief that profit should be the main goal of human activity.|例句：The rampant commercialism of modern society is destroying our cultural heritage. 现代社会的泛滥商业主义正在破坏我们的文化遗产。|近义词：materialism, mercantilism|反义词：idealism, altruism


USAGE->

Commercialism drives many decisions in modern society. = 商业主义驱动着现代社会的许多决策。
The museum fights against excessive commercialism. = 博物馆反对过度的商业主义。
