reapplications:/ˌriːæplɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 再申请；重复申请 | The act of applying again, typically for something previously denied or for which a previous application was unsuccessful.  It refers to multiple applications for the same thing. |例句：Due to the high volume of applications, reapplications will not be considered. 由于申请数量巨大，将不再考虑再次申请。|例句：The university accepted his reapplication after he improved his qualifications.  大学在他提高了资质后接受了他的再次申请。|近义词：renewals, repeated applications|反义词：withdrawals, cancellations|名词
