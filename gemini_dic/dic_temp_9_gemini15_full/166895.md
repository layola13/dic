prestandardizes: /ˌpriːˈstændərdʌɪzɪz/ | vt. |预先标准化，预先规范化 | To standardize something beforehand; to establish standards for something in advance.  |The company prestandardizes its processes to ensure efficiency and consistency. 公司预先对其流程进行标准化，以确保效率和一致性。| standardize, normalize | customize, individualize | 动词
