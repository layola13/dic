crows:/krəʊz/| n.|乌鸦|A large, black bird known for its intelligence and cawing sound.  Crows are often seen in groups called murder. |例句：Several crows perched on the telephone wire. 几只乌鸦栖息在电线上。|例句：The crows were cawing loudly in the trees. 乌鸦在树上大声地叫着。|近义词：raven, corvid|反义词：无
