oxidant:/ˈɒksɪdənt/| n.|氧化剂，氧化物|A substance that oxidizes another substance, especially by removing electrons from it or adding oxygen to it. Oxidants are often used in bleaching agents and disinfectants.|Example: Hydrogen peroxide is a powerful oxidant used in many cleaning products.|近义词：oxidizer, oxidizing agent|反义词：reductant, reducing agent
