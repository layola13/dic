fasttracker:/ˈfæstˌtrækər/| n. |快速发展者；快速晋升者；快速推进项目的人|A fast-tracker is someone who is promoted quickly within a company or organization, often because of exceptional ability or performance.  They are typically ambitious and driven individuals who consistently exceed expectations.  The term can also refer to a project or initiative that is being expedited or pushed forward at an accelerated pace.|例句：He was a fast-tracker, rising through the ranks in just five years. 他是一个快速晋升者，仅仅五年就升职加薪了。

She's known as a fast-tracker in the tech industry.  她在科技行业被称为快速发展者。


近义词：high-flyer, high-achiever, go-getter, rising star|反义词：slowpoke, underachiever
