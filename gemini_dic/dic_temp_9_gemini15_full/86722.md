helminthoid: /ˈhɛlmɪnθɔɪd/ | adj. |蠕虫状的，线虫样的 | Resembling a helminth (worm); worm-like in appearance or form.  The term often refers to a structure or organism that resembles a helminth, not necessarily implying parasitic nature. | Example:  The microscopic organism exhibited a helminthoid morphology.  显微生物表现出蠕虫状的形态。  The long, slender appendage had a distinctly helminthoid appearance.  又长又细的附属物具有明显的蠕虫状外观。 | 近义词：wormlike, vermiform, serpentine | 反义词：None specifically, but antonyms might describe contrasting shapes (e.g., spherical, cubical) | 词性: adjective
