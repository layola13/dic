frags:/fræɡz/| n.|碎片；片段；(游戏)碎片|Fragments, typically of something broken or destroyed.  The word often appears in the context of video games, referring to in-game items or resources. |例句：The explosion left only frags of the building. 爆炸只留下建筑物的碎片。

The game rewards players with frags for completing quests.  游戏奖励玩家完成任务获得碎片。|近义词：shards, pieces, bits, remnants|反义词：whole, intact
