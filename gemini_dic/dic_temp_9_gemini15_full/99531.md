intergenic: /ˌɪntərˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |基因间的；基因间隔区|  relating to the region of DNA between genes.  This region often contains regulatory sequences that control gene expression, or it may be non-coding DNA with unknown function.  | Example: Intergenic regions can be quite large, sometimes spanning many kilobases.  基因间隔区可以相当大，有时跨越数千个碱基对。 |近义词:intercistronic (if referring specifically to regions between genes in prokaryotes)|反义词:genic, intragenic
