sealed:/siːld/ | adj., v. | 密封的；盖章的；决定了的； | sealed表示封闭的，密封的，也指已决定，不可更改的状态。|The letter was sealed with wax. 这封信用蜡封上了。|The deal is sealed. 这笔交易已敲定。|近义词：closed, shut, decided, finalized |反义词：open, unsealed, undecided


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seals the envelopes every day. 他每天都封信封。
She seals the jars with lids. 她用盖子封住罐子。


一般过去时:
They sealed the contract yesterday. 他们昨天签署了合同。
He sealed the package with tape. 他用胶带封上了包裹。


现在完成时:
She has sealed all the documents. 她已经封好了所有文件。
They have sealed the fate of the company. 他们已经决定了公司的命运。


过去完成时:
He had sealed the letter before he went to the post office. 他在去邮局之前已经封好了信。
They had sealed the room before the investigation. 调查前他们已经封锁了房间。


将来时:
We will seal the agreement tomorrow. 我们明天将签署协议。
She will seal the box with a lock. 她将用锁封住箱子。


虚拟语气:
If we sealed the deal, we would have a better future. 如果我们达成协议，我们将会有更好的未来。
I wish I had sealed the package properly. 我真希望我当时把包裹封好。


被动语态:
The documents were sealed by the court. 文件由法院封存。
The room was sealed off by the police. 房间被警察封锁了。


疑问句:
Is the letter sealed? 信封好了吗？
Has the deal been sealed? 交易已经敲定了吗？


否定句:
He didn't seal the package properly. 他没有把包裹封好。
The contract wasn't sealed yet. 合同还没有签署。


PHRASE->
sealed with a kiss  一吻封缄
sealed fate 注定的命运
sealed lips 紧闭的嘴唇
sealed order 密封令
hermetically sealed 密封的


词源:
源自古英语 *seol,  意思是“密封的”，和seal(印章、海豹) 同源。
