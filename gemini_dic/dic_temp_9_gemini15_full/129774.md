non-fat: /ˌnɑːnˈfæt/ | adj. | 脱脂的，不含脂肪的 |  Describes food or dairy products that have had most or all of the fat removed.  | Example: I prefer non-fat milk in my coffee. 我喜欢在我的咖啡里加脱脂牛奶。  I bought some non-fat yogurt for breakfast. 我买了些脱脂酸奶当早餐。| 近义词: skim, fat-free | 反义词: full-fat, whole-milk
