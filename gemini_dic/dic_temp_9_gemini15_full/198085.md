shone:/ʃoʊn/| v.|照耀；闪耀|Past tense and past participle of shine.  It describes the act of emitting or reflecting light brilliantly. |例句：The sun shone brightly that day.  那天阳光灿烂。
The stars shone in the clear night sky. 清朗的夜空中星光闪耀。|近义词：beamed, glowed, glittered|反义词：dimmed, dulled

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun shines brightly today.  = 太阳今天照得很亮。
The diamond shines beautifully. = 这颗钻石闪闪发光。

一般过去时:
The sun shone brightly yesterday. = 昨天阳光灿烂。
The star shone in the dark sky. = 星星在黑暗的天空中闪耀。

现在完成时:
The sun has shone all day. = 太阳一整天都照耀着。
The diamond has shone brightly for years. = 这颗钻石多年来一直闪闪发光。

过去完成时:
The sun had shone all morning before the clouds came. = 在乌云来临之前，太阳整个上午都照耀着。
The star had shone brightly before it disappeared behind the clouds. = 在被云层遮挡之前，星星曾明亮地闪耀。

将来时:
The sun will shine tomorrow. = 明天太阳会照耀。
I hope the stars will shine tonight. = 我希望今晚星星会闪耀。

虚拟语气:
If the sun shone, we could go to the beach. = 如果太阳照耀，我们就可以去海滩。
I wish the stars shone brighter tonight. = 我希望今晚星星能更亮一些。

被动语态:
(The word "shine" is rarely used in the passive voice because it is an intransitive verb.)

疑问句:
Did the sun shine yesterday? = 昨天阳光灿烂吗？
Does the diamond shine brightly? = 这颗钻石闪闪发光吗？

否定句:
The sun didn't shine yesterday. = 昨天没有阳光。
The stars didn't shine because of the clouds. = 因为有云，星星没有闪耀。


PHRASE->
shine brightly = 闪耀
shine through = 显露；透过
shine a light on = 阐明；揭示
let your light shine = 发光发热
under the sun = 在阳光下
