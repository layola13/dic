counterfires:/ˈkaʊntərfaɪrz/| n.| 对抗性火灾；反向火烧 | A counterfire is a fire deliberately set to stop the advance of another fire.  It's often used in wildfire suppression. | Example: Firefighters set counterfires to create a firebreak and prevent the wildfire from spreading further.  消防员设置对抗性火灾以创造防火带并防止野火蔓延。 |近义词：backfire, controlled burn |反义词：wildfire, uncontrolled fire |名词
