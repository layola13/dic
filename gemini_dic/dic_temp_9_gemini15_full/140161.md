offendress: /əˈfɛndrɛs/ | n. |冒犯者，犯罪女子| A woman who has committed an offense or crime.  The word is rarely used in modern English and often considered archaic or overly formal. It emphasizes the female gender in a way that is now viewed as potentially sexist or unnecessarily specific. | Example:  The offendress was sentenced to three years in prison.  这名女罪犯被判处三年监禁。|近义词：offender, culprit, wrongdoer |反义词：victim, innocent
