radiator: /ˈreɪdieɪtər/ | n. |散热器，暖气片 | A device used to radiate heat, typically in a car or building.  It is often filled with a liquid that is heated and then circulates to distribute warmth. | Example: The car's radiator overheated and caused the engine to fail.  这辆车的散热器过热导致发动机故障。


The radiator in my apartment keeps the room warm in winter. 我的公寓里的暖气片在冬天让房间保持温暖。 |近义词：heater, cooler (in some contexts, referring to cooling radiators)|反义词：None directly, but  "insulator" could be considered an antonym in the context of heat transfer.


Etymology: From Latin *radiātor, from *radiāre "to radiate," from radius "ray, spoke of a wheel."
