gives:/ɡɪvz/| v.|给予，赠送，产生|to present (something) to someone or something; to produce or yield (something)|例句：He gives generously to charity. 他慷慨地捐赠给慈善机构。

例句：The tree gives shade in the summer. 这棵树夏天提供阴凉。


近义词：provides, offers, presents, bestows|反义词：takes, receives, withdraws


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gives me a book every year. 他每年给我一本书。
She gives blood regularly. 她定期献血。


一般过去时:
She gave him a gift for his birthday. 她送给他一件生日礼物。
They gave a speech at the conference. 他们在会议上发表了演讲。


现在完成时:
He has given me much help. 他给了我很多帮助。
She has given birth to twins. 她生了一对双胞胎。


过去完成时:
She had given him the news before he left.  在她离开之前已经告诉了他这个消息。
They had given up hope by then. 到那时他们已经放弃了希望。


将来时:
I will give you a call tomorrow. 我明天给你打电话。
She will give a presentation next week. 她下周将做一个演示。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would give it to you. 如果我有钱，我会把它给你。
I wish I could give you more information. 我希望我能给你更多信息。


被动语态:
The prize was given to the best student. 奖品颁给了最好的学生。
The information was given to the police. 信息已提供给警方。


疑问句:
What does he give to the poor? 他给穷人什么？
Does she give enough time to her children? 她给孩子足够的时间吗？


否定句:
He doesn't give up easily. 他不容易放弃。
She didn't give me an answer. 她没有给我答案。


PHRASE->
gives way:  让路，屈服
gives in:  屈服，让步
gives off: 发出（气味、热量等）
gives rise to: 引起，导致
gives out: 分发；用完；发出（声音）
