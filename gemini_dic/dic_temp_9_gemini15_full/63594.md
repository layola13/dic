envois:/ˈɒ̃vwɑːz/ | n. |  邮寄的物品；发送的邮件；派遣；特使团 |  Things sent; dispatches; a sending; a mission;  a consignment of goods.  | The envois arrived safely.  邮寄的物品安全抵达了。  The envois from the embassy were well-received.  大使馆的特使团受到了热烈的欢迎。 |  consignment, shipment, dispatch, mission |  receipt, arrival |  名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company sends many envois every day. = 公司每天发送很多邮件。
He often sends envois to his family. = 他经常给家人邮寄东西。


一般过去时:
They sent an envoy to negotiate a peace treaty. = 他们派特使去谈判和平条约。
She sent a package of gifts as an envoi. = 她寄了一包礼物。


现在完成时:
We have sent several envois this month. = 这个月我们已经寄出了几批货物。
They have received all the envois. = 他们已经收到了所有的邮件。


过去完成时:
The company had sent the envois before the deadline. = 公司在截止日期之前已经寄出了邮件。
He had already sent the envois when the problem occurred. = 问题发生时，他已经寄出了邮件。


将来时:
We will send the envois tomorrow. = 我们明天将寄出邮件。
They will send a special envoy to the conference. = 他们将派一名特使去参加会议。


虚拟语气:
If I had known earlier, I would have sent the envois sooner. = 如果我早知道，我会早点寄出邮件。
I wish I had sent the envois by express mail. = 我希望我用快递寄出了邮件。


被动语态:
The envois were sent by express mail. = 邮件是通过快递寄出的。
The envois were received yesterday. = 邮件昨天收到了。


疑问句:
When were the envois sent? = 邮件是什么时候寄出的？
Have the envois arrived yet? = 邮件到了吗？


否定句:
They did not send the envois on time. = 他们没有按时寄出邮件。
The envois were not received. = 邮件没有收到。
