magnesian:/mæɡˈniːʒən/| adj.|含镁的；镁质的|Containing magnesium or resembling magnesium.  The term often refers to minerals or rocks rich in magnesium.  |例句：The soil is magnesian, contributing to the lush vegetation. 这片土壤富含镁，有助于植物茂盛生长。|例句：This magnesian limestone is ideal for building materials.这种镁质石灰石是理想的建筑材料。|近义词：magnesiferous|反义词：无|词性:形容词
