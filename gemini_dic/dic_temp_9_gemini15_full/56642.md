donning:/ˈdɒnɪŋ/| v.|穿着，戴上|To put on (a piece of clothing) or (something such as spectacles).|例句：He was donning his coat. 他正在穿上他的外套。
例句：She donned her glasses and started reading. 她戴上眼镜开始阅读。|近义词：putting on, wearing|反义词：doffing, removing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dons his hat every morning. = 他每天早上都戴上帽子。
She dons a new dress for the party. = 她为派对穿了一件新衣服。


一般过去时:
He donned his coat before going out. = 他出门前穿上了外套。
She donned her helmet before riding the bike. = 她骑自行车前戴上了头盔。


现在完成时:
He has donned his winter coat because it is cold. = 因为他冷，所以他穿上了冬衣。
She has donned a new persona for her role in the play. = 为了在戏剧中扮演角色，她塑造了一个新形象。


过去完成时:
He had donned his uniform before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前，他已经穿上了制服。
She had donned her running shoes before the race started. = 比赛开始前，她已经穿好了跑鞋。


将来时:
He will don his graduation gown for the ceremony. = 他将在典礼上穿上毕业袍。
She will don her best outfit for the wedding. = 她将穿上她最好的衣服去参加婚礼。


虚拟语气:
If it were cold, I would don my warmest coat. = 如果天气冷，我会穿上我最暖和的外套。
I wish I had donned a warmer jacket; I'm freezing! = 我真希望我穿了件更暖和的夹克；我快冻死了！


被动语态:
The uniform was donned by the soldier before the inspection. = 在检查之前，士兵穿上了制服。
The costume was donned by the actor quickly before the scene. = 演员在场景开始前迅速穿上了戏服。


疑问句:
Is he donning his uniform? = 他正在穿制服吗？
Is she donning her mask? = 她戴着面具吗？


否定句:
He didn't don his helmet before riding the motorcycle. = 他骑摩托车前没有戴头盔。
She didn't don her glasses, so she couldn't see clearly. = 她没戴眼镜，所以看不清。
