permits:/pərˈmɪts/| v. n.|许可证；允许；许可|英文解析：To allow or authorize something.  As a noun, it refers to a document that grants permission.|例句：The building permits were finally approved. 建筑许可证终于获批了。

The government permits the import of certain goods. 政府允许进口某些商品。


近义词：allows, authorizes, licenses, sanctions|反义词：prohibits, forbids, bans


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He permits smoking only in designated areas. 他只允许在指定区域吸烟。
The school permits students to use cell phones during lunch. 学校允许学生在午餐时间使用手机。


一般过去时:
She permitted her daughter to go to the party. 她允许女儿去参加聚会。
The judge permitted the evidence to be presented. 法官允许提交证据。


现在完成时:
They have permitted the construction of a new highway. 他们已经批准了新建一条高速公路。
The authorities have permitted the demonstration to proceed. 当局已允许示威游行继续进行。


过去完成时:
He had permitted the use of his car before the accident. 事故发生前，他已允许使用他的车。
The company had permitted the release of the information. 公司已经允许发布该信息。


将来时:
The council will permit the development of the land. 理事会将允许开发这块土地。
They will permit visitors to enter the building after 10 am. 他们将在上午10点后允许游客进入大楼。


虚拟语气:
If only they permitted me to attend the conference.  如果他们允许我去参加会议就好了。
I wish they permitted dogs in the park. 我希望他们允许在公园里遛狗。


被动语态:
Smoking is not permitted in this building.  此建筑物内禁止吸烟。
The application was permitted after a thorough review. 申请经过仔细审查后获准。


疑问句:
Does the law permit this kind of behavior?  法律允许这种行为吗？
Are we permitted to leave early today? 我们今天允许提前离开吗？


否定句:
The rules do not permit the use of personal devices. 规定不允许使用个人设备。
My parents did not permit me to go out late at night. 我父母不允许我晚上很晚出去。
