evacuates:/ɪˈvækjuˌeɪts/| vt.|撤离；疏散；排空|To remove (people) from a place of danger to a safer place.  To empty or clear out (a place or thing).|例句：The authorities evacuated the city before the hurricane hit.  当局在飓风来袭前疏散了这座城市。


The firefighters evacuated the burning building. 消防员疏散了正在燃烧的建筑物。|近义词：remove, clear, empty|反义词：populate, fill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army evacuates the civilians from the war zone. 军队将平民从战区撤离。
The pump evacuates the air from the container.  泵将容器中的空气排出。


一般过去时:
They evacuated their homes due to the flood warning. 他们根据洪水预警撤离了家园。
The staff evacuated the building after the fire alarm sounded.  火警响起后，工作人员疏散了大楼。


现在完成时:
The government has evacuated thousands of people from the disaster area. 政府已将数千人从灾区疏散。
They have evacuated the contaminated area. 他们已经撤离了受污染的地区。


过去完成时:
They had evacuated the village before the volcano erupted. 在火山爆发之前，他们已经撤离了村庄。
The residents had evacuated their homes before the earthquake hit.  地震发生前，居民们已经撤离了家园。


将来时:
The authorities will evacuate the residents if the dam breaks. 如果大坝决堤，当局将疏散居民。
We will evacuate the building if there is a fire. 如果发生火灾，我们将疏散大楼。


虚拟语气:
If the warning had been given earlier, they would have evacuated sooner. 如果警告发布得更早，他们就会更早撤离。
If a fire broke out, we would evacuate immediately. 如果发生火灾，我们将立即撤离。


被动语态:
The city was evacuated in anticipation of the storm.  为了应对风暴，这座城市被疏散了。
The building is being evacuated because of a bomb threat.  由于炸弹威胁，大楼正在被疏散。


疑问句:
Have they evacuated the hospital yet? 医院已经被疏散了吗？
Will the residents be evacuated tomorrow? 居民们明天会被疏散吗？


否定句:
The people did not evacuate even though they were warned. 尽管受到了警告，人们还是没有撤离。
The company did not evacuate its employees until the fire reached the building. 直到火势蔓延到建筑物内，公司才疏散员工。
