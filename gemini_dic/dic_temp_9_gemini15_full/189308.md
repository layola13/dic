safariing:/ˌsæfəˈriːɪŋ/| v.|狩猎旅行；进行野生动物观赏|The act of going on a safari; engaging in activities associated with safaris, such as wildlife viewing, game drives, and exploring nature.  |例句：We spent two weeks safariing in Tanzania, witnessing incredible wildlife. 我们在坦桑尼亚进行了为期两周的狩猎旅行，目睹了令人难以置信的野生动物。|近义词：wildlife viewing, game viewing, exploring the wilderness|反义词：staying indoors, city life
