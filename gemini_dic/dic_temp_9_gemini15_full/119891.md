militarisms:/ˈmɪlɪtərɪzəm/| n. |军国主义；军事主义|The belief or desire of a government or people that a country should maintain a strong military capability and be prepared to use it aggressively to defend or promote national interests.  It often involves the extensive militarization of society and the prioritization of military needs over civilian ones. |Example: The rise of militarism in the country led to increased tensions with its neighbors.  这个国家的军国主义抬头导致与其邻国关系紧张加剧。|近义词：jingoism, bellicosity, warmongering |反义词：pacifism, antimilitarism |名词
