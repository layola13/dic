non-voter: /ˈnɑːnˌvoʊtər/ | n. | 不投票的人，未投票者 | A person who does not vote in an election.  | Example:  The high percentage of non-voters in this election is concerning.  这次选举中高比例的不投票者令人担忧。 The number of non-voters was surprisingly high.  不投票的人数出乎意料地高。| 近义词：abstainer (from voting), non-participant (in the election) | 反义词：voter
