hinter:/ˈhɪntər/| n. |隐蔽处，后方，障碍|A hidden or secluded place; something that obstructs or hinders; a person who is behind or in the background.|例句：The house is nestled in a quiet hinterland, far from the city's hustle and bustle. 这栋房子坐落在一个安静的偏远地区，远离城市的喧嚣。|近义词：backwoods, back country, outback|反义词：forefront, vanguard
