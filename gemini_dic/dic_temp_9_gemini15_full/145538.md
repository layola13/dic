overcommercializations:/ˌoʊvərˌkəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |过度商业化；商业化过度|The excessive promotion or sale of goods or services, often at the expense of other values.  This can lead to a decline in quality, an increase in prices, and a loss of cultural or artistic integrity. |例句：The overcommercialization of Christmas has led to a loss of its spiritual meaning. 圣诞节的过度商业化导致其精神意义的丧失。

例句：The overcommercialization of social media platforms is a growing concern. 社交媒体平台的过度商业化日益令人担忧。


近义词：commercial excess, excessive commercialism,  market saturation


反义词：non-commercialization,  decommercialization


词性:名词
