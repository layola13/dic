monkishly:/ˈmʌŋkɪʃli/| adv.|像和尚一样地；苦行地；禁欲地 |In a manner characteristic of a monk; austerely; ascetically.  The word implies a life of seclusion, simplicity, and often self-denial.|例句：He lived monkishly, devoting himself entirely to study and prayer. 他过着像和尚一样的生活，完全致力于学习和祈祷。He ate monkishly, consuming only simple fare. 他吃得像和尚一样，只吃简单的食物。|近义词：ascetically, austerely, rigorously, simply|反义词：luxuriously, indulgently, lavishly
