minoring:/ˈmaɪnərɪŋ/| v.|辅修，少量存在|To study a subject as a minor, a secondary subject of study in addition to a major.|例句：She is majoring in English and minoring in French. 她主修英语，辅修法语。|近义词：subsidiary, secondary|反义词：majoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am minoring in history. = 我辅修历史。
He minors in music. = 他辅修音乐。

一般过去时:
I minored in art history in college. = 我大学辅修艺术史。
She minored in psychology during her undergraduate studies. = 她在本科学习期间辅修心理学。

现在完成时:
I have minored in two subjects during my college years. = 我在大学期间辅修了两门科目。
She has minored in mathematics and is now pursuing a major in engineering. = 她辅修了数学，现在正在攻读工程专业。

过去完成时:
I had minored in computer science before I switched to engineering. = 我在转到工程专业之前辅修过计算机科学。
Before graduating, she had minored in both economics and political science. = 在毕业之前，她辅修了经济学和政治学。

将来时:
I will minor in biology next semester. = 下学期我将辅修生物学。
He will minor in philosophy to broaden his academic perspective. = 他将辅修哲学以拓宽他的学术视野。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would minor in creative writing. = 如果我有时间，我会辅修创意写作。
I wish I had minored in a foreign language during college. = 我希望我在大学期间辅修过一门外语。

被动语态:
That subject is not commonly minored in. = 那门课通常不被辅修。
A minor in music is often chosen by students who love music. = 音乐辅修专业通常被热爱音乐的学生选择。

疑问句:
Are you minoring in anything besides your major? = 除了你的主修专业，你还辅修什么吗？
What subject did she minor in during her college years? = 她大学期间辅修了什么科目？

否定句:
I am not minoring in any other subject. = 我没有辅修任何其他科目。
He did not minor in mathematics, he chose physics instead. = 他没有辅修数学，而是选择了物理。


PHRASE->
minor in something = 辅修某科目
a minor in music = 音乐辅修
a minor field of study = 一个次要的学习领域
minor in arts = 辅修艺术
minor subject = 辅修科目
