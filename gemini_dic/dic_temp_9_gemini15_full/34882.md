clipboard:/ˈklɪpbɔːrd/| n. |剪贴板|A temporary storage area in a computer's memory where data can be copied and pasted.|Example: I copied the text to the clipboard and then pasted it into the document. 我把文本复制到剪贴板，然后将其粘贴到文档中。|近义词：buffer, memory|反义词:None

He uses the clipboard frequently. = 他经常使用剪贴板。
