non-confidentially: /ˌnɒnˌkɒnfɪˈdɛnʃəli/ | adv. | 非机密地，公开地 |  This adverb indicates that something is not treated as confidential or secret information. It implies openness and a lack of secrecy. |例句：The information was shared non-confidentially with all employees.  信息以非机密的方式与所有员工共享。
The committee discussed the matter non-confidentially during the open session. 委员会在公开会议上公开讨论了此事。|近义词：openly, publicly, frankly |反义词：confidentially, secretly, privately |
