feedback:/ˈfiːdbæk/| n. |反馈，回应|Information about reactions to a product, a person's performance of a task, etc., which is used as a basis for improvement.|The teacher gave us feedback on our essays.老师对我们的论文进行了反馈。|近义词：response, reaction, comment |反义词：silence, inaction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The customer gives feedback regularly. = 客户定期提供反馈。
I provide feedback to my team weekly. = 我每周向我的团队提供反馈。


一般过去时:
She gave positive feedback on the presentation. = 她对演示文稿给予了积极的反馈。
They received feedback from their clients. = 他们收到了客户的反馈。


现在完成时:
We have received valuable feedback from our users. = 我们已经收到了用户宝贵的反馈。
The company has implemented changes based on customer feedback. = 公司根据客户反馈实施了更改。


过去完成时:
They had already received the feedback before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经收到了反馈。
The team had considered the feedback in their final report. = 团队在最终报告中考虑了反馈意见。


将来时:
We will gather feedback after the product launch. = 我们将在产品发布后收集反馈。
They will provide feedback on the proposal next week. = 他们下周将对提案提供反馈。


虚拟语气:
If I had received earlier feedback, I could have improved my work. = 如果我早点收到反馈，我就能改进我的工作了。
I wish I had received more constructive feedback. = 我希望我能收到更多建设性的反馈。


被动语态:
The survey results were used to provide feedback to the management. = 调查结果被用来向管理层提供反馈。
Feedback is being collected through online forms. = 反馈正在通过在线表格收集。


疑问句:
What feedback did you receive? = 你收到了什么反馈？
Have you given any feedback yet? = 你已经给出任何反馈了吗？


否定句:
The project didn't receive much feedback. = 该项目没有收到很多反馈。
They haven't provided any feedback on the new policy. = 他们没有对新政策提供任何反馈。


PHRASE->
positive feedback = 积极反馈
constructive feedback = 建设性反馈
negative feedback = 消极反馈
immediate feedback = 即时反馈
customer feedback = 客户反馈
