globalizing: /ˈɡloʊbəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 全球化；使全球化 | The process of making something worldwide in scope or application.  It often refers to the increasing interconnectedness and interdependence of countries through trade, technology, and cultural exchange. | 例句：The company is globalizing its operations by opening offices in several countries. 公司正在通过在多个国家开设办事处来使其业务全球化。


The rapid globalizing of the internet has transformed communication.  互联网的快速全球化改变了交流方式。


近义词：internationalizing, worldwide, universalizing | 反义词：localizing, regionalizing

词源:
The word "globalizing" is derived from the word "global," which comes from the word "globe," meaning "a round body."  The suffix "-izing" indicates the process of making something global.  The evolution reflects the increasing interconnectedness of the world.
