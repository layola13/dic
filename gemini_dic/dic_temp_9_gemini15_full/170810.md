pseudoemotionally:/ˌsuːdoʊɪˈmoʊʃənəli/| adj. |伪情感地，假装动情地 |Showing or expressing emotion in a way that is not genuine or sincere; superficially emotional.  |例句：He spoke pseudoemotionally about his lost love, but his eyes remained cold and distant. 他伪装动情地谈论着他失去的爱人，但是他的眼睛仍然冰冷而疏远。 |近义词：affectedly, theatrically, insincerely, artificially |反义词：genuinely, sincerely, authentically
