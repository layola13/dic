mount:/maʊnt/ /ˈmaʊnt/| n., v.|山；安装；登上；骑上|

英文解析:
As a noun, "mount" refers to a mountain, hill, or raised area of land.  As a verb, it means to climb or go up something, to attach or fix something to something else, or to put something on a support.  It can also refer to riding an animal, especially a horse.


例句:
Noun:
The hikers reached the summit of the mountain after a long climb. = 登山者经过长时间攀登后到达了山顶。

The castle was built on a high mount overlooking the valley. = 这座城堡建在一座高高的山上，俯瞰着山谷。


Verb:
They mounted the horse and rode into the sunset. = 他们骑上马，朝着夕阳的方向骑去。

The soldiers mounted a fierce attack on the enemy position. = 士兵们对敌人的阵地发起了猛烈的攻击。

We mounted the new television on the wall. = 我们把新电视安装在墙上。


近义词:
Noun: mountain, hill, peak, elevation
Verb: ascend, climb, install, fix, attach, ride


反义词:
Noun: valley, plain, lowland
Verb: dismount, descend


词性:
Noun, Verb
