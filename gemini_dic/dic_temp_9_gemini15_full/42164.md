couchy: /ˈkaʊtʃi/ | adj. |舒适的，懒散的，像沙发的|  Descriptive of something resembling or suggesting a couch; comfortable, relaxed, or indolent.  It often implies a lack of energy or motivation, and a preference for relaxation. |例句：The couchy afternoon was spent reading and napping.  舒适的下午时光在阅读和午睡中度过。  The couchy atmosphere of the hotel encouraged guests to relax.|近义词：comfortable, relaxed, lazy, languid, slothful |反义词：energetic, active, dynamic
