learnable:/ˈlɜːrnəbl/| adj. | 可学习的，易学的 | Capable of being learned or acquired.  Something that is learnable can be learned or mastered through study or practice. | Example:  The software has a learnable interface.  这个软件有一个易于学习的界面。 The task, though challenging, is still learnable with persistence.  这项任务虽然具有挑战性，但只要坚持不懈，仍然是可以学会的。 |近义词：teachable, comprehensible, understandable |反义词：unlearnable, incomprehensible, inscrutable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This new language is learnable with dedication. =  这种新的语言只要有恒心就能学会。
The concept is learnable through repeated practice. =  这个概念可以通过反复练习来掌握。

一般过去时:
That skill was learnable in a short time. = 那项技能在短时间内是可以学会的。

虚拟语气:
