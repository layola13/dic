digraphs:/ˈdaɪɡræfs/| n. |双字母组合，双元音|A digraph is a combination of two letters that represent one sound.  Digraphs are common in English spelling and can involve consonants or vowels. For example, 'sh' in 'ship' and 'oo' in 'moon' are digraphs.|The word "school" contains the digraph "oo".|近义词：double letter combination|反义词: single letter
