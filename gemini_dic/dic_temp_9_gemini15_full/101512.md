iritis:/aɪˈraɪtɪs/| n. |虹膜炎|Inflammation of the iris, the colored part of the eye.  Symptoms can include pain, redness, blurred vision, and sensitivity to light. It can be caused by various factors, including infections, autoimmune diseases, and injuries.|The doctor diagnosed her with iritis and prescribed eye drops.|近义词：uveitis (if involving other uveal structures)|反义词：无
