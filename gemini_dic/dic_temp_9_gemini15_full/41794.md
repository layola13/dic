corsets:/ˈkɔːrsɛts/| n. |紧身胸衣 |A corset is a garment worn to shape the body, typically worn by women to cinch the waist and create a more curvaceous figure.  Historically, corsets were often tightly laced, resulting in a very small waist. Modern corsets can be more comfortable and less restrictive. |Example: She wore a beautiful Victorian corset under her dress. 她穿着一件美丽的维多利亚时代紧身胸衣在裙子底下。  The actress wore a corset to achieve the desired silhouette for her role.  这位女演员穿了紧身胸衣来达到她角色所需的轮廓。|近义词：stays, waist cincher |反义词：loose clothing
