sawhorse:/ˈsɔːhɔːrs/| n.|木马，马凳|A sawhorse is a simple, portable, usually A-frame structure made of wood or metal, used to support materials during sawing or other tasks.  It typically has two legs splayed outward for stability and a horizontal top. |例句：The carpenter placed the lumber on the sawhorse before cutting it.  木匠在锯木头之前把木料放在马凳上。The artist used a sawhorse to hold her canvas while painting.艺术家用马凳支起画布作画。|近义词：trestle, sawbuck|反义词：无
