incompliance:/ɪn.kəmˈplaɪ.əns/| n. |不遵守，违反 |The state of not complying with a rule, law, or order.  It often refers to a failure to meet a specific requirement or standard. |例句：His incompliance with safety regulations led to a serious accident.  他的不遵守安全条例导致了一起严重事故。
The company faces fines for its incompliance with environmental laws. 这家公司因违反环境法规而面临罚款。|近义词：noncompliance, violation, breach, infringement |反义词：compliance, obedience, conformity |名词
