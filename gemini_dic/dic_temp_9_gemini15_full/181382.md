reinvoke:/ˌriːɪnˈvoʊk/ | vt. |重新启用；再次援引；重新调用 | To invoke or call upon again; to put something back into effect.  |例句：The court reinvoked the earlier ruling. 法院再次援引了之前的裁决。

例句：The system administrator reinvoked the backup process after the server crashed. 系统管理员在服务器崩溃后重新启动了备份进程。


近义词：reactivate, reinstate, resume | 反义词：revoke, abolish, cancel | 词性：动词
