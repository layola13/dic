premuddles:/ˈprɛmʌdəlz/| v.|使混乱；使困惑；弄糟 |To make something or someone confused or muddled.  It suggests a more thorough and less easily reversible state of confusion than simply "to muddle".|例句：The new regulations completely premuddled the accounting system. 新规定完全搞乱了会计系统。

The constant interruptions premuddled my concentration.  不断的打断扰乱了我的注意力。|近义词：confuse, bewilder, muddle, jumble |反义词：clarify, organize, simplify |动词
