shrewish:/ˈʃruːɪʃ/| adj.|泼辣的； сварливая; 尖刻的|Characterized by or given to nagging; ill-tempered; scolding.  A shrewish woman is often perceived as being constantly critical, irritable, and argumentative.  The term often implies a persistent and unpleasant nature, not just occasional bad temper.|例句：Her shrewish remarks made everyone uncomfortable.  她的尖刻话语让每个人都感到不舒服。

例句：The shrewish old woman constantly complained about everything.  那个泼辣的老妇人总是抱怨一切。


近义词： сварливая,  cantankerous,  ill-tempered,  testy,  vindictive,  choleric,  irascible

反义词： amiable,  good-natured,  sweet-tempered,  pleasant,  agreeable


词源：源自古英语 *scrēawa  " shrew, shrew-mouse,"  a small, aggressive mammal. The meaning shifted to describe a woman with similar qualities.
