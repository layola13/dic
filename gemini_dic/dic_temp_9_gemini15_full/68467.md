familiarizers:/fəˈmɪljəraɪzərz/ | n. |熟悉者；使熟悉的事物 |Things or people that make something more familiar or easier to understand.  They help to bridge the gap between the unfamiliar and the known.  |例句：The museum's interactive displays served as excellent familiarizers for children learning about ancient Egypt.  博物馆的互动展示为学习古埃及的孩子们提供了极好的熟悉工具。  |近义词：introducers, guides, aids |反义词：obstacles, barriers, confounders |名词
