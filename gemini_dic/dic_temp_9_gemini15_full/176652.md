reach:/riːtʃ/| vt. n. |到达；够到；达成；延伸；影响|To arrive at a place, especially after traveling a long distance; to extend to a particular place or point; to achieve something after trying for a long time; to communicate with someone.|例句：We reached the summit at noon. 我们中午到达了山顶。|例句：The tree branches reach over the fence. 树枝伸到篱笆上面。|近义词：arrive, attain, achieve, extend, contact|反义词：miss, fail, depart


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I reach my office by 8 am every day. = 我每天早上8点到达办公室。
They reach a consensus after a long debate. = 经过长时间的辩论后，他们达成了一致。


一般过去时:
He reached the top of the mountain yesterday. = 他昨天到达了山顶。
She reached out to her friend for help. = 她向朋友寻求帮助。


现在完成时:
We have reached a new understanding. = 我们达成了新的共识。
They have reached the age of retirement. = 他们已达到退休年龄。


过去完成时:
The train had reached the station before the accident happened. = 事故发生前火车已到达车站。
She had reached her breaking point. = 她已经到了崩溃的边缘。


将来时:
We will reach our destination soon. = 我们很快就会到达目的地。
They will reach a decision by the end of the week. = 他们将在本周末做出决定。


虚拟语气:
If I could reach him, I would ask for his advice. = 如果我能联系到他，我会征求他的意见。
I wish I could reach the top shelf. = 我希望我能够到顶层架子。


被动语态:
The agreement was reached after several meetings. = 协议在几次会议后达成。
The mountain peak can be reached only by experienced climbers. = 只有经验丰富的登山者才能到达山峰。


疑问句:
Can you reach the top shelf? = 你够得到顶层架子吗？
Have they reached a conclusion yet? = 他们得出结论了吗？


否定句:
I couldn't reach him by phone. = 我无法通过电话联系到他。
They didn't reach their goal. = 他们没有达到目标。


PHRASE->
within reach = 在够得着的地方
out of reach = 够不着
reach out to = 联系；向…伸出援手
reach an agreement = 达成协议
reach a compromise = 达成妥协
