digested:/daɪˈdʒɛstɪd/| adj. |消化的；已消化的；领会的| The food has been digested. 食物已经被消化了。  The information was digested slowly. 信息被慢慢消化理解了。|近义词：absorbed, assimilated, comprehended|反义词：undigested, raw


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The body digests food efficiently. = 身体有效地消化食物。
He digests information quickly. = 他迅速消化信息。

一般过去时:
I digested the entire book in a week. = 我一周内消化了整本书。
She digested the news with difficulty. = 她费力地消化了这个消息。

现在完成时:
I have digested all the chapters. = 我已经消化了所有章节。
She has digested the implications of the report. = 她已经消化了报告的含义。

过去完成时:
He had digested the main points before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经消化了要点。
They had digested the evidence carefully. = 他们仔细地消化了证据。

将来时:
I will digest this information carefully before making a decision. = 我会在做决定之前仔细消化这些信息。
We will digest the feedback and improve our service. = 我们将消化反馈意见并改进我们的服务。

虚拟语气:
If I had digested the instructions more carefully, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我更仔细地阅读说明，我就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had digested the information before the presentation. = 我希望我在演示文稿之前已经消化了这些信息。

被动语态:
The food is easily digested. = 食物容易消化。
The information was quickly digested by the audience. = 观众很快消化了信息。

疑问句:
Has the food been digested properly? = 食物消化得好吗？
Is the information easily digested? = 信息容易理解吗？

否定句:
The food wasn't digested properly. = 食物没有被充分消化。
He hasn't digested the implications yet. = 他还没有消化其含义。
