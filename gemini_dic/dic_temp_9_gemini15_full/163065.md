一般现在时:
Preclusion of certain rights is a common issue in many legal systems.  许多法律体系中，某些权利的排除是一个常见问题。
The policy preludes any further discussion on the matter.  这项政策排除了对此事进一步讨论的可能性。


一般过去时:
The court's decision precluded any further legal action. 法院的裁决排除了任何进一步的法律行动。
His past convictions precluded him from holding public office.  他过去的定罪使他无法担任公职。


现在完成时:
Numerous factors have precluded the project from being completed on time.  许多因素导致该项目未能按时完成。
The severe weather has precluded travel to the affected areas.  恶劣的天气阻碍了前往受灾地区的旅行。


过去完成时:
The lack of funding had precluded them from starting the research.  资金不足已使他们无法开始研究。
His prior commitments had precluded him from attending the meeting. 他之前的承诺使他无法参加会议。


将来时:
The new regulations will preclude the use of certain chemicals. 新的法规将禁止使用某些化学品。
A lack of resources will likely preclude the project from moving forward. 资源匮乏可能会阻止项目推进。


虚拟语气:
If the law had not precluded it, we would have tried a different approach. 如果法律没有禁止，我们会尝试不同的方法。
I wish the circumstances had not precluded my participation. 我真希望情况没有阻止我的参与。


被动语态:
Further investigation was precluded by the lack of evidence. 由于缺乏证据，进一步调查被阻止了。
His application was precluded due to his criminal record. 由于他的犯罪记录，他的申请被拒绝了。


疑问句:
What preclusions are there to applying for the job? 申请这份工作有哪些障碍？
Does the contract preclude us from making changes later? 这份合同是否禁止我们以后进行修改？


否定句:
Nothing should preclude you from achieving your goals. 没有什么应该阻止你实现你的目标。
The agreement does not preclude future collaborations.  该协议并不排除未来的合作。
