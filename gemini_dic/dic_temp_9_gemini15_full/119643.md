middle-ranking: /ˈmɪdlˌræŋkɪŋ/ | adj. | 中等级别的；中层管理的 |  Describing something or someone that is in the middle of a hierarchy or ranking system, neither high nor low.  It often refers to positions or individuals within an organization. |

例句：
He held a middle-ranking position in the company. = 他在公司担任中等职位。

She is a middle-ranking officer in the army. = 她是军队中的一名中级军官。


近义词：intermediate, mid-level, average, moderate

反义词：high-ranking, top-level, low-ranking, junior


词性：形容词
