rev:/rev/| v. n.|反转；回顾；检查；修改|To reverse or invert the position, order, or direction of something.  Also, to review or revise something.|例句：The car suddenly revved its engine.  车突然加大油门。|例句：Let's rev the meeting minutes before we submit them.  让我们在提交会议记录之前检查一下。|近义词：reverse, invert, review, revise|反义词：forward, advance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revs his motorcycle engine. = 他加大摩托车油门。
They rev their engines before the race. = 比赛前他们加大油门。

一般过去时:
She revved the engine before speeding off. = 她加大油门然后迅速驶离。
He revved up the motorcycle and rode away. = 他加大摩托车的油门然后骑走了。

现在完成时:
They have revved the engine several times. = 他们已经加大过几次油门了。
She has revved up the project significantly. = 她已经大幅度地推动了这个项目。

过去完成时:
He had revved the engine before the car started. = 汽车启动前他已经加大过油门了。
They had revved up the marketing campaign prior to the launch. = 产品发布前他们已经大力宣传了。

将来时:
I will rev the engine to start the car. = 我将加大油门启动汽车。
They will rev up their efforts to meet the deadline. = 他们将加倍努力以赶上期限。

虚拟语气:
If I revved the engine harder, the car might start. = 如果我加大油门，车也许就能启动了。
I wish I had revved the engine earlier. = 我真希望我早点加大油门。

被动语态:
The engine was revved before the race. = 比赛前油门被加大了。
The project was revved up by the new manager. = 新经理推动了这个项目。

疑问句:
Did you rev the engine? = 你加大油门了吗？
Have they revved up the campaign yet? = 他们已经大力宣传了吗？

否定句:
He didn't rev the engine. = 他没有加大油门。
They haven't revved up the sales yet. = 他们还没有提高销量。


PHRASE->
rev up:  加大油门；提升；促进
rev down:  降低转速；减速
rev counter: 转速表
rev limit:  转速限制器
rev head: 狂热者 (通常指汽车爱好者)
