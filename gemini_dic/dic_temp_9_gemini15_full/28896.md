carr:/kɑːr/| n.|沼泽地，泥炭地；(尤指英格兰北部)荒野；浓密的灌木丛|A carr is a type of wetland, typically characterized by peat deposits and acidic conditions.  It often features dense vegetation, including shrubs and trees tolerant of wet, boggy conditions. The term is primarily used in northern England.|例句：The hikers carefully navigated the treacherous carr.  徒步旅行者小心翼翼地穿过险峻的沼泽地。|近义词：bog, marsh, moor, fen|反义词：desert, dry land
