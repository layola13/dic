horror:/ˈhɒrə(r)/, /ˈhɔːrər/| n.|恐怖，恐惧；令人恐惧的事物| a feeling of great fear and horror; something that causes great fear and horror.|例句：The horror of the accident haunted him for years.  事故的恐怖景象多年来一直困扰着他。|例句：The film is a horror story about vampires. 这部电影是一个关于吸血鬼的恐怖故事。|近义词：terror, fright, dread|反义词：delight, joy, pleasure|名词
