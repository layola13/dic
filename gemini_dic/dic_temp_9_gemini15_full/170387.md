provocatory: /prəˈvɒkətəri/ /prɑːˈvɑːkətɔːri/ | adj. | 挑衅的；煽动的；刺激的 |  Intended to provoke, excite, or stimulate; tending to excite anger or other strong emotions.  | 例句：His provocative remarks ignited a heated debate.  他的挑衅性言论引发了一场激烈的辩论。

例句：The artist's provocative artwork pushed the boundaries of acceptable expression.  这位艺术家的挑衅性作品突破了可接受表达的界限。


近义词：inflammatory, stimulating, irritating, challenging, agitating | 反义词：soothing, calming, conciliatory, appeasing


词源:  From  "provoke" + "-atory" (suffix forming adjectives). "Provoke" comes from Old French *provoquer, from Latin *provocāre "to call forth," from *pro- "forth" + *vocāre "to call."
