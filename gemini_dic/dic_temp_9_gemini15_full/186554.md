rideau:/ˈriːdoʊ/| n.|窗帘；幕布|A curtain, especially one for a window or doorway.  |例句：She pulled the rideau across the window to block out the sunlight. 她拉上窗帘遮挡阳光。|例句：The velvet rideau was heavy and luxurious.  天鹅绒窗帘厚重而奢华。|近义词：curtain, drape, blind|反义词：none
