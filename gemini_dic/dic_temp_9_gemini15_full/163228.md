preconceal:/ˌpriːkənˈsiːl/| vt.|预先隐藏；预先掩盖|To hide or conceal something beforehand.  It implies a deliberate act of hiding something before it is discovered or becomes apparent.|例句：He preconcealed his intentions, making it difficult to guess his next move. 他预先隐藏了他的意图，让人很难猜到他的下一步行动。

例句：The magician preconcealed the rabbit in the hat before the performance. 魔术师在表演前就把兔子藏在了帽子里。


近义词：hide, conceal, secrete, mask

反义词：reveal, disclose, unveil

词性：动词
