interstitials:/ˌɪntərˈstɪʃəlz/| n. |间质；间隙；插播广告|Something that is inserted into a space or gap between other things.  Interstitial ads are short commercials that appear between sections of a video or game.|例句：The doctor examined the interstitial fluid. 医生检查了间质液。

例句：The company plans to run interstitials during the popular TV show. 这家公司计划在热门电视剧中播放插播广告。


近义词：intercalary, intermediate, intermediate substance, intercalated


反义词：peripheral, exterior


词性：名词
