seamanship:/ˈsiːmənʃɪp/| n. |航海术；水手技艺|The skill and knowledge in handling a ship or boat; the art of seafaring.  It encompasses navigation, sailing, boat handling, and maintenance, as well as the ability to work effectively as part of a crew. |例句：His years at sea had honed his seamanship to a fine art.  他多年的海上生活磨练了他的航海技术。

例句：The captain's exceptional seamanship saved the ship from disaster. 船长的精湛航海技术使船只免遭灾难。


近义词：navigation, sailing skills, nautical skill

反义词：None (Antonyms are not directly applicable as it describes a skill)

词性：Noun
