insaneness:/ɪnˈseɪnɪs/| n. |疯狂；精神错乱；荒谬 |The state of being insane; madness; extreme foolishness or unreasonableness.|例句：His actions bordered on insaneness.他的行为近乎疯狂。

例句：The insaneness of his plan was evident to everyone.他的计划的荒谬之处显而易见。


近义词：madness, lunacy, craziness, folly


反义词：sanity, reasonableness, sense


词源:源自insane(疯狂的)加上后缀-ness(名词后缀)，表示“……的状态”。insane来自拉丁语in- (否定前缀) + sanus (健全的，神智正常的)。
