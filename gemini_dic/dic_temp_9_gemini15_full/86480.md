height:/haɪt/| n. |高度，身高，海拔|The height of the building is impressive. 这座建筑物的高度令人印象深刻。|近义词：altitude, elevation |反义词：depth, lowness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The height of the mountain is 3000 meters. = 这座山的高度是3000米。
What is the height of that tree? = 那棵树有多高？


一般过去时:
The height of the water was unusually high last year. = 去年水位异常高。
He measured the height of the fence. = 他测量了栅栏的高度。


现在完成时:
The water level has reached a dangerous height. = 水位已经达到危险的高度。
She has increased her height significantly since last year. = 自去年以来，她的身高显著增加。


过去完成时:
The water had already reached its maximum height before the storm. = 在暴风雨来临之前，水位已经达到了最高点。
They had never seen a building of such height before. = 他们以前从未见过这么高的建筑物。


将来时:
The water will reach a dangerous height if it continues to rain. = 如果继续下雨，水位将达到危险的高度。
What will be the height of the new tower? = 新塔的高度将会是多少？


虚拟语气:

I wish the ceiling height were higher. = 我希望天花板更高一些。


被动语态:
The height of the building was measured accurately. = 建筑物的高度被精确测量。
The height of the plant is being monitored closely. = 植物的高度正在被密切监测。


疑问句:

否定句:

The tree's height is not as great as it seems. = 这棵树的高度并不像看起来那么高。
