gasohols:/ˈɡæsəhɔːlz/| n.|汽油醇混合物|A gasoline-alcohol fuel blend, typically containing ethanol or methanol mixed with gasoline.  Gasohol is used as a fuel in internal combustion engines, often as a way to reduce reliance on fossil fuels and potentially decrease emissions.|例句：Many gas stations now offer gasohol as an alternative fuel.|近义词：fuel blend, ethanol-gasoline blend|反义词:pure gasoline
