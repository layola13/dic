interpollinating: /ˌɪntərˈpɒləneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 交叉授粉；互相授粉 |  The act of transferring pollen between different flowers or plants of the same species, promoting genetic diversity.  This process is crucial for plant reproduction and adaptation. | Example:  Bees are important agents in interpollinating many flowering plants.  蜜蜂是许多开花植物交叉授粉的重要媒介。

|近义词: cross-pollinating | 反义词: self-pollinating | 词性: Verb
