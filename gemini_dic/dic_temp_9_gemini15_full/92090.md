hyperrealized: /ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 超现实化的，过分真实的 |  Exaggeratedly or excessively realistic; surpassing the level of ordinary reality, often to the point of being artificial or unreal.  It suggests a reality that is heightened or intensified beyond normal experience, sometimes to the point of being artificial or unbelievable. | The film's hyperrealized visuals were both stunning and unsettling. 这部电影的超现实画面既令人惊叹又令人不安。  The hyperrealized depiction of violence felt gratuitous.  暴力场景的过度真实描绘感觉多余了。 | near synonyms: surreal, over-the-top, exaggerated, intensified | antonyms: understated, realistic (in a simple sense), muted |  adj.
