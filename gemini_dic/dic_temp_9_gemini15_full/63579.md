environmentally:/ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəli/ | adj. | 环境地；从环境角度来看 | Relating to the environment; concerning the environment.  This adjective describes something that is related to or has an effect on the environment. |例句：Environmentally friendly products are becoming increasingly popular.  环保产品越来越受欢迎。

例句：The environmentally damaging effects of deforestation are well documented.  森林砍伐对环境的破坏性影响已被充分记录。
