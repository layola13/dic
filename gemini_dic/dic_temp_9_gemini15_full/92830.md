iconomachy:/ˌaɪkənˈɒməki/ | n. |圣像破坏；反对偶像崇拜|The destruction or attack of religious images.  Iconoclasm is the doctrine or practice of opposing or rejecting the use of religious images. The term often refers to the Byzantine Iconoclastic Controversy of the 8th and 9th centuries. |例句：The Iconoclastic Controversy resulted in the destruction of many religious icons.  圣像破坏争议导致许多宗教圣像被毁坏。|近义词：iconoclasm, image breaking |反义词：iconolatry, image worship |名词
