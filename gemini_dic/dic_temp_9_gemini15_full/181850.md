relocating: /ˌriːˈloʊkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 重新安置；迁移；搬迁 |  The act of moving to a new place, either oneself or something else.  It often implies a change of residence or business location. |

例句：
The company is relocating its headquarters to a new city. = 公司正在将其总部迁至一座新城市。

They are relocating to the countryside next year. = 他们明年将搬到乡下。


近义词：moving, transferring, transplanting, shifting

反义词：remaining, staying put


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are relocating our office next month. = 我们下个月要搬办公室。
They relocate employees frequently. = 他们经常调动员工。


一般过去时:
She relocated to London after graduation. = 她毕业后搬到了伦敦。
The factory relocated to a more rural area. = 工厂搬到了一个更偏远的地区。


现在完成时:
He has relocated three times in the last five years. = 他在过去五年里搬了三次家。
The company has relocated its operations overseas. = 公司已将其业务迁至海外。


过去完成时:
They had relocated before the hurricane hit. = 飓风来临之前，他们已经搬走了。
The family had relocated several times before settling down. = 这个家庭在定居之前搬了好几次家。


将来时:
We will be relocating to a larger house soon. = 我们很快就会搬到一栋更大的房子里。
The project team will relocate to the site next week. = 项目团队下周将前往现场。


虚拟语气:
If they relocated to a warmer climate, their health would improve. = 如果他们搬到气候较温暖的地方，他们的健康状况就会改善。
I wish we could relocate to a quieter neighborhood. = 我希望我们能搬到一个更安静的社区。


被动语态:
The employees were relocated to different branches. = 员工们被调到了不同的分公司。
The statue was relocated to a more prominent position. = 雕像被搬到了一个更显眼的位置。


疑问句:
Are they relocating their business to another state? = 他们要把生意搬到另一个州吗？
When will the family be relocating? = 这个家庭什么时候搬家？


否定句:
We are not relocating this year. = 我们今年不搬家。
The museum did not relocate its exhibits. = 博物馆没有迁移其展品。


PHRASE->
Relocating expenses = 搬迁费用
Relocating package = 搬迁配套
Relocating allowance = 搬迁津贴
Relocating services = 搬迁服务
Successfully relocating = 成功搬迁


词源:

re- (prefix meaning "again" or "back") + locate (verb meaning "to find the place of" or "to settle")

The prefix "re-" indicates a repetition or renewal of the action of locating, hence "relocating" signifies moving to a new location.
