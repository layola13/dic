inhumanities:/ˌɪnˌhjuːˈmænɪtiz/ | n. | 不人道行为；残暴行为 | The plural form of inhumanity, referring to multiple instances of cruel or brutal acts.  It emphasizes the collective nature of such acts, highlighting the widespread or systemic nature of the cruelty. | Example: The inhumanities inflicted upon the prisoners of war were shocking and widespread.  二战期间强加在战俘身上的暴行令人震惊且普遍存在。 |近义词:atrocities, cruelties, barbarities, outrages |反义词:humaneness, kindness, compassion, mercy |
