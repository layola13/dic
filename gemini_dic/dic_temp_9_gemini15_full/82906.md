gumbos:/ˈɡʌmboʊz/| n. |秋葵汤；浓汤|A gumbo is a thick stew or soup, typically containing okra, shellfish, and/or meat.  It's a staple of Cajun and Creole cuisine. |例句：The restaurant is famous for its seafood gumbo. 这家餐馆以海鲜秋葵汤而闻名。  |例句：My grandmother's recipe for chicken and sausage gumbo is a family favorite. 我祖母的鸡肉香肠秋葵汤食谱是家人的最爱。|近义词：stew, soup, chowder|反义词：None directly applicable.
