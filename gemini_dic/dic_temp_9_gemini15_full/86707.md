helmers:/ˈhɛlmərz/| n. |导演，舵手|This word is a plural form of "helmer," which is an informal term for a director, especially in the context of film or theater.  It refers to the person who is in charge of the creative and logistical aspects of a production.|Example: The helmers decided to reshoot the final scene.  导演们决定重拍最后一幕。|近义词：directors, film directors, stage directors|反义词:actor, actress, crew member
