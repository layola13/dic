equability:/ˌiːkwəˈbɪləti/| n. |公平；平静；均匀性 |The state of being equable; evenness of temper or mind; calmness; composure.  It refers to a steady, balanced, and unvarying state, often applied to temperament or climate. |例句：His equability under pressure was remarkable. 他在压力下的平静令人称奇。

例句：The equability of the climate made the island a popular destination.  该岛屿气候稳定，成为热门旅游目的地。

近义词：evenness, calmness, composure, steadiness, uniformity

反义词：variability, inconsistency, volatility, turbulence, unevenness

词性：名词

词源:  From Latin *aequabilis, from aequus ("equal, level, just") + -abilis ("-able").  The sense evolved from "capable of being made equal" to "even-tempered," reflecting the association of evenness with calmness and stability.
