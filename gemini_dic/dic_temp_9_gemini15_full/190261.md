sappily:/ˈsæpɪli/| adv.| 感伤地；多愁善感地；甜腻地 | In a sentimental or overly sweet manner.  It suggests excessive emotionality or sweetness, often to an irritating degree. |例句：He talked sappily about his childhood memories. 他感伤地谈论着他的童年回忆。

例句：She smiled sappily at the puppy. 她对小狗甜腻地笑了。


近义词：sentimentally, mawkishly, gushingly, mushily

反义词：dryly, cynically, realistically, unsentimentally

词性:副词
