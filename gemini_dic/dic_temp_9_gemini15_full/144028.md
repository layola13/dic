outreproduces:/ˌaʊtˈrəʊprədjuːsɪz/ | v. |超过……繁殖；繁殖数量超过 | To reproduce or produce offspring at a greater rate than.  It implies exceeding another in terms of reproductive output. | Example:  The invasive species outreproduced the native plants, leading to their decline.  入侵物种繁殖数量超过本地植物，导致本地植物数量下降。|近义词: surpass, exceed, outbreed | 反义词: underproduce, fail to reproduce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rabbits outreproduce the foxes in this area. = 兔子在这个区域的繁殖数量超过狐狸。
This plant outreproduces all others in the garden. =  这种植物的繁殖数量超过花园里所有其他的植物。


一般过去时:
The weeds outreproduced the crops last year. = 杂草去年的繁殖数量超过了庄稼。
The bacteria outreproduced the antibiotics. = 细菌的繁殖数量超过了抗生素的杀灭速度。


现在完成时:
The invasive species have outreproduced the native flora. = 入侵物种已经超过了本地植物的繁殖数量。
The pests have outreproduced the control measures. = 害虫的繁殖数量已经超过了控制措施。


过去完成时:
The rabbits had outreproduced their predators before the winter arrived. = 在冬天到来之前，兔子的繁殖数量已经超过了它们的捕食者。
The insects had outreproduced the pesticides before the farmer noticed. = 在农民注意到之前，昆虫的繁殖数量已经超过了杀虫剂的杀灭速度。


将来时:
The weeds will outreproduce the flowers if left unchecked. = 如果任其发展，杂草的繁殖数量将会超过鲜花。
The algae will outreproduce the other aquatic life in the polluted lake. = 在污染的湖泊中，藻类的繁殖数量将会超过其他水生生物。


虚拟语气:
If the rabbits outreproduced the foxes, the ecosystem would be unbalanced. = 如果兔子的繁殖数量超过狐狸，生态系统就会失衡。
If the weeds had outreproduced the crops, the harvest would have failed. = 如果杂草的繁殖数量超过了庄稼，收成就会歉收。


被动语态:
The native plants were outreproduced by the invasive species. = 本地植物的繁殖数量被入侵物种超过了。
The crops were outreproduced by the weeds in the field. = 田里的庄稼繁殖数量被杂草超过了。


疑问句:
Does the species outreproduce its competitors? = 这种物种的繁殖数量是否超过了它的竞争对手？
Will the pest outreproduce our efforts to control it? = 害虫的繁殖数量是否会超过我们控制它的努力？


否定句:
The native birds did not outreproduce the introduced species. = 本地鸟类的繁殖数量没有超过引入物种。
The controlled breeding program prevented the rabbits from outreproducing the available food supply. =  控制性繁殖计划阻止了兔子繁殖数量超过可用的食物供应。
