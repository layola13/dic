retheorizes:/ˌriːˈθiːəraɪzɪz/| vt.|重新理论化，重新解释|To reconsider and reinterpret existing theories or explanations, often in light of new evidence or perspectives.  It involves reformulating or revising existing theoretical frameworks.|

例句：The new evidence retheorizes the origins of the universe. = 新证据促使人们重新解释宇宙的起源。

The scientist retheorizes his previous findings after discovering a crucial error. = 科学家在发现一个关键错误后，重新解释了他之前的发现。


近义词：revise, reinterpret, reformulate, re-evaluate

反义词：accept, confirm, solidify


词性：动词
