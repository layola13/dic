postosseous:/ˌpoʊstoʊsiəs/ | adj. | 骨后的，骨骼后的 | Situated or occurring behind a bone.  This term is primarily used in anatomical descriptions, referring to structures or processes located behind or posterior to a bone.  | Example: The postosseous muscle was identified during the dissection. 骨后肌在解剖过程中被发现。 | 近义词: retro-osseous, posterior to bone | 反义词: preosseous, anterior to bone
