cheiloplasty:/ˈkaɪləplæsti/| n. |唇裂修复术|Surgical repair of a cleft lip.  Cheiloplasty is a surgical procedure used to correct cleft lip deformities, often performed on infants. |The baby underwent cheiloplasty to repair his cleft lip.  婴儿接受了唇裂修复手术。|  cleft lip repair, lip repair |  (无明确反义词，可考虑uncorrected cleft lip) | 名词
