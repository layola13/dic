self-divided:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈvaɪdəd/| adj.| 内部分裂的，意见分歧的 |  Describes a group or entity that is internally conflicted or divided, often into opposing factions.  It implies a lack of unity or consensus within the group. | Example: The self-divided nation struggled to overcome its internal conflicts.  这个四分五裂的国家努力克服内部冲突。| 近义词：fractured, divided, fragmented, discordant | 反义词：united, unified, harmonious
