parqueted:/pɑːrˈkeɪtɪd/| adj.|镶木地板的；用镶木地板铺设的|Covered or decorated with parquetry.  Parquet flooring is a type of flooring made of small pieces of wood arranged in geometric patterns.|The living room floor was beautifully parqueted in a herringbone pattern.|近义词：panelled, inlaid|反义词：unfloored, bare
