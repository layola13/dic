nonbiographical:/ˌnɒnˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. |非传记的，与传记无关的 |Not relating to or based on a person's life.  This adjective describes something that doesn't concern or depict a person's life story. |Example: The novel, while inspired by real events, is ultimately a nonbiographical work of fiction. 这部小说虽然取材于真实事件，但最终还是一部非传记虚构作品。|近义词：unbiographical, fictional, non-factual |反义词：biographical, factual
