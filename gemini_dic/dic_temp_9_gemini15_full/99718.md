interlude:/ˈɪntərluːd/| n. |插曲；间奏；幕间休息 |A short period of time between two longer periods or events, or a short piece of music played between the parts of a longer piece.|The concert featured a flute interlude between the two main movements. 音乐会中两大乐章之间有一段长笛独奏。  There was a brief interlude of peace before the fighting resumed. 在战斗重新开始之前，有一段短暂的和平时期。|近义词：intermission, break, pause |反义词：continuation, main event
