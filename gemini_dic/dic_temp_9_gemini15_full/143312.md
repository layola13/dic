outdure:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʊr/| vt.|持续时间比…长；比…持久|To last longer than; to survive or endure beyond.|The old oak tree outlasted the storm. 这棵古老的橡树挺过了风暴。|endure, last, survive|perish, expire, cease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The stone statue outdures the changing seasons. = 石像经受住了季节的变化。
This love outdures all challenges. = 这份爱克服了所有的挑战。


一般过去时:
The ancient civilization outlasted many empires. = 这个古老的文明比许多帝国都存在的时间长。
Her determination outlasted her physical limitations.  = 她的决心战胜了她的身体局限性。


现在完成时:
His legacy has outlasted his life. = 他的遗产延续至今。
The building has outlasted many renovations. = 这座建筑经受住了多次翻修。


过去完成时:
The castle had outlasted numerous sieges. = 这座城堡经受住了无数次围攻。
The tradition had outlasted generations. = 这一传统延续了几代人。


将来时:
Their love will outlast time itself. = 他们的爱情将超越时间本身。
This material will outlast any other currently available. = 这种材料将比目前任何其他材料都耐用。


虚拟语气:
If only my strength could outdure this pain. = 但愿我的力量能战胜这种痛苦。
I wish my courage could outdure my fear. = 但愿我的勇气能战胜我的恐惧。


被动语态:
The ancient ruins were outlasted by newer constructions. = 古代遗址被较新的建筑物取代了。
The old bridge was outlasted by the new highway. = 旧桥被新公路取代了。


疑问句:

Does his fame outdure his works? = 他的名声是否比他的作品更持久？


否定句:
The empire did not outdure its rivals. = 这个帝国没有比它的对手存在的时间长。
The short-lived flower did not outdure the harsh winter. = 短命的花朵没有挺过严酷的冬天。
