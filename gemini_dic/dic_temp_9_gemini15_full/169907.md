prospectus:/prɒˈspektəs/| /prɑːˈspektəs/ | n. | 计划书；说明书；招股说明书 | A prospectus is a formal document that describes a proposed project or business venture, typically including details of its financial aspects and intended goals.  It is often used to attract investors or potential participants. | 例句：The company issued a prospectus outlining its plans for expansion. 这家公司发布了一份说明其扩张计划的计划书。


The university published a prospectus detailing its courses.  大学出版了一本详细介绍其课程的招生简章。
