pyrexical:/paɪˈrɛksɪkəl/| adj.|发热的，发烧的| Relating to or characterized by pyrexia (fever).  The term is less common in modern medical usage, often replaced by "febrile".|例句：The patient presented with pyrexical symptoms, including a high fever and chills.  这位病人出现了发热症状，包括高烧和寒战。


The patient's pyrexical condition necessitated immediate medical attention. 病人的发热情况需要立即就医。


近义词：febrile, feverish, pyretic|反义词：afebrile, apyrexial

词性:形容词
