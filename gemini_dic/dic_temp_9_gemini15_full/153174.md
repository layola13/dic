pellagra:/pəˈleɪɡrə/| n. |糙皮病|A disease caused by a deficiency of niacin (vitamin B3), characterized by dermatitis, diarrhea, and dementia.|例:  The doctor diagnosed her condition as pellagra due to severe vitamin B3 deficiency.  医生诊断她的病情为糙皮病，因为严重缺乏维生素B3。

The symptoms of pellagra often include rough, scaly skin, digestive problems, and mental confusion. 糙皮病的症状通常包括粗糙、鳞状的皮肤、消化问题和精神错乱。


近义词：niacin deficiency (缺乏烟酸),  vitamin B3 deficiency (缺乏维生素B3)
反义词：None (糙皮病本身是一种疾病，没有直接的反义词)
