forswore:/fɔːˈswɔːr/| v.|发誓放弃；否认；弃绝|To renounce or reject something under oath; to deny solemnly.  |例句：He forswore all allegiance to the crown. 他发誓放弃对王室的一切效忠。

例句：She forswore smoking after her doctor's warning.  她听从医生的警告后发誓戒烟。


近义词：renounce, abjure, forgo, repudiate, recant

反义词：affirm, avow, uphold, maintain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forswears his former beliefs. 他放弃了他以前的信仰。
She forswears allegiance to the enemy. 她发誓效忠敌人。


一般过去时:
He forswore his loyalty to the king. 他发誓不再效忠国王。
She forswore the use of drugs. 她发誓不再吸毒。


现在完成时:
He has forsworn violence. 他已经发誓不再使用暴力。
She has forsworn all contact with him. 她已经发誓与他断绝一切联系。


过去完成时:
He had forsworn his ambition before he met her. 在遇到她之前，他已经放弃了他的野心。
She had forsworn her love for him. 她已经放弃了她对他的爱。


将来时:
He will forswear his right to the throne. 他将放弃他继承王位的权利。
She will forswear drinking alcohol. 她将发誓戒酒。


虚拟语气:
If he forswore his evil ways, he might have a better life. 如果他放弃邪恶的行为，他可能会过上更好的生活。
If she forswore her pride, things might have been different. 如果她放下她的骄傲，事情可能会有所不同。


被动语态:
His loyalty was forsworn. 他的忠诚被放弃了。
Her claim was forsworn by the court. 法院否决了她的说法。


疑问句:
Did he forswear his oath? 他是否违背了他的誓言？（注意这里forswear 的含义略有不同，指违背誓言）
Will she forswear her beliefs? 她会放弃她的信仰吗？


否定句:
He did not forswear his allegiance. 他没有放弃他的效忠。
She would not forswear her love. 她不会放弃她的爱。


PHRASE->
