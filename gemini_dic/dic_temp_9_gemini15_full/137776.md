nonvegetarians:/ˌnɒnˌvedʒɪˈteəriənz/| n. |非素食者|People who eat meat.  Nonvegetarians include those who eat meat regularly and those who occasionally eat meat but don't follow a vegetarian diet.|Example: Many nonvegetarians enjoy a good steak. 许多非素食者喜欢吃牛排。  The restaurant caters to both vegetarians and nonvegetarians. 这家餐厅同时迎合素食者和非素食者的需求。|近义词：meat-eaters, carnivores |反义词：vegetarians
