ration-out:/ˈræʃən aʊt/| v.|配给；分发；供应|To distribute something, especially food or supplies, in limited amounts to people who need it, often during a time of shortage or emergency.|The government rationed-out sugar during the war. 战争期间政府对食糖实行配给。|The company rationed-out the new equipment to its employees. 公司将新设备按比例分配给员工。|allocate, distribute, dispense|hoard, oversupply
