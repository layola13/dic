miking:/ˈmaɪkɪŋ/ | v. | 收音，录音 | The act of using a microphone to record sound.  It can refer to the process of recording audio, or the act of setting up microphones for a recording or live performance. |例句：The band is currently miking up their instruments for the concert. 乐队正在为音乐会收录乐器的音效。

例句：The sound engineer spent hours miking the stage for optimal sound quality. 音响工程师花了数小时为舞台布设麦克风以获得最佳音质。


近义词：recording, sound recording, microphoning


反义词：muting, silencing


词性:动词
