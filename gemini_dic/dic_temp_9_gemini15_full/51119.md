despisement:/dɪˈspaɪzmənt/| n. |轻蔑；鄙视；蔑视|The feeling of contempt or scorn for someone or something.  It implies a strong feeling of dislike and a belief that the object of despisement is worthless or inferior. |例句：His despisement of the poor was evident in his actions.  他轻蔑穷人的态度在他的行为中显而易见。

例句：Their despisement of traditional values led to social unrest. 他们对传统价值观的蔑视导致了社会动荡。


近义词：contempt, scorn, disdain, disregard, contempt


反义词：admiration, respect, esteem, reverence


词性：名词
