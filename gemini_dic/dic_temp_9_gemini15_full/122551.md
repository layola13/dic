modellers:/ˈmɒdələz/ | n. | 模型制作人员；建模者 |  People who create models, often in fields like computer science, engineering, or fashion design. They use various techniques and tools to build representations of real-world objects, systems, or concepts. | The modellers created a detailed 3D model of the building.  模型制作人员创建了一个详细的建筑物3D模型。 | designers, creators, architects (depending on context) |  — | noun
