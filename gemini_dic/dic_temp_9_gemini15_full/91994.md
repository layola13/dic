hypernutrition: /ˌhaɪpərnjuˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |营养过剩，过度营养| Excessive intake of nutrients, leading to an accumulation of body fat and potential health problems.  It is often characterized by obesity and related metabolic disorders. |例句：Hypernutrition, especially in childhood, can lead to obesity and long-term health issues. 营养过剩，尤其是在儿童时期，会导致肥胖和长期的健康问题。|例句：The study linked hypernutrition during pregnancy to increased risk of gestational diabetes. 研究表明怀孕期间营养过剩与妊娠糖尿病风险增加有关。|近义词：overfeeding, excessive nutrition, nutritional excess |反义词：malnutrition, undernutrition, starvation |名词
