demagogued:/ˈdɛməɡɒɡd/| v.|煽动；利用民众情绪煽动|To lead or persuade (people) by appealing to their prejudices and emotions; to act as a demagogue.|例句：The politician demagogued the crowd into a frenzy of anger.  政治家煽动人群陷入愤怒的狂潮。

例句：He demagogued the issue, twisting facts to manipulate public opinion. 他歪曲事实，操纵公众舆论，煽动这个议题。


近义词：incite, agitate, inflame, manipulate

反义词：moderate, pacify, conciliate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demagogues the public with his inflammatory speeches. 他用煽动性的演讲煽动公众。
She demagogues her followers, promising impossible things. 她煽动追随者，许诺不可能实现的事情。


一般过去时:
The speaker demagogued the audience with promises of easy solutions. 演讲者向听众许诺轻松的解决办法来煽动他们。
The dictator demagogued the nation into war. 独裁者煽动全国发动战争。


现在完成时:
He has demagogued the electorate for years. 他多年来一直在煽动选民。
She has demagogued the issue to gain political advantage. 她煽动这个问题以获得政治优势。


过去完成时:
The candidate had demagogued his supporters before the election. 候选人在选举前煽动了他的支持者。
They had demagogued the public into believing false information. 他们煽动公众相信虚假信息。


将来时:
He will demagogue the crowd with his populist rhetoric. 他将用民粹主义言论煽动人群。
She will demagogue the situation to her advantage. 她会利用这种情况来达到自己的目的。


虚拟语气:
If he demagogued the people, they would revolt. 如果他煽动人民，他们就会反抗。
If she had demagogued the situation differently, the outcome would have been better. 如果她对局势的煽动方式有所不同，结果就会更好。


被动语态:
The crowd was demagogued into a riot. 人群被煽动起来暴动。
The public was demagogued by false promises. 公众被虚假的承诺所煽动。


疑问句:
Was the audience demagogued by the speaker? 演讲者是否煽动了听众？
How was the public demagogued into supporting the policy? 公众是如何被煽动起来支持这项政策的？


否定句:
He did not demagogue the people; he appealed to their reason. 他没有煽动人民；他诉诸他们的理性。
She did not demagogue the situation; she handled it calmly. 她没有煽动局势；她冷静地处理了它。
