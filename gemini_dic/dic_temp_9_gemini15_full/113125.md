lunging:/ˈlʌndʒɪŋ/| v.|猛冲，突刺；突然向前扑|The act of making a lunge; a sudden forward movement.  |例句：The tiger lunged at its prey.  老虎猛扑向猎物。|例句：He lunged forward to catch the ball. 他猛扑向前接球。|近义词：plunging, darting, thrusting|反义词：retreating, withdrawing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lunges at the opponent. 他向对手猛扑过去。
She lunges forward to grab the book. 她向前猛扑去抓书。

一般过去时:
The cat lunged at the mouse. 猫向老鼠猛扑过去。
He lunged for the phone, nearly knocking over the table. 他猛地扑向电话，差点把桌子撞倒。

现在完成时:
The boxer has lunged several times in this round. 拳击手这一回合已经猛冲了几次。
She has lunged at every opportunity to advance her career. 她抓住每一个机会来提升她的事业。


过去完成时:
The thief had lunged at the guard before being apprehended. 小偷在被捕之前曾向警卫猛扑过去。
Before the accident, he had lunged forward to avoid an obstacle. 事故发生前，他曾向前猛扑以躲避障碍物。


将来时:
He will lunge at the first chance he gets. 他一有机会就会猛扑过去。
The lion will lunge when it spots its prey. 狮子一旦发现猎物就会猛扑过去。


虚拟语气:
If he lunged, he would have caught the ball. 如果他猛扑过去，他就能接住球了。
If she had lunged faster, she would have won the race. 如果她冲刺得更快，她就赢了比赛。


被动语态:
The ball was lunged at. 球被猛扑过去。
The attacker was lunged at by the victim. 袭击者被受害者猛扑过去。


疑问句:
Did he lunge at you? 他向你猛扑过去吗？
Will she lunge at the opportunity? 她会抓住机会猛扑过去吗？


否定句:
He didn't lunge at the ball. 他没有猛扑向球。
She didn't lunge at her attacker. 她没有向袭击者猛扑过去。
