ferruled:/ˈfɛrʊld/| adj.| 带套管的，装有金属箍的 |Having a ferrule or ferrules; fitted with a ferrule.  A ferrule is a metal ring or cap fitted to the end of something, typically to protect or strengthen it. |例句：The artist used a ferruled brush for detail work.  艺术家用带金属箍的画笔进行细节描绘。 The handle of the tool was ferruled for extra durability.  该工具的手柄装有金属箍以增强耐用性。|近义词：ringed, capped, tipped|反义词：unprotected, bare
