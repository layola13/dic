self-sovereignties:/ˌselfˈsɑːvrəntiz/| n. | 自主性；自我主权；自我管理 | The concept refers to individuals or groups having control over their own data, identities, and digital lives, free from external control or interference.  It emphasizes personal autonomy and the right to self-determination in the digital sphere.  This is particularly relevant in discussions around digital identity, data privacy, and blockchain technology. | Example:  The rise of blockchain technology is fostering increased self-sovereignties for users.  崛起中的区块链技术正在促进用户越来越多的自主性。


|近义词: self-determination, autonomy, independence |反义词: dependence, subjugation, control |
