duplex:/ˈduːpleks/| n. adj.|复式住宅；双倍的；双工的|A duplex is a building with two separate housing units, often one above the other.  It can also refer to something that is double or twofold, such as duplex printing (printing on both sides of a page).|例句：They bought a charming duplex in the city center. 他们在市中心买了一套迷人的复式住宅。|例句：The printer has a duplex function. 这台打印机具有双面打印功能。|近义词：double, twin, two-family|反义词：simplex, single|名词，形容词
