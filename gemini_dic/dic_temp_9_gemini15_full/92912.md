ideation:/ˌaɪdiˈeɪʃən/| n. |构思；创意；观念形成|The process of having new ideas.  It involves the generation, development, and evaluation of ideas.  This can range from simple brainstorming to complex problem-solving. |Example: The ideation phase of the project was crucial for its success.  这个项目的构思阶段对其成功至关重要。|近义词：conception, invention, innovation, brainstorming|反义词：stagnation, inertia, inaction|名词
