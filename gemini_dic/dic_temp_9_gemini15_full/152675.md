peaks:/piːks/ | n. |山峰，顶点，高峰 | The highest points of mountains or hills; the highest level or point reached.  | 例句：The snow-capped peaks towered above the valley. 白雪皑皑的山峰高耸于山谷之上。

例句：The company reached its peak in sales last year.  公司去年的销售额达到了顶峰。


近义词： summits, tops, crests, culminations | 反义词： bottoms, valleys, troughs


词源:  From Old English *pēoc, from Proto-Germanic *pēkō ("peak, point").  Related to pike (a pointed weapon).
