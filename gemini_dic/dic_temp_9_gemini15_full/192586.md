sculptures:/ˈskʌlptʃərz/| n.|雕塑；雕刻作品|The art of carving or shaping figures from stone, wood, metal, etc., or the figures so produced.|例句：The museum boasts a stunning collection of sculptures from around the world.  这个博物馆拥有来自世界各地的令人惊叹的雕塑收藏。

例句：Many ancient sculptures are still preserved in good condition. 许多古代雕塑至今保存完好。


近义词：statues, carvings, figures|反义词：N/A|名词
