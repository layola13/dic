/ˈprɛntɪsˌhʊd/| n. |学徒时期；初学阶段|The period of time someone spends as an apprentice learning a trade or skill.  It often implies a period of hardship and learning, and the transition from inexperience to competence. |例句：His prenticeship as a carpenter taught him valuable skills.  他的木匠学徒生涯教会了他宝贵的技能。

His prenticeship was a challenging but rewarding experience. 他的学徒生涯充满挑战，但也收获颇丰。|近义词：apprenticeship, noviceship, probation|反义词：mastership, expertise
