hemimetabolic:/ˌhɛmiˌmɛtəˈbɑːlɪk/| adj. |半变态的，不完全变态的 |Relating to or characterized by incomplete metamorphosis, in which the immature form (nymph) gradually develops into the adult form without passing through a pupal stage.  |Example: Grasshoppers undergo hemimetabolic development.  蝗虫经历半变态发育。|近义词：incomplete metamorphosis|反义词：holometabolic
