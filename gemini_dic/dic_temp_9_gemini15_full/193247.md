seedcorn:/ˈsidˌkɔːrn/| n. |种子玉米；（用于播种的）种子；投资；启动资金|Seed corn refers to seeds used for planting crops, especially corn.  Figuratively, it represents an investment made to ensure future growth or success. |Example:  The company reinvested its profits as seedcorn for future expansion. 这家公司将利润再投资作为未来扩张的启动资金。|近义词：seed, investment, capital |反义词：harvest, return, profit
