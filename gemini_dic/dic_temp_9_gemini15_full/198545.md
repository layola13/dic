shrow:/ʃroʊ/| n.|泼妇；悍妇|A shrew is a woman who is bad-tempered and quarrelsome.|例句：She was known as a shrew and a scold. 她以泼妇和骂街的妇人而闻名。|近义词：vixen, termagant, scold|反义词：gentlewoman, angel
