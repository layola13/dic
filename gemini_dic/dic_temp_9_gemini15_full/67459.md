extoller:/ɪkˈstɔːlər/| n. |赞扬者；吹捧者 | One who praises or glorifies enthusiastically.  |The extoller of the new product spoke for an hour.  新的产品的赞扬者滔滔不绝地讲了一个小时。|praiser, eulogist, panegyrist |critic, detractor, denier |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He extols the virtues of hard work. = 他赞扬努力工作的优点。
She extols the benefits of regular exercise. = 她赞扬定期锻炼的好处。

一般过去时:
The speaker extolled the achievements of the company. = 演讲者赞扬了公司的成就。
They extolled the beauty of the countryside. = 他们赞美了乡村的美景。

现在完成时:
He has extolled the benefits of meditation for years. = 多年来，他一直赞扬冥想的好处。
She has extolled the virtues of kindness in her writings. = 在她的著作中，她一直赞扬善良的优点。

过去完成时:
They had extolled the importance of education before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就赞扬了教育的重要性。
He had extolled the merits of the plan to the board. = 他曾向董事会赞扬了该计划的优点。

将来时:
She will extol the virtues of patience in her next speech. = 她将在下一次演讲中赞扬耐心的优点。
They will extol the achievements of the team at the awards ceremony. = 他们将在颁奖典礼上赞扬团队的成就。

虚拟语气:
If I were to extol his virtues, I would do so with sincerity. = 如果我要赞扬他的优点，我会真诚地去做。
I wish I could extol the beauty of the sunset more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地赞美日落的美丽。

被动语态:
His achievements were extolled by the media. = 他的成就受到了媒体的赞扬。
The virtues of the product were extolled in the advertisement. = 产品的优点在广告中得到了赞扬。

疑问句:
Does he extol the virtues of frugality? = 他是否赞扬节俭的优点？
Will they extol the benefits of this new technology? = 他们会赞扬这项新技术的益处吗？

否定句:
He did not extol the virtues of laziness. = 他没有赞扬懒惰的优点。
She does not extol violence in any way. = 她绝不赞扬暴力。
