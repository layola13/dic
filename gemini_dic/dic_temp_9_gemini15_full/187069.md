riverbed:/ˈrɪvərbed/| n. |河床|The bottom or channel of a river.  It is the land under the water of a river, often composed of sand, gravel, or rock.|例句：The riverbed was dry during the drought.  旱季河床干涸了。|例句：We found many interesting fossils embedded in the riverbed. 我们在河床上发现了许多有趣的化石。|近义词：river bottom, river channel|反义词：riverbank, river surface
