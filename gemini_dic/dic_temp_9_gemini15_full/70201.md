fibroin:/ˈfaɪbroʊɪn/| n. |纤维蛋白|A fibrous protein that is the main constituent of silk and spiderwebs.  It is characterized by its high content of glycine and alanine, which allow it to form beta-sheets that give the fiber its strength and flexibility. |例句：The spider spun a web of remarkable strength, thanks to the high-quality fibroin in its silk.  蜘蛛吐出的丝网强度惊人，这要归功于其丝中高质量的纤维蛋白。|近义词：protein, silk protein|反义词：无
