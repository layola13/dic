outpassed:/ˌaʊtˈpæst/| v.|超过，胜过|To surpass or exceed; to go beyond in performance or achievement.|例句：He outpassed all his competitors in the race. 他在比赛中超过了所有竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, excel, outstrip|反义词：underperform, lag behind

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outpasses his colleagues in terms of productivity. = 他在生产力方面超过了他的同事。
She consistently outpasses her goals. = 她总是超额完成目标。

一般过去时:
The team outpassed their rivals in the final match. = 该队在决赛中超过了对手。
Last year, our sales outpassed expectations. = 去年，我们的销售额超过了预期。

现在完成时:
He has outpassed his previous best score. = 他已经超过了他之前的最高分。
She has outpassed all her previous achievements. = 她已经超越了她以前的所有成就。

过去完成时:
By the end of the project, they had outpassed their initial budget. = 到项目结束时，他们已经超过了最初的预算。
Before the deadline, she had outpassed her own expectations. = 在截止日期之前，她已经超过了她自己的预期。

将来时:
We expect that our company will outpass its competitors in the next quarter. = 我们预计公司下个季度将超过竞争对手。
He believes he will outpass his personal record. = 他相信他会打破自己的个人记录。


虚拟语气:
If he studied harder, he might outpass his classmates. = 如果他学习更努力，他可能会超过他的同学们。
I wish I could outpass my limitations. = 我希望我能克服我的局限性。

被动语态:
His performance was outpassed by the newcomer. = 他的表现被新人超越了。
Their record was outpassed by the younger generation. = 他们的记录被年轻一代打破了。

疑问句:
Did he outpass the record? = 他打破记录了吗？
Will they outpass their targets this year? = 他们今年会超过目标吗？

否定句:
He didn't outpass the qualifying time. = 他没有超过合格时间。
They failed to outpass their previous sales figures. = 他们未能超过之前的销售额。
