deskilled:/ˈdiːskɪld/| adj.|缺乏技能的，技能退化的|Having lost skills through lack of practice or use.  The term often implies a decline in proficiency due to job displacement or changes in technology.|例句：Many factory workers felt deskilled after the automation of the production line. 许多工厂工人自动化生产线后感觉技能退化了。

例句：Years of unemployment left him feeling deskilled and unemployable.  多年的失业让他感到技能退化，无法就业。


近义词：unskilled, untrained, incompetent, unskilled,  de-skilled

反义词：skilled, proficient, expert, trained

词性：形容词
