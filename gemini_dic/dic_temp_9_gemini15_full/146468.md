overfrustration: /ˌoʊvərfrʌˈstreɪʃən/ | n. |过度挫败感，极度失望 | The feeling of being extremely frustrated; exceeding the limits of normal frustration.  It often implies a prolonged or intense experience of frustration leading to feelings of helplessness and despair. | Example:  The constant delays and setbacks led to overfrustration and a sense of hopelessness among the project team.  =  持续的延误和挫折导致项目团队过度沮丧和绝望。|近义词：extreme frustration, intense disappointment, despair, disillusionment |反义词：satisfaction, fulfillment, contentment |  词性:名词
