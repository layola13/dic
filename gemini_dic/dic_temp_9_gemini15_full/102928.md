jaywalked:/ˈdʒeɪwɔːk/| v.|违规穿越马路|To cross a street illegally, without using a pedestrian crossing.  This is often against traffic laws.|例句：He jaywalked across the busy street, narrowly avoiding a car. 他违规穿越繁忙的街道，险些被车撞到。 He was fined for jaywalking. 他因违规穿越马路而被罚款。|近义词：cross illegally, disregard traffic laws|反义词：use a crosswalk, obey traffic laws
