inhabiting:/ɪnˈhæbɪtɪŋ/ | v. |居住；栖息；占据 |To live in or occupy a place.  |例句：Many different species of birds are inhabiting the rainforest.  许多不同种类的鸟类栖息在雨林中。

例句：Millions of people are inhabiting this vast city. 数百万的人口居住在这个巨大的城市里。


近义词：occupying, dwelling in, residing in, populating | 反义词：evacuating, leaving, deserting | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds are inhabiting the trees. = 鸟儿栖息在树上。
They are inhabiting the old house. = 他们居住在老房子里。


一般过去时:
The family inhabited that house for many years. = 这家人在那所房子里住了许多年。
 Dinosaurs inhabited the Earth millions of years ago. = 恐龙在数百万年前居住在地球上。


现在完成时:
They have inhabited this area for generations. = 他们世世代代居住在这个地区。
We have inhabited this apartment for two years. = 我们已经在这套公寓住了两年了。


过去完成时:
Before the flood, many families had inhabited the coastal region. = 在洪水之前，许多家庭居住在沿海地区。
They had inhabited the island for centuries before the Europeans arrived. = 在欧洲人到来之前，他们已经在这个岛屿上居住了几个世纪。


将来时:
We will inhabit a new house next year. = 我们明年将住进一所新房子。
They will inhabit the newly built apartments soon. = 他们很快就会住进新建成的公寓。


虚拟语气:
If they inhabited a larger house, they would have more space. = 如果他们住在一所更大的房子里，他们就会有更多空间。
I wish we inhabited a place closer to the city. = 我希望我们住的地方离市区更近些。


被动语态:
The island was inhabited by indigenous people for thousands of years. = 这个岛屿几千年来一直居住着土著居民。
The area is currently being inhabited by a growing population. = 这个地区目前正被不断增长的人口居住。


疑问句:

Is anyone inhabiting the remote island? = 有人在那个偏远的岛屿上居住吗？


否定句:
No one is inhabiting the deserted village. = 没有人居住在这个荒凉的村庄里。
The building has not been inhabited for decades. = 这座建筑几十年来一直无人居住。
