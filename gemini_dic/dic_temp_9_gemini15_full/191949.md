scoper:/ˈskoʊpər/ | n. | 范围界定者；调查者；勘测者 | A person who determines the scope of something; someone who investigates or surveys.  | Example: The project manager acted as the scoper, defining the project's boundaries and objectives.  项目经理担任范围界定者，定义项目的界限和目标。|近义词：investigator, surveyor, examiner | 反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scoper defines the project's scope. = 范围界定者定义项目的范围。
He scopes out the market before launching a new product. = 他在推出新产品之前会先考察市场。

一般过去时:
The scoper determined the project's limitations. = 范围界定者确定了项目的局限性。
She scoped the area for potential hazards. = 她勘察了该区域的潜在危险。

现在完成时:
The team has scoped out several potential locations. = 团队已经考察了几个潜在的地点。
He has scoped the project and identified key risks. = 他已经对项目进行了范围界定并确定了关键风险。

过去完成时:
They had scoped the problem before proposing a solution. = 他们在提出解决方案之前已经对问题进行了界定。
She had scoped the site thoroughly before construction began. = 在施工开始之前，她已经彻底勘察了现场。

将来时:
The company will scope the project next week. = 公司下周将对项目进行范围界定。
We will scope the market to understand customer needs. = 我们将考察市场以了解客户需求。

虚拟语气:
If they had scoped the project properly, they would have avoided delays. = 如果他们对项目进行了适当的范围界定，他们本可以避免延误。

被动语态:
