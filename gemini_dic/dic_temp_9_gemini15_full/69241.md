feast:/fiːst/| n. v.|盛宴；宴会；尽情享用|A feast is a large meal, especially one held to celebrate a special occasion.  It can also refer to a large amount of something enjoyable. As a verb, it means to eat and drink heartily.|例句：We had a magnificent feast to celebrate our wedding anniversary. 我们举行了一场盛大的宴会来庆祝我们的结婚周年纪念日。

They feasted on lobster and champagne.  他们尽情享用龙虾和香槟。|近义词：banquet, celebration, festival|反义词：famine, starvation|名词，动词
