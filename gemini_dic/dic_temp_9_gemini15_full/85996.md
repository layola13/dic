headstones:/ˈhɛdˌstoʊnz/| n. |墓碑|Upright stones or markers placed at the head of a grave, often inscribed with the deceased's name and dates.|The old headstones were covered in moss.  古老的墓碑上覆盖着苔藓。|The headstones in the cemetery bore silent witness to the passage of time. 墓地里的墓碑默默地见证了时间的流逝。|tombstones, gravestones|—|
