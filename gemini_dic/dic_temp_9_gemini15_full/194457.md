self-reproaching:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈproʊtʃɪŋ/| adj. |自责的，悔恨的 |Feeling or expressing strong disapproval of oneself for one's actions or behavior.  |例句：He felt a pang of self-reproaching guilt after shouting at his mother. 他对着母亲大喊后感到一阵自责的内疚。

例句：Her self-reproaching tone suggested she regretted her harsh words. 她自责的语气暗示她后悔说了那些刻薄的话。


近义词：self-critical, remorseful, repentant, regretful


反义词：self-satisfied, complacent, unrepentant


词源：self- (表示“自己”) + reproaching (来自动词reproach，意思是“责备，谴责”)。  reproach 的词源追溯到古法语 reprochier 和拉丁语 reprobare (表示“拒绝，责备”)。
