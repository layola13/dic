prattles:/ˈprætəlz/| v.|喋喋不休地说话，闲聊 |To talk continuously and rapidly, especially about unimportant things.|The children prattled on about their day at school. 孩子们喋喋不休地谈论他们在学校的一天。|近义词：babble, chatter, gabble |反义词：be silent, be quiet|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prattles incessantly about his travels. 他喋喋不休地谈论他的旅行。
She prattles away happily to her doll. 她高兴地对她娃娃喋喋不休。


一般过去时:
The old woman prattled on about her childhood. 老妇人喋喋不休地谈论她的童年。
He prattled away for hours without stopping. 他喋喋不休地讲了几个小时，没有停下来。


现在完成时:
He has prattled on about the same topic for days. 他已经喋喋不休地谈论同一个话题好几天了。
She has prattled away all afternoon. 她整个下午都在喋喋不休。


过去完成时:
She had prattled on for so long that everyone was tired. 她喋喋不休地说了那么久，以至于每个人都累了。
They had prattled away about nothing important before the meeting. 会议前，他们一直在喋喋不休地谈论一些无关紧要的事情。


将来时:
He will prattle on about politics all evening. 他整个晚上都会喋喋不休地谈论政治。
She will prattle away to anyone who will listen. 她会对任何愿意倾听的人喋喋不休。


虚拟语气:
If he prattled less, people would listen more. 如果他少说些废话，人们就会多听一些。
I wish she wouldn't prattle so much. 我希望她不要说得那么多。


被动语态:
His stories are often prattled about by the townspeople. 他的故事经常被镇上的人们津津乐道。
The news was prattled all over the town. 这个消息在镇上被到处传播。


疑问句:
Does he always prattle on like that? 他总是那样喋喋不休吗？
Why is she prattling away so loudly? 她为什么这么大声地喋喋不休？


否定句:
He didn't prattle much at the party. 他在派对上没怎么说话。
She didn't prattle on about her problems. 她没有喋喋不休地谈论她的问题。
