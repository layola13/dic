re-lacing: /ˌriːˈleɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 重新系带；重新绑扎 | The act of replacing or tightening laces, typically on shoes or other items with laces.  It implies a process of undoing and redoing the lacing. | Example:  I spent five minutes re-lacing my boots after one of the laces broke. 我花了五分钟重新系我的靴子，因为其中一条鞋带断了。|近义词：relacing, retying, refastening |反义词：unlacing, untying
