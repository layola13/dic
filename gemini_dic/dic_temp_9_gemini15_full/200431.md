slacklining:/ˈslækˌlaɪnɪŋ/| n. |走软绳，走平衡绳 |The activity of walking across a length of slackline.  It's a sport that requires balance, coordination, and concentration.| 例句：He enjoys slacklining in the park every weekend. 他每个周末都喜欢在公园里走软绳。

She's been practicing slacklining to improve her balance. 她一直在练习走软绳以提高平衡能力。


近义词：tightrope walking (though technically different)


反义词：无


词性：名词
