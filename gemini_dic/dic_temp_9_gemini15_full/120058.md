millstone:/ˈmɪlstəʊn/| n.|磨石，沉重的负担|A millstone is a heavy stone used in a mill to grind grain.  Figuratively, it refers to something that is a heavy burden or a great difficulty.|例句：The debt was a millstone around his neck. 这笔债务是他沉重的负担。

The constant pressure of work was a millstone around her neck, preventing her from enjoying life. 工作的持续压力像磨石一样压在她脖子上，让她无法享受生活。|近义词：burden, weight, encumbrance|反义词：relief, ease, liberation|
