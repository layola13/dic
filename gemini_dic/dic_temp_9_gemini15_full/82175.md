groping:/ˈɡroʊpɪŋ/| v.|摸索，探索，尝试|To feel about or search blindly or uncertainly with the hands.  It can also refer to a clumsy or uncertain attempt to do something.|例句：He groped for the light switch in the dark. 他在黑暗中摸索着找电灯开关。

例句：The company is groping for a solution to its financial problems. 这家公司正在努力寻找解决其财务问题的办法。


近义词：fumbling, searching, struggling


反义词：finding, discovering, knowing


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gropes for his keys in the dark. = 他在黑暗中摸索着找钥匙。
She gropes around for the right word. = 她四处摸索着寻找合适的词语。


一般过去时:
He groped for the doorknob, but missed. = 他摸索门把手，但是没摸到。
She groped her way through the crowded room. = 她摸索着穿过拥挤的房间。


现在完成时:
I have been groping for an answer all day. = 我整天都在苦苦寻找答案。
They have groped in the dark for years, trying to find a cure. = 他们多年来一直在黑暗中摸索，试图找到治疗方法。


过去完成时:
He had groped for the phone before he finally found it. = 他在最终找到电话之前一直在摸索着找电话。
She had groped around in her bag before she found her wallet. = 她在她包里翻找了好久才找到她的钱包。


将来时:
He will grope for the answer in the exam. = 他将在考试中摸索答案。
She will grope her way through the unfamiliar city. = 她将摸索着穿过陌生的城市。


虚拟语气:
If I had a light, I wouldn't be groping in the dark. = 如果我有灯，我就不会在黑暗中摸索了。
I wish I hadn't groped around so much; I could have found it sooner. = 我真希望我没有摸索那么久；我本来可以早点找到的。


被动语态:
The solution was groped for by the committee. = 解决方案是由委员会摸索出来的。（很少用被动语态）
The answer was groped at, but never found. = 答案被尝试摸索，但从未找到。（很少用被动语态）


疑问句:
Is he groping blindly in the dark? = 他在黑暗中盲目地摸索吗？
Are they still groping for a solution? = 他们还在努力寻找解决方案吗？


否定句:
He didn't grope for the switch; he knew where it was. = 他没有摸索开关；他知道它在哪里。
They didn't grope around for long before they found what they were looking for. = 他们没有摸索很久就找到了他们想要的东西。


PHRASE->
groping in the dark = 在黑暗中摸索
groping for words = 寻找合适的词语
groping for solutions = 寻找解决方案
groping for meaning = 寻找意义
groping towards understanding = 努力理解


词源:
The word "groping" comes from the Middle English word "gropen," which means "to feel about,"  ultimately deriving from the Proto-Germanic *grōpan.  The meaning has evolved to encompass both physical and metaphorical searching.
