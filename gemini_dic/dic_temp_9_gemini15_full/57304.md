downstairs:/ˌdaʊnˈsteərz/| adv. n.|楼下，往楼下|Adverb:  Downstairs indicates movement or location on a lower floor of a building. Noun: Downstairs can refer to the lower floor itself. |例句：The children are playing downstairs. 孩子们正在楼下玩耍。

The meeting will be held downstairs in the conference room. 会议将在楼下的会议室举行。|近义词：below,beneath|反义词：upstairs


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goes downstairs every morning. 他每天早上都下楼。
They live downstairs. 他们住在楼下。


一般过去时:
She went downstairs to get a glass of water. 她下楼去拿一杯水。
We walked downstairs quietly. 我们悄悄地下了楼。


现在完成时:
I have already been downstairs. 我已经下楼了。
They have moved downstairs. 他们搬到楼下了。


过去完成时:
He had already gone downstairs before I called. 我打电话之前他已经下楼了。
She had been downstairs for an hour. 她已经下楼一个小时了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
