ichthyophagy:/ɪkθiˈɒfædʒi/| n. |食鱼；鱼食性|The practice of eating fish; the consumption of fish as food.  It can also refer to the diet of an organism that primarily eats fish.|Example: Ichthyophagy is a common practice in many coastal communities. 食鱼在许多沿海社区是一种常见的习俗。  The study focused on the ichthyophagy of the local seabird population. 这项研究集中在当地海鸟的食鱼习性上。|近义词：fish-eating, piscivory|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with vegetarianism or herbivory.
