self-enriching: /ˌsɛlfɪnˈrɪtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 自肥的；谋取私利的 |  Enriching oneself, often illicitly or at the expense of others.  This adjective describes actions or systems that primarily benefit a select individual or group, often through unethical or corrupt means. | 例句：The self-enriching schemes of the corrupt officials led to widespread public outrage.  贪污官员的自我敛财计划导致了公众的广泛愤怒。  The self-enriching nature of the proposed legislation raised concerns among voters.  拟议立法中的自肥性质引起了选民的担忧。 | 近义词：self-serving, corrupt, avaricious, venal | 反义词：altruistic, selfless, benevolent
