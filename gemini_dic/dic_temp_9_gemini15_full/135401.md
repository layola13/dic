nonpermission:/ˌnɒn pəˈmɪʃən/| n. |未经许可，未获授权|The act or state of not having permission; lack of authorization.  It implies a prohibition or lack of consent.|例句：Entering the restricted area without nonpermission is a serious offense. 未经许可进入禁区是严重的违规行为。  The use of the company's intellectual property without nonpermission is illegal. 未经许可使用公司知识产权是违法的。|近义词：unauthorized access,lack of consent,prohibition|反义词：permission,authorization


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, grammatical tenses, voice, and mood are not applicable.  The word itself expresses a state of being.

PHRASE->
without nonpermission: 未经许可
lack of nonpermission: 许可不足 (This is redundant; simply "permission" or "authorization" is preferred)
grant nonpermission:  (This is illogical;  "deny permission" is the correct phrasing)
obtain nonpermission: (This is illogical; one obtains permission, not its absence)
evidence of nonpermission: 未经许可的证据
