encompass:/ɛnˈkʌmpəs/| vt.|包含，包围，包括|To include or contain (something) within its scope; to surround or encircle.|The study encompasses a wide range of topics. 这项研究涵盖了广泛的主题。  His ambition encompassed the whole world. 他的雄心包罗万象。|include, contain, embrace, surround|exclude, omit

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The course encompasses a variety of subjects. = 这门课程包含各种科目。
Her responsibilities encompass managing the team and overseeing the budget. = 她的职责包括管理团队和监督预算。


一般过去时:
The empire encompassed vast territories. = 这个帝国包含广阔的领土。
The ancient city encompassed a large area. = 古城占地很大。


现在完成时:
Their influence has encompassed many aspects of society. = 他们的影响已经波及社会的许多方面。
The project has encompassed more challenges than we initially anticipated. = 这个项目遇到的挑战比我们最初预期的要多。


过去完成时:
The investigation had encompassed all available evidence before the conclusion. = 在得出结论之前，调查已经涵盖了所有可用证据。
By the time the meeting ended, the discussion had encompassed several key issues. = 到会议结束时，讨论已经涵盖了几个关键问题。


将来时:
The new strategy will encompass all departments. = 新战略将涵盖所有部门。
The updated curriculum will encompass more practical applications. = 更新后的课程将包含更多实践应用。


虚拟语气:
If the project encompassed all our needs, we would be satisfied. = 如果这个项目满足了我们所有的需求，我们会很满意。
I wish the research encompassed a larger sample size. = 我希望这项研究包含更大的样本量。


被动语态:
Many aspects of life are encompassed by this philosophy. = 许多方面的生活都被这种哲学所包含。
The problem was encompassed by a larger issue that needed addressing. = 这个问题被一个需要解决的更大的问题所包含。


疑问句:

What aspects of the project does your role encompass? = 你的角色包含项目的哪些方面？


否定句:
The agreement did not encompass the intellectual property rights. = 该协议不包含知识产权。
The analysis did not encompass all the relevant data. = 分析没有涵盖所有相关数据。
