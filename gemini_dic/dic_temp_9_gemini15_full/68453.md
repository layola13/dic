familiar:/fəˈmɪliər/ | adj. | 熟悉的，常见的，亲密的 | Knowing something or someone well; often encountered; closely acquainted. | 例句：I'm familiar with that song. = 我熟悉那首歌。 | 例句：He's familiar with the local customs. = 他熟悉当地的风俗习惯。|近义词：known, acquainted, intimate |反义词：unfamiliar, strange, foreign


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is familiar with the software. = 他熟悉这个软件。
The music sounds familiar to me. = 这音乐听起来很熟悉。

一般过去时:
I was familiar with his work before we met. = 在我们见面之前我就熟悉他的作品了。
She was familiar with the process. = 她熟悉这个流程。


现在完成时:
I have become familiar with the city. = 我已经熟悉了这座城市。
They have familiarized themselves with the new rules. = 他们已经熟悉了新规。


过去完成时:
I had already familiarized myself with the subject before the meeting. = 在会议之前我已经熟悉了这个主题。
They had become familiar with each other over the years. = 他们多年来彼此已经很熟悉了。


将来时:
I will be familiar with the material by next week. = 到下周我就会熟悉这些材料了。
She will familiarize herself with the new system soon. = 她很快就会熟悉这个新系统了。


虚拟语气:
If I were familiar with the technology, I could help you. = 如果我熟悉这项技术，我就能帮你了。
I wish I were more familiar with this area. = 我希望我对这个领域更熟悉一些。


被动语态:
The students are being familiarized with the new curriculum. = 学生们正在熟悉新的课程。
The procedures were made familiar to all employees. = 这些程序已被所有员工熟知。


疑问句:
Are you familiar with this author? = 你熟悉这位作者吗？
Is he familiar with the situation? = 他熟悉情况吗？


否定句:
I am not familiar with his writing style. = 我不熟悉他的写作风格。
She wasn't familiar with the local dialect. = 她不熟悉当地的方言。



PHRASE->
be familiar with = 熟悉...
familiar face = 熟面孔
familiar ground = 熟路；熟悉领域
familiar tone = 熟络的语气
a familiar sight = 常见景象
