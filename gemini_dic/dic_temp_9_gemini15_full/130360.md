non-understanding: /ˌnɒnˌʌndərˈstændɪŋ/ | adj. | 不理解的；缺乏理解的 | Lack of understanding; failure to comprehend.  It implies a state of not grasping or comprehending something.  The word itself is not commonly used as a noun or verb. |  His non-understanding of the instructions led to errors.  他的指示理解错误导致了错误。

The non-understanding between the two parties caused the negotiations to collapse. 双方之间的缺乏理解导致谈判破裂。


近义词： incomprehension, miscomprehension, lack of comprehension, misunderstanding

反义词： understanding, comprehension, grasp


词性：形容词 (adjective)


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb or noun conjugations in the way "to understand" does.  Therefore, tense variations are not applicable.  The word itself describes a state.
