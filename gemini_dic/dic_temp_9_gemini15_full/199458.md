sinai:/ˈsaɪnaɪ/| n.|西奈山，西奈半岛|A mountainous region in northeastern Egypt, forming a peninsula between the Gulf of Suez and the Gulf of Aqaba.  The area is significant in Judaism, Christianity, and Islam.  It is also known for its diverse landscapes, including deserts and mountains.|例句：Moses received the Ten Commandments on Mount Sinai. 摩西在西奈山上接受了十诫。|例句：The Sinai Peninsula is a popular destination for hikers and climbers. 西奈半岛是徒步旅行者和登山者的热门目的地。|近义词：Mount Sinai, Peninsula|反义词：无
