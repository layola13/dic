counterculture: /ˈkaʊntərkʌltʃər/ | n. | 反文化；对立文化 | A way of life and set of attitudes opposed to or rejecting those of the established culture.  It often involves the rejection of mainstream values, norms, and beliefs.  | Example: The hippie movement of the 1960s was a significant counterculture.  六十年代嬉皮士运动是一场重要的反文化运动。|近义词：subculture, alternative culture | 反义词：mainstream culture, established culture | 名词
