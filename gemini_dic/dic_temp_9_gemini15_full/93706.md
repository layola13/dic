imitatrix:/ˌɪmɪˈteɪtrɪks/| n. |模仿者，仿效者 | A female who imitates; one that imitates.  The word refers specifically to a female who copies or mimics someone or something.  It's less common than the general term "imitator".|例句：The young bird was an imitatrix of its mother's song.  这只小鸟模仿着它母亲的歌声。|例句：Her performance was a clever imitatrix of the original. 她的表演巧妙地模仿了原作。|近义词：imitator, copier, mimic, emulator |反义词：original, innovator, pioneer |名词
