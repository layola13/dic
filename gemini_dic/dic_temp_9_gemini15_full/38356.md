concentration:/ˌkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən/| n. |专注；浓度；集中 |The ability to focus one's attention or mental effort on a particular object, task, or activity.  It can also refer to the amount of a substance in a defined space. |例句：The student needed better concentration to finish the exam.  学生需要更好的专注力才能完成考试。

例句：The concentration of salt in the solution is too high.  溶液中盐的浓度太高了。|近义词：focus, attention, density |反义词：distraction, diffusion


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He maintains a high level of concentration during his work. 他工作期间保持高度的注意力。
Her concentration on the problem is impressive. 她对这个问题的专注令人印象深刻。


一般过去时:
The scientists measured the concentration of the chemicals. 科学家们测量了化学物质的浓度。
My concentration faltered during the noisy meeting. 在吵闹的会议上，我的注意力涣散了。


现在完成时:
She has improved her concentration significantly through practice. 通过练习，她已经显著提高了专注力。
They have achieved a high concentration of the desired product. 他们已经获得了高浓度的目标产品。


过去完成时:
He had lost his concentration before the important presentation.  他在重要演示之前已经失去了注意力。
The factory had already achieved a high concentration of pollutants in the river. 工厂已经导致河里污染物浓度很高了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
