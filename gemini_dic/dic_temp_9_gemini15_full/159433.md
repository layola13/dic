polecats:/ˈpəʊlˌkæt/| n. | 鼬; 貂; |A polecat is a small, carnivorous mammal belonging to the Mustelidae family.  They are known for their strong musky odor.  They are often confused with ferrets, but have distinct differences in appearance and behaviour.|例句：The polecat darted into its burrow when it sensed danger.  这只鼬鼠感觉到危险时，迅速窜进了它的洞穴。  The farmer set traps to control the polecat population that was raiding his henhouse. 农民设置陷阱来控制袭击他的鸡舍的鼬鼠数量。|近义词：ferret, weasel|反义词:None specifically.
