psalmed:/ˈsɑːmd/| v.|吟诵，诵诗；用诗歌表达|To recite or sing Psalms or other sacred songs; to express in poetic form.|例句：The choir psalmed the ancient hymn.  唱诗班吟诵了古老的圣歌。|例句：He psalmed his love for her in a sonnet. 他在一首十四行诗中表达了他对她的爱。|近义词：chanted, recited, sang, versified|反义词：prosed, mumbled
