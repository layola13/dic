nondoubting:/ˌnɒnˈdaʊtɪŋ/| adj.|毫无疑问的，确信的，坚定不移的|  Describes someone who does not doubt or question; characterized by unwavering belief or confidence.  It implies a complete absence of skepticism or uncertainty.| 例句：He had a nondoubting faith in his abilities. 他对自己的能力深信不疑。

He showed a nondoubting acceptance of the evidence presented. 他毫无疑问地接受了所提出的证据。|近义词：certain, sure, confident, unwavering, unquestioning|反义词：doubtful, skeptical, uncertain, hesitant, wavering|词性:形容词
