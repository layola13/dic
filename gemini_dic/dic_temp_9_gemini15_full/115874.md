marlin:/ˈmɑːrlɪn/| n.|马林鱼|A large, powerful game fish with a long, pointed bill.  Marlins are highly prized by sport fishermen.|例句：He caught a magnificent marlin during his fishing trip. 他在钓鱼旅行中钓到了一条壮观的马林鱼。  例句：The marlin leaped high out of the water, its silver flanks gleaming. 马林鱼从水中跃起，银色的侧翼闪闪发光。|近义词：swordfish, spearfish|反义词：无|名词
