/ɪst/ | suf. |  后缀，表示…的人；…的事物；信仰者 |  A suffix forming nouns denoting: a person or thing belonging to a particular class or having a particular quality or function; an adherent or supporter of a particular belief, cause, or system.  |  Scientists study the world around us.  科学家研究我们周围的世界。 |  近义词：-er, -or,-ian | 反义词：无


USAGE->
The suffix "-ist" doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It only modifies nouns.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->
artist: 艺术家
scientist: 科学家
communist: 共产主义者
capitalist: 资本家
terrorist: 恐怖分子
