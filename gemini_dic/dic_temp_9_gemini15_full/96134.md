indemnity:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪti/| n. |赔偿；补偿；保证；免责|Protection or exemption from loss or harm; a sum of money paid in compensation for loss or damage.|The insurance policy provided indemnity against fire damage. 这份保险单提供火灾损失赔偿。|compensation, recompense, protection|liability, risk|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The insurance company provides indemnity for accidents. = 保险公司为意外事故提供赔偿。
This contract offers full indemnity against losses. = 这份合同提供全部损失赔偿。


一般过去时:
The company paid indemnity for the damaged goods. = 公司赔偿了损坏的货物。
They received indemnity after the flood. = 洪水过后他们收到了赔偿。


现在完成时:
The government has promised indemnity to the victims. = 政府已承诺对受害者进行赔偿。
He has received indemnity for his medical expenses. = 他已经收到了医疗费用的赔偿。


过去完成时:
They had already received indemnity before the lawsuit. = 诉讼之前他们已经收到了赔偿。
The company had provided indemnity for similar incidents in the past. = 公司过去已经为类似事件提供了赔偿。


将来时:
The insurance policy will provide indemnity for any damages. = 保险单将赔偿任何损失。
They will seek indemnity for their losses from the responsible party. = 他们将向责任方寻求损失赔偿。


虚拟语气:
If the company had provided indemnity, the situation would have been different. = 如果公司提供了赔偿，情况就会不同。
I wish they had given me indemnity for the damaged property. = 我希望他们赔偿我损坏的财产。


被动语态:
Indemnity was paid to the injured party. = 赔偿金已支付给受伤方。
Indemnity is being sought for the environmental damage. = 正在寻求对环境破坏的赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the company offer indemnity for this claim? = 公司会为这个索赔提供赔偿吗？
What type of indemnity is available? = 有哪些类型的赔偿？


否定句:
The contract did not offer indemnity for consequential losses. = 合同没有提供间接损失的赔偿。
The insurance policy does not cover this type of indemnity. = 保险单不涵盖这种类型的赔偿。
