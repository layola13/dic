postimplantation:/ˌpɒstimplænˈteɪʃən/ | adj. | 植入后；着床后 |  relating to the period after implantation of an embryo or fetus.  Describes the developmental stage following the successful embedding of a fertilized egg in the uterine wall. | Example: Postimplantation development is a critical phase in embryonic growth.  植入后发育是胚胎发育的关键阶段。|近义词：post-implantation, post-nidation |反义词：preimplantation, pre-nidation
