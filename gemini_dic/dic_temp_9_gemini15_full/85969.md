headrope:/ˈhɛdroʊp/| n.|船首缆绳；船头缆绳|A rope used to secure the bow of a ship or boat to a dock or other object.  It's typically one of the first lines secured when mooring and one of the last to be released when departing.|Example: The sailor carefully secured the headrope to the bollard before leaving the boat.|近义词：bowline, mooring line|反义词：stern rope
