holist:/ˈhəʊlɪst/ | adj. |整体论者;整体主义的 |Holistic refers to the idea that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts.  It emphasizes interconnectedness and the importance of considering all aspects of a system or organism, rather than focusing on individual components.  For example, a holistic approach to healthcare considers the patient's physical, mental, and emotional well-being, not just their symptoms. |例句：She takes a holistic approach to parenting, considering the child's emotional, social, and intellectual development. 她采取一种整体的育儿方式，考虑到孩子的感情、社会和智力发展。  The doctor recommended a holistic treatment plan that includes both medication and lifestyle changes. 医生推荐了一个整体的治疗方案，包括药物和生活方式的改变。|近义词：integrated, comprehensive, synergistic |反义词：reductionist, fragmented, atomistic |词性: adjective
