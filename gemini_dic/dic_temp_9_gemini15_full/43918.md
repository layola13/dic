crevicing:/ˈkrɛvɪsɪŋ/| n.|裂缝的形成；裂缝处理|The process of forming or filling crevices.  The action of creating or repairing cracks or fissures in something.|例句：The crevicing of the rock face was caused by erosion. 岩石表面的裂缝是由侵蚀造成的。

例句：The gardener carefully addressed the crevicing in the brick wall to prevent water damage. 园丁仔细地处理了砖墙上的裂缝，以防止水损坏。


近义词：fissuring, cracking


反义词：smoothing, sealing


词性：名词
