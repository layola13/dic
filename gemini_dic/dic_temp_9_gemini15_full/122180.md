misty-eyed:/ˈmɪsti aɪd/| adj.|含泪的，泪汪汪的，感伤的|Having eyes that are slightly wet with tears, often because of strong emotion such as sadness, joy, or nostalgia.  It suggests a gentle, tender sadness rather than outright weeping. |例句：She looked at the old photos with misty eyes. 她含泪看着旧照片。

例句：The reunion brought a misty-eyed smile to her face.  这次重逢让她脸上露出了含泪的微笑。


近义词：tearful, weepy, sentimental, emotional

反义词：dry-eyed, unemotional, apathetic

词性：形容词
