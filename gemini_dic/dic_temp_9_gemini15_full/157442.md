piri-piri: /ˌpɪriˈpɪri/ | adj. n. |皮里皮里（一种辣椒）；辛辣的 |  A type of small, very hot chili pepper from Africa, also known as African bird's eye chili.  It is used to make peri-peri sauce.  The word is also used to describe something spicy or hot. |例句：The peri-peri chicken was incredibly spicy.  这道皮里皮里鸡非常辣。


The peri-peri sauce had a fiery kick.  这皮里皮里酱汁辣劲十足。|近义词：spicy, hot, pungent |反义词：mild, bland

词源:
The word "piri-piri" originates from the Swahili language of East Africa.  It's an onomatopoeic word, imitating the sound of sizzling.  Its use spread with the spread of the chili pepper itself throughout Africa and then globally.
