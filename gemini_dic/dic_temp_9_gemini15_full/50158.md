deny:/dɪˈnaɪ/ | vt. |否认，拒绝 | To state that something is not true; to refuse to give or grant something. |例句：He denied all knowledge of the affair. 他否认对这件事件有任何了解。|例句：The bank denied his application for a loan. 银行拒绝了他的贷款申请。|近义词：refute, contradict, reject |反义词：affirm, admit, concede |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He denies the allegations. = 他否认这些指控。
They deny access to the building. = 他们拒绝进入该建筑物。


一般过去时:
She denied seeing the suspect. = 她否认见过嫌疑人。
The court denied his appeal. = 法院驳回了他的上诉。


现在完成时:
He has denied any involvement in the crime. = 他否认参与了这起犯罪。
They have denied him his rights. = 他们剥夺了他的权利。


过去完成时:
He had denied the truth for years. = 他多年来一直否认真相。
She had denied him everything he wanted. = 她拒绝了他想要的一切。


将来时:
He will deny the accusations until the end. = 他会一直否认这些指控到最后。
They will deny entry to anyone without a ticket. = 他们将拒绝任何没有票的人入场。


虚拟语气:
If he denied the evidence, he would be lying. = 如果他否认证据，他就是在撒谎。
I wish he denied the rumors. = 我希望他否认这些谣言。


被动语态:
His claims were denied by the authorities. = 他的说法被当局否认了。
Access to the information was denied to the public. = 公众被拒绝获取这些信息。


疑问句:
Does he deny his involvement? = 他是否否认他的参与？
Will they deny the request? = 他们会拒绝这个请求吗？


否定句:
He doesn't deny the facts. = 他没有否认事实。
They didn't deny the possibility. = 他们没有否认这种可能性。


PHRASE->
deny oneself = 克己，节制
deny access to = 拒绝...进入
deny the existence of = 否认...的存在
cannot deny that = 不可否认的是
deny responsibility for = 否认对...负责
