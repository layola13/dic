gang:/ɡæŋ/| n.|帮派，团伙|A gang is a group of people, often criminals, who associate together for mutual support and protection.  They often engage in criminal activities.|The gang terrorized the neighborhood.|近义词：crew, mob, clique|反义词：individual, loner|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gang robs banks. = 这个帮派抢劫银行。
That gang controls the drug trade. = 那个帮派控制着毒品交易。

一般过去时:
The gang escaped after the robbery. = 抢劫后，该帮派逃跑了。
The police arrested the entire gang. = 警方逮捕了整个帮派。


现在完成时:
The gang has committed numerous crimes. = 该帮派犯下了无数罪行。
The police have been investigating the gang for months. = 警方已经调查该帮派数月了。


过去完成时:
The police had already surrounded the building before the gang arrived. = 在该帮派到达之前，警方已经包围了大楼。
The gang had planned the heist for weeks. = 该帮派策划了数周的抢劫案。


将来时:
The gang will be arrested soon. = 该帮派很快就会被逮捕。
The police believe the gang will strike again. = 警方认为该帮派还会再次作案。


虚拟语气:
If the gang were caught, they would face serious consequences. = 如果该帮派被抓，他们将面临严重的后果。
I wish the gang would disappear. = 我希望这个帮派消失。


被动语态:
The bank was robbed by the gang. = 银行被该帮派抢劫了。
The gang members were sentenced to long prison terms. = 该帮派成员被判处长期监禁。


疑问句:
Was the gang involved in the murder? = 该帮派是否参与了谋杀案？
Who is the leader of the gang? = 该帮派的领导者是谁？


否定句:
The gang did not commit the crime. = 该帮派没有犯下罪行。
The police have not yet identified all the gang members. = 警方尚未确认所有帮派成员。
