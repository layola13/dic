humidifiers:/ˈhjuːmɪdaɪzərz/ | n. |加湿器 |A humidifier is an appliance that adds moisture to the air.  It is often used in dry climates or during winter months to improve air quality and prevent respiratory problems. |Example:  We bought a humidifier to combat the dry air in our apartment. 我们买了一个加湿器来对抗我们公寓干燥的空气。

The doctor recommended using a humidifier for my dry cough. 医生建议我用加湿器治疗干咳。|近义词：moisturizer (in some contexts)|反义词：dehumidifier |名词
