nonaromatic:/ˌnɒnˌæroʊˈmætɪk/| adj. |非芳香族的；非芳香性的 |Not possessing the properties of an aromatic compound; lacking a delocalized pi electron system in a cyclic structure.  Nonaromatic compounds do not follow Hückel's rule. |例句：Cyclohexane is a nonaromatic compound. 环己烷是一种非芳香族化合物。

例句：Many saturated hydrocarbons are nonaromatic. 许多饱和烃是非芳香族的。


词源: non- (否定前缀) + aromatic (芳香族的)


近义词：aliphatic (脂肪族的) (in some contexts)


反义词：aromatic (芳香族的)
