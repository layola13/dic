muschelkalk:/ˈmʊʃəlˌkɑːlk/| n. |壳灰岩；泥灰岩|A type of Triassic sedimentary rock, typically composed of limestone and marl, characterized by its distinctive shelly fossils.  It is named after the abundant shells found within it. Muschelkalk is an important geological formation in central Europe. |The Muschelkalk formation in Germany is rich in fossils.|近义词：limestone, marl|反义词:无
