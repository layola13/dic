dispersals: /dɪˈspɜːrsəlz/ | n. | 散布；传播；驱散 | The act of dispersing or the state of being dispersed;  the scattering or spreading of something over a wide area.  It often refers to the movement of people, animals, or seeds. |例句：The dispersals of seeds by the wind ensured the plant's survival. 风力传播种子确保了植物的生存。

The dispersals of refugees across the country put a strain on resources. 难民在全国各地的分散给资源带来了压力。|近义词：scattering, dissemination, distribution |反义词：concentration, gathering, accumulation |
