juliet:/ˈdʒuːliət/| n. |朱丽叶|Juliet is a female given name of French origin, ultimately derived from the Roman name Julia.  It is famously associated with Shakespeare's play Romeo and Juliet, where Juliet is the heroine. The name evokes imagery of romance, beauty, and tragedy.|Example:  Juliet Capulet is a well-known character in literature. 朱丽叶·卡普莱特是文学中一个广为人知的人物。|近义词:None (proper noun)|反义词:None (proper noun)|名词
