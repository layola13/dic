creditors:/ˈkrɛdɪtərz/| n. |债权人，债主 |People or organizations to whom money is owed.  They are entitled to receive payment from a debtor. |例句：The company is struggling to pay its creditors. 这家公司正在努力偿还债务。|例句：The creditors filed a lawsuit against the bankrupt business. 债权人对破产企业提起诉讼。|近义词：lenders, claimants |反义词：debtors


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The creditors demand repayment. = 债权人要求偿还债务。
Our creditors regularly review our financial statements. = 我们的债权人定期审查我们的财务报表。

一般过去时:
The creditors accepted a reduced payment. = 债权人接受了减少的付款。
The creditors investigated the company's finances. = 债权人调查了公司的财务状况。

现在完成时:
The creditors have filed a claim against the company. = 债权人已对该公司提出索赔。
The creditors have shown patience in waiting for payment. = 债权人在等待付款方面表现出耐心。

过去完成时:
The creditors had already warned the company about its debt. = 债权人已经警告该公司其债务问题。
Before the bankruptcy, the creditors had tried to negotiate a settlement. = 破产前，债权人曾试图协商解决办法。

将来时:
The creditors will likely pursue legal action. = 债权人可能会采取法律行动。
The creditors will review the proposed restructuring plan. = 债权人将审查拟议的重组计划。

虚拟语气:
If the creditors were more lenient, the company might survive. = 如果债权人更宽容一些，公司或许能生存下来。
I wish the creditors understood our difficult situation. = 我希望债权人能理解我们的困境。

被动语态:
The company's assets were seized by the creditors. = 公司的资产被债权人扣押了。
A payment plan is being negotiated with the creditors. = 正在与债权人协商付款计划。

疑问句:
Who are the company's main creditors? = 该公司的主要债权人是谁？
Have all the creditors been notified of the bankruptcy? = 所有债权人都收到破产通知了吗？

否定句:
The company did not pay its creditors on time. = 公司没有按时支付债权人款项。
The creditors were not satisfied with the explanation. = 债权人对解释并不满意。
