pauldron:/ˈpɔːldʒən/| n.|肩甲,护肩|A pauldrons is a piece of armor that protects the shoulder.  It is typically part of a suit of armor, often extending to cover part of the upper arm.  Pauldrons can be made of various materials, including metal, leather, and even cloth, depending on their purpose and the era in which they were made.|例句：The knight wore shining pauldrons, reflecting the sunlight.  这名骑士穿着闪亮的肩甲，反射着阳光。

The soldier's pauldrons were heavily damaged in the battle.  士兵的护肩在战斗中严重受损。|近义词：shoulder piece, shoulder guard|反义词：None
