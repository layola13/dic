hoaxes:/ˈhoʊksɪz/| n.| 骗局；恶作剧 | A hoax is a deliberate deception intended to trick or fool someone, often for amusement or to cause embarrassment or damage.  It can involve false information, fabricated evidence, or a staged event. | Example:  The moon landing was once a popular hoax theory.  月球登陆曾经是一个流行的骗局理论。

The news report about aliens landing in the town was a complete hoax.  关于外星人降落在城镇的消息报道是一个彻头彻尾的骗局。

|近义词：trick, deception, fraud, sham, fabrication |反义词：truth, reality, fact |名词
