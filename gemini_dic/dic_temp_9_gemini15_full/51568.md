deuteropathic:/ˌdjuːtərəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|继发的；继发性的；继发病的|Relating to or caused by a secondary disease or condition.  It describes a disease or condition that arises as a consequence of a primary disease or condition, rather than being the initial problem.  The term often appears in medical contexts.|例句：The patient's fatigue was a deuteropathic symptom, a secondary manifestation of her underlying heart condition.  患者的疲劳是一种继发性症状，是她潜在心脏疾病的继发表现。|近义词：secondary, consequential, derivative|反义词：primary, initial, original
