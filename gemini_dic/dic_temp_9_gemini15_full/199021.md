signaller:/ˈsɪɡnələr/| n. |信号员，发信号者 | A person who gives signals, especially in the armed forces or on railways.  | The signaller relayed the urgent message to headquarters. 信号员将紧急信息转达给了总部。 | signaler, messenger | receiver | n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The signaller sends signals regularly. = 信号员定期发送信号。
He signals the train to stop. = 他发出信号让火车停下来。


一般过去时:
The signaller sent a warning signal. = 信号员发出警告信号。
She signalled the approaching train. = 她向即将到来的火车发出了信号。


现在完成时:
The signaller has sent many signals today. = 信号员今天已经发送了很多信号。
He has successfully signalled the emergency. = 他已经成功地发出了紧急信号。


过去完成时:
The signaller had already sent the signal before the incident. = 在事故发生之前，信号员已经发出信号了。
She had signalled the danger several times. = 她已经几次发出危险信号了。


将来时:
The signaller will send a signal at dawn. = 信号员将在黎明时分发送信号。
He will signal the start of the race. = 他将发出比赛开始的信号。


虚拟语气:
If the signaller had sent the signal earlier, the accident could have been avoided. = 如果信号员早点发出信号，事故本来是可以避免的。
I wish the signaller had warned us sooner. = 我希望信号员早点警告我们。


被动语态:
The signal was sent by the signaller. = 信号是由信号员发出的。
The emergency was signalled effectively. = 紧急情况得到了有效的信号传递。


疑问句:
Did the signaller send the correct signal? = 信号员发出的是正确的信号吗？
Who signalled the train to stop? = 谁发出信号让火车停下来的？


否定句:
The signaller didn't send the signal on time. = 信号员没有按时发送信号。
He didn't signal the approaching danger. = 他没有发出即将到来的危险信号。
