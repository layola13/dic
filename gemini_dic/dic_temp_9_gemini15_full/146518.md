overgentle: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛntl/ | adj. | 过分温柔的，过于温和的 | Excessively gentle; too kind or mild.  This word is not commonly used and is considered somewhat informal. It implies a gentleness that is excessive to the point of being ineffective or even detrimental. | 例句：His overgentle approach to discipline didn't work with the unruly children. = 他过于温和的管教方式对那些不听话的孩子不起作用。  His overgentle handling of the situation only prolonged the problem. = 他过于温和的处理方式只会延长问题。 | 近义词：excessively gentle, overly kind, too soft,  | 反义词：harsh, severe, strict, firm
