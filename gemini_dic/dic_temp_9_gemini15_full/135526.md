nonpleadingly: /ˌnɒnˈpliːdɪŋli/ | adv. | 不恳求地；不哀求地 |  Not in a pleading or begging manner; without entreaty.  |  He looked at her nonpleadingly, his eyes conveying a quiet strength. 他不卑不亢地看着她，眼神里流露出一种平静的力量。 | 近义词: impassively, calmly, stoically | 反义词: pleadingly, beseechingly

USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" example.  The usage focuses on how an action is performed.

Example Sentences:

She spoke nonpleadingly, stating her request clearly and directly. 她不卑不亢地说话，清楚而直接地表达了她的请求。

He accepted the punishment nonpleadingly, showing no sign of remorse or fear. 他不卑不亢地接受惩罚，没有表现出任何悔恨或恐惧。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "nonpleadingly."  Its usage is typically within a sentence describing manner.
