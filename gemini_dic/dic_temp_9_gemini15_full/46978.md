darwinian:/dɑːrˈwɪniən/| adj.|达尔文的；达尔文主义的|Relating to or characteristic of Charles Darwin or his theories, especially the theory of evolution by natural selection.|例句：His ideas were highly controversial at the time, challenging the prevailing darwinian views. 他的想法当时极具争议，挑战了盛行的达尔文主义观点。|例句：The darwinian principle of "survival of the fittest" is often misunderstood. 达尔文的“适者生存”原则经常被误解。|近义词：evolutionary, biological|反义词：creationist, Lamarckian
