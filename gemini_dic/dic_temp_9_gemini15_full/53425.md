disappeared:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪərd/| v.|消失，不见了|To cease to be visible, to vanish from sight or existence.  It implies a complete and often sudden loss from view or knowledge.|例句：The magician disappeared in a puff of smoke. 魔术师在一阵烟雾中消失了。

例句：The child disappeared from the playground. 孩子在操场上消失了。


近义词：vanished, faded, evaporated, gone, lost|反义词：appeared, emerged, materialized|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun disappears below the horizon every evening. 太阳每天晚上都消失在地平线以下。
He disappears into the crowd easily. 他很容易消失在人群中。


一般过去时:
My keys disappeared from my bag. 我的钥匙从包里不见了。
The plane disappeared from radar. 飞机从雷达上消失了。


现在完成时:
The missing documents have disappeared without a trace. 丢失的文件已无影无踪。
My favorite pen has disappeared again. 我最喜欢的钢笔又不见了。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, the snow had disappeared. 我们到达时，雪已经融化了。
The evidence had disappeared before the police arrived.  警方到达之前证据就消失了。


将来时:
The snow will disappear as the weather warms up. 天气暖和起来后，雪就会融化。
The pain will disappear eventually. 疼痛最终会消失。


虚拟语气:
If the problem disappeared, everything would be perfect. 如果问题消失了，一切都会完美。
I wish the headache would disappear. 我希望头痛会消失。


被动语态:
The painting was discovered after it had disappeared for many years.  这幅画在消失多年后被发现了。
The evidence was carefully hidden so that it would not be easily disappeared. 证据被小心地隐藏起来，以免轻易被发现。


疑问句:
Where did the car disappear to?  车消失到哪里去了？
Has the pain disappeared yet? 疼痛消失了没有？


否定句:
The symptoms didn't disappear overnight. 症状并没有一夜之间消失。
The problem hasn't disappeared yet, despite our efforts. 尽管我们努力了，但问题还没有解决。
