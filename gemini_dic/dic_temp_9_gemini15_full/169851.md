proselytizers:/ˈprɒsɪlaɪzərz/| n. |传教者；积极拉拢者|People who attempt to convert others to their religion or belief system. They actively seek to persuade others to adopt their own faith or ideology.  |The proselytizers distributed pamphlets on the street corner, hoping to attract new members to their church. 传教者们在街角分发传单，希望能吸引新成员加入他们的教堂。|近义词：missionaries, evangelists, converts, recruiters|反义词：apostates, skeptics
