cresting:/ˈkrestɪŋ/ | v. |达到顶峰；达到高潮；形成浪峰；加冠于|Reaching a peak or high point; forming a crest or wave peak; adding a crest to.|The bird was cresting the mountain peak. 那只鸟正在飞越山峰。 The waves were cresting over the rocks. 海浪拍打着岩石。|近义词：peaking, culminating, topping|反义词：bottoming, declining, falling


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The river is cresting after the heavy rains. = 大雨过后，河水达到最高水位。
He is cresting the hill on his bike. = 他骑自行车到达山顶。


一般过去时:
The flood waters crested yesterday. = 洪水昨天达到最高水位。
The stock market crested last week. = 股市上周达到顶峰。


现在完成时:
The river has crested and is now receding. = 河水已达到最高水位，现在正在下降。
The project has crested in terms of its complexity. = 项目在复杂性方面已达到顶峰。


过去完成时:
The river had crested before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，河水已经达到最高水位。
The excitement had crested before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，兴奋已经达到顶峰。


将来时:
The river will crest tomorrow morning. = 河水将在明天早上达到最高水位。
The balloon will crest over the trees. = 气球将会飞过树梢。


虚拟语气:
If the river crested, we would have to evacuate. = 如果河水达到最高水位，我们就必须撤离。
If the stock market had crested earlier, we would have sold. = 如果股市早些达到顶峰，我们就应该卖出。


被动语态:
The waves were crested by the strong wind. = 巨浪是由强风造成的。(这里“cresting”是被动语态的特殊用法，指浪峰的形成)
The mountain peak was crested by the climbers. = 登山者到达了山峰顶。(这里也指达到顶峰)


疑问句:
Has the river crested yet? = 河水达到最高水位了吗？
Will the project crest next month? = 项目下个月会达到顶峰吗？


否定句:
The river hasn't crested yet. = 河水还没有达到最高水位。
The project hasn't crested yet. = 项目还没有达到顶峰。



PHRASE->

crest of a wave: 浪峰
crest a hill: 爬上山顶
crest of a mountain: 山顶
crest of a sound:  声音的高潮
crestfallen: 垂头丧气的 (注意：这是一个形容词，而非短语)
