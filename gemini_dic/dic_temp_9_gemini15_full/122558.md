modems:/ˈmoʊdəmz/| n.|调制解调器|A modem is a device that modulates an analog carrier signal to encode digital information, and also demodulates such a carrier signal to decode the transmitted information.  Modems are used to transmit digital data over analog lines such as telephone lines. |Example: My internet connection is slow because my modem is outdated. 我的网络连接很慢，因为我的调制解调器过时了。


The new modem improved our internet speed significantly. 新的调制解调器显著提高了我们的网速。|近义词：data transmitter, communication device |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词

词源:
The word "modem" is a portmanteau of "modulator-demodulator".  It combines the functions of modulation (converting digital signals to analog for transmission) and demodulation (converting analog signals back to digital for reception).
