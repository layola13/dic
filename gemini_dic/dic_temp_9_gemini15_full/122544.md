modeling:/ˈmɒd(ə)lɪŋ/| n.| 建模；造型；塑造；模仿；样式 |The act of creating a model; the process of representing something in a simplified or abstract form.  It can refer to creating physical models, mathematical models, or fashion modeling.|例句：The architect presented a detailed modeling of the new building. 建筑师展示了这座新建筑的详细模型。|例句：Her modeling career took off after she won the beauty contest. 她赢得选美比赛后，模特生涯腾飞了。|近义词：modelling, simulation, representation, imitation|反义词：reality, actuality|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is modeling a new product. = 公司正在为一款新产品建模。
She is doing fashion modeling. = 她从事时尚模特工作。


一般过去时:
They modeled the airplane in clay. = 他们用粘土制作了飞机模型。
He modeled for a famous photographer. = 他为一位著名的摄影师做模特。


现在完成时:
We have modeled several scenarios. = 我们已经模拟了几种情况。
She has been modeling for five years. = 她做模特已经五年了。


过去完成时:
They had modeled the system before the test. = 他们测试前已经对系统进行了建模。
She had modeled for many designers before becoming famous. = 在成名之前，她已经为许多设计师做过模特。


将来时:
We will model the impact of climate change. = 我们将模拟气候变化的影响。
He will be modeling in Milan next month. = 他下个月将在米兰做模特。


虚拟语气:
If we had better data, we could model the situation more accurately. = 如果我们有更好的数据，我们就能更准确地模拟这种情况。
If she were taller, she would be a better fashion model.  = 如果她更高一点，她会成为一个更好的时尚模特。


被动语态:
The data was modeled using a statistical software. = 数据是使用统计软件建模的。
The building's design was modeled after a famous cathedral. = 建筑的设计模仿了一座著名的教堂。


疑问句:
What are you modeling today? = 你今天在建模什么？
Is she still modeling? = 她还在做模特吗？


否定句:
They did not model the system correctly. = 他们没有正确地对系统进行建模。
She doesn't want to model anymore. = 她不想再做模特了。
