premonetary:/ˌpriːˈmʌnɪtəri/| adj.|货币化之前的；先于货币制度的 | Relating to or existing before the use of money.  This adjective describes a period or system that existed before the widespread adoption of money as a medium of exchange.  It often implies a barter system or other non-monetary forms of economic exchange. | Example: Premonetary societies relied on barter for trade.  先于货币制度的社会依靠物物交换进行贸易。|近义词：pre-currency, non-monetary |反义词：monetary
