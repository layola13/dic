monographist:/ˈmɒnəɡræfɪst/ | n. | 专著作者；论文作者 | A person who writes monographs.  A monographist specializes in in-depth studies of a single subject or a narrow field of study, producing detailed and comprehensive works. |  He is a renowned monographist on the life and works of Shakespeare. 他是莎士比亚生平和作品研究方面的著名专著作者。|近义词：author, scholar, specialist |反义词：generalist
