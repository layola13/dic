ottar:/ˈɒtɑːr/ | n. |海豹油；水獭；|Old Norse word referring to sea otter, later also used for river otter.  The word has its roots in Old Norse and has evolved through various languages.  It is now mainly used as a historical term or in specialized contexts like zoology.|The Vikings used otar to describe the sea otter.|近义词：sea otter (for the sea mammal),  river otter (for the freshwater mammal)|反义词：无
