cutesiest:/ˈkjuːtiəst/| adj.|最可爱的|Superlative form of "cute," indicating the highest degree of cuteness.  It describes something or someone that is extremely charming, endearing, and attractive in a sweet and innocent way.  |例句：That kitten is the cutesiest thing I've ever seen! 那只小猫是我见过的最可爱的东西！

That baby's laugh is the cutesiest sound. 那个婴儿的笑声是最可爱的声音。|近义词：adorable, darling, sweet, charming, endearing|反义词：ugly, repulsive, unpleasant, hideous
