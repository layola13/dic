mounter:/ˈmaʊntər/| n. v.|安装者；安装工；安装器|A person or thing that mounts something.  Someone who installs or fits something. A device used for mounting.|例句：He's a skilled mounter of electronic components. 他是一位熟练的电子元件安装工。  The picture mounter is easy to use.  这个图片安装器很容易使用。|近义词：installer, fitter, fixer |反义词：dismantler, remover
