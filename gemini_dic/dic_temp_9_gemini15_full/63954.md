episcopate:/ˈɛpɪskəpeɪt/| n. |主教职位；主教的任期；主教全体|The period of time a bishop holds office;  the office or dignity of a bishop; the body of bishops.  The episcopate is a crucial part of the church's governance.|例句：His episcopate was marked by significant reforms. 他的主教任期以重大改革为标志。|例句：The entire episcopate met to discuss the matter. 全体主教聚会讨论此事。|近义词：bishopric, prelacy|反义词：layman (反义词并非完全对等，layman 指的是非神职人员)|名词
