destin: /ˈdɛstɪn/ | n. | 天命；命运；目的地 |  The inherent course of events; fate.  A place to which someone or something is going or being sent. | 例句：It was her destin to become a great artist.  =  成为伟大的艺术家是她的命运。

例句：The final destin of the package is unknown. =包裹的最终目的地不明。


近义词：fate, destiny, lot, end, goal, objective | 反义词：chance, accident, coincidence | 词性：名词
