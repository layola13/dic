lovelorn: /ˈlʌvˌlɔːrn/ | adj. | 渴望爱情的；失恋的；相思病的 |  Sadly longing for love, especially after a romantic loss.  The word implies a state of sadness and yearning due to unrequited love or the loss of a loved one. |

例句：
She became lovelorn after her boyfriend left her. = 她男友离开后变得非常思念他。

He wrote a lovelorn poem about his lost love. = 他写了一首关于失去爱情的相思诗。


近义词：lovesick, heartbroken, forlorn, despondent

反义词：joyful, elated, happy, contented


词性：形容词
