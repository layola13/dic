enacting:/ɪˈnæktɪŋ/ | v. | 制定；颁布；扮演；演出 |  The act of putting a law or plan into effect; to represent or perform (a role or part).  | 例句：The government is enacting new environmental regulations.  政府正在制定新的环境法规。

The play involved enacting a historical battle scene. 这出戏包含了再现一场历史战役的场景。


近义词：implementing, enforcing, performing, staging | 反义词：repealing, abolishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are enacting a new law to protect wildlife. = 他们正在制定一项保护野生动物的新法律。
The theatre group is enacting Shakespeare's Hamlet. = 剧团正在演出莎士比亚的《哈姆雷特》。


一般过去时:
Congress enacted the Civil Rights Act of 1964. = 国会通过了1964年的民权法案。
The actors enacted the scene with great passion. = 演员们充满激情地演绎了这个场景。


现在完成时:
They have enacted several new laws this year. = 他们今年已经颁布了几项新法律。
She has enacted many different roles throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她扮演过许多不同的角色。


过去完成时:
They had enacted the emergency measures before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经实施了紧急措施。
The director had enacted the scene many times before the final performance. = 在最终演出之前，导演已经排练过很多次这个场景。


将来时:
They will enact a new tax law next year. = 他们明年将实施一项新的税收法律。
The students will enact a play for the school festival. = 学生们将为学校节日演出一个戏剧。


虚拟语气:
If they enacted stricter rules, the problem might be solved. = 如果他们制定更严格的规定，这个问题也许就能解决。
If he had enacted the role differently, the audience would have reacted differently. = 如果他以不同的方式扮演这个角色，观众的反应就会不同。


被动语态:
The bill was enacted by the Senate last week. = 该法案在上周由参议院通过。
The play was enacted with great skill by the students. = 这部戏是由学生们巧妙地演出的。


疑问句:
When will the new regulations be enacted? = 新规章何时会颁布？
Who will be enacting the lead role in the play? = 谁将在剧中扮演主角？


否定句:
The government did not enact the proposed legislation. = 政府没有颁布拟议中的立法。
He did not enact the role convincingly. = 他没有令人信服地扮演这个角色。
