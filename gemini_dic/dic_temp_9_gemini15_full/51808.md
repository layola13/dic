dew:/duː/| n.|露水，朝露|Water that has condensed on a cool surface, especially at night.|The grass was covered in dew.  草地上覆盖着露水。|The leaves glistened with dew. 叶子闪耀着露水。|近义词：condensation, moisture|反义词：dryness


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->
a drop of dew 一滴露水
morning dew  晨露
heavy dew  浓重的露水
pearls of dew 露珠
point of dew 露点
