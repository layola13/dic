petrifying: /ˈpɛtrɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. |令人恐惧的；令人震惊的；使人石化的|  Something that is petrifying is extremely frightening or shocking. It can also refer to the process of turning something into stone. |

英文解析: The word "petrifying" derives from the verb "petrify," meaning to turn to stone or to be extremely frightened.  As an adjective, it describes something that causes intense fear or shock.
