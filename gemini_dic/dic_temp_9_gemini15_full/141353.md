operate:/ˈɒpəreɪt/ /ˈɑːpəreɪt/| vt. vi. n.|操作；运转；经营；手术；起作用|To control the functioning of a machine or process. To perform a surgical procedure. To conduct business. To be in effect or action.|例句：The machine is easy to operate. 这台机器很容易操作。

The surgeon will operate tomorrow morning. 外科医生将在明天上午动手术。


近义词：function, run, manage, work|反义词：idle, cease, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He operates the machine every day. 他每天操作这台机器。
She operates a successful bakery. 她经营着一家成功的面包店。

一般过去时:
They operated the equipment carefully. 他们小心地操作了设备。
The company operated at a loss last year. 公司去年亏损经营。

现在完成时:
I have operated this software for years. 我已经使用这个软件很多年了。
She has successfully operated on many patients. 她已经成功地为许多病人做了手术。

过去完成时:
He had operated the crane for ten years before the accident. 事故发生前，他已经操作起重机十年了。
The factory had operated at full capacity before the strike. 罢工前，工厂一直满负荷运转。

将来时:
We will operate the new system next week. 我们下周将操作新系统。
The hospital will operate on him as soon as possible. 医院将尽快为他手术。

虚拟语气:
If I operated the machine correctly, it would work properly. 如果我正确操作机器，它就会正常工作。
If he had operated more carefully, the accident wouldn't have happened. 如果他操作更仔细一些，事故就不会发生了。

被动语态:
The machine is operated by a skilled technician. 这台机器由一名熟练的技术人员操作。
The company is operated by a family. 这家公司由一个家族经营。

疑问句:
How does this machine operate? 这台机器是如何操作的？
Does this business operate profitably? 这家企业盈利吗？

否定句:
The machine is not operating correctly. 机器运转不正常。
The factory did not operate over the weekend. 工厂周末没有运转。


PHRASE->
