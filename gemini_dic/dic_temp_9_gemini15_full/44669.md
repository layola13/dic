crucification:/ˌkruːsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |钉死在十字架上；残酷的折磨；彻底的失败|The act of crucifying someone; the act of inflicting intense suffering or criticism;  a complete failure.|例句：The crucifixion of Jesus is a central event in Christianity.  耶稣的受难是基督教的核心事件。

例句：His public criticism was a crucifixion of his reputation. 他公开的批评彻底毁了他的名声。


近义词：execution, torment, destruction, decimation|反义词：salvation, liberation, success, triumph|词性：名词
