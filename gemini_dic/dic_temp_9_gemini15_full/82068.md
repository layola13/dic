grittily:/ˈɡrɪtɪli/ | adv. | 坚毅地；毅然决然地；粗糙地；沙哑地 |  In a gritty manner; with grit.  This adverb describes actions or speech performed with determination and resilience, often in the face of difficulty, or in a harsh, rough way. It can also describe a sound, typically a voice, as rough and gravelly. | 例句：He spoke grittily about his struggles. 他坚毅地谈论了他的挣扎。

She grittily refused to give up. 她毅然决然地拒绝放弃。


近义词：determinedly, resolutely, stubbornly, harshly, roughly | 反义词：weakly, timidly, gently, softly
