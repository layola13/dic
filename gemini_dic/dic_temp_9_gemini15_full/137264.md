nonsymbolicalness:/ˌnɒnˌsɪmˈbɒlɪkəlnəs/| n. |非象征性，非象征意义|The lack of symbolic meaning or representation; the quality or state of being nonsymbolical.  It refers to something that does not use symbols to convey meaning or represent something else.  For example, a purely literal description of an event lacks nonsymbolicalness. |The nonsymbolicalness of the painting made it difficult to interpret.|近义词：literalness, concreteness|反义词：symbolism, figurativeness|名词
