headier:/ˈhedɪər/ | adj. |令人头晕的；令人兴奋的；浓烈的；醉人的 |  More potent or intoxicating; causing dizziness or exhilaration.  Headier refers to something that is stronger, more intense, or more intoxicating, whether literally (like a drink) or figuratively (like an experience). |例句：The headier scent of the roses filled the air.  玫瑰花更浓烈的香味充满了空气。

The headier wine made her feel lightheaded. 这更烈的酒让她感到头晕。

近义词：stronger, more potent, intoxicating, exhilarating, heady | 反义词：mild, weak, sobering, dull
