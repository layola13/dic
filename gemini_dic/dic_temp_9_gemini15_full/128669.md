nicotianine:/ˌnɪkəˈtiːneɪn/| n.|烟碱类生物碱|A group of alkaloids found in tobacco plants, related to nicotine.  They are often less toxic than nicotine but still exhibit some biological activity.|Nicotianine compounds are found in various parts of the tobacco plant and contribute to its complex chemical profile. |近义词：nicotine alkaloid, tobacco alkaloid|反义词：无
