downgrades:/ˈdaʊnɡreɪdz/| vt. n.|降级；降低；贬低|To reduce something to a lower grade, rank, or level; to lower the quality, value, or status of something.|例句：The company downgraded its profit forecast. 公司下调了盈利预测。|例句：The hotel downgraded my room without notice. 酒店未经通知就将我的房间降级了。|近义词：demote, reduce, devalue|反义词：upgrade, promote, enhance|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They downgrade the software regularly. = 他们定期对软件进行降级。
The airline downgrades passengers to economy class. = 航空公司将乘客降级到经济舱。


一般过去时:
The government downgraded the country's credit rating. = 政府下调了该国的信用评级。
He was downgraded to a lower position. = 他被降职到一个较低的职位。


现在完成时:
They have downgraded the security protocols. = 他们已经降低了安全协议的等级。
The company has downgraded its expectations for the quarter. = 公司已经下调了对本季度的预期。


过去完成时:
They had downgraded the system before the failure. = 他们曾在故障发生前对系统进行了降级。
The manager had downgraded the employee's performance review. = 经理已经下调了员工的业绩评估。


将来时:
They will downgrade the service if the usage decreases. = 如果使用量减少，他们将降低服务级别。
The company will downgrade its products to meet the new standards. = 公司将降低其产品等级以符合新的标准。


虚拟语气:
If they downgraded the software, we would have more problems. = 如果他们降低软件版本，我们将会有更多的问题。
I wish they hadn't downgraded my account. = 我希望他们没有降低我的账户等级。


被动语态:
The software was downgraded last week. = 软件上周被降级了。
His position was downgraded due to poor performance. = 由于表现不佳，他的职位被降级了。


疑问句:
Will they downgrade the system? = 他们会降级系统吗？
Has the software been downgraded? = 软件是否已被降级？


否定句:
They did not downgrade the product. = 他们没有降低产品的等级。
The company will not downgrade its service. = 公司不会降低其服务等级。



PHRASE->
downgrade to = 降级到…
downgrade from = 从…降级
downgrade in = 在…方面降级
downgrade the risk = 降低风险
downgrade the impact = 降低影响
