cocultivated:/ˌkoʊˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/| v. | 共同培养；共培养 | To cultivate (cells, tissues, or organisms) together in the same culture.  This technique is often used in biological research to study the interactions between different cells or organisms. |例句：Researchers cocultivated human immune cells with cancer cells to study their interactions. 研究人员将人体免疫细胞与癌细胞共同培养以研究它们的相互作用。

例句：The scientists cocultivated bacteria and plant cells to investigate the effects of microbial communities on plant growth. 科学家们共同培养细菌和植物细胞，以研究微生物群落对植物生长的影响。


近义词：jointly cultured, co-cultured


反义词：cultured separately, individually cultivated


词性: Verb
