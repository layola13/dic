pascha:/ˈpɑːskə/| n.|逾越节；基督教复活节|The word "Pascha" is the Greek word for Passover, the Jewish festival commemorating the liberation of the Israelites from slavery in Egypt. In Christianity, it became associated with the resurrection of Jesus Christ, and is often used interchangeably with Easter.|例句：The Pascha feast is a time of great celebration and reflection. 逾越节的盛宴是庆祝和反思的时刻。

The Paschal Lamb is a symbol of Christ's sacrifice.逾越节的羔羊是基督牺牲的象征。|近义词：Passover, Easter|反义词：无
