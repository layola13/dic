molest:/məˈlest/| vt.|骚扰；猥亵；侵犯|To annoy or disturb someone, especially by repeatedly interrupting them or by doing something that they find unpleasant.  It also refers to the sexual abuse of a child or other vulnerable person.|例句：He was constantly molested by his classmates. 他不断受到同学的骚扰。|例句：The girl was molested by a stranger.  这个女孩被一个陌生人猥亵了。|近义词：harass, annoy, abuse, assault|反义词：protect, comfort, aid
