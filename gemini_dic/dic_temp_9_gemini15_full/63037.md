enraptures: /ɪnˈræptʃərz/ | vt. | 使狂喜；使着迷 | To fill (someone) with great delight; to enchant or fascinate. | 例句：The beauty of the sunset enraptured her.  = 日落的美丽使她着迷。

The breathtaking performance enraptured the audience. = 令人惊叹的表演使观众着迷。


近义词：delight, enchant, fascinate, captivate, enthrall, charm

反义词：disappoint, bore, disenchant


词性：动词


USAGE->

The music enraptures the listener. = 音乐使听众着迷。
The scenery enraptures the tourists. = 风景使游客着迷。
