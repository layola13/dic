butte:/bjut/| n.|平顶山，孤山|A butte is an isolated hill with steep sides and a flat top, similar to but smaller than a mesa.  Buttes are formed by erosion, where a cap of resistant rock protects the underlying softer rock from being eroded away.|例句：The butte stood majestically against the desert sky. 那座平顶山雄伟地矗立在沙漠的天空下。

The butte was a prominent landmark in the landscape. 那座平顶山是这片景观中一个突出的地标。|近义词：mesa, hill, mound|反义词：plain, valley
