sextant:/ˈsekstənt/| n. |六分仪|A sextant is a navigational instrument used to measure the angle between any two visible objects.  It's primarily used to determine latitude by measuring the altitude of celestial bodies, especially the sun or stars.  |例句：The sailor used a sextant to determine their latitude at sea.  水手用六分仪确定他们在海上的纬度。

The captain relied on his sextant for accurate navigation before GPS. 船长在GPS出现之前依靠他的六分仪进行精确导航。|近义词：astrolabe (古代天文测量仪器)|反义词：None.
