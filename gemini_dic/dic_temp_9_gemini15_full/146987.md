overkeep:/ˈoʊvərkiːp/| vt.|过度保养；过度维护；|To maintain or preserve something excessively or beyond what is necessary or beneficial.  This often implies a wasteful or impractical approach.|例句：He overkeeps his classic car, spending more on maintenance than it's worth. 他过度保养他的老爷车，花费的维护费用超过了它的价值。


例句：The museum overkeeps its collection, resulting in unnecessary storage costs.  博物馆过度维护其藏品，导致不必要的仓储成本。


近义词：overmaintain, excessively maintain, pamper excessively


反义词：neglect, abandon, undermaintain


词性:动词
