saute:/soʊˈteɪ/| vt.|  翻炒；爆炒 | To cook (food, especially vegetables or meat) quickly in a little hot fat.  |例句：I sauteed the mushrooms in butter before adding them to the pasta.  我把蘑菇在黄油里翻炒一下再加到意面里。

例句：She sauteed the garlic and onions until fragrant. 她把蒜和洋葱翻炒至香。


近义词：stir-fry, fry, pan-fry| 反义词：boil, stew


USAGE->

She sautés onions and peppers for her tacos. = 她为她的玉米卷翻炒洋葱和青椒。
