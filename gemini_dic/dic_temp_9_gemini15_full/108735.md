leapfrogger:/ˈliːpfrɒɡər/| n. |跳蛙者；超越者；(尤指在发展中)快速超越者|A leapfrogger is someone or something that surpasses others rapidly, often by using innovative methods or strategies.  This term is frequently used in the context of economic development, where a country might leapfrog an intermediate stage of development to achieve a more advanced state. |Example:  China has become a leapfrogger in technological innovation, bypassing some stages of development seen in Western nations.  中国在技术创新方面已成为一个超越者，绕过了西方国家经历的一些发展阶段。|近义词：overtaker, surpasser, innovator |反义词：laggard, underachiever

一般现在时:
The company leapfrogs its competitors with its new technology. = 这家公司凭借其新技术超越了竞争对手。

一般过去时:
The small country leapfrogged its neighbors in economic growth. = 这个小国在经济增长方面超越了邻国。
She leapfrogged several grades in school. = 她跳过了学校的几个年级。


现在完成时:

They have leapfrogged the traditional methods to achieve success. = 他们跳过了传统方法取得了成功。


过去完成时:

将来时:
