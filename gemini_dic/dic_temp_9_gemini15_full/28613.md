caressing:/kəˈresɪŋ/ | v. |爱抚，抚摸 |The act of gently stroking or touching someone or something with affection or tenderness.  |例句：She was caressing the soft fur of her cat. 她温柔地抚摸着她的猫柔软的毛发。|例句：The gentle breeze caressed her face. 轻柔的微风拂过她的脸庞。|近义词：stroking, fondling, touching |反义词：hitting, slapping, striking |动词


USAGE->

He caresses his dog every evening. = 他每天晚上都抚摸他的狗。
She caresses the baby's cheek gently. = 她轻轻地抚摸着婴儿的脸颊。
