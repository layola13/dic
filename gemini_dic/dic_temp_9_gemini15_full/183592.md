requisites:/ˈrɛkwɪzɪts/| n. |必要条件，必备品，先决条件 |Things required as a prior condition; necessary things.  |The requisites for admission to the university include a high school diploma and satisfactory test scores.  大学入学的必要条件包括高中毕业证和令人满意的考试成绩。| prerequisites, essentials, necessities | non-essentials, superfluities |名词
