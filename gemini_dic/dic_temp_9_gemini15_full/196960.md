shaggy:/ˈʃæɡi/| adj.|蓬乱的；毛茸茸的；粗糙的|Having a long, thick, and unkempt appearance, like an animal's fur or a person's hair.  It can also describe a surface that is rough and uneven.|例句：The dog had a shaggy coat. 这只狗有一身蓬乱的毛。


例句：The old rug had a shaggy texture. 这块旧地毯质地粗糙。


近义词：fuzzy, hairy, woolly, unkempt, rough


反义词：smooth, sleek, neat, tidy


词性:形容词
