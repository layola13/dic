predella:/prɪˈdɛlə/| n. |小型祭坛画;前板画|A predella is a narrow panel or series of panels painted with scenes relating to the main altarpiece above it.  It is typically placed at the base of an altarpiece, often depicting scenes from the life of the saint or subject represented in the main panel. |The predella of the altarpiece depicted scenes from the life of Saint Francis.  这幅祭坛画的前板画描绘了圣方济各的一生。|altar painting, base panel|N/A|
