curates:/ˈkjʊreɪts/ | vt. | 策展；精心挑选；管理；照料 | To curate something means to select, organize, and present it, often in a museum or gallery setting, but also in other contexts like social media.  It implies careful selection and arrangement to create a specific effect or experience. | The museum curates a collection of modern art. 这个博物馆收藏了一批现代艺术作品。|  The chef curates a special menu for the holiday.  这位厨师为节日精心策划了一份特别的菜单。| selects, organizes, arranges, assembles | destroys, neglects, abandons
