importuned:/ɪmˈpɔːrtjuːnd/| vt.|恳求；纠缠；强求 |To ask someone repeatedly and urgently for something.  To persistently request or demand something.|The beggar importuned passersby for money.  乞丐向过路人乞讨钱财。|He importuned her to change her mind. 他苦苦恳求她改变主意。|beseech, implore, entreat, solicit|refuse, deny, reject|动词


USAGE->

He importunes his boss for a raise. = 他不断地向老板要求加薪。
She importunes her parents for a new car. = 她不断地向父母要一辆新车。


一般过去时:
The child importuned his mother for candy. = 孩子不断地向母亲要糖果。

He has importuned me for weeks to help him. = 他已经缠了我几周，让我帮他。
