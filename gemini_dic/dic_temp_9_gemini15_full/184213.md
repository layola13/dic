resoliciting:/ˌriːˈsɒlɪsɪtɪŋ/ | v. |再次请求；再次征求；再次索取 |The act of soliciting again; to request or seek something again.  This often implies a previous attempt that was unsuccessful or incomplete.|例句：The company is resoliciting bids for the project after the initial round proved unsatisfactory.  公司在第一轮招标不满意后，再次征求该项目的投标。


The marketing team is resoliciting feedback from customers on the new product design. 市场团队正在再次征求客户对新产品设计的反馈。|近义词：re-requesting, soliciting again, seeking again |反义词：rejecting, refusing, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are resoliciting donations for the charity. = 他们正在再次为慈善机构募捐。
He resolicitates clients every quarter. = 他每季度都再次联系客户。


一般过去时:
The museum resolicited memberships after a period of low enrollment. =  博物馆在一段时间报名人数低迷后再次征集会员。
She resolicited the job application after being rejected initially. = 她第一次申请被拒绝后再次申请了这份工作。


现在完成时:
They have resolicited funding for their research project. = 他们已经为他们的研究项目再次申请资金。
The company has resolicited bids from several contractors. = 公司已经向几家承包商再次征求报价。


过去完成时:
They had resolicited the contract before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经再次申请了合同。
She had resolicited the information before making a final decision. = 在做出最终决定之前，她已经再次索要了信息。


将来时:
The organization will resolicit volunteers for the upcoming event. = 该组织将为即将举行的活动再次征集志愿者。
He will resolicit his former clients next month. = 他下个月将再次联系他的前任客户。


虚拟语气:
If they resolicited the funds, they might be successful this time. = 如果他们再次申请资金，他们这次可能会成功。
I wish they had resolicited my opinion before making the changes. = 我希望他们在做出更改之前再次征求我的意见。


被动语态:
The proposal was resolicited due to inconsistencies. = 由于不一致，该提案被再次征求。
Bids were resolicited following the initial rejection. = 在最初的拒绝之后，再次征求了投标。


疑问句:

否定句:
They did not resolicit the contract after the initial refusal. = 在第一次被拒绝后，他们没有再次申请合同。
The company will not resolicit applications for the position. = 公司不会再次为该职位申请。
