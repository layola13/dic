freeze-framed:/friːzfreɪmd/| adj.|冻结画面；定格的|Describes a scene or moment that is stopped and held, like a still image from a video. It often implies a significant or dramatic moment.|例句：The final scene, freeze-framed on his shocked expression, left the audience speechless.  最终场景定格在他震惊的表情上，让观众说不出话来。  The accident was freeze-framed in my memory.  事故在我的记忆中定格了。|近义词：still, frozen, static|反义词：moving, dynamic
