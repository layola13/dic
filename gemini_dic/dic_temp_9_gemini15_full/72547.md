flutes:/fluːts/| n.|笛子；笛声|A flute is a musical instrument in the woodwind family that is played by blowing across a hole to produce a sound.  Flutes are known for their sweet and melodious tone. |例句：The orchestra began with a beautiful flute solo.  乐队以一首优美的长笛独奏开始。

The children played their flutes in the park. 孩子们在公园里吹奏他们的笛子。|近义词：pipes, whistles|反义词：None  (No direct antonym)|名词
