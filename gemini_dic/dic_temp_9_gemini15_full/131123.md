nonbituminous:/ˌnɒnˌbɪˈtjuːmɪnəs/| adj. |非沥青质的；非沥青的|Not containing or composed of bitumen.  This term is primarily used in geology and materials science to describe substances that lack the properties of bitumen, a naturally occurring black viscous mixture of hydrocarbons. |The nonbituminous coal was used in the power plant.  非沥青质煤用于发电厂。| bituminous, asphaltic |  petroliferous  |形容词
