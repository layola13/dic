reschooled:/ˌriːˈskuːld/| vt.| 再培训；重新教育 | To provide someone with new training or education, often in a different field or to update existing skills.  |例句：The company reschooled its employees on new software. 公司对员工进行了新软件的再培训。|例句：After the factory closure, the government reschooled many of the unemployed workers. 工厂倒闭后，政府对许多失业工人进行了再培训。|近义词：retrain, re-educate, upskill |反义词：untrain, de-skill |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reschools its employees annually. = 公司每年对员工进行再培训。
He reschools himself in coding every weekend. = 他每个周末都进行编程的再培训。

一般过去时:
They reschooled the miners in computer programming. = 他们对矿工进行了电脑编程的再培训。
She reschooled herself to become a teacher. = 她重新学习成为一名老师。

现在完成时:
The government has reschooled thousands of workers since the economic downturn. = 自经济衰退以来，政府已经对数千名工人进行了再培训。
He has reschooled himself in several different fields. = 他已经在几个不同的领域进行了再培训。

过去完成时:
They had reschooled the staff before launching the new product. = 他们在推出新产品之前已经对员工进行了再培训。
She had reschooled herself in accounting before applying for the job. = 她在申请这份工作之前已经进行了会计方面的再培训。

将来时:
The company will reschool its employees on the new safety regulations. = 公司将对员工进行新的安全规章的再培训。
They will reschool themselves in renewable energy technology. = 他们将学习可再生能源技术。

虚拟语气:
If the company reschooled its workers, productivity would increase. = 如果公司对员工进行再培训，生产力将会提高。
I wish I had reschooled myself earlier in my career. = 我希望我早些在我的职业生涯中进行再培训。

被动语态:
