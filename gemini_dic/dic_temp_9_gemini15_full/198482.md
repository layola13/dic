shrills:/ʃrɪlz/| n. v.|尖锐刺耳的声音，尖叫；发出尖锐刺耳的声音|A shrill sound is a loud, high-pitched sound that is unpleasant to listen to.  The verb refers to the act of making such a sound.|例句：The child shrieked in terror.  孩子恐惧地尖叫起来。 The whistle shrilled loudly.  哨子尖锐地鸣叫。|近义词：scream, shriek, screech, squeal|反义词：murmur, whisper


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird shrills its song. = 鸟儿鸣叫着它的歌声。
She shrills with laughter. = 她尖声笑着。


一般过去时:
The siren shrilled all night. = 警报声整夜尖鸣。
He shrilled his protest. = 他尖声抗议。


现在完成时:
The wind has shrilled through the trees all day. = 风整天都在树林里尖啸。
She has shrilled her complaints to anyone who will listen. = 她向任何愿意倾听的人大声抱怨。


过去完成时:
The whistle had shrilled before the train arrived. = 火车到达之前，哨子已经尖锐地鸣叫过了。
The baby had shrilled for hours before finally falling asleep. = 宝宝在最终睡着之前已经尖叫了几个小时。


将来时:
The alarm will shrill at 6 a.m. = 警报将在早上6点鸣响。
The audience will shrill their approval. = 观众将尖声喝彩。


虚拟语气:
If the alarm shrilled, I would wake up. = 如果警报响了，我会醒过来。
I wish the music wouldn't shrill so loudly. = 我希望音乐不要这么尖锐。


被动语态:
The song was shrilled by a nightingale. = 这首歌是由夜莺唱出来的（此处被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常见）。
His complaints were shrilled to the manager. = 他的抱怨被大声地向经理提出。


疑问句:
Does the whistle shrill often? = 哨子经常鸣叫吗？
Will the audience shrill their displeasure? = 观众会尖声表达不满吗？


否定句:
The bird didn't shrill this morning. = 今早鸟儿没有鸣叫。
She didn't shrill her objections. = 她没有大声反对。


PHRASE->
a shrill cry = 一声尖锐的叫声
a shrill voice = 一个尖锐的声音
a shrill laugh = 一个尖锐的笑声
shrill with anger = 怒气冲冲地尖声叫喊
shrill with excitement = 兴奋地尖声叫喊
