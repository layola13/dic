mart:/mɑːrt/| n.|市场，集市，商店|A mart is a market or a store, often a large one specializing in a particular type of goods.  It can also refer to a bustling marketplace.|例句：The farmer's mart was bustling with activity.  农贸市场热闹非凡。  The department mart offered a wide selection of goods. 这家百货商场提供各种各样的商品。|近义词：market, store, shop, supermarket, bazaar|反义词：none|名词
