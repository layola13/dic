economise:/ɪˈkɒnəmaɪz/| /ˈɛkənəmaɪz/| vt.  |节约；节省；精简|To use something sparingly or carefully; to reduce expenditure.  | We need to economise on water usage. 我们需要节约用水。|They economised by buying cheaper food. 他们通过购买更便宜的食物来节省开支。| save, conserve, husband | squander, waste | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We economise on energy by switching off lights. = 我们通过关灯来节省能源。
She economises on her clothing budget. = 她精打细算她的服装预算。


一般过去时:
They economised on fuel during the war. = 战争期间他们节省燃料。
He economised by walking to work instead of driving. = 他通过步行上班而不是开车来节省开支。


现在完成时:
We have economised significantly on our household bills this year. = 今年我们在家庭账单上节省了很多。
She has economised by cooking at home more often. = 她通过在家做饭更频繁地节省开支。


过去完成时:
They had economised for months to afford the holiday. = 他们已经省了好几个月才能负担得起度假。
We had economised on everything to save for a down payment on a house. = 我们省吃俭用攒钱付房子的首付。


将来时:
We will economise on travel expenses by using video conferencing. = 我们将通过使用视频会议来节省差旅费。
They will economise by reducing their energy consumption. = 他们将通过减少能源消耗来节省开支。


虚拟语气:
If we economised more, we could afford a new car. = 如果我们更节俭一些，我们就能买一辆新车。
I wish we had economised more in the past. = 我希望我们过去能更节俭一些。


被动语态:
Resources must be economised. = 资源必须节约。
Energy is economised through efficient appliances. = 通过高效的电器来节约能源。


疑问句:
How can we economise on our heating bills? = 我们如何节省取暖费用？
Are you economising on anything at the moment? = 你现在正在节约什么吗？


否定句:
They didn't economise enough and went into debt. = 他们没有足够节俭，导致负债。
We didn't economise on quality when building our house. = 我们在建造房屋时没有在质量上节省开支。


PHRASE->
economise on fuel = 节省燃料
economise on time = 节省时间
economise on resources = 节约资源
economise in spending = 节省开支
economise on food = 节省食物


词源:
源自古法语 *econemiser，来自古法语 econemie（经济）+ -iser（动词后缀）。  最终源自古希腊语 οἰκονομία (oikonomia)，字面意思为“家政管理”，由 οἶκος (oikos, “家”) + νέμειν (nemein, “管理”) 构成。
