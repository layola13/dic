rewashing: /ˌriːˈwɒʃɪŋ/ | n. |再洗；重新洗涤 | The act of washing something again.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "re-" to "washing," indicating a repetition of the action.  It's primarily used in a context where something has already been washed but needs another wash. | Example: The rewashing of the towels was necessary after the dog jumped in the laundry basket.  重新洗涤毛巾是必要的，因为狗跳进了洗衣篮。 | 近义词: rewashing, second wash, washing again | 反义词: drying, leaving
