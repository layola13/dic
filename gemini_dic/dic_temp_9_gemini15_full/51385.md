determinately:/dɪˈtɜːmɪnətli/ | adv. | 坚决地；果断地；一定地 | In a determined manner; resolutely; with firmness of purpose.  The adverb "determinately" signifies carrying out an action with resolve and unwavering commitment. It emphasizes the firm and unwavering nature of the action or decision. |例句：She answered the question determinately. 她坚决地回答了这个问题。

He spoke determinately, leaving no room for doubt. 他斩钉截铁地说，不容置疑。| 近义词：resolutely, firmly, decisively, definitely | 反义词：hesitantly, irresolutely, uncertainly |  词性：副词
