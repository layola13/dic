misorganization:/ˌmɪsˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |混乱，组织不力|Lack of organization; a state of being poorly organized.  It refers to a situation where things are not arranged or managed effectively, leading to inefficiency and chaos.|The misorganization of the office led to missed deadlines and frustrated employees.  办公室的混乱导致了截止日期的错过和员工的沮丧。|disorder, chaos, disarray |order, organization, systematization |名词
