newscaster:/ˈnjuːzkɑːstər/ | n. |新闻播音员|A person who reads or presents news on television or radio.  |Example: The newscaster announced the breaking news with a calm demeanor.  新闻播音员以平静的态度宣布了突发新闻。|近义词：anchor, broadcaster, reporter |反义词：audience, viewer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The newscaster delivers the news every evening. = 新闻播音员每天晚上播报新闻。
He reports on current events. = 他报道时事。


一般过去时:
The newscaster read the headlines yesterday. = 新闻播音员昨天播报了新闻标题。
She presented the evening news last night. = 她昨晚播报了晚间新闻。


现在完成时:
The newscaster has covered many important stories. = 该新闻播音员报道了许多重要的新闻。
She has worked as a newscaster for ten years. = 她做新闻播音员已经十年了。


过去完成时:
The newscaster had already finished the broadcast before the power went out. = 在停电之前，新闻播音员已经完成了广播。
He had reported on the election results before the final count. = 在最终统计结果出来之前，他已经报道了选举结果。


将来时:
The newscaster will announce the winner of the election tonight. = 新闻播音员今晚将宣布选举获胜者。
She will be reporting live from the scene of the accident. = 她将从事故现场进行现场报道。


虚拟语气:
If the newscaster were more experienced, she would handle the crisis better. = 如果这位新闻播音员更有经验，她就能更好地处理这场危机。
I wish the newscaster had been more accurate in her reporting. = 我希望新闻播音员的报道更准确一些。


被动语态:
The news was presented by a well-known newscaster. = 新闻是由一位著名的新闻播音员播报的。
The breaking news is being covered by several newscasters. = 多位新闻播音员正在报道突发新闻。


疑问句:
Which newscaster will be anchoring the show tonight? = 今晚谁将主持这个节目？
What did the newscaster say about the storm? = 新闻播音员关于风暴说了什么？


否定句:
The newscaster did not report on the scandal. = 新闻播音员没有报道这起丑闻。
He wasn't able to deliver the news due to technical difficulties. = 由于技术故障，他无法播报新闻。
