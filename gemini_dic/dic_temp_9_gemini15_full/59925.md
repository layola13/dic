edge:/ɛdʒ/ | n. v. |边缘；优势；刀刃；尖锐；使锋利；|

英文解析:
As a noun, "edge" refers to the sharp side of a blade, the outer or furthest point of something, or a slight advantage.  As a verb, it means to make something sharper or more effective.

例句:
The knife has a very sharp edge. 这把刀刃非常锋利。

He had an edge over his competitors. 他比他的竞争对手更有优势。


近义词:
border, brink, margin, rim, advantage, sharpness

反义词:
center, middle, interior, bluntness


词性:
Noun, Verb
