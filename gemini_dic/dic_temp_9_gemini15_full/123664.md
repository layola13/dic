moochers:/ˈmuːtʃərz/ | n. | 蹭饭者；依赖他人生活的人 | A moocher is someone who habitually relies on others for financial support or favors without giving anything in return. They are often seen as parasitic and exploitative. |例句：He's a moocher; he never pays his share. 他是个蹭饭的，他从来不付他应付的钱。

例句：Stop being such a moocher and get a job! 不要再当个寄生虫了，去找份工作！

近义词：sponger, parasite, freeloader, leech

反义词：giver, benefactor, contributor


USAGE->

He mooches off his parents. = 他靠父母养活。
They mooch money from their friends. = 他们向朋友骗钱。

He mooched a cigarette from me. = 他向我讨了一根烟。
She mooched around the house all day. = 她整天在家里闲逛。
