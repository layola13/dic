filets:/fɪˈleɪz/| n.|鱼片，牛柳|Thin strips of meat, typically fish or beef, cut from a larger piece.  Often used in cooking. |例句：The chef prepared delicate salmon filets.  厨师准备了精致的三文鱼片。
例句：We ordered the beef filets with red wine reduction. 我们点了红酒汁牛柳。|近义词：strips, slices, fillets|反义词：whole (as in a whole fish or piece of meat)
