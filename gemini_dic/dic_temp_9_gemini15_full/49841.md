demoralises: /dɪˈmɒrəlaɪz/ (UK) /dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪz/ (US) | v. | 使士气低落；使沮丧；使道德败坏 | To lower someone's spirits; to discourage; to weaken the morale of.  |例句：The team was demoralized by their repeated losses. =  球队接连失利，士气低落。

The constant criticism demoralized the young artist. =  持续的批评使这位年轻的艺术家灰心丧气。

近义词：discourage, dispirit, depress, disheartened | 反义词：encourage, inspire, uplift | 动词
