profuseness:/prəˈfjuːsnis/ | n. | 丰富；充沛；浪费；过度 |  The abundance or copiousness of something.  It often implies an excessive or extravagant amount. |例句：The profuseness of the flowers in the garden was breathtaking. 花园里花朵的繁茂令人叹为观止。

例句：His profuseness in spending money led to his financial ruin. 他花钱大手大脚导致了他经济破产。


近义词：abundance, profusion, extravagance, lavishness, exuberance | 反义词：scarcity, dearth, paucity, meagerness


词源：源自于拉丁语 *profusus，意思是“倾泻的，泼洒的”，由pro-（向前）和fundere（倾泻）构成。
