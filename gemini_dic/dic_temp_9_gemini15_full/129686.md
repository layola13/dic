non-diplomatic: /ˌnɒnˌdɪpləˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 不外交的；直率的；无外交手腕的 |  Lacking in tact, courtesy, or discretion; blunt.  It implies a lack of the careful consideration and politeness typically expected in diplomatic interactions. |


例句：

His non-diplomatic remarks offended the ambassador.  他的不外交言论冒犯了大使。

The general's non-diplomatic approach to negotiations stalled the peace talks.  将军处理谈判的不外交手段使和平谈判陷入僵局。


近义词：blunt, frank, forthright, outspoken, tactless, undiplomatic


反义词：diplomatic, tactful, courteous, discreet


词性：形容词
