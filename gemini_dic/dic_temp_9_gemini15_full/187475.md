rollable: /ˈroʊləbl/ | adj. | 可卷起的；可滚动的 | Able to be rolled up or rolled.  Something that is rollable can be easily rolled into a smaller, more compact form.  | 例句：The rug is rollable for easy storage.  地毯可以卷起来方便存放。

The map is rollable and easy to carry. 这张地图可以卷起来，便于携带。


近义词：foldable, roll-up,  curlable


反义词：rigid, inflexible, unrollable


词性: adjective
