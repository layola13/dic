cotter:/ˈkɒtə(r)/| n. |梢子，销子，键 |A cotter is a split pin or wedge-shaped piece of metal used to secure a bolt or other fastening.  It prevents the bolt from loosening or coming out. |例句：The cotter pin secured the wheel to the axle.  梢子将轮子固定在轴上。  The mechanic used a cotter to fasten the connecting rod. 机械师用梢子固定连杆。|近义词：pin, key, wedge |反义词：None
