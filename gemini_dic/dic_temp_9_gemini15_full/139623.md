occidentals:/ˌɒksɪˈdentəlz/ /ˌɑːksɪˈdentəlz/ | n. |西方人；西方国家的人|People from the West, especially Western Europe and North America.  The term can sometimes carry a colonial or Eurocentric bias.|例句：Many occidentals are unfamiliar with the customs of East Asian cultures. 许多西方人对东亚文化习俗不熟悉。

例句：The study compared the attitudes of occidentals and orientals towards work-life balance.  这项研究比较了西方人和东方人对工作与生活平衡的态度。


近义词：westerners, people from the West


反义词：orientals (this term is now considered outdated and potentially offensive)


词性：名词
