词源:
photo-  (from Greek phōs, phot-), meaning "light"
trans- (from Latin trans-), meaning "across," "through," or "beyond"
-duction (from Latin dūcō, ductum), meaning "to lead" or "to bring"

Therefore, phototransduction literally means "the process of leading or bringing light across or through."
