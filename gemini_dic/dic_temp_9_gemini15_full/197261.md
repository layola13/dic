sharks:/ʃɑːrks/ | n. |鲨鱼 |A shark is a type of fish that has sharp teeth and lives in the ocean.  Sharks are known for their predatory behavior and powerful jaws. |Example: Great white sharks are apex predators. 大白鲨是顶级掠食者。

The sharks circled the boat.  鲨鱼在船周围盘旋。|近义词：predatory fish, maneater |反义词： (no direct antonym, but could consider) prey
