ibidem:/ˈaɪbɪˌdɛm/| adv.| 同上；同上书；同前|  From the same place; in the same place; from the same work or book.  It is used in academic writing to refer to a source that was previously cited. |例句：The author mentions this point again on page 10, ibidem. 作者在第10页再次提到这一点，同上。|例句：As stated ibidem, the results were inconclusive. 正如前面提到的那样，结果并不确定。|近义词：op. cit., loc. cit., ibid.|反义词：N/A
