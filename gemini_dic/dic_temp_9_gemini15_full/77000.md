gardens:/ˈɡɑːrdənz/| n.|花园；菜园| Enclosed areas of land cultivated for the growing of flowers, fruits, vegetables, or other plants, often for aesthetic or recreational purposes.  They can range from small, private plots to large, public parks.|例句：The gardens were ablaze with colorful flowers. 花园里鲜花盛开，五彩缤纷。|例句：He spends his weekends tending his vegetable gardens. 他周末都花在侍弄他的菜园上。|近义词：grounds, plots, orchards|反义词：wilderness, desert


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The gardens are beautiful. = 花园很漂亮。
We grow tomatoes in our gardens. = 我们在花园里种植西红柿。

一般过去时:
The gardens were neglected last year. = 去年花园被忽视了。
They visited the botanical gardens yesterday. = 他们昨天参观了植物园。

现在完成时:
We have planted many roses in the gardens. = 我们已经在花园里种了许多玫瑰花。
They have been working in the gardens all morning. = 他们整个上午都在花园里工作。

过去完成时:
The gardens had been overgrown before they started to work on them. = 在他们开始工作之前，花园已经杂草丛生了。
She had already watered the gardens before the rain started. = 在下雨之前，她已经浇过花园了。

将来时:
We will visit the gardens next week. = 我们下周将参观花园。
They will plant new trees in the gardens this spring. = 他们今年春天将在花园里种新树。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
