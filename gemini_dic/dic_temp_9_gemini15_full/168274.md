pro-Liberian: /ˌproʊ lɪˈbɪəriən/ | adj. | 亲利比里亚的，支持利比里亚的 |  Supporting or favoring Liberia or its people.  This term describes individuals, policies, or movements that advocate for Liberia's interests or well-being.  It can also refer to something originating from or characteristic of Liberia. |

例句：

The pro-Liberian sentiment was strong in the neighboring countries during the civil war.  = 在内战期间，邻国对利比里亚的支持情绪强烈。

The pro-Liberian candidate won the election by a landslide. = 亲利比里亚的候选人在选举中以压倒性优势获胜。


近义词：Liberian-friendly, pro-Liberia, supportive of Liberia

反义词：anti-Liberian, opposed to Liberia


词性：形容词
