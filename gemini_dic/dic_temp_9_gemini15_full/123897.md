mordanted:/mɔːrˈdæntɪd/ | adj. | 媒染的；经媒染处理的 | Treated with a mordant to fix a dye.  Mordanting is a process used in dyeing to improve the fastness of the dye to the fiber. It helps the dye to bind more strongly to the fabric, making the color more permanent and resistant to fading or washing. | The mordanted silk scarf retained its vibrant color even after many washes.  经媒染处理的丝巾即使洗了很多次也保持着鲜艳的颜色。 | dyed, treated |  untreated (反义词相对而言，并非严格反义) | adjective
