hug: /hʌɡ/ | vt. n. |拥抱，紧抱|  An act of holding someone close in your arms to show affection, comfort, or support. |例句：She gave her child a hug. 她抱了抱她的孩子。|近义词：embrace, cuddle |反义词：push away, shun |动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hug my dog every day. = 我每天都拥抱我的狗。
He hugs his children before bedtime. = 他在睡前拥抱他的孩子们。


一般过去时:
She hugged her friend tightly. = 她紧紧地拥抱了她的朋友。
They hugged each other after the game. = 比赛结束后他们互相拥抱。


现在完成时:
I have hugged him many times. = 我已经拥抱过他很多次了。
She has hugged her teddy bear since childhood. = 她从小就抱着她的泰迪熊。


过去完成时:
He had hugged his mother before she left. = 他在他母亲离开之前拥抱了她。
They had hugged each other before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前他们互相拥抱了。


将来时:
I will hug you when I see you. = 我见到你时会拥抱你。
She will hug her daughter at the airport. = 她会在机场拥抱她的女儿。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could hug my grandmother one last time. = 我希望我能最后一次拥抱我的祖母。
If I saw him, I would hug him. = 如果我看到他，我会拥抱他。


被动语态:
The child was hugged by his father. = 孩子被他的父亲抱住了。
The teddy bear was hugged countless times. = 泰迪熊被拥抱了无数次。


疑问句:
Did you hug your sister? = 你拥抱你的姐姐了吗？
Will you hug me? = 你会拥抱我吗？


否定句:
I didn't hug her. = 我没有拥抱她。
He doesn't hug strangers. = 他不拥抱陌生人。


PHRASE->
give someone a hug = 给某人一个拥抱
a bear hug = 熊抱 (非常紧的拥抱)
a warm hug = 温暖的拥抱
a tight hug = 紧抱
a long hug = 长时间的拥抱

词源:
The word "hug" comes from the Old Norse word "hugga," meaning "to comfort" or "to console."  This reflects the primary function of a hug – to provide comfort and emotional support.  The modern English word evolved through Middle English.
