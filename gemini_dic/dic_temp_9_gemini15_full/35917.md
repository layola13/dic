cocreators:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪtərz/| n.|共同创造者，合作创作人|People who create something together.  They share the credit and responsibility for the creation.|例句：The film's cocreators are renowned for their innovative storytelling. 这部电影的共同创作人以其创新的故事讲述而闻名。|例句：The app's cocreators announced a new update today.  这个应用程序的共同创造者今天宣布了一个新的更新。|近义词：collaborators, joint creators|反义词：sole creator, individual creator|名词
