colza: /ˈkɒlzə/ | n. |油菜，芸苔|Rapeseed, a plant cultivated for its oil-rich seeds.  Colza oil is a type of vegetable oil extracted from these seeds. The term is often used interchangeably with rapeseed, although some distinctions exist in certain regions. |例句：Colza is a significant crop in many parts of Europe. 油菜是欧洲许多地区的重要作物。|例句：The farmer harvested a bountiful crop of colza this year. 今年农民收获了大量的油菜。|近义词：rapeseed, canola (in some contexts)|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, typical verb tenses are not applicable.  We can demonstrate usage in different sentence structures.


一般现在时 (using 'is'/'are'):
The colza is blooming beautifully. = 油菜开得非常漂亮。
There are many fields of colza in this region. = 这个地区有很多油菜田。


一般过去时 (using 'was'/'were'):
The colza was harvested last week. = 油菜上周收割了。
The fields were full of colza last summer. = 去年夏天田里长满了油菜。


现在完成时 (using 'has'/'have' + past participle 'been'):
The colza has been processed into oil. = 油菜已经被加工成油了。
The farmers have been growing colza for generations. = 农民世世代代种植油菜。


过去完成时 (using 'had' + past participle 'been'):
The colza had been planted before the rains came. = 油菜在雨季来临之前就已经种下了。
The oil had been extracted from the colza seeds. = 油已经从油菜籽中提取出来了。


将来时 (using 'will' + base form 'be'):
The colza will be ready for harvest next month. = 油菜下个月就可以收割了。
They will be planting colza in the spring. = 他们将在春天种植油菜。


被动语态:
The colza is often used to produce biodiesel. = 油菜常被用来生产生物柴油。
The oil was extracted using a modern press. = 油是用现代压榨机提取的。


疑问句:
Is this colza suitable for biodiesel production? = 这种油菜适合生产生物柴油吗？
Was the colza crop affected by the drought? = 油菜作物受干旱影响了吗？


否定句:
