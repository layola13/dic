commented:/ˈkɑːmɛntɪd/ | adj. v. | 加了注释的；评论的；发表评论 |  Expressed an opinion or provided explanatory notes.  | 例句：The professor commented on the student's essay. 教授对学生的论文做了评论。 | 例句：The code was well commented, making it easy to understand. 代码注释得很好，很容易理解。 | 近义词：remarked, observed, annotated | 反义词：unremarked, unannotated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comments on the news every day. 他每天都评论新闻。
She comments thoughtfully on the current situation. 她对当前局势作了深思熟虑的评论。


一般过去时:
They commented on the quality of the food. 他们评论了食物的质量。
He commented that the meeting was unproductive. 他评论说会议没有效率。


现在完成时:
She has commented extensively on the political climate. 她对政治气候做了广泛的评论。
They have commented favorably on the company's new product. 他们对该公司的新产品给予了好评。


过去完成时:
He had commented on the issue before the meeting. 他在会议之前就对这个问题发表了评论。
She had commented that she was tired before she fell asleep. 她睡觉前曾说过她很累。


将来时:
They will comment on the results of the study next week. 他们下周将对研究结果发表评论。
He will comment on the proposal after careful review. 他将在仔细审查后对提案发表评论。


虚拟语气:
If he commented honestly, people might disagree. 如果他诚实地评论，人们可能会不同意。
I wish he had commented more constructively. 我希望他能够更有建设性地评论。


被动语态:
The article was commented on by many readers. 这篇文章受到了许多读者的评论。
His work has been commented on favorably by critics. 他的作品受到了评论家的好评。


疑问句:
Have you commented on this topic before? 你以前评论过这个话题吗？
Did she comment on the performance? 她对演出做了什么评论？


否定句:
He didn't comment on the incident. 他没有评论这起事件。
She didn't comment on the rumors. 她没有评论那些谣言。


PHRASE->
well-commented: 注释良好的
commented out: 注销的（代码）
commented upon: 被评论的
highly commented: 被高度评价的
publicly commented: 公开评论的
