discerner:/dɪˈsɜːrnər/| n. |辨别者；有洞察力的人 |A discerner is a person who is skilled at distinguishing subtle differences or making fine judgments. They possess keen insight and perception.  |Example: He was a discerning art collector, able to spot a forgery from a masterpiece. 他是一位眼光独到的艺术收藏家，能够辨别赝品和杰作。|近义词：judge, critic, connoisseur, evaluator |反义词：ignoramus, simpleton
