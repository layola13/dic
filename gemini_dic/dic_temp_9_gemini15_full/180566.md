region:/ˈriːdʒən/| n.|地区，区域，范围|An area, especially part of a country or the world, having definable characteristics but not always fixed boundaries.|例句：The northern region of the country is known for its cold winters.  这个国家的北部地区以其寒冷的冬天而闻名。|例句：The Amazon rainforest is a vast region of incredible biodiversity. 亚马逊雨林是一个生物多样性极高的广大区域。|近义词：area, zone, district, territory|反义词：point, locality
