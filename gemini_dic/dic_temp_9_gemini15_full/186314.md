rhomboid:/ˈrɒmbɔɪd/ /ˈrɑːmbɔɪd/| n. |菱形，斜方形|A quadrilateral with opposite sides parallel and equal in length, but with unequal angles.  A parallelogram that is not a rectangle or a rhombus.|The tiles on the floor were arranged in a rhomboid pattern. 地板上的瓷砖排列成菱形图案。|近义词：parallelogram, rhombus|反义词：rectangle, square|名词
