scallywag:/ˈskæliˌwæɡ/| n. |淘气鬼，小捣蛋鬼 | A mischievous or playful child; a rascal.  |例句：That scallywag spilled juice all over the carpet!  那个淘气鬼把果汁洒了一地！

例句：The scallywag hid my keys.  那个小捣蛋鬼藏了我的钥匙。


近义词：rascal, rogue,imp, urchin |反义词：goody-goody, angel


词源：The word's origin is uncertain but possibly a blend of "scallawag" (a variant of "scalawag," meaning a rogue or scoundrel) and "wag" (meaning a mischievous person).  The term "scalawag" itself has uncertain origins, possibly relating to "scald" (to scold or scald) or other related terms with connotations of mischief.
