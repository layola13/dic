goodness:/ˈɡʊdnəs/| n.|善良；优秀；好处；(表示惊讶、愤怒等的感叹词)|The goodness of his heart was evident.  他的善良显而易见。|近义词：virtue, excellence, benefit |反义词：evil, wickedness, harm


英文解析:Goodness can refer to the quality of being good, morally excellent, or virtuous. It can also describe something that is excellent or of high quality, or it can be used as an exclamation expressing surprise, anger, or other strong emotions.


例句:

The goodness of the cake was undeniable. 蛋糕的美味是不可否认的。

She was known for her kindness and goodness. 她以她的善良和仁慈而闻名。


词源:

Old English *gōdnes, from gōd "good" + -nes "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of quality).
