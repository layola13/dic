recurring:/rɪˈkɜːrɪŋ/ | adj. |反复出现的，循环的 | Happening again and again.  |例句：He suffers from recurring nightmares. 他反复遭受噩梦的困扰。|例句：The recurring theme in her novels is the loss of innocence. 她小说中反复出现的主题是天真的丧失。|近义词：repeated, recurrent, cyclical |反义词：unique, infrequent, isolated
