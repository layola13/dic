museums: /mjuˈziːəmz/ | n. |博物馆 | A building in which objects of historical, scientific, or artistic interest are kept and displayed.  | The Louvre Museum in Paris is world-renowned. 巴黎的卢浮宫博物馆世界闻名。 | galleries, institutes, centers |  (反义词无直接对应词，可考虑与"private collections"相对) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Museums house artifacts from various cultures. = 博物馆收藏着来自不同文化的文物。
Museums often host educational programs. = 博物馆经常举办教育项目。

一般过去时:
I visited many museums during my trip to Europe. = 我在欧洲旅行期间参观了许多博物馆。
The museum opened its new wing last year. = 博物馆去年开放了它的新展馆。

现在完成时:
They have visited several museums this month. = 这个月他们已经参观了好几个博物馆。
The museum has expanded its collection significantly. = 博物馆的藏品已大幅扩展。

过去完成时:
I had already seen that exhibit before I visited the museum. = 在我去博物馆之前我已经看过那个展览了。
The museum had been closed for renovations for several months. = 博物馆已经关闭整修好几个月了。

将来时:
We will visit the science museum next weekend. = 我们下周末将参观科学博物馆。
The museum will open a new exhibit on ancient Egypt. = 博物馆将开设一个关于古埃及的新展览。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would visit more museums. = 如果我有更多时间，我会参观更多博物馆。
I wish I could visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art. = 我希望我能参观大都会艺术博物馆。

被动语态:
The artifacts are carefully preserved in the museum. = 文物在博物馆里被小心地保存着。
The museum is currently being renovated. = 博物馆目前正在进行翻修。

疑问句:
Which museums are you planning to visit? = 你计划参观哪些博物馆？
What kind of museums are there in this city? = 这个城市有哪些类型的博物馆？

否定句:
There aren't many museums in this small town. = 这个小镇没有很多博物馆。
He didn't visit any museums on his vacation. = 他在假期里没有参观任何博物馆。
