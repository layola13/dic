outgush:/ˈaʊtɡʌʃ/| v.|涌出；奔流；倾泻；滔滔不绝地说|To flow out suddenly and abundantly; to pour forth.|例句：Her tears outgushed uncontrollably.  她的眼泪不受控制地涌了出来。|例句：The river outgushed from the mountain.  河水从山上奔流而下。|近义词：gush, pour forth, overflow, erupt|反义词：trickle, seep, ooze
