rubefacient:/ruːˈbiːfeɪʃənt/| adj. |红润剂；使皮肤发红的 |Producing redness of the skin; causing superficial inflammation.  A rubefacient is a substance that causes redness of the skin. This effect is usually achieved through irritation and increased blood flow to the area.  |Example:  The doctor applied a rubefacient ointment to the patient's chest to relieve congestion.  医生在病人的胸部涂抹了一种红润剂软膏以缓解充血。 |近义词：irritant, stimulant, counterirritant |反义词：anesthetic, analgesic |
