curtesy:/ˈkɜːtəsi/| n.|礼貌；谦恭；敬意；(尤指妇女的)点头致意|Politeness and respect.  A courteous act or expression.|例句：He showed great courtesy to his guests. 他对客人非常有礼貌。

例句：She acknowledged him with a curtsey. 她对他点头致意。


近义词：politeness, respect, civility, deference|反义词：rudeness, discourtesy, impoliteness


词源:  From Old French *curtesie, from curteis "courteous," from court "court."  The word's evolution reflects the historical association of politeness and refined manners with the courts of medieval Europe.
