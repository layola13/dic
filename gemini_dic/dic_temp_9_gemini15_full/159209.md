poem:/ˈpoʊəm/| n. |诗歌，诗|A poem is a piece of writing that uses beautiful or imaginative language to express feelings or ideas, often in a rhythmical pattern.  Poems can be written in various forms and styles, such as sonnets, haikus, free verse, etc. |Example:  He wrote a beautiful poem about nature. 他写了一首关于自然的优美诗歌。


Example:  She recited a poignant poem at the funeral.  她在葬礼上朗诵了一首感人的诗歌。


近义词：verse, rhyme, composition


反义词：prose (散文)


词性：名词
