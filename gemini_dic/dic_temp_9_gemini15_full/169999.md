protectabilities:/ˌprɒtɛktəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可保护性，保护能力|The term "protectabilities" refers to the qualities or characteristics of something that make it possible to protect or the degree to which something can be protected.  It is not a common word and might be considered a newly coined or less frequently used term.  The word suggests a potential for protection, rather than protection itself. |例句：The company is assessing the protectabilities of its new software to determine the necessary security measures. 公司正在评估其新软件的可保护性，以确定必要的安全措施。 The study explored the protectabilities of endangered species in the face of habitat loss.  该研究探讨了濒危物种在栖息地丧失面前的可保护性。|近义词：defensibility, vulnerability (反义词)|名词
