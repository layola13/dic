outdrives:/ˌaʊtˈdraɪvz/| v.|超过，胜过|To surpass or exceed in performance or effectiveness.|例句：His new car outdrives all the others in its class. 他的新车超过了同级别所有其他车辆。|例句：Her determination outdrives any obstacle. 她的决心克服了任何障碍。|近义词：surpasses, exceeds, outperforms|反义词：underperforms, lags behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new engine outdrives the old one. = 新引擎的性能超过旧引擎。
This strategy outdrives all the others. = 这个策略胜过所有其他策略。


一般过去时:
Last year's model outdrove its competitors. = 去年的车型超过了它的竞争对手。
His dedication outdrove his fatigue. = 他的奉献精神战胜了他的疲惫。


现在完成时:
The company has outdriven its rivals in the market. = 这家公司在市场上已经超过了它的竞争对手。
Their hard work has outdriven their initial expectations.  = 他们的努力工作已经超出了他们最初的预期。


过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outdriven all the other racers. = 到比赛结束时，他已经超过了所有其他赛车手。
She had outdriven her fears and anxieties. = 她已经克服了她的恐惧和焦虑。


将来时:
This technology will outdrive current solutions in the future. = 这项技术将来会超越目前的解决方案。
We believe this plan will outdrive the competition. = 我们相信这个计划将胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, we would have outdriven our competitors. = 如果我们投资更多，我们就会超过我们的竞争对手。
If he had trained harder, he would have outdriven his opponent. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他就会超过他的对手。


被动语态:
The old model was outdriven by the new one. = 旧款车型被新款车型超越了。
Their efforts were outdriven by unforeseen circumstances. = 他们的努力被不可预见的情况所超越。


疑问句:
Does this car outdrive its predecessor? = 这辆车比它的前代车型性能更好吗？
Will this new software outdrive the existing one? = 这个新软件会比现有的软件更好吗？


否定句:
The smaller engine did not outdrive the larger one. = 小型引擎的性能没有超过大型引擎。
His efforts did not outdrive the challenges he faced. = 他的努力没有克服他面临的挑战。


PHRASE->
