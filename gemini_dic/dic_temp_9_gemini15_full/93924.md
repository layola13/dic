immunising: /ˈɪmjʊnaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 免疫接种；使免疫 | The act of making someone or something immune to a particular disease or infection, typically by vaccination.  | 例句：The doctor is immunising the child against measles.  医生正在给孩子接种麻疹疫苗。 | 例句：The government launched a campaign to immunise the entire population against the flu. 政府发起了一项运动，以使全体居民免受流感侵害。| 近义词：vaccinating, inoculating | 反义词：infecting, exposing


USAGE->
