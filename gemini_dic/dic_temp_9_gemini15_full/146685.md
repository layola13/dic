overheard:/ˌoʊvərˈhɜːrd/| /ˌəʊvəˈhɜːd/| vt.|无意中听到，偷听到|To hear (something) without the speaker's knowledge or intention.  The act of hearing something unintentionally.|例句：I overheard them talking about the surprise party. 我无意中听到他们在谈论惊喜派对。

例句：She overheard a conversation between her parents. 她无意中听到她父母之间的谈话。


近义词：eavesdrop, catch, pick up|反义词：intend to hear, deliberately listen to|动词
