erosion:/ɪˈroʊʒən/ | n. | 侵蚀，腐蚀 | The gradual destruction or diminution of something.  Erosion can refer to the wearing away of something by natural processes (like wind, water, or ice) or by chemical processes.  | 例句：The erosion of the coastline is a serious problem.  海岸线的侵蚀是一个严重的问题。 | 例句：Years of wind and rain caused significant erosion to the stone statues.  多年的风吹雨打导致石像严重侵蚀。|近义词：corrosion, deterioration, wearing away | 反义词：construction, accretion, building up |
