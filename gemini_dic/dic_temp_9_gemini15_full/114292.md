mainpin:/ˈmeɪnpɪn/| n. |主要销轴；关键人物；核心；中坚分子|The mainpin of a machine is its most important part, the one on which everything else depends.  It can also refer to a crucial person or thing. |例句：The mainpin of the engine seized up.  发动机的关键部件卡住了。

The CEO is the mainpin of this company.  首席执行官是这家公司的核心人物。|近义词：keystone, linchpin, pivotal figure|反义词：periphery, insignificance
