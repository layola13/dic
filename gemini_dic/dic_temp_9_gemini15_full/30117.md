cede:/siːd/| vt.|放弃，割让，让与|To yield or grant something, especially territory or rights, to another.|The king ceded the territory to his ally. 国王将领土割让给了他的盟友。|grant, yield, relinquish|retain, keep, annex|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cedes control of the company to his son. 他把公司的控制权让给了他的儿子。
The government cedes power to the people. 政府把权力让给了人民。

一般过去时:
France ceded Alsace-Lorraine to Germany after the Franco-Prussian War. 法国在普法战争后将阿尔萨斯-洛林割让给了德国。
She ceded her position to a younger colleague. 她把自己的职位让给了年轻的同事。

现在完成时:
They have ceded control of the project to a new team. 他们已将项目的控制权交给了一个新的团队。
The company has ceded market share to its competitors. 这家公司已经将其市场份额让给了竞争对手。

过去完成时:
The country had ceded its independence before the invasion. 这个国家在入侵之前就已经失去了独立性。
He had ceded his rights to the property before his death. 他在死前已经放弃了他对该房产的权利。

将来时:
The government will cede control of the airport to a private company. 政府将把机场的控制权交给一家私营公司。
She will cede her place in the queue to the elderly woman. 她将把她在队伍中的位置让给那位老年妇女。

虚拟语气:
If the king ceded the territory, peace might have been achieved. 如果国王割让领土，和平或许就能实现。
I wish he had ceded his authority earlier. 我真希望他早点放弃权力。

被动语态:
The territory was ceded to the neighboring country. 该领土被割让给了邻国。
The rights were ceded to the rightful heir. 权利被授予了合法继承人。

疑问句:
Will the government cede the land to developers? 政府会把土地让给开发商吗？
Did they cede their rights to the property? 他们放弃了对该房产的权利吗？

否定句:
He refused to cede his position. 他拒绝放弃自己的职位。
They would not cede an inch of ground. 他们寸土不让。


PHRASE->

cede possession: 交出所有权
cede rights: 放弃权利
