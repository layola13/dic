derestricting: /ˌdiːrɪˈstrɪktɪŋ/ | v. | 取消限制；解除管制 | Removing or lessening restrictions or limitations.  The process involves making something more accessible or freely available. |

例句：

The government is derestricting the import of certain goods. = 政府正在取消对某些商品的进口限制。

The company is derestricting access to its internal network. = 公司正在取消对其内部网络的访问限制。


近义词：liberalizing, freeing, unburdening, deregulating

反义词：restricting, limiting, confining, constricting

词性：动词
