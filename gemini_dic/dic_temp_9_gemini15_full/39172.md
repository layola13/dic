congealed:/kənˈdʒild/| adj.|凝固的；冻结的|To change from a fluid to a solid or semi-solid state; thickened.|The gravy had congealed into a thick, gelatinous mass.  肉汁已经凝固成厚厚的胶状物。|近义词：solidified, thickened, coagulated|反义词：melted, liquefied


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The jelly congeals slowly in the refrigerator. = 果冻在冰箱里慢慢凝固。
The soup congeals if left to cool. = 汤如果放凉就会凝固。

一般过去时:
The blood congealed quickly in the cold air. = 血在寒冷的空气中迅速凝固。
The fat congealed on top of the broth. = 油脂凝固在肉汤上面。

现在完成时:
The jam has congealed, so it's ready to eat. = 果酱已经凝固了，可以吃了。
The mixture has congealed into a solid mass. = 混合物已经凝固成固体。

过去完成时:
The sauce had congealed before we served it. = 我们上菜之前，酱汁就已经凝固了。
The gravy had congealed overnight in the refrigerator. = 肉汁在冰箱里过夜后就凝固了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
