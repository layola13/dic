outgrossing: /ˌaʊtˈɡroʊsɪŋ/ | v. | 超过…的总收入 |  exceeding in gross receipts or revenue.  This word describes the act of surpassing another's total earnings, specifically referring to gross revenue. It's a comparison of overall income, not net profit. |

例句：

The new movie is outgrossing all expectations. = 这部新电影的票房收入超出了所有人的预期。

That product is outgrossing its competitors. = 那款产品的总收入超过了它的竞争对手。


近义词：outperforming, surpassing, exceeding, topping


反义词：underperforming, lagging behind


词性:动词
