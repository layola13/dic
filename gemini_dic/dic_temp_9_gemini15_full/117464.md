medium-sized: /ˈmiːdiəm saɪzd/ | adj. | 中等的，中等大小的 |  Describes something that is neither large nor small, but of average size.  | 例句：He bought a medium-sized car. 他买了一辆中等大小的车。  | 例句：The restaurant is medium-sized, seating about 50 people. 这家餐馆中等大小，大约能容纳50人。 | 近义词：moderate, average, middling | 反义词：large, small, gigantic, tiny |
