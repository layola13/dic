détente:/ˈdeɪtɑːnt/| n. |缓和；(国际关系的)缓和时期|A lessening of tension between countries.|The détente between the two superpowers did not last long.|近义词：relaxation, easing, thaw|反义词：escalation, intensification


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is a détente between the two countries. = 两国之间关系缓和。
The situation shows signs of détente. = 局势显示出缓和的迹象。


一般过去时:
A period of détente followed the war. = 战争之后是缓和时期。
Détente characterized the relationship in the 1970s. = 20 世纪 70 年代两国关系的特点是缓和。


现在完成时:
Significant détente has occurred since the signing of the treaty. = 条约签署以来，已经取得了显著的缓和。
Détente has not yet been achieved in the region. = 该地区尚未实现缓和。


过去完成时:
Before the summit, little détente had been achieved. = 峰会之前，几乎没有取得任何缓和。
By the end of the year, a sense of détente had settled over the region. = 到年底，该地区已经平静下来，出现了缓和的迹象。


将来时:
We hope that détente will be achieved through diplomacy. = 我们希望通过外交能够实现缓和。
The future may hold further détente between the nations. = 未来两国之间可能会有进一步的缓和。


虚拟语气:
If there were détente, peace talks could begin. = 如果关系缓和，和谈就可以开始了。
I wish there were more détente in the world. = 我希望世界上有更多的缓和。


被动语态:
Détente was achieved through years of negotiation. = 缓和是通过多年的谈判达成的。
The agreement was expected to bring about détente. = 预计该协议将带来缓和。


疑问句:
Is there any hope for détente in the near future? = 近期是否有缓和的希望？
Will détente lead to lasting peace? = 缓和会带来持久的和平吗？


否定句:
There was no détente between the warring factions. = 交战各方之间没有任何缓和。
The situation did not show signs of détente. = 局势没有显示出缓和的迹象。
