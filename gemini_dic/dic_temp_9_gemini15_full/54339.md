disheritor:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtər/| n. |剥夺继承权的人，被剥夺继承权的人|One who disinherits or is disinherited.  A disinheritor is someone who deprives another of an inheritance, or someone who has been deprived of an inheritance. |例句：The angry father was a disinheritor of his own son.|近义词：heir remover, one deprived of inheritance|反义词：heir, beneficiary
