ghillies:/ˈɡɪliz/| n.|盖利鞋；高地鞋|Lightweight, soft leather shoes traditionally worn by Scottish gamekeepers and stalkers, often with laces that wrap around the ankle and up the leg.  They are designed for quiet movement and good traction on uneven terrain.|例句：The ghillies allowed him to move silently through the heather.  盖利鞋让他能够悄无声息地穿过石南丛。
The hunter wore ghillies to avoid disturbing the deer. 猎人穿着盖利鞋以避免惊扰鹿群。|近义词：brogues, moccasins|反义词:boots, heavy shoes
