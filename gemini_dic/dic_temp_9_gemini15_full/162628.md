preapplying:/ˌpriːəˈplaɪɪŋ/| v.|预先申请，提前申请|The act of applying beforehand, typically for something like a job, program, or permit.  It emphasizes the action of applying before the official deadline or opening.|例句：Preapplying for a visa allows you to travel sooner. = 预先申请签证可以让你更早出行。

例句：She's preapplying to several universities to increase her chances of acceptance. = 她正在提前申请几所大学，以增加被录取的机会。


近义词：applying in advance, early application|反义词：postponing application, delaying application|动词
