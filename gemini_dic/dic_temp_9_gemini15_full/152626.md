peachlike:/ˈpiːtʃlaɪk/| adj. |像桃子的；桃红色的|Resembling a peach in color, texture, or form.  The term often refers to a soft, smooth, and delicate quality, similar to the skin of a peach.|例句：Her skin had a lovely peachlike bloom.她的皮肤有着可爱的桃色光泽。

例句：The dessert had a peachlike sweetness and texture. 这道甜点有着像桃子一样的甜味和质地。


近义词：rosy, peach-colored, downy

反义词：rough, coarse, harsh


词源：peach (桃子) + -like (类似的)  The word is a compound adjective formed by combining the noun "peach" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.
