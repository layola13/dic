jugglery:/ˈdʒʌɡləri/| n. |杂耍；戏法；欺骗；诡计 |The art or practice of juggling; skillful manipulation of objects.  It can also refer to deceptive or tricky practices. |例句：The circus performer's jugglery was amazing. 马戏团表演者的杂耍令人惊叹。

例句：His business dealings were full of jugglery and deception. 他的生意往来充满了欺骗和诡计。


近义词：sleight of hand, trickery, deception, manipulation |反义词：honesty, straightforwardness


词源:  From Middle English *jugglerie, from Old French *juglerie, from jugler "juggler".  The ultimate origin is uncertain, but possibly related to a Germanic word meaning "to play."
