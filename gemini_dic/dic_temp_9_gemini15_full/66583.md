exhortatory: /ɪɡˈzɔːrtətɔːri/ | adj. | 劝告的；规劝的；激励的 |  Characterized by exhortation; encouraging or urging someone to do something.  | 例句：The speaker delivered an exhortatory speech, urging the audience to action. 演讲者发表了一篇劝告性演讲，敦促听众采取行动。

The general gave an exhortatory address to his troops before the battle.将军在战斗前对他的部队发表了一篇激励人心的讲话。


近义词：urging, encouraging, persuasive, stimulating, inciting | 反义词：discouraging, dissuasive, discouraging, inhibiting


词源:  From "exhort," meaning "to urge strongly,"  ultimately from Latin *exhortārī  ("to encourage").  The suffix "-atory" creates an adjective meaning "relating to" or "characterized by."
