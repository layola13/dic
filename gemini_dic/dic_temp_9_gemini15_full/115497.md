manuscript:/ˈmænjuˌskrɪpt/| n. |手稿；原稿|A handwritten or typed document before it is printed or published.  It can also refer to a piece of writing submitted for publication.|例句：The author submitted his manuscript to the publisher. 作者将他的手稿提交给了出版商。

例句：The professor carefully reviewed the student's manuscript before giving feedback. 教授在给出反馈之前仔细审查了学生的稿件。


近义词：original, draft, text, copy

反义词：publication, printed version


词源:  The word "manuscript" comes from the Late Latin word *manuscriptum*, which is the neuter past participle of *manuscribere*.  *manus* means "hand," and *scribere* means "to write."  Therefore, a manuscript is literally something "hand-written."
