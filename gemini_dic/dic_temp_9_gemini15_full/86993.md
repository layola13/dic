hennery:/ˈhɛnəri/| n. |鸡舍，养鸡场 |A hennery is a building where chickens are kept.  It often provides housing, nesting boxes, and protection from predators.|例句：The farmer cleaned the hennery every morning.  农夫每天早上都清理鸡舍。
The old hennery needed repairs.  旧鸡舍需要修理。|近义词：chicken coop, poultry house |反义词:  None.
