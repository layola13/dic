hucksterer:/ˈhʌkstərər/| n. |江湖郎中；骗子；小贩；兜售者|A hucksterer is someone who sells things in a dishonest or pushy way, often exaggerating the quality or value of their goods. They are often associated with scams and deception.  |例句：He was a smooth-talking hucksterer who sold fake miracle cures. 他是一个油嘴滑舌的江湖郎中，兜售假冒的灵丹妙药。

例句：The hucksterer at the market tried to convince me to buy his overpriced trinkets.  市场上的小贩试图说服我购买他价格过高的饰品。


近义词：peddler, hawker, sharper, swindler, con man

反义词：truth-teller, honest merchant


词性：名词
