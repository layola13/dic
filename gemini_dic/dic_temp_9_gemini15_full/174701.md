quotes:/kwəʊts/| n. |引语，报价，引号|A quote is a passage or expression that is repeated or cited from a book, speech, or other source.  It can also refer to a price quotation. |例句：He included a quote from Shakespeare in his essay. 他在他的论文中引用了莎士比亚的语录。

例句：The company gave me a quote for the repair work. 公司给了我维修工作的报价。|近义词：quotation, citation, excerpt |反义词：paraphrase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The article quotes several experts. = 这篇文章引用了几位专家的观点。
He quotes Shakespeare frequently. = 他经常引用莎士比亚的著作。


一般过去时:
She quoted a famous poem in her speech. = 她在演讲中引用了一首著名的诗歌。
The newspaper quoted the mayor's statement. = 报纸引用了市长的声明。


现在完成时:
They have quoted many sources in their research. = 他们在研究中引用了许多来源。
She has quoted that passage several times. = 她已经多次引用过那段话。


过去完成时:
He had quoted the same statistic in his previous presentation. = 他在之前的演讲中引用过相同的统计数据。
The author had quoted extensively from other works. = 作者大量引用了其他作品。


将来时:
She will quote the relevant sections of the law. = 她将引用法律的相关条款。
They will quote the latest market research data. = 他们将引用最新的市场调研数据。


虚拟语气:
If he quoted the correct source, his argument would be stronger. = 如果他引用了正确的来源，他的论点就会更有力。
I wish he had quoted a more reliable source. = 我希望他引用一个更可靠的来源。


被动语态:
The passage was quoted out of context. = 这段话被断章取义地引用了。
His work is often quoted by other researchers. = 他的作品经常被其他研究人员引用。


疑问句:
Where did you quote that from? = 你是从哪里引用那句话的？
Did he quote the author correctly? = 他正确地引用了作者吗？


否定句:
He didn't quote his sources correctly. = 他没有正确地引用他的来源。
The article didn't quote any statistics. = 这篇文章没有引用任何统计数据。


PHRASE->
quote unquote = 原话是（常用于引语前后）
to quote somebody = 引用某人的话
to quote something = 引用某事物
a direct quote = 直接引语
a loose quote =  意译，概括引用
