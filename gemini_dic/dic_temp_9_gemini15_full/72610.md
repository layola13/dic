flyability:/ˌflaɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. |适航性，飞行性能 |The ability of an aircraft or other flying object to fly safely and effectively.  This encompasses various factors including design, construction, and operational characteristics.  | 例句：The aircraft's flyability was tested rigorously before certification. = 该飞机的适航性在认证前进行了严格测试。

The new design improved the flyability of the helicopter significantly. = 新的设计显著提高了直升机的飞行性能。 |近义词：airworthiness, maneuverability |反义词：unflyability
