skipper:/ˈskɪpər/| n. v.|船长；队长；跳过；|英文解析：A skipper is the captain of a ship or boat, especially a small one.  It can also refer to the captain of a sports team. As a verb, it means to skip or jump over something. |例句：The skipper steered the boat through the rough seas. 船长驾驶着船只穿过汹涌的海面。

The team's skipper made an inspiring speech before the game. 球队的队长在比赛前发表了一番鼓舞人心的演讲。


近义词：captain, leader, commander, skip(v.) |反义词：follower, subordinate(n.)

词源:
The word "skipper" originates from the Middle Dutch word "schipper," meaning "shipman" or "boatman."  This ultimately derives from the Old Dutch word "scip," meaning "ship."  The nautical meaning developed first, and later the use broadened to include the leader of a team.
