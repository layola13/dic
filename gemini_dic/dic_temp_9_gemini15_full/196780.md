exotic:/ɪɡˈzɑːtɪk/| adj.|异国的；奇异的；外来的|英文解析：Coming from another part of the world; foreign.  Often refers to something unusual, exciting, or attractive because it is different and unfamiliar.|例句：She wore an exotic flower in her hair. 她头上戴着一朵奇异的花。|例句：The market was filled with exotic fruits and vegetables.  市场上摆满了奇异的水果和蔬菜。|近义词：foreign, unusual, strange, rare, curious|反义词：ordinary, commonplace, familiar, native
