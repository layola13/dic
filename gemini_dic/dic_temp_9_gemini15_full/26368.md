by-heart:/baɪ hɑːrt/ | adv. | 熟记于心，牢记在心 |  To know something by heart means to have memorized it perfectly.  |  She learned the poem by heart. 她熟记了这首诗。 | 近义词：memorized, learned, committed to memory | 反义词：forget, unlearned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I learn new vocabulary by heart every day. = 我每天都熟记新的词汇。
She knows the multiplication tables by heart. = 她熟记乘法口诀表。

一般过去时:
He learned the speech by heart for the competition. = 为了比赛，他熟记了演讲稿。
They memorized the lyrics by heart before the concert. = 演唱会之前，他们把歌词背熟了。

现在完成时:
I have learned the entire script by heart. = 我已经把整个剧本背熟了。
She has committed the poem to memory by heart. = 她已经把这首诗熟记于心。

过去完成时:
He had learned the song by heart before the audition. = 试镜前他已经把歌背熟了。
They had memorized the lines by heart before the play began. =  戏剧开始前，他们已经把台词背熟了。


将来时:
I will learn this essay by heart before the exam. = 我考试前会把这篇论文背熟。
She will have memorized the entire book by heart by next week. = 下周她将会把整本书背熟。


虚拟语气:
If I had learned the poem by heart, I would have recited it perfectly. = 如果我当时把诗背熟了，我就会完美地背诵出来。
I wish I had learned the vocabulary by heart for the test. = 我真希望我考试前把词汇背熟了。


被动语态:
The speech was learned by heart by all the students. = 这篇演讲稿被所有学生熟记于心。
The lines were memorized by heart by the actors. = 台词被演员们熟记于心。


疑问句:
Did you learn the song by heart? = 你把歌背熟了吗？
Have you memorized the poem by heart yet? = 你把诗背熟了吗？


否定句:
I didn't learn the poem by heart. = 我没有把诗背熟。
She hadn't memorized the script by heart before the rehearsal. = 排练前她没有把剧本背熟。


PHRASE->
learn something by heart = 熟记某事
know something by heart = 熟知某事
commit something to memory by heart = 熟记某事于心
recite something by heart = 脱口背诵某事
have something by heart = 记住某事
