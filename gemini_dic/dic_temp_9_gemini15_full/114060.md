magnetizability:/ˌmæɡnɪtɪˈzeɪbɪləti/| n. | 磁化率，可磁化性 | The ability of a material to become magnetized.  This property is influenced by factors such as the material's composition and its temperature. | The high magnetizability of iron makes it ideal for use in electromagnets.  铁的高磁化率使其成为电磁铁的理想材料。 The magnetizability of the alloy was tested under various conditions.  该合金的可磁化性在各种条件下进行了测试。 |  permeability, susceptibility | diamagnetism | 名词
