inconsisting:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪstɪŋ/| adj. |前后矛盾的；不一致的；不协调的 |Lacking consistency; not compatible or in agreement.  It refers to a state where different elements or parts do not match or align, creating a sense of contradiction or disharmony. |例句：His testimony was inconsistent with the evidence presented. 他的证词与提出的证据不符。

例句：The team's performance has been inconsistent throughout the season.  本赛季球队的表现一直不稳定。


近义词：contradictory, conflicting, incompatible, discordant, discrepant

反义词：consistent, harmonious, compatible, accordant


词性：形容词
