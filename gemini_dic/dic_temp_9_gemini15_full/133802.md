nongerminating: /ˌnɒnˈdʒɜːmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不发芽的，不能发芽的 | Not germinating; unable to germinate.  This adjective describes seeds or plants that are incapable of sprouting and growing.  It implies a failure in the natural process of germination.


例句：

The nongerminating seeds were discarded. = 不发芽的种子被丢弃了。

The farmer was concerned about the high percentage of nongerminating seeds in his latest shipment. = 农民担心他最新一批种子中不发芽的比例很高。


近义词：dormant (休眠的), sterile (不育的), unviable (不可行的)


反义词：germinating (发芽的), viable (可行的)


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **germinating**: present participle of the verb "germinate," meaning "to begin to grow or develop."  "Germinate" comes from Latin *germināre* ("to sprout," "to bud").


用法说明:

由于nongerminating是形容词，它主要用于描述名词，例如种子、植物或其他能够发芽的事物。它通常出现在对植物生长或种子发芽率的描述中。  它不具有动词或名词的用法。  没有不同时态的变化。
