overgrace:/ˈoʊvərɡreɪs/| n.|过度恩典，过分的恩惠|  A favor or act of kindness that is excessive or undeserved.  It often implies an embarrassing or overwhelming level of generosity.|例句：His overgrace in giving me such a lavish gift made me feel uncomfortable. 他给予我如此奢华的礼物，让我感到不舒服。|近义词：excessive kindness, undue generosity, overwhelming favor|反义词：stinginess, parsimony, meagerness|名词
