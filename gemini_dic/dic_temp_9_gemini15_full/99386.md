interdiction: /ˌɪntərˈdɪkʃən/ | n. | 禁止；阻止；禁令 | The act of prohibiting or forbidding something; an official or legal prohibition.  | Example: The interdiction of alcohol during Prohibition led to a rise in organized crime.  禁止在禁酒令期间饮酒导致有组织犯罪的增加。 |近义词：prohibition, ban, embargo, restraint | 反义词：permission, allowance, authorization


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government imposes an interdiction on the import of certain goods. = 政府禁止进口某些商品。
This area is under interdiction due to military operations. = 由于军事行动，该地区禁止通行。


一般过去时:
The court issued an interdiction against the defendant. = 法院对被告发布了禁令。
The king's interdiction prevented the rebellion. = 国王的禁令阻止了叛乱。


现在完成时:
Several countries have imposed interdictions on the sale of weapons. = 几个国家已经禁止出售武器。
The authorities have implemented an interdiction on fishing in this area. = 当局已实施禁止在该区域捕鱼的禁令。


过去完成时:
The rebels had ignored the prior interdiction and continued their attacks. = 叛军无视先前的禁令并继续发动袭击。
Before the war, there had been an interdiction on all exports. = 在战争之前，所有出口都被禁止。


将来时:
The new law will likely bring about an interdiction on smoking in public places. = 新法律可能会导致禁止在公共场所吸烟。
They will announce an interdiction on travel to affected areas. = 他们将宣布禁止前往受影响地区。


虚拟语气:
If there were an interdiction on plastic bags, we would see less pollution. = 如果禁止使用塑料袋，我们会看到更少的污染。
I wish there were an interdiction on hate speech online. = 我希望网上有禁止仇恨言论的禁令。


被动语态:
The import of these chemicals was interdicted by the government. = 这些化学品的进口被政府禁止了。
The planned demonstration was interdicted by the police. = 计划中的示威活动被警方禁止了。


疑问句:
Is there an interdiction on the use of drones in this airspace? = 在这个空域使用无人机是否被禁止？
What is the interdiction policy regarding the transportation of hazardous materials? = 关于危险物质运输的禁令政策是什么？


否定句:
There was no interdiction on the gathering. = 这次集会没有被禁止。
The general did not issue an interdiction against retreat. = 将军没有发布禁止撤退的命令。

词源:
From Old French *interdiccion, from Latin interdictio, from interdicere "to forbid, prohibit," from inter- "between" + dicere "to say, speak."  The word's core meaning relates to the act of speaking against or placing a barrier between something and its intended action.
