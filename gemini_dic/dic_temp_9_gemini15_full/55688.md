ditziest:/ˈdɪtsiəst/| adj.|最傻的，最糊涂的 | Most silly or foolish;  characterized by silliness or foolishness to the highest degree. It is the superlative form of "ditzy".|例句：She's the ditziest person I know.  = 她是我认识的最傻的人。


例句：He acted in the ditziest way imaginable. = 他做出了你能想象到的最傻的行为。


近义词：goofy, silly, scatterbrained, flighty, airheaded


反义词：sensible, shrewd, intelligent, clever, wise


词源：源自"ditzy"，可能与“dizzy”（头晕的）有关，暗示一种精神恍惚、迷糊的状态。  The exact etymology is uncertain, but it's likely related to "dizzy," suggesting a state of mental confusion or disorientation.  The "-iest" suffix indicates the superlative degree.
