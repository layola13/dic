resinic: /rɪˈzɪnɪk/ | adj. | 树脂的；松脂的 | Relating to or resembling resin; containing resin.  | The resinic acid in the amber gave it its unique yellow hue.  琥珀中的树脂酸赋予了它独特的黄色色调。 | resinous, gummy | non-resinic | 形容词


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  Therefore, tense variations are not applicable.  Examples will demonstrate its usage in different sentence structures.

肯定句:
The painting had a resinic varnish. = 这幅画涂有树脂清漆。
The air smelled strongly resinic near the sawmill. = 在锯木厂附近，空气中弥漫着浓烈的松脂味。


否定句:
The material was not resinic, but rather synthetic. =这种材料不是树脂的，而是合成的。
The fragrance wasn't entirely resinic; floral notes were also present. = 这香味并非完全是树脂味；也带有花香。


疑问句:
Is the sealant resinic in nature? = 密封剂是树脂性的吗？
Are these beads made from a resinic substance? = 这些珠子是用树脂物质制成的吗？


PHRASE->
resinic acid: 树脂酸

resinic material: 树脂材料

词源:
From "resin" + "-ic" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Resin" ultimately derives from Old French *resine, from Latin resina, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Greek rhētinē.
