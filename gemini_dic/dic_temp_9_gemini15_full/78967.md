girlies:/ˈɡɜːrliz/| n.|女孩们，姑娘们|A colloquial term referring to a group of girls or young women.|例句：The girlies are going to the mall. 女孩们要去购物中心。|近义词：girls, young women, ladies (less informal)|反义词：None
