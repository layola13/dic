correctible:/kəˈrektəbl/ | adj. | 可改正的，可纠正的 | Capable of being corrected or amended.  Something that is correctible can be fixed, improved, or made right. |


例句：

The errors in the manuscript are correctible.  = 手稿中的错误是可以改正的。

His bad habits are correctible with professional help. = 他的坏习惯在专业人士的帮助下是可以纠正的。


近义词：amendable, rectifiable, fixable


反义词：irreparable, incorrigible, unfixable


词源:

From "correct" + "-ible" (suffix indicating capability).  "Correct" ultimately derives from Latin *corrigere, meaning "to straighten, set right".
