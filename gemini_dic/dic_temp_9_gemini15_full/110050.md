licensors:/ˈlaɪsɛnsərz/ | n. |许可人，授权者 |Those who grant licenses or permissions.  They hold the rights to a property (intellectual property, real estate, etc.) and allow others to use it under specific terms and conditions. |Example: The licensors of the software retain ownership of the copyright.  许可软件的授权者保留版权所有权。|近义词:grantors, authors (in some contexts)|反义词:licensees
