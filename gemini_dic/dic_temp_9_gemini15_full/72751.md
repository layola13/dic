fogey:/ˈfoʊɡi/| n. |老顽固，守旧的人 | An old-fashioned and often stubborn person, resistant to new ideas or ways of life.  |He's such a fogey; he refuses to use a computer. 他是个老顽固，拒绝使用电脑。|近义词：old-timer, fuddy-duddy, reactionary |反义词：progressive, modern, forward-thinking |名词


USAGE->

He is a fogey. = 他是个老顽固。
They are fogeys. = 他们都是老顽固。
