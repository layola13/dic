flagellates:/ˈflædʒəleɪts/| n. |鞭毛虫；鞭毛生物|  Flagellates are single-celled organisms that use a whip-like structure called a flagellum for movement. They are found in various environments, from freshwater to saltwater, and even within other organisms.  |Example: Many species of flagellates are found in the ocean.  许多种鞭毛虫都存在于海洋中。|近义词：protozoa (原生动物)|反义词：无|名词
