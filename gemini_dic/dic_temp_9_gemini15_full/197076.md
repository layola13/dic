shamed:/ʃeɪmd/| adj.|羞愧的，蒙羞的|Feeling or showing shame or embarrassment.|He felt ashamed of his behavior. 他为自己的行为感到羞愧。|近义词：humiliated, disgraced, mortified|反义词：proud, honored


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is ashamed of his actions. 他为自己的行为感到羞愧。
She is ashamed to admit her mistake. 她羞于承认自己的错误。


一般过去时:
I was ashamed when I tripped and fell. 我绊倒摔倒时感到很羞愧。
They were ashamed of their lies. 他们为自己的谎言感到羞愧。


现在完成时:
I have been ashamed of myself ever since. 从那以后我一直为自己感到羞愧。
She has never been ashamed to express her opinions. 她从来不羞于表达自己的观点。


过去完成时:
He had been ashamed of his past before he met her. 在遇到她之前，他一直为自己的过去感到羞愧。
She had already felt ashamed of her impulsive decision. 她已经为自己的冲动决定感到羞愧了。


将来时:
I will be ashamed if I fail the exam. 如果考试不及格，我会感到羞愧。
They will be ashamed of their actions tomorrow. 明天他们会为自己的行为感到羞愧。


虚拟语气:
I would be ashamed to ask for help. 我会羞于求助。
If I were you, I'd be ashamed of myself. 如果我是你，我会为自己感到羞愧。


被动语态:
He was shamed into silence. 他被羞辱得哑口无言。
She was shamed by the public accusations. 她被公众的指责羞辱了。


疑问句:
Are you ashamed of what you did? 你为你的所作所为感到羞愧吗？
Isn't he ashamed of his behavior? 他难道不为自己的行为感到羞愧吗？


否定句:
He is not ashamed of his beliefs. 他不为自己的信仰感到羞愧。
She was not ashamed to stand up for what she believed in. 她不羞于为自己的信仰挺身而出。


PHRASE->
feel ashamed  感到羞愧
be ashamed of 为...感到羞愧
put to shame 使蒙羞
ashamed of oneself 为自己感到羞愧
ashamed to do sth 羞于做某事
