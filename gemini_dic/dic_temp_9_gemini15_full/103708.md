jordan:/ˈdʒɔːrdən/|ˈdʒɔːrdən| |n.|约旦（河）；乔丹（人名）|Jordan refers to both a river in the Middle East and a common given name, often associated with the famous basketball player Michael Jordan.  As a river, it is historically and geographically significant. As a name, it carries connotations of strength, athleticism, and success. |例句：The Jordan River flows through Israel, the West Bank, and Jordan. 约旦河流经以色列、约旦河西岸和约旦。

The basketball player, Michael Jordan, is a global icon. 篮球运动员迈克尔·乔丹是一位全球偶像。|近义词：river (for the river meaning),  (for the name meaning, depends on context -  e.g.,  name of a person)|反义词： (no direct antonyms, depends on context)|n.
