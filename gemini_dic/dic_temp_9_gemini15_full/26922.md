calamite:/ˈkæləmaɪt/| n.|灾难；不幸|A calamite is a disaster or misfortune, often referring to a sudden and devastating event.  It often implies a significant loss or hardship.|例句：The hurricane was a calamite that devastated the coastal town.  飓风是一场摧毁了沿海城镇的灾难。|例句：The loss of his job was a personal calamite. 失去工作对他来说是一场个人灾难。|近义词：catastrophe, disaster, calamity, tragedy|反义词：blessing, fortune, boon
