polyester:/ˌpɒliˈɛstər/ /ˌpɑːliˈɛstər/| n. |聚酯纤维，涤纶|A synthetic fiber made from a polymer; it's strong, wrinkle-resistant, and water-resistant.  It's widely used in clothing and other textiles.|例句：That shirt is made of 100% polyester. 那件衬衫是100%涤纶的。|例句：Polyester fabrics are often used in sportswear because of their durability. 由于涤纶面料的耐用性，它们常用于运动服。|近义词：polyester fiber, synthetic fiber|反义词：natural fiber (e.g., cotton, wool)|名词
