isolationism:/ˌaɪsəˈleɪʃənɪzəm/| n. |孤立主义；闭关自守|A policy of remaining apart from the affairs or interests of other groups, especially the political affairs of other countries.  It's a political ideology that emphasizes national self-reliance and minimizes international involvement. |The country's isolationism prevented it from participating in international trade agreements. 这个国家的孤立主义使其无法参与国际贸易协定。  The rise of nationalism often leads to a surge in isolationist sentiment.  民族主义的兴起往往导致孤立主义情绪高涨。|internationalism, interventionism|globalism, international cooperation|名词
