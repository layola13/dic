implications:/ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |含意；暗示；结果；影响 |The possible implications of this decision are enormous.  这个决定的可能影响是巨大的。| consequences, ramifications, effects, repercussions | insignificance, triviality | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The implications of this research are still unclear. = 这项研究的含义仍不清楚。
These actions have serious implications for the future. = 这些行动对未来有严重的影响。

一般过去时:
The implications of the treaty were debated for months. = 条约的含义被争论了几个月。
His words had far-reaching implications. = 他的话具有深远的影响。

现在完成时:
The implications of climate change have become increasingly apparent. = 气候变化的影响已日益明显。
Scientists have discovered several implications of this new drug. = 科学家们已经发现了这种新药的几个影响。

过去完成时:
By the time the decision was made, the implications had already been considered. = 当做出决定时，影响已经考虑过了。
They hadn't fully grasped the implications of their actions before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，他们还没有完全理解他们行为的影响。

将来时:
The implications of this technology will be far-reaching. = 这项技术的意义将是深远的。
We will need to consider the implications before we proceed. = 在我们继续之前，我们需要考虑其影响。


虚拟语气:
If the implications were more clearly understood, the decision would be easier. = 如果更清楚地理解其含义，那么决策就会更容易。
I wish the implications had been clearer before we started. = 我希望在我们开始之前，这些影响更清晰一些。

被动语态:
The implications are being carefully studied by experts. = 这些影响正在被专家们仔细研究。
The implications were misrepresented in the media. = 媒体歪曲了这些影响。

疑问句:
What are the implications of this policy change? = 这一政策变化有何影响？
What are the broader implications of this discovery? = 这一发现的更广泛的影响是什么？

否定句:
The study did not fully explore the implications of its findings. = 这项研究并没有完全探讨其研究结果的影响。
There were no negative implications for the company. = 对公司没有任何负面影响。
