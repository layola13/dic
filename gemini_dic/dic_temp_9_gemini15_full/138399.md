notturno:/nɔkˈtɜːrnoʊ/| n. |夜曲；夜景|A nocturnal musical composition, typically for piano, characterized by a romantic and dreamy atmosphere.  It can also refer to a piece of art depicting a night scene.|Example: Chopin's nocturnes are renowned for their beautiful melodies and expressive harmonies.  肖邦的夜曲以其优美的旋律和富有表现力的和声而闻名。 The painting captured the serene notturno of the moonlit lake. 这幅画捕捉到了月光下宁静的湖泊夜景。|近义词：serenade, nocturne (music)|反义词：diurno|名词
