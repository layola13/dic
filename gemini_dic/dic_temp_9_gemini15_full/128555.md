niacin: /ˈnaɪəsɪn/ | n. | 烟酸，尼克酸 |  A vitamin of the B complex, essential for normal growth and metabolism.  It is found in liver, yeast, and some cereals. Deficiency can cause pellagra. | 例句：Niacin is crucial for maintaining healthy skin and nerve function. = 烟酸对于维持健康的皮肤和神经功能至关重要。
