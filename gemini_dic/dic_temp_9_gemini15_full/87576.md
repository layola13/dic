heteropod:/ˈhɛtərəpɒd/|/ˈhɛtəroʊpɑːd/| n. |异足类动物；（某些）腹足类软体动物|A heteropod is a type of pelagic sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk that swims freely in the open ocean.  They are characterized by their laterally compressed bodies and modified feet used for swimming.|  The heteropod *Atlanta* is a common example found in warmer waters. |近义词：pelagic gastropod|反义词：benthic gastropod
