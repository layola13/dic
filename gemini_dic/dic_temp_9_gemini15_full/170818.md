pseudoethically: /ˌsuːdoʊˈɛθɪkəli/ | adv. |伪道德地，貌似合乎道德地 | Acting in a way that appears ethical but is not genuinely so;  showing a false or superficial appearance of ethical behavior.  |例句：He pseudoethically justified his actions by claiming they were for the greater good. 他伪善地为自己的行为辩护，声称这是为了更大的利益。|近义词：hypocritically, sanctimoniously, deceptively |反义词：ethically, honestly, genuinely |词性:副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and thus does not conjugate like a verb.  There's no tense to consider. Its usage always modifies a verb.

Example sentences illustrating its usage:


He acted pseudoethically to gain favor with his boss. = 他伪装成道德的样子以讨好老板。

She spoke pseudoethically, concealing her true motives. = 她说话貌似道德，却掩盖了她的真实动机。


PHRASE->

There are no common set phrases that specifically use "pseudoethically".  The word is used to modify actions or speech.  Examples of phrases that *could* incorporate it:

pseudoethically motivated = 出于伪道德动机的
pseudoethically justified = 伪善地证明正当的
pseudoethically argued = 伪道德地争论的
acting pseudoethically = 伪装成道德的样子行动
spoke pseudoethically = 伪善地说话
