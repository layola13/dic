dinner:/ˈdɪnər/| n.|正餐，晚餐|The main meal of the day, typically eaten in the evening.|例句：We're having roast chicken for dinner tonight.  今晚我们吃烤鸡做晚餐。|例句：Dinner is served!  晚餐准备好了！|近义词：supper, evening meal|反义词：breakfast, lunch


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What do you usually have for dinner? = 你通常晚餐吃什么？
What time do you eat dinner? = 你几点吃晚餐？


一般过去时:
What did you have for dinner last night? = 你昨晚吃了什么晚餐？
Where did you have dinner last night? = 你昨晚在哪儿吃的晚餐？


现在完成时:
Have you had dinner yet? = 你吃过晚饭了吗？
Have you ever had dinner at that restaurant? = 你在那家餐厅吃过饭吗？


过去完成时:
Had you finished dinner before the guests arrived? = 客人到达之前你吃完了晚饭吗？

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I had had time to prepare a better dinner. = 我希望我有时间准备一顿更好的晚餐。


被动语态:
