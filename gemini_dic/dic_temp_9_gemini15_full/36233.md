cognitional:/ˌkɒɡnɪˈʃənəl/| adj.|认知的；认识论的；关于认识的|Relating to cognition; involving or using the process of acquiring knowledge and understanding through thought, experience, and the senses.  It emphasizes the process of knowing, rather than the knowledge itself.|The study explored the cognitional aspects of language acquisition. 这项研究探讨了语言习得的认知方面。|近义词：cognitive, epistemological|反义词：non-cognitive, intuitive
