reptiloid:/ˈrɛptaɪlɔɪd/| n. |爬虫人；类爬行动物|A reptiloid is a fictional humanoid reptile, often depicted in science fiction as an extraterrestrial being or a secret controlling power on Earth.  They are typically portrayed as having reptilian features such as scales, forked tongues, and cold blood. |例句：The movie featured a reptiloid conspiracy to control the world. 这部电影讲述了一个控制世界的爬虫人阴谋。

The theory suggests that reptoids are secretly running the world.  这个理论认为爬虫人正在秘密统治世界。|近义词：reptilian, saurian|反义词：humanoid (in the context of contrasting physical form)
