semiexclusively:/ˌsemiɪkˈskluːsɪvli/ | adv. | 半专属地，部分排他地 |  Partially or to a limited extent exclusively; not entirely exclusive.  It suggests a situation where something is primarily, but not completely, restricted to a specific group or purpose. | The club is semiexclusively for members, meaning non-members can sometimes attend with an invite.  俱乐部对会员半专属，这意味着非会员有时可以应邀参加。|近义词：mostly exclusively, primarily exclusively | 反义词：inclusively, universally
