magnifical:/mæɡˈnɪfɪkəl/| adj.|壮丽的；宏伟的；极好的|Magnificent is an adjective meaning extremely impressive in appearance or style. It suggests grandeur, splendor, and magnificence.  The word implies something that evokes a sense of awe or wonder. |例句：The view from the mountaintop was magnifical. 山顶的景色壮丽无比。

The castle was magnifical in its design and construction. 这座城堡的设计和建造都非常宏伟。|近义词：grand, splendid, superb, glorious, majestic|反义词：meager, paltry, insignificant, humble
