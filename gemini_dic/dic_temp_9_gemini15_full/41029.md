coped:/koʊpt/| v.|应付；克服；处理|To deal successfully with a difficult situation.|例句：She coped well with the pressure of the job. 她很好地应对了工作压力。|例句：He found it hard to cope with his wife's death. 他发现很难应对妻子的去世。|近义词：manage, handle, deal with|反义词：fail, succumb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He copes well under pressure. = 他在压力下应付得很好。
She copes with her anxiety through meditation. = 她通过冥想来应对焦虑。


一般过去时:
They coped admirably with the crisis. = 他们出色地应对了危机。
I coped with the difficult assignment by breaking it down into smaller tasks. = 我通过将困难的任务分解成更小的任务来应对。


现在完成时:
We have coped with many challenges in our business. = 我们在业务中克服了许多挑战。
She has coped successfully with the loss of her job. = 她成功地应对了失业。


过去完成时:
He had coped with similar problems before. = 他以前处理过类似的问题。
By the time the storm hit, they had coped with several smaller crises. = 当风暴来临时，他们已经应对了几个较小的危机。


将来时:
They will cope with the increased workload by hiring more staff. = 他们将通过雇佣更多员工来应对增加的工作量。
We will cope with the difficult situation as best we can. = 我们将尽最大努力应对困境。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would cope better. = 如果我有更多时间，我会应付得更好。
I wish I could cope with the stress more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地应对压力。


被动语态:

否定句:
