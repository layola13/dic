reserved:/rɪˈzɜːvd/ | adj. | 保留的；预定的；沉默寡言的；拘谨的 |  reserved means kept for a special purpose; or unwilling to show one's feelings or thoughts.  It can also refer to something that has been booked or set aside. |例句：He reserved a table for two at the restaurant. 他在餐厅预订了一张两人桌。

例句：She's a very reserved person and doesn't talk much. 她是一个非常内向的人，不太说话。


近义词：booked,  restrained, shy, quiet, taciturn | 反义词：outgoing, extroverted, talkative, open


词性: 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The seats are reserved for VIP guests. = 座位是为贵宾保留的。
He always reserves a seat on the train. = 他总是预订火车上的座位。

一般过去时:
They reserved a room at the hotel. = 他们在酒店预订了一个房间。
She reserved a table for dinner last night. = 她昨晚预订了一张晚餐桌。

现在完成时:
I have reserved a ticket for the concert. = 我已经预订了一张音乐会的票。
We have reserved a space for the meeting. = 我们已经为会议预留了一个空间。

过去完成时:
He had reserved the best seats before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他已经预定了最好的座位。
They had reserved a private room for the celebration. = 他们为庆祝活动预订了一个私人房间。

将来时:
I will reserve a car for the airport pickup. = 我将预订一辆车去机场接机。
She will reserve a suite at the luxury hotel. = 她将在豪华酒店预订一套套房。

虚拟语气:
If I had reserved a ticket earlier, I wouldn't have missed the show. = 如果我早点预订票，我就不会错过演出。
If she had reserved a table, we wouldn't have had to wait. = 如果她预订了桌子，我们就不用等了。

被动语态:
A table was reserved for us at the restaurant. = 餐厅为我们预订了一张桌子。
The tickets were reserved weeks in advance. = 门票提前几周就预订了。

疑问句:
Has a table been reserved under my name? = 我名下是否预订了桌子？
Were the rooms reserved for the conference? = 会议的房间是否预订了？

否定句:
He didn't reserve a seat on the plane. = 他没有预订飞机上的座位。
They hadn't reserved a hotel room. = 他们没有预订酒店房间。
