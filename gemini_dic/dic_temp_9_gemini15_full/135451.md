nonpertinency:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːtɪnənsi/| n. |不相关；不相干；无关性 |The nonpertinency of his remarks to the subject at hand was evident.  他的话与当前主题无关, 这很明显。| irrelevance, immateriality, insignificance | pertinence, relevance |名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun, and as such, it doesn't have verb conjugations like tense.  It's used in sentences to describe a lack of relevance or connection.

Example Sentences:

The judge dismissed the argument due to its nonpertinency to the case. = 由于该论点与案件无关，法官驳回了该论点。

The committee noted the nonpertinency of the proposed amendment to the main bill. = 委员会指出，拟议的修正案与主要法案无关。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "nonpertinency."  Phrases expressing similar ideas include:


lack of relevance
irrelevant information
beside the point
off-topic
has nothing to do with it
