nose:/noʊz/|n.|鼻子|The nose is the part of the face that is used for smelling and breathing.|例句：He has a big nose. 他有一个大鼻子。|例句：She wiped her nose. 她擦了擦鼻子。|近义词：snout, proboscis|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My nose is running. = 我的鼻子在流鼻涕。
He smells with his nose. = 他用鼻子闻。


一般过去时:
I broke my nose last year. = 我去年摔断了鼻子。
She sniffed the flower with her nose. = 她用鼻子闻了闻花。


现在完成时:
He has never broken his nose. = 他从未摔断过鼻子。
I have blown my nose several times today. = 我今天已经擤了几次鼻涕了。


过去完成时:
I had already blown my nose before he asked. = 在他问之前我已经擤过鼻涕了。
She had cut her nose on a sharp object. = 她之前被锋利的物体割伤了鼻子。


将来时:
I will have a surgery on my nose next week. = 我下周要进行鼻部手术。
He will surely smell the smoke with his nose. = 他一定会用鼻子闻到烟味。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better nose, I could smell it. = 如果我的鼻子更好，我就能闻到它。
I wish my nose wasn't so stuffy. = 我希望我的鼻子不要这么堵。


被动语态:
His nose was broken in the accident. = 他的鼻子在事故中被撞断了。
Her nose is being examined by the doctor. = 她的鼻子正在接受医生的检查。


疑问句:
Do you have a runny nose? = 你鼻子流鼻涕吗？
What does your nose smell? = 你的鼻子闻到什么了？


否定句:
I don't have a stuffy nose. = 我的鼻子不堵。
He didn't smell anything unusual with his nose. = 他用鼻子闻到什么异常的气味。
