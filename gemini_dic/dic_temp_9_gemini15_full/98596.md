instructed:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪd/ | v. (过去式和过去分词) | 指示；命令；教导 |  To have given instructions or orders to (someone).  To have taught (someone) a skill, subject, etc. |例句：The teacher instructed the students to complete the assignment.  老师指示学生完成作业。

The police instructed the crowd to disperse. 警方指示人群散开。


近义词：directed, ordered, commanded, taught, trained | 反义词：ignored, disobeyed | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher instructs the students every day. = 老师每天都指导学生。
He instructs his team on the new project. = 他指导他的团队进行新的项目。


一般过去时:
She instructed me to wait outside. = 她指示我在外面等候。
They instructed the workers to follow safety regulations. = 他们指示工人遵守安全规章。


现在完成时:
The manager has instructed us to finish the report by Friday. = 经理已指示我们在周五之前完成报告。
She has instructed her assistant on the new procedure. = 她已经指导她的助理新的程序。


过去完成时:
He had instructed his son to be careful before leaving. = 他在离开前已指示他的儿子要小心。
They had instructed the team to prepare for the presentation. = 他们已指示团队为演示做准备。


将来时:
The professor will instruct the class on quantum physics next week. = 教授下周将指导课堂量子物理。
The coach will instruct the players on new strategies. = 教练将指导球员新的策略。


虚拟语气:
If he had instructed me properly, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果他正确地指导我，我就不会犯那个错误。
I wish I had been instructed earlier on this matter. = 我希望我早点被告知此事。


被动语态:
The students were instructed to be quiet. = 学生们被指示保持安静。
The workers were instructed on the use of new equipment. = 工人们接受了关于使用新设备的指导。


疑问句:
Were the participants instructed on the rules of the game? = 参加者是否被告知了比赛规则？
How were the students instructed to conduct the experiment? = 学生们被指示如何进行实验？


否定句:
The manager did not instruct the employees to work overtime. = 经理没有指示员工加班。
He wasn't instructed to proceed with the plan. = 他没有被指示继续执行计划。


PHRASE->
instructed to do something: 被指示做某事
clearly instructed: 清晰地指示
fully instructed:  充分地指导
properly instructed: 正确地指导
carefully instructed: 小心地指导
