longly:/ˈloʊŋli/| adj.|孤单的，寂寞的|Feeling sad because one is alone or without companionship.|She felt lonely after her friends moved away. 她朋友搬走后感到很孤单。|solitary, isolated, alone|happy, cheerful, sociable|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feels lonely every night. = 他每晚都感到孤独。
She is lonely without her family. = 没有家人她感到很孤单。

一般过去时:
I felt lonely when I was a child. = 我小时候感到很孤独。
They were lonely after their pet died. = 他们的宠物死后，他们感到很孤独。

现在完成时:
She has felt lonely since her husband died. = 自从她丈夫去世后，她就一直感到孤独。
He has been lonely for many years. = 他孤独多年了。

过去完成时:
I had felt lonely before I met my best friend. = 在我遇到我最好的朋友之前，我感到很孤独。
They had been lonely for a long time before they adopted a dog. = 在他们收养一只狗之前，他们孤独了很久。

将来时:
