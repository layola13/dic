chook:/tʃʊk/| n. |母鸡；小鸡|A chook is a chicken, especially a hen.  The term is primarily used in Australia and New Zealand.|例句：The chook laid a beautiful brown egg. 这只母鸡下了一个漂亮的棕色蛋。|例句： We have several chooks in our backyard. 我们后院养了几只鸡。|近义词：hen, chicken, pullet|反义词：rooster, cock
