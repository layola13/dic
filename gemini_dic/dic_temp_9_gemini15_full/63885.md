epiloguing: /ˈɛpɪlɒɡɪŋ/ | v. |  写结语；作结尾陈述 | Adding an epilogue to a work, typically a literary or dramatic work.  It involves writing or delivering the concluding remarks or summary. |

例句：

The author spent weeks epiloguing his novel, trying to find the perfect concluding sentence. = 作者花了几个星期来写他的小说的结语，试图找到完美的结尾句。

She epilogued her speech with a powerful call to action. = 她以强有力的行动号召结束了她的演讲。


近义词：concluding, summarizing, finishing, wrapping up

反义词：beginning, starting, commencing


词性: 动词
