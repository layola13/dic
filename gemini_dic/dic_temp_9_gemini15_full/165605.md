premia:/ˈpriːmiə/| n. |奖金；保险费；溢价|Premiums are payments made to an insurance company in exchange for coverage.  They can also refer to a bonus or reward.|例句：The insurance premia are quite high this year.今年的保险费相当高。|例句：She received a substantial premia for her excellent work.她因出色工作获得了巨额奖金。|近义词：bonus, reward, surcharge, extra charge|反义词：discount, rebate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The insurance company charges high premia. = 保险公司收取高额保费。
He receives a premia for each successful sale. = 他每成功销售一笔就获得一笔奖金。

一般过去时:
The premia for this policy were lower last year. = 去年这项保单的保费较低。
She received a large premia for her contributions to the project. = 她因对项目的贡献而获得了巨额奖金。

现在完成时:
They have paid all their premia on time. = 他们已按时支付了所有保费。
He has earned significant premia through his hard work. = 他通过努力工作赚取了可观的奖金。

过去完成时:
The company had already paid the premia before the deadline. = 公司已在截止日期前支付了保费。
She had received several premia for her research before winning the Nobel Prize. = 在获得诺贝尔奖之前，她已经因为自己的研究获得过几次奖金。

将来时:
We will increase the premia next year. = 我们明年将提高保费。
He will likely receive a sizeable premia for completing the project on time. = 他很可能因为按时完成项目而获得可观的奖金。

虚拟语气:
If the premia were lower, more people would buy insurance. = 如果保费更低，更多人会购买保险。
I wish the premia for my health insurance were less expensive. = 我希望我的健康保险保费更便宜些。

被动语态:
The premia are calculated based on risk assessment. = 保费是根据风险评估计算的。
The premia were paid directly to the insurance company. = 保费直接支付给了保险公司。

疑问句:
What are the premia for this type of insurance? = 这种保险的保费是多少？
How much premia did he receive for his work? = 他因工作获得了多少奖金？

否定句:
The company did not increase the premia this year. = 公司今年没有提高保费。
He did not receive any premia for his efforts. = 他没有因为他的努力获得任何奖金。
