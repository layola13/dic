/juːdiˈɒmətri/ | n. | 气量测定法；气体分析法 | The quantitative analysis of gases, especially the determination of the proportions of oxygen and other gases in a mixture.  Eudiometry is a technique used in chemistry to measure the volume of gases involved in a chemical reaction. | The eudiometry experiment showed a significant decrease in oxygen volume after combustion. 气量测定实验表明燃烧后氧气体积显著减少。 | gas analysis, volumetric analysis |  (暂无明确反义词) | 名词
