muddle-through:/ˈmʌd(ə)l θruː/| v.|勉强应付；混过去；克服困难|To manage to cope with a difficult situation, even though you are not very skillful or organized.|We muddled through the project, despite the lack of resources. 我们尽管缺乏资源，还是勉强完成了项目。|cope, manage, survive|fail, collapse|动词

We muddle through most challenges. = 我们克服大多数挑战。
