nonreflectors: /ˌnɒn.rɪˈflɛktərz/ | n. |非反射体，非反射物 |Objects or surfaces that do not reflect light or other electromagnetic radiation significantly.  They absorb or transmit most of the incident energy. |Example:  Black clothing is often made from nonreflectors to absorb heat from the sun.  黑色衣服通常由非反射材料制成，以吸收来自太阳的热量。|近义词：absorbers, transmitters|反义词：reflectors
