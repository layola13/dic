field's:/fiːldz/| n. |田地；领域；范围|The possessive form of "field," indicating ownership or association.  It refers to something belonging to or connected with a field.  For example, "the field's boundaries" refers to the boundaries of a particular field. |例句：The farmer surveyed the field's yield.  这个农民查看了田地的产量。 The field's research was groundbreaking.  该领域的这项研究具有开创性。|近义词：area, domain, realm|反义词：N/A
