driveways:/ˈdraɪveɪz/| n.|车道，私人车道|A driveway is a private road, usually paved, leading from a street or road to a house or garage.|例句：Our new house has a long, winding driveway.  我们的新房子有一条又长又弯曲的车道。|例句：The driveway was covered in snow. 车道上覆盖着积雪。|近义词：access road, lane, approach|反义词：无
