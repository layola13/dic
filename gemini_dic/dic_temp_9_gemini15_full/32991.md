/ˈkɔːriɔɪd/ | n. |脉络膜 |The choroid is the vascular layer of the eye, located between the retina and the sclera.  It provides nourishment to the outer layers of the retina.  |例句：The choroid's rich blood supply is crucial for maintaining retinal function. 脉络膜丰富的血液供应对于维持视网膜功能至关重要。|例句：Damage to the choroid can lead to vision impairment. 脉络膜损伤会导致视力障碍。|近义词：choriocapillaris (脉络毛细血管层) |反义词：无
