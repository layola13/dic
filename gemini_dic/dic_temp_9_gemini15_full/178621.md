recoverabilities: /rɪˌkʌvərəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可恢复性；可回收性 | The ability to be recovered or reclaimed.  This term often refers to the potential for recovering resources, data, or functionality after a disruption or failure.  It can also refer to the degree to which something can be recovered. | Example:  The system's high recoverabilities ensured minimal downtime after the server crash.  系统的强大恢复能力确保了服务器崩溃后的停机时间最短。 The recoverabilities of the lost files were questionable due to the extent of the hard drive damage. 由于硬盘损坏严重，丢失文件的可恢复性令人怀疑。| 近义词：recoverability, restoration, retrievability | 反义词：irrecoverability, irretrievability |  名词
