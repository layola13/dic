orthometry:/ɔːrˈθɒmɪtri/| n. |正高程测量法；正高测量|The science or practice of determining elevations or heights above a reference surface, usually the geoid,  taking into account the curvature of the Earth.|Example: Orthometry is crucial for accurate mapping and surveying, especially in large-scale projects. 正高程测量法对于精确的测绘和测量至关重要，尤其是在大型项目中。|近义词：geodetic leveling, height measurement|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with methods that don't account for Earth's curvature.|名词
