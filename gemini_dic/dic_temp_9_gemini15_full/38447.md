concessible:/kənˈsɛsəbl/ | adj. | 可让步的；可承认的；可容许的 | capable of being conceded or yielded; admitting of compromise or concession.  |The terms of the contract were concessible to a certain extent. 合同条款在一定程度上是可以让步的。|近义词：allowable, permissible, acceptable |反义词：unyielding, inflexible, uncompromising

This point is concessible in the negotiation. = 在谈判中这一点是可以让步的。

Her argument was concessible, so we reached an agreement. = 她的论点是可以接受的，所以我们达成了协议。
