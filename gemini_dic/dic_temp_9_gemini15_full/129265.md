noddle:/ˈnɒdl/ | n. |傻瓜，笨蛋；头；脑袋 |A noddle is an informal and slightly derogatory term for a foolish or stupid person.  It can also refer to the head. |例句：He's a right noddle for getting caught like that. 他那样被抓住真是个傻瓜。 He nodded his noddle in agreement. 他点头表示同意。|近义词：fool, idiot, dunce, head |反义词：genius, intellect, smart
