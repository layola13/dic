semisocialistic: /ˌsɛmiˌsoʊʃəlˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 半社会主义的，部分社会主义的 |  Relating to or characterized by a system combining elements of socialism and capitalism. It suggests a partial or incomplete implementation of socialist principles within a predominantly capitalist framework.  | The country's economic policy is described as semisocialistic, with state control over key industries but a significant private sector.  这个国家的经济政策被描述为半社会主义的，国家控制关键产业，但私营部门也很重要。| partially socialistic, mixed economy | purely capitalistic, laissez-faire | 形容词
