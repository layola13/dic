old-time:/ˈoʊldˌtaɪm/| adj.|老式的，旧时的，过去的|belonging to or characteristic of a past era; antiquated.  The adjective describes something or someone from a bygone era, often evoking a sense of nostalgia or a simpler time. |例句：He loves listening to old-time radio shows. 他喜欢听老式广播节目。


例句：This old-time photograph shows my great-grandparents on their wedding day. 这张老照片显示了我曾祖父母结婚当天的照片。

近义词：antique, vintage, bygone, antiquated, archaic

反义词：modern, contemporary, new, up-to-date

词性：形容词
