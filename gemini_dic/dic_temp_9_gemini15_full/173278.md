quasi-continual:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kənˈtɪnjuəl/| adj.|几乎连续的，近乎持续的|Almost continuous; nearly constant.  It suggests a degree of interruption or irregularity, unlike "continuous" which implies unbroken consistency.|例句：The quasi-continual rain made travel difficult. 几乎连续的雨使出行困难。
例句：He experienced quasi-continual headaches for weeks. 他几周来一直遭受着几乎持续的头痛。|近义词：nearly continuous, almost constant, intermittent, sporadic|反义词：constant, continuous, sporadic, infrequent
