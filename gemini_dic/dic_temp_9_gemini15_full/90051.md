hospitalizations: /ˌhɒspɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | /ˌhɑːspɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 住院，住院治疗 | The act or process of being admitted to a hospital for treatment.  It refers to multiple instances of hospitalization. | 例句：The rising number of hospitalizations due to the flu is concerning.  流感导致的住院人数不断增加令人担忧。

The hospitalizations related to the car accident were extensive.  与车祸相关的住院治疗非常广泛。


近义词：hospital stays, admissions (to hospital) | 反义词：discharges (from hospital)


词性: 名词 (plural)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Hospitalizations are increasing in the city. = 城市里的住院人数正在增加。
Many hospitalizations result from preventable accidents. = 许多住院是由可预防的事故造成的。


一般过去时:
