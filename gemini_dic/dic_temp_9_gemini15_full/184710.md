resubmitted:/ˌriːsəbˈmɪtɪd/| v. |重新提交，再次提交 |To submit (something) again.  This typically happens after an initial submission has been rejected or needs revision. |例句：The application was resubmitted with the necessary corrections.  申请表已附上必要的更正后重新提交。

例句：The professor asked him to resubmit his essay with the appropriate citations. 教授要求他用适当的引文重新提交他的论文。


近义词：resubmit, re-submit, submit again | 反义词：withdraw, reject


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They resubmit their work regularly. 他们定期重新提交他们的工作。
He resubmits the proposal every week. 他每周重新提交提案。


一般过去时:
She resubmitted the document yesterday. 她昨天重新提交了文件。
We resubmitted the application after making the changes. 我们在做了修改后重新提交了申请。


现在完成时:
They have resubmitted the report three times. 他们已经三次重新提交报告了。
I have resubmitted my application for a second time. 我已经第二次重新提交我的申请了。


过去完成时:
She had resubmitted her thesis before the deadline.  她在截止日期前已经重新提交了她的论文。
He had resubmitted the assignment multiple times before it was finally accepted. 在他的作业最终被接受之前，他已经多次重新提交了。


将来时:
They will resubmit the proposal next week. 他们下周将重新提交提案。
She will resubmit her application after consulting with her advisor. 她将在咨询她的导师后重新提交她的申请。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the requirements, I would have resubmitted my work earlier. 如果我知道要求，我就会早点重新提交我的工作。
If they were to resubmit the project, they would need to address the concerns. 如果他们要重新提交项目，他们需要解决这些问题。


被动语态:
The document was resubmitted by the author. 文件由作者重新提交。
The application was resubmitted after careful review. 申请经过仔细审查后被重新提交。


疑问句:
Has the report been resubmitted yet? 报告已经重新提交了吗？
Will the application be resubmitted before the deadline? 申请会在截止日期前重新提交吗？


否定句:
They did not resubmit the project on time. 他们没有按时重新提交项目。
She has not yet resubmitted her assignments. 她还没有重新提交她的作业。
