regain:/riˈɡeɪn/| vt.|重新获得，恢复|To get something back that you had lost or that was taken from you.  |例句：He regained consciousness after the accident. 他在事故后恢复了知觉。

例句：She regained her composure after a moment of panic. 她惊慌过后很快恢复了镇静。


近义词：recover, retrieve, recoup, repossess|反义词：lose, forfeit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regains his strength slowly after illness. 他生病后慢慢恢复了体力。
She regains her composure easily after stressful situations. 她很容易在压力大的情况下恢复镇静。


一般过去时:
They regained control of the situation quickly. 他们迅速地重新掌控了局面。
The team regained its lead in the final minutes of the game. 球队在比赛的最后几分钟重新取得了领先。


现在完成时:
She has regained her confidence after a period of self-doubt.  她经过一段时间的自我怀疑后重新获得了自信。
He has regained his sight after the surgery. 手术后他恢复了视力。


过去完成时:
They had regained their balance before falling. 他们在跌倒前已经恢复了平衡。
She had regained her composure before addressing the crowd.  她在向人群讲话前已经恢复了镇静。


将来时:
He will regain his health soon. 他很快就会恢复健康。
The company will regain its market share with the launch of the new product. 公司将通过推出新产品来重新获得市场份额。


虚拟语气:
If only he could regain his lost youth! 如果他能够找回失去的青春就好了！
I wish I could regain the trust of my friends. 我希望我能重新获得朋友们的信任。


被动语态:
His lost possessions were never regained. 他丢失的财产从未找回。
Control of the company was quickly regained by the original owners.  公司的控制权很快被原所有者夺回。


疑问句:
Can he regain his position in the company? 他能重新获得公司里的职位吗？
Will they ever regain their lost fortune? 他们会重新获得失去的财富吗？


否定句:
He couldn't regain his balance and fell. 他无法恢复平衡，然后跌倒了。
She failed to regain her composure during the interview.  她在面试期间未能保持镇静。
