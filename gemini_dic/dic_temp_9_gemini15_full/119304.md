microanalysis: /ˌmaɪkroʊəˈnælɪsɪs/ | n. | 微量分析 | The chemical analysis of extremely small samples.  It involves techniques that are sensitive enough to detect and measure very small amounts of substances.  This is often used in fields such as forensic science, environmental monitoring, and materials science. | Example: Microanalysis of the paint sample revealed the presence of a rare pigment, helping to identify the artwork's origin.  微量分析的油漆样本揭示了一种稀有颜料的存在，这有助于确定艺术品的起源。 | 近义词: trace analysis, minute analysis | 反义词: macroanalysis
