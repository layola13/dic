curlable: /ˈkɜːrləbl/ | adj. | 可卷曲的，可弯曲的 | Capable of being curled or bent.  Something that is curlable can easily be shaped into a curl or curve. |例句：Her hair is beautifully curlable. 她的头发非常容易卷曲。

例句：The wire is curlable and easy to shape into different forms. 这根金属丝易于弯曲，可以塑造成不同的形状。


近义词：bendable, flexible, pliable,  twistable

反义词：inflexible, rigid, unyielding, straight
