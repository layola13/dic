corset:/ˈkɔːrsɛt/| n. |紧身胸衣 |A corset is a close-fitting garment worn to support and shape the body, typically worn by women to cinch the waist and enhance the bust.  Historically, corsets were often tightly laced to create a very slender waist.  Modern corsets can be less restrictive and are sometimes worn for fashion or support. |She wore a beautiful, antique corset under her dress. 她穿着一件美丽的古董紧身胸衣在裙子下面。|  The Victorian era saw the peak of corset popularity. 维多利亚时代是紧身胸衣流行的顶峰。| stays, waist cincher | loose clothing, comfortable clothing |
