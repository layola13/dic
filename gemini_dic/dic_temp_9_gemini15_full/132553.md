nondiabetics:/ˌnɒnˌdaɪəˈbɛtɪks/ | n. |非糖尿病患者| People who do not have diabetes.  This term refers to individuals who do not suffer from diabetes mellitus, a metabolic disorder characterized by high blood sugar levels. | Example: The study compared the health outcomes of diabetics and nondiabetics.  这项研究比较了糖尿病患者和非糖尿病患者的健康结果。The researchers found no significant difference in heart disease rates between nondiabetics and those with well-controlled diabetes. 研究人员发现，非糖尿病患者与血糖控制良好的糖尿病患者的心脏病发病率没有显著差异。|近义词:individuals without diabetes, people without diabetes|反义词:diabetics|名词
