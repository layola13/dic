lads:/lædz/| n.|小伙子们，孩子们|A group of boys or young men.|例句：The lads are playing football in the park.  小伙子们正在公园里踢足球。
例句：Those lads are always getting into trouble. 那些小伙子总是惹麻烦。|近义词：boys, young men, fellows|反义词：old men, women
