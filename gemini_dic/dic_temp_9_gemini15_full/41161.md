copy-cat:/ˈkɒpi kæt/ | n. | 仿效者；模仿者 | A person who imitates another, especially in a servile or unoriginal way.  | Example: He's such a copy-cat; he even wears the same clothes as his idol. 他真是个模仿者；他甚至穿着和他偶像一样的衣服。 |近义词：imitator, mimic, follower |反义词：original, innovator
