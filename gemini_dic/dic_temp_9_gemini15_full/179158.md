redigest:/ˌriːˈdaɪdʒɛst/| vt.|重新消化；重新考虑；|To digest again; to reconsider or re-examine (something previously considered).|例句：He decided to redigest the information before making a decision. 他决定在做决定之前重新考虑这些信息。例句：The committee will redigest the proposal before voting on it. 委员会将在投票表决之前重新审议该提案。|近义词：reconsider, review, rethink |反义词：ignore, disregard

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redigests the information daily. = 他每天重新消化信息。
She redigests her notes before the exam. = 她考试前重新温习笔记。

一般过去时:
They redigested the data after finding an error. = 他们发现错误后重新处理了数据。
He redigested the contract before signing. = 他在签字前重新审阅了合同。

现在完成时:
I have redigested the chapter three times. = 我已经重新阅读了这一章节三次。
She has redigested the evidence and changed her mind. = 她重新审视了证据并改变了主意。

过去完成时:
