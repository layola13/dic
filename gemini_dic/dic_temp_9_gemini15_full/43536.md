crawling: /ˈkrɔːlɪŋ/ | v. | 爬行；缓慢移动；充满（讨厌的东西） |  To move on the hands and knees, or with the body close to the ground; to move slowly; to be full of (something unpleasant) | 近义词：creeping, inching, slithering | 反义词：running, sprinting, soaring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The baby is crawling across the floor. = 婴儿正在地板上爬行。
The traffic is crawling along the highway. = 车辆在高速公路上缓慢行驶。


一般过去时:
The snake crawled away into the bushes. = 蛇爬进灌木丛里不见了。
He crawled out of the wreckage. = 他从残骸中爬了出来。


现在完成时:
The plant has been crawling up the wall all summer. = 整个夏天植物都沿着墙往上爬。
She has been crawling with guilt ever since. = 从那以后她就一直感到内疚。


过去完成时:
After the earthquake, survivors crawled from the rubble. = 地震后，幸存者们从瓦砾堆中爬了出来。
He had crawled under the bed to hide from the storm. = 他为了躲避暴风雨而爬到了床底下。


将来时:
The baby will be crawling soon. = 婴儿很快就会爬了。
The line will be crawling with people. = 那条队伍将挤满人。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the ground was so rough, I wouldn't have crawled. = 如果我知道地面这么粗糙，我就不爬了。
I wish I hadn't crawled through that muddy ditch. = 我真希望我没有爬过那个泥泞的水沟。


被动语态:
The floor was crawled over by the baby. = 地板被婴儿爬过了。(不常用)
The wall is being crawled up by ivy. = 常春藤正在爬墙。(不常用)


疑问句:
Is the baby crawling yet? = 婴儿会爬了吗？
Is the traffic crawling again? = 车辆又堵车了吗？


否定句:
The baby isn't crawling yet. = 婴儿还不会爬。
The line wasn't crawling with people. = 那条队伍并没有挤满人。

crawling with insects = 爬满了昆虫

crawling feeling = 令人毛骨悚然的感觉
