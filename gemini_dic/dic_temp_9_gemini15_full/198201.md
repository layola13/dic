short-termism: /ˈʃɔːrt ˈtɜːrmɪzəm/ | n. | 短期主义；目光短浅 |

英文解析:  A tendency to prioritize short-term gains over long-term considerations.  This often involves neglecting the potential negative consequences of decisions made solely for immediate benefits. It can apply to individuals, businesses, or governments.


例句:

The company's short-termism led to a decline in product quality and customer loyalty. 公司的短期主义导致产品质量下降和客户忠诚度下降。

Their short-termism prevented them from investing in research and development, hindering their future growth. 他们的短期主义阻止了他们在研发上的投资，阻碍了他们的未来发展。


近义词:  shortsightedness, myopia, expediency, opportunism

反义词:  long-termism, foresight, vision, strategic planning


词性: Noun
