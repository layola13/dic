prewash's:/ˈpriːwɒʃz/ | n. | 预洗 | The act of washing something before the main wash.  It refers to a preliminary wash, often used to remove loose dirt or stains before a more thorough cleaning. | Example: The prewash cycle on my washing machine is great for removing mud stains from my hiking clothes.  预洗程序在我的洗衣机上非常适合去除我的登山服上的泥污。 | 近义词：pre-cleaning, preliminary wash | 反义词：final wash, main wash
