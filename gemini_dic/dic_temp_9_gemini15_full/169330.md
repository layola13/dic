promoting:/prəˈmoʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 推广；提升；促进 |  The act of furthering the growth or progress of something; advocating or supporting something.  | 例句：The company is promoting its new product through television commercials. 公司正在通过电视广告推广其新产品。  | 例句：He was promoted to manager after working hard for many years. 他努力工作多年后被提升为经理。 | 近义词：advancing, furthering, supporting, advocating | 反义词：hindering, obstructing, suppressing, demoting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are promoting a new healthy lifestyle. = 他们在推广一种新的健康生活方式。
The company promotes its products through social media. = 公司通过社交媒体推广其产品。


一般过去时:
She promoted her business by attending trade shows. = 她通过参加贸易展来推广她的业务。
The government promoted economic growth through tax cuts. = 政府通过减税来促进经济增长。


现在完成时:
They have promoted several employees this year. = 今年他们已经提升了几名员工。
The campaign has successfully promoted awareness of the issue. = 该活动成功地提高了人们对这个问题的认识。


过去完成时:
He had promoted his ideas before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经宣传了他的想法。
The company had promoted its product extensively before the launch. = 公司在产品发布前进行了广泛的推广。


将来时:
They will promote the new film at the Cannes Film Festival. = 他们将在戛纳电影节上宣传这部新电影。
The company will promote its employees based on their performance. = 公司将根据员工的表现来提升员工。


虚拟语气:
If they promoted the product more effectively, they would sell more. = 如果他们更有效地推广产品，他们就会卖得更多。
I wish they had promoted me sooner. = 我希望他们早点提拔我。


被动语态:
The product is being promoted through various channels. = 该产品正在通过各种渠道进行推广。
The employee was promoted to a senior position. = 该员工被提升到高级职位。


疑问句:
Are they promoting the new product effectively? = 他们有效地推广新产品了吗？
How will the company promote its brand this year? = 公司今年将如何推广其品牌？


否定句:
They are not promoting the new product aggressively enough. = 他们对新产品的推广不够积极。
The company did not promote the employee despite his hard work. = 尽管该员工努力工作，但公司没有提升他。
