mattering:/ˈmætərɪŋ/| v. |要紧；有关系；有意义|To be of importance or significance; to have a significant effect or influence.|例句：His opinion matters a great deal to me.  他的意见对我非常重要。

例句：Does it really matter if we're a few minutes late? 我们迟几分钟真的要紧吗？


近义词：importing, signifying, counting, concerning|反义词：unimportant, insignificant, inconsequential|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It matters to me what you think. = 我在乎你的想法。
This detail matters a lot to the overall design. = 这个细节对整体设计非常重要。


一般过去时:
It mattered a great deal to him that he finished the project. = 完成这个项目对他来说非常重要。
What I said yesterday didn't really matter. = 我昨天说的话并不重要。


现在完成时:
It has mattered to her career that she stayed late. = 她为了事业加班，这对她很重要。
It has mattered little what he said; no one listened. = 他说什么都没什么关系，没人听。


过去完成时:
It had mattered to them that they arrived early. = 他们早到对他们来说很重要。
It had mattered little whether he went or not. 他去不去都无关紧要。


将来时:
It will matter to the final result whether you participate or not. = 你是否参与将会影响最终结果。
It won't matter much if we miss the bus. = 即使我们错过公共汽车也没什么大不了的。


虚拟语气:
It would matter if he didn't show up. = 如果他不出现，那将会很重要。
If it mattered, I would have done it differently. = 如果这很重要，我会做得不一样。


被动语态:
It is mattered about that we should protect the environment. = 保护环境这件事很重要。
The matter is being mattered by the committee.  这件事正在由委员会处理。


疑问句:
Does it matter if I'm late? = 我迟到要紧吗？
Does it matter which one I choose? = 我选哪个重要吗？


否定句:
It doesn't matter what he thinks. = 他怎么想都没关系。
It didn't matter to her what others said. = 她不在乎别人说什么。


PHRASE->
It matters to me. = 这对我来说很重要
What matters most is... = 最重要的是...
It doesn't matter. = 没关系
No matter what... = 无论如何...
It matters little... = ...几乎无关紧要
