frenum:/ˈfriːnəm/| n. |系带，小带|A frenum is a small fold of mucous membrane that connects two parts of the body.  It's often found in the mouth, connecting the tongue to the floor of the mouth (lingual frenulum) or the lip to the gum (labial frenulum).|The baby had a tight lingual frenum, which restricted his tongue movement.|近义词：fold, membrane |反义词：N/A
