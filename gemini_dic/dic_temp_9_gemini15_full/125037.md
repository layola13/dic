mulatto:/məˈlætoʊ/| n. |混血儿（尤指黑人和白人的混血儿）|A mulatto is a person of mixed African and European ancestry.  The term is now considered outdated and offensive due to its historical association with racial prejudice and the inherent hierarchy it implies.  It’s often replaced with more inclusive and respectful terms like "biracial" or "mixed-race".|The novel featured a mulatto protagonist who struggled with her identity.|近义词：biracial, mixed-race |反义词：purebred(in the context of race, this is inappropriate and offensive)
