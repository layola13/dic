sireship:/ˈsaɪərʃɪp/| n. |父权；祖先身份；血统；世袭；传承|The right or authority of a sire (father) over his offspring;  the state of being a sire; lineage or ancestry transmitted from a sire.  It can also refer to the inheritance of a title or position. |例句：The sireship of the throne passed to his eldest son. 王位继承权传给了他的长子。|例句：His sireship was unquestioned in the village, due to his family’s long history there.  由于他家族在那里悠久的历史，他在村里的父权是毋庸置疑的。|近义词：fatherhood, patriarchy, lineage, ancestry, heritage |反义词：matriarchy (in contrast to patriarchy)
