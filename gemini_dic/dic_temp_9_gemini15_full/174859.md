racism: /ˈreɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 种族主义 | Prejudice, discrimination, or antagonism directed against a person or people on the basis of their membership in a particular racial or ethnic group, typically one that is a minority or marginalized.  It involves the belief that one's own race is superior to others. |

例句：The company was accused of racism after failing to hire any minority candidates.  这家公司因未能雇佣任何少数族裔候选人而被指控犯有种族歧视罪。

例句：His comments revealed a deep-seated racism that shocked many. 他的言论暴露出根深蒂固的种族主义，令许多人震惊。


近义词：racial prejudice, racial discrimination, bigotry, xenophobia

反义词：racial equality, tolerance, inclusivity


词性：名词
