divulgation:/ˌdɪvʌlˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |泄露；公开；揭露 | The act of divulging; making something known that was previously secret or confidential.  It often implies a revealing of something that should have remained hidden. |例句：The divulgation of the company's secret formula led to a significant drop in sales. 公司秘密配方的泄露导致销售额大幅下降。

例句：The journalist's divulgation of the politician's affair damaged his reputation.  记者披露这位政治家的婚外情损害了他的名誉。


近义词：disclosure, revelation, exposure, unveiling | 反义词：concealment, secrecy, suppression | 词性：名词
