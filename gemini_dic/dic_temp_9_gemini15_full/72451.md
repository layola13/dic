flume:/fluːm/| n.|水槽；矿井水槽；冲刷槽；峡谷|A flume is a channel, typically artificial, for conveying water, especially for irrigation or mining.|例句：The miners used a flume to carry water to their sluice boxes. 矿工们用水槽将水输送到淘金槽中。|近义词：channel, conduit, trough|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water flows through the flume. = 水流经水槽。
This flume carries a significant amount of water. = 这个水槽输送大量的水。


一般过去时:
The old flume collapsed during the storm. = 老水槽在暴风雨中坍塌了。
