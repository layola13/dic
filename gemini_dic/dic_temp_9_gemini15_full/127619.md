needlecord: /ˈniːdlkɔːrd/ | n. | 灯芯绒 | A type of corduroy fabric with very fine wales (raised cords).  It is typically softer and smoother than wider-wale corduroy.  The term "needle" refers to the fineness of the cords. | Example:  The dress was made of a luxurious, deep-blue needlecord.  这件裙子是用奢华的深蓝色灯芯绒制成的。|近义词：corduroy (but with a finer texture), velvet (but with distinct cords) | 反义词：无
