outcompass:/ˌaʊtˈkʌmpəs/ | vt. |超越，胜过 |To surpass or exceed in extent, range, or scope.  |例句：Her ambition outcompasses her abilities.  她的雄心壮志超过了她的能力。

例句：The new bridge outcompasses the old one in length and strength.  新桥在长度和强度上都超过了旧桥。

近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend, outstrip

反义词：be inferior to, be surpassed by


词性:动词
