reindeer:/ˈreɪnˌdɪr/| n. |驯鹿|A type of deer with large antlers, found in arctic and subarctic regions.  They are known for their ability to survive in extremely cold climates and are often used by indigenous peoples for transportation and food. |Example: Santa Claus's sleigh is pulled by reindeer. 圣诞老人的雪橇由驯鹿拉动。 The reindeer migrated south for the winter. 驯鹿向南迁徙过冬。|近义词：caribou (in North America)|反义词：无
