chaplets:/ˈtʃæplɪts/| n. |花冠；念珠；串珠；花环|A chaplet is a wreath or garland, especially one of flowers, worn on the head or used for decoration.  It can also refer to a string of beads used in prayer. |例句：The bride wore a chaplet of roses in her hair. 新娘头上戴着玫瑰花冠。

The nuns used a chaplet to count their prayers. 修女们用念珠数祈祷次数。|近义词：wreath, garland, rosary, beads |反义词:None specifically applicable.
