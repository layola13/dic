deconstructs:/ˌdiːkənˈstrʌkts/| vt.|解构；分析；拆解|To analyze and critically examine the structure or meaning of (something, typically a text or idea) in order to expose its hidden assumptions and contradictions.|例句：The essay deconstructs the traditional notion of heroism. 这篇文章解构了传统的英雄主义观念。

例句：Literary critics often deconstruct classic novels to reveal underlying themes. 文学评论家经常解构经典小说以揭示其潜在主题。


近义词：analyze, dissect, dismantle, break down

反义词：construct, build, synthesize, create

词性：动词
