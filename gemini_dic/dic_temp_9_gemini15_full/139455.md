obstacle: /ˈɒbstəkl/ /ˈɑːbstəkl/ | n. |障碍；阻碍 | Something that blocks one's way or progress.  An obstacle can be physical, like a wall, or metaphorical, like a problem or difficulty. |例句：The biggest obstacle to peace is the lack of trust between the two sides.  最大的和平障碍是双方之间缺乏信任。

例句：He overcame many obstacles to achieve his goal. 他克服了许多障碍才实现了他的目标。


近义词：hindrance, impediment, obstruction, barrier, hurdle

反义词：assistance, help, aid, advantage


词性：名词
