metrosexual:/ˌmɛtroʊˈsɛkʃuəl/| n. |都市型男性；注重个人形象的男性|A metrosexual is a heterosexual man who enjoys shopping, fashion, and grooming, typically spending significant time and money on these pursuits.  They are often associated with a refined and stylish image.  The term implies a man who is comfortable with and embraces traditionally feminine aspects of self-care and presentation. |Example: He's a metrosexual; he spends hours in the salon each week. 他是个都市型男性；他每周都会在沙龙里待上好几个小时。|近义词：stylish man, well-groomed man, fashion-conscious man|反义词：sloppy man, unkempt man
