nonrhetorically:/ˌnɒnˌrɛˈtɒrɪkli/| adv.|非修辞地，非花言巧语地|Without rhetorical flourish or exaggeration; literally.  It emphasizes a direct and straightforward approach, devoid of embellishment or persuasive language.|例句：He answered nonrhetorically, "I don't know." 他直截了当地回答说：“我不知道。”

例句：She stated nonrhetorically that the project was behind schedule. 她直截了当地说项目进度落后了。


近义词：literally, directly, plainly, frankly


反义词：rhetorically, figuratively, poetically


词性:副词
