retuned:/riˈtjuːnd/ | v. | 重新调整；重新校准 | To adjust or calibrate something again; to tune something again.  |例句：The mechanic retuned the engine to improve its performance. 技工重新调整了发动机以提高其性能。

The guitarist retuned his instrument before the concert. 吉他手在音乐会前重新调音了乐器。 |近义词：readjusted, recalibrated, re-tuned |反义词：misaligned, out of tune
