cannery:/ˈkænəri/| n.|罐头厂，鱼类加工厂|A cannery is a factory where food, especially fish or fruit, is canned.  |例句：The town's economy relies heavily on its cannery. 这个镇的经济严重依赖其罐头厂。

例句：The workers at the salmon cannery were on strike.  鲑鱼罐头厂的工人正在罢工。


近义词：packing plant, processing plant|反义词：无|词性:名词
