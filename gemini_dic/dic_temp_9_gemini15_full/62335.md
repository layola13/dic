endemic:/ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 地方性的；特有的；（疾病）地方流行的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a particular place or people; native or indigenous.  An endemic disease is one that is regularly found among particular people or in a certain area. |

例句：

Malaria is endemic in many tropical countries.  疟疾在许多热带国家是地方性疾病。

The koala is endemic to Australia. 考拉是澳大利亚特有的动物。


近义词：native, indigenous, local, prevalent

反义词：exotic, foreign, alien, cosmopolitan


词性：形容词
