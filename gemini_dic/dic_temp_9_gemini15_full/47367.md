deactivated:/ˈdiːæktɪveɪtɪd/| adj. |失效的；被停用的；钝化的|  Made inactive; rendered powerless or ineffective.  Deactivated accounts can no longer be used to log in.  |近义词：disabled, inactive, inoperative |反义词：activated, enabled, active


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system is deactivated. = 系统已停用。
This button is deactivated. = 此按钮已停用。

一般过去时:

My account was deactivated yesterday. = 我的账户昨天被停用了。

过去完成时:

将来时:
