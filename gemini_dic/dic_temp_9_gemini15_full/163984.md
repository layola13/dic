predicts:/prɪˈdɪkts/| vt.|预言，预测|To say or estimate that (something) will happen in the future or will be a consequence of something.|The weatherman predicts rain for tomorrow.  气象员预测明天会下雨。|The scientists predict a rise in sea levels. 科学家预测海平面会上升。|forecasts, anticipates, foresees| surprises, defies |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He predicts the stock market will rise. 他预测股市会上涨。
She predicts that it will snow tomorrow. 她预测明天会下雪。


一般过去时:
The fortune teller predicted a long and happy life for me.  算命先生预言我将拥有漫长而幸福的一生。
They predicted the earthquake accurately. 他们准确地预测了地震。


现在完成时:
Scientists have predicted a significant increase in global temperatures. 科学家已经预测全球气温会显著上升。
She has predicted the winner of the race three times in a row. 她已经连续三次预测了比赛的获胜者。


过去完成时:
They had predicted a storm, so they stayed home. 他们预料到会有暴风雨，所以待在家里。
She had predicted the outcome before the experiment began. 实验开始之前她就预测了结果。


将来时:
Experts will predict the election results tonight. 专家们将在今晚预测选举结果。
I will predict the future of your life, but I will charge you a hefty price. 我将预测你未来的生活，但我将收取你一大笔费用。


虚拟语气:
If I had predicted the market crash, I would have made a fortune. 如果我预测到了市场崩盘，我就会发财了。
I wish I had predicted the winner of the lottery. 我真希望我预测到了彩票的赢家。


被动语态:
The outcome of the election was predicted by many experts.  许多专家预测了选举的结果。
The weather was predicted to be sunny. 天气预报是晴天。


疑问句:
What does he predict will happen next? 他预测接下来会发生什么？
Does she predict a successful outcome? 她预测结果会成功吗？


否定句:
I cannot predict the future. 我无法预测未来。
They did not predict the economic downturn. 他们没有预测到经济衰退。
