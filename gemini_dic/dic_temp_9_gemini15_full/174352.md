quicksand: /ˈkwɪksænd/ | n. |流沙 |Loose sand that behaves like a liquid when disturbed, often found on riverbanks or coastlines. It is characterized by its ability to trap and engulf objects or people.  |例句：He nearly got stuck in the quicksand. 他差点陷进流沙里。|例句：The explorer was swallowed by the quicksand. 探险家被流沙吞噬了。|近义词：mire, bog, morass |反义词：solid ground, firm ground |名词
