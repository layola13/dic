nonpermeative: /ˌnɒnˈpɜːrmɪətiv/ | adj. | 非渗透性的；不能渗透的 | Not allowing substances to pass through.  This adjective describes something that resists the passage of liquids, gases, or other substances.  It is often used in the context of membranes or barriers. |


例句：

The nonpermeative membrane prevented the leakage of chemicals.  =  非渗透性膜阻止了化学物质的泄漏。

The cell wall acts as a nonpermeative barrier to many substances. = 细胞壁对许多物质起着非渗透性屏障的作用。


近义词：impermeable, impervious, occlusive


反义词：permeable, penetrable


词性：形容词
