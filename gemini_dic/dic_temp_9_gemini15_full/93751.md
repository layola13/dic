immaturity:/ˌɪmæˈtʃʊərɪti/| n. |不成熟；幼稚；未成熟|The state or quality of being immature; lack of maturity.  It can refer to a lack of emotional, mental, or physical development appropriate for one's age or stage of life.|例句：His immaturity prevented him from understanding the consequences of his actions.  他的不成熟使他无法理解自己行为的后果。

例句：The immaturity of the fruit made it unsuitable for eating. 水果未成熟，不适合食用。


近义词：infantilism, childishness, juvenility|反义词：maturity, adulthood, ripeness|词性：名词

词源:
The word "immaturity" comes from the prefix "im-" (meaning "not") + "maturity."  "Maturity" itself comes from the Latin word "maturitas," meaning "ripeness," "full development."  Therefore, "immaturity" literally means "not ripeness" or "lack of full development."
