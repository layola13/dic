semistiffness:/ˈsɛmiˈstɪfnɪs/| n.|半刚性，半硬度|The property of being somewhat stiff but not completely rigid.  It describes a material or substance that exhibits some resistance to bending or deformation but is not entirely inflexible. |例句：The semistiffness of the brush allowed for controlled application of paint.  半刚性的画笔允许对油漆进行受控的涂抹。
The semistiffness of the fabric made it ideal for the design.  这种布料的半刚性使其成为设计的理想选择。|近义词：partial rigidity, moderate stiffness |反义词：flexibility, pliability
