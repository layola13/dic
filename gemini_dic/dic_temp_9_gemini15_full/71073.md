firsts:/fɜːrstz/| n.|首次；第一批；最初的东西|The plural of "first".  Refers to the initial occurrences or instances of something.  It can also refer to the things that came first in a series or group.|例句：His firsts in the race were remarkable. 他比赛中的几次领先都令人瞩目。

例句：The museum showcased the artist's early firsts. 博物馆展出了这位艺术家早期的作品。


近义词：beginnings, debuts, premiers,initials


反义词：lasts, finales, conclusions


词性：名词
