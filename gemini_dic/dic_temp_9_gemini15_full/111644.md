locate:/ləˈkeɪt/ | vt. | 定位；找出；位于 | To find the place or position of something or someone.  |  例句：Police are trying to locate the missing child.  警方正在努力寻找失踪的孩子。|例句：The factory is located in the countryside. 工厂位于农村。| 近义词：find, discover, pinpoint, situate | 反义词：misplace, lose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I locate my keys every morning. = 我每天早上都找到我的钥匙。
We locate the problem in the software. = 我们找到了软件中的问题。

一般过去时:
They located the missing document. = 他们找到了丢失的文件。
She located her friend's house easily. = 她很容易就找到了她朋友的家。

现在完成时:
I have located the source of the error. = 我已经找到了错误的根源。
They have located the missing person. = 他们已经找到了失踪的人。

过去完成时:
We had located the treasure before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，我们已经找到了宝藏。
She had located the perfect dress before the party started. = 派对开始前，她已经找到了完美的裙子。

将来时:
I will locate the file for you tomorrow. = 我明天会帮你找到那个文件。
They will locate the best solution to the problem. = 他们将找到解决这个问题的最佳方案。

虚拟语气:
If I could locate the information, I would share it with you. = 如果我能找到这些信息，我会和你分享。
I wish I could locate my lost phone. = 我真希望我能找到我丢失的手机。


被动语态:
The missing car was located by the police. = 失踪的汽车被警方找到了。
The problem was quickly located and fixed. = 问题很快就被找到并解决了。

疑问句:
Can you locate the information on the website? = 你能在这个网站上找到信息吗？
Where is the library located? = 图书馆位于何处？

否定句:
I couldn't locate the restaurant on the map. = 我在地图上找不到那家餐馆。
They failed to locate the missing aircraft. = 他们没能找到失踪的飞机。


PHRASE->
locate oneself = 安顿下来
locate something precisely = 精确地找出某物
be located in/at/on = 位于……
locate a resource = 找到资源
locate a problem = 找到问题


词源:
The word "locate" comes from the Latin word "locus," meaning "place."  The verb form evolved through Old French and Middle English.
