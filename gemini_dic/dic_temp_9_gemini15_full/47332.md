depressingly:/dɪˈprɛsɪŋli/ | adv. |令人沮丧地，令人灰心地 |  The adverb "depressingly" describes something in a way that causes sadness, discouragement, or dejection. It emphasizes the negative and disheartening nature of a situation or event. |例句：The weather has been depressingly grey all week.  = 这周的天气一直令人沮丧地阴沉。
The sales figures were depressingly low. = 销售额低得令人沮丧。|近义词：discouragingly, dishearteningly, dispiritingly |反义词：encouragingly, upliftingly, inspiringly
