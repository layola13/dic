rotatoria:/ˌroʊtəˈtɔːriə/| n. |轮虫，轮虫动物|Rotatoria is the plural of rotatorian, referring to a phylum of microscopic aquatic invertebrates characterized by a rotating corona of cilia used for feeding and locomotion. They are found in various freshwater and marine habitats. | Example: The pond water sample contained numerous rotatoria.  该池塘水样中含有许多轮虫。|近义词：rotifer (singular)|反义词：无
