schooners:/ˈskuːnərz/| n. |双桅纵帆船；（尤指）双桅平底帆船|A schooner is a sailing vessel with two or more masts, typically with the foremast shorter than the mainmast and the sails rigged fore-and-aft (i.e., running parallel to the keel).  Schooners were historically important for cargo transport and fishing.|例句：The old schooner sailed gracefully across the bay. 这艘旧帆船优雅地驶过海湾。

The captain steered the schooner through the stormy seas. 船长驾着双桅船驶过暴风雨的海面。|近义词：sailing ship, brigantine|反义词：motorboat, steamship
