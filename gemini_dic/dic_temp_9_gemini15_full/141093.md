onsen:/ˈɒnzen/ | n. | 温泉；日式温泉浴场 | A hot spring in Japan; a public bathhouse using hot spring water.  | Example: We relaxed in a traditional onsen after a long hike. 我们在长途跋涉后在一个传统的温泉里放松身心。|近义词：hot spring, spa | 反义词：cold spring
