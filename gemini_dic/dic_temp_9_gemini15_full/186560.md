riders:/ˈraɪdərz/| n.|骑手；乘客；条款；(法律文件等的)附加条款|People who ride horses or other animals, or who travel on a vehicle.  A rider can also refer to a clause added to a document. |例句：The riders galloped across the field. 骑手们飞驰过田野。
The bus was full of riders. 公共汽车上挤满了乘客。


例句：The contract included a rider protecting the buyer. 合同中包含一个保护买方的附加条款。

近义词：horsemen, passengers, clauses, addenda

反义词：drivers (in the context of vehicles), pedestrians
