duplicated:/ˈduːplɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 重复的，复制的 | Made to be exactly like something else.  Existing in two or more identical forms. |例句：The document was a duplicated copy.  这份文件是复印件。

The system flagged duplicated entries in the database. 系统标记了数据库中重复的条目。


近义词：repeated, copied, replicated, reproduced | 反义词：original, unique, singular | 词性：形容词

词源:
