dialyzed:/ˈdaɪəlaɪzd/| v. adj.|透析过的；已透析的|  To remove waste products from (blood) by dialysis.  Dialyzed refers to a substance, typically blood, that has undergone dialysis, a process of removing excess water, salts, and waste products from the body. |例句：The patient's blood was dialyzed to remove toxins. 病人的血液经过透析以去除毒素。The dialyzed solution was clear and free of impurities. 透析后的溶液清澈透明，无杂质。|近义词：purified, cleansed|反义词：contaminated, impure|动词
