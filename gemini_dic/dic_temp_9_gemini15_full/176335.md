re-equip:/ˌriːɪˈkwɪp/| vt.|重新装备；再配备|To provide (someone or something) again with equipment.  The verb "re-equip" implies a previous equipping, which is being renewed or replaced.|例句：The army will re-equip its soldiers with the latest technology.  军队将用最新技术重新装备其士兵。

例句：The factory was re-equipped with modern machinery. 工厂重新配备了现代化机器。


近义词：refit, rearm, restock|反义词：disarm, deplete|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-equip the classrooms every year. = 他们每年都重新装备教室。
The company re-equips its factories regularly. = 这家公司定期重新装备其工厂。


一般过去时:
The school re-equipped its science labs last summer. = 学校去年夏天重新装备了它的科学实验室。
The army re-equipped its troops before the mission. = 部队在执行任务之前重新装备了部队。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The team had re-equipped itself with new tools before the competition. = 比赛前，团队已经用新的工具重新装备了自己。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
