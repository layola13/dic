fierily:/ˈfaɪərɪli/ | adv. | 猛烈地；生气地；热情地 | In a fiery manner; with great intensity of feeling or action.  |例句：He spoke fierily about the injustice he had suffered. 他愤怒地谈论了他遭受的不公正待遇。 He argued fierily for his beliefs. 他热情地为自己的信仰辩论。|近义词：passionately, intensely, vehemently, angrily |反义词：calmly, placidly, gently
