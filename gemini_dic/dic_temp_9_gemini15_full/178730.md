rectificator: /ˈrɛktɪfaɪkətər/ | n. | 整流器 | A device that converts alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC).  It is often used in power supplies to provide a stable DC voltage from an AC source. |  The rectifier converts the AC power from the wall outlet to the DC power needed by the computer.  整流器将来自墙壁插座的交流电转换为计算机所需的直流电。 |  converter, transformer | inverter | n.
