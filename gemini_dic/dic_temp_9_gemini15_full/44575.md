crouchingly:/ˈkraʊtʃɪŋli/| adv. | 弯腰地；蹲伏地；卑躬屈膝地 |  Describes an action performed in a crouching or low posture, often implying stealth, subservience, or awkwardness. |例句：He moved crouchingly through the undergrowth. 他弯着腰穿过灌木丛。

例句：She answered crouchingly, avoiding eye contact. 她弯腰回答，避免眼神接触。


近义词：stealthily, submissively, humbly, awkwardly


反义词：uprightly, confidently, boldly


词性：副词
