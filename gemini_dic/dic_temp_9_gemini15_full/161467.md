potashes:/pəˈtæʃiz/| n.|草碱；碳酸钾；钾肥|Potashes are the ashes of plants, particularly those rich in potassium, that have been leached with water to extract the potassium carbonate (potassium salts).  They are used primarily as a fertilizer due to their high potassium content.|例句：The farmer used potashes to enrich the soil.  这位农民用草碱来改良土壤。|近义词：potassium carbonate, potash|反义词：N/A
