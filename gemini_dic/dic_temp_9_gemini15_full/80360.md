goldstar:/ˈɡoʊldˌstɑːr/| n. |金星；金星奖；优秀奖 |A gold star is a star-shaped decoration, often gold-colored, given as a reward for achievement or good behavior.  The term can also refer to a prestigious award or recognition. |例句：She received a gold star for her excellent work. 她因为优秀的工作获得了金星奖。

例句：He earned a gold star for perfect attendance. 他因为全勤获得了金星奖。


近义词：award, prize, commendation, accolade |反义词：punishment, penalty, reprimand |词性:名词
