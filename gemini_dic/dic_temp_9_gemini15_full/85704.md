haves:/hævz/ | n. |所有物；财产；（have的第三人称复数形式）|The word "haves" refers to those who possess wealth, power, or other advantages.  It's often used in contrast to "have-nots".|The haves control most of the resources.  有产者控制着大部分资源。|The haves and have-nots are growing further apart.  富人和穷人之间的差距越来越大。|possessions, assets |have-nots, deprived, needy|名词
