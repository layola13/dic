moderateness:/ˈmɒdəritnis/ /ˈmɑːdəreɪtnəs/ | n. |适度；温和；中庸 |The quality or state of being moderate; avoidance of extremes.  Moderateness in all things is a virtue.  |例句：His approach to the problem showed great moderateness. 他解决问题的方法显示出极大的适度。

例句：The recipe calls for moderateness in the use of spices.  食谱要求适量使用香料。


近义词：temperance, moderation, mildness |反义词：extremism, excess, immoderation
