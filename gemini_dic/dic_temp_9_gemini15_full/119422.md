microgametophyte:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈɡæmɪtoʊfaɪt/| n. |小配子体|A small, multicellular haploid gametophyte, typically found in plants, that produces gametes (sex cells).  It's usually much smaller than the megagametophyte. |Example: In seed plants, the microgametophyte is represented by the pollen grain.  在种子植物中，小配子体由花粉粒代表。|近义词: pollen grain (in seed plants), male gametophyte|反义词: megagametophyte
