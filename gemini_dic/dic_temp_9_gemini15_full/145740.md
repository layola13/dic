overcrowded:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊdɪd/| adj.|拥挤的，过度拥挤的| excessively crowded; filled to excess.  The adjective describes a place or situation where there are too many people or things for the available space. |例句：The train was overcrowded, and I had to stand the entire journey. 这列火车非常拥挤，我不得不站着全程。

The city streets were overcrowded during the festival. 节日期间，城市街道非常拥挤。

近义词：packed, congested, jammed, crowded

反义词：sparse, empty, uncrowded


词性：形容词
