finger: /ˈfɪŋɡər/ | n. |手指 | A finger is one of the five long, thin parts at the end of your hand.  Fingers are used for grasping, pointing, and other fine motor skills.  |例句：I cut my finger while chopping vegetables. = 切菜的时候我割伤了手指。

例句：She pointed her finger at the culprit. = 她指着罪犯。


近义词：digit | 反义词：None


词源:  The word "finger" comes from the Old English word "finger," which is related to other Germanic words for "finger."  The ultimate origin is Proto-Germanic *fingraz.
