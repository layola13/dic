milt:/mɪlt/| n.|鱼白；精液；脾脏|The soft roe of a fish, especially the male, containing sperm.  It is also sometimes used to refer to the spleen.|例句：The milt of the salmon is highly prized in some cuisines. 三文鱼的鱼白在某些菜系中备受推崇。
例句：The fisherman carefully removed the milt from the freshly caught cod. 渔民小心地从刚捕获的鳕鱼中取出鱼白。|近义词：soft roe, sperm (in the context of fish)|反义词：roe (referring to the female fish's eggs)|名词
