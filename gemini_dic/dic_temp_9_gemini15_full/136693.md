nonsedentary: /ˌnɒnˈsedəntəri/ | adj. |非久坐的，好动的| Not sedentary; active.  The word describes a lifestyle or activity that involves a lot of movement and physical activity, as opposed to sitting for long periods.  It emphasizes the opposite of a sedentary lifestyle. | Example:  He leads a nonsedentary life, spending his days hiking and biking. 他过着非久坐的生活，每天都在徒步旅行和骑自行车。  She chose a nonsedentary job that involved a lot of travel. 她选择了一份需要大量旅行的非久坐的工作。|近义词：active, mobile, dynamic, itinerant |反义词：sedentary, inactive, stationary
