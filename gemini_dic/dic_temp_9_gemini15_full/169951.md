prostration:/ˈprɒstreɪʃən/| n. |沮丧；衰竭；卧倒；俯伏 | The state of being prostrate;  a state of extreme physical or mental weakness.  It can refer to lying flat on the ground, often in worship or submission, or to a state of exhaustion or helplessness. |例句：His prostration after the marathon was evident. 他马拉松后的虚脱很明显。

The intense heat caused prostration among the workers. 酷热导致工人们虚脱。


近义词：collapse, exhaustion, debility, weakness, helplessness, submission, defeat

反义词：strength, vigor, energy, vitality, uprightness, defiance


词源:From Old French *prostracion, from Latin prostratio, from prostratus, past participle of prosternere "to throw down, prostrate," from pro- "forward" + sternere "to spread, strew."
