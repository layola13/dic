overclocked:/ˌoʊvərˈklɑːkt/|/ˈoʊvərˌklɒkt/| adj.|超频的；超速运转的|Operating a computer component (such as a CPU or GPU) at a higher clock speed than its rated speed. This can improve performance but may also lead to instability or damage.|例句：My computer runs much faster now that I've overclocked the CPU. = 我的电脑超频CPU后运行速度快多了。
The overclocked graphics card delivered stunning visuals. = 超频的显卡提供了令人惊叹的视觉效果。|近义词：speed up, boosted|反义词：underclocked


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The system is overclocked. = 系统超频了。
He overclocks his computer regularly. = 他经常超频他的电脑。


一般过去时:
I overclocked my GPU yesterday. = 我昨天超频了我的显卡。
They overclocked the server to handle the increased load. = 他们超频了服务器来处理增加的负载。


现在完成时:
We have overclocked the processor several times. = 我们已经多次超频了处理器。

过去完成时:

将来时:
