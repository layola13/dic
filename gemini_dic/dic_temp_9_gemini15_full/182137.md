remittee:/rɛmɪˈtiː/ | n. | 受款人，汇款受领人 | The person or entity to whom money is sent or remitted.  A remittee receives funds through a remittance. | Example: The remittee received the funds promptly after the transaction was completed.  受款人迅速收到了交易完成后汇出的款项。 The bank confirmed that the remittee's account had been credited. 银行确认受款人的账户已入账。 |近义词：payee, recipient | 反义词：remitter (汇款人) | 名词
