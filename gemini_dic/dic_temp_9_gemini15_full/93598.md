imbarn: /ɪmˈbɑːrn/ | v. | 收进谷仓；储存 | To store something, typically grain, in a barn.  |  The farmer imbarned the wheat after the harvest.  农夫收割后把小麦收进谷仓。  | 近义词：store, garner, hoard | 反义词：unload, remove, disburse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imbarns the hay every autumn. 他每年秋天都把干草收进谷仓。
They imbarn the harvested grain for winter. 他们把收割的谷物储存在谷仓里过冬。


一般过去时:
The farmer imbarned all the corn last week.  农夫上周把所有的玉米都收进谷仓了。
They imbarned the apples before the first frost. 他们在第一次霜冻来临之前把苹果收进了谷仓。


现在完成时:
The farmer has imbarned all his crops this year.  今年农夫已经把所有的农作物都收进了谷仓。
They have imbarned a significant amount of grain this season. 这个季节他们已经收进了大量的谷物。


过去完成时:
They had imbarned the majority of the harvest before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把大部分收成收进了谷仓。
The farmer had imbarned the wheat before the price dropped.  价格下跌之前，农夫已经把小麦收进了谷仓。

He will imbarn the potatoes next week. 他下周将把土豆收进谷仓。
They will imbarn the barley as soon as it is dry enough.  大麦干透后，他们就会把它收进谷仓。

If it rained tomorrow, we would imbarn the crops immediately. 如果明天下雨，我们会立即把庄稼收进谷仓。
I wish we had imbarned the hay before the rain started.  我希望我们在下雨之前就把干草收进谷仓了。
