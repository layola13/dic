fissions:/ˈfɪʃənz/| n.|裂变；分裂；分歧|The process by which the nucleus of an atom splits into two or more smaller nuclei, releasing a large amount of energy.  It also refers to the splitting or division of something into parts. |例句：Nuclear fission is a powerful source of energy. 核裂变是强大的能源。

The political party experienced several fissions due to internal disagreements.  由于内部意见分歧，该政党经历了几次分裂。|近义词：splits, divisions, separations |反义词：fusions, unifications, mergers
