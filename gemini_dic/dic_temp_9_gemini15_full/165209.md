preinitiate:/ˌpriːɪˈnɪʃieɪt/| vt.|预先启动；预先发起；预先开始|To begin or start something before it is officially or formally begun.|The committee decided to preinitiate discussions before the official meeting. 委员会决定在正式会议之前预先开始讨论。|近义词：initiate, commence, begin, start|反义词：conclude, finish, end|动词


USAGE->

The company preinitiates marketing campaigns months in advance. = 公司提前几个月预先启动了营销活动。


一般过去时:
