polling-booths:/ˈpoʊlɪŋ buːθz/ | n. |投票站；投票间 |  A polling booth is a small, enclosed space where voters cast their ballots in an election.  It is designed to ensure secrecy and privacy. | Example:  The polling booths were crowded on election day.  选举日投票站人满为患。  The new polling booths are more accessible to people with disabilities. 新的投票站更方便残疾人使用。|近义词：voting booth, ballot box (less precise)|反义词：N/A
