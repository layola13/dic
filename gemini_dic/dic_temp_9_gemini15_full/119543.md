/ˈmaɪkroʊsiːm/| n. |语义单位，微义素|A microseme is the smallest unit of meaning in a word or morpheme. It is a component of a seme, and it represents a single, indivisible aspect of meaning.  Microsemes are often difficult to isolate and define precisely, as they are often interwoven and interdependent.  They are a key concept in componential analysis, a method used in semantics to break down the meaning of words into their constituent parts.|The word "bachelor" has several microsemes, including "unmarried," "adult," and "male."|近义词：seme (but smaller and more specific)|反义词：N/A
