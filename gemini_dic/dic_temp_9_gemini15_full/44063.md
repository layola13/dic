crinite:/ˈkraɪnaɪt/| adj.|发状的，毛发的|  Descriptive of a rock or mineral with a fibrous or hair-like texture.  It often refers to a variety of asbestos or other fibrous minerals resembling hair.|例句：The crinite asbestos was once widely used in insulation.  这种发状石棉曾经广泛用于绝缘。|The rock sample exhibited a distinct crinite structure.  岩石样本呈现出明显的毛发状结构。|近义词：fibrous, hairy, capillary|反义词：amorphous, massive
