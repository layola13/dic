calvinist:/ˈkælvɪnɪst/| adj. n. |加尔文主义的；加尔文主义者|Relating to or characteristic of Calvinism, a major branch of Protestant Christianity emphasizing God's sovereignty, predestination, and the absolute authority of the Bible.  A follower of John Calvin and his theological teachings.|例句：His strict moral code stemmed from his Calvinist upbringing.  他的严格道德准则源于他的加尔文主义教育。

例句：Many Calvinist churches are known for their austere simplicity. 许多加尔文教教堂以其朴素而闻名。


近义词：Puritan (in some contexts), Reformed


反义词：Arminian (theological opposite)


词性：adjective, noun
