duvet:/ˈduːveɪ/| n. |羽绒被，羽绒被罩 | A duvet is a type of bedding consisting of a soft, flat bag filled with down, feathers, or synthetic filling, used as a bed covering.  It is typically used with a duvet cover. | Example:  I snuggled under the warm duvet. 我依偎在温暖的羽绒被下。  She changed the duvet cover for a fresh one. 她换上了干净的被套。|近义词：comforter, quilt |反义词：sheet (as a direct contrast, not an antonym)
