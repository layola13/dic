horologist:/hɒˈrɒlədʒɪst/| n. |钟表匠；计时器专家|A horologist is a skilled craftsperson who makes, repairs, and restores clocks and watches.  They possess expert knowledge of horological mechanisms and techniques.|例句：The horologist meticulously cleaned and repaired the antique clock.  这位钟表匠一丝不苟地清洁和修理了那座古董钟。  The master horologist was renowned for his intricate designs.  这位大师级的钟表匠以其精巧的设计而闻名。|近义词：watchmaker, clockmaker|反义词：无
