evangelist:/ɪˈvæn(d)ʒəlɪst/| n.| 福音传道者，热心拥护者 | A person who actively seeks to convert others to a particular belief or cause.  An evangelist is someone who zealously promotes a particular cause or idea.  In a religious context, it typically refers to a preacher of the Gospel. |例句：He is a passionate evangelist for environmental protection. 他是一位热情的环保宣传者。

例句：The evangelist traveled the country, spreading the Gospel.  这位福音传道者走遍全国各地传播福音。


近义词：preacher, missionary, advocate, proponent, crusader

反义词：opponent, critic, skeptic


词性:名词
