non-performance:/ˌnɒn pərˈfɔːməns/| n. |不履行；未履行；未完成 |The failure to fulfill a contractual obligation or expectation.  It refers to a situation where a party fails to perform its duties as agreed upon in a contract or agreement. This can lead to legal consequences and remedies for the aggrieved party. |例句：The contractor's non-performance of the contract resulted in significant delays. 承包商未履行合同导致了严重的延误。

The company faced legal action due to its non-performance on the project. 公司因项目未完成而面临法律诉讼。


近义词：breach, default, failure, omission |反义词：performance, fulfillment, completion |名词
