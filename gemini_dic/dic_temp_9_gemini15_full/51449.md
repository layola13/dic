dethronization:/ˌdiːθroʊnaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |废黜；退位；罢黜|The act of dethroning someone, removing them from a position of power or authority.  It often refers to the removal of a monarch from their throne.|The dethronization of the king led to a period of political instability. 国王的废黜导致了一段时间的政治动荡。 The dethronization of the champion was unexpected. 冠军被击败是意料之外的。|近义词：deposition, ousting, removal|反义词：enthronement, installation, elevation|名词
