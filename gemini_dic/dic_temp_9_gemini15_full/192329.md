例句：Many scribes worked tirelessly to preserve the knowledge of the past. 许多抄写员不知疲倦地工作以保存过去的知识。


近义词：copyist, writer, author, chronicler | 反义词：reader, interpreter


词源：源自古法语 *scribe, 来自拉丁语 scriba，意为“抄写员”。  The Latin word is related to the verb scribere, "to write".
