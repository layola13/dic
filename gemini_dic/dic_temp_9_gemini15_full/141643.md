optimism:/ˈɒptɪmɪzəm/| /ˈɑːptɪmɪzəm/ | n. |乐观主义；乐观；乐观态度 |The belief that good things will happen in the future.  A general tendency to expect good outcomes. |例句：Her optimism is infectious.  她的乐观精神具有感染力。

例句：Despite the challenges, she maintained a spirit of optimism. 尽管面临挑战，她仍然保持着乐观的精神。


近义词：hopefulness, cheerfulness, positivism, sanguinity | 反义词：pessimism, negativity, cynicism, despair


词源:
The word "optimism" originated in the 17th century, derived from the Latin word "optimus," meaning "best."  It was coined by the philosopher Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz, who contrasted it with "pessimism."  The "-ism" suffix indicates a belief system or doctrine.
