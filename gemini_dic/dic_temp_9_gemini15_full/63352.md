entombment:/ɪnˈtuːmbmənt/ | n. | 埋葬；入葬 | The act of placing a body in a tomb or grave; burial.  |例句：The entombment of the pharaoh was a complex and elaborate ritual. 法老的安葬是一个复杂而精细的仪式。

例句：His entombment took place in the family mausoleum. 他的安葬仪式在家族陵墓举行。


近义词：burial, interment, sepulture | 反义词：exhumation
