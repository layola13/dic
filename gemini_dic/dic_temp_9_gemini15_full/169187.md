proliferator:/prəˈlɪfəreɪtər/ | n. | 增殖剂，增生剂 | A substance or agent that promotes or causes proliferation (rapid increase in numbers).  It often refers to cells or organisms. | Example:  The researchers identified a novel proliferator that stimulated significant cell growth in the laboratory.  研究人员发现了一种新型增殖剂，它在实验室中刺激了显著的细胞生长。|近义词：stimulant, growth factor | 反义词：inhibitor, suppressor | 名词
