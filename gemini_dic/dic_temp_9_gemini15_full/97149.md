infinitesimally:/ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməli/ | adv. |无限小地；微不足道地 | Extremely small; to an immeasurable extent.  The change was so infinitesimally small that it was practically undetectable.  | 近义词：immeasurably, minutely, slightly | 反义词：vastly, enormously, significantly |


例句：

The needle moved infinitesimally, indicating a slight change in pressure.  = 指针微动，表明压力略有变化。

The difference between the two values is infinitesimally small. = 两个值之间的差异微乎其微。


词源：

From  infinitesimal + -ly.  Infinitesimal derives from Late Latin *infinitesimus ("infinitely small"), from Latin infinitus ("infinite") + -esimus ("-th").


用法说明：

infinitesimally 作为一个副词，主要用于修饰动词、形容词或整个句子，表示程度极小、微不足道。它通常用来描述数量、变化或差异极其微小的情况，以至于几乎无法察觉或测量。


(由于infinitesimally本身是副词，没有其他词性变化，因此省略了其他时态，语态等用法说明)
