monopolizable: /məˈnɒpəlaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可垄断的，可独占的 | Capable of being monopolized; able to be controlled exclusively by one person or group.  | The small town's water supply is monopolizable by a single company. 这个小镇的供水可以被一家公司垄断。 |近义词：controllable,  dominable | 反义词：uncontrollable, competitive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The market is monopolizable by a large corporation. = 市场可以被一家大公司垄断。
This resource is monopolizable by the government. = 这种资源可以被政府垄断。


一般过去时:
The industry was monopolizable before deregulation. = 在放松管制之前，这个行业是可以被垄断的。
That market was easily monopolizable in the past. = 过去那个市场很容易被垄断。


现在完成时:
The technology has become monopolizable by a few tech giants. = 这项技术已经被少数几家科技巨头垄断。
This sector has been monopolizable for decades.  = 这个领域几十年来一直处于垄断状态。


过去完成时:
The market had already been monopolizable by the time we entered. = 当我们进入的时候，市场已经被垄断了。
It had become clear that the resource was monopolizable. = 很明显，这种资源是可以被垄断的。


将来时:
This new technology will be easily monopolizable. = 这项新技术将很容易被垄断。
The market will likely be monopolizable by a single player. = 市场很可能会被一个参与者垄断。


虚拟语气:
If the rules were different, the market wouldn't be monopolizable. = 如果规则不同，市场就不会被垄断。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The technology is not monopolizable due to open-source alternatives. = 由于存在开源替代方案，这项技术无法被垄断。
