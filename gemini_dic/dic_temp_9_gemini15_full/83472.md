haciendas:/hɑːˈsiɛndɑːz/| n.|大庄园；种植园|Large estates or ranches, especially in Spanish-speaking countries, often including a large house and extensive land used for farming or ranching.  They typically encompass significant agricultural production and often include residential areas for workers.|The haciendas of Mexico were once centers of agricultural production, employing thousands of workers.|近义词：plantation, ranch, estate|反义词：small farm, cottage
