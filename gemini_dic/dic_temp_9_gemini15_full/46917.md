darkrooms:/ˈdɑːrkruːmz/ | n. |暗房 | A darkroom is a room that is completely or nearly completely dark, used for developing and printing photographic film and paper.  It needs to be dark to prevent the photosensitive materials from being exposed to light before they are processed. | Example:  He carefully loaded the film into the darkroom's developing tank. 他小心地将胶卷装入暗房的显影槽中。

The photographer spent hours in the darkroom, meticulously printing his photographs. 摄影师在暗房里花了数小时，一丝不苟地冲洗他的照片。|近义词：photography lab, processing room |反义词：bright room, light room |名词
