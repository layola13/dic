holler:/ˈhɑːlər/ /ˈhɒlər/| vt. vi. n.| 大喊；叫喊；大声喊叫 | to shout or yell loudly; a loud shout or yell | 例句：He hollered for help. 他大声呼救。

He let out a loud holler when he saw the car. 他看到汽车时大声喊叫。


近义词：shout, yell, scream, bellow | 反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hollers at the top of his lungs. = 他扯着嗓子喊叫。
She hollers instructions to her team. = 她向她的团队大声喊叫指示。


一般过去时:
The children hollered with laughter. = 孩子们大声笑着。
He hollered his name until he was hoarse. = 他大声喊叫自己的名字直到嗓子嘶哑。


现在完成时:
They have hollered at me all day. = 他们整天对我大声喊叫。
She has hollered so much that she has a sore throat. = 她喊叫太多，嗓子都痛了。


过去完成时:
He had hollered a warning before the accident happened. = 事故发生前他已经大声警告过。
They had hollered at each other for hours before they finally resolved the argument. = 他们在最终解决争论之前已经互相大声喊叫了好几个小时。


将来时:
I will holler if I see anything suspicious. = 如果我看到任何可疑的东西，我会大声喊叫。
They will holler their approval at the end of the performance. = 他们在演出结束后会大声表示赞同。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a bear, I would holler for help. = 如果我看到熊，我会大声呼救。
I wish I hadn't hollered so loudly yesterday. = 我真希望昨天不要喊那么大声。


被动语态:
His name was hollered from across the street. = 他的名字从街对面大声喊叫出来。
Warnings were hollered to the workers before the machinery started. = 机器启动前，对工人们大声喊叫警告。


疑问句:
Did you holler for help when you fell? = 你摔倒时有没有大声呼救？
Will they holler when they win the game? = 他们赢得比赛时会大声欢呼吗？


否定句:
He didn't holler, he just sighed. = 他没有喊叫，只是叹了口气。
She didn't holler even though she was scared. = 即使她很害怕，她也没有喊叫。


PHRASE->
holler back:  大声回应
holler at someone: 对某人大喊
let out a holler: 发出一声喊叫
holler help: 大声呼救
holler something out: 大声喊出某事

词源:
The word "holler" ultimately derives from the Middle English word "holen," meaning "to shout," which itself has roots in Old Norse and possibly even older Germanic languages.  The evolution reflects a consistent meaning of loud vocalization.
