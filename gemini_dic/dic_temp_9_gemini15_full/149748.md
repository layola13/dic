palatability:/ˌpæləˈtæbɪliːti/| n. |适口性，美味可口，可接受性 |The palatability of the food is crucial for its success.  食物的适口性对其成功至关重要。|近义词：acceptability, deliciousness, savoriness |反义词：unpalatability, distastefulness


英文解析:The quality of being palatable; pleasant or acceptable to the taste.  It can also refer to the acceptability or agreeableness of something, not just food.


例句:

The chef focused on improving the palatability of the new dish.  厨师专注于改进新菜肴的适口性。

The palatability of the proposed solution was questioned by some members of the committee.  委员会的一些成员对所提出方案的可接受性提出了质疑。


词源:From palatable + -ity.  Palatable itself comes from the Late Latin *palatābilis, from palātum "palate".
