insidious:/ɪnˈsɪdiəs/| adj.|阴险的；潜伏的；隐伏的 | Gradually and subtly harmful; treacherous; stealthy.  It refers to something harmful that is not immediately obvious or apparent, often developing slowly and insidiously. |例句：The insidious spread of the disease went unnoticed for months.  这种疾病的潜伏传播几个月都没有被注意到。

例句：The insidious influence of propaganda can subtly manipulate people's beliefs. 煽动宣传的阴险影响可以巧妙地操纵人们的信仰。

近义词：treacherous, subtle, cunning, stealthy, malignant, pernicious

反义词：obvious, harmless, benign, overt
