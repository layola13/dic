morning-glory: /ˈmɔːrnɪŋˌɡlɔːri/ | n. |牵牛花；喇叭花 | A climbing plant with trumpet-shaped flowers, often brightly colored.  Many varieties exist, with flowers blooming in the morning and closing later in the day. | The morning glories climbed the fence, their vibrant purple flowers a beautiful sight. | 近义词: bindweed, convolvulus | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其相反的植物特性，例如不攀爬的植物)
