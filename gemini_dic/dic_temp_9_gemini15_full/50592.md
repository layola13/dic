deranged:/dɪˈreɪndʒd/| adj.|精神错乱的；疯狂的；混乱的；错乱的 | Mentally disordered; insane; characterized by a chaotic or disordered state.  |例句：He exhibited deranged behaviour after the accident. 事故后，他表现出精神错乱的行为。|例句：The deranged killer was finally apprehended. 那个精神错乱的杀手最终被逮捕了。|近义词：insane, mad, crazy, lunatic, unhinged, disturbed |反义词：sane, rational, composed, calm, stable
