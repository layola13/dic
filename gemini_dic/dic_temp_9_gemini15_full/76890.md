It often includes slang, jargon, and expressions associated with criminal activity and a tough, defiant attitude.|例句：The movie was full of gangsterisms, from the clipped slang to the violent threats.  这部电影充满了黑帮用语，从简洁的俚语到暴力威胁。

例句：His speech was peppered with gangsterisms, making it difficult for outsiders to understand. 他的讲话中夹杂着黑帮俚语，使外人难以理解。
