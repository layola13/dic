overcooks:/ˌoʊvərˈkʊks/ | vt. |煮过头；烧过头；过度烹调 | To cook (something) for too long, resulting in it being dry, hard, or otherwise unpalatable.  | Example: I overcooked the chicken, and now it's dry and tough. 我把鸡肉煮过头了，现在又干又硬。  |近义词：burn, scorch, char | 反义词：undercook |  动词
