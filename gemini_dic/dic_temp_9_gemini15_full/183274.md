reprinted:/ˌriːˈprɪntɪd/| adj.| 重印的；再版的 | Having been printed again.  A reprinted edition of a book is a new printing of the same book. |例句：This is a reprinted edition of the classic novel. 这是一本该经典小说的重印版。


例句：The article was reprinted in several newspapers. 这篇文章在几家报纸上重印了。

近义词：reissued, republished

反义词：original (in the sense of the first printing)

词性：形容词
