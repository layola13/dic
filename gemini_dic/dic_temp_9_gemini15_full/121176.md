misdirected:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪrektɪd/| adj.| 方向错误的；目标错误的；被误导的 |Sent wrongly to a person or place; wrongly guided or aimed.  |例句：The letter was misdirected and arrived three weeks late. 这封信寄错了地址，迟到了三周。

The campaign's efforts were misdirected, failing to reach the target audience.  这场运动的努力方向错误，未能接触到目标受众。|近义词：wrongly addressed, misguided, misapplied |反义词：correctly addressed, well-directed, properly guided

USAGE->
一般现在时:

His efforts are misdirected. = 他的努力方向错误。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
