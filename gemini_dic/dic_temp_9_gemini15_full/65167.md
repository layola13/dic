ethnographers:/ˌɛθˈnɒɡrəfərz/ | n. | 人类学家 |  People who study the customs, beliefs, and social organization of different cultures. Ethnographers conduct fieldwork, often living among the people they study, to gather data through observation, interviews, and participation. |  The ethnographers spent a year living with the tribe to study their rituals.  人类学家花了一年时间与部落一起生活，研究他们的仪式。| anthropologists, sociologists |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps "those who ignore cultural differences") | 名词
