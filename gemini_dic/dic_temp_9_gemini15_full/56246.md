dog-legged:/ˈdɒɡˌlɛɡd/| adj.|狗腿状的，弯弯曲曲的|Having bends or angles like a dog's leg;  irregular and crooked in shape.  It describes a route or path that is not straight, but has sharp turns or bends.|例句：The road was dog-legged and difficult to navigate. 这条路弯弯曲曲，难以通行。

The river follows a dog-legged course through the valley. 河流蜿蜒穿过山谷。|近义词：crooked, winding, meandering, tortuous|反义词：straight, direct
