nonappealable:/ˌnɒnəˈpiːləbl/ | adj. | 不可上诉的，不能提出上诉的 |  Not subject to appeal; incapable of being appealed against.  This typically refers to a court ruling or decision that cannot be challenged in a higher court. | Example: The judge's decision was nonappealable, leaving the defendant with no recourse.  法官的判决是不可上诉的，被告无法采取任何补救措施。|近义词：final, unappealable, irreversible | 反义词：appealable
