noncomplaisantly:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpleɪzəntli/ | adv. | 不顺从地；不讨好地；不客气地 |  Acting in a way that shows a lack of willingness to please or obey;  disagreeably.  |例句：He noncomplaisantly ignored her request. 他不客气地无视了她的请求。

例句：She responded to his questions noncomplaisantly, offering only short, curt answers. 她不情愿地回答他的问题，只给出简短、粗鲁的答案。


近义词：disagreeably, ungraciously, sullenly, reluctantly, disobligingly


反义词：complaisantly, obligingly, graciously, readily


词源：来自complaisant的否定前缀non- + 副词后缀-ly。 complaisant源于法语 complaisant，意为“讨好的，顺从的”，最终源于拉丁语complacēre，意为“取悦”。
