raced:/reɪst/| v.|赛跑；急速行进；迅速移动 |To move or proceed at a rapid pace; to compete in a race. |例句：The children raced each other to the swings.孩子们争先恐后地跑到秋千那里。

例句：The car raced down the highway.汽车飞驰在高速公路上。

近义词：sped, hurried, dashed, rushed, flew

反义词：crawled, crept, inched

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He races his friends every Saturday. = 他每个星期六都和朋友们赛跑。
The river races towards the sea. = 这条河奔腾入海。

一般过去时:
She raced to the hospital after receiving the call. = 她接到电话后飞奔到医院。
The train raced through the countryside. = 火车飞驰穿过乡村。

现在完成时:
They have raced in several marathons this year. = 他们今年已经参加了几场马拉松比赛。
The car has raced through several towns. = 这辆车已经飞驰穿过好几个小镇。

过去完成时:
He had raced to the finish line before anyone else. = 他在其他人之前冲过了终点线。
The storm had raced across the ocean. = 暴风雨已经横扫了海洋。

将来时:
We will race each other in the upcoming competition. = 我们将在即将到来的比赛中互相竞争。
The rocket will race into space. = 火箭将飞向太空。

虚拟语气:
If I were younger, I would race in the Olympics. = 如果我年轻一些，我会参加奥运会比赛。
I wish I had raced faster in the last competition. = 我希望我在上次比赛中跑得更快。

被动语态:
The race was raced by athletes from all over the world. = 比赛由来自世界各地的运动员参加。
The car was raced recklessly down the mountain. = 汽车鲁莽地飞驰下山。

疑问句:
Did you race in the marathon? = 你参加马拉松比赛了吗？
Will they race again next week? = 他们下周还会比赛吗？

否定句:
He didn't race in the last competition. = 他没有参加上次的比赛。
The car didn't race down the street. = 车没有在街上飞驰。
