/ɛnd/ | n. | 结束；终止；目的；末端 | The end of the movie was very sad.  电影的结尾非常悲伤。|近义词：conclusion, termination, finish | 反义词：beginning, start, commencement |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting ends at 5 pm. = 会议下午5点结束。
The show ends soon. = 节目很快就要结束了。


一般过去时:
The game ended in a draw. = 比赛以平局告终。
The party ended late last night. = 晚会昨晚很晚才结束。


现在完成时:
The project has finally ended. = 项目终于结束了。
The rain has ended, and the sun is shining. = 雨停了，阳光明媚。


过去完成时:
The play had ended before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，戏剧已经结束了。
The storm had ended by morning. = 到早晨暴风雨已经停了。


将来时:
The concert will end at 10 pm. = 音乐会晚上10点结束。
The meeting will end shortly. = 会议很快就会结束。


虚拟语气:
If the war ended tomorrow, we would celebrate. = 如果战争明天结束，我们将庆祝。
I wish the pain would end. = 我希望痛苦能结束。


被动语态:
The meeting was ended early. = 会议提前结束了。
The game was ended due to rain. = 比赛因雨提前结束。


疑问句:
When does the movie end? = 电影几点结束？
Where does this road end? = 这条路在哪里结束？


否定句:
The meeting didn't end on time. = 会议没有按时结束。
The story doesn't end happily. = 故事的结局并不快乐。
