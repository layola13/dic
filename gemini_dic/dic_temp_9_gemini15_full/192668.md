scutter:/ˈskʌtər/| v.|疾跑，飞奔，慌忙逃窜|To run quickly and hurriedly, often in a panicked or disorderly manner.|The children scuttled away when they saw the dog.|近义词：dash, scamper, scurry, flee|反义词：walk leisurely, stroll


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scuttles across the street. = 他飞奔过马路。
The mice scuttle when the cat arrives. = 猫来了，老鼠们四处逃窜。

一般过去时:
The thief scuttled away when he saw the police. = 小偷看到警察就慌忙逃跑了。
She scuttled into the house to avoid the rain. = 她急忙跑进屋里躲雨。

现在完成时:
The children have scuttled off to play. = 孩子们已经跑出去玩了。
The rats have scuttled into their holes. = 老鼠们已经躲进洞里了。

过去完成时:
They had scuttled to safety before the explosion. = 爆炸发生前，他们已经逃到了安全的地方。
The birds had scuttled into their nests before the storm hit. = 风暴来临前，鸟儿们已经躲进了巢穴。

将来时:
He will scutter away if he sees me. = 如果他看到我，他会跑掉的。
The mice will scutter when they hear the noise. = 老鼠听到声音就会逃跑。

虚拟语气:
If I saw a spider, I would scutter away. = 如果我看到蜘蛛，我会跑掉的。
I wish I hadn't scuttled away so quickly. = 我真希望我当时没有跑那么快。

被动语态: (Scutter is rarely used in the passive voice.)
The papers were scattered, not scuttled. = 文件散落了，而不是被慌忙地藏起来。


疑问句:
Did the cat scutter away? = 猫跑掉了么？
Will they scutter when they see the dog? = 他们看到狗会跑吗？

否定句:
He didn't scutter; he walked calmly. = 他没有跑；他平静地走着。
The children didn't scutter; they walked slowly. = 孩子们没有跑；他们慢慢地走着。


PHRASE->

scutter off = 匆匆离去

scutter down = 飞奔而下
