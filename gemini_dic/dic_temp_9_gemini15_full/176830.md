readvertising: /ˌriːˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 再广告，重新投放广告 | The act of advertising something again, often after a period of time or with a revised approach.  It often involves repeating a previous advertising campaign or adapting it to a new audience or context. | Example: The company decided on readvertising their product after a slump in sales. 公司决定在销售额下滑后重新投放其产品的广告。 |近义词:re-advertising, renewed advertising, repeated advertising |反义词: discontinuing advertising, ceasing advertising |名词
