picnicking:/ˈpɪkˌnɪkɪŋ/| v. |野餐；进行野餐活动 |The act of having a picnic; a leisurely outdoor meal.|例句：We're picnicking in the park this afternoon. 我们今天下午在公园野餐。|例句：The family enjoyed picnicking by the lake.  这个家庭享受在湖边野餐的乐趣。|近义词：outing, excursion |反义词：N/A |动词


USAGE->

We picnic in the park every Sunday. = 我们每周日都在公园野餐。
