padishah: /pəˈdɪʃɑː/ | n. |帕迪沙，皇帝，君主 |  A title of the emperors of the Ottoman Empire, equivalent to "emperor" or "king".  It's derived from Persian and ultimately from Old Persian.  The word suggests power, authority, and supreme rule. | Example: The padishah ruled over a vast empire.  帕迪沙统治着一个庞大的帝国。 | 近义词：emperor, sovereign, monarch | 反义词：subject, vassal
