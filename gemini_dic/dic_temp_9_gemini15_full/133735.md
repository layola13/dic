nonfunctionally:/ˌnɒnˈfʌŋkʃənəli/ | adv. |非功能性地，非功能方面地 |  Describes aspects of a system or design that are not directly related to its core functionality but are still important for its overall success.  This often includes aspects like usability, security, performance, and maintainability. | Example: The system performed well functionally, but nonfunctionally it was slow and difficult to use.  系统在功能上运行良好，但在非功能方面却速度缓慢且难以使用。 | 近义词: nonfunctionally,  operationally (in a contrasting sense) | 反义词: functionally
