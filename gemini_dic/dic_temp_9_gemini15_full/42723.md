counterweigh:/ˈkaʊntə(r)weɪ/| n. v.|对抗物；制衡；抵消；平衡|To counteract or balance the effect of something.|The company's strong marketing campaign counterweighed the negative publicity.|近义词：balance, offset, counteract|反义词：unbalance, exacerbate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong wind counterweighs the boat's progress. = 强风阻碍了船的前进。
This advantage is counterweighed by several disadvantages. = 这个优势被几个缺点抵消了。


一般过去时:
The good news counterweighed the bad news we received earlier. = 好消息抵消了我们之前收到的坏消息。
His charm counterweighed his lack of experience. = 他的魅力弥补了他缺乏经验的不足。


现在完成时:
The positive effects of the program have counterweighed the initial costs. = 该项目的积极影响已经抵消了最初的成本。
Her hard work has counterweighed her lack of talent. = 她的努力工作弥补了她缺乏天赋的不足。


过去完成时:
The team's strong performance had counterweighed their earlier setbacks. = 球队的出色表现已经抵消了他们之前的挫折。
His previous experience had counterweighed his lack of formal qualifications. = 他之前的经验弥补了他缺乏正式资格的不足。


将来时:
The new policy will counterweigh the negative impact of the previous one. = 新政策将抵消先前政策的负面影响。
Their efforts will counterweigh the challenges they face. = 他们的努力将抵消他们面临的挑战。


虚拟语气:
If we had a stronger marketing campaign, it would counterweigh the negative publicity. = 如果我们有一个更强大的营销活动，它就能抵消负面宣传。
If only his talent counterweighed his laziness.  = 要是他有天赋能抵消他的懒惰就好了。


被动语态:
The negative effects were counterweighed by the positive outcomes. = 负面影响被积极成果所抵消。
The initial investment will be counterweighed by future profits. = 初始投资将被未来的利润所抵消。


疑问句:
Does the benefit counterweigh the risk? = 收益是否抵消了风险？
Will the positive aspects counterweigh the negative ones? = 积极方面会抵消消极方面吗？


否定句:
The positive aspects did not counterweigh the negative ones. = 积极方面没有抵消消极方面。
His efforts did not counterweigh the difficulties he faced. = 他的努力并没有抵消他面临的困难。


PHRASE->
counterweighing factors = 对抗因素
counterweighing influence = 制衡影响
counterweighing effect = 抵消作用
counterweighing argument = 反驳论点
to counterweigh against = 与...对抗
