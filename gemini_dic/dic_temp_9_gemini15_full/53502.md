disassembly: /ˌdɪsˈæsembli/ | n. | 拆卸；分解；反汇编 | The process of taking something apart, especially a machine or piece of equipment, into its separate components.  It can also refer to the conversion of machine code into assembly language. | 例句：The disassembly of the engine revealed a serious fault.  引擎的拆卸揭示了一个严重的故障。

The disassembly of the software showed a clever algorithm. 软件的反汇编显示了一个巧妙的算法。|近义词：dismantling, decomposition, deconstruction |反义词：assembly, construction, compilation | 词性：名词
