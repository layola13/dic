dogmatism:/ˈdɒɡmətɪzəm/|/ˈdɑːɡmətɪzəm/| n. |教条主义；独断论|The rigid adherence to doctrines, often without regard to evidence or reason.  It's characterized by an unwillingness to consider alternative viewpoints.|His dogmatism prevented him from seeing the flaws in his own argument. 他固执己见，看不到自己论证中的缺陷。|例句：The professor's teaching was criticized for its dogmatism. 教授的教学因其教条主义而受到批评。

|近义词：dogmaticism, absolutism, rigidity, inflexibility|反义词：open-mindedness, tolerance, flexibility, skepticism|名词
