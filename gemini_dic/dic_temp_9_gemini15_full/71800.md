fledgling:/ˈfledʒlɪŋ/| n., adj.|雏鸟；新手；刚起步的；|A fledgling is a young bird that has recently left the nest and is learning to fly.  It can also refer to something new and inexperienced. |例句：The fledgling company is struggling to gain a foothold in the market. 这家初创公司正在努力在市场上站稳脚跟。


The fledgling bird took its first tentative flight.  这只雏鸟进行了第一次试飞。|近义词：novice, beginner, newcomer, tyro |反义词：experienced, veteran, established
