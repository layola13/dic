overhurry:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌri/| v.|过度匆忙，操之过急|To hurry excessively; to act too quickly or rashly.  It implies a lack of careful consideration and often leads to mistakes or inefficiencies.|例句：Don't overhurry the process; take your time to ensure accuracy. 不要过度匆忙这个过程；花时间确保准确性。

例句：He overhurried his painting, resulting in a sloppy finish. 他画画太匆忙，导致结尾粗糙。


近义词：rush, hasten, precipitate, accelerate


反义词：dawdle, procrastinate, delay, deliberate


词性:动词
