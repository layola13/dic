halters:/ˈhɔːltərz/| n.|缰绳；系绳；束缚；(女式)无袖紧身衣|A halter is a rope or strap used to control or restrain an animal, especially a horse. It can also refer to a type of sleeveless garment that ties around the neck.|例句：The horse was led by a halter. 这匹马用缰绳牵着。The dancer wore a stylish halter top. 舞者穿着一件时尚的无袖紧身衣。|近义词：rein, bridle, tether, halter top|反义词：freedom, liberty
