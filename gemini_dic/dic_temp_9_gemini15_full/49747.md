demoiselle:/dɛmˈzɛl/| n.|少女；贵妇；（昆虫）豆娘|A young woman, especially a young lady of noble birth.  The word also refers to a type of damselfly (a delicate insect).|例句：The demoiselle in the painting was exquisitely dressed.  绘画中的少女穿着精美。The demoiselle flew gracefully near the pond. 豆娘优雅地飞翔在池塘附近。|近义词：maiden, young woman, lady|反义词：crone, old woman
