presumingly:/prɪˈzjuːmɪŋli/ | adv. |据推测；大概；想必 |  It is generally accepted that something is true, although it has not been definitely proved.  |例句：Presumably, he'll be at the meeting.  = 大概他会参加会议。

例句：Presumably, the delay is due to bad weather. = 想必延误是由于恶劣天气造成的。


近义词：presumably, supposedly, apparently, likely, probably | 反义词：certainly, definitely, undoubtedly


词源:  Derived from the verb "presume," which comes from the Old French "presumer" and ultimately from the Latin "praesumere," meaning "to take beforehand" or "to assume."  The suffix "-ingly" adds an adverbial sense.

过去完成时:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
