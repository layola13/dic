ricker:/ˈrɪkər/| n.|里克尺，里克波|A ricker wavelet is a type of wavelet used in seismic exploration to represent the seismic source. It is characterized by a sharp initial peak followed by a damped oscillation.  The term often refers to both the waveform and the corresponding filter. |例句：The geophysicist analyzed the seismic data using a Ricker wavelet transform. 地球物理学家使用里克小波变换分析地震数据。|近义词：wavelet (in the context of seismic exploration)|反义词：N/A|名词
