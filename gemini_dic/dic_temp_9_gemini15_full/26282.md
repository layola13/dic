buttressed:/ˈbʌtrəst/| v. |支撑，加固；支持|To support or strengthen (something, especially a building or argument)|The old wall was buttressed by wooden beams.  这堵旧墙由木梁支撑。  The argument was buttressed by new evidence.  这个论点得到了新的证据的支持。|strengthen, reinforce, support|weaken, undermine, destabilize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pillars buttress the cathedral. = 柱子支撑着大教堂。
He buttresses his arguments with facts. = 他用事实来支持他的论点。


一般过去时:
The government buttressed the economy with stimulus packages. = 政府用刺激经济的方案来支持经济。
They buttressed the failing dam with additional concrete. = 他们用额外的混凝土加固了快要倒塌的大坝。


现在完成时:
The company has buttressed its position in the market. = 公司已经巩固了它在市场上的地位。
Recent discoveries have buttressed the theory. = 最近的发现巩固了这个理论。


过去完成时:
They had buttressed the bridge before the heavy rains arrived. = 大雨来临之前，他们已经加固了桥梁。
The lawyer had buttressed his case with numerous witnesses. = 律师已经用许多证人来支持他的案情。


将来时:
The engineers will buttress the building's foundation. = 工程师们将加固建筑物的基础。
We will buttress our claims with irrefutable evidence. = 我们将用无可辩驳的证据来支持我们的说法。


虚拟语气:
If we had buttressed the structure properly, it wouldn't have collapsed. = 如果我们当时正确地加固了结构，它就不会倒塌了。
If they had buttressed their argument, they might have won the debate. = 如果他们当时支持了他们的论点，他们也许就能赢得辩论了。


被动语态:
The crumbling walls were buttressed with new stone. = 倒塌的墙壁用新的石头加固了。
The theory is buttressed by recent research. = 该理论得到了最新研究的支持。


疑问句:
How will the bridge be buttressed against the floods? = 桥梁将如何抵御洪水？
What evidence will buttress his claims? = 什么证据能支持他的说法？


否定句:
The weak foundation was not buttressed adequately. = 薄弱的基础没有得到充分的加固。
His arguments were not buttressed by any solid evidence. = 他的论点没有任何确凿证据支持。


PHRASE->
buttress against = 抵御
buttress up = 加固
buttress with = 用...支撑
buttress a claim = 支持一个说法
