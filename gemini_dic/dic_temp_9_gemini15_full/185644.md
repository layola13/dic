revered:/rɪˈvɪərd/| adj.|受尊敬的，敬爱的|held in high regard; deeply respected.|例句：Nelson Mandela is a revered figure throughout the world.  纳尔逊·曼德拉是全世界受人尊敬的人物。

例句：The ancient temple was revered by the local people. 当地人民敬重这座古老的寺庙。

近义词：respected, honored, esteemed, venerated|反义词：despised, scorned, reviled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is revered for his wisdom. = 他因其智慧而受人尊敬。
The teacher is revered by her students. = 老师受到学生的尊敬。

一般过去时:
The king was revered by his subjects. = 国王受到臣民的尊敬。
She was revered for her kindness and compassion. = 她因其善良和同情心而受人敬重。

现在完成时:
He has been revered as a saint for centuries. = 他几个世纪以来一直被尊为圣人。
This ancient tree has been revered by generations. = 这棵古树世世代代受到人们的敬重。

过去完成时:
The leader had been revered before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，这位领导人一直受到尊敬。
The artist had been revered for his innovative style.  这位艺术家因其创新风格而备受推崇。


将来时:
He will be revered for his contributions to science. = 他将因其对科学的贡献而受到尊敬。
Future generations will revere her artistic genius. = 后代将敬仰她的艺术天才。


虚拟语气:
If he were still alive, he would be revered even more. = 如果他还活着，他将更加受人尊敬。
I wish I could have met the revered author. = 我真希望我能见到那位受人尊敬的作家。


被动语态:
He was revered throughout the land. = 他在全国各地都受到尊敬。
The painting is revered as a masterpiece. = 这幅画被视为杰作。


疑问句:
Is he revered in your culture? = 在你的文化中，他受人尊敬吗？
Was the ancient artifact revered by the civilization? = 这个古代文物受到那个文明的尊敬吗？


否定句:
He is not revered by everyone. = 他并非人人敬重。
The politician was not revered by his constituents.  这位政治家没有得到其选民的尊敬。
