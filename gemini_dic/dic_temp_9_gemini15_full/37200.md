come ons:/ˈkʌmɑːnz/ | int. n. | 算了吧；得了；快来；劝诱；煽动 |  An expression of impatience, disbelief, or encouragement; also, a tempting offer or enticement. It can be used to dismiss something, urge someone to act, or express disapproval. | 例句：Come ons, let's go! = 得了吧，咱们走吧！
Come ons, don't be silly. = 得了吧，别傻了。

|近义词：encouragement, persuasion, inducement, urging, plea |反义词：discouragement, dissuasion, deterrent |词性：感叹词，名词
