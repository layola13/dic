managements:/ˈmænɪdʒmənts/ | n. | 管理；经营；管理层 | The act or process of managing; the control and direction exercised over something.  It also refers to the collective group of managers. | 例句：The managements of the two companies are discussing a merger. = 两家公司的管理层正在讨论合并事宜。  The managements of the project are excellent. = 项目的管理很出色。 | 近义词：administration, direction, supervision, leadership | 反义词：chaos, disorder
