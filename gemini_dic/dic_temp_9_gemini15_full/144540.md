outvoiced:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪst/| vt.|大声说出；压过…的声音|To speak louder than; to surpass in vocal power.|例句：He outvoiced the protestors with his powerful microphone. 他用他强大的麦克风压过了抗议者的声音。

例句：The singer outvoiced the orchestra during the powerful crescendo. 在强烈的渐强中，歌手的声音盖过了管弦乐队。


近义词：overshadow, drown out, surpass|反义词：be outvoiced by, be silenced by


词性：动词
