nonpriestly:/ˈnɒnˈpriːstli/ | adj. |非祭司的，非神职人员的 |Not pertaining to priests or the priesthood; lay; secular.  The term describes something or someone not associated with the clergy or religious duties. |Example:  The nonpriestly members of the community were responsible for the harvest.  社区的非神职人员负责收割。

近义词: lay, secular, nonclerical
反义词: priestly, clerical, ecclesiastical
