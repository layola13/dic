cashless:/ˈkæʃləs/| adj.| 无现金的，不使用现金的 | Without the use of cash; using electronic methods of payment instead.  | Example: Many countries are moving towards a cashless society. 许多国家正在向无现金社会迈进。  The transaction was cashless, completed entirely online. 这笔交易是无现金的，完全在线完成。|近义词：cash-free, digital, electronic |反义词：cash-based, cash-only
