self-seeking:/ˈsɛlfˌsiːkɪŋ/| adj. |自私的，唯利是图的 |Self-seeking behavior is motivated by personal gain, often at the expense of others.  It implies a disregard for the needs and feelings of others. |例句：His self-seeking ambition led him to betray his friends. 他的自私野心使他背叛了朋友。

例句：The self-seeking politician only cared about his own re-election.  这位唯利是图的政客只关心自己的连任。


近义词：selfish, egoistic, self-centered, opportunistic, mercenary


反义词：altruistic, selfless, unselfish, benevolent


词源:  The word is a compound of "self" and "seeking."  "Self" refers to oneself, and "seeking" implies the act of pursuing or striving for something.  The combination suggests an individual primarily focused on their own interests.

用法说明:

self-seeking 通常作形容词，用来修饰名词，例如：self-seeking behavior (自私的行为), self-seeking politician (唯利是图的政客), self-seeking ambition (自私的野心).  它也可以用作名词，但相对不常见。


PHRASE->
self-seeking behaviour: 自私的行为
self-seeking ambition: 自私的野心
self-seeking motives: 自私的动机
self-seeking policies: 唯利是图的政策
a self-seeking individual: 一个自私自利的人
