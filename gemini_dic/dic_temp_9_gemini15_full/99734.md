intermarriage:/ˌɪntərˈmærɪdʒ/| n.|通婚；异族通婚|The marriage of people from different social groups, races, or religions.|例句：Intermarriage between different religious groups is becoming more common. 不同宗教团体之间的通婚越来越普遍了。

例句：The study examined the social implications of intermarriage in a multi-ethnic community. 这项研究考察了多种族社区中通婚的社会影响。


近义词：mixed marriage, cross-cultural marriage|反义词：endogamy (近亲结婚)|词性:名词
