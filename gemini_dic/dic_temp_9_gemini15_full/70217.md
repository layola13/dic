fibster:/ˈfɪbstər/| n. |说谎者，骗子 |A fibster is someone who tells lies, especially small, insignificant ones.  They are not typically seen as malicious liars, but rather as someone who embellishes the truth or avoids telling the whole truth. |例句：He's a bit of a fibster, always exaggerating his accomplishments. 他有点爱说谎，总是夸大他的成就。|例句：Don't believe everything she says; she's a notorious fibster.  别相信她说的所有话；她是个出了名的说谎者。|近义词：liar, prevaricator, storyteller (in a negative context)|反义词：truth-teller, honest person|名词
