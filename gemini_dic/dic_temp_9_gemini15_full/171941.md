puppeting: /ˈpʌpɪtɪŋ/ | n. |木偶表演；操纵；控制 | The act of manipulating puppets; controlling or influencing someone or something as if they were a puppet.  |例句：The show featured amazing puppeting skills. 这场演出展现了令人惊叹的木偶技艺。

例句：The politician was accused of puppeting the media.  这位政客被指控操纵媒体。


近义词： manipulation, control, orchestration


反义词： freedom, autonomy, independence


词性：名词
