libertarian:/ˌlɪbərˈteəriən/| adj., n. |自由主义者；自由意志主义者|An advocate of libertarianism, a political philosophy that upholds liberty as a core principle and emphasizes individual rights, limited government, and free markets.  Libertarians believe in maximizing individual autonomy and minimizing state intervention.|He is a staunch libertarian who believes in minimal government regulation.|近义词：liberal, individualist|反义词：authoritarian, totalitarian
