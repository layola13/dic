extinct:/ɪkˈstɪŋkt/| adj.|灭绝的；绝种的；熄灭的|No longer existing, especially having died out completely.|The dinosaurs are extinct.|近义词：extinguished, defunct, vanished |反义词：extant, existing, alive


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dodo bird is extinct. = 渡渡鸟已经灭绝了。
This species of flower is extinct in the wild. = 这种花在野外已经灭绝了。


一般过去时:
The woolly mammoth became extinct thousands of years ago. = 长毛猛犸象在几千年前灭绝了。
The passenger pigeon was declared extinct in 1914. = 旅鸽在1914年被宣布灭绝。


现在完成时:
Many plant species have become extinct in recent decades. = 近几十年来许多植物物种已经灭绝。
Several animal populations have gone extinct due to habitat loss. = 由于栖息地丧失，一些动物种群已经灭绝。


过去完成时:
The saber-toothed tiger had already gone extinct before humans arrived in North America. = 在人类到达北美洲之前，剑齿虎就已经灭绝了。
By the time the explorers arrived, the native population had become extinct. = 当探险家到达时，当地居民已经灭绝了。


将来时:
If we don't act now, more species will become extinct. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，更多的物种将会灭绝。
Without conservation efforts, this particular type of orchid will likely be extinct by 2050. = 如果没有保护工作，这种兰花很可能在2050年灭绝。


虚拟语气:
If the dinosaurs hadn't gone extinct, the world would be a very different place. = 如果恐龙没有灭绝，这个世界将会大不相同。
I wish that the giant panda were not endangered and heading towards extinction. = 我希望大熊猫没有濒临灭绝。


被动语态:
Many species have been driven to extinction by human activity. = 许多物种都因人类活动而灭绝。
The volcano was extinguished and is now extinct. = 火山熄灭了，现在已经不活跃了。


疑问句:
Is the Tasmanian tiger extinct? = 袋狼灭绝了吗？
Are all dinosaurs extinct? = 所有恐龙都灭绝了吗？


否定句:
The giant panda is not yet extinct, but it is critically endangered. = 大熊猫还没有灭绝，但它极度濒危。
That rumor that the species is extinct is untrue. = 那种物种已经灭绝的谣言是不真实的。
