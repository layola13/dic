ombudsman:/ˈɒmbʊdzmən/ /ˈɑːmbədzmən/| n.|监察员，申诉专员|An official appointed to investigate complaints and act as a mediator between the public and an organization.|The ombudsman investigated the complaints about the airline's handling of baggage. 监察员调查了有关航空公司处理行李的投诉。|mediator, arbitrator, advocate| oppressor, tyrant

The ombudsman investigates complaints. = 申诉专员调查投诉。
He acts as a mediator between the public and the company. = 他充当公众和公司之间的调解人。
