jostle:/ˈdʒɒsəl/ /ˈdʒɑːsəl/| vt. & vi. |推挤；争先恐后；撞；挤|

英文解析:To push roughly against someone or something, especially in a crowd.  To compete with someone for something.

例句:
The shoppers jostled each other to get the best bargains. = 购物者们为了抢到最划算的商品而互相推挤。

He jostled his way to the front of the queue. = 他挤到了队伍的最前面。


近义词:push, shove, nudge, elbow, crowd, struggle

反义词:yield, give way


词性:动词
