guffaws:/ˈɡʌfɔːz/| n. v.| 狂笑；哄堂大笑 | A guffaw is a loud, boisterous burst of laughter.  It often implies a hearty and somewhat unrestrained amusement.  |例句：The comedian's joke elicited a series of guffaws from the audience. 喜剧演员的笑话引来观众一阵阵哄堂大笑。

The audience erupted in guffaws. 观众爆发出阵阵狂笑。|近义词：laugh, roar, chuckle (less intense), cackle (harsher sound)|反义词：cry, sob, weep|名词，动词
