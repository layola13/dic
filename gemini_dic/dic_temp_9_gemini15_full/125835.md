murrelet:/ˈmʌrəlɛt/| n. |海雀|A small alcidae (a type of seabird) characterized by its small size, short wings, and often, a distinctive appearance. Murrelets are known for their diving abilities and preference for nesting near the water.|例句：The tiny marbled murrelet is an endangered species.  这种小小的斑海雀是一种濒危物种。|例句：We saw several least murrelets diving for fish near the shore. 我们看到几只小海雀在岸边潜水捕鱼。|近义词：auk (a broader term)|反义词:None.
