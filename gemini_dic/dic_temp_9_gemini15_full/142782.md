oued:/uːed/| n.|奥德河（摩洛哥河流）|Oued is an Arabic word referring to a riverbed, often dry except during periods of rain.  It is commonly used in North Africa to describe seasonal watercourses or wadis.|例句：The oued was dry, but evidence of recent flooding was visible. 奥德河干涸了，但可以看到最近洪水的痕迹。|近义词：wadi, riverbed, creek (in some contexts)|反义词：无|名词
