footbrake:/ˈfʊtbreɪk/| n.|脚刹，脚制动器|A footbrake is a brake operated by a pedal that is pressed by the foot. It is commonly used in automobiles and other vehicles to slow down or stop.|例句：He slammed on the footbrake to avoid hitting the pedestrian. 他猛踩脚刹以避免撞到行人。

例句：The car's footbrake failed, causing a serious accident.  这辆车的脚刹失灵，导致一起严重的事故。|近义词：brake, foot pedal|反义词：accelerator
