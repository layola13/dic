pleuras:/ˈplʊərəz/ | n. | 胸膜 | The pleuras are two thin membranes that line the chest cavity and surround the lungs.  They secrete a fluid that allows the lungs to expand and contract smoothly during breathing. | Example:  Inflammation of the pleuras can cause pleurisy, a painful condition.  = 胸膜发炎会导致胸膜炎，这是一种痛苦的疾病。  The doctor examined the patient's pleuras to determine the extent of the lung damage. = 医生检查了病人的胸膜以确定肺部损伤的程度。 |近义词：pleural membranes | 反义词：无
