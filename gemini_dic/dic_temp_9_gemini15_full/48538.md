defaces:/dɪˈfeɪs/| vt.|毁坏…的外观；污损；玷污 |to spoil the surface or appearance of something, especially by writing or drawing on it|The building was defaced with graffiti. 这座建筑物被涂鸦破坏了。  The vandals defaced the monument. 这些破坏分子毁坏了纪念碑。|近义词：mar, damage, spoil, vandalize |反义词：beautify, enhance, restore
