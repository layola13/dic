longer:/ˈlɒŋɡər/ /ˈlɔːŋɡər/| adj.|更长的，更久的|Longer means exceeding the usual or expected length in time or space. It is the comparative form of "long".|例句：The movie was longer than I expected. = 这部电影比我预期的要长。

例句：The journey was much longer this time. = 这次旅程长得多。


近义词：lengthier, extended, prolonged|反义词：shorter, briefer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rope is longer than the stick. = 这根绳子比那根棍子长。
This meeting is longer than usual. = 这个会议比平时长。

一般过去时:
The line was longer yesterday. = 昨天队伍更长。
The concert lasted longer than we anticipated. = 音乐会持续的时间比我们预期的要长。

现在完成时:
The project has taken longer than planned. = 项目花费的时间比计划的要长。
My hair has grown longer recently. = 最近我的头发长长了。

过去完成时:
The meeting had already been longer than expected before the break. = 中断之前，会议已经比预期时间更长了。
We had waited longer than we thought we would. = 我们等待的时间比我们想象的要长。


将来时:
The trip will be longer than we thought. = 这趟旅程将比我们想象的要长。
The presentation will likely be longer than the allotted time. = 演示文稿很可能会超过规定的时间。


虚拟语气:
I wish the vacation was longer. = 我希望假期更长一些。
If the road were longer, we would need more fuel. = 如果路更长，我们就需要更多燃料。


被动语态:
The meeting was made longer by the many questions. = 许多问题使会议延长了。
The deadline was extended; the project is now longer. = 最后期限延长了；项目现在更长了。


疑问句:
Is this route longer? = 这条路线更长吗？
Is the film longer than the book? = 电影比书长吗？


否定句:
The journey wasn't longer than we thought. = 旅途并没有比我们想象的更长。
The meeting wasn't as long as we expected, it was shorter. = 会议并没有我们预期的那么长，而是更短。



PHRASE->
no longer = 不再
longer than = 比……更长
how long = 多久
longer term = 长期
for longer = 更久
