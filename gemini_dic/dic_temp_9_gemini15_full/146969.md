overjet:/ˈoʊvərˌdʒɛt/ | n. | 上突，前突（指牙齿）| The horizontal projection of the upper teeth beyond the lower teeth.  It is a common orthodontic concern. | Example:  He has a significant overjet, requiring orthodontic treatment. 他有明显的牙齿上突，需要正畸治疗。|近义词：protrusion, projection |反义词：underjet
