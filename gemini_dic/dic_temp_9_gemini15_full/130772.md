nonanalytically:/ˌnɒnænəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 非分析地，非解析地 |  Not in a manner that involves analysis or logical reasoning.  It implies a method or approach that is intuitive, empirical, or based on non-mathematical methods. | The problem was solved nonanalytically, relying on intuition and experience.  这个问题是非分析地解决的，依靠直觉和经验。| analytically, systematically |  synthetically, intuitively | 副词
