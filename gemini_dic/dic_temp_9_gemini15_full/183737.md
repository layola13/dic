rescan:/riːˈskæn/ | vt. |重新扫描|To scan again; to scan (something) a second time, often to correct an error or find something missed in the first scan.|例句：The technician had to rescan the document to fix a blurry area. 技术人员不得不重新扫描文件以修复模糊区域。
例句：After the initial scan failed, we decided to rescan the hard drive. 初次扫描失败后，我们决定重新扫描硬盘。|近义词：re-scan, scan again|反义词：N/A
