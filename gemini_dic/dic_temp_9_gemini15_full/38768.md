condottiere:/ˌkɒndɒˈtɪərɛ/| n. |意大利佣兵队长|A condottiere was a captain of a mercenary army, especially in Italy from the 14th to 16th centuries.  They were often powerful figures who commanded significant military influence and sometimes even political power.|Example:  During the Renaissance, powerful condottieri frequently changed sides, depending on who offered the highest pay.  文艺复兴时期，强大的佣兵队长经常根据谁出价最高而改变立场。|近义词：mercenary captain, military leader, captain of mercenaries|反义词：N/A
