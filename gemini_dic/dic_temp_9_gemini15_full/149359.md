pachyote:/ˈpækiˌoʊt/| n. |帕奇奥特（一种仙人掌）|A pachyote is a type of cactus, specifically referring to several species in the genus *Stenocereus*, known for their edible fruits.  These cacti are native to Mexico and Central America.|The pachyote fruit is a valuable source of food and drink in its native regions.|近义词：pitaya (in some contexts), cactus fruit|反义词：无
