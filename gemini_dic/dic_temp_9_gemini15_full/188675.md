rumpled:/ˈrʌmpəld/ | adj. | 皱巴巴的；弄皱的 | Creased or wrinkled; disordered in appearance.  |例句：His shirt was rumpled and stained. 他的衬衫皱巴巴的，还沾上了污渍。
例句：The bed linens were rumpled from a restless night’s sleep.  床单因为一夜不安的睡眠而皱巴巴的。|近义词：creased, wrinkled, crumpled, disheveled | 反义词：smooth, unwrinkled, neat

词源:
The word "rumpled" comes from the Middle English word "rompilen," meaning "to wrinkle or crease."  This in turn likely has Germanic origins, related to words suggesting a crushing or tumbling action.
