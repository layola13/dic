politicization: /ˌpɒlətɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌpɑːlətɪsəˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |政治化 | The act of giving a political character to something; the process of becoming political.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting that something is being inappropriately or unfairly influenced by politics. | The politicization of the environmental movement has made it difficult to find common ground on climate change.  环境运动的政治化使得在气候变化问题上难以找到共同点。  |  politicization,  making political,  politicizing | depoliticization,  apoliticism | 名词
