matchmaking:/ˈmætʃˌmeɪkɪŋ/ | n. |媒合；婚介；撮合 | The act of arranging marriages or other partnerships.  This can refer to romantic relationships, business partnerships, or even friendships.  It often involves identifying compatible individuals and facilitating their interaction. | Example:  The matchmaking service helped her find her perfect partner.  这家婚介所帮她找到了完美的伴侣。


Example:  His matchmaking skills were legendary in his social circle. 他的撮合技艺在他社交圈里是出了名的。


近义词：couple-finding, pairing, partnering


反义词：separation, divorce (in the context of marriage)


词性：名词
