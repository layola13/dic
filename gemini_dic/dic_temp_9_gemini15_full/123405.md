monophthong:/ˈmɒnɒfθɒŋ/|/ˈmɑːnəˌfɒŋ/| n. |单元音|A single vowel sound, in contrast to a diphthong or triphthong.|Example: The vowel sound in "see" is a monophthong, while the vowel sound in "say" is a diphthong.  单元音在“see”中的元音发音是一个单元音，而“say”中的元音发音是一个双元音。|近义词:pure vowel, simple vowel |反义词:diphthong, triphthong


词源:
From Middle French *monophtongue, from Late Latin monophtongus, from Greek monophtongos, from monos "single" + phthongos "sound".


用法说明:

This word is primarily used in linguistics and phonetics to describe a type of vowel sound.  It's less commonly used in everyday conversation.


例句：

1.  English has fewer monophthongs than some other languages.  英语的单元音比其他一些语言少。

2.  The pronunciation of this word involves a pure monophthong.  这个词的发音包含一个纯单元音。


由于monophthong本身是名词，不涉及动词的各种时态变化。  以下列出一些与monophthong相关的句子，展示不同句式：


疑问句：

1.  How many monophthongs are there in the English language? 英语中有多少个单元音？

2.  Is the vowel sound in "boot" a monophthong or a diphthong? "boot"中的元音是单元音还是双元音？


否定句：

1.  The vowel sound in "house" is not a monophthong; it's a diphthong. "house"中的元音不是单元音；它是双元音。

2.  This word does not contain any monophthongs. 这个词不包含任何单元音。



被动语态:

1. Monophthongs are often described as pure vowel sounds. 单元音通常被描述为纯元音。

2. The number of monophthongs in a language can be analysed.  一种语言中单元音的数量可以被分析。


词组：

由于monophthong本身是一个比较专业的术语，其搭配的词组相对较少，以下列举一些相关词汇的搭配：
