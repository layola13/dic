iambs:/ˈaɪæmbz/| n. |抑扬格；抑扬格诗|Iambs are a metrical foot consisting of an unstressed syllable followed by a stressed syllable.  This rhythmic pattern is frequently used in poetry and drama. For example, the line "Shall I compare thee to a summer's day?" follows an iambic pentameter pattern (five iambs per line).|例句：The poem is written in iambic pentameter, using five iambs per line.|近义词：trochee (反义), spondee (反义)|反义词：trochee, spondee|词性 n.
