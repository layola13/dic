semoule:/səˈmuːl/| n.|粗粒小麦粉，粗面粉|A coarse flour made from durum wheat, often used in North African and Middle Eastern cuisine. It is similar to, but coarser than, farina.  |例句：This recipe calls for semoule, which gives the couscous its characteristic texture. 这个食谱需要用粗粒小麦粉，这样才能使蒸粗麦粉具有其特有的质地。|例句：She made a delicious semoule pudding for dessert. 她做了一道美味的粗粒小麦粉布丁作为甜点。|近义词：farina, coarse flour|反义词：fine flour
