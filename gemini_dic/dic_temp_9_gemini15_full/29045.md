carts:/kɑːrts/| n.|手推车；购物车；(尤指)运货马车|A cart is a vehicle with two wheels that is pulled by a person or an animal, and is used for carrying things.|例句：The farmer used a cart to transport his vegetables to the market.  农民用一辆手推车把蔬菜运到市场。|例句：She pushed a shopping cart full of groceries. 她推着一辆装满杂货的购物车。|近义词：wagon, trolley, carriage|反义词：N/A
