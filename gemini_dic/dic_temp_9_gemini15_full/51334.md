detected:/dɪˈtɛktɪd/ | adj. | 被探测到的，被发现的 |  Past participle of detect.  It describes something that has been discovered or identified. |Example: The problem was quickly detected and addressed. 问题很快就被发现并解决了。The thief was detected by the security cameras. 小偷被监控摄像头发现了。|近义词：discovered, found, identified, observed | 反义词：missed, overlooked, unnoticed |  形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sensor detects movement. = 传感器检测到移动。
The system detects errors automatically. = 系统自动检测错误。

一般过去时:
The doctor detected a problem with my heart. = 医生发现我的心脏有问题。
Police detected traces of drugs at the scene. = 警方在现场发现了毒品痕迹。

现在完成时:
They have detected a new strain of the virus. = 他们已经检测到一种新的病毒株。
Scientists have detected a significant increase in pollution levels. = 科学家们已经检测到污染水平的显著增加。

过去完成时:
The security system had detected the intruder before he reached the vault. = 安全系统在他到达金库之前就已经探测到入侵者了。
Investigators had detected inconsistencies in his alibi. = 调查人员已经发现他的不在场证明前后矛盾。

将来时:
The new software will detect malware more efficiently. = 新软件将更有效地检测恶意软件。
The satellite will detect changes in the earth's atmosphere. = 卫星将探测到地球大气层的变化。

虚拟语气:
If the system had detected the error earlier, the damage would have been less severe. = 如果系统早点检测到错误，损失就不会那么严重了。
I wish they had detected the problem sooner. = 我希望他们早点发现这个问题。

被动语态:
The leak was detected by a worker. = 泄漏被一名工人发现。
The fraud was quickly detected and reported. = 欺诈行为很快就被发现并报告了。

疑问句:
Was the signal detected? = 信号被探测到了吗？
Have any problems been detected in the system? = 系统中检测到任何问题了吗？

否定句:
The sensor did not detect any motion. = 传感器没有检测到任何运动。
The test did not detect any abnormalities. = 测试没有检测到任何异常。
