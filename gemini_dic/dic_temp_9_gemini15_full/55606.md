discuss:/dɪˈskʌs/| vt.|讨论，议论|To talk about something with someone, especially in order to make a decision or reach an agreement.|例句：We need to discuss the problem before we make a decision. 我们需要在做决定之前讨论这个问题。|例句：They discussed the merits of different approaches. 他们讨论了不同方法的优缺点。|近义词：talk about, debate, consider|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We discuss this issue every week. = 我们每周都讨论这个问题。
They discuss their plans for the future. = 他们讨论他们未来的计划。

一般过去时:
We discussed the project yesterday. = 我们昨天讨论了这个项目。
They discussed the matter thoroughly. = 他们彻底讨论了这件事。

现在完成时:
We have discussed this many times. = 我们已经多次讨论过这个了。
They have discussed the proposal with the client. = 他们已经和客户讨论了这个提案。

过去完成时:
We had discussed the budget before the meeting. = 我们在会议之前已经讨论过预算。
They had discussed the implications before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经讨论过其影响。

将来时:
We will discuss the next steps tomorrow. = 我们明天将讨论下一步措施。
They will discuss the contract details later. = 他们稍后将讨论合同细节。

虚拟语气:
If we discussed it openly, we might find a solution. = 如果我们公开讨论它，我们可能会找到解决方案。
I wish we had discussed the problem earlier. = 我希望我们早点讨论这个问题。

被动语态:
The issue was discussed at length. = 这个问题被详细讨论了。
The proposal will be discussed at the next meeting. = 这个提案将在下次会议上讨论。

疑问句:
Will we discuss the budget today? = 我们今天讨论预算吗？
Have they discussed the options yet? = 他们已经讨论过这些选择了吗？

否定句:
We did not discuss the matter with him. = 我们没有和他讨论这件事。
They haven't discussed the details yet. = 他们还没有讨论细节。


PHRASE->
discuss a topic = 讨论一个话题
discuss the matter = 讨论这件事
discuss in detail = 详细讨论
discuss thoroughly = 彻底讨论
open to discussion = 接受讨论
