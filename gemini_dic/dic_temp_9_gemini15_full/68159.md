failsafe:/ˈfeɪlˌseɪf/ | adj. | 防错的；安全可靠的 |  A failsafe system or device is designed to prevent failure or to ensure safety in the event of a malfunction.  It's built with redundancy to mitigate the risk of complete system failure. | Example: The aircraft has a failsafe mechanism to prevent engine failure from causing a crash. 这架飞机有一个防错机制，以防止发动机故障导致坠机。 The software includes a failsafe to protect against data loss. 该软件包含一个防错功能，以防止数据丢失。|近义词：safeguard, backup, foolproof |反义词：unreliable, unsafe, vulnerable
