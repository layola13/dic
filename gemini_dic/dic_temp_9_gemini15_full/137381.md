nontemporary: /ˌnɒnˈtɛmpəreri/ | adj. |非暂时的，永久的| Not temporary; lasting or intended to last for a considerable time.  The word is formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "temporary." |例句：This is a nontemporary solution to the problem; we expect it to last for many years.  这是一个永久的解决方案；我们预计它会持续很多年。

例句：The museum acquired a nontemporary exhibit of ancient artifacts.  博物馆获得了一个永久性的古代文物展览。


近义词：permanent, lasting, enduring, abiding, fixed


反义词：temporary, transient, ephemeral, fleeting, short-lived


词性: adjective
