incognita:/ɪnˈkɒɡnɪtə/| n. |未知数；身份不明的女人；未确定的事物 |An unknown woman, thing, or quantity.  It often refers to a woman whose identity is concealed or unknown.|例句：She traveled incognita, hoping to avoid the press. 她隐姓埋名旅行，希望能避开媒体。

例句：The problem remains an incognita for the moment. 目前这个问题仍然是一个未知数。


近义词：unknown, mystery, enigma, secret


反义词：known, identified, revealed
