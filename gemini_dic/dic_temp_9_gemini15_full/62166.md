encodements:/ɪnˈkoʊdmənts/ | n. |编码方式，编码方案 |The process or method of encoding information; a system of encoding.  It refers to the specific techniques and rules used to transform data into a coded format.  Different encodements exist for various purposes, such as character encoding (e.g., UTF-8, ASCII) or data compression (e.g., ZIP, gzip).|Example:  The website uses a variety of character encodements to support different languages.  网站使用多种字符编码来支持不同的语言。|近义词: coding schemes, encoding methods, character sets|反义词:decodings
