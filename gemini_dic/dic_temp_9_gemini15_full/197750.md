shill:/ʃɪl/| n., v.| 掮客；托儿；暗中推销； | A shill is a person who pretends to be a genuine customer in order to promote a product or service.  They may give fake testimonials or otherwise create a false sense of popularity or demand.  As a verb, it means to act as a shill. |例句：The used car salesman had a shill in the crowd to create the impression of high demand.  这个二手车销售员在人群中安排了一个托儿，以营造高需求的印象。

The company hired shills to post positive reviews online. 这家公司雇佣托儿在网上发布正面评价。


近义词：tout, plant, decoy, stooge |反义词：genuine customer, honest reviewer |词性：n., v.
