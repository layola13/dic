coving:/ˈkʌvɪŋ/ | n. | 拱形装饰线，护墙板 | A decorative molding around the top of a wall, often curved or rounded.  It can also refer to the act of installing such molding. | Example: The coving added a touch of elegance to the room.  拱形装饰线使房间增添了一丝优雅。 The builder is coving the walls in the new extension.  建筑工人正在给新扩建部分的墙壁安装护墙板。 | 近义词：molding, cornice, trim | 反义词：None
