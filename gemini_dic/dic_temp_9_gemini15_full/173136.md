quasi-alternating: /ˈkwɑːziˈɔːltəneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 准交替的，近似交替的 |  Describes a sequence or pattern that resembles an alternating sequence but doesn't strictly adhere to it. It might have some irregularities or deviations from a perfectly alternating pattern.  | Example: The quasi-alternating pattern of black and white squares on the chessboard was slightly disrupted in the corner.  | 近义词: near-alternating, approximately alternating | 反义词: strictly alternating, consistently alternating
