chihuahua:/tʃɪˈwɑːwɑː/|/ˈtʃiːwɑːwɑː/| n. |吉娃娃犬|A very small breed of dog originating from the Mexican state of Chihuahua.|例句：My aunt owns a tiny chihuahua with big, expressive eyes.  我的阿姨养了一只眼睛又大又有神的小吉娃娃。|例句：The chihuahua barked ferociously at the much larger dog. 那只吉娃娃对着那只大得多的狗凶猛地吠叫。|近义词：dog, puppy|反义词：N/A
