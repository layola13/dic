intercede:/ˌɪntərˈsiːd/| vt.|调解；说情；代祷|To intercede is to act as a mediator or advocate between two parties, often to help resolve a conflict or plead on behalf of someone else.  It implies intervening in a situation to influence the outcome. |例句：The priest interceded on behalf of the prisoner.  牧师为囚犯求情。

例句：She interceded with the manager to get me a better job. 她向经理求情，帮我争取一份更好的工作。


近义词：mediate, plead, intervene, advocate |反义词：obstruct, hinder, oppose |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intercedes for his friend with the boss. = 他向老板为他的朋友求情。
She often intercedes in family disputes. = 她经常调解家庭纠纷。


一般过去时:
The lawyer interceded on his client's behalf. = 律师为他的当事人求情。
My mother interceded with the teacher for me. = 我妈妈向老师为我求情。


现在完成时:
He has interceded many times for his employees. = 他多次为他的员工求情。
She has interceded successfully in several disputes. = 她成功地调解了几起纠纷。


过去完成时:
They had interceded before the judge issued his ruling. = 在法官做出裁决之前，他们已经求情了。
The mediator had already interceded before the conflict escalated. = 在冲突升级之前，调解员已经介入调解了。


将来时:
I will intercede for you if you need my help. = 如果你需要我的帮助，我会为你求情。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would intercede with the higher authority. = 如果我是你，我会向更高的权威求情。
I wish someone would intercede in this matter. = 我希望有人能介入此事。


被动语态:
The prisoner was interceded for by his family. = 囚犯得到了家人的求情。
The dispute was interceded in by a neutral party. = 纠纷由中立方进行调解。


疑问句:
Will you intercede for me? = 你会为我求情吗？
Did anyone intercede on his behalf? = 有人为他求情吗？


否定句:
He did not intercede in the argument. = 他没有介入这场争论。
They refused to intercede on his behalf. = 他们拒绝为他求情。



PHRASE->
intercede with someone = 向某人求情
intercede on someone's behalf = 为某人求情
intercede in a dispute = 调解纠纷
intercede for a friend = 为朋友求情
successfully intercede = 成功调解


词源:
The word "intercede" comes from the Latin "intercedere,"  which is composed of "inter" (between) and "cedere" (to yield, go, or proceed).  Therefore, the original meaning suggests going between parties.
