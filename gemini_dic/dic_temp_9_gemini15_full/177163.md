rearguard:/ˈrɪəɡɑːrd/| n.|后卫；殿后部队；保护者|A rearguard is the part of an army that protects the rest of the army as it retreats.  It is also used figuratively to describe someone or something that protects or supports something else from behind.|例句：The rearguard fought bravely to allow the main force to escape. 后卫英勇作战，以掩护主力部队撤退。

例句：The old traditions formed a rearguard action against the changing times.  古老的传统构成了抵制时代变迁的最后防线。


近义词：rear,guard,protection,support|反义词：vanguard,advance guard|名词
