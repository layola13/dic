fondness:/ˈfɒndnəs/ /ˈfɑːndnəs/| n. |喜爱，爱好，癖好 |A feeling of affection or liking; fondness for something.  It refers to a strong liking or preference for someone or something.  |例句：He has a fondness for old cars. 他喜欢老爷车。

例句：Her fondness for chocolate is legendary.  她对巧克力的喜爱是出了名的。


近义词：liking, affection, attachment, preference, partiality |反义词：dislike, aversion, hatred, antipathy


词性：名词
