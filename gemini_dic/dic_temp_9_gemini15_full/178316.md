recondemned:/ˌriːkənˈdemd/| v.|重新谴责，再次定罪|To condemn again; to pronounce judgment against anew.|The court recondemned the criminal after new evidence surfaced.  法庭在出现新的证据后再次判决该罪犯有罪。|The judge recondemned the defendant for his heinous crime.  法官因被告犯下滔天罪行而再次对其定罪。|condemn, denounce|acquit, pardon|动词
