predeposit:/ˌpriːdɪˈpɒzɪt/| n. |预存，预付|A sum of money paid in advance.  It is often used in contexts like securing a service or product or making a payment before the actual service or product is delivered or rendered. |例句：The company requires a predeposit of $1000 to start the project. 公司要求预付1000美元才能启动项目。

例句：We made a predeposit on the apartment to secure it before the official lease signing. 我们预付了公寓的押金，以便在正式签署租赁协议之前将其预订。


近义词：advance payment, down payment, deposit in advance |反义词：postpayment, delayed payment |词性:名词
