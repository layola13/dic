hampered:/ˈhæmpərd/| vt.|阻碍，妨碍|to hinder or impede the movement or progress of |The strong wind hampered our progress.  强风阻碍了我们的前进。
The heavy rain hampered rescue efforts. 大雨妨碍了救援工作。|hinder, impede, obstruct, restrict|facilitate, aid, assist|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bad weather hampers our outdoor activities.  糟糕的天气阻碍了我们的户外活动。
This problem hampers the company's development.  这个问题阻碍了公司的发展。

一般过去时:
The snowstorm hampered travel for several days.  暴风雪阻碍了旅行好几天。
His lack of experience hampered his progress in the company. 他缺乏经验，阻碍了他的职业发展。

现在完成时:
The constant rain has hampered the harvest.  持续的降雨已经阻碍了收成。
Technical difficulties have hampered the project's completion. 技术难题已经阻碍了项目的完成。

过去完成时:
The previous delays had hampered our ability to meet the deadline.  之前的延误已经阻碍了我们按时完成任务的能力。
A lack of funding had hampered their research for years.  资金不足多年来一直阻碍着他们的研究。

将来时:
The expected snowstorm will hamper travel next week. 预计的暴风雪将阻碍下周的出行。
Further complications will hamper the negotiations.  进一步的复杂情况将会阻碍谈判。

虚拟语气:
If the rain hadn't hampered our journey, we would have arrived earlier. 如果雨没有阻碍我们的旅程，我们就会早到。
If the government hadn't hampered the economy, we would have had stronger growth. 如果政府没有阻碍经济发展，我们将会有更强劲的增长。

被动语态:
Our progress was hampered by unexpected delays. 我们的进展因意外延误而受阻。
The team's work was hampered by a lack of resources.  团队的工作因缺乏资源而受阻。

疑问句:
What hampered your ability to finish the project on time? 什么阻碍了你按时完成项目？
Were there any factors that hampered the experiment's success? 有什么因素阻碍了实验的成功？

否定句:
Nothing hampered their determination to succeed.  没有什么能阻碍他们成功的决心。
The project wasn't hampered by any major setbacks.  该项目没有受到任何重大挫折的阻碍。
