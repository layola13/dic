centred: /ˈsɛntəd/ | adj. | 以…为中心; 集中的 |  centred means having a particular thing as its central feature or most important part. It suggests a focus or concentration on a specific point or idea.  | 例句：The debate was centred around the issue of climate change.  这场辩论的中心议题是气候变化。

The whole project is centred on improving customer service.  整个项目都集中在改善客户服务上。


近义词：focused, concentrated, focused on, centered (US spelling) | 反义词：decentralized, dispersed, scattered | 词性：形容词
