encumber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/ | vt. |妨碍；阻碍；拖累 |To hinder or impede the movement or progress of; to burden.  |例句：The heavy backpack encumbered his progress.  沉重的背包妨碍了他的前进。

例句：Excessive regulations encumber small businesses. 过多的规章制度妨碍了小企业的经营。


近义词：hamper, hinder, impede, obstruct, burden, weigh down | 反义词：aid, assist, facilitate, expedite | 动词
