replicated:/ˈrɛplɪkeɪtɪd/ | v. |复制，重复，模拟 | To make or do something again in exactly the same way; to copy something exactly.  |  The experiment was replicated several times to ensure accuracy.  实验重复进行了好几次以确保准确性。 The software successfully replicated the original program's functions. 该软件成功地复制了原始程序的功能。| duplicated, reproduced, copied | unique, original, unlike |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists replicate experiments to verify results.  科学家重复实验以验证结果。
They replicate the process every year. 他们每年都重复这个过程。


一般过去时:
The study was replicated by another research team.  这项研究被另一个研究小组复制了。
He replicated the painting with great skill. 他巧妙地复制了这幅画。


现在完成时:
Researchers have replicated the findings of the initial study. 研究人员已经复制了初步研究的结果。
She has replicated her success in the competition. 她已经复制了她比赛中的成功。


过去完成时:
They had replicated the experiment before publishing their findings. 他们在发表研究结果之前已经重复了实验。
The artist had replicated the masterpiece before it was destroyed. 这幅杰作被毁坏之前，艺术家已经复制了它。


将来时:
We will replicate the test under different conditions. 我们将在不同的条件下重复测试。
They will replicate the design for mass production. 他们将复制该设计以进行大规模生产。


虚拟语气:
If we replicated the experiment, we might get different results. 如果我们重复实验，可能会得到不同的结果。
It is important that the results are replicated independently. 重要的是，结果必须被独立地复制。


被动语态:
The experiment was replicated by a different laboratory. 该实验由不同的实验室重复进行。
The findings have been replicated in numerous studies.  这些发现已经在许多研究中被复制。


疑问句:
Can this result be replicated?  这个结果可以被复制吗？
Have they replicated the software yet? 他们已经复制软件了吗？


否定句:
The experiment could not be replicated due to equipment failure. 由于设备故障，实验无法重复。
They failed to replicate the original results. 他们未能复制原始结果。
