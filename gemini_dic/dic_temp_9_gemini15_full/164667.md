preexplanation:/ˌpriːˌɛksplæˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 预先解释；初步解释 | A preexplanation is an explanation given before the main explanation, often to provide context or background information.  It can also refer to a preliminary or introductory explanation. | Example: The professor gave a brief preexplanation of the complex theory before delving into the details.  教授在深入讲解复杂理论的细节之前，先做了简短的预先解释。| 近义词：introduction, preface, prelude, preliminary explanation | 反义词：postscript, afterthought
