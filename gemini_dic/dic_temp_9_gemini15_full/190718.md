sauter:/soʊˈteɪ/| vt.|  跳跃；（尤指烹调时）快速翻炒 |To jump or leap; to cook (food) quickly in a pan with a small amount of fat. |例句：He sautered over the fence. 他跳过栅栏。  The chef sautered the vegetables in butter. 厨师用黄油快速翻炒蔬菜。|近义词：jump, leap, stir-fry|反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sautés the onions until they are translucent. = 他把洋葱翻炒到半透明。
She sautés the garlic for a minute before adding the chicken. = 她先把蒜翻炒一分钟，然后再加入鸡肉。


一般过去时:
I sautéed the mushrooms with some herbs. = 我用一些香料翻炒了蘑菇。
He sautéed the shrimp until they turned pink. = 他把虾翻炒到变成粉红色。


现在完成时:
She has sautéed many dishes in her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经翻炒过许多菜肴。
I have never sautéed fish before. = 我以前从未翻炒过鱼。


过去完成时:
The chef had sautéed the vegetables before adding the sauce. = 厨师在加入酱汁之前已经翻炒过蔬菜了。
They had sautéed the potatoes until they were crispy. = 他们把土豆翻炒到酥脆。


将来时:
I will sauté the chicken with some ginger and garlic. = 我将用生姜和大蒜翻炒鸡肉。
She will sauté the vegetables in olive oil. = 她将用橄榄油翻炒蔬菜。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would sauté the prawns. = 如果我有更多时间，我会翻炒虾。
I wish I had sautéed the peppers earlier. = 我真希望我早点把青椒翻炒了。


被动语态:
The vegetables were sautéed in butter. = 蔬菜是用黄油翻炒的。
The shrimp will be sautéed until pink. = 虾将会被翻炒到粉红色。


疑问句:
Did you sauté the onions? = 你翻炒洋葱了吗？
How long should I sauté the garlic? = 我应该翻炒蒜多久？


否定句:
I didn't sauté the potatoes, I boiled them. = 我没有翻炒土豆，我是煮的。
She doesn't usually sauté the meat. = 她通常不翻炒肉类。
