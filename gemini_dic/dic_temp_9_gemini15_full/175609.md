rangier:/ˈreɪndʒər/| n.|护林员，管理员；（汽车）路虎揽胜|A ranger is a person who protects and manages a natural area, such as a forest or park.  The word can also refer to a specific model of Land Rover vehicle.|例句：The park ranger gave us a safety briefing before we started our hike. 公园护林员在我们开始徒步旅行之前给我们做了安全简报。|例句：He drives a classic Range Rover. 他开着一辆经典的路虎揽胜。|近义词：warden, gamekeeper, forest guard|反义词：None specifically.
