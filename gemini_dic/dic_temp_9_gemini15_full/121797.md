misreference:/ˌmɪsˈrɛfərəns/| n.|误引，错引，误称|The act of referring to something incorrectly or inappropriately; a mistaken or inaccurate reference.  It often involves misquoting, misinterpreting, or wrongly attributing a source.|例句：The article contained several misreferences to historical events. 这篇文章对历史事件有几处错误引用。

例句：His misreference to the study led to a flawed conclusion. 他对这项研究的错误引用导致了错误的结论。


近义词：misquotation, misattribution, error, mistake

反义词：accurate reference, correct citation


词性:名词
