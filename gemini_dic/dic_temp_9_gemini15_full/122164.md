mistrustless: /mɪsˈtrʌstləs/ | adj. | 不怀疑的；信任的 |  Free from mistrust; characterized by trust.  This word is relatively rare and often replaced by synonyms.  It implies a complete absence of suspicion or doubt. |


例句：

The team worked together in a mistrustless environment, sharing ideas openly and honestly.  团队在一个互相信任的环境中一起工作，公开诚实地分享想法。

Her mistrustless nature made her vulnerable to manipulation. 她不设防的性格使她容易受到操纵。


近义词：trusting, trusting, confident, believing


反义词：suspicious, distrustful, skeptical


词性:形容词
