coneine:/ˈkoʊniːn/| n. |锥宁，毒芹碱|A highly poisonous alkaloid found in hemlock (Conium maculatum).  It is a potent neurotoxin that affects the nervous system, causing paralysis and potentially death.|例句：Coneine is a major toxic component of poison hemlock, responsible for its lethal effects. 锥宁是毒芹中主要的毒性成分，是其致死作用的原因。|例句：Ingestion of even small amounts of coneine can lead to serious health consequences. 即使摄入少量锥宁也会导致严重的健康后果。|近义词：conine (same meaning)|反义词：无|名词
