ponderate:/ˈpɒndəreɪt/| v.|权衡；深思熟虑；慎重考虑|To consider something carefully and thoughtfully before making a decision.  It implies a weighing of different factors and options.|He pondered the implications of his decision before announcing it to the team. 他在向团队宣布决定之前仔细权衡了其影响。|deliberate, contemplate, reflect, weigh|impulsive, rash|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ponderates the pros and cons of each option. = 他权衡着每个选项的利弊。
She ponderates her next move in the chess game. =  她在仔细考虑国际象棋比赛中的下一步棋。


一般过去时:
He pondered the situation carefully before acting. = 他仔细权衡了情况后才采取行动。
She pondered her options before choosing a career path. = 她仔细考虑了自己的选择后才选择了职业道路。


现在完成时:
He has pondered the problem for days without a solution. = 他已经思考这个问题好几天了，仍然没有找到解决方案。
She has pondered the meaning of life for many years. = 她多年来一直在思考人生的意义。


过去完成时:
He had pondered the consequences before making the decision. = 他在做出决定之前已经考虑过后果。
She had pondered the offer for a long time before accepting it. = 她接受这个提议之前已经考虑了很久。


将来时:
He will ponder the matter further before responding. = 他将在进一步考虑此事后再回复。
She will ponder her options before making a commitment. = 她会在做出承诺之前权衡自己的选择。


虚拟语气:
If he had pondered the risks, he might not have taken the chance. = 如果他当时权衡了风险，他可能就不会冒险了。
If she pondered the implications more carefully, she would have made a different decision. = 如果她更仔细地考虑了影响，她会做出不同的决定。


被动语态:
The decision was pondered by the committee for several weeks. = 这个决定被委员会考虑了几周。
The proposal was pondered by the board before a final decision was made. = 在做出最终决定之前，该提案被董事会仔细考虑。


疑问句:
Has he pondered the ethical implications of his research? = 他是否考虑过他研究的伦理意义？
Will she ponder the offer for a while before giving her answer? = 她会在给出答案之前考虑一下这个提议吗？


否定句:
He did not ponder the consequences of his actions. = 他没有考虑他行为的后果。
She did not ponder the issue long enough to make an informed decision. = 她没有花足够的时间考虑这个问题，无法做出明智的决定。


PHRASE->
ponder over:  仔细考虑...
ponder upon: 仔细思考...
ponder a decision: 仔细考虑一个决定
ponder the implications: 权衡影响
ponder the possibilities: 思考可能性
