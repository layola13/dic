defects:/ˈdiːfekts/ | n. | 缺陷；缺点；毛病 | flaws, imperfections, shortcomings in a person, thing, or plan;  a lack of something considered necessary or desirable.  |例句：The car has several defects that need to be repaired. 这辆车有几个需要修理的缺陷。  The report highlighted several serious defects in the company's safety procedures. 报告强调了公司安全程序中的一些严重缺陷。|近义词：flaws, imperfections, blemishes, shortcomings |反义词：perfections, strengths, advantages


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The product has many defects. = 这个产品有很多缺陷。
These designs contain several defects. = 这些设计包含几个缺陷。

一般过去时:
The inspectors found many defects in the bridge. = 检查员在桥上发现了许多缺陷。
The software had several critical defects. = 该软件存在几个严重的缺陷。

现在完成时:
They have identified all the defects in the system. = 他们已经找到了系统中的所有缺陷。
We have corrected all the known defects. = 我们已经纠正了所有已知的缺陷。

过去完成时:
The engineers had already discovered the defects before the launch. = 工程师在发布之前就已经发现了缺陷。
They had overlooked several crucial defects in the planning stage. = 他们在计划阶段忽略了几个关键的缺陷。

将来时:
We will address these defects in the next update. = 我们将在下一个更新中解决这些缺陷。
They will hopefully find and fix all the defects soon. = 他们有望很快找到并修复所有缺陷。

虚拟语气:
If the product had fewer defects, it would sell better. = 如果产品缺陷较少，它会卖得更好。
I wish the software didn't have so many defects. = 我希望软件没有这么多缺陷。

被动语态:
Many defects were found during the testing phase. = 在测试阶段发现了许多缺陷。
The defects will be addressed in the next release. = 这些缺陷将在下一个版本中得到解决。

疑问句:
Are there any defects in this batch of goods? = 这批货物有缺陷吗？
What defects were reported by the users? = 用户报告了哪些缺陷？

否定句:
The product has no significant defects. = 该产品没有明显的缺陷。
There were no noticeable defects in the finished product. = 成品没有明显的缺陷。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "defect" comes from the Old French word "defec," meaning "to fail," "to be wanting," or "to be deficient." This, in turn, derives from the Latin word "deficere," which has the same basic meaning.  The suffix "-ect" is a common noun-forming suffix.
