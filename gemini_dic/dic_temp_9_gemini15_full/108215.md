laudatorily:/ˈlɔːdeɪtərɪli/ | adj. | 赞美地；称赞地 | In a laudatory manner; expressing praise.  |例句：The critic wrote a laudatory review of the new play.  评论家对这部新剧写了一篇赞美的评论。

例句：He spoke laudatorily about his team's achievements. 他赞扬地谈论了他团队的成就。
