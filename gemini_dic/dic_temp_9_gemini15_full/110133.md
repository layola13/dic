lidos:/ˈliːdoʊz/| n.|海滩；浴场|A lido is an area of a beach or a place with a swimming pool, especially one that is open to the public and often has other recreational facilities.|例句：The lido was crowded with people enjoying the sunshine.  这个海滩挤满了享受阳光的人们。|例句：We spent the afternoon at the lido, swimming and sunbathing. 我们在浴场度过了下午，游泳和日光浴。|近义词：beach, seaside, swimming pool|反义词：None specifically, but perhaps "inland" could be considered a contextual antonym.
