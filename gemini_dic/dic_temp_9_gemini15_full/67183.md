expounds:/ɪkˈspaʊndz/| vt.|详细解释；阐述|To explain or discuss something in detail.  The professor expounded on the theory of relativity.|近义词：explain, elaborate, elucidate|反义词：obscure, conceal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The professor expounds on the subject of quantum physics every Tuesday. = 教授每周二都会详细讲解量子物理学。
He expounds his views on social justice in his latest book. = 他在他的最新著作中阐述了他对社会正义的观点。

一般过去时:
She expounded the rules of the game to the newcomers. = 她向新来的人详细解释了比赛规则。
The lawyer expounded the details of the case to the jury. = 律师向陪审团详细解释了案件的细节。

现在完成时:
He has expounded this theory in numerous publications. = 他已在许多出版物中阐述了这一理论。
The politician has expounded on her economic policies throughout the campaign. = 在整个竞选过程中，这位政治家一直在阐述她的经济政策。

过去完成时:
After the lecture, she had expounded on the implications of the research. = 讲座结束后，她详细解释了这项研究的意义。
They had expounded their concerns about the project before the meeting. = 会议前，他们已经详细阐述了他们对该项目的担忧。


将来时:
She will expound on the historical context of the novel in her next lecture. = 她将在下次讲座中详细解释这部小说的历史背景。
The speaker will expound his ideas during the conference. = 演讲者将在会议期间阐述他的想法。

虚拟语气:
If he had time, he would expound further on the topic. = 如果他有时间，他会进一步详细解释这个话题。
I wish I could expound more clearly on this complex issue. = 我希望我能更清晰地阐述这个复杂的问题。

被动语态:
The theory was expounded in great detail in the textbook. = 这本书对该理论做了非常详细的解释。
His ideas have been expounded by many scholars. = 许多学者都阐述过他的思想。

疑问句:
How will the speaker expound on this controversial subject? = 演讲者将如何阐述这个有争议的话题？
Will the professor expound on the implications of the study? = 教授会阐述这项研究的意义吗？

否定句:
He did not expound on the reasons for his decision. = 他没有详细解释他做出决定的原因。
The article did not expound sufficiently on the underlying causes. = 这篇文章没有充分阐述根本原因。
