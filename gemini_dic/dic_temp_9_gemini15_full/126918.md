nargile:/nɑːrˈɡɪliː/ | n. |水烟，水烟筒 |A narghile (also spelled narghileh, nargile, or hookah) is a water pipe that is used to smoke flavored tobacco.  It's a popular method of smoking in many Middle Eastern and South Asian cultures. The smoke is cooled and filtered by passing it through water before inhalation. |He enjoyed smoking his narghile on the balcony every evening. 他每天晚上都喜欢在阳台上抽水烟。|hookah, shisha, hubble-bubble |  |noun
