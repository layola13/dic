rig-out:/ˈrɪɡaʊt/| n. vt.|盛装；全套装备；打扮；装饰|To dress oneself or someone else in a particular style of clothing, often for a special occasion.  It can also refer to the clothing itself.|例句：He rigged himself out in a sharp suit for the wedding. 他为了婚礼精心打扮了一番。(vt.)

He showed off his new rig-out at the party. 他在派对上炫耀他的新行头。(n.)|近义词：outfit, attire, costume, get-up|反义词：strip down, undress
