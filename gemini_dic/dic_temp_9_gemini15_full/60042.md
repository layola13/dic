educationalist:/ˌedʒuˈkeɪʃənəlɪst/| n. |教育家，教育工作者|A person who is professionally involved in education, often at a high level of policy or administration.  They may be involved in curriculum development, teacher training, or educational research.|例句：Many educationalists advocate for more inclusive teaching methods. 许多教育家提倡更具包容性的教学方法。  The educationalist presented a compelling argument for reforming the education system.这位教育家提出了一个令人信服的教育体系改革论点。|近义词：educator, pedagogue, teacher (in a broader sense)|反义词：N/A|名词
