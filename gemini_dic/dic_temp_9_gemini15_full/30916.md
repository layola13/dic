chain-smokes:/tʃeɪnˈsmoʊks/ | v. | 连续抽烟 | To smoke cigarettes one after another, without pausing.  This implies a rapid and habitual pattern of smoking. | He chain-smokes throughout the day. 他整天不停地抽烟。  She chain-smoked nervously before the interview. 面试前她紧张地一根接一根地抽烟。 | smokes incessantly, puffs away continuously | quits smoking, stops smoking | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chain-smokes every day. = 他每天都连续抽烟。
They chain-smoke during meetings. = 他们在会议期间连续抽烟。

一般过去时:
He chain-smoked during the whole flight. = 他在整个飞行过程中不停地抽烟。
She chain-smoked all night. = 她整夜不停地抽烟。

现在完成时:
He has chain-smoked for twenty years. = 他已经连续抽烟二十年了。
She has chain-smoked since she was a teenager. = 她从十几岁起就一直连续抽烟。

过去完成时:
He had chain-smoked before he finally quit. = 他在最终戒烟之前一直不停地抽烟。
She had chain-smoked throughout the stressful period. = 在那段压力很大的时期，她一直不停地抽烟。

将来时:
He will chain-smoke if he's stressed. = 如果他压力很大，他会不停地抽烟。
She will probably chain-smoke during the exam. = 她考试期间可能会不停地抽烟。
