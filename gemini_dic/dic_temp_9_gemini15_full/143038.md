outblossoming: /ˌaʊtˈblɑːsəmɪŋ/ | v. |盛开，茂盛生长，超越 |The act of blossoming or flourishing to a greater extent than something else; surpassing in beauty or growth.  |例句：The roses outblossomed all the other flowers in the garden.  玫瑰花盛开，胜过花园里其他的花。

The apple tree outblossomed the pear tree this year.今年苹果树比梨树开得更茂盛。|近义词：surpass, excel, outshine |反义词：wither, fade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The roses outblossom the tulips every year. = 玫瑰每年都比郁金香开得更茂盛。
The sunflowers outblossom their neighbors. = 向日葵比它们的邻居长得更茂盛。

一般过去时:
Last year, the cherry blossoms outblossomed the plum blossoms. = 去年，樱花比梅花开得更茂盛。
The daffodils outblossomed the other spring flowers. = 水仙花比其他春季花朵开得更茂盛。


现在完成时:
This year's sunflowers have outblossomed last year's. = 今年的向日葵比去年的长得更茂盛。
The garden has outblossomed all expectations. = 花园比预期的还要茂盛。


过去完成时:
By the time the roses bloomed, the tulips had already outblossomed. = 当玫瑰花开的时候，郁金香已经开败了。(此处用过去完成时略显牵强，更合适的表达方式是：The tulips had already finished blooming before the roses opened.)
The orchids had outblossomed the lilies by the end of the week. = 到周末的时候，兰花已经比百合花开得更茂盛了。


将来时:
The hydrangeas will outblossom the petunias next month. = 下个月绣球花将会比矮牵牛花开得更茂盛。
We expect the roses to outblossom the other flowers this year. = 我们预计今年玫瑰花会比其他花开得更茂盛。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were better, the flowers would outblossom. = 如果天气更好，花朵会开得更茂盛。
I wish my roses would outblossom my neighbor’s. = 我希望我的玫瑰花能比我邻居的开得更茂盛。


被动语态:
The other flowers were outblossomed by the roses. = 其他花朵被玫瑰花比下去了。 (被动语态在此处略显不自然，较少使用)


疑问句:
Will the lilies outblossom the tulips this year? = 今年百合花会比郁金香开得更茂盛吗？
Do the sunflowers usually outblossom the daisies? = 向日葵通常比雏菊开得更茂盛吗？


否定句:
The daisies did not outblossom the sunflowers this year. = 今年雏菊没有向日葵开得茂盛。
The tulips didn't outblossom the roses. = 郁金香没有玫瑰开得茂盛。


PHRASE->
There is no common phrase directly using "outblossoming".  However, we can use phrases with similar meanings:

outdo in beauty: 超越在美丽方面
excel in growth: 在生长方面胜过
surpass in bloom: 在开花方面超越
shine brighter than: 比...更闪耀
bloom more profusely than: 比...开得更茂盛


词源:

out- (prefix):  表示"超越"，"超过"
blossom (verb):  开花，茂盛生长
