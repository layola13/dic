forests:/ˈfɒrɪsts/ /ˈfɔːrɪsts/| n.|森林，林地|A large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth.|例句：We hiked through dense forests. 我们徒步穿越了茂密的森林。|例句：The forests are home to many endangered species.  森林是许多濒危物种的家园。|近义词：woods, woodland, jungles|反义词：desert, plains


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The forests provide habitat for many animals. = 森林为许多动物提供了栖息地。
Forests cover a significant portion of the Earth's surface. = 森林覆盖了地球表面相当大的一部分。

一般过去时:
The ancient forests were destroyed by logging. = 古老的森林被伐木摧毁了。
Forests once covered much of this area. = 森林曾经覆盖了这个地区的很大一部分。

现在完成时:
Deforestation has destroyed many forests. = 滥砍滥伐已经摧毁了许多森林。
We have planted new trees to replenish the forests. = 我们种植了新树来补充森林。

过去完成时:
The indigenous people had lived in harmony with the forests for centuries. = 土著居民几个世纪以来一直与森林和谐相处。
Before the fire, the forests were lush and green. = 在火灾发生之前，森林郁郁葱葱。

将来时:
Scientists predict that forests will continue to shrink. = 科学家预测森林将继续缩小。
We will protect the forests for future generations. = 我们将为子孙后代保护森林。

虚拟语气:
If we protected the forests, we would have cleaner air. = 如果我们保护森林，我们将拥有更清洁的空气。
I wish the forests were not being destroyed at such a rapid rate. = 我希望森林不会以如此快的速度被破坏。

被动语态:
The forests are being cleared for farmland. = 森林正在被清理用于农田。
Many forests have been damaged by pollution. = 许多森林受到了污染的破坏。

疑问句:
Are these forests protected? = 这些森林受到保护吗？
What types of trees grow in these forests? = 这些森林里生长着什么类型的树木？

否定句:
These forests are not easily accessible. = 这些森林不容易到达。
That area does not contain many forests. = 那个地区没有很多森林。
