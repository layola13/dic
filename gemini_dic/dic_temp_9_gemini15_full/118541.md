mesometrium:/ˌmɛzoʊˈmiːtriəm/| n. |子宫系膜|The mesometrium is the largest part of the broad ligament of the uterus, a fold of the peritoneum that supports the uterus and its associated structures.  It is a double layer of peritoneum extending from the sides of the uterus to the lateral pelvic wall. It houses the uterine artery, vein, and nerve supply. |The mesometrium provides support and contains vital blood vessels and nerves for the uterus.  The mesometrium is crucial for the health and function of the uterus.|近义词:broad ligament (partially)|反义词:|词性:名词
