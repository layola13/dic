overhauler:/ˈoʊvərˌhɔːlər/| n. |彻底检修者；大修工|A person who overhauls something, especially a machine or system.  It can also refer to a tool or device used in overhauling.|例句：The mechanic is a skilled overhauler, capable of restoring old engines to their former glory.  这位机械师是一位熟练的检修工，能够将旧发动机修复到以前的辉煌状态。|近义词：repairer, mechanic, reviser|反义词：neglecter, destroyer
