congest:/kənˈdʒɛst/| vt. & adj.|堵塞；拥挤；充血 |To block or obstruct (a place, passage, etc.) with excessive quantities of something; to make or become crowded or congested.|例句：The traffic is congested this morning.  今天早上交通堵塞。|例句：The city's roads are congested during rush hour.  在高峰时段，该市的道路十分拥挤。|近义词：block, clog, overcrowd |反义词：clear, unclog, relieve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The traffic congests the city center every day. = 交通每天都堵塞市中心。
The hospital congests with patients during flu season. = 流感季节医院病人爆满。

一般过去时:
The pipes congested after years of neglect. = 由于多年疏忽，管道堵塞了。
The streets congested with people celebrating the victory. = 街道上挤满了庆祝胜利的人们。

现在完成时:
The heavy snow has congested the airport. = 大雪已经导致机场拥堵。
The overuse of antibiotics has congested my sinuses.  = 抗生素的过度使用堵塞了我的鼻窦。

过去完成时:
The road had congested before the accident happened. = 事故发生前道路就已经堵塞了。
The server had congested before the website crashed. = 网站崩溃前服务器就已经超负荷了。

将来时:
The new shopping mall will congest the already busy streets. = 新建的购物中心将使本来就繁忙的街道更加拥挤。
The expected influx of tourists will congest the town. = 预计涌入的游客将使小镇拥挤不堪。

虚拟语气:
If the roads weren't congested, I would have arrived on time. = 如果道路不堵车，我就准时到了。
If the hospital wasn't congested, she would have been admitted immediately. = 如果医院不拥挤，她就会立即被收治。

被动语态:
The highway was congested by a major accident. = 高速公路因一起重大交通事故而堵塞。
The network was congested by too many users. = 网络因用户过多而拥塞。

疑问句:
Is the freeway congested right now? = 高速公路现在堵车吗？
Are the streets congested around the stadium? = 体育场周围的街道拥挤吗？

否定句:
The roads weren't congested yesterday. = 昨天的道路没有堵塞。
The airport wasn't congested despite the bad weather. = 尽管天气恶劣，机场也没有拥挤。
