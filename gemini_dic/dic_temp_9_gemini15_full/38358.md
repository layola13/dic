concentrative: /ˌkɒnsənˈtreɪtɪv/ /ˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 专注的；集中的 |  Relating to or characterized by concentration; requiring or involving concentration.  |例句：The teacher praised the students for their concentrative efforts during the exam. 老师称赞学生们在考试期间的专注努力。

例句：She needed a quiet environment for concentrative work. 她需要一个安静的环境才能专注工作。


近义词：focused, attentive, absorbed, engrossed | 反义词：distracted, inattentive, unfocused


词源：源于动词concentrate，加上形容词后缀-ive。 concentrate本身来自拉丁语concentrāre，意思是“集中在一起”。
