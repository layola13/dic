senility:/səˈnɪləti/| n.|衰老；老年性痴呆|The state of being senile; the loss of mental faculties associated with old age.  It often involves memory loss, confusion, and difficulty with thinking and reasoning.  Senility is not a specific disease, but rather a general term describing the decline in cognitive function that can accompany aging.|例句：His senility made it difficult for him to recognize his own grandchildren.  他的老年痴呆症使他难以认出自己的孙子孙女。


例句：The doctor attributed his erratic behavior to senility. 医生认为他古怪的行为是老年痴呆造成的。


近义词：dementia, dotage, senescence|反义词：youthfulness, vigor
