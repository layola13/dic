nondegradable: /ˌnɒnˈdɛɡrədeɪbəl/ | adj. | 不可降解的 | Not capable of being decomposed by natural biological processes.  Substances that are nondegradable persist in the environment for a long time, often causing pollution. |  例句：Plastic bags are a major source of nondegradable waste.  塑料袋是不可降解垃圾的主要来源。

例句：The nondegradable nature of certain plastics poses a significant environmental challenge.  某些塑料的不可降解特性构成了重大的环境挑战。


近义词：indecomposable, persistent, persistent, unbreakable(in the context of decomposition)


反义词：degradable, biodegradable, compostable
