leucocytic: /ˌluːkoʊˈsɪtɪk/ | adj. | 白细胞的，与白细胞有关的 | Relating to or characteristic of leucocytes (white blood cells).  | The leucocytic count was elevated, indicating an infection. 白细胞计数升高，表明有感染。 |  leukocytic |  (None readily available for direct antonym.  The opposite would be something like "erythrocytic," relating to red blood cells.)


WORD ORIGIN->
From  "leuco-" (white) + "-cytic" (relating to cells).  "Leuco-" is from the Greek word λευκός (leukos) meaning "white." "-Cytic" comes from the Greek word κύτος (kytos), meaning "cell," via the combining form "-cyte."
