maladministering:/ˌmælædmɪnɪˈstɜːrɪŋ/| v.| 拙劣管理；处理不当；滥用职权 |The act of managing or handling something badly or improperly, often involving abuse of power or neglect of duty.  It implies incompetence, negligence, or corruption in the administration of something. |例句：The hospital was accused of maladministering funds, leading to a shortage of essential medical supplies. 医院被指控资金管理不善，导致基本医疗用品短缺。

The government's maladministering of the social welfare program resulted in widespread dissatisfaction.  政府对社会福利计划的管理不善导致了广泛的不满。|近义词：mismanage, mishandle, misgovern, abuse |反义词：administer well, manage effectively, govern justly
