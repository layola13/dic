retelephone:/ˌriːˈtɛləfoʊn/|v.|再打电话，复电|To telephone again or to telephone someone again.|例句：I need to retelephone the client to confirm the details. 我需要再给客户打电话确认细节。|近义词：call back, telephone again|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retelephones his boss every evening. 他每天晚上都给老板回电话。
She retelephones her mother regularly to check on her. 她定期给母亲打电话问候。


一般过去时:
I retelephoned the company after I hung up. 我挂断电话后又给公司打了电话。
They retelephoned the hotel to confirm their reservation. 他们又给酒店打电话确认预订。


现在完成时:
She has retelephoned him three times this morning. 今天早上她已经给他打了三次电话。
He has retelephoned the office to clarify the instructions. 他再次给办公室打电话澄清指示。


过去完成时:
I had retelephoned the doctor before I went to the hospital. 在去医院之前，我已经再次给医生打了电话。
They had retelephoned the airline to reschedule their flight. 他们已经再次给航空公司打电话改签航班。


将来时:
I will retelephone them tomorrow morning. 我明天早上会再给他们打电话。
She will retelephone the supplier if she doesn't receive the package. 如果她没有收到包裹，她会再次联系供应商。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would retelephone him right now. 如果我有时间，我现在就给他回电话。
If I were you, I would retelephone and apologize. 如果我是你，我会再次打电话道歉。


被动语态:
The client was retelephoned by the sales representative. 客户被销售代表再次打电话联系了。
The order was retelephoned to the warehouse. 订单再次被电话通知到仓库。


疑问句:
Will you retelephone the client later? 你稍后会再次给客户打电话吗？
Did he retelephone the office? 他给办公室回电话了吗？


否定句:
I didn't retelephone him because I had already solved the problem. 我没有给他回电话，因为我已经解决了问题。
She didn't retelephone the shop because she found the item elsewhere. 她没有给商店回电话，因为她在别处找到了那件商品。
