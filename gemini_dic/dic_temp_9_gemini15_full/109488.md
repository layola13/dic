leprosy:/ˈlɛprəsi/| n.|麻风病|A chronic infectious disease caused by the bacterium Mycobacterium leprae, affecting the skin, nerves, mucosa of the upper respiratory tract, and eyes.  It can lead to disfigurement and disability if left untreated.|例句：Leprosy is a curable disease with early detection and treatment. 麻风病是一种可治愈的疾病，及早发现和治疗即可。|近义词：Hansen's disease|反义词：无
