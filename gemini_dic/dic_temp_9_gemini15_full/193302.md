seene:/siːn/| n.|场景，景象，事件|A scene is a part of a play or film, or a single event in a longer story.  It can also refer to a view or picture.|例句：The opening scene of the play was set in a forest.  这出戏的开场是在森林里。

The accident scene was chaotic. 事故现场一片混乱。|近义词：view, sight, spectacle, event|反义词：background, nothing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scene is beautiful. = 场景很美。
This scene depicts a bustling marketplace. = 这个场景描绘了一个热闹的市场。

一般过去时:
The scene changed dramatically. = 场景戏剧性地发生了变化。
