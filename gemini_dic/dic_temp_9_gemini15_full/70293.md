fidget:/ˈfɪdʒɪt/| v. n.|烦躁不安；坐立不安；|to make small movements, especially because you are nervous, bored, or impatient.|例句：He fidgeted in his seat, waiting for the interview to begin. 他在座位上坐立不安，等待面试开始。
例句：Stop fidgeting!  别动来动去了！|近义词：restless, uneasy, twitch|反义词：calm, still, relaxed|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fidgets constantly. = 他不停地动来动去。
She fidgets with her pen during meetings. = 她在会议期间不停地玩弄她的钢笔。

一般过去时:
He fidgeted nervously during the exam. = 他在考试期间紧张地动来动去。
She fidgeted with her necklace while she waited. = 她等待时不停地摆弄她的项链。

现在完成时:
I have been fidgeting all morning. = 我整个上午都坐立不安。
He has fidgeted through the entire presentation. = 他在整个演示过程中都坐立不安。

过去完成时:
She had fidgeted all day before the big announcement. = 在重大宣布之前，她整天都坐立不安。
He had fidgeted throughout the long, boring lecture. = 在冗长而乏味的讲座中，他一直坐立不安。

将来时:
I think I will fidget during the flight. = 我想我会在飞行过程中坐立不安。
She believes she will fidget less if she takes a calming pill. = 她认为如果吃一颗镇静药，她会少动些。

虚拟语气:
If I weren't so nervous, I wouldn't fidget so much. = 如果我不那么紧张，我就不会这么坐立不安了。
I wish I didn't fidget so much during important events. = 我希望我在重要场合不要那么坐立不安。

被动语态:  (The verb "fidget" is rarely used in the passive voice.)
His nervousness was evident as he fidgeted. = 他坐立不安，这表明他很紧张。(这里被动的是"nervousness"而不是"fidget")
It is observed that he fidgets a lot. = 人们观察到他经常坐立不安。(这里强调的是观察到的行为)


疑问句:

Why are you fidgeting so much? = 你为什么这么坐立不安？


否定句:
He didn't fidget at all during the performance. = 他在表演期间一点也不动。
She tried not to fidget, but she couldn't help it. = 她试图不要动来动去，但她忍不住了。
