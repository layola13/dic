quasi-enthused:/ˈkwɑːzaɪɪnˈθuːzd/| adj. |略微兴奋的，不怎么热情的|Showing only slight or lukewarm enthusiasm; half-hearted.|He gave a quasi-enthused nod to the suggestion. 他对这个建议略微点头表示同意。|近义词：mildly enthusiastic, unenthusiastic, apathetic|反义词： enthusiastic, passionate, fervent
