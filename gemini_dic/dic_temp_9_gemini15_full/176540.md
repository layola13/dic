re-routes:/ˌriːˈraʊts/| vt.|重新规划路线；改道|To change the route or course of something, especially traffic or a journey.|例句：The traffic was re-routed due to the accident.  交通事故导致道路改道。|例句：The airline re-routed the flight to avoid the storm. 航空公司为了避开风暴而改变了航班路线。|近义词：redirect, reroute, divert|反义词：maintain the original route


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system re-routes traffic automatically. = 系统自动重新规划交通路线。
They re-route packages daily. = 他们每天重新规划包裹的路线。


一般过去时:
The police re-routed traffic around the accident. = 警察将交通改道绕过事故现场。
We re-routed the hike to avoid the muddy trail. = 我们重新规划了徒步路线以避开泥泞的小路。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The engineers had re-routed the water pipes to prevent flooding. = 工程师们已经重新规划了水管路线以防止洪水。
