librarianship:/ˌlaɪˈbrɛəriənʃɪp/| n. |图书馆学；图书馆工作|The profession, practice, or principles of librarianship.  Librarianship involves the organization, management, and provision of access to library resources.  |例句：She dedicated her life to librarianship, striving to make knowledge accessible to all. 她毕生致力于图书馆事业，努力使知识惠及所有人。


例句：The course covers all aspects of modern librarianship, including digital archiving and information retrieval. 这门课程涵盖了现代图书馆学的各个方面，包括数字存档和信息检索。
