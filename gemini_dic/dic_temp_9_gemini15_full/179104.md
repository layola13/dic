redetermined:/ˌriːdiˈtɜːrmɪnd/ | v. |重新决定；再次确定 | To determine again; to decide or establish something anew.  |例句：The committee redetermined the budget after reviewing the new data.  委员会在审查了新的数据后重新确定了预算。
例句：After careful consideration, they redetermined their strategy.经过仔细考虑，他们重新确定了策略。|近义词：reassess, redefine, revise |反义词：maintain, uphold
