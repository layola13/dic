iplayer:/ˈaɪˌpleɪər/| n.|iPlayer(英国广播公司BBC的在线视频点播服务)|iPlayer is the BBC's online on-demand video streaming service, allowing users to watch programs after they have been broadcast.|例句：I watched the latest episode of Doctor Who on BBC iPlayer. 我在BBC iPlayer上观看了《神秘博士》的最新一集。|近义词：streaming service, video on demand|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与传统电视广播相对)|名词
