garancin:/ˈɡærənsɪn/| n. |茜素，茜草红| A red dye obtained from the madder plant (Rubia tinctorum).  It was historically important as a textile dye and is now sometimes used in cosmetics.| Example: Garancin was a crucial dye in the production of many historical textiles.  茜素是许多历史纺织品生产中的一种重要染料。|近义词：madder dye, alizarin (a component of garancin)|反义词：None specifically, but could consider terms associated with other colors. |名词
