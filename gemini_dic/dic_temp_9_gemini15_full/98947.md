inter-clasping: /ˌɪntərˈklæspɪŋ/ | adj. | 交互紧扣的，互相紧握的 |  Describes the action or state of things being clasped or held together, often in a way that suggests intertwining or mutual embrace.  It implies a reciprocal action, unlike simply "clasping." |


例句：

The dancers performed an inter-clasping routine, their hands and arms intertwined throughout.  = 舞蹈演员表演了一个互相紧扣的舞蹈，他们的手和胳膊在整个过程中交织在一起。

The vines grew in an inter-clasping pattern, creating a dense, impenetrable wall. = 葡萄藤以互相缠绕的方式生长，形成了一堵浓密、难以逾越的墙。


近义词：intertwined, interlocked, interwoven, clasped together


反义词：separated, unclasped, loose, disengaged


词性：形容词
