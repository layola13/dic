reaffiliation: /ˌriːæˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/ | n. |重新加入；重新联系；重新加入组织| The act of rejoining or being rejoined to an organization or group.  It implies a previous affiliation that was severed and is now being restored. |例句：His reaffiliation with the political party surprised many.  他的重新加入该政党让许多人感到意外。

例句：The university's reaffiliation with the national research consortium strengthened its research capabilities. 大学重新加入国家研究联盟增强了其研究能力。


近义词：rejoining, reaffiliation, reinstatement

反义词：disaffiliation, withdrawal, secession

词性：名词
