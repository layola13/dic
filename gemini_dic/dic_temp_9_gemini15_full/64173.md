equates:/ɪˈkweɪts/ | vt. | 等同于；使相等 | To consider or represent as equivalent; to be equal to. |例句：He equates success with money. 他把成功等同于金钱。|例句：The two situations equate in terms of difficulty. 这两种情况在难度上是相同的。|近义词：equals, corresponds to, is equivalent to |反义词：differs from, contrasts with |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher equates hard work with success. = 老师把努力学习等同于成功。
This strategy equates to a significant cost saving. = 这一策略相当于节省了大量的成本。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
