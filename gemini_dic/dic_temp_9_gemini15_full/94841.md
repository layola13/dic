impute:/ɪmˈpjuːt/| vt.|归咎于；归因于；(尤指错误地)指责|To attribute (something) to someone or something.  To ascribe (guilt, fault, etc.) to someone. |例句：They imputed the failure to his incompetence.  他们把失败归咎于他的无能。

例句：The police imputed the crime to the suspect.  警方将罪行归咎于嫌疑人。


近义词：attribute, ascribe, assign, charge


反义词：exonerate, absolve


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imputes his success to hard work. = 他把他的成功归功于努力工作。
They impute the delay to unforeseen circumstances. = 他们将延误归咎于不可预见的情况。


一般过去时:
She imputed her unhappiness to her childhood. = 她将她的不幸归咎于她的童年。
The judge imputed malice to the defendant's actions. = 法官将恶意归咎于被告的行为。


现在完成时:
He has imputed his problems to external factors. = 他一直将他的问题归咎于外部因素。
They have imputed the company's losses to poor management. = 他们一直将公司的损失归咎于管理不善。


过去完成时:
She had imputed the accident to faulty brakes before the investigation. = 在调查之前，她曾将事故归咎于刹车故障。
They had imputed the rumors to their rivals' jealousy. = 他们曾将谣言归咎于竞争对手的嫉妒。


将来时:
He will impute the blame to his assistant. = 他将把责任归咎于他的助手。
They will impute the damage to the storm. = 他们将把损失归咎于风暴。


虚拟语气:
If I were to impute blame, I would impute it to the system. = 如果我要责备的话，我会责备这个系统。
I wish they hadn't imputed such negativity to my intentions. = 我希望他们没有将如此负面的评价归咎于我的意图。


被动语态:
The failure was imputed to poor planning. = 失败被归咎于糟糕的计划。
The mistake was imputed to a clerical error. = 错误被归咎于文书错误。


疑问句:
To whom do you impute this responsibility? = 你把这个责任归咎于谁？
What factors do you impute the success to? = 你把成功归因于哪些因素？


否定句:
I do not impute any blame to you. = 我不责备你。
She did not impute any ill will to his actions. = 她没有将任何恶意归咎于他的行为。
