parentally:/pəˈrentəli/| adv.|父母地，亲代地|In a way relating to or characteristic of parents.|The child was parentally neglected.  孩子受到了父母的忽视。|The situation was handled parentally.  情况得到了父母的处理。|近义词：paternally, maternally|反义词：None.
