gastromancy:/ˈɡæstrəˌmænsi/| n.|胃占卜；腹卜|Divination by examining the stomach of a sacrificed animal.  Gastromancy is an ancient form of divination where the entrails (specifically, the stomach) of a sacrificed animal are examined to predict the future or gain insight into a particular situation.  The practice is rooted in the belief that the animal's internal organs contain divine messages or clues about the future.  |例句：The priest performed gastromancy, carefully inspecting the sacrificed goat's stomach for signs of the future.  祭司进行了胃占卜，仔细检查牺牲的山羊的胃寻找未来的迹象。


The practice of gastromancy is rarely practiced today, having largely been superseded by other forms of divination. |近义词：divination, haruspicy, prophecy|反义词：N/A
