figpecker:/ˈfɪɡˌpɛkər/| n. |无花果啄食者，（尤指）一种鸟|A figpecker is a bird that feeds on figs, especially referring to certain species of birds in the family Dicaeidae.  The term might also be used informally for any bird or animal that eats figs.|例句：The figpecker flitted from branch to branch, searching for ripe figs.  无花果啄食者在树枝间飞来飞去，寻找成熟的无花果。|近义词：fig-eating bird, fruit-eating bird|反义词：N/A|名词
