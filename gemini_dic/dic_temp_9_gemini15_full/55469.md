distorting:/dɪˈstɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 扭曲；歪曲；曲解 | To change the shape, appearance, or sound of something so that it is no longer true or accurate.  |例句：The news report distorted the facts. 新闻报道歪曲了事实。

The loud music distorted the sound of her voice. 嘈杂的音乐使她的声音变得失真。


近义词：misrepresent, falsify, warp, twist, pervert | 反义词：straighten, clarify, correct
