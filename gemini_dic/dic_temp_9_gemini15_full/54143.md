disengagement:/ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt/| n. |脱离；解脱；退婚；撤兵；(机器)脱开|The act or process of disengaging; separation or detachment.  It can refer to the separation of two things that were previously connected, or the withdrawal of involvement from something. |例句：The disengagement of the two warring factions led to a period of peace.  双方交战势力的脱离导致了一段和平时期。

例句：The gradual disengagement of the troops from the conflict zone was carefully planned.  军队从冲突区逐步撤兵是经过精心策划的。


近义词：separation, detachment, withdrawal, disconnection, severance|反义词：engagement, involvement, connection, attachment|词性 n.
