/ˈfreɪnjuːlə/| n. |小带；系带；缰绳|A small frenulum is a fold of mucous membrane that connects two parts of the body, such as the frenulum of the tongue or the frenulum of the prepuce.  It is often a thin band of tissue that restricts movement to a certain degree. | Example: The baby's tongue frenulum was clipped to improve feeding.  婴儿的舌系带被剪短以改善喂养。The doctor examined the frenulum of the prepuce. 医生检查了包皮系带。|近义词：ligament, band, fold |反义词：None specifically;  the antonym would depend on the context (e.g.,  "unrestricted" movement if referring to restricted movement).
