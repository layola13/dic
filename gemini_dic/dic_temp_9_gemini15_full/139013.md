o'clock:/əˈklɒk/ | n. |几点钟|  It is a contraction of "of the clock," indicating a specific hour on a clock. It's used to tell time. | It's three o'clock. =现在是三点钟。|  | | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is now five o'clock. =现在是五点钟。
The meeting starts at two o'clock. =会议在两点开始。


一般过去时:
The train left at eight o'clock. =火车在八点离开。
We arrived at ten o'clock. =我们十点到达。


现在完成时:
The movie has already started; it's past eight o'clock. =电影已经开始了；现在已经过了八点。
He has been waiting since two o'clock. =他从两点钟就开始等了。


过去完成时:
The play had already begun by the time we arrived at seven o'clock. =在我们七点到达的时候，戏剧已经开始了。
She had left before noon o'clock. =她在中午之前离开了。


将来时:
The concert will begin at seven o'clock. =音乐会将在七点开始。
I will meet you at one o'clock tomorrow. =我明天一点钟和你见面。


虚拟语气:
If it were three o'clock, we would leave. =如果现在是三点钟，我们就走了。
I wish it were already five o'clock. =我希望现在已经是五点了。


被动语态:
The time was announced as being exactly twelve o'clock. =时间被宣布为正好十二点。
The meeting was scheduled for two o'clock. =会议安排在两点钟。


疑问句:
What time is it? =现在几点了？
Is it five o'clock yet? =现在是五点了吗？


否定句:
It is not yet three o'clock. =现在还不是三点。
The party didn't start until after twelve o'clock. =聚会直到十二点之后才开始。
