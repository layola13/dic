circumlocutory:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈlɒkjʊtəri/| adj.| 迂回的；绕圈子的；冗长的 |  Speaking or writing in a roundabout way; indirect.  It involves using many more words than necessary to express an idea.  |例句：His circumlocutory explanation only confused the issue further.  他的迂回解释只会使问题更加混乱。

例句：The politician's answer was so circumlocutory that no one understood his stance on the issue.  这位政治家的回答过于冗长，以至于没有人理解他对这个问题的立场。


近义词：indirect, roundabout, meandering, verbose, prolix

反义词：direct, concise, succinct, brief


词性:形容词
