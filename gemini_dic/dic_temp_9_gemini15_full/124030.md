morosous:/ˈmɔːrəsəs/| adj.|忧郁的；脾气暴躁的；阴沉的 | Gloomy and ill-tempered.  A morose person is sullen and bad-tempered.  They often express their unhappiness openly. |例句：He sat in morose silence. 他默默地坐着，一脸阴郁。

例句：The morose atmosphere in the room was palpable.  房间里阴沉的气氛令人感觉明显。


近义词：sulky, sullen, grumpy, gloomy, glum, ill-tempered, sour

反义词：cheerful, happy, jovial, merry, lighthearted
