ooephorectomy:/oʊəpəˈrɛktəmi/| n. | 卵巢切除术 | The surgical removal of one or both ovaries.  This procedure is often performed to treat ovarian cancer, endometriosis, or other gynecological conditions.  | Example: The doctor recommended an oophorectomy to address her persistent pelvic pain.  医生建议进行卵巢切除术以解决她持续的盆腔疼痛。 |近义词:ovariectomy |反义词:ovary transplantation
