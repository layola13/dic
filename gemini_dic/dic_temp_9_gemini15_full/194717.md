self-worships:/selfˈwɜːrʃɪps/| n.| 自我崇拜；自我陶醉 | The act of excessively admiring or praising oneself; self-centered behavior characterized by an inflated sense of self-importance.  |例句：His self-worships were evident in his constant boasting and disregard for others' opinions. 他自我崇拜的行为表现在他不断的吹嘘和无视别人的意见上。
His self-worships blinded him to his own flaws. 他的自我崇拜使他看不到自身的缺点。|近义词：narcissism, egotism, self-adoration, vanity |反义词：humility, modesty, self-deprecation |名词
