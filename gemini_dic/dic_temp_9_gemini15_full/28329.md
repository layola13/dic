carambola:/ˌkærəmˈboʊlə/| n. | 杨桃 |  Carambola refers to the fruit of the Averrhoa carambola tree, known for its star-shaped cross-section.  It's a tropical fruit with a slightly tart and sweet flavor. | Example:  The carambola slices were a beautiful garnish on the dessert. 杨桃片是甜点上美丽的装饰。  |近义词：star fruit |反义词：无
