multi-cultural:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 多文化的 | Relating to or encompassing several different cultures.  It describes societies, groups, or institutions that embrace diversity in cultural backgrounds, traditions, and beliefs.  | Example:  The school boasts a multi-cultural student body, representing numerous countries and ethnicities.  这所学校拥有一个多元文化的学生群体，代表着许多国家和民族。

The city is known for its vibrant and multi-cultural atmosphere. 这个城市以其充满活力和多元文化的氛围而闻名。


近义词：multicultural, cosmopolitan, diverse, heterogeneous

反义词：monocultural, homogeneous
