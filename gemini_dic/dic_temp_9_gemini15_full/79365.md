glens:/ɡlenz/| n.|峡谷，山谷|A glen is a small, secluded valley, typically one with a stream running through it. Glens are often characterized by their natural beauty and tranquility.|例句：We hiked through a picturesque glen, surrounded by towering trees and wildflowers. 我们徒步穿越了一个风景如画的山谷，周围环绕着高耸的树木和野花。
The children played hide-and-seek in the shady glen.孩子们在阴凉的山谷里玩捉迷藏。|近义词：valley, dell, ravine|反义词：mountain peak, highland
