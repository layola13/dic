particularizing: /pɑːrˈtɪkjʊləraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 特指；详细说明；具体化 |  To mention or describe something in detail; to give specific examples or instances.  It implies moving from a general statement to specific details or examples. |


例句：

The professor particularized the historical context of the poem, providing specific dates and events. 教授详细阐述了这首诗的历史背景，提供了具体的日期和事件。

She particularized her concerns, listing each problem point by point. 她详细说明了她的担忧，逐条列出了每个问题。


近义词： specifying, detailing, elaborating, illustrating, itemizing


反义词： generalizing, summarizing, abstracting


词性：动词
