/ˌsiːpiˈeɪ/ | n. | 每行动成本；每次行动付费 | Cost Per Action, a marketing term referring to a pricing model where advertisers pay only when a specific action is taken by a user, such as a purchase or sign-up.  This differs from cost-per-impression (CPM) or cost-per-click (CPC) models. | Example:  The CPA for this campaign was surprisingly low. 这场活动的每次行动成本出奇地低。


The CPA for our new product launch exceeded expectations. 我们新产品发布的每次行动成本超出了预期。 |近义词：cost per acquisition (CPA), pay-per-action (PPA) |反义词：cost per mille (CPM), cost per click (CPC) |名词
