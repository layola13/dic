puring:/pɔːrɪŋ/| v.|倾倒；大量涌出；下着倾盆大雨|The act of pouring something, typically a liquid, from one container to another, or the act of something falling or flowing rapidly and abundantly.|例句：She was pouring the milk into the cereal. 她正在把牛奶倒进麦片里。

例句：The rain was pouring down. 雨下得倾盆大雨。|近义词：pouring, gushing, streaming, deluging|反义词：drizzling, trickling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pours milk into his coffee every morning. = 他每天早上都往咖啡里倒牛奶。
She pours her heart out to her best friend. = 她向她最好的朋友倾诉衷肠。


一般过去时:
He poured the tea into the cups. = 他把茶倒进杯子里。
The rain poured down relentlessly. = 雨无情地下着倾盆大雨。


现在完成时:

It has poured all day. =  雨下了一整天。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
