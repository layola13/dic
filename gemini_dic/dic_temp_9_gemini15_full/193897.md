self-doctrines:/ˈsɛlf ˈdɑːktrɪnz/| n.| 自我教条；个人信条 |Self-doctrines are the personal beliefs and principles that guide an individual's actions and decisions, often rigidly adhered to.  They can be based on religion, philosophy, or personal experiences and may not always align with accepted societal norms.  |例句：His self-doctrines prevented him from accepting any help, even when desperately needed. = 他的个人信条阻止了他接受任何帮助，即使在迫切需要的时候。


例句：He clung to his self-doctrines despite overwhelming evidence to the contrary. = 尽管有压倒性的证据证明相反，他仍然坚持自己的信条。


近义词：personal beliefs, convictions, tenets, principles, creeds


反义词：open-mindedness, flexibility, adaptability


词性:名词
