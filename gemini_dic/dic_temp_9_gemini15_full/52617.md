digestions:/daɪˈdʒɛstʃənz/| n. |消化；领悟|The process of breaking down food in the body into a form that can be absorbed and used as energy.  It also refers to the mental process of understanding and assimilating information.|例句：Good digestion is essential for good health. 良好的消化对健康至关重要。

例句：He struggled with the digestion of such complex information. 他努力消化这些复杂的信息。


近义词：assimilation, absorption, comprehension|反义词：maldigestion, indigestion
