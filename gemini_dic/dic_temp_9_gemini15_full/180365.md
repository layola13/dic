refurbishment:/rɪˈfɜːbɪʃmənt/ | n. | 翻新；修理；整修 | The act of repairing and improving something, especially a building or a piece of equipment, so that it is in good condition again.  | The refurbishment of the old building cost a fortune. 老建筑的翻新花费了一大笔钱。| renovation, restoration, overhaul | demolition, destruction


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The hotel is undergoing refurbishment.  酒店正在进行翻修。
Refurbishment typically takes several months. 翻新通常需要几个月的时间。


一般过去时:
The museum underwent a complete refurbishment last year. 博物馆去年进行了全面翻修。
They completed the refurbishment of the kitchen quickly.  他们很快就完成了厨房的翻新。


现在完成时:
The company has completed the refurbishment of its headquarters. 公司已经完成了其总部的翻修。
They have just finished the refurbishment of their old house. 他们刚刚完成了他们老房子的翻修。


过去完成时:
The work had been completed before the grand opening.  在盛大开幕之前，工作已经完成了。
