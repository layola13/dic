relatedness:/rɪˈleɪtɪdnəs/ | n. | 相关性；关联性；亲属关系 | The state or quality of being related; connection or association.  It can refer to the degree of kinship between individuals or the degree of similarity or connection between things. | 例句：The study explored the relatedness between diet and health.  这项研究探讨了饮食和健康之间的相关性。

The high degree of genetic relatedness among the siblings was evident. 兄弟姐妹之间高度的基因相关性是显而易见的。 | 近义词：connection, association, kinship, affinity, correlation | 反义词：unrelatedness, dissimilarity, independence
