precombats:/ˌpriːˈkɒmbæts/ | n. | 预先战斗；预防性战斗 | Actions taken in advance to prevent or mitigate a future conflict.  Precombats involve preparing for potential threats before they escalate into full-scale conflict.  This can include diplomatic efforts, military exercises, or economic sanctions. | Example: The precombats between the two nations involved a series of escalating diplomatic exchanges.  预先战斗，两个国家之间的预先冲突包括一系列不断升级的外交交流。|近义词：prophylactic measures, preventative actions, preemptive strikes | 反义词：post-conflict actions, reactive measures | 名词
