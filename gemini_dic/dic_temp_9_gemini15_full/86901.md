hemitone:/ˈhɛmɪtoʊn/| n. |半音|A musical interval equal to half a tone; the smallest interval in Western music.  It's the difference between two adjacent notes on a piano that are not separated by a whole tone.  |Example: The guitarist used a hemitone bend to create a bluesy effect. 吉他手使用了半音弯音来创造布鲁斯效果。|近义词：half step|反义词：whole tone, whole step|名词
