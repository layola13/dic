It often involves a contrast between two seemingly irreconcilable entities, emphasizing their differences and independent existence.  The term is less common than its synonyms, and its usage is primarily confined to philosophical and theological discussions. | Example: The debate over free will versus determinism is a classic example of diorism in philosophy.  辩论自由意志与决定论是哲学中二元论的经典例子。 | 近义词: dualism, dichotomy, antithesis | 反义词: monism, unity, synthesis | 名词
