penchute:/ˈpɛntʃuːt/| n.| 降落伞 |A parachute designed for use with a specific type of equipment or in a particular situation.  It often implies a smaller or specialized parachute. |例句：The small penchute gently lowered the scientific equipment to the ground. 小降落伞轻轻地将科学设备降落到地面。The pilot deployed the penchute as a backup in case of main parachute failure.  飞行员在主降落伞发生故障的情况下部署了备用小降落伞。|近义词：parachute, drogue chute |反义词:无|名词
