mossbanker:/ˈmɒsbæŋkə(r)/| n. |苔藓银行家；（指从事非法金融活动的人）|A mossbanker is a slang term, primarily used in the context of illicit financial activities, particularly in the context of  money laundering or other illegal financial dealings. The term evokes an image of someone hiding money or assets in obscure or hidden places, similar to moss growing unnoticed in a bank.  It lacks a formal definition and its use is largely confined to informal settings or investigative journalism related to financial crime.|Example: The authorities suspected the seemingly legitimate businessman was a mossbanker, secretly funneling money through offshore accounts.|近义词：money launderer, racketeer, embezzler|反义词：banker (legitimate), philanthropist|名词
