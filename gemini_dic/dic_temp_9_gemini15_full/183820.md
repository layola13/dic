reseating: /ˌriːˈsiːtɪŋ/ | v. |重新安排座位；重新就座 |The act of arranging seats again, or the act of sitting down again.  It can refer to rearranging furniture or people in seats. |例句：The theater manager oversaw the reseating of the audience after the intermission. 剧院经理在中场休息后监督观众重新安排座位。

例句：After the fire alarm, everyone needed reseating.  火警之后，每个人都需要重新就座。


近义词：rearrangement, repositioning, reshuffling


反义词：N/A (no direct opposite, context-dependent)


词性:动词
