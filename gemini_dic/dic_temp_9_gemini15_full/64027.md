epithalamium:/ˌɛpɪθəˈleɪmiəm/| n. |结婚颂歌；婚礼祝歌| A song or poem celebrating a marriage.  It typically expresses joy and good wishes for the couple's future. |Example: The bride's father commissioned an epithalamium to be sung at the wedding reception.  新娘的父亲委托在婚礼招待会上演唱一首结婚颂歌。|近义词：wedding song, nuptial hymn, bridal ode|反义词：None specifically, but perhaps a dirge or lament if contrasting the joyous nature.
