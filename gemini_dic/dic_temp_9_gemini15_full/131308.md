noncertitude:/ˌnɒnsɜːtʃuːdɪti/| n. |不确定性；不确定的事物 | The lack of certainty; doubt; uncertainty.  Noncertitude is a state of being unsure or uncertain about something. It implies a lack of definitive knowledge or conviction. | Example: The noncertitude of the future weighed heavily on her mind.  未来的不确定性让她感到沉重。  The noncertitude surrounding the election results led to widespread anxiety.  选举结果的不确定性导致了广泛的焦虑。 |近义词：uncertainty, doubt, precariousness, dubiety |反义词：certainty, assurance, confidence
