quinoline:/ˈkwɪnəliːn/| n. |喹啉|A heterocyclic aromatic organic compound with the formula C9H7N.  It is a colorless oily liquid with a strong, unpleasant odor.  Quinoline is a basic compound and forms salts with acids. It is found in coal tar and is used in the manufacture of dyes, pharmaceuticals, and other chemicals.|Quinoline is a key component in the synthesis of many drugs and other compounds.  The synthesis of quinoline derivatives has been a major focus of medicinal chemistry for decades.|近义词：暂无直接近义词 |反义词：暂无直接反义词
