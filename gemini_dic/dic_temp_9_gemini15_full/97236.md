inflectional: /ˌɪnflɛkʃənəl/ | adj. | 词尾变化的；屈折的 | Relating to or characterized by inflection; involving the modification of a word to express different grammatical categories such as tense, number, gender, case, or mood.  Inflectional changes typically involve adding prefixes or suffixes to a word's stem. |  The inflectional endings of verbs show tense.  词尾变化的动词结尾表示时态。


The grammatical changes that are made to words are called inflections.  对单词进行的语法变化称为屈折变化。


近义词：flexional, grammatical


反义词：derivational (词缀的)


词性:形容词
