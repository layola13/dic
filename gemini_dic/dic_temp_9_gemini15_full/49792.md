demonizes:/ˈdiːmənʌɪzɪz/| vt.|妖魔化；将…描绘成恶魔|To represent (someone or something) as evil or wicked.  The media often demonizes political opponents.|例句：The media often demonizes political opponents.媒体经常妖魔化政治对手。

例句：The campaign demonized its rival with false accusations. 该竞选活动用虚假指控妖魔化了其竞争对手。


近义词：vilify, slander, defame, malign

反义词：glorify, praise, exalt, commend

词性:动词
