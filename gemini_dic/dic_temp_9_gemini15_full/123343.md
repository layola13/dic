monolingual: /ˌmɒnəˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. | 只使用一种语言的；单语的 |  A person who speaks only one language.  Someone who is proficient in only one language. | 例句：He is monolingual, speaking only English. 他只会说英语，是单语者。

例句：She grew up in a monolingual environment and struggled to learn a second language later in life.  她在单一语言环境中长大，后来学习第二语言时很费力。


近义词：unilingual


反义词：bilingual, multilingual, polyglot


词性: adjective
