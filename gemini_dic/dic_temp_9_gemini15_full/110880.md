lionizes:/ˈlaɪənaɪzɪz/| vt.| 崇拜；把…当作名人；吹捧 | To treat (someone) as a celebrity. To give great praise and admiration to (someone).|

例句：The town lionized the local hero after his Olympic victory.  =  这个小镇在当地英雄获得奥运胜利后，将他奉为名人。

例句：The media lionized the young actress, making her a household name. = 媒体将这位年轻女演员捧为明星，使她家喻户晓。


近义词：celebrate, glorify, idolize, extol

反义词：ignore, neglect, denigrate, criticize

过去完成时:
