hermaphroditical:/ˌhɜːrmæˈfrɒdɪtɪkəl/| adj. |雌雄同体的；两性的|Possessing both male and female reproductive organs.  The term often refers to plants and invertebrates, but can also describe certain rare cases in humans and other animals.| Example:  The snail is hermaphroditical, meaning each individual can produce both eggs and sperm.  这只蜗牛是雌雄同体的，这意味着每个个体都能产生卵子和精子。|近义词: androgynous, bisexual |反义词: unisexual, dioecious
