plasters:/ˈplɑːstərz/ | n. |膏药；石膏；灰泥 |A plaster is a piece of material, typically fabric or plastic coated with a medicated substance, that is applied to the skin to treat a minor injury or ailment.  It can also refer to a layer of plaster used in building or construction. |例句：He put a plaster on his scraped knee. 他在擦伤的膝盖上贴了块膏药。

例句：The walls were covered with a thick layer of plaster. 墙壁上覆盖着一层厚厚的灰泥。


近义词：bandage, dressing, adhesive bandage |反义词：None specifically, but depending on context, words implying removal or absence of a plaster could be considered antonyms. |词性:名词
