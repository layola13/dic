intermediated:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdieɪtɪd/| v. |居中调解；做中间人；|to act as an intermediary between people or groups; to be involved in a process as an intermediate step or agent.  |例句：The bank intermediated the loan between the borrower and the lender. = 银行在借款人和贷款人之间居中调解贷款。|例句：The chemical reaction intermediated the formation of the final product. = 化学反应促成了最终产物的形成。|近义词：mediate, arbitrate, facilitate |反义词：directly involved


USAGE->

The bank intermediates loans between borrowers and lenders. = 银行在借款人和贷款人之间进行贷款中介。
He intermediates disputes between neighbors. = 他在邻居之间调解纠纷。


一般过去时:
The government intermediated the peace talks. = 政府促成了和谈。
She intermediated a deal between two companies. = 她促成了两家公司之间的交易。
