ignivomous:/ɪɡˈnɪvəməs/| adj.|喷火的，吐火的|Belching or discharging fire; literally, "fire-vomiting."  The word is derived from the Latin roots "igni" (fire) and "vomous" (vomiting, spewing).|例句：The ignivomous dragon incinerated the village.  喷火的巨龙烧毁了村庄。 The volcano's ignivomous eruption sent ash miles into the sky. 火山的喷火喷发将火山灰送入数英里高的天空。|近义词：fiery, flaming, fiery-breathed|反义词：fireless, non-fiery
