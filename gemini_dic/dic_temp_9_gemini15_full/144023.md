outreigned:/ˌaʊtˈreɪnd/| v.|超过...的统治时期；在位时间超过；|To rule or reign for a longer period than (someone else).  This word is rarely used and often appears in historical contexts.|例句：King Henry VIII outreigned his father, Henry VII.|近义词：outlast, surpass, exceed|反义词：be out-reigned by

一般现在时:
The king outreigns his predecessor. = 国王在位时间超过了他的前任。
This dynasty outreigns all others in this region. = 这个王朝的统治时间超过了该地区所有其他王朝。


一般过去时:
Queen Victoria outreigned many other European monarchs. = 维多利亚女王的统治时间超过了许多其他欧洲君主。
The Roman Empire outreigned many smaller kingdoms. = 罗马帝国的统治时间超过了许多较小的王国。


现在完成时:
This emperor has outreigned all his ancestors. = 这位皇帝的统治时间超过了所有他的祖先。
The current government has outreigned the previous one by several years. = 现任政府的执政时间比前任政府长了好几年。


过去完成时:
By the time he died, the king had outreigned his father by a decade. = 他死时，国王的统治时间比他父亲长了十年。
Before the revolution, the dynasty had outreigned its rivals for centuries. = 在革命之前，这个王朝的统治时间比它的竞争对手长了几个世纪。


将来时:
It is predicted that this president will outreign his predecessor. = 预计这位总统的执政时间将超过他的前任。
Historians believe this kingdom will outreign its neighbours for many years to come. = 历史学家认为这个王国将在未来许多年里统治时间超过它的邻国。


虚拟语气:
If he had been a stronger leader, he might have outreigned his rivals. = 如果他是一位更强大的领导人，他本可以统治时间超过他的竞争对手。
If the queen had lived longer, she would have outreigned her grandmother. = 如果女王活得更久，她的统治时间就会超过她的祖母。


被动语态:
His reign was outreigned by his son. = 他的统治时间被他的儿子超过了。
The previous dynasty was outreigned by the current one. = 前朝的统治时间被现朝超过了。


疑问句:
Did the emperor outreign his father? = 皇帝的统治时间超过了他的父亲吗？
Will this government outreign its predecessor? = 现任政府的执政时间会超过前任政府吗？


否定句:
The short-lived emperor did not outreign his predecessor. = 短命皇帝的统治时间没有超过他的前任。
The former king did not outreign his grandfather. = 前任国王的统治时间没有超过他的祖父。
