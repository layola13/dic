recruiting:/rɪˈkruːtɪŋ/ | n. | 招募；招聘 | The process of attracting, selecting, and appointing suitable candidates for job vacancies.  It involves various stages, from advertising the position to onboarding the new employee. | Example:  The company's recruiting process is very thorough. 这家公司的招聘流程非常严格。  |近义词：enlistment, hiring |反义词：firing, dismissal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is recruiting for a new marketing manager. = 公司正在招聘一位新的市场经理。
We are recruiting new graduates this year. = 我们今年正在招聘应届毕业生。

一般过去时:
They recruited several new employees last month. = 他们上个月招聘了几名新员工。
The army recruited many young men during the war. = 战争期间，军队招募了许多年轻男子。

现在完成时:
They have recruited over 100 new staff this year. = 他们今年已经招聘了100多名新员工。
The firm has been recruiting aggressively to expand its operations. = 该公司一直在积极招聘以扩大其业务。

过去完成时:
They had recruited all the necessary staff before the project began. = 项目开始前，他们已经招聘了所有必要的员工。
The company had already recruited a replacement before the employee resigned. = 在员工辞职之前，公司已经招聘了替补人员。

将来时:
We will be recruiting for several positions next quarter. = 我们下个季度将招聘几个职位。
They will recruit more staff if the business expands. = 如果业务扩张，他们将招聘更多员工。

虚拟语气:
If they recruited more experienced staff, the project would be more successful. = 如果他们招募更有经验的员工，项目会更成功。
I wish they had recruited more people for the project. = 我希望他们为该项目招募更多人。

被动语态:
Many candidates were recruited for the interview process. = 许多候选人被招募参加面试流程。
New employees are being recruited constantly. = 新员工正在不断被招聘。

疑问句:
Are you recruiting for any new positions? = 你在招聘任何新的职位吗？
How many people are they recruiting? = 他们正在招聘多少人？

否定句:
The company isn't recruiting at the moment. = 公司目前没有招聘。
We didn't recruit anyone last week. = 我们上周没有招聘任何人。
