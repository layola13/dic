educationist:/ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃənɪst/| n. |教育家，教育工作者 |A person who is professionally engaged in education; an expert in education.  An educationist often works in academia, policy-making, or educational administration, specializing in pedagogy, curriculum development, or educational research. |例句：Many leading educationists advocate for more hands-on learning in schools.  许多主要的教育家都提倡在学校进行更多实践学习。

例句：The educationist presented a compelling case for reforming the national curriculum.这位教育家提出了一个令人信服的改革国家课程的理由。


近义词：educator, pedagogue, teacher (in a broad sense) |反义词：None directly opposite, but "ignorant person" could be considered a contextual antonym. |词性:名词
