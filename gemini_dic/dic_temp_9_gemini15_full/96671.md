industry:/ˈɪndəstri/ | n. |工业；产业；行业 |The manufacturing industry is booming. 制造业正在蓬勃发展。|近义词：trade, commerce, business |反义词：agriculture, farming

英文解析：
Industry refers to the production of goods or services within an economy.  It encompasses a wide range of activities, from manufacturing and mining to agriculture and technology.  It can also refer to a specific sector of the economy, such as the automotive industry or the entertainment industry.  Additionally, it can describe the diligent and energetic application of oneself to a task or field of activity.

例句：
The film industry is highly competitive. 电影业竞争非常激烈。

The steel industry is facing challenges. 钢铁行业面临挑战。


词源:
From Old French *industrie, from Latin industria, from industrius "skillful, diligent," from industria "diligence, activity," from indu- "in" + -st- (possibly connected to stare "to stand") + -ia "-ness."  The modern sense of "industrial production" developed in the 18th century.
