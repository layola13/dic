outsinging: /ˌaʊtˈsɪŋɪŋ/ | v. |胜过（歌唱）；比…唱得好 |  The act of singing better than someone else; surpassing someone in singing ability.  |例句：The young soprano outsang all her competitors.  年轻的女高音歌唱家胜过了她所有的竞争对手。
例句：Her powerful voice outsang the entire choir. 她强大的嗓音盖过了整个合唱团。|近义词：outperform, surpass, excel |反义词：be outsung by, be inferior to |
