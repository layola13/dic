cognateness:/ˈkɒɡnɪtnəs/ /ˈkɑːɡnɪtnəs/ | n. |同源性；同族关系；同源词|The state or fact of being related by descent from a common ancestor; the relationship between words having a common etymological origin.  This refers to both linguistic and biological relationships. In linguistics, it describes words with shared ancestry, often exhibiting similar forms and meanings across different languages. |例句：The cognateness of "brother" in English and "bruder" in German is clear.  英语中的“brother”和德语中的“bruder”的同源性很明显。

The cognateness between these two species suggests a recent common ancestor. 这两个物种之间的同源性表明它们最近有共同的祖先。|近义词：kinship, relationship, affinity, common origin |反义词： unrelatedness, divergence
