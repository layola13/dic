leeches:/ˈliːtʃɪz/ | n. | 水蛭；寄生虫；榨取他人钱财的人 |  A leech is a bloodsucking worm that lives in fresh water.  They are also used figuratively to describe someone who exploits others for personal gain. | 例句：Medical leeches were once used to treat illness.  医学上曾用水蛭治疗疾病。

The businessman was accused of being a leech on society.  那个商人被指控是社会的寄生虫。| 近义词：bloodsucker, parasite, exploiter | 反义词：benefactor, helper
