interradial: /ˌɪntərˈreɪdiəl/ | adj. |辐射状的，径向间的|Lying between radii; situated between rays or radiating lines. The interradial canals connect the radial canals in the water vascular system of echinoderms. 径向间管连接棘皮动物水管系统的辐射管。

近义词：intersectoral, intermediate (in some contexts) | 反义词：radial (in the sense of directly along a radius) | 词性：形容词
