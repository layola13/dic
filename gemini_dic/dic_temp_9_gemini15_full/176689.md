react:/riˈækt/| vi. vt. n.|反应，回应，起作用|to act in response to something; to show a response to something;  a response to something.|例句：He reacted angrily to the news. 他对这个消息愤怒地做出了反应。  The chemical reacted violently with water.  这种化学物质与水发生剧烈反应。|近义词：respond, answer, reply|反义词：ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
How does she react to criticism? = 她如何应对批评？
The chemical reacts with water to produce heat. = 这种化学物质与水反应产生热量。

一般过去时:
He reacted quickly to the emergency. = 他对紧急情况反应迅速。
She reacted badly to the medication. = 她对药物反应很差。

现在完成时:
How have you reacted to the changes? = 你对这些变化作何反应？
The company has reacted swiftly to the market demand. = 公司迅速应对市场需求。

过去完成时:
I had already reacted before I understood the situation. = 在我理解情况之前，我已经做出了反应。
They had reacted negatively to my proposal. = 他们对我的提议反应消极。

将来时:
How will the market react to this new product? = 市场将如何应对这款新产品？
He will react violently if you provoke him. = 如果你激怒他，他会激烈地反抗。

虚拟语气:
If he reacted differently, the outcome might have been better. = 如果他的反应不同，结果可能会更好。
I wish I had reacted more calmly. = 我希望我当时能更冷静地应对。

被动语态:
The proposal was reacted to negatively by the board. = 该提案受到了董事会的负面回应。
His actions were reacted to with surprise. = 他的行为引起了人们的惊讶。

疑问句:
How did you react to the news? = 你对这个消息有什么反应？
How will they react to our decision? = 他们将如何回应我们的决定？

否定句:
He didn't react at all to my comments. = 他对我的评论根本没有反应。
She didn't react positively to the suggestion. = 她对这个建议的反应并不积极。


PHRASE->
react to = 对…做出反应
react against = 反抗，反对
react with = 与…发生反应
overreact = 过度反应
chain reaction = 连锁反应
