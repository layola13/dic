croisade:/kruˈseɪd/| n.|十字军东征；宗教战争；运动；斗争|A crusade is a religious war, especially one of the medieval expeditions to recover the Holy Land from the Muslims.  It can also refer to a vigorous campaign for a cause. |例句：The Children's Crusade was a tragic event.  孩子们东征是一场悲剧性的事件。 The organization launched a crusade against poverty. 这个组织发起了一场反对贫困的运动。|近义词：campaign, movement, struggle, fight|反义词：peace, truce, quiet


USAGE->

The church supports many crusades. = 教会支持许多运动。
He leads a crusade against injustice. = 他领导着一场反对不公正的运动。
