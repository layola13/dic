facade: /fəˈsɑːd/ | /fəˈsæd/ | n. | 外观；表面；立面 | The exterior face of a building; a superficial appearance that disguises reality.  | The building's facade was beautifully restored. 这座建筑物的正面得到了美丽的修复。 The politician maintained a facade of calm despite the crisis. 尽管发生了危机，这位政治家仍然保持着平静的外表。 | frontage, front, exterior | reality, truth, substance | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The facade hides the building's decay. = 外观掩盖了建筑物的腐朽。
This facade presents a false image of success. = 这个外观呈现出虚假的成功形象。


一般过去时:
The old facade was replaced with a modern one. = 旧的立面被现代的立面取代了。
His cheerful facade hid his deep sadness. = 他快乐的外表掩盖了他深深的悲伤。


现在完成时:
They have restored the facade to its former glory. = 他们已将立面恢复到昔日的辉煌。
The company has maintained a facade of profitability despite losses. = 尽管亏损，该公司仍然保持着盈利的外表。


过去完成时:
Before the renovation, the facade had been severely damaged. = 翻新之前，立面已经严重损坏。
He had carefully constructed a facade of indifference. = 他精心营造了一种冷漠的外表。


将来时:
The new facade will be completed next month. = 新的立面将于下个月完工。
They will maintain a facade of unity despite their disagreements. = 尽管存在分歧，他们仍将保持团结的外表。


虚拟语气:
If the facade were repaired, the building would look much better. = 如果立面得到修复，建筑物看起来会好得多。
I wish the facade didn't hide such a depressing reality. = 我希望这个外观不会隐藏如此令人沮丧的现实。


被动语态:
The facade is being cleaned. = 立面正在清洗。
The facade was damaged by the storm. = 立面被暴风雨损坏。


疑问句:

What material is the facade made of? = 立面是用什么材料制成的？


否定句:

The facade didn't reflect the building's true age. = 外观没有反映出建筑物的真实年龄。
