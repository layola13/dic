indicators: /ˈɪndɪkeɪtərz/ | n. | 指标，标志，指示器 | Signs or pieces of information that indicate a situation or trend.  They are used to measure or monitor something. |


例句：

Economic indicators suggest a slowdown in growth.  经济指标显示经济增长正在放缓。

The rising temperature is an indicator of global warming.  气温上升是全球变暖的标志。


近义词：signs, signals, measures, symptoms, gauges, markers

反义词：None directly opposite, but antonyms could relate to the opposite of what the indicator is measuring (e.g., if the indicator is "high inflation," the antonym would relate to "low inflation").


词性：名词
