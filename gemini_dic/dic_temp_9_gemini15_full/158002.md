planning:/ˈplænɪŋ/| n.|计划，规划|The act or process of making or carrying out plans; the plans themselves.|例句：The planning for the new bridge is underway. 新桥的规划正在进行中。|近义词：preparation, scheming, strategizing|反义词：improvisation, spontaneity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are planning a trip to Europe next year. = 我们正计划明年去欧洲旅行。
They plan to open a new restaurant. = 他们计划开一家新餐馆。

一般过去时:
She planned a surprise party for her friend. = 她为她的朋友策划了一个惊喜派对。
They planned their wedding meticulously. = 他们精心策划了他们的婚礼。

现在完成时:
We have planned everything for the conference. = 我们已经为会议计划好了一切。
They have been planning this project for months. = 他们已经策划这个项目几个月了。

过去完成时:
They had planned to go to the beach, but it rained. = 他们原计划去海滩，但下雨了。
We had planned a picnic, but the weather was bad.  = 我们原计划野餐，但天气不好。

将来时:
We will be planning our vacation soon. = 我们很快就会开始计划我们的假期。
They will plan the next steps after the meeting. = 会议结束后，他们将计划下一步行动。

虚拟语气:
If we had planned better, the event would have been more successful. = 如果我们计划得更好，活动就会更成功。
I wish we had planned this trip earlier. = 我希望我们早点计划这次旅行。

被动语态:
The event was planned by a professional event planner. = 这个活动是由一位专业的活动策划师策划的。
The project is being planned carefully. = 这个项目正在仔细地规划中。

疑问句:
Are you planning anything special for the weekend? = 你周末有什么特别的计划吗？
What are they planning to do next? = 他们接下来计划做什么？

否定句:
We are not planning to go on vacation this year. = 我们今年不打算去度假。
They didn't plan ahead, so they were unprepared. = 他们没有提前计划，所以他们没有准备。
