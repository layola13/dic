rhizanthous:/ˌraɪˈzænθəs/| adj. |根生的；根出花的 |Producing flowers from the root or underground stem.  The term refers to plants that develop flowers directly from the root or rhizome, rather than from stems or branches above ground.  |例句：Many orchids are rhizanthous, producing flowers directly from their rhizomes.  许多兰花是根出花的，直接从根茎上开花。

例句：The rhizanthous plant had beautiful blooms emerging directly from the soil. 这株根出花的植物从土壤中直接长出了美丽的花朵。


近义词：root-flowered, radical-flowered


反义词：caulescent (stem-bearing)


词性：形容词
