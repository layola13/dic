plucked:/plʌkt/ | /pʌkt/ | vt. | 摘，采；弹拨；拔（毛）；猛地拉；猛拉；揪；(非正式)偷窃；| to pull or take something, especially something small, quickly and with a slight pulling movement |  She plucked a flower from the vase. 她从花瓶里摘了一朵花。| He plucked a guitar string. 他拨动了一根吉他弦。 |近义词：pick, pull, snatch | 反义词：plant, sow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She plucks the feathers from the chicken. = 她拔掉鸡身上的羽毛。
He plucks the strings of his guitar. = 他弹奏吉他的琴弦。

一般过去时:
I plucked a rose from the garden. = 我从花园里摘了一朵玫瑰。
The bird plucked at the seeds. = 鸟啄食着种子。


现在完成时:
She has plucked all the ripe tomatoes. = 她已经摘完了所有成熟的西红柿。
He has plucked many songs from his heart. = 他已经从心底创作了许多歌曲。


过去完成时:
They had plucked the fruit before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经摘完了水果。
She had plucked up her courage before speaking. = 她鼓起勇气后才开始说话。


将来时:
I will pluck the weeds from the garden tomorrow. = 我明天将把花园里的杂草拔掉。
He will pluck a song on his guitar. = 他将弹奏一首歌曲。


虚拟语气:
If I plucked that flower, she would be angry. = 如果我摘了那朵花，她会生气的。
I wish I had plucked more berries. = 我真希望我摘了更多的浆果。


被动语态:
The feathers were plucked from the goose. = 羽毛是从鹅身上拔下来的。
The fruit will be plucked tomorrow. = 水果将在明天采摘。


疑问句:
Did you pluck the flowers? = 你摘了花吗？
Has she plucked the chicken? = 她拔过鸡毛了吗？


否定句:
He didn't pluck the fruit. = 他没有摘水果。
She hasn't plucked the guitar yet. = 她还没有弹吉他。
