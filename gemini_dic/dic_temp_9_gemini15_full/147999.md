overrighteously:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪtʃəsli/ | adj. | 伪善地；自以为义地 |  Acting in a self-righteously superior manner; showing excessive moral superiority.  It implies a display of righteousness that is excessive, hypocritical, or not genuinely felt. |例句：He overrighteously condemned the actions of others, despite his own questionable behavior. 他自以为是地谴责别人的行为，尽管他自己也有值得怀疑的行为。

He lectured us overrighteously on the importance of saving money, even though he was deeply in debt. 他自以为是地教训我们储蓄的重要性，即使他自己负债累累。|近义词：self-righteously, sanctimoniously, hypocritically, holily |反义词： humbly, modestly, unassumingly
