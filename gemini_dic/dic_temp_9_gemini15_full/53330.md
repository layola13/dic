disaccustom:/ˌdɪsəˈkʌstəm/| vt.|使不习惯，使脱习惯|To make someone less accustomed to something.|He quickly disaccustomed himself to the luxuries of city life. 他很快就不习惯城市生活的奢华了。|accustom, habituate|accustom, habituate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They disaccustom themselves to the noise gradually. = 他们逐渐地使自己不习惯噪音。
The company disaccustoms its employees to working overtime. = 公司让员工逐渐不习惯加班。


一般过去时:
She disaccustomed herself to the cold climate after moving to the south. = 她搬到南方后就不再习惯寒冷的气候了。
The long vacation disaccustomed him to the routine of school. = 长假使他不习惯学校的常规作息了。


现在完成时:
He has disaccustomed himself to sleeping late after starting a new job. = 他自从找到新工作后就不再习惯睡懒觉了。
We have disaccustomed ourselves to the quiet life in the countryside. = 我们已经不习惯乡村的宁静生活了。


过去完成时:
They had disaccustomed themselves to the heat before they moved to a colder region. = 他们在搬到较冷的地区之前已经不再习惯炎热的天气了。
She had disaccustomed herself to regular exercise before the injury. = 在受伤之前，她已经不习惯规律的锻炼了。


将来时:
He will disaccustom himself to smoking gradually. = 他将逐渐戒烟。
The new schedule will disaccustom the students to their old routine. = 新的日程安排将使学生们不习惯以前的常规。


虚拟语气:
If I were to move to a new city, I would disaccustom myself to my old habits. = 如果我要搬到一个新的城市，我会让自己改掉旧习惯。
I wish I had disaccustomed myself to procrastination earlier. = 我希望我早点改掉拖延的坏习惯。


被动语态:
He was disaccustomed to the noise by the gradual introduction of soundproofing. = 通过逐渐引入隔音措施，他逐渐适应了安静的环境。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常主动语态更常用)
The employees were slowly disaccustomed to the long working hours. = 员工们慢慢地不再习惯长时间工作。


疑问句:
How long will it take to disaccustom myself to this? = 我要多久才能改掉这个习惯？
Did he disaccustom himself to spicy food? = 他戒掉辛辣食物了吗？


否定句:
He hasn't disaccustomed himself to coffee yet. = 他还没戒掉咖啡。
They didn't disaccustom themselves to the late nights easily. = 他们并没有轻易地改掉熬夜的习惯。


PHRASE->
disaccustom oneself to...  = 使自己不再习惯于……
disaccustom children to... = 使孩子们不再习惯于……
disaccustom someone to... = 使某人不习惯于……
gradually disaccustom = 逐渐地使…不习惯
quickly disaccustom = 快速地使…不习惯
