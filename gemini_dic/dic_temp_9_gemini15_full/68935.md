fatals:/ˈfeɪtəlz/| adj.| 致命的，毁灭性的 |  causing death; deadly.  The accident resulted in several fatal injuries.  |  例句：The fatal blow ended the fight.  致命的一击结束了战斗。

例句：The storm caused fatal damage to the crops.  暴风雨对庄稼造成了毁灭性的破坏。


近义词：lethal, mortal, deadly | 反义词：harmless, innocuous, safe | 词性：形容词


词源:  The word "fatal" comes from the Latin word "fatalis," meaning "fateful" or "destined."  This, in turn, is derived from the Latin word "fatum," meaning "fate" or "destiny."  The connection lies in the idea that something fatal is determined by fate or unavoidable, often leading to death or destruction.
