overhasten:/ˌoʊvərˈheɪsn/| vt.|轻率从事，仓促行事|To act too quickly or hastily; to rush into something without sufficient consideration.  |例句：He overhastened the project, leading to several costly mistakes. 他仓促行事，导致一些代价高昂的错误。|例句：Don't overhasten your decision; take your time and think it through. 不要仓促做决定；花时间好好想想。|近义词：rush, hurry, precipitate, accelerate|反义词：delay, procrastinate, deliberate
