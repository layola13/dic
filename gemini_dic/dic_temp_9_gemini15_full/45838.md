customizing:/ˈkʌstəmaɪzɪŋ/| v.|定制，个性化|The act of modifying something to meet a particular need or preference.  It involves making something unique or tailored to a specific user or purpose.|例句：The company offers customizing options for its software. 这家公司为其软件提供定制选项。|例句：She's customizing her wedding dress. 她在定制她的婚纱。|近义词：personalizing, tailoring, adapting, modifying|反义词：standardizing, generalizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are customizing the car. = 他们正在定制这辆车。
We customize our products to meet customer needs. = 我们定制产品以满足客户需求。

一般过去时:
He customized his motorcycle. = 他定制了他的摩托车。
She customized her website last year. = 她去年定制了她的网站。

现在完成时:
I have customized my phone settings. = 我已经定制了我的手机设置。
They have customized their marketing strategy. = 他们已经定制了他们的营销策略。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, they had customized the slides. = 在演示之前，他们已经定制了幻灯片。
She had customized her profile before applying for the job. = 在申请工作之前，她已经定制了她的个人资料。

将来时:

They will customize their approach based on the feedback. = 他们将根据反馈定制他们的方法。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
