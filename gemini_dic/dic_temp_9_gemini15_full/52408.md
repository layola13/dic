diddy:/ˈdɪdi/| n. |小曲；小家伙；(非正式)歌曲；|A short, simple song, especially one for children.  Also, an informal term of endearment or a diminutive. |例句：The children sang a little diddy about a frog. 孩子们唱了一首关于青蛙的小曲。|例句：He's a little diddy, isn't he? 他是个小家伙，不是吗？|近义词：tune, song, ditty, youngster, kid |反义词：giant, adult
