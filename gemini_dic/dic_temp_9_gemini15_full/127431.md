neatnesses:/ˈniːtnəsɪz/ | n. |整洁；整齐；干净 |The neatnesses of her apartment were remarkable.  她的公寓整洁得令人惊叹。|近义词：tidiness, orderliness, cleanliness |反义词：messiness, disorder, sloppiness

英文解析:The state or quality of being neat; tidiness.  指整洁的状态或品质；整齐。


例句:

The neatnesses of her handwriting were praised by her teacher.  她老师称赞她字迹工整。

The contrasting neatnesses of the two gardens highlighted the difference in their owners’ personalities.  两个花园的整洁程度形成对比，突出了主人性格的差异。


词源:

From neat + -ness.  源自neat（整洁的）+ -ness（名词后缀）。  Neat itself ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *nautaz, meaning "neat, trim, tidy".


用法说明:

neatnesses 主要用作名词，通常是复数形式，表示多种整洁的状态或情况，强调的是多种整洁并存的情况，而非单一的整洁。  它不如 tidiness 使用广泛。
