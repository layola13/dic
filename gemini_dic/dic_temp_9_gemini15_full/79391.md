glides:/ɡlaɪdz/| v.|滑行，滑动，悄悄地移动|to move smoothly and quietly, as if without effort.|The swan glided gracefully across the lake. 天鹅优雅地滑过湖面。The cat glided silently through the grass. 猫悄悄地穿过草地。|slides, slips, sails|jolts, bumps|动词


USAGE->

He glides on ice with great skill. 他熟练地滑冰。
