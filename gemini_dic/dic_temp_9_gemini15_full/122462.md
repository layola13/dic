mobilize:/ˈmoʊbəlaɪz/| vt. |动员；调动；使...动员起来|To prepare and organize (something, such as troops or resources) for action or use.  |例句：The government mobilized the army to quell the rebellion. 政府动员军队平息叛乱。

例句：The company mobilized its resources to launch the new product. 公司动员资源推出新产品。


近义词：muster, rally, activate, marshal

反义词：demobilize, disband
