phytologist:/faɪˈtɒlədʒɪst/|/ˈfaɪtɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |植物学家|A phytologist is a scientist who studies plants, including their physiology, genetics, ecology, and classification.  Phytologists conduct research, teach, and work in various settings such as universities, government agencies, and private companies.|Example: The phytologist carefully examined the rare orchid specimen.|近义词：botanist|反义词：无
