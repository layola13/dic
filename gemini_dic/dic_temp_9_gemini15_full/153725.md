pepperbox:/ˈpepərbɒks/ | n. |胡椒盒；多管枪；喋喋不休的人|A pepperbox is a container for pepper, typically with a perforated top for sprinkling.  It can also refer to a type of revolver with multiple barrels arranged in a circle, or to a person who talks incessantly and rapidly. |例句：She shook the pepperbox vigorously, adding a generous amount of seasoning to her stew. 她用力摇晃胡椒盒，往炖菜里加了大量的调料。

The old west gunslinger drew his pepperbox revolver, ready for a shootout.  西部的老枪手拔出了他的多管左轮手枪，准备决斗。


近义词：pepper shaker (for the container), revolver (for the firearm), chatterbox (for the person)
反义词：silent (for the person)
