posthospital:/ˌpoʊstˈhɑːspɪtəl/| adj.|医院后的，出院后的|Relating to the period after a person has left hospital.  This term often refers to the period of recovery and rehabilitation following a hospital stay.|例句：Posthospital care is crucial for a patient's full recovery. 出院后的护理对于病人的完全康复至关重要。
例句：The posthospital appointment is scheduled for next week.  出院后的复诊预约在下周。|近义词：postdischarge, aftercare|反义词：prehospital, inpatient
