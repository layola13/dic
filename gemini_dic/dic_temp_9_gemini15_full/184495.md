restarting:/ˌriːˈstɑːrtɪŋ/| v. |重新启动；重新开始 |The act of starting something again after it has been stopped.  It often implies overcoming a disruption or failure. |例句：The computer is restarting after a system failure.  电脑在系统故障后正在重新启动。

例句：After a brief interruption, the meeting is restarting.  短暂中断后，会议正在重新开始。


近义词：rebooting, recommencing, resuming, beginning again

反义词：stopping, ceasing, halting, ending


词性：动词(现在分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is restarting his computer. = 他正在重新启动他的电脑。
They are restarting the project. = 他们正在重启项目。


一般过去时:
I restarted my car after it stalled. = 我的车熄火后，我重新启动了它。
She restarted the washing machine. = 她重新启动了洗衣机。


现在完成时:
We have restarted the negotiations. = 我们已经重启了谈判。
They have restarted the engine several times. = 他们已经多次重新启动发动机。


过去完成时:
He had restarted the computer before I arrived. = 我到达之前，他已经重新启动了电脑。
She had restarted the experiment after the initial failure. = 初次失败后，她已经重新开始了实验。


将来时:
I will restart the program tomorrow. = 我明天将重新启动程序。
They will restart the machine after maintenance. = 维护后，他们将重新启动机器。


虚拟语气:
If the system crashed, we would restart it. = 如果系统崩溃，我们将重新启动它。
I wish I had restarted the download earlier. = 我希望我早点重新启动下载。


被动语态:
The server was restarted remotely. = 服务器被远程重新启动了。
The engine is being restarted by the mechanic. = 发动机正在由机械师重新启动。


疑问句:
Are you restarting the application? = 你要重新启动应用程序吗？
Will they be restarting the project soon? = 他们很快会重启项目吗？


否定句:
I didn't restart the computer. = 我没有重新启动电脑。
They haven't restarted the machine yet. = 他们还没有重新启动机器。

restarting a computer = 重新启动电脑
