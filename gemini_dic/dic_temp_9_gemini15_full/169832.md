prosecuting:/ˈprɑːsɪkjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 起诉；检举；进行；从事 |  The act of initiating and pursuing criminal charges against someone.  It involves bringing a case to court and attempting to prove guilt. | 例句：The district attorney is prosecuting the case. = 地方检察官正在起诉此案。

The police are prosecuting the investigation into the robbery. = 警方正在对抢劫案进行调查。


近义词：indicting, prosecuting, pursuing, litigating | 反义词：defending, acquitting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police are prosecuting several suspects. = 警方正在起诉几名嫌疑人。
He is prosecuting his claim for damages. = 他正在起诉索赔损失。


一般过去时:
They prosecuted the case vigorously. = 他们积极地进行了起诉。
The company prosecuted the thief for theft. = 公司起诉了窃贼的盗窃行为。


现在完成时:
The government has prosecuted many criminals this year. = 政府今年已经起诉了许多罪犯。
They have prosecuted the case for three years. = 他们已经起诉这个案子三年了。


过去完成时:
They had prosecuted several similar cases before. = 他们之前已经起诉过几个类似的案件。
The judge had already prosecuted the accused before. = 法官之前已经审判过被告了。(虽然过去完成时不太常用在此处，但可以表达先前的审判经历影响到当前案件)


将来时:
The authorities will prosecute the offenders. = 当局将起诉违法者。
We will prosecute this matter further. = 我们将进一步处理此事。


虚拟语气:
If they had more evidence, they would prosecute. = 如果他们有更多证据，他们就会起诉。
I wish the police would prosecute the bullies. = 我希望警方能起诉那些恶霸。


被动语态:
The case is being prosecuted by the state. = 此案由州政府起诉。
The suspect was prosecuted and found guilty. = 嫌疑人被起诉并被判有罪。


疑问句:
Is the company prosecuting the case? = 公司正在起诉这个案子吗？
Who is prosecuting this case? = 谁在起诉这个案子？


否定句:
The police are not prosecuting the case at this time. = 警方目前没有起诉此案。
They decided not to prosecute because of insufficient evidence. = 由于证据不足，他们决定不起诉。
