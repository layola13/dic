fawningly:/ˈfɔːnɪŋli/ | adv. | 谄媚地；奉承地 |  acting in an excessively flattering or subservient manner.  | Example: He fawned over the celebrity, fawningly asking for an autograph. 他谄媚地讨好那位名流，奉承地请求签名。 |近义词:obsequiously, servilely, sycophantically | 反义词: bluntly, frankly, honestly

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He speaks fawningly to his boss. = 他对老板说话很谄媚。
She always acts fawningly towards the wealthy. = 她总是对有钱人阿谀奉承。


一般过去时:
He spoke fawningly to the manager to get a promotion. = 他对经理谄媚地说话以求升职。
She fawned fawningly on the teacher to get a better grade. = 她为了得到更好的成绩而对老师奉承讨好。


现在完成时:
He has consistently fawned fawningly on his superiors. = 他一直对上司阿谀奉承。
She has fawningly complimented her colleagues to gain favor. = 她为了获得好感而谄媚地恭维她的同事。


过去完成时:
He had fawningly praised the CEO before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就对首席执行官阿谀奉承。
She had fawningly sought the approval of her mentor. = 她之前一直讨好她的导师以求得认可。


将来时:
He will fawningly approach the investor for funding. = 他将谄媚地接近投资者寻求资金。
She will fawningly ask her boss for a raise. = 她将奉承地向老板要求加薪。


虚拟语气:
If he were to speak fawningly, he might get what he wants. = 如果他谄媚地说话，他可能会得到他想要的东西。
I wish she wouldn't speak so fawningly to her clients. = 我希望她不要对她客户那样谄媚地说话。


被动语态:
The proposal was fawningly presented to the board. = 这份提案被谄媚地呈递给了董事会。
His talents were fawningly described in the article. = 他的才能在文章中被谄媚地描述。


疑问句:
Does he always speak fawningly? = 他总是那么谄媚吗？
Is it necessary to act fawningly to succeed? = 为了成功，有必要那样谄媚吗？


否定句:
He didn't speak fawningly; he was direct and honest. = 他没有谄媚地说话；他直接而诚实。
She didn't fawn fawningly; she presented her ideas confidently. = 她没有奉承讨好；她自信地提出了她的想法。
