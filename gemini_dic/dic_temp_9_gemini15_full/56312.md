doghouse:/ˈdɒɡhaʊs/ /ˈdɔːɡhaʊs/| n. |狗屋；困境；窘境|A small house for a dog;  a difficult or unpleasant situation.|例句：The dog sleeps in the doghouse every night.  这只狗每天晚上都睡在狗屋里。|例句：He's in the doghouse with his wife after forgetting her birthday. 他因为忘了妻子的生日而处于困境。|近义词：kennel,  hot water|反义词：paradise, comfort


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dog lives in the doghouse. = 狗住在狗屋里。
He is always in the doghouse with his boss. = 他总是让老板不高兴。


一般过去时:
The dog slept in the doghouse last night. = 狗昨晚睡在狗屋里。
He was in the doghouse after breaking the vase. = 他打破花瓶后就陷入困境。


现在完成时:
The dog has been in the doghouse all week. = 狗整个星期都待在狗屋里。
He has been in the doghouse since he lied to his parents. = 从他对父母撒谎以来，他就一直处于困境。


过去完成时:
The dog had been in the doghouse before we got home. = 我们回家之前，狗就一直在狗屋里。
He had been in the doghouse for weeks before he finally apologized. = 在他最终道歉之前，他已经处于困境好几个星期了。


将来时:
The dog will be in the doghouse if he chews the furniture again. = 如果狗再咬家具，它就会被关进狗屋。
He will be in the doghouse if he misses the deadline again. = 如果他再次错过截止日期，他将陷入困境。


虚拟语气:
If he had finished his work, he wouldn't be in the doghouse. = 如果他完成了他的工作，他就不会陷入困境。
I wish I wasn't in the doghouse with my friend. = 我希望我没有让我朋友生气。


被动语态:
The doghouse was built by my father. = 狗屋是我父亲建的。(This is less common for the figurative meaning)
(Figurative meaning is rarely used in passive voice.)


疑问句:
Is the dog in the doghouse? = 狗在狗屋里吗？
Is he in the doghouse again? = 他又惹麻烦了吗？


否定句:
The dog isn't in the doghouse. = 狗不在狗屋里。
He isn't in the doghouse anymore. = 他不再处于困境了。


PHRASE->

avoid the doghouse = 避免惹麻烦
