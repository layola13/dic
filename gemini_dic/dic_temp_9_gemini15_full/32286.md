chiefly:/ˈtʃiːfli/| adv.| 主要地，大体上 | mainly, primarily, essentially |  It means for the most part or mainly.  It indicates that something is the most important or significant factor. | 例句：He was chiefly concerned with his own welfare. 他主要关心的是自己的福利。

例句：The damage was chiefly caused by the storm.  损害主要由暴风雨造成。


近义词：primarily, mainly, principally, essentially

反义词：secondarily, subordinately, minimally


词性：副词
