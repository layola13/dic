scares:/skɛərz/ | v. | 使害怕；使恐惧 | To cause someone to feel fear or anxiety.  | The loud noise scared the children.  巨大的响声吓坏了孩子们。 | The dark night scared her. 黑暗的夜晚让她害怕。 | frightens, terrifies | calms, soothes | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog scares the cat. = 这只狗吓唬那只猫。
Loud noises scare me. = 响亮的声音吓到我了。

一般过去时:
The storm scared everyone. = 暴风雨吓到了所有人。
The movie scared the kids. = 这部电影吓坏了孩子们。

现在完成时:
Nothing has scared me like that before. = 从来没有什么东西像那样吓到我。
That experience has scared her for life. = 那次经历把她吓坏了，一辈子都忘不了。

过去完成时:
I had never been so scared before. = 我以前从未如此害怕过。
She had already been scared by the previous incident. = 她已经被之前的事件吓到了。

将来时:
The thunder will scare the birds. = 雷声会吓到鸟儿们。
I think the new dog will scare the neighbors. = 我想这只新狗会吓到邻居们。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a ghost, I would be scared. = 如果我看到鬼，我会害怕。
I wish I wasn't scared of spiders. = 我希望我不害怕蜘蛛。


被动语态:
He was scared by the sudden noise. = 他被突然的噪音吓到了。
The children were scared by the spooky story. = 孩子们被那个恐怖的故事吓到了。


疑问句:
What scares you the most? = 什么最让你害怕？
Does the dark scare you? = 黑暗吓到你了吗？


否定句:
The darkness didn't scare me. = 黑暗没有吓到我。
The loud noise didn't scare the baby. = 响亮的声音没有吓到婴儿。


PHRASE->
scare away = 吓跑
scare off = 吓退
scare the living daylights out of someone = 把某人吓得要死
scare someone stiff = 把某人吓僵了
scare someone to death = 把某人吓死
