pamperer: /ˈpæmpərər/ | n. | 娇惯者；溺爱者 | One who pampers or indulges excessively.  A person who shows excessive indulgence or fondness towards someone. | Example:  He was a pamperer of his only child, giving her everything she wanted. 他是他独生女的溺爱者，给了她想要的一切。  | 近义词：indulger, coddler, spoil | 反义词：disciplinarian, strict parent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pamperer gives the child whatever they want. = 溺爱者给孩子他们想要的任何东西。
She pampers her dog excessively. = 她过度地溺爱她的狗。


一般过去时:
He pampered his son throughout his childhood. = 他在儿子童年时期一直溺爱着他。
My grandmother pampered me with delicious treats. = 我祖母用美味的点心来溺爱我。


现在完成时:
She has pampered her pet cat for years. = 她多年来一直溺爱她的宠物猫。
They have pampered their children to the point of spoiled. = 他们把孩子宠坏了。


过去完成时:
He had pampered his daughter so much that she became entitled. = 他过去如此溺爱他的女儿，以至于她变得自以为是。
The parents had pampered the child before realizing the negative effects. = 父母在意识到负面影响之前一直溺爱着孩子。


将来时:
She will continue to pamper her grandchild. = 她将继续溺爱她的孙子。
They will pamper themselves at the spa this weekend. = 他们这个周末将在水疗中心享受一番。


虚拟语气:
If I were a pamperer, I would give my children anything they desired. = 如果我是个溺爱者，我会给我的孩子他们想要的任何东西。
I wish I hadn't pampered my child so much. = 我真希望我没有那么溺爱我的孩子。


被动语态:
Children are often pampered by their grandparents. = 孩子们经常被他们的祖父母溺爱。
The dog was pampered with expensive toys. = 那只狗被昂贵的玩具宠坏了。


疑问句:
Is he a pamperer of his children? = 他是不是个溺爱他孩子的家长？
Does she pamper her plants excessively? = 她是否过度地溺爱她的植物？


否定句:
He is not a pamperer; he disciplines his children strictly. = 他不是个溺爱者；他严格地管教他的孩子。
She doesn't pamper her employees; she expects them to work hard. = 她不溺爱她的员工；她期望他们努力工作。
