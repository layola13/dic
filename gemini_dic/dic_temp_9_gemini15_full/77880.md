词源:
The name "Geneva" comes from the Latin "Genava," which is believed to be of Celtic origin, possibly meaning "mouth of the Rhône" or a similar geographical description referring to the city's location at the outflow of the Rhône River from Lake Geneva.  The precise etymology is still debated.
