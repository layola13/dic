new-build:/ˈnjuːbɪld/| n. |新建建筑，新建房屋|A newly constructed building or house.  New builds are often more energy-efficient than older properties.|例句：They're moving into a lovely new-build on the outskirts of town. 他们搬进了城郊一座漂亮的新建住宅。  We viewed several new-builds before deciding which one to buy. 我们看了几处新建建筑后才决定买哪一处。|近义词：newly built property, newly constructed building|反义词：old building, existing property|名词
