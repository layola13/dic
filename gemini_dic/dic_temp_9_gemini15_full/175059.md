radiologists:/ˌreɪdiˈɑːlədʒɪsts/| n. |放射科医生|Medical doctors specializing in the use of imaging techniques (like X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and ultrasounds) to diagnose and treat diseases.  They interpret medical images to detect abnormalities and guide treatment plans.|例句：The radiologists reviewed the patient's CT scans to determine the extent of the lung cancer. 放射科医生审查了病人的CT扫描结果以确定肺癌的程度。

The radiologists at the hospital are highly skilled in interpreting complex medical images. 医院的放射科医生非常擅长解读复杂的医学影像。|近义词：radiographers (although radiographers primarily take the images, while radiologists interpret them)|反义词:None.
