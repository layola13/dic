pistacia:/pɪˈsteɪʃə/| n. |开心果属植物；开心果|Pistacia is a genus of about eleven species of trees and shrubs in the cashew family, Anacardiaceae.  They are native to the Mediterranean region, southwest Asia, and Mexico. The most well-known species is Pistacia vera, which produces the edible pistachio nut.|The pistachios we eat come from the Pistacia vera tree.|近义词：None (genus name)|反义词：None (genus name)
