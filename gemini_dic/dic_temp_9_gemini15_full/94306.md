impervious:/ɪmˈpɜːrviəs/ | adj. | 不受影响的；不能渗透的；无动于衷的 |  Not allowing fluid to pass through; impenetrable. Also, not affected or influenced by something. |

例句：
The raincoat is impervious to water.  = 这件雨衣防水。

The prisoner remained impervious to the interrogator's threats. = 囚犯对审讯者的威胁无动于衷。


近义词：
resistant, impenetrable, unaffected, immune, insensitive

反义词：
permeable, penetrable, susceptible, vulnerable, affected
