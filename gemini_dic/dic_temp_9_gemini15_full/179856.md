reexplicating: /ˌriːˈɛkspləkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再解释；重新阐释 |  To explain or interpret something again, often in more detail or from a different perspective.  It implies a previous explanation that may have been insufficient or needs further clarification. |

例句：
The professor reexplicated the complex theorem, providing additional examples to ensure student understanding. 教授重新解释了这个复杂的定理，并提供了额外的例子以确保学生理解。

The author reexplicated his controversial theory in a follow-up article, addressing critics' concerns. 作者在一篇后续文章中重新阐述了他有争议的理论，回应了批评者的担忧。


近义词：re-explain, reinterpret, clarify, elucidate, expound upon


反义词：obscure, obfuscate, confuse


词性：动词
