methanolic:/ˌmɛθəˈnɑːlɪk/ | adj. | 甲醇的；含甲醇的 | Relating to or containing methanol (methyl alcohol).  | Example: The methanolic extract showed significant antibacterial activity. 甲醇提取物显示出显著的抗菌活性。 |近义词：methanol-containing |反义词：water-based


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  Therefore, tense conjugations are not applicable.  The usage is always descriptive.


Example 1: The researchers used a methanolic solution to dissolve the compound.  研究人员使用甲醇溶液来溶解化合物。

Example 2: A methanolic extract of the plant was analyzed for its chemical components.  对该植物的甲醇提取物进行了化学成分分析。


PHRASE->
methanolic extract: 甲醇提取物
methanolic solution: 甲醇溶液
methanolic solvent: 甲醇溶剂
methanolic reaction: 甲醇反应
methanolic suspension: 甲醇悬浮液
