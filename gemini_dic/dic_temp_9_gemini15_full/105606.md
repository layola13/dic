kine:/kaɪn/| n.|牛；母牛|Cattle, especially cows.  The word refers to female cattle, but is often used to refer to cattle in general.|例句：The farmer milked his kine before sunrise.  农夫在日出前挤了他家的牛。

例句：Many kine grazed peacefully in the meadow.  许多母牛在草地上平静地吃草。|近义词：cow, cattle, oxen|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with other livestock like sheep or pigs.|名词
