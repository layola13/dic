imploration:/ˌɪmplɔːˈreɪʃən/| n. |恳求；祈求；哀求 |The act of imploring; earnest or humble entreaty.  It implies a strong feeling of need and desperation. |例句：His impetration for mercy fell on deaf ears.  他的求饶无人理会。

例句：Her impetration for help was met with indifference. 她的求助遭到了冷漠的回应。


近义词：supplication, entreaty, plea, begging, prayer |反义词：demand, command, order |词性: 名词


词源:  From Late Latin *imploratio, from Latin implorare "to implore," from im- "into" + plorare "to weep, cry out."  The sense evolved from a crying out in distress to a formal request made out of desperation.
