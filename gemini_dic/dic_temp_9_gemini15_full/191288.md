scavenge:/ˈskævɪndʒ/| vt. & n.| 觅食；拾荒；搜索；|To search for and collect discarded or usable items.|例句：The birds scavenged for food in the garbage.  鸟儿在垃圾堆里觅食。|例句：He scavenged the junkyard for parts to repair his car. 他在垃圾场里搜寻零件来修理他的车。|近义词：forage, rummage, hunt, glean|反义词：discard, throw away, donate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dogs scavenge for scraps of food. = 狗在寻找食物残渣。
They scavenge the streets for recyclable materials. = 他们在街上搜寻可回收的材料。

一般过去时:
The family scavenged for food after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，这家人四处寻找食物。
The hikers scavenged the forest for firewood. = 远足者在森林里寻找柴火。


现在完成时:
We have scavenged enough materials to build the shelter. = 我们已经收集到足够的材料来搭建庇护所。
The birds have scavenged the leftovers from the picnic. = 鸟儿已经啄食了野餐剩下的食物。


过去完成时:
They had scavenged all the usable parts before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经收集了所有可用的零件。
The animals had scavenged the carcasses for days. = 动物们已经啃食那些尸体好几天了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
