liegest:/ˈlaɪdʒɪst/| v.|躺着；平躺|This word is the second-person singular present subjunctive form of the verb "to lie," meaning to recline or be in a horizontal position.  It is an archaic form rarely used in modern English. |Example:  Thou liegest down to sleep. 你躺下睡觉。|近义词:recline, repose, rest |反义词:stand, sit

USAGE->

The usage would be limited to contexts employing the archaic subjunctive mood, such as in older literature or religious texts.
