exemplarism:/ɪɡˈzɛmplərɪzəm/| n. |典范主义；榜样论|The belief that society should be organized around exemplary individuals and their actions.  It emphasizes the importance of role models and emulation in shaping social values and behavior. Exemplarism often contrasts with approaches that focus on abstract principles or systemic change. |Example:  The company's success is a testament to its commitment to exemplarism, fostering a culture where high-achievers set the standard for others.  这家公司的成功证明了它对典范主义的承诺，它培养了一种高成就者为他人设定标准的文化。|近义词：role-modeling, emulation, idealization|反义词：anarchism, nihilism
