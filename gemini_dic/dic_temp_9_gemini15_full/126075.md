mustiest:/ˈmʌstiɪst/| adj.|最霉味的，最陈腐的 |Most stale, moldy, or musty; having a stale, moldy, or damp smell.  The mustiest corner of the old house was filled with cobwebs and forgotten belongings.  那间老房子最霉的角落里布满了蜘蛛网和被遗忘的物品。|近义词：moldy, fusty, stale, mildewed |反义词：fresh, clean, sweet-smelling

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old book smells mustiest in the basement. = 那本旧书在地下室闻起来最霉。
This room is mustiest compared to the others. = 与其他房间相比，这个房间最霉。
