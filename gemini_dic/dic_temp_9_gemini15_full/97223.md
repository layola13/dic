inflatingly:/ɪnˈfleɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 膨胀地；通货膨胀地 |  Describes something increasing rapidly, often in a negative or unsustainable way, similar to "rapidly" or "excessively," but with the connotation of swelling or expansion.  It often refers to prices, costs, or economic indicators. | The national debt is inflatingly increasing. 国家债务正在膨胀式地增长。 | rapidly, excessively, exponentially | deflatingly, decreasingly | 副词
