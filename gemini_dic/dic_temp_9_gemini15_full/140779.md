omphalocele:/ˈɒmfəloʊˌsiːl/| n. |脐膨出|A birth defect in which a baby's intestines, liver, or other organs stick outside the belly through the navel (belly button). This happens because the muscles around the navel don't close all the way during pregnancy.  The severity varies greatly, depending on which organs are involved and the extent of the protrusion.|The baby was born with an omphalocele, requiring immediate surgical intervention.|gastroschisis, exomphalos|  (Antonyms are less relevant, as it describes a specific congenital condition.)|名词
