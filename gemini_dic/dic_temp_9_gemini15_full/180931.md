reigns:/reɪnz/| v. n.|统治；支配；盛行；|英文解析：To rule as a monarch; to be prevalent or widespread.  As a noun, it refers to the period of a monarch's rule. |例句：The king reigns over the kingdom. 国王统治着王国。


The queen's reign lasted for many years. 女王的统治持续了很多年。|近义词：rules, governs, predominates, prevails|反义词：is ruled, is governed, is subject to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The queen reigns over the country. = 女王统治着这个国家。
He reigns supreme in the world of business. = 他在商界占据主导地位。

一般过去时:
The emperor reigned for fifty years. = 这位皇帝统治了五十年。
His reign of terror ended abruptly. = 他恐怖的统治突然结束。

现在完成时:
She has reigned for over two decades. = 她已经统治了二十多年。
His influence has reigned supreme in the industry. = 他在业界的影响力一直占据主导地位。

过去完成时:
Before the new king, his father had reigned for thirty years. = 在新国王之前，他的父亲统治了三十年。
The old system had reigned for centuries before it was overthrown. = 旧制度在被推翻之前已经存在了几个世纪。

将来时:
Who will reign after the current monarch dies? = 现任君主死后谁将继位？
The new technology will reign supreme in the marketplace. = 新技术将在市场上占据主导地位。

虚拟语气:
If he had reigned wisely, the kingdom would have prospered. = 如果他明智地统治，王国就会繁荣。
I wish the dictator's reign had ended sooner. = 我希望独裁者的统治早点结束。

被动语态:
The country was reigned over by a just king. = 这个国家由一位公正的国王统治。
The market is reigned over by a few powerful companies. = 市场由几家强大的公司控制。

疑问句:
Who reigns in that country? = 哪个国家在统治那个国家？
For how long did the dynasty reign? = 这个王朝统治了多久？

否定句:
The king did not reign justly. = 国王统治得不公正。
The old traditions no longer reign supreme. = 旧传统不再占据主导地位。
