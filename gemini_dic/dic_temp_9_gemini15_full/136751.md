nonsententious:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈtɛnʃəs/ | adj. |不爱教训人的；不教诲的；不训诫的 | Not given to moralizing or excessive moralizing; lacking sententiousness.  Characterized by the absence of pompous or moralizing pronouncements.  | 例句：His approach to the problem was practical and nonsententious, focusing on solutions rather than lectures. 他的方法很实际，不爱教训人，专注于解决问题而不是说教。

例句：The novel's strength lies in its nonsententious portrayal of everyday life. 这部小说的力量在于它对日常生活的真实描绘，没有说教意味。


近义词：unmoralizing, unpreachy, practical, straightforward, realistic

反义词：sententious, preachy, moralistic, didactic
