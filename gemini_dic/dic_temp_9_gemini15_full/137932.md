nonvoluntary:/ˌnɒnvɒləntəri/| adj. |非自愿的，被迫的 |Not done by choice; compulsory or involuntary.  Describes actions or states that occur without the individual's conscious will or consent. |Example:  The confession was obtained through nonvoluntary means.  这句供词是通过非自愿手段获得的。 The nonvoluntary commitment of the patient to the hospital was due to his mental health crisis. 病人非自愿住进医院是由于他的精神健康危机。|近义词：involuntary, coerced, forced, compelled, compulsory|反义词：voluntary, willing, optional
