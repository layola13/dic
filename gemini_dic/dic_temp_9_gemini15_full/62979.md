enoki:/eˈnəʊki/| n. |金针菇|Enoki mushrooms are a type of edible fungus with long, thin stems and small, white caps. They are popular in East Asian cuisine.  They are known for their delicate texture and mild flavor.|例句：I added some enoki mushrooms to my stir-fry. 我在我的炒菜里加了一些金针菇。|例句：Enoki mushrooms are a good source of dietary fiber. 金针菇是膳食纤维的良好来源。|近义词：needle mushroom, velvet stem|反义词：无
