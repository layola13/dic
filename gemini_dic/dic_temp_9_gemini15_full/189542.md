sale:/seɪl/ | n. |出售，销售；廉价出售；销售额 | The act of selling something; the exchange of goods or services for money.  It can also refer to a period of reduced prices. |例句：The sale of the house was completed last week.  房屋的出售在上周完成。

The store is having a big sale on summer clothes. 这家商店正在进行夏季服装大减价。


近义词：selling, bargain, discount, clearance | 反义词：purchase, buy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop sells clothes. = 这家商店卖衣服。
They sell fresh produce daily. = 他们每天都卖新鲜农产品。


一般过去时:
He sold his car last month. = 他上个月卖了他的车。
We sold all our tickets. = 我们把所有的票都卖掉了。


现在完成时:
They have sold over 1000 units this year. = 今年他们已经卖出了超过1000台。
She has sold her paintings at several exhibitions. = 她在几次展览会上卖出了她的画作。


过去完成时:
They had sold all the bread before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经把所有的面包都卖掉了。
We had sold our old house before we bought the new one. = 我们在买新房子之前已经卖掉了旧房子。


将来时:
I will sell my bike next week. = 我下周将卖掉我的自行车。
They will sell their products online. = 他们将在网上销售他们的产品。


虚拟语气:
If I sold my house, I would buy a bigger one. = 如果我卖掉我的房子，我会买一个更大的。
I wish I had sold my stocks before the market crashed. = 我真希望在市场崩盘之前我已经卖掉了我的股票。


被动语态:
The tickets were sold out quickly. = 票很快就被卖光了。
This product is sold worldwide. = 此产品在全球销售。


疑问句:
Do they sell books here? = 他们这里卖书吗？
Where do you sell your crafts? = 你在哪里卖你的手工艺品？


否定句:
The shop doesn't sell electronics. = 这家商店不卖电子产品。
We didn't sell anything yesterday. = 我们昨天什么都没卖出去。


PHRASE->
for sale:待售
on sale:特价销售
put up for sale:挂牌出售
sale price:售价
sales figures:销售额


词源:
Old English *sǣl, from Old Norse sala “sale, selling,” related to selja “to sell.”  The word's history reflects the importance of trade and commerce throughout history.
