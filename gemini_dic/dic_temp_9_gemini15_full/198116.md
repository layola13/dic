shop-bought: /ʃɒp bɔːt/ | adj. | 商店买的；在商店买到的 |  Describes goods or items that are purchased from a shop rather than being homemade or produced elsewhere.  |  例句：I prefer home-baked bread to shop-bought loaves. = 我更喜欢自制的面包而不是商店买的面包。

例句：The shop-bought cake was delicious, but not as good as my grandmother's. = 商店买的蛋糕很好吃，但不如我奶奶做的那么好。


近义词：store-bought, commercially-produced


反义词：homemade, home-baked, hand-made


词性：形容词
