mugget:/ˈmʌɡɪt/| n. |金块；笨蛋；（尤指）小型金块|A nugget is a small, irregular lump of a precious metal, especially gold, found naturally.  It can also be used informally to refer to a person considered foolish or stupid.|例句：He struck gold, finding a large nugget in the riverbed. 他在河床上发现了一大块金子。 He's such a nugget; he fell for that scam. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然上当受骗了。|近义词：lump, chunk, fool, idiot|反义词：genius, intelligent
