emulsifiers:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪərz/| n. |乳化剂|Substances that stabilize emulsions by reducing the interfacial tension between two immiscible liquids, such as oil and water.  They prevent the liquids from separating. |Example: Many food products use emulsifiers to create a smooth, creamy texture.  许多食品使用乳化剂来创造光滑，奶油般的质地。|近义词：emulsifying agents|反义词:None specifically, but substances that *promote* separation could be considered antonyms.
