convertibles:/kənˈvɜːrtəblz/ | n. |敞篷车；可转换的证券|A convertible is a vehicle, typically a car, with a roof that can be folded or removed, allowing for open-air driving.  It can also refer to a type of security that can be converted into another type of security, such as a bond that can be converted into stock. |例句：He bought a red convertible for his birthday. 他买了辆红色的敞篷车作为生日礼物。

例句：The convertible bond offered a higher yield than traditional bonds. 这只可转换债券比传统债券提供了更高的收益。|近义词：cabriolet, roadster, open-top car |反义词：sedan, coupe, hardtop
