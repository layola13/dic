ocean:/ˈoʊʃən/|n.|海洋；大海|A large body of salt water that covers most of the earth's surface.  It is usually divided into five main oceans: the Atlantic, Pacific, Indian, Arctic, and Southern.|例句：The Pacific Ocean is the largest ocean in the world. 太平洋是世界上最大的海洋。

例句：We sailed across the ocean. 我们横渡了海洋。|sea, sea, main|land, lake|名词
