ceramics:/sərˈæmɪks/| n.|陶瓷制品；陶瓷学|The art and science of making objects from clay and other ceramic materials.  Ceramics are known for their durability, heat resistance, and aesthetic qualities.|例句：She collects antique ceramics. 她收集古董陶瓷。|例句：He's studying ceramics in college. 他在大学学习陶瓷学。|近义词：pottery, earthenware, porcelain|反义词：无
