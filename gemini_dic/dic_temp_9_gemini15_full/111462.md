local:/ˈloʊkəl/| adj.| 当地的；地方的；局部的 | relating to or occurring in a particular area or place.  |例句：The local newspaper reported on the accident. 当地报纸报道了这起事故。 |例句：We visited several local shops. 我们参观了几家当地商店。|近义词：regional, provincial, municipal |反义词：national, international, global


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The local bakery sells delicious bread. = 当地的面包店卖美味的面包。
This local market offers fresh produce. = 这个当地市场提供新鲜农产品。


一般过去时:
The local library closed early yesterday. = 当地图书馆昨天早早关门了。
We ate at a local restaurant last night. = 我们昨晚在一家当地餐馆吃饭。


现在完成时:
They have lived in this local community for years. = 他们在这个当地社区住了好几年了。
She has supported many local charities. = 她支持过许多当地慈善机构。


过去完成时:
The local government had already implemented the new policy. = 当地政府已经实施了新政策。
Before we moved, we had explored the local area. = 在我们搬家之前，我们已经探索过当地地区了。


将来时:
The local council will build a new park next year. = 当地议会明年将建造一个新的公园。
We will visit the local museum next week. = 我们下周将参观当地博物馆。


虚拟语气:
If I lived in a local village, I would be much happier. = 如果我住在当地村庄，我会快乐得多。
I wish the local shops stayed open later. = 我希望当地商店关门时间晚一些。


被动语态:
The local roads are being repaired this week. = 当地道路本周正在维修。
The local event was well-organized. = 当地活动组织得很好。


疑问句:
Is there a local bus stop nearby? = 附近有当地的公交车站吗？
What is the local time? = 当地时间是多少？


否定句:
The local news wasn't very interesting. = 当地新闻不太有趣。
There aren't many local jobs available. = 没有很多当地的工作机会。
