self-survey:/ˈsɛlf ˈsɜːrveɪ/| n. |自我调查，自我评估 |A self-survey is a questionnaire or assessment that individuals complete to gather information about themselves.  It's often used for self-reflection, personal development, or research purposes. |Example: The company conducted a self-survey to gauge employee satisfaction. 公司进行了一次自我调查以衡量员工满意度。|近义词：self-assessment, introspection, self-evaluation |反义词：external assessment, observation by others |名词
