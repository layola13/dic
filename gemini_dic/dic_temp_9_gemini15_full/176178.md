re-addressed:/ˌriːˈædrɛst/| vt.|重新处理；再次提出；重新寄送 |To address again; to reconsider or deal with again; to send (a letter, etc.) to a new or corrected address.  |例句：The company re-addressed the issue of employee satisfaction. 公司重新处理了员工满意度的问题。

例句：The letter was re-addressed to the correct recipient. 这封信被重新寄给了正确的收件人。

近义词：reconsider, review, redirect

反义词：ignore, neglect

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-address the problem every year. = 他们每年都重新处理这个问题。
The company re-addresses customer complaints promptly. = 公司迅速地重新处理客户投诉。


一般过去时:
The committee re-addressed the budget proposal. = 委员会重新审议了预算提案。
She re-addressed the package to her new apartment. = 她把包裹重新寄到了她的新公寓。


现在完成时:
They have re-addressed the safety concerns raised by the inspectors. = 他们已经重新处理了检查员提出的安全问题。
He has re-addressed the email to include the correct attachment. = 他已重新发送邮件，并附上了正确的附件。


过去完成时:
They had re-addressed the issue before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经重新处理了这个问题。
She had re-addressed the letter several times before it finally reached its destination. = 在这封信最终到达目的地之前，她已经重新寄了好几次。


将来时:
They will re-address the matter in the next meeting. = 他们将在下一次会议上重新讨论此事。
The government will re-address the issue of healthcare reform. = 政府将重新处理医疗改革问题。


虚拟语气:
If they re-addressed the concerns, the project would be successful. = 如果他们重新处理这些担忧，项目就会成功。
I wish they had re-addressed the problem earlier. = 我希望他们早点重新处理这个问题。
