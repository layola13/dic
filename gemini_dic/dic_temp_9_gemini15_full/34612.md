cleck:/klɛk/ | n. |咔哒声，轻微的敲击声 | A short, sharp sound, like that made by two things clicking together.  |例句：I heard a cleck as the latch closed. 我听到闩锁关闭时发出咔哒一声。  |例句：The old clock made a satisfying cleck every second. 老钟每秒都会发出令人满意的咔哒声。|近义词：click, tick, snap |反义词：无
