familiarizing:/fəˈmɪliəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使熟悉；使了解 | The act of making someone or something familiar.  It involves the process of becoming acquainted with something or someone. | Example:  She spent weeks familiarizing herself with the company's procedures. 她花了几个星期的时间熟悉公司的规章制度。  The teacher is familiarizing the students with new vocabulary. 老师正在让学生们熟悉新的词汇。 |近义词: acquainting, introducing, orienting | 反义词: alienating, estranging | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am familiarizing myself with the new software. = 我正在熟悉新的软件。
He is familiarizing his team with the project details. = 他正在让他的团队熟悉项目细节。

一般过去时:
We familiarized ourselves with the local customs. = 我们熟悉了当地的风俗习惯。
She familiarized the students with the research methods. = 她让学生们熟悉了研究方法。

现在完成时:
I have familiarized myself with the company's policies. = 我已经熟悉了公司的政策。
They have familiarized themselves with the safety procedures. = 他们已经熟悉了安全规程。

过去完成时:
He had familiarized himself with the script before the audition. = 他在试镜前已经熟悉了剧本。
They had familiarized the volunteers with the task before they started. = 他们在志愿者开始工作之前已经让他们熟悉了任务。

将来时:
We will familiarize ourselves with the new equipment before the launch. = 我们将在发布之前熟悉新设备。
She will familiarize the participants with the agenda. = 她将让参与者熟悉议程。

虚拟语气:
If I had familiarized myself with the map, I wouldn't have gotten lost. = 如果我熟悉了地图，我就不会迷路了。
It would be helpful if they familiarized themselves with the instructions. = 如果他们熟悉说明书，那就有帮助了。

被动语态:
The students were familiarized with the historical context. = 学生们对历史背景进行了熟悉。
The new employees will be familiarized with the company culture. = 新员工将熟悉公司文化。

疑问句:
Are you familiarizing yourself with the new regulations? = 你正在熟悉新的规章制度吗？
How long will it take to familiarize the staff with the changes? = 让员工熟悉这些变化需要多长时间？

否定句:
I am not familiarizing myself with that software. = 我没有在熟悉那个软件。
He didn't familiarize himself with the terms of the contract. = 他没有熟悉合同的条款。
