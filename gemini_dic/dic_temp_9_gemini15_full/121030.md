miscomputing:/ˌmɪskəmˈpjuːtɪŋ/| v.|误算，计算错误|The act of computing incorrectly; an incorrect calculation.  It implies a mistake in the process of calculation, not necessarily a deliberate error. |例句：The miscomputing of the budget led to a significant shortfall. 预算计算错误导致了严重的短缺。

例句：His miscomputing of the trajectory resulted in the rocket missing its target. 他轨迹计算错误导致火箭偏离目标。


近义词：miscalculation, error, mistake, blunder


反义词：accurate calculation, correct computation


词性：动词
