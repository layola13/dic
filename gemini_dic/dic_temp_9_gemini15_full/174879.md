racketeers:/ˌrækiˈtɪrz/| n. |敲诈者，勒索者，诈骗犯 |People involved in organized crime, especially those who make money through illegal activities such as extortion, bribery, or fraud.  They often operate in a systematic and coordinated manner, using threats and violence to achieve their goals. |The racketeers were finally brought to justice after a long investigation.  敲诈犯们经过长时间的调查后终于受到了法律制裁。|  extortionists, gangsters, criminals | law-abiding citizens, honest people |名词
