kiddingly:/ˈkɪdɪŋli/| adv.|开玩笑地，戏谑地|In a joking or playful manner; not seriously.  It implies a lighthearted and often teasing tone.|例句：He said kiddingly, "I'm going to eat all your cookies." 他开玩笑地说：“我要吃掉你所有的饼干。”
例句：She kiddingly suggested we go skydiving. 她开玩笑地建议我们去跳伞。|近义词：jokingly, playfully, teasingly|反义词：seriously, earnestly
