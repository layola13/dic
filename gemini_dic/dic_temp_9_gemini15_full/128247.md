neurolinguists:/ˌnʊrəʊlɪŋˈɡwɪsts/| n. |神经语言学家|Experts who study the relationship between the brain, language, and cognition.  They investigate how the brain processes language, how language influences thought, and the neurological basis of language disorders.|Neurolinguists are exploring the neural mechanisms underlying language acquisition.|近义词：cognitive scientists, psycholinguists|反义词：无
