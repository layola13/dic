omnitolerant: /ˌɒmnɪˈtɒlərənt/ | adj. | 全能耐受的，无所不耐的 | Able to tolerate all conditions or circumstances;  capable of surviving or growing under a wide range of conditions.  This term is often used in biology and ecology to describe organisms that can thrive in diverse environments. | 例句：The newly discovered bacteria strain is omnitolerant, surviving in both extreme heat and extreme cold.  这种新发现的细菌菌株具有全能耐受性，能在极热和极冷的环境中生存。


The omnitolerant plant species colonized the harsh, rocky terrain with ease.这种耐受性极强的植物物种轻松地占据了恶劣的岩石地形。


近义词：versatile, adaptable, ubiquitous, resilient | 反义词：intolerant, sensitive, fastidious
