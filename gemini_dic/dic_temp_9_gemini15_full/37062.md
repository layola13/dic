combated:/kəmˈbeɪtɪd/| v.|战斗，对抗，与…作斗争|to fight against something; to struggle against something.|例句：The firefighters bravely combated the blaze. 消防员英勇地与大火搏斗。

例句：The government combated inflation with new economic policies. 政府用新的经济政策来对抗通货膨胀。


近义词：fought, battled, struggled, opposed, resisted|反义词：supported, aided, assisted|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army combats terrorism. = 军队打击恐怖主义。
She combats prejudice in her daily life. = 她每天都在与偏见作斗争。


一般过去时:
They combated the enemy forces fiercely. = 他们与敌军进行了激烈的战斗。
He combated his illness with determination. = 他以坚定的意志与疾病作斗争。


现在完成时:
We have combated the spread of misinformation. = 我们一直在与虚假信息的传播作斗争。
She has combated discrimination for many years. = 她多年来一直在与歧视作斗争。


过去完成时:
They had combated the disease for months before a cure was found. = 在找到治疗方法之前，他们已经与疾病斗争了几个月。
The soldiers had combated the enemy successfully before reinforcements arrived. = 在增援部队到达之前，士兵们已经成功地击退了敌人。


将来时:
We will combat climate change through sustainable practices. = 我们将通过可持续发展措施来应对气候变化。
The company will combat counterfeiting with new security measures. = 公司将采取新的安全措施来打击假冒伪劣产品。


虚拟语气:
If we combated poverty effectively, we could reduce inequality. = 如果我们有效地打击贫困，就能减少不平等。
I wish we had combated the issue more aggressively in the past. = 我希望我们过去能更积极地处理这个问题。


被动语态:
The disease was combated with new medicines. = 这种疾病是用新药治疗的。
The problem was combated by introducing new regulations. = 通过引入新的规章制度来解决这个问题。


疑问句:
How did they combat the crisis? = 他们是如何应对危机的？
What methods are being used to combat the spread of the virus? = 正在使用什么方法来对抗病毒的传播？


否定句:
They did not combat the rumors effectively. = 他们没有有效地打击谣言。
The government failed to combat the economic downturn. = 政府未能有效应对经济低迷。
