remittor:/rɪˈmɪtər/| n. |汇款人，支付人 | The person or entity sending money.  A remittor initiates a transaction to send funds to a recipient.  | Example: The remittor's name and address are required on the money order.  汇款人的姓名和地址需要写在汇票上。 |近义词:payer, sender, consignor |反义词:recipient, payee
