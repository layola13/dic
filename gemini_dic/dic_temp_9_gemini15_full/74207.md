foursome:/ˈfɔːrsəm/| n. |四人一组；四人赛；四人同游|A group of four people, especially one playing a game together.  It often refers to a group of four golfers playing together. |例句：We're a foursome playing golf this afternoon.  我们今天下午是四人一组打高尔夫球。

例句：They went on a foursome to the beach.他们四人一起去了海滩。


近义词：quartet, quad, group of four|反义词：无
