frazzles:/ˈfræzəlz/ | v. | 使筋疲力尽；使烦躁不安 | to make someone extremely tired and irritable | The long journey frazzled her nerves. 长途旅行使她神经紧张。|近义词：exhaust, tire, wear out |反义词：refresh, revitalize


USAGE->

This work frazzles him every day. = 这份工作每天都让他筋疲力尽。


一般过去时:
