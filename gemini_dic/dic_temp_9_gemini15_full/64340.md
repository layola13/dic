eras:/ˈɪərəz/ | n. |时代；年代；时期 | Eras are periods of time in history, often characterized by significant events or cultural shifts.  |Example: The Victorian era was a time of great industrial growth.  维多利亚时代是工业快速发展的时期。|近义词：period, epoch, age |反义词：N/A

一般现在时:
The current era is defined by technological advancements. = 当前时代以科技进步为特征。
This era witnesses rapid social change. = 这个时代见证了快速的社会变革。


一般过去时:
The Bronze Age era ended around 1200 BC. = 青铜时代大约在公元前1200年结束。
That era saw the rise and fall of many empires. = 那個時代見證了許多帝國的興衰。

Several eras have come and gone since the beginning of civilization. = 自文明开始以来，已经过去了几个时代。

过去完成时:

将来时:
The next era will likely be defined by artificial intelligence. = 下一个时代很可能以人工智能为特征。
We can only speculate what the future eras will bring. = 我们只能推测未来的时代会带来什么。
