currents:/ˈkʌrənts/| n.|水流；潮流；趋势；电流|A continuous flowing movement of something, especially of water or air.|例句：The ocean currents are strong near the coast. 近海洋流很强。

例句：The current trend is toward smaller cars. 目前的趋势是转向小型汽车。|近义词：flow, stream, trend, tendency|反义词：stillness, stagnation
