goalkeeper:/ˈɡoʊlˌkiːpər/ | n. |守门员，门将 | A goalkeeper is a player in team sports such as soccer, hockey, and water polo who defends the goal.  Their primary role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring. | Example: The goalkeeper made a spectacular save to prevent the goal.  守门员做出精彩扑救阻止了进球。

The goalkeeper dived to stop the penalty.  守门员下潜扑救点球。


近义词：goalie (informal)
反义词：attacker, forward

词源:
The word "goalkeeper" is a compound word formed from "goal" and "keeper."  "Goal" refers to the objective in a game, and "keeper" means someone who guards or protects something.  The word's origins trace back to the development of organized team sports in the 19th century.
