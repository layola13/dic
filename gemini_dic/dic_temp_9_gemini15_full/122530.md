modality:/moʊˈdælɪti/ /məˈdælɪti/ | n. |方式，模式；情态；(逻辑学)模态|The way in which something exists or is experienced or done.  It can refer to a method, manner, or style. In logic, it deals with possibility, necessity, and contingency.|

例句：

The doctor considered different modalities of treatment.  医生考虑了不同的治疗方法。

The painting's modality is highly abstract. 这幅画的风格非常抽象。


近义词：method, manner, mode, approach, system

反义词：None directly; antonyms depend on the context (e.g., if modality means "a specific method," the antonym might be "a lack of a specific method").


词性：名词
