focal:/ˈfoʊkəl/| adj.|焦点；中心的；主要的|The focal point of the painting is the central figure.  这幅画的焦点是中心人物。|近义词：central, main, principal|反义词：peripheral, marginal


The focal point of the discussion was the budget.  讨论的焦点是预算。


focal length:焦距

词源:
From Latin *focālis, from focus ("hearth, fireplace").  The original meaning related to the central point of a hearth or fireplace.  The modern meaning evolved from the optical sense of a lens's focal point to the broader metaphorical meaning of a central point of interest or activity.
