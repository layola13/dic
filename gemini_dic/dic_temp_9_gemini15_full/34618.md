clef:/klef/| n. |谱号|A clef is a symbol placed at the beginning of a musical staff that indicates the pitch of the notes.  It essentially sets the reference point for the notes written on the staff.  Different clefs indicate different ranges of notes.|Example: The treble clef is used for higher-pitched notes, while the bass clef is used for lower-pitched notes.  The alto clef and tenor clef are less common.|近义词:musical symbol, notation|反义词:None
