dispenser:/dɪˈspɛnsər/| n. |分配器；自动售货机|A dispenser is a device or container that releases a substance, such as soap, drinks, or cash, in controlled amounts.|例句：The soap dispenser in the bathroom is empty.  浴室里的肥皂分配器空了。|例句：The airport has many drink dispensers. 机场有很多饮料自动售货机。|近义词：dispensary, distributor, vendor|反义词：receiver, collector
