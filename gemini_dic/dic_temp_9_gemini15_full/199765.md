sirloins:/ˈsɜːrlɔɪnz/ | n. |西冷牛排 | A sirloin is a cut of beef from the sirloin subprimal cut, typically considered tender and flavorful. It's a popular choice for grilling, roasting, or pan-frying.  | Example: We had sirloins for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了西冷牛排。|近义词：tenderloin, loin |反义词：tough cut of meat
