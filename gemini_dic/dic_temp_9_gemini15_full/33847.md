circumnavigator:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪtər/| n. |环球航行者，环球航海家 |A circumnavigator is a person who has sailed or flown around the world.  This term typically implies a continuous journey, though some definitions allow for stops. |Example: Magellan was a famous circumnavigator. 麦哲伦是一位著名的环球航行者。  Sir Francis Drake was another renowned circumnavigator. 弗朗西斯·德雷克爵士是另一位著名的环球航行者。|近义词：global traveler, world traveler |反义词：landlubber (informal)
