comfort:/ˈkʌmfərt/| n., v.|舒适；安慰；令人安慰的事物；使舒适；安慰|

英文解析：
As a noun, comfort refers to a state of physical ease and freedom from pain or distress, or something that provides this. As a verb, it means to give someone support, consolation, or relief in their distress or suffering.

例句：
The soft bed provided great comfort.  柔软的床提供了极大的舒适。

He offered comfort to the grieving family. 他安慰了悲伤的家庭。


近义词：
ease, solace, relief, consolation, support

反义词：
discomfort, distress, misery, anguish, pain


词性：
Noun, Verb
