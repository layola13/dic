financist: /faɪˈnænɪst/ | n. |金融专家，金融家 | A person who is skilled in or knowledgeable about financial matters, often working in the financial industry.  | Example:  The financist advised the company on its investment strategy.  这位金融专家就公司的投资策略向其提供了建议。 |近义词: financial expert, financial professional, investment banker | 反义词: None directly opposite, but could be contrasted with someone lacking financial knowledge.
