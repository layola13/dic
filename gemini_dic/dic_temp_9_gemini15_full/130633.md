nonadmissibility:/ˌnɒnædˌmɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可接受性，不许可|The state or quality of being inadmissible;  the fact of not being allowed or acceptable.  It often refers to evidence or arguments that are not allowed in a legal proceeding or other formal context. |例句：The court ruled on the nonadmissibility of the evidence due to its questionable origin. 法院裁定证据因来源可疑而不可采信。 The nonadmissibility of hearsay evidence is a fundamental principle of law. 证词证据的不可采信是法律的基本原则。|近义词：inadmissibility, unacceptability, rejection|反义词：admissibility, acceptability
