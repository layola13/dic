naturalist:/ˈnætʃrəlɪst/ | n. |博物学家，自然主义者 |A naturalist is a person who studies or is an expert in natural history, especially zoology, botany, or geology.  They observe and record the characteristics of living organisms and their environments. |例句：Jane Goodall is a renowned naturalist who dedicated her life to studying chimpanzees. 简·古道尔是一位著名的博物学家，她毕生致力于研究黑猩猩。  |例句：The naturalist led the group on a fascinating hike through the rainforest, pointing out various plants and animals. 这位博物学家带领大家在热带雨林中进行了令人着迷的徒步旅行，并指出了各种动植物。|近义词：natural historian, field biologist, environmentalist |反义词：N/A
