musket:/ˈmʌskɪt/| n. |火绳枪|A musket is a muzzle-loading, smoothbore firearm that was widely used by infantry from the 16th to the 19th centuries.  It was a significant weapon in many wars and conflicts. |Example: The soldier fired his musket at the approaching enemy.  士兵用火绳枪射击逼近的敌人。 The museum displayed a collection of antique muskets. 博物馆展出了一批古董火绳枪。|近义词：firearm, rifle (though rifles are rifled, muskets are not), gun|反义词：无
