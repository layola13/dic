rediscuss:/ˌriːdɪˈskʌs/| vt.|重新讨论，再讨论|To discuss something again, often in more detail or with different people.|例句：We need to rediscuss the budget before finalizing it. 我们需要在最终确定预算之前重新讨论一下。例句：The committee decided to rediscuss the proposal after receiving new information. 委员会决定在收到新的信息后重新讨论该提案。|近义词：reconsider, review, debate again|反义词：conclude, finalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rediscuss the project every week. = 他们每周都重新讨论这个项目。
We rediscuss the plan regularly. = 我们定期重新讨论这个计划。

一般过去时:
They rediscussed the contract terms. = 他们重新讨论了合同条款。
We rediscussed the proposal last night. = 我们昨晚重新讨论了这个提案。

现在完成时:
They have rediscussed the strategy multiple times. = 他们已经多次重新讨论了这个策略。
We have rediscussed the issue since the meeting. = 自从会议以来，我们已经重新讨论了这个问题。

过去完成时:
They had rediscussed the problem before the deadline. = 他们早在截止日期之前就重新讨论过这个问题。
We had rediscussed the plan before making a decision. = 我们在做决定之前已经重新讨论过这个计划。

将来时:
They will rediscuss the matter tomorrow. = 他们明天将重新讨论此事。
We will rediscuss the budget next week. = 我们下周将重新讨论预算。

虚拟语气:
If they rediscussed the issue, we might find a better solution. = 如果他们重新讨论这个问题，我们可能会找到一个更好的解决方案。
I wish they had rediscussed the proposal before voting. = 我希望他们在投票前重新讨论过这个提案。

被动语态:
The proposal was rediscussed by the board. = 该提案受到了董事会的重新讨论。

疑问句:

否定句:
They did not rediscuss the findings. = 他们没有重新讨论研究结果。
