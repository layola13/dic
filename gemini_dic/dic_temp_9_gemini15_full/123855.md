moralizes:/ˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪz/ | v. |说教；教训；道德说教 | To talk or write about morals in a way that is intended to teach or instruct, often in a pompous or preachy manner.  |He constantly moralizes about the evils of modern society. 他不断地教训现代社会的罪恶。|preaches, lectures, sermonizes |  (反义词较难直接对应，可根据语境选择)  acts, behaves, indulges


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He moralizes about the dangers of drugs. = 他对毒品的危害说教。
She moralizes to her children about the importance of honesty. = 她告诫孩子们诚实的重要性。


一般过去时:
The priest moralized about the importance of forgiveness. = 牧师对宽恕的重要性进行说教。
My grandfather moralized on the value of hard work. = 我祖父对努力工作的价值进行了说教。


现在完成时:
He has moralized incessantly since his wife's death. = 自妻子去世以来，他一直喋喋不休地进行道德说教。
She has moralized to me about my lifestyle choices. = 她对我生活方式的选择进行过说教。


过去完成时:
He had moralized extensively before finally offering any practical help. = 在最终提供任何实际帮助之前，他已经进行了大量的道德说教。
They had moralized at length about the situation before reaching a decision. = 他们在做出决定之前，已经就此事进行了冗长的说教。


将来时:
He will moralize again at the dinner table. = 他将在餐桌上再次进行说教。
She will moralize about the importance of saving money. = 她将对储蓄的重要性进行说教。


虚拟语气:
If he moralized less, people would listen more. = 如果他少说教一些，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't moralize so much. = 我希望他不那么说教。


被动语态:
His actions were moralized upon by the community. = 他的行为受到了社区的道德批判。
The situation was moralized about in the media. = 媒体对这种情况进行了道德说教。


疑问句:
Does he often moralize to his friends? = 他经常对朋友说教吗？
Why does she moralize so much? = 她为什么如此爱说教？


否定句:
He doesn't moralize often. = 他不经常说教。
She didn't moralize at the meeting. = 她在会议上没有说教。


PHRASE->
moralizes on/upon: 对…说教
moralizes about:  关于…说教
constantly moralizes: 不断地进行道德说教
frequently moralizes: 经常说教
openly moralizes: 公开说教
