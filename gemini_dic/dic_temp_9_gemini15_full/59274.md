eagerness:/ˈiːɡənəs/| n. |热切；渴望；热衷 |A strong desire or enthusiasm to do something or to achieve something.  It often implies a sense of anticipation and excitement.|例句：Her eagerness to learn was evident in her constant questioning.  她的求知欲在她不断的提问中显而易见。


例句：His eagerness to please his boss sometimes led him to make mistakes. 他渴望讨好老板，有时导致他犯错。


近义词：enthusiasm, zeal, avidity, fervor


反义词：apathy, indifference, reluctance
