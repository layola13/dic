megaliths:/ˈmɛɡəlɪθs/| n. |巨石；巨石建筑|Large stones, especially those used in prehistoric structures.  Megaliths are often arranged in specific patterns to create monumental structures like stone circles, dolmens, and standing stones. These structures, built thousands of years ago, are a testament to the engineering and cultural achievements of prehistoric societies.|The Stonehenge megaliths are a famous example of prehistoric architecture.|近义词：monoliths, stone monuments|反义词：small stones, miniature structures
