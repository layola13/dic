deliberators:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtərz/| n. |审议者；仔细考虑者 |People who are engaged in careful consideration or discussion, especially as part of a formal process.  They thoughtfully weigh options before making decisions. |Example: The deliberators on the jury took several hours to reach a verdict. 陪审团的成员们花了几个小时才达成裁决。|近义词：considerers, ponderers, discussants |反义词：deciders (in the sense of quick decision-makers)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The jury deliberates on the evidence. = 陪审团正在仔细审议证据。
The committee deliberates the proposal. = 委员会正在审议该提案。


一般过去时:
The council deliberated for hours before voting. = 理事会投票前讨论了数小时。
The judges deliberated carefully before announcing their decision. = 裁判们仔细斟酌后宣布了他们的决定。


现在完成时:
The panel has deliberated extensively on the matter. = 专家组已经对这件事进行了广泛的讨论。
They have deliberated for days, but haven't reached a conclusion yet. = 他们已经讨论了几天，但还没有达成结论。


过去完成时:
After they had deliberated, they announced their findings. = 他们经过讨论后，宣布了他们的调查结果。
The committee had deliberated all afternoon before adjourning. = 委员会在休会前讨论了整个下午。


将来时:
The committee will deliberate the budget next week. = 委员会下周将审议预算。
The judges will deliberate on the case before issuing a ruling. = 裁判们将在发布裁决前审议此案。


虚拟语气:
If the jury deliberated more carefully, they might have reached a different verdict. = 如果陪审团更仔细地审议，他们可能会得出不同的裁决。
If the council had deliberated longer, they might have avoided the mistake. = 如果理事会讨论得更久一些，他们或许就能避免这个错误。


被动语态:
The proposal was deliberated by the board. = 该提案由董事会进行了审议。
The matter is being deliberated by the committee. = 该事项正在由委员会审议。


疑问句:
Are the judges still deliberating? = 裁判们还在审议吗？
Has the jury deliberated long enough? = 陪审团审议的时间够长了吗？


否定句:
The committee did not deliberate thoroughly enough. = 委员会的审议不够透彻。
The council did not deliberate before making a decision. = 理事会在做决定之前没有进行讨论。
