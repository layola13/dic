While similar to "reduction," it is less common and often implies a more gradual or subtle decrease. | Example: The reducement of the workforce was gradual to minimize disruption.  工作队伍的缩减是循序渐进的，以尽量减少混乱。 | 近义词：reduction, decrease, diminution, lessening | 反义词：increase, augmentation, enhancement | 名词
