nepotisms:/ˈnepəˌtɪzəm/| n.|裙带关系；任人唯亲|The practice of favoring relatives or friends, especially by giving them jobs or other advantages.  It often involves unfairness and lack of meritocracy.|例句：The company is known for its nepotism, with many senior positions filled by relatives of the CEO. 这家公司以任人唯亲而闻名，许多高级职位都由首席执行官的亲属担任。|例句：His promotion was seen as a clear case of nepotism, as he lacked the necessary experience. 他的晋升被视为明显的裙带关系，因为他缺乏必要的经验。|近义词：favoritism, cronyism|反义词：meritocracy, impartiality
