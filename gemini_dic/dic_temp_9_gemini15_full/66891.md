expediently:/ɪkˈspiːdiəntli/ | adv. | 方便地；权宜地；应急地 | In a way that is convenient and practical, although possibly improper or immoral.  It emphasizes the practicality and speed of a solution, often at the expense of other considerations. | 例句：The problem was solved expediently, though not necessarily ethically.  问题得到迅速解决，但未必符合道德。

The government acted expediently to prevent a riot.  政府采取权宜措施以防止暴乱。


近义词：conveniently, practically, efficiently, opportunistically | 反义词：inefficiently, impractically, scrupulously, ethically


词源：源自于expedient (adj./n.)，其词根为拉丁语的 *expedire，意思是“使顺利进行”、“解开”。
