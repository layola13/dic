It often involves preparing data, setting up resources, or verifying conditions.  Pre-execution can improve efficiency and ensure the success of the main execution. | Example: The pre-execution phase of the program involved loading necessary libraries and verifying the input data.  预执行阶段包括加载必要的库和验证输入数据。|近义词：preparation, preliminary execution, pre-processing |反义词：post-execution, completion
