heretofore:/ˌhɪərtuˈfɔːr/| adv. |迄今为止，到目前为止 | Up to this or that time; until now.  It indicates a point in time before the present moment or a specific event being discussed. |例句：Heretofore, the company had never experienced such a significant downturn. 迄今为止，该公司从未经历过如此严重的衰退。

例句：The solution, heretofore unknown, was finally discovered.  这个此前未知的解决方案最终被发现了。


近义词：previously, until now, so far, up to now, hitherto


反义词：hereafter, henceforth, from now on


词性：副词
