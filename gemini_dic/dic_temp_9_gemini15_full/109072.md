legatary:/ˈlɛɡəˌtɛri/| n. |遗嘱受益人，受遗赠人 |A legatee is a person who inherits money or property from someone who has died, as specified in a will.  |Example:  John was named as a legatee in his uncle's will, inheriting a substantial sum of money.  约翰被指定为叔叔遗嘱中的受益人，继承了一大笔钱。|近义词：beneficiary, heir |反义词：testator (遗嘱人)
