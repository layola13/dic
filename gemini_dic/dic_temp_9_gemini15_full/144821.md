over-imports:/ˈoʊvərɪmpɔːrts/ | n. |过度进口，进口过剩 |Excessive imports of goods into a country, often leading to negative economic consequences.  |Example: The country's over-imports of consumer goods led to a significant trade deficit.  国家的消费品过度进口导致了严重的贸易逆差。|近义词：excess imports, surplus imports |反义词：under-imports, import restrictions |名词
