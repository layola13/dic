check-out:/tʃɛkˈaʊt/ | n., v. | 退房；结账；查看；检查 |

英文解析:As a noun, "check-out" refers to the process of leaving a hotel or other accommodation after paying for your stay, or the act of paying for goods at a store. As a verb, it means to leave a hotel or other accommodation, to pay for goods and leave a store, or to examine or inspect something carefully.

例句:
n.  The check-out time is 11 a.m. = 退房时间是上午11点。

n.  There was a long line at the check-out. = 结账处排着长队。


v. We checked out of the hotel this morning. = 我们今天早上退了房。

v.  Please check out the details before submitting the report. = 请在提交报告之前仔细检查细节。


近义词: (n.)departure, payment; (v.) leave, depart, inspect, examine

反义词: (v.) check in


词性: noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He checks out of the hotel every Sunday. = 他每周日都从酒店退房。
She checks out the groceries at the supermarket. = 她在超市结账买菜。

一般过去时:
They checked out of the motel last night. = 他们昨晚离开了汽车旅馆。
I checked out the new restaurant downtown. = 我查看了市中心那家新开的餐厅。

现在完成时:
We have checked out of the Airbnb. = 我们已经退了Airbnb的房。
She has checked out all the books in the library. = 她已经查看了图书馆所有的书。

过去完成时:
They had checked out of the hotel before the earthquake. = 地震发生前他们已经退了酒店的房。
He had checked out the car thoroughly before buying it. = 他在买车之前已经彻底检查了那辆车。

将来时:
We will check out of the hotel tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将要退房。
They will check out the new software next week. = 他们下周将要检查新的软件。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would check out that museum. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去参观那个博物馆。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
