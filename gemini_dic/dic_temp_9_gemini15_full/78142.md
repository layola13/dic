geophagism:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈfæɡɪzəm/| n. |食土癖，吞土症 |The practice of eating earth, clay, or soil.  This behavior is often observed in pregnant women or individuals with nutritional deficiencies.  |Example:  Geophagism is a common practice in some cultures and can be linked to mineral deficiencies. 食土癖在某些文化中是一种常见的习俗，可能与矿物质缺乏有关。|近义词：clay-eating, earth-eating |反义词：N/A
