regalvanizes: /ˌriːˈɡæl vəˌnaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使重新充满活力；使复兴；重新激励 | To renew or revive the energy or enthusiasm of (something or someone).  It implies a restoration of something that had previously lost its vigor or spirit.  The word combines "re-" (again) and "galvanize" (to shock into action).|


The new marketing campaign regalvanized the struggling company. = 新的营销活动使这家苦苦挣扎的公司重新焕发了活力。

The coach's speech regalvanized the team's spirits before the final game. = 教练的讲话在决赛前重新鼓舞了球队的士气。


近义词: revitalize, reinvigorate, re-energize, resuscitate, refresh


反义词:  de-energize, exhaust, drain, deplete


词性: 动词
