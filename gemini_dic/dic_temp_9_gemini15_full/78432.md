get-together:/ˈɡetˌtuɡɛðər/ | n. |聚会，联欢会 |A casual social gathering of people.  It often implies informality and a relaxed atmosphere. |例句：We're having a get-together at my place next Saturday. = 我们下周六在我家举行一个聚会。

例句：The family had a get-together to celebrate Grandma's birthday. =  家人聚在一起庆祝奶奶的生日。

近义词：gathering, party, meeting, reunion | 反义词：separation, parting
