edgier:/ˈɛdʒiər/| adj.|更尖锐的；更前卫的；更刺激的|More sharp, intense, or exciting;  suggests a greater degree of something already present, often in a positive or edgy way.  It implies a thrilling or slightly dangerous quality.|例句：The new album has a much edgier sound than their previous work. 这张新专辑的声音比他们之前的作品更尖锐。The film's edgier themes explored violence and social unrest. 这部电影更尖锐的主题探讨了暴力和社会动荡。|近义词：sharper, more intense, more exciting, bolder, more radical|反义词：milder, smoother, gentler, more conventional
