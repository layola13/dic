disoxidation:/ˌdɪsˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 脱氧；去氧化作用 | The process of removing oxygen from a substance.  This is often done to prevent oxidation or corrosion.  | Example: The disoxidation of the metal prevented rust formation.  脱氧处理防止了金属生锈。 |近义词：deoxidation, reduction | 反义词：oxidation
