nonfreeman:/ˈnɒnˈfriːmæn/ | n. | 非自由民，奴隶 | A nonfreeman is a person who is not free, typically a slave or serf in historical contexts.  The term emphasizes the lack of legal and social freedoms enjoyed by free individuals.  | Example: In feudal society, nonfreemen constituted a significant portion of the population, bound to the land and their lords.  在封建社会中，非自由民构成了人口的很大一部分，他们被束缚在土地和他们的领主身上。 | 近义词：serf, slave, bondsman | 反义词：freeman, free person | 名词
