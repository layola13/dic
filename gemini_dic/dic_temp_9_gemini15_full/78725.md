giggle:/ˈɡɪɡəl/| v. n.|咯咯地笑，傻笑|A giggle is a short, light laugh, often made by children or people who are amused by something silly or slightly inappropriate.  It implies a somewhat suppressed or nervous laughter. |例句：The children giggled at the clown's funny antics. 孩子们被小丑滑稽的动作逗得咯咯地笑。

例句：She let out a nervous giggle when he asked her to dance. 当他邀请她跳舞时，她紧张地咯咯地笑了起来。


近义词：chuckle, snicker, titter|反义词：sob, weep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She giggles at every joke. = 她对每一个笑话都咯咯地笑。
They giggle during the movie. = 他们在电影放映期间咯咯地笑。


一般过去时:
The baby giggled in its sleep. = 婴儿在睡梦中咯咯地笑。

She had giggled nervously before the interview. = 面试之前，她紧张地咯咯地笑了。
