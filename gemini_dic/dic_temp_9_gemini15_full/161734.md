power-walks:/ˈpaʊər wɔːks/| n. |快走；健步走 |A power walk is a brisk walk, often done for exercise.  It involves a faster pace and more intense effort than a casual stroll.  It's a popular form of cardiovascular exercise. |例句：I go for a power walk every morning to stay fit. 我每天早上快走以保持健康。
She incorporates power walks into her weekly fitness routine. 她把快走融入到她每周的健身计划中。|近义词：brisk walk, fast walk, speed walking |反义词：stroll, saunter

She power-walks in the park. = 她在公园里健步走。
