nonascendant: /ˌnɒnˈæsɛndənt/ | adj. | 非直系祖先的；非祖先的 | Not a direct ancestor.  This term refers to individuals who are related but not in a direct line of ancestry, such as cousins, uncles, aunts, or more distant relatives. | Example:  The historian examined both ascendant and nonascendant lineages to understand the family's history comprehensively.  历史学家检查了直系祖先和非直系祖先的血统，以全面了解家族历史。 |近义词：collateral, unrelated (in the context of ancestry) | 反义词：ascendant
