pervasively: /pərˈveɪsɪvli/ | adv. | 普遍地；渗透地；广泛地 |  widely spread; prevalent throughout; penetrating all aspects |

英文解析: The adverb "pervasively" describes something that is spread throughout something else, affecting or influencing every part. It suggests a complete and thorough influence, often in a subtle or insidious way.

例句:

The influence of social media is pervasively felt in modern society. = 社交媒体的影响在现代社会中无处不在。

Corruption pervasively affected the government's operations. = 腐败普遍影响了政府的运作。


近义词:  widely, extensively, thoroughly, universally, comprehensively

反义词:  locally, sparsely, rarely, minimally


词性: 副词
