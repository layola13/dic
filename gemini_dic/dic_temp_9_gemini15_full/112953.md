lugubrious:/ˈluːɡʊbrɪəs/| adj.|悲伤的，忧郁的，阴沉的|Melancholy; mournful; dismal.  The word suggests a gloomy and exaggerated sadness, often with a theatrical or affected quality. |例句：The lugubrious music perfectly matched the somber mood of the funeral.  这首悲伤的音乐与葬礼的沉闷气氛完美地融合在一起。

例句：His lugubrious expression hinted at the bad news he was about to deliver. 他忧郁的表情暗示了他即将带来的坏消息。


近义词：mournful, melancholy, doleful, dismal, gloomy, somber, depressing

反义词：cheerful, joyful, merry, bright, optimistic, upbeat


词性：形容词
