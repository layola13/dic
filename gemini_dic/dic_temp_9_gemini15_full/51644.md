deviantly:/ˈdiːviəntli/ | adv. | 异常地；偏离地；乖僻地 | In a way that deviates from what is considered normal or acceptable.  It implies a departure from established norms or expectations, often with negative connotations. | 例句：He behaved deviantly, ignoring all social conventions. 他行为异常，无视所有社会习俗。  | 例句：The experiment yielded deviantly high results, prompting further investigation. 实验结果异常的高，促使了进一步的调查。 | 近义词：abnormally, unconventionally, oddly, strangely, atypically | 反义词：normally, conventionally, typically, regularly
