preheats:/priːˈhiːt/| vt.|预热|To heat (something, such as an oven or engine) beforehand to a specified temperature or condition.|例句：Please preheat the oven to 350 degrees before baking the cake.  请在烘焙蛋糕之前将烤箱预热到350度。
例句：The mechanic preheated the engine before starting the car. 机械师在启动汽车之前预热了发动机。|近义词：warm up, heat up|反义词：cool down


USAGE->

He preheats his car engine every morning. = 他每天早上都会预热他的汽车发动机。
