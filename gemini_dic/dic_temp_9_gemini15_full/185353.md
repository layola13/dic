retroactivity:/ˌretrəʊækˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |追溯效力；溯及既往；反向作用 |The quality or state of being retroactive; the extension of the effects of a law, decision, etc., to a period before its enactment or establishment.  |例句：The new law has retroactivity, applying to actions taken before its passage. 这项新法律具有追溯力，适用于其通过之前的行为。

The company's policy change had retroactivity, affecting employees' salaries from the previous year. 公司的政策变更具有追溯力，影响到员工去年的工资。


近义词：backdating, back action,  ex post facto application |反义词：prospectivity, future effect
