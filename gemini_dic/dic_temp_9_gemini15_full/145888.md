overdeveloped:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛvlɒpt/ | adj. |过度发展的；发展过度的 | Excessively developed; beyond the normal or desirable level of development.  It often implies negative consequences resulting from excessive growth or expansion.  | The overdeveloped tourist industry has destroyed the natural beauty of the island.  过度发展的旅游业破坏了岛屿的自然美景。 The city's overdeveloped infrastructure is straining its resources. 这个城市的过度发展的基础设施正在消耗其资源。 | underdeveloped, undeveloped | underdeveloped, undeveloped |
