parasocial:/ˌpærəˈsoʊʃəl/ | adj. |  寄生社交的，准社会关系的 |  Describing a relationship in which one person invests emotional energy, interest, and time in a media persona, but the relationship is one-sided and non-reciprocal.  The media persona is unaware of the individual and does not interact with them in any meaningful way.  | Example:  She developed a parasocial relationship with her favorite YouTuber, spending hours watching their videos and feeling a connection despite the lack of personal interaction.  | 近义词: one-sided, unidirectional, imagined, illusionary | 反义词: reciprocal, mutual, balanced
