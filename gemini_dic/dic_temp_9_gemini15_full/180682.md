regressed:/rɪˈɡrɛst/ | v. | 倒退；退化；回归 | To return to a former or less developed state.  |  The patient regressed to an earlier stage of development after the trauma. 病人在创伤后退回到了发育的早期阶段。 The economy has regressed significantly. 经济已经严重倒退。 | progressed, advanced | progressed, advanced | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He regresses under pressure. 他在压力下退缩。
The company regresses in its technological advancements. 这家公司在技术进步方面倒退了。

一般过去时:
She regressed after the loss of her job. 她失业后变得消沉。
The country regressed to a state of civil war. 这个国家倒退到了内战状态。

现在完成时:
The team has regressed since their last victory. 自从上次获胜以来，球队已经退步了。
The environment has regressed due to pollution. 由于污染，环境已经恶化了。

过去完成时:
He had regressed to childhood behaviors before the therapy started. 在治疗开始之前，他已经退回到了儿童的行为模式。
The society had regressed significantly before the reforms were implemented. 在改革实施之前，社会已经严重倒退了。


将来时:
If we don't act now, the situation will regress further. 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况将会进一步恶化。
The patient's condition is expected to regress unless he follows the treatment plan. 除非病人遵循治疗方案，否则他的病情预计会恶化。

虚拟语气:
If only he hadn't regressed so much, he would have succeeded. 如果他当时没有那么退步，他就会成功了。
I wish the project hadn't regressed to such a state. 我希望这个项目不会退化到这种程度。

被动语态:
The data was regressed against the control variable. 数据根据控制变量进行了回归分析。
The symptoms were regressed with the new medication. 症状在新药的作用下得到了缓解（退化）。


疑问句:
Has the patient regressed since the last assessment? 自上次评估以来，病人的病情是否恶化了？
Did the economy regress after the financial crisis? 金融危机后经济是否倒退了？


否定句:
The child did not regress despite the difficult circumstances. 尽管环境艰难，孩子并没有退步。
The technology has not regressed; it has continued to improve. 这项技术没有倒退，它一直在改进。


PHRASE->

regress to the mean: 回归平均值
regress to childhood: 倒退回童年
regress on a variable:  对变量进行回归分析
multiple regression: 多元回归
logistic regression: 逻辑回归
