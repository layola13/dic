overexplanation:/ˌoʊvərˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 过度解释；解释过多 | The act of explaining something in excessive detail, often to the point of being unclear or unhelpful.  It can also imply a lack of trust in the listener's understanding. | Example:  His overexplanation of the simple instructions only confused the students further.  他的过度解释简单的说明反而让学生们更加困惑。 | 近义词：verbose explanation, excessive detail, superfluity, wordiness | 反义词：conciseness, brevity, succinctness |  名词
