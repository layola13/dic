detracts:/dɪˈtrækt/| vt.|使减损；使降低；转移…的注意力|To reduce or take away from the value, importance, or effectiveness of something.|The loud music detracted from our enjoyment of the movie.  吵闹的音乐影响了我们看电影的乐趣。|The constant interruptions detracted from her concentration.  不断的打扰分散了她的注意力。|近义词：diminish, reduce, impair, lessen|反义词：enhance, improve, augment


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The loud noise detracts from the peace and quiet. = 嘈杂的噪音破坏了宁静。
This minor flaw detracts from the overall beauty of the painting. = 这个小小的瑕疵降低了这幅画的整体美感。


一般过去时:
The scandal detracted from the politician's reputation. = 丑闻损害了这位政治家的名誉。
The bad weather detracted from our enjoyment of the picnic. = 恶劣的天气影响了我们野餐的乐趣。


现在完成时:
The constant criticism has detracted from his confidence. =  持续不断的批评已经削弱了他的信心。
Years of neglect have detracted from the building's value. = 多年的忽视降低了这栋建筑的价值。


过去完成时:
The previous failures had detracted from their enthusiasm for the project. = 之前的失败已经削弱了他们对这个项目的热情。
The earlier setbacks had detracted from the team's overall performance. = 早期的挫折影响了团队的整体表现。


将来时:
The upcoming election will detract from the government's focus on economic issues. =  即将到来的选举将分散政府对经济问题的关注。
Future budget cuts will detract from the quality of education. = 未来的预算削减将降低教育质量。


虚拟语气:
If the noise hadn't detracted from the presentation, it would have been a success. = 如果噪音没有影响演讲，它本来会很成功。
If only the distractions hadn't detracted from her work, she would have finished on time. =  如果干扰没有影响她的工作，她本来就能按时完成。


被动语态:
His credibility was detracted from by the false accusations. = 他可信度因虚假指控而受损。
The beauty of the landscape was detracted from by the litter. =  景色的美丽被垃圾破坏了。


疑问句:
Does the poor lighting detract from the photograph? =  昏暗的灯光是否影响了照片？
Will the negative comments detract from the product's sales? =  负面评论会影响产品的销量吗？


否定句:
The positive feedback did not detract from the team's morale. = 积极的反馈并没有影响团队的士气。
The minor errors did not detract significantly from the overall quality of the work. =  这些小错误并没有显著影响工作的整体质量。


PHRASE->

detract from the beauty = 降低美感
