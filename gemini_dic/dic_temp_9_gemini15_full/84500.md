halwa:/ˈhɑːlvɑː/|/ˈhæl.wɑː/| n. |甜点， हलवा |A sweet confection, typically made from semolina, sugar, ghee (clarified butter), and nuts, varying widely in ingredients and preparation across different cultures.  It is a popular dessert in many parts of the Middle East, South Asia, and the Balkans. | Example:  For dessert, we had delicious halwa made with pistachios.  晚饭后甜点，我们吃了美味的开心果哈尔瓦。|近义词：sweet, dessert, confection |反义词：N/A
