phytoclimates:/ˈfaɪtoʊˌklaɪmɪts/| n. |植物气候|The climates of different regions as they affect plant life.  The study of the relationship between climate and plant distribution and growth. |例句：The study of phytoclimates helps us understand the distribution of plant species across different geographical areas.  研究植物气候有助于我们了解植物物种在不同地理区域的分布。

The diverse phytoclimates of the Amazon rainforest support an incredible variety of plant life. 亚马逊雨林多样化的植物气候支持着种类繁多的植物生命。|近义词：plant climates, vegetation climates |反义词：无
