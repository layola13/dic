cuckoo-clocks:/ˈkʊkuːklɒks/| n. |布谷鸟钟|A cuckoo clock is a type of clock that chimes the number of times corresponding to the hour, using a small carved wooden bird that emerges from a door to make the sound.|例句：The cuckoo clock in the living room chimed eight times, announcing the hour. 客厅里的布谷鸟钟敲响了八下，报时八点。  The antique cuckoo clock was a prized possession, passed down through generations. 这座古董布谷鸟钟是传家宝，代代相传。|近义词：clock, timepiece|反义词：None|名词
