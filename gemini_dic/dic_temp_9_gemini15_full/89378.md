homophobic:/ˌhoʊmoˈfoʊbɪk/| adj.|恐同的，仇视同性恋的|Having or showing a dislike of or prejudice against homosexual people.|His homophobic remarks were met with outrage. 他的恐同言论招致了众怒。|近义词：anti-gay, bigoted|反义词：homophile, tolerant


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is homophobic and refuses to associate with gay people. 他恐同，拒绝与同性恋者交往。
She is homophobic and believes that homosexuality is immoral. 她恐同，认为同性恋是不道德的。


一般过去时:
He was homophobic in his youth, but has since changed his views. 他年轻时恐同，但后来改变了看法。
Society was more homophobic in the past than it is today. 过去社会比现在更恐同。


现在完成时:
He has been homophobic his entire life, and it's unlikely to change. 他一生都恐同，不太可能改变。
Society has become less homophobic in recent decades. 近几十年来，社会变得不那么恐同了。


过去完成时:
Before the movement for LGBTQ+ rights, society had been much more homophobic. 在争取LGBTQ+权利的运动之前，社会更加恐同。
He had been homophobic, but a personal experience changed his perspective. 他曾经恐同，但一次个人经历改变了他的观点。


将来时:
He will likely remain homophobic unless he undergoes a significant change in perspective. 除非他改变观点，否则他很可能会继续恐同。
Hopefully, society will become even less homophobic in the future. 希望将来社会会变得更加不那么恐同。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't homophobic, he would be more accepting of diverse communities. 如果他不恐同，他会更能接受多元化的群体。
I wish he wasn't so homophobic and would try to understand different lifestyles. 我希望他不那么恐同，并尝试理解不同的生活方式。


被动语态:
His homophobic views were challenged by many people. 他的恐同观点受到了许多人的质疑。
Homophobic attitudes are often learned, not inherent. 恐同的态度往往是后天习得的，而不是与生俱来的。


疑问句:
Is he homophobic? 他恐同吗？
Are her comments homophobic? 她的言论是恐同的吗？


否定句:
He is not homophobic; he supports LGBTQ+ rights. 他并不恐同；他支持LGBTQ+权利。
She is not homophobic and treats everyone with respect. 她并不恐同，并且尊重每一个人。
