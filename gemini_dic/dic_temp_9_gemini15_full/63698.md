ephah:/ˈeɪfə/| n.|厄法(古代希伯来容积单位)|An ephah was an ancient Hebrew unit of dry measure, approximately equal to 1.1 bushels or 22 liters.  It was used to measure grain and other dry goods. The exact volume varied slightly depending on the period and location.|The farmer harvested an ephah of wheat.  = 农民收获了一厄法的小麦。  The baker needed several ephahs of flour for his bread. = 面包师需要几厄法的面粉来做面包。|近义词：bushel (approximate)|反义词：None|名词
