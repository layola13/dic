dogwatch:/ˈdɒɡwɒtʃ/ | n. | 看守时间，值夜班时间 | A period of four hours (from 4 to 8 pm and from 8 pm to midnight) during which sailors on a ship were on watch.  This was done to prevent any one watch from becoming too fatigued. | Example: The dog watch was particularly grueling on that stormy night. 那个暴风雨的夜晚，看守时间特别艰难。|近义词：watch, shift, duty |反义词：rest, break
