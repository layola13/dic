noctiluca:/ˌnɒktɪˈluːkə/| n. |夜光虫，夜光藻|A genus of marine dinoflagellates that are bioluminescent, meaning they produce light. They are single-celled organisms and can cause red tides.| Example:  The bioluminescence of *Noctiluca scintillans* created a mesmerizing display in the ocean at night. 夜光藻的生物发光在夜晚创造了令人着迷的海上景象。|近义词:bioluminescent organism, dinoflagellate|反义词:None
