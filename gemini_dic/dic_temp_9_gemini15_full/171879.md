punks:/pʌŋks/ | n. |朋克；小流氓；无赖 |A punk is a young person, typically male, who is regarded as being rebellious, anti-establishment, and often involved in petty crime.  The term can be offensive and is often used derisively. |Example: The punks spray-painted graffiti on the building.  那些朋克在建筑物上喷涂涂鸦。  The police arrested several punks after the riot. 暴乱后警方逮捕了几名小流氓。|近义词：hooligan, delinquent, hoodlum, ruffian |反义词：gentleman, law-abiding citizen |名词
