languished:/ˈlæŋɡwɪʃt/| v.|衰弱；憔悴；苦苦挣扎；消沉|to exist in a state of depression or inactivity|The project languished for lack of funding.  这个项目由于缺乏资金而停滞不前。|The plants languished in the dry soil.  植物在干燥的土壤中枯萎了。|flourished, thrived|thrived, prospered|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He languishes in prison. = 他在监狱里苦苦挣扎。
The economy languishes under the current government. = 在现任政府领导下，经济停滞不前。


一般过去时:
She languished in bed all day. = 她整天躺在床上虚弱无力。
The company languished after the CEO resigned. = 首席执行官辞职后，公司业绩下滑。


现在完成时:
The town has languished since the factory closed. = 自从工厂倒闭以来，小镇就日渐衰败。
He has languished in poverty for years. = 他多年来一直生活在贫困之中。


过去完成时:
The business had languished for months before it finally went bankrupt. = 这家企业在最终破产前已经萧条了几个月。
She had languished in obscurity until her recent success. = 在她最近取得成功之前，她一直默默无闻。


将来时:
Without investment, the industry will languish. = 如果没有投资，这个行业将会衰退。
If he continues to neglect his health, he will languish. = 如果他继续忽视自己的健康，他将会日渐衰弱。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been imprisoned, he wouldn't have languished. = 如果他没有被监禁，他就不会如此痛苦。
I wish my career hadn't languished. = 我希望我的事业没有停滞不前。


被动语态:
The project was languished due to a lack of resources. = 由于缺乏资源，该项目停滞不前。
His hopes were languished by repeated failures. = 他屡屡失败，希望破灭。


疑问句:
Why is the economy languishing? = 为什么经济停滞不前？
Has the project languished because of funding issues? = 项目停滞不前是因为资金问题吗？


否定句:
The business didn't languish; it thrived. = 这家企业并没有衰败，而是蓬勃发展。
The plants didn't languish; they flourished. = 植物没有枯萎，而是茂盛地生长。


PHRASE->

languish in despair  在绝望中消沉
languish in illness  病倒
languish in love  陷入爱河（积极含义，暗指痴迷的状态）
