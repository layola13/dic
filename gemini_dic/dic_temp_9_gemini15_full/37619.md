communions:/kəˈmjuːnjənz/ | n. |圣餐；共享；交谊；分享 |The act of sharing or participation in something, especially a religious rite or a feeling of fellowship.  It often refers to the Eucharist in Christianity. |例句：The church holds weekly communions. 教堂每周举行圣餐仪式。

例句：They felt a sense of communion with nature. 他们感到与大自然的融洽。|近义词： fellowship, sharing, participation, sacrament |反义词：separation, isolation, estrangement
