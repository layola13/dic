hexagram:/ˈhɛksəˌɡræm/| n. |六角星形；八卦；六芒星|A six-pointed star; a figure composed of two equilateral triangles superimposed so that their vertices interlock; in I Ching, one of the 64 combinations of six lines, each line being either broken or unbroken, used in divination.|例句：The hexagram symbolizes protection and good fortune.  六芒星象征着保护和好运。

The hexagram in the I Ching reading suggested a time of change. 易经占卜中的卦象暗示着变革的时期。|近义词：six-pointed star,  symbol|反义词：N/A
