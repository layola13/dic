hemipter:/ˈhɛmɪptər/| n. |半翅目昆虫|Hemiptera is an order of insects, commonly known as true bugs.  They are characterized by their piercing-sucking mouthparts, often used to feed on plant sap or the blood of animals. Many hemipterans possess forewings that are partially hardened (hemelytra) and partially membranous.  This order includes a wide variety of insects, such as cicadas, aphids, leafhoppers, and bed bugs.|Example:  The researcher studied the feeding habits of various hemipteran species. 研究人员研究了各种半翅目昆虫的摄食习惯。  Many hemipterans are significant agricultural pests. 许多半翅目昆虫是重要的农业害虫。|近义词:true bug|反义词:无
