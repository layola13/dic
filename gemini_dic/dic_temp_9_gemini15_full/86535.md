held-in:/heldˈɪn/| adj.|压抑的；忍住的；被抑制的|Held-in refers to something that is restrained, suppressed, or kept under control.  It often describes emotions or reactions that are not outwardly expressed. |例句：She had a held-in laugh, barely suppressing her amusement. 她忍住笑，几乎抑制不住她的娱乐。

例句：He tried to maintain a held-in composure despite the stressful situation. 尽管情况紧张，他还是努力保持镇静。


近义词：suppressed, restrained, contained, repressed, bottled up|反义词：expressed, unrestrained, uncontained, overt, free|词性:形容词
