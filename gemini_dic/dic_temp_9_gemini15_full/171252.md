pteranodontia: /ˌtɛrəˈnɒdɒntiə/| n. |翼手龙类|A clade of extinct pterosaurs characterized by their large size, relatively short tails, and toothless beaks.  Pteranodontia includes the well-known genus *Pteranodon* and other closely related pterosaurs. They were primarily flying reptiles that lived during the Late Cretaceous period.  |The discovery of a new *Pteranodon* species expanded our understanding of pteranodontia.  翼手龙类的一个新物种的发现扩展了我们对翼手龙类的理解。|  近义词: (Lacking direct synonyms due to being a clade;  related terms include "pterosaurs", "pterodactyloids") |反义词: (No direct antonyms)|名词
