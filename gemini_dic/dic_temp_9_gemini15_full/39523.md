conquerers:/ˈkɒŋkərərz/| n.|征服者，胜利者|People or groups who have successfully taken control of a territory or people by force.  They are often associated with military might and the imposition of their will on the conquered. |例句：The Roman conquerors built roads and aqueducts throughout their empire. 罗马征服者在他们的帝国各地修建了道路和渡槽。

The Mongol conquerors spread their influence across a vast swathe of Asia.  蒙古征服者将他们的影响力传播到亚洲广阔的地区。|近义词：victors, vanquishers, subduers|反义词：conquered, defeated, subjugated|名词
