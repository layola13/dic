reattiring:/ˌriːˈtaɪərɪŋ/| adj.| 退休的；隐退的；害羞的；孤僻的 |Retiring describes someone who is shy, quiet, and prefers to avoid social interaction.  It can also refer to the act of withdrawing from active service or a position, typically a job. |例句：He led a retiring life after his retirement from the army. 他从军队退休后过着隐居的生活。

She has a retiring personality and rarely attends social gatherings. 她性格内向，很少参加社交聚会。|近义词：shy, reserved, withdrawn, secluded |反义词：outgoing, extroverted, sociable
