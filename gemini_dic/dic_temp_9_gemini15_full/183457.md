republicans:/rɪˈpʌblɪkənz/ | n. | 共和党人；共和主义者 | Members of the Republican Party, a major political party in the United States.  The term can also refer to supporters of a republican form of government. | Example: Many Republicans voted against the new healthcare bill. 许多共和党人投票反对新的医疗法案。  The senator is a staunch republican, believing in the power of the people.  这位参议员是一位坚定的共和主义者，相信人民的力量。|近义词：GOP members (Grand Old Party), conservatives (often, but not always) |反义词：Democrats
