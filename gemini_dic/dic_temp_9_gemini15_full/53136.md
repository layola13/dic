diplomacy:/dɪˈpləʊməsi/| n. |外交；外交手腕；策略；圆滑；|The art and practice of conducting negotiations between representatives of states. It involves skillful communication and compromise to achieve peaceful resolutions.  |例句：The success of the peace talks depended heavily on the diplomacy of the negotiators. 和谈的成功很大程度上取决于谈判代表的外交手腕。|例句：He handled the difficult situation with considerable diplomacy. 他以相当圆滑的手腕处理了这个棘手的情况。|近义词：negotiation, statecraft, tact, finesse |反义词：confrontation, aggression, bluntness
