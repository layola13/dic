gunshots:/ˈɡʌnʃɒts/| n. |枪声；枪击声 |The sound of gunfire; the act of firing guns.  |例句：We heard several gunshots in the night.  我们夜里听到几声枪响。

例句：The police investigated the scene of the gunshots. 警方调查了枪击案现场。

近义词：shots, gunfire, explosions (if caused by firearms)

反义词：silence
