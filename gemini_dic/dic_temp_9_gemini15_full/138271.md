notables:/ˈnoʊtəbəlz/ | n. | 重要人物；值得注意的事物 |  Notable people are those who are well-known and respected for their achievements or contributions in a particular field.  Notables can also refer to things that are worthy of attention or notice. | 例句：The conference attracted many notables from the business world.  这场会议吸引了许多商界的知名人士。

The city's notables gathered to discuss the new development plan.  城市的要人们聚集在一起讨论新的发展计划。


近义词：celebrities, dignitaries, luminaries, prominent figures, significant events


反义词：unknowns, insignificant things, obscure individuals
