cashew:/ˈkæʃuː/| n. |腰果|A kidney-shaped edible seed of a tropical American tree, eaten roasted or used in sweets and curries.  It is technically a drupe, meaning it has a hard shell containing a seed.|例句：I love to eat roasted cashews as a snack.  我喜欢吃烤腰果作为零食。

例句：She used cashew nuts in her delicious curry. 她在她美味的咖喱中使用了腰果。

近义词：None (it's a specific type of nut)

反义词：None (no direct antonym)

词性:Noun
