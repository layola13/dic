silt:/sɪlt/| n.|淤泥，细沙|Fine sand, clay, or other material carried by running water and deposited as sediment, especially in a riverbed or harbor.|The river silt was rich in nutrients, making the land fertile.|近义词：mud, sediment, alluvium|反义词：gravel, rock


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river silt carries valuable minerals. = 河流淤泥携带宝贵的矿物质。
Silt deposits accumulate in the harbor. = 淤泥沉积物在港口堆积。


一般过去时:
The flood deposited a thick layer of silt. = 洪水沉积了一层厚厚的淤泥。
Farmers used the river silt to enrich their fields. = 农民们用河里的淤泥来肥沃他们的田地。


现在完成时:
The dam has trapped a significant amount of silt. = 水坝已经截留了大量的淤泥。
Years of erosion have deposited silt along the coastline. = 多年的侵蚀已在海岸线上沉积了淤泥。


过去完成时:
Before the dam was built, the silt had filled the riverbed. = 在建坝之前，淤泥已经填满了河床。
The heavy rains had washed away much of the topsoil and silt. = 大雨冲走了大部分表层土壤和淤泥。


将来时:
The next flood will likely deposit more silt. = 下一次洪水可能会沉积更多的淤泥。
They predict that the silt will clog the irrigation canals. = 他们预测淤泥会堵塞灌溉渠。


虚拟语气:
If there were less silt, the river would flow faster. = 如果淤泥少一些，河水就会流得更快。
I wish the silt wouldn't clog the drainage system. = 我希望淤泥不会堵塞排水系统。


被动语态:
The harbor is regularly dredged to remove silt. = 港口定期进行疏浚以清除淤泥。
The fields were enriched by the silt deposited by the river. = 田地因河流沉积的淤泥而肥沃起来。


疑问句:
Is the silt causing problems for the irrigation system? = 淤泥是否给灌溉系统造成了问题？
How much silt is deposited annually in the reservoir? = 水库每年沉积多少淤泥？


否定句:
The water isn't clear; it's full of silt. = 水不清澈；它充满了淤泥。
The soil doesn't contain much silt. = 土壤里不含多少淤泥。
