jumping:/ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ/ | v. |跳跃；跳动；骤增 |The act of propelling oneself into the air using one's legs; a sudden increase or rise. |例句：The children were jumping for joy. 孩子们高兴地跳了起来。|例句：The price of gas has been jumping lately. 近期汽油价格一直在上涨。|近义词：leaping, hopping, soaring |反义词：stillness, standing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jumps rope every morning. = 他每天早上跳绳。
The rabbit jumps across the field. = 兔子跳过田野。

一般过去时:
She jumped over the fence. = 她跳过栅栏。
The horse jumped the hurdle. = 马跳过了障碍物。

现在完成时:
I have jumped out of a plane before. = 我以前跳过伞。
He has jumped to the wrong conclusion. = 他得出了错误的结论。

过去完成时:
The dog had jumped the wall before the owner arrived. = 主人到达之前，狗已经跳过了墙。
They had jumped to conclusions before hearing all the facts. = 他们在听到所有事实之前就下了结论。

将来时:
I will jump into the pool later. = 我待会儿要跳进游泳池。
He will jump at the opportunity. = 他将会抓住这个机会。

虚拟语气:
If I were younger, I would jump higher. = 如果我年轻一些，我会跳得更高。
I wish I could jump over that building. = 我希望我能跳过那栋楼。

被动语态:
The hurdle was jumped by the horse. = 障碍物被马跳过了。
The conclusion was jumped to too quickly. = 结论下得太快了。

疑问句:
Can he jump high? = 他能跳得很高吗？
Did she jump the queue? = 她插队了吗？

否定句:
He doesn't jump very high. = 他跳不高。
She didn't jump to conclusions. = 她没有贸然下结论。


PHRASE->

jump on the bandwagon = 赶时髦
jump at the chance = 抓住机会
