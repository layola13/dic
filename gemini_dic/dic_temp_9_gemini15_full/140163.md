offense:/ˈɒfɛns/ /əˈfɛns/| n. v.|冒犯；犯罪；进攻；攻击|The act of attacking someone or something; a violation of a law or rule; something that causes someone to feel upset, angry, or insulted.  |例句：He committed a serious offense. 他犯了重罪。
例句：Her remarks caused great offense. 她的言论引起了极大的冒犯。|近义词：attack, crime, assault, transgression, insult|反义词：defense, protection, compliment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commits many offenses. = 他犯下许多罪行。
The team launches an offense. = 球队发起进攻。

一般过去时:
The soldier committed an offense against the enemy. = 这位士兵对敌人犯下了罪行。
They launched a surprise offense. = 他们发起了突然袭击。

现在完成时:
The criminal has committed several offenses. = 这个罪犯已经犯下几起罪行。
The team has mounted a strong offense this season. = 这个赛季球队发起了强有力的进攻。

过去完成时:
The police had already identified the offender before the offense was committed. = 在罪行发生之前，警方已经确定了罪犯。
The team had planned a complex offense before the game. = 球队在比赛前策划了一套复杂的进攻战术。

将来时:

He will likely commit further offenses if not apprehended. = 如果不被逮捕，他很可能会继续犯罪。


虚拟语气:
