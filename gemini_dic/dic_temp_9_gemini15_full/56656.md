dons:/dɒnz/ | v. |穿上；戴上|To put on (clothing), especially in a formal or ceremonial way.  |He dons his tuxedo for the gala. 他穿上燕尾服参加盛会。|put on, assume, attire |take off, remove |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dons a new suit every morning. = 他每天早上都穿一套新西装。
She dons her glasses before reading. = 她看书前戴上眼镜。

一般过去时:
The knight donned his armor before the battle. = 骑士在战斗前穿上了盔甲。
She donned a beautiful dress for the party. = 她为了参加派对穿了一件漂亮的裙子。

现在完成时:
He has donned his winter coat for the cold weather. = 由于天气寒冷，他已经穿上了冬衣。
She has donned a new hat to match her outfit. = 她戴了一顶新帽子来搭配她的衣服。

过去完成时:
He had donned his uniform before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前，他已经穿上了制服。
She had donned her best jewelry for the occasion. = 她为了这个场合戴上了她最好的珠宝。

将来时:
He will don his graduation gown for the ceremony. = 他将在典礼上穿上毕业礼服。
She will don a special costume for the Halloween party. = 她将在万圣节派对上穿一件特殊的服装。

虚拟语气:
If I were going to a wedding, I would don my finest suit. = 如果我去参加婚礼，我会穿上我最好的西装。
I wish I could don a superhero costume and fly. = 我真希望我能穿上超级英雄的服装飞翔。


被动语态:
The uniform was donned by the soldier before the parade. =  士兵在阅兵式之前穿上了制服。
The crown was donned by the queen during the coronation. = 女王在加冕典礼上戴上了王冠。


疑问句:
Will he don the traditional costume? = 他会穿上传统的服装吗？
What will she don for the interview? = 她面试会穿什么？


否定句:
He didn't don his helmet before riding his motorcycle. = 他骑摩托车之前没有戴头盔。
She wouldn't don that revealing dress. = 她不会穿那件暴露的衣服。
