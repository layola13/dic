cosseting:/ˈkɒsɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 娇惯的；溺爱的；呵护备至的 |  The act of treating someone with excessive care and attention, often to the point of spoiling them. It implies a shielding from hardship or responsibility. |例句：The cosseting environment of the boarding school did not prepare the students for the realities of the outside world.  娇惯的寄宿学校环境并没有让学生们为外部世界的现实做好准备。


The cosseting care she received after her surgery helped her recover quickly. 她手术后受到的精心呵护帮助她快速康复。|近义词：pampering, spoiling, indulging, cherishing |反义词：neglecting, neglecting, harsh, strict |词性:形容词
