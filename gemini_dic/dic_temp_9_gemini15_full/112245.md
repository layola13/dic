looter:/ˈluːtər/| n. |掠夺者，洗劫者 |A looter is a person who steals goods, especially during a riot or war.  |例句：Looters ransacked the shops after the earthquake. 地震后，抢劫者洗劫了商店。
例句：The city was overrun by looters after the hurricane. 飓风过后，城市被抢劫者淹没了。|近义词：thief, robber, plunderer |反义词：protector, benefactor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loots abandoned buildings. 他洗劫被遗弃的建筑物。
She often loots online games. 她经常在网络游戏中掠夺资源。

一般过去时:
They looted the museum during the war. 战争期间他们洗劫了博物馆。
The soldiers looted the village. 士兵们洗劫了村庄。

现在完成时:
The rebels have looted several towns. 叛军已经洗劫了几个城镇。
Looters have taken everything of value. 抢劫者已经拿走了所有值钱的东西。

过去完成时:
They had looted the bank before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，他们已经洗劫了银行。
The city had been looted extensively before the army arrived.  军队到达之前，城市已经被大肆洗劫。

将来时:
He will loot the store if he gets the chance. 如果有机会，他会抢劫这家商店。
They will likely loot the houses if the power goes out. 如果停电，他们很可能会洗劫民宅。

虚拟语气:
If he were a looter, he would be in jail. 如果他是抢劫犯，他会坐牢。
I wish they hadn’t looted the shops. 我真希望他们没有洗劫那些商店。

被动语态:
The shops were looted by angry protesters. 商店被愤怒的抗议者洗劫一空。
The city was looted extensively during the civil unrest. 在内乱期间，这座城市遭到大规模洗劫。

疑问句:
Who looted the house? 谁洗劫了这所房子？
Were the stores looted last night? 商店昨晚被洗劫了吗？

否定句:
He didn't loot the store; he just borrowed some items. 他没有洗劫商店，他只是借了一些东西。
The soldiers did not loot the village. 士兵们没有洗劫村庄。
