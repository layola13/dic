investigate: /ɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪt/ | vt. |调查；研究；审查 | To carefully examine the facts of a situation, crime, etc. to find out the truth. |例句：The police are investigating the murder. 警方正在调查这起谋杀案。|例句：Scientists are investigating the causes of cancer. 科学家们正在研究癌症的病因。|近义词：examine, explore, inquire into |反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police investigate the crime scene carefully. = 警方仔细调查了犯罪现场。
Scientists investigate the effects of climate change. = 科学家们正在研究气候变化的影响。

一般过去时:
The detective investigated the suspicious package. = 侦探调查了那个可疑包裹。
The committee investigated the allegations of misconduct. = 委员会调查了不当行为的指控。

现在完成时:
They have investigated the matter thoroughly. = 他们已经彻底调查了此事。
The authorities have investigated several similar cases. = 当局已经调查了几个类似的案件。

过去完成时:
The journalist had investigated the story for months before publishing it. = 这名记者在发表报道前对这个故事进行了几个月的调查。
The investigators had investigated the lead before they discovered the truth. = 调查人员在发现真相之前已经调查了这条线索。

将来时:
The team will investigate the problem further next week. = 该团队将在下周进一步调查这个问题。
The prosecutor will investigate the witness's testimony. = 检察官将调查证人的证词。

虚拟语气:
If they investigated the matter properly, they would find the truth. = 如果他们妥善调查此事，他们就能找到真相。
I wish the government had investigated the issue sooner. = 我希望政府早点调查这个问题。

被动语态:
The case is being investigated by the FBI. = 此案正在由联邦调查局调查。
The accident was investigated thoroughly. = 事故得到了彻底的调查。

疑问句:
Are they investigating the cause of the fire? = 他们正在调查火灾的原因吗？
Will the company investigate the customer's complaint? = 公司会调查顾客的投诉吗？

否定句:
The police did not investigate the incident properly. = 警方没有妥善调查这起事件。
They have not yet investigated all the possibilities. = 他们还没有调查所有可能性。


PHRASE->
investigate a crime = 调查一起犯罪案件
investigate a complaint = 调查一项投诉
investigate a theory = 调查一个理论
investigate a problem = 调查一个问题
investigate a claim = 调查一项说法


词源:
From Latin *investigāre, from vestigium ("track, trace, footprint").  The prefix "in-" indicates intensification or thoroughness.  Thus, the word originally meant to "track down" or "trace thoroughly."
