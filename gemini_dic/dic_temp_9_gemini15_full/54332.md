disheartenments:/dɪsˈhɑːrtnmənts/ | n. | 沮丧；灰心；失望 | The feeling of being discouraged or losing hope.  It often refers to multiple instances or a general state of discouragement.  |  例句：His repeated failures led to a series of disheartening events and deep dishearteningments. 他屡屡失败导致一系列令人沮丧的事件和深深的失望。

The constant negativity caused widespread dishearteningments among the team members.  持续的消极情绪在团队成员中造成了普遍的沮丧。| 近义词：disappointments, discouragements, despairs | 反义词：encouragements, inspirations, hopes |  词性：名词
