floatplane:/ˈfloʊtpleɪn/| n. |水上飞机|A floatplane is an aircraft that is designed to take off and land on water.  It uses floats instead of wheels for landing gear. |Example: The floatplane landed gracefully on the calm lake.水上飞机优雅地降落在平静的湖面上。|近义词： seaplane |反义词： landplane
