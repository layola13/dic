conquerable: /ˈkɒŋkərəbl/ | /ˈkɑːŋkərəbl/ | adj. | 可征服的；可克服的 |  Able to be conquered or overcome.  It suggests that something, whether a territory, a problem, or a challenge, can be defeated or mastered. |


例句：

The enemy fortress, once thought impregnable, proved to be conquerable.  =  曾经被认为坚不可摧的敌方堡垒，证明是可以征服的。

The seemingly insurmountable task of writing a novel proved conquerable with dedication and hard work. = 看似难以克服的小说写作任务，通过奉献和努力工作证明是可以克服的。


近义词：subduable, vincible, surmountable


反义词：insurmountable, unconquerable, invincible


词性：形容词
