sirup:/ˈsɪrəp/| n. |糖浆，糖水 |A sweet, viscous liquid made by dissolving sugar in water or other liquid, often used as a sweetener or flavoring agent.  |Example:  I added some syrup to my pancakes. 我往我的煎饼上加了一些糖浆。

|Example: The bartender made a cocktail with fruit syrup. 酒保用水果糖浆调制了一杯鸡尾酒。|近义词：syrup, molasses, honey |反义词：None (no direct antonym, but opposites could include things like "dryness", "unsweetened")|名词
