mensch:/mɛnʃ/| n. |好人，正直的人，有担当的人 |A mensch is a person of integrity and honor, someone who is kind, compassionate, and responsible.  They are often seen as role models and are deeply respected by others.  The term carries a strong positive connotation and implies a sense of moral uprightness and trustworthiness.|He's a real mensch; always willing to lend a hand.|近义词：good person, honorable person, upright person |反义词：scoundrel, villain, knave|名词
