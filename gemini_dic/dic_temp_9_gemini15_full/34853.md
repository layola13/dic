clinkers:/ˈklɪŋkərz/| n.|炉渣，熔渣；烧结物；（尤指）劣质砖；失败的作品|The clinkers are the hard, glassy residue left after the burning of fuel, such as coal or wood, in a furnace or boiler.  They are often composed of ash and partially melted minerals. Clinkers can also refer to poorly made bricks or other products, implying a substandard quality resulting from a flawed process.  The term can also be used metaphorically to describe something of poor quality or a failed attempt.|例句：The furnace was clogged with clinkers.  炉子被炉渣堵住了。

The artist discarded the painting, calling it a clinker.  艺术家丢弃了这幅画，称之为失败之作。|近义词：dross, slag, refuse, rubble, rejects|反义词：masterpiece, gem, treasure
