reaudit:/ˌriːˈɔːdɪt/| v.|复审，重新审计|To audit again; to subject to a second audit.  It implies a more thorough or detailed examination than the initial audit. |例句：The company decided to reaudit its financial records after discovering inconsistencies. 公司在发现不一致之处后决定重新审计其财务记录。

例句：The external auditor recommended a reaudit of the internal control systems. 外部审计师建议对内部控制系统进行复审。


近义词：re-examine, review, reinspect|反义词：approve (in the context of accepting the initial audit without further review)|词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reaudit the accounts annually. = 他们每年对账目进行复审。
The team reaudits the data for accuracy. = 团队复审数据以确保准确性。


一般过去时:
We reaudited the project's budget last month. = 我们上个月对项目的预算进行了复审。
The firm reaudited the financial statements. = 公司对财务报表进行了复审。


现在完成时:
They have reaudited the company's books three times this year. = 今年他们已经三次对公司的账簿进行了复审。
The government has reaudited the tax returns of several high-profile individuals. = 政府已经对几位知名人士的纳税申报进行了复审。


过去完成时:
The auditors had reaudited the accounts before the deadline. = 审计师在截止日期前已经对账目进行了复审。
The management had reaudited the expenses before presenting the report. = 管理层在提交报告之前已经对支出进行了复审。


将来时:
We will reaudit the financial statements next quarter. = 我们将在下个季度对财务报表进行复审。
The company will reaudit its processes to improve efficiency. = 公司将重新审计其流程以提高效率。


虚拟语气:
If we had time, we would reaudit the entire system. = 如果我们有时间，我们将对整个系统进行复审。
If the irregularities were more serious, they would have reaudited everything. = 如果不规则情况更严重，他们会对所有东西进行复审。


被动语态:
The accounts were reaudited by an independent firm. = 账目由一家独立公司进行了复审。
The data will be reaudited before publication. = 数据将在出版前进行复审。


疑问句:
Will the accounts be reaudited? = 账目会进行复审吗？
Have the financial statements been reaudited? = 财务报表已经复审了吗？


否定句:
They did not reaudit the accounts this year. = 他们今年没有对账目进行复审。
The company will not reaudit the project unless necessary. = 除非必要，公司不会对项目进行复审。

reaudit the process = 复审流程
