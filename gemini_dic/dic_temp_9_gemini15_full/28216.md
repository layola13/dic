caprylic:/kæˈprɪlɪk/| adj.|辛酸的；辛酸味的；| Relating to or denoting caprylic acid, a saturated fatty acid found in coconut and palm kernel oils.  It has a characteristic strong, unpleasant odor. |The soap contained caprylic acid, giving it a distinctive scent.|近义词：octanoic (referring to the chemical name)|反义词：sweet, pleasant (in terms of scent)
