pyroligneous:/ˌpaɪrəˈlɪnɪəs/| adj.|焦木的；木焦油的 |  Derived from the pyrolysis of wood, referring to the acidic, dark brown liquid obtained by the destructive distillation of wood.  It contains acetic acid, methanol, and other organic compounds. | The pyroligneous acid was collected and used to make vinegar. 焦木酸被收集起来用来制作醋。|  acetous, empyreumatic |  (No direct antonym, but "pure" or "unadulterated" could be considered opposites in the context of wood distillation products.)|adj.
