dictature:/ˈdɪktətʃər/| n.|独裁统治，专政|The exercise of absolute power, especially by a ruler, without constitutional constraints.|例句：The country suffered under decades of brutal dictatorship.  这个国家在残酷的独裁统治下遭受了几十年的苦难。|例句：His rule was characterized by a ruthless dictatorship and the suppression of dissent. 他的统治以残酷的独裁和压制异见为特征。|近义词：autocracy, tyranny, despotism|反义词：democracy, republic|名词
