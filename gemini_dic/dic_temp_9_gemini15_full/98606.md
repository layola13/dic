instructions:/ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz/| n.|指示，说明，指令|Instructions are directions or orders given to someone.  They tell someone what to do.|Please follow the instructions carefully. 请仔细按照说明操作。|directions, guidelines, commands|disobedience, neglect|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The instructions say to add water first. = 说明书上说先加水。
These instructions are very clear. = 这些说明非常清晰。

一般过去时:
The teacher gave clear instructions. = 老师给了清晰的指示。
I followed the instructions carefully. = 我仔细地按照说明做了。

现在完成时:
Have you read the instructions yet? = 你已经读过说明了吗？
I have followed all the instructions. = 我已经按照所有说明做了。

过去完成时:
I had already read the instructions before I started. = 我开始之前已经读过说明了。
She had followed the instructions perfectly. = 她已经完美地按照说明做了。

将来时:
I will follow the instructions tomorrow. = 我明天将按照说明操作。
The instructions will be updated next week. = 说明书下周将更新。


虚拟语气:

It would be helpful if the instructions were clearer. = 如果说明更清晰一些就好了。


被动语态:

否定句:
