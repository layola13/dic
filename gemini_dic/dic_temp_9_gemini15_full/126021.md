mussed:/mʌst/| adj.|凌乱的；弄皱的；蓬乱的|Slightly disordered or disarranged; rumpled.  The word often refers to hair or clothing that is not neatly arranged.|例句：His hair was mussed from the wind.  他的头发被风吹乱了。|例句：The sheets were all mussed.  床单都皱巴巴的。|近义词：rumpled, disheveled, tousled, unkempt|反义词：neat, tidy, orderly
