gassily:/ˈɡæsɪli/| adv.|喧闹地；喋喋不休地；轻浮地 |  Characterized by excessive talkativeness or boisterousness; in a frivolous or showy manner.  |例句：He spoke gassily about his latest business venture. 他夸夸其谈地谈论着他最新的商业冒险。

例句：She dressed gassily, attracting unwanted attention. 她衣着轻浮，吸引了不必要的注意。

近义词：boisterously, noisily, frivolously, showily, loudly

反义词：quietly, calmly, subtly, modestly
