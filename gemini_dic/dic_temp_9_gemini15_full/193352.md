segments:/ˈsɛɡmənts/ | n. | 部分，片段，节 | Pieces or portions of something larger; sections.  | The book is divided into several segments. 这本书被分成几个部分。| parts, sections, portions | whole, entirety | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is divided into several segments. = 项目被分成几个部分。
This segment of the highway is under construction. = 这段高速公路正在施工。

一般过去时:
The teacher divided the class into smaller segments for the activity. = 老师为了活动把班级分成更小的部分。
We analyzed each segment of the data separately. = 我们分别分析了数据的每个部分。

现在完成时:
The company has completed several segments of the pipeline. = 公司已经完成了管道项目的几个部分。
We have already analyzed the first three segments of the report. = 我们已经分析了报告的前三个部分。

过去完成时:
They had finished all the segments of the project before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前完成了项目的所有部分。
Before the presentation, she had revised all segments of her speech. = 在演示文稿之前，她修改了她演讲的所有部分。

将来时:
We will divide the presentation into three main segments. = 我们将把演示文稿分成三个主要部分。
They will analyze each segment of the market carefully. = 他们将仔细分析市场的每个部分。

虚拟语气:
If the segments were longer, the analysis would be easier. = 如果片段更长，分析会更容易。
I wish the segments were more clearly defined. = 我希望这些部分定义得更清晰。

被动语态:
The data was divided into meaningful segments for the study. = 数据被分成有意义的部分用于研究。
The video is segmented into chapters for easy navigation. = 视频被分割成章节以便于导航。

疑问句:
How many segments does the presentation have? = 演示文稿有多少部分？
Into how many segments is the data divided? = 数据被分成多少部分？

否定句:
The data was not divided into segments. = 数据没有被分成部分。
The report did not contain all the necessary segments. = 报告没有包含所有必要的章节。
