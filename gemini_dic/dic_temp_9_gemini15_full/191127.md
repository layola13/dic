scapiform:/ˈskæpɪfɔːrm/| adj.|  scaphoid 形的；舟状的 | Resembling a scaphoid bone in shape;  shaped like a boat or a short curved blade.  It is used to describe objects or structures that have a boat-like or curved, somewhat concave shape. |例句：The scapiform leaves of the plant were easily identifiable.  植物的舟状叶很容易辨认。

The petals of the flower had a distinctive scapiform structure. 这朵花的花瓣具有独特的舟状结构。


近义词：boat-shaped, navicular,  lunate
