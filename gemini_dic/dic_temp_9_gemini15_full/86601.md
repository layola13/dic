heliolite:/ˈhiːliəʊlaɪt/| n. |日光石，太阳石|A variety of feldspar exhibiting aventurescence, a shimmering effect caused by the reflection of light from tiny inclusions within the stone.  It's typically orange-red or golden in color.|例句：The heliolite pendant shimmered beautifully in the sunlight.  日光石吊坠在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：aventurine, sunstone|反义词：无
