carcinogenic:/ˌkɑːrsɪnəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|致癌的；致癌性的|Causing cancer or likely to cause cancer.|例：Smoking is carcinogenic. 吸烟致癌。  The study found that the chemical is carcinogenic to rats. 研究发现这种化学物质对老鼠具有致癌性。|近义词：cancer-causing, oncogenic|反义词：non-carcinogenic
