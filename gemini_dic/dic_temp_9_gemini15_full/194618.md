self-teaching:/selfˈtiːtʃɪŋ/| adj.| 自学的；自己教自己的 |  Relating to or characterized by teaching oneself.  It implies learning independently, without formal instruction from a teacher or institution. | Example:  She pursued self-teaching in order to master Japanese calligraphy.  她为了掌握日本书法而进行自学。| 近义词：independent learning, autodidactic | 反义词：formal instruction, classroom learning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is self-teaching himself programming. = 他正在自学编程。
She is self-teaching Mandarin through online courses. = 她通过在线课程自学普通话。


一般过去时:
He self-taught himself guitar. = 他自学了吉他。
She self-taught French using apps and books. = 她使用应用程序和书籍自学了法语。


现在完成时:
He has self-taught himself several languages. = 他自学了几门外语。
She has self-taught herself how to code websites. = 她自学了如何编写网站代码。


过去完成时:
He had self-taught himself calculus before entering university. = 他在大学入学前自学了微积分。
She had self-taught herself piano for several years before taking lessons. = 在上课之前，她自学钢琴已经好几年了。


将来时:
He will self-teach himself Python next semester. = 下学期他将自学Python。
She will self-teach herself to bake a cake for her birthday. = 她将在生日那天自学烘焙蛋糕。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would self-teach myself astrophysics. = 如果我有更多时间，我会自学天体物理学。
I wish I could self-teach myself to play the violin. = 我希望我能自学小提琴。


被动语态:
The new software is self-taught through tutorials. = 新软件可以通过教程自学。  (Note: Passive voice is less common with self-teaching)
This skill is not easily self-taught. = 这项技能不容易自学。(Note: Passive voice is less common with self-teaching)


疑问句:
Is she self-teaching Spanish? = 她正在自学西班牙语吗？
Are you self-teaching for the exam? = 你正在为考试自学吗？


否定句:
He is not self-teaching; he's taking a class. = 他没有自学；他正在上课。
She did not self-teach; she hired a tutor. = 她没有自学；她请了家教。
