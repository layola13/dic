predominatingly:/prɪˈdɒmɪneɪtɪŋli/| adv.|主要地，卓越地，支配性地|This adverb describes something that is primarily or chiefly characterized by a particular feature or quality. It suggests a significant or overwhelming dominance.  |例句：The city's architecture is predominantly Victorian.  这座城市建筑主要为维多利亚风格。|例句：The meeting was predominantly focused on budget issues. 会议主要集中在预算问题上。|近义词：mainly, chiefly, primarily, largely, mostly|反义词：minimally, insignificantly, subordinately|无
