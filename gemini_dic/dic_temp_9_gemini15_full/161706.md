powdered:/ˈpaʊdɚd/| adj.|粉状的，成粉末状的|Reduced to a fine dust or powder.  Something that has been ground into a fine powder.|例句：The powdered sugar is perfect for dusting cakes.  = 糖粉很适合用来撒在蛋糕上。
例句：He added powdered milk to his coffee. = 他在咖啡里加了奶粉。|近义词：ground, pulverized, milled|反义词：whole, intact


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The milk is powdered. = 牛奶是粉状的。
The spice is powdered finely. = 香料被细细研磨成粉末。


一般过去时:
She powdered her nose. = 她扑了粉。
They powdered the sugar. = 他们把糖磨成粉。


现在完成时:
We have powdered the herbs for the stew. = 我们已经把药草磨成粉末用来做炖菜。
They have powdered the milk for the baby. = 他们已经把牛奶磨成粉末给婴儿喝。


过去完成时:
She had powdered her face before going to the party. = 她去参加派对之前已经扑过粉了。
The chef had powdered the spices before adding them to the dish. = 厨师在把香料添加到菜肴之前已经把它们磨成粉末了。


将来时:
