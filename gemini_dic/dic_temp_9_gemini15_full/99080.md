interbasin:/ˌɪntərˈbeɪsən/| n. |流域间的，跨流域的|Relating to or involving more than one drainage basin.  It often refers to water transfer or ecological connections between different watersheds. |例句：The interbasin water transfer project aims to alleviate water scarcity in the arid region. 跨流域调水项目旨在缓解干旱地区的缺水问题。

例句：The study investigated the ecological impacts of interbasin water diversions. 该研究调查了跨流域调水对生态的影响。


近义词：transbasin, cross-basin


反义词：intrabasin (within a single basin)


词性: 名词
