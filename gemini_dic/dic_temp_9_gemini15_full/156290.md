phyllostomid:/ˌfɪləˈstɒmɪd/ | n. |叶口蝠科动物|A phyllostomid is any member of the family Phyllostomidae, a diverse group of bats found primarily in the Americas.  They are characterized by their leaf-nosed appearance, and encompass a wide range of feeding habits, from nectarivory to carnivory. |Example:  Many species of phyllostomid bats play crucial roles in seed dispersal in their ecosystems.  许多叶口蝠科蝙蝠在其生态系统中在种子传播方面发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词: leaf-nosed bat (less precise)|反义词: None (it's a family classification)|词性: noun
