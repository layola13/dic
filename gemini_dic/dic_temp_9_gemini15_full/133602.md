nonfluent: /ˈnɑːnˈfluːənt/ | adj. |不流利的，不流畅的 | Not flowing smoothly; halting or hesitant in speech or movement.  | 例句：His speech was nonfluent, punctuated by long pauses.  他的说话不流畅，时不时停顿很久。

例句：The student's English was nonfluent, making communication difficult. 这个学生的英语不流利，使得沟通困难。


近义词：halting, hesitant, stumbling, faltering, broken, impeded


反义词：fluent, articulate, smooth, effortless


词性: 形容词
