manifestoes:/ˌmænɪˈfɛstoʊz/| n. |宣言；声明；纲领 | A public declaration of intentions, motives, or views.  Manifestoes are often used by political parties or groups to outline their goals and beliefs. | Example: The communist manifesto outlined the party's aims for a classless society.  共产主义宣言概述了该党建立无阶级社会的目标。

The artist released a manifesto explaining their creative philosophy.  这位艺术家发布了一份宣言，解释了他们的创作理念。|近义词：declaration, proclamation, program, platform |反义词：secrecy, concealment
