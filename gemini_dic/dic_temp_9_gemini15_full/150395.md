panoramas:/ˌpænəˈræmɑːz/| n. |全景，概览 | A panorama is a wide view of a landscape or scene, especially one that is impressive or beautiful.  It can also refer to a comprehensive overview of a subject or situation. | 例句：The mountaintop offered a breathtaking panorama of the valley below. 山顶提供了令人叹为观止的山谷全景。

The book provides a panorama of 20th-century history. 这本书提供了20世纪历史的全景概览。


近义词：vista, view, overview, survey | 反义词：close-up, detail


词源:From Greek *pan- (all) + *orama (sight, view).  The word was coined in the late 18th century, reflecting the growing popularity of panoramic paintings.
