chiropractics:/kaɪ.rəˈpræktɪks/ | n. |整脊疗法，脊骨神经疗法 |The practice of diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal disorders, especially those of the spine, by manipulation.  Chiropractics focuses on the relationship between the body's structure, particularly the spine, and its overall function.  |例句：Many people seek chiropractics for back pain relief. 许多人寻求整脊疗法来缓解背痛。|例句：She's been undergoing chiropractics treatment for several weeks. 她已经接受了几周的脊骨神经疗法治疗。|近义词：manipulative therapy, spinal manipulation, osteopathy |反义词：conventional medicine, allopathic medicine
