poeticule:/ˈpoʊɪtɪkjuːl/| n. |小诗集；小诗；小巧的诗意之物|A small collection of poems, often a small book or booklet containing poems. It can also refer to something small and delicate that evokes a poetic feeling.|例句：She received a poeticule as a gift, filled with sonnets about nature. 她收到一本小诗集作为礼物，里面充满了关于自然的十四行诗。  She found the delicate seashell to be a poeticule, reminding her of a tiny, perfect poem. 她发现精致的海贝像一首小诗，让她想起一首微小而完美的诗歌。|近义词：collection of poems, anthology (for larger collections), booklet|反义词：epic poem (for a large-scale work)
