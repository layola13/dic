gayal:/ˈɡeɪæl/| n. |印度野牛|A wild bovine of northeastern India and Southeast Asia, similar to a gaur but smaller and with shorter horns.  It is domesticated in some areas and is valued for its meat and milk.|The gayal is a valuable livestock animal in many parts of its range.  印度野牛在其分布区域的许多地方是一种有价值的牲畜。|近义词：gaur, mithun|反义词：无
