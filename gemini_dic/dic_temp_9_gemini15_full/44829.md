crusta:/ˈkrʌstə/| n. |地壳；硬壳；外壳 |A hard outer layer, shell, or crust.  For example, the Earth's crust, the crust of a pie, or the crust of a crustacean. |例句：The Earth's crust is made up of several tectonic plates. 地球的地壳由几块构造板块组成。

The crab has a hard crust. 这只螃蟹有一个坚硬的外壳。|近义词：shell, rind, coating |反义词：core, interior
