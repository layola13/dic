gambol:/ˈɡæmbəl/ | v. |嬉戏；雀跃；跳跃 | To run or skip playfully.  |例句：The lambs gambolled in the meadow.  小羊们在草地上嬉戏。

The children gambolled on the beach. 孩子们在沙滩上跳跃嬉戏。|近义词：frolic, frisk, caper, skip, leap |反义词：trudge, plod

词源:
The word "gambol" comes from the Middle French "gamboler," which likely evolved from Old French "gambaudier" (to dance, leap, skip).  The ultimate origin is uncertain, but it may be related to words suggesting leaping or dancing.
