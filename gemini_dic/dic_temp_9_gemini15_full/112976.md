lulls:/lʌlz/| n.|平静期，间歇，催眠曲|a temporary pause or decline in activity, intensity, or sound; a period of calm or quiet.|The storm lulled for a few hours before resuming with renewed fury.暴风雨平静了几个小时，然后又重新爆发了。|近义词：pause, respite, calm, lull, break|反义词：turmoil, chaos, storm, frenzy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
A lull in the conversation allows me to catch my breath. = 对话中的平静让我得以喘口气。
The music lulls the baby to sleep. = 音乐哄婴儿睡觉。


一般过去时:
The fighting lulled for a few days. = 战斗平静了几天。
The wind lulled as the storm passed. = 暴风雨过后，风平息了。


现在完成时:
The tension has lulled since the agreement was reached. = 达成协议后，紧张气氛已经缓和了。
The doctor’s words have lulled my fears. = 医生的话消除了我的恐惧。


过去完成时:
The storm had lulled before we set sail. = 我们启航前暴风雨已经平息了。
The arguments had lulled before the meeting concluded. = 会议结束前争论已经平息了。


将来时:
The rain will lull soon. = 雨很快就会停了。
Hopefully, the protests will lull after the election. = 希望选举后抗议活动会平息。


虚拟语气:
If the music lulled me to sleep, I would have slept soundly. = 如果音乐能让我入睡，我会睡得很香。
I wish the noise would lull so I could concentrate. = 我希望噪音能小一点，这样我就能集中精力了。


被动语态:
The audience was lulled into a state of complacency. = 观众被哄骗得自满起来。
The children were lulled to sleep by the gentle music. = 孩子们被轻柔的音乐哄睡着了。


疑问句:
Does the music lull you to sleep? = 音乐能让你入睡吗？
Will the storm lull tonight? = 暴风雨今晚会停吗？


否定句:
The noise didn't lull the baby to sleep. = 噪音并没有哄婴儿睡觉。
The argument didn't lull; instead it intensified. = 争论并没有平息；相反，它加剧了。


PHRASE->

a lull in the fighting = 战斗的间歇
a lull in activity = 活动的间歇
a lull in the conversation = 对话的间歇
a deceptive lull = 虚假的平静
