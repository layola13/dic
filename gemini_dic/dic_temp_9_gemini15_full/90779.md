humorousness:/ˈhjuːmərəsnəs/| n. |幽默感，滑稽|The quality of being humorous; the ability to perceive, express, or appreciate humor.  It refers to the tendency to find things funny and to express that amusement.  |例句：Her humorousness lightened the mood of the tense meeting.  她的幽默感减轻了紧张会议的气氛。

例句：His presentation was full of humorousness, keeping the audience engaged. 他的演讲充满了幽默，让观众始终保持兴趣。


近义词：wit, humor, jocularity, mirth, drollery

反义词：seriousness, solemnity, gravity
