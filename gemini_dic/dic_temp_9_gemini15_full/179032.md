redemand:/ˌriːdɪˈmænd/| v.|再要求，再次索取|To demand again; to make a second demand.  It implies a previous demand that was not met or was not satisfactory.|例句：The customer redemanded a refund after finding further defects in the product. 客户在发现产品存在更多缺陷后再次要求退款。

例句：The union redemanded better working conditions after the initial negotiations failed.  工会在初步谈判失败后再次要求改善工作条件。


近义词：reclaim, request again, demand anew
反义词：cede, relinquish, concede

词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redemands a higher salary every year. 他每年都要求更高的薪水。
They redemand the return of their stolen property. 他们再次要求归还被盗的财产。


一般过去时:
She redemanded her money back after discovering the fraud.  她在发现欺诈后再次要求退款。
The protestors redemanded the release of the political prisoners.  抗议者再次要求释放政治犯。


现在完成时:
He has redemanded a response to his previous email. 他已经再次要求回复之前的邮件。
They have redemanded an explanation for the delay. 他们已经再次要求解释延误的原因。


过去完成时:
The manager had redemanded the report before the meeting.  经理在会议前再次要求提交报告。
They had redemanded a clarification of the contract terms. 他们之前已要求澄清合同条款。


将来时:
She will redemand the payment next week. 她下周将再次要求付款。
They will redemand a full investigation into the accident.  他们将再次要求对事故进行全面调查。


虚拟语气:
If he were to redemand the goods, we would comply. 如果他再次要求货物，我们会照办。
I wish he hadn't redemanded the contract. 我真希望他不要再次要求合同。


被动语态:
A higher price was redemanded by the supplier. 供应商再次要求更高的价格。
Better safety measures were redemanded by the workers. 工人们再次要求采取更好的安全措施。


疑问句:
Will they redemand their investment? 他们会再次要求投资吗？

否定句:
He did not redemand the missing parts. 他没有再次要求丢失的零件。
They will not redemand the contract's renegotiation.  他们不会再次要求重新谈判合同。
