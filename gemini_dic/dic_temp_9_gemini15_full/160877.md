post-climax:/ˈpoʊstˈklaɪmæks/| adj.|高潮后；结局后|Following the climax of a story or event.|The post-climax section of the novel felt rushed. 小说的高潮后部分感觉很仓促。|近义词：aftermath, epilogue, denouement|反义词：pre-climax


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't conjugate like a verb. Therefore, standard verb tenses are not applicable.  The usage is descriptive, describing the period after a climax.


PHRASE->
post-climax reflection: 高潮后的反思
post-climax tension: 高潮后的紧张
post-climax events: 高潮后的事件
post-climax analysis: 高潮后的分析
the post-climax scene: 高潮后的场景
