misplanning:/ˌmɪsˈplænɪŋ/ | n. | 错误的计划；计划失误 | The failure of the project was due to poor misplanning.  项目的失败是由于计划不周造成的。|近义词：bad planning, faulty planning, poor planning |反义词：good planning, careful planning
