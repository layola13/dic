retelegraphed: /ˌriːˈtɛləɡræft/ | v. | 再电报；重复电报 | To telegraph again; to send a telegram a second time, often to ensure the message was received or to clarify a previous message.  | 例句：The urgent message was retelegraphed to ensure its delivery.  紧急信息被再次电报以确保送达。


The company retelegraphed the order to correct a previous error. 公司再次发电报订正之前的错误。 | 近义词：retransmit, resend, relay | 反义词：N/A
