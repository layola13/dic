chiles:/ˈtʃiliːz/| n. |辣椒；智利人（复数）|Chiles are peppers of the genus Capsicum, known for their pungency.  They are used extensively in cuisines around the world.  The word can also refer to the people of Chile.|例句：These chiles are incredibly hot. 这些辣椒非常辣。

例句：Many tourists visit Chile to experience its unique culture. 许多游客访问智利以体验其独特的文化。


近义词：peppers, chilies (alternative spelling), red peppers|反义词：None (depending on context)|词性:Noun
