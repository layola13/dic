/koʊˈkaʊnsəlz/| n. |共同律师，协同律师 | cocounsels refers to a group of lawyers working together on a legal case, often representing the same client.  This is common in large or complex cases where multiple lawyers' expertise is needed.  | Example: The plaintiff's cocounsels worked collaboratively to prepare the case for trial.  原告的共同律师们通力合作准备了庭审。|  co-counsel (singular) |  -- | n.
