magnifies:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪz/ | vt. |放大；夸大；赞美 | To increase the size or apparent size of something.  |例句：The microscope magnifies the image a hundred times. 显微镜将图像放大一百倍。

例句：He magnifies his achievements to impress others. 他夸大自己的成就以给他人留下深刻印象。


近义词：amplify, enlarge, exaggerate | 反义词：diminish, reduce, belittle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The telescope magnifies distant stars. = 望远镜放大遥远的星星。
This lens magnifies the image by ten times. = 这镜头将影像放大十倍。


一般过去时:
The magician magnified the rabbit. = 魔术师变大了兔子（在视觉上）。
The speaker magnified the problem to create urgency.  = 演讲者夸大了问题以制造紧迫感。


现在完成时:
The company has magnified its market share. = 公司已经扩大了其市场份额。
He has magnified the importance of his role. = 他夸大了自己角色的重要性。


过去完成时:
She had magnified the risks before making the decision. = 她做出决定前夸大了风险。
They had magnified the differences between the groups. = 他们夸大了群体间的差异。


将来时:
The new technology will magnify the power of the computer. = 新技术将增强计算机的性能。
The media will magnify the scandal. = 媒体将夸大丑闻。


虚拟语气:
If the lens magnified the image more, we could see more detail. = 如果镜头放大影像更多，我们就能看到更多细节。
I wish the report didn't magnify the negative aspects. = 我希望报告不要夸大负面方面。


被动语态:
The image was magnified by the lens. = 影像被镜头放大了。
The problem was magnified by the media. = 这个问题被媒体夸大了。


疑问句:
Does this telescope magnify the image effectively? = 这台望远镜能有效放大图像吗？
How much does the microscope magnify the specimen? = 显微镜将样本放大了多少倍？


否定句:
The camera doesn't magnify the objects enough. = 相机没有足够放大物体。
The report did not magnify the positive aspects of the project. = 报告没有夸大项目的积极方面。


PHRASE->
magnify the importance of something: 夸大某事的重要性
magnify the problem: 夸大问题
magnify one's voice: 提高嗓音
magnify an image: 放大图像
magnify a situation: 夸大某种情况


词源:
magnify 源自于拉丁语 "magnificare",  由 "magnus" (大的，伟大的) 和 "facere" (做) 构成。因此，其字面意思是“使变大”。
