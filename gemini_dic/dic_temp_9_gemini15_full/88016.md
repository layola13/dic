high-tech's: /ˈhaɪˌtɛks/ | n. | 高科技的（所有格） |  The possessive form of "high-tech," referring to something belonging to or associated with high technology.  It indicates ownership or relationship. | Example:  High-tech's rapid advancements are transforming industries.  高科技的快速发展正在改变各个行业。 | 近义词: advanced technology's, cutting-edge technology's | 反义词: low-tech's, traditional technology's | 名词
