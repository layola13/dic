plenum:/ˈpliːnəm/| n. |全体会议；全体成员；充实；充满|A plenum is a meeting of all the members of a group or organization.  It can also refer to a completely filled space or a situation where something is completely full.|例句：The plenum of the party decided on the new policy.  全体党员大会决定了新政策。 The chamber was filled to its plenum with gas.  腔室充满了气体。|近义词：meeting, assembly, session, fullness|反义词：vacuum, emptiness
