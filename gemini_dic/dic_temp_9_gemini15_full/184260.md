resonants:/ˈrezənənts/ | adj. | 共振的；共鸣的；回响的 |  Producing or characterized by resonance; having a full, rich sound.  It describes something that produces a strong, clear, and sustained sound, often due to sympathetic vibration or amplification.  It can also refer to a feeling of strong agreement or shared understanding. |

例句：

The resonant voice of the singer filled the hall.  = 歌手的浑厚嗓音充满了整个大厅。

The poem’s resonant themes of loss and redemption touched many readers. = 这首诗歌中关于失去和救赎的共鸣主题打动了许多读者。


近义词：vibrant, sonorous, echoing, resounding, sympathetic

反义词：muted, dull, flat, lifeless


词性:形容词
