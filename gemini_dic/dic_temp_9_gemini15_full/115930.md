marquetries:/mɑːrˈkeɪtriːz/| n. |镶嵌细工；镶嵌工艺品|Decorative inlaid work, typically of wood, ivory, or mother-of-pearl.  Marquetries are often used to create intricate and beautiful patterns on furniture and other objects.|The antique table was adorned with exquisite marquetries of exotic woods.|近义词：inlay, mosaic|反义词：plain surface
