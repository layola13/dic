harper:/ˈhɑːrpər/| n.|竖琴演奏者；（尤指）游吟诗人；（姓氏）哈珀|A harper is a person who plays the harp, especially a traveling musician of the past who sang and played the harp.|例句：She is a talented harper, known for her beautiful melodies.  她是一位才华横溢的竖琴演奏者，以其优美的旋律而闻名。|例句：Many ancient tales were passed down through generations by wandering harpers. 许多古老的故事是由游荡的吟游诗人一代一代流传下来的。|近义词：lyrist, minstrel|反义词：无|名词
