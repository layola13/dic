hypospadias:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈspeɪdiəs/ | n. | 尿道下裂 | A congenital condition in males in which the opening of the urethra is on the underside of the penis, instead of at the tip.  This condition can range in severity from mild to severe, affecting urination and sexual function. | Example: The infant was diagnosed with hypospadias at birth and required surgery.  婴儿出生时被诊断出患有尿道下裂，需要手术治疗。 | 近义词：urethral malformation | 反义词：epispadias
