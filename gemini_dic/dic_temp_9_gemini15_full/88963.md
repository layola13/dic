holiday:/ˈhɒlədeɪ/ /ˈhɑːlədeɪ/| n.|假日，节日，假期|A day on which people celebrate a religious or national event, or a period of time when people have a break from work or school.|例句：Christmas is a major holiday in many countries. 圣诞节是许多国家的重大节日。

例句：We're going on holiday to the beach next week. 我们下周要去海滩度假。


近义词：vacation, break, festival|反义词：workday, workday


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Holidays are often celebrated with family and friends. = 假日通常与家人和朋友一起庆祝。
This year, the holiday falls on a Tuesday. = 今年的节日是在星期二。


一般过去时:
We had a wonderful holiday in Italy last year. = 我们去年在意大利度过了一个美好的假期。
She took a holiday from work to visit her parents. = 她休假去看望父母。


现在完成时:
I have had many holidays this year already. = 我今年已经休过很多假了。
They haven't had a holiday in years. = 他们多年没有休假了。


过去完成时:
We had already planned our holiday before the tickets went on sale. = 机票开售前我们就已经计划好假期了。
I had finished all my work before taking a holiday. = 我在休假前已经完成了所有工作。


将来时:
We will have a holiday in the mountains next month. = 我们下个月将在山区度假。
I will take a holiday as soon as I finish this project. = 我完成这个项目后就会休假。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would go on holiday more often. = 如果我有更多钱，我会更经常度假。
I wish I were on holiday right now. = 我真希望我现在正在度假。


被动语态:
The holiday was celebrated with great enthusiasm. = 节日以极大的热情庆祝。
Many public holidays are observed annually. = 许多公共假日每年都被庆祝。


疑问句:
When is your next holiday? = 你下一次休假是什么时候？

否定句:
