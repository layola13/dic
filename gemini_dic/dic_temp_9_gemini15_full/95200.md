inauthentic:/ˌɪnɔːˈθɛntɪk/| adj.|不真实的；伪造的；非正宗的|Lacking authenticity; not genuine; artificial.  It suggests something is a copy or imitation, lacking the genuine qualities of the original.|例句：The painting was deemed inauthentic by art experts.  艺术专家认为这幅画是赝品。

例句：His apology felt inauthentic; it lacked sincerity. 他的道歉听起来不真诚，缺乏诚意。


近义词：fake, spurious, artificial, counterfeit, bogus, sham

反义词：authentic, genuine, real, true, original
