rehospitalization:/ˌriːˌhɒspɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |再次住院；再入院|The act of being admitted to a hospital again after a previous discharge.  It often refers to readmission within a short period after the initial discharge.  This can be due to complications from the original illness or treatment, or a new health issue.|例句：His rehospitalization was due to complications from his surgery. = 他再次住院是因为手术并发症。

例句：The high rate of rehospitalization among heart failure patients is a major concern. = 心力衰竭患者的高再入院率是一个主要问题。


近义词：readmission, readmittance|反义词：discharge, release
