immigrate:/ˈɪmɪɡreɪt/ | v. | 移民；移居 | To come to live permanently in a foreign country. |  例句：Millions of people immigrated to the United States in the 19th century.  十九世纪数百万人民移民到美国。

例句：My grandparents immigrated from Ireland to Canada seeking better opportunities. 我的祖父母从爱尔兰移民到加拿大寻求更好的机会。


近义词：emigrate (from), migrate, relocate


反义词：emigrate (to), expatriate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They immigrate to Australia every year. 他们每年都移民到澳大利亚。
We immigrate to a new country for a better life. 我们为了更好的生活移民到一个新的国家。


一般过去时:
My family immigrated to the US in 1990. 我的家人在1990年移民到美国。
He immigrated to Canada after finishing his studies. 他完成学业后移民到加拿大。


现在完成时:
She has immigrated to Brazil and started a new business. 她已经移民到巴西并开始了一项新业务。
They have immigrated to France for several years. 他们已经移民法国好几年了。


过去完成时:
They had immigrated to Germany before the war broke out. 战争爆发前他们已移民德国。
She had immigrated to New Zealand before her brother joined her. 在她哥哥加入她之前，她已经移民到新西兰。


将来时:
We will immigrate to Spain next year. 我们明年将移民到西班牙。
They will immigrate to the UK once they find a job. 他们一旦找到工作就会移民到英国。


虚拟语气:
If I had the chance, I would immigrate to Italy. 如果我有机会，我会移民到意大利。
I wish I could immigrate to a warmer country. 我希望我能移民到一个更温暖的国家。


被动语态:
Many people are immigrated to this country every year. 每年都有很多人移民到这个国家。(注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
Thousands of refugees were immigrated to the neighboring countries. 数千名难民移民到邻国。(同样，主动语态更佳)


疑问句:
Will they immigrate to Japan? 他们会移民到日本吗？
Did your parents immigrate to this country? 你的父母移民到这个国家了吗？


否定句:
He did not immigrate to France. 他没有移民到法国。
We will not immigrate to that country. 我们不会移民到那个国家。


PHRASE->
illegal immigration 非法移民
skilled immigration 技术移民
economic immigration 经济移民
family immigration 家庭移民
immigration policy 移民政策

词源:
The word "immigrate" comes from the Latin prefix "im-" (into) and the Latin verb "migrare" (to move).  It literally means "to move into".
