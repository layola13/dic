presuspending:/ˌpriːsəˈspɛndɪŋ/| v.|预先暂停，暂时搁置|The action of temporarily suspending something before it is fully implemented or activated.  It suggests a proactive pause, often with the intention of resuming later.|例句：The company is presuspending the project until market conditions improve. 公司正在暂时搁置该项目，直到市场状况改善。

例句：The judge presuspended the sentence pending further investigation. 法官暂时中止判决，以待进一步调查。


近义词：postponing, delaying, deferring, suspending

反义词：proceeding, continuing, resuming


词性：动词
