choosing:/ˈtʃuːzɪŋ/ | v. | 选择；挑选 |  The act of selecting or making a choice. It is the present participle of the verb "choose". | Example: He is choosing a new car. 他正在选一辆新车。  She's choosing a dress for the party. 她在为聚会选衣服。|近义词：selecting, picking, opting | 反义词：rejecting, refusing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am choosing a new phone. = 我正在选择一部新手机。
They are choosing their vacation destination. = 他们在选择他们的度假目的地。

一般过去时:
I was choosing a gift for my friend. = 我当时正在为我的朋友挑选礼物。
She was choosing between two different jobs. = 她当时正在两个不同的工作之间选择。

现在完成时:
I have been choosing gifts all morning. = 我整个上午都在挑选礼物。
They have chosen their new home. = 他们已经选好了新家。

过去完成时:
I had chosen my outfit before the party started. = 派对开始之前我已经选好了我的衣服。
They had been choosing for hours before they finally decided. = 他们在最终决定之前已经挑选了好几个小时。

将来时:
I will be choosing a college next year. = 明年我将要选择一所大学。
They will have chosen a winner by the end of the week. = 他们将在本周末之前选出一个获胜者。

虚拟语气:
If I were choosing, I would pick the blue one. = 如果让我选择，我会选蓝色的那个。
I wish I had chosen a different path in life. = 我希望我的人生选择了一条不同的道路。

被动语态:
The winner will be chosen randomly. = 获胜者将被随机选出。
The best candidate has been chosen for the position. = 最合适的候选人已经被选拔到这个职位。

疑问句:
Are you choosing a new car? = 你正在选新车吗？
What are you choosing for dinner tonight? = 你今晚打算吃什么？

否定句:
I am not choosing that option. = 我不选择那个选项。
They were not choosing wisely. = 他们选择的并不明智。


PHRASE->
Choosing wisely = 明智地选择
Choosing sides = 选择立场
Choosing between = 在…之间选择
Choosing your battles = 选择你的战斗（谨慎选择需要应对的事情）
Choosing a career = 选择职业
