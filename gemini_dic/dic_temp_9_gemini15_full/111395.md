living:/ˈlɪvɪŋ/ | adj. n. | 活着的；生活；生计 |  alive, active, current;  means of support, livelihood |  例句：He's still living. 他还活着。The cost of living is high. 生活成本很高。|近义词：alive, existing, extant | 反义词：dead, deceased


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is living in London. = 他住在伦敦。
They are living a comfortable life. = 他们过着舒适的生活。

一般过去时:
She lived in Paris for five years. = 她曾在巴黎住了五年。
We lived on a farm when we were children. = 我们小时候住在农场。

现在完成时:
I have lived here since 2010. = 我自2010年以来一直住在这里。
They have lived a long and happy life. = 他们过着漫长而幸福的生活。

过去完成时:
He had lived in New York before moving to Los Angeles. = 他在搬到洛杉矶之前住在纽约。
She had lived a difficult life before finding success. = 在获得成功之前，她过着艰难的生活。

将来时:
We will be living in a new house next year. = 我们明年将住在一所新房子里。
They will live in the countryside after retirement. = 他们退休后将住在乡下。

虚拟语气:
If I were living in a different country, I would learn the language. = 如果我住在另一个国家，我会学习那里的语言。
I wish I were living closer to my family. = 我希望我住得离我的家人更近。

被动语态:
The house is being lived in by a family of five. = 这所房子住着五口之家。
The issue is being lived with rather than addressed. = 这个问题正在被忍受而不是被解决。

疑问句:
Where are you living now? = 你现在住在哪里？
Are they living together? = 他们住在一起吗？

否定句:
He isn't living in the city anymore. = 他不再住在城市里了。
They don't live near their parents. = 他们不住在父母附近。
