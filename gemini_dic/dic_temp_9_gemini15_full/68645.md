far-off:/fɑːrˈɔːf/| adj.|遥远的，久远的|distant in time or space; remote.  The adjective "far-off" describes something that is a considerable distance away, either geographically or temporally.|例句：The far-off mountains were shrouded in mist. 远处的山峦笼罩在雾中。

例句：Her far-off childhood memories were starting to fade. 她遥远的童年记忆开始褪色。|近义词：distant, remote, faraway|反义词：nearby, close, proximate|词性：形容词
