dodgily:/ˈdɒdʒɪli/| adj. |可疑地；不正当地；偷偷摸摸地 | done in a dishonest or suspicious way;  deceitfully. The deal was handled very dodgily, raising concerns about its legality. 这笔交易处理得很可疑，让人对其合法性产生担忧。|近义词：shady, shifty, suspect, underhand|反义词：honestly, openly, legitimately|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acts dodgily around his boss. = 他在他老板面前行为可疑。
The business operates dodgily, avoiding taxes. = 这家公司经营不正当，偷税漏税。


一般过去时:
