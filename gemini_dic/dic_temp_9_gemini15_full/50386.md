deports:/dɪˈpɔːrts/| vt.|驱逐出境；放逐；举止；表现|To expel (a foreigner) from a country;  to banish or exile.  It can also refer to the way someone behaves or conducts themselves.|例句：The government deported the illegal immigrants.  政府驱逐了非法移民。|例句：He deported himself with great dignity. 他举止非常有尊严。|近义词：expel, banish, exile|反义词：admit, welcome
