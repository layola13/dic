revamps:/ˌriːˈvæmps/| vt.|彻底改造，改进|To renovate or improve something, especially a building or system, by making major changes.|例句：The company is revamping its marketing strategy.  公司正在彻底改造其营销策略。|例句：They've completely revamped the old building. 他们已经彻底改造了那栋旧建筑。|近义词：renovate, overhaul, modernize, update|反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company revamps its website every year. = 公司每年都会改版其网站。
She revamps her wardrobe every season. = 她每个季度都会改造自己的衣橱。


一般过去时:
They revamped the old engine. = 他们改造了旧引擎。
He revamped his presentation before the meeting. = 他在会议前修改了他的演示文稿。


现在完成时:
The government has revamped its healthcare system. = 政府已经改革了其医疗体系。
She has revamped her image since changing jobs. = 她换工作后就改变了自己的形象。


过去完成时:
They had revamped the software before launching the new product. = 他们在新产品发布前已经修改了软件。
She had revamped her resume before submitting it. = 她提交简历前已经修改了简历。


将来时:
We will revamp the kitchen next month. = 我们下个月将改造厨房。
They will revamp their business plan next year. = 他们明年将修改他们的商业计划。


虚拟语气:
If they revamped the system, it would be more efficient. = 如果他们改造了系统，它将会更高效。
I wish they had revamped the website sooner. = 我希望他们早点改版网站。


被动语态:
The website is being revamped at the moment. = 网站目前正在被改版。
The old building was revamped last year. = 这栋旧建筑去年被改造了。


疑问句:
Will the company revamp its product line? = 公司会修改其产品线吗？
Is the software being revamped? = 软件正在被修改吗？


否定句:
They did not revamp the outdated equipment. = 他们没有改造过时的设备。
The company will not revamp its strategy this year. = 公司今年不会修改其策略。


PHRASE->
revamp a system = 改造系统
revamp a website = 改版网站
revamp a product = 改进产品
revamp a strategy = 修改策略
revamp an image = 改变形象
