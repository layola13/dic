consents:/kənˈsents/| v. n.|同意，赞成；准许|to give permission or agree to something.  |例句：He consents to the plan. 他同意这个计划。|近义词：agree, approve, assent|反义词：object, refuse, dissent|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consents to the proposal. = 他同意这个提议。
They consent to our marriage. = 他们同意我们的婚姻。


一般过去时:
She consented to the surgery. = 她同意了手术。
The king consented to their request. = 国王同意了他们的请求。


现在完成时:
Have they consented to the terms? = 他们是否同意了条款？
She has consented to participate in the study. = 她已同意参与这项研究。


过去完成时:
He had consented to the deal before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经同意了这笔交易。
They had consented to the changes earlier. = 他们之前已经同意了这些更改。


将来时:
Will he consent to the interview? = 他会同意接受采访吗？
She will consent to the plan if we address her concerns. = 如果我们解决她的担忧，她会同意这个计划的。


虚拟语气:
If he consented, the project would begin. = 如果他同意，项目就会开始。
I wish he consented to my request. = 我希望他同意我的请求。


被动语态:
His request was consented to. = 他的请求得到了同意。
The changes were consented to by the committee. = 这些变化得到了委员会的同意。


疑问句:
Does he consent to the terms? = 他同意这些条款吗？
Will they consent to the changes? = 他们会同意这些更改吗？


否定句:
He did not consent to the operation. = 他不同意手术。
They did not consent to our plan. = 他们不同意我们的计划。


PHRASE->
give consent = 给予同意
with one's consent = 经某人同意
without consent = 未经同意
obtain consent = 获得同意
informed consent = 知情同意
