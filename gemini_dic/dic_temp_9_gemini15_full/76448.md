gainsaid:/ˌɡeɪnˈsɛd/ | v. | 反驳；否认；违背 | To deny or contradict (something).  To speak or act against. |例句：No one gainsaid his authority.  没有人反驳他的权威。

例句：
His claims were never gainsaid. 他的说法从未被反驳过。


近义词：contradict, deny, oppose, refute


反义词：affirm, confirm, support, endorse


词性:动词


词源:
Old English *gænsægan, from ge- + *ægsægan "to say against," from *ægan "to say" + *sægan "to say."  The prefix *ge-  intensified the verb.  The word evolved through Middle English gainsayen.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gainsays everything I say. 他反驳我说的每一句话。
They gainsay the evidence presented. 他们否认提出的证据。


一般过去时:
She gainsaid the accusations against her. 她否认了对她的指控。
He gainsaid his previous statement. 他否认了他之前的声明。


现在完成时:
No one has gainsaid the findings of the report. 没有人反驳报告的调查结果。
They have gainsaid all attempts at reconciliation. 他们反对所有和解的尝试。


过去完成时:
He had gainsaid the prophecy before it came to pass.  在他预言成真之前，他就已经否认了。
They had gainsaid the order, leading to chaos. 他们违抗了命令，导致了混乱。


将来时:
She will gainsay his authority if he continues to act unjustly. 如果他继续不公正地行事，她将反驳他的权威。
They will gainsay the proposed changes to the law. 他们将会反对拟议中的法律变更。


虚拟语气:
If he gainsaid the truth, he would be punished. 如果他否认真相，他将受到惩罚。
If they had gainsaid the evidence, the case would have been lost. 如果他们否认证据，案件就会败诉。


被动语态:
His arguments were gainsaid by the opposition. 他的论点遭到反对派的驳斥。
The facts were gainsaid by the witness. 事实被证人否认了。


疑问句:
Did anyone gainsay the decision? 有人反对这个决定吗？
Will they gainsay the proposal? 他们会反对这项提议吗？


否定句:
No one dared gainsay the king's decree. 没有人敢违抗国王的法令。
They did not gainsay the obvious truth. 他们没有否认显而易见的真相。
