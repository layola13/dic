palpator:/ˈpælpeɪtər/| n. |触诊者；触诊器|A palpator is a person who performs palpation, or an instrument used for palpation.  It refers to someone who uses their hands to examine the body by feeling, or a device that assists in this process.  |例句：The doctor used a palpator to examine the patient's abdomen. 医生用触诊器检查病人的腹部。

The experienced palpator could detect a subtle lump.  经验丰富的触诊者可以检测到一个细微的肿块。|近义词：examiner,prober,explorer|反义词：observer (in the context of visual examination)
