penney:/ˈpɛni/| n.|便士；一分钱|A penny is a unit of currency equal to one hundredth of a dollar or pound.  It's a small coin.|例句：I found a penny on the sidewalk. 我在人行道上找到了一分钱。

例句：He only had a few pennies in his pocket. 他口袋里只有几个便士。


近义词：cent (in US), pence (in UK)|反义词：N/A|词性：名词
