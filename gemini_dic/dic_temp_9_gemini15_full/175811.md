rashly:/ˈræʃli/| adv. |轻率地；鲁莽地|Acting without careful consideration of the consequences.  It implies a lack of caution or foresight, often leading to negative outcomes. |例句：He rashly invested all his savings in a risky venture. 他轻率地把所有积蓄都投资到一个风险很大的项目中。|例句：She rashly promised more than she could deliver. 她轻率地许诺了她无法兑现的事情。|近义词：recklessly, hastily, impulsively, imprudently|反义词：carefully, cautiously, deliberately, prudently|无
