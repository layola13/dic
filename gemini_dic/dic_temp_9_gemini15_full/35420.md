co-ordinating: /koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 协调；整合；配合 |  The act of bringing different elements into a harmonious or consistent whole. This is the present participle of the verb "coordinate". It describes the ongoing process of coordinating. |例句：The project manager is co-ordinating the efforts of different teams. 项目经理正在协调不同团队的工作。

例句：She's co-ordinating the logistics for the conference. 她正在协调会议的物流工作。


近义词：harmonizing, integrating, synchronizing, unifying | 反义词：disorganizing, disrupting, separating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is co-ordinating the event. 他正在协调这个活动。
They are co-ordinating their schedules. 他们正在协调他们的日程安排。


一般过去时:
She co-ordinated the team's activities. 她协调了团队的活动。
We co-ordinated our efforts to achieve the goal. 我们协调了我们的努力以实现目标。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
