plastered:/ˈplæstərd/| v. adj.|涂抹；敷上；粘贴；（俚语）喝醉的；|to cover a surface with plaster or a similar substance; (informal) very drunk|例句：He plastered the wall with posters. 他用海报把墙贴满了。 The children were plastered with mud after playing outside.孩子们在外面玩耍后身上沾满了泥巴。 He was completely plastered after the party. 聚会后他喝得烂醉。|近义词：covered, coated, plastered, drunk, intoxicated|反义词：sober, clean


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He plasters the walls with wallpaper. = 他用壁纸把墙贴起来。
She plasters her face with makeup. = 她用化妆品把脸涂抹起来。


一般过去时:
They plastered the cracks in the wall. = 他们用泥灰填补了墙上的裂缝。
He plastered a smile on his face. = 他强作欢颜。


现在完成时:
She has plastered all the pictures on the wall. = 她已经把所有的照片都贴到墙上了。
They have plastered the house with advertisements. = 他们把房子贴满了广告。


过去完成时:
He had plastered the room before the guests arrived. = 客人到来之前，他已经把房间粉刷好了。
They had plastered the wound before calling the ambulance. = 他们给伤口敷了药才叫救护车。


将来时:
We will plaster the ceiling tomorrow. = 我们明天将粉刷天花板。
They will plaster the broken vase back together. = 他们将会把破裂的花瓶粘合起来。


虚拟语气:
If I had plastered the wall properly, it wouldn't have cracked. = 如果我当时把墙粉刷好，它就不会裂了。
I wish I had plastered the wound sooner. = 我真希望我早点给伤口敷药。


被动语态:
The wall was plastered with posters. = 墙壁上贴满了海报。
The wound was plastered and bandaged. = 伤口被敷药并包扎了。


疑问句:
Have you plastered the cracks yet? = 你已经把裂缝填补了吗？
Will they plaster the ceiling today? = 他们今天会粉刷天花板吗？


否定句:
He didn't plaster the wall properly. = 他没有把墙粉刷好。
She didn't plaster her hair. = 她没有把头发梳理好。



PHRASE->
plaster over = 遮掩；掩盖
plaster on = 涂抹；敷上
plaster up = 修补；粉刷
plaster down = 压平；弄平
get plastered = 喝醉


词源:
源自古法语 *plasterer，意为“石膏匠”。  最终源自拉丁语 *emplastrum，意为“膏药”。
