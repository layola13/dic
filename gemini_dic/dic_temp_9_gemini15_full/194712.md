self-worn:/ˈsɛlfˌwɔːrn/ | adj. | 自己穿坏的，穿旧的 | Worn or damaged by the wearer themselves, through use and time.  The term emphasizes the personal use and resulting deterioration. | Example:  Her self-worn boots showed years of hiking.  她的旧靴子显示出多年的徒步旅行痕迹。


近义词: well-worn, worn-out, used, shabby

反义词: new, unworn, pristine


词性:形容词
