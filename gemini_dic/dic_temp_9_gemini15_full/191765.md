scintillating: /ˈsɪntɪleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 闪闪发光的；闪烁的；才华横溢的；令人兴奋的 |  Sparkling or shining brightly; brilliantly and excitingly clever or skillful.  | 例句：The scintillating diamond necklace caught everyone's eye.  闪闪发光的钻石项链吸引了所有人的目光。

The scintillating performance left the audience breathless.  令人兴奋的表演让观众屏住了呼吸。


近义词： sparkling, glittering, brilliant, dazzling, exciting | 反义词： dull, boring, tedious, unexciting


词源:  From Middle French *scintillant, present participle of scintiller "to sparkle," from Latin scintilla "spark," diminutive of scintilla "a spark."
