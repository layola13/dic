drizzlier:/ˈdrɪzlɪər/| adj.|下毛毛雨的，毛毛雨更厉害的|More rainy than a drizzle;  characterized by a light, fine rain.|The weather became drizzlier as the afternoon progressed. 天气在下午变得更阴雨了。|近义词：rainier, showery |反义词：sunny, dry


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It drizzles lightly today. = 今天下着毛毛雨。
The weather is drizzlier in the mountains. = 山区天气更阴雨。

一般过去时:
It drizzled yesterday afternoon. = 昨天午后下过毛毛雨。
The weather was drizzlier than expected. = 天气比预期的更阴雨。

现在完成时:
It has drizzled all morning. = 一上午都在下毛毛雨。
The weather has become much drizzlier since this morning. = 自今早以来天气变得阴雨多了。

过去完成时:
It had drizzled for hours before the sun came out. = 太阳出来前已经下毛毛雨好几个小时了。
The weather had been drizzlier in the previous week. = 上周天气更阴雨。

将来时:
It will drizzle tomorrow. = 明天会下毛毛雨。
The weather will be drizzlier in the coming days. = 未来几天天气会更阴雨。

虚拟语气:
If it drizzled tomorrow, I would stay home. = 如果明天下毛毛雨，我就待在家里。
I wish it hadn't been so drizzlier yesterday. = 我真希望昨天不要那么阴雨。

被动语态:  (Drizzlier is an adjective, it doesn't take a passive voice)


疑问句:
Is it drizzlier outside? = 外面是不是更阴雨了？
Will the weather be drizzlier later? = 晚些时候天气会更阴雨吗？

否定句:
It's not drizzlier than yesterday. = 今天没有昨天阴雨。
The weather wasn't drizzlier than we thought. = 天气没有我们想象的那么阴雨。
