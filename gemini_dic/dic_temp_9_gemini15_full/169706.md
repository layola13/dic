proprietaries:/prəˈpraɪəˌtɛriːz/| n.|所有权；专有物；专利品|Things owned; exclusive rights or ownership; patented goods or processes.  The term often refers to software or technology that is owned by a specific company and not freely available. |例句：The company fiercely protects its intellectual property and its proprietary software. 这家公司严密保护其知识产权和专有软件。

例句：  Many pharmaceutical companies hold numerous proprietaries on their medications. 许多制药公司拥有其药物的众多专利。


近义词：patents, trademarks, copyrights, exclusive rights, ownership

反义词：public domain, open source, common property


词性:名词
