inbreeding: /ˈɪnˌbriːdɪŋ/ | n. | 近亲繁殖 | The breeding of closely related individuals, especially of animals or plants.  This practice often results in offspring with undesirable traits due to the increased likelihood of inheriting recessive genes. |  例句：Inbreeding can lead to a higher incidence of genetic disorders. 近亲繁殖会导致遗传疾病发病率升高。

例句：The farmer avoided inbreeding in his herd to maintain genetic diversity.  这位农民避免在他的牛群中进行近亲繁殖以保持遗传多样性。


近义词：consanguinity, close breeding | 反义词：outbreeding, crossbreeding
