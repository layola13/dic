quiddler:/ˈkwɪdlər/| n. |含糊其辞的人，支吾其词的人，玩弄文字游戏的人 |A person who evades giving a direct answer, often by using ambiguous language or making excuses.  |例句：He's such a quiddler; I can never get a straight answer out of him. 他真是个含糊其辞的人；我从来无法从他那里得到直接的答案。


例句：The lawyer was a master quiddler, twisting words to confuse the jury.  这位律师是个玩弄文字游戏的高手，他歪曲事实以迷惑陪审团。

近义词：equivocator, prevaricator, tergiversator, dodger

反义词：candid person, straightforward person, forthright person

词性：名词
