disputers:/dɪˈspjuːtərz/| n. |争论者，辩论者 |Those who engage in disputes or arguments. People who are prone to arguing or quarreling. |例句：The disputers argued vehemently about the meaning of the ancient text. 争论者们激烈地争论着古代文本的含义。  The disputers failed to reach a consensus. 争论者们未能达成共识。|近义词：arguers, contenders, debaters, quarrelers |反义词：peacemakers, conciliators
