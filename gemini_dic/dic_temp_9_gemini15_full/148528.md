oversureness:/ˈoʊvərˈʃʊrnəs/| n.|过分自信，自负|Excessive confidence; overconfidence.  It can lead to poor decision-making and a lack of careful consideration.|例句：His oversureness about his abilities led him to underestimate the task. 他的过分自信让他低估了任务的难度。

例句：Her oversureness in the debate cost her the victory. 她辩论中的过分自信让她输掉了比赛。


近义词：conceit, arrogance, self-assurance (in a negative context), presumption


反义词：humility, modesty, self-doubt, diffidence


词性：名词
