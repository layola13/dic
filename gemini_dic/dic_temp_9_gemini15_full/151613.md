participants:/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpənts/| n. |参与者，参加者|People who take part in something.  The study involved 100 participants.|例句：The conference attracted participants from all over the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的参与者。|近义词：members, attendants, involved, contributors|反义词：observers, onlookers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The participants are discussing the project. = 参与者们正在讨论这个项目。
Many participants contribute actively to the group. = 许多参与者积极地为小组做贡献。

一般过去时:
The participants completed the survey. = 参与者完成了调查。
All participants left the meeting early. = 所有参与者都提前离开了会议。

现在完成时:
The participants have submitted their reports. = 参与者们已经提交了他们的报告。
Several participants have already expressed their opinions. = 一些参与者已经表达了他们的意见。

过去完成时:
The participants had finished the experiment before the deadline. = 参与者们在截止日期前完成了实验。
By the time the presentation started, all participants had taken their seats. = 到演示开始时，所有参与者都已经就座了。

将来时:
The participants will receive their certificates next week. = 参与者们下周将收到他们的证书。
All participants will be notified of the results soon. = 所有参与者很快就会收到结果通知。

虚拟语气:
If the participants were more engaged, the discussion would be more productive. = 如果参与者更投入，讨论会更有成效。
I wish the participants had more time to share their ideas. = 我希望参与者有更多时间分享他们的想法。

被动语态:
The participants were asked to fill out a questionnaire. = 要求参与者填写一份问卷。
The data from the participants is being analyzed. = 参与者提供的数据正在被分析。

疑问句:
How many participants are there in the study? = 这项研究有多少参与者？
Were all participants satisfied with the outcome? = 所有参与者都对结果满意吗？

否定句:
Not all participants agreed with the conclusion. = 并非所有参与者都同意这个结论。
Few participants responded to the survey. = 很少有参与者回应调查。
