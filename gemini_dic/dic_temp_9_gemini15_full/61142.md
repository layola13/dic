eloquence:/ˈɛləkwəns/ | n. |雄辩；口才；流畅；雄辩的表达 |The power of fluent, forceful, and persuasive speaking or writing.  It refers to the skill of using language effectively and persuasively. |例句：His eloquence captivated the audience.  他的雄辩迷住了听众。

例句：The speech was filled with eloquence and passion. 这篇演讲充满激情和雄辩。


近义词：fluency, expressiveness, articulation, oratory, rhetoric |反义词：inarticulateness, incoherence, stammering


词源：  From Middle French *éloquence, from Latin eloquentia, from eloquī to speak out, from e- + loquī to speak.
