persistence: /pərˈsɪstəns/ | n. |坚持；持久；持续 | The quality or fact of continuing to exist or happen, or of continuing to do something despite difficulties.  It implies a steadfast refusal to give up despite obstacles or setbacks. |

例句：

Her persistence finally paid off when she was offered the job. = 她坚持不懈，最终得到了这份工作。

The persistence of the rumor was surprising. = 谣言的持续存在令人惊讶。


近义词： perseverance, tenacity, steadfastness, determination

反义词： surrender, giving up, quit
