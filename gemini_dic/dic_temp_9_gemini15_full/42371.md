counterattacking: /ˌkaʊntərəˈtækɪŋ/ | adj. | 反攻的；反击的 |  relating to or characterized by a counterattack.  | The counterattacking force regained lost ground. 反攻部队收复失地。 |近义词：offensive, retaliatory |反义词：defensive, retreating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team is counterattacking with renewed vigor. = 球队正在以新的活力进行反攻。
Their strategy is counterattacking primarily through the flanks. = 他们的策略主要是通过侧翼进行反攻。


一般过去时:
The army counterattacked at dawn. = 军队在黎明时分进行了反攻。
They counterattacked fiercely, repelling the enemy. = 他们猛烈反击，击退了敌人。
