mont:/mɒnt/| n.|月份，山峰|A month is one of the twelve periods into which a year is divided.|例句：January is the first month of the year. 一月是一年的第一个月。|例句：The climbers reached the top of the mountain. 登山者到达了山顶。|近义词：period, peak |反义词:day, valley


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This month has 31 days. = 这个月有31天。
The mountain stands tall and proud. = 这座山高耸而自豪。

一般过去时:
Last month, I traveled to France. = 上个月，我去法国旅行了。
We climbed that mountain last summer. = 我们去年夏天爬了那座山。

现在完成时:
I haven't seen him this month. = 这个月我还没见过他。
They have conquered many mountains. = 他们征服了许多山峰。

过去完成时:
By last month, we had finished the project. = 到上个月为止，我们已经完成了项目。
Before they reached the summit, they had faced many challenges. = 在他们到达山顶之前，他们面临着许多挑战。

将来时:
Next month, I will go on vacation. = 下个月，我要去度假。
We will climb that mountain next year.  = 我们明年将攀登那座山。

虚拟语气:
If it were not for the mountains, the view would be different. = 如果没有这些山，景色就会不一样。
If I had more time, I would climb Mont Blanc. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去攀登勃朗峰。

被动语态:
The month is often referred to as October. = 这个月通常被称为十月。
The mountain was formed millions of years ago. = 这座山是在几百万年前形成的。

疑问句:
What month is it? = 现在是几月份？
Which mountain is the highest in the world? = 世界上最高的山峰是哪座？

否定句:
This is not the month we planned to travel. = 这不是我们计划旅行的月份。
That isn't a mountain; it's a hill. = 那不是山；那是小山。
