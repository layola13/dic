regurgitates:/ˌriːˈɡɜːrdʒɪteɪts/| vt.|反刍；吐出；(不加思考地) 重复|To bring (swallowed food) up again to the mouth; to eject from the stomach. To repeat or imitate without original thought or creativity.|例句：The bird regurgitated the undigested seeds.  这只鸟反刍出未消化的种子。
The student simply regurgitated facts from the textbook. 这个学生只是机械地重复课本上的事实。|近义词：vomit, repeat, reiterate|反义词：digest, originate, create


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regurgitates his food after eating. 他吃完饭后会反刍食物。
She regurgitates information without understanding it. 她不理解信息就机械地重复。

一般过去时:
The owl regurgitated a pellet of undigested bones. 猫头鹰吐出了一个由未消化的骨头组成的食团。
He regurgitated the teacher's lecture word for word. 他逐字逐句地重复老师的讲课内容。

现在完成时:
The baby has regurgitated milk several times today. 婴儿今天已经吐了几次奶了。
She has regurgitated the same arguments repeatedly. 她反复重复着同样的论点。


过去完成时:
He had regurgitated his dinner before he went to bed. 他睡觉前已经把晚饭吐出来了。
The parrot had regurgitated phrases it had heard before. 那只鹦鹉已经重复了它之前听到的短语。

将来时:
The sick child will likely regurgitate his food.  生病的孩子很可能会吐出食物。
He will probably regurgitate the same old excuses. 他可能会再次重复那些老生常谈的借口。

虚拟语气:
If he ate too quickly, he might regurgitate. 如果他吃得太快，他可能会吐出来。
If she had studied more, she wouldn't have had to regurgitate information during the exam. 如果她学习更努力，考试时就不必死记硬背了。

被动语态:
The food was regurgitated by the bird. 食物被鸟反刍出来。
The information was regurgitated by the student without any critical thinking.  学生机械地重复信息，没有任何批判性思维。

疑问句:
Does the baby often regurgitate? 宝宝经常吐奶吗？
Did he regurgitate the entire speech? 他是否重复了整个演讲？

否定句:
The cat did not regurgitate its hairball. 猫没有吐出毛球。
He didn't regurgitate the information; he understood and explained it. 他并没有死记硬背信息；他理解并解释了它。

词源:
The word "regurgitate" comes from the Latin words "re-" (again) and "gurgitare" (to gush, to pour forth).  It literally means "to pour forth again," referring to the act of bringing swallowed food back up.
