sandblast:/ˈsændˌblæst/| vt. |喷砂；砂喷|To clean or etch a surface by forcing sand against it with compressed air.|例句：We sandblasted the metal to remove the rust. 我们用喷砂法去除金属表面的锈蚀。例句：The artist sandblasted the glass to create a frosted effect. 艺术家用喷砂法在玻璃上 tạo ra hiệu ứng mờ. |近义词：abrasive blast, grit blast|反义词：polish, smooth
