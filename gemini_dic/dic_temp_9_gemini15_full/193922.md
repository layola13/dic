self-emancipation: /ˌsɛlfˌɛmænˌsɪˈpeɪʃən/ | n. | 自我解放；自主；自立 | The act of freeing oneself from slavery, oppression, or any other form of subjugation.  It emphasizes the individual's agency and initiative in achieving liberation. | Example:  The enslaved people's self-emancipation was a testament to their resilience and determination.  = 被奴役的人民的自我解放证明了他们的韧性和决心。  |近义词：liberation, self-liberation, autonomy |反义词：enslavement, subjugation, oppression | 名词
