ngl:/ˌɛn ˈdʒiːˈɛl/ | int. | 不说实话，老实说 |  Not going to lie. It's an internet slang abbreviation used to preface a statement that might be considered boastful, controversial, or otherwise unpleasant.  It softens the blow of the following statement. | 例句：NGL, I was kinda scared. = 老实说，我有点害怕。

NGL, that movie was amazing. = 老实说，那部电影太棒了。


近义词：honestly, truthfully, frankly, to be honest, in all honesty | 反义词：lying, deceiving, misleading | 词性：interjection (感叹词)
