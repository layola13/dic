dressier:/ˈdrɛsiər/| adj.|更衣着讲究的，更时髦的，更华丽的 | More formal or elegant in dress.  It suggests a higher level of sophistication and style in clothing compared to something less dressy. |例句：She wore a dressier outfit to the wedding. 她穿了一套更正式的衣服去参加婚礼。

例句：For the evening event, I opted for a dressier gown.  为了晚上的活动，我选择了一件更华丽的礼服。


近义词：more elegant, more stylish, more formal, fancier

反义词：less dressy, more casual, informal

词性：形容词
