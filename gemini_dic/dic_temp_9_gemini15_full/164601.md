preexecution: /ˌpriːˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃən/ | n. | 预执行，预先执行 | The act of executing something before its scheduled time or in anticipation of a future event.  It often involves preparing or setting up for a later execution.  Preexecution is commonly used in computing and software development, but can also apply to other fields. | Example: The preexecution of the database queries significantly improved the application's performance.  预先执行数据库查询显著提高了应用程序的性能。  The preexecution of the safety protocols prevented a major accident. 预先执行安全协议避免了一次重大事故。 | 近义词：advance preparation, prior execution, preliminary execution | 反义词：postexecution
