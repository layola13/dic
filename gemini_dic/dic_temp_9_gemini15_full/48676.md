deferment:/dɪˈfɜːrmənt/ | n. | 延期；缓期；暂缓 | The postponement of something to a later time.  It often refers to the postponement of payment, military service, or other obligations. | Example:  He received a deferment from military service due to his studies. 他因为学业获得了服兵役的延期。

The company requested a deferment of the loan payment. 公司要求延期偿还贷款。 | 近义词：postponement, delay, adjournment | 反义词：acceleration, hastening
