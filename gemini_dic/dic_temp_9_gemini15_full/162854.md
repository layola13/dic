precautional: /prɪˈkɔːʃənəl/ | adj. | 预防性的，谨慎的 | Relating to or characterized by precaution; cautious.  | 例句：Precautional measures were taken to prevent the spread of the virus.  = 采取了预防措施以防止病毒传播。

例句：The company implemented precautional safety protocols. = 公司实施了预防性安全规程。


近义词：preventive, precautionary, careful, cautious | 反义词：reckless, careless, negligent | 词性: adjective
