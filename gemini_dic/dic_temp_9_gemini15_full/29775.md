cattinesses:/ˈkætɪnɪsɪz/| n.|刻薄的行为；猫一样的行为；刁蛮；刻薄的言行|The plural of "cattiness," referring to instances or examples of behavior characterized by spitefulness, malicious gossip, or catty remarks.|例句：Her cattinesses were legendary among her colleagues.  她的刻薄行为在同事中是出了名的。

例句：The office was rife with cattinesses, making it a difficult environment to work in. 办公室里充斥着刻薄的行为，使得工作环境非常艰难。


近义词：spitefulness, catfights, bitchiness, pettiness, meanness


反义词：kindness, generosity, amiability, good nature


词性:名词


词源:源自于单词"cattiness",  其本身源于"cat" (猫)，暗指猫的某些行为特征，如偷偷摸摸、嫉妒、争斗等，引申为人的刻薄、恶意中伤等行为。  "-ness"是名词后缀，表示状态或性质。  "-es"是复数后缀。
