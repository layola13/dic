extorter:/ɪkˈstɔːrtər/ | n. |勒索者，敲诈者 | A person who uses threats to force someone to give them money or other things.  | The extorter demanded $10,000 or he would reveal the company's secrets.  勒索者要求支付1万美元，否则他就会泄露公司的秘密。 | blackmailer, racketeer | benefactor, philanthropist | 名词
