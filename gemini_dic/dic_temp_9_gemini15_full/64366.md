ere:/ɛər/ | adv. | прежде, раньше, до этого | Before; previously; earlier.  | Example: Ere long, the storm broke.  不久，暴风雨就爆发了。 |近义词: before, previously, earlier | 反义词: after, later


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He arrives ere nightfall. = 他在夜幕降临前到达。
She sings ere she sleeps. = 她睡觉前唱歌。


一般过去时:
They had left ere dawn. = 他们在黎明前就离开了。
He finished the task ere the deadline. = 他在截止日期前完成了任务。


现在完成时:
We have arrived ere the scheduled time. = 我们在预定时间前到达了。
She has completed her work ere the boss came. = 老板来之前，她已经完成了工作。


过去完成时:
They had already eaten ere we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经吃过了。
She had finished reading the book ere the movie started. = 电影开始之前，她已经读完了书。


将来时:
He will be there ere sunset. = 他会在日落前到达那里。
She will complete the project ere the next meeting. = 她会在下次会议前完成项目。


虚拟语气:
If he had known, he would have left ere then. = 如果他知道的话，他就会在那之前离开了。
I wish I had arrived ere the meeting started. = 我希望我在会议开始前就到了。


被动语态:
The work was completed ere the deadline. = 工作在截止日期前完成了。
The report will be submitted ere the end of the week. = 报告将在周末前提交。


疑问句:
Did they arrive ere the rain started? = 雨开始下之前他们到了吗？
Had he finished his work ere he left? = 他离开之前完成工作了吗？


否定句:
They did not arrive ere the concert started. = 他们在音乐会开始前没有到达。
He had not finished his homework ere his mother called. = 他妈妈打电话之前没有完成作业。


PHRASE->
Ere long = Soon
Ere now = By now
Ere then = Before that time
Ere this = Before this time
Ere while = A short time ago
