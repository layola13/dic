self-erected: /ˌsɛlfɪˈrɛktɪd/ | adj. | 自建的；自行竖立的 |  Something that is self-erected is built or constructed without outside assistance or professional help.  It implies a degree of independent effort and resourcefulness. |例句：The self-erected fence was surprisingly sturdy.  自建的篱笆出奇的结实。|例句：He built a self-erected shed in his backyard. 他在后院建了一个自建的棚屋。|近义词：homemade, home-built, DIY (do-it-yourself) |反义词：professionally built, commercially built |词性：形容词
