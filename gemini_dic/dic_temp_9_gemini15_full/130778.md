nonanemic: /ˌnɒnəˈniːmɪk/ | adj. | 非贫血的 | Not exhibiting or suffering from anemia; having normal levels of hemoglobin in the blood.  | Example:  The patient's blood test revealed a nonanemic profile.  这位病人的血液检测结果显示为非贫血状态。 | 近义词: healthy (in terms of blood), normochromic, normocytic | 反义词: anemic
