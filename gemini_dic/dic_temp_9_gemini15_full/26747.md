caducary:/ˈkædjʊəri/| n.|早落性，早落|The shedding of leaves, flowers, or fruit before they are fully mature.  It refers to a characteristic of certain plants where organs fall off prematurely. |Example: The caducary of the tree's leaves was a sign of the approaching autumn.  这棵树的叶子早落是秋天临近的标志。|近义词：premature dropping, early abscission|反义词：persistence, longevity|名词
