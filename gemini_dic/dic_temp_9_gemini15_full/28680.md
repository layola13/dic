carjacked:/ˈkɑːrdʒækt/ | v. | 劫车 | To steal a car from someone by force, usually while the victim is still inside.  | Example: The woman was carjacked at gunpoint in broad daylight.  这名女子在光天化日之下被持枪劫车。 | 近义词: hijack, rob (a car) | 反义词:  None specifically, but the opposite would be to legally purchase or receive a car.

She was carjacked last night near her home. = 她昨晚在家附近被劫车。
