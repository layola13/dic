munific:/mjuːˈnɪfɪk/| adj.|慷慨的，丰富的| generous or lavish in giving; characterized by great generosity |The munificent donation allowed the hospital to purchase much-needed equipment.  这位慷慨的捐赠使医院能够购买急需的设备。|近义词：generous, bountiful, lavish, magnanimous|反义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious
