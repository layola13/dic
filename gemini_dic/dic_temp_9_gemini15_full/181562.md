This often involves significant alterations, such as removing existing features and adding new ones.  It implies a more extensive and planned change than simply gardening or landscaping.|例句：The town council decided to relandscape the park to make it more accessible.  镇议会决定重新规划公园景观，使其更方便通行。

The company plans to relandscape the entire property before selling it. 公司计划在出售房产前对其进行整体景观改造。
