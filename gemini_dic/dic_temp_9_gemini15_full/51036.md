desmog:/ˈdɛzmɒɡ/| n.| 去除烟雾；揭露虚假信息；揭露谎言 | A website and organization dedicated to debunking misinformation and promoting climate change awareness.  It aims to "clear the air" of misleading information surrounding environmental issues, particularly climate change. | Example: The desmog blog published an article exposing the fossil fuel industry's campaign to spread climate change denial.  去污博客发表了一篇文章，揭露化石燃料行业散布气候变化否认论的活动。 | 近义词：debunking, fact-checking, exposing | 反义词：propaganda, misinformation, disinformation | 名词
