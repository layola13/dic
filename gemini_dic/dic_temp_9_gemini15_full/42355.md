counterargue:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuː/|/ˈkaʊntərɑːrɡjuː/| vt.|反驳，驳斥 |To present a contrary argument; to argue against something that has been said.  |例句：He counterargued that the proposed policy would be economically damaging. 他反驳说，拟议中的政策将对经济造成损害。

例句：The defense lawyer counterargued the prosecution's claims of premeditation.  辩护律师反驳了控方关于预谋的指控。


近义词：refute, contradict, oppose, rebut, challenge

反义词：agree, concur, support, endorse


词性:动词
