decriminalise: /ˌdiːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使非犯罪化 | To remove the criminal status of something.  To stop something from being a crime. |  例句：The government is considering decriminalising the possession of small amounts of marijuana.  政府正在考虑使持有少量大麻合法化。

例句：Many activists are campaigning to decriminalise drug use. 许多活动家正在开展运动，以使吸毒合法化。


近义词：legalise, legitimize

反义词：criminalise
