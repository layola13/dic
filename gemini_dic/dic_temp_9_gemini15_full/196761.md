sexing:/ˈsɛksɪŋ/| v.|性别鉴定；确定性别|The act of determining the sex of an organism, often used in animal husbandry, fisheries management, and wildlife research.  It involves identifying whether an organism is male or female.|例句：The researchers are sexing the turtles to study their population dynamics. 研究人员正在对海龟进行性别鉴定以研究其种群动态。
例句：We need to sex the chicks before we separate them. 我们需要在把小鸡分开之前确定它们的性别。|近义词：gender determination, sex identification|反义词：None.
