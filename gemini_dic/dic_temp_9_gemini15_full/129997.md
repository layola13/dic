non-Newtonian:/ˌnɒn njuːˈtəʊniən/| adj.|非牛顿流体|Describing a fluid whose viscosity changes under applied stress or force.  Its viscosity increases with increasing shear rate (shear thickening) or decreases with increasing shear rate (shear thinning).|例句：Cornstarch mixed with water is a classic example of a non-Newtonian fluid; it behaves like a solid when struck hard but flows like a liquid when left undisturbed.  玉米淀粉和水混合是一种典型的非牛顿流体的例子；当用力击打时，它表现得像固体，而当静置不动时，它像液体一样流动。


例句：The behavior of non-Newtonian fluids in different conditions is a complex subject of study in fluid mechanics.  非牛顿流体在不同条件下的行为是流体力学中一个复杂的研究课题。

近义词：shear-thinning, shear-thickening (depending on the specific behavior)

反义词：Newtonian  (牛顿流体)


词性:形容词
