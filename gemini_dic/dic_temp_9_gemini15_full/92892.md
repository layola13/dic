idealizes:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzɪz/| vt.|理想化；美化；赋予理想色彩|To represent or think of someone or something as being perfect or better than they really are.|例句：She idealizes her childhood, remembering only the good times. 她把童年理想化了，只记得美好的时光。|例句：He idealizes his relationship with his father, ignoring the flaws. 他将他与父亲的关系理想化了，忽略了缺点。|近义词：romanticize, glorify,  exaggerate|反义词：denigrate, criticize, disparage|动词
