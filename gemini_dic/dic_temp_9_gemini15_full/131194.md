nonbuoyantly:/ˌnɒnˈbɔɪəntli/| adv. |不轻快地，不活泼地 |Not in a buoyant or cheerful manner; lacking buoyancy or liveliness.  |The balloon descended nonbuoyantly to the ground. 气球不轻快地落到了地上。|近义词：drably, dejectedly, dispiritedly |反义词：buoyantly, cheerfully, lively

USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and thus doesn't follow verb tenses in the same way as verbs.  Its usage depends on the verb it modifies.

Example Sentences showing modification of different verb types:

He spoke nonbuoyantly about his future. 他不轻快地谈论着他的未来。

She walked nonbuoyantly through the park. 她无精打采地穿过公园。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "nonbuoyantly".  Its usage is generally as a single adverb modifying a verb describing an action or state.
