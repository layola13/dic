cacophonic:/ˌkækəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌkækoʊˈfɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 刺耳的；不和谐的；嘈杂的 |  Characterized by a harsh, discordant mixture of sounds.  It describes a sound that is unpleasant to the ear because of a lack of harmony or a mixture of unpleasant sounds. |The cacophony of the city traffic was deafening. 城市交通的嘈杂声震耳欲聋。

The market was a cacophony of sounds, smells, and sights. 市场充满了各种刺耳的声音、难闻的气味和令人眼花缭乱的景象。


近义词：discordant, jarring, harsh, raucous, strident

反义词：harmonious, melodious, euphonious
