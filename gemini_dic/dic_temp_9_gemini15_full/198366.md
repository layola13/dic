showerheads:/ˈʃaʊərhe(d)z/| n.|淋浴喷头|A showerhead is a device fitted to a pipe that sprays water for showering.  It's typically found in bathrooms and can come in various designs and spray patterns.| Example:  We need to replace the old showerhead; it's not working properly. 我们需要更换旧的淋浴喷头；它不工作了。|近义词：shower rose, spray head|反义词：无

This showerhead saves water. = 这个淋浴喷头节约用水。

词源:
shower (n.) + head (n.)  淋浴 + 头部 (指喷水的头部)  组合而成。
shower 来自古法语 *schouere,  最终源于拉丁语 *excutia （倾泻物）。
head 来自古英语 heafod，与古高地德语 houbit，古诺尔斯语 höfuð，拉丁语 caput 同源，意为头部。
