pita-bread: /ˈpiːtɑː bred/ | n. |皮塔饼 | A type of flatbread originating in the Middle East, characterized by a pocket-like structure formed during baking.  It's often used to make sandwiches or as a side dish. | 例句：I had a pita bread filled with falafel for lunch.  我午餐吃了一个夹鹰嘴豆泥的皮塔饼。  We served warm pita bread with hummus. 我们用鹰嘴豆泥搭配了热皮塔饼。 | 近义词：flatbread, pocket bread | 反义词：无
