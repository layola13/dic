limbos:/ˈlɪmboʊz/ | n. |炼狱，中间状态，不确定状态 | A state of uncertainty or stagnation; an intermediate or transitional state.  It can also refer to a limbo dance or a place of confinement. |例句：He's been in limbo since he lost his job. 他自从失业后就一直处于困境中。


The band's future is in limbo. 乐队的未来尚不确定。


近义词：uncertainty, purgatory, suspension, twilight | 反义词：certainty, clarity, resolution


词源: The word "limbo" ultimately derives from the Latin word "limbus," meaning "border," "edge," or "hem."  In medieval theology, "limbus" referred to the borderland between heaven and hell.  The modern English meaning evolved from this theological context.
