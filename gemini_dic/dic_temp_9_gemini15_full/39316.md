conirostral: /kənˈaɪroʊstrəl/ | adj. | 锥形的喙的；锥状嘴的 |  Having a conical or cone-shaped beak or bill.  This term is primarily used in zoology and ornithology to describe the shape of a bird's beak.  | Example: The conirostral bird easily cracked the hard seeds. 锥形的喙的鸟很容易啄开坚硬的种子。 | 近义词: coniform, conical | 反义词:  flat-billed, broad-billed
