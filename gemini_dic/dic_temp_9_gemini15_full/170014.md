protectionistic: /ˌproʊtɛkʃənˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 保护主义的 |

英文解析: Favoring or characterized by protectionism;  relating to policies designed to restrict imports and protect domestic industries.  It implies a belief that government intervention is necessary to shield domestic businesses from foreign competition.

例句:

The government implemented protectionistic trade policies to safeguard its struggling textile industry.  政府实施了保护主义贸易政策以保护其苦苦挣扎的纺织业。

His protectionistic views led him to advocate for higher tariffs on imported goods. 他的保护主义观点导致他主张对进口商品征收更高的关税。


近义词: protectionist, restrictive, isolationist, mercantilist

反义词: free-trade, liberal, laissez-faire
