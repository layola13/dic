carped:/kɑːpt/| vt.|吹毛求疵，抱怨，挑剔|To find fault with (someone or something) in a petty or persistent way.|例句：He constantly carped about the food. 他不断抱怨食物。|例句：She carped at her husband's driving. 她挑剔她丈夫的驾驶技术。|近义词：criticize, complain, nag, quibble|反义词：praise, compliment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He carps about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
They carp at every little detail. = 他们对每一个小细节都吹毛求疵。

一般过去时:
She carped at the waiter about the service. = 她向服务员抱怨服务。
He carped about his low salary last week. = 他上周抱怨他的低薪。

现在完成时:
He has carped about his job for years. = 他多年来一直在抱怨他的工作。
They have carped incessantly about the noise. = 他们一直在不停地抱怨噪音。

过去完成时:
She had carped about the movie before we even started watching it. = 我们还没开始看电影她就抱怨了。
They had carped about the lack of funding for months. = 他们几个月来一直在抱怨资金不足。

将来时:
I know he will carp about the new rules. = 我知道他会抱怨新规定的。
They will carp about the decision until the end. = 他们会一直抱怨这个决定到最后。

虚拟语气:
If he carped less, people would like him more. = 如果他不那么吹毛求疵，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish he wouldn't carp about everything. = 我希望他不必对所有事情都吹毛求疵。

被动语态:
His work was constantly carped at by his boss. = 他的工作经常受到老板的批评。
The proposal was carped at by the committee. = 这个提案受到了委员会的挑剔。

疑问句:
Does he often carp about trivial matters? = 他经常为琐事抱怨吗？
Why are they always carping at each other? = 他们为什么总是互相抱怨？

否定句:
He didn't carp about the long meeting. = 他没有抱怨漫长的会议。
She did not carp at his suggestions.  = 她没有对他的建议吹毛求疵。


PHRASE->
carp at someone = 吹毛求疵某人
carp about something = 抱怨某事
constantly carp = 不断抱怨
relentlessly carp = 持续不断地抱怨
carp and complain = 抱怨和抱怨
