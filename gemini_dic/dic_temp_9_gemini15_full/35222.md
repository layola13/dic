clueing:/ˈkluːɪŋ/| v.|线索提供；提示；暗示|The process of providing clues or hints, often to solve a puzzle or mystery.  It can also refer to the act of giving someone subtle indications or suggestions.|例句：The detective was carefully clueing the witnesses in to the suspect's movements.  侦探正在仔细地向证人暗示嫌疑人的行踪。


The teacher's subtle questions were clueing the students toward the answer. 老师微妙的问题正在引导学生找到答案。|近义词：hinting, suggesting, indicating|反义词：concealing, hiding, obscuring|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is clueing me in on the secret. 他正在向我暗示这个秘密。
She clues her students in on the exam format. 她向学生们暗示考试的格式。


一般过去时:
The newspaper article cleverly clued the readers into the conspiracy. 这篇报刊文章巧妙地向读者暗示了这场阴谋。
He clued me in on the latest gossip. 他向我透露了最新的八卦。


现在完成时:
They have clued us in on the changes to the project. 他们已经向我们暗示了项目的变更。
She has cleverly clued the audience in on the plot twist. 她巧妙地向观众暗示了剧情的反转。


过去完成时:
He had already clued her in on the surprise party. 他已经向她暗示了惊喜派对。
The clues had been carefully planted, clueing the detective in on the perpetrator. 线索已经被小心地埋下，引导侦探找到罪犯。


将来时:
I will clue you in on the details later. 我稍后会告诉你细节。
She will clue the team in on the new strategy. 她将向团队暗示新的策略。


虚拟语气:
If I had clued him in earlier, he wouldn't have made that mistake. 如果我早点告诉他，他就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had clued her in on the potential risks. 我真希望我早点提醒她潜在的风险。


被动语态:
The audience was cleverly clued in on the murderer's identity. 观众被巧妙地告知了凶手的身份。
The students were being clued in on the upcoming changes to the curriculum. 学生们正在被告知即将对课程进行的更改。


疑问句:
Are you clueing me in on something? 你是不是在暗示我什么？
Was he clueing her in on the affair? 他是不是在暗示她婚外情？


否定句:
I didn't clue him in on the problem. 我没有告诉他这个问题。
She didn't clue me in on her plans. 她没有告诉我她的计划。


PHRASE->
clue someone in: 向某人暗示
clue someone into something: 向某人暗示某事
clue in on: 暗示…
clue up:  了解情况；掌握信息
clue out:  理解；领会


词源:  Derived from the noun "clue," ultimately from the Old Norse word "klu".  The verb form "clueing" emerged later as a natural extension of the noun's meaning.
