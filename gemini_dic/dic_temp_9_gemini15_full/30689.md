certifiable:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪəbl/|/ˈsɝːtɪfaɪəbl/| adj.| 可证明的；合格的；(非正式) 疯疯癫癫的 |  capable of being certified; officially recognized or approved; (informal)  crazy, insane | The documents are certifiable as genuine.  这些文件可证明是真实的。|  His behavior is becoming increasingly certifiable.他的行为越来越疯狂。| verifiable, provable, credible |  sane, rational, normal |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is certifiable as a qualified accountant. 他被认证为合格会计师。
This statement is certifiable as true.  这个说法是可以证明为真的。


一般过去时:
The judge certified him as insane. 法官认定他精神失常。
The painting was certified as authentic. 这幅画被鉴定为真迹。


现在完成时:
The committee has certified the election results. 委员会已经认证了选举结果。
She has been certified as a yoga instructor. 她已被认证为瑜伽教练。


过去完成时:
The doctor had certified him as fit to work. 医生已证明他适合工作。
The documents had been certified before submission. 文件在提交之前已获得认证。


将来时:
The lab will certify the results tomorrow. 实验室明天将认证结果。
They will be certified as professionals once they pass the exam. 通过考试后他们将被认证为专业人士。


虚拟语气:
If the results were certifiable, we would proceed with the project. 如果结果是可证明的，我们将继续进行该项目。
If he were certifiable as sane, he could leave the hospital. 如果他被证明神智清醒，他就可以出院了。


被动语态:
The documents are certified by a notary public. 文件由公证人认证。
He was certified as unfit for duty. 他被证明不适合工作。


疑问句:
Is this product certifiable according to international standards?  这款产品是否符合国际标准认证？
Are these claims certifiable? 这些说法可信吗？


否定句:
The witness's testimony was not certifiable. 证人的证词不可信。
His mental state was not certifiable as normal. 他的精神状态并非正常。
