plea:/pliː/| n.|恳求，请求，抗辩|A plea is an urgent and emotional request for something.  It can also refer to a formal statement made in a law court. |例句：She made a plea for mercy. 她恳求宽恕。|例句：The defendant entered a plea of not guilty.  被告提出无罪抗辩。|近义词：request, appeal, supplication|反义词：demand, order


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes a plea for help. = 他恳求帮助。
She makes a plea to the court. = 她向法庭提出请求。

一般过去时:
He made a heartfelt plea to his father. = 他向父亲发出了衷心的请求。
The lawyer made a plea of innocence for his client. = 律师为他的当事人作了无罪辩护。

现在完成时:
She has made several pleas for funding. = 她已经多次恳求资金。
The defendant has entered a plea of guilty. = 被告已认罪。

过去完成时:
He had made a plea for forgiveness before he died. = 他死前曾请求原谅。
The lawyer had made a plea for leniency before the sentencing. = 律师在判刑前曾请求从宽处理。

将来时:
She will make a plea for the release of the prisoners. = 她将恳求释放囚犯。
The lawyer will make a plea for a lighter sentence. = 律师将请求从轻判刑。

虚拟语气:
If he made a plea, they might reconsider. = 如果他提出请求，他们可能会重新考虑。
I wish he had made a plea for help sooner. = 我希望他早点求助。

被动语态:
A plea for peace was made by the president. = 总统提出了和平请求。
The plea was rejected by the judge. = 法官驳回了请求。

疑问句:
What was his plea? = 他的请求是什么？
Will they accept her plea? = 他们会接受她的请求吗？

否定句:
He didn't make a plea for forgiveness. = 他没有请求原谅。
The defendant didn't enter a plea. = 被告没有提出抗辩。


PHRASE->

plea for mercy = 请求宽恕
