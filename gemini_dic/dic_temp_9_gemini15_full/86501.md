heir:/ɛr/| n.|继承人，后裔|A person legally entitled to inherit property or a title.|The king's heir will inherit the throne upon his death. 国王去世后，他的继承人将继承王位。|近义词：inheritor, successor|反义词：predecessor


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is the heir to a vast fortune. 他是一个巨额财富的继承人。
The heir apparent is expected to take the throne soon. 预计王储很快就会继承王位。


一般过去时:
She was the sole heir to her grandmother's estate. 她是祖母遗产的唯一继承人。
He became heir to the family business after his father's retirement. 他父亲退休后成为家族企业的继承人。


现在完成时:
He has become the heir to the company. 他已成为这家公司的继承人。
She has remained the heir to the throne for many years. 她多年来一直是王位的继承人。


过去完成时:
Before his death, he had named his son as his heir. 他去世前已指定儿子为继承人。
The previous owner had left no clear heir to the property.  前任业主没有留下明确的房产继承人。


将来时:
She will be the heir to the family's legacy. 她将是家族遗产的继承人。
He will inherit the title when his father passes away. 他父亲去世后将继承爵位。


虚拟语气:
If he were the heir, he would live a different life. 如果他是继承人，他就会过着不同的生活。
I wish I were the heir to that fortune. 我希望我是那笔财富的继承人。


被动语态:
The throne was inherited by the rightful heir. 王位由合法继承人继承。
The estate is being contested by multiple heirs. 多位继承人正在争夺遗产。


疑问句:
Who is the heir to the estate? 谁是这笔遗产的继承人？
Is she the heir apparent? 她是不是王储？


否定句:
He is not the heir to the throne. 他不是王位的继承人。
She was not named as an heir in the will. 遗嘱中没有将她列为继承人。
