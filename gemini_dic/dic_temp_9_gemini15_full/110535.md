limed:/laɪmd/| v. adj. n.|涂石灰于；用石灰处理；石灰处理过的|to cover or treat with lime|The farmer limed the soil to improve its alkalinity.  农民用石灰处理土壤以提高其碱度。|近义词：plastered, coated, whitewashed |反义词：unlimed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He limes the trees every spring. = 他每年春天都给树木涂石灰。
They lime the field to improve the soil. = 他们给田地施石灰来改善土壤。


一般过去时:
The gardener limed the garden last year. = 园丁去年给花园涂了石灰。
We limed the walls of the old building. = 我们给旧建筑的墙壁涂了石灰。


现在完成时:
They have limed the orchard to protect the trees. = 他们已经给果园涂了石灰来保护树木。
Farmers have limed the fields for generations. = 几代农民都在给田地施石灰。


过去完成时:
The trees had been limed before the winter frost. = 在冬季霜冻来临之前，树木已经涂上了石灰。
They had limed the walls many times before they finally peeled. = 在墙壁最终剥落之前，他们已经多次涂过石灰了。


将来时:
We will lime the driveway next week. = 我们下周将给车道涂石灰。
The council will lime the streets to prevent ice. = 理事会将给街道涂石灰以防止结冰。


虚拟语气:
If I had limed the garden, the pests wouldn't have infested it. = 如果我给花园涂了石灰，害虫就不会侵害它了。
I wish I had limed the trees earlier this year. = 我真希望今年早些时候给树木涂过石灰。


被动语态:
The walls were limed a brilliant white. = 墙壁被涂成了明亮的白色。
The soil is limed annually to maintain its pH. = 土壤每年都用石灰处理以保持其pH值。


疑问句:
Have you limed the fruit trees yet? = 你给果树涂石灰了吗？
Did they lime the path before the winter? = 他们在冬天之前给小路涂石灰了吗？


否定句:
We didn't lime the fence this year. = 我们今年没有给栅栏涂石灰。
They hadn't limed the fields before the planting. = 种植之前他们没有给田地施石灰。
