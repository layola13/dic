plevin:/ˈplɛvɪn/| n. |保证；担保；抵押|A plevin is a surety or pledge given to secure the release of someone from prison or to guarantee the appearance of someone in court.|例句：He posted plevin to secure his brother's release from jail. 他提供保证金以确保他兄弟从监狱获释。|近义词：bail, surety, security, guarantee |反义词:  None specifically.  The opposite concept would be a failure to provide security or a breach of guarantee. |名词
