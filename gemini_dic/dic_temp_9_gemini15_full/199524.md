singalong:/ˈsɪŋəˌlɒŋ/| n. |合唱，一起唱歌|A singalong is a performance or event where a group of people sing together, often informally and without instrumental accompaniment.  It implies a shared, joyful experience of singing. |例句：We had a singalong around the campfire. 我们在篝火旁一起唱歌。|例句：The children enjoyed the singalong at the end of the concert. 孩子们很喜欢音乐会结束时的合唱。|近义词：group singing, chorus, communal singing|反义词：solo performance, individual singing
