mediate:/ˈmiːdieɪt/| vt. n. adj.|调解；斡旋；居中；中间的；| to settle a dispute or argument between people or groups; to act as an intermediary between parties.|例句：The UN is mediating in the peace talks. 联合国正在调解和平谈判。|例句：He mediated a settlement between the two warring factions. 他在两个交战派系之间促成了和解。|近义词：arbitrate, conciliate, negotiate|反义词：aggravate, exacerbate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He mediates disputes between neighbors. 他调解邻居之间的纠纷。
The council mediates conflicts within the community. 理事会调解社区内的冲突。

一般过去时:
She mediated a successful agreement between the company and the union. 她促成了公司和工会之间的一份成功的协议。
The diplomat mediated a ceasefire between the two countries. 外交官促成了两国之间的停火。

现在完成时:
They have mediated several international conflicts. 他们已经调解了几起国际冲突。
The mediator has successfully mediated numerous disputes. 调解员已经成功调解了许多纠纷。

过去完成时:
Before the intervention, they had mediated several smaller disagreements. 在干预之前，他们已经调解了几次较小的分歧。
The judge had mediated the case before the trial began. 法官在审判开始前已经调解了此案。

将来时:
The government will mediate in the upcoming labor negotiations. 政府将在即将到来的劳资谈判中进行调解。
She will mediate the argument between the two brothers. 她将调解两个兄弟之间的争吵。

虚拟语气:
If he mediated the conflict, it would have been resolved sooner. 如果他调解了冲突，它本来可以更快地解决。
I wish she had mediated the dispute before it escalated. 我希望她在冲突升级之前就进行了调解。

被动语态:
The dispute was mediated by a neutral third party. 纠纷由一个中立的第三方调解。
The conflict is being mediated by a team of experienced negotiators. 冲突正在由一支经验丰富的谈判团队进行调解。

疑问句:
Will the government mediate the trade dispute? 政府会调解贸易纠纷吗？
Who will mediate the disagreement between the two parties? 谁将调解双方之间的分歧？

否定句:
He did not mediate the conflict. 他没有调解冲突。
The union refused to mediate the labor dispute. 工会拒绝调解劳资纠纷。


PHRASE->

mediate between: 在…之间进行调解
mediate a dispute: 调解纠纷
mediate an agreement: 促成协议
mediate a settlement: 促成和解
act as a mediator: 充当调解人


词源:

源自拉丁语 *mediāre "to be in the middle, to be between," from medius "middle."  字面意思就是“处于中间”。
