regularizing: /ˈrɛɡjələˌraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使规范化；使正规化 |  The process of making something conform to a standard or rule;  introducing constraints or penalties to a model to prevent overfitting. This often involves adding a penalty term to the loss function. | 例句：The government is regularizing the import of agricultural products. 政府正在规范农产品的进口。 | 例句：Regularizing the neural network prevents it from memorizing the training data and improves its generalization ability. 正则化神经网络可以防止其记住训练数据并提高其泛化能力。 | 近义词：standardizing, normalizing, regulating | 反义词：randomizing, deregulating | 动词
