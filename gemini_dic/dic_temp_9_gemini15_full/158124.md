plateau:/ˈplætoʊ/| n.|高原；稳定时期；停滞期 |A plateau is a relatively high, flat area of land.  It can also refer to a period of little or no progress or change in a particular activity or process.|例句：The hikers reached a high plateau after a long climb. 登山者经过长时间攀登后到达了一个高高的高原。

The company's profits reached a plateau this year. 今年公司利润达到了一个稳定期。|近义词：highland, tableland, standstill, stagnation|反义词：decline, descent, drop, upswing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The plateau stretches for miles. = 高原绵延数英里。
This project is currently on a plateau. = 这个项目目前处于停滞状态。

一般过去时:
The climbers rested on the plateau. = 登山者在高原上休息。
Sales plateaued after the initial surge. = 初始激增后，销量趋于平稳。

现在完成时:
The economy has plateaued after several years of growth. = 经济在经历了几年的增长后趋于平稳。
We have reached a plateau in our learning. = 我们的学习已经进入停滞期。

过去完成时:
The researchers had already reached a plateau in their experiments. = 研究人员在实验中已经达到了一个停滞期。
Before the breakthrough, the project had plateaued for months. = 在取得突破之前，该项目已经停滞了几个月。


将来时:
The researchers predict the plateau will last for several months. = 研究人员预测高原期将持续数月。
We expect the market to plateau next year. = 我们预计市场明年将趋于平稳。

虚拟语气:
If the economy hadn't plateaued, we would have seen further growth. = 如果经济没有趋于平稳，我们将看到进一步的增长。
I wish the negotiations hadn't plateaued at this point. = 我希望谈判不要在此时停滞不前。

被动语态:
The area was identified as a high plateau. = 该地区被确定为一个高高原。
Progress has been plateaued by unexpected challenges. = 由于意外的挑战，进展陷入了停滞。


疑问句:
Has the project reached a plateau? = 项目是否已经进入停滞期？
Is this plateau suitable for farming? = 这个高原适合耕作吗？

否定句:
The economy did not plateau despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，经济并没有趋于平稳。
The sales figures did not plateau; they continued to climb. = 销售数据并没有趋于平稳；它们继续攀升。
