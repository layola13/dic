hoofing:/ˈhuːfɪŋ/| v.| 徒步旅行；（用力）猛踢；| Walking, especially for a long distance.  The act of hitting something with one's foot forcefully. |例句：We spent the afternoon hoofing it across the countryside.  我们下午在乡间徒步旅行。

例句：He hoofed the ball across the field. 他用力把球踢过球场。|近义词：hiking, trekking, walking, kicking, stamping|反义词：driving, riding
