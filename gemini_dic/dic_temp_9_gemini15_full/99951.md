internuncius:/ˌɪntərˈnʌŋʃiəs/| n. |使节，中间人，信使|A messenger or envoy sent between two parties to negotiate or convey messages.  It often implies a neutral or mediating role.|例句：The internuncius traveled between the warring kingdoms, attempting to broker a peace treaty. 使节往来于交战的王国之间，试图调解达成和平条约。|例句：The Pope appointed a special internuncius to resolve the dispute between the two factions. 教皇任命了一位特使来解决两派之间的争端。|近义词：envoy, messenger, mediator, emissary|反义词：combatant, belligerent|名词
