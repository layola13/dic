esotericism:/ˌɛzəˈtɛrɪsɪzəm/ | n. |神秘主义；秘教；深奥难懂 |The study of esoteric knowledge, beliefs, or practices.  It often involves hidden or secret teachings, and is typically associated with spiritual or mystical traditions.  |例句：The book delves into the esotericism of ancient Egyptian rituals. 这本书深入探讨了古埃及仪式的奥秘。

例句：Her interest in esotericism led her to study Kabbalah. 她对神秘主义的兴趣使她去学习卡巴拉。


近义词：mysticism, occultism, mysticism, hermeticism |反义词：exotericism, common knowledge
