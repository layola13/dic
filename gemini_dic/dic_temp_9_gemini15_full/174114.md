quavers:/ˈkweɪvərz/| n. v.|颤抖；颤音；虚弱的声音|a trembling of the voice or body; a shaky sound; a short musical note equal to 1/8 of a semibreve|例句：Her voice quavered with emotion.  她的声音因激动而颤抖。
Her hands quavered as she held the fragile vase. 她拿着易碎的花瓶，双手颤抖。


近义词：tremble, shake, shiver, vibrate|反义词：steady, firm, stable|词性:n.v.
