mythologists:/ˌmɪθˈɑːlədʒɪsts/| n. |神话学家，神话研究者|People who study myths and legends. They analyze the origins, development, and cultural significance of myths across different societies and time periods.  Mythologists often employ interdisciplinary approaches, drawing on anthropology, linguistics, history, and religious studies to interpret myths.|Example:  Many mythologists believe the story of Persephone reflects the changing seasons.  许多神话学家认为珀耳塞福涅的故事反映了季节的变化。

Example:  The mythologists debated the symbolic meaning of the dragon in various cultures.  神话学家们就不同文化中龙的象征意义进行了辩论。


近义词:myth scholars, folklore experts, legend researchers

反义词:无
