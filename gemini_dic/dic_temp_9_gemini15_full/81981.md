grinds:/ɡraɪndz/| v. n.|磨碎；折磨；努力学习|To reduce to powder or small particles by friction; to oppress or harass; to study diligently.|例句：He grinds the coffee beans every morning. 他每天早上都磨咖啡豆。She grinds her teeth in her sleep. 她睡觉时磨牙。|近义词：crush, pulverize, oppress, torment, study hard|反义词：polish, smooth, soothe, relax


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grinds the wheat into flour. 他把小麦磨成面粉。
She grinds her teeth at night.  她晚上磨牙。


一般过去时:
They ground the spices before adding them to the stew.  他们在把香料加入炖菜之前把它们磨碎了。
The constant noise ground down my patience.  持续的噪音磨损了我的耐心。


现在完成时:
He has ground the lenses to a perfect curve. 他已经把镜片磨成完美的曲线。
The constant pressure has ground her down.  持续的压力把她压垮了。


过去完成时:
She had ground the pepper before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，她已经磨好了胡椒粉。
He had ground himself down to exhaustion. 他把自己累垮了。


将来时:
I will grind the coffee beans before we have guests. 我们有客人之前我会磨咖啡豆。
She will grind through this difficult period. 她会度过这段艰难时期。


虚拟语气:
If I had a grinder, I would grind my own spices. 如果我有一个研磨机，我会自己磨香料。
If she hadn't ground herself so hard, she wouldn't be so tired. 如果她没有那么努力，她就不会这么累了。


被动语态:
The coffee beans are ground by machine. 咖啡豆是用机器磨的。
The students are ground down by the heavy workload. 学生们被繁重的作业压垮了。


疑问句:
Do you grind your own coffee beans? 你自己磨咖啡豆吗？
Does this machine grind wheat effectively? 这台机器有效地磨小麦吗？


否定句:
He doesn't grind his own coffee beans; he buys pre-ground coffee. 他不自己磨咖啡豆；他买预磨咖啡。
She didn't grind the pepper fine enough. 她胡椒粉没有磨得足够细。
