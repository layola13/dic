disillusionist: /ˌdɪsɪˈluʒənɪst/ | n. | 幻灭主义者；理想破灭者 | A person who exposes or is disillusioned by illusions, idealism, or false beliefs.  Someone who reveals the falseness or disappointing nature of something previously idealized. | Example: The disillusionist writer exposed the hypocrisy of the political system.  幻灭主义作家揭露了政治体制的虚伪。  The disillusionist speaker challenged the audience’s naive optimism.  幻灭主义的演讲者挑战了听众天真的乐观主义。 | 近义词：cynic, realist, skeptic | 反义词：idealist, dreamer, optimist |
