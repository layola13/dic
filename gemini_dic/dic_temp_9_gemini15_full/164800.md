prefiguratively:/ˌpriːfɪɡjʊˈreɪtɪvli/| adv.| 预示性地，预兆性地 |  In a way that foreshadows or prefigures something; symbolically anticipating something that will happen later.  |例句：The artist's early works prefiguratively hinted at the mature style he would later develop.  这位艺术家的早期作品预示性地暗示了他后来将发展成熟的风格。

例句：The current economic anxieties prefiguratively reflect a larger societal shift. 当前的经济焦虑预示着更大的社会变革。


近义词：symbolically, prophetically, foreshadowingly


反义词：literally, directly, explicitly


词性：副词
