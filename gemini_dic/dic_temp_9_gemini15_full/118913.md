词源:
The word "metazoan" is derived from the Greek words "meta" (after, beyond) and "zoon" (animal).  It literally means "beyond animals," or more accurately, "animals that are more than just a single cell."  The term distinguishes multicellular animals from single-celled organisms (protozoa).
