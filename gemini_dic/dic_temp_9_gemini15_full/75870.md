functionarism:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. | 功能主义；机能主义 | Functionalism is a theory that emphasizes the functions of objects, institutions, or social structures.  It focuses on how things contribute to the overall stability and functioning of a system.  For example, in sociology, functionalism views society as a complex system whose parts work together to promote solidarity and stability. |  Example:  His architectural design reflects a strong belief in functionalism, prioritizing practicality over ornamentation.  他的建筑设计体现了他对功能主义的强烈信念，优先考虑实用性而不是装饰性。| 近义词：pragmatism, utilitarianism | 反义词：formalism, idealism
