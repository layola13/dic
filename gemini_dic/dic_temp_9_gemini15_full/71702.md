flavored:/ˈfleɪvərd/ | adj. | 调味的，有…味道的 | Having a particular flavor added.  | 例句：The cake was flavored with chocolate.  这个蛋糕加了巧克力味。

例句：This yogurt is flavored with strawberry. 这款酸奶是草莓味的。


近义词：seasoned, scented, infused, aromatized | 反义词：unflavored, plain, tasteless


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ice cream is flavored with vanilla. = 冰淇淋是香草味的。
This tea is flavored with lemon. = 这茶是柠檬味的。


一般过去时:
The cookies were flavored with cinnamon. = 饼干是肉桂味的。
The soup was flavored with herbs. = 汤里加了香草。


现在完成时:
The chef has flavored the dish with exotic spices. = 厨师用异域香料调味了这道菜。
Many desserts have been flavored with artificial sweeteners. = 许多甜点都用人造甜味剂调味。


过去完成时:
The cake had been flavored with almond extract before baking. = 蛋糕在烘焙前已经用杏仁精调味了。
She had flavored the sauce with a hint of chili. = 她用少许辣椒给酱汁调味。


将来时:
I will flavor the meat with garlic and pepper. = 我将用大蒜和胡椒粉给肉调味。
The pastry chef will flavor the croissants with orange zest. = 糕点厨师将用橙皮屑给羊角面包调味。


虚拟语气:
If I flavored the soup with more salt, it would taste better. = 如果我往汤里加更多的盐，味道会更好。
I wish I had flavored the stew with more herbs. = 我真希望我当时在炖菜里加更多香草。


被动语态:
The bread is flavored by a special blend of spices. = 面包是用一种特殊的香料混合物调味的。
The drink is being flavored with fresh fruit juices. = 饮料正在用新鲜的果汁调味。


疑问句:
Is the pudding flavored with chocolate? = 布丁是巧克力味的？
How is the coffee flavored? = 咖啡是怎么调味的？


否定句:
The yogurt isn't flavored with anything artificial. = 这款酸奶没有添加任何人工香料。
The water wasn't flavored, it was plain. = 水没有调味，是纯净水。
