lair:/ler/| n.|兽穴；藏身处；巢穴 |A lair is the home or den of a wild animal, especially a large one.  It can also refer to a secret or hidden place where someone lives or hides. |例句：The lion retreated to its lair.  狮子退回到它的巢穴。

The criminal hid in his secret lair. 罪犯躲藏在他的秘密藏身处。|近义词：den, hideout, hideaway, retreat|反义词：open, public, exposed|名词
