词源:
The word "noncoincidentally" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not"), the adjective "coincident" (meaning "occurring at the same time; simultaneous"), and the adverbial suffix "-ally" (meaning "in a manner").  Therefore, it literally means "not coincidentally."
