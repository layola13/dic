reabridging:/ˌriːˈbrɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|重新缩短；重新简缩 |To shorten or condense something again.  This word is a relatively rare and less common usage, formed by combining "re-" (again) and "abridging" (shortening). It implies a second shortening or condensation of something already shortened.|例句：The editor decided to reabridge the already condensed novel, making it even more concise. 编辑决定再次缩短这部已经浓缩的小说，使其更加简洁。  The committee reabridged the lengthy report, focusing only on the key findings. 委员会再次缩短了冗长的报告，只关注了主要发现。|近义词：revise, condense, shorten, abridge |反义词：expand, lengthen, elaborate
