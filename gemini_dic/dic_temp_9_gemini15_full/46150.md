/ˈkʌmrʊ/| n. |威尔士|The Welsh name for Wales.  It is also used by Welsh speakers as a term of endearment for Wales. |Example:  Cymru is a land of stunning natural beauty. 威尔士是一个拥有令人惊叹自然美景的国度。|近义词：Wales (English name)|反义词：无|名词


USAGE->

This word is a proper noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs or adjectives.  Therefore, grammatical tenses and other conjugations are not applicable.  Its usage is limited to referring to Wales.


PHRASE->

There are no common phrases directly built around "Cymru"  as it is primarily a proper noun. However, phrases like "Croeso i Gymru" (Welcome to Wales) are commonly used.
