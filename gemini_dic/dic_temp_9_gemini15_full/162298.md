preaccord:/priːəˈkɔːrd/| n. |预先协议，初步协议 |A preaccord is a preliminary agreement, often informal, that sets the stage for a more formal and legally binding contract.  It outlines the key terms and conditions but lacks the full detail and legal enforceability of a final agreement.  |Example: The two companies reached a preaccord on the merger, pending final due diligence.  两家公司就合并达成初步协议，但需等待最终尽职调查。|近义词： preliminary agreement, tentative agreement, understanding |反义词：final agreement, binding contract |名词
