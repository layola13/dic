relinquishments: /rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃmənts/ | n. |放弃；放弃的行为；让步 | The act of relinquishing something; giving up possession, control, or claim.  Relinquishments can refer to tangible things (property, rights) or intangible things (power, responsibility).|


例句：

The treaty involved significant territorial relinquishments. = 这项条约涉及重大的领土让步。

His relinquishments of his managerial position surprised everyone. = 他放弃管理职务的行为让所有人都感到惊讶。


近义词：surrenders, abandonments, concessions, waivers


反义词：acquisitions, retentions, assertions, claims


词性：名词
