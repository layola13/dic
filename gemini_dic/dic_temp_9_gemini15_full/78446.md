getaways:/ˈɡɛteweɪz/ | n. | 短途旅行；逃亡；逃避现实的方式 | A getaway is a short trip taken for pleasure or relaxation, often to a place that is different from one's usual environment. It can also refer to an escape from a difficult or unpleasant situation. |例句：We took a weekend getaway to the beach. 我们去海滩度过了一个周末短途旅行。

We need a getaway from the pressures of work. 我们需要逃离工作压力。|近义词：vacation, escape, holiday, break |反义词：stay, confinement, routine
