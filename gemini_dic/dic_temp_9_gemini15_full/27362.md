cambrics:/ˈkæmbrɪks/| n.|细棉布；细布|A fine, lightweight cotton fabric, originally from Cambrai, France.  It is known for its smooth, even texture and is often used for clothing and linens.|例句：The shirt is made of exquisite cambrics. 这件衬衫是用精美的细棉布做的。|例句：She used cambrics to make delicate handkerchiefs. 她用细棉布制作精美的手帕。|近义词：muslin, batiste|反义词:denim, canvas
