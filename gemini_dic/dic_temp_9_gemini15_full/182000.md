remeant:/rɪˈment/| v.|意指；本来打算；意味着 |  The word "remeant" is the past tense of "remean," which itself is a less common variant of "mean."  It signifies an intended meaning or implication, often retrospectively.  It suggests a reconsideration or re-evaluation of the original meaning. | 例句：He realized what he'd remeant by his earlier comments. 他意识到他之前评论的本意。  |  近义词：intended, implied, signified, meant | 反义词：misunderstood, misinterpreted | 动词
