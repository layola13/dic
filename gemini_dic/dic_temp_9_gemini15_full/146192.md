overexertions:/ˌoʊvərɪˈzɜːrʃənz/| n. |过度劳累；过度使用|The overuse or excessive strain of a body part or system resulting in injury or exhaustion.  It often refers to physical exertion but can also encompass mental or emotional strain. |例句：His overexertions led to a severe muscle strain.  他的过度劳累导致了严重的肌肉拉伤。

例句：Years of overexertions at work finally caught up with him, leading to a burnout.  多年的工作过度劳累最终让他筋疲力尽。


近义词：overexertion, strain, overuse, fatigue, exhaustion, burnout

反义词：rest, relaxation, recuperation

词性:名词
