receed:/rɪˈsiːd/| vi.|消退；后退；减弱|To move back or away from a previous position; to become less intense or noticeable.|The flood waters finally receded.  洪水最终退去了。|The pain gradually receded.  疼痛逐渐减轻了。|advance, retreat, subside|intensify, increase, advance|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tide recedes twice a day. = 潮汐一天退两次。
His anger recedes slowly. = 他的怒气慢慢消退。

一般过去时:
The storm receded after several hours. = 暴风雨持续数小时后消退了。
The symptoms receded after taking the medicine. = 服药后症状消退了。

现在完成时:
The floodwaters have receded, leaving behind mud and debris. = 洪水已经退去，留下了泥土和碎屑。
The effects of the drug have receded. = 药效已经减退了。

过去完成时:
The pain had receded before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，疼痛已经消退了。
The swelling had receded significantly by morning. = 到早上，肿胀已经明显消退了。

将来时:
The flood is expected to recede by tomorrow. = 预计洪水将于明天消退。
Hopefully, the inflammation will recede in a few days. = 希望炎症会在几天内消退。


虚拟语气:
If the fever receded, he would feel better. = 如果退烧了，他会感觉好些。
If the tide had receded earlier, we could have gone to the beach. = 如果潮水早些退去，我们就可以去海滩了。

被动语态:
The receding tide was observed by the scientists. = 科学家们观察到了退潮。 (This is less common as 'recede' is intransitive)


疑问句:
Has the swelling receded yet? = 肿胀消退了吗？
Will the pain recede soon? = 疼痛很快就会消退吗？

否定句:
The flood waters haven't receded yet. = 洪水还没有退去。
The inflammation didn't recede as quickly as expected. = 炎症没有像预期的那样快速消退。


PHRASE->
recede into the background = 退居幕后
recede from view = 从视野中消失
recede into memory = 逐渐淡忘
recede into the distance = 远去
recede into obscurity = 逐渐被遗忘
