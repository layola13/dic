nonadvocacies:/ˌnɒnˈædvəkəsiːz/ | n. |非拥护；不支持；反对立场 |The absence of advocacy or support for a particular cause or viewpoint.  It refers to the lack of active promotion or defense of something.  This word is relatively uncommon and often found in academic or formal contexts. |例句：The report highlighted the nonadvocacies surrounding the issue of climate change in certain political circles.  这份报告强调了某些政治圈子对气候变化问题的漠视。

The study revealed a significant level of nonadvocacies for stricter gun control laws. 这项研究揭示了对更严格的枪支管制法的强烈反对。 |近义词：opposition, lack of support, indifference, apathy |反义词：advocacy, support, championship, promotion
