credentialism:/krɪˌdɛnʃəlɪzəm/ | n. |凭证主义；文凭至上主义 |The emphasis on academic or professional credentials as the basis for evaluating a person's ability or worth.  It often involves prioritizing formal qualifications over practical experience or other relevant skills.  | Example:  The company's credentialism led them to overlook highly skilled but less formally educated candidates. = 这家公司的文凭至上主义导致他们忽视了那些高技能但受教育程度较低的候选人。  |近义词：elitism, meritocracy (in a negative context) |反义词：practicalism, experience-based hiring |
