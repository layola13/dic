outshoot:/ˌaʊtˈʃuːt/| vt.|胜过；射中更多目标|To surpass in shooting; to shoot more than (someone else).  This word implies a competition or contest involving shooting, such as archery, hunting, or target practice. |例句：The archer outshot all his competitors.  弓箭手射中目标的数量超过所有竞争对手。

例句：Our team outshot the opponents by ten goals.  我们的球队进球数比对方多十个。


近义词：outperform, surpass, exceed

反义词：be outshot by, undershoot
