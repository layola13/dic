preinterest:/ˌpriːˈɪntrəst/| n. |预先的兴趣；先前的兴趣|Interest that exists before a particular event or action.  It often refers to a level of interest or engagement that precedes a specific trigger or intervention.|The preinterest in the topic was low, but after the engaging presentation, it increased significantly.|近义词：prior interest, antecedent interest|反义词：postinterest
