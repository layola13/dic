parodied:/ˈpærədiːd/| v. |模仿，戏仿|To imitate the style of something, typically in a humorous or satirical way.|The comedian parodied the president's speech.  喜剧演员模仿了总统的演讲。|mimicked, satirized, lampooned | serious, earnest
