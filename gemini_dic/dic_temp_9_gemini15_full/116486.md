materialized:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzd/ | v. | 实现；具体化；显现 | To become real or actual; to take on a physical form.  |例句：His dreams materialized into a successful business. 他的梦想变成了成功的企业。

例句：The ghost materialized before our very eyes.  鬼魂在我们眼前显现。


近义词：realized, manifested, appeared, emerged | 反义词：disappeared, vanished, evaporated
