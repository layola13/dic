rates:/reɪts/ | n. |比率；速度；价格；等级|The word "rates" refers to a measure, quantity, or frequency typically expressed as a ratio. It can denote the speed at which something happens, the price of something, or a level or rank within a system.  |例句：Interest rates are rising. 利率正在上升。

The exchange rates are favorable today.今天的汇率很优惠。


近义词：ratios, prices, speeds, tariffs, quotas, rankings | 反义词：None (depends on context)|名词
