extremism: /ɪkˈstriːmɪzəm/ | n. | 极端主义 | The holding of extreme political or religious views; the use of extreme methods to achieve political aims.  | Example:  Religious extremism led to the violence.  宗教极端主义导致了暴力。 The rise of political extremism is a serious concern. 极端政治思想的兴起令人担忧。| 近义词：radicalism, fanaticism | 反义词：moderation, tolerance |  名词
