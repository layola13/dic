niggling:/ˈnɪɡlɪŋ/| adj.|令人烦扰的，微不足道的，琐碎的| causing slight but persistent annoyance or worry; trifling.|例句：I have a niggling doubt about the project's success. 我对项目的成功有点儿挥之不去的怀疑。|例句：It's just a niggling detail, but it needs to be addressed. 这只是一个微不足道的细节，但需要解决。|近义词：annoying, irritating, bothersome, trifling, petty|反义词：major, significant, important
