integration:/ˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. |整合；集成；一体化 |The process of combining different parts or elements into a whole.  It involves bringing things together to form a unified system or entity.  This can apply to various contexts, from social structures to computer systems. |例句：The integration of new technologies into the workplace has improved efficiency.  工作场所新技术的整合提高了效率。
The successful integration of immigrants into society is a complex challenge.  成功地将移民融入社会是一个复杂的挑战。|近义词：combination, unification, consolidation, synthesis |反义词：segregation, separation, division


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company integrates new software every year. = 公司每年整合新的软件。
This system integrates seamlessly with others. = 这个系统可以与其他系统无缝集成。

一般过去时:
They integrated the feedback into the final product. = 他们将反馈整合到最终产品中。
The two companies integrated last quarter. = 这两家公司在上个季度合并了。


现在完成时:
We have integrated all the data into the report. = 我们已将所有数据整合到报告中。
The project has been successfully integrated. = 项目已成功整合。


过去完成时:
They had integrated the new system before the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前整合了新系统。
The team had already integrated the changes before the meeting. = 团队在会议之前就已经整合了更改。


将来时:
We will integrate the new features next month. = 我们下个月将整合新功能。
The company will integrate its two branches. = 公司将合并其两个分支机构。


虚拟语气:
If we integrated the two systems, it would improve efficiency. = 如果我们将这两个系统整合，效率将会提高。
It would be beneficial if we integrated more diverse voices.  如果我们整合更多不同的声音，将是有益的。


被动语态:
The new software was integrated into the system. = 新软件已集成到系统中。
The data is being integrated into the database. = 数据正在被集成到数据库中。


疑问句:
How will the new system be integrated? = 新系统将如何集成？
Have they integrated the feedback yet? = 他们是否已经整合了反馈？


否定句:
The two systems did not integrate properly. = 这两个系统没有正确集成。
They haven't yet integrated the new employee into the team. = 他们还没有将新员工融入团队。
