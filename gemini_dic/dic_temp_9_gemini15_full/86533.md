held:/held/| v.| 举行；持有；控制；包含 | to have something in your hands or arms; to possess something; to organize or be in charge of an event; to contain something. |例句：She held the baby gently. 她轻轻地抱着婴儿。
The museum held a special exhibition. 博物馆举行了一次特别的展览。|近义词：grasp, possess, organize, contain |反义词：release, relinquish, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He holds the door open for her. = 他为她扶着门。
She holds a senior position in the company. = 她在公司担任高级职位。


一般过去时:
They held a meeting yesterday. = 他们昨天开会了。
He held my hand tightly. = 他紧紧地握着我的手。


现在完成时:
She has held that job for five years. = 她已经做了那份工作五年了。
They have held many successful events. = 他们已经举办了许多成功的活动。


过去完成时:
He had held the position before he retired. = 他退休前担任过这个职位。
They had held a secret meeting before the announcement. = 他们在宣布之前举行了一次秘密会议。


将来时:
They will hold a conference next month. = 他们下个月将举行一次会议。
She will hold onto her beliefs. = 她将坚持自己的信念。


虚拟语气:
If she held a higher position, she would have more power. = 如果她担任更高的职位，她就会更有权力。
I wish I had held onto that opportunity. = 我真希望我当时抓住了那个机会。


被动语态:
The meeting was held in the conference room. = 会议在会议室举行。
The election will be held next Tuesday. = 选举将于下周二举行。


疑问句:
Does he hold any grudges? = 他怀恨在心吗？
Who held the responsibility? = 谁负有责任？


否定句:
He didn't hold back his tears. = 他没有忍住眼泪。
They didn't hold the event as planned. = 他们没有按照计划举行活动。
