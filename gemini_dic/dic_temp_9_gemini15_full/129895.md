non-ionizing: /ˌnɒnˈaɪənaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. |非电离的|Not producing ions; referring to radiation that does not have enough energy to remove electrons from atoms or molecules.  This type of radiation is generally considered less harmful than ionizing radiation. |Example:  Ultraviolet light is a form of non-ionizing radiation. 紫外线是一种非电离辐射。


Example:  Microwaves are another example of non-ionizing radiation used in many household appliances. 微波是另一种在许多家用电器中使用的非电离辐射。


近义词: non-ionising (British spelling)


反义词: ionizing


词性: adjective
