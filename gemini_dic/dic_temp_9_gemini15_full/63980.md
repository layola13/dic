epistemological:/ˌɛpɪstɪməˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/| adj.|认识论的；认识论上的|Relating to the theory of knowledge, especially with regard to its methods, validity, and scope.  It deals with questions like "What is knowledge?", "How is knowledge acquired?", and "What are the limits of knowledge?".|The philosopher presented an epistemological argument for the existence of God.  这位哲学家提出了一个关于上帝存在的认识论论证。|近义词：cognitive, theoretical|反义词：empirical, practical
