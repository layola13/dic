pasteurising: /ˈpæs.tʃər.aɪ.zɪŋ/ | v. |巴氏消毒法；低温消毒 |The process of heating a liquid, such as milk, to a specific temperature for a specific length of time to kill harmful bacteria without significantly altering the taste or nutritional value.  |例句：The milk is pasteurised to kill harmful bacteria. 这牛奶经过巴氏消毒杀死了有害细菌。 |例句：The company pasteurizes its juices before bottling. 公司在装瓶前对果汁进行巴氏消毒。|近义词：sterilizing (更彻底的消毒) |反义词：None (没有完全的反义词，因为巴氏消毒不是一个二元对立的概念)|动词
