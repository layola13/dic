misattributes:/ˌmɪsˈætrɪbjuːts/| vt.|错误归因，误归于|To attribute (something) to the wrong cause, person, or source.|例句：He misattributes the quote to Shakespeare, when it was actually written by Marlowe. 他错误地将这句名言归于莎士比亚，而实际上是马洛写的。|近义词：misassign, miscredit|反义词：attribute correctly, correctly assign
