grammar:/ˈɡræmər/ | n. | 语法 | The system and structure of a language.  It includes the rules that govern its sentence structure, word formation, and the ways different parts of speech combine to create meaning.  Grammar encompasses morphology (word formation), syntax (sentence structure), phonology (sound system), and semantics (meaning).| Example:  He has excellent grammar. 他语法很好。
| Example: Learning grammar can improve your writing skills. 学习语法可以提高你的写作能力。|近义词：syntax, morphology, linguistics | 反义词：None.
