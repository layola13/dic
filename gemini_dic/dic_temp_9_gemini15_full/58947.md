duster:/ˈdʌstər/| n.|除尘器，掸子，旧汽车|A duster is a tool used for removing dust, typically a long-handled brush or a cloth. It can also refer to an old car, especially one used for racing or rallying.|例句：She used a feather duster to clean the antique furniture. 她用羽毛掸子擦拭古董家具。|例句：That old Ford is a real duster. 那辆旧福特车真是一辆老爷车。|近义词：cloth, brush, mop|反义词：None specifically.|名词
