distress:/dɪˈstrɛs/ | n. v. |痛苦，不幸；使痛苦，使悲伤 |  Distress is a state of extreme anxiety, sorrow, or pain.  As a verb, it means to cause someone to feel this way. |例句：The news caused her great distress.  这消息让她非常痛苦。

The accident distressed him deeply.  这起事故让他深感痛苦。|近义词：anguish, suffering, misery, torment |反义词：comfort, joy, happiness, peace |名词，动词
