evolving: /ɪˈvɒlviŋ/ | adj. | 进化的；发展的 |  Describes something that is changing gradually over time, often in a positive or progressive way.  It emphasizes the process of change itself. | 例句：The evolving technology is changing the way we live.  正在发展的科技正在改变我们的生活方式。


The company's evolving strategy is showing promising results. 公司正在发展的战略显示出可喜的成果。


近义词：developing, progressing, changing, unfolding, maturing | 反义词：static, unchanging, stagnant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is evolving rapidly. = 情况正在迅速发展。
The technology is evolving at an incredible pace. = 科技正在以惊人的速度发展。


一般过去时:
The relationship evolved slowly over time. = 他们的关系随着时间的推移慢慢发展。
The design evolved from a simple sketch. = 设计是从简单的草图发展而来的。


现在完成时:
The company has evolved significantly since its inception. = 自成立以来，该公司已经有了显著的发展。
The software has evolved to meet the changing needs of its users. = 该软件已经发展到满足用户不断变化的需求。


过去完成时:
By the time the project finished, the technology had evolved considerably. = 到项目结束时，技术已经有了相当大的发展。
The understanding of the disease had evolved significantly by then.  到那时为止，对这种疾病的理解已经有了显著的提高。


将来时:
The situation will evolve further in the coming weeks. = 情况将在未来几周进一步发展。
We expect the market to evolve in a predictable manner. = 我们预计市场将以可预测的方式发展。


虚拟语气:
If the technology had evolved faster, we would have had better results. = 如果技术发展得更快，我们将会有更好的结果。
I wish the situation had evolved differently. = 我希望情况的发展有所不同。


被动语态:
The plan is being evolved by the committee. = 该计划正在由委员会制定发展。
The theory was evolved over many years of research. = 这一理论是经过多年的研究发展而来的。


疑问句:
How is the project evolving? = 项目进展如何？
Is the situation evolving as expected? = 情况是否按预期发展？


否定句:
The design hasn't evolved much since the initial prototype. = 自最初的原型以来，设计并没有太大变化。
The company's strategy hasn't evolved to meet the new challenges. = 公司的战略并没有发展以应对新的挑战。



PHRASE->
evolving situation = 正在发展的情况
evolving market = 发展中的市场
evolving technology = 发展中的科技
evolving relationship = 发展中的关系
evolving world = 发展中的世界


词源:
From the present participle of the verb *evolve*, which comes from the Latin word *evolvere*, meaning "to unroll, unfold."  The sense of gradual development and change is derived from the image of something unfolding or gradually revealing itself.
