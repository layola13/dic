heifer:/ˈhiːfər/| n. |小母牛，未生育的母牛 | A young cow that has not yet had a calf.  Heifers are typically raised for breeding or for meat production. |例句：The farmer carefully selected the best heifers for breeding.  这位农民仔细挑选了最好的小母牛用于繁殖。

The rancher is raising several heifers for the upcoming market.  这位牧场主正在为即将到来的市场饲养几头小母牛。


近义词：young cow, heifer calf |反义词：cow (in the sense of a mature, breeding cow)
