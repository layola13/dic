pre-Mongolian:/ˌpriːˈmɒŋɡəliən/| adj.| 前蒙古的，蒙古之前的 |  Describing a period or culture existing before the rise of the Mongol Empire.  The term usually refers to the various Turkic and other nomadic groups inhabiting the Eurasian steppe before the unification under Genghis Khan. | Example: Pre-Mongolian cultures in Central Asia often engaged in pastoral nomadism.  前蒙古时期中亚的文化通常从事游牧生活。|近义词：pre-imperial, antecedent |反义词：post-Mongolian
