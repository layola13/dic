proverb:/ˈprɒvɜːrb/ /ˈprɑːvɜːrb/| n. |谚语，格言|A proverb is a short, well-known saying that expresses a common truth or piece of advice.  |Example:  "A stitch in time saves nine" is a well-known proverb.  一句及时缝补可省九针是一句众所周知的谚语。

|Example: The proverb "Look before you leap" warns against acting rashly.  谚语“三思而后行”告诫人们不要轻率行事。|近义词：saying, maxim, adage, aphorism |反义词：无
