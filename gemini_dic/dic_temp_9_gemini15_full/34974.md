clodpoll:/ˈklɒdˌpɒl/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜 |A clodpoll is a stupid or foolish person.  The term is considered archaic and somewhat offensive. It's a compound word combining "clod" (a lump of earth) and "poll" (head), suggesting a person with a head as dull and unfeeling as a clod of earth.|He was called a clodpoll for his incredibly foolish decision. 他因为极其愚蠢的决定而被称为笨蛋。|  The farmer, though hardworking, was sometimes considered a clodpoll by the townsfolk.  虽然勤劳，但这位农民有时会被镇上的人认为是个傻瓜。|dunce, idiot, fool |genius, intellectual |名词
