panicked:/ˈpænɪkt/| adj.|惊慌的，恐慌的|Feeling or showing sudden uncontrollable fear.|The child panicked when he got lost in the woods.  孩子在森林里迷路时惊慌失措了。|He panicked at the sight of the snake. 他看到蛇就惊慌了。|近义词： terrified, frightened, scared, alarmed|反义词：calm, composed, tranquil

USAGE->

一般现在时:
He panics easily. 他很容易惊慌。
She panics when she sees spiders. 她看到蜘蛛就惊慌。

一般过去时:
I panicked when I heard the news. 我听到这个消息时惊慌了。
They panicked and ran for their lives. 他们惊慌失措地逃命。

现在完成时:
She has panicked several times during the competition.  她在比赛中好几次都惊慌失措了。
He has never panicked in a crisis before. 他以前从未在危机中惊慌过。

过去完成时:
I had panicked before I even realized what was happening.  在我意识到发生了什么之前，我已经惊慌失措了。
They had panicked earlier, resulting in a poor decision.  他们早些时候就惊慌失措了，导致做出了糟糕的决定。


将来时:
I will panic if I fail the exam. 如果考试不及格，我会惊慌失措。
She thinks she will panic during the presentation. 她认为自己在演示过程中会惊慌失措。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't panic. 如果我是你，我就不会惊慌。
I wish I hadn't panicked so much. 我真希望自己当时不要那么惊慌。


被动语态:
He was panicked by the sudden noise. 他被突如其来的噪音吓坏了。(被动语态更常用的是"He was frightened by...")
The crowd was panicked by the fire.  人群被大火惊慌了。(被动语态更常用的是"The crowd was frightened by...")


疑问句:
Did you panic when you saw the accident? 你看到事故时惊慌了吗？
Will they panic if the plan fails? 如果计划失败，他们会惊慌吗？


否定句:
He didn't panic during the emergency. 他在紧急情况下没有惊慌。
She didn't panic, even though she was scared. 尽管害怕，她也没有惊慌。
