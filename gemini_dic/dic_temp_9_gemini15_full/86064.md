hear-things:/hɪər θɪŋz/| v. | 听觉幻觉，胡思乱想 | To perceive sounds or voices that are not actually present; to imagine or believe things that are not true.  This is often associated with mental health conditions. | Example: She started hear-things after the accident, claiming to hear voices whispering her name.  她事故后开始出现幻听，声称听到有人在低声呼唤她的名字。|近义词：hallucinate, imagine, fantasize |反义词：hear clearly, perceive accurately

They hear things that aren't there. = 他们听到一些不存在的声音。
