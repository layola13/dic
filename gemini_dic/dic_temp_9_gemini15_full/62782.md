engraving:/ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/| n.|雕刻；雕版印刷|The process of cutting or carving a design into a hard surface, such as wood, metal, or stone; also refers to the resulting image or print.|例句：The antique box featured intricate wood engraving. 这只古董盒子饰有精美的木刻图案。

例句：He admired the fine detail in the copperplate engraving. 他欣赏铜版雕刻的精细细节。


近义词：etching, carving, embossing|反义词：smoothing, polishing|词性：名词
