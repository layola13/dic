periuterine: /ˌpɛriˈjuːtəraɪn/ | adj. | 子宫周围的 |  Relating to the area around the uterus.  This term describes the location or position of something in proximity to the uterus.  It does not inherently describe a specific structure or condition.

例句：
The periuterine tissues were carefully examined during the surgery. 手术期间仔细检查了子宫周围的组织。

The pain was localized to the periuterine region. 疼痛局限于子宫周围区域。


近义词：perimetric (if referring to the perimeter), circumuterine


反义词：intrauterine (within the uterus)


词性：形容词
