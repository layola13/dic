introspecting: /ˌɪntrəˈspektɪŋ/ | v. |内省的；沉思的 | Examining one's own thoughts and feelings.  |例句：She spent hours introspecting, trying to understand her recent behavior. 她花了几个小时进行内省，试图理解她最近的行为。  |近义词：reflecting, contemplating, meditating |反义词：ignoring, disregarding |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is introspecting about his life choices. = 他正在反省自己的人生选择。
She introspects regularly to maintain mental well-being. = 她定期进行内省以保持身心健康。


一般过去时:
I introspected deeply after the argument. = 争吵之后我进行了深刻的反省。
They introspected on their roles in the project's failure. = 他们反省自己在项目失败中扮演的角色。


现在完成时:
He has been introspecting since the breakup. = 从分手以来，他一直在进行自我反省。
She has introspected a lot lately and feels much clearer about her goals. = 她最近做了很多反省，对自己的目标感觉清晰多了。


过去完成时:
I had introspected for weeks before making the decision. = 在做决定之前，我已经反省了好几个星期了。
They had introspected extensively before presenting their findings. = 在陈述他们的发现之前，他们已经进行了广泛的反省。


将来时:
I will introspect more carefully next time. = 下次我会更仔细地进行反省。
She will introspect on her performance after the presentation. = 演示结束后，她会反省自己的表现。


虚拟语气:
If I had introspected earlier, I might have avoided that mistake. = 如果我早点反省，也许就能避免那个错误了。
I wish I had introspected more before making that commitment. = 我希望在做出那个承诺之前，我能够多反省一些。


被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
His behavior was introspected upon by the therapist. = 他的行为受到了治疗师的深入分析（虽然grammatically correct，但在实际运用中很少这样表达）。
The situation was introspected upon by the committee. = 委员会对这种情况进行了反省（同理，实际使用中很少见）。


疑问句:
Are you introspecting about your future? = 你在反省你的未来吗？
Was she introspecting during the meditation session? = 她在冥想时在进行内省吗？


否定句:
He didn't introspect enough before acting. = 他在行动之前没有进行足够的反省。
She doesn't often introspect on her emotions. = 她不经常反省自己的情绪。


PHRASE->
deep introspection = 深度反省
quiet introspection = 安静的沉思
self-introspection = 自我反省
introspection and reflection = 内省与反思
productive introspection = 有成效的内省
