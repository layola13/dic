self-indulgers:/selfɪnˈdʌldʒərz/| n.|放纵者，沉溺者|People who allow themselves to enjoy pleasures and luxuries without restraint. They prioritize their own desires and gratification above other considerations.  |Example:  He was surrounded by self-indulgers who cared little for the consequences of their actions. 他被一群不在乎自己行为后果的放纵者包围着。|近义词：hedonists, pleasure-seekers, sybarites|反义词：ascetics, disciplinarians, self-deniers|名词
