demos:/ˈdɛmoʊz/| n.|示范，演示，样品|A demo is a demonstration or sample of a product or service.  It is often used to show potential customers how something works or what it can do.|例句：The company gave a demo of their new software.  这家公司演示了他们的新软件。|例句：They showed us a demo of the car's features. 他们向我们演示了汽车的功能。|近义词：demonstration, presentation, sample, trial|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company demos its new product every week. = 这家公司每周都会演示它的新产品。
We demo new software to potential clients. = 我们向潜在客户演示新软件。


一般过去时:
They demoed the software to the investors last week. = 他们上周向投资者演示了该软件。
The team demoed a new feature at the conference. = 团队在会议上演示了一个新功能。


现在完成时:
We have demoed this product at several trade shows. = 我们已经在几个贸易展会上演示了这款产品。
The developers have demoed the updated version. = 开发人员已经演示了更新版本。


过去完成时:
They had demoed the prototype before the official launch. = 他们在正式发布之前已经演示了原型。
We had already demoed the system to the management team. = 我们已经向管理团队演示了该系统。


将来时:
We will demo the software at the upcoming conference. = 我们将在即将举行的会议上演示该软件。
They will demo their new app next month. = 他们下个月将演示他们的新应用程序。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would demo all the features. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会演示所有功能。
It would be helpful if they demoed the product in person. = 如果他们能亲自演示产品，那就太好了。


被动语态:
The new software was demoed at the trade show. = 新软件在贸易展会上进行了演示。
The product will be demoed to potential buyers. = 该产品将向潜在买家进行演示。


疑问句:
Will the product be demoed at the meeting? = 产品会在会议上演示吗？
Have they demoed the new features yet? = 他们已经演示新功能了吗？


否定句:
They didn't demo the software properly. = 他们没有正确地演示软件。
We won't demo the product until next week. = 我们要到下周才会演示产品。


PHRASE->
demo reel: 演示卷轴 (通常指视频)
product demo: 产品演示
software demo: 软件演示
live demo: 现场演示
interactive demo: 交互式演示
