neurophysiologists:/ˌnʊroʊˌfɪziˈɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |神经生理学家| Specialists who study the functions and workings of the nervous system.  They investigate the relationship between the nervous system and behavior, using a combination of anatomical, physiological, and behavioral techniques. | Neurophysiologists are investigating the effects of stress on the brain. 神经生理学家正在研究压力对大脑的影响。| neuroscientists, neurobiologists |  (no clear antonym)| 名词
