hypodermis:/ˈhaɪpədɜːrmɪs/| n. |皮下组织|The hypodermis is the deepest layer of skin, composed primarily of fat and connective tissue. It provides insulation, cushioning, and energy storage.  |Example: The hypodermis plays a crucial role in regulating body temperature. 皮下组织在调节体温方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：subcutis, subcutaneous tissue |反义词：epidermis, dermis
