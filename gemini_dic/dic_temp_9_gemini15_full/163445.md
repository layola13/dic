precontemplation:/ˌpriːkənˈtɛmpleɪʃən/| n. |预备阶段，未考虑阶段 |The stage of change in which people are not intending to take action in the foreseeable future.  It is the first stage in the transtheoretical model of behavior change. |例句：Many smokers are in the precontemplation stage and haven't even considered quitting.  许多吸烟者处于预备阶段，甚至都没有考虑过戒烟。

例句：Before seeking professional help, many individuals experiencing depression linger in the precontemplation stage for extended periods. 在寻求专业帮助之前，许多经历抑郁症的个人会在预备阶段停留很长时间。

近义词：unawareness, denial, inaction |反义词：action, contemplation, preparation


词性:名词
