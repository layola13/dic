manurement: /məˈnʊərmənt/ | n. |肥料，粪肥|The application of manure or other organic matter to land as fertilizer.|例句：The farmer diligently applied manurement to his fields to improve crop yields.  这位农民勤奋地向他的田地施肥以提高作物产量。|近义词：fertilizer, dung, compost |反义词：None specifically, but "lack of fertilization" could be considered an antonym.
