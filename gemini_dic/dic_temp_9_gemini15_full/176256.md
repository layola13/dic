re-compensate:/ˌriːˈkɒmpənseɪt/| vt. |补偿，赔偿；抵消|To compensate again or further; to make amends for a loss or injury a second time.  The company re-compensated the victims for their additional losses after the initial payment proved insufficient.|近义词：reimburse, indemnify, repay|反义词：deprive, cheat


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company re-compensates its employees for travel expenses. = 公司报销员工的差旅费。
They re-compensate for lost time by working overtime. = 他们通过加班来弥补失去的时间。


一般过去时:
The insurance company re-compensated the homeowner for the damage caused by the fire. = 保险公司赔偿房主因火灾造成的损失。
He re-compensated her for the broken vase with a more expensive one. = 他用一个更贵的花瓶来赔偿她打碎的花瓶。


现在完成时:
The government has re-compensated the farmers for the crop failure. = 政府已经赔偿农民的歉收损失。
They have re-compensated me for the inconvenience caused. = 他们已经赔偿我造成的诸多不便。


过去完成时:
The court had re-compensated the plaintiff before the appeal was heard. = 法院在听取上诉之前已经赔偿了原告。
She had re-compensated him for the lost opportunity with a better offer. = 她已经用更好的提议赔偿了他失去的机会。


将来时:
The company will re-compensate the employees for the delay in their salaries. = 公司将赔偿员工工资延误。
They will re-compensate the customers for the faulty products. = 他们将赔偿顾客有缺陷的产品。


虚拟语气:
If the company had acted sooner, they would have re-compensated the customers more effectively. = 如果公司早点采取行动，他们就能更有效地赔偿客户。
I wish they would re-compensate me for the emotional distress. = 我希望他们能赔偿我的精神损失。


被动语态:
The employees were re-compensated for their extra work. = 员工因额外工作而获得赔偿。
The damages were re-compensated by the responsible party. = 损害由责任方赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the insurance company re-compensate me for the stolen goods? = 保险公司会赔偿我被盗的货物吗？
Has the company re-compensated all the affected customers? = 公司是否已经赔偿所有受影响的客户？


否定句:
The company did not re-compensate the employees for their lost wages. = 公司没有赔偿员工损失的工资。
They refused to re-compensate for the damage they had caused. = 他们拒绝赔偿他们造成的损失。



PHRASE->
re-compensate fully = 全额赔偿
re-compensate adequately = 充分赔偿
re-compensate fairly = 公平赔偿
re-compensate promptly = 及时赔偿
re-compensate for damages = 赔偿损失
