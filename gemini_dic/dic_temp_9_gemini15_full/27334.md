camarasaurus:/ˌkæməˈsɔːrəs/| n. |卡马拉龙|A genus of large, quadrupedal, herbivorous sauropod dinosaur that lived in the Late Jurassic period.|Camarasaurus was a massive herbivore with a long neck and tail, and a relatively small head.  It is known for its distinctive, spoon-shaped teeth.|The Camarasaurus fossils have been found in North America.|近义词：brachiosaurus, apatosaurus|反义词：无
