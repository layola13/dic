shake:/ʃeɪk/ | vt.&vi., n. | 摇动；颤抖；震动；使震惊 | To move or cause to move rapidly to and fro or up and down.  |例句：He shook his head in disbelief. 他难以置信地摇了摇头。

例句：The earthquake shook the city violently. 地震剧烈地摇动了这座城市。


近义词：tremble, quiver, vibrate, jiggle | 反义词：still, steady, calm | 词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shakes his head. 他摇头。
She shakes the bottle vigorously. 她用力摇晃瓶子。


一般过去时:
I shook the tree and some apples fell down. 我摇了摇树，一些苹果掉了下来。
The ground shook during the earthquake. 地震时地面在晃动。


现在完成时:
The building has shaken violently during several earthquakes. 这座建筑物在几次地震中剧烈震动过。
He has shaken hands with the president. 他和总统握过手。


过去完成时:
The house had shaken before the roof collapsed. 屋顶倒塌前房子已经摇晃了。
She had shaken the bottle before drinking it. 她喝之前已经摇晃过瓶子了。


将来时:
I will shake your hand when I see you. 我见到你时会和你握手。
The building will shake if the earthquake is strong enough. 如果地震足够强烈，建筑物将会摇晃。


虚拟语气:
If I shook the tree harder, more apples would fall. 如果我更用力地摇树，会有更多的苹果掉下来。
I wish I hadn't shaken the bottle so hard; now the liquid is everywhere. 我真希望我没有那么用力地摇瓶子；现在液体洒得到处都是。


被动语态:
The tree was shaken by the wind. 树被风吹得摇晃。
The bottle was shaken before being opened. 瓶子在打开之前被摇晃过。


疑问句:
Do you shake hands when you meet someone? 你见面时握手吗？
Is the building shaking? 建筑物在摇晃吗？


否定句:
He didn't shake my hand. 他没有和我握手。
The earthquake didn't shake the city. 地震没有震动这座城市。


PHRASE->
shake hands 握手

shake down 敲诈
shake up 震动；使震惊
shake a leg 快点


词源:
The word "shake" comes from Old English *scacan, meaning "to shake, tremble," related to Old High German *scahan and Old Norse *skaka.  The root is thought to be Proto-Germanic *skaikōn and ultimately Proto-Indo-European *skeik-.  The noun usage developed from the verb.
