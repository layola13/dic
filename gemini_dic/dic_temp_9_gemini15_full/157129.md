pinchfist: /ˈpɪntʃfɪst/ | adj. | 吝啬的；小气的 |

英文解析:  A pinchfist is a person who is unwilling to spend money; stingy or miserly.  The word combines "pinch," suggesting a tight grip on resources, and "fist," symbolizing a closed hand unwilling to give.

例句:

He's such a pinchfist; he wouldn't even buy a round of drinks.  = 他太小气了，甚至都不愿意请一轮酒。

The pinchfist landlord refused to repair the broken window. =  那个吝啬的房东拒绝修理破窗户。


近义词: miserly, stingy, frugal (although frugal can have a positive connotation), parsimonious, tightfisted

反义词: generous, openhanded, munificent, lavish


词性: adjective (形容词)
