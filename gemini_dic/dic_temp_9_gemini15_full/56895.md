dossier:/ˈdɒsieɪ/ /ˈdoʊsieɪ/| n. |档案；卷宗|A dossier is a collection of documents containing information about a person, event, or subject.  It is often used in official contexts, such as government or law enforcement. |The police compiled a detailed dossier on the suspect.  警方收集了关于嫌疑人的详细档案。| file, record, report |  |
