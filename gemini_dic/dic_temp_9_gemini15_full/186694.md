righteous:/ˈraɪtʃəs/| adj.| 正义的，正直的；虔诚的 |  morally right or justifiable; virtuous.  Characterized by or possessing righteousness; morally upright. |例句：He felt a righteous anger at the injustice. 他对这种不公正感到义愤填膺。

例句：She considered herself a righteous woman, always striving to do what was right. 她认为自己是一个正直的女人，总是努力去做正确的事情。


近义词：upright, virtuous, just, moral, holy, godly | 反义词：wicked, sinful, unjust, immoral, ungodly


词源：源自古英语 *rīhtwīs,  由rīht（正确，正直）和wīs（方式，方法）组成。
