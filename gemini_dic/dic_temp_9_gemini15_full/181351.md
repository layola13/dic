reinventing:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛntɪŋ/ | v. |  重新发明；彻底改造；改头换面 | The act of creating something new from something old or existing, often with significant changes and improvements.  It implies a radical transformation rather than a simple modification. | 例句：The company is reinventing itself as a technology firm. = 这家公司正在将自己改造成一家科技公司。

例句：They are reinventing the wheel by developing a system that already exists. = 他们正在重新发明轮子，开发一个已经存在的系统。(负面例子，表示重复造轮子)


近义词：reimagine, redesign, transform, revolutionize, innovate

反义词：maintain, preserve, conserve


词性: 动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reinventing their marketing strategy. = 他们正在重新设计他们的营销策略。
The company is constantly reinventing its products. = 这家公司不断地改进它的产品。


一般过去时:
She reinvented herself as a successful entrepreneur. = 她把自己改造成一个成功的企业家。
The artist reinvented the traditional landscape painting. =这位艺术家彻底改变了传统的风景画。


现在完成时:
They have reinvented the classic novel into a modern play. = 他们已将这部经典小说改编成现代戏剧。
The chef has reinvented many traditional dishes. =这位厨师已经重新演绎了许多传统菜肴。


过去完成时:
He had reinvented the company's image before the merger. = 在合并之前，他已经重塑了公司的形象。
They had reinvented their business model after several years of losses. = 经过几年的亏损后，他们已经重塑了他们的商业模式。


将来时:
They will reinvent their approach to customer service. = 他们将重新设计他们的客户服务方法。
She will reinvent the way we think about education. = 她将彻底改变我们对教育的思考方式。


虚拟语气:
If they reinvented their product line, they might increase sales. = 如果他们改进产品线，他们可能会增加销售额。
I wish they had reinvented their marketing campaign sooner. = 我希望他们早点重新设计他们的营销活动。


被动语态:
The old building is being reinvented as a modern art gallery. = 这座旧建筑正在被改造成一座现代美术馆。
The company's image was reinvented after a major scandal. = 在一场重大丑闻之后，公司的形象得到了重塑。


疑问句:
Are they reinventing their business model? = 他们正在重新设计他们的商业模式吗？
How are they reinventing the traditional methods? = 他们如何改进传统方法？


否定句:
They are not reinventing the wheel; they are simply improving existing technology. = 他们不是在重新发明轮子；他们只是在改进现有技术。
The company did not reinvent itself successfully. = 这家公司没有成功地重塑自身。


PHRASE->
reinvent oneself = 重塑自我
reinvent the wheel = 重复发明轮子（徒劳无功）
reinvent the future = 创造未来
reinvent the industry = 彻底改变行业
reinvent the experience = 重新设计体验
