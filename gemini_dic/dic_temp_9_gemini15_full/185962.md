rewarder:/rɪˈwɔːrdər/| n. |奖赏者，报答者 |One who gives rewards.  A rewarder is someone who bestows rewards or recompense for good deeds, services, or achievements. They are often associated with positive reinforcement and encouragement. |例句：The company is a great rewarder of employee loyalty. 这家公司非常重视员工的忠诚度，并给予奖励。

例句：He was a generous rewarder of talent, always seeking out and promoting skilled individuals. 他是一位慷慨的赏识人才的人，总是寻找并提拔有才能的人。|近义词：benefactor, patron, compensator |反义词：punisher, malefactor
