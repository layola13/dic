intitule:/ˈɪntaɪtl/| v.|给…命名；给…题名；授权|To give a title to (something); to entitle.|The book was intituled "The Adventures of Tom Sawyer". 这本书名为《汤姆·索亚历险记》。|近义词：name, entitle, title|反义词：deprive
