goodship:/ˈɡʊdʃɪp/ | n. | 好船；友谊；（尤指船舶间的）合作关系 | A goodship is a term used to refer to a vessel, especially in a figurative or affectionate sense, highlighting its seaworthiness, reliability, or the positive relationship between its crew or with another vessel.  It can also metaphorically refer to a strong friendship or partnership. | Example: The *goodship* Enterprise sailed through the storm unscathed. 这艘好船企业号安然度过了风暴。  The *goodship* friendship between them lasted for decades.  他们之间的友谊持续了几十年。 | 近义词：vessel, ship, friendship, partnership | 反义词：unworthy vessel, broken friendship
