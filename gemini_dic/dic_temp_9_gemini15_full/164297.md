preedit:/ˌpriːˈɛdɪt/| v.|预编辑，预先编辑 | To edit something before it is officially published or released.  It often involves making changes to improve clarity, accuracy, or style.  |例句：The editor asked the author to preedit the manuscript before submitting it. 编辑要求作者在提交稿件之前进行预编辑。


例句：The team preedited the video footage to remove any unwanted scenes. 团队预先编辑了视频素材，以去除任何不需要的场景。


近义词：edit, revise, prepare


反义词：post-edit, finalize


词性:动词
