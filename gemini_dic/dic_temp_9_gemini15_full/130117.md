non-racial: /ˌnɒnˈreɪʃəl/ | adj. | 非种族的；不涉及种族歧视的 | Not relating to or based on race; free from racial prejudice or discrimination.  | 例句：The company adopted a non-racial hiring policy.  公司采取了不歧视种族的招聘政策。


例句：The school aims to create a non-racial and inclusive environment for all students. 学校的目标是为所有学生创造一个非种族主义和包容的环境。


近义词：race-neutral, colorblind, impartial, unbiased


反义词：racial, racist, discriminatory, prejudiced


词性：形容词
