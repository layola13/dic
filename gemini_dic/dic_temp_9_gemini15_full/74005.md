fortuned:/ˈfɔːrtʃʊnd/| adj. |幸运的，富有的|Having good fortune; wealthy.|例句：The fortuned family donated generously to the charity.  = 富裕的家庭慷慨地捐赠给了慈善机构。|近义词：lucky, wealthy, affluent |反义词：unlucky, impoverished, destitute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is fortuned. = 他很幸运。
They are fortuned enough to travel the world. = 他们足够幸运可以环游世界。

一般过去时:
She was fortuned to find a good job. = 她很幸运找到了一个好工作。
We were fortuned to escape the accident unharmed. = 我们很幸运毫发无伤地逃过了事故。

现在完成时:
He has been fortuned in his business ventures. = 他在商业冒险中一直很幸运。
They have been fortuned to have such supportive parents. = 他们很幸运拥有如此支持他们的父母。

过去完成时:
She had been fortuned to have received a good education. = 她很幸运接受过良好的教育。
They had been fortuned to witness a historical event. = 他们很幸运见证了一次历史性事件。

将来时:
He will be fortuned to win the lottery. = 他将很幸运赢得彩票。
We will be fortuned to see our family soon. = 我们很快就能见到家人，真是幸运。

虚拟语气:
If I were fortuned, I would travel the world. = 如果我幸运的话，我会环游世界。
I wish I were fortuned enough to buy that house. = 我希望我足够幸运能买下那栋房子。

被动语态:
He was fortuned by his inheritance. = 他因为继承而变得富有。
The project was fortuned by the government's support. = 该项目由于政府的支持而幸运地成功了。

疑问句:
Are you fortuned enough to afford a new car? = 你足够幸运能买得起一辆新车吗？
Is he fortuned in his career? = 他在事业上很幸运吗？

否定句:
He is not fortuned enough to own a house. = 他不够幸运，没有自己的房子。
They were not fortuned to win the competition. = 他们没有幸运地赢得比赛。


PHRASE->
fortuned enough to... = 足够幸运能够...
