hobo:/ˈhoʊboʊ/ | n. |流浪汉，漂泊者 | A hobo is a transient, usually unemployed, person who travels from place to place, often by hopping freight trains.  They are typically characterized by their itinerant lifestyle and often lack permanent housing. | Example: During the Great Depression, many people became hobos, seeking work and survival across the country.  在经济大萧条时期，许多人变成了流浪汉，在全国各地寻找工作和生存。|近义词：tramp, vagrant, bum, drifter |反义词：homeowner, resident
