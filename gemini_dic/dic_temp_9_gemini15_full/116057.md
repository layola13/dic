martian:/ˈmɑːrʃən/| adj. n.|火星的；火星人|Relating to the planet Mars or its inhabitants.  A Martian is a hypothetical inhabitant of the planet Mars.|例句：The Martian landscape is barren and desolate. 火星地貌荒凉凄惨。
The movie "The Martian" depicts an astronaut stranded on Mars. 电影《火星救援》描述了一名宇航员被困在火星上的故事。|近义词：Martian (n.) - alien (if referring to inhabitant)|反义词：Earthling, terrestrial|形容词，名词
