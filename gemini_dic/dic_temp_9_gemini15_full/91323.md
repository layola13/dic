hydrophanous:/haɪˈdrɒfənəs/| adj.|吸水的，水化的|Becoming transparent or translucent when immersed in water;  capable of absorbing water and becoming more transparent.  This term is primarily used to describe certain gemstones, particularly opal.|例句：The hydrophanous opal absorbed water and became noticeably more translucent. 这块吸水蛋白石吸收了水分，变得明显更半透明。|近义词：water-absorbing, translucent when wet|反义词：opaque, non-absorbent
