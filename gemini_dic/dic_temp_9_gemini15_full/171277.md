pterygium:/ˈtɛrɪdʒiəm/| n. |翼状胬肉|A pterygium is a noncancerous growth of tissue that develops on the conjunctiva, the clear membrane covering the white part of the eye. It usually starts on the nasal (nose) side of the eye and may spread across the cornea, the transparent front part of the eye.  | Example:  The ophthalmologist examined the patient's eye and diagnosed a pterygium. 眼科医生检查了病人的眼睛，诊断为翼状胬肉。|近义词：conjunctival growth, fleshy growth |反义词：None
