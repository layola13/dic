putts:/pʌts/| n. v.|轻推；轻击（高尔夫球）；（球）轻滚|The act of hitting a golf ball gently, usually aiming for a short distance.  It often refers to the final stroke on the green to get the ball into the hole. |例句：He sank the putt on the 18th hole to win the match. 他在第18洞推进了球，赢得了比赛。

He carefully studied the green before taking his putt. 他仔细研究了果岭之后才推球。

近义词：tap, nudge, roll|反义词：slam, bash|动词


USAGE->

She usually putts well under pressure. = 她通常在压力下推球推得很好。
