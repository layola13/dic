faddily:/ˈfædɪli/| adv.|  古怪地；反复无常地；任性地 | In a faddish or capricious manner; whimsically.  It describes actions or behavior characterized by whimsy, inconsistency, or a tendency to follow fleeting trends. |例句：She faddily changed her mind about the color scheme for the living room, going from pastels to bold primary colors in a single afternoon. 她反复无常地改变了客厅的配色方案，一下是柔和的色彩，一下又是大胆的原色。|近义词：capriciously, whimsically, erratically, ficklely |反义词：steadfastly, consistently, reliably |副词
