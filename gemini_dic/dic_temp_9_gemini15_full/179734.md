reequipments:/ˌriːˈɪkwɪpmənts/ | n. | 再装备；重新装备 | The reequipments of the army with modern weapons took several years.  军队用现代化武器重新装备花费了几年时间。 | 近义词：rearmament, refitting | 反义词：disarmament

一般现在时:
The company reequips its factories regularly. = 公司定期重新装备工厂。
This program reequips underprivileged students with laptops. = 该项目为贫困学生重新配备笔记本电脑。


一般过去时:
The army reequipped its soldiers with new uniforms. = 军队为士兵重新配备了新制服。
They reequipped the laboratory with state-of-the-art equipment. = 他们用最先进的设备重新装备了实验室。


现在完成时:
The factory has reequipped its production line with new machinery. = 工厂已经用新机器重新装备了生产线。

过去完成时:

The company had reequipped its offices before the merger. = 公司在合并之前已经重新装备了办公室。


将来时:

The military will reequip its forces with advanced technology. = 军方将用先进技术重新装备部队。
