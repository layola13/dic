词源:
From "dissertation" (a long essay or treatise, especially one written as a requirement for a degree),  + "-al" (forming adjectives).  The word "dissertation" itself comes from the Latin "dissertare" (to discuss or debate), which is related to "disserere" (to discuss, explain).
