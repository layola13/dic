/ˈkɒnfərənsɪz/ | n. |会议；讨论会 | Conferences are meetings where people gather to discuss specific topics.  Conferences often involve presentations, workshops, and networking opportunities. | 例句：The annual technology conference attracted thousands of attendees.  年度科技大会吸引了数千名与会者。| 例句：We need to schedule a conference call to discuss the project. 我们需要安排一次电话会议来讨论这个项目。|近义词：meeting, convention, seminar, symposium |反义词：None
