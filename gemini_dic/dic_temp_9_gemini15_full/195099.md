semicolon:/ˈsɛmɪˌkoʊlən/| n. |分号|A punctuation mark (;) used to connect two closely related independent clauses or to separate items in a list when the items themselves contain commas.|例句：He went to the store; he bought milk and bread. 他去了商店；他买了牛奶和面包。|近义词：comma (in some contexts)|反义词：period, full stop
