cautery:/ˈkɔːtəri/| n. |灼烧；烧灼法；烙印 |The application of heat to tissue to destroy it or to stop bleeding.  It is a medical procedure used to destroy abnormal tissue or to seal blood vessels.| 例句：The surgeon used cautery to stop the bleeding. = 外科医生用烧灼法止血。  The cautery mark on his skin was a permanent reminder of the accident. = 他皮肤上的烧灼印记是事故的永久性提醒。|近义词：burning, searing, scarification |反义词：healing, repair
