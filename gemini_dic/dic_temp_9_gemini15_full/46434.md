daggy:/ˈdæɡi/| adj.|邋遢的，不时尚的，过时的|Unfashionable, dowdy, or unkempt;  lacking style or sophistication.  The term often implies a slightly endearing or humorous lack of style, rather than outright criticism. |例句：His clothes were a bit daggy, but he was a nice guy.  他的衣服有点邋遢，但他是个好人。

例句：That hairstyle is so daggy!  那个发型太土了！


近义词：shabby, dowdy, unfashionable, unstylish,  sloppy, scruffy


反义词：stylish, fashionable, chic, smart, sophisticated


词性：形容词
