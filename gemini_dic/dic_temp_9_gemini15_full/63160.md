ensurers:/ɪnˈʃʊərərz/| n. | 保险人；保证人 | People or organizations that provide insurance.  They assume the financial risk of insured events. |Example: The insurers paid out millions of dollars after the hurricane.  保险公司在飓风过后支付了数百万美元。  The insurers are reviewing the claim carefully. 保险公司正在仔细审查索赔。|近义词：underwriters, guarantors |反义词：insured (反义词并非完全对等，而是指保险的受益方)
