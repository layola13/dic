mentha:/ˈmɛnθə/ | n. |薄荷属植物；薄荷 |The genus *Mentha* includes various aromatic herbs commonly known as mints.  They are characterized by their square stems, opposite leaves, and strong, often refreshing scent.  Many species are cultivated for their culinary and medicinal uses. |Example: Peppermint and spearmint are both species of *Mentha*. 薄荷和绿薄荷都是薄荷属植物。|近义词:mint (as a general term)|反义词:None|名词
