modest:/ˈmɑːdɪst/ /ˈmɒdɪst/ | adj. | 谦虚的；适度的；不大的 |

英文解析：
1.  **谦虚的(Humble):**  Not boastful or arrogant; having or showing a moderate or humble estimate of one's own importance. This is the most common meaning.
2.  **适度的(Moderate):**  Of moderate size, amount, or extent; not excessive or extravagant.  This refers to things like size, price, or ambition.
3.  **不大的(Small):**  Relatively small in size, amount, or extent. This usage often implies unpretentiousness.


例句：
He is a modest man despite his great achievements. = 尽管他取得了巨大的成就，他仍然是一个谦虚的人。

She wore a modest dress to the wedding. = 她穿着一件朴素的裙子去参加婚礼。

They live in a modest house in the suburbs. = 他们在郊区住在一间不太大的房子里。


近义词：humble, unassuming, unpretentious, moderate, restrained


反义词：boastful, arrogant, extravagant, immoderate, ostentatious


词性：形容词
