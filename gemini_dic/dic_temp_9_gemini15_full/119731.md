midtones:/ˈmɪdˌtoʊnz/ | n. | 中音；中音色；中等色调 |  Midtones refer to the range of colors, tones, or shades that fall between the lightest and darkest points in a spectrum or scale.  In art, music, and photography, midtones represent the intermediate values.  | Example: The painting's beauty lies in its masterful use of midtones, creating a sense of depth and harmony.  这幅画的美在于其巧妙地运用中音色，营造出一种深度和和谐感。 |近义词：intermediate tones, medium tones, average tones | 反义词：highlights, shadows, extremes
