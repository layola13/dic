kegs:/keɡz/| n.|桶；酒桶|A keg is a small barrel, especially one for beer or other liquids.|例句：The brewery delivered five kegs of their new IPA.  啤酒厂送来了五桶他们新的印度淡啤酒。

例句：We need to get more kegs for the party. 我们需要为派对准备更多酒桶。


近义词：barrel, cask|反义词：bottle (smaller container)


词源:  The word "keg" comes from the Middle Dutch word "kage," meaning "cask" or "barrel."  This term eventually evolved into the Modern English "keg," retaining its association with small barrels, particularly for liquids.
