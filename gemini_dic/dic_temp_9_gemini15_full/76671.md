galvanizer:/ˈɡæl vəˌnaɪzər/ | n. |镀锌工；镀锌设备| A person or machine that galvanizes, especially one that applies a zinc coating to metal to protect it from rust.  |例句：The galvanizer carefully coated the steel pipes with zinc. 镀锌工小心翼翼地用锌涂层覆盖钢管。  |例句：The automatic galvanizer increased the factory's production efficiency. 自动镀锌设备提高了工厂的生产效率。|近义词：zinc coater|反义词：无
