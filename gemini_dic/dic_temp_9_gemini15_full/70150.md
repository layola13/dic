fiaschi:/fɪˈɑːʃi/ | n. |意大利小酒瓶；(复数)| Italian small wine bottles; (plural) |  Fiaschi are small, typically flask-shaped bottles used traditionally in Italy for wine. They are often made of glass or ceramic. | Example:  He brought a basket filled with fiaschi of Tuscan red wine. 他带来一篮子托斯卡纳红葡萄酒的小酒瓶。 | 近义词：flask, bottle, carafe | 反义词： (无直接反义词，可考虑与大容量酒瓶对比) large wine bottle
