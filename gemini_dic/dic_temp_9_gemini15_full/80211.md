godness:/ˈɡɒdnəs/ /ˈɡɑːdnəs/| n.|女神；神性；卓越；极好|The word "godness" refers to the female equivalent of a god, embodying divine qualities. It can also describe something exceptionally good or excellent.|例句：Athena is the goddess of wisdom. 雅典娜是智慧女神。Her beauty was a goddess-like. 她的美貌宛如女神。|近义词：divinity, deity, excellence|反义词：evil, wickedness
