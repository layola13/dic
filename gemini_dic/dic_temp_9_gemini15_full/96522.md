indoctrinator:/ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪtər/ | n. |灌输者，教条主义者 | A person who indoctrinates others; someone who teaches a set of beliefs or principles in a forceful or persuasive way, often without allowing questions or critical thinking. |例句：The cult leader was a powerful indoctrinator, shaping the beliefs of his followers.  那个邪教领袖是一个强大的灌输者，塑造了他追随者的信仰。|近义词：propagandist, proselytizer, brainwasher |反义词：educator (in a sense of fostering critical thinking)
