resighted:/riˈsaɪtɪd/| v. |重新定位；重新瞄准；再次看见|To locate or see something again after it has been lost or obscured.  This word is primarily used in contexts related to surveying, aiming, or visual perception. |例句：After the storm, the surveyors resighted the boundary markers.  暴风雨过后，测量员们重新定位了界标。

例句：The hunter resighted the deer after it ducked behind a tree. 猎人在鹿躲到树后后再次看到了它。

近义词：relocate, realign, rediscover, glimpse again

反义词：lose sight of, mislocate

词性：动词
