feeblest: /ˈfiːblist/ | adj. | 最弱的；最虚弱的 |  The weakest; lacking strength, power, or vigor.  |例句：He made the feeblest of attempts to defend himself. 他做出了最微弱的自我辩护尝试。

例句：The feeblest light flickered in the distance.  远处闪烁着微弱的光芒。


近义词：weakest, faintest, most feeble, puny, frail, delicate

反义词：strongest, powerful, robust, vigorous


词源:  From Old English *fēbla, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch fiebel ‘weakling’, Old High German fiebila ‘weak’.  The word's development reflects a shift from a noun referring to a weakling to an adjective describing weakness.


用法说明:

feeblest 是 feeble 的最高级形式，表示“最弱的，最虚弱的”。它可以用来形容人、动物、物体或抽象概念，强调其力量、能力或活力的缺乏。  它通常用于强调程度，表示相比其他事物，某事物是最弱的。


例句补充:


He offered the feeblest of excuses. 他提出了最苍白的借口。


Her feeblest whisper could barely be heard. 她最轻微的耳语几乎听不见。


The feeblest link in the chain broke. 链条中最薄弱的一环断了。


The plant showed only the feeblest signs of life.  这株植物只显现出最微弱的生命迹象。
