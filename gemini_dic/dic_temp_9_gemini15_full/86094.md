heart-searcher: /ˈhɑːrtˌsɜːrtʃər/ | n. | 衷心寻求者，苦苦寻找的人 | A person who earnestly seeks something, especially a romantic partner or a spiritual truth.  This word emphasizes the deep emotional involvement and intense effort in the search. |例句：After years of searching, she finally found her heart-searcher.  多年寻找后，她终于找到了她的真命天子/她苦苦寻找的对象。|近义词：seeker, searcher, soul mate (if romantic), pilgrim (if spiritual) |反义词：indifferent person, apathetic person
