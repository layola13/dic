prepromote:/ˌpriːprəˈmoʊt/| v.|预先提升；提前晋升|To promote someone to a higher position before the usual time.  This often involves bypassing some levels of seniority.|例句：They prepromoted her to manager after only two years in the company.  他们只在她公司工作两年后就提前把她提升为经理。

例句：The company decided to prepromote him due to his exceptional performance. 公司决定由于他的杰出表现而提前晋升他。


近义词：promote, advance, elevate|反义词：demote, downgrade
