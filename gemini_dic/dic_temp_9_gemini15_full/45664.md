curtain-calls:/ˈkɜːrtən kɔːlz/| n.|谢幕;演员谢幕|The plural form of "curtain call," referring to the appearances of actors on stage after a performance to acknowledge applause from the audience.  It can also refer to the applause itself.|例句：The actors received several enthusiastic curtain-calls after their brilliant performance.  演员们在精彩的演出后获得了多次热烈的谢幕。 The play ended with a series of curtain-calls, each met with thunderous applause.  这出戏以一系列的谢幕结束，每一次都伴随着雷鸣般的掌声。|近义词：encores, bows|反义词:None directly applicable.
