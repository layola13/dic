debauchee:/ˈdɛbəʃiː/| n. |酒色之徒，放荡者| A person who is excessively addicted to pleasure and sensual indulgence, especially drinking and sexual activity.  They are characterized by unrestrained and immoral behavior.|The debauchee spent his inheritance on wine, women, and song.  这位酒色之徒把他的遗产都花在了酒、女人和歌曲上。|He lived the life of a notorious debauchee. 他过着臭名昭著的酒色之徒的生活。|libertine, rake, roué| ascetic, prude, saint
