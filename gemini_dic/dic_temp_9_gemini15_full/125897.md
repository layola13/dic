muscularly: /ˈmʌskjʊləli/ | adv. |肌肉地；强有力地 | In a way that shows strength and power, typically of muscles.  |例句：He moved muscularly, each action precise and powerful. 他肌肉发达地移动着，每一个动作都精确有力。

He lifted the heavy weight muscularly. 他用肌肉的力量举起了重物。


近义词： powerfully, strongly, forcefully | 反义词：weakly, feebly
