holla:/ˈhɒlə/ /ˈhɑːlə/| int. v. n.| 喊叫；大声招呼；大叫 |To shout or call loudly.  It can be used as an exclamation, a verb, or a noun (though less common as a noun).|例句：Holla! I'm over here! 嘿！我在这儿！

例句：He hollaed for help. 他大声呼救。


近义词：shout, yell, scream, call out, bellow | 反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I holla at my friends when I see them. = 我看到朋友就大声喊他们。
They holla across the street to each other. = 他们在街上互相大声喊话。

一般过去时:
She hollaed my name from across the room. = 她从房间的另一边大声喊我的名字。
He hollaed at the top of his lungs. = 他用最大的嗓音喊叫。

现在完成时:
I have hollaed at him several times, but he hasn't responded. = 我已经喊了他好几次了，但他没有回应。
They have hollaed for help, but no one came. = 他们已经大声呼救了，但没有人来。

过去完成时:
I had hollaed before he even turned around. = 我在他转身之前就已经喊了他。
She had hollaed at the waiter several times before he noticed her. = 在服务员注意到她之前，她已经喊了他好几次了。

将来时:
I will holla at you when I arrive. = 我到了会大声喊你。
They will holla if they see anything suspicious. = 如果他们看到任何可疑的东西，他们就会大声喊叫。

虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would holla. = 如果我看到他，我会大声喊他。
I wish I had hollaed sooner. = 我真希望我早点喊。

被动语态:
His name was hollaed across the field. = 他的名字在田野上传来。
Help was hollaed for, but no one came. = 大家都呼救了，但没有人来。

疑问句:
Did you holla at him? = 你有没有大声喊他？
Will they holla if they are in danger? = 如果他们有危险，他们会大声喊叫吗？

否定句:
I didn't holla at her. = 我没有大声喊她。
They didn't holla for help. = 他们没有大声呼救。
