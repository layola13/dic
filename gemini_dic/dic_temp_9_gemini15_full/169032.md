progesterone:/proˈdʒɛstərəʊn/|/prəˈdʒɛstərɔːn/| n. |孕酮，黄体酮|A steroid hormone released by the corpus luteum in the ovary and the placenta, which acts to prepare the uterus for pregnancy and to maintain pregnancy.  It's also involved in the regulation of the menstrual cycle.|例句：Progesterone levels rise sharply after ovulation.  ovulation之后孕酮水平急剧上升。|例句：Taking progesterone supplements can help alleviate some symptoms of menopause.服用孕酮补充剂可以帮助缓解更年期的一些症状。|近义词：progestin|反义词：estrogen
