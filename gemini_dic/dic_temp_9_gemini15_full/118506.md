mesoamerican:/ˌmɛzoʊəˈmɛrɪkən/| adj.|中美洲的；中美洲文化的|Relating to Mesoamerica, the region of Central America that includes Mexico's southern states, Belize, Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador, and sometimes Nicaragua.  It encompasses the civilizations and cultures of this region, particularly those pre-Columbian societies. |Example:  The exhibit showcased mesoamerican art and artifacts.  这个展览展示了中美洲的艺术和文物。 The ancient mesoamerican calendar system is incredibly complex. 古代中美洲的历法系统极其复杂。|近义词: Central American (less specific)|反义词:  None specifically, but terms referring to other geographical regions could be considered opposites.
