dialyzer:/ˈdaɪəlaɪzər/| n. |透析器|A dialyzer is a medical device used in dialysis to remove waste products and excess fluid from the blood of patients whose kidneys are not functioning properly.  It works by using a semi-permeable membrane to filter the blood. |例句：The patient was connected to a dialyzer for several hours.  病人连接到透析器上数小时。|近义词：artificial kidney, hemodialyzer|反义词：N/A
