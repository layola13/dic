outcountenance:/ˌaʊtˈkaʊntənəns/| vt.|使丢脸；使尴尬；使难堪|To cause someone to feel ashamed, embarrassed, or disconcerted; to disconcert or abash. |例句：His rude behavior outcountenanced his mother.  他的粗鲁行为使他母亲难堪。

例句：The scandal outcountenanced the politician, forcing his resignation.  这起丑闻使这位政客颜面尽失，迫使他辞职。


近义词：disconcert, embarrass, shame, abash, mortify

反义词：embolden, encourage, reassure


词源：out- (away, completely) + countenance (facial expression, composure)  字面意思是“完全失去表情/镇定”，引申为使某人感到尴尬或羞愧。
