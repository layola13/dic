carpet-bombed: /ˈkɑːrpɪt bɒmbd/ | v. |地毯式轰炸|

英文解析:To carpet-bomb an area is to attack it with bombs dropped over a wide area, without aiming at specific targets.  This is done to cause widespread destruction and damage.  The term evokes the image of a carpet being laid down over the area, covering it completely.

例句:

The city was carpet-bombed during the war, leaving little intact. = 战争期间，这座城市遭到地毯式轰炸，几乎没有留下什么完整的建筑。

Allied forces carpet-bombed the enemy positions, hoping to break their lines. = 盟军对敌军阵地进行了地毯式轰炸，希望能突破他们的防线。


近义词:  blanketed (with bombs), saturated (with bombs), indiscriminately bombed

反义词: precision bombing, surgical strike


词性: Verb (past participle)
