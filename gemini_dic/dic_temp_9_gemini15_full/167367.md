prettifying:/ˈprɛtɪfaɪɪŋ/| v. |使漂亮；美化；打扮 |The act of making something or someone more attractive or beautiful.  It often involves adding decorative elements or enhancing existing features. |例句：She spent hours prettifying the garden. 她花了数小时美化花园。


例句：He was prettifying the cake with icing. 他正在用糖霜装饰蛋糕，使它更漂亮。


近义词：beautifying, decorating, embellishing, enhancing


反义词：uglifying, marring, spoiling, defacing


词性:动词
