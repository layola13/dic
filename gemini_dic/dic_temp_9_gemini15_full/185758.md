reviewable: /rɪˈvjuːəbl/ | adj. | 可审查的，可复查的 | Capable of being reviewed or examined.  Something that is reviewable can be looked at again, assessed, or reconsidered. | 例句：The contract is reviewable by both parties.  合同双方都可以复查。

The decision is reviewable by the appeals court.  上诉法院可以复查该决定。


近义词：assessable, examinable, inspectable | 反义词：unreviewable, unassessable

词性：形容词
