fast-tracked:/ˈfæst trækt/| adj. v.|快速推进的；快速处理的|To expedite or accelerate the progress of something, typically a project or a person's career.|The project was fast-tracked to meet the deadline.  这个项目被快速推进以满足最后期限。|近义词：expedited, accelerated, prioritized|反义词：delayed, slowed, postponed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fast-track promising employees. = 他们快速提拔有前途的员工。
We fast-track the application process. = 我们快速处理申请流程。


一般过去时:
The company fast-tracked the development of the new product. = 公司快速推进新产品的开发。
They fast-tracked her promotion after her successful project. = 她成功完成项目后，公司快速提升了她的职位。


现在完成时:
The government has fast-tracked the infrastructure project. = 政府已经快速推进基础设施项目。
They have fast-tracked several talented individuals into management positions. = 他们已经快速提拔了几位有才华的人进入管理职位。


过去完成时:
They had fast-tracked the legislation before the election. = 他们在选举前已经快速推进立法。
The team had fast-tracked the report to meet the client's urgent request. = 团队为了满足客户的紧急要求，已经快速完成了报告。


将来时:
The company will fast-track the hiring process for the new position. = 公司将快速推进新职位的招聘流程。
We will fast-track the design phase to launch the product sooner. = 我们将快速推进设计阶段，以便尽早推出产品。


虚拟语气:
If we fast-tracked the project, we could finish it by the end of the month. = 如果我们快速推进这个项目，我们可以在月底前完成。
I wish they had fast-tracked my application. = 我希望他们能快速处理我的申请。


被动语态:
The proposal was fast-tracked through the committee. = 该提案被委员会快速推进。
He was fast-tracked to senior management. = 他被快速提拔到高级管理层。


疑问句:
Will the new policy be fast-tracked? = 新政策会被快速推进吗？
Were any applications fast-tracked? = 有任何申请被快速处理吗？


否定句:
The application wasn't fast-tracked because of missing documents. = 由于文件缺失，申请没有被快速处理。
They didn't fast-track the project due to budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，他们没有快速推进该项目。
