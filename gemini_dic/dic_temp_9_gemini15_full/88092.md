highs:/haɪz/| n.|最高点；顶峰；兴奋点 |The highs and lows of life.  生活的起伏。|近义词：peaks, summits, crests |反义词：lows, depths


英文解析：The plural form of "high," referring to the highest points or levels of something, often used metaphorically to describe peak experiences or emotions.  It can refer to literal heights (like mountain highs) or figurative highs (like emotional highs).


例句：

The highs of her career were marked by numerous awards.  她职业生涯的巅峰时期获得了无数奖项。

We experienced the emotional highs and lows of the rollercoaster ride. 我们体验了过山车带来的情绪上的高潮和低谷。



词源：From Old English *hēah, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch hoog and German hoch.  The plural form "-s" is a regular English pluralization.
