darken:/ˈdɑːkən/| /ˈdɑːrkən/| vt. vi.|使变暗；变暗；使忧郁；使变黑|To make or become dark or darker.|The clouds darkened the sky.  乌云遮暗了天空。|The room darkened as the sun set.  日落时房间暗了下来。|近义词：obscure, dim, shade|反义词：brighten, illuminate, lighten|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm darkens the sky. = 暴风雨使天空变暗。
He darkens the room by closing the curtains. = 他拉上窗帘使房间变暗。

一般过去时:
The sky darkened before the rain started. = 下雨前天空变暗了。
She darkened the hallway by turning off the lights. = 她关掉灯，使走廊变暗。

现在完成时:
The clouds have darkened considerably. = 云层已经变得相当阴暗。
The news has darkened his mood.  这个消息使他的心情沉重了。

过去完成时:
The room had darkened before he realized it. = 他意识到之前房间已经变暗了。
The sky had darkened significantly by the time we left. = 我们离开时天空已经明显变暗了。

将来时:
The sky will darken as the sun sets. = 太阳落山时天空将会变暗。
The room will darken when the power goes out. = 停电时房间将会变暗。

虚拟语气:
If the lights failed, the room would darken quickly. = 如果灯坏了，房间会很快变暗。
I wish the news wouldn't darken my day. = 我希望这个消息不会让我一天都心情不好。

被动语态:
The room was darkened by the heavy curtains. = 厚重的窗帘使房间变暗了。
The picture was darkened by the aging process. = 图片由于老化而变暗了。

疑问句:
Does the room darken when you close the blinds? = 你拉下百叶窗时房间会变暗吗？
Will the sky darken before the storm arrives? = 暴风雨来临之前天空会变暗吗？

否定句:
The sky didn't darken until late afternoon. = 直到下午晚些时候天空才变暗。
He didn't darken the mood of the party. = 他没有使聚会的气氛变得沉闷。
