contended:/kənˈtɛndɪd/| adj. v.|争论的；坚决主张的；满足的；舒适的|The adjective "contended" describes something that is disputed or argued about, or someone who holds a firm belief or opinion.  It can also mean feeling satisfied and happy. |例句：The researchers contended that their findings were statistically significant.  研究人员坚称他们的发现具有统计学意义。

The family lived a contented life in the countryside.  这个家庭在乡下过着满足的生活。|近义词：disputed, argued, satisfied, pleased |反义词：uncontested, undisputed, dissatisfied, unhappy |词性：adj. v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contends that the earth is flat. = 他坚称地球是平的。
They contend for the championship title. = 他们争夺冠军头衔。

一般过去时:
She contended that she was innocent. = 她辩称自己是无辜的。
The two teams contended fiercely for the victory. = 两队为胜利而激烈争夺。

现在完成时:
They have contended with many challenges in their business. = 他们的生意已经面临许多挑战。
She has contended for years that her rights were violated. = 多年来她一直声称自己的权利受到侵犯。

过去完成时:
He had contended for years before he finally won the case. = 他奋斗多年后才最终赢得了官司。
They had contended with harsh weather conditions during their expedition. = 在探险期间，他们经受了恶劣的天气条件的考验。

将来时:
They will contend for the top spot in the rankings. = 他们将争夺排行榜的榜首位置。
She will contend that the evidence is insufficient. = 她将争辩说证据不足。

虚拟语气:
If he contended more forcefully, he might have won the argument. = 如果他更有力地争辩，他可能就赢得了这场辩论。
I wish I had contended for a better position. = 我希望我当时争取了一个更好的职位。

被动语态:
It was contended by many that the policy was unfair. = 许多人认为这项政策是不公平的。
The issue was contended throughout the debate. = 这个问题在整个辩论中都存在争议。

疑问句:
Does he contend that the theory is correct? = 他是否认为该理论是正确的？
What did they contend for in the election? = 他们在选举中争夺什么？

否定句:
He did not contend with the difficulties. = 他没有克服困难。
They did not contend that the results were accurate. = 他们没有认为结果是准确的。


PHRASE->

contend against = 与…对抗；竞争
contend that = 坚决主张；认为
contend over = 为…而争论
