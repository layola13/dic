diagnosed:/ˈdaɪəɡnoʊzd/| v.|诊断，确诊|To identify a disease or problem by examining its symptoms.|The doctor diagnosed her with pneumonia. 医生诊断她患有肺炎。|determined, identified, ascertained|healthy, well


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The doctor diagnoses patients every day. = 医生每天都诊断病人。
He diagnoses illnesses based on symptoms. = 他根据症状诊断疾病。


一般过去时:
The doctor diagnosed the disease quickly. = 医生迅速诊断出这种疾病。
They diagnosed the problem as a software bug. = 他们诊断出问题是一个软件错误。


现在完成时:
She has been diagnosed with breast cancer. = 她被诊断出患有乳腺癌。
The engineers have diagnosed the malfunction. = 工程师们已经诊断出故障。


过去完成时:
The patient had been diagnosed with the flu before the symptoms appeared. = 在症状出现之前，病人已经被诊断出患有流感。
They had already diagnosed the problem before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经诊断出问题了。


将来时:
They will diagnose the cause of the accident soon. = 他们很快就会诊断出事故的原因。
The doctor will diagnose the patient tomorrow. = 医生明天将诊断病人。


虚拟语气:
If I were diagnosed with a serious illness, I would seek a second opinion. = 如果我被诊断出患有严重的疾病，我会寻求第二种意见。
I wish I had been diagnosed earlier. = 我希望我被诊断得更早一些。


被动语态:
The disease was diagnosed early. = 这种疾病被及早诊断出来。
The problem is being diagnosed by the experts. = 这个问题正在由专家诊断。


疑问句:
Has she been diagnosed yet? = 她已经被诊断了吗？
How was the problem diagnosed? = 这个问题是如何被诊断出来的？


否定句:
The doctor could not diagnose the illness. = 医生无法诊断出这种疾病。
The problem hasn't been diagnosed yet. = 这个问题还没有被诊断出来。


PHRASE->
be diagnosed with = 被诊断患有…
diagnose a problem = 诊断出一个问题
receive a diagnosis = 得到诊断结果
misdiagnose a condition = 误诊一种疾病
accurate diagnosis = 准确的诊断
