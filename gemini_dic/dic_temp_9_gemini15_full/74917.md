fretfulness:/ˈfretfʊlnəs/| n. |烦躁；焦虑；不安 |A state of being irritable, restless, and easily upset.  It often stems from underlying anxieties or dissatisfaction.  |例句：Her fretfulness was evident in her restless movements and agitated sighs.  她的烦躁不安从她不安分的举动和焦虑的叹息中显而易见。  The baby's fretfulness kept the parents awake all night.  婴儿的烦躁不安让父母整夜睡不着觉。|近义词：irritability, anxiety, restlessness, agitation, unease |反义词：calmness, serenity, peacefulness, composure
