misreport:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrt/| vt.|错误报道，歪曲报道|To give a false or inaccurate account of something.|The newspaper misreported the details of the accident. 报纸错误报道了事故的细节。|近义词：distort, misrepresent, falsify|反义词：accurately report, truthfully report


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misreports the facts regularly. 他经常歪曲事实。
The company misreports its profits every year. 这家公司每年都虚报利润。

一般过去时:
They misreported the election results. 他们错误报道了选举结果。
The journalist misreported the story to protect his source. 记者歪曲报道了这个故事以保护他的消息来源。

现在完成时:
The media has misreported the situation several times. 媒体已经多次歪曲了这一情况。
The government has misreported the number of casualties. 政府已经错误报道了伤亡人数。

过去完成时:
They had misreported the findings before the publication. 他们在出版物发表前就已经歪曲了调查结果。
The company had misreported its financial status for years. 这家公司多年来一直虚报财务状况。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
