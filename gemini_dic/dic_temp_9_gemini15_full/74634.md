The hostel offered a free-and-easy lifestyle. 旅社提供轻松自在的生活方式。|近义词：relaxed, informal, casual, unconcerned, easygoing |反义词：formal, stiff, rigid, tense, uptight

词源:  The term is a compound adjective, combining "free" (meaning unconstrained or liberated) and "easy" (meaning without difficulty or effort).  It's a descriptive phrase that emerged in the English language over time, with its earliest recorded usage dating back to the 19th century. The exact etymology is difficult to pinpoint to a single source, but its meaning is clearly derived from the combined meanings of its constituent words.  It reflects a cultural shift towards less rigid social norms and a greater emphasis on comfort and informality.
