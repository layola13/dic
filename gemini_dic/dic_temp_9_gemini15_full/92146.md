hypersensitizing: /ˌhaɪpəˈsɛnsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 超敏化；使过敏 | To make (someone or something) excessively sensitive or responsive, often to a specific stimulus.  This often refers to increasing the sensitivity of a biological system, such as the immune system, or a technological system, such as a sensor. |  The drug hypersensitized her skin to sunlight.  这种药使她的皮肤对阳光过敏。


The continuous exposure to loud noise hypersensitized his hearing. 持续暴露在巨大的噪音中使他的听力变得超敏锐。| sensitize, oversensitize | desensitize,  hyposensitize | 动词
