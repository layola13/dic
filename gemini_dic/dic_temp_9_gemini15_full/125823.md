murmurs:/ˈmɜːrmərz/ | n. v. | 低语；喃喃自语；低沉的声音 |  A soft, indistinct sound made by many people speaking quietly or a continuous low sound. |


英文解析:Murmurs can refer to the low, indistinct sound of many voices speaking softly, or a continuous low humming or buzzing sound.  It can also refer to the act of speaking softly or indistinctly.  In a medical context, it refers to an abnormal sound heard through a stethoscope, usually indicating a heart or blood vessel problem.

例句:

The crowd murmured their approval. = 人群低声表示赞同。

A low murmur came from the crowd. = 人群中传来低低的嗡嗡声。


近义词: whisper, mutter, rumble, drone, susurrus

反义词: shout, roar, bellow, clamor


词性: noun, verb
