notifications:/ˌnoʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |通知，通告，通知功能 |Information or a message informing someone of something.  Notifications can be about various events, updates, or alerts. |例句：I received a notification that my order had shipped. 我收到通知说我的订单已经发货了。

例句：The app sends notifications whenever there's a new message.  这个应用程序每当有新消息时都会发送通知。


近义词：alerts, announcements, advisories, warnings |反义词：silence, secrecy
