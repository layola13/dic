calibrators:/ˈkælɪbreɪtərz/| n.|校准器；校准仪|Instruments or devices used to calibrate other instruments or equipment, ensuring accuracy and precision.  They are essential for maintaining the reliability of various measuring tools and systems. |Example: The technician used precision calibrators to ensure the accuracy of the laboratory scales.  技师使用精密校准器来确保实验室秤的准确性。|近义词：gauges, standards, adjusters |反义词： (no direct antonyms, but the opposite concept would be "malfunctioning instruments" or "inaccurate devices")|名词
