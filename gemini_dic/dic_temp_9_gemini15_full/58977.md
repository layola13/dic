dutchy:/ˈdʌtʃi/| n.|公爵夫人；公国；（非正式）荷兰人；|A dutchy is a female ruler of a duchy, or a woman who holds the title of duchess. It can also refer informally to a person from the Netherlands, or a small area or region associated with something Dutch.|例句：She is a dutchy of great influence. 她是一位很有影响力的公爵夫人。|近义词：duchess, princess|反义词：commoner
