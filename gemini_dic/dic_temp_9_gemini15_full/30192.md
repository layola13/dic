celestify:/ˈsɛləstaɪf/| vt.|使…成天体；使…神圣化；|To raise to the heavens; to make heavenly or divine; to glorify.|例句：The artist sought to celestify the mundane aspects of daily life in her paintings.  艺术家试图在她绘画中将日常生活的平凡方面神圣化。

例句：They celestify their heroes, placing them on a pedestal above mortal failings. 他们将他们的英雄神化，把他们放在一个高于凡人缺点的基座上。


近义词：deify, sanctify, apotheosize|反义词：desecrate, profane, degrade|动词
