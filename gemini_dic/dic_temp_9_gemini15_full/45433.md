curabilities:/ˌkjʊərəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可治愈性；可治疗性|The curability of a disease refers to the extent to which it can be cured or its symptoms alleviated.  It often depends on factors like early detection and the availability of effective treatments.  The study focused on the curability of various cancers.|近义词：treatability, remediability|反义词：incurability
