cries:/kraɪz/| n. v.|哭泣；哭声|The noun "cries" refers to the sounds of crying, often expressing sadness, pain, or distress.  The verb "cries" is the third-person singular present tense of the verb "to cry," meaning to shed tears, usually from sadness, pain, or distress. |例句：The baby's cries filled the room.  婴儿的哭声充满了整个房间。  She cries every time she watches that movie. 她每次看那部电影都会哭。|近义词：tears, sobs, wails, weeping|反义词：laughter, joy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cries easily. = 他很容易哭。
She cries every time she sees a sad movie. = 她每次看到悲伤的电影都会哭。


一般过去时:
The child cried all night. = 孩子哭了一整夜。
He cried when he heard the bad news. = 他听到坏消息时哭了。


现在完成时:
She has cried herself to sleep. = 她哭着睡着了。
The baby has cried for hours. = 婴儿已经哭了好几个小时了。


过去完成时:
He had cried before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经哭过了。
She had cried until her eyes were swollen. = 她哭到眼睛都肿了。


将来时:
I think she will cry when she hears the news. = 我想她听到这个消息会哭。
He will cry if he fails the exam. = 如果考试不及格，他会哭的。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would cry too. = 如果我是你，我也会哭。
I wish I hadn't cried so much yesterday. = 我真希望昨天不要哭那么多。


被动语态: (很少用于cries)  The situation was such that many people were moved to cries. =  情况是这样的，许多人都被感动得哭了。

否定句:
