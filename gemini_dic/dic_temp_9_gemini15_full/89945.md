horsemanship:/ˈhɔːsmənʃɪp/ | n. | 骑术；马术；马艺 | The skill of riding and managing horses.  It encompasses a range of abilities, from basic riding skills to advanced equestrian disciplines. |例句：Her horsemanship is exceptional; she handles the horse with grace and skill.  她的马术非凡；她优雅娴熟地驾驭着马匹。


例句：He demonstrated impressive horsemanship during the competition. 他在比赛中展现了令人印象深刻的马术。


近义词：equestrianism, riding skill


反义词：None (lack of horsemanship could be expressed with words like clumsiness or ineptitude)


词性：名词
