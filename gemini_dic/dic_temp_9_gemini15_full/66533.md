exhaustive:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪv/| adj.|彻底的；详尽的；穷尽的|  Thorough and complete; including everything.  It leaves no aspect unexplored or unconsidered. |例句：The police conducted an exhaustive search for the missing child.  警方对失踪儿童进行了彻底的搜寻。

例句：She gave an exhaustive account of her travels. 她详细地讲述了她的旅行经历。


近义词：comprehensive, thorough, complete, detailed, meticulous

反义词：incomplete, superficial, cursory, brief, partial

词性:形容词
