hiss:/hɪs/| v. n.|嘶嘶声；发出嘶嘶声|To make a long, thin sound like the letter 's'.  It can also refer to a sound made by a snake or escaping steam.|例句：The snake hissed at me. 那条蛇朝我嘶嘶作响。

The radiator hissed as the steam escaped.  散热器发出嘶嘶声，因为蒸汽泄漏了。|近义词：sizzle, whisper, sputter|反义词：boom, roar


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The snake hisses menacingly. = 蛇发出威胁性的嘶嘶声。
He hisses when he's angry. = 他生气时会发出嘶嘶声。

一般过去时:
The cat hissed at the dog. = 猫朝狗嘶嘶作响。
The kettle hissed loudly as it boiled. = 水壶沸腾时发出很大的嘶嘶声。

现在完成时:
The engine has hissed and sputtered all morning. = 引擎整个上午一直在嘶嘶作响和喷溅。
The pressure cooker has hissed steam for the last hour. = 高压锅已经嘶嘶地喷出蒸汽一个小时了。

过去完成时:
The boiler had hissed before it finally exploded. = 锅炉在最终爆炸之前已经发出嘶嘶声了。
The tires had hissed as the car skidded. = 汽车打滑时轮胎发出嘶嘶声。

将来时:
The steam will hiss from the pipe. = 蒸汽将从管道中嘶嘶地喷出。
The escaping gas will hiss loudly. = 泄漏的气体会发出很大的嘶嘶声。

虚拟语气:
If the snake hissed again, I would run away. = 如果蛇再次嘶嘶作响，我会跑开。
I wish the radiator wouldn't hiss so loudly. = 我希望散热器不要发出这么大的嘶嘶声。

被动语态:
A hissing sound was heard from the engine. = 从引擎中听到嘶嘶声。
The escaping steam was hissed out of the valve. = 蒸汽从阀门中嘶嘶地喷出。


疑问句:
Did you hear that hissing sound? = 你听到那个嘶嘶声了吗？
Is the tea kettle hissing yet? = 茶壶已经在嘶嘶地响了吗？


否定句:
The snake didn't hiss at all. = 蛇根本没有嘶嘶作响。
The engine wasn't hissing, it was knocking. = 引擎不是嘶嘶作响，而是在敲打。
