repays:/riˈpeɪz/| v.|偿还；报答；值得|To give someone something back in return for something they have done, either good or bad.  It can refer to financial repayment, a return of a favor, or the consequences of an action.|例句：He repays his debts promptly. 他及时偿还债务。|例句：Her kindness repays my help. 她的友善回报了我的帮助。|近义词：reimburse, compensate, reward, avenge|反义词：owe, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repays his loans every month. = 他每月偿还贷款。
The investment repays itself over time. = 这项投资最终会获得回报。

一般过去时:
She repaid the favor by helping him move. = 她通过帮助他搬家来报答他的恩情。
They repaid the money they borrowed. = 他们偿还了借款。

现在完成时:
He has repaid all his debts. = 他已经还清了所有债务。
The company has repaid its investors handsomely. = 公司已经慷慨地回报了投资者。

过去完成时:
She had repaid the loan before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前已经还清了贷款。
They had repaid the kindness shown to them years ago. = 他们已经报答了多年以前对他们的好意。

将来时:
He will repay the money next week. = 他下周将偿还这笔钱。
The project will repay the initial investment. = 该项目将收回初始投资。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would repay you immediately. = 如果我有钱，我会立即还你。
I wish I could repay you for your generosity. = 我希望我能报答你的慷慨。

被动语态:
The loan will be repaid in installments. = 这笔贷款将分期偿还。
The favor was repaid with interest. = 这份恩情得到了丰厚的回报。

疑问句:
Will he repay the debt? = 他会偿还债务吗？
How will the investment repay itself? = 这项投资将如何获得回报？

否定句:
He didn't repay the money on time. = 他没有按时还钱。
The investment did not repay itself quickly. = 这项投资没有很快获得回报。


PHRASE->
repay a loan = 还清贷款
repay a debt = 偿还债务
repay a favor = 报答恩情
repay kindness = 报答好意
repay someone's trust = 辜负某人的信任 (负面用法)
