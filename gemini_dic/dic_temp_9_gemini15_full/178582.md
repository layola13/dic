recorrecting:/ˌriːkəˈrektɪŋ/| v. |重新校正，再次校对|The process of correcting something that has already been corrected, often to improve accuracy or consistency.  It implies a second or further round of correction. |例句：The editor spent hours recorrecting the manuscript after the first round of edits. 编辑花了数小时在第一次编辑之后重新校对稿件。

例句：After finding several more errors, the teacher decided to recorrect the student's essay.  在发现更多错误后，老师决定重新批改学生的论文。


近义词：revising, proofreading, editing, amending


反义词：leaving uncorrected, accepting errors


词性：动词
