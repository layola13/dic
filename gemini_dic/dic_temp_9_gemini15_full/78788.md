gillless:/ˈdʒɪllɪs/| adj.|无鳃的|Lacking gills.  The term typically refers to animals, particularly aquatic animals, that do not possess gills for respiration.  This characteristic often implies that they breathe air or use other respiratory mechanisms.|例句：The gillless fish uses its lungs to breathe air.  这种无鳃鱼用肺呼吸空气。|近义词：agill|反义词：gilled
