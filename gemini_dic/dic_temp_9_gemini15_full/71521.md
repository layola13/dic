flap's: /flæps/ | n. | 拍打声；（翅膀等的）拍打；活门；襟翼 |  The possessive form of "flap," referring to something belonging to a flap.  It describes a sound, a motion, or a part of something. |例句：The bird's flaps were powerful, carrying it high above the trees.  鸟的翅膀拍打有力，带着它飞到树梢之上。


The sound of the engine's flaps closing was deafening. 发动机襟翼关闭的声音震耳欲聋。|近义词：beat, clap,  (for a hinged part)  lid, cover |反义词： (depending on context)  silence, stillness, closure
