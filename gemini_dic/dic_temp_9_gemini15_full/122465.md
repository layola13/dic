mobilizers:/ˈmoʊbəlaɪzərz/ | n. |动员者，发动者 |People or things that mobilize something, especially resources or people for a particular purpose.  They are agents of change, initiating and coordinating efforts to achieve a specific goal. |例句：The government mobilizers worked tirelessly to ensure sufficient resources were available for disaster relief. 政府动员者夜以继日地工作，以确保有足够的资源用于救灾。

The community mobilizers organized a fundraising campaign to support the local hospital. 社区动员者组织了一场筹款活动来支持当地医院。|近义词：activists, organizers, agitators |反义词：deactivators, hinderers, obstructers
