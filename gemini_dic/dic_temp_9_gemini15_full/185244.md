retranscribe:/ˌriːˈtrænskraɪb/| vt.|重新抄写；重新转录|To write or type something again, especially from a recording or other source.|例句：The court reporter had to retranscribe the entire testimony. 法庭速记员不得不重新抄写全部证词。
例句：We need to retranscribe this audio file to improve the quality.我们需要重新转录此音频文件以提高质量。|近义词：rewrite, transcribe again|反义词：delete, erase|动词
