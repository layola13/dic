quasi-fraternal:/ˈkwɑːzaɪfreɪˈtɜːrnəl/| adj. |兄弟般的，近似兄弟的 |  Resembling or suggesting a brotherly relationship, but not truly fraternal;  characterized by a close bond of friendship or camaraderie similar to that between brothers.  |例句：Their bond, though not born of blood, was quasi-fraternal. 他们的联系并非血缘关系，但却像兄弟般亲密。  Their quasi-fraternal relationship developed over years of shared adventures.  他们近似兄弟般的关系在多年的共同冒险中发展起来。|近义词：brotherly, comradely, close, intimate |反义词：estranged, distant, unfriendly
