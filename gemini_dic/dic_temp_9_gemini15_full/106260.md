knowledgeable: /ˈnɑːlədʒəbl/ | adj. |  知识渊博的，有见识的 | Having or showing a lot of knowledge.  |例句：He is a knowledgeable expert in ancient history. 他是一位知识渊博的古代史专家。

例句：She's a knowledgeable source of information on local wildlife. 她是当地野生动物信息的权威人士。


近义词：informed, learned, erudite, well-informed, expert | 反义词：ignorant, uninformed, unlearned


词源:  The word "knowledgeable" comes from the Old English word "cnawan," meaning "to know,"  plus the suffix "-able," indicating capability or capacity.  The word evolved through Middle English "knowen" and "knowleche" (knowledge) before arriving at its modern form.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is knowledgeable about computers. = 他对电脑很了解。
She is knowledgeable in many fields. = 她精通很多领域。

一般过去时:
He was knowledgeable about the subject. = 他对这个话题很了解。
She was knowledgeable in classical music. = 她精通古典音乐。

现在完成时:
He has become very knowledgeable about wine. = 他对葡萄酒的了解已经非常深入了。
She has become knowledgeable in several languages. = 她已经掌握了几门外语。

过去完成时:
He had already become knowledgeable in the field before starting his PhD. = 他在攻读博士学位之前就已经在这个领域积累了丰富的知识。
She had been knowledgeable about art history for many years. = 她对艺术史的了解已经有许多年了。

将来时:
He will be knowledgeable in artificial intelligence by next year. = 到明年他将会成为人工智能方面的专家。
She will be more knowledgeable about the topic after the research. = 研究之后，她将会对这个话题更加了解。

虚拟语气:
If he were more knowledgeable, he would have made a better decision. = 如果他更有知识，他会做出更好的决定。
I wish I were more knowledgeable about this issue. = 我希望我对这个问题更了解。

被动语态:  (The adjective "knowledgeable" is rarely used in the passive voice.  The passive voice would apply to the verb "to know.")
The subject was well known to him. = 他对这个主题很熟悉。( This uses a related verb, not the adjective.)
The information is widely known. = 这信息广为人知。(This uses a related verb, not the adjective.)


疑问句:
Is he knowledgeable about the project? = 他了解这个项目吗？
Are you knowledgeable in this area? = 你在这个领域很精通吗？

否定句:
He is not knowledgeable about that topic. = 他对那个话题不了解。
She is not very knowledgeable in science. = 她对科学不太了解。
