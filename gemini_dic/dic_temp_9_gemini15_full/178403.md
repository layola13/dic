reconnoiter:/rɪˈkɑːnɪtər/| vt. |侦察；勘察；探测 |To make a preliminary survey of (an area or region) in order to gain information, especially about the enemy's position and strength.  |例句：The scouts reconnoitered the enemy's position before the attack.  侦察兵在进攻前侦察了敌人的阵地。

例句：The general sent a patrol to reconnoiter the terrain. 将军派出一支巡逻队去勘察地形。


近义词：survey, scout, explore, examine | 反义词：None specifically, but words implying a lack of investigation or preparation could be considered antonyms.


USAGE->

They reconnoiter the area before each mission. = 他们在每次任务之前都会侦察该地区。
The team reconnoiters the terrain for potential hazards. = 该团队侦察地形以寻找潜在的危险。


一般过去时:
The soldiers reconnoitered the enemy's defenses. = 士兵们侦察了敌人的防御工事。
We reconnoitered the route before starting our journey. = 我们在开始旅程之前侦察了路线。


现在完成时:

The explorers have reconnoitered several mountain passes. = 探险家们已经侦察了几个山口。


过去完成时:
The army had reconnoitered the battlefield before the main attack. = 大部队在主要进攻之前已经侦察了战场。
The scouts had reconnoitered the area for several days. = 侦察兵们已经侦察了该地区好几天了。

虚拟语气:
If we had reconnoitered the area properly, we would have avoided the ambush. = 如果我们充分侦察了该地区，我们就不会遭遇伏击。
It would be wise to reconnoiter the enemy's position before engaging. = 在交战之前侦察敌人的位置是明智的。


被动语态:

The route has been reconnoitered several times. = 该路线已经被侦察了好几次。


疑问句:

否定句:
They did not reconnoiter the terrain before advancing. = 他们在前进之前没有侦察地形。

词源:
From Old French *reconnoistre, from re- "again" + connoistre "to know," from Latin cognoscere "to get to know," from com- "together" + gnoscere "to know."  The meaning evolved from "to know again" to "to investigate thoroughly."
