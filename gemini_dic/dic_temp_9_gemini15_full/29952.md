cavemen:/ˈkeɪvmæn/| n. |穴居人，原始人 |A caveman is a person who lived in a cave, especially during the Paleolithic period.  They are often depicted as being uncivilized and primitive. |例句：The movie depicted cavemen hunting mammoths. 这部电影描绘了穴居人猎杀猛犸象。

例句：Scientists found evidence of early cavemen tools near the river. 科学家在河边发现了早期穴居人工具的证据。

近义词：early humans, prehistoric people, troglodyte


反义词：modern human, civilized person


词性:名词
