outdesign:/ˌaʊtˈdɪzaɪn/| vt.|胜过…的设计，设计得比…好|To surpass in design; to design something better than.|The new building outdesigns all the others in the area. 新建筑的设计胜过该地区所有其他建筑。|近义词：outshine, surpass, excel|反义词：underdesign


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outdesign their competitors every year. = 他们每年都在设计上胜过竞争对手。
This company outdesigns all its rivals in terms of innovation. = 在创新方面，这家公司设计超越了所有竞争对手。


一般过去时:
The architect outdesigned his previous work with this stunning creation. = 建筑师用这件令人惊叹的作品超越了他以前的作品。
Last year, they outdesigned the market leader with their new product. = 去年，他们凭借新产品在设计上超越了市场领导者。


现在完成时:
They have outdesigned the competition with their revolutionary design. = 他们凭借革命性的设计已经超越了竞争对手。
The company has outdesigned its rivals for several consecutive years. = 这家公司连续几年在设计上都胜过竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the time the deadline arrived, they had outdesigned all the other teams. = 到截止日期到来时，他们已经超越了所有其他团队的设计。
They had outdesigned their competitors' initial design before even presenting their own. = 甚至在展示自己的设计之前，他们就已经超越了竞争对手的初始设计。


将来时:
We believe that we will outdesign the current market leader with our next product. = 我们相信，我们的下一款产品将在设计上超越目前的市场领导者。
They predict they will outdesign their competitors by implementing AI in their design process. = 他们预测，通过在其设计过程中实施人工智能，他们将超越竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If they had focused more on user experience, they would have outdesigned the app. = 如果他们更关注用户体验，他们本可以设计出比这个应用更好的产品。
I wish we had outdesigned our competitors' product. = 我希望我们能设计出比竞争对手的产品更好的产品。


被动语态:
Their design was outdesigned by a smaller startup. = 他们的设计被一家规模较小的创业公司超越。
The product was outdesigned in terms of both functionality and aesthetics. = 该产品在功能和美学方面都被超越了。


疑问句:
Will our design outdesign the competitor's proposal? = 我们的设计能否胜过竞争对手的提案？
Does this new model outdesign the previous version significantly? = 这个新模型的设计是否显著优于之前的版本？


否定句:
We did not outdesign the competition this year. = 今年我们没有在设计上超越竞争对手。
The new design did not outdesign the existing product; it only improved a few minor details. = 新设计并没有超越现有产品；它只改进了一些细微之处。


PHRASE->
outdesign the competition = 超越竞争对手的设计
outdesign the market = 超越市场的设计
outdesign the expectation = 超越预期设计
outdesign the previous model = 超越前代模型的设计
outdesign the standard = 超越标准的设计
