semi-solid:/ˈsɛmiˌsɑːlɪd/| adj.|半固体的，半流体的|Having a consistency between a solid and a liquid; neither completely solid nor completely liquid.  It describes substances that hold their shape but can be deformed or poured with some effort.|例句：Toothpaste is a semi-solid substance. 牙膏是一种半固体物质。

例句：The mud was semi-solid, making it difficult to walk through. 泥巴是半流体的，很难走过去。


近义词：viscous, pasty, gelatinous

反义词：solid, liquid


词性:形容词
