presmooths: /ˌpriːˈsmuːðz/ | v. | 预先平滑；预处理平滑 | To make something smoother or more even before further processing.  This term is primarily used in technical contexts, often related to image processing, data analysis, or manufacturing.  It involves a pre-processing step to improve the quality or efficiency of subsequent operations. | Example: The software presmooths the image data to reduce noise before edge detection.  软件在边缘检测之前预先平滑图像数据以减少噪声。| 近义词: pre-process, smooth, refine | 反义词: roughen, coarsen | 动词
