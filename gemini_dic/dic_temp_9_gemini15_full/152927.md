pedestrianizing: /pɛˈdɛstriənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |  使行人专用；禁止车辆通行 | To make an area accessible primarily or exclusively to pedestrians by restricting or prohibiting vehicular traffic.  | The city is pedestrianizing the downtown area to improve safety and encourage walking.  城市正在将市中心区域改造成行人专用区，以提高安全性并鼓励步行。 |  close off to traffic, make car-free | allow vehicular traffic, open to traffic | 动词
