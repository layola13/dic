self-employed:/ˌsɛlf ɪmˈplɔɪd/| adj.|自雇的，自由职业的|Someone who is self-employed works for themselves, rather than working for an employer.  They are their own boss and responsible for finding their own work and managing their own business.|  Many artists are self-employed. 许多艺术家都是自由职业者。|She's self-employed as a consultant. 她以顾问身份自己经营生意。|employed, freelance|employed, salaried


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is self-employed. = 他是自由职业者。
They are self-employed consultants. = 他们是自由职业顾问。


一般过去时:
She was self-employed for many years. = 她自己经营生意很多年了。
He was self-employed before he got this job. = 在他找到这份工作之前，他是自由职业者。


现在完成时:
He has been self-employed since 2010. = 他从2010年以来一直是自由职业者。
She has been self-employed for over a decade. = 她自己经营生意超过十年了。


过去完成时:
She had been self-employed before she started working for the company. = 在她开始为公司工作之前，她一直是自由职业者。
He had been self-employed for five years when he decided to take a salaried position. = 当他决定接受一份有薪工作时，他已经自己经营生意五年了。


将来时:
I will be self-employed next year. = 明年我将成为自由职业者。
They will likely be self-employed in the future. = 他们将来很可能成为自由职业者。


虚拟语气:
If I were self-employed, I would have more flexibility. = 如果我是自由职业者，我会更有灵活性。
I wish I were self-employed so I could set my own hours. = 我希望我是自由职业者，这样我就可以自己安排时间了。


被动语态:
(This adjective doesn't typically use passive voice.  The passive voice would apply to the verb describing the action of becoming self-employed.)  He was declared self-employed by the tax authorities. = 他被税务机关认定为自由职业者。
She was recently made self-employed after leaving her corporate job. = 她离开公司工作后，最近成为了自由职业者。


疑问句:
Are you self-employed? = 你是自由职业者吗？
Is he self-employed or does he work for a company? = 他是自由职业者还是为公司工作？


否定句:
He is not self-employed; he works for a large corporation. = 他不是自由职业者；他在一家大公司工作。
She is not currently self-employed, but she plans to be in the future. = 她目前不是自由职业者，但她计划将来成为自由职业者。


PHRASE->
be self-employed = 自己经营生意
become self-employed = 成为自由职业者
remain self-employed = 保持自由职业者身份
highly self-employed = 高度自由职业的 (implies a high level of independence)
newly self-employed = 新近成为自由职业者的
