coagent:/ˈkoʊeɪdʒənt/| n.| 合作者，共同代理人 | A coagent is a person or entity that acts in cooperation with another to achieve a common goal.  Coagents often share responsibilities and work together to complete a task or project. | Example: The two companies acted as coagents in the development of the new technology.  两个公司在开发新技术方面担任合作者。 |近义词：collaborator, partner, associate |反义词：opponent, adversary
