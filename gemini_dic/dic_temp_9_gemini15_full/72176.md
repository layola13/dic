floozy:/ˈfluːzi/| n.|轻浮的女人，荡妇|A floozy is a woman considered to be promiscuous and of low morals.  The term is highly offensive and should be avoided.  It implies a lack of respectability and suggests a woman is only valued for her sexual appeal.|例句：He called her a floozy after she rejected his advances. 他在她拒绝他的示好后称她为荡妇。|近义词：slut, tart, hussy, tramp|反义词：lady, virtuous woman|名词
