exoplanets:/ˈɛksoʊˌplænɪts/| n. |系外行星|Planets that orbit stars other than the Sun.  Exoplanets are located outside our solar system. The study of exoplanets is a rapidly growing field of astronomy. |例句：Scientists have discovered thousands of exoplanets orbiting distant stars.  科学家们已经发现了数千颗围绕遥远恒星运行的系外行星。

例句：The Kepler mission was instrumental in finding many exoplanets. 开普勒任务在发现许多系外行星方面发挥了重要作用。


近义词： extrasolar planets | 反义词：planets in our solar system
