directory:/dɪˈrɛktəri/| n.|目录；文件夹；姓名地址录；指南|A directory is a list of names, addresses, or other information, often arranged alphabetically or in some other systematic way.  It can also refer to a file or folder on a computer that organizes other files.|例句：The phone book is a directory of names and numbers.电话簿是一个姓名和号码的目录。|例句：I saved the document in the Documents directory on my computer. 我将文档保存在电脑上的“文档”文件夹中。|近义词：index, catalog, guide, list|反义词：chaos, disorder
