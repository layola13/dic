cohabiter:/koʊˈhæbɪtər/| n. |同居者，合住者 |A person who lives with another person, especially a romantic partner, without being married.  |Example:  They are cohabiters who have lived together for five years. 他们是一对同居五年了的伴侣。|近义词：partner, roommate, companion |反义词：stranger


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cohabits with his girlfriend. = 他和他的女朋友同居。
They cohabit peacefully. = 他们和平地同居。


一般过去时:
They cohabited for many years before getting married. = 他们在结婚前同居了很多年。
We cohabited in a small apartment. = 我们住在一个小公寓里同居。


现在完成时:
They have cohabited since college. = 他们从大学时代就开始同居了。
We have cohabited for over a decade. = 我们同居已经超过十年了。


过去完成时:
They had cohabited for two years before they decided to get engaged. = 他们在决定订婚之前已经同居了两年。
Before moving in together, they had cohabited in separate apartments. = 在搬到一起住之前，他们分别住在不同的公寓里同居。


将来时:
They will cohabit after they graduate. = 他们毕业后将会同居。
We will cohabit until we can afford a house. = 我们会同居，直到我们买得起房子为止。


虚拟语气:
If they cohabited, they would save money on rent. = 如果他们同居，他们就能省下房租钱。
I wish we had cohabited sooner. = 我希望我们早点同居。


被动语态:
Cohabitation is often seen as a trial run for marriage. = 同居通常被视为婚姻的试金石。
The issue of cohabitation was discussed at length. = 同居问题被详细讨论了。


疑问句:
Do they cohabit? = 他们同居吗？
Have you ever cohabited? = 你曾经同居过吗？


否定句:
They do not cohabit. = 他们没有同居。
We have never cohabited. = 我们从未同居过。


PHRASE->
cohabit with = 与...同居
long-term cohabiter = 长期同居者
unmarried cohabiter = 未婚同居者
cohabiting couple = 同居情侣
cohabitation agreement = 同居协议
