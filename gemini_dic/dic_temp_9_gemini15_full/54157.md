disentangled: /ˌdɪsɪnˈtæŋɡld/ | v. | 解开；理清；摆脱；使解脱 | To free from entanglement; to unravel or untangle.  | 例句：He disentangled himself from the complicated legal proceedings. 他摆脱了复杂的法律诉讼。

She carefully disentangled the headphone wires. 她仔细地解开了耳机线。


近义词：untangle, unravel, extricate, unknot | 反义词：entangle, complicate, involve
