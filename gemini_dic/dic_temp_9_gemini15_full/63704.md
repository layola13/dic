ephemeralism:/ɪˈfɛmərəlɪzəm/| n. |短暂性；瞬息万变；短暂的事物|The philosophy or belief that all things are temporary and fleeting; the emphasis on the transient nature of existence and experience.  It often involves an appreciation of beauty and experience in the face of inevitable change and decay. |例句：The artist's work explores themes of ephemeralism, capturing fleeting moments of beauty and decay.  这位艺术家的作品探索了短暂性的主题，捕捉了美丽和衰败转瞬即逝的时刻。


例句：His paintings celebrate the ephemeralism of life, focusing on the beauty of things that are soon gone.  他的画作赞美了生命的短暂性，关注那些很快消失的事物的美。


近义词：transitoriness, transience, brevity, fleetingness


反义词：permanence, eternity, perpetuity


词性:名词
