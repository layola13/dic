cooking: /ˈkʊkɪŋ/ | n. |烹饪，烹调|The act or process of preparing food by applying heat.  It involves various methods and techniques to transform raw ingredients into palatable dishes.| 例句：I enjoy cooking Italian food. = 我喜欢烹饪意大利菜。

例句：The aroma of cooking wafted from the kitchen. = 烹饪的香味从厨房飘来。


近义词：preparing, cooking, culinary, culinary arts | 反义词：eating, consuming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She cooks dinner every night. = 她每天晚上做饭。
He cooks a mean steak. = 他煎牛排的技术一流。


一般过去时:
I cooked pasta for lunch yesterday. = 我昨天午餐做了意大利面。
They cooked a delicious meal for the party. = 他们为派对做了一顿美味的饭菜。


现在完成时:
I have cooked this recipe many times. = 我做过很多次这个菜谱了。
She has cooked for the family for over twenty years. = 她为家人做饭超过二十年了。


过去完成时:
We had cooked the chicken before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，我们已经把鸡肉做好了。
He had cooked dinner before she got home. = 她回家之前，他已经做好了晚饭。


将来时:
I will cook dinner tomorrow night. = 我明天晚上做晚饭。
They will cook a special meal for their anniversary. = 他们将为他们的周年纪念日准备一顿特别的饭菜。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would cook a more elaborate meal. = 如果我有更多时间，我会做一顿更精美的饭菜。
I wish I could cook as well as my mother. = 我希望我能像我妈妈一样擅长烹饪。


被动语态:

疑问句:
