familial:/fəˈmɪliəl/ | adj. | 家庭的；家族的；家族似的 | Relating to or characteristic of a family.  The adjective "familial" describes something connected to or typical of a family. It can refer to relationships, traits, or diseases. |例句：He had a familial resemblance to his father. 他和他父亲长得像。

例句：The doctor suspects a familial link to the disease. 医生怀疑这种疾病与家族遗传有关。

近义词：family, kinship, domestic

反义词：unrelated, alien
