demobs:/diːˈmɒbz/ | vt. |复员；遣散（军队）|To discharge (soldiers, sailors, etc.) from military service; to disband (a military unit).|例句：The government demobbed thousands of soldiers after the war. 战争结束后，政府遣散了数千名士兵。
例句：The regiment was demobbed after serving overseas for three years.  该团在海外服役三年后被遣散。|近义词：discharge, dismiss, disband |反义词：mobilize, recruit |动词


USAGE->
