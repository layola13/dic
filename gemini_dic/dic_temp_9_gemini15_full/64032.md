epithelioid:/ˌɛpɪˈθiːlɔɪd/| adj.|上皮样的，上皮细胞样的|Resembling epithelial cells in shape or arrangement.  The term is often used to describe cells that are not actually epithelial cells but share their morphological characteristics, such as a polygonal shape and abundant cytoplasm.|例句：The granulomas showed epithelioid histiocytes.  肉芽肿显示上皮样组织细胞。|例句：The tumor cells exhibited an epithelioid morphology. 肿瘤细胞呈现上皮样形态。|近义词：epithelial-like, cell-like|反义词：fibrous, mesenchymal
