hypnotize:/ˈhɪpnətaɪz/ | vt. |催眠；迷惑；强烈吸引 |To put (someone) into a hypnotic state; to fascinate or influence (someone) powerfully. |例句：The magician hypnotized the volunteer on stage. 魔术师催眠了舞台上的志愿者。

例句：The beautiful scenery hypnotized me; I forgot all my troubles.  美丽的景色让我着迷，我忘记了所有的烦恼。


近义词：mesmerize, fascinate, enthrall, captivate | 反义词：awaken, rouse
