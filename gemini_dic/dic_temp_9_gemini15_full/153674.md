penuriousness:/pəˈnʊəriəsnəs/ | n. | 吝啬；贫困；缺乏 | Extreme unwillingness to spend money; stinginess; poverty.  The state of being extremely poor or lacking resources. |例句：His penuriousness prevented him from enjoying life's simple pleasures.  他的吝啬使他无法享受生活中的简单乐趣。

His family lived in penuriousness after his father's death.  他父亲去世后，他的家人生活贫困。


近义词：stinginess, miserliness, frugality (if extreme), poverty, indigence |反义词：generosity, extravagance, affluence, wealth


词源:  From Middle English *penurious, from Old French penurie "poverty, scarcity," from Latin pauper "poor" + -itas (suffix indicating state or condition).  The meaning shifted over time from simply "poverty" to "stinginess" due to the association between poverty and the need to be frugal.
