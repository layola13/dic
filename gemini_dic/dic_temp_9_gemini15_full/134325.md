noninstructiveness: /ˌnɒnɪnˈstrʌktɪvnəs/ | n. |非指导性，缺乏指导性 | The lack of clear instructions or guidance; the state of being uninstructive.  It refers to something that fails to provide helpful information, direction, or teaching. |例句：The noninstructiveness of the manual led to many frustrated users.  = 手册缺乏指导性导致许多用户感到沮丧。

The noninstructiveness of the lecture left the students confused. = 讲座缺乏指导性，让学生们感到困惑。|近义词：unhelpfulness, vagueness, ambiguity |反义词：instructiveness, clarity, guidance
