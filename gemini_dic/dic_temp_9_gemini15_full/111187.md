lithographies:/lɪˈθɑːɡrəfiz/| n. |平版印刷术；平版印刷品|The process of printing from a flat surface, typically a stone or metal plate, on which the image is drawn or etched.  Lithography is a unique printing method that uses the principle of oil and water repelling each other. |例句：The museum showcased a stunning collection of lithographies by renowned artists.  博物馆展出了著名艺术家令人惊叹的平版印刷品收藏。

例句：He learned the art of lithography in Paris. 他在巴黎学习了平版印刷术。


近义词：printmaking, stone printing |反义词：digital printing (in the context of printing methods) |名词
