gloves:/ɡlʌvz/| n.|手套|Protective coverings for the hands, typically made of fabric, leather, or other materials.|例句：I need to wear gloves when I handle chemicals.  我处理化学品时需要戴手套。|例句：She wore warm gloves to protect her hands from the cold. 她戴着暖和的手套来保护她的手免受寒冷。|近义词：mittens, gauntlets|反义词: bare hands
