reinstruction:/ˌriːɪnˈstrʌkʃən/| n.| 再指示；重新指导；再教导 |The act of instructing again or differently; a repetition or modification of instructions.|例句：The reinstruction of the employees on safety procedures is essential after the accident. 事故发生后，对员工进行安全程序的再指导至关重要。


例句：The reinstruction of the software clarified the ambiguous steps. 软件的重新指导阐明了模棱两可的步骤。


近义词：reinforcement, reiteration, re-education|反义词：misinstruction, misunderstanding|词性:名词
