drin:/drɪn/| v.|饮，喝|To drink is to swallow liquid.|例句：I drin water every day.我每天喝水。  I drin coffee in the morning. 我早上喝咖啡。|近义词：drink, imbibe, quaff|反义词：pour out, spill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drinks tea every afternoon. = 他每天下午都喝茶。
She drinks water with her meals. = 她吃饭时喝水。

一般过去时:
They drank milk before bed. = 他们睡前喝了牛奶。
He drank too much beer last night. = 他昨晚喝了太多啤酒。

现在完成时:
I have drunk all the juice. = 我已经喝完所有的果汁了。
She has drunk three cups of coffee today. = 她今天已经喝了三杯咖啡。

过去完成时:
They had drunk all the wine before the party started. = 派对开始前他们已经喝完所有的酒了。
She had drunk several glasses of water by the time she arrived. = 她到达时已经喝了几杯水了。

将来时:
I will drink a glass of wine with dinner. = 我晚饭时会喝一杯酒。
They will drink champagne to celebrate. = 他们会喝香槟庆祝。

虚拟语气:
If I were thirsty, I would drink some water. = 如果我渴了，我会喝些水。
I wish I had drunk less coffee yesterday. = 我真希望昨天少喝点咖啡。

被动语态:
The milk was drunk by the children. = 牛奶被孩子们喝了。
The water is being drunk quickly. = 水正在迅速被喝掉。

疑问句:
Do you drink coffee? = 你喝咖啡吗？
What do you drink in the morning? = 你早上喝什么？

否定句:
I don't drink alcohol. = 我不喝酒。
He didn't drink anything all day. = 他一整天什么都没喝。
