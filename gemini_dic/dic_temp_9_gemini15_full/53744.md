discolorment:/dɪsˈkʌləmənt/| n. |变色；褪色；污渍|The alteration of the natural color of something, typically resulting in an unattractive or undesirable appearance.  It can be caused by various factors such as staining, aging, or chemical reactions.|例句：The discoloration on the wall was caused by a water leak. 墙上的变色是由漏水造成的。

例句：The sun caused significant discoloration to the antique furniture.  阳光导致古董家具严重褪色。


近义词：stain, fading, bleaching, discoloration, staining


反义词：enhancement, brightening, restoration



词性:名词
