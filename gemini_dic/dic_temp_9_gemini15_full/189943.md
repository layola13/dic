sanctification:/ˌsæŋktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |圣化；神圣化；成圣|The act of making something or someone holy; the process of becoming holy.  It often refers to a spiritual process of being set apart for God's purpose and transformed by God's grace.|例句：The sanctification of the church was a solemn and beautiful ceremony. 教堂的圣化仪式庄严而美丽。

例句：Through prayer and devotion, she sought sanctification. 通过祈祷和奉献，她寻求圣化。


近义词：consecration, hallowing, purification, dedication|反义词：profanation, desecration, pollution|词性：名词
