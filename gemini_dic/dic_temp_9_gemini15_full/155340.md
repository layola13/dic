pharmacists:/ˈfɑːrməsɪsts/ | n. | 药剂师 | A pharmacist is a licensed professional who prepares and dispenses medications, counsels patients on drug use, and provides other pharmaceutical care services.  |  The pharmacist checked the prescription before dispensing the medication. 药剂师在分发药物之前检查了处方。 | druggist, chemist (BrE) |  | noun
