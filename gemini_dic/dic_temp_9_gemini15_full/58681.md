dump:/dʌmp/| n., vt.|倾倒；丢弃；倾销；沮丧；大量；垃圾堆；|to get rid of something unwanted, especially in a careless or hurried way; a place where waste is dumped; a state of depression or unhappiness.|例句：They dumped a load of rubbish by the river. 他们把一堆垃圾倒在河边。He dumped his girlfriend. 他甩了他的女朋友。|近义词：discard, throw away, dispose of, dejected, depressed|反义词：keep, retain, cherish, elated, cheerful|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dumps his trash every week. = 他每周都倒垃圾。
She dumps her old clothes at the charity shop. = 她把旧衣服捐给了慈善商店。


一般过去时:
They dumped the old furniture in the landfill. = 他们把旧家具扔到了垃圾填埋场。
He dumped the project due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，他放弃了这个项目。


现在完成时:
He has dumped all his old electronics. = 他已经处理掉了所有旧电子产品。
The company has dumped millions of gallons of wastewater. = 这家公司已经倾倒了数百万加仑的废水。


过去完成时:
They had dumped the evidence before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经销毁了证据。
She had dumped her boyfriend before she met him. = 在遇到他之前，她已经和前男友分手了。


将来时:
They will dump the toxic waste responsibly. = 他们将负责任地处理有毒废物。
He will dump his old car at the junkyard. = 他将把旧车丢弃在废车场。


虚拟语气:
If I had more space, I wouldn't dump things so quickly. = 如果我有更多空间，我就不会这么快地扔东西了。
If he hadn't dumped her, they might still be together. = 如果他没有甩了她，他们可能还会在一起。


被动语态:
The trash was dumped illegally in the forest. = 垃圾被非法倾倒在森林里。
The old building was dumped by the developers. = 这栋旧楼被开发商拆除了。


疑问句:
Where did they dump the body? = 他们把尸体扔到哪里去了？
Will they dump the project or continue? = 他们会放弃项目还是继续？


否定句:
They didn't dump the chemicals into the river. = 他们没有把化学品倒进河里。
She didn't dump him, he left her. = 不是她甩了他，是他离开了她。
