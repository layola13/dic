fissures:/ˈfɪʃərz/| n.|裂缝；裂隙；分歧|A fissure is a long, narrow crack or opening, especially one in the ground or in a rock.  It can also refer to a division or disagreement between people or groups.|例句：The earthquake caused deep fissures in the earth. 地震在地面上造成了深深的裂缝。
The growing fissures in the party threatened to split it apart.  党内日益加剧的分裂威胁到要将其分裂。|近义词：crack, cleft, rift, split, breach|反义词：union, unity, closure
