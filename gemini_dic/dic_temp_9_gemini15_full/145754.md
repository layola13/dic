overcultivating: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌltɪveɪtɪŋ/ | v. |过度耕作，竭泽而渔 |  The act of cultivating land excessively, leading to soil depletion and reduced fertility.  It often involves repeated planting of the same crop without proper soil management practices. | Example: Overcultivating the land led to soil erosion and decreased crop yields.  过度耕作导致土壤侵蚀和作物减产。|近义词：overfarming, exhausting, depleting | 反义词：conserving, preserving, restoring
