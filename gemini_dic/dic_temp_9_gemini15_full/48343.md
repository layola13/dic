decrumbing:/dɪˈkrʌmbɪŋ/| v.|去除面包屑；清除碎屑|The act of removing crumbs, typically from a surface or food.  It implies a careful and thorough process of cleaning.|例句：She spent the afternoon decrumbing the tablecloth after the party. = 她花了整个下午在派对后清理桌布上的面包屑。
例句：The baker carefully decrumbed the countertop before starting his next batch of bread. = 面包师在开始下一批面包之前仔细地清理了工作台上的面包屑。|近义词：crumb cleaning, cleaning, brushing|反义词：crumbing, scattering
