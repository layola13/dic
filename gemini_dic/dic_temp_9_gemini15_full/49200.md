delayers:/dɪˈleɪərz/| n.|耽搁者，延误者，拖延者|People or things that cause a delay.  Those who habitually postpone tasks or responsibilities.|例句：The constant meetings were the main delayers in the project.  不断的会议是项目延误的主要原因。

例句：Road construction and traffic accidents are common delayers during rush hour.  道路施工和交通事故是高峰时段常见的延误因素。


近义词：procrastinators, obstructers, hinderers


反义词：accelerators, facilitators


词性:名词
