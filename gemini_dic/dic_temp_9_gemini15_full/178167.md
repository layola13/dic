recollections:/ˌrekəˈlekʃənz/ | n. |回忆，回忆录 |The act or process of remembering something.  It can refer to specific memories or a collection of memories.  The plural form, "recollections," often implies a number of memories, perhaps forming a narrative or account. |例句：His recollections of childhood were filled with joy and laughter. = 他对童年的回忆充满了快乐和笑声。

His recollections of the war were vivid and detailed. = 他对战争的回忆生动而详细。


近义词：memories, reminiscences, recollections, memoirs |反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion

词性: 名词
