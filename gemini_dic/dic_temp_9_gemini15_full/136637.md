nonscholarly: /ˌnɑːnˈskɑːləli/ | adj. | 非学术的；通俗的 | Not relating to or connected with academic scholarship or study; popular or general rather than academic.  | Example:  The article offered a nonscholarly overview of the subject, focusing on practical applications rather than theoretical analysis. 这篇文章对该主题进行了非学术性的概述，重点是实际应用而不是理论分析。 | 近义词：unacademic, popular, lay, unprofessional | 反义词：scholarly, academic, erudite
