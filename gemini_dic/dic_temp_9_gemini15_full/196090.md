sepiment:/ˈsɛpɪmənt/| n. |沉积物；沉淀物 |Sediment is material that has been deposited by some natural process.  It is typically composed of minerals and organic matter.  Sediment can be found in various environments, such as rivers, lakes, and oceans.|例句：The riverbed was thick with sediment. 河床堆积着厚厚的沉积物。|例句：The sediment in the wine glass settled to the bottom.酒杯里的沉淀物沉到了底部。|近义词：deposit, precipitate, residue |反义词：suspension
