damodar:/ˈdɑːmədɑːr/| n. |达摩达尔（印度教神名）|Damodar is a name of Hindu origin, primarily associated with a form of the god Krishna. It's also used as a river name.  The name translates roughly to "giver of happiness" or "remover of sorrow."|The Damodar River flows through the states of Jharkhand and West Bengal in India.  Many people are named Damodar. |近义词：Krishna (in some contexts)|反义词：无
