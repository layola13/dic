self-valuation:/ˌsɛlfˌvæljuˈeɪʃən/| n.|自我评价，自我估价|The act of assessing one's own worth, abilities, or qualities.  It involves a critical examination of one's strengths and weaknesses.|例句：Regular self-valuation is crucial for personal growth. 定期进行自我评估对个人成长至关重要。
例句：Her self-valuation was surprisingly low despite her many achievements. 尽管她取得了很多成就，但她对自己的评价却出奇地低。|近义词：self-assessment, self-appraisal, introspection|反义词：external evaluation, objective assessment|名词
