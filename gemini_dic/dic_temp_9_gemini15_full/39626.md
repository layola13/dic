consequences:/ˈkɒnsɪkwɛnsɪz/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwɛnsɪz/ | n. |结果，后果 |The results or effects of an action or condition.  Consequences can be positive or negative, depending on the action that caused them. |例句：He had to face the consequences of his actions. 他必须承担自己行为的后果。
 
例句：The consequences of climate change are already being felt worldwide.  气候变化的后果已经在全世界范围内感受到。


近义词：effects, results, outcomes, repercussions, ramifications |反义词：causes, origins, sources


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The consequences of this decision are unclear. = 这个决定的后果尚不清楚。
What are the consequences of failing the exam? = 考试不及格的后果是什么？

一般过去时:
The consequences of the war were devastating. = 战争的后果是毁灭性的。
What were the consequences of their argument? = 他们争吵的后果是什么？

现在完成时:
The consequences of his actions have been far-reaching. = 他行为的后果影响深远。
What consequences have we faced due to the pandemic? = 我们因为疫情面临了哪些后果？

过去完成时:
The consequences of their earlier mistakes had already become apparent. = 他们先前错误的后果已经显现。
What consequences had they anticipated before making the investment? = 他们在投资之前预料到了什么后果？

将来时:
The consequences of this policy will be severe. = 这项政策的后果将十分严重。
What consequences will we face if we don't act now? = 如果我们现在不采取行动，我们将面临什么后果？

虚拟语气:
If he had thought about the consequences, he wouldn't have done it. = 如果他考虑过后果，他就不会那样做了。
I wish I had considered the consequences before I spoke. = 我希望我在说话之前考虑过后果。

被动语态:
The consequences were felt throughout the community. = 后果影响了整个社区。
The consequences are being carefully considered by the government. = 政府正在仔细考虑后果。

疑问句:
What are the consequences? = 后果是什么？
What will be the long-term consequences? = 长期后果将会是什么？

否定句:
There were no serious consequences. = 没有严重的后果。
He didn't consider the consequences of his actions. = 他没有考虑自己行为的后果。
