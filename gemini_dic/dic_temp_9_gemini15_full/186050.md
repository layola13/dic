reworn:/ˌriːˈwɔːrn/| adj.| 再穿的；旧的；穿旧的 |  Describes clothing or items that have been worn previously and are being worn again. It emphasizes the reuse of the item.  | Example:  She wore her reworn jeans to the garden party. 她穿着她旧牛仔裤去参加花园派对。 | 近义词：used, secondhand, pre-owned | 反义词：new, unworn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dress is reworn by many people. = 这件裙子被很多人再次穿。
That sweater is reworn every winter. = 那件毛衣每年冬天都会再穿。


一般过去时:
She reworn the dress to her sister's wedding. = 她把这件裙子又穿去她姐姐的婚礼了。
He reworn his favorite jacket. = 他再次穿上了他最喜欢的夹克。


现在完成时:
I have reworn this shirt several times. = 我已经多次再穿这件衬衫了。
She has reworn that beautiful gown for several special occasions. = 她已经把那件美丽的礼服穿了好几次参加特殊场合了。


过去完成时:
She had reworn that outfit before. = 她之前已经穿过那套衣服了。
They had reworn their costumes for the second performance. = 他们已经把戏服再次穿上准备第二次演出。


将来时:
I will reworn this coat next winter. = 我明年冬天会再穿这件外套。
She will reworn that dress to the anniversary party. = 她会把那件裙子再穿去周年纪念派对。


虚拟语气:
If I had a suitable dress, I would reworn it. = 如果我有一件合适的裙子，我会再穿它。
I wish I could reworn my favorite jeans, but they're too small now. = 我真希望我能再穿我最喜欢的牛仔裤，但是现在太小了。


被动语态:
The clothes were reworn by the actors. = 这些衣服被演员们再次穿上了。
The costumes will be reworn for the next show. = 戏服将会在下一场演出中再次穿上。


疑问句:
Has this dress been reworn before? = 这件裙子以前穿过吗？
Will that jacket be reworn again? = 那件夹克还会再穿吗？


否定句:
I haven't reworn that dress since last year. = 从去年以来我都没再穿过那件裙子了。
He didn't reworn his old shoes for the hike. = 他这次远足没有再穿他的旧鞋子。
