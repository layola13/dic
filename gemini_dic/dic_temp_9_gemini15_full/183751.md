rescinded:/rɪˈsɪnd/| vt.|撤销；废除；取消|To make void; annul; cancel.|The court rescinded the previous ruling. 法院撤销了之前的判决。  The company rescinded its offer of employment. 公司撤回了录用通知。|近义词：revoke, annul, cancel, repeal|反义词：affirm, ratify, confirm|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company rescinds contracts that violate its terms. = 公司撤销违反其条款的合同。
He rescinds his previous statement regularly. = 他经常撤回他之前的声明。


一般过去时:
The government rescinded the new tax law. = 政府撤销了新的税法。
She rescinded her application for the job. = 她撤回了她的求职申请。


现在完成时:
They have rescinded the order due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，他们已经撤销了该命令。
The university has rescinded his scholarship. = 大学已经取消了他的奖学金。


过去完成时:
The judge had rescinded the warrant before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，法官已经撤销了逮捕令。
They had rescinded their agreement before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经取消了他们的协议。


将来时:
The board will rescind the policy if the public outcry continues. = 如果公众的强烈抗议持续下去，董事会将撤销该政策。
He will rescind his offer if you don't accept it soon. = 如果你不尽快接受，他将撤回他的提议。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were presented, the court would rescind the verdict. = 如果证据被提交，法院将撤销判决。
I wish the company had not rescinded my job offer. = 我真希望公司没有撤销我的工作邀请。


被动语态:
The contract was rescinded by mutual agreement. = 合同是双方协商一致撤销的。
The decision was rescinded after public protest. = 在公众抗议之后，该决定被撤销了。


疑问句:
Will the contract be rescinded? = 合同会被撤销吗？
Has the order been rescinded? = 命令已经被撤销了吗？


否定句:
The company did not rescind the contract. = 公司没有撤销合同。
They will not rescind their decision easily. = 他们不会轻易改变他们的决定。


PHRASE->
