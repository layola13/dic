northerly:/ˈnɔːrðərli/| adj.|北方的；向北的；来自北方的|  Going in a direction towards the north.  It can also describe something originating from the north or having a northerly characteristic. | 例句：The wind is blowing northerly today. = 今天风从北方吹来。

The ship sailed on a northerly course. = 那艘船向北航行。|近义词：northern, northward|反义词：southerly, southern
