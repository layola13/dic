mispronounce:/ˌmɪsprəˈnaʊns/ | vt. |发音错误，读错|To pronounce (a word or words) incorrectly.|He mispronounced the word "epistemology". 他把“认识论”这个词读错了。|近义词：pronounce wrongly, butcher, mangle |反义词：pronounce correctly
