gracies:/ˈɡreɪsiːz/| n.|谢谢，感谢|Gracies is a less formal and more playful way of saying "thank you" in English, particularly common in certain dialects or informal settings. It's often used as a shortened version of "gracias" (Spanish for "thank you").  |Example: "Gracies for the help!" = "谢谢你的帮助！" |近义词：thanks, thank you, cheers|反义词：None.
