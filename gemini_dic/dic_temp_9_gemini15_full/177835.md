recentralized: /ˌriːˈsentrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 再集中化；重新集中 |  Describes a process or system that has been centralized again after a period of decentralization. It implies a return to a more concentrated or unified structure or control. |  例句：The company recentralized its operations after realizing the inefficiencies of its decentralized structure. 公司在意识到分散结构的低效率后，重新集中了业务运营。


例句：The government recentralized control over education, reversing years of regional autonomy. 政府重新集中了对教育的控制权，扭转了多年来的区域自治。


近义词：reconsolidated, reunified, centralized


反义词：decentralized, dispersed, distributed


词性：形容词
