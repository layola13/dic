self-proclaimed: /ˌsɛlf prəˈkleɪmd/ | adj. | 自封的，自称的 |  Describes someone who has given themselves a title or position without official recognition.  It often implies a lack of legitimate authority or credibility. | 例句：He's a self-proclaimed expert on ancient civilizations, but his claims lack evidence. 他自称是古代文明专家，但他的说法缺乏证据。


He is a self-proclaimed genius. 他自称是天才。


近义词：self-styled, self-appointed, self-designated | 反义词：officially recognized, accredited, certified | 词性：形容词


USAGE->

The artist is a self-proclaimed master of his craft. =这位艺术家自称是技艺大师。

一般过去时:
