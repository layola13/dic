homoeopathy:/ˌhoʊmiˈɒpəθi/| n. |顺势疗法|A system of alternative medicine that treats diseases by using highly diluted substances, which would produce similar symptoms in a healthy person if given in larger doses.  It is based on the principle of "like cures like".|  Many people believe in the effectiveness of homeopathy, while others are highly skeptical. |alternative medicine, complementary medicine|conventional medicine, allopathy
