outsells:/ˌaʊtˈsɛlz/| vt.|胜过…的销量，比…卖得好 |To sell more of a product than another product or company.  |例句：This new model outsells all previous versions.这款新车型销量超过了之前的全部版本。|例句：Our company's product outsells the competitor's by a significant margin. 我们公司的产品销量显著超过竞争对手。|近义词：outsold, surpasses, exceeds |反义词：undersells


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That new phone outsells its predecessor. = 那款新手机的销量超过了它的前代产品。
This brand consistently outsells its rivals. = 这个品牌始终比竞争对手卖得好。


一般过去时:
Last year, our product outsells all competitors. = 去年，我们的产品销量超过所有竞争对手。
The book outsells all expectations. = 这本书的销量超出了所有预期。


现在完成时:
This product has outsells all others in the market for three consecutive years. = 这款产品连续三年销量领先市场上的其他产品。
The company has outsells its competitors for the past decade. = 这家公司在过去十年里一直比竞争对手卖得好。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the new game had outsells its predecessor. = 到年底，这款新游戏已经超过了其前作的销量。
Before the new marketing campaign, the product had outsells the other product in the same category. = 新营销活动开展之前，该产品的销量已超过同类其他产品。


将来时:
Analysts predict that the new car will outsells its competitors next year. = 分析师预测，这款新车明年将超过竞争对手的销量。
We expect that our new product line will outsells the current one. = 我们预计我们的新产品线将超过目前的销量。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested more in marketing, we might have outsells our competitors. = 如果我们对市场营销投入更多资金，我们本可以超过竞争对手的销量。
If the price were lower, the product would outsells its rivals. = 如果价格更低，该产品的销量将超过竞争对手。


被动语态:
Our product was outsells by a new competitor. = 我们的产品被一个新的竞争对手超过了销量。
The older model is being outsells by the newer one. = 旧款车型正在被新款车型超过销量。


疑问句:
Does this product outsells its competitors? = 这款产品销量超过竞争对手吗？
How many products outsells this one? = 有多少产品销量超过这款？


否定句:
Our product doesn't outsells the competitor's product. = 我们的产品销量没有超过竞争对手的产品。
Despite the marketing campaign, the new product didn't outsells the old one. = 尽管进行了营销活动，但新产品的销量并没有超过旧产品。


PHRASE->
outsells the competition = 销量超过竞争对手
consistently outsells = 一直比...卖得好
significantly outsells = 显著地比...卖得好
outsells by a large margin = 以很大的优势胜过...的销量
outsells expectations = 超出预期销量
