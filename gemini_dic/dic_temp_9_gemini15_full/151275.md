pareto:/ˈpærɪtoʊ/| n. |帕累托|The Pareto principle, also known as the 80/20 rule, is an observation that roughly 80% of effects come from 20% of causes.| 例句：The Pareto principle suggests that 80% of your sales come from 20% of your customers. 帕累托原则表明，你80%的销售额来自20%的客户。|近义词：80/20 rule, vital few|反义词：N/A|名词
