skype:/skaɪp/ | n. |Skype（网络电话软件）|A software application that allows users to make voice and video calls over the Internet, as well as send instant messages and files.|I use Skype to video chat with my family overseas. 我用Skype与海外的家人视频聊天。|近义词：messenger, FaceTime, WhatsApp|反义词：无


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I Skype with my friends every week. = 我每周都和朋友用Skype聊天。
We Skype our family regularly. = 我们定期用Skype和家人联系。


一般过去时:
I Skyped my boss yesterday. = 我昨天用Skype联系了我的老板。
They Skyped for hours last night. = 他们昨晚用Skype聊了几个小时。


现在完成时:
I have Skyped with him several times this month. = 这个月我已经和他用Skype聊过几次了。
She has Skyped her parents every Sunday since moving away. = 自从搬走后，她每个星期天都用Skype和父母联系。


过去完成时:
I had Skyped him before he called me. = 我在他打电话给我之前已经用Skype和他联系过了。
They had Skyped for an hour before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经用Skype聊了一个小时。


将来时:
I will Skype you later tonight. = 我今晚晚些时候会用Skype联系你。
We will Skype the client tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上会用Skype联系客户。


虚拟语气:
If I had Skype, I would call my grandmother. = 如果我有Skype，我会打电话给我奶奶。
I wish I could Skype with my friends right now. = 我真希望现在能用Skype和朋友们聊天。


被动语态:
Skype is used by millions of people worldwide. = Skype被全球数百万人使用。
The meeting was Skyped instead of held in person. = 会议是用Skype进行的，而不是当面举行。


疑问句:
Do you Skype often? = 你经常用Skype吗？
Did they Skype before the presentation? = 他们在演示之前用Skype联系过吗？


否定句:
I don't Skype very often. = 我不经常用Skype。
They didn't Skype last night. = 他们昨晚没有用Skype联系。
