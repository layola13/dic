paver:/ˈpeɪvər/| n. v.|铺路者；铺路机；铺路石|A paver is a person or machine that lays paving stones or bricks.  It can also refer to the paving stones or bricks themselves. |例句：The paver worked tirelessly to finish the driveway. 铺路工人辛勤工作以完成车道铺设。The new pavers on the sidewalk are a beautiful shade of grey. 人行道上的新铺路石是美丽的灰色。|近义词：layer, installer, roadmaker|反义词：remover, destroyer
