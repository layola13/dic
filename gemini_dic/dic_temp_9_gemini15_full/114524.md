malak:/ˈmælæk/| n. |天使，使者；(伊斯兰教)天使长|The word "malak" (ملاك) is Arabic, meaning "angel" or "messenger." In Islam, it often refers to a high-ranking angel, sometimes even an archangel.  The plural is "malakāt" (ملائكة).|例句：In Islamic tradition, Jibril (Gabriel) is a powerful malak.  在伊斯兰教传统中，吉布里尔（加百列）是一位强大的天使。|例句：Many malakāt are believed to carry out God's will.  许多天使被认为是执行上帝旨意的。|近义词：angel, messenger, seraph|反义词：demon, devil|名词
