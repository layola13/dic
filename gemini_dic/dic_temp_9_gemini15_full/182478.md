renouncer:/rɪˈnaʊnsər/ | n. |放弃者；抛弃者； отрекающийся |One who renounces something, typically a belief, claim, or right.  A person who formally gives up a right, title, claim, etc. |例句：He became a renouncer of worldly possessions after his spiritual awakening. 他在灵性觉醒后成为一个放弃尘世财产的人。  The renouncer of the throne lived a peaceful life in the countryside.  放弃王位的王子在乡下过着平静的生活。|近义词：abandoner, relinquisher, repudiator |反义词：supporter, claimant, adherent |名词
