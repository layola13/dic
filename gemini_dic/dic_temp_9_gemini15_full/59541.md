eating:/ˈiːtɪŋ/ | v. | 吃；正在吃 | The act of consuming food.  It can also refer to the process of slowly destroying something. | 例句：I enjoy eating sushi. = 我喜欢吃寿司。

He's eating a big piece of cake. = 他正在吃一块大蛋糕。


近义词：consuming, devouring, munching | 反义词：starving, fasting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I eat breakfast every morning. = 我每天早上吃早餐。
She eats a lot of vegetables. = 她吃很多蔬菜。

一般过去时:
We ate dinner at a fancy restaurant. = 我们在一家高档餐厅吃了晚饭。
He ate all the cookies. = 他把所有的饼干都吃掉了。

现在完成时:
I have eaten all the pizza. = 我已经把所有的披萨都吃掉了。
They have eaten lunch already. = 他们已经吃过午饭了。

过去完成时:
I had eaten before he arrived. = 他来之前我已经吃过了。
She had eaten a large meal earlier. = 她早些时候吃了一顿丰盛的饭。

将来时:
I will eat dinner with my family tonight. = 我今晚将和家人一起吃晚饭。
We will be eating at a new restaurant. = 我们将在一家新餐厅吃饭。

虚拟语气:
If I ate less, I would be healthier. = 如果我少吃点，我会更健康。
I wish I had eaten something before the movie. = 我真希望在看电影之前吃点东西。

被动语态:
The cake was eaten quickly. = 蛋糕很快就被吃掉了。
All the food was eaten at the party. = 派对上的所有食物都被吃光了。

疑问句:
Are you eating dinner now? = 你现在在吃晚饭吗？
What are you eating? = 你在吃什么？

否定句:
I am not eating anything right now. = 我现在什么都没吃。
She didn't eat breakfast this morning. = 她今天早上没吃早餐。


PHRASE->
eating habits =  饮食习惯
eating disorder =  饮食紊乱
eating out =  在外吃饭
eating well =  吃得好
finish eating =  吃完
