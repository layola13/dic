immovables:/ɪˈmuːvəbəlz/| n. |不动产，固定资产|  Things that cannot be moved, especially land and buildings.  This term is often used in legal contexts, referring to property that is permanently affixed to the land.|例句：The lawyer specialized in immovables, handling property disputes and land transfers.  律师专门处理不动产，负责处理财产纠纷和土地转让。

例句：The family's immovables included a large estate and several commercial buildings.  这个家庭的不动产包括一个大型庄园和几座商业建筑。


近义词：real estate, realty, property

反义词：movables, chattels
