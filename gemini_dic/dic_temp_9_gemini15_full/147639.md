overpolices:/ˌoʊvərˈpoʊlɪsɪz/ | v. |过度管制，警察管辖过度 | To police excessively or to a degree considered excessive or oppressive.  This often implies an intrusive or disproportionate level of policing, potentially leading to negative consequences. | 例句：The city is accused of overpolicing low-income neighborhoods. = 这座城市被指控过度管辖低收入社区。 The heavy-handed approach to security overpolices the event and alienates attendees. = 这种强硬的安全措施过度管制了活动，并疏远了与会者。 | 近义词：overregulate, overcontrol, oppress | 反义词：underpolice, neglect
