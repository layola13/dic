chinwagged:/ˈtʃɪnwæɡd/| v.|闲聊，聊天 |To have a casual conversation; to chat.|例句：We chinwagged for hours about old times. 我们聊了好几个小时的往事。|近义词：chatted, gossiped, conversed|反义词： remained silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chinwag regularly about the latest gossip. = 他们经常闲聊最新的八卦。
We chinwag whenever we meet. = 我们每次见面都会聊天。


一般过去时:
We chinwagged about our childhood memories last night. = 我们昨晚聊了聊童年回忆。
They chinwagged for hours at the party. = 他们在派对上聊了好几个小时。


现在完成时:
I have chinwagged with her several times this week. = 这周我已经和她聊过几次了。
They have chinwagged about the project all morning. = 他们整个上午都在讨论这个项目。


过去完成时:
We had chinwagged before the meeting started. = 会议开始前我们已经聊过了。
They had chinwagged about the issue for days before reaching a decision. = 他们在做出决定之前已经讨论这个问题好几天了。


将来时:
We will chinwag after the meeting. = 会议结束后我们会聊聊。
They will chinwag about the plans for the weekend. = 他们会聊聊周末的计划。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would chinwag longer. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会聊更久。
I wish we could chinwag more often. = 我希望我们能更经常地聊天。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Did they chinwag much at the party? = 他们在派对上聊得很多吗？
Have you ever chinwagged with the boss? = 你有没有和老板聊过？


否定句:
We didn't chinwag much yesterday. = 我们昨天聊得不多。
They haven't chinwagged about the problem yet. = 他们还没讨论过这个问题。


PHRASE->
chinwag about something: 闲聊某事
have a chinwag: 闲聊一会儿
chinwag with someone: 和某人聊天
a good chinwag: 一次愉快的聊天
a long chinwag: 一次长时间的聊天
