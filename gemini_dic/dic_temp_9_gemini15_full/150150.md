pan-fries: /ˈpæn fraɪz/ | v. | 煎锅煎，平底锅煎 | To cook (food) in a frying pan, typically with a small amount of fat or oil.  |  例句：I pan-fried the chicken breasts until they were golden brown. 我用平底锅煎鸡胸肉直到金黄。

例句：She pan-fried some vegetables with garlic and herbs. 她用大蒜和香草煎了一些蔬菜。


近义词：fry, sauté, stir-fry | 反义词：boil, bake, steam | 词性：动词
