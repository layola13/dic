commercialize:/ˈkɔːmərsəlʌɪz/|/ˈkɑːmɜːrʃəlaɪz/| vt.|商业化；使商品化 |To adapt (something) for sale or use in commerce; to exploit commercially.  |例句：The company is looking to commercialize its new technology.  公司正寻求将其新技术商业化。|例句：They commercialized the process and made a fortune. 他们将这一流程商业化并赚了一大笔钱。|近义词：market, monetize, exploit |反义词：decommercialize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They commercialize their inventions. = 他们将他们的发明商业化。
The company commercializes new products every year. = 这家公司每年都会商业化新产品。


一般过去时:
The university commercialized its research findings. = 这所大学将其研究成果商业化。
They commercialized the software and sold it worldwide. = 他们将该软件商业化并销往全球。


现在完成时:
They have commercialized several innovative products. = 他们已经将几种创新产品商业化。
The company has commercialized its technology successfully. = 该公司已成功将其技术商业化。


过去完成时:
The company had already commercialized the product before the competitor did. = 在竞争对手之前，该公司已经将该产品商业化了。
They had commercialized the idea before they realized its full potential. = 在他们意识到其全部潜力之前，他们已经将这个想法商业化了。


将来时:
They will commercialize the drug next year. = 他们将在明年将这种药物商业化。
The startup will commercialize its AI platform soon. = 这家初创公司将很快将其AI平台商业化。


虚拟语气:
If they commercialized their research, they would make a profit. = 如果他们将他们的研究商业化，他们将获利。
It would be wise if they commercialized their new technology. = 如果他们将他们的新技术商业化将是明智之举。


被动语态:
The technology was commercialized by a large corporation. = 这项技术被一家大公司商业化了。
The new product is being commercialized aggressively. = 新产品正在积极商业化。


疑问句:
Will the company commercialize its invention? = 公司会将其发明商业化吗？
When will the new drug be commercialized? = 新药何时会商业化？


否定句:
They did not commercialize the software. = 他们没有将该软件商业化。
The company has not yet commercialized its research. = 该公司尚未将其研究成果商业化。


PHRASE->
commercialize a product = 将产品商业化
commercialize an idea = 将想法商业化
commercialize technology = 将技术商业化
commercialize research = 将研究成果商业化
commercialize a process = 将流程商业化


词源:
commercial + -ize (动词后缀)
commercial 源自commercialize的词根，意思是“商业的”，最终源自拉丁语"commercium" (trade, commerce)。 "-ize" 是一个常见的动词后缀，表示“使成为……；使……化”。
