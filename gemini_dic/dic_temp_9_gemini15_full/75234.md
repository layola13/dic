frolickers:/ˈfrɒlɪkərz/| n. |嬉戏的人，玩耍的人|People who are playfully active; those engaging in frolicking.  The word refers to a group of individuals enjoying themselves in a lively and carefree manner. |例句：The frolickers on the beach were enjoying the sunshine and waves. 海滩上嬉戏的人们享受着阳光和海浪。The children, a group of joyous frolickers, ran and laughed in the park. 孩子们，一群快乐的嬉戏者，在公园里跑跑跳跳，欢声笑语。|近义词：players, merrymakers, revellers|反义词：gloomers, mourners
