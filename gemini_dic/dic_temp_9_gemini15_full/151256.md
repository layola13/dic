parenthetically:/ˌpærənˈθɛtɪkli/| adv.|  括号地；插入地；补充地 |  In a parenthetical manner; as a parenthesis; by way of parenthesis.  It adds information that is not essential to the main point but provides extra detail or clarification.  | 例句：He mentioned (parenthetically) that he'd be late. 他(顺便提一句)提到他会迟到。  |近义词：incidentally, incidentally, aside, by the way | 反义词：directly, explicitly

例句：

The author parenthetically noted the historical context of the event. 作者(顺便)提到了事件的历史背景。

She parenthetically added a comment about the weather. 她(补充一句)评论了一下天气。
