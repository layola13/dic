nominations:/ˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |提名，推荐 |The act of nominating someone or something for an award, position, or other honor.  It refers to the process of suggesting candidates or choices for a particular role or recognition. |例句：The nominations for the Academy Awards were announced last night.  昨晚公布了奥斯卡奖的提名名单。

例句：The committee considered all the nominations before making a final decision. 委员会在做出最终决定之前考虑了所有提名。


近义词：appointments, selections, proposals |反义词：rejections
