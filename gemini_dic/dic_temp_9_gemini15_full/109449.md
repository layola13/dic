The word implies a harsh and unfair treatment, similar to that historically inflicted upon people with leprosy.|例句：The community leperized him after he was wrongly accused of a crime. 社区在他被错误地指控犯下罪行后将他排斥在外。
例句：His controversial views leperized him from mainstream society. 他有争议的观点使他被主流社会排斥。|近义词：ostracize, shun, isolate, exclude|反义词：include, accept, welcome|动词
