nonrepresentative: /ˌnɒnˌreprɛzˈɛntətɪv/ | adj. | 不具有代表性的，非典型的 | Not representative; not typical or characteristic of a larger group or class.  It implies a lack of accuracy in reflecting the whole. |

例句：

The sample was nonrepresentative of the population as a whole. = 这个样本并不能代表整个人群。

This case is nonrepresentative of the typical customer experience. = 这个案例不能代表典型的客户体验。


近义词：untypical, atypical, uncharacteristic, anomalous, exceptional

反义词：representative, typical, characteristic

词性: adjective
