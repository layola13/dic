pancultural:/ˌpænˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj.|泛文化的；跨文化的；| Relating to or encompassing many or all cultures.  It suggests a perspective that transcends individual cultural boundaries and seeks commonalities or universals across different cultures.|Example: The research employed a pancultural approach, comparing data from diverse societies.  这项研究采用了泛文化的方法，比较了来自不同社会的数据。|近义词：cross-cultural, intercultural, universal|反义词：culture-specific, ethnocentric
