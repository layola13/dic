furtherer: /ˈfɜːðərər/ | n. | 促进者；推进者 | A person or thing that furthers something.  Someone who promotes or advances a cause, project, or idea. |  He was a staunch furtherer of women's rights. 他是妇女权利的坚定支持者。 The new software is a furtherer of efficiency.  这个新软件提高了效率。 |近义词：promoter, advocate, supporter, champion | 反义词：opponent, adversary, obstructer

词源:

The word "furtherer" is derived from the verb "further," which ultimately comes from the Old English word "fyrþran," meaning "to advance" or "to progress."  The "-er" suffix is a common agentive suffix in English, indicating a person or thing that performs the action of the verb.
