repopulating: /ˌriːˈpɒpjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新填充；使人口重新稠密|  The act of re-establishing a population in an area that has been depleted or deserted. This involves bringing in new inhabitants or encouraging the growth of an existing population.  |

例句：

The government is investing heavily in repopulating the rural areas. = 政府正在大力投资农村地区的人口重新稠密。

Efforts are underway to repopulate the decimated wildlife sanctuary. = 人们正在努力使被摧毁的野生动物保护区重新充满生机。


近义词：replenishing, restocking, recolonizing

反义词：depopulating, evacuating, depleting
