pistole:/pɪˈstɒl/|/ˈpɪstəl/| n. |小手枪；西班牙金币|A small handgun; formerly, a Spanish gold coin.  The word refers to both a type of firearm and a former Spanish gold coin.  The firearm's name derives from the coin's shape. |例句：He pulled out a pistole and pointed it at the intruder. 他掏出一把手枪指着入侵者。  The old chest contained several pistoles.  那个旧箱子里装着几枚西班牙金币。|近义词：handgun, revolver, pistol |反义词：无
