puttyroot:/ˈpʌtiruːt/| n. |芋头属植物|A plant of the genus *Arisema*, having a corm resembling putty.  These plants are known for their distinctive, often oddly shaped, leaves and inflorescences (flower structures).  Some species are also valued for their medicinal properties. |Example: The puttyroot's corm was carefully dug up for medicinal use.  芋头的块茎被小心地挖出来用于药用。|近义词：Indian turnip (for some species)|反义词：无|名词
