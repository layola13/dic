shaved:/ʃeɪvd/ | v. | 刮（胡子）；剃（毛发） | To remove hair from (a part of the body) using a razor or other sharp instrument.  | He shaved his beard this morning. 他今天早上刮了胡子。  She shaved her legs before going to the beach. 她去海滩前刮了腿毛。 | trimmed, clipped | grew, cultivated | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He shaves every morning. = 他每天早上都刮胡子。
She shaves her legs twice a week. = 她一周刮两次腿毛。

一般过去时:
He shaved his head yesterday. = 他昨天剃了头。
She shaved off her eyebrows. = 她剃掉了眉毛。

现在完成时:
I have shaved my head for the charity event. = 为了慈善活动我已经剃了头。
He has shaved his beard many times. = 他刮过很多次胡子了。

过去完成时:
He had shaved before he went to the party. = 他去参加派对之前已经刮过胡子了。
She had shaved her legs before getting into the pool. = 她下水之前已经刮过腿毛了。

将来时:
I will shave tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会刮胡子。
He will shave his beard before the wedding. = 他会在婚礼前刮胡子。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would shave. = 如果我有时间，我会刮胡子。
I wish I had shaved before going out. = 我真希望出门前刮过胡子。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
