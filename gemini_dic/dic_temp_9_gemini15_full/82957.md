gunfires: /ˈɡʌnfaɪrz/ | n. | 枪声；炮火 | The sound of gunfire indicates a dangerous situation. 枪声表明情况危险。|近义词：shots, explosions, barrage |反义词:silence, peace

Gunfire often accompanies violent conflicts. = 枪声常常伴随着暴力冲突。
The soldiers hear constant gunfire throughout the night. = 士兵整夜都听到持续不断的枪声。
