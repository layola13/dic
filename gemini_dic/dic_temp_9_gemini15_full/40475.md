contribution: /ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃən/ /ˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːʃən/ | n. | 贡献；捐款；投稿 |

英文解析:Something that is contributed; a gift or payment.  It can refer to an individual's efforts, a monetary donation, or a piece of writing submitted for publication.

例句:

His contribution to the project was invaluable.  他的项目贡献非常宝贵。

She made a generous contribution to the charity. 她慷慨地捐款给慈善机构。


近义词: donation, offering, contribution, contribution, input, addition


反义词: hindrance, obstruction, detriment


词性: noun
