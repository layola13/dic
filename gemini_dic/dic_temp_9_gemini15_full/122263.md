mithril:/ˈmɪθrɪl/| n. |精金，秘银|A fictional, silvery-grey, exceptionally strong and light metal appearing in J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium. It is prized for its strength, lightness, and resistance to corrosion.|Example: The dwarves mined mithril in the depths of the mountains.|近义词:adamant,  (in a fictional context)|反义词: (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with weaker metals like lead or iron)|名词
