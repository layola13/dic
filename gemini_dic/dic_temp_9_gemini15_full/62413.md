endognath:/ˈɛndoʊnæθ/| adj.|内颚的；内生的|Relating to or denoting animals whose mandibles are not externally visible, as in insects.  It refers to the condition where the mandibles are enclosed within the mouthparts.|例句：Many insects are endognathous, meaning their mandibles are hidden within their mouthparts. 许多昆虫是内颚的，这意味着它们的 mandibles 隐藏在口器内。  The study focused on the endognathous characteristics of certain beetle species.  这项研究集中在某些甲虫物种的内颚特征上。|近义词：internal-jawed|反义词：exognathous
