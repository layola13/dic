quasi-powerfully: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈpaʊərfəli/ | adv. | 准强有力地，近乎强有力地 |  The word combines "quasi," meaning "seemingly," "apparently," or "to a certain extent," with "powerfully," indicating strength or effectiveness.  It describes something that possesses a degree of power or effectiveness, but not to a full or complete extent. It suggests a partial or incomplete manifestation of power. | 例句：The quasi-powerfully built machine struggled to lift the heavy load.  这台构造近乎强劲的机器费力地举起了重物。


The argument, while quasi-powerfully presented, lacked sufficient evidence. 尽管论证看起来很有力，但缺乏足够的证据。 | 近义词：somewhat powerfully, relatively powerfully, moderately powerfully | 反义词：weakly, ineffectively, feebly | 词性: 副词
