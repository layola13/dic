pitheads:/ˈpɪtˌhedz/| n.|矿井井口；矿井口建筑物|The structures at the top of a mine shaft, including buildings and equipment.  These structures often housed facilities for winding up coal or other materials, as well as changing rooms and offices for miners.|例句：The pitheads were silhouetted against the stormy sky. 矿井井口在暴风雨的天空中构成剪影。


例句：The miners gathered at the pitheads before their shift.  矿工们在轮班前聚集在矿井井口。
