osmose:/ɒzˈməʊz/| /ɑːzˈmoʊz/| n. |渗透；渗透作用|The process by which molecules of a solvent tend to pass through a semipermeable membrane from a less concentrated solution into a more concentrated one, thus equalizing the concentrations on each side of the membrane.  This process is crucial in many biological systems.|例句：Water osmosis is essential for plant growth.  水渗透作用对植物生长至关重要。|例句：Reverse osmosis is used to purify water. 反渗透法用于净化水。|近义词：diffusion, permeation|反义词：active transport|名词
