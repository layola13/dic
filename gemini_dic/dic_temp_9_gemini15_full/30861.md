chads:/tʃædz/| n.|碎屑；废弃物；(尤指选举中)无效选票|Small pieces of something, especially waste material; specifically, in elections, ballot papers that are spoiled or rejected.|例句：The floor was covered in chads after the demolition.  拆迁后地板上到处都是碎屑。|例句：The election recount involved examining thousands of disputed chads.  这次选举重新计票包括检查数千张有争议的无效选票。|近义词：fragments, debris, scraps|反义词：whole, intact
