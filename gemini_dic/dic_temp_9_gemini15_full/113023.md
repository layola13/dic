lumens:/ˈluːmɛnz/| n. |流明（光通量单位）|A lumen (symbol: lm) is the SI derived unit of luminous flux, a measure of the total amount of visible light emitted by a source.  It's a measure of perceived power, weighted to match the sensitivity of the human eye.  |The lamp has a luminous flux of 1000 lumens.|近义词：flux (in the context of light)|反义词：无
