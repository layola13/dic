entropically:/ˌɛntrɒˈpɪkəli/ | adv. | 熵地；依据熵的原理 | Adverb describing something that happens or is done in accordance with the principles of entropy.  It refers to a process or state characterized by an increase in disorder or randomness. | The system evolved entropically, gradually increasing its disorder.  系统熵增地演化，逐渐增加了无序性。 |  randomly, chaotically, spontaneously |  (No direct antonym, but opposites would describe processes decreasing entropy, such as highly ordered systems) | 副词
