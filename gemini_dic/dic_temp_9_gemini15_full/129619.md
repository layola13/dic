non-conscious:/ˌnɒnˈkɒnʃəs/| adj. |无意识的，下意识的 |Not involving or done with conscious thought or awareness.  It refers to mental processes that occur outside of conscious awareness. |例句：Many bodily functions, such as breathing, are non-conscious.  许多身体机能，例如呼吸，都是无意识的。

例句：He made a non-conscious gesture of annoyance. 他无意识地做了一个表示恼怒的动作。

近义词：unconscious, subconscious, involuntary | 反义词：conscious, deliberate, intentional | 词性:形容词
