/ˈnɛmətoʊ/| n. |线虫；线虫动物|A nematode is a type of worm belonging to the phylum Nematoda.  They are mostly microscopic and live in a wide range of environments, including soil, water, and the bodies of plants and animals. Many are free-living, but some are parasitic.|Example:  The soil sample contained a high number of nematodes.|近义词：roundworm|反义词：无
