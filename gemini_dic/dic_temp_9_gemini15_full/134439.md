nonirrigated: /ˌnɒnˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未灌溉的；旱地的 | Not supplied with water artificially.  This adjective describes land or crops that are not watered artificially, relying solely on natural rainfall.  |

例句：

The nonirrigated fields suffered greatly during the drought. = 旱地在大旱期间遭受了巨大的损失。

Many crops in this region are nonirrigated, relying entirely on monsoon rains. = 该地区的许多农作物都是旱地种植的，完全依靠季风降雨。


近义词：unirrigated, dry-farmed

反义词：irrigated


词性：形容词
