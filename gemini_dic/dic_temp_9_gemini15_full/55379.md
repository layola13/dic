distemperate:/dɪsˈtɛmpərɪt/ | adj. |性情暴躁的；不温和的；无节制的；过度的|  lacking moderation or self-control; excessive or extreme.  It describes a person or their behavior as being prone to outbursts of anger, lacking self-control, or exhibiting extreme behavior.|

例句：He had a distemperate outburst of anger. 他勃然大怒。

例句：Her distemperate spending habits led to financial ruin. 她无节制的消费习惯导致了经济破产。


近义词：irritable, intemperate, passionate, uncontrolled, immoderate

反义词：calm, temperate, moderate, controlled, restrained


词源：来自dis- (否定前缀) + temperate (温和的，有节制的)，字面意思是“非温和的”。  The root "temper" comes from the Latin "temperare," meaning "to mix in due proportions,"  implying balance and moderation.  The prefix "dis-" negates this meaning.
