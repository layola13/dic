covering:/ˈkʌvərɪŋ/ | n., v. |覆盖物；覆盖；报道 |  covering refers to something that covers or protects something else, or the act of covering something. As a verb, it means to place something over or on top of something else to conceal, protect, or decorate it. |例句：The snow provided a thick covering for the ground.  积雪为地面提供了一层厚厚的覆盖物。


The reporter is covering the election.  记者正在报道选举。|近义词：covering (n.):  blanket, sheet, veil; covering (v.): concealing, enveloping, shielding |反义词：uncovering, exposing, revealing | 词性:Noun, Verb
