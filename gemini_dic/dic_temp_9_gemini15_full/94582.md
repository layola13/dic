impracticabilities: /ˌɪmpræktɪkəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不切实际性；不可行性 | The inherent difficulties or impossibilities of something; the state of being impractical.  |  例句：The sheer impractability of the plan led to its abandonment.  这项计划的不切实际性导致其被放弃。

例句：Despite its many benefits, the high cost of the project presented significant impractabilities. 尽管它有很多好处，但这个项目的高成本带来了重大的不可行性。


近义词：infeasibilities, impossibilities, difficulties, challenges


反义词：feasibilities, possibilities, practicality


词性：名词
