scavenges:/ˈskævɪndʒɪz/| v.| 觅食；拾荒；清理；搜索 | To search for and collect discarded or usable items.  |例句：The birds scavenged for food in the rubbish bins. 鸟儿在垃圾桶里觅食。

例句：The dog scavenged the leftovers from the dinner table. 狗把餐桌上剩下的食物都吃掉了。


近义词：forage, rummage, search, hunt, glean |反义词：discard, throw away, waste


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds scavenge for food every day. = 鸟儿每天都在觅食。
He scavenges discarded materials for his art projects. 他搜集废弃材料用于他的艺术创作。

一般过去时:
They scavenged for scraps after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，他们四处寻找残羹剩饭。
The animals scavenged the remains of the hunt. 动物们清理了狩猎剩下的残骸。

The family has scavenged enough food to last the winter. = 这家人已经收集了足够的食物来过冬。
She has scavenged many interesting items at flea markets.她在跳蚤市场上淘到了许多有趣的东西。


过去完成时:
The wolves had scavenged the carcass before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，狼群已经吃掉了尸体。
They had scavenged the battlefield for supplies.他们在战场上搜寻补给。


将来时:
