nosed:/noʊzd/| v.|用鼻子碰；嗅到；探查；|to touch, push, or smell something with one's nose; to investigate or search for something.|例句：The dog nosed around the bushes.  狗在灌木丛周围嗅来嗅去。
例句：She nosed her way through the crowd. 她挤过人群。|近义词：sniff, nudge, investigate |反义词：ignore, overlook
