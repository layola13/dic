measures: /ˈmɛʒərz/ | n. v. | 措施；方法；尺寸；量度 |  To determine the size, amount, or degree of something; steps taken to achieve a particular end. | 近义词：steps, actions, dimensions, calculations | 反义词：chaos, disorder


n. 措施；方法；尺寸；量度
The government is taking measures to reduce unemployment. 政府正在采取措施以减少失业。

The dimensions of the room are 10 meters by 5 meters. 这个房间的尺寸是10米乘5米。

We need to take accurate measures of the land. 我们需要对土地进行精确测量。


v. 量度；衡量；判定
The tailor measured me for a new suit.  裁缝为我量体裁衣做新西装。

The success of the project will be measured by its impact on the community.  项目的成功将以其对社区的影响来衡量。

I measured the ingredients carefully before I started baking. 在开始烘焙之前，我仔细测量了配料。
