extrapolatory: /ɪkˈstræpəleɪtəri/ | adj. | 推断的；推测的 | Relating to or involving extrapolation; based on extrapolation.  It describes something that involves extending known information beyond its original range, often to predict future trends or values. |

例句：

The extrapolatory analysis of the data suggested a continued increase in sales.  推断性数据分析表明销售额将继续增长。

His extrapolatory remarks about the economic future were met with skepticism. 他关于经济未来的推测性言论遭到了怀疑。


近义词：predictive, projective, inferential

反义词：interpolative (插值的), factual (事实的)
