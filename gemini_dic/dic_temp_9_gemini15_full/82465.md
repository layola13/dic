grumping:/ˈɡrʌmpɪŋ/ | v. | 脾气暴躁；抱怨；发牢骚 | The act of being grumpy or complaining; expressing displeasure or irritation. |例句：He spent the afternoon grumping about the weather. 他一下午都在抱怨天气。


例句：The children's constant grumping annoyed their parents. 孩子们不停地抱怨惹恼了他们的父母。


近义词：complaining, grumbling, sulking, complaining, muttering


反义词：being cheerful, being happy, being pleasant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is always grumping. = 他总是脾气暴躁。
She grumps about everything. = 她对所有事情都抱怨。


一般过去时:
He grumped about his job yesterday. = 他昨天抱怨他的工作。
She grumped all day after the argument. = 争吵后她整天都闷闷不乐。


现在完成时:
He has been grumping all week. = 他抱怨了一个星期了。
She has grumped about the traffic again. = 她又开始抱怨交通了。


过去完成时:
He had been grumping for days before he finally snapped. = 他抱怨了好几天，最后终于爆发了。
She had grumped about the project before realizing the problem. = 在意识到问题之前，她一直在抱怨这个项目。


将来时:
He will be grumping if he doesn't get his way. = 如果他不遂心愿，他会发脾气的。
She will probably grump about the long journey. = 她可能会抱怨漫长的旅程。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so tired, he wouldn't be grumping. = 如果他不那么累，他就不会发脾气了。
I wish he wouldn't grump so much. = 我希望他不要抱怨那么多。


被动语态:  (Grumping is rarely used in the passive voice)


疑问句:
Is he grumping again? = 他又在发脾气了吗？
Why is she grumping? = 她为什么发脾气？


否定句:
He isn't grumping today. = 他今天没发脾气。
She didn't grump at all during the meeting. = 会议期间她一点都没抱怨。


PHRASE->
grumping around = 脾气暴躁地四处走动
grumping about = 抱怨…
grumping to oneself = 自言自语地抱怨
grumping and groaning = 嘟嘟囔囔地抱怨
feeling grumpy = 感觉脾气暴躁
