receipter:/rɪˈsiːptər/| n. |收据开具人；收据签收人 | A person who issues or signs receipts.  The term is less common in modern English and often replaced with more specific terms depending on the context. | Example: The receipter confirmed the delivery of the goods.  收据签收人确认货物已交付。 |近义词：receipt issuer, signatory, receiver |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He receipts all the incoming payments. 他签收所有到账的付款。
She receipts every invoice before filing. 她在存档前为每张发票签收。


一般过去时:
The clerk receipted the package yesterday. 职员昨天签收了包裹。
He receipted the payment and gave a receipt. 他签收了付款并给了收据。


现在完成时:
She has receipted all the documents this week. 她本周已签收所有文件。
He has receipted numerous payments today. 他今天已签收了大量的付款。


过去完成时:
They had receipted the shipment before the inspection. 他们在检查之前已签收了货物。
She had receipted the money before leaving the office. 她在离开办公室之前已签收了钱。


将来时:
He will receipt the delivery tomorrow morning. 他明天早上将签收货物。
She will receipt the payment as soon as it arrives. 收到付款后，她会立即签收。


虚拟语气:
If he were the receipter, he would have signed it. 如果他是签收人，他会签收的。
If she had been the receipter, the process would have been smoother. 如果她是签收人，流程会更顺畅。


被动语态:
The payment was receipted by the manager. 付款由经理签收。
All documents are receipted in the accounting department. 所有文件都在会计部门签收。


疑问句:
Who receipted the package? 谁签收了包裹？
Has the payment been receipted yet? 付款已经签收了吗？


否定句:
He did not receipt the package because it was damaged. 他没有签收包裹，因为它损坏。
She hasn't receipted the payment yet. 她还没签收付款。
