confraternity's: /kənˈfræːtənɪtiz/ | n. | 兄弟会（的）；联谊会（的） | The fellowship or association of a religious order, brotherhood, or guild.  It refers to something belonging to or associated with a confraternity. |例句：The confraternity's annual meeting was well-attended.  兄弟会的年度会议出席率很高。


The confraternity's charitable work benefits the local community. 兄弟会的慈善工作使当地社区受益。


近义词：brotherhood's, guild's, association's, society's | 反义词：N/A


词源:

From Middle English *confraternite, from Old French confraternite, from Latin confraternitas, from con- + fraternitas brotherhood.  The Latin root "frater" means "brother," reflecting the original sense of brotherhood and fellowship within religious orders.
