consolato:/kɒnsəˈlɑːtoʊ/| n. |领事馆；领事职位|A consulate is a diplomatic office representing a country in a foreign city, typically headed by a consul.  It provides consular services to citizens of the represented country and often promotes trade and cultural exchange.|例句：I visited the Italian consulate to renew my passport. 我去意大利领事馆续签护照。|例句：He was appointed consul at the consulate in Shanghai. 他被任命为上海领事馆领事。|近义词：embassy, mission|反义词：无
