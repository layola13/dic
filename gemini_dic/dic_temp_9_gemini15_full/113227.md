lusk:/lʌsk/ | n. |懒汉，游手好闲的人 |A lusk is a lazy, idle person who avoids work.  The word often implies a degree of shiftlessness and a lack of ambition.|例句：He was considered a lusk by his peers for his constant avoidance of responsibilities. 他因为总是逃避责任而被同龄人认为是懒汉。 He's become a bit of a lusk since he retired. 他退休后变得有点懒惰了。|近义词：loafer, idler, sluggard, drone |反义词：industrious, diligent, hardworking |名词
