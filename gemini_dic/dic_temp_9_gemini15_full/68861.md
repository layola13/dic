fast-forward:/ˌfæstˈfɔːrwərd/| vt. n. adj.|快进；快速发展；提前；|To move quickly through a recording or a period of time, skipping over less important parts.  Also used figuratively to describe rapid progress or advancement.|例句：I fast-forwarded through the boring parts of the movie. 我快进跳过了电影中无聊的部分。

例句：The company fast-forwarded its plans for expansion after securing a large investment.  公司在获得巨额投资后加快了扩张计划。


近义词：advance, accelerate, expedite, skip |反义词：rewind, regress, decelerate|动词，名词，形容词
