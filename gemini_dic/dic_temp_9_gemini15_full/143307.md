outdueled: /ˌaʊtˈduːəld/ | v. |  胜过；技高一筹 | To surpass or defeat someone in a contest, especially a duel or competition requiring skill.  |例句：The seasoned swordsman outdueled his younger opponent with superior technique.  经验丰富的剑客以高超的技巧击败了他的年轻对手。

The champion outdueled all challengers, securing his title for another year. 冠军击败了所有挑战者，从而确保了他另一年的冠军头衔。| 近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, defeat, surpass | 反义词：be defeated by, be outmatched by | 动词

词源:  The word "outdueled" is derived from the word "duel," ultimately from the Old French "duel" (a fight between two people). The prefix "out-" indicates exceeding or surpassing. Therefore, "outdueled" means to have won a duel or competitive contest by surpassing one's opponent.
