illiteral: /ɪˈlɪtərəl/ | adj. | 不按字面意思的；非字面的；缺乏文字素养的 |  Not literal; figurative.  Lacking literacy. |

英文解析：The word "illiteral" has two distinct meanings. Primarily, it means not literal or figurative, contrasting with a direct, explicit meaning.  Secondly, and less commonly used, it describes someone lacking literacy skills, meaning unable to read or write.  The first meaning is far more prevalent in modern usage.


例句：

The poem's meaning is illiteral; it uses symbolism to convey deeper emotions. = 这首诗的意思不是字面的；它用象征手法来表达更深层次的情感。

His illiteral response showed a lack of understanding of the question. = 他不按字面意思的回答表明他并不理解这个问题。


近义词：figurative, metaphorical, symbolic (for the first meaning); illiterate, unlettered (for the second meaning)


反义词：literal, verbatim (for the first meaning); literate (for the second meaning)


词性：形容词
