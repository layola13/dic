partition:/pɑːrˈtɪʃən/| n., vt.| 分区；隔断；划分 | To divide into parts; a dividing wall or structure. |例句：The room was partitioned into two smaller rooms.  房间被隔成了两个小房间。
|例句：We need to partition the hard drive to install the new operating system. 我们需要分区硬盘才能安装新的操作系统。|近义词：division, separation, subdivision |反义词：unification, combination, merging |名词, 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He partitions his time between work and family. = 他把时间分配在工作和家庭之间。
The wall partitions the living room from the dining room. = 这堵墙隔开了起居室和餐厅。

一般过去时:
She partitioned the garden into different sections. = 她把花园划分成不同的区域。
They partitioned the company into several departments. = 他们把公司划分成几个部门。

现在完成时:
We have partitioned the database for better performance. = 为了更好的性能，我们已经对数据库进行了分区。
They have partitioned the land among the heirs. = 他们已经把土地分给了继承人。

过去完成时:
The programmer had partitioned the hard drive before installing the software. = 程序员在安装软件之前已经对硬盘进行了分区。
They had partitioned the house before the family moved in. = 他们在这个家庭搬进来之前已经把房子隔开了。

将来时:
We will partition the office space next week. = 我们下周将划分办公空间。
They will partition the data based on user location. = 他们将根据用户位置对数据进行分区。

虚拟语气:
If we partitioned the data, the search would be faster. = 如果我们对数据进行分区，搜索速度将会更快。
I wish we had partitioned the project earlier. = 我希望我们早点把项目划分一下。

被动语态:
The room was partitioned by a thin curtain. = 房间被一层薄薄的窗帘隔开了。
The hard drive will be partitioned by the technician. = 硬盘将由技术人员进行分区。

疑问句:
How many partitions are there on your hard drive? = 你的硬盘上有多少个分区？
Will the land be partitioned fairly? = 土地将被公平地划分吗？

否定句:
The company did not partition the profits equally. = 公司没有平均分配利润。
We have not yet partitioned the database. = 我们还没有对数据库进行分区。
