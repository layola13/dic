previewed:/ˈpriːvjuːd/ | v. | 预览；预先查看 | To view or show something beforehand, typically a film, play, or other performance, to provide an idea of what it will be like. |  例句：The film was previewed at a special screening.  这部电影在特别放映会上进行了预演。

例句：We previewed the new software before releasing it to the public. 我们在向公众发布新软件之前对其进行了预览。


近义词：viewed, screened, showed, examined, inspected | 反义词：concealed, hidden


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preview the movie before its official release. = 他们在电影正式上映前进行预告片放映。
He previews his presentations before giving them. = 他在做演示之前会先预览一下。

一般过去时:
She previewed the document before sending it. = 她发送文件之前先预览了一遍。
We previewed the website before launching it. = 我们在启动网站之前先预览了网站。

现在完成时:
They have previewed the new car at several events. = 他们已经在几次活动中预览了新车。
I have previewed the chapter and found it interesting. = 我已经预览了这一章，发现它很有趣。

过去完成时:
He had previewed the script many times before the rehearsal. = 排练之前他已经多次预习了剧本。
They had previewed the product before putting it on the market. = 他们在将产品投放市场之前已经预览了该产品。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I had previewed the contract, I would have noticed the error. = 如果我之前预览过合同，我就会注意到错误了。
It would be helpful if they previewed the design before printing. = 如果他们在印刷前预览一下设计会很有帮助。
