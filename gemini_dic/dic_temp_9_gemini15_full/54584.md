dismally:/ˈdɪzməli/ | adj. | 凄凉地；悲惨地；令人沮丧地 | In a dismal manner; depressingly; gloomily.  It describes something done or happening in a way that is depressing, gloomy, or without hope. | 例句：The rain fell dismally all day.  = 雨整天下着，令人沮丧。

The team performed dismally, losing by a large margin. = 球队表现糟糕，输得很惨。


近义词：gloomily, drearily, sadly, miserably, depressingly

反义词：joyfully, cheerfully, brightly, happily, triumphantly

词性：形容词
