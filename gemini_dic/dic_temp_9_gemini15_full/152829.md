peculiarity:/ˌpɪkjuːlˈjærəti/| n. |奇特；特性；古怪 |A peculiarity is a strange or unusual characteristic or feature of something or someone.  It refers to something that is distinctive or odd, often in a way that is interesting or noteworthy. |例句：His most striking peculiarity was his habit of talking to himself. 他最显著的特点是他有自言自语的习惯。

例句：The peculiarity of the situation was that nobody seemed to notice.  情况的奇特之处在于似乎没有人注意到。


近义词：oddity, quirk, idiosyncrasy, singularity, eccentricity


反义词：normality, regularity, commonness


词性：名词


词源:  The word "peculiarity" comes from the Middle English word "peculiarite," which in turn derives from the Old French word "peculiarité" and ultimately from the Latin word "peculiaris," meaning "one's own," "special," or "particular."
