marbly:/ˈmɑːrblɪ/| adj. |大理石似的；像大理石的；有斑点的|Resembling marble in appearance; having a variegated or mottled appearance like marble.  The word evokes the smooth, often veined or swirled texture and coloration of marble.|例句：The cake had a marbly texture, with streaks of chocolate throughout. 这块蛋糕有着大理石般的纹理，巧克力条纹遍布其中。

The countertop had a marbly white and gray pattern.  台面呈现出大理石般白色和灰色的图案。|近义词：mottled, veined, variegated |反义词：uniform, plain, solid
