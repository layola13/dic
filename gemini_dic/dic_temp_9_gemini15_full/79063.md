giving-back:/ˈɡɪvɪŋbæk/| n. |回馈；付出；奉献 |The act of donating time, money, or resources to a cause or community.  It emphasizes the reciprocal nature of giving, suggesting a benefit both to the receiver and the giver. |例句：The company's giving-back program supports local charities. 公司的回馈计划支持当地慈善机构。

例句：Volunteering is a wonderful form of giving-back to the community. 志愿服务是回馈社区的一种绝佳方式。|近义词：contribution, donation, philanthropy, altruism |反义词：selfishness, greed, taking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Giving-back is important to many people. = 回馈对很多人来说很重要。
The company supports giving-back initiatives. = 公司支持回馈计划。


一般过去时:
She gave back to her community through volunteering. = 她通过志愿服务回馈了她的社区。
They gave back a significant portion of their profits. = 他们回馈了他们利润的一大部分。


现在完成时:
We have given back to this charity for many years. = 我们多年来一直向这家慈善机构捐款。
The foundation has given back millions to various causes. = 该基金会已向各种事业捐赠了数百万美元。


过去完成时:
Before retiring, he had given back extensively to his alma mater. = 在退休前，他已经大量地回馈了他的母校。
They had given back more than they had received. = 他们已经回馈的比得到的更多。


将来时:
We will give back a portion of our earnings to the homeless shelter. = 我们将把我们的一部分收入捐给流浪者收容所。
She plans to give back to the arts community after her retirement. = 她计划退休后回馈艺术界。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would give back more generously. = 如果我有更多钱，我会更慷慨地回馈。
I wish I could give back more to those in need. = 我希望我能更多地帮助那些需要帮助的人。


被动语态:
Millions of dollars were given back to the community through various programs. = 通过各种项目，数百万美元被回馈给了社区。
The funds are being given back to support education. = 这些资金正在被用来支持教育事业。


疑问句:
How can we give back effectively? = 我们如何才能有效地回馈？
What is the best way to give back to the environment? = 回馈环境的最佳方式是什么？


否定句:
He didn't give back to the community as much as he could have. = 他没有尽他所能地回馈社区。
They chose not to give back to the organization. = 他们选择不回馈该组织。
