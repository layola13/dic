ligo:/ˈlaɪɡoʊ/| n. |激光干涉引力波天文台|Laser Interferometer Gravitational-Wave Observatory;  a large-scale physics experiment and observatory to detect cosmic gravitational waves.  It is designed to detect the extremely subtle stretching and compression of spacetime caused by passing gravitational waves.|Example: LIGO's detection of gravitational waves confirmed Einstein's theory of general relativity.|近义词:  (无直接近义词，因其指代特定设备)|反义词: (无直接反义词)|名词
