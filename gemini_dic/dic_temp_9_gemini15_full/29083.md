/kɑːrˈvɪst/| n. |雕刻家，刻字匠 |A carvist is a person who carves, especially in wood or stone.  They are skilled artisans who create art or functional objects through the process of carving. |例句：The carvist meticulously carved the intricate details of the wooden bird.  这位雕刻家一丝不苟地雕刻着木鸟的复杂细节。|例句：Many talented carvists participated in the wood carving competition. 许多有天赋的雕刻家参加了木雕比赛。|近义词：sculptor, carver, engraver |反义词：N/A
