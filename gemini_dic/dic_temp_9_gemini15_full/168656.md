proconservation: /ˌproʊkɒnsərˈveɪʃən/ | adj. | 保护主义的；支持环保的 |  Supporting or promoting conservation. This term combines the prefix "pro-" (meaning "in favor of" or "supporting") with "conservation" (the act of preserving, protecting, or restoring something).
