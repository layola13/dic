sacrileges:/ˈsækrɪlɪdʒɪz/| n.|渎圣罪；亵渎；冒犯|The act of violating or profaning anything sacred.  It often refers to religious acts considered disrespectful or impious.|例句：The destruction of the ancient temple was considered a terrible sacrilege.  古代神庙的毁坏被认为是可怕的渎圣罪。

例句：His disrespectful comments about the deceased were seen as a sacrilege by many. 他对死者的不敬言论被许多人视为亵渎。


近义词：blasphemy, desecration, profanation, impiety

反义词：reverence, piety, sanctity


词性：名词
