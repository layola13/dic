princesse:/ˈprɪnsɛs/| n. |公主|A female member of a royal family, typically the daughter of a king or queen.  A princess is often associated with elegance, grace, and privilege. |She dreamed of becoming a princess and living in a castle. 她梦想成为一名公主并住在城堡里。|Queen, duchess, lady|commoner, peasant|名词
