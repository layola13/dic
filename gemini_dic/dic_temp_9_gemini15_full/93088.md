idumean:/ˌaɪˈduːmiən/| n. adj.|以土买人，以土买人的|Of or relating to Idumea, an ancient region in southern Palestine, inhabited by the Edomites.  The term often carries historical and biblical connotations. |例句：The Idumean king Herod was a complex and controversial figure in Jewish history. 以土买人国王希律是犹太历史上一位复杂且有争议的人物。  The Idumean desert was harsh and unforgiving. 以土买人沙漠是严酷无情的。|近义词：Edomite|反义词：无
