It can also refer to a period of rest or respite from work. |例句：The family observed the sabbaton with prayer and a special meal.  这个家庭以祈祷和特别的饭菜来庆祝安息日。

The sabbaton was a much-needed break after a long week of work.  漫长的一周工作之后，安息日是急需的休息。|近义词：rest, respite, break, Sabbath|反义词：workday, labor
