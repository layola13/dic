nonjoiners:/ˌnɒnˈdʒɔɪnərz/| n. |不参与者，局外人 |People who choose not to participate in social groups or activities.  Those who prefer solitude or independent pursuits over group involvement. |Example:  The study showed a significant increase in the number of nonjoiners in recent years.  这项研究表明，近年来不参与者的人数显著增加。


Example: The survey revealed a growing population of nonjoiners, particularly among young adults.  调查显示，不参与者的人口数量正在增长，尤其是在年轻人中。


近义词: loners, isolates, individualists, outsiders


反义词: joiners, participants, members, groupies


词性:名词
