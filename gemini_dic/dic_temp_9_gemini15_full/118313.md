mere:/mɪər/| adj.|仅仅的，只不过的，纯粹的|  only; nothing more than; simply.  The word suggests insignificance or triviality. |例句：It was a mere trifle. 这仅仅是一件小事。

例句：The difference is mere semantics.  区别仅仅在于措辞。


近义词：only, just, simply, sheer, pure

反义词：significant, important, substantial, considerable

词源:源自古英语 *mære,  meaning "pure, unmixed," from Proto-Germanic *mēr-.
