future-proof: /ˈfjuːtʃəpruːf/ | adj. | 未来可靠的；防患于未然的；经得起未来考验的 |  Designed or built to be unaffected by future developments or changes.  Something that is future-proof is unlikely to become obsolete or irrelevant in the future. |


例句：

This software is future-proof, meaning it can adapt to new operating systems. = 这个软件具有未来可靠性，这意味着它可以适应新的操作系统。

We need a future-proof strategy to address the challenges of climate change. = 我们需要一个防患于未然的策略来应对气候变化的挑战。


近义词：forward-looking,  long-term, sustainable, adaptable, resilient

反义词：obsolete, outdated, short-sighted, unsustainable


词性:形容词
