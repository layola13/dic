hardwood:/ˈhɑːrdˌwʊd/| n.|硬木，阔叶木|Hardwood is a type of wood from broadleaf trees, known for its density and durability.  It's often used in flooring, furniture, and musical instruments.|例句：The table is made of solid hardwood. 这张桌子是用硬木做的。

例句：Oak and maple are examples of hardwood. 橡木和枫木是硬木的例子。


近义词：hard timber, deciduous wood|反义词：softwood|词性:名词
