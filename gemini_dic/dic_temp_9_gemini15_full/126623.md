nailed:/neɪld/| v. |钉住；彻底解决；成功做到|To fix or secure something with a nail; to successfully accomplish something.|例句：He nailed the board to the wall. 他把木板钉在了墙上。

例句：She nailed that presentation. 她成功完成了那次演示。|近义词：fastened, secured, accomplished, mastered|反义词：failed, missed, botched|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nails the picture to the wall every year. = 他每年都把画钉在墙上。
I nail my plans for the future. = 我认真计划我的未来。


一般过去时:
He nailed the shelf to the wall yesterday. = 他昨天把架子钉在了墙上。
She nailed the interview. = 她成功通过了面试。


现在完成时:
He has nailed all the shelves. = 他已经把所有的架子都钉好了。
She has nailed her exams. = 她已经成功通过了考试。


过去完成时:
He had nailed the door shut before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他已经把门钉上了。
She had nailed the deal before lunch. = 她在午餐前就完成了交易。


将来时:
He will nail the poster to the tree. = 他会把海报钉在树上。
She will nail her speech. = 她会成功完成她的演讲。


虚拟语气:
If he nailed the exam, he would be very happy. = 如果他考试及格了，他会非常高兴。
If she had nailed the project, she would have gotten a promotion. = 如果她完成了项目，她就会得到晋升。


被动语态:
The picture was nailed to the wall. = 这幅画被钉在墙上。
The plan was nailed down. = 计划已经确定下来了。


疑问句:
Did he nail the test? = 他考试及格了吗？
Has she nailed the project yet? = 她完成项目了吗？


否定句:
He didn't nail the project. = 他没有完成项目。
She didn't nail the landing. = 她没有顺利着陆。


PHRASE->
nail down: 敲定；确定
nail it: 做到；成功
get nailed: 受罚；被抓住
hammer and nail: 钉锤和钉子
nail-biting: 令人紧张的
