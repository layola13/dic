safeguard:/ˈseɪfɡɑːrd/|/ˈseɪfɡɑːrd/| n. vt.| 保护措施；保护；捍卫 | A measure taken to protect someone or something.  |例句：The company has put in place safeguards to prevent data breaches. 公司已实施保护措施以防止数据泄露。

例句： We need safeguards against unauthorized access.我们需要防止未经授权访问的保护措施。


近义词：protection, defense, security, precaution |反义词：risk, danger, threat |词性: noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government safeguards the interests of its citizens. = 政府维护公民的利益。
This system safeguards against errors. = 这个系统可以防止错误。


一般过去时:
The company safeguarded its intellectual property. = 公司保护了它的知识产权。
They safeguarded the children from harm. = 他们保护孩子们免受伤害。


现在完成时:
The new laws have safeguarded the environment. = 新法律已经保护了环境。
We have safeguarded our data against loss. = 我们已经保护了我们的数据免受丢失。


过去完成时:
They had safeguarded the documents before the raid. = 他们在突袭之前已经保护了文件。
She had safeguarded her reputation throughout her career. = 她在整个职业生涯中都维护了自己的声誉。


将来时:
The company will safeguard its assets. = 公司将保护其资产。
We will safeguard the future of our children. = 我们将保障孩子们的未来。


虚拟语气:
If we had safeguarded our data properly, we wouldn't have lost it. = 如果我们正确地保护了我们的数据，我们就不会丢失它。
It is vital that we safeguard the environment for future generations. = 保护环境以造福子孙后代至关重要。


被动语态:
The data is safeguarded by advanced encryption. = 数据通过高级加密得到保护。
The interests of the minority are safeguarded by law. = 少数人的利益受法律保护。


疑问句:
How can we safeguard our privacy online? = 我们如何保护在线隐私？
What measures are in place to safeguard the environment? = 为了保护环境，有哪些措施到位？


否定句:
The company failed to safeguard its employees' data. = 公司未能保护其员工的数据。
They did not safeguard the building against fire. = 他们没有保护建筑物免受火灾。


PHRASE->
safeguard against = 预防；防止
safeguard interests = 保护利益
safeguard the rights of = 保护…的权利
safeguard national security = 保护国家安全
safeguard the environment = 保护环境

词源:
The word "safeguard" comes from the Old French "sauvegarde," which is a combination of "sauf" (safe) and "garde" (guard).  It entered English in the 14th century.
