reconciliation:/ˌrɛkənˈsɪlieɪʃən/ | n. |和解；调和；协调；对账 |The act of making one thing compatible with another; the process of resolving conflict and establishing harmony.  It can refer to personal relationships, political disputes, or financial accounts. |例句：After years of estrangement, the family finally achieved reconciliation. 多年疏远后，这个家庭最终实现了和解。

例句：The accountant performed a reconciliation of the bank statement and the company's records. 会计对银行对账单和公司的记录进行了对账。


近义词：settlement, agreement, compromise, atonement, rapprochement |反义词：estrangement, discord, conflict, antagonism


词源:  From Old French *reconciliacion, from Latin reconciliatio, from reconciliare "to reconcile," from re- "again" + conciliare "to unite, bring together," from concilium "council, assembly."
