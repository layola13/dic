dress:/drɛs/| n., v.|连衣裙；服装；打扮；穿着|

英文解析:
Noun:  A garment, typically made of fabric, worn by women or girls, usually covering the body from the shoulders to below the knees. It can also refer to any kind of clothing or attire.
Verb: To put clothes on oneself or someone else; to clothe oneself or someone. To adorn or decorate something.


例句:
She wore a beautiful red dress to the party. = 她穿着一件漂亮的红色连衣裙去参加聚会。

The children were dressed in their best clothes for the occasion. = 孩子们为了这次场合穿上了他们最好的衣服。


近义词:
Noun: gown, frock, outfit, garment
Verb: clothe, attire, adorn, array


反义词:
Noun: nudity (反义词较为宽泛，无完全对应词)
Verb: undress


词性:
Noun, Verb
