progenitor:/ˈprɒdʒɪnɪtə(r)/|/ˈprɑːdʒənɪtər/| n. |祖先；始祖；发明者；开创者 |A progenitor is an ancestor, especially a very distant one.  It can also refer to the originator or founder of something. |例句：The ancient Greeks are considered progenitors of Western philosophy. 古希腊人被认为是西方哲学的始祖。

The discovery of penicillin was a significant achievement for its progenitor, Alexander Fleming. 青霉素的发现对其发明者亚历山大·弗莱明来说是一项重大成就。|近义词：ancestor, forefather, originator, founder |反义词：descendant, offspring
