shipwrecks:/ˈʃɪpˌrɛks/| n. |船舶失事；沉船；遇难船只|The remains of sunken vessels, often resulting from accidents, storms, or warfare.  Shipwrecks can be fascinating historical artifacts and popular diving sites, but they also pose environmental concerns. |例句：Divers explored the ancient shipwrecks off the coast. 潜水员探索了海岸附近的古代沉船。

例句：The coastline is scattered with the ghostly remains of countless shipwrecks. 海岸线上散布着无数沉船的幽灵般的残骸。


近义词：wrecks, sunken ships, maritime disasters|反义词：safe arrival, successful voyage|词性:noun
