gusher:/ˈɡʌʃər/| n. |喷涌者；涌出物；（尤指）自喷油井|A gusher is something that gushes forth, especially a well that produces a large amount of oil or gas.  It can also refer to a person who expresses their feelings or opinions excessively and effusively.|例句：The newly drilled well proved to be a gusher, producing thousands of barrels of oil a day.  新钻的油井证明是一个自喷油井，每天生产数千桶石油。

例句：He's a gusher; he talks endlessly about his children. 他是个滔滔不绝的人；他没完没了地谈论他的孩子。


近义词：spouter, fountain, outpouring | 反义词：trickle, seep, dribble

词性:名词
