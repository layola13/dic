polytungstic: /ˌpɒliˈtʌŋstɪk/ | adj. |多钨的|  Relating to or containing several tungsten atoms, typically in the form of polyoxometalates.  These compounds are characterized by complex structures with multiple tungsten atoms linked together through oxygen atoms. They often exhibit interesting catalytic and electronic properties. |  The study focused on the synthesis and characterization of novel polytungstic acids.  这项研究集中在新型多钨酸的合成和表征上。 |近义词: polyoxometalate (containing multiple metal atoms, often tungsten)|反义词: montungstic (containing a single tungsten atom)
