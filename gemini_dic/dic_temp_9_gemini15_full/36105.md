coequally:/ˈkoʊiːkwəli/| adv.|平等地；同等地 |Equally in all respects; to the same extent.  This adverb combines the prefix "co-" (meaning together or jointly) with "equally" (meaning to the same degree or extent). It emphasizes the simultaneous and equal application of something to multiple entities. |例句：The two teams competed coequally for the championship.  两个队同等竞争冠军。The resources were distributed coequally among the three departments. 资源在三个部门之间平均分配。|近义词：equally, uniformly, proportionately|反义词：unequally, disproportionately|副词
