extolled:/ɪkˈstɔːld/ | vt. | 赞扬；颂扬；吹捧 | To praise highly; to glorify.  |The speaker extolled the virtues of hard work.  演讲者赞扬了努力工作的优点。|  praised, lauded, commended, glorified | criticized, condemned, denounced |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He extols the benefits of regular exercise. 他赞扬规律运动的好处。
They extol their country's achievements. 他们赞扬他们国家的成就。


一般过去时:
She extolled the beauty of the sunset. 她赞扬了日落的美丽。
The critics extolled the actor's performance. 批评家们赞扬了这位演员的表演。


现在完成时:
He has extolled the virtues of patience throughout his career. 他在整个职业生涯中都赞扬了耐心的美德。
The media has extolled the success of the new product. 媒体盛赞新产品的成功。


过去完成时:
They had extolled the benefits of the new technology before its release. 他们在新技术发布之前就赞扬了它的好处。
She had extolled the importance of education to her children. 她曾向孩子们强调教育的重要性。


将来时:
He will extol the achievements of the team at the ceremony. 他将在仪式上赞扬团队的成就。
They will extol the virtues of kindness in their speeches. 他们将在演讲中赞扬仁慈的优点。


虚拟语气:
If I were to write a book, I would extol the importance of family. 如果我要写一本书，我会赞扬家庭的重要性。
I wish I could extol the beauty of nature more effectively. 我希望我能更有效地赞扬大自然的美丽。


被动语态:
The benefits of yoga are extolled by many practitioners. 瑜伽的好处被许多练习者所赞扬。
His contributions to the field were extolled by his colleagues. 他对该领域的贡献受到了同事们的赞扬。


疑问句:
Does he often extol the virtues of his company? 他经常赞扬他公司的优点吗？
What qualities does she extol in her writing? 她在写作中赞扬什么品质？


否定句:
He did not extol the virtues of laziness. 他没有赞扬懒惰的优点。
The article did not extol the achievements of the government. 这篇文章没有赞扬政府的成就。


PHRASE->

extol someone's praises  高度赞扬某人

词源:
From Old French *extoller, from Latin extollere, from ex- + tollere "to lift, raise."  The meaning shifted from "to lift up" to "to praise highly."
