sleuthhound:/ˈsluːθhaʊnd/| n.|侦探犬；擅长追踪的警犬|A sleuthhound is a dog, typically a breed known for its keen sense of smell and tracking abilities, used in detective work or hunting.  It combines "sleuth," meaning detective, and "hound," a type of dog. |例句：The police used a highly trained sleuthhound to track the suspect through the woods. 警局用一只训练有素的侦探犬追踪嫌疑犯穿过树林。|近义词：detective dog, tracking dog, bloodhound|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a pet dog lacking tracking skills.
