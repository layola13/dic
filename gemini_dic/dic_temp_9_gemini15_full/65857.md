evocative: /ɪˈvɒkətɪv/ | adj. | gợi nhớ, gợi lên, gợi cảm |  Evoking or suggesting (a feeling, image, or memory)  | The evocative power of music can transport us to another time and place.  音乐的唤起力量可以将我们带到另一个时空。| suggestive, reminiscent, evocative | unevocative
