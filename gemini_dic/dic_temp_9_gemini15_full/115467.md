manufacture:/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/ | vt. & n. | 制造；生产；加工 | To make something on a large scale using machinery.  |例句：This company manufactures cars. 这家公司生产汽车。
The manufacture of cars is a complex process. 汽车的制造是一个复杂的过程。|近义词：produce, make, fabricate |反义词：destroy, demolish

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They manufacture cars in that factory. = 他们在那个工厂制造汽车。
The company manufactures various types of clothing. = 这家公司生产各种类型的服装。

一般过去时:
They manufactured thousands of toys last year. = 他们去年生产了数千个玩具。
The factory manufactured steel for many years. = 这个工厂生产钢铁很多年了。


现在完成时:
They have manufactured over a million units this year. = 他们今年已经生产了超过一百万件产品。
The company has manufactured high-quality products for decades. = 这家公司几十年来一直在生产高质量的产品。


过去完成时:
They had manufactured all the parts before the assembly began. = 在组装开始之前，他们已经制造好了所有零件。
The company had manufactured similar products in the past. = 这家公司过去生产过类似的产品。


将来时:
They will manufacture a new model of phone next year. = 他们明年将生产一款新型手机。
The factory will manufacture more sustainable products in the future. = 工厂将来会生产更环保的产品。


虚拟语气:
If they manufactured more efficiently, they would make more profit. = 如果他们生产效率更高，他们就会赚更多钱。
I wish they manufactured more eco-friendly products. = 我希望他们生产更多环保的产品。


被动语态:
Cars are manufactured in many countries. = 汽车在许多国家生产。
The product is manufactured using advanced technology. = 该产品采用先进技术制造。


疑问句:
Where are these shoes manufactured? = 这些鞋在哪里生产的？
What kind of materials are used to manufacture this product? = 制造这款产品使用了什么材料？


否定句:
They do not manufacture any weapons. = 他们不生产任何武器。
The company doesn't manufacture low-quality goods. = 这家公司不生产低质量的商品。



PHRASE->
mass manufacture: 大规模生产
domestic manufacture: 国内制造
local manufacture: 地方制造
small-scale manufacture: 小规模生产
high-volume manufacture: 大批量生产
