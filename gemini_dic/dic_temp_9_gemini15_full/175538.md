ranching:/ˈræntʃɪŋ/ | n. |牧场经营；畜牧业 |The practice of raising livestock, especially cattle, on a large ranch.  Ranching involves managing land, animals, and resources for profit. |例句：He made his fortune through ranching in the American West. 他通过在美国西部的牧场经营发了财。|例句：Modern ranching techniques emphasize sustainability and efficient land use. 现代牧场经营技术强调可持续性和高效的土地利用。|近义词：cattle raising, livestock farming |反义词：urban farming, intensive agriculture
