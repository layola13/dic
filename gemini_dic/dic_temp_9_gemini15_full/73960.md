fortification:/ˌfɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |防御工事；强化；设防|The act of fortifying or strengthening a place, especially with military defenses.  It can also refer to the structures themselves, or the process of strengthening something, such as the body. |例句：The city's fortifications were designed to withstand a siege.  这座城市的防御工事是为了抵御围攻而设计的。

The addition of nutrients to food to improve its nutritional value.  例句：Fortification of foods with vitamins is common practice.  用维生素强化食物是很常见的做法。|近义词：defense, strengthening, reinforcement |反义词：weakening, demolition


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government fortifies the border. = 政府加强边境防御。
She fortifies her diet with vitamins. = 她用维生素强化她的饮食。


一般过去时:
They fortified the castle walls. = 他们加固了城堡的城墙。
He fortified himself with a large breakfast. = 他吃了一顿丰盛的早餐来增强体力。


现在完成时:
They have fortified the city against attack. = 他们已经加强了城市的防御以抵御攻击。
She has fortified her immune system with exercise and healthy eating. = 她通过运动和健康饮食增强了免疫力。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had fortified the position before the enemy arrived. = 士兵们在敌人到达之前已经构筑了防御工事。
He had fortified himself with coffee before the long drive. = 长途驾车前，他喝了咖啡提神。


将来时:
We will fortify the bridge against flooding. = 我们将加固桥梁以防洪水。
They will fortify their position with additional troops. = 他们将增派军队以加强他们的防御阵地。


虚拟语气:
If we fortified the walls, the castle would be much safer. = 如果我们加固城墙，城堡就会安全得多。
I wish I had fortified myself against the cold. = 我真希望我当时已经做好防寒准备。


被动语态:
The castle was fortified centuries ago. = 这座城堡几个世纪前就已经设防了。
The food is fortified with essential vitamins and minerals. = 食品中添加了必需的维生素和矿物质。


疑问句:
Were the walls sufficiently fortified? = 城墙加固得够了吗？
Is this bread fortified with iron? = 这面包添加了铁吗？


否定句:
The army did not fortify the outpost. = 军队没有加固前哨。
The company did not fortify its position in the market. = 公司没有加强其在市场上的地位。
