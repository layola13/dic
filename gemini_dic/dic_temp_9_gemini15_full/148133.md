oversees:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːz/ | vt. |监督；管理；俯瞰 | To supervise or manage (a process, activity, or organization).  It can also mean to look down upon from a high position. |例句：The project manager oversees all aspects of the project. 项目经理负责监督项目的各个方面。

The CEO oversees the entire company.  首席执行官负责整个公司。


近义词：supervise, manage, control, direct, superintend | 反义词：neglect, ignore, overlook


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He oversees the daily operations. 他监督日常运作。
She oversees a team of ten engineers. 她负责管理一个由十名工程师组成的团队。


一般过去时:
The manager oversaw the construction of the new building. 经理监督了新建筑的施工。
He oversaw the implementation of the new policy. 他监督了新政策的实施。


现在完成时:
She has overseen numerous successful projects. 她已经监督了许多成功的项目。
He has overseen the company's expansion into new markets. 他已经监督了公司向新市场的扩张。


过去完成时:
The board had overseen the company's finances for many years. 董事会多年来一直监督着公司的财务。
Before the merger, she had overseen the marketing department. 在合并之前，她负责营销部门。


将来时:
He will oversee the development of the new software. 他将监督新软件的开发。
She will oversee the training of new employees. 她将负责培训新员工。


虚拟语气:
If she oversaw the project, it would be completed on time. 如果她监督这个项目，它就能按时完成。
It would be better if someone else oversaw this process.  最好让其他人来监督这个过程。


被动语态:
The project was overseen by a highly experienced team. 这个项目由一个经验丰富的团队负责监督。
The company's finances are overseen by an independent auditor. 公司的财务由独立审计师负责监督。


疑问句:
Who oversees the department? 谁负责这个部门？
Does he oversee the entire operation? 他是否监督整个运作？


否定句:
He doesn't oversee the marketing department. 他不负责营销部门。
The project wasn't overseen properly.  这个项目没有得到适当的监督。


PHRASE->
oversee the project: 监督项目
oversee the implementation: 监督实施
oversee the team: 监督团队
oversee the operations: 监督运作
oversee the development: 监督发展


词源:
The word "oversees" comes from the Old English *ofersēon, which is a combination of *ofer ("over") and *sēon ("to see").  It literally means "to look over."  The modern meaning developed from the literal sense of watching over something or someone.
