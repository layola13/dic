newtonian:/njuːˈtəʊniən/| adj.|牛顿的；牛顿力学的；|Of or relating to Isaac Newton or his work, especially his laws of motion and universal gravitation.  Newtonian physics describes the motion of macroscopic objects and is based on Newton's three laws of motion and the law of universal gravitation.|例句：Newtonian mechanics explains the motion of objects under the influence of forces. 牛顿力学解释了物体在受力作用下的运动。

例句：The calculations were based on Newtonian physics.  这些计算是基于牛顿物理学的。


近义词：classical (in the context of physics)


反义词：relativistic (in the context of physics)

词性:形容词
