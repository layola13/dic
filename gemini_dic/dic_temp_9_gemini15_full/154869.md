perverted:/pərˈvɜːrtɪd/|/pərˈvɜːrtɪd/| adj.| 变态的；扭曲的；堕落的 |  Describes something that has been distorted from its original or natural state, often in a morally objectionable way. It can refer to sexual behavior, but also to other things that are twisted or unnatural.  | Example: His perverted sense of humor offended many people. 他变态的幽默感冒犯了许多人。  The painting was a perverted representation of reality. 这幅画是对现实的扭曲表现。|近义词：depraved, corrupt, warped, aberrant |反义词：normal, wholesome, virtuous, upright
