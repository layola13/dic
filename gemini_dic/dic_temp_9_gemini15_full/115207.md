manifestations:/ˌmænɪfɛˈsteɪʃənz/ | n. | 表现；显示；示威游行 |  The outward signs or symptoms of an inward state, a disease, or a feeling.  It can also refer to a public demonstration of protest or support. | 例句：The symptoms were early manifestations of the disease.  症状是该疾病的早期表现。

例句：
Thousands participated in the peaceful manifestations against the new law.数千人参加了反对新法律的和平示威活动。


近义词：demonstrations, displays, exhibitions, signs, symptoms

反义词：concealment, suppression, hiding


词源:
From Latin *manifestat-, past participle stem of manifestare "to make clear, show," from manus "hand" + -festus (past participle of fateri "to confess").  The sense evolved from "making something clear" to "making something apparent" to the modern meanings.
