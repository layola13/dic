disoccupation:/ˌdɪsˌɑːkjuˈpeɪʃən/| n. |脱离职业；失业；闲散；|The state of not being occupied or employed; unemployment.  It can also refer to the act of leaving a job or ceasing to occupy a place.|例句：The disoccupation of the factory led to widespread unemployment in the town. 工厂停产导致城镇大面积失业。

例句：His sudden disoccupation from his post surprised everyone. 他突然辞去职务让每个人都感到惊讶。


近义词：unemployment, idleness, vacancy, dereliction

反义词：employment, occupation, engagement


词性:名词
