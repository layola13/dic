demoted:/dɪˈmoʊtɪd/| v. |降职；贬低；使…失宠|To reduce someone to a lower rank or position.  It can also refer to reducing the importance or significance of something.|例句：He was demoted from captain to lieutenant. 他被从上尉降职为中尉。

例句：The company demoted him for his unprofessional conduct.  公司因其不专业的行为而将其降职。


近义词：relegated, downgraded, reduced, debased


反义词：promoted, elevated, advanced


词性:动词(过去分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They demote employees for poor performance. = 他们因表现不佳而降职员工。
The company demotes anyone who violates the code of conduct. = 公司会降职任何违反行为准则的人。


一般过去时:
She was demoted last year. = 她去年被降职了。
He was demoted after the scandal. = 丑闻之后他被降职了。


现在完成时:
He has been demoted twice this year. = 他今年已被降职两次。
The team has been demoted to a lower league. = 该队已被降级到较低的联赛。


过去完成时:
They had been demoted before the new manager arrived. = 新经理到来之前，他们已被降职。
She had been demoted several times in her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她曾被降职几次。


将来时:
He will be demoted if he continues to make mistakes. = 如果他继续犯错，他将被降职。
They will demote him for his repeated lateness. = 他们会因为他反复迟到而降职他。


虚拟语气:
If he had worked harder, he wouldn't have been demoted. = 如果他工作更努力，他就不会被降职。
I wish I hadn't been demoted. = 我真希望我没有被降职。


被动语态:
He was demoted for his negligence. = 他因疏忽而被降职。
The project was demoted in priority. = 项目的优先级被降低了。


疑问句:
Was he demoted because of his mistakes? = 他是因为犯错而被降职的吗？
Will she be demoted if she doesn't improve? = 如果她不改进，她会被降职吗？


否定句:
He wasn't demoted; he resigned. = 他没有被降职；他辞职了。
They didn't demote her, despite her mistakes. = 尽管她犯了错误，他们也没有降职她。


PHRASE->
be demoted to = 被降职到…
demoted from = 从…降职
demoted in rank = 军衔被降
demoted for cause = 因故被降职
demoted without prejudice = 不带偏见地降职
