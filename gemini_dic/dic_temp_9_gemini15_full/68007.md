facto:/ˈfæktəʊ/|adj.|事实上的，实际上，按照实际情况|In fact, as a matter of fact; actually, in reality;  used to emphasize that a statement is true.|例句：De facto segregation is still a problem in many parts of the country. 事实上的种族隔离在该国许多地区仍然是一个问题。|近义词：actual, real, true|反义词：de jure, theoretical
