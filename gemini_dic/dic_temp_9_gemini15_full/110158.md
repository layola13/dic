/ˈliːdʒəns/| n. |忠诚；效忠；拥戴|Loyalty or faithfulness to a person, country, group, cause, etc.  It implies a strong sense of duty and obligation.|例句：He swore allegiance to the king. 他向国王宣誓效忠。

例句：The soldiers pledged their allegiance to their commander.  士兵们向他们的指挥官宣誓效忠。


近义词：loyalty, fidelity, faithfulness, devotion | 反义词：treachery, disloyalty, betrayal | 词性：名词


词源：源自古法语 *legeance，来自古法语 lige，意思是“绑定”，最终源自日耳曼语系。
