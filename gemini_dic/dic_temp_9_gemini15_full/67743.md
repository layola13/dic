eyeballer:/ˈaɪˌbɔːlər/| n. |注视者；凝视者；盯着看的人| A person who stares intently or watches closely.  It often implies a sense of scrutiny or suspicion. |例句：The eyeballer in the back of the room made me nervous.  房间后面那个盯着我看的人让我很紧张。|例句：He felt like an eyeballer under the intense gaze of the crowd. 在人群紧张的注视下，他感觉自己像个被盯着的人。|近义词：gazer, watcher, observer, scrutinizer|反义词：ignorer, overlooker
