chainsaw:/ˈtʃeɪnsɔː/|/ˈtʃeɪnˌsɔː/| n. |链锯，电锯 |A chainsaw is a powered saw with a chain of cutting teeth.  It's used for cutting wood, typically trees or logs. |例句：He used a chainsaw to cut down the fallen tree. 他用链锯砍倒了倒下的树。


例句：The logger skillfully wielded the chainsaw, effortlessly felling the giant redwood.  伐木工人熟练地挥舞着链锯，轻松地砍倒了巨大的红杉树。


近义词：power saw, motorized saw |反义词：hand saw, axe
