ducal:/ˈdʒuːkəl/| adj.|公爵的；与公爵有关的|Of or relating to a duke or dukedom.  The word derives from the Latin word "dux," meaning "leader" or "commander," which evolved into the Old French word "duc," meaning "duke."  The suffix "-al" indicates an adjective form.|例句：The ducal family owned vast estates.  公爵家族拥有广阔的庄园。

例句：She inherited a ducal title. 她继承了一个公爵的头衔。


近义词：princely, aristocratic

反义词：common, plebeian

词性：形容词
