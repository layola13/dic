humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| n. |胡说八道；骗局；讨厌的人 |Humbug refers to nonsense or something deceptive, or a person who is disliked. It can also be used as an exclamation to express disbelief or scorn.|例句：His story is pure humbug. 他的故事纯属胡说八道。

例句：That salesman is a real humbug. 那个推销员是个十足的骗子。


近义词：nonsense, rubbish, fraud, hoax, charlatan|反义词：truth, reality, honesty


词性: 名词
