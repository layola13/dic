词源：
The word "demountable" is derived from the verb "demount," which itself is a combination of the prefix "de-" (meaning "off" or "away") and the noun "mount" (meaning "to fix or attach").  Therefore, "demountable" literally means "capable of being taken apart" or "capable of being dismounted."
