fulcrums:/ˈfʌlkrʌmz/| n. |支点，枢纽|A fulcrum is a support on which a lever rests or is pivoted.  It's the point around which a lever rotates.  The fulcrum is crucial for leverage, allowing a small force to move a much larger object.|例句：The seesaw's fulcrum was worn down, making it unbalanced.  跷跷板的支点磨损了，导致它失去平衡。

The rock acted as a fulcrum for the lever. 这块石头充当了杠杆的支点。|近义词：pivot, support, bearing|反义词：N/A
