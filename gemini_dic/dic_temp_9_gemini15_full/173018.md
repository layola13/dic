quarks:/kwɔːrks/| n. |夸克|A quark is an elementary particle and a fundamental constituent of matter. Quarks combine to form composite particles called hadrons, the most stable of which are protons and neutrons, the components of atomic nuclei.|例句：Protons and neutrons are made up of three quarks each. 质子和中子各由三个夸克组成。|近义词：fundamental particle, elementary particle |反义词:None.
