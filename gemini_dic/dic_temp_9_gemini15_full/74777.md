freezing:/ˈfriːzɪŋ/ | adj. |极冷的，冰冻的 | Extremely cold; at or below the freezing point of water.  |例句：It's freezing outside; you'll need a coat.  外面冷极了，你需要穿件外套。
例句：The lake is freezing over. 湖面正在结冰。|近义词：icy, frigid, glacial |反义词：boiling, scorching, sweltering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather is freezing today. = 今天天气很冷。
It's freezing in here; can we turn up the heat? = 这里很冷，我们可以把暖气开大点吗？


一般过去时:
It froze last night. = 昨晚结冰了。
The pipes froze during the cold snap. = 在寒流期间管道冻住了。


现在完成时:

否定句:
