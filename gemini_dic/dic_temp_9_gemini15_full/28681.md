carjacker:/ˈkɑːrdʒækə(r)/| n. |汽车劫匪|A person who steals a car by force or threat of force.  Carjackers typically use weapons to intimidate their victims.|例句：The carjacker threatened the driver with a gun and stole his car. 汽车劫匪用枪威胁司机，抢走了他的车。

The police arrested the carjacker after a high-speed chase.  警方在高速追逐后逮捕了汽车劫匪。|近义词：thief, robber, highwayman|反义词：victim, car owner
