hotfix:/ˈhɑːtˌfɪks/ | n. | 热修复，紧急补丁 | A hotfix is a small software update that quickly fixes a critical bug or problem. It's typically released between major software updates and is designed to address urgent issues.  | Example:  The developers released a hotfix to address the security vulnerability.  开发人员发布了一个热修复程序来解决安全漏洞。 |近义词：patch, quick fix |反义词：major update, full release | 名词
