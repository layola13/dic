exoplanetary:/ˌɛksoʊˈplænɪtəri/| adj.|系外行星的；围绕其他恒星运转的行星的|Relating to or characteristic of exoplanets (planets orbiting stars other than the Sun).  The term combines "exo-" (meaning "outside" or "external") and "planetary."|例句：Exoplanetary research has revealed a surprising diversity of planetary systems. 系外行星的研究揭示了令人惊讶的行星系统多样性。
The discovery of exoplanetary systems has revolutionized our understanding of planet formation. 系外行星系统的发现彻底改变了我们对行星形成的理解。|近义词：extrasolar|反义词：solar
