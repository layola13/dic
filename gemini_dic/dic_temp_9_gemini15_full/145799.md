overdecorates:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəreɪts/ | vt. |过度装饰 |To decorate excessively or to a degree that is considered excessive or inappropriate.  It implies that the decoration overwhelms or detracts from the original object or space. |例句：She overdecorates her apartment with too many knick-knacks. = 她用太多的小摆设过度装饰了她的公寓。

The bakery overdecorates its cakes, making them look gaudy. = 面包店过度装饰蛋糕，使它们看起来俗气。


近义词：embellish excessively, overgild, overload (with decoration), adorn excessively |反义词：underdecorate, simplify, minimalist
