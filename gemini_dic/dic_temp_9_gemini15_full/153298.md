pencil-in: /ˈpɛnsəlɪn/ | vt. | 暂时安排；初步安排；预先安排 | To tentatively schedule or arrange something.  It suggests a less firm commitment than a definite arrangement. | 例句：I've pencilled in a meeting for next Tuesday, but it's not confirmed yet. 我初步安排了周二开会，但还没确定。

例句：Let's pencil in a time to discuss this further.  让我们初步安排一个时间进一步讨论此事。


近义词：tentatively schedule, provisionally arrange, tentatively book, schedule tentatively


反义词：firmly schedule, definitively arrange, confirm


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pencil in appointments for the whole week. = 我把整个星期的预约都初步安排好了。
We pencil in a meeting every Friday. = 我们每周五都初步安排一次会议。


一般过去时:
She pencilled in a vacation for December. = 她初步安排了十二月份的假期。
They pencilled in a trip to Europe. = 他们初步安排了一次欧洲之旅。


现在完成时:
I have pencilled in a meeting with the client next week. = 我已经初步安排了与客户下周的会议。
They have pencilled in several appointments this month. = 他们这个月已经初步安排了几次预约。


过去完成时:
He had pencilled in a golf game before the rain started. = 在下雨之前，他已经初步安排了一场高尔夫球赛。
We had pencilled in a dinner party before the unexpected guests arrived. = 在意外的客人到来之前，我们已经初步安排了一个晚宴。


将来时:
I will pencil in a call with my manager next week. = 我下周将会初步安排与我的经理通话。
We will pencil in some time for research. = 我们将初步安排一些时间进行研究。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pencil in a trip to the mountains. = 如果我有更多的时间，我就会初步安排一次去山上的旅行。
If I were you, I would pencil in a backup plan. = 如果我是你，我会初步安排一个备用计划。


被动语态:
A meeting was pencilled in for Friday afternoon. = 周五下午初步安排了一次会议。
The appointment was pencilled in for next Tuesday. = 下周二的预约已被初步安排。


疑问句:
Have you pencilled in a time to meet? = 你是否初步安排了见面时间？
Has she pencilled in a holiday yet? = 她是否已经初步安排了假期？


否定句:
I haven't pencilled in anything for next week yet. = 我下周还没有初步安排任何事情。
They didn't pencil in a meeting for today. = 他们今天没有初步安排会议。
