enrichers: /ɪnˈrɪtʃərz/ | n. | 丰富者；使富裕者 | Those who or that which enriches something or someone.  They can be individuals, organizations, experiences, or even ideas that add value, improve quality, or increase knowledge. | Example:  Educational programs are key enrichers of our society.  教育项目是我们社会的重要丰富者。|近义词: improvers, benefactors, enhancers | 反义词: impoverishers, depleters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new software enriches the user experience. = 新软件丰富了用户体验。
These experiences enrich our lives. = 这些经历丰富了我们的生活。


一般过去时:
The artist's work enriched the community. = 艺术家的作品丰富了社区。
Traveling enriched my perspective. = 旅行拓宽了我的视野。


现在完成时:
Their donations have enriched the museum's collection. = 他们的捐款丰富了博物馆的藏品。
This book has enriched my understanding of history. = 这本书加深了我对历史的理解。


过去完成时:
The previous administration had enriched the country's infrastructure. = 上届政府已经改善了国家的基础设施。
Years of study had enriched her knowledge. = 多年的学习丰富了她的知识。


将来时:
The new project will enrich the lives of many people. = 新项目将丰富许多人的生活。
This experience will enrich your resume. = 这段经历将使你的简历增色不少。


虚拟语气:
If we invested more, it would enrich the community. = 如果我们投入更多资金，它将会使社区更加富裕。
I wish my education had enriched my life more. = 我希望我的教育能更多地丰富我的生活。


被动语态:
The community was enriched by the artist's work. = 社区因艺术家的作品而更加丰富多彩。
Our lives are enriched by our relationships. = 我们的生活因人际关系而更加丰富。


疑问句:
What enriches your life the most? = 什么最能丰富你的生活？
How can we enrich the soil? = 我们如何改良土壤？


否定句:
The policy did not enrich the lives of the poor. = 这一政策并没有改善穷人的生活。
The experience didn't enrich her understanding of the subject. = 这段经历并没有加深她对该主题的理解。
