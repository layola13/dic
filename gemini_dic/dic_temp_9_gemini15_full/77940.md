genomes:/ˈdʒinoʊmz/| n.|基因组|The complete set of genes or genetic material present in a cell or organism.| 例句：Scientists are studying the genomes of various species to understand evolution. 科学家们正在研究各种物种的基因组以了解进化。|近义词：genome, karyotype|反义词:无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist studies the genomes of different plants. = 科学家研究不同植物的基因组。
We analyze the genomes for disease markers. = 我们分析基因组以寻找疾病标志物。


一般过去时:
Researchers sequenced the genomes of several bacteria. = 研究人员测序了几种细菌的基因组。
They compared the genomes to find similarities. = 他们比较基因组以寻找相似之处。


现在完成时:
Scientists have mapped the human genome. = 科学家已经绘制了人类基因组图谱。
Researchers have identified several genes within the genomes. = 研究人员已经鉴定了基因组中的几个基因。
