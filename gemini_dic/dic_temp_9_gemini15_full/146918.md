overintellectualizations: /ˌoʊvərɪntɛləktʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度知识化；过度思考；过度解释 | The act of overthinking or overanalyzing something, often to the point of obscuring the simple truth or practical solution.  It involves interpreting situations or ideas with excessive intellectual complexity, often unnecessarily. |例句：His overintellectualizations prevented him from enjoying the simple pleasures of life.  他的过度思考妨碍了他享受生活中的简单快乐。

His constant overintellectualizations of everyday occurrences made him seem aloof and out of touch. 他对日常事件的过度思考让他显得冷漠而脱离实际。


近义词：overanalysis, overthinking, overcomplication, intellectualizing,  sophistication (in a negative context) | 反义词：simplification, pragmatism, common sense


词性：名词
