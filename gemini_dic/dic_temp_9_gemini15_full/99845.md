intermixer:/ˈɪntərmɪksər/| n. |混合器，搅拌器|A device or person that mixes things together.  It can refer to a machine that blends ingredients or a person who facilitates interaction between different groups.|例句：The intermixer efficiently blended the flour and water.  混合器有效地混合了面粉和水。


例句：He acted as an intermixer, bringing together the opposing factions. 他充当调解人的角色，将对立的派系团结起来。|近义词：mixer, blender, combiner|反义词：separator, divider|名词
