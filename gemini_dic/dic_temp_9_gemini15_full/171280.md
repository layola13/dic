WORD ORIGIN->

From  "pterygo-" (wing-like, from Greek πτέρυξ (ptéryx) "wing") + "palatine" (relating to the palate, from Latin palatum "palate").  The term describes the anatomical location, referencing both the wing-like pterygoid process of the sphenoid bone and the palate.
