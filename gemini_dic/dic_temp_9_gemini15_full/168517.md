proboscidiform:/ˌproʊbɒsɪˈdɔːrm/| adj.|长鼻类的；象鼻类的|Of or relating to the Proboscidea, the order of mammals that includes elephants.  The term describes animals possessing a trunk-like proboscis.|Example:  Elephants are proboscidiform mammals.  象是长鼻类的哺乳动物。|近义词：elephantiform (less common)|反义词：无
