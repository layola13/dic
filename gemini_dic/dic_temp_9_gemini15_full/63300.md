enthusiasts:/ɪnˈθuːziæsts/| n. |狂热者，爱好者 |Someone who is very interested in a particular activity or subject.  |Example:  She's an enthusiast for vintage cars. 她是一个古董车的爱好者。

Example: The concert attracted many music enthusiasts. 这场音乐会吸引了许多音乐爱好者。|近义词：devotees, aficionados, fans |反义词：apathetic people, indifferent people


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an enthusiastic supporter of the team. 他是该队的狂热支持者。
They are enthusiasts for sustainable living. 他们是可持续生活的爱好者。


一般过去时:
She was an enthusiast for classical music. 她曾是古典音乐的爱好者。
They were enthusiastic participants in the marathon. 他们是马拉松比赛的热情参与者。


现在完成时:
He has been an enthusiast for bird watching for many years. 他多年来一直是观鸟爱好者。
She has become an enthusiast for yoga since last year. 从去年开始她就成了瑜伽爱好者。


过去完成时:
He had been an enthusiast for stamp collecting before he took up photography. 在他开始摄影之前，他一直热衷于集邮。
They had been enthusiastic supporters of the charity for years. 他们多年来一直是该慈善机构的热心支持者。


将来时:
She will be an enthusiastic participant in the upcoming conference. 她将成为即将举行的会议的热情参与者。
They will remain enthusiasts for this new technology. 他们将继续是这项新技术的爱好者。


虚拟语气:
If he were an enthusiast for hiking, he would join us. 如果他喜欢远足，他会加入我们的。
I wish I were an enthusiast for opera. 我希望我是一个歌剧爱好者。


被动语态:
The new product was promoted by enthusiasts on social media.  这款新产品由爱好者在社交媒体上推广。
The event was well-attended by enthusiastic supporters.  这次活动受到了热心支持者的欢迎。


疑问句:
Are you an enthusiast for any particular sport? 你热衷于任何特定的运动吗？
Is he an enthusiast for classic literature? 他是古典文学爱好者吗？


否定句:
He is not an enthusiast for reality television. 他不喜欢真人秀电视节目。
She was not an enthusiast for the new policy. 她并不热衷于这项新政策。



PHRASE->
enthusiast for: 对…的爱好者
avid enthusiast: 热情的爱好者

词源:
The word "enthusiast" comes from the Greek word "enthousiastes," which means "inspired by a god."  It later evolved to describe someone with passionate zeal or intense interest in something.
