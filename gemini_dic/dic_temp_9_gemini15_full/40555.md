controverting:/kənˈtrɒvɜːtɪŋ/| v.| 反驳；驳斥；争论 | To speak or write against; to oppose or deny; to dispute or debate.  |例句：He spent the entire debate controverting the opposition's claims. 他在整个辩论中都在反驳反对派的论点。 |例句：The scientist controverted the theory with compelling evidence. 科学家用令人信服的证据驳斥了这一理论。|近义词：refuting, disputing, contradicting, challenging |反义词：agreeing, supporting, confirming


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He controverts the new policy every chance he gets. = 他抓住任何机会都反驳这项新政策。
They controvert the findings of the study regularly. = 他们经常反驳这项研究的结果。


一般过去时:
She controverted his arguments effectively during the meeting. = 她在会议上有效地反驳了他的论点。
The lawyer controverted the witness's testimony. = 律师反驳了证人的证词。


现在完成时:
He has controverted the same arguments for years. = 多年来，他一直在反驳同样的论点。
Scientists have controverted the initial hypothesis with new data. = 科学家们用新的数据反驳了最初的假设。


过去完成时:
They had controverted the idea before the final decision was made. = 在做出最终决定之前，他们就已经反驳了这个想法。
She had controverted his claims several times before giving up. = 在放弃之前，她已经几次反驳了他的说法。


将来时:
He will controvert the proposal at the next board meeting. = 他将在下次董事会上反驳该提案。
They will controvert the decision in court. = 他们将在法庭上反驳这一决定。


虚拟语气:
If he controverted the evidence, he would be in trouble. = 如果他反驳证据，他就会有麻烦。
I wish he had controverted the misleading information earlier. = 我希望他早点反驳那些误导性信息。


被动语态:
The theory was controverted by many scientists. = 许多科学家都反驳了这一理论。
His arguments were controverted in the subsequent debate. = 他的论点在随后的辩论中被反驳了。


疑问句:
Does she controvert the conclusions of the research? = 她是否反驳了这项研究的结论？
Will they controvert the judge's ruling? = 他们会反驳法官的裁决吗？


否定句:
He did not controvert the evidence presented. = 他没有反驳提出的证据。
She did not controvert the claims made against her. = 她没有反驳针对她的指控。
