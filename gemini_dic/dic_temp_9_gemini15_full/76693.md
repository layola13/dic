galvanotropism:/ˌɡælˌvænəˈtroʊpɪzəm/ | n. | 向电性刺激弯曲的特性；电性向性 | The directional growth or turning of an organism in response to an electric current.  It's a type of tropism,  a biological phenomenon where an organism grows or turns in response to a stimulus.  In galvanotropism, the stimulus is an electric current. | Example:  The roots of the plant exhibited a clear galvanotropism, bending towards the positive electrode. 植物的根系表现出明显的电性向性，弯向正极。  The experiment demonstrated a strong galvanotropism in the algae. 实验表明藻类具有很强的电性向性。 | 近义词: electrotropism | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but the opposite would be a response away from the electric current)
