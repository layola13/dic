courier:/ˈkʊəriə(r)/| n. v.|信使；快递员；快递服务；|A courier is a person or company that delivers messages, packages, or documents.  |例句：I sent the documents by courier. 我通过快递公司寄送了文件。|例句：The courier arrived late. 快递员来晚了。|近义词：messenger, delivery person, express service |反义词：recipient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The courier delivers packages every day. = 快递员每天送包裹。
He works as a courier for a large company. = 他在一家大公司做快递员。


一般过去时:
The courier delivered the package yesterday. = 快递员昨天送来了包裹。
She worked as a courier before she got her current job. = 在她找到现在的工作之前，她做过快递员。


现在完成时:
The courier has already delivered all the packages. = 快递员已经送完所有包裹了。
He has been a courier for five years. = 他做快递员已经五年了。


过去完成时:
The package had already been delivered before I got home. = 我到家之前包裹已经被送到了。
The courier had left before I called. = 我打电话之前快递员已经离开了。


将来时:
The courier will deliver the package tomorrow. = 快递员明天会送来包裹。

虚拟语气:
If I had a courier, I would send the documents immediately. = 如果我有快递员，我会立即送文件。
I wish the courier had arrived earlier. = 我希望快递员早点到。


被动语态:
The package was delivered this morning. = 包裹今天早上送到了。

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "courier" comes from the Old French word "courrier," meaning "runner," "messenger," or "one who runs."  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "currere," meaning "to run."
