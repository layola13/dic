repatterned:/ˌriːˈpætnɚd/| adj.|重新模式化的；重新规划的|Having been changed in structure or form, especially to improve efficiency or effectiveness.  The system has been repatterned to be more user-friendly.|例句：The company repatterned its marketing strategy after a series of unsuccessful campaigns. 公司在连续几轮营销活动失败后重新规划了其营销策略。

例句：The software has been repatterned to fix the bugs and improve performance. 该软件经过重新设计，以修复错误并提高性能。


近义词：redesigned, restructured, reorganized, reformed

反义词：unchanged, unaltered, unmodified

词性：形容词
