duel's:/ˈduːəlz/| n.|决斗；竞争|A duel is a prearranged fight between two people, typically using weapons.  It often symbolizes a contest of honor or revenge. |例句：The two men fought a duel at dawn.  = 黎明时分，两人决斗。

The famous duel between Hamilton and Burr had tragic consequences. = 汉密尔顿和伯尔之间的著名决斗带来了悲惨的后果。|近义词：fight, combat, contest |反义词：peace, agreement
