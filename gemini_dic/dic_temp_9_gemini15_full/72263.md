flosculous: /flɒˈskjʊləs/ | adj. | 花状的；小花状的 |  Having many small flowers clustered together, resembling a floret or small flower.  The term often describes inflorescences where numerous small flowers form a dense cluster. | Example: The flosculous inflorescence of the sunflower is a striking example of botanical design. 向日葵的花序是植物设计的突出例子。| 近义词：floriferous,  flowery (in the sense of abundant flowers) | 反义词：solitary (referring to single flowers)
