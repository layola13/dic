pugnaciousness:/pʌɡˈneɪʃəsnəs/| n. |好斗；好战；好争论 |The state or quality of being pugnacious; a disposition to fight or quarrel readily.  It refers to a person's aggressive and combative nature, a tendency to readily engage in arguments or fights. |例句：His pugnaciousness often got him into trouble. 他的好斗常常给他惹麻烦。  The pugnaciousness of the debate surprised the moderator.  辩论的好战性让主持人吃了一惊。|近义词：combativeness, aggressiveness, belligerence, contentiousness |反义词：peacefulness, docility, submissiveness, meekness
