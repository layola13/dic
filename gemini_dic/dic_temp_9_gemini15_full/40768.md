convincednesses:/kənˈvɪnstnəsɪz/| n. |确信的状态；确信的程度|The plural form of convincedness, referring to multiple instances or levels of being convinced.  It describes the states or degrees of certainty or belief.  This word is rarely used in everyday conversation and is considered somewhat awkward.|例句：The varying convincednesses of the jury members led to a hung jury.  陪审员们不同的确信程度导致了陪审团意见不一。  The professor noted the wide range of convincednesses among his students regarding the validity of the theory. 教授注意到他的学生们对该理论有效性的确信程度差异很大。|近义词：certainties, assurances, convictions|反义词：doubts, uncertainties, hesitations|名词
