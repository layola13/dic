omander:/əˈmændər/| n.|指挥官，司令官|A commander is a person who is in charge of a military unit or group.|例句：The commander led his troops into battle. 指挥官率领他的部队投入战斗。|近义词：leader, officer, chief|反义词:subordinate, follower

The commander gives orders. = 指挥官下达命令。
The commander plans the strategy. = 指挥官计划战略。

The commander issued a command. = 指挥官发布了一项命令。
