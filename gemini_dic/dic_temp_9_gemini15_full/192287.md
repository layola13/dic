screw-up:/skruː ˈʌp/| n. vt.|搞砸，错误，失败|a mistake, a blunder, or a failure.|例句：His screw-up cost the company thousands of dollars.他的错误给公司造成了数千美元的损失。|近义词：mistake, blunder, error, mess-up|反义词：success, triumph, achievement|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He screws up his work frequently. = 他经常把工作搞砸。
They screw up the project again and again. = 他们一次又一次地搞砸了这个项目。


一般过去时:
I screwed up the interview badly. = 我把面试搞砸了。
She screwed up the presentation last night. = 她昨晚把演示搞砸了。


现在完成时:
He has screwed up many times this year. = 今年他已经搞砸过很多次了。
We have screwed up the deadline for the report. = 我们错过了报告的截止日期。


过去完成时:
They had screwed up the plan before I joined the team. = 我加入团队之前，他们就已经把计划搞砸了。
She had screwed up her chances of promotion. = 她已经错过了晋升的机会。


将来时:
I think he will screw up the exam. = 我认为他会考试不及格。
They will screw up the whole thing if they are not careful. = 如果他们不小心，他们会把一切都搞砸。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I wouldn't have screwed up the test. = 如果我学习更努力，我就不会考试不及格了。
I wish I hadn't screwed up the opportunity. = 我真希望我没有错过这个机会。


被动语态:
The project was screwed up by the lack of communication. = 项目由于缺乏沟通而失败了。
The experiment was screwed up due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，实验失败了。


疑问句:
Did you screw up the assignment? = 你把作业搞砸了吗？
Have they screwed up the deal? = 他们把交易搞砸了吗？


否定句:
He didn't screw up the speech. = 他没有把演讲搞砸。
She didn't screw up the cooking. = 她没有把饭菜做砸。


PHRASE->
a real screw-up = 一个真正的失败
a major screw-up = 一个重大的错误
make a screw-up = 犯错
avoid a screw-up = 避免犯错
one big screw-up = 一个大错误
