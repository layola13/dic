countermand:/ˈkaʊntərmænd/| vt.|撤销，取消（命令）；反令|To cancel or reverse an order or instruction.  The general countermanded the order to attack. 将军撤销了进攻命令。|近义词：revoke, annul, rescind, recall|反义词：affirm, confirm, ratify|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The general countermands the order. = 将军撤销了命令。
He countermands his previous instructions. = 他撤销了他之前的指示。


一般过去时:
The officer countermanded the captain's order. = 军官撤销了上尉的命令。
She countermanded the earlier decision. = 她撤销了之前的决定。


现在完成时:
They have countermanded the evacuation order. = 他们已经撤销了疏散令。
The CEO has countermanded the merger proposal. = 首席执行官已经撤销了合并提案。


过去完成时:
He had countermanded the troops' movement before the enemy attacked. = 在敌人进攻之前，他已经下令部队停止行动。
She had countermanded the shipment after discovering a defect. = 在发现缺陷后，她已经下令停止发货。


将来时:
The commander will countermand the advance if the weather worsens. = 如果天气恶化，指挥官将下令停止前进。
They will countermand the order if necessary. = 必要时，他们将撤销命令。


虚拟语气:
If the situation changed, the President would countermand the executive order. = 如果情况改变，总统将撤销行政命令。
I wish he had countermanded the decision earlier. = 我希望他早点撤销这个决定。


被动语态:
The order was countermanded by the superior officer. = 命令被上级军官撤销了。
The plan was countermanded due to unforeseen circumstances. = 计划由于不可预见的情况而被取消。


疑问句:
Will the order be countermanded? = 命令会被撤销吗？
Has the previous instruction been countermanded? = 之前的指示被撤销了吗？


否定句:
The officer did not countermand the order. = 军官没有撤销命令。
They will not countermand the decision under any circumstances. = 无论如何，他们都不会撤销这个决定。


PHRASE->
countermand an order = 撤销命令
