conjecture:/kənˈdʒɛktʃər/ | n. | 推测；猜想 | A conjecture is an opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information.  It is an educated guess, often based on some evidence but not fully proven. |  The scientist's conjecture about the origin of the universe was met with skepticism. 科学家关于宇宙起源的推测受到了怀疑。

The police formed a conjecture about the suspect's whereabouts based on circumstantial evidence. 警方根据间接证据对嫌疑人的下落做出了推测。| hypothesis, speculation, supposition | certainty, fact |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
His conjecture often proves to be correct. = 他的推测经常被证明是正确的。
Her conjecture is based on limited data. = 她的推测是基于有限的数据。


一般过去时:
They conjectured that the earthquake caused the tsunami. = 他们推测地震引发了海啸。
The detective conjectured the motive behind the crime. = 侦探推测了犯罪背后的动机。


现在完成时:
Scientists have conjectured about the existence of dark matter for decades. = 几十年来，科学家们一直在推测暗物质的存在。
She has conjectured that the painting is a forgery. = 她推测这幅画是赝品。


过去完成时:
They had conjectured that the ship had sunk. = 他们曾推测那艘船沉没了。
Historians had conjectured about the lost city for centuries. = 史学家们几个世纪以来一直在推测失落的城市。


将来时:
Experts will conjecture about the election results. = 专家们将推测选举结果。
We will conjecture on the likely outcome. = 我们将推测可能的结果。


虚拟语气:
If I were to conjecture, I'd say he's lying. = 如果我要推测的话，我会说他在撒谎。
I conjectured that, if I were in his position, I would have acted differently. = 我推测如果我处于他的位置，我会采取不同的行动。


被动语态:
It was conjectured that the disease was caused by a virus. = 人们推测这种疾病是由病毒引起的。
The cause of the accident was conjectured to be driver error. = 事故的原因被推测为驾驶员错误。


疑问句:
What do you conjecture about the future? = 你对未来有什么推测？
What are they conjecturing about the cause of the fire? = 他们对火灾的原因有什么推测？


否定句:
I don't conjecture about things I don't understand. = 我不会推测我不理解的事情。
They didn't conjecture that the situation would escalate so quickly. = 他们没有推测到情况会这么快升级。
