gulpingly:/ˈɡʌlpiŋli/| adv.| 吞咽地；急促地；狼吞虎咽地 | In a manner suggesting gulping; hastily and greedily.  |例句：He gulped down the water gulpingly. 他狼吞虎咽地喝下水。

He ate the food gulpingly, as if he hadn't eaten in days. 他狼吞虎咽地吃着食物，好像好几天没吃东西了。|近义词：hastily, greedily, ravenously |反义词：leisurely, slowly, delicately |词性: 副词
