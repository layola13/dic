method's:/ˈmɛθədz/| n. |方法的；办法的 |The possessive form of "method," indicating belonging or relation to a method.  It's used to show ownership or connection. |Example:  The project's success depends on the method's effectiveness.  这个项目的成功取决于方法的有效性。|近义词：approach's, technique's, procedure's |反义词：None (possessive forms don't have direct antonyms) |名词
