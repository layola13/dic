glean:/ɡliːn/| vt.|收集，拾穗，一点一点地收集|To gather (something, especially information or material) little by little; to collect gradually.|She gleaned information from various sources. 她从各种来源收集信息。|The farmer gleaned the fields after the harvest. 农民收割后在田里拾穗。|gather, collect, harvest|waste, squander


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gleans information from books. = 他从书本中收集信息。
Farmers glean the leftover crops after harvest. = 农民在收割后收集剩余的庄稼。


一般过去时:
She gleaned valuable insights from the meeting. = 她从会议中收集到宝贵的见解。
The researchers gleaned data from multiple experiments. = 研究人员从多个实验中收集数据。


现在完成时:
They have gleaned much wisdom from their experiences. = 他们从经验中积累了很多智慧。
I have gleaned enough information to write my report. = 我已经收集到足够的信息来写我的报告。


过去完成时:
She had gleaned all the necessary information before starting her project. = 在开始她的项目之前，她已经收集了所有必要的信息。
By the end of the day, they had gleaned little useful data. = 到一天结束时，他们几乎没有收集到有用的数据。


将来时:
We will glean useful data from the upcoming survey. = 我们将从即将进行的调查中收集有用的数据。
Scientists expect to glean new discoveries from the research. = 科学家们期望从这项研究中获得新的发现。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would glean more information about this topic. = 如果我有时间，我会收集更多关于这个主题的信息。
I wish I could glean more insights from the article. = 我希望我能从这篇文章中获得更多见解。


被动语态:
The information was gleaned from confidential documents. = 信息是从机密文件中收集的。
Useful data can be gleaned from various sources. = 有用的数据可以从各种来源收集。


疑问句:
What can we glean from this research? = 我们能从这项研究中得到什么？
What information did you glean from the interview? = 你从采访中收集到了什么信息？


否定句:
They failed to glean any useful information from the report. = 他们未能从报告中收集到任何有用的信息。
We couldn't glean enough evidence to support our theory. = 我们无法收集到足够的证据来支持我们的理论。
