fine:/faɪn/| adj.|好的；美好的；健康的；精细的；纯粹的 |Excellent, satisfactory, or acceptable in quality, condition, or appearance.  |例句：The weather is fine today. 今天天气很好。|例句：She's fine, thank you. 她很好，谢谢你。|近义词：good, excellent, superb, satisfactory, delicate|反义词：bad, poor, awful, terrible, coarse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cake is fine. = 蛋糕很好。
The weather is fine today. = 今天天气很好。

一般过去时:
The movie was fine. = 电影不错。
Everything was fine until yesterday. = 直到昨天一切都很好。


现在完成时:
Everything has been fine since then. = 从那时起一切都很好。
I have been fine since my recovery. = 我康复后一直很好。


过去完成时:
Everything had been fine before the accident. = 事故发生前一切都很好。
I had been fine until I caught a cold. = 直到我感冒之前我一切都很好。


将来时:
I think everything will be fine. = 我想一切都会好起来的。
The weather will be fine tomorrow. = 明天天气会很好。


虚拟语气:
It would be fine if you could help me. = 如果你能帮我，那就太好了。
I wish everything were fine. = 我希望一切顺利。


被动语态:
The details are fine-tuned. = 细节经过了微调。
The matter was handled fine. = 这件事处理得很好。


疑问句:
Is everything fine? = 一切都好吗？
Are you fine? = 你还好吗？


否定句:
The work is not fine enough. = 这工作做得不够好。
I don't feel fine today. = 我今天感觉不舒服。


PHRASE->
fine art: 美术
fine print: 小字部分；细则
fine line: 微妙的界限
in fine: 总之；简而言之
fine-tune: 微调
