dissents:/dɪˈsent/| v.|不同意，异议|To disagree with a decision or opinion, especially a formal one.  |The judge dissented from the majority opinion.  法官对多数意见表示异议。|The dissenting members of the committee voted against the proposal. 委员会中持异议的成员投票反对该提案。|agree, object|conform, concur|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dissents from the committee's decision. = 他不同意委员会的决定。
She dissents on many issues. = 她对很多问题都持异议。

一般过去时:
The judge dissented from the jury's verdict. = 法官对陪审团的裁决表示异议。
He dissented from the majority opinion. = 他对多数意见表示异议。

现在完成时:
The council member has dissented on this matter for years. = 这位议员多年来一直对这件事表示异议。
They have dissented from the party line on several occasions. = 他们曾多次与党的路线相左。

过去完成时:
He had dissented from the plan before it was finalized. = 在计划最终确定之前，他就已经表示反对了。
She had dissented on similar issues in the past. = 她过去也对类似问题表示过异议。

将来时:
He will dissent from the ruling if he believes it is unjust. = 如果他认为判决不公正，他会表示异议。
They will likely dissent from the proposed changes. = 他们很可能会反对拟议的更改。

虚拟语气:
If he dissented, he would have to explain his reasoning. = 如果他表示异议，他将不得不解释他的理由。
I wish he hadn't dissented so publicly. = 我真希望他没有公开表示异议。

被动语态:
His opinion was dissented from by many. = 许多人对他的意见表示异议。
The proposal was dissented from by several members. = 几位成员对该提案表示异议。

疑问句:
Does he dissent from the ruling? = 他对裁决有异议吗？
Did they dissent from the initial proposal? = 他们对最初的提议有异议吗？

否定句:
He did not dissent from the decision. = 他没有对该决定表示异议。
She did not dissent from the majority view. = 她没有反对多数人的观点。


PHRASE->
dissent from = 对…表示异议
dissent against = 反对
dissent on = 就…表示异议
a dissenting opinion = 异议意见
dissent and debate = 异议和辩论
