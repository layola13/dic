hottest:/ˈhɒtɪst/ /ˈhɑːtɪst/| adj.|最热的；最辣的；最热门的 | The superlative form of "hot," indicating the highest degree of heat, spiciness, or popularity.  It can describe temperature, food spiciness, or trends. |例句：This summer is the hottest on record. 今年夏天是有记录以来最热的一年。

例句：This chili is the hottest I've ever tasted.  这个辣椒是我吃过最辣的。


近义词：most scorching, most intense, most popular, most sought-after

反义词：coldest, coolest, least popular


词性:形容词
