dongles:/ˈdɒŋɡəlz/ | n. |小型适配器；小型连接器 | A small device that plugs into a port to connect a peripheral device, such as a USB flash drive or a printer, to a computer or other device.  | Example: I need a dongle to connect my printer to the laptop. 我需要一个适配器来连接我的打印机到笔记本电脑。|近义词：adapter, connector |反义词： N/A
