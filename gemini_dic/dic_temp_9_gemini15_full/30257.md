celsius-scale:/ˈsɛlsiəs skeɪl/| n. |摄氏温标|The Celsius scale is a temperature scale where 0 degrees is the freezing point of water and 100 degrees is the boiling point of water at standard atmospheric pressure.  It's named after Anders Celsius, a Swedish astronomer who developed the scale.|Example: The temperature today is 25 degrees Celsius. 今天的温度是摄氏25度。  The water boiled at 100 degrees Celsius. 水在摄氏100度沸腾。|近义词：centigrade scale|反义词：Fahrenheit scale, Kelvin scale|名词
