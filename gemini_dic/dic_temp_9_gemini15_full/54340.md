dishes:/ˈdɪʃɪz/ | n. | 餐具；菜肴；盘子 | Dishes are typically made of ceramic, glass, or metal and are used for serving and eating food.  |  We need to wash the dishes after dinner.  我们晚饭后需要洗碗。  The restaurant is famous for its delicious dishes. 这家餐馆以其美味的菜肴而闻名。 | plates, crockery, tableware |  (无明确反义词，可考虑cleanliness) | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She washes the dishes every night. = 她每天晚上都洗碗。
They often order different dishes from the menu. = 他们经常点菜单上不同的菜。


一般过去时:
I washed the dishes yesterday. = 我昨天洗了碗。
We enjoyed many delicious dishes at the party. = 我们在派对上享用了许多美味佳肴。


现在完成时:
She has already washed the dishes. = 她已经洗好碗了。
Have you tried all the dishes on the menu? = 你试过菜单上的所有菜了吗？


过去完成时:
I had already finished the dishes before they arrived. = 他们到达之前我已经洗好碗了。
They had prepared many elaborate dishes for the banquet. = 他们为宴会准备了许多精致的菜肴。


将来时:
I will wash the dishes later. = 我晚点会洗碗。
They will be serving a variety of international dishes at the festival. = 节日上将供应各种国际菜肴。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would cook more interesting dishes. = 如果我有更多时间，我会做更多有趣的菜。
I wish I could cook as many delicious dishes as my mother. = 我希望我能像我母亲一样做很多美味的菜。


被动语态:
The dishes were washed by the dishwasher. = 碗是由洗碗机洗的。
Many dishes are served in this restaurant. = 这家餐馆供应许多菜肴。


疑问句:
What kind of dishes do you like? = 你喜欢什么类型的菜？
Whose dishes are these? = 这些是谁的碗？


否定句:
I didn't wash the dishes today. = 我今天没有洗碗。
They don't serve many vegetarian dishes. = 他们不供应很多素菜。


PHRASE->

do the dishes = 洗碗
a pile of dishes = 一堆碗盘
a course of dishes = 一道菜
wash the dishes = 洗碗
set the dishes = 摆放餐具

词源:

源自古法语 "deschis" 和古英语 "disc",  都意为"盘子"。  演变至今，含义扩展到包括所有用于盛放食物的器皿，以及菜肴本身。
