implosive:/ɪmˈploʊsɪv/ | adj., n. | 向内爆破的；内爆；内爆音 |  Relating to or denoting a sound produced by an inward rush of air, as in some clicks and some sounds in some languages.  As a noun, it refers to such a sound or an event where something collapses inward. | The building experienced a catastrophic implosive collapse.  The Xhosa language uses several implosive consonants. |  inward collapsing, collapsing | explosive | adj., n.
