landstorm:/ˈlændˌstɔːrm/ | n. |沙暴，尘暴 |A landstorm is a violent storm of wind and sand or dust, especially in a desert.  It is characterized by strong winds that lift and transport large quantities of sand or dust, reducing visibility and creating hazardous conditions. |例句：The landstorm reduced visibility to near zero. 沙暴导致能见度几乎为零。

例句：The caravan was forced to halt due to the intense landstorm.  商队被迫因猛烈的沙暴而停止前进。


近义词：sandstorm, dust storm, haboob


反义词：calm, stillness


词性：名词
