gymnosophist:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈsɒfɪst/| n. |裸体哲学家；禁欲主义者；|A gymnosophist was a member of an ancient Indian philosophical sect, known for their ascetic lifestyle, often involving nudity and extreme self-discipline.  They were known for their wisdom and philosophical discussions. The term is often used to refer to any ascetic or wise person, but its original meaning is tied to this specific group.|The ancient Greeks encountered gymnosophists and described their practices in detail. |近义词：ascetic, hermit, sage |反义词：hedonist, sensualist
