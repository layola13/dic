rapt:/ræpt/| adj.|全神贯注的；着迷的；被迷住的 | completely fascinated by something; giving it all one's attention.  |例句：She was rapt in her book, oblivious to the world around her. 她全神贯注地看书，对周围的世界一无所知。|例句：The audience was rapt by the singer's performance. 观众被歌手的表演迷住了。|近义词：engrossed, absorbed, captivated, enthralled |反义词：distracted, uninterested, bored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is rapt in thought. = 他全神贯注地思考着。
The children are rapt with excitement. = 孩子们兴奋极了。

一般过去时:
She was rapt with delight at the news. = 她听到这个消息欣喜若狂。
The audience was rapt by the magician's performance. = 观众被魔术师的表演迷住了。

现在完成时:
I have been rapt in this project for weeks. = 我已经全神贯注于这个项目几周了。
They have been rapt listeners to the speaker. = 他们一直专心致志地听着演讲者讲话。

过去完成时:
She had been rapt in conversation before the phone rang. = 电话响之前，她一直在全神贯注地交谈。
He had been rapt in a book all afternoon. = 他整个下午都全神贯注地看书。


将来时:
I will be rapt with joy if I win the lottery. = 如果我中了彩票，我会欣喜若狂。
The crowd will be rapt with anticipation for the concert. = 观众们将满怀期待地等待音乐会。

虚拟语气:
If I were rapt in the work, I would finish it sooner. = 如果我全神贯注地工作，我会更快完成。
I wish I had been rapt in my studies instead of playing games. = 我真希望当时我专心学习而不是玩游戏。


被动语态:
The audience was easily rapt by his speech. = 观众很容易被他演讲所吸引。
The children were rapt by the storyteller's tales. = 孩子们被讲故事的人的故事迷住了。


疑问句:
Was she rapt in her work? = 她当时专心工作吗？
Are the students rapt with attention? = 学生们专心听讲吗？


否定句:
He was not rapt by the movie. = 他对这部电影并不着迷。
The children were not rapt by the teacher's lesson. = 孩子们对老师的课不感兴趣。
