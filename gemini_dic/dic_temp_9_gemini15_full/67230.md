expropriating: /ɪkˈsprɒprɪeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 征用；没收；剥夺 | The act of taking possession of something, especially property, by the state or other authority, often without the owner's consent or compensation.  | The government is expropriating land for the construction of a new highway. 政府正在征用土地修建一条新的高速公路。 | confiscating, seizing, appropriating | restoring, returning, compensating | 动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government expropriates land for public projects. = 政府征用土地用于公共项目。
They are expropriating the farmer's land to build a dam. = 他们正在征用农民的土地来修建水坝。

一般过去时:
The king expropriated the temple's wealth. = 国王没收了寺庙的财富。
The company expropriated the mineral rights from the local community. = 公司从当地社区征用了矿产权利。

现在完成时:
The state has expropriated much of the farmland. = 国家已经征用了大部分农田。
They have expropriated the property without compensation. = 他们未经赔偿就征用了该房产。

过去完成时:
The revolutionaries had expropriated the factories before the government could intervene. = 革命者在政府干预之前已经没收了工厂。
The landlord had expropriated the tenant's belongings before they could escape. = 房东在租户逃跑前已经没收了他们的财物。

将来时:
The council will expropriates the derelict building. = 理事会将征用这栋废弃的建筑物。
The government will likely expropriate more land for the new airport. = 政府很可能会为新机场征用更多土地。

虚拟语气:
If the government expropriated the land, they would face strong opposition. = 如果政府征用土地，他们将面临强烈的反对。
I wish they hadn't expropriated my family's ancestral home. = 我真希望他们没有没收我家祖传的房子。

被动语态:
The land was expropriated for a new railway line. = 这块土地被征用用于修建一条新的铁路。
The factory was expropriated by the rebels. = 工厂被叛军征用。

疑问句:
Will the government be expropriating any more properties? = 政府还会征用更多房产吗？
Has the company been expropriated from the land? = 这家公司已经被征用土地了吗？

否定句:
The government did not expropriate the land without offering compensation. = 政府在没有提供赔偿的情况下没有征用土地。
They will not expropriate private property without due process. = 他们不会在没有正当程序的情况下征用私有财产。
