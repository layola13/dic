selling:/ˈsɛlɪŋ/| v.|销售，出售，推销|The act of selling goods or services in exchange for money or other valuable consideration.  It involves persuading customers to buy products or services. |例句：He's selling his car. 他正在卖他的车。|例句：The company is selling a new line of products. 公司正在销售一系列新产品。|近义词：marketing, merchandising, peddling, hawking|反义词：buying, purchasing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are selling their house. = 他们在卖他们的房子。
We sell organic produce at the farmer's market. = 我们在农贸市场销售有机农产品。


一般过去时:
She sold her old bicycle last week. = 她上周卖掉了她的旧自行车。
They sold out of tickets within an hour. = 他们在一小时内就把票卖光了。


现在完成时:
We have sold over 1000 units this month. = 我们这个月已经卖出了超过1000个单位。
He has been selling insurance for twenty years. = 他卖保险已经二十年了。


过去完成时:
They had already sold all the cakes before we arrived. =在我们到达之前，他们已经把所有的蛋糕都卖掉了。
The company had sold its subsidiary before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经出售了其子公司。


将来时:
We will be selling our products at the trade show. = 我们将在贸易展销会上销售我们的产品。
They will sell the property next month. = 他们下个月将出售该房产。


虚拟语气:
If I were selling this product, I would emphasize its durability. = 如果我在卖这个产品，我会强调它的耐用性。
I wish I were selling more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地推销。


被动语态:
The tickets are being sold rapidly. = 票正在迅速售罄。
Many houses are being sold at below market value. = 许多房屋正以低于市场价值的价格出售。


疑问句:
Are you selling this car? = 你在卖这辆车吗？
What are they selling at the store today? = 他们今天在商店里卖什么？


否定句:
We are not selling that model anymore. = 我们不再销售那个型号了。
They didn't sell any of their artwork at the auction. = 他们在拍卖会上没有卖出任何艺术品。


PHRASE->
selling point: 卖点
selling price: 售价
selling strategy: 销售策略
selling season: 销售旺季
best-selling: 畅销的
