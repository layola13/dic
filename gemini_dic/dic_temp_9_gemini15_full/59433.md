earthquaked:/ˈɜːθkweɪkt/| v.|地震；发生地震|The building earthquaked violently during the tremor. 建筑物在地震中剧烈震动。|近义词：trembled, shook, quaked|反义词：remained still, stood firm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ground earthquakes rarely in this region.  = 这个地区很少发生地震。
The building earthquakes slightly every time a heavy truck passes. = 每当重型卡车经过时，建筑物都会轻微震动。


一般过去时:
The city earthquaked last night. = 昨晚城市发生了地震。
The old house earthquaked so violently that it collapsed. = 老房子剧烈震动，以至于倒塌了。


现在完成时:
The region has earthquaked several times this year. = 今年该地区已经发生过几次地震。
The building has earthquaked so many times that it's structurally unsound. = 这座建筑物已经发生过多次地震，因此结构不稳定。


过去完成时:
The villagers had earthquaked before, so they knew what to do. = 村民们以前经历过地震，所以他们知道该怎么做。
The scientist had earthquaked many times during his research career. = 科学家在他研究生涯中经历过多次地震。


将来时:
The seismologist predicts that the area will earthquake again soon. = 地震学家预测该地区很快会再次发生地震。
If the fault line shifts, the city will earthquake dramatically. = 如果断层移动，这座城市将发生剧烈的地震。


虚拟语气:
If the earthquake had earthquaked less violently, fewer buildings would have collapsed. = 如果地震震动较小，倒塌的建筑物就会更少。
I wish the ground hadn't earthquaked so fiercely. = 我真希望地面没有那么剧烈地震动。


被动语态:

The old bridge was earthquaked beyond repair. = 那座旧桥在地震中震坏了，无法修复。(被动语态中earthquaked使用较少，通常用"was damaged by an earthquake"等表达)
