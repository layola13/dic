concordancing:/kənˈkɔːrdənsiŋ/ | n. | 索引；检索；词语搭配分析 | The process of identifying and analyzing the occurrences of a word or phrase in a text corpus to understand its usage and context.  Concordance software helps researchers study linguistic patterns. | Example:  The concordancing of "happy" revealed its frequent association with words like "joyful" and "cheerful". =  对“快乐”一词的索引分析揭示了它与“快乐的”和“愉快的”等词语的频繁搭配。|近义词: collocation analysis, keyword analysis | 反义词:  N/A | 名词
