defragmentations:/ˌdiːˌfræɡmənˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 磁盘碎片整理；碎片化 | The process of reorganizing data on a hard disk to improve performance.  It involves moving fragmented files to contiguous locations. | Example: Regular defragmentations are recommended to maintain optimal hard drive speed. 定期进行磁盘碎片整理以保持最佳硬盘速度。 |近义词：disk optimization, data consolidation | 反义词：fragmentation | 词性: 名词
