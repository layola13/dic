cradling:/ˈkreɪd(ə)lɪŋ/ | v. |轻抱；轻轻摇晃；支撑；呵护 |Holding gently and protectively, often in one's arms; supporting or nurturing something. |例句：She was cradling her baby in her arms. 她轻轻地抱着她的婴儿。|例句：The mountains cradled the small village. 群山环抱着这个小山村。|近义词：holding, supporting, nurturing, cherishing |反义词：rejecting, abandoning, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She cradles her baby every night. = 她每晚都抱着她的婴儿。
He cradles the fragile glass carefully. = 他小心翼翼地托着易碎的玻璃杯。


一般过去时:
The mother cradled her sick child. = 母亲抱着她生病的孩子。
He carefully cradled the injured bird. = 他小心地抱着受伤的小鸟。


现在完成时:
She has been cradling her newborn for hours. = 她已经抱着新生儿好几个小时了。
The farmer has cradled his crops throughout the season. = 农民整个季节都在精心照料他的庄稼。


过去完成时:
She had cradled the kitten all night before it finally settled down. = 小猫最终平静下来之前，她整晚都在抱着它。
The artist had cradled the sculpture for weeks before unveiling it. = 艺术家在展出雕塑之前呵护了它几周。


将来时:
She will cradle her baby to sleep tonight. = 她今晚会抱着婴儿哄他睡觉。
They will cradle the injured animal until help arrives. = 他们会抱着受伤的动物直到救援到来。


虚拟语气:
If I had a baby, I would cradle it gently. = 如果我有婴儿，我会轻轻地抱着它。
If she were here, she would be cradling her grandchild. = 如果她在，她会抱着她的孙子。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
