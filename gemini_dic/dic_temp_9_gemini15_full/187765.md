ropers:/ˈroʊpərz/ | n. | 绳索工，套索手，(尤指)牛仔 | People who work with ropes, especially in cattle herding.  | The ropers skillfully lassoed the runaway calf.  套索手熟练地套住了逃跑的小牛。 | cowboys, wranglers |  (无明确反义词，可考虑 unskilled laborers) | n.
