refereeing: /ˌrɛfəˈriːɪŋ/ | v. | 裁判；仲裁；当裁判 | The act of acting as a referee, making judgments, or settling disputes.  |例句：He is refereeing the boxing match. 他正在担任拳击比赛的裁判。

例句：The committee is refereeing the dispute between the two companies.  委员会正在仲裁两家公司之间的纠纷。


近义词：judging, arbitrating, mediating


反义词：participating (in a competitive sense), ignoring


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He referees football matches every weekend. = 他每个周末都担任足球比赛的裁判。
She is refereeing the debate tonight. = 她今晚正在担任辩论赛的裁判。


一般过去时:
He refereed the final game of the season. = 他执法了本赛季的决赛。
She refereed a contentious match last week. = 她上周执法了一场有争议的比赛。


现在完成时:
He has refereed many important matches in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中执法过许多重要的比赛。
She has never refereed a professional game before. = 她以前从未执法过职业比赛。


过去完成时:
He had refereed several matches before he became famous. = 他成名之前已经执法过几场比赛了。
She had never refereed a game of that magnitude before. = 她以前从未执法过那样规模的比赛。


将来时:
He will referee the championship game next month. = 他下个月将执法冠军赛。
She will be refereeing the regional finals. = 她将执法地区决赛。


虚拟语气:
If he were refereeing the game, the result might be different. = 如果他来执法这场比赛，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish I were refereeing that crucial match. = 我真希望我能执法那场关键的比赛。


被动语态:
The match was refereed fairly. = 比赛的裁判工作很公平。
The dispute is being refereed by a neutral party. = 纠纷正在由中立方进行仲裁。


疑问句:
Who is refereeing the game tonight? = 今晚谁来执法比赛？
Is anyone refereeing the debate? = 有人担任辩论赛的裁判吗？


否定句:
He didn't referee the match because he was ill. = 他因为生病而没有执法比赛。
She wasn't refereeing the final due to a scheduling conflict. = 由于时间冲突，她没有执法决赛。



PHRASE->
refereeing a match = 执法一场比赛
refereeing a dispute = 仲裁一场争端
refereeing a debate = 担任辩论赛的裁判
chief refereeing = 首席裁判
refereeing decisions = 裁判的判决


词源:  Derived from "referee," which ultimately comes from the French "référer," meaning "to refer" or "to submit to."  The sense of settling disputes evolved from the act of referring a matter to someone for judgment.
