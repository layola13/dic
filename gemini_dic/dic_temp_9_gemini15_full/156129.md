photosynthesis:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈsɪnθəsɪs/| n. |光合作用|The process by which green plants and some other organisms use sunlight to synthesize foods from carbon dioxide and water.  Photosynthesis in plants generally involves the green pigment chlorophyll and generates oxygen as a byproduct.|例句：Plants use photosynthesis to convert sunlight into energy. 植物利用光合作用将阳光转化为能量。

Plants rely on photosynthesis for their survival. 植物依靠光合作用生存。


近义词：photosynthetic process|反义词：cellular respiration
