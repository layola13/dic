kiwi:/ˈkiːwiː/ | n. |几维鸟；猕猴桃 |A small flightless bird native to New Zealand, known for its brown fuzzy feathers and long beak.  Also refers to the fuzzy, brown fruit of a vine also native to New Zealand. |例句：The kiwi is New Zealand's national symbol.  几维鸟是新西兰的国鸟。

例句：I bought some delicious kiwifruit at the market. 我在市场上买了一些美味的猕猴桃。

|近义词： (for the bird) apteryx; (for the fruit)  kiwifruit |反义词：无 |词性：名词
