misrule:/ˈmɪsruːl/| n. v.| 暴政；坏统治；统治不善；管理不当 | The unjust exercise of power; bad or wicked government.  |例句：The people suffered under years of misrule.  人民在多年的暴政下受苦。

The king's misrule led to widespread rebellion. 国王的暴政导致了广泛的叛乱。| 近义词：tyranny, oppression, despotism | 反义词：good governance, just rule, equitable administration | 名词，动词
