digression:/daɪˈɡrɛʃən/| n. |离题；枝节；题外话 |A digression is a temporary departure from the main subject in speech or writing.  It's a wandering from the central topic. |例句：His lecture contained many interesting digressions. 他的演讲包含许多有趣的离题内容。

例句：The author's digression on the history of the region was fascinating, though somewhat irrelevant to the main plot. 作者关于该地区历史的离题讨论非常引人入胜，尽管与主要情节有些无关。


近义词：deviation, detour, aside, ramble, excursion

反义词：relevance, focus, concentration


词性：名词
