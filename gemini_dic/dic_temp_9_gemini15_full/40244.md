continence:/ˈkɒntɪnəns/ /ˈkɑːntɪnəns/| n. |节制；自制；克制；大小便控制|The ability to control the urge to urinate or defecate.  It can also refer to self-control in general, especially regarding sexual impulses. |例句：She showed remarkable continence in the face of such provocation. 她面对如此挑衅表现出非凡的自制力。

例句：The elderly patient is struggling with urinary continence.  这位老年病人正在努力控制大小便。


近义词：self-control, restraint, moderation, abstinence

反义词：incontinence, lack of self-control, indulgence


词源:  From Latin *continentia, from continere "to hold together, contain," from com- "together" + tenere "to hold."  The sense of "self-restraint" developed from the idea of holding oneself together, controlling one's impulses.
