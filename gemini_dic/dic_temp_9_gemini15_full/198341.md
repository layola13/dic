show-piece:/ˈʃoʊpiːs/| n. |样品，展品，杰作|A showpiece is an object, building, or event that is particularly impressive and is intended to attract attention and admiration.  It is often displayed prominently to showcase something special or outstanding. |例句：The new stadium is a showpiece of modern architecture.  这座新体育馆是现代建筑的杰作。

The company's latest product is a showpiece of innovative technology.  这家公司的最新产品是创新技术的典范。


近义词：masterpiece, exhibit, spectacle, paragon

反义词：dud, failure, disappointment

词性：名词
