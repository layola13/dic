bystanders:/ˈbaɪˌstændərz/ | n. |旁观者，围观者 |A bystander is a person who is present at an event or incident but does not take part.  Bystanders are often witnesses to something happening but are not directly involved.| 例句：Several bystanders witnessed the accident.  几个旁观者目睹了这场事故。| 例句：The bystanders watched in horror as the building collapsed. 旁观者们惊恐地看着大楼倒塌。|近义词：observer, onlooker, spectator|反义词：participant, actor, player| 名词
