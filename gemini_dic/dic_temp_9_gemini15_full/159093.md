poblano:/poʊˈblɑːnoʊ/| n. |普瓦布拉诺辣椒|A mild, large, dark green chili pepper from Puebla, Mexico.  It has a slightly sweet and earthy flavor.  Often roasted before use. |例句：The recipe calls for roasted poblano peppers. 这道菜需要烤普瓦布拉诺辣椒。

例句：I used poblano peppers to make a delicious chili. 我用普瓦布拉诺辣椒做了一道美味的辣椒酱。


近义词：ancho chili (when mature), pasilla (when dried) |反义词：None (antonyms describe opposite flavor profiles, not the pepper itself)|名词
