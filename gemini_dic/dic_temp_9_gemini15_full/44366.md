cross-contaminate:/krɔːs kənˈtæmɪneɪt/| vt.|交叉污染；交互污染|To contaminate something with something else, especially by transferring harmful bacteria or other microorganisms.|例句：Improper handwashing can cross-contaminate food surfaces.  不正确的洗手会交叉污染食物表面。

例句：The raw chicken cross-contaminated the salad. 生鸡肉交叉污染了沙拉。


近义词：contaminate, infect, pollute|反义词：sterilize, purify|动词
