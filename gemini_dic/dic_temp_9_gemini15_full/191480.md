/ʃleɪ/ | v. | 艰难地走；拖着脚走；费力地移动 | To walk heavily or without energy; to drag oneself along; to move with difficulty or effort.  | He schlepped his heavy suitcase up the stairs. 他费力地把沉重的行李箱拖上楼梯。|  trudged, lumbered, plodded |  sprinting, dashing, running | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He schleps his groceries home every Saturday. = 他每个星期六都费力地把杂货扛回家。
She schleps around the city all day. = 她整天在城里艰难地走来走去。


一般过去时:
I schlepped my backpack across campus. = 我费力地把背包背过校园。
They schlepped all the furniture to the new apartment. = 他们把所有的家具都搬到了新公寓。


现在完成时:
He has schlepped through the snow all morning. = 他整个上午都在雪地里艰难地跋涉。
She has schlepped around all day looking for a parking space. = 她整天都在四处寻找停车位，非常辛苦。


过去完成时:
I had schlepped all the way to the store before realizing I forgot my wallet. = 我已经费力地走到商店了，才意识到忘了带钱包。
They had schlepped the equipment for hours before setting up. = 他们花了几个小时才把设备搬到合适位置，然后才开始安装。


将来时:
We will schlep all the boxes to the basement tomorrow. = 我们明天要把所有的箱子都搬到地下室。
He will schlep his way through the jungle. = 他将在丛林中艰难跋涉。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it was this far, I wouldn't have schlepped all this way. = 如果我知道这么远，我就不费力地走这么远了。
I wish I hadn't schlepped all that luggage. = 我真希望我没有搬那么多行李。


被动语态:
The heavy boxes were schlepped to the storage room. = 那些沉重的箱子被费力地搬到了储藏室。
The equipment was schlepped across the field. = 设备被费力地搬过了田野。


疑问句:
Why did you schlep all those books? = 你为什么要费力地搬这么多书？
Did he schlep the furniture by himself? = 他是自己一个人把家具搬的吗？


否定句:
He didn't schlep the groceries home; he had them delivered. = 他没有费力地把杂货扛回家；他叫人送来了。
They didn't schlep the heavy boxes; they used a handtruck. = 他们没有费力地搬那些沉重的箱子；他们用了手推车。


PHRASE->
schlep around = 到处走动(疲惫地)
schlep something home = 把某物扛回家
schlep something to = 把某物艰难地运到...
schlep through = 艰难地穿过
schlep it out = 费力地完成
