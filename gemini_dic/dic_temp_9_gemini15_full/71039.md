firs: /fɝːz/ | n. | 冷杉树；枞树 | A fir is an evergreen coniferous tree of the genus Abies, typically having aromatic wood and needles borne singly on the shoots.  They are known for their conical shape and are common in mountainous regions of the Northern Hemisphere. | The Christmas tree was a beautiful fir.  这棵圣诞树是一棵美丽的冷杉。|  pine, spruce, cedar |  (No direct antonym, but deciduous trees are the opposite in terms of leaf retention) | 名词
