fled:/fled/| v.|逃离，逃走|To run away from a place or situation, especially because of danger or fear.|The family fled their home during the war.  家人在战争期间逃离了家园。|The thieves fled the scene of the crime. 窃贼逃离了犯罪现场。|escape, run away, bolt|stay, remain, face


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He flees from danger. = 他逃离危险。
She flees every time she sees a spider. = 她每次看到蜘蛛就跑。


一般过去时:
They fled the burning building. = 他们逃离了燃烧的建筑物。
The prisoners fled from jail. = 囚犯们从监狱逃跑了。


现在完成时:
He has fled the country. = 他已经逃离了这个国家。
They have fled to safety. = 他们已经逃到了安全的地方。


过去完成时:
She had fled before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前她就逃跑了。
They had fled to the mountains when the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临时，他们已经逃到了山里。


将来时:
We will flee if the volcano erupts. = 如果火山爆发，我们将逃离。
They will flee the city if the fighting starts. = 如果战斗开始，他们将逃离这座城市。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would flee. = 如果我是你，我会逃跑。
I wish I had fled sooner. = 我真希望我早点逃跑。


被动语态:
The city was fled by its inhabitants. = 城市被居民们逃离了。(This usage is less common.)
The house was fled in a hurry. = 房子被匆忙地逃离了。(This usage is less common.)


疑问句:
Did they flee? = 他们逃跑了？
Where did he flee to? = 他逃到哪里去了？


否定句:
He did not flee. = 他没有逃跑。
They did not flee the country. = 他们没有逃离这个国家。


PHRASE->
flee the country = 逃离国家
flee from danger = 逃离危险
flee for one's life = 为了性命而逃
put to flight = 使溃逃
take flight = 逃走;起飞
