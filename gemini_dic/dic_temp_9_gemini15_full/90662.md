hummingbird:/ˈhʌmɪŋbɜːrd/| n. |蜂鸟|A tiny bird with brilliant plumage, a slender bill, and a long, rapidly beating tongue, feeding on nectar from flowers.|例句：The hummingbird hovered near the flower, sipping nectar with its long beak.  蜂鸟在花附近盘旋，用长喙吸食花蜜。|例句： We watched the hummingbird dart from blossom to blossom in the garden. 我们看着蜂鸟在花园里从一朵花飞到另一朵花。|近义词： none  |反义词： none |名词
