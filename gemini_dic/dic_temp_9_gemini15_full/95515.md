inclose: /ɪnˈkloʊz/ | vt. | 包围；装入；附上 | To surround or enclose something; to put something inside an envelope or container; to include something as part of a larger whole. | 例句：The garden was enclosed by a high wall.  花园被一堵高墙围着。
The letter enclosed a photograph. 这封信附带一张照片。


近义词：envelop, surround, contain, include, attach | 反义词：open, exclude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encloses the documents in a folder. 他把文件装进文件夹里。
She always encloses a handwritten note with her gifts. 她总是送礼时附上一张手写的便条。


一般过去时:
They enclosed the area with barbed wire. 他们用铁丝网围起了这个区域。
I enclosed a check with my application. 我在申请表里附了一张支票。


现在完成时:
He has enclosed the necessary documents. 他已经附上了必要的文档。
She has enclosed a self-addressed stamped envelope. 她已经附上了一个贴好邮票的回邮信封。


过去完成时:
They had enclosed the park before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经围起了公园。
She had enclosed the letter in a special envelope. 她已将信装入一个特别的信封。


将来时:
I will enclose the payment with the order. 我将把付款与订单一起寄出。
They will enclose the area with a fence. 他们将用栅栏围住该区域。


虚拟语气:
If I enclosed a larger check, they might approve the loan. 如果我附上更大的支票，他们可能会批准贷款。
I wish I had enclosed the correct form. 我希望我已附上正确的表格。


被动语态:
The area was enclosed by a high fence. 该区域被高高的栅栏围了起来。
The documents were enclosed in a sealed envelope. 文件被装在一个密封的信封里。


疑问句:
Did you enclose the payment slip? 你附上付款单了吗？
Is the area enclosed by a wall? 这个区域是被围墙围起来的吗？


否定句:
I did not enclose the instructions. 我没有附上说明书。
The package was not properly enclosed. 包裹没有妥善包装。


PHRASE->
enclose with: 用…包围
enclose in: 把…装进
enclose under: 把…封在…下
enclose together:  一起附上
enclose separately: 分别附上

词源:
The word "inclose" comes from the Old French word "enclos," meaning "enclosed," which is a combination of the prefix "en-" (meaning "in") and "clos" (meaning "closed" or "enclosure").  It entered English in the 14th century.
