outfront: /ˌaʊtˈfrʌnt/ | adj. | 领先的；最前沿的；公开的；显著的 |  Leading, at the forefront; openly and conspicuously; prominent.  |The company is out front in developing new technology. 这家公司在开发新技术方面处于领先地位。|  leading, foremost, pioneering | behind, in the background | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is out front in innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面处于领先地位。
This issue is out front in the public's mind. = 这个议题在公众心目中非常突出。


一般过去时:
Their team was out front for most of the race. = 他们的团队在比赛的大部分时间都处于领先地位。
The problem was out front and needed immediate attention. = 这个问题非常突出，需要立即处理。


现在完成时:
They have been out front in advocating for change. = 他们一直走在倡导变革的前列。
The environmental crisis has been out front in the news lately. = 近期环境危机一直是新闻的头条。


过去完成时:
The company had been out front in the market before its competitors entered. = 在竞争对手进入市场之前，该公司一直处于领先地位。
The issue had been out front for months before any action was taken. = 在采取任何行动之前，这个问题已经突出存在数月了。


将来时:
We will be out front in the fight for social justice. = 我们将在争取社会正义的斗争中走在前列。
This technology will be out front in the coming years. = 这项技术将在未来几年处于领先地位。


虚拟语气:
If we were out front on this issue, we could make a difference. = 如果我们在这个问题上处于领先地位，我们就能有所作为。
I wish our company were out front in sustainability. = 我希望我们公司在可持续发展方面处于领先地位。


被动语态:
The importance of this issue was made out front by the media. = 媒体突出了这个问题的重要性。
The new policy was put out front by the government. = 政府将这项新政策摆在了显著位置。


疑问句:
Is our company out front in customer service? = 我们公司在客户服务方面领先吗？
Is the problem out front enough to warrant immediate action? = 这个问题是否足够突出，值得立即采取行动？


否定句:
The company is not out front in this area of research. = 这家公司在该研究领域并不领先。
The issue wasn't out front enough to attract public attention. = 这个问题不够突出，无法引起公众关注。


PHRASE->

stay out front: 保持领先
come out front: 脱颖而出
put something out front: 突出某事
bring something out front:  使某事突出
