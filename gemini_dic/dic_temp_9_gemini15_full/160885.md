post-consonantal: /ˌpoʊst kənˈsɒnəntəl/ | adj. | 后辅音的 | Following or relating to a consonant.  This term is primarily used in linguistics and phonetics to describe sounds or linguistic phenomena that occur after a consonant. For example, a post-consonantal vowel is a vowel that follows a consonant.


例句：

The study focused on post-consonantal voicing in the language.  这项研究集中于该语言中辅音后的浊音。


The aspiration of stops is often influenced by the context, especially whether they are pre-consonantal or post-consonantal.  塞音的送气常受语境影响，特别是它们是辅音前还是辅音后。


近义词：postconsonantic (less common)


反义词：pre-consonantal


词性：adjective (形容词)
