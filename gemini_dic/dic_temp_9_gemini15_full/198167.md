shorelines:/ˈʃɔːrlaɪnz/| n. |海岸线，岸边|The boundary between land and a body of water, such as an ocean, sea, lake, or river.  It includes the beach, the foreshore, and the immediate land adjacent to the water's edge. |例句：The shorelines of the island were breathtaking.  岛屿的海岸线令人叹为观止。

例句：We walked along the shorelines, collecting seashells. 我们沿着海岸线散步，收集贝壳。


近义词：coastlines, shores, beaches, waterfronts|反义词：inland, interior
