galactophorous:/ɡælæktˈɒfərəs/| adj.|输乳的；导乳的| Relating to or conveying milk.  The term refers to the ducts and sinuses that transport milk from the mammary glands to the nipple. | Example: The galactophorous ducts can become blocked, leading to mastitis.  输乳管可能会堵塞，导致乳腺炎。|近义词：lactiferous|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to terms describing blockage or absence of milk flow.
