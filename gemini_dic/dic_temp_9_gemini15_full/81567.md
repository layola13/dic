grazes:/ɡreɪz/ | v. | 轻微擦伤；放牧；吃草；浏览 | To touch or rub lightly; to feed on grass; to look at something casually. |例句：The cat grazed its leg on the table. 猫的腿在桌子上蹭破了皮。 The sheep were grazing peacefully in the field. 绵羊在田野里安静地吃草。  | 近义词：scratch, browse, graze, nibble | 反义词：devour, gorge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cow grazes in the pasture every day. = 这头奶牛每天都在牧场吃草。
He often grazes the internet for news. = 他经常浏览互联网获取新闻。


一般过去时:
The horse grazed on the lush green grass. = 马儿在青翠的草地上吃草。
She grazed her knee in the fall. = 她摔倒时擦伤了膝盖。


现在完成时:
The cattle have grazed the entire field. = 牛群已经吃光了整个田野的草。
I have grazed my elbow on the sharp edge of the table. = 我胳膊肘蹭到了桌子锋利的边缘。


过去完成时:
The sheep had grazed all the grass before the farmer arrived. = 在农民到达之前，羊群已经吃光了所有的草。
She had grazed her arm before she went to the doctor. = 在去看医生之前，她已经擦伤了胳膊。


将来时:
The goats will graze on the hillside tomorrow. = 山羊明天将在山坡上吃草。
I will graze my hand if I don't be careful. = 如果我不小心，我会擦伤我的手。


虚拟语气:
If the sheep had grazed more slowly, there would be more grass left. = 如果羊群吃草慢一点，就会剩下更多的草。
I wish I hadn't grazed my knee; it hurts so much! = 我真希望我没有擦伤膝盖，它太疼了！


被动语态:
The field was grazed bare by the sheep. = 田野里的草被羊吃光了。
His leg was grazed by a stray dog. = 他的腿被一只流浪狗蹭伤了。


疑问句:
Are the cows grazing in the field? = 牛群在田野里吃草吗？
Did you graze your knee in the accident? = 你在事故中擦伤膝盖了吗？


否定句:
The sheep didn't graze in that area. = 羊群没有在那片区域吃草。
He didn't graze his arm. = 他没有擦伤他的胳膊。
