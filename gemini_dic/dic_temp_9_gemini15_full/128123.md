net-worker:/ˈnɛtˌwɜːrkər/| n. |网络工作者，网络经营者|A person who works in or uses networks, especially computer networks, to achieve professional or business goals.  They often build and maintain relationships online to advance their careers or businesses. |Example:  She's a successful net-worker; her LinkedIn profile is brimming with connections. 她是一位成功的网络工作者；她的领英个人资料充满了人脉。|近义词：networker, online connector, relationship builder |反义词：loner, recluse
