ouroscopy:/ˌaʊˈrɒskəpi/ | n. | 尿镜检查 | The examination of urine using a microscope.  This procedure helps in the diagnosis of various urinary tract infections and kidney diseases. | Example: The ouroscopy revealed the presence of bacteria in the urine, indicating a urinary tract infection.  The doctor ordered an ouroscopy to check for kidney stones. | 近义词：urinalysis, urinary examination | 反义词：无
