myogram:/ˈmaɪəɡræm/| n. |肌电图；肌动图 | A visual record of muscle contractions, typically obtained using electromyography (EMG). It shows the electrical activity of muscles.  |Example: The myogram showed a clear increase in muscle activity during the exercise. 肌电图显示运动期间肌肉活动明显增加。|近义词:electromyogram|反义词: N/A
