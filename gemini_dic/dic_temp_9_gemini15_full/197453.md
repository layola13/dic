sheepishly:/ˈʃiːpɪʃli/| adv.|羞怯地；胆怯地；窘迫地 |In a shy or embarrassed way, often because one has done something wrong or foolish.  |例句：He sheepishly admitted he had eaten all the cookies. 他羞怯地承认他吃掉了所有的饼干。|例句：She sheepishly returned the book, a little late. 她羞怯地还了书，稍微晚了点。|近义词：bashfully, timidly, diffidently |反义词：boldly, confidently, brazenly
