indogenide:/ɪnˈdɒdʒɪnaɪd/| n. |内生苷|A glycoside produced by plants or microorganisms within their own tissues.  Indogenides are often biologically active compounds with various functions, including defense mechanisms against herbivores or pathogens.  They are studied extensively in fields like phytochemistry and microbiology.|例句：Researchers are investigating the potential medicinal properties of a newly discovered indogenide from a rare Amazonian plant.  研究人员正在调查一种从稀有的亚马逊植物中新发现的内生苷的潜在药用特性。|近义词：glycoside, phytochemical|反义词：exogenide (a glycoside originating outside the organism)|名词
