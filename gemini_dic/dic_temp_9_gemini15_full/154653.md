personage:/ˈpɜːrsənɪdʒ/|/ˈpɜːrsənɪdʒ/| n. |要人；人物；角色 |A personage is an important or famous person.  The word often implies a sense of dignity or status. |The Queen is a personage of great importance.  女王是一位非常重要的人物。

The renowned physicist was a personage of international acclaim.  这位著名的物理学家是一位享誉国际的人物。


近义词：celebrity, dignitary, figure, notable, luminary
反义词：nobody, unknown, insignificant
