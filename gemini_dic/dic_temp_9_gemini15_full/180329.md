refugeeism:/ˌrefjuˈdʒiːɪzəm/| n. |难民主义；寻求政治庇护的行为|The act of seeking refuge, often associated with political asylum or escape from persecution.  It can also refer to the social and political phenomenon of large-scale refugee movements.|例句：The increasing refugeeism in the region is placing a strain on resources.  这个地区的难民数量的增加给资源带来了压力。|近义词：asylum seeking, displacement, migration (in the context of fleeing persecution)|反义词：settled life, permanent residence|名词
