interlaminated:/ˌɪntərˈlæmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. |层压的，层状的 | Composed of or relating to layers that are laminated together.  The term implies multiple layers bonded together. | Example: The interlaminated glass provided superior strength and insulation.  层压玻璃提供了优越的强度和隔热性能。|近义词：layered, stratified, composite | 反义词：unlayered, monolithic
