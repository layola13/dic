outpaces:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪsɪz/| vt.|超过，胜过，比…发展快|To move or develop faster than; surpass.|The company's growth outpaces the industry average. 这家公司的增长速度超过了行业平均水平。| surpass, exceed, outstrip, transcend | lag behind, fall short of | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The economy outpaces inflation this year. 今年经济增长速度超过通货膨胀。
Our sales team consistently outpaces its targets. 我们的销售团队始终超额完成目标。


一般过去时:
Last year, our profits outpaced those of our competitors. 去年，我们的利润超过了竞争对手。
The runner outpaced his rivals in the final sprint.  跑步者在最后的冲刺中超过了他的对手。


现在完成时:
Technological advancements have outpaced our ability to adapt. 技术进步的速度已经超过了我们适应的能力。
The demand for renewable energy has outpaced supply in recent years. 近年来，对可再生能源的需求超过了供应。


过去完成时:
By the time the rescue team arrived, the flood waters had outpaced their efforts. 当救援队到达时，洪水已经超过了他们的救援能力。
The project had outpaced its initial budget before the halfway point. 项目在过半之前就超支了。


将来时:
Analysts predict that demand will outpace supply in the coming months. 分析师预测，未来几个月需求将超过供应。
We anticipate that our production will outpace the current market needs. 我们预计我们的产量将超过目前的市场需求。


虚拟语气:
If the company continued to innovate, it would outpace its competitors. 如果公司继续创新，它将超过竞争对手。
If only we had acted sooner, we might have outpaced the market decline. 如果我们早点采取行动，我们也许就能避免市场下滑。


被动语态:
The growth of the city was outpaced by the increase in population. 城市的增长速度不如人口增长速度快。(被动语态不太常用此词)
The project's progress was outpaced by unforeseen circumstances. 项目的进展被不可预见的情况拖慢了。(被动语态不太常用此词，通常用其他动词表达被动含义)


疑问句:
Does the rate of technological change outpace our ability to understand it? 技术变革的速度是否超过了我们理解它的能力？
Will the company's new strategy outpace its rivals' strategies? 公司的新战略能否超过竞争对手的战略？


否定句:
The company's efforts to improve efficiency did not outpace rising costs. 公司提高效率的努力并没有超过不断上涨的成本。
His progress in learning the language did not outpace his expectations. 他学习语言的进度没有超过他的预期。

outpace expectations 超越预期
outpace growth 超越增长
