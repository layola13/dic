didjeridoo:/ˌdɪdʒəˈriːduː/| n. |迪吉里杜管|A long, cylindrical Aboriginal Australian wind instrument, typically made of eucalyptus wood, played by blowing across one end.  It produces a deep, resonant sound. |The musician played a haunting melody on his didjeridoo.  演奏家用他的迪吉里杜管演奏了一首令人难忘的旋律。|近义词：drone pipe (less common)|反义词：None
