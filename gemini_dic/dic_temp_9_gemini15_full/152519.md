payroll:/ˈpeɪroʊl/| n. |工资单；薪水总额；工资支付 |A payroll is a list of employees and the amount of money each employee is to be paid.  It also refers to the total amount of money paid to employees. |例句：The company's payroll is quite high this month. 这个月公司的工资总额相当高。

The payroll department is responsible for processing employee salaries.  薪资部门负责处理员工薪资。

|近义词：salary list, pay sheet, wages |反义词：无 |名词
