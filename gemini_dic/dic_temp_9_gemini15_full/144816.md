over-hunt:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌnt/| vt.|过度捕猎；猎杀过多|To hunt to excess, resulting in a decline in the population of the hunted species.  This often leads to the endangerment or extinction of the species.|例句：The over-hunting of whales nearly drove them to extinction.  过度捕猎鲸鱼几乎使它们灭绝。

例句：Over-hunting of the passenger pigeon led to its extinction. 过度捕猎旅鸽导致其灭绝。|近义词：overkill, deplete, exhaust|反义词：conservation, preservation, protect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They over-hunt deer in this region. = 他们在该地区过度捕猎鹿。
Farmers over-hunt rabbits to protect their crops. = 农民们过度捕猎兔子以保护他们的庄稼。

一般过去时:
They over-hunted the bison almost to extinction. = 他们过度捕猎野牛，几乎使其灭绝。
Indigenous people over-hunted the local bird population. = 土著居民过度捕猎当地的鸟类。

现在完成时:
Poachers have over-hunted many endangered species. = 偷猎者已经过度捕猎了许多濒危物种。
Humans have over-hunted several species to the brink of extinction. = 人类已经过度捕猎几种物种，使其濒临灭绝。

过去完成时:
By the time the law was passed, they had already over-hunted the seals. = 法律通过时，他们已经过度捕猎海豹了。
Before the conservation efforts, they had over-hunted the salmon population for years. = 在采取保护措施之前，他们多年来一直过度捕猎鲑鱼。

将来时:
If regulations aren't enforced, they will over-hunt the fish in the lake. = 如果不执行规章制度，他们将会过度捕猎湖里的鱼。
Without stricter laws, they will likely over-hunt the local wildlife. = 如果没有更严格的法律，他们很可能会过度捕猎当地野生动物。

虚拟语气:
