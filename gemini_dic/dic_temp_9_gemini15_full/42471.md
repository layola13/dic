counterfeitness:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪtnəs/ | n. |伪造；假冒；赝品性 |The counterfeitness of the painting was revealed by an expert.  伪造画作的真伪被专家揭露了。|近义词：falseness, spuriousness, artificiality |反义词：genuineness, authenticity


英文解析:The state or quality of being counterfeit; the condition of being false or imitation.  It refers to the characteristic of something that is not genuine but is made to appear so.


例句：

The investigation uncovered the counterfeitness of the documents.  调查揭露了文件的伪造性质。

The sheer counterfeitness of the jewelry was immediately apparent.  珠宝的彻底伪造性是立刻显而易见的。


词源:

The word "counterfeitness" is derived from the word "counterfeit," which comes from the Anglo-French word "contrefait," meaning "made against" or "counterfeited."  The suffix "-ness" is added to create the noun form, indicating the state or quality of being counterfeit.  The word's origins trace back to the Latin "contrafacere," meaning "to make against" or "to imitate."
