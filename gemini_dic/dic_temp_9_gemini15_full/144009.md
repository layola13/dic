outrebounded:/ˌaʊtˈraʊndɪd/| v. |超过…的篮板数|To rebound the ball more times than an opponent or opponents.  This is a term primarily used in basketball. It describes a team or player gaining more rebounds than the opposing team or player.|例句：Our team outrebounded their team by 15 rebounds. = 我们的球队篮板数比对方多15个。

The Lakers outrebounded the Celtics in the fourth quarter. = 湖人队在第四节的篮板数超过了凯尔特人队。


近义词：out-rebound (less common) | 反义词：underrebounded (less common)


词性:动词
