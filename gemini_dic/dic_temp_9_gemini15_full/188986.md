/hwɑːt/| /wɒt/| pron.|什么，什么东西|What is your name?|你的名字是什么？|which, how|nothing, something


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does she do for a living? = 她靠什么谋生？


一般过去时:
What did you eat for lunch? = 你午餐吃了什么？
What happened at the party last night? = 昨晚派对发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she accomplished in her career? = 她在职业生涯中取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they already discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们已经讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do this weekend? = 你这个周末打算做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what the answer was. = 我真希望知道答案是什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个区域正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What time is it now? = 现在几点了？
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道说什么。
She didn't understand what he meant. = 她不明白他的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's new? = 有什么新鲜事吗？
What's the matter? = 怎么了？
What are you doing? = 你在做什么？
What do you think? = 你觉得呢？
