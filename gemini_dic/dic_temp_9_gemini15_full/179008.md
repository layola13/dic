redelegated:/ˌriːˈdɛlɪɡeɪtɪd/| v. |重新委派，再次委托|To delegate (a task or responsibility) again; to assign anew.|The project was redelegated to a different team after the initial team failed to meet deadlines. 项目因最初的团队未能按时完成而被重新委派给另一个团队。|delegate,reassign,transfer|keep,retain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redelegate tasks frequently. = 他们经常重新委派任务。
He redelegates responsibilities to his team members. = 他将责任重新委派给他的团队成员。

一般过去时:
The manager redelegated the project to a more experienced team. = 经理将项目重新委派给了一个更有经验的团队。
She redelegated the task after realizing her initial choice was unsuitable. = 她意识到最初的选择不合适后，重新委派了任务。


现在完成时:
They have redelegated the project several times. = 他们已经几次重新委派了这个项目。
The company has redelegated its marketing efforts to a new agency. = 公司已将其营销工作重新委派给了一家新的代理机构。


过去完成时:
He had redelegated the responsibility before leaving for vacation. = 他在休假前已经重新委派了责任。
She had redelegated the task because the original assignee was unavailable. = 她因为最初的受让人无法工作而重新委派了任务。


将来时:
They will redelegate the project if the current team fails to deliver. = 如果目前的团队未能交付，他们将重新委派该项目。
The CEO will redelegate the decision-making authority to her senior managers. = 首席执行官将把决策权重新委派给她的高级经理。


虚拟语气:
If the deadline were closer, they would redelegate the task immediately. = 如果截止日期更近，他们会立即重新委派任务。
I wish I had redelegated the project sooner. = 我希望我早点重新委派这个项目。


被动语态:
The task was redelegated due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，任务被重新委派。
The responsibility was redelegated to ensure timely completion. = 为了确保按时完成，责任被重新委派。


疑问句:
Was the project redelegated? = 项目是否被重新委派？
Will the responsibilities be redelegated? = 责任会被重新委派吗？


否定句:
They did not redelegate the task. = 他们没有重新委派任务。
The manager did not redelegate the project despite the delays. = 尽管有延误，经理也没有重新委派项目。
