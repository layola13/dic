parareligious:/ˌpærəˈlɪdʒəs/| adj.|准宗教的，类似宗教的|Relating to or resembling religion but not officially recognized as a religion.  It often describes groups or beliefs that share characteristics with established religions but lack formal structure or canonical texts.|例句：The New Age movement is often described as parareligious.  新时代运动通常被描述为准宗教的。

例句：Some scholars consider certain spiritual practices as parareligious phenomena. 一些学者认为某些灵性实践是准宗教现象。


近义词：quasi-religious, cultic, sectarian, spiritual

反义词：secular, irreligious, atheistic


词性：形容词
