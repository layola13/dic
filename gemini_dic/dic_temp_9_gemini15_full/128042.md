nereis:/ˈnɪəriːz/| n. |海蚯蚓，沙蚕|A genus of polychaete worms, commonly known as ragworms or clam worms, found in marine environments. They are characterized by their segmented bodies, parapodia (fleshy appendages), and often bright colors.  They play a significant role in marine ecosystems as both predators and prey.|例句：The nereis wriggled through the sand.  = 海蚯蚓在沙子里蠕动。

例句：Researchers studied the feeding habits of the nereis species in the intertidal zone. = 研究人员研究了潮间带海蚯蚓物种的摄食习性。
