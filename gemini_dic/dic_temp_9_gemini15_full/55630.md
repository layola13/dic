ditched:/dɪtʃt/| vt.|抛弃；放弃；丢弃；|to get rid of something or someone because you no longer want or need them; to abandon something or someone.|例句：He ditched his old car and bought a new one. 他抛弃了他的旧车，买了一辆新的。

例句：They ditched their plans for a weekend trip because of the bad weather. 因为天气不好，他们放弃了周末旅行的计划。


近义词：abandon, discard, forsake, dump|反义词：keep, retain, preserve|动词
