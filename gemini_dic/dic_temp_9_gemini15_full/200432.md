slackly:/ˈslækli/ | adv. | 松弛地；懈怠地；松懈地 |  Describes something done in a loose, careless, or negligent manner. It implies a lack of tension, firmness, or effort. |例句：He tied the rope slackly. 他松松垮垮地系上了绳子。
例句：The soldiers stood slackly at attention. 士兵们无精打采地立正站着。 | 近义词：loosely, carelessly, negligently, laxly | 反义词：tightly, firmly, diligently, attentively
