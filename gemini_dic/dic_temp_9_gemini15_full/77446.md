gawky:/ˈɡɔːki/| adj.|笨拙的；傻气的；瘦高的； | Awkward in movement or appearance.  Someone who is gawky is tall, thin, and moves in an awkward way. |例句：He was a gawky teenager with long limbs and an uncertain gait. 他是个瘦高、步态不稳的笨拙少年。

例句：The gawky giraffe stretched its long neck to reach the highest leaves.  那只笨拙的长颈鹿伸长脖子去够最高的树叶。


近义词：awkward, clumsy, ungainly, lanky, gangling |反义词：graceful, elegant, poised, graceful, debonair
