cephalosome:/ˈsɛfələˌsoʊm/| n. |头胸部|The fused head and thorax of a crustacean.  The cephalosome is the anterior region of the body, encompassing the head and the thorax, which are fused together in crustaceans. It contains important sensory organs and appendages involved in feeding and locomotion.|Example: The cephalosome of the copepod was clearly visible under the microscope.  = 在显微镜下，桡足类的头胸部清晰可见。|近义词：cephalothorax |反义词：abdomen
