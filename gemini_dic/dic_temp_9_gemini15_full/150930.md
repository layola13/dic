parallelopipedon:/ˌpærəleləˈpɪpɪdən/ | n. | 平行六面体 | A parallelopipedon is a three-dimensional figure whose six faces are parallelograms.  It is a prism whose bases are parallelograms.  | Example:  The crystal structure resembled a distorted parallelopipedon.  晶体结构类似于一个扭曲的平行六面体。 |近义词：parallelepiped, rhomboid |反义词：无

词源:
The word "parallelopipedon" comes from the Greek words "parallelos" (παράλληλος), meaning "parallel," and "epipedon" (ἐπίπεδον), meaning "plane surface."  It literally means "a figure with parallel plane surfaces."  The "-on" ending is a Greek noun suffix.
