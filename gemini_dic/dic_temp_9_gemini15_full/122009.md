misstart:/ˈmɪsˌstɑːrt/ | n. | 失败的开始；不顺利的开端 | A misstart is a bad or unsuccessful beginning to something.  It often implies a setback or difficulty at the outset, hindering progress. |例句：The project suffered a misstart due to inadequate planning.  这个项目由于计划不足而有一个不顺利的开端。

The race was plagued by misstarts, with several runners having to restart.  这场比赛因为多次假跑而受阻，几个运动员不得不重新开始。|近义词：false start, bad beginning, setback, poor start |反义词：successful start, smooth beginning, auspicious beginning |词性：名词
