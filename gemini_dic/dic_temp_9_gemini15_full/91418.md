hyenas:/haɪˈiːnəz/| n. |鬣狗|Hyenas are carnivorous mammals known for their distinctive laugh-like vocalizations and scavenging habits. They are highly social animals, living in clans with complex social structures.  Different species of hyenas exhibit varying hunting strategies, with some being primarily scavengers while others actively hunt prey.|例句：The hyenas scavenged the remains of the zebra carcass.  鬣狗们啃食斑马尸体的残骸。|例句：Spotted hyenas are known for their powerful jaws and cooperative hunting techniques.  斑鬣狗以其强大的下颚和合作的狩猎技巧而闻名。|近义词：wild dogs, jackals |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with animals known for their grace or gentleness, such as deer or cats.|名词
