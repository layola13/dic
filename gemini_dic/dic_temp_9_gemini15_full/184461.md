restabilized:/ˌriːˈsteɪbəlaɪzd/| v. |使重新稳定；使恢复稳定 |To restore stability to something that has become unstable.  The process involved stabilizing a previously unstable system or situation. |例句：After the earthquake, the government worked to restabilize the economy. 地震过后，政府努力稳定经济。

例句：The doctor's treatment restabilized the patient's heart rate. 医生治疗后使病人的心跳恢复稳定。


近义词：stabilize, re-establish, consolidate, reinforce


反义词：destabilize, upset, disrupt, unsettle


词性：动词
