/nɑːnˈkaʊntiz/| n. |非县辖区；非县级单位|Areas or regions that are not governed as counties.  These might be independent cities, incorporated towns, or areas under direct state or federal control.  The term is primarily used in the United States, where county governance is a significant aspect of local administration.|Example:  Many independent cities in California function as noncounties, operating outside the jurisdiction of a surrounding county.|近义词: independent city, incorporated town, municipality|反义词: county, county-level jurisdiction|
