headmold:/ˈhedmoʊld/| n.|头模，头型模具|A headmold is a mold used to create a three-dimensional representation of a head, often used in the creation of wigs, helmets, or prosthetic devices.  It can also refer to the shape or form of a head.|例句：The sculptor carefully created a headmold from clay before beginning the bronze casting.  雕塑家在开始青铜铸造之前小心地用粘土制作了一个头模。

The wigmaker used a precise headmold to ensure a perfect fit. 假发制造商使用精确的头模以确保完美贴合。|近义词：head form, mold, cast|反义词：无
