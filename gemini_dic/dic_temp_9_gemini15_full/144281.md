outsparkled:/ˌaʊtˈspɑːrkəld/ | v. |  超越...的光彩；胜过...的闪耀 | To surpass in brilliance or splendor; to outshine. |例句：The diamond necklace outsparkled all the other jewelry. 这条钻石项链的光彩胜过所有其他珠宝。 |The newly renovated hotel outsparkled its competitors.  这家新装修的酒店的光彩胜过了它的竞争对手。|近义词：outshine, eclipse, surpass |反义词：be outshone, be eclipsed
