noncolonials:/ˌnɒnˈkɒləniəlz/ | n. |非殖民地居民；非殖民地人 |People or things not related to or characteristic of colonialism.  This term usually refers to people who are not from or associated with a colonial power, or those who actively oppose colonialism.  It can also refer to the cultures, societies, or practices that are independent of colonial influence. | Example: The museum showcased the art of noncolonials, highlighting indigenous traditions unaffected by Western imperialism.  The noncolonials fought bravely for their independence. | 近义词: natives, indigenous people, anti-colonialists | 反义词: colonials, imperialists
