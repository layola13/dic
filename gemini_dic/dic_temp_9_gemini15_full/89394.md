homopteran:/ˈhoʊmɒptərən/ | n. |同翅目昆虫 |A homopteran is an insect belonging to the order Homoptera, characterized by having piercing-sucking mouthparts and typically exhibiting incomplete metamorphosis.  They often feed on plant sap. |Example:  Aphids are a common example of a homopteran insect.  蚜虫是同翅目昆虫的一个常见例子。|近义词:hemipteran (although technically a different order)|反义词:  None directly applicable, as it describes an entire order.
