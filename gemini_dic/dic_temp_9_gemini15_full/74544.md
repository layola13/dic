fratricide:/ˈfrætərɪsaɪd/| n. |杀兄（弟）罪；兄弟相残 |The killing of one's brother.  Fratricide can refer to the act of killing one's brother, or the act of killing one's sibling in general, although the term is more commonly used to describe the killing of a brother.  |Example: The Cain and Abel story is a classic example of fratricide.  该隐和亚伯的故事是兄弟相残的经典例子。|近义词：sibling murder, homicide, murder |反义词：fraternal love, reconciliation |名词
