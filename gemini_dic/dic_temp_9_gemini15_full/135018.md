nonnegotiability: /ˌnɒn.nɪˌɡoʊʃiəˈbɪlɪti/ | n. | 不可转让性，非议价性 | The state or quality of being nonnegotiable;  inability to be transferred, changed, or bargained over.  It often refers to legal documents or contracts. | Example: The terms of the contract were declared nonnegotiable by the company. 公司宣布合同条款不可协商。  The nonnegotiability of the ticket prevented him from reselling it.  该票据的不可转让性阻止了他转售。 | 近义词:  inalienability, inflexibility, immutability | 反义词: negotiability, flexibility
