countdown:/ˈkaʊntdaʊn/| n. |倒计时，倒数|A countdown is the act of counting down from a number, usually to zero, often to mark the time remaining until an event.  It can also refer to the period of time being counted down.|例句：The countdown to the launch of the rocket began. 火箭发射的倒计时开始了。

The countdown to the New Year is always exciting. 新年倒计时总是令人兴奋的。|近义词：countback,  timer|反义词：count up
