neptunist:/ˈneptuːnɪst/ | n. |海王星论者；(地质学)水成论者 | A believer in Neptunism, the now-discredited theory that all rocks were precipitated from a primeval ocean.  | Example:  The Neptunists believed that basalt was formed from precipitation in a vast ocean.  海王星论者相信玄武岩是由广阔海洋中的沉淀物形成的。 | 近义词：waterist (较少用) | 反义词： Plutonist
