prewiring:/ˈpriːwaɪərɪŋ/ | n. |预接线，预先布线 |The process of installing wiring before other components are added.  This is common in manufacturing and construction to streamline the process and improve efficiency.  |例句：The prewiring of the house saved us a lot of time during construction.  预先布线节省了我们在施工过程中的很多时间。
The prewiring in this new car is incredibly complex. 这辆新车的预接线非常复杂。|近义词：preinstallation, preliminary wiring |反义词：post-wiring,retrofitting |名词
