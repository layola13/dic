logically:/ˈlɒdʒɪkli/ /ˈlɑːdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 逻辑上地，合乎逻辑地 | In a logical manner; in accordance with the principles of logic.  It refers to reasoning or conclusions that are sound, coherent, and consistent. | Example: Logically, if you add 2 and 2, you get 4.  逻辑上，如果你把2加2，你得到4。


The argument was logically flawed.  这个论点在逻辑上是有缺陷的。


近义词：rationally, reasonably, sensibly, consistently


反义词：illogically, irrationally, unreasonably


词性：副词
