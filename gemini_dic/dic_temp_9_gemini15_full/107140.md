ladylove:/ˈleɪdiˌlʌv/| n. |心爱的女子，恋人|A ladylove is a woman who is dearly loved, often a romantic partner.  The term carries a slightly old-fashioned or poetic connotation, suggesting a more idealized or romantic love than the simple term "girlfriend" or "lover."|例句：He wrote poems to his ladylove, showering her with affection. 他为他心爱的女子写诗，对她充满爱意。

例句：My ladylove and I are going on a romantic getaway this weekend.  我和我的爱人这个周末要去浪漫的短途旅行。


近义词： sweetheart, darling, beloved, lover, girlfriend


反义词：  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与“敌人”、“仇人”等词语相对，但语义关联性较弱)


词性：名词
