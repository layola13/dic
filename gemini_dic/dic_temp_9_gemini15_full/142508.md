osprey:/ˈɒspreɪ/| n.|鱼鹰|A large bird of prey with a white head and breast, brown back, and long wings, which dives to catch fish.  It is also known as the sea hawk. |例句：The osprey swooped down and snatched a fish from the river. 鱼鹰俯冲下来，从河里抓起一条鱼。|例句：We saw an osprey nest high up in a tall tree. 我们看到一只鱼鹰的巢穴建在一棵高高的树上。|近义词：fish hawk, sea hawk|反义词：无
