deifier: /ˈdiːɪfaɪər/ | vt. |神化；尊为神 | To regard or represent as a god or goddess; to ascribe divine attributes to.  |例句：The ancient Greeks deified many of their heroes. 古希腊人将许多英雄神化。 |例句：They deified the emperor, building temples and offering sacrifices in his name. 他们将皇帝神化，建造庙宇并以他的名义献祭。 |近义词：idolize, apotheosize, canonize |反义词：degrade, denigrate, vilify
