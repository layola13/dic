chiral:/ˈkaɪrəl/| adj.|手性的，对映异构的|Possessing chirality; having the property of asymmetry such that a molecule or structure cannot be superimposed on its mirror image.|The molecule is chiral, meaning it exists in two mirror-image forms.|近义词：asymmetrical, enantiomeric|反义词：achiral, symmetric
