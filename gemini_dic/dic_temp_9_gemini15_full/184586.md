It often refers to the ability of an environment or experience to promote relaxation, rejuvenation, and a sense of well-being.|The quiet solitude of the forest had a remarkable restorativeness, calming my mind and soothing my soul.  森林的宁静孤独具有非凡的恢复活力，使我的身心平静安宁。|近义词：rejuvenation, refreshment, revitalization |反义词：deterioration, exhaustion, depletion
