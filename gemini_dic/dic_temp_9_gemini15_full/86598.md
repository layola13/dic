heliogravure:/ˌhiːliəʊˈɡrævjuːr/| n. |太阳刻印术，日光凹版印刷|A photomechanical intaglio printing process using a copper plate etched by the action of light on a bichromated sensitized surface.  This process produces high-quality prints with fine detail and rich tones.|The artist used heliogravure to create stunningly detailed prints of his landscapes.|近义词：photogravure|反义词：None.
