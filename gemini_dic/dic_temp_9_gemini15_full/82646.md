guerdon:/ˈɡɜːrdən/| n. |报酬；奖赏 | A guerdon is a reward, typically for service or merit.  It often implies something given in recognition of achievement or bravery. |Example:  The knight received a generous guerdon for his valiant efforts in battle.  骑士因其在战斗中的英勇表现而获得了丰厚的奖赏。|近义词：reward, recompense, prize, award |反义词：punishment, penalty
