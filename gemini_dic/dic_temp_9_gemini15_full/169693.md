propound:/prəˈpaʊnd/| vt.|提出（问题、建议等）；阐述（学说、理论等）|to offer or state (an idea, theory, etc.) for consideration; to put forward (a question, proposal, etc.)|The philosopher propounded a new theory of knowledge.  哲学家提出了一种新的知识理论。|He propounded a series of questions to the panel. 他向专家小组提出了一系列问题。|近义词：suggest, propose, advance, posit|反义词：reject, dismiss, refute|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He propounds a new theory every year. = 他每年都提出一个新理论。
The professor propounds complex questions to his students. = 教授向他的学生提出复杂的问题。

一般过去时:
She propounded her ideas to the committee. = 她向委员会提出了她的想法。
The scientist propounded a revolutionary hypothesis. = 科学家提出了一种革命性的假设。

现在完成时:
He has propounded several innovative solutions to the problem. = 他已经为这个问题提出了几个创新的解决方案。
They have propounded a comprehensive plan for urban development. = 他们已经为城市发展制定了一个全面的计划。

The author had propounded his views in several earlier publications. = 作者在之前的几篇出版物中已经阐述了他的观点。
