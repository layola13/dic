consumeristic:/ˌkənˌsjuːməˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 消费主义的；重视物质享受的 | Characterized by or emphasizing consumerism; relating to or characterized by the beliefs and activities of consumers.  It often implies an excessive focus on acquiring material possessions. | Example:  The consumeristic culture of modern society often leads to unsustainable practices.  现代社会的消费主义文化常常导致不可持续的实践。  The advertisement promoted a highly consumeristic lifestyle. 这则广告推崇高度消费主义的生活方式。|近义词：materialistic, acquisitive, materialistic | 反义词：ascetic, minimalist, frugal
