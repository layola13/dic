gladly:/ˈɡlædli/ | adv. | 乐意地，欣然地 | Willingly; with pleasure.  | 例句：I'd gladly help you with your project. = 我乐意帮你做你的项目。  | 例句：She gladly accepted the invitation. = 她欣然接受了邀请。 | 近义词：willingly, readily, cheerfully | 反义词：reluctantly, unwillingly | 副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I gladly accept your offer. = 我乐意接受你的提议。
He gladly shares his knowledge. = 他乐意分享他的知识。

一般过去时:
She gladly helped me with my homework. = 她乐意帮我做作业。
They gladly attended the party. = 他们欣然参加了聚会。

现在完成时:
I have gladly assisted many clients. = 我已经乐意地帮助了许多客户。
She has gladly donated to the charity. = 她已经乐意地向慈善机构捐款。

过去完成时:
He had gladly agreed to the terms before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经欣然同意了条款。
They had gladly finished the task by the time I arrived. = 当我到达时，他们已经欣然完成了任务。

将来时:
I will gladly assist you in the future. = 我将来乐意协助你。
She will gladly participate in the event. = 她将乐意参加此次活动。

虚拟语气:
I would gladly go if I had the time. = 如果我有时间，我会乐意去的。
She would gladly help if she weren't so busy. = 如果她不那么忙，她会乐意帮忙的。

被动语态:
His offer was gladly accepted. = 他的提议被欣然接受了。

疑问句:
Would you gladly accept this task? = 你会乐意接受这个任务吗？

否定句:
I wouldn't gladly do that. = 我不会乐意那样做。
