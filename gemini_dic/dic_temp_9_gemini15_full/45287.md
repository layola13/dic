cultured:/ˈkʌltʃərd/| adj.|有教养的；有文化的；栽培的；养殖的| possessing or exhibiting a refined taste and appreciation, especially in the arts;  showing the effects of training and education;  grown or produced under controlled conditions.|例句：She is a cultured woman with a wide range of interests. 她是一位兴趣广泛、有教养的女士。

例句：The cultured pearls are indistinguishable from natural ones.  这些养殖珍珠与天然珍珠难以区分。|近义词：refined, sophisticated, educated, cultivated|反义词：uncultured, unrefined, ignorant, barbaric
