emigrated:/ˈɛməɡreɪtɪd/| v.| 移民；移居国外 | To leave one's own country to settle permanently in another. |例句：Many people emigrated from Europe to America in the 19th century.  十九世纪许多人从欧洲移居美国。

例句：My grandparents emigrated to Canada after World War II. 我的祖父母在二战后移民到加拿大。


近义词：migrate, relocate, expatriate |反义词： immigrate, repatriate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emigrates to Australia next year. 他明年移居澳大利亚。
They emigrate for better job opportunities. 他们为了更好的工作机会而移民。


一般过去时:
She emigrated to France in 2010. 她于2010年移居法国。
My uncle emigrated from Ireland many years ago. 我叔叔多年前就从爱尔兰移民出去了。


现在完成时:
They have emigrated to New Zealand and started a new life. 他们已经移民新西兰，开始新的生活了。
He has emigrated several times in his life. 他一生中多次移民。


过去完成时:
She had emigrated before the war broke out.  战争爆发前她就移民了。
They had emigrated to Brazil long before I was born. 我出生很久以前，他们就移民到巴西了。


将来时:
We will emigrate to Spain once we save enough money.  我们攒够钱后就会移居西班牙。
She will emigrate after she graduates from university.  她大学毕业后会移民。


虚拟语气:
If I had the chance, I would emigrate to Italy. 如果我有机会，我会移民到意大利。
I wish I had emigrated sooner. 我真希望我早点移民。


被动语态:
Many people were emigrated during the war.  许多人在战争期间被迫移民。 (Note:  Passive voice is less common with this verb)
The family was forced to be emigrated. 这个家庭被迫移民。 (Note:  Passive voice is less common with this verb)


疑问句:
Did he emigrate to Canada? 他移民到加拿大了吗？
When did your family emigrate? 你家什么时候移民的？


否定句:
He didn't emigrate to the US. 他没有移民到美国。
They decided not to emigrate after all. 他们最终决定不移民了。


PHRASE->
emigrate to a new country 移居新国家
emigrate from one's homeland 离开祖国
emigrate for economic reasons 因为经济原因而移民
emigrate with one's family  举家移民
emigrate illegally 非法移民


词源:
The word "emigrated" comes from the Latin word "e-migrare," meaning "to move out" or "to remove oneself."  The prefix "e-" means "out" or "from," and "migrare" means "to move."
