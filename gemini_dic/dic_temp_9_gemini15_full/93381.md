illiberalize:/ɪˈlɪbərəlaɪz/ | vt. |使不自由，压制，限制自由 | To make (something) less liberal or tolerant; to restrict freedoms or rights.  | The government's policies are increasingly illiberalizing the media. 政府的政策越来越压制媒体。 |近义词：restrict, repress, suppress, curb |反义词：liberalize, emancipate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They illiberalize the press through censorship. = 他们通过审查来压制新闻界。
The government illiberalizes the education system with strict regulations. = 政府以严格的法规压制教育体系。


一般过去时:
The regime illiberalized the political landscape after the coup. = 政变后，该政权压制了政治格局。
The dictator illiberalized the country by silencing dissent. = 独裁者通过压制异见来压制这个国家。


现在完成时:
The ruling party has illiberalized many aspects of society. = 执政党已经压制了社会的许多方面。
Recent legislation has illiberalized access to information. = 最近的立法已经限制了获取信息的途径。


过去完成时:
They had illiberalized the voting system before the election. = 他们在选举前已经压制了投票制度。
The authorities had illiberalized the internet access prior to the protests. = 当局在抗议活动之前已经限制了互联网访问。


将来时:
They will illiberalize the legal system further if re-elected. = 如果他们再次当选，他们将进一步压制法律体系。
The new laws will illiberalize the rights of minorities. = 新法律将压制少数群体的权利。


虚拟语气:
If they were to illiberalize the economy, it would harm growth. = 如果他们要压制经济，将会损害经济增长。
I wish they wouldn't illiberalize the public discourse. = 我希望他们不要压制公共舆论。


被动语态:
The media was illiberalized by the government's actions. = 媒体被政府的行为压制了。
Freedom of speech has been illiberalized in many countries. = 在许多国家，言论自由受到了压制。


疑问句:
Will they illiberalize the healthcare system? = 他们会压制医疗体系吗？
Have they illiberalized the freedom of assembly? = 他们已经压制了集会自由吗？


否定句:
The reforms did not illiberalize the society. = 改革并没有压制社会。
The new constitution will not illiberalize the country's legal framework. = 新宪法不会压制该国的法律框架。
