obeys:/oʊˈbeɪz/ | v. | 服从；遵守 | To comply with or follow (a rule, command, etc.).  |例句：The dog obeys its master.  这条狗服从它的主人。|例句：She always obeys the law. 她总是遵守法律。|近义词：complies with, follows, conforms to |反义词：disobeys, defies, resists |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He obeys his parents. 他听从父母的话。
The students obey the school rules. 学生们遵守校规。

一般过去时:
The soldiers obeyed the officer's orders. 士兵们服从了军官的命令。
She obeyed her conscience. 她遵从了自己的良心。

现在完成时:
He has always obeyed the law. 他一直遵守法律。
She has obeyed her parents all her life. 她一生都听从父母的话。

过去完成时:
He had obeyed his father's wishes before he left home. 他在离家前已经遵从了父亲的意愿。
The child had obeyed the teacher's instructions. 孩子已经听从了老师的指示。

将来时:
They will obey the new regulations. 他们将遵守新的规章制度。
He will obey the court order. 他将服从法院的命令。

虚拟语气:
If he obeyed the rules, he wouldn't be in trouble. 如果他遵守规则，就不会有麻烦了。
I wish he would obey his doctor's advice. 我希望他能听从医生的建议。

被动语态:
The rules must be obeyed. 规则必须遵守。
His orders were obeyed without question. 他的命令毫无疑问地被服从了。

疑问句:
Does he always obey the rules? 他总是遵守规则吗？
Will they obey the law? 他们会遵守法律吗？

否定句:
He doesn't obey his parents. 他不听父母的话。
The children didn't obey the teacher. 孩子们没有听老师的话。
