lift: /lɪft/ | n., v. | 举起；电梯；搭便车 |

英文解析:As a noun, "lift" refers to the action of raising something, an elevator, or a free ride. As a verb, it means to raise something or someone.

例句:

(Noun):
The lift to the top floor was broken. = 通往顶层的电梯坏了。

He gave me a lift home. = 他载我回家了。


(Verb):
She lifted the heavy box with ease. = 她轻松地举起了沉重的箱子。

He lifted his glass in a toast. = 他举起酒杯敬酒。


近义词: (Noun) elevator, elevation, ride; (Verb) raise, hoist, elevate


反义词: (Noun)  lowering, descent; (Verb) lower, drop


词性:Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lifts weights every day. = 他每天举重。
The elevator lifts people to the upper floors. = 电梯把人们送到高层。

一般过去时:
She lifted the child gently. = 她轻轻地抱起了孩子。
They lifted the fallen tree from the road. = 他们把倒下的树从路上移开了。

现在完成时:
He has lifted the ban on imports. = 他已经取消了进口禁令。
They have lifted the car with a jack. = 他们用千斤顶把汽车顶了起来。

过去完成时:
The workers had lifted all the boxes before lunch. = 工人们在午饭前已经搬完了所有的箱子。
She had lifted her spirits after the bad news. = 在听到坏消息后，她振作起来了。

将来时:
They will lift the bridge for the passing ship. = 他们将为过往的船只抬起桥梁。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "lift" originates from Old English *lyftan, meaning "to raise, to elevate."  This is related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.
