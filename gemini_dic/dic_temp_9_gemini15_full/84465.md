halos:/ˈheɪloʊz/ | n. |光环；晕轮；光晕 | A halo is a ring of light appearing around the sun, moon, or a saint in a picture.  It can also refer to a circle of light around the head of a saint or holy person in religious art.  In a more figurative sense, it can refer to a glory or aura surrounding someone or something. |例句：The setting sun created a halo around the mountain peaks.  落日给山峰周围 tạo ra một vòng hào quang.

The saint in the painting had a golden halo around his head. 画中圣人的头上有一圈金色光环。

|近义词：aura, nimbus, glory |反义词：shadow, darkness
