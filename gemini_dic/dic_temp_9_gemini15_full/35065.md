closet: /ˈklɑːzɪt/ /ˈklɒzɪt/ | n. | 壁橱，衣橱 | A small room or a cupboard used for storing clothes or other things.  |  例句：She keeps her winter clothes in the closet. 她把冬衣放在壁橱里。

例句：The cleaning supplies are stored in the closet under the stairs. 清洁用品储存在楼梯下的壁橱里。


近义词：wardrobe, cupboard, locker | 反义词：open, exposed


词源:  Middle English: from Old French *clos, from close 'closed', from Latin clausus, past participle of claudere 'to shut'.
