reviewing:/rɪˈvjuːɪŋ/| v.|复习；审查；评论|The act of looking over or examining something again, often for the purpose of study, evaluation, or correction.  It can also refer to writing a critical assessment of something.|例句：I'm reviewing my notes for the exam. 我正在复习考试笔记。


例句：The editor is reviewing the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前正在审查稿件。


近义词：examining, studying, assessing, critiquing


反义词：ignoring, overlooking, neglecting


词性:动词
