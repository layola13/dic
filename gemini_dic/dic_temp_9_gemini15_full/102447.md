jackfruit:/ˈdʒækfruːt/ | n. | 菠萝蜜 | A large tropical fruit with a spiky rind and sweet, yellow flesh.  It is known for its size and unique flavor profile, often described as a combination of banana, pineapple, and mango. The fruit can be eaten ripe or unripe, and its young, green fruit is often used in savory dishes. | Example: The jackfruit was so large it took two people to carry it.  菠萝蜜大得需要两个人才能搬动。 |近义词: durian (but with a very different flavor profile) |反义词: None directly applicable.
