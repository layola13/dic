ostrogoths:/ˈɒstrəɡɒθs/ | n. |东哥特人 |The Ostrogoths were a Germanic people who migrated from Scandinavia to the Black Sea region and eventually settled in various parts of Europe, notably Italy.  They played a significant role in the decline of the Western Roman Empire and the formation of several successor states.|The Ostrogoths sacked Rome in 455 AD.  东哥特人在公元455年洗劫了罗马。|Visigoths, Goths |Vandals, Romans|名词
