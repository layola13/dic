metallic: /məˈtælɪk/ | adj. | 金属的；金属似的 |

英文解析:Having the properties or appearance of metal; relating to or containing metal.  The word suggests a shiny, hard, and often cold quality associated with metals.

例句:
The sculpture had a beautiful metallic sheen. = 这座雕塑有着美丽的金属光泽。

The car had a metallic blue paint job. = 这辆车漆着金属蓝的漆。


近义词:  metalloid, metal-like, steely

反义词: nonmetallic, organic
