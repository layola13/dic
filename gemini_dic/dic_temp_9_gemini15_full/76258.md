fuzz-up:/ˈfʌzʌp/| vt.|弄乱；使模糊；使混乱|To make something unclear, indistinct, or messy; to become unclear, indistinct, or messy.|例句：He fuzzed up the details of the story. 他把故事的细节弄糊涂了。|例句：The image fuzzed up on the old TV.  旧电视上的图像变得模糊了。|近义词：blur, confuse, obscure|反义词：clarify, sharpen, define|动词
