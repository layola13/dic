hot-bedding: /ˈhɑːtˌbɛdɪŋ/ | n. | 热床；密集安置；快速周转 | A system where beds are used continuously by different people, often in emergency situations or with limited resources.  It is frequently used in situations with high patient volume and limited beds.  The term can also refer to a situation where something is rapidly developing or spreading, metaphorically like a hotbed of activity. | Example:  The hospital resorted to hot-bedding patients due to the influx of injured people after the earthquake.  医院由于地震后涌入大量伤员而采取了病人热床制度。

The term also applies to situations where items or resources are rapidly reused or recycled. Example: The factory employed a hot-bedding system for its machinery to maximize production.这家工厂对机器采用了热床系统以最大限度地提高产量。| Near synonyms: rapid turnover, intensive use, quick turnaround | Antonyms:  sparse use, underutilization |  词性: 名词
