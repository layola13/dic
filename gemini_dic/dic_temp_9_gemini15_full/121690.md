mispositioning:/ˌmɪsˌpəˈzɪʃənɪŋ/| n.|错误定位，错位|The act of placing something in the wrong position;  incorrect placement or arrangement.  It can refer to physical objects, but also to abstract concepts like marketing or strategic planning.|例句：The mispositioning of the emergency exit caused confusion during the evacuation.  紧急出口的错误摆放导致疏散期间混乱。|例句：The company's mispositioning of its new product led to poor sales. 该公司对其新产品的错误定位导致销售不佳。|近义词：malpositioning, misplacement, improper placement|反义词：correct positioning, proper placement|名词
