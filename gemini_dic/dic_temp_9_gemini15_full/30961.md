chalan:/tʃɑːlən/ | n. | 罚单；传票；清单 | A chalan is an official receipt or slip, often used in South Asia (particularly India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh) as proof of payment or a citation for an offense.  It can also refer to a list or inventory. | Example: The police officer issued a chalan for speeding.  警察开了一张超速罚单。 | 近义词：receipt, ticket, citation, invoice, bill | 反义词：N/A
