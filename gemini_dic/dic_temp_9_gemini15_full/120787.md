misappropriations:/ˌmɪsˌæproʊpriˈeɪʃənz/| n.|挪用；盗用；滥用|The act of using something in a way that is not appropriate or legal, especially money or property that is not yours.  It often implies a breach of trust.|例句：The company is being investigated for serious financial misappropriations.  这家公司正因严重的财务挪用而接受调查。

例句：The investigation revealed numerous misappropriations of funds by the charity's treasurer.  调查揭露了慈善机构财务主管多次挪用资金的行为。


近义词：embezzlement, theft, fraud, defalcation|反义词：proper use, rightful use, legitimate use|名词
