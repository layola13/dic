self-damning: /ˌsɛlfˈdæmɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我谴责的；自我毁坏的 |  Something that is self-damning is inherently incriminating or reveals guilt. It provides evidence against itself.  |例句：His testimony was self-damning, revealing his involvement in the crime.  他的证词是自我谴责的，揭露了他参与犯罪的事实。

His arrogant attitude was self-damning in the job interview. 他傲慢的态度在求职面试中对他很不利。| 近义词：incriminating, self-incriminating, self-condemnatory | 反义词：exculpatory, self-exculpatory
