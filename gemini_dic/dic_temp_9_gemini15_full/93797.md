immerses:/ɪˈmɜːrsɪz/| v.|沉浸，使沉浸；使专心致志|To surround or cover completely; to absorb fully.|例句：The children were immersed in their game. 孩子们全神贯注于他们的游戏。

例句：She immersed herself in her work. 她全身心地投入工作。


近义词：submerge, engross, absorb|反义词：emerge, detach, extract|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He immerses himself in his studies every night. 他每天晚上都埋头苦读。
The artist immerses the canvas in vibrant colors. 艺术家将画布浸在鲜艳的色彩中。
