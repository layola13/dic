dominating: /ˈdɒmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 统治的；支配的；占优势的；显著的 |  Describes something or someone that has a powerful and controlling influence over others or a situation. It can also describe something that is very noticeable or prominent.  |  例句：His dominating personality made him a natural leader. 他强势的个性使他成为天生的领导者。

例句：The dominating color in the painting is blue.  这幅画中主要的色彩是蓝色。


近义词：predominant, prevailing, controlling, overpowering, influential, conspicuous, outstanding | 反义词：submissive, subservient, insignificant, minor, weak
