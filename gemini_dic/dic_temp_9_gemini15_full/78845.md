ginga:/ˈdʒɪŋɡə/| n.|金戈，一种巴西战舞|Ginga is a term used in Capoeira, a Brazilian martial art, to describe the fluid, rhythmic, and elusive movement style characteristic of the art. It involves subtle shifts in weight, deceptive footwork, and a constant state of readiness.  It's not just a physical skill; it's a mindset and a way of life. | 例句：The ginga is the foundation of Capoeira, allowing for quick changes of direction and unpredictable movements. = 金戈是 capoeira 的基础，它允许快速改变方向和不可预测的动作。

The master demonstrated the ginga with effortless grace. = 这位大师以轻松优雅的动作展示了金戈。|近义词： fluidity, agility, grace, rhythm |反义词： rigidity, clumsiness, stillness
