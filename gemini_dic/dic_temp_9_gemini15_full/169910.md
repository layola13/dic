prosper:/ˈprɒspə(r)/|/ˈprɑːspər/| v.|繁荣，兴旺，成功|To be successful and thriving, especially in financial terms; to grow and develop successfully.|The business prospered under his leadership.  他的领导下，生意兴隆。

The community prospered after the new factory opened. 新工厂开业后，社区蓬勃发展。|flourish, thrive, succeed|fail, decline, struggle|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company prospers in the current market. = 这家公司在目前的市场上蓬勃发展。
They prosper because of their hard work. = 他们因为努力工作而兴旺发达。

一般过去时:
The farm prospered during the good harvest. = 丰收期间，农场生意兴隆。
His business prospered after he invested in new technology. = 他投资新技术后，生意兴隆。

现在完成时:
The city has prospered significantly in recent years. = 近年来，这座城市发展显著。
Our family has prospered thanks to his wise decisions. = 多亏了他的明智决定，我们家过上了富足的生活。

过去完成时:
The kingdom had prospered for centuries before the war. = 在战争爆发前，这个王国已经繁荣了几个世纪。
They had prospered greatly before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退之前，他们已经非常富裕了。

将来时:
With the new policies, the country will prosper. = 在新的政策下，国家将会繁荣。
I believe that your business will prosper in the future. = 我相信你的生意将来会兴旺发达。

虚拟语气:
If they had worked harder, they would have prospered. = 如果他们更加努力工作，他们就会成功。
I wish my business would prosper as much as his. = 我希望我的生意也能像他的一样兴旺。

被动语态:
The region was prospered by the discovery of oil. = 该地区因石油的发现而繁荣起来。(注意：被动语态用得较少)
The town was prospered by tourism. = 这个小镇因旅游业而繁荣。

疑问句:
Will the project prosper? = 这个项目会成功吗？
Does the company prosper despite the crisis? = 尽管面临危机，这家公司仍然兴旺发达吗？

否定句:
The business did not prosper after the scandal. = 丑闻之后，这家公司没有兴旺起来。
The crops did not prosper due to the drought. = 由于干旱，庄稼没有生长茂盛。


PHRASE->
prosper greatly = 大大成功
prosper economically = 经济繁荣
prosper under (someone's leadership) = 在（某人的领导下）兴旺发达
prosper in business = 商业兴隆
prosper through hard work = 通过努力工作而成功
