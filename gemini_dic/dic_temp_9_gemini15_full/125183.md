multicast:/ˈmʌltɪkæst/ | n. v. |多点广播，多播 |The transmission of data to multiple recipients simultaneously over a network.  It is a one-to-many communication method, as opposed to unicast (one-to-one) or broadcast (one-to-all).|例句：The server uses multicast to distribute updates to all the clients. 服务器使用多播向所有客户端分发更新。

The company implemented a multicast system for video conferencing. 公司实施了一个用于视频会议的多播系统。|近义词：broadcast (in some contexts), distribution |反义词：unicast
