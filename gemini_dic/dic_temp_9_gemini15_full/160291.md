pooh-pooh:/ˈpuːpuː/| vt. |嗤之以鼻，不屑一顾 |To express strong disapproval of something; to scorn or dismiss something as unimportant or worthless.|He pooh-poohed my suggestion. 他对我的建议嗤之以鼻。|近义词：scoff at, dismiss, scorn, disdain|反义词：approve, endorse, praise
