prehesitating: /ˌpriːˈhezɪteɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 犹豫不决的，迟疑的 |  Describes someone who is hesitant or unsure before doing something; showing signs of hesitation before speaking or acting.  | Example:  He gave a prehesitating nod, unsure of his next move. 他迟疑地点了点头，拿不定主意下一步该做什么。


近义词: hesitant, reluctant, wavering, irresolute, uncertain


反义词: decisive, resolute, certain, confident


词性: 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is prehesitating before answering the question. 他在回答问题之前犹豫不决。
She is prehesitating about accepting the job offer.  她对接受这份工作犹豫不决。


一般过去时:
He was prehesitating when asked to speak in public. 当被要求公开演讲时，他犹豫不决。
She was prehesitating to sign the contract. 她犹豫着要不要签合同。


现在完成时:
He has been prehesitating all morning about what to wear.  他整个上午都在犹豫穿什么。
She has been prehesitating about making a decision for weeks. 她几周来一直在犹豫要不要做决定。


过去完成时:
He had been prehesitating for days before finally making a decision. 他在最终做出决定之前犹豫了好几天。
She had been prehesitating to call him, fearing his reaction. 她一直犹豫要不要给他打电话，担心他的反应。


将来时:
He will be prehesitating to take the risk. 他将犹豫是否要冒险。
She will be prehesitating to reveal her secret. 她将犹豫是否要透露她的秘密。


虚拟语气:
If he were less prehesitating, he would have achieved more. 如果他不那么犹豫，他就能取得更大的成就。
If she hadn't been so prehesitating, she would have applied for the job. 如果她那时不那么犹豫，她就申请那份工作了。


被动语态:
His decision was prehesitated for a long time. 他的决定犹豫了很久。
Her response was prehesitated by doubt. 她的回应因怀疑而犹豫不决。


疑问句:
Is he prehesitating to commit? 他是否犹豫要不要承诺？
Are they prehesitating about signing the deal? 他们是否在犹豫要不要签协议？


否定句:
He was not prehesitating at all when he made the decision. 他在做决定时一点也不犹豫。
She was not prehesitating to express her opinion. 她毫不犹豫地表达了自己的意见。


PHRASE->
prehesitatingly accept : 犹豫地接受
prehesitatingly agree : 犹豫地同意
prehesitatingly approach : 犹豫地接近
prehesitatingly answer : 犹豫地回答
prehesitatingly speak: 犹豫地说话

词源:  The word is a combination of "pre-" (before, beforehand) and "hesitating" (showing uncertainty or doubt).  It suggests a hesitation that is evident even *before* the action or speech begins.
