melanosis:/ˌmɛləˈnoʊsɪs/| n. |黑变病，黑色素沉着 |A condition characterized by the deposition of melanin in tissues, causing darkening of the skin or other organs.  It can be caused by various factors, including sun exposure, hormonal changes, and certain medical conditions.  |Example:  Excessive sun exposure can lead to melanosis, resulting in dark spots on the skin.  过度日晒会导致黑变病，从而导致皮肤上出现黑斑。


|近义词: hyperpigmentation |反义词:hypopigmentation |词性:n.
