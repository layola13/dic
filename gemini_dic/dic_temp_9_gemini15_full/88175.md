hilltops:/ˈhɪlˌtɑːps/| n.|山顶；山峰|The highest points of hills or mountains.|例句：The hilltops were covered in snow. 山顶覆盖着积雪。
The sun set behind the distant hilltops. 太阳落在了远处的山顶后面。|近义词：peaks, summits, crests|反义词：valleys, bottoms
