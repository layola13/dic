词源:
From hydro- +‎ cephalic.  Hydro- comes from Greek ὑδρο- (hydro-), from ὕδωρ (hydōr, "water"). Cephalic comes from Greek κεφαλή (kephalē, "head").  Therefore, hydrocephalic literally means "relating to water in the head".
