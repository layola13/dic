rebreed:/ˌriːˈbriːd/| vt.|重新培育，再繁殖|To breed again; to breed (animals) of a particular type or with particular characteristics again.|例句：They are trying to rebreed the near-extinct panda. 他们正在尝试重新培育濒临灭绝的大熊猫。|例句：The farmer rebred his prize-winning cow with a bull of similar lineage.  这位农民用血统相似的公牛再次培育了他的获奖母牛。|近义词：breed again, repopulate|反义词：sterilize, extinguish
