mistress:/ˈmɪstrəs/| n.|女主人；情妇；女教师；女主人；|A woman who has control or authority over something or someone; a woman who is having a sexual relationship with a man who is married to someone else; a woman who is in charge of a household or establishment; a woman teacher.  |例句：She is the mistress of her own destiny. 她掌握着自己的命运。|例句：He had a mistress on the side. 他在外养了个情妇。|近义词：governess, mistress, madam, concubine |反义词：master, servant
