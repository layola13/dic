quivered:/ˈkwɪvərd/| v.|颤抖，哆嗦|to shake slightly; tremble|The leaves quivered in the breeze.  树叶在微风中颤抖。

The frightened bird quivered in my hand.  受惊的鸟在我手中颤抖。


近义词：tremble, shake, shiver, vibrate|反义词：be still, be steady|动词
