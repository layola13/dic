dory:/ˈdɔːri/ | n. |多莉（女性名）；(鱼类)多利鱼 | A type of fish, specifically a John Dory; also a popular female given name.  The name's origin is unclear, but possibly related to the French word "dorée," meaning "gilded" referring to the fish's golden color. |例句：The restaurant is famous for its pan-fried dory. 这家餐厅以其煎多利鱼而闻名。
例句：My friend's daughter's name is Dory.  我朋友的女儿名叫多莉。|近义词：Zeus faber (scientific name for John Dory)|反义词：无
