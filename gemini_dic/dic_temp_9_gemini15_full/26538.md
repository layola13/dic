cabiai:/kæbiˈaɪ/| n. |卡比亚伊（一种啮齿动物）|Capybara,  also known as the chigüire, is the largest extant rodent in the world. It is a semi-aquatic, herbivorous mammal native to South America.  Capybaras are known for their docile nature and social behavior. They live in groups near bodies of water. |例句：The capybara is a remarkable creature, known for its size and friendly disposition. 卡比亚伊是一种非凡的生物，以其体型和友好的性情而闻名。|  例句：We saw a family of capybaras relaxing by the river. 我们看到一群卡比亚伊在河边休息。|近义词：rodent,  water hog |反义词：无
