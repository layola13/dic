leatherback:/ˈleðərbæk/| n. |棱皮龟|A large sea turtle with a leathery carapace (shell) instead of a hard shell like other turtles.  They are known for their immense size and global migration patterns. | The leatherback turtle is the largest living turtle species. 棱皮龟是现存最大的龟类。|  sea turtle, marine turtle |  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with hard-shelled turtles)| 名词
