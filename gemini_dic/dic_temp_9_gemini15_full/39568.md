consciousness: /ˈkɒnʃəsnəs/ /ˈkɑːnʃəsnəs/ | n. |意识，知觉；觉知；清醒状态 |

英文解析：The state of being aware of and responsive to one's surroundings.  It involves subjective experience, self-awareness, and the ability to think and feel.  The term can refer to different levels, from simple awareness of sensory input to higher-order cognitive functions like self-reflection and abstract thought.  Philosophers and neuroscientists continue to debate the nature and origins of consciousness.


例句：

He regained consciousness after the accident.  = 他在事故后恢复了意识。

She lost consciousness and fell to the ground. = 她失去了意识，倒在了地上。


近义词：awareness, sentience, perception, wakefulness

反义词：unconsciousness, insensibility, unawareness


词性：名词
