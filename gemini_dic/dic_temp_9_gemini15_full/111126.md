literately:/ˈlɪtərətli/| adv. |  字面地；确实地；文雅地 |  In a literal sense; exactly as stated; with literary skill or elegance.  |例句：He literally jumped for joy. 他欣喜若狂。（字面意思）
例句：I literally couldn't believe my eyes. 我简直不敢相信自己的眼睛。（确实地）


近义词：literally, actually, really, truly, precisely
反义词：figuratively, metaphorically


词性:副词
