merging:/ˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/| v.|合并；融合|The act of combining or uniting two or more things into one.  This can refer to physical objects, ideas, or data streams.  It often implies a gradual or seamless process.|例句：The two companies are merging to create a larger, more powerful entity. = 两家公司正在合并，以创建一个更大、更有实力的实体。|例句：The colors in the painting are beautifully merging, creating a sense of harmony. = 画中的颜色融合得非常漂亮，营造出一种和谐感。|近义词：combining, uniting, consolidating, amalgamating|反义词：separating, dividing, splitting|动词
