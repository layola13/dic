kicks-in:/kɪks ɪn/| v. | 开始起作用；生效；突然开始 | To begin to have an effect; to start suddenly. | The painkillers will kick in soon. 止痛药很快就会起作用了。|The music kicked in and everyone started dancing. 音乐响起来，每个人都开始跳舞。| commence, begin, start | cease, stop, end | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medication kicks in after about 30 minutes. = 药物大约30分钟后开始起效。
This new system kicks in next month. = 这个新系统下个月开始启用。


一般过去时:
The anesthesia kicked in quickly. = 麻醉药很快起效了。
The rain kicked in during the afternoon. = 下午开始下雨了。


现在完成时:
The effects of the drug have already kicked in. = 药物的效果已经开始显现了。
My allergies have kicked in again.  = 我的过敏症又犯了。


过去完成时:
By the time we reached the hospital, the pain had kicked in. = 等我们到达医院时，疼痛已经开始了。
Before the meeting, the tension had kicked in. = 会议开始前，紧张气氛已经出现。


将来时:
The new law will kick in next year. = 新法律明年生效。
The effects of the alcohol will kick in later. = 酒精的作用会在稍后显现。


虚拟语气:
If the medicine kicked in sooner, he would feel better. = 如果药效更快些，他会感觉好些。
I wish the inspiration would kick in soon. = 我希望灵感能尽快到来。


被动语态:
The new rules were kicked in last week. = 新规是上周开始实施的。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The changes will be kicked in gradually. = 这些改变将会逐步实施。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
When will the effects of the medicine kick in? = 药物何时开始起效？
Has the plan kicked in yet? = 计划开始实施了吗？


否定句:
The pain relief didn't kick in for hours. = 止痛药几个小时才起效。
The system hasn't kicked in yet. = 系统还没开始运行。


PHRASE->

kick in the habit  戒除坏习惯
kick in the butt  激励；刺激
kick in the gears  加速；努力工作
