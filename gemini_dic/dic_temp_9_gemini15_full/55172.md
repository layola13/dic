dissemination:/ˌdɪsɛmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |传播，散布 | The act of spreading something, especially information, widely; the process of being spread widely.  |例句：The dissemination of information is crucial in a crisis.  危机中信息的传播至关重要。

例句：The rapid dissemination of the news across social media platforms was unprecedented.  新闻在社交媒体平台上的快速传播是前所未有的。


近义词：spread, distribution, propagation, diffusion | 反义词：suppression, concealment, withholding | 词性：名词
