peaky:/ˈpiːki/ | adj. |憔悴的，苍白的，顶峰的 |形容人脸色苍白，身体虚弱，也指山峰的顶端。It can describe someone who looks pale and unhealthy, often due to illness or exhaustion.  It can also refer to the peak or summit of a mountain. |例句：He looked peaky after his illness. 他病后脸色苍白。  The peaky summit of the mountain was shrouded in mist. 山峰的顶端笼罩在雾中。|近义词：pale, sickly, wan, peaked |反义词：healthy, robust, ruddy
