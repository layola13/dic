nonreticently:/ˌnɒnˈrɛtɪsəntli/ | adv. |不隐瞒地；坦率地；直言不讳地 |  Not reticently; frankly; openly.  The word combines the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with "reticently" (meaning "in a reticent manner"). Reticent means reserved or uncommunicative in speech; reluctant to speak. Therefore, nonreticently signifies the opposite: a lack of reserve or reluctance in speaking. |She spoke nonreticently about her past mistakes. 她坦率地谈论了她过去的错误。| openly, frankly, candidly, forthrightly | reticently, reservedly, shyly, taciturnly | 副词
