cubing:/ˈkjuːbɪŋ/| n.|立方；魔方游戏；三次方|The act of solving a Rubik's Cube or similar puzzles, or the activity of creating cubes.|例句：He spends hours each day cubing. 他每天花数小时玩魔方。  例句：Cubing is a popular hobby for many people.  玩魔方是许多人的热门爱好。|近义词：cube solving, puzzle solving|反义词：无|名词
