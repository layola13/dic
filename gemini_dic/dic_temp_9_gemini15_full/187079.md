riverless:/ˈrɪvərləs/| adj.|无河流的，缺水的|Without rivers; lacking rivers.  The desert landscape was riverless and barren.|近义词：waterless, arid, dry |反义词：riverine, well-watered

一般现在时:
The area is riverless and unsuitable for agriculture. = 这个地区没有河流，不适合农业发展。
This region remains riverless despite the recent rainfall. = 尽管最近下了雨，这个地区仍然没有河流。

一般过去时:
The land was riverless before the dam was built. = 在水坝建成之前，这片土地上没有河流。
The once fertile valley became riverless after the prolonged drought. = 持续的干旱之后，曾经肥沃的山谷变得没有河流了。

The region has remained riverless for centuries. = 这个地区几个世纪以来一直没有河流。
The drought has left this once-lush area completely riverless. = 干旱使得这个曾经郁郁葱葱的地区完全没有河流了。

过去完成时:
The explorers discovered that the area had been riverless for many years. = 探险家们发现，这个地区多年来一直没有河流。
Before the great flood, the plains had been largely riverless. = 在大洪水之前，平原大部分地区都没有河流。

将来时:
Without intervention, the region will likely remain riverless. = 如果不采取干预措施，这个地区很可能仍然没有河流。
Climate change predictions suggest the area will become riverless in the coming decades. = 气候变化预测表明，该地区将在未来几十年内变成无河流地区。

虚拟语气:
If the river were to dry up, the land would become riverless. = 如果河流干涸，这片土地将变得没有河流。
I wish this arid landscape weren't so riverless. = 我希望这片干旱的土地不会如此缺乏河流。

被动语态:
The area was left riverless by the receding glacier. = 冰川消退后，该地区没有河流了。
The land has been rendered riverless by deforestation. = 森林砍伐使得这片土地没有河流了。

疑问句:
Is this region naturally riverless, or is it a result of human activity? = 这个地区天生就没有河流，还是人为活动的结果？
Are there any plans to address the riverless condition of this area? = 有什么计划来解决这个地区缺水的状况吗？

否定句:
The area is not entirely riverless; there are a few small streams. = 这个地区并非完全没有河流；有一些小溪流。
This land was not always riverless; historical records show a thriving river system. = 这片土地并非一直没有河流；历史记录显示曾经有一个繁荣的河流系统。
