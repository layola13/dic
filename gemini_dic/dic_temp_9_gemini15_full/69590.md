feltness:/ˈfɛltnəs/| n. |触感，质感|The quality of being felt; the tactile quality of something.  It refers to the sensation experienced when touching a surface or object, encompassing texture, temperature, and other physical properties.  |例句：The feltness of the silk was exquisite. 丝绸的触感极其细腻。

例句：The rough feltness of the stone contrasted sharply with the smoothness of the wood. 石头的粗糙触感与木材的光滑触感形成鲜明对比。


近义词：texture, tactile quality, surface feel


反义词：smoothness (in contrast), lack of texture


词性：名词
