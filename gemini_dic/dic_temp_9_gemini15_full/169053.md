programmers:/ˈproʊɡræmər z/| n. |程序员，编程人员|A programmer is a person who writes computer programs.  Programmers design, write, test, and maintain the code that makes software work.|Example:  She's a talented programmer who specializes in artificial intelligence. 她是一位擅长人工智能的才华横溢的程序员。|近义词：coder, developer, software engineer|反义词：None
