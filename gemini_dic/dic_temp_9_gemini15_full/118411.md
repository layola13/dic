merriment:/ˈmɛrɪmənt/| n. |欢闹；欢乐；嬉戏|The feeling of great happiness and enjoyment.  It often involves laughter and lively activity.  |例句：Their merriment filled the room.  他们的欢笑充满了整个房间。

例句：The children's merriment was infectious. 孩子们的欢乐是具有感染力的。


近义词：mirth, gaiety, jollity, hilarity, glee|反义词：sadness, gloom, sorrow, misery|词性：名词
