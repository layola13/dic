catchiest:/ˈkætʃiest/| adj.|最吸引人的，最容易记住的|Most likely to attract attention or be remembered.  It's the superlative form of "catchy."|例句：That song has the catchiest melody I've heard all year.  今年我听到的歌里，这首歌的旋律最吸引人。

例句：Her marketing campaign used the catchiest slogan to promote the new product.她的营销活动使用了最吸引人的口号来推广新产品。


近义词：most memorable, most appealing, most attractive, most striking|反义词：unmemorable, forgettable, dull, uninteresting|词性：形容词
