monomaniacally:/ˌmɒnəʊˈmeɪniəkəli/| adj.|偏执狂似的，着魔般地|characterized by excessive concentration on a single object or idea; obsessively.  It describes actions or behavior driven by a single, all-consuming obsession.|例句：He worked monomaniacally on his project, neglecting his health and relationships. 他痴迷于他的项目，忽略了他的健康和人际关系。 He pursued his goal monomaniacally, refusing to consider any alternative. 他痴迷地追求他的目标，拒绝考虑任何替代方案。|近义词：obsessively, maniacally, fanatically, fixatedly|反义词：calmly, rationally, objectively, dispassionately|词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acts monomaniacally. = 他行为偏执。
She thinks monomaniacally about her work. = 她痴迷于工作。

一般过去时:
He acted monomaniacally during the presentation. = 他在演示过程中行为偏执。
She thought monomaniacally about the problem all day. = 她整天都痴迷地想着这个问题。

现在完成时:
He has acted monomaniacally for years. = 他多年来一直行为偏执。
She has thought monomaniacally about this project since last month. = 自从上个月以来，她一直痴迷于这个项目。

过去完成时:
He had acted monomaniacally before anyone noticed. = 在任何人注意到之前，他就已经行为偏执了。
She had thought monomaniacally about the problem for weeks before finding a solution. = 在找到解决方案之前，她已经痴迷地思考这个问题好几个星期了。

将来时:
He will act monomaniacally if he doesn't get his way. = 如果他不顺心，他就会行为偏执。
She will think monomaniacally about the upcoming exam. = 她会痴迷地思考即将到来的考试。

虚拟语气:
If he were not so monomaniacal, he would have more friends. = 如果他不那么偏执，他就会有更多朋友。
If she hadn't been so monomaniacally focused, she might have noticed the error. = 如果她没有那么痴迷于目标，她可能会注意到这个错误。


被动语态:
His behavior was described as monomaniacal. = 他的行为被描述为偏执的。
Her focus was seen as monomaniacal by her colleagues. = 她的专注被她的同事视为偏执的。


疑问句:
Is his behavior monomaniacal? = 他的行为是偏执的吗？
Was her approach to the problem monomaniacal? = 她解决问题的方法是偏执的吗？


否定句:
His actions were not monomaniacal; he was simply very focused. = 他的行为并非偏执；他只是非常专注。
Her approach to the problem was not monomaniacal; she considered various options. = 她解决问题的方法并非偏执；她考虑了各种选择。


PHRASE->
monomaniacally focused = 极度专注的
monomaniacally driven = 被单一目标驱使的
monomaniacally obsessed = 痴迷于…的
monomaniacally determined = 坚定地决心…的
monomaniacally pursued = 痴迷地追求…的


词源:
From mono- (meaning "one" or "single") + maniac (referring to a person exhibiting mania, or excessive excitement or enthusiasm), ultimately from Greek *manikos ("mad").  The suffix "-ally" indicates manner or way.
