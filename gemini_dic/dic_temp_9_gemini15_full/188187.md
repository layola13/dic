rovingly:/ˈroʊvɪŋli/ | adv. | 漫游地；漂泊地；四处游荡地 | Moving from place to place without a fixed plan; wandering.  It implies a lack of direction or purpose, often suggesting a sense of freedom or exploration, but sometimes also implying aimlessness. | 例句：He looked rovingly about the room, taking in every detail. 他漫不经心地环顾房间，注意着每一个细节。

He travelled rovingly across the country, never staying in one place for long. 他漫无目的地游历全国，从未在一个地方久留。


近义词：wanderingly, aimlessly, erratically, haphazardly | 反义词：purposefully, systematically, methodically | 词性:副词
