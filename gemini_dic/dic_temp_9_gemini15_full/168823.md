productivity:/ˌprɒdʌkˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌprɑːdʌkˈtɪvɪti/| n. |生产力；生产率；生产能力|The ability to produce goods or services efficiently.  It often refers to the amount of output produced per unit of input (e.g., labor, capital).  High productivity is a key driver of economic growth.|例句：The company's productivity increased significantly after they implemented the new software.  这家公司在实施新软件后生产力显著提高。

例句：Improved working conditions led to a boost in worker productivity.  改善的工作条件导致工人生产力提高。


近义词：output, efficiency, yield, effectiveness|反义词：inefficiency, idleness, stagnation|词性：n.
