maharashtra: /ˌmʌhəˈrɑːʃtrə/ | n. | 马哈拉施特拉邦 |

英文解析:Maharashtra is a state in western India. It is the second-most populous state in India and the third-largest by area.  The state is known for its diverse geography, culture, and history.  It's home to the city of Mumbai (formerly Bombay), a major financial center.

词源:
