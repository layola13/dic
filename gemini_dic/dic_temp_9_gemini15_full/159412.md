polarity:/poʊˈlærəti/| n. |极性；极化；正负性 |The inherent property of having two opposite poles or ends.  This can refer to physical phenomena like magnetism or electricity, or to more abstract concepts like opinions or attitudes.  For example, a magnet has a north and south pole, demonstrating polarity.  Similarly, a debate often exhibits polarity with opposing viewpoints. |例句：The polarity of the battery determines the direction of the current. 电池的极性决定电流的方向。


The experiment showed a clear polarity in the responses. 实验结果显示反应存在明显的极性差异。

近义词：opposition, duality, dichotomy |反义词：neutrality, uniformity, homogeneity |名词
