midpoints:/ˈmɪdˌpɔɪnts/| n. |中点，中值 |The midpoints represent the average values of the data points.| 中点代表数据点的平均值。| average, median | extremes, endpoints |名词


The midpoint of a line segment is the point that divides the segment into two equal parts.  In mathematics, a midpoint can refer to the middle point of a line segment or the average of a set of numbers.  The concept extends to higher dimensions as well.


例句：

The midpoint of the line segment connecting (1, 2) and (3, 4) is (2, 3).  连接(1,2)和(3,4)的线段的中点是(2,3)。

We calculated the midpoint of the data set to find the average price. 我们计算了数据集的中点以找到平均价格。


近义词:

center, middle, average

反义词:

endpoints, extremes
