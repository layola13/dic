refiled:/ˌriːˈfaɪld/| v.|重新提交；再次提出|To file again; to submit something again after it has been rejected or withdrawn.|例句：The amended application was refiled with the court.  经修改后的申请已重新提交法院。

例句：He refiled his tax return after discovering an error. 他发现一个错误后重新提交了他的纳税申报单。


近义词：resubmit, re-present|反义词：withdraw, reject|词性:动词
