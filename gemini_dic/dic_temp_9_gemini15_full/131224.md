noncalculative: /ˌnɒnˈkælkjuleɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非计算的；不计较的；不精打细算的 | Not involving or based on calculation; not calculating or shrewd.  It implies a lack of deliberate planning or strategic thinking, often suggesting spontaneity or impulsiveness. |


例句：

His noncalculative generosity surprised everyone.  他的不计较的慷慨让每个人都吃惊。

Her noncalculative approach to life led to unexpected adventures. 她不精打细算的生活方式带来了意想不到的冒险。


近义词：uncalculating, impulsive, spontaneous, unthinking, instinctive


反义词：calculating, shrewd, strategic, calculating, premeditated
