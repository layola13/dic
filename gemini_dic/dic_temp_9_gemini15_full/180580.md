register:/ˈrɛdʒɪstər/ /ˈrɛdʒɪstɚ/ | n., v. |登记；注册；记录；显示；表达；（尤指乐器）音域；（尤指语言）风格；（尤指社会地位）等级；|

英文解析：The noun "register" refers to an official record or list of names, items, or events.  As a verb, it means to record something officially or to show or express something.  It can also refer to a range of musical notes or a level of formality in language.


例句：

n. (名词):
The hotel register showed that a Mr. Smith had checked in. = 旅馆登记簿显示史密斯先生已经入住。

The cash register showed a shortage of $50. = 收银机显示少了50美元。


v. (动词):
I need to register for the class before the deadline. = 我需要在截止日期之前注册这门课。

He registered his disapproval with a frown. = 他皱着眉头表示他的不满。


近义词：

n. (名词): record, list, log, roll, catalogue,  scale (musical), level, style
v. (动词): enroll, sign up, record, enter, show, express, indicate


反义词：

n. (名词):  (部分反义词取决于语境，没有直接的反义词)
v. (动词): ignore, conceal, suppress
