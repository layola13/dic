preconvey:/ˌpriːkənˈveɪ/| vt.|预先传达；预先告知|To communicate or impart something beforehand; to give advance notice of.|例句：The manager preconveyed the bad news to the employees before the official announcement.  经理在正式宣布之前就预先向员工传达了这个坏消息。
例句：They preconveyed their intentions to negotiate a new contract. 他们预先告知了他们要协商一份新合同的意图。|近义词：preinform, forewarn, preannounce|反义词：conceal, withhold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preconveys his plans to his team every week. = 他每周都向他的团队预先传达他的计划。
The company preconveys its marketing strategies to its partners. = 公司向合作伙伴预先传达其营销策略。
