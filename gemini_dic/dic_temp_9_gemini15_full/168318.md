pro-Philippine: /ˌproʊ ˈfɪlɪpiːn/ | adj. |亲菲律宾的，支持菲律宾的 | Favoring or supporting the Philippines.  This term describes people, policies, or actions that show support for the Philippines, its people, or its government. It can refer to political stances, economic relationships, or cultural affiliations. | 例句：The pro-Philippine sentiment was high after the disaster relief efforts. = 灾难救援工作之后，亲菲律宾情绪高涨。

The pro-Philippine candidate won the election by a landslide. = 支持菲律宾的候选人在选举中以压倒性优势获胜。


近义词：pro-Filipino, Philippine-supporting, favorable to the Philippines | 反义词：anti-Philippine
