group:/ɡruːp/| n. v.|群体，小组；分组，归类 |A group of people gathered to discuss the issue.一群人聚集在一起讨论这个问题。| team,cluster,bunch | individual, single | 名词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They group the students by ability. = 他们按能力对学生分组。
The birds group together for warmth. = 鸟儿们聚集在一起取暖。


一般过去时:
She grouped the flowers by color. = 她按颜色把花分好组。
We grouped the data into categories. = 我们把数据分成几类。


现在完成时:
They have grouped the participants into teams. = 他们已将参与者分成几个小组。
Scientists have grouped these species according to their characteristics. = 科学家们根据这些物种的特征对它们进行了分类。


过去完成时:
They had grouped the evidence before presenting their case. = 他们在陈述案情之前就已经把证据归类了。
The students had grouped themselves into study partners before the exam. = 学生们在考试前就已经自行组成学习小组了。


将来时:
We will group the items by weight. = 我们将按重量对物品进行分组。
They will group the volunteers according to their skills. = 他们将根据志愿者的技能对他们进行分组。


虚拟语气:
If we grouped the data differently, the results might be different. = 如果我们对数据进行不同的分组，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish I had grouped the tasks more effectively. = 我希望我之前能更有效地安排任务。


被动语态:
The students were grouped alphabetically. = 学生们按字母顺序分组。
The data is grouped by region. = 数据按地区分组。


疑问句:
How are the participants grouped? = 参与者是如何分组的？
How should we group these items? = 我们应该如何对这些物品进行分组？


否定句:
They did not group the data correctly. = 他们没有正确地对数据进行分组。
The students were not grouped by age. = 学生们没有按年龄分组。
