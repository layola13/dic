flagrantly:/ˈfleɪɡrəntli/ | adv. |公然地；明目张胆地 |  Openly and unashamedly; conspicuously.  It describes an action done in a very noticeable and shameless way, often implying a disregard for rules or morality. | 例句：He flagrantly disregarded the safety regulations. 他公然无视安全条例。

They flagrantly lied to the police.  他们公然向警方撒谎。


近义词：blatantly, openly, shamelessly, conspicuously, audaciously

反义词：secretly, subtly, discreetly, cautiously


词性：副词
