pitbulls:/ˈpɪtˌbʊlz/| n.|比特犬（复数）|A type of dog breed, known for its muscular build and powerful jaws.  Pit bulls are often associated with aggression, but their temperament can vary greatly depending on training and socialization.|例句：Pitbulls require strong training and socialization to become well-behaved companions. 比特犬需要严格的训练和社会化才能成为行为良好的伴侣。|例句：Many responsible owners prove that pitbulls can be loving and loyal family pets. 许多负责任的主人证明比特犬可以成为充满爱和忠诚的家庭宠物。|近义词：American Pit Bull Terrier, Staffordshire Bull Terrier, American Staffordshire Terrier|反义词：无
