hypothesizes:/haɪˈpɒθəsaɪzɪz/ | v. | 假设；提出假设 | To formulate a hypothesis; to propose a theory as a possible explanation.  | The scientist hypothesizes that climate change is accelerating. 科学家假设气候变化正在加速。|  postulate, theorize, conjecture |  disprove, confirm | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hypothesizes that the earth is flat. 他假设地球是平的。
She hypothesizes a connection between diet and disease. 她假设饮食和疾病之间存在联系。


一般过去时:
The researcher hypothesized that stress contributes to heart disease. 研究人员假设压力会导致心脏病。
They hypothesized a link between pollution and respiratory illness. 他们假设污染和呼吸系统疾病之间存在联系。


现在完成时:
Scientists have hypothesized about the origins of the universe for centuries. 科学家们几个世纪以来一直在假设宇宙的起源。
She has hypothesized a new treatment for cancer. 她已经提出了一种新的癌症治疗方法。


过去完成时:
The investigators had hypothesized that the suspect was guilty before finding the evidence. 在找到证据之前，调查人员就假设嫌疑人有罪。
They had hypothesized a different mechanism before the new data became available. 在新的数据可用之前，他们假设了一种不同的机制。


将来时:
The team will hypothesize about the cause of the anomaly. 该团队将假设异常的原因。
She will hypothesize a new model to explain the phenomenon. 她将假设一个新的模型来解释这种现象。


虚拟语气:
If I were to hypothesize, I would say that the problem stems from poor management. 如果我要假设的话，我会说问题源于管理不善。
I wish I had hypothesized differently before starting the experiment. 我希望在开始实验之前我能够有不同的假设。


被动语态:
It was hypothesized that the drug would have side effects.  人们假设这种药物会有副作用。
A new theory was hypothesized to explain the results.  人们提出了一种新的理论来解释结果。


疑问句:
What does the author hypothesize about the causes of war? 作者对战争的原因作了什么假设？
What did the experiment hypothesize to test? 实验假设检验什么？


否定句:
The study did not hypothesize a direct relationship between the variables.  这项研究没有假设变量之间存在直接关系。
He did not hypothesize that this would be the outcome. 他没有假设结果会是这样。


PHRASE->
hypothesize about  关于…作假设
hypothesize that  假设…
hypothesize a link  假设一种联系
formulate a hypothesis  形成一个假设
test a hypothesis  检验一个假设
