facilitating:/fəˈsɪlɪteɪtɪŋ/| v. |促进；帮助；使容易|Making (a process or activity) easier or more likely to happen.  |例句：The new software is facilitating communication between departments. 新软件正在促进部门间的沟通。
例句：The government is facilitating the development of small businesses. 政府正在促进小型企业的发展。|近义词：assisting, aiding, easing, simplifying |反义词：hindering, obstructing, impeding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher is facilitating a discussion among the students. = 老师正在促进学生之间的讨论。
They facilitate the smooth running of the project. = 他们促进项目的顺利进行。


一般过去时:
The mediator facilitated a settlement between the two parties. = 调解人促成了双方之间的和解。
The workshop facilitated a better understanding of the issue. = 研讨会促进了对该问题的更好理解。


现在完成时:
The new policy has facilitated economic growth. = 新政策促进了经济增长。
We have facilitated many successful business deals. = 我们促成了许多成功的商业交易。


过去完成时:
They had facilitated the process before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前促进了这个过程。
The company had facilitated the merger smoothly. = 公司顺利地促成了合并。


将来时:
The conference will facilitate networking opportunities. = 会议将促进交流机会。
The training program will facilitate employee development. = 培训项目将促进员工发展。


虚拟语气:
If we had better resources, we could facilitate the project more effectively. = 如果我们有更好的资源，我们可以更有效地促进项目。
I wish the government would facilitate the process of obtaining visas. = 我希望政府能简化获得签证的过程。


被动语态:
The meeting was facilitated by the project manager. = 会议由项目经理主持。
The agreement was facilitated through negotiation. = 协议是通过谈判达成的。


疑问句:
How can we facilitate the learning process? = 我们如何才能促进学习过程？
What measures will facilitate the transition? = 采取什么措施才能促进过渡？


否定句:
The lack of funding did not facilitate the research. = 资金不足并没有促进研究。
The bureaucracy did not facilitate the process. = 官僚主义并没有简化流程。
