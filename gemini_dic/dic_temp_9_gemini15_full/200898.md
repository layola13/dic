slipcovered:/ˈslɪpkʌvərd/| adj.|带滑套的，罩着套子的|Covered with a slipcover.  A slipcover is a loose-fitting cover for furniture, typically a sofa or chair.|例句：The slipcovered sofa looked brand new after cleaning. 清洁后，带滑套的沙发看起来焕然一新。  The slipcovered armchair was comfortable and easy to clean. 带滑套的扶手椅舒适且易于清洁。|近义词：covered, upholstered (if the slipcover is permanent)|反义词：uncovered, bare
