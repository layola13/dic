poseidon:/ˈpɒsɪdɒn/ /pɒˈsaɪdn/| n.|波塞冬，海神|The Greek god of the sea, earthquakes, storms, and horses.  He is the equivalent of the Roman god Neptune.|例句：Poseidon was a powerful god in Greek mythology. 波塞冬是希腊神话中一位强大的神。

例句：The raging storm was attributed to the wrath of Poseidon. 肆虐的风暴被归咎于波塞冬的愤怒。


近义词：Neptune


反义词：无


词性：名词
