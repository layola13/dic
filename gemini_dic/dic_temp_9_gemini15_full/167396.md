preuniversity: /ˌpriːjuːnɪˈvɜːrsɪti/ | adj. |大学预科的；大学前的|  Relating to or denoting the period of education before university.  It often refers to the final years of secondary school education that prepare students for university. |例句：He is currently enrolled in a preuniversity program. 他目前正在参加大学预科课程。  |例句：Preuniversity courses often focus on preparing students for university entrance exams. 大学预科课程通常侧重于为大学入学考试做准备。|近义词：pre-college, secondary, high school |反义词：postgraduate, university
