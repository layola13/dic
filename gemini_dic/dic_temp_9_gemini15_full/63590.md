envisioned:/ɪnˈvɪʒənd/| v. |预见；想象；设想 | To have a mental picture of something that is likely to happen in the future.  It often implies a hopeful or positive expectation. |例句：The architect envisioned a building that would be both functional and aesthetically pleasing. 建筑师设想了一座既实用又美观的建筑。

例句：She envisioned a future where everyone had access to clean water. 她设想了一个每个人都能获得清洁用水的未来。


近义词：foresee, imagine, visualize, contemplate, picture


反义词：ignore, overlook, disregard


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He envisions a bright future for his company. = 他设想公司拥有光明的未来。
They envision a world without poverty. = 他们设想一个没有贫困的世界。


一般过去时:
She envisioned a beautiful garden. = 她想象了一个美丽的庭院。
The artist envisioned a vibrant cityscape. = 艺术家设想了一个充满活力的城市景观。


现在完成时:
We have envisioned a new strategy for marketing. = 我们已经设想了一个新的营销策略。
They have envisioned a solution to the problem. = 他们已经设想出了问题的解决方案。


过去完成时:
He had envisioned a successful career before the accident. = 事故发生前，他曾设想过一个成功的职业生涯。
She had envisioned a different life for herself. = 她曾设想过另一种生活。


将来时:
They will envision a better future for their children. = 他们将为孩子们设想一个更美好的未来。
We will envision a more sustainable way of living. = 我们将设想一种更可持续的生活方式。


虚拟语气:
If I envisioned success, I would work harder. = 如果我预见到成功，我会更加努力工作。
I wish I had envisioned the consequences. = 我真希望我当时预见到后果。


被动语态:
A brighter future was envisioned by the community leaders. = 社区领导人设想了一个更光明的未来。
A new approach was envisioned by the team. = 团队设想了一种新方法。


疑问句:
What did you envision for your future? = 你对你的未来有什么设想？
What do they envision achieving this year? = 他们设想今年能取得什么成就？


否定句:
He did not envision the difficulties he would face. = 他没有预见到他将面临的困难。
They did not envision such a dramatic change. = 他们没有预料到如此剧烈的变化。
