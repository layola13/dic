pillock:/ˈpɪlək/| n.|蠢货，笨蛋|A pillock is a British English informal term for a foolish or stupid person.  It's considered vulgar and offensive. |Example:  He's such a pillock; he forgot his own birthday.|近义词：fool, idiot, twit, buffoon|反义词：genius, intellectual, mastermind|名词
