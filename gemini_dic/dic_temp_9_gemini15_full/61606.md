emetics:/ɪˈmɛtɪks/| n. |催吐剂；催吐药|Substances that induce vomiting.  Emetics are used in some cases of poisoning to remove toxins from the body, although their use is becoming less common due to potential risks.|Example:  The doctor administered an emetic to the patient who had ingested poison. 医生给误服毒药的病人服用了一种催吐剂。|近义词：vomitive, emetoc |反义词：anti-emetic
