The word "collaborer" is not a standard English word.  There is no entry for it in major dictionaries. The correct spelling is "collaborator".


collaborator: /kəˈlæbəreɪtər/ | n. |合作者，协作者，勾结者 | a person who works jointly on an activity or project |例句：He was a collaborator on several successful films. 他是几部成功电影的合作者。


英文解析：A collaborator works with others, especially on a project or piece of work.  The word can have a negative connotation, especially in historical contexts, referring to someone who aids the enemy during wartime.


例句：

He was a valued collaborator on the research project. 他是该研究项目中一位宝贵的合作者。

The artist collaborated with a musician on the soundtrack.这位艺术家与一位音乐家合作创作了电影配乐。


近义词：co-worker, colleague, associate, partner


反义词：opponent, adversary, antagonist (in the context of cooperation vs. opposition)


词性: noun
