noncustomers:/ˌnɒnˈkʌstəməz/ | n. |非顾客，潜在顾客 |People who are not currently customers but could become customers.  This term is often used in marketing and business strategy to refer to groups of people who might be interested in a product or service but haven't purchased it yet. | Example:  The company is trying to attract noncustomers through targeted advertising campaigns. 这家公司正在尝试通过有针对性的广告宣传吸引非顾客。  |近义词：prospects, potential customers, leads |反义词：customers, clients |名词
