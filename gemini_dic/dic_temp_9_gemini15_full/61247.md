USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and thus doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It describes how something happens.  The usage is primarily descriptive within a sentence.

Example 1:
The warmth emanated emanatively from the fireplace.  壁炉里散发着温暖。

Example 2:
Her joy emanated emanatively, brightening the room.  她的喜悦洋溢而出，照亮了房间。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "emanatively".  Its use is typically within a sentence describing the manner of emanating.  Phrases that might include it would be built around the verb "emanate".  For example:

emanate emanatively from = 从...散发出来
emanate emanatively with = 散发出...
