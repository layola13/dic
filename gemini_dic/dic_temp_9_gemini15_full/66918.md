expenses:/ɪkˈspɛnsɪz/ | n. | 费用，支出 | Expenses are the money spent on something.  |  例句：Our monthly expenses are too high. = 我们每月的支出太高了。

例句：The company needs to cut its expenses to stay profitable. = 公司需要削减开支以保持盈利。


近义词：costs, outlays, expenditures | 反义词：income, revenue, profit | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Our expenses are high this month. = 我们这个月的开支很高。
His expenses cover travel and accommodation. = 他的费用包括差旅和住宿。


一般过去时:
Last year's expenses were lower than this year's. = 去年的支出低于今年的。
The project expenses exceeded the budget. = 项目支出超过了预算。


现在完成时:
We have incurred significant expenses this year. = 今年我们已经产生了大量的支出。
The company has covered all its expenses this quarter. = 公司本季度已经支付了所有费用。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, we had exhausted all our expenses. = 到项目结束时，我们已经花光了所有的钱。
They had already paid all their expenses before the trip. = 他们在旅行前已经支付了所有的费用。


将来时:
Our expenses will increase next year. = 明年的支出将会增加。
We will carefully track our expenses for the next quarter. = 我们将仔细跟踪下个季度的支出。


虚拟语气:
If our expenses were lower, we could save more money. = 如果我们的支出更低，我们就能省下更多的钱。
I wish our expenses were not so high. = 我希望我们的支出不要那么高。


被动语态:
The expenses were carefully reviewed by the accountant. = 这些费用被会计仔细审查过了。
All expenses are paid by the company. = 所有费用都由公司支付。


疑问句:
What are the expenses for this project? = 这个项目的费用是多少？
How much are the monthly expenses? = 每月的费用是多少？


否定句:
The expenses were not as high as we expected. = 支出并不像我们预期的那样高。
He did not account for all his expenses. = 他没有计算他所有的支出。


PHRASE->
business expenses: 业务支出
living expenses: 生活费用
travel expenses: 差旅费
household expenses: 家庭开支
unexpected expenses: 意外开支
