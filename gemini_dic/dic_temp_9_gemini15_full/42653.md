counterstate:/ˈkaʊntərˌsteɪt/| n.|对抗性国家，敌对国家|A counterstate is a political entity that opposes or challenges the authority of an established state.  It often lacks formal recognition but exerts significant influence within a territory.  This could be a rebel group, a separatist movement, or a shadow government operating in defiance of the legitimate government.|例句：The rebel group declared itself a counterstate and began issuing its own currency.  这个叛乱组织宣布自己是一个对抗性国家，并开始发行自己的货币。|近义词：rebel group, insurgent state, opposition movement|反义词：established state, legitimate government|名词
