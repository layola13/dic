sidecars:/ˈsaɪdˌkɑːrz/| n.|边车；（摩托车的）侧车|A sidecar is a vehicle, usually with three wheels, that is attached to the side of a motorcycle and used to carry a passenger.  It's often used in racing and as a mode of transportation.|例句：He rode a motorcycle with a sidecar. 他骑着一辆带边车的摩托车。|例句：The vintage sidecar was a beautiful piece of machinery. 这辆复古边车是一件精美的机器。|近义词：motorcycle with a sidecar, three-wheeled vehicle|反义词:|词性:名词
