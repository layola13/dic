prisms:/ˈprɪzəm/| n.|棱镜，棱柱体|A prism is a solid geometric figure whose two ends are similar, equal, and parallel rectilinear figures, and whose sides are parallelograms.  It can also refer to a device that separates light into its constituent colors.|例句：The prism separated the white light into a spectrum of colors.  棱镜将白光分离成光谱的颜色。  The crystal had the shape of a prism.  这个水晶的形状像个棱镜。|近义词：crystal, refractor|反义词：无
