coverings:/ˈkʌvərɪŋz/| n. |覆盖物；遮盖物|Things that cover or protect something else.  They can be physical objects or abstract concepts.|例句：The coverings on the furniture were elegant brocade. 家具上的罩子是华丽的织锦。

例句：The earth's coverings are diverse, from lush rainforests to barren deserts. 地球上的覆盖物多种多样，从茂密的热带雨林到荒凉的沙漠。


近义词：cover, sheaths, wraps, linings, overlays |反义词：exposures, unveilings |词性:名词


词源:  From the verb "cover" + the suffix "-ings," indicating a collection or result of an action.  The root of "cover" traces back to Old French "cuvrir," ultimately from Latin "cooperire" meaning "to cover over."
