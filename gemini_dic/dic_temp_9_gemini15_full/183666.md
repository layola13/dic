/ˌriːrɪˈvjuː/ | v. | 再审查；复审 | To review something again, typically with more thoroughness or from a different perspective.  |  The judge decided to rereview the case due to new evidence.  法官决定由于新的证据而重新审查此案。| review, reconsider |  accept, approve | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rereview the contract every year. = 他们每年都会重新审查合同。
We rereview the data to ensure accuracy. = 我们复查数据以确保准确性。

一般过去时:
The committee rereviewed the proposal last week. = 委员会上周重新审查了提案。
She rereviewed her notes before the exam. = 她考试前复习了笔记。

现在完成时:
The team has rereviewed the design several times. = 该团队已经多次重新审查了设计。
He has rereviewed the manuscript and made corrections. = 他已经重新审阅了手稿并进行了更正。

过去完成时:
They had rereviewed the evidence before making a decision. = 他们做出决定之前已经重新审查了证据。
She had rereviewed the document before submitting it. =  她在提交文件之前已经重新审查过它了。

将来时:
We will rereview the budget next month. = 我们下个月将重新审查预算。
The court will rereview the sentence. = 法院将重新审查判决。

虚拟语气:
If they rereviewed the evidence, they might change their minds. = 如果他们重新审查证据，他们可能会改变主意。
I wish they had rereviewed the contract before signing. = 我希望他们在签字前重新审查过合同。

被动语态:
The report will be rereviewed by the board. = 该报告将由董事会重新审查。
The decision was rereviewed and overturned. = 该决定被重新审查并推翻。

疑问句:
Will the application be rereviewed? = 申请会被重新审查吗？
Have they rereviewed the findings? = 他们重新审查了调查结果吗？

否定句:
They did not rereview the data thoroughly enough. = 他们没有充分彻底地审查数据。
We will not rereview the case again. = 我们不会再次审查此案。


PHRASE->

rereview the proposal = 重新审查提案
