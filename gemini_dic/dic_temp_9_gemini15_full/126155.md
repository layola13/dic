mutter:/ˈmʌtər/| vt. vi. n.|低声抱怨；咕哝；喃喃自语|To speak in a low, indistinct tone, often expressing dissatisfaction or displeasure.  It can also refer to the act of speaking indistinctly or to the sound of such speech.|例句：He muttered something under his breath. 他低声咕哝着什么。

He muttered his disapproval. 他低声表达了他的不满。


近义词：murmur, grumble, mumble|反义词：shout, yell, articulate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mutters to himself constantly. = 他不停地自言自语。
She mutters complaints about her job. = 她低声抱怨她的工作。


一般过去时:
He muttered a curse under his breath. = 他低声咒骂了一句。
She muttered something about being tired. = 她低声说了些什么关于疲倦的话。


现在完成时:
He has muttered his displeasure all morning. = 他整个上午都在低声表达不满。
She has muttered about the unfairness of it all. = 她一直在低声抱怨这一切的不公平。


过去完成时:
He had muttered his concerns before the meeting. = 会议前他曾低声表达过他的担忧。
She had muttered apologies before she left. = 她离开前低声说了几句道歉的话。


将来时:
He will mutter his complaints to anyone who will listen. = 他会向任何愿意倾听的人抱怨。
She will mutter her concerns to her friend. = 她会向她的朋友低声倾诉她的担忧。


虚拟语气:
If he muttered less, people would understand him better. = 如果他少咕哝一些，人们就会更好地理解他。
I wish she wouldn't mutter so much. = 我希望她不要那么多抱怨。


被动语态:
His complaints were muttered throughout the meeting. = 他的抱怨在整个会议上都被低声表达出来。
The answer was muttered indistinctly. = 答案含糊不清地被低声说出。


疑问句:
What was he muttering about? = 他在咕哝些什么？
What are you muttering under your breath? = 你在低声嘀咕什么呢？


否定句:
He didn't mutter a word. = 他一句话也没说。
She didn't mutter any complaints. = 她没有抱怨。


PHRASE->
mutter to oneself  = 自言自语
mutter under one's breath = 低声嘀咕
mutter a curse = 低声咒骂
mutter an apology = 低声道歉
mutter discontent = 低声表达不满
