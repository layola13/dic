cusps:/kʌsps/| n.|尖端；尖顶；临界点|The pointed end of a crescent moon or similar shape; a point or summit.  Also, a point of transition or crisis. |例句：The cusps of the moon were barely visible. 月亮的尖端几乎不可见。

例句：The company is at a cusp of major change. 这家公司正处于重大变革的关头。|近义词：peak, apex, summit, point, critical point |反义词：base, bottom, nadir
