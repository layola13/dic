miscounts:/mɪsˈkaʊnts/| v.| 误算，算错 | To count incorrectly; to make a mistake in counting.  | Example:  He miscounts the votes during the election. 他在选举期间误算了选票。  The cashier miscounts the change, giving me too little. 收银员算错了找零，给了我太少。 |近义词：err, miscalculate, underestimate |反义词：accurately count, correctly calculate
