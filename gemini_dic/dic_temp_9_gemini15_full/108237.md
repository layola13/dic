laughing-stock: /ˈlæfɪŋstɑːk/| n. |笑柄，嘲笑的对象 | a person or thing that is the object of ridicule or laughter.  |例句：He became the laughing-stock of the town after his failed attempt to climb the mountain. 他在尝试爬山失败后成了镇上的笑柄。
例句：His ridiculous proposal made him a laughing-stock at the meeting. 他荒谬的提议使他在会议上成了笑柄。|近义词：butt of jokes, joke, ridicule |反义词：hero, champion

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is the laughing-stock of the company. = 他是公司里的笑柄。
The new policy is a laughing-stock. = 这项新政策是个笑柄。

一般过去时:
He was the laughing-stock of the entire school. = 他曾是全校的笑柄。
The project became a laughing-stock after its failure. = 项目失败后成了笑柄。

现在完成时:
He has become the laughing-stock of the industry. = 他已成为业界的笑柄。
The plan has proven to be a laughing-stock. = 这项计划已被证明是个笑柄。

过去完成时:
He had already become a laughing-stock before the news broke. = 在消息传出之前，他已经成了笑柄。
The idea had been a laughing-stock for years. = 这个主意多年来一直是个笑柄。

将来时:
He will be the laughing-stock of the nation. = 他将成为全国的笑柄。
This decision will undoubtedly make them a laughing-stock. = 这个决定无疑会使他们成为笑柄。

虚拟语气:
If he continues like this, he'll be the laughing-stock of the town. = 如果他继续这样下去，他将成为镇上的笑柄。
I wish I hadn't made such a fool of myself; I became a laughing-stock. = 我真希望自己没有那么傻，我成了笑柄。

被动语态:
He was made a laughing-stock by the media. = 他被媒体嘲讽成了笑柄。
The proposal was ridiculed and became a laughing-stock. = 这项提案遭到嘲笑并成了笑柄。

疑问句:
Is he the laughing-stock of the class? = 他是班上的笑柄吗？
Will this new design become a laughing-stock? = 这个新设计会成为笑柄吗？

否定句:
He is not the laughing-stock; he is respected. = 他不是笑柄；他受到尊重。
The attempt, though unsuccessful, was not a laughing-stock. = 尽管尝试失败了，但这并非笑柄。


PHRASE->

a national laughing-stock = 全国性的笑柄
