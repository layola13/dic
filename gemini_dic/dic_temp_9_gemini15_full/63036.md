enrapturer:/ɪnˈræpʃərər/| n. |使着迷的人；使人入迷的事物 |One who enraptures; something that enraptures.  It refers to a person or thing that captivates and delights, filling someone with intense pleasure or fascination. |The magician was a true enrapturer, captivating the audience with his illusions.  魔术师真是个令人着迷的人，他的幻觉迷住了观众。 The breathtaking scenery was an enrapturer, leaving us speechless.  令人叹为观止的景色令人着迷，让我们说不出话来。|charmer, captivator, fascinator |repeller, bore|名词
