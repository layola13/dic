imbathe:/ɪmˈbeɪð/| vt.|沉浸；沐浴；笼罩|To surround or cover completely with a liquid, light, or atmosphere.  It implies a thorough and often pleasant immersion.|例句：The setting sun imbathed the valley in a golden light.  夕阳给山谷披上了一层金色的光芒。

例句：The warm water imbathed her aching muscles.  温暖的水浸透了她酸痛的肌肉。


近义词：submerge, immerse, envelop, bathe


反义词：expose, uncover


词性:动词
