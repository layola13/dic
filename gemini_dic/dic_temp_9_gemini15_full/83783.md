hairier:/ˈheəriər/| adj.|毛茸茸的，多毛的；更毛的|More hairy; having more hair.  The adjective "hairier" is the comparative form of "hairy," indicating a greater degree of hairiness.|例句：The dog's fur is hairier this winter than last winter.  今年冬天这只狗的毛比去年冬天更毛茸茸了。The mountain gorilla is hairier than the lowland gorilla.  山地大猩猩比低地大猩猩毛多。|近义词：fuzzier, bushier, shaggier|反义词：balder, smoother, less hairy
