penologist:/ˌpiːnəˈlɒdʒɪst/| n. |监狱学家；刑罚学家|A penologist is a specialist in the study of prisons and punishment. They study the effectiveness of different prison systems, the causes of crime, and the rehabilitation of offenders.|The penologist presented his findings on the effectiveness of restorative justice programs.  监狱学家展示了他关于恢复性司法项目有效性的研究结果。|criminologist, correctional specialist|—|
