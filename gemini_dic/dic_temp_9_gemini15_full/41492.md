cornucopia:/ˌkɔːrnjuˈkoʊpiə/| n. |丰饶角；丰收；应有尽有|A cornucopia is a symbol of abundance and plenty, often depicted as a goat's horn overflowing with fruits, vegetables, and other goods.  It represents prosperity and the bounty of nature. |The Thanksgiving table was a cornucopia of delicious food.  感恩节的餐桌上摆满了各种美味佳肴。|abundance, plenty, wealth|scarcity, dearth, poverty|名词
