cricket:/ˈkrɪkɪt/| n. |蟋蟀；板球；板球运动|A cricket is a jumping insect that makes a chirping sound. Cricket is also a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. |例句：I heard a cricket chirping in the grass. 我听到草丛里蟋蟀在鸣叫。  The cricket match was thrilling. 板球比赛非常刺激。|近义词：insect (for the insect), baseball (similar bat-and-ball game)|反义词：无
