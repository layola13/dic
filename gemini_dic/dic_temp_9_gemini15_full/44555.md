crotchet:/ˈkrɒtʃɪt/ | /ˈkrɑːtʃɪt/ | n. |  八分音符；古怪的想法；脾气暴躁；钩针编织 | A crochet is a musical note equal to half a minim or a quarter of a semibreve. It can also refer to a whimsical, odd, or eccentric idea, or a bad temper.  In addition, it can refer to the act of crocheting. |例句：He played a series of rapid crotchets on the piano. 他在钢琴上演奏了一系列快速的八分音符。  His crotchets about conspiracy theories were starting to annoy everyone. 他关于阴谋论的古怪想法开始惹恼所有人。|近义词：quaver (music), whim, quirk, eccentricity, bad temper |反义词：straightforward idea (opposite of eccentric idea), good temper
