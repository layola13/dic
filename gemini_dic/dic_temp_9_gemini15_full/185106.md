retimber:/riːˈtɪmbər/| vt.|重新装上木材；更换木材|To replace the timber in (a structure, etc.).  The process involves removing old, damaged, or decayed timber and replacing it with new timber to restore structural integrity and stability. |例句：The old bridge needed to be retimbered to ensure its safety.  老桥需要重新安装木材以确保其安全。  The house was extensively retimbered after the fire.  火灾后，房子进行了大规模的木材更换。|近义词：reconstruct, repair, renovate|反义词：destroy, demolish|动词
