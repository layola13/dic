redeceiving:/ˌriːdɪˈsiːvɪŋ/| v.|重新接收，再次接收 |The act of receiving something again, or anew.  This word is not commonly used and might be a misspelling.|例句：The package was redelivered, meaning it was redeceiving a second attempt at delivery.包裹被重新投递，这意味着它正在第二次尝试投递中被重新接收。  The signal was redeceiving after the brief interruption.信号在短暂中断后重新接收。|近义词：re-receiving, receiving again|反义词：losing, rejecting
