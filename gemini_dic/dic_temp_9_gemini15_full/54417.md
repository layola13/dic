disincorporate:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/| vt.|使…不再组成公司；解散公司；取消公司资格|To dissolve or cease to be a corporation.  The process involves the legal termination of a corporation's existence.  This often happens when a company merges with another, is liquidated, or chooses to end its operations. |The company decided to disincorporate after years of financial losses. 公司决定在多年亏损后解散。|The small business was disincorporated due to lack of profitability. 小企业因缺乏盈利而被解散。|incorporate, integrate|form a corporation, establish a corporation|v.
