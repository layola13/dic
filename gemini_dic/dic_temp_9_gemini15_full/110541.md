limelighter:/ˈlaɪmˌlaɪtər/| n. |聚光灯下的人；引人注目的人|A person or thing that attracts a lot of attention or admiration, often temporarily.  Limelight refers to the intense light used in theaters, and a limelighter is someone who is in the center of that light, thus, famous or popular.|The new movie star is a true limelighter, constantly pursued by paparazzi.|近义词：celebrity, star, sensation |反义词：nobody, unknown|名词
