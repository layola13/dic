showcasing: /ˈʃoʊkeɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 展示；展出；突显 |  To display or exhibit something prominently; to highlight or emphasize something. | 例句：The museum is showcasing a new collection of Impressionist paintings.  博物馆正在展出新的印象派绘画收藏。

The company is showcasing its new product at the trade show.  这家公司正在贸易展会上展示其新产品。


近义词：displaying, exhibiting, highlighting, featuring, presenting | 反义词：hiding, concealing, obscuring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gallery showcases local artists' work. = 画廊展出当地艺术家的作品。
He showcases his skills in the competition. = 他在比赛中展示了他的技能。


一般过去时:
They showcased their latest invention at the conference. = 他们在会议上展示了他们的最新发明。
The shop showcased a range of handmade jewelry. = 这家商店展示了一系列手工珠宝。


现在完成时:
The company has showcased its commitment to sustainability. = 该公司已经展示了其对可持续发展的承诺。
She has showcased her talent in numerous performances. = 她曾在无数场演出中展示过她的才华。


过去完成时:
They had showcased their designs before the official launch. = 他们在正式发布之前已经展示了他们的设计。
The museum had showcased ancient artifacts for decades. = 该博物馆几十年来一直在展出古代文物。


将来时:
We will showcase our new product line next month. = 我们下个月将展示我们的新产品线。
The artist will showcase his paintings in a solo exhibition. = 该艺术家将在个人展览中展出他的绘画作品。


虚拟语气:
If they showcased their product more effectively, they would sell more. = 如果他们更有效地展示他们的产品，他们会卖得更多。
I wish they had showcased the historical context better. = 我希望他们能更好地展示历史背景。


被动语态:

The new technology is being showcased at the tech conference. = 新技术正在科技会议上展出。


疑问句:

否定句:

They chose not to showcase their controversial artwork. = 他们选择不展示他们有争议的艺术作品。

词源:
The word "showcase" originally referred to a glass case used to display goods.  The verb "showcase" developed from this noun meaning, extending to the act of prominently displaying anything.  The suffix "-ing" creates the present participle, functioning as a gerund (a verb acting as a noun).
