interrogations:/ˌɪntərəˈɡeɪʃənz/| n. |审问；讯问；疑问 |The process of asking questions, especially in a systematic way, to obtain information.  This often involves questioning suspects or witnesses in a legal context.  It can also refer to a series of questions used to test understanding or knowledge. |例句：The police conducted a series of interrogations to solve the crime.  警方进行了一系列审问以破案。|例句：The exam consisted of a series of interrogations on the material covered in class.  考试由一系列关于课堂上讲授内容的提问构成。|近义词：questioning, inquiry, examination, investigation |反义词：answers, responses, statements |名词
