complex:/ˈkɒmpleks/ /ˈkɑːmpleks/| adj., n.|复杂的；复合体；情结|

英文解析:As an adjective, "complex" means consisting of many different and connected parts. As a noun, it refers to a system of interconnected things or a group of related things.  It can also refer to a group of buildings or a system of buildings.  In psychology, it refers to a pattern of repressed anxieties and desires that affect a person's behavior.

例句:
The instructions for assembling the furniture were incredibly complex. = 组装家具的说明非常复杂。

The software program is a complex system of interconnected modules. = 该软件程序是一个相互关联的模块的复杂系统。


近义词: intricate, complicated, sophisticated, multifaceted

反义词: simple, uncomplicated, straightforward

词性: adjective, noun
