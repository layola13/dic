overexposes: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊz/ | vt. | 过度曝光 | To expose (something, especially photographic film or a sensor) to too much light, resulting in a loss of detail and contrast.  |  例句：The photographer overexposed the shot, washing out the details in the sky. 摄影师过度曝光了照片，冲淡了天空的细节。

The bright sun overexposed the film.  强烈的阳光使胶卷过度曝光了。


近义词：overlight, bleach out | 反义词：underexpose
