miaowed:/mɪˈaʊd/| v.|猫叫|The verb "miaowed" is the past tense of "meow," which is the sound a cat makes.  It describes the act of a cat making its characteristic sound.|例句：The cat miaowed loudly when it was hungry.  这只猫饿了，大声喵喵叫。The kitten miaowed softly for its mother. 小猫轻轻地叫着找妈妈。|近义词：meowed, purred (if a softer sound)|反义词:  无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat meows loudly. = 猫大声叫。
The kitten meows softly for its mother. = 小猫轻轻地叫着找妈妈。

一般过去时:
The cat miaowed all night. = 猫整夜都在叫。
The kitten miaowed when it saw the mouse. = 小猫看到老鼠时喵喵叫。

现在完成时:
The cat has miaowed several times. = 猫已经叫了好几次了。
The kitten has miaowed since I came home. = 自从我回家后，小猫一直在叫。


过去完成时:
The cat had miaowed before I opened the door. = 我开门之前，猫就已经叫了。
The kitten had miaowed several times before its mother arrived. = 小猫在妈妈来之前已经叫了好几次了。


将来时:
The cat will meow when it sees food. = 猫看到食物就会叫。
The kitten will meow for attention. = 小猫会喵喵叫来吸引注意。


虚拟语气:
If the cat miaowed, I would know it was hungry. = 如果猫叫了，我就知道它饿了。
I wish the kitten would meow louder. = 我希望小猫能叫大声点。
