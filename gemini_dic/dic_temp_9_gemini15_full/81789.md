grenaded:/ɡrəˈneɪdəd/| vt.|用榴弹轰炸；投掷手榴弹|To attack or destroy something with grenades.  The building was grenaded during the night.|近义词：bombarded, shelled, attacked|反义词：repaired, rebuilt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They grenade enemy positions regularly. = 他们定期用榴弹袭击敌军阵地。
The soldiers grenade the building to destroy it. = 士兵们用榴弹轰炸建筑物以摧毁它。

一般过去时:
The terrorists grenaded the market. = 恐怖分子用榴弹袭击了市场。
He grenaded the car to disable it. = 他用榴弹袭击了汽车以使其失效。

现在完成时:
The rebels have grenaded several government buildings. = 叛乱分子已经用榴弹袭击了几座政府大楼。
They have grenaded the area multiple times. = 他们多次用榴弹袭击了该地区。

过去完成时:
The police had grenaded the suspect's hideout before they arrived. = 警察在他们到达之前已经用榴弹袭击了嫌疑人的藏匿处。
The army had grenaded the bridge to prevent the enemy's advance. = 军队已经用榴弹袭击了桥梁以阻止敌人的前进。

将来时:
They will grenade the target at dawn. = 他们将在黎明时分用榴弹袭击目标。
The troops will grenade the enemy bunker. = 部队将用榴弹袭击敌人的掩体。

虚拟语气:
If they grenaded the building, the fire would have spread. = 如果他们用榴弹袭击了那座建筑物，火势就会蔓延。
I wish they hadn't grenaded the village. = 我真希望他们没有用榴弹袭击那个村庄。

被动语态:
The building was grenaded by terrorists. = 这座建筑物被恐怖分子用榴弹袭击了。
The bridge was grenaded during the battle. = 桥梁在战斗中被榴弹袭击了。

疑问句:
Were the civilians grenaded? = 平民被榴弹袭击了吗？
Has the enemy been grenaded? = 敌人被榴弹袭击了吗？

否定句:
They did not grenade the innocent people. = 他们没有袭击无辜的人。
The soldiers did not grenade the school. = 士兵们没有用榴弹袭击学校。
