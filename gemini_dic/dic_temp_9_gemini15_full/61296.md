embargo:/ɛmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ | n. v. |禁运；贸易禁运 | A government order that restricts commerce or trade with a specified country or the exchange of specific goods.  |The government imposed an embargo on oil imports. 政府对石油进口实施了禁运。|近义词：ban, prohibition, boycott |反义词：free trade, open market |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government embargoes certain goods. = 政府禁止某些商品贸易。
They embargo arms exports to the conflict zone. = 他们禁止向冲突地区出口武器。


一般过去时:
The country embargoed all trade with its neighbor. = 这个国家禁止了与邻国的全部贸易。
They embargoed the shipment of goods. = 他们禁止了货物的运输。


现在完成时:
The UN has embargoed weapons to that region for years. = 联合国多年来一直对该地区实行武器禁运。
Several countries have embargoed goods from the nation. = 几个国家已经禁止了来自该国的商品。


过去完成时:
The company had already embargoed the product before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，该公司已经禁止了该产品。
They had embargoed the shipment after receiving intelligence reports. = 在收到情报报告后，他们禁止了货物的运输。


将来时:
The government will embargo all imports from the rogue state. = 政府将禁止来自流氓国家的所有进口商品。
They will embargo the sale of certain chemicals. = 他们将禁止销售某些化学品。


虚拟语气:
If they embargoed the goods, the price would increase. = 如果他们禁止这些商品，价格就会上涨。
I wish they hadn’t embargoed the essential medicines. = 我希望他们没有禁止必要的药品。


被动语态:
The goods were embargoed by the government. = 这些商品被政府禁止了。
The shipment was embargoed due to safety concerns. = 由于安全问题，货物运输被禁止了。


疑问句:
Was the trade embargoed last year? = 去年贸易被禁止了吗？
Are the sanctions an embargo or a tariff? = 这些制裁是禁运还是关税？


否定句:
The government did not embargo the food supplies. = 政府没有禁止粮食供应。
They didn’t embargo the technology, despite the concerns. = 尽管存在担忧，他们并没有禁止这项技术。


PHRASE->
trade embargo = 贸易禁运
oil embargo = 石油禁运
weapons embargo = 武器禁运
economic embargo = 经济禁运
embargo on imports = 进口禁运
