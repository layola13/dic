cowardie:/ˈkaʊədi/| n.|懦夫，胆小鬼|A coward is someone who lacks courage and is easily frightened.  It is an informal and somewhat archaic term.|例句：He's such a cowardie, he wouldn't even stand up to a bully.|近义词：coward, poltroon, craven|反义词：brave, courageous, valiant
