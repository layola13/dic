quasi-grateful:/ˈkwɑːzi ˈɡreɪtfl/ | adj. |似感激的，不情愿的感激|

英文解析:Showing or feeling a limited or reluctant gratitude; not genuinely grateful.  It implies a feeling of gratitude that is not fully heartfelt or sincere, perhaps due to obligation or a sense of being forced into expressing it.

例句:

He gave a quasi-grateful nod, clearly not thrilled with the gift. 他不情愿地点点头表示感谢，显然对这份礼物并不兴奋。

She offered a quasi-grateful smile, her eyes betraying her true feelings. 她勉强地笑了笑，眼神出卖了她的真实感受。


近义词: half-hearted, grudging, reluctant, unenthusiastic


反义词: genuinely grateful, thankful, appreciative


词性: adjective (形容词)
