desalination:/ˌdiːˌsælɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |脱盐；海水淡化|The process of removing salt from seawater or brackish water to make it suitable for drinking or irrigation.  It is a crucial technology for addressing water scarcity in many parts of the world.|例句：Desalination plants are becoming increasingly common in arid regions. 海水淡化厂在干旱地区变得越来越普遍。|例句：The high cost of desalination remains a significant barrier to widespread adoption. 海水淡化的成本仍然是广泛采用的一大障碍。|近义词：desalting, purification|反义词：salinization
