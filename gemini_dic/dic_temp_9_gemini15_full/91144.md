hybridizing:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzɪŋ/| v.|杂交；混合；使杂交|The process of combining different varieties or species of plants or animals to create a hybrid offspring with desirable traits.  It can also refer to combining different elements or styles, resulting in a blended or mixed form.|例句：Scientists are hybridizing different types of wheat to create a more drought-resistant strain. 科学家们正在将不同类型的麦子杂交，以培育出更耐旱的品种。

例句：The artist's work is a fascinating hybridization of traditional and modern techniques.  这位艺术家的作品是传统和现代技法的迷人混合体。|近义词：crossbreeding, interbreeding, blending, mixing|反义词：purifying, separating, isolating
