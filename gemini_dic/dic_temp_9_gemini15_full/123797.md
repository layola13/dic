mopeful: /ˈmoʊpful/ | adj. | 忧郁的；沮丧的；愁眉苦脸的 | Feeling or showing dejection; gloomy.  | 例句：He looked mopeful after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后看起来很沮丧。


例句：Her mopeful expression revealed her disappointment. 她愁眉苦脸的表情泄露了她的失望。


近义词：depressed, despondent, downcast, gloomy, melancholy


反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful, upbeat, optimistic
