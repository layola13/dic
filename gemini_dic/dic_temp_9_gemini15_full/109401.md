lentoid:/ˈlɛntoɪd/| n. |透镜状体，透镜状物 |A lentoid is a lens-shaped object or structure.  It is often used in geology to describe lens-shaped bodies of rock or sediment.  |例句：The geologist identified a lentoid of quartzite within the shale formation. 地质学家在页岩层中发现了一个石英岩透镜体。


The discovery of a lentoid deposit altered the mining plan. 透镜状矿床的发现改变了采矿计划。|近义词：lens, nodule |反义词：无
