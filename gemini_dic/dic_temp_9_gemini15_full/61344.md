embedments:/ɪmˈbedmənts/| n. |嵌入物；嵌入；植入物|The process of embedding something within something else; the things that are embedded.  This refers to the act of embedding or the result of embedding.  It can refer to physical objects embedded in a material, or abstract concepts embedded within a larger system or text.|例句：The archaeologists discovered several ancient artifacts embedded in the rock. 考古学家在岩石中发现了几件古代文物。

例句：The researcher analyzed the subtle embedments of meaning within the poem. 研究人员分析了诗歌中细微的意义嵌入。

近义词：insertions, implants, inclusions|反义词：extractions, removals|名词
