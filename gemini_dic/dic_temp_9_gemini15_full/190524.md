satiated:/ˈseɪʃieɪtɪd/| adj.| 饱足的；满足的；厌倦的 |  Completely satisfied; having had enough of something.  |例句：After the large meal, I felt completely satiated.  一顿丰盛的饭后，我感到非常饱足。

例句：His desire for power was finally satiated. 他对权力的渴望最终得到了满足。

近义词：satisfied, full, replete, glutted | 反义词：hungry, unsatisfied, famished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is satiated after eating a big lunch. 他吃了一顿丰盛的午餐后感到饱了。
The market is satiated with similar products. 市场已经被类似的产品充斥了。


一般过去时:
She was satiated by the delicious dessert. 她被美味的甜点吃饱了。
The audience was satiated by the lengthy concert. 观众对冗长的音乐会感到厌倦了。


现在完成时:
I have been satiated with work this week. 我这周工作已经做得够多了。
The company has satiated its customer's demand for new features. 公司已经满足了客户对新功能的需求。


过去完成时:
He had been satiated with the news before the announcement. 在公告之前，他已经对新闻感到厌倦了。
She had satiated her curiosity about the painting. 她已经满足了她对这幅画的好奇心。


将来时:
They will be satiated by the upcoming holiday. 即将到来的假期会让他们感到满足。
The new product will likely satiate the growing demand. 新产品可能会满足日益增长的需求。


虚拟语气:
If I had eaten more, I would have been satiated. 如果我多吃点，我就饱了。
If only I could satiate my thirst for knowledge. 但愿我能满足我对知识的渴望。


被动语态:
His hunger was satiated by the hearty meal. 他旺盛的食欲被丰盛的饭菜满足了。
The audience's curiosity was satiated by the speaker's explanation. 听众的好奇心被演讲者的解释所满足。


疑问句:
Are you satiated with this information? 你对这些信息感到满足了吗？
Is the market satiated with this type of product? 市场对这种产品感到厌倦了吗？


否定句:
I am not satiated yet; I could eat more. 我还没吃饱；我还能多吃点。
The public is not satiated by the government's response to the crisis. 公众对政府应对危机的回应并不满意。


PHRASE->
satiate one's hunger  满足某人的饥饿
satiate one's thirst  满足某人的口渴
satiate one's curiosity 满足某人的好奇心
satiate one's desire 满足某人的欲望
satiate the market  满足市场需求

词源:
Comes from the Latin word "satiāre," meaning "to satisfy fully."
