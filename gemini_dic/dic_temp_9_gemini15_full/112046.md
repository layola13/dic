longboards:/ˈlɒŋbɔːrdz/| n.|长板(滑板)|A longboard is a type of skateboard that is longer than a traditional skateboard, typically ranging from 36 to 50 inches in length.  They are designed for cruising, downhill riding, and carving, and often have larger wheels than standard skateboards, providing a smoother ride.|例句：He loves riding his longboard down the hill. 他喜欢骑着他的长板下山。

例句：My friend showed me some amazing longboard tricks. 我的朋友向我展示了一些令人惊叹的长板技巧。


近义词：skateboard (general term), cruiser board


反义词：None (no direct antonym, but "shortboard" could be considered a contrasting term in the context of skateboarding)


词性: 名词
