fairier:/ˈferɪər/| adj.|更仙的，更像仙女的，更超凡脱俗的|More fairy-like; more enchanting or magical.  The painting depicted a fairier landscape than the one I had seen in reality.|近义词：more magical, more ethereal, more enchanting|反义词：more mundane, more ordinary, more realistic


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The forest seems fairier in the morning light. = 森林在晨光中显得更像仙境。
This dress makes her look fairier than ever. = 这件衣服让她看起来比以往更像仙女。


一般过去时:
The story sounded fairier than I had expected. = 这个故事听起来比我预期的更像童话。
The old castle looked fairier under the moonlight. = 在月光下，古老的城堡看起来更像仙境。


现在完成时:
The experience has made the world seem fairier to me. = 这段经历让我觉得世界更像仙境了。
Everything has become fairier since she moved in. = 自从她搬进来后，一切都变得更梦幻了。


过去完成时:
I had never seen a fairier scene before. = 我以前从未见过如此美丽的景色。
The garden had appeared fairier after the rain. = 雨后，花园显得更加梦幻。


将来时:
The future will seem fairier if we work hard. = 如果我们努力工作，未来将会更加美好。
The new park will look fairier once the flowers bloom. = 花儿盛开后，新公园将会更加美丽。


虚拟语气:
If I had wings, my life would be fairier. = 如果我有翅膀，我的生活将会更加梦幻。
I wish the world were fairier and kinder. = 我希望世界更加美好和善良。


被动语态:
The story is made fairier by the author's vivid description. = 作者生动的描述使故事更具梦幻色彩。
The scene was made fairier by the soft lighting. = 柔和的灯光使场景更加梦幻。


疑问句:
Is this place fairier than the other one? = 这个地方比另一个地方更像仙境吗？
Doesn't the music make the atmosphere fairier? = 音乐难道没有让气氛更梦幻吗？


否定句:
The reality wasn't as fairier as the dream. = 现实并不像梦境中那样梦幻。
The situation is not fairier than before; it's worse. = 情况并没有比以前更美好；反而更糟了。
