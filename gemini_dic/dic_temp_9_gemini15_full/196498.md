serviced:/ˈsɜːvɪst/ | v. |维修的；服务的；提供服务的 | To perform maintenance or repairs on; to provide a service to.  |例句：The car was serviced regularly.  这辆车定期保养。

The hotel serviced its guests with a smile. 这家酒店的服务员面带微笑地为客人服务。|近义词：maintained, repaired, attended to |反义词：neglected, ignored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mechanic services cars every day. = 技工每天都修理汽车。
This company services clients all over the world. = 这家公司为全世界的客户提供服务。


一般过去时:
They serviced the air conditioner last week. = 他们上周维修了空调。
The restaurant serviced us quickly and efficiently. = 餐厅为我们提供快速有效的服务。


现在完成时:
The building has been serviced regularly since its construction. = 自建成以来，这座建筑一直定期维护。
We have serviced over 1000 customers this year. = 今年我们已经为超过1000名客户提供了服务。


过去完成时:
The engine had been serviced before the long journey. = 长途旅行之前，发动机已经过维修。
The company had serviced many accounts before it went bankrupt. = 公司破产前已经为许多客户提供过服务。


将来时:
The car will be serviced next week. = 汽车下周将进行维修。
They will service the equipment after the event. = 活动结束后，他们将维修设备。


虚拟语气:
If the computer had been serviced earlier, it wouldn't have crashed. = 如果电脑早点维修，就不会崩溃了。
I wish the hotel had serviced our needs more effectively. = 我希望酒店能更有效地满足我们的需求。


被动语态:
The equipment is serviced monthly. = 设备每月维护一次。
Our requests were serviced promptly. = 我们的请求得到了迅速处理。


疑问句:
Is your car serviced regularly? = 你的车定期保养吗？
Does this company service businesses as well as individuals? = 这家公司既为企业也为个人提供服务吗？


否定句:
The machine wasn't serviced properly. = 机器没有得到适当的维修。
They didn't service the customer's complaint. = 他们没有处理客户的投诉。
