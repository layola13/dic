haircuttings:/ˈhɛərkʌtɪŋz/| n.|理发后的头发；修剪的头发|Cut or trimmed hair from a haircut.  The term usually refers to the discarded hair after a haircut.|例句：The hairdresser swept up the haircuttings after finishing the style. 理发师在完成造型后扫掉了理发后的头发。

例句：The floor was covered in haircuttings after the busy salon day.  忙碌的沙龙一天后，地板上满是理发后的头发。


近义词：clippings (hair clippings), trimmings


反义词：无


词性：名词
