processors: /ˈprɑːsɛsərz/ | n. |处理器，处理者 |  A processor is a device that performs a sequence of operations in accordance with a set of instructions.  This typically refers to a central processing unit (CPU) in a computer, but it can also refer to a person or machine that processes something. | Example:  Modern computers have powerful processors. 现代计算机拥有强大的处理器。 The food processor quickly chopped the vegetables. 食品加工机快速切碎了蔬菜。|近义词：CPU, central processing unit, microprocessors,  computers,  handlers |反义词：output device, storage device

词源:
The word "processor" comes from the verb "process," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "processus," meaning "a going forward," "progress," or "advance."  The suffix "-or" is added to form the noun denoting the agent or instrument of the action of processing.
