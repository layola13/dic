mature:/məˈtʃʊr/| adj., v.| 成熟的；使成熟 | Fully developed; ripe; adult.  |例句：He's a mature young man for his age. 他年纪轻轻却很成熟。|  例句：The fruit needs more time to mature.  水果还需要更多时间才能成熟。 |近义词：ripe, adult, developed |反义词：immature, unripe, juvenile


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fruit matures quickly in the warm sun. = 水果在温暖的阳光下很快成熟。
He matures his wine in oak barrels. = 他用橡木桶陈酿他的葡萄酒。

一般过去时:
The plan matured over several months. = 这个计划经过数月的酝酿才成熟。
The cheese matured perfectly. = 奶酪完美地成熟了。

现在完成时:
The wine has matured beautifully. = 这葡萄酒陈酿得很好。
She has matured considerably since college. = 自从大学毕业后，她成熟了很多。

过去完成时:
The fruit had matured before the harvest. = 水果在收获前就已经成熟了。
They had matured their business plan before seeking funding. = 他们在寻求资金之前已经完善了他们的商业计划。

将来时:
The grapes will mature in the fall. = 葡萄将在秋季成熟。
This wine will mature well with age. = 这款酒随着时间的推移会越陈越香。

虚拟语气:
If the plan had matured sooner, we would have launched it earlier. = 如果计划早点成熟，我们就早点推出了。
I wish the fruit had matured before the storm. = 我希望水果在暴风雨来临之前就已经成熟了。

被动语态:
The cheese is matured in cool cellars. = 奶酪在阴凉的酒窖里陈酿。
The plan was matured by a team of experts. = 这个计划是由一个专家团队制定的。

疑问句:
Has the fruit matured yet? = 水果成熟了吗？
Is the wine mature enough to drink? = 这葡萄酒成熟到可以饮用了？

否定句:
The fruit hasn't matured yet. = 水果还没有成熟。
The plan didn't mature as quickly as we hoped. = 计划没有像我们希望的那样快成熟。
