reccy:/ˈrɛki/ | n. |侦察；侦查；勘察 | A reconnaissance, typically a short one.  It's a shortened form of "reconnaissance". |例句：We did a quick reccy of the area before launching the attack. 我们在发动攻击之前对该地区进行了快速侦察。  The team completed a reccy of the building to assess its security.  小组对建筑物进行了勘察以评估其安全性。|近义词：reconnaissance, scouting, survey |反义词：none
