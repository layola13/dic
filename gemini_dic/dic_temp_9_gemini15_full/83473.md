hack:/hæk/| n./v./adj.| 砍；劈；入侵；黑客；拙劣的；| To cut or chop with repeated blows; to gain unauthorized access to a computer system; a clever or skillful solution. |例句：He hacked at the tree with an axe. 他用斧头砍树。 |例句：The hackers gained access to the company's database. 黑客访问了公司的数据库。|近义词：chop, cut, intrude, penetrate |反义词：repair, fix, protect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hacks the code every day. = 他每天都编写代码。
She hacks into the system regularly. = 她定期入侵系统。


一般过去时:
They hacked the server last night. = 他们昨晚入侵了服务器。
He hacked down the old tree. = 他砍倒了那棵老树。


现在完成时:
I have hacked my way through the jungle. = 我已经砍掉了丛林的道路。
She has hacked into several accounts. = 她已经入侵了几个账户。


过去完成时:
They had hacked the website before the security update. = 他们在安全更新之前已经入侵了网站。
He had hacked away at the ice for hours. = 他已经砍了几个小时的冰了。


将来时:
We will hack our way through the undergrowth. = 我们将砍掉灌木丛的路。
They will hack into the system tomorrow. = 他们明天将入侵系统。


虚拟语气:
If I knew how to hack, I would do it. = 如果我知道如何入侵，我会这么做的。
I wish I had hacked the problem earlier. = 我希望我早点解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The system was hacked last week. = 系统在上周被入侵了。
The tree was hacked down by the lumberjack. = 这棵树被伐木工人砍倒了。


疑问句:
Did they hack the account? = 他们入侵了账户吗？
Have you ever hacked a computer? = 你曾经入侵过电脑吗？


否定句:
I didn't hack the system. = 我没有入侵系统。
He doesn't hack into computers. = 他不入侵电脑。



PHRASE->
hack into:  入侵
hack away at:  努力地砍、持续地做某事
hack together:  粗略地拼凑
hack a solution:  想出一个解决方法
data hack: 数据入侵
