purtenance:/ˈpɜːrtənəns/| n. |附属物，从属性，配件|Things belonging to something else; appurtenances.  The term often refers to items that are considered integral to a larger entity, though not necessarily physically attached. For example, the purtenances of a house might include the garden, outbuildings, or even certain rights of way.|The dilapidated outbuildings were considered purtenances of the main house.  附属建筑被认为是主屋的附属物。
