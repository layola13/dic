point-and-shoots:/ˈpɔɪnt ænd ʃuːts/| n.|傻瓜相机，简单相机|A point-and-shoot camera is a type of camera that is easy to use, requiring minimal technical expertise.  It typically has automatic settings for focus, exposure, and other functions.  The user simply points the camera at the subject and presses the shutter button to take a picture.|例句：My first camera was a simple point-and-shoot; it was perfect for beginners.|近义词：compact camera, snapshot camera|反义词：SLR camera, DSLR camera
