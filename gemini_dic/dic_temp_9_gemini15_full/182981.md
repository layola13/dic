replaying:/ˌriːˈpleɪɪŋ/| v.|重播；重放；再次上演|The act of playing something again, such as a recording, a game, or a scene.|例句：I'm replaying the game to try and find my mistake. 我正在重播游戏，试图找到我的错误。

例句：The director decided to replay the final scene of the play.  导演决定重演戏剧的最后一幕。


近义词：replaying, re-enacting, repeating


反义词：stopping, ending, concluding


词性:动词
