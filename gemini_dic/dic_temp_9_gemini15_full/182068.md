remerging: /ˌriːˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 重新出现；再次浮现 |  The act of emerging again; reappearing after a period of absence or dormancy.  It describes something that has previously appeared, disappeared, and is now appearing again. |


例句：

The remerging of the old rivalries threatened to destabilize the region.  | 旧有的竞争关系再次出现，威胁到该地区稳定。

After a period of decline, the company is remerging as a major player in the market. | 在经历了一段时间的衰落后，这家公司正重新成为市场上的主要参与者。


近义词：reappearing, resurfacing, returning, reviving


反义词：disappearing, vanishing, fading


词性：动词
