deoxygenated:/ˌdiːˈɒksɪdʒənˌeɪtɪd/| adj.|脱氧的；缺氧的|Lacking oxygen.  Describes blood or tissue that has lost its oxygen. |例句：Deoxygenated blood is dark red or purplish in color.  脱氧血液呈暗红色或紫红色。

例句：The deoxygenated blood returns to the heart to be re-oxygenated in the lungs.  脱氧血返回心脏，在肺部重新充氧。


近义词：anoxic, anaerobic, oxygen-poor

反义词：oxygenated, aerated

词性：形容词
