persecute:/ˈpɜːrsɪkjuːt/| vt.|迫害；烦扰；纠缠|To subject (someone) to hostility and ill-treatment, usually because of their race, religion, or beliefs.|The minority group was persecuted for their religious beliefs. 少数民族因其宗教信仰而受到迫害。|近义词：oppress, harass, torment|反义词：protect, defend, support|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They persecute him for his political views. = 他们因为他的政治观点迫害他。
The company persecutes its employees with unreasonable demands. = 公司用不合理的要求来折磨它的员工。


一般过去时:
The government persecuted dissidents during the war. = 战争期间，政府迫害异议人士。
She was persecuted for speaking out against injustice. = 她因为公开反对不公正而受到迫害。


现在完成时:
They have persecuted this community for generations. = 他们世世代代都迫害这个社群。
Religious minorities have been persecuted throughout history. = 宗教少数群体在历史上一直受到迫害。


过去完成时:
They had persecuted the family for years before they finally fled. = 在他们最终逃亡之前，他们已经迫害这个家庭多年了。
The community had been persecuted for centuries before gaining independence. = 在获得独立之前，这个社群已经受到几个世纪的迫害。


将来时:
They will persecute anyone who opposes their regime. = 他们将迫害任何反对他们政权的人。
If they gain power, they will persecute their political opponents. = 如果他们掌握权力，他们将迫害他们的政治对手。


虚拟语气:
If they were in power, they would persecute their enemies. = 如果他们掌权，他们就会迫害他们的敌人。
I wish they wouldn't persecute those innocent people. = 我希望他们不要迫害那些无辜的人。


被动语态:
Many people were persecuted for their beliefs. = 许多人因为他们的信仰而受到迫害。
The minority group has been persecuted for decades. = 少数民族群体几十年来一直受到迫害。


疑问句:
Were they persecuted because of their ethnicity? = 他们是因为种族而受到迫害吗？
Why are they being persecuted? = 他们为什么受到迫害？


否定句:
They did not persecute anyone unfairly. = 他们没有不公平地迫害任何人。
We will not persecute those who disagree with us. = 我们不会迫害那些与我们意见不同的人。


PHRASE->
religious persecution = 宗教迫害
political persecution = 政治迫害
racial persecution = 种族迫害
persecution complex = 受迫害妄想症
persecution mania = 迫害狂


词源:
From Latin *persequī ("to follow after, pursue"), from per- ("through, completely") + sequi ("to follow").  The original sense implied a relentless pursuit, which evolved into the modern meaning of hostile and oppressive pursuit.
