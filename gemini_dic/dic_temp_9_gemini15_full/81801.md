grenadine:/ˌɡrenəˈdiːn/ | n. |石榴汁；一种甜味酒 |A sweet, non-alcoholic syrup made from pomegranate juice, often used in cocktails.  It can also refer to a sweet red wine. |例句：The bartender mixed the grenadine with gin and lime juice to make a refreshing cocktail. 调酒师将石榴汁与杜松子酒和青柠汁混合，调制出一款清爽的鸡尾酒。

例句：She added a splash of grenadine to her champagne for a touch of sweetness. 她往香槟里加了一点石榴汁，增加一点甜味。


近义词：pomegranate syrup, fruit syrup |反义词：无


词源：The word comes from the French word "grenade," meaning "pomegranate," ultimately derived from the Latin word "granatum," meaning "grain." The suffix "-ine" indicates a substance or derivative.  Therefore, grenadine literally means "pomegranate juice" or "pomegranate-derived substance."
