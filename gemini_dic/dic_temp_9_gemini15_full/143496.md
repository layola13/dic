outglitters: /ˌaʊtˈɡlɪtərz/ | v. |胜过（光彩）；比…更闪耀|To surpass in brilliance or splendor; to shine more brightly than.|The diamond outglitters all the other jewels. 这颗钻石比其他所有珠宝都更闪耀。|近义词：outshine, outshine, eclipse |反义词：be dimmed by, be eclipsed by


USAGE->

The gold outglitters the silver. = 黄金比白银更闪耀。
Her beauty outglitters the stars. = 她的美貌胜过群星。
