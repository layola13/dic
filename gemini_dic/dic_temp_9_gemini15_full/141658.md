optimizing:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 优化；使最优 | To make something as good or effective as possible.  This often involves making small adjustments or improvements to achieve the best possible result.  | 例句：The company is optimizing its production processes to reduce costs. 公司正在优化其生产流程以降低成本。


例句：We are optimizing the website for better search engine rankings. 我们正在优化网站以获得更好的搜索引擎排名。


近义词：improving, enhancing, refining, perfecting


反义词：worsening, deteriorating, impairing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are optimizing the software for speed. = 他们正在优化软件速度。
We optimize our marketing campaigns regularly. = 我们定期优化我们的营销活动。

一般过去时:
The engineer optimized the engine's performance. = 工程师优化了发动机的性能。
They optimized the code for efficiency last week. = 他们上周优化了代码效率。

现在完成时:
We have optimized the website for mobile devices. = 我们已经针对移动设备优化了网站。
They have optimized their supply chain for faster delivery. = 他们已经优化了他们的供应链以加快交付速度。

过去完成时:
The team had optimized the algorithm before the launch. = 该团队在发布之前已经优化了算法。
She had optimized her training plan for peak performance. = 她已经优化了她的训练计划以达到最佳状态。

将来时:
We will optimize the database for faster queries. = 我们将优化数据库以加快查询速度。
They will optimize the manufacturing process next quarter. = 他们将在下个季度优化制造流程。

虚拟语气:
If we optimized the system, it would run faster. = 如果我们优化系统，它将运行得更快。
I wish I had optimized my investment strategy earlier. = 我希望我早点优化我的投资策略。

被动语态:
The system is being optimized for security. = 系统正在被优化以提高安全性。
The software was optimized for compatibility. = 该软件已针对兼容性进行了优化。

疑问句:
Are you optimizing your workflow? = 你正在优化你的工作流程吗？

否定句:

We are not optimizing the website for that specific browser. = 我们没有针对那个特定的浏览器优化网站。
