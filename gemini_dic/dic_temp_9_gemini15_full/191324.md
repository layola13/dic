scenist:/ˈsiːnɪst/| n. |场景设计师，场景画家|A scenist is a person who designs and creates sets and scenery for theatrical productions, films, or television shows.  They are responsible for the visual aspects of a performance, transforming a stage or location into a believable and engaging environment. |例句：The scenist meticulously crafted the set to reflect the play's gothic atmosphere. 场景设计师精心制作布景以反映戏剧哥特式的气氛。
