shuts:/ʃʌts/| v.| 关闭；停止；砰地关上 | To close something, especially with a bang or forcefully.  |例句：He shuts the door with a bang. 他砰地一声关上了门。|例句：The company shuts down for the holidays. 公司在假期期间关闭。|近义词：closes, slams, stops |反义词：opens


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shuts the door every night. = 他每晚都关门。
She shuts her eyes when she's scared. = 她害怕的时候会闭上眼睛。


一般过去时:
I shut the window because it was raining. = 我关上了窗户，因为下雨了。
They shut the shop early yesterday. = 他们昨天早早关了店。


现在完成时:
She has shut the book and put it on the shelf. = 她已经合上了书，把它放在书架上了。
He has shut down his computer for the night. = 他已经关掉了电脑准备睡觉了。


过去完成时:
I had shut the door before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经关上门了。
They had shut the factory down before the strike. = 罢工前他们已经关闭了工厂。


将来时:
I will shut the window if it gets cold. = 如果变冷了，我会关上窗户。
She will shut the door when she leaves. = 她离开的时候会关上门。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would shut my mouth. = 如果我是你，我会闭嘴。
I wish he would shut up. = 我希望他能闭嘴。


被动语态:
The door was shut by the wind. = 门被风吹关上了。
The factory was shut down last year.  = 工厂去年关闭了。


疑问句:
Did you shut the gate? = 你关上大门了吗？
Will she shut the window? = 她会关上窗户吗？


否定句:
He didn't shut the door properly. = 他没有好好关上门。
She doesn't shut her eyes easily. = 她不容易睡着。


PHRASE->
shut down: 关闭（电脑、工厂等）
shut off: 关闭（水、电等）
shut up: 闭嘴
shut out: 排斥；阻止进入
shut in:  困在里面；幽禁
