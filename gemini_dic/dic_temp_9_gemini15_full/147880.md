overreactive: /ˌoʊvərriˈæktɪv/ | adj. |反应过度的，过度敏感的 | Excessively reactive; responding more strongly than is appropriate or necessary to a stimulus or situation.  | 例句：He's overreactive to criticism; even mild suggestions upset him. 他对批评反应过度；即使是温和的建议也会让他心烦。

例句：Her overreactive response to the minor inconvenience surprised everyone. 她对这件小麻烦的过度反应让每个人都感到惊讶。


近义词：hypersensitive, overly sensitive, touchy, thin-skinned | 反义词：calm, composed, even-tempered, phlegmatic


词源：over- (prefix indicating excess) + reactive (adjective meaning readily reacting)  The word "reactive" itself comes from "react" (verb), ultimately from Latin *re- (back, again) + *agere (to do, to act).
