medlar:/ˈmɛdlər/| n. |欧楂树；欧楂果|A medlar is a small tree that produces a fruit of the same name. The fruit is small, brown, and somewhat astringent when fresh, but becomes edible and sweet after it has been "bletted" – allowed to soften and decay slightly.  |例句：The medlar tree bore a plentiful crop this year.  今年欧楂树结了很多果实。|例句：We made a medlar jelly from the fruit we picked. 我们用采摘的果子做了一罐欧楂果酱。|近义词：none (no direct synonym)|反义词：none (no direct antonym)|名词
