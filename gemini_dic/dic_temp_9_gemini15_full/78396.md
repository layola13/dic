get-away:/ˈɡɛtəweɪ/| n. v.| 短期度假；逃脱；逃跑 | A short trip taken to escape from everyday life; an escape from a difficult situation or place. |例句：We need a get-away to relax and recharge. 我们需要一个短暂的假期来放松身心。They made a daring get-away from the prison. 他们大胆地从监狱逃脱了。|近义词：vacation, escape, holiday, break |反义词：stay, confinement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The family gets away to the beach every summer. = 这家人每年夏天都去海滩度假。
He gets away with cheating on his taxes. = 他逃脱了逃税的惩罚。


一般过去时:
We got away to the mountains last weekend. = 我们上周末去了山区度假。
The thief got away before the police arrived. = 窃贼在警察到达之前逃跑了。


现在完成时:
They have gotten away with murder. = 他们逍遥法外。
We haven't gotten away for a vacation in years. = 我们多年没去度假了。


过去完成时:
They had gotten away before the alarm sounded. = 警报响起之前他们已经逃跑了。
She had gotten away with lying to her boss for months. = 她对老板撒谎好几个月都没被发现。


将来时:
We will get away to Hawaii next month. = 我们下个月要去夏威夷度假。
The criminals will get away unless we catch them soon. = 除非我们很快抓住他们，否则罪犯就会逃脱。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would get away more often. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会更频繁地度假。
If he hadn't been so careless, he wouldn't have gotten away. = 如果他不那么粗心大意，他就不会逃脱了。


被动语态:
The criminals were gotten away by the police.  = 罪犯被警察抓住了。(注意：get away 在被动语态中使用较少，此句表达稍显不自然，更常用的表达是：The criminals were caught by the police.)
The crime was not gotten away with. = 这罪行没有被放过。


疑问句:
How often do you get away? = 你多久度假一次？
Will they get away with this? = 他们能逃脱惩罚吗？


否定句:
We couldn't get away this weekend. = 我们这个周末不能度假。
He didn't get away with his crime. = 他没有逃脱罪责。


PHRASE->
get away with sth = 侥幸逃脱
get away from sth = 摆脱
get away from it all = 远离尘嚣
a quick get-away = 快速的逃脱
a romantic get-away = 浪漫的度假
