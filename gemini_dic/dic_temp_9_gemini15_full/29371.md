casts:/kæsts/| v. n.| 投掷；演员阵容；铸件 | to throw something; a group of actors in a play or film; something made by casting molten material into a mold |例句：He casts a fishing line into the lake. 他把钓鱼线扔进湖里。 The play boasts a stellar cast. 这部戏拥有一个明星阵容。The statue is a bronze cast. 这尊雕像是青铜铸件。|近义词：throws, fling, ensemble, mold |反义词：receives, catches


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He casts a shadow on the wall. = 他在墙上投下阴影。
She casts her vote for the candidate. = 她投票给这位候选人。


一般过去时:
The fishermen cast their nets into the sea. = 渔民们把渔网扔进海里。
The director cast a young actor in the lead role. = 导演让一位年轻演员担任主角。


现在完成时:
They have cast their ballots. = 他们已经投票了。
She has cast aside her old habits. = 她已经抛弃了旧习惯。


过去完成时:
He had cast his lot with the rebels. = 他已经和叛军同流合污了。
The company had already cast the parts before I auditioned. = 我试镜之前，公司已经选好演员了。


将来时:
They will cast their votes next week. = 他们下周将投票。
The studio will cast the movie in the spring. = 电影公司将在春天选角。


虚拟语气:
If he cast his vote, the result would be different. = 如果他投票，结果就会不同。
I wish I had cast a wider net in my job search. = 我希望我在求职时能有更广泛的搜索范围。


被动语态:
The votes were cast early this morning. = 选票今天早上很早就投完了。
The play was cast last month. = 这部戏在上个月选角。


疑问句:
When will they cast the new movie? = 他们什么时候选角拍新电影？
Who was cast in the lead role? = 谁扮演主角？


否定句:
He did not cast his vote. = 他没有投票。
The director didn't cast me in the movie. = 导演没有让我演这部电影。
