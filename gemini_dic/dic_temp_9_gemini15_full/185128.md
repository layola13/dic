retinitis:/ˌrɛtɪˈnaɪtɪs/| n. |视网膜炎|Inflammation of the retina, the light-sensitive tissue lining the back of the eye.  Symptoms can include blurred vision, floaters, and light sensitivity.  The condition can be caused by various factors, including infections, autoimmune diseases, and complications from other conditions like diabetes. Treatment depends on the underlying cause.|The doctor diagnosed him with retinitis pigmentosa, a type of retinitis that causes progressive vision loss.|近义词：retinal inflammation|反义词：无
