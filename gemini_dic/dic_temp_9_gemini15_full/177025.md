reanalyzing:/ˌriːˈænəlaɪzɪŋ/| v. |重新分析，再分析 |The process of analyzing something again, often with a different perspective or new information.  It implies a more thorough or critical examination than a simple review. |例句：We are reanalyzing the data to account for the unexpected results. 我们正在重新分析数据以解释意外的结果。

例句：The researchers are reanalyzing the evidence to see if they can find any new clues. 研究人员正在重新分析证据，看看是否能找到新的线索。


近义词：re-examining, reviewing, reconsidering, reassessing

反义词：accepting (without further analysis), ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reanalyzing the data. = 他们正在重新分析数据。
We reanalyze the market trends every quarter. = 我们每个季度都会重新分析市场趋势。

一般过去时:
She reanalyzed the sample. = 她重新分析了样本。
They reanalyzed the situation after the accident. = 事故发生后，他们重新分析了情况。

现在完成时:
He has reanalyzed the evidence several times. = 他已经多次重新分析了证据。
We have reanalyzed the budget and found some savings. = 我们已经重新分析了预算并发现了一些节省之处。

过去完成时:
They had reanalyzed the data before presenting their findings. = 他们在提交研究结果之前已经重新分析了数据。
She had reanalyzed the problem before proposing a solution. = 在提出解决方案之前，她已经重新分析了这个问题。

将来时:
We will reanalyze the results next week. = 我们下周将重新分析结果。
They will reanalyze the patient's medical history. = 他们将重新分析病人的病史。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would reanalyze the data more thoroughly. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会更彻底地重新分析数据。
It would be helpful if they reanalyzed the situation from a different angle. = 如果他们能从不同的角度重新分析情况，那将很有帮助。

被动语态:
The data is being reanalyzed by the team. = 数据正在由团队重新分析。
The results were reanalyzed by an independent expert. = 结果由一位独立专家重新分析。

疑问句:

否定句:
