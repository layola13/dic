kard:/kɑːrd/| n. |卡;卡片;纸牌|A small piece of stiff paper or thin card, used for writing messages, making records, or playing games.  |例句：I received a birthday card from my friend. 我收到了朋友寄来的生日贺卡。|例句：He shuffled the cards before dealing them. 他在发牌前洗牌。|近义词：cardboard,  paper,  leaflet |反义词： (无明确反义词)
