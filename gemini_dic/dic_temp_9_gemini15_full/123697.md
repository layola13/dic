mooncake:/ˈmuːnkeɪk/| n. |月饼 |A small round cake, traditionally eaten during the Mid-Autumn Festival in China and other East Asian countries.  It typically has a sweet filling, such as red bean paste, lotus seed paste, or salted egg yolk, and is often decorated with symbolic designs. |例句：We enjoyed eating mooncakes with our family during the Mid-Autumn Festival.  我们中秋节期间和家人一起享用月饼。

例句：She baked delicious mooncakes for the festival. 她为节日烤制了美味的月饼。
