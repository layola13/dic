gamb:/ɡæmb/ | n. | 赌博；冒险 | A gamble is a risky action with the hope of a significant reward.  It often involves uncertainty and chance. | 例句：He took a gamble on the new business venture. 他在新业务风险投资上赌了一把。 | 例句：She gambled away her savings. 她把积蓄都赌光了。 | 近义词：bet, wager, risk, speculation | 反义词：certainty, sure thing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gambles every weekend. = 他每个周末都赌博。
They gamble on horse races. = 他们赌赛马。

一般过去时:
He gambled away his inheritance. = 他把他的遗产都赌掉了。
She gambled on a winning lottery ticket. = 她买了一张彩票，赌它会中奖。

现在完成时:
He has gambled all his money away. = 他把所有的钱都赌光了。
She has never gambled before. = 她以前从未赌博过。

过去完成时:
He had gambled away his fortune before he realized his mistake. = 他在他意识到错误之前就已经把他的财产都赌掉了。
They had gambled on the wrong horse. = 他们赌错了马。

将来时:
He will gamble again next week. = 他下周还会再赌。
She will not gamble anymore. = 她不会再赌博了。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would gamble. = 如果我有更多钱，我会赌博。
I wish I hadn't gambled so much. = 我真希望我没有赌这么多。

被动语态:  (The verb "gamble" is rarely used in the passive voice because it requires a conscious actor.)


疑问句:
Does he gamble often? = 他经常赌博吗？
Will they gamble tonight? = 他们今晚会赌博吗？

否定句:
He doesn't gamble anymore. = 他不再赌博了。
She didn't gamble on the election. = 她没有在选举上赌博。


PHRASE->
take a gamble = 冒险一试
high-stakes gamble = 高风险赌博
a gamble on = 对…进行冒险
a calculated gamble = 精算的赌博
gamble away = 赌掉
