fatting: /ˈfætiŋ/ | v. | 喂养（使肥胖）；使肥沃 | The act of making something or someone fat, typically animals for slaughter or land for agriculture.  | 例句：The farmer is fatting his pigs for the market.  = 农民正在为市场养肥他的猪。  | 例句：Intensive fatting of cattle led to environmental problems. = 集约化养牛导致了环境问题。| 近义词：feeding, plumping, enriching | 反义词：starving, emaciating, depleting | 动词
