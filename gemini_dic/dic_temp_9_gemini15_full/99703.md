interlocutory: /ɪnˈtɜːləkjuːtəri/ | adj. | 中间的；中间的；暂时的； interlocutory order 中间令；临时命令 |  Relating to or connected with a matter pending in a court of law.  An interlocutory order is an order made during the course of a lawsuit which does not finally dispose of the case. | 例句：The judge issued an interlocutory order to preserve evidence. = 法官发布了一项中间命令以保存证据。


The interlocutory hearing addressed procedural matters before the trial. = 在审判之前，中间听证会处理程序问题。| 近义词：interim, provisional, temporary | 反义词：final, definitive
