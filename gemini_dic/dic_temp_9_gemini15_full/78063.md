geocentrically:/ˌdʒioʊˈsɛntrɪkəli/| adv.|地心说地；以地球为中心的 |Adverb describing something done or viewed from the perspective of the Earth being the center of the universe.  It implies a view that is outdated in astronomy but may be relevant in other contexts.|例句：The ancient Greeks viewed the cosmos geocentrically. 古希腊人以地球为中心来看待宇宙。


例句：  Early astronomers interpreted celestial movements geocentrically. 早期的天文学家用地心说的方法解释天体的运动。


近义词：earth-centeredly


反义词：heliocentrically (日心说地)


词性：副词
