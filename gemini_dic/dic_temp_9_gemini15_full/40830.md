convulsibility:/ˌkɒnvʌlsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |易于抽搐；痉挛性；发作性 | The susceptibility or proneness to convulsions or seizures.  It refers to the degree to which an individual or organism is likely to experience convulsive episodes.  |Example: The patient's high fever increased his convulsibility.  这个病人的高烧增加了他的易发惊厥性。|近义词：susceptibility to seizures, proneness to convulsions |反义词：resistance to convulsions, seizure-free
