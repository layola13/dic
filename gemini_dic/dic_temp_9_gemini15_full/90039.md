hospitably:/ˈhɒspɪtəbli/| adj.|好客地；殷勤地|In a hospitable manner; showing friendliness and generosity to guests or strangers.  |例句：They were welcomed hospitably into the warm home. 他们受到了热情好客的欢迎，被迎进温暖的家中。|例句：The villagers hospitably offered us food and shelter for the night. 村民们热情地为我们提供食物和住所过夜。|近义词：warmly, kindly, graciously|反义词：inhospitably, unkindly, ungraciously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hospitably welcome every visitor. = 他们热情地欢迎每一位访客。
The hotel staff hospitably assists guests with their luggage. = 酒店员工热情地帮助客人搬运行李。
