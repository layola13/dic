nagana:/nəˈɡɑːnə/| n. |那加那病，非洲锥虫病 |A disease caused by Trypanosoma brucei, a parasite transmitted by the tsetse fly.  Symptoms include fever, headache, joint pain, and, in the chronic stage, neurological problems.  It is prevalent in sub-Saharan Africa.  |Example: Nagana is a serious disease affecting livestock in many parts of Africa. 那加那病是非洲许多地区牲畜的严重疾病。|近义词：African trypanosomiasis|反义词：无|名词
