calumet:/ˈkæləˌmɛt/| n. |和平烟斗；礼仪烟斗|A ceremonial pipe, especially one used by Native Americans in religious ceremonies or to seal treaties.  It symbolizes peace and friendship. |Example: The chiefs exchanged calumets as a sign of peace.  酋长们交换了和平烟斗以示和平。|近义词：ceremonial pipe, peace pipe|反义词：weapon, instrument of war
