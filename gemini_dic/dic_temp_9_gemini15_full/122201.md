misused:/ˌmɪsˈjuːzd/ | adj. | 误用的；被滥用的 |  Used in a wrong or inappropriate way.  | 例句：The power of the internet has been misused by some individuals for illegal activities.  互联网的力量被一些人滥用于非法活动。

例句：He misused his authority by firing employees without just cause. 他滥用职权，无故解雇员工。


近义词：abused, misapplied, wrongly used | 反义词：correctly used, properly used, appropriately used | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tool is misused by many users. = 许多用户误用了这个工具。
This law is misused to discriminate against minorities. = 这项法律被滥用于歧视少数民族。


一般过去时:
The funds were misused for personal gain. = 这些资金被挪作私用。
The software was misused to create fraudulent documents. = 该软件被误用于创建虚假文件。


现在完成时:
The system has been misused repeatedly. = 该系统已被反复滥用。
The company's logo has been misused in several advertisements. = 该公司的标识在几则广告中被误用。


过去完成时:
The data had been misused before the security breach was discovered. = 在发现安全漏洞之前，数据已被滥用。
The research findings had been misused to support a flawed argument. = 研究结果已被误用于支持一个有缺陷的论点。


将来时:
The new technology will likely be misused if proper safeguards are not in place. = 如果没有适当的保障措施，这项新技术很可能会被滥用。
The policy will be misused by those seeking to exploit loopholes. = 这项政策会被那些试图利用漏洞的人滥用。


虚拟语气:
If the information had not been misused, the project would have succeeded. = 如果信息没有被滥用，项目就会成功。
If the law were not so easily misused, there would be fewer legal challenges. = 如果法律不容易被滥用，就会减少法律挑战。


被动语态:
The resources were misused by the management team. = 资源被管理团队滥用。
The regulations are often misused by businesses to avoid responsibility. = 法规经常被企业滥用来逃避责任。


疑问句:
Was the software misused in the production of that report? = 该软件是否被误用于制作该报告？
How was the information misused in this case? = 在这种情况下，信息是如何被滥用的？


否定句:
The data was not misused; it was simply misinterpreted. = 数据没有被滥用；只是被误解了。
The regulations were not misused; they were followed correctly. = 法规没有被滥用；而是被正确遵守。

词源:
The word "misused" comes from the prefix "mis-" (meaning wrongly or badly) and the verb "use".  It's a past participle form functioning as an adjective.
