cementatory: /ˌsɪˈmɛntətɔːri/ | adj. | 胶结的；水泥的 | Relating to or promoting cementation or consolidation.  The process of cementation involves the binding together of particles or materials, often using a cementing agent.  This adjective describes something that facilitates or is characteristic of this process. |


例句：

The cementatory process solidified the loose sand into a solid mass. = 胶结过程将松散的沙子凝固成固体。

The rock formation showed clear evidence of cementatory action. = 岩层显示出明显的胶结作用证据。


近义词：consolidating, binding, unifying

反义词：disintegrating, separating, disuniting

词性：形容词
