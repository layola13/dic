cornier:/ˈkɔːrnɪər/| adj.|俗气的，矫揉造作的，土气的| overly sentimental or sentimental in a naive or affected way; excessively or unpleasantly sweet or sentimental.  |例句：The movie had a cornier plot than I expected. 这部电影的情节比我预期的更俗套。

例句：Her overly sweet and sentimental style of writing felt cornier than genuine emotion. 她过于甜腻和感伤的写作风格感觉比真挚的情感更做作。


近义词：sentimental, mawkish, schmaltzy, kitschy, cheesy


反义词：sophisticated, understated, subtle, genuine, realistic


词性：形容词
