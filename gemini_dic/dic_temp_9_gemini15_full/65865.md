evokers:/ɪˈvoʊkərz/ | n. |唤起者；引起回忆的人或事物 | A person or thing that evokes something. Evokers are often used in the context of art, music, or experiences that bring about a specific feeling or memory.  |Example: The old photographs were powerful evokers of nostalgia.  那些旧照片强烈地唤起了人们的怀旧之情。The evocative music was a powerful evoker of emotion in the audience.  那催人泪下的音乐强烈地激起了观众的情绪。|近义词：reminders, stimulators, triggers |反义词： inhibitors, suppressants |名词
