haul-up:/ˈhɔːl ʌp/| vt.|拉起，提升；逮捕；使停泊|To pull or lift something heavy with effort.  Also to arrest someone.|例句：The sailors hauled up the anchor. 水手们拉起了锚。

例句：The police hauled him up for speeding. 警察以超速的罪名逮捕了他。


近义词：hoist, lift, raise, arrest, apprehend|反义词：lower, drop, release


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They haul up the nets every evening. = 他们每天晚上都拉起渔网。
The crane hauls up heavy loads. = 起重机吊起重物。


一般过去时:
The fisherman hauled up a large fish. = 渔夫拉起了一条大鱼。
The police hauled him up for questioning. = 警察将他逮捕审问。


现在完成时:
They have hauled up all the equipment. = 他们已经把所有设备都拉上来了。
The police have hauled up several suspects. = 警察已经逮捕了几名嫌疑犯。


过去完成时:
They had hauled up the sails before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经收起了帆。
The detective had hauled him up for interrogation several times. = 侦探已经将他传唤审问过好几次了。


将来时:
They will haul up the anchor at dawn. = 他们将在黎明时分拉起锚。
The construction crew will haul up the beams tomorrow. = 建筑队明天将吊起梁木。


虚拟语气:
If they hauled up the net carefully, they wouldn't have lost the fish. = 如果他们小心地拉起渔网，就不会丢失那条鱼了。
If the police had hauled him up sooner, the crime might have been prevented. = 如果警方早点逮捕他，也许就能阻止这起犯罪了。


被动语态:
The anchor was hauled up by the crew. = 锚被船员拉了上来。
The suspect was hauled up before the judge. = 嫌疑人被带到法官面前。


疑问句:
Were the supplies hauled up successfully? = 补给品是否成功地被运送上来了？
Will the heavy machinery be hauled up easily? = 重型机械容易被吊起来吗？


否定句:
They didn't haul up the trapdoor. = 他们没有拉起活板门。
The police did not haul him up immediately. = 警察没有立即逮捕他。


PHRASE->
haul up the anchor 拉起锚
haul up the sails 收起帆
haul up a boat  把船拖上来

haul up the net 拉起渔网

词源:
The word "haul" originates from Old Norse "hala," meaning "to drag" or "to pull." The addition of "up" simply specifies the direction of the pulling action.
