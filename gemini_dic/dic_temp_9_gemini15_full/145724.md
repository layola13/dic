overcredulous:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdʒələs/ | adj. | 过分轻信的，轻信的 | Excessively trusting; gullible.  Someone who is overcredulous readily believes things without sufficient evidence. | He was overcredulous and fell victim to a scam. 他过于轻信，成为骗局的受害者。|近义词：gullible, naive, trusting | 反义词：skeptical, cynical, distrustful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overcredulous about everything. 他对所有事情都过于轻信。
She is overcredulous, often believing rumors. 她很轻信，经常相信谣言。


一般过去时:
He was overcredulous in his youth. 他年轻时过于轻信。
She was overcredulous and easily tricked. 她很轻信，很容易被欺骗。


现在完成时:
He has been overcredulous throughout his life. 他一生都过于轻信。
She has been overcredulous, leading to many disappointments. 她一直很轻信，导致了很多失望。


过去完成时:
He had been overcredulous, which explained his financial troubles. 他以前过于轻信，这解释了他的经济困境。
She had been overcredulous, so she didn't suspect the deception. 她以前很轻信，所以她没有怀疑到欺骗。


将来时:
He will be overcredulous even in his old age. 他即使上了年纪，也会过于轻信。
She will be overcredulous and make the same mistake again. 她会很轻信，再次犯同样的错误。


虚拟语气:
If he were less overcredulous, he wouldn't have lost so much money. 如果他不那么轻信，他就不会损失那么多钱。
If she weren't so overcredulous, she wouldn't have believed that lie. 如果她不那么轻信，她就不会相信那个谎言。


被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used passively)  The claim was seen as being promoted by an overcredulous audience.  这个说法被认为是由一个过于轻信的观众推动的。


疑问句:
Is he overcredulous to the point of foolishness? 他轻信到愚蠢的地步了吗？
Was she overcredulous enough to fall for that obvious lie? 她轻信到足以相信那个明显的谎言吗？


否定句:
He is not overcredulous; he always questions claims. 他并不轻信；他总是质疑说法。
She is not overcredulous; she is highly skeptical. 她并不轻信；她非常怀疑。


PHRASE->
