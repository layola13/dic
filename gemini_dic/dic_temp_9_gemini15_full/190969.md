scaleboard:/ˈskeɪlbɔːrd/| n. |滑板|A skateboard is a flat, board-like object with wheels, used for recreational riding.  It's typically ridden by standing on the board and pushing off the ground with one foot, or by using the momentum from gravity on inclines. | Example: He learned to ride a scaleboard at the local skatepark. 他在当地的滑板公园学会了滑滑板。|近义词：skateboard|反义词：None
