infuse:/ɪnˈfjuːz/ | vt. | 注入；灌输；冲泡 | to fill something or someone with a particular quality or feeling; to steep (tea, etc.) in hot water | 例句：The tea infused with lemon is very refreshing.  柠檬茶很清爽。


The music infused the air with a sense of joy. 音乐给空气注入了快乐的感觉。

近义词：inject, imbue, instill, steep | 反义词：extract, remove, deplete | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He infuses his speeches with humor. = 他在他的演讲中注入了幽默。
She infuses the tea with fresh mint. = 她用新鲜薄荷冲泡茶。

一般过去时:
The artist infused his paintings with vibrant colors. = 艺术家在他的画作中注入了鲜艳的色彩。
They infused the fabric with a water-repellent solution. = 他们用防水溶液处理了织物。

现在完成时:
The aroma of spices has infused the kitchen. = 香料的香味已经弥漫了整个厨房。
She has infused her work with a strong sense of purpose. = 她已经赋予她的工作强烈的目标感。

过去完成时:
He had infused the story with elements of fantasy. = 他已经在他的故事中融入了奇幻元素。
They had infused the cake with rum before baking. = 他们在烘焙前在蛋糕里注入了朗姆酒。

将来时:
She will infuse the fabric with a natural dye. = 她将用天然染料染色织物。
He will infuse his next novel with social commentary. = 他将在他的下一部小说中加入社会评论。

虚拟语气:
If I infused the tea longer, it would be stronger. = 如果我把茶泡久一点，它会更浓。
I wish I had infused more flavor into the dish. = 我真希望我在菜里多加点调味料。

被动语态:
The fabric was infused with a special treatment. = 该织物经过特殊处理。
The air was infused with the scent of roses. = 空气中弥漫着玫瑰的香味。

疑问句:
How long do you infuse the tea? = 你泡茶要泡多久？
Does she infuse her writing with personal experiences? = 她的写作中是否融入了个人经历？

否定句:
He did not infuse the speech with enough passion. = 他没有在演讲中注入足够的激情。
They did not infuse the cake with enough sugar. = 他们没有在蛋糕中加入足够的糖。


PHRASE->
infuse with = 注入...
infuse into = 注入到...中
infuse a sense of = 灌输一种...的感觉
infuse energy into = 给...注入活力
infused water = 注入水的 (指泡过的水)
