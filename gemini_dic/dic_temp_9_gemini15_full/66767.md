exostome:/ˈɛksoʊstoʊm/| n. |外口; 外唇|The exostome is the outer lip or opening of a structure, particularly in botany and zoology.  It often refers to the external opening of a structure like a capsule or a shell. For example, in some moss capsules, the exostome refers to the outer layer of teeth surrounding the peristome. |例句：The exostome of the moss capsule was clearly visible under the microscope.  苔藓蒴果的外口在显微镜下清晰可见。

The exostome of the snail shell was damaged. 蜗牛壳的外唇受损了。|近义词：aperture, opening, orifice |反义词：endostome (内口)
