grouping:/ˈɡruːpɪŋ/ | n. |分组，组合，群组 |The act or process of grouping things together; a collection of things grouped together.  This refers to the act of classifying or arranging items into categories or sets based on shared characteristics or purposes.  It can also refer to the resulting collection itself. |例句：The grouping of students into classes was based on their academic abilities. 学生的分班是根据他们的学习能力进行的。

例句：The artistic grouping of colors created a stunning visual effect.  颜色的艺术化组合创造了令人惊叹的视觉效果。

近义词：classification, categorization, aggregation, cluster, assembly |反义词：separation, scattering, dispersal |词性: 名词
