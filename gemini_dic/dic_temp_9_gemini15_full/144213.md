outskate:/ˌaʊtˈskeɪt/| v.|胜过(在滑冰方面)|To skate faster or better than (someone).  This verb implies a competition or comparison of skating skills.|例句：He outskated all his opponents in the final race. 他在决赛中滑冰速度超过所有对手。

例句：The young skater outskated her more experienced rival.  年轻的滑冰者滑冰技术胜过她更有经验的对手。


近义词：outperform, surpass, excel


反义词：be outskated by


词性：动词
