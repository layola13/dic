nonsuccession: /ˌnɒn.səkˈsɛʃ.ən/ | n. |非继承；未继承；未接替 | The lack of succession; failure to succeed or be succeeded.  The absence of a continuous line of inheritance or succession.  | Example: The nonsuccession of the throne led to a period of instability. 王位的无人继承导致了一段动荡时期。 The nonsuccession of his business to his son caused a major disruption. 他的生意没有传给儿子，导致了严重的混乱。 |近义词：failure, default, interruption |反义词：succession, inheritance, continuation |
