ledgers:/ˈlɛdʒərz/| n. |分类账，账簿|A ledger is a book or a computer file used for recording financial transactions.  It provides a systematic record of debits and credits, enabling businesses and individuals to track their financial activities.  Ledgers are essential for maintaining accurate financial records and complying with accounting standards.|例句：The accountant meticulously updated the ledgers at the end of each month. 会计员每个月底都会仔细更新分类账。

The company's ledgers showed a significant increase in profits this quarter.  公司的账簿显示本季度利润大幅增长。|近义词：account book, record book|反义词：无
