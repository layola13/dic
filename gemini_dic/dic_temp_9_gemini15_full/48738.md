define:/dɪˈfaɪn/ | vt. | 定义，解释；限定，规定；使明确；使突出 | To explain the meaning of something, especially a word or concept; to state the exact limits of something.  |  The dictionary defines "happy" as "feeling or showing pleasure or contentment."  字典将“快乐”定义为“感到或表现出快乐或满足”。 | clarify, explain, specify, delineate | obscure, confuse,  | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dictionary defines "happy" as "feeling or showing pleasure or contentment." = 字典将“快乐”定义为“感到或表现出快乐或满足”。
We define success as achieving our goals. = 我们把成功定义为实现我们的目标。


一般过去时:
The scientist defined the parameters of the experiment. = 科学家定义了实验的参数。
She clearly defined her expectations for the project. = 她清楚地界定了她对项目的期望。


现在完成时:
Scientists have defined the structure of DNA. = 科学家已经定义了DNA的结构。
They have clearly defined their roles in the company. = 他们已经清楚地定义了他们在公司中的角色。


过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had already defined his key points. = 在演示之前，他已经定义了他的要点。
By that time, they had carefully defined the scope of the work. = 到那时，他们已经仔细地界定了工作的范围。


将来时:
We will define the terms of the contract tomorrow. = 我们明天将定义合同的条款。
The committee will define the criteria for the award. = 委员会将定义该奖项的标准。


虚拟语气:
If we defined the problem differently, we might find a better solution. = 如果我们对问题的定义有所不同，我们可能会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish I had defined my objectives more clearly at the beginning. = 我希望我在一开始就更清楚地定义了我的目标。


被动语态:
The terms of the agreement were defined in the contract. = 协议条款在合同中已定义。
The project's scope was clearly defined before it began. = 项目的范围在开始之前已明确定义。


疑问句:
How do you define success? = 你如何定义成功？
What defines a good leader? = 什么定义了一个好领导？


否定句:
The law does not clearly define the limits of its jurisdiction. = 法律并没有明确界定其管辖范围的界限。
He failed to define his position on the issue. = 他未能就这个问题表明自己的立场。
