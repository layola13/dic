rhapsodises:/ˈræpsədaɪzɪz/| v.|狂喜地说话；热情洋溢地讲述； |to speak or write with great enthusiasm and feeling, often in a rambling or disorganized way.  |例句：He rhapsodised about his travels in the Himalayas. 他热情洋溢地讲述他在喜马拉雅山的旅行经历。

例句：She rhapsodised for hours about her new grandson. 她兴奋地谈论了她新出生的孙子好几个小时。


近义词：rave, enthuse, expatiate, declaim


反义词：criticize, condemn, disparage


词性:动词
