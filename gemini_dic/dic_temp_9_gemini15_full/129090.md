nitrosyl: /ˈnaɪtrəsɪl/ | n. | 亚硝酰基 | A nitrosyl group is a chemical group consisting of a nitrogen atom double-bonded to an oxygen atom (–NO). It is a ligand in many coordination complexes.  Nitrosyl groups are important in various chemical processes and are found in many compounds, including some that play roles in biological systems. | Example: The nitrosyl ligand in this complex exhibits a linear geometry. 这个配合物中的亚硝酰基配体呈线性几何形状。 |近义词:  (no direct synonym,  related terms: nitro, nitroso) |反义词: (no direct antonym) |词性:名词
