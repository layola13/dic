quarried:/ˈkwɒrid/ /ˈkwɔːrid/| v. |采石；开采；挖掘|To extract stone or other materials from a quarry.  The verb "quarry" also implies a laborious process of extracting something valuable or important.|The workers quarried granite from the mountainside. 工人们从山坡上采石。 They quarried information from various sources. 他们从各种来源收集信息。|mined, extracted|filled, concealed|动词
