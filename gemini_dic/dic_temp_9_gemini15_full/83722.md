hah:/hɑː/| int.|哈，哈哈（表示笑声）|An exclamation expressing laughter.  |例句：He let out a loud "hah!" after hearing the joke. 听到笑话后，他大声笑了出来“哈！”|近义词：ha, hehe, hoho|反义词:None.


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't follow typical grammatical tense structures.  It's used to express laughter.

PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "hah" as a core component.  It's primarily used on its own to represent laughter.
