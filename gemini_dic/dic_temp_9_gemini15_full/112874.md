lucubrate:/ˈluːkjuːbreɪt/| v.|潜心研究；苦思冥想|To study or write diligently and laboriously, especially at night; to ponder or work over something painstakingly.|例句：He lucubrated for months on his latest novel. 他潜心研究了几个月才完成他的最新小说。
例句：She lucubrated over the complex mathematical problem. 她苦思冥想这道复杂的数学题。|近义词：ponder, contemplate, meditate, study|反义词：neglect, ignore

词源:
The word "lucubrate" comes from the Latin word "lucubrare," meaning "to work by candlelight."  This reflects the historical practice of scholars and writers working late into the night, often illuminated only by candles.
