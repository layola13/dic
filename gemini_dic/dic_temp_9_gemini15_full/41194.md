copywriting:/ˈkɒpiˌraɪtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːpiˌraɪtɪŋ/| n.|文案撰写；广告文案创作|The art and practice of writing text for the purpose of advertising or other forms of marketing.|例句：Effective copywriting is crucial for successful marketing campaigns.  有效的文案撰写对成功的营销活动至关重要。

例句：Her copywriting skills helped the company increase sales significantly. 她的文案写作技巧帮助公司显著增加了销售额。


近义词：advertising writing, marketing writing, content creation


反义词：None (lack of copywriting isn't an antonym, but rather an absence of a specific skill)


词性:名词
