protest:/ˈprəʊtest/| /ˈproʊtest/| n. v. |抗议，反对；声明…不真实|The act of expressing strong disapproval and opposition to something; a statement that something is untrue. |例句：The students staged a protest against the tuition fee increase. 学生们举行示威抗议学费上涨。|例句：He protested his innocence. 他声明自己是无辜的。|近义词：demonstration, objection, complaint, denial|反义词：acceptance, approval, consent|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They protest the government's policies. = 他们抗议政府的政策。
He protests his innocence every day. = 他每天都声明自己无辜。


一般过去时:
The workers protested the unfair treatment. = 工人们抗议不公平待遇。
She protested that she hadn't stolen the money. = 她抗议说她没有偷钱。


现在完成时:
They have protested against the war for months. = 他们已经抗议这场战争好几个月了。
He has protested his innocence repeatedly. = 他一再声明自己无辜。


过去完成时:
They had protested the decision before it was made. = 他们在决定作出之前就对此提出抗议。
She had protested her innocence to no avail. = 她声明自己无辜，但没有用。


将来时:
They will protest the new law next week. = 他们下周将抗议这项新法律。
He will protest the unfair judgment. = 他将抗议不公平的判决。


虚拟语气:
If they protested more forcefully, they might have achieved more. = 如果他们更强有力地抗议，也许能取得更多成果。
I wish I had protested the decision at the time. = 我真希望当时就对那个决定提出抗议。


被动语态:
The decision was protested by many people. = 许多人都对这个决定表示抗议。
His innocence was protested by his lawyer. = 他的律师为他声明无辜。


疑问句:
Did they protest the decision? = 他们抗议这个决定了吗？
Will he protest the unfairness? = 他会抗议这种不公平吗？


否定句:
They did not protest the government's new policy. = 他们没有抗议政府的新政策。
He didn't protest the decision, though he disagreed with it. = 他虽然不同意这个决定，但他并没有抗议。


PHRASE->
protest against = 抗议…
file a protest = 提出抗议
lodge a protest = 提出抗议
under protest = 在抗议下
strong protest = 强烈的抗议

词源:
From Old French *protester, from Latin *protestari "to testify, bear witness", from pro- "forth" + testari "to testify".  The modern meaning developed from the earlier sense of formally declaring something.
