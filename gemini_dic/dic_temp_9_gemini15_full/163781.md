predeliveries:/ˌpriːdɪˈlɪvəriːz/| n. |预先交付，提前交付|Deliveries that occur before the scheduled delivery date.  These might be partial shipments or deliveries made in anticipation of higher demand.|例句：The predeliveries helped alleviate the supply chain bottlenecks. 预先交付帮助缓解了供应链瓶颈。|例句： Unexpected predeliveries caused a temporary storage problem.意想不到的提前交付导致了暂时的存储问题。|近义词：advance deliveries, early deliveries|反义词：postdeliveries, late deliveries
