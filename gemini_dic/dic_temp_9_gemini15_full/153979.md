perfectly:/ˈpɜːrfɪktli/ | adv. |完美地，完全地 | perfectly describes something that is complete, flawless, or exactly as it should be. It emphasizes the completeness and lack of imperfection.  | The cake was perfectly baked.  这蛋糕烤得完美无瑕。 | completely, flawlessly, ideally | imperfectly, poorly, inadequately | 副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perfectly understands the instructions. 他完全理解了指示。
She perfectly executes the dance routine. 她完美地完成了舞蹈套路。

一般过去时:
The musician perfectly played the sonata. 音乐家完美地演奏了奏鸣曲。
They perfectly planned the surprise party. 他们完美地策划了惊喜派对。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
They had perfectly prepared for the exam. 他们已经为考试做好了充分的准备。
She had perfectly cleaned the house before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，她已经把房子打扫得干干净净。

将来时:

被动语态:

否定句:
