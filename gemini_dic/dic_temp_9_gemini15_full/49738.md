demographers:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfərz/ | n. | 人口统计学家 |  Experts who study the characteristics of human populations, such as size, growth, density, distribution, and vital statistics.  They analyze data to understand population trends and their impact on society. | The demographers predicted a significant increase in the elderly population over the next two decades.  人口统计学家预测未来二十年老年人口将大幅增加。 |  population scientists, population analysts |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
