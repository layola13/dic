leeched:/liːtʃt/| v.| 榨取；吸取；慢慢渗出 |  To extract or drain something gradually. It often implies an undesirable or parasitic action.  |例句：The company leeched money from its employees through excessive fees. 公司通过过高的费用从员工那里榨取钱财。

例句：The color slowly leeched from the fabric after being washed. 洗涤后颜色慢慢从织物中渗出。


近义词：extract, drain, deplete, suck, sap


反义词： replenish, restore, enrich


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The parasite leeches nutrients from its host. = 寄生虫从宿主身上吸取营养。
He leeches money from his parents. = 他从父母那里榨取钱财。


一般过去时:
The drought leeched the moisture from the soil. = 干旱使土壤中的水分流失。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
