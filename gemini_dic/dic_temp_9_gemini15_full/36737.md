collophore:/ˈkɒləfɔːr/| n. |粘附器；胶腺|A collophore is a ventral adhesive tube found in springtails (Collembola), used for clinging to surfaces.  It's a tubular structure that secretes a sticky substance allowing the insect to adhere to various substrates.|Example: The springtail used its collophore to climb the smooth glass surface.  这只弹尾虫用它的粘附器爬上了光滑的玻璃表面。|近义词：adhesive organ, ventral tube|反义词：N/A
