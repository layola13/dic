contact:/ˈkɒntækt/ /ˈkɑːntækt/| n., v.| 接触；联系；联络 | To communicate with someone; to touch or come into physical proximity with something or someone. | 例句：I will contact you later. 我稍后会联系你。


例句：I need to contact my lawyer. 我需要联系我的律师。


近义词：communicate, reach, touch, link


反义词：disconnect, separate, isolate


词性: 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I contact my friends regularly. = 我定期联系我的朋友。
He contacts his clients by email. = 他通过电子邮件联系他的客户。


一般过去时:
I contacted the company yesterday. = 我昨天联系了那家公司。
She contacted her doctor about her symptoms. = 她联系了她的医生，谈论她的症状。


现在完成时:
I have contacted them several times. = 我已经联系他们好几次了。
She has contacted the authorities about the incident. = 她已经就此事联系了有关部门。


过去完成时:
I had contacted him before he left. = 他离开之前我已经联系过他了。
They had contacted the police before the robbery. = 抢劫发生前他们已经报警了。


将来时:
I will contact you as soon as possible. = 我会尽快联系你。
He will contact the supplier tomorrow. = 他明天将联系供应商。


虚拟语气:
If I contacted him, he might help me. = 如果我联系他，他可能会帮助我。
I wish I had contacted her sooner. = 我真希望我早点联系她。


被动语态:
He was contacted by the police. = 他受到了警方的联系。
The company will be contacted next week. = 公司将在下周联系。


疑问句:
Have you contacted the bank yet? = 你已经联系银行了吗？
Will you contact me when you arrive? = 你到达时会联系我吗？


否定句:
I didn't contact him because I was busy. = 我没有联系他，因为我很忙。
She hasn't contacted me yet. = 她还没联系我。


PHRASE->
make contact = 取得联系
in contact with = 与...保持联系
lose contact with = 与...失去联系
contact information = 联系方式
contact lens = 隐形眼镜
