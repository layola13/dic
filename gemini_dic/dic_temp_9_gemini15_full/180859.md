rehibition:/ˌriːɪˈbɪʃən/ | n. |再展览，再展出|The act of exhibiting something again; a second exhibition.|例句：The museum announced a rehibition of the Van Gogh paintings. = 博物馆宣布了梵高画作的再次展出。|近义词：re-exhibition, second showing|反义词：removal, withdrawal|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum rehibits the paintings every five years. = 博物馆每五年重新展出这些画作。
This gallery rehibits the artwork of local artists. = 这个画廊展出当地艺术家的作品。


一般过去时:
They rehibited the sculptures last month. = 他们上个月重新展出了雕塑。
The gallery rehibited the collection after renovations. = 装修后，画廊重新展出了收藏品。


现在完成时:
The museum has rehibited the ancient artifacts several times. = 博物馆已经多次重新展出了这些古代文物。
The artist has rehibited his most famous work in a new location. = 这位艺术家在他的新工作室里重新展出了他最著名的作品。


过去完成时:
Before the fire, they had rehibited the paintings twice. = 在火灾之前，他们已经两次重新展出了这些画作。
The curator had rehibited the exhibit before the conference. = 馆长在会议前重新展出了展览。


将来时:
They will rehibit the collection next spring. = 他们将在明年春天重新展出收藏品。
The gallery will rehibit the photographs in a larger space. = 画廊将在更大的空间内重新展出照片。


虚拟语气:
If they had more space, they would rehibit all the paintings. = 如果他们有更多空间，他们就会重新展出所有画作。
I wish they would rehibit the sculptures again next year. = 我希望他们明年能再次重新展出这些雕塑。


被动语态:
The paintings were rehibited last year. = 这些画作去年重新展出了。
The exhibit is being rehibited for the anniversary. = 为了纪念周年庆，展览正在重新展出。


疑问句:
Will the museum rehibit the artifacts this year? = 博物馆今年会重新展出这些文物吗？
When will the gallery be rehibiting the paintings? = 画廊什么时候会重新展出这些画作？


否定句:
They did not rehibit the collection this year. = 他们今年没有重新展出收藏品。
The gallery will not be rehibiting the sculptures. = 画廊不会重新展出这些雕塑。
