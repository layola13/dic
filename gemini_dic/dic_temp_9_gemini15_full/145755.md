overcultivation:/ˌoʊvərˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/| n. |过度耕作，竭泽而渔|The practice of cultivating land to an excessive degree, resulting in soil degradation and depletion of nutrients.  This often leads to reduced yields and environmental damage.|例句：Overcultivation of the land led to soil erosion and desertification.  过度耕作导致土壤侵蚀和沙漠化。

例句：The farmers' overcultivation of the fields resulted in a significant decrease in crop production. 农民对田地的过度耕作导致农作物产量显著下降。


近义词：overfarming, excessive cultivation, soil exhaustion


反义词：conservation tillage, sustainable agriculture, crop rotation

词性:名词
