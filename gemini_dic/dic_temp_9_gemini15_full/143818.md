outpassing: /ˌaʊtˈpæsɪŋ/ | adj. |超越的，胜过的| surpassing or exceeding others; exceeding in excellence or achievement.  | The outpassing beauty of the sunset left everyone speechless.  日落的美景令人叹为观止。|近义词：surpassing, excelling, surpassing, exceeding |反义词：underperforming, inferior


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The athlete's performance is outpassing all expectations.  这位运动员的表现超出了所有人的预期。
His skills outpass those of his peers.  他的技能超过了他的同龄人。


一般过去时:
Her artistic talent outpassed that of her teachers.  她的艺术天赋超过了她的老师。
The team's score outpassed their rivals'.  该队的得分超过了他们的对手。


现在完成时:
The innovation has outpassed previous attempts.  这项创新已经超越了之前的尝试。
His achievements have outpassed those of his predecessors.  他的成就已经超过了他的前辈。


过去完成时:

The damage had outpassed initial assessments.  损失超过了最初的评估。


将来时:
This technology will outpass current limitations.  这项技术将超越目前的局限性。
Their influence will outpass geographical boundaries.  他们的影响力将超越地域界限。


虚拟语气:

If only I could outpass my limitations.  如果我能超越我的局限就好了。


被动语态:
His work was outpassed by a younger generation.  他的作品被年轻一代超越了。

否定句:
