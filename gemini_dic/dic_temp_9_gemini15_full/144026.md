outreproduce:/ˌaʊt.rəˈpruːs/ | vt. |超过…繁殖；繁殖数量超过 | To produce or reproduce more than; to surpass in reproduction.  | Example: Rabbits outreproduce most other mammals in their natural habitat.  兔子在它们的自然栖息地繁殖数量超过大多数其他哺乳动物。 | 近义词：surpass, exceed, outbreed | 反义词：underproduce, fail to reproduce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weeds outreproduce the cultivated plants. = 杂草繁殖数量超过栽培植物。
This species outreproduces its competitors. = 这个物种的繁殖数量超过其竞争对手。


一般过去时:
The rabbits outreproduced the available food supply. = 兔子的繁殖数量超过了可获得的食物供应。
The invasive species outreproduced the native plants. = 侵入物种的繁殖数量超过了本地植物。


现在完成时:
The pest has outreproduced all our efforts to control it. = 害虫的繁殖数量已经超过了我们控制它的所有努力。
This plant has outreproduced the others in the garden. = 这株植物的繁殖数量已经超过了花园里的其他植物。


过去完成时:
The insects had outreproduced their predators before the pesticides were applied. = 在使用杀虫剂之前，昆虫的繁殖数量已经超过了它们的捕食者。
The bacteria had outreproduced the antibiotics' effectiveness. = 细菌的繁殖数量已经超过了抗生素的有效性。


将来时:
If we don't take action, the rats will outreproduce the available resources. = 如果我们不采取行动，老鼠的繁殖数量将会超过可用的资源。
The algae will outreproduce the fish in the polluted lake. = 在污染的湖泊中，藻类的繁殖数量将会超过鱼类。


虚拟语气:
If the rabbits hadn't outreproduced the food supply, the ecosystem would be stable. = 如果兔子的繁殖数量没有超过食物供应，生态系统就会稳定。
If the weeds hadn't outreproduced the crops, the farmer would have had a good harvest. = 如果杂草的繁殖数量没有超过庄稼，农民就会有一个好收成。


被动语态:
The native species were outreproduced by the invasive plants. = 本地物种的繁殖数量被入侵植物超过了。
The existing plants were outreproduced by a new, fast-growing species. = 现有的植物的繁殖数量被一种新的快速生长的物种超过了。


疑问句:
Does this species outreproduce its competitors in this environment? = 这个物种在这个环境中繁殖数量是否超过其竞争对手？
Does the rabbit population outreproduce the carrying capacity of the habitat? = 兔子的数量是否超过了栖息地的承载能力？


否定句:
The population did not outreproduce the available resources. = 人口的繁殖数量没有超过可用的资源。
The farmer's crops did not outreproduce the weeds this year. = 农民的庄稼今年的繁殖数量没有超过杂草。
