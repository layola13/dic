nonlinear:/ˌnɒnˈlɪniər/| adj.|非线性的，非直线的|Not forming a straight line; having a curved or irregular form.  Describing a system or process where the output is not directly proportional to the input;  the relationship between cause and effect is not linear.  |例句：The relationship between stress and strain is often nonlinear.  应力与应变之间的关系通常是非线性的。

例句：The growth of a population is often modeled using nonlinear equations. 人口增长通常使用非线性方程建模。


近义词：curvilinear, curved, indirect, complex

反义词：linear, straight, direct, simple

词性:形容词
