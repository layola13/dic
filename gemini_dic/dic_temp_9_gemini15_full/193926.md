self-emitted:/ˌsɛlfɪˈmɪtɪd/| adj.| 自发发射的；自发辐射的 |  Something that is self-emitted produces or releases light, radiation, or other energy without external stimulation.  It describes a process where the emission originates from within the object or system itself. | Example: The  LED display showed a self-emitted light, bright even in daylight. 自发光LED显示屏即使在白天也很明亮。 The experiment measured the self-emitted radiation from the radioactive substance. 实验测量了放射性物质的自发辐射。| 近义词：intrinsic, spontaneous | 反义词：externally stimulated, induced
