sarcophile:/ˈsɑːrkəfaɪl/| n. |嗜肉者，食肉动物|A sarcophile is a person or animal that enjoys eating flesh. The term is often used in a more figurative sense to describe someone who enjoys violent or gruesome imagery or narratives.|例句：While some people find horror movies terrifying, others are sarcophiles, drawn to the graphic depictions of violence.|近义词：carnivore, flesh-eater|反义词：herbivore, vegetarian|名词
