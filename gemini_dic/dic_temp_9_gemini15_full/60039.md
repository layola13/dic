educating: /ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 教育；培养；训练 | The act of imparting knowledge or skills.  |例句：Educating children requires patience and dedication. 教育孩子需要耐心和奉献。

例句：The school is committed to educating students from diverse backgrounds. 学校致力于教育来自不同背景的学生。


近义词：teaching, instructing, training, schooling, developing

反义词：ignoring, neglecting

We educate our children to be responsible citizens. = 我们教育我们的孩子成为负责任的公民。
