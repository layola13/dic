day:/deɪ/ | n. | 天，日子，白天 | A day is a period of 24 hours, or the time between sunrise and sunset.  |  例句：Today is a beautiful day. 今天是个好天气。 | 例句： We had a busy day yesterday. 昨天我们度过了忙碌的一天。|近义词：period, time | 反义词：night


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun rises every day. = 太阳每天升起。
What day is it today? = 今天星期几？


一般过去时:
We went to the beach yesterday. = 昨天我们去了海滩。
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？


现在完成时:
I have seen that movie three times. = 我看过那部电影三次了。
Have you finished your work today? = 你今天完成工作了吗？


过去完成时:
I had already eaten before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经吃过了。
They had left before the storm began. = 暴风雨开始之前他们已经离开了。


将来时:
We will go to the park tomorrow. = 我们明天要去公园。
What will you do next day? = 你明天打算做什么？


虚拟语气:
If it were a sunny day, we would go to the beach. = 如果是晴天，我们就去海滩。
I wish it were a holiday today. = 我希望今天是假日。


被动语态:
The work was completed yesterday. = 工作昨天完成了。
The meeting was postponed to another day. = 会议推迟到另外一天。


疑问句:
What day is it today? = 今天星期几？
Which day do you prefer? = 你更喜欢哪一天？


否定句:
It wasn't a good day. = 那不是一个好日子。
I didn't go to work yesterday. = 我昨天没去上班。


PHRASE->
day after day = 日复一日
day by day = 一天一天地
day in and day out = 日复一日地
one day = 有一天
from day to day = 朝不保夕
