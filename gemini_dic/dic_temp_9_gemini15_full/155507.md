philanthrope:/ˈfɪlænθrəʊp/|/ˈfɪlənθrɔːp/| n. |慈善家，乐善好施者 |A philanthropist is a person who donates time and/or money to charitable causes.|例句：Bill Gates is a well-known philanthropist.比尔·盖茨是一位著名的慈善家。|例句：Many philanthropists support medical research.许多慈善家支持医学研究。|近义词：benefactor, donor, patron |反义词：miser, skinflint

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He donates to many charities. = 他捐款给许多慈善机构。
She volunteers at a local hospital. = 她在当地医院做志愿者。


一般过去时:
The philanthropist gave a large sum to the museum. = 这位慈善家向博物馆捐赠了一大笔款项。
They supported the orphanage last year. = 他们去年资助了那家孤儿院。


现在完成时:
He has contributed millions to education. = 他已经为教育事业捐赠了数百万美元。
She has volunteered her time for many years. = 她多年来一直做志愿者工作。


过去完成时:
Before the crisis, they had already donated significant funds. = 在危机爆发前，他们已经捐赠了大量的资金。
She had supported numerous charities throughout her career. = 在她职业生涯中，她一直支持许多慈善机构。


将来时:
He will continue his philanthropic work. = 他将继续他的慈善事业。
They will donate to the new hospital building fund. = 他们将向新的医院建设基金捐款。


虚拟语气:
If I were a philanthropist, I would fund this project. = 如果我是一位慈善家，我会资助这个项目。
I wish I could donate more to those in need. = 我希望我能向那些需要帮助的人捐款更多。


被动语态:

疑问句:
