pastime:/ˈpɑːstaɪm/ | n. |消遣；娱乐 | A pastime is an activity that people do for enjoyment when they are not working.  It is something that people do in their free time. | Example: Reading is a relaxing pastime for many people.  阅读对许多人来说是一种轻松的消遣。

Gardening is her favorite pastime. 园艺是她最喜欢的消遣。|近义词：hobby, recreation, amusement, leisure activity |反义词：work, chore, duty

USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your favorite pastime? = 你最喜欢的消遣是什么？
His pastime is playing video games. = 他的消遣是玩电子游戏。

一般过去时:
What was your pastime when you were a child? = 你小时候的消遣是什么？
Yesterday, my pastime was watching movies. = 昨天，我的消遣是看电影。

现在完成时:
What pastimes have you enjoyed this year? = 今年你享受过哪些消遣？
She has enjoyed many pastimes throughout her life. = 她一生中享受过许多消遣。

过去完成时:
What pastimes had he pursued before retirement? = 他退休前从事过哪些消遣？
Before the pandemic, my pastime had been traveling. = 在疫情爆发之前，我的消遣是旅行。


将来时:
What pastime will you take up next year? = 你明年会从事什么消遣？
I will spend my retirement pursuing my pastimes. = 我将把退休时间花在我的消遣上。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pursue more pastimes. = 如果我有更多时间，我会从事更多消遣。
I wish I had the time for more pastimes. = 我希望我有时间从事更多消遣。

被动语态:
(Pastime is rarely used in the passive voice.)  消遣很少以被动语态使用。
Many pastimes are enjoyed by people of all ages.  各种年龄段的人都喜欢许多消遣活动。(This focuses on the pastimes being enjoyed, not the action of enjoying them being done to the pastimes.)


疑问句:
What is a good pastime for someone who likes to be creative? = 对于喜欢创意的人来说，有什么好的消遣？
What are some popular pastimes in your country? = 你们国家有哪些流行的消遣？

否定句:
He doesn't have much time for pastimes. = 他没有多少时间用于消遣。
She doesn't consider watching TV a worthwhile pastime. = 她不认为看电视是一种有价值的消遣。


PHRASE->
favorite pastime = 最喜欢的消遣
leisure pastime = 休闲消遣
relaxing pastime = 放松的消遣
challenging pastime = 富有挑战性的消遣
engaging pastime = 令人参与的消遣
