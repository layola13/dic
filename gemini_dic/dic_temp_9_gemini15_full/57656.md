drawbench:/ˈdrɔːbentʃ/ | n. |拉拔机，拉丝机 | A drawbench is a machine used to draw or pull metal through a die to reduce its diameter or change its shape.  It's commonly used in the manufacturing of wire, rods, and tubes.  |例句：The factory uses a powerful drawbench to produce high-tensile steel wire.  这家工厂使用一台强大的拉拔机来生产高张力钢丝。

The new drawbench increased production efficiency significantly. 这台新的拉拔机显著提高了生产效率。|近义词：wire drawing machine, rod drawing machine |反义词：None  |词性:名词
