latins:/ˈlætnz/| n. |拉丁人；拉丁语使用者|People of Latin America or of Latin descent.  The term is often used in a broad sense to encompass various ethnic groups and nationalities with origins in Latin America, including those of Spanish, Portuguese, and French descent.  However, the term can be considered controversial due to its potential for oversimplification and homogenization of diverse cultures.|例句：Many latins celebrate Dia de los Muertos. 许多拉丁裔庆祝亡灵节。|近义词：Hispanics, Latinos |反义词：None.
