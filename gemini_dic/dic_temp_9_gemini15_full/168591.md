processor:/ˈprɑːsɛsər/ /ˈprɒsɛsə(r)/| n. |处理器，处理机|A processor is an electronic circuit that executes instructions comprising a computer program.  It is the central processing unit (CPU) of a computer. Processors perform arithmetic, logical, control, and input/output operations specified by the instructions.  Different types of processors exist, optimized for various tasks.| Example:  My computer's processor is quite powerful. 我的电脑处理器很强大。 The image processor enhanced the photo quality. 图像处理器提升了照片质量。|近义词：CPU, central processing unit, microchip |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
