component: /kəmˈpoʊnənt/ | n. | 成分；部件；组件 | A constituent part of a whole; a part or element of a larger whole.  | The main components of the machine are the engine, the transmission, and the chassis. 这台机器的主要部件是发动机、变速器和底盘。| element, part, constituent, ingredient |  whole, entirety
