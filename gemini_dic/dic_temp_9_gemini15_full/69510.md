fell:/fɛl/| /fel/ | v. | 掉落；跌倒；砍伐；降临；(感情)骤降 | to drop or fall from a higher to a lower position; to cut down (a tree); to happen suddenly or unexpectedly; to experience a sudden decline in (emotion)|

例句：
The apple fell from the tree.  苹果从树上掉了下来。

The old oak tree fell during the storm.  那棵古老的橡树在暴风雨中倒塌了。


近义词：drop, tumble, plunge, descend, collapse

反义词：rise, ascend, climb, elevate
