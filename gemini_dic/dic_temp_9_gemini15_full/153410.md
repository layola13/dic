penitentially:/pɛnɪˈtɛnʃəli/| adv.|忏悔地；悔恨地|In a repentant manner; showing or feeling penitence.|She spoke penitentially about her past mistakes. 她忏悔地谈论了她过去的错误。|近义词：contritely, remorsefully|反义词：unrepentantly, defiantly

疑问句:
Does he speak penitentially about his past? 他是否忏悔地谈论他的过去？

否定句:
