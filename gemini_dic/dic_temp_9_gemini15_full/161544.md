potluck:/ˈpɑːtlʌk/| n.|百宝宴，自带食物聚餐|A potluck is a meal where each person brings a dish to share.  It's a social gathering where the food is contributed by all the participants, rather than being provided by a single host.|Example: We're having a potluck dinner next Saturday; please bring a dish to share.  下周六我们有个百宝宴；请带一道菜来分享。|近义词：shared meal, buffet, gathering|反义词：N/A
