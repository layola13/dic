federative:/ˈfedəreɪtɪv/| adj.|联邦的，联邦制的|Relating to or denoting a system of government in which several states, regions, or other entities unite under a central authority but retain certain degrees of self-government.  A federative system is characterized by a division of powers between a central government and constituent units.|例句：The United States has a federative system of government.  美国采用联邦政府体制。

例句：Many countries adopted federative structures to balance regional autonomy with national unity. 许多国家采用联邦结构来平衡区域自治和国家统一。


近义词：federal, confederal, unified (in a sense of shared governance)|反义词：unitary, centralized
