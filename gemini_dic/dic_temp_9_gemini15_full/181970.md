remasters:/ˈriːmæstərz/| n. |重新制作的版本；重新掌握的技能|A remastered version of a song or album is a new version that has been improved in terms of sound quality.  It often involves updating the audio to modern standards using advanced techniques.|例句：The band released a remastered version of their debut album. 乐队发行了一张他们首张专辑的重新制作版本。

例句：He has remastered his skills in photography after years of practice. 他经过多年的练习，重新掌握了摄影技巧。|近义词：re-releases, improved versions|反义词：original versions, unimproved versions
