nonproximity: /ˌnɒnprɒkˈsɪməti/ | n. |非临近性，非近似性 | The state or quality of being not near or close; remoteness.  It often refers to a lack of physical closeness, but can also apply to conceptual or relational distance.  | 例句：The nonproximity of the two villages made communication difficult.  两个村庄的距离遥远使得沟通困难。

The nonproximity of their ideas prevented a collaborative project. 他们思想上的差异阻碍了合作项目的进行。| 近义词：distance, remoteness, separation | 反义词：proximity, closeness, nearness |
