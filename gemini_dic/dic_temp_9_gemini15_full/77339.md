gauchest:/ɡoʊˈʃeɪst/| adj.|最笨拙的；最不灵活的；最缺乏技巧的|Most awkward, clumsy, or unskillful.  It implies a lack of grace and social dexterity, often resulting in embarrassment.|例句：He felt gauchest at the formal dinner party, unsure of which fork to use. 他在正式的晚宴上感到很笨拙，不知道该用哪个叉子。

例句：Her gauchest attempt at a dance move only served to make everyone laugh. 她最笨拙的舞蹈动作只逗笑了大家。


近义词：awkward, clumsy, ungainly, inept, maladroit|反义词：graceful, elegant, skillful, dexterous, adroit


词性：形容词
