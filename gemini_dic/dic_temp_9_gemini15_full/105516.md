kilolitre:/ˈkɪləˌliːtər/ | n. | 千升 | A kilolitre (kl) is a unit of volume equal to 1,000 liters.  It is a metric unit commonly used to measure large volumes of liquids, such as water or fuel. | The farmer harvested a kilolitre of apples this year.  = 今年农民收获了一千升苹果。(Note:  This example uses kilolitre metaphorically to represent volume of apples, not liquid.) The swimming pool holds 50 kilolitres of water. = 游泳池容纳50千升水。| megalitre, hectolitre | millilitre, litre |
