parthenogenetic:/ˌpɑːrθənoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|孤雌生殖的；单性生殖的|Relating to or characterized by parthenogenesis, a form of asexual reproduction in which an egg develops into a new individual without fertilization.|例句：Some species of lizards are parthenogenetic.  一些蜥蜴物种是孤雌生殖的。|例句：The parthenogenetic development of the eggs was observed under the microscope.  在显微镜下观察到卵的孤雌生殖发育。|近义词：asexual (in this context)|反义词：sexual
