punkahs:/ˈpʌŋkəz/| n.|蒲扇；风扇|A punkah is a large, usually manually operated, fan made of a frame covered with cloth or other material, used to create a breeze, traditionally in hot climates.|例句：The servants pulled the punkah to keep the air circulating in the room.  仆人们拉动蒲扇以保持房间空气流通。|近义词：fan, cooler|反义词：heater
