handhelds:/ˈhændˌheldz/| n.|手持设备，掌上电脑| Devices that are small enough to be held and operated in one hand.  They are commonly used for various purposes, such as gaming, communication, or data collection.|例句：Modern handhelds offer incredible processing power for their size. 现代手持设备在其尺寸上提供了令人难以置信的处理能力。|例句：She used a handheld GPS device to navigate the unfamiliar terrain. 她使用手持式GPS设备来导航陌生的地形。|近义词：portable devices, pocket devices|反义词：stationary devices, desktop computers|名词
