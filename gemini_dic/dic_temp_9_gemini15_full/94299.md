imperturbable: /ˌɪmpərˈtɜːrbəbl/ | adj. | imperturbable | 沉着冷静的；泰然自若的 |  Not easily perturbed or excited; calm.  It implies a remarkable ability to remain calm and composed even under pressure or stress. |


例句：

Despite the chaos around him, he remained imperturbable.  尽管周围一片混乱，他仍然保持冷静。

Her imperturbable demeanor during the crisis inspired confidence in her team.  她在危机期间镇定自若的态度，增强了她团队的信心。


近义词：calm, composed, serene, tranquil, unflappable, unruffled


反义词：agitated, flustered, nervous, perturbed, ruffled, upset


词性: 形容词
