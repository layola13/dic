shareowner: /ˈʃɛərˌoʊnər/ | n. | 股东 | A person who owns shares in a company.  Shareowners have a stake in the company's success or failure and may receive dividends based on the company's profits. |  The shareowners voted to approve the merger.  股东投票批准了合并。| stockholder, shareholder | nonshareholder | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a shareowner of Apple. = 他是苹果公司的股东。
She owns shares in several companies. = 她拥有几家公司的股份。

一般过去时:
He was a major shareowner in the firm. = 他曾是该公司的大股东。
They became shareowners last year. = 他们去年成为股东。

现在完成时:
She has been a shareowner for five years. = 她已经当了五年股东了。
They have acquired more shares and increased their shareholding. = 他们收购了更多股份，增加了持股量。

过去完成时:
He had been a shareowner before the company went public. = 在公司上市之前，他就是股东了。
They had already sold their shares before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经卖掉了他们的股份。

将来时:
He will be a shareowner once he buys the shares. = 一旦他购买了股份，他将成为股东。
They will become major shareowners if the deal goes through. = 如果交易达成，他们将成为主要股东。

虚拟语气:
If I were a shareowner, I would vote against the proposal. = 如果我是股东，我会投票反对这项提案。
I wish I were a shareowner in that successful company. = 我真希望我是那家成功公司的股东。

被动语态:
The company's shareowners were informed of the decision. = 公司的股东们被告知了这个决定。
New shareowners are being sought to fund the expansion. = 正在寻找新的股东来为扩张提供资金。

疑问句:
Are you a shareowner in this company? = 你是这家公司的股东吗？
How many shareowners does the company have? = 这家公司有多少股东？

否定句:
He is not a shareowner of that company. = 他不是那家公司的股东。
They were not shareowners at the time of the acquisition. = 他们在那次收购时还不是股东。
