gravidated:/ˈɡrævɪdeɪtɪd/| v.|趋向，倾向，被吸引|To move toward or be drawn to something; to tend to become.|The discussion gravitated towards the topic of climate change.  讨论逐渐转向气候变化的话题。|The project gravitated towards a more practical approach.  项目逐渐转向更实际的方法。|tend, incline, drift|digress, stray


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He gravitates towards classical music. 他倾向于古典音乐。
She gravitates toward people who are optimistic. 她倾向于乐观的人。


一般过去时:
The conversation gravitated towards politics.  谈话转向了政治。
The company gravitated toward a more sustainable business model. 公司转向更可持续的商业模式。


现在完成时:
The research has gravitated towards a new hypothesis.  研究已经转向了一个新的假设。
Public opinion has gravitated towards supporting the new policy.  公众舆论已经转向支持这项新政策。


过去完成时:
The team had gravitated towards a single solution before the deadline.  在截止日期之前，团队已经倾向于一个解决方案。
The artist had gravitated towards abstract art in his later years.  这位艺术家在晚年转向了抽象艺术。


将来时:
The focus will gravitate towards improving efficiency.  重点将转向提高效率。
I think the trend will gravitate towards using renewable energy. 我认为趋势将转向使用可再生能源。


虚拟语气:
If he were honest, he would not gravitate toward lies. 如果他诚实，他就不会倾向于说谎。
I wish she would gravitate toward a healthier lifestyle.  我希望她能转向更健康的生活方式。


被动语态:
The conversation was gravitated towards a more serious tone. 谈话转向了更严肃的语气。(This is less common and often sounds unnatural.  A more natural phrasing would be used.)
His attention was gravitated to the loud noise. 他的注意力被大声响吸引住了。(This is less common and often sounds unnatural.  A more natural phrasing would be used.)


疑问句:
Why does he gravitate toward such negative people? 他为什么总是被这样消极的人吸引？
Where did the conversation gravitate? 谈话转向哪里了？


否定句:
He did not gravitate toward the challenges. 他没有迎难而上。
She didn't gravitate toward conflict. 她不喜欢冲突。


PHRASE->
gravitate towards (something): 趋向于...
gravitate to (someone/something): 被...吸引
gravitate around (a topic): 围绕...展开
gravitate in (a direction): 朝...方向发展
gravitate between (two options): 在两者之间摇摆不定
