outings:/ˈaʊtɪŋz/| n. |郊游；外出；远足|An outing is a short trip or excursion, usually for pleasure.  It often involves a group of people going to a particular place for a specific activity. |例句：We had a lovely outing to the beach yesterday.  我们昨天去海滩玩得很开心。

例句：The school organized an outing to the zoo for the children. 学校为孩子们组织了一次去动物园的郊游。


近义词：excursion, trip, excursion, expedition

反义词：staying in, confinement
