salade:/səˈlɑːd/|/ˈsæləd/| n. |沙拉|A cold dish of various mixtures of raw or cooked vegetables, usually served with a dressing.|例句：I made a delicious salad with fresh tomatoes and cucumbers. 我用新鲜的番茄和黄瓜做了一份美味的沙拉。|例句：The chef prepared a vibrant salad with mixed greens and grilled chicken.  厨师准备了一份色彩鲜艳的沙拉，里面有各种绿叶蔬菜和烤鸡肉。|近义词：salad bowl, side dish|反义词：main course
