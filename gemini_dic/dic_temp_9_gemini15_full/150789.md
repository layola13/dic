paradiso:/ˌpærəˈdaɪsoʊ/| n. |天堂，乐园|Paradise is often depicted as a place of perfect happiness and beauty.  天堂通常被描绘成一个充满完美幸福和美丽的地方。|近义词：heaven, Eden, utopia |反义词：hell, inferno

Paradise is a concept found in many cultures. = 天堂是一个在许多文化中都能找到的概念。
