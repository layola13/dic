re-loses:/ˌriːˈluːzɪz/| v.|重新失去；再次丢失|To lose something again after having previously lost it and perhaps recovered it.|The company re-loses market share after a brief recovery.  公司在短暂复苏后再次失去市场份额。|He re-loses his keys just after finding them. 他刚找到钥匙又丢了。| regain, recover | gain, find | v.
