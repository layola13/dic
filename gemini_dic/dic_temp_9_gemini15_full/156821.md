piggies:/ˈpɪɡiz/| n.|小猪；脚趾|The word "piggies" is the plural of "piggy," a diminutive of "pig." It refers to young pigs or, informally, to someone's toes.  |例句：The piggies were playing in the mud. 小猪们在泥里玩耍。 The children were wiggling their piggies. 孩子们晃动着他们的脚趾。|近义词：little pigs, toes (informal)|反义词:N/A
