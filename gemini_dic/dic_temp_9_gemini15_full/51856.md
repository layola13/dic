dextrin:/ˈdɛkstrɪn/| n.|糊精，麦芽糖精|A group of low-molecular-weight carbohydrates produced by the hydrolysis of starch. Dextrins are used as adhesives, thickeners, and in food processing.|例句：The enzyme amylase breaks down starch into dextrins. 淀粉酶将淀粉分解成糊精。|例句：Dextrins are used in the production of paper and textiles. 糊精用于造纸和纺织品的生产。|近义词：starch, gum|反义词：None
