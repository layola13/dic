purest: /ˈpjʊərɪst/ | adj. | 最纯的，最纯粹的 |  Most free from any admixture; unmixed; completely clear.  It emphasizes the highest degree of purity. | 例句：The purest form of gold is 24 karat. = 最纯的黄金是24K金。

The purest water comes from mountain springs. = 最纯净的水来自山泉。


近义词：cleanest, most unadulterated, most pristine | 反义词：impurest, dirtiest, most contaminated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water is purest in the mountains. = 山里的水最纯净。
This diamond is the purest I've ever seen. = 这是我见过的最纯净的钻石。


一般过去时:
The air was purest before the industrial revolution. = 工业革命前空气最纯净。
His intentions were purest. = 他的意图最纯洁。


现在完成时:
The scientist has found the purest sample yet. = 科学家已经找到了迄今为止最纯净的样本。
We have never tasted purer honey. = 我们从未尝过更纯正的蜂蜜。


过去完成时:
The river had been purest before the factory started polluting it. = 在工厂开始污染河流之前，河水最纯净。
The air had been purest before the wildfire. = 野火之前，空气最纯净。


将来时:
The technology will produce the purest materials ever. = 这项技术将生产出有史以来最纯净的材料。
They hope the air will be purest after the cleanup. = 他们希望清理之后空气会更纯净。


虚拟语气:
If it were purest gold, it would be worth a fortune. = 如果它是纯金，将价值不菲。
If the water were purest, we wouldn't need filters. = 如果水最纯净，我们就无需过滤器。


被动语态:
The purest samples are selected for analysis. = 最纯净的样品被选中进行分析。
The purest ingredients are used in this product. = 该产品使用最纯净的成分。


疑问句:
Is this the purest form of the substance? = 这是该物质最纯净的形式吗？
Which sample is the purest? = 哪一个样本最纯净？


否定句:
The water isn't purest in this area. = 这个地区的水不纯净。
The sample isn't the purest one available. = 这个样本并非最纯净的。


PHRASE->
purest form: 最纯净的形式
purest intentions: 最纯洁的意图
purest water: 最纯净的水
purest love: 最纯粹的爱
purest heart: 最纯洁的心


词源:
源自古英语 *hūre,  意思是“纯净的，未受污染的”。  后缀“-est”表示最高级。  其演变与古英语的“pure”保持了一致的语义发展。
