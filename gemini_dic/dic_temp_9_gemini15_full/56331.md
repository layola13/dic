dogmatist:/ˈdɒɡmətɪst/ | n. | 教条主义者 | A person who is uncompromising and rigid in their adherence to doctrines or beliefs.  They often refuse to consider alternative viewpoints. | He's a religious dogmatist who refuses to engage in any meaningful interfaith dialogue. 他是一个宗教教条主义者，拒绝参与任何有意义的宗教间对话。 | 近义词：bigot, doctrinaire, traditionalist | 反义词：liberal, open-minded, skeptic
