revengement:/rɪˈvendʒmənt/ | n. |报复；复仇 | The act of taking revenge; retribution.  It is an archaic and less common term than "revenge."  |例句：He sought revengement for the wrongs done to him. 他试图报复对他所造成的伤害。

例句：The revengement he exacted was swift and brutal. 他实施的报复迅速而残酷。


近义词：revenge, retribution, vengeance | 反义词：forgiveness, pardon
