mottoed:/ˈmɒtəʊd/| adj.|带有格言的；饰有格言的|Having a motto; adorned with a motto.|The book was mottoed with a quote from Shakespeare. 这本书的扉页上印着莎士比亚的名言。|近义词：inscribed,  emblazoned|反义词：unmarked, plain
