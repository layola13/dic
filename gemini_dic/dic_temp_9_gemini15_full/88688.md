/ˈhɒbə(r)/|/ˈhɑːbər/| n. |业余爱好者；从事某项业余爱好的人|A hobber is someone who engages in a hobby, often with enthusiasm and skill.  They dedicate time and effort to their chosen pursuit, whether it's collecting stamps, knitting, or woodworking.|He's a keen hobber, spending his weekends restoring antique furniture. 他是一位热衷于业余爱好的爱好者，周末都用来修复古董家具。|enthusiast, amateur, devotee|professional, expert|名词
