meowing:/ˈmiːoʊɪŋ/| n. |猫叫声；喵喵叫|The sound a cat makes.|例句：The meowing of the kitten woke me up. 小猫的喵喵叫声把我吵醒了。|近义词：purring, caterwauling |反义词:silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is meowing loudly. = 猫大声地叫着。
He meows every time he wants food. = 他每次想要食物的时候都会喵喵叫。


一般过去时:
The cat meowed all night. = 猫叫了一整夜。
She meowed softly when I petted her. = 当我抚摸她时，她轻轻地喵喵叫。


现在完成时:
The cat has been meowing for hours. = 猫已经叫了几个小时了。
It has meowed incessantly since I got home. = 自从我回家后，它就一直不停地喵喵叫。


过去完成时:
The cat had meowed before I even opened the door. = 我还没开门，猫就已经叫了。
They had meowed until they were hoarse. = 它们喵喵叫到声音嘶哑。


将来时:
The cat will meow when it's hungry. = 猫饿了就会喵喵叫。
I think the cat will meow all night again. = 我想猫今晚又会叫一整夜。


虚拟语气:
If the cat meowed louder, I would hear it. = 如果猫叫得更大声，我就能听到。
I wish the cat wouldn't meow so much at night. = 我希望猫晚上不要叫那么多。


被动语态:
(This is less common for "meowing" as it's a sound, not an action performed on something.)  A similar sentence could be: The sound of meowing was heard throughout the house. = 整个房子都听到了猫叫声。
The loud meowing was finally silenced. = 吵闹的猫叫声最终安静下来了。


疑问句:
Is the cat meowing? = 猫在叫吗？
Why is the cat meowing so much? = 猫为什么叫这么多？


否定句:
The cat isn't meowing right now. = 猫现在没叫。
The cat didn't meow when I called its name. = 我叫它的名字时，它没有叫。


PHRASE->
a continuous meowing = 持续不断的猫叫声
a soft meowing = 轻柔的猫叫声
a loud meowing = 响亮的猫叫声
a high-pitched meowing = 高音调的猫叫声
a pitiful meowing = 可怜的猫叫声
