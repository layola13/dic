iguana: /ɪˈɡwɑːnə/ | n. |鬣蜥| A large tropical American lizard with a crest of scales along its back.  Iguanas are herbivores and are known for their docile nature. They are popular pets, but require specific care. |Example: The iguana basking in the sun is a vibrant green.  这条在阳光下晒太阳的鬣蜥是鲜艳的绿色。  |近义词: lizard (更广义)|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑体型小的爬行动物作为对比)|

词源:
The word "iguana" comes from the Taíno word *iwana*, referring to the lizard.  The Taíno were the indigenous people of the Caribbean islands.
