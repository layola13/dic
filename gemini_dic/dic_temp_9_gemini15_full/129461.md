non-adventurous: /ˌnɒnædˈvɛntʃərəs/| adj.|不爱冒险的，胆小的|Not inclined to take risks or participate in adventurous activities.  Lacking excitement or daring.| He led a non-adventurous life, content with his routine job and quiet evenings at home. 他过着不爱冒险的生活，对日常工作和安静的夜晚感到满足。|近义词：cautious, timid, unadventurous, risk-averse|反义词：adventurous, daring, bold, intrepid
