lakelet:/ˈleɪklɪt/| n. |小湖，湖泊|A lakelet is a small lake.  It is often used to describe a body of water that is smaller than a typical lake but larger than a pond. |例句：A small lakelet nestled between the hills.  山丘之间坐落着一个小型湖泊。

例句：We spent the afternoon swimming in the secluded lakelet. 我们在僻静的小湖里度过了下午的游泳时光。


近义词：small lake, pond, pool


反义词：ocean, sea


词性：名词
