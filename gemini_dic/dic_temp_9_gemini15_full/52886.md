It can also describe something extremely small. |例句：He called her "Liz," a diminutive of Elizabeth. 他叫她“Liz”，这是伊丽莎白的昵称。  The toy car was diminutive in size. 这辆玩具车非常小。|近义词：tiny, small, miniature, little, pet name|反义词：huge, enormous, gigantic, immense
