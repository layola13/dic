connotation:/ˌkɒnəˈteɪʃən/| /ˌkɑːnəˈteɪʃən/ | n. |含义；内涵；联想意义 |The word "home" has warm connotations for most people.  这个词“家”对大多数人来说有着温暖的含义。|近义词：implication, suggestion, implication |反义词：denotation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The word "home" has positive connotations. = “家”这个词有积极的含义。
This image connotes feelings of peace. = 这幅图像暗示着平静的感觉。


一般过去时:
The painting connoted a sense of loss. = 这幅画暗示了一种失落感。
That phrase connoted a hidden meaning to me. = 那句话对我来说暗示了一个隐藏的含义。


现在完成时:
His actions have connoted a lack of respect. = 他的行为暗示了缺乏尊重。
The symbol has connoted power for centuries. = 这个符号几个世纪以来一直象征着权力。


过去完成时:
The old house had connoted a sense of mystery. = 那座老房子曾暗示着一种神秘感。
By then, the phrase had connoted a completely different meaning. = 到那时，这句话已经暗示了完全不同的含义。


将来时:
This new technology will connote efficiency and innovation. = 这项新技术将暗示效率和创新。
The artist hopes his work will connote a sense of hope. = 艺术家希望他的作品能暗示一种希望感。


虚拟语气:
If the word connoted violence, I wouldn't use it. = 如果这个词暗示暴力，我不会使用它。
I wish the title connoted a more exciting story. = 我希望标题能暗示一个更精彩的故事。


被动语态:
The painting is connoted as a masterpiece. = 这幅画被认为是杰作（这句略有不自然，主动语态更常用）。
The symbol was connoted with religious significance. = 这个符号与宗教意义相关联。


疑问句:
What does this symbol connote? = 这个符号暗示着什么？
What connotations does this phrase have? = 这个短语有什么含义？


否定句:
The word doesn't connote anything negative. = 这个词没有任何负面含义。
The image didn't connote any sense of danger. = 这幅图像没有暗示任何危险。
