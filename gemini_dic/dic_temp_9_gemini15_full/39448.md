connectives:/kəˈnɛktɪvz/ | n. | 连接词，关联词 | Words or phrases that connect clauses, sentences, or paragraphs. They show the relationship between ideas and help create a smooth flow of text. Examples include conjunctions (and, but, or), adverbs (however, therefore, moreover), and prepositions (in, on, at). | The author used effective connectives to link the different sections of the essay. |近义词: conjunctions, linking words, transitional words |反义词: (no direct antonyms, but words that create disjointed writing could be considered opposite in function) |名词
