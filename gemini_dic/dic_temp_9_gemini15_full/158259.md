play-it-straight:/pleɪ ɪt streɪt/| phrasal verb |  直率地；坦诚地；不耍花招 | To behave in an honest and straightforward manner; to be sincere and not try to deceive or mislead.  |例句：He decided to play it straight with the police and tell them everything. 他决定对警察坦白一切。
例句：She played it straight during the interrogation, refusing to lie. 她在审讯中直言不讳，拒绝撒谎。|近义词：be honest, be frank, be truthful, be upfront |反义词：be deceitful, be dishonest, be deceptive, be evasive
