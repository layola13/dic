protection:/prəˈtɛkʃən/ | n. | 保护，防护 | The act of protecting or the state of being protected; something that protects.  It can refer to shielding from harm, danger, or loss. | Example: The government provides protection for its citizens. 政府为其公民提供保护。

The company's new security system offers enhanced protection against cyberattacks.  这家公司新的安全系统提供了增强的网络攻击防护。 | safeguard, defense, security, shielding | danger, harm, vulnerability, risk | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government provides protection for its citizens. = 政府为其公民提供保护。
This insurance policy offers excellent protection against financial loss. = 这份保险单提供了极好的经济损失保障。


一般过去时:
The police offered protection to the witness. = 警方为证人提供了保护。
He sought protection from the storm. = 他寻求躲避暴风雨的保护。


现在完成时:
They have received protection from the authorities. = 他们已经收到了当局的保护。
The company has invested heavily in data protection. = 公司已大力投资数据保护。


过去完成时:
She had sought protection from her abusive husband. = 她曾寻求摆脱虐待丈夫的保护。
The castle had offered protection to the villagers for centuries. = 这座城堡几个世纪以来一直为村民提供保护。


将来时:
The new law will offer better protection to consumers. = 新法律将为消费者提供更好的保护。
We will need extra protection against the cold weather. = 我们需要额外的防寒保护。


虚拟语气:
If we had better protection, we wouldn't have suffered such a loss. = 如果我们有更好的保护措施，我们就不会遭受这么大的损失。
I wish I had more protection against online scams. = 我希望我有更多防范网络诈骗的保护措施。


被动语态:
The city is well protected by the military. = 该城市受到军队的良好保护。
The environment needs to be protected from pollution. = 环境需要免受污染的保护。


疑问句:
What kind of protection do we need? = 我们需要什么样的保护？
Does this product offer any protection against water damage? = 此产品是否提供任何防范水损坏的保护？


否定句:
The building offered no protection from the earthquake. = 这座建筑物没有提供任何防震保护。
They received little protection from the authorities. = 他们几乎没有得到当局的保护。
