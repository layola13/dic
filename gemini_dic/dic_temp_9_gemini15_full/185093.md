retie:/riˈtaɪ/| vt.|重新系，再绑|To tie again or anew.|例句：He had to retie his shoelaces. 他不得不重新系鞋带。
例句：The package needed to be retied securely. 包裹需要被牢固地重新捆绑。|近义词：rebind, refasten|反义词：untie, loosen|动词
