conference:/ˈkɒnfərəns/ /ˈkɑːnfərəns/| n. |会议，研讨会|A formal meeting of people with a shared interest, typically for discussion and debate.|The conference on climate change was attended by delegates from many countries.  气候变化会议有来自许多国家的代表参加。|meeting, convention, seminar|dispute, disagreement|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The conference begins tomorrow. = 会议明天开始。
We attend many conferences each year. = 我们每年参加很多会议。

一般过去时:
The conference ended yesterday. = 会议昨天结束了。
They held a successful conference last week. = 他们上周举办了一次成功的会议。

现在完成时:
The organizers have already planned the conference. = 组织者已经计划好了会议。
We have attended several conferences on this topic. = 我们已经参加过几次关于这个主题的会议。

过去完成时:
The preparations for the conference had been completed before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，会议的准备工作已经完成了。
They had already discussed the agenda before the conference started. = 会议开始之前，他们已经讨论了议程。


将来时:
The conference will be held in London next year. = 会议将于明年在伦敦举行。
We will participate in the international conference. = 我们将参加国际会议。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would attend the conference. = 如果我有更多时间，我会参加会议。
I wish I could attend the conference next week. = 我希望我下周能参加会议。


被动语态:
The conference was organized by the university. = 会议是由大学组织的。
The results of the conference will be published soon. = 会议的结果将很快公布。


疑问句:
When is the conference? = 会议是什么时候？
Where is the conference taking place? = 会议在哪里举行？


否定句:
