nonmunicipally: /ˌnɒnˌmjuːnɪsɪpəlɪ/ | adv. | 非市政地；非市辖地 | Not under the administration or jurisdiction of a municipality.  This adverb describes something that is not related to or controlled by a municipal government. | Example:  The land was developed nonmunicipally, meaning it wasn't subject to city zoning regulations.  这块土地是非市政开发的，这意味着它不受城市分区条例的约束。| 近义词: extramunicipally, privately | 反义词: municipally


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like a verb.  It doesn't have tenses.  Its usage is primarily in describing the manner in which something is done or exists.

Example 1: The park was maintained nonmunicipally by a private conservation group. 公园由一个私人保护组织而非市政部门维护。
Example 2:  The water supply was handled nonmunicipally, leading to inconsistencies in service. 供水是非市政部门管理的，导致服务不一致。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "nonmunicipally". Its usage is quite specific and limited.
