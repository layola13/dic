cormous:/ˈkɔːrməs/| adj.|块茎状的，球茎状的|Having a corm.  Relating to or resembling a corm. A corm is a short, thickened underground stem that serves as a storage organ for food and water, enabling the plant to survive unfavorable conditions and to produce new growth.|The plant has a cormous base.|近义词：bulbous, tuberous|反义词：non-cormous
