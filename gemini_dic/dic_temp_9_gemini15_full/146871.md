overinflations:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪʃənz/ | n. |过度通货膨胀 |The excessive increase in the general price level of goods and services in an economy over a period of time.  This is typically characterized by a rapid and unsustainable rise in prices, leading to a decline in the purchasing power of money. |例句：The country is suffering from severe overinflations, making it difficult for people to afford basic necessities.  这个国家正遭受严重的过度通货膨胀，使人们难以负担基本生活必需品。

例句：Years of overinflations have eroded the value of savings and pensions. 多年的过度通货膨胀已经侵蚀了储蓄和养老金的价值。


近义词：hyperinflation, runaway inflation |反义词：deflation, disinflation |词性：名词
