nontalker:/ˈnɑːntɔːkər/ | n. |沉默寡言的人，不爱说话的人 | A nontalker is a person who doesn't talk much or is reluctant to engage in conversation.  They may be shy, introverted, or simply prefer to listen rather than speak. | He's a bit of a nontalker; you have to draw him out to get him to speak. 他有点沉默寡言；你必须引导他才能让他说话。|近义词：quiet person, reserved person, shy person |反义词：talkative person, chatterbox
