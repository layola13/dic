eschews:/ɪˈskuːz/ | vt. | 避免；避开；戒绝 | To avoid or shun (something, typically something regarded as wrong or undesirable).  | He eschews violence and advocates peaceful solutions. 他避免暴力并提倡和平解决。 | 近义词：avoid, shun, abstain from, forgo | 反义词：embrace, adopt, welcome | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eschews all forms of social media. = 他避免使用任何形式的社交媒体。
She eschews rich foods in favor of a healthy diet. = 她避免食用油腻的食物，而选择健康饮食。

一般过去时:
They eschewed the traditional methods and tried a new approach. = 他们避免使用传统方法，尝试了一种新方法。
He eschewed the opportunity for promotion. = 他放弃了晋升的机会。


现在完成时:
She has eschewed meat for several years. = 她已经吃素好几年了。
He has eschewed alcohol since his health scare. = 自从健康受损后，他就戒酒了。


过去完成时:
Having eschewed all other options, they chose to surrender. = 在排除了所有其他选择后，他们选择了投降。
She had eschewed luxury her entire life. = 她一生都过着简朴的生活。


将来时:
They will eschew any form of political involvement. = 他们将避免任何形式的政治参与。
She will eschew expensive clothing in favor of practical wear. = 她将避免购买昂贵的衣服，而选择实用性强的衣服。


虚拟语气:
If he eschewed bad habits, he would be healthier. = 如果他不染上坏习惯，他会更健康。
I wish he would eschew such negativity. = 我希望他能避免如此消极的态度。


被动语态:
Expensive materials were eschewed in favor of cheaper alternatives. = 昂贵的材料被避免使用，而采用更便宜的替代品。
Traditional methods have been eschewed by many modern businesses. = 许多现代企业都避免使用传统方法。


疑问句:
Does he eschew all forms of gambling? = 他避免所有形式的赌博吗？
Why do they eschew modern technology? = 为什么他们避免使用现代科技？


否定句:
He doesn't eschew challenges; he embraces them. = 他并不回避挑战，而是欣然接受。
They didn't eschew the opportunity to invest. = 他们没有放弃投资的机会。
