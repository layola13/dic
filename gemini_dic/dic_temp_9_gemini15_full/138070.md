normalities:/nɔːrˈmælɪtiz/ | n. |正常状态；常规；常态 |The normalities of daily life were disrupted by the unexpected storm. 日常生活的常态被突如其来的暴风雨打乱了。|近义词：regularities, routines, usualness |反义词：abnormalities, irregularities, exceptions |名词

否定句:

词源:
normality源自于norm (规范，标准) + -ality (名词后缀)。  norm本身来自古法语norme，最终源自拉丁语 norma（规尺，模型）。
