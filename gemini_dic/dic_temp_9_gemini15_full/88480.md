一般现在时:
Historians study past events. = 历史学家研究过去发生的事件。
Historians often disagree on interpretations. = 历史学家对解释往往存在分歧。

一般过去时:
Historians researched the topic extensively. = 历史学家广泛研究了这个课题。
Historians presented their findings at the conference. = 历史学家在会议上展示了他们的研究成果。

现在完成时:
Historians have uncovered new evidence. = 历史学家发现了新的证据。
Historians have revised their understanding of the event. = 历史学家已经修正了他们对这一事件的理解。

过去完成时:
Historians had already published their work before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，历史学家们已经出版了他们的作品。
Historians had debated the issue for years before reaching a consensus. = 在达成共识之前，历史学家们已经争论这个问题多年了。

将来时:
Historians will continue to study this period. = 历史学家将继续研究这一时期。
Historians will likely discover more information in the future. = 历史学家很可能会在未来发现更多信息。

虚拟语气:
If historians had access to more primary sources, they would have a clearer picture. = 如果历史学家能够获得更多的一手资料，他们就会有更清晰的认识。
I wish historians paid more attention to marginalized voices. = 我希望历史学家们能够更多地关注边缘群体的声音。

被动语态:
The historical narrative is often shaped by historians' biases. = 历史叙事往往受到历史学家偏见的影响。
The event was misinterpreted by many historians in the past. = 过去许多历史学家误解了这一事件。


疑问句:
What do historians say about this period? = 历史学家对这段时期有什么说法？
How do historians interpret this evidence? = 历史学家如何解读这些证据？


否定句:
Historians do not always agree on interpretations. = 历史学家并不总是同意某种解释。
Historians did not have access to all the relevant documents. = 历史学家无法获得所有相关的文件。
