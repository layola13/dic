curer:/ˈkjʊərər/| n. |治疗者；治愈者；(食品)保鲜剂|A curer is a person or thing that cures something.  This can refer to a person who heals illnesses, or a substance used to preserve food. |例句：The skilled curer healed the injured soldier.  熟练的治疗者治愈了受伤的士兵。|例句：The fishmonger used a special curer to preserve the salmon. 鱼贩使用特殊的保鲜剂来保存鲑鱼。|近义词：healer, preserver, conservant|反义词：infector, corrupter
