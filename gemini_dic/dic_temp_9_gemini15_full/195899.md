sensei:/ˈsɛnseɪ/| n.|老师，师傅，指导者|A sensei is a teacher, instructor, or master, especially in the context of martial arts or Japanese traditional arts.  The term implies respect and deference to the teacher's expertise and experience.|例句：My sensei taught me the importance of discipline and perseverance. 我的老师教导我自律和毅力的重要性。  My karate sensei is a national champion. 我的空手道老师是全国冠军。|近义词：master, teacher, instructor, mentor|反义词：student, pupil
