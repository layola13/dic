pre-Norman:/ˌpriːˈnɔːrmən/| adj.|诺曼征服之前的|Relating to the period in British history before the Norman Conquest of 1066.  This era is characterized by Anglo-Saxon culture and language.  |例句：Pre-Norman England was a land of small kingdoms and strong regional identities. = 诺曼征服前的英格兰是一个由许多小王国组成的国家，区域认同感很强。

例句：Many pre-Norman place names in England have survived to the present day. = 许多诺曼征服前的英格兰地名至今仍保留着。


近义词：Anglo-Saxon, pre-Conquest


反义词：post-Norman, Norman


词性:形容词
