excerpts:/ˈɛksɜːrps/| n. |摘录；节选 |Selected portions from a book, article, or other text.  Excerpts are chosen parts that represent the whole. |例句：The article included excerpts from several interviews. 这篇文章包含了几次采访的节选。|例句：She read excerpts from her new novel to the audience. 她向听众朗读了她新小说的节选。|近义词： extracts, selections, passages |反义词：whole, entirety
