mechanising: /ˈmɛkənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 机械化；使机械化 |  The process of making something operate automatically using machines.  It involves replacing manual labor with machinery to increase efficiency and productivity. | 例句：The factory is mechanising its production line to improve output. 工厂正在将其生产线机械化以提高产量。

例句：They are mechanising the farming process to reduce labor costs. 他们正在使农业生产过程机械化以降低劳动力成本。


近义词：automating, robotizing | 反义词：manualizing, hand-crafting

词源:  Comes from "mechanism" (a system of parts working together), which itself derives from the Greek word "mēkhanē" (machine, contrivance). The "-ising" suffix indicates the process of making something.
