grandkid:/ˈɡrændˌtʃaɪld/| n. |孙子，孙女，外孙，外孙女|A grandchild is a child of one's son or daughter.  It is a term of endearment used to refer to one's grandchildren.|My grandkids are coming to visit this weekend.  我的孙子孙女们这周末要来拜访。|My grandkids are all grown up now. 我的孙子孙女们都长大了。|grandchild,  descendant|ancestor, parent
