nondeterminable: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈtɜː.mɪ.nə.bl/| adj. |不能确定的，无法确定的 | Unable to be determined or ascertained; incapable of being definitively established or known.  It implies a lack of sufficient information or evidence to reach a conclusion. |The exact cause of the accident remains nondeterminable. 事故的确切原因仍然无法确定。| determinable, ascertainable | determinable, definable |
