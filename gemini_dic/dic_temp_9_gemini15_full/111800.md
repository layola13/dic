log-off:/ˈlɒɡ ɒf/| /ˈlɑːɡ ɔːf/ | vt. |注销；登出；签退|To end a computer session; to disconnect from a network.|例句：Please log off before leaving your workstation. 请离开工作站前注销。

例句：I forgot to log off my computer last night. 昨晚我忘记注销电脑了。


近义词：sign out, disconnect, shut down (in some contexts)
反义词：log on, log in, connect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He logs off his computer every night. 他每天晚上都注销电脑。
She logs off after finishing her work. 她完成工作后就注销了。


一般过去时:
I logged off the system after saving my file. 我保存文件后注销了系统。
He logged off before the meeting started. 会议开始前他就注销了。


现在完成时:
I have logged off my account. 我已经注销了我的账户。
She has logged off several times today. 她今天已经注销了好几次了。


过去完成时:
I had logged off before the power went out. 断电之前我已经注销了。
He had logged off before the server crashed. 服务器崩溃之前他已经注销了。


将来时:
I will log off when I finish my work. 我完成工作后就会注销。
She will log off at 5 o'clock. 她五点钟会注销。


虚拟语气:
If I had remembered to log off, I wouldn't have lost my data. 如果我记得注销，我就不会丢失数据了。
I wish I had logged off before the storm hit. 我真希望暴风雨来临之前我已经注销了。


被动语态:
The computer was logged off automatically. 电脑自动注销了。
The system was logged off by the administrator. 系统由管理员注销了。


疑问句:
Have you logged off yet? 你注销了吗？
Did he log off before leaving? 他离开之前注销了吗？


否定句:
I didn't log off my computer. 我没有注销我的电脑。
She hasn't logged off yet. 她还没注销。
