pardners:/ˈpɑːrtnərz/ | n. |伙伴们，搭档们 | partners refers to a group of people who are associated together, often in a business or work context, but also in a more general sense of companionship.  It is a plural form of "partner". |例句：My pardners and I are going to start a new business.  我的伙伴们和我打算开一家新公司。

例句：Those pardners helped each other throughout the tough times.  那些伙伴们在艰难时期互相帮助。


近义词：companions, colleagues, associates, teammates

反义词：enemies, opponents, rivals


词性：名词
