grill:/ɡrɪl/| n. vt.|烤架；烤肉；严厉盘问|A grill is a device used for cooking food over hot coals or a flame, often with a grid or grate.  It can also refer to the act of cooking food on a grill, or a restaurant specializing in grilled food. As a verb, it means to cook food on a grill or to question someone intensely.|例句：We grilled some burgers on the barbecue. 我们在烧烤架上烤了一些汉堡。

例句：The police grilled the suspect for hours.  警方严厉盘问了嫌疑犯数小时。|近义词：broil, barbecue, interrogate|反义词：boil, stew
