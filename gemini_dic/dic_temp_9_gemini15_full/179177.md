redirecting:/ˌriːˈdaɪrektɪŋ/ | v. | 重定向，改道 | The act of changing the direction or course of something, especially data or a request.  It often involves sending something to a different location or destination. | Example: The website is redirecting me to a login page.  网站正在将我重定向到登录页面。  The traffic was redirected due to the accident.  由于事故，交通被改道了。|近义词：routing, diverting, channeling | 反义词：directing, leading straight to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The server is redirecting users to the new website. = 服务器正在将用户重定向到新网站。
They are redirecting all incoming calls to the main office. = 他们将所有来电都重定向到总机。

一般过去时:
The system redirected me to the error page. = 系统把我重定向到了错误页面。
They redirected the flow of water to prevent flooding. = 他们改道了水流以防止洪水。

现在完成时:
The website has redirected me several times. = 该网站已经把我重定向了好几次。
We have redirected all our efforts to this project. = 我们已经将所有精力都投入到这个项目中。

过去完成时:
The program had redirected the file before the error occurred. = 在错误发生之前，程序已经重定向该文件。
They had redirected the resources before the budget cuts. = 预算削减之前，他们已经重新分配了资源。

将来时:
The software will redirect you to the download page. = 该软件将把你重定向到下载页面。
We will redirect the traffic once the repairs are complete. = 修理完成后，我们将改道交通。

虚拟语气:
If the link worked, it would redirect me to the correct page. = 如果链接有效，它会把我重定向到正确的页面。
I wish the website would redirect me to the mobile version. = 我希望网站能把我重定向到移动版本。

被动语态:
Users are being redirected to a secure server. = 用户正在被重定向到一个安全的服务器。
The traffic was being redirected to alleviate congestion. = 交通正在被改道以缓解拥堵。

疑问句:
Is the system redirecting correctly? = 系统是否正在正确地重定向？
Will the email be redirected to my alternate address? = 电子邮件会被重定向到我的备用地址吗？

否定句:
The link didn't redirect me to the expected page. = 该链接没有把我重定向到预期的页面。
The website is not redirecting properly. = 网站没有正确地重定向。
