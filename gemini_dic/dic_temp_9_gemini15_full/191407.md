schemers:/ˈskimərz/| n.|策划者，阴谋家|People who make or carry out secret plans, especially plans to do something harmful or illegal.  They often devise intricate plots to achieve their goals, sometimes at the expense of others. |例句：The schemers plotted to overthrow the government. 那些阴谋家密谋推翻政府。

The schemers were finally caught and brought to justice.  那些阴谋家最终被抓住并绳之以法。|近义词：conspirators, plotters, intriguers|反义词：do-gooders, benefactors, altruists|名词
