omber:/ˈɒmbə(r)/| adj.|暗淡的，阴暗的；忧郁的|Dark or gloomy in color or appearance; suggesting melancholy or gloom.|The somber sky mirrored her mood.  阴沉的天空反映了她的心情。 The room was decorated in somber tones. 房间装饰着暗淡的色调。|近义词：dark, gloomy, dreary, muted|反义词：bright, cheerful, sunny, vibrant|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sky is somber today. = 今天的天空阴沉沉的。
Her expression remains somber. = 她的表情依然阴郁。

The music was somber and slow. = 音乐低沉而缓慢。

The future looks rather somber for the company. = 这家公司的未来看起来相当黯淡。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
