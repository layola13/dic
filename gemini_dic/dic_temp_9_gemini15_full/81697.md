greenhead:/ˈɡriːnhed/| n.|绿头苍蝇；（尤指）大蚊；脾气暴躁的人|A greenhead is a type of large fly, especially a horsefly, known for its aggressive biting behavior.  The term can also be used informally to refer to someone with a bad temper.|例句：The greenheads were swarming around the horses. 绿头苍蝇在马匹周围成群结队。

例句：He's a real greenhead; always getting into arguments. 他真是个脾气暴躁的人；总是吵架。|近义词：horsefly, gadfly, bully|反义词：gentle, mild-mannered|名词
