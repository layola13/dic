masula:/məˈsuːlə/| n.| 马苏拉船；小帆船 | A masula boat is a type of small, traditionally built sailing boat, particularly associated with the Coromandel Coast of India.  These boats are known for their distinctive design and their role in fishing and coastal trade. They are typically characterized by their sturdy construction, relatively shallow draft, and their ability to navigate shallow waters. | The masula boats were crucial for fishing and transporting goods along the coast. |近义词：fishing boat, small boat |反义词：large ship, ocean liner
