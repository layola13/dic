downshifts:/ˈdaʊnʃɪfts/| v.|减速换挡；降低档位；（经济）减速；降低；放慢速度 | To change a vehicle's gears to a lower gear, typically to slow down or increase power.  Also refers to a general slowing down or decrease in activity or intensity. |例句：The driver downshifted as he approached the curve.  司机在接近弯道时减速换挡了。

The economy is downshifting, leading to job losses. 经济正在减速，导致失业。|近义词：reduce speed, decelerate, slow down, shift down|反义词：upshift, accelerate, speed up
