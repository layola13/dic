latch-key:/ˈlætʃkiː/| n.|带锁的钥匙；（尤指）放学后独自回家孩子的钥匙|A latch key is a key used to open a door that has a latch lock.  It's often associated with children who are old enough to let themselves into their home after school without adult supervision.|例句：The child fumbled with her latch key, anxious to get inside.  这个孩子笨手笨脚地拿着她的钥匙，急着想进屋。

例句：Many latchkey kids learn to be self-reliant at a young age. 许多放学后独自回家的孩子很小就学会了自力更生。|近义词：key, house key|反义词：N/A|名词
