myelocoele:/ˌmaɪəlɵˈsiːl/| n. |脊髓膨出|A myelocoele is a type of neural tube defect in which the spinal cord does not close completely during fetal development, resulting in a sac-like protrusion of the spinal cord and meninges through an opening in the spine.  This can cause various neurological deficits depending on the severity and location of the defect. |Example: The baby was diagnosed with a myelocoele at the 20-week ultrasound.  这个婴儿在20周的超声波检查中被诊断出患有脊髓膨出。|近义词：myeloschisis (less common)|反义词：spina bifida occulta (a less severe type of neural tube defect)|名词
