mediation: /ˌmiːdiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 调解；仲裁；调停 | The process of resolving a dispute or conflict through the intervention of a neutral third party.  It involves facilitating communication and negotiation between disputing parties to help them reach a mutually acceptable agreement. | Example:  The mediation session helped the couple resolve their differences and save their marriage.  调解会议帮助这对夫妇解决了分歧，挽救了他们的婚姻。


Example: The labor dispute was resolved through successful mediation. 劳资纠纷通过成功的调解得以解决。

近义词: arbitration, conciliation, negotiation

反义词: litigation, confrontation
