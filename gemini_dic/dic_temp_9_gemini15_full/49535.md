demarcators:/dɪˈmɑːrkeɪtərz/| n.|界标，分界线，分隔符|Things that mark or indicate a boundary or limit.  They can be physical objects or abstract concepts used to separate or distinguish things.|Example: The river serves as a natural demarcator between the two countries. 这条河是两国之间的天然分界线。  The commas act as demarcators in this sentence.  这些逗号在这个句子中充当分隔符。|近义词：markers, dividers, separators, boundaries, limits|反义词：unifiers, connectors, integrators|名词
