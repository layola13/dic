motiva:/moʊˈtiːvə/ | n. | 动机；动力；激励因素 | a reason or reasons for acting or behaving in a particular way.  | Example:  The motiva for his actions remains unclear.  他的行为动机仍不明朗。|近义词：incentive, stimulus, drive | 反义词：disincentive, deterrent | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What motivates you to work so hard? = 什么激励你如此努力工作？
What motivates her artistic expression? = 什么激励她的艺术表达？


一般过去时:
What motivated him to quit his job? = 什么促使他辞职？
What motivated the change in policy? = 什么促使政策改变？


现在完成时:
What has motivated your recent success? = 什么促成了你最近的成功？
What has motivated the company's growth? = 什么促进了公司的发展？


过去完成时:
What had motivated her decision to travel abroad? = 什么促使她决定去国外旅行？
What had motivated the rebellion? = 什么引发了这场叛乱？


将来时:
What will motivate you to achieve your goals? = 什么将激励你实现你的目标？
What will motivate the team to improve performance? = 什么将激励团队改进业绩？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what motivated him, I could help him. = 如果我知道他的动机，我就能帮助他。
I wish I knew what motivated her sudden change of heart. = 我真希望知道是什么导致她突然改变主意。


被动语态:
The team was motivated by the prospect of a bonus. = 团队被奖金的预期所激励。
The decision was motivated by a desire for efficiency. = 这一决定是出于提高效率的愿望。


疑问句:
What is the motivation behind this project? = 这个项目的动机是什么？
What motivates their aggressive behavior? = 什么是他们具有攻击性行为的动机？


否定句:
He lacked the motivation to finish the task. = 他缺乏完成任务的动力。
She was unmotivated by the lack of recognition. = 缺乏认可使她缺乏动力。


PHRASE->
motivational speaker:  励志演讲者
motivational interviewing:  积极鼓励式访谈
lack of motivation:  缺乏动力
intrinsic motivation: 内在动机
extrinsic motivation: 外在动机
