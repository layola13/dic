regathers:/riːˈɡæðərz/| v.|重新聚集；重新收集；恢复；重获|To gather again; to collect again; to recover or regain something.|例句：The scattered sheep regathered in the field. 散落的羊群重新聚集在田野里。

例句：After the storm, she regathered her scattered belongings. 暴风雨过后，她重新收集了她散落的财物。

近义词：reassemble, reunite, recover, regain|反义词：disperse, scatter, lose
