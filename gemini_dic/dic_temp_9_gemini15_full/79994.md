go in for:/ɡoʊ ɪn fɔːr/| phrasal verb|参加；爱好；致力于；从事|To participate in a competition, activity, or pursuit.  It can also imply a preference or enthusiasm for something.|例句：He's going in for the marathon next month. 他下个月要参加马拉松比赛。

例句：She goes in for all kinds of extreme sports. 她喜欢各种极限运动。


近义词：participate in, engage in, take part in, pursue, be keen on|反义词：avoid, shun, abstain from|词性:phrasal verb
