non-allergenic:/ˌnɒnælˈdʒɛːnɪk/ | adj. | 不致敏的，不过敏的 | Not causing an allergic reaction.  This term describes substances or products that are unlikely to trigger an allergic response in most individuals.  | Example:  This detergent is non-allergenic and suitable for sensitive skin.  这款清洁剂不致敏，适合敏感肌肤使用。


Example:  The new baby clothes are made from non-allergenic materials. 这套婴儿新衣服是用不过敏的材料制成的。


近义词：hypoallergenic, allergy-free


反义词：allergenic
