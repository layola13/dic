overmannered:/ˌoʊvərˈmænərd/ | adj. | 过分讲究礼仪的；做作的 | excessively polite or formal in a way that seems unnatural or affected.  It suggests an artificiality and a lack of genuineness in one's manners. |  He was overmannered and stiff, making it difficult to relax in his presence. 他过于讲究礼仪，显得拘谨，让人很难在他面前放松。


The child's overmannered greeting felt forced and insincere.  孩子的过于做作的问候听起来很勉强，不真诚。|近义词：affected, artificial, pretentious, stilted |反义词：natural, informal, easygoing
