finiteness:/faɪˈnaɪtnəs/ | n. | 有限性，有限，有限范围 | The quality or state of being finite; having limits or boundaries.  It often refers to the concept that something has a definite end or limit, in contrast to infinity.  This concept applies to various fields, including mathematics, philosophy, and computer science. | Example: The finiteness of resources necessitates careful planning.  有限的资源需要仔细规划。The finiteness of life makes every moment precious. 生命的有限使每一刻都弥足珍贵。| 近义词：limitation, restriction, boundedness | 反义词：infinity, boundlessness, illimitability | 名词
