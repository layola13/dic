maunderer:/ˈmɔːndərər/| n. |漫无目的闲逛的人，唠唠叨叨的人 |A maunderer is someone who wanders aimlessly or talks ramblingly. They may be lost in thought or simply lacking direction.  |例句：The old man was a maunderer, often lost in his own thoughts and wandering the streets for hours.  这位老人是个漫无目的闲逛的人，经常沉思冥想，在街上游荡数小时。
例句：He was a maunderer, his conversation filled with irrelevant details and digressions. 他是个唠唠叨叨的人，他的谈话充满了无关紧要的细节和离题之词。|近义词：wanderer, rambler, rambler, loiterer, drifter|反义词：focused person, decisive person, purposeful person|名词
