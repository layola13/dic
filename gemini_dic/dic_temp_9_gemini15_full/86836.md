hematoxylin:/ˌhiːmætˈɒksɪlɪn/ | n. | 苏木精 | A natural dye extracted from the heartwood of the logwood tree, used as a stain in histology and cytology to color cell nuclei purple or blue. It's a basic dye that binds to acidic components in cells. | Example: Hematoxylin is commonly used in staining techniques to visualize cell structures.  苏木精通常用于染色技术中以观察细胞结构。 | 近义词:  None (as it's a specific stain) | 反义词: Eosin (a counterstain often used with hematoxylin) |
