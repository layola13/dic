cue-up:/kjuː ʌp/| vt. |预先准备好；准备好播放；预备；准备|To prepare something, especially a piece of equipment or a recording, for use or playback.|The DJ cued up the next song.  DJ 准备播放下一首歌。| The technician cued up the projector before the presentation. 技术人员在演示之前准备好了投影仪。|prepare, ready, set|unprepare, disable|动词
