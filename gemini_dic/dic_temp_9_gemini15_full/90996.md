hurts:/hɜːrts/ | v. | 疼痛；伤害；使痛苦 | To cause physical pain or emotional distress.  |例句：My head hurts. = 我的头疼。
The criticism really hurt his feelings. = 批评真的伤害了他的感情。|近义词：aches, pains, injures, wounds |反义词：heals, soothes, comforts


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My back hurts every morning. = 我每天早上背都疼。
This medicine hurts when you swallow it. = 这药吞下去的时候会痛。


一般过去时:
My knee hurt after the fall. = 我摔倒后膝盖疼。
His words hurt me deeply. = 他的话深深地刺痛了我。


现在完成时:
My foot has hurt all day. = 我的脚痛了一整天。
His betrayal has hurt me more than I can say. = 他的背叛伤我至深，我无法言表。


过去完成时:
My arm had hurt for weeks before I saw the doctor. = 在我看医生之前，我的胳膊已经疼了好几个星期了。
She had hurt her feelings long before he apologized. = 在他道歉很久以前，她就受伤了。


将来时:
I think my tooth will hurt tonight. = 我想我今晚牙会疼。
This decision will hurt many people. = 这个决定会伤害很多人。


虚拟语气:
If I touched it, it would hurt. = 如果我碰它，它会疼。
I wish it didn't hurt so much. = 我希望它不会这么痛。


被动语态:
His feelings were hurt by her words. = 他的感情被她的话伤害了。
His reputation was hurt by the scandal. = 他的名誉被丑闻损害了。


疑问句:
Does your head hurt? = 你头疼吗？
Does this cut hurt? = 这个伤口疼吗？


否定句:
My arm doesn't hurt anymore. = 我的胳膊不疼了。
It didn't hurt as much as I thought it would. = 它没有我想象的那么疼。


PHRASE->
hurt feelings = 受伤的感情
hurt someone's pride = 伤害某人的自尊
hurt oneself = 伤到自己
a hurt look = 受伤的表情
deeply hurt = 深受伤害
