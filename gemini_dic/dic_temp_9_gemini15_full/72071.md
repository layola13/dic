flitting:/ˈflɪtɪŋ/| adj.|飞逝的；短暂的；轻快的| fleeting or transient; light and quick in movement.  |例句：She had a flitting glimpse of the bird before it flew away. 她瞥见那只鸟一眼，它就飞走了。
例句：The flitting shadows danced across the wall.  飞逝的阴影在墙上跳跃。|近义词：transient, ephemeral, fleeting |反义词：permanent, lasting, enduring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The butterfly is flitting from flower to flower.  蝴蝶在花间飞舞。
His thoughts flit between work and family. 他的思绪在工作和家庭之间来回切换。


一般过去时:
A shadow flitted across the room. 一道影子掠过房间。
The image flitted through my mind. 那幅画面在我的脑海中一闪而过。


现在完成时:
The hummingbird has been flitting around the feeder all morning.  蜂鸟整个上午一直在喂食器周围飞来飞去。
Opportunities have flitted by, and now I regret it. 机会已经错过了，我现在后悔了。


过去完成时:
Before I knew it, the moment had flitted by.  不知不觉中，那一刻已经过去了。
The thought had flitted across her mind several times before she acted.  在她采取行动之前，这个想法在她脑海中出现过好几次。


将来时:
The birds will be flitting about in the garden tomorrow. 明天鸟儿会在花园里飞来飞去。
I suspect that the opportunity will flit by if I don't act soon.  我怀疑如果我不尽快行动，机会就会溜走。


虚拟语气:
If only those happy days hadn't flitted by so quickly!  要是那些快乐的日子没有这么快过去就好了！
I wish those fleeting moments hadn't flitted so quickly. 我希望那些短暂的时刻没有那么快过去。


被动语态:
The image was flitted across the screen.  图像在屏幕上快速闪过。
The thought was flitted away by other concerns.  这个想法被其他的担忧冲淡了。


疑问句:
Is that a bird flitting through the trees?  那是只鸟在树林间飞过吗？
Are your thoughts flitting all over the place? 你的思绪是不是漫无边际？


否定句:
The thought didn't flit across my mind.  这个想法没有在我的脑海中闪过。
The opportunity didn't flit away unnoticed. 机会并没有被忽视而溜走。
