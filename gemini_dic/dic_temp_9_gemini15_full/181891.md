remagnetizes:/ˌriːˈmæɡnɪtaɪzɪz/| vt.|重新磁化|To magnetize again; to restore the magnetic properties of (something).  It involves the process of realigning the magnetic domains within a material to enhance or restore its magnetic field.|例句：The technician remagnetizes the hard drive to recover lost data. 技术人员重新磁化硬盘以恢复丢失的数据。

The engineer remagnetizes the compass needle after it was demagnetized.工程师在指南针磁针消磁后对其重新磁化。|近义词：re-magnetize, magnetize anew|反义词：demagnetize
