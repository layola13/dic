sauroidichnite:/ˈsɔːrɔɪdɪknaɪt/| n. |蜥蜴足迹化石|A sauroidichnite is a fossilized footprint of a reptile, specifically one resembling a lizard or lizard-like creature.  The term combines elements referring to lizard-like features and the suffix "-ichnite," indicating a footprint.  It's primarily used in paleontology to describe specific types of reptilian trace fossils.|The paleontologist identified a series of sauroidichnites as belonging to a previously unknown species of small reptile.|近义词: reptilian footprint, reptile trackway|反义词:  mammalian footprint (depending on context)
