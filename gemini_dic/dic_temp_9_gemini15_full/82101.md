grocers:/ˈɡroʊsɚz/ | n. | 食品杂货商；食品店 | A grocer is a person who owns or works in a grocery store, selling food and household goods.  |  The local grocer always had the freshest produce. 当地杂货店的农产品总是最新鲜的。 The grocer weighed the potatoes for me. 杂货店老板为我称了土豆。 |  merchant, shopkeeper, retailer |  (无明确反义词，可考虑consumer) | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The grocer sells fresh bread daily. = 杂货店每天卖新鲜面包。
He stocks the shelves with groceries. = 他用杂货来补充货架。

一般过去时:
The grocer delivered groceries to our house last week. = 杂货店老板上周把杂货送到我们家。
She bought all her groceries at the farmer's market. = 她所有的杂货都在农贸市场买的。

现在完成时:
The grocer has increased prices recently. = 杂货店最近涨价了。
We have run out of milk; we need to go to the grocer. = 我们牛奶喝完了；我们需要去杂货店。

过去完成时:
I had already bought all the groceries before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前我已经买好了所有的杂货。
They had closed the grocer before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，杂货店已经关门了。


将来时:
The grocer will open a new branch next month. = 杂货店下个月将开设新分店。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
