scratchy: /ˈskræʧi/ | adj. | 刺耳的；沙哑的；粗糙的 | Having a rough or harsh surface or sound.  The sound is unpleasant because it's rough or jarring. | 例句：The wool sweater felt scratchy against my skin. 这件羊毛衫摸起来很粗糙。

The old record had a scratchy sound. 这张旧唱片声音沙沙的。


近义词：rough, harsh, grating, raspy, abrasive | 反义词：smooth, soft, silky


词源:  Derived from the verb "scratch," ultimately from Old English *scræccian, meaning "to scratch."  The adjective form developed to describe things that cause a scratching sensation or produce a scratching sound.
