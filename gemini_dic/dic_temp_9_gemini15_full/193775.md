self-defence:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈfɛns/| n.|自卫，自我防卫|The act of defending oneself from attack or harm.  It involves using reasonable force to protect oneself from immediate danger.|例句：He used self-defence to protect himself from the attacker. 他用自卫来保护自己免受袭击者攻击。|例句：Taking a self-defence class can empower you to protect yourself. 参加自卫课程可以增强你的自我保护能力。|近义词：self-protection, protection, defence|反义词：attack, aggression|名词
