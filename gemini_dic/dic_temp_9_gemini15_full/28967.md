carrying:/ˈkæriɪŋ/ | v. | 携带；搬运；支撑；具有；意味；表现 | Carrying heavy bags can be tiring. 搬运重物会很累。 The beam is carrying the weight of the roof. 梁支撑着屋顶的重量。 This song carries a message of hope. 这首歌传达着希望的信息。 | 近义词：transporting, bearing, conveying, supporting | 反义词：dropping, abandoning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He carries his backpack to school every day. 他每天背着书包去上学。
She carries herself with grace. 她举止优雅。


一般过去时:
I carried the groceries into the kitchen. 我把杂货搬进了厨房。
They carried the injured man to safety. 他们把受伤的人抬到了安全的地方。


现在完成时:
She has carried out all her responsibilities. 她已经完成了她所有的责任。
We have been carrying this burden for too long. 我们背负这个重担太久了。


过去完成时:
He had carried the heavy box before he felt the pain. 他搬动那个沉重的箱子后才感到疼痛。
They had carried out many experiments before finding a solution. 他们在找到解决方案之前已经进行了许多实验。


将来时:
I will carry your luggage for you. 我会帮你提行李。
They will carry out the plan next week. 他们下周将执行计划。


虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I could carry that heavy suitcase. 如果我更强壮，我就能搬动那个沉重的行李箱。
I wish I could carry the weight of the world. 我希望我能承担世界的重量。


被动语态:
The injured player was carried off the field. 受伤的球员被抬下了球场。
The message was carried by the wind.  信息被风吹散了。


疑问句:
Can you carry this for me? 你能帮我拿这个吗？
What are you carrying in that bag? 你包里装着什么？


否定句:
I couldn't carry the box; it was too heavy. 我搬不动那个箱子，它太重了。
He didn't carry out the instructions. 他没有执行指示。
