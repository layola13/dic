devising:/dɪˈvaɪzɪŋ/| v. |设计；发明；策划 |The act of planning or inventing something, typically a complex scheme or system.  |例句：They are devising a new strategy to increase sales.  他们正在设计一个新的策略来增加销售额。
例句：The engineers are devising a new system for recycling waste. 工程师们正在设计一个新的废物回收系统。|近义词：planning, inventing, creating, conceiving, formulating|反义词：destroying, dismantling, ruining|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are devising a new marketing campaign. = 他们正在策划一个新的营销活动。
We devise new ways to solve problems every day. = 我们每天都在想方设法解决问题。

一般过去时:
She devised a clever solution to the puzzle. = 她想出了一个巧妙的解谜方法。
He devised a plan to escape from prison. = 他策划了一个越狱计划。

现在完成时:
They have devised a new method for producing energy. = 他们已经设计出一种新的能源生产方法。
Scientists have devised a vaccine against the virus. = 科学家们已经研制出一种针对该病毒的疫苗。

过去完成时:
They had devised a plan, but it failed. = 他们曾制定了一个计划，但失败了。
She had devised a strategy to win the election before the campaign started. = 在竞选开始之前，她就已经制定了一套赢得选举的策略。

将来时:
We will devise a better system next year. = 我们明年将设计一个更好的系统。
The company will devise a new product to compete in the market. = 公司将设计一款新产品来参与市场竞争。

虚拟语气:
If I were an engineer, I would devise a better car. = 如果我是一位工程师，我会设计一辆更好的汽车。
I wish I had devised a better plan. = 我真希望我制定了一个更好的计划。

被动语态:
A new system was devised to improve efficiency. = 一个新的系统被设计出来以提高效率。
The plan was devised by a team of experts. = 该计划是由一个专家团队制定的。

疑问句:
What are they devising? = 他们在策划什么？
How did you devise this solution? = 你是怎么想出这个解决方法的？

否定句:
They did not devise a successful strategy. = 他们没有制定出一个成功的策略。
I couldn't devise a way to solve the problem. = 我想不出解决这个问题的方法。


PHRASE->
devise a plan = 制定计划
devise a method = 制定方法
devise a strategy = 制定策略
devise a solution = 想出解决方法
devise a system = 设计一个系统
