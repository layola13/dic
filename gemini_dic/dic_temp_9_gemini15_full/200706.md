sleepwalker: /ˈsliːpwɔːkər/ | n. |梦游者，梦游症患者 | A person who walks while asleep.  |Example:  The sleepwalker wandered down the hallway in his pajamas.  梦游者穿着睡衣在走廊里闲逛。

The sleepwalker stumbled and fell down the stairs. 这个梦游者绊倒了，从楼梯上摔了下来。|近义词：somnambulist |反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "sleepwalker" is a compound word combining "sleep" and "walker."  "Sleep" comes from Old English *slēp, and "walker" derives from Old English *walc-ere, meaning someone who walks.  Therefore, the word literally means "one who walks while sleeping".
