relaunches:/ˌriːˈlɔːntʃɪz/ | v. |重新启动；再次推出 | To launch (a product, project, etc.) again after a period of inactivity or following a failure.  |例句：The company relaunched its flagship product with a new marketing campaign. 公司以新的营销活动重新推出了其旗舰产品。


例句：After a period of low sales, the company decided to relaunch its product with improved features. 经过一段时间的低销量后，公司决定改进产品功能后重新推出。


近义词：restart, relaunch, revive, resuscitate


反义词：terminate, discontinue, abandon


词性：动词
