recyclable:/ˌriˈsaɪkləbl/| adj.|可回收的，可再利用的|Able to be recycled; capable of being processed to recover materials for reuse.|Many plastic bottles are recyclable. 许多塑料瓶都是可回收的。  Glass jars are also recyclable. 玻璃罐子也是可回收的。|reusable,  reclaimable|non-recyclable, disposable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This plastic is recyclable. = 这种塑料是可回收的。
That paper is recyclable. = 那张纸是可回收的。

一般过去时:
The bottle was recyclable. = 这个瓶子是可回收的。(强调过去的状态)
Those newspapers were recyclable. = 那些报纸是可回收的。(强调过去的状态)

现在完成时:
Many materials have become recyclable. = 许多材料已经可以回收利用了。
The city has made many items recyclable. = 这个城市已经让许多物品可以回收利用了。

过去完成时:
Before the new plant opened, this material hadn't been recyclable. = 在新工厂开业之前，这种材料不可回收。
We realized that the packaging hadn't been recyclable. = 我们意识到包装之前不可回收。

将来时:
More products will be recyclable in the future. = 未来将会有更多产品可以回收利用。
This type of plastic will be recyclable soon. = 这种类型的塑料很快就可以回收利用了。

虚拟语气:
If this were recyclable, I would put it in the blue bin. = 如果这是可回收的，我会把它扔进蓝色垃圾桶。
I wish all packaging were recyclable. = 我希望所有包装都是可回收的。

被动语态:
Recyclable materials are collected weekly. = 可回收材料每周收集一次。
This item can be recycled, so it is considered recyclable. = 此物品可以回收，因此它被认为是可回收的。

疑问句:
Is this container recyclable? = 这个容器可回收吗？
Are all plastics recyclable? = 所有塑料都可回收吗？

否定句:
This type of plastic isn't recyclable. = 这种类型的塑料不可回收。
That food container is not recyclable. = 那个食物容器不可回收。
