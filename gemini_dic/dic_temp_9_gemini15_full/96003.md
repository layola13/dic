incurring:/ɪnˈkɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 招致；承担；引起 |  The act of bringing upon oneself something undesirable, usually a cost, penalty, or obligation.  | 例句：The company is incurring significant losses this quarter.  这家公司本季度正在遭受重大损失。 | 例句：By ignoring the problem, they are incurring further risks. 通过忽视这个问题，他们正在承担更大的风险。| 近义词：suffering, sustaining, experiencing | 反义词：avoiding, escaping, preventing | 动词
