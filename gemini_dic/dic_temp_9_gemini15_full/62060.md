encash: /ɪnˈkæʃ/ | vt. |兑现；变现| To convert something into cash.  To exchange a check, bond, etc., for cash. |例句：I need to encash this check. 我需要兑现这张支票。

例句：He encashed his savings bonds to pay for his daughter's education. 他兑现了他的储蓄债券来支付女儿的教育费用。


近义词：cash, redeem, realize


反义词：invest, deposit


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encashes his paycheck every Friday. = 他每个星期五都兑现他的工资支票。
She regularly encashes her dividend checks. = 她定期兑现她的股息支票。


一般过去时:
I encashed the traveler's checks at the bank. = 我在银行兑现了旅行支票。
They encashed their winning lottery ticket. = 他们兑现了他们中奖的彩票。


现在完成时:
He has encashed all his bonds. = 他已经兑现了他所有的债券。
She has already encashed the check. = 她已经兑现了支票。


过去完成时:
Before the trip, she had encashed a large sum of money. = 旅行前，她已经兑现了一大笔钱。
He had encashed his shares before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，他已经兑现了他的股票。


将来时:
I will encash the cheque tomorrow. = 我明天将兑现支票。
She will encash her savings bonds next week. = 她下周将兑现她的储蓄债券。


虚拟语气:
If I had a winning lottery ticket, I would encash it immediately. = 如果我有一张中奖彩票，我会立即兑现它。
I wish I could encash my dreams. = 我希望我能实现我的梦想。(Figurative use)


被动语态:
The check was encashed yesterday. = 支票昨天被兑现了。
The bonds will be encashed soon. = 债券很快就会被兑现。


疑问句:
Can I encash this cheque here? = 我能在这里兑现这张支票吗？
Where can I encash this money order? = 我在哪里可以兑现这张汇票？


否定句:
I cannot encash this check without proper identification. = 没有适当的身份证明，我无法兑现这张支票。
The bank refused to encash the damaged check. = 银行拒绝兑现损坏的支票。


PHRASE->
encash a check = 兑现支票
encash a bond = 兑现债券
encash a money order = 兑现汇票
encash a dividend = 兑现股息
encash a certificate = 兑现凭证


词源:
The word "encash" is a combination of "en-" (a prefix indicating "to make" or "to cause to be") and "cash."  It developed as a verb from the noun "cash."
