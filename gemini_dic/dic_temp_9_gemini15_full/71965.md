flighty:/ˈflaɪti/| adj.|轻浮的；反复无常的；难以捉摸的；易兴奋的 |A flighty person is irresponsible and likely to change their mind frequently. They may also be easily distracted or excited.  The word often implies a lack of seriousness or stability. |例句：Her flighty nature made it difficult to rely on her for anything.  她的轻浮性格让她难以让人信赖。

She's a flighty young woman with a string of failed relationships. 她是个轻浮的年轻女子，一段接一段的恋情都失败了。


近义词：fickle, capricious, erratic, volatile, irresponsible, giddy, frivolous|反义词：steady, reliable, dependable, responsible, serious, stable|词性：形容词
