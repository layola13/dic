semiactive:/ˈsɛmiˈæktɪv/| adj.|半主动的；半自动的|Partially active; involving a combination of active and passive elements.  It often describes systems or devices that combine some degree of autonomous control with external input or intervention.|例句：The semiactive suspension system adjusts its damping force based on road conditions.  半主动悬挂系统根据路况调整其阻尼力。

例句：This semiactive security system uses both sensors and human oversight. 这个半主动安全系统同时使用传感器和人工监督。


近义词：partially active,  self-regulating (in some contexts)

反义词：passive, fully active, fully automatic


词性：形容词
