reverse-charge:/rɪˈvɜːrs ˌtʃɑːrdʒ/| adj. n.|反向收费，逆向收费 |  Relating to a system where the recipient of a good or service, rather than the supplier, is responsible for paying the associated charges.  This is often seen in international transactions or in situations where a business wants to simplify invoicing. | Example:  The company uses a reverse-charge mechanism for VAT on imports. 这家公司对进口商品使用反向增值税机制。  |近义词：  indirect charge, recipient-pays | 反义词：direct charge, supplier-pays |  adj.
