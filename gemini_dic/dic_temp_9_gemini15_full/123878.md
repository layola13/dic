moray:/ˈmɔːreɪ/| n. |海鳗|A moray is a type of eel that lives in the sea. They are known for their powerful jaws and sharp teeth.  Morays are often found hiding in crevices and holes in coral reefs. | Example: We saw a giant moray eel while scuba diving in the coral reef.  我们潜入珊瑚礁时看到一条巨大的海鳗。|近义词：eel (a more general term)|反义词：无
