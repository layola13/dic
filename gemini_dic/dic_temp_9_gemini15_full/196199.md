sequential:/sɪˈkwɛnʃəl/| adj.| 连续的；顺序的；相继的 |Following one after another in a particular order.  |例句：The events are presented in sequential order.  事件按顺序呈现。|例句：The tasks should be performed in a sequential manner. 任务应该按顺序执行。|近义词：consecutive, successive, serial |反义词：random, simultaneous, haphazard
