conserve:/kənˈsɜːrv/| vt. & n.|保存；保护；节约|To protect something from harm or destruction; to use something carefully and not waste it. |例句：We need to conserve our natural resources.  我们需要保护我们的自然资源。|例句： Try to conserve energy by turning off lights when you leave a room. 离开房间时关灯以节约能源。|近义词：preserve, protect, save, safeguard |反义词：waste, squander, deplete|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We conserve water by taking shorter showers. = 我们通过洗更短的淋浴来节约用水。
They conserve energy by using energy-efficient appliances. = 他们通过使用节能电器来节约能源。

一般过去时:
She conserved her strength for the final race. = 她为最后的比赛节省体力。
They conserved food by canning and preserving it. = 他们通过罐装和保存食物来保存食物。

现在完成时:
We have conserved a significant amount of energy this year. = 今年我们已经节省了大量的能源。
They have conserved the old building by renovating it. = 他们通过翻新来保护这座古老的建筑。

过去完成时:
They had conserved all their resources before the winter arrived. = 冬天到来之前，他们已经节省了所有的资源。
She had conserved her energy for the important meeting. = 她已经为重要的会议节省了精力。

将来时:
We will conserve more water next year. = 明年我们将节约更多的水。
They will conserve the forest by planting more trees. = 他们将通过种植更多的树木来保护森林。

虚拟语气:
If we conserved more energy, we would reduce our carbon footprint. = 如果我们节约更多的能源，我们将减少碳足迹。
I wish we had conserved more resources in the past. = 我希望我们过去能节约更多的资源。

被动语态:
Natural resources should be conserved for future generations. = 自然资源应该为后代保存。
The old house was conserved by a team of experts. = 这座老房子由一个专家团队进行了保护。

疑问句:
How can we conserve water effectively? = 我们如何才能有效地节约用水？
Are they conserving enough energy in their home? = 他们在家里是否节约了足够的能源？

否定句:
We didn't conserve enough energy last month. = 上个月我们没有节约足够的能源。
They haven't conserved enough resources for the long journey. = 他们为漫长的旅程没有节省足够的资源。
