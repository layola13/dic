moralize:/ˈmɒrəlaɪz/ /ˈmɔːrəlaɪz/| vt.|说教；道德说教；(对…进行)道德解释|To talk or write about morals, often in a way that is intended to teach or improve someone's character.  It often implies a judgmental or preachy tone. |例句：He moralized about the evils of gambling. 他教训赌博的恶习。|例句：The story moralizes on the dangers of greed. 这个故事阐述了贪婪的危险。|近义词：preach, lecture, sermonize|反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moralizes constantly about the shortcomings of others. = 他不断地对别人的缺点说教。
She moralizes to her children about the importance of honesty. = 她向孩子们讲述诚实的重要性。


一般过去时:
The priest moralized on the importance of forgiveness. = 牧师就宽恕的重要性进行了说教。
My grandfather moralized about the value of hard work. = 我的祖父教训勤劳的可贵。


现在完成时:
He has moralized about this issue for years. = 他多年来一直在就这个问题说教。
She has moralized to her students about responsible citizenship. = 她一直以来都对学生们进行负责任的公民意识教育。


过去完成时:
He had moralized to them about the dangers of drugs before they even tried it. = 在他们尝试毒品之前，他就已经就毒品的危害告诫过他们了。
She had moralized extensively on the subject of charity before she finally donated. = 在她最终捐款之前，她已经对慈善这个话题进行了广泛的论述。


将来时:
The teacher will moralize to her class about the importance of respecting others. = 老师将对她的班级进行关于尊重他人的重要性的说教。
He will moralize again about the dangers of social media.  = 他将再次就社交媒体的危害进行说教。


虚拟语气:
If he moralized less, people might listen more. = 如果他少说教一些，人们可能会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't moralize so much. = 我希望他不那么爱说教。


被动语态:
The story's message is often moralized upon by critics. = 故事的寓意经常被评论家们进行道德解读。
His actions were moralized by the media as an example of civic virtue. = 他的行为被媒体解读为公民美德的典范。


疑问句:
Does he always moralize when discussing politics? = 他讨论政治时总是说教吗？
Why does she feel the need to moralize constantly? = 她为什么觉得需要不断地说教？


否定句:
He didn't moralize; he simply stated the facts. = 他没有说教；他只是陈述了事实。
She didn't moralize about their choices; she simply offered support. = 她没有对他们的选择说教；她只是提供了支持。


PHRASE->
moralize about something = 就某事说教
moralize on a theme = 就一个主题说教
moralize to someone = 对某人说教
moralize against something = 反对某事说教
moralize in a story = 在故事中进行道德说教
