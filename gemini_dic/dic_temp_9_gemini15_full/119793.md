migrating:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 迁移的；移居的；迁徙的 |  Describes the act of moving from one place to another, especially to a more favorable environment or to breed.  It can refer to animals, people, or even data. |

例句：
Millions of birds are migrating south for the winter.  数百万只鸟正在向南迁徙过冬。

The family is migrating to a warmer climate.  这家人正在迁移到气候更温暖的地方。


近义词：moving, relocating, travelling, transferring

反义词：settling, remaining, staying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Birds are migrating south. 鸟类正在向南迁徙。
People are migrating to the cities. 人们正在迁移到城市。

一般过去时:
The whales migrated to warmer waters. 鲸鱼迁移到了温暖的水域。
Our ancestors migrated across continents. 我们的祖先跨越大陆迁徙。

现在完成时:
Many species have migrated to new habitats. 许多物种已经迁移到了新的栖息地。
The population has migrated away from the rural areas. 人口已经从农村地区迁移出去。

过去完成时:
The birds had migrated before the storm arrived. 鸟类在暴风雨来临之前就已经迁徙了。
They had migrated to the coast before the winter set in. 他们在冬天到来之前已经迁移到了海岸。

将来时:
The butterflies will migrate next spring. 蝴蝶将在明年春天迁徙。
We will migrate to a new country next year. 我们明年将移居到一个新的国家。

虚拟语气:
If the climate changed drastically, many animals would migrate. 如果气候发生剧烈变化，许多动物都会迁徙。
I wish I could migrate to a tropical island. 我希望我能迁移到一个热带岛屿。

被动语态:
The data is being migrated to a new server. 数据正在迁移到新的服务器。
The refugees were migrated to a safer location. 难民被迁移到一个更安全的地方。

疑问句:
Are the birds migrating yet? 鸟类正在迁徙吗？
When are you migrating? 你什么时候迁移？

否定句:
The animals are not migrating this year. 动物们今年没有迁徙。
The company is not migrating its servers. 公司没有迁移它的服务器。


PHRASE->

词源:
From Latin *migrāre "to wander, remove, depart."  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *mey-  "to change, move, go."
