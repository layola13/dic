It suggests a deep and interconnected relationship where the boundaries between the elements are blurred. | Example: The two cultures had an interpenetrative relationship, influencing each other's art, language, and customs.  两个文化之间存在着相互渗透的关系，彼此影响着对方的艺术、语言和习俗。|近义词:intertwined, interconnected, mutually influencing, reciprocal | 反义词:separate, distinct, isolated, independent
