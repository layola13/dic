oha:/ˈoʊhɑː/| int.|哦哈，(日语)表示问候或感叹|An exclamation used in Japanese, similar to "hello" or an expression of surprise or understanding.|例句：Oha, genki desu ka? = 哦哈，你好吗？|近义词：hello, hi, konnichiwa|反义词:N/A


USAGE->
This word doesn't have grammatical usage like "what" in English.  It's an interjection.  Therefore, tense, voice, and other grammatical structures are not applicable.

PHRASE->
Ohayou gozaimasu (おはようございます) = Good morning.
Ohayou (おはよう) = Good morning (informal).
Ohayo (おはよう) = Good morning (very informal).
Ohayou! (おはよう！) = Good morning! (informal, with exclamation)
Ohayo, [Name]! (おはよう、[名前]！) = Good morning, [Name]! (informal)
