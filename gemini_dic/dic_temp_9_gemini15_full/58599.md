dullard:/ˈdʌlərd/| n. |迟钝的人，笨蛋 |A dullard is a person who is slow to understand or learn things; someone who is stupid or unintelligent. They may be considered boring or unresponsive due to their lack of mental agility. |例句：He's such a dullard; he couldn't follow the simplest instructions. 他真是个笨蛋，连最简单的指示都听不懂。|例句：She found him a dullard, incapable of engaging in stimulating conversation. 她觉得他是个迟钝的人，无法进行刺激性的谈话。|近义词：dunce, idiot, fool, simpleton |反义词：genius, intellect, bright spark
