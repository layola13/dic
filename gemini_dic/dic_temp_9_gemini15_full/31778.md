checkerberry: /ˈtʃɛkərˌbɛri/ | n. |越橘;美洲红珠果| A small, creeping evergreen plant of the heath family, bearing bright red berries with a spicy, slightly sweet flavor.  Checkerberries are often used in candies, jams, and other desserts. They are also known as wintergreen. | Example:  The children enjoyed picking checkerberries in the woods. 孩子们喜欢在树林里采摘美洲红珠果。
