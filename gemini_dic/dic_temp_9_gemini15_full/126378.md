myrmecophyte:/ˌmɜːrmɪˈkɒfaɪt/| n. |蚁生植物|A plant that lives in a symbiotic relationship with ants.  These plants often provide shelter or food for the ants, and in return, the ants may protect the plant from herbivores or other threats.  Examples include certain species of Acacia and Cecropia.|The Cecropia trees are classic examples of myrmecophytes, offering hollow stems for ant colonies.|近义词：ant-plant|反义词：无
