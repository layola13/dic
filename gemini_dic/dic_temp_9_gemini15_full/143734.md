outlove:/ˌaʊtˈlʌv/| v.|超越…的爱，胜过…的爱|to love (someone or something) more than someone or something else|例句：I outlove my dog; he is my best friend. 我胜过爱我的狗；他是我的好朋友。|近义词：surpass in love, exceed in affection|反义词：underlove


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outloves his brother. = 他比他哥哥更爱。
She outloves all her friends. = 她比所有朋友更爱。


一般过去时:
They outloved their old car. = 他们比他们的旧车更爱。
He outloved his previous girlfriend. = 他比他之前的女朋友更爱。


现在完成时:
I have outloved all my previous relationships. = 我已经比我以前的所有恋情更爱。
She has outloved her initial passion for the hobby. = 她已经比她对这个爱好最初的热情更爱了。


过去完成时:
He had outloved his fear of heights. = 他已经克服了他对高空恐惧症。
They had outloved their initial reservations about the project. = 他们已经克服了他们对这个项目的最初顾虑。


将来时:
She will outlove her current anxieties. = 她将克服她目前的焦虑。
He will outlove his previous disappointments. = 他将战胜他以前的失望。


虚拟语气:
If I outloved my fear, I would try skydiving. = 如果我战胜了我的恐惧，我会尝试跳伞。
I wish I outloved my procrastination. = 我希望我能战胜我的拖延症。


被动语态:
His fear was outloved by his determination. = 他的决心战胜了他的恐惧。
Her initial doubts were outloved by her growing confidence. = 她日益增长的自信战胜了她最初的疑虑。


疑问句:
Does he outlove his work? = 他是否胜过爱他的工作？
Did she outlove her ambition? = 她是否胜过爱她的野心？


否定句:
He doesn't outlove his family. = 他并不胜过爱他的家人。
She didn't outlove her comfort zone. = 她没有战胜她的舒适区。


PHRASE->
outlove one's fears = 战胜恐惧
outlove one's doubts = 战胜疑虑
outlove one's insecurities = 战胜不安全感
outlove one's past = 战胜过去
outlove one's limitations = 战胜局限性
