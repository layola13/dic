protect:/prəˈtɛkt/| vt. |保护，防护|to keep someone or something safe from harm or damage.  |例句：The police are protecting witnesses from the media. 警方正在保护证人免受媒体的骚扰。|例句：We need to protect our environment. 我们需要保护我们的环境。|近义词：shield, safeguard, defend, preserve|反义词：endanger, harm, jeopardize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He protects his family from harm. = 他保护家人免受伤害。
The government protects the rights of its citizens. = 政府保护公民的权利。

一般过去时:
She protected her children from the storm. = 她保护她的孩子们免受暴风雨的侵害。
The soldiers protected the city from attack. = 士兵们保护城市免受攻击。

现在完成时:
They have protected the wildlife reserve for many years. = 他们多年来一直保护着野生动物保护区。
The organization has protected countless animals from extinction. = 该组织保护了无数动物免于灭绝。

过去完成时:
He had protected his secret for years before it was revealed. = 在他的秘密被揭露之前，他已经保护了它很多年了。
They had protected the building from fire before the firefighters arrived. = 在消防员到达之前，他们已经保护了建筑物免受火灾的侵害。

将来时:
We will protect our planet from pollution. = 我们将保护我们的星球免受污染。
The company will protect its intellectual property. = 公司将保护其知识产权。

虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I could protect you. = 如果我更强壮，我就能保护你了。
I wish I could protect everyone from suffering. = 我希望我能保护每个人免受苦难。

被动语态:
The environment is protected by law. = 环境受法律保护。
The children were protected from the dangers of the street. = 孩子们受到保护，免受街道的危险。

疑问句:
How can we protect ourselves from cyberattacks? = 我们如何才能保护自己免受网络攻击？
Does this insurance policy protect my property? = 这份保险单能保护我的财产吗？

否定句:
He didn't protect his investment properly. = 他没有妥善保护他的投资。
The castle failed to protect its inhabitants from the siege. = 城堡未能保护其居民免受围攻。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "protect" comes from the Old French word "protegier," which itself is derived from the Latin word "protegere."  "Pro" means "before" or "in front of," and "tegere" means "to cover."  Therefore, the original meaning was "to cover before," implying shielding or defense.
