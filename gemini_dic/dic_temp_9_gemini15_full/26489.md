cabala: /ˈkæbəlɑː/ | n. | 卡巴拉；神秘的秘密结社；秘密阴谋 | A cabal is a small group of secret plotters, especially a group who conspire to overthrow a government. It can also refer to the mystical Jewish tradition of Kabbalah.  |  例句：The king was overthrown by a cabal of his advisors. 国王被他的顾问们组成的秘密集团推翻了。  | 近义词：conspiracy, clique, secret society | 反义词：openness, transparency |  名词
