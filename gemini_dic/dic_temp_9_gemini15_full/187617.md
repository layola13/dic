romps:/rɒmps/| /rɑːmps/ | v. n. |嬉戏；玩耍；轻松获胜 |To play boisterously; frolic.  Also, to win easily. |例句：The children romped in the park.孩子们在公园里嬉戏。|例句：Our team romped to victory. 我们队轻松获胜。|近义词：play, frolic, gambol, romp home (轻松获胜)|反义词：lose, struggle, toil|动词，名词


USAGE->

She romps with her dog every afternoon. = 她每天下午都和她的狗嬉戏。
The children romp in the garden. = 孩子们在花园里玩耍。
