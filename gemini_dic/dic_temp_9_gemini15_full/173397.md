quasi-efficient:/ˈkwɑːzi ɪˈfɪʃənt/| adj.|准有效的，近似有效的 |Approaching but not fully meeting the criteria of efficiency.  It suggests a level of efficiency that is less than perfect but still acceptable or useful in a given context.  The term often implies a trade-off between efficiency and other desirable qualities, such as simplicity or robustness. |例句：The algorithm, while not perfectly efficient, is quasi-efficient enough for our purposes.  这个算法虽然不是完全有效的，但对于我们的目的来说已经足够准有效了。|近义词：partially efficient, reasonably efficient, fairly efficient |反义词：inefficient, completely inefficient
