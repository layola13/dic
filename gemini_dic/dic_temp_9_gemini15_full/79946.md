/dʒiːˌɛnˈpiː/ | n. | 国内生产总值 | Gross National Product; the total value of goods produced and services provided by a country during one year.  GNP is a broad measure of a nation's overall economic output and is often used to compare the economic performance of different countries.  However, it has limitations as it doesn't account for factors such as income inequality or environmental damage. | The country's GNP increased by 3% last year.  该国的国内生产总值去年增长了3%。

The government announced a plan to boost the GNP. 政府宣布了一项提高国内生产总值的计划。
