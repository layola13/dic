plans:/plænz/ | n. |计划，打算 |Plans are a set of actions or steps designed to achieve a particular goal. They can be formal and detailed, or informal and flexible.  |例句：We need to make plans for the future. 我们需要为未来制定计划。

例句：Her plans for the weekend included a picnic and a hike. 她周末的计划包括野餐和远足。

近义词：schemes, projects, intentions, designs | 反义词：chaos, disorder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plans a trip to Europe. = 他计划去欧洲旅行。
She plans her day carefully. = 她仔细地计划她的一天。

一般过去时:
They planned a surprise party. = 他们计划了一个惊喜派对。
He planned his escape carefully. = 他仔细地策划了他的逃跑。

现在完成时:
We have planned everything for the wedding. = 我们已经为婚礼计划好了一切。
She has planned her career path for years. = 她多年来一直规划着她的职业道路。

过去完成时:
They had planned to go to the beach, but it rained. = 他们原计划去海滩，但下雨了。
I had planned to finish the report yesterday, but I got sick. = 我原计划昨天完成报告，但我病了。

将来时:
I will plan a vacation next month. = 我下个月将计划一次假期。
They will plan their budget carefully. = 他们将仔细计划他们的预算。

虚拟语气:
If I had planned better, I wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果我计划得更好，我就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had planned my time more efficiently. = 我真希望我更有效率地安排我的时间。

被动语态:
The meeting was planned meticulously. = 会议被精心策划。
The trip was planned by a travel agent. = 旅行是由旅行社安排的。

疑问句:
What are your plans for tonight? = 你今晚有什么计划？
What are their plans for the future? = 他们未来的计划是什么？

否定句:
I didn't plan to stay so long. = 我没打算待这么久。
They didn't plan on attending the concert. = 他们没打算参加音乐会。


PHRASE->
make plans = 制定计划
have plans = 有计划
cancel plans = 取消计划
change plans = 改变计划
go according to plan = 按计划进行
