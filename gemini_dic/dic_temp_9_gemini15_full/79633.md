glossery: /ˈɡlɒsəri/ | n. |词汇表，术语表|A glossary is an alphabetical list of terms in a particular domain of knowledge with the definitions for each term. It is often included at the back of books or documents to help readers understand specialized vocabulary.|例句：The textbook included a glossary of technical terms at the end. 这本教科书在结尾处包含了一个技术术语词汇表。|近义词：lexicon, vocabulary, index|反义词：无
