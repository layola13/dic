hurling: /ˈhɜːrlɪŋ/ | n. v. |掷，猛投；盖尔式投掷球 | a game played in Ireland using a stick to hit a small ball | 例句：The boy was hurling stones at the window. = 男孩正往窗户扔石头。  He's a skilled hurler. = 他是个熟练的掷球手。| 近义词：throwing, pitching, flinging | 反义词：catching, receiving | 词性: 名词和动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hurls the ball with great force. = 他用力地投掷球。
They are hurling insults at each other. = 他们在互相辱骂。
