prewarming:/ˈpriːwɔːrmɪŋ/| n. |预热；预先加热|The process of heating something beforehand to a desired temperature.  Prewarming ensures optimal performance and efficiency. For example, prewarming an engine before driving can improve fuel economy and reduce wear and tear.|近义词：preheating, warming up|反义词：cooling down
