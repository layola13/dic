nonreceipt: /ˌnɒn.rɪˈsiːt/ | n. | 未收到；未收到通知 | The failure to receive something; lack of receipt.  It often refers to a failure to receive something expected or required. | The nonreceipt of the payment led to a late fee.  未收到付款导致滞纳金。


The nonreceipt of his application caused a delay in the process. 未收到他的申请导致流程延误。


近义词：lack of receipt, non-delivery, failure to receive, omission of receipt

反义词：receipt, acknowledgement, reception
