pluviograph:/ˈpluːviəɡræf/| n. |雨量自记器|A pluviograph is a self-recording rain gauge that continuously measures and records the amount of rainfall over a period of time. It typically uses a tipping bucket mechanism or a float mechanism to measure rainfall, and the data is recorded on a chart or electronically.|The pluviograph recorded a steady rainfall throughout the night.  雨量自记器记录了整夜的持续降雨。|rain gauge, ombrometer| (no direct antonym)|名词
