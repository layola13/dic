pat:/pæt/| n./v.|轻拍；抚摸；轻打|to strike lightly, typically with the hand;  to touch gently|例句：She patted the dog's head. 她轻轻拍了拍狗的头。|例句：He patted his pockets to check for his keys. 他拍了拍口袋检查钥匙。|近义词：tap, stroke, caress|反义词：punch, hit, slap|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pats the dog every day. = 他每天都拍拍那只狗。
She pats the child gently on the back. = 她轻轻地拍了拍孩子的背。


一般过去时:
I patted him on the shoulder. = 我拍了拍他的肩膀。
She patted the dough to flatten it. = 她拍了拍面团使其变平。


现在完成时:
I have patted the cat many times. = 我已经摸过很多次猫了。
She has patted her child's head to comfort him. = 她已经拍了拍孩子的头安慰他。


过去完成时:
I had patted the horse before getting on. = 我在骑上马之前已经拍了拍马。
He had patted the dust off his coat before entering. = 他在进去之前已经拍掉了外套上的灰尘。


将来时:
I will pat the dog when I see it. = 我看到狗的时候会拍拍它。
She will pat the child's head to show her affection. = 她会拍拍孩子的头以表达她的爱意。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pat the dog. = 如果我是你，我会拍拍那只狗。
I wish I had patted the baby before it cried. = 我真希望宝宝哭之前我拍拍它。


被动语态:
The dog was patted by the child. = 那只狗被孩子拍打过。
The horse was patted gently before the race. = 赛马前，马被轻轻地拍了拍。


疑问句:
Did you pat the dog? = 你拍过那只狗吗？
Will you pat the baby's back? = 你会拍拍婴儿的背吗？


否定句:
I didn't pat the dog. = 我没有拍那只狗。
She didn't pat the child's head. = 她没有拍孩子的头。


PHRASE->
pat on the back:  = 赞扬；鼓励
pat down: = 搜身
on the pat: = 恰好；适时
a pat answer: = 现成的答案；套话
pat oneself on the back: = 自我赞扬
