launch:/lɔːntʃ/ | /lɑːntʃ/ | vt., n. |发射；启动；开始；上市；发布 | To propel (something, such as a rocket or spacecraft) into the air or space. To begin or introduce something new, such as a product, project, or campaign. |例句：The company launched a new marketing campaign. 公司发起了一场新的营销活动。

例句：NASA successfully launched the space shuttle. 美国宇航局成功发射了航天飞机。


近义词：initiate, start, begin, introduce, unveil | 反义词：end, conclude, finish, terminate | 词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They launch a new product every year. = 他们每年都发布一款新产品。
The company launches its marketing campaign in the spring. = 公司在春天启动它的营销活动。


一般过去时:
The rocket launched successfully. = 火箭成功发射了。
They launched the new software last week. = 他们上周发布了新的软件。


现在完成时:
The company has launched three new products this year. = 公司今年已经发布了三款新产品。
They have launched several successful projects. = 他们已经启动了几个成功的项目。


过去完成时:
They had launched the website before the conference. = 他们在会议之前就已经上线了网站。
The project had launched successfully before the budget was exceeded. = 在预算超支之前，项目已经成功启动了。


将来时:
We will launch the new website next month. = 我们下个月将上线新的网站。
They will launch a new advertising campaign next year. = 他们明年将启动一个新的广告宣传活动。


虚拟语气:
If they had launched the product earlier, they would have had more sales. = 如果他们早点发布产品，他们就会有更多销量。
I wish we had launched the project sooner. = 我真希望我们早点启动这个项目。


被动语态:
The new product was launched last month. = 新产品是上个月发布的。
The rocket will be launched tomorrow morning. = 火箭将于明天早上发射。


疑问句:
When will the new game be launched? = 新游戏什么时候发布？
Has the project been launched yet? = 项目已经启动了吗？


否定句:
The rocket did not launch successfully. = 火箭没有成功发射。
They haven't launched the new website yet. = 他们还没有上线新的网站。


PHRASE->
launch a product = 发布产品
launch an attack = 发动攻击
launch a campaign = 发起一场活动
launch an investigation = 开始调查
launch into = 开始（热烈地讨论或做某事）


词源:
The word "launch" ultimately derives from the Old French word "lancer," meaning "to throw" or "to hurl."  This reflects the original sense of the word, relating to the forceful projection of something.  Over time, its meaning broadened to encompass the initiation or beginning of various endeavors.
