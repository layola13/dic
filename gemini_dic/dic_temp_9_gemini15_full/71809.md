fleeces:/ˈfliːsɪz/| n.|羊毛；羊毛衫；欺骗；掠夺|The soft, woolly covering of a sheep, or a garment made from this.  It can also refer to the act of defrauding someone.|例句：The shepherd carefully sheared the fleeces from his sheep. 牧羊人小心翼翼地从羊身上剪下羊毛。

例句：The con artist fleeced the elderly woman out of her life savings. 骗子骗走了这位老妇人的毕生积蓄。|近义词：wool, fleece jacket, swindle, defraud|反义词：none/opposite action (e.g., giving, restoring)|名词
