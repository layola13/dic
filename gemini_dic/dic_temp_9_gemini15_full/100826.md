introduce:/ˌɪntrəˈduːs/ | vt. |介绍，引进，提出，采用 | To present someone or something formally to others.  |例句：Let me introduce you to my friend, John.  让我把你介绍给我的朋友约翰。|例句：The company introduced a new product last month.  这家公司上个月推出了一款新产品。|近义词：present, acquaint, initiate |反义词：conceal, hide, withdraw


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He introduces himself at every meeting. = 他在每次会议上都自我介绍。
She introduces new ideas to the team regularly. = 她定期向团队介绍新的想法。

一般过去时:
They introduced a new policy last year. = 他们去年推出了一项新政策。
He introduced me to his parents. = 他把我介绍给了他的父母。

现在完成时:
She has introduced many innovative concepts throughout her career. = 她在职业生涯中引进了许多创新的概念。
They have introduced several changes to the system. = 他们已经对系统进行了一些更改。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had introduced himself to the audience. = 在演示之前，他已向观众做了自我介绍。
She had introduced the concept in her previous research. = 她已在之前的研究中介绍了这一概念。

将来时:
The company will introduce a new software next month. = 公司下个月将推出一个新软件。
We will introduce you to our team members. = 我们将把你介绍给我们的团队成员。

虚拟语气:
If I were to introduce myself, I would start with my name and profession. = 如果我要自我介绍，我会先说出我的名字和职业。
I wish I had introduced myself sooner. = 我真希望我早点自我介绍。

被动语态:
The new product was introduced to the market last week. = 新产品上周投放市场。
The bill was introduced to the parliament. = 该法案已提交议会。

疑问句:
May I introduce myself? = 我可以自我介绍一下吗？
Who introduced you to this company? = 谁把你介绍到这家公司的？

否定句:
He didn't introduce his wife to us. = 他没有把他的妻子介绍给我们。
She didn't introduce the new policy properly. = 她没有适当地介绍新的政策。



PHRASE->
