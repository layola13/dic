shipload:/ˈʃɪpˌloʊd/| n. |船装货物量；一船货|A shipload refers to the amount of cargo that a ship can carry in one voyage.  It can also be used figuratively to refer to a large quantity of something. |例句：The shipload of bananas arrived at the port this morning.  这船香蕉今天早上抵达港口。  例句： We received a shipload of complaints after the product launch. 产品发布后我们收到了大量的投诉。 |近义词：cargo, shipment, load |反义词:少量，少量货物
