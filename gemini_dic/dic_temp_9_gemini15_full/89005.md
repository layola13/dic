hollies:/ˈhɒliz/| n.|冬青属植物；冬青树；冬青树叶|The word "hollies" is the plural of "holly," referring to plants of the genus *Ilex*, which are evergreen shrubs or trees with prickly leaves and often red berries.  They are popular for Christmas decorations.|例句：The garden was decorated with sprigs of hollies. 花园里装饰着冬青枝条。|例句：We saw many hollies growing wild in the woods. 我们在树林里看到许多野生冬青树。|近义词：evergreens, shrubs|反义词：无
