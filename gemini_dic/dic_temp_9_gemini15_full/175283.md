一般现在时:
Rainforests cover a significant portion of the Earth's surface. 雨林覆盖了地球表面很大一部分。
The rainforest thrives on consistent rainfall. 雨林依靠持续的降雨而繁茂。


一般过去时:
The rainforest once extended much further inland. 雨林曾经延伸到更远的内陆地区。
Deforestation significantly reduced the size of the rainforest.  森林砍伐大大减少了雨林的面积。


现在完成时:
Scientists have studied rainforests for decades. 科学家们已经研究雨林几十年了。
Humans have significantly impacted rainforests globally. 人类已经对全球的雨林产生了重大影响。


过去完成时:
Before the logging began, the rainforest was even more extensive. 在伐木开始之前，雨林的面积更大。
They had already lost a large area of rainforest before the conservation efforts began. 在保护工作开始之前，他们已经失去了大片雨林。


将来时:
Climate change will likely affect the rainforests significantly. 气候变化可能会对雨林产生重大影响。
We will need to protect rainforests to prevent biodiversity loss. 我们需要保护雨林以防止生物多样性丧失。


虚拟语气:
If we didn't protect the rainforests, many species would become extinct. 如果我们不保护雨林，许多物种将会灭绝。
I wish the deforestation of rainforests would stop immediately. 我希望雨林砍伐能够立即停止。


被动语态:
The rainforest is being destroyed at an alarming rate. 雨林正在以惊人的速度被破坏。
Many rainforest species are threatened by habitat loss. 许多雨林物种受到栖息地丧失的威胁。


疑问句:
What are the main threats to rainforests? 雨林面临的主要威胁是什么？

Rainforests are not just located in the tropics. 雨林并不仅仅位于热带地区。
