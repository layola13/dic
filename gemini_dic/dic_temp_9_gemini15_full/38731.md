conditioner:/kənˈdɪʃənər/| n.|护发素，空调，调节器|A substance, typically a liquid, used to improve the condition of hair or skin, or a device that regulates temperature or humidity.|例句：I use a conditioner after every shampoo. 我每次洗完头后都会用护发素。|例句：The air conditioner is broken. 空调坏了。|近义词：hair treatment, balm, regulator, air conditioning unit|反义词：damager (in the context of hair)|词性:名词

词源:
The word "conditioner" is derived from the verb "condition," meaning "to improve the state or quality of something."  The suffix "-er" denotes an agent or instrument.  Therefore, a conditioner is something that conditions.  The specific application (hair, skin, air) is determined by context.
