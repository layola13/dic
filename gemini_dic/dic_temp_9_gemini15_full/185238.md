retrain:/ˌriːˈtreɪn/| vt.| 再培训，重新训练 |To train (someone or oneself) again, especially in a new skill or for a new job.|例句：The company will retrain its employees in new software. 公司将对员工进行新软件的再培训。|例句：He decided to retrain as a teacher after losing his job.  他失业后决定改行当老师。|近义词：re-educate, reskill, retool|反义词：untrain (less common)|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They retrain employees annually. = 他们每年都对员工进行再培训。
The company retrains its staff in new technologies. = 公司对员工进行新技术的再培训。


一般过去时:
She retrained as a nurse after working in finance. =  她在金融行业工作后，重新培训成为一名护士。
The factory retrained its workers on new safety procedures. = 工厂对工人进行了新的安全规程的再培训。


现在完成时:
He has retrained as a software engineer. = 他已经重新培训成为一名软件工程师。
They have retrained several times throughout their careers. = 他们在职业生涯中已经多次接受再培训。


过去完成时:
She had retrained before she applied for the new job. = 在申请新工作之前，她已经进行了再培训。
The team had retrained extensively before the project launch. = 在项目启动之前，团队进行了广泛的再培训。


将来时:
The government will retrain unemployed workers. = 政府将对失业工人进行再培训。
I will retrain in data science next year. = 我明年将接受数据科学的再培训。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would retrain as a chef. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新培训成为一名厨师。
It would be beneficial if the company retrained its staff on new equipment. = 如果公司对员工进行新设备的再培训将是有益的。


被动语态:
The workers were retrained on new safety protocols. = 工人们接受了新的安全协议的再培训。
Employees will be retrained in the new system next month. = 员工们将在下个月接受新系统的再培训。


疑问句:
Will they retrain the staff on the new software? = 他们会对员工进行新软件的再培训吗？
Has the company retrained its employees in cybersecurity? = 公司是否对员工进行了网络安全的再培训？


否定句:
The company did not retrain its employees last year. = 公司去年没有对员工进行再培训。
They won't retrain unless funding is provided. = 除非提供资金，否则他们不会进行再培训。
