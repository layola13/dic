/ˌmeməˈræbɪliː/| n.|值得纪念的事物，难忘的经历|Something worth remembering; a memorable event or experience.  It refers to an event, experience, or object that is so significant or impactful that it is easily recalled and cherished.  |例句：The trip to Italy was a memorabile for the whole family.  意大利之旅对全家来说都是一次难忘的经历。

例句：Her graduation ceremony was a memorabile moment in her life.  她的毕业典礼是她生命中值得纪念的时刻。

近义词：souvenir, keepsake, remembrance |反义词：forgettable, insignificant, commonplace|名词
