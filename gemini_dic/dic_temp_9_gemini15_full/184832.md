resweeten:/ˌriːˈswiːtn/ | vt. | 再加糖；使重新变甜 | To sweeten again; to make sweet again.  |例句：I need to resweeten my tea. 我需要再加点糖到我的茶里。

例句：The chef decided to resweeten the dessert after tasting it.  厨师在品尝后决定再给甜点加糖。


近义词：sweeten again, add more sugar | 反义词：unsweeten, make bitter


词性：动词
