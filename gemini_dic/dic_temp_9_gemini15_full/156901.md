pilafs:/ˈpɪlæfs/| n. |pilaf(土耳其大米饭)|A pilaf is a dish of rice that is cooked with other ingredients, such as vegetables, meat, or spices.  It's often prepared by sautéing the rice in butter or oil before adding broth and simmering until tender.|Example:  The restaurant is famous for its delicious chicken pilaf. 这家餐馆以其美味的鸡肉饭而闻名。|近义词：rice dish, casserole|反义词：无
