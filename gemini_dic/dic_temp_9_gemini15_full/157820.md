plage:/plɑːʒ/|/plɑːʒ/| n. |海滩，沙滩 |A plage is a shore of a sea or lake, especially one that is sandy or pebbly and is used for recreation.  It often features stretches of sand or pebbles along the water's edge.|例句：We spent the afternoon relaxing on the plage. 我们在海滩上度过了下午的休闲时光。
例句：The children built sandcastles on the plage. 孩子们在沙滩上堆沙堡。|近义词：beach, shore, coast |反义词：None.
