词源:
The word "scattergun" is a compound word.  "Scatter" comes from the Old English *scaterian, meaning "to disperse" or "to throw in different directions," and "gun" comes from the Old French *gonne, a type of cannon.  The combination thus describes a gun that scatters its shot widely.
