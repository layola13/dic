creaks:/kriːks/| v.|吱吱作响，发出咯吱声|To make a series of short, high-pitched sounds, typically those made by something old and/or made of wood when it is moved or strained.|The old house creaked ominously in the wind.  古老的房子在风中发出不祥的吱吱声。The floorboards creaked under his weight.地板在他体重下发出咯吱声。| groans, rattles, squeaks | silent, smooth | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The door creaks every time it's opened. = 门每次打开都会发出吱吱声。
The old chair creaks when I sit on it. = 我坐在旧椅子上时，它会发出吱吱声。

一般过去时:
The floorboards creaked loudly last night. = 昨晚地板发出很大的吱吱声。
The branch creaked as I climbed the tree. = 我爬树时树枝咯吱作响。

现在完成时:
The hinges have creaked all night. = 铰链整夜都在吱吱作响。
The stairs have creaked since we moved in. = 自从我们搬进来以来，楼梯就一直吱吱作响。

过去完成时:
The door had creaked before I even touched it. = 我还没碰门，它就已经吱吱作响了。
The old ship had creaked and groaned throughout the storm. = 老船在整个暴风雨中一直吱吱嘎嘎地响着。

将来时:
The floor will creak when you walk across it. = 你走过时地板会发出吱吱声。
That old bridge will probably creak when we drive across it. = 我们开车经过那座旧桥时，它可能会发出吱吱声。

虚拟语气:
If the floor creaked, I would know someone was there. = 如果地板发出吱吱声，我就知道有人在那里。
I wish the stairs didn't creak so loudly at night. = 我希望楼梯晚上不要发出那么大的吱吱声。

被动语态:
The sound of the creaking door was heard throughout the house. = 吱吱作响的门的声音传遍了整个房子。(The door was heard creaking throughout the house.)
The old ship's timbers were creaked by the waves. = 老船的木材被海浪弄得吱吱作响。(The old ship's timbers were subjected to creaking by the waves.)


疑问句:

否定句:
The door didn't creak when I opened it this time. = 我这次打开门时，它没有发出吱吱声。
The new floor doesn't creak at all. = 新地板一点也不吱吱作响。
