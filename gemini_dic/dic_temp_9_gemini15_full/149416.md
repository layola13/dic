packeted:/ˈpækɪtɪd/| adj.|打包的；分组的|Packaged or bundled into packets.  This usually refers to data or information being organized into discrete units for transmission or storage.|例句：The data was packeted before transmission to improve efficiency. 数据在传输前被分组以提高效率。
The documents were packeted and ready for mailing. 文件已打包好，准备邮寄。|近义词：bundled, grouped, segmented|反义词：ungrouped, unbundled
