miscalculates:/ˌmɪsˈkælkjuleɪts/ | vt. | 误算；算错；估计错误 | To make a wrong calculation or estimate.  |例句：He miscalculated the distance and ran out of fuel. 他算错了距离，结果燃料用完了。|例句：The company miscalculated the market demand and lost a lot of money.  公司误判了市场需求，损失了很多钱。|近义词：misjudge, underestimate, overestimate |反义词：calculate accurately, correctly assess


USAGE->

She miscalculates the amount of ingredients needed for the recipe. 她每次都算错食谱所需的食材数量。
