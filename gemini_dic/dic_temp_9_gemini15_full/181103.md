reinducts:/ˌriːɪnˈdʌkts/ | v. |重新介绍；重新接纳；重新任命 | To induct someone again into a position, organization, or office.  This word implies a prior induction followed by a subsequent re-induction. |例句：The disgraced officer was reindicted after a thorough investigation cleared him of wrongdoing.  被撤职的官员在彻底调查证明他无罪后被重新任命。

例句：After a period of suspension, the university reindicted the professor to his teaching position.  经过一段时间的停职后，大学将教授重新任命为他的教职。


近义词：reinstate, reinstall, reappoint | 反义词：dismiss, remove, discharge
