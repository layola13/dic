recharts:/ˈriːtʃɑːrts/| n.|重制图表；重新绘制图表 |Recharts is a composable charting library built on top of React. It provides a declarative way to create various types of charts, such as line charts, bar charts, scatter plots, and more.  It's designed to be flexible and easy to use, allowing developers to customize the appearance and behavior of their charts.|Example: We used Recharts to create an interactive line chart showing sales trends over the past year.  我们使用 Recharts 创建了一个交互式折线图，显示了过去一年的销售趋势。|近义词：charting library, visualization library|反义词：无|名词
