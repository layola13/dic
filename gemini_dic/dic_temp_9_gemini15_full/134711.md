nonmatrimonially:/ˌnɒnˌmætrɪˈmoʊniəli/| adv.|非婚生地，非婚姻地 |  Not within the bonds of marriage; outside of marriage.  |例句：The child was born nonmatrimonially.  = 这个孩子是非婚生子女。

例句：They lived together nonmatrimonially for many years before getting married. = 他们在结婚前非婚同居多年。


近义词：illegitimately, out of wedlock, informally


反义词：matrimonially
