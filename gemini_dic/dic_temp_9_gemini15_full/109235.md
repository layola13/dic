leisure:/ˈliːʒə(r)/| n. |休闲；闲暇；悠闲 |  Time free from work or duties; free time.  |  例句：I enjoy spending my leisure time reading. 我喜欢在休闲时间阅读。

例句：She has little leisure time because of her busy schedule. 因为日程繁忙，她几乎没有休闲时间。


近义词：free time, relaxation, recreation | 反义词：work, occupation, toil


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enjoys his leisure time. = 他享受他的休闲时光。
We have little leisure during the busy season. = 在繁忙的季节我们几乎没有休闲时间。


一般过去时:
They spent their leisure playing golf. = 他们把休闲时间花在了打高尔夫球上。
She had plenty of leisure after retirement. = 她退休后有很多休闲时间。


现在完成时:
I have had little leisure to pursue my hobbies recently. = 最近我几乎没有时间去追求我的爱好。
They haven't had much leisure this month. = 这个月他们没有多少休闲时间。


过去完成时:
She had already finished her work and had some leisure time. = 她已经完成了工作，有一些休闲时间。
Before the project, he had more leisure than now. = 在项目开始之前，他比现在有更多的休闲时间。


将来时:
I will have more leisure after finishing this project. = 完成这个项目后我将会有更多休闲时间。
They will spend their leisure traveling next year. = 他们明年将把休闲时间花在旅行上。


虚拟语气:
If I had more leisure, I would learn to play the piano. = 如果我有更多休闲时间，我会学习弹钢琴。
I wish I had more leisure to spend with my family. = 我希望我有更多时间和家人一起度过。


被动语态:
Leisure time is often undervalued. = 休闲时间常常被低估。
His leisure time was often interrupted by phone calls. = 他的休闲时间经常被打扰。


疑问句:
Do you have much leisure time? = 你有很多休闲时间吗？
How do you spend your leisure time? = 你如何度过你的休闲时间？


否定句:
She doesn't have much leisure time. = 她没有很多休闲时间。
He didn't enjoy his leisure time that day. = 他那天没有享受他的休闲时间。
