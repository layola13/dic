phenolized: /ˈfɛnəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 苯化的；用苯酚处理过的 | Treated with phenol; containing or relating to phenol.  Phenol is a powerful disinfectant.  The term often implies sterilization or preservation. |

例句：

The lab technician phenolized the instruments before the surgery. = 实验室技师在手术前用苯酚处理了器械。

The phenolized wood was resistant to decay. = 用苯酚处理过的木材具有抗腐烂性。


近义词： disinfected, sterilized, preserved

反义词： unsterilized, contaminated


词性：形容词
