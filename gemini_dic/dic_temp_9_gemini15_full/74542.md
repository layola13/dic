fratricelli:/ˌfræt.rɪˈtʃɛ.li/| n. |小兄弟会成员；（尤指13-14世纪）贫穷派教徒| A member of a religious sect, especially one of the various groups of radical Franciscans in the 13th and 14th centuries who rejected the authority of the pope and the Church hierarchy, emphasizing absolute poverty and a return to the original spirit of St. Francis of Assisi.|The fratricelli were fiercely independent and often clashed with the established church.|近义词：heretic, dissident|反义词：conformist, orthodox|名词
