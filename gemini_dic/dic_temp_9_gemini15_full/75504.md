fryer:/ˈfraɪər/| n. |油炸锅；油炸器| A fryer is a kitchen appliance used for deep frying food.  It typically contains a large amount of hot oil and a mechanism for keeping the oil at a consistent temperature. |例句：She used a deep fryer to make crispy potato wedges. 她用油炸锅做了脆薯条。|例句：The restaurant's new fryer is much more efficient than the old one. 这家餐馆的新油炸锅比旧的效率高得多。|近义词：deep fat fryer, deep fryer|反义词：boiler, oven
