girl's: /ɡɜːrlz/ | n. | 女孩子的；女孩的 |  This word is the possessive form of the noun "girl," indicating ownership or belonging.  It's used to show that something belongs to a girl.  | 例句：That's the girl's bike. = 那是那个女孩的自行车。


That's a girl's toy. = 那是女孩的玩具。| 近义词：woman's (if referring to a young woman), maiden's | 反义词：boy's, man's | 名词所有格
