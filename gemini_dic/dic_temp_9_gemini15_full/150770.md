paradigms:/ˈpærədaɪmz/ | n. |范例；典范；模式；语法范式 | A paradigm is a typical example or pattern of something; a set of assumptions, concepts, values, and practices that constitutes a way of viewing reality.  In linguistics, it refers to a set of inflected forms of a word. |

例句：
The new paradigm of sustainable energy is gaining traction.  新的可持续能源模式正在获得关注。

The teacher used simple sentences as a paradigm for the students to follow. 老师用简单的句子作为学生模仿的范例。


近义词：model, example, pattern, archetype, exemplar

反义词：anomaly, exception
