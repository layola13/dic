rampantly:/ˈræmpəntli/| adv.| 猖獗地；蔓生地；不受控制地 |  In a wildly uncontrolled way; excessively.  It suggests a rapid and uncontrolled spread or growth, often with negative connotations. |例句：Crime is rampant in the city.  城市里犯罪猖獗。
The weeds were growing rampantly across the garden.  杂草在花园里疯长。|近义词：uncontrollably, wildly, excessively, furiously |反义词：controlledly, moderately, calmly
