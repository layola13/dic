capsule: /ˈkæpsəl/ | n. | 胶囊；太空舱；密封舱 | A small container or case, typically cylindrical, containing medicine or other substances to be swallowed whole.  Also refers to a spacecraft designed to carry people or equipment into space. |例句：The doctor prescribed a course of capsules for my cold.  医生为我的感冒开了胶囊药。|例句：The astronauts landed safely in their capsule. 宇航员安全地降落在他们的太空舱里。|近义词：container, pod, compartment |反义词：N/A
