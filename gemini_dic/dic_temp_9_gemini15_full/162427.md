preadministration: /ˌpriːædmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 预先管理；事前管理 | The act or process of managing or directing something before it officially begins.  It often involves planning, preparation, and resource allocation in anticipation of a project or event. | 例句：The preadministration of the medication ensured the patient's comfort during the procedure. = 术前用药确保了患者在手术过程中的舒适。

The preadministration of funds allowed the project to start on time. = 预先拨款使项目能够按时启动。


近义词：advance preparation, prior management, preliminary planning | 反义词：postadministration, aftermath management | 词性：名词

词源:
The word "preadministration" is a combination of the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") and the noun "administration" (meaning "the process of managing or directing").  Therefore, it literally means "before the administration" or "the management or direction that occurs beforehand".
