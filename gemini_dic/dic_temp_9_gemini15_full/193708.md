self-confining: /ˌsɛlf kənˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. |自我禁闭的，自我限制的 |  Restricting oneself; limiting one's own actions, activities, or opportunities.  | 例句：He imposed a self-confining routine to focus on his studies. 他强迫自己遵循自我禁闭的日常作息来专注于学业。


例句：Her self-confining lifestyle led to feelings of isolation. 她自我禁闭的生活方式导致了孤独感。


近义词：self-imposed, self-restricting, secluded, solitary


反义词：outgoing, extroverted, expansive, unrestrained
