hoarsely:/ˈhɔːrsli/ | adv. |嘶哑地，沙哑地 |  Describes the sound of a voice that is rough and raspy, often due to illness or overuse. | He coughed hoarsely. 他嘶哑地咳嗽。|She spoke hoarsely from shouting. 她喊叫后声音嘶哑。|近义词：raspy, gratingly |反义词：clearly, smoothly
