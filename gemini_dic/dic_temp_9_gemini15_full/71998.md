flings:/flɪŋz/| n., v.|抛，扔；短暂的恋爱；一阵；猛冲|To throw or cast something with a sweeping movement; a brief romantic relationship; a sudden or forceful movement; a vigorous attempt.|例句：He flung the ball across the field. 他用力把球扔过了球场。


例句：They had a brief fling last summer. 他们去年夏天有过一段短暂的恋情。


近义词：toss, throw, hurl, fling (n.), affair, romance|反义词：catch, receive, keep (n.), commitment, lasting relationship|词性:Noun, Verb
