semipeaces:/ˈsemiˌpiːsiz/| n.|半和平状态，局部和平|A state of partial peace; a situation where there is peace in some areas or aspects but not others.  It often implies a fragile or temporary peace, with the potential for conflict to resume.|例句：The ceasefire brought a brief semipeace to the war-torn region.  停火为饱受战争蹂躏的地区带来了短暂的半和平状态。


The fragile semipeace was shattered by renewed fighting.  脆弱的半和平状态被再次爆发的战斗打破了。|近义词：truce, armistice, quiet, calm|反义词：war, conflict, hostility, turmoil|名词
