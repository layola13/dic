puja:/ˈpuːdʒə/| n. |普迦（印度教的祭祀仪式）|A puja is a Hindu ritual of worship, typically involving offerings and prayers to a deity.  It can be performed individually or communally.  |例句：We attended a puja at the temple to celebrate Diwali. 我们去寺庙参加了排灯节的普迦仪式。

例句：The family performed a puja at home to seek blessings for their child's exam.  这个家庭在家中进行了普迦仪式，为孩子的考试祈福。


近义词：worship, ritual, ceremony |反义词：None. |词性：名词
