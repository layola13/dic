lawmakings:/ˈlɔːmeɪkɪŋz/| n.|立法，法律制定|The process of making laws.  It involves drafting, debating, and voting on proposed legislation.  This process varies depending on the political system.|例句：The current lawmakings are focused on environmental protection.  当前的立法工作重点是环境保护。|例句：The complexity of the lawmakings often leads to delays. 立法的复杂性常常导致延误。|近义词：legislation, lawmaking, statute-making|反义词：lawbreaking, lawlessness
