dissatisfiedly:/ˌdɪsˈsætɪsfaɪdli/| adv. |不满地，不满意地 |In a manner showing dissatisfaction or displeasure.  It describes the way someone acts or speaks when they are not content or happy. |He looked dissatisfiedly at the half-finished painting. 他不满地看了看那幅未完成的画。 She grumbled dissatisfiedly about the poor service. 她不满地抱怨服务差。|unhappily, discontentedly, disgruntledly| contentedly, happily, gratefully
