colonize:/ˈkɑːlənaɪz/ /ˈkɒlənaɪz/ | vt. |殖民；拓居；使殖民地化 |To establish a colony or colonies in (a place). To spread over and establish control over (an area).  |例句：The Romans colonized much of Europe. 罗马人殖民了欧洲大部分地区。

The European powers colonized Africa in the 19th century.  19世纪欧洲列强殖民了非洲。


近义词：settle, occupy, inhabit, conquer


反义词：decolonize, liberate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They colonize new lands. 他们殖民新的土地。
The company colonizes the market. 这家公司占据了市场。


一般过去时:
Spain colonized much of South America. 西班牙殖民了南美洲的大部分地区。
The settlers colonized the western frontier. 定居者开拓了西部边疆。


现在完成时:
Europe has colonized vast areas of the world. 欧洲已经殖民了世界的大片地区。
Many countries have been colonized at some point in history. 许多国家在历史上某个时期都曾被殖民。


过去完成时:
The indigenous people had been colonized long before the arrival of Europeans. 在欧洲人到来之前很久，土著人民就已经被殖民了。
The area had been colonized for centuries. 这个地区已经被殖民了几个世纪。


将来时:
Will they colonize Mars in the future? 他们将来会殖民火星吗？
The company will colonize the emerging market. 这家公司将进军新兴市场。


虚拟语气:
If they colonized that area, they would have access to valuable resources. 如果他们殖民那个地区，他们将获得宝贵的资源。
I wish they hadn't colonized our land. 我真希望他们没有殖民我们的土地。


被动语态:
Many parts of the world were colonized by European powers. 世界许多地方都被欧洲列强殖民了。
The new market is being colonized by several large companies. 新兴市场正被几家大公司瓜分。


疑问句:
Did they colonize that island? 他们殖民了那个岛屿吗？
Have they successfully colonized the new market? 他们成功地占领了新市场了吗？


否定句:
They did not colonize the entire continent. 他们没有殖民整个大陆。
The company hasn't yet colonized the target market. 这家公司尚未占领目标市场。
