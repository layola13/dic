discompose:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊz/| vt.|使不安；使心烦意乱；扰乱；分解|To disturb the composure or tranquility of; unsettle; to decompose or decay.|例句：The news discomposed her completely.  这个消息让她彻底不安。|例句：The heat discomposed the delicate pastries. 炎热的天气使精致的糕点变质。|近义词：disturb, upset, unsettle, disquiet, decompose |反义词：calm, soothe, compose, tranquilize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music discomposes me. = 吵闹的音乐让我心烦意乱。
This problem discomposes the whole system. = 这个问题扰乱了整个系统。


一般过去时:
The unexpected news discomposed him greatly. =  意外的消息使他非常不安。
The sudden storm discomposed the carefully planned picnic. = 突如其来的暴风雨扰乱了精心策划的野餐。


现在完成时:
The constant noise has discomposed my concentration. =  持续的噪音已经扰乱了我的注意力。
The prolonged heat has discomposed the food. =  长时间的炎热已经使食物变质。


过去完成时:
He had been discomposed by the earlier argument. = 他已经被早些时候的争吵弄得心烦意乱了。
The structure had been discomposed by the earthquake. =  建筑物已经被地震破坏了。


将来时:
The announcement will discompose many people. =  这个公告将会使许多人不安。
The changing weather will discompose the delicate balance of the ecosystem. =  变化无常的天气将会扰乱生态系统的微妙平衡。


虚拟语气:
If the news were to discompose him, he wouldn't show it. = 如果这个消息让他不安，他也不会表现出来。
I wish the noise wouldn't discompose my sleep. =  我希望噪音不会扰乱我的睡眠。


被动语态:
He was discomposed by the criticism. = 他被批评弄得心烦意乱。
The cake was discomposed by the high temperature. = 蛋糕被高温破坏了。


疑问句:
Will this news discompose the market? = 这个消息会扰乱市场吗？
Does the heat discompose the paint? =  高温会损坏油漆吗？


否定句:
The situation did not discompose him at all. =  这种情况丝毫没有让他不安。
The cold storage prevented the food from being discomposed. = 冷藏防止了食物的变质。
