paralyses:/ˈpærəlˌaɪsɪz/| n.|麻痹；瘫痪；使无力；|The plural form of paralysis, referring to multiple instances of paralysis or the state of being paralyzed in multiple areas.|The disease caused paralyses in his legs and arms.  这种疾病导致了他的腿和胳膊瘫痪。  The multiple paralyses resulting from the stroke left him completely debilitated.中风导致的多处瘫痪使他完全虚弱无力。|paralysis, palsy|mobility, function|n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Paralysis affects many people. = 瘫痪影响着许多人。
The disease paralyses the muscles. = 这种疾病使肌肉瘫痪。


一般过去时:
The accident paralysed him from the waist down. = 那次事故使他从腰部以下瘫痪。
The fear paralysed her with terror. = 恐惧使她吓得僵住了。


现在完成时:
The stroke has paralysed his left side. = 中风使他的左侧瘫痪了。
The cold has paralysed my fingers. = 寒冷使我的手指麻木了。


过去完成时:
The shock had paralysed him before he could react. = 震惊使他在还没来得及反应之前就瘫痪了。
She had been paralysed by indecision for weeks. = 她几周来一直被犹豫不决所束缚。


将来时:
The doctor fears the injury will paralyse him permanently. = 医生担心这次受伤会使他永久性瘫痪。
Exposure to the cold will paralyse your limbs. = 暴露在寒冷中会使你的四肢麻痹。


虚拟语气:
If the toxin had been injected, it would have paralysed him instantly. = 如果注射了毒素，它会立即使他瘫痪。
If I were paralysed, I would need constant care. = 如果我瘫痪了，我需要持续的照顾。


被动语态:
His arm was paralysed by the gunshot wound. = 他的手臂因枪伤而瘫痪。
Her movements were paralysed by fear. = 她的动作被恐惧所束缚。


疑问句:
Is he paralysed? = 他瘫痪了吗？
Was she paralysed by the cold? = 她被冻僵了吗？


否定句:
The treatment did not paralyse him. = 治疗并没有使他瘫痪。
The cold did not paralyse her completely. = 寒冷并没有使她完全麻痹。
