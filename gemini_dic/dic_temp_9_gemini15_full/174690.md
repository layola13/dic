quotas:/ˈkwoʊtəz/| n.|配额，限额|A quota is a limited quantity of something that is officially allowed.|The company has quotas for each sales representative.|近义词：limit, allowance, allocation|反义词：unlimited, unrestricted

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory meets its production quotas every month. = 工厂每个月都完成生产配额。
Our sales team consistently exceeds its monthly quotas. = 我们的销售团队始终超过月度配额。

一般过去时:
Last year, we failed to meet our export quotas. = 去年，我们未能达到出口配额。
The government imposed strict quotas on imported goods. = 政府对进口商品实行严格配额限制。

现在完成时:
They have already reached their quotas for this quarter. = 他们本季度已经达到配额了。
The company has exceeded its sales quotas for three consecutive years. = 该公司连续三年超过销售配额。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, we had already surpassed our sales quotas. = 到年底，我们已经超过了销售配额。
Before the new policy, they had never met their production quotas. = 在新政策出台之前，他们从未达到过生产配额。

将来时:
Next year, we will increase our production quotas. = 明年，我们将增加我们的生产配额。
The company anticipates exceeding its quotas in the next fiscal year. = 公司预计下个财政年度将超过配额。

虚拟语气:
If we had higher quotas, we could produce more. = 如果我们的配额更高，我们可以生产更多。
I wish we had set more realistic quotas at the beginning. = 我希望我们一开始就设定更现实的配额。

被动语态:
Production quotas are set by the management team. = 生产配额由管理团队设定。
Import quotas are often revised to adjust to market conditions. = 进口配额经常被修改以适应市场条件。

疑问句:
What are the quotas for this project? = 这个项目的配额是多少？
Have the quotas for this year been determined yet? = 今年的配额确定了吗？

否定句:
The factory did not meet its production quotas last month. = 工厂上个月没有完成生产配额。
We did not exceed our sales quotas this quarter. = 我们本季度没有超过销售配额。
