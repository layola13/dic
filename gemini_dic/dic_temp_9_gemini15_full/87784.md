hibernated:/ˈhaɪbərˌneɪtɪd/ | v. |冬眠；蛰伏；停止活动|  To spend the winter in a dormant state, characterized by slowed metabolism and reduced activity.  This term is often used for animals, but can also be applied metaphorically to people or organizations. |例句：The bear hibernated in its cave all winter.  熊整个冬天都在洞穴里冬眠。|例句：The project hibernated for several months due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目搁置了几个月。|近义词：dormant, inactive, dormant, quiescent |反义词：active, awake, bustling
