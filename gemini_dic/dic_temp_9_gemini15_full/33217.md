chronicling:/ˈkrɒnɪklɪŋ/|/ˈkrɑːnɪklɪŋ/| v. |编年史；记录；记载 |The act of recording events in chronological order, especially in a detailed or narrative way.  It implies a systematic and often detailed account, often over a significant period.|例句：The historian spent years chronicling the lives of the royal family. = 这位历史学家花了数年时间记录皇室成员的生活。

The author is chronicling the social changes of the 20th century. = 作者正在记录20世纪的社会变革。|近义词：recording, documenting, narrating, recounting |反义词：forgetting, ignoring, overlooking |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chronicles the events of the war. = 他记录着战争的事件。
She chronicles her daily life in a blog. = 她在一个博客上记录她的日常生活。


一般过去时:
The monk chronicled the history of the monastery. = 这位僧侣记录了寺院的历史。
They chronicled their journey across the country. = 他们记录了他们穿越全国的旅程。


现在完成时:
She has chronicled the rise and fall of several empires. = 她已经记录了几个帝国的兴衰。
He has chronicled the changing landscape of the city for decades. = 几十年来，他一直在记录这座城市不断变化的景象。


过去完成时:
The historian had chronicled the events leading up to the revolution. = 这位历史学家已经记录了导致革命发生的事件。
Before writing the book, she had chronicled her experiences in a journal. = 在写这本书之前，她已经把她当时的经历记录在日记里。


将来时:
He will chronicle the development of artificial intelligence. = 他将记录人工智能的发展。
The journalist will chronicle the upcoming election. = 这位记者将记录即将到来的选举。


虚拟语气:
If I had a time machine, I would chronicle the past. = 如果我有一台时光机，我会记录过去。
I wish I could chronicle the lives of my ancestors. = 我希望我能记录我祖先的生活。


被动语态:
The events of the year were chronicled in the newspaper. = 这年的事件被记录在报纸上。
The history of the company is being chronicled in a new book. = 这家公司历史正在一本新书中被记录。


疑问句:
How will you chronicle the progress of the project? = 你将如何记录项目的进展？
What methods will she use to chronicle the social unrest? = 她将使用什么方法来记录社会动荡？


否定句:
He did not chronicle the details of the meeting. = 他没有记录会议的细节。
They chose not to chronicle their personal struggles. = 他们选择不记录他们的个人挣扎。


PHRASE->
chronicle the history of = 记录...的历史
chronicle the events of = 记录...的事件
chronicle the life of = 记录...的生活
faithfully chronicle = 忠实地记录
accurately chronicle = 准确地记录

词源:
源于古法语“cronique”，最终源于希腊语“chronos”（时间）。  这意味着“chronicling”这个词的根源在于对时间的记录和编排。
