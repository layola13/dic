heartfulness:/ˈhɑːrtfʊlnəs/ | n. | 心灵的充实；心地善良；真诚；专注 | The quality of being heartfelt and sincere;  a state of deep inner peace and connection. It emphasizes a mindful approach to life, characterized by compassion, self-awareness, and a sense of connection with something larger than oneself.  | 例句：He approached the task with great heartfulness. 他以极大的专注投入到这项任务中。

例句：Her heartfulness was evident in her kind actions.  她的善良行为体现了她的真诚。


近义词：sincerity, compassion, mindfulness, devotion, earnestness

反义词：callousness, insincerity, apathy, indifference, heartlessness
