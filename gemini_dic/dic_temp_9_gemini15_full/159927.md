polyphagy:/pɒˈlɪfədʒi/| n. |多食性；杂食性 |The ability of an organism to feed on a wide variety of different foods.  This is in contrast to monophagy (feeding on only one type of food) and oligophagy (feeding on a few types of food).|例句：The polyphagy of the cockroach allows it to survive in diverse environments. 蟑螂的多食性使其能够在不同的环境中生存。
The polyphagy observed in this insect species contributes to its wide distribution.  这种昆虫的多食性有助于其广泛分布。|近义词：omnivory, generalism |反义词：monophagy, oligophagy


词源:

* **poly-**:  from Greek πολύς (polús), meaning "much," "many," or "several."
* **-phagy**: from Greek φαγεῖν (phagein), meaning "to eat."

因此，polyphagy字面意思是“吃很多东西”。
