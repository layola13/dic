familiarise:/fəˈmɪliəraɪz/ | vt. |使熟悉；使了解 |To make someone familiar with something.  |例句：The course aims to familiarise students with the latest research. 这门课程旨在让学生熟悉最新的研究成果。|例句：We need to familiarise ourselves with the new software before we start using it. 我们需要在开始使用新软件之前熟悉它。|近义词：acquaint, introduce, accustom |反义词：alienate, estrange


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I familiarise myself with the rules before the game. = 我在比赛前熟悉规则。
He familiarises his students with the new technology. = 他让他的学生熟悉这项新技术。

一般过去时:
She familiarised herself with the city map before her trip. = 她旅行前熟悉了城市地图。
They familiarised the audience with the project's background. = 他们让观众了解了项目的背景。

现在完成时:
I have familiarised myself with the company's policies. = 我已经熟悉了公司的政策。
She has familiarised herself with the new software. = 她已经熟悉了新的软件。

过去完成时:
He had familiarised himself with the subject before the debate. = 他在辩论前已经熟悉了这个主题。
They had familiarised the team with the strategy before the launch. = 他们在启动前已经让团队熟悉了策略。

将来时:
We will familiarise ourselves with the new system next week. = 我们下周将熟悉新系统。
They will familiarise the employees with the updated procedures. = 他们将使员工熟悉更新后的程序。

虚拟语气:
If I familiarised myself with the instructions, I would have been able to assemble the furniture. = 如果我熟悉说明书，我就能组装家具了。
It is important that he familiarise himself with the safety regulations. =  重要的是他应该熟悉安全条例。

被动语态:
The students were familiarised with the historical context. = 学生们对历史背景进行了学习。

疑问句:

否定句:
