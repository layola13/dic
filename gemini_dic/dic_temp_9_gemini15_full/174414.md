quiets:/ˈkwaɪəts/| v.|使安静；使平静；|to make or become quiet or still|The teacher quieted the noisy children.老师使吵闹的孩子们安静下来。|近义词：calm, hush, still, silence|反义词：agitate, excite, rouse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quiets the baby with a lullaby. = 他用摇篮曲哄婴儿睡觉。
She always quiets down after a long day. = 她漫长的一天后总是平静下来。


一般过去时:
The rain quieted the wind. = 雨使风平静下来。
He quieted his anxieties with deep breaths. = 他通过深呼吸平息了他的焦虑。


现在完成时:
The storm has quieted down. = 暴风雨已经平息了。
The protesters have quieted their voices. = 抗议者们已经安静下来了。


过去完成时:
The audience had quieted before the speaker began. = 演讲者开始之前，观众已经安静下来了。
She had quieted her fears before entering the room. = 她进入房间前已经平息了恐惧。


将来时:
The medicine will quiet the pain. = 这药会止痛。
He hopes the situation will quiet down soon. = 他希望局势很快平静下来。


虚拟语气:
If only the wind would quiet! = 但愿风能停下来！
I wish the noise would quiet down. = 我希望噪音能小一些。


被动语态:
The angry crowd was quieted by the police. = 愤怒的人群被警察平息了。
The room was quieted by the soft music. = 柔和的音乐使房间安静下来。


疑问句:

否定句:
