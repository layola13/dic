/ˌpriːdɪˈplɒməsiz/ | n. | 预先外交；事前外交 |  The actions and communications undertaken by states before formal diplomatic relations are established, often involving informal contacts and negotiations to lay the groundwork for future agreements or collaborations.  It aims to prevent conflicts and build trust. | The prediplomacies between the two nations paved the way for a lasting peace treaty.  = 这两个国家之间的预先外交为持久和平条约铺平了道路。  The prediplomacies surrounding the trade deal were complex and involved many secret meetings. = 围绕贸易协议的预先外交非常复杂，涉及许多秘密会议。 |  Preliminary negotiations, back-channel diplomacy |  Formal diplomacy |  n.
