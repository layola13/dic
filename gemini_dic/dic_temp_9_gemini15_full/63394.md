entourages:/ˈɑːntʊrɑːʒ/| n. |随行人员；随从；(明星等的) 随行人员 |A group of people attending or accompanying an important person.  |例句：The singer arrived with his entourage. 歌手带着他的随行人员到达了。

例句：The president's entourage included several advisors and security personnel.总统的随行人员包括几位顾问和安保人员。


近义词：retinue, company, following |反义词：solitude, isolation |词性:名词
