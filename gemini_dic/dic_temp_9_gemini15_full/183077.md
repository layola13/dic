responders: /rɪˈspɒndərz/ | n. | 应答者，响应者 | People or things that respond.  Those who answer or react to a stimulus, question, or request. For example, in emergency services, responders are those who answer emergency calls and provide assistance. In a survey, responders are those who complete and submit the survey. | Example: The emergency responders arrived quickly and efficiently.  紧急救援人员迅速有效地到达了现场。


The first responders saved many lives.  第一批救援人员挽救了许多生命。


近义词：reactors, answerers, participants


反义词：ignores (in the context of not responding)


词性: 名词
