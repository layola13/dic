counterfoils:/ˈkaʊntərfɔɪlz/ | n. | 存根；副本 | Detachable parts of a document, such as a cheque or ticket, that provide a record of the original.  These are often kept for accounting or reference purposes. |  The bank teller carefully detached the counterfoils from the chequebook.  银行出纳员仔细地从支票簿上撕下存根。


The counterfoils of the raffle tickets were collected to verify the number of tickets sold.  彩票的存根被收集起来以核实已售彩票的数量。


近义词：stubs, duplicates, copies | 反义词：originals
