myosis:/maɪˈoʊsɪs/| n.|瞳孔缩小|The constriction of the pupil of the eye.  This is a natural physiological response to bright light or near vision.  It can also be caused by certain medications or medical conditions.|例句：The doctor noticed the patient's myosis, indicating a possible neurological problem.  医生注意到病人的瞳孔缩小，这表明可能存在神经系统问题。|近义词：pupillary constriction|反义词：mydriasis
