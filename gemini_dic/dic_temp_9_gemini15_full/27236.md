calms:/kɑːmz/ | n. v. |平静；使平静 |The calming effect of the music soothed her nerves. 音乐的平静效果抚慰了她的神经。|近义词：tranquilizes, soothes, pacifies |反义词：agitates, excites, disturbs


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sea calms down at night. = 海晚上平静下来。
The medication calms her anxiety. = 药物减轻了她的焦虑。


一般过去时:
The storm calmed quickly. = 暴风雨很快就平息了。
He calmed the crying baby. = 他使哭闹的婴儿平静下来。

过去完成时:
