singlets:/ˈsɪŋɡləts/| n. |紧身衣，单件内衣|A singlet is a close-fitting garment, typically sleeveless and worn under other clothing.  It's often made of cotton or a synthetic fabric.|例句：He wore a white singlet under his shirt. 他衬衫里面穿着一件白色紧身衣。|例句：She bought a pack of cotton singlets for the summer. 她买了一包棉质紧身衣过夏天。|近义词：undershirt, vest (US), vest top (UK)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with layered clothing.
