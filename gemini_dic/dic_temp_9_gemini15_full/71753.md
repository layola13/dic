flea: /fliː/ | n. |跳蚤 | A small, wingless insect that feeds on the blood of mammals and birds.  Fleas are known for their ability to jump great distances relative to their size and can transmit diseases. | Example:  The dog was covered in fleas. 这只狗身上长满了跳蚤。

The cat had a flea infestation. 这只猫得了跳蚤病。| 近义词: louse, parasite | 反义词: N/A
