glitter:/ˈɡlɪtər/| n., v.|闪光；闪耀；装饰…以闪光物|The quality of shining with light reflected from many small surfaces.  To shine with light reflected from many small surfaces; sparkle. To decorate with something that glitters.|例句：The glitter of the diamonds caught my eye. 钻石的光芒吸引了我的目光。

The children's eyes glittered with excitement. 孩子们的眼睛闪耀着兴奋的光芒。|近义词：sparkle, shimmer, gleam|反义词：dullness, drabness


USAGE->

The disco ball glitters brightly.  = 迪斯科球闪闪发光。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
