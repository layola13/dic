nonnutritiousness:/ˌnɒnnuːˈtrɪʃəsnəs/| n. |非营养性，缺乏营养|The nonnutritiousness of the diet led to health problems.  这种饮食缺乏营养，导致了健康问题。|近义词：lack of nutrition, unwholesomeness|反义词：nutritiousness


USAGE->
This word is a noun and does not conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense, voice, and mood are not applicable.  We can, however, demonstrate its use in different sentence structures.


例句1:  The study highlighted the nonnutritiousness of processed foods. 这项研究强调了加工食品缺乏营养。
例句2:  Concerns about the nonnutritiousness of the snacks prompted a change in the school's menu. 对零食缺乏营养的担忧促使学校改变了菜单。


PHRASE->
This word is not commonly used in phrases.  It's primarily used as a single noun to describe a lack of nutritive value.  Examples of phrases where the concept might be expressed include:

lack of nutritive value: 缺乏营养价值
poor nutritional content: 营养成分差
low in nutrients: 营养成分低
deficient in essential nutrients: 缺乏必需营养素
unhealthy diet: 不健康的饮食 (This is a broader term, but often implies nonnutritiousness)
