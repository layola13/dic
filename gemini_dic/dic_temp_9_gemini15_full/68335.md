fall-on:/fɔːl ˈɒn/|/ˈfɑːl ɑːn/| vt.|倒下；落在；袭击；突然降临 | to become affected by a feeling or event | The full weight of the news fell on her.  消息的全部重量落在了她身上。 | The enemy suddenly fell on our position. 敌人突然袭击了我们的阵地。| befall, strike, descend | rise, ascend  |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The rain falls on the roof. = 雨落在屋顶上。
The responsibility falls on me. = 责任落在我身上。


一般过去时:
The tree fell on the car. = 树倒在了车上。
The news fell on him like a ton of bricks. = 这个消息对他来说如同晴天霹雳。


现在完成时:
The weight of the world has fallen on her shoulders. = 全世界的重担都压在了她的肩上。
Many misfortunes have fallen on our family. = 许多不幸降临到了我们家。


过去完成时:
The decision had fallen on him before the meeting. = 在会议之前，决定已经落在了他身上。
The news had fallen on deaf ears. = 这个消息没有引起任何注意。


将来时:
The responsibility will fall on the next generation. = 责任将落在下一代身上。
The full force of the storm will fall on the coast. = 暴风雨的全部威力将袭击海岸。


虚拟语气:
If the responsibility fell on me, I would accept it. = 如果责任落在我身上，我会接受。
If the meteor fell on the city, it would cause great destruction. = 如果陨石落到城市，将会造成巨大的破坏。


被动语态:
The city was fallen on by the enemy. = 这个城市被敌人袭击了。
The decision was fallen on by the committee. = 委员会做出了决定。


疑问句:
Where did the tree fall on? = 树倒在哪里了？
When will the responsibility fall on me? = 责任什么时候会落到我身上？


否定句:
The responsibility did not fall on him. = 责任没有落在他身上。
The rain did not fall on the parade. = 雨没有下在游行队伍上。



PHRASE->
