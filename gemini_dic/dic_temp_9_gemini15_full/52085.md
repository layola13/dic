diamagnetic:/ˌdaɪæmæɡˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 逆磁性的，反磁性的 | Exhibiting diamagnetism; repelled by a magnetic field.  Diamagnetic materials are weakly repelled by a magnetic field. This is in contrast to paramagnetic and ferromagnetic materials, which are attracted to a magnetic field.  | Example:  Bismuth is a diamagnetic material. 铋是一种反磁性材料。


Example: Water is a weakly diamagnetic substance. 水是一种弱反磁性物质。

近义词:non-ferromagnetic (in some contexts)

反义词:paramagnetic, ferromagnetic

词性:形容词
