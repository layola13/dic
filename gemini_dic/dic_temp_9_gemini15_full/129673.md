/ˌnɒnˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/ /ˌnɑːnˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 非民主的；反民主的 | Not characterized by or consistent with democratic principles; lacking democratic processes or institutions.  It refers to systems or governments that do not allow for free and fair elections, citizen participation, and protection of basic human rights. |


The government's non-democratic policies led to widespread protests.  = 政府的反民主政策导致了大规模抗议。

The country has a long history of non-democratic rule. = 这个国家有着漫长的非民主统治历史。


近义词：authoritarian, totalitarian, dictatorial, undemocratic


反义词：democratic


词性：形容词
