outsider:/ˈaʊtsaɪdər/| n. |局外人，门外汉|A person who is not involved in or accepted by a particular group.|例句：He felt like an outsider in the exclusive club. 他在那个独家俱乐部里感觉像个局外人。|例句：She's an outsider in this field, but her innovative ideas are gaining recognition. 她是这个领域的局外人，但她的创新理念正在获得认可。|近义词：stranger, newcomer, alien|反义词：insider, member
