copyhold:/ˈkɒpihoʊld/ /ˈkɑːpihoʊld/ | n. | 复制保有权；依复制保有权而持有的土地 |  Land held by copyhold tenure.  This is a form of land tenure in which the tenant holds land according to the customs of the manor, and the terms of the tenure are recorded in the manor court rolls.  The tenant's rights are evidenced by a copy of the court roll. | The farmer held his land by copyhold.  这个农民以复制保有权持有他的土地。|  freehold, leasehold | freehold | 名词
