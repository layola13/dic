caribou:/ˈkærɪbuː/| n. |北美驯鹿|A large deer of the North American Arctic and subarctic regions, with large antlers in both sexes.  Caribou are known for their migratory patterns and their importance to indigenous cultures.|Example:  The caribou herd migrated across the tundra.  这群驯鹿迁移穿过苔原。
The indigenous people relied on caribou for food and clothing.  土著居民依靠驯鹿获取食物和衣物。|近义词：reindeer (though technically distinct)|反义词：无
