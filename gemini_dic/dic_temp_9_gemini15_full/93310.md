ill-equipped:/ˌɪlɪˈkwɪpt/| adj. |装备不足的，准备不足的 |Lacking the necessary equipment or supplies.  It implies a deficiency in resources or preparation for a task or situation. |例句：The expedition was ill-equipped for the harsh winter conditions. 探险队对严酷的冬季条件准备不足。The firefighters were ill-equipped to handle the massive blaze. 消防员无力应对大火。|近义词：underprepared, unprepared, insufficiently equipped |反义词：well-equipped, prepared, ready

一般过去时:
