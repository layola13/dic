digraph:/ˈdaɪɡræf/| n. |双字母组合，双字母音位|A digraph is a combination of two letters that represents a single sound.  For example, 'sh' in 'ship' and 'ch' in 'chair' are digraphs.|The word "ship" contains the digraph "sh".|近义词：double letter combination|反义词:monograph
