rectify:/ˈrɛktɪfaɪ/ | vt. |矫正；纠正；调整；改进 |To make or set right; correct.  |例句：We need to rectify the situation immediately. 我们需要立即纠正这种情况。 |例句：The government is trying to rectify the economic imbalance. 政府正在努力纠正经济失衡。|近义词：correct, amend, adjust, improve |反义词：worsen, exacerbate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rectifies mistakes diligently. = 他勤勉地纠正错误。
The technician rectifies the faulty equipment. = 技师正在修理有故障的设备。

一般过去时:
She rectified the error in the report. = 她纠正了报告中的错误。
The company rectified the problem with the software. = 公司纠正了软件的问题。

现在完成时:
They have rectified the misunderstanding. = 他们已经澄清了误解。
We have rectified the faulty wiring. = 我们已经纠正了错误的线路连接。

过去完成时:
He had rectified the problem before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经解决了这个问题。
She had rectified the mistake in the document. = 她已经在文件中纠正了错误。

将来时:
They will rectify the situation as soon as possible. = 他们将尽快纠正这种情况。
The engineers will rectify the malfunctioning system. = 工程师们将修理故障系统。

虚拟语气:
If only he had rectified his behaviour earlier. = 如果他早点改正他的行为就好了。
I wish they would rectify the injustice. = 我希望他们能纠正这种不公正。

被动语态:
The error was rectified promptly. = 错误很快得到了纠正。
The situation will be rectified in due course. = 情况将在适当的时候得到纠正。

疑问句:
Can this problem be rectified easily? = 这个问题容易解决吗？
How can we rectify the damage? = 我们如何弥补损失？

否定句:
He did not rectify his mistake. = 他没有纠正他的错误。
They failed to rectify the situation. = 他们未能纠正这种情况。


PHRASE->

词源:
From Latin *rectificāre, from rectus ("right, straight") + -ficāre (a verb-forming suffix denoting "to make").  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *reg- ("to rule, to lead straight").
