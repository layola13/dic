foreshadowed:/fɔːrˈʃædəʊd/| v. |预示，预兆|To indicate or suggest beforehand; to prefigure.|The dark clouds foreshadowed a storm.  乌云预示着一场暴风雨。 The author foreshadowed the tragic ending in the first chapter. 作者在第一章就预示了悲剧的结局。|预示，预兆，暗示|无|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The writer foreshadows the ending in the prologue. = 作者在序言中预示了结局。
These events foreshadow a major conflict. = 这些事件预示着一场重大冲突。


一般过去时:
The dream foreshadowed her future success. = 这个梦预示了她未来的成功。
The clues foreshadowed the detective's solution. = 线索预示了侦探的解决方案。


现在完成时:
The news reports have foreshadowed a difficult economic year. = 新闻报道预示了艰难的经济年景。
Recent events have foreshadowed a change in government policy. = 最近发生的事件预示着政府政策的变化。


过去完成时:
The ancient prophecy had foreshadowed the king's downfall. = 古代预言预示了国王的垮台。
Her earlier actions had foreshadowed her current predicament. = 她之前的行为预示了她目前的困境。


将来时:
The current trends will foreshadow a technological revolution. = 当前的趋势将预示着一场技术革命。
The upcoming elections will foreshadow the political landscape for years to come. = 即将来临的选举将预示着未来几年的政治格局。


虚拟语气:
If only we had heeded the signs that foreshadowed the disaster. = 如果我们能注意到预示灾难的迹象就好了。
If the signs had been clearer, they would have better foreshadowed the crisis. = 如果迹象更明显，他们就能更好地预示这场危机。


被动语态:
The impending war was foreshadowed by increased military activity. = 即将到来的战争因军事活动增加而被预示。
The hero's tragic fate was foreshadowed throughout the novel. = 英雄悲惨的命运在小说中得到了预示。


疑问句:
Does this event foreshadow a larger problem? = 这件事是否预示着更大的问题？
What does this symbol foreshadow in the story? = 这个象征在故事中预示着什么？


否定句:
The initial signs did not foreshadow the magnitude of the earthquake. = 最初的迹象并没有预示地震的规模。
Nothing foreshadowed the sudden turn of events. = 没有什么预示着事态的突然转变。
