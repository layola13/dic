harmless:/ˈhɑːrmləs/ | adj. | 无害的，无恶意的 | Not causing or likely to cause any harm; innocent. |例句：The dog seemed harmless enough.  这只狗看起来很温顺。

The snake is harmless to humans.这条蛇对人类无害。

近义词：innocent, safe, benign, innocuous | 反义词：harmful, dangerous, perilous, noxious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chemical is harmless to the environment.  这种化学物质对环境无害。
This game is harmless fun for children. 这个游戏对孩子们来说是无害的乐趣。

一般过去时:
The incident was harmless, but still alarming.  事件是无害的，但仍然令人担忧。
He thought his joke was harmless, but it offended some people. 他认为他的笑话是无害的，但它冒犯了一些人。

现在完成时:
The experiment has proven harmless to the test subjects. 实验已被证明对受试者无害。
The medicine has been harmless for most patients.  这种药对大多数病人都是无害的。

过去完成时:
The scientists had determined the substance was harmless before the experiment.  科学家们在实验前已经确定该物质是无害的。
We had assumed the situation was harmless, but we were wrong. 我们曾认为情况是无害的，但我们错了。

将来时:
The new policy will hopefully be harmless to small businesses. 新政策有望对小型企业无害。
The results of the test will show whether the substance is harmless or not. 测试结果将显示该物质是否有害。

虚拟语气:
If the substance were harmless, we would use it more widely. 如果该物质是无害的，我们将更广泛地使用它。
I wish the effects of the medication were harmless. 我希望药物的副作用是无害的。

被动语态:
The product was declared harmless by the regulatory agency. 该产品被监管机构宣布为无害。
The substance is considered harmless by most experts.  大多数专家认为该物质是无害的。

疑问句:
Is the chemical harmless to animals?  这种化学物质对动物无害吗？
Are the ingredients in this product harmless?  该产品中的成分无害吗？

否定句:
The chemical is not harmless to the environment.  这种化学物质对环境有害。
His actions were not harmless; they caused considerable damage.  他的行为并非无害；它们造成了相当大的损失。

词源:
The word "harmless" is derived from "harm" + "-less,"  "-less" being a suffix indicating "without" or "free from."  "Harm" itself has Old English roots, ultimately stemming from Proto-Germanic.
