ratifying: /ˈrætɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 批准；确认；使正式生效 |  The act of giving formal consent to a treaty, contract, or agreement, making it officially valid.  | The Senate is currently ratifying the new trade agreement. 参议院目前正在批准新的贸易协定。


The government is ratifying the environmental protection law. 政府正在批准环境保护法。

近义词:approving, confirming, sanctioning, validating | 反义词:rejecting, annulling, invalidating | 动词
