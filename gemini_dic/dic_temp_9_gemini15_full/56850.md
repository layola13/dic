dorse:/dɔːrs/| n.|背书；(书的)背面|The back of something, especially a book or document.  It can also refer to the act of endorsing or supporting something.|例句：He wrote his name on the dorse of the check. 他在支票背面签了名。
例句：The dorse of the book contained important information. 书的背面包含重要信息。|近义词：back, reverse|反义词：front, obverse|名词
