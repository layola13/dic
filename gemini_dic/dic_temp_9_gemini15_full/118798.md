metalworkers:/ˈmɛtəlˌwɜːrkərz/ | n. |金属工人|People who work with metal, shaping, forming, or joining it to create various objects.  They may specialize in different metalworking techniques like forging, welding, casting, or machining. |The metalworkers skillfully crafted the intricate iron gates. 金属工人熟练地制作了复杂的铁门。  The skilled metalworkers were able to repair the damaged engine block. 熟练的金属工人能够修理损坏的发动机缸体。| blacksmiths, machinists, welders, fabricators | (no direct antonyms, but could be contrasted with e.g.,  software developers, textile workers)|名词
