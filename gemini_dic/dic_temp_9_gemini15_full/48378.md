decussation:/ˌdɛkəˈseɪʃən/| n. |交叉；交叉现象|The crossing of a nerve fiber from one side of the body to the other.  Decussation is a crucial process in the nervous system, allowing for the coordination of movement and sensory information between the two hemispheres of the brain.|Example: The optic nerves undergo decussation at the optic chiasm.  视神经在视交叉处发生交叉。|近义词：crossing, intersection, interchange|反义词：convergence (in some contexts)
