preclude:/prɪˈkluːd/| vt.|阻止；排除；妨碍|To prevent something from happening; to make something impossible.|The heavy rain precluded a picnic.  大雨阻止了野餐。|The prior engagement precluded his attendance.  之前的约会使他无法出席。|permit, allow|allow, permit|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy rain precludes a picnic. = 大雨阻止了野餐。
This rule precludes any possibility of cheating. = 这条规则排除了任何作弊的可能性。

一般过去时:
The storm precluded our trip to the beach. = 暴风雨阻止了我们去海滩的旅行。
His illness precluded him from attending the meeting. = 他的病阻止了他参加会议。

现在完成时:
His injury has precluded him from playing in the game. = 他的伤势使他无法参加比赛。
The new regulations have precluded further development of the project. = 新规章阻止了该项目的进一步发展。

过去完成时:
The previous commitment had precluded her from accepting the invitation. = 此前的承诺使她无法接受邀请。
A sudden illness had precluded his participation in the marathon. = 突然的疾病使他无法参加马拉松比赛。

将来时:
The bad weather will likely preclude any outdoor activities. = 恶劣的天气可能会阻止任何户外活动。
A scheduling conflict will preclude her from attending the conference. = 时间冲突将使她无法参加会议。

虚拟语气:
If the snow didn't preclude travel, we would have gone skiing. = 如果雪不阻止旅行，我们就去滑雪了。
I wish that financial constraints didn't preclude me from pursuing my dream. = 我希望经济限制不会阻止我追求我的梦想。

被动语态:
Our plans were precluded by the unexpected storm. = 我们的计划被突如其来的暴风雨打乱了。
His participation in the project was precluded by his lack of experience. = 由于缺乏经验，他被排除在该项目之外。

疑问句:
Does the contract preclude us from subcontracting the work? = 合同是否禁止我们分包工作？
Would his busy schedule preclude him from attending the dinner? = 他繁忙的日程会阻止他参加晚宴吗？

否定句:
Nothing will preclude us from achieving our goals. = 没有什么能够阻止我们实现目标。
The lack of funds did not preclude the project from going ahead. = 资金短缺并没有阻止项目继续进行。
