reincurred:/ˌriːɪnˈkɜːr/ | v. |再次遭受；重新招致 | To incur again; to suffer or experience again.  |例句：The company reincurred losses after the new product launch failed. 公司在新产品发布失败后再次遭受损失。

例句：After recovering from the illness, she reincurred the same symptoms a few weeks later. 在从疾病中恢复后，几周后她再次出现相同的症状。


近义词：re-experience,  suffer again,  incur anew | 反义词：avoid, prevent, escape | 词性：动词
