modestness:/ˈmɑːdəstnis/| n. |谦逊；适度；稳重|The quality of being modest; freedom from vanity, boastfulness, or arrogance; humility.  It also refers to something of moderate size, extent, or amount.|例句：Her modestness prevented her from boasting about her achievements.  她的谦逊使她无法夸耀自己的成就。

例句：The modestness of the apartment surprised us; it was much smaller than we expected.  公寓的简朴让我们吃了一惊；它比我们预期的要小得多。


近义词：humility, unpretentiousness, self-effacement, meekness

反义词：arrogance, vanity, boastfulness, ostentation

词源：源自于中古英语 *modestié，来自古法语 modestie，最终源于拉丁语 modestus，“适度地；谦虚地”，来自 modus，“方式；程度”。
