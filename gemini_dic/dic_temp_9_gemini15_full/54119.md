disenchant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃɑːnt/| vt.|使不再着迷；使醒悟；使幻灭|To free from enchantment; to disillusion; to deprive of illusion or charm.  |例句：His experiences in the city disenchanted him with the idea of living there.  他的城市经历使他不再迷恋住在那里。  例句：The film disenchanted many viewers with its unrealistic plot.这部电影以其不现实的情节使许多观众感到幻灭。|近义词：disillusion, disillusionize, disenthrall|反义词：enchant, bewitch, fascinate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disenchants his followers with the truth. = 他用真相使他的追随者们醒悟。
The magician disenchants the princess. = 魔术师解除了公主的魔法。

一般过去时:
The harsh reality disenchanted her of her dreams. = 现实的残酷使她对梦想感到幻灭。
The experience disenchanted him with politics. = 那次经历使他对政治感到失望。


现在完成时:
His cynicism has disenchanted me with the human race. = 他的愤世嫉俗让我对人类感到失望。
Travel has disenchanted her with the romanticized idea of Paris. = 旅行让她对巴黎浪漫化的印象破灭了。


过去完成时:
She had been disenchanted with her job long before she quit. = 在她辞职很久之前，她就对她的工作感到失望了。
He had been disenchanted with the company's policies for years. = 多年来，他对公司的政策一直感到失望。


将来时:
This news will disenchant many of his supporters. = 这个消息将会使他的许多支持者感到失望。
The truth will eventually disenchant them. = 真相最终会使他们醒悟。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the truth earlier, I would not have been so disenchanted. = 如果我早知道真相，我就不会这么失望了。
If only I hadn't been so naive, I wouldn't have been so disenchanted. = 要是我当初没有那么天真就好了，我就不会那么失望了。


被动语态:
Many were disenchanted by the government's response to the crisis. = 许多人对政府应对危机的反应感到失望。
His followers were disenchanted by his betrayal. = 他的追随者们因为他背叛而感到失望。


疑问句:
Was he disenchanted by the outcome of the election? = 他对选举的结果感到失望吗？
Are you disenchanted with your current job? = 你对你目前的工作感到失望吗？


否定句:
The magic show did not disenchant the children. = 魔术表演并没有让孩子们感到失望。
I was not disenchanted by his performance. = 我并没有因为他表现而感到失望。
