sabian:/ˈseɪbiən/| adj. n.|萨比安教徒的；萨比安教徒|Relating to or characteristic of the Sabians, a religious sect mentioned in the Quran and believed to have lived in Harran (ancient Carrhae).  The exact nature of the Sabians is debated by scholars.|The Sabian community, though mentioned in religious texts, remains somewhat mysterious to historians.|近义词:Mandaean (in some contexts)|反义词:None.
