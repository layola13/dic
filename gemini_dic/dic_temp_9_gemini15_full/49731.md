democratizes:/dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪz/|/ˈdɛməkrəˌtaɪzɪz/| vt.|使民主化；普及；使大众化 |To make something accessible to or usable by a larger number of people.|例句：The internet democratizes information, making it available to everyone with access.  互联网使信息民主化，让每个有网络连接的人都能获取信息。

例句：The company's new policy democratizes access to its products, lowering prices and expanding distribution.  公司的这项新政策使产品更容易获得，降低了价格并扩大了分销范围。


近义词：popularize, equalize, universalize

反义词：centralize, restrict, limit

词性：动词
