cantiniere:/ˌkæntiˈnjɛr/| n. |食堂老板娘；小酒馆女招待|A cantiniere is a woman who runs a canteen or small restaurant, often associated with military settings or providing food and drink to workers.  The term often implies a somewhat informal or homely atmosphere.|Example: The soldiers frequented the cantiniere's establishment for hot meals and camaraderie.  士兵们经常光顾食堂老板娘的店，享用热饭菜并增进友谊。|近义词：canteen keeper, restaurateur (more formal)|反义词：customer, patron|名词
