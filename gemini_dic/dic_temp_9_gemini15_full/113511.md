ma'am:/mæm/ | n. | 女士，夫人 |  A polite term of address for a woman, especially one older than oneself. It is a contraction of "madam". | Examples: "Yes, ma'am."  是的，夫人。"Excuse me, ma'am, could you help me?"  对不起，夫人，你能帮我一下吗？ | 近义词：madam, lady | 反义词：sir
