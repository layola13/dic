daltonism:/ˈdɔːltənɪzəm/| n.|色盲|Color blindness, specifically red-green color blindness.  It refers to the inability to distinguish between certain colors, most commonly red and green. This condition is often named after John Dalton, a scientist who himself experienced this type of color vision deficiency and described it in detail.|He suffers from daltonism and has trouble distinguishing between red and green.|近义词：color blindness, color vision deficiency|反义词：normal color vision
