It implies excessive intellectualizing, often leading to inaction or paralysis by analysis. | 例句：He overphilosophizes every simple problem, making it impossible to find a solution. 他对每一个简单的问题都过度思考，以至于无法找到解决方案。


He overphilosophized the decision, delaying the project indefinitely. 他对这个决定过度思考，导致项目无限期推迟。


近义词：overthink, overintellectualize, ponder excessively, analyze to death | 反义词：act decisively, be pragmatic, be practical
