outwished:/ˌaʊtˈwɪʃt/| v.|被超越愿望，愿望被他人超过 |To have one's wishes surpassed or exceeded by another's.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered a neologism or nonce word.  It implies a competition or comparison of desires where one person's wish is deemed stronger, more effective, or more successful than another's. |例句：He outwished his brother for the last piece of cake. 他比他兄弟更渴望得到最后一块蛋糕。  She felt outwished by her friend's ambition. 她感觉朋友的雄心壮志让她相形见绌。|近义词：outshone, surpassed, exceeded |反义词：underwished (not a standard word)
