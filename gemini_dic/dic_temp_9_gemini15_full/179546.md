reelectrifying: /ˌriːɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 再次通电；使重新充满活力；使再次兴奋 | To electrify again; to revitalize or re-energize something.  The word combines "re-" (again) and "electrifying" (making exciting or thrilling). |例句：The new CEO's innovative ideas reelectrified the stagnant company.  新的首席执行官的创新理念使停滞不前的公司重新焕发了活力。

The concert was reelectrifying; the band's performance was even better than before.  这场音乐会再次令人兴奋；乐队的表演比以前更好。|近义词：revitalize, reinvigorate, re-energize |反义词：de-energize, drain, deplete | 动词
