frye:/fraɪ/| n. |弗莱（姓氏）；一种高筒靴|A Frye is a type of high-top boot, often made of leather, known for its durability and classic style.  The name is also a surname.|例句：My grandmother always wore her Frye boots when gardening. 我祖母总是穿着她的弗莱靴子去园艺。

例句：The Frye Company is known for its high-quality leather boots.  弗莱公司以其高质量的皮靴而闻名。


近义词：boot (general term),  high-top


反义词：sandal, slipper (opposite footwear types)
