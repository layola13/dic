cheetah:/ˈtʃiːtə/ | n. |猎豹|A large, slender, spotted cat that is the fastest land animal.  It is known for its speed and agility in hunting prey. |例句：The cheetah is known for its incredible speed. 猎豹以其惊人的速度而闻名。|例句：The cheetah chased the gazelle across the savanna. 猎豹在草原上追赶瞪羚。|近义词：leopard,  wildcat|反义词：slow animal
