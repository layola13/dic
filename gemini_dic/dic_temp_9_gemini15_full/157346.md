piped:/paɪpt/| adj. |用管道输送的；有管道的|Conveyed through pipes; having pipes.|例句：The water is piped directly from the spring. 水直接从泉水中管道输送。|例句：The house has piped gas. 这房子有管道煤气。|近义词：channeled, conveyed|反义词：unpiped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water pipes the water to the houses. = 水管把水输送到房子里。
They pipe the oil to the refinery. = 他们用管道输送石油到炼油厂。

一般过去时:
They piped the gas into the building. = 他们把煤气管道输送到大楼里。
The farmer piped water to his fields. = 农民用管道把水输送到他的田地里。


现在完成时:
They have piped natural gas to the entire neighborhood. = 他们已经把天然气管道输送到整个社区。
The company has piped millions of gallons of water. = 这家公司已经输送了数百万加仑的水。


过去完成时:
They had piped the water before the drought began. = 干旱开始之前，他们就已经铺设了输水管道。
The city had piped the sewage into the river before the new regulations were implemented. = 在新的规定实施之前，城市已经将污水通过管道排放到河里。


将来时:
They will pipe the new gas line to the development next year. = 他们明年将把新的天然气管道铺设到开发区。
The engineers will pipe the wastewater treatment plant to the ocean. = 工程师们将把污水处理厂的污水通过管道排放到海洋。


虚拟语气:
If they piped the water directly, it would be more efficient. = 如果他们直接用管道输水，效率会更高。
I wish they had piped the water to the village earlier. = 我希望他们早点把水管铺设到村里。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
