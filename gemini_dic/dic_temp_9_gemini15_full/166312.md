prerefinement:/ˌpriːrɪˈfaɪnmənt/ | n. | 预先改进；事先的精致 |  The act or process of refining something before it is further processed or used;  preparation for refinement.  It suggests a preliminary stage of improvement or sophistication. |  The prerefinement of the ore involved crushing and washing.  预先精炼矿石包括破碎和清洗。

The prerefinement of the data significantly improved the accuracy of the analysis. 数据的预先细化大大提高了分析的准确性。| 近义词：preliminary refinement, preparation, pre-processing | 反义词：post-refinement, finalization | 名词
