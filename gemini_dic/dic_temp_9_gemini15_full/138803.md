nummulitic:/ˌnʌmjuˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|货币石的；含货币石的|Relating to or containing nummulites, a genus of large, lenticular foraminifera whose fossilized shells are common in Eocene rocks.  Nummulites are significant index fossils, used to date and correlate rock strata.|例句：The Eocene limestone is characterized by its nummulitic content.  始新世石灰岩的特点是其含有货币石。|例句：The nummulitic layers were clearly visible in the cliff face. 悬崖峭壁上货币石层清晰可见。|近义词：foraminiferal (if referring to the fossils themselves)|反义词：None specifically.
