demonstratory: /ˌdɛmənˈstreɪtəri/ | adj. | 示范的；证明的；表达的 |

英文解析:  Relating to or serving as a demonstration; showing or explaining something clearly.  Characterized by or intended to demonstrate something.

例句:

He gave a demonstratory lecture on the new software. 他做了一个关于新软件的示范性讲座。

Her demonstratory actions clearly showed her anger.  她的示威行为清楚地表明了她的愤怒。


近义词:  expository, illustrative, explanatory, evidential

反义词:  obscure, unclear, inexpressive, ambiguous


词源: From  demonstrate + -ory.  "Demonstrate" comes from Latin *demonstrare, from *de- "completely" + monstrare "to show."
