laudably:/ˈlɔːdəbli/ | adj. |值得赞扬地；令人钦佩地 |  Worthy of praise; commendable.  It describes an action or quality that deserves praise. |例句：He laudably tried to help, even though he knew it was a difficult task. 他值得赞扬地尝试帮忙，即使他知道这是一项艰巨的任务。


例句：She acted laudably in a crisis situation.  她在危机情况下表现得令人钦佩。


近义词：commendably, admirably, praiseworthily, creditably


反义词：blameworthily, reprehensibly, discreditably
