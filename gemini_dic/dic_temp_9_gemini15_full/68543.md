fandoms:/ˈfændəm z/| n. |粉丝群体；粉丝圈 |A fandom is a community of fans who share a common interest in a particular thing, such as a book series, a movie franchise, a musical group, or a video game.  Fandoms often engage in creative activities related to their shared interest, such as fan fiction, fan art, and cosplay. |Example: The Harry Potter fandom is one of the largest and most active fandoms in the world. 哈利波特粉丝群体是世界上最大最活跃的粉丝群体之一。  The online K-pop fandoms are known for their passionate support of their idols.  网络韩流粉丝群体以其对偶像的热情支持而闻名。|近义词：fan base, fanbase, following |反义词：N/A
