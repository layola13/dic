cinematographer: /ˌsɪnəməˈtɑːɡrəfər/ | n. | 电影摄影师 | A cinematographer, also known as a director of photography (DP), is a person who is responsible for the visual aspects of a film, television show, or video.  They work closely with the director to create the overall look and feel of the project, making decisions about lighting, camera angles, and composition. |  The cinematographer Roger Deakins is renowned for his stunning visuals.  |  director of photography, camera operator |  (no direct antonym, but a contrast could be "film editor") | 名词
