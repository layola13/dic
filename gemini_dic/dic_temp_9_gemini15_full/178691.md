recrossed:/ˌriːˈkrɒst/ | v. | 再横过；重新越过 | To cross again; to cross over something a second time. |例句：After pausing to consider his options, he recrossed the street.  在停下来考虑他的选择后，他又一次穿过街道。


例句：The hiker recrossed the mountain pass to retrieve his lost water bottle.  徒步旅行者重新越过山口去取回他丢失的水壶。


近义词：repassed, retraversed, recrossed over


反义词：crossed (once)


词性：动词
