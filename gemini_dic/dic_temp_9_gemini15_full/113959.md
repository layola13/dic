magellan:/məˈdʒɛlən/| n. |麦哲伦；麦哲伦海峡|The word "Magellan" refers to Ferdinand Magellan, the Portuguese explorer who led the first expedition to circumnavigate the globe.  It is also used to refer to things named after him, such as the Magellan Strait.|Example: Magellan's voyage was a monumental achievement in exploration. 麦哲伦的航行是探险史上的伟大成就。|近义词:explorer, navigator |反义词:None.
