grammatist:/ˈɡræmətɪst/| n. |语法学家|A grammatist is a person who studies or teaches grammar. They are experts in the structure and rules of language.  They may specialize in a particular language or in general linguistic theory. |例句：The grammatist explained the subtle differences between the two verb tenses. 语法学家解释了两种动词时态之间的细微差别。

例句：Many grammatists debate the merits of prescriptive versus descriptive grammar. 许多语法学家都在争论规范语法与描述语法的优缺点。


近义词：linguist, philologist |反义词：None  |词性：n.
