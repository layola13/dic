overturned:/ˌoʊvərˈtɜːrnd/| vt. adj.|推翻；颠覆；倾覆；被推翻的；被颠覆的|To turn (something) over; to cause (something) to turn over.  To overthrow or defeat (a government, system, etc.).|例句：The strong wind overturned the boat.  强风把船掀翻了。

The government was overturned in a military coup.  政府在军事政变中被推翻了。


近义词：upset, capsize, overthrow, subvert |反义词：uphold, support, establish, maintain|动词


USAGE->

He overturns the established order with his radical ideas. = 他用激进的思想颠覆了既定的秩序。
