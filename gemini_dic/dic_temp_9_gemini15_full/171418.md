puddling:/ˈpʌdliŋ/| n. v.| 积水；(使)成泥坑 |The formation of puddles on the road after the rain. 雨后路面上形成了水坑。|近义词：pooling, slushing |反义词：drying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain is puddling on the patio. = 雨水在天井里积成了水坑。
Water puddles on the flat roof. = 水在平顶上积成了水坑。


一般过去时:
The heavy rain puddled the streets. = 大雨使街道积满了水。
The spilled milk puddled on the floor. = 洒出的牛奶在地板上积成了水坑。


现在完成时:
The rain has puddled the entire field. = 雨水已经把整个田地都积成了水坑。
The snow has puddled in the sun. = 雪在阳光下融化成水坑了。


过去完成时:
The ground had puddled before the race began. = 比赛开始前，地面已经积满了水。
The streets had puddled significantly before the storm subsided. = 暴雨平息之前，街道已经积满了相当多的水。


将来时:
The forecast predicts that rain will puddle the streets tomorrow. = 预报说明天下雨会使街道积水。
If it rains heavily, the yard will puddle. = 如果下大雨，院子就会积水。


虚拟语气:
If it hadn't rained so hard, the streets wouldn't have puddled. = 如果雨下得不大，街道就不会积水。
I wish the rain hadn't puddled the playing field. = 我真希望雨没有把运动场积成水坑。


被动语态:
The streets were puddled by the heavy rain. = 街道被大雨积满了水。
The field was puddled after the storm. = 暴雨过后，田地里积满了水。


疑问句:
Is the water puddling in the basement? = 地下室是不是积水了？
Does the rain usually puddle on this street? = 这条街通常会因为下雨而积水吗？


否定句:
The rain didn't puddle on the driveway. = 雨没有在车道上积水。
The new pavement doesn't puddle easily. = 新铺的路面不容易积水。
