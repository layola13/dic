non-committalism:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtəlzəm/| n. |不表态；含糊其辞；模棱两可|The refusal to make a definite statement or commitment; a policy of avoiding commitment.  It often involves deliberately vague or ambiguous language to avoid taking a position or expressing an opinion. |例句：The politician's response was marked by non-committalism, offering no clear indication of his stance on the issue.  这位政治家的回应含糊其辞，没有明确表明他对这个问题的立场。
The government's non-committalism on the proposed reforms fueled public discontent. 政府对拟议改革的模棱两可加剧了公众的不满。

|近义词：ambiguity, equivocation, vagueness, hedging, fence-sitting|反义词：commitment, decisiveness, forthrightness|名词
