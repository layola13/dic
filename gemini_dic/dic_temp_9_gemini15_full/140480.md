old-timer:/oʊldˈtaɪmər/ | n. |老前辈；老家伙；经验丰富的人 | An old-timer is someone who has been involved in a particular activity or place for a long time.  They often possess extensive knowledge and experience. |例句：He's an old-timer in the construction business, having worked for over 40 years. 他是建筑业的老前辈，工作了40多年。|例句：The old-timer at the bar spun a tale of the old west. 酒吧里的老家伙讲了一个关于美国西部的传奇故事。|近义词：veteran, veteran, old hand, seasoned professional |反义词：newbie, novice, beginner
