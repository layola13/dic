reproduction:/ˌriːprəˈdʌkʃən/| n. v.|复制；繁殖；再现|The act or process of reproducing or being reproduced.|例句：The reproduction of the painting was surprisingly accurate.  这幅画的复制品惊人地准确。

例句：Asexual reproduction is common in many plant species.  无性繁殖在许多植物物种中很常见。


近义词：duplication, replication, propagation|反义词：destruction, annihilation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist reproduces famous paintings. = 艺术家复制名画。
She reproduces her work for sale. = 她复制她的作品出售。


一般过去时:
They reproduced the experiment last year. = 他们去年复制了这个实验。
The company reproduced the brochure in bulk. = 公司大量复制了小册子。


现在完成时:
Scientists have reproduced the results of the study. = 科学家们已经复制了这项研究的结果。
She has reproduced several of her paintings. = 她已经复制了她的一些画作。


过去完成时:
The printer had reproduced thousands of pages before it malfunctioned. = 打印机在出现故障之前已经打印了数千页。
He had reproduced the data before the hard drive crashed. = 硬盘崩溃之前他已经复制了数据。


将来时:
They will reproduce the image on a larger scale. = 他们将以更大的规模复制图像。
We will reproduce the findings in our next report. = 我们将在下一份报告中复制这些发现。


虚拟语气:
If they reproduced the experiment, they might get different results. = 如果他们重复实验，他们可能会得到不同的结果。
I wish I could reproduce her beautiful paintings. = 我真希望我能复制她美丽的画作。


被动语态:
The image was reproduced many times. = 这张图片被复制了很多次。

疑问句:

否定句:

The artist did not reproduce the painting faithfully. = 艺术家没有忠实地复制这幅画。
