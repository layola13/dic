latest:/ˈleɪtɪst/ | adj. |最新的，最近的 |The latest news reports indicate a significant shift in public opinion. 最新的新闻报道表明公众舆论发生了重大转变。|近义词：newest, most recent, current |反义词：oldest, earliest
