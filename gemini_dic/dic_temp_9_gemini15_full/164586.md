preexcluding: /ˌpriːɪkˈskluːdɪŋ/ | v. | 预先排除；事先剔除 |  Precluding something beforehand; preventing something from happening or being included.  It is the present participle form of the verb "preexclude".|

The company preexcluded candidates with insufficient experience from the interview process.  公司在面试过程中预先排除了经验不足的候选人。

The strict regulations preexcluded any possibility of bribery.  严格的法规预先排除了任何贿赂的可能性。


近义词:  excluding beforehand, preventing, barring, eliminating, ruling out

反义词: including, admitting, allowing


词性: Verb (present participle)
