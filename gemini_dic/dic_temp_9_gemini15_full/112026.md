long-sightedness:/ˈlɒŋˈsaɪtɪdnəs/| n. |远视；远见卓识 |The ability to see things clearly only at a distance, due to a refractive error of the eye; also, the ability to think about the future and plan for it.  |例句：His long-sightedness allowed him to anticipate the market crash.他的远见卓识使他预料到了市场崩盘。

例句：Her long-sightedness, a common condition, required corrective lenses. 她的远视是一种常见病，需要矫正镜片。|近义词：farsightedness, foresight |反义词：shortsightedness, myopia
