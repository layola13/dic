induna:/ɪnˈduːnə/| n. |酋长，首领|A Zulu word for a chief or headman, often a leader of a specific group or clan within a larger community.  The induna held considerable authority and responsibility within their respective areas.|Example: The induna addressed the assembled villagers, outlining the plans for the upcoming harvest.  酋长向聚集的村民们讲话，概述了即将到来的收成的计划。|近义词：chief, headman, leader |反义词：follower, subordinate |名词
