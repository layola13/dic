ryder:/ˈraɪdər/| n. |附加条款；(合同的)补充协议；骑手；摩托车手|A rider is an additional clause or condition added to a contract or document.  It can also refer to a person who rides a horse or motorcycle.|例句：The contract included a rider specifying the delivery date.  这份合同包含一个详细说明交货日期的附加条款。|例句：He's a skilled motorcycle rider. 他是个熟练的摩托车手。|近义词：addendum, amendment, supplement|反义词：N/A
