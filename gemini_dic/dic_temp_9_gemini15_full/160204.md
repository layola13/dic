ponds:/pɑːndz/| n.|池塘；水塘|A pond is a small body of still water, smaller than a lake.  It is often found in natural depressions or artificially created.|例句：There are several ponds in the park. 公园里有几个池塘。|例句：The children were playing by the pond. 孩子们在池塘边玩耍。|近义词：pool, lake, reservoir|反义词：ocean, sea


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pond freezes over in winter. 冬天池塘结冰。
Many ducks swim on the pond. 许多鸭子在池塘里游泳。

一般过去时:
We fished in the pond yesterday. 我们昨天在池塘里钓鱼。
The pond was full of lilies last summer. 去年夏天池塘里长满了睡莲。

现在完成时:
The children have played by the pond all afternoon. 孩子们整个下午都在池塘边玩耍。
The pond has dried up because of the drought. 由于干旱，池塘干涸了。

过去完成时:

We had never seen such clear water in the pond before. 我们以前从未见过池塘里这么清澈的水。


将来时:
We will visit the pond tomorrow. 我们明天将去池塘。
The pond will be full of water after the rain. 雨后池塘将充满水。

虚拟语气:
If the pond were bigger, we could swim in it. 如果池塘更大一些，我们就能在里面游泳。

疑问句:

否定句:
