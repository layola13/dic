enophile:/ˈɛnəfaɪl/| n. |爱酒之人，葡萄酒爱好者 | A person who loves and has a good knowledge of wine.  An enophile is someone who appreciates fine wines and has a deep understanding of their production, history, and tasting notes. They often collect wines, attend wine tastings, and travel to wine regions. | He's a true enophile, his cellar boasts a remarkable collection of rare vintages. 他是一位真正的爱酒之人，他的酒窖里收藏着许多珍稀的葡萄酒。| wine connoisseur, wine lover | teetotaler (反义词不完全对等，指滴酒不沾者) | 名词
