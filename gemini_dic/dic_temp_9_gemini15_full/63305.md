enti:/ˈɛnti/ | n. |实体，存在，本质 |An entity is something that exists independently.  It can be a physical object, a concept, or an organization.  |例句：The company is treated as a separate legal entity. 这家公司被视为一个独立的法人实体。|例句：The soul is often considered a separate entity from the body. 灵魂通常被认为是独立于身体的实体。|近义词：being, thing, object |反义词：nothingness, nonexistence |名词
