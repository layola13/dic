photofluoroscope:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈflʊərəskoʊp/ | n. | X光透视机 | A device that uses X-rays to produce a continuous image of the internal structures of the body, often used for visualizing the movement of organs or contrast media.  | Example: The doctor used a photofluoroscope to examine the patient's digestive system.  医生用X光透视机检查病人的消化系统。 | 近义词: fluoroscope, image intensifier | 反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑与静态成像设备相对) | 名词
