quasi-disastrously:/ˈkwɑːzaɪˌdɪˈzæstrəsli/ | adv. | 几乎灾难性地；差点造成灾难地 |  The project proceeded quasi-disastrously, narrowly avoiding complete failure due to unforeseen complications.  | 近义词：nearly disastrously, almost catastrophically | 反义词：triumphantly, successfully | 副词

PHRASE->
There is no common phrase with "quasi-disastrously".  The word itself is used as a standalone adverbial modifier.


词源:
quasi- (拉丁语，意为“好像；似乎”) + disastrously (disastrous 的副词形式，disastrous 来自 disaster， disaster 源于法语 désastre，最终来自意大利语 disastro，意为“坏运气；不幸”)
