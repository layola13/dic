misappoints:/ˌmɪsæˈpɔɪnts/| vt.|使失望；未能达到期望|to fail to meet someone's expectations or hopes; to disappoint someone.|例句：The movie misappoints because of its weak plot. 这部电影因为情节薄弱而令人失望。|例句：His performance misappointed his coach. 他的表现让他的教练失望了。|近义词：disappoint, let down, frustrate|反义词：satisfy, please, delight|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather misappoints many tourists every year.  = 天气每年都让许多游客失望。
He misappoints his parents with his poor grades. = 他糟糕的成绩让他父母失望。


一般过去时:
The restaurant misappointed us with its poor service. = 那家餐馆糟糕的服务让我们失望。
The results of the experiment misappointed the scientists. = 实验的结果让科学家们失望。


现在完成时:
The team has misappointed its fans this season. = 这个赛季球队让球迷们失望了。
The new product has misappointed consumers with its many defects. = 新产品因其诸多缺陷而让消费者失望。


过去完成时:
I had misappointed my friends by forgetting their birthday party. = 我忘记了朋友们的生日派对，让他们失望了。
She had misappointed her boss by missing the deadline. = 她错过了截止日期，让老板失望了。


将来时:
The upcoming movie might disappoint many people. = 即将来临的电影可能会让很多人失望。
I fear that the project will disappoint the investors.  = 我担心这个项目会让投资者失望。


虚拟语气:
If the team played better, they wouldn't have misappointed their fans. = 如果球队表现更好，他们就不会让球迷失望了。
I wish the concert hadn't misappointed me so much. = 我真希望这场音乐会没有让我如此失望。


被动语态:
Many people were misappointed by the poor quality of the product. = 许多人对产品的低劣质量感到失望。
The audience was misappointed by the actor's weak performance. = 观众对演员糟糕的表演感到失望。


疑问句:
Did the movie disappoint you? = 这部电影让你失望了吗？
Will the results disappoint the researchers? = 结果会让研究人员失望吗？


否定句:
The performance didn't disappoint the audience. = 这场演出并没有让观众失望。
The book did not disappoint me; it was excellent. = 这本书没有让我失望；它很棒。
