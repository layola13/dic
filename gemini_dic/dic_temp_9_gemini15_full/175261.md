rain-out: /ˈreɪn aʊt/ | n. |因雨取消 | An event that is canceled due to rain.  |例句：The baseball game was a rain-out.  棒球比赛因雨取消了。|The picnic was rained out. 野餐因下雨取消了。|近义词：cancellation, postponement |反义词：go-ahead
