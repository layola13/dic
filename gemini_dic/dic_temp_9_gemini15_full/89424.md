homozygotic:/ˌhoʊmoʊzaɪˈɡɑːtɪk/| adj.| 同型合子的；纯合的 | Having two identical alleles of a particular gene or genes.  An organism that is homozygous for a particular gene carries two copies of the same allele. For example, if an organism has two copies of the allele for purple flowers, it is homozygous for flower color. |Example: The homozygous recessive genotype for the trait is denoted by aa.  同型合子隐性基因型用aa表示。

The plant shows a homozygous phenotype for tallness. 这株植物表现出高大的纯合性状。


近义词：purebred, true-breeding | 反义词：heterozygous


词源:  From "homo-" (same) + "zygote" (a fertilized egg cell).  The term reflects that the alleles are the same within the individual's paired chromosomes.


USAGE->
(由于homozygotic是形容词，没有动词变化，以下例句展示不同句型用法)


陈述句:
The offspring are homozygous for the dominant allele. 后代对于显性等位基因是纯合的。
This individual is homozygous for the gene controlling eye color.  这个个体对于控制眼睛颜色的基因是纯合的。


疑问句:
Is the individual homozygous or heterozygous for this trait? 这个个体对于这个性状是纯合的还是杂合的？
Are all the plants in this population homozygous?  这个群体中的所有植物都是纯合的吗？


否定句:
The results show that the organism is not homozygous for the gene in question. 结果表明该生物体对于所讨论的基因不是纯合的。
The offspring are not homozygous for this particular gene.  后代对于这个特定基因不是纯合的。


比较句:
Homozygous individuals show a consistent phenotype, unlike heterozygous individuals. 与杂合个体不同，纯合个体表现出一致的表型。
Homozygous recessive individuals are more susceptible to certain diseases than homozygous dominant individuals. 纯合隐性个体比纯合显性个体更容易患某些疾病。
