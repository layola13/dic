hospices:/ˈhɒspɪsɪz/ | n. |临终关怀机构；收容所 |Places that provide care and support for people who are dying.  Hospices offer palliative care, focusing on pain and symptom management, emotional support, and spiritual guidance. They may be independent organizations or part of larger healthcare systems. |例句：Many hospices offer a range of services, including counseling and bereavement support. 许多临终关怀机构提供一系列服务，包括咨询和丧亲辅导。

例句：My grandmother spent her final days in a hospice, surrounded by her loved ones. 我的祖母在临终关怀机构度过了最后的日子，身边环绕着她的亲人。


近义词：palliative care centers, end-of-life care facilities


反义词：N/A (No direct antonym, as it refers to a specific type of care)

词性：名词
