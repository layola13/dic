gibed:/dʒaɪbd/ | vt. |嘲笑；讥讽；戏弄 |To speak to someone in a mocking or sarcastic way.  |例句：The children gibed at the clumsy old man.孩子们嘲笑那个笨拙的老人。

例句：He gibed at her for her poor performance. 他因她糟糕的表现而嘲笑她。


近义词：mock, taunt, jeer, sneer, ridicule | 反义词：praise, compliment

词源:
The word "gibe" comes from the Middle English word "gibe," which in turn has roots in Old Norse "geipa," meaning "to mock" or "to jeer."  The evolution of the word reflects a long history of the act of mocking and teasing within the Germanic languages.
