peltated:/pɛlˈteɪtɪd/| adj.|盾状的；佩尔塔状的|Having a peltate leaf or appendage;  a peltate leaf or appendage is one where the petiole is attached to the lower surface of the blade, rather than to the margin or apex.  It's often described as shield-shaped.|The water lily has peltate leaves.|近义词：shield-shaped, orbicular|反义词：cordate (heart-shaped), lanceolate (lance-shaped)
