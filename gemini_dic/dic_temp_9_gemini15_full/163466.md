precontributed: /ˌpriːkənˈtrɪbjuːtɪd/ | adj. | 预先捐献的；预先贡献的 |  Previously contributed; given or done in advance.  This word is not commonly used and is essentially a combination of "pre-" and "contributed." It describes something that has been contributed before a specific time or event. |  The precontributed funds allowed the project to begin immediately. = 预先捐献的资金使得项目能够立即开始。

The organization relied heavily on precontributed materials for the event. = 该组织严重依赖为活动预先捐献的材料。


近义词: advance-paid, beforehand-given, pre-donated

反义词: post-contributed, subsequently-donated


词性: 形容词
