debentured:/dɪˈbentʃərd/| adj.| 债券担保的；以债券抵押的 | Secured by debentures;  relating to or secured by a debenture.  |The company's assets were debentured to secure the loan.  这家公司的资产以债券作抵押来担保贷款。| secured, mortgaged | unsecured, unmortgaged |形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is debentured to secure the loan. = 这家公司以债券作抵押来担保贷款。
This project is debentured by the government. = 这个项目由政府以债券担保。


一般过去时:
The assets were debentured to pay off the debt. = 资产以债券作抵押来偿还债务。
The building was debentured last year. = 这栋建筑物去年以债券抵押。


现在完成时:
The company has been debentured several times. = 这家公司已经多次以债券作抵押。
The project has been successfully debentured. = 该项目已成功以债券抵押。


过去完成时:
The loan had already been debentured before the meeting. = 会议之前贷款已经以债券抵押了。
The property had been debentured for several years. = 这处房产已经抵押了几年了。


将来时:
The equipment will be debentured next month. = 设备将在下个月以债券抵押。
The factory will be fully debentured to secure the investment. = 工厂将全面以债券抵押以确保投资。


虚拟语气:
If the project were debentured, it would attract more investors. = 如果项目以债券抵押，它将吸引更多投资者。
I wish the loan had been debentured earlier. = 我希望贷款早点以债券抵押。


被动语态:
The loan was debentured by the bank. = 这笔贷款由银行以债券抵押。
The property is being debentured to raise capital. = 正在以债券抵押房产以筹集资金。


疑问句:
Is the property debentured? = 这处房产有抵押吗？
Were the assets debentured properly? = 资产是否被妥善地以债券抵押？


否定句:
The company is not debentured. = 这家公司没有以债券抵押。
The equipment was not debentured in time. = 设备没有及时以债券抵押。


PHRASE->
debentured assets: 债券担保的资产
debentured debt: 债券债务
debentured company: 以债券抵押的公司
fully debentured: 全部以债券抵押的
highly debentured: 高度以债券抵押的


词源:
From "debenture," ultimately from Old French *debenre, from Latin *de- + *benere "to owe, to be indebted."  A debenture is a type of debt instrument, so the adjective "debentured" describes something secured by such an instrument.
