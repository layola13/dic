carry-off: /ˈkæri ɔːf/ | vt. | 搬走；夺走；成功完成；抢走；(疾病)夺去…的生命 |


英文解析:To carry off means to take something away, often secretly or forcefully. It can also mean to successfully accomplish something difficult or to win a prize or competition.  In a less common usage, it can refer to death caused by illness.

例句:

The thieves carried off all the valuable jewels.  窃贼偷走了所有贵重的珠宝。

He carried off the first prize in the competition. 他在比赛中获得了第一名。(成功完成)


近义词: remove, steal, abduct, accomplish, achieve, win

反义词: bring, return, fail


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He carries off the package every day. 他每天都搬走包裹。
They carry off the project successfully. 他们成功完成了这个项目。


一般过去时:
She carried off the gold medal. 她赢得了金牌。
The disease carried him off.  疾病夺去了他的生命。


现在完成时:
The robbers have carried off all the money. 强盗已经偷走了所有的钱。
She has carried off many awards. 她获得过许多奖项。


过去完成时:
They had carried off the evidence before the police arrived.  警察到达之前，他们已经转移了证据。
The illness had carried her off before the doctor could arrive.  医生赶到之前，疾病已经夺走了她的生命。


将来时:
He will carry off the presentation with confidence. 他将自信地完成演示。
They will carry off the plan. 他们将成功实施计划。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he would have carried off the race. 如果他训练更刻苦，他就会赢得比赛。
If only she could carry off that difficult task.  但愿她能完成那项艰巨的任务。


被动语态:
The painting was carried off during the robbery.  这幅画在抢劫中被偷走了。
The award was carried off by a young student. 这个奖项被一名年轻学生获得。


疑问句:
Can he carry off such a difficult role? 他能胜任如此困难的角色吗？
Has the thief carried off anything valuable? 小偷偷走了什么贵重的东西吗？


否定句:
He couldn't carry off the joke. 他讲不好这个笑话。(未能成功完成)
They didn't carry off the project on time. 他们没有按时完成项目。


PHRASE->

carry off a role: 胜任一个角色
carry off a prize: 赢得奖项
carry off a coup:  取得重大成功
carry off an election:赢得选举
carry off the day: 获胜；成功
