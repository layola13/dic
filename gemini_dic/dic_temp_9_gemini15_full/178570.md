recordist:/rɪˈkɔːrdɪst/| n. |录音员，记录员 |A recordist is a person who makes sound recordings, typically for professional purposes such as film, television, or music production.  They are responsible for operating recording equipment, managing audio levels, and ensuring high-quality sound capture. | The recordist meticulously adjusted the microphone levels before the singer began. 录音员在歌手开始演唱前仔细调整了麦克风音量。  The film's recordist was praised for capturing the subtle sounds of the rainforest. 这部电影的录音师因捕捉到雨林的细微声音而受到赞扬。| sound engineer, audio engineer, sound technician|  (无明确反义词，可考虑  non-recording personnel) |名词
