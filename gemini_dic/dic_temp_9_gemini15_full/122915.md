molts:/moʊlts/| v.|蜕皮，脱落|To shed or cast off (a covering, such as skin, feathers, or an exoskeleton).  The process of shedding an outer covering, such as skin or feathers.|The snake molted its skin.  蛇蜕皮了。|The bird molted its feathers in the fall.  鸟在秋天换毛了。|shed, cast off, slough|retain, keep


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The snake molts its skin regularly. = 蛇定期蜕皮。
The bird molts its feathers every year. = 鸟每年换羽。

一般过去时:
The caterpillar molted its skin before becoming a butterfly. = 毛毛虫在变成蝴蝶之前蜕皮了。
The lizard molted its skin last week. = 蜥蜴上周蜕皮了。

现在完成时:
The snake has molted twice this year. = 蛇今年已经蜕皮两次了。
The insect has molted several times during its growth. = 昆虫在其生长过程中已经蜕皮好几次了。

过去完成时:
The bird had molted before migrating south. = 鸟在向南迁徙之前已经换羽了。
The insect had molted three times before reaching adulthood. = 昆虫在成年之前已经蜕皮三次了。

将来时:
The snake will molt again soon. = 蛇很快会再次蜕皮。
The cicada will molt its exoskeleton next spring. = 蝉明年春天会蜕掉它的外骨骼。

虚拟语气:
If the snake molted its skin more frequently, it would grow faster. = 如果蛇更频繁地蜕皮，它会长得更快。
I wish the butterfly would molt its chrysalis soon. = 我希望蝴蝶很快能破茧而出。

被动语态:
The skin was molted by the snake. = 蛇蜕掉了皮。(被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The feathers are molted by the bird annually. = 鸟每年换羽。(被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:

When will the bird molt its feathers? = 鸟什么时候换羽？

否定句:

The lizard didn't molt its skin properly. = 蜥蜴没有正常蜕皮。
