idiocracy:/ɪˈdiːəkrəsi/ | n. |愚民政治，白痴统治；愚蠢的行为 |The state or government of the stupid or ignorant; a system of government where the least intelligent members of society have the most power.  It can also refer to the behavior or characteristics of such a system, or simply to generally foolish behavior. |例句：The film "Idiocracy" depicts a dystopian future ruled by idiocracy.  这部电影《蠢蛋进化论》描绘了一个由愚民政治统治的反乌托邦未来。


例句：His constant complaining and irrational decisions are a clear example of idiocracy. 他不断的抱怨和非理性的决定是愚蠢行为的明显例子。


近义词：stupidity, foolishness, inanity, imbecility |反义词：intelligence, wisdom, sagacity


词源：源自希腊语 *idios* ("own," "private") + *kratos* ("power, rule").  字面意思是“自己的统治”，暗指由无知或无能的人掌握权力。
