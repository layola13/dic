heelpiece:/ˈhiːlpiːs/| n. |后跟, 鞋跟块 |The heelpiece is the part of a shoe that supports the heel.  It is usually made of leather or other durable material and is attached to the sole.  A broken heelpiece can make a shoe uncomfortable and unstable. |The heelpiece of my favorite boots broke, so I need to get them repaired. = 我最喜欢的靴子的鞋跟坏了，所以我需要去修理一下。  The cobbler replaced the worn-out heelpiece on my shoes. = 鞋匠换掉了我鞋子上磨损的后跟。|近义词：heel, backstay |反义词：toe
