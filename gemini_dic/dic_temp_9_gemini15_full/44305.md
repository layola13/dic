crookneck:/ˈkrʊkneɪk/| adj. n.|弯颈的；弯颈南瓜|Having a bent or hooked neck or stem.  A variety of winter squash with a crooked neck.|例句：The crookneck squash is a popular choice for many recipes.  弯颈南瓜是许多食谱中受欢迎的选择。  The shepherd's crook is a crookneck staff. 牧羊人的杖是弯曲的拐杖。|近义词：bent, curved, hooked|反义词：straight
