moneran:/ˈmɒnərən/| n. |原核生物；细菌| A moneran is a member of the kingdom Monera, which includes bacteria and archaea.  These are single-celled organisms that lack a membrane-bound nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles.  They are prokaryotes. | Example:  Bacteria are monerans. = 细菌是原核生物。  Many monerans play crucial roles in nutrient cycling. = 许多原核生物在养分循环中扮演着至关重要的角色。|近义词：prokaryote, bacterium (if referring specifically to bacteria)|反义词：eukaryote|名词
