settle:/ˈsɛtl/ | vt. vi. n. | 解决；定居；安顿；沉淀 | To bring to a state of quiet or rest; to resolve or adjust; to establish oneself in a place;  to sink to the bottom. |例句：They finally settled their dispute. 他们最终解决了他们的争端。|近义词：resolve, adjust, decide, establish |反义词：agitate, disturb, unsettle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He settles his accounts every month. = 他每月结算账目。
She settles down to read after dinner. = 晚饭后她安顿下来看书。

一般过去时:
The dust settled on the furniture. = 灰尘落在了家具上。
They settled in a new town last year. = 他们去年在一个新的城镇定居。

现在完成时:
We have settled the matter amicably. = 我们已经友好地解决了这个问题。
She has settled into her new job nicely. = 她已经很好地适应了她的新工作。

过去完成时:
The argument had settled before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，争论已经平息了。
They had settled into their new home before the holidays. = 他们在假期前已经安顿好了新家。

将来时:
We will settle the bill tomorrow. = 我们明天将结清账单。
They will settle down in the countryside after retirement. = 退休后他们将在乡下定居。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would settle the problem myself. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会自己解决这个问题。
I wish I could settle down and start a family. = 我希望我能安定下来，组建一个家庭。

被动语态:
The dispute was settled by arbitration. = 争端通过仲裁解决。
The matter will be settled soon. = 这件事很快就会解决。

疑问句:
Has the issue been settled yet? = 这个问题已经解决了吗？
Where did they settle after leaving their hometown? = 离开家乡后他们在哪里定居？

否定句:
They haven't settled the account yet. = 他们还没有结账。
The argument didn't settle easily. = 争论不容易平息。


PHRASE->
settle down = 定居；平静下来
settle for = 勉强接受
settle up = 结清账目
settle in = 安顿下来
settle a dispute = 解决争端
