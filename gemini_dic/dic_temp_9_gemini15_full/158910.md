plunk-down:/plʌŋk daʊn/| vt.| 猛地放下；付清（款项）|to put or drop something down heavily and without care; to pay a sum of money in one go. |例句：He plunked down a hundred dollars on the table. 他猛地把一百美元摔在桌子上。 He plunked down the deposit for the car. 他付清了汽车的定金。|近义词：drop, slam, deposit, pay|反义词：carefully place, gradually pay


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plunks down his briefcase every day. = 他每天都把公文包重重地放下。
They plunk down their money without hesitation. = 他们毫不犹豫地付钱。

一般过去时:
She plunked down the heavy box onto the floor. = 她把沉重的箱子重重地扔到地板上。
He plunked down the full amount for the painting. = 他一次性付清了这幅画的全部款项。

现在完成时:
I have plunked down a significant amount on my new house. = 我已经为我的新房子付了一大笔钱。
They have plunked down their bags and settled in for the night. = 他们放下背包，安顿下来过夜了。

过去完成时:
She had plunked down her purse before realizing she'd lost her keys. = 她放下钱包后才意识到丢了钥匙。
They had plunked down the initial investment before securing further funding. = 在获得进一步资金之前，他们已经支付了最初的投资。

将来时:
He will plunk down the cash as soon as he gets paid. = 他一拿到工资就会付钱。
She will plunk down her luggage and relax after the long journey. = 长途跋涉后，她会放下行李，放松一下。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would plunk down the money for that car. = 如果我有更多钱，我会买那辆车。
I wish I could plunk down the entire amount and be done with it. = 我真希望我能一次性付清所有款项。

被动语态:
The money was plunked down on the counter. = 钱被重重地放在柜台上。
The heavy package was plunked down carelessly. = 那件沉重的包裹被粗心地扔下。

疑问句:
Did he plunk down the money? = 他付钱了吗？

否定句:
He didn't plunk down the money; he paid gradually. = 他没有一次性付清，而是分期付款。
She didn't plunk down her bag; she carefully placed it on the chair. = 她没有把包扔下，而是小心地把它放在椅子上。
