handoff:/ˈhændɔːf/ | n., vt. | 交接；移交；传球 | The act of handing something over to someone else; the process of transferring responsibility or control.  | 例句：The handoff of the project to the new team was seamless. 项目顺利移交给新团队。  | 例句：The quarterback executed a perfect handoff to the running back. 四分卫完美地将球传给了跑卫。 | 近义词：transfer, handover, pass, relay | 反义词：retention, keep, hold


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He handoffs the ball to his teammate. = 他把球传给他的队友。
The company handoffs the project to the new department. = 公司将项目移交给新部门。

一般过去时:
She handoffed the documents to her colleague. = 她把文件交给她的同事。
The manager handoffed the responsibility to his assistant. = 经理将责任移交给他的助理。

现在完成时:
They have handoffed all the necessary information. = 他们已经移交了所有必要的信息。
The team has handoffed the project successfully. = 团队已成功移交项目。

过去完成时:
He had handoffed the keys before leaving. = 他离开前已经交出了钥匙。
The previous team had handoffed the project with detailed documentation. = 前一个团队已移交了包含详细文档的项目。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
