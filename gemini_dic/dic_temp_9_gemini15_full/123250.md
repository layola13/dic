monocytic: /ˌmɒnəˈsaɪtɪk/ | adj. | 单核细胞的 | Relating to or characterized by monocytes.  Monocytic refers to something pertaining to or consisting of monocytes, a type of white blood cell.  It's often used in medical contexts to describe conditions or processes involving an increase or abnormality in monocytes.


例句：

The patient presented with a monocytic leukocytosis. = 病人表现出单核细胞增多症。

The bone marrow biopsy showed a significant monocytic infiltration. = 骨髓活检显示明显的单核细胞浸润。


近义词：mononuclear (in some contexts)


反义词：  There isn't a direct antonym, but terms like "lymphocytic" (relating to lymphocytes, another type of white blood cell) could be considered contrasting in the context of blood cell types.


词性：形容词
