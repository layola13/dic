mated:/ˈmeɪtɪd/| v. adj. n.| 配对的；交配的；结婚的 |  To join or unite as partners or spouses; to have sexual intercourse and produce offspring;  a matched or paired set; a married couple | 例句：The birds have mated and built a nest.  鸟儿已经交配筑巢了。 The two companies have mated to create a larger enterprise. 这两家公司合并创建了一个更大的企业。 |近义词：paired, coupled, married, joined |反义词：unmated, single, unmarried


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They mate every spring. = 它们每年春天交配。
He mates his horses carefully. = 他仔细地为他的马匹配对。


一般过去时:
The birds mated successfully last year. = 这些鸟去年成功地交配了。
The two companies mated in 2020. = 这两家公司在2020年合并了。

过去完成时:
