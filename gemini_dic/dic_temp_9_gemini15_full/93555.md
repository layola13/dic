imagineering:/ˌɪmædʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/| n.|想象工程；创意工程|The blending of imagination and engineering to create innovative products, experiences, or environments.  It's a portmanteau of "imagination" and "engineering".  Disney is famous for its imagineering.|例句：Disneyland's success is a testament to its masterful imagineering. 迪斯尼乐园的成功证明了其精湛的想象工程。|例句：The new theme park is a product of years of meticulous imagineering.  新的主题公园是多年细致想象工程的产物。|近义词：creative engineering, innovative design, inventive construction|反义词：unimaginative design, conventional engineering|名词
