recomparing:/ˌriːkəmˈpɛəriŋ/| v. |重新比较；再比较 |The act of comparing again, often after new information or a change in perspective has been introduced.  It implies a more thorough or nuanced comparison than the initial one. |例句：After reviewing the updated data, we are recomparing the two proposals.  重新审查更新后的数据后，我们正在重新比较这两个提案。

例句：The judges are recomparing the contestants' performances to ensure fairness. 评委们正在重新比较参赛者的表演以确保公平。


近义词：re-evaluating, reconsidering, reviewing


反义词：accepting (without further comparison), ignoring

词性：动词
