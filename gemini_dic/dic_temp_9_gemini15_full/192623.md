scurrier:/ˈskʌriər/| n. |奔忙的人；快跑的人；小动物|A person or animal that scurries.  It often implies haste and nervousness.  |例句：The scurrier dashed across the road.  = 那个人飞快地穿过马路。

例句：A small scurrier darted through the undergrowth. = 一只小动物快速穿过灌木丛。


近义词：runner, hustler, scamperer, scuttler


反义词：loafer, idler


词性：名词


词源：源于动词“scurry”，意为“快速移动”，其词根可能与古英语词根“scirran”有关，意为“奔跑”。
