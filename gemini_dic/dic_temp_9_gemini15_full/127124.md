natives: /ˈneɪtɪvz/ | n. |本地人，土著居民 |The indigenous people of a land, especially those who were there before settlers arrived.  They are often distinguished from immigrants or newcomers. |例句：The natives welcomed the explorers with open arms.  当地居民张开双臂欢迎探险家们。|例句：The museum has a fascinating exhibit on the culture of the natives of the Amazon. 这个博物馆有一个关于亚马逊土著文化的迷人展览。|近义词：indigenous people, aboriginals, inhabitants |反义词：foreigners, immigrants, outsiders |名词
