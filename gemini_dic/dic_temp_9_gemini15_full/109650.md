leucaniline:/ˌluːkəˈnɪlɪn/| n. |无色品红，亮氨酸苯胺|A colorless triamino derivative of triphenylmethane; a leuco base.  It is a precursor to pararosaniline, a magenta dye. |The synthesis of leucaniline involves the reduction of pararosaniline.  无色品红的合成涉及对品红的还原。|近义词：leuco base (general term)|反义词：pararosaniline (its oxidized form)
