contraceptions:/ˌkɒntrəˈsɛpʃənz/ | n. |避孕措施；避孕方法 |Means or devices used to prevent pregnancy.  Contraception involves preventing fertilization of an egg by sperm, or preventing a fertilized egg from implanting in the uterus.  |例句：The clinic offers a range of contraceptives.  诊所提供各种避孕措施。

例句：She is considering different methods of contraception. 她正在考虑不同的避孕方法。


近义词：birth control, family planning


反义词：None (Antonyms are not directly applicable, as it refers to a process rather than a state)
