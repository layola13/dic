fecundation:/ˌfiːkʌnˈdeɪʃən/| n. |受精；使肥沃|The process by which a sperm fertilizes an egg, resulting in the formation of a zygote.  It is a crucial step in sexual reproduction. |例句：The fecundation of the ovum marks the beginning of a new life. 受精卵的受精标志着新生命的开始。|例句：The success rate of in vitro fecundation varies depending on several factors. 体外受精的成功率取决于多种因素。|近义词：fertilization, impregnation|反义词：sterility, barrenness|名词
