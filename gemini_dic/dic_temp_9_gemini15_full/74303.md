fractioning:/ˈfrækʃənɪŋ/| v.|分馏；分级；分离|The process of separating a mixture into its components, especially by distillation or other methods of separating substances with different boiling points or other physical properties.|例句：The crude oil was fractionated into gasoline, kerosene, and other products.  原油被分馏成汽油、煤油和其他产品。

例句：The scientist used chromatography to fractionate the complex mixture of proteins. 科学家使用色谱法分离复杂的蛋白质混合物。


近义词：separation, distillation, fractionation, resolving


反义词：combination, mixing, amalgamation


词性：动词
