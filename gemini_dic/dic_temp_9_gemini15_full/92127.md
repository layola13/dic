His hyperscrupulosity prevented him from completing the project on time.  = 他过分的谨慎导致他无法按时完成项目。

词源:

The word is derived from "scrupulosity," which comes from the Latin word "scrupulus," meaning "a small sharp stone" or "a small obstacle."  This evokes the feeling of being hampered by minor doubts or worries. The prefix "hyper-" intensifies the meaning, indicating an excessive or exaggerated degree of scrupulosity.
