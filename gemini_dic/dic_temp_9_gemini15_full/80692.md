gorgon:/ˈɡɔːrɡən/| n. |蛇发女妖，可怕的女人|A Gorgon is a monstrous female creature in Greek mythology, typically depicted with snakes for hair and a gaze that could turn people to stone.  They are often associated with death and destruction.  The most famous Gorgon is Medusa.|Example: The hero faced the terrifying Gorgon Medusa, her eyes filled with deadly power.  英雄面对着可怕的蛇发女妖美杜莎，她的眼睛充满了致命的魔力。|近义词：monster, fiend, hag|反义词：beauty, angel
