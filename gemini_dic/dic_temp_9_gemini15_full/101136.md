investigator: /ɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtər/ | n. |调查员，研究者 | A person who investigates; someone who conducts inquiries or research to discover facts or information.  |The investigator meticulously examined the crime scene.  调查员仔细检查了犯罪现场。| researcher, examiner, inquirer |  (no direct antonym, but could use terms like "obstructor" or "withholder of information" depending on context) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The investigator interviews witnesses. = 调查员正在采访证人。
The investigator collects evidence. = 调查员正在收集证据。

一般过去时:
The investigator interviewed the suspect. = 调查员采访了嫌疑人。
The investigator completed the report. = 调查员完成了报告。

现在完成时:
The investigator has interviewed all the witnesses. = 调查员已经采访了所有证人。
The investigator has gathered substantial evidence. = 调查员已经收集了大量证据。

过去完成时:
The investigator had already examined the scene before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，调查员已经检查过现场了。
The investigator had interviewed the key witness several times. = 调查员已经多次采访了关键证人。

将来时:
The investigator will analyze the data tomorrow. = 调查员明天将分析数据。
The investigator will present her findings next week. = 调查员下周将提交她的调查结果。

虚拟语气:
If the investigator had more time, he would have interviewed more witnesses. = 如果调查员有更多时间，他会采访更多证人。
It would be helpful if the investigator had access to more resources. = 如果调查员能够获得更多资源，将会很有帮助。

被动语态:
The crime scene was investigated thoroughly by the investigator. = 犯罪现场被调查员彻底调查了。
The evidence was analyzed by the investigator. = 证据由调查员分析。

疑问句:
Who is the lead investigator on this case? = 这个案子的主导调查员是谁？
What methods did the investigator use? = 调查员使用了什么方法？

否定句:
The investigator did not find any evidence of foul play. = 调查员没有发现任何犯规的证据。
The investigator was not able to locate the missing witness. = 调查员无法找到失踪的证人。
