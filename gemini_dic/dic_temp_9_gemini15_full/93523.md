imagers: /ˈɪmɪdʒərz/ | n. | 成像器；图像生成器 | Devices or systems that create images, often used in various scientific, medical, or industrial applications.  They can be used to capture, process, and display images.  Examples include digital cameras, medical imaging scanners (like MRI or CT scanners), and satellite sensors. | Example:  The astronomers used advanced imagers to capture images of distant galaxies. 天文学家们使用先进的成像器拍摄了遥远星系的图像。


The new imagers provide superior resolution and clarity. 新的成像器提供了更高的分辨率和清晰度。

近义词: image sensors, image producers, visualizers

反义词:  (No direct antonyms, as it's a device, not a concept with an opposite)

词源:  The word "imager" is derived from the noun "image" (a likeness or representation)  and the suffix "-er" (which denotes an agent or doer).  Therefore, "imager" literally means a device or system that creates or produces images.
