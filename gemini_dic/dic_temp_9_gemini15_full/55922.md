divvy:/ˈdɪvi/| vt. |分配，分享|To divide or share something, especially money or goods, among several people.|They divvied up the profits among themselves. 他们把利润分给了自己。|allocate, distribute, share|concentrate, hoard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They divvy up the work every week. = 他们每周都会分工。
We divvy the costs fairly. = 我们公平地分担费用。

一般过去时:
We divvied the pizza among the six of us. = 我们把披萨分给了我们六个人。
They divvied the spoils after the battle. = 战斗结束后，他们瓜分了战利品。

现在完成时:
They have divvied up the inheritance. = 他们已经分配了遗产。
We have divvied the responsibilities between the two teams. = 我们已经将责任分配给了两个团队。

过去完成时:
They had divvied the land before the settlers arrived. = 定居者到来之前，他们已经划分了土地。
She had divvied up her savings before the trip. = 旅行前，她已经分配了她的积蓄。

将来时:
We will divvy the cake after dinner. = 我们晚饭后会分蛋糕。
They will divvy the workload equally. = 他们将平均分配工作量。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would divvy it among the needy. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会把它分给穷人。
I wish they would divvy the resources more fairly. = 我希望他们能更公平地分配资源。

被动语态:
The profits were divvied up among the shareholders. = 利润被分给了股东们。
The tasks were divvied out to the team members. = 任务被分配给了团队成员。

疑问句:
How will we divvy up the expenses? = 我们将如何分摊费用？
How did they divvy the responsibility? = 他们是如何分担责任的？

否定句:
They didn't divvy the money fairly. = 他们没有公平地分配钱。
We won't divvy the workload unequally. = 我们不会不平均分配工作量。


PHRASE->
divvy up: 分配，分享
divvy out: 分发
divvy something between/among: 在…之间/之间分配某物
divvy the spoils: 瓜分战利品
divvy and conquer: 分而治之
