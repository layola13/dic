lauds:/lɔːdz/ | v. | 赞扬；赞美 | To praise highly; to extol.  |例句：The critics lauded his performance as brilliant. 评论家们高度赞扬了他的精彩表演。
例句：She was lauded for her tireless work for charity. 她因其为慈善事业的辛勤工作而受到赞扬。|近义词：praise, commend, acclaim, extol |反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce |动词

一般现在时:
He lauds his team's efforts regularly. = 他经常赞扬他团队的努力。
They laud the new policy's positive impact. = 他们赞扬这项新政策的积极影响。

一般过去时:
The newspaper lauded the athlete's achievements. = 报纸赞扬了这位运动员的成就。
The professor lauded the students' insightful questions. = 教授赞扬了学生们富有洞察力的提问。

现在完成时:
She has lauded the benefits of healthy eating for years. = 多年来，她一直在赞扬健康饮食的好处。
The company has lauded its employees' dedication to innovation. = 公司一直赞扬员工们对创新的奉献精神。

过去完成时:
They had lauded the project's success before the final presentation. = 在最终演示之前，他们已经赞扬了项目的成功。
The critics had lauded the director's previous film. = 评论家们之前已经赞扬过这位导演的上一部电影。

将来时:
The mayor will laud the volunteers' contributions at the ceremony. = 市长将在仪式上赞扬志愿者的贡献。
We will laud the team's hard work once the project is completed. = 项目完成后，我们将赞扬团队的辛勤工作。

虚拟语气:
If I were to win, my family would laud my achievement. = 如果我赢了，我的家人会赞扬我的成就。
I wish they would laud the efforts of the support staff. = 我希望他们能赞扬支持人员的努力。

被动语态:
His work was lauded by the art community. = 他的作品受到艺术界的赞扬。
The new design was lauded for its simplicity and elegance. = 新的设计因其简洁优雅而受到赞扬。

疑问句:
Will the company laud its employees' performance? = 公司会赞扬员工的表现吗？
Does the public laud the government's response to the crisis? = 公众是否赞扬政府对危机的应对？

否定句:
The judge did not laud the defendant's actions. = 法官没有赞扬被告的行为。
They did not laud the company's questionable practices. = 他们没有赞扬公司有问题的做法。
