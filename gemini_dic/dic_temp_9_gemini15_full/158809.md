plumage:/ˈpluːmɪdʒ/| n.| 羽毛；羽衣 |The external covering of feathers on a bird.  It encompasses all the feathers, their arrangement, and their coloration.  Plumage can vary significantly depending on the species, age, sex, and even the season. |例句：The peacock's plumage is renowned for its iridescent beauty.  孔雀的羽毛以其彩虹般的美丽而闻名。

The bird's vibrant plumage attracted many admirers. 这只鸟鲜艳的羽毛吸引了许多仰慕者。|近义词：feathers, coat (of feathers) |反义词：bare skin
