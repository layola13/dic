histogram:/ˈhɪstəˌɡræm/| n. |直方图，柱状图 |A histogram is a graphical representation of the distribution of numerical data.  It is an estimate of the probability distribution of a continuous variable.  A histogram is constructed by dividing the entire range of values into a series of intervals, and then counting how many values fall into each interval. The counts are then plotted as bars, with the height of each bar representing the frequency of values in that interval. |The histogram showed a clear normal distribution of the data.  这个直方图显示数据呈现明显的正态分布。
