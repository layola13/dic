protomartyr: /ˌproʊtoʊˈmɑːrtər/ | n. |早期殉道者，先驱殉道者 | A protomartyr is a person who is considered to be among the first martyrs of a particular cause, religion, or movement.  They often paved the way for later martyrs by their example and sacrifice. |Example: Stephen is considered a protomartyr of Christianity, as he was one of the earliest Christians to be killed for his faith.  史蒂芬被认为是基督教的早期殉道者之一，因为他是最早为信仰而被杀害的基督徒之一。|近义词:  pioneer martyr, early martyr |反义词: persecutor, oppressor |名词
