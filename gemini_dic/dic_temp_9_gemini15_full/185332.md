retrieved:/rɪˈtriːvd/| v.|检索；取回；找回 |To bring or get something back from a place where it was stored or kept; to obtain information from a database or other source.  |例句：I retrieved my coat from the cloakroom. 我从衣帽间取回了我的外套。
例句：The police retrieved the stolen jewels. 警方找回了被盗的珠宝。|近义词：recovered, regained, fetched|反义词：lost, misplaced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retrieves information from the internet daily. = 他每天从互联网上检索信息。
She retrieves her lost dog every time it runs away. = 每次她的狗跑丢了，她都会把它找回来。


一般过去时:
The firefighters retrieved the injured people from the burning building. = 消防员从燃烧的建筑物中救出了受伤的人。
I retrieved the file from the trash can. = 我从回收站找回了那个文件。


现在完成时:
They have retrieved all the missing documents. = 他们已经找回了所有丢失的文件。
She has retrieved the data from the server. = 她已经从服务器上检索到了数据。


过去完成时:
After searching for hours, he had retrieved his lost keys. = 搜寻数小时后，他找回了丢失的钥匙。
The investigators had retrieved crucial evidence before the trial. = 调查人员在审判前已经找到了关键证据。


将来时:
I will retrieve the package from the post office tomorrow. = 我明天将从邮局取回包裹。
The team will retrieve the satellite data next week. = 团队下周将检索卫星数据。


虚拟语气:
If I had remembered the password, I would have retrieved my files. = 如果我记得密码，我就能找回我的文件了。
It would be helpful if you could retrieve that information for me. = 如果你能帮我检索到那些信息，那就太好了。


被动语态:
The lost data was retrieved by the IT team. = 丢失的数据被IT团队找回了。
The information was retrieved from the company's database. = 信息是从公司的数据库中检索到的。


疑问句:

否定句:
I couldn't retrieve the document because of a system error. = 由于系统错误，我无法检索到该文档。
She failed to retrieve the lost memory card. = 她没能找回丢失的存储卡。
