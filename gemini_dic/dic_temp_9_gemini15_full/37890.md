complaint:/kəmˈpleɪnt/ | n. |抱怨，投诉，疾病 | a statement that something is unsatisfactory or wrong; an expression of dissatisfaction or disapproval.  |例句：He made a complaint about the noise. 他抱怨噪音。|例句：The customer lodged a complaint about the faulty product.  顾客就该有缺陷的产品提出了投诉。|近义词：grievance, protest, objection |反义词：praise, commendation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes many complaints. = 他有很多抱怨。
She complains about the weather every day. = 她每天都抱怨天气。

一般过去时:
They complained about the service. = 他们抱怨服务。
He made a complaint to the manager. = 他向经理投诉。

现在完成时:
I have complained to the authorities several times. = 我已经向当局投诉过几次了。
She has had many complaints about her job. = 她对她的工作有很多抱怨。

过去完成时:
He had complained about the problem before, but nothing was done. = 他之前就抱怨过这个问题，但是什么也没做。
They had lodged several complaints before they finally got a response. = 他们提出好几次投诉后才最终得到回应。

将来时:
I will complain to the head office if the problem is not solved. = 如果问题得不到解决，我将向总部投诉。
She will likely complain again if the service doesn't improve. = 如果服务没有改善，她很可能会再次抱怨。

虚拟语气:
I would complain if I were you. = 如果我是你，我会投诉。
If she had complained earlier, the situation might have been different. = 如果她早点投诉，情况可能就不一样了。

被动语态:
The complaint was investigated thoroughly. = 投诉受到了彻底的调查。
Many complaints are received daily. = 每天都会收到许多投诉。

疑问句:
Do you have any complaints? = 你有什么抱怨吗？
What is your complaint? = 你的投诉是什么？

否定句:
I have no complaints about the food. = 我对食物没有意见。
He didn't complain about the long wait. = 他没有抱怨漫长的等待。


PHRASE->
file a complaint = 提出投诉
lodge a complaint = 提出正式投诉
make a complaint = 抱怨；投诉
handle a complaint = 处理投诉
address a complaint = 处理投诉
