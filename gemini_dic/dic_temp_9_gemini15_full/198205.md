shortages:/ˈʃɔːrtɪdʒɪz/| n.|短缺，不足 |A lack or insufficiency of something.  Shortages often occur due to increased demand, decreased supply, or disruptions in the supply chain.|例句：The country is facing serious food shortages. 这个国家正面临严重的粮食短缺。|例句：Due to the pandemic, there were shortages of essential medical supplies. 由于疫情，重要的医疗用品短缺。|近义词：deficiencies, scarcities, deficits |反义词：abundance, surplus, plenty |名词
