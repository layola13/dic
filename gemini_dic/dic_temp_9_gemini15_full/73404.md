/fɒrɪn/|adj.|外国的；外来的；异国的|Of, from, or characteristic of a country other than one's own.|Foreign countries often have different customs.  外国通常有不同的风俗习惯。|The foreign policy of that country is complex.  那个国家的对外政策很复杂。|domestic, international|domestic, native|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This restaurant serves foreign food. = 这家餐厅供应外国菜肴。
He speaks several foreign languages. = 他会说几种外语。


一般过去时:
She lived in a foreign country for many years. = 她在一个外国住了很多年。
They studied foreign history in college. = 他们在大学学习外国历史。


现在完成时:
I have visited many foreign countries. = 我已经访问过许多外国。
She has learned a lot about foreign cultures. = 她已经学习了很多关于外国文化的知识。


过去完成时:

将来时:
