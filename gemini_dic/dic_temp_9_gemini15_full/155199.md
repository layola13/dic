pezizoid:/ˈpɛzɪzɔɪd/| adj.|  pezizoid 的| 形似盘菌的；盘菌状的 |Resembling a peziza; cup-shaped.  The term is primarily used in mycology (the study of fungi) to describe the shape of certain fungal structures, particularly ascocarps (fruiting bodies).  These structures are characterized by a cup or saucer-like form.|Example: The fungus exhibited a distinct pezizoid ascocarp.|近义词：cup-shaped, saucer-shaped|反义词：flat, elongated
