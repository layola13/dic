pen-name: /ˈpen neɪm/ | n. | 笔名 | A pen name is a fictitious name used by an author instead of their real name, often for professional or personal reasons.  It is also known as a pseudonym or nom de plume. | Example:  Many authors use pen names to protect their privacy or to market their work to a different audience.  许多作家使用笔名来保护他们的隐私或将作品推销给不同的受众。 |近义词：pseudonym, nom de plume |反义词：real name


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.
