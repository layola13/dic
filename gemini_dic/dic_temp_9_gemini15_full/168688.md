procrastinate:/prəˈkræstɪneɪt/ | v. |拖延；耽搁 | To delay or postpone something.  |例句：I keep procrastinating on my essay. = 我一直拖延写我的论文。|例句：He procrastinated until the last minute to finish the project. = 他拖到最后一分钟才完成项目。|近义词：delay, postpone, defer |反义词：expedite, hasten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He procrastinates on his homework every day. = 他每天都拖延做作业。
They procrastinate frequently, leading to poor time management. = 他们经常拖延，导致时间管理不善。


一般过去时:
She procrastinated on her assignment and submitted it late. = 她拖延了作业，提交晚了。
We procrastinated about making a decision, which ultimately hurt our chances. = 我们在做决定上拖延了，最终损害了我们的机会。


现在完成时:
I have procrastinated for too long; now I'm in a rush. = 我拖延太久了，现在我赶时间了。
They have procrastinated so much that the deadline is approaching. = 他们拖延了太多，截止日期快到了。


过去完成时:
He had procrastinated throughout the semester, which led to his failing grade. = 他整个学期都在拖延，导致他不及格。
She had procrastinated on her preparations, and therefore wasn't ready for the presentation. = 她拖延了准备工作，因此没有准备好演讲。


将来时:
I will procrastinate no more; I will start working on the project immediately. = 我不会再拖延了；我将立即开始这个项目。
They will likely procrastinate again, unless they set firm deadlines for themselves. = 他们可能会再次拖延，除非他们为自己设定明确的截止日期。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't procrastinated, I would have finished the report on time. = 如果我没有拖延，我就能按时完成报告。
I wish I hadn't procrastinated; I would have had more free time now. = 我真希望我没有拖延；我现在会有更多空闲时间。


被动语态:
The task was procrastinated on by the team, causing a delay in the project. = 团队拖延了这项任务，导致项目延误。
The decision was continually procrastinated, leading to uncertainty and frustration. = 决定不断被拖延，导致不确定性和沮丧。


疑问句:
Why do you procrastinate so much? = 你为什么总是拖延？
Are they procrastinating on purpose, or are they simply overwhelmed? = 他们是在故意拖延，还是仅仅是被压垮了？


否定句:
I don't procrastinate; I always get my work done on time. = 我不拖延；我总是按时完成我的工作。
They didn't procrastinate this time; they finished the project ahead of schedule. = 他们这次没有拖延；他们提前完成了项目。


PHRASE->
procrastinate on: 拖延...
procrastinate about:  犹豫是否...
procrastinate no more: 不再拖延
stop procrastinating: 停止拖延
avoid procrastinating: 避免拖延
