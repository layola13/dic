polygastrian:/ˌpɒliˈɡæstriən/| adj.|多胃的；多胃动物的|Having or relating to many stomachs.  The term is primarily used in zoology to describe animals with multiple stomachs, each with a specific digestive function.|Example:  The polygastrian ruminant efficiently digests cellulose. 多胃反刍动物有效地消化纤维素。|近义词：multi-stomached|反义词：monogastric
