place:/pleɪs/ | n. | 地方，位置，场所 | A place where people live, work, or spend time.  一个人们居住、工作或消遣的地方。| The park is a nice place to relax. 公园是放松的好地方。 | location, spot, site | nowhere, absence | 名词
