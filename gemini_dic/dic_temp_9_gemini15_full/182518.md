rent-free:/rentˈfriː/| adj. | 免费居住的；不付房租的 |  Without having to pay rent.  This typically refers to a situation where someone is living in a property without paying rent, often because it's provided by someone else, or they are occupying it illegally. | Example: He's been living rent-free in his parents' basement for five years. 他在他父母的地下室免费住了五年。  |近义词：free of charge, gratis | 反义词：rent-paying, costly
