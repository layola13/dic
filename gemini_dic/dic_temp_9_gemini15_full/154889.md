pervious:/ˈpɜːrviəs/| adj.|可渗透的；能穿透的；易受影响的|Allowing liquid or gas to pass through; permeable.  Also, easily influenced or affected.|The soil is permeable, allowing water to seep through. 这土壤是渗透性的，允许水渗透通过。|The young man was pervious to flattery.  这位年轻人容易受奉承影响。|porous, permeable, penetrable|impermeable, impervious, resistant|形容词
