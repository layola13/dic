falterer:/ˈfɔːltərər/|/ˈfɔːltərə(r)/| n. |犹豫者，迟疑者，动摇者 |A falterer is someone who hesitates or wavers in their actions or beliefs.  They lack decisiveness and may be easily discouraged. |Example:  He was a falterer in the face of adversity, constantly doubting his abilities. 他面对逆境时犹豫不决，不断怀疑自己的能力。|近义词：hesitator, waverer, doubter |反义词：decisive person, resolute person


USAGE->
