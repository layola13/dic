over-honoring: /ˌoʊvərˈɒnərɪŋ/ | v. | 过度推崇；过分尊重 |  The act of excessively honoring or praising someone or something, often to the point of being excessive or inappropriate. It implies an imbalance where the honor bestowed surpasses the deserving merit.  |  例句：The company's over-honoring of its CEO led to resentment among other employees. 公司对首席执行官的过度推崇导致其他员工不满。

例句：His over-honoring of tradition prevented him from adapting to modern methods. 他对传统的过度推崇阻止了他适应现代方法。


近义词：excessive praise, overestimation, overvaluation, hyperbole


反义词：underestimation, undervaluation, neglect, disregard


词性：动词
