malagasy:/ˌmæləˈɡæsi/| adj. n.|马达加斯加的；马达加斯加人；马达加斯加语|Of or relating to Madagascar, its people, or their language.  |例句：She is studying Malagasy culture. 她正在研究马达加斯加文化。

例句：He speaks fluent Malagasy. 他说一口流利的马达加斯加语。


词源:From Malagasy (the language of Madagascar), ultimately from Proto-Malayo-Polynesian.


用法说明:

Malagasy can be used as both an adjective and a noun.  As an adjective, it describes things related to Madagascar, its people, or their language. As a noun, it refers to the people of Madagascar or their language.


近义词: (as an adjective): Madagascan


反义词:  N/A (There isn't a direct antonym, as it describes a specific geographic and cultural location.)


词性: adjective, noun
