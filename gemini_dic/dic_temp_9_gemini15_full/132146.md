noncultured:/ˌnɒnˈkʌltʃəd/| adj. |未开化的；未培养的；粗鲁的 |Lacking refinement or cultivation; unsophisticated; rude.  It often implies a lack of exposure to art, literature, or refined social customs.  The term can be considered somewhat outdated and potentially offensive, as it carries a judgmental connotation. |例句：His noncultured behavior at the dinner party embarrassed his family.  他的粗鲁行为在晚宴上让家人很尴尬。

例句：The noncultured soil yielded a poor harvest.  未经改良的土壤收成很差。


近义词：uncultivated, unrefined, boorish, uncouth, uncivilized, rough, crude

反义词：cultured, refined, sophisticated, polished, civilized
