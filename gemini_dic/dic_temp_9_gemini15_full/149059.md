owns:/oʊnz/| v.|拥有，拥有；持有|To have possession of (something); to be the legal owner of (something).  It can also refer to possessing a characteristic or quality.|例句：She owns a beautiful house. 她拥有一栋美丽的房子。

例句：He owns a successful business. 他拥有一家成功的企业。


近义词：possesses, controls, holds, has


反义词：lacks, loses


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He owns a car. = 他拥有一辆车。
They own a large farm. = 他们拥有一座大型农场。


一般过去时:
She owned several businesses before retiring. = 她退休前拥有几家企业。
We owned a dog when I was a child. = 我小时候我们养过一条狗。


现在完成时:
I have owned this watch for twenty years. = 我拥有这块手表已经二十年了。
They have owned that property since 1990. = 自1990年以来他们就拥有那处房产。


过去完成时:
He had owned the company for five years before selling it. = 他拥有那家公司五年后才卖掉。
She had owned many cars in her life. = 她一生中拥有过许多汽车。


将来时:
They will own a bigger house next year. = 他们明年将拥有更大的房子。
I will own my own business one day. = 我总有一天会拥有自己的生意。


虚拟语气:
If I owned a yacht, I would sail around the world. = 如果我拥有一艘游艇，我就会环游世界。
I wish I owned a house in the countryside. = 我希望我在乡下拥有一所房子。


被动语态:
The land is owned by the government. = 这块土地归政府所有。
The painting was owned by a wealthy collector. = 这幅画属于一位富有的收藏家。


疑问句:
Do you own a computer? = 你拥有电脑吗？
Does she own that car? = 那辆车是她拥有的吗？


否定句:
He doesn't own a car. = 他没有车。
They don't own any property. = 他们没有任何房产。


PHRASE->
own up: 承认；坦白
own goal: (足球)乌龙球
on one's own: 独自地；单独地
of one's own: 自己的
to own: 归于；属于
