dosology:/doʊˈsɒlədʒi/ | n. |剂量学，给药学 |The study of dosage and its effects.  It encompasses the principles of determining the appropriate amount of a medication to administer to achieve a desired therapeutic outcome, considering factors like patient characteristics, drug properties, and route of administration. |Example:  Accurate dosology is crucial for effective treatment and minimizing adverse effects.  精确的剂量学对于有效治疗和最大限度地减少不良反应至关重要。|近义词：pharmacokinetics, posology |反义词：N/A
