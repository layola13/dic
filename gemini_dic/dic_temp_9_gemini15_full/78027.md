gentrifies:/ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪz/| v.|使中产阶级化；改善；改良|The process of changing a place from being a mainly working-class area to a mainly middle-class one, often causing the original working-class people to move out because they cannot afford to live there anymore.  |例句：The neighborhood is rapidly gentrifying, pushing out long-term residents.  这片街区正在迅速中产阶级化，迫使长期居民搬迁。|例句：The city council is trying to gentrify the downtown area by investing in new infrastructure and businesses. 市议会正试图通过投资新的基础设施和企业来改善市中心地区。|近义词：improve, upgrade, renovate, upscale|反义词：deteriorate, decline, decay|动词
