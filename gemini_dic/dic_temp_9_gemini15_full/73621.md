forewoman:/ˈfɔːrwʊmən/ | n. | 女工头，女领班 | A woman who supervises a group of workers, especially in a factory or on a construction site.  | The forewoman instructed her team on the day's tasks. 女工头指示她的团队当天要做的任务。| supervisor, manager, superintendent |  subordinate, worker | 名词
