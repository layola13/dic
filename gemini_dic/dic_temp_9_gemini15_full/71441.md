flambeed:/flæmˈbeɪd/| adj.| flambé过的，用酒点燃的 |  Describes food that has been flamed with liquor, usually brandy or rum, just before serving to add flavor and a dramatic presentation. | Example: The chef prepared a delicious flambéed banana dessert.  厨师准备了一道美味的火焰香蕉甜点。|近义词：flamed, ignited |反义词：uncooked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef flambées the crêpes with Grand Marnier. = 厨师用君度橙酒将薄饼 flambé。
He flambées his desserts regularly. = 他经常将他的甜点 flambé。


一般过去时:
She flambéed the bananas with rum. = 她用朗姆酒将香蕉 flambé。
The waiter flambéed the cherries at the table. = 服务员在桌边将樱桃 flambé。


现在完成时:
We have flambéed the strawberries for the guests. = 我们已经为客人 flambé 了草莓。
