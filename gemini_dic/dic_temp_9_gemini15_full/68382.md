fallout: /ˈfɔːlˌaʊt/ | n. | 后果；辐射尘；（核爆炸后的）放射性尘埃 | The consequences or results of an action or event, especially a negative one; radioactive particles dispersed into the atmosphere by a nuclear explosion. |


例句：

The fallout from the scandal damaged his reputation.  这场丑闻的后果损害了他的名誉。

The nuclear fallout contaminated the water supply. 核辐射尘污染了水源。


近义词：consequences, aftermath, repercussions, residue, debris


反义词：cause, origin, source (in the sense of being the opposite of the result)


词性：名词
