reputing:/rɪˈpjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 认为；声称；说；传闻 |  To speak of or think of in a particular way; to have a reputation.  This word is less common in modern English and often replaced by synonyms. |  例句：He was reputing himself as a great scientist. 他自称是一位伟大的科学家。

例句：The rumor reputing him dishonest spread quickly.  关于他不诚实的谣言迅速传播开来。


近义词：considering, regarding, deeming, alleging, claiming, reporting

反义词：disproving, refuting, denying


词性：动词
