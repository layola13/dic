scenarioize:/ˌsɪˈnærioʊaɪz/ | vt. | 将…设想成情景；使…具体化；为…编写场景 | To create or describe a possible scenario for something; to make something into a scenario.  | Example: Let's scenarioize the potential impact of climate change on coastal communities.  让我们设想一下气候变化对沿海社区的潜在影响。|近义词：visualize, imagine, envision, project | 反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We scenarioize the risks of the new project every week. = 我们每周都会设想新项目的风险。
They scenarioize different marketing strategies. = 他们设想不同的营销策略。


一般过去时:
The team scenarioized the worst-case scenario. = 团队设想了最坏的情况。
She scenarioized her future career path. = 她设想了她未来的职业道路。


现在完成时:
We have scenarioized several possible outcomes. = 我们已经设想了几种可能的结果。
They have scenarioized the launch of the new product. = 他们已经为新产品的发布制定了场景。


过去完成时:
They had scenarioized the crisis before it happened. = 危机发生之前，他们就已经设想了。
We had scenarioized all the potential problems before the meeting. = 会议之前，我们已经设想了所有潜在的问题。


将来时:
We will scenarioize the impact of the new policy. = 我们将设想新政策的影响。
They will scenarioize the effects of the new technology. = 他们将设想这项新技术的效用。


虚拟语气:
If we scenarioized the problem earlier, we could have avoided it. = 如果我们早点设想这个问题，我们就能避免它了。
I wish we had scenarioized the potential difficulties. = 我真希望我们当时设想了潜在的困难。


被动语态:
The potential risks were scenarioized by the experts. = 潜在的风险由专家们进行了设想。
The different possibilities were scenarioized in the report. = 报告中设想了不同的可能性。


疑问句:
Have you scenarioized the consequences? = 你设想过后果了吗？
How did they scenarioize the problem? = 他们是如何设想这个问题的？


否定句:
We didn't scenarioize the possibility of failure. = 我们没有设想失败的可能性。
They didn't scenarioize the long-term effects. = 他们没有设想长远的影响。


PHRASE->
scenarioize the future = 设想未来
scenarioize the risks = 设想风险
scenarioize the impact = 设想影响
scenarioize a crisis = 设想危机
