self-improvements: /ˌsɛlf ɪmˈpruːvmənts/ | n. | 自我提升；自我改进 |  The act of making oneself better or more effective, often through conscious effort.  This typically involves identifying areas for growth and actively working to improve them.  | 例句：He embarked on a program of self-improvements to enhance his career prospects. 他开始了一系列自我提升计划以提高他的职业前景。


She dedicated her weekends to self-improvements, including learning a new language and taking up yoga. 她把周末都用来自我提升，包括学习一门外语和练习瑜伽。


近义词：self-development, personal growth, betterment, advancement | 反义词：self-neglect, self-destruction, deterioration | 词性：名词
