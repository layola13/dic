rebukers: /rɪˈbjuːkərz/ | n. | 责备者；训斥者 | Those who rebuke or reprimand.  People who express strong disapproval of someone or something. |例句：The rebukers in the congregation were known for their harsh words. 会众中的责备者以言语刻薄而闻名。

例句：The teacher, a stern rebuker of laziness, kept the class in order. 老师，一个严厉责备懒惰的人，让课堂井然有序。


近义词：critics, censurers, admonishers, reprovers | 反义词：praisers, flatterers, supporters
