industries:/ˈɪndəstriːz/| n. |产业，工业，行业|The word "industries" refers to a group of businesses or companies that are involved in the same type of economic activity.  It can encompass a wide range of sectors, from manufacturing and technology to agriculture and services.  The plural form of "industry".|例句：The manufacturing industries are facing a challenging economic climate. 制造业正面临充满挑战的经济环境。

The service industries are booming in this city.  这座城市的服務業蓬勃發展。


近义词：trades, businesses, sectors, branches|反义词：None (antonyms are context-dependent and would describe the opposite of a specific industry)|名词
