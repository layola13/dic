guttiferous:/ɡʌˈtɪfərəs/| adj.|分泌树脂的，产胶的|Producing or containing resin or gum.  The guttiferous trees of the rainforest provide valuable resources.|近义词：resinous, gummy|反义词：resinless


例句：

The guttiferous sap of the tree was used by the indigenous people to make adhesives.

树木分泌的树脂被当地人用来制作粘合剂。


Many guttiferous plants are found in tropical regions.

许多产胶植物都生长在热带地区。
