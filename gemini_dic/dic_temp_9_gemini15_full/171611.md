pulmoniferous:/ˌpʌlˈmoʊnɪfərəs/| adj.|具有肺的；有肺的 |Having lungs or lung-like structures.  The term refers to animals that breathe using lungs or lung-like organs.  | Example:  Many pulmoniferous animals inhabit terrestrial environments. 许多有肺动物栖息在陆地环境中。|近义词：lung-bearing, lunged|反义词：gill-bearing, aquatic (in the context of respiration)
