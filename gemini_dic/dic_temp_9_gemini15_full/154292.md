peristomial:/ˌpɛrɪˈstoʊmiəl/| adj.|口周的；围绕口的|Relating to or surrounding the mouth or opening of a structure, especially the mouth of a gastropod shell.  The term is often used in biology and zoology to describe structures associated with the mouth of an organism.|例句：The peristomial membrane protects the delicate gills of the snail. 口周膜保护着蜗牛娇嫩的鳃。The peristomial teeth are used for scraping algae from rocks. 口周齿用于刮除岩石上的藻类。|近义词：oral, buccal, circumoral|反义词：apical
