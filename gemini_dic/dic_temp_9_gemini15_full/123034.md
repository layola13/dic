mone:/moʊn/| n.|货币；钱|Money is the medium of exchange. 货币是交换媒介。|近义词：cash, funds, currency|反义词：poverty, destitution|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have some money. = 我有一些钱。
He spends a lot of money. = 他花很多钱。

一般过去时:
She earned a lot of money last year. = 她去年赚了很多钱。
They lost all their money. = 他们失去了所有的钱。


现在完成时:
We have saved enough money for the trip. = 我们已经为这次旅行存够了钱。
He has already spent all his money. = 他已经花光了他的所有钱。


过去完成时:
They had saved a considerable amount of money before they retired. = 他们退休前已经存了一大笔钱。
I had lost my money before I realized it. = 我在意识到之前就已经丢了钱。


将来时:
I will earn more money next year. = 我明年会赚更多钱。
She will need more money to buy a car. = 她需要更多钱才能买车。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would travel the world. = 如果我有更多钱，我就会环游世界。
I wish I had more money. = 我真希望我有更多钱。


被动语态:
The money was stolen. = 钱被偷了。
The money is being counted. = 钱正在被清点。


疑问句:
How much money do you have? = 你有多少存款？
Do you have enough money? = 你有足够的钱吗？


否定句:
I don't have any money. = 我没有钱。
He didn't have enough money to buy the house. = 他没有足够的钱买房子。


PHRASE->
make money = 赚钱
run out of money = 花光钱
save money = 省钱
borrow money = 借钱
waste money = 浪费钱
