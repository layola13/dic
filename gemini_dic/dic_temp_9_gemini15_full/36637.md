collectivized:/kəˈlɛktaɪzd/| v. |集体化；公有化|To bring under collective ownership or control; to organize or manage as a collective.|The government collectivized the farms. 政府对农场进行了集体化。|The kibbutzim collectivized their resources. 基布兹集体化了他们的资源。|nationalized, socialized|privatized, individualized


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They collectivize the land. = 他们将土地集体化。
The company collectivizes its resources. = 这家公司将其资源进行集体化。

The Soviet Union collectivized agriculture. = 苏联对农业进行了集体化。

现在完成时:
Many countries have collectivized their industries. = 许多国家已经将它们的工业集体化了。
The government has collectivized the transportation system. = 政府已经将交通系统集体化了。


过去完成时:
Before the revolution, they had collectivized some factories. = 在革命之前，他们已经将一些工厂集体化了。
