posthumanism:/ˈpoʊsthjʊmənɪzəm/ | n. | 后人类主义 |  A philosophical and cultural movement that questions the traditional humanist assumptions about the centrality of human beings in the universe and the unique status of human reason and consciousness.  It explores the blurring boundaries between humans and machines, humans and animals, and humans and the environment, often questioning the very concept of a stable human identity.  |  The rise of artificial intelligence has fueled much discussion within posthumanism. | 近义词：transhumanism (部分重叠), anti-humanism (部分重叠) | 反义词：humanism | 词性：名词
