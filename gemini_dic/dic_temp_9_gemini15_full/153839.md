perceptibly:/pərˈsɛptəbli/| adv. |可感知地；明显地 |  Noticeably or in a way that can be easily seen or felt.  |The room perceptibly cooled as the sun went down.  房间在太阳下山后明显变凉了。 |The change in his mood was perceptible to everyone. 他情绪的变化每个人都能感觉到。| noticeably, visibly, appreciably |imperceptibly, imperceivably |副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature perceptibly rises during the day. 白天温度明显上升。
He perceptibly slows down when he gets tired. 他累了的时候明显慢下来了。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

虚拟语气:
If the change were perceptible, we would have noticed it. 如果变化是明显的，我们会注意到的。
If the improvement were more perceptible, we would be more confident. 如果改进更明显，我们会更有信心。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
