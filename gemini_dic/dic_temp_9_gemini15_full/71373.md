flagellum: /fləˈdʒɛləm/ | n. |鞭毛；纤毛 | A slender, threadlike appendage that enables many protozoa, bacteria, spermatozoa, etc., to swim.  It's a whip-like structure used for locomotion or movement. | Example: The bacterium propelled itself through the liquid using its single flagellum.  细菌利用其单根鞭毛在液体中推进自身。


The flagella of the sperm cell allow it to swim towards the egg. 精子的鞭毛使它能够游向卵子。

近义词：cilia (if referring to multiple short appendages), appendage, filament

反义词：无直接反义词，可根据语境选择相对的反义词，例如：immobile structure

词源:
