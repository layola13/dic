self-representations:/ˌsɛlfˌrɛprɪˈzɛnˈteɪʃənz/| n. |自我表征，自我呈现 |The ways in which individuals or groups portray themselves to others.  This can involve conscious choices about how to present oneself, or unconscious processes that shape how one is perceived. Self-representations can vary depending on context and audience.  |Example:  Her self-representations on social media were carefully curated to project an image of success and happiness.  她的社交媒体自我形象经过精心策划，以展现成功和快乐的形象。|近义词：self-portrayals, self-presentations, images, portrayals|反义词：N/A
