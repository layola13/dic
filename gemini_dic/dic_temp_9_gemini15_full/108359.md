lavishes:/ˈlævɪʃɪz/| v.|挥霍，浪费；慷慨给予 |To spend or use (something) in a very generous or extravagant way.|例句：She lavishes affection on her grandchildren. 她对孙子孙女非常疼爱。|例句：They lavished money on their new house. 他们在新房上挥霍了钱财。|近义词：squander, spendthrift, bestow |反义词：scrimp, save, conserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lavishes praise on his employees. = 他对他的员工赞不绝口。
She lavishes attention on her garden. = 她非常细致地打理她的花园。

一般过去时:
They lavished gifts on their friends. = 他们慷慨地赠送礼物给朋友们。
He lavished money on a luxurious vacation. = 他在奢华的假期上挥霍了金钱。

现在完成时:
She has lavished care on her sick mother. = 她悉心照料她生病的母亲。
They have lavished resources on this project. = 他们在这个项目上投入了大量的资源。

过去完成时:
He had lavished attention on his painting for months. = 他几个月来一直精心创作他的画作。
They had lavished praise on the new film before its release. = 在新电影上映之前，他们对其赞赏有加。

将来时:
She will lavish her love on her newborn child. = 她将把爱倾注在她新生儿身上。
They will lavish money on a new car. = 他们将在一辆新车上挥霍金钱。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would lavish it on travel. = 如果我有钱，我会把它花在旅行上。
I wish I could lavish more time on my hobbies. = 我希望我能有更多的时间在我的爱好上。

被动语态:
The team was lavished with praise after their victory. = 团队在获胜后受到了赞扬。
The project was lavished with funding. = 该项目获得了充足的资金。

疑问句:
Does she lavish attention on her pets? = 她对她的宠物很关注吗？
Do they lavish gifts on their children? = 他们对孩子很慷慨吗？

否定句:
He doesn't lavish money on unnecessary things. = 他不会把钱花在不必要的东西上。
She didn't lavish praise on his work. = 她并没有对他的作品赞赏有加。


PHRASE->
