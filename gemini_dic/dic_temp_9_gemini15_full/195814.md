send:/send/| vt.|发送，寄送，派遣|to cause (something) to go or be taken to a person or place.|例句：Please send the package to my office. 请把包裹寄到我的办公室。|例句：The company sent a representative to the meeting. 公司派了一名代表去参加会议。|近义词：transmit, dispatch, forward|反义词：receive, collect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I send emails every day. = 我每天都发邮件。
He sends flowers to his wife on their anniversary. = 他在结婚纪念日那天给他妻子送花。

一般过去时:
She sent a postcard from Paris. = 她从巴黎寄来一张明信片。
They sent a rescue team to the disaster area. = 他们向灾区派遣了一支救援队。

现在完成时:
I have sent the documents to the client. = 我已经把文件寄给了客户。
She has sent several applications for jobs. = 她已经寄出了几份求职申请。

过去完成时:
He had sent the letter before he left. = 他离开之前已经寄出了信。
They had sent the package before the deadline. = 他们在大限之前已经寄出了包裹。

将来时:
I will send you the information tomorrow. = 我明天会把信息发给你。
We will send a car to pick you up at the airport. = 我们会派一辆车去机场接你。

虚拟语气:
If I had known his address, I would have sent him a gift. = 如果我知道他的地址，我就会给他送礼物了。
I wish I had sent her a birthday card. = 我真希望我给她寄了一张生日贺卡。

被动语态:
The package was sent yesterday. = 包裹昨天寄出去了。
The invitations were sent by email. = 请柬是通过电子邮件发送的。

疑问句:
Did you send the email? = 你发送邮件了吗？
When will the goods be sent? = 货物什么时候会被发送？

否定句:
I didn't send the letter. = 我没有寄信。
He hasn't sent the report yet. = 他还没寄报告。


PHRASE->
send off = 送别；寄出
send for = 派人去请；索取
send out = 发送；发出
send in = 递交；送交
send back = 退回；送回
