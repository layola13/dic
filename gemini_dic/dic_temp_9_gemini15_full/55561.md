distrustless:/dɪsˈtrʌstləs/ | adj. | 不怀疑的；深信不疑的 | Free from distrust; having or showing complete trust.  |  He had a distrustless nature, readily believing everything he was told. 他天性不疑人， readily believing everything he was told. | trusting, believing, credulous | suspicious, doubtful, skeptical | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is distrustless of his friends' intentions. = 他不怀疑朋友们的意图。
She remains distrustless, even after the betrayal. = 即使在背叛之后，她仍然深信不疑。


一般过去时:
He was distrustless of her motives. = 他从不怀疑她的动机。
The child was distrustless of strangers. = 这个孩子对陌生人不设防。


现在完成时:
I have been distrustless of politicians for years. = 多年来我一直不信任政客。
They have remained distrustless despite the evidence. = 尽管有证据，他们仍然深信不疑。


过去完成时:
He had been distrustless until the recent scandal. = 直到最近的丑闻发生之前，他一直都深信不疑。
She had been distrustless of his promises before. = 她以前从不怀疑他的承诺。


将来时:
I will be distrustless of his advice. = 我将不会怀疑他的建议。
She will likely remain distrustless, regardless of the circumstances. = 不管情况如何，她可能会继续深信不疑。


虚拟语气:
If I were distrustless, I would have lent him the money. = 如果我不怀疑他，我就把钱借给他了。
If she had been distrustless, she wouldn't have been hurt. = 如果她不那么轻信，她就不会受伤了。


被动语态:
His intentions were not perceived as distrustless. = 他的意图不被认为是值得信任的。(This is a less natural usage for this word; it’s better to use the active voice.)
His claims were initially received as distrustless. = 他的说法最初被认为是可信的。(This is a less natural usage for this word; it’s better to use the active voice.)


疑问句:
Is he distrustless of his colleagues? = 他不怀疑他的同事吗？
Are you always so distrustless of new ideas? = 你总是对新想法如此深信不疑吗？


否定句:
He is not distrustless of everyone. = 他并非不怀疑所有人。
She is not entirely distrustless of his promises. = 她并非完全不怀疑他的承诺。

a distrustless nature = 天性不疑人
