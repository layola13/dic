prepenetrate:/ˌpriːˈpɛnɪtreɪt/| v.|预先渗透；彻底渗透|To penetrate beforehand or thoroughly.  It implies a more complete and pervasive penetration than a simple "penetrate."|例句：The marketing campaign aimed to prepenetrate the target market before the product launch.  市场营销活动旨在产品发布前就彻底渗透目标市场。

例句：The roots prepenetrated the soil, ensuring a strong foundation for the plant. 根部深入土壤，确保植物有坚实的基础。


近义词：permeate, saturate, infiltrate|反义词：superficially affect, skim

词性:动词
