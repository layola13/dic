intensify:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪ/| vt. |增强，加剧，强化 |To increase in degree or intensity; to make or become more intense. |例句：The storm intensified as the day went on.  暴风雨随着一天的过去而加剧了。|例句：He intensified his efforts to find a solution. 他加紧努力寻找解决办法。|近义词：aggravate, heighten, strengthen, exacerbate |反义词：weaken, diminish, lessen, mitigate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun intensifies during midday. = 中午太阳变得更强烈。
The government intensifies its efforts to control inflation. = 政府加大了控制通货膨胀的力度。

一般过去时:
The pain intensified after the surgery. = 手术后疼痛加剧了。
The argument intensified until it became a shouting match. = 争论越来越激烈，最后变成了大声喊叫。

现在完成时:
The conflict has intensified significantly in recent weeks. = 近几周冲突已显著加剧。
The company has intensified its marketing campaign. = 公司加大了营销力度。

过去完成时:
The situation had intensified before the peace talks began. = 和谈开始之前，局势已经恶化。
Tensions had intensified considerably before the outbreak of war. = 在战争爆发之前，紧张局势已经大大加剧。

将来时:
The heat will intensify throughout the afternoon. = 下午气温将会升高。
The pressure on the government will intensify as the crisis continues. = 随着危机的持续，政府面临的压力将会加大。

虚拟语气:
If the drought intensifies, the water shortage will worsen. = 如果旱情加剧，缺水情况将会恶化。
I wish the pressure wouldn't intensify. = 我希望压力不要加大。

被动语态:
The security measures were intensified after the attack. = 袭击发生后，安全措施得到了加强。
Efforts to resolve the dispute are being intensified. = 正在加紧努力解决争端。

疑问句:
Will the economic sanctions intensify? = 经济制裁会加剧吗？
Has the rivalry between the two companies intensified recently? = 这两家公司之间的竞争最近加剧了吗？

否定句:
The government did not intensify its efforts to fight poverty. = 政府没有加大力度打击贫困。
The symptoms did not intensify overnight. = 症状并没有一夜之间加剧。
