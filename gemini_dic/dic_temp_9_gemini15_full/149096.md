oxbridge:/ˈɒksbrɪdʒ/| n. |牛津剑桥（大学）|A blend of "Oxford" and "Cambridge," referring collectively to the universities of Oxford and Cambridge in England.  These universities are prestigious and often considered together due to their shared history, academic excellence, and similar characteristics.|Example:  Many Oxbridge graduates go on to successful careers in law and finance.  许多牛津剑桥毕业生在法律和金融领域取得了成功的事业。|近义词：Oxford and Cambridge (individually), elite universities|反义词：None (the term doesn't have a direct antonym)|名词
