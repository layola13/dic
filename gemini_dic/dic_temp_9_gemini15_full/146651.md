overharvested:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrvɪstɪd/ | adj. | 过度捕捞的；过度收获的 |  Describes resources, especially natural resources like fish or timber, that have been harvested to an extent that is unsustainable, harming the resource's ability to replenish itself.  | Example: The overharvested fishing grounds showed signs of depletion.  过度捕捞的渔场显示出枯竭的迹象。  The overharvested forests are now struggling to regenerate. 过度砍伐的森林现在正在努力再生。|近义词：depleted, exhausted, exploited | 反义词：underexploited, conserved, sustainably managed |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fish population is overharvested in many parts of the ocean. = 在许多海域，鱼类资源遭到过度捕捞。
This forest is overharvested for its valuable timber. = 这片森林因为其珍贵的木材而遭到过度砍伐。

过去完成时:

The population of this bird species had been overharvested prior to the conservation efforts. = 在开展保护工作之前，这种鸟类的数量已经被过度捕捞了。
