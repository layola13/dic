desperate:/ˈdɛsprət/| adj.|绝望的；不顾一切的；极度渴望的|Feeling or showing a strong hopeless need or want. Characterized by extreme urgency or need.|例句：She was desperate for a job. 她急需一份工作。
例句：The desperate refugees fled their homes. 绝望的难民逃离了家园。|近义词：hopeless, frantic, urgent|反义词：calm, hopeful, relaxed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is desperate for money. 他急需钱。
They are desperate to find a solution. 他们不顾一切地想找到一个解决方案。


一般过去时:
She was desperate after losing her job. 她失业后感到绝望。
He was desperate to escape the burning building. 他不顾一切地想逃离燃烧的建筑物。


现在完成时:
I have been desperate for a holiday all year.  我已经渴望度假一整年了。
They have become desperate since the company closed. 自从公司倒闭后，他们变得绝望了。


过去完成时:
He had been desperate to see her before she left.  在她离开之前，他一直渴望见到她。
They had become desperate after searching for days.  他们搜寻了好几天后变得绝望了。


将来时:
She will be desperate if she fails the exam. 如果她考试不及格，她会绝望的。
They will be desperate to win the game. 他们将不顾一切地赢得比赛。


虚拟语气:
If I were desperate, I would sell my car. 如果我走投无路，我会卖掉我的车。
I wish I weren't so desperate for approval. 我希望我不那么渴望得到认可。


被动语态:  (Desparate本身很少用被动语态)
His situation was seen as desperate. 他的处境被认为是绝望的。
The need for help was described as desperate.  对帮助的需求被形容为绝望的。


疑问句:
Is he desperate for help? 他很需要帮助吗？
Are they desperate to leave? 他们急于离开吗？


否定句:
He is not desperate for attention. 他并不渴望得到关注。
They were not desperate to fight. 他们并不急于战斗。


PHRASE->
desperate measures 绝望的措施
desperate attempt 绝望的尝试
desperate need 迫切的需求
become desperate 变得绝望
feel desperate 感到绝望
