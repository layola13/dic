hyperstimulate:/ˌhaɪpərˈstɪmjuleɪt/ | vt. |过度刺激，过度兴奋|To stimulate excessively or to an extreme degree.  It often refers to overstimulating a bodily system or a part of the body.|The bright lights hyperstimulated his already sensitive eyes.  明亮的灯光过度刺激了他本已敏感的眼睛。|The caffeine hyperstimulated his nervous system. 咖啡因过度刺激了他的神经系统。|近义词：overstimulate, overexcite, hyperactivate |反义词：under stimulate, depress, calm
