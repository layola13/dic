disparagers: /dɪˈspærɪdʒərz/ | n. | 贬低者，诋毁者 | Those who belittle or speak slightingly of someone or something.  They often express contempt or ridicule. |  例句：The disparagers of the new policy were quick to point out its flaws. 新政策的贬低者很快就指出了它的缺陷。

例句：Ignoring the disparagers, she continued to work on her project.  她无视那些贬低者，继续进行她的项目。


近义词：critics, detractors, belittlers, scorners, denigrators

反义词：supporters,praisers,admirers, champions


词性：名词
