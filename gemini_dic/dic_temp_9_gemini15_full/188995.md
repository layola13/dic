saag:/sɑːɡ/| n.|萨格（一种印度菜）|A spinach-based curry dish, a staple in Punjabi cuisine.  It can include other greens and spices depending on the regional variation.|Example:  My favorite Indian restaurant makes the best saag paneer. 我最喜欢的印度餐厅做最好吃的菠菜奶酪咖喱。|近义词：spinach curry, palak paneer (if paneer is included)|反义词：无
