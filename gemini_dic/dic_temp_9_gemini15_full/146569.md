overgrassed:/ˌoʊvərˈɡræst/| adj.|过度放牧的，草木过盛的|Having too much grass; excessively covered with grass.  This often implies a negative impact, such as on biodiversity or land management.|例句：The pasture was overgrassed, leading to soil erosion. 这片牧场草木过盛，导致土壤侵蚀。

The hillside became overgrassed after years of neglect.  多年无人管理后，山坡上草木过盛了。|近义词：overgrown, densely vegetated|反义词：undergrassed, barren, sparse
