delactation:/ˌdiːlækˈteɪʃən/| n. |断奶；停止泌乳|The process of ceasing lactation; the weaning of an infant from the breast.  It can also refer to the suppression of milk production in a mother. |例句：The mother began delactation after her baby turned one.  母亲在孩子一岁后开始断奶。|例句：The doctor prescribed medication to aid in delactation after the baby's death. 医生开了药帮助婴儿死后停止泌乳。|近义词：weaning, cessation of lactation|反义词：lactation, milk production|名词
