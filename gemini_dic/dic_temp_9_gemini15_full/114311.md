maintained:/meɪnˈteɪnd/ | v. | 维持，保持，坚持 | To keep something in good condition or working order; to continue to have or support something. | 例句：The house has been well maintained. 这房子一直保养得很好。

The government maintained its position on the issue. 政府坚持其在该问题上的立场。|近义词：preserve, uphold, sustain, conserve | 反义词：neglect, abandon, destroy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maintains a healthy lifestyle. = 他保持健康的生活方式。
They maintain a high standard of work. = 他们保持高标准的工作。


一般过去时:
She maintained her innocence throughout the trial. = 在整个审判过程中，她一直坚持自己的清白。
The company maintained its market share despite the recession. = 尽管经济衰退，该公司仍保持了其市场份额。


现在完成时:
We have maintained this level of production for five years. = 我们已经保持了五年的这种产量。
They have maintained a strong relationship despite their differences. = 尽管存在分歧，他们仍然保持着牢固的关系。


过去完成时:
He had maintained his composure even in the face of danger. = 即使面对危险，他也保持了镇静。
The organization had maintained its charitable work for decades. = 该组织几十年来一直坚持其慈善工作。


将来时:
We will maintain our commitment to quality. = 我们将坚持对质量的承诺。
The company plans to maintain its current level of employment. = 公司计划维持目前的就业水平。


虚拟语气:
If only he had maintained his focus, he would have succeeded. = 如果他当时能保持专注，他就会成功了。
I wish I had maintained better contact with my old friends. = 我真希望我与老朋友保持更好的联系。


被动语态:
The park is maintained by the local council. = 公园由当地议会负责维护。
Order must be maintained at all times. = 必须随时保持秩序。


疑问句:
How do you maintain such a positive attitude? = 你怎么能保持如此积极的态度？
Can we maintain this pace of work? = 我们能保持这种工作速度吗？


否定句:
He failed to maintain his balance and fell. = 他没能保持平衡，摔倒了。
The government did not maintain its promise to reduce taxes. = 政府没有履行减税的承诺。
