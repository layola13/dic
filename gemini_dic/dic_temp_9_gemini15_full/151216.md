pardoning: /pɑːrdənɪŋ/ | v. | 赦免；原谅；宽恕 | The act of forgiving or excusing an offense; granting pardon.  |例句：The president is considering pardoning the convicted criminal. 总统正在考虑赦免这个被判刑的罪犯。

例句：He received a full pardoning after serving five years in prison. 服刑五年后，他获得了完全赦免。


近义词：forgiving, excusing, absolving, remitting | 反义词：condemning, punishing, sentencing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pardons the offender. 他赦免了罪犯。
She pardons his mistake. 她原谅了他的错误。


一般过去时:
The king pardoned the traitor. 国王赦免了叛徒。
The judge pardoned the young man. 法官宽恕了那个年轻人。


现在完成时:
The governor has pardoned several prisoners. 州长已经赦免了几名囚犯。
She has pardoned him for his rudeness. 她已经原谅了他的粗鲁。


过去完成时:
The court had pardoned him before the new evidence emerged. 在新的证据出现之前，法院已经赦免了他。
They had pardoned her many times before. 他们以前已经原谅过她很多次了。


将来时:
The president will pardon him next week. 总统下周将赦免他。
I hope he will pardon my interruption. 我希望他会原谅我的打断。


虚拟语气:
If he were to pardon them, many would be grateful. 如果他赦免他们，很多人都会感激。
I wish he would pardon my ignorance. 我希望他会原谅我的无知。


被动语态:
The prisoner was pardoned by the president. 囚犯被总统赦免了。
His mistakes were pardoned by his family. 他的错误被家人原谅了。


疑问句:
Will the judge pardon the defendant? 法官会赦免被告吗？
Did the governor pardon the convicted man? 州长赦免了那个罪犯吗？


否定句:
The judge did not pardon the criminal. 法官没有赦免罪犯。
The court will not pardon his actions. 法院不会原谅他的行为。
