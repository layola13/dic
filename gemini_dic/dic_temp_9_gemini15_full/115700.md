marginalizes:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəlaɪz/| vt. |边缘化；使...处于社会边缘 |To treat (a person, group, or concept) as insignificant or peripheral.  To put or keep (someone) in a powerless or unimportant position within a society or group. |例句：The media often marginalizes the voices of the poor and marginalized. 媒体常常边缘化穷人和弱势群体的呼声。

The government's policies have marginalized rural communities. 政府的政策已经边缘化了农村社区。

近义词：exclude, ostracize, sideline,  disenfranchise |反义词：empower,  centralize, include, mainstream |动词
