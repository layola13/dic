butchering:/ˈbʊtʃərɪŋ/| v.|屠宰；粗制滥造；糟蹋 |The act of cutting up and preparing meat for sale;  to treat something badly, destroying its quality or value. |例句：He's butchering that song! 他把那首歌唱得乱七八糟！

例句：The butcher is butchering the lamb.  屠夫正在宰杀小羊羔。


近义词：slaughtering, mangling, mutilating, ruining, spoiling

反义词：preserving, improving, enhancing
