cosecant:/ˈkoʊsiːkənt/ | n. |余割 |The cosecant is the reciprocal of the sine function;  it is the ratio of the hypotenuse to the side opposite a given angle in a right-angled triangle. In other words, cosecant(x) = 1/sin(x).  It's primarily used in trigonometry. |The cosecant of 30 degrees is 2. |secant, reciprocal |cosine, sine |名词
