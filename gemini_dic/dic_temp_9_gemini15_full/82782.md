guilting:/ˈɡɪltɪŋ/| v.|使感到内疚；使负罪|The act of making someone feel guilty.  It often involves subtle manipulation or pressure to induce a feeling of remorse or responsibility.|例句：Her constant guilting made him finally agree to help.  她的不断施压让他最终同意帮忙。

例句：He tried guilting his son into apologizing. 他试图让儿子感到内疚从而道歉。


近义词：remorse, reproaching, blaming, shaming|反义词：praising, complimenting, forgiving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He guilts her into doing the dishes. = 他让她感到内疚从而去洗碗。
They are always guilting me about my weight. = 他们总是因为我的体重让我感到内疚。


一般过去时:
She guilted him into buying her a new car. = 她让他感到内疚从而给他买了一辆新车。
He guilted me into helping him move. = 他让我感到内疚从而帮我搬家。


现在完成时:
I have guilted myself into feeling bad about not calling my parents. = 我因为没有打电话给父母而让自己感到内疚。
She has guilted him into doing all the housework. = 她让他感到内疚从而做了所有的家务。


过去完成时:
He had guilted her into attending the boring party. = 他让她感到内疚从而参加了那个无聊的聚会。
They had guilted me into taking on extra work. = 他们让我感到内疚从而承担了额外的工作。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I guilted him, he might help. = 如果我让他感到内疚，他可能会帮忙。
I wish I hadn't guilted her into going. = 我真希望我没有让她感到内疚从而去了。


被动语态:

She was guilted into accepting the job. = 她被逼得不得不接受那份工作。


疑问句:

否定句:

He wouldn't be guilted into doing it. = 他不会因为内疚而那样做。
