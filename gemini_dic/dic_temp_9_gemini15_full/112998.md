lumbars:/ˈlʌmbɑːrz/| n.|腰椎；腰部|The lumbars are the five vertebrae in the lower back, between the thoracic vertebrae and the sacrum.  They support much of the body's weight and allow for flexion, extension, and lateral bending.|例句：The doctor examined his lumbars after the fall.  医生在他摔倒后检查了他的腰椎。|例句：She experienced constant pain in her lumbars.|她腰部经常疼痛。|近义词：lower back, lumbar spine|反义词：cervicals (cervical vertebrae), thoracics (thoracic vertebrae)
