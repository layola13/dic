represents:/ˌreprɪˈzents/ | vt. |代表；象征；表现；描绘|To stand for or be a sign of something; to act on behalf of someone or something; to depict or portray something. |例句：The flag represents the nation.  这面旗帜代表这个国家。|例句：He represents his company at the trade fair. 他代表他的公司参加贸易展销会。|近义词：symbolize, signify, embody, stand for |反义词：misrepresent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The symbol represents peace. = 这个符号代表和平。
This painting represents a landscape. = 这幅画描绘的是一幅风景。

一般过去时:
The painting represented a historical event. = 这幅画描绘的是一个历史事件。
She represented her country at the Olympics. = 她代表她的国家参加奥运会。

现在完成时:
The artist has represented many different subjects in his career. =这位艺术家在他的职业生涯中描绘过许多不同的主题。
This symbol has represented hope for centuries. = 这个符号几个世纪以来一直代表着希望。

过去完成时:
They had represented the company for over ten years. = 他们已经代表该公司超过十年了。
The artist had represented the scene many times before. =这位艺术家之前已经多次描绘过这个场景。

将来时:
She will represent her school at the debate. = 她将代表她的学校参加辩论。
The artist will represent the beauty of nature in his next painting. =这位艺术家将在他的下一幅画中展现大自然的美丽。

虚拟语气:
If this symbol represented love, it would be more widely recognized. = 如果这个符号代表爱，它将会被更广泛地认可。
I wish this statue represented something more meaningful. = 我希望这座雕像代表更有意义的东西。

被动语态:
The company is represented by a skilled lawyer. = 这家公司由一位熟练的律师代表。
The issue was represented unfairly in the media. = 这个问题在媒体中被不公平地报道。

疑问句:
What does this flag represent? = 这面旗帜代表什么？
Does this painting accurately represent the scene? = 这幅画是否准确地描绘了这个场景？

否定句:
This image does not represent reality. = 这幅图像并不代表现实。
He did not represent his views honestly. = 他没有诚实地表达他的观点。


PHRASE->
represent oneself = 自我介绍

fairly represent = 公平地代表
