preintended:/ˌpriːɪnˈtɛndɪd/ | adj. | 预先计划的；预先打算的 |  Something preintended is planned or intended beforehand. It implies a deliberate action or decision made before the event.  | The preintended route was blocked by a landslide, forcing us to find an alternative. 预先计划的路线被山体滑坡堵塞了，迫使我们寻找替代路线。 | planned, intended, premeditated | unplanned, accidental, spontaneous | 形容词
