pandits:/ˈpændɪts/| n. |权威人士；专家；学者(尤指印度教的学者)|Learned men, especially Hindu scholars.  Pandits are often consulted for their expertise on religious matters, scriptures, and traditions.|例句：The pandit explained the complex rituals with great clarity.  这位权威人士清晰地解释了复杂的仪式。

例句：Many pandits gathered to discuss the ancient texts. 许多学者聚集在一起讨论古代文本。


近义词：expert, scholar, authority, guru  |反义词：ignoramus, novice, amateur |词性：名词
