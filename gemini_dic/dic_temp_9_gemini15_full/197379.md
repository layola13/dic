shearwater:/ˈʃɛərwɔːtər/|/ˈʃɪərwɔːtər/| n. |海燕|A seabird of the family Procellariidae, characterized by its long wings and gliding flight.  Shearwaters are typically found in the open ocean, and are known for their long migrations.|例句：The shearwater soared effortlessly over the waves. 海燕轻松地飞翔在波浪之上。

例句：We observed a flock of shearwaters feeding near the coastline. 我们观察到一群海燕在海岸线附近觅食。


近义词：petrel (a broader term for similar seabirds)
