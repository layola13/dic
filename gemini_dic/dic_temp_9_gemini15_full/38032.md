componentize:/kəmˈpoʊnənˌtaɪz/ | vt. | 将…分解成组件；使…模块化 | To break something down into its constituent parts or components; to modularize.  | Example:  We need to componentize the software to make it more maintainable. 我们需要将软件分解成组件以使其更易于维护。

Example: The engineers decided to componentize the complex system for easier debugging.工程师们决定将复杂的系统分解成组件以便更容易调试。


近义词: modularize, decompose, dissect, break down | 反义词: integrate, combine, unify | 词性: Verb
