cozying: /ˈkoʊziɪŋ/ | v. | 变得舒适；使舒适；依偎 |  The act of making or becoming cozy; snuggling up.  | 例句：They were cozying up by the fireplace. 他们在壁炉旁取暖依偎着。


例句：She spent the evening cozying up with a good book and a cup of tea. 她花了一个晚上抱着好书和一杯茶，让自己舒服。


近义词：snuggling, cuddling, nestling, settling in


反义词：uncomfortable, distressing, agitating


词性：动词


USAGE->

He is cozying up to the boss. = 他在讨好老板。
They are cozying down for the night. = 他们正在准备睡觉。


一般过去时:
We cozied up by the fire after dinner. = 晚饭后，我们依偎在火炉旁取暖。

过去完成时:

She had cozied up to the fireplace for hours. = 她已经在壁炉旁取暖好几个小时了。

虚拟语气:
