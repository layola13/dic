professorship:/prəˈfɛsərʃɪp/| n. |教授职位；教授身份 |The position or rank of a professor.  A professorship is a prestigious academic appointment requiring significant expertise and experience in a specific field.  |例句：He was awarded a professorship in astrophysics. 他获得了一个天体物理学教授职位。

例句：She held a prestigious professorship at Harvard University.  她在哈佛大学担任着声望很高的教授职位。


近义词：professorship, chair, chair of department,  lectureship (less senior)


反义词：studentship, assistantship (opposite in terms of seniority)


词性：名词
