nonmeats:/ˈnɒnmiːts/| n. |非肉类食品，素食替代品 | Nonmeats are food products that substitute for meat, often made from plant-based sources like soy, tofu, mushrooms, or seitan. They are increasingly popular among vegetarians, vegans, and flexitarians. |例句：Many nonmeats are designed to mimic the texture and flavor of meat. 许多非肉类食品的设计旨在模仿肉类的质地和味道。

例句：The restaurant offers a wide variety of nonmeats, including veggie burgers and plant-based sausages. 这家餐厅提供各种各样的非肉类食品，包括素食汉堡和植物性香肠。


近义词：meat substitutes, plant-based proteins, vegetarian alternatives, vegan options

反义词：meats
