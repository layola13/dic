cropout:/ˈkrɒpˌaʊt/| n. |（岩石等的）露头；出露；显露 | A cropout is an exposed area of bedrock or a geological formation that is visible at the surface.  It is often used in geology to describe the part of a rock formation that is not covered by soil or other materials. |例句：The geologist carefully examined the cropout of granite.  地质学家仔细检查了花岗岩的露头。

The hikers discovered a cropout of ancient fossils.  远足者发现了一个古代化石的露头。


近义词：outcrop, exposure |反义词：buried, concealed |词性:名词
