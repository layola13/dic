jumpiness:/ˈdʒʌmpinɪs/ | n. | 烦躁不安；易激动的状态；跳跃性 | A state of being restless, fidgety, or easily excited;  a tendency to jump or twitch.  It can also describe something that is jerky or uneven in movement. | 例句：Her jumpiness was a clear sign of her anxiety. 她的烦躁不安清楚地表明了她很焦虑。  The jumpiness of the video made it difficult to watch. 视频的跳跃性使得观看变得困难。 | 近义词：restlessness, fidgetiness, nervousness, excitability | 反义词：calmness, tranquility, placidity
