excave:/ˈɛkskeɪv/| vt.|挖掘；开凿；发掘|To remove earth or other material from (a place), especially in order to find buried objects or remains, or to create a hole or structure.  It also implies a careful and systematic process of uncovering something. |例句：Archaeologists excavated the site for weeks, uncovering ancient artifacts. 考古学家们挖掘了这个遗址数周，发现了古代文物。

例句：The workers excavated a large pit to build the foundation of the new building. 工人们挖了一个大坑来建造新建筑的地基。


近义词：dig, unearth, disinter|反义词：bury, fill, conceal|动词
