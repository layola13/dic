exhibitors:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtərz/ | n. | 展览商，参展商 | People or organizations that display goods or services at an exhibition or trade show.  They are usually businesses showcasing their products or services to potential customers or clients. | The exhibitors at the technology trade show showcased their latest innovations.  展会上的参展商展示了他们的最新创新。  Many exhibitors at the art fair sold their work directly to the public.  艺术博览会上许多参展商直接向公众出售他们的作品。 | participants, presenters, vendors |  non-exhibitors | 名词
