picturize:/ˈpɪktʃəraɪz/| vt.|描绘，想象，使形象化|To form a mental image of; imagine vividly.  To represent or depict in pictures. |例句：I tried to picturize the scene he described. 我试图描绘他描述的场景。

例句：The writer picturized the battle vividly in his novel.  作家在他的小说中生动地描绘了这场战斗。


近义词：visualize, imagine, depict, portray, represent

反义词：abstract, dematerialize


词性:动词
