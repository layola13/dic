misconstrues:/ˌmɪskənˈstruːz/| vt.|误解，曲解|To misunderstand the meaning of (something, especially a statement, action, or situation).|例句：He completely misconstrued my remarks.|近义词：misinterpret, misunderstand, misconceive|反义词：understand, comprehend|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misconstrues my intentions every time. = 他每次都误解我的意图。
They often misconstrues the meaning of the instructions. = 他们经常误解指示的含义。


一般过去时:
I misconstrued her silence as disapproval. = 我误解了她的沉默，认为是不赞成。
The journalist misconstrued the politician's statement. = 记者误解了这位政治家的声明。


现在完成时:
She has misconstrued my actions repeatedly. = 她反复误解了我的行为。
He has misconstrued the evidence presented to the court. = 他误解了提交给法庭的证据。


过去完成时:
I had misconstrued the map, which led me astray. = 我误解了地图，导致我迷路了。
They had misconstrued the agreement, causing a significant dispute. = 他们误解了协议，导致了重大的争议。


将来时:
He will misconstrues the situation again if you don't clarify it. = 如果你不解释清楚，他会再次误解情况。
They will likely misconstrues the new regulations. = 他们很可能会误解新的规章制度。


虚拟语气:
If I had explained it more clearly, he wouldn't have misconstrued my words. = 如果我解释得更清楚些，他就不会误解我的话了。
I wish he hadn't misconstrued my intentions. = 我真希望他没有误解我的意图。


被动语态:
His words were misconstrued by the media. = 他的话被媒体误解了。
The agreement was misconstrued by both parties. = 双方都误解了协议。


疑问句:
Did he misconstrues my message? = 他是否误解了我的信息？
Could the instructions have been misconstrued? = 指示是否可能被误解了？


否定句:
I did not misconstrues his meaning; I understood him perfectly. = 我没有误解他的意思；我完全理解他。
She did not misconstrues the data; her analysis was accurate. = 她没有误解数据；她的分析是准确的。

词源:
mis- (错误地) + construe (解释，理解)  The verb "construe" itself originates from the Latin "construere" meaning "to build," "to arrange," or "to interpret."  The sense of interpretation developed over time.
