conditioning:/kənˈdɪʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 条件作用；制约；训练；调节 | The process of learning through association, where a neutral stimulus becomes associated with a meaningful stimulus, eventually eliciting a similar response.  It's a fundamental concept in behavioral psychology.  | Example: Classical conditioning involves pairing a neutral stimulus with an unconditioned stimulus to create a conditioned response. 经典条件反射包括将中性刺激与非条件刺激配对以产生条件反射。


Example: Operant conditioning uses reinforcement and punishment to shape behavior. 操作性条件反射利用强化和惩罚来塑造行为。


近义词：training, preparation, adjustment, adaptation | 反义词：neglect, ignoring, disregard | 词性：名词
