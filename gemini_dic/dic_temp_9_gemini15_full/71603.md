flat-packing:/ˈflætˌpækɪŋ/| n. |扁平包装；预制件组装|The process of designing and manufacturing products that can be easily disassembled and shipped in a flat, compact form for assembly at the point of use.|例句：This furniture is sold flat-packed, requiring assembly at home. 这款家具是扁平包装的，需要在家组装。|近义词：knock-down, ready-to-assemble|反义词：fully assembled
