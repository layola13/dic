parmesan-cheese:/ˌpɑːrməˈzæn ˈtʃiːz/|/ˈpɑːrmɪˈzɑːn ˈtʃiːz/| n. |帕尔马干酪|Parmesan cheese is a hard, granular Italian cheese made from cow's milk. It is known for its sharp, salty, and slightly nutty flavor. It is often grated and used as a topping for pasta dishes, salads, and other foods.  |The pasta dish was topped with generous shavings of Parmesan cheese.|近义词：Parmigiano-Reggiano, hard cheese|反义词：None (antonyms are not applicable for this type of word)|名词
