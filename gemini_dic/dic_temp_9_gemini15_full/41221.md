corallaceous:/ˌkɒrəˈleɪʃəs/| adj.|珊瑚状的，珊瑚色的|Resembling coral; having a delicate, branching structure like coral.  The term often describes the appearance or texture of something, not necessarily its composition.|例句：The corallaceous structure of the fungus was quite striking.  这种真菌的珊瑚状结构非常引人注目。 The delicate, corallaceous pattern of the lace was exquisite. 这精致的珊瑚色花边图案非常精美。|近义词：branching, ramified, dendritic, arborescent|反义词：smooth, uniform, flat
