/klɔː/| n.|爪，脚爪|a sharp curved claw or nail on an animal's foot|例句：The cat sharpened its claws on the tree. 猫在树上磨爪子。|近义词：talon, nail|反义词: paw


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The cat uses its claws to climb. = 猫用爪子爬树。
Birds use their claws to catch prey. = 鸟用爪子捕捉猎物。


一般过去时:
The lion used its claws to kill the zebra. = 狮子用爪子杀死了斑马。
The eagle grasped its prey with its claws. = 老鹰用爪子抓住了猎物。


现在完成时:
The cat has sharpened its claws. = 猫已经磨过爪子了。
The bird has caught a mouse with its claws. = 鸟用爪子抓到了一只老鼠。


过去完成时:
The tiger had already used its claws before the fight. = 老虎在战斗前已经用过爪子了。
The hawk had injured its claws before the hunt. = 鹰在狩猎前已经伤了爪子。


将来时:
The kitten will use its claws to play. = 小猫会用爪子玩耍。
The bear will use its claws to dig for food. = 熊会用爪子挖食物。


虚拟语气:
If the cat had sharper claws, it could climb better. = 如果猫的爪子更锋利，它就能爬得更好。
If the bird had stronger claws, it would catch more prey. = 如果鸟的爪子更强壮，它就能捕捉到更多猎物。


被动语态:
The claws were used to defend itself. = 爪子被用来自卫。
The prey was caught with sharp claws. = 猎物被锋利的爪子抓住了。


疑问句:
Does the cat have sharp claws? = 猫的爪子锋利吗？
Do birds use their claws for climbing? = 鸟用爪子爬树吗？


否定句:
The cat didn't use its claws to attack. = 猫没有用爪子攻击。
The bird couldn't catch the fish because its claws were weak. = 鸟因为爪子无力而抓不到鱼。
