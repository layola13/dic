grainering:/ˈɡreɪnərɪŋ/ | n. | 木纹装饰，仿木纹装饰 |  The process of creating a wood-grain effect on a surface, typically by painting or printing.  It aims to mimic the natural appearance of wood grain. | Example: The cabinet doors featured exquisite grainering, making them look like real cherry wood.  木柜门饰有精美的木纹装饰，使它们看起来像真正的樱桃木。 The furniture was cheap, but the grainering was surprisingly realistic. 家具很便宜，但木纹装饰却出奇地逼真。|近义词：wood graining, wood finishing, veining | 反义词：plain finish, solid color
