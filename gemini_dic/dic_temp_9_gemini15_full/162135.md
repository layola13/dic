pre-enter:/ˌpriːˈɛntər/| v.|预先进入，预先登记|To enter or register beforehand; to enter before the main event or official time.|例句：Participants are requested to pre-enter the race by Friday. 参赛者请在周五之前预先报名参加比赛。|近义词：preregister, register in advance|反义词：post-enter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pre-enter their names for the competition. = 他们预先报名参加比赛。
He pre-enters the data into the system. = 他预先将数据输入系统。

一般过去时:
We pre-entered the conference last month. = 我们上个月预先登记参加了会议。
She pre-entered her details on the website. = 她提前在网站上填写了她的详细信息。


现在完成时:
Have you pre-entered the lottery yet? = 你已经预先购买彩票了吗？
They have pre-entered all the necessary information. = 他们已经预先输入所有必要的信息。


过去完成时:
We had pre-entered the address before we left. = 我们出发前已经预先输入了地址。
She had pre-entered the data before the meeting. = 会议前她已经预先输入了数据。


将来时:
I will pre-enter the marathon next week. = 我下周将预先报名参加马拉松比赛。
They will pre-enter the details of the project. = 他们将预先输入项目的详细信息。


虚拟语气:
If I had pre-entered the concert, I would have gotten better seats. = 如果我预先订了演唱会门票，我就能拿到更好的座位。
I wish I had pre-entered my application. = 我真希望我预先提交了申请。


被动语态:
The names were pre-entered into the database. = 名字被预先输入数据库。
The data is being pre-entered by the assistant. = 数据正在由助理预先输入。


疑问句:
Did they pre-enter the contest? = 他们预先报名参加比赛了吗？
Have you pre-entered your credit card information? = 你已经预先输入你的信用卡信息了吗？


否定句:
I did not pre-enter the competition. = 我没有预先报名参加比赛。
They haven't pre-entered the details yet. = 他们还没有预先输入详细信息。
