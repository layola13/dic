scarifies:/ˈsækrɪfaɪzɪz/| n. |牺牲（祭品）；祭祀|The act of sacrificing something valuable or important for a purpose believed to be of greater value.  It often refers to religious rituals involving the offering of animals or other items to a deity.  It can also refer to the act of giving up something personal for a greater good.|例句：The ancient people made sacrifices to appease the gods. 古代人祭祀以平息神灵。 The company sacrificed short-term profits for long-term growth. 公司牺牲短期利润以换取长期增长。|近义词：offerings, immolations, oblations|反义词：gains, acquisitions|名词
