mislearn:/ˌmɪsˈlɜːrn/| vt.|误学，学错|To learn something incorrectly or incompletely.  It implies a misunderstanding or a flawed learning process, resulting in inaccurate or incomplete knowledge.|例句：He mislearned the pronunciation of that word. 他把那个词的发音学错了。
He mislearned the rules of grammar, leading to frequent errors in his writing. 他语法规则学错了，导致写作中经常出错。|近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret,  learn wrongly |反义词：learn correctly, master, understand
