recompensable:/ˌrɛkəmˈpɛnsəbəl/| adj.| 可补偿的，可赔偿的 | capable of being compensated for; able to be repaid or made good.  |The damages are recompensable under the terms of the insurance policy.  保险条款规定可以赔偿这些损失。|compensable, repayable|uncompensable, irredeemable

一般现在时:
The company recompenses its employees for their hard work. = 公司补偿员工辛勤工作。
This policy recompenses victims of crime. = 这项政策补偿犯罪受害者。


一般过去时:

They recompensed me for the damage to my car. = 他们赔偿了我汽车的损失。
