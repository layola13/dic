one-liner:/ˈwʌnˌlaɪnər/ | n. |单行本，一句话；俏皮话 | A one-liner is a short, witty saying or joke, typically delivered in a single line.  It can also refer to a concise piece of writing or code that fits on a single line. | Example:  His stand-up routine was filled with clever one-liners. 他的脱口秀节目充满了巧妙的俏皮话。  |近义词：short joke, quip, bon mot | 反义词：long speech, essay
