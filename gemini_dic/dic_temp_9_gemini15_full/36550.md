collaging:/kəˈlɑːʒɪŋ/| v.|拼贴(艺术)；制作拼贴画 |The act of creating a collage.  It involves assembling different materials, such as paper, fabric, photographs, or other found objects, onto a base surface to create a new image or artwork. |例句：She spent the afternoon collaging old photographs and magazine clippings. = 她下午花了时间拼贴旧照片和杂志剪报。

例句：The artist is known for his innovative collaging techniques. =这位艺术家以其创新的拼贴技术而闻名。


近义词：assembling, composing, constructing

反义词：disassembling, deconstructing

词性：动词
