diselenide:/ˌdaɪˈsɛlaɪd/| n. |二硒化物|A chemical compound containing two selenium atoms bonded together.  Diselenides are often found in organic chemistry, where they are formed by the oxidation of selenols (RSeH). They can also be inorganic compounds. |Example:  Dimethyl diselenide is a volatile organoselenium compound. 二甲基二硒化物是一种挥发性有机硒化合物。

The synthesis of diselenides involves the oxidation of selenols. 二硒化物的合成涉及硒醇的氧化。|近义词：selenide (but with only one selenium atom)|反义词：无
