sacrificing:/ˈsækrɪfaɪsɪŋ/| v.|牺牲；献祭；奉献|The act of giving up something valuable for the sake of something else, often something more important or beneficial.  It implies a loss or deprivation, but for a greater purpose. |例句：She is sacrificing her career to raise her children. 她为了抚养孩子而牺牲了自己的事业。


例句：He is sacrificing his free time to volunteer at the homeless shelter. 他牺牲自己的空闲时间去无家可归者收容所做志愿者。


近义词：yielding, relinquishing, surrendering, forgoing


反义词：gaining, acquiring, profiting, keeping


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is sacrificing her comfort for her family. = 她为了家庭牺牲自己的舒适。
They are sacrificing their time to help others. = 他们牺牲时间来帮助他人。


一般过去时:
He sacrificed his reputation to save his friend. = 他牺牲了自己的名誉去救他的朋友。
She sacrificed her sleep to finish the project. = 她牺牲睡眠来完成项目。


现在完成时:
They have sacrificed a lot to achieve their goals. = 为了实现目标，他们已经牺牲了很多。
We have sacrificed our personal lives for our careers. = 我们为了事业牺牲了个人生活。


过去完成时:
She had sacrificed her dreams before she met him. = 在遇到他之前，她已经牺牲了自己的梦想。
They had sacrificed everything to build their business. = 为了建立他们的公司，他们已经牺牲了一切。


将来时:
He will sacrifice his position to protect his integrity. = 他将牺牲自己的职位来维护自己的正直。
They will sacrifice their savings for their children's education. = 他们将牺牲自己的积蓄来供孩子上学。


虚拟语气:
If I had to, I would sacrifice everything for my family. = 如果必须的话，我会为我的家人牺牲一切。
I wish I hadn't sacrificed my health for my work. = 我真希望我没有为了工作牺牲自己的健康。


被动语态:
Many lives were sacrificed during the war. = 战争中许多生命被牺牲了。
His comfort was sacrificed for the sake of the mission. = 为了完成任务，他的舒适被牺牲了。


疑问句:
What are you sacrificing for this project? = 你为了这个项目牺牲了什么？
What will be sacrificed in order to achieve peace? = 为了实现和平将会牺牲什么？


否定句:
He did not sacrifice his principles for money. = 他没有为了钱牺牲自己的原则。
They would not sacrifice their children's future for their own desires. = 他们不会为了自己的欲望牺牲孩子们的未来。
