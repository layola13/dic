diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ | n. |腹泻 | Loose, watery stools occurring frequently.  It is a symptom of various illnesses and conditions. | Example: The traveler suffered from diarrhea after drinking contaminated water.  旅行者喝了受污染的水后患上了腹泻。  |近义词: loose bowels, loose stools |反义词:constipation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has diarrhea. = 他腹泻。
She experiences diarrhea frequently. = 她经常腹泻。


一般过去时:
I had diarrhea yesterday. = 我昨天腹泻了。
The child had severe diarrhea. = 这个孩子严重腹泻。


现在完成时:
She has had diarrhea for three days. = 她腹泻三天了。
He has never had diarrhea before. = 他以前从未腹泻过。


过去完成时:
He had had diarrhea before the trip. = 他在旅行前就腹泻了。
She had not had diarrhea in years. = 她多年来没有腹泻过。


将来时:
I think I will have diarrhea after eating that. = 我想吃了那个之后我会腹泻。
She might have diarrhea if she eats too much spicy food. = 如果她吃太多辛辣食物，她可能会腹泻。


虚拟语气:
If I ate that, I would probably have diarrhea. = 如果我吃了那个，我可能会腹泻。
I wish I hadn't eaten that; I have diarrhea now. = 我真希望我没有吃那个；我现在腹泻了。


被动语态:
(Diarrhea is rarely used in the passive voice.  The focus is usually on the person experiencing it.)


疑问句:
Do you have diarrhea? = 你腹泻吗？
Is he suffering from diarrhea? = 他正在遭受腹泻吗？


否定句:
I don't have diarrhea. = 我没有腹泻。
She doesn't usually have diarrhea. = 她通常不会腹泻。


PHRASE->
acute diarrhea 急性腹泻
chronic diarrhea 慢性腹泻
infectious diarrhea 感染性腹泻
traveler's diarrhea 旅行者腹泻
diarrhea medication 腹泻药
