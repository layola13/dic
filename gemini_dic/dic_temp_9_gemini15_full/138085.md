norman:/ˈnɔːrmən/| /ˈnɔːrmæn/| n. adj.|诺曼人；诺曼式的；规矩的|The word "Norman" refers to the people of Normandy, a region in northwestern France.  It also describes things relating to their culture, language, or architecture. As an adjective, it can also mean "conforming to a standard; regular; typical." |例句：The Norman Conquest of England had a profound impact on the English language and culture.  诺曼征服英格兰深刻地影响了英语和文化。
The Norman architecture of the cathedral is breathtaking.  大教堂的诺曼式建筑令人叹为观止。
