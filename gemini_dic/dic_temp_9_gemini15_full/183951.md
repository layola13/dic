resettlement:/ˌriːˈsɛtlmənt/| n. |重新安置；移民安置 |The act or process of settling people in a new place, especially after they have been displaced or forced to leave their homes.  It often involves providing housing, jobs, and other essential services. |例句：The government initiated a large-scale resettlement program for refugees.  政府启动了一个大规模的难民重新安置计划。

例句：The resettlement of the villagers was completed successfully after months of planning. 村民的重新安置在数月的计划之后成功完成。


近义词：relocation, transplantation, relocation of population

反义词：displacement, eviction


词性：名词
