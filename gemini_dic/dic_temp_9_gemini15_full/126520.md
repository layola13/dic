naan:/nɑːn/| n. |印度烤饼|A type of leavened, oven-baked flatbread from the Indian subcontinent.  It is typically made with flour, water, yogurt, and oil or ghee, and is often cooked in a tandoor oven.| 例句：I ordered a naan to go with my curry. 我点了一份印度烤饼配咖喱。

例句：The naan was soft and fluffy, perfectly complementing the spicy vindaloo.  这印度烤饼柔软蓬松，完美地补充了辛辣的文达鲁咖喱。


近义词：roti, chapati (although these are slightly different)


反义词：无
