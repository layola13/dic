carnivore:/ˈkɑːrnɪvɔːr/|/ˈkɑːrnɪvɔːr/| n. |食肉动物，肉食动物 | An animal that eats meat.  Carnivores have specialized teeth and digestive systems adapted for consuming and processing animal flesh. | Lions are apex carnivores.  狮子是顶级食肉动物。

The great white shark is a fearsome carnivore. 大白鲨是一种可怕的食肉动物。


近义词：predator, flesh-eater
反义词：herbivore, omnivore, vegetarian


词源:
From Latin *carnis (“flesh”) + vorāre (“to devour”).  The word combines the root for "flesh" with a verb meaning "to eat" or "devour," directly reflecting the carnivore's dietary habits.
