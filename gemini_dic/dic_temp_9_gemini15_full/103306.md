jiffies:/ˈdʒɪfiz/| n.|瞬间，极短时间|A very short period of time; an instant.|例句：It only took a few jiffies to finish the task. 完成这项任务只花了几个瞬间。例句：He fixed the problem in a jiffy. 他立刻解决了这个问题。|近义词：moment, instant, second|反义词：eon, age, eternity|名词
