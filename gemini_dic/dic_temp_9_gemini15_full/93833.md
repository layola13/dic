imminution: /ɪmɪˈnjuːʃən/ | n. | 减少；减弱 | The lessening or reduction of something, especially in size, amount, or intensity.  It often refers to a gradual decrease. |例句：The imminution of his power was evident in the dwindling support he received. 他的权力逐渐减弱，从他日益减少的支持中可以看出。

例句：The gradual imminution of the forest's biodiversity is a serious concern. 森林生物多样性的逐渐减少是一个严重的问题。


近义词：reduction, decrease, diminution, lessening, abatement | 反义词：increase, augmentation, enhancement
