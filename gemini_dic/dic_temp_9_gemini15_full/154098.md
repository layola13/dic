pericranium:/ˌpɛrɪˈkreɪniəm/| n. |颅骨外膜，帽状腱膜|The pericranium is the tough, fibrous outer layer of the scalp, covering the skull bones. It is continuous with the periosteum of the skull.  It provides protection and serves as an attachment point for muscles and blood vessels.| The surgeon carefully dissected the pericranium to access the underlying skull fracture.|  periosteum |  (no direct antonym)|名词
