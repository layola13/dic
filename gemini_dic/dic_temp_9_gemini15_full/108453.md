lay:/leɪ/ | vt. vi. n.|放置；产卵；铺设；呈…状态|to put or place (something) down gently; to produce (eggs); to spread something out over a surface; to be situated or positioned|例句：Please lay the book on the table. 请把书放在桌子上。  I'm going to lay the carpet tomorrow. 我打算明天铺地毯。|近义词：place, put, set, position|反义词：raise, lift, erect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I lay the book on the table every day. = 我每天把书放在桌子上。
The hen lays an egg every morning. = 母鸡每天早上产一个蛋。


一般过去时:
She laid the baby gently in the crib. = 她轻轻地把婴儿放在婴儿床里。
The birds laid their eggs in the nest. = 鸟儿把蛋下在窝里。


现在完成时:
I have laid the groundwork for the project. = 我已经为这个项目打下了基础。
The chicken has laid ten eggs this week. = 这只鸡这周下了十个蛋。


过去完成时:
She had laid the table before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经摆好桌子了。
The hen had laid all her eggs by nightfall. = 到夜幕降临的时候，母鸡已经下了所有的蛋。


将来时:
I will lay the foundation tomorrow. = 我明天将奠定基础。
The farmer will lay his trap for the foxes tonight. = 农民今晚将为狐狸设下陷阱。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would lay out a detailed plan. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会制定一个详细的计划。
I wish I could lay my worries to rest. = 我希望我能放下我的担忧。


被动语态:
The foundation was laid last year. = 地基是去年打好的。
The eggs are laid in the nest. = 蛋下在窝里。


疑问句:
Where did you lay the keys? = 你把钥匙放在哪里了？
Does the turtle lay eggs? = 乌龟下蛋吗？


否定句:
I didn't lay the blame on him. = 我没有责怪他。
The hen didn't lay any eggs today. = 母鸡今天没下蛋。


PHRASE->
lay down = 放下；制定；牺牲
lay off = 解雇；停止
lay out = 布置；设计；展开
lay into = 痛打；猛烈抨击
lay over = 停留；延期


WORD ORIGIN->
Old English *lecgan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch leggen and German legen.  The meaning has evolved over time from a general sense of "placing" to its various specific uses.
