cybercrimes:/ˈsaɪbərkraɪmz/| n. |网络犯罪|Crimes committed using computers and the internet.  These can include a wide range of offenses, from hacking and data theft to online fraud and cyberstalking.|例句：The police are investigating a series of cybercrimes targeting online banking systems.  警方正在调查一系列针对网上银行系统的网络犯罪。|近义词：computer crimes, online crimes, internet crimes|反义词：offline crimes, traditional crimes
