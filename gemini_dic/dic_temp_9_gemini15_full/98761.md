intaglio:/ɪnˈtæɡliˌoʊ/| n.|凹版雕刻；凹版印刷|An intaglio is a kind of printmaking process where the image is incised into a surface, and the ink is held in the grooves.  The printing is done by pressing paper onto the inked surface.  It is the opposite of relief printing, where the image is raised.|Example: The museum showcased a stunning collection of intaglio prints from the Renaissance.  博物馆展出了文艺复兴时期令人惊叹的凹版印刷品收藏。|近义词：engraving, etching|反义词：relief printing|名词
