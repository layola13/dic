epitomizers: /ɪˈpɪtəmaɪzərz/ | n. | 典型代表；完美体现者 | Those who perfectly exemplify or represent something.  They embody the essence of a particular quality, characteristic, or concept. | Example:  The skyscrapers of New York City are epitomizers of modern architecture.  纽约市的摩天大楼是现代建筑的典型代表。 | 近义词：exemplifiers, embodiments, personifications | 反义词：antithesis, opposites
