cravingly:/ˈkreɪvɪŋli/| adv. |渴望地，极度渴望地 |  expressing or characterized by a strong desire or longing.  It suggests an intense, almost desperate need. |例句：He looked at the cake cravingly. 他渴望地望着那块蛋糕。

例句：She stared cravingly at the chocolate. 她渴望地盯着巧克力。


近义词：longingly, yearningly, eagerly, wistfully

反义词：indifferently, apathetically, unenthusiastically


词性：副词
