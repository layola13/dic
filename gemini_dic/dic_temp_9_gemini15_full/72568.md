fluviatile:/ˈfluːviətaɪl/ | adj. |河流的；河流形成的；水成的|Relating to or formed by a river or rivers.  The term often describes geological formations, sediments, or deposits shaped by river action.|例句：The fluviatile deposits in the valley are rich in minerals.  山谷中的河流沉积物富含矿物质。

The fluviatile plains were once submerged under a vast lake.  这些河流平原曾经被一个巨大的湖泊淹没。


近义词：riverine, alluvial |反义词：marine (海洋的)


词性：形容词
