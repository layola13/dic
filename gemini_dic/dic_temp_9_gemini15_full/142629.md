ostiary:/ˈɒstiəri/ | n. | 门房；门卫；入口 | A person who guards a door or entrance, especially to a church or monastery;  the entrance itself, especially to a church.  | Example: The ostiary greeted the parishioners as they entered the church. 门房在教区居民进入教堂时向他们问好。  |近义词：doorkeeper, porter, janitor | 反义词：N/A
