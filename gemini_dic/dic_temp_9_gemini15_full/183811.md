researches:/rɪˈsɜːrtʃɪz/ | n. | 研究；调查 | The plural form of "research," referring to multiple studies or investigations.  It can also refer to the act of conducting multiple research projects. | Example: His researches into the history of the region spanned decades. 他的地区历史研究跨越了几十年。  The university is funding several important researches. 这所大学正在资助几项重要的研究。 | studies, investigations, inquiries |  (No direct antonym, but opposite concepts could include: speculation, assumption, guesswork) | 名词

His researches focus on climate change. = 他的研究重点是气候变化。
Their researches are published in prestigious journals. = 他们的研究发表在著名的期刊上。


一般过去时:
Her researches yielded surprising results. = 她的研究产生了令人惊讶的结果。
His researches were funded by a private grant. = 他的研究由私人拨款资助。


现在完成时:
Scientists have conducted numerous researches on the topic. = 科学家们已经对这个话题进行了许多研究。
Researchers have published their researches in a peer-reviewed journal. = 研究人员已将他们的研究发表在同行评审期刊上。


过去完成时:

将来时:
We will conduct further researches to verify the findings. = 我们将进行进一步的研究以验证这些发现。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would conduct more researches. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会进行更多的研究。
If they had better funding, they could conduct more researches. = 如果他们有更好的资金，他们可以进行更多的研究。


被动语态:

These researches are being funded by a government grant. = 这些研究正在接受政府拨款的资助。


疑问句:
What researches are currently underway? = 目前正在进行哪些研究？
What researches have been published on this subject? = 关于这个主题已经发表了哪些研究？


否定句:

They haven't completed their researches yet. = 他们还没有完成他们的研究。
