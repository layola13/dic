ladies:/ˈleɪdiz/| n.|女士们；妇女们|The word "ladies" is the plural form of "lady," referring to women, especially in a polite or formal context. It can be used as a term of address or as a general reference to a group of women.|例句：Ladies and gentlemen, welcome to our show! 女士们先生们，欢迎来到我们的节目！ The ladies enjoyed the afternoon tea. 女士们享受着下午茶。|近义词：women, females, womenfolk|反义词：gentlemen, men
