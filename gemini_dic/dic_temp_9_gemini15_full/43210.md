crabwise:/ˈkræbˌwaɪz/| adv.|横着地，像螃蟹一样地|Moving sideways like a crab.  This adverb describes movement in a sideways or oblique direction, similar to how a crab moves.|例句：The crab moved crabwise across the sand.  螃蟹横着穿过沙滩。

例句：The children crabwise across the playground. 孩子们在操场上横着走。


近义词：sideways, laterally, obliquely|反义词：straightforwardly, directly


词性：副词
