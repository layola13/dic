refeed:/ˌriːˈfiːd/| vt.|重新喂养，再次喂食|To feed (someone or something) again, often after a period of restricted feeding.  This term is often used in the context of dieting or weight training, referring to a planned increase in caloric intake after a period of calorie restriction.|例句：After a week of calorie restriction, she decided to refeed herself with a healthy, balanced meal.  在一周的卡路里限制后，她决定用健康均衡的膳食来重新进食。

例句：The athlete refeeds after intense training to replenish glycogen stores. 运动员在高强度训练后重新进食以补充糖原储备。


近义词：refuel, replenish, feed again|反义词：starve, deprive
