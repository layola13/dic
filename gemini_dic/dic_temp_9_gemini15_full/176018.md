ravagers:/ˈrævɪdʒərz/| n. |破坏者，劫掠者|Those who cause widespread destruction or devastation, often through violence or plunder.  They are typically associated with acts of violence, looting, or damage to property and resources. |例句：The locusts were ravagers, stripping the fields bare in their path.  蝗虫是破坏者，它们所过之处田野一片荒芜。


例句：The invading army were ravagers, leaving behind a trail of destruction. 侵略军是破坏者，留下一路破坏。


近义词：destroyers, plunderers, pillagers, marauders, vandals


反义词：preservers, protectors, benefactors, rebuilders


词性:名词
