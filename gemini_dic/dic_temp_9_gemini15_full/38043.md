comportment:/kəmˈpɔːrtmənt/ | n. |举止；行为；态度 |  The outward behavior or bearing of a person.  It often implies a controlled or refined manner. | 例句：Her comportment was impeccable throughout the ceremony.  她的举止在整个仪式中无可挑剔。

例句：His comportment at the dinner party was rather awkward. 他在晚宴上的举止相当笨拙。


近义词：demeanor, behavior, conduct, bearing

反义词：misbehavior, misconduct


词源:  From Old French *comportment, from the verb *comporter "to behave," ultimately derived from Latin *comportare "to bear together, behave."
