philosophy:/fɪˈlɒsəfi/ /fɪˈlɑːsəfi/| n. |哲学，人生观|The study of fundamental nature of knowledge, reality, and existence, especially when considered as an academic discipline.|  He studied philosophy at university. 他在大学学习哲学。|近义词：wisdom, metaphysics, ideology |反义词：ignorance, nonsense
