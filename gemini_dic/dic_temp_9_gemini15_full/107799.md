lapse:/læps/| n., v.|失效；流逝；失误；疏忽；衰退 | a period of time during which something is temporarily stopped; a slight failure of attention, memory, or concentration;  to lose validity or effect; to fall into a less active or functional state |例句：There was a lapse in security. 安全方面出现漏洞。

There was a lapse of memory.  记忆出现中断。


His driving license lapsed. 他的驾照过期了。

The company's profits lapsed after the merger. 并购后，公司的利润下降了。


近义词：slip, error, omission, failure, decline |反义词：continuation, maintenance, improvement, progress


词性:名词和动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My subscription lapses next month. = 我的订阅下个月到期。
He often lapses into silence. = 他经常沉默不语。


一般过去时:
The plan lapsed due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，计划搁浅了。
She lapsed into unconsciousness. = 她昏过去了。


现在完成时:
My memory has lapsed on that detail. = 我对那个细节的记忆已经模糊了。
His concentration has lapsed several times today. = 他今天分心好几次了。


过去完成时:
The agreement had lapsed before the dispute arose. = 在争议出现之前，协议就已经失效了。
His enthusiasm had lapsed after repeated failures. = 屡次失败后，他的热情消退了。


将来时:
Your visa will lapse in six months. = 你的签证六个月后过期。
His patience will lapse soon if we don't hurry. = 如果我们不快点，他的耐心就要耗尽了。


虚拟语气:
If my memory hadn't lapsed, I would have remembered his name. = 如果我的记忆没有衰退，我就记得他的名字了。
If the agreement had not lapsed, we would have avoided this conflict. = 如果协议没有失效，我们就不会避免这场冲突。


被动语态:
The contract was allowed to lapse. = 合同被允许失效。
His attention was easily lapsed. = 他的注意力很容易分散。


疑问句:
Has your insurance policy lapsed? = 你的保险单过期了吗？
Did your membership lapse? = 你的会员资格过期了吗？


否定句:
The system did not lapse into failure.  = 系统没有出现故障。
His attention did not lapse during the meeting. = 他在会议期间没有分心。


PHRASE->
lapse into (something):  陷入（某种状态）
lapse of time: 一段时间
lapse in judgment: 判断失误
lapse of memory: 记忆中断
time lapse: 时间流逝
