slakes:/sleɪks/| vt.|使满足；缓和；(使)平静；(使)减弱|To satisfy or quench (a thirst or appetite).  To make (something) less intense or severe.|例句：The cool water slaked his thirst.  凉水解了他的渴。

The long rain finally slaked the drought.  长时间的雨水最终缓解了旱情。

近义词：quench, satisfy, alleviate, mitigate|反义词：intensify, aggravate, exacerbate|动词
