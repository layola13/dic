hedgeless:/ˈhɛdʒləs/ | adj. | 无篱笆的；无掩护的；鲁莽的 | Lacking a hedge or hedges; unprotected; reckless.  |例句：The hedgeless field was exposed to the harsh winds.  无篱笆的田地暴露在强风中。

The hedgeless approach to the negotiations proved disastrous.  这种鲁莽的谈判方法最终导致了灾难。 | 近义词：unprotected, exposed, reckless, rash | 反义词：protected, sheltered, cautious, prudent

词源:
The word "hedgeless" is formed by adding the prefix "hedge-" (meaning "hedge" or "enclosure") and the suffix "-less" (meaning "without").  Therefore, it literally means "without a hedge."  The meaning has expanded to include the metaphorical sense of being unprotected or reckless.
