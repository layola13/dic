reglaze:/riːˈɡleɪz/| vt.|重新上釉；重新涂釉|To apply a new glaze to (something, especially pottery or windows).  This usually involves removing the old glaze first.|例句：The artist carefully reglazed the cracked ceramic vase.艺术家仔细地为破裂的陶瓷花瓶重新上釉。

例句：We need to reglaze the bathroom windows to improve insulation.我们需要重新为浴室窗户上釉以提高隔热性。

近义词：re-enamel, refinish

反义词：unglaze

She reglazes the kitchen tiles. 她给厨房瓷砖重新上釉。
