cinchona:/sɪnˈkoʊnə/| n. |金鸡纳树；金鸡纳霜|A genus of trees native to the Andes, the bark of which yields quinine and other alkaloids used to treat malaria.  The word also refers to the bark itself, which is a source of quinine.|例句：Cinchona trees are a crucial source of quinine, a vital antimalarial drug. 金鸡纳树是奎宁的重要来源，奎宁是一种重要的抗疟药。|例句：The bark of the cinchona tree was used for centuries to treat fevers.  金鸡纳树的树皮几个世纪以来一直被用来治疗发烧。|近义词：quinine tree (less precise)|反义词：N/A|名词
