ligaments:/ˈlɪɡəmənts/| n. |韧带|Tough, fibrous bands of connective tissue that connect bones to other bones, and help stabilize joints.  They provide support and limit the range of motion in joints.|例句：The knee ligaments were severely damaged in the accident.  膝盖韧带在事故中严重受损。|例句：Strong ligaments are essential for athletes. 强壮的韧带对运动员至关重要。|近义词：tendons, bands|反义词：N/A
