douceur:/ˈduːsɜːr/| n. |温柔；甜美；宜人；贿赂；礼物|A quality of being gentle, soft, or agreeable;  a small gift or bribe.|例句：The douceur of the summer evening was delightful. 夏夜的温柔令人愉快。

例句：He offered a small douceur to ensure the smooth completion of the deal. 他提供了一小笔贿赂以确保交易顺利完成。


近义词：sweetness, gentleness, charm, bribe, gratuity

反义词：harshness, roughness, rudeness, cruelty


词源：源自古法语 *douceur，意思是“甜蜜，温柔”，最终源自拉丁语 *dulcis，意思是“甜”。
