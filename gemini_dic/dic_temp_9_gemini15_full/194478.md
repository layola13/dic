self-restraints:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈstreɪnts/| n.|自我约束，克制|The act of controlling one's own impulses, desires, or behavior.  It implies a conscious effort to refrain from something tempting or potentially harmful.|例句：She showed remarkable self-restraint in the face of provocation.  她面对挑衅表现出非凡的克制力。


例句：He exercised great self-restraint, refusing to retaliate despite the insults. 他克制住了自己，尽管受到了侮辱也没有进行报复。


近义词：self-control, discipline, moderation, abstinence


反义词：indulgence, lack of control, impulsiveness


词性：名词
