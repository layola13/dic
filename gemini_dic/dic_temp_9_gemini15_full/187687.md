room:/ruːm/| n. |房间，空间，余地 |A space within a building enclosed by walls, floor, and ceiling.  |例句：We have a spare room for guests. 我们有一个空房间供客人使用。|例句：There's not much room in the car. 车里空间不大。|近义词：chamber, space, compartment |反义词： outdoors, outside
