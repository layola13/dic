compacts:/ˈkɒmpækt/ /ˈkɑːmpækt/ | n., v. | 契约；合同；压缩；使紧凑 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "compact" refers to a small, easily carried container, a solid mass made by pressing together, or a formal agreement between people or groups.  As a verb, it means to press or pack together tightly.


例句:
(Noun): The two countries signed a compact to cooperate on trade.  两个国家签署了一项关于贸易合作的协议。

(Noun): She carries a compact mirror in her purse. 她随身携带一面小镜子。

(Verb):  The snow compacted into a hard, icy surface.  雪压实成坚硬的冰面。

(Verb):  Try to compact your luggage to save space.  尽量压缩你的行李以节省空间。


近义词:
(Noun): agreement, treaty, contract, pact, accord
(Verb): condense, compress, consolidate, squeeze


反义词:
(Noun): disagreement, conflict, dispute
(Verb): expand, enlarge, spread out


词性:
Noun, Verb
