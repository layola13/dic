me-too:/ˌmiːˈtuː/| adj., n.|我也是；我也是运动|  A term used to express solidarity or agreement, especially in relation to shared experiences of sexual harassment or assault; also used more generally to indicate agreement or imitation.  It gained significant prominence as a hashtag (#MeToo) in 2017, used to raise awareness of sexual harassment and assault. |例句：She said she had been harassed, and I said "me too." 她说到她曾受到骚扰，我回应道“我也是”。  Many women shared their stories using the #MeToo hashtag. 许多女性使用#MeToo标签分享了她们的故事。|近义词：also, likewise, similarly |反义词：only I, uniquely me
