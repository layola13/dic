horsefoot:/ˈhɔːrsfʊt/ | n. |马蹄；马蹄铁| A horsefoot is the foot of a horse, or sometimes used to refer to a horseshoe.  The term is less common than "horseshoe" when referring to the metal protective covering.|例句：The blacksmith shaped the iron into a horsefoot.  铁匠将铁打造成马蹄铁。

The muddy horsefoot left prints in the field. 泥泞的马蹄在田野里留下了印记。|近义词：horseshoe, hoof |反义词：无
