gimlet:/ˈɡɪmlət/| n. |锥子；螺丝锥；鸡尾酒|A gimlet is a small tool with a pointed end and a spiral thread, used for boring holes, especially in wood.  It can also refer to a cocktail made with gin or vodka, lime juice, and simple syrup.|例句：He used a gimlet to make a hole in the wood. 他用锥子在木头上钻了个孔。The bartender expertly mixed a refreshing gimlet. 酒保熟练地调制了一杯清爽的鸡尾酒。|近义词：awl, drill|反义词：None specifically.
