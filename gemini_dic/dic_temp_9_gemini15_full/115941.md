marram:/ˈmærəm/| n. |海麦草|A type of tough, perennial grass that grows in sandy areas, especially coastal dunes. It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that roll up to reduce water loss.  Marram grass plays a vital role in stabilizing sand dunes and preventing erosion.|例句：Marram grass is planted to help stabilize the shifting sands of the dunes. 海麦草被种植来帮助稳定沙丘中流动的沙子。|近义词：beach grass, dune grass|反义词:无
