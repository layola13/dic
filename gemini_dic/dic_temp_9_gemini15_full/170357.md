provisional: /prəˈvɪʒənəl/ | adj. | 临时的；暂定的；有条件的 | Temporary; subject to change or confirmation.  It suggests something is not final or permanent, but is in place until a more permanent solution is found. |

例句：

The agreement is provisional and may be altered later.  = 这项协议是暂定的，以后可能会修改。

The government announced provisional figures for unemployment. = 政府公布了暂时的失业人数。


近义词：temporary, tentative, interim, preliminary

反义词：permanent, final, definitive


词性：形容词
