defense: /dɪˈfɛns/ | n. | 防御，保卫；辩护；国防 | The act of defending against attack or criticism; the means of defense.  |  例句：The country's defense system is strong.  这个国家的防御系统很强大。 | 例句：Her defense lawyer presented a compelling case.  她的辩护律师提出了一个令人信服的案例。| 近义词：protection, safeguard, justification, vindication | 反义词：attack, offense, accusation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army provides defense for the country. = 军队保卫国家。
Our company's defense against cyberattacks is robust. = 我们公司抵御网络攻击的防御能力很强。

一般过去时:
The soldiers mounted a strong defense against the enemy. = 士兵们对敌人进行了强烈的防御。
The lawyer successfully defended his client. = 律师成功地为他的当事人进行了辩护。

现在完成时:
The government has invested heavily in national defense. = 政府已大力投资国防。
She has consistently defended her position on this issue. = 她一直坚持捍卫她在这个问题上的立场。

过去完成时:
They had prepared a strong defense before the trial began. = 审判开始前，他们已经准备好了强有力的辩护。
The castle had withstood many attacks before its final defense crumbled. = 在最终防线崩溃之前，这座城堡抵御过许多次攻击。

将来时:
We will strengthen our defense against future threats. = 我们将加强防御，以应对未来的威胁。
The team will present a vigorous defense in court. = 该团队将在法庭上进行强有力的辩护。

虚拟语气:
If we had a better defense, we would have won the game. = 如果我们有更好的防守，我们就赢得了比赛。
I wish I had a better defense against criticism. = 我希望我有更好的方法来应对批评。

被动语态:
The city was successfully defended against the siege. = 该城市成功地抵御了围攻。
His arguments were easily defended. = 他的论点很容易得到辩护。

疑问句:
What is our best defense against this disease? = 我们对抗这种疾病的最佳防御是什么？
What defense will the accused use in court? = 被告将在法庭上使用什么辩护？

否定句:
The team had no defense against the opponent's attack. = 该队对对手的进攻毫无还手之力。
He offered no defense against the accusations. = 他对指控没有做出任何辩护。
