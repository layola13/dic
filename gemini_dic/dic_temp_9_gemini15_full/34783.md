climatize: /ˈklaɪmətaɪz/ | vt. | 使适应气候；使习惯于某种气候 | To accustom (a person, animal, or plant) to a particular climate.  | 例句：The plants need time to climatize to the new environment. 植物需要时间来适应新的环境。
The family quickly climatized to the tropical climate.  这家人很快适应了热带气候。| 近义词：acclimatize, acclimate | 反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be to experience difficulty adjusting to a climate.

He climatizes his plants gradually to avoid shock. 他逐渐地使他的植物适应气候，以避免冲击。
The gardener climatizes the seedlings before transplanting them. 园丁在移植幼苗之前使它们适应气候。
