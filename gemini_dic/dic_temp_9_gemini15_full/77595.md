geekdom:/ˈɡiːkdəm/| n. |极客文化；极客圈；宅文化|The world of geeks; the culture and activities of geeks.  It refers to the community, shared interests, and subculture associated with geeks.  This can include aspects of technology, gaming, science fiction, fantasy, and other nerdy pursuits.|例句：He immersed himself completely in the vibrant geekdom of online gaming. 他完全沉浸在充满活力的网络游戏极客文化中。|例句：The rise of geekdom has led to a greater appreciation for science and technology in popular culture. 极客文化的兴起导致大众文化对科学和技术的欣赏程度更高。|近义词：nerd culture, tech culture, fandom|反义词：mainstream culture
