pigmentations:/ˌpɪɡmenˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |色素沉着，着色 |The process of coloring or being colored, especially the natural coloring of animal or plant tissue.  It refers to the distribution of pigments in the skin, hair, or eyes.  |例句：Sun exposure can lead to uneven pigmentations on the skin.  阳光照射会导致皮肤色素沉着不均。

The variations in skin pigmentations are vast across different ethnicities.不同种族肤色的色素沉着差异很大。


近义词：coloring, pigmentation (singular), coloration | 反义词：depigmentation, lack of color


词源：源自于拉丁语词根 "pigmentum,"  meaning "paint, pigment,"  which further originates from the verb "pingere," meaning "to paint." The suffix "-ation" indicates a process or action.
