misprescribed:/ˌmɪsˈpraɪskraɪbd/ | v. | 处方开错；误开药方 | To prescribe (a medicine or treatment) incorrectly or inappropriately.  This implies a mistake in the prescription itself, not necessarily a negligent act.  |  The doctor misprescribed the medication, leading to adverse effects. = 医生开错了药，导致了不良反应。

The patient was misprescribed a high dosage of antibiotics. = 病人被错误地开了高剂量的抗生素。


近义词：prescribe incorrectly, wrongly prescribe, incorrectly administer


反义词：prescribe correctly, appropriately prescribe


词性:动词
