slimes:/slaɪmz/| n.|粘液；泥浆；卑鄙的人|Slimes are thick, sticky substances, often found in nature or produced by certain organisms.  They can also refer to something unpleasant or despicable.|例句：The slime was thick and green.  这粘液又厚又绿。

例句：He's a slime, always trying to cheat people. 他是个卑鄙小人，总是想骗人。


近义词：ooze, muck, mire, scum,  (for the despicable meaning) scoundrel, villain


反义词：cleanliness, purity (for the substance meaning);  honesty, integrity (for the despicable meaning)


词性:名词
