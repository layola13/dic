nectar:/ˈnektər/| n. |花蜜；甘露；美味佳肴|Sweet liquid produced by flowers, which attracts insects and other animals for pollination.  It is also used figuratively to describe something delicious or delightful.|例句：The bees were busy collecting nectar from the flowers. 蜜蜂正忙着从花朵中采集花蜜。

例句：The dessert was pure nectar; a delightful treat. 这款甜点简直就是甘露；美味佳肴。|近义词：honey, ambrosia|反义词：poison, bitterness|词性:名词
