cluelessly:/ˈkluːləsli/| adv.|茫然地；不知所措地；愚蠢地|In a way that shows a lack of understanding or awareness; foolishly.|例句：He wandered cluelessly through the streets, lost and confused. 他茫然地走在街上，迷路且困惑。

例句：She answered the complex question cluelessly, revealing her lack of knowledge. 她茫然地回答了那个复杂的问题，暴露了她缺乏知识。


近义词：helplessly, aimlessly, ignorantly, stupidly

反义词：knowingly, intelligently, purposefully, skillfully
