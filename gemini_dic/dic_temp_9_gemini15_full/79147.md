gladstone:/ˈɡlædˌstən/ | n. |格莱斯顿包;格莱斯顿（人名）| A Gladstone bag is a type of travel bag, typically made of leather, with a flat, rectangular shape and a hinged top.  It's named after William Ewart Gladstone, a British Prime Minister who was known to use such a bag. The name can also refer to the person, William Ewart Gladstone. |例句：He packed his toiletries in his Gladstone bag before the trip. 他在旅行前把洗漱用品装进了他的格莱斯顿包。
