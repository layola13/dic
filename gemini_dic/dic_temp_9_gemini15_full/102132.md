isp:/ˈɪsp/| n. |互联网服务提供商|Internet Service Provider; a company that provides access to the internet.|Example:  My ISP is experiencing an outage. = 我的互联网服务提供商正在中断服务。|近义词：Internet provider, network provider|反义词：N/A
