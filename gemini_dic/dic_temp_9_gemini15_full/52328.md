dicotyledon: /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ | n. |双子叶植物|A flowering plant with two cotyledons (seed leaves) in the embryo.  Dicotyledons are characterized by their net-veined leaves, usually four or five petals (or multiples thereof), and vascular bundles arranged in a ring in the stem.|例句：Roses and oaks are examples of dicotyledons. 玫瑰和橡树是双子叶植物的例子。|近义词：dicot |反义词：monocotyledon
