hyperrhythmic: /ˌhaɪpərˈrɪðmɪk/ | adj. | 超节奏的，过节奏的 | Characterized by an excessive or irregular rhythm; exceeding the normal or expected rhythm.  It often implies a sense of excitement, intensity, or even chaos due to the disrupted rhythm. |


例句：

The hyperrhythmic music filled the dance floor with a frenetic energy. =  超节奏的音乐充满了舞池，充满了狂热的能量。

His hyperrhythmic speech made it difficult to follow his train of thought. = 他语速过快，让人难以理解他的思路。


近义词：

frenetic, frantic, agitated, tumultuous, over-rhythmic


反义词：

monotonous, rhythmical (in a regular, expected way), calm, subdued
