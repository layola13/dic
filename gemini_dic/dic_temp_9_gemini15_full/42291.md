counter-indication:/ˌkaʊntərˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |禁忌症，反适应症 |A counter-indication is a condition or factor that makes a particular treatment or procedure inadvisable or dangerous.  It's a reason why a particular treatment should *not* be used. |Example:  The patient's allergy to penicillin is a strong counter-indication to prescribing that antibiotic.  这位病人的青霉素过敏是禁止开这种抗生素的强烈禁忌症。|近义词：contraindication, warning, prohibition |反义词：indication
