landing:/ˈlændɪŋ/| n. v.|着陆，登陆；码头，平台；(飞机的)降落；(商品的)上市；(尤指)成功，获得|The act of an aircraft, spacecraft, etc., touching down on a surface.  Also, the place where such an act occurs.  Figuratively, it can refer to the arrival or achievement of something.|例句：The pilot skillfully executed the landing.  飞行员熟练地完成了降落。

例句：The new product's landing was met with great enthusiasm. 新产品的上市受到了极大的热情。


近义词：arrival, touchdown, disembarkation, debut|反义词：takeoff, departure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plane lands at 3 pm. = 飞机下午三点降落。
He lands a new client every week. = 他每周都会争取到一个新客户。


一般过去时:
The spacecraft landed on the moon. = 宇宙飞船着陆在月球上。
She landed the lead role in the play. = 她获得了该剧的主角。


现在完成时:
The company has landed several major contracts. = 公司已经签订了几份重大合同。
The bird has landed safely on the branch. = 鸟安全地落在了树枝上。


过去完成时:
The plane had landed before the storm hit. = 飞机在暴风雨来临之前已经降落了。
They had landed a big fish in their negotiations. = 他们在谈判中获得了一个很大的成功。


将来时:
The astronauts will land on Mars next year. = 宇航员明年将登陆火星。
We will land the deal if we negotiate well. = 如果我们谈判顺利，我们将达成协议。


虚拟语气:
If the plane landed safely, everyone would be relieved. = 如果飞机安全着陆，每个人都会松一口气。
I wish I had landed that job. = 我真希望我得到了那份工作。


被动语态:
The plane was landed smoothly by the pilot. = 飞机被飞行员平稳地降落了。
The contract was finally landed after months of negotiation. = 合同经过几个月的谈判后最终达成。


疑问句:
When will the plane land? = 飞机什么时候降落？
Did they successfully land the deal? = 他们成功达成协议了吗？


否定句:
The plane didn't land on time. = 飞机没有按时降落。
They failed to land the account. = 他们未能拿下这个客户。


PHRASE->
a soft landing = 平稳着陆
a hard landing = 艰难着陆/紧急着陆
make a landing = 着陆
emergency landing = 紧急着陆
forced landing = 迫降

词源:
The word "landing" comes from the Old English word "landung," meaning "act of coming ashore" or "place of coming ashore."  It evolved through Middle English and into its modern form.  The verb sense evolved from the noun meaning.
