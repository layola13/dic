monogramming:/mɒnəˈɡræmɪŋ/|/ˈmɑːnəɡræmɪŋ/| n. v.| 装饰（织物、皮革等）|The process of decorating fabric, leather, or other materials with a monogram.| 例句：The towels were monogramming with our initials. 毛巾上绣上了我们的姓名首字母。|  The boutique offers monogramming services for its products. 这家精品店为其产品提供刻字服务。|近义词：embossing, lettering, initialing|反义词：plain, undecorated
