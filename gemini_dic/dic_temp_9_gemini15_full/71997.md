flinging:/ˈflɪŋɪŋ/| v.| 猛扔；猛掷；挥舞；(非正式)狂欢； |To throw or cast something with force.| The child was flinging stones at the window.  孩子正用力往窗户扔石头。 He flung the door open in anger. 他生气地猛地把门打开了。|throwing, tossing, hurling| catching, receiving| 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is flinging the ball to his dog. = 他正把球扔给他的狗。
She is flinging her arms around in excitement. = 她兴奋地挥舞着手臂。


一般过去时:
They flung themselves onto the sofa. = 他们猛地扑倒在沙发上。
He flung the letter into the fire. = 他把信扔进了火里。


现在完成时:
She has flung herself into her work. = 她全身心地投入到工作中。
He has flung his old clothes away. = 他已经把旧衣服扔掉了。


过去完成时:
They had flung the rubbish into the bin before leaving. = 他们在离开之前已经把垃圾扔进了垃圾桶。
She had flung her coat on the chair. = 她已经把外套扔在了椅子上。


将来时:
He will fling the javelin with all his might. = 他将用尽全力投掷标枪。
They will fling a party to celebrate. = 他们将举行派对庆祝。


虚拟语气:
If I had the strength, I would fling the rock across the river. = 如果我有力气，我会把石头扔到河对面。
I wish I could fling my worries away. = 我希望我能摆脱我的烦恼。


被动语态:
The ball was flung across the field. = 球被扔过了田野。
The door was flung open by the wind. = 门被风吹开了。


疑问句:
Was the ball flung intentionally? = 球是故意扔的吗？
Who flung the first stone? = 谁扔的第一个石头？


否定句:
He didn't fling the stone hard enough. = 他没有用力地扔石头。
She didn't fling the door shut. = 她没有用力关上门。


PHRASE->

fling around = 挥舞
fling a party = 举办派对
