pretranscribing:/ˌpriːˈtrænskraɪbɪŋ/| v.|预先转录|The act of transcribing something before it is officially recorded or published.  It often involves preparing a draft or preliminary version of a text or audio recording.  This can be done for various reasons, such as to prepare for a more formal transcription, to create a working copy, or to identify key information early on.|例句：The intern was tasked with pretranscribing the interview recordings before the official transcriptionist began their work. 实习生在正式速记员开始工作之前负责对采访录音进行预先转录。|近义词：pre-recording, preliminary transcription, drafting|反义词：post-transcription, final transcription|动词
