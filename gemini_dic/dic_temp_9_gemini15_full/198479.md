shrillish:/ʃrɪlɪʃ/| adj.|略微尖锐的，有点刺耳的|Slightly shrill; somewhat high-pitched and piercing in sound.  The word suggests a less intense or less unpleasant version of "shrill".|例句：The singer's voice was a little shrillish, but not unpleasant.  这位歌手的声音有点尖锐，但并不令人反感。

The music had a shrillish quality that grated on my nerves after a while. 这音乐有点刺耳，过了一会儿就磨损了我的神经。|近义词：slightly shrill, somewhat high-pitched,  a bit piercing|反义词：soft, mellow, muted
