characterize: /ˈkærəktəraɪz/ | vt. |描绘…的特性；使具有…的特征；是…的特征|To describe the qualities or features of someone or something; to be characteristic of.|例句：The novel is characterized by its dark humor. 这部小说以其黑色幽默为特色。|例句：His arrogance characterized his behavior. 他的傲慢是其行为的特征。|近义词：describe, depict, define, distinguish|反义词：misrepresent, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist characterizes the subject's sadness in the painting. = 艺术家在画中描绘了主题人物的悲伤。
We characterize this material as highly durable. = 我们把这种材料描述为高度耐用的。


一般过去时:
The historian characterized the era as one of great social change. = 历史学家将那个时代描述为一个伟大的社会变革时期。
They characterized the new policy as unfair. = 他们认为这项新政策是不公平的。


现在完成时:
Scientists have characterized the virus as highly contagious. = 科学家们已经将这种病毒描述为具有高度传染性。
Researchers have characterized the effects of the drug on the body. = 研究人员已经描述了该药物对人体的作用。


过去完成时:
Historians had characterized the previous regime as oppressive. = 历史学家们之前将之前的政权描述为压迫性的。
Scientists had characterized the substance as inert before further research. = 在进一步研究之前，科学家们将该物质描述为惰性的。


将来时:
Future studies will characterize the long-term effects of climate change. = 未来的研究将描述气候变化的长期影响。
Experts predict that they will characterize this new technology as revolutionary. = 专家预测他们会将这项新技术描述为革命性的。


虚拟语气:
If we characterized the problem differently, we might find a better solution. = 如果我们对问题的描述有所不同，我们可能会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish the media characterized the situation more accurately. = 我希望媒体能更准确地描述这种情况。


被动语态:
The period was characterized by rapid technological advancement. = 那个时期以快速的科技进步为特征。
The painting is characterized by its vibrant colors. = 这幅画的特点是色彩鲜艳。


疑问句:
How would you characterize the current economic climate? = 你如何描述当前的经济形势？
How can we best characterize the relationship between these two variables? = 我们如何才能最好地描述这两个变量之间的关系？


否定句:
We cannot characterize this behavior as normal. = 我们不能将这种行为描述为正常的。
The report failed to adequately characterize the complexity of the issue. = 该报告未能充分描述问题的复杂性。


PHRASE->
be characterized by = 以……为特征
characterized as = 被描述为
well-characterized = 特征明确的
poorly characterized = 特征不明确的
fully characterized = 完全表征的
