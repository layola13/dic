innocently:/ˈɪnəsntli/| adv.|天真地；无辜地；清白地 | in a way that shows a lack of knowledge of evil or wickedness; in a way that is not guilty of a crime or wrongdoing. | 例句：She smiled innocently. 她天真地笑了。

She innocently believed his lies. 她天真地相信了他的谎言。

近义词：guilelessly, naively, simply, purely | 反义词：guiltily, wickedly, deliberately, knowingly
