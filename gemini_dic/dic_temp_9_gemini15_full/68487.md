famine:/ˈfæmɪn/| n.|饥荒，饥馑|A widespread scarcity of food, caused by several factors such as war, inflation, crop failure, or government policies.  It often results in widespread starvation and death.|The potato famine in Ireland in the 1840s led to mass emigration.|近义词：starvation, dearth, hunger|反义词：abundance, plenty
