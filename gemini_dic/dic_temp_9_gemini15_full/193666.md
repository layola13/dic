This often involves using internal knowledge or algorithms to assign itself to a specific group or class.  | Example:  The machine learning model performed self-classification by analyzing its own internal parameters and assigning itself a performance level.  机器学习模型通过分析其自身的内部参数并为自己分配性能级别来执行自我分类。|近义词：auto-classification, automatic categorization |反义词：external classification, manual categorization |名词
