raffles:/ˈræfəlz/| n. v.|抽彩，彩票；分配，分发|A raffle is a type of lottery where participants buy tickets for a chance to win a prize.  The verb "to raffle" means to organize or participate in a raffle, or to distribute something by chance.|例句：The school held a raffle to raise money for new equipment. 学校举办了一场抽彩活动为新设备筹款。

The company raffled off a car to one lucky winner. 公司将一辆汽车作为奖品抽奖送给了幸运的赢家。|近义词：lottery, drawing, giveaway |反义词: auction (though both involve prizes, the methods differ significantly)
