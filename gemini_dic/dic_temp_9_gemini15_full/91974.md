hypermystical: /ˌhaɪpərˈmɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 超神秘的，极度神秘的 | Extremely mystical; exceeding the normal bounds of mysticism.  The term suggests a level of mystery or spiritual depth that transcends ordinary mystical experiences.  It implies something profoundly enigmatic and beyond common understanding. |


例句：

The ancient texts described a hypermystical experience involving a journey to another dimension.  古代文本描述了一种超神秘的体验，包括一次通往另一个维度的旅程。

The artist's work evoked a hypermystical atmosphere, filled with symbolic imagery and ambiguous meaning.  这位艺术家的作品营造了一种超神秘的氛围，充满了象征性的意象和含糊不清的含义。


近义词：supernatural, transcendental, profoundly mysterious, ultra-mystical


反义词：mundane, commonplace, ordinary, clearly explained
