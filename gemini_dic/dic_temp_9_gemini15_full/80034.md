go-together:/ˌɡoʊ təˈɡɛðər/| phrasal verb|搭配，相配，协调|This phrase describes things that complement each other well or are naturally associated.  It suggests a harmonious relationship or compatibility.|例句：Red wine and cheese go together perfectly. 红酒和奶酪完美搭配。|近义词：complement, match, suit, harmonize|反义词：clash, conflict, mismatch


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These colors go together well. = 这些颜色很搭配。
That shirt and tie go together nicely. = 那件衬衫和领带很相配。


一般过去时:
The music and lighting went together beautifully at the concert. = 音乐和灯光在音乐会上完美地融合在一起。
The cake and ice cream went together perfectly at the party. = 蛋糕和冰淇淋在派对上完美搭配。


现在完成时:
The new furniture has gone together well with the existing decor. = 新家具与现有装饰很搭配。
The team's efforts have gone together to achieve great success. = 团队的努力共同取得了巨大的成功。


过去完成时:
The evidence had gone together to convincingly prove his guilt. = 证据已经充分证明了他的罪行。
The ingredients had gone together to create a delicious meal. = 食材融合在一起创造了一顿美味的饭菜。


将来时:
The new curtains will go together beautifully with the wallpaper. = 新窗帘与墙纸将会完美搭配。
Their plans will go together to make a successful project. = 他们的计划将共同促成一个成功的项目。


虚拟语气:
If those two patterns went together, the dress would look stunning. = 如果这两种图案搭配起来，这件衣服会非常漂亮。
I wish those colors went together better. = 我希望这些颜色搭配得更好。


被动语态:
The ingredients are carefully chosen so they go together perfectly. = 食材经过精心挑选，以确保完美搭配。
The components were designed to go together seamlessly. = 这些组件的设计目的是无缝衔接。


疑问句:
Do these shoes go together with this outfit? = 这双鞋和这套衣服搭配吗？
Do you think these colors go together? = 你觉得这些颜色搭配吗？


否定句:
Those colors don't go together at all. = 那些颜色根本不搭配。
