cosmopolitanism:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənɪzəm/ | n. |世界主义；国际化；大都会的特性 | The ideology that all human beings belong to a single community, based on a shared morality.  Cosmopolitanism emphasizes a shared humanity and interconnectedness, advocating for global cooperation and understanding.  It often involves a rejection of narrow nationalism or parochialism in favor of a broader, more inclusive worldview. | Example:  The city's cosmopolitanism is evident in its diverse population and vibrant cultural scene.  这个城市的国际化在其多元化的人口和充满活力的文化景象中可见一斑。

Example:  Her cosmopolitan outlook allowed her to easily adapt to life in a new country. 她具有世界眼光，让她很容易适应在新的国家的生活。


近义词：internationalism, globalism, universalism | 反义词：provincialism, nationalism, parochialism
