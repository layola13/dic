USAGE->
一般现在时:
The construction company builds houses. = 这家建筑公司建造房屋。
Construction work is underway. = 建筑工程正在进行中。

一般过去时:
They constructed a new school last year. = 他们去年建了一所新学校。
The building's construction was completed in 2020. = 这座建筑的建设在2020年完成。

现在完成时:
They have constructed many buildings in this city. = 他们在该城市建造了许多建筑物。
The construction of the dam has been completed. = 水坝的建设已经完成。

过去完成时:
They had constructed the foundation before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经建好了地基。
The construction of the road had been delayed due to the rain. = 由于下雨，道路的建设被推迟了。

将来时:
They will construct a new hospital next year. = 他们明年将建造一所新医院。
The construction of the tunnel will begin next month. = 隧道的建设将于下个月开始。

虚拟语气:
If they had more money, they would construct a larger building. = 如果他们有更多的钱，他们会建造一座更大的建筑物。
I wish the construction of the bridge had been faster. = 我希望桥梁的建设能够更快一些。

被动语态:
The building is being constructed by a local company. = 这座建筑正在由一家当地公司建造。
The bridge was constructed in the 1980s. = 这座桥是在20世纪80年代建造的。

疑问句:
