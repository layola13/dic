microbicidal:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈbaɪsɪdəl/ | adj. | 杀菌的，具有杀菌作用的 |  Able to kill microorganisms, such as bacteria, fungi, and viruses.  | Example:  The new cleaning solution has strong microbicidal properties.  这种新型清洁溶液具有强大的杀菌性能。

The microbicidal agent effectively eliminated the bacteria in the wound.  这种杀菌剂有效地消除了伤口中的细菌。|近义词：germicidal, bactericidal, antimicrobial | 反义词：microbiostatic

词源:

* **micro-**:  From Greek *mikros*, meaning "small."
* **-bicide**: From Latin *bicida*, meaning "killer" (from *bī-*, "two," and *caedere*, "to kill").  The "-cide" suffix commonly indicates something that kills.

Therefore, "microbicidal" literally means "small-life killer."
