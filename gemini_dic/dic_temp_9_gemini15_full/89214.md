hometown:/ˈhoʊmˌtaʊn/| n.|家乡，故乡|The place where one was born and raised, or where one has lived for a long time and feels a strong connection to.|例句：My hometown is a small village nestled in the mountains. 我的家乡是一个依偎在山中的小山村。

例句：I often visit my hometown during the holidays. 我经常在节假日回家乡。

近义词：native place, birthplace

反义词：foreign country, abroad


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My hometown is a quiet place. = 我的家乡是一个安静的地方。
His hometown boasts a rich history. = 他的家乡拥有丰富的历史。


一般过去时:
I lived in my hometown until I was 18. = 我在我家乡住了18年。
She left her hometown to pursue her dreams. = 她离开家乡去追求她的梦想。


现在完成时:
I have lived in my hometown for most of my life. = 我大部分时间都住在我的家乡。
She has returned to her hometown after many years abroad. = 她在国外多年后回到了家乡。


过去完成时:
I had already left my hometown before the war broke out. = 在战争爆发之前我已经离开了家乡。
She had never forgotten her hometown, even after all those years. = 即使过了这么多年，她从未忘记过自己的家乡。


将来时:
I will visit my hometown next summer. = 我明年夏天将回我的家乡。
She will probably return to her hometown after she retires. = 她退休后可能会回到家乡。


虚拟语气:
If I could, I would live in my hometown forever. = 如果可以，我会永远住在我的家乡。
I wish I were still living in my hometown. = 我希望我仍然住在我的家乡。


被动语态:
My hometown was devastated by the flood. = 我的家乡被洪水摧毁了。
The history of my hometown is well-documented. = 我家乡的历史有很好的记载。


疑问句:
Where is your hometown? = 你的家乡在哪里？
What is your hometown like? = 你的家乡是什么样的？


否定句:
My hometown isn't very big. = 我的家乡不是很大。
He doesn't live in his hometown anymore. = 他不再住在他的家乡了。
