prejudiced:/ˈprɛdʒʊdɪst/| adj.|有偏见的；抱成见的；偏颇的|Having or showing a prejudice; biased.  Prejudiced people form opinions about others based on stereotypes rather than individual merit. They may act in unfair or discriminatory ways toward those they prejudice against.|例句：He was prejudiced against people from other countries. 他对来自其他国家的人有偏见。


例句：The judge's prejudiced remarks influenced the jury's decision. 法官有偏见的言论影响了陪审团的判决。


近义词：biased, partial, bigoted, discriminatory|反义词：unbiased, impartial, objective, fair


词性：形容词
