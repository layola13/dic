officeless:/ˈɒfɪsləs/| adj.|无办公室的；不用办公室的|Without an office; not requiring or using an office.  The term often describes work arrangements where employees work remotely, from home, or in co-working spaces, rather than a traditional office setting. |例句：Many companies are adopting officeless work models to attract and retain talent. 许多公司正在采用无办公室的工作模式以吸引和留住人才。
例句：The officeless startup thrived on its flexible work arrangements. 这家无办公室的初创公司在其灵活的工作安排中蓬勃发展。|近义词：remote, decentralized, virtual |反义词：office-based, traditional
