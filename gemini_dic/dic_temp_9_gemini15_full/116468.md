mater:/ˈmeɪtər/| n.|母亲，母体；物质；（大学的）系主任|  A mother;  the physical substance of which something is made; a head of a department in a university.|例句：My mother is my mater. 我的母亲是我的母亲。

She is the mater of the family. 她是这个家庭的家长。


The mater of the universe is unknown. 宇宙的物质是未知的.


He is the mater of the physics department. 他是物理系的系主任。


近义词：mother, parent, substance, material|反义词：father, offspring, spirit
