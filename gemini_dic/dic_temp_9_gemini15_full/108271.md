launderettes:/ˌlɔːndərˈɛts/| n. |自助洗衣店|A launderette is a place where people can wash and dry their clothes using machines, usually for a fee.  It's often equipped with washing machines, dryers, and sometimes ironing facilities.  Launderettes are commonly found in residential areas and provide a convenient alternative to doing laundry at home.|There's a launderette just around the corner from my apartment. = 我公寓拐角处就有一家自助洗衣店。

The new launderette offers a wide range of services, including dry cleaning. = 这家新开的自助洗衣店提供各种服务，包括干洗。|洗衣房，洗衣店|dry cleaner's, self-service laundry|N/A
