huffiness:/ˈhʌfɪnɪs/| n. |愠怒，生气，傲慢 | A feeling of being offended or annoyed; sulkiness.  It often involves a display of anger or resentment, but it can also be a more subtle feeling of being put out.  | Example:  Her huffiness was evident in the way she ignored him.  她的愠怒在她无视他的方式中显而易见。


Example: The child's huffiness after being told no was palpable.  孩子被拒绝后的愠怒是显而易见的。|近义词：resentment, indignation, anger, sulkiness, petulance |反义词：calmness, serenity, placidity, good humor
