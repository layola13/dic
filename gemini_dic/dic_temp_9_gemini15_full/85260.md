harmonica: /ˌhɑːrˈmɒnɪkə/ | n. |口琴| A small musical instrument that is played by blowing air through holes to produce sound.  It typically has a rectangular shape and a series of holes along its length.  |例句：He played a soulful melody on his harmonica. 他用口琴演奏了一首深情的旋律。|近义词：mouth organ |反义词：None
