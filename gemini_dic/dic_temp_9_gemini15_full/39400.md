conjuror:/ˈkʌndʒərər/ | n. |魔术师，变戏法的人 |A conjuror is a person who performs magic tricks, illusions, and other feats of deception. They often use sleight of hand and misdirection to create the illusion of magic.  |The conjuror amazed the audience with his incredible card tricks.  魔术师以他令人难以置信的纸牌戏法让观众惊叹不已。 |magician, illusionist, prestidigitator | (无明显反义词) |名词
