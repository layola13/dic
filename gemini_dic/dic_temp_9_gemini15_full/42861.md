词源:

The word "courtesan" originates from the Old French word *cortisane*, which itself derives from the Italian word *cortigiana*.  The Italian word ultimately traces back to *corte*, meaning "court," referring to the royal or aristocratic courts where these women often resided and served.  The implication is that they were associated with the court and its influential members.
