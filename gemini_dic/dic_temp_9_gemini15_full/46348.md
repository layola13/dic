dactylic:/dækˈtɪlɪk/| adj.|达克体的；三音步的|Of or relating to a dactyl or dactylic verse.  A dactyl is a metrical foot consisting of one stressed syllable followed by two unstressed syllables.  Dactylic verse is poetry written in dactyls.|例句：The poem is written in dactylic hexameter. 这首诗是用达克体六音步写的。|近义词：trochaic (反义，指抑扬格)|反义词：trochaic


USAGE->
无。dactylic本身为形容词，没有动词或名词用法，无需列举时态等。

dactylic pentameter: 达克体五音步
dactylic tetrameter: 达克体四音步
