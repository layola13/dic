punic:/ˈpjuːnɪk/| adj.|迦太基的；腓尼基的；狡猾的|Of or relating to Carthage or its people;  characterized by trickery or deceit.  The word often carries a negative connotation, referring to Carthaginian treachery during the Punic Wars. |例句：The Punic Wars were a series of three major conflicts between Rome and Carthage. = 迦太基战争是罗马和迦太基之间发生的三次重大冲突。

The Punic faithlessness was legendary. = 迦太基人的背信弃义是出了名的。|近义词：Carthaginian, treacherous, deceitful, cunning |反义词：trustworthy, honest, faithful
