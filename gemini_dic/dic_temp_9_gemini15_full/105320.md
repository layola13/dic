kiboshed:/kɪˈbɒʃt/ | vt. | 彻底破坏；使泡汤；阻止 | To put a stop to something; to ruin or spoil something completely.  |例句：The rain kiboshed our picnic plans.  雨水彻底破坏了我们的野餐计划。
The deal was kiboshed at the last minute. 这笔交易在最后一刻泡汤了。| 近义词：ruin, spoil, scuttle, thwart, nix | 反义词：further, advance, promote, support | 动词
