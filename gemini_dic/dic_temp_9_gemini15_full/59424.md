earthliness:/ˈɜːθlinəs/ | n. |尘世；世俗；现世 |The quality or state of being earthly; worldliness;  the concerns and characteristics of earthly life as opposed to spiritual or heavenly matters. It often implies a lack of spirituality or a focus on material things. |例句：He felt a deep sense of earthliness after spending time in the bustling city. 他在繁华的城市待了一段时间后，感到一种强烈的尘世感。


例句：Her paintings, while beautiful, lacked the spiritual depth and transcended the mere earthliness of the subject matter.  她的画虽然很美，但却缺乏精神深度，仅仅停留在对主题的世俗描绘上。


近义词：worldliness, temporality, materialism, carnality |反义词：spirituality, otherworldliness, transcendence, holiness
