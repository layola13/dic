conceptual:/kənˈsɛptʃuəl/| adj. | 概念的；观念的；理论上的 | Relating to or based on concepts; abstract or theoretical.  | The project is still in the conceptual stage.  这个项目仍处于概念阶段。| theoretical, abstract, notional | concrete, practical, tangible | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist's conceptual approach is fascinating. = 艺术家的概念化方法很吸引人。
His conceptual framework is quite complex. = 他的概念框架相当复杂。


一般过去时:
The philosopher's ideas were highly conceptual. =这位哲学家的思想非常概念化。
The presentation was largely conceptual, lacking in practical details. = 这次演示很大程度上是概念性的，缺乏实际细节。


现在完成时:
They have developed a new conceptual model for the process. = 他们已经为此过程开发了一个新的概念模型。
Scientists have long conceptualized the universe in various ways. = 科学家们长期以来以各种方式将宇宙概念化。


过去完成时:
Before the presentation, they had conceptualized the project differently. = 在演示之前，他们对项目的构想有所不同。
She had conceptualized a solution, but it proved impractical. = 她已经构想出一个解决方案，但事实证明它不切实际。


将来时:
We will conceptualize a new marketing strategy next week. = 我们下周将构想一个新的营销策略。
The architects will conceptualize the building design in the coming months. = 建筑师们将在接下来的几个月里构想建筑设计。


虚拟语气:
If we had conceptualized the problem differently, we might have found a better solution. = 如果我们对问题的构想有所不同，我们可能会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish I had conceptualized the project more thoroughly before starting. = 我希望在开始之前能够更彻底地构想这个项目。


被动语态:
The project was conceptualized by a team of experts. = 该项目是由专家团队构思的。
The new policy was conceptualized over several months. = 新政策经过数月才形成概念。


疑问句:
How was the new product conceptualized? = 新产品是如何构思出来的？
Was the artwork conceptual or purely representational? = 这件艺术品是概念性的还是纯粹的写实性的？


否定句:
The design wasn't fully conceptualized before construction began. = 在施工开始之前，设计并没有完全概念化。
The plan wasn't well-conceptualized and therefore failed. = 计划构思不周，因此失败了。
