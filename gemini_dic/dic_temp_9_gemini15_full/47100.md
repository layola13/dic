daughter: /ˈdɔːtər/ /ˈdɑːtər/ | n. | 女儿 | A female offspring.  A woman in relation to her parents. | Example:  She is the eldest daughter in her family. 她是她家里的长女。


Example: My daughter is studying abroad. 我的女儿在国外留学。


近义词： girl, offspring, child (in some contexts) | 反义词：son


词性: 名词


USAGE->

My daughter plays the piano. = 我的女儿弹钢琴。
She loves her daughter very much. = 她非常爱她的女儿。

My daughter went to school yesterday. = 我的女儿昨天去上学了。
