octopath:/ˈɒktəpɑːθ/| n. |八条路径；八路游戏|A video game with eight different protagonists, each with their own story and gameplay.|Example: Octopath Traveler is a popular role-playing game.|近义词：octuple path, eightfold path|反义词：single path


USAGE->
