scholiastic: /ˌskɒlɪˈæstɪk/ | adj. | 学术的；学院派的；书本气的 | Relating to schools, scholars, or scholarship;  pedantic or academic in character.  | 例句：His scholiastic approach to the problem ignored the practical realities.  他的学术方法忽略了实际情况。

The debate was characterized by scholiastic arguments that failed to address the core issue. 这场辩论的特点是学院派的论证，未能解决核心问题。


近义词：academic, scholarly, pedantic | 反义词：practical, pragmatic


词源:  From Late Latin scholasticus, from schola "school".  Ultimately derived from the Greek word σχολή (scholē), meaning "leisure," "school," or "place of discussion."
