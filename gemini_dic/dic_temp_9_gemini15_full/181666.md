releasor:/rɪˈliːsər/| n. | 释放者；放弃者；让与人 | A person who releases something, such as property or a right.  This term often appears in legal contexts, referring to the party relinquishing a claim or right. | Example: The releasor signed the document, transferring ownership of the land.  释放者签署了文件，转移了土地的所有权。|近义词：transferor, grantor, assignor |反义词：releasee


USAGE->

The releasor signs the contract. = 释放者签署合同。
He is the releasor in this transaction. = 他是这笔交易中的释放者。
