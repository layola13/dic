lisps:/lɪps/| n.| 说话口齿不清；(尤指)齿音不准| A lisp is a speech impediment in which the sounds /s/ and /z/ are pronounced as /θ/ and /ð/ respectively (or similar sounds).  It's often caused by the tongue placement during articulation. |例句：He has a slight lisp when he speaks. 他说话有点儿口齿不清。|例句：The child's lisp was gradually corrected with speech therapy.  孩子的齿音不准通过语言治疗逐渐纠正了。|近义词：speech impediment, articulation problem|反义词：clear articulation, distinct speech|名词
