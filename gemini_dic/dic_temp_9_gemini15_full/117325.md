meddlesome:/ˈmɛdəlsəm/| adj.| 爱管闲事的；多事的；干涉的 |  Interfering in other people's affairs; inquisitive and intrusive.  |例句：He was a meddlesome old man who always poked his nose into other people's business. 他是个爱管闲事的老头，总是把鼻子伸到别人的事里。

例句：Her meddlesome interference nearly ruined the project. 她爱管闲事的干预几乎毁了这个项目。


近义词：interfering, intrusive, nosy, busybody


反义词：unobtrusive, uninvolved, indifferent


词性：形容词
