nonharmonic:/ˌnɒnˈhɑːmɒnɪk/|/ˌnɑːnˈhɑːrmɒnɪk/| adj. |不和谐的；非谐和的|Not in accordance with the rules of harmony; dissonant.  It refers to musical notes or chords that do not belong to the established harmony of a piece of music, creating a sense of tension or dissonance.  It can also be used figuratively to describe things that are incongruous or out of sync.|例句：The composer deliberately used nonharmonic tones to create a sense of unease. 作曲家故意使用不和谐音来营造不安感。


The jarring clash of colors created a nonharmonic visual effect. 刺耳的色彩碰撞产生了不和谐的视觉效果。|近义词：dissonant, discordant, atonal, incongruous|反义词：harmonic, consonant, concordant
