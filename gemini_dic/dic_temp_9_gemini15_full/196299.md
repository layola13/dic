serfism:/ˈsɜːrfɪzəm/| n. |农奴制度 |The system of serfdom, where peasants were bound to the land and obligated to work for a lord.  Serfism was a characteristic feature of feudal societies in Europe. |The rise of towns and the growth of trade gradually undermined serfism across Europe.  欧洲城镇的兴起和贸易的增长逐渐削弱了欧洲的农奴制度。|feudalism, bondage|freedom, liberty|名词
