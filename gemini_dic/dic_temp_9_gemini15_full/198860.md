sides:/saɪdz/ | n. | 面，边，方面；侧面；一方；（身体的）侧面 |  A side is a surface of an object that is not a top, bottom, or front. Sides can also refer to a part or aspect of something, or a person involved in a conflict or argument. | 例句：The cube has six sides.  这个立方体有六个面。

The two sides of the argument were equally compelling.  这场辩论的双方都有着同样令人信服的论点。


近义词：aspects, facets, parts, flanks | 反义词：center, middle, core | 词性：名词

The house has four sides. = 这房子有四面墙。
He considers all sides of the issue. = 他考虑了这个问题的各个方面。

虚拟语气:
