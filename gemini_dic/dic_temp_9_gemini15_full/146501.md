overgaze: /ˈoʊvərɡeɪz/ | vt. |凝视；注视；仔细察看 | To look at someone or something for a long time, often with intense or careful attention.  |例句：He overgazed the painting, trying to decipher its hidden meaning. 他凝视着这幅画，试图解读它隐藏的意义。

例句：She overgazed the map, searching for the quickest route. 她仔细察看地图，寻找最快的路线。


近义词：gaze at, stare at, scrutinize, contemplate | 反义词：glance at, skim


词性：动词
