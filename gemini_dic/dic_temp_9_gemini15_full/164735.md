prefashion:/ˈpriːfæʃən/| n. |预先流行，先锋时尚|The word "prefashion" refers to styles, trends, or designs that are emerging but haven't yet become mainstream or widely adopted. It's about predicting and influencing future fashion trends.  It is often associated with avant-garde designers and subcultures who experiment with new ideas and aesthetics. | Example:  The designer's latest collection showcases prefashion elements, hinting at next season's trends.  设计师的最新系列展示了先锋时尚元素，暗示着下季的潮流。|近义词：avant-garde, cutting-edge, trendsetting |反义词：retro, outdated, passé|名词
