overflies: /ˌoʊvərˈflaɪz/ | v. | 飞越；掠过 | To fly over or above.  |例句：The plane overflies the mountains.飞机飞越山脉。 The bird overflies the field.鸟儿飞过田野。 |近义词：fly over, soar over, pass over |反义词：underflies


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird overflies the tree. = 鸟飞越树木。
The plane overflies the city. = 飞机飞越城市。

一般过去时:
The eagle overflew the valley. = 老鹰飞越山谷。
The helicopter overflew the accident site. = 直升机飞越事故现场。

现在完成时:
The pilot has over flown several countries. = 飞行员已经飞越了好几个国家。
The flock of birds has over flown the lake. = 那群鸟已经飞越了湖泊。

过去完成时:
The plane had over flown the target before the bomb was dropped. = 炸弹投下之前，飞机已经飞越了目标。
The birds had over flown the mountains before nightfall. = 夜幕降临前，鸟儿们已经飞越了山脉。

将来时:
The drone will overfly the area tomorrow. = 无人机明天将飞越该区域。
The squadron will overfly the parade route. = 中队将飞越阅兵路线。

虚拟语气:
If the weather permitted, the plane would overfly the route. = 如果天气允许，飞机就会飞越这条路线。
I wish the plane would overfly the city at a lower altitude. = 我希望飞机能以较低的飞行高度飞越这座城市。

被动语态:
The area was over flown by the reconnaissance aircraft. = 该区域被侦察机飞越。
The city is being over flown by numerous drones. = 城市正被无数无人机飞越。

疑问句:
Does the flight path overfly the residential area? = 航线是否飞越居民区？
Will the helicopter overfly the hospital? = 直升机将飞越医院吗？

否定句:
The plane did not overfly the restricted airspace. = 飞机没有飞越禁飞空域。
The birds didn't overfly the forest. = 鸟儿们没有飞越森林。
