panderers:/ˈpændərərz/| n. | 迎合者；逢迎者；拉皮条的人 |  A panderer is someone who caters to or exploits the weaknesses or desires of others, often for personal gain.  This can range from subtly flattering someone to gain favor to engaging in more serious exploitation, such as prostitution. | Example 1: The politician was accused of being a panderer to the public's base desires.  这个政客被指控迎合公众的低级欲望。| Example 2:  The website is filled with panderers selling cheap imitations. 这个网站充斥着兜售廉价仿冒品的奸商。|近义词：sycophant, flatterer, procurer,  exploiter |反义词：critic, opponent, moralist |名词
