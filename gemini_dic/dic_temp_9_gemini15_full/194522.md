self-secure: /ˌsɛlf sɪˈkjʊr/ | adj. | 自信的，有安全感的 | Confident and self-assured; not needing reassurance or approval from others.  |  She is self-secure in her abilities and rarely doubts herself. 她对自己能力很有信心，很少怀疑自己。 He projected an air of self-secure confidence. 他表现出一副自信满满的样子。 |近义词：confident, self-assured, assured, self-possessed |反义词：insecure, anxious, apprehensive, diffident


USAGE->

She feels self-secure in her relationship. = 她对自己的感情很有安全感。
