enumerate:/ɪˈnjuːməreɪt/ | vt. |列举，枚举|To mention (things) one by one; to count; to list.  It involves systematically listing items, often in a numbered or ordered sequence. |The teacher enumerated the rules of the class.老师列举了课堂规则。  She enumerated the reasons for her decision. 她逐条列举了她做出决定的理由。|list, catalog, itemize|group, combine|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enumerates the advantages of the new system. 他列举了新系统的优势。
The report enumerates the key findings of the study. 报告列举了研究的主要发现。


一般过去时:
She enumerated all the problems she faced. 她列举了她面临的所有问题。
The speaker enumerated the main points of his argument. 演讲者列举了他论点的主要内容。


现在完成时:
He has enumerated all the possible solutions. 他已经列举了所有可能的解决方案。
The scientist has enumerated the species found in the region. 科学家已经列举了该地区发现的物种。


过去完成时:
The historian had enumerated the significant events of the era before writing the book. 历史学家在写书之前已经列举了那个时代的重要事件。
Before the meeting, she had enumerated the items on the agenda. 会议之前，她已经列举了议程上的项目。


将来时:
The professor will enumerate the key concepts in the next lecture. 教授将在下一堂课上列举关键概念。
The lawyer will enumerate the charges against the defendant. 律师将列举对被告的指控。


虚拟语气:
If I were to enumerate all the possibilities, it would take hours. 如果我要列举所有可能性，将会花费数小时。
I wish I had enumerated the risks before making the investment. 我希望在投资之前已经列举了风险。


被动语态:
The benefits of the program were enumerated in the brochure.  宣传册中列举了该项目的益处。
The points of disagreement were enumerated in the final report.  最终报告中列举了分歧点。


疑问句:
Can you enumerate the steps involved in the process? 你能列举出该流程中涉及的步骤吗？
How many points will you enumerate in your presentation? 你的陈述中将列举多少要点？


否定句:
The contract did not enumerate all the terms and conditions. 合同没有列举所有条款和条件。
The article failed to enumerate the potential drawbacks. 文章未能列举潜在的缺点。
