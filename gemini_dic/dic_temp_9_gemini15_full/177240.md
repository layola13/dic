reasons:/ˈriːzənz/| n. |原因，理由 |Reasons are explanations for why something happened or why someone did something. They can be based on facts, logic, or emotions.  |例句：He gave several reasons for his absence. 他解释了他缺席的几个理由。|例句：What are your reasons for wanting this job? 你想要这份工作的理由是什么？|近义词：causes, grounds, motives, justifications |反义词：results, effects


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are the reasons for the delay? = 延误的原因是什么？
What reasons does she give for her decision? = 她为她的决定提供了什么理由？


一般过去时:
What were the reasons for the accident? = 事故的原因是什么？
What reasons did he give for quitting his job? = 他辞职的理由是什么？


现在完成时:
What reasons have you found for the problem? = 你找到了什么问题的理由？
What reasons have they given for their actions so far? = 到目前为止，他们为他们的行为提供了什么理由？


过去完成时:
What reasons had they given before the meeting? = 会议前他们给出了什么理由？
What reasons had she already discovered before she announced her conclusion? = 在她宣布结论之前，她已经发现了什么原因？


将来时:
What reasons will they give for the change? = 他们将为这一变化给出什么理由？
What reasons will you provide to support your claim? = 你将提供什么理由来支持你的说法？


虚拟语气:
If I knew the reasons, I would understand. = 如果我知道原因，我就会理解。
I wish I knew the reasons for her behavior. = 我真希望知道她行为的原因。


被动语态:
The reasons for the failure were explained in the report. = 报告中解释了失败的原因。
The reasons for the decision are being carefully considered. = 正在仔细考虑决定的原因。


疑问句:
What are the reasons for the conflict? = 冲突的原因是什么？
What reasons are there to believe him? = 有什么理由相信他？


否定句:
There are no good reasons to refuse. = 没有充分的理由拒绝。
She gave no reasons for her anger. = 她没有解释她生气的原因。


PHRASE->
by reason of = 由于
for (good) reason = 有充分的理由
with reason = 有道理地
beyond reason = 无法解释
give reason = 给出理由
