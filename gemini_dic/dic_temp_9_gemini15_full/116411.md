/ˌmætəˈbɛliː/| n. |马塔贝莱人；马塔贝莱语|The Matabele are a Nguni people of southern Africa, primarily located in Zimbabwe and Botswana.  Their language, also called Matabele, is a Nguni language closely related to Zulu.| The Matabele people are known for their rich cultural heritage and history. |Ndebele (alternative name)|  Zulu| noun
