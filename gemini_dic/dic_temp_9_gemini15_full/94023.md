impanels:/ɪmˈpænəl/| vt.|编入陪审团；选入名单|To select or enroll (a person or group of people) for a specific purpose, especially to serve on a jury.|The judge impaneled a jury of twelve people.  法官选出了12人的陪审团。|The company impaneled a committee to investigate the matter. 公司组建了一个委员会来调查此事。|appoint, select, enlist|exclude, dismiss|动词
