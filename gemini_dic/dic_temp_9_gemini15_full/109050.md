legalises:/ˈliːɡəlaɪzɪz/| v. |使合法化；合法化 |To make something legal.  |The government legalized marijuana in several states. 政府在几个州将大麻合法化了。|  legalize, validate, authorize | prohibit, outlaw, ban | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government legalises certain drugs for medicinal use. = 政府将某些药物合法化用于医疗用途。
They legalise gambling in some parts of the country. = 他们在该国某些地区将赌博合法化。


一般过去时:
The country legalized same-sex marriage last year. = 该国去年将同性婚姻合法化。
They legalized alcohol after a long period of prohibition. = 经过长时间的禁酒令后，他们将酒精合法化了。


现在完成时:
Many countries have legalized abortion in recent decades. = 近几十年来，许多国家已经将堕胎合法化。
The state has legalized recreational marijuana use. = 该州已经将娱乐性大麻使用合法化。


过去完成时:
They had legalized divorce before the social reforms. = 他们在社会改革之前已经将离婚合法化。
The court had legalized the previous agreement before the appeal. = 法院在上诉之前已经将之前的协议合法化。


将来时:
The bill will legalise online gambling next year. = 该法案将在明年将在线赌博合法化。
They will legalise euthanasia if the bill passes. = 如果该法案通过，他们将安乐死合法化。


虚拟语气:
If they legalized it, many people would benefit. = 如果他们将它合法化，许多人将受益。
I wish they legalized that technology sooner. = 我希望他们早点将那项技术合法化。


被动语态:
Marijuana was legalized in several states. = 大麻在几个州被合法化。
Same-sex marriage was legalized across the country. = 同性婚姻在全国范围内被合法化。


疑问句:
Will they legalise assisted suicide? = 他们会将辅助自杀合法化吗？
Has the government legalized the new drug yet? = 政府是否已经将这种新药合法化了？


否定句:
The government did not legalise that practice. = 政府没有将那种做法合法化。
They will not legalise that type of gambling. = 他们不会将那种类型的赌博合法化。

词源:
legalise 源自于法语 "légaliser"，最终来自拉丁语 "legalis" (合法的)，由 "lex, legis" (法律)派生而来。  The suffix "-ise" is a common English suffix to create verbs, meaning "to make or become."
