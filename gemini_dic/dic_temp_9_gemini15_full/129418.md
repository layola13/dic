nomination:/ˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |提名，推荐 |The act of nominating someone or something for an award, position, or honor.  It involves officially suggesting a candidate for a particular role or position.|例句：His nomination for the award was a surprise to everyone.  他的获奖提名让所有人都感到意外。

例句：The committee will consider all nominations before making a decision. 委员会将在做出决定之前考虑所有提名。


近义词：appointment, proposal, candidacy |反义词：rejection


词源:  From the Latin word "nominatio," meaning "naming" or "appointment," derived from "nominare," meaning "to name."
