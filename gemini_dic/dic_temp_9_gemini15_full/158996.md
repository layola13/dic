pluvial:/ˈpluːviəl/| adj.|雨的；雨成的；多雨的|Relating to or caused by rain; rainy.  The word often describes geological periods or formations shaped by rain.|The pluvial period following the last Ice Age saw significant rises in lake levels.  冰河时代后的多雨时期导致湖泊水位显著上升。|The pluvial deposits in this region are extensive.  该地区雨成沉积物很广阔。|rainy, wet, aqueous|arid, dry|形容词
