shipbuilding:/ˈʃɪpˌbɪldɪŋ/| n. |造船业，造船；造船术|The construction of ships.  It encompasses the design, engineering, and manufacturing processes involved in creating vessels of all types, from small boats to large ocean-going ships.|例句：The shipbuilding industry is a significant contributor to the national economy. 造船业是国民经济的重要组成部分。|例句：Modern shipbuilding techniques utilize advanced computer-aided design and manufacturing. 现代造船技术利用先进的计算机辅助设计和制造。|近义词：ship construction, naval architecture |反义词：shipbreaking (拆船)|名词
