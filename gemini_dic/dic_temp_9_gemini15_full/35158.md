cloves:/kloʊvz/| n.|丁香(丁香花蕾)；丁香粉|The dried flower buds of the clove tree, used as a spice.  They have a strong, pungent aroma and a warm, sweet flavor.|例句：She added a few cloves to the stew to enhance the flavor. 她往炖菜里加了几颗丁香以增强味道。|例句：Ground cloves are often used in baking.  研磨的丁香常用于烘焙。|近义词：spice, seasoning|反义词：无
