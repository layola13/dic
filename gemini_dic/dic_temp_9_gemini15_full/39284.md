congruism:/ˈkɒŋɡruɪzəm/ | n. |一致性；和谐；符合；相容性 | The philosophical doctrine that there is a necessary correspondence between thought and reality.  It emphasizes the agreement or harmony between different elements or aspects of something.  |Example: The congruism between his words and actions impressed everyone.  他的言行一致给每个人留下了深刻的印象。 |近义词：accordance, agreement, consistency, harmony |反义词：incongruity, discrepancy, dissonance
