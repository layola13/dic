rescreening:/ˌriːˈskriːnɪŋ/| n.| 重新放映；复审；再筛查 |The act of showing a film or other visual media again, often after a period of time or in a different location.  It can also refer to the process of reviewing something again, such as applications or medical tests. |例句：The classic film is undergoing rescreening at several cinemas across the country. 这部经典电影正在全国多家影院重新上映。


The hospital is conducting a rescreening of all patients with a history of heart disease. 医院正在对所有有心脏病史的病人进行复查。|近义词：re-showing, review, re-examination |反义词：initial screening, first showing
