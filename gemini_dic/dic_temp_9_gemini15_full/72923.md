fondest:/ˈfɒndɪst/ | adj. | 最喜爱的，最疼爱的 | Most beloved; cherished above all others.  The adjective "fondest" is the superlative form of "fond," indicating the highest degree of affection or liking.  |例句：My fondest memory is of summer evenings spent playing in the garden. 我最美好的回忆是在花园里度过的夏夜。

例句：Her fondest wish is to see her grandchildren succeed. 她最大的愿望是看到她的孙子孙女们成功。


近义词：dearest, most cherished, most beloved | 反义词：least favorite, most disliked
