periwinkle: /ˈpɛrɪwɪŋkəl/ | n. adj. |紫杉;海螺;淡紫色|

英文解析:Periwinkle can refer to a small, edible sea snail, a shade of purple-blue color, or a flowering plant with small, blue or purple flowers.  The word's multiple meanings stem from the shared visual similarity of the flower, shell, and color.

例句:
The periwinkles clung tightly to the rocks. = 海螺紧紧地依附在岩石上。

Her dress was a lovely shade of periwinkle. = 她的裙子是一种可爱的淡紫色。


近义词: (depending on the sense):  snail (for the sea snail), lilac, lavender (for the color), vinca (for the plant)

反义词: (no direct antonyms, but contrasting colors could be used):  orange, yellow

词性: noun, adjective

词源:
The word "periwinkle" ultimately derives from the Old French "pervinke," which itself comes from the Latin "vinca," the name of the plant.  The association with the sea snail likely arose from the similar color of the plant and the shell.
