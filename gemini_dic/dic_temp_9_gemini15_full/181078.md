reindicating:/ˌriːˈɪndɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新指示；再次表明；重新指出 |  The act of indicating again or anew; to show or point out again.  It implies a previous indication that needs reiteration or clarification. |例句：The manager reindicated the new safety procedures, emphasizing the importance of wearing protective gear.  经理再次指明新的安全程序，强调了佩戴防护装备的重要性。

例句：The flickering light reindicated the faulty wiring in the old house. 闪烁的灯光再次表明老房子里的线路故障。


近义词：reiterate, reaffirm, restate, point out again, show again | 反义词：conceal, hide, obscure, suppress | 词性：动词
