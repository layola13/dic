permissioned:/pərˈmɪʃənd/| adj.| 许可的，授权的 |  Describes a system or network that requires explicit permission for access or participation.  It contrasts with "permissionless" systems, which allow open access. | Example:  The permissioned blockchain network only allows pre-approved members to participate.  这个许可的区块链网络只允许预先批准的成员参与。| 近义词：authorized, approved, sanctioned | 反义词：permissionless, open, public |  形容词
