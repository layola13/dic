nonseparation: /ˌnɒnˌsepəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 非分离，不分离 | The state or condition of not being separated; the absence of separation.  It often refers to a situation where things that might normally be separated remain together or connected.  | Example: The nonseparation of powers led to corruption.  权力不分立导致了腐败。 The nonseparation of church and state is a controversial issue in some countries. 教会和国家不分离在一些国家是一个有争议的问题。 | 近义词：unity, togetherness, cohesion | 反义词：separation, division, segregation |  词性：名词
