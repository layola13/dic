procarcinogenic: /ˌproʊˌkɑːrsɪnəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |致癌的，促癌的 |  An agent or substance that promotes or enhances the development of cancer.  It doesn't necessarily cause cancer directly, but it increases the likelihood of cancer development.  | Example:  Exposure to certain chemicals can have procarcinogenic effects.  长期接触某些化学物质可能会产生致癌作用。  |近义词: carcinogenic, oncogenic, tumorigenic | 反义词: anticarcinogenic
