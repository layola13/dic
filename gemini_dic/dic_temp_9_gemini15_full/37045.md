comatose:/ˈkoʊmətoʊs/|/ˈkɒmətoʊs/| adj.|昏迷的，无意识的|In a state of deep unconsciousness; unresponsive.  Characterized by a prolonged state of unconsciousness, typically caused by severe injury or illness.|例句：The accident left him comatose for several weeks.  事故使他昏迷了几周。

例句：After the surgery, she remained comatose for days.  手术后，她昏迷了几天。


近义词：unconscious, insensible, stupefied, lethargic

反义词：conscious, awake, alert

词性：形容词
