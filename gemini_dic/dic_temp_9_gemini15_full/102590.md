jail:/dʒeɪl/ | n. |监狱 |A jail is a place where people are kept as a punishment for committing a crime, or while they are waiting for trial.  |He was sent to jail for theft. 他因盗窃被送进监狱。| prison, gaol | freedom, liberty |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prisoner is in jail. = 犯人正在监狱里。
She spends her days in jail. = 她每天都在监狱里度过。


一般过去时:
He was jailed for five years. = 他被判五年监禁。
They went to jail for their crimes. = 他们因犯罪入狱。


现在完成时:
He has been in jail for a month. = 他入狱一个月了。
She has never been to jail before. = 她以前从未进过监狱。


过去完成时:
He had already been in jail before the new charges. = 在新的指控出现之前，他已经入狱了。
They had spent years in jail before their release. = 在获释之前，他们在监狱里待了好几年。


将来时:
He will go to jail if he's found guilty. = 如果他被判有罪，他将入狱。
They will likely be in jail for a long time. = 他们很可能要在监狱里待很长时间。


虚拟语气:
If he had not committed the crime, he wouldn't be in jail now. = 如果他没有犯罪，他现在就不会在监狱里了。
I wish I hadn't been sent to jail. = 我真希望我没有被送进监狱。


被动语态:
The criminal was jailed for his actions. = 罪犯因其行为而被监禁。
Many people are unfairly jailed each year. = 每年都有许多人被不公平地监禁。


疑问句:
Is he still in jail? = 他还在监狱里吗？
How long will he be in jail? = 他要在监狱里待多久？


否定句:
He is not in jail. = 他不在监狱里。
She was not sent to jail. = 她没有被送进监狱。
