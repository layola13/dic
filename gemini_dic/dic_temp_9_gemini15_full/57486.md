drag-and-drop:/dræɡ ænd drɒp/ | n. | 拖放，拖拽 |  A user interface technique that allows users to move graphical elements (such as icons, files, or text) by dragging them with a mouse or similar device and dropping them into a designated area.  | Example:  Drag and drop the files into the folder to upload them.  拖动文件到文件夹中以上传它们。 | 近义词：click-and-place, point-and-click | 反义词：None. | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The user drags and drops the icon onto the desktop. = 用户将图标拖放到桌面上。
We drag and drop files to organize them. = 我们拖放文件来组织它们。


一般过去时:
I dragged and dropped the image into the document. = 我将图像拖放到文档中。
She dragged and dropped the email into the trash. = 她将电子邮件拖放到垃圾桶中。


现在完成时:
They have dragged and dropped all the necessary files. = 他们已经拖放了所有必要的文件。
He has dragged and dropped the photos into the album. = 他已将照片拖放到相册中。


过去完成时:
I had dragged and dropped the file before the system crashed. = 系统崩溃之前，我已经拖放了该文件。
She had dragged and dropped all the data before the deadline. = 截止日期之前，她已经拖放了所有数据。


将来时:
We will drag and drop the documents into the shared folder. = 我们将把文档拖放到共享文件夹中。
He will drag and drop the application icon to launch it. = 他将拖动应用程序图标以启动它。


虚拟语气:
If I could, I would drag and drop the entire library. = 如果可以，我会拖放整个图书馆。
It would be easier if we could drag and drop these elements. = 如果我们可以拖放这些元素，那就容易多了。


被动语态:
The files were dragged and dropped into the wrong folder. = 文件被拖放到错误的文件夹中。
The images are being dragged and dropped onto the canvas. = 图片正在被拖放到画布上。


疑问句:
Can you drag and drop this file? = 你能拖放这个文件吗？
Have you ever used drag-and-drop functionality? = 你曾经使用过拖放功能吗？


否定句:
I cannot drag and drop this file; it's locked. = 我不能拖放这个文件；它被锁定了。
They didn't drag and drop the files in the correct order. = 他们没有按正确的顺序拖放文件。
