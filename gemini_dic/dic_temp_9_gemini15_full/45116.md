cucujo:/kuˈkuʒoʊ/| n. |萤火虫；拟萤火虫|A small, bioluminescent beetle, often called a firefly or glow-worm, especially in the Americas.  The term can sometimes refer to similar insects that aren't true fireflies.|例句：The children were mesmerized by the cucujos flashing in the night.孩子们被夜空中闪烁的萤火虫迷住了。|近义词：firefly, glow-worm, lightning bug|反义词：无
