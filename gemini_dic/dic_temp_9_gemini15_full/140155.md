offended:/əˈfɛndɪd/| adj.|被冒犯的；生气了的|Feeling resentment or anger caused by an insult or injury.|His remarks offended many people.  他的话冒犯了许多人。|近义词：insulted, hurt, aggrieved|反义词：pleased, delighted


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is offended by your joke. = 他被你的笑话冒犯了。
She is often offended by insensitive remarks. = 她经常被不敏感的言论冒犯。


一般过去时:
I was offended by his rude behavior. = 我被他粗鲁的行为冒犯了。
They were offended that they weren't invited. = 他们因为没有被邀请而感到生气。


现在完成时:
I have been offended by his actions many times. = 我多次被他 的行为冒犯。
She has been offended by his constant criticism. = 她一直被他不断的批评所冒犯。


过去完成时:
He had been offended by her words before the argument. = 在争吵之前，他已经因她的话而生气了。
We had been offended by their behavior for a long time. = 我们很久以前就对他们的行为感到反感了。


将来时:
I will be offended if you say that again. = 如果你再说一次，我会生气的。
They will be offended by your lack of respect. = 他们会因为你缺乏尊重而生气。


虚拟语气:
I would be offended if you spoke to me like that. = 如果你那样和我说话，我会生气的。
I wish you hadn't said that; I was offended. = 我希望你没说那话；我被冒犯了。


被动语态:
No one should be offended by this film. = 没有人应该被这部电影冒犯。
Many people were offended by the insensitive comments. = 许多人对那些不敏感的评论感到反感。


疑问句:
Are you offended by my question? = 我的问题冒犯你了吗？
Was he offended by what I said? = 他被我说的话冒犯了吗？


否定句:
I wasn't offended by his joke. = 我并没有被他 的笑话冒犯。
They weren't offended by the criticism. = 他们并没有因为批评而生气。


PHRASE->
feel offended = 感到被冒犯
deeply offended = 深感冒犯
take offense = 生气；感到被冒犯
give offense = 冒犯；得罪
be easily offended =  容易被冒犯
