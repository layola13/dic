hypnotizability: /ˌhɪpnəʊtɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可催眠性，易受催眠暗示性 | The susceptibility to hypnosis; the degree to which a person can be hypnotized.  It refers to the individual's capacity to enter a hypnotic state and respond to hypnotic suggestions.  | Example: The study examined the relationship between hypnotizability and age. 这项研究考察了催眠性和年龄之间的关系。 | 近义词：suggestibility, susceptibility | 反义词：resistance to hypnosis
