euchre:/ˈjuːkər/| n. |尤克尔牌戏|A trick-taking card game played with a 24-card deck (nine cards from each of four suits). The game involves melding (showing certain combinations of cards),  trumping (playing a trump card to beat a higher-ranking card of another suit), and trying to win tricks to score points. |  We played a game of euchre after dinner.  晚餐后我们玩了一局尤克尔牌戏。| 近义词：card game, trick-taking game | 反义词：无
