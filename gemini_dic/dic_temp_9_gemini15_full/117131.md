meandrous:/mɪˈændrəs/| adj.|蜿蜒的；曲折的；冗长的|winding, rambling, and intricate.  Characterized by many turns and bends; winding.  It can also describe something that is long and complicated, often unnecessarily so.|The meandering river flowed through the valley. 这条蜿蜒的河流流经山谷。 The meandrous speech lost the audience's attention.冗长的演讲失去了听众的注意力。|winding, serpentine, tortuous|straight, direct, concise|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river meanders through the valley. = 河流蜿蜒流过山谷。
The road meanders through the mountains. = 这条路蜿蜒穿过群山。


一般过去时:
The river meandered through the valley. = 河流蜿蜒流过山谷。
The path meandered through the woods. = 小路蜿蜒穿过树林。


现在完成时:
The river has meandered for miles. = 这条河已经蜿蜒流淌了数英里。
The speaker has meandered through several unrelated topics. = 演讲者已经涉及了好几个不相关的主题。


过去完成时:
The river had meandered for centuries before reaching the sea. = 这条河在到达大海之前已经蜿蜒流淌了几个世纪。
The discussion had meandered for hours before reaching a conclusion. = 讨论进行了数小时才得出结论。


将来时:
The river will meander through the plains. = 河流将蜿蜒流过平原。
The conference will meander into the evening. = 会议将持续到晚上。


虚拟语气:
If the river meandered less, it would be easier to navigate. = 如果河流不那么蜿蜒，航行就会更容易。
If the speech had not meandered so much, it would have been more effective. = 如果演讲没有那么冗长，效果会更好。


被动语态:
The path was meandered by the hikers. = 小路被远足者走得蜿蜒曲折。 (This usage is less common and often sounds unnatural)
The route had been meandered by previous explorers. = 这条路线已经被以前的探险家走得蜿蜒曲折。(This usage is less common and often sounds unnatural)


疑问句:
Does the river meander much in this area? = 这片区域的河流蜿蜒程度大吗？
Does the plot of the novel meander too much? = 这部小说的情节过于冗长吗？


否定句:
The road does not meander; it is straight. = 这条路不蜿蜒；它是笔直的。
The presentation did not meander; it was concise and to the point. = 演示文稿没有冗长；它简洁明了。
