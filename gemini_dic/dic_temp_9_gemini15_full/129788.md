non-flammable:/ˌnɒnˈflæməbəl/ | adj. |不易燃的，不燃的 | Not easily ignited or burning; resistant to fire.  | Example:  This material is non-flammable and safe for use near open flames.  这种材料不易燃，在明火附近使用安全。

The new curtains are non-flammable, ensuring safety in the house. 新窗帘不易燃，确保房屋安全。|近义词：fire-resistant, fireproof, incombustible |反义词：flammable, inflammable, combustible
