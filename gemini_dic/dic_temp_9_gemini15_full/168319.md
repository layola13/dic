pro-polish:/ˌproʊˈpɑːlɪʃ/| adj.|预先抛光;预先润饰的|  Relating to or denoting a process or treatment applied before the final polishing of something.  It suggests a preliminary stage of polishing or refinement aimed at improving the surface or quality before the final, high-quality finish.|例句：The pro-polish stage involved removing minor surface imperfections. 预先抛光阶段包括去除细微的表面缺陷。  The wood received a pro-polish before the final varnish was applied. 木材在涂上最后一道清漆之前进行了预先抛光。|近义词：preliminary polishing, pre-finishing|反义词：final polishing, finishing
