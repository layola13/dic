familiarize:/fəˈmɪliəraɪz/ | vt. | 使熟悉；使了解 | To make (someone) familiar with something.  | 例句：The training program is designed to familiarize employees with new software. 培训项目旨在让员工熟悉新软件。 | 例句：I need to familiarize myself with the city before I start my new job. 我需要在开始新工作之前熟悉一下这座城市。 | 近义词：acquaint, introduce, accustom | 反义词：estrange, alienate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I familiarize myself with the new software every day. = 我每天都熟悉新的软件。
She familiarizes her students with classic literature. = 她让她的学生熟悉古典文学。

一般过去时:
He familiarized himself with the city map before the trip. = 他在旅行前熟悉了城市地图。
They familiarized the audience with the historical context. = 他们让观众熟悉了历史背景。

现在完成时:
I have familiarized myself with the company's policies. = 我已经熟悉了公司的政策。
She has familiarized the children with the rules of the game. = 她已经让孩子们熟悉了游戏的规则。

过去完成时:
He had familiarized himself with the research before writing the report. = 他在写报告之前已经熟悉了研究内容。
They had familiarized the team with the project goals. = 他们已经让团队熟悉了项目目标。

将来时:
I will familiarize myself with the new system before Monday. = 我会在星期一前熟悉新系统。
She will familiarize the students with the experimental procedure. = 她会让学生熟悉实验程序。
