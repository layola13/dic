hedgepig:/ˈhɛdʒpɪɡ/| n. |刺猬|A small nocturnal mammal with a spiny covering.  Hedgehogs are insectivores known for their ability to curl into a ball for protection.|例句：The hedgehog curled into a tight ball when it felt threatened.  刺猬感到受到威胁时蜷缩成一团。  The children were fascinated by the tiny hedgehog in the garden. 孩子们对花园里的小刺猬很着迷。|近义词：hedgehog|反义词:None
