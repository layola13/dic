relooked:/ˌriːˈlʊkt/| v.|重新审视，再看一遍，重新考虑|To look at or consider something again.|例句：We relooked at the budget and found some savings. 我们重新审视了预算，发现了一些节省之处。

例句：After the initial review, the committee relooked at the proposal. 初步审查后，委员会重新审视了该提案。


近义词：review, reconsider, re-examine|反义词：ignore, overlook


词性：动词


USAGE->

They relook at the data every month. = 他们每个月都会重新审视数据。
We relook at our strategies regularly. = 我们定期重新审视我们的策略。

He relooked the contract before signing. = 他在签字之前重新审视了合同。
