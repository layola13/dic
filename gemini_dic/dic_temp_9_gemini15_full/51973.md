diadelphia:/ˌdaɪəˈdɛlfiə/| n. |双子体,合蕊 | A diadelphous stamen is one in which the filaments of the stamens are united into two bundles.  This is a common feature in plants of the family Fabaceae (legumes). |Example: Many pea flowers exhibit diadelphous stamens, with nine filaments fused together and one remaining free.  许多豌豆花都表现出双子体雄蕊，其中九个花丝融合在一起，一个保持自由。|近义词:  (No direct synonyms, as it describes a specific botanical structure) |反义词: polyadelphous (stamens united into many bundles), monadelphous (stamens united into one bundle) |名词
