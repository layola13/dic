good-timer:/ˈɡʊdˌtaɪmər/| n. |乐天派；会玩的人；善于交际的人 |A good-timer is someone who is fun to be around and enjoys socializing and having a good time. They are typically outgoing, positive, and easygoing.  |例句：He's a real good-timer; everyone enjoys being around him. 他是个十足的乐天派；大家都喜欢和他在一起。

例句：She's a great good-timer and always brings the party to life. 她是个很棒的会玩的人，总是能把派对气氛搞起来。|近义词：social butterfly, party animal, life of the party |反义词：loner, introvert, killjoy
