indigos:/ˈɪndɪɡoʊz/| n. |靛蓝(复数)|Indigos are a deep blue dye or pigment obtained from plants of the genus Indigofera.  The word refers to both the dye itself and the deep blue color it produces.|The artist used indigos to create a rich, vibrant blue in the painting.  艺术家用靛蓝在画中创造出一种浓郁、鲜艳的蓝色。|  blues, azure|  (无明显反义词，可考虑light colors)|名词
