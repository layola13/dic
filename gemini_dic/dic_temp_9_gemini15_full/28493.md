card:/kɑːrd/|ˈkɑːrd/| n.|卡片；纸牌；信用卡；明信片|A small piece of stiff paper or thin cardboard, typically rectangular, used for writing or printing messages, or for identification.|例句：I received a birthday card from my friend. 我收到朋友寄来的生日贺卡。|例句：He dealt the cards for the poker game. 他发牌开始玩扑克。|近义词：postcard, note, letter|反义词：


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sends cards to his family every year. 他每年都给家人寄贺卡。
She plays cards with her friends every week. 她每周都和朋友一起玩牌。


一般过去时:
I sent a postcard from my vacation. 我从度假的地方寄了一张明信片。
They played a game of cards last night. 他们昨晚玩了一局牌。


现在完成时:
I have received many birthday cards this year. 今年我已经收到了很多生日贺卡。
She has been playing cards for many years. 她玩牌已经很多年了。


过去完成时:
I had already sent the cards before he arrived.  在他到达之前我已经寄出卡片了。
They had played several rounds of cards before I joined them. 我加入他们之前，他们已经玩了几轮牌了。


将来时:
I will send you a Christmas card. 我会寄给你一张圣诞贺卡。
We will play cards after dinner. 晚饭后我们将玩牌。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would send you a card. 如果我有更多时间，我会给你寄张卡片。
I wish I had remembered to send a card. 我真希望我记得寄卡片。


被动语态:
The cards were dealt by the dealer. 牌是由发牌员发的。
The invitations were printed on thick cards. 请柬是印在厚卡片上的。


疑问句:
Do you have any playing cards? 你有扑克牌吗？
What kind of card is this? 这是什么牌？


否定句:
I didn't send a card this year. 今年我没有寄卡片。
He doesn't play cards often. 他不经常玩牌。
