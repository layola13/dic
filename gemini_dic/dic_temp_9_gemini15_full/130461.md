nonabstainer: /ˌnɒnæbˈsteɪnə(r)/ | n. | 不弃权者，不放弃者 | A person who does not abstain from something, typically voting or drinking alcohol.  | Example:  He was a nonabstainer in the recent election, casting his vote despite his reservations. 他在最近的选举中没有弃权，尽管心存疑虑，但他还是投了票。 | 近义词: voter (in the context of voting), drinker (in the context of alcohol) | 反义词: abstainer
