rhea:/ˈriːə/| n. |美洲鸵 |A large, flightless bird native to South America, similar to an ostrich but smaller.  Rheas are known for their long necks and legs, and their ability to run at high speeds.|Example: The rhea gracefully ran across the pampas.  美洲鸵优雅地穿过潘帕斯草原。|近义词：ostrich (but smaller)|反义词：None directly applicable.
