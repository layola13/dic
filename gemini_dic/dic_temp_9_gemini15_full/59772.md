economizing: /ˈɪkənəmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 节约；节省；精打细算 |  The act of using resources carefully and avoiding waste.  It involves being frugal and efficient in the use of money, time, materials, or energy. | 例句：They are economizing on fuel by cycling to work. = 他们骑自行车上班以节省燃油。

例句：The company is economizing by reducing its workforce. = 公司正在通过裁员来节省开支。


近义词：saving, conserving, thrifting, frugality


反义词：squandering, wasting, extravagance


词源:  Comes from the word "economy," which originates from the Ancient Greek word "οἰκονομία" (oikonomia), meaning "management of a household,"  combining "οἶκος" (oikos, "house") and "νόμος" (nomos, "law" or "custom").  The "-izing" suffix indicates the act of doing something.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is economizing on his expenses. = 他正在节省开支。
They are economizing on energy consumption. = 他们正在节约能源消耗。


一般过去时:
She economized on her food budget last month. = 她上个月节省了伙食预算。
We economized by buying used furniture. = 我们通过购买二手家具来节省开支。


现在完成时:
They have economized significantly since moving to a smaller house. = 搬到小房子后，他们已经节省了很多。
We have economized on water usage this summer. = 今年夏天我们节约了用水。


过去完成时:
They had economized for years before they could afford the trip. = 他们多年来一直省吃俭用，才能负担得起这次旅行。
She had economized on everything to pay for her son's education. = 她为了支付儿子的教育费用，在所有事情上都精打细算。


将来时:
We will be economizing on our holiday this year. = 今年我们的假期将节省开支。
They will economize by taking the bus instead of a taxi. = 他们将通过乘坐公交车而不是出租车来节省开支。


虚拟语气:
If we economized more, we could afford a new car. = 如果我们更节俭一些，我们就能买得起一辆新车。
I wish we had economized earlier. = 我真希望我们早点开始省钱。


被动语态:
Resources are being economized in the project. = 项目中正在节约资源。
Fuel consumption is being economized through new technologies. = 通过新技术正在节约燃油消耗。


疑问句:

否定句:
They are not economizing enough. = 他们节省得不够。
He did not economize on his spending. = 他没有节约开支。
