colonies:/ˈkɑːləniz/ | n. |殖民地；群体；菌落 |A colony is a country or area under the full or partial political control of another country, typically a distant one, and occupied by settlers from that country.  It can also refer to a group of people or organisms living together. |例句：The British Empire had many colonies across the globe. 英国帝国在全球拥有许多殖民地。

例句：A colony of ants built a nest under the patio. 一群蚂蚁在天井下筑巢。

近义词：settlement, dominion, outpost

反义词：mother country, independent nation
