chthonophagy:/ˈθɒnəfədʒi/ | n. |食土症，食土癖 |The consumption of earth or clay, especially by animals, sometimes as a nutritional supplement or to alleviate gastrointestinal distress.  This behavior is often observed in animals deficient in certain minerals.  In humans, it can be a symptom of various medical conditions.|Example:  The farmer observed chthonophagy in his goats, a sign of a mineral deficiency in their diet.  这位农民观察到他的山羊有食土症，这是它们饮食中缺乏矿物质的迹象。|近义词：geophagy, clay-eating|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms might describe behaviors opposite to self-medication, like avoiding minerals entirely.|
