drowsihead:/ˈdraʊziˌhed/| n. |瞌睡虫，嗜睡的人|A drowsyhead is someone who is very sleepy or lethargic.  It is an informal term. |例句：The drowsyhead missed the important announcement.  这个瞌睡虫错过了重要的通知。

例句：He was such a drowsyhead that he fell asleep during the movie. 他是个十足的瞌睡虫，电影中途就睡着了。


近义词：sleepyhead, sluggard, laggard |反义词：alert, vigilant |词性：noun
