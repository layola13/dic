charterist:/ˈtʃɑːrtərɪst/| n. |宪章运动支持者；特许经营者|A supporter of the Chartist movement, which advocated for political reforms in 19th-century Britain.  They pushed for universal male suffrage, secret ballots, and other democratic reforms.|Example: Many prominent figures of the time were charterists, advocating for the rights of the working class.  许多当时的知名人士都是宪章运动的支持者，为工人的权利大声疾呼。|近义词:reformer, democrat|反义词:reactionary, conservative|名词
