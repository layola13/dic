hoagies:/ˈhoʊɡiz/| n. |长面包三明治|A long, often Italian-style, submarine sandwich.  Hoagies are typically filled with meats, cheeses, vegetables, and sauces.| 例句：I ordered a hoagie with ham, provolone, and peppers.  我点了一个火腿、普罗沃洛干酪和辣椒的长面包三明治。

例句：The best hoagies in town are at Sal's Deli. 镇上最好的长面包三明治在萨尔的熟食店。


近义词：submarine sandwich, grinder, hero, Italian sub
