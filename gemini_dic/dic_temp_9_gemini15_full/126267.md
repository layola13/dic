myeloma:/ˌmaɪəˈloʊmə/| n. |骨髓瘤|A cancer that starts in plasma cells in the bone marrow.  Plasma cells are part of the immune system and help fight infection.  Myeloma occurs when these cells become cancerous and multiply uncontrollably, often leading to bone damage, kidney problems, and other complications.|Example:  The patient was diagnosed with multiple myeloma and is currently undergoing chemotherapy.  患者被诊断出患有多发性骨髓瘤，目前正在接受化疗。|近义词：plasma cell malignancy, plasmacytoma|反义词：None specifically, but terms implying absence of cancer could be considered opposite.
