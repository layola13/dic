reprodu:/ˈriːprədjuːs/ | v. | 再现；复制；繁殖 | To make or do something again; to create a copy of something; to produce offspring. | 例句：The experiment was designed to reproduce the conditions of a volcanic eruption.  实验旨在重现火山爆发的条件。


例句：The artist tried to reproduce the painting perfectly.艺术家试图完美地复制这幅画。


近义词：duplicate, replicate, copy, regenerate | 反义词：destroy, erase, annihilate | 词性:动词
