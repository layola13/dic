intercepting: /ˌɪntərˈsɛptɪŋ/ | v. |拦截；截取；打断|  The act of intercepting something, typically a message or object, before it reaches its intended destination.  It can also refer to interrupting a process or conversation. |

例句：

The police intercepted a shipment of illegal drugs. = 警方拦截了一批非法毒品。

The enemy intercepted our communication. = 敌人截获了我们的通讯。


近义词：blocking, seizing, interrupting, catching

反义词：delivering, transmitting, allowing, facilitating

词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are intercepting enemy transmissions. = 他们正在拦截敌人的通讯。
She intercepts emails intended for others. = 她拦截了发给其他人的电子邮件。

一般过去时:
The spy intercepted a secret message. = 间谍截获了一条秘密信息。
He intercepted the ball before it reached the goal. = 他在球到达球门前拦截了球。

现在完成时:
We have intercepted several suspicious packages. = 我们已经拦截了几个可疑包裹。
She has intercepted all incoming calls. = 她已经拦截了所有来电。

过去完成时:
They had intercepted the communication before the enemy could react. = 他们在敌人做出反应之前就截获了通讯。
The police had intercepted the suspect's car before he reached the border. = 警方在嫌疑人到达边境前拦截了他的汽车。

将来时:
We will intercept the missile before it hits its target. = 我们将在导弹击中目标前将其拦截。
They will intercept any attempts to smuggle drugs. = 他们将拦截任何走私毒品的企图。

虚拟语气:
If we could intercept the message, we would know their plan. = 如果我们能拦截到信息，我们就会知道他们的计划。
I wish we had intercepted the package before it was opened. = 我希望我们在包裹被打开前就拦截到它。

被动语态:
The message was intercepted by the enemy. = 这条信息被敌人拦截了。
The shipment is being intercepted by customs officials. = 这批货物正在被海关官员拦截。

疑问句:
Were the emails intercepted by a third party? = 邮件是被第三方拦截的吗？
Will the missile be intercepted successfully? = 导弹能成功拦截吗？

否定句:
They did not intercept the package. = 他们没有拦截到包裹。
We were unable to intercept the signal. = 我们无法拦截信号。
