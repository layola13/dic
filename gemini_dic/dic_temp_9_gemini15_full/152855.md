pedagoguery:/ˌpɛdəˈɡɑːɡəri/| n. |教育学说；教学方法；夸夸其谈的教育理论|The excessive use of pedagogical techniques, often characterized by showy displays of knowledge or skill rather than genuine effectiveness.  It often implies an overly theoretical or impractical approach to teaching.|例句：His lecture was full of pedagoguery, more concerned with impressing the audience than with truly educating them.  他的演讲充满了夸夸其谈的教育理论，更关注于给听众留下深刻印象而不是真正地教育他们。|近义词：didacticism, scholasticism, schoolmastering|反义词：practicality, effectiveness
