compiled:/kəmˈpaɪld/ | adj. v. | 已编译的；汇编的 |  Collected and arranged systematically; gathered from various sources.  |例句：The compiled code runs faster than the source code.  已编译的代码运行速度比源代码快。| The final report was compiled from data gathered over many years. 最终报告是根据多年收集的数据编制的。|近义词：assembled, collected, gathered | 反义词：disassembled, scattered, uncompiled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The program compiles successfully. = 程序编译成功。
She compiles data for her research. = 她为她的研究收集数据。


一般过去时:
He compiled a list of names. = 他编制了一个姓名列表。
The team compiled the report last week. = 团队上周编制了报告。


现在完成时:
I have compiled all the necessary documents. = 我已经收集了所有必要的文档。
She has compiled a comprehensive bibliography. = 她已经编制了一份全面的参考书目。


过去完成时:
They had compiled the data before the presentation. = 他们在演示文稿之前已经收集了数据。
He had compiled a detailed report by the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经编制了一份详细的报告。


将来时:
We will compile the results tomorrow. = 我们明天将整理结果。
