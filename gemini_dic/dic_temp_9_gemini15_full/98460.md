inspirers:/ɪnˈspaɪrərz/ | n. | 鼓舞者；激励者 | People or things that inspire others; sources of inspiration.  They provide motivation, encouragement, and ideas that lead to creative work or positive action. | Example:  Nelson Mandela was an inspirer to many fighting for social justice.  曼德拉是许多为社会正义而战的人的灵感来源。  The breathtaking scenery served as an inspirer for the artist's latest painting.  令人叹为观止的风景为艺术家的最新绘画提供了灵感。 |近义词: motivators, influencers, role models |反义词: discouragers, demotivators
