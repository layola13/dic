counterdemonstrating: /ˌkaʊntərˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 反示威，对抗性示威 |  Engaging in a demonstration or protest against another demonstration or protest.  It implies a direct and active opposition to the views or actions of the original demonstration. | 例句：The police were overwhelmed by the scale of the counterdemonstrating.  警方被大规模的反示威所压倒。
  
The far-right group organized a counterdemonstration to the anti-racism march. 极右翼团体组织了一场针对反种族主义游行的反示威活动。


近义词： counter-protesting, opposing, protesting against | 反义词：supporting, endorsing, participating in
