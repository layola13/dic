sideswipe:/ˈsaɪdswaɪp/| n. vt.|擦撞；轻微碰撞；侧面攻击；间接批评|A sideswipe is a glancing blow or collision, often unintentional.  It can also refer to an indirect or oblique attack, criticism, or remark.|例句：The car suffered a sideswipe during the accident. 这辆车在事故中发生轻微擦撞。|例句：His comment was a sideswipe at the government's policies. 他的评论是对政府政策的间接批评。|近义词：glancing blow, brush, graze, oblique attack, indirect criticism|反义词：direct attack, head-on collision|名词，动词
