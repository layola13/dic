over-cunningness: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌnɪŋnəs/ | n. | 过于狡猾；狡诈；奸诈 | Excessive shrewdness or craftiness; cunning to a degree that is excessive or harmful.  It implies a degree of deception or manipulation that goes beyond what is considered acceptable or wise. |

His over-cunningness led to his downfall.  他的过度狡猾导致了他的垮台。

The over-cunningness of the politician backfired, revealing his true intentions.  这位政治家的过度狡猾适得其反，暴露了他的真实意图。
