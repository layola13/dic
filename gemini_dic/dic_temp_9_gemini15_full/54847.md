displacer:/dɪsˈpleɪsər/ | n. | 排气器；置换器；位移器 | A device that displaces a fluid or gas, or an object that is displaced.  It can refer to a mechanical component,  a volume of fluid, or an object causing displacement. | The submarine used a water displacer to control its buoyancy.  潜艇使用排水器来控制其浮力。| The piston acts as a displacer in the engine. 活塞在发动机中充当排量器。 | 近义词：dislodger, remover | 反义词：fixer, installer


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The pump displaces a large volume of water. = 这台泵排出了大量的清水。
The object displaces the liquid in the container. = 物体取代了容器中的液体。


一般过去时:
The earthquake displaced thousands of people from their homes. = 地震使数千人流离失所。
The flood displaced the river's course. = 洪水改变了河道。


现在完成时:
The new technology has displaced many traditional methods. = 新技术已经取代了许多传统方法。
The rising sea level has displaced coastal communities. = 海平面上升已经导致沿海社区流离失所。


过去完成时:
The storm had displaced the ship before the rescue arrived. = 在救援到来之前，风暴已经使船舶偏离航向。
The company had already displaced several workers before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经解雇了几名工人。


将来时:
The project will displace a significant amount of soil. = 该项目将要移走大量的土壤。
Automation will likely displace many human jobs in the future. = 自动化很可能会在未来取代许多人力工作。


虚拟语气:
If the dam were to fail, it would displace a vast quantity of water. = 如果水坝垮塌，它将排放大量的清水。
I wish the new policy wouldn't displace so many people. = 我希望新政策不会导致这么多人流离失所。


被动语态:
The residents were displaced by the hurricane. = 居民们被飓风迫使离开家园。
Millions of liters of water were displaced by the submerged object. = 数百万升的水被沉没的物体取代。


疑问句:
What volume of fluid is displaced by the pump? = 这台泵排出了多少体积的液体？
How many people were displaced by the natural disaster? = 自然灾害导致多少人流离失所？


否定句:
The machine did not displace enough air to function properly. = 机器没有排出足够的空气以正常运行。
The new system did not displace the old one completely. = 新系统并没有完全取代旧系统。
