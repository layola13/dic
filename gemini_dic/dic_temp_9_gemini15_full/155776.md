phonographically:/ˌfoʊnəˈɡræfɪkli/ | adv. | 语音记录地；根据语音书写地 |  Describing something done by means of phonography (a system of writing that uses symbols to represent sounds).  It indicates that something is written or transcribed based on the sounds heard, rather than the spelling or conventional writing. | The lecture was phonographically recorded and later transcribed.  这次讲座进行了语音记录，之后又进行了转录。|  transcribed, recorded |  (no direct antonym, the opposite would be "orthographically" - based on spelling)| 副词
