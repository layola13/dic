grapheme:/ˈɡræfiːm/| n. |语素，音位文字|A grapheme is the smallest unit in a writing system that can represent a phoneme or a sequence of phonemes.  It's a letter or a combination of letters that represents a sound or a syllable. For example, the letter "a" is a grapheme, as is the combination "th".|The grapheme "ph" represents the /f/ sound in "phone".|近义词：letter, character, symbol|反义词：N/A
