例句：His immodesty in boasting about his achievements was off-putting. 他夸耀自己成就的行为非常不谦虚，令人反感。


近义词：shamelessness, indecency, impropriety, boldness, audacity


反义词：modesty, humility, decency, propriety, reserve


词性: 名词
