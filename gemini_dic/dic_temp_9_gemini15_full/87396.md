herrick:/ˈherɪk/| n. |赫里克（姓氏）|A surname of English origin.|The name Herrick is a surname of English origin.  It's believed to be a topographic name for someone who lived near a grove of hawthorn trees, derived from the Old English *haerig, meaning "hairy" or "rough," referring to the texture of the hawthorn bush.|Example: Robert Herrick was a famous English poet.  罗伯特·赫里克是一位著名的英国诗人。|近义词: (N/A - as a surname)|反义词: (N/A - as a surname)|noun
