precoce:/ˈpreɪkoʊs/| adj.|早熟的；早发的；过早出现的|Showing unusually early development or maturity, especially in mental abilities.  |例句：She was a precocious child, reading at age three. 她是个早熟的孩子，三岁就会阅读。|例句：The precocious blooming of the cherry trees surprised everyone.  樱花过早的盛开让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：advanced, gifted, talented, premature|反义词：backward, retarded, undeveloped
