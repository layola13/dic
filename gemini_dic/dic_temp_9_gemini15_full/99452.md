interests:/ˈɪntrəsts/ | n. |兴趣，爱好；利益；利息 | Things that a person enjoys doing or learning about;  the advantage or profit gained from something; money earned from investments. |例句：My interests include reading and hiking. 我的兴趣包括阅读和远足。  The company's interests are primarily in the energy sector.  这家公司的主要利益在于能源领域。|近义词：hobbies, passions, pursuits, advantages, profits |反义词：disinterests, disadvantages, losses |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your interests? = 你的兴趣是什么？
His interests lie in classical music. = 他的兴趣在于古典音乐。

一般过去时:
Her interests changed drastically after she moved to the city. = 她搬到城市后，兴趣发生了巨大的变化。
What were his interests in his youth? = 他年轻时的兴趣是什么？


现在完成时:
My interests have broadened considerably since I started university. = 自从我上大学以来，我的兴趣范围已经大大拓宽了。
Have your interests shifted recently? = 你最近的兴趣有转变吗？


过去完成时:
Before he retired, his interests had primarily been focused on his career. = 在他退休之前，他的兴趣主要集中在他的事业上。
She had never realized her interests in painting until she took a class. = 直到她上了课，她才意识到自己对绘画的兴趣。


将来时:
I believe my interests will evolve as I get older. = 我相信随着年龄的增长，我的兴趣会发生变化。
What do you think your interests will be in the future? = 你认为你未来的兴趣会是什么？


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pursue my interests in astronomy. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会追求我对天文学的兴趣。
I wish I had the resources to pursue all my interests. = 我希望我有资源来追求我所有的兴趣。


被动语态:
The company's interests are often overlooked in favor of short-term profits. = 公司的利益往往被为了短期利润而忽视。
His interests were damaged by the scandal. = 他的利益受到了丑闻的损害。


疑问句:
What interests you most about this project? = 你对这个项目最感兴趣的是什么？
Whose interests are we serving here? = 我们在这里为谁的利益服务？


否定句:
He has no interest in politics. = 他对政治没有兴趣。
The changes did not serve the interests of the workers. = 这些变化并没有符合工人的利益。



PHRASE->
in the interests of = 为了…的利益
vested interests =既得利益者
conflict of interests =利益冲突
best interests =最大利益
in someone's interests =对某人有利
