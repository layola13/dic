losses:/ˈlɒsɪz/| n.|损失，亏损|The plural form of "loss," referring to instances of losing something, such as money, possessions, or opportunities.  It can also refer to defeats or setbacks.|例句：The company reported heavy losses this quarter.  这家公司本季度报告了巨额亏损。|例句：The team suffered a series of losses in the tournament.  这支队伍在锦标赛中遭受了一系列的失败。|近义词：defeats, setbacks, damages, deficits|反义词：gains, profits, wins

一般现在时:
Our company experiences significant losses every year. = 我们公司每年都会遭受巨大的损失。
His investment strategy always results in losses. = 他的投资策略总是导致亏损。

一般过去时:
They suffered heavy losses during the economic downturn. = 他们在经济衰退期间遭受了巨大的损失。
The hurricane caused billions of dollars in losses. = 飓风造成了数十亿美元的损失。


现在完成时:
We have incurred significant losses due to the pandemic. = 由于疫情，我们已经遭受了巨大的损失。
The company has reported losses for three consecutive quarters. = 该公司连续三个季度亏损。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had already accumulated substantial losses. = 到年底，他们已经积累了大量的损失。
Before the merger, the company had experienced several years of losses. = 在合并之前，该公司已经经历了几年的亏损。


将来时:
We anticipate further losses if the market continues to decline. = 如果市场继续下跌，我们预计还会进一步亏损。
They predict significant losses in the coming financial year. = 他们预测在即将到来的财政年度将遭受重大损失。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested wisely, we wouldn't have suffered such heavy losses. = 如果我们投资明智的话，就不会遭受如此巨大的损失。
I wish we hadn't experienced these losses. = 我希望我们没有经历这些损失。


被动语态:
Significant losses were reported by the company. = 公司报告了巨额损失。
Many losses are attributed to poor management. = 许多损失归咎于管理不善。


疑问句:
What were the losses caused by the fire? = 火灾造成的损失是什么？
How many losses did the team endure this season? = 这个赛季球队遭受了多少次失败？


否定句:
The company did not experience any losses this year. = 公司今年没有遭受任何损失。
They did not report significant losses in their financial statements. = 他们在财务报表中没有报告重大损失。
