gonorrhoeal:/ˌɡɒnəˈriːəl/| adj.|淋病的；由淋病引起的| Relating to or caused by gonorrhoea.  The term describes conditions or symptoms resulting from gonorrhea infection. | Example:  He was diagnosed with gonorrhoeal infection. 他被诊断出患有淋病感染。  The patient presented with gonorrhoeal symptoms. 病人出现了淋病症状。|近义词: gonococcal|反义词:  None specifically, but words describing the absence of gonorrhea could be considered antonyms in context.
