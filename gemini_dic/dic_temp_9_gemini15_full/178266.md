recompress:/ˌriːkəmˈprɛs/ | vt. |重新压缩 | To compress something again, especially after it has been decompressed.  This usually refers to digital data or gases. |例句：The software automatically recompresses the files after editing. 软件会在编辑后自动重新压缩文件。|例句：The scuba diver recompressed after a rapid ascent. 潜水员在快速上升后进行了重新减压。|近义词：compress again, re-encode (for data) |反义词：decompress
