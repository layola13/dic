rowhouse: /ˈroʊhaʊs/ | n. |排屋，联排别墅 | A row house is a house that is one of a series of identical or similar houses joined together in a row.  They share one or more walls with adjacent houses. |  例句：We live in a charming row house in the historic district. 我们住在历史街区一栋迷人的排屋里。

例句：Many Victorian row houses line the street. 许多维多利亚风格的排屋排列在街道两旁。


近义词：terraced house, town house, attached house | 反义词：detached house, freestanding house | 词性：名词
