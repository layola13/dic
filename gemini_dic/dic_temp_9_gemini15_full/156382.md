physiotherapists:/ˌfɪzioʊˈθerəpɪsts/ | n. | 物理治疗师 |  A physiotherapist is a healthcare professional who helps people recover from illness or injury through physical methods.  They assess, diagnose, and treat movement disorders.  |  例句：Many physiotherapists work in hospitals, clinics, and private practices.  许多物理治疗师在医院、诊所和私人诊所工作。

例句：The physiotherapist designed a rehabilitation program to help her regain her mobility after the accident.  物理治疗师设计了一个康复计划，以帮助她在事故后恢复行动能力。


近义词：physical therapist, rehabilitation specialist | 反义词：N/A | 词性：n.
