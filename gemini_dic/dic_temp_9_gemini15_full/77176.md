gasoline:/ˈɡæsəliːn/ (美式)/ˈɡæsəliːn/ (英式)| n. |汽油|A flammable liquid obtained by refining petroleum, used as fuel for internal combustion engines.|例句：My car needs gasoline. 我的车需要汽油。|例句：The price of gasoline has increased recently. 最近汽油价格上涨了。|近义词：petrol (英式), gas (美式，非正式)|反义词：无
