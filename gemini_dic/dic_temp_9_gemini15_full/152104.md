pathogen:/ˈpæθədʒən/| n. |病原体，致病菌|A pathogen is a biological agent that causes disease or illness to its host.  This includes bacteria, viruses, fungi, prions, protozoa, and helminths. Pathogens can infect plants and animals. |Example:  The flu virus is a common pathogen. 流感病毒是一种常见的病原体。

The bacteria were identified as the pathogen responsible for the outbreak. 细菌被确定为导致疫情爆发的病原体。|近义词：germ, microbe, infectious agent |反义词：prophylactic (预防剂), innocuous (无害的)
