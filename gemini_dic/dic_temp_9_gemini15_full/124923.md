mudslides:/ˈmʌdslaɪdz/| n. |泥石流 |A mudslide is a mass of mud and other debris that flows down a slope, often after heavy rain or snowmelt.  Mudslides can be very destructive, burying homes, roads, and other infrastructure. |例句：The heavy rains triggered several mudslides in the mountain region.  暴雨引发了山区几处泥石流。

The recent mudslides caused significant damage to the town. 最近的泥石流给小镇造成了严重的破坏。|近义词：landslide, debris flow, mudflow |反义词：None.
