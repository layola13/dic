enquiry's:/ɪnˈkwaɪəriːz/| n. |询问；查询；探究|The possessive form of "enquiry," indicating belonging to or relating to an enquiry.  It refers to the questions, investigations, or requests for information associated with a specific inquiry. |Example: The enquiry's results were inconclusive.  （该次询问的结果并不确定。） The enquiry's conclusion was delayed. (调查的结论被推迟了).|近义词：investigation's, inquiry's, question's|反义词：answer's, solution's


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  It shows possession, not action.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.

PHRASE->
make an enquiry = 进行询问
lines of enquiry = 调查方向
subject of enquiry = 询问主题
the course of enquiry = 调查过程
a line of enquiry = 一种调查途径
