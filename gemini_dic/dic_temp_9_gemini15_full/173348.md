/ˈkwɑːzi dɪˈtɜːrmɪnz/|/ˈkwɑːzaɪ diˈtɜːmɪnz/ | v. | 准确定; 半决定 | Quasi-determines means to determine or define something almost completely, but not entirely.  It suggests a degree of uncertainty or incompleteness in the determination.  The word combines "quasi," meaning "seemingly," "apparently," or "to a certain extent," with "determines," meaning "to establish something definitely or exactly." | The initial conditions quasi-determine the system's future behavior.  初始条件几乎决定了系统的未来行为。

The initial parameters quasi-determine the outcome of the experiment. 初始参数几乎决定了实验的结果。


近义词: largely determines, mostly influences, nearly fixes,virtually sets

反义词:  has no bearing on, is unrelated to, is independent of

词性: Verb
