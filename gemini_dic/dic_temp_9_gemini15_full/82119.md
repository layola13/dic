grogs:/ɡrɒɡz/| n. |酒，朗姆酒；醉汉|A strong alcoholic drink, typically rum diluted with water.  The term can also refer to a person intoxicated by alcohol.|例句：He mixed a grog of rum and water. 他调制了一杯朗姆酒加水的混合酒。

例句：The old sailor was a grog, stumbling along the dock.  那个老水手喝醉了，在码头上踉踉跄跄地走着。


近义词：rum, liquor, booze


反义词：sober drink (as a contrast to the alcoholic drink aspect)


词性:名词
