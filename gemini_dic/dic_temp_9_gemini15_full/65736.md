everest:/ˈɛvərɪst/| n. |珠穆朗玛峰|The highest mountain in the world, located in the Mahālangūr Himāl sub-range of the Himalayas.  It is known as Chomolungma in Tibetan and Sagarmatha in Nepali.|例句：Mount Everest is the world's highest peak. 珠穆朗玛峰是世界最高峰。

例句：Climbers often face extreme weather conditions while attempting to ascend Everest.  攀登珠穆朗玛峰的登山者经常面临极端的天气状况。


近义词：Chomolungma, Sagarmatha |反义词：None|词性:名词
