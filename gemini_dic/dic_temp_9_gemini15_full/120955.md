mischanneling:/ˌmɪsˈtʃænəlɪŋ/ | v. | 误导；错导；使用不当 | The act of directing something (resources, efforts, etc.) in a wrong or ineffective way.  It implies a deviation from the intended or optimal course. |例句：The company's mischanneling of funds led to its bankruptcy. 公司资金使用不当导致其破产。

The government's mischanneling of resources hampered the relief efforts. 政府资源配置不当阻碍了救灾工作。


近义词：misdirecting, misapplying, diverting, misusing | 反义词：channeling, directing, guiding, optimizing | 词性：动词
