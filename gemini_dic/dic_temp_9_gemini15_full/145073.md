overanalyzes:/ˌoʊvərˈænəlˌaɪzɪz/| v.|过度分析|To analyze something excessively or to a degree that is unnecessary or unproductive. It implies going into too much detail or finding hidden meanings where there are none.|He overanalyzes every email he receives, looking for hidden meanings and subtext. 他过度分析他收到的每一封邮件，寻找隐藏的意义和潜台词。|analyze, scrutinize, dissect|accept, ignore, overlook|动词
