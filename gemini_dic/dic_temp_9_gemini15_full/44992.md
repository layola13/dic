crystallizing: /ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使结晶；使明确；使具体化 |  The process of forming crystals; to become or cause to become clear or definite.  | 例句：The solution is crystallizing slowly.  溶液正在缓慢结晶。

The sugar is crystallizing out of the syrup. 糖正在从糖浆中析出结晶。


近义词：solidifying, clarifying, defining, concretizing | 反义词：dissolving, blurring, obscuring, abstracting | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sugar is crystallizing. 糖正在结晶。

一般过去时:

词源:
crystallizing comes from the word "crystal," ultimately derived from the Greek word "krustallos," meaning "ice" or "rock crystal."  The suffix "-izing" indicates the process of making something into a crystal or becoming more defined and clear, like a crystal.
