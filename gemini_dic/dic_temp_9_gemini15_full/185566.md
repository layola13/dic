revealingly:/rɪˈviːlɪŋli/ | adv. |  意味深长地；露骨地；揭示性地 | In a way that reveals something previously unknown or hidden.  It suggests that something significant is being shown or implied, often unintentionally. |


例句：

He looked at her revealingly, a flicker of sadness in his eyes. 他意味深长地望着她，眼中闪过一丝悲伤。

She laughed revealingly, and her secret was out. 她意味深长地笑了，她的秘密就这样暴露了。


近义词：significantly, pointedly, tellingly, suggestively

反义词：unrevealingly, obscurely, subtly


词性: 副词
