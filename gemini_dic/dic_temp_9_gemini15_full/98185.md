insculption:/ɪnˈskʌlpʃən/| n. |铭刻；雕刻；碑文 |The inscription on the tombstone read "Beloved Wife."  墓碑上的铭文写着“亲爱的妻子”。|近义词：engraving, carving, inscription |反义词：erasure, deletion

一般现在时:
The inscription commemorates the founding of the city. = 这块铭文是为了纪念这座城市的建立。
This inscription describes the historical event. = 这段铭文描述了这一历史事件。

一般过去时:
The artist carefully inscribed the name on the plate. = 艺术家小心地在盘子上刻上了名字。
They inscribed a message on the time capsule. = 他们在时间胶囊上刻上了信息。

现在完成时:
Archaeologists have discovered many ancient inscriptions. = 考古学家们已经发现了许多古代铭文。
Researchers have deciphered the inscriptions on the ancient tablets. = 研究人员已经破译了古代石碑上的铭文。

过去完成时:

将来时:
