disclaimer:/dɪˈskleɪmər/| n. |免责声明|A statement that denies responsibility.  A disclaimer is a statement, often included in contracts, warnings, or product information, that limits or denies liability. It clarifies that the creator or provider of something is not responsible for any potential harm or damages that might arise from its use.  |Example: The website contains a disclaimer stating that it is not responsible for the accuracy of the information provided.  网站上有一份免责声明，声明其不对所提供信息的准确性负责。|近义词：denial, repudiation, disclaimer of liability|反义词：acceptance, endorsement, guarantee|名词
