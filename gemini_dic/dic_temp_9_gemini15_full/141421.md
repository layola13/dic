ophiurida:/ˌoʊfiˈjʊərɪdə/| n. |蛇尾纲|The Ophiurida are an order of brittle stars, echinoderms characterized by their slender, flexible arms that are sharply demarcated from a small, central disc.  They are found in a wide range of marine habitats. |Example:  The deep-sea expedition discovered several new species of ophiurids belonging to the order Ophiurida.|近义词:brittle stars|反义词:None specifically, but could be contrasted with other echinoderm classes like Asteroidea (sea stars).|名词
