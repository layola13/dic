quarry-tiles:/ˈkwɒri taɪlz/| n.| 采石场砖瓦|Quarry tiles are thick, durable, square tiles traditionally made from clay quarried from the earth. They are commonly used in areas with high foot traffic, such as kitchens and hallways, due to their resilience to wear and tear.  |例句：The kitchen floor was covered in red quarry tiles.|近义词：floor tiles, paving stones|反义词：N/A
