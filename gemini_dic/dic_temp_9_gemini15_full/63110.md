ensign:/ˈɛnsaɪn/| n. |少尉；旗帜；标记 |A junior commissioned officer in the navy, or an ensign's rank; a flag or banner, especially a national one; a sign or symbol. |例句：He was promoted to ensign after a year of service.  他服役一年后被晋升为少尉。
The American flag is a powerful ensign of freedom. 美国国旗是自由的有力象征。|近义词：flag, banner, standard, symbol |反义词：None specifically |词性：Noun
