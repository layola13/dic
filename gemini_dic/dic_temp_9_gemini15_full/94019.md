impanelled:/ɪmˈpænld/| v.| 挑选陪审员；组成陪审团 | To select and swear in a jury.  | The judge impanelled a jury of twelve. 法官挑选了一个由十二人组成的陪审团。|近义词：select, choose, summon|反义词：dismiss
