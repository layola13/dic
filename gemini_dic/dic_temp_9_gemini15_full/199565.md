singleton:/ˈsɪŋɡltən/| n. |单例，单件，单身人士|A singleton is a class that can have only one instance (object) throughout the lifetime of the application.  In other contexts, it refers to a single, unique item or a person who is not married or in a relationship.|例句：In our design pattern, we utilize a singleton to manage database connections. 在我们的设计模式中，我们使用单例来管理数据库连接。


例句：She's a singleton, preferring her independent life to a committed relationship. 她是个单身人士，更喜欢独立生活而不是稳定的关系。


近义词：single, unique instance, sole example|反义词：multiple, plural, many
