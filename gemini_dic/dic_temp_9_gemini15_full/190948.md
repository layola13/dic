scalaria:/skəˈlɛəriə/| n. |旋梯螺，海螺 | A genus of marine gastropod mollusks in the family Scalariidae, characterized by their tall, slender shells with numerous whorls and prominent axial ribs.  They are often found in shallow waters. | Example: The diver spotted a beautiful scalaria clinging to a rock.  潜水员发现一只美丽的旋梯螺粘在岩石上。 |近义词：wentletrap (a common name for some species)|反义词：N/A
