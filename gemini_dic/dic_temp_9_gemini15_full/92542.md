hyrax:/ˈhaɪræks/ | n. |蹄兔|A small, herbivorous mammal of the order Hyracoidea, resembling a rodent but more closely related to elephants. They are found in Africa and the Middle East.  |例句：The hyrax sunned itself on the rock.  蹄兔在岩石上晒太阳。 |例句：We saw a family of hyraxes near the watering hole. 我们在水坑附近看到了一群蹄兔。|近义词：dassie (in South Africa)|反义词：无
