handlers:/ˈhændlərz/ | n. | 处理程序；操作者；经手人 |  A handler is a person or thing that handles something, such as a computer program that processes events or a person who manages a specific task or situation.  | Example 1: The software includes several event handlers to manage user input.  软件包含多个事件处理程序来管理用户输入。 Example 2: The flight attendants acted as handlers for the passengers during the emergency.  空乘人员在紧急情况下充当乘客的处理人员。|近义词：processors, managers, agents, operators | 反义词：inert, inactive
