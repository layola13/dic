magnates:/ˈmæɡneɪts/| n.|巨头；大亨；磁铁|A person of great influence and power in a particular industry or field.  They are often very wealthy and successful.|例句：The oil magnates met to discuss prices. 石油巨头们会面讨论价格。

例句：Several media magnates attended the conference.  好几名媒体大亨出席了会议。


近义词：tycoon, mogul, baron, leader


反义词：nobody, insignificant person


词性:名词

词源:源自于拉丁语词根"magnus,"意思是"great"或者"large," 指的是具有强大力量或影响力的人。  The connection to magnets comes from the powerful attraction associated with both magnets and influential figures.

过去完成时:
