hypertoxicities:/ˌhaɪpərˌtɒksɪsɪtiz/ | n. |高毒性；过度毒性 |  The study of hypertoxicities focuses on the excessive toxicity caused by substances exceeding safe levels.  This includes investigating the mechanisms, symptoms, and treatments for such conditions.  Hypertoxicities can manifest in various ways, depending on the substance and the level of exposure. |  The patient experienced severe hypertoxicities after an overdose of the medication.  病人服药过量后出现了严重的过度毒性反应。|  Toxicity, Intoxication |  Non-toxicity, Hypotoxicity | 名词
