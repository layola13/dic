meteors:/ˈmiːtiɔːrz/| n. |流星，陨星|A meteor is a streak of light in the sky caused by a meteoroid entering the atmosphere.  Meteors are often called "shooting stars".|Example:  We saw several bright meteors during the meteor shower.  我们在那场流星雨中看到了几颗明亮的流星。


Example: The Perseid meteor shower is a spectacular celestial event.  英仙座流星雨是一个壮观的宇宙事件。


近义词：shooting star, meteoroid (before entering atmosphere)


反义词：无


词性：名词
