debates:/dɪˈbeɪts/ | n. v. | 辩论，讨论 | A formal discussion on a particular matter in a public meeting or legislative assembly.  The act of debating. |例句：The political debates are heated this year. 今年的政治辩论非常激烈。

The students engaged in lively debates about the novel. 学生们就这部小说进行了热烈的讨论。


近义词：discussions, arguments, disputations | 反义词：agreements, accords, consents | 词性：n. v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They debate the issue every week. = 他们每周都辩论这个问题。
He debates the topic with great passion. = 他充满热情地辩论这个话题。


一般过去时:
We debated the merits of the proposal. = 我们讨论了这项提案的优缺点。
They debated the topic for hours. = 他们就这个话题辩论了好几个小时。


现在完成时:
They have debated this topic many times. = 他们已经多次讨论过这个话题。
She has debated professionally for years. = 她多年来一直从事专业的辩论。


过去完成时:
They had debated the issue before the vote. = 他们在投票前已经讨论过这个问题。
We had debated the plan thoroughly before presenting it. = 我们在提交计划之前已经彻底讨论过它。


将来时:
They will debate the bill next week. = 他们下周将对该法案进行辩论。
We will debate the merits of the new policy. = 我们将讨论这项新政策的优缺点。


虚拟语气:
If they debated more calmly, they might reach an agreement. = 如果他们更冷静地辩论，他们可能会达成一致。
I wish they had debated the issue more thoroughly before making a decision. = 我希望他们在做决定之前能更彻底地讨论这个问题。


被动语态:
The issue was debated extensively. = 这个问题被广泛讨论。
The proposal is being debated in parliament. = 该提案正在议会中进行辩论。


疑问句:
Are they debating the new law? = 他们正在讨论新法律吗？
Will they debate the topic openly? = 他们会公开讨论这个话题吗？


否定句:
They did not debate the matter seriously. = 他们没有认真讨论这件事。
The students did not debate the issues effectively. = 学生们没有有效地辩论这些问题。


PHRASE->
engage in debates = 参与辩论

academic debates = 学术辩论


词源:
The word "debate" comes from the Old French word "debat," meaning "strife, combat, struggle."  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "battuere," meaning "to beat" or "to fight."  The modern sense of "formal discussion" evolved over time.
