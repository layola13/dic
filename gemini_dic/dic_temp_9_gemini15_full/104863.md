keen-about:/kiːn əˈbaʊt/| adj. |热衷于，对…很感兴趣|Enthusiastic or eager about something.  It expresses a strong liking or interest.|例句：She's keen about collecting stamps. 她热衷于集邮。
He's really keen about his new job. 他对他的新工作非常感兴趣。|近义词：enthusiastic, eager, passionate, fond of |反义词：indifferent to, apathetic towards|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are keen about travelling. = 他们热衷于旅行。
I am keen about learning new languages. = 我热衷于学习新语言。


一般过去时:
She was keen about photography in her youth. = 她年轻时热衷于摄影。
He was keen about joining the football team. = 他渴望加入足球队。


现在完成时:
We have been keen about this project since its inception. = 自从这个项目开始以来，我们就一直很热衷于它。
They have been keen about environmental protection for years. = 他们多年来一直热衷于环境保护。


过去完成时:
She had been keen about painting before she became a writer. = 在她成为作家之前，她热衷于绘画。
He had been keen about music since he was a child. = 他从小就热衷于音乐。


将来时:
I will be keen about pursuing a master's degree next year. = 明年我将热衷于攻读硕士学位。
They will be keen about exploring the Amazon rainforest. = 他们将热衷于探索亚马逊雨林。


虚拟语气:
If I were keen about cooking, I would open a restaurant. = 如果我热衷于烹饪，我会开一家餐馆。
I wish I were keen about exercising more. = 我希望我更热衷于锻炼。


被动语态: (This phrase is rarely used passively)  The activity was not something that many people were keen about. 这项活动并不是很多人热衷的。
The new policy was something people were not keen about. 人们并不热衷于这项新政策。



疑问句:
Are you keen about going to the concert? = 你热衷于去音乐会吗？
Is he keen about trying that new restaurant? = 他热衷于尝试那家新餐馆吗？


否定句:
I'm not keen about spicy food. = 我不热衷于辛辣食物。
She wasn't keen about the idea at first. = 她一开始并不热衷于这个主意。


PHRASE->
keen about + 名词/动名词: keen about sports, keen about gardening, keen about learning, keen about photography, keen about helping others
