semihysterical: /ˌsɛmihaɪˈstɛrɪkəl/ | adj. |近乎歇斯底里的；半歇斯底里的 |  Approaching or characterized by hysteria; partly hysterical.  It suggests a state of heightened emotion, anxiety, or excitement that is less extreme than full-blown hysteria but still significantly disruptive. |

例句：

She became semihysterical when she heard the news. = 她听到这个消息后变得近乎歇斯底里。

His semihysterical laughter was unsettling to everyone present. = 他近乎歇斯底里的笑声让在场的所有人都感到不安。


近义词：frantic, agitated, distraught, hysterical (but less extreme)

反义词：calm, composed, tranquil, serene


词性：形容词
