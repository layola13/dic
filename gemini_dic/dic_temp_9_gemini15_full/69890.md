fertilise: /ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪz/ /ˈfɜːrtlʌɪz/ | vt. |施肥；使受精；使肥沃 | To make (land) more fertile; to make (an egg or ovum) capable of developing into a new organism. |例句：Farmers fertilise their fields to increase crop yields.  农民给田地施肥以提高作物产量。


The farmer fertilised the soil with organic compost. 这位农民用有机堆肥给土壤施肥。

近义词：impregnate, inseminate, enrich, manure


反义词：sterilize, deplete


词性:动词
