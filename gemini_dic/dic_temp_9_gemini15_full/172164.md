pursuances:/pərˈsuːənsɪz/| n. |追求；实行；从事；遵循|The act of pursuing something; the carrying out of something; the following of something.  It often refers to a course of action or a series of actions taken to achieve a goal.|例句：His pursuits in art led him to open his own gallery. 他的艺术追求让他开设了自己的画廊。

例句：The company's pursuances in new technology resulted in a revolutionary product. 这家公司在新技术的追求中取得了革命性的产品。


近义词：pursuits, endeavors, undertakings, efforts|反义词：neglect, abandonment, disregard|词性:noun
