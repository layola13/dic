nonjournalistic: /ˌnɒnˌdʒɜːrnəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非新闻性的；非新闻风格的 | Not characteristic of or suitable for journalism; lacking the style or objectivity of journalistic writing.  |  The professor's writing style, while detailed and informative, was considered nonjournalistic. 教授的写作风格虽然详尽且信息丰富，却被认为缺乏新闻性。  |近义词: unjournalistic, unprofessional, academic | 反义词: journalistic, newsworthy
