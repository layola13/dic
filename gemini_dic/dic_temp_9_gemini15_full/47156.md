dawdlers:/ˈdɔːdlərz/| n. |磨蹭的人，慢吞吞的人 |People who dawdle are slow and waste time. They delay or are slow in acting or doing things.  |Example: The dawdlers held up the entire group. 磨蹭的人耽误了整个团队。  The teacher warned the dawdlers to hurry up.老师警告磨蹭的学生快点。|近义词：procrastinators, lingerers, dillydalliers |反义词：hurriers, speedsters
