chromatography:/krəʊməˈtɒɡrəfi/| n. |色谱法，层析法 |The separation of a mixture by passing it in solution or suspension or as a vapor (as in gas chromatography) through a medium in which the components move at different rates.|Chromatography is a powerful technique used to separate and identify the components of a mixture. 色谱法是一种强大的技术，用于分离和鉴定混合物的成分。| electrophoresis, fractionation |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
