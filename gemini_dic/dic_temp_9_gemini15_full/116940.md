maze:/meɪz/| n. |迷宫；混乱；困惑 | A maze is a network of paths and hedges designed to confuse people.  迷宫是由路径和树篱组成的网络，旨在迷惑人们。 The city streets formed a confusing maze. 城市街道形成了一个令人困惑的迷宫。 | labyrinth, network | order, clarity |名词
