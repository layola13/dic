nonguarantees:/ˌnɒnˌɡærənˈtiːz/| n. |无保证；不保证|This term refers to a situation where no guarantees are provided. It indicates a lack of assurance or certainty about an outcome or a promise.  The word is often used in the context of  financial transactions, contracts, or promises where no liability is assumed. |Example:  The investment carries nonguarantees of profit. 这项投资没有获利的保证。The company offers services with nonguarantees of success.该公司提供的服务没有成功的保证。|近义词：uncertainties, assurances, risks |反义词：guarantees, assurances, certainties |名词
