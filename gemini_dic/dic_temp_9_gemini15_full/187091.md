riveting:/ˈrɪvɪtɪŋ/| adj.|扣人心弦的，极度吸引人的|extremely interesting and exciting; captivating.|The movie was so riveting that I couldn't take my eyes off the screen.  这部电影太扣人心弦了，我无法移开视线。|engrossing, captivating, fascinating, gripping|boring, dull, uninteresting|形容词


USAGE->

The plot is riveting. = 情节扣人心弦。
This book is riveting. = 这本书非常吸引人。
