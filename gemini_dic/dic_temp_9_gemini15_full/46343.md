dacron:/ˈdeɪkrɒn/| n. |涤纶，达克纶|A brand name for a type of polyester fiber, known for its strength, wrinkle resistance, and water repellency.  It's often used in clothing and other textiles.|例句：The shirt is made of dacron, so it's very wrinkle-resistant. 这件衬衫是用涤纶做的，所以非常不容易起皱。|例句：Her dress, a shimmering dacron creation, caught the light beautifully. 她的裙子，一件闪闪发光的涤纶制品，漂亮地捕捉到了光线。|近义词：polyester, terylene|反义词：cotton (in terms of material properties)|名词
