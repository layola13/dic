pyogenic:/paɪəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|化脓性的，生脓的|Producing or caused by pus.  Pyogenic infections are characterized by the formation of pus, a thick, yellowish-white fluid containing white blood cells, dead tissue, and bacteria.|例句：The wound became infected and pyogenic.  伤口感染并化脓了。|例句：The bacteria are known to cause pyogenic infections. 这种细菌已知会引起化脓性感染。|近义词：purulent, suppurative|反义词：aseptic, sterile|词性：形容词
