inputting: /ɪnˈpʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 输入，录入 | The act of entering data or information into a computer system or other device.  | Example: Inputting the data took several hours.  输入数据花了几个小时。 |近义词:entering, feeding, keying, inserting | 反义词:outputting, extracting

She inputs customer information daily. 她每天输入客户信息。

I inputted the address incorrectly. 我输入了错误的地址。
