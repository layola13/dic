forgetter: /fərˈɡɛtər/ | n. | 健忘的人，遗忘者 | A person who frequently forgets things.  | He's a bit of a forgetter; he constantly misplaces his keys. 他有点健忘；他经常找不到他的钥匙。 | absent-minded person, scatterbrain |  rememberer (反义词并非完全对立，而是指习惯性记性好的人)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a forgetter. = 他是一个健忘的人。
She is often a forgetter when it comes to appointments. =说到约会，她经常很健忘。


一般过去时:
He was a forgetter in his youth. = 他年轻时很健忘。
She was a forgetter, always losing her glasses. = 她很健忘，总是丢眼镜。


现在完成时:
He has been a forgetter all his life. = 他一辈子都很健忘。
She has become a forgetter since her accident. = 自从她出了事故后，她就变得健忘起来。


过去完成时:
He had been a forgetter for years before he got diagnosed. = 在他被诊断出来之前，他已经健忘多年了。
She had been a forgetter, so she wrote everything down. = 她以前很健忘，所以她把一切都写下来了。


将来时:
He will be a forgetter even in old age. = 他即使到了老年也会健忘。
She will likely be a forgetter as she gets older. = 她随着年龄的增长可能会变得健忘。


虚拟语气:
If he were a forgetter, he would need a reminder app. = 如果他健忘，他需要一个提醒应用程序。
If she were less of a forgetter, she would be more organized. = 如果她不那么健忘，她就会更有条理。


被动语态:
(This word doesn't typically take passive voice)  被动语态不适用


疑问句:
Is he a forgetter? = 他健忘吗？
Is she a forgetter, too? = 她也健忘吗？


否定句:
He isn't a forgetter; he just has a lot on his mind. = 他并不健忘；他只是有很多事情要操心。
She isn't always a forgetter; she's just very busy. = 她并不总是健忘；她只是非常忙碌。


PHRASE->
