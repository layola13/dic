evangelicals:/ɪˌvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/| adj. n.|福音派的；福音派信徒|Referring to or denoting a movement within Protestant Christianity that emphasizes the experience of conversion and the authority of the Bible.  Evangelicals often focus on the importance of personal salvation through faith in Jesus Christ and the spreading of the Gospel.|例句：Many evangelical churches are actively involved in missionary work.  许多福音派教会积极参与传教工作。  The evangelical movement has had a significant impact on American politics. 福音派运动对美国政治产生了重大影响。|近义词：Protestant, born-again, Pentecostal|反义词：secular, non-religious
