disallowing:/ˌdɪsəˈlaʊɪŋ/ | v. | 不允许；禁止 | The act of refusing to allow something; prohibiting.  |例句：The new rules are disallowing the use of personal devices during work hours.  新的规章禁止在工作时间使用个人设备。|例句：The court disallowing his appeal left him feeling defeated. 法院驳回他的上诉使他感到沮丧。|近义词：prohibiting, forbidding, preventing |反义词：allowing, permitting |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disallows smoking on the premises. = 公司不允许在公司内吸烟。
They disallow any form of discrimination. = 他们不允许任何形式的歧视。


一般过去时:
The teacher disallowed the use of calculators during the exam. = 老师在考试期间不允许使用计算器。
The judge disallowed the evidence as inadmissible. = 法官认定证据不可采纳而将其驳回。


现在完成时:
The government has disallowed the import of certain goods. = 政府已禁止进口某些商品。
The school has disallowed students from using social media during class. = 学校已禁止学生在课堂上使用社交媒体。


过去完成时:
They had disallowed the project due to budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，他们之前已否决了该项目。
The referee had disallowed the goal for offside. = 裁判之前因越位而判罚无效进球。


将来时:
The authorities will disallow unauthorized access to the building. = 当局将不允许未经授权进入该建筑物。
They will disallow any attempt to interfere with the investigation. = 他们将不允许任何企图干预调查的行为。


虚拟语气:
If they disallowed the proposal, we would have to reconsider our strategy. = 如果他们否决了该提案，我们就必须重新考虑我们的策略。
I wish they disallowed those loud noises at night. = 我希望他们能禁止夜间那些吵闹的声音。


被动语态:
The application was disallowed due to incomplete information. = 由于信息不完整，该申请被拒绝。
The request was disallowed by the board of directors. = 该请求被董事会否决。


疑问句:
Does the rule disallow the use of outside resources? = 规则是否不允许使用外部资源？
Will they disallow our participation in the competition? = 他们会不允许我们参加比赛吗？


否定句:
The policy doesn't disallow the use of personal laptops. = 该政策并不禁止使用个人笔记本电脑。
They didn't disallow the suggestion; they simply ignored it. = 他们并没有否决这个建议；他们只是忽略了它。
