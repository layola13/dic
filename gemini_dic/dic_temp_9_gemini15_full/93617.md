imbibed:/ɪmˈbɪbd/| v.| 吸收；饮用；接受（思想或感情）| to absorb or drink in (a liquid) or to take in (ideas or information)|The dry soil imbibed the rainwater. 干燥的土壤吸收了雨水。 He imbibed the wisdom of his elders. 他吸收了长者的智慧。|近义词：absorb, soak up, assimilate|反义词：expel, reject|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imbibes knowledge readily. = 他很容易吸收知识。
She imbibes a glass of wine every evening. = 她每天晚上喝一杯葡萄酒。

一般过去时:
They imbibed the local culture during their travels. = 他们在旅行中吸收了当地文化。
I imbibed a large quantity of water after the run. = 跑步后我喝了大量的水。

现在完成时:
She has imbibed the principles of non-violence. = 她已经吸收了非暴力的原则。
He has imbibed too much alcohol and is feeling unwell. = 他喝了太多酒，感觉不舒服。

过去完成时:
They had imbibed several beers before the argument started. = 争吵开始前他们已经喝了几瓶啤酒。
She had imbibed the teachings of her mentor for many years. = 她多年来一直学习她导师的教诲。

将来时:
He will imbibe the wisdom of the ages. = 他将吸收各个时代的智慧。
They will imbibe deeply from the local culture. = 他们将深入体验当地文化。

虚拟语气:
If I imbibed less caffeine, I would sleep better. = 如果我少喝咖啡因，我会睡得更好。
I wish I had imbibed more knowledge in my youth. = 我希望我年轻时吸收了更多知识。

被动语态:
The information was imbibed by the students. = 学生们吸收了这些信息。
The sponge imbibed the spilled liquid. = 海绵吸收了溢出的液体。

疑问句:
Did he imbibe the meaning of the lecture? = 他理解了讲座的意思吗？
What did she imbibe during her trip to India? = 她去印度期间吸收了什么？

否定句:
He did not imbibe the warning about the storm. = 他没有吸收关于暴风雨的警告。
She did not imbibe any alcohol at the party. = 她在派对上没有喝酒。
