rules:/ruːlz/ | /ruːls/| n. |规则，规章，条例 |Rules govern our behavior. 规则支配着我们的行为。  The rules of the game are simple. 游戏规则很简单。|近义词：regulations, principles, guidelines |反义词：chaos, disorder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These rules apply to everyone. = 这些规则适用于所有人。
What are the rules of this game? = 这个游戏的规则是什么？


一般过去时:
The old rules were abolished. = 旧的规则被废除了。
They followed the rules yesterday. = 他们昨天遵守了规则。


现在完成时:
We have broken several rules. = 我们已经违反了几条规则。
Have you read the rules carefully? = 你仔细阅读过规则了吗？


过去完成时:
They had already learned the rules before the game started. = 游戏开始前他们已经学会了规则。
I had never broken the rules before. = 我以前从未违反过规则。


将来时:
The new rules will be implemented next month. = 新规则将于下个月实施。
Will these rules change in the future? = 这些规则将来会改变吗？


虚拟语气:
If there were no rules, society would collapse. = 如果没有规则，社会就会崩溃。
I wish these rules were simpler. = 我希望这些规则更简单一些。


被动语态:
The rules are enforced by the police. = 这些规则由警方执行。
The rules were changed without our consent. = 规则在我们不知情的情况下被更改了。


疑问句:
Are there any rules we should know? = 我们应该知道哪些规则？
What are the rules for parking here? = 这里停车的规则是什么？


否定句:
