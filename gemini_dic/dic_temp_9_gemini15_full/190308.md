These bacteria are known for their ability to form these characteristic packets.  The term is also used more generally to refer to any bundle or package of things. |  The microbiologist observed sarcinae under the microscope.  显微镜下，微生物学家观察到了成束排列的球菌。|近义词: packet, bundle, cluster |反义词: single, solitary | 名词
