misperceive:/ˌmɪspərˈsiːv/| vt.|误解，曲解|To understand or interpret something incorrectly.|He misperceived her silence as agreement. 他误解了她的沉默为同意。|近义词：misinterpret, misunderstand|反义词：understand, comprehend|动词


USAGE->

They misperceive my intentions. = 他们误解了我的意图。


一般过去时:

She misperceived his actions. = 她误解了他的行为。

They have misperceived the risks involved. = 他们误解了所涉及的风险。


过去完成时:
