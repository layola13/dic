ezekiel:/ɪˈziːkiəl/ | n. |以西结；以西结书 | The name Ezekiel comes from the Hebrew name יְחֶזְקֵאל (Yəḥezqēl), meaning "God strengthens" or "God will strengthen". It refers to both the biblical prophet and the book of the Bible named after him.  The book of Ezekiel is a significant part of the Hebrew Bible and the Old Testament, known for its prophetic visions and symbolic language. | Example: The prophet Ezekiel delivered powerful messages from God. 以西结先知传达了来自上帝的有力信息。 |近义词: prophet (先知) |反义词: N/A
