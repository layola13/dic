calcification:/ˌkæl.sɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/| n.|钙化；钙质沉着|The process of depositing calcium salts in tissues.  This can be a normal physiological process in some tissues, or it can be an abnormal process associated with disease.  For example, calcification of arteries is a common sign of atherosclerosis. |例句：The X-ray showed significant calcification in his arteries. X光显示他的动脉有明显的钙化。

例句：The doctor suspected calcification after observing the patient's stiff joints.  医生在观察到患者关节僵硬后怀疑是钙化。


近义词：petrification, ossification|反义词：decalcification
