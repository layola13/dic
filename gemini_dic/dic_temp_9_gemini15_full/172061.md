purlieu:/ˈpɜːrluː/| n. |近郊；边缘地区；势力范围；活动范围|The area immediately surrounding a particular place, especially a place of authority or power; the environs or outskirts; a neighborhood or district.  |例句：The purlieus of the castle were patrolled by guards. 城堡周围地区由卫兵巡逻。

例句：The artist found inspiration in the purlieus of the city.  艺术家在城市的边缘地带找到了灵感。


近义词：vicinity, environs, outskirts, neighborhood, precincts


反义词：center, core, heart


词性:名词
