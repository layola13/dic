microfibrillar:/ˌmaɪkroʊfaɪˈbrɪlər/| adj. |微纤丝的；微纤维的 | Relating to or consisting of microfibrils.  Microfibrils are extremely fine fibers, often found in biological materials. The term describes the structure or composition of something containing these tiny fibers. | Example: The microfibrillar structure of cellulose contributes to its strength.  微纤丝的纤维素结构使其具有强度。|近义词：fibrillar, fine-fibred|反义词：macroscopic, coarse-fibred|
