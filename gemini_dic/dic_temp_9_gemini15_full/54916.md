disposedly:/dɪˈspoʊzɪdli/ | adv. | 轻易地；乐意地；欣然地 | readily; willingly; cheerfully |She disposedly agreed to help. 她欣然同意帮忙。|近义词：willingly, readily, cheerfully |反义词：reluctantly, unwillingly, grudgingly |副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb,  so it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  However, it can be used in various sentence structures.

Simple Sentence:
He disposedly offered his assistance. 他欣然提供帮助。

Complex Sentence:
Because she was disposedly inclined, she volunteered for the extra work, even though it was challenging. 因为她乐意去做，所以她自愿承担了额外的工作，即使那项工作很有挑战性。


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases specifically using "disposedly."  Its usage is more straightforward and integrated within sentences.  Phrases using synonyms like "willingly" or "readily" could be considered analogous.  For example:

willingly helped: 乐意帮忙
readily available: 随时可用
cheerfully accepted: 开心地接受
readily agreed: 欣然同意
happily complied: 开心地遵从
