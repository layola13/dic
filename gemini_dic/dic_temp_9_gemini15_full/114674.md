Malignances can refer to harmful actions or a state of being harmful.  In a medical context, it specifically refers to the cancerous nature of a tumor. |例句：The malignances of the dictator led to the suffering of his people. 独裁者的恶行导致人民的痛苦。

The doctor discovered several malignances during the biopsy.  医生在活检中发现了几处恶性肿瘤。|近义词：wickedness, evil, malevolence, villainy, malignancies (in a medical context: cancers, tumors)|反义词：benevolence, kindness, goodness, benignity (in a medical context: benign)
