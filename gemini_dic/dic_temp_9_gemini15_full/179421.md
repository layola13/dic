reducent:/ˈriːdjuːsnt/ | n. |还原剂，还原物 |A substance that reduces another substance by donating electrons;  a reducing agent.  It is often used in chemical reactions to cause reduction, which is the gain of electrons by an atom, ion, or molecule. |例句：Sodium borohydride is a common reducent used in organic chemistry.  硼氢化钠是有机化学中常用的还原剂。

例句：The experiment involved using a strong reducent to convert the oxidized compound.  该实验使用了强还原剂来转化氧化合物。


近义词：reducing agent, reductant

反义词：oxidant, oxidizing agent


词性:名词
