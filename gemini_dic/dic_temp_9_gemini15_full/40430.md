contrariously:/kənˈtreərili/| adv.|相反地；矛盾地；倔强地 | In a contrary manner; oppositely; perversely.  It describes actions or statements that are opposite to what is expected or usual. | 例句：Contrariously, he decided to quit his job just when he was about to get a promotion.  相反地，他就在即将升职的时候决定辞职了。


例句：The evidence, contrariously, pointed to his innocence.  证据却相反地指向了他的清白。


近义词：conversely, conversely, on the contrary, in contrast, paradoxically


反义词：consistently, accordingly, correspondingly


词性:副词
