preutilize:/ˌpriːˈjuːtɪlaɪz/ | v. |预先利用，提前使用 |To use something before its intended or usual time; to make use of something in advance.  |例句：The company preutilized its resources to gain a competitive advantage.  公司预先利用其资源以获得竞争优势。
The team preutilized the testing equipment to ensure everything was working properly before the official launch. 团队提前使用了测试设备，以确保在正式启动之前一切正常运行。|近义词：pre-empt, anticipate, forestall |反义词：postpone, delay
