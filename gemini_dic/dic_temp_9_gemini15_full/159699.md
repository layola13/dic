polyblast:/ˈpɒliblæst/| n. |多功能炸弹，多用途炸弹 | A polyblast is a type of explosive device designed to have multiple effects, such as blast, fragmentation, and incendiary effects, all within a single unit.  It's often used in military applications. | Example: The military deployed a series of polyblasts to clear a heavily fortified enemy position.  = 军方部署了一系列多功能炸弹来清除敌人坚固的阵地。 The effectiveness of the polyblast was evident in the widespread damage inflicted. = 多功能炸弹的有效性在其造成的广泛破坏中显而易见。|近义词：multipurpose bomb, combined-effects bomb |反义词：conventional bomb (lacking multiple effects)|名词
