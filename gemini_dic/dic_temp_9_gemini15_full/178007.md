recites:/rɪˈsaɪts/| vt.|背诵；朗诵；叙述|To repeat aloud from memory, especially a poem or passage.|例句：She recited the poem beautifully. 她优美地朗诵了这首诗。|例句：He recited the names of all the players. 他背诵了所有球员的名字。|近义词：repeats, recitals, narrates|反义词：forgets, omits


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recites poetry every evening. 他每天晚上都背诵诗歌。
She recites the alphabet to her child. 她给孩子背诵字母表。

一般过去时:
The student recited the lesson perfectly. 学生完美地背诵了课文。
She recited a Shakespearean sonnet at the party. 她在派对上朗诵了一首莎士比亚十四行诗。

现在完成时:
He has recited this poem many times. 他已经多次背诵这首诗了。
She has recited the entire play from memory. 她已经完整地背诵了整部戏剧。

过去完成时:
He had recited the poem before the audience arrived.  观众到达之前他已经背诵了这首诗。
She had recited the speech many times before the big day.  在大日子之前她已经多次练习了演讲。


将来时:
He will recite the Gettysburg Address at the ceremony. 他将在仪式上朗诵《葛底斯堡演说》。
She will recite her own poem at the open mic night. 她将在公开麦克风之夜朗诵自己的诗歌。

虚拟语气:
If I were to recite the whole thing, it would take hours. 如果我要背诵全文，需要几个小时。
I wish I could recite the entire play flawlessly. 我希望我能完美地背诵整部戏。

被动语态:
The poem was recited by many students. 这首诗被许多学生朗诵。
The speech was recited with great passion. 这篇演讲充满激情地被朗诵出来。

疑问句:
Will you recite the poem for us? 你会为我们朗诵这首诗吗？
Can she recite the entire script? 她能背诵整个剧本吗？

否定句:
He did not recite the poem correctly. 他没有正确地背诵这首诗。
She refused to recite the passage in front of the class. 她拒绝在课堂上朗诵这篇文章。
