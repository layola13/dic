imprimis:/ˈɪmpraɪmɪs/|adv.|首先，第一；起初|Firstly; in the first place; to begin with.  The word is used to introduce the first point in a series of arguments or statements.|例句：Imprimis, let me state my objections. 首先，让我陈述我的异议。|例句：Imprimis, it is important to note that this is a preliminary study. 首先，重要的是要注意，这是一项初步研究。|近义词：firstly, first, initially, primarily|反义词：lastly, finally
