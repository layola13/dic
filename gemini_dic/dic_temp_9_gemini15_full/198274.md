shotgunners:/ˈʃɑːtɡʌnərz/| n. |猎枪手；霰弹枪射手|People who use shotguns, often in a competitive or hunting context.  The term can also refer to passengers riding in the back of a pickup truck, especially in a less-than-safe manner.|例句：The shotgunners competed fiercely in the clay pigeon shooting competition. 猎枪手们在飞碟射击比赛中激烈竞争。

The experienced shotgunners easily hit their targets.  经验丰富的猎枪手们轻松击中了目标。


近义词：shotgun users, shooters (in a broader sense)|反义词：None directly applicable|词性：Noun
