sleighing:/ˈsleɪɪŋ/| v.|乘雪橇，雪橇运动|The act of riding in or using a sleigh.  It can also refer to the sport or activity of sleighing.|例句：We went sleighing in the snowy mountains. 我们在雪山上乘坐雪橇。

例句：The children were delighted by the sleighing party. 孩子们对雪橇派对很高兴。


近义词：sledding (though sledding often implies a smaller, simpler vehicle)

反义词：None directly;  antonyms would relate to activities contrasting with winter snow sports, e.g., swimming, sunbathing.


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They sleigh every winter. = 他们每年冬天都乘雪橇。
We sleigh down the snowy hill. = 我们沿着雪坡乘雪橇而下。


一般过去时:
He sleighed through the snowy forest. = 他乘雪橇穿过雪林。
She sleighed with her family last Christmas. = 她去年圣诞节和家人一起乘雪橇。


现在完成时:
They have sleighed many times this season. = 这个季节他们已经多次乘雪橇了。
I have never sleighed before. = 我以前从未乘过雪橇。


过去完成时:
We had sleighed for hours before we got tired. = 我们已经乘了几个小时的雪橇才感到疲倦。
They had sleighed down that hill many times. = 他们已经多次从那座山上乘雪橇下来了。


将来时:
We will sleigh tomorrow if it snows. = 如果明天下雪，我们将去乘雪橇。
They will sleigh in the Alps next year. = 他们明年将在阿尔卑斯山乘雪橇。


虚拟语气:
If it snowed, we would sleigh. = 如果下雪，我们将乘雪橇。
I wish I could sleigh this weekend. = 我希望这个周末我能去乘雪橇。


被动语态:
The sleigh was often used for sleighing. = 这辆雪橇经常被用来乘雪橇。 (This is a less common usage)
The horses were prepared for sleighing. = 马匹已准备好拉雪橇。


疑问句:
Do you like sleighing? = 你喜欢乘雪橇吗？
Have you ever gone sleighing before? = 你以前乘过雪橇吗？


否定句:
They did not sleigh yesterday because it didn't snow. = 昨天因为不下雪，他们没有乘雪橇。
We will not sleigh if the weather is bad. = 如果天气不好，我们将不去乘雪橇。
