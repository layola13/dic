necroscopic:/nɛkrəˈskɒpɪk/| adj. |验尸的；尸检的| Relating to or used in a necroscopy (post-mortem examination).  It describes the examination of a corpse to determine the cause of death. |The necroscopic findings revealed the cause of death to be blunt force trauma.|近义词：autopsic, post-mortem|反义词：ante-mortem
