petter:/ˈpɛtər/| n. |轻抚；爱抚；宠物|A petter is a gentle stroking or patting, often given to a pet or a child as a sign of affection.  It can also refer to a person who frequently pets animals.|例句：The cat enjoyed the petter from its owner.这只猫很喜欢主人给它的轻抚。  The child received a loving petter on the head.  孩子得到了一个充满爱的抚摸。|近义词：caress, stroke, pat|反义词：abuse, neglect
