familiarly:/fəˈmɪljərli/ | adv. | 亲切地；熟悉地；随便地 | In a familiar manner; intimately; on friendly terms.  It describes an action performed in a way that shows familiarity or intimacy. |例句：He greeted me familiarly. 他亲切地向我打招呼了。|例句：She spoke familiarly about her childhood. 她随意地谈论着自己的童年。|近义词：intimately, casually, informally |反义词：formally, distantly, stiffly
