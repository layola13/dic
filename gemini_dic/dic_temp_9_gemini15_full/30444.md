centre-left: /ˈsɛntə(r) lɛft/ | adj. | 中左翼的 | Describing a political position or party that is slightly left of centre, advocating for social reforms but generally within a capitalist framework.  This term typically refers to a moderate left-wing stance, less radical than far-left ideologies. | The centre-left party proposed moderate tax increases to fund social programs.  = 中左翼政党提议适度提高税收以资助社会项目。  The election saw a strong showing from centre-left candidates. =  选举中，中左翼候选人表现强劲。|近义词：moderate left, left-of-centre, liberal (in some contexts)|反义词：centre-right, right-wing, conservative
