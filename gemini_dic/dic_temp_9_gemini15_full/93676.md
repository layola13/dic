imessage:/ˈaɪmɛsɪdʒ/| n.|苹果信息|Apple's proprietary instant messaging service for iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and watchOS devices.  It allows users to send text messages, photos, videos, and other media files.  iMessage integrates seamlessly with other Apple services.|例句：I sent him an iMessage, but he hasn't replied yet. 我给他发了一条iMessage，但他还没回复。|例句：iMessage is a great way to stay in touch with friends and family. iMessage是与朋友和家人保持联系的好方法。|近义词：text message, instant message|反义词：None.
