bygones:/ˈbaɪɡɒnz/| n. |过去的事；往事|Things that happened in the past, especially unpleasant things that should be forgotten.  Bygones are often used in the phrase "Let bygones be bygones," which means to forget about past quarrels or disagreements and move on. |Let bygones be bygones and let's start over.  让我们忘记过去，重新开始吧。|forgotten things, past events, old grievances |current events, present issues |名词
