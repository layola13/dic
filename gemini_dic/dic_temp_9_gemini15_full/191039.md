scan:/skæn/ | vt., n. | 扫描；浏览；细查 | To examine something quickly but not in detail; to read something quickly to find a particular piece of information. |例句：I scanned the newspaper for news about the election. 我快速浏览了报纸上的选举新闻。  |例句：The doctor scanned my head for any injuries. 医生扫描了我的头部查看是否有损伤。|近义词：browse, skim, peruse |反义词：study, examine thoroughly |动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scans the document for errors. 他扫描文件查找错误。
She scans the barcode at the checkout. 她在收银处扫描条形码。

一般过去时:
I scanned the article quickly before the meeting. 我在会议前快速浏览了这篇文章。
They scanned the area for any signs of life. 他们扫描了该区域寻找任何生命迹象。

现在完成时:
I have scanned all the documents. 我已经扫描了所有文件。
She has scanned the book for relevant information. 她已经浏览了这本书寻找相关信息。

过去完成时:
He had scanned the report before he presented it. 他在提交报告之前已经浏览过报告了。
They had scanned the area several times. 他们已经扫描过该区域好几次了。

将来时:
I will scan the picture for you. 我会帮你扫描这张照片。
She will scan the document and send it to you. 她会扫描文件并发送给你。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would scan the entire book. 如果我有时间，我会通读整本书。
It would be helpful if you scanned the document first. 如果你先扫描一下文件会很有帮助。

被动语态:
The document was scanned by the secretary. 文件由秘书扫描。
The area was scanned by the police for evidence. 该区域被警方扫描以寻找证据。

疑问句:
Have you scanned the document yet? 你扫描了文件了吗？
Did they scan the entire building? 他们扫描了整栋建筑物吗？

否定句:
I didn't scan the email properly. 我没有正确扫描邮件。
He hasn't scanned the documents yet. 他还没扫描文件。
