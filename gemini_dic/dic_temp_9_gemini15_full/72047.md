flirtatious: /flɝːˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 卖弄风情的；调情的；轻浮的 |  Characterized by or given to flirting; playfully amorous or coquettish.  | 例句：Her flirtatious behavior made him uncomfortable.  她的轻浮行为让他感到不舒服。

例句：The flirtatious glances exchanged between them suggested a budding romance.  他们之间眉来眼去的暗示着一段新恋情的开始。


近义词：coquettish, teasing, playful, amorous, romantic

反义词：reserved, shy, modest, demure


词源:  Derived from the verb "flirt," which ultimately comes from the Middle Dutch word "fliteren," meaning "to flutter" or "to move quickly." The sense of playful or lighthearted behavior developed over time.  The "-atious" suffix indicates a quality or characteristic.
