defrosts:/diːˈfrɔːsts/| vt.|解冻；除霜|To remove frost or ice from something, typically by using heat.|例句：I defrosted the chicken overnight in the refrigerator. 我在冰箱里把鸡肉解冻了一整夜。|例句：The car defrosted itself after a few hours in the sun.  几小时后，汽车在阳光下自行解冻了。|近义词：thaw, melt|反义词：freeze


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun defrosts the car windscreen. = 太阳融化了汽车挡风玻璃上的霜。
He defrosts the refrigerator regularly. = 他定期除冰箱的霜。


一般过去时:
I defrosted the vegetables before cooking them. = 我在烹饪蔬菜之前解冻了它们。
She defrosted the bread in the microwave. = 她在微波炉里解冻了面包。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
