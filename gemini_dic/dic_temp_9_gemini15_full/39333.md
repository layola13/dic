conjecturer:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərər/| n. |推测者，猜测者 |A conjecturer is someone who forms opinions or ideas based on incomplete information; someone who makes conjectures.  They often engage in speculation and hypothesis formation. | Example: The detective, a keen conjecturer, pieced together clues to solve the mystery.  这位侦探，一位敏锐的推测者，拼凑线索以解开谜团。 |近义词：speculator, guesser, theorizer |反义词：knower, verifier
