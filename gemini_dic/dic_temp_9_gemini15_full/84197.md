half-reclining:/ˈhæf rɪˈklaɪnɪŋ/| adj.|半躺着的；半倚靠着的|Partially reclining; in a position between sitting and lying down.  The term describes a posture where one is neither fully upright nor fully lying down, but somewhere in between.|例句：He was half-reclining in his armchair, reading a book. 他半躺在扶手椅里看书。


例句：She spent the afternoon half-reclining on the sofa, enjoying the sunshine. 她下午躺在沙发上享受阳光。


近义词：semi-reclined, partly reclining, lounging


反义词：upright, fully reclined, prostrate
