gurus:/ˈɡʊruːz/| n. |大师，权威，专家 |Experts in a particular field, often spiritual or philosophical.  They are typically seen as having great wisdom and knowledge.|例句：Many gurus offer advice on achieving inner peace. 许多大师提供关于获得内心平静的建议。|近义词：experts, masters, authorities, sages |反义词：novices, beginners, amateurs
