oxalan:/ɒkˈsælən/| n.|草酸盐|Oxalate is a salt of oxalic acid, a naturally occurring compound found in many plants.  It's often studied for its various properties and roles in biological processes.|Example: Spinach contains a significant amount of oxalates.  菠菜含有大量的草酸盐。|近义词：oxalic acid salt|反义词：无
