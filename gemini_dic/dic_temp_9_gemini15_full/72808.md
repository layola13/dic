folded-up: /ˈfoʊldɪd ʌp/ | adj. | 折叠起来的，对折的 |  Describes something that has been folded.  It implies a compact or flattened state achieved through folding. |


例句：

The folded-up map was tucked into his pocket. = 折叠好的地图塞进了他的口袋里。

She found a folded-up letter on her desk. = 她在桌子上发现了一封叠好的信。


近义词：creased, crumpled, folded


反义词：unfolded, spread out


词性：形容词
