non-procreative: /ˌnɒn prəˈkreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非生殖的，不生育的 | Not intended to or capable of producing offspring.  It refers to actions, practices, or relationships that do not result in the creation of new life. |


例句：

The couple chose a non-procreative lifestyle. 这对夫妇选择了一种非生育的生活方式。

Many modern birth control methods are non-procreative. 许多现代避孕方法是非生育的。


近义词：sterile, infertile, childless (when referring to individuals), asexual (in some contexts)


反义词：procreative, reproductive, fertile


词性：形容词
