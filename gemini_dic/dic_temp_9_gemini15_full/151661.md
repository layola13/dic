parting:/ˈpɑːrtɪŋ/ | n., adj. | 分离；分别；道别；分开的；离别的 |  The act of separating or the state of being separated; a leave-taking.  |例句：Their parting was sad and tearful.  他们的离别悲伤而泪流满面。

例句：The parting of ways was amicable.  分道扬镳是友好的。


近义词：separation, farewell, leave-taking, adieu | 反义词：meeting, reunion, joining


词性：名词，形容词
