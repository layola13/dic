reassignments:/ˌriːəˈsaɪnmənts/ | n. |任务重新分配；人员重新安排 | The act of assigning something or someone to a different task, role, or position.  It often implies a change in responsibility or duty. |例句：The project manager oversaw the reassignments of team members to different tasks. 项目经理负责对团队成员进行任务重新分配。

例句：Due to budget cuts, several employees faced unexpected reassignments. 由于预算削减，几名员工面临着意外的职位重新安排。


近义词：redeployments, reallocations, reassignments of duties | 反义词：original assignments, initial allocations | 词性：名词
