outdwells:/ˌaʊtˈdwelz/| v.|超过…居住；比…住得久|To surpass in duration of residence; to remain longer than.|The old house outdwells the family who once lived there.  这栋老房子比曾经住在那里的家庭住的时间还要长。|His love for the mountains outdwells his desire for city life. 他对山区的热爱超过了他对城市生活的渴望。|outlast, outstay|depart, leave


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outdwells his neighbors in this town. 他在这个镇上比他的邻居住的时间长。
The old tree outdwells many generations. 这棵老树比许多代人都活得久。

一般过去时:
