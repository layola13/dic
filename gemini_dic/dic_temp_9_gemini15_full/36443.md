colchicine:/ˈkɒlkɪsiːn/| n. |秋水仙碱|An alkaloid extracted from the autumn crocus (Colchicum autumnale) used medically to treat gout and familial Mediterranean fever.  It works by inhibiting cell division.  |例句：The doctor prescribed colchicine to alleviate the inflammation caused by the gout attack.  医生开了秋水仙碱来缓解痛风发作引起的炎症。
