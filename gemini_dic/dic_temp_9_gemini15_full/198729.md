sycamore: /ˈsɪkəmɔːr/ | n. | 梧桐树；美国梧桐；英国梧桐 | A sycamore is a type of large tree with broad leaves, known for its distinctive bark and seeds.  Different species are found in Europe, North America, and Asia. The wood is often used for furniture and other purposes. | Example: The old sycamore tree in the park provides shade for many people.  公园里那棵古老的梧桐树为许多人遮阴。  The children loved to climb the sycamore tree in their backyard.孩子们喜欢爬自家后院的梧桐树。| 近义词：plane tree, maple (in some contexts) | 反义词：无 |  名词
