phosphorogenic:/ˌfɒsfəroʊˈdʒɛnɪk/|/ˌfɒsfərəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj. |磷光产生的; 发磷光的 |Producing or relating to phosphorescence.  The term describes a substance or process that generates or is associated with phosphorescence, the emission of light by a substance after it has been exposed to radiation.  |Example: The phosphorogenic properties of the mineral were studied extensively.  该矿物的磷光特性被广泛研究。|近义词：phosphorescent, luminescent |反义词：non-luminescent
