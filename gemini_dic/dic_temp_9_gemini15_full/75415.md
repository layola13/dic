frugalities:/fruːˈɡælɪtiz/| n. |节俭；俭省；节省；朴素 |The noun "frugalities" refers to instances of frugality, or the practice of being economical in the use of resources. It suggests a careful and thrifty way of life, often involving avoiding waste and unnecessary expense.  It can also refer to the specific things that demonstrate frugality. |例句：Their frugalities were evident in their simple home and modest lifestyle. 他们的俭朴生活从他们简朴的房屋和朴素的生活方式中可见一斑。

Their frugalities allowed them to save enough money for a down payment on a house. 他们的节俭使他们攒够了足够的钱支付房屋的首付款。|近义词：economies, savings, thrift, parsimony |反义词：extravagance, profligacy, wastefulness |名词
