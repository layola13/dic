foamable:/ˈfoʊməbl/| adj.|可发泡的，能起泡的|Capable of being made into a foam or froth.  The adjective describes materials or substances that can be transformed into a foam-like structure.|例句：This type of plastic is foamable, making it ideal for packaging.  这种塑料是可发泡的，使其成为理想的包装材料。|例句：The new detergent is highly foamable, producing a rich lather.  这种新型清洁剂具有很强的起泡性，能产生丰富的泡沫。|近义词：frothable, bubbly|反义词：non-foamable
