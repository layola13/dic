carnations:/kɑːˈneɪʃənz/| n.|康乃馨|Carnations are a type of flower, known for their vibrant colors and sweet fragrance. They are popular as cut flowers and are often given as gifts to express love and affection.  |例句：She received a bouquet of carnations for her birthday. 她收到了生日康乃馨花束。  |例句：The garden was filled with the sweet scent of blooming carnations. 花园里充满了盛开的康乃馨的甜香味。|近义词：dianthus (植物学名)|反义词：无|名词
