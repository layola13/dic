embellishing:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪŋ/| v. |装饰；润色；美化 |To make something more attractive by adding decorative details or features.  It can also refer to exaggerating or enhancing a story or account.| The artist embellished the painting with gold leaf.  艺术家用金箔装饰了这幅画。She embellished her story with exciting details. 她用激动人心的细节润色了她的故事。 | adorn, decorate, ornament, enhance | simplify, plain, blunt |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She embellishes her cakes with fresh berries. = 她用新鲜浆果装饰她的蛋糕。
He embellishes his reports with unnecessary details. = 他在报告中添加了不必要的细节。


一般过去时:
The writer embellished the historical facts to make the story more interesting. = 作家润色了历史事实，使故事更有趣。
They embellished the room with antique furniture. = 他们用古董家具装饰了房间。


现在完成时:
She has embellished her resume to make it more impressive. = 她已经润色了她的简历，使其更令人印象深刻。
He has embellished the truth to avoid punishment. = 他为了避免惩罚而夸大了事实。


过去完成时:
They had embellished the tale so much that no one believed it. = 他们把故事润色得太多，以至于没有人相信。
She had embellished the dress with pearls before the party. = 派对前她用珍珠装饰了那件裙子。


将来时:
They will embellish the garden with colorful flowers. = 他们将用色彩鲜艳的花朵装饰花园。
She will embellish her presentation with relevant images. = 她将在演示文稿中添加相关的图像。


虚拟语气:
If I were to embellish this story, it would be much more engaging. = 如果我要润色这个故事，它会更引人入胜。
I wish I hadn't embellished my report; it made me look dishonest. = 我真希望我没有润色我的报告；这让我看起来不诚实。


被动语态:
The cake was embellished with intricate icing designs. = 蛋糕装饰着复杂的糖霜图案。
The story was embellished to create a more dramatic effect. = 故事被润色以创造更戏剧性的效果。


疑问句:
Does she embellish her writing with metaphors? = 她是否用比喻来润色她的写作？
Will they embellish the building with sculptures? = 他们会用雕塑装饰建筑物吗？


否定句:
He didn't embellish his account of the events. = 他没有夸大他对事件的描述。
She didn't embellish the simple dress with any accessories. = 她没有用任何配饰来装饰这件简单的连衣裙。
