self-perpetuating: /ˌsɛlf pərˈpɛtʃueɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我延续的；自我维持的 |  Continuing or occurring indefinitely without external assistance or intervention.  Something that is self-perpetuating maintains itself and continues without outside help.  |

例句：

The cycle of poverty is often self-perpetuating. = 贫困的循环往往是自我延续的。

The system is designed to be self-perpetuating, requiring minimal outside input. = 这个系统的设计是自我维持的，需要最少的外部输入。


近义词：self-sustaining, self-maintaining, self-replicating


反义词：self-destructive, unsustainable


词性：形容词
