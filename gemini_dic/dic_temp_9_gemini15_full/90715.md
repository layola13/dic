humidifies:/ˈhjuːmɪdaɪz/| vt.|使潮湿；加湿|To make something more humid; to add moisture to the air.|The humidifier humidifies the dry air in my bedroom.|近义词：moisturize, dampen|反义词：dehumidify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The humidifier humidifies the air constantly. = 加湿器持续地使空气湿润。
He humidifies his home in winter. = 他在冬天给家里加湿。

We humidified the air to alleviate dryness. = 我们加湿空气以减轻干燥。
