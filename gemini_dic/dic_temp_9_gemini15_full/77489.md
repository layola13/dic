gazellelike:/ɡəˈzɛlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像瞪羚一样的；轻盈的；优雅的；敏捷的 |Resembling a gazelle; characterized by grace, speed, and elegance.  The word evokes the image of a gazelle's graceful and swift movements. |例句：She moved with a gazellelike grace across the dance floor. 她优雅地像一只瞪羚一样在舞池中移动。

例句：The gazellelike speed of the runner amazed the crowd.  跑步者像瞪羚一样迅速的速度让观众惊叹。


近义词：graceful, elegant, swift, agile, nimble

反义词：clumsy, awkward, slow, sluggish


词性：形容词
