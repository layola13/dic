shave:/ʃeɪv/| vt. n.|刮（胡子）；剃（毛）；削减；刮掉|to cut off hair very close to the skin, especially from the face|He shaves every morning. 他每天早上刮胡子。 I need to shave my legs. 我需要刮腿毛。|cut, trim|grow, cultivate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shaves every morning. = 他每天早上刮胡子。
She shaves her legs twice a week. = 她一周刮两次腿毛。


一般过去时:
I shaved my head last year. = 我去年剃了光头。
He shaved off his beard. = 他刮掉了胡子。

I have shaved my head three times. = 我已经剃了三次光头了。

He had shaved before he went to the party. = 他去参加派对之前已经刮过胡子了。
She had shaved her legs before going to the beach. = 她去海滩之前已经刮过腿毛了。
