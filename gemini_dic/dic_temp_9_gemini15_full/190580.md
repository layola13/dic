satisfiers:/ˈsætɪsfaɪərz/ | n. | 令人满意的事物；满足者 | Things or people that provide satisfaction;  elements that fulfill a need or desire.  | 例句：The new features are major satisfiers for our customers.  新的功能是我们客户的主要满意点。

例句：  Good salary and benefits are key satisfiers for many job seekers. 良好的薪水和福利是许多求职者追求的关键满意点。


近义词：pleasers, fulfillments, gratifications

反义词：dissatisfiers, frustrations, disappointments

词性：名词
