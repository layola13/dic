nonprofessions:/ˌnɒnprəˈfɛʃənz/ | n. | 非专业人员，非专业领域 | The term "nonprofessions" refers to occupations or fields of work that are not considered professions in the traditional sense.  These typically lack the same level of formal education, licensing, or professional bodies associated with established professions like medicine or law.  They may still require skill and training, but they don't share the same level of prestige, autonomy, or ethical codes. | Example:  Many skilled trades, such as carpentry or plumbing, are sometimes considered nonprofessions, although they require significant expertise and training. | 近义词: nonprofessional occupations, trades, vocations | 反义词: professions, professional fields
