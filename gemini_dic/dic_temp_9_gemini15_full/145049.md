overagitating: /ˌoʊvərˈædʒɪteɪtɪŋ/ | v. |过度激动，过度焦虑 |  The act of agitating excessively or beyond a reasonable level, often resulting in unnecessary worry or stress.  It implies a state of being overly worked up or anxious.  | 例句：He was overagitating himself about the upcoming presentation. 他对即将到来的演示过度焦虑。


She was overagitating over a minor setback. 她为一个小挫折过度焦虑。


近义词：overwrought, hyperventilating, overanxious, stressed, frantic | 反义词：calm, composed, relaxed, tranquil

词性: 动词
