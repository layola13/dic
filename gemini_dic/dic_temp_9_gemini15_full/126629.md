nailing:/ˈneɪlɪŋ/| v.|钉牢；敲定；成功做到|The act of fastening something with nails; to hit or strike something forcefully; to achieve something successfully.|例句：He was nailing the boards to the wall. 他正在把木板钉到墙上。


例句：She nailed her presentation. 她成功完成了她的演示。|近义词：hammering, fixing, securing, mastering|反义词：removing, detaching, failing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nails the boards to the wall every day. = 他每天都把木板钉到墙上。
She nails her presentation perfectly. = 她完美地完成了她的演示。

一般过去时:
He nailed the picture to the wall yesterday. = 他昨天把画钉在了墙上。
She nailed the exam last week. = 她上周考试考得很棒。

现在完成时:
He has nailed all the shelves to the wall. = 他已经把所有的架子都钉到墙上了。
She has nailed the deal with the new client. = 她已经和新客户达成了协议。

过去完成时:
He had nailed the roof before the storm came. =暴风雨来临之前，他已经把屋顶钉好了。
She had nailed the interview before she felt nervous. = 面试之前她就已经很自信了。

将来时:
He will nail the door shut tonight. = 他今晚要把门钉牢。
She will nail that job interview, I'm sure. = 我确信她会通过那次求职面试。

虚拟语气:
If I had a hammer, I would nail the picture to the wall. = 如果我有一把锤子，我会把画钉到墙上。
If she had practiced more, she would have nailed the presentation. = 如果她多练习一下，她就能完美地完成演示了。

被动语态:
The picture was nailed to the wall. = 这幅画被钉在了墙上。
The deal was nailed down after hours of negotiation. =经过数小时的谈判，这笔交易终于敲定了。

疑问句:
Are you nailing the shelves correctly? = 你在正确地钉架子吗？
Did she nail the audition? = 她试镜成功了吗？

否定句:
He didn't nail the shelf to the wall properly. = 他没有把架子正确地钉到墙上。
She didn't nail the landing of her jump. = 她跳跃落地时没有成功。


PHRASE->
nail down: 敲定；确定
nail it: 做成；成功
nail something to something: 把...钉到...上
get nailed: 被抓住；被惩罚
drive a nail: 钉钉子
