imperils:/ɪmˈpɛrəlz/ | vt. |危及；使陷入危险 | To put something or someone in danger; to endanger. |例句：The rising sea levels imperil coastal communities.  上升的海平面危及沿海社区。|例句： Reckless driving imperils not only the driver but also others on the road.  鲁莽驾驶不仅危及驾驶员自身，也危及道路上的其他人。|近义词：endanger, jeopardize, threaten, risk |反义词：protect, safeguard, secure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm imperils the coastal towns. = 暴风雨危及沿海城镇。
His actions imperil the company's future. = 他的行为危及公司的未来。

一般过去时:
The earthquake imperiled many lives. = 地震危及了许多人的生命。
The faulty brakes imperiled the passengers. = 刹车失灵危及了乘客的安全。

现在完成时:
The prolonged drought has imperiled the harvest. = 长期的干旱已经危及了收成。
Years of pollution have imperiled the health of the river. = 多年的污染已经危及了河流的健康。

过去完成时:
The captain had imperiled the ship by ignoring the warnings. = 船长因无视警告而危及了船只的安全。
They had imperiled their careers by speaking out against the government. = 他们因公开反对政府而危及了自己的职业生涯。

将来时:
The approaching hurricane will imperil the island's inhabitants. = 即将到来的飓风将危及岛上居民的安全。
Further cuts in funding will imperil the hospital's services. = 进一步削减资金将危及医院的服务。

虚拟语气:
If he continued that behavior, he would imperil his reputation. = 如果他继续这种行为，他将危及自己的名誉。
I wish the government wouldn’t imperil the environment. = 我希望政府不要危及环境。

被动语态:
The project was imperiled by a lack of funding. = 该项目因缺乏资金而陷入危险。
The lives of the miners were imperiled by the cave-in. = 矿工的生命因塌方而受到威胁。

疑问句:
Does this policy imperil national security? = 这一政策是否危及国家安全？
Does the new law imperil the rights of the citizens? = 新法是否危及公民的权利？

否定句:
The new safety measures will not imperil the workers. = 新的安全措施不会危及工人的安全。
He tried to avoid actions that might imperil his friends. = 他试图避免可能危及朋友的行动。


PHRASE->
imperil lives = 危及生命
imperil the environment = 危及环境
imperil national security = 危及国家安全
imperil the economy = 危及经济
imperil public health = 危及公共卫生


词源:
imperil 源自于古法语 *empariller，意为“使平等；使处于危险中”。  最终来自拉丁语 *in- + periculum（危险）。
