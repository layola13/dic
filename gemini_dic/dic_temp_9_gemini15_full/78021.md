gentrie:/ˈdʒɛntri/ | n. | 绅士阶层，上流社会 | The gentry refers to the class of people in society who are of gentle birth or good social position, typically those who own land.  They are usually considered to be below the aristocracy but above the commoners. | Example: The local gentry were known for their extravagant parties and charitable donations.  当地绅士阶层以其奢华的派对和慈善捐款而闻名。|近义词：aristocracy, nobility, elite |反义词：commoners, peasantry
