repulsive: /rɪˈpʌlsɪv/ | adj. |令人厌恶的，令人反感的 | Causing intense distaste or disgust.  Something repulsive is extremely unpleasant and makes you feel sick or disgusted. | 例句：The smell of rotting garbage was repulsive.  腐烂垃圾的气味令人作呕。

The sight of the injured animal was repulsive to her. 受伤动物的景象让她感到厌恶。


近义词：disgusting, abhorrent, loathsome, offensive, repellent, nauseating | 反义词：attractive, appealing, pleasing, delightful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The food smells repulsive. = 这食物闻起来令人作呕。
That behavior is repulsive to me. = 那种行为让我反感。


一般过去时:
The film's violence was repulsive. = 电影的暴力场面令人厌恶。
I found his mannerisms repulsive.  = 我觉得他的举止令人反感。


现在完成时:
His actions have been repulsive to many people. = 他的行为已令许多人反感。
The smell has been repulsive all day. = 一整天都臭不可闻。


过去完成时:
I had found his proposal repulsive before he even finished. = 他还没说完我就觉得他的提议令人反感了。
The results of the experiment had been repulsive to the scientists. = 实验结果让科学家们感到厌恶。


将来时:
The new policy will be repulsive to many voters. = 新政策会让许多选民反感。
I think the taste will be repulsive. = 我认为这个味道会令人作呕。


虚拟语气:
If the taste were repulsive, I wouldn't eat it. = 如果味道令人作呕，我不会吃它。
I wish the smell wasn't so repulsive. = 我真希望气味不要这么难闻。


被动语态:
The idea was found repulsive by many. = 许多人觉得这个主意令人反感。
His behavior was considered repulsive by the public. = 他的行为被公众认为令人厌恶。


疑问句:
Is the idea of eating insects repulsive to you? = 你觉得吃昆虫的想法令人作呕吗？
Do you find that smell repulsive? = 你觉得那个气味令人作呕吗？


否定句:
The painting wasn't repulsive, it was quite beautiful. = 这幅画并不令人厌恶，它相当漂亮。
I didn't find his actions repulsive at all. = 我一点也不觉得他的行为令人反感。



PHRASE->
a repulsive smell = 一股令人作呕的气味
a repulsive character = 一个令人厌恶的角色
repulsive behavior = 令人厌恶的行为
a repulsive insect = 一种令人厌恶的昆虫
repulsive force = 斥力


词源:
From Latin *repulsīvus, from repellere "to drive back", from re- "back" + pellere "to drive".  The sense evolved from "driving back" (as in a repulsive force) to "causing aversion or disgust".
