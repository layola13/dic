reordering: /ˌriˈɔːrdərɪŋ/ | n. |重新排序，重新排列 |The act of rearranging items into a new order.  It can refer to a process or the result of that process.  This could apply to lists, data, words, or physical objects. |例句：The reordering of the chapters improved the flow of the book.  重新排列章节使书的流程得到了改善。|例句：The reordering of the database took several hours. 数据库的重新排序花费了几个小时。|近义词：rearrangement, reorganization, reshuffling |反义词：maintaining the original order


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team is reordering the supplies. = 团队正在重新整理物资。
She reorders her priorities every year. = 她每年都会重新排序她的优先事项。


一般过去时:
He reordered the files alphabetically. = 他按字母顺序重新排列了文件。
They reordered the furniture in the living room. = 他们重新布置了客厅的家具。


现在完成时:
I have reordered the photos chronologically. = 我已经按时间顺序重新排列了照片。
She has reordered her schedule to accommodate the meeting. = 她已经重新安排了她的日程表以适应会议。


过去完成时:
They had reordered the agenda before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经重新安排了议程。
He had reordered his thoughts before giving the presentation. = 他在做演示之前已经重新整理了他的思路。


将来时:
We will reorder the books by author. = 我们将按作者重新排列书籍。
She will reorder the ingredients for the cake. = 她将重新整理蛋糕的配料。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reorder the entire project. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新安排整个项目。
I wish I had reordered my priorities earlier. = 我希望我早点重新安排我的优先事项。


被动语态:
The files were reordered by the assistant. = 文件由助理重新排列。
The schedule is being reordered to fit the new deadline. = 日程表正在被重新安排以适应新的截止日期。


疑问句:

Has the data been reordered correctly? = 数据是否被正确地重新排序了？


否定句:
They did not reorder the chapters in the book. = 他们没有重新排列书中的章节。
I will not reorder the supplies until next week. = 我不会在下周之前重新整理物资。
