maintenances:/ˈmeɪntənənsɪz/ | n. | 保养；维护；维修 | The plural form of "maintenance," referring to multiple acts or instances of maintaining something.  It encompasses all activities required to keep something in good working order. |  The building requires regular maintenances to prevent deterioration. 这栋建筑需要定期维护以防止损坏。 The car’s frequent maintenances kept it running smoothly. 这辆车的频繁维护使其运行平稳。 | upkeep, servicing, repair | neglect, destruction | 名词
