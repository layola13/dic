counterattacked:/ˌkaʊntərəˈtæk/| v. |反击；反攻|To make a counterattack; to attack in response to an attack.  |例句：The army counterattacked after being pushed back.  军队在被击退后进行了反击。|例句：The boxer counterattacked with a powerful left hook. 拳击手用有力的一记左勾拳进行了反击。|近义词：retaliate, retaliated, strike back, fight back|反义词：retreat, surrender, yield

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team counterattacks aggressively. = 球队积极地反击。
She counterattacks every criticism. = 她反驳每一个批评。

一般过去时:
The soldiers counterattacked at dawn. = 士兵们在黎明时分进行了反击。
He counterattacked with a witty remark. = 他以一句妙语进行了反击。

现在完成时:
They have counterattacked several times. = 他们已经反击了好几次。
The company has counterattacked its competitors' claims. = 公司已经反驳了其竞争对手的主张。

过去完成时:
The general had counterattacked before the enemy could regroup. = 将军在敌人能够重整旗鼓之前就进行了反击。
She had counterattacked every argument he made. = 她反驳了他提出的每一个论点。

将来时:
We will counterattack if they attack again. = 如果他们再次进攻，我们将进行反击。
The government will counterattack the opposition's accusations. = 政府将反驳反对派的指控。


虚拟语气:
If we had counterattacked sooner, we might have won. = 如果我们早点反击，我们也许会赢。
I wish I had counterattacked more effectively. = 我希望我反击得更有效。

被动语态:
The city was counterattacked by the rebels. = 这个城市遭到叛军的反击。
His arguments were counterattacked with facts. = 他的论点被事实驳斥了。

疑问句:
Did they counterattack? = 他们反击了吗？
Will the government counterattack the economic crisis? = 政府会反击经济危机吗？

否定句:
They did not counterattack immediately. = 他们没有立即反击。
The company did not counterattack the negative publicity. = 公司没有反击负面宣传。
