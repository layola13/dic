pulverising: /ˈpʌlvəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 粉碎；磨成粉末；彻底摧毁 | To reduce to powder or dust; to destroy completely. |  例句：The earthquake pulverised the city. 地震将城市夷为平地。

The constant bombardment pulverised the enemy's defenses.  持续的轰炸彻底摧毁了敌人的防御工事。


近义词：crushing, grinding, smashing, destroying, obliterating | 反义词：building, constructing, creating, preserving
