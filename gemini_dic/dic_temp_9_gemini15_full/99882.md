internalises: /ɪnˈtɜːnəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使内化；吸收；将…变成自己的一部分 | To make (something, such as a belief, idea, or value) part of one's own way of thinking, feeling, or behaving.  |例句：He internalized his father's lessons on honesty. 他将父亲关于诚实的教诲内化了。

例句：The company successfully internalized the new technology. 这家公司成功地将新技术内化了。


近义词：assimilate, absorb, integrate, incorporate | 反义词：externalize, reject, ignore | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He internalizes new information quickly. = 他很快就能吸收新的信息。
She internalizes her emotions. = 她把自己的情绪内化。


一般过去时:
He internalized the lessons from his mistakes. = 他从错误中吸取了教训。
The team internalized the new project goals. = 团队将新的项目目标内化了。


现在完成时:
She has internalized the company culture. = 她已经融入了公司文化。
They have internalized the importance of teamwork. = 他们已经理解了团队合作的重要性。


过去完成时:
He had internalized the skills needed for the job before he started. = 他在开始工作之前已经掌握了这项工作所需的技能。
She had internalized the trauma from her childhood. = 她已经从童年的创伤中恢复过来。


将来时:
He will internalize the new software quickly. = 他很快就会掌握这个新软件。
The students will internalize the concepts through practice. = 学生们将通过实践来掌握这些概念。


虚拟语气:
If he internalized the feedback, he would improve. = 如果他能吸取反馈意见，他就会进步。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
