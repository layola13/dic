endemics:/ɛnˈdɛmɪks/| n.|地方性疾病；地方特有物种|  A disease or condition that is regularly found among particular people or in a certain area.|The island is known for its unique endemics, including several species of birds found nowhere else.|近义词：endemism, native species|反义词：pandemic, cosmopolitan


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These islands have many plant endemics. = 这些岛屿有很多地方特有的植物。
The area is known for its endemic diseases. = 这个地区以地方性疾病而闻名。


一般过去时:
Scientists studied the endemic species of the region. = 科学家们研究了该地区的地方特有物种。
Researchers documented the historical prevalence of endemic diseases. = 研究人员记录了地方性疾病的历史流行情况。


现在完成时:
Researchers have identified several new endemic plant species. = 研究人员已经鉴定出几种新的地方特有植物物种。
The government has implemented programs to control endemic diseases. = 政府已经实施了控制地方性疾病的计划。


过去完成时:
Before the introduction of new species, the island had only endemic flora. = 在引进新物种之前，该岛屿只有地方特有的植物。
By the 19th century, several endemic diseases had been eradicated in the region. = 到19世纪，该地区几种地方性疾病已被根除。


将来时:
Scientists will continue to study the unique endemic species. = 科学家们将继续研究独特的特有物种。
The program will focus on preventing the spread of endemic diseases. = 该计划将重点放在预防地方性疾病的传播。


虚拟语气:

It would be beneficial if we could understand the causes of endemic diseases better. = 如果我们能够更好地了解地方性疾病的病因，那将是有益的。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Are there any endemic diseases in this region? = 这个地区有什么地方性疾病吗？
What endemic species are found in that area? = 在那个地区发现了什么地方特有物种？


否定句:

The species is not endemic to this part of the world. = 这个物种并非这个地区特有。
