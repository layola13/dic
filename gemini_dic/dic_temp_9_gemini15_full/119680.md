midget:/ˈmɪdʒɪt/| n. |侏儒；矮子 | A person of unusually small stature.  The term is now considered offensive and outdated due to its historical association with exploitation and discrimination against people with dwarfism.  | Example:  He was a midget in a circus. 他是马戏团里的一个侏儒。| 近义词：dwarf, little person | 反义词：giant, colossal | 名词
