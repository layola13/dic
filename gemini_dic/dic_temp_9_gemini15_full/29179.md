casher:/kæʃər/| n. |收银员|A person who handles cash transactions in a store or other business.  |例句：The cashier counted the money and gave me my change. 收银员数了钱并找了我零钱。|例句：The cashier was very friendly and helpful.  这位收银员非常友好和乐于助人。|近义词：teller, cashier clerk|反义词：customer, shopper


USAGE->
