hydrotheca:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθiːkə/| n. |水螅体，水母体杯|A hydrotheca is a protective cup or sheath secreted by a hydroid polyp, typically enclosing the polyp's body and providing shelter and support.  It is a characteristic feature of many hydrozoans.|The hydrotheca of *Obelia* is bell-shaped and provides protection for the polyp.|近义词：hydrothecium|反义词：无
