quasi-deaf:/ˈkwɑːziˌdef/ | adj. | 几乎聋的，半聋的 |  Partially deaf; having some degree of hearing loss.  The term suggests a level of hearing impairment that is significant but not complete deafness. |  He's quasi-deaf, needing hearing aids to understand conversations in noisy environments. 他几乎聋了，需要助听器才能在嘈杂的环境中听懂对话。|近义词：hard of hearing, partially deaf | 反义词：fully hearing
