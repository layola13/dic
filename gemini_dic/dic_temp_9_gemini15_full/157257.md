pinnipedia:/pɪˈnɪpɪdiə/| n. |鳍足类动物|A group of marine mammals characterized by flippers instead of separate digits on their limbs.  They include seals, sea lions, and walruses.|Example: Pinnipeds are well adapted to aquatic life.|近义词：seal, sea lion, walrus (referring to individual animals, not the order)|反义词：None (no direct antonym, but could contrast with terrestrial mammals)|
