sarcoidosis:/ˌsɑːrkɔɪˈdoʊsɪs/| n. |结节病|A systemic disease characterized by the formation of granulomas (small clumps of inflammatory cells) in various organs of the body, most commonly the lungs and lymph nodes.  The cause is unknown. Symptoms vary widely depending on the organs affected and can range from none to severe. |例句：Her fatigue and cough were eventually diagnosed as sarcoidosis.|近义词：granulomatosis|反义词：无
