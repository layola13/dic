semiopaque:/ˌsemiˈoʊpeɪk/| adj.|半透明的，半不透明的|Partially opaque; allowing some light to pass through but diffusing it so that objects beyond cannot be clearly seen.|The stained-glass window was semiopaque, allowing a soft, diffused light to filter into the room.  = 彩绘玻璃窗是半透明的，允许柔和的漫射光线过滤到房间里。


The curtains were semiopaque, providing privacy while still letting in some light. = 窗帘是半透明的，既能保证隐私，又能透进一些光线。


近义词：translucent, partly transparent|反义词：opaque, transparent
