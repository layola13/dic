farewelling:/ˌfɛərˈwɛlɪŋ/| n. |告别；欢送会 |The act of saying farewell; a farewell.  It often refers to a ceremony or event marking a departure. |例句：The farewelling ceremony was emotional and heartfelt. 告别仪式感人至深。  The company held a grand farewelling for its retiring CEO. 公司为即将退休的首席执行官举行了一场盛大的欢送会。|近义词：goodbye, departure, leave-taking |反义词：arrival, welcome


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ship is farewelling the port. = 船正在告别港口。
He is farewelling his friends. = 他正在告别他的朋友们。


一般过去时:
They farewelled their families at the airport. = 他们在机场向家人告别。
She farewelled her childhood home with a heavy heart. = 她依依不舍地告别了她童年的家。


现在完成时:
We have farewelled many colleagues this year. = 今年我们已经送走了许多同事。
She has farewelled her old life and embraced a new chapter. = 她已经告别了旧生活，开始新的篇章。


过去完成时:
They had farewelled their friends before the train departed. = 火车出发前，他们已经和朋友们道别了。
She had farewelled her dreams before accepting the reality. = 在接受现实之前，她已经告别了自己的梦想。


将来时:
We will farewell our guests after the party. = 派对结束后，我们将送走我们的客人。
They will farewell the old year with a special celebration. = 他们将以特别的庆祝活动告别旧的一年。


虚拟语气:
If I were to farewell my home, I would feel sad. = 如果我要告别我的家，我会感到难过。
I wish I could have farewelled him properly. = 我真希望我能好好地和他告别。


被动语态:
The departing soldiers were farewelled by their families. = 即将离去的士兵们受到了家人的告别。
The retiring teacher was farewelled with a special assembly. = 退休的老师在一次特别的集会上受到了欢送。


疑问句:
Have you farewelled your family yet? = 你已经和你家人告别了吗？
Are they farewelling their friends before leaving? = 他们离开前正在和朋友告别吗？


否定句:
They didn't farewell their neighbors before moving. = 搬家前他们没有和邻居们告别。
She hadn't farewelled her old apartment before she moved in. = 在搬进新公寓之前，她没有告别她的旧公寓。
