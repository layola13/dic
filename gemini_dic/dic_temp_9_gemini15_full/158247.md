play-ball:/pleɪ bɔːl/| v. |开始合作；同意；接受挑战|To agree to cooperate or participate; to accept a challenge or proposal.  It often implies a willingness to compromise or work together.|例句：Let's play ball and get this project finished. =咱们合作把这个项目完成吧。

Let's play ball on this deal, and we can both make a profit. =让我们在这个交易中合作，我们都能获利。


近义词：cooperate, collaborate, agree, comply, accept|反义词：refuse, reject, balk, obstruct, disagree|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They play ball on most issues. =他们在大多数问题上都合作。
We play ball whenever we can. =我们只要有机会就合作。

一般过去时:
They played ball and reached an agreement. =他们合作并达成了一致。
He played ball with the police and gave them information. =他与警方合作并提供了信息。


现在完成时:
We've played ball with them for years. =我们和他们合作多年了。
They haven't played ball since the disagreement. =自从发生分歧后，他们就不再合作了。


过去完成时:
They had played ball earlier, but then changed their minds. =他们之前曾合作过，但后来改变了主意。
She had played ball with the investigation, hoping for leniency. =她配合了调查，希望能从轻发落。


将来时:
We will play ball if they meet our demands. =如果他们满足我们的要求，我们将合作。
They'll play ball only if they see a benefit. =只有当他们看到好处时，他们才会合作。


虚拟语气:
If they would play ball, we could finish the project on time. =如果他们愿意合作，我们就能按时完成项目。
I wish they would play ball and compromise. =我希望他们能合作并妥协。


被动语态:
The ball wasn't played by either side. =双方都没有合作。
The proposal was played ball with by the board. =董事会接受了这个提案。


疑问句:
Will they play ball this time? =他们这次会合作吗？
Are they willing to play ball with us? =他们愿意和我们合作吗？


否定句:
They refused to play ball. =他们拒绝合作。
He wouldn't play ball with their demands. =他不愿接受他们的要求。
