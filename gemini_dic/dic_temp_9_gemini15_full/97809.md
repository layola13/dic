injustice:/ɪnˈdʒʌstɪs/ | n. |不公正，不公平 | The state or quality of being unjust; unfairness.  It often refers to a violation of rights or a failure to uphold justice. |例句：The injustice of the system led to widespread protests.  这个制度的不公正导致了大规模的抗议。

The long-standing injustice against marginalized communities needs to be addressed.  针对边缘化社群的长期不公正需要得到解决。|近义词：unfairness, inequity, wrong, oppression |反义词：justice, fairness, equity
