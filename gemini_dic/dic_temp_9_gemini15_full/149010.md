/ˈɒvjʊlaɪt/ | n. | 卵细胞；卵泡细胞 | An ovulite is a cell in the ovary that develops into an ovum (egg cell).  It is also sometimes used to refer to a small ovarian follicle.  The term is less common than "oocyte" or "ovum". | Example: The study focused on the development of ovulites within the ovarian follicles.  这项研究关注卵巢卵泡内卵细胞的发育。 | 近义词：oocyte, germ cell | 反义词：sperm cell
