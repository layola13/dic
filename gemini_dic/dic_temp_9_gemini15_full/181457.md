rejoice:/rɪˈdʒɔɪs/| vt. vi.|欣喜，庆祝，高兴|To feel or express great happiness or joy.  |例句：We rejoiced at the good news. 我们为这个好消息而欣喜。

例句：They rejoiced in their victory. 他们为他们的胜利而欢欣鼓舞。


近义词：celebrate, exult, delight, jubilate|反义词：grieve, mourn, lament|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I rejoice in my success. = 我为我的成功而高兴。
We rejoice at their arrival. = 我们为他们的到来而高兴。

一般过去时:
She rejoiced at the news of her promotion. = 她为晋升的消息而欣喜。
They rejoiced when they won the game. = 比赛获胜时他们欣喜若狂。

现在完成时:
We have rejoiced over this happy event for days. = 我们为这件喜事高兴了好几天了。
They have rejoiced at the birth of their child. = 他们为孩子的出生而欢欣雀跃。

过去完成时:
He had rejoiced at the prospect of a holiday. = 他曾对假期的前景感到高兴。
She had rejoiced when she heard the good news. = 当她听到好消息时，她欣喜若狂。

将来时:
We will rejoice when the project is finally finished. = 当项目最终完成时，我们将欢欣鼓舞。
They will rejoice at the news of their success. = 他们将为成功的消息而欢欣鼓舞。

虚拟语气:
I would rejoice if I won the lottery. = 如果我中了彩票，我会欣喜若狂。
She would have rejoiced if she had known sooner. = 如果她早点知道，她会非常高兴。

被动语态:
The news was rejoiced over by everyone. = 这个消息使每个人都欣喜若狂。(被动语态用法较少见)
His success was rejoiced at by his family. = 他的成功使他的家人感到欣喜。(被动语态用法较少见)

疑问句:
Do you rejoice at your child's achievements? = 你为孩子的成就感到高兴吗？
Will they rejoice at the good news? = 他们会为这个好消息而高兴吗？


否定句:
I did not rejoice at his failure. = 我并没有为他失败而高兴。
They do not rejoice in other people's misfortunes. = 他们不会为别人的不幸而高兴。


PHRASE->
rejoice in = 为…而高兴
rejoice over = 为…而庆贺
rejoice at = 为…而欣喜
rejoice with = 与…一起庆祝
to rejoice one's heart = 使某人欣喜若狂
