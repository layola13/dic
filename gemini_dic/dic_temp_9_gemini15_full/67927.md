facet:/ˈfæsɪt/| n.|方面，刻面，小平面|A facet is one side or aspect of a subject, problem, or situation.  It can also refer to a small, flat, polished surface on a gem. |例句：The report examines every facet of the problem.  这份报告考察了这个问题的各个方面。

例句：The diamond has 58 facets. 这颗钻石有58个刻面。


近义词：aspect, side, phase, angle|反义词：whole, entirety
