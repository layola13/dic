dealth:/delθ/| v.|处理，分配，给予，死亡| dealt (过去式), dealt (过去分词)  |The word "dealt" is the past tense and past participle of the verb "deal." It has various meanings depending on the context.  It can refer to the distribution or handling of something,  the act of engaging in a transaction or negotiation, or the act of delivering a blow or a card. It can also mean to die or be killed, though this usage is less common.  |例句：He dealt the cards fairly. 他公平地发牌了。  The company dealt with the crisis effectively. 公司有效地处理了危机。 He dealt a blow to his opponent. 他给了对手一击。  |近义词：handle, manage, distribute, administer, dispense,  dispose of,  kill, die. |反义词：neglect, ignore, fail to address, live.
