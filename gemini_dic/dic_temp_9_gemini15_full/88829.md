hogging:/ˈhɒɡɪŋ/|/ˈhɑːɡɪŋ/| vt. n.|霸占；贪婪地吃；独占|To use or take more than your fair share of something.  |例句：He was hogging the conversation. 他一直在霸占谈话。
He was hogging all the food. 他把所有的食物都吃光了。|近义词：monopolize, dominate, grab, gobble|反义词：share, distribute, relinquish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hogs the blankets every night. = 他每晚都霸占着毯子。
They are hogging all the resources. = 他们独占了所有的资源。

一般过去时:
She hogged the spotlight during the presentation. = 她在演示过程中抢尽了风头。
He hogged the road, forcing other cars to slow down. = 他霸占着道路，迫使其他车辆减速。

现在完成时:
They have been hogging the bathroom for an hour. = 他们已经霸占了浴室一个小时了。
She has hogged the conversation all evening. = 她整个晚上都在霸占着谈话。

过去完成时:
He had hogged all the cookies before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他已经把所有的饼干都吃光了。
They had hogged the best seats, leaving us with the worst. = 他们已经占了最好的座位，把最差的留给了我们。

将来时:
I predict he will hog the microphone again at the meeting. = 我预测他会在会议上再次霸占麦克风。
She will hog the computer all day if we don't stop her. = 如果我们不阻止她，她会一整天都霸占着电脑。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't hogged the resources, the project would have been completed on time. = 如果他没有霸占资源，项目就能按时完成了。
I wish he wouldn't hog the remote control all the time. = 我希望他不要总是霸占遥控器。

被动语态:
The resources were hogged by the larger companies. = 资源被大公司垄断了。

疑问句:

否定句:
