habitan:/ˈhæbɪtænt/| n. |居民，栖息者 |A habitant is an inhabitant of a particular place.  It often refers to residents of a rural area or a specific type of environment. |Example: The small village boasts a friendly community of long-time habitants.  这个小村庄拥有一个友好的长期居民社区。The desert habitants are well-adapted to the harsh climate. 沙漠居民很好地适应了恶劣的气候。|近义词：inhabitant, resident, dweller |反义词：visitor, stranger |名词
