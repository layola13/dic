reestimates:/ˌriːˈɛstɪmeɪts/| v.|重新估算，重新评估|To estimate again; to make a new calculation of the value, size, or amount of something.|The committee reestimates the project's budget after discovering unforeseen costs. 委员会在发现意外成本后重新估算了项目的预算。|recalculate, reassess, revalue|underestimate, overestimate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reestimate the project cost every quarter. = 他们每个季度都会重新估算项目成本。
The company reestimates its market share annually. = 公司每年都会重新评估其市场份额。


一般过去时:
The engineer reestimated the bridge's load capacity. = 工程师重新估算了桥梁的承载能力。
We reestimated the time needed to complete the task. = 我们重新估算了完成任务所需的时间。


现在完成时:
They have reestimated the damage caused by the storm. = 他们已经重新评估了风暴造成的损失。
The government has reestimated the population of the city. = 政府已经重新估算了该城市的人口。


过去完成时:
After reviewing the data, they had reestimated the profits. = 在审查数据后，他们重新估算了利润。
Before the final report, they had reestimated the project timeline. = 在最终报告之前，他们已经重新估算了项目时间表。


将来时:
They will reestimate the value of the property next week. = 他们下周将重新估算房产的价值。
The team will reestimate the project's completion date. = 团队将重新估算项目的完成日期。


虚拟语气:
If we had more information, we would reestimate the budget. = 如果我们有更多信息，我们会重新估算预算。
It would be wise to reestimate the risks involved. = 重新评估所涉及的风险将是明智的。


被动语态:
The budget was reestimated due to rising material costs. = 由于材料成本上涨，预算被重新估算。

否定句:
