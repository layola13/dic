coacher:/ˈkoʊtʃər/| n.|教练，辅导员|A coacher is a person who trains or instructs someone, especially in sports or a particular skill.|例句：My soccer coacher is very strict but also very supportive. 我的足球教练很严格，但也非常支持我。|近义词：trainer, instructor, tutor, mentor|反义词：trainee, student


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The coacher trains the athletes every day. = 教练每天训练运动员。
He coaches the team in strategy. = 他指导球队制定策略。


一般过去时:
The coacher coached the winning team last year. = 教练去年执教了获胜的球队。
She coached me on my presentation skills. = 她指导我练习演讲技巧。


现在完成时:
The coacher has trained many successful athletes. = 教练已经培养了许多成功的运动员。
He has coached the team for five years. = 他执教这支球队五年了。


过去完成时:

She had coached him extensively before the competition. = 比赛前她对他进行了大量的指导。


将来时:
