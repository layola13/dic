gandering:/ˈɡændərɪŋ/| n.|闲逛；漫步；漫游|The act of wandering or strolling idly; aimless rambling.|例句：He spent the afternoon gandering through the park. 他下午在公园里闲逛。|近义词：strolling, rambling, wandering, meandering|反义词：hurrying, rushing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is gandering through the fields. = 他正在田野里闲逛。
They are gandering aimlessly around the city. = 他们在城里漫无目的地闲逛。

一般过去时:
She gandered along the beach yesterday. = 她昨天沿着海滩漫步。
We gandered through the antique shops. = 我们在古董店闲逛。

现在完成时:
I have been gandering for hours. = 我已经闲逛了好几个小时了。
They have gandered all over town. = 他们已经在镇上四处闲逛了。

过去完成时:
He had gandered for miles before he found the path. = 他走了好几英里才找到路。
They had gandered through the forest before it started to rain. = 下雨前他们已经在森林里闲逛了。

将来时:
We will be gandering through the countryside tomorrow. = 我们明天将在乡下漫步。
She will gander around the market. = 她将在市场上闲逛。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would gander along the river. = 如果我有时间，我会沿着河边散步。
I wish I could gander through those mountains. = 我真希望我能去那些山里闲逛。

被动语态: (Gandering is rarely used in the passive voice)  The passive voice is unusual and unnatural for this verb.

疑问句:
Are you gandering around without a purpose? = 你漫无目的地闲逛吗？
Where have you been gandering all day? = 你今天整天都在哪里闲逛？

否定句:
He didn't gander around much this weekend. = 他这个周末没有怎么闲逛。
They won't be gandering in the park today. = 他们今天不会在公园里闲逛。
