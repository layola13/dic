dandyism:/ˈdændiɪzəm/| n.| 紈绔习气；花花公子习气；讲究穿着打扮|The excessive concern with one's appearance and stylish dress;  a lifestyle characterized by elegance, refinement, and often an affected manner of speech and behavior.  It often implies superficiality and a lack of substance.|例句：His dandyism was evident in his impeccably tailored suits and perfectly polished shoes.  他的纨绔习气体现在他无可挑剔的定制西装和擦得锃亮的皮鞋上。|近义词：foppery, affectation,  fashionableness|反义词：slovenliness, simplicity, plainness|名词
