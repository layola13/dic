love-sick:/ˈlʌvˌsɪk/| adj.|恋爱中的，相思病的，痴情的|Extremely lovesick; affected by or showing the symptoms of lovesickness.  Characterized by an intense longing for someone.|例句：She was lovesick and pined for him constantly. 她得了相思病，不停地思念着他。

例句：He wrote lovesick poems to his beloved. 他为他的爱人写下了充满相思之情的诗歌。


近义词： lovestruck, enamored, infatuated, smitten

反义词：indifferent, apathetic, unconcerned


词源:  The word "lovesick" combines "love" and "sick."  "Sick" in this context doesn't refer to physical illness, but rather to a metaphorical illness caused by intense romantic longing.  The term emerged in the late Middle Ages, reflecting the romantic ideals of the time.
