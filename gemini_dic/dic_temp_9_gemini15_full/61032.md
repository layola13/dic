elitisms:/ɪˈliːtɪzəm/| n.|精英主义；精英统治|The belief that a society or system should be led by an elite.  It often involves the attitudes and behaviors of those who believe themselves to be superior and deserving of special privileges.  This can manifest in various forms, from political systems to social interactions.|例句：His elitisms were evident in his disdain for those from less privileged backgrounds.  他的精英主义在他对出身贫寒者的轻蔑中显而易见。|近义词：classism, snobbery,  superiority complex|反义词：egalitarianism, populism, democracy
