demigrate:/ˈdɛmɪɡreɪt/ | v. | 迁徙，移居(尤指大批人口的迁移) | To migrate, especially in large numbers.  The term often implies a less organized or less permanent migration than emigration or immigration.  It can also suggest a movement of a population to a less desirable location. | Example:  Large numbers of people demigrated to the fertile valley after the drought. 大批人口在旱灾后迁徙到肥沃的山谷。


Example: The rural population demigrated to the cities in search of work.  农村人口为了寻找工作而迁移到城市。


近义词:migrate, move, relocate, emigrate (though less precise and formal) | 反义词: remain, stay, immigrate (in a reverse sense) | 词性: verb

mass demigration: 大规模迁移

internal demigration: 国内迁移
