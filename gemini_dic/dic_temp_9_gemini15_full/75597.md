fuelled:/ˈfjuːəld/ | vt. | 供给…燃料；激励；刺激 | to supply something with fuel; to increase the intensity or strength of something. | 例句：The car was fuelled with petrol. 这辆车加的是汽油。  The news fuelled the already intense debate.  这条新闻加剧了本来就激烈的辩论。 | 近义词：powered, energized, stimulated | 反义词：depleted, extinguished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine fuels the car. = 引擎驱动汽车。
He fuels his body with healthy food. = 他用健康的食物滋养身体。


一般过去时:
They fuelled the fire with wood. = 他们用木头生火。
The discovery fuelled his ambition. = 这个发现激发了他的雄心。


现在完成时:
The protests have fuelled social unrest. = 抗议活动加剧了社会动荡。
This success has fuelled his confidence. = 这次成功增强了他的信心。


过去完成时:
The rumours had fuelled public anxiety. = 谣言已加剧了公众的焦虑。
His previous experience had fuelled his determination. = 他之前的经验增强了他的决心。


将来时:
This policy will fuel economic growth. = 这项政策将促进经济增长。
The new evidence will fuel further investigation. = 新证据将促使进一步调查。


虚拟语气:
If we fuelled the car properly, we wouldn't have broken down. = 如果我们正确地给汽车加油，我们就不会抛锚了。
If this project fuelled my creativity, I would be happier. = 如果这个项目激发我的创造力，我会更快乐。


被动语态:
The plane was fuelled before take-off. = 飞机在起飞前加过油。
The debate was fuelled by media reports. = 辩论因媒体报道而升温。


疑问句:
What fuels your passion for art? = 什么激发你对艺术的热情？
How is the rocket fuelled? = 火箭用什么燃料？


否定句:
The lack of support didn't fuel his enthusiasm. = 缺乏支持并没有激发他的热情。
The company didn't fuel the project with enough resources. = 公司没有为该项目提供足够的资源。
