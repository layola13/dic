ringettes:/rɪŋˈɛts/| n.|女子冰球(一种使用六名球員的冰球比賽)|A ringette is a team sport played on ice, similar to ice hockey, but with a ring instead of a puck and different rules.  It is primarily played by women and girls. The objective is to shoot the ring into the opponent's net.|The local ringette league is very competitive.|近义词：ice hockey (but with key differences)|反义词：无
