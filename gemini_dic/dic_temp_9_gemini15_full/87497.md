heterochlamydeous:/ˌhɛtərəʊˈklæmɪdiəs/| adj.| 具有异被花的；（花被）异形的 | Having sepals and petals that are different in shape, size, or color.  This refers to flowers where the calyx (sepals) and corolla (petals) are distinctly different.  |Example: Many dicotyledonous plants exhibit heterochlamydeous flowers, showcasing a clear distinction between the sepals and petals.|近义词：differently-clad, dissimilar-petaled|反义词：homochlamydeous
