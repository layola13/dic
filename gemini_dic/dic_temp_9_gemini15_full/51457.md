detonates:/ˈdɛtəneɪts/| v.|爆炸；使爆炸；突然爆发|To explode or cause to explode violently.  The verb can also refer to a sudden, forceful outburst or revelation.|The bomb detonated at noon. 炸弹在中午爆炸了。
The news of his betrayal detonated a storm of anger within the family.  他背叛的消息在家庭内部引发了愤怒的风暴。|explode, burst, erupt|implode, defuse, neutralize|动词
