sailorizing:/ˈseɪləraɪzɪŋ/| v.|使具有水手特征；使适应航海；使像水手|To give something the characteristics of a sailor or sailing; to adapt something for use at sea; to make something resemble a sailor. |例句：The boat was sailorized for the transatlantic voyage. 这艘船经过改装，适合跨大西洋航行。


例句：He sailorized his language, peppering it with nautical terms. 他用航海术语来丰富他的语言，使其更具有水手特色。


近义词：nauticalize, marinize


反义词：landlubberize (非正式，使失去水手特征)


词性:动词
