flisk:/flɪsk/| n.|轻快地移动；轻弹；轻拂|A quick, light movement or flick.  It can refer to a quick darting movement, a light flick of something, or a brief, lively piece of music.|例句：The bird flisk away into the bushes. 鸟儿轻快地飞进灌木丛中。The magician flisk the card across the table. 魔术师轻弹卡片穿过桌子。|近义词：flick, dart, flit|反义词：lumber, plod
