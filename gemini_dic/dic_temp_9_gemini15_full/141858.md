orchestrates:/ˈɔːrkɪstreɪts/ | vt. | 精心策划；协调；安排；指挥（乐队） | To plan and organize something complex, especially in a skillful way.  |例句：The general orchestrated a brilliant military campaign.  将军精心策划了一场辉煌的军事战役。

The conductor orchestrated the symphony beautifully. 指挥优美地指挥了这首交响乐。

| 近义词：coordinate, arrange, organize, manage | 反义词：disorganize, disrupt, confuse | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She orchestrates all the events for the company. = 她为公司策划所有活动。
He orchestrates his schedule meticulously. = 他一丝不苟地安排自己的日程。

一般过去时:
The team orchestrated a successful product launch. = 团队精心策划了一次成功的产品发布。
They orchestrated a surprise party for her birthday. = 他们为她的生日策划了一个惊喜派对。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

They had orchestrated a complex deception. = 他们策划了一个复杂的骗局。

虚拟语气:
