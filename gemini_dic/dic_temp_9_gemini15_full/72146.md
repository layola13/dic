floodwater:/ˈflʌdˌwɔːtər/|/ˈflʌdˌwɒtər/| n. |洪水；泛滥的河水 |Water overflowing its normal confines, especially from a river, lake, or sea, during a flood.  Floodwater often carries debris and can cause significant damage. |例句：The floodwater reached the rooftops of many houses. 洪水淹没了许多房屋的屋顶。

例句：The streets were inundated with floodwater after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，街道被洪水淹没了。


近义词：high water, deluge, inundation

反义词：dry land, drought


词性：名词
