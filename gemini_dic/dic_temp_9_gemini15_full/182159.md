remodeled:/ˌriˈmɑːdəld/| adj.|重新装修的；改建的|Having been remodeled or renovated; changed in appearance or structure.|例句：The remodeled kitchen is stunning.  重新装修的厨房令人惊艳。|例句：Their remodeled house is much more spacious. 他们重新装修的房子宽敞多了。|近义词：renovated, refurbished, redesigned|反义词：original, unrenovated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The house is remodeled. = 房子已经重新装修了。
They remodel the kitchen every year. = 他们每年都重新装修厨房。

一般过去时:
We remodeled the bathroom last year. = 我们去年重新装修了浴室。
She remodeled her entire apartment. = 她重新装修了她的整套公寓。

现在完成时:
