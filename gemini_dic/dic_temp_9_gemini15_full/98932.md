intents:/ɪnˈtents/ | n. |意图；目的；意向 |The speaker's intents were clear from his tone.  说话者的意图从他的语气中可以清楚地看出。| purpose, aim, intention | indifference, apathy | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your intents? = 你的意图是什么？
What is her intent behind this action? = 她这么做的意图是什么？


一般过去时:
What were their intents in starting the company? = 他们创办这家公司有何意图？
What was his intent when he said that? = 他那样说的时候意图是什么？


现在完成时:
What have your intents been lately? = 你最近的意图是什么？
What has been her intent throughout this project? = 在整个项目中，她的意图是什么？


过去完成时:
What had been their intents before the incident? = 事发前他们的意图是什么？
What had his intent been leading up to the decision? = 在做出决定之前，他的意图是什么？


将来时:
What will your intents be in the future? = 你未来的意图是什么？
What will her intent be regarding the new proposal? = 她对新的提案有何意图？


虚拟语气:
If I knew their intents, I would act differently. = 如果我知道他们的意图，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I knew his intent before I responded. = 我希望在我回应之前就知道他的意图。


被动语态:
Their intents were misunderstood. = 他们的意图被误解了。
His intent was misinterpreted by the audience. = 他的意图被听众误解了。


疑问句:
What are the intents of this policy? = 这项政策的意图是什么？
What is the intent behind this change? = 这一变化背后的意图是什么？


否定句:
Their intents were not clear. = 他们的意图并不明确。
His intent wasn't malicious. = 他的意图并非恶意。



PHRASE->
with the intent to = 打算...
intent and purpose = 目的和意图
good intent = 善意
with malicious intent = 恶意地
to all intents and purposes = 事实上
