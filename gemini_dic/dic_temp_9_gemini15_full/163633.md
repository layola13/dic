predators:/ˈprɛdə(r)z/| n.|掠食者，捕食者|An animal that naturally preys on others.|例句：Lions are predators that hunt zebras. 狮子是捕猎斑马的掠食者。|例句： Sharks are apex predators in the ocean. 鲨鱼是海洋中的顶级掠食者。|近义词：hunters, carnivores,  killers|反义词：prey, victim


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Predators hunt their prey. = 掠食者猎捕它们的猎物。
The lion stalks its prey. = 狮子潜伏着猎物。


一般过去时:
The wolves hunted deer last night. = 狼群昨晚猎捕了鹿。
The eagle captured a fish. = 老鹰抓到了一条鱼。


现在完成时:
The cat has caught many mice. = 猫已经抓到很多老鼠了。
The sharks have eaten many fish. = 鲨鱼已经吃了很多鱼。


过去完成时:
The bear had already eaten before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，熊已经吃过了。
The birds had flown away before the predators appeared. = 在掠食者出现之前，鸟儿们已经飞走了。


将来时:
The hunters will track the bears tomorrow. = 猎人明天将追踪熊。
The wolves will hunt in the forest tonight. = 狼群今晚将在森林里狩猎。


虚拟语气:
If I were a predator, I would hunt efficiently. = 如果我是掠食者，我会有效率地狩猎。
If the birds had not flown away, they would have been eaten. = 如果鸟儿没有飞走，它们就会被吃掉了。


被动语态:
The deer was hunted by the wolves. = 鹿被狼猎杀了。
The fish was caught by the eagle. = 鱼被老鹰抓住了。


疑问句:
What kind of predators live in this area? = 这个地区住着什么类型的掠食者？
Are lions apex predators? = 狮子是顶级掠食者吗？


否定句:
The lions did not hunt today. = 狮子今天没有狩猎。
The birds were not caught by the predators. = 鸟儿没有被掠食者捕获。


PHRASE->
apex predators: 顶级掠食者
natural predators: 天敌
predator-prey relationship: 捕食者-猎物关系
human predators: 人类掠食者（通常指罪犯）
predator control: 掠食者控制（例如，为了保护其他物种）
