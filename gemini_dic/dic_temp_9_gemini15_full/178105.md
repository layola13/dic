recoagulation:/ˌriːkoʊæɡjuˈleɪʃən/| n. |再凝固；重新凝结|The process by which blood or other fluid clots again after having been prevented from clotting.  This often involves the reactivation of clotting factors. |Example:  The recoagulation of the blood was crucial to stopping the bleeding. 再凝固血液对于止血至关重要。

The rapid recoagulation of the blood prevented further blood loss. 血液的快速再凝固阻止了进一步的失血。|近义词：re-clotting, clotting again|反义词：defibrillation, anticoagulation|名词
