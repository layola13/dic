obituary:/əˈbɪtʃuəri/| n. |讣告，死亡公告 | A notice of a person's death, especially one in a newspaper, often including a brief biography. | The newspaper published an obituary for the renowned scientist. 报纸刊登了这位著名科学家的讣告。| eulogy, necrology |  |  noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The newspaper publishes obituaries daily. = 报纸每天刊登讣告。
An obituary provides information about the deceased. = 讣告提供有关死者信息。


一般过去时:
