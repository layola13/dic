expenders:/ɪkˈspɛndərz/| n. |花费者；支出者；消费者|Those who spend money or resources.  People or entities that use up resources. The term often implies significant or excessive spending.|例句：The government are major expenders in the infrastructure sector. 政府是基础设施部门的主要支出者。|例句：Large corporations are often the biggest expenders of advertising budgets.大型公司通常是广告预算的最大花费者。|近义词：spenders, consumers, users|反义词：savers, hoarders


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government expends billions on defense. = 政府在国防上花费数十亿美元。
They expend a lot of energy on their hobbies. = 他们在爱好上花费很多精力。

一般过去时:
The company expended all its resources on the project. = 公司将所有资源都花在了这个项目上。
They expended considerable effort to find a solution. = 他们为寻找解决方案付出了相当大的努力。

现在完成时:
We have expended all our savings on the house. = 我们已将所有积蓄都花在了房子上。
They have expended much time researching the topic. = 他们花费了大量时间研究这个话题。

过去完成时:
They had expended all their ammunition before the enemy surrendered. = 在敌人投降之前，他们已经耗尽了所有弹药。
She had expended her energy on the race and felt exhausted. = 她把精力都花在了比赛上，感到精疲力竭。


将来时:
We will expend more resources on renewable energy in the future. = 我们将来会在可再生能源上投入更多资源。
The company will expend significant funds on marketing next year. = 公司明年将在市场营销上投入大量资金。


虚拟语气:
If they expended more effort, they might succeed. = 如果他们付出更多努力，他们可能会成功。
I wish I had not expended so much time on that project. = 我真希望我没有在这个项目上花费这么多时间。


被动语态:
Millions of dollars were expended on the bridge project. = 数百万美元花在了桥梁项目上。
A great deal of effort was expended in trying to save the company. = 为拯救公司付出了巨大的努力。


疑问句:
How much money did they expend on the trip? = 他们这次旅行花了多少钱？
How much time will be expended on this task? =这项任务需要花费多少时间？


否定句:
They did not expend much time on planning. = 他们没有花太多时间做计划。
The company did not expend any more funds on the project. = 公司没有在这个项目上再投入任何资金。
