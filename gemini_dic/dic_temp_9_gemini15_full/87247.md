hereunto:/ˌhɪərˈʌntuː/| adv.|到此，至此，于此|This adverb indicates something is added or appended to a previously mentioned document or statement. It implies a connection or reference to something preceding.|例句：All exhibits hereunto attached are considered part of the evidence.  所有附于此处的证据都被视为证据的一部分。|近义词：herein, hereto, therewith|反义词：henceforth, hereafter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The document refers to matters hereunto attached. = 文件提及附于此处的相关事宜。
This addendum is hereunto appended. = 此补充说明书附于此。


一般过去时:
The court considered all documents hereunto appended. = 法院审阅了所有附于此处的文件。
The agreement referred to clauses hereunto attached. = 该协议提及附于此处的条款。


现在完成时:
We have attached all documents hereunto required. = 我们已经附上了所有此处要求的文件。
The lawyer has submitted all evidence hereunto pertaining. = 律师已提交所有与此相关的证据。


过去完成时:
The judge had already reviewed the exhibits hereunto submitted. = 法官已经审查了提交于此处的证据。
They had previously appended the documents hereunto referenced. = 他们之前已经附加了此处引用的文件。


将来时:
We will attach all necessary documents hereunto. = 我们将附加所有此处必要的文档。
The contract will specify all conditions hereunto related. = 合同将规定所有与此相关的条件。


虚拟语气:
If all documents were hereunto attached, the case would be complete. = 如果所有文件都附于此处，案件就完整了。
I wish all relevant information were hereunto appended. = 我希望所有相关信息都附于此处。


被动语态:
All relevant documents were hereunto attached by the clerk. = 所有相关文件都由书记员附于此处。
The necessary exhibits will be hereunto appended by the end of the week. = 本周结束前，必要的证据将附于此处。


疑问句:
Are all necessary documents hereunto attached? = 所有必要的文档都附于此处了吗？
Is the addendum properly hereunto appended? = 补充说明书是否已正确地附于此处？


否定句:
The contract did not include the clauses hereunto mentioned. = 合同未包含此处提到的条款。
No further documents are hereunto required. = 此处不再需要其他文件。
