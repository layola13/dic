quasi-damaged:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈdæmɪdʒd/| adj.| 准损坏的，近乎损坏的 |  Describes something that is almost damaged or shows signs of being close to damage, but isn't fully or completely damaged. It suggests a state between undamaged and severely damaged.  |例句：The car was quasi-damaged after the accident; some scratches but nothing major.|近义词：partially damaged, slightly damaged, near-damaged|反义词：undamaged, intact
