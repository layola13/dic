myopism:/ˈmaɪəʊpɪzəm/| n. |近视；缺乏远见；目光短浅|Nearsightedness, lack of foresight; shortsightedness.  It refers to a defect of vision where distant objects appear blurred because the eye focuses images in front of the retina instead of on it.  It can also refer to a lack of imagination, foresight, or intellectual depth. |例句：His myopism prevented him from seeing the long-term consequences of his actions.  他的近视（缺乏远见）使他看不到自己行为的长期后果。|近义词：shortsightedness, nearsightedness, lack of foresight, tunnel vision |反义词：farsightedness, foresight, prescience
