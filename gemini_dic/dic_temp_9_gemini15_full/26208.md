buttercup: /ˈbʌtərkʌp/ | n. |毛茛，金凤花 |A buttercup is a type of flowering plant with bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers.  They are common wildflowers found in meadows and fields. |例句：The meadow was carpeted with buttercups.  这片草地长满了毛茛。

例句：Children often pick buttercups and hold them under their chins to see if they like butter. 孩子们经常采摘毛茛并将它们放在下巴下，看看他们是否喜欢黄油。


近义词：crowfoot (a common name for some buttercup species)


反义词：无


词性：名词
