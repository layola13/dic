rampager:/ræmˈpeɪdʒər/| n. |暴怒者，破坏者|A rampager is someone who behaves in a violent and destructive way, often uncontrollably.  They engage in a rampage, causing chaos and damage. |例句：The rampager smashed windows and overturned cars during his furious outburst.  这个暴怒者在他暴怒的爆发中砸碎了窗户，推翻了汽车。|近义词：wrecker, destroyer, vandal, hooligan |反义词：peacemaker, conciliator
