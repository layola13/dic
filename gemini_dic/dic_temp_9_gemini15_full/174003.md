quasi-technical:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈtɛknɪkəl/| adj.|准技术的，似技术的|Approaching or resembling a technical subject but not fully technical; often used to describe something that has some technical aspects but is also accessible to non-experts.  It suggests a blend of technical information and a simplified explanation.|例句：The article presented a quasi-technical explanation of quantum physics, making it understandable to a wider audience. 这篇文章对量子物理学做了一个准技术的解释，使其更容易被更广泛的读者理解。


例句：The software's documentation was surprisingly quasi-technical, avoiding overly complex jargon while still explaining the functionalities effectively. 该软件的文档出奇地接近技术性，避免了过于复杂的术语，同时仍然有效地解释了功能。|近义词：semi-technical, technical-sounding,  popularized technical|反义词：highly technical, purely technical, layman's
