canyon:/ˈkænjən/| n. |峡谷，深谷 |A canyon is a deep, narrow valley with steep sides, often carved by a river.  |例句：The Grand Canyon is a breathtaking natural wonder.  大峡谷是一个令人叹为观止的自然奇观。

例句：We hiked down into the canyon to reach the river. 我们徒步走下峡谷去河边。


近义词：gorge, ravine, chasm |反义词：plain, plateau


词源:  From Spanish cañón, from earlier *canón, from Latin canna "reed, cane, pipe", referring to the shape of the valley.  The word entered English in the 19th century, largely through the exploration and naming of canyons in the American West.
