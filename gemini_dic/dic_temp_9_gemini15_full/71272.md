fixable:/ˈfɪksəbl/ | adj. | 可修复的，可解决的 | capable of being repaired or corrected; able to be fixed.  |  The problem is fixable; we just need the right tools.  这个问题是可以解决的；我们只需要合适的工具。 The broken vase is unfortunately not fixable.  不幸的是，这个破碎的花瓶无法修复。| repairable, mendable, correctable | irreparable, unfixable

词源:
fixable is derived from the verb "fix" + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).  The verb "fix" itself has roots in Old French and Latin, ultimately related to ideas of making firm or secure.
