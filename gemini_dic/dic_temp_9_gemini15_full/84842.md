hang-together: /ˌhæŋ təˈɡɛðər/ | v. | 齐心协力；团结一致；互相支撑 | To remain united or supportive of each other; to stay together as a group.  |  The team hung together despite setbacks.  团队尽管遇到挫折，仍然团结一致。|近义词：cohere, stick together, unite | 反义词：disintegrate, fall apart, separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hang together through thick and thin. = 他们风雨同舟。
We hang together to achieve our goals. = 我们团结一致以实现目标。


一般过去时:
The group hung together during the crisis. = 在危机期间，这个团队团结一致。
They hung together despite the disagreements. = 尽管意见不合，他们仍然团结一致。


现在完成时:
We have hung together for many years. = 我们已经团结了很多年了。
The community has hung together through countless hardships. = 这个社区经历了无数的磨难，仍然团结一心。


过去完成时:
They had hung together before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们一直团结一致。
The company had hung together even when facing bankruptcy. = 即使面临破产，公司也一直团结一致。


将来时:
We will hang together no matter what happens. = 不管发生什么，我们都会团结一致。
They will hang together to fight for their rights. = 他们将团结一致，为争取自己的权利而斗争。


虚拟语气:
If they hung together, they would succeed. = 如果他们团结一致，他们就会成功。
I wish they had hung together during the difficult times. = 我希望他们在困难时期能够团结一致。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes a collective action.)


疑问句:
Will they hang together in the face of adversity? = 面对逆境，他们会团结一致吗？
Do you think they can hang together for the long term? = 你认为他们能长期团结一致吗？


否定句:
They didn't hang together during the argument. = 他们在争吵中没有团结一致。
The team failed to hang together, leading to their defeat. = 团队未能团结一致，导致了他们的失败。


PHRASE->

hang together to support each other = 互相支持，团结一致
