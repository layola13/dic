cash-starved: /ˈkæʃstɑːvd/ | adj. | 资金短缺的，缺钱的 |  Lacking sufficient cash; financially strapped.  |例句：The cash-starved startup struggled to meet payroll.  这家资金短缺的初创公司难以支付工资。

例句：Many small businesses are cash-starved due to the economic downturn.  由于经济低迷，许多小型企业都面临资金短缺的问题。


近义词：financially strapped, broke, impoverished, insolvent, penniless, impecunious

反义词：wealthy, affluent, well-funded, flush with cash


词性：形容词
