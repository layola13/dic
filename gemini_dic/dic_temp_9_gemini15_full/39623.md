consentive:/kənˈsɛntɪv/| adj.| 同意的，赞成的； consentient 的 | 表示同意或赞成的；愿意接受的 | The consentive atmosphere made the negotiation easier.  同意的气氛使谈判更容易进行。|近义词：agreeable, consenting, approving | 反义词：disagreeing, dissenting, objecting


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is consentive to the plan. = 他同意这个计划。
They are consentive to the changes. = 他们同意这些改变。


一般过去时:
She was consentive to his proposal. = 她同意了他的提议。
We were consentive to the terms of the contract. = 我们同意合同的条款。


现在完成时:
They have been consentive throughout the process. = 他们在整个过程中一直都表示同意。
We have been consentive to their requests. = 我们一直同意他们的要求。


过去完成时:
He had been consentive until the last minute. = 直到最后一刻他还都表示同意。
She had been consentive to the idea before changing her mind. = 在改变主意之前，她一直都赞同这个想法。


将来时:
They will be consentive to the decision. = 他们将会同意这个决定。
We will be consentive to your suggestions. = 我们将会同意你的建议。


虚拟语气:
If he were consentive, we could proceed. = 如果他同意，我们就可以继续了。
I wish she were consentive to my plan. = 我希望她能同意我的计划。


被动语态:
His suggestion was not consented to by the board.  他的建议没有得到董事会的同意。(Note:  "consentive" itself isn't typically used in the passive voice.  The related verb "consent" is used instead.)
The proposal was consented to by the majority.  这个提议得到了大多数人的同意。


疑问句:
Is everyone consentive to the new policy? = 大家都同意新政策吗？
Are they consentive to the terms? = 他们同意这些条款吗？


否定句:
He was not consentive to the idea. = 他不同意这个主意。
She is not consentive to your request. = 她不同意你的请求。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "consentive". The word itself is relatively uncommon.  Phrases using the related word "consent" would be more appropriate.  For example:


with one consent = 一致同意
give consent = 同意
obtain consent = 获得同意
seek consent = 寻求同意
withhold consent = 拒绝同意
