otoscope:/ˈoʊtəskoʊp/| n.|耳镜，检耳镜|An otoscope is a medical instrument used to examine the external auditory canal and tympanic membrane (eardrum). It typically consists of a magnifying lens, a light source, and a speculum that is inserted into the ear canal.|例句：The doctor used an otoscope to examine my ear. 医生用耳镜检查了我的耳朵。The nurse carefully inserted the otoscope speculum into the patient's ear canal. 护士小心地将耳镜的窥镜插入病人的耳道。|近义词：ear speculum|反义词：无
