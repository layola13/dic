over-inflated:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度膨胀的；被夸大的 | Excessively inflated; exaggerated or pretentious.  | The over-inflated housing market led to a crash.  过度膨胀的房地产市场导致了崩盘。 The company's claims about its new product were over-inflated. 公司对其新产品的宣传过于夸大。 | inflated, exaggerated, hyped | deflated, understated, realistic | 形容词
