intreat:/ɪnˈtriːt/| vt.|恳求；哀求；乞求|to ask someone earnestly or beg someone for something |例句：I entreat you to reconsider your decision. 我恳求你重新考虑你的决定。

例句：She entreated him to help her. 她恳求他帮助她。


近义词：beg, implore, plead, supplicate| 反义词：command, order, demand| 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I entreat you to listen. = 我恳求你听我说。
He entreats his boss for a raise. = 他恳求老板给他加薪。

一般过去时:
She entreated him for mercy. = 她恳求他怜悯她。
They entreated the king to pardon the prisoner. = 他们恳求国王赦免囚犯。

现在完成时:
I have entreated them many times, but they refuse. = 我已经多次恳求他们了，但他们拒绝了。
She has entreated God for help. = 她一直在向上帝祈求帮助。

过去完成时:
He had entreated her forgiveness before he died. = 他死前曾恳求过她的原谅。
They had entreated the authorities for assistance but to no avail. = 他们曾向当局恳求过援助，但毫无结果。

将来时:
I will entreat him to change his mind. = 我将恳求他改变主意。
We shall entreat the committee for a further extension. = 我们将恳求委员会进一步延期。

虚拟语气:
I would entreat you to be more careful. = 我恳求你更小心一些。
If I were you, I would entreat him for help. = 如果我是你，我会恳求他帮忙。

被动语态:
He was entreated to speak on the matter. = 他被恳求就此事发表讲话。
The judge was entreated to show leniency. = 法官被恳求宽大处理。

疑问句:
Will you entreat him to come? = 你会恳求他来吗？
Did you entreat her for forgiveness? = 你恳求她原谅了吗？


否定句:
I did not entreat him to do that. = 我没有恳求他那样做。
She wouldn't entreat him even if he begged. = 即使他苦苦哀求，她也不会恳求他。


PHRASE->
entreat earnestly = 恳切地恳求
entreat humbly = 谦卑地恳求
entreat repeatedly = 反复恳求
entreat someone to do something = 恳求某人做某事
entreat for mercy = 恳求怜悯


词源:
Old French *entraiter (from Latin *interrogare, "to question," from inter, "between" + rogare, "to ask").  The modern meaning developed from the earlier sense of "to treat," "to converse with."
