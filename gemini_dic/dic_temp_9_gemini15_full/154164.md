perineurium:/ˌpɛrɪˈnʊəriəm/| n.|神经束膜|A layer of connective tissue surrounding a fascicle (bundle) of nerve fibers within a peripheral nerve.  It provides structural support and a barrier to the diffusion of substances. |The perineurium plays a crucial role in protecting the nerve fibers within the fascicle. |近义词：fascicular sheath|反义词：epineurium
