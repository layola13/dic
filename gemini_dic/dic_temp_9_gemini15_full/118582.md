mesotheca:/ˌmɛsoʊˈθiːkə/| n. |间皮;胸膜;心包膜|The mesotheca is a serous membrane that lines the body cavities and covers the organs within those cavities.  It plays a crucial role in reducing friction during organ movement. |例句：The mesothelioma is a cancer arising from the mesotheca.  间皮瘤是一种起源于间皮的癌症。|例句：The inflammation of the mesotheca can cause significant pain and discomfort. 间皮的炎症会导致严重的疼痛和不适。|近义词：serosa, serous membrane|反义词：无
