persuasion:/pərˈsweɪʒən/ | n. |说服；劝说；令人信服的力量 | The act of persuading someone or of being persuaded; the power to persuade.  |例句：Her persuasion was so strong that he finally agreed.  她的劝说非常有力，他最终同意了。 |例句：The salesman used persuasion and charm to sell his product. 销售员用劝说和魅力销售他的产品。|近义词：influence, inducement, coercion (if forceful), conversion |反义词：dissuasion
