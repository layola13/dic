resprinkling:/ˌriːˈsprɪŋklɪŋ/| n. | 再撒，再喷洒 | The act of sprinkling again; a second or further sprinkling.  |例句：The baker gave the cake a resprinkling of powdered sugar. 面包师再次撒上糖粉。

例句：After the initial rain, there was a resprinkling of showers throughout the afternoon. 初雨过后，下午又下了一阵阵小雨。


近义词：reapplication, second sprinkling, additional sprinkling


反义词：removal, clearing, wiping


词性:名词
