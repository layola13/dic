orb:/ɔːrb/| n.|球体；天体；眼球；光环|A round object; a sphere; specifically, a celestial body; the eyeball; a halo or circle of light.  |例句：The moon is a celestial orb. 月亮是一个天体球体。

The artist painted a golden orb of light around the angel's head.艺术家在天使的头顶画了一个金色的光环。|近义词：sphere, globe, ball, circle|反义词：point, line
