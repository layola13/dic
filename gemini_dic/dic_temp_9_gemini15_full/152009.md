patcher:/ˈpætʃər/| n. v.|修补程序，补丁程序；修补者|A patcher is a program or tool used to apply patches to software or a system.  It often automates the process of updating or fixing bugs in existing software.|例句：The game patcher automatically downloaded and installed the latest updates. 游戏补丁程序自动下载并安装了最新的更新。
The system administrator used a patcher to fix the security vulnerability. 系统管理员使用修补程序来修复安全漏洞。|近义词：updater, installer, fixer|反义词：breaker, destroyer
