nonsane: /ˈnɒnˌseɪn/ | adj. | 荒谬的，无意义的 |  Not sane; insane; mad.  Characterized by lack of sense or reason. | 例句：His nonsane ramblings were impossible to follow. = 他那些荒谬的胡言乱语根本无法理解。


例句：The idea of a cat flying a spaceship is utterly nonsane. = 猫驾驶宇宙飞船的想法完全是荒谬的。


近义词：absurd, ridiculous, illogical, irrational, insane, mad, crazy


反义词：sane, rational, logical, sensible, reasonable
