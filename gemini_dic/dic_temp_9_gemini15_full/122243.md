miszoned:/mɪsˈzoʊnd/| adj.| 错误分区，规划错误 | Incorrectly zoned;  assigned to the wrong zoning category. This typically refers to land or property that has been improperly classified for development or use according to local zoning regulations. | Example: The developer was fined for building on a miszoned lot. 开发商因在错误分区的地块上建造而被罚款。  | 近义词：improperly zoned, incorrectly designated, wrongly classified | 反义词：correctly zoned, properly zoned
