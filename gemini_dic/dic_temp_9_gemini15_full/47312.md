de-licenses:/ˌdiːˈlaɪsnsɪz/ | v. |吊销许可证；取消许可 | To revoke or withdraw a license or permit.  The government de-licensed the company for violating safety regulations.  政府因违反安全条例而吊销了该公司的许可证。|近义词：revoke, cancel, rescind |反义词：license, grant, authorize |动词
