noneligible:/ˌnɒnˈɛlɪdʒəbəl/ | adj. | 不合格的，无资格的 | Not eligible; not qualified or entitled.  This word describes someone or something that does not meet the necessary requirements or criteria for something. | Example: He was declared noneligible for the scholarship due to his low GPA. 他因为GPA低而被宣布不符合奖学金申请资格。 |近义词：ineligible, disqualified, unqualified | 反义词：eligible, qualified
