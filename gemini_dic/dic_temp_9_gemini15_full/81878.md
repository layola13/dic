grieve:/ɡriːv/| vt. vi.|悲伤，忧愁；悼念|To feel intense sorrow, especially for the death of a loved one.  It involves feeling deep sadness and distress.|例句：She grieved deeply over the loss of her pet. 她为失去宠物而深感悲痛。

例句：The family is still grieving the death of their father.  这个家庭仍在为父亲的去世而悲伤。


近义词：mourn, lament, sorrow, bewail|反义词：rejoice, celebrate, exult|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grieves for his lost friend. 他为失去的朋友感到悲伤。
They grieve the loss of their home. 他们为失去家园而悲伤。


一般过去时:
She grieved for many months after her mother's death. 她母亲去世后悲伤了好几个月。
We grieved the loss of our dog. 我们为失去我们的狗而悲伤。


现在完成时:
They have grieved over this loss for years. 他们为这场损失悲伤了好几年了。
I have grieved the loss of my childhood. 我为失去我的童年而悲伤。


过去完成时:
He had grieved for a long time before he finally started to heal. 在他最终开始痊愈之前，他已经悲伤了很长时间。
She had grieved the loss of her job before she found a new one. 在找到新工作之前，她为失去工作而悲伤。


将来时:
They will grieve deeply when they hear the news. 当他们听到这个消息时，他们会非常悲伤。
I know I will grieve the loss of my grandparents. 我知道我会为祖父母的去世而悲伤。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have grieved with her. 如果我知道，我会和她一起悲伤。
I wish I hadn't grieved so much. 我希望我没有那么悲伤。


被动语态:
The loss was deeply grieved by the community.  这个损失让整个社区深感悲痛。
His death was grieved by everyone who knew him.  认识他的人都为他的死而悲伤。


疑问句:
Does he still grieve for his wife? 他仍然为他的妻子悲伤吗？
How long did you grieve after the accident? 事故后你悲伤了多久？


否定句:
He didn't grieve openly, but his sadness was evident. 他没有公开悲伤，但他的悲伤是显而易见的。
She tried not to grieve too much, but it was difficult. 她试图不要太悲伤，但这很难。


PHRASE->
grieve over: 为...而悲伤
grieve for: 为...而悲伤
grieve the loss of: 为...的失去而悲伤
grieve at: 对...感到悲伤
to grieve deeply: 深深地悲伤
