entitled:/ɪnˈtaɪtld/| adj. |有权的；有资格的；以…为标题的 |Having the right to do or have something; giving a title or name to something. |例句：She is entitled to a pension. 她有权领取养老金。  He is entitled to free medical care. 他有权享受免费医疗。 |近义词：authorized, qualified, titled |反义词：disqualified, unauthorized


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is entitled to a discount. = 他有权享受折扣。
They are entitled to their opinions. = 他们有权表达自己的意见。

一般过去时:
She was entitled to inherit the property. = 她有权继承这处房产。
We were entitled to a refund. = 我们有权获得退款。

现在完成时:
He has been entitled to vote since he turned 18. = 自从他18岁以来，他就有投票权。
They have been entitled to benefits for years. = 他们多年来一直有资格享受福利。

过去完成时:
She had been entitled to the position before her promotion. = 在晋升之前，她就有资格担任这个职位。
They had been entitled to leave early, but they stayed late. = 他们本来可以早点离开，但他们却加班了。

将来时:
He will be entitled to a bonus if he meets his targets. = 如果他达到目标，他将有权获得奖金。
She will be entitled to maternity leave soon. = 她很快就有权享受产假。

虚拟语气:
If he were entitled to the promotion, he would accept it. = 如果他有资格晋升，他会接受的。
I wish I were entitled to a longer vacation. = 我希望我有资格享受更长的假期。

被动语态:
The book is entitled "The Adventures of Tom Sawyer". = 这本书的标题是《汤姆·索亚历险记》。
The award was entitled "The Nobel Prize in Literature". = 该奖项被称为“诺贝尔文学奖”。

疑问句:
Is he entitled to a raise? = 他有资格加薪吗？
Are they entitled to compensation? = 他们有权获得赔偿吗？

否定句:
He is not entitled to access classified information. = 他无权访问机密信息。
She was not entitled to a free upgrade. = 她无权免费升级。


PHRASE->
be entitled to = 有权…
entitled opinion = 有资格的意见
entitled to claim = 有权索赔
entitled to a share = 有权分得一份
entitled to benefits = 有权享受福利
