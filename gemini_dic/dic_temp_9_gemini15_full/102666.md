jambing:/ˈdʒæmbɪŋ/| v.|门框镶嵌；卡住；阻碍|The process of fitting a door or window frame into a wall opening.  It can also refer to something getting stuck or jammed.|例句：The carpenter spent hours jambing the new door frame into the rough opening. 木匠花了数小时将新的门框镶嵌到粗糙的洞口里。

例句：The drawer was jambing, so I had to force it open. 抽屉卡住了，所以我不得不强行把它打开。|近义词：fitting, wedging, jamming, obstructing|反义词：unblocking, freeing, releasing|词性：动词
