skip:/skɪp/ | v. n. |跳过；略过；跳跃 | To move quickly by leaping or hopping, or to omit or leave out something.  |例句：He skipped happily down the street. 他快乐地沿着街道跳跃着。
He skipped a few chapters in the book. 他跳过了书中的一些章节。|近义词：jump, omit, miss |反义词：include, consider


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I skip breakfast most days. = 我大多数日子都跳过早餐。
They skip rope during recess. = 他们在课间跳绳。

一般过去时:
She skipped school yesterday. = 她昨天逃学了。
He skipped the boring parts of the meeting. = 他跳过了会议中无聊的部分。

现在完成时:
I have skipped lunch several times this week. = 这周我已经好几次没吃午饭了。
They have skipped several steps in the process. = 他们在这个过程中跳过了几个步骤。

过去完成时:
She had skipped several questions on the test. = 她在考试中跳过了几个问题。
We had skipped the dessert course because we were full. = 我们因为吃饱了所以跳过了甜点。

将来时:
I will skip the meeting tomorrow. = 我明天将跳过会议。
He will skip dinner tonight. = 他今晚将不吃晚餐。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would skip the tutorial. = 如果我有更多时间，我会跳过教程。
I wish I could skip the long commute. = 我希望我可以跳过漫长的通勤。

被动语态:
The meeting was skipped by many attendees. = 许多与会者都缺席了会议。
Several steps in the instructions were skipped. = 说明书中跳过了几个步骤。

疑问句:
Will you skip the introduction? = 你会跳过引言吗？
Did he skip class again today? = 他今天又逃课了吗？

否定句:
I will not skip my workout today. = 我今天不会跳过我的锻炼。
She didn't skip a single detail in her report. = 她在报告中没有遗漏任何细节。
