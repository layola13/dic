crosswords:/ˈkrɒswɜːdz/| n. |纵横字谜；填字游戏|A word puzzle in which the player has to fill in words in a grid of squares, using clues given for each word. The words usually intersect, so that letters are shared between adjacent words.|I enjoy solving crosswords in the newspaper every morning. 我每天早上都喜欢解报纸上的纵横字谜。|puzzle, word game, crossword puzzle|  (无明确反义词)|名词
