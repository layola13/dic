preproving:/ˌpriːˈpruːvɪŋ/| v.|预先证明；预先验证 |The act of proving something beforehand, often to establish a basis for further action or argument. It implies a preliminary or anticipatory proof, not necessarily a conclusive one.  |例句：The team spent weeks preproving the software's stability before the official launch.  团队在正式发布前花了数周时间预先验证软件的稳定性。  |近义词：pre-testing, verifying, validating |反义词：disproving, refuting|动词
