denudements:/dɪˈnuːdmənts/| n.|剥蚀；剥露；裸露|The process of removing the covering or surface layer of something, often through natural processes like erosion or weathering.  It refers to the state of being stripped bare or exposed.|例句：The denudements of the mountain slopes were evident after the landslide. 山坡上的剥蚀在山体滑坡后很明显。
例句：The extensive denudements left the land barren and unproductive.  广泛的剥蚀使土地变得贫瘠且不生产。|近义词：erosion, stripping, exposure, denudation|反义词：covering, concealment, protection|名词
