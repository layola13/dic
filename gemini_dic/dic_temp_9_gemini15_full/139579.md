obviate:/ˈɒbvieɪt/ /ˈɑːbvieɪt/| vt.|消除，排除，避免|To remove a need or difficulty.|The new bridge obviates the need to take the long, roundabout route. 新桥消除了走漫长迂回路线的必要。|近义词：avoid, prevent, eliminate, preclude|反义词：necessitate, require


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new system obviates many of the previous problems.  新的系统消除了许多以前的问题。
Regular exercise obviates the risk of heart disease.  规律的运动可以避免患心脏病的风险。


一般过去时:
The invention of the airplane obviated the need for long sea voyages.  飞机的发明消除了长途海运的必要。
His quick thinking obviated a potential disaster.  他的快速思考避免了一场潜在的灾难。


现在完成时:
Technological advancements have obviated the need for manual labor in many industries.  技术进步已经消除了许多行业对体力劳动的需求。
Improved safety measures have obviated many of the previous risks.  改进的安全措施已经消除了许多以前存在的风险。


过去完成时:
They had obviated the problem before it became serious.  他们在问题变得严重之前就解决了这个问题。
She had obviated the difficulties by careful planning.  她通过周密的计划避免了困难。


将来时:
The new software will obviate the need for manual data entry.  新软件将消除手动数据录入的需要。
This new policy will obviate future conflicts.  这项新政策将避免未来的冲突。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have obviated the crisis.  如果我们早点采取行动，就能避免这场危机。
If only we had foreseen the problem, we could have obviated it.  如果我们预见了这个问题，我们就能避免它。


被动语态:
The problem was obviated by a simple solution.  这个问题通过一个简单的解决方案得到了解决。
Many difficulties were obviated through careful preparation.  许多困难都通过仔细的准备得到了避免。


疑问句:
Can this new technology obviate the need for human intervention?  这项新技术能否消除人工干预的需要？
Does this solution obviate all potential problems?  这个解决方案能解决所有潜在的问题吗？


否定句:
The new regulations did not obviate the need for further investigation.  新的规章并没有消除进一步调查的必要。
Unfortunately, this approach did not obviate the underlying issues.  不幸的是，这种方法并没有解决根本问题。
