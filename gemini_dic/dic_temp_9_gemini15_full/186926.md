ripening:/ˈraɪpənɪŋ/| n. |成熟；成熟期|The process of becoming ripe; the state of being ripe.  It refers to the development of fruits, vegetables, or other things to a state of maturity and readiness for consumption or use. |例句：The ripening of the tomatoes took longer than expected. 番茄成熟的时间比预期的长。 The ripening of the fruit is crucial for its flavor and sweetness. 水果的成熟对其风味和甜度至关重要。|近义词：maturation, mellowing |反义词：rotting, decaying


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The fruit is ripening nicely. 水果正在慢慢成熟。
The bananas are ripening quickly in this warm weather.  香蕉在这种温暖的天气下成熟得很快。

一般过去时:
The mangoes ripened quickly after being picked. 芒果采摘后很快就成熟了。
The grapes ripened on the vine in the autumn sun. 葡萄在秋日的阳光下在藤蔓上成熟。

现在完成时:
The peaches have ripened beautifully this year.  今年桃子成熟得非常漂亮。
The avocados have ripened perfectly; they're ready to eat. 牛油果已经完全成熟了；可以吃了。

过去完成时:
The apples had ripened before the first frost. 苹果在第一次霜冻来临之前就已经成熟了。
The pears had ripened fully by the time we harvested them.  在我们收获梨子的时候，它们已经完全成熟了。

将来时:
The melons will ripen in a few more days.  瓜类再过几天就会成熟。
The plums will have ripened by the weekend. 李子到周末就会成熟了。

虚拟语气:
If the weather were warmer, the oranges would ripen faster. 如果天气更暖和，橙子就会成熟得更快。
I wish the strawberries had ripened sooner; we missed the peak season. 我希望草莓早点成熟；我们错过了旺季。

被动语态:
The cheese is being ripened in a cool cellar. 奶酪正在凉爽的地窖中成熟。
The grapes were ripened slowly to enhance their flavor. 葡萄被缓慢地成熟以增强其风味。

疑问句:
Are the mangoes ripening yet? 芒果成熟了吗？
Is the cheese ripening properly? 奶酪成熟得合适吗？

否定句:
The tomatoes aren't ripening properly; they're too green. 番茄成熟得不好；它们太青了。
The corn didn't ripen because of the early frost. 玉米因为早霜而没有成熟。
