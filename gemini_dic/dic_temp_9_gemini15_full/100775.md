intreatance:/ɪnˈtriːtəns/| n. |恳求；哀求；央求 |The act of entreating; earnest or humble petition; supplication.  It refers to the act of begging or pleading earnestly for something.  |例句：His entreatance for mercy fell on deaf ears.他的恳求怜悯没有得到回应。

例句：Despite her fervent entreatance, he refused to change his mind.  尽管她恳求得很热切，他还是拒绝改变主意。


近义词：supplication, plea, begging, imploration

反义词：demand, command, order

词性：名词
