salute:/səˈluːt/ | n., vt. |敬礼；致敬；祝酒 |To show respect or honor, typically by raising the hand to the forehead.  It can also refer to an expression of respect or approval. |例句：The soldiers saluted the flag. 士兵们向国旗敬礼。

The general saluted his superior officer. 将军向他的上级军官敬礼。


近义词：greet, honor, hail, acknowledge | 反义词：ignore, disregard, slight | 词性：n., vt.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He salutes the flag every morning. 他每天早上都向国旗敬礼。
She salutes her colleagues with a smile. 她笑着向同事们致意。

一般过去时:
The soldiers saluted the commanding officer. 士兵们向指挥官敬礼。
He saluted politely before leaving. 他离开前礼貌地敬了个礼。


现在完成时:
The troops have saluted the new president. 部队已经向新总统敬礼。
She has saluted the bravery of the firefighters. 她已经赞扬了消防员的英勇行为。


过去完成时:
They had saluted before the ceremony began. 典礼开始前他们已经敬礼了。
He had saluted his mentor before addressing the audience. 他在向观众讲话之前已经向他的导师致敬。


将来时:
The soldiers will salute the general. 士兵们将向将军敬礼。
She will salute the graduating class. 她将向毕业班致敬。


虚拟语气:
If I were a soldier, I would salute my commander. 如果我是个士兵，我会向我的指挥官敬礼。
I wish I could salute those who fought for our freedom. 我希望我能向那些为我们的自由而战的人致敬。


被动语态:
The flag was saluted by the troops. 国旗受到了部队的敬礼。
The brave firefighters were saluted for their heroism. 英勇的消防员们因其英雄主义精神而受到赞扬。


疑问句:
Did you salute the officer? 你向军官敬礼了吗？
Will they salute the flag? 他们会向国旗敬礼吗？


否定句:
He didn't salute the flag. 他没有向国旗敬礼。
She didn't salute the guests. 她没有向客人致意。
