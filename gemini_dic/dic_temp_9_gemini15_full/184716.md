resubscribed:/ˌriːsəbˈskraɪbd/| v.|重新订阅|To subscribe again; to renew a subscription.|例句：I resubscribed to the magazine after a brief hiatus. 我在短暂中断后重新订阅了该杂志。|近义词：renewed, reinscribed|反义词：canceled, unsubscribed

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resubscribes to the streaming service every year. = 他每年都重新订阅流媒体服务。
They resubscribe to the newsletter after changing their email address. = 他们更改电子邮件地址后重新订阅了通讯。

一般过去时:
She resubscribed to the yoga studio after a long break. = 她长时间休息后重新订阅了瑜伽工作室。
We resubscribed to the magazine because we missed the articles. = 我们重新订阅了杂志，因为我们错过了文章。

现在完成时:
I have resubscribed to the newspaper since moving to a new city. = 自从搬到新城市后，我就重新订阅了报纸。
They have resubscribed to the online course to review the material. = 他们重新订阅了在线课程来复习材料。

过去完成时:
He had resubscribed to the service before the free trial ended. = 在免费试用期结束之前，他已经重新订阅了该服务。
She had resubscribed to the gym membership before her vacation. = 在度假之前，她已经重新订阅了健身房会员资格。

将来时:
I will resubscribe to the software next month. = 我下个月将重新订阅该软件。
They will resubscribe to the magazine when they have more free time. = 当他们有更多空闲时间时，他们将重新订阅杂志。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would resubscribe to the premium version. = 如果我有更多钱，我会重新订阅高级版本。
I wish I had resubscribed to the streaming service sooner. = 我希望我早点重新订阅流媒体服务。

被动语态:
The subscription was resubscribed automatically. = 订阅已自动续订。(此处被动语态略显生硬，主动语态更常用)
The account will be resubscribed after verification. = 验证后将重新订阅帐户。(此处被动语态略显生硬，主动语态更常用)

疑问句:
Did you resubscribe to the magazine? = 你重新订阅了杂志吗？
Will they resubscribe to the service next year? = 他们明年会重新订阅服务吗？

否定句:
I did not resubscribe to the online course. = 我没有重新订阅在线课程。
He will not resubscribe to the newspaper until the price drops. = 在价格下降之前，他不会重新订阅报纸。
