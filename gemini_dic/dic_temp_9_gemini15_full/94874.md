in-box:/ˈɪnbɒks/| n. |收件箱|The inbox is where emails are received and stored before being read.|例句：I have over a hundred unread emails in my inbox. 我的收件箱里有超过一百封未读邮件。|近义词：email box, message box|反义词：outbox


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My inbox is full. = 我的收件箱满了。
Emails arrive in my inbox constantly. = 邮件不断地到达我的收件箱。

一般过去时:
I checked my inbox yesterday. = 我昨天查看了我的收件箱。
My inbox was empty last week. = 上周我的收件箱是空的。

现在完成时:
I have checked my inbox several times today. = 我今天已经查看了几次收件箱。
She has not received any emails in her inbox. = 她收件箱里没有收到任何邮件。

过去完成时:
I had already cleared my inbox before the meeting. = 我在会议前已经清空了我的收件箱。
He had not checked his inbox for days. = 他好几天没查看他的收件箱了。


将来时:
I will check my inbox later. = 我稍后会查看我的收件箱。
They will have many emails in their inbox tomorrow. = 他们明天收件箱里会有很多邮件。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would check my inbox. = 如果我有时间，我会查看我的收件箱。
I wish I had less spam in my inbox. = 我希望我的收件箱里垃圾邮件少一些。

被动语态:
The inbox is regularly checked by the administrator. = 收件箱由管理员定期检查。
The inbox is being flooded with spam emails. = 收件箱正被垃圾邮件淹没。


疑问句:
