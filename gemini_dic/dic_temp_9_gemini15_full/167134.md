presweetens: /ˌpriːˈswiːtnz/ | v. |预先加糖，事先加甜味剂 | To add a sweetener to something before it is used or consumed.  This often refers to adding sugar or artificial sweeteners to food products during manufacturing. | Example: The manufacturer presweetens the cereal to enhance its flavor. 制造商预先在谷物中加糖以增强其风味。 |近义词：sweeten beforehand, pre-sweeten |反义词：暂无直接反义词，可考虑“leave unsweetened” 或 “add bitterness beforehand”|动词
