radicated:/ˈrædɪkeɪtɪd/| v. |根除；铲除；消灭|To remove or destroy completely; to eradicate something is to get rid of it completely.  The word implies a thorough and complete removal, often of something harmful or undesirable.|

例句：
The government launched a campaign to eradicate poverty in rural areas. = 政府发起了一场运动，以根除农村地区的贫困。

The disease was finally eradicated after years of research and vaccination. = 多年研究和疫苗接种后，这种疾病终于被根除了。


近义词：eliminate, exterminate, abolish, extirpate, remove

反义词：propagate, foster, cultivate, spread


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They eradicate weeds from their garden every week. = 他们每周都清除花园里的杂草。
The company eradicates harmful insects from its crops. = 这家公司根除其农作物中的有害昆虫。


一般过去时:
The farmer eradicated the pest infestation last year. = 农民去年根除了害虫侵扰。
Scientists eradicated the virus from the lab. = 科学家们从实验室中根除了病毒。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:
We will eradicate this problem soon with the new technology. = 我们将很快利用新技术解决这个问题。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
They haven't yet eradicated all the weeds from the field. = 他们还没有从田地里清除所有杂草。
We cannot eradicate prejudice overnight. = 我们无法一夜之间消除偏见。
