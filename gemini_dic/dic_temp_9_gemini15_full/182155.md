remobilized:/ˌriːˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/| v. |重新动员；重新部署|To mobilize again; to put back into active service or operation.|The army was remobilized after the border incident.  军队在边境事件后重新动员起来。|The company remobilized its sales force after a period of decline. 公司在一段时间的衰退后重新动员了其销售队伍。|mobilize, reactivate |demobilize
