counterinstances:/ˈkaʊntərɪnstənsɪz/ | n. |反例，反证 | Instances that contradict or disprove a hypothesis, theory, or statement.  Counterinstances are crucial in scientific inquiry and logical reasoning, as they highlight limitations and inaccuracies.  They often lead to refinements or revisions of existing ideas. | The professor presented several counterinstances to refute the student's claim. 教授提出了几个反例来反驳学生的论点。 | counterexamples, refutations | supporting instances, confirmations | 名词
