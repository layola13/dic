lapstrake:/ˈlæpstreɪk/| n. |搭接板船体；| A method of boat building where the planks of the hull overlap each other like shingles on a roof.  This creates a distinctive appearance with visible seams. |The old fishing boat had a classic lapstrake hull.|近义词：clinker-built|反义词：carvel-built
