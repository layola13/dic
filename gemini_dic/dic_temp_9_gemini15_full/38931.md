confidant:/ˈkɒnfɪdænt/ /ˈkɑːnfɪdænt/ | n. | 知己，心腹朋友 | A confidant is a close friend or trusted person to whom you can tell your secrets and personal thoughts.  | She confided her deepest fears to her confidant. 她向她的知己吐露了她最深的恐惧。  He has always been a loyal confidant. 他一直都是一位忠诚的知己。 | friend, trusted friend, intimate | enemy, stranger
