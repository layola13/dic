collaterals: /kəˈlætərəlz/ | n. | 抵押品；担保品；附属物 | Securities or other assets pledged as security for a loan.  Collateral is something of value that a borrower pledges to a lender as security for a loan. If the borrower fails to repay the loan, the lender can seize and sell the collateral to recover its losses.  |  例句：The bank required collateral before approving the loan. = 银行在批准贷款之前要求提供抵押品。

例句：He used his house as collateral for the business loan. = 他用他的房子作为商业贷款的抵押品。


近义词：security, guarantee, pledge, surety | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词
