goshawks:/ˈɡɒsˌhɔːk/| n. |游隼，苍鹰|A goshawk is a medium-to-large bird of prey in the genus Accipiter.  They are known for their agility and speed in hunting. They have short, rounded wings and a long tail, which helps them maneuver through forests and other dense environments.|Example: The goshawk swooped down and caught a rabbit in its talons.  这只苍鹰俯冲下来，用爪子抓到了一只兔子。  The photographer spent hours patiently waiting to capture an image of a goshawk in flight. 摄影师耐心地等待了数小时，才拍到一只苍鹰飞行的照片。|近义词：hawk, falcon, accipiter|反义词：无
