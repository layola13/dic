fusileer:/ˌfjuːzɪˈlɪər/| n. | 燧发枪兵；轻步兵 | A fusilier is a soldier armed with a fusil, a type of light musket.  Historically, fusiliers were often considered elite light infantry troops.  The term is also used for a type of fish. | Example: The fusiliers advanced across the open field, their fusils ready.  燧发枪兵横穿开阔的田野前进，他们的燧发枪已准备就绪。  The coral reef teemed with life, including brightly colored fusiliers.  珊瑚礁充满了生机，包括色彩鲜艳的燧鱼。 | 近义词：rifleman, light infantryman | 反义词：heavy infantryman
