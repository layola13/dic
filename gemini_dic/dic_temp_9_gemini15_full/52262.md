dicast:/ˈdaɪkæst/| n.|陪审员；法官；裁决者|A dicast was a member of a jury or a court in ancient Greece.  The term refers to those who judged cases and rendered verdicts.|例句：In ancient Athens, dicasts played a vital role in the judicial system.  在古雅典，陪审员在司法系统中扮演着至关重要的角色。|近义词：juror, judge, arbitrator|反义词：defendant, litigant
