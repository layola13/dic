exhilaration:/ɪɡˌzɪləˈreɪʃən/| n. |兴奋；激动；高兴|A feeling of great happiness and excitement.  It often refers to a feeling of invigoration and heightened energy.|The exhilaration of skiing down a mountain was incredible.|近义词：elation, thrill, excitement |反义词：depression, dejection, boredom
