mudflat:/ˈmʌdˌflæt/| n. |泥滩，泥地 |A mudflat is a coastal wetland that is alternately exposed and submerged by the tides.  It's typically composed of mud and silt, supporting a unique ecosystem of specialized plants and animals. |例句：The mudflats teemed with life, attracting flocks of wading birds.  泥滩上生机勃勃，吸引了成群的涉禽。

The children played on the mudflats at low tide. 孩子们在退潮时在泥滩上玩耍。|近义词：muddy shore, tidal flat, salt marsh |反义词：dry land, upland
