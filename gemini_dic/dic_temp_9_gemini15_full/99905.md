/ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlɪst/| n. adj.|国际主义者；国际主义的|A person who supports internationalism; an advocate of international cooperation.  Someone who believes in the importance of international cooperation and understanding. |例句：Many internationalists advocate for global cooperation on climate change.  许多国际主义者倡导在气候变化问题上进行全球合作。|例句：The internationalist movement gained momentum after World War I.  第一次世界大战后，国际主义运动获得了发展势头。|近义词：globalist, cosmopolitan, internationalist (as noun)|反义词：nationalist, isolationist, protectionist|名词，形容词

词源:
The word "internationalist" is derived from "international" + "-ist".  "International" itself comes from "inter-" (between) + "national" (of or relating to a nation).  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who believes in or practices something.  Therefore, an "internationalist" is someone who believes in and advocates for international cooperation.
