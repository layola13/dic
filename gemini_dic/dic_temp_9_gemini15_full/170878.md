pseudoinspirational:/ˌsuːdoʊˌɪnspəˈreɪʃənəl/ | adj. |伪励志的，貌似鼓舞人心的 | Seemingly inspirational but actually lacking genuine substance or sincerity; superficially motivating.  It often involves clichés, empty promises, or manipulative tactics disguised as encouragement. | 例句：His pseudoinspirational speech was filled with empty platitudes and lacked any real substance.  他的伪励志演讲充满了空洞的陈词滥调，缺乏任何实际内容。

例句：The self-help book, with its pseudoinspirational quotes, offered little practical advice. 这本自助书籍，充斥着伪励志的语录，几乎没有提供任何实际建议。


近义词：motivational-sounding (but insincere), superficially inspiring, falsely encouraging,  uninspiring (in reality)

反义词：genuinely inspiring, truly motivating, authentically encouraging,  sincere


词性：形容词
