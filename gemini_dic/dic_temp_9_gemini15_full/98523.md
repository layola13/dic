instead of:/ɪnˈsted əv/| prep.|代替，而不是|Instead of going to the movies, we stayed home and watched TV.  代替去看电影，我们待在家里看电视了。|近义词：in place of, rather than|反义词：because of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We eat salad instead of potatoes. = 我们吃沙拉而不是土豆。
They take the bus instead of driving. = 他们坐公交车而不是开车。


一般过去时:
He walked instead of driving. = 他步行而不是开车。
She chose tea instead of coffee. = 她选择了茶而不是咖啡。


现在完成时:
I have used email instead of writing letters. = 我用电子邮件代替写信。
They have chosen cycling instead of driving. = 他们选择了骑自行车而不是开车。


过去完成时:
I had taken the train instead of flying. = 我坐火车而不是坐飞机。
She had cooked pasta instead of pizza. = 她煮了意大利面而不是披萨。


将来时:
We will take the train instead of the bus. = 我们将坐火车而不是公共汽车。
They will walk instead of driving. = 他们将步行而不是开车。


虚拟语气:
I would have gone to the party instead of staying home. = 我本该去参加派对而不是待在家里。
It would have been better to swim instead of sunbathe. = 游泳比晒太阳更好。


被动语态:
Buses are used instead of cars in the city center. = 市中心使用公共汽车而不是汽车。
Email is often used instead of traditional mail. = 电子邮件经常被用来代替传统邮件。


疑问句:
Would you prefer coffee instead of tea? = 你想喝咖啡而不是茶吗？
Should we use this method instead of that one? = 我们应该使用这种方法而不是那种方法吗？


否定句:
We did not go to the beach; instead, we went to the mountains. = 我们没有去海滩；相反，我们去了山区。(注意此例中instead用作副词)
They didn’t choose the red one; they chose the blue one instead. = 他们没有选择红色的，而是选择了蓝色的。(注意此例中instead用作副词)


PHRASE->
instead of that = 代替那件事
rather than = 而不是
in place of = 取代
as an alternative to = 作为...的替代品
to substitute for =  以...替代
