overplenteous: /ˌoʊvərˈplɛntɪəs/ | adj. | 非常丰富的；过量的；极多的 | Extremely abundant; excessive.  | The overplenteous harvest led to lower prices for grain.  丰收导致谷物价格下降。 | abundant, plentiful, copious | scarce, meager, deficient | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The garden is overplenteous with flowers this year. = 今年花园里的花非常繁茂。
His generosity is overplenteous. = 他的慷慨非常大方。


一般过去时:
The land was overplenteous with game in those days. = 那时候这片土地上猎物很多。
The table was overplenteous with food. = 桌上的食物非常丰盛。


现在完成时:
The farmer has been blessed with an overplenteous harvest. = 农民获得了丰收。
The region has experienced an overplenteous rainfall this season. = 该地区本季降雨量过大。


过去完成时:
The pantry had been overplenteous before the long winter. = 漫长的冬天来临之前，食品储藏室里食物非常充足。
They had already consumed an overplenteous amount of wine. = 他们已经喝了过量的葡萄酒。


将来时:
The upcoming season promises an overplenteous yield of fruit. = 即将来临的季节预示着水果丰收。
We expect an overplenteous supply of vegetables this year. = 我们预计今年蔬菜供应过剩。


虚拟语气:
If the harvest were overplenteous, we would have lower prices. = 如果收成丰盛，价格就会下降。
If only the resources were overplenteous, we could solve the problem easily. = 如果资源充足的话，我们就能很容易地解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The land was overplenteously blessed with fertile soil. = 这片土地富饶的土壤得到了上天的眷顾。
The market was overplenteously supplied with goods. = 市场商品供应过剩。


疑问句:
Is the supply of resources overplenteous enough to meet the demand? = 资源供应是否充足以满足需求？
Was the harvest truly overplenteous this year? = 今年的收成真的非常丰盛吗？


否定句:
The harvest was not overplenteous this year, unfortunately. = 不幸的是，今年的收成并不丰盛。
The resources are not overplenteous; we need to conserve them. = 资源并不充足；我们需要节约资源。

词源:
over- (超过) + plenteous (丰富的)  plenteous 源自古法语 *plenteus,  最终源自拉丁语 *plenus (满的，充满的)
