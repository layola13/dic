meatballs:/ˈmiːtˌbɔːlz/|/ˈmitˌbɔlz/| n. |肉丸|Small balls of ground meat, often mixed with breadcrumbs, eggs, and seasonings, then cooked by frying, baking, or simmering.  Meatballs are a popular dish worldwide, varying in ingredients and preparation methods depending on regional cuisine.| 例句：My grandmother's recipe for Swedish meatballs is legendary.  我奶奶的瑞典肉丸食谱非常有名。

My favorite restaurant serves delicious meatballs in a rich tomato sauce. 我最喜欢的餐厅供应美味的肉丸配浓郁的番茄酱。

近义词：meat balls,  meat roll (less common)
反义词：无
