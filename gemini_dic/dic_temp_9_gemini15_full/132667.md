nondisparate:/ˌnɒnˈdɪspərət/| adj.|非歧视的，无差别的|Not showing or involving prejudice against particular groups of people.  This adjective is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "disparate," indicating the absence of disparity or inequality.|The company's hiring practices were found to be nondisparate, ensuring equal opportunities for all applicants. 公司的招聘行为被认定为非歧视性的，确保所有申请者拥有平等的机会。|近义词：equal, unbiased, impartial, fair|反义词：discriminatory, prejudiced, biased, unequal
