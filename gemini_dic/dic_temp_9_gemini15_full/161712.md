powdering: /ˈpaʊdərɪŋ/ | v. | 撒粉；涂粉；磨成粉 | The act of applying powder to a surface or substance, or the process of reducing something to a powder.  | 例句：She was powdering her nose. = 她正在扑粉。

She is powdering the cake with sugar. = 她正在往蛋糕上撒糖粉。

近义词：dusting, sprinkling, pulverizing | 反义词：暂无直接反义词，可考虑与“凝固”、“结块”等相对


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He powders his face every morning. = 他每天早上都擦粉。
She powders the baby's bottom with talcum powder. = 她用爽身粉给婴儿的屁股擦粉。


一般过去时:
The baker powdered the donuts with cinnamon. = 面包师在甜甜圈上撒了肉桂粉。
He powdered his wig before the performance. = 他在演出前给假发扑粉。


现在完成时:
She has powdered her nose several times today. = 她今天已经扑过几次粉了。
They have powdered the entire batch of spices. = 他们已经把整批香料都磨成粉了。


过去完成时:
He had powdered his face before he went on stage. = 他上台前已经扑过粉了。
The chef had powdered the sugar before adding it to the batter. = 厨师在把糖加入面糊之前已经把它磨成粉了。

词源:
The word "powdering" derives from the Old French word "poudre," which means "powder."  This ultimately traces back to the Latin word "pulvis," also meaning "dust" or "powder."  The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle or gerund, signifying the act of doing something.
