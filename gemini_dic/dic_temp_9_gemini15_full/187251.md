robocall: /ˈroʊboʊkɔːl/ | n. | 机器人电话，自动拨打电话 | An automated telephone call, typically made using a computer system to deliver a recorded message.  Robocalls are often used for telemarketing, political campaigning, or scams. | Example: I received a robocall trying to sell me extended car warranty. 我接到一个推销汽车延保的机器人电话。  I blocked the number after receiving several robocalls. 我在收到几次机器人电话后屏蔽了那个号码。 | 近义词：automated call, autodialed call | 反义词：person-to-person call
