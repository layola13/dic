/lɪŋɡwætˈjuːlɪdə/ | n. |舌形动物|  A linguatulid is a parasitic worm belonging to the phylum Pentastomida.  These worms are characterized by their tongue-like appearance and their parasitic lifestyle, typically inhabiting the respiratory tracts of various vertebrate hosts.  They have a complex life cycle often involving intermediate hosts.

例句：The researchers discovered several linguatulid parasites in the lungs of the captured snake. = 研究人员在捕获的蛇的肺部发现了几个舌形动物寄生虫。
