paymaster:/ˈpeɪmɑːstər/ | n. | 支付员；主计员；发薪人 | A person responsible for paying employees' wages or salaries; someone who controls or manages financial payments. | The company paymaster distributed the salaries to all the employees. 公司的支付员向所有员工发放工资。  The paymaster is responsible for ensuring accurate and timely payment of wages. 支付员负责确保工资的准确和及时发放。| treasurer, cashier, bursar | debtor | noun
