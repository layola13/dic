precontribution: /ˌpriːkɒntriˈbjuːʃən/ | n. | 预缴款，预先捐款 | A payment or contribution made in advance of a larger payment or contribution.  It's often used in contexts where a full payment is due later, and the precontribution serves as a down payment or initial installment. | Example: The organization requested a precontribution of $100 to secure a place at the conference, with the remaining balance due before the event.  = 该组织要求预缴100美元以确保在会议上有席位，剩余余额需在活动前支付。  | 近义词：advance payment, down payment, installment, deposit | 反义词：post-contribution, arrears, outstanding balance | 名词
