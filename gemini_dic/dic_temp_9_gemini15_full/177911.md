recharted:/ˌriˈtʃɑːrtɪd/| v. |重新绘制图表；重新排榜|To create a new chart or graph based on updated data, or to re-enter a chart after a period of absence.|The band recharted with their new single, reaching number one. 乐队凭借他们的新单曲重新登上排行榜，并获得冠军。|近义词：re-plot, re-map, re-list|反义词：remove from chart, delete from chart


USAGE->

They rechart the sales figures every month. = 他们每月都会重新绘制销售数据图表。
The company recharts its marketing strategies regularly. = 这家公司定期重新规划其营销策略。
