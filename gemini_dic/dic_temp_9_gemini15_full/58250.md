drumhead:/ˈdrʌmhed/| n.|鼓面，鼓皮|The taut membrane stretched across the frame of a drum, on which the drummer strikes to produce sound.  It can also refer to a military court-martial.|例句：The drummer tightened the drumhead before the parade.  鼓手在游行前收紧了鼓面。

The drumhead was damaged during the concert.  音乐会期间鼓面受损了。|近义词：drumskin|反义词：无
