discoursed:/dɪsˈkɔːrsd/| v.|谈论；论述；演讲|To speak or write formally and at length on a particular subject.|例句：The professor discoursed eloquently on the history of philosophy. 教授雄辩地论述了哲学史。|例句：They discoursed for hours on the merits of different investment strategies. 他们就不同投资策略的优缺点讨论了好几个小时。|近义词：discussed, lectured, expounded, elaborated|反义词：kept silent, remained mum

一般过去时:
