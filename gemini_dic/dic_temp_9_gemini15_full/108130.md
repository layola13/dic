latinesque:/ˌlætiˈnesk/| adj. |拉丁风格的，具有拉丁特色的|Resembling or suggestive of Latin; characterized by qualities reminiscent of Latin language, literature, or culture.  The term often implies a formal, elegant, or somewhat archaic style.|例句：His prose style is quite latinesque, full of elaborate sentence structures and learned vocabulary.  他的散文风格颇具拉丁风格，充满了复杂的句子结构和学究气的词汇。

例句：The architecture of the building has a distinctly latinesque feel, with its columns and arches. 这座建筑的建筑风格明显具有拉丁风格，其柱子和拱门是其特征。


近义词：classical, Roman, learned, scholarly

反义词：modern, vernacular, colloquial, informal


词性:形容词
