melliphagan:/ˌmɛlɪˈfæɡən/| n. |食蜜鸟|A meliphagan is any bird belonging to the family Meliphagidae, a large family of passerine birds found in Australasia.  They are known for their diverse diets, often including nectar, insects, and fruits.  Many species have specialized beaks and tongues adapted to their particular food sources.|Example: The noisy miner is a common example of a meliphagan found in Australia.  澳大利亚嘈杂矿工是澳大利亚常见的食蜜鸟的例子。|近义词：honeyeater (though not all honeyeaters are meliphagans)|反义词：无|名词
