hiccoughing:/ˈhɪkʌpɪŋ/| v. |打嗝；呃逆| The act of repeatedly making a short, sharp sound caused by a spasm of the diaphragm.  It's an involuntary action. |例句：He was hiccoughing uncontrollably after drinking too much soda. 他喝了太多苏打水后，止不住地打嗝。|例句：The baby kept hiccoughing, which worried her mother.  宝宝一直打嗝，这让她妈妈很担心。|近义词：hiccuping|反义词：None
