nonoptimistic: /ˌnɒnˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. | 不乐观;悲观的 | Not optimistic; pessimistic.  It implies a lack of hope or confidence in a positive outcome.  | 例句：The company's nonoptimistic outlook on the market caused investors to sell their shares.  这家公司对市场的悲观展望导致投资者抛售股票。

The economic forecast is nonoptimistic, predicting a recession. 经济预测并不乐观，预计将出现经济衰退。| 近义词：pessimistic, gloomy, negative, cynical | 反义词：optimistic, hopeful, positive, sanguine
