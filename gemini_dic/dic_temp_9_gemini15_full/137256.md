nonsweetened:/ˌnɒnˈswiːtnd/| adj.|不加糖的；无糖的|Not containing any added sugar.  This term is used to describe food and drinks that are naturally free of added sugars or have had no sugar added during processing. |例句：I prefer nonsweetened tea. = 我喜欢不加糖的茶。

例句：She bought nonsweetened yogurt for her breakfast. = 她买了不加糖的酸奶作为早餐。


近义词：sugar-free, unsweetened, plain


反义词：sweetened, sugared


词性:形容词
