requoting:/rɪˈkwoʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 再报价；重新报价 | To quote again, especially at a different price.  This often happens in negotiations or when there's been a change in circumstances (e.g., material costs). | Example:  After reviewing the project scope, the contractor was forced to requote the job at a higher price. = 在审查项目范围后，承包商被迫以更高的价格重新报价。|近义词：repricing, quoting again |反义词：accepting the initial quote
