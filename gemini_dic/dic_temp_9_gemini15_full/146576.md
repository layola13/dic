overgratified: /ˌoʊvərˈɡrædəˌfaɪd/ | adj. | 过度满足的，过度溺爱的 | Excessively gratified; excessively indulged or pampered.  The adjective describes a state of being excessively satisfied or a person who has been given excessive indulgence.  It implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the excessive gratification may lead to negative consequences. | Example: The overgratified child expected everything to be given to him immediately.  过度满足的孩子期望一切都能立即得到满足。 |近义词: spoiled, pampered, indulged, satiated | 反义词: deprived, unsatisfied, neglected
