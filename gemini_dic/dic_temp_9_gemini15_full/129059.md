nitro:/ˈnaɪtroʊ/| n. |硝基，硝化物|A nitro group is a chemical group consisting of a nitrogen atom bonded to two oxygen atoms (-NO2).  It is a common component in many organic and inorganic compounds. Nitro compounds are often explosive or highly reactive. |Example:  Nitroglycerin is a powerful explosive containing nitro groups. 硝化甘油是一种含有硝基的强力炸药。|近义词:nitrate (in some contexts)|反义词:None directly applicable.
