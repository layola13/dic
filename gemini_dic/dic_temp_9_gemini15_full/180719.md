regroomed:/ˌriːˈɡruːmd/| v. |重新打扮；重新整理；重新塑造形象 |To groom someone or something again, often to improve their appearance or to give them a new image.|The stylist regroomed the model before the photoshoot.  造型师在拍摄前重新为模特做了造型。|The politician regroomed his image after the scandal.  这位政治家在丑闻后重新塑造了自己的形象。| groomed, ungroomed |


USAGE->

He regrooms his dog every week. = 他每周都给他的狗梳理毛发。
They regroom the garden after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，他们重新整理了花园。
