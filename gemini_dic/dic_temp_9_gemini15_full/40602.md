convective:/kənˈvɛktɪv/ | adj. | 对流的；对流产生的 | Relating to or caused by convection. Convection is the movement of heat through a fluid (liquid or gas) by the circulation of the fluid itself.  Hotter, less dense fluid rises, while cooler, denser fluid sinks, creating a cycle of movement that transfers heat. | Example: Convective currents in the Earth's mantle drive plate tectonics.  地球地幔中的对流流驱动板块构造。

The rising hot air created a convective updraft.  上升的热空气产生了一个对流上升气流。


近义词：convectional


反义词：conductive (referring to heat transfer), radiative (referring to heat transfer)


词性：形容词
