canyons:/ˈkænjən/| n.|峡谷，深谷|A canyon is a deep, narrow valley with steep sides, often carved out by a river.|例句：The Grand Canyon is a breathtaking natural wonder.  大峡谷是一个令人叹为观止的自然奇观。

例句：We hiked through a narrow canyon, shaded by towering cliffs. 我们徒步穿过一个狭窄的峡谷，两旁是高耸的悬崖。


近义词：gorge, ravine, chasm | 反义词：plain, plateau
