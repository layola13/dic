quoits:/kwɔɪts/| n.| quoit (单数)|套圈游戏；套圈环|A quoit is a heavy ring, typically of iron, that is thrown at a peg or other target in a game.  The game itself is also called quoits.|例句：We played quoits in the park.我们在公园里玩套圈游戏。

例句：He threw the quoit with great precision. 他以极高的精确度投掷套圈环。


近义词：ring toss (不太正式)

反义词：无


词性:名词
