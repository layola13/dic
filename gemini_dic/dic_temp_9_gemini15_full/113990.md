magisterial:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəl/| adj.|权威的；威风的；有权威的；像专家一样的|possessing or showing great authority; commanding and impressive.|The professor delivered a magisterial lecture on the subject. 教授就该主题发表了一篇权威的演讲。| authoritative, imperious, commanding | submissive, humble |形容词
