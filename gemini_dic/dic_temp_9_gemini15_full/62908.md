enlistment:/ɪnˈlɪstmənt/ | n. | 入伍；征募；招募 | The act of enlisting in the armed forces or of being enlisted; the state of being enlisted.  It can also refer to the process of recruiting people for a cause or organization. | Example: His enlistment in the army was a defining moment in his life.  他的入伍是他人生中一个重要的时刻。  The company's enlistment of new employees was crucial to its expansion.  公司招募新员工对其扩张至关重要。 | 近义词：enrollment, recruitment, induction | 反义词：discharge, dismissal
