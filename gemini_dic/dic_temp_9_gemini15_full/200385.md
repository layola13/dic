skylarking:/ˈskaɪlɑːrkɪŋ/| n. v.|玩乐；嬉戏；胡闹|Engaging in frivolous or mischievous activity; playfully boisterous behavior.  |例句：The children were skylarking in the park.孩子们在公园里嬉戏玩耍。

例句：The students were skylarking in class, disrupting the lesson. 学生们在课堂上胡闹，扰乱了课堂秩序。


近义词：frolicking, romping, playing pranks, clowning around, larking


反义词：being serious, behaving responsibly, working diligently


词性:Noun and Verb
