foodlessness:/ˈfuːdləsnəs/| n.|缺乏食物；饥饿；粮食短缺|The state of being without food; hunger; famine.  It refers to a condition where there is a lack of access to sufficient food to meet basic nutritional needs.  This can range from mild hunger to severe starvation.|例句：The prolonged drought led to widespread foodlessness in the region. 长期干旱导致该地区普遍缺乏食物。 The foodlessness of the refugees was a major concern for aid workers. 难民的饥饿是援助工作者主要关注的问题。|近义词：starvation, hunger, famine, deprivation|反义词：abundance, plenty, satiety
