interhaemal:/ˌɪntərˈhiːməl/| adj.| 髓内，骨髓间的 | Relating to the spaces or structures between the haemal arches of vertebrates.  This term is primarily used in zoological and anatomical contexts, referring to the arrangement of blood vessels and supporting structures within the vertebrae of animals. | Example:  The interhaemal veins are crucial for oxygen transport within the caudal region.  = 髓内静脉对于尾部区域的氧气运输至关重要。|近义词:intervertebral (in some contexts)|反义词:intrahaemal
