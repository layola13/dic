procrastinatory:/prəˈkræstɪnətɔːri/ | adj. | 拖延的；耽搁的 |  Characterized by procrastination; tending to postpone or delay.  It describes a person, behavior, or habit of delaying or postponing actions. |  His procrastinatory habits led to missed deadlines. 他的拖延习惯导致错过最后期限。  Her procrastinatory nature made her a less effective employee. 她的拖延性格使她成为一个效率较低的员工。 | dilatory, tardy, delaying | prompt, punctual, expeditious | 形容词
