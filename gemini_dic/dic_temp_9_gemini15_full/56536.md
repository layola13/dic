dominate:/ˈdɒmɪneɪt/ /ˈdɑːmɪneɪt/| vt. n.|支配；统治；控制；主导|To have control over a place or person.|The company dominates the market. 这家公司主导着市场。|近义词：control, govern, rule, influence|反义词：submit, obey, yield


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dominates the conversation. 他主导着谈话。
The large building dominates the skyline. 那座大楼占据了天际线。


一般过去时:
She dominated the chess game. 她赢得了这场象棋比赛。
The empire dominated the region for centuries. 这个帝国统治了这个地区几个世纪。


现在完成时:
This company has dominated the industry for years. 这家公司多年来一直主导着这个行业。
His ideas have dominated the debate. 他的观点主导了这场辩论。


过去完成时:
The team had dominated the previous game.  该队在上场比赛中占据了主导地位。
Before the merger, they had dominated the local market. 在合并之前，他们主导着当地市场。


将来时:
This technology will dominate the future.这项技术将主导未来。
It is predicted that she will dominate the sport for years to come.  预计她将在未来几年主导这项运动。


虚拟语气:
If he dominated the game, we would win.如果他主导比赛，我们会赢。
I wish I could dominate my fears.我希望我能战胜我的恐惧。


被动语态:
The market is dominated by a few large companies. 市场被几家大公司所主导。
The discussion was dominated by his aggressive tone. 讨论被他咄咄逼人的语气所主导。


疑问句:
Does one person dominate the group?  一个人是否主导着这个小组？
Which company dominates the global market? 哪家公司主导着全球市场？


否定句:
He doesn't dominate the conversation anymore. 他不再主导谈话了。
No single player dominated the match.  没有一个球员在比赛中占据主导地位。
