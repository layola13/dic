odalisque:/ˈɒdəlɪsk/ | n. | 宫女，侍妾 | A female slave or concubine in a harem, especially in the Ottoman Empire.  The term is often used to describe the subject of paintings and other works of art, frequently depicted in a reclining or semi-nude pose. | Example:  Ingres's famous painting depicts a beautiful odalisque in a richly decorated room.  英格斯的著名画作描绘了一位身处装饰华丽房间中的美丽宫女。 |近义词：concubine, slave girl, harem woman | 反义词：mistress (in the sense of a dominant female figure)
