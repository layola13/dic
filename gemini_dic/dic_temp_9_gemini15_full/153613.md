pentateuch:/ˈpɛnteɪtjuːk/| n. |五经；摩西五经 |The Pentateuch is the first five books of the Bible.  五经是圣经的前五卷书。|近义词：Torah, first five books of Moses |反义词：无


USAGE->

词源:
From Late Latin *pentateuchus, from Greek pentateuchos  (πεντάτευχος), from pente "five" + teuchos "book, volume".  The word ultimately reflects the five books comprising the collection: Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers, and Deuteronomy.
