desmine:/ˈdɛzmiːn/| n.|脱氢去甲肾上腺素|A naturally occurring sympathomimetic amine that acts as a neurotransmitter and is found in the adrenal medulla and brain. It is structurally similar to norepinephrine (noradrenaline) but lacks a hydroxyl group.  Desmine is involved in the regulation of blood pressure and other physiological processes.|例句：The study investigated the role of desmine in regulating cardiovascular function. 这项研究调查了脱氢去甲肾上腺素在调节心血管功能中的作用。|近义词：norepinephrine, noradrenaline|反义词：无|词性:n.
