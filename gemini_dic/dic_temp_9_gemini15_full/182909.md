reperusing:/ˌriːˈpɛruːzɪŋ/| v.|再读；重读；仔细检查 | To read or examine again; to reread.  It implies a more thorough and careful reading than a simple rereading. |例句：After the initial reading, she spent an hour reperusing the contract, checking for any loopholes.  再次阅读后，她花了一个小时仔细检查合同，寻找任何漏洞。

例句：He reperused the manuscript, making several corrections. 他重读了手稿，并做了一些修改。


近义词：rereading, reviewing, re-examining, scrutinizing

反义词：skimming, glancing

词性：动词
