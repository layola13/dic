recirculating:/ˌriːˈsɜːrkjʊleɪtɪŋ/| adj. |再循环的；循环使用的|Relating to or involving the process of recirculating, typically a fluid or gas, so that it is used repeatedly.|The recirculating system in the building keeps the air clean and fresh. 建筑物中的再循环系统使空气保持清洁和新鲜。|近义词：recycled, reused |反义词：one-time use, disposable
