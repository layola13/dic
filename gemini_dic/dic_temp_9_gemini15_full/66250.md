excluder:/ɪkˈskluːdər/| n. |排除者；排斥者；过滤器|A person or thing that excludes something or someone.  It can also refer to a device that prevents something from passing through.|例句：The new policy is seen by some as an excluder of low-income families. 新政策被一些人视为排斥低收入家庭。

The air filter acts as an excluder of dust and pollen. 空气过滤器起到过滤灰尘和花粉的作用。|近义词：rejector, remover, filter |反义词： includer, acceptor


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The machine excludes impurities from the water. = 这台机器将杂质从水中排除。
He excludes himself from the group's activities. = 他把自己排除在团队活动之外。


一般过去时:
They excluded her from the competition. = 他们把她排除在比赛之外。
The editor excluded several paragraphs from the article. = 编辑从文章中删除了几段。


现在完成时:
They have excluded him from the decision-making process. = 他们已经将他排除在决策过程之外。
The company has excluded several products from its catalog. = 公司已将其目录中的几种产品排除在外。


过去完成时:
She had excluded all possibilities before reaching a conclusion. = 在得出结论之前，她已经排除了所有可能性。
They had excluded the evidence from the trial. = 他们已将证据排除在审判之外。


将来时:
They will exclude him from future projects. = 他们将把他排除在未来的项目之外。
The company will exclude certain chemicals from its products. = 公司将从其产品中排除某些化学物质。


虚拟语气:
If they excluded that data, the results would be different. = 如果他们排除该数据，结果将不同。
I wish they had excluded me from that meeting. = 我希望他们把我排除在那次会议之外。


被动语态:
He was excluded from the team because of his poor performance. = 由于表现不佳，他被排除在团队之外。
Many details were excluded from the report. = 报告中排除了许多细节。


疑问句:
Who was excluded from the guest list? = 谁被排除在宾客名单之外？
What factors are excluded from the study? = 哪些因素被排除在研究之外？


否定句:
They did not exclude the possibility of error. = 他们没有排除出错的可能性。
We should not exclude any option at this stage. = 在这个阶段，我们不应该排除任何选择。


PHRASE->

exclude from: 从……中排除

to be excluded: 被排除在外
