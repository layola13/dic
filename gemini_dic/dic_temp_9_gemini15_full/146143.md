overencourage:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒ/| vt.|过度鼓励，过分激励|To encourage excessively; to give someone too much encouragement, often leading to negative consequences.  The word combines "over-" (excessively) with "encourage".|例句：The coach overencouraged the young athlete, leading to burnout. 教练对年轻运动员过度鼓励，导致他们筋疲力尽。
例句：Her parents overencouraged her artistic pursuits, creating unrealistic expectations.  她的父母过度鼓励她追求艺术，造成了不切实际的期望。|近义词：excessively encourage, overstimulate|反义词：discourage, dissuade|动词
