recap:/ˈriːkæp/| vt. n.|总结；概括；重述|To summarize or briefly restate something.|例句：Let's recap what we've learned today.  让我们总结一下我们今天学到的东西。|例句：The teacher gave a recap of the previous lesson.老师对上一课做了总结。|近义词：summarize, review, summarize, restate|反义词：detail, elaborate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recaps the main points at the end of each meeting. 他在每次会议结束时总结要点。
She recaps the news for her elderly mother every evening. 她每天晚上为她年迈的母亲总结新闻。


一般过去时:
The manager recapped the project's progress yesterday. 经理昨天总结了项目的进展。
I recapped the key findings of the report for my boss. 我为我的老板总结了报告的主要发现。


现在完成时:
She has recapped the events of the past week in her diary. 她已在日记中总结了过去一周的事件。
We have recapped the meeting's decisions and sent them to the team. 我们已经总结了会议的决定并将其发送给了团队。


过去完成时:
After the presentation, she recapped what had been discussed.  演示结束后，她总结了讨论的内容。
He recapped what had happened before the accident. 他总结了事故发生前的情况。


将来时:
I will recap the main points at the end of the presentation. 我将在演示结束时总结要点。
She will recap the day's events in her journal tonight. 她今晚将在日记中总结一天的事件。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would recap the entire chapter. 如果我有时间，我会总结整章内容。
It would be helpful if he recapped the instructions. 如果他能重述一下说明，将会很有帮助。


被动语态:
The key findings were recapped in the final report. 主要发现已在最终报告中总结。
The meeting's decisions will be recapped in an email. 会议的决定将通过电子邮件总结。


疑问句:
Can you recap the instructions, please? 请您总结一下说明好吗？
Will you recap the main points of the lecture? 你会总结讲座的主要内容吗？


否定句:
He didn't recap the meeting's details. 他没有总结会议的细节。
She didn't recap the story properly. 她没有正确地总结故事。
