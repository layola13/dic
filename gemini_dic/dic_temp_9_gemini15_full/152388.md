paves:/peɪvz/| v.|铺设，铺平；为…铺路 | to cover a surface with a material such as stones or bricks to make a path or road.  It can also mean to create a way or path towards something.|例句：They paved the road with new asphalt.  他们用新的沥青铺路。|例句：His hard work paved the way for his success. 他的努力为他的成功铺平了道路。|近义词：lay, cover, construct |反义词：destroy, dismantle


USAGE->

The workers pave the streets every year. = 工人们每年都铺设街道。
She paves her garden path with stones. = 她用石头铺设花园小路。
