libelists:/ˈlaɪbəlɪsts/| n. |诽谤者；造谣者|A person who writes or publishes libelous statements.  Someone who makes false and damaging statements about someone else, typically in writing.|例句：The libelists were sued for defamation.  诽谤者因诽谤罪被起诉。

The libelists spread false rumors about the candidate to damage their reputation.  诽谤者散布关于候选人的谣言以损害他们的声誉。|近义词：slanderers, calumniators, defamers|反义词：truth-tellers, defenders of reputation|名词
