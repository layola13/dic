panels:/ˈpænlz/| n. |嵌板；面板；分组；讨论小组 |A panel is a flat, rectangular piece of something, often wood or metal, that forms part of a larger surface.  It can also refer to a group of people chosen to discuss a particular subject. |Example: The artist created a stunning painting using different colored panels.  艺术家用不同颜色的面板创作了一幅令人惊叹的画作。The discussion panel consisted of experts in the field. 讨论小组由该领域的专家组成。|近义词：section, board, group |反义词：whole, single
