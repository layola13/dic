segregate:/ˈseɡrɪɡeɪt/| vt.|隔离；隔离；分开|To separate or keep separate from others or a main group, typically based on race, religion, or gender.  |例句：The school was segregated by race. 学校按种族隔离。|例句：They segregated the infected patients from the healthy ones. 他们将感染的病人与健康的病人隔离。|近义词：separate, isolate, divide|反义词：integrate, unite, combine|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government segregates funds for different projects. = 政府将资金按不同的项目进行隔离。
They segregate waste into different bins. = 他们将垃圾分类到不同的垃圾桶中。

一般过去时:
The company segregated its employees by department. = 公司按部门将员工分开。
They segregated the wheat from the chaff. = 他们将麦粒与谷壳分开。

现在完成时:
South Africa has segregated its population for many years. = 南非多年来一直对人口进行隔离。
The city has segregated its recycling program. = 该市已经将回收计划分开。

过去完成时:
Before the Civil Rights Act, schools had been segregated by race. = 在民权法案之前，学校按种族隔离。
They had segregated the evidence before the trial. = 审判前，他们已经将证据隔离。

将来时:
The new law will segregate certain types of waste. = 新法律将对某些类型的废物进行隔离。
The company will segregate its marketing budget. = 公司将隔离其营销预算。

虚拟语气:
If they segregated the data, the analysis would be more accurate. = 如果他们将数据隔离，分析会更准确。
I wish they had segregated the recyclables from the trash. = 我希望他们已经将可回收物与垃圾分开。

被动语态:
Funds are segregated to prevent misuse. = 资金被隔离以防止滥用。
The students were segregated based on their academic performance. = 学生根据他们的学习成绩被隔离。

疑问句:
Are the chemicals segregated properly? = 化学品是否被正确隔离？
How are the different types of waste segregated? = 不同类型的废物是如何分类的？

否定句:
They did not segregate the data correctly. = 他们没有正确地隔离数据。
The hospital didn't segregate the infectious patients. = 医院没有隔离传染病人。


PHRASE->

religious segregation = 宗教隔离
segregate from = 将...与...分开
