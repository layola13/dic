hoodie:/ˈhuːdi/| n. |帽衫，卫衣|A sweatshirt with a hood.  Hoodies are typically made of fleece or cotton and are popular for their warmth and comfort.|例句：He wore a gray hoodie and jeans. 他穿着灰色帽衫和牛仔裤。|例句：My favorite hoodie is the one with the funny cat on it. 我最喜欢的帽衫是印着有趣猫咪的那一件。|近义词：sweatshirt,pullover|反义词：None.
