passively:/ˈpæsɪvli/| adv.|被动地；消极地|In a passive manner; without active participation or resistance.|例句：He passively accepted his fate. 他被动地接受了自己的命运。|近义词：submissively, unresistingly, lethargically|反义词：actively, aggressively, energetically
