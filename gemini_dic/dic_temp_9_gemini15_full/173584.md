quasi-important:/ˈkwɑːzaɪɪmˈpɔːrtənt/|/ˈkwɑːziːɪmˈpɔːrtənt/| adj. |准重要的，似重要的|  Something that is quasi-important is almost important, but not quite. It might be significant in some contexts but not in others. It lacks the full weight or consequence of something truly important.  |The quasi-important meeting ran over schedule.  这次准重要的会议超出了预定时间。| important, significant | unimportant, trivial, insignificant | 形容词
