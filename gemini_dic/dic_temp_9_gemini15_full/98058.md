This often involved dividing a sample of metal into four equal parts for separate analysis or processing. |Example: The assayer performed an inquartation of the gold sample before analysis.  化验员在分析前对金样品进行了四分法处理。|近义词：quartering, division|反义词：unification, combination|名词
