repromising:/ˌriːˈprɒmɪsɪŋ/| adj.| 再许诺的；再次承诺的；有希望的 |  A repromising action or statement is one that repeats a promise that has already been made.  The word also implies something hopeful or potentially successful.  It's not a common word and often used in a context highlighting the renewed hope or commitment. | Example:  The company's repromising of its commitment to environmental sustainability after the recent scandal was met with skepticism. 公司在最近的丑闻后再次承诺其对环境可持续性的承诺，受到了怀疑。  | 近义词：reassuring, hopeful, promising, encouraging | 反义词：discouraging, disheartening, dispiriting |  adj.
