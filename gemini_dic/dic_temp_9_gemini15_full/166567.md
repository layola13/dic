prescribe:/prɪˈskraɪb/| vt.|开处方；规定；指示；建议|to order or recommend the use of a medicine, treatment, diet, etc.|The doctor prescribed me some antibiotics for my infection. 医生给我开了些抗生素治感染。|recommend, order, dictate|forbid, prohibit|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor prescribes medication for many patients. = 医生为许多病人开药。
She prescribes regular exercise to her clients. = 她建议她的客户定期锻炼。


一般过去时:
My doctor prescribed me painkillers after the surgery. = 手术后我的医生给我开了止痛药。
The teacher prescribed a reading list for the students. = 老师为学生们规定了一份阅读书单。


现在完成时:
The physician has prescribed this treatment for years. = 这名医生多年来一直采用这种疗法。
She has prescribed a strict diet for weight loss. = 她为减肥制定了严格的饮食计划。


过去完成时:
The doctor had prescribed the medicine before I arrived. = 我到达之前医生已经开了药。
They had prescribed a course of action before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经制定了一个行动方案。


将来时:
The specialist will prescribe a new course of treatment next week. = 专科医生下周将开出新的治疗方案。
He will prescribe a stricter diet if the initial one fails. = 如果最初的饮食计划失败，他将开出更严格的饮食计划。


虚拟语气:
If the symptoms worsened, the doctor would prescribe stronger medication. = 如果症状恶化，医生会开更强的药。
I wish the doctor had prescribed a different treatment. = 我希望医生开了不同的治疗方案。


被动语态:
Medication was prescribed to alleviate the pain. = 开药是为了减轻疼痛。
A strict diet was prescribed to improve her health. = 开出严格的饮食是为了改善她的健康。


疑问句:
What medication did the doctor prescribe? = 医生开了什么药？
What treatment has been prescribed for this condition? = 这种疾病采用了什么治疗方法？


否定句:
The doctor did not prescribe any antibiotics. = 医生没有开任何抗生素。
The judge did not prescribe any punishment for the minor offense. = 法官没有对轻微罪行处以任何惩罚。


PHRASE->
prescribe medication  开药
prescribe a treatment 制定治疗方案
prescribe a course of action 制定行动方案
prescribe rules 制定规则
prescribe limits 设定限制
