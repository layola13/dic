mastodonsaurus:/ˈmæstədɒnsɔːrəs/| n. |巨螈|A genus of large, extinct temnospondyl amphibians that lived during the Triassic period.  They were characterized by their massive size, flat heads, and sharp teeth. They are considered among the largest amphibians ever to exist.|Example:  The fossil remains of *Mastodonsaurus* are found in various parts of Europe and Asia.|近义词: temnospondyl (a broader taxonomic group)|反义词: None (as it refers to a specific extinct genus)
