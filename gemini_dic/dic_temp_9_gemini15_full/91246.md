hydrodynamics:/ˌhaɪdroʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/| n. |流体动力学|The branch of physics that deals with the motion of fluids and the forces acting on them.  It encompasses both liquids and gases and considers their behavior under various conditions, including laminar and turbulent flow, pressure gradients, and viscosity.|Example:  The principles of hydrodynamics are crucial in designing efficient ship hulls. 流体动力学的原理对于设计高效的船体至关重要。

The study of hydrodynamics is essential in understanding ocean currents.  对流体动力学的研究对于理解洋流至关重要。|近义词：fluid mechanics|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
