sleuth:/sluːθ/| n. |侦探，侦探人员|A detective, especially one who is skillful and clever.  A sleuth investigates crimes and mysteries.|例句：The famous sleuth solved the baffling case.|近义词：detective, investigator, private eye|反义词：criminal, culprit

一般现在时:
The sleuth investigates the crime scene. = 侦探正在调查犯罪现场。
She often follows leads in her work as a sleuth. = 她经常在侦探工作中追踪线索。

一般过去时:
The sleuth investigated the murder thoroughly. = 侦探彻底调查了这起谋杀案。
He successfully solved the case thanks to his skills as a sleuth. = 多亏了他作为侦探的技能，他成功地破案了。

现在完成时:
The police have hired a renowned sleuth to solve the case. = 警方已经聘请了一位著名的侦探来破案。
