mudflats:/ˈmʌdˌflæts/| n.|泥滩，潮滩|Areas of mud exposed at low tide, especially along the shores of estuaries and seas.|例句：The mudflats teemed with life, attracting various birds and invertebrates.  泥滩上充满了生机，吸引了各种鸟类和无脊椎动物。

例句：We walked across the vast mudflats at low tide, careful not to sink.  我们在退潮时走过广阔的泥滩，小心不要陷入泥中。


近义词：tidal flats, mudflats, salt marsh (部分重叠含义)|反义词：dry land, upland
