happiness:/ˈhæpɪnəs/ | n. |快乐，幸福|Happiness is a state of being content and joyful.  It is a feeling of well-being and satisfaction. |例句：She found happiness in her family. 她从她的家庭中找到了快乐。|例句：True happiness comes from within. 真正的快乐来自内心。|近义词：joy, bliss, contentment, felicity |反义词：sadness, misery, unhappiness, despair |名词
