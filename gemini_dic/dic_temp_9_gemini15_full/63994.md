epistolary:/ɪˈpɪstələri/| adj.|书信体的；书信形式的| Relating to or denoting letters or letter writing.  The term often refers to novels or other works told through the exchange of letters. |例句：The novel is written in an epistolary style, unfolding the story through a series of letters between two characters. 这部小说采用书信体形式，通过两个角色之间的一系列书信展开故事。|例句：Her epistolary account of her travels was fascinating. 她关于旅行的书信记录非常引人入胜。|近义词：letter-based, correspondence-based|反义词：non-epistolary
