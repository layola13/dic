governmental:/ˌɡʌvərˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 政府的；官方的 | Relating to or characteristic of a government.  | Example: Governmental regulations are designed to protect consumers. 政府法规旨在保护消费者。 |近义词：official, state, public | 反义词：private, individual
