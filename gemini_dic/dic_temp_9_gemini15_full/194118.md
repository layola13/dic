self-improver:/ˈsɛlf ɪmˈpruːvər/| n. |自我提升者，自我改进者 |A person who actively works on improving themselves, often through self-education, personal development, or other means.  |He is a dedicated self-improver, constantly seeking new knowledge and skills.|近义词：self-developer, self-learner |反义词：stagnant, complacent
