nonreliance: /ˌnɒnˈraɪəns/ | n. | 不依赖；不依靠 | The state or condition of not relying on something or someone.  It emphasizes the absence of dependence or trust. |  The company's success is based on nonreliance on any single supplier. 这家公司的成功是建立在不依赖任何单一供应商的基础上。  The policy promotes self-reliance and nonreliance on government assistance.  这项政策提倡自力更生，不依赖政府援助。 | self-reliance, independence | dependence, reliance
