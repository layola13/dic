matched:/mætʃt/ | v. adj. | 匹配的；相配的；相符的 |  To be similar or equal to something else; to find something that is similar or equal.  | 例句：His tie matched his shirt. = 他的领带和衬衫很配。

The two samples matched exactly. = 这两个样本完全匹配。


近义词：paired, coordinated, corresponding, alike, similar | 反义词：mismatched, unsuited, different, dissimilar | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The colors match perfectly. = 这些颜色完美地匹配。
His account matches hers. = 他的说法与她的说法一致。

一般过去时:
The shoes matched her dress. = 这双鞋和她那件衣服很配。
The police matched the fingerprints to the suspect. = 警方将指纹与嫌疑人进行了比对。

现在完成时:
We have matched all the donations to the recipients. = 我们已经将所有捐款与受款人进行了匹配。
The investigators have matched the DNA evidence to the criminal. = 调查人员已将DNA证据与罪犯进行了比对。

过去完成时:
They had matched the suspect's alibi with the witness testimony. = 他们已经将嫌疑人的不在场证明与证人证词进行了比对。
She had matched the fabric before starting the project. = 她开始项目之前已经匹配好面料了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If the colours matched, the painting would be perfect. = 如果颜色匹配，这幅画就完美了。
I wish my socks matched! = 我希望我的袜子能配对！

被动语态:
The results were matched with the control group. = 结果与对照组进行了匹配。

疑问句:

否定句:
The furniture doesn't match the decor. = 家具与装饰不协调。
Her account of events didn't match the witness statements. = 她对事件的描述与证词不符。
