/ˈfækʃənz/| n.|派系；派别；内讧|Groups or cliques within a larger group, often characterized by differing opinions or goals.  These groups may engage in power struggles or conflict within the larger organization.|The political party was torn apart by internal factions.  这个政党被内部派系撕裂了。|The warring factions finally agreed to a ceasefire.  交战的派系最终同意停火。|cliques, sects, groups|unity, harmony|名词
