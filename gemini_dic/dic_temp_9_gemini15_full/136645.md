nonsciatic: /ˌnɒnˈsaɪætɪk/ | adj. | 非坐骨神经的 | Not relating to or affecting the sciatic nerve.  This term describes conditions or pain that do not originate from the sciatic nerve. | Example:  The patient's leg pain was diagnosed as nonsciatic, ruling out sciatica.  患者的腿痛被诊断为非坐骨神经痛，排除了坐骨神经痛的可能性。| 近义词: extrasciatic (less common)| 反义词: sciatic | 词性:形容词
