relighting:/ˌriːˈlaɪtɪŋ/ | n. |重新照明；重新点亮 | The act of lighting something again, or the state of being lit again.  It often refers to the process of illuminating a space or object after a period of darkness or after the previous lighting has failed.  | Example: The relighting of the Christmas tree was a joyful moment for the children.  重新点亮圣诞树是孩子们快乐的时刻。 The emergency relighting system kicked in when the power failed.  当电力中断时，应急照明系统启动了。 | 近义词：reillumination, rekindling | 反义词：extinguishing, darkening |
