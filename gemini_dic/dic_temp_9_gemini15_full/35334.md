co-assessor: /ˌkoʊəˈsɛsər/ | n. | 共同评估员，协同评估人 | A person who assists a main assessor in evaluating something, typically in a formal setting like a tax assessment or legal case.  They share the responsibility and often work collaboratively. | Example: The co-assessor reviewed the evidence alongside the lead assessor before making a final decision.  共同评估员在做出最终决定之前与首席评估员一起审查了证据。 | 近义词: assistant assessor, associate assessor, evaluator | 反义词: principal assessor (in the sense of being the sole assessor)
