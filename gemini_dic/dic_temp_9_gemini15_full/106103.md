knifers:/ˈnaɪfərz/| n.|持刀者；用刀者|People who use knives, often in a threatening or violent way.  It can also refer to a group of people who use knives.|例句：The knifers were apprehended by the police.  持刀者被警方逮捕了。|例句：The gang of knifers terrorized the neighborhood.  持刀团伙恐吓了整个街区。|近义词：knife-wielders, knife-men, thugs|反义词：peacemakers, helpers
