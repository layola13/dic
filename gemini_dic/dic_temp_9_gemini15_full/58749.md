dunghill:/ˈdʌŋhɪl/| n. |粪堆；垃圾堆；卑贱的人 |A dunghill is a heap of dung, typically manure from animals, used as fertilizer.  It can also be used figuratively to refer to something worthless or contemptible. |例句：The farmer spread the dunghill on his fields.  这位农民把粪堆撒在田里。
The politician was called a dunghill by his opponent.  这位政治家被他的对手称为卑鄙小人。|近义词：manure heap, rubbish heap, pile of dung|反义词：treasure, goldmine
