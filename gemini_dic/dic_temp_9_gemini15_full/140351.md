ohps:/ɔːps/| int.|哎呀，哎哟（表示轻微的意外或错误）|An interjection expressing a minor mishap or mistake. It is a shortened and softened version of "oops".|Ohps, I spilled my coffee.  哎呀，我把咖啡洒了。|近义词：oops, whoops|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't conjugate like verbs or nouns.  Therefore, grammatical tense descriptions (present, past, future, etc.) are not applicable.  It stands alone as an expression.


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "ohps".  It is used independently to express a minor mistake or accident.
