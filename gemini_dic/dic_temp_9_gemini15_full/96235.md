/ɪnˈdɪkəl/| adj.|指示的；象征的；预示的|Indicative; suggestive; pointing to something.  It refers to something that points out, shows, or suggests something else.  The word can describe a sign, symptom, or action that suggests a particular meaning or outcome.|例句：The dark clouds are indicial of an approaching storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。

例句：His flushed face was indicial of his anger. 他涨红的脸表明他很生气。


近义词：suggestive, symptomatic, indicative, representative

反义词：unindicative, inconclusive, ambiguous

词性:形容词
