judasim:/ˈdʒuːɪzəm/ | n. |犹太教 |The monotheistic religion of the Jews, characterized by a belief in one God and the observance of the law of Moses.  It is based on the Hebrew Bible, which includes the Torah (the first five books of the Bible) and other writings.  Jewish practices vary widely depending on the specific branch of Judaism (e.g., Orthodox, Conservative, Reform).| Example:  Judaism is one of the world's oldest religions. 犹太教是世界上最古老的宗教之一。 |近义词:Jewish faith, Hebraism |反义词:Christianity, Islam
