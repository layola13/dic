schoolmaster:/ˈskuːlˌmɑːstər/| n. |男校长；男教师|A schoolmaster is a male teacher, especially the head teacher of a school, particularly in the past.  The term is now largely archaic or used in a historical context, often carrying connotations of strictness or traditional methods.|例句：The schoolmaster ruled the school with an iron fist.  这位校长铁腕统治学校。|例句：He was a kind schoolmaster, beloved by his pupils. 他是一位和蔼的校长，深受学生的爱戴。|近义词：teacher, headmaster, master|反义词：pupil, student
