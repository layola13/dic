Noncarbohydrates provide essential nutrients and energy in the diet, playing crucial roles in various bodily functions. |Example:  Fats and proteins are examples of noncarbohydrates.  脂肪和蛋白质是非碳水化合物的例子。|近义词: non-CHO,  |反义词: carbohydrates |名词
