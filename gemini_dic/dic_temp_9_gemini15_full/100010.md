interpenetrated:/ˌɪntərˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪd/ | v. |相互渗透；互相交织 | To penetrate or pass into each other; to be interwoven or interconnected.  The two cultures had interpenetrated, influencing each other's traditions and customs. |近义词：intermingle, permeate, interweave |反义词：separate, isolate
