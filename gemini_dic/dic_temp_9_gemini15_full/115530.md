map:/mæp/| n. /ˈmæp/ |地图，图，示意图|A map is a symbolic representation of geographic features.  Maps may be used to represent many things such as roads, buildings, etc.  |例句：I need a map to find my way around this city. 我需要一张地图才能找到在这座城市里走动的方法。

例句：This map shows the distribution of population. 这张地图显示了人口分布。|近义词：chart, plan, diagram|反义词：territory, area
