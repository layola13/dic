nope:/noʊp/| int.|不，才不|An informal way of saying "no".  It is a more emphatic and often playful way of disagreeing or rejecting something. |例句： "Do you want to go to the movies?" "Nope, I'm tired."  "你想去看电影吗？" "不，我累了。"

例句："Are you coming to the party?" "Nope, I have other plans." "你参加派对吗？" "不，我有其他计划。"


近义词：no, nah


反义词：yes, yeah


词性: interjection (感叹词)
