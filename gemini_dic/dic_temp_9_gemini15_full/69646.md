femur:/ˈfiːmər/| n.|股骨|The femur is the longest and strongest bone in the human body, located in the thigh.  It connects the hip to the knee.|例句：The fracture of his femur required surgery.他的股骨骨折需要手术。

例句：The doctor examined the patient's femur for signs of injury.医生检查了病人的股骨是否有受伤的迹象。
