fireable: /ˈfaɪərəbl/ | adj. | 可解雇的，可开除的 | Capable of being fired or dismissed from a job.  It implies that the person's actions or performance warrant dismissal. |例句：His behavior was completely unprofessional and made him fireable. 他的行为完全不专业，使他面临被解雇的风险。

His consistent lateness made him fireable. 他经常迟到，让他面临被解雇的风险。


近义词：dismissable, dischargeable, terminable | 反义词：indispensable, irreplaceable

词性：形容词
