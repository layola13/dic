slaughters:/ˈslɔːtərz/| n.|屠杀；大量杀戮|The act of slaughtering animals, typically for food.  It can also refer to the killing of a large number of people.|例句：The factory slaughters thousands of chickens daily.  这家工厂每天屠宰数千只鸡。

例句：The massacre was a brutal slaughter of innocent civilians.这场大屠杀是对无辜平民的残酷屠杀。


近义词：massacre, carnage, butchery, killing

反义词：saving, preservation, protection


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory slaughters thousands of chickens every day. = 工厂每天屠宰数千只鸡。
He slaughters pigs for a living. = 他以屠宰猪为生。


一般过去时:
They slaughtered the cattle before the festival. = 他们在节日之前宰杀了牛。
The soldiers slaughtered the villagers. = 士兵们屠杀了村民。


现在完成时:
The company has slaughtered millions of animals this year. = 今年该公司已经屠杀了数百万动物。
The rebels have slaughtered many innocent people. = 反叛分子已经杀害了许多无辜的人。


过去完成时:
They had slaughtered all the sheep before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经宰杀完了所有的羊。
The army had slaughtered the enemy before reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，军队已经消灭了敌人。


将来时:
They will slaughter the pigs tomorrow morning. = 他们明天早上将宰杀猪。
The farmers will slaughter their livestock in the fall. = 农民们将在秋季宰杀他们的牲畜。


虚拟语气:
If they slaughtered the animals humanely, it would be better. = 如果他们人道地宰杀动物，那就好了。
I wish they hadn't slaughtered so many innocent people. = 我真希望他们没有屠杀那么多无辜的人。


被动语态:
Thousands of chickens are slaughtered daily at the factory. = 工厂每天屠宰数千只鸡。

疑问句:

否定句:
