neurography:/nʊˈrɒɡrəfi/ | n. |神经影像学；神经图谱；神经造影术 | The study of the nervous system using imaging techniques.  Neurography involves creating images of nerves to diagnose and monitor various neurological conditions.  | Example:  The neurography revealed damage to the ulnar nerve. 神经影像学显示尺神经受损。 |近义词：nerve imaging, nerve study |反义词：N/A
