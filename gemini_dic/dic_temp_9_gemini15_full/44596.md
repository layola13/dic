crowbarred:/ˈkraʊˌbɑːrd/| vt.|撬开；强行插入；勉强塞进|To force something open or into place using a crowbar.  It implies a forceful and often damaging action.|例句：He crowbarred the window open. 他用撬棍撬开了窗户。

例句：They crowbarred their way into the conversation. 他们强行插进谈话中。


近义词：pry open, force open, lever open, wedge open

反义词：close gently, open smoothly


USAGE->

He crowbars the box open every day. = 他每天都用撬棍撬开箱子。
They crowbar new ideas into the discussion. = 他们强行把新的想法塞进讨论中。
