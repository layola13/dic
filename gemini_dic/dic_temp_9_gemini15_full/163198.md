precompliant:/ˌpriːkəmˈplaɪənt/| adj. |预先符合的，提前符合的|  Describes something that meets requirements or standards before they are officially enforced or implemented.  It suggests proactive compliance, going above and beyond minimum expectations.|

例句：

The company implemented precompliant security measures to avoid future data breaches.  = 公司实施了预先符合的安全措施，以避免未来的数据泄露。

Their precompliant environmental practices earned them a prestigious award. = 他们超前的环保措施为他们赢得了声誉卓著的奖项。


近义词：proactive, anticipatory, ahead-of-the-curve


反义词：noncompliant, defiant,  delinquent


词性：形容词
