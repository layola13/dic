recovery: /rɪˈkʌvəri/ | n. | 康复；恢复；痊愈；挽回；复原 | The process of regaining or returning to a normal state after illness, injury, loss, or hardship.  It can refer to physical health, mental well-being, economic conditions, or data. | 例句：Her recovery from the surgery was remarkably quick.  她的手术恢复得非常快。

例句：The economy is showing signs of recovery after the recession.  经济在衰退后显示出复苏的迹象。


近义词：rehabilitation, recuperation, restoration, retrieval, comeback | 反义词：deterioration, decline, relapse


USAGE->

Recovery is a slow process. = 康复是一个缓慢的过程。
The company is focusing on market recovery. = 公司正专注于市场复苏。


一般过去时:
