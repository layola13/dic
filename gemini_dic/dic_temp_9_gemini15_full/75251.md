from the ground up:/frɒm ðə ɡraʊnd ʌp/| adj. phrase|从零开始；彻底地；完全地|Completely; from the beginning, with no prior foundation.|The company was built from the ground up by a small team. 这家公司是由一个小团队从零开始创建的。|近义词：from scratch, anew, entirely|反义词：pre-existing, established

We are designing the software from the ground up. = 我们正在从头开始设计这个软件。
