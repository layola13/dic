cantered:/ˈkæntəd/| v.|慢跑，缓步走|To move at a slow, easy pace, especially of a horse.|The horse cantered gracefully across the field.  那匹马优雅地慢跑过田野。The children cantered along the beach. 孩子们沿着海滩慢跑。|trot, amble|gallop, sprint|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He canters his horse every morning. 他每天早上骑马慢跑。
She canters along the path. 她沿着小路慢跑。


一般过去时:
The rider cantered his horse through the woods. 骑手骑着马慢跑穿过树林。
They cantered down the lane. 他们沿着小路慢跑。


现在完成时:
He has cantered several miles this morning. 他今天早上已经慢跑了数英里。
She has cantered along the beach many times. 她沿着海滩慢跑过很多次。


过去完成时:
They had cantered for hours before they reached the village. 在到达村庄之前，他们已经慢跑了几个小时。
She had cantered most of the way before her horse stumbled. 在她的马绊倒之前，她已经慢跑了一大部分路程。


将来时:
We will canter along the river tomorrow. 我们明天将在河边慢跑。
He will canter his horse in the competition. 他将在比赛中骑马慢跑。


虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would canter across the fields. 如果我有一匹马，我会在田野里慢跑。
I wish I could canter as gracefully as she does. 我希望我能像她一样优雅地慢跑。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The horse was cantered gently by the rider.  这匹马被骑手轻轻地慢跑着。(不太自然)
The race was won by the rider who cantered steadily throughout. 比赛是由始终稳步慢跑的骑手赢得的。(更侧重比赛结果)


疑问句:
Does he often canter his horse? 他经常骑马慢跑吗？
Will they canter in the race? 他们会在比赛中慢跑吗？


否定句:
He didn't canter his horse today. 他今天没有骑马慢跑。
She doesn't usually canter that fast. 她通常不会跑那么快。


PHRASE->
canter along: 沿着…慢跑
canter away: 跑开
canter past: 跑过
canter to: 跑向
at a canter: 以慢跑的速度
