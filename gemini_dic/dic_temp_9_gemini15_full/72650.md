flyweights:/ˈflaɪˌweɪts/| n.|轻量级拳击手；轻量级选手；轻量级物件|A flyweight is a boxer or other competitor who weighs in at the lowest weight class for their sport.  It can also refer to something that is very light. |例句：He's a flyweight boxer, known for his speed and agility. 他是一位轻量级拳击手，以速度和敏捷著称。

例句：The new laptop is a flyweight, weighing in at less than two pounds. 这款新笔记本电脑非常轻便，重量不到两磅。


近义词：lightweight (adj./n.)


反义词：heavyweight (adj./n.)


词源:The word "flyweight" comes from the boxing term.  The "fly" part refers to the small size and weight,  like a fly.  "Weight" is the standard English word for mass.
