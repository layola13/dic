shearbill:/ˈʃɪərbɪl/| n. |剪嘴鸥|A seabird of the family Procellariidae, characterized by a laterally compressed bill with a sharp cutting edge. Shearwaters are known for their long, narrow wings and their gliding flight. They are found in oceans worldwide.  |Example: The shearbill effortlessly glided over the ocean waves.|近义词：None precisely, but other Procellariidae members like petrel might be considered similar. |反义词：None.
