overtenacious: /ˌoʊvərteˈneɪʃəs/ | adj. | 过于固执的，顽固的 | Excessively tenacious; clinging too firmly to something.  It implies an undesirable degree of persistence or stubbornness.  | He was overtenacious in his pursuit of the deal, alienating potential partners. 他在追求这笔交易时过于固执，疏远了潜在的合作伙伴。|近义词：obstinate, stubborn, inflexible, pertinacious |反义词：yielding, flexible, compliant, adaptable
