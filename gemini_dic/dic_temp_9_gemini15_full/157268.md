pinochet:/pɪnɔˈʃeɪ/| n. |皮诺切特|Augusto Pinochet Ugarte (1915-2006), a Chilean army general and dictator who ruled Chile from 1973 to 1990.  He seized power in a coup d'état that overthrew the democratically elected government of Salvador Allende. His regime was marked by widespread human rights abuses, including torture, murder, and disappearances.  He was later indicted on charges of human rights violations but died before his trial concluded.|The Pinochet dictatorship was a dark chapter in Chilean history.|近义词：dictator, tyrant, autocrat |反义词：democrat, reformer
