It can refer to a machine that reconditions something, or a chemical product used for reconditioning.|例句：The car mechanic used a special reconditioner to restore the engine's performance. 汽车修理工使用了一种特殊的修复剂来恢复发动机的性能。

例句：The factory uses a sophisticated reconditioner to refurbish used car parts.  这家工厂使用先进的翻新设备来翻新旧汽车零件。
