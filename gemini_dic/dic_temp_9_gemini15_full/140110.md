off-putting: /ˌɒfˈpʊtɪŋ/ | /ˌɔːfˈpʊtɪŋ/ | adj. |令人厌恶的；使人反感的；讨厌的 |  Something that is off-putting is unpleasant or makes someone dislike it. It creates a feeling of distaste or aversion.  |


The smell of the rotting fish was extremely off-putting.  = 腐烂鱼的臭味非常令人作呕。

His aggressive manner was off-putting to many people. = 他咄咄逼人的态度让很多人反感。


近义词: repulsive, repellent, disagreeable, unpleasant, distasteful, objectionable, unappealing


反义词: appealing, attractive, pleasant, agreeable, charming
