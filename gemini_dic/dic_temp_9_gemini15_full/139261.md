obliges:/əˈblaɪdʒ/| vt.| 使…感到必须；迫使；施恩惠于；使感激 | To make someone feel that they must do something.  |例句：The law obliges companies to provide details of their emissions. 法律迫使公司提供其排放物的详细信息。|例句：I'm obliged to you for your help.  承蒙你帮忙，我非常感激。|近义词：compel, force, require, necessitate |反义词：free, allow, permit |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contract obliges us to pay on time. = 合同迫使我们按时付款。
His beliefs oblige him to act honestly. = 他的信仰迫使他诚实地行事。


一般过去时:
The storm obliged us to stay indoors. = 暴风雨迫使我们待在室内。
She obliged me by singing a song. = 她为我唱歌，让我非常感激。


现在完成时:
We have been obliged to cancel the trip. = 我们不得不取消旅行了。
The circumstances have obliged me to act differently. = 情况使我不得不采取不同的行动。


过去完成时:
The bad weather had obliged them to postpone the match. = 恶劣的天气迫使他们推迟了比赛。
I had been obliged to borrow money to pay the bills. = 我不得不借钱来支付账单。


将来时:
We will be obliged to find another solution. = 我们将不得不找到另一个解决方案。
The new regulations will oblige businesses to adapt. = 新规将迫使企业适应。


虚拟语气:
If he were obliged to choose, he would choose the latter. = 如果他被迫选择，他会选择后者。
I wish I weren't obliged to work overtime. = 我希望我不必加班。


被动语态:
We are obliged to follow the rules. = 我们必须遵守规则。(被动语态更常用于表达义务)
The company was obliged to pay a fine. = 公司被迫支付罚款。


疑问句:
Are we obliged to attend the meeting? = 我们必须参加会议吗？
Is he obliged to repay the loan? = 他必须偿还贷款吗？


否定句:
I am not obliged to explain myself. = 我没有义务解释我自己。
They are not obliged to accept the offer. = 他们没有义务接受这个提议。


PHRASE->
be obliged to do sth. = 不得不做某事
feel obliged to do sth. = 觉得有义务做某事
obliged to someone = 感激某人
under obligation to someone = 对某人有义务
feel obliged = 觉得有义务
