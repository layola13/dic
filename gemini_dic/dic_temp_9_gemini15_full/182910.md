repetend:/ˈrɛpɪtɛnd/ | n. |循环小数 |The repeating part of a decimal;  a repeating sequence of digits in a decimal representation of a rational number.  For example, in the decimal representation of 1/3 (0.333...), the repetend is 3. |The repetend of the decimal expansion of 1/7 is 142857.  这个分数1/7的十进制展开式中的循环节是142857。|近义词：repeating decimal, recurring decimal |反义词：terminating decimal
