pathological:/ˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/|/ˌpæθəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/| adj. |病理学的；病态的；患病的 | Relating to or caused by disease; departing from normal function.  It often implies a condition that is extreme, uncontrollable, or obsessive. |例句：His pathological need for control led to strained relationships. 他的控制欲极强，导致人际关系紧张。

例句：The doctor found pathological changes in the patient's lungs. 医生在病人的肺部发现了病理变化。
