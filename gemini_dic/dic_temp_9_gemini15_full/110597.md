limits:/ˈlɪmɪts/| n.|限制，界限，极限|The limits of human endurance are often tested in extreme situations. 人类的耐力极限经常在极端情况下受到考验。|近义词：boundaries, restrictions, constraints|反义词：infinity, boundlessness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The speed limit is 60 miles per hour. = 限速每小时60英里。
There are limits to what we can achieve. = 我们的成就也是有限度的。


一般过去时:
The doctor set limits on his activity. = 医生限制了他的活动。
We reached the limits of our knowledge. = 我们达到了我们知识的极限。


现在完成时:

The project has exceeded its budget limits. = 这个项目已经超过了预算限制。


过去完成时:
