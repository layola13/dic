overinstructiveness:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌktɪvnəs/| n. |过度指导，指令过多|Excessive guidance or instruction, often to the point of being counterproductive.  It can stifle creativity, independent thinking, and problem-solving skills. |The teacher's overinstructiveness led to student frustration and a lack of engagement.|近义词：overdirection, micromanagement|反义词：underguidance, laissez-faire
