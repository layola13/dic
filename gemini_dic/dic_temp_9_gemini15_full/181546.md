relabels: /ˌriːˈleɪbəlz/ | v. |重新标记，重新贴标签 | To change the labels on something.  This usually involves replacing old labels with new ones, often to correct errors or reflect a change in categorization. | Example: The store relabels its products every season to reflect the latest trends.  = 商店每季都会重新贴上产品标签以反映最新的潮流。 The librarian relabels the books after the library's reorganization. = 图书馆员在图书馆重组后重新给书籍贴标签。|近义词：retag, remark, redesignate |反义词：retain original labels
