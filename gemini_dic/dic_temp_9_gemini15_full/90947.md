hurricane:/ˈhɜːrɪkeɪn/| n. |飓风，暴风|A hurricane is a powerful storm with very strong winds that forms over warm ocean water.  |例句：The hurricane caused widespread damage.  飓风造成了广泛的破坏。|近义词：typhoon, cyclone |反义词：calm, breeze
