saxifragaceous:/ˌsæksɪˈfræɡeɪʃəs/| adj.|虎耳草科的|Of or relating to the Saxifragaceae, a family of flowering plants that includes saxifrages and related genera.  The family is characterized by its usually five-petaled flowers and often succulent foliage. Many species are found in mountainous or rocky regions.|The saxifragaceous plants are well-adapted to harsh, rocky environments.|近义词：saxifrage-like|反义词：无
