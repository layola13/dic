europhobe:/ˈjʊərəfoʊb/| n. |欧洲恐惧症患者；反欧洲情绪者 |A person who is strongly prejudiced against or dislikes Europe or Europeans.  |例句：He is considered a europhobe due to his outspoken criticism of European integration. 他因公开批评欧洲一体化而被认为是欧洲恐惧症患者。  |近义词：anti-European, Europhobic (adj.) |反义词：philhellene, Europhile
