cembalo:/tʃɛmˈbɑːloʊ/| n. |古钢琴；羽管键琴|An early keyboard instrument, a precursor to the piano, with strings plucked by plectra.  It was popular in the Baroque period.|The cembalo provided the continuo in many Baroque compositions.|近义词：harpsichord, clavichord|反义词：piano (in terms of mechanism)|名词
