geraniums:/dʒəˈreɪniəmz/| n. |天竺葵|Geraniums are flowering plants known for their vibrant colors and ease of cultivation. They are popular choices for gardens and window boxes.|例句：The geraniums in her garden were in full bloom.  她花园里的天竺葵盛开了。|例句：She bought several potted geraniums to brighten her balcony. 她买了几盆天竺葵来装饰她的阳台。|近义词：pelargoniums|反义词：无
