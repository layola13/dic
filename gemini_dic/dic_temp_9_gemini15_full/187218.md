robbers:/ˈrɑːbərz/ | n. |强盗，盗贼 |People who rob others.  They steal money or property from others illegally. |例句：The robbers escaped with a large sum of money. 强盗们带着一大笔钱逃跑了。|例句：The police caught the robbers red-handed. 警察当场抓获了强盗。|近义词：thieves, burglars, bandits |反义词：victims, police
