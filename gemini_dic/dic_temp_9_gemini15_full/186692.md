righted:/ˈraɪtɪd/ | adj. | 纠正的；已改正的；已扶正的 | Corrected; made right; set upright.  |例句：The righted ship sailed smoothly.  已纠正航向的船顺利航行。|例句：The righted picture now hangs straight. 已经扶正的画现在挂得笔直了。|近义词：corrected, rectified, adjusted |反义词：wrong, incorrect, tilted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The book is righted. = 书已经摆正了。
The mistake is righted. = 错误已经纠正了。

一般过去时:
He righted the fallen vase. = 他扶起了摔倒的花瓶。
She righted the wrongs of the past. = 她纠正了过去的错误。

现在完成时:
They have righted the situation. = 他们已经纠正了局面。
The problem has been righted. = 问题已经解决了。

过去完成时:
He had righted the wrong before anyone noticed. = 他在任何人注意到之前就纠正了错误。
She had righted the ship before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，她已经扶正了船。

将来时:
We will right the wrongs. = 我们将会纠正错误。
They will right the injustice. = 他们将会纠正不公正。

虚拟语气:
If he had righted the situation earlier, the consequences wouldn't have been so severe. = 如果他早点纠正情况，后果就不会那么严重了。
I wish I had righted my mistake sooner. = 我希望我早点纠正我的错误。

被动语态:
The situation was righted quickly. = 局面很快得到纠正。
The wrongs will be righted eventually. = 错误最终会被纠正。

疑问句:
Has the problem been righted? = 问题解决了吗？
Is the picture righted? = 图片扶正了吗？

否定句:
He didn't right the wrong. = 他没有纠正错误。
The situation hasn't been righted yet. = 局面还没有得到纠正。
