gamed:/ɡeɪmd/| v.|玩游戏；操纵；设圈套|To play a game or games; to manipulate or exploit a system or situation to one's advantage; to trick or deceive.|例句：He gamed all night. 他玩了一整夜的游戏。|例句：They gamed the system to get a tax break. 他们操纵系统以获得减税。|近义词：played, manipulated, cheated, tricked|反义词：lost, failed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He games every evening. = 他每天晚上都玩游戏。
She games online with her friends. = 她和朋友们一起在线玩游戏。

一般过去时:
We gamed until dawn. = 我们玩游戏直到黎明。
They gamed the lottery numbers. = 他们操纵了彩票号码。

现在完成时:
I have gamed this strategy before. = 我以前用过这个策略。

过去完成时:

They had gamed the entire tournament before the final match. = 在决赛之前，他们已经控制了整个锦标赛。


将来时:
