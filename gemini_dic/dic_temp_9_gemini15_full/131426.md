nonclinically: /ˌnɒnˈklɪnɪkli/ | adj. | 非临床地 | Not in a clinical setting; outside of a clinical trial or medical practice.  This term describes something that happens or is done outside of a formal medical environment, such as a hospital or doctor's office. |


例句：

The drug's effectiveness was observed nonclinically in animal studies.  = 该药物的有效性在动物实验中得到非临床观察。

Researchers gathered nonclinical data through surveys and interviews. = 研究人员通过调查和访谈收集了非临床数据。


近义词：experimentally, preclinically, informally


反义词：clinically


词性：形容词
