kids:/kɪdz/| n. |孩子们，小孩们 |Children; young people.  The word is the plural of "kid".|例句：The kids are playing in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩耍。
例句：We took the kids to the zoo. 我们带孩子们去了动物园。|近义词：children, youngsters, minors |反义词：adults, grown-ups
