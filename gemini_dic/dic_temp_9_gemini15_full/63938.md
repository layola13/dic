epipterygoid:/ˌɛpɪˈtɛrɪɡɔɪd/| n. |翼蝶骨|A small bone in the skull of many vertebrates, situated behind the pterygoid and articulating with the quadrate and the braincase.  It contributes to the suspension of the jaw. |The epipterygoid bone is a crucial element in the jaw suspension system of many reptiles.|近义词：None (highly specific anatomical term)|反义词：None (highly specific anatomical term)|名词
