intellectual:/ˌɪntəˈlektʃuəl/ | adj., n. |知识分子；智力的；聪明的 |  Relating to or involving intelligence, especially of a high level.  A person who uses their mind creatively and is interested in things such as art, science, literature, etc. |例句：He's a highly intellectual person with a wide range of interests. 他是一位知识渊博、兴趣广泛的人。
He showed great intellectual curiosity. 他表现出极大的求知欲。|近义词：intelligent, brainy, academic, scholarly |反义词：unintelligent, ignorant, uncultured

She demonstrates intellectual prowess. = 她展现出非凡的智力。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

词源:

The word "intellectual" comes from the Latin word "intellectus," meaning "understanding" or "intelligence."  It entered English in the 16th century.
