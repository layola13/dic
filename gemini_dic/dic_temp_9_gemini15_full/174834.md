rachiodont: /ˈreɪkiədɒnt/ | adj. | 脊椎齿的；指具有脊状齿的 | Relating to or characterized by having teeth with ridges or crests.  The term typically refers to the teeth of certain extinct reptiles and amphibians. | The *rachiodont* dentition of the fossil reptile is a key feature in its classification.  | 近义词: ridged-toothed (informal) | 反义词:  acrodont, pleurodont (referring to different tooth attachment types)
