overextrapolations:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstræpəleɪʃənz/| n. |过度外推；过度推断|The act of extending or applying a conclusion, principle, or method beyond its legitimate limits or range of applicability.  It often leads to inaccurate or misleading predictions.  |例句：The company's overextrapolations of their initial market success led to significant financial losses.  这家公司对其最初市场成功的过度推断导致了巨大的经济损失。

例句：His overextrapolations from a small sample size resulted in flawed conclusions. 他从小样本量中过度推断导致结论有误。|近义词：overgeneralization, extrapolation beyond the data, unwarranted inference|反义词：cautious prediction, interpolation, careful analysis|词性:名词
