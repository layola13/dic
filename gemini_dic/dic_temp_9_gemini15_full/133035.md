It often refers to a situation, condition, or object that is undesirable or unattractive, lacking qualities that would inspire envy.  | Example: The nonenviableness of his job was evident in his perpetually tired expression.  他的工作不令人羡慕，这从他总是疲惫的表情中就能看出来。| 近义词: undesirability, unattractiveness, repulsiveness | 反义词: enviableness, desirability, attractiveness | 名词
