dolphinariums:/ˌdɒlfɪˈneəriəm/| n. |海豚馆|A dolphinarium is a facility where dolphins are kept and trained for display or research.  It is often part of a larger marine park or zoo.| Example: The children were excited to visit the dolphinarium and see the dolphins perform. 孩子们很兴奋能参观海豚馆并观看海豚表演。|近义词：marine park, oceanarium|反义词：N/A
