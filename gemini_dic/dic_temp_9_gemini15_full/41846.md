corymb:/ˈkɒrɪmb/| n. |圆锥花序；伞房花序|A corymb is a type of inflorescence (flower cluster) where the individual flower stalks (pedicels) arise from different points on the stem, but reach approximately the same height, creating a flat-topped or slightly convex cluster. Unlike umbels, where all pedicels arise from a common point, corymbs have pedicels arising at varying heights along the stem.  The outer flowers typically open first. |The hydrangea boasts a beautiful corymb of flowers.  绣球花拥有美丽的圆锥花序。|近义词：raceme, panicle, umbel|反义词：无
