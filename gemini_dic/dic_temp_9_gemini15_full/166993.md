presubmitted:/ˌpriːsəbˈmɪtɪd/| adj.| 预先提交的，已预先提交的 |  Already submitted before the official deadline or designated time.  This word is formed by combining the prefix "pre-" (meaning before) with the past participle "submitted". It describes something that has been handed in or presented earlier than expected or required. | Example: The presubmitted documents were reviewed by the committee. 预先提交的文件已由委员会审核。 | 近义词：pre-filed, beforehand submitted | 反义词：unsubmitted, late-submitted
