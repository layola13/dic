madrina:/mɑːˈdriːnɑː/| n. |教母；女教父|Godmother; a woman who acts as a sponsor at a baptism.  It also refers to a woman who is like a godmother in terms of close guidance and support.|例句：She is my madrina and has always been a strong influence in my life. 她是我的教母，一直对我影响很大。

例句：My madrina helped me through a difficult time. 我的教母帮助我度过了一段艰难的时期。


近义词：godmother, sponsor, mentor


反义词：godfather (masculine equivalent)


词性:名词


词源:源自西班牙语，由madre（母亲）演变而来，加上表示女性的后缀-ina。  It reflects the maternal role and close relationship a madrina has with her godchild.
