saddlebags:/ˈsædəlˌbæɡz/| n.|马鞍袋；(摩托车等的)边箱|Bags attached to the sides of a saddle or a motorcycle.  They are used for carrying items.|例句：The cowboy packed his saddlebags with supplies for the long journey. 牛仔把他的马鞍袋装满了长途旅行的补给品。

例句：The motorcycle's saddlebags were full of his camping gear. 这辆摩托车的边箱里装满了他的露营装备。
