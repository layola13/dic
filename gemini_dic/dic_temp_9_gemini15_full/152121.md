pathologies:/pəˈθɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 病理学；疾病；病态 | The study of the nature of disease and its causes, processes, development, and consequences.  It also refers to specific diseases or abnormal conditions. | 例句：The doctor ordered further tests to investigate the pathologies underlying his symptoms.  医生下令进行进一步检查以调查他症状背后的病理。

The study revealed several pathologies associated with long-term alcohol abuse. 研究揭示了几种与长期酗酒相关的疾病。| 近义词：diseases, disorders, ailments, dysfunctions | 反义词：health, wellness, normalcy
