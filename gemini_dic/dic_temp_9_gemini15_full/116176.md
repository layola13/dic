masculinize:/ˈmæskjʊlaɪz/ | vt. |使男性化；使具有男子气概|To make or become more masculine in appearance or character.  The process or result of making something more masculine. |例句：He tried to masculinize his appearance by growing a beard. 他试图通过留胡子来使自己的外表更男性化。

例句：The film's portrayal of the female character attempts to masculinize her role. 这部电影对女性角色的刻画试图使其角色更男性化。


近义词：manly, virility, toughen

反义词：feminize


词性：动词
