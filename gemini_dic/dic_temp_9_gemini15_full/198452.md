shrews:/ʃruːz/| n. | shrew鼠;泼妇;坏女人|A shrew is a small, mouse-like mammal with a long snout and tiny eyes.  Shrews are also sometimes used to describe a bad-tempered woman. |例句：The shrew darted through the undergrowth.  (鼩鼱在灌木丛中窜过。)  She's a real shrew, always nagging at her husband. (她是个十足的泼妇，总是唠叨她丈夫。) |近义词：vixen, termagant, scold|反义词：angel, saint
