catalysis:/kəˈtæləsɪs/| n. |催化作用；催化 |The process by which a catalyst accelerates a chemical reaction without being consumed.  Catalysis is crucial in many industrial processes and biological systems. |Example: The use of a platinum catalyst speeds up the hydrogenation of alkenes.  铂催化剂的使用加速了烯烃的氢化反应。|近义词：acceleration, facilitation |反义词：inhibition

WORD ORIGIN->
