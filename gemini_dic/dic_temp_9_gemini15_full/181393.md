reirrigating:/ˌriːˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/| v.|再次灌溉；重新灌溉|The act of irrigating again; to water (land) again.|例句：After the drought, the farmers were busy reirrigating their fields. 干旱过后，农民们忙着再次灌溉他们的田地。
例句：The parched land needed reirrigating immediately. 干涸的土地需要立即重新灌溉。|近义词：rewatering, irrigating again|反义词：dehydrating, drying out|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reirrigate the fields every week. = 他们每周都重新灌溉田地。
Farmers reirrigate their crops when needed. = 农民在需要时会重新灌溉农作物。

一般过去时:
The farmers reirrigated their fields after the long dry spell. = 长期干旱后，农民们重新灌溉了他们的田地。
We reirrigated the lawn after the hot summer. = 炎热的夏天过后，我们重新灌溉了草坪。

现在完成时:
They have reirrigated the parched land twice this month. = 这个月他们已经两次重新灌溉了干旱的土地。
The gardener has reirrigated the flower beds since the recent heatwave. = 园丁自从最近的热浪以来，已经重新灌溉了花坛。

过去完成时:
The crops had been reirrigated before the inspectors arrived. = 检查员到达之前，农作物已经重新灌溉过了。
The land had reirrigated naturally before the rain arrived. = 下雨之前，土地已经自然地重新灌溉了。

将来时:
We will reirrigate the garden tomorrow if it doesn't rain. = 如果明天不下雨，我们将重新灌溉花园。
The farmers will reirrigate their fields once the new irrigation system is installed. = 一旦新的灌溉系统安装完毕，农民们将重新灌溉他们的田地。

虚拟语气:
If we had reirrigated the plants earlier, they wouldn't have died. = 如果我们早点重新灌溉植物，它们就不会死了。
It would be beneficial if they reirrigated the crops more frequently. = 如果他们更频繁地重新灌溉农作物，那将是有益的。
