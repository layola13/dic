revise:/rɪˈvaɪz/ | vt. | 修订；修改；复习；校正 | To reconsider and alter (something, especially a plan or text) in the light of further information.  |例句：The editor asked me to revise the manuscript before publication. 编辑让我在出版前修改稿件。

例句：I need to revise for the upcoming exam. 我需要复习即将到来的考试。


近义词：amend, correct, edit, modify | 反义词：originate, create


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revises his work every day. = 他每天都修改他的作品。
She revises her lesson plans regularly. = 她定期修改她的教学计划。


一般过去时:
They revised the contract last week. = 他们上周修改了合同。
He revised his essay before submitting it. = 他在提交论文前修改了他的论文。


现在完成时:
I have revised the document three times. = 我已经修改了这个文件三次了。
She has revised her opinion on the matter. = 她已经改变了她对这件事的看法。


过去完成时:
He had revised the chapter before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经修改了那一章。
They had revised the strategy several times before implementing it. = 他们实施战略之前已经修改过好几次了。


将来时:
We will revise the budget next month. = 我们下个月将修改预算。
She will revise her presentation before the conference. = 她将在会议前修改她的演示文稿。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would revise my paper more thoroughly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更彻底地修改我的论文。
It would be better if they revised their approach. = 如果他们修改他们的方法会更好。


被动语态:
The document was revised by the editor. = 文件由编辑修改。
The plans were revised to accommodate the new data. = 计划已被修改以适应新的数据。


疑问句:
Have you revised your notes yet? = 你复习你的笔记了吗？
Will the contract be revised? = 合同会修改吗？


否定句:
He did not revise his notes before the test. = 他考试前没有复习笔记。
They haven't revised their proposal yet. = 他们还没有修改他们的提案。



PHRASE->
revise a draft = 修改草稿
revise a text = 修改文本
revise a plan = 修改计划
revise a policy = 修改政策
revise for an exam = 为考试复习
