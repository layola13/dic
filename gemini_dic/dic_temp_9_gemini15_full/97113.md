infield:/ˈɪnfiːld/| n. |内野，内场|The infield is the area of a baseball or softball field inside the bases.  It's where most of the action happens during the game, with infielders positioned to field ground balls and make plays. |The shortstop made a diving catch in the infield. 短停在内野做了一个鱼跃接球。The infield was covered in mud after the rain. 雨后内野满是泥土。|近义词：inner field|反义词：outfield|名词
