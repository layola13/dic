condoler:/kənˈdoʊlər/| vt.|慰问，表示同情|To express sympathy to someone, especially on the occasion of a death.  |例句：I condoled with her on the loss of her father. 我向她表示慰问，因为她父亲去世了。|近义词：sympathize, commiserate, console|反义词：celebrate, rejoice


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I condole with you on your loss. = 我对你失去...表示慰问。
They condole with the bereaved family. = 他们向丧亲的家人表示慰问。


一般过去时:
She condoled with him after his wife's death. = 他妻子去世后，她向他表示慰问。
We condoled with our friend on the passing of his grandmother. = 我们对朋友祖母去世表示慰问。


现在完成时:
I have condoled with several families this week. = 这周我已经向好几个家庭表示了慰问。
She has condoled with the family since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，她一直向这个家庭表示慰问。


过去完成时:
They had condoled with her before she left for the funeral. = 在她去参加葬礼之前，他们已经向她表示慰问了。
We had condoled with the family when we heard the news. = 当我们听到这个消息时，我们已经向这个家庭表示慰问了。


将来时:
I will condole with her when I see her. = 我见到她时会向她表示慰问。
They will condole with him at the funeral. = 他们将在葬礼上向他表示慰问。


虚拟语气:
If I knew how to properly condole, I would. = 如果我知道如何恰当地慰问，我会这样做。
I wish I could have condoled with him sooner. = 我希望我能早点向他表示慰问。


被动语态:
The family was condoled with by many friends. = 这个家庭受到了许多朋友的慰问。
He was condoled with by his colleagues. = 他受到了同事们的慰问。


疑问句:
Did you condole with the family? = 你向这个家庭表示慰问了吗？
Will you be condoling with them at the service? = 你会在仪式上向他们表示慰问吗？


否定句:
I didn't condole with him, I didn't know what to say. = 我没有向他表示慰问，我不知道该说什么。
They did not condole with the family publicly. = 他们没有公开向这个家庭表示慰问。


PHRASE->
condole with someone = 向某人表示慰问
offer condolences = 表示慰问
express condolences = 表示哀悼
send condolences = 致以慰问
receive condolences = 收到慰问
