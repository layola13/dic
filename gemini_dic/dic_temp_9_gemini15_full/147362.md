overnationalizes: /ˌoʊvərˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |使超越国家；使高于国家利益|To attribute or explain (something, such as a phenomenon or behavior) in terms of factors that transcend national boundaries or interests; to give an interpretation that emphasizes supranational or international aspects over national ones.|例句：The treaty overnationalizes the issue of environmental protection, treating it as a global rather than a national concern.  该条约将环境保护问题置于国家利益之上，将其视为全球性而非国家性问题。

例句：Some critics argue that the EU overnationalizes certain policy areas, thus diminishing the sovereignty of member states. 一些批评人士认为，欧盟将某些政策领域置于国家利益之上，从而削弱了成员国的自主权。


近义词：internationalize, globalize, transcend


反义词：nationalize, localize


词性：动词
