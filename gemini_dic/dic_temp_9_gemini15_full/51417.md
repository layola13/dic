deterrents: /dɪˈtɛrənts/ | n. |威慑物；制止因素 | Something that discourages or prevents a certain action.  A deterrent is something that makes someone less likely to do something.  It can be a threat, a penalty, or simply something unpleasant. |

例句：The high price of cigarettes acts as a deterrent to young smokers. 高昂的香烟价格对年轻吸烟者起到了威慑作用。

例句：Fear of punishment is a powerful deterrent to crime. 对惩罚的恐惧是对犯罪的有力威慑。


近义词：disincentive, restraint, curb, check

反义词：incentive, encouragement

词性：名词
