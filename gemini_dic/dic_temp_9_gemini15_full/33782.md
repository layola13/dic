circumcenter:/ˈsɜːrkəmsɛntər/| n. |外心|The point where the perpendicular bisectors of the sides of a triangle intersect; the center of the circumscribed circle.  It is equidistant from the three vertices of the triangle. |Example: The circumcenter of an equilateral triangle is also its centroid and orthocenter.|近义词：center (in this specific geometric context)|反义词：incenter (中心)
