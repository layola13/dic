judgment:/ˈdʒʌdʒmənt/ | n. | 判断，判决，意见 | The ability to make considered decisions or come to sensible conclusions.  It also refers to a formal decision made by a court or similar body. |例句：Her judgment in matters of art is impeccable.她的艺术鉴赏力无可挑剔。

例句：The judge delivered his judgment after a long deliberation. 法官经过长时间的审议后作出了判决。


近义词：decision, verdict, opinion, assessment, appraisal | 反义词：ignorance, misunderstanding, bias
