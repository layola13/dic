investors: /ɪnˈvɛstərz/ | n. |投资者 |People or organizations that invest money in something, typically a business, with the expectation of achieving a profit.  |Example:  Many investors are interested in the new technology company. 许多投资者对这家新科技公司很感兴趣。  |近义词: shareholders, backers, financiers |反义词: borrowers, debtors

Investors fund many startups. = 投资者资助许多初创公司。
Investors carefully analyze market trends. = 投资者仔细分析市场趋势。


一般过去时:
Investors withdrew their funds last year. = 投资者去年撤回了资金。
Investors purchased a significant stake in the company. = 投资者收购了该公司的大量股份。


现在完成时:
Investors have already committed significant capital. = 投资者已经投入了大量资金。
Investors have seen substantial returns on their investments. = 投资者已经从投资中获得了可观的回报。


过去完成时:
