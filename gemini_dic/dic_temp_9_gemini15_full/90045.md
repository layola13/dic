hospitalises:/ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪz/ /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪz/ | vt. | 将…送入医院；使…住院 | To place someone in a hospital for treatment.  | The doctor hospitalised the patient after the accident.  医生在事故后将病人送进了医院。 | He was hospitalised for a week due to his injuries. 他因伤住院一周。 | admit, confine, commit | discharge, release | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hospital routinely hospitalises patients with severe injuries. 医院例行将严重受伤的病人送入医院。
They hospitalise children with infectious diseases to prevent spreading. 他们将患有传染病的儿童送入医院以防止传播。

一般过去时:
The surgeon hospitalised the patient immediately after the operation.  外科医生在手术后立即将病人送入医院。
She was hospitalised for three days following a heart attack. 她心脏病发作后住院三天。


现在完成时:
They have hospitalised over 100 patients this month.  这个月他们已经收治了100多名病人。
The hospital has hospitalised many patients suffering from the flu. 医院已经收治了许多患流感的病人。


过去完成时:
He had been hospitalised twice before this incident. 在此事件之前，他曾两次住院。
She had already been hospitalised for a week when her family visited.  当她的家人探望时，她已经住院一周了。


将来时:
The doctor believes he will be hospitalised for at least a week.  医生认为他至少要住院一周。
They predict that many more people will be hospitalised during the flu season. 他们预测在流感季节会有更多的人住院。


虚拟语气:
If he had gone to the doctor sooner, he wouldn't have been hospitalised. 如果他早点去看医生，他就不会住院了。
It would be best if she were hospitalised immediately to receive proper treatment. 最好立即将她送往医院接受适当治疗。


被动语态:
Many patients were hospitalised during the pandemic. 大流行期间许多病人住院。
He was hospitalised against his will. 他被强迫住院。


疑问句:
Was the patient hospitalised successfully? 病人是否成功送入医院？
How many people were hospitalised after the earthquake? 地震后有多少人住院？


否定句:
They did not hospitalise the patient because his condition was stable. 他们没有将病人送入医院，因为他的病情稳定。
The doctor decided not to hospitalise her at that time. 医生当时决定不让她住院。
