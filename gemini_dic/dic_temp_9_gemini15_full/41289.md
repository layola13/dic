cordite:/ˈkɔːdaɪt/| n. |柯达火药，无烟火药 | A smokeless propellant explosive consisting of guncotton, nitroglycerin, and a mineral jelly.  It was invented in 1889 by Sir Frederick Abel and James Dewar. | Example: Cordite was widely used in military applications throughout the 20th century.  柯达火药在20世纪被广泛用于军事用途。 |近义词：gunpowder, explosive |反义词：None  |名词
