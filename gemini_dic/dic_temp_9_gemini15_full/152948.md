pedicellina:/ˌpɛdɪsɛˈlɪnə/| n. |柄体动物；柄体虫|A small, sessile, marine invertebrate belonging to the phylum Entoprocta.  They are characterized by a stalk-like pedicel and a crown of tentacles used for feeding.  Pedicellina are filter feeders, trapping small organisms from the water with their tentacles.| Example:  Pedicellina are found attached to various substrates in marine environments. 柄体动物生活在海洋环境中，附着在各种基质上。|近义词:entoproct (more general term)|反义词:None (no direct antonym)|名词
