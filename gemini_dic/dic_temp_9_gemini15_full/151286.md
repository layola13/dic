pargeboard:/ˈpɑːrdʒbɔːrd/ | n. |灰泥板，抹灰板 | A pargeboard is a type of building material used to create a smooth, even surface on walls, typically made of a mixture of lime, cement, and sand. It's applied over a rougher base to provide a better surface for paint or wallpaper.  |The builders used pargeboard to finish the exterior walls.  建造者使用灰泥板来完成外墙。| plasterboard, lath | rough, uneven surface | 名词
