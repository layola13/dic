词源:
The word "mealworm" is a compound word.  "Meal" refers to the flour or ground grain often found in the environments where these larvae are found.  "Worm" is a general term for a long, slender, soft-bodied invertebrate. Therefore, "mealworm" literally means a "worm found in meal".  The etymology reflects its common habitat and appearance.
