invigilate:/ɪnˈvɪdʒɪleɪt/ | vt. |监考；监督|To supervise (an examination) to prevent cheating.  It implies a watchful and authoritative presence to ensure fair play.|例句：Two teachers invigilated the exam. = 两位老师监考了考试。|例句：The professor invigilated the students during the test to maintain order and prevent cheating. = 教授在考试期间监督学生，以维持秩序并防止作弊。|近义词：supervise, oversee, monitor, proctor |反义词：leave unattended
