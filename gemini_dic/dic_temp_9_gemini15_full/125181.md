multicarbon:/ˈmʌltiˌkɑːrbən/| adj.|多碳的；含有多个碳原子的|Having or containing many carbon atoms.  Multicarbon compounds are common in organic chemistry and are essential to life.|例句：Many plastics are multicarbon polymers. 许多塑料都是多碳聚合物。

例句：The research focused on the synthesis of multicarbon molecules.  该研究集中在多碳分子的合成上。


近义词：polycarbon (less common, implies many carbons linked in a chain), multi-atomic (more general, not specific to carbon)
反义词：monocarbon (containing only one carbon atom)
词性：形容词
