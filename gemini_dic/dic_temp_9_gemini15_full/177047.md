reannotating:/ˌriːˈænəˌteɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新注释，重新标注|The process of adding or changing annotations on something, typically data or text.|例句：The researchers are reannotating the dataset to improve accuracy. 研究人员正在重新标注数据集以提高准确性。例句：We need to reannotating the images with more precise labels.我们需要用更精确的标签重新标注图像。|近义词：relabeling, revising, amending |反义词：leaving unchanged, ignoring
