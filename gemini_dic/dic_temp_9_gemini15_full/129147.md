no-good:/ˈnoʊ ɡʊd/| adj.|没用的；不好的；无赖的|  Describes something or someone that is useless, ineffective, or morally reprehensible. It can refer to things, people, or actions.  |例句：He's a no-good bum. 他是个没用的流浪汉。
That no-good dog tore up my garden. 那条不成器的狗把我的花园毁了。|近义词：worthless, useless, bad, wicked, worthless,  scoundrel |反义词：good, excellent, valuable, virtuous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That plan is no good. =  那个计划没用。
This knife is no good for cutting meat. = 这把刀不适合切肉。


一般过去时:
His explanation was no good. = 他的解释没有说服力。
The old car was no good anymore. = 那辆旧车已经没用了。


现在完成时:

All my efforts have been no good. = 我所有的努力都白费了。


过去完成时:
The previous strategy had been no good. = 先前的策略行不通。
Their previous attempts had been no good. = 他们之前的尝试都失败了.


将来时:

This plan will be no good in the long run. = 从长远来看，这个计划不可行。


虚拟语气:
If I tried that, it would be no good. = 如果我尝试那样做，那将不会奏效。
If he had tried harder, it might not have been no good. = 如果他更努力一些，结果可能不会那么糟。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
