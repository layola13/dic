effeminize:/ɪˈfɛmɪnaɪz/ | vt. | 使柔弱；使女性化 | To cause (a person or thing) to take on feminine characteristics; to make less masculine.  | Example:  He felt the pressure to effeminize his behavior to fit in. 他感到压力，必须使自己的行为女性化才能融入其中。  | 近义词: feminize, unman  | 反义词: masculinize, harden
