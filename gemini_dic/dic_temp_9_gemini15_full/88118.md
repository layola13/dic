hijacking:/ˈhaɪdʒækɪŋ/| n. |劫持；劫机；篡夺|The illegal seizure of a vehicle, especially an aircraft, by force.|例句：The hijacking of the plane resulted in the deaths of several passengers.  飞机劫持导致几名乘客死亡。|例句：The company accused its competitor of hijacking its marketing strategy.这家公司指责其竞争对手窃取了它的营销策略。|近义词：seizure, commandeering, seizure, appropriation|反义词：release, surrender


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The terrorists are hijacking the bus. = 恐怖分子正在劫持公共汽车。
He hijacks the conversation every time. = 他每次都抢话。


一般过去时:
The pilot successfully prevented the hijacking. = 飞行员成功阻止了劫持事件。
They hijacked the meeting to discuss their own agenda. = 他们劫持了会议来讨论他们自己的议程。


现在完成时:
Pirates have hijacked several ships this year. = 海盗今年劫持了几艘船。
They have hijacked the public discourse on this issue. = 他们在这个问题上操纵了公众舆论。


过去完成时:
The police had already apprehended the hijackers before the hostages were released. = 在人质获释之前，警方已经逮捕了劫机者。
The company had been hijacked by its own employees. = 公司已经被自己的员工篡夺了。


将来时:
They will hijack the next flight leaving for London. = 他们将劫持下一班飞往伦敦的航班。
He will hijack the project and take all the credit. = 他将窃取这个项目并窃取所有功劳。


虚拟语气:
If they had hijacked the plane, many lives would have been lost. = 如果他们劫持了飞机，许多生命都会丧失。
I wish they hadn't hijacked the conversation. = 我希望他们没有打断谈话。


被动语态:
The plane was hijacked by terrorists. = 这架飞机被恐怖分子劫持了。
The meeting was hijacked by a group of protestors. = 会议被一群抗议者劫持了。


疑问句:
Was the car hijacked? = 汽车被劫持了吗？
Has the company's image been hijacked by negative publicity? = 公司的形象是否被负面宣传所损害？


否定句:
The police did not allow the hijacking to succeed. = 警方没有允许劫持成功。
The company did not let its brand be hijacked by competitors. = 公司没有让竞争对手窃取其品牌。
