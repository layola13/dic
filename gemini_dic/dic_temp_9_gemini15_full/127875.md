neocons:/ˈniːoʊkɒnz/| n.| 新保守主义者 |New conservatives;  a group of intellectuals and policymakers who advocate for a strong military, assertive foreign policy, and a free-market economy.  They often support military intervention and are skeptical of international organizations. | Example: The neoconservatives in the administration pushed for the Iraq War.  新保守主义者们在政府中推动了伊拉克战争。|近义词：conservative hawks, interventionists |反义词：liberal internationalists, pacifists
