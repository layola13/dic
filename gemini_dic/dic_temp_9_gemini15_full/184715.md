resubscribe:/ˌriːsəbˈskraɪb/| v. |重新订阅，再次订阅 |To subscribe again to a service or publication after a period of not subscribing.  |例句：I decided to resubscribe to the magazine after a year's break.  一年后我决定重新订阅这本杂志。|例句：After a trial period, many users choose to resubscribe to the premium service. 试用期后，许多用户选择重新订阅高级服务。|近义词：renew, subscribe again |反义词：unsubscribe, cancel
