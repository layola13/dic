chitin:/ˈkaɪtɪn/| n. |几丁质，甲壳素|A tough, protective polysaccharide found in the exoskeletons of arthropods (such as insects, crustaceans, and spiders) and in the cell walls of some fungi.|例句：The crab's exoskeleton is made of chitin.  螃蟹的外骨骼是由几丁质构成的。|例句：Chitin is a biopolymer with many potential applications. 几丁质是一种具有许多潜在应用的生物聚合物。|近义词：cellulose, polysaccharide |反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑与易分解物质对比)|
