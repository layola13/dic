reshifted:/ˌriːˈʃɪftɪd/| v. |重新移动；重新调整；重新分配| To shift something again; to reposition or redistribute something.  This word is the past tense and past participle of "reshift".|例句：The furniture had been reshifted to accommodate the new guests. 家具已经被重新摆放以容纳新客人。

例句：The company reshifted its marketing budget to focus on digital advertising. 公司重新调整了营销预算，重点放在数字广告上。


近义词：relocate, reposition, rearrange, redistribute|反义词：leave untouched, keep in place|词性：动词
