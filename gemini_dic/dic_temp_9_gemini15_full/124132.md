mortgagors:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɔːrz/| n. |抵押人，借款人 |A mortgagor is a person who takes out a mortgage loan and uses their property as collateral.  They are legally obligated to repay the loan according to the terms of the mortgage agreement. |例句：The mortgagors defaulted on their payments, leading to foreclosure.  抵押人未能按时还款，导致房屋被拍卖。

例句：The bank filed a lawsuit against the mortgagors for breach of contract. 银行因违约起诉抵押人。


近义词：borrower, debtor |反义词：mortgagee (抵押权人)
