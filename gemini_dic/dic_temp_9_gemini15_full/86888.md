hemispheral:/ˈhɛmɪsfɪərəl/ | adj. | 半球的；大脑半球的 | Relating to or affecting a hemisphere.  The term often refers to the two hemispheres of the brain. | Example: Hemispheral specialization refers to the different functions performed by the left and right hemispheres of the brain. 大脑半球特化指的是大脑左右半球执行的不同功能。  |近义词：cerebral (when referring to the brain) | 反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑与“whole-brain”相对。|词性:形容词
