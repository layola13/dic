hindu:/ˈhɪnduː/ | n. |印度教徒；印度人 | A follower of Hinduism; a person of Indian origin.  | Example:  Many Hindus celebrate Diwali.  许多印度教徒庆祝排灯节。 |近义词: Indian (referring to a person), follower of Hinduism |反义词: None directly applicable.  The antonym would depend on the context (e.g., Muslim, Christian, etc., if referring to religion; non-Indian, if referring to nationality).
