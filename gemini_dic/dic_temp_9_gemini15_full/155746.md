To pronounce or articulate (a word or sound) clearly and distinctly.|例句：The linguist phonetized the unfamiliar word for the students.  语言学家为学生们将那个不熟悉的单词进行了语音标注。|例句：She carefully phonetized each syllable of the difficult word. 她仔细地将那个难词的每个音节都进行了语音标注。|近义词：transcribe (phonetically), articulate, pronounce|反义词：mispronounce, mumble
