nonmanufacture:/ˌnɒnˌmænjuˈfæktʃə(r)/ | n. |非制造业；非制造品 | The term "nonmanufacture" refers to industries or sectors that are not involved in the production of tangible goods.  It encompasses a wide range of activities, including services, agriculture, and resource extraction.  | 例句：The growth of the nonmanufacture sector has outpaced that of manufacturing in recent years. 近年来，非制造业的增长速度超过了制造业。 |近义词：service sector, tertiary sector |反义词：manufacture, production
