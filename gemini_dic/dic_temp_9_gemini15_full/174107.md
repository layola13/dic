quaver:/ˈkweɪvər/| v. n.|颤抖；颤音；发颤的声音|To shake or tremble, especially with a slight rapid movement; a shaky or unsteady sound or tone.|The leaves quavered in the breeze. 叶子在微风中颤抖。|His voice quavered with emotion. 他的声音因激动而颤抖。|近义词：tremble, shake, vibrate|反义词：steady, firm
