contingencies:/kənˈtɪndʒənsiz/| n.|偶然事件；意外事件；可能性；意外情况|Possible events that might occur in the future, especially those that could have a significant impact.  They often involve uncertainty or risk. |例句：The company has contingency plans in place to deal with a sudden drop in sales.  这家公司制定了应急计划来应对销售额的突然下降。

例句：We need to consider all possible contingencies before launching the new product. 在推出新产品之前，我们需要考虑所有可能的意外情况。


近义词：eventualities, possibilities, chances, eventualities, circumstances


反义词：certainties, guarantees


词性:名词
