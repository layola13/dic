fightback:/ˈfaɪtˌbæk/| n./v.|反击；反攻；抵抗|A forceful response to an attack or challenge; a renewed effort to overcome difficulties.|例句：The team mounted a strong fightback in the second half.  球队在下半场发起了猛烈的反击。|例句：The government is determined to fight back against rising inflation. 政府决心抗击通货膨胀。|近义词：counterattack, resistance, comeback|反义词：surrender, capitulation, defeat|动词/名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fight back against injustice. = 他们反抗不公正。
We fight back against the invading army. = 我们抵抗入侵的军队。

一般过去时:
The soldiers fought back bravely. = 士兵们勇敢地进行了反击。
The company fought back against its competitors. = 这家公司反击竞争对手。

现在完成时:
They have fought back against the oppression for years. = 他们多年来一直反抗压迫。
She has fought back against the disease with courage. = 她勇敢地与疾病作斗争。

过去完成时:
The rebels had fought back fiercely before surrendering. = 反叛者在投降前进行了猛烈的抵抗。
He had fought back against the temptation to quit. = 他曾努力抵抗放弃的诱惑。

将来时:
We will fight back against any attempt to undermine our freedom. = 我们将抵抗任何削弱我们自由的企图。
The city will fight back against the rising sea levels. = 城市将抵抗不断上升的海平面。

虚拟语气:
If we were stronger, we could fight back more effectively. = 如果我们更强大，就能更有效地反击。
I wish I could fight back against the injustice I see. = 我希望我能反抗我看到的那些不公正。

被动语态:
The attack was fought back successfully. = 这次袭击被成功地击退了。
The invasion was fought back by the determined citizens. = 坚决的市民击退了入侵。

疑问句:
Will they fight back against the new regulations? = 他们会反抗新的规章制度吗？
How will we fight back against this crisis? = 我们将如何应对这场危机？

否定句:
They did not fight back against the bullies. = 他们没有反抗恶霸。
The country failed to fight back against the economic downturn. = 这个国家未能抵御经济衰退。
