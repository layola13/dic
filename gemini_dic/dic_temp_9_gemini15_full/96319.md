indigestion's: /ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛʃ(ə)nz/ | n. | 消化不良的 | The discomfort or pain resulting from poor digestion.  It often involves symptoms like bloating, heartburn, and stomach cramps. |例句：My indigestion's making it hard to sleep. 我的消化不良让我很难入睡。

例句：The doctor prescribed medication for her indigestion's persistent symptoms. 医生开了药来治疗她持续的消化不良症状。


近义词：dyspepsia, upset stomach | 反义词：good digestion


词性：名词所有格 (noun possessive)  它指的是"消化不良的"，是indigestion 的所有格形式，表示所属关系。  并非一个独立的词。
