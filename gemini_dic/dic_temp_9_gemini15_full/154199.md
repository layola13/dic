periostitis:/ˌpɛriːɒsˈtaɪtɪs/ | n. |骨膜炎|Inflammation of the periosteum, the membrane covering the bone.  It can be caused by trauma, infection, or other underlying conditions. Symptoms can include pain, swelling, and tenderness to the touch. |例句：The athlete suffered from periostitis in his shin after months of intense training.  这位运动员在经过几个月的强化训练后，胫骨患上了骨膜炎。|例句：Chronic periostitis can lead to bone thickening and deformity.  慢性骨膜炎会导致骨骼增厚和畸形。|近义词：osteitis (骨炎), bone inflammation |反义词：无
