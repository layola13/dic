individualizations: /ˌɪndɪvɪdʒuəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 个体化；个性化 | The process of making something unique to an individual or group.  It involves adapting or tailoring something to meet the specific needs or preferences of a particular person or group. |例句：The school's curriculum promotes individualization of learning, catering to students' diverse learning styles and paces. 学校的课程注重学习的个性化，以满足学生不同的学习风格和进度。

The increasing individualization of media consumption has led to the rise of personalized news feeds and targeted advertising.  媒体消费的日益个性化导致个性化新闻推送和定向广告的兴起。| 近义词：personalization, customization, differentiation | 反义词：standardization, generalization
