non-competent:/ˌnɒnˈkɒmpɪtənt/| adj. |无能力的，不胜任的 |Not having the necessary ability or skills to do something successfully.  Lacking the required qualifications or competence. |例句：He was declared non-competent to stand trial. 他被宣告无能力出庭受审。

例句：The non-competent employee was eventually fired.  这位不胜任的员工最终被解雇了。


近义词：incompetent, incapable, unqualified, unfit


反义词：competent, capable, qualified, proficient
