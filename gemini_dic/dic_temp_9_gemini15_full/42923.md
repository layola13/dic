coverings:/ˈkʌvərɪŋz/ | n. |覆盖物；覆盖层；遮盖物 | Coverings refer to materials or things that cover or protect something else.  They can be natural or man-made and serve various purposes, from insulation to decoration. | Example: The building's coverings were damaged in the storm.  建筑物的覆盖物在暴风雨中受损了。 The floor coverings were luxurious and soft. 地面覆盖物奢华柔软。|近义词：cover, sheathing, lining, casing |反义词：exposure, unveiling
