glad:/ɡlæd/ | adj. |高兴的，愉快的 | feeling pleasure and happiness; pleased.  |例句：I'm glad to hear that you're feeling better.  我很高兴听到你感觉好些了。|例句：She was glad she had made the decision. 她很高兴自己做了这个决定。|近义词：happy, pleased, delighted, joyful |反义词：sad, unhappy, displeased
