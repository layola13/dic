fungicides:/ˈfʌŋɡɪsaɪdz/| n.|杀菌剂|A chemical substance used to kill fungi.|The farmer used fungicides to prevent blight on his potato crop.|近义词：antifungal, mycocide|反义词:None specifically.  Fungicides are used to *combat* fungal growth, not to promote it.
