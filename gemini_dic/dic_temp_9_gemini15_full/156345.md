physio-psychology: /ˌfɪzioʊsaɪˈkɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 生理心理学 | The study of the biological bases of behavior and mental processes.  It investigates how the structure and function of the nervous system and other bodily systems influence behavior, thoughts, and feelings. | Example: Physio-psychology explores the relationship between brain activity and emotional responses.  生理心理学探究大脑活动和情绪反应之间的关系。|近义词:biological psychology, psychobiology, behavioral neuroscience | 反义词: (None directly, but fields like purely cognitive psychology could be considered contrasting)| 词性:名词
