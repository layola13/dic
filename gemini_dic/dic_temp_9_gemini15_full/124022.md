morose:/məˈroʊs/| adj.|忧郁的；阴沉的；脾气坏的| sullen and ill-tempered.  A morose person is unhappy, bad-tempered, and unwilling to talk to people. |例句：He became morose and withdrawn after his wife's death. 他妻子去世后变得忧郁孤僻。

例句：She sat in morose silence, refusing to speak to anyone. 她忧郁地沉默着，拒绝和任何人说话。


近义词：gloomy, sullen, glum, grumpy, surly,  dysphoric

反义词：cheerful, happy, jovial, merry, upbeat, buoyant


词性:形容词
