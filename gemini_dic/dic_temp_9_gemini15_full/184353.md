respiration:/ˌrɛspəˈreɪʃən/| n.|呼吸，呼吸作用|The process of breathing; the taking in of oxygen and giving out of carbon dioxide.  It is essential for life and involves the exchange of gases between an organism and its environment. |例句：Deep breathing exercises can improve respiration. 深呼吸练习可以改善呼吸。

例句：The plant's respiration rate increased in the sunlight. 植物的光合作用速率在阳光下有所提高。


近义词：breathing, ventilation, gas exchange |反义词：asphyxiation, suffocation


词源：From Latin *respirare "to breathe again," from re- "again" + spirare "to breathe."
