milliners:/ˈmɪlɪnərz/ | n. | 女帽商；制帽者 | A milliner is a person who makes and sells women's hats.  Milliners create hats using various materials and techniques, often incorporating decorative elements. |  She worked as a milliner in Paris, creating exquisite hats for the elite. 她曾在巴黎当女帽商，为精英人士制作精致的帽子。|  hatmaker, hatter |  (无明确反义词)
