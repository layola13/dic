hearings:/ˈhɪərɪŋz/| n.|听证会，听力；审讯|Formal meetings or sessions where evidence is presented and considered, often in a court or other official setting.  Hearings can also refer to the act of listening or the ability to hear.|例句：The Senate hearings on the nominee's qualifications lasted for three days. 参议院对被提名人的资格听证会持续了三天。

The judge scheduled a hearing to determine custody of the child. 法官安排了一次听证会来决定孩子的监护权。


近义词：trials, investigations, inquiries, sessions|反义词：silence, inaudibility
