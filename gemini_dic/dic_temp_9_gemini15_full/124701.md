mower:/ˈmoʊ.ɚ/ | n. |割草机；修剪机 | A machine used to cut grass. |例句：He used a lawn mower to cut the grass. 他用割草机割草。|近义词：grass cutter, lawnmower |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mows the lawn every Saturday. = 他每个星期六都修剪草坪。
The gardener mows the large field. = 园丁修剪那块大田地。

一般过去时:
I mowed the lawn yesterday. = 我昨天割了草。
They mowed the field with a tractor. = 他们用拖拉机割草。

现在完成时:
He has mowed the lawn three times this week. = 他本周已经割了三次草。
She has never mowed a lawn before. = 她以前从未割过草。

过去完成时:
I had mowed the lawn before it started to rain. = 下雨之前我已经割过草了。
They had mowed the entire field by sunset. = 日落前他们已经割完了整块田地。

将来时:
I will mow the lawn tomorrow. = 我明天将要割草。
They will mow the field next week. = 他们下周将要割草。

虚拟语气:
If I had a mower, I would mow the lawn myself. = 如果我有割草机，我会自己割草。
I wish I had mowed the lawn earlier. = 我真希望我早点割草。

被动语态:
The lawn is mowed every week. = 草坪每周都被修剪。
The field was mowed by the farmer. = 田地被农民割了。

疑问句:
Do you mow your own lawn? = 你自己割草吗？
Who mows the lawn in your house? = 你们家谁割草？

否定句:
I didn't mow the lawn this week. = 我这周没割草。

词源:
The word "mower" comes from the Old English word "mawan," meaning "to mow" or "to cut down grass."  The suffix "-er" was added to create the noun form, indicating a person or thing that performs the action of mowing.
