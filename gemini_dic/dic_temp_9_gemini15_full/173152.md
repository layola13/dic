quasi-apologetic: /ˈkwɑːzi əˌpɒləˈdʒɛtɪk/ | adj. |略带歉意的，似是而非地道歉的 |  Seemingly apologetic, but not truly or fully so; containing an element of apology but lacking full sincerity or conviction.  It implies a hesitant or half-hearted apology. |

例句：He offered a quasi-apologetic shrug, avoiding direct eye contact. 他耸了耸肩，显得有些歉意，却避开了眼神接触。

例句：Her quasi-apologetic tone suggested she wasn't entirely sorry for her actions. 她语气略带歉意，暗示她并没有完全为自己的行为感到抱歉。


近义词：half-hearted, insincere, mildly apologetic, tentatively apologetic

反义词：unrepentant, unapologetic, contrite (fully apologetic)
