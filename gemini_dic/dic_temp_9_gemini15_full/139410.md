observers:/əbˈzɜːrvərz/ | n. |观察者，旁观者 | People or things that observe something.  Observers noted a significant increase in bird populations. 观察员注意到鸟类数量显著增加。|近义词：spectators, viewers, watchers |反义词：participants, actors
