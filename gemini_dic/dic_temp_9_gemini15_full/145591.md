overconcerns:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsɜːrnz/| n. |过度担忧；过分关注|Excessive worry or attention to something; anxieties that are too great.  It often implies that the worry is disproportionate to the actual threat or problem. |例句：Her overconcerns about her child's safety prevented her from letting him play outside.  她的过度担忧孩子的安全让她无法让孩子在外面玩耍。

例句：His overconcerns about his job performance led to burnout. 他对工作表现的过度担忧导致了倦怠。


近义词： anxieties, worries, apprehensions, preoccupations |反义词： indifference, unconcern, calmness, composure |词性：名词
