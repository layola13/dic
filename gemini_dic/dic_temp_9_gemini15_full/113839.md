made-out:/meɪdˈaʊt/| v.| 完成；理解；辨认出；编造；亲吻；调情 | To complete or finish something; to understand or interpret something; to identify or recognize something; to invent or fabricate something; to kiss passionately; to flirt. | 例句：He finally made out the cheque. 他终于开好了支票。  They made out the meaning of the cryptic message. 他们弄懂了这份隐晦信息的含义。|近义词：completed, understood, identified, invented, kissed, flirted |反义词：failed, misunderstood, missed, ignored
