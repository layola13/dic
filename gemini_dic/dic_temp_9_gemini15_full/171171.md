psycholinguistics: /ˌsaɪkoʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/ | n. |心理语言学|The study of the psychological processes involved in language comprehension and production.  It investigates how people understand, produce, and acquire language, drawing on insights from psychology, linguistics, and neuroscience.  |Example:  Psycholinguistics explores how the brain processes language, from the perception of sounds to the understanding of meaning.  心理语言学探索大脑如何处理语言，从声音的感知到意义的理解。|近义词:cognitive linguistics, linguistic psychology|反义词:N/A
