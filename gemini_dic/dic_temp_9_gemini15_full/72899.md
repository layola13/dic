follows-up:/ˈfɑːloʊz ʌp/|/ˈfɒləʊz ʌp/| v. |后续行动，跟进|To take further action or make further inquiries concerning something.  It often implies checking on progress or ensuring a task is completed.|例句：The sales team follows up on all leads to ensure potential customers are contacted. 销售团队会跟进所有潜在客户，以确保联系到他们。
