settler:/ˈsɛtlər/ | n. | 定居者，移民 | A person who settles in a new place, especially a new country.  | Example: The early settlers of America faced many hardships.  早期美国的定居者面临许多艰难险阻。  |近义词：colonist, immigrant, pioneer | 反义词：native, indigenous | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The settler builds a new home. = 定居者正在建造新家。
Many settlers farm the land. = 许多定居者耕种土地。


一般过去时:
The settler built a cabin in the woods. = 定居者在树林里建了一间小屋。
Settlers migrated west in search of land. = 定居者向西迁移寻找土地。


现在完成时:
The settlers have established a thriving community. = 定居者们已经建立了一个繁荣的社区。
She has been a settler in this town for twenty years. = 她在这个镇上已经定居了二十年。


过去完成时:
Before the settlers arrived, the land was untouched. = 在定居者到来之前，这片土地未被开发。
They had already built several houses before the winter set in. = 在冬天来临之前，他们已经建了几座房子。


将来时:
The settlers will cultivate more land next year. = 定居者们明年将耕种更多的土地。
More settlers will arrive in the spring. = 春天将会有更多的定居者到来。


虚拟语气:
If the settlers had arrived earlier, they would have found more resources. = 如果定居者早点到达，他们就能找到更多资源。
I wish the settlers had treated the indigenous people with more respect. = 我希望定居者们能更尊重当地土著居民。


被动语态:
The land was settled by pioneers. = 这片土地是由拓荒者开发的。
New towns are being settled by immigrants. = 新城镇正在被移民开发。


疑问句:
Were the settlers peaceful or aggressive? = 这些定居者是和平的还是具有侵略性的？
How many settlers came to this region? =有多少定居者来到这个地区？


否定句:
The settlers did not find gold in the river. = 定居者们在河里没有找到金子。
He was not a settler, he was just a visitor. = 他不是定居者，他只是一个游客。
