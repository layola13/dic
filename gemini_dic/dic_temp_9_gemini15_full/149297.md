/hwɑːt/ /wɒt/ | pron. | 什么 |  What refers to something unknown or unspecified. It is used to ask questions, to express uncertainty, or to introduce a statement or clause. | What is your name? | which, which thing | nothing |  pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you felt sick? = 你在感到不舒服之前吃了什么？
What had they already discussed? = 他们之前已经讨论了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气将会怎么样？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do. = 我真希望我知道该做什么。

被动语态:
What is being built there? = 那里正在建造什么？
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
I didn't understand what he meant. = 我不明白他的意思。

虚拟语气:
What would you have done if you had known? = 如果你知道的话，你会怎么做？
I wish I had known what to do. = 我真希望我知道该怎么做。

被动语态:
