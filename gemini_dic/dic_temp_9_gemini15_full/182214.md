remonstrations:/ˌrɛmənˈstreɪʃənz/| n.|抗议；反对；强烈不满的表示|The act of remonstrating; a protest or objection.  It often involves strong expressions of disapproval or dissent.|例句：The government faced widespread remonstrations over its new economic policy.  政府的新经济政策面临着广泛的抗议。

例句：Despite the public remonstrations, the company refused to change its practices. 尽管公众强烈反对，该公司仍拒绝改变其做法。


近义词：protests, objections, complaints, demonstrations, protests


反义词：approvals, endorsements, acceptances


词性:名词
