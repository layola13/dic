pseudocritical:/ˌsuːdoʊˈkrɪtɪkəl/| adj.|伪批判的，似是而非的批判的| seeming critical but not genuinely so; superficially critical.  It often implies a lack of genuine insight or understanding, and may be used to describe criticism that is shallow, biased, or politically motivated.| Example: His pseudocritical review of the novel revealed more about his own prejudices than about the book's merits.  他的伪批判性评论更暴露了他自己的偏见，而不是小说的优点。|近义词：superficial, shallow, specious, disingenuous |反义词： insightful, profound, genuine, objective
