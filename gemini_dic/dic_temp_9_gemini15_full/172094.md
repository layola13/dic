purporter:/pərˈpɔːrtər/| n. |自称者；声称者；冒充者 |One who purports something; one who claims or alleges something.  A purporter often lacks genuine authority or evidence to support their claim. |例句：The purporter of the lost treasure map proved to be a fraud.  = 声称拥有失落宝藏地图的人被证明是个骗子。 The purporter of the new theory lacked credible evidence. = 新理论的提出者缺乏可信的证据。|近义词：pretender, claimant, asserter |反义词：verifier, authenticator |名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He purports to be a doctor. = 他自称是医生。
She purports to have inside information. = 她声称拥有内幕消息。

一般过去时:
He purported to be a wealthy businessman. = 他自称是一位富有的商人。
They purported to have solved the mystery. = 他们声称已经解开了这个谜团。

现在完成时:
He has purported to be an expert for years. = 他多年来一直自称是专家。
She has purported to have witnessed the crime. = 她声称自己目睹了这起犯罪。

过去完成时:
He had purported to be a friend of the family for many years. = 多年来，他一直自称是这个家庭的朋友。
They had purported to have finished the project before the deadline. = 他们声称在截止日期前完成了项目。

将来时:
He will purport to have new evidence next week. = 他将在下周声称有新的证据。
She will purport to know the answer to the question. = 她将声称知道这个问题的答案。

虚拟语气:
If he purported to be honest, I would trust him. = 如果他自称诚实，我会相信他。
I wish he didn't purport to know everything. = 我希望他不必自称无所不知。

被动语态:
The document was purported to be authentic. = 该文件据称是真实的。
The painting was purported to be a masterpiece. = 这幅画据称是杰作。

疑问句:
Does he purport to be the rightful heir? = 他自称是合法继承人吗？
Does she purport to have the necessary skills? = 她声称拥有必要的技能吗？

否定句:
He does not purport to be an expert on this subject. = 他并不自称是这方面的专家。
She did not purport to have seen anything unusual. = 她没有声称看到任何异常情况。


PHRASE->
purport to do something = 声称做某事
purport to be something = 声称是某物
purport of the letter = 信件的大意
purport of the speech = 演讲的要旨
purportedly =据称；据说
