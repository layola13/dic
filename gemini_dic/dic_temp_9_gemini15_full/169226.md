prologuing:/ˈproʊlɒɡɪŋ/|/ˈproʊˌlɒɡɪŋ/| v.|开场白；序言；前言|The act of providing a prologue or introduction.  It refers to the process of introducing something, often a story, play, or speech, with a preliminary statement.|例句：The speaker began by prologuing the main points of his address.  演讲者以概述其讲话要点作为开场白。|例句：The author spent several pages prologuing before getting to the main narrative. 作者在进入主要叙述之前用了好几页篇幅做序言。|近义词：introducing, prefacing, preambling|反义词：concluding, ending
