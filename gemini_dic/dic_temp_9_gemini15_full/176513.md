re-proofing:/ˌriːˈpruːfɪŋ/ | v. | 再校对，重新校对 | The act of reviewing and correcting something again, typically a printed proof or a text.  It implies a previous proofing attempt has already occurred. | Example:  We need to re-proof the brochure before sending it to the printer. 我们需要在把宣传册送去印刷之前重新校对一下。| 近义词：rechecking, reviewing, revising | 反义词： None specifically, but the opposite concept would be accepting an uncorrected proof.
