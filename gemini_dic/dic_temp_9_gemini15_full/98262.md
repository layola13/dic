inserter:/ɪnˈsɜːrtər/ | n. |插入器，插入物 | A device or person that inserts something.  An inserter is a tool or mechanism used to insert something into another object or location. This could be anything from a small component in a machine to a large piece of equipment. | Example: The factory uses a robotic inserter to place components onto circuit boards.  工厂使用机器人插入器将元件放置到电路板上。  |近义词：inserting device, implant, introducer |反义词：remover, extractor |名词
