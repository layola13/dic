homodemic:/ˌhoʊməˈdɛmɪk/| adj.|同族流行的，同种流行的|Relating to or denoting a disease that affects only members of the same species.|The study focused on the homodemic spread of the virus within the primate population. 这项研究侧重于该病毒在灵长类动物种群中的同种流行传播。|近义词：species-specific, intraspecific |反义词：pandemic, epidemic
