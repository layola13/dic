rhododendrons:/ˌroʊdəˈdɛndrənz/| n. |杜鹃花属植物|Rhododendrons are flowering shrubs or small trees belonging to the genus Rhododendron, known for their vibrant and diverse flowers.  They are popular ornamental plants in gardens worldwide. |The garden is ablaze with color, thanks to the numerous rhododendrons in full bloom.|近义词：azaleas (a closely related genus)|反义词：无
