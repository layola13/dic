clarity:/ˈklærəti/ | n. |清晰度，明晰；明朗；透明 |The clarity of the explanation helped me understand the concept. 解释的清晰度帮助我理解了这个概念。|近义词：clearness, lucidity, transparency |反义词：confusion, ambiguity, obscurity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The clarity of the water is remarkable. = 水的清澈程度令人惊叹。
His explanation lacks clarity. = 他的解释缺乏清晰度。

一般过去时:
The clarity of the image improved after editing. = 图片在编辑后清晰度提高了。
Yesterday's presentation lacked clarity. = 昨天的演示缺乏清晰度。


现在完成时:
The project has achieved remarkable clarity. = 项目已经达到了显著的清晰度。
We have improved the clarity of our communication. = 我们已经改善了我们沟通的清晰度。


过去完成时:
The meaning had become clearer after further clarification.  = 经过进一步澄清后，含义变得更加清晰。
Before the revision, the document lacked clarity. = 修改之前，文档缺乏清晰度。


将来时:
The new software will improve the clarity of our reports. = 新软件将提高我们报告的清晰度。
We expect the situation to gain clarity next week. = 我们预计下周情况将会明朗。


虚拟语气:
If the instructions were clearer, we would have fewer mistakes. = 如果说明更清晰，我们就会犯更少的错误。
I wish the presentation had more clarity. = 我希望演示更清晰一些。


被动语态:
The issue was addressed with remarkable clarity. = 这个问题以非凡的清晰度得到了解决。
The benefits of the plan were explained with great clarity. = 计划的好处得到了非常清晰的解释。


疑问句:
Is there any clarity on the situation? = 情况是否明朗？
Can you explain this with more clarity? = 你能更清晰地解释一下吗？


否定句:
The instructions lacked clarity. = 说明不够清晰。
The speaker's message was not clear; there was a lack of clarity. = 演讲者的信息不清楚；缺乏清晰度。


PHRASE->
mental clarity:  精神清晰
with clarity: 清晰地
lack of clarity: 缺乏清晰度
gain clarity:  变得清晰
bring clarity to: 使…清晰


词源:
源自拉丁语 *claritas，是clarus（清晰的，明亮的）的名词形式。clarus 的词根可以追溯到原始印欧语根 *ḱel-（闪耀）。
