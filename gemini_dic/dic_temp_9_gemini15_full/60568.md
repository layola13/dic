elasticities:/ˌɪˌlæstɪˈsɪtiz/| n. |弹性；弹力；伸缩性|The plural form of elasticity, referring to multiple instances of the capacity of a substance or object to return to its original shape after being stretched or compressed;  also used in economics to describe the responsiveness of one variable to changes in another.|例句：The elasticities of demand for various goods differ significantly.  各种商品的需求弹性差异很大。|近义词：flexibilities, resiliencies|反义词：rigidities, inflexibilities|名词
