decides:/dɪˈsaɪdz/ | vt. |决定，判决|To make a decision or judgment about something.|例句：She decided to go to college. 她决定去上大学。|例句：The judge decided the case in favor of the plaintiff.  法官判决原告胜诉。|近义词：determine, resolve, conclude|反义词：hesitate, waver, procrastinate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He decides what to eat every morning. = 他每天早上决定吃什么。
She decides on her outfit before going out. = 她出门前决定好要穿什么衣服。

一般过去时:
They decided to take a vacation. = 他们决定去度假。
He decided against applying for the job. = 他决定不申请这份工作。

现在完成时:
She has decided to move to a new city. = 她已经决定搬到一个新的城市。
We have decided on the menu for the party. = 我们已经决定好派对的菜单了。

过去完成时:
They had decided to leave before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经决定离开了。
She had decided against going, even before she received the invitation. = 甚至在收到邀请函之前，她就决定不去。

将来时:
He will decide tomorrow whether to accept the offer. = 他明天将决定是否接受这个提议。
They will decide on a course of action after careful consideration. = 经过仔细考虑后，他们将决定采取行动方案。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would decide to go. = 如果我是你，我会决定去。
I wish I had decided to study harder. = 我真希望我当时决定努力学习。

被动语态:
The date of the meeting was decided last week. = 会议日期上周就决定了。
The winner will be decided by a panel of judges. = 获胜者将由评审团决定。

疑问句:
Have you decided what to wear? = 你决定好穿什么了吗？
What have they decided to do? = 他们决定做什么？

否定句:
I haven't decided yet. = 我还没决定。
He didn't decide to come. = 他决定不来了。


PHRASE->
decide on: 决定…
decide against: 决定反对…
decide to: 决定做…
decide for: 为…做决定
make up one's mind to decide:下定决心决定
