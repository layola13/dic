iconize:/ˈaɪkənˌaɪz/ | vt. |使成为偶像；使象征化；赋予偶像地位 | To make someone or something into an icon; to represent something symbolically.  |  The media quickly iconized the young athlete after his victory.媒体在他获胜后迅速将他塑造成偶像。  His rebellious spirit was iconized by the counter-culture movement. 他的叛逆精神被反文化运动象征化了。 |symbolize, epitomize |de-iconize, denigrate |动词


USAGE->

They iconize their favorite musicians. = 他们把他们最喜欢的音乐家视为偶像。

虚拟语气:
