precontends:/ˌpriːkənˈtɛndz/| v.|预先声称，预先主张|To assert or claim something in advance; to state something beforehand.  |例句：The company precontended that the product was safe before releasing it to the market. 公司在将产品投放市场之前预先声称该产品是安全的。
例句：He precontended his innocence before the trial even began.  审判开始之前，他就预先声称自己是无辜的。|近义词：assert beforehand, claim in advance, allege previously|反义词：deny beforehand, refute in advance|词性：动词
