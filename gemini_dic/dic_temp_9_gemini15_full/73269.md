foreappointment:/fɔːrəˈpɔɪntmənt/| n.| 预先约定；提前任命 | A foreappointment is an appointment made in advance.  It refers to arranging something, typically a meeting or an official position, before the actual time or event. |例句：The foreappointment of the new CEO was announced last week. = 新任首席执行官的提前任命在上周宣布。  The foreappointment of the committee members ensured a smooth transition. = 委员会成员的预先任命确保了平稳过渡。|近义词：prearrangement, prior appointment, advance scheduling |反义词：last-minute arrangement, impromptu appointment|名词
