hashes:/ˈhæʃɪz/| n.|哈希值，散列值|A hash is a function that converts a string of characters into a shorter, fixed-length value or key that represents the original string.  Hashes are used in cryptography and data storage to ensure data integrity and security. |例句：The system uses hashes to verify the integrity of downloaded files.  系统使用哈希值来验证下载文件的完整性。|近义词：digest, checksum|反义词：无
