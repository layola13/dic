quasi-reasonably:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈriːzənəbli/ | adj. | 勉强合理地，近乎合理地 |  Approaching or somewhat resembling reasonableness;  partially reasonable, but not entirely so. It suggests a degree of justification but falls short of being completely logical or rational. | The argument, while quasi-reasonably constructed, ultimately lacked sufficient evidence.  这个论点虽然勉强合理，但最终缺乏足够的证据。|近义词：plausibly, arguably, seemingly reasonable | 反义词：unreasonably, illogically, absurdly
