plucks:/plʌks/ | vt. n. |拔；采；弹奏；猛拉；(鸟的)羽毛 | To pull or pick something, especially with a sudden movement.  |例句：She plucked a flower from the vase. 她从花瓶里摘了一朵花。
The guitarist plucked at the strings. 吉他手拨动琴弦。|近义词：pull, pick, tug, pluck at |反义词：plant, sow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plucks the guitar strings gently. 他轻轻地拨动吉他弦。
She plucks her eyebrows every week. 她每周都修眉毛。

一般过去时:
He plucked a rose from the bush. 他从灌木丛中摘了一朵玫瑰花。
The bird plucked a worm from the ground. 鸟从地上啄了一条虫子。


现在完成时:
She has plucked all the ripe tomatoes. 她已经摘了所有成熟的西红柿。
He has plucked up the courage to speak. 他鼓起勇气说话了。


过去完成时:
She had plucked the chicken before cooking it. 她做鸡之前已经拔了鸡毛。
He had plucked all the weeds from the garden. 他已经把花园里的杂草都拔掉了。


将来时:
I will pluck the feathers from the turkey tomorrow. 我明天将把火鸡的羽毛拔掉。
She will pluck a few leaves for her tea. 她将摘几片叶子泡茶。


虚拟语气:
If I plucked that flower, it would die. 如果我摘了那朵花，它就会死掉。
I wish I had plucked more strawberries. 我希望我摘了更多的草莓。


被动语态:
The chicken was plucked and cleaned. 鸡已经被拔毛清洗干净了。
The fruit was plucked before it was fully ripe. 水果在完全成熟前就被摘了。


疑问句:
Did you pluck the weeds from the garden? 你把花园里的杂草拔了吗？
Will she pluck the flowers for the vase? 她会摘花插进花瓶吗？


否定句:
He didn't pluck the fruit because it wasn't ripe. 果子没熟，所以他没有摘。
She didn't pluck up the courage to ask him. 她没有勇气问他。


PHRASE->
pluck up courage: 鼓起勇气
pluck at: 猛拉；用力拉扯
pluck off: 摘掉；拔掉
pluck out: 拔出；除去
pluck something from: 从…拔/摘…
