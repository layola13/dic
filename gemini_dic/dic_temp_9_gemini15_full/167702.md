pried:/praɪd/| vt.|撬开，撬动，费力地打听|to force something open using a tool, or to obtain information from someone unwilling to give it|He pried the lid off the jar. 他撬开了罐子的盖子。|She pried information out of him. 她费力地从他口中套出了信息。| pry, lever, wrest|close, conceal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pries open the window every morning. 他每天早上撬开窗户。
She pries into other people's business. 她打探别人的私事。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
