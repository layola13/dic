raphaelism:/ˌræfeɪəlɪzəm/| n. |拉斐尔主义|A style of painting characterized by idealized beauty, harmonious composition, and meticulous detail, inspired by the works of Raphael. It was a reaction against the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood's style, emphasizing classical forms and restraint.|The painting exhibits a clear influence of Raphaelism, with its graceful figures and balanced composition.  这幅画明显受到了拉斐尔主义的影响，其人物优美，构图平衡。|Classicism, Idealism|Realism, Naturalism|名词
