faulting:/ˈfɔːltɪŋ/| n. v.|断层；故障；过失；挑剔|The process by which rocks fracture and move along a fault.  It can also refer to a mechanical defect or a mistake. |例句：The earthquake was caused by faulting along a major geological fault.  这次地震是由一条主要地质断层上的断层作用引起的。

The engineer identified a critical faulting in the engine's design.工程师发现了发动机设计中的一个严重故障。

近义词：failure, defect, flaw, error, criticism|反义词：perfection, success, accuracy
