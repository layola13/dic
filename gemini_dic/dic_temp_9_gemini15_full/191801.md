scission:/ˈsɪʒən/| n.|分裂；断裂；分离|The splitting or severing of something into two or more parts.  It often refers to a clean break, rather than a gradual separation. |例句：The scission of the cell resulted in two daughter cells. 细胞分裂产生了两个子细胞。
例句：The scission of the treaty led to renewed conflict.  条约的撕毁导致了冲突的再次爆发。|近义词：division, separation, cleavage, rupture|反义词：union, fusion, joining, connection|名词
