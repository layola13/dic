overassumption:/ˌoʊvərəˈsʌmpʃən/| n. |过度假设，想当然|The act of making assumptions that are excessive, unwarranted, or incorrect;  presuming too much.  It often leads to misunderstandings and errors in judgment.|Example:  His overassumption that I would agree to his proposal led to a heated discussion.  他的想当然认为我会同意他的提议导致了一场激烈的讨论。|近义词：presumption, supposition, conjecture|反义词：careful consideration, verification
