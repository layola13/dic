slimed:/slaɪmd/| v. |涂满粘液；使沾上污泥；诽谤；陷害 |to cover something or someone with slime; to smear or besmirch someone's reputation; to trick or deceive someone |例句：The children slimed each other with mud during the playground game.孩子们在操场上玩耍时互相涂抹泥巴。

The politician was slimed by his opponents in the press.  这位政治家在媒体上被对手诽谤。


近义词：covered, smeared, besmirched, defamed, tricked |反义词：cleaned, purified, exonerated
