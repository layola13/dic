shemitic:/ʃɪˈmɪtɪk/| adj.|闪米特的；闪族语的|Relating to the Semitic languages or peoples.  The term refers to a large family of languages spoken in Southwest Asia and North Africa, including Hebrew, Arabic, and Aramaic, and the cultures associated with them.|Example:  Arabic is a Semitic language.  阿拉伯语是一种闪米特语系语言。|近义词：Semitic-speaking|反义词：None
