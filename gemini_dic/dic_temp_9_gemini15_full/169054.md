programmers:/ˈproʊɡræmərz/ | n. |程序员，程序编写者 |People who write and maintain computer programs.  They design, code, test, and debug software applications. |Example:  The programmers worked late into the night to fix the bug. 程序员们为了修复bug工作到深夜。

The company hired several experienced programmers to develop their new software. 公司聘请了几位经验丰富的程序员来开发他们的新软件。|近义词：coders, developers, software engineers |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
