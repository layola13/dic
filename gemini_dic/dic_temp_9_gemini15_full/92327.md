hypnotizable: /ˌhɪpnəˈtaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可催眠的，易受催眠的 | Susceptible to being hypnotized.  Someone who is hypnotizable is easily influenced by hypnosis. |


例句：

He was highly hypnotizable and went into a deep trance quickly. 他非常容易被催眠，很快就进入了深度催眠状态。

The study showed that certain personality traits correlated with being more hypnotizable. 研究表明，某些性格特征与更容易被催眠有关。


近义词：suggestible, susceptible

反义词：resistant to hypnosis, immune to hypnosis


词源:

The word "hypnotizable" is derived from "hypnosis," which in turn comes from the Greek word "hypnos" (ὕπνος), meaning "sleep." The suffix "-able" indicates capability or susceptibility.  The word evolved through the French word "hypnotisme" before entering English.
