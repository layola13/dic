satisfies:/ˈsætɪsfaɪz/ | v. | 使满意；满足；符合 | To fulfill a requirement, desire, or expectation.  |例句：This meal satisfies my hunger. 这顿饭满足了我的饥饿感。 |例句：The solution satisfies the equation.  这个解满足了方程。|近义词：gratifies, pleases, fulfills, meets |反义词：disappoints, dissatisfies, frustrates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cake satisfies my sweet tooth. = 这块蛋糕满足了我对甜食的渴望。
His explanation satisfies my curiosity. = 他的解释满足了我的好奇心。


一般过去时:
The result satisfied the scientists. = 结果让科学家们满意。
The ending of the movie satisfied the audience. = 电影的结局让观众满意。


现在完成时:
The new policy has satisfied many people. = 新政策已让许多人满意。
We have satisfied all the requirements. = 我们已经满足了所有要求。


过去完成时:
They had satisfied their thirst before the long hike. = 他们长途跋涉前已经解渴了。
She had satisfied her curiosity before asking questions. = 在提问之前，她已经满足了自己的好奇心。


将来时:
This new product will satisfy the market demand. = 这款新产品将满足市场需求。
I hope this plan will satisfy everyone's needs. = 我希望这个计划能满足每个人的需求。


虚拟语气:
If only this job satisfied my ambition! = 要是这份工作能满足我的雄心就好了！
I wish this explanation satisfied everyone. = 我希望这个解释能令每个人满意。


被动语态:
His needs were satisfied by his family. = 他的需求得到了家人的满足。
All the conditions were satisfied before the experiment began. = 在实验开始之前，所有条件都已满足。


疑问句:
Does this solution satisfy the requirements? = 这个方案满足要求吗？
Does this answer satisfy your question? = 这个答案解答了你的问题吗？


否定句:
The explanation didn't satisfy me. = 这个解释没有让我满意。
The results did not satisfy the expectations. = 结果没有达到预期。


PHRASE->
satisfy a need = 满足需求
satisfy a condition = 满足条件
satisfy a requirement = 满足要求
satisfy a demand = 满足需求
satisfy one's hunger = 满足饥饿


词源:
From Old French *satisfier, from Latin satisfacere "to satisfy," from satis "enough" + facere "to make, do."
