larderer:/ˈlɑːrdərər/| n.| 食品储藏室管理员；（旧）卖猪油的人 | A larderer is a person who manages a larder, a room or cupboard for storing food. Historically, the term also referred to someone who sold lard (rendered pig fat).  |The larderer ensured the pantry was well-stocked for the feast.|近义词：storekeeper, pantryman |反义词：consumer
