lathis:/ˈlæθɪs/| n.|  细木条，柳条；格栅；（尤指）屋顶的细木条| A lath is a thin, narrow strip of wood, used especially in building, for supporting plaster or similar material.  The word also refers to a framework of interwoven strips, such as a lattice. |例句：The ceiling was made of laths and plaster.  天花板是用细木条和灰泥做的。

例句：The gardener used laths to support the climbing roses. 园丁用细木条支撑攀爬的玫瑰。


近义词：strip, slat, batten |反义词：N/A|词性：名词
