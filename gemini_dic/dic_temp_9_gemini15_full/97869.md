inmate's:/ˈɪnmeɪts/| n.|囚犯的；住院者的|belonging to or relating to an inmate.  An inmate is a person confined to an institution, such as a prison or hospital.|例句：The inmate's belongings were searched. 囚犯的随身物品被搜查了。


例句：The inmate's request for a transfer was denied. 住院者的转院请求被拒绝了。


近义词：prisoner's, patient's


反义词：free person's


词性：名词所有格
