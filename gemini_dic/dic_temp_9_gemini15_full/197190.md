shaped:/ʃeɪpt/| v. adj.|成形的，塑造的；影响的|To give a particular form or shape to something.|The sculptor shaped the clay into a beautiful bird.  雕塑家把粘土塑造成一只美丽的鸟。|The events of his childhood shaped his personality. 他童年的经历塑造了他的性格。|formed, molded, influenced|shapeless, formless


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He shapes the clay with his hands. = 他用手塑造粘土。
The wind shapes the sand dunes. = 风塑造了沙丘。


一般过去时:
She shaped the dough into rolls. = 她把面团揉成卷。
The artist shaped the wood into a sculpture. = 艺术家把木头雕刻成雕塑。


现在完成时:
They have shaped their lives according to their beliefs. = 他们已经按照自己的信仰塑造了他们的人生。
The experience has shaped my perspective. = 这段经历塑造了我的观点。


过去完成时:
The storm had shaped the coastline over centuries. = 风暴几个世纪以来塑造了海岸线。
He had shaped his argument carefully before the debate. = 他在辩论前仔细地构思了他的论点。


将来时:
She will shape the future of the company. = 她将塑造公司的未来。
The new policy will shape the way we work. = 新政策将改变我们的工作方式。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would shape the project differently. = 如果我有更多时间，我会以不同的方式来规划这个项目。
I wish I could shape my destiny. = 我希望我能掌控自己的命运。


被动语态:
The clay was shaped by the potter. = 粘土被陶工塑造。
The landscape is shaped by erosion. = 地貌是由侵蚀形成的。


疑问句:
How was the metal shaped? = 金属是如何成形的？
What factors shaped his decision? = 哪些因素影响了他的决定？


否定句:
The sculptor did not shape the statue perfectly. = 雕塑家没有完美地塑造雕像。
The events did not shape her life as much as she thought. = 这些事件并没有像她想象的那样改变她的人生。


PHRASE->

shapeless: 无形的

out of shape:  身材走样
