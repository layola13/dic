saccharimetry:/ˌsækəˈrɪmətri/| n. |糖量测定法|The process of measuring the amount of sugar in a substance, typically using a polarimeter or other suitable instrument.  Saccharimetry is essential in various industries, including food processing and brewing, to determine the sugar content of raw materials and finished products. |The saccharimetry results showed a higher sugar concentration than expected. 糖量测定结果显示糖浓度高于预期。 The brewer used saccharimetry to monitor the fermentation process. 酿酒师用糖量测定法监测发酵过程。|暂无|暂无|名词
