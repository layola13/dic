disesteemer: /ˌdɪsɪˈstiːmər/ | n. |轻视者，贬低者 | A person who disesteems or belittles others.  Someone who holds others in low regard; someone who habitually undervalues or disrespects others. |  He is a disesteemer of all things conventional. 他是一个轻视所有传统事物的人。  She became a disesteemer of her former friends after their betrayal.  背叛之后，她开始轻视她以前的朋友。 |近义词：detractor, belittler, deprecator, disparager |反义词：admirer, respecter, admirer, approver |
