WORD ORIGIN->
The word "pointer" originates from the Old French word "pointe," meaning "point," which in turn derives from the Latin word "punctum," also meaning "point."  Its usage in computer science evolved from the conceptual idea of a variable "pointing" to a specific location in memory.
