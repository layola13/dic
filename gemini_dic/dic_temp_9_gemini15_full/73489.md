forerunner:/ˈfɔːrˌrʌnər/|/ˈfɔːrˌrʌnə(r)/| n. |先驱；先兆；前身 |A forerunner is a person or thing that precedes and indicates the approach of another.  It can refer to someone who precedes others in a particular field, paving the way for future developments, or it can refer to something that serves as a precursor or sign of something else to come.  |Example:  The Wright brothers' airplane was a forerunner of modern aviation.  莱特兄弟的飞机是现代航空业的先驱。

The invention of the printing press was a forerunner of the information age. 印刷术的发明是信息时代的先兆。|近义词：predecessor, precursor, pioneer, harbinger |反义词：successor, follower
