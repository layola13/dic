outdodged:/ˌaʊtˈdɑːdʒd/ | v. |巧妙避开，智胜 | To cleverly avoid or outsmart someone or something.  It implies a skillful evasion, often involving wit or strategy. |例句：The detective outdodged the criminal's attempts to mislead him.  侦探巧妙地避开了罪犯试图误导他的企图。

The cunning fox outdodged the hunters' traps.  狡猾的狐狸巧妙地避开了猎人的陷阱。|近义词：outwit, outmaneuver, evade, circumvent |反义词：be caught, be tricked
