ghost:/ɡoʊst/|ˈɡəʊst/| n. |幽灵；鬼魂；影子；微弱的迹象|A ghost is the spirit of a dead person, believed to appear to the living.  Ghosts are often depicted as translucent figures. |例句：I saw a ghost in the old house. 我在老房子里看到一个鬼魂。

例句：The ghost of his laughter echoed in the empty room. 他笑声的回响在空荡的房间里回荡。


近义词：specter, phantom, apparition |反义词：body, reality
