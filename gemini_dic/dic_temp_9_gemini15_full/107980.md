lashes:/ˈlæʃɪz/| n. |睫毛；鞭打；猛击 |The word "lashes" refers to the fringe of hairs growing on the edge of the eyelid.  It can also refer to a sudden forceful blow or a whipping action. |例句：Her long lashes framed her beautiful eyes. 她的长睫毛衬托着她美丽的眼睛。


The wind lashed against the windows. 风猛烈地拍打着窗户。|近义词：eyelashes (for the hair), blows, strokes, whips |反义词：None directly, but contextually opposites might include gentle brushing or soft touches.
