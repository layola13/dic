pantheistic:/ˌpænθiˈɪstɪk/| adj.|泛神论的|Adjective.  Relating to or characterized by pantheism; believing that God is everything and everywhere, and that everything is a part of God.  Pantheism is the belief that all is God, and God is all. This contrasts with theism, which posits a separate, personal God, and atheism, which denies the existence of God.  |例句：His pantheistic worldview sees the divine in all aspects of nature. 他的泛神论世界观认为自然界的各个方面都有神性。

His philosophy is deeply pantheistic, identifying the divine with the universe itself.他的哲学是深刻的泛神论，将神与宇宙本身等同起来。|近义词：universalistic, cosmic|反义词：theistic, atheistic
