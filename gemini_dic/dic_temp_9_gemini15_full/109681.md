leucoryx: /ˈlʊkɒrɪks/ | n. |白羚羊| An antelope of the genus Oryx, characterized by its white coat and long, straight horns.  It is native to the Arabian Peninsula and is critically endangered. |The Arabian leucoryx was once hunted to near extinction, but conservation efforts have helped increase its population.  阿拉伯白羚羊曾经被猎杀到几乎灭绝，但保护工作帮助增加了它的数量。|近义词：antelope, oryx |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与其他颜色羚羊对比)|名词
