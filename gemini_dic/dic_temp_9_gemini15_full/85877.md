headboard:/ˈhɛdˌbɔːrd/ | n. |床头板 | A headboard is the vertical panel at the head of a bed, often upholstered or decorated.  It can provide support for pillows and can be a decorative feature in a bedroom. | Example:  She bought a beautiful carved headboard for her antique bed. 她为她的古董床买了一个漂亮雕刻的床头板。 | 近义词: bedboard | 反义词: None
