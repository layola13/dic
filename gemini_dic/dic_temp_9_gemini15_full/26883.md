cakewalkers: /ˈkeɪkˌwɔːkərz/ | n. |轻松的事；容易的事；轻而易举的事 |Things that are easy to do or achieve, requiring little effort.  It often implies a condescending attitude towards the task or those who find it difficult.| The whole project was a cakewalk for her, she finished it in a week.  整个项目对她来说易如反掌，她一周就完成了。|  easy task, breeze, cinch | difficult task, challenge, struggle | 名词


USAGE->
