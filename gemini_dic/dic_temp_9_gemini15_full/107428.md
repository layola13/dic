lampblack: /ˈlæmpˌblæk/ | n. | 炭黑，烟炱 | A fine black pigment made by burning oil or other substances in a closed container.  It's historically used in paints, inks, and other applications. | The artist used lampblack to create the deep, rich shadows in his painting.  艺术家用炭黑在他的画中创造出深邃丰富的阴影。|  carbon black, soot |  (no direct antonym, but lighter pigments could be considered opposites)| 名词
