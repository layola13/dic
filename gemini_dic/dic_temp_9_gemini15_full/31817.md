checksums:/ˈtʃɛksʌmz/ | n. |校验和(复数) |A checksum is a small-sized datum derived from a block of digital data for the purpose of detecting errors that may have been introduced during its transmission or storage.  It's a simple but effective way to verify data integrity. |Example: The program calculated the checksum of the downloaded file to ensure it wasn't corrupted during the download.  程序计算了下载文件的校验和以确保它在下载过程中没有损坏。|近义词：hash, check value, control sum |反义词：无
