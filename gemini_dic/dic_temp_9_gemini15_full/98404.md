insolvents: /ɪnˈsɒlvənts/ | adj. |无力偿债的，破产的 |  Unable to pay outstanding debts.  Describes a person or company that is bankrupt or financially unable to meet their obligations. | The company declared insolvency after years of mounting losses.  这家公司在多年亏损累积后宣布破产。|  bankrupt, insolvent, bankrupt, broke | solvent, affluent, wealthy
