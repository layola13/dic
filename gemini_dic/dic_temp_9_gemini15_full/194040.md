self-furnished:/ˌsɛlfˈfɜːrnɪʃt/| adj.| 自带家具的；设施齐全的 |  Describes a property (usually an apartment or house) that comes with furniture already in place.  This furniture is typically basic but sufficient for living.  It contrasts with "unfurnished" properties which have no furniture. | Example: The apartment is self-furnished, so you won't need to bring any of your own furniture.  这套公寓自带家具，所以你不需要带任何自己的家具。|近义词：furnished, equipped|反义词：unfurnished
