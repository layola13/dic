de-litters: /ˌdiːˈlɪtərz/ | v. |  清除杂物；清理垃圾 | To remove litter or debris from a place.  The act of removing litter. | 例句：The volunteers de-littered the park after the festival. 志愿者们在节日结束后清理了公园的垃圾。


例句：The city council is implementing a new program to de-litter the streets. 市议会正在实施一项新的计划来清理街道垃圾。


近义词：clean up, clear up, tidy up, remove debris


反义词：litter, trash, pollute


词性：动词
