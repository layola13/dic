quartic:/ˈkwɔːrtɪk/| adj.|四次的；四次方的|Of or relating to the fourth degree.  A quartic equation is a polynomial equation of the fourth degree.  A quartic curve is a curve defined by a quartic equation.|例句：The quartic equation x⁴ + 2x² - 3 = 0 has two real roots and two imaginary roots.  这个四次方程x⁴ + 2x² - 3 = 0有两个实根和两个虚根。

例句：The mathematician analyzed the quartic curve to determine its properties. 数学家分析了四次曲线以确定其特性。


近义词：fourth-degree (in the mathematical context)


反义词：linear, quadratic, cubic (referring to polynomial degrees)

词性：形容词
