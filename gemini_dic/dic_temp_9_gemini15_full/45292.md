culturist:/ˈkʌltʃərɪst/| n. |文化学者，文化工作者|A person who studies or promotes culture.  Someone who is deeply involved in a particular culture and actively participates in or promotes its preservation, development, or dissemination. |例句：The culturist gave a fascinating lecture on the traditions of the Maori people. 文化学者就毛利人的传统作了一个引人入胜的讲座。|例句：She's a dedicated culturist, working to revive traditional crafts in her community. 她是一位致力于在社区复兴传统工艺的文化工作者。|近义词：anthropologist, ethnologist, cultural expert |反义词：无
