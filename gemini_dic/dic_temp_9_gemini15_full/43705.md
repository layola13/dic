creditorship:/ˈkrɛdɪtərʃɪp/ | n. |债权人身份；债权；贷方地位 |The state or position of being a creditor; the rights and duties of a creditor.  It refers to the legal relationship between a creditor and a debtor.  | Example:  The creditorship established through the loan agreement granted certain rights to the lender.  债权人身份通过贷款协议确立，赋予贷款人某些权利。|近义词：credit, creditor's rights |反义词：debtorship
