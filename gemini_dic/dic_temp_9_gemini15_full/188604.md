rumbled:/ˈrʌmbəld/ | v. | 隆隆作响；低沉地说；(肚子)咕咕叫 | To make a deep, continuous sound like distant thunder.  It can also refer to speaking in a low, grumbling voice, or to the rumbling sound of one's stomach. |例句：The distant thunder rumbled in the distance. 远处传来隆隆的雷声。
The old engine rumbled to life. 旧引擎隆隆地启动了。

近义词：rumbled, growled, boomed, thundered | 反义词：whispered, murmured


USAGE->

The train rumbles along the tracks. = 火车沿着铁轨隆隆行驶。
He rumbles something under his breath. = 他低声咕哝着什么。
