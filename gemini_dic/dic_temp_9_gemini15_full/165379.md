prejunior: /ˌpriːˈdʒuːniər/ | adj. | 预备初级的；小学预科的 |  Describing a level of education or a program for students younger than those in junior school. This term is less common and often used in specific school systems. It typically refers to a preparatory year or program before entering junior school (usually equivalent to elementary school). | 例句：The prejunior program focuses on developing fundamental literacy and numeracy skills.  预备初级课程侧重于培养基本的读写和算术能力。


The prejunior class prepares children for the challenges of junior school. 预备初级班为孩子们应对小学的挑战做准备。| 近义词：preparatory, preliminary, introductory | 反义词：senior, advanced, graduate
