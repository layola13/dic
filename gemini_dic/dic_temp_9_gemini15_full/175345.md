rake-off:/ˈreɪkɒf/|/ˈreɪkɔːf/| n. |回扣；分赃；好处；报酬|A share of profits or proceeds, especially one that is secretly or dishonestly obtained.  It often refers to a bribe or illicit payment.|例句：The police officer was accused of taking a rake-off from the drug dealers.  警察被指控收受毒贩的回扣。

The construction company was suspected of receiving a significant rake-off on the project. 建设公司涉嫌在该项目中获得巨额回扣。


近义词：bribe, kickback, backhander, cut, share|反义词：honestly earned payment, legitimate payment|名词
