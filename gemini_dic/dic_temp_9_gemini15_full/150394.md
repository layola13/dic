panorama:/ˌpænəˈræmə/| n. |全景；概观；一系列事件|A wide, unbroken view of a surrounding area; a complete picture or survey of a subject or situation.|The panoramic view from the mountaintop was breathtaking.  山顶的全景令人叹为观止。|The book offers a panorama of 20th-century history. 这本书提供了20世纪历史的全景概览。|vista, overview, survey| close-up, detail |名词
