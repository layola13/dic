festivous:/ˈfɛstɪvəs/| adj.|欢乐的；喜庆的；节日的| characterized by festivity; joyous; merry; celebratory.  The word evokes a sense of lively celebration and merriment, often associated with holidays or special occasions.  It suggests a cheerful and exuberant atmosphere.|例句：The festivous atmosphere of the carnival was infectious.  狂欢节的欢乐气氛极具感染力。


The town square was decorated in a festivous manner for the holiday.  为了节日，小镇广场装饰得喜庆洋洋。

近义词：joyful, merry, celebratory, jovial, festive

反义词：somber, gloomy, mournful, depressing
