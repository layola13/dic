proappointment:/ˌproʊəˈpɔɪntmənt/| n. | 事先约定；预先任命 | A proappointment is an arrangement made in advance, typically for a meeting or appointment.  It emphasizes the pre-arranged nature of the event. |Example: The proappointment for the meeting was set for 3 PM.  事先约定的会议时间是下午3点。 |近义词：prearrangement, prior engagement, scheduling |反义词：impromptu, spontaneous, unplanned |名词
