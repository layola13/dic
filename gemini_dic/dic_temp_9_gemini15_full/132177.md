noncyclical: /ˌnɒnˈsaɪklɪkəl/ | adj. | 非周期性的，非循环的 | Not occurring in cycles; not repetitive or recurring in a regular pattern.  This adjective describes something that doesn't follow a repeating pattern or cycle.  It contrasts with cyclical or periodic phenomena. |


例句：

The company's profits showed a noncyclical pattern of growth, defying typical economic fluctuations.  这家公司的利润呈现出非周期性的增长模式，无视典型的经济波动。

The noncyclical nature of the project made budgeting more challenging.  该项目的非周期性使得预算编制更具挑战性。


近义词：irregular, aperiodic, sporadic, intermittent


反义词：cyclical, periodic, recurrent, recurring


词性：形容词
