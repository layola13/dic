natant:/ˈneɪtənt/| adj.|漂浮的；游泳的；水生的|Floating on or near the surface of water; swimming.  The term often describes organisms or objects that are buoyant and remain on or near the surface of water.|例句：The lily pads were natant on the still pond.  睡莲叶片漂浮在平静的池塘上。

The natant larvae of the insect drifted downstream.  昆虫的漂浮幼虫顺流漂向下游。|近义词：floating, buoyant, swimming, aquatic|反义词：submerged, sunken, benthic
