lemuriform:/ˈlɛmjʊrɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 狐猴形的，似狐猴的 | Resembling a lemur; characteristic of lemurs.  The term often refers to the physical characteristics, such as their eyes, limbs, and locomotion. | Example:  The newly discovered primate possessed several lemuriform characteristics, including large forward-facing eyes and a long tail. = 新发现的灵长类动物拥有几种狐猴形的特征，包括大的朝前的眼睛和一条长尾巴。 The lemuriform skull was easily identifiable due to its unique dental formula. = 由于其独特的牙齿结构，狐猴形的头骨很容易识别。| 近义词：lemur-like | 反义词：无
