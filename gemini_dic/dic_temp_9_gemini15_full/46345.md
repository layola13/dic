dactylar: /ˈdæktɪlɑːr/ | n. | 音节诗节，达克提尔；指状的 | A dactyl is a metrical foot consisting of one stressed syllable followed by two unstressed syllables.  The term can also refer to something resembling a finger or toe. |例句：The poem is written in dactylic hexameter. 这首诗是用达克提尔六音步写的。|近义词：dactylic |反义词：spondee (a metrical foot with two stressed syllables)
