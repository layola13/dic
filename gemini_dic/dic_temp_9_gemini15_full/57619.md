draught:/drɑːft/ /dræft/| n. |  通风；气流；草稿；（一）杯（酒）；（鱼类）拖网；棋步| A current of air; a preliminary version of a piece of writing; a drink of liquid; a net used for fishing; a single move in a game of chess. |The draught in the room was making me cold.  房间里的通风让我感到很冷。| There is a strong draught coming from the window. 窗户边刮来一阵强风。|近义词：current, breeze, draft, sketch,  |反义词：stillness, calm


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The draught blows through the window. = 风从窗户吹进来。
A cold draught enters the room. = 一股冷风进入房间。


一般过去时:
A strong draught blew the papers off the desk. = 一阵强风把桌子上的文件吹走了。
The draught extinguished the candle. = 风把蜡烛吹灭了。


现在完成时:
The draught has cooled the room down. = 通风使房间凉快下来了。
A draught has blown the door shut. = 一阵风把门吹关上了。


过去完成时:
The draught had already subsided before we entered. = 我们进去之前，风已经停了。
The draught had blown out the fire before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，风已经把火吹灭了。


将来时:
A strong draught will blow tomorrow. = 明天会刮大风。
The draught will cool the room nicely. = 通风会使房间凉快。


虚拟语气:
If there were no draught, the room would be warmer. = 如果没有风，房间会更暖和。
I wish there weren't such a strong draught in here. = 我希望这里没有这么强的风。

否定句:
