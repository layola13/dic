ideographical:/ˌaɪdiəˈɡræfɪkəl/| adj.|表意文字的；象形文字的| Relating to or denoting a system of writing in which each symbol represents a word or morpheme rather than a sound.|The ideographical nature of Chinese writing makes it difficult for non-native speakers to learn.|近义词：pictographic, logographic|反义词：alphabetic, phonetic
