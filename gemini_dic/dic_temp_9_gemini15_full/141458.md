opines:/əˈpaɪnz/| v.|表达观点；认为；主张|To express an opinion; to believe or hold as an opinion.|The professor opined that the research was groundbreaking. 教授认为这项研究具有开创性。|believes, thinks, suggests, maintains|disagrees, refutes, denies|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He opines that the movie is excellent. = 他认为这部电影很棒。
She opines that the policy is flawed. = 她认为这项政策有缺陷。

一般过去时:
The critic opined that the play was a failure. = 批评家认为这部戏是失败的。
He opined that the weather would improve. = 他认为天气会好转。

现在完成时:
They have opined that the project is viable. = 他们认为该项目是可行的。
She has opined on many occasions that the system is unfair. = 她多次表示该系统不公平。

过去完成时:
The committee had opined before the vote that the proposal was unwise. = 委员会在投票前认为该提案不明智。
He had opined earlier that the evidence was inconclusive. = 他早些时候认为证据不足。

将来时:
She will opine on the matter at the meeting. = 她将在会议上就此发表意见。
The expert will opine that the situation is critical. = 专家将认为情况危急。


PHRASE->
opine on = 对…发表意见
opine that = 认为
opine freely = 自由地发表意见
in my opinion = 依我看
to my mind = 依我看


词源:
源自拉丁语opinari，意为“相信，认为”。
