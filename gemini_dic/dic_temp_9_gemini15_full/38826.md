concern:/kənˈsɜːrn/| /kənˈsɝːn/| n., v.|担心；关心；关注；（利害）关系；公司；事务|

英文解析:
As a noun, "concern" refers to a feeling of worry or anxiety, a matter that involves or affects someone, or a business or company.  As a verb, it means to worry, to be relevant to, or to involve.

例句:
My main concern is the safety of my family. = 我主要担心的是家人的安全。

The company's main concern is to increase profits. = 公司最关心的是增加利润。

近义词: worry, anxiety, interest, involvement, business, company

反义词: indifference, apathy, unconcern


词性:Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My job concerns me greatly. = 我的工作让我非常担心。
Their business concerns the import of goods. = 他们的生意涉及商品进口。


一般过去时:
The news concerned her deeply. = 这个消息让她深感担忧。
The meeting concerned the future of the project. = 会议讨论了项目的未来。


现在完成时:
This issue has concerned me for many years. = 这个问题多年来一直困扰着我。
The problem has concerned the entire community. = 这个问题一直困扰着整个社区。


过去完成时:
I had already concerned myself with the matter before they informed me. = 在他们通知我之前，我已经关注过这件事了。
They had concerned themselves with the issue for months. = 他们已经关注这个问题好几个月了。


将来时:
The results of the experiment will concern the scientific community. = 实验的结果将引起科学界的关注。
This will concern us deeply if not addressed. = 如果不解决这个问题，这将让我们深感担忧。


虚拟语气:
If I knew more, I would concern myself with it immediately. = 如果我知道更多，我会立即关注它。
I wish I hadn’t concerned myself with such trivial matters. = 我真希望自己没有为这些琐碎的事情操心。


被动语态:
The community was deeply concerned about the pollution. = 社区对污染问题深感担忧。
He was concerned by the news. = 他对这个消息感到担忧。


疑问句:
Does this concern you? = 这让你担心吗？
What concerns you most about this project? = 你对这个项目最担心什么？


否定句:
I'm not concerned about the outcome. = 我不担心结果。
The government was unconcerned about the plight of the poor. = 政府对穷人的困境漠不关心。


PHRASE->
show concern for = 表示关心
express concern about = 表达对…的担忧
cause for concern = 令人担忧的原因
raise concerns = 引起担忧
a matter of concern = 令人担忧的事情
