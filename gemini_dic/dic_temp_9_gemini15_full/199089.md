signwriters:/ˈsaɪnˌraɪtərz/| n. |手语书写者，手语翻译员|People who translate spoken or written language into sign language, often for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. They are skilled in both languages and the visual aspects of sign language.|The signwriters at the conference ensured everyone understood the presentations.  手语翻译员在会议上确保每个人都理解了演讲内容。| interpreters, translators |  non-signwriters
