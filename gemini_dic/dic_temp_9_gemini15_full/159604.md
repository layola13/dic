pollinating: /ˈpɒləneɪtɪŋ/ | v. |授粉；使受粉 |The act of transferring pollen from the anther (male part) of a flower to the stigma (female part) of a flower, enabling fertilization and seed production.  This process is crucial for the reproduction of many plants. |例句：Bees are essential for pollinating many crops. 蜜蜂对许多作物的授粉至关重要。

例句：The wind helps to pollinate many grasses. 风有助于许多草类的授粉。


近义词：fertilizing, inseminating

反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider terms implying prevention of pollination, like sterilizing or isolating.
