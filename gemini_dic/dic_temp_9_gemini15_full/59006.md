DVD:/ˌdiːviːˈdiː/| n. |数字视频光盘|A digital versatile disc (DVD) is an optical disc used to store digital data.  It's most commonly used for storing movies and other videos, but can also store other types of data.  DVDs have a larger storage capacity than CDs.| 例句：I rented a DVD of the new Star Wars movie. = 我租了一张新的星球大战电影的DVD。

例句：We backed up our important files onto DVDs. = 我们将重要的文件备份到DVD上。

近义词：optical disc, video disc

反义词：None (no direct antonym)

词性: 名词
