own:/oʊn/|pron./adj./v.|自己的；拥有；承认|The word "own" can function as a pronoun, adjective, or verb. As a pronoun, it refers to something belonging to oneself. As an adjective, it modifies a noun to indicate possession. As a verb, it means to possess or have something as one's property.  |例句：This is my own car. 这是我自己的车。  She owns a large house. 她拥有一栋大房子。 He owned up to his mistake. 他承认了自己的错误。|近义词：possess, have, admit, acknowledge |反义词：lose, relinquish, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I own a dog. = 我养了一条狗。
They own a small business. = 他们拥有一家小企业。


一般过去时:
She owned a bookstore. = 她以前拥有一家书店。
He owned up to the crime. = 他承认了罪行。


现在完成时:
I have owned this car for five years. = 我拥有这辆车五年了。
They have owned the company since 2000. = 自从2000年以来，他们就拥有这家公司了。


过去完成时:
She had owned several houses before buying this one. = 在买下这栋房子之前，她已经拥有过几栋房子了。
He had owned the land for generations. = 他家几代人都拥有这块土地。


将来时:
I will own my own house someday. = 总有一天我会拥有自己的房子。
They will own a fleet of cars by next year. = 到明年他们将拥有一个车队。


虚拟语气:
If I owned a yacht, I would sail around the world. = 如果我拥有一艘游艇，我就会环游世界。
I wish I owned a bigger apartment. = 我希望我有一套更大的公寓。


被动语态:
The land is owned by the government. = 这块土地归政府所有。
The business was owned by her family for many years. = 这家企业多年来一直由她家所有。


疑问句:
Do you own a car? = 你有车吗？
Does she own a computer? = 她有电脑吗？


否定句:
I don't own a bicycle. = 我没有自行车。
They don't own any property. = 他们没有任何房产。
