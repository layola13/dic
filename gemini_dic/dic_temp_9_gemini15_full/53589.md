disbursed:/dɪsˈbɜːrst/| vt.|支付；分配；散布|To distribute or pay out (money or supplies).  It implies a formal or official distribution, often from a central source.|例句：The company disbursed the bonuses to its employees. 公司把奖金发放给了员工。
The government disbursed emergency aid to the flood victims. 政府向洪灾受害者发放了紧急援助。|近义词：pay out, distribute, allocate|反义词：collect, receive, withhold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disburses bonuses annually. = 公司每年发放奖金。
The government disburses funds to various projects. = 政府向各个项目拨款。

一般过去时:
They disbursed the funds quickly and efficiently. = 他们迅速有效地支付了款项。
The school disbursed the textbooks to the students. = 学校把课本分发给学生。

现在完成时:
The organization has disbursed millions of dollars in aid. = 该组织已经发放了数百万美元的援助款项。
The bank has disbursed all the loan applications. = 银行已经处理了所有的贷款申请。

过去完成时:
The company had disbursed all the salaries before the end of the month. = 公司在月底之前已经发放了所有工资。
They had disbursed the food supplies before the hurricane hit. = 飓风来临之前，他们已经分发了食物供应。

将来时:
The government will disburse the funds next week. = 政府下周将拨款。
The organization will disburse the donations to the charities. = 该组织将把捐款发放给慈善机构。

虚拟语气:
If the government disbursed more funds, the project would be completed sooner. = 如果政府拨款更多，项目就能更快完成。
I wish they had disbursed the money earlier. = 我希望他们早点把钱发放了。

被动语态:
The funds were disbursed according to the budget. = 资金按照预算进行了分配。
The aid was disbursed fairly among the victims. = 援助款项在受害者中公平地分配了。

疑问句:
When will the funds be disbursed? = 资金什么时候发放？
How was the money disbursed? = 钱是怎么分配的？

否定句:
The company did not disburse the dividends this year. = 公司今年没有发放股息。
They haven't yet disbursed the grants. = 他们还没有发放拨款。
