centure:/ˈsɛntʃər/| n.|世纪；百年；一百年|A century is a period of 100 years.|例句：The 20th century saw many significant technological advancements.  20世纪见证了许多重大的技术进步。|近义词：hundred years|反义词：None|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A century lasts one hundred years. = 一个世纪持续一百年。
This century is full of challenges. = 这个世纪充满了挑战。

一般过去时:
Last century was a time of great change. = 上个世纪是一个巨大的变革时期。
The previous century ended with a war. = 上个世纪以一场战争结束。

现在完成时:
Several centuries have passed since then. = 从那时起已经过去了几个世纪。
Much has changed in the past century. = 在过去的一个世纪里，发生了很多变化。

过去完成时:
By the end of the last century, many things had changed. = 到上个世纪末，许多事情都发生了变化。
They had lived through two centuries. = 他们经历了两个世纪。

将来时:
The next century will bring new technologies. = 下个世纪将带来新技术。
What will the world be like in the next century? = 下个世纪世界将会是什么样子？

虚拟语气:
If the last century had been peaceful, things would be different. = 如果上个世纪是和平的，情况就会不同。
I wish this century were more peaceful. = 我希望这个世纪更加和平。


被动语态:
This century is often described as the information age. = 这个世纪通常被称为信息时代。
The previous century was greatly impacted by war. = 上个世纪受到了战争的巨大影响。

疑问句:
Which century are we living in? = 我们生活在哪个世纪？
What significant events happened in the 19th century? = 19世纪发生了哪些重大事件？

否定句:
This isn't the first century to face these problems. = 这不是第一个面临这些问题的世纪。
The last century did not see peace everywhere. = 上个世纪并非到处都是和平。
