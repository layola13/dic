chaffed:/tʃæft/| vt. n.| 嘲弄；开玩笑；磨损；(谷物)脱壳 | To tease or make fun of someone playfully; to rub against something causing irritation or wear. |例句：He chaffed his friend about his new haircut. 他开玩笑地取笑朋友的新发型。  |例句：The rope chafed against the sailor's hands. 绳子磨擦着水手的双手。 |近义词：tease, banter, jest,  irritate |反义词：praise, compliment, soothe


USAGE->

He chaffs his brother constantly. = 他不断地取笑他的兄弟。
The rough fabric chafes against my skin. = 粗糙的布料摩擦我的皮肤。


一般过去时:
