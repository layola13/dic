choppers:/ˈtʃɑːpərz/| n.| 砍刀；直升机；摩托车；(俚语)牙齿 |Choppers are large knives used for chopping wood.  直升机是用于砍柴的大刀。|The police arrived in helicopters. 警察乘直升机到达。|近义词：helicopters, motorcycles, knives |反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mechanic chops the wood. = 机械师砍柴。
He chops vegetables every day. = 他每天都切蔬菜。


一般过去时:
The woodcutter chopped down the tree. = 伐木工人砍倒了树。
She chopped the onions finely. = 她把洋葱切得很细。


现在完成时:
They have chopped all the wood for the winter. = 他们已经砍好了过冬的柴火。
He has chopped the vegetables for the stew. = 他已经把炖菜的蔬菜切好了。


过去完成时:
He had chopped the vegetables before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经把蔬菜切好了。
She had chopped down the old tree before building the house. = 在建房子之前，她已经砍掉了那棵老树。


将来时:
They will chop the wood tomorrow. = 他们明天将砍柴。
She will chop the vegetables for the salad.  = 她将为沙拉切蔬菜。


虚拟语气:
If he chopped the wood, we could have a fire. = 如果他砍柴，我们就能生火。
I wish I had chopped the vegetables earlier. = 我真希望我早点把蔬菜切好。


被动语态:
The wood was chopped by the lumberjack. = 木材被伐木工人砍倒了。
The vegetables are being chopped for the soup. = 蔬菜正在被切碎做汤。


疑问句:
Did he chop the wood? = 他砍柴了吗？
Will she chop the vegetables? = 她会切蔬菜吗？


否定句:
He didn't chop the wood. = 他没有砍柴。
She won't chop the vegetables. = 她不会切蔬菜。
