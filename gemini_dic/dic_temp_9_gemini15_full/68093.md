faders:/ˈfeɪdərz/| n.|推子，衰减器|A fader is a sliding control on a mixing console or audio equipment used to adjust the volume or level of an audio signal.  It's typically a vertical slider that allows for precise control over the signal's amplitude.|例句：The sound engineer carefully adjusted the faders to balance the mix.  声音工程师仔细地调整推子以平衡混音。
例句：He used the faders on the mixing desk to gradually lower the music volume. 他使用调音台上的推子逐渐降低音乐音量。|近义词：slider, attenuator|反义词: amplifier(in the context of opposite function)
