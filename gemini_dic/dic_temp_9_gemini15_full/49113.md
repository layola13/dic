deintegrate:/ˌdiːˈɪntɪɡreɪt/| vt. |取消种族隔离；使…解体；使…瓦解|To end a system of racial segregation; to break down or disintegrate.|例句：The school board voted to deintegrate the schools. 学校董事会投票决定取消学校的种族隔离。

例句：The empire began to deintegrate after the death of the emperor. 皇帝死后，帝国开始瓦解。

近义词：desegregate, disintegrate, break down

反义词：integrate, segregate

词性：动词
