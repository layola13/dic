nontransient: /nɑːnˈtrænzɪənt/ | adj. |非暂时的；永久的；持久的| Not temporary; lasting; permanent.  The effects of the medication are nontransient, lasting for several weeks.  |近义词：permanent, lasting, enduring, abiding | 反义词：temporary, transient, ephemeral

例句：

The company's success is due to its nontransient commitment to quality.  公司的成功源于其对质量的持久承诺。

His influence on the community is nontransient; he's made a lasting impact. 他对社区的影响是持久的；他留下了持久的影响。


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of"
* **transient**:  adj.  meaning "lasting only for a short time; temporary"  (from Latin *transire* "to go across, pass over")


用法说明：


由于nontransient是一个形容词，它不涉及动词的各种时态变化。  以下是一些例句展示其在不同语境中的用法:

1.  **指事物性质:**  The damage to the building was nontransient; it required extensive repairs.  建筑物的损坏是永久性的；需要大规模维修。

2.  **指影响:** The impact of the policy change was nontransient, affecting the economy for years to come.  政策变化的影响是持久的，将在未来几年影响经济。

3.  **指记忆或感受:** Her feelings of gratitude were nontransient; she remembered his kindness for a long time.  她感激之情是持久的；她很长时间都记得他的好意。

4.  **与反义词对比:** Unlike transient residents, the nontransient population of the town has remained relatively stable.  与暂住居民不同，该镇的常住人口保持相对稳定。


PHRASE->

由于nontransient本身就是一个完整的词，它没有常见的词组搭配。  与其搭配的词语通常是它所修饰的名词，例如：nontransient effects (持久的影响), nontransient population (常住人口), nontransient memory (长期记忆).
