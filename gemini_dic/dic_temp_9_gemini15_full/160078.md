pom-pom:/ˈpɑːmpɑːm/ | n. | 啦啦队手中的蓬蓬球；毛绒球 |  A pom-pom is a fluffy ball, often made of yarn or other soft material, used especially by cheerleaders to add visual excitement to their routines. They are typically attached to handles or held directly. | Example: The cheerleaders waved their pom-poms enthusiastically. 啦啦队员们热情地挥舞着他们的蓬蓬球。 |近义词：cheerleader's prop, fluffy ball | 反义词：无
