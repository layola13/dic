externalize: /ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使外部化；客观化；外化 | To make something external or outward; to express or manifest an internal state or process outwardly.  |例句：He externalized his anger by shouting. 他通过大喊来发泄怒气。

例句：The company decided to externalize its IT services.  公司决定将其IT服务外包。


近义词：objectify, manifest, express, project


反义词：internalize, repress, conceal


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They externalize their anxieties through compulsive behaviors. = 他们通过强迫行为来表达他们的焦虑。
The company externalizes its customer support. = 这家公司将客户支持外包。


一般过去时:
She externalized her grief through painting. = 她通过绘画来表达她的悲伤。
The factory externalized its production to a cheaper location. = 工厂将其生产转移到一个更便宜的地方。


现在完成时:
They have externalized many of their business functions. = 他们已经将许多业务职能外包。
The artist has externalized his inner turmoil through his work. = 这名艺术家通过他的作品表达了他的内心冲突。


过去完成时:
He had externalized his problems before seeking help. = 他在寻求帮助之前已经将他的问题表达出来。
The company had externalized its manufacturing process years ago. = 这家公司几年前就已经将其制造流程外包。


将来时:
They will externalize their data storage to the cloud. = 他们将把数据存储外包到云端。
The business will externalize its marketing efforts next quarter. = 该企业将在下个季度将营销工作外包。


虚拟语气:
If he externalized his feelings more, he might feel better. = 如果他能更多地表达自己的感受，他可能会感觉更好。
I wish I could externalize my thoughts more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地表达我的想法。


被动语态:
The company's IT services are externalized to a third-party provider. = 公司的IT服务外包给第三方提供商。
His anxieties were externalized through his restless behavior. = 他的焦虑通过他不安的行为表现出来。


疑问句:

Does the company externalize any of its operations? = 这家公司是否将任何业务外包？


否定句:
He did not externalize his fear, keeping it bottled up inside. = 他没有表达他的恐惧，而是把它压抑在心里。
The company does not externalize its research and development. = 这家公司没有将其研发工作外包。

词源:
The word "externalize" is derived from the prefix "ex-" (out, from) and the word "external" (relating to the outside or exterior).  The suffix "-ize" indicates the process of making something into a particular state.  Therefore, "externalize" literally means "to make something external."
