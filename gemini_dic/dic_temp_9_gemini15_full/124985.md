muggins:/ˈmʌɡɪnz/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋；容易受骗的人|A foolish or gullible person.  It is often used playfully or affectionately.|例句：Don't be such a muggins and fall for that trick!  别傻乎乎的，上当受骗！

例句：He's a bit of a muggins, always believing everything people tell him. 他有点傻，总是相信别人告诉他的每一件事。


近义词：fool, idiot, sap, dupe

反义词：clever, intelligent, shrewd


词性：名词
