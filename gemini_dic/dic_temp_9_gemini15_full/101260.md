invoker:/ɪnˈvoʊkər/| n. |调用者，祈求者，倡导者 | A person or thing that invokes something; someone who calls upon a deity or spirit; one who advocates or promotes something. | Example: The programmer acted as the invoker, initiating the method call.  程序员充当调用者，启动方法调用。  The priest acted as the invoker, calling upon the gods for guidance.  牧师充当祈求者，向神灵祈求指引。|近义词：caller, initiator, proponent, advocate |反义词：responder, recipient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The user invokes the function regularly. = 用户定期调用该函数。
The shaman invokes ancient spirits. = 萨满祭司召唤古代精灵。

一般过去时:
He invoked the emergency protocol. = 他启动了紧急规程。
She invoked the name of the goddess. = 她呼唤女神的名字。

现在完成时:

The team has invoked the backup system. = 团队已经调用了备份系统。

过去完成时:
She had invoked the spell before the battle. = 她在战斗前念过咒语。
He had invoked his right to silence. = 他行使过沉默权。
