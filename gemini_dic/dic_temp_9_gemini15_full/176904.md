realest: /ˈriːəlist/ | adj. | 最真实的；最实际的；最优秀的 |  The superlative form of "real," emphasizing the highest degree of authenticity, practicality, or excellence.  It's often used informally and can carry a slightly hyperbolic or ironic tone. | 例句：That's the realest advice I've ever received. = 那是听了多年以来我收到的最实在的建议。

That's the realest pizza I've ever eaten. = 这是我吃过最棒的披萨。


近义词：truest, most authentic, most genuine, most practical, most excellent | 反义词：falsest, most artificial, most impractical, least excellent | 词性：形容词
