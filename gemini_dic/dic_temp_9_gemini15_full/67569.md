extrapolation:/ɪkˌstræpəˈleɪʃən/| n. |推断；推论；外推法|The act of estimating, beyond the known range of data, the value of a variable on the basis of its relationship with another variable.  It involves extending a trend or pattern observed in a data set to predict values outside the range of the original data.  This is often used in statistical analysis, forecasting, and scientific modeling.|例句：The scientist used extrapolation to predict the future population growth based on current trends.  科学家根据目前的趋势，用外推法预测未来的增长人口。

例句：Based on the extrapolation of existing sales data, we project a 15% increase in revenue next quarter. 基于现有销售数据的推断，我们预计下季度收入将增长15%。


近义词：projection, prediction, estimation, inference|反义词：interpolation
