hygienists: /haɪˈdʒiːnɪsts/ | n. | 卫生学家，保健学家 | A hygienist is a healthcare professional who practices hygiene, especially dental hygiene.  They are trained to prevent and treat oral diseases. | Example: The hygienist cleaned my teeth and gave me advice on proper brushing techniques.  卫生学家为我清洁了牙齿，并就正确的刷牙技巧给了我建议。| 近义词：dental hygienist, health professional | 反义词： (无直接反义词，可考虑与"unhygienic"相关联的词语)
