second-guess:/ˌsekəndˈɡɛs/| vt.|事后批评；对…妄加猜测|To criticize (a decision or action) after it has been made, often implying doubt about its wisdom or correctness.  It can also mean to guess or speculate about something that has already happened.|例句：Don't second-guess my decisions. 不要对我的决定妄加猜测。

例句：I keep second-guessing myself about whether I should have taken that job. 我一直在怀疑自己是否应该接受那份工作。


近义词：criticize, question, doubt, speculate,  reconsider


反义词：endorse, approve, support, confirm


词性：动词
