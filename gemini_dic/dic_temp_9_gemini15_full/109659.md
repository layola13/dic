leucocyte:/ˈluːkəsaɪt/| n. |白细胞|A colorless cell that circulates in the blood and body fluids and is involved in counteracting foreign substances and disease; a white blood cell.|例句：The doctor ordered a blood test to count the number of leucocytes. 医生下令进行血液检查以计算白细胞的数量。|近义词：white blood cell, WBC|反义词：erythrocyte
