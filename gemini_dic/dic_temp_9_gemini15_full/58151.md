drought:/draʊt/| n. |干旱，旱灾 |A prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water.  |The drought caused widespread crop failure. 这场干旱导致了广泛的农作物歉收。|近义词：aridness, dryness |反义词：deluge, downpour |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A severe drought affects the region every year.  = 每年都有严重的干旱影响该地区。
The drought continues to impact water supplies. = 干旱持续影响着供水。


一般过去时:
A terrible drought hit the country last year. = 去年该国遭受了严重的干旱。
The farmers suffered greatly during the drought. = 农民们在干旱期间遭受了巨大的损失。


现在完成时:
The drought has devastated the farmland. = 干旱已经摧毁了农田。
We have experienced several droughts in recent years. = 近年来我们经历了几次干旱。


过去完成时:
The crops had already withered before the rain arrived. = 雨水到来之前，庄稼已经枯萎了。
The river had dried up completely due to the prolonged drought. = 由于长时间的干旱，河水已经完全干涸了。


将来时:
Scientists predict a severe drought next year. = 科学家预测明年将出现严重的干旱。
The drought will likely worsen if it doesn't rain soon. = 如果不久不下雨，干旱可能会加剧。


虚拟语气:
If there had been more rain, the drought wouldn't have been so severe. = 如果雨水更多一些，干旱就不会那么严重了。
I wish the drought would end soon. = 我希望干旱能尽快结束。


被动语态:
The region was severely affected by the drought. = 该地区受到了干旱的严重影响。
The crops are being threatened by the ongoing drought. = 庄稼正受到持续干旱的威胁。


疑问句:
How long will this drought last? = 这场干旱将持续多久？
Is this the worst drought we've ever experienced? = 这是否是我们经历过的最严重的干旱？


否定句:
The drought didn't affect the coastal areas as much. = 干旱对沿海地区的影响没有那么大。
There wasn't enough rain to alleviate the drought. = 雨水不足以缓解干旱。
