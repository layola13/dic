corp:/kɔːrp/| n.|公司，团体，集团|A corporation or other large group of people working together.|Example:  The corp is planning a new marketing campaign. 这个公司正在策划一个新的营销活动。|近义词：company, organization, firm|反义词：individual, sole proprietorship
