pergola:/pərˈɡoʊlə/ | n. |凉棚；葡萄架 | A pergola is an outdoor structure, typically made of wood or metal, with a roof of beams or rafters that supports climbing plants.  It provides shade and a decorative feature in gardens and other outdoor spaces. |The pergola was covered with fragrant jasmine.  这凉棚上爬满了芬芳的茉莉花。

The pergola offered a shady spot to relax. 这凉棚提供了一个阴凉的休息场所。


近义词：arbor, trellis, bower | 反义词：无
