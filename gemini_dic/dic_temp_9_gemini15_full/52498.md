differ:/ˈdɪfər/| vi.|不同，相异|To be unlike or dissimilar.|The twins differ in their personalities. 双胞胎的性格不同。|近义词：vary, disagree, deviate|反义词：agree, resemble, correspond|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Their opinions differ on this matter. = 他们在这件事上的意见不同。
His story differs from hers. = 他的说法与她的说法不同。

一般过去时:
The results differed significantly from our predictions. = 结果与我们的预测大相径庭。
Their accounts of the accident differed in several key details. = 他们对事故的描述在几个关键细节上有所不同。

现在完成时:
The two plans have differed significantly in their implementation. = 这两个计划在实施上存在显著差异。
His views have differed from mine since we first met. = 从我们第一次见面起，他的观点就与我的不同。

过去完成时:
The witness's testimony differed from what he had previously stated.  = 目击证人的证词与他先前所说的不同。
Before the merger, the two companies differed greatly in their organizational structures. = 在合并之前，两家公司在组织结构上差异很大。


将来时:
The outcomes will likely differ depending on various factors. = 结果可能会因各种因素而异。
Their approaches to the problem will differ dramatically. = 他们解决问题的方法将大相径庭。

虚拟语气:
If their opinions differed so greatly, they should have discussed it more thoroughly. = 如果他们的意见如此不同，他们应该更仔细地讨论一下。
I wish my plans didn't differ so much from yours. = 我希望我的计划与你的计划差别不要这么大。


被动语态:
The two products are differentiated by their unique features. = 这两种产品因其独特的特性而有所区别。
The results were differentiated statistically. = 结果在统计上有所区分。


疑问句:
How do their approaches differ? = 他们的方法有何不同？
In what ways do these two theories differ? = 这两种理论在哪些方面有所不同？


否定句:
Their accounts of the event did not differ significantly. = 他们对事件的描述没有显著差异。
The two samples did not differ in their chemical composition. = 这两个样本的化学成分没有差异。


PHRASE->
differ from = 与…不同
differ in = 在…方面不同
differ widely = 大相径庭
differ significantly = 显著不同
differ substantially = 大致不同
