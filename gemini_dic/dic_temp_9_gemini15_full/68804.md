fasciate:/ˈfæʃieɪt/| adj.|束状的；带状的；条纹状的|  Describes a plant stem or leaf that is flattened and ribbon-like, often due to a genetic mutation or environmental factors. This results in a fused or bundled appearance of normally separate structures. |例句：The fasciated stem of the cactus was unusually broad and flat.  这株仙人掌的束状茎异常宽阔而扁平。

例句：The genetically modified plant exhibited a striking fasciated leaf structure.  转基因植物展现出引人注目的束状叶结构。


近义词：banded, ribbon-like, flattened, fused

反义词：unfasciated, cylindrical, round

词性:形容词
