pogo-stick:/ˈpoʊɡoʊˌstɪk/ | n. | pogo stick;跳跳板 | A pogo stick is a toy consisting of a stick with a spring mechanism at the bottom and footrests, used for bouncing up and down.  |例句：He bounced along on his pogo stick, a blur of motion. 他骑着跳跳板蹦蹦跳跳，像一道模糊的运动轨迹。

例句：The children were having a great time playing with their pogo sticks. 孩子们玩跳跳板玩得很开心。


近义词：spring stick ( less common)


反义词：None
