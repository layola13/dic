gluttonies:/ˈɡlʌtəniz/| n.|暴食；贪食；贪婪|The excessive consumption of food or drink.  It can also refer to excessive indulgence in anything.|例句：His gluttonies led to serious health problems. 他的暴食导致了严重的健康问题。

例句：Her gluttonies for power were well known. 她的权力欲是众所周知的。|近义词：overindulgence, avarice, greed|反义词：temperance, moderation, abstinence|名词
