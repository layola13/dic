nonadmitted:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪtɪd/| adj. |未被承认的，未被接纳的 | Not admitted; not accepted.  This term often refers to a claim, application, or person not being officially recognized or allowed entry. |Example: His application for the scholarship was nonadmitted due to insufficient qualifications. 他的奖学金申请由于资格不足而未被录取。|近义词：unaccepted, rejected, disallowed |反义词：admitted, accepted, approved
