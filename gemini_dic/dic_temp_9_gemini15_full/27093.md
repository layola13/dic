calicos:/ˈkælɪkoʊz/| n. |花布，花 calico 布|Calicos are a type of cotton fabric with a distinctive printed pattern, often featuring brightly colored flowers or other designs.  They are known for their vibrant colors and often used for quilting, clothing, and home décor. |例句：The quilt was made from scraps of colorful calicos. 这床被子是用五颜六色的花布碎片缝制的。|例句：She wore a dress made of a pretty calico print. 她穿着一件印着漂亮花纹的花布连衣裙。|近义词：chintz, printed cotton|反义词：plain cotton, solid-color fabric
