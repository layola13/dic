clean-living: /ˈkliːn ˈlɪvɪŋ/ | adj. n. | 健康的生活方式；清心寡欲的生活 |  A lifestyle characterized by abstinence from alcohol, tobacco, and other vices, often emphasizing health, fitness, and moral purity.  It often involves a focus on wholesome food choices, regular exercise, and avoidance of harmful substances. |  He leads a clean-living life, abstaining from alcohol and tobacco. 他过着健康的生活，不喝酒也不吸烟。  She advocates for clean living as a path to better health and well-being. 她提倡健康的生活方式，认为这是通往健康和福祉的途径。 | 近义词：healthy lifestyle, virtuous life, temperate life | 反义词：debauchery, dissipation, hedonistic lifestyle
