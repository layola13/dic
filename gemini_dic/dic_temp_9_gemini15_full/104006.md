jugum:/ˈdʒuːɡəm/| n.|轭，枷锁；（尤指）山脊|A yoke; especially, a ridge of mountains.  The word refers to a wooden bar used to join two oxen for plowing, and metaphorically extends to anything that binds or constrains.|例句：The farmer used a jugum to control his oxen.  这个农民用轭来控制他的牛。

例句：The high mountain range formed a natural jugum across the valley.  高耸的山脉在山谷中形成了一道天然的山脊。


近义词：yoke, bond, constraint, ridge

反义词：freedom, liberation


词性：名词
