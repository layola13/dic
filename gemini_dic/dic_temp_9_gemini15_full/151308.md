parietal:/pəˈraɪɪtəl/| adj. |壁的；顶骨的；顶叶的 | Relating to the wall of a body cavity or organ, or to a parietal bone.  The term is often used in anatomy and biology to describe structures related to the walls of cavities. For example, the parietal lobe of the brain is located near the top and sides of the head. |例句：The parietal cells in the stomach secrete hydrochloric acid. 胃壁细胞分泌盐酸。

例句：The parietal bone forms part of the skull. 顶骨构成颅骨的一部分。

近义词：wall-like,  (depending on context) membranous

反义词：visceral (referring to internal organs)
