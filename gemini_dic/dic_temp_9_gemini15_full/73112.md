for fear that:/fɔːr fɪr ðæt/| conj.|以免；唯恐；生怕|This conjunction expresses a reason for doing something, often to prevent something undesirable from happening.  It indicates a cautionary motive.|例句：He locked the door for fear that someone might break in. 他锁上门，以免有人闯进来。|例句：She studied hard for fear that she would fail the exam. 她努力学习，唯恐考试不及格。|近义词：lest, in case, so that…not|反义词：to ensure that, to make sure that|连词
