词源:
The word "orthopedical" comes from the Greek words "orthos" (ὀρθός), meaning "straight," and "pais" (παῖς), meaning "child."  The term was originally coined to refer to the straightening of children's bone deformities.  The "-ical" suffix indicates an adjective form.
