/pʊlˈdɛvəl/| n.|小鬼，淘气鬼|A mischievous or troublesome child or person.  The word is a blend of "pull" and "devil," suggesting someone who pulls pranks like a devil.|例句：He's a real pulldevil; he's always getting into trouble. 他真是个小淘气，总是惹麻烦。|例句：The pulldevil spilled the juice all over the floor.  这个小鬼把果汁洒了一地。|近义词：imp, rascal, scamp, urchin|反义词：angel, saint
