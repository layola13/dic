non-unionist: /ˌnɒnˈjuːnjənɪst/ | n. |非工会会员；反对工会者 | A person who is not a member of a labor union.  |例句：Many non-unionist workers found themselves at a disadvantage during negotiations. 许多非工会会员在谈判中发现自己处于不利地位。


例句：The company hired mostly non-unionist workers to avoid union influence.  这家公司主要雇佣非工会会员以避免工会的干预。


近义词：nonmember, anti-unionist


反义词：unionist, union member


词性：名词
