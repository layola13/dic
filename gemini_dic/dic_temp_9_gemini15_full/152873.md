pedantesque:/ˌpɛdənˈtɛsk/| adj.|卖弄学问的；炫耀学识的；学究式的|Characterized by a display of learning or scholarship that is excessive, showy, or pompous; ostentatious in one's learning.  It suggests an irritating or pretentious manner.|例句：His pedantesque pronouncements on grammar bored everyone. 他关于语法的卖弄学问的声明让每个人都厌烦了。|近义词：pompous, ostentatious, affected, pretentious|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming
