shorer:/ˈʃɔːrər/| n. |海岸居民；沿海居民|A shorer is a person who lives near the shore or coast.  They often work in industries related to the sea, such as fishing or shipping.|例句：Many shorers rely on the ocean for their livelihood. 许多沿海居民依靠海洋为生。|例句：The shorer's cottage was battered by the storm. 沿海居民的小屋被暴风雨摧残了。|近义词：coastal resident, beach resident |反义词：inlander
