closedness:/ˈkloʊzdnəs/ | /ˈkləʊzdnəs/ | n. |封闭性；亲密；排斥性 | The state or quality of being closed; a lack of openness or accessibility.  It can also refer to the feeling of intimacy or closeness in a relationship. | 例句：The closedness of the community made it difficult for newcomers to integrate. 社区的封闭性使得新来者难以融入。

The closedness of their relationship was evident in their tender glances. 他们关系的亲密性从他们温柔的眼神中可见一斑。|近义词：secretiveness, intimacy, seclusion, isolation |反义词：openness, accessibility, extroversion
