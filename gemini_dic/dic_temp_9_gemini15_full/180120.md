refocused:/ˌriːˈfoʊkəst/| v. |重新聚焦，重新集中注意力 |To focus again or differently; to shift one's attention.|例句：After a break, she refocused on her work.  休息后，她重新集中精力工作。|例句：The company refocused its marketing strategy on younger consumers. 公司将其营销策略重新定位于年轻消费者。|近义词：refocus, concentrate, center|反义词：distract, diffuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refocuses his energy on his studies. = 他把精力重新集中在学习上。
She refocuses her attention on the task at hand. = 她把注意力重新集中到手头的工作上。


一般过去时:
They refocused their efforts after the setback. = 他们在遭受挫折后重新集中了精力。
He refocused his camera on the distant mountains. = 他把相机重新对准远处的山脉。


现在完成时:
She has refocused her career goals. = 她已经重新调整了她的职业目标。
The company has refocused its brand image. = 公司已经重新调整了其品牌形象。


过去完成时:
They had refocused their strategy before launching the new product. = 他们在推出新产品之前已经重新调整了战略。
She had refocused her attention before making the crucial decision. = 在做出关键决定之前，她已经重新集中了注意力。


将来时:
He will refocus his efforts on completing the project. = 他将把精力重新集中在完成项目上。
They will refocus their marketing campaign next quarter. = 他们将在下个季度重新调整他们的营销活动。


虚拟语气:
If he refocused his efforts, he would succeed. = 如果他重新集中精力，他就会成功。
I wish she had refocused her attention earlier. = 我希望她早点把注意力集中起来。


被动语态:
The discussion was refocused on the main issue. = 讨论重新集中在主要议题上。
The project's objectives were refocused to meet the new requirements. = 项目的目标被重新调整以满足新的要求。


疑问句:
Has he refocused his priorities? = 他是否重新调整了他的优先级？
Will they refocus their resources? = 他们是否会重新分配资源？


否定句:
He didn't refocus his attention, and missed the important detail. = 他没有重新集中注意力，错过了重要的细节。
They haven't yet refocused their efforts on the core business. = 他们还没有将精力重新集中到核心业务上。
