lodging:/ˈlɒdʒɪŋ/| /ˈlɑːdʒɪŋ/ | n. |住所，住宿 |Temporary accommodation; a place to stay temporarily.  Lodging can refer to a single room or a whole building offering accommodation.  |例句：We found decent lodging in a small village. 我们在一个小山村找到了像样的住所。

例句：The hotel offers a variety of lodging options, from single rooms to suites. 这家酒店提供各种住宿选择，从单人间到套房。


近义词：accommodation, quarters, shelter, housing | 反义词：homelessness


词源:源于古英语 *lodgian，意为“居住，停留”。  This ultimately derives from the Proto-Germanic *laubjan, meaning "to lie down."
