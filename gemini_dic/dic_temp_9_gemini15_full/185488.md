reupholstered:/ˌriːˈuːpɒlstəd/| adj.|重新装饰内饰的；重新装潢的|Having been re-covered or re-stuffed with new upholstery.|The old armchair was reupholstered in a vibrant blue fabric.  那把旧扶手椅重新用鲜艳的蓝色布料装饰了。|近义词：refurbished, renovated, redecorated|反义词：worn, dilapidated
