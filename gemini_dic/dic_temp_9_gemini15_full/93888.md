immortalised: /ɪˈmɔːrtəlaɪzd/ | vt. | 使不朽；使永垂不朽 | To make someone or something famous or remembered for a long time.  | 例句：His heroic actions were immortalised in a famous painting. 他的英勇事迹在一幅名画中被永垂不朽。 | 例句：The author's works have immortalised her name in literary history.  这位作家的作品使她的名字在文学史上永垂不朽。| 近义词：memorialize, immortalize, perpetuate | 反义词：obliterate, forget, extinguish | 动词
