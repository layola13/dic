redistributing:/ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪŋ/ | v. |重新分配，再分配 | The act of distributing something again, especially resources or wealth, in a different way.  This often implies a fairer or more efficient allocation than the previous distribution. | Example: The government is redistributing wealth through new tax policies. = 政府正在通过新的税收政策重新分配财富。  The company is redistributing its marketing budget to focus on digital channels. = 公司正在重新分配其营销预算，重点放在数字渠道上。 | 近义词：reallocating, reallotment, reapportioning | 反义词：centralizing, concentrating
