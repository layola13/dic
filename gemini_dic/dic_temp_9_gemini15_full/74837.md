frenziedly:/ˈfrɛnzidli/| adv.|狂乱地，疯狂地|in a wild and uncontrolled way, showing great excitement or panic.|例句：The crowd surged forward frenziedly.  人群疯狂地涌上前。

例句：He worked frenziedly to finish the project before the deadline. 他疯狂地工作以赶在截止日期前完成项目。


近义词：frantically, wildly, madly, desperately|反义词：calmly, peacefully, serenely


词源:  Derived from the adjective "frenzied," which comes from the Old French word "frenetique," meaning "mad" or "frantic."  This, in turn, originates from the Greek word "phrenitis," referring to inflammation of the brain, hence, madness.
