deconglomerate: /ˌdiːkənˈɡlɒməreɪt/ | vt. |使（企业集团）分解；使（集团）解体|To separate a large company into smaller, independent companies.  This often involves selling off different parts of the business. The process is the opposite of conglomerate. |例句：The company decided to deconglomerate, selling off its non-core businesses to focus on its main product line.  这家公司决定分解，出售非核心业务，专注于主要产品线。

The government pressured the giant corporation to deconglomerate to promote competition.  政府向这家巨型公司施压，要求其分解以促进竞争。


近义词：dismember, divest, break up |反义词：conglomerate
