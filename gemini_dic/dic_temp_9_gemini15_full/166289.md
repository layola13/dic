prereconcilement:/ˌpriːˈrɛkənˌsaɪlmənt/| n. |预对账，预先调节|The process of preparing for a reconciliation, often involving preliminary checks and data preparation before a formal reconciliation takes place.  It aims to identify and resolve discrepancies before the final reconciliation. |Example: The prereconcilement stage identified several minor errors in the data, allowing for correction before the final reconciliation. 预对账阶段发现了数据中的一些小错误，从而可以在最终对账之前进行纠正。|近义词：preliminary reconciliation, pre-reconciliation, data cleansing|反义词：final reconciliation, post-reconciliation|名词
