ginger:/ˈdʒɪndʒər/| n. adj.|生姜；姜黄；红发|A pungent spice obtained from the rhizome of a ginger plant; also refers to a redhead.  The word also describes a shade of reddish-orange.|例句：I added a little ginger to the stir-fry. 我在炒菜里加了一点姜。

例句：Her hair is a fiery ginger. 她的头发是火红的姜黄色。|近义词：spice, redhead, reddish-orange|反义词:pale, bland
