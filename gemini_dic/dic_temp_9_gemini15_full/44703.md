crudities:/ˈkruːdətiz/| n.|粗俗；粗糙；拙劣之作；生疏；未加工品|Crude or unrefined things; things that lack sophistication or polish.  It can refer to manners, language, art, or materials.|例句：His jokes were full of crudities. 他的笑话充满了粗俗之处。

例句：The painting, despite its crudities, showed a raw talent.  这幅画虽然粗糙，但却展现了其原始的天赋。


近义词：vulgarities, improprieties, coarsenesses, gaucheries, rawness


反义词：refinements, sophistications, elegance, polish
