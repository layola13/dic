intolerant:/ɪnˈtɑːlərənt/| adj.|不宽容的，不能容忍的| unwilling or unable to accept different or opposing views;  showing intolerance.|例句：He is intolerant of other people's opinions. 他不能容忍别人的意见。|例句：She's intolerant of criticism. 她受不了批评。|近义词：bigoted, prejudiced, narrow-minded |反义词：tolerant, open-minded, broad-minded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is intolerant of criticism. = 他不能容忍批评。
She is intolerant of others' religions. = 她对别人的宗教信仰很不能容忍。

一般过去时:
They were intolerant of dissenting views. = 他们不能容忍异议。
He was intolerant of delays. = 他不能容忍延误。

现在完成时:
People have become increasingly intolerant of injustice. = 人们对不公正越来越不能容忍。
She has always been intolerant of laziness. = 她一直不能容忍懒惰。

过去完成时:
They had been intolerant of his behavior for years. = 几年来他们一直不能容忍他的行为。
I had never been so intolerant before. = 我以前从未如此不能容忍。


将来时:
We will be intolerant of any further breaches of the rules. = 我们将不容忍任何进一步违反规定的行为。
Society will become more intolerant of violence. = 社会将对暴力更加不容忍。


虚拟语气:
If he were more tolerant, he would have more friends. = 如果他更宽容一些，他就会有更多朋友。(与intolerant相反)
I wish I weren't so intolerant of his flaws. = 我希望我不那么不能容忍他的缺点。(与intolerant相反)


被动语态:
His views were considered intolerant by many. = 许多人认为他的观点是不能容忍的。
His behavior was found to be intolerant and unacceptable. = 发现他的行为是不宽容和不可接受的。


疑问句:
Is he intolerant of other cultures? = 他对其他文化是否不能容忍？
Are they intolerant of different viewpoints? = 他们是否不能容忍不同的观点？


否定句:
He is not intolerant; he is actually quite understanding. = 他并非不宽容；实际上他相当善解人意。
They were not intolerant of his mistakes. = 他们并不不能容忍他的错误。



PHRASE->
intolerant of: 对...不宽容的
intolerant attitude: 不宽容的态度
intolerant society: 不宽容的社会
intolerant behavior: 不宽容的行为
intolerant regime: 不宽容的政权


词源:
The word "intolerant" comes from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") + "tolerant".  "Tolerant" originates from the Latin word "tolerare," meaning "to bear, endure, or tolerate."  Therefore, "intolerant" literally means "not able to bear or endure."
