freakiest: /ˈfriːkiːst/ | adj. | 最怪异的，最奇特的 | Most strange, unusual, or bizarre.  It is the superlative form of "freaky." | 例句：That's the freakiest movie I've ever seen! = 那是我看过的最怪异的电影！  The freakiest thing happened to me yesterday. = 昨天发生在我身上最怪异的事情。| 近义词：strangest, weirdest, oddest, most bizarre, most unusual | 反义词：most normal, most ordinary, most typical
