cavorters:/kəˈvɔːrtərz/| n.|嬉戏的人，嬉戏的动物|People or animals playfully jumping or leaping about.|The children were cavorting in the park, their laughter echoing through the trees. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏，他们的笑声回荡在树林里。|近义词：frolic, gambol, romp, frisk|反义词：stand still, be quiet.
