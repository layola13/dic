imagineers:/ˌɪmædʒɪˈnɪrz/| n.|幻想工程师；创意工程师|Those who combine imagination and engineering skills to create innovative products, experiences, or environments, especially in the entertainment industry.  They are typically employed by companies like Disney to design and build theme park attractions, shows, and experiences.|Example: Disney's imagineers are renowned for their creativity and technical expertise. 迪士尼的幻想工程师以其创造力和技术专长而闻名。|近义词：creative engineers, innovative designers|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms might relate to words representing lack of creativity or practicality.|名词
