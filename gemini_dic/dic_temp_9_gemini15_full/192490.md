scrunch-dry:/skrʌntʃ draɪ/| adj.|揉搓至干的，皱巴巴的，弄皱后弄干的|Describes hair that has been dried after being scrunched or crumpled to create texture and volume.  It implies a slightly wrinkled or textured appearance.|Example: I scrunch-dried my hair to give it some waves. 我揉搓着把头发弄干，让它形成一些波浪。|近义词：crumple-dry, air-dry (with texture)|反义词：blow-dry (smooth),straighten
