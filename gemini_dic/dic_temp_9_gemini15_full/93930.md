immunized:/ˈɪmjuːnaɪzd/ | v. | 使免疫；使免除 | To protect someone or something from a disease by giving them a vaccine.  | Example: The child was immunized against measles.  这个孩子接种了麻疹疫苗。 |近义词：vaccinated, protected | 反义词：vulnerable, susceptible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He immunizes his children annually. = 他每年给他的孩子们接种疫苗。
The doctor immunizes patients against various diseases. = 医生为病人接种各种疾病的疫苗。


一般过去时:
They immunized the entire village against polio. = 他们为整个村庄接种了小儿麻痹症疫苗。
The nurse immunized the baby against diphtheria. = 护士为婴儿接种了白喉疫苗。


现在完成时:
She has immunized her dog against rabies. = 她已经为她的狗接种了狂犬病疫苗。
The government has immunized millions against influenza. = 政府已经为数百万人口接种了流感疫苗。


过去完成时:
The researchers had immunized the mice before the experiment. = 研究人员在实验前已经给老鼠接种了疫苗。
They had immunized the population against smallpox decades ago. = 几十年前他们已经让民众接种了天花疫苗。


将来时:
The clinic will immunize the new arrivals against typhoid. = 诊所将为新来的人接种伤寒疫苗。
They will immunize all the children in the school next month. = 他们下个月将为学校里所有的孩子接种疫苗。


虚拟语气:
If they had immunized the population earlier, there would have been fewer cases of the disease. = 如果他们早些为民众接种疫苗，疾病病例就会少得多。
It's essential that all children be immunized. = 所有儿童都必须接种疫苗。


被动语态:
The patients were immunized against the flu. = 病人们接种了流感疫苗。
The community was effectively immunized against the outbreak. = 社区有效地预防了疫情的爆发。


疑问句:
Have you been immunized against tetanus? = 你接种过破伤风疫苗吗？
Were the animals immunized before the study? = 在研究之前动物们接种了疫苗吗？


否定句:
He wasn't immunized against yellow fever before his trip. = 他在旅行前没有接种黄热病疫苗。
The children were not immunized against polio. = 孩子们没有接种小儿麻痹症疫苗。


PHRASE->
be immunized against = 对…免疫
immunize oneself against =  使自己免受…侵害
immunization program = 免疫接种计划
herd immunity = 群体免疫
immunize from = 免于


词源:
immunized源于免疫 (immunity),  最终来自拉丁语 *immunis,  意思是“免于服务或赋税”，后来引申为“免除疾病”。  "-ized" 是一个动词后缀，表示“使…化”。
