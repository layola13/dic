hurtfully:/ˈhɜːrtfʊli/| adj.|令人痛苦地；伤人地；恶意地 |Causing hurt or pain, either physically or emotionally; in a way that shows malice or ill will.|例句：He spoke hurtfully to his sister, causing her great distress. 他伤人地对妹妹说话，让她非常痛苦。|例句：Her comments were unintentionally hurtfully, but still caused offense. 她的评论并非故意伤人，但仍然造成了冒犯。|近义词：painfully, cruelly, maliciously, spitefully |反义词：kindly, gently, compassionately, considerately
