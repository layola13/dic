monistically:/məˈnɪstɪkli/| adv.| 一元论地；唯物论地 |  Adverb meaning in accordance with monism; asserting or implying the unity or oneness of all things.  It describes something done or existing from a monistic perspective.  | Example: He interpreted the universe monistically, reducing all phenomena to a single fundamental substance. 他从一元论的角度解释宇宙，将所有现象都归结为单一的根本物质。 | 近义词：unitarily, integrally | 反义词：pluralistically, dualistically
