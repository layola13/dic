peristaltic: /ˌpɛrɪˈstælɪk/ | adj. |蠕动的；蠕动性的 | Relating to or denoting movements like those of the alimentary canal involving a progressive wave of contraction that pushes the contents onward.  | 例句：The food moves through the digestive system by peristaltic action. 食物通过蠕动作用在消化系统中移动。|近义词：wavelike, undulating |反义词:stationary, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The food moves through the esophagus by peristaltic action. = 食物通过蠕动作用在食道中移动。
Peristaltic contractions help to propel the food bolus forward. = 蠕动收缩有助于推动食团向前。


一般过去时:
The patient experienced decreased peristaltic activity after surgery. = 患者术后蠕动活动减少。
The doctor observed strong peristaltic waves in the intestines. = 医生观察到肠道强烈的蠕动波。

词源:
