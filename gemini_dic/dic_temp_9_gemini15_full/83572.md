haematachometer: /ˌhiːmætəˈkɒmɪtər/ | n. | 血细胞计数器 | A haematachometer is an instrument used to count blood cells.  It typically uses a combination of optical and electronic methods to detect and count individual cells as they pass through a flow chamber.  Modern haematachometers can also provide information about cell size and other characteristics. | The doctor used a haematachometer to analyze the patient's blood sample.  医生用血细胞计数器分析了病人的血液样本。|  blood cell counter, cell counter |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
