intercomparisons:/ˌɪntər kəmˈpærɪzənz/ | n. |相互比较；对比；对照 |The process of comparing multiple things to find similarities and differences.  It often involves data from different sources or methods.  |例句：The study involved intercomparisons of different climate models to assess their reliability. 这项研究涉及对不同气候模型的相互比较，以评估其可靠性。

例句：Intercomparisons of satellite data with ground-based measurements are crucial for validation.  将卫星数据与地面测量数据进行对比对于验证至关重要。


近义词：comparisons, contrasts, correlations, analyses

反义词：无

词性：名词
