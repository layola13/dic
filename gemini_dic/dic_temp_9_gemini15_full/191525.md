词源:
Schmucky is a Yiddish word, derived from the Yiddish word "schmuck," meaning "fool" or "jerk."  The suffix "-y" adds an adjectival quality, transforming the noun into a descriptive adjective.  Therefore, "schmucky" describes someone or something possessing the qualities of a schmuck.
