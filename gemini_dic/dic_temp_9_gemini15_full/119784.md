migraine:/ˈmaɪɡreɪn/| n.|偏头痛，剧烈头痛|A migraine is a severe headache that can be accompanied by nausea, vomiting, and sensitivity to light and sound.|例句：I suffered from a migraine yesterday and had to stay in bed all day. 昨天我遭受了偏头痛，不得不整天躺在床上。|近义词：headache, severe headache|反义词:none
