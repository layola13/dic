hang tough:/hæŋ tʌf/| v. |坚持下去；顽强面对；不屈不挠|To remain strong and determined in a difficult situation; to persevere despite challenges.|Example:  We need to hang tough and fight for what we believe in. 我们需要坚持下去，为我们所信仰的而奋斗。

Example: Even though the business is struggling, we have to hang tough and find a solution. 即使生意正在艰难经营，我们也必须坚持下去并找到解决方案。

近义词：persevere, endure, persist, hold on

反义词：give up, surrender, quit, yield

词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hang tough in the face of adversity. = 他们在逆境中坚持不懈。
We hang tough even when things get difficult. = 即使事情变得困难，我们也坚持不懈。

一般过去时:
He hung tough throughout the entire ordeal. = 他在整个磨难中都坚持了下来。
She hung tough and eventually won the competition. = 她坚持不懈，最终赢得了比赛。

现在完成时:
They have hung tough for many years. = 他们坚持了很多年。
We have hung tough despite numerous setbacks. = 尽管屡遭挫折，我们仍然坚持不懈。

过去完成时:
He had hung tough until the very end. = 他一直坚持到最后。
She had hung tough even when everyone else had given up. = 即使其他人放弃了，她仍然坚持不懈。

将来时:
We will hang tough no matter what happens. = 无论发生什么，我们都会坚持下去。
They will hang tough and overcome this challenge. = 他们会坚持下去并克服这个挑战。

虚拟语气:
If I were in their shoes, I would hang tough. = 如果我是他们，我会坚持下去。
I wish I could hang tough like he does. = 我希望我能像他一样坚持不懈。

被动语态: (Hang tough is rarely used in the passive voice because it's an intransitive verb.)  The situation cannot be hung tough. (This sentence is grammatically correct but unnatural.)  情况不能被坚持下去。(这句话语法正确但不自然)


疑问句:
Will you hang tough through this difficult time? = 你会度过这段艰难时期吗？
Can we really hang tough and make it through? = 我们真的能坚持到底吗？

否定句:
They didn't hang tough and ended up failing. = 他们没有坚持下去，最终失败了。
We couldn't hang tough any longer; we had to give up. = 我们再也坚持不下去了；我们不得不放弃。


PHRASE->
hang tough and fight = 坚持战斗
hang tough through = 坚持度过
hang tough in the face of = 面对...坚持下去
hang tough and see = 坚持看看
just hang tough = 只需坚持
