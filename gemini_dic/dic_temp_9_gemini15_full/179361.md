redrawing: /ˌriːˈdrɔːɪŋ/ | v. |重新绘制；重画|The act of drawing something again, often with changes or improvements.  It implies a previous drawing existed and is being redone.|例句：The architect is redrawing the blueprints to incorporate the client's feedback. 建筑师正在重新绘制蓝图以纳入客户的反馈。

例句：She spent the afternoon redrawing the map, correcting several inaccuracies. 她花了一下午时间重新绘制地图，纠正了几处错误。


近义词：redrafting, re-sketching, revising


反义词：original drawing, first draft


词性：动词
