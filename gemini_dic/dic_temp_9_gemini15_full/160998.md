/pɔːstəzˈmætɪk/ | adj. |哮喘后的；哮喘缓解后的 | Postasthmatic refers to the period or condition following an asthma attack or episode. It describes the state of a person after their asthma symptoms have subsided, potentially including lingering effects.  The term is primarily used in medical contexts. | Example:  The patient experienced postasthmatic fatigue and shortness of breath.  哮喘发作后，病人感到疲劳和呼吸短促。| 近义词：post-asthmatic, post-attack (in a broader context)| 反义词：asthmatic (during an attack) |  形容词
