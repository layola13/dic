eked:/ɛkt/| vt.|勉强维持，设法获得|To obtain or get something with difficulty; to manage to make something last|She eked out a living by cleaning houses. 她靠打扫房子勉强维持生计。|近义词：scrape by, get by, make do|反义词：surplus, abundance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ekes out a living as a freelance writer. 他靠做自由撰稿人勉强维持生计。
They eke out their meager savings. 他们勉强维持着微薄的积蓄。


一般过去时:
She eked out a living by selling handmade crafts. 她靠卖手工制品勉强维持生计。
We eked out the remaining food for another day. 我们设法让剩下的食物又吃了一天。


现在完成时:
He has eked out a comfortable life for his family. 他为家人创造了舒适的生活（虽然过程艰难）。
They have eked out a living on this small farm for generations.  世世代代，他们在这小农场上勉强维持生计。


过去完成时:
They had eked out a living on the farm before moving to the city. 搬到城市之前，他们在农场上勉强维持生计。
She had eked out her resources before asking for help.  在她寻求帮助之前，她已经竭尽所能地利用了她的资源。


将来时:
We will eke out the remaining fuel until we reach the next town. 我们会省着用剩下的燃料，直到到达下一个城镇。
They will eke out a living from the land, no matter how difficult it is. 不管有多难，他们都会靠土地勉强维持生计。


虚拟语气:
If they had eked out their resources more carefully, they wouldn't be in this situation. 如果他们更仔细地节省资源，就不会陷入这种境地。
I wish I had eked out my savings better before retirement. 我希望我在退休前能更好地节省积蓄。


被动语态:
A living can be eked out through various means. 生计可以通过各种方式勉强维持。
The resources were eked out until the crisis passed. 资源被省着用，直到危机过去。


疑问句:
How did they eke out a living in such harsh conditions? 他们在如此恶劣的条件下是如何勉强维持生计的？
Can we eke out the supplies until help arrives? 我们能省着用这些物资等到救援到来吗？


否定句:
They didn't eke out a living in the city; they thrived. 他们并没有在城市里勉强维持生计；他们过得很滋润。
We couldn't eke out the food for another week. 我们无法让食物再维持一个星期。
