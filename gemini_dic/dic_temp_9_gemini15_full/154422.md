permission:/pəˈmɪʃən/| n. |许可，允许，批准 |Permission is the act of allowing someone to do something.  It can refer to the consent given or the right granted.  The word often implies a formal or official allowance. |例句：I need your permission to leave early. 我需要你的允许才能提前离开。

例句：She gave permission for the use of her photograph. 她允许使用她的照片。


近义词：consent, authorization, approval, allowance |反义词：prohibition, ban, denial


词性:名词
