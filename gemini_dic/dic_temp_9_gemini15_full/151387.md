parliamentarization:/ˌpɑːrliˈmɛntəraɪzeɪʃən/ | n. |议会化；议会制度化 | The process of establishing or strengthening parliamentary institutions and procedures.  This involves the development of representative government, the rule of law, and checks and balances on executive power.  It often includes the adoption of a written constitution, the establishment of independent judiciaries, and the creation of mechanisms for citizen participation in government. | Example: The parliamentarization of the country was a slow and difficult process, marked by periods of both progress and setbacks.  国家的议会化是一个缓慢而艰难的过程，既有进步也有挫折。 | 近义词：democratization, institutionalization | 反义词：authoritarianism, dictatorship |  名词
