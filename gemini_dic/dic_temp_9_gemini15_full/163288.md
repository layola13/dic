preconducted:/ˌpriːkənˈdʌktɪd/| adj.|预先进行的；预先完成的|Preconducted refers to something that has been done or carried out beforehand or in advance.  It implies planning and preparation before the main event or action.|例句：The preconducted survey showed a strong preference for the new design. 预先进行的调查显示，人们强烈偏爱新的设计。

The preconducted experiments provided valuable data for the study. 预先完成的实验为该研究提供了宝贵的数据。|近义词：prior, preliminary, beforehand, advance, antecedent |反义词：subsequent, following, later
