ideates:/aɪˈdiːeɪts/| vt.|构思；想出；设想 |To form or develop ideas or concepts in the mind.|The architect ideated a new design for the building.  建筑师构思了一个新的建筑设计。|The team ideated several solutions to the problem.  团队设想了几个解决问题的方案。|conceive, imagine, envision|criticize, reject|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ideates innovative solutions daily. = 他每天都构思创新的解决方案。
She ideates new marketing strategies. = 她构思新的营销策略。

一般过去时:
They ideated a plan to increase sales. = 他们构思了一个增加销售额的计划。
The artist ideated a masterpiece. = 艺术家构思了一幅杰作。

现在完成时:
We have ideated several product concepts this month. = 这个月我们已经构思了几个产品概念。
She has ideated a groundbreaking theory. = 她已经构思了一个具有突破性的理论。

过去完成时:
They had ideated a detailed proposal before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前已经构思了一个详细的提案。
He had ideated several ideas before deciding on the best one. = 他在决定最佳方案之前已经构思了几个想法。

将来时:
We will ideate new strategies for next year's campaign. = 我们将为明年的活动构思新的策略。
She will ideate a new approach to the problem. = 她将构思一种解决这个问题的新方法。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ideate a more comprehensive plan. = 如果我有更多时间，我会构思一个更全面的计划。
I wish I could ideate a solution to this complex issue. = 我希望我能构思出解决这个复杂问题的方案。


被动语态:
A new marketing campaign was ideated by the team. = 一个新的营销活动是由团队构思的。
Innovative solutions are constantly being ideated. = 创新的解决方案正在不断被构思。


疑问句:
Have you ideated any new ideas for the project? = 你为这个项目构思了任何新想法吗？
What are they ideating at the moment? = 他们目前正在构思什么？


否定句:
They did not ideate any practical solutions. = 他们没有构思出任何切实可行的解决方案。
I couldn't ideate a way to solve the puzzle. = 我无法构思出解决这个难题的方法。
