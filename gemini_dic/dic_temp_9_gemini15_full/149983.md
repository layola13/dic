palmettos:/pæˈmɛtoʊz/| n. |棕榈树(复数)|Palmettos are small palm trees, typically found in warm climates. They are characterized by their fan-shaped leaves and are often used in landscaping.  |例句：The Florida landscape was dotted with palmettos and other subtropical vegetation. 佛罗里达州的景色点缀着棕榈树和其他亚热带植被。

例句：We saw several palmettos growing near the beach. 我们在海滩附近看到几棵棕榈树。


近义词：palm trees, cabbage palms|反义词:|无
