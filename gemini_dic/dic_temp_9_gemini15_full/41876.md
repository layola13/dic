cosentient:/koʊˈsɛnʃənt/| adj.|  consentimento, 同意的；赞成的 | Consentient means giving consent; agreeing; approving.  It implies a willingness and understanding of what is being agreed to. |The consentient parties signed the contract.  同意各方签署了合同。|近义词：agreeing, consenting, approving |反义词：objecting, dissenting, disagreeing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is consentient to the plan. = 他同意这个计划。
They are consentient with the new rules. = 他们同意新的规章制度。


一般过去时:
She was consentient to the marriage. = 她同意这桩婚事。
