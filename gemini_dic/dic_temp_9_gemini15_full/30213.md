cellaress:/ˈsɛləres/| n.|女酒窖管理员，女管酒者|A woman in charge of a cellar, especially one containing wine.  The term often implies responsibility for the wine's storage and quality.|The cellaress meticulously checked the temperature and humidity of the wine cellar.|近义词：cellarer (gender-neutral), wine steward (more formal)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone responsible for other aspects of a building or establishment.
