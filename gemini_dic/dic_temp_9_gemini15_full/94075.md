impassively:/ɪmˈpæsɪvli/ | adv. | 冷淡地；无动于衷地 |  Showing no feeling or emotion; indifferent.  |例句：She listened impassively to the accusations. 她冷漠地听着指控。|例句：He received the news impassively, showing no sign of surprise or disappointment. 他冷漠地接受了这个消息，没有表现出任何惊讶或失望的迹象。|近义词：calmly, apathetically, indifferently, unconcernedly |反义词：passionately, emotionally, feelingly
