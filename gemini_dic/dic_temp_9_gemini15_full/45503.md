curds:/kɜːrdz/| n.|凝乳，豆腐渣|The thick, white substance formed when milk sours or is treated with rennet.  It is often used in cheesemaking.|例句：The milk had curdled and formed lumps of curds. 这牛奶已经凝结成块状的凝乳了。

例句：We used the curds from the milk to make cheese. 我们用牛奶里的凝乳来做奶酪。


近义词：coagulated milk, clots


反义词：liquid milk, whey


词性:名词
