rapeseeds:/ˈreɪpˌsiːdz/| n. |油菜籽；芸苔籽|The seeds of the rapeseed plant, used to produce rapeseed oil.  Rapeseeds are a significant source of vegetable oil and are also used in animal feed.|例句：We use rapeseeds to make biodiesel fuel. 我们用油菜籽制造生物柴油。|例句：The farmer harvested a bumper crop of rapeseeds this year.  今年农民收获了大量的油菜籽。|近义词：colza seeds|反义词: N/A
