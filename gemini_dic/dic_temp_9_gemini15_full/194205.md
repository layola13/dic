self-life:/selfˈlaɪf/| n.|自我生活，个人生活|The term "self-life" refers to the individual's personal existence, encompassing their experiences, thoughts, feelings, and actions as they relate to their own identity and well-being. It emphasizes the subjective and internal aspects of life as opposed to external factors.  |例句：He dedicated his self-life to the pursuit of knowledge. 他致力于追求知识，将其奉献于自己的生活。|近义词：personal life, individual life, inner life |反义词：collective life, social life|名词
