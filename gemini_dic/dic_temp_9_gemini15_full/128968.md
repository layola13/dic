niobate:/ˈnaɪəbeɪt/| n. |铌酸盐|A salt or ester of niobic acid. Niobates are compounds containing the niobium(V) oxide anion (NbO3−). They are often used in various applications due to their unique dielectric, ferroelectric, and piezoelectric properties.  For example, lithium niobate (LiNbO3) is widely used in optical devices. |The researchers synthesized a new type of niobate for use in high-temperature superconductors.  研究人员合成了一种新型铌酸盐，用于高温超导体。|  lithium niobate, potassium niobate, sodium niobate |  (no direct antonym, as it's a chemical compound)| 名词
