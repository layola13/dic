reformational:/ˌriːfɔːrˈmeɪʃənəl/| adj.|改革的；变革的；重组的|Relating to or resulting from reform or reformation.  It often implies a significant and often fundamental change in a system, institution, or practice. |例句：The reformational movement in the church led to significant changes in doctrine and practice. 教会改革运动导致了教义和实践的重大变化。

例句：The company underwent a reformational process to improve efficiency.  这家公司经历了改革过程以提高效率。


近义词：reformatory, transformative, revolutionary|反义词：conservative, traditional, stagnant|词性：形容词
