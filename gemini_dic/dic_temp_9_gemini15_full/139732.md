The term often describes the sheath-like base of a stem or leaf. |例句：The plant had ochreated stipules at the base of its leaves. 这植物的叶基部有靴状托叶。 The ochreated basal leaves of the plant were a distinguishing feature. 这植物靴状的基生叶是一个显著的特征。|近义词：boot-shaped, sheathed, ensheathed|反义词：uncovered, exposed
