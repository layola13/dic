refederate:/ˌriːˈfɛdəreɪt/| v.|重新联合，重新结盟|To unite or form a federation again; to reunite.  This typically refers to states, groups, or organizations that were previously united and have since separated. |例句：The warring factions finally agreed to refederate and work towards a common goal.  = 交战各派最终同意重新联合并为共同目标而努力。

The southern states attempted to refederate after the Civil War. = 南方各州在内战后试图重新联合。


近义词：reunite, consolidate, unify, federate (again)|反义词：separate, divide, disunite|动词
