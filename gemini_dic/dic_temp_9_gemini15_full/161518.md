potgun:/ˈpɑːtɡʌn/| n. |小火枪；儿童玩具枪| A small, often homemade, gun typically used for firing small projectiles such as pebbles or small shot.  It is often a toy and not intended for serious use. |例句：The boys made a potgun out of a pipe and some other scrap metal. = 男孩们用一根管子和一些废金属做了一支小火枪。


例句：He fired a small pebble from his homemade potgun. = 他用自制的小火枪射出一颗小鹅卵石。


近义词：toy gun, peashooter


反义词：high-powered rifle, cannon


词性:名词
