chalking:/ˈtʃɔːkɪŋ/ | v. | 用粉笔写；用粉笔画；| To mark or draw with chalk.  |  例句：He was chalking the score on the blackboard. 他正在黑板上用粉笔记分。| 例句：The children were chalking pictures on the pavement.孩子们正在人行道上用粉笔画画。|近义词：marking, drawing, scribbling | 反义词：erasing, rubbing out


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chalks the board every morning. = 他每天早上都用粉笔擦黑板。
She chalks her name on the wall. = 她把她的名字写在墙上。

一般过去时:
They chalked the message on the sidewalk. = 他们在人行道上用粉笔写了信息。
He chalked a large circle on the ground. = 他在地面上画了一个大圆圈。

现在完成时:
She has chalked all the numbers on the wall. = 她已经把所有的数字都写在墙上了。
The children have chalked a beautiful picture. = 孩子们画了一幅美丽的图画。

过去完成时:
They had chalked the directions before the game started. = 比赛开始前，他们已经用粉笔写好了路线。
She had chalked the outline before painting the mural. = 在绘制壁画之前，她已经用粉笔勾勒出了轮廓。

将来时:
He will chalk the winning team's name on the board. = 他将在黑板上写下获胜队的名字。
They will chalk a welcome message on the street. = 他们将在街上用粉笔写一条欢迎信息。

虚拟语气:
If I had chalk, I would chalk a picture. = 如果我有粉笔，我会画一幅画。
I wish I had chalked the date on the calendar. = 我真希望我把日期写在日历上了。

被动语态:
The board was chalked with the daily schedule. = 黑板上写着每日日程安排。
The pavement was chalked with colourful drawings. = 人行道上画满了五彩缤纷的图画。

疑问句:
Did he chalk the answer on the board? = 他把答案写在黑板上吗？
Will they chalk the new rules on the wall? = 他们会在墙上用粉笔写下新规则吗？

否定句:
He didn't chalk the correct answer. = 他没有写下正确的答案。
They didn't chalk anything on the blackboard. = 他们没有在黑板上写任何东西。
