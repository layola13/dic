gman:/ɡmæn/| n.|政府特工，秘密警察|A gman is a slang term for a government agent, often associated with the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI).  The term evokes a sense of mystery and power, often implying a clandestine operation.|Example: The gman slipped into the crowd unnoticed.  政府特工悄悄地混入人群中。  The gmen were investigating the organized crime ring. 秘密警察正在调查这个有组织犯罪集团。|近义词：federal agent, secret agent, undercover agent, operative|反义词：civilian, informant
