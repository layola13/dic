refugee:/ˌrefjuˈdʒiː/| n. |难民，避难者 | A person who has been forced to leave their country in order to escape war, persecution, or natural disaster.  |  Millions of refugees fled the war-torn country. 数百万难民逃离了饱受战争摧残的国家。  The refugee camp was overcrowded and lacked basic necessities. 难民营人满为患，缺乏基本生活必需品。| immigrant, asylum seeker | citizen, native


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Refugees seek safety in neighboring countries. 难民在邻国寻求安全。
He often helps refugees find shelter. 他经常帮助难民寻找住所。


一般过去时:
Many refugees escaped the violence last year. 许多难民去年逃离了暴力。
The government provided aid to refugees who arrived last month. 政府向上个月抵达的难民提供了援助。


现在完成时:
The organization has helped thousands of refugees this year. 该组织今年已帮助数千名难民。
She has been a refugee for five years. 她已经做了五年难民了。


过去完成时:
Before the war broke out, they had never thought they would become refugees. 在战争爆发之前，他们从未想过自己会成为难民。
The refugees had already lost everything before they reached the border. 难民们到达边境之前就已经失去了一切。


将来时:
More refugees will arrive in the coming weeks. 未来几周将会有更多难民到来。
The international community will provide support to refugees. 国际社会将向难民提供支持。


虚拟语气:
If I were a refugee, I would understand their struggles. 如果我是难民，我会理解他们的挣扎。
I wish the world could offer more help to refugees. 我希望世界能向难民提供更多帮助。


被动语态:
Refugees are often denied basic human rights. 难民经常被剥夺基本人权。
Aid is being distributed to refugees in need. 正在向需要帮助的难民分发援助物资。


疑问句:
Are there enough resources for all the refugees? 所有难民都有足够的资源吗？
How many refugees have crossed the border this year? 今年有多少难民越过边境？


否定句:
The government did not provide enough support for the refugees. 政府没有为难民提供足够的支援。
They were not allowed to enter the country as refugees. 他们不被允许以难民身份进入该国。
