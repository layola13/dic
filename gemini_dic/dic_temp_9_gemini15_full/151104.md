parasital:/ˌpærəˈsaɪtəl/| adj.|寄生的；寄生虫的| Relating to or characteristic of a parasite.  A parasitic relationship is one in which one organism benefits at the expense of another.  The term can also describe something that lives off or exploits something else.|例句：The plant displayed parasital characteristics, drawing nutrients from its host.  这植物展现出寄生的特性，从寄主身上汲取养分。


例句：Many parasital diseases can be prevented with proper hygiene. 许多寄生虫病可以通过适当的卫生预防。


近义词：parasitic, dependent


反义词：self-sufficient, independent


词性：形容词
