slippered:/ˈslɪpərd/| adj.|穿着拖鞋的；拖鞋似的|Wearing slippers; resembling a slipper.  The adjective "slippered" describes something or someone wearing slippers or having a slipper-like quality. It often implies a relaxed, informal, or comfortable state.|例句：The slippered feet rested comfortably by the fire.  穿着拖鞋的双脚舒服地靠在火炉旁。

He sat in a slippered chair by the fire. 他坐在火炉旁一张软椅上，穿着拖鞋。|近义词：sandalled, shod (in slippers)|反义词：shoeless, barefooted
