edelweisses:/ˈeːdəlˌvaɪsɪz/| n.|高山雪绒花（复数）|The plural of edelweiss, referring to multiple plants. Edelweiss are small, white, woolly-leaved plants found high in the mountains.|例句：The hiker admired the edelweisses clinging to the rocky cliff.  登山者欣赏着依附在悬崖峭壁上的高山雪绒花。
例句： We saw fields of edelweisses during our alpine trek.  我们在阿尔卑斯山徒步旅行时看到了成片的高山雪绒花。|近义词：mountain flower, alpine flower|反义词：无
