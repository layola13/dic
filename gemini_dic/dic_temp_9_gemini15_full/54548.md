dislink:/dɪsˈlɪŋk/| vt.|取消连接，断开连接|To separate or disconnect something from something else.  The term is often used in the context of computer networks or other systems.|例句：The technician dislinked the server from the network. 技术人员将服务器从网络断开连接。|例句：The two companies dislinked their marketing campaigns after a disagreement. 两家公司在发生分歧后取消了彼此的营销活动。|近义词：disconnect, detach, sever, unlink|反义词：link, connect, join
