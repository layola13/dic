preintegrated:/ˌpriːˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先集成的，预先整合的 | Already integrated beforehand; combined or unified in advance.  This term typically describes systems or components that have been combined or integrated before deployment or use, often to streamline the process or improve efficiency. | Example: The preintegrated software package saved us considerable time and effort during setup.  预先集成的软件包节省了我们在安装过程中的大量时间和精力。 |近义词：integrated, combined, unified | 反义词：separate, unintegrated
