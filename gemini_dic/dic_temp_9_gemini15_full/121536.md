mismarked:/ˌmɪsˈmɑːrkt/| adj. | 标记错误的；错标的 | Incorrectly marked or labeled.  The product was mismarked, leading to customer confusion. |近义词：mislabeled, wrongly marked, incorrectly tagged | 反义词：correctly marked, properly labeled


USAGE->

The price tag is mismarked. = 价格标签标错了。
The map is mismarked in several places. = 地图好几个地方标错了。
