eudemonism:/juːˈdiːməˌnɪzəm/| n. |幸福论；快乐主义|The philosophical belief that happiness or well-being is the highest good and the ultimate aim of human life.  It emphasizes living a virtuous life to achieve eudaimonia, often translated as "flourishing" or "living well".| Example:  Eudemonism, unlike hedonism, focuses on the pursuit of a meaningful and fulfilling life rather than simply pleasure.| 近义词：hedonism (though with important distinctions), virtue ethics| 反义词：nihilism, pessimism
