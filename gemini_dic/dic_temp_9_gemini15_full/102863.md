jaundices:/ˈdʒɔːndɪsɪz/| n.|黄疸病；偏见；嫉妒|A condition characterized by yellowing of the skin and whites of the eyes, caused by an accumulation of bilirubin in the blood.|例句：The baby developed jaundice shortly after birth. 婴儿出生后不久就患上了黄疸病。

例句：His jaundiced view of politics prevented him from seeing the good in any candidate. 他对政治的偏见使他看不到任何候选人的优点。|近义词：yellowing, icterus, prejudice, envy, bitterness|反义词：health, objectivity, impartiality
