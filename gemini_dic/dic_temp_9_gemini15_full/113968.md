maggot:/ˈmæɡət/| n. |蛆，蛴螬 |A maggot is the larva of a fly, typically having a soft, elongated body without legs.  Maggots are often found in decaying organic matter. |例句：The rotting meat was teeming with maggots.腐烂的肉里挤满了蛆。|例句：The wound was infested with maggots.伤口感染了蛆虫。|近义词：grub, larva, worm |反义词:  None specifically, but  "adult fly" could be considered an antonym in terms of life cycle.
