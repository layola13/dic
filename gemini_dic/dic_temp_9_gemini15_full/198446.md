shrewishly:/ˈʃruːɪʃli/| adv.|泼辣地；尖刻地；刻薄地|In a shrewish manner; tartly; naggingly.  It describes behavior characterized by ill-temper, nagging, and a sharp tongue.|She spoke shrewishly to her husband about his lateness. 她尖刻地对丈夫说他迟到了。|He scolded her shrewishly for spilling the milk. 他尖刻地责备她洒了牛奶。|bitterly, tartly, snappishly|kindly, sweetly, gently|副词
