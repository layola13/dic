participatory: /pɑːrˈtɪsɪpətɔːri/ | adj. | 参与的；参与式的；共享的 | Relating to or characterized by participation; involving the participation of many people.  | 例句：The participatory budgeting process allowed citizens to directly influence how public funds were spent.  参与式预算流程允许公民直接影响公共资金的使用方式。

例句：The school encourages a participatory learning environment where students actively engage in discussions and projects. 学校鼓励学生积极参与讨论和项目，营造参与式学习环境。


近义词：inclusive, collaborative, interactive, shared


反义词：exclusive, authoritarian, dictatorial, passive


词性：形容词
