mugger:/ˈmʌɡər/| n.|抢劫犯，行凶抢劫者|A mugger is a person who attacks and robs another person, typically in a public place.|例句：The mugger threatened the victim with a knife.  这个抢劫犯用刀威胁受害者。|例句：Police are searching for the mugger who stole the woman's purse.  警方正在搜捕抢劫该女子钱包的抢劫犯。|近义词：robber, bandit, thief |反义词：victim
