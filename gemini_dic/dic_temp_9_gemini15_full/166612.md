presence:/ˈprɛzəns/| n. |存在；出席；影响力；仪态|The physical or perceptible existence of something or someone.  It can also refer to the effect or influence of someone or something, or the manner of bearing oneself.|例句：Her presence filled the room.  她的出现使整个房间都充满了活力。|例句：The presence of harmful bacteria was confirmed.  证实存在有害细菌。|近义词：existence, attendance, influence, comportment|反义词：absence, lack
