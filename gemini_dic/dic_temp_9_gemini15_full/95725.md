inconsequentially:/ˌɪnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəli/ | adv. | 无足轻重地；无关紧要地 |  In a way that is unimportant or has no significant effect.  It suggests a lack of serious consequence or importance. | Example: He inconsequentially mentioned the incident, as if it were of no real significance. 他不经意地提到了这件事，好像它并不重要。  | 近义词：unimportantly, trivially, insignificantly | 反义词：importantly, significantly, consequentially
