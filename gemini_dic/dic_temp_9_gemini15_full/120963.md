mischaracterizes:/ˌmɪskærəktəraɪzɪz/| vt.|歪曲，误解，曲解|To represent (someone or something) in a way that is inaccurate or unfair.|The media mischaracterized the politician's statement, leading to public outrage.媒体歪曲了这位政治家的声明，导致公众愤怒。|近义词：distort, misrepresent, falsify|反义词：accurately represent, clarify

The article mischaracterizes the findings of the study. 这篇文章曲解了研究的结果。
