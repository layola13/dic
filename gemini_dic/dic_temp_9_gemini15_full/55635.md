ditchwater:/ˈdɪtʃˌwɔːtər/| n. |污水，沟渠水|Dirty or stagnant water found in a ditch.  The water is typically polluted and unpleasant.|Example: The ditchwater was thick with algae and smelled foul.  沟渠里的水长满了藻类，闻起来很臭。 The farmer used ditchwater to irrigate his parched fields, though it was not ideal. 农民用沟渠水灌溉他干旱的田地，虽然这并不理想。|近义词：sludge, sewage, foul water|反义词：pure water, clean water
