repelled:/rɪˈpɛld/ | v. |击退；驱逐；使厌恶 |To drive or force back; to cause aversion or disgust in. |例句：The army repelled the enemy attack.  军队击退了敌人的进攻。
The smell repelled me.  那气味使我作呕。|近义词：drive back, repulse, disgust |反义词：attract, allure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong magnet repels the other magnet. = 强磁铁排斥另一块磁铁。
This fabric repels water. = 这种布料防水。

一般过去时:
The soldiers repelled the invaders. = 士兵们击退了侵略者。
His rudeness repelled her. = 他的粗鲁使她反感。

现在完成时:
The new coating has repelled all attempts at penetration. = 新涂层抵御了所有穿透尝试。
The evidence has repelled all doubts. = 证据消除了所有疑虑。

过去完成时:
They had repelled several attacks before reinforcements arrived. = 在增援部队到达之前，他们已经击退了几次袭击。
She had repelled all his advances before he finally gave up. = 在他最终放弃之前，她已经拒绝了他所有的追求。

将来时:
We will repel any attack on our country. = 我们将击退对我们国家的任何攻击。
I believe this new material will repel stains effectively. = 我相信这种新材料将有效地防污。

虚拟语气:
If we had better weapons, we could have repelled the invasion more easily. = 如果我们有更好的武器，我们就能更容易地击退入侵。
If the smell didn't repel me so much, I might have tried the food.  如果气味没有那么让我反感，我或许会尝试一下食物。

被动语态:
The attack was repelled with heavy losses. = 这次袭击在付出巨大损失后被击退了。
His advances were repelled by her coldness. = 他的追求被她的冷漠所拒绝。

疑问句:
Was the enemy repelled? = 敌人被击退了吗？
Will this treatment repel insects? = 这种处理方法能驱虫吗？

否定句:
The shield did not repel the blow. = 护盾没有挡住打击。
His charm did not repel her. = 他的魅力并没有让她反感。


PHRASE->
repel an attack = 击退攻击
repel an invasion = 击退入侵
repel water = 防水
repel insects = 驱虫
repel advances = 拒绝追求


词源:
repelled 源自于古法语 repeler，意为“驱逐，击退”，进一步源于拉丁语 repellere，由 re- (“back”) 和 pellere (“to drive, push”) 组成。  因此，其核心含义是“往回推，驱逐”。
