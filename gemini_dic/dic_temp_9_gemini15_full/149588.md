paijama:/pəˈdʒɑːmə/ | n. |睡衣 |Pajamas are a type of loose-fitting clothing worn for sleeping. They typically consist of a top and bottom, often made of soft and comfortable materials like cotton or silk.  |She put on her pajamas and went to bed. 她穿上睡衣睡觉了。|nightclothes,sleepwear |  |n.
