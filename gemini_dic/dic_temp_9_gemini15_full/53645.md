discession:/dɪˈskʌʃən/| n. |讨论，议论|A discussion is a conversation where people talk about something and express different opinions.  It involves exchanging ideas and information to reach a conclusion or understanding. |例句：The team had a lengthy discession about the project's budget. 团队就项目的预算进行了长时间的讨论。|例句：After a lively discession, they finally reached an agreement.  经过热烈的讨论，他们最终达成了一致。|近义词：discussion, debate, deliberation, conference|反义词：silence, agreement (in the sense of lack of discussion)|名词
