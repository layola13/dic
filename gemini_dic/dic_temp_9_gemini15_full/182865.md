repentantly:/rɪˈpɛntəntli/| adv.|懊悔地；悔恨地|Showing or feeling regret for having done wrong.  It describes the manner in which someone acts or speaks after feeling remorse for their actions.|He spoke repentantly about his past mistakes. 他懊悔地谈论着他过去的错误。|She looked repentantly at her mother. 她懊悔地望着她的母亲。|contritely, ruefully, regretfully|unrepentantly, defiantly
