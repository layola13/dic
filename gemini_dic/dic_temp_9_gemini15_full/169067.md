programmer:/ˈproʊɡræmər/|/ˈprəʊɡræmər/| n. |程序员，编程人员 |A programmer is a person who writes computer programs.  Programmers use various programming languages to create software applications, websites, and other digital systems. They design, write, test, and debug code to solve problems and create functional programs. |Example:  She is a skilled programmer who specializes in artificial intelligence. 她是一位擅长人工智能的熟练程序员。

He's a talented programmer who built this amazing game. 他是一位有才华的程序员，他创造了这款令人惊叹的游戏。|近义词：coder, developer, software engineer |反义词：user, end-user
