/ɛs/ | pron. | 这；那个；那些 |  This word is a demonstrative pronoun, referring to something previously mentioned or easily understood from the context. It can refer to singular or plural things, depending on the context. |  This is my car.  这辆车是我的。

That is a beautiful painting.  那是一幅美丽的画。

Those are my books.  那些是我的书。


|近义词: that, those |反义词: this (in some contexts) | pron.
