multiunit:/ˌmʌltiˈjuːnɪt/ | adj. |多单元的，多单位的 | Having or consisting of many units or parts.  This term is frequently used to describe buildings, businesses, or systems composed of multiple individual components.  | 例句：The apartment complex is a large multiunit building.  这个公寓楼是一栋大型的多单元建筑。

The company operates a multiunit retail chain across the country. 这家公司在全国经营着多家零售连锁店。


近义词：multiple-unit, multi-part, composite


反义词：single-unit, unitary


词性：形容词
