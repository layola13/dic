checkmate:/ˈtʃɛkmeɪt/| n., vt.|将死，将军；挫败|The act of placing a player's king under an unavoidable threat of capture;  to defeat decisively.|例句：The grandmaster delivered a checkmate in just 15 moves.  国际象棋大师只用了15步就将死了对手。
The sudden corporate takeover checkmated the smaller company's plans.  突然的企业收购彻底挫败了这家小公司的计划。|近义词： defeat, overcome, vanquish |反义词：victory, triumph
