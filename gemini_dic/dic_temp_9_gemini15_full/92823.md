iconolater:/aɪˈkɒnəleɪtər/ | n. |偶像崇拜者|A person who venerates or worships icons, especially religious icons.  Iconolatry is the veneration of icons, especially religious images.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting excessive or blind devotion to images or symbols, rather than genuine religious faith.|The iconolater knelt before the image of the saint, lost in prayer.  这位偶像崇拜者跪在圣像前祈祷。|idolater, worshipper|iconoclast|名词
