greyed:/ɡreɪd/ | adj. | 灰色的，变灰的，暗淡的 |  Describes something that has become gray or has a grayish appearance.  This can refer to a physical change in color, or a metaphorical dimming or dulling. | 例句：The old photograph had greyed with age. 这张旧照片随着时间的推移已经变灰了。  The once vibrant colors of the painting had greyed over time.  这幅画曾经鲜艳的色彩随着时间的推移已经暗淡了。 | 近义词：gray, faded, dull, muted | 反义词：bright, vivid, colorful


USAGE->

The sky is greyed over. = 天空阴沉沉的。

一般过去时:
