down-loading: /ˈdaʊnˌloʊdɪŋ/ | v. | 下载 | The process of receiving data from a remote system, typically from the internet, to a local computer or device.  | Example: I'm downloading a new game. 我正在下载一个新的游戏。  |近义词：transferring, receiving | 反义词：uploading


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am downloading a movie. = 我正在下载一部电影。
We download music every week. = 我们每周都下载音乐。

一般过去时:
I downloaded the file yesterday. = 我昨天下载了这个文件。
She downloaded all the photos. = 她下载了所有的照片。

现在完成时:
I have downloaded the latest software update. = 我已经下载了最新的软件更新。
They have downloaded several videos. = 他们已经下载了好几个视频。

过去完成时:
I had downloaded the document before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经下载了文档。
She had downloaded the app before leaving home. = 她离开家之前已经下载了这个应用。

将来时:
I will download the album later. = 我稍后会下载这张专辑。
He will download the presentation tomorrow. = 他明天会下载演示文稿。


被动语态:
The file is being downloaded. = 文件正在下载中。
The software was downloaded automatically. = 软件自动下载了。

疑问句:
Are you downloading anything right now? = 你现在正在下载什么吗？
Did he download the program successfully? = 他成功下载程序了吗？

否定句:
I am not downloading anything at the moment. = 我现在什么也没有下载。
He didn't download the movie because of the slow internet. = 由于网速慢，他没有下载电影。
