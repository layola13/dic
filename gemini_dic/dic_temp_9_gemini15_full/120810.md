misassign:/ˌmɪs.əˈsaɪn/| vt.|错误分配，误派|To assign incorrectly or to the wrong person or place.|例句：The company misassigned the new employee to the wrong department. 公司错误地将新员工分配到了错误的部门。

例句：They misassigned the tasks, leading to delays in the project. 他们错误地分配了任务，导致项目延误。


近义词：misallocate, misdirect, wrongly assign|反义词：assign correctly, properly allocate
