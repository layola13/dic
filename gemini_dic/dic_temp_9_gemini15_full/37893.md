complaints:/kəmˈpleɪnts/ | n. |抱怨，投诉 |  Expressions of dissatisfaction or protest; grievances.  |例句：The manager received numerous complaints about the poor service.  经理收到了许多关于服务差的投诉。

例句：  He lodged a formal complaint against the company. 他对该公司正式提出了投诉。


近义词：grievances, protests, objections, criticisms | 反义词：praise, compliments, approval


词性：名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They have many complaints about the new system. = 他们对新系统有很多抱怨。
He often makes complaints about his job. = 他经常抱怨他的工作。


一般过去时:
She made several complaints last week. = 她上周提出了几项投诉。
They filed complaints about the noise. = 他们投诉噪音。


现在完成时:
We have received numerous complaints this month. = 这个月我们收到了大量的投诉。
She has voiced her complaints repeatedly. = 她一再表达了她的不满。


过去完成时:
He had made several complaints before they finally took action. = 在他们最终采取行动之前，他已经提出了几次投诉。
They had filed complaints with the authorities. = 他们已经向当局投诉了。


将来时:
They will likely file complaints if the problem isn't solved. = 如果问题得不到解决，他们可能会提出投诉。
He will lodge a formal complaint tomorrow. = 他明天将提出正式投诉。


虚拟语气:
If I had fewer complaints, I would be happier. = 如果我的抱怨少一些，我会更快乐。
I wish I didn't have so many complaints. = 我希望我没有这么多抱怨。


被动语态:
Many complaints were received by the company. = 公司收到了许多投诉。
The complaints are being investigated thoroughly. = 投诉正在被彻底调查。


疑问句:
Do you have any complaints about the food? = 你对食物有什么不满吗？
What are the main complaints about this product? = 对这款产品的主要投诉是什么？


否定句:
He had no complaints about the hotel. = 他对酒店没有任何抱怨。
They didn't make any complaints. = 他们没有提出任何投诉。


PHRASE->
handle complaints = 处理投诉
address complaints =  解决投诉
investigate complaints =  调查投诉
receive complaints = 收到投诉
resolve complaints =  化解投诉
