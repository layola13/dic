disfigures:/dɪsˈfɪɡər/| vt.|毁容；损毁；使变形 | to spoil the appearance of something or someone, especially the face, by damaging it | The accident disfigured his face.  事故毁了他的容貌。|The storm disfigured the landscape. 暴风雨破坏了景观。|近义词：deface, mar, deform, spoil | 反义词：beautify, enhance, improve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scar disfigures her face. = 伤疤毁了她容貌。
He constantly disfigures his arguments with irrelevant details. = 他总是用不相干的细节来歪曲他的论点。


一般过去时:
The fire disfigured the old building. = 火灾毁坏了那座旧建筑。
The vandals disfigured the statue with graffiti. = 那些破坏分子在雕像上涂鸦，使其面目全非。


现在完成时:
Years of sun exposure have disfigured her skin. = 多年的日晒已使她的皮肤受损。
The accident has disfigured his career prospects. = 这场事故毁了他的职业前景。


过去完成时:
The painting had been disfigured by a careless restorer. = 这幅画已被一个粗心的修复者毁坏。

将来时:
The planned development will disfigure the natural beauty of the coastline. = 计划中的开发项目将破坏海岸线的自然美景。
If we don't act soon, pollution will disfigure this pristine environment. = 如果我们不尽快采取行动，污染将破坏这片原始环境。


虚拟语气:

I wish the war hadn't disfigured so many lives. = 我真希望战争没有毁掉那么多人的生活。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
