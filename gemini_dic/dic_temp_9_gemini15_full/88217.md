hinderer:/ˈhɪndərər/| n. |阻碍者，妨碍者|A hinderer is a person or thing that prevents progress or success.|The constant rain was a hinderer to our hiking plans.  持续的雨水阻碍了我们的远足计划。|obstructor, impediment, obstacle|helper, facilitator|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The noise hinders my work. = 噪音妨碍我的工作。
That obstacle hinders progress. = 那个障碍阻碍了进展。


一般过去时:
The storm hindered our travel plans. = 暴风雨阻碍了我们的旅行计划。
His laziness hindered his success. = 他的懒惰阻碍了他的成功。


现在完成时:
The bad weather has hindered the harvest. = 恶劣的天气已经阻碍了收成。
Numerous problems have hindered the project's completion. = 许多问题阻碍了项目的完成。


过去完成时:
Technical difficulties had hindered the launch of the satellite. = 技术故障已经阻碍了卫星的发射。
A lack of funding had hindered the research. = 资金不足已经阻碍了研究。


将来时:
The strike will hinder production. = 罢工将阻碍生产。
Further delays will hinder the project's timeline. = 进一步的延误将阻碍项目的进度。


虚拟语气:
If the rain hadn't hindered us, we would have reached the summit. = 如果雨没有阻碍我们，我们本可以到达山顶。
I wish nothing would hinder my progress. = 我希望没有什么会阻碍我的进步。


被动语态:
His ambition was hindered by his lack of confidence. = 他的雄心壮志由于缺乏自信而受阻。
The project was hindered by unforeseen circumstances. = 该项目因不可预见的情况而受阻。


疑问句:
What hindered your progress? = 什么阻碍了你的进步？
What factors hindered the development? = 哪些因素阻碍了发展？


否定句:
Nothing hindered his determination. = 没有什么能阻碍他的决心。
The plan was not hindered by the unexpected rain. = 计划并没有因为意外的降雨而受阻。
