oscule:/ˈɒskjuːl/|/ˈɑːskjuːl/| n. |水孔；出水孔 |A small opening, pore, or aperture, especially one in the body wall of a sponge through which water is expelled.  |Example: Water flows out of the sponge through its oscules. 水通过海绵的水孔流出。|近义词：pore, aperture, opening |反义词：incurrent pore (进水孔) |名词
