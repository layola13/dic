correlative:/kɔːˈrelətɪv/| adj.|关联的；相互依存的；相关的|  Of or relating to correlation; mutually related.  Correlative words or phrases work together to connect words or groups of words in a sentence.  They show a relationship between the items being compared or contrasted. |例句：The correlative conjunctions "either...or" and "neither...nor" are used to present choices. 关联连词“either...or”和“neither...nor”用于表达选择。

例句：His success is correlative with his hard work.  他的成功与其努力工作相关。


近义词：related, connected, interdependent, reciprocal |反义词：unrelated, independent, unconnected |词性：adjective
