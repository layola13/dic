helicoid:/ˈhɛlɪkɔɪd/| n. adj.|螺旋形的，螺旋状的|A helicoid is a three-dimensional surface generated by a line moving along a helix while remaining parallel to the axis of the helix.  It resembles a screw or a spiral staircase. As an adjective, it describes something having a helical shape.|The DNA molecule has a helicoid structure.  DNA分子具有螺旋结构。|The staircase is helicoid in design. 这座楼梯设计成螺旋形的。|spiral, helical, screw-shaped|straight, planar|名词，形容词
