fluidity:/fluːˈɪdəti/| n. |流畅性；流动性；易变性 |The ability to flow easily; the state of being fluid.  It can also refer to the ease with which something can be changed or adapted. |例句：The fluidity of the dancer's movements was breathtaking. 舞者的动作流畅得令人叹为观止。

例句：The fluidity of the market makes it difficult to predict prices.市场的波动性使得价格难以预测。

近义词：smoothness, flow, flexibility, adaptability |反义词：rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility, viscosity
