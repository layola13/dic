frappes:/ˈfræpeɪz/| n.|冰沙；奶昔|A frappe is a blended iced beverage, typically made with ice cream, milk or cream, and flavorings such as chocolate, coffee, or fruit.  It's usually thicker and colder than a milkshake.|例句：I ordered a chocolate frappe to cool down on a hot day.  今天天气很热，我点了一杯巧克力冰沙降温。

例句：The coffee shop offers a wide variety of frappes, from classic vanilla to exotic fruit flavors. 这家咖啡店提供各种各样的冰沙，从经典的香草味到异国情调的水果味。


近义词：milkshake, smoothie, iced beverage|反义词：hot beverage|词性:名词
