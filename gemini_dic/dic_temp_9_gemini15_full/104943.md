kelpfish: /ˈkɛlpfɪʃ/ | n. |海藻鱼| A small fish that lives among kelp forests.  Kelpfish are typically masters of camouflage, blending seamlessly with their surroundings. They are often found in shallow, rocky coastal waters. | The kelpfish darted between the swaying kelp fronds, almost invisible against the brown and green hues.  这海藻鱼在摇曳的海藻叶之间快速游动，几乎隐身于棕色和绿色的色调之中。|近义词：None directly equivalent, but similar habitat dwellers might include certain types of gobies or blennies. |反义词：None directly applicable.
