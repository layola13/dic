preredeems:/ˌpriːrɪˈdiːmz/| v.|预先赎回，提前赎回|To redeem (something) before the scheduled time or date.|The company offered to preredeem the bonds at a premium. 公司提出以溢价提前赎回债券。|近义词：redeem early, prepay|反义词：defer, postpone

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preredeems his bonds every year. = 他每年都提前赎回他的债券。
The company preredeems its debt frequently. = 这家公司经常提前偿还债务。


一般过去时:
They preredeemed their investment last month. = 他们上个月提前赎回了投资。
She preredeemed her mortgage early. = 她提前还清了抵押贷款。


现在完成时:
He has preredeemed all his shares. = 他已经提前赎回了所有股份。
The investors have preredeemed a significant portion of the bonds. = 投资者已经提前赎回了一大部分债券。


过去完成时:
They had preredeemed the bonds before the interest rate hike. = 他们在利率上涨之前已经提前赎回了债券。
She had preredeemed her loan before the due date. = 她已在到期日前提前还清了贷款。


将来时:
We will preredeem the loan next year. = 我们明年将提前偿还贷款。
They will preredeem their investment once the market improves. = 一旦市场好转，他们将提前赎回投资。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would preredeem my mortgage. = 如果我有钱，我会提前还清抵押贷款。
If they could, they would preredeem their bonds now. = 如果可以，他们现在就会提前赎回债券。


被动语态:
The bonds were preredeemed by the company. = 债券被公司提前赎回。
The loan was preredeemed last week. = 贷款在上周提前还清。


疑问句:
Can I preredeem my investment without penalty? = 我可以无罚款提前赎回我的投资吗？
Will the company preredeem the bonds soon? = 公司很快就会提前赎回债券吗？


否定句:
They did not preredeem their shares. = 他们没有提前赎回他们的股份。
The bank does not allow preredeeming loans before maturity. = 银行不允许在到期日前提前偿还贷款。
