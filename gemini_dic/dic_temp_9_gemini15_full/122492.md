moche:/ˈmɔːtʃeɪ/| adj. n.|莫奇卡的；莫奇卡文化（的）|Moche refers to a pre-Columbian culture that flourished in the northern coast of Peru from about AD 100 to AD 800.  Known for its sophisticated pottery, metalwork, and monumental architecture.|The Moche culture is famous for its intricate pottery depicting scenes of daily life and mythology.|近义词：pre-Columbian (as an adjective)|反义词：无|形容词，名词
