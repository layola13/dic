carburettors:/ˌkɑːrˈbjuːreɪtərz/ | n. |化油器(复数) | A carburetor is a device that mixes air and fuel in an internal combustion engine.  It meters the correct amount of fuel into the air stream and creates a combustible mixture for the engine. Carburetors are largely replaced by fuel injection systems in modern vehicles. |例句：The old car's carburettors needed cleaning. 这辆旧车的化油器需要清洗。


The mechanic adjusted the carburettors to improve the engine's performance. 技工调整了化油器以提高发动机的性能。|近义词：fuel system (广义)|反义词：fuel injection system|名词
