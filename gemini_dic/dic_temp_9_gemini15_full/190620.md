saturnalianly:/ˌsætərˈneɪliənli/| adv.|狂欢的；放荡的；纵情的|In a manner characteristic of the Saturnalia; wildly festive and unrestrained.  The adjective "Saturnalian" derives from the Roman festival of Saturnalia, a time of revelry and role reversal.|The party became saturnalianly wild, with guests dancing on tables and singing at the top of their lungs.|近义词：revelrously, riotously, debauchedly|反义词：soberly, restrainedly, demurely
