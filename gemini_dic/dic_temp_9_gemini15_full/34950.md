clocking:/ˈklɒkɪŋ/|/ˈklɑːkɪŋ/| v.|记录时间；计时；达到；(非正式)偷窃 |To record the time of something; to achieve a particular speed or time; (informal) to steal something. |例句：The police clocked his speed at 90 mph.  警察测得他的车速为每小时90英里。


例句：They clocked in at 8 am and clocked out at 5 pm.  他们早上8点上班，下午5点下班。


近义词：timing, registering, recording, stealing(informal) |反义词：N/A |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He clocks his daily runs. 他记录他每天的跑步时间。
The factory clocks the production output. 工厂记录生产产量。


一般过去时:
She clocked a new personal best. 她创造了新的个人最佳成绩。
The police clocked the suspect leaving the scene. 警察记录到嫌疑犯离开现场。


现在完成时:
They have clocked over 1000 miles this week. 他们本周行驶里程超过1000英里。
We have clocked countless hours on this project. 我们在这个项目上投入了无数的时间。


过去完成时:
They had clocked in before the manager arrived. 他们在经理到达之前就已经打卡了。
She had clocked a personal best before the competition. 她在比赛前就创造了个人最佳成绩。


将来时:
He will clock a faster time next race. 他将在下一场比赛中跑出更快的成绩。
We will clock out early today. 我们今天要早点下班。


虚拟语气:
If he clocked in earlier, he would have avoided the traffic. 如果他早点打卡，就能避免交通堵塞。
If she had clocked a better time, she would have won the race. 如果她跑出更好的成绩，她就能赢得比赛。


被动语态:
His speed was clocked at 100mph. 他的速度被测得是每小时100英里。
The time was clocked accurately. 时间被精确地记录下来。


疑问句:
Did they clock in on time? 他们按时打卡了吗？
How much did you clock this month? 这个月你工作了多少小时？


否定句:
He didn't clock out until 6 pm. 直到下午6点他才下班。
The machine didn't clock the error. 机器没有记录到错误。
