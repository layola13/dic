reidentification:/ˌriːˌaɪdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |再识别，重新识别|The act of identifying something or someone again, especially after a period of time or a change in appearance or circumstances.  It often involves comparing new data with previously collected data to confirm an identity.  This is commonly used in fields like security, surveillance, and forensic science.|例句：The police used facial recognition software for reidentification of the suspect after he changed his appearance.  警方在他改变容貌后使用面部识别软件重新识别嫌疑人。

例句：The reidentification of the missing child was a relief to her family.  失踪儿童的重新识别让她的家人松了一口气。


近义词：re-identification, reinspection, confirmation, verification|反义词：misidentification, anonymity
