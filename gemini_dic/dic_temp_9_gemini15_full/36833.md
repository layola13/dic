colonist:/ˈkɑːlənɪst/| n. |殖民地居民，移民 |A colonist is a person who has settled in a colony, typically one established by their own country.  They are often involved in the development and governance of the new territory. |Example: The American colonists fought for independence from Great Britain.  美国殖民者为争取脱离英国独立而战。|近义词：settler, immigrant, pioneer |反义词：native, indigenous
