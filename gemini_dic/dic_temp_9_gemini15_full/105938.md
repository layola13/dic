klick:/klɪk/| n. v.|咔哒声；点击；快速移动|A short, sharp sound, like that made by something clicking into place;  an act of clicking a computer mouse or similar device; a quick movement.|例句：I heard a klick as the lock engaged. 我听到锁扣上的咔哒声。  例句：She gave the mouse a quick klick to open the file. 她快速点击鼠标打开了文件。|近义词：click, snap, tap|反义词：boom, thud


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The lock clicks shut. = 锁咔哒一声关上了。
He clicks the mouse repeatedly. = 他反复点击鼠标。


一般过去时:
The door clicked shut behind me. = 门在我身后咔哒一声关上了。
She clicked the link to open the website. = 她点击链接打开了网站。


现在完成时:
I have clicked the button several times, but nothing happened. = 我已经点击了按钮好几次，但什么也没发生。
He has clicked on every link on the page. = 他已经点击了页面上的每一个链接。


过去完成时:
The system had clicked off before I could save the file. = 系统在我保存文件之前已经断开了。
She had clicked the "submit" button before realizing her mistake. = 她意识到错误之前已经点击了“提交”按钮。


将来时:
I will click the button when I'm ready. = 我准备好了就会点击按钮。
He will click on the advertisement to learn more. = 他会点击广告了解更多信息。


虚拟语气:
If I clicked this button, what would happen? = 如果我点击这个按钮，会发生什么？
I wish I hadn't clicked that link. = 我真希望我没有点击那个链接。


被动语态:
The button is clicked frequently. = 这个按钮经常被点击。
The link was clicked thousands of times. = 这个链接被点击了数千次。


疑问句:
Did you click the link? = 你点击了链接了吗？
Will you click the button for me? = 你能帮我点击一下按钮吗？


否定句:
The button didn't click. = 按钮没有发出咔哒声。
I didn't click on the advertisement. = 我没有点击那个广告。


PHRASE->
klick-klack = 咔哒咔哒声
single klick = 单击
double klick = 双击
klick the link = 点击链接
klick on = 点击
