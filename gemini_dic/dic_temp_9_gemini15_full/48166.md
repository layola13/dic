decompartmentalizations:/ˌdiːkəmˈpɑːrtmɛntəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |去部门化；消除部门界限；打破部门壁垒 | The process of breaking down or eliminating departmental barriers and promoting collaboration and integration between different departments or areas of an organization.  It aims to improve efficiency, communication, and innovation. | Example: The company's recent decompartmentalizations have led to significant improvements in project management. 公司最近的去部门化措施导致项目管理有了显著改进。|近义词：integration, collaboration, interdepartmental cooperation |反义词：compartmentalization, segregation, isolation |名词
