USAGE->

This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs do.  Its usage is descriptive and modifies a verb or adjective.  Examples illustrate its function:


The artist applied the paint semitexturally. = 艺术家半纹理地涂抹颜料。
The surface felt semitexturally smooth. = 表面感觉半纹理地光滑。


PHRASE->

Because "semitexturally" is a relatively uncommon adverb,  it doesn't have common set phrases associated with it.  Its usage is highly dependent on the context of its application within a sentence.
