piddled:/ˈpɪdld/| v.| 琐碎地做；少量地做；胡乱做；撒尿 | To work or do something in a slow, ineffective way; to do something in small amounts; to urinate (informal). |例句：He piddled around in the garden all afternoon. 他整个下午都在花园里懒洋洋地干活。  He piddled away his inheritance. 他挥霍了他的遗产。|近义词：trifle, potter, mess about,  (for urination) pee, wee|反义词：work diligently, accomplish,  (for urination) N/A
