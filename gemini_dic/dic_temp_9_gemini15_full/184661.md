restrings:/riːˈstrɪŋz/| v.|重新穿弦；更换琴弦|To restring something means to replace its strings, typically referring to musical instruments.|例句：I need to restring my guitar; the old strings are broken. 我需要重新为我的吉他换弦；旧弦断了。 He restrung his tennis racquet after a match. 他在一场比赛后重新穿了他网球拍的弦。|近义词：replace strings, re-string|反义词：leave unstrung
