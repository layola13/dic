casked:/kæskt/| v. |把…装入桶中；把…放入酒桶中|To put something into a cask or barrel, typically liquid.|The wine was casked last autumn.  这酒是去年秋天装入酒桶的。|They casked the beer before shipping it. 他们在运输啤酒之前将其装入桶中。|近义词：barrel, keg, bottle|反义词：uncase, unpack


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He casks the wine every year. 他每年都把酒装进酒桶。
They cask the beer before shipment. 他们装桶啤酒后再进行运输。


一般过去时:
The brewer casked the ale last week. 酿酒师上周把麦芽酒装桶了。
We casked the applesauce for the winter. 我们把苹果酱装桶以备过冬。


现在完成时:
He has casked all the wine for this year's harvest. 他已经把今年收成的所有酒都装桶了。
The winery has casked the new vintage.  酒庄已经把新酿的葡萄酒装桶了。


过去完成时:
They had casked the cider before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把苹果酒装桶了。
The farmer had casked his honey before the market opened.  农民在市场开张前已将蜂蜜装桶。


将来时:
We will cask the beer tomorrow. 我们明天将啤酒装桶。
The vintner will cask the wine next week.  酿酒师下周将葡萄酒装桶。


虚拟语气:
If we had more casks, we would cask more wine. 如果我们有更多的酒桶，我们会装更多的酒。
I wish I had casked the beer sooner. 我希望我早点把啤酒装桶。


被动语态:
The wine was casked in oak barrels.  葡萄酒被装在橡木桶里。
The beer is being casked as we speak.  啤酒正在被装桶。


疑问句:
Was the wine casked properly? 葡萄酒装桶了吗？
Have they casked all the produce? 他们把所有产品都装桶了吗？


否定句:
They didn't cask the wine this year.  他们今年没有把酒装桶。
We haven't casked the beer yet. 我们还没把啤酒装桶。
