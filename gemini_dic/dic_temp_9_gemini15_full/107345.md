lame-brained:/ˌleɪmˈbreɪnd/| adj. |愚蠢的，笨的，头脑简单的 |  Describes someone who is stupid or foolish; lacking intelligence or good judgment.  It is an informal and somewhat offensive term. |例句：That was a lame-brained idea; it's never going to work.  那是个愚蠢的主意；它永远不会奏效。

例句：He made a series of lame-brained decisions that cost the company a fortune. 他做了一系列愚蠢的决定，导致公司损失了一大笔钱。


近义词：stupid, foolish, idiotic, silly, unintelligent, dense, obtuse

反义词：intelligent, clever, bright, smart, brilliant, shrewd


词性：形容词
