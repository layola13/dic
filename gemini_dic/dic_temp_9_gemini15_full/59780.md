ecosystems:/ˈiːkoʊsɪstəmz/ | n. | 生态系统 |  A biological community of interacting organisms and their physical environment.  Ecosystems are complex networks of living things and their surroundings, including the air, water, soil, and other non-living components.  They are characterized by energy flow and nutrient cycling. | Example: The Amazon rainforest is a diverse ecosystem teeming with life. 亚马逊雨林是一个充满生命的多样化生态系统。  The coral reef ecosystem is under threat from pollution. 珊瑚礁生态系统受到污染的威胁。|近义词：environment, habitat, biosphere | 反义词：None (反义词不适用)
