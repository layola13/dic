malassimilation:/ˌmælæsɪmɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |吸收不良；同化不良|The imperfect absorption or integration of food or cultural elements.  It refers to the failure of the body to properly digest and absorb nutrients or the failure of a society to fully integrate cultural elements. | Example:  Malassimilation of nutrients can lead to various health problems. 营养吸收不良会导致各种健康问题。The malassimilation of immigrant cultures often leads to social tension.  移民文化的同化不良常常导致社会紧张。|近义词：poor absorption, faulty integration, indigestion |反义词：assimilation, absorption, digestion
