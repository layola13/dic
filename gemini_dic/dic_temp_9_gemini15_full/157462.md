piscator:/pɪsˈkeɪtər/| n. |渔民，捕鱼者|A piscator is a person who fishes, especially as an occupation.  The word evokes a sense of someone skilled and knowledgeable in fishing techniques.| Example: The old piscator knew the best spots to find salmon.  这位老渔民知道哪里最容易钓到鲑鱼。|近义词：fisherman, angler, fisher|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with farmer, hunter etc. depending on the context.|名词
