paprikas:/ˈpæprikəs/ | n. |红辣椒粉，红椒粉 | Paprika is a spice made from ground dried red peppers. It ranges in color from mild to deep red, and in flavor from sweet to hot.  |例句：This goulash recipe calls for a generous amount of paprika. 这道牛肉炖菜需要大量的红椒粉。

例句：The paprika added a vibrant color and smoky flavor to the dish. 红椒粉为这道菜增添了鲜艳的色彩和烟熏味。


近义词：red pepper powder, chili powder | 反义词：无


词源：From Hungarian *paprika, from Turkish biber "pepper".  Ultimately from Sanskrit pippali "long pepper".


用法说明:

paprikas在英语中通常用作不可数名词，表示红椒粉这种香料。  虽然有时可以见到复数形式paprikas，但这主要用于指代不同种类或来源的红椒粉，而非指数量上的增多。
