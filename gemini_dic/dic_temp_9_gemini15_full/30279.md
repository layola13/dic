cementers:/sɪˈmɛntərz/| n. |水泥工；胶结剂；接合器|People or things that cement or bind together;  Those who work with cement, applying it in construction.  The term can also refer to devices or materials that join or unite things firmly.|例句：The cementers worked quickly to finish the foundation before the rain.  水泥工迅速工作，以便在下雨前完成地基。

The skilled cementers ensured a strong and lasting bond between the bricks. 熟练的水泥工确保砖块之间牢固持久的结合。

近义词：construction workers, binders, joiners|反义词：separators, dividers|名词
