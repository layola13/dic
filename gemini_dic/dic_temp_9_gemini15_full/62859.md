enjoyers:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪərz/| n. |享受者，爱好者|People who derive pleasure from something.  Those who actively engage in and appreciate a particular activity, hobby, or interest.|例句：The concert was a delight for the enjoyers of classical music.  这场音乐会让古典音乐爱好者们大饱耳福。

例句：Many enjoyers of this online game spend hours playing daily.  许多这个网络游戏的爱好者每天花费数小时玩游戏。


近义词：enthusiasts, aficionados, devotees, fans|反义词：detractors, critics
