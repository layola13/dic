measurers: /ˈmɛʒərərz/ | n. | 测量者，计量者 | People or devices that measure something.  Those who determine or ascertain the quantity, extent, dimensions, or capacity of something. | 例句：The surveyors acted as measurers of the land. = 测量员充当了土地测量员的角色。 The scientists used precise measurers to collect data. = 科学家们使用精确的测量仪器收集数据。 | 近义词：gaugers, surveyors, quantifiers | 反义词：None directly applicable.  (The antonym would relate to the *lack* of measurement, not to the measurers themselves.)
