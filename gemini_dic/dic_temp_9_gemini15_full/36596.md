collators:/kəˈleɪtərz/ | n. | 对照者；校对者；整理者 | A person or thing that collates.  Collators are often used in the context of comparing, organizing, or assembling items. For example, a collator might be a machine that sorts papers, or a person who checks the accuracy of data. | Example: The publishing house hired several collators to check the manuscript for errors before printing. 出版社雇佣了几位校对员在印刷前检查手稿是否有错误。 |近义词：comparers, checkers, organizers, assemblers | 反义词：disorganizers, separators
