refertilizable: /ˌriːˈfɜːrtlɪzeɪbəl/ | adj. | 可再次施肥的；可再次受精的 | Capable of being fertilized again.  This word is formed by combining "re-" (again), "fertilize" (to make fertile or productive), and "-able" (capable of being). It implies the ability to undergo a second fertilization process, applicable to both agricultural contexts (land) and biological contexts (e.g., eggs). | The field is refertilizable after the harvest. 这块地收割后可以再次施肥。 The egg was refertilizable after the first fertilization attempt failed.  第一次受精尝试失败后，卵子可以再次受精。 |  fertilizable, reusable | infertile, unfertilizable
