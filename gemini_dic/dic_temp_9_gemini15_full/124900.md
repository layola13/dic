muddler:/ˈmʌdlər/ | n. |笨拙的人；捣碎器；混乱的人 | A person who is ineffective or inefficient; a clumsy or confused person; also a tool used for muddling or crushing.  |例句：He was a muddler, always losing his keys and forgetting appointments. 他是个笨手笨脚的人，总是丢钥匙，忘记约会。
He used a muddler to crush the herbs for his cocktail. 他用捣碎器捣碎草药做鸡尾酒。|近义词：bungler, incompetent, klutz |反义词：expert, efficient, skilled
