reemphasizing:/ˌriːˈɛmfəsaɪzɪŋ/| v. |再次强调；重新强调|  The act of stressing something again;  giving renewed emphasis to a point or idea.  It implies a previous emphasis that may have been overlooked or needs reinforcement.|例句：The teacher reemphasized the importance of studying hard for the exam. =老师再次强调了努力学习考试的重要性。

例句：The spokesperson reemphasized the company's commitment to environmental sustainability. =发言人再次强调了公司对环境可持续性的承诺。


近义词：restating, reiterating, stressing, underlining, highlighting

反义词：downplaying, ignoring, overlooking, neglecting


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reemphasizes the need for patience. = 他再次强调需要耐心。
She reemphasizes her commitment to the project. = 她再次强调她对项目的承诺。

一般过去时:
He reemphasized the importance of safety regulations. = 他再次强调了安全规定的重要性。
She reemphasized the deadline for the report. = 她再次强调了报告的截止日期。

现在完成时:
They have reemphasized their support for the initiative. = 他们已经再次强调了他们对这项倡议的支持。
She has reemphasized the need for better communication. = 她已经再次强调了改进沟通的必要性。

过去完成时:
He had reemphasized the urgency of the situation before leaving. = 他在离开之前再次强调了情况的紧迫性。
They had reemphasized the risks involved before proceeding. = 他们在继续之前再次强调了所涉及的风险。

将来时:
He will reemphasize the key points in his presentation. = 他将在他的演讲中再次强调要点。
She will reemphasize the importance of teamwork. = 她将再次强调团队合作的重要性。

虚拟语气:

I wish he had reemphasized the instructions more clearly. = 我希望他更清楚地再次强调了指示。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
He did not reemphasize the details. = 他没有再次强调细节。
She did not reemphasize the need for caution. = 她没有再次强调谨慎的必要性。

词源:
re- (prefix indicating repetition or again) + emphasize (verb meaning to give special importance or prominence to something)  The word "emphasize" itself comes from the Greek word "emphasizein," meaning "to show clearly," from "en-" (in, on, into) + "phainein" (to show, to appear).
