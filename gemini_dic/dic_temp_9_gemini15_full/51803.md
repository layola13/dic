devove:/dɪˈvoʊv/ | vt. |献身；致力于；吞没；专心致志于| To dedicate (oneself or one's time or efforts) completely to something.  To engulf or swallow up.|例句：He devoted his life to helping the poor. 他将一生奉献给了帮助穷人。|例句：The flames devoted the entire building. 火焰吞噬了整栋建筑。|近义词：dedicate, commit, consecrate |反义词：neglect, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He devotes his free time to gardening. = 他把空闲时间都用来园艺。
She devotes herself to her work. = 她全身心地投入到工作中。

一般过去时:
They devoted their weekend to cleaning the house. = 他们把周末时间都用来打扫房子。
He devoted much of his youth to studying. = 他把青春的大部分时间都用在了学习上。

现在完成时:
She has devoted many years to researching this topic. = 她多年来一直致力于研究这个课题。
He has devoted his entire career to public service. = 他将整个职业生涯都奉献给了公共服务。

过去完成时:
They had devoted years to the project before it was finally completed. = 在项目最终完成之前，他们已经为此投入了数年时间。
She had devoted her life savings to the charity. = 她已经把毕生的积蓄都捐给了慈善机构。

将来时:
He will devote the next few months to writing his novel. = 他将把接下来的几个月时间用来写他的小说。
She will devote her energies to finding a cure. = 她将把精力集中在寻找治疗方法上。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would devote it to learning a new language. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会把它用来学习一门外语。
I wish I could devote more time to my family. = 我希望我能花更多的时间陪伴家人。

被动语态:
Much time was devoted to the discussion. = 大量时间被用于讨论。
His life was devoted to the cause of peace. = 他的一生都致力于和平事业。

疑问句:
How much time do you devote to exercise each week? = 你每周花多少时间锻炼？
What aspects of your life do you devote yourself to? = 你把你的生活中的哪些方面奉献给了什么？

否定句:
He doesn't devote enough time to his studies. = 他没有花足够的时间学习。
She didn't devote herself to that project. = 她没有全身心地投入到那个项目中。


PHRASE->
devote oneself to = 献身于；致力于
devote time to = 花时间在…上
devote energy to = 把精力放在…上
devote attention to = 注意；关注
devote resources to = 将资源投入到…
