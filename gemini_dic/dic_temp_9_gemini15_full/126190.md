muzzleloaders:/ˈmʌzəlˌloʊdɚz/| n. |前膛枪（复数）|Firearms loaded from the muzzle (the open end of the barrel).  Muzzleloaders are typically older firearms, often used for hunting or historical reenactments. They require a separate loading process for each shot.|例句：Many historical reenactors use muzzleloaders to accurately depict battles of the past. 许多历史再现者使用前膛枪来准确地描绘过去的战斗。

例句：He cleaned his muzzleloader carefully after a day of target practice. 他在一天的靶场练习后仔细地清洁了他的前膛枪。


近义词：black powder firearms, flintlocks (a specific type of muzzleloader)
反义词：modern firearms (breech-loading firearms)
