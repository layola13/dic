cross-path:/ˈkrɒs pæθ/| n.|交叉路径，意外相遇|The term "cross-path" refers to the instance where two or more individuals or things intersect or encounter each other unexpectedly or unintentionally.  It often implies a brief and possibly insignificant interaction.|例句：We crossed paths at the conference last week.  上周我们在会议上不期而遇。|例句：Their lives crossed paths briefly in college.  他们的生活在大学里短暂地交汇过。|近义词：encounter, intersect, meet, collide|反义词：avoid, bypass, shun|名词
