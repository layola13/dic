hotpressed:/ˈhɒtˌprɛst/| adj.|热压的；热压处理过的|  Hotpressed refers to paper or other materials that have been pressed while still hot, resulting in a smooth, hard surface. This process is often used to create a high-quality finish, particularly for writing and drawing.  | Example: The artist used hotpressed watercolor paper for its smooth surface.  艺术家使用热压水彩纸是因为其光滑的表面。  The hotpressed board provided a perfect surface for his detailed work. 热压板为他细致的工作提供了完美的表面。|近义词：smooth, polished, finished|反义词：rough, textured, unpressed|形容词
