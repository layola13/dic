pentameran:/pɛnˈtæmərən/| adj. |五聚体的；由五个亚基组成的|Consisting of five subunits or parts.  Pentameran refers to a structure or molecule composed of five sub-units. This term is commonly used in biochemistry and molecular biology to describe proteins, nucleic acids, or other molecules with five constituent parts.|例句：The protein complex is a pentameran structure, exhibiting significant stability.  该蛋白质复合物是一种五聚体结构，表现出显著的稳定性。|近义词：pentameric|反义词：monomeric, dimeric, trimeric, tetrameric
