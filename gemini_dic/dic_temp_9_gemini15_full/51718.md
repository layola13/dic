devisor:/dɪˈvaɪzər/| n. |立遗嘱者，遗赠者|A person who devises or bequeaths something in a will, especially real estate.|The devisor of the property left it to his nephew.|近义词：testator, bequeather|反义词：devisee


USAGE->
