ramble-on:/ˈræmbəl ɒn/| v.|喋喋不休，漫无目的地谈论|To talk or write at length in a rambling or incoherent way.|例句：He rambled on for hours about his childhood. 他喋喋不休地谈论了他童年时代的事情好几个小时。|近义词：babble, chatter, drone, prattle|反义词：be concise, be brief


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rambles on about politics all the time. = 他总是喋喋不休地谈论政治。
She rambles on incessantly during meetings. = 她在会议上没完没了地唠叨。


一般过去时:
He rambled on about his trip for ages. = 他没完没了地谈论他的旅行。
She rambled on about her problems last night. = 她昨晚喋喋不休地抱怨她的问题。


现在完成时:
He has rambled on about that topic for years. = 多年来，他一直喋喋不休地谈论那个话题。
She has rambled on so much that everyone is tired. = 她说得太多了，以至于每个人都厌倦了。


过去完成时:
He had rambled on for so long that everyone had left. = 他已经喋喋不休地说了很久，以至于每个人都离开了。
She had rambled on about her cat before I could even say hello. = 我还没来得及打招呼，她就一直在喋喋不休地谈论她的猫。


将来时:
He will ramble on about his achievements at the party. = 他将在派对上喋喋不休地谈论他的成就。
She will ramble on for hours if you let her. = 如果你让她说，她会喋喋不休地谈论几个小时。


虚拟语气:
If he rambled on less, people would listen more. = 如果他少说一些废话，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't ramble on so much. = 我希望他不要说那么多废话。


被动语态: (This verb is rarely used passively)
The topic was rambled on about extensively. = 这个话题被冗长地讨论了。 (This sounds unnatural.)


疑问句:
Does he always ramble on like that? = 他总是这样喋喋不休吗？
Will she ramble on during the presentation? = 她会在演示过程中喋喋不休吗？


否定句:
He didn't ramble on as much as usual. = 他没有像往常那样喋喋不休。
She tried not to ramble on, but it was difficult. = 她试图不喋喋不休，但这很难。


PHRASE->
ramble on and on = 没完没了地唠叨
ramble on about something = 喋喋不休地谈论某事
ramble on endlessly = 没完没了地闲聊
ramble on incoherently = 语无伦次地喋喋不休
ramble on at length = 长篇大论地谈论
