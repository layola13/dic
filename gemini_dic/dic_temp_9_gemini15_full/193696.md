self-condemnations: /ˌsɛlfˌkɑːndɛmˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |自我谴责；自我责备 | The act of condemning oneself; self-reproach.  It involves criticizing, blaming, or judging oneself harshly for perceived flaws or shortcomings. |例句：His self-condemnations were relentless, gnawing at his conscience day and night.  他的自我谴责毫不留情，日夜啃噬着他的良心。

例句：Her self-condemnations stemmed from a deep-seated insecurity and a fear of failure. 她的自我谴责源于根深蒂固的不安全感和对失败的恐惧。


近义词：self-reproach, self-criticism, self-blame, remorse, guilt

反义词：self-acceptance, self-forgiveness, self-compassion


词性：名词 (plural)
