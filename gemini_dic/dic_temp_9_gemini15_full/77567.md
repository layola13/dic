gearhead:/ˈɡɪrˌhed/| n. |汽车迷；机械迷；对机械非常着迷的人| A gearhead is someone who is very enthusiastic about cars, engines, and other machinery. They often have a deep understanding of how these things work and enjoy working on them.  |He's a real gearhead; he spends all his free time tinkering with his classic car. 他是个真正的汽车迷；他把所有空闲时间都花在了修理他的老爷车上。|car enthusiast, petrolhead, motorhead |non-gearhead
