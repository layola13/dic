putrescent:/ˈpjuːtrəsɛnt/| adj.|腐败的，腐烂的|Beginning to decay or rot; characterized by decay or rotting.|The putrescent smell coming from the garbage was unbearable.  垃圾散发出的腐败气味令人难以忍受。

The decaying fish emitted a putrescent odor.  腐烂的鱼散发出腐败的气味。|rotten, decaying, decomposing|fresh, clean, wholesome|形容词
