chugs:/tʃʌɡz/| v.|  chugging, chugged | 快速地喝；发出隆隆声；（车、船等）发出嘎吱嘎吱地响声 |  To drink something quickly in large gulps. To make a chugging sound, as of an engine. | 例句：He chugged down the whole bottle of water in one go. 他一口气喝完了一整瓶水。

例句：The old train chugged slowly up the hill.  这列老火车缓慢地爬上了山坡。


近义词：gulp, guzzle, gulp down,  sputter


反义词：sip, savor


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chugs his beer every night. 他每晚都很快地喝啤酒。
The engine chugs along steadily. 引擎稳稳地发出隆隆声。


一般过去时:
She chugged the soda quickly. 她很快地喝掉了苏打水。
The bus chugged up the steep incline. 公共汽车嘎吱嘎吱地爬上了陡坡。


现在完成时:
He has chugged three glasses of water already. 他已经喝了三杯水。
The old car has chugged along for years. 这辆旧车已经运行了好几年了。


过去完成时:
They had chugged all the beer before we arrived. 我们到之前他们已经把所有的啤酒都喝完了。
The plane had chugged along for hours before the engine failed.  飞机引擎故障前已经飞行数小时，发出隆隆声。


将来时:
She will chug the rest of the juice. 她会把剩下的果汁喝完。
The train will chug along to the next station. 火车会继续发出隆隆声开往下一站。


虚拟语气:
If I were thirsty, I would chug the water. 如果我渴了，我会一口气喝掉水。
I wish I could chug a whole bottle of soda without stopping.  我希望我能一口气喝完一整瓶苏打水而不用停下来。


被动语态:
The water was chugged down quickly. 水很快就被喝掉了。
The engine is chugging along smoothly. 引擎正在平稳地运行，发出隆隆声。


疑问句:
Does he chug his coffee every morning? 他每天早上都很快地喝咖啡吗？
Is the engine chugging normally? 引擎运行正常吗？（发出正常的隆隆声）


否定句:
He didn't chug the milk; he sipped it slowly. 他没有很快地喝牛奶；他慢慢地啜饮着。
The car wasn't chugging; it was making a strange noise. 车子不是发出隆隆声；它发出奇怪的声音。


PHRASE->
chug along: 缓慢而稳定地运行
chug down: 快速地喝掉
chugging sound: 隆隆声
chugging engine: 发出隆隆声的引擎
to chug something down:  快速地喝下某物
