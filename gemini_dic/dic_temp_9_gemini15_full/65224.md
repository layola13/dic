etiquette:/ˈetɪkɛt/| n. |礼仪，礼节 |The customary code of polite behavior in society or among members of a particular profession or group.  Etiquette dictates proper behavior in various social situations. |Example:  Good table etiquette is important for a formal dinner.  良好的餐桌礼仪对于正式晚餐很重要。

Example: He showed impeccable etiquette throughout the evening. 他整晚都表现得无可挑剔。|近义词：protocol, manners, decorum, politeness |反义词：rudeness, impoliteness, bad manners |名词
