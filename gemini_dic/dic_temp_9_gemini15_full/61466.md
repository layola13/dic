embracers:/ɪmˈbreɪsərz/| n. | 欣然接受者；拥抱者；支持者 | Those who readily accept or adopt something new or different; people who hug; supporters of a cause or idea. | Example:  The new technology has found many enthusiastic embracers in the business world.  新的技术在商界找到了许多热情的拥护者。


The early embracers of this software reported significant improvements in efficiency.  这项软件的早期使用者报告说效率显著提高。

近义词:  supporters, adopters, advocates, huggers

反义词:  rejectors, opponents, critics


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company embraces new ideas. = 公司欣然接受新想法。
Many people embrace change. = 许多人欣然接受改变。


一般过去时:
She embraced her new life in the city. = 她欣然接受她在城市的新生活。
They embraced the opportunity to travel. = 他们抓住机会去旅行。（这里拥抱指抓住机会）


现在完成时:
He has embraced his role as a father. = 他已经欣然接受了他作为父亲的角色。
We have embraced sustainable practices in our business. = 我们已经在我们的业务中采用了可持续的实践。


过去完成时:
They had embraced the new technology before the competitors. = 在竞争对手之前，他们就已经采用了新技术。
She had embraced her fear and faced it head-on. = 她战胜了恐惧并勇敢地面对它。（这里拥抱指克服）


将来时:
They will embrace the challenges ahead. = 他们将迎接未来的挑战。
She will embrace her new career with enthusiasm. = 她将热情地拥抱她的新事业。


虚拟语气:
If she embraced the opportunity, she would succeed. = 如果她抓住机会，她就会成功。
I wish he embraced change more readily. = 我希望他能更容易地接受改变。


被动语态:
The new policy was embraced by the community. = 社区欣然接受了这项新政策。
The proposal was not embraced by the board. = 这项提案没有得到董事会的采纳。


疑问句:
Does he embrace different cultures? = 他是否包容不同的文化？
Will they embrace the new strategy? = 他们会采纳新的策略吗？


否定句:
The company didn't embrace the new technology. = 公司没有采用新技术。
They refused to embrace the change. = 他们拒绝接受改变。


PHRASE->
embrace change = 拥抱改变
embrace opportunity = 抓住机会
embrace diversity = 包容多样性
embrace life = 热爱生活
embrace the future = 拥抱未来
