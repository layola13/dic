helminthology:/ˌhɛlˈmɪnθɒlədʒi/ | n. |蠕虫学|The branch of zoology that deals with helminths (worms).  It encompasses the study of their morphology, physiology, ecology, life cycles, and their medical and veterinary importance.  |Example:  Helminthology is a crucial field in understanding parasitic infections and developing effective treatments. 蠕虫学是理解寄生虫感染和开发有效治疗方法的关键领域。|近义词：parasitology (if focusing on parasitic worms)|反义词：None
