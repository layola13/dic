greeniest:/ˈɡriːniəst/| adj.| 最绿的；最环保的 |Most green; most environmentally friendly.  The adjective "greeniest" is the superlative form of "green," emphasizing the highest degree of greenness.  It can refer to color, vegetation, or environmental consciousness.|例句：That's the greeniest lawn on the street. = 那是街上最绿的草坪。

The rainforest is the greeniest place on Earth. = 雨林是地球上最绿的地方。


近义词：most verdant, most lush, most eco-friendly |反义词：most barren, most arid, least eco-friendly |词性:形容词
