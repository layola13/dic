rebaptizer:/ˌriːˈbæptʌɪzər/| n. |再施洗者；再次洗礼者 |A person who rebaptizes someone.  Someone who performs a second baptism, often implying a belief that the previous baptism was invalid. |Example:  He was considered a rebaptizer by the established church because he insisted on re-baptizing converts from other denominations. 他被正统教会视为再施洗者，因为他坚持要为来自其他教派的皈依者重新施洗。|近义词:re-baptizer, re-baptist (less common)|反义词:baptizer (in the context of a first baptism)
