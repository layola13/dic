gasalier:/ˌɡæsəˈliər/| n. |枝形吊灯|A gasalier is a type of chandelier that uses gaslight instead of candles or electricity.  They were popular in the 19th century before the widespread adoption of electric lighting. The term is a blend of "gas" and "chandelier."|The grand ballroom was illuminated by a magnificent gasalier.|近义词：chandelier, gas lamp|反义词：None specifically, but terms like "table lamp" or "wall sconce" could be considered opposites in terms of function and placement.|名词
