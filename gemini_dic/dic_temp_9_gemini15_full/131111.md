nonbindingness:/ˌnɒnˈbaɪndɪŋnəs/| n. |非约束性，不具约束力|The lack of legal force or obligation;  the state of not being legally binding.  This refers to agreements or contracts that are not enforceable in a court of law.|例句：The agreement was declared nonbinding because it lacked the necessary signatures.  该协议因缺少必要的签名而被宣布无效力。 The nonbinding nature of the resolution means it's not legally enforceable.  该决议的不具约束力性质意味着它在法律上不可强制执行。|近义词：unobligatory, unenforceable, informal|反义词：bindingness, enforceability
