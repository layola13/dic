deceptible: /dɪˈsɛptəbəl/ | adj. | 易受骗的；易被欺骗的 |  easily deceived or tricked; misleading in appearance; capable of deceiving | The deceptive advertisement lured many customers into buying the useless product.  这则具有欺骗性的广告诱使许多顾客购买了无用的产品。|  gullible, credulous, naive |  astute, shrewd, perceptive | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is easily deceived. = 他很容易被骗。
The advertisement is deceptive. = 这则广告具有欺骗性。


一般过去时:
She was deceived by his charm. = 她被他的魅力所迷惑。
The magician's trick was deceptive. = 魔术师的戏法具有欺骗性。


现在完成时:
I have been deceived many times. = 我已经被骗过很多次了。
The company has been deceptive in its dealings. = 这家公司在交易中一直都在欺骗。


过去完成时:
They had been deceived by the false promises. = 他们已经被虚假的承诺欺骗了。
He realized he had been deceptive in his report. = 他意识到他在报告中有所隐瞒。


将来时:
He will be easily deceived by that con artist. = 他很容易被那个骗子骗到。
The marketing campaign will be deceptive. = 这个营销活动将具有欺骗性。


虚拟语气:
If he were more perceptive, he wouldn't be so deceptive. = 如果他更敏锐一些，他就不会这么容易被骗。
I wish I hadn't been so deceptive in my youth. = 我真希望我年轻时不要那么欺骗别人。


被动语态:
Many people were deceived by the fake news. = 许多人被假新闻欺骗了。
The truth was deceptively hidden. = 真相被巧妙地隐藏了。


疑问句:
Is he easily deceived? = 他容易受骗吗？

否定句:

The advertisement was not deceptive. = 广告没有欺骗性。
