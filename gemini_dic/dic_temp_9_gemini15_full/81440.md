gratifications:/ˌɡrædəfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|满足；令人满意的事物；乐趣|The feeling of pleasure or satisfaction derived from something.  It often refers to the fulfillment of a desire or need.|例句：The gratifications of a job well done are immense.  一份工作完成得很出色带来的满足感是巨大的。

例句：He found great gratification in helping others. 他从帮助他人中获得了极大的满足感。

近义词：satisfactions, pleasures, delights, enjoyments

反义词：disappointments, frustrations, dissatisfactions

词性：名词
