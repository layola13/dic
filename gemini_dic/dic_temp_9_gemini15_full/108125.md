latin-americans:/ˌlætn əˈmɛrɪkənz/| n. |拉丁美洲人 |People who live in Latin America.  This term encompasses a vast and diverse population with various ethnicities, nationalities, and cultures. The term itself can be considered a broad generalization and potentially problematic due to its inherent simplification of a complex region. |Example: Many Latin Americans migrated to the United States in search of better economic opportunities.  许多拉丁美洲人为了寻找更好的经济机会而移居美国。|近义词：Hispanics (often used interchangeably but not entirely accurate), Latinos/Latinas (also used interchangeably but not encompassing all) |反义词：None directly, but terms representing specific nationalities within Latin America could be considered antonyms in a contextual sense (e.g., North Americans).|名词
