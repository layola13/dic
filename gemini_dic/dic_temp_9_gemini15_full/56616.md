doner:/ˈdəʊnər/ | n. |肉卷；捐赠者 | A doner is a type of kebab, consisting of meat (usually lamb, beef, chicken, or turkey) that is cooked on a vertical rotisserie and then sliced thinly and served in a sandwich or wrap.  It can also refer to someone who gives something, especially money, to a charity or other organization. | 例句：He bought a doner kebab for lunch. 他午餐买了一个肉卷。She is a generous doner to the charity. 她是这个慈善机构的大方捐赠者。 | 近义词：kebab, giver, contributor | 反义词：receiver, recipient
