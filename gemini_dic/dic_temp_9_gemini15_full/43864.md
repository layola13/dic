crescents:/ˈkrɛs(ə)nts/| n.|新月，月牙形，新月状物|A crescent is the shape of the moon when it is less than half full, looking like a curved line. It can also refer to something that has this shape.|例句：The young moon was a slender crescent in the evening sky.  年轻的月亮在傍晚的天空中呈现出一弯细细的月牙。|例句：The bakery sold delicious crescent rolls. 面包店卖美味的新月形面包卷。|近义词：half-moon, sickle|反义词：full moon
