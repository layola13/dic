predelinquency:/ˌpriːdɪˈlɪŋkwənsi/ | n. | 预犯罪行为，犯罪前兆 | The state or condition of being on the verge of delinquency; exhibiting behaviors that are precursors to delinquent acts.  It often involves a pattern of increasingly serious misbehavior.  | Example:  The school's early intervention program aims to identify students exhibiting predelinquency and provide them with support. 学校的早期干预计划旨在识别出表现出预犯罪行为的学生并为他们提供支持。|近义词：early warning signs, risk factors, at-risk behavior | 反义词：law-abiding behavior, conformity
