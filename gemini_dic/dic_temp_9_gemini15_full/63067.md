enrollment:/ɪnˈroʊlmənt/ | n. |注册；入学；登记；入会 | The act of enrolling or the state of being enrolled; the number of people enrolled.  It refers to the process of officially registering or signing up for something, typically a course, program, or organization. | Example: The enrollment for this semester is higher than expected.  这个学期的注册人数比预期高。 |The university saw a significant increase in enrollment this year. 今年大学的入学人数显著增加。|近义词：registration, enrolment, sign-up, admission |反义词：withdrawal, deregistration
