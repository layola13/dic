emoticon:/ɪˈmoʊtɪkɒn/| n. |表情符号|A pictorial representation of a facial expression, used in electronic communication to convey emotion.  It's typically formed using punctuation marks and letters. |Example:  She sent me a smiley emoticon :) to show she was happy. 她给我发了一个笑脸表情符号:)表示她很开心。|近义词：emoji, smiley, graphic|反义词:None
