respooled:/riːˈspuːld/| v.|重新卷绕，重新上纱|To wind (yarn, thread, etc.) onto a spool again.  This usually implies that the yarn, thread, or similar material had been previously unraveled or used.|例句：The weaver respooled the thread before starting the new tapestry. 织工在开始新的挂毯之前重新卷绕了线。  The factory worker respooled the damaged yarn onto a new spool. 工厂工人将损坏的纱线重新卷到一个新的筒子上。|近义词：rewind, re-roll|反义词：unwind, unravel|动词
