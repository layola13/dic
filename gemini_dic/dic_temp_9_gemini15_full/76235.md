futures:/ˈfjuːtʃərz/ | n. |期货；未来；将来 | Futures contracts are agreements to buy or sell an asset at a specific price on a future date. 期货合约是同意在未来某个日期以特定价格买卖资产的协议。|近义词：future, prospects |反义词：past, present


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The futures market is volatile. = 期货市场动荡不定。
Futures contracts are traded on exchanges. = 期货合约在交易所交易。


一般过去时:
Oil futures prices fell sharply last week. = 上周油价期货大幅下跌。
They bought soybean futures contracts last month. = 他们上个月购买了大豆期货合约。

The company has hedged its risk using futures contracts. = 公司利用期货合约对冲了风险。
Investors have made significant profits from futures trading. = 投资者从期货交易中获得了巨额利润。
