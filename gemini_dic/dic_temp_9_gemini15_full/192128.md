/skræb/ | v. | 刮擦；挠；挖；拼写单词| To scrape, scratch, or dig.  Also, to search for words in a game of Scrabble. | 例句：He scraped his knee on the pavement. 他在人行道上擦破了膝盖。 | 例句：She scraped the mud off her boots. 她把靴子上的泥刮掉了。|近义词：scratch, scrape, rub, dig | 反义词：polish, smooth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrapes the paint off the wall. = 他刮掉墙上的油漆。
The cat scrapes at the door. = 猫抓挠着门。


一般过去时:
She scraped her car on a wall. = 她把车刮蹭到了墙上。
They scraped the bottom of the barrel. = 他们把最后一点东西都用光了。


现在完成时:
He has scraped together enough money for the trip. = 他已经攒够了旅行的钱。
We have scraped the ice from the windscreen. = 我们已经把挡风玻璃上的冰刮掉了。


过去完成时:
She had scraped the burnt food off the pan before I arrived. = 我到达之前，她已经把锅里烧焦的食物刮掉了。
They had scraped by on very little money for years. = 他们多年来一直靠微薄的收入勉强维持生计。


将来时:
I will scrape the snow off the car before I leave. = 我离开之前会把车上的雪刮掉。
They will scrape the paint off the furniture. = 他们会把家具上的油漆刮掉。


虚拟语气:
If I scraped my car, I would be very upset. = 如果我把车刮花了，我会非常沮丧。
I wish I hadn't scraped my knee. = 我真希望我没有擦破膝盖。


被动语态:
The paint was scraped off the table. = 桌子上的油漆被刮掉了。

疑问句:
Did you scrape your knee? = 你擦破膝盖了吗？
Has he scraped the car? = 他把车刮了吗？


否定句:
I didn't scrape the car. = 我没有刮坏车。
They haven't scraped the paint yet. = 他们还没有刮掉油漆。
