paradises:/ˈpærədaɪsɪz/| n. |天堂，乐园|Plural of paradise.  Refers to multiple idyllic or perfect places.  The word evokes feelings of bliss, beauty, and tranquility.|例句：Many people dream of escaping to tropical paradises.  许多人梦想逃到热带天堂。

例句：The photographs depicted various paradises, from lush rainforests to snow-capped mountains. 照片描绘了各种天堂，从茂密的雨林到白雪皑皑的山脉。


近义词：heavens, utopias, earthly delights|反义词：hells, nightmaresh, dystopias|词性:noun
