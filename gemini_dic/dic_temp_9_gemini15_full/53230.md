directives:/daɪˈrɛktɪvz/ | n. |指令；指示；法规 | Instructions or commands; guidelines or rules that must be followed.  Directives are often issued by an authority figure or organization and serve as a guide for action or behavior. | Example: The company issued new directives regarding workplace safety.  公司发布了关于工作场所安全的新指令。

The government implemented strict directives to control the spread of the virus. 政府实施了严格的指令以控制病毒的传播。|近义词：instructions, commands, guidelines, mandates, orders |反义词：suggestions, recommendations (these imply a lack of obligation) |名词
