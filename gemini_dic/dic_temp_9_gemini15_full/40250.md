continental-crust: /ˌkɒntɪˈnɛntəl krʌst/ | n. |大陆地壳| The Earth's crust that forms the continents.  It is generally thicker and less dense than oceanic crust.  Continental crust is primarily composed of felsic rocks, which are rich in silicon and aluminum. | Example: The Himalayas were formed by the collision of two continental crust plates. 喜马拉雅山是由两块大陆板块碰撞形成的。  The thickness of continental crust varies greatly depending on location. 大陆地壳的厚度因地而异。 |近义词: continental lithosphere |反义词: oceanic crust
