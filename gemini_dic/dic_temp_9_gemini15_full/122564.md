moderate:/ˈmɒdərət/ /ˈmɑːdərət/| adj., v.|适度的；温和的；主持；节制|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "moderate" means being within reasonable limits; not extreme or excessive.  It can describe things like temperature, speed, intensity, or opinions. As a verb, it means to preside over or control something, often a meeting or discussion, or to lessen the intensity or severity of something.

例句:
He maintained a moderate pace throughout the marathon. 他在马拉松全程保持着适中的速度。

She has moderate political views. 她持有温和的政治观点。


The chairman moderated the debate. 主席主持了这场辩论。


The doctor helped to moderate his patient's pain. 医生帮助减轻了病人的疼痛。


近义词:
temperate, mild, gentle, reasonable, average, control, regulate

反义词:
extreme, excessive, intense, radical, severe, uncontrolled


词性:
adjective, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moderates the discussion skillfully. 他熟练地主持讨论。
She moderates her food intake to stay healthy. 她控制饮食以保持健康。


一般过去时:
The teacher moderated the students' arguments. 老师缓和了学生们的争论。
He moderated his tone to avoid offending anyone. 他缓和了语气，以免冒犯任何人。


现在完成时:
They have moderated their spending habits. 他们已经改变了消费习惯（变得节制）。
She has moderated several conferences this year. 她今年主持了几次会议。


过去完成时:
He had moderated his expectations before the meeting. 会议前他已经降低了预期。
They had moderated the volume of the music before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，他们已经调低了音乐音量。


将来时:
She will moderate the upcoming debate. 她将主持即将举行的辩论。
He will moderate his alcohol consumption. 他将减少酒精摄入量。


虚拟语气:
If he moderated his language, the argument would have ended sooner. 如果他语气缓和一点，争论就会早点结束。
I wish she moderated her criticism a bit more. 我希望她能稍微缓和一下批评。


被动语态:
The discussion was moderated by a neutral party. 讨论由中立方主持。
His excessive spending was finally moderated by his wife's intervention. 他过度的消费最终在他的妻子的干预下得到了控制。


疑问句:
Does he moderate his diet? 他控制饮食吗？
Will someone moderate the online forum? 会有人主持这个在线论坛吗？


否定句:
He didn't moderate his temper. 他没有控制住脾气。
She doesn't moderate her exercise regime enough. 她对自己的运动计划不够节制。
