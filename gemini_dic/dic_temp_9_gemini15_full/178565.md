recorded:/rɪˈkɔːrdɪd/| v. adj.|记录的，被记录的|The past participle of "record," indicating something has been documented or registered. It can function as an adjective describing something that has been recorded or as part of a passive verb construction.|例句：The concert was recorded live.  这场音乐会是现场录制的。
The recorded message informed callers of the office closure.  录音信息通知来电者办公室已关闭。|近义词：documented, registered, logged|反义词：unrecorded, undocumented


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The band records a new album every year.乐队每年录制一张新专辑。
She records her lectures for students who miss class.她录制她的讲座给错过课程的学生。

一般过去时:
He recorded his thoughts in a journal.他把他的想法记录在日记里。
They recorded the temperature every hour.他们每小时记录一次温度。

现在完成时:
She has recorded over 100 songs in her career.她职业生涯中已录制超过100首歌。
They have recorded their findings in a detailed report.他们已在详细报告中记录了他们的发现。

过去完成时:
The police had recorded his statement before releasing him.警方在他获释前已记录了他的证词。
She had recorded all the evidence before presenting her case.在陈述她的案情之前，她已记录了所有证据。

将来时:
We will record the interview tomorrow morning.我们明天早上将录制采访。
He will record the results of the experiment.他将记录实验的结果。

虚拟语气:

It would be helpful if we recorded all the data.如果我们记录所有数据，那将会很有帮助。


被动语态:
The data was recorded automatically.数据是自动记录的。

否定句:
