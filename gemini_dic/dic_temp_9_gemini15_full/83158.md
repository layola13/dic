It can also refer to a person of low moral character. The word evokes images of the grime and decay found in gutters. |例句：The alley reeked of a gutterling mixture of refuse and stagnant water. 这条小巷散发着垃圾和污水的下水道污水混合物的恶臭。  He considered his rival a gutterling, unfit for polite society. 他认为他的对手是个卑鄙小人，不配进入上流社会。|近义词：filth, dregs, scum, rabble|反义词：purity, refinement, elite
