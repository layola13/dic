dragoness:/ˈdræɡənɛs/| n. |母龙，雌龙 |A female dragon.  The word is formed by adding the feminine suffix "-ess" to "dragon".  It is less common than "dragon," but conveys a specific gender. |Example:  The dragoness guarded her hoard fiercely. 这条母龙凶猛地守护着她的宝藏。|近义词：female dragon|反义词：dragon (in the sense of implying a male dragon)
