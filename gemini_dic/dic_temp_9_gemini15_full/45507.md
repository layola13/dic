cure-alls:/ˈkjʊrɔːlz/ | n. | 万灵药；包治百病的药；| a remedy or solution claimed to cure all diseases or problems.  It often implies that the cure is ineffective or even misleading. |  He was selling a cure-all for cancer, which turned out to be a scam. 他在兜售一种包治癌症的万灵药，结果被证明是个骗局。| 近义词：panacea, universal remedy | 反义词：ineffective treatment, placebo
