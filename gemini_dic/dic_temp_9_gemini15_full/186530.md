rictal:/ˈrɪktəl/| adj.|口角的，嘴角的；喙的|Relating to the angle of the mouth or beak.  The term is often used in zoology and ornithology to describe features related to the mouth or beak of an animal.|例句：The bird exhibited a distinctive rictal bristle pattern.  这只鸟表现出独特的口角须结构。|例句：The study focused on the rictal flanges of several bat species. 这项研究侧重于几种蝙蝠物种的口角缘。|近义词：oral, buccal (partially)|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be related to features far from the mouth.
