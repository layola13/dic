positivist:/ˈpɒzɪtɪvɪst/ /ˈpɑːzɪtɪvɪst/| n. adj.| 实证主义者；实证主义的 | A positivist believes that knowledge can only be gained through sensory experience and scientific methods.  实证主义者相信知识只能通过感官经验和科学方法获得。|  The positivist approach to social science emphasizes empirical observation and quantitative data. 实证主义的社会科学方法强调经验观察和定量数据。|empiricist, scientist| idealist, metaphysician| 名词，形容词
