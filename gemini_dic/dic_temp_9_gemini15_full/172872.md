quaffed:/kwɑːft/| vt.|痛饮；大口喝|To drink heartily or in one or more large draughts.|He quaffed a mug of ale. 他痛饮了一大杯啤酒。|She quaffed the champagne. 她痛饮香槟。|drink, gulp, swig|sip, nibble|动词
