revamping:/ˌriːˈvæmpɪŋ/| v.|改造，翻新，改进|The act of renovating, repairing, or improving something, often to give it a new look or function.  It implies a significant change or overhaul, not just minor adjustments.|例句：The company is revamping its marketing strategy to attract younger customers. 公司正在改进其营销策略以吸引年轻客户。|例句：They're revamping the old building into a modern apartment complex. 他们正在将旧建筑改造成现代化的公寓楼。|近义词：renovate, refurbish, overhaul, modernize, update|反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin
