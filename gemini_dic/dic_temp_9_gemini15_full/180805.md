rehammer:/ˌriːˈhæmər/| vt.|重新锤打，重击|To hammer again; to strike repeatedly with a hammer.|例句：The blacksmith had to rehammer the horseshoe to make it fit.  铁匠不得不重新锤打马蹄铁以使其合适。
例句：He rehammered the dented panel until it was smooth. 他反复锤打凹陷的面板直到它变得平滑。|近义词：reforge, rework, restrike|反义词：polish, smooth (as antonyms to the action of hammering)|动词
