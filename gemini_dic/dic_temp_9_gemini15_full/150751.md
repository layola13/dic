paracrostic: /ˌpærəˈkrɒstɪk/ | adj. |  旁文字谜的；副标题的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a paracrostic, a type of word puzzle where the solution is found by reading selected letters from a vertical or diagonal sequence within a longer text.  It differs from an acrostic where the answer is spelled out by the first letter of each line. |  The cryptic crossword contained a cleverly hidden paracrostic.  | 近义词:  cryptic, enigmatical | 反义词:  obvious, straightforward
