hornfish:/ˈhɔːrnfɪʃ/| n. |角鱼|A hornfish is a type of fish characterized by a long, slender body and a sharp, pointed snout resembling a horn.  They are often found in saltwater environments. |例句：The fisherman caught a large hornfish near the reef.  渔夫在礁石附近钓到了一条大角鱼。
The hornfish darted away quickly when the shadow passed over it.  角鱼在影子掠过时迅速逃走了。|近义词：garfish, needlefish|反义词：None specifically.
