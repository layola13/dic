lullabied:/ˈlʌlˌeɪbiːd/ | v. | 哄睡；轻声细语地唱歌使…入睡 | To soothe or lull to sleep with a lullaby.  | 例句：The mother lullabied her baby to sleep. 妈妈轻声哼着歌把婴儿哄睡了。 | 例句：The gentle music lullabied me into a deep slumber. 轻柔的音乐把我哄睡了。| 近义词：soothed, calmed, lulled | 反义词：awakened, roused
