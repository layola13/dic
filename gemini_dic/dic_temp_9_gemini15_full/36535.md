collaborations:/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |合作，协作|The act of working with someone or something else to create or achieve something.  It often implies a joint effort involving multiple parties. |例句：The success of the project was due to the excellent collaborations between the different teams.  这个项目的成功归功于不同团队之间的出色合作。

例句：The artist's new album showcases collaborations with several renowned musicians.这位艺术家的新专辑展示了与几位著名音乐家的合作。


近义词： partnerships, joint ventures, teamwork, alliances

反义词： isolation, individualism, competition


词源:  From "collaborate," which comes from the French "collaborer," meaning "to work together." The French word itself is a combination of "col-" (meaning "together") and "laborer" (meaning "to work").
