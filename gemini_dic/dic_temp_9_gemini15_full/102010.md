isodrome:/ˈaɪsəˌdroʊm/| n. |等值线；等值图|A line on a map connecting points of equal range or distance from a source.  It can refer to lines connecting points of equal depth, equal speed, equal temperature, or other equal values. |例句：The isodromes on this map show the equal-distance contours from the epicenter of the earthquake.|近义词：isopleth, contour line|反义词：N/A
