kittiwake:/ˈkɪtɪweɪk/| n. |海鸥的一种|A small white seabird of the gull family, with a short, stout bill. Kittiwakes are known for their acrobatic flight and their ability to nest on cliffs.|例句：We saw a flock of kittiwakes circling the cliffs. 我们看到一群海鸥在悬崖上盘旋。|例句：The kittiwake chicks were huddled together for warmth.  海鸥幼雏挤在一起取暖。|近义词：gull, seabird|反义词：无
