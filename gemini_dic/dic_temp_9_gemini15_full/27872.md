canopying:/ˈkænəpiɪŋ/| v.|树冠覆盖；遮盖；|The act of forming a canopy or being covered by a canopy.  It often refers to trees forming a dense overhead cover.|例句：The forest canopy was so dense that little sunlight reached the forest floor. 森林的树冠非常茂密，几乎没有阳光能照射到地面。

例句：The branches of the old oak tree canopied the picnic area, offering shade from the sun. 老橡树的树枝遮盖了野餐区，提供了遮阳。


近义词：overarching, overshadowing, sheltering|反义词：uncovering, exposing|词性：动词
