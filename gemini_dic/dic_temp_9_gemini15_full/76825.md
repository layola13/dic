gandalf:/ˈɡændælf/| n.|甘道夫（指环王中的巫师）|A powerful wizard in J.R.R. Tolkien's fantasy world of Middle-earth, known for his wisdom, magical abilities, and guidance to the Fellowship of the Ring.  He is a member of the order of Istari.|例句：Gandalf the Grey guided the Fellowship on their quest to destroy the One Ring.  甘道夫灰袍引导着魔戒圣战的成员去摧毁至尊魔戒。

例句：In the books and films, Gandalf is a key figure in the struggle against Sauron. 在书籍和电影中，甘道夫是反抗索伦斗争中的关键人物。|近义词：wizard, sorcerer, mage |反义词：none.
