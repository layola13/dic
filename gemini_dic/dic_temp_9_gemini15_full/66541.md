exhaust /ɪɡˈzɔːst/ | n. | 废气；排气；精疲力竭 |  The exhaust fumes from the cars filled the street.  汽车的废气充满了街道。|  The long journey left me completely exhausted. 漫长的旅程让我筋疲力尽。|  emission, fumes |  rest, energy | 名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car exhausts noxious fumes. = 这辆汽车排放有害气体。
The factory exhausts a lot of smoke. = 这家工厂排放大量的烟雾。

一般过去时:
The engine exhausted all its fuel. = 引擎用光了所有的燃料。
The runner exhausted himself during the race. = 跑步者在比赛中筋疲力尽了。


现在完成时:
They have exhausted their resources. = 他们已经耗尽了资源。
She has exhausted all her options. = 她已经用尽了所有办法。


过去完成时:
He had exhausted his patience with her. = 他对她已经失去了耐心。
The soldiers had exhausted their ammunition. = 士兵们已经弹尽粮绝了。


将来时:
We will exhaust our supplies soon. = 我们很快就会用完我们的补给。
They will exhaust their savings on the new house. = 他们会在新房子上花光积蓄。


虚拟语气:
If we exhausted our funds, we would have to borrow money. = 如果我们用光了资金，我们就得借钱。
I wish I hadn't exhausted myself before the game. = 我真希望比赛前我没有把自己累垮。


被动语态:
The resources were exhausted quickly. = 资源很快就被耗尽了。
The patient was exhausted by the treatment. = 病人被治疗弄得筋疲力尽了。


疑问句:
Has the car exhausted its fuel? = 车子用光汽油了吗？
Are you exhausted from the workout? = 你运动完累了吗？


否定句:
The project didn't exhaust our budget. = 这个项目没有耗尽我们的预算。
I am not exhausted yet. = 我还没累呢。

词源:
源自中古英语 *exausten，来自古法语 *exhauster，源自拉丁语 *exhaurire，意为“抽干，耗尽”。
