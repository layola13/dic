overresoluteness: /ˌoʊvərˌrezəˈluːtnəs/ | n. |过度坚决；过于果断 | Excessive firmness or determination; often to a fault.  It suggests an inflexibility that prevents consideration of alternative viewpoints or compromise.  | 例句：His overresoluteness in pursuing this strategy, despite clear warnings, led to the company's downfall.  他的策略过于坚决，尽管有明确的警告，最终导致公司倒闭。

例句：Her overresoluteness prevented her from seeing the flaws in her plan. 她的过度坚决让她看不到计划中的缺陷。


近义词：obstinacy, inflexibility, stubbornness, dogmatism, rigidity


反义词：flexibility, adaptability, open-mindedness, compromise, yielding


词性：名词
