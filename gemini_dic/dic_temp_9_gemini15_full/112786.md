lubricators:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪtərz/| n.|润滑器，加润滑剂者|A lubricator is a device or person that lubricates something.  It can refer to a mechanical device that applies lubricant to machinery, or a person who applies lubricant. |例句：The mechanic used a grease lubricator to oil the engine.  机械师用黄油润滑器给发动机加油。

The factory uses automatic lubricators to keep the machinery running smoothly. 工厂使用自动润滑器以保持机器平稳运行。|近义词：oiler, greaser |反义词：None specifically, but words implying dryness or friction could be considered antonyms in context.
