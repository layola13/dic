nasopalatal:/ˌneɪzoʊˈpælətl/| adj.|鼻腭的|Relating to both the nose and the palate.  The term refers to the anatomical area where the nasal cavity and the palate (roof of the mouth) meet.|Example: The nasopalatal valve plays a crucial role in speech production. 鼻腭瓣在语音产生中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：nasal, palatal|反义词：None


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb or noun forms that follow typical tense conjugations.  Therefore, tense-based examples are not applicable.
