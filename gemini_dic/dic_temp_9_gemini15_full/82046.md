grist:/ɡrɪst/| n.|磨粉的谷物；（供讨论或利用的）材料；|The material with which a mill works; something that is ground or processed.  It can also refer to material used for discussion or other purposes.|例句：The farmer took his grist to the mill. 农民把他的谷物拿到磨坊去磨。


The news provided plenty of grist for the gossip mill.  这个新闻为流言蜚语提供了很多素材。|近义词：material, substance, fodder|反义词：None specifically.  The opposite would be a lack of material or something unusable.
