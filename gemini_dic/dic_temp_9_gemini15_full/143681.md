outlaw:/ˈaʊtlɔː/| n. v. |歹徒；逃犯；被逐者；宣布...为非法；使...成为非法|a person who has broken the law and is hiding from the police; to make something illegal|The outlaw was finally caught after years on the run.  这个逃犯最终在逃亡多年后被抓获。|He was outlawed for treason. 他因叛国罪而被通缉。|criminal, fugitive, bandit |law-abiding citizen, legal resident |名词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They outlaw certain activities in this city. = 他们在这个城市禁止某些活动。
The government outlaws the sale of illegal drugs. = 政府取缔非法毒品的销售。

一般过去时:
The sheriff outlawed gambling in the town. = 治安官在镇上禁止赌博。
They outlawed the practice of dueling. = 他们取缔了决斗的做法。

现在完成时:
Many countries have outlawed the production of certain weapons. = 许多国家已经禁止生产某些武器。
They have outlawed smoking in public places. = 他们已经禁止在公共场所吸烟。

过去完成时:
Before the new law, they had outlawed the import of certain goods. = 在新法律颁布之前，他们已经禁止进口某些商品。
The tribe had outlawed the use of fire arms for hunting. = 该部落已经禁止使用火器进行狩猎。


将来时:
They will outlaw the use of plastic bags next year. = 他们明年将禁止使用塑料袋。
The council will outlaw the construction of new high-rises in the area. = 理事会将禁止在该地区建造新的高层建筑。

虚拟语气:
If they outlawed that substance, crime would decrease. = 如果他们取缔那种物质，犯罪就会减少。
I wish they would outlaw those harmful chemicals. = 我希望他们能禁止使用那些有害的化学物质。


被动语态:
Gambling was outlawed in the state in 1920. = 1920 年，该州禁止赌博。
The sale of that product has been outlawed in many countries. = 许多国家已经禁止销售该产品。

疑问句:
Has smoking been outlawed in your country? = 贵国是否禁止吸烟？
Will they outlaw the use of drones in the future? = 他们将来会禁止使用无人机吗？

否定句:
They did not outlaw the practice. = 他们没有禁止这种做法。
The government has not yet outlawed the use of those pesticides. = 政府尚未禁止使用那些杀虫剂。
