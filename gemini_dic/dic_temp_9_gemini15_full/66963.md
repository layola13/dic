experimentalize:/ɪkˌsperɪˈmɛntəlaɪz/| v.|进行实验；试验|To perform experiments; to try something new or different, often in a scientific or systematic way.  |The scientists experimentalized with different chemicals to find a cure.|近义词：experiment, test, trial|反义词：prove, verify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They experimentalize with new teaching methods. = 他们正在尝试新的教学方法。
Scientists experimentalize daily in their labs. = 科学家们每天都在实验室里做实验。

一般过去时:
The researchers experimentalized extensively on the subject. = 研究人员对这个课题进行了广泛的实验。
He experimentalized with various paint colors. = 他尝试了各种颜色的油漆。

现在完成时:
We have experimentalized with several approaches to solve the problem. = 我们已经尝试了几种解决问题的方法。
They have experimentalized extensively in this area of research. = 他们在该研究领域进行了大量的实验。

过去完成时:
She had experimentalized with different techniques before finding the right one. = 在找到正确的方法之前，她尝试过不同的技术。
The team had experimentalized for months before publishing their findings. = 该团队在发表研究成果之前已经实验了几个月。

将来时:
They will experimentalize further to confirm the results. = 他们将进一步进行实验以确认结果。
We will experimentalize with a new marketing strategy. = 我们将尝试一种新的营销策略。

虚拟语气:
If they experimentalized more carefully, they might have gotten better results. = 如果他们更仔细地进行实验，他们可能会得到更好的结果。
I wish I had experimentalized with different materials earlier. = 我希望我早些时候尝试过不同的材料。

被动语态:
New techniques are being experimentalized on in the field. = 该领域正在尝试新的技术。
The new drug is being experimentalized on animals before human trials. = 在进行人体试验之前，这种新药正在动物身上进行试验。

疑问句:
Are they experimentalizing with the new software? = 他们正在尝试新软件吗？
Have you ever experimentalized with home brewing? = 你曾经尝试过家庭酿造吗？

否定句:
They did not experimentalize enough to draw firm conclusions. = 他们没有进行足够的实验来得出确定的结论。
We chose not to experimentalize with the untested method. = 我们选择不尝试未经测试的方法。
