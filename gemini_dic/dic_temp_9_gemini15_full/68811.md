fasciculate:/ˈfæsɪkjuleɪt/ | v. | 使成束；丛生；|To arrange or grow in bundles or clusters.  The word implies a grouping of similar elements into a larger, more organized structure. |Example: The flower stalks fasciculate at the base of the plant.  花茎在植物底部丛生。

The leaves fasciculate in groups of three. 叶子三叶丛生。|近义词：cluster, bunch, group |反义词：scatter, disperse |动词
