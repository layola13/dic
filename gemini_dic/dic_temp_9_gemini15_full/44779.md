crun:/krʌn/| v.|嘎吱嘎吱地响；用力咀嚼；压碎|To make a crunching sound; to chew something noisily and forcefully; to crush something.|例句：The leaves crunched underfoot. 树叶在脚下嘎吱作响。The dog crunched on the bone. 狗用力啃着骨头。|近义词：crunch, crush, grind|反义词:glide, flow


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The leaves crunch underfoot. = 树叶在脚下嘎吱作响。
He crunches on the ice cream noisily. = 他大声地咀嚼着冰淇淋。

一般过去时:
The snow crunched under my boots. = 雪在我的靴子下嘎吱作响。
She crunched the nuts between her teeth. = 她用牙齿咬碎坚果。

现在完成时:
We have crunched all the data. = 我们已经处理完所有数据。(此处"crunch"引申为“处理大量数据”)
He has crunched the numbers and found the answer. = 他已经计算过数字并找到了答案。(此处"crunch"引申为“计算”)

过去完成时:
They had crunched the numbers before presenting the report. = 他们提交报告前已经计算过数字。
The car had crunched into the wall before I could brake. = 在我能够刹车之前，汽车已经撞到了墙上。

将来时:
The ice will crunch under your weight. = 冰会在你的体重下嘎吱作响。
I will crunch the data tomorrow. = 我明天将处理数据。

虚拟语气:
If I crunched the numbers, I might find a solution. = 如果我计算这些数字，我可能会找到解决方案。
I wish I hadn't crunched those crackers so loudly. = 我真希望我没有那么大声地吃那些饼干。

被动语态:
The data was crunched by the computer. = 数据是由电脑处理的。
The bones were crunched by the dog. = 骨头被狗咬碎了。

疑问句:
Does the snow crunch under your feet? = 雪会在你的脚下嘎吱作响吗？
Will the data be crunched by the end of the week? = 数据会在本周末处理完毕吗？

否定句:
The gravel didn't crunch under the tires. = 碎石在轮胎下没有嘎吱作响。
He didn't crunch the numbers properly. = 他没有正确地计算数字。
