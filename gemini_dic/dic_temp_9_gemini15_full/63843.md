epiglottiditis: /ˌɛpɪɡlɒtɪˈdaɪtɪs/ | n. | 会厌炎 | Inflammation of the epiglottis, a flap of cartilage at the root of the tongue, which prevents food from entering the windpipe during swallowing.  The inflammation can be caused by a bacterial or viral infection. Symptoms include difficulty swallowing, sore throat, fever, and drooling.  |  The doctor diagnosed her with epiglottiditis after examining her throat.  医生检查了她的喉咙后，诊断她患有会厌炎。| laryngitis, tonsillitis |  healthy epiglottis |
