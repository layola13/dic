reconfiscate:/ˌriːˈkɒnfɪskeɪt/ | vt. |重新没收；再次充公 | To confiscate something again; to seize something that has already been confiscated.  The term implies a second or subsequent seizure, often after an initial confiscation has been overturned or ignored. |例句：The government reconfiscated the property after the initial court ruling was reversed. = 政府在最初的法院裁决被推翻后重新没收了该房产。

例句：The authorities reconfiscated the smuggled goods after they were briefly released. = 当局在走私货物被短暂释放后再次没收了它们。


近义词：seize, impound, appropriate


反义词：release, return, restore


词性:动词

词源:
The word "reconfiscate" is a combination of the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back") and the verb "confiscate" (from Latin *confiscare, from *confiscare "to put into the treasury," from *con- "together" + *fiscus "treasury").  Therefore, it literally means "to confiscate again."
