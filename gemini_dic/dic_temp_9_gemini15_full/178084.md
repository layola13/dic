reclining:/rɪˈklaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 躺着的；斜倚着的 |  Describes something in a leaning or lying-down position, often suggesting comfort and relaxation.  | Example:  He enjoyed a reclining position in the hammock. 他享受着吊床上斜倚的姿势。  |近义词：leaning, lounging, supine, prone | 反义词：upright, erect, standing |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun is reclining in the west. = 太阳正缓缓西下。
She is reclining on the sofa, reading a book. = 她斜躺在沙发上看书。


一般过去时:
He reclined in his chair after a long day. = 长途跋涉后，他靠在椅子上休息。
They reclined on the beach, enjoying the sunset. = 他们躺在沙滩上，享受着日落。


现在完成时:
She has reclined by the pool all afternoon. = 她整个下午都躺在游泳池边。
He has reclined on the couch since arriving home. = 他自从回家后就一直斜躺在沙发上。


过去完成时:
He had reclined for hours before the phone rang. = 电话响之前他已经躺了几个小时了。
They had reclined on the grass, watching the clouds drift by. = 他们躺在草地上，看着云朵飘过。


将来时:
I will recline by the fire tonight. = 今晚我要靠在火炉边休息。
We will recline on the deck chairs, watching the ocean. = 我们将躺在躺椅上，看着大海。


虚拟语气:
If I had a hammock, I would recline in it all day. = 如果我有一张吊床，我就会整天躺在上面。
I wish I could recline on a beach right now. = 我真希望现在能躺在沙滩上。


被动语态:
The chair is designed to be reclined in. = 这把椅子设计成可以斜靠的。
The seats in the theatre can be reclined. = 剧院的座位可以放倒。


疑问句:
Is she reclining comfortably? = 她躺得舒服吗？
Are they reclining by the pool? = 他们躺在游泳池边吗？


否定句:
He did not recline; he sat upright. = 他没有躺下；他是坐直的。
They were not reclining; they were standing and talking. = 他们没有躺着；他们站着说话。


PHRASE->
reclining chair = 躺椅
reclining sofa = 沙发床
reclining position = 躺卧姿势
fully reclining = 完全躺平
reclining figure = 躺卧的人像


词源:
From the Old French *recliner, from Latin reclinare, from re- + clinare "to bend, incline".  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *klei- "to lean, bend".
