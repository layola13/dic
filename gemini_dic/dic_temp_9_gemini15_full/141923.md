ordinarier:/ɔːrdɪˈnɛəriər/| n. |平凡的人；庸俗的人；平庸之辈 | An ordinarier is a person who is considered to be ordinary, commonplace, or lacking in distinction or refinement.  They may be seen as dull, uninteresting, or even vulgar.  The term often carries a negative connotation. |He considered himself above the ordinariers of the town. 他认为自己比镇上的那些庸俗之辈高出一筹。|近义词：commoner, nobody, philistine |反义词：extraordinary person, elite, celebrity |名词
