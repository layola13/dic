rows:/roʊz/ | n. | 行；排；列 | A series of things arranged in a line.  The spreadsheet has ten rows and five columns. | 近义词：line, rank, string | 反义词：column


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are many rows of seats in the stadium. = 体育场里有许多排座位。
The farmer plants his crops in neat rows. = 农民整齐地种植他的庄稼。


一般过去时:
The soldiers marched in straight rows. = 士兵们排成直线行进。
She planted the flowers in rows along the path. = 她沿着小路一排排地种花。


现在完成时:
We have already planted all the rows of potatoes. = 我们已经种完了所有土豆。
They have rearranged the rows of books in the library. = 他们已经重新整理了图书馆的书架。


过去完成时:
The gardener had already weeded the rows before the rain started. = 下雨之前，园丁已经锄过草了。
They had finished planting the rows of corn before sunset. = 日落之前，他们已经种完了玉米。


将来时:
We will plant more rows of vegetables next spring. = 明年春天我们将种植更多的蔬菜。
The construction workers will lay the bricks in neat rows. = 建筑工人将会把砖头整齐地排列好。


虚拟语气:
If we had more space, we could plant more rows of flowers. = 如果我们有更多空间，我们可以种更多的花。
I wish I had planted the rows of seeds earlier. = 我真希望我早点种下种子。


被动语态:
The rows of chairs were arranged for the meeting. = 椅子被排成排准备会议。
The seeds were planted in carefully spaced rows. = 种子被种在精心间隔的行里。


疑问句:
How many rows of seats are there in the theater? = 剧院有多少排座位？
Are the plants growing well in the rows? = 植物在行里长得好吗？


否定句:
The rows of trees were not straight. = 树木的行不直。
He didn't plant the seeds in straight rows. = 他没有把种子种成直线。
