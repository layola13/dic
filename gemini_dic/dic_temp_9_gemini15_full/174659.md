/kwɒdiz/ | n. | 小海湾，小港湾 | A small, sheltered bay or cove, especially one on a rocky coast.  Quoddies are often characterized by their relatively calm waters and are frequently used as sheltered anchorages for small boats. | Example: The fishing boats sought shelter from the storm in the quiet quoddy.  渔船在宁静的小海湾中躲避风暴。|近义词：cove, inlet, creek | 反义词：ocean, sea
