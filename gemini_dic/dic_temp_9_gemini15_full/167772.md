primates:/ˈpraɪmeɪts/| n. |灵长类动物|Primates are mammals characterized by features such as relatively large brains, forward-facing eyes, and grasping hands and feet.  They include monkeys, apes, and humans.|例句：Humans are primates, belonging to the order Primates. 人类是灵长类动物，属于灵长目。|例句：The study focused on the social behavior of various primate species. 这项研究关注各种灵长类动物的社会行为。|近义词：mammals, apes, monkeys|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with other animal orders.
