nonnotification:/ˌnɒnˌnəʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |未通知；未告知 |The absence of notification; the state of not being notified.  This word highlights the lack of any formal or informal communication regarding a particular event, situation, or decision. It emphasizes the passive nature of the absence of information.  |Example: The nonnotification of the change in policy caused widespread confusion.  未更改政策的通知造成了广泛的混乱。|近义词：lack of notification, omission, failure to notify |反义词：notification, informing, announcement |名词
