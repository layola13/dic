noematic:/noʊˈmætɪk/| adj.|意念的；意象的；观念的 | Relating to or of the nature of noema; the intentional object of consciousness.  The term is primarily used in phenomenology, referring to the intentional content of consciousness, the "what" that is intended or presented to consciousness.  It contrasts with the noesis, which is the act of consciousness itself. | Example:  The noematic aspect of perception is the perceived object, while the noetic aspect is the act of perceiving.  意向性的内容，即意识所指向的“事物”本身。知觉的意念性方面是被感知的对象，而认知性方面是感知的行为。 | 近义词: intentional, mental, conceptual | 反义词: physical, real
