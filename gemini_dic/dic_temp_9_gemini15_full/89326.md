homogenization: /ˌhoʊmədʒənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 均质化；均化；使均匀 | The process of making something uniform or homogeneous in composition or character.  This often involves breaking down larger particles or clumps into smaller, more evenly distributed units.  It's used in various fields, from food processing to materials science. | Example: The homogenization of milk prevents the cream from separating.  牛奶的均质化防止了奶油的分离。

The homogenization of the soil improved crop yields. 土壤的均质化提高了作物产量。


近义词：uniformity, standardization, equalization

反义词：heterogenization, stratification, segregation

词性：名词
