exhibit:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/ | n., vt. | 展览品；展览；展示；表现 | To show something publicly; a thing shown publicly. |  例句：The museum is hosting an exhibit of Impressionist paintings. = 博物馆正在举办印象派绘画展览。 |例句：She exhibited remarkable courage in the face of danger. = 面对危险，她表现出非凡的勇气。 |近义词：display, show, demonstrate, presentation |反义词：conceal, hide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum exhibits ancient artifacts. = 博物馆展出古代文物。
He exhibits signs of stress. = 他表现出压力迹象。


一般过去时:
They exhibited their artwork at the gallery. = 他们在画廊展出他们的艺术作品。
She exhibited great patience with the children. = 她对孩子们表现出极大的耐心。


现在完成时:
The artist has exhibited his paintings in many cities. =这位艺术家已经在许多城市展出过他的画作。
He has exhibited a remarkable talent for music. = 他展现出非凡的音乐天赋。


过去完成时:
The company had exhibited its new product at the trade show before the official launch. = 公司在正式发布之前已在贸易展会上展出了其新产品。
She had exhibited similar symptoms before. = 她以前也表现出类似的症状。


将来时:
They will exhibit their latest collection next month. = 他们下个月将展出最新的系列作品。
He will exhibit his paintings at the upcoming art fair. = 他将在即将到来的艺术博览会上展出他的绘画作品。


虚拟语气:
If he exhibited more confidence, he would be more successful. = 如果他表现出更多自信，他会更成功。
I wish she had exhibited more understanding. = 我希望她能表现出更多的理解。


被动语态:
The paintings were exhibited in a prestigious gallery. = 这些画作在一个著名的画廊展出。
His talent was exhibited early in his career. = 他的天赋在他职业生涯早期就展现出来了。


疑问句:
Will the paintings be exhibited in the main hall? = 这些画作会在主展厅展出吗？
Does he often exhibit his work? = 他经常展出他的作品吗？


否定句:
The museum didn't exhibit any contemporary art. = 博物馆没有展出任何当代艺术。
He didn't exhibit any signs of nervousness. = 他没有表现出任何紧张的迹象。


PHRASE->
