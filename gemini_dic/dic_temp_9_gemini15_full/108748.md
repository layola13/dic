learn of: /lɜːrn ɒv/ | phrasal verb | 得知；获悉 | To become aware of something; to receive information about something. |例句：I learned of his death from a mutual friend.  我从一位共同的朋友那里得知了他的死讯。
I learned of the accident through the news. 我通过新闻得知了这起事故。|近义词：discover, find out, hear about |反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We learn of new developments regularly. = 我们定期了解新的进展。
They learn of important events through social media. = 他们通过社交媒体了解重要事件。


一般过去时:
She learned of the promotion last week. = 她上周得知了晋升的消息。
He learned of the cancellation this morning. = 他今天早上得知了取消的消息。


现在完成时:
I have learned of several new opportunities. = 我已经了解到几个新的机会。
We have learned of significant changes in the market. = 我们已经了解到市场上的一些重大变化。


过去完成时:
They had learned of the problem before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经了解到这个问题。
She had learned of his departure before he left. = 他离开之前，她就得知了他要离开的消息。


将来时:
You will learn of the results next week. = 你下周会知道结果。
We will learn of any updates as soon as possible. = 我们会尽快了解任何更新。


虚拟语气:
If I had learned of the problem earlier, I would have acted differently. = 如果我早点知道这个问题，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had learned of the opportunity sooner. = 我希望我早点知道这个机会。


被动语态:
The news was learned of by many people quickly. = 这个消息很快就被很多人知道了。(This is an awkward phrasing.  Passive voice is not commonly used with "learn of".)
The changes were learned of only after they were implemented. = 只有在这些改变实施之后，人们才得知这些改变。(This is also an awkward phrasing.  Passive voice is not commonly used with "learn of".)


疑问句:
When did you learn of the incident? = 你是什么时候知道这件事的？
How did they learn of the emergency? = 他们是怎么知道紧急情况的？


否定句:
I did not learn of his arrival until later. = 直到后来我才得知他到了。
She hadn't learned of the changes before the meeting. = 会议之前她还没有了解到这些变化。


PHRASE->
learn of something new = 了解一些新事物
learn of an opportunity = 了解一个机会
learn of a problem = 了解一个问题
learn of a death = 得知某人去世
learn of a situation = 了解某种情况
