prespecialize:/ˌpriːˈspeʃəlaɪz/| v. |预先专门化；提前专门化 |To specialize in advance; to focus on a particular area of study or expertise before it is formally required or expected.  The term is relatively uncommon and often used in academic or professional contexts.  |例句：The students prespecialized in organic chemistry before starting their graduate studies. 学生们在开始研究生学习之前就提前专门学习了有机化学。

例句：The company prespecialized in software development to gain a competitive edge in the market. 公司提前专门从事软件开发以获得市场竞争优势。


近义词：predetermine, specialize beforehand, focus early on


反义词：generalize, diversify
