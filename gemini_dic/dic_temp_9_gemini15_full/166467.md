prerighteous:/ˌpriːˈraɪtʃəs/| adj.|过分正直的；假装正直的；自以为是的|Excessively or ostentatiously righteous; self-righteously moralistic.  It implies a pretense of righteousness, often masking hypocrisy or self-interest.|例句：His prerighteous pronouncements on morality rang hollow given his own shady dealings.  他的关于道德的过分正直的声明，鉴于他自己的不正当行为，听起来空洞无物。

例句：She adopted a prerighteous tone, criticizing everyone else's flaws while ignoring her own. 她摆出一副过分正直的姿态，批评其他人的缺点，却忽略了自己的缺点。


近义词：self-righteous, sanctimonious, holier-than-thou, pietistic


反义词：humble, unassuming, modest, self-effacing


词性：形容词
