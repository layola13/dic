sabbatarianism:/ˌsæbəˈteəriənɪzəm/| n. |安息日教义；严格遵守安息日的习俗|The strict observance of the Sabbath, often involving the prohibition of certain activities on that day.  Sabbatarianism emphasizes the religious significance of the Sabbath and its observance as a day of rest and worship.  Different religious groups have varying interpretations and practices regarding sabbatarianism.|The strict sabbatarianism of the Puritans led to laws prohibiting work and recreation on Sundays.|近义词：Sabbath observance, Sabbath keeping|反义词：Secularism,  Profanation of the Sabbath
