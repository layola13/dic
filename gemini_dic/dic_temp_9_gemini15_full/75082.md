frijole:/frɪˈhoʊli/ | n. | 芸豆；菜豆 | A small, dried bean, typically kidney-shaped, often used in Mexican and Southwestern cuisine.  They are also called kidney beans. | Example:  We used frijoles in the chili. = 我们在辣椒里用了芸豆。

Example: This recipe calls for pinto frijoles. = 这个食谱需要用斑豆。

近义词：kidney bean, bean

反义词：无
