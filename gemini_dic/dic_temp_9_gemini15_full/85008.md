harangued:/həˈræŋɡd/| v.| 冗长地训斥；大声疾呼；滔滔不绝地演说 | To deliver a long, passionate, and typically angry speech, often to a large audience.  |例句：The politician harangued the crowd about the dangers of the proposed legislation.  这位政治家向人群慷慨激昂地痛斥了拟议立法中的危险。

The speaker harangued the audience for over an hour. 演讲者滔滔不绝地对听众讲了一个多小时。|近义词：lectured, scolded, addressed, tirade, declaimed |反义词：praised, complimented, soothed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harangues the audience every week. 他每周都向观众发表长篇大论。
She harangues her children about their chores. 她经常唠叨她的孩子做家务。

一般过去时:
The general harangued his troops before the battle.  将军在战斗前向他的部队发表了慷慨激昂的演说。
He harangued me for hours about my mistakes. 他就我的错误对我进行了几个小时的训斥。

现在完成时:
The professor has harangued his students about plagiarism all semester.  教授整个学期都在向学生们大讲剽窃的危害。
She has harangued the board for years about budget cuts.  多年来，她一直在向董事会大声疾呼预算削减的问题。

过去完成时:
He had harangued the crowd for an hour before they finally dispersed.  他在人群中慷慨激昂地讲了一个小时，然后他们才最终散去。
They had harangued the government about the issue for months before any action was taken. 他们在采取任何行动之前，已经就这个问题向政府大声疾呼了好几个月。


将来时:
The candidate will harangue the voters on the economy next week.  候选人下周将向选民们就经济问题发表慷慨激昂的演说。
He will harangue his employees about productivity. 他将向他的员工们大声疾呼提高生产力。

虚拟语气:
If he harangued them less, they might listen more. 如果他少训斥他们一些，他们可能会多听一些。
I wish he hadn't harangued me so harshly. 我真希望他不要那样严厉地训斥我。

被动语态:
The audience was harangued by the speaker for hours.  听众被演讲者滔滔不绝地训斥了好几个小时。
The students were frequently harangued by the strict teacher.  学生们经常受到那位严厉老师的训斥。

疑问句:
Was the crowd harangued by the politician?  政治家是否对人群进行了慷慨激昂的演说？
Why was he harangued by his boss? 为什么他的老板要训斥他？

否定句:
He didn't harangue the group; he simply explained the situation. 他没有对小组进行训斥；他只是简单地解释了情况。
She didn't harangue her employees; she simply requested their cooperation. 她没有训斥她的员工；她只是请求他们的合作。


PHRASE->
harangue against:  反对...而大声疾呼
harangue on:  就...而滔滔不绝地演说
harangue about:  就...而冗长地训斥
harangue someone into:  通过训斥说服某人做某事
harangue someone out of: 通过训斥劝说某人不要做某事
