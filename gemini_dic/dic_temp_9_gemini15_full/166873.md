pressuring: /ˈprɛʃərɪŋ/ | v. |施压，迫使 | The act of putting pressure on someone or something.  It implies trying to influence someone to do something they may not want to do. | 例句：He's pressuring me to make a decision. 他在逼我做决定。


例句：The company is pressuring its employees to work overtime. 公司正在强迫员工加班。


近义词：coercing, compelling, forcing, urging


反义词：supporting, encouraging, assisting


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pressuring his son to study medicine. 他正在逼迫他的儿子学习医学。
They are pressuring the government for change. 他们正在向政府施压要求改变。


一般过去时:
She pressured him into signing the contract. 她迫使他签署了合同。
The boss pressured the employees to meet the deadline. 老板给员工施压让他们赶上截止日期。


现在完成时:
They have been pressuring me all week. 他们整个星期都在给我施压。
The media has pressured the company to respond. 媒体向公司施压要求其做出回应。


过去完成时:
They had pressured him into confessing. 他们之前已经迫使他承认了。
She had pressured her friend to quit her job. 她之前已经劝说她的朋友辞职了。


将来时:
He will pressure her to accept the offer. 他将向她施压让她接受这份工作。
They will pressure the authorities to act. 他们将向当局施压促使其采取行动。


虚拟语气:
If they pressured him harder, he might confess. 如果他们对他施加更大的压力，他可能会招供。
I wish they weren't pressuring me so much. 我希望他们不要给我这么大的压力。


被动语态:
He was pressured into resigning. 他被迫辞职。
The workers were pressured to accept lower wages. 工人们被迫接受较低的工资。


疑问句:
Are they pressuring you to leave? 他们正在逼你离开吗？
Is the government pressuring the company? 政府正在给这家公司施压吗？


否定句:
They did not pressure me to make a decision. 他们没有逼我做决定。
He wasn't pressured into signing the deal. 他并没有被迫签署协议。


PHRASE->
under pressure: 在压力下
put pressure on: 向…施压
come under pressure: 承受压力
feel the pressure: 感到压力
release the pressure: 释放压力
