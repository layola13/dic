real-life: /ˈriːəl laɪf/ | adj. | 真实的；现实生活的 |  Relating to or characteristic of everyday life as opposed to fiction or imagination.  |例句：He's facing real-life problems, not just fantasy. 他面临的是现实问题，而不是幻想。

例句：The movie portrays a real-life situation. 这部电影描绘了一个现实生活中的情景。


近义词：actual, authentic, genuine | 反义词：fictional, imaginary, unreal | 词性：形容词
