potpourri: /ˌpɒtˈpʊriː/ /ˌpɑːtˈpʊriː/ | n. | 混杂物；杂烩；（干花）香包 | A mixture of various things; a medley.  Potpourri can refer to a mixture of dried flowers and spices used as a scent or a collection of diverse things. | 例句：The antique shop was a potpourri of furniture, artwork, and trinkets. 这家古董店里摆满了各种各样的家具、艺术品和小玩意儿。

The garden was a potpourri of colors and fragrances. 花园里五彩缤纷，芳香扑鼻。


近义词：medley, mixture, assortment, miscellany | 反义词：uniformity, homogeneity | 词性：名词

词源:
The word "potpourri" comes from the French word "pot-pourri," which literally translates to "rotten pot."  Originally, it referred to a stew of leftover meats and vegetables. Over time, its meaning shifted to describe a mixture of various things, particularly the fragrant mixture of dried flowers and spices.
