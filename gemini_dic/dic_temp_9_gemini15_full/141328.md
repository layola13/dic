openings:/ˈoʊpənɪŋz/ | n. | 开幕式；空缺；开头；开始；通路 | Openings refer to the act of opening something, or the state of being open.  It can also refer to a vacant position or job, or the beginning of something, such as a speech or a book. |例句：The theater's grand opening was a huge success. 剧院盛大的开幕式非常成功。

The company is looking for talented individuals to fill several openings. 公司正在寻找有才能的个人来填补几个空缺职位。

|近义词：beginnings, commencements, opportunities, vacancies, apertures |反义词：closings, conclusions, ends |名词
