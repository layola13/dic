interstratification:/ˌɪntərˌstrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |层间插入；层状交错；互层|The geological process where layers of different materials are deposited on top of each other, creating a layered structure.  It's commonly used in geology and materials science to describe the arrangement of different layers within a substance. For example, in sedimentary rocks, interstratification may refer to the alternation of layers of sandstone and shale. In materials science, it can refer to the layering of different polymers or metals. |例句：The interstratification of sandstone and shale is a common feature of this geological formation.  这种地质构造的常见特征是砂岩和页岩的互层。The study revealed a complex interstratification of various mineral phases within the sample. 研究揭示了样品内部各种矿物相的复杂层状交错。|近义词：stratification, layering, lamination|反义词：homogeneity, uniformity|名词
