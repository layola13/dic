narcotine:/ˈnɑːrkətiːn/| n. |罂粟碱|A naturally occurring alkaloid found in opium, it is a weak opioid with analgesic and antitussive properties.  It's less potent than morphine or codeine and has limited medicinal use.  | Example:  Narcotine, while present in opium, is only a minor component and contributes little to its overall analgesic effect.  罂粟碱虽然存在于鸦片中，但只是次要成分，对鸦片整体的镇痛作用贡献不大。|近义词:opium alkaloid, papaverine (a related alkaloid)|反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could consider strong opioid agonists like morphine as contrasting in potency)|名词
