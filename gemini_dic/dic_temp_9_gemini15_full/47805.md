decarbonises:/ˌdiːˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/| v. |脱碳；减少碳排放|To remove or reduce the carbon dioxide emissions of (something, especially a process, industry, or economy).|例句：The company is decarbonising its operations to meet environmental goals.  该公司正在对其运营进行脱碳以实现环境目标。|例句：Many countries are committed to decarbonising their energy sectors.许多国家致力于使其能源部门脱碳。|近义词：decarbonize, decarbonate|反义词：carbonize, increase carbon emissions|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company decarbonises its supply chain. = 公司正在对其供应链进行脱碳。
The government decarbonises the energy sector gradually. = 政府正在逐步对能源部门进行脱碳。


一般过去时:
The factory decarbonised its production process last year. = 该工厂去年对其生产流程进行了脱碳。
The city decarbonised its transportation system through investment in electric buses. = 该城市通过投资电动公交车对其交通系统进行了脱碳。


现在完成时:
The country has decarbonised significantly since the Paris Agreement. = 自巴黎协定以来，该国已大幅度脱碳。
We have decarbonised our operations by switching to renewable energy. = 我们通过改用可再生能源使其运营脱碳。


过去完成时:
Before the new regulations, the industry had not decarbonised sufficiently. = 在新的规章制度出台之前，该行业尚未充分脱碳。
They had decarbonised their operations before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前完成了其运营脱碳。


将来时:
The company will decarbonise its manufacturing process by 2030. = 该公司将在2030年前对其制造流程进行脱碳。
We will decarbonise our transportation system through increased use of public transport. = 我们将通过增加公共交通的使用来使其交通系统脱碳。


虚拟语气:
If the government invested more in renewable energy, the country would decarbonise faster. = 如果政府对可再生能源投资更多，该国将更快地脱碳。
It is important that the world decarbonises to avoid climate change. =  世界脱碳以避免气候变化至关重要。


被动语态:
The process is being decarbonised by the introduction of new technologies. = 通过引入新技术，该过程正在脱碳。
The energy sector will be decarbonised through government policies. = 能源部门将通过政府政策实现脱碳。


疑问句:
How can we decarbonise the economy effectively? = 我们如何才能有效地使经济脱碳？
Is the company decarbonising its operations? = 该公司是否正在对其运营进行脱碳？


否定句:
The company has not yet decarbonised its operations fully. = 该公司尚未完全对其运营进行脱碳。
The government is not doing enough to decarbonise the transportation sector. = 政府在使交通部门脱碳方面做得不够。
