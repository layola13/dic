fast-forwarded:/ˌfæstˈfɔːrwərdɪd/| adj. v. |快进的；快速前进的|The past tense and past participle of fast-forward.  It describes something that has been sped up or advanced rapidly, often skipping over less important details.  The adjective form describes something that is characterized by rapid progress or advancement.|例句：We fast-forwarded through the boring parts of the movie. 我们快进跳过了电影中无聊的部分。

The project was fast-forwarded due to the urgency of the situation.  由于情况紧急，项目被加快了进度。|近义词：accelerated, expedited, advanced|反义词：slowed, delayed, retarded|
