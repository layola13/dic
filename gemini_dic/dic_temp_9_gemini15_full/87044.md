hephaestus:/hɪˈfɛstəs/| n. |赫菲斯托斯（希腊神话中的火神）|Hephaestus is the Greek god of fire, metalworking, crafts, and volcanoes. He is the son of Zeus and Hera.  He is often depicted as a blacksmith, working in his forge. |The myths surrounding Hephaestus are complex and often involve his lameness and his skill in crafting powerful weapons and objects.  |近义词：Vulcan (Roman equivalent)|反义词：无
