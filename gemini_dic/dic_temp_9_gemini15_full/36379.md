coining:/ˈkɔɪnɪŋ/| n. v.|创造（新词）；铸造（硬币）|The act of creating a new word or phrase, or the process of making coins.  |例句：The coining of new words is a constant process in language. 新词的创造是语言中一个持续的过程。

例句：The mint is responsible for the coining of currency. 造币厂负责铸造货币。


近义词：creation, invention, minting


反义词：abolishing, discarding


词性:noun, verb
