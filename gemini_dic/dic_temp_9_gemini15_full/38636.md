concupiscences:/kənˈkjuːpɪsənsɪz/ | n. | 淫欲；强烈的欲望 | Strong sexual desires;  intense longing or craving, often for something forbidden or unattainable.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting an uncontrolled or sinful desire. | 例句：He was consumed by concupiscences, unable to control his urges. 他被淫欲吞噬，无法控制自己的冲动。


例句：Her concupiscences led her down a path of self-destruction.  她的强烈的欲望将她引向自我毁灭的道路。


近义词：lust, cravings, desires, passions, longings


反义词：chastity, abstinence, self-control, temperance


词性: 名词 (plural)
