grayback:/ˈɡreɪbæk/| n.|灰背, 灰背虱|A grayback is a louse, specifically the body louse (Pediculus humanus corporis), known for its grayish color.  The term is often used in historical contexts, particularly relating to the American Civil War and other times of hardship where infestations were common.|Example: The soldiers suffered terribly from graybacks and other diseases.  士兵们饱受灰背虱和其他疾病的折磨。|近义词：body louse, head louse (depending on context)|反义词：N/A|名词
