caterwaulers:/ˈkætərˌwoʊlərz/| n.|猫叫声；吵闹的人；发出刺耳声音的人|People or things that make a harsh, unpleasant noise, similar to the sound of cats fighting.  The word often refers to people making loud, unpleasant noises, or to the noise itself.|例句：The caterwaulers outside kept me awake all night.  外面的吵闹声吵得我一夜没睡。|The incessant caterwauling of the protesters was distracting.  抗议者不绝于耳的吵闹声让人分心。|近义词：shriekers, howlers, yowlers|反义词：whisperers, murmurers
