harpsichordist:/ˌhɑːrpˈsɪkɔːdɪst/| n. |拨弦琴演奏家|A harpsichordist is a musician who plays the harpsichord.  The harpsichord is a keyboard instrument that produces sound when its strings are plucked by plectra.  Harpsichordists are often skilled in Baroque and Classical music. |Example:  The renowned harpsichordist captivated the audience with her virtuosic performance.  著名的拨弦琴演奏家以其精湛的演奏征服了观众。  The young harpsichordist showed great promise in the competition. 年轻的拨弦琴演奏家在比赛中展现了极大的潜力。|近义词：keyboard player,  musician (in a broader sense)|反义词：N/A
