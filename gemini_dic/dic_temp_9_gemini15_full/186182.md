rhetorical:/rɪˈtɒrɪkl/|/rɪˈtɔːrɪkl/| adj.|修辞的；雄辩的；问而不答的 | Relating to or using rhetoric; skilled in the art of rhetoric.  It can also describe a question that is asked to make a point rather than to get an answer. |例句：His speech was full of rhetorical flourishes.  他的演讲充满了华丽的辞藻。

例句：That's a rhetorical question; I don't expect an answer.  那是个反问句；我不指望你回答。|近义词：figurative, eloquent, ornate |反义词：literal, plain, unelaborate
