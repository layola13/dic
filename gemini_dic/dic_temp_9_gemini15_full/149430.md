packs:/pæks/ | n. |包裹；包；一群；组 |a collection of things or people tied or fastened together; a container for carrying things |例句：He packed his bags and left. 他收拾好行李离开了。 |近义词：bundles, groups, sets |反义词：unpacks


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He packs his lunch every day. = 他每天都打包午餐。
She packs her suitcase carefully. = 她仔细地打包行李箱。

一般过去时:
They packed the boxes yesterday. = 他们昨天打包了箱子。
I packed my things before the trip. = 我在旅行前收拾好了我的东西。

现在完成时:
We have packed all the gifts. = 我们已经打包好所有礼物了。
She has packed her bags for the journey. = 她已经为旅程打包好行李了。

过去完成时:
They had packed the car before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把车装满了。
He had packed his suitcase the night before. = 他前一天晚上就打包好了行李箱。

将来时:
I will pack my things tomorrow. = 我明天会收拾我的东西。
We will pack the boxes for shipping. = 我们将打包箱子以便运输。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pack more carefully. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地打包。
I wish I had packed a warmer coat. = 我真希望我带了一件更暖和的外套。

被动语态:
The boxes were packed by the movers. = 箱子是由搬运工打包的。
The luggage was packed securely for the flight. = 行李被安全地打包好以便飞行。

疑问句:
Have you packed your lunch? = 你打包午餐了吗？
Did they pack the fragile items carefully? = 他们是否小心地打包易碎物品？

否定句:
I didn't pack my umbrella. = 我没有带伞。
He hasn't packed his suitcase yet. = 他还没打包行李箱。


PHRASE->
pack a punch:  有很大影响力
pack it in: 停止；放弃
pack a bag: 收拾行李
pack a room:  使房间拥挤
pack one's bags: 收拾行装
