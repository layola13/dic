lowkey:/ˈloʊkiː/| adj.|低调的，不张扬的，秘密的 |Lowkey is an adverb and adjective used to describe something that is done or kept in a quiet or understated way.  It often implies a degree of secrecy or a desire to avoid attention. |例句：He lowkey admitted he was wrong. 他低调地承认自己错了。  She lowkey celebrated her birthday with close friends. 她低调地和密友一起庆祝了生日。|近义词：subtle, understated, discreet, quiet, secret|反义词：loud, ostentatious, flamboyant, public, overt|
