expendabilities:/ɪkˌspɛndəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |消耗品；可消耗的东西；开支；费用 |The expendables are the items that are consumed or used up during the operation of a business or project.  Expendabilities refer to the costs associated with these items.  |例句：The company's expendabilities for the quarter were higher than projected.  这家公司本季度的消耗品支出高于预期。

The project's expendabilities included fuel, ammunition, and food.  该项目的消耗品包括燃料、弹药和食物。|近义词：consumables, supplies, expenses, costs |反义词：assets, capital
