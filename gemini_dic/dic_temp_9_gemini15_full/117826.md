mellification:/ˌmɛlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |蜜的制造；酿蜜|The process by which bees transform nectar into honey.  This involves enzymatic action, water evaporation, and the addition of other substances. |The mellification of nectar into honey is a complex process. 蜜转化为蜂蜜是一个复杂的过程。|honey production, honeymaking|honey destruction(无直接反义词，可理解为破坏蜂蜜生产过程)|名词
