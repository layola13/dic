sightsman: /ˈsaɪtsmən/ | n. | 景物管理员；观光向导 | A person responsible for showing visitors around a place of interest, often with expertise in the area's history or features.  This term is less common than "tour guide" or "guide". | Example: The sightsman led us through the ancient ruins, explaining the historical significance of each structure.  | 近义词：tour guide, guide, docent | 反义词：N/A
