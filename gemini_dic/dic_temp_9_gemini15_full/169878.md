prosocoele: /ˈprɒsəʊsiːl/ | n. |前脑室|The prosocoele is the cavity of the forebrain in the developing embryo.  It is the anterior part of the neurocoel, the fluid-filled cavity within the neural tube.  In humans, the prosocoele eventually develops into the lateral ventricles and third ventricle of the brain. |The developing embryo's prosocoele is crucial for brain development.|近义词:forebrain cavity, neurocoel (partially)|反义词: 无
