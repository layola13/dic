propension:/prəˈpen(t)sən/| n. |倾向；癖好；习性 |A natural inclination or tendency; a predisposition.  It often refers to a strong inclination towards something, sometimes negative. |He has a propension for exaggeration. 他有夸大的倾向。|She has a propension towards laziness. 她有懒惰的倾向。|近义词：inclination, tendency, predisposition, propensity |反义词：aversion, dislike, reluctance
