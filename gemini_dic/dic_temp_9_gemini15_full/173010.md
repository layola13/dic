quarantine:/ˈkwɒrənˌtiːn/ /ˈkwɔːrənˌtiːn/| n. v.|隔离；检疫|The state, period, or place of being kept apart from others to prevent the spread of disease;  a period of enforced isolation to prevent the spread of disease.  Also, to place (a person or animal) in quarantine.|例句：The ship was placed under quarantine. 这艘船被隔离了。

The government imposed a strict quarantine to curb the spread of the virus. 政府实施了严格的隔离措施以遏制病毒的传播。

近义词：isolation, confinement, segregation |反义词：freedom, liberty

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quarantines himself to avoid infection. 他自我隔离以避免感染。
We quarantine all incoming packages. 我们对所有入境包裹进行隔离。

一般过去时:
They quarantined the affected area. 他们对受影响地区进行了隔离。
The doctor quarantined the patient immediately. 医生立即将病人隔离。

现在完成时:
They have quarantined the city for weeks. 他们已经对这座城市进行了数周的隔离。
She has been quarantined since last week. 她从上周开始就被隔离了。

过去完成时:
The city had been quarantined before the virus spread widely. 在病毒广泛传播之前，这座城市就已经被隔离了。
He had quarantined himself for several days before the test results came out. 在检测结果出来之前，他已经自我隔离了几天。

将来时:
They will quarantine anyone showing symptoms. 他们将隔离任何出现症状的人。
The hospital will quarantine the new patient. 医院将隔离新病人。


虚拟语气:
If I had known about the quarantine, I would have stayed home. 如果我知道有隔离令，我就待在家了。
It's as if the whole country is under quarantine. 就好像整个国家都在隔离一样。


被动语态:
The village was quarantined to prevent the spread of disease. 村庄被隔离以防止疾病传播。
He was quarantined for two weeks after his travel. 他旅行后被隔离了两周。


疑问句:
Is the area quarantined?  该地区是否被隔离？
Were they quarantined after contact with the infected person?  他们在与感染者接触后是否被隔离了？


否定句:
They did not quarantine the suspect. 他们没有隔离嫌疑人。
The infected person was not properly quarantined. 感染者没有得到适当的隔离。



PHRASE->

词源:
From the Italian word "quaranta" (forty), referring to the forty-day period of isolation historically imposed on ships arriving from plague-stricken areas.  This practice developed during the Middle Ages as a preventative measure against the spread of infectious diseases.
