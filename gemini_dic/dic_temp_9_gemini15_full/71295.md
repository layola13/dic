fixing:/ˈfɪksɪŋ/| n. v.|修理，固定；准备，安排；（非正式）贿赂|The act of repairing, mending, or adjusting something; the process of making something secure or stable;  preparation or arrangement; (informal) bribery. |例句：The mechanic is fixing my car. = 修理工正在修理我的车。 The artist is fixing the painting to the wall. = 艺术家正在把画固定在墙上。 |近义词：repairing, mending, adjusting, securing, preparing, arranging |反义词：breaking, damaging, destroying, loosening


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He fixes computers every day. = 他每天修理电脑。
She fixes appointments for the meeting. = 她为会议安排约会。


一般过去时:
I fixed the broken chair yesterday. = 我昨天修好了坏掉的椅子。
They fixed the price for the goods. = 他们为商品定好了价格。


现在完成时:
He has fixed the leak in the roof. = 他已经修好了屋顶的漏水处。
She has fixed a time to meet. = 她已经确定了会面的时间。


过去完成时:
They had fixed the dinner before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经准备好晚餐了。
She had fixed the problem before I even noticed it. = 我还没注意到之前，她就解决了这个问题。


将来时:
I will fix the broken window tomorrow. = 我明天会修好坏掉的窗户。
They will fix the schedule for the conference. = 他们将会安排会议的日程。


虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would fix the bicycle. = 如果我有工具，我会修好自行车。
I wish I had fixed the problem sooner. = 我希望我早点解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The car is being fixed at the garage. = 车子正在修理厂修理。
The meeting time will be fixed later. = 会议时间稍后会确定。


疑问句:
Are you fixing the broken lamp? = 你在修理坏掉的台灯吗？
Will they fix the problem soon? = 他们会很快解决问题吗？


否定句:
I cannot fix the computer. = 我修不好电脑。
They did not fix the date for the party. = 他们没有确定聚会的日期。



PHRASE->

fix a date: 确定日期
fix a problem: 解决问题
fix a price: 定价
fix something up: 修理；改善
fix one's eyes on: 目不转睛地盯着
