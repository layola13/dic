overdoses:/ˈoʊvərdəʊsiːz/ | n. |过量服用；药物过量 |The act of taking an excessive amount of a drug or other substance, often unintentionally or with suicidal intent.  It can lead to serious health consequences, including death. |例句：The number of drug overdoses has increased dramatically in recent years. 近年来，药物过量服用的人数急剧增加。

例句：He died from a heroin overdose. 他死于海洛因过量。


近义词：drug overdose, poisoning, intoxication

反义词：underdosage
