satiric:/səˈtærɪk/| adj.|讽刺的，挖苦的|Using irony, sarcasm, or ridicule to expose, denounce, or deride vice, folly, etc.  It aims to improve society by criticizing its flaws through humor.|例句：The cartoonist's work is often satiric, targeting politicians and their policies.  漫画家的作品常常带有讽刺意味，目标是政客及其政策。

例句：His satiric novel exposed the hypocrisy of the upper class.  他的讽刺小说揭露了上流社会的虚伪。|近义词：ironic, sarcastic, mordant, cynical|反义词：serious, earnest, complimentary
