presuggesting:/ˌpriːsəˈdʒɛstɪŋ/| v. |预先建议；提前提出建议 |The act of suggesting something before it is officially proposed or considered.  It implies a proactive and anticipatory approach to suggestion-making. |例句：The committee was criticized for presuggesting the outcome of the debate before all evidence had been presented. = 委员会因在所有证据提交之前就预先暗示辩论结果而受到批评。  The consultant's presuggesting a solution proved helpful in accelerating the project. = 顾问提前提出的解决方案有助于加快项目进度。|近义词：preemptively suggesting, foreshadowing, hinting at |反义词：postponing suggestion, delaying suggestion
