/læmz/| n. |羔羊，小羊|A young sheep, especially one less than a year old.  |例句：The farmer raised a flock of lambs. 农民养了一群羔羊。|例句：The lamb was bleating for its mother. 小羊正在咩咩叫着找妈妈。|近义词：lambkin, yeanling |反义词：sheep (adult)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lamb drinks milk. = 小羊喝牛奶。
The lambs graze in the field. = 小羊在田里吃草。

一般过去时:
The lamb ate all the grass. = 小羊吃了所有的草。

The farmer has sheared the lambs. = 农民已经剪了小羊的羊毛。
