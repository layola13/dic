foyers:/ˈfɔɪeɪz/| n.|门厅；休息室；剧场门厅|A foyer is an entrance hall or lobby, especially in a public building such as a theatre or hotel.  It's a space where people can gather before or after an event.|Example: The theatre foyer was buzzing with excitement before the performance. 剧院门厅在演出前热闹非凡。|The foyer was crowded with people waiting for the concert to begin. 门厅里挤满了等待音乐会开始的人。|lobby, hall, vestibule|outside, exit
