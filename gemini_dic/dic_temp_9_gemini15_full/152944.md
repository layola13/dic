pedicel:/ˈpɛdɪsɛl/| n. |花梗；小梗 |A small stalk that supports a single flower or fruit in a plant.  It connects the flower or fruit to the main stem or inflorescence.|The rose's pedicel was long and slender. 这朵玫瑰的花梗又细又长。|  The apple hung from a short, thick pedicel. 苹果悬挂在短而粗的花梗上。|  stalk, stem |  (无明显反义词,  可考虑用"main stem"作对比)| 名词
