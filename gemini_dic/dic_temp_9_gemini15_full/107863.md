largely:/ˈlɑːrdʒli/ | adv. | 主要地，大部分地 |  Primarily; to a great extent.  It indicates that something is true or happens to a significant degree, but not necessarily completely or entirely. | 例句：The project was largely successful. = 这个项目大部分是成功的。

The success of the business is largely due to the hard work of the team. =  这家企业的成功很大程度上归功于团队的辛勤工作。


近义词：mostly, mainly, primarily, substantially, significantly

反义词：slightly, minimally, marginally, insignificantly

词性：副词
