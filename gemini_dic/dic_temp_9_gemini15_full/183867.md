reseller's: /ˌriːˈsɛlərz/ | n. | 转售商的；经销商的 |  The term "reseller's" is the possessive form of the noun "reseller," referring to something that belongs to or is associated with a reseller. A reseller is an individual or business that purchases goods or services from a manufacturer or wholesaler and then sells them to end-users at a higher price.  | 例句：The reseller's margin on this product is quite high.  这个产品的经销商利润率相当高。  |  The reseller's contract clearly outlines the terms of the agreement.  经销商合同清楚地概述了协议条款。| 近义词：dealer's, distributor's, vendor's | 反义词：manufacturer's, producer's | 名词所有格


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't conjugate in the same way as verbs.  Therefore, tense, voice, etc., are not applicable to "reseller's".  The grammatical function is entirely determined by the noun it modifies.
