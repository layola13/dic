It often involves formally announcing or registering something before it officially begins or becomes active. |例句：The predeclaration of variables is a common practice in some programming languages. = 在某些编程语言中，预先声明变量是一种常见的做法。

例句：The company's predeclaration of profits was overly optimistic. = 公司对利润的预先声明过于乐观。


近义词：advance notice, prior announcement, preliminary statement|反义词：postdeclaration, afterthought|词性:noun
