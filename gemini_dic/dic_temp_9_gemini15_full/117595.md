megalosaur:/ˈmɛɡələˌsɔr/| n. |巨型蜥蜴；巨龙 |A megalosaur is a large extinct carnivorous dinosaur belonging to the Megalosauridae family.  They were among the first dinosaurs to be discovered and described scientifically.  They were bipedal, with powerful legs and jaws filled with sharp teeth. |Example:  The fossil remains suggest this megalosaur was a formidable predator.  巨型蜥蜴的化石遗骸表明它是一种可怕的掠食者。|近义词:  large theropod dinosaur|反义词:  small herbivore dinosaur|名词
