restrictor:/rɪˈstrɪktər/ | n. |限制器，调节器 | A device or mechanism that limits or regulates something, such as flow, pressure, or movement.  |例句：The carburetor contains a restrictor to control the amount of fuel entering the engine.  化油器包含一个限制器来控制进入发动机的燃油量。|例句：The valve acts as a flow restrictor, preventing excessive water from entering the system. 该阀门充当流量限制器，防止过多的水进入系统。|近义词：limiter, regulator, controller |反义词：expander, enhancer
