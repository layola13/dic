non-integration:/ˌnɒnɪnˈteɪɡreɪʃən/| adj. |非整合；未整合|Lack of integration or unification; a state of being unintegrated.|The non-integration of the new software caused significant problems. 新软件的未整合造成了严重的问题。|近义词：disintegration, fragmentation, separation|反义词：integration, unification

USAGE->

The non-integration of the system is a major concern. 系统的未整合是一个主要问题。
Non-integration of the data leads to inconsistencies. 数据的未整合会导致不一致。
