crawlers:/ˈkrɔːlərz/| n.|爬行动物；网络爬虫；履带式车辆|Things that crawl, such as insects or reptiles;  computer programs that automatically browse the World Wide Web; vehicles with tracks instead of wheels.|例句：Spiders are crawlers.  蜘蛛是爬行动物。

例句：Search engine crawlers index websites to help users find information. 搜索引擎爬虫索引网站以帮助用户查找信息。


近义词：creepers,  spiders (for insects/reptiles), bots (for computer programs), tracked vehicles


反义词：flyers (for insects/animals),  walkers (for vehicles)


词性:名词
