scoffs:/skɒfs/| /skɑːfs/ | vt. n.| 嘲笑；嗤之以鼻；轻蔑地吃；狼吞虎咽 | To express scorn or contempt; to eat something quickly and with little elegance. |例句：She scoffed at his suggestion. 她嘲笑他的建议。

He scoffed down the sandwich. 他狼吞虎咽地吃掉了三明治。|近义词：deride, mock, sneer, jeer |反义词：praise, admire, commend |动词
