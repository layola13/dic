hideaways:/ˈhaɪdeɪweɪz/| n.|隐匿处；秘密地点；隐居地|A hideaway is a secluded or secret place, often used for relaxation or escape.  It can be a small, private dwelling, a remote cabin, or even a hidden alcove.|例句：Their mountain cabin served as their perfect hideaway from the city's hustle and bustle. 他们的山间小屋是他们远离城市喧嚣的完美隐匿处。

例句：The artist found inspiration in his secluded hideaway by the sea.  这位艺术家在海边的隐居地找到了灵感。


近义词：retreat, sanctuary, refuge, haven|反义词：public place, open space

词源:
Hideaway is a compound word formed from "hide" (meaning to conceal or secrete) and "away" (meaning at a distance or in a secluded place).  The meaning therefore directly reflects its components, signifying a place hidden away from view or typical activity.
