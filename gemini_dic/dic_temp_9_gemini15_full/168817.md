It encompasses the entire process from inception to completion. | Examples:  The factory's productions increased by 10% this year.  (工厂的产量今年增长了10%)

The theatre's productions of Shakespeare are always popular. (剧院的莎士比亚戏剧作品总是很受欢迎)
