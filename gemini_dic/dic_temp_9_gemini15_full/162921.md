precessor:/ˈprɛsɪsər/| n. |前任，前辈；前身；[计] 前驱|A person or thing that comes before another, especially in a series or sequence.  A predecessor is someone or something that came before something else.  It can refer to a person who held a job before someone else, an earlier model of a product, or an earlier event in a series.|例句：His predecessor was a highly respected figure in the company.  他的前任在公司是一位备受尊敬的人物。|例句：The new software is a significant improvement over its predecessor.  新软件比其前身有了显著的改进。|近义词：antecedent, forerunner, precursor|反义词：successor|名词
