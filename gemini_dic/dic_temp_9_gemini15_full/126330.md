myopia:/maɪˈoʊpiə/| n. |近视，近视眼 |Nearsightedness; the inability to see distant objects clearly.  Myopia is a common refractive error of the eye. |例句：His myopia was corrected with glasses.  他的近视通过眼镜矫正了。|例句：She suffers from severe myopia and needs strong prescription glasses. 她患有严重的近视，需要度数很高的眼镜。|近义词：nearsightedness, shortsightedness |反义词：hyperopia (farsightedness)
