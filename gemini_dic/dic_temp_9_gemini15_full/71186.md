fistful: /ˈfɪstfʊl/ | n. | 一把（之量） | A quantity that can be held in a closed fist.  | Example: He scooped up a fistful of sand. 他抓起一把沙子。 | 近义词：handful, clutch | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He holds a fistful of coins. = 他拿着一把硬币。
She grabs a fistful of grass. = 她抓了一把草。


一般过去时:
The child gathered a fistful of berries. = 孩子摘了一把浆果。
He took a fistful of pills. = 他抓了一把药丸。


现在完成时:
She has collected a fistful of seashells. = 她已经收集了一把贝壳。
I have just eaten a fistful of nuts. = 我刚吃了一把坚果。


过去完成时:
He had already spent a fistful of dollars. = 他已经花了一把美元。
She had given him a fistful of keys. = 她给了他一把钥匙。


将来时:
He will need a fistful of courage. = 他将需要很大的勇气。（一把勇气，引申义）
She will gather a fistful of flowers. = 她将采摘一束花。


虚拟语气:
If I had a fistful of gold, I would be rich. = 如果我有一把金子，我就富有了。
I wish I had a fistful of magic beans. = 我希望我有一把魔豆。


被动语态:
A fistful of papers was scattered on the floor. = 一把纸散落在地板上。
A fistful of candies was given to the children. = 一把糖果被给了孩子们。


疑问句:
Did he take a fistful of money? = 他拿了一把钱吗？
How many coins are there in that fistful? = 那把硬币有多少枚？


否定句:
He didn't have a fistful of evidence. = 他没有足够的证据。
She didn't offer me a fistful of help. = 她没有给我任何帮助（一把帮助，引申义）。
