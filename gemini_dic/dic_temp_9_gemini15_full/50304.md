depleted:/dɪˈpliːtɪd/ | adj. | 耗尽的；枯竭的；减少的 |  Reduced in number or quantity; exhausted.  |例句：The drought has depleted the water supply.  干旱耗尽了水源。

例句：The long war severely depleted the nation's resources. 长期的战争严重地消耗了国家的资源。


近义词：exhausted, drained, emptied, reduced | 反义词： replenished, restored, increased | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant use depletes the battery. =  持续使用会耗尽电池。
This policy depletes the national treasury. =这项政策耗尽了国库。


一般过去时:
The flood depleted the town's food supplies. = 洪水耗尽了城镇的粮食供应。
Years of overfishing have depleted fish stocks. = 多年的过度捕捞已经使鱼类资源枯竭。


现在完成时:
The prolonged drought has depleted the reservoir. = 长期的干旱已经耗尽了水库。
Our resources have been depleted by this project. = 这个项目耗尽了我们的资源。


过去完成时:
They had depleted their savings by the time they bought the house. =  他们在买房时已经花光了积蓄。
The army had depleted its ammunition by the end of the battle. = 在战斗结束时，军队已经弹尽粮绝。


将来时:
Continued logging will deplete the rainforest. = 持续的伐木将会耗尽雨林资源。
The overuse of antibiotics will deplete their effectiveness. = 抗生素的过度使用将会降低其疗效。


虚拟语气:
If we continue this way, we will deplete our resources. = 如果我们继续这样下去，我们将耗尽资源。
If they had not depleted their savings, they would have been able to buy a bigger house. = 如果他们没有花光积蓄，他们就能买更大的房子了。


被动语态:
The ozone layer has been depleted by pollution. = 臭氧层已被污染所破坏。
The aquifer has been depleted by excessive groundwater pumping. = 含水层因过度抽取地下水而枯竭。


疑问句:
Has the drought depleted the lake significantly? = 干旱是否已经严重地耗尽了湖泊的水量？
Are our energy reserves depleted? = 我们的能源储备是否已经耗尽？


否定句:
The well has not been depleted yet. = 井水还没有枯竭。
The company's resources have not been significantly depleted. = 公司的资源尚未被大量消耗。

词源:
The word "depleted" comes from the Latin word "deplere," meaning "to empty completely" or "to drain."  The prefix "de-" means "away from" or "down," and the root "-plere" relates to "filling" or "fullness".  Thus, "depleted" literally means "emptied out" or "reduced."
