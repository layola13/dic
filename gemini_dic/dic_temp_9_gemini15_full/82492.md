grunting:/ˈɡrʌntɪŋ/| v.|哼哼声，嘟囔声|The act of making a low, guttural sound, typically expressing effort, discomfort, or displeasure.  |例句：He was grunting with exertion as he lifted the heavy weight. 他在举起重物时吃力地哼哼着。|例句：The pig was grunting contentedly in its pen. 猪在猪圈里舒服地哼哼着。|近义词： groaning, grumbling, snorting |反义词：whispering, murmuring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grunts when he lifts weights. = 他举重时会哼哼。
She grunts in her sleep. = 她睡觉时会哼哼。

一般过去时:
The athlete grunted as he finished the race. = 运动员冲过终点线时哼了一声。
He grunted in response to my question. = 他哼了一声回答我的问题。

现在完成时:
He has been grunting all morning while he works out. = 他整个上午锻炼时都在哼哼。
She has grunted her displeasure at the news.  = 她哼哼着表达了她对这个消息的不满。

过去完成时:
He had grunted with pain before he fainted. = 他晕倒前痛苦地哼哼了一声。
She had been grunting all day, indicating fatigue. = 她整天都在哼哼，表明她很疲倦。

将来时:
He will grunt when he tries to lift that heavy box. = 当他试图举起那个沉重的箱子时，他会哼哼。
She will probably grunt with effort during the competition. = 在比赛中，她可能会因为用力而哼哼。

虚拟语气:
If he lifted that weight, he would grunt. = 如果他举起那个重量，他会哼哼。
If she had tried harder, she would have grunted in exertion. = 如果她更努力尝试，她就会因为用力而哼哼。

被动语态:
A grunt was heard from the next room. = 从隔壁房间传来哼哼声。
No grunts were made during the silent meditation. = 在无声的冥想中没有哼哼声。

疑问句:
Is he grunting because he's in pain? = 他哼哼是因为他疼吗？
Was she grunting with satisfaction? = 她哼哼是因为满意吗？

否定句:
He didn't grunt when he lifted the light box. = 他举起轻便的箱子时没有哼哼。
She didn't grunt at all during the quiet moment. = 在安静的时刻，她根本没有哼哼。
