maps:/mæps/| n.|地图；地图集|A map is a symbolic depiction emphasizing relationships between elements of some space, such as objects, regions, or themes.  Maps are used to represent the Earth's surface or a portion of it; they are also used to represent other things.  |例句：I need a map to find my way around this city. 我需要一张地图才能在这座城市里找到方向。

例句：This atlas contains maps of all the continents. 这本地图集包含所有大洲的地图。


近义词：chart, plan, atlas|反义词：None|词性：名词
