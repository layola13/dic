rebooking:/ˌriːˈbʊkɪŋ/| n. |重新预订；改签|The act of booking something again, typically a flight or hotel room, often after a previous booking has been canceled or changed.|例句：Due to unforeseen circumstances, I need to make a rebooking for my flight. 由于不可预见的情况，我需要重新预订我的航班。


例句：The airline offered a free rebooking after the flight was delayed. 航空公司在航班延误后提供了免费改签。


近义词：re-reservation, rescheduling


反义词：cancellation


词性:名词
