fraudulence:/ˈfrɔːdʒələns/| n. |欺诈；欺骗；骗局 |The act of deceiving someone in order to gain something, typically money, through dishonest means.  It involves intentional misrepresentation or concealment of facts. |例句：The investigation uncovered widespread fraudulence in the company's accounting practices.  调查揭露了公司会计实践中普遍存在的欺诈行为。


例句：He was accused of fraudulence and sentenced to prison. 他被指控犯有欺诈罪并被判入狱。


近义词：deception, trickery, deceit, swindle, dishonesty


反义词：honesty, integrity, truthfulness


词性：名词
