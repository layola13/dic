GDP:/ˌdʒiːdiːˈpiː/| n. |国内生产总值|Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is a monetary measure of the market value of all the final goods and services produced within a country's borders in a specific time period.  It's one of the primary indicators used to gauge the health of a country's economy.|例句：The country's GDP grew by 3% last year. = 去年该国的GDP增长了3%。|例句：Experts predict a slowdown in GDP growth next quarter. = 专家预测下个季度GDP增长将放缓。|近义词：national income, national output|反义词：None.

The GDP of China is very high. = 中国的GDP非常高。
