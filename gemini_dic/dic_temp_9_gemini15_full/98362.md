insisting:/ɪnˈsɪstɪŋ/ | v. | 坚持；坚决主张 |  To demand something forcefully, repeatedly, or resolutely.  | 例句：He insisted on paying for the meal. = 他坚持要付饭钱。 | 例句：She insisted that she was innocent. = 她坚持说自己是无辜的。 | 近义词：persisting, urging, demanding | 反义词：yielding, giving in, conceding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He insists on his rights. = 他坚持自己的权利。
They insist that it's true. = 他们坚持这是真的。

一般过去时:
He insisted on seeing the manager. = 他坚持要见经理。
She insisted that I stay. = 她坚持让我留下。

现在完成时:
He has insisted on this for years. = 多年来他一直坚持这一点。
They have insisted that we should go. = 他们一直坚持我们应该去。

过去完成时:
He had insisted that he was right. = 他曾经坚持他是对的。
She had insisted on a private room. = 她曾经坚持要一个单人房间。

将来时:
He will insist on his opinion. = 他将会坚持自己的意见。
They will insist that we attend the meeting. = 他们将会坚持我们参加会议。


虚拟语气:
I wish he wouldn't insist on such a difficult solution. = 我真希望他不坚持这么困难的解决方案。
If he insisted, I would have to agree. = 如果他坚持，我不得不同意。

被动语态:
It was insisted that the rules be followed. = 人们坚持必须遵守规则。(注意被动语态中，insist 常和that从句搭配)
His opinion was insisted upon by the committee. = 委员会坚持了他的意见。


疑问句:
Does he insist on a refund? = 他坚持要退款吗？
Why do they insist on this point? = 他们为什么坚持这一点？


否定句:
He didn't insist on coming along. = 他没有坚持要一起来。
They didn't insist on an immediate answer. = 他们没有坚持要立即答复。


PHRASE->
insist on/upon: 坚持…
insist that: 坚决认为…
insist on doing sth: 坚持做某事
cannot/could not insist enough: 再怎么强调也不为过
insistently: 坚持地
