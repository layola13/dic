gutters:/ˈɡʌtərz/| n.|排水沟，檐槽|Channels at the edge of a roof designed to carry rainwater away from a building.  They can also refer to the channels at the side of a road to drain away water.|例句：The gutters were overflowing after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，排水沟溢满了。|例句：Leaves clogged the gutters, preventing proper drainage. 叶子堵塞了排水沟，阻止了正常的排水。|近义词：drains, channels, conduits|反义词：ridge, peak
