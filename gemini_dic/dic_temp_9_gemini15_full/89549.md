honorer:/ˈɒnə(r)/| /ˈɑːnər/| vt.|尊敬，给予荣誉，表彰|To show great respect for someone or something.|例句：The company honored its employees with a bonus. 公司以奖金的方式表彰了员工。|例句：We honored our commitment to the project. 我们履行了对项目的承诺。|近义词：respect, admire, venerate|反义词：disrespect, dishonor, insult|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They honor their parents. = 他们尊敬他们的父母。
The school honors academic excellence. = 学校表彰学术优秀。


一般过去时:
She honored her promise. = 她遵守了她的诺言。
The city honored the war hero. = 城市向这位战争英雄致敬。


现在完成时:
They have honored their guests with a feast. = 他们用盛宴款待了他们的客人。
The company has honored its obligations. = 公司履行了它的义务。


过去完成时:
They had honored the agreement before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前履行了协议。
She had honored her commitment to the charity. = 她已经履行了对慈善事业的承诺。


将来时:
They will honor the contract. = 他们将履行合同。
The organization will honor its founder with a statue. = 该组织将用雕像纪念其创始人。


虚拟语气:
If they honored their word, things would be different. = 如果他们遵守诺言，情况就会不同。
I wish they honored my request. = 我希望他们能满足我的要求。


被动语态:
The winner will be honored at the ceremony. = 获胜者将在仪式上受到表彰。
His contributions were honored posthumously. = 他的贡献在死后得到了表彰。


疑问句:
Will they honor the request? = 他们会满足这个请求吗？
How will the organization honor its volunteers? = 该组织将如何表彰其志愿者？


否定句:
They did not honor their agreement. = 他们没有遵守他们的协议。
The company failed to honor its warranty. = 公司未能履行其保修义务。


PHRASE->
honor one's word = 遵守诺言
honor a debt = 还债
honor a check = 兑现支票
in honor of = 为纪念…；为了庆祝…
honor system = 荣誉制度
