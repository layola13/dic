revelate:/ˈrɛvəleɪt/| v.|揭示，展现|To reveal something previously unknown or hidden.|The scientist's research revealed the secrets of the universe. 科学家的研究揭示了宇宙的秘密。|近义词：reveal, disclose, unveil|反义词：conceal, hide|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The experiment reveals interesting results. = 实验揭示了有趣的结果。
This letter reveals his true feelings. = 这封信揭示了他的真实感受。


一般过去时:
The investigation revealed a shocking truth. = 调查揭示了一个令人震惊的真相。
The painting revealed a hidden message. = 这幅画揭示了一个隐藏的信息。


现在完成时:
The study has revealed a new species of bird. = 这项研究已经发现了一种新的鸟类。
Recent research has revealed the dangers of smoking. = 最近的研究揭示了吸烟的危害。


过去完成时:
After the accident, the investigation had revealed the cause. = 事故发生后，调查已经揭示了事故原因。
They had revealed the secret before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经泄露了秘密。


将来时:
The upcoming report will reveal more details about the incident. = 即将来临的报告将揭示有关该事件的更多细节。
Further research will reveal the full extent of the damage. = 进一步的研究将揭示损害的全部程度。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence revealed the truth, the case would be solved. = 如果证据揭示了真相，案件就能破案。
I wish the report revealed more information. = 我希望报告能提供更多信息。


被动语态:
The truth was revealed to the public. = 真相被公之于众。
The results of the experiment were revealed last week. = 实验结果上周公布了。


疑问句:
What did the study reveal about the disease? = 这项研究揭示了关于这种疾病的什么信息？
What will the next chapter reveal? = 下一章将揭示什么？


否定句:
The investigation did not reveal any clues. = 调查没有发现任何线索。
The report did not reveal the identity of the suspect. = 报告没有透露嫌疑人的身份。
