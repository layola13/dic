recontinue:/ˌriːkənˈtɪnjuː/| v.|重新开始，继续|To begin or continue something again after it has been interrupted or stopped.  This word is not a standard English word and is considered a non-standard or invented word.  It's a combination of "re-" (again) and "continue". While understandable, its use should be avoided in formal writing or speech in favor of clearer alternatives.|例句：After the power outage, they had to recontinue their work.  停电后，他们不得不重新开始工作。|近义词：resume, restart, recommence|反义词：stop, cease, discontinue|动词
