glimmers:/ˈɡlɪmərz/| v. n.|闪烁；微光；闪现 | To shine faintly or intermittently; a faint or unsteady light. |例句：The candle glimmed in the darkness. 蜡烛在黑暗中闪烁。

The stars glimmered in the night sky. 星星在夜空中闪烁。


近义词：twinkle, shimmer, sparkle, flicker|反义词：blaze, flare

词性:动词，名词


USAGE->

The candle glimmers in the wind. = 蜡烛在风中闪烁。
