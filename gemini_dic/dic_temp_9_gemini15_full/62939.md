enmure:/ɪnˈdʒʊər/| vt.|忍受；忍耐；容忍|to accept or endure something unpleasant or difficult|He had to endure many hardships during his childhood. 他童年时期不得不忍受许多苦难。|tolerate, bear, suffer|reject, avoid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They endure the harsh weather conditions. = 他们忍受恶劣的天气条件。
She endures long working hours without complaint. = 她忍受漫长的工作时间而没有抱怨。

一般过去时:
He endured the pain silently. = 他默默地忍受着痛苦。
We endured a long and boring journey. = 我们忍受了一次漫长而无聊的旅程。

现在完成时:
She has endured much suffering in her life. = 她一生忍受了很多苦难。
They have endured many challenges to achieve their goals. = 为了达到目标，他们经受住了许多挑战。

过去完成时:
He had endured years of poverty before finding success. = 在获得成功之前，他忍受了多年的贫困。
She had endured numerous setbacks before finally achieving her dream. = 在最终实现梦想之前，她经受了无数的挫折。

将来时:
We will endure whatever comes our way. = 我们将忍受任何降临在我们身上的事情。
They will endure the pressure of the competition. = 他们将经受住竞争的压力。

虚拟语气:
If I had to endure that again, I wouldn't know what to do. = 如果我不得不再次忍受那件事，我不知道该怎么办。
I wish I didn't have to endure this pain. = 我希望我不必忍受这种痛苦。

被动语态:
The hardship was endured by the people with great courage. = 这种苦难被人们以极大的勇气忍受着。
The pressure was endured by the team despite the difficult circumstances. = 尽管情况艰难，但压力还是被团队承受住了。

疑问句:
Can we endure this much longer? = 我们还能忍受多久？
How long can they endure this situation? = 他们能忍受这种情况多久？

否定句:
He could not endure the humiliation any longer. = 他再也忍受不了这种羞辱了。
She would not endure such injustice. = 她不会容忍这种不公正。
