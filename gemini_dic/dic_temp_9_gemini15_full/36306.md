cohesion:/koʊˈhiːʒən/ | n. |内聚力；凝聚力；结合；一致性 | The state of sticking together or the property of sticking together.  It refers to the action or state of uniting or being united into a whole.  This can be applied to physical materials, social groups, or even ideas. | Example: The cohesion of the team was crucial to their success.  团队的凝聚力对他们的成功至关重要。 |近义词：unity, solidarity, adhesion, bonding |反义词：fragmentation, division, separation
