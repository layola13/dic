colour-coded:/ˈkʌlə(r)ˌkoʊdɪd/ | adj. | 颜色编码的 | Having different colours used to represent different categories or information.  This is a system used to organize or categorize information visually, making it easier to understand and interpret. For example, a map might be colour-coded to show different elevations or geographical features.  | 例句：The map is colour-coded to show different levels of risk. 这张地图采用颜色编码来显示不同级别的风险。

The spreadsheet was colour-coded by department. 这个电子表格按部门进行了颜色编码。


近义词：color-coded (American spelling), categorized, classified | 反义词：monochromatic, uncategorized
