knitback:/ˈnɪtbæk/| n.|针织反面, 反面针织物|A knit fabric with a textured back, often used for sweaters and other garments.  The reverse side of a knitted fabric, typically showing a more textured or less smooth surface than the face.|例句：The sweater is made from a soft knitback fabric. 这件毛衣是用柔软的针织反面料制成的。

The knitback has a distinctive pebbled texture.  这种针织反面具有独特的鹅卵石纹理。|近义词：reverse side, back fabric|反义词：knit face, right side
