quasi-instructed:/ˈkwɑːzaɪɪnˈstrʌktɪd/ | adj. |准指导的，似指导的 |  Describes something that resembles or partially fulfills the function of instruction, but is not fully or formally instructional. It suggests an indirect or implicit guidance, rather than explicit and direct teaching.  | Example: The quasi-instructed nature of the online tutorial left some students confused about the next steps.  准指导性质的在线教程让一些学生对下一步感到困惑。|近义词: partially instructed, implicitly guided, indirectly taught |反义词: explicitly instructed, fully taught, directly guided |形容词
