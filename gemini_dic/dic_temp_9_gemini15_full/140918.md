oncology:/ɒŋˈkɒlədʒi/| n. |肿瘤学；癌症学|The branch of medicine that deals with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer.  It encompasses various aspects of cancer care, from surgical removal of tumors to chemotherapy and radiation therapy. | 例句：She is a leading expert in oncology. = 她是肿瘤学领域的领先专家。

例句：He's undergoing oncology treatment for his lung cancer. = 他正在接受肺癌的肿瘤学治疗。


近义词：cancerology (less common)


反义词：无


词性：名词
