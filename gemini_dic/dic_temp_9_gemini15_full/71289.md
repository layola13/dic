fixer:/ˈfɪksər/| n. |解决者；修理工；中间人；（尤指）贿赂者|A person who solves problems, especially in a practical way.  This can range from a person who repairs things to someone who acts as a go-between in illicit deals or who arranges bribes. |例句：He hired a fixer to resolve the issue with the city hall. 他雇了一个解决者来处理市政厅的问题。|例句：The old fixer could repair almost any machine.  这位老修理工几乎能修理任何机器。|近义词：repairman, mediator, go-between, agent, facilitator|反义词：obstructor, hinderer
