graceful:/ˈɡreɪsfl/| adj.|优雅的，优美的，雅致的|Possessing or displaying grace; elegant and refined in appearance or manner.  It suggests a pleasing smoothness and elegance of movement, form, or style.|例句：The dancer moved with graceful ease.  舞者优雅地轻松地移动。

例句：The graceful curve of the bridge was admired by all. 大桥优雅的曲线受到所有人的赞赏。


近义词：elegant, refined, polished, stylish, poised

反义词：awkward, clumsy, ungainly, graceless, crude


词源:  From Old French *gracieux, from Latin gratia "grace, favor, thanks."  The sense evolved from "pleasing" to "elegant" and "graceful."
