satirists:/ˈsætərɪsts/| n. |讽刺作家；讽刺家 |People who use satire, irony, or sarcasm to expose and criticize folly or wickedness.  Satirists often use humor to critique society, politics, or individuals. | Example:  Many satirists used their wit to comment on the absurdity of war. 许多讽刺作家用他们的才智来评论战争的荒谬性。  Jonathan Swift was a master satirist known for his sharp wit and biting social commentary.乔纳森·斯威夫特是一位以其敏锐的才智和辛辣的社会评论而闻名的杰出讽刺作家。|近义词：humorists, comedians, critics|反义词：flatterers, apologists
