exclamation:/ˌɛkskləˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |感叹；惊叹词 | A sudden cry or remark, especially expressing surprise, anger, or pain.  An exclamation mark (!). |例句：Her exclamation of surprise startled me.  她的惊叹声吓了我一跳。|例句：The letter ended with an exclamation mark. 这封信以感叹号结尾。|近义词： outcry, cry, shout |反义词：silence, whisper
