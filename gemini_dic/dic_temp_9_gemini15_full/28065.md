capered:/ˈkeɪpərd/| v.|雀跃；跳跃；嬉戏|To move about in a lively or playful way, especially by skipping or leaping.|The children capered with joy in the park.孩子们在公园里高兴地蹦蹦跳跳。|近义词：skip, hop, leap, frisk, gambol|反义词：trudge, lumber


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He capers around the room. = 他在房间里蹦蹦跳跳。
The lambs caper in the field. = 小羊在田野里嬉戏。

一般过去时:
The clown capered on the stage. = 小丑在舞台上跳跃。
She capered with delight. = 她高兴地跳跃。

现在完成时:
The children have capered all afternoon. = 孩子们整个下午都在蹦蹦跳跳。
They have capered through the streets. = 他们在大街上嬉戏。

过去完成时:
He had capered before the audience. = 他曾在观众面前跳跃过。
The goat had capered over the fence. = 山羊曾跳过篱笆。

将来时:
The dancers will caper in the moonlight. = 舞者将在月光下跳跃。
The puppies will caper playfully. = 小狗们将嬉戏地跳跃。

虚拟语气:

I wish I could caper like a young deer. = 我希望我能像小鹿一样跳跃。
