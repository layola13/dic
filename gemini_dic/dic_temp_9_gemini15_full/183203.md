represcribing:/ˌriːˈprɪˌskraɪbɪŋ/| v.|再次开药，重新处方|The act of prescribing a medication again, often for a patient who has previously received the same medication.  This can involve the same doctor or a different one.|例句：The doctor's represcribing of the antibiotics was necessary due to the patient's recurring infection.  医生再次开出抗生素是必要的，因为患者的感染反复发作。

例句：Represcribing the same medication without reviewing the patient's condition is considered risky.  在没有审查病人病情的情况下再次开出相同的药物被认为是有风险的。


近义词：re-prescribing, renewing prescription, issuing a new prescription|反义词：discontinuing medication, stopping prescription
