choke-up: /ˈtʃoʊk ʌp/ | v. |哽咽；卡住；堵塞；情绪激动；（尤指因紧张而）发挥失常 |

英文解析：To choke up means to become unable to speak because of strong emotion, typically sadness or anger. It can also refer to something becoming blocked or clogged, or to a player's performance deteriorating due to nervousness.

例句：

He choked up when he talked about his deceased father.  = 他谈到已故的父亲时哽咽了。

She choked up during the emotional scene in the movie. =  她在电影中感人的场景中哽咽了。


近义词：become emotional, well up, get choked, falter, stumble


反义词：remain composed, stay calm, perform well
