fronded:/ˈfrɒndɪd/| adj.|叶状的，长着叶子的|Having fronds or fern-like leaves.  Characterized by or resembling fronds.|The fronded ferns unfurled their delicate leaves in the morning sun.  长着叶子的蕨类植物在清晨的阳光下舒展着它们细致的叶子。|leafy, foliated|bare, leafless
