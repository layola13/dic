flickers:/ˈflɪkərz/| v.|闪烁，摇曳，(光线等)忽明忽暗 |To shine with an unsteady light; to burn unsteadily; to appear intermittently.|The candle flickered in the breeze.  烛光在微风中闪烁。|The screen flickered and then went black. 屏幕闪烁了一下然后就黑了。|近义词：flutter, shimmer, glimmer|反义词：steadily shine, burn brightly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The candle flickers in the draft. = 蜡烛在通风处闪烁。
The light flickers intermittently. = 灯光间歇性地闪烁。


一般过去时:
The lights flickered and died. = 灯光闪烁了一下然后熄灭了。
The image flickered on the screen. = 图像在屏幕上闪烁。


现在完成时:
The bulb has flickered all night. = 灯泡整夜都在闪烁。
The screen has flickered several times. = 屏幕闪烁了好几次。


过去完成时:
The light had flickered several times before going out completely. = 灯光在完全熄灭之前闪烁了好几次。
The image had flickered intermittently throughout the presentation. = 在整个演示过程中，图像一直在间歇性地闪烁。


将来时:
The lights will flicker if the power supply is unstable. = 如果电源不稳定，灯就会闪烁。
The screen will likely flicker if you overload it. = 如果你超载它，屏幕可能会闪烁。


虚拟语气:

I wish the screen wouldn't flicker so much. = 我希望屏幕不要闪烁那么多。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
