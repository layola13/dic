colours: /ˈkʌlərz/ | n. | 颜色 | The word "colours" refers to the different hues or shades of light, as perceived by the human eye. It can also refer to the pigments or dyes used to create these hues.  |  The artist used vibrant colours in his painting.  艺术家在他的画中使用了鲜艳的色彩。 The flag is a symbol of our country and its colours. 国旗是象征我们国家及其颜色的标志。 | hues, shades, tints, tones | black and white (as an antonym for colorful) | 名词
