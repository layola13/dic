calcaneum:/kælˈkeɪniəm/| n. |跟骨|The calcaneum, also known as the heel bone, is the largest bone in the foot. It forms the heel and is crucial for weight-bearing and locomotion.  It articulates with the talus superiorly and the cuboid laterally.  Fractures of the calcaneus are common, often resulting from falls or high-impact injuries.|The fracture of his calcaneum required surgery.  他的跟骨骨折需要手术。 The doctor examined the patient's calcaneum for any signs of damage. 医生检查了病人的跟骨是否有任何损伤迹象。|talus, os calcis|—|名词
