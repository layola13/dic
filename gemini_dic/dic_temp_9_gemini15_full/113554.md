macaque:/məˈkæk/| n. |猕猴|A macaque is a type of monkey, typically with a short tail and cheek pouches, belonging to the genus Macaca.  They are found in Asia and Africa and are known for their intelligence and adaptability.|例句：Researchers studied the social behavior of Japanese macaques. 研究人员研究了日本猕猴的社会行为。

例句：The rhesus macaque is a common model organism in biomedical research.  恒河猴是生物医学研究中常用的模式生物。
