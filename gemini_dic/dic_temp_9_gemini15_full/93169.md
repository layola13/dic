ignition:/ɪɡˈnɪʃən/ | n. | 点火；着火；启动 | The process of setting something on fire or starting a machine's engine.  It also refers to the state of being ignited or the point at which something ignites. | Example: The car's ignition system failed.  这辆车的点火系统失灵了。

The ignition of the rocket was spectacular. 火箭的点火场面壮观。| 近义词：kindling, firing, sparking | 反义词：extinction, quenching
