non-punctual:/nɒnˈpʌŋktʃuəl/ | adj. | 不准时的，迟到的 | Not arriving or happening at the agreed or expected time.  |例句：He is notoriously non-punctual; he's always late. 他以不守时而闻名；他总是迟到。

例句：The non-punctual delivery of the goods caused a delay in production. 货物送达不及时导致生产延误。


近义词：late, tardy, behind schedule, unpunctual

反义词：punctual, on time, prompt


词性：形容词
