diverts:/daɪˈvɜːrts/ | vt. |转移；使转向；消遣；转移（注意力） | To change the course or direction of something; to distract or entertain someone. |例句：The river diverts around the mountain. 河水绕过山峰而流。

The comedian diverted the audience's attention from the boring speech. 喜剧演员转移了观众对无聊演讲的注意力。


近义词：redirect, deflect, distract, amuse, entertain | 反义词：focus, concentrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river diverts around the mountain. = 河水绕过山峰而流。
He diverts his attention to his work. = 他把注意力转移到工作上。


一般过去时:
The road diverted traffic away from the accident. = 道路将交通从事故现场转移开。
She diverted her savings into a new business.  = 她把积蓄投入到新的生意中。


现在完成时:
They have diverted funds from the project. = 他们已经从该项目中转移了资金。
The government has diverted resources to the disaster relief efforts. = 政府已将资源转移到救灾工作中。


过去完成时:
He had diverted the conversation to a safer topic. = 他已经将谈话转移到一个更安全的话题上。
They had diverted the water supply to the drought-stricken area. = 他们已将供水转移到遭受干旱的地区。


将来时:
They will divert the flight to another airport. = 他们将把航班改到另一个机场。
The company will divert its profits to research and development. = 公司将把利润用于研发。


虚拟语气:
If they had diverted the funds earlier, the project might have succeeded. = 如果他们早点转移资金，项目也许就成功了。
I wish they had diverted more resources to education. = 我希望他们能把更多的资源投入到教育中。


被动语态:
The traffic was diverted due to road closures. = 由于道路封闭，交通被转移了。
The river's course was diverted to prevent flooding. = 河流的走向被改变以防止洪水。


疑问句:
Will the flight be diverted? = 航班会被改道吗？
Does this road divert to the city center? = 这条路通往市中心吗？


否定句:
The company did not divert any profits this year. = 公司今年没有转移任何利润。
The driver failed to divert the car in time. = 司机没能及时避开车辆。



PHRASE->
divert attention = 转移注意力

divert funds = 转移资金
divert traffic = 转移交通
divert water = 转移水流

词源:
divert  源自于古法语 devertir，意思是“to turn aside, amuse”，最终源自于拉丁语vertere，意思是"to turn".
