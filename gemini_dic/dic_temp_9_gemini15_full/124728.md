mozilla:/məˈzɪlə/| n. |莫齐拉；火狐浏览器|Mozilla is the name of a foundation and a web browser.  The Mozilla Foundation is a non-profit organization that supports the development of open-source software, most notably the Firefox web browser. |Example: I use Mozilla Firefox to browse the internet. 我用火狐浏览器上网。|近义词: Firefox (as a browser)|反义词: None.


USAGE->
This word is primarily a proper noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, typical verb conjugations are not applicable.  The usage is limited to referring to the organization or the browser.


PHRASE->
Mozilla Firefox: 火狐浏览器
Mozilla Foundation: 莫齐拉基金会
Mozilla Thunderbird:  (邮件客户端) 火狐邮箱
Mozilla Developer Network (MDN): 莫齐拉开发者网络
Mozilla community: 莫齐拉社区
