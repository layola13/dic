follows:/ˈfɒləʊz/ /ˈfɑːloʊz/| v. |跟随；遵循；发生；结果是|To come or be after something else in time or order; to do or act according to something.|The instructions follow below. 指示如下。|precedes, succeeds|anticipates, leads.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He follows the rules. = 他遵守规则。
She follows my instructions carefully. = 她仔细地按照我的指示去做。

一般过去时:
They followed the map. = 他们按照地图走。
The storm followed a period of intense heat. = 一段酷暑之后暴风雨来临了。

现在完成时:
I have followed this case closely. = 我一直密切关注此案。
She has followed her dreams. = 她一直追逐她的梦想。

过去完成时:
He had followed the trail for miles. = 他沿着小路走了几英里。
After she had followed the directions, she arrived at her destination. = 她按照指示走后，到达了目的地。

将来时:
We will follow your advice. = 我们会听从你的建议。
The consequences will follow naturally. = 后果将自然而然地发生。

虚拟语气:
If I followed his advice, I would have succeeded. = 如果我听了他的建议，我就成功了。
I wish I had followed my intuition. = 我真希望我当时听了我的直觉。

被动语态:
The rules are followed strictly. = 规则被严格遵守。
His instructions were followed to the letter. = 他的指示被一丝不苟地执行了。

疑问句:
What follows next? = 接下来会发生什么？
Does this conclusion follow logically from the evidence? = 这个结论从证据上看是否合乎逻辑？

否定句:
He didn't follow the instructions. = 他没有按照指示做。
The argument doesn't follow. = 这个论点站不住脚。


PHRASE->
as follows = 如下
it follows that = 由此可见
follow suit = 效仿
follow up = 跟进
follow through = 坚持到底
