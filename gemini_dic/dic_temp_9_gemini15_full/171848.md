punishable: /ˈpʌnɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 可惩罚的，应受惩罚的 |  capable of being punished; deserving punishment.  | The crime is punishable by imprisonment. 这项罪行可判处监禁。|  blameworthy, culpable, liable | unpunishable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That action is punishable by law. = 那项行为触犯法律，应受惩罚。
His behavior is punishable by suspension from school. = 他的行为将会受到学校停学的处罚。

一般过去时:
The offense was punishable by a fine. = 此罪行过去可处以罚款。
The crime was punishable with death. = 这罪行过去曾处以死刑。

现在完成时:
Many crimes have been declared punishable by life imprisonment. = 许多罪行已被判处无期徒刑。
The judge has ruled that the act is punishable. = 法官裁决该行为应受惩罚。

过去完成时:
The law had already declared that action punishable. = 法律已经宣布该行为应受惩罚。
They had found that the act was punishable under the existing law. = 他们发现根据现有法律，该行为应受惩罚。

将来时:
This crime will be punishable by a heavy fine. = 这项罪行将处以巨额罚款。
The new law states that this offense will be punishable by up to five years in prison. = 新法规定，这项罪行最高可判处五年监禁。

虚拟语气:
If the crime were punishable by death, there would be fewer cases. = 如果该罪行可处死刑，案件就会减少。
I wish that such behavior were more severely punishable. = 我希望这种行为能受到更严厉的惩罚。

被动语态:
The crime is punishable by a term in prison. = 这项罪行可判处监禁。
His actions were found to be punishable under international law. = 他的行为被发现违反国际法，应受惩罚。

疑问句:
Is this action punishable under the new law? = 根据新法，这项行为是否应受惩罚？
Are all forms of fraud punishable by law? = 所有形式的欺诈行为都应受法律惩罚吗？


否定句:
That offense is not punishable by law. = 那项罪行不受法律惩罚。
The act was deemed not punishable under the circumstances. = 在这种情况下，该行为被认为不应受惩罚。
