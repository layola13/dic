crawlingly:/ˈkrɔːlɪŋli/| adv.| 缓慢地，匍匐地，卑躬屈膝地 | In a crawling manner; slowly and awkwardly, like something crawling.  It can also imply a sense of subservience or degradation. |例句：He moved crawlingly across the floor. 他缓慢地匍匐着穿过地板。

例句：She apologized crawlingly to her boss. 她卑躬屈膝地向老板道歉。


近义词：slowly, creepingly, abjectly, servilely, humbly

反义词：quickly, swiftly, proudly, arrogantly

词性：副词
