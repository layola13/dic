shooed:/ʃuːd/| vt.|驱赶，喝退|To urge someone or something to go away, typically by making a hissing or shooing sound.|例句：The farmer shooed the chickens away from the garden. 农夫把鸡从花园里赶走了。She shooed the cat out of the room. 她把猫赶出了房间。|近义词：chase, drive away, scare away|反义词：invite, welcome


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I shoo the birds away from my garden every morning. = 我每天早上都把鸟从我的花园里赶走。
He shoos away annoying customers regularly. = 他经常把讨厌的顾客赶走。


一般过去时:
She shooed the dog out of the house. = 她把狗赶出了房子。
The farmer shooed the crows away from the field. = 农夫把乌鸦从田里赶走了。


现在完成时:
I have shooed away several wasps from my picnic. = 我已经从我的野餐中赶走了几只黄蜂。
They have shooed away all the unwanted guests. = 他们已经把所有不受欢迎的客人赶走了。


过去完成时:
She had shooed the children away before starting her work. = 在开始工作之前，她已经把孩子们赶走了。
The guard had shooed away the loiterers before the event began. = 在活动开始之前，警卫已经把闲逛的人赶走了。


将来时:
I will shoo the flies away from the food. = 我会把苍蝇从食物上赶走。
He will shoo the stray cat out of the yard tomorrow. = 他明天会把流浪猫赶出院子。


虚拟语气:
If I had a broom, I would shoo the pesky pigeons away. = 如果我有一把扫帚，我会把讨厌的鸽子赶走。
I wish I could shoo away all my worries. = 我希望我能把所有的烦恼都赶走。


被动语态:
The pigeons were shooed away by the park keeper. = 鸽子被公园管理员赶走了。
The stray dogs were shooed away from the playground. = 流浪狗被从操场上赶走了。


疑问句:
Did you shoo the cat out? = 你把猫赶出去了吗？
Will you shoo the children away from the road? = 你会把孩子们从路上赶走吗？


否定句:
I didn't shoo the dog away because it was friendly. = 我没有把狗赶走，因为它很友好。
He didn't shoo the birds away; he let them be. = 他没有把鸟赶走；他让它们待着。
