immigrant:/ˈɪmɪɡrənt/| n. |移民 |A person who has come to live permanently in a foreign country.|例句：Many immigrants have enriched the cultural landscape of the United States. 许多移民丰富了美国的文化景象。|近义词：migrant, settler |反义词：emigrant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an immigrant from Mexico. 他是一位来自墨西哥的移民。
Many immigrants work hard to build new lives. 许多移民努力建设新的生活。


一般过去时:
She immigrated to Canada ten years ago. 她十年前移民到加拿大。
My grandparents were immigrants who arrived in this country in the 1950s. 我的祖父母是 20 世纪 50 年代来到这个国家的移民。


现在完成时:
Millions of people have immigrated to the United States. 数百万的人已经移民到美国。
She has been an immigrant in this country for five years. 她在这个国家已经做了五年的移民。


过去完成时:
They had immigrated to Australia before the war broke out. 战争爆发前他们已经移民到澳大利亚。
Before he moved, he had already been an immigrant for 20 years. 在他搬家之前，他已经做过20年的移民了。


将来时:
He will immigrate to France next year. 他明年将移民到法国。
They will become immigrants once they arrive in their new home. 一旦他们到达新家，他们就会成为移民。


虚拟语气:
If I were an immigrant, I would learn the local language quickly. 如果我是个移民，我会很快学习当地语言。
I wish I had immigrated sooner. 我真希望我早点移民。


被动语态:
Many immigrants are welcomed by their new communities. 许多移民受到新社区的欢迎。
The policy was designed to help immigrants integrate into society. 这项政策旨在帮助移民融入社会。


疑问句:
Is he an immigrant? 他是移民吗？
Are there many immigrants in your city? 你们城市有很多移民吗？


否定句:
He is not an immigrant; he is a citizen. 他不是移民；他是公民。
She didn't want to be an immigrant anymore. 她不想再做移民了。
