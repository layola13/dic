disk-braked:/ˈdɪskbreɪkt/ | adj. | 盘式制动的；碟刹的 |  Equipped with disk brakes.  Having brakes that use a caliper to squeeze a rotor (disk). |  例句：My new bike is disk-braked and provides superior stopping power.我的新自行车是盘式制动的，制动效果更好。

例句：The car's disk-braked system failed during the emergency stop.  这辆车的盘式制动系统在紧急刹车时失效了。

近义词：disc-braked (同义词，美式拼写)

反义词：rim-braked (鼓式制动的，边刹的)

词性：形容词
