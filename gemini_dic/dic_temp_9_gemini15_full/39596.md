consecratory: /ˈkɒnsɪkrətəri/| adj. |奉献的；祝圣的；священнический| Relating to or connected with consecration; serving to consecrate.  The act of consecrating something, especially a building or object, makes it holy or sacred.  This adjective describes things that are used in or related to this act. |The consecratory ceremony was held in the grand cathedral.|近义词：dedicatory, sanctifying |反义词：profane, desecrating
