neurologist:/nʊərˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |神经科医生| A neurologist is a medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and disorders of the nervous system.  This includes the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. |She consulted a neurologist about her persistent headaches. 她去咨询了一位神经科医生关于她持续的头痛问题。  My father is a neurologist who works at a large hospital. 我的父亲是一位在大型医院工作的神经科医生。| physician, neuroscientist |  (no direct antonym)
