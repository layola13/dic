overarranges:/ˌoʊvərəˈreɪndʒɪz/ | v. |过度安排，安排过多 | To arrange something to an excessive degree, often leading to inefficiency or stress.  |例句：He overarranges his schedule every week, leading to burnout. 他每周都过度安排自己的日程，导致筋疲力尽。 He overarranged the conference, resulting in logistical chaos. 他过度安排会议，导致后勤混乱。|近义词：overburden, overschedule, overcrowd |反义词：underarrange, simplify
