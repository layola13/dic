non-sectarian:/ˌnɒnsekˈteəriən/| adj.|非宗派的；无教派之分的|Not associated with or restricted to a particular religious denomination.  It implies inclusivity and tolerance towards different religious beliefs. |例句：The school has a non-sectarian policy, welcoming students of all faiths. 这所学校奉行非宗派政策，欢迎所有信仰的学生。

例句：Our community center offers non-sectarian services to everyone regardless of their religious background.  我们的社区中心向所有人提供非宗派的服務，无论他们的宗教背景如何。


近义词：interfaith, ecumenical, undenominational, nonreligious (in some contexts)

反义词：sectarian, denominational, partisan


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective, so there are no verb conjugations.  The usage is straightforward: it describes something as not belonging to or supporting a specific religious sect.
