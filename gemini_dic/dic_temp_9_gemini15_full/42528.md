countermanded:/ˌkaʊntəˈmændɪd/ | v. | 反令；取消；撤销 | To cancel or revoke (an order or instruction).  | The general countermanded the troops' advance. 将军下令部队停止前进。The court countermanded the previous ruling. 法院撤销了先前的裁决。 | revoked, annulled, rescinded | obeyed, confirmed | 动词
