gobies:/ˈɡoʊbiz/ | n. |虾虎鱼 |Gobies are a diverse group of small, bottom-dwelling fish found in various marine and freshwater habitats worldwide. They are characterized by their fused pelvic fins, which often form a suction cup used for clinging to rocks or other substrates. Many species exhibit complex social behaviors and reproductive strategies. |例句：Many gobies live in burrows in the sand.  许多虾虎鱼生活在沙中的洞穴里。|例句：The tide pools were teeming with small gobies. 潮池里挤满了小虾虎鱼。|近义词：fish (general term) |反义词：N/A
