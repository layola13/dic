caregivers:/ˈkɛərˌɡɪvərz/ | n. | 守护者；照顾者；照料者 | People who provide care for others, typically those who are young, elderly, sick, or disabled.  They can be family members, friends, or professional healthcare workers. | Example:  My grandparents rely heavily on their caregivers for daily assistance.  我的祖父母非常依赖他们的看护人员进行日常帮助。

The dedicated caregivers at the nursing home provide compassionate care to their residents. 养老院里尽职尽责的护理人员为他们的居民提供充满爱心的护理。


近义词：caretakers, attendants, guardians, nurses (in a professional context)
反义词：None directly opposite, but "neglectors" could be considered an antonym in a contextual sense.


词性：名词
