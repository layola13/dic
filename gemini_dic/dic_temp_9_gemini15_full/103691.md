jonathan:/ˈdʒɑːnəθən/ | n. |乔纳森（男子名） | Jonathan is a Hebrew name meaning "God has given." It is a popular name in many English-speaking countries.  The name has biblical origins, associated with figures like Jonathan, the son of Saul in the Old Testament, known for his strong friendship with David. |My friend Jonathan is a talented musician.  我的朋友乔纳森是一位有才华的音乐家。 He named his son Jonathan. 他给他的儿子取名乔纳森。|近义词：John,  Nathaniel |反义词：无
