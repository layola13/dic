rechannels: /ˌriːˈtʃænəlz/ | vt. |重新定向；重新分配；改变…的渠道 |To redirect or reallocate something, especially resources or information, through a different channel or system.  |例句：The company rechanneled its marketing efforts towards social media. 公司将营销工作重新导向社交媒体。

例句：The government rechanneled funds from road building to education. 政府将用于修路的资金重新分配给了教育。


近义词：redirect, reroute, reallocate, divert |反义词：concentrate, consolidate |词性：动词


USAGE->

The company rechannels its profits into research and development. = 公司将其利润重新投入研发。
He rechannels his energy into his new hobby. = 他将精力重新投入到他的新爱好中。
