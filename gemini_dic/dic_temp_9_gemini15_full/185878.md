revoking:/rɪˈvoʊkɪŋ/ | v. |撤销；废除；取消 | The act of making something invalid or null; canceling or taking back something previously granted or given.  |例句：The government is revoking the licenses of several companies. 政府正在吊销几家公司的执照。

例句：They revoked his driving permit after the accident. 事故后，他们吊销了他的驾驶执照。


近义词：annulling, canceling, rescinding, repealing


反义词：granting, confirming, reinstating


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revokes the contract. = 他撤销合同。
The company revokes the offer. = 公司撤销了提议。


一般过去时:
She revoked her decision. = 她撤回了她的决定。
They revoked his membership. = 他们取消了他的会员资格。


现在完成时:
The court has revoked his citizenship. = 法院已经撤销了他的公民身份。
He has revoked his previous statement. = 他已经撤回了之前的声明。


过去完成时:
They had revoked the permit before he arrived. = 他到达之前，他们已经撤销了许可证。
She had revoked her offer before he accepted. = 他接受之前，她已经撤回了她的提议。


将来时:
The government will revoke the law next year. = 政府明年将废除这项法律。
He will revoke his permission tomorrow. = 他明天将撤回他的许可。


虚拟语气:
If he revoked the order, everything would be different. = 如果他撤销命令，一切都会不同。
I wish he had revoked the contract earlier. = 我希望他早点撤销合同。


被动语态:
The contract was revoked by the court. = 合同被法院撤销了。
His license was revoked due to his misconduct. = 由于他的不当行为，他的执照被吊销了。


疑问句:
Will the company revoke the offer? = 公司会撤销提议吗？
Has he revoked his statement yet? = 他已经撤回他的声明了吗？


否定句:
He did not revoke the agreement. = 他没有撤销协议。
The government will not revoke the new policy. = 政府不会撤销这项新政策。


PHRASE->
revoke a license = 吊销执照
revoke a permit = 撤销许可证
revoke a contract = 撤销合同
revoke an order = 撤销命令
revoke a decision = 撤销决定

词源：
From Latin *revocare (to call back), from re- (back) + vocare (to call).  The word's evolution reflects its meaning: calling something back, effectively canceling it.
