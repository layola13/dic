marchioness:/ˈmɑːrʃənəs/| n. |侯爵夫人|The title of a marchioness is a noble title, ranking below a duchess and above a countess.  A marchioness is the wife of a marquess (or a woman who holds the title in her own right).|The marchioness hosted a lavish ball at her country estate.  侯爵夫人在她的乡间别墅举办了一场盛大的舞会。|Duchess, Countess|Commoner|名词
