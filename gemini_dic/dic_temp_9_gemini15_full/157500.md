pist:/pɪst/| n. |手枪|A pistol is a small firearm that is held and fired with one hand.  Pistols are often used for self-defense or in close-quarters combat. They are typically shorter and lighter than rifles or shotguns.|例句：He pulled out a pistol and pointed it at the robber. 他掏出手枪指着抢劫犯。|近义词：handgun, revolver|反义词：rifle, shotgun
