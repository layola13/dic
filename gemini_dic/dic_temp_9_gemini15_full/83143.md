gutsier:/ˈɡʌtsiər/| adj.|更勇敢的；胆量更大的|More courageous or daring.  It implies a greater degree of bravery than "gutsy".|例句：She was gutsier than I expected, facing the angry crowd without hesitation. 她比我预料的更勇敢，毫不犹豫地面对愤怒的人群。  例句：He showed a gutsier performance in the second half of the game.  他在下半场比赛中表现得更勇敢。|近义词：braver, bolder, more courageous, more daring|反义词：cowardly, timid, fearful
