punkish:/ˈpʌŋkɪʃ/| adj.|朋克风的，有点朋克的；反叛的，不守规矩的|Slightly resembling or suggestive of punk rock; characterized by rebelliousness and unconventionality.|例句：Her punkish hairstyle and ripped jeans turned heads.  她的朋克发型和破洞牛仔裤引人注目。

例句：He had a punkish attitude, always questioning authority. 他有一种朋克式的态度，总是质疑权威。


近义词：rebellious, unconventional, defiant, nonconformist


反义词：conventional, conforming, obedient, submissive


词性:形容词
