goods:/ɡʊdz/| n. |商品，货物 |Merchandise or wares; articles of trade.  Goods can refer to physical items bought and sold, or more broadly, to anything of value offered for sale.|例句：The store sells a wide variety of goods. 这家商店出售各种各样的商品。|例句：The company's goods are exported worldwide.  这家公司的商品出口到世界各地。|近义词：merchandise, wares, commodities, articles |反义词：services (often used in contrast)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store sells many goods. = 这家商店卖很多商品。
These goods are popular with customers. = 这些商品受顾客欢迎。

一般过去时:
They bought many goods yesterday. = 他们昨天买了许多商品。
We sold all the goods last week. = 我们上周卖掉了所有商品。

现在完成时:
We have sold all the goods. = 我们已经卖掉了所有商品。
They have received the goods. = 他们已经收到了货物。

过去完成时:
They had already sold the goods before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经卖掉了货物。
We had ordered the goods a week earlier. = 我们一周前就订购了这些商品。

将来时:
We will order more goods next week. = 我们下周会订购更多商品。
They will ship the goods tomorrow. = 他们明天会发货。

虚拟语气:
If we had better goods, we would sell more. = 如果我们有更好的商品，我们会卖得更多。
I wish we had ordered more goods. = 我希望我们订购了更多商品。

被动语态:
The goods are shipped by sea. = 货物通过海运。
The goods were delivered on time. = 货物准时送达。

疑问句:
What goods are you selling? = 你卖什么商品？
Where are the goods stored? = 货物储存在哪里？

否定句:
We don't sell faulty goods. = 我们不卖有瑕疵的商品。
They didn't receive the goods on time. = 他们没有准时收到货物。
