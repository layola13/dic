pyrocoll:/ˌpaɪroʊˈkɒl/| n. |焦胶；热胶|A pyrocoll is a type of adhesive or sealant that is cured or hardened by heat.  It often contains a resin and a hardener that react when heated.  Pyrocolls are used in various applications where high-temperature resistance is required.|Example: The engineers used a specialized pyrocoll to seal the high-temperature components of the engine. 工程师们使用了一种特殊的焦胶来密封发动机的耐高温部件。|近义词: heat-cured adhesive, thermal adhesive |反义词:  cold-cure adhesive (This is a functional antonym, indicating a different curing method)|名词
