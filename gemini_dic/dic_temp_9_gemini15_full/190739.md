savages:/ˈsævɪdʒɪz/| n.|野蛮人；未开化的人；凶残的人|People considered uncivilized or primitive, often characterized by violence or lack of refinement.  The term is often considered offensive and outdated.|例句：The explorers encountered several tribes of savages in the jungle. 探险家在丛林中遇到了好几个野蛮部落。

例句：He described the ancient inhabitants as savages who lived in caves. 他把古代居民描述成住在山洞里的野蛮人。


近义词：barbarians, brutes, heathens, primitives|反义词：civilized people, cultured people|名词
