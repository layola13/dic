lithomarge:/ˈlɪθəˌmɑːrdʒ/| n. |锂云母；锂质页岩|A variety of claystone that is rich in lithium and often associated with hydrothermal alteration of granitic rocks. It's a potential source of lithium for batteries and other applications.  |Example:  The discovery of a large lithomarge deposit in the region significantly boosted the local economy. 这地区发现一个大型锂云母矿床，极大地促进了当地经济发展。|近义词：claystone, lithium-rich clay |反义词：granite (in the context of its formation)
