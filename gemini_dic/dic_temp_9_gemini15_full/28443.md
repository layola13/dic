They are the body's primary source of energy and are found in a wide variety of foods, including grains, fruits, vegetables, and dairy products.  Different types of carbs are digested at different rates, affecting their impact on blood sugar levels.|例句：I'm trying to cut down on carbs to lose weight. 我正在努力减少碳水化合物的摄入以减肥。
