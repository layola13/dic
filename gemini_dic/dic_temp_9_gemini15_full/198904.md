sieged:/siːdʒd/| v. adj.| 围攻，被围攻的 | To attack (a fortified place) by surrounding it with armed forces and cutting off its supplies, in order to force its surrender.  It can also describe a state of being under siege. |例句：The city was sieged for months before it finally surrendered.  这座城市被围攻了几个月才最终投降。 The besieged city ran out of food. 被围困的城市粮食耗尽了。|近义词：besieged, blockaded, invested |反义词：relieved, liberated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army sieges the city. = 军队围攻这座城市。
They siege the castle every night. = 他们每晚都围攻城堡。


一般过去时:
The enemy sieged the town last year. = 敌人去年围攻了这个城镇。
The troops sieged the fort for weeks. = 部队围攻要塞数周。


现在完成时:
The rebels have sieged the capital for months. = 反叛军已经围攻首都数月了。
They have sieged the stronghold since last spring.  他们自去年春天以来就一直围攻那个据点。


过去完成时:
The citizens had been sieged for a year before the rescue arrived. = 在救援到来之前，市民们已经被围困了一年。
The army had sieged the fortress before reinforcements came. = 增援部队到来之前，军队已经围攻了要塞。


将来时:
The general will siege the enemy's base next week. = 将军下周将围攻敌人的基地。
They will siege the fortress once they gather enough troops. = 他们一旦集结足够的军队就会围攻要塞。


虚拟语气:
If the enemy sieged the city, we would defend it bravely. = 如果敌人围攻这座城市，我们将勇敢地保卫它。
I wish the enemy hadn't sieged our village. = 我希望敌人没有围攻我们的村庄。


被动语态:
The city was sieged for three years. = 这座城市被围攻了三年。
The castle was sieged by the invading army. = 城堡被入侵的军队围攻了。


疑问句:
Was the castle sieged last year? = 城堡去年被围攻了吗？
Have they sieged the fort yet? = 他们已经围攻要塞了吗？


否定句:
The army did not siege the town. = 军队没有围攻那个城镇。
They haven't sieged the enemy's position. = 他们还没有围攻敌人的阵地。
