retraced:/rɪˈtreɪst/| v.|追溯，重新走过，回顾|To go back over (a route, journey, etc.); to follow the course of (something) in reverse; to trace back the origin or development of (something).|例句：He retraced his steps to find his lost wallet. 他重新走过他的路，寻找他丢失的钱包。


例句：The detectives retraced the killer's movements.  侦探们追溯了杀手的行动轨迹。


近义词：refollow, retrace one's steps, review, revisit|反义词：advance, proceed, forge ahead|动词
