cognation:/kɒɡˈneɪʃən/|n.|同源词；血亲关系；族亲关系|The study of cognates helps to understand the historical relationships between languages.  学习同源词有助于理解语言之间的历史关系。|近义词：kinship, relationship, affinity|反义词：estrangement, dissimilarity


英文解析:
Cognation refers to the relationship between words that share a common ancestor.  This relationship can exist across different languages, demonstrating a historical connection between them.  Cognation also refers to the relationship between people who are related by blood, sharing a common ancestor.


例句:
The English word "brother" and the German word "Bruder" are cognates.  英语单词“brother”和德语单词“Bruder”是同源词。

The family celebrated their close cognation at the reunion.  这个家庭在聚会上庆祝他们亲密的家族关系。
