词源:
The word "dragee" comes from the Old French word "dragée," which in turn derives from the Medieval Latin word "druggea," meaning "a sweetmeat."  This ultimately traces back to the Arabic word "duruğ," meaning "pearls," reflecting the small, round, and often pearly appearance of the confection.
