inhabitation:/ˌɪnˌhæbɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |居住；栖息；占有|The act of inhabiting or the state of being inhabited; the presence of inhabitants in a place.  It refers to the process of people or animals settling and living in a particular area.|例句：The habitation of the island began centuries ago.  岛屿的居住始于几个世纪以前。

例句：The remote area showed signs of past habitation.  偏远地区显示出过去有人居住的迹象。


近义词：occupation, settlement, occupancy| 反义词：desertion, abandonment| 词性：名词
