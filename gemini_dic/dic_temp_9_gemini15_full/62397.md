endoderm:/ˈɛndoʊdɜːrm/| n. |内胚层 |The innermost layer of cells or tissue of an embryo in early development, which develops into the lining of the gut and associated structures.  |Example: The endoderm gives rise to the lining of the digestive tract and respiratory system. 内胚层发育为消化道和呼吸系统的内衬。|近义词：germ layer (in the context of embryonic development)|反义词：ectoderm, mesoderm|名词
