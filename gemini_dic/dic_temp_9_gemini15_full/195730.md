semita:/ˈsɛmɪtɑː/| n.|小路，羊肠小道|A narrow path or track.  Often used in a metaphorical sense to describe a difficult or obscure path.|例句：The hikers followed a semita through the dense forest.  徒步旅行者沿着一条羊肠小道穿过茂密的森林。

例句：He found himself on a semita of self-discovery. 他发现自己走上了一条自我发现的小路。

近义词：path, trail, track, way

反义词：highway, avenue, boulevard


词源:  From Latin *semĭta, diminutive of *sema "path, way," of unknown origin.  The word is related to the Sanskrit *śámati "path."
