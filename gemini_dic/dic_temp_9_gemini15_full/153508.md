pensioning:/ˈpɛnʃənɪŋ/| n. v.| 养老金发放；退休金制度；使退休 |The act of providing or receiving a pension; the system of pensions.  The process of putting someone on a pension. |例句：The company's pensioning scheme is generous. = 这家公司的退休金制度很优厚。

The government is reviewing its pensioning policy. = 政府正在审查其养老金政策。|近义词：retirement, superannuation |反义词：employment, active service|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pensioning off his employees. = 他正在让他的员工退休。
The company pensions its employees after 30 years of service. = 这家公司在员工服务30年后为其发放退休金。


一般过去时:
They pensioned him off at the age of 65. = 他们在65岁时让他退休。
The factory pensioned off many workers last year. = 去年这家工厂让许多工人退休了。


现在完成时:
He has been pensioned off for five years. = 他退休五年了。
The company has pensioned off a significant number of employees this year. = 今年该公司已经让相当数量的员工退休了。


过去完成时:

They had pensioned him off long before his health deteriorated. = 他的健康恶化很久之前，他们就已经让他退休了。

词源:
The word "pension" comes from the Old French word "pension," meaning "payment," which in turn derives from the Latin word "pensio," meaning "payment, weighing."  The "-ing" suffix turns the noun "pension" into a gerund, indicating the act or process of pensioning.
