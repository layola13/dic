self-rule:/ˌsɛlfˈruːl/| n. |自治；自我管理|The ability of a group or people to govern themselves without external interference.  It implies independence and autonomy in decision-making. |例句：Many countries fought for self-rule from colonial powers. 许多国家为了摆脱殖民统治而争取自治。|例句：The island achieved self-rule after a long struggle for independence.  这个岛屿经过长期争取独立的斗争后实现了自治。|近义词：autonomy, home rule, self-government |反义词：colonialism, subjugation, dependence|名词
