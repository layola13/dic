prods:/prɒdz/ /prɑːdz/| v.|戳，刺；推动；激励；反复劝说|To poke or push something with a pointed object; to urge or encourage someone to do something; to repeatedly try to persuade someone.|例句：He prodded the meat with his fork. 他用叉子戳了戳肉。|例句：She prodded him to finish his work. 她催促他完成工作。|近义词：poke, nudge, urge, goad|反义词： discourage, dissuade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prods the sleeping dog. = 他戳了戳睡着的狗。
She prods her friend to apply for the job. = 她怂恿她的朋友申请这份工作。

一般过去时:
The farmer prodded the reluctant cow. = 农夫催促那头不情愿的奶牛。
He prodded the ground with his stick. = 他用棍子戳了戳地面。

现在完成时:
She has prodded him repeatedly to change his mind. = 她反复劝说他改变主意。
He has prodded the dough several times. = 他已经多次揉捏面团了。

过去完成时:
They had prodded him for weeks before he finally agreed. = 在他最终同意之前，他们已经劝说了他几周。
She had prodded the soil before planting the seeds. = 种植种子之前，她已经松土了。

将来时:
I will prod him again tomorrow. = 我明天还会再催他一下。
He will prod the mixture until it's smooth. = 他会搅拌混合物直到它变得光滑。

虚拟语气:
If I prodded him harder, he might finish on time. = 如果我更用力地催促他，他可能会按时完成。
I wish I hadn't prodded him so much. = 我真希望我没有那么用力地催促他。

被动语态:
The sleeping cat was prodded by the child. = 睡着的猫被孩子戳了。
He felt prodded by his boss to work faster. = 他感到老板在催促他加快工作速度。

疑问句:
Did you prod the cake to check if it's done? = 你戳了蛋糕检查是否烤好了吗？
Should we prod them to make a decision? = 我们应该催促他们做决定吗？

否定句:
He didn't prod the fire. = 他没有拨弄火堆。
I won't prod him any further. = 我不会再催促他了。


PHRASE->
prod someone into doing something = 催促某人做某事
prod someone along = 鼓励某人继续前进
prod someone to action = 激励某人采取行动
prod someone with a question = 用问题试探某人
a prod in the right direction = 一个正确的指引
