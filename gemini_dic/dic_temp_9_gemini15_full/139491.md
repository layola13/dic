obstructers:/əbˈstrʌktərz/| n.|阻碍者，妨碍者|People or things that obstruct or hinder.  Those who create obstacles or impediments.|例句：The protesters were acting as obstructers, blocking the road and preventing traffic from flowing.  抗议者们充当了阻碍者，封锁了道路，阻止了交通通行。

例句：The fallen tree was a major obstructer, preventing access to the trail. 倒下的树木是一个主要的障碍物，阻止了进入小径的通道。


近义词：blockers, hinderers,impeders,obstructors|反义词：helpers, facilitators, assistants|名词
