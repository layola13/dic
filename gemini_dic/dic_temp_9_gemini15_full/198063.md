shoeshiner:/ˈʃuːʃaɪnər/ | n. |擦鞋匠|A person whose job is to clean and shine shoes.  |The shoeshiner polished my shoes to a brilliant shine.  擦鞋匠把我的鞋子擦得锃亮。| cobbler, bootblack |  (无明显反义词) | noun


USAGE->

The shoeshiner shines shoes every day. = 擦鞋匠每天都擦鞋。
He shines shoes for a living. = 他靠擦鞋为生。


一般过去时:
The shoeshiner shined my shoes yesterday. = 擦鞋匠昨天擦了我的鞋。
He shined many pairs of shoes before closing. = 他在关门前擦了很多双鞋。

The shoeshiner has shined hundreds of shoes this week. = 擦鞋匠本周已经擦了数百双鞋。
