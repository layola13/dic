fossilization:/ˌfɒsɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 化石化；石化；僵化 | The process by which organic matter is converted into a fossil.  This can refer to both the geological process and the metaphorical process of ideas or languages becoming fixed and unchanging. | Example: The fossilization of dinosaur bones took millions of years. 恐龙骨骼的石化过程花费了数百万年。  The fossilization of their language skills prevented them from further improvement. 他们语言技能的僵化阻碍了他们进一步提高。 | 近义词：petrification, mineralization, fixation | 反义词：change, development, evolution
