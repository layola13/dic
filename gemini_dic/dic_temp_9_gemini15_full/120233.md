mingers:/ˈmɪŋɡərz/| n. |讨厌的人，邋遢的人|A "minger" is a slang term used primarily in British English to refer to someone considered unattractive or unpleasant.  The term carries a strong negative connotation and is often used informally and dismissively.  It can refer to physical appearance, personality, or both.|例句：He called her a minger, which really hurt her feelings. 他称她为讨厌鬼，这真的伤了她的感情。|近义词：ugly duckling, dog, eyesore,  scruff|反义词：beauty, heartthrob, dreamboat
