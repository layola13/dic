right-handedly:/ˈraɪtˈhændɪdli/| adv. | 右手地，用右手 |  Using the right hand.  This adverb describes actions performed with the right hand. | He wrote the letter right-handedly. 他用右手写信。She skillfully manipulated the tools right-handedly. 她熟练地用右手操作工具。| left-handedly, ambidextrously | left-handedly |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He writes right-handedly. = 他用右手写字。
She eats right-handedly. = 她用右手吃饭。


一般过去时:
He wrote right-handedly. = 他用右手写字。
She ate right-handedly. = 她用右手吃饭。


现在完成时:
He has always written right-handedly. = 他一直用右手写字。
She has consistently eaten right-handedly. = 她一直用右手吃饭。


过去完成时:
He had always written right-handedly before his accident. = 他在事故发生前一直用右手写字。
She had eaten right-handedly her whole life. = 她一生都用右手吃饭。


将来时:
He will continue to write right-handedly. = 他将继续用右手写字。
She will probably eat right-handedly in the future. = 她将来可能会继续用右手吃饭。


虚拟语气:
If he were ambidextrous, he wouldn't write right-handedly. = 如果他是左撇子，他就不会用右手写字。(This example is a bit unnatural,  as it's more likely to say "If he were left-handed...")
I wish I could write right-handedly as well as he does. = 我希望我的右手也能像他一样写字。


被动语态:
(This adverb doesn't typically appear in passive constructions.  The action is inherently active.)


疑问句:
Does he write right-handedly or left-handedly? = 他是右手写字还是左手写字？
How does she perform that task right-handedly? = 她如何熟练地用右手完成这项任务？


否定句:
He doesn't write right-handedly; he's left-handed. = 他不用右手写字；他是左撇子。
She couldn't open the jar right-handedly, so she tried with her left hand. = 她无法用右手打开罐子，所以她尝试用左手。


PHRASE->
There are not common phrases directly using "right-handedly". The adverb is typically used to describe a specific action.  Instead, phrases related to handedness are more common:


right-handed person = 右撇子
left-handed person = 左撇子
ambidextrous person = 左右手都能用的人
to be right-handed = 是右撇子
to be left-handed = 是左撇子
