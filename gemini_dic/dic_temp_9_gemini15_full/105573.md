kinder:/ˈkɪndər/ | adj. |善良的，仁慈的，温和的 |Kind describes someone who is friendly, generous, and considerate towards others. It suggests a gentle and caring nature.  |He is a kind and gentle man. 他是一个善良温和的人。|She was kind enough to help me with my bags. 她好心地帮我提包。|cruel, unkind, harsh |mean


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is kind to animals. = 他对动物很善良。
She is always kind to everyone. = 她总是对每个人都很友善。


一般过去时:
He was kind to me when I was sick. = 我生病的时候，他对我很好。
They were kind enough to offer us a ride. = 他们好心地载我们一程。


现在完成时:
She has always been kind to her neighbours. = 她一直对她邻居都很友善。
He has been kind enough to donate to charity. = 他好心地捐款给了慈善机构。


过去完成时:
They had been kind to him for years. = 他们多年来一直对他很好。
She had been kind to her employees, but they still didn't appreciate it. = 她一直对员工很好，但他们仍然不领情。


将来时:
I hope he will be kind to the new students. = 我希望他会对新生友善。
She will be kind to the animals at the shelter. = 她会对收容所的动物们友善。


虚拟语气:
If he were kinder, he would have more friends. = 如果他更善良一些，他就会有更多朋友。
I wish she were kinder to her sister. = 我希望她对她妹妹更友善一些。


被动语态:
The children were kindly treated by the teacher. = 孩子们受到了老师的善待。
The animals are kindly cared for at the sanctuary. = 动物们在保护区得到了很好的照顾。


疑问句:
Are you kind to your friends? = 你对你的朋友们友善吗？
Is he kind enough to help others? = 他够善良去帮助别人吗？


否定句:
He isn't kind to strangers. = 他对陌生人不友善。
She wasn't kind to the waiter. = 她对服务员不友善。
