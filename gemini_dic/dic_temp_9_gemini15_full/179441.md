reductionists:/rɪˈdʌkʃənɪsts/| n.|还原论者，简化论者|Those who believe that complex systems can be understood by reducing them to their simplest components.  Reductionists often focus on the parts rather than the whole.  They believe that understanding the individual components will provide a complete understanding of the system.|Example:  Some reductionists believe that the study of individual cells will fully explain the workings of a complex organism.  一些还原论者认为，对单个细胞的研究将完全解释复杂生物体的运作方式。|近义词：simplifiers, minimalists|反义词：holists, systems thinkers|名词
