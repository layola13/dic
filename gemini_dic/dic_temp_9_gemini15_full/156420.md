phytological:/ˌfaɪtəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|植物学的；植物的|Relating to the study of plants.  The term encompasses the structure, function, genetics, ecology, and evolution of plants.|例句：He's pursuing a career in phytological research. 他正在从事植物学研究。|例句：The phytological diversity of the rainforest is astonishing. 雨林的植物多样性令人惊叹。|近义词：botanical|反义词：zoological
