remastering:/ˌriːˈmæstərɪŋ/| n. |重新制作母版；重新制作母带|The process of improving the quality of an existing audio or video recording by using modern techniques.  This often involves enhancing the sound or image quality, removing noise, and improving the overall presentation. |例句：The band decided to remaster their classic album for its 25th anniversary. 乐队决定为他们经典专辑的25周年纪念重新制作母带。

例句：The film studio announced plans for a 4K remastering of the original movie. 电影公司宣布了对这部原始电影进行4K重新制作母带的计划。|近义词：re-recording, refurbishment, restoration |反义词：degradation, deterioration
