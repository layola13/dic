cuddle:/ˈkʌd(ə)l/| vt. & n.|拥抱；依偎|To hold close and affectionately; to embrace tenderly.|例句：The mother cuddled her baby close. 母亲紧紧地抱着她的婴儿。|例句：He gave his dog a cuddle. 他抱了抱他的狗。|近义词：hug, embrace, snuggle|反义词：push away, ignore

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I cuddle my cat every night. = 我每晚都抱我的猫。
She cuddles her teddy bear before bed. = 她睡前都会抱着她的泰迪熊。

一般过去时:
They cuddled on the sofa watching TV. = 他们依偎在沙发上看电视。
He cuddled his daughter to comfort her. = 他抱着女儿安慰她。

现在完成时:
I have cuddled with my partner many times. = 我和我伴侣拥抱过很多次了。
She has cuddled her baby to sleep tonight. = 她今晚已经抱着她的宝宝睡着了。

过去完成时:
They had cuddled before the movie started. = 电影开始前他们已经拥抱过了。
The child had cuddled his blanket all night. = 孩子整晚都抱着他的毯子。


将来时:
We will cuddle by the fire tonight. = 我们今晚会在火炉旁依偎。
I will cuddle my dog after work. = 我下班后会抱抱我的狗。

虚拟语气:
If it were cold, I would cuddle up in a blanket. = 如果天气冷，我会裹着毯子依偎着。
I wish I could cuddle my pet right now. = 我真希望我现在能抱抱我的宠物。

被动语态:
The baby was cuddled by its mother. = 婴儿被母亲抱着。
The stuffed animal was cuddled by the child. = 毛绒玩具被孩子抱着。

疑问句:
Do you like to cuddle? = 你喜欢拥抱吗？
Did they cuddle last night? = 他们昨晚拥抱了吗？

否定句:
I don't cuddle strangers. = 我不拥抱陌生人。
She didn't cuddle the cat today. = 她今天没有抱猫。


PHRASE->
cuddle up:  依偎，蜷缩
cuddle close: 紧紧拥抱
cuddle together: 互相拥抱
give someone a cuddle: 抱抱某人
cuddle bunny:  (昵称) 乖乖兔 (指喜欢拥抱的人)
