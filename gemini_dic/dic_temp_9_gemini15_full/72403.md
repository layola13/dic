fluent:/ˈfluːənt/| adj.| 流利的，流畅的 |Able to speak or write a language easily and smoothly.  Characterized by a smooth, easy flow. |例句：She's fluent in French and German. 她法语和德语都很流利。|例句：His fluent writing style made the book a pleasure to read. 他流畅的写作风格使这本书读起来很愉快。|近义词：articulate, smooth, effortless |反义词：halting, hesitant, stumbling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He speaks fluent English. = 他说一口流利的英语。
She writes fluent prose. = 她写流畅的散文。
