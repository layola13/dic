erasable: /ɪˈreɪsəbl/ | adj. | 可擦除的，可抹去的 |  capable of being erased or rubbed out.  This word describes something from which markings can be removed. | Example:  The whiteboard is erasable, so we can reuse it.  白板是可擦写的，所以我们可以重复使用它。  The pen uses erasable ink. 这支笔使用可擦墨水。|近义词：removable, deletable | 反义词：permanent, indelible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This pen is erasable. = 这支笔是可擦的。
The marker is erasable. = 这支记号笔是可擦的。

一般过去时:
The writing was erasable. = 字迹是可以擦掉的。
The mistake was easily erasable. = 错误很容易擦掉。

现在完成时:
The board has been erasable for years. = 这个黑板多年来一直是可擦的。
The ink has proven to be erasable. = 墨水已被证明是可擦的。

过去完成时:
The notes had been erasable before the rain. = 在下雨之前，笔记是可以擦掉的。
The information had been erasable until the heat set in. = 直到天气变热之前，信息都是可以擦除的。

将来时:
The new whiteboard will be erasable. = 新的白板将是可擦写的。
This feature will make the writing erasable. = 此功能将使书写可以擦除。

虚拟语气:
If the ink were erasable, I would use it. = 如果墨水是可擦的，我会用它。
I wish the marker were erasable. = 我希望这支记号笔是可擦的。

疑问句:

否定句:
