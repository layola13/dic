词源:
The word "nonclerical" is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "clerical," which derives from the Latin word "clericus" meaning "clerk" or "member of the clergy."  Therefore, "nonclerical" literally means "not related to clerical work or the clergy."
