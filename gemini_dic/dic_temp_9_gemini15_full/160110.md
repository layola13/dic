pomme:/pɒm/| n.|苹果，(法语)|Apple, especially in French contexts.  It can also refer to things shaped like an apple, such as the pomme de terre (potato).|The children were picking pommes from the tree.孩子们正在从树上摘苹果。|近义词：apple, fruit|反义词：无
