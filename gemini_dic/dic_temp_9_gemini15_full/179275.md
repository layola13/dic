redistributions:/ˌriːdɪsˌtrɪbjuːʃənz/| n. |再分配；重新分配|The act of distributing something again, especially after it has been collected or centralized.  It often implies a fairer or more equitable distribution than the original. |例句：The government announced new policies for the redistributions of wealth. 政府宣布了新的财富再分配政策。|例句：The company is undertaking redistributions of its resources to improve efficiency. 公司正在进行资源重新分配以提高效率。|近义词：reallocation, redistribution, reallotment |反义词：centralization, concentration |名词
