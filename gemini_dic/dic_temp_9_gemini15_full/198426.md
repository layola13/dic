shredded:/ʃˈredɪd/ | adj. | 切碎的，撕碎的 | Torn into thin strips or pieces; reduced to fragments.  | Example: The shredded cheese was sprinkled on the pizza.  (磨碎的奶酪撒在比萨饼上。)  The documents were shredded to protect confidential information. (文件被切碎以保护机密信息。) |近义词：torn, fragmented, minced, ripped |反义词：whole, intact, unbroken


USAGE->

The machine shreds paper efficiently. = 这台机器高效地切碎纸张。
She shreds vegetables for her salad. = 她把蔬菜切碎做沙拉。
