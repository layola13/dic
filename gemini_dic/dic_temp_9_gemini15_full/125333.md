multilateralisms:/ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlɪzəm/| n.|多边主义；多边关系|The system of international relations in which states cooperate through international organizations and agreements.  It emphasizes collaboration and interdependence among multiple actors to address global challenges.|例句：The success of multilateralisms depends on the willingness of states to compromise and cooperate. 多边主义的成功取决于各国愿意妥协和合作。|例句：The rise of nationalism poses a significant threat to multilateralisms.民族主义的兴起对多边主义构成了重大威胁。|近义词：internationalism, globalism, cooperation|反义词：unilateralism, isolationism, protectionism|名词
