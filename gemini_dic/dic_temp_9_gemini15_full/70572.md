fillet:/ˈfɪlət/ | n. v. |鱼片；去骨的肉；(动词)切成鱼片；去除…的骨头 | A fillet is a boneless cut of meat or fish.  | The chef expertly filleted the salmon.  厨师熟练地切了鲑鱼片。  The butcher filleted the chicken breast.  屠夫去掉了鸡胸肉的骨头。| steak, slice | whole, bone-in | noun, verb
