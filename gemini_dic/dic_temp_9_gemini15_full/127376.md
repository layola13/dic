neand:/niːænd/|n.|穴居人；尼安德特人|Neanderthal is an archaic human species that lived in Eurasia until about 40,000 years ago.  They are known for their robust build and distinctive brow ridges.|Example:  Scientists are still studying the DNA of Neanderthals to understand their relationship to modern humans.|近义词：Neanderthal |反义词：Homo sapiens
