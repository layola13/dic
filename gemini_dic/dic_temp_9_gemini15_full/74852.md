frequented:/frɪˈkwɛntɪd/ | v. | 常去的，常访问的 |  To visit (a place) often.  |例句：He frequented the local pub. 他经常去当地酒吧。| 近义词：visited, patronized, haunted | 反义词：avoided, shunned | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frequents that coffee shop. = 他经常去那家咖啡店。
They frequent the same restaurant every week. = 他们每周都光顾同一家餐馆。


一般过去时:
She frequented that bookstore before it closed. = 那家书店关门前，她经常去那里。
We frequented the beach during the summer. = 夏天期间，我们经常去海滩。


现在完成时:
I have frequented this gym for three years. = 我已经在这家健身房锻炼三年了。
They have frequented that bar since college. = 他们从大学时代就经常去那家酒吧。


过去完成时:
He had frequented that club before he met her. = 他在遇到她之前就经常去那个俱乐部了。
We had frequented that restaurant many times before it changed owners. = 在那家餐馆易主之前，我们已经去过很多次了。


将来时:
I will frequent this library more often now that I have a membership. = 现在我有会员卡了，我将更经常去这个图书馆。
They will frequent the new park once it opens. = 新公园开业后，他们将会经常去那里。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would frequent the museum more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常去博物馆。
I wish I could frequent that restaurant more often, but it's too expensive. = 我希望我能更经常去那家餐馆，但它太贵了。


被动语态:
The cafe was frequented by many students. = 这家咖啡馆常被许多学生光顾。
That bar is frequented by locals and tourists alike. = 那家酒吧深受当地人和游客的喜爱。


疑问句:
Do you frequent any local pubs? = 你经常去哪些当地的酒吧？
Where did you frequent during your trip to London? = 你在伦敦旅行期间经常去哪里？


否定句:
He doesn't frequent that restaurant anymore. = 他不再经常去那家餐馆了。
She didn't frequent that shop after the service declined. = 服务下降后，她就不再经常去那家店了。


PHRASE->
frequently frequented = 经常光顾
well-frequented = 顾客很多的，常客很多的
less frequented = 不常去的
highly frequented = 非常受欢迎的
seldom frequented = 很少有人去的
