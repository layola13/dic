pricklier:/ˈprɪkləri/| adj. |更刺的；更易怒的；更棘手的 | More prickly; more irritable; more difficult.  The path became pricklier as we went higher.  |近义词：thornier, more irritating, more challenging |反义词：smoother, gentler, easier |形容词
