reinhabiting:/ˌriːɪnˈhæbɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新居住；重新栖息|The act of moving back into a place or area that has been empty or abandoned,  often by people or animals.  It implies a return after a period of absence or desertion. |例句：After the flood subsided, the villagers began reinhabiting their homes.  洪水退去后，村民们开始重新居住他们的家园。

例句：The abandoned building is slowly being reinhabited by birds.  那座废弃的建筑物正慢慢地被鸟类重新栖息。


近义词：reoccupy, repopulate, resettle

反义词：abandon, vacate, desert


词性:动词
