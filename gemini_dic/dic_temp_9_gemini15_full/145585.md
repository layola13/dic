overconcentrating: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度集中；过度专注 |  The act of focusing too intensely on a single task or aspect, often to the detriment of other important matters. It implies an unhealthy level of concentration that can lead to stress, anxiety, or reduced efficiency.  | 例句：He was overconcentrating on the details and missed the bigger picture. 他过于专注于细节而忽略了大局。

He was overconcentrating on his work and neglected his health. 他过度专注于工作而忽略了自己的健康。

近义词：hyperfocus, overfocus, fixating

反义词：distracted, unfocused, inattentive

词性: 动词
