rewilding:/ˌriːˈwaɪldɪŋ/| n. v.|重返自然；野化；让…恢复自然状态|The process of restoring areas of land to their natural state, allowing wildlife to flourish.  It involves reintroducing native species, removing invasive species, and allowing natural processes to take over.|例句：The project aims to rewild large tracts of land, restoring habitats for endangered species.  该项目旨在让大片土地重返自然，恢复濒危物种的栖息地。

例句：Scientists are experimenting with different methods of rewilding degraded ecosystems. 科学家们正在尝试不同的方法来使退化的生态系统恢复自然。


近义词：renaturalization, restoration, regeneration|反义词：urbanization, development, deforestation|名词
