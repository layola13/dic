righto:/ˈraɪtəʊ/| int.| 好吧；行；知道了 | An informal British English interjection expressing agreement, acceptance, or understanding.  It's a shortened and slightly altered version of "all right".|例句： "Shall we go then?" "Righto!"  “那我们走吧？”“好吧！”


例句： "Can you help me with this?" "Righto, I'll be right there." “你能帮我一下吗？”“好的，我马上来。”


近义词：okay, alright, all right, yes, sure


反义词：no, nope


词性:int. (感叹词)
