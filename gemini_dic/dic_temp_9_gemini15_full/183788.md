rescuing: /ˈreskjuːɪŋ/ | v. |营救；援救；解救 | The act of saving someone or something from a dangerous or difficult situation.  |例句：The firefighters were busy rescuing people from the burning building. 消防队员正忙着从燃烧的建筑物中救人。

例句：He spent his life rescuing animals from abusive situations. 他一生都在将动物从虐待环境中解救出来。


近义词：saving, liberating, delivering, freeing | 反义词：abandoning, endangering, jeopardizing | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rescuing the trapped miners. = 他们正在营救被困的矿工。
He rescues stray cats every weekend. = 他每个周末都救助流浪猫。


一般过去时:
The police rescued the hostages. = 警方解救了人质。
She rescued a kitten from a tree. = 她从树上救下了一只小猫。


现在完成时:
They have rescued over 100 people from the flood. = 他们已经从洪水中救出了100多人。
We have rescued several abandoned dogs this year. = 今年我们已经救助了几只被遗弃的狗。


过去完成时:
The firefighters had rescued everyone before the building collapsed. = 在建筑物倒塌之前，消防队员已经救出了所有人。
She had rescued him from a burning car before the explosion. = 在爆炸发生之前，她已经把他从燃烧的汽车中救了出来。


将来时:
They will rescue the survivors as soon as possible. = 他们将尽快营救幸存者。
The Coast Guard will be rescuing the sailors from the sinking ship. = 海岸警卫队将营救沉船上的水手。


虚拟语气:
If I had a boat, I would rescue them. = 如果我有一艘船，我会去救他们。
I wish I could have rescued the bird. = 我真希望我能救下那只鸟。


被动语态:
The hostages were rescued by the police. = 人质被警方解救了。
The cat was rescued from the tree by a kind neighbour. = 那只猫被一位好心的邻居从树上救了下来。


疑问句:
Are they rescuing the animals? = 他们正在救助动物吗？
Will the soldiers be rescued soon? = 士兵们很快会被救出来吗？


否定句:
They did not rescue the stranded boaters. = 他们没有营救被困的划船者。
The rescuers couldn't rescue all the victims. = 救援人员无法营救所有受害者。
