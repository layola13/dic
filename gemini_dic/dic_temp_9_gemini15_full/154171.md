periodical:/ˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkəl/| n. |期刊，杂志；定期刊物 |A periodical is a publication that is issued at regular intervals, such as weekly, monthly, or quarterly.  It often contains articles, essays, stories, or other forms of writing on a variety of topics. |Example:  The library subscribes to many scientific periodicals. 图书馆订阅了许多科学期刊。|Example: She writes for several fashion periodicals. 她为几家时尚杂志撰稿。|近义词：magazine, journal, publication |反义词：None


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He reads a periodical every week. 他每周读一本期刊。
This magazine publishes a new periodical monthly. 这本杂志每月出版一本新期刊。


一般过去时:

They subscribed to that periodical last year. 他们去年订阅了那本期刊。


现在完成时:
I have read several periodicals this month. 这个月我已经读了几本期刊。
She has been writing for this periodical for five years. 她已经为这份期刊撰稿五年了。
