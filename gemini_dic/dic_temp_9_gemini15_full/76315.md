gabfest:/ˈɡæbfɛst/| n. |闲聊；长篇大论；喋喋不休|A gabfest is a lengthy and often informal conversation, typically involving much talk and little action.  It often implies a casual and sometimes gossipy nature. |例句：The office Christmas party turned into a massive gabfest about everyone's personal lives.  办公室圣诞派对变成了关于每个人私生活的冗长闲聊。

例句：After the game, the players engaged in a lively gabfest about the referee's controversial calls.  比赛结束后，球员们就裁判有争议的判罚展开了热烈的讨论。


近义词：chat, conversation, discussion, bull session, chinwag

反义词：silence, quiet, brevity


词性：名词
