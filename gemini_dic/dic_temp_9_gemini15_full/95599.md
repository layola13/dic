incommode:/ˌɪnkənˈmoʊd/ | vt. |使不安；打扰；使不便 | To cause inconvenience or discomfort to; to trouble or bother. |例句：I'm sorry to incommode you, but could you help me with this?  对不起打扰您了，您能帮我一下吗？|例句：The constant noise incommoded the residents.  持续的噪音扰乱了居民。|近义词：bother, disturb, inconvenience, trouble |反义词：help, assist, accommodate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music incommodes me. = 吵闹的音乐让我不舒服。
This policy incommodes many small businesses. = 这项政策给许多小型企业带来了不便。

一般过去时:
The unexpected rain incommoded our picnic plans. = 突如其来的雨水打乱了我们的野餐计划。
The delay incommoded the passengers. = 延误给乘客带来了不便。

现在完成时:
The construction work has incommoded local residents for months. = 建设工程已经扰乱了当地居民几个月了。
This situation has incommoded us greatly. = 这种情况给我们带来了很大的不便。

过去完成时:
They had already incommoded themselves by packing too much. = 他们因为打包太多东西而给自己带来了不便。
The bad weather had incommoded our journey. = 恶劣的天气已经影响了我们的旅程。

将来时:
The new road construction will incommode traffic for the next year. = 新道路的建设将在未来一年内给交通带来不便。
This change will incommode some users. = 这一改变会给一些用户带来不便。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it would incommode you, I wouldn't have asked. = 如果我知道这会给你带来不便，我不会要求的。
I wish this situation wouldn't incommode us any further. = 我希望这种情况不会给我们带来更多不便。

被动语态:
We were incommoded by the heat. = 我们被酷暑所困扰。
The residents were incommoded by the late-night noise. = 居民们被深夜的噪音所打扰。

疑问句:
Does this arrangement incommode you? = 这种安排会给你带来不便吗？
Will the new rules incommode the staff? = 新规会给员工带来不便吗？

否定句:
The new system doesn't incommode users. = 新系统不会给用户带来不便。
I tried to avoid incommoding anyone. = 我试图避免打扰任何人。


PHRASE->
to be incommoded by = 被…所打扰
highly incommoded = 非常不便
seriously incommoded = 严重打扰
deeply incommoded = 深受其扰
utterly incommoded = 完全被打扰


词源:
From Old French *incommoder, from Latin incommodus "unsuitable, inconvenient," from in- "not" + commodus "suitable, convenient."
