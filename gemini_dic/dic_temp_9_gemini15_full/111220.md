lithosphere:/ˈlɪθəˌsfɪər/| n. |岩石圈|The rigid outer part of the earth, consisting of the crust and upper mantle.|例句：The lithosphere is broken into several large and small plates.  岩石圈被分成几个大小不等的板块。|例句：The movement of the lithosphere plates causes earthquakes and volcanic eruptions. 岩石圈板块的运动导致地震和火山爆发。|近义词：crust (in this context)|反义词：atmosphere, hydrosphere
