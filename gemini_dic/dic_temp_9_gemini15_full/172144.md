purs:/pɜːrz/| n.|钱包；钱袋；手提包|A purse is a small bag, typically carried by women, used to hold money, cards, and other small items.|例句：She reached into her purse for her keys. 她伸手进钱包拿钥匙。|近义词：handbag, wallet, pouch|反义词:None

USAGE->

She carries a small purse. = 她提着一个小的钱包。
He always keeps his wallet in his purse. = 他总是把钱包放在他的手提包里。
