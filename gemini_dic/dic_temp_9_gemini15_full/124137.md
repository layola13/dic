morticing:/ˈmɔːtɪsɪŋ/| v.|榫眼制作；榫接；|The act of cutting a mortise (a hole) in a piece of wood to receive a tenon (a projecting piece of wood).  This is a woodworking technique used to join pieces of wood together.|例句：He's morticing the joints for extra strength. 他正在制作榫眼以增强强度。

例句：The carpenter skillfully morticed the two pieces of wood together. 木匠熟练地将两块木头榫接在一起。


近义词：joining, fitting, assembling|反义词：disassembling, separating, disconnecting
