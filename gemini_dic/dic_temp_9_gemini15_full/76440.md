gainfulness:/ˈɡeɪnfʊlnəs/| n. |获利能力；有益性；有价值性|The state or quality of being gainful; profitability; usefulness.|例句：The gainfulness of the investment was questionable. 这项投资的获利能力值得怀疑。

例句：The project's gainfulness is dependent on market conditions.  这个项目的获利能力取决于市场环境。


近义词：profitability, usefulness, value, advantage |反义词：unprofitability, uselessness, worthlessness


词源:  Derived from the adjective "gainful," meaning producing profit or advantage.  The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective into a noun, indicating the state or quality of being gainful.  Ultimately, it traces back to the Old English verb "gewinnan," meaning "to gain" or "to win."
