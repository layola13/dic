filtered:/ˈfɪltərd/ | adj. | 过滤的；经过筛选的 |  Having been passed through a filter; purified or selected.  | Example: The filtered water is safe to drink.  过滤后的水可以安全饮用。 The filtered results showed a clear trend. 过滤后的结果显示出一个明显的趋势。|近义词：screened, purified, refined |反义词：unfiltered, raw, unprocessed |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water is filtered before it is bottled. = 水在装瓶前经过过滤。
This app filters out unwanted content. = 这个应用程序过滤掉不需要的内容。


一般过去时:
They filtered the coffee grounds from the brewed coffee. = 他们把咖啡渣从冲好的咖啡里过滤掉。

现在完成时:
We have filtered all the spam emails. = 我们已经过滤掉了所有垃圾邮件。
She has filtered the images to improve their quality. = 她已经对图像进行了过滤以提高其质量。


过去完成时:
