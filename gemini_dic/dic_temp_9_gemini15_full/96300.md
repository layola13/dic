indigenous:/ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs/ | adj. | 本地的；土著的；土生土长的 | Belonging originally to a place; native.  |例句：The kangaroo is indigenous to Australia. 袋鼠是澳大利亚特有的动物。 |例句：Indigenous peoples often have unique cultural traditions. 土著居民通常拥有独特的文化传统。|近义词：native, aboriginal, autochthonous |反义词：foreign, alien, exotic

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plants indigenous to this region are well-adapted to the climate. = 这个地区的本地植物非常适应当地的气候。
Many indigenous languages are disappearing. = 许多土著语言正在消失。


一般过去时:
The indigenous population of the island was decimated by disease. = 岛屿上的土著居民因疾病而大量减少。
Researchers studied the indigenous cultures of the Amazon. = 研究人员研究了亚马逊地区的土著文化。


现在完成时:
Scientists have identified several new indigenous species in the rainforest. = 科学家们在雨林中发现了几个新的本地物种。
The government has promised to protect the rights of indigenous communities. = 政府承诺保护土著居民的权利。


过去完成时:
Before colonization, the indigenous people had a thriving society. = 在殖民之前，土著人民拥有一个繁荣的社会。
The indigenous population had already suffered greatly from drought before the flood. = 在洪水来临之前，土著居民已经遭受了严重的旱灾。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
