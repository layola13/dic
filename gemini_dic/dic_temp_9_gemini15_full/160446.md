popularizers:/ˈpɑːpjʊləraɪzərz/ | n. |普及者；推广者 |Those who make something popular or widely known. They simplify complex ideas or subjects, making them accessible to a wider audience.  | Examples:  Science popularizers often use analogies and storytelling to explain difficult concepts. 科学普及者经常使用比喻和讲故事来解释复杂的概念。 Many popularizers of yoga have simplified the practice to make it more approachable. 许多瑜伽推广者简化了练习，使其更容易上手。 |近义词：propagandists, disseminators, advocates |反义词：obscurantists, elitists
