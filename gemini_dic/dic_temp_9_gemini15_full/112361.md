lorry:/ˈlɒri/| n.|卡车，货车|A lorry is a large motor vehicle used for transporting goods.  It is typically larger than a van but smaller than a semi-trailer truck.|例句：The lorry was carrying a heavy load of bricks. 这辆卡车装载着大量的砖块。|例句：The driver carefully maneuvered the lorry through the narrow streets. 司机小心地驾驶着卡车穿过狭窄的街道。|近义词：truck, van (smaller),  articulated lorry (larger)|反义词：car, bicycle
