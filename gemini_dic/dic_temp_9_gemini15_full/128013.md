nephrostome:/ˈnef(r)oʊstoʊm/| n. |肾口；肾管口|The nephrostome is the ciliated funnel-shaped opening of a nephridium, a primitive excretory organ found in invertebrates.  It filters waste products from the coelom (body cavity).|Example:  The nephrostomes of the earthworm are responsible for collecting waste fluids from its body cavity.|近义词:nephridial opening|反义词:nephridiopore
