cosmorama:/ˌkɒzməˈrɑːmə/| n. |全景画，宇宙全景图 |A cosmorama is a type of panoramic painting or diorama that presents a wide-angle view of a landscape or scene, often featuring extensive perspectives and detailed depictions.  It aims to give the viewer a sense of being immersed in the depicted environment.  The term often evokes a sense of wonder and vastness. | Example: The artist created a breathtaking cosmorama of the ancient city, capturing its grandeur in exquisite detail.  艺术家创作了一幅令人叹为观止的古城全景画，以精美的细节捕捉了它的宏伟。 |近义词：panorama, diorama, vista |反义词：close-up, detail shot

词源:

The word "cosmorama" is a blend of "cosmos" (meaning "universe" or "world") and "orama" (a suffix indicating a panoramic view). It reflects the intention of the artistic representation to capture a vast and comprehensive view of a scene, often evoking a sense of the entire world or a significant portion thereof.
