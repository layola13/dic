piperazine:/ˈpaɪpəzɪn/| n. |哌嗪|A heterocyclic organic compound with two nitrogen atoms in a six-membered ring. It is used in the manufacture of various pharmaceuticals, including anthelmintics (drugs that kill parasitic worms) and tranquilizers.  Piperazine is a colorless crystalline solid that is soluble in water. |Example: Piperazine is a common component in some deworming medications.  哌嗪是某些驱虫药物中的常见成分。|近义词: (no close synonyms, as it's a specific chemical compound)|反义词: (no direct antonyms)|名词
