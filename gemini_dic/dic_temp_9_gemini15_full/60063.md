edutainment:/ˌɛdʒuˈteɪnmənt/| n. |寓教于乐；教育娱乐|  The blending of education and entertainment to make learning fun and engaging.  Edutainment aims to inform and entertain simultaneously, often using interactive or engaging formats.  |例句：That museum's new exhibit is a great example of edutainment, combining historical facts with interactive displays.|近义词：infotainment, entertainment education|反义词：boredom, tedium
