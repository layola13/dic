/nɑːnpleɪz/ | n. |非戏剧作品；非戏剧性事件 |Nonplays refers to works or events that are not theatrical performances or dramatic in nature.  They might be literary works, musical compositions, or any other form of artistic expression that doesn't involve dramatic staging. |例句：His latest work is a collection of nonplays, focusing on lyrical prose and evocative imagery.他的最新作品是一系列非戏剧作品，侧重于抒情散文和富有感染力的意象。  |近义词：non-dramatic works, non-theatrical pieces |反义词：plays, dramas
