rejournment:/rɪˈdʒɜːrnmənt/ | n. |休会；延期；中止 | The adjournment of the meeting was announced after a heated debate.  会议在激烈的辩论后宣布休会。|近义词：postponement, suspension, recess |反义词：continuation, resumption |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee usually adjourns at 5 pm. = 委员会通常下午5点休会。
Adjournment often follows a long debate. = 漫长的辩论之后通常会休会。


一般过去时:
The judge adjourned the court until tomorrow. = 法官将法庭休庭至明天。
The meeting adjourned after three hours of discussion. = 会议在进行了三个小时的讨论后休会。


现在完成时:
The court has adjourned for the day. = 法庭已经休庭一天了。
They have adjourned the meeting several times. = 他们已经几次休会了。


过去完成时:
The meeting had already adjourned when he arrived. = 他到达时会议已经休会了。
The committee had adjourned before the crucial vote. = 关键投票之前委员会已经休会了。


将来时:
The court will adjourn until next week. = 法庭将休庭到下周。
The committee will adjourn after the presentation. = 演示结束后，委员会将休会。


虚拟语气:
If the judge adjourned the court, we could leave. = 如果法官休庭，我们就可以离开了。
I wish the meeting had adjourned earlier. = 我希望会议早点休会。


被动语态:
The meeting was adjourned due to a lack of quorum. = 由于缺乏法定人数，会议被休会了。
The proceedings were adjourned until further notice. = 诉讼程序暂停，直到另行通知。


疑问句:
When will the meeting be adjourned? = 会议什么时候休会？
Has the court adjourned yet? = 法庭休庭了吗？


否定句:
The meeting did not adjourn until late at night. = 会议直到深夜才休会。
The judge did not adjourn the court despite the objections. = 尽管有异议，法官也没有休庭。


PHRASE->
adjournment sine die:  无限期休会
adjournment until further notice:  无限期休会，直到另行通知
adjournment of the court: 法庭休庭
adjournment of the meeting: 会议休会
adjournment in favour of:  为了…而休会
