pipeline:/ˈpaɪplaɪn/| n.|管道；管线；流程；渠道|A pipeline is a system of pipes or tubes that is used to transport liquids or gases over long distances.  It can also refer to a series of processes or stages in a system. |例句：The oil pipeline transports crude oil from the oil field to the refinery.  这条输油管道将原油从油田输送到炼油厂。

The company has a new pipeline for developing new products. 这家公司有一个开发新产品的新流程。|近义词：conduit, channel, system|反义词：None directly, but contextually could be "disruption" or "interruption"|名词
