preconfessed:/ˌpriːkənˈfɛst/| adj.| 预先承认的；事先坦白的 | Previously confessed; admitted beforehand.  |The preconfessed criminal showed remorse.  预先承认罪行的罪犯表现出了悔恨。 |近义词：pre-admitted, previously acknowledged |反义词：unconfessed, undisclosed

He preconfesses his mistakes regularly. 他定期预先承认自己的错误。
