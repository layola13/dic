philologically:/ˌfɪləˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 从语言学的角度；语言学地 |  From the perspective of philology; in a way that relates to the study of language in its historical and cultural development.  | The text was analyzed philologically to determine its origins. 这篇文章被从语言学的角度分析以确定其来源。 |  linguistically, etymologically | unphilologically


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs do.  It describes *how* something is done.  Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.


PHRASE->
philologically analyzed:  语言学分析的

philologically investigated: 语言学研究的
