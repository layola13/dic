market:/ˈmɑːrkɪt/ /ˈmɑːrkət/ | n. v. | 市场；集市；市场需求；销售；上市 |  A market is a place where goods and services are bought and sold.  It can refer to a physical location, like a farmers' market, or a broader concept, like the stock market.  As a verb, it means to sell or promote something. | 例句：The farmer's market is bustling with activity on Saturday mornings. 周六早上农贸市场热闹非凡。


The company is trying to market its new product to a younger demographic.  这家公司正试图向更年轻的人群推销其新产品。| 近义词：marketplace, bazaar, exchange | 反义词：monopoly
