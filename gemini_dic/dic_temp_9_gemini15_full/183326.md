reprobate:/ˈreproʊbeɪt/| n. adj. v.| 堕落者；不肖子；谴责；|a person who is morally corrupt or depraved; to condemn or disapprove of someone or something.|The reprobate spent his inheritance on gambling and alcohol.  这个堕落者把他的遗产都花在了赌博和酒精上。|He was a reprobate in his youth, but later found religion. 他年轻时是个堕落的人，但后来信了教。|saint, angel | virtuous, righteous | noun, verb, adjective
