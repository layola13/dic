grizzling:/ˈɡrɪzəlɪŋ/| v.|抱怨；发牢骚；淅淅沥沥地下雨 | to complain or grumble in a whining or petulant manner; to rain lightly and persistently.|例句：The children were grizzling all the way home. 孩子们一路上都在抱怨。The rain was grizzling down. 雨淅淅沥沥地下着。|近义词：complaining, grumbling, whining, muttering|反义词：praising, complimenting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is grizzling about his work. = 他在抱怨他的工作。
She grizzles whenever she doesn't get her way. = 她只要不如意就发牢骚。


一般过去时:
The baby grizzled all night. = 婴儿整夜都在哭闹。
He grizzled about the unfairness of the situation. = 他抱怨情况的不公平。


现在完成时:
She has been grizzling all afternoon. = 她整个下午都在抱怨。
He has grizzled about the same thing for weeks. = 他几周来一直在抱怨同一件事。


过去完成时:
They had grizzled for hours before anyone noticed. = 他们在别人注意到之前已经抱怨了好几个小时。
She had grizzled about the cold all morning. = 她整个上午都在抱怨寒冷。


将来时:
I think he will grizzle if we don't invite him. = 我想如果我们不邀请他，他会抱怨的。
The children will probably grizzle during the long car journey. = 孩子们在漫长的车程中可能会抱怨。


虚拟语气:
If I were him, I wouldn't grizzle so much. = 如果我是他，我不会抱怨那么多。
I wish he wouldn't grizzle all the time. = 我希望他不要总是抱怨。


被动语态:  (Grizzle is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Is she grizzling again? = 她又在抱怨了吗？
Why is he grizzling? = 他为什么抱怨？


否定句:
He didn't grizzle at all during the trip. = 他在旅途中一点也没有抱怨。
She didn't grizzle about the extra work. = 她没有抱怨额外的工作。


PHRASE->
grizzling about something = 抱怨某事
to be grizzling = 正在抱怨
constantly grizzling = 不停地抱怨
low grizzling = 轻微的抱怨
start grizzling = 开始抱怨
