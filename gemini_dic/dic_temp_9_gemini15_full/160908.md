post-haste:/ˈpoʊst heɪst/| adj.| 非常迅速地；急忙地 |  With great speed; in a hurry.  The term combines "post" (referring to mail delivery, implying speed) and "haste" (meaning urgency).|例句：He sent the letter post-haste. 他火速寄出了这封信。|近义词：quickly, speedily, rapidly, expeditiously |反义词：slowly, leisurely, deliberately |形容词
