encrinite:/ˈɛnkrɪnaɪt/ | n. |海百合化石|A fossil of an encrinite (sea lily).  Encrinites are the fossilized remains of crinoids, which are marine animals resembling a plant.  They are composed of calcium carbonate and are often found in limestone. |The limestone pavement revealed numerous encrinite fossils, evidence of a thriving ancient ocean.  那块石灰岩路面露出了许多海百合化石，证明了古代海洋的繁荣。|Fossil, crinoid remains|  (no direct antonym)|名词
