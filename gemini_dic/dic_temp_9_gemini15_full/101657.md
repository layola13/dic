irregulars:/ɪˈreɡjələrz/| n. |不规则的;不规则变化的词|Irregulars refer to words or grammatical forms that do not follow the standard rules or patterns.  They often deviate from typical conjugations or declensions.| Example:  English verbs have many irregulars, such as "go," "went," "gone."  英语动词有很多不规则变化，例如“go”、“went”、“gone”。|近义词：exceptions, anomalies, deviations|反义词：regulars, standard, normal|名词
