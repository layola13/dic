recane:/rɪˈkeɪn/| n.|再砍伐，再割；再种植|The act of cutting or harvesting again, typically referring to vegetation or crops.  It can also refer to replanting. |例句：The farmer planned a recane of the sugarcane field after the first harvest.  = 农民计划在第一次收割后再次砍伐甘蔗田。|例句：The damaged forest requires a recane program to restore its previous density. = 受损的森林需要一个再造林计划来恢复其先前的密度。|近义词：replant, reharvest, recultivate|反义词：felling (in the sense of the first cutting), leaving untouched|名词
