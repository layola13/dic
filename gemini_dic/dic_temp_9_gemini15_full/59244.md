e-cigarette: /ˌiːˈsɪɡəˌrɛt/ | n. | 电子烟 | An electronic device that simulates tobacco smoking by heating a nicotine-containing liquid to create an aerosol, which the user then inhales.  It typically consists of a battery, a heating element, and a cartridge containing the e-liquid. | Example:  Many people use e-cigarettes as a way to quit smoking traditional cigarettes.  许多人使用电子烟来戒掉传统香烟。  He switched to vaping with an e-cigarette to reduce his nicotine intake. 他改用电子烟来减少尼古丁摄入量。 | 近义词: vape, electronic vaporizer | 反义词:  traditional cigarette, tobacco cigarette | 名词
