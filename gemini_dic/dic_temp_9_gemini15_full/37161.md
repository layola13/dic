come-to-your-senses: /ˌkʌm tuː jɔːr ˈsɛnsɪz/ | phrasal verb |  恢复理性；醒悟过来；恢复理智 | To regain your reason and good judgment after a period of foolishness or irrational behavior.  |例句：He finally came to his senses and apologized for his actions. 他最终恢复了理智，为自己的行为道歉了。


例句：After spending all his money on lottery tickets, he finally came to his senses and started saving.  在他把所有的钱都花在彩票上之后，他终于恢复了理智，开始存钱了。


近义词：recover your senses, regain your composure, see reason, come to your right mind


反义词：remain irrational, act foolishly, lose your mind, be delirious


词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comes to his senses after a long time. = 他很久之后才恢复理智。
They come to their senses and admit their mistakes. = 他们恢复理智并承认了他们的错误。


一般过去时:
She came to her senses and broke up with him. = 她恢复了理智，并和他分手了。
He came to his senses and realized the danger. = 他恢复了理智，意识到危险。


现在完成时:
He has finally come to his senses and stopped drinking. = 他终于恢复了理智，戒酒了。
She has come to her senses about her impulsive spending. = 她已经意识到自己冲动消费的问题了。


过去完成时:
By then, she had come to her senses and regretted her decision. = 到那时，她已经恢复了理智，后悔自己的决定了。
He had come to his senses before it was too late. = 在一切都太迟之前，他已经恢复了理智。


将来时:
I hope he will come to his senses soon. = 我希望他很快就会恢复理智。
She will come to her senses eventually. = 她最终会恢复理智的。


虚拟语气:
If only he had come to his senses earlier! = 但愿他早点恢复理智！
I wish she would come to her senses and see the truth. = 我希望她能恢复理智，看清真相。


被动语态: (This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Will he ever come to his senses? = 他会恢复理智吗？
Has she come to her senses yet? = 她已经恢复理智了吗？


否定句:
He hasn't come to his senses yet. = 他还没恢复理智。
She never came to her senses. = 她从未恢复理智。



PHRASE->
come to your senses and... = 恢复理智并...
come to your senses about... = 意识到...
finally come to your senses = 最终恢复理智
come to your senses after... = 在...之后恢复理智
It's time you came to your senses. = 你该恢复理智了。
