interceded:/ˌɪntərˈsiːd/| vt.|调解；说情；代祷|To intervene or plead on behalf of another.|例句：The priest interceded with the king on behalf of the prisoners.  牧师为囚犯向国王求情。|例句：She interceded in the dispute between her neighbors. 她调解了她邻居之间的纠纷。|近义词：mediate, intervene, plead|反义词：ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intercedes for his friend. = 他为他朋友求情。
She intercedes in family disputes. = 她调解家庭纠纷。

一般过去时:
The lawyer interceded on my behalf. = 律师为我求情。
They interceded to prevent a war. = 他们调解以防止战争。

现在完成时:
He has interceded on many occasions. = 他多次求过情。
She has interceded for the accused. = 她为被告求过情。

过去完成时:
He had interceded before the judge made a decision. = 在法官做出决定之前，他已求过情。
They had interceded unsuccessfully. = 他们的调解没有成功。

将来时:
I will intercede for you with the manager. = 我将为你在经理面前说情。
She will intercede with the authorities. = 她将向当局求情。

虚拟语气:
If he had interceded earlier, the situation might have been different. = 如果他早点调解，情况可能会有所不同。
I wish she had interceded on my behalf. = 我希望她能为我求情。

被动语态:
The matter was interceded upon by the elders.  长老们为此事进行了调解。
His request was interceded for by his family. 他的家人为他求情。

疑问句:
Will anyone intercede for us? = 会有人为我们求情吗？
Did she intercede in the argument? = 她有没有调解那场争论？

否定句:
He did not intercede in the conflict. = 他没有介入冲突。
She refused to intercede for him. = 她拒绝为他求情。


PHRASE->
