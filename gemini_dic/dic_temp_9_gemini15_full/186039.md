reworded:/ˌriːˈwɜːrdɪd/ | adj. | 改写的；重新措辞的 |  Having been expressed in different words; rephrased.  | 例句：The reworded paragraph was much clearer.  改写后的段落清晰多了。 | 例句：The contract was reworded to clarify the terms. 合同进行了改写以澄清条款。 | 近义词：rephrased, paraphrased, reformulated | 反义词：original, unchanged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The instructions are reworded for clarity. = 指令被改写以求清晰。
The essay is reworded to improve its flow. = 这篇文章被改写以改善其流畅性。

一般过去时:
He reworded his answer to avoid confusion. = 他改写了他的回答以避免混淆。
She reworded the letter to make it more polite. = 她改写了这封信使其更礼貌。

现在完成时:
They have reworded the policy several times. = 他们已经多次改写了这项政策。
The author has reworded the manuscript to meet the deadline. = 作者已经改写了手稿以赶上截止日期。

过去完成时:
He had reworded his speech before delivering it. = 他在发表演讲前已经改写了他的演讲稿。
They had reworded the agreement before signing it. = 他们在签署协议之前已经改写了协议。


将来时:
