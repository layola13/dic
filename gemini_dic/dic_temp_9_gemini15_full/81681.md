greened:/ɡriːnd/| adj.|变绿的；长满绿色的|Turned green or become covered in green vegetation.|The fields greened after the spring rain. 春雨过后，田野变绿了。 The hillsides greened beautifully in the spring. 春天，山坡变得非常翠绿。| leafy, verdant | barren, brown |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The grass greens quickly in the spring. 春天草长得很快。
The leaves green up after the winter. 冬天过后，树叶变绿了。


一般过去时:
The landscape greened dramatically after the rain. 雨后，风景变得生机勃勃。
The plants greened up after we watered them. 我们浇水后，植物变绿了。


现在完成时:
The trees have greened significantly this year. 今年树木长得格外茂盛。
The hills have greened up since the last rain. 自从上次下雨后，山坡已经变绿了。


过去完成时:
The valley had greened before the summer arrived. 夏季到来之前，山谷就已经绿意盎然了。
The fields had greened considerably by the time the harvest began. 收割开始时，田地已经变得相当翠绿了。


将来时:
The desert will green if we implement the water conservation project. 如果我们实施节水项目，沙漠将会变绿。
The fields will green up once the snow melts. 一旦积雪融化，田野就会变绿。


虚拟语气:
If we planted more trees, the area would green quickly. 如果我们种更多的树，这个地区会很快变绿。
I wish the city would green up a bit. 我希望城市能多一些绿意。


被动语态:
The hills were greened by the spring rains. 春雨使山丘变得一片葱茏。
The park is being greened with the new plantings. 公园正在种植新植物以增加绿化。


疑问句:
Has the landscape greened yet? 风景变绿了吗？
Will the trees green up soon? 树木很快会变绿吗？


否定句:
The plants didn't green up this year. 今年植物没有变绿。
The desert hasn't greened in many years. 多年来，沙漠都没有变绿。
