provokes:/prəˈvoʊks/| vt.|激怒；挑衅；引起；导致|to stimulate or incite to action, feeling, or thought; to cause (a reaction or feeling) |His aggressive behavior provoked a fight.他的侵略性行为挑起了争斗。|The news provoked a storm of protest. 这则新闻激起了抗议浪潮。| incite, instigate, trigger| appease, soothe, calm|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music provokes my neighbors. = 吵闹的音乐激怒了我的邻居。
His jokes often provoke laughter. = 他的笑话经常引起笑声。


一般过去时:
The speech provoked a heated debate. = 这篇演讲引发了一场激烈的辩论。
Her words provoked a strong reaction from the audience. = 她的话语引起了观众强烈的反应。


现在完成时:
His actions have provoked widespread criticism. = 他的行为引发了广泛的批评。
The incident has provoked a lot of anger. = 这件事引起了很多愤怒。


过去完成时:
The delay had provoked considerable frustration. = 延误已引起了相当大的沮丧。
They had provoked the attack by their aggressive actions. = 他们因其侵略性行为而激怒了袭击。


将来时:
The announcement will provoke a lot of controversy. = 这则公告将引发很多争议。
I think his behavior will provoke a response from the police. = 我认为他的行为会引起警方的回应。


虚拟语气:
If he provoked her any further, she might explode. = 如果他再激怒她，她可能会爆发。
I wish he hadn't provoked that argument. = 我真希望他没有挑起那场争论。


被动语态:
The riot was provoked by a single careless remark. = 骚乱是由一句粗心的话引起的。
He felt provoked by their constant teasing. = 他感到被他们不断的取笑激怒了。


疑问句:
What provoked this sudden change of plan? = 什么导致了这个突然的计划变更？
Did anything provoke his anger? = 有什么激怒了他吗？


否定句:
His actions did not provoke any response. = 他的行为没有引起任何反应。
The speech didn't provoke much discussion. = 这篇演讲并没有激起太多的讨论。


PHRASE->
provoke a reaction  引发反应
provoke a response  引发回应
provoke a debate  引发辩论
provoke anger  激怒
provoke conflict 挑起冲突
