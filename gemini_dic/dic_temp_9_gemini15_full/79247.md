USAGE->
一般现在时:
He wears glasses every day. 他每天都戴眼镜。
She needs glasses to read. 她需要戴眼镜才能阅读。

一般过去时:
I broke my glasses yesterday. 我昨天把眼镜弄坏了。
He lost his glasses on the bus. 他在公交车上丢了眼镜。

现在完成时:
I have worn glasses since I was ten. 我从十岁起就戴眼镜了。
She has had those glasses for years. 她戴那副眼镜已经好多年了。

过去完成时:
He had already broken his glasses before he went to the optician. 他去看验光师之前已经把眼镜弄坏了。
She had lost her glasses, so she couldn't see the board. 她丢了眼镜，所以看不清黑板。

将来时:
I will buy new glasses next week. 我下周要买副新眼镜。
He will need glasses when he gets older. 他年纪大了以后需要戴眼镜。

虚拟语气:
If I had glasses, I could read this. 如果我有眼镜，我就能读这个了。
I wish I had a spare pair of glasses. 我真希望我有一副备用眼镜。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "glasses" comes from the Middle English word "glassen,"  plural of "glas," ultimately deriving from the Old English word "glæs," meaning "glass."  The meaning evolved to refer to spectacles made of glass lenses in the 14th century.
