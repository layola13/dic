/ˈpærbreɪk/ | n. | 段落；分段符 | A parbreak is a formatting command or symbol that creates a new paragraph, inserting a line break between paragraphs in a text document or other formatted text.  It is often used in typesetting and word processing to separate blocks of text. | Example: The parbreak command created a clear separation between the introduction and the main body of the text.  段落命令在引言和正文之间创建了清晰的分隔。|近义词：paragraph break, line break, new paragraph |反义词：none
