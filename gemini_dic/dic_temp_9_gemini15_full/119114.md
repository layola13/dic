metronymic:/ˌmɛtrəˈnɪmɪk/| adj.|  母系氏族的；源于母系的；|  Relating to or denoting a name derived from one's mother or a female ancestor.  A metronymic surname is one that is passed down through the female line, often reflecting the mother's name or family name. | Example:  Many ancient societies used metronymic naming conventions, unlike the more common patrilineal systems.  许多古代社会使用母系命名约定，这与更常见的父系制度不同。| 近义词：matronymic | 反义词：patronymic
