electrophysiologically:/ɪˌlektrəʊˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 电生理学地 |  Relating to or determined by electrophysiology; in a manner characteristic of electrophysiology.  The study of the electrical properties of living cells and tissues. | 例句：The neurons were studied electrophysiologically to determine their firing patterns.  神经元经过电生理学研究以确定其放电模式。

The heart's activity was monitored electrophysiologically. 心脏活动通过电生理学方法监测。

近义词：electrically (in the context of biological systems), neurophysiologically

反义词：None directly opposite, but  "mechanically" or "chemically" could be considered contrasting approaches.


词性: 副词
