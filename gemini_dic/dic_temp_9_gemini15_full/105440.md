kieserite:/ˈkiːzəraɪt/| n. |水菱镁矿|A hydrous magnesium sulfate mineral, MgSO₄·H₂O.  It is a common evaporite mineral found in salt deposits.  It is named after the German geologist Wilhelm Kieser.|The kieserite sample showed high purity.|近义词：Epsomite (hydrated magnesium sulfate, but with a different crystal structure)|反义词：无
