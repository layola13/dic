inceptive:/ɪnˈsɛptɪv/| adj.|起始的；开端的；初期的| Relating to or denoting the beginning or origin of something.  The adjective "inceptive" describes something that is at the beginning stage or the point of origin of a process, event, or action.  It emphasizes the initial phase or commencement. |例句：The inceptive stages of the project were fraught with difficulties.  项目的初期阶段充满了困难。

The inceptive phase of the disease is often characterized by mild symptoms.  这种疾病的初期阶段通常表现为轻微的症状。|近义词：initial, beginning, nascent, rudimentary|反义词：final, conclusive, terminal, ultimate
