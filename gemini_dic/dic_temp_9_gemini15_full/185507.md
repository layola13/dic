reutilizes:/ˌriːˈjuːtəlaɪzɪz/| v.|重复利用；再利用 | To use something again after it has been used once.  |例句：The company reutilizes its plastic waste to create new products.  这家公司重复利用其塑料垃圾来制造新产品。

例句：This program reutilizes data from previous years to improve accuracy. 这个程序重复利用往年的数据以提高准确性。


近义词：recycle, reuse, repurpose | 反义词：discard, waste, dispose of | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory reutilizes waste materials. = 工厂重复利用废料。
She reutilizes old jars for storage. = 她重复利用旧罐子来储物。

一般过去时:
They reused the containers last week. = 他们上周重复使用了这些容器。
The artist reused old paintings for his collage. = 艺术家将旧画作重复用于他的拼贴画。


现在完成时:
We have reused many plastic bottles this month. = 这个月我们已经重复使用了许多塑料瓶。
The city has reused water from the treatment plant.  = 城市已经重复使用了污水处理厂的水。


过去完成时:
They had reused the packaging before sending it. = 他们寄送之前已经重复使用了包装。
The company had reused the components in several products. = 公司已经在几个产品中重复使用了这些组件。


将来时:
We will reuse the wood for the new project. = 我们将把木材重复用于新的项目。
The factory will reuse the water in the cooling system. = 工厂将重复利用冷却系统中的水。


虚拟语气:
If we reused more materials, we could reduce waste. = 如果我们重复利用更多材料，我们就能减少浪费。
I wish they had reused the boxes instead of throwing them away. = 我希望他们能重复利用那些盒子而不是扔掉它们。


被动语态:

疑问句:
