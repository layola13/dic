incision:/ɪnˈsɪʒən/| n.|切口，切开；割开|A cut made in something, especially in a person's body during surgery.  It can also refer to a sharp cut or division in a more general sense.|例句：The surgeon made a small incision to remove the tumor.  外科医生做了一个小切口来切除肿瘤。

例句：The sharp stone caused a deep incision in the leather.  锋利的石头在皮革上造成一个很深的切口。

近义词：cut, gash, slit, wound|反义词：closure, stitching, healing
