/foʊnoʊˈtaɪpi/ | n. | 语音类型学 | The study of the phonetic characteristics of a language or dialect, particularly focusing on the sounds and their distribution and relationships.  It involves analyzing the inventory of sounds, their patterns of combination, and the ways they are used to distinguish meaning.  Phonotypy often involves detailed transcription and analysis of speech data.  | Example: The phonotypy of the dialect revealed a unique system of vowel harmony.  语音类型学的研究揭示了一种独特的元音和谐系统。 | 近义词: phonology, phonetics | 反义词: N/A
