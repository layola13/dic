flatterer:/ˈflætərər/| n. | 奉承者，拍马屁者 | A flatterer is someone who excessively praises someone else, often insincerely, to gain favor or advantage.  They are skilled at saying what they think others want to hear. | Example: He was a skilled flatterer, always ready with a compliment to win someone over. 他是个老练的奉承者，总是准备好赞美来讨好别人。|近义词：sycophant, fawner, adulator |反义词：critic, detractor |名词
