playbook:/ˈpleɪbʊk/| n.|剧本；策略手册；游戏规则手册|A playbook is a book containing the plays or strategies of a team, especially in sports or business. It can also refer to a detailed plan or strategy for achieving a goal. |例句：The coach reviewed the playbook with the team before the game. 教练在比赛前与队员一起复习了战术手册。

例句：The marketing team developed a detailed playbook for launching the new product. 营销团队制定了详细的新产品发布策略手册。

近义词：strategy guide, game plan, manual, guide

反义词：improvisation, ad-lib
