ludicrousnesses:/luːˈdɪkrəsnɪsəz/| n. |荒谬之事；可笑的行为；滑稽可笑的事情 |The ludicrousnesses of the situation were almost unbearable.  情况的荒谬可笑简直令人难以忍受。|近义词：absurdities, follies, sillinesses |反义词：sensibilities, reasonablenesses


英文解析：The plural form of "ludicrousness," referring to multiple instances of something absurd, ridiculous, or wildly unreasonable.  It highlights a collection of nonsensical or comical actions, statements, or situations.


例句：

His ludicrousnesses during the presentation made it impossible to focus. 他在演示期间的荒谬行为让人无法集中注意力。

The series of ludicrousnesses unfolding before their eyes defied logic.  他们眼前发生的一系列荒谬事情都违反了逻辑。
