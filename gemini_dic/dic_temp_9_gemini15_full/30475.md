centripetally:/ˌsɛntrɪˈpɛtəli/| adj.|向心的；趋向中心的 |Moving or directed toward a center.  The term describes a movement or force directed towards the center of rotation or attraction. |例句：The planets move centripetally around the sun. 行星绕太阳作向心运动。

例句：The water in a whirlpool moves centripetally towards the center.  漩涡中的水向中心作向心运动。


近义词：inward, converging


反义词：centrifugal


词性：形容词
