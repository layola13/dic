emphasises:/ˈɛmfəsaɪzɪz/ | vt. |强调，重视 |To give special importance or prominence to (something) in speaking or writing.|The report emphasises the need for improved safety procedures.这份报告强调需要改进安全程序。|stress, highlight, underline|downplay, ignore|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emphasises the importance of education. 他强调教育的重要性。
She emphasises the need for patience. 她强调需要耐心。

一般过去时:
The speaker emphasised the urgency of the situation. 演讲者强调了局势的紧急性。
The teacher emphasised the key points of the lesson. 老师强调了课程的重点。

现在完成时:
She has emphasised the importance of teamwork throughout her career.  她在整个职业生涯中一直强调团队合作的重要性。
He has emphasised this point in several interviews. 他在几次采访中都强调了这一点。

过去完成时:
He had emphasised the risks before we started the project.  在我们开始项目之前，他已经强调了风险。
They had emphasised the deadline multiple times. 他们已经多次强调了截止日期。

将来时:
She will emphasise the benefits of the new policy. 她将强调新政策的好处。
He will emphasise the importance of collaboration. 他将强调合作的重要性。

虚拟语气:
I wish he had emphasised the dangers more clearly. 我希望他更清楚地强调危险。
If she had emphasised the deadlines earlier, we might have avoided this problem. 如果她早些强调截止日期，我们或许就能避免这个问题。

被动语态:
The importance of safety was emphasised in the training session. 在培训课程中强调了安全的重要性。
The need for change was emphasised by the report. 报告中强调了变革的必要性。

疑问句:
Does he emphasise the practical aspects of the job? 他是否强调工作的实际方面？
What points did the presentation emphasise? 演示文强调了哪些要点？

否定句:
He didn't emphasise the potential risks enough. 他没有充分强调潜在的风险。
The report doesn't emphasise the positive aspects of the situation.  报告没有强调局势的积极方面。
