folk-rock:/ˈfoʊkˌrɑːk/| n. |民谣摇滚|Folk rock is a genre of rock music that incorporates elements of folk music.  It typically features acoustic instruments, storytelling lyrics, and a focus on social or political themes.  The genre emerged in the 1960s, drawing inspiration from both folk and rock traditions. |Example: The band's folk-rock sound blended acoustic guitars with electric instrumentation. 这支乐队的民谣摇滚风格融合了原声吉他与电声乐器。|近义词：acoustic rock, folk-influenced rock|反义词：heavy metal, electronic music|名词
