overnationalizations:/ˌoʊvərˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 超国家化；超越国家行为 | The act or process of extending beyond national boundaries or jurisdictions; the transfer of power or authority from national governments to supranational organizations.  | Example: The increasing trend towards overnationalizations in economic policy is a concern for some nations.  越来越多的经济政策走向超国家化，这令一些国家担忧。 |近义词: supranationalism, internationalization | 反义词: nationalism, isolationism | 名词
