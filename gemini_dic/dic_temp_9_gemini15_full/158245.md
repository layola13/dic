play-at:/pleɪ æt/ | v. |假装，扮演；尝试；从事(业余的)|To pretend to be or do something; to engage in something casually or amateurishly.|例句：He played at being a doctor. 他假装是医生。|例句：She plays at the piano, but she's not very good. 她弹钢琴，但她不太好。|近义词：pretend, affect, simulate|反义词：be serious about, master


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plays at being a superhero. = 他假装自己是超级英雄。
She plays at the piano every evening. = 她每天晚上弹钢琴。
