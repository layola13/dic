reactionary:/riˈækʃənəri/ | adj. | 反动的；保守的；复古的 |  Opposed to progress or reform; tending to revert to earlier, often outdated, conditions or practices.  It often implies a desire to restore a previous political or social order, often characterized by authoritarianism or resistance to change. |  His reactionary views on women's rights were shocking to many.  他的关于妇女权利的反动观点让许多人感到震惊。

The government's reactionary policies led to widespread protests. 政府的反动政策导致了大规模抗议。| 近义词：conservative, traditionalist, retrogressive,  anti-progressive | 反义词：progressive, liberal, reformist, radical |
