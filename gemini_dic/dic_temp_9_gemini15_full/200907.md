slipper:/ˈslɪpər/| n.|拖鞋，软底鞋|A slipper is a type of light, soft footwear, typically worn indoors.  They are often made of soft materials like fabric, leather, or fur, and are designed for comfort rather than support or protection.|例句：I put on my slippers before going to bed.  我睡前穿上了拖鞋。|例句：These slippers are so comfortable! 这些拖鞋太舒服了！|近义词：sandal, mule, house shoe|反义词：boot, shoe
