reassessment:/ˌriːəˈsɛsmənt/| n. |重新评估，再评估 |The act of assessing something again; a second evaluation.  It often implies a more thorough or critical examination than the initial assessment. |例句：The company undertook a reassessment of its marketing strategy after poor sales figures. 公司在销售业绩不佳后对营销策略进行了重新评估。


例句：A reassessment of the risks involved is necessary before we proceed. 在我们继续之前，有必要重新评估所涉及的风险。


近义词：reevaluation, reappraisal, review |反义词：initial assessment, first evaluation |词性：名词
