mib:/mɪb/|abbr.|兆比特，百万比特|A unit of information equal to one million bits.|例句：The internet speed is 100 mib.  互联网速度是100兆比特。|近义词：MB (megabit)|反义词:  无


USAGE->
This abbreviation is primarily used in technical contexts and doesn't have typical verb conjugations or grammatical variations as a word itself.  It's a unit of measurement.  The grammatical usage depends on the sentence it's within.

Example Sentences showing usage in different sentence structures:

Declarative:  The download speed was 50 mib. 下载速度是50兆比特。

Interrogative: Is the server transfer rate at least 100 mib? 服务器传输速率至少是100兆比特吗？

Comparative:  This connection is faster, providing 200 mib versus the old 50 mib. 这个连接更快，提供200兆比特，而旧的只有50兆比特。
