entomologist:/ˌɛntəˈmɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |昆虫学家|A scientist who studies insects.|例句：The entomologist spent years researching the life cycle of the monarch butterfly.  这位昆虫学家花了数年时间研究帝王蝶的生命周期。|近义词：insect specialist, entomologic researcher|反义词：无
