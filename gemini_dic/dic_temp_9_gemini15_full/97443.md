infuse:/ɪnˈfjuːz/ | vt. | 注入；灌输；冲泡；使充满 | To fill or permeate something with a particular quality or substance.  | Examples:  The tea infused a calming scent into the room. 茶叶在房间里散发出令人平静的香味。  He tried to infuse his speech with passion. 他试图给他的演讲注入激情。|近义词：inject, instill, imbue, steep | 反义词：extract, remove, deplete |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He infuses the tea with hot water. = 他用热水冲泡茶。
She infuses her work with creativity. = 她将创造力融入她的工作中。

一般过去时:
They infused the fabric with a vibrant dye. = 他们用鲜艳的染料染织物。
The aroma infused the entire house. = 香味弥漫了整个房子。

现在完成时:
The artist has infused his paintings with deep meaning. = 艺术家在他的绘画中注入了深刻的含义。
She has infused her life with purpose. = 她的人生充满了目标。

过去完成时:
He had infused the cake with rum before baking it. = 他在烘焙蛋糕之前已经往里面注入了朗姆酒。
They had infused the community with a sense of hope. = 他们已经给社区带来了希望。

将来时:
We will infuse the new product with innovative technology. = 我们将用创新技术来改进新产品。
She will infuse her presentation with humor. = 她将用幽默来使她的演示更生动。

虚拟语气:
If I infused the soil with nutrients, the plants would grow faster. = 如果我给土壤施加营养，植物就会长得更快。
I wish I could infuse more joy into my daily life. = 我希望我能给我的日常生活带来更多快乐。

被动语态:
The fabric was infused with a special dye. = 织物被一种特殊的染料染过。
The air was infused with the scent of flowers. = 空气中弥漫着花香。

疑问句:
How do you infuse your tea? = 你是怎么冲泡茶的？
Will you infuse the story with suspense? = 你会给故事增加悬念吗？

否定句:
He didn't infuse enough flavor into the sauce. = 他没有在酱汁中加入足够的调味料。
The music didn't infuse the scene with excitement. = 音乐并没有使场景更激动人心。


PHRASE->
infuse with = 注入...
infuse into = 注入...
infuse energy into = 注入能量
infuse hope into = 注入希望
infuse life into = 注入活力

词源:
From Middle English infusen, from Old French infuser, from Latin infundere "to pour in," from in- "in" + fundere "to pour."
