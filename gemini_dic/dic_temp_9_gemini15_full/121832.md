misreligion:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlɪdʒən/| n. |错误的宗教信仰；歪理邪说 |A belief or practice that is considered a distortion or perversion of true religion.  It often involves a misinterpretation or misapplication of religious teachings or practices. Misreligion can manifest in various forms, from minor doctrinal disagreements to extreme and harmful cults.  | Example:  The cult leader promoted a misreligion that encouraged self-harm and isolation from society.  = 邪教教主宣扬一种歪理邪说，鼓励自我伤害和与社会隔绝。|近义词：heresy, false religion, superstition, cult |反义词：true religion, orthodoxy
