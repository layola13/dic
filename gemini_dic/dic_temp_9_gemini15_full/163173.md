precompelled:/ˌpriːkəmˈpɛld/ | adj. | 预先强迫的；被迫的 |  Precompelled describes something that has been forced or compelled beforehand. It implies a lack of choice or free will in the action or decision.  | The witness felt precompelled to testify against his friend.目击者感到被迫要指证他的朋友。  | compelled, forced, obliged | voluntary, free, uncoerced | 形容词
