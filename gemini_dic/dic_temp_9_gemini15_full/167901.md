principality:/ˌprɪnsɪˈpælɪti/| n. |公国；首位；主要地位 | A principality is a territory ruled by a prince or princess.  It often implies a small, independent state. |Example: Monaco is a tiny but wealthy principality. 摩纳哥是一个袖珍但富有的公国。  The principality of Liechtenstein is known for its beautiful scenery. 列支敦士登公国以其美丽的风景而闻名。|近义词：principate, duchy, kingdom |反义词：empire, republic
