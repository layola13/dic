reimprisoned:/ˌriːɪmˈprɪzən/| v. |再次监禁；重新关押 |To imprison someone again after they have been released from prison.  |例句：He was reimprisoned for violating his parole. 他因为违反假释而再次被监禁。|例句：After a brief period of freedom, she was reimprisoned on new charges.  短暂的自由后，她因新的指控而再次入狱。|近义词：reincarcerate, reconfine |反义词：release, free
