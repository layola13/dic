maleficence:/məˈlɛfɪs(ə)ns/| n. |作恶；恶意；危害 |The deliberate infliction of harm or injury.  It refers to the act of doing something harmful, often with ill intent.  |例句：The witch was accused of maleficence and imprisoned.  女巫被指控作恶而被监禁。

例句：His maleficence towards his rivals was well-known. 他对竞争对手的恶意是众所周知的。|近义词：harm, mischief, wickedness, evil, malevolence |反义词：benevolence, beneficence, kindness
