proaction:/proʊˈækʃən/| n. |主动性；先发制人；预防措施|The act of anticipating and taking action to prevent problems or capitalize on opportunities before they arise.  It emphasizes taking control and initiative rather than simply reacting to events.  |例句：The company's proaction in addressing the potential crisis prevented a major setback. 公司在解决潜在危机方面的主动性避免了一场重大挫折。

例句：Proaction is key to success in a competitive market. 在竞争激烈的市场中，主动性是成功的关键。


近义词：initiative, preventative measures, preemptive action, foresight

反义词：reactivity, inaction, passivity

词性:名词
