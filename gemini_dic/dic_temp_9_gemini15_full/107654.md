languish:/ˈlæŋɡwɪʃ/| v.|衰弱；受苦；消沉|to become weak or feeble; to suffer neglect; to lose vigor or vitality|例句：The plants languished in the shade.  植物在阴凉处枯萎了。

例句：He languished in prison for many years. 他在监狱里受苦多年。

近义词：decline, fade, wither, droop|反义词：flourish, thrive, prosper

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flowers languish without water. = 花儿缺水就枯萎了。
He languishes in his current job. = 他对现在的工作感到厌倦和沮丧。


一般过去时:
The economy languished after the war. = 战后经济萧条。
She languished in despair after the loss. = 她在失去之后陷入绝望。


现在完成时:
The project has languished for years due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，这个项目已经搁置多年了。
He has languished in poverty since he lost his job. = 自从失业后，他就一直贫困潦倒。


过去完成时:
The business had languished for months before they finally closed. = 在他们最终倒闭之前，这家企业已经萧条了几个月。
The patient had languished in pain before receiving treatment. = 病人在接受治疗之前一直饱受痛苦。


将来时:
Without investment, the company will languish. = 没有投资，公司将会衰败。
If he continues this way, he will languish in obscurity. = 如果他继续这样下去，他将会默默无闻。


虚拟语气:
If only he hadn't languished so long, he might have recovered. = 如果他不那么长时间地消沉下去，他也许就康复了。
I wish I hadn't languished in self-doubt. = 我真希望我没有那么长时间地自我怀疑。


被动语态:
The proposal was languished in committee for months. = 这个提案在委员会搁置了几个月。
His talent was languished due to lack of opportunity. = 由于缺乏机会，他的才能被埋没了。


疑问句:
Why is the project languishing? = 为什么这个项目进展缓慢？
Are you languishing in your current role? = 你对现在的工作感到厌倦和沮丧吗？


否定句:
The business did not languish; it thrived. = 这家企业并没有衰败；它蓬勃发展。
He didn't languish in despair; he fought back. = 他并没有陷入绝望；他奋起反抗。
