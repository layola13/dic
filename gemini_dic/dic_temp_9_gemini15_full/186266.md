rhizogen:/ˈraɪzədʒən/| n. |生根剂，生根刺激素|A substance that stimulates root formation in plants.  Rhizogens are often used in horticulture and agriculture to promote root growth and improve plant establishment.|Example: The application of rhizogen significantly increased the rooting rate of the cuttings.|近义词：rooting hormone, root stimulant|反义词：无
