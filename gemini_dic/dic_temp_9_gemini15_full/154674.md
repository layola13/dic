personas:/pərˈsoʊnəz/ | n. |人物角色；人格；化身 |  Personas are fictional representations of users or customers that help designers understand their needs and behaviors.  人物角色是用户的虚构代表，帮助设计师了解他们的需求和行为。 |  近义词：characters, roles, archetypes | 反义词：reality, actuality


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The marketing team creates detailed personas for their campaigns. = 营销团队为他们的活动创建详细的人物角色。
These personas represent different customer segments. = 这些角色代表不同的客户细分市场。


一般过去时:
We developed several personas based on user research. = 我们根据用户研究开发了几个角色。
The designer used personas to guide the website redesign. = 设计师使用人物角色来指导网站的重新设计。


现在完成时:
They have already defined the key personas for the project. = 他们已经为该项目定义了关键的人物角色。
The company has successfully used personas in its product development. = 该公司已成功地将其产品开发中使用了人物角色。


过去完成时:
Before starting the project, we had already established our target personas. = 在项目开始之前，我们就已经确定了我们的目标人物角色。
The team had carefully considered the needs of each persona before making design decisions. = 在做出设计决策之前，团队仔细考虑了每个角色的需求。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If we had better personas, the product would be more user-friendly. = 如果我们有更好的角色，产品将更易于用户使用。
