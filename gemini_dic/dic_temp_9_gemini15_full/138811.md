numskulls:/ˈnʌmskʌlz/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜，蠢货 |A numskull is a stupid or foolish person. The term is informal and often used in a humorous or mildly insulting way.  It emphasizes a lack of intelligence or common sense. |例句：He's such a numskull; he can't even change a tire. 他真是个笨蛋，连换个轮胎都不会。

例句：Don't be such a numskull! Think before you act! 别那么傻！行动前先想想！


近义词：idiot, fool, moron, dunce, simpleton

反义词：genius, intellectual, bright, clever, intelligent

词性:名词
