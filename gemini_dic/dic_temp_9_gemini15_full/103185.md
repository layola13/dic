jesuitess:/ˈdʒɛzuɪtɛs/| n.|耶稣会女修女|A woman member of the Society of Jesus (Jesuits).  The term is less common than "Jesuit," which refers to both men and women.|例句：She dedicated her life to serving others as a Jesuitess in a remote village. 她作为一名在偏远村庄的耶稣会女修女，将自己的一生奉献给了服务他人。  There are few Jesuitesses compared to the number of Jesuits. 与耶稣会士相比，耶稣会女修女人数较少。|近义词：nun, sister (in religious context)|反义词：layperson
