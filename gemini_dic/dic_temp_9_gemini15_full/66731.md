exorcised:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzd/| vt.|驱除(邪灵)；消除(坏念头等)|To drive out (an evil spirit) or get rid of (something unwanted or unpleasant).|例句：The priest exorcised the demon from the possessed woman.  牧师将附在那个女人身上的恶魔驱逐了出去。


例句：She tried to exorcise the bad memories from her mind. 她试图从脑海中驱除那些不好的回忆。


近义词：expel, banish, eliminate, eradicate


反义词：summon, invite, harbor, cultivate


词性:动词
