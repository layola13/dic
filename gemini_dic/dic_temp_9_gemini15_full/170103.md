proto-historical:/ˌproʊtoʊhɪˈstɔːrɪkəl/|/ˌprəʊtəʊhɪˈstɒrɪkəl/| adj.|史前史的；属于史前史的 | Relating to the period of time before written records, but about which information can be obtained from archaeological evidence.  It bridges the gap between prehistory and history. | Example:  The proto-historical period in this region is characterized by the emergence of sophisticated pottery techniques.  这个地区的史前史时期以复杂的陶器技术出现为特征。 |近义词：prehistoric (but with a nuance of having some archaeological evidence)| 反义词：historical |词性:形容词
