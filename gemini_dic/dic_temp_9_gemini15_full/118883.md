metastatically:/ˌmɛtəstætɪkəli/ | adv. |转移性地，转移地 |  Adverbial form of "metastatic," describing the process or manner of metastasis (the spread of cancer cells from one part of the body to another). It indicates that something happens through or relates to the spread of cancer. | 例句：The cancer had spread metastatically to her lungs.  癌细胞已经转移到她的肺部。 The tumor grew metastatically, quickly reaching other organs. 肿瘤转移性生长，迅速扩散到其他器官。| 近义词：secondarily, diffusely | 反义词：locally, primarily | 副词
