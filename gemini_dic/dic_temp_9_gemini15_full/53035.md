dioecian: /daɪˈiːsiən/ | adj. |雌雄异株的；雌雄异体的 | Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.  Dioecious plants have separate male and female flowers on different plants.  This term is often used in botany and zoology to describe species with separate sexes. |


例句：

Holly is a dioecian plant; you need both a male and a female plant to get berries.  | 冬青是雌雄异株的植物；你需要雄性和雌性植物才能结出浆果。


Many species of fish are dioecian, exhibiting clear sexual dimorphism. | 许多鱼类物种是雌雄异株的，表现出明显的性二态性。


近义词：gonochoristic, unisexual


反义词：monoecious, hermaphroditic


词性：形容词
