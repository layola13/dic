lumberjacks:/ˈlʌmbərdʒæks/ | n. |伐木工人 |A lumberjack is a person who cuts down trees for a living.  They typically work in forests and use various tools and machinery to fell trees and prepare them for transport. |The lumberjacks worked tirelessly all day.  伐木工人整天辛勤工作。|  loggers, woodsmen |  office workers, clerks | n.
