fuzziest:/ˈfʌziɪst/| adj.|最模糊的，最不清晰的|Most unclear, vague, or indistinct.  It is the superlative form of "fuzzy".|例句：The photo was too fuzzy; the details were the fuzziest.  照片太模糊了；细节是最模糊的。The fuzziest memories are those from my early childhood.  最模糊的记忆是我的幼儿时期。|近义词：haziest, vaguest, most indistinct, most blurry|反义词：clearest, sharpest, most distinct
