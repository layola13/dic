chauvinist:/ˈʃoʊvənɪst/| n. adj.|沙文主义者；沙文主义的|A chauvinist is a person who believes in the superiority of their own country, group, or sex.  They often display aggressive patriotism or sexism.  |例句：He's a notorious chauvinist who believes women are inferior to men. 他是一个臭名昭著的大男子主义者，认为女性不如男性。

例句：Her chauvinistic views on national identity are outdated and harmful. 她关于民族认同的沙文主义观点已经过时且有害。


近义词：jingoist, bigot, sexist, nationalist

反义词：egalitarian, feminist, internationalist

词性: 名词，形容词
