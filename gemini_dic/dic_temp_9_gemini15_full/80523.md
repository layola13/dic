goodbye:/ɡʊdˈbaɪ/| int.|再见|A contraction of "God be with ye", an archaic farewell.|例句：Goodbye, I'll see you tomorrow. 再见，明天见。|近义词：farewell, adieu, so long|反义词:hello


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I say goodbye every day. = 我每天都说再见。
They say goodbye to their friends. = 他们向朋友们告别。


一般过去时:
I said goodbye to my family. = 我向我的家人道别了。
She said goodbye before leaving. = 她离开前说了再见。


现在完成时:
I have said goodbye to him many times. = 我已经和他道别过很多次了。
We have said goodbye to our old house. = 我们已经和我们的老房子告别了。


过去完成时:
I had already said goodbye before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经道别了。
They had said goodbye before the plane took off. = 飞机起飞前他们已经道别了。


将来时:
I will say goodbye tomorrow. = 我明天会说再见。
She will say goodbye before the trip. = 她旅行前会说再见。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had said goodbye. = 我真希望我说了再见。
If I had known it would be the last time, I would have said goodbye properly. = 如果我知道那是最后一次，我会好好地道别的。


被动语态:
Goodbye was said with a heavy heart. = 再见是带着沉重的心情说的。
(Note:  "Goodbye" itself isn't typically used in passive voice constructions.  The sentiment of saying goodbye can be.)


疑问句:
Have you said goodbye? = 你道别了吗？
Did you say goodbye to your mother? = 你和你妈妈道别了吗？


否定句:
I didn't say goodbye. = 我没有说再见。
She didn't say goodbye before leaving. = 她离开前没有说再见。


PHRASE->
say goodbye = 说再见
bid farewell = 正式告别
to say goodbye to someone = 向某人道别
a fond farewell = 深情的告别
to see someone off = 送别某人
