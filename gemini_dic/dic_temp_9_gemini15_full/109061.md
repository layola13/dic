legalizations:/ˌliːɡəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |合法化(行为)；合法化(的实例)|The process of making something legal; instances of something being made legal.  It refers to the act of giving legal sanction or authority to something that was previously illegal or unregulated. |例句：The legalizations of marijuana in several states have sparked debate.  几个州的大麻合法化引发了辩论。|例句：Recent legalizations of same-sex marriage have been celebrated by many. 最近同性婚姻的合法化受到了许多人的庆祝。|近义词：authorization, sanction, validation|反义词：prohibition, banning, outlawry|名词
