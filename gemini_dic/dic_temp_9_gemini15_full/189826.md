salvoes:/ˈsælvəʊz/ | n. |齐射；一连串；突然爆发 | A salvo is a simultaneous discharge of a number of guns or a burst of gunfire.  It can also refer to a sudden outburst of something, such as words or actions. | 例句：The warships unleashed a salvo of shells at the enemy.军舰向敌人发射了一轮炮弹。

The politician's speech was met with a salvo of boos and jeers. 这位政治家的演讲遭到了一阵嘘声和嘲笑。


近义词： volley, burst, barrage | 反义词：silence, calm
