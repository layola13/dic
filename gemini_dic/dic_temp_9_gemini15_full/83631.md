haemodromometer:/ˌhiːməʊˈdrɒməmɪtə(r)/| n. |血流速度计|A haemodromometer is an instrument used to measure the velocity of blood flow.  It typically involves techniques like Doppler ultrasound to assess the speed at which blood moves through vessels. |The doctor used a haemodromometer to assess the blood flow in the patient's leg after the surgery.|近义词：flowmeter (更广义)|反义词：无
