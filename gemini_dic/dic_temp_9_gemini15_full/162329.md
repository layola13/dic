preached:/priːtʃt/| v.|布道；宣讲；鼓吹|To deliver a sermon or religious address; to advocate or promote (a belief, cause, etc.) forcefully.|例句：The minister preached a sermon on forgiveness. 牧师作了一篇关于宽恕的布道。

例句：He preached the importance of hard work. 他宣扬努力工作的重要性。|近义词：sermonize, advocate, promote|反义词：suppress, silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preaches every Sunday. 他每周日都布道。
She preaches tolerance and understanding. 她宣扬容忍和理解。


一般过去时:
The priest preached a powerful sermon.  牧师作了一篇很有力的布道。
They preached against violence and hatred. 他们谴责暴力和仇恨。


现在完成时:
He has preached to thousands of people. 他已经向成千上万人布道。
She has preached this message for many years.  她已经宣扬这个信息很多年了。


过去完成时:
He had preached the gospel for over twenty years before retiring. 他退休前已经传福音二十多年了。
They had preached the same message repeatedly.  他们已经反复宣讲同样的信息。


将来时:
He will preach on the topic of faith next Sunday. 他下周日将以信心为题布道。
She will preach a sermon about love and compassion. 她将宣讲一篇关于爱与同情心的布道。


虚拟语气:
If he preached with more passion, more people would listen. 如果他布道更有激情，更多人会倾听。
I wish he preached less about politics and more about faith. 我希望他少谈论政治，多谈论信仰。


被动语态:
The sermon was preached to a large congregation. 布道传给了大量的会众。
The importance of education was preached to the students.  向学生们宣讲了教育的重要性。


疑问句:
Did he preach about forgiveness? 他有讲到宽恕吗？
What did the preacher preach about today?  今天传道人讲了什么？


否定句:
He didn't preach hate; he preached love. 他没有宣扬仇恨；他宣扬的是爱。
They didn't preach violence; instead, they advocated for peace. 他们没有宣扬暴力；相反，他们倡导和平。


PHRASE->
preach to the choir:对知情者讲道理 (对那些已经相信的人再次强调)
preach a sermon: 作布道
preach the gospel: 传福音
preach peace: 宣扬和平
preach tolerance: 宣扬宽容
