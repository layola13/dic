demonstrable:/ˈdɛmənstrəbl/| adj.| 可证明的；明显的；显而易见的 | capable of being demonstrated or proved; evident; obvious.  |The company's success is demonstrable in its rising profits. 公司的成功在其不断增长的利润中可见一斑。|  evident, clear, obvious, provable |  unprovable, questionable, dubious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His talent is demonstrable. = 他的才能是显而易见的。
The benefits of exercise are demonstrable. = 运动的好处是显而易见的。

一般过去时:
The scientist demonstrably proved his theory. = 科学家无可辩驳地证明了他的理论。
The evidence demonstrably supported the prosecution's case. = 证据明显地支持了检方的说法。


现在完成时:
They have demonstrably improved their performance. = 他们已经明显提高了他们的表现。
The results have demonstrably shown the effectiveness of the treatment. = 结果已经明确地显示了治疗的有效性。


过去完成时:
It had demonstrably been a mistake to trust him. = 事实证明，信任他是一个错误。
The damage had demonstrably been caused by the storm. = 损害显然是由风暴造成的。


将来时:
The new technology will demonstrably increase efficiency. = 新技术将明显提高效率。
The upcoming study will demonstrably show the impact of climate change. = 即将来临的研究将明显地显示气候变化的影响。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were demonstrably clear, the case would be closed. = 如果证据确凿，案件就会结案。
If the results were demonstrably better, we would continue the project. = 如果结果明显更好，我们将继续该项目。


被动语态:
The theory was demonstrably proven wrong. = 该理论已被证明是错误的。
The benefits of the program are demonstrably shown through statistics. = 该项目的益处通过统计数据得到了明显的体现。


疑问句:
Is the claim demonstrably true? = 这一说法是否可以证明是正确的？
Is the connection between the two demonstrably clear? = 两者之间的联系是否显而易见？


否定句:
The claims were not demonstrably true. = 这些说法并非可以证明是正确的。
His guilt was not demonstrably proven. = 他的罪行并未得到明确的证明。


PHRASE->
demonstrably true = 可证明的真理
demonstrably false = 可证明的谬误
demonstrably superior = 明显优越
demonstrably effective = 明显有效
demonstrably better = 明显更好
