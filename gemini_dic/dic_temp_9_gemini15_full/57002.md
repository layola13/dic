double-header: /ˈdʌbəlˌhedər/ | n. |双头赛；双重赛程| A double-header is two games played consecutively, usually on the same day, by the same team or competing teams.  It's often used in baseball, but can apply to other sports as well.| Example:  The Yankees played a double-header against the Red Sox, winning both games.  纽约洋基队与波士顿红袜队进行了一场双头赛，赢得了两场比赛。


Example:  We're going to a double-header at the stadium this weekend.  这个周末我们要去体育场看一场双头赛。|近义词:twin bill, two-game series|反义词:single game|词性:名词
