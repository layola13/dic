mitt:/mɪt/| n. |手套（尤指棒球手套）|A mitt is a type of glove, especially a baseball glove. It's typically made of leather or a similar material and is used to catch balls.|He caught the fly ball with his mitt. 他用他的手套接住了飞球。|glove, gauntlet| bare hand
