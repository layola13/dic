drag-around:/dræɡ əˈraʊnd/| v.|拖来拖去，四处拖拽，使疲惫不堪|To pull or carry something or someone around in a careless or rough way, often implying exhaustion or annoyance.|例句：He dragged his suitcase around the airport all day. 他一整天在机场拖着他的行李箱到处走。  例句：The constant travel dragged her around until she was completely exhausted.  不断的旅行把她拖来拖去，直到她筋疲力尽。|近义词：haul, lug, tow,  shuffle|反义词：carry carefully,  support gently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They drag the furniture around the room. = 他们把家具在房间里拖来拖去。
He drags his feet around all day. = 他整天磨磨蹭蹭的。

一般过去时:
She dragged the heavy boxes around the house. = 她把沉重的箱子拖来拖去。
The children dragged their toys around the playground. = 孩子们在操场上拖着他们的玩具到处跑。

现在完成时:
They have dragged the equipment around for hours. = 他们已经把设备拖来拖去好几个小时了。
I have dragged myself around all day and I’m tired. = 我整天都拖着疲惫的身躯到处走，我很累了。

过去完成时:
He had dragged the supplies around before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他已经把物资拖来拖去了。
They had dragged the injured man around before finding help. = 他们在找到帮助之前把受伤的人拖来拖去。

将来时:
We will drag the boat around to the other side of the lake. = 我们将会把船拖到湖的另一边。
She will drag her feet around, avoiding her responsibilities. = 她会拖拖拉拉，逃避她的责任。


虚拟语气:
If I had more energy, I wouldn’t drag myself around so much. = 如果我有更多精力，我就不会这么拖着自己到处走了。
If he hadn’t dragged those boxes around, he wouldn’t have hurt his back. = 如果他没有把那些箱子拖来拖去，他就不会伤到背了。


被动语态:
The old piano was dragged around by several movers. = 那架旧钢琴被几个搬运工拖来拖去。
The project was dragged around for months before completion. = 这个项目在完成之前被拖延了好几个月。


疑问句:
Why are they dragging that heavy object around? = 他们为什么要拖着那个沉重的物体到处走？
Did they drag the car around the block? = 他们把车拖着绕街区跑了吗？


否定句:
They didn't drag the boxes around carelessly. = 他们没有粗心地把箱子拖来拖去。
I won't drag this issue around any longer. = 我不会再拖延这个问题了。


PHRASE->
drag oneself around =  拖着疲惫的身躯到处走
drag one's feet = 拖延；磨蹭
drag someone down =  使某人沮丧；拖累某人
drag something out = 拖延；延长
drag on =  持续过长；拖延
