overprecision:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈsɪʒən/| n. |过度精确; 过分精确|The act of being excessively precise or accurate, often to the point of being impractical or misleading.  It implies an unnecessary level of detail or accuracy that doesn't reflect the reality or the need of the situation.  |例句：His overprecision in measuring the ingredients led to a minor disaster in the kitchen. 他的配料测量过于精确，导致厨房里发生了一点小事故。

例句：The report suffered from overprecision; the minor details obscured the main points. 这份报告过于精确；次要细节掩盖了要点。


近义词：excessive accuracy, meticulousness (when negative), pedantry


反义词：approximation, vagueness, imprecision


词性:noun
