/ɪkˈstræktər/ | n. |萃取器，提取器 |A device or process used to extract something, typically a substance from a mixture or a component from a system.  |Example: The coffee maker uses a filter as an extractor to separate the grounds from the brewed coffee. 咖啡机使用过滤器作为萃取器将咖啡渣与煮好的咖啡分开。|近义词：separator, remover, isolator |反义词：inserter, adder
