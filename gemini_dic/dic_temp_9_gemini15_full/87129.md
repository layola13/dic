herbarian:/ˌɜːrˈbeəriən/| n. |植物标本室管理员；植物学家|A person who studies, collects, or preserves dried plants.  Specifically, a curator or keeper of a herbarium. |例句：The herbarian carefully cataloged the newly acquired specimens. 植物标本室管理员仔细地为新获得的标本编目。

例句：The experienced herbarian identified the rare orchid species.  经验丰富的植物学家鉴定了这种稀有的兰花品种。


近义词：botanist, plant taxonomist


反义词：无


词性:名词
