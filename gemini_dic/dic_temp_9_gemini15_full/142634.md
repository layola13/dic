ostler:/ˈɒstlər/| n. |马夫，马厩管理员| A person who attends to horses at an inn or stable.  An ostler was responsible for the care of horses, including feeding, grooming, and stabling them.  The role was common in the past, when horse-drawn transportation was prevalent.|The ostler led the horse to the stable and gave it a feed of oats. = 马夫把马带到马厩，并喂它燕麦。


The ostler carefully groomed the weary traveler's horse. = 马夫仔细地为疲惫的旅行者的马梳理毛发。| stablehand, groom | (近义词无明显反义词)| 名词
