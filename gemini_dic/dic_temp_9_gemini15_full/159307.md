pointillism:/ˌpɔɪnˈtɪlɪzəm/| n. |点彩画法 |A technique of painting in which small, distinct dots of color are applied in patterns to form an image.  The colors are blended by the viewer's eye. |The painting is a prime example of pointillism, with its thousands of tiny dots of color. 这幅画是点彩画法的典型代表，它由数千个细小的彩色点组成。|Impressionism, Divisionism|Abstract art|
