mundation:/mʌnˈdeɪʃən/ | n. |净化；清洗；涤净 | The act of cleansing or purifying, especially ritually or spiritually.  It often refers to a thorough cleaning or purification process. | 例句：The mundation of the temple was a sacred ceremony.  寺庙的净化是一场神圣的仪式。

The ritual mundation involved chanting and incense.  净化仪式包括吟唱和焚香。


近义词：purification, cleansing, cleaning, ablution | 反义词：pollution, contamination, defilement


词源：源自拉丁语 *mundare (to clean, to purify),  related to mundus (clean, pure, world).  The suffix "-ation" denotes the process or action.
