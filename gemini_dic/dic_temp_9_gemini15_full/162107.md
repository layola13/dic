pre-curricular:/ˌpriːkəˈrɪkjələr/| adj. |课程前的，课程预备的 | Relating to activities or learning that take place before a formal curriculum begins.  It often refers to preparatory or introductory activities designed to build foundational knowledge or skills. |Example: The pre-curricular activities included a summer reading list and introductory workshops.  课程预备活动包括暑期阅读清单和入门研讨会。|近义词：preparatory, preliminary, introductory |反义词：post-curricular
