repenter:/rɪˈpɛntər/| n. |忏悔者，悔过的人 |A person who repents; one who feels regret for past wrongdoing and seeks forgiveness.  |例句：The repenter sought solace in prayer and acts of charity. 忏悔者在祈祷和慈善行为中寻求慰藉。|近义词：penitent, convert|反义词：hardened sinner, unrepentant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repents his actions daily. = 他每天都为自己的行为忏悔。
She repents her past mistakes. = 她为过去的错误而忏悔。

一般过去时:
He repented his sins after the sermon. = 布道后他忏悔了他的罪过。
She repented her hasty decision. = 她为她草率的决定而后悔。

现在完成时:
He has repented his wrongdoing and asked for forgiveness. = 他已经为他的错误行为忏悔并请求原谅。
She has repented her harsh words and apologized. = 她已经为她尖刻的话语忏悔并道歉了。


过去完成时:
He had repented his actions before he confessed. = 他在坦白之前就已经为他的行为忏悔了。
She had repented her lies before telling the truth. = 她早在说出真相之前就已经为她的谎言忏悔了。


将来时:
He will repent his actions eventually. = 他最终会为自己的行为忏悔。
She will repent her decision soon enough. = 她很快就会后悔自己的决定。


虚拟语气:
If he repented his sins, he would feel better. = 如果他忏悔他的罪过，他会感觉更好。
If she had repented earlier, things would have been different. = 如果她早点忏悔，情况就会不一样了。


被动语态:
His sins were repented for by the priest. = 他的罪过由牧师代为忏悔。
Her mistakes were repented for in a public apology. = 她的错误通过公开道歉得到了忏悔。


疑问句:
Does he repent his actions? = 他为自己的行为忏悔吗？
Will she repent her decision? = 她会后悔她的决定吗？


否定句:
He does not repent his actions. = 他不为自己的行为忏悔。
She did not repent her mistakes. = 她没有为自己的错误忏悔。


PHRASE->
repent one's sins = 悔过自己的罪孽

true repentance = 真诚的悔过
deep repentance = 深切的悔恨
