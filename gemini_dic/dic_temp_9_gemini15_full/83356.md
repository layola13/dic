gyre:/dʒaɪər/| n.|回旋，旋转；环流|A gyre is a rotating system, especially a large system of rotating ocean currents.|例句：The ocean gyre is a large system of circulating currents.  海洋环流是一个大型的环流系统。|例句：The birds flew in a graceful gyre above the valley.  鸟儿在山谷上方优雅地盘旋飞行。|近义词：whirlpool, vortex, eddy, swirl|反义词：straight line
