reinducting:/ˌriːɪnˈdʌktɪŋ/ | v. |重新引入；重新入伍；重新介绍 | The act of inducting again; bringing someone back into a position or group.  It often refers to military service or a formal organization. |例句：The company is reinducting several former employees into new management roles. 公司正在将几名前雇员重新引入新的管理职位。

The retired general was reinducting into active service during the crisis.  退休将军在危机期间被重新召回服役。

近义词：reintroducing, reinstating, readmitting | 反义词：dismissing, discharging, expelling |
词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reinducting him into the team. = 他们正在把他重新引入团队。
The school is reinducting alumni to the honor society. = 学校正在将校友重新纳入荣誉社团。


一般过去时:
They reinducted him into the army. = 他们把他重新招入军队。

过去完成时:

The organization had reinducted her before the annual meeting. =  组织在年度会议之前已经把她重新接纳了。
