resected:/rɪˈsɛktɪd/| v. |切除；摘除；解剖| Removed surgically.  The term implies a complete or nearly complete removal of a tissue or organ. |例句：The surgeon resected the tumor successfully. 外科医生成功切除了肿瘤。
The doctor had to resect a portion of his lung. 医生不得不切除他的一部分肺部。|近义词：excise, remove, extirpate |反义词：implant, insert, graft
