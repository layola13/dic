relacing:/rɪˈleɪsɪŋ/| v. |重新系上；重新连接；更换；替换 |The act of fastening or connecting something again, or replacing something.  It often refers to laces, but can be used more broadly. |例句：She spent the afternoon relacing her hiking boots. 她花了整个下午重新系她的登山鞋。|例句：The mechanic relaced the broken engine part. 机械师更换了损坏的发动机部件。|近义词：refastening, reattaching, replacing, renewing |反义词：unlacing, disconnecting, removing

He relaces his shoes every morning. = 他每天早上都重新系鞋带。
She relaces the curtain cord regularly. = 她定期更换窗帘绳。
