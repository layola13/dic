overdiscreet:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪskriːt/| adj. |过分谨慎的，过于小心谨慎的|Excessively discreet; showing excessive caution or secrecy.  It implies a level of caution that is not only unnecessary but possibly detrimental.|He was overdiscreet in his dealings with the press, avoiding any contact whatsoever. 他在与媒体打交道时过于谨慎，避免任何接触。  She was so overdiscreet that she never revealed her true feelings. 她过于谨慎，从未透露她的真实感受。|近义词：overcautious, excessively careful, hypervigilant|反义词：indiscreet, careless, reckless|
