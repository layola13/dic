semilunar:/ˈsɛmɪˌluːnər/| adj.|半月形的，新月形的|Shaped like a half-moon.  The term derives from the Latin words "semi" (half) and "luna" (moon).|Example: The semilunar valves in the heart control blood flow. 心脏里的半月瓣控制着血流。The crescent moon has a semilunar shape. 新月是半月形的。|近义词：crescent-shaped, lunate|反义词：circular, full
