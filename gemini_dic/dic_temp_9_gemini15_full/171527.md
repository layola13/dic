puking:/ˈpjuːkɪŋ/| v.|呕吐；恶心；|The act of vomiting.  It can also refer to the feeling of nausea leading to vomiting.|例句：He spent the night puking his guts out. 他整晚都在呕吐。

例句：The smell made me feel like puking.  这气味让我感觉想吐。

近义词：vomiting, throwing up, retching

反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is puking in the bathroom. = 他正在浴室里呕吐。
She pukes every morning. = 她每天早上都呕吐。


一般过去时:
He puked all over the carpet. = 他把地毯都吐脏了。
She puked after eating the spoiled food. = 她吃了变质的食物后呕吐了。


现在完成时:
He has been puking all day. = 他一整天都在呕吐。
She has puked three times already. = 她已经吐了三次了。


过去完成时:
He had puked before the doctor arrived. = 医生到达之前他已经呕吐了。
She had puked several times before calling for help. = 她求救之前已经吐了好几次了。


将来时:
He will puke if he keeps drinking. = 如果他继续喝酒，他会吐的。
She thinks she will puke on the rollercoaster. = 她觉得在过山车上会吐。


虚拟语气:
If he ate less, he wouldn't puke. = 如果他少吃点，他就不会吐了。
I wish I hadn't eaten that, I feel like I'm going to puke. = 我真希望我没有吃那个，我觉得我要吐了。


被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
The food was so bad that many people were made to puke. = 食物太糟糕了，以至于很多人都被迫呕吐。


疑问句:
Is he puking again? = 他又在呕吐了吗？
Has she puked yet? = 她吐了吗？


否定句:
He didn't puke after the surgery. = 他手术后没有呕吐。
She doesn't usually puke. = 她通常不会呕吐。


PHRASE->
feeling like puking = 感觉想吐

constant puking = 持续呕吐
