intimidators: /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtərz/ | n. | 恐吓者，威胁者 | People or things that intimidate.  Those who use threats or violence to frighten or overpower others. |例句：The bullies were the main intimidators in the school.  学校里的主要恐吓者是那些欺凌者。

例句：The constant threat of violence from the gang made them intimidators in the neighborhood.  帮派持续不断的暴力威胁使他们成为街区的恐吓者。


近义词：bullies, threats, menaces, harassers

反义词：comforters, protectors, supporters


词性: 名词
