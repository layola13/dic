crinoidean:/kraɪˈnɔɪdiən/| n. |海百合类动物|A crinoidean is a marine animal belonging to the class Crinoidea, characterized by a cup-shaped body and feathery arms.  They are echinoderms, related to starfish and sea urchins. Many are sessile, attaching themselves to the seafloor, while others are free-swimming.|Example:  The fossil record shows a rich diversity of crinoidean species throughout geological time.|近义词：sea lily (informal)|反义词：无
