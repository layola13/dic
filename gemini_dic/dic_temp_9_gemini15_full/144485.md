outthrowing: /ˌaʊtˈθroʊɪŋ/ | v. | 投掷；超过；胜过 | The act of throwing something further or with more force than someone else; surpassing or excelling someone.  | 例句：He won the competition by outthrowing all his rivals. 他以超过所有对手的投掷距离赢得了比赛。  The new technology outthrows the old in terms of efficiency. 新技术在效率方面胜过旧技术。 | 近义词：surpassing, excelling, exceeding, outperforming | 反义词：underperforming, lagging behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outthrows his opponents in every game. = 他在每一场比赛中都投掷得比对手远。
The new model outthrows its predecessor in terms of range. = 新型号的射程超过了它的前代产品。


一般过去时:
She outthrew her best personal record by a significant margin. = 她比她最好的个人记录超出很多。
The team outthrew the opposing team in the final match. = 该队在决赛中投掷距离超过了对方球队。


现在完成时:
He has outthrown every other competitor this season. = 这个赛季他投掷距离超过了其他所有竞争对手。
The company has outthrown its rivals in terms of market share. = 在市场份额方面，该公司已经超过了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the tournament, she had outthrown every other participant. = 到锦标赛结束时，她已经超过了所有其他参赛者。
The athlete had outthrown his previous best before the final attempt. = 在最后一次尝试之前，这位运动员已经超过了他之前的最好成绩。


将来时:
They believe they will outthrow the current world record holder. = 他们相信他们将超过现任世界纪录保持者。
With this new technique, she expects to outthrow her competitors next year. = 使用这种新技术，她预计明年将超过她的竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outthrown his opponent. = 如果他训练更努力，他可能会超过他的对手。
I wish I could outthrow the current champion. = 我希望我能超过现任冠军。


被动语态:

The old technology has been outthrown by the latest advancements. = 旧技术已被最新的进步所超越。


疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't outthrow his rival in the final round. = 他在决赛中没有超过他的对手。
The team failed to outthrow the opposing team in the competition. = 该队未能在此次比赛中超过对方球队。
