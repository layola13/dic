gargarize:/ˈɡɑːrɡəl/| vt.|漱口，含漱|To rinse the mouth and throat with a liquid, typically a medicated solution, by moving it around in the mouth and throat and then spitting it out.|例句：The doctor told me to gargle with salt water to soothe my sore throat.医生告诉我用盐水漱口以缓解我的喉咙痛。|例句：She gargled with antiseptic mouthwash after brushing her teeth. 她刷牙后用消毒漱口水漱口。|近义词：rinse, swish|反义词：N/A|动词
