reconfigurable:/ˌriːkənˈfɪɡjərəbl/| adj.|可重构的，可重新配置的 | Capable of being reconfigured; able to be reorganized or restructured.  This adjective describes something that can be changed or adapted to a new purpose or configuration. It often refers to hardware or software systems that can be altered without needing to be physically rebuilt or replaced. |例句：The reconfigurable antenna system can adapt to different frequencies.  可重构的天线系统可以适应不同的频率。

The reconfigurable software allows for customized workflows.  可重构的软件允许自定义工作流程。

近义词：adaptable, adjustable, modifiable, customizable | 反义词：fixed, inflexible, immutable

词源:
The word "reconfigurable" is derived from the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back"), the verb "configure" (meaning "to arrange or set up"), and the suffix "-able" (meaning "capable of being").  Therefore, it literally means "capable of being configured again."
