self-development:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈvɛləpmənt/| n. |自我发展，自我提升 |The process of improving oneself through learning, training, and experience.  It often involves setting goals, identifying strengths and weaknesses, and taking action to achieve personal and professional growth.|例句：He is committed to self-development and regularly attends workshops.|近义词：personal growth, self-improvement, self-actualization|反义词：stagnation, decline|名词
