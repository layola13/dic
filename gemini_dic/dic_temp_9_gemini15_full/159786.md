polygastrica: /ˌpɒliˈɡæstrɪkə/ | adj. |多胃的；多室胃的 |  Relating to or characteristic of animals with more than one stomach compartment.  The term is primarily used in zoology to describe the digestive systems of certain animals, particularly ruminants.  | Example:  Ruminants are polygastric animals, possessing a complex four-chambered stomach.  反刍动物是多胃动物，拥有一个复杂的四室胃。|近义词: multi-chambered, ruminant (when referring to ruminants specifically) |反义词: monogastric
