incommensuratenesses:/ˌɪnkəˈmɛnʃərətnəsɪz/ | n. |不相称性；无法衡量性 |The incommensuratenesses between the two cultures were striking.  两者的文化差异非常显著。|近义词：disproportion, disparity, discrepancy |反义词：commensurability, congruity


英文解析:The plural form of "incommensurability," referring to the state or quality of being incommensurate;  lack of proportion; inability to be measured against a standard.  It highlights multiple instances or aspects of this lack of proportion or measurability.

例句:

The incommensuratenesses of their goals and resources led to the project's failure. 他们的目标和资源不相称导致了项目的失败。


The many incommensuratenesses between the two systems made integration difficult. 两个系统之间诸多不相称之处使得整合变得困难。
