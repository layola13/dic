paws: /pɔːz/ | n. | 爪子，脚爪 | The paws of a cat are soft and padded. 猫的爪子柔软而有肉垫。 | 近义词：claws, feet | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat paws at the door. = 猫用爪子挠门。
The dog paws the ground excitedly. = 狗兴奋地刨地。


一般过去时:
The bear pawed at the fish. = 熊用爪子拍打鱼。
The kitten pawed at the ball of yarn. = 小猫用爪子玩毛线球。


现在完成时:
The dog has pawed a hole in the garden. = 狗已经在花园里刨了个洞。
The cat has pawed at the curtains all morning. = 猫整个上午都在挠窗帘。


过去完成时:
The lion had pawed the ground before attacking. = 狮子在攻击之前已经刨了地。
The dog had pawed at the door for hours before someone let it in. = 狗在有人开门之前已经挠门好几个小时了。


将来时:
The puppy will paw at your hand when it wants attention. = 小狗想要关注时会用爪子挠你的手。
The bear will paw the ground before it hibernates. = 熊在冬眠前会刨地。


虚拟语气:
If the dog pawed at the door, I would let it in. = 如果狗挠门，我会让它进来。
I wish the cat wouldn't paw at the furniture. = 我希望猫不要挠家具。


被动语态:
The door was pawed at by the cat. = 门被猫挠了。
The ground was pawed by the dog. = 地被狗刨了。


疑问句:
Does the cat paw at the furniture? = 猫挠家具吗？
Did the dog paw at the door? = 狗挠门了吗？


否定句:
The cat didn't paw at the furniture. = 猫没有挠家具。
The dog doesn't paw at the ground anymore. = 狗不再刨地了。
