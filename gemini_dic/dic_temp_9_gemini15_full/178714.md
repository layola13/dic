recrystallizable: /ˌriːˈkrɪstəlˌaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可再结晶的 | Able to be recrystallized; capable of undergoing recrystallization.  This refers to a substance's ability to dissolve in a solvent, then reform into crystals upon cooling or evaporation. |  The solution yielded recrystallizable impurities.  这溶液产生了可再结晶的杂质。


The salt is recrystallizable from water.这种盐可以从水中再结晶。| 近义词：re-crystallizable | 反义词：amorphous (无定形的), non-crystallizable (不可再结晶的)


词源:

* **re-**:  prefix meaning "again" or "back"
* **crystal-**: from  Greek "krustallos" meaning "ice" or "rock crystal"
* **-ize**: suffix meaning "to cause to become"
* **-able**: suffix meaning "capable of being"

Therefore, "recrystallizable" means capable of being crystallized again.
