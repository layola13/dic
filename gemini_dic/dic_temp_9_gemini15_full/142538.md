ossuary:/ˈɒsjuəri/ | n. |骸骨存放处；骨灰盒；盛骨灰的容器 | A place where bones, especially human bones, are kept.  It can refer to a building, a chamber, or a container. |The ossuary contained the remains of countless soldiers.  骸骨存放处里存放着无数士兵的遗骸。 | charnel house, bone house |  | noun
