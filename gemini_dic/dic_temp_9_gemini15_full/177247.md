reassembled:/ˌriːəˈsɛmbəl/| v. |重新组装，重新聚集|To put something together again after it has been taken apart; to gather something together again after it has been scattered or dispersed. |例句：The mechanic reassembled the engine after repairing it. 机械师修理后重新组装了发动机。

The scattered pieces of the broken vase were carefully reassembled. 打碎的花瓶碎片被仔细地重新拼装起来。


近义词：reconstruct, rebuild, reunite, regroup|反义词：disassemble, dismantle, scatter, disperse|动词
