hanyang: /ˈhɑːnjæŋ/ | n. |汉阳| Hanyang refers to a historical name for several places in Korea, most prominently the former name for Seoul's western district.  It also refers to  Hanyang University. | Example:  The Gyeongbokgung Palace is located in what was once called Hanyang. 汉阳宫位于曾经被称为汉阳的地方。| 近义词: Seoul (in certain contexts)| 反义词: N/A
