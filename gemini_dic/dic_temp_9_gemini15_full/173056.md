quarterbacked:/ˈkwɔːrtərˌbækt/| v.|担任四分卫；由四分卫指挥（进攻）|to act as a quarterback; to direct (an offensive play) as a quarterback|例句：He expertly quarterbacked the team to victory. 他熟练地指挥球队取得了胜利。  The coach quarterbacked the final offensive drive. 教练指挥了最后的进攻推进。|近义词：led, directed, managed|反义词：followed, obeyed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quarterbacks the team every Sunday. = 他每周日都担任球队的四分卫。
The coach quarterbacks the offensive plays. = 教练指挥进攻战术。

一般过去时:
He quarterbacked the winning touchdown drive. = 他指挥了赢得达阵的进攻推进。
The veteran quarterback expertly quarterbacked the team to victory. = 这位经验丰富的四分卫熟练地指挥球队取得了胜利。

现在完成时:
He has quarterbacked this team for five seasons. = 他已经为这支球队担任四分卫五个赛季了。
The coach has quarterbacked many successful drives this year. = 今年教练指挥了许多成功的进攻推进。

过去完成时:
He had quarterbacked the team to several victories before the injury. = 在受伤之前，他曾带领球队取得过多次胜利。
The team had rarely been quarterbacked so effectively before that game. = 在那场比赛之前，球队很少被如此有效地指挥过。

将来时:
He will quarterback the team next season. = 下个赛季他将担任球队的四分卫。
The new coach will quarterback the team's offensive strategy. = 新教练将指挥球队的进攻策略。

虚拟语气:
If he had quarterbacked better, they might have won. = 如果他指挥得更好，他们也许就赢了。
I wish he had quarterbacked the team more aggressively. = 我希望他更积极地指挥球队。

被动语态:
The team was expertly quarterbacked to victory. = 球队被熟练地指挥着取得了胜利。
The final drive was poorly quarterbacked. = 最后的进攻推进指挥得很糟糕。

疑问句:

否定句:
