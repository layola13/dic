eulogised:/ˈjuːlədʒaɪzd/ | v. | 赞扬；颂扬；悼词 | To praise highly, especially in a speech or writing; to deliver a eulogy for. | The speaker eulogised the deceased's contributions to the community.  演讲者高度赞扬了死者对社区的贡献。|  praised, extolled, celebrated | criticised, condemned, denigrated | 动词
