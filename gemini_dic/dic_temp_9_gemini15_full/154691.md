personifying: /pərˈsɒnɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. |拟人化；赋予人格| Giving human qualities or characteristics to something that is not human, such as an animal, object, or idea.  |例句：The poet personified the wind as a mischievous sprite.  诗人将风拟人化成一个淘气的精灵。

例句：The author personifies nature, giving the mountains voices and the rivers personalities. 作者将自然拟人化，赋予山峦声音，河流个性。


近义词：anthropomorphizing, embodying, representing, personifying

反义词：dehumanizing, depersonalizing


词性: 动词
