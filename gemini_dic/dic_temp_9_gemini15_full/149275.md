ozonation:/ˌoʊzəˈneɪʃən/| n.|臭氧处理，臭氧消毒|The process of treating something with ozone.  Ozone is a highly reactive gas that can be used to disinfect water, air, and surfaces. It works by oxidizing contaminants, breaking them down into less harmful substances.|例句：The water treatment plant uses ozonation to kill harmful bacteria.  这个水处理厂使用臭氧处理来杀死有害细菌。|例句：Ozonation is an effective method for removing odors from the air. 臭氧处理是去除空气异味的有效方法。|近义词：disinfection, sterilization|反义词：contamination
