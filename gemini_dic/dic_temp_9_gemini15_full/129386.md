nome:/ˈnoʊmi/ | n. |名字；名称|The word "nome" refers to a name or designation, often used in a specific context like a geographic location or a specific type of thing.  It can also imply a proper noun.| 例句：The nome of this ancient city is lost to history.  这个古代城市的名称已湮没于历史之中。|近义词：name, appellation, designation |反义词：anonymity, unnamable
