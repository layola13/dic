WORD ORIGIN->

* **quasi-**:  From Latin *quasi*, meaning "as if," "almost," or "somewhat."
* **mediaeval**: From Latin *medius* ("middle") + *aevum* ("age").  This refers to the Middle Ages.


Therefore, *quasi-mediaeval* literally means "as if of the middle ages," or "resembling the middle ages" but not entirely or perfectly.
