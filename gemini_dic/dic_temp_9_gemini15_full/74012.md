forum:/ˈfɔːrəm/| n. |论坛；讨论会；公开场合|A forum is a place where people can discuss issues openly.  A forum can be a physical location or an online platform.  It provides a space for exchanging ideas and opinions. |例句：The online forum is a great place to get help with coding problems.  这个在线论坛是寻求编码问题帮助的好地方。

例句：The town hall meeting served as a forum for discussing local issues.  市政厅会议是讨论当地问题的论坛。


近义词：discussion, meeting, assembly, gathering, debate

反义词：silence, secrecy, solitude
