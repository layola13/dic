nonconventional:/ˌnɒnkənˈvɛnʃənəl/| adj. |非传统的，另类的|Not conforming to accepted standards; unorthodox.  It implies a departure from established norms, often suggesting innovation or unconventionality. |例句：He employed nonconventional methods in his artwork, resulting in stunning, original pieces. 他在他的艺术作品中采用了非常规的方法，创作出了令人惊叹的原创作品。

例句：The company's nonconventional marketing strategy proved surprisingly effective. 这家公司非常规的营销策略证明出奇有效。


近义词：unconventional, unorthodox, atypical, nonstandard, radical, avant-garde

反义词：conventional, traditional, orthodox, standard, customary
