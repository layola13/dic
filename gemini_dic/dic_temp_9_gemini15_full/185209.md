retracked:/riːˈtrækt/ | vt. |重新追踪；重新检查；收回；撤回 | To trace or follow again; to re-examine; to withdraw a statement or claim.  |例句：The police retraced the suspect's steps.  警方重新追踪了嫌疑人的足迹。

The engineers retracked the railway line after the landslide. 工程师们在山体滑坡后重新勘测了铁路线路。

近义词：re-examine, review, reconsider, withdraw, retract

反义词：affirm, confirm, assert
