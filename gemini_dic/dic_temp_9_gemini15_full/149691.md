pakistani:/ˌpækɪˈstɑːni/ | adj., n. | 巴基斯坦的；巴基斯坦人 |

英文解析:Relating to or characteristic of Pakistan or its people.  As a noun, it refers to a person who is a citizen of Pakistan.

例句:
He is a Pakistani. = 他是巴基斯坦人。

She's proud of her Pakistani heritage. = 她为自己的巴基斯坦血统感到自豪。


近义词:Pakistani-American (if referring to a Pakistani living in America), etc. (depending on context)


反义词:None (it's an ethnicity/nationality, not something that has a direct opposite)


词性:adjective, noun
