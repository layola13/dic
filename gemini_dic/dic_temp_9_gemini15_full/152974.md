pedimental:/pɛdɪˈmɛntəl/| adj.|三角楣的；山墙形的；| Relating to or resembling a pediment.  A pediment is the triangular gable end of a building.  The term often describes architectural features that mimic the shape of a pediment.|The building's pedimental roof was a striking feature.  这栋建筑的山墙式屋顶是一个引人注目的特色。| triangular, gable | flat, rectangular |形容词
