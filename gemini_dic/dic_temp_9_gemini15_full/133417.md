nonfamilies:/ˈnɒnˌfæməliz/ | n. |非家庭，非家庭单位 |People or groups of people who do not constitute a family unit, such as single individuals, roommates, or unrelated individuals living together.  This term is often used in sociological and demographic contexts. | Example: The census data included both families and nonfamilies.  人口普查数据包括家庭和非家庭单位。  The increasing number of nonfamilies reflects changing social structures.  非家庭数量的增加反映了社会结构的变化。 |近义词：single-person households, unrelated individuals, non-family households |反义词：families, family units
