concomitancy: /ˌkɒnkɒˈmɪtənsi/ | /ˌkɑːnkɑːˈmɪtənsi/ | n. |同时发生；伴随；共存 | The simultaneous occurrence of two or more things;  accompaniment.  It often implies a causal or close relationship, though not necessarily a direct cause-and-effect relationship. | The study showed a strong concomitancy between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究表明吸烟和肺癌之间有很强的关联性。  There was a concomitancy of economic growth and social unrest. 经济增长和社会动荡同时发生。 | simultaneity, coincidence, correlation | independence, disjunction | 名词
