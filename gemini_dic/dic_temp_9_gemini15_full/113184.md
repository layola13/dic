lurer:/ˈlʊər/| v.| 诱惑；引诱；吸引 | To tempt or entice someone, usually with the promise of something desirable.  |例句：The siren song lured sailors to their doom.  引诱水手的歌声导致了他们的灭亡。

例句：The promise of riches lured many to the gold rush.  致富的承诺吸引了许多人参加淘金热。


近义词：tempt, entice, attract, decoy, allure

反义词：repel, deter, discourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lures birds with seeds. = 他用种子引诱鸟类。
She lures customers with discounts. = 她用折扣吸引顾客。


一般过去时:
The sweet smell lured me into the bakery. = 甜甜的味道把我吸引进了面包店。
The advertisement lured many people to buy the product. = 广告吸引许多人购买该产品。


现在完成时:
They have lured many investors into their scheme. = 他们已经诱使许多投资者进入他们的计划。
The company has lured top talent from its competitors. = 公司已经从竞争对手那里吸引了顶尖人才。


过去完成时:
He had lured the thief into a trap. = 他已经把小偷诱入陷阱。
The con artist had lured her into giving him all her money. = 骗子已经诱使她交出所有钱财。


将来时:
They will lure tourists with beautiful scenery. = 他们将用美丽的风景吸引游客。
The new marketing campaign will lure more customers to the store. = 新的营销活动将吸引更多顾客到商店。


虚拟语气:
If I had lured him with a better offer, he might have accepted. = 如果我用更好的条件引诱他，他可能会接受。
I wish I had lured him to my side. = 我希望我把他吸引到我这边来。


被动语态:
Many people were lured by the promise of easy money. = 许多人被轻松赚钱的承诺所吸引。
The unsuspecting victims were lured into a dangerous situation. = 毫无戒心的受害者被诱入危险境地。


疑问句:
What lured you to this city? = 什么吸引你来到这座城市？
What can we do to lure more talent to our company? = 我们能做些什么来吸引更多人才到我们公司？


否定句:
Nothing could lure him away from his work. = 没有什么能把他从工作中吸引走。
The low salary didn't lure many applicants. = 低薪没有吸引很多申请者。


PHRASE->
lure away = 诱走
lure into = 诱使...进入
lure out = 诱出
lure from = 从…引诱
lure on = 继续引诱
