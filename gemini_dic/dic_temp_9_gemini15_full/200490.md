slanders:/ˈslændərz/| n. v.|诽谤；中伤；污蔑|To utter false statements that damage someone's reputation.|例句：He was slandered by his rivals. 他被他的竞争对手诽谤了。|例句：She slandered him in the newspaper. 她在报纸上诽谤他。|近义词：defame, libel, calumniate|反义词：praise, commend, extol|动词
