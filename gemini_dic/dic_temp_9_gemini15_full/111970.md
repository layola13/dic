lomentaceous:/ˌloʊmɛnˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. |莢果狀的；有莢果的 | Having or producing loments.  Loments are fruits that break into one-seeded segments.  The term is primarily used in botany to describe the fruit of certain plants. | Example:  The legume displayed lomentaceous characteristics, readily splitting into several one-seeded sections.  莢果植物展示了莢果狀的特徵，很容易分成幾個單粒種子的部分。| 近义词：articulated (in the context of fruits), segmented | 反义词：indehiscent (for fruits that don't split open)
