doctor:/ˈdɒktər/ /ˈdɑːktər/| n. v.|医生；博士；篡改|A doctor is a person qualified to practice medicine.  A doctor can also refer to someone holding a doctoral degree.|例句：The doctor examined the patient carefully. 医生仔细检查了病人。|例句：She's a doctor of philosophy. 她是一位哲学博士。|近义词：physician, physician, MD,  professor|反义词：patient
