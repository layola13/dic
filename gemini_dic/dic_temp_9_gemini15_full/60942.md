elevators:/ˈɛləveɪtərz/| n.|电梯；升降机|A machine that carries people or goods from one floor to another in a building.|例句：The elevators are out of service. 电梯停运了。|例句：We took the elevator to the top floor. 我们乘电梯到了顶楼。|近义词：lifts (British English)|反义词：stairs, escalator
