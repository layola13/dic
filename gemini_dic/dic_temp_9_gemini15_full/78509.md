ghee:/ɡiː/| n.|酥油，澄清黄油|Clarified butter, a type of butter fat that is produced by slowly heating butter and removing the milk solids and water.  It has a higher smoke point than butter and a nutty flavor. |The recipe calls for ghee instead of regular butter.|近义词：clarified butter|反义词：无
