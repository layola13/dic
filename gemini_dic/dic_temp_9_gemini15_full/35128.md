cloudless:/ˈklaʊdləs/| adj. |无云的，晴朗的 |Free from clouds; clear.  |例句：The sky was cloudless and blue. 天空无云，一片湛蓝。|例句：We enjoyed a cloudless day at the beach. 我们在海滩上享受了晴朗的一天。|近义词：clear, sunny, fair |反义词：cloudy, overcast


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sky is cloudless today. = 今天天空晴朗。
The weather forecast predicts a cloudless afternoon. = 天气预报预测下午天气晴朗。


一般过去时:
The sky was cloudless yesterday. = 昨天天空晴朗。
It was a cloudless day for our picnic. = 我们野餐那天天气晴朗。


现在完成时:
It has been cloudless for several days. = 好几天都是晴天。
We have enjoyed many cloudless days this summer. = 今年夏天我们享受了许多晴朗的日子。


过去完成时:
It had been cloudless all morning. = 一上午都是晴天。
Before the storm, it had been cloudless for weeks. = 暴风雨来临之前，好几个星期都是晴天。


将来时:
The forecast predicts that it will be cloudless tomorrow. = 预报说明天将是晴天。
We hope it will be cloudless for the wedding. = 我们希望婚礼那天是晴天。


虚拟语气:
If it were cloudless tomorrow, we would go hiking. = 如果明天是晴天，我们就去远足。
I wish it were cloudless for my vacation. = 我希望我的假期是晴天。


被动语态: (此形容词不适用被动语态)


疑问句:
Is the sky cloudless today? = 今天天空晴朗吗？
Will it be cloudless for the game? = 比赛时天气会晴朗吗？


否定句:
The sky is not cloudless; it's cloudy. = 天空不晴朗，而是阴天。
It wasn't cloudless when we left the house. = 我们离开家的时候，天气不晴朗。


PHRASE->
cloudless sky = 晴朗的天空
cloudless day = 晴朗的一天
cloudless night = 晴朗的夜晚
cloudless blue = 湛蓝无云
a cloudless future = 光明美好的未来


词源:
The word "cloudless" is formed by adding the suffix "-less" (meaning "without") to the noun "cloud."  This indicates the absence of clouds.
