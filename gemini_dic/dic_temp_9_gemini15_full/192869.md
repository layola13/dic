seasonable:/ˈsiːzənəbl/ | adj. | 应时的；合季节的；适时的 | Suitable for the season; timely.  The word implies appropriateness to the time of year or a particular occasion. |例句：The weather was seasonable for October, with crisp air and fallen leaves. 十月份的天气很应景，空气清新，树叶飘落。

例句：He gave a seasonable speech, addressing the concerns of the audience perfectly. 他发表了一篇适时的演讲，完美地回应了听众的担忧。


近义词：timely, appropriate, opportune, suitable | 反义词：untimely, inappropriate, inopportune, unsuitable


词源：源自中古英语 *seisonable，由古法语 saisonable演变而来，其中saison意为“季节”。


USAGE->

The weather is seasonable for this time of year. = 现在的天气适合这个季节。
This fruit is seasonable and tastes delicious. =  这种水果应季，味道鲜美。

将来时:
