foregone:/fɔːrˈɡɒn/| /fɔːrˈɡɔːn/| adj.| 预料之中的；已往的；放弃的 | Already known or decided; inevitable.  Something that has already happened or been decided. |例句：The outcome of the election was a foregone conclusion.  选举的结果是意料之中的。

例句：His success was a foregone conclusion given his talent and hard work.  鉴于他的天赋和努力，他的成功是意料之中的。


近义词：inevitable, certain, preordained, settled, expected

反义词：uncertain, unexpected, unforeseen, improbable

词性：形容词
