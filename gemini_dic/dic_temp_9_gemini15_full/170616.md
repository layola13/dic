pseudo-Greeks:/ˌsuːdəʊˈgriːks/| adj.|伪希腊的；貌似希腊的 |  Appearing to be Greek in origin or style but actually not genuinely so.  The term often refers to words or styles that mimic Greek forms but lack genuine etymological connection to the Greek language.  |例句：The building's architecture, while impressive, is considered pseudo-Greek, lacking the authenticity of true Hellenic design.  这栋建筑的建筑风格虽然令人印象深刻，却被认为是伪希腊风格，缺乏真正的希腊风格的真实性。|近义词：pseudoclassical, sham, imitation, artificial|反义词：authentic, genuine, real, true
