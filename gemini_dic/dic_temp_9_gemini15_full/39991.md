consult-with:/kənˈsʌlt wɪð/| v.|与…商量；咨询；请教|To seek information or advice from (someone) especially in a professional capacity.|例句：I need to consult with my lawyer before I sign this contract. 我在签署这份合同之前需要咨询我的律师。  例句：She consulted with several doctors before deciding on a treatment plan.  她在决定治疗方案之前咨询了几位医生。|近义词：confer with, discuss with, advise with|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I consult with my advisor weekly. = 我每周都与我的顾问商量。
They consult with experts in the field. = 他们咨询该领域的专家。

一般过去时:
He consulted with his colleagues before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前咨询了他的同事。
She consulted with a financial planner about her retirement. = 她就退休事宜咨询了一位理财规划师。

现在完成时:
I have consulted with several specialists about my condition. = 我已经就我的病情咨询了几位专家。
We have consulted with the stakeholders throughout the process. = 在整个过程中，我们一直在与利益相关者协商。

过去完成时:
She had consulted with her parents before accepting the job offer. = 在接受这份工作邀请之前，她已经咨询了她的父母。
They had consulted with a lawyer before filing the lawsuit. = 在提起诉讼之前，他们已经咨询了律师。

将来时:
I will consult with my doctor about my symptoms. = 我将就我的症状咨询我的医生。
We will consult with the board of directors before proceeding. = 在继续之前，我们将与董事会协商。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would consult with a professional. = 如果我是你，我会咨询专业人士。
I wish I had consulted with an expert earlier. = 我真希望我早点咨询专家。

被动语态:
The CEO was consulted with before the final decision was made. = 在做出最终决定之前，已经咨询了首席执行官。
The team was consulted with extensively on the project. = 该团队在项目上得到了广泛的咨询。

疑问句:
Have you consulted with anyone about this problem? = 你有没有就这个问题咨询过任何人？
Will they consult with the union before implementing changes? = 他们在实施变革之前会与工会协商吗？

否定句:
I did not consult with anyone before making the purchase. = 我在购买之前没有咨询任何人。
They did not consult with the community about the proposed development. = 他们没有就拟议中的开发项目与社区协商。
