mestizoes:/mɛsˈtiːzoʊz/| n. |混血儿（复数）|Persons of mixed European and Indigenous ancestry, particularly in Latin America and Spain.  The term often specifically refers to individuals of mixed European (usually Spanish) and Indigenous American ancestry.|例句：Many of the inhabitants of Mexico are mestizos. 墨西哥的许多居民是混血儿。| The term "mestizo" is increasingly viewed as problematic due to its historical association with colonialism and racial hierarchy.  More neutral terms like "mixed-race" or specific ethnic descriptions are often preferred. |近义词：mixed-race, mixed-blood (less preferred)|反义词：purebred (in a racial context), unmixed
