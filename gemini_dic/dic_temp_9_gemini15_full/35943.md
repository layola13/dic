coddle:/ˈkɒdl/ /ˈkɑːdl/| vt. n.| 溺爱；悉心照料；轻声细语地安慰；（尤指用文火）炖煮 |To treat someone with excessive care and attention, often to the point of spoiling them; to treat tenderly; to cook (food) gently in simmering liquid.|例句：She coddled her son, never letting him face any challenges. 她溺爱她的儿子，从不让孩子面对任何挑战。|例句：The chef coddled the eggs in a gentle simmer. 厨师用文火慢慢炖鸡蛋。|近义词：pamper, spoil, indulge, cherish |反义词：neglect, ignore, abuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She coddles her child excessively. = 她过度溺爱她的孩子。
He coddles his plants with meticulous care. = 他细致入微地呵护他的植物。

一般过去时:
My grandmother coddled me when I was sick. = 我生病的时候，奶奶悉心照料我。
They coddled the baby all night. = 他们整夜都在细心照料婴儿。

现在完成时:
She has coddled her son for years. = 她多年来一直溺爱她的儿子。
They have coddled the vegetables until they are tender. = 他们把蔬菜炖得很嫩。

过去完成时:
She had coddled him since he was a child. = 从他还是个孩子的时候，她就一直溺爱他。
The cook had coddled the fish for an hour. = 厨师把鱼炖了一个小时。


将来时:
I will coddle my new plants until they are strong. = 我会呵护我的新植物直到它们茁壮成长。
She will coddle the soup for another fifteen minutes. = 她还会把汤炖十五分钟。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't coddle him so much. = 如果我是你，我不会那么溺爱他。
I wish I had coddled my plants more carefully. = 我真希望我更仔细地呵护我的植物。

被动语态:
The child was coddled by his parents. = 这个孩子被他的父母溺爱着。
The vegetables were coddled until they were soft. = 蔬菜炖到变软为止。

疑问句:
Do you often coddle your children? = 你经常溺爱你的孩子吗？
Does she coddle her plants regularly? = 她经常呵护她的植物吗？

否定句:
He doesn't coddle his employees. = 他不溺爱他的员工。
They didn't coddle the eggs long enough. = 他们没有把鸡蛋炖够时间。
