esquire:/ɪˈskwʌɪər/| n.|先生，准男爵|A title of respect formerly given to young men of good family, now often used as a form of address or as part of a name (e.g., John Smith Esquire). It is also a title of respect for lawyers.|例句：He was addressed as "Mr. Jones Esquire." 他被称为“琼斯先生”。|近义词：Mr., gentleman, sir|反义词:None
