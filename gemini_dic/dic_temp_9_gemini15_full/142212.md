ornithologically:/ˌɔːrnɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkli/| adv. | 鸟类学地；从鸟类学的角度 |Adverb describing something done or considered from the perspective of ornithology (the branch of zoology dealing with birds).  It indicates an approach or analysis related to the study of birds. |The birds' behavior was analyzed ornithologically, revealing intricate social structures. 鸟类的行为经过鸟类学分析，揭示了复杂的社会结构。|  |  |adverb
