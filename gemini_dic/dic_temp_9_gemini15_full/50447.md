depreciates:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪt/ | v. |贬值；贬低；使...降低价值 |To decrease in value over time; to belittle or undervalue something. |例句：The value of the car depreciates over time. 这辆车的价值随着时间推移而贬值。|例句：Don't depreciate your own accomplishments. 不要贬低你自己的成就。|近义词：devalue, lessen, diminish, undervalue |反义词：appreciate, increase, enhance, overvalue |动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dollar depreciates against the euro. = 美元兑欧元贬值。
My skills depreciate without practice. = 我的技能不用就贬值了。

一般过去时:
The property depreciated significantly last year. = 去年房产大幅贬值。
He depreciated her efforts to help him. = 他贬低了她帮助他的努力。

现在完成时:
The car has depreciated considerably since we bought it. = 自从我们买了这辆车以来，它的价值已经大幅贬值。
She has depreciated herself in front of her boss. = 她在她老板面前贬低了自己。

过去完成时:
The company's stock had depreciated sharply before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，该公司的股票已经大幅贬值。
He had depreciated his own abilities before the interview. = 他在面试前贬低了自己的能力。

将来时:
The value of this artwork will depreciate over the next few years. = 这件艺术品的价值在未来几年将会贬值。
He will depreciate her work if she doesn't finish the project on time. = 如果她没有按时完成项目，他会贬低她的工作。

虚拟语气:
If the market continues to fall, our investments would depreciate further. = 如果市场继续下跌，我们的投资将会进一步贬值。
If he had not depreciated her ideas, she might have felt more confident. = 如果他没有贬低她的想法，她可能会更有信心。

被动语态:
The currency was depreciated by government policy. = 货币因政府政策而贬值。
Her contributions were depreciated by the team leader. = 她的贡献被团队领导贬低了。

疑问句:
Does the equipment depreciate over time? = 设备会随着时间推移而贬值吗？
Will my work be depreciated if I miss the deadline? = 如果我错过截止日期，我的工作会被贬低吗？

否定句:
The house did not depreciate in value during that period. = 那段时间房价没有贬值。
He did not depreciate her hard work and dedication. = 他没有贬低她的辛勤工作和奉献精神。


PHRASE->
depreciate in value = 贬值
rapidly depreciate = 快速贬值
significantly depreciate = 大幅贬值
depreciate over time = 随着时间推移而贬值
depreciate an asset = 计提资产折旧


词源:
depreciate 源自于拉丁语 "deprecari"，意思是"恳求，祈求"。  后来演变为 "depretiare"，意思是“降低价格”。  最终通过法语进入英语。
