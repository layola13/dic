disarmament:/dɪsˈɑːrməmənt/| n. |裁军；解除武装|The process of reducing or eliminating a country's armed forces and weapons.  It often involves international agreements and treaties aimed at promoting peace and security.|例句：The success of disarmament talks depends heavily on mutual trust between nations. 裁军谈判的成功很大程度上取决于各国之间的相互信任。|例句：  Following the end of the war, there was a significant push for disarmament across the globe. 战争结束后，全球范围内掀起了一股强大的裁军浪潮。|近义词：demilitarization, demilitarisation|反义词：armament, militarization
