nonprescriber: /ˌnɒnprɪˈskraɪbər/ | n. | 非处方药使用者，非处方药销售者 | A person who does not prescribe medication; or a seller of non-prescription medication.  | Example:  The nonprescriber bought over-the-counter pain relievers at the pharmacy.  非处方药使用者在药店买了非处方止痛药。 |近义词:  over-the-counter drug user, non-physician dispenser | 反义词: prescriber, physician, doctor | noun
