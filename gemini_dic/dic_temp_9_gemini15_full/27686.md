candlenut:/ˈkændəlˌnʌt/| n. |烛果，烛果树|The candlenut is the seed of the Aleurites moluccana tree, used in cooking and making oil.  It's also known as kukui nut.|例句：Candlenuts are a staple ingredient in many Southeast Asian dishes.  烛果是许多东南亚菜肴中的主要食材。

例句：The oil extracted from candlenuts is used in traditional medicine and cosmetics. 从烛果中提取的油用于传统医药和化妆品。


近义词：kukui nut (same plant)


反义词：无


词性：名词
