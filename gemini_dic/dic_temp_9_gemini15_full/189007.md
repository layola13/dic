sabaean:/səˈbiːən/| adj. n. |萨巴人；萨巴语的 |Of or relating to the Sabaeans, an ancient South Arabian people who lived in what is now Yemen.  Their civilization flourished from the 8th century BC to the 6th century AD, known for their advanced irrigation systems, trade, and distinctive art and architecture.  The Sabaean language belongs to the South Semitic branch of the Afro-Asiatic language family. |例句：The Sabaean inscriptions found at Marib provide valuable insights into the kingdom's history.  萨巴铭文在马里卜被发现，为王国历史提供了宝贵的见解。  The museum houses a remarkable collection of Sabaean artifacts.  博物馆收藏了大量非凡的萨巴文物。|近义词：South Arabian, Yemeni (in a historical context) |反义词：无
