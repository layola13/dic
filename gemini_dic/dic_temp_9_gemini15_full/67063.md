explicit:/ɪkˈsplɪsɪt/ | adj. | 明确的；详尽的；露骨的 | Clearly and openly stated or shown; leaving no room for confusion or doubt.  | The contract was explicit about the terms of payment. 合同明确规定了付款条件。The movie contains explicit sexual scenes. 这部电影包含露骨的性爱场面。 | implicit, clear, unambiguous | implicit, vague, subtle | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The instructions are explicit. = 说明很明确。
The contract is explicit about the deadlines. = 合同对截止日期规定得很明确。

一般过去时:
He gave explicit directions. = 他给了明确的指示。

现在完成时:
She has been explicit about her intentions. = 她已经明确表达了她的意图。
They have made their position explicit in a public statement. = 他们在公开声明中明确表达了他们的立场。

过去完成时:
He had already made his intentions explicit. = 他早已明确表达了他的意图。

将来时:
