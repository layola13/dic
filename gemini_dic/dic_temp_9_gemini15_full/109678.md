leucoplastid: /ˈluːkəplæstɪd/ | n. |白色体；无色体 | A colorless plastid, a type of organelle found in plant cells, that does not contain chlorophyll and is involved in the synthesis and storage of starch, lipids, or proteins.  | Example:  Leucoplasts are important for storing energy reserves in plants.  白色体对植物储存能量储备很重要。 |近义词：plastid (更广义的词)|反义词：chloroplast
