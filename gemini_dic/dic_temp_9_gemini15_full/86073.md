hearken:/ˈhɑːrkən/| vt.|仔细听，倾听|To listen attentively; to give heed.  The word implies a deliberate act of listening, often to something important or significant.|例句：The crowd hearkened to the speaker's every word.  人群仔细聆听着发言人的每一句话。

例句：He hearkened to the old man's wise counsel. 他仔细聆听了老人的明智建议。


近义词：listen, attend, heed|反义词：ignore, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hearkens to the advice of his elders. 他倾听长辈的建议。
They hearken to the wisdom of the ages. 他们倾听着时代的智慧。


一般过去时:
She hearkened to the siren's call. 她倾听了海妖的呼唤。
We hearkened to the rustling leaves in the wind. 我们倾听着风中沙沙作响的树叶。


现在完成时:
I have hearkened to many different philosophies. 我已经倾听了许多不同的哲学思想。
She has hearkened to the warnings of the experts. 她已经倾听了专家的警告。


过去完成时:
They had hearkened to the rumors before making a decision. 他们在做决定之前已经听取了谣言。
He had hearkened to the stories of his grandfather. 他已经听过他祖父的故事了。


将来时:
We will hearken to the judge's verdict. 我们将倾听法官的判决。
They will hearken to the advice of their mentors. 他们将倾听导师的建议。


虚拟语气:
If I hearkened to my intuition, I would have avoided that mistake. 如果我倾听我的直觉，我就会避免那个错误。
I wish I had hearkened to her warning. 我真希望我听了她的警告。


被动语态:
His words were hearkened to by all present.  在场的人都倾听了他的话。
The advice was hearkened to with great care.  这条建议被非常仔细地倾听了。


疑问句:
Will you hearken to my plea? 你会倾听我的请求吗？
Did they hearken to the evidence presented? 他们倾听了提出的证据吗？


否定句:
He did not hearken to the warnings of the storm. 他没有倾听暴风雨的警告。
They refused to hearken to reason.  他们拒绝倾听道理。


PHRASE->

hearken unto: 听从
hearken to the call of: 响应……的号召

give heed to: 注意，留心 (近义词组)
