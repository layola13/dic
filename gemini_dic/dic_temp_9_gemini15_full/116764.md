maus:/maʊs/ | n. |老鼠，鼠标 | A small rodent that typically has a pointed snout, relatively large ears, and a long tail.  The term also refers to a computer input device. |例句：The cat chased the mouse. 猫追赶老鼠。|例句：I need a new mouse for my computer. 我需要一个新的电脑鼠标。|近义词：rodent, computer mouse |反义词：cat (predator)
