shut: /ʃʌt/ | vt. vi. adj. | 关闭；停止；禁闭；沉默 | to close something, or to become closed; to stop something from happening or continuing;  to keep someone or something inside a place | 近义词：close, stop, silence | 反义词：open, start


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shuts the door every night. = 他每晚都关门。
She shuts her eyes when she's scared. = 她害怕时会闭上眼睛。


一般过去时:
I shut the window because it was raining. = 我关上了窗户，因为下雨了。
They shut the shop early yesterday. = 他们昨天早早关了店。


现在完成时:
She has shut the book and put it on the shelf. = 她已经合上书并把它放在书架上了。
We have shut down the computer for the night. = 我们已经关掉了电脑准备过夜了。


过去完成时:
He had shut the door before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他已经关上了门。
They had shut the factory down before the strike. = 他们在罢工前已经关闭了工厂。


将来时:
I will shut the door when I leave. = 我离开时会关门。
He will shut off the water supply tomorrow. = 他明天将关闭供水。


虚拟语气:
If I shut my eyes, I can't see anything. = 如果我闭上眼睛，我就什么也看不见。
I wish I could shut out all the noise. = 我真希望我能屏蔽掉所有的噪音。


被动语态:
The door was shut by the wind. = 门被风吹关上了。
The factory was shut down last year. = 工厂去年关闭了。


疑问句:
Is the window shut? = 窗户关上了吗？
Did you shut the gate? = 你关上大门了吗？


否定句:
Don't shut the door! = 不要关门！
He didn't shut off the engine. = 他没有关闭引擎。


PHRASE->
shut down: 关闭
shut up: 闭嘴
shut off: 关闭（水、电等）
shut out: 排除；挡住
shut in: 困住；封闭


词源:
The word "shut" comes from Old English *sceotan, meaning "to shoot, push, thrust,"  related to words like "shoot" and "shot."  Its meaning evolved to encompass the action of closing something tightly, much like a bolt being shot into place.
