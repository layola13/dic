outriders:/ˈaʊtraɪdərz/| n.|先锋；前锋；侦察兵|People or things that go ahead of a larger group, especially to explore or scout.|例句：The outriders led the cavalry across the plains. 先锋带领骑兵穿过平原。
例句：The company sent outriders to check the road ahead for any potential hazards. 公司派出了先锋去检查前方道路上是否有潜在的危险。|近义词：scouts, vanguard, pioneers|反义词：rear guard, followers
