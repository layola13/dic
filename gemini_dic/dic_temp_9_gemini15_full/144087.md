outsails:/ˌaʊtˈseɪlz/| vt.|超越(航速), 胜过(速度)|To sail faster than; to exceed in speed of sailing.|例句：Our boat outsails all others in the race.我们的船在比赛中胜过所有其他船只。
例句：The new yacht outsails its predecessor by a considerable margin. 新游艇的速度大大超过了它的前身。|近义词：outstrip, surpass, outpace|反义词：be outsailed by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outsails all his competitors. 他比所有竞争对手都快。
The swift ship outsails the slower ones. 这艘快速船比那些慢船速度快。


一般过去时:
The small sailboat outsailed the larger one in the race. 小型帆船在比赛中超过了大型帆船。
Yesterday, the wind helped our boat outsail the others. 昨天，风帮助我们的船超过了其他船只。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

Before the race ended, the underdog had outsailed the favorites. 在比赛结束之前，黑马已经超过了热门选手。

虚拟语气:
If the wind had been stronger, we would have outsailed them easily. 如果风更大一些，我们就能轻松地超过他们。
If only we had a faster boat, we could outsail the current leader. 如果我们有一艘更快的船，我们就能超过目前的领先者。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
