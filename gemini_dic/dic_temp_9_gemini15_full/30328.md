censured:/ˈsɛnʃərd/| vt.|谴责；非难；批评；审查|To express strong disapproval of; to officially reprimand.|The committee censured the member for misconduct.  委员会因行为不端而谴责该成员。|The teacher censured the student for cheating on the exam. 老师因考试作弊而批评学生。|criticize, condemn, rebuke, reprimand|praise, commend, approve|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The council censures unethical behavior. = 理事会谴责不道德行为。
He frequently censures his colleagues' work. = 他经常批评同事的工作。

一般过去时:
The judge censured the lawyer for his unprofessional conduct. = 法官因律师的不专业行为而谴责他。
The school censured the student for plagiarism. = 学校因剽窃而谴责该学生。


现在完成时:
The government has censured the company for environmental violations. = 政府因违反环境法规而谴责了该公司。
The media has censured the politician for his misleading statements. = 媒体因其误导性言论而谴责了这位政治家。


过去完成时:
The committee had censured him twice before this incident. = 在此事件之前，委员会已经谴责过他两次。
The board had censured the CEO for his mismanagement. = 董事会因其管理不善而谴责了首席执行官。


将来时:
The senate will likely censure the senator for his involvement in the scandal. = 参议院很可能会因其卷入丑闻而谴责这位参议员。
The organization will censure any member found guilty of misconduct. = 该组织将谴责任何被发现有不当行为的成员。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, they would censure him. = 如果证据更充分，他们就会谴责他。
I wish the committee had censured the offender more severely. = 我希望委员会对违规者处罚更严厉一些。


被动语态:
He was censured for his inflammatory remarks. = 他因煽动性言论而受到谴责。
The proposal was censured by the board of directors. = 该提案被董事会否决。


疑问句:
Will the organization censure him for his actions? = 该组织会因他的行为而谴责他吗？
Was the employee censured for their mistake? = 员工因其错误而受到谴责了吗？


否定句:
The committee did not censure the member despite the accusations. = 尽管有指控，委员会也没有谴责该成员。
They chose not to censure him, hoping he would learn from his mistakes. = 他们选择不谴责他，希望他能从错误中吸取教训。


PHRASE->
to censure severely = 严厉谴责
public censure = 公开谴责
to be censured by = 被…谴责
to deserve censure = 应该受到谴责
under censure = 受到谴责


词源:
源自拉丁语 *censere “评估；判断；税收”，是census（人口普查）的词根。  其含义演变为表达强烈的不赞成。
