shun:/ʃʌn/| vt.|避免，躲避，规避|to avoid or keep away from (someone or something)|He shunned all contact with his former associates. 他避开了与他以前同伙的一切联系。|近义词：avoid, evade, escape|反义词：seek, embrace


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shuns social gatherings. = 他避免参加社交聚会。
They shun the limelight. = 他们躲避公众视线。


一般过去时:
She shunned responsibility. = 她逃避责任。
He shunned his family after the argument. = 争吵后，他躲避了他的家人。


现在完成时:
I have shunned unhealthy foods for years. = 多年来我一直避免食用不健康食品。
She has shunned her old friends since moving. = 自从搬家后，她一直回避她的老朋友。


过去完成时:
He had shunned the offer before they even made it. = 他们甚至还没提出邀请，他就已经拒绝了。
She had shunned contact with her ex-husband for months. = 她已经几个月没有与前夫联系了。


将来时:
I will shun that path in the future. = 我将来会避开那条路。
They will shun any further involvement in the project. = 他们将避免进一步参与该项目。


虚拟语气:
I would shun that opportunity if I were you. = 如果我是你，我会避开这个机会。
If I had known better, I would have shunned the deal. = 如果我当时知道得更多，我就会避免那笔交易。


被动语态:
The dangerous area is shunned by most people. = 大多数人都避开那个危险区域。
His advice was shunned by the board. = 他的建议被董事会否决了。


疑问句:
Why do they shun this topic? = 为什么他们回避这个话题？
Will you shun your responsibilities? = 你会逃避你的责任吗？


否定句:
He doesn't shun hard work. = 他不逃避辛苦的工作。
She didn't shun the challenge. = 她没有回避挑战。
