embezzle:/ɪmˈbezəl/| vt.|挪用；盗用；侵吞|To steal or misappropriate (money or funds) entrusted to one's care.  This typically involves a breach of trust and often occurs within a position of authority or responsibility.|例句：The accountant embezzled thousands of dollars from the company. 会计挪用了公司数千美元。

例句：He was found guilty of embezzling funds from his clients' accounts. 他被判犯有挪用客户账户资金的罪行。


近义词：misappropriate, misapply, defraud, pilfer, steal

反义词：donate, contribute, give


词性：动词

She embezzles funds regularly. 她定期挪用资金。
