lounging:/ˈlaʊndʒɪŋ/| v. |闲逛；懒洋洋地坐着或躺着|The act of relaxing in a comfortable and leisurely manner, often implying a lack of urgency or activity.|例句：He spent the afternoon lounging by the pool. 他在游泳池边悠闲地度过了下午。
例句：She was lounging on the sofa, reading a book. 她懒洋洋地躺在沙发上看书。|近义词：relaxing, reclining, loafing|反义词：hustling, rushing, working


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lounges around the house all day. = 他整天在家里闲逛。
They lounge by the pool every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在泳池边休息。

一般过去时:
She lounged on the beach all day. = 她整天在海滩上闲逛。
We lounged in the park after the picnic. = 野餐后我们在公园里休息。

现在完成时:
I have been lounging around since morning. = 我从早上开始就一直闲逛。
They have lounged by the fire all evening. = 他们整个晚上都懒洋洋地坐在火炉旁。

过去完成时:
He had lounged on the sofa before the guests arrived. = 客人到来之前，他一直在沙发上休息。
She had lounged by the pool all day before she finally went swimming. = 在她最终下水游泳之前，她一整天都在泳池边休息。

将来时:
We will lounge on the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海滩上休息。

虚拟语气:

I wish I could lounge on a beach right now. = 我真希望现在能躺在沙滩上。

被动语态:  (Lounging is rarely used in the passive voice.)
