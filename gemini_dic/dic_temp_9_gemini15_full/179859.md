reexplored:/ˌriːɪkˈsplɔːrd/| v.|重新探索，再次探索|To explore again or anew; to examine or investigate something again.|例句：The team reexplored the cave system, discovering new passages. 团队重新探索了洞穴系统，发现了新的通道。

例句：Historians reexplored the archives, hoping to find new evidence. 历史学家们重新查阅了档案，希望能找到新的证据。


近义词：reinvestigate, reexamine, revisit|反义词：ignore, neglect|词性：动词
