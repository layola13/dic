prosector:/ˈprɒsɪkjuːtər/ | n. |检察官，公诉人 |A prosecutor is a legal professional who conducts criminal proceedings on behalf of the state or government.  They are responsible for investigating crimes, presenting evidence in court, and arguing for convictions.  | Example: The prosecutor presented compelling evidence against the defendant.  检察官提供了令人信服的证据来指控被告。 |近义词：public prosecutor, district attorney, state's attorney |反义词：defendant, accused

词源:

The word "prosecutor" comes from the Latin word "prosecutus," the past participle of "prosequi," meaning "to follow up," "to pursue," or "to prosecute."  This reflects the prosecutor's role in pursuing legal action against those accused of crimes.
