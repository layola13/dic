piquet:/pɪˈkeɪ/| n.|皮克特（纸牌游戏）；警戒；哨兵；|A card game for two players using 32 cards.  It is a trick-taking game with a strong element of strategy.  The term can also refer to a military picket or guard. |例句：He enjoys playing piquet in the evenings. 他喜欢在晚上玩皮克特。
例句：The soldiers were posted on piquet along the border. 士兵们被派往边境站岗。|近义词：sentry, guard, patrol, picket line|反义词：None specifically applicable|名词
