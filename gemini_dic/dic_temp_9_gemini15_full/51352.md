detent: /dɪˈtɛnt/ | n. |棘爪；锁止；阻挡装置 | A small mechanical device that prevents movement or holds something in position; a catch or pawl.  It's often used in mechanisms to temporarily stop or hold a moving part. | The detent keeps the gear from slipping.  棘爪防止齿轮打滑。

The detent on the camera's shutter button ensures it doesn't fire accidentally. 相机快门按钮上的棘爪确保它不会意外触发。| pawl, catch, latch, click |  free-running (antonym, only applicable in certain contexts) | 名词
