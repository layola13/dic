gabions:/ˈɡæbiɒnz/| n. |格宾，格宾网|A gabion is a cage, cylindrical or rectangular, typically made of wire mesh, filled with rocks, concrete, or other materials, used for erosion control, retaining walls, or other structural applications.|例句：Gabions were used to stabilize the riverbank. 格宾用于稳定河岸。  例句：The retaining wall was constructed using gabions filled with stones.  用装满石头的格宾建造了挡土墙。|近义词：retaining wall, crib wall|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑与之功能相反的结构，如混凝土墙)|名词
