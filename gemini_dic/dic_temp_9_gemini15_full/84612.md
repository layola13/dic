hamulus:/ˈhæmjʊləs/| n. |小钩，钩状物 |A small hook-shaped structure.  The term is used in anatomy, zoology, and botany to describe various hooked parts. For example, in anatomy, it refers to the hook-like projection on the sphenoid bone. In botany, it might describe a hooked bristle or spine. |Example: The hamular process of the sphenoid bone is an important anatomical landmark.  这个词指的是蝶骨上的钩状突，是一个重要的解剖学标志。|近义词：hook, barb, crochet |反义词：straight projection, smooth surface |
