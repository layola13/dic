gownsman:/ˈɡaʊnzˌmæn/| n. |穿长袍的人，大学成员|A gownsman is a member of a university, especially one who wears a gown.  The term often evokes a traditional image of a scholar or academic. |Example: He was a distinguished gownsman at Oxford University. 他是牛津大学一位杰出的大学成员。  The gownsmen processed in their academic robes. 穿着学位袍的大学成员列队前进。|近义词：academic, scholar, university member |反义词：layperson, non-academic
