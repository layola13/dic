improbableness:/ɪmˈprɒbəblənəs/ | n. | 不可能性；难以置信 | The improbableness of the event made it hard to believe.  事件的极小可能性使其难以置信。|近义词： unlikelihood, improbability | 反义词： likelihood, probability


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  Therefore, grammatical tenses (present, past, future, etc.) and voice (active/passive) are not applicable.  The word itself expresses a state of being.
