outtricking:/ˌaʊtˈtrɪkɪŋ/| v.|智胜，技高一筹|To surpass in cunning or skill; to outsmart.|The magician outtricked the audience with his amazing illusions. 魔术师以其令人惊叹的幻象技压全场。|近义词：outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver|反义词：being outsmarted, being tricked
