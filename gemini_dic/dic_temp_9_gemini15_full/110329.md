lighterman:/ˈlaɪtərmən/| n.|驳船夫，纤夫|A lighterman is a person who guides or pulls barges or other vessels on canals or rivers, especially using a rope or other method from the towpath.|例句：The lighterman skillfully navigated the barge through the narrow canal. 纤夫熟练地将驳船驶过狭窄的运河。  例句：He worked as a lighterman on the Thames for many years. 他在泰晤士河上做了多年的纤夫。|近义词：bargeman, boatman|反义词：None specifically.
