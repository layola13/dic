kmph:/ˌkeɪ ˈɛm piː ˈeɪtʃ/|abbr.|公里每小时|Kilometers per hour; a unit of speed.|例句：The car was traveling at 100 kmph. 这辆车正以每小时100公里的速度行驶。|近义词：km/h|反义词：无


USAGE->
This is an abbreviation and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  It functions solely as a unit of measurement.  There are no tenses, voices, or moods to conjugate.
