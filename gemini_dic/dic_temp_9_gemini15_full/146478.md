overfunds:/ˈoʊvərˌfʌndz/| v.|超额拨款；提供过多资金|To provide more funds than are needed or requested.  |例句：The project was overfunded by nearly 10%, leading to budget surpluses.|近义词：overfinance, superfluously fund|反义词：underfund


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government overfunds certain programs. = 政府为某些项目超额拨款。
They often overfund their marketing campaigns. = 他们经常为他们的营销活动提供过多的资金。


一般过去时:
The committee overfunded the research project last year. = 委员会去年为该研究项目超额拨款。
They overfunded the construction of the new bridge. = 他们为新建桥梁的建设提供了过多的资金。


现在完成时:
The company has overfunded its retirement plan for years. = 多年来，该公司一直为其退休计划超额拨款。
They have overfunded this project, resulting in waste. = 他们为这个项目超额拨款，导致了浪费。


过去完成时:
They had overfunded the project before realizing their mistake. = 他们在意识到自己的错误之前就为该项目超额拨款了。
The organization had overfunded its charitable giving campaign. = 该组织为其慈善捐赠活动提供了过多的资金。


将来时:
We will likely overfund the expansion if we're not careful. = 如果我们不小心，我们很可能会为扩张项目超额拨款。
They will overfund the next phase of the development. = 他们将为开发的下一阶段提供过多的资金。


虚拟语气:
If they had not overfunded the project, they would have saved money. = 如果他们没有为该项目超额拨款，他们本来可以节省资金。
I wish they hadn't overfunded the marketing campaign. = 我真希望他们没有为营销活动超额拨款。


被动语态:
The project was overfunded by the board of directors. = 该项目获得了董事会的超额拨款。
The research was overfunded, leading to inefficient spending. = 研究经费超支，导致资金使用效率低下。


疑问句:
Was the project overfunded? = 该项目是否超额拨款？
Have they overfunded the development of the new software? = 他们是否为新软件的开发提供了过多的资金？


否定句:
The school did not overfund its sports program. = 学校没有为其体育项目超额拨款。
They did not overfund the project; they were actually underfunded. = 他们没有为该项目超额拨款；实际上资金不足。
