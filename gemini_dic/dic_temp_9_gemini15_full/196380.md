sermonising: /ˈsɜːrmənɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 说教；教训；长篇大论地劝诫 |  The act of delivering a sermon or preaching;  to lecture someone in a long and tedious way.  | 例句：He was sermonising about the importance of hard work. 他正在长篇大论地劝诫努力工作的重要性。


例句：Stop sermonising!  别再说教了！


近义词：preaching, lecturing, moralising, hectoring


反义词：praising, encouraging, supporting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sermonises constantly about the dangers of smoking. 他不断地宣讲吸烟的危害。
She sermonises to her children about good manners. 她告诫她的孩子们要讲礼貌。


一般过去时:
The priest sermonised on the theme of forgiveness. 牧师就宽恕的主题进行了布道。
My grandfather sermonised at length about the importance of saving money. 我祖父冗长地劝诫储蓄的重要性。


现在完成时:
He has sermonised against the evils of gambling for years.  多年来，他一直都在谴责赌博的恶习。
She has sermonised so much that people avoid her. 她说了太多教诲的话，以至于人们都躲着她。


过去完成时:
He had sermonised to the congregation for an hour before he finally concluded. 他向会众布道了一个小时才最终结束。
They had sermonised extensively about the virtues of charity before the collection plate was passed. 他们在募捐盘传递之前，已经广泛地宣讲了慈善的美德。


将来时:
The minister will sermonise on the parable of the Good Samaritan next Sunday. 牧师下星期天将就好撒玛利亚人的比喻进行布道。
She will sermonise again about the importance of education to her son. 她将再次向儿子宣讲教育的重要性。


虚拟语气:
If he sermonised less, people would listen more. 如果他少说教一些，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't sermonise so much. 我希望他不那么爱说教。


被动语态:
The congregation was sermonised to about the importance of community service. 会众被教导要重视社区服务。
The topic of environmental protection was sermonised upon at length.  环境保护的主题被冗长地宣讲。


疑问句:
Is he sermonising again about politics? 他又在对政治说教了吗？
Does she often sermonise to her employees about punctuality? 她经常对她的员工说教准时吗？


否定句:
He didn't sermonise at all during the meeting. 他在会议期间根本没有说教。
She doesn't sermonise often, only when necessary. 她不经常说教，只有在必要的时候才会。


PHRASE->
sermonise on (a topic): 就（某个话题）说教
sermonise at (someone):  对（某人）说教
sermonise endlessly:没完没了地说教
sermonise about (something):  对（某事）说教
sermonise to (someone): 对（某人）说教
