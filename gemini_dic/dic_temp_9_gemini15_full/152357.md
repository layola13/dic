pauperous:/ˈpɔːpərəs/| adj.|贫穷的，贫困的| extremely poor; lacking necessities of life.  The pauperous conditions in the slum were shocking. 贫民窟里的贫困状况令人震惊。|近义词：impoverished, indigent, destitute, needy|反义词：wealthy, affluent, rich
