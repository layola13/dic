/kən/ | prep. conj. | 关于；至于；比；虽然；尽管 |  A preposition and conjunction indicating a relationship, often of opposition or contrast. It can express various meanings depending on the context, including "regarding," "concerning," "with respect to," "although," "despite," "in comparison with," and "rather than." |

例句：
He was angry with her, concerning her behavior. = 他对她很生气，关于她的行为。

I will go, despite the rain. = 尽管下雨，我还是会去。


近义词：regarding, concerning, about, although, despite, notwithstanding, in comparison with

反义词：without, regardless of


USAGE->

I am concerned about his health. = 我担心他的健康。
What do you think concerning this issue? = 关于这个问题，你怎么想？


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

We have discussed concerning the next step. = 我们已经讨论了下一步该怎么走。


过去完成时:
She had been concerned about his safety before he left. = 他离开之前，她一直担心他的安全。
They had argued concerning the project before the meeting. = 会议之前，他们就这个项目争论过。


将来时:
I will be concerned about the results. = 我将会关注结果。
We will consult concerning the matter. = 我们将就此事进行磋商。


虚拟语气:

I wish I were not concerned about the exam. = 我希望我不必担心考试。


被动语态:
His health was concerned by the stress. = 他的健康受到了压力的影响。
The matter was concerned in the meeting. = 会议上讨论了此事。


疑问句:
Are you concerned about anything? = 你担心什么吗？
What are your concerns concerning the project? = 你对这个项目有什么担忧？


否定句:
I am not concerned about his opinion. = 我不关心他的意见。
They were not concerned with the details. = 他们不关心细节。

词源:
From Old French *con-, from Latin cum "with,"  The preposition and conjunction evolved from its original meaning of "with" to express a range of relationships, including those of accompaniment, association, and comparison.
