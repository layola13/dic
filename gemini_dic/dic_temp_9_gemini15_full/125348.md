multilingualisms: /ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəm/ | n. | 多语言现象，多语现象 | The coexistence of multiple languages within a specific geographical area or community.  It encompasses various aspects, including language use, language policy, and the social and cultural implications of language diversity.  It's not simply about the presence of multiple languages, but the dynamic interplay between them. | Example:  The multilingualisms of Singapore are a fascinating case study in language contact and code-switching.  新加坡的多语言现象是语言接触和代码转换的一个引人入胜的案例研究。| 近义词：linguistic diversity, polyglottism | 反义词：monolingualism
