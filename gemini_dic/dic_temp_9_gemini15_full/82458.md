grump:/ɡrʌmp/| n. v. adj.|脾气暴躁的人；抱怨；脾气暴躁的|A grump is a person who is habitually bad-tempered and irritable.  The word can also be used as a verb, meaning to complain or grumble, and as an adjective describing someone who is grumpy.|例句：He's a real grump in the mornings. 他早上脾气很坏。  He grumbled and grumbled, a real grump. 他抱怨个不停，真是个脾气暴躁的人。|近义词：grouch, curmudgeon, crab|反义词：cheerful, happy, jovial|名词，动词，形容词
