opposi: /ɒˈpɔːzi/ | adj. | 相反的，对面的 |  Opposite means being different from something else, or being in a position on the other side.  It can describe things that are contrary or contrasting, or locations that are directly across from each other. | Examples:  The opposite of hot is cold. = 热的反义词是冷。; They live on opposite sides of the street. = 他们住在街对面。 |近义词: contrary, reverse, converse | 反义词: same, identical, alike | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun rises in the east and sets in the west; they are opposite directions. = 太阳从东边升起，从西边落下；它们是相反的方向。
His opinion is opposite to mine. = 他的意见与我的相反。


一般过去时:
Their houses were opposite each other. = 他们的房子彼此相对。
The answer was the opposite of what I expected. = 答案与我预期的相反。


现在完成时:
We have always held opposite views on this matter. = 我们在这个问题上一直持有相反的观点。
The situation has become the opposite of what it was before. = 情况已经变成与之前相反了。


过去完成时:
I had thought the opposite before I heard the news. = 在听到这个消息之前，我曾想过相反的情况。
They had been on opposite sides of the argument for years. = 多年来，他们在论点上一直站在对立面。


将来时:
The results will likely be the opposite of what we hoped for. = 结果很可能与我们希望的相反。
The two teams will be playing opposite each other in the final. = 两支球队将在决赛中对阵。


虚拟语气:
If the situation were opposite, things would be much easier. = 如果情况相反，事情会容易得多。
I wish the outcome were opposite to what it is now. = 我希望结果与现在相反。


被动语态:
The two images were placed opposite each other in the gallery. = 两幅图像在画廊中被并列摆放。
His arguments were shown to be opposite to established facts. = 他的论点被证明与既定事实相反。


疑问句:
Is there an opposite meaning to this word? = 这个词有相反的意思吗？
Are the two concepts truly opposite? = 这两个概念真的是相反的吗？


否定句:
The two ideas are not opposite at all. = 这两个想法根本不相反。
His actions were not opposite to his words. = 他的行动并不与他的话语相反。
