dusts:/dʌsts/| v.|掸去灰尘，洒上灰尘|To remove dust from something using a cloth or brush; to sprinkle or cover with dust.|例句：She dusted the furniture before the guests arrived. 她来宾到来之前掸去了家具上的灰尘。|例句：The wind dusted the car with sand. 风把沙子洒在了汽车上。|近义词：clean, wipe, sprinkle|反义词: N/A


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He dusts his desk every morning. = 他每天早上都擦拭他的桌子。
She dusts the shelves regularly. = 她定期擦拭架子。


一般过去时:
I dusted the furniture yesterday. = 我昨天擦拭了家具。
They dusted the antique before putting it on display. = 他们把古董擦拭干净后才展出。


现在完成时:
She has dusted the entire house. = 她已经打扫了整个房子。
He has dusted off his old books and started reading. = 他掸去了旧书上的灰尘，开始阅读。


过去完成时:
I had dusted the table before the party started. = 派对开始前我已经擦拭了桌子。
They had dusted the artifacts carefully before transporting them. = 他们在运输文物之前小心翼翼地擦拭了它们。
