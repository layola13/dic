devolve:/dɪˈvɒlv/| /dɪˈvɑːlv/ | v. |移交；衰退；退化|To transfer or delegate power or responsibility to a lower level; to gradually decline or deteriorate. |例句：The company devolved responsibility for marketing to regional offices. 公司将市场营销的责任下放给地区办事处。

例句：The empire gradually devolved into a collection of squabbling principalities.  帝国逐渐衰落成为一群争吵不休的小公国。

近义词：delegate, transfer, deteriorate, decline

反义词：evolve, improve, ascend, centralize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government devolves power to local authorities. = 政府将权力下放给地方当局。
They devolve their responsibilities to their subordinates. = 他们将责任下放给下属。


一般过去时:
The empire devolved into several smaller kingdoms. = 帝国分裂成几个较小的王国。
The project devolved into chaos. = 项目陷入混乱。


现在完成时:
Power has devolved to the regional governments. = 权力已经下放给地方政府。
The situation has devolved into a crisis. = 局势已经演变成危机。


过去完成时:
The company had devolved its manufacturing operations overseas. = 公司已将其制造业务转移到海外。
The family had devolved into a state of constant conflict. = 这个家庭已经陷入持续不断的冲突状态。


将来时:
The company will devolve more responsibility to its employees. = 公司将赋予员工更多责任。
The situation will likely devolve into violence. = 局势很可能会演变成暴力冲突。


虚拟语气:
If they devolved more power, things might improve. = 如果他们下放更多权力，情况可能会改善。
I wish the situation hadn't devolved so quickly. = 我希望情况没有恶化得这么快。


被动语态:
Responsibility was devolved to the junior staff. = 责任被下放给初级员工。
The decision to devolve power was widely criticized. = 将权力下放的决定受到了广泛批评。


疑问句:
Will the company devolve its marketing strategy? = 公司会下放其市场营销策略吗？
Has the situation devolved to the point of no return? = 局势是否已经发展到无法挽回的地步？


否定句:
The government did not devolve sufficient power to the local councils. = 政府没有将足够的权力下放给地方议会。
The situation did not devolve into a full-blown crisis. = 局势并没有演变成全面危机。
