obey:/oʊˈbeɪ/ | vt. |服从，遵守 |To comply with or follow (a rule, order, instruction, etc.).  |例句：You must obey the law. 你必须遵守法律。|例句：The soldiers obeyed their commander's orders. 士兵们服从了指挥官的命令。|近义词：comply with, follow, heed |反义词：disobey, defy, resist |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I obey my parents. = 我服从我的父母。
He obeys all the rules. = 他遵守所有规则。

一般过去时:
She obeyed the traffic laws. = 她遵守交通法规。
They obeyed their teacher's instructions. = 他们服从了老师的指示。

现在完成时:
I have always obeyed the rules. = 我一直遵守规则。
She has obeyed her conscience. = 她一直遵循她的良心。

过去完成时:
He had obeyed his father's wishes. = 他已经服从了他父亲的愿望。
They had obeyed the orders before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经服从了命令。

将来时:
I will obey the court order. = 我将服从法院的命令。
We will obey the new regulations. = 我们将遵守新的规章制度。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would obey the rules. = 如果我是你，我会遵守规则。
I wish he would obey his doctor's advice. = 我希望他会听从医生的建议。

被动语态:
The law must be obeyed. = 法律必须遵守。
His orders were obeyed immediately. = 他的命令立即得到了执行。

疑问句:
Will you obey my instructions? = 你会服从我的指示吗？
Does he always obey the rules? = 他总是遵守规则吗？

否定句:
I will not obey such an unreasonable request. = 我不会服从如此不合理的要求。
He didn't obey the captain's orders. = 他没有服从船长的命令。


PHRASE->
obey the law = 遵守法律
obey orders = 服从命令
obey rules = 遵守规则
obey instructions = 服从指示
obey regulations = 遵守规章


词源:
From Old French obeir, from Latin obedire, from ob- "toward, against" + audire "to hear, listen," hence "to listen to," "to heed," "to obey."
