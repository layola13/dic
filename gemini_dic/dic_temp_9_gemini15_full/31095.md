champion:/ˈtʃæmpiən/| n., v.|冠军，拥护者；支持，拥护| a person who has won a competition, especially in sports |例句：He is the champion of the world. 他是世界冠军。  He championed the cause of the poor. 他拥护穷人的事业。|近义词：winner, advocate, supporter|反义词：loser, opponent


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He champions the rights of the underprivileged. = 他拥护弱势群体的权利。
She champions a healthy lifestyle. = 她提倡健康的生活方式。


一般过去时:
He championed the cause of environmental protection. = 他曾大力支持环境保护事业。
They championed the underdog team. = 他们支持那支弱势球队。


现在完成时:
She has championed women's rights for many years. = 她多年来一直为妇女权益奔走呼吁。
He has championed this cause since his college days. = 从大学时代起，他就一直支持这一事业。


过去完成时:
Before the competition, he had championed many local events. = 在比赛之前，他已经获得过许多地方赛事的冠军。
They had championed the idea for years before it was finally adopted. = 在这个想法最终被采纳之前，他们已经为之奔走呼吁多年了。


将来时:
She will champion the cause of education reform. = 她将为教育改革事业而奋斗。
They will champion the new technology at the conference. = 他们将在会议上大力推广这项新技术。


虚拟语气:
If he were to champion this cause, he would gain much support. = 如果他支持这项事业，他会获得很多支持。
I wish she had championed the idea earlier. = 我希望她早点支持这个想法。


被动语态:
The cause was championed by many influential figures. = 这项事业得到了许多有影响力的人物的大力支持。
His work was championed by critics. = 他的作品受到评论家们的好评。


疑问句:
Who will champion the team in the next game? = 下场比赛谁将代表这个队参赛？
Does he champion any particular political party? = 他支持哪个政党？


否定句:
He did not champion the losing team. = 他不支持输的球队。
She does not champion violence as a means of resolving conflict. = 她不支持暴力作为解决冲突的手段。


PHRASE->

champion of: …的拥护者/冠军
world champion: 世界冠军
champion for: 为…而斗争
champion against: 反对…而斗争
to champion a cause: 支持一项事业
