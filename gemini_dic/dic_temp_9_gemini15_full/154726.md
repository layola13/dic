perspire:/pərˈspaɪər/ | v. | 出汗，冒汗 | To secrete sweat through the pores of the skin.  This is a physiological response to heat, exertion, or stress.  |例句：He perspired profusely after the run. 他跑步后汗流浃背。

例句：I perspire easily in hot weather. 我在热天很容易出汗。


近义词：sweat, sweat profusely

反义词：None (antonyms describe the absence of perspiration, not the act itself)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perspires heavily during exercise. = 他运动时汗流浃背。
She perspires easily in the heat. = 她热天很容易出汗。


一般过去时:
I perspired a lot during the marathon. = 我在马拉松比赛中出了很多汗。
They perspired profusely in the sauna. = 他们在桑拿房里汗流浃背。


现在完成时:
He has perspired so much that his shirt is soaked. = 他出了很多汗，衬衫都湿透了。
She has perspired throughout the entire meeting. = 整个会议她都一直在出汗。


过去完成时:
I had perspired before I even began to work out. = 我还没开始锻炼就已经出汗了。
They had perspired for hours before they reached the summit. = 他们在到达山顶之前已经流汗好几个小时了。


将来时:
I will perspire heavily if I run a marathon. = 如果我跑马拉松，我会大量出汗。
She will perspire in the summer heat. = 她在夏日的酷暑中将会出汗。


虚拟语气:
If I were exercising, I would perspire. = 如果我在运动，我会出汗。
I wish I didn't perspire so easily. = 我希望我不那么容易出汗。


被动语态: (This verb is rarely used passively)  A passive construction would be awkward and unnatural.


疑问句:
Does he perspire easily? = 他容易出汗吗？
Do you perspire a lot when you exercise? = 你运动时出很多汗吗？


否定句:
He doesn't perspire much, even during strenuous activity. = 他即使在剧烈运动时也不怎么出汗。
She didn't perspire at all during the presentation. = 她在演示过程中一点汗都没出。


PHRASE->
There are few common idiomatic phrases directly using "perspire."  The word itself is relatively formal.  Phrases would usually use synonyms like "sweat."
