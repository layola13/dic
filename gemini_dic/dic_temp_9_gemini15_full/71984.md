flincher:/ˈflɪntʃər/ | n. |畏缩者，胆小鬼 | A person who flinches; someone who shrinks from difficulty or danger.  |例句：He's no flincher; he'll face any challenge. 他不是个胆小鬼；他会面对任何挑战。|例句：Don't be a flincher when things get tough.  事情艰难时不要畏缩。|近义词：coward, craven, weakling |反义词：brave, courageous, valiant
