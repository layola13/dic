non-spiritual:/ˌnɒnˈspɪrɪtʃuəl/ | adj. |非精神的，世俗的 |Lacking spiritual qualities or concerns; worldly.  It refers to things or people that are not connected to or guided by religious or spiritual beliefs. | 例句：He led a strictly non-spiritual life, focusing solely on material success. 他过着完全世俗的生活，只关注物质上的成功。

He found the non-spiritual aspects of modern life unsatisfying. 他发现现代生活的非精神方面令人不满意。

近义词：secular, material, worldly, earthly, temporal

反义词：spiritual, religious, sacred, holy

词性:形容词
