lapper:/ˈlæpər/| n. v.|轻拍者；舔食者；拍打声；(机器的)磨损部件|A person or thing that laps.  A lapper is someone or something that laps, whether it's a dog lapping up water, a machine part wearing down, or a sound of repeated gentle impacts.|例句：The dog was a happy lapper, drinking from its bowl with gusto.  这只狗兴高采烈地舔着碗里的水。 The machine's lapper needed replacing due to excessive wear.  机器的磨损部件需要更换。|近义词：licker (for licking), striker (for hitting repeatedly)|反义词：None directly applicable, depending on context.
