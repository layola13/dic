disconvenient:/ˌdɪskənˈviːniənt/| adj. | 不方便的，令人不快的 | inconvenient 的错误拼写，并非标准英语词汇 |  This word is not a standard English word; it's a misspelling of "inconvenient".  Using "inconvenient" is correct. | 近义词：inconvenient, troublesome, bothersome | 反义词：convenient, easy


USAGE->
There is no usage for "disconvenient" as it's not a real word.  The correct word is "inconvenient".  Here's the usage for "inconvenient":


一般现在时:
The meeting is inconvenient for me. = 这个会议对我来说不方便。
This schedule is inconvenient to follow. = 这个时间表很难遵守。


一般过去时:
The flight was inconveniently scheduled. = 飞机的预定时间很不方便。
The late-night delivery was inconvenient. = 这么晚送货很不方便。


现在完成时:
The changes have been inconvenient for everyone. = 这些变化对每个人都不方便。
This new system has proven inconvenient to use. = 这个新系统用起来很不方便。


过去完成时:
The earlier cancellation had been inconvenient, but we managed. = 之前的取消很不方便，但我们还是应对了。
They had found the previous arrangement inconvenient. = 他们发现之前的安排很不方便。


将来时:
The new rules will be inconvenient for small businesses. = 新规定对小型企业来说将很不方便。
The construction will be inconvenient for residents. = 施工将给居民带来不便。


虚拟语气:
If the meeting were at a more convenient time, I would attend. = 如果会议时间更方便一些，我会参加。
I wish the traffic weren't so inconvenient. = 我希望交通不要这么不方便。


被动语态:
The new system is considered inconvenient by many users. = 许多用户认为新系统不方便。
The schedule was found to be inconvenient for the participants. = 这个时间安排被认为对参与者不方便。


疑问句:
Is this time convenient for you? = 这个时间对你方便吗？
Isn't this location inconvenient? = 这个地点是不是不方便？


否定句:
The location is not inconvenient at all. = 这个地点一点也不不方便。
The arrangements were not inconvenient for the majority. = 这些安排对大多数人来说都不不方便。
