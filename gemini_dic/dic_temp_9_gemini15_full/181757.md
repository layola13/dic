relieves:/rɪˈliːvz/| vt.|解除，减轻，缓解|To make a problem, bad feeling, or difficult situation less severe.|例句：The medicine relieved my headache. 这药缓解了我的头痛。

例句：Taking a break relieved her stress.  休息一下减轻了她的压力。


近义词：alleviate, ease, mitigate|反义词：aggravate, worsen, intensify|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medication relieves my pain. = 这种药缓解我的疼痛。
This music relieves my stress. = 这音乐缓解我的压力。

一般过去时:
The rain relieved the drought. = 雨水缓解了旱情。
The news relieved her anxiety. = 这个消息减轻了她的焦虑。

现在完成时:
The new policy has relieved the burden on small businesses. = 新政策已经减轻了小型企业的负担。
This treatment has relieved my symptoms. = 这种治疗已经缓解了我的症状。

过去完成时:
She had relieved her stress by going for a walk before the meeting. = 会议前她通过散步来缓解压力。
He had relieved the pressure by delegating tasks. = 他通过委派任务来缓解压力。

将来时:
This medicine will relieve your symptoms. = 这种药会缓解你的症状。
The new system will relieve the workload. = 新系统将减轻工作量。

虚拟语气:
If I had taken the medicine earlier, it would have relieved my headache sooner. = 如果我早点吃药，头痛就会早点缓解。
I wish this rain would relieve the drought. = 我希望这场雨能缓解旱情。

被动语态:
His pain was relieved by the medication. = 他的疼痛被药物缓解了。
The pressure was relieved by the new strategy. = 压力通过新的策略得到了缓解。

疑问句:
Does this cream relieve itching? = 这款面霜能缓解瘙痒吗？
Will this treatment relieve my back pain? = 这种治疗能缓解我的背痛吗？

否定句:
The medication didn't relieve my headache. = 这药没有缓解我的头痛。
Nothing relieved his anxiety. = 什么也无法减轻他的焦虑。
