forswearers:/fɔːˈswɛərərz/| n. |发誓放弃…的人；否认者|People who have renounced or sworn off something, often something considered harmful or sinful.  They may have made a vow or promise to abstain from it. |The forswearers of alcohol found solace in community support groups.  发誓戒酒的人在社区互助小组中找到了慰藉。
The forswearers of violence advocated for peaceful conflict resolution. 否认暴力的人提倡和平解决冲突。|近义词：renouncers, abstainers, deniers|反义词：affirmers, supporters, indulgers|名词
