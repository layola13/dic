quote:/kwoʊt/| n. v.|引用；报价；引言|A direct quotation of someone's words; the act of repeating or citing something.|例句：The article contains a quote from the President. 这篇文章包含总统的引言。|例句：He quoted a passage from Shakespeare. 他引用了莎士比亚的一段话。|近义词：citation, quotation, excerpt|反义词：paraphrase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quotes Shakespeare often. = 他经常引用莎士比亚的作品。
She quotes prices for her services. = 她报出了她的服务价格。

一般过去时:
The teacher quoted a poem in class. = 老师在课堂上朗诵了一首诗。
He quoted a high price for the car. = 他为这辆车要价很高。

现在完成时:
She has quoted many famous authors in her book. = 她在书中引用了许多著名作家的作品。
They have quoted a competitive price for the project. = 他们为该项目报了一个有竞争力的价格。

过去完成时:
He had quoted the passage incorrectly. = 他错误地引用了那段话。
They had quoted a higher price before the negotiation. = 谈判前他们报过更高的价格。

将来时:
She will quote the relevant law in her argument. = 她将在她的论证中引用相关的法律。
He will quote a reasonable price for the repair. = 他会为修理报一个合理的价格。

虚拟语气:
If he quoted a lower price, I would buy it. = 如果他报低一点的价格，我会买。
I wish he had quoted the source correctly. = 我希望他正确地引用了来源。

被动语态:
The poem was quoted extensively in the essay. = 这首诗在论文中被大量引用。
The price was quoted in US dollars. = 价格以美元报价。

疑问句:
What did he quote from the book? = 他从书中引用了什么？
Can you quote me the exact words? = 你能把确切的话告诉我吗？

否定句:
He did not quote his sources accurately. = 他没有准确地引用他的来源。
She didn't quote a price for the service. = 她没有报出服务的价钱。


PHRASE->
quote someone = 引用某人的话
quote a price = 报价
quote unquote = 原话是
to quote = 引用
out of quote = 结束引用
