foremother:/ˈfɔːrˌmʌðər/| n.|女祖先，始祖女性|A female ancestor, especially one of considerable distance in the past.  The term often implies influence or importance in shaping a family, lineage, or culture.|例句：My foremothers were strong women who overcame many obstacles. 我的女祖先们是克服了许多障碍的坚强女性。

例句：She researched her family history, tracing her lineage back to its foremothers. 她研究了自己的家族史，追溯到家族的始祖女性。


近义词：ancestress, ancestress, progenitor (if female)
反义词：descendant

词性:名词
