gassing-up: /ˈɡæsɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 加油；给…加油 |  To fill a vehicle's fuel tank with gasoline or other fuel.  | 例句：I need to gas up before we hit the road. = 我需要在出发前加油。

例句：
He's gassing up his car at the station. = 他正在加油站给他的车加油。


近义词：refueling, filling up


反义词：None directly opposite, but "running out of gas" is a contextual antonym.
