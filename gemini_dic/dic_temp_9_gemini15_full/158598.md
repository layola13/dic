pleurothotonus:/ˌplʊroʊˈθoʊtənəs/| n. |胸侧强直|A state of muscular spasm causing the body to be bent or drawn to one side.  It's a severe symptom often associated with tetanus or other neurological disorders.  The condition involves involuntary contraction of the muscles on one side of the body, causing lateral bending of the trunk. |The patient presented with pleurothotonus, a telltale sign of tetanus.  这位病人出现了胸侧强直，这是破伤风的典型症状。  |近义词：opisthotonos (后弓反张), emprosthotonos (前弓反张)|反义词：muscle relaxation (肌肉放松)|名词
