equilateral:/ˈiːkwɪˌlætərəl/| adj.|等边的，等角的|Having all sides equal.  An equilateral triangle has three equal sides and three equal angles.  |例句：An equilateral triangle has three equal sides.  一个等边三角形有三条相等的边。

例句：All sides of a regular hexagon are equilateral. 正六边形的各边都相等。


近义词：equiangular, isosceles (if referring to triangles only)
反义词：scalene


词源:
From Latin *aequilateralis, from aequus ("equal") + lateralis ("of the side").
