cantering:/ˈkæntərɪŋ/| v.|轻快地跑，缓步跑|To move at a moderate pace, especially of a horse; a gait of a horse between a trot and a gallop.|例句：The horse was cantering gracefully across the field. 这匹马优雅地轻快地跑过田野。
例句：She enjoyed the feeling of cantering along the beach. 她喜欢沿着海滩轻快地跑的感觉。|近义词：trotting, galloping, loping|反义词：walking, standing
