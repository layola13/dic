siamang:/ˈsaɪæmæŋ/| n. |黑冠长臂猿|A large, black-furred gibbon found in the forests of Sumatra and the Malay Peninsula.  Known for its loud, resonant calls. |Example:  The siamang's call echoed through the rainforest. 黑冠长臂猿的叫声在雨林中回荡。  The researchers observed the social behavior of a group of siamangs. 研究人员观察了一群黑冠长臂猿的社会行为。|近义词：gibbon|反义词：无
