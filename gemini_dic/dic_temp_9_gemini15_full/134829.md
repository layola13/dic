/ˌnɒnˈmɪrəkjuələsnəsiz/ | n. |非奇迹性，非神迹性 |The absence of miraculous events or qualities; the state of being ordinary or unexceptional.  This word is extremely rare and largely theoretical, formed by combining "non-" (not), "miraculous" (supernatural), and "-ness" (state or quality), then adding a plural "-es". It describes the lack of miraculous occurrences or characteristics. |The study focused on the nonmiraculousnesses of everyday life, highlighting the mundane aspects of human experience.  |近义词：ordinariness, commonness, banality |反义词：miraculousness, extraordinariness
