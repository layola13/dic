greenlet:/ˈɡriːnˌlɛt/ | n. |小绿鸟；（计算机科学）微线程 | A small green bird; In computer science, a lightweight, cooperative multitasking unit of code.  A greenlet is similar to a thread but doesn't rely on operating system threads. Instead, it's scheduled by a library, offering fine-grained control and potentially better performance in some cases. | Example:  The geckos hunted the greenlets in the trees.  壁虎在树上猎捕小绿鸟。  | Example (computer science): We used greenlets to improve the concurrency of our web server. 我们使用微线程来提高我们的web服务器的并发性。|近义词：(bird)  small bird, fledgling  (computer science) coroutine, microthread |反义词: N/A
