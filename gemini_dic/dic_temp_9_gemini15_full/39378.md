conjunctions:/kənˈdʒʌŋkʃənz/| n.|连词|Words that connect words, phrases, clauses, or sentences.  They show the relationship between the elements they connect. Examples include and, but, or, so, yet, for, nor, etc.|例句：He likes apples and oranges. 他喜欢苹果和橘子。  She is tired, but she will finish the work. 她很累，但她会完成工作。|近义词：connectors, linkers|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat sits and sleeps. = 猫坐着睡觉。
Birds sing and fly. = 鸟儿唱歌飞翔。

一般过去时:
He walked and talked. = 他走着说话。
She ate and slept. = 她吃饱了就睡。

现在完成时:
I have read and understood. = 我已经阅读并理解了。
They have finished and left. = 他们已经完成并离开了。

过去完成时:
She had eaten and drunk. = 她已经吃喝过了。
He had studied and practiced. = 他已经学习和练习过了。

将来时:
We will sing and dance. = 我们将唱歌跳舞。
They will study and work hard. = 他们将努力学习和工作。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would buy and sell. = 如果我有钱，我会买卖。
If she were here, she would help and support. = 如果她在，她会帮助和支持。

被动语态:
The cake was baked and decorated. = 蛋糕被烘焙和装饰了。
The report was written and submitted. = 报告被撰写并提交了。

疑问句:
Is it raining or snowing? = 是在下雨还是在下雪？
Do you like tea or coffee? = 你喜欢茶还是咖啡？


否定句:
He doesn't eat meat nor fish. = 他不吃肉也不吃鱼。
She neither sings nor dances. = 她既不唱歌也不跳舞。
