falling-away: /ˈfɔːlɪŋ əˈweɪ/ | adj. n. | 衰落；坍塌；逐渐消失 |  Describes something that is declining, collapsing, or gradually disappearing. It can be used as an adjective modifying a noun or as a noun referring to the act or process of decline.  |

例句：
The falling-away of support for the government was alarming.  = 政府支持率的下降令人担忧。

The building showed signs of a serious falling-away in its structure. = 这座建筑物显示出其结构严重坍塌的迹象。


近义词：decline, deterioration, collapse, disintegration, fading, depletion

反义词：growth, increase, improvement, strengthening, resurgence


词性：Adjective, Noun
