mobilized:/ˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/ /ˈməʊbəlaɪzd/ | vt. |动员；调动；组织；促使…活动|To organize and prepare (something, such as troops or resources) for action or use; to rouse or excite to action or energetic activity.|例句：The army was mobilized to defend the country.  军队被动员起来保卫国家。

The government mobilized resources to fight the pandemic. 政府动员资源来抗击疫情。


近义词：muster, rally, activate, marshal|反义词：demobilize, disband

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The general mobilizes his troops. = 将军动员他的军队。
She mobilizes her energy for the task. = 她为这项任务集中精力。


一般过去时:
They mobilized quickly in response to the crisis. = 他们对危机迅速作出了反应。
The company mobilized its marketing team. = 公司动员了其市场营销团队。


现在完成时:
The charity has mobilized significant funds. = 该慈善机构已筹集了大量资金。

过去完成时:

The protestors had mobilized a strong response.  抗议者们已经组织了强烈的回应。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
