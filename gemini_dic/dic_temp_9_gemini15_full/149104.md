oxfly: /ˈɒksflaɪ/| n. |牛虻|A large, robust fly, typically with a stout body and piercing mouthparts, that bites livestock and sometimes humans.  Oxflies are often found near water and are known for their aggressive biting behavior. They belong to the family Tabanidae.  |例句：The cattle were tormented by swarms of oxflies. 牛群被成群的牛虻折磨得痛苦不堪。

例句：Several oxflies landed on my arm and bit me repeatedly.好几只牛虻落在我胳膊上，反复叮咬我。


近义词：horsefly, clegs, tabanid


反义词：无


词性:名词
