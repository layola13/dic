commissions:/kəˈmɪʃənz/| n.|佣金；委员会；委任；授权 |  The payment someone receives for completing a task, especially a sale; a group of people authorized to perform a task; the act of giving someone authority to do something. | 例句：She earns a commission on every sale she makes. 她每卖出一件商品都会赚取佣金。  | 例句：The committee is reviewing the proposal. 委员会正在审查该提案。 | 近义词：fees, percentages, board, delegation | 反义词：salary(固定工资), retainers(预付酬劳)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The salesperson receives a commission on each sale. = 销售员每卖出一件商品都会收到佣金。
The commission meets every month. = 委员会每月开会一次。


一般过去时:
The artist received a large commission for the painting. = 艺术家因这幅画获得了巨额佣金。
The government formed a commission to investigate the accident. = 政府成立了一个委员会来调查事故。


现在完成时:
She has earned a significant amount of commission this year. = 她今年已经赚取了大量的佣金。
The committee has already submitted its report. = 委员会已经提交了报告。


过去完成时:
They had already paid the commission before the project started. = 项目开始前他们已经支付了佣金。
The commission had completed its investigation before the public hearing. = 公众听证会之前，委员会已经完成了调查。


将来时:
He will receive a commission for his work on the film. = 他将因在电影中的工作而获得佣金。
The government will appoint a new commission to study the issue. = 政府将任命一个新的委员会来研究这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If I were to receive a larger commission, I would work harder. = 如果我能拿到更高的佣金，我会更努力工作。
If the commission had been more thorough, the outcome would have been different. = 如果委员会更彻底一些，结果就会不同。


被动语态:
The painting was commissioned by a wealthy collector. = 这幅画是由一位富有的收藏家委托创作的。
A new commission was formed to address the environmental concerns. = 成立了一个新的委员会来解决环境问题。


疑问句:
What commission percentage do you receive? = 你能拿到多少佣金比例？
What are the commission's recommendations? = 委员会的建议是什么？


否定句:
He didn't receive a commission for that job. = 他那份工作没有拿到佣金。
The committee did not approve the proposal. = 委员会没有批准该提案。
