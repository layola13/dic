gunpoint:/ˈɡʌnˌpɔɪnt/| n. |枪口；在枪口下|At gunpoint means under threat of a gun.  It describes a situation where someone is forced to do something because they are being pointed at with a gun.|例句：He was forced to hand over the money at gunpoint. 他被迫在枪口下交出钱财。

例句：The robbers held up the bank at gunpoint.  歹徒在枪口下抢劫了银行。


近义词：threat, coercion, duress|反义词：safety, security|词性:noun
