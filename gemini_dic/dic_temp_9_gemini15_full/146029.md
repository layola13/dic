overdrawing: /ˌoʊvərˈdrɑːɪŋ/ | v. |透支；超支；过度绘制 | To withdraw more money from a bank account than is available, resulting in a negative balance.  It also refers to drawing or painting more than is necessary or appropriate. |例句：He was charged a fee for overdrawing his checking account. 他因为透支支票账户而被收取费用。

例句：The artist avoided overdrawing the details, letting the viewer's imagination fill in the gaps.  艺术家避免过度绘制细节，让观众的想象力填补空白。


近义词：overspending, exceeding, exceeding the limit | 反义词：underspending, having sufficient funds

词源:
The word "overdrawing" is a combination of "over" (meaning exceeding or beyond) and "drawing" (the act of creating a picture or withdrawing money).  It reflects the action of exceeding a limit, either in financial terms or artistic representation.
