overexpands:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspændz/| v.|过度扩张；膨胀过度|To expand beyond reasonable or safe limits; to become excessively large or inflated.  |例句：The company overexpanded its operations and subsequently faced financial difficulties. 公司过度扩张业务，随后面临财务困难。

例句：The balloon overexpanded and burst. 气球过度膨胀然后爆裂了。


近义词：inflate excessively, swell excessively, extend too far|反义词：contract, shrink, deflate
