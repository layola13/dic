self-exaggerated: /ˌsɛlf ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 自夸的；夸大的；言过其实的 |  Describes something or someone that is characterized by excessive self-promotion or an inflated view of one's own importance or abilities. It implies a deliberate or unconscious overstatement of one's qualities, achievements, or significance. |

例句：

His self-exaggerated claims of success were easily debunked by his lack of concrete evidence. = 他夸大的成功说法很容易就被他缺乏确凿证据的事实驳斥了。

Her self-exaggerated portrayal of her role in the project irritated her colleagues. = 她对自己在项目中作用的夸大描述激怒了她的同事。


近义词：boastful, vainglorious, pompous, inflated, overblown, self-important

反义词：modest, humble, understated, self-deprecating


词性：形容词
