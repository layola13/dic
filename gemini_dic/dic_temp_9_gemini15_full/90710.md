humid:/ˈhjuːmɪd/| adj.|潮湿的，湿润的|Containing or characterized by a high level of water vapor in the air.  It often feels hot and uncomfortable.|例句：The humid air made it difficult to breathe.  潮湿的空气让人难以呼吸。

例句：The humid climate of the tropics is not for everyone. 热带潮湿的气候并不适合每一个人。


近义词：damp, moist, muggy|反义词：dry, arid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The air is humid today. = 今天空气潮湿。
The climate here is usually humid. = 这里的气候通常潮湿。


一般过去时:
It was humid yesterday. = 昨天很潮湿。
The room felt humid after the shower. = 淋浴后房间感觉很潮湿。


现在完成时:
It has been humid all week. = 整周都闷热潮湿。
The weather has become increasingly humid lately. = 最近天气变得越来越潮湿。


过去完成时:
It had been humid for days before the rain came. = 下雨前已经潮湿了好几天了。
They had never experienced such humid weather before. = 他们以前从未经历过如此潮湿的天气。


将来时:
It will be humid tomorrow. = 明天会很潮湿。
The forecast predicts humid conditions next week. = 预报下周天气潮湿。


虚拟语气:
If it weren't so humid, we would go for a walk. = 如果天气不那么潮湿，我们就去散步了。
I wish it weren't so humid in here. = 我希望这里不那么潮湿。


被动语态:
The air is felt to be humid. = 空气感觉潮湿。
The room was made humid by the steam. = 蒸汽使房间变得潮湿。


疑问句:
Is it humid outside? = 外面潮湿吗？
Does the humid weather bother you? = 潮湿的天气会困扰你吗？


否定句:
The air is not humid today. = 今天空气不潮湿。
The desert climate is not humid. = 沙漠气候不潮湿。

词源:
From Latin *humidus, from humus "earth, ground".  The connection is that the earth is often moist.
