reargued: /ˌriˈɑːrɡjuːd/ | v. | 再辩论，重新辩论 | To argue again or to present an argument again, often after a previous argument or decision.  | The Supreme Court reargued the case after receiving new evidence.  最高法院在收到新的证据后重新审理了此案。 The lawyer reargued his client's innocence. 律师再次为其当事人的清白辩护。 |  reargue, reconsider, review |  accept, concede | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reargue the points every meeting. = 他们每次会议都重新争论这些要点。
He reargued his case with the judge. = 他向法官重新陈述了他的案情。

一般过去时:
The council reargued the budget last night. = 理事会昨晚重新讨论了预算。
She reargued her position to the committee. = 她向委员会重新阐述了自己的立场。

现在完成时:
They have reargued the contract terms several times. = 他们已经多次重新协商合同条款。
The parties have reargued the matter in court. = 各方已在法庭上重新争论此事。

过去完成时:
After they had reargued the details, they finally reached an agreement. = 在他们重新讨论细节之后，他们最终达成了一致。
He had reargued the case before the appellate court. = 他曾在上诉法院重新辩论过此案。

将来时:
We will reargue the proposal at the next meeting. = 我们将在下次会议上重新讨论这项提案。
The lawyers will reargue the case before the judge. = 律师们将在法官面前重新辩论此案。

虚拟语气:
If they had reargued their case more effectively, they might have won. = 如果他们更有效地重新陈述了他们的案情，他们也许会赢。
I wish they had reargued the points more clearly. = 我希望他们能更清楚地重新阐述这些要点。

被动语态:
The case was reargued last week. = 此案上周重新进行了辩论。

疑问句:

否定句:
