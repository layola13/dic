filigreeing: /ˌfɪlɪˈɡriːɪŋ/ | v. |细工制作；镂空雕刻| The process of creating delicate, intricate designs, often in metal, by soldering together fine wires or threads.  It involves a high level of skill and precision. |例句：The jeweler spent weeks filigreeing the delicate silver pendant.  珠宝匠花了数周时间制作精细的银质吊坠。

The artisan meticulously filigreeing the gold bracelet showed incredible patience.  工匠细致地制作金手镯，展现出令人难以置信的耐心。


近义词：engraving, embossing, chasing,  inlaying


反义词：粗制滥造 (粗糙的制作方法没有对应的单一英文单词，需用短语表达，例如: crude workmanship)


词性：动词
