detainees:/ˌdiːteɪˈniːz/| n. |被拘留者，被羁押者 |People who are held in custody, often by law enforcement or military authorities,  but who have not yet been formally charged with a crime.  They are being held for questioning, investigation, or other reasons. |例句：The human rights organization is concerned about the treatment of detainees in the prison. 人权组织关注监狱中被拘留者的待遇。

例句：Reports suggest that the detainees were subjected to inhumane conditions. 报告显示被拘留者遭受了不人道的待遇。


近义词：prisoners, captives, inmates, suspects |反义词：free people, released individuals |词性：名词
