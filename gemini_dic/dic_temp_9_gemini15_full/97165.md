infirm:/ɪnˈfɜːrm/| adj.|虚弱的；无效的；意志薄弱的|Weak or feeble; lacking physical strength or vigor; not valid or legally sound; lacking firmness of purpose or will.  |例句：His infirm body could barely support his weight. 他的虚弱的身体几乎无法支撑他的体重。

例句：The court declared the contract infirm due to a lack of proper signature. 法院宣布该合同因缺乏适当的签名而无效。


近义词：weak, feeble, frail, delicate, invalid, shaky, powerless


反义词：strong, robust, sturdy, valid, powerful, firm


词性:形容词
