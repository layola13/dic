disputed:/dɪˈspjuːtɪd/| adj.|有争议的，有异议的|  Relating to or characterized by disagreement or argument; contested.  |例句：The border between the two countries is a disputed territory.  两国之间的边界是一个有争议的地区。

例句：The authorship of the painting is still disputed among art historians. 这幅画的作者在艺术史学家之间仍然存在争议。


近义词：controversial, contested, debated, questioned| 反义词：uncontested, undisputed, agreed-upon| 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ownership of the land is disputed. 这块土地的所有权存在争议。
They dispute the findings of the report. 他们对报告的调查结果提出异议。


一般过去时:
The election results were disputed by the opposition party. 选举结果遭到反对党的质疑。
He disputed her claims vigorously. 他强烈反驳了她的说法。


现在完成时:
The treaty has been disputed since its inception. 这项条约自签订以来一直存在争议。
Scientists have disputed the validity of the experiment. 科学家们对实验的有效性提出了质疑。


过去完成时:
The agreement had been disputed for months before it was finally resolved.  这项协议在最终解决之前已经争论了几个月。
They had disputed the terms of the contract before signing. 他们在签署合同之前就已经对合同条款提出了异议。


将来时:
The court will dispute the evidence presented by the prosecution. 法院将对检方提出的证据进行质疑。
They will likely dispute the decision. 他们很可能会对这个决定提出异议。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, the claim would not be disputed. 如果证据更充分，就不会有人质疑这一说法。
I wish the facts were not so disputed. 我希望事实不会如此有争议。


被动语态:
The results of the study are being disputed by several researchers. 这项研究的结果正在受到几位研究人员的质疑。
The validity of the contract was disputed by the court. 合同的有效性受到了法院的质疑。


疑问句:
Is the date of the event disputed? 事件的日期有争议吗？
Are the findings of this research widely disputed? 这项研究的结果是否受到了广泛质疑？


否定句:
The evidence is not disputed. 证据没有争议。
The agreement was not disputed by either party. 双方都没有对协议提出异议。


PHRASE->

bitterly disputed:  尖锐争论的
long-disputed: 长期争论的
widely disputed: 广泛争议的
