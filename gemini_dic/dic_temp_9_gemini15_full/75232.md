frolicked:/ˈfrɒlɪkt/| v.|嬉戏，玩耍|To play and move about in a lively and cheerful way.|例句：The children frolicked on the beach. 孩子们在海滩上嬉戏。|例句：The lambs frolicked in the green pasture.  小羊在绿色的牧场上嬉戏。|近义词：play, romp, gambol, cavort|反义词：mope, sulk
