indiscrete:/ɪnˈdɪskriːt/| adj.|不谨慎的；不适当的；不分场合的|Lacking discretion; imprudent in speech or action.  It implies a lack of judgment or tact, often resulting in revealing private or sensitive information. |例句：It was indiscrete of him to mention her salary in front of everyone. 他在大家面前提到她的薪水，真是不谨慎。

例句：Her indiscrete remarks caused a great deal of embarrassment. 她不经意的话语引起了极大的尴尬。


近义词：imprudent, tactless, careless, thoughtless, indiscreet

反义词：discreet, prudent, tactful, careful, thoughtful
