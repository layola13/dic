sideswipers:/ˈsaɪdswaɪpərz/ | n. |侧面碰撞；擦边球；意外事件 |A sideswiper is a collision in which a vehicle hits the side of another vehicle.  It can also refer to something that is done indirectly or unexpectedly, causing damage or disruption.  |例句：The accident was a sideswiper, with minimal damage to both cars. 这起事故是一次侧面碰撞，两辆车受损程度很小。


The sudden job cuts felt like a sideswiper to the whole team. 突然的裁员对整个团队来说如同晴天霹雳。|近义词：fender bender, glancing blow, brush, unexpected event |反义词：head-on collision, direct hit
