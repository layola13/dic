naturize:/ˈneɪtʃəraɪz/| vt.|使自然化；使具有自然性|To make something more natural or to give something the qualities of nature.  It often implies a process of making something artificial seem more natural or integrating something into a natural environment. |例句：The park service is working to naturize the landscape after years of industrial use. 公园管理部门正在努力使经过多年工业用途的景观恢复自然。  The company aims to naturize its production processes by using sustainable materials.该公司旨在通过使用可持续材料使生产过程更自然化。|近义词：naturalize,  make natural|反义词：artificialize,  unnaturalize|动词
