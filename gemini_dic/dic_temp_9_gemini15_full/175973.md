rattiest:/ˈrætiəst/| adj.|最肮脏的；最破烂的；最像老鼠的|Most dirty, dilapidated, or resembling a rat.  It is the superlative form of "ratty".|例句：The rattiest part of town was avoided by everyone. 城里最肮脏的地方都被大家避开了。
The rattiest old sweater I own is still warm. 我拥有的最破旧的旧毛衣仍然很暖和。|近义词：filthy, dilapidated, shabby, run-down|反义词：clean, pristine, new, immaculate
