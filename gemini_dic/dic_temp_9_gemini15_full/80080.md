goatee:/ɡoʊˈtiː/| n. |山羊胡 |A goatee is a type of facial hair for men, consisting of hair grown on the chin, often with the cheeks shaved.  It is typically trimmed neatly and is often pointed or somewhat round in shape. |He sports a neatly trimmed goatee. 他留着修剪整齐的山羊胡。|beard, chin beard |mustache, clean-shaven
