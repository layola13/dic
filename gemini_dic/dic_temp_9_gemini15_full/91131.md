hybridisms:/ˈhaɪbrɪdɪzəm/| n.|杂交；混合；混杂|The blending of different elements or styles, often resulting in something new and unique.  Hybridisms are common in language, where words or grammatical structures from different languages are combined.  It can also refer to the mixing of different breeds of animals or plants.|例句：The novel showcases a fascinating hybridism of science fiction and historical fiction. 这部小说展现了科幻小说和历史小说的迷人融合。|例句：The garden displayed a remarkable hybridism of plant species from different continents. 花园展示了来自不同大陆的植物物种的显著混合。|近义词：mixture, blend, fusion, amalgamation|反义词：purity, uniformity, homogeneity
