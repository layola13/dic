mulberries:/ˈmʌlbəriːz/ | n. |桑葚，桑果 | The sweet, dark-colored berries of the mulberry tree. Mulberries are often used in jams, pies, and wines.  They are a good source of antioxidants. |例句：The children enjoyed picking mulberries from the bushes. 孩子们喜欢从灌木丛中采摘桑葚。

例句：We made a delicious mulberry pie using freshly picked berries. 我们用刚采摘的新鲜桑葚做了一个美味的桑葚派。


近义词：blackberries (in some contexts)


反义词：None directly applicable.


词性：名词(复数)


Etymology: From Old English *mōlberie, from  *mōl (mulberry tree) + berie (berry). The word ultimately traces back to Latin *mōrus (mulberry).
