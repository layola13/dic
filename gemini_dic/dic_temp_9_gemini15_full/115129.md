mango:/ˈmæŋɡoʊ/| n. |芒果|A tropical fruit with juicy, sweet flesh and a large, hard stone.  Mangoes are grown in many tropical and subtropical regions and come in a variety of colors and sizes.|例句：I bought a delicious mango at the market. 我在市场上买了一个美味的芒果。
例句：Mangoes are a good source of vitamins. 芒果是维生素的良好来源。|近义词：fruit|反义词:None
