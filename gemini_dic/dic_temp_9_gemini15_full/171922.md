pupillage:/ˈpjuːpɪlɪdʒ/| n. |实习期；学徒期；受训期|The period of time a newly qualified lawyer spends working under the supervision of an experienced lawyer to gain practical experience.  It's a crucial stage in their professional development.|例句：Her pupillage was spent at a prestigious law firm in London. = 她的实习期是在伦敦一家著名的律师事务所度过的。|例句：After completing his pupillage, he was offered a permanent position. = 完成实习期后，他获得了一份正式职位。|近义词：apprenticeship, traineeship, probationary period|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be established practice or full qualification.
