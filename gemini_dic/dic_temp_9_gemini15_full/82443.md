grumblers:/ˈɡrʌmblərz/| n. |抱怨者，牢骚者|People who habitually complain or grumble.  They often express dissatisfaction or resentment. |例句：The grumblers in the office constantly complain about the workload.  办公室里的抱怨者不断抱怨工作量太大。

例句：Despite the initial grumblings, the team eventually completed the project successfully. 尽管一开始有抱怨，团队最终还是成功完成了项目。


近义词：complainer, whiner, malcontent

反义词：praiser, complimenter


词性: 名词
