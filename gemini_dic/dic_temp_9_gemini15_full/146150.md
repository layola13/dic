overengineering: /ˌoʊvərˈɛndʒɪnɪərɪŋ/ | n. |过度工程设计；设计过度；功能过剩|The act of designing a product or system with more complexity or features than are necessary, often resulting in increased cost, reduced efficiency, and increased risk of failure.  It's characterized by unnecessary complexity, excessive features, and an overreliance on advanced technologies.|例句：The software project suffered from overengineering, resulting in delays and cost overruns.  软件项目因过度设计而导致延误和成本超支。

The bridge's design was a classic case of overengineering; it was far stronger than necessary.|这座桥的设计是过度工程的典型案例；它远比必要的要坚固得多。|近义词：overcomplication, overkill, redundancy|反义词：simplicity, minimalism, streamlining|名词
