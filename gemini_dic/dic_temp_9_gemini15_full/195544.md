semiphenomenal: /ˌsɛmiˌfɛnɑːmɪnəl/ | adj. | 半现象的；部分现象的 |  Describing something that is partly phenomenal or based on observable phenomena, but also incorporates other elements, often theoretical or abstract. It suggests a blend of empirical observation and theoretical interpretation.  | The semiphenomenal model of consciousness attempts to bridge the gap between subjective experience and objective neural activity.  | 近义词: partly phenomenal, partially observable | 反义词: purely phenomenal, purely theoretical
