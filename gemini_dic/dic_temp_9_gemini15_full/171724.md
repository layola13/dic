pump:/pʌmp/| n. vt.|泵；抽水机；抽吸；打气；灌注|A pump is a mechanical device used to move liquids, gases, or other materials.  It can be used to pump water, air, or other fluids.|例句：The farmer used a pump to water his crops.  这位农民用泵来灌溉庄稼。
例句：He pumped up his bicycle tires. 他给自行车轮胎打气。|近义词：sieve, filter|反义词: none.


USAGE->

He pumps water from the well. 他从井里抽水。
The heart pumps blood throughout the body. 心脏把血液泵送到全身。
