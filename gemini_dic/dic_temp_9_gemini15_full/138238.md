nostalgically:/nɒˈstældʒɪkli/ | adj. |怀旧地，渴望过去地 | feeling or expressing nostalgia.  The adjective form of "nostalgia," describing something done or felt with a longing for the past. |例句：She smiled nostalgically as she looked at the old photographs. 她看着旧照片，怀旧地笑了。

例句：He spoke nostalgically of his childhood summers spent at the seaside. 他怀旧地谈起童年在海边度过的夏天。


近义词：longingly, yearningly, wistfully


反义词：unfeelingly, apathetically
