exultations:/ˌɛkˌsʌlˈteɪʃənz/| n. |欢欣鼓舞；狂喜；庆祝|The feeling of great happiness and triumph.  It often involves outward expressions of joy. |例句：The team's exultations after their victory were deafening.  球队获胜后的欢呼声震耳欲聋。|例句：Her exultations over her promotion were shared by her colleagues. 她晋升后的喜悦之情与同事们分享。|近义词：jubilations, rejoicings, celebrations|反义词：despair, dejection, gloom|名词
