overmixing:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪksɪŋ/ | n. |过度混合|The act of mixing something, such as a batter or dough, for too long, resulting in a less desirable texture or outcome.  Overmixing can lead to tough bread, rubbery cookies, or a dense cake.|Example: Overmixing the batter resulted in tough pancakes.  过度混合面糊导致煎饼变硬。|近义词：overbeating, excessive mixing|反义词：undermixing
