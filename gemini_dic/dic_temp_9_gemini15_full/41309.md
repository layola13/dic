cordwainer:/ˈkɔːrdweɪnər/| n. |制鞋匠|A cordwainer is a person who makes and repairs shoes, especially fine leather shoes.  The term derives from the medieval French "cordouan," referring to the high-quality leather from Córdoba, Spain. |The old cordwainer crafted exquisite shoes using traditional methods.  古老的制鞋匠使用传统方法制作精美的鞋子。| shoemaker, cobbler |  (无明确反义词，可考虑与“shoemaker”的反义词对应，例如，那些破坏鞋子的人)|名词
