dicing:/ˈdaɪsɪŋ/| v.|切成丁；掷骰子|The act of cutting something into small cubes or the act of throwing dice.  |例句：She was dicing the vegetables for the soup. 她正在把蔬菜切成丁做汤。  He was dicing with his friends at the casino. 他在赌场和朋友们掷骰子。|近义词：cubing, chopping,  gambling|反义词：grinding, pulverizing
