carry-over:/ˈkæriˌoʊvər/ | n. | 结转；延续；剩余；递延 |  Something that continues from one period to the next.  It refers to something that is carried forward from one time, place, or situation to another.  This can apply to various contexts, including financial accounts, academic grades, or even ongoing projects. |例句：The company's losses from last year resulted in a significant carry-over into the current fiscal year.  去年公司的亏损导致本财政年度出现重大结转。

The professor decided to carry over the student's excellent project into the next semester. 教授决定将学生的优秀项目延续到下个学期。|近义词：continuation, transfer, remainder, residue |反义词：conclusion, termination, end


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The surplus from last quarter carries over to this one. = 上季度的盈余结转到本季度。
This project carries over into next year's budget. = 这个项目延续到明年的预算中。


一般过去时:
The company carried over its losses from the previous year. = 公司将前一年的亏损结转。
They carried over the unused vacation days to the next year. = 他们将未使用的休假天数结转到下一年。


现在完成时:
The government has carried over several policies from the previous administration. = 政府已将前任政府的几项政策延续下来。
We have carried over the unfinished tasks to the next project phase. = 我们已将未完成的任务延续到项目的下一阶段。


过去完成时:
The team had carried over the design elements from their previous project. = 该团队已将他们先前项目的设计元素延续下来。
Before the merger, they had carried over a significant amount of debt. = 在合并之前，他们已结转了大量的债务。


将来时:
The accounting department will carry over the balance to the next financial year. = 会计部门将把余额结转到下一个财政年度。
We will carry over the research findings to the next stage of the study. = 我们将把研究结果延续到研究的下一阶段。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would carry over the extra work. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会把额外的工作延续下去。
I wish we could carry over the positive momentum into the new campaign. = 我希望我们能够将积极的势头延续到新的活动中。


被动语态:
The excess inventory was carried over to the next season. = 多余的库存被结转到下一季。
The unfinished business will be carried over to the next meeting. = 未完成的事务将被延续到下次会议。


疑问句:
Will the remaining funds be carried over? = 剩余资金会被结转吗？
Does this policy carry over to other departments? = 这项政策适用于其他部门吗？


否定句:
The company did not carry over any surplus from the previous year. = 公司没有结转前一年的任何盈余。
We will not carry over any incomplete assignments. = 我们不会延续任何未完成的任务。
