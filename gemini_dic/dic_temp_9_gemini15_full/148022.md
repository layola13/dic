overroast:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊst/| vt.|烤焦；过度烘烤|To cook (something, especially meat) for too long, resulting in dryness and sometimes burning.|例句：I overroasted the chicken; it's dry and tough.我把鸡肉烤焦了；它又干又硬。例句：Don't overroast the vegetables, or they'll lose their nutrients. 不要把蔬菜烤焦了，否则它们会失去营养。|近义词：burn, scorch, char|反义词：undercook
