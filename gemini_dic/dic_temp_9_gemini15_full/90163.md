hothouses:/ˈhɑːthaʊzɪz/| n.|温室；催熟环境|A hothouse is a greenhouse, typically made of glass or plastic, used for growing plants, especially delicate or out-of-season ones, in a controlled environment.|例句：The gardener carefully tended the orchids in the hothouse. 园丁细心地照料温室里的兰花。|例句：The political climate was described as a hothouse of ambition and intrigue.  政治气候被形容为一个充满野心和阴谋的温室。|近义词：greenhouse, conservatory|反义词：open field, natural environment|名词
