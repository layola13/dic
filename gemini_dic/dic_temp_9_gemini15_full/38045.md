comports:/kəmˈpɔːrts/ | v. | 举止；相符；符合；容纳 | To behave in a particular way; to be in agreement or harmony; to contain or accommodate. |  例句：He comports himself with great dignity. 他举止非常端庄。 The new evidence comports with our theory. 新证据与我们的理论相符。 | 近义词：behaves, conforms, agrees, fits | 反义词：misbehaves, clashes, conflicts


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comports himself well in public. = 他在公众场合举止得体。
The data comports with the prediction. = 数据与预测相符。

一般过去时:
She comported herself gracefully at the ceremony. = 她在仪式上举止优雅。
His actions did not comport with his words. = 他的行为与他的话语不符。

现在完成时:
The results have comported with our expectations. = 结果与我们的预期相符。
How has he comported himself since the incident? = 自那件事发生后，他一直如何表现？

过去完成时:
He had comported himself admirably throughout the ordeal. = 在整个磨难中，他一直表现得令人钦佩。
The evidence had comported with the initial hypothesis. = 证据与最初的假设相符。


将来时:
She will comport herself professionally in the meeting. = 她将在会议上表现得专业。
The findings will likely comport with existing research. = 研究结果很可能与现有研究相符。


虚拟语气:
If he comported himself better, he would have more friends. = 如果他表现得更好，他就会有更多朋友。
I wish he would comport himself with more respect. = 我希望他能表现得更有礼貌。

被动语态:
His behavior was not well comported. = 他的行为举止不得体。
The new data must be carefully comported with the previous results. = 新数据必须与之前的结果仔细比对。

疑问句:
How did he comport himself at the interview? = 他在面试中表现如何？
Does this version of events comport with the facts? = 这一版本的事件与事实相符吗？

否定句:
His actions did not comport with his claims of innocence. = 他的行为与他声称的清白不符。
The witness's testimony did not comport with the physical evidence. = 目击证人的证词与物证不符。


PHRASE->
