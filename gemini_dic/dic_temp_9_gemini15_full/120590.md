mirrorball: /ˈmɪrəˌbɔːl/ | n. | 镜面球 | A disco ball; a ball covered with many small mirrors that reflects light to create a sparkling effect.  It is often used in discotheques, nightclubs, and stage productions to create a visually appealing atmosphere. | Example: The mirrorball spun, casting shimmering light across the dance floor.  镜面球旋转着，在舞池上投下闪闪发光的灯光。


The mirrorball hung from the ceiling, creating a dazzling spectacle. 镜面球悬挂在天花板上，创造出一场令人眼花缭乱的奇观。
