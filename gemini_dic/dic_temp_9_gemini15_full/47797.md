decapod:/ˈdekəpɑːd/| n. |十足类动物；十足动物|A decapod is a crustacean with ten legs, including claws.  Examples include crabs, lobsters, and shrimps.  The term refers to the order Decapoda within the class Malacostraca.|例句：Crabs, lobsters, and shrimps are all examples of decapods.  螃蟹、龙虾和虾都是十足类动物的例子。|近义词：crustacean (更广义)|反义词：无|名词
