hyperflexible: /ˌhaɪpərˈfleksəbl/ | adj. | 超柔韧的，过度灵活的 | Extremely flexible; capable of bending or flexing beyond the normal range of motion.  This term often refers to joints that can bend further than typically considered healthy or normal. |  例句：She is hyperflexible and can touch her toes easily without bending her knees. 她非常柔软，可以很容易地不用弯曲膝盖就够到脚趾。  Her hyperflexible joints made certain activities painful. 她的过度灵活的关节使某些活动变得痛苦。 | 近义词：overflexible, excessively flexible, supple, limber | 反义词：inflexible, stiff, rigid
