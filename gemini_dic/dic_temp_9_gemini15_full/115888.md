marmalade:/ˈmɑːməleɪd/| n. |橘子酱，果酱|A sweet preserve made from citrus fruits, especially oranges, and usually containing pieces of the fruit.|例句：I like to spread marmalade on my toast.我喜欢在我的吐司上涂抹橘子酱。|例句：She made a delicious homemade marmalade.她做了一种美味的自制橘子酱。|近义词：jam, preserve|反义词：无
