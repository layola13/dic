indemnified:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪd/| v. |赔偿；保护；使免受损失 | To protect (someone) against loss or damage by paying or agreeing to pay compensation. | The insurance company indemnified the homeowner for the damage caused by the fire.  保险公司赔偿房主因火灾造成的损失。| compensate, protect, secure |  harm, damage


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The insurance company indemnifies its clients against losses. = 保险公司赔偿客户的损失。
We indemnify our employees against work-related accidents. = 我们赔偿员工因工伤事故造成的损失。


一般过去时:
The company indemnified the customer for the faulty product. = 公司赔偿顾客有缺陷的产品。
They indemnified him against any future legal claims. = 他们赔偿了他未来可能面临的任何法律索赔。


现在完成时:
The government has indemnified the victims of the natural disaster. = 政府已经赔偿了自然灾害的受害者。
The company has indemnified all shareholders for their losses. = 公司已经赔偿了所有股东的损失。


过去完成时:
The court had indemnified the plaintiff before the final judgment. = 法院在最终判决前已经赔偿了原告。
The insurance company had indemnified the business for previous losses. = 保险公司已经赔偿了该企业之前的损失。


将来时:
The contract stipulates that the contractor will indemnify the client against any damages. = 合同规定承包商将赔偿客户任何损失。
The company will indemnify its employees for any medical expenses. = 公司将赔偿员工的任何医疗费用。


虚拟语气:
If the company had indemnified me, I wouldn't have sued. = 如果公司赔偿我，我就不会起诉了。
I wish the insurance company had indemnified us sooner. = 我希望保险公司早点赔偿我们。


被动语态:
The victims were indemnified by the government. = 受害者得到了政府的赔偿。
The contractor was indemnified for the unexpected costs. = 承包商获得了意外费用的赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the company indemnify us for the damaged goods? = 公司会赔偿我们损坏的货物吗？
Has the insurance company indemnified you yet? = 保险公司已经赔偿你了吗？


否定句:
The company did not indemnify the customer for the lost shipment. = 公司没有赔偿客户丢失的货物。
They were not indemnified for the damages caused by the flood. = 他们没有得到因洪水造成的损失赔偿。


PHRASE->

hold harmless and indemnify = 保护…免受损害并赔偿
indemnify against loss = 保护免受损失
indemnify for damages = 赔偿损失
indemnify and hold harmless = 赔偿并保护免受损害
to be fully indemnified = 得到充分的赔偿
