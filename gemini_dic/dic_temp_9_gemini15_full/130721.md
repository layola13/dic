nonallergic:/ˌnɒnælˈɜːrdʒɪk/ | adj. | 不过敏的，无过敏反应的 | Not causing or resulting from an allergic reaction.  This term describes a substance, person, or reaction that does not trigger an allergic response. | Example: He uses nonallergic detergent to wash his clothes. 他用无过敏性洗衣液洗衣服。  |近义词: hypoallergenic, non-reactive | 反义词: allergic


USAGE->

The new fabric is nonallergic and soft. = 这种新型面料不致敏且柔软。

现在完成时:
