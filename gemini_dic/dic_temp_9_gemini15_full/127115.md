nationals:/ˈnæʃənəlz/ | n. |国民；国家队；（尤指）美国职业棒球大联盟球队 | Nationals refers to citizens of a nation or a team representing a nation, especially in sports.  The term can also refer to things specific to a nation. |Example 1: The nationals celebrated their country's independence day.  (国民庆祝了他们国家的独立日。) |Example 2: The Chinese national team won the gold medal. (中国国家队获得了金牌。) |近义词: citizens, countrymen, team, nationals (US baseball team)|反义词: foreigners, aliens
