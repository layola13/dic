middens:/ˈmɪdən(z)/| n.|垃圾堆；粪堆；废物堆 |A midden is a refuse heap, typically an accumulation of household waste.  Archaeologists study middens to learn about the diets and lifestyles of past peoples.  |Example: The archaeologists unearthed many interesting artifacts from the ancient midden. 考古学家从古代垃圾堆中挖掘出许多有趣的文物。|近义词：rubbish heap, dump, refuse pile |反义词：cleanliness, order
