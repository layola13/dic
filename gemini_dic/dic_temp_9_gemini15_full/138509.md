now's:/naʊz/| pron.|现在|The contraction of "now is". It is used to indicate the present time or a current situation. |例句：Now's the time to act.  现在是行动的时候了。|例句：Now's not a good time to call. 现在不是打电话的好时机。|近义词：currently, presently, at present|反义词：then, later


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Now's the time to make a decision. = 现在是做出决定的时间了。
Now's a good opportunity to invest. = 现在是投资的好机会。


一般过去时:
(Now's cannot be used in past tense.  The contraction "now is" implies present tense.)


现在完成时:
Now's the time we've been waiting for. = 这是我们一直在等待的时刻。
Now's when the hard work pays off. = 现在是努力得到回报的时候了。


过去完成时:
(Now's cannot be used in past perfect tense.  The contraction "now is" implies present tense.)


将来时:
Now's when things will start to change. = 现在是事情开始改变的时候了。
Now's the moment we'll see the results. = 现在是我们看到结果的时候了。


虚拟语气:
If now's the time to act, then we should act. = 如果现在是行动的时候，那么我们就应该行动。
I wish now's the day I finally succeed. = 我希望今天是我最终成功的一天。


被动语态:
Now's the moment the decision is being made. = 现在是做出决定的时刻。
(Now's is less commonly used in passive voice constructions.)



疑问句:
Now's what? = 现在是什么？ (informal)
Now's the question, what next? = 现在的问题是，接下来做什么？


否定句:
Now's not the time to panic. = 现在不是恐慌的时候。
Now's not the appropriate moment for that. = 现在不是做那件事的合适时机。


PHRASE->
right now = 现在

as of now = 截至目前
for now = 目前
