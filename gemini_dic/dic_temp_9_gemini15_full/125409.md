multiparameter:/ˌmʌltiˈpærəmiːtər/| adj. n.|多参数的；多参数系统|Having or involving many parameters.  A multiparameter system is one that measures or controls multiple variables simultaneously.  |例句：The multiparameter monitoring system tracked heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen saturation.  多参数监测系统跟踪心率、血压和血氧饱和度。
The researcher used a multiparameter analysis to study the complex interactions within the ecosystem.  研究人员使用多参数分析来研究生态系统内的复杂相互作用。|近义词：multivariate, polyparametric|反义词：single-parameter, uniparametric
