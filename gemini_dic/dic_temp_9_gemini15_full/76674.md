galvanizing: /ˈɡæl.vən.aɪ.zɪŋ/ | v. |镀锌；激励；使震惊 | To coat (metal) with zinc to protect it from rust.  Also, to shock or excite someone into taking action. |

例句：

The factory galvanizes steel pipes to prevent corrosion. = 工厂对钢管进行镀锌以防止腐蚀。

His passionate speech galvanized the crowd into action. = 他充满激情的演讲激励了人群采取行动。


近义词：zincing, stimulating, exciting, electrifying

反义词：dampening, depressing, discouraging
