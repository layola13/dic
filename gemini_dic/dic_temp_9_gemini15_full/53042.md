diomedea:/ˌdaɪəˈmiːdiːə/ | n. |信天翁属 |The genus *Diomedea* comprises large seabirds known as albatrosses.  They are characterized by their large wingspans, graceful flight, and pelagic lifestyle.  They are found in the Southern Ocean and some species range into the North Pacific. |Example:  Several species of *Diomedea* are threatened by human activities such as longline fishing.  几种信天翁属的鸟类正面临着诸如延绳钓等人类活动的威胁。 |近义词: albatross (泛指，而非属名) |反义词: None  |词性: noun
