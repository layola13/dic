counter-order:/ˈkaʊntərˈɔːrdər/| n. |反命令；撤销命令 |An order that cancels or reverses a previous order.  It is often issued in military or emergency situations to prevent the execution of a potentially harmful or obsolete command. |例句：The general issued a counter-order, stopping the advance.将军发布了反命令，停止了前进。

例句：The captain received a counter-order to abort the mission.  船长收到了一条反命令，中止任务。


近义词：reversal, revocation, cancellation |反义词：confirmation, ratification |词性:noun
