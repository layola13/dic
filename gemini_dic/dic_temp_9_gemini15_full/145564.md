overcomplacencies:/ˌoʊvər kəmˈpleɪsənsiz/ | n. | 自满情绪；过分自信 | A feeling of excessive complacency; an excessive amount of self-satisfaction.  It often leads to negligence and a lack of preparedness.  |例句：Their overcomplacencies led to their downfall. 他们的自满导致了他们的失败。
Their years of overcomplacencies regarding safety protocols resulted in a major accident.  他们多年来对安全规程的过于自满导致了一起重大事故。|近义词：complacency, smugness, self-satisfaction |反义词：vigilance, alertness, anxiety
