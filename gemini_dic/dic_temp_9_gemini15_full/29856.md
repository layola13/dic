causator:/kɔːˈseɪtər/ | n. |致因物，原因；引起者 | The agent or thing that causes something.  A causator is the entity or event that brings about a particular effect or outcome. It's often used in philosophical discussions of causation. |Example: The impact of the asteroid was the causator of the dinosaurs' extinction.  小行星撞击是恐龙灭绝的致因物。|近义词：cause, agent, origin, source |反义词：effect, result, consequence |名词
