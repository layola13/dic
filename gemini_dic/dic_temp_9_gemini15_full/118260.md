merchandizes:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzɪz/ | v. |商品化；推销商品 | To treat (something) as a commodity to be bought and sold; to promote the sale of (goods) | 例句：The company merchandizes its products aggressively.  公司积极推销其产品。

例句：They merchandized the new technology effectively, reaching a large market share. 他们有效地将新技术商品化，获得了很大的市场份额。


近义词：commodify, market, promote, sell


反义词：decommodify


词性：动词
