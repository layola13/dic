/əkˈsɛpt/ | vt. | 接受，认可 | To receive or take something offered; to agree to something; to believe something to be true. |  He accepted the gift graciously. 他欣然接受了礼物。  She accepted his apology. 她接受了他的道歉。 | receive, admit, acknowledge | reject, refuse, decline | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He accepts all kinds of credit cards. 他接受各种信用卡。
She accepts her fate with dignity. 她以尊严的态度接受了自己的命运。

一般过去时:
I accepted the job offer yesterday. 我昨天接受了这份工作邀请。
They accepted his explanation without question. 他们毫无疑问地接受了他的解释。

现在完成时:
She has accepted the consequences of her actions. 她已经接受了她行为的后果。
He has accepted many awards for his work. 他因其作品获得了许多奖项。

过去完成时:
I had already accepted the invitation before she called.  在她打电话之前我已经接受了邀请。
They had accepted the terms of the contract before signing. 他们在签署合同之前已经接受了合同条款。

将来时:
I will accept your invitation if I am free. 如果我有空，我会接受你的邀请。
She will accept the challenge with enthusiasm. 她将以热情接受挑战。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would accept the offer. 如果我是你，我会接受这个提议。
I wish I had accepted his help earlier. 我真希望我早点接受他的帮助。

被动语态:
His proposal was accepted by the committee. 他的提案被委员会接受了。
The application was accepted with conditions.  申请在附带条件下被接受了。

疑问句:
Will you accept my invitation? 你会接受我的邀请吗？
Does she accept credit cards? 她接受信用卡吗？

否定句:
I do not accept your explanation. 我不接受你的解释。
He did not accept the responsibility for the mistake. 他没有为这个错误承担责任。
