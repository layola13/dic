embroidery:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri/ | n. | 刺绣；绣花 | The art or process of decorating fabric or other materials with needlework.  It involves stitching designs onto a surface using thread.  | Example: The tablecloth was adorned with exquisite embroidery. 这张桌布上装饰着精美的刺绣。 |近义词：needlework, needlepoint | 反义词：plain weaving, undecorated
