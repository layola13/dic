sculker:/ˈskʌlkər/| n. |潜行者，偷偷摸摸的人 |A skulker is someone who avoids work or responsibility by hiding or being secretive.  They tend to shirk their duties and responsibilities.  |例句：He was a skulker at school, always trying to avoid his classes. 他在学校是个逃课的学生，总是想逃避上课。

The teacher was disappointed by the skulkers who didn't complete their assignments. 老师对那些没有完成作业的逃课者感到失望。|近义词：shirker, slacker, malingerer |反义词：hard worker, diligent, dedicated
