nubuck:/ˈnuːbæk/| n. |磨砂皮革|A type of leather with a slightly napped or sueded finish, created by sanding the surface of the leather after tanning. It is softer and more supple than full-grain leather but less durable.  |例句：The shoes are made of high-quality nubuck leather. 这双鞋是用高质量的磨砂皮革制成的。


例句：Her handbag is made from beautiful brown nubuck. 她的手提包是用漂亮的棕色磨砂皮革制成的。
