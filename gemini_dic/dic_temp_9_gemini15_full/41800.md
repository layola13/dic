cortex:/ˈkɔːrteks/ /ˈkɔːrtɛks/| n. |皮质；大脑皮层；树皮|The cortex is the outer layer of an organ, especially the brain.  It plays a crucial role in higher-level cognitive functions such as thinking, perceiving, and remembering.|例句：The cerebral cortex is responsible for higher-level cognitive functions. 大脑皮层负责高级认知功能。|例句：The bark of the tree is actually the cortex of the trunk. 树皮实际上是树干的皮层。|近义词：rind, shell, outer layer |反义词：medulla, core
