containerized:/kənˈteɪnəraɪzd/| adj.|集装箱化的；容器化的|Relating to or using containers, especially standardized shipping containers.|例句：The port handles a large volume of containerized cargo.  这个港口处理大量的集装箱货物。
例句：The company is transitioning to a containerized infrastructure for its software deployment. 公司正在将其软件部署迁移到容器化基础设施。|近义词：packaged, modularized |反义词：unpackaged, loose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company containerizes its applications for easier deployment. = 公司将其应用程序容器化以便于部署。
This system containerizes data for efficient storage. = 此系统将数据容器化以实现高效存储。
