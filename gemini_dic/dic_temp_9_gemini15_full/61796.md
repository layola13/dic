emphasizes:/ˈɛmfəsaɪzɪz/ | vt. |强调；重视 |To give special importance or prominence to (something), to stress.  |The teacher emphasizes the importance of reading.|近义词：stress, highlight, underscore |反义词：downplay, minimize, ignore |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emphasizes the need for patience. = 他强调需要耐心。
The report emphasizes the risks involved. = 这份报告强调了所涉及的风险。

一般过去时:
She emphasized the urgency of the situation. = 她强调了情况的紧急性。
The speaker emphasized his main points. = 演讲者强调了他的要点。

现在完成时:
They have emphasized the importance of teamwork. = 他们一直强调团队合作的重要性。
We have emphasized the need for change. = 我们一直强调变革的必要性。

过去完成时:
He had emphasized the dangers of smoking before. = 他之前就强调过吸烟的危害。
She had emphasized the importance of punctuality many times. = 她多次强调过准时的重要性。

将来时:
He will emphasize the benefits of the new program. = 他将强调新项目的益处。
She will emphasize the importance of safety regulations. = 她将强调安全规定的重要性。

虚拟语气:
If he emphasized his strengths, he would get the job. = 如果他强调自己的优势，他就会得到这份工作。
I wish he emphasized the details more clearly. = 我希望他更清楚地强调细节。

被动语态:
The importance of education is emphasized in many cultures. = 在许多文化中都强调教育的重要性。
The need for reform was emphasized by the committee. = 委员会强调了改革的必要性。

疑问句:
Does she emphasize the positive aspects of her work? = 她是否强调了她工作中的积极方面？
What points does the author emphasize in this chapter? = 作者在本章中强调了哪些要点？

否定句:
He did not emphasize the negative consequences. = 他没有强调负面后果。
The study does not emphasize the long-term effects. = 这项研究没有强调长期影响。
