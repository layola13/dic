butter:/ˈbʌtər/| n.|黄油，奶油|A yellowish fatty substance made by churning cream and used as a spread or in cooking.|例句：I spread butter on my toast. 我在我的吐司上涂黄油。|例句：This cake is made with a lot of butter. 这个蛋糕是用很多黄油做的。|近义词：margarine, shortening|反义词:None.
