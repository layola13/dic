mensches:/ˈmɛnʃəs/| n. |好人，正直的人，靠谱的人 |A mensch is a person of integrity and kindness; someone who is good and decent.  The term carries a strong connotation of moral excellence and trustworthiness.  It's often used to describe someone who is genuinely good-hearted and reliable. |He's a real mensch; always willing to lend a hand.|近义词：good person, decent person, honorable person, upright person |反义词：scoundrel, villain, knave

USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.

Example Sentences:

*   She's a true mensch, always looking out for others.  = 她是个真正的好人，总是为他人着想。
*   He acted like a mensch, taking responsibility for his mistakes. = 他像个好人一样，对自己的错误承担责任。


PHRASE->
This word is not typically used in common phrases in the same way as common English words.  Its usage is primarily as a standalone noun.
