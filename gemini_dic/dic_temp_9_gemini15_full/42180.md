coulda:/ˈkʊdə/| v.|可能，也许|Contraction of "could have," indicating a past possibility or a missed opportunity. It is primarily used in informal speech and writing.|例句：I coulda gone to the party, but I was tired. 我本来可以去派对的，但我累了。|例句：She coulda won the race, but she fell. 她本来可以赢得比赛的，但她摔倒了。|近义词：might have, should have|反义词:must have, had to
