functioning: /ˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/ | adj. |运作的；起作用的；功能正常的 |  Describes something that is operating or working as intended.  It emphasizes the state of being active and effective. |例句：The machine is functioning perfectly. 这台机器运转良好。

The company is functioning at a high level. 这家公司运作效率很高。


近义词：operating, working, running, active | 反义词：broken, malfunctioning, inactive, defunct


词源:  From the verb "function," ultimately derived from the Latin "fungi" meaning "to perform," "to discharge," or "to fulfill."  The "-ing" suffix creates the present participle, used here adjectivally.
