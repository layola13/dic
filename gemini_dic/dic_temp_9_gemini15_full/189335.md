saffrony:/ˈsæfrəni/| adj.|藏红花的；番红花的；橙黄色的|Having the color of saffron; orange-yellow.  The word refers to the color of saffron, a spice derived from the Crocus sativus flower.  |例句：The saffrony sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues.  橙黄色的日落给天空涂抹上鲜艳的色彩。 The cake was decorated with saffrony icing. 蛋糕装饰着番红花糖霜。|近义词：orange, yellowish-orange, crocus-colored|反义词：blue, green
