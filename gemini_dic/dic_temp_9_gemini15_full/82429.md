gruffer:/ˈɡrʌfər/| adj.|粗暴的，粗鲁的，脾气暴躁的|More abrupt or rude in manner.  A gruffer tone implies a harsher, less polite way of speaking or behaving.  It suggests a lack of smoothness or gentleness in one's demeanor.|例句：He spoke in a gruffer voice than usual. 他说话的声音比平时粗暴。

例句：The usually gentle old man became gruffer in his old age. 通常温和的老年人在他年老时变得脾气暴躁了。

近义词：surly, brusque, curt, rough, harsh|反义词：gentle, polite, kind, courteous, amiable
