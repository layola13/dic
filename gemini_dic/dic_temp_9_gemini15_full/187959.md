rotovators:/ˈroʊtəveɪtərz/| n.|旋转耕耘机|A rotovator is a type of agricultural machinery used to cultivate soil.  It has rotating blades that break up the soil, removing weeds and preparing it for planting.  Rotovators are also known as rotary tillers.|例句：The farmer used a rotovator to prepare the field for planting.  这个农民用旋转耕耘机准备播种田地。

The gardener rented a rotovator to loosen the compacted soil in her garden. 园丁租了一台旋转耕耘机来松动花园里压实的土壤。|近义词：rotary tiller, cultivator|反义词：None specifically, but tools for minimal soil disturbance could be considered opposites.
