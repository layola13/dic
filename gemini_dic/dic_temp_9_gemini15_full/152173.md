patriarchate:/ˈpeɪtriɑːrkeɪt/| n. |父权制；家长统治；族长统治|A social system in which the eldest male is the head of the family and descent is traced through the male line.  It also refers to a system of society or government controlled by men. |例句：Many feminists argue against the continued existence of the patriarchate. 许多女权主义者反对父权制的继续存在。

例句：The dominance of the patriarchate in many cultures has led to significant gender inequality.  许多文化中父权制的统治导致了严重的性别不平等。


近义词：male dominance, patriarchy, paternalism |反义词：matriarchy, egalitarianism |词性:noun
