hastened:/ˈheɪs(ə)nd/| vt.|赶快；急忙；加速|To move or act quickly; to accelerate the progress of something.|例句：He hastened his steps to catch the bus. 他加快脚步去赶公共汽车。
例句：The bad news hastened her decision to leave.  坏消息促使她加快了离开的决定。|近义词：hurried, rushed, accelerated, sped up|反义词：delayed, slowed, procrastinated|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hastens his pace when he is late. = 他迟到时会加快脚步。
They hasten to complete the project before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前赶着完成项目。


一般过去时:
She hastened to help the injured man. = 她赶紧去帮助受伤的男子。
He hastened to reassure her that everything was alright. = 他赶紧向她保证一切都会好起来的。


现在完成时:
They have hastened the process to meet the demands. = 他们为了满足需求而加快了进程。
We have hastened our preparations for the upcoming event. = 我们加快了对即将到来的活动的准备工作。


过去完成时:
He had hastened to inform his family about the accident. = 他已经赶紧通知家人发生事故的消息了。
She had hastened to finish her work before the meeting. = 她在会议前赶紧完成了她的工作。


将来时:
He will hasten to the hospital as soon as he hears the news. = 他一听到消息就会赶往医院。
They will hasten the delivery to ensure timely arrival. = 他们将加快送货速度以确保准时到达。


虚拟语气:
If he had hastened, he would have caught the train. = 如果他当时赶快的话，就能赶上火车了。
I wish I had hastened to respond to his email. = 我真希望我当时能快点回复他的邮件。


被动语态:
The process was hastened by the new technology. = 新技术加快了这个过程。
The decision was hastened by the urgency of the situation. = 情况的紧急性加快了决策。


疑问句:
Did he hasten to leave the party? = 他是不是赶紧离开了聚会？
Will they hasten the completion of the project? = 他们会加快项目的完成速度吗？


否定句:
He did not hasten to answer the question. = 他没有急于回答这个问题。
They did not hasten their steps despite the approaching storm. = 尽管暴风雨即将来临，他们也没有加快脚步。
