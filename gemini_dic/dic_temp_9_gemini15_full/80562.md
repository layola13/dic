goody's:/ˈɡʊdiz/| n.|好东西；糖果店；（Goody's）古迪氏（鞋店名）|A goody is something enjoyable, especially a sweet treat. Goody's can also refer to a type of store selling candies and other treats, or a brand name, such as Goody's Family Clothing.|例句：The children were excited to receive a bag of goodies.孩子们很兴奋收到一袋好东西。|近义词：treat, sweets, candy, confectionery |反义词：nothing, junk
