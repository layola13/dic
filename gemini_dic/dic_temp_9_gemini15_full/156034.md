photometry:/fəˈtɒmɪtri/ | n. | 光度测量学 | The science and technology of measuring light and its properties, including intensity, spectral distribution, and color.  It involves the quantitative measurement of visible light, as well as other forms of electromagnetic radiation. | Example:  The astronomer used photometry to determine the brightness of the distant star. 天文学家使用光度测量来确定遥远恒星的亮度。 |近义词:radiometry,luminometry|反义词:None
