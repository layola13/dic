garçon:/ɡɑːrˈsɔːn/|/ˈɡɑːrsɒn/| n. |男服务员，男孩|A garçon is a male waiter, especially in a French restaurant.  The word can also be used informally to refer to a young boy. |例句：The garçon brought us the wine list. 男服务员给我们送来了酒单。

The garçon politely cleared our plates.  男服务员礼貌地收走了我们的盘子。


近义词：waiter, boy (informal)  |反义词：waitress


词源:
From French *garçon*, meaning "boy," ultimately from Vulgar Latin * *garcio*.  The word's use as "waiter" developed from its original meaning of a young male servant.
