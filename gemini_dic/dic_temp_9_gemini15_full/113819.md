madbrained:/ˈmædˌbreɪnd/| adj. |头脑发热的；疯狂的；愚蠢的 | Extremely foolish or reckless; insane.  The plan was madbrained from the start.  | 例句：His madbrained scheme to rob the bank ended in disaster. = 他抢劫银行的疯狂计划以失败告终。

His madbrained idea to cross the ocean in a bathtub was quickly dismissed. = 他用浴缸横渡大洋的疯狂想法很快就被否决了。


近义词：insane, crazy, foolish, reckless, harebrained, lunatic, demented | 反义词：sane, rational, sensible, wise, prudent


词性：形容词
