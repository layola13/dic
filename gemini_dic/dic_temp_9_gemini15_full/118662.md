meta:/ˈmɛtə/ | adj. n. | 元，超越，元数据 |  relating to the underlying structure of a system, especially a computer system; relating to a specified system;  beyond or transcending the ordinary or usual;  data about data. | 例句：The meta-analysis of the studies showed a significant effect.  (对这些研究的元分析表明存在显著影响。) The metadata provides information about the file. (元数据提供了有关文件的信息。) | 近义词：higher-order, overarching,  transcendental | 反义词：concrete, specific, immediate | 形容词，名词
