pre-Chinese:/ˌpriːˈtʃaɪniːz/| adj.| 前中国的，中国以前的 |  Describes something that existed or occurred before the Chinese civilization or a specific period in Chinese history.  It often refers to a historical period or cultural influence preceding the rise of Chinese civilization. | 例句：Pre-Chinese artifacts were found during the excavation.  考古发掘中发现了前中国时期的文物。 |近义词：prehistoric, antecedent |反义词：post-Chinese
