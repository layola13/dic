enforcers:/ɪnˈfɔːrsərz/| n.|执行者；执法人员；强制执行者|Those who enforce rules, laws, or agreements.  They ensure compliance and may use force if necessary. |例句：The police are the enforcers of the law.  警察是法律的执行者。

例句：The school enforcers made sure students followed the dress code. 学校的执法人员确保学生遵守着装规定。


近义词：executors, implementers, law enforcement officers

反义词：violators, lawbreakers


词性:名词
