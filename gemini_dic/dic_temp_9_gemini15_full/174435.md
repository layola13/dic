quilter:/ˈkwɪltər/| n. |被子缝制者，拼布匠|A person who makes quilts.  A quilter skillfully stitches together layers of fabric to create warm and often decorative bed coverings.|例句：My grandmother was a talented quilter, her quilts were famous in the town.  我的祖母是一位才华横溢的被子缝制者，她的被子在镇上很有名。

例句：She's a quilter who specializes in modern designs. 她是一位专门从事现代设计的拼布匠。


近义词：patchworker, seamstress (if female), needleworker


反义词：无


词性：名词
