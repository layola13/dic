deuteriums:/ˈdjuːtəriəmz/| n. |氘（的复数）|Deuteriums are isotopes of hydrogen with one proton and one neutron in the nucleus.  Deuterium is sometimes called heavy hydrogen because it is twice as massive as the most common isotope of hydrogen (protium).|例句：The experiment involved using deuteriums to study nuclear fusion.  实验涉及使用氘来研究核聚变。
The researchers analyzed the ratio of deuteriums to protium in the sample. 研究人员分析了样品中氘与氕的比例。|近义词：heavy hydrogen (非正式)|反义词：protium (氕), hydrogen (泛指)|名词
