parrotlike:/ˈpærətlaɪk/| adj.|鹦鹉似的；机械地重复的；模仿的|Resembling a parrot;  characterized by mindless repetition or imitation.  The word implies a lack of understanding or originality.|例句：His parrotlike repetition of the teacher's words showed he hadn't grasped the concept. 他鹦鹉学舌般重复老师的话，表明他并没有理解这个概念。
例句：The parrotlike responses of the interviewee were unconvincing. 受访者的鹦鹉学舌般的回答令人难以信服。|近义词：mimicking, imitative, rote, mechanical|反义词：original, creative, innovative|
