ebbs:/ɛbz/| v.|退潮；衰退；减弱 |to recede, as the tide; to decline or fade away |例句：The tide ebbs and flows. 潮涨潮落。 The energy of the movement is ebbing.  运动的能量正在减弱。|近义词：recede, decline, wane |反义词：flow, increase, wax


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tide ebbs twice a day. = 潮汐一天退落两次。
The economy is ebbing slowly. = 经济正在缓慢衰退。


一般过去时:
The river ebbed after the flood. = 洪水过后，河水退了。
His strength ebbed away. = 他的力气逐渐消失了。


现在完成时:
The enthusiasm has ebbed since the initial excitement. = 自从最初的兴奋之后，热情已经消退了。
His influence has ebbed considerably over the years. = 这些年来，他的影响力已经大大减弱了。


过去完成时:
The water had ebbed significantly before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，水位已经显著下降了。
His confidence had ebbed before the crucial meeting. = 在关键会议之前，他的信心已经减弱了。


将来时:
The tide will ebb at midnight. = 海潮将在午夜退去。
His popularity will ebb if he doesn't address the public concerns. = 如果他不解决公众的担忧，他的声望将会下降。


虚拟语气:
If the tide ebbed earlier, we could have gone fishing. = 如果潮水早点退去，我们就可以去钓鱼了。
If his power ebbed, the revolution would succeed. = 如果他的权力减弱，革命就会成功。


被动语态:
The water level is being ebbed by the strong current. = 水位正在被强劲的水流冲刷而下降。
His courage was ebbed by the continuous failures. = 他持续的失败让他勇气消退。


疑问句:
Does the tide ebb quickly here? = 这儿的潮水退得快吗？
Is his influence ebbing away? = 他的影响力正在减弱吗？


否定句:
The tide doesn't ebb at this time of year. = 一年中的这个时候，潮水不会退去。
The support for the plan hasn't ebbed yet. = 对该计划的支持尚未减弱。
