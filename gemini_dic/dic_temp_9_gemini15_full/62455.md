endorsers:/ɪnˈdɔːrsərz/| n.|认可者；代言人；背书人|People or organizations that publicly support or recommend something or someone.  They lend their name or reputation to a product, cause, or person.|例句：Many celebrities are paid to be endorsers for various products. 许多名人都被付费成为各种产品的代言人。
例句：The company secured several prominent endorsers for its new campaign. 这家公司为其新活动争取到几位著名的代言人。|近义词：supporters, sponsors, advocates|反义词：critics, detractors
