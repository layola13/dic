helpings:/ˈhɛlpɪŋz/| n.|份；一客；(食物的)一份|A helping is a portion of food served to one person.  It refers to the amount of food given or taken at one time.  The word often implies a generous or substantial amount.|例句：I had two helpings of the delicious pie. 我吃了两块美味的馅饼。

例句：He served himself a generous helping of mashed potatoes. 他给自己盛了一大份土豆泥。


近义词：portion, serving, share|反义词：None specifically, but "small amount" or "tiny portion" could be considered antonyms in context.|词性:名词
