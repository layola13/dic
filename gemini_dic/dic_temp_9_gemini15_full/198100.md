shooks:/ʃʊks/| v.|震动；颤抖；震惊；(俚语)偷窃|The earthquake shook the houses violently. 地震剧烈地震动了房屋。|近义词：tremble, shake, quiver|反义词：still, calm


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ground shakes constantly. = 地面不断地摇晃。
He shakes his head in disbelief. = 他难以置信地摇摇头。


一般过去时:
The building shook during the storm. = 建筑物在暴风雨中摇晃。
She shook her head sadly. = 她悲伤地摇摇头。


现在完成时:
The earthquake has shaken the city. = 地震已经震动了这座城市。
He has shaken hands with the president. = 他已经和总统握过手了。


过去完成时:
The house had shaken violently before collapsing. = 房子剧烈摇晃后才倒塌。
She had shaken her head before speaking. = 她在说话之前摇了摇头。


将来时:
The ground will shake if there's an earthquake. = 如果发生地震，地面将会震动。
He will shake your hand when you arrive. = 你到达时他会和你握手。


虚拟语气:
If I shook that tree, the apples would fall. = 如果我摇晃那棵树，苹果就会掉下来。
I wish I hadn't shaken his confidence. = 我真希望我没有动摇他的信心。


被动语态:
The building was shaken by the explosion. = 建筑物被爆炸震动了。
His hand was shaken warmly. = 他的手被热情地握着。


疑问句:
Does the building shake often? = 这栋建筑物经常摇晃吗？
Did the earthquake shake your house? = 地震震动了你的房子吗？


否定句:
The ground didn't shake at all. = 地面根本没有震动。
He didn't shake my hand. = 他没有和我握手。
