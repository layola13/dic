gazelle:/ɡəˈzɛl/ | n. |瞪羚 |A small, slender, graceful antelope with long, thin legs and large, expressive eyes. Gazelles are known for their speed and agility. They are herbivores and live in herds in various habitats across Africa and Asia. |例句：The gazelle gracefully leaped over the low bush.  那只瞪羚优雅地越过了矮树丛。


The gazelle darted across the savanna, escaping the lion's pursuit.  瞪羚飞快地穿过草原，逃脱了狮子的追捕。|近义词：antelope, deer |反义词：N/A
