okay:/oʊˈkeɪ/ | int. adj. adv.| 好吧；可以；不错 |  Okay is an interjection, adjective, and adverb used to express agreement, acceptance, or acknowledgment. It can also mean "all right" or "satisfactory". |例句：Okay, I understand. 好吧，我明白了。  It's okay to leave now. 现在离开可以。|近义词：all right, alright, fine, good |反义词：bad, unacceptable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It's okay to go now. = 现在走可以。
Everything is okay. = 一切都好。


一般过去时:
It was okay yesterday. = 昨天还不错。
Everything was okay. = 一切都好。


现在完成时:
Everything has been okay. = 一切都很好。
It has been okay so far. = 到目前为止一切顺利。


过去完成时:
Everything had been okay until then. = 直到那时为止一切都很好。
It had been okay before the accident. = 事故发生前一切都还好。


将来时:
It will be okay. = 一切都会好起来的。
Everything will be okay tomorrow. = 明天一切都会好起来的。


虚拟语气:
It would be okay if you helped me. = 如果你帮我，那就太好了。
I wish it were okay to leave early. = 我希望可以早点离开。


被动语态:
It is okay to be helped. = 被帮助是可以的。(被动语态用在okay作形容词时)
The problem is okayed. = 问题得到了批准。(被动语态用在okay作动词时)


疑问句:
Is everything okay? = 一切都好吗？
Is it okay to smoke here? = 在这里吸烟可以吗？


否定句:
It's not okay to lie. = 说谎是不对的。
That's not okay. = 那不行。


PHRASE->
Okay then = 那么好吧
