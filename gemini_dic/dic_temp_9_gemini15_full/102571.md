jaghir:/dʒəˈɡɪər/| n. | 土地授予；世袭领地 | A jaghir is a grant of land, often hereditary, made by a ruler to a subordinate or favorite, typically in return for services rendered.  The land's revenue went to the jaghirdar, but the ultimate ownership remained with the ruler. | Example: The Mughal emperor granted him a jaghir in recognition of his military prowess.  莫卧儿皇帝授予他一块世袭领地以表彰他的军事才能。| 近义词：fief, grant, endowment | 反义词：confiscation, forfeiture | 名词
