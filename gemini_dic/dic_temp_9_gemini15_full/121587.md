misoccupies:/ˌmɪsˈɒkjupaɪz/| vt.|滥用；误用；占据（不当）； |To use or occupy something wrongly or inappropriately.  This implies a misuse or a taking up of space or time that is not justified or productive.|例句：He misoccupies his time playing video games instead of studying. 他把时间浪费在玩电子游戏上而不是学习。

例句：The company misoccupies a large office space, given its small staff.  这家公司员工很少，却占据着很大的办公空间。


近义词：misuse, misapply, waste, squander

反义词：utilize, employ effectively, optimize
