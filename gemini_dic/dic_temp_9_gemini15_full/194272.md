self-occupation:/ˌsɛlfˌɑːkjuˈpeɪʃən/| n. |自我职业；自己经营的职业|The term "self-occupation" refers to a situation where an individual is engaged in a profession or business that they own and operate independently, without employing others or being employed by someone else.  It emphasizes the self-directed and autonomous nature of the work.  The individual is both the owner and the primary worker.|例句：He found fulfillment in self-occupation, running his own carpentry business. 他在他自己经营的木匠生意中找到了满足感。|近义词：self-employment, independent work, sole proprietorship|反义词：employment, salaried position, working for others|
