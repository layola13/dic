incorpse:/ɪnˈkɔːrps/ | v. | 使结合；使并入；吸收；包含 | To unite or combine into one body; to embody or incorporate.  | The company incorporated several smaller firms.  这家公司并购了几家小公司。|近义词：integrate, include, absorb, consolidate | 反义词：separate, divide, exclude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company incorporates new technologies into its products. = 公司将新技术融入其产品中。
She incorporates yoga into her daily routine. = 她将瑜伽融入她的日常生活中。


一般过去时:
They incorporated the feedback into the final design. = 他们将反馈意见融入最终设计中。
He incorporated several quotes from the book into his essay. = 他在他的论文中引用了书中的几句话。


现在完成时:
The artist has incorporated various styles into his work. =这位艺术家已将各种风格融入他的作品中。
We have incorporated your suggestions into the updated plan. = 我们已将您的建议纳入更新后的计划中。


过去完成时:
They had incorporated all the necessary elements before submitting the proposal. = 提交提案前，他们已整合所有必要元素。
She had incorporated the new data into her analysis before presenting the findings. =她在展示研究结果之前已将新数据纳入她的分析中。


将来时:
They will incorporate the new software into the system next month. = 他们下个月将把新软件整合到系统中。
We will incorporate your feedback into the next version of the product. = 我们将在产品的下一个版本中整合您的反馈。


虚拟语气:
If they incorporated more diverse voices, the project would be more successful. = 如果他们纳入更多元的声音，项目将会更成功。
I wish they had incorporated my suggestions earlier. = 我希望他们早点采纳我的建议。


被动语态:
The new features were incorporated into the latest update. = 最新更新中包含了新功能。
The amendments will be incorporated into the final draft. = 修正案将被纳入最终草案。


疑问句:
Will the changes be incorporated into the next release? = 下一个版本中会包含这些更改吗？
Have all the necessary elements been incorporated into the plan? = 所有必要的元素都已纳入计划中了吗？


否定句:
The design did not incorporate any sustainable materials. = 该设计未采用任何可持续材料。
They did not incorporate my feedback into the final report. = 他们没有将我的反馈意见纳入最终报告。


PHRASE->
incorporate into = 融入...
incorporate with = 与...合并
fully incorporate = 完全融合
incorporate by reference = 通过引用合并
partially incorporate = 部分融合
