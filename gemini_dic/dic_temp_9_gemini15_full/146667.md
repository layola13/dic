overhaul:/ˈoʊvərˌhɔːl/ | /ˈəʊvəˌhɔːl/| vt. n. | 大修；彻底检查；改进；改革 | To examine thoroughly and repair or renovate; to make major improvements or changes to something. |例句：The car needs a complete overhaul. 这辆车需要彻底大修。

The company is undergoing a major overhaul of its operations. 这家公司正在对其运营进行重大改革。


近义词：repair, renovate, revamp, reform, revise | 反义词：neglect, deteriorate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mechanic overhauls engines every day. = 机械师每天都大修发动机。
They overhaul the system regularly. = 他们定期检修系统。


一般过去时:
The engineer overhauled the engine last week. = 工程师上周检修了发动机。
We overhauled the marketing strategy last year. = 我们去年彻底修改了营销策略。


现在完成时:
They have overhauled the entire production line. = 他们已经彻底检修了整条生产线。
The government has overhauled its education policy. = 政府已经彻底改革了教育政策。


过去完成时:
They had overhauled the equipment before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经检修了设备。
The team had overhauled its approach before the project commenced. = 该团队在项目开始之前已经修改了其方法。


将来时:
We will overhaul the website next month. = 我们下个月将对网站进行大修。
The company will overhaul its IT infrastructure next year. = 公司明年将对其IT基础设施进行大修。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would overhaul the entire system. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们将彻底检修整个系统。
It is essential that the company overhaul its policies. = 公司必须彻底修改其政策。


被动语态:
The engine is being overhauled at the moment. = 发动机正在修理中。
The software will be overhauled by the end of the year. = 软件将在年底前进行大修。


疑问句:
Will they overhaul the building soon? = 他们会很快对建筑物进行大修吗？
Is the car being overhauled today? = 这辆车今天正在大修吗？


否定句:
They did not overhaul the machinery. = 他们没有检修机器。
The company will not overhaul its marketing strategy this year. = 公司今年不会修改其营销策略。


PHRASE->
a complete overhaul = 彻底大修
a major overhaul = 重大检修
undergo an overhaul = 经历大修
require an overhaul = 需要大修
an overhaul of the system = 系统的彻底检修
