demilitarising: /ˌdiːˌmɪlɪtəˈraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 非军事化；解除武装 | The act of removing military forces, weapons, or fortifications from a place or area.  It involves making a place or region less military in nature. |

例句：

The government is committed to demilitarising the border region. = 政府致力于将边境地区非军事化。

The treaty calls for the complete demilitarisation of the disputed islands. = 该条约要求完全非军事化有争议的岛屿。


近义词：disarming, neutralizing, pacifying

反义词：militarizing, arming, fortifying


词源:

The word "demilitarising" is derived from the prefix "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "to reverse"), "military" (referring to the armed forces or warfare), and the suffix "-izing" (indicating the process of making something).  Therefore, it literally means "making something less military."
