outflows:/ˈaʊtˌfloʊz/ | n. |流出，外流 |The movement of something out of a place or system.  It often refers to money, liquid, or gas leaving a specific area or container. |例句：The outflows of capital from the country caused economic instability.  资金外流导致了经济不稳定。

例句：The river's outflows into the sea were significant. 河流流入大海的水量很大。


近义词：efflux, outflowing, discharge, emission |反义词：inflows, influx


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water outflow is steady. = 水流稳定。
The company's cash outflows are high this quarter. = 公司本季度的现金流出量很大。

一般过去时:
There were significant outflows of refugees last year. = 去年有大量的难民外流。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
