overhaul: /ˈoʊvərhɔːl/ | /ˈəʊvəhɔːl/ | vt., n. | 大修；彻底检修；彻底改革 | To thoroughly examine and repair something, especially a machine or system, replacing parts as necessary.  Also, to make major changes to a system or organization in order to improve it. |

例句：

The car needs a complete overhaul. = 这辆车需要彻底大修。

The company is undergoing a major overhaul of its operations. = 这家公司正在对其运营进行彻底改革。


近义词：renovate, revamp, overhaul, reconstruct, refit


反义词：neglect, abandon, deteriorate


词性: 动词，名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The mechanic overhauls engines every day. = 技工每天都大修发动机。
They overhaul the system regularly. = 他们定期检修系统。


一般过去时:
The engineer overhauled the engine last week. = 工程师上周大修了发动机。
We overhauled the marketing strategy last year. = 我们去年彻底改革了营销策略。


现在完成时:
They have overhauled the entire production line. = 他们已经彻底检修了整条生产线。
The government has overhauled its education policy. = 政府已经对其教育政策进行了彻底改革。


过去完成时:
They had overhauled the equipment before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经检修了设备。
The company had overhauled its financial procedures before the audit. = 公司在审计之前已经彻底改革了其财务程序。


将来时:
We will overhaul the computer system next month. = 我们下个月将大修计算机系统。
They will overhaul the entire building. = 他们将彻底翻修整栋建筑物。


虚拟语气:
If we overhauled the system, it would run more efficiently. = 如果我们大修系统，它将运行得更有效率。
I wish they had overhauled the software sooner. = 我希望他们早点更新软件。


被动语态:
The engine is being overhauled at the moment. = 发动机正在维修中。
The policy will be overhauled next year. = 该政策将于明年进行修订。


疑问句:
Will they overhaul the bridge soon? = 他们会很快维修这座桥吗？
Is the company planning to overhaul its management structure? = 公司是否计划改革其管理结构？


否定句:
They didn't overhaul the brakes. = 他们没有检修刹车。
The government has not yet overhauled its healthcare system. = 政府尚未改革其医疗系统。


PHRASE->
