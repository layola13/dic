screwdrivers:/ˈskruːdraɪvərz/| n. |螺丝刀|A tool used to drive screws, typically having a shaft with a shaped head that fits into the head of a screw.|例句：I need a screwdriver to tighten this screw. 我需要一把螺丝刀来拧紧这个螺丝。

例句：He used a Phillips head screwdriver to assemble the furniture. 他用一把十字螺丝刀来组装家具。


近义词：screw-driver|反义词:None


词源:screw + driver.  "Screw" refers to the fastener, and "driver" indicates the tool's function of driving or turning the screw. The word evolved naturally from its descriptive components.
