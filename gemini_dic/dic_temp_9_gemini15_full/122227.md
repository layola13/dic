misworship:/ˌmɪsˈwɜːrʃɪp/| v.|错误崇拜，误拜|The act of worshipping something incorrectly or inappropriately;  giving reverence or adoration to something that is not worthy of it, or worshipping in a wrong way.|例句：The ancient tribe misworshipped a stone statue, believing it to be a god.  古代部落错误地崇拜着一尊石像，认为它是神。|例句：Their misworship of power led to their downfall.  他们对权力的错误崇拜导致了他们的垮台。|近义词：idolatry, false worship, misbelief |反义词：true worship, right worship
