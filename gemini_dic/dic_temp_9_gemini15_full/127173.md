例句：He achieved a remarkable naturality in his portrayal of the character. 他在刻画人物形象方面达到了非凡的自然程度。


近义词：spontaneity, genuineness, realism, authenticity|反义词：artificiality, unnaturalness, affectation


词源：源自法语"naturalité"，最终源自拉丁语"naturalis" (自然的)。
