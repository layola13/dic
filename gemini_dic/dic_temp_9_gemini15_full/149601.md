painful:/ˈpeɪnfl/| adj.|痛苦的，令人痛苦的|causing or characterized by physical or mental suffering.  It describes something that causes discomfort, distress, or agony, either physically or emotionally.|例句：The injury was incredibly painful. 这伤非常痛。|例句：The breakup was a painful experience. 分手是一次痛苦的经历。|近义词：aching, agonizing, sore, hurting|反义词：pleasant, enjoyable, comfortable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wound is painful. = 伤口很痛。
This task is painful for me. = 这项任务对我来说很痛苦。


一般过去时:
The operation was very painful. = 手术非常痛苦。
My back was painful yesterday. = 昨天我的背很痛。


现在完成时:
My head has been painful all day. = 我今天头痛了一整天。

过去完成时:

将来时:

被动语态:

否定句:
