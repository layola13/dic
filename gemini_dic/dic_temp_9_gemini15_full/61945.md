emulsifiability:/ɪˌmʌlsɪfɪəˈbɪləti/| n. |乳化性，乳化能力|The ability of a substance to form an emulsion.  This property is crucial in many industries, including food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals, where the creation of stable mixtures of oil and water is essential.|The emulsifiability of this oil is excellent, allowing for easy mixing with water.|近义词：emulsification capacity|反义词：non-emulsifiability
