psychology: /saɪˈkɒlədʒi/ /saɪˈkɑːlədʒi/ | n. |心理学 |The scientific study of the human mind and its functions, especially those affecting behavior in a given context.  It encompasses a broad range of topics, including perception, cognition, emotion, motivation, and social behavior. | Example:  She's studying psychology at university. 她正在大学学习心理学。

| Example: The psychology of crowds is a fascinating subject.  人群的心理学是一个引人入胜的课题。 |近义词：mental science, psych |反义词：N/A |名词
