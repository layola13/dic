interthreaded:/ˌɪntərˈθredɪd/| adj.| 交织的；互相穿插的 |  Describes something that is interwoven or intertwined, with threads or strands running through and among each other. It often implies complexity and interconnectedness.  | Example: The narrative is interthreaded with flashbacks, making it more engaging.  叙述中穿插着倒叙，使之更引人入胜。 |近义词：interwoven, intertwined, interwoven, interlaced |反义词：separate, distinct, unconnected
