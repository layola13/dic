overabstemiousness:/ˌoʊvəræbˈstiːmiəsnəs/ | n. |过分节制；极其克制 |Excessive restraint or moderation, often to a point of being harmful.  This word describes a state of being overly cautious and restrictive, often to the detriment of one's health or well-being.  It suggests a level of self-denial that goes beyond simple moderation. | His overabstemiousness led to malnutrition.他的过分节制导致了营养不良。|近义词：extreme restraint, excessive moderation, self-denial, asceticism |反义词：indulgence, intemperance, excess
