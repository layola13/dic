juices:/ˈdʒuːsɪz/| n.|果汁；汁液|The word "juices" is the plural form of "juice," referring to the liquid extracted from fruits, vegetables, or other substances.  It can also refer to the essence or vital fluids of something.|例句：The juices of the freshly squeezed oranges were delicious. 新鲜橙汁非常美味。

例句：The chef used the juices from the roasted chicken to make a gravy.  厨师用烤鸡的汁做成肉汁。


近义词：extracts, liquids, essences
反义词：solids, pulp (in the context of fruit juice)
词性：名词
