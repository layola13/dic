chumming:/ˈtʃʌmɪŋ/| v.|用诱饵引诱(鱼)|The act of attracting fish by throwing chum (ground-up fish or other bait) into the water.|例句：The fishermen were chumming the water to attract sharks. 渔民们正在往水里扔诱饵吸引鲨鱼。|例句：They spent hours chumming before they caught anything. 他们在捕到任何东西之前花了几个小时诱鱼。|近义词：baiting, luring|反义词：repelling, scaring away|动词

They chum the water every morning. = 他们每天早上都在往水里扔诱饵。
He chums the water with ground-up fish. = 他用磨碎的鱼做诱饵引鱼。
