daubers:/ˈdɔːbərz/| n.|涂抹者；(尤指)用泥土或颜料涂抹的人；一种蜂类|A person who daubs, especially one who applies mud or paint.|例句：The daubers were busy repairing the damaged wall. 涂抹者正忙着修理损坏的墙壁。  The children were acting like daubers, painting all over the walls. 孩子们像涂抹者一样，把墙上涂满了颜料。|近义词：painter, plasterer, smearer|反义词：cleaner, restorer|名词
