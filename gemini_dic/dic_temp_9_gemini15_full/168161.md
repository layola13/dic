pro-Cambodia: /ˌproʊ kæmˈboʊdiə/ | adj. | 亲柬埔寨的，支持柬埔寨的 |  Favoring or supporting Cambodia.  This term expresses a positive stance towards Cambodia, its people, or its policies.  It can be used to describe individuals, organizations, or political viewpoints. |

例句：

The pro-Cambodia demonstrations were held in front of the embassy. = 亲柬埔寨的示威活动在使馆前举行。

He expressed his pro-Cambodia sentiments in his speech. = 他在演讲中表达了他对柬埔寨的支持。


近义词：pro-Cambodian, supportive of Cambodia, favorable to Cambodia

反义词：anti-Cambodia, opposed to Cambodia, hostile to Cambodia


词性:形容词
