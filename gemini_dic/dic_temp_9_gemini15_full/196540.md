sesanian:/sɛˈseɪniən/| adj.|萨珊王朝的；萨珊王朝风格的|Of or relating to the Sasanian Empire (224-651 CE) of Persia.  The term often describes art, architecture, or culture from that period, characterized by its elaborate and sophisticated style.|例句：The museum boasts a stunning collection of Sasanian silverwork.  博物馆拥有令人惊叹的萨珊王朝银器收藏。|例句：The intricately carved relief is a prime example of Sasanian artistry. 这件雕刻精美的浮雕是萨珊王朝艺术的典型代表。|近义词：Persian (in a broad sense), Parthian (for a related but earlier period)|反义词：无
