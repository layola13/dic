redefines:/ˌriːˈfaɪnz/ | vt. |重新定义；重新界定 | To define something again or in a new way.  This often implies a change or improvement to the original definition.  |例句：The new law redefines the boundaries of acceptable behavior. 新法律重新界定了可接受行为的界限。


例句：The company redefines its marketing strategy every year.  公司每年都会重新定义其营销策略。


近义词：revise, redefine, reinterpret, reformulate, recast


反义词：maintain, uphold (in the context of maintaining an existing definition)


词性：动词
