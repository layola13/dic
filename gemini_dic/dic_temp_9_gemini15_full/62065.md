encashment: /ɪnˈkæʃmənt/ | n. |兑现；变现；现金支付 | The act of converting something into cash.  It often refers to the process of exchanging a check,  promissory note, or other negotiable instrument for cash.  | 例句：The encashment of the cheque was immediate.支票立即兑现。  The bank facilitated the rapid encashment of the traveler's cheques.银行方便了旅行支票的快速兑现。| 近义词：cashing, realization, conversion | 反义词：non-conversion, deferment
