sirenia:/saɪˈriːniə/| n. |海牛目动物；海牛|A sirenian is any of a group of fully aquatic, herbivorous mammals.  They are also known as sea cows.  Sirenians are characterized by their large size, paddle-like flippers, and a horizontal tail fluke. |例句：Manatees and dugongs are examples of sirenians.  海牛和儒艮是海牛目的例子。|近义词：sea cow |反义词：N/A
