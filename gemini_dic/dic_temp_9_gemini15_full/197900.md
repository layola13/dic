shirker:/ˈʃɜːrkər/| n. |逃避责任者；偷懒者| a person who avoids work or duty.  |He was branded a shirker for refusing to fight.|近义词：slacker, malingerer, idler |反义词：hard worker, diligent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shirks his responsibilities. = 他逃避责任。
She shirks her duties at work. = 她逃避工作职责。

一般过去时:
He shirked his duties yesterday. = 他昨天逃避了他的职责。
She shirked her responsibilities last week. = 她上周逃避了她的责任。

现在完成时:
He has shirked his responsibilities for years. = 他多年来一直逃避责任。
She has shirked her work consistently. = 她一直逃避工作。

过去完成时:
He had shirked his duties before the inspection. = 检查之前他就已经逃避责任了。
She had shirked her responsibilities for months. = 她已经好几个月逃避责任了。

将来时:
He will shirk his duties if he can. = 他如果可以的话就会逃避责任。
She will shirk her responsibilities again. = 她还会再次逃避责任。

虚拟语气:
If he were not a shirker, he would be more successful. = 如果他不是个逃避责任的人，他会更成功。
I wish he wouldn't shirk his duties so often. = 我希望他不那么经常逃避责任。

被动语态:
His responsibilities were shirked by him. = 他的责任被他逃避了。(This is less common)
His duties were constantly shirked. = 他的职责总是被逃避。


疑问句:
Is he a shirker? = 他是个逃避责任的人吗？
Does she often shirk her work? = 她经常逃避工作吗？

否定句:
He doesn't shirk his responsibilities. = 他不逃避责任。
She didn't shirk her duties. = 她没有逃避她的职责。


PHRASE->
shirk one's duty = 逃避责任

shirk work = 逃避工作
shirk one's obligations = 逃避义务
a confirmed shirker = 一个惯于逃避责任的人
