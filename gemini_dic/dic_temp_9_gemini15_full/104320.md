jut-out:/dʒʌt aʊt/| v.|突出，伸出|To extend or project beyond a surrounding surface or line.|The balcony juts out from the building.  阳台从建筑物中突出。|The roof juts out over the porch. 屋顶延伸到门廊上方。|protrude, project, extend|recede, indent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The window juts out from the wall.  窗户从墙上突出。
The roofline juts out over the patio. 屋顶线突出到露台上方。

一般过去时:
The branch jutted out into the street. 树枝伸到街上。
The cliff jutted out sharply into the sea.悬崖陡峭地伸入大海。

现在完成时:
The porch has jutted out since the house was built. 自房子建成以来，门廊就一直突出。
That part of the land has jutted out for centuries. 那块土地已经突出了几个世纪。

过去完成时:
The sign had jutted out further before the storm.暴风雨前，标志更加突出。
The old pier had jutted out much farther into the water before it collapsed. 老码头倒塌之前，它伸入水中的距离更远。

将来时:
The new building will jut out significantly from the street. 新建筑将从街道上明显突出。
The extension will jut out from the existing structure.扩建部分将从现有结构突出。

虚拟语气:
If the balcony jutted out less, it would be safer. 如果阳台不那么突出，它会更安全。
I wish the shelf didn't jut out so much into the room.我希望架子不要那么突出到房间里。

被动语态:
The stone was jutting out dangerously. 那块石头危险地突了出来。
The ledge is jutting out from the cliff face. 岩架从悬崖峭壁上突出。

疑问句:
Does that part of the building jut out? 建筑物的那个部分突出吗？
How far does the rock jut out into the water? 岩石伸入水中的距离有多远？

否定句:
The wall doesn't jut out. 墙壁没有突出。
The window sill doesn't jut out at all. 窗台一点也不突出。
