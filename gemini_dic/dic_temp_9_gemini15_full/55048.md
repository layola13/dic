disquisitional:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃənəl/| adj.| 详尽论证的；好辩的；爱争论的 | Relating to or characterized by disquisition; given to or characterized by disquisition.  It implies a thorough and detailed examination of a subject, often with a formal and scholarly tone. | The professor's disquisitional lecture on the intricacies of quantum physics lasted for three hours. 教授关于量子物理复杂性的详尽论证的演讲持续了三个小时。|近义词：argumentative, discursive, analytical, elaborate |反义词：brief, concise, succinct
