limiting: /ˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 限制性的，限定的 | Restricting or confining within limits.  |例句：The limiting factor in our project is the budget.  我们的项目的限制因素是预算。

例句：There are limiting regulations on the amount of chemicals we can use. 我们在使用的化学药品数量上有一些限制性规定。


近义词：restrictive, confining, restricting, circumscribing | 反义词：unlimited, unrestricted, boundless


词源:  From "limit" + "-ing".  "Limit" itself comes from Old French *limite, from Latin *limit-, stem of limes "boundary, limit".


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The budget is limiting our options. = 预算限制了我们的选择。
This rule is limiting the students' creativity. = 这条规则限制了学生们的创造力。

一般过去时:
The rain limited our activities yesterday. = 昨天的雨限制了我们的活动。
The time limit limited the number of questions we could answer. = 时间限制限制了我们能够回答的问题数量。

现在完成时:
The lack of resources has limited our progress. = 资源匮乏限制了我们的进步。
Government regulations have limited the company's expansion. = 政府法规限制了公司的扩张。

过去完成时:
The previous restrictions had limited their options. = 先前的限制已经限制了他们的选择。
The broken machine had limited their production. = 损坏的机器限制了他们的生产。

将来时:
The new law will limit the power of the corporation. = 新法律将限制公司的权力。
The shortage of materials will limit production next month. = 材料短缺将限制下个月的生产。

虚拟语气:

If the rules weren't so limiting, we could be more innovative. = 如果规则不那么限制，我们就能更有创新性。

否定句:
