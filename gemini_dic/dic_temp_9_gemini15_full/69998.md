fete:/fet/| n.|节日；庆祝；宴会|A fete is a festive gathering or celebration, often outdoors, typically involving games, entertainment, and food stalls.  It can also refer to a large party or celebration.|例句：The village held a summer fete with music, games, and food stalls.  村里举行了一个夏季节日，有音乐、游戏和食品摊位。 The company threw a lavish fete to celebrate its anniversary. 公司举办了一场盛大的宴会来庆祝周年纪念。|近义词：festival, celebration, party, festivity|反义词：mourning, funeral
