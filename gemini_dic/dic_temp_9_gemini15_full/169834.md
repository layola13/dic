prosecutions:/ˌprɒsɪˈkjuːʃənz/| n. |起诉；检举；诉讼 |The act of prosecuting someone or something.  It refers to the legal process of bringing criminal charges against someone.  The plural form indicates multiple instances of prosecution.|例句：The prosecutions for fraud were lengthy and complex.  这些欺诈案的起诉过程漫长而复杂。

例句：Multiple prosecutions against the company led to its eventual bankruptcy. 多次对该公司的起诉最终导致其破产。


近义词：proceedings, lawsuits, indictments |反义词：acquittals, exonerations


词性: 名词
