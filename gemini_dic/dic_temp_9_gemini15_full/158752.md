plowman:/ˈplaʊmən/| n. |耕作者，农夫 |A plowman is a person who uses a plow to cultivate land.  The word evokes images of traditional farming methods and a simpler way of life.  It often implies hard work and a close connection to the land. |例句：The plowman worked tirelessly from sunrise to sunset.  耕作者从日出到日落辛勤工作。

The plowman's hands were rough and calloused from years of toil.  农夫的手因多年的辛劳而粗糙而起茧。|近义词：farmer, agricultural worker, tiller |反义词：landowner, aristocrat
