flyers:/ˈflaɪərz/ | n. |传单，宣传单 |A flyer is a small printed advertisement or announcement, often distributed by hand.  |Example:  I picked up some flyers advertising the local farmers' market. 我捡了一些宣传当地农贸市场的传单。|近义词：handbill, leaflet, circular |反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We distribute flyers every week. = 我们每周分发传单。
The company sends out flyers to promote its products. = 公司发送传单来推广其产品。

一般过去时:
They handed out flyers at the concert. = 他们在音乐会上分发了传单。
I received a flyer in the mail yesterday. = 我昨天收到了一张邮寄的传单。

现在完成时:
They have already distributed all the flyers. = 他们已经分发了所有传单。
We have seen many flyers advertising the same event. = 我们看到很多宣传同一活动的传单。

过去完成时:
They had already posted the flyers before the event started. = 活动开始前他们已经张贴了传单。
I had never seen such creative flyers before. = 我以前从未见过如此有创意的传单。

将来时:
We will distribute the flyers tomorrow. = 我们明天将分发传单。
They will post flyers all over the city. = 他们将在全城张贴传单。

虚拟语气:
If we had more flyers, we could reach more people. = 如果我们有更多传单，我们可以接触到更多人。
I wish I had designed the flyers myself. = 我真希望我自己设计了那些传单。

被动语态:
The flyers were distributed throughout the neighborhood. = 传单在整个街区分发。
The flyers are being printed right now. = 传单正在印刷中。

疑问句:
Are these flyers for the new restaurant? = 这些传单是关于新餐馆的吗？
Where did you get those flyers? = 你在哪里拿到那些传单的？

否定句:
