rhubarb:/ˈruːbɑːrb/|/ˈruːbɑːr/| n. |大黄|A plant with thick stalks that are used in cooking, especially in pies and jams.  It has a tart flavor.|例句：My grandmother used to make delicious rhubarb pies.|近义词：sorrel (酸模，味道相似)|反义词：N/A
