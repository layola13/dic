oversedation: /ˌoʊvərˌseɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. |过度镇静；药物过量 | The state of being excessively sedated, often resulting in drowsiness, slowed breathing, and decreased responsiveness.  It can be a serious complication of medication. | Example: The patient experienced oversedation after receiving a larger-than-prescribed dose of pain medication.  病人服用超过规定的剂量止痛药后出现了过度镇静。 |近义词：drug overdose, excessive sedation, deep sedation |反义词：understimulation, hyperactivity
