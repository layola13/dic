ringleaders:/ˈrɪŋˌliːdərz/| n.|主谋；首领|The leaders of a group, especially one involved in illegal activities.  They are the individuals who organize and direct others.|例句：The police finally caught the ringleaders of the gang.  警方最终抓获了该团伙的主谋。

例句：The ringleaders of the protest were arrested.  抗议活动的组织者被捕了。


近义词：leaders, masterminds, instigators, chiefs

反义词：followers, subordinates

词源:
The word "ringleader" is a compound word.  "Ring" here refers to a circle or group, often implying a clandestine or secret group. "Leader" denotes the person in charge. Therefore, a ringleader is the leader of a ring or group, often one engaged in illicit activities.  The word's origins trace back to the 18th century.
