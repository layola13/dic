cameramen:/ˈkæmərəmən/ | n. |摄影师（复数）| Men who operate cameras, especially in filmmaking or television.  They are responsible for capturing visual content. |例句：The cameramen skillfully captured the breathtaking scenery.  摄影师们巧妙地捕捉到了令人叹为观止的景色。


The cameramen worked tirelessly to get the perfect shot.  摄影师们不知疲倦地工作以获得完美的镜头。|近义词：cinematographers, film crew, camera operators |反义词：N/A
