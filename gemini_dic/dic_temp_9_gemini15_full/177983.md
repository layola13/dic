recirculating: /ˌriːˈsɜːrkjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再循环；重新循环 |  Describes the process of something being circulated again, often referring to materials or information.  | Example: The factory is implementing a new system for recirculating wastewater.  这家工厂正在实施一个新的废水再循环系统。 |近义词：recycling, reusing, circulating anew | 反义词：dispersing, discarding

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system recirculates the water continuously. = 系统不断地循环用水。
The company recirculates its marketing materials. = 公司重新循环利用其营销材料。


一般过去时:
They recirculated the air in the room. = 他们重新循环了房间里的空气。
The old newspapers were recirculated for use as packing material. = 旧报纸被再次循环利用作为包装材料。


现在完成时:
The plant has recirculated millions of gallons of water. = 该工厂已经循环利用了数百万加仑的水。
