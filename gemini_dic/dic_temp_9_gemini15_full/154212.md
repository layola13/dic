peripheral's:/pəˈrɪfərəl/| n.|外围设备；周边设备|Peripheral's refer to the devices that are connected to a computer but are not part of the central processing unit (CPU).  They enhance the computer's capabilities.|例句：The peripherals included a printer, a mouse, and a keyboard.  外围设备包括打印机、鼠标和键盘。|近义词：accessory, add-on|反义词：central, core
