hemostat:/ˈhiːməʊstæt/| n. |止血钳|A hemostat is a surgical instrument used to clamp or compress blood vessels to control bleeding.  It typically has two opposing blades that can be closed and locked together. |Example: The surgeon used a hemostat to stop the bleeding from the artery.|近义词：clamp, forceps|反义词：None.
