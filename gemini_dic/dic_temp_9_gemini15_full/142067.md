orgeat:/ˈɔːrdʒiːæt/| n. |杏仁露，杏仁糖浆|A sweet syrup made from almonds, used in cocktails and desserts.|例句：The orgeat syrup added a delightful almond flavor to the Mai Tai. 杏仁糖浆为麦泰酒增添了令人愉悦的杏仁味。

例句：Many classic cocktails, like the Mai Tai, use orgeat syrup as a key ingredient.许多经典鸡尾酒，如麦泰酒，都使用杏仁糖浆作为主要成分。


词源:  From Old French *orgeat, from Arabic *aurǧāt, from *aurǧ (almond).  The word's journey reflects the historical trade routes of almonds from the Middle East to Europe.


近义词：almond syrup (杏仁糖浆)


反义词：无


词性: 名词
