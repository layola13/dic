neurophysiologist:/ˌnʊroʊˌfɪziˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |神经生理学家|A neurophysiologist is a scientist who studies the physiology of the nervous system.  They investigate the functions and mechanisms of the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. This includes studying things like how nerve impulses are transmitted, how the brain controls movement and sensation, and the effects of diseases on the nervous system.| Dr. Ramirez is a renowned neurophysiologist specializing in epilepsy.|近义词:neuroscientist|反义词: (no direct antonym)
