easting:/ˈiːstɪŋ/| n. |东距，东向距离|The eastward coordinate of a point, especially in surveying or mapping.  It represents the distance east of a reference meridian. |例句：The easting of the monument was recorded as 500 meters.  纪念碑的东距记录为500米。


例句：We need to calculate the northing and easting to determine the exact location.我们需要计算南北向和东西向距离来确定确切位置。


近义词：longitude (in a specific context)|反义词：westing


词源:源自古英语ēast，意为“东方”。  "-ing" 作为名词后缀，表示动作或结果。
