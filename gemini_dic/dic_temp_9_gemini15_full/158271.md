play-up:/pleɪ ʌp/| v.|夸大；渲染；炫耀；(机器)出故障；(孩子)调皮捣蛋|To exaggerate or make something seem more important than it is; to behave in a troublesome or unruly manner; for a machine to malfunction.|例句：The media played up the scandal.媒体夸大了这起丑闻。The children were playing up in class.孩子们在课堂上捣乱。My car is playing up again.我的车又出故障了。|近义词：exaggerate, hype, magnify, misbehave|反义词：downplay, minimize, understate
