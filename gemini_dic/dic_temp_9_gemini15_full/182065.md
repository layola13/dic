remerge:/ˌriːˈmɜːrdʒ/| v.|重新出现，再次浮现|To appear or become visible again after being hidden, submerged, or obscured.|The sun remerged from behind the clouds. 太阳从云层后面重新出现。 The company remerged after a period of financial difficulty. 公司在经历了一段时间的财务困境后重新出现。|emerge, reappear, resurface|submerge, disappear, vanish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun reemerges from behind the clouds every morning.  = 太阳每天早晨都从云层后面重新出现。
He reemerges into the public eye after years of seclusion. = 他在隐居多年后重新出现在公众视野中。

一般过去时:
The lost dog reemerged near its home after three days. = 走失的狗三天后在它家附近重新出现。
Hope reemerged in their hearts after the difficult news. = 在听到那个令人沮丧的消息后，希望重新燃起了他们心中的希望。

现在完成时:
The issue has reemerged as a major point of contention. = 这个问题已再次成为主要争议点。
Since the pandemic, the debate about remote work has reemerged. = 自疫情以来，关于远程工作的辩论再次出现。

过去完成时:
After the storm subsided, the island reemerged from the floodwaters.  = 风暴过后，岛屿从洪水中重新出现。
They realized that the old problem had reemerged before they could solve it permanently. = 他们意识到旧问题在他们能够永久解决它之前又出现了。

将来时:
The comet will reemerge in the night sky next year. = 这颗彗星明年将在夜空中再次出现。
Experts predict that the economic crisis will reemerge if the government doesn't act. = 专家预测，如果政府不采取行动，经济危机将会卷土重来。

虚拟语气:
If the economy improves, the housing market might reemerge strongly. = 如果经济好转，房地产市场可能会强劲复苏。
I wish the old feelings wouldn't reemerge so painfully. = 我希望旧情不要如此痛苦地再次浮现。

被动语态:
The sunken treasure was reemerged by a team of divers. = 一队潜水员将沉没的宝藏重新打捞上来。
The topic was reemerged in the discussion after a long period of silence. = 在长时间的沉默之后，这个话题在讨论中再次被提出。

疑问句:
Will the lost painting ever reemerge? = 失窃的画作还会出现吗？
When will the symptoms reemerge? = 症状什么时候会再次出现？

否定句:
The lost city has not reemerged despite many expeditions. = 尽管进行了多次探险，失落的城市仍未现身。
The old fears did not reemerge during his therapy sessions. = 在他的治疗过程中，旧有的恐惧并没有再次出现。
