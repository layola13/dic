nonconsignment:/ˌnɒnkənˈsaɪnmənt/ | adj. |非寄售的，非托运的 | Not involving consignment;  not sent or delivered on consignment.  This term typically refers to a transaction where goods are sold outright, not on a consignment basis where payment is dependent on the sale of the goods. | Example: The company opted for a nonconsignment sale to receive immediate payment.  这家公司选择非寄售销售以获得立即付款。|近义词：outright sale, direct sale | 反义词：consignment, consignment sale |
