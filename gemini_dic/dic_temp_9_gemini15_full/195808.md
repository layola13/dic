senators:/ˈsɛneɪtərz/| n. |参议员们|Members of a senate, especially in the US Congress or similar legislative bodies.  Senators are elected officials who represent their states or constituencies in the upper house of a bicameral legislature.|The senators debated the new bill for hours. 参议员们就新法案进行了数小时的辩论。|legislators, representatives|citizens, constituents|名词
