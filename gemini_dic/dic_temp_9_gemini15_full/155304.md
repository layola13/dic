phantasmality:/ˌfænˈtæzmælɪti/| n. |幻影性；虚幻性；幽灵般的事物|The quality or state of being a phantasm; the state of being unreal or illusory; something existing only in the imagination or as a figment of the imagination.  It often refers to something seemingly real but ultimately illusory or deceptive.  The term suggests an elusive and often unsettling quality to the perceived reality. |The phantasmality of the experience left her questioning her sanity.  这经历的虚幻性让她质疑自己的理智。|近义词：illusion, unreality, spectrality, chimera |反义词：reality, concreteness, tangibility
