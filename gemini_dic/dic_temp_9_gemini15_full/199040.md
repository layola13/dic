signer:/ˈsaɪnər/ | n. |签字人，签署者，手语者 | A person who signs a document or uses sign language.  |例句：The signer of the contract is responsible for its terms.  合同签字人对其条款负责。|例句：The signer skillfully conveyed complex ideas through sign language.  这位手语者熟练地通过手语传达了复杂的想法。|近义词：subscriber, signatory, interpreter |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The signer signs the document. = 签字人签署文件。
He signs his name on the cheque. = 他在支票上签上他的名字。

一般过去时:
The signer signed the contract yesterday. = 签字人昨天签署了合同。
She signed the petition last week. = 她上周签署了请愿书。

现在完成时:
The signer has signed all the documents. = 签字人已经签署了所有文件。
He has signed several important agreements this year. = 他今年签署了几项重要的协议。

过去完成时:
The signer had already signed the document before the meeting. = 在会议之前，签字人已经签署了文件。
She had signed the contract before leaving the office. = 她在离开办公室之前签署了合同。

将来时:
The signer will sign the contract tomorrow. = 签字人明天将签署合同。
He will sign the agreement next week. = 他下周将签署协议。

虚拟语气:
If the signer were here, he would sign the document. = 如果签字人在这儿，他会签署文件。
I wish the signer had signed the contract earlier. = 我希望签字人早点签署合同。

被动语态:
The document was signed by the signer. = 文件由签字人签署。
The contract is being signed by the signer now. = 合同现在正在由签字人签署。

疑问句:
Who is the signer of this document? = 这份文件的签字人是谁？
Has the signer signed the agreement yet? = 签字人已经签署协议了吗？

否定句:
The signer did not sign the document. = 签字人没有签署文件。
He hasn't signed the contract yet. = 他还没有签署合同。
