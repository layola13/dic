cross-vault:/krɔːsˈvɔːlt/| n.|交叉拱顶，十字拱顶|A cross-vault is a type of vault formed by the intersection of two barrel vaults at right angles.  It creates a four-part groin, or a cruciform plan.|例句：The cathedral boasts magnificent cross-vaults in its nave. 这座大教堂的中殿拥有壮丽的交叉拱顶。

The medieval church featured intricate cross-vaults supporting the massive stone roof. 这座中世纪教堂以其支撑巨大石头的复杂交叉拱顶为特色。|近义词：groin vault, ribbed vault|反义词：flat ceiling, simple vault|名词
