misbestowed:/ˌmɪsbɪˈstoʊd/| adj.| 错置的；滥用的；误用的 | Improperly bestowed; wrongly given or applied.  The word implies a negative connotation, suggesting that something valuable or important has been given or used in a way that is wasteful, inappropriate, or harmful. | Example: The misbestowed funds led to the company's bankruptcy.  这笔资金的滥用导致了公司的破产。 | 近义词：misapplied, misused, misdirected, wasted | 反义词：well-bestowed, properly used, wisely applied | 词性：形容词
