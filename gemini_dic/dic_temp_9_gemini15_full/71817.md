fleeing: /ˈfliːɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 逃跑的；逃离的 |  The act of running away from danger or an unpleasant situation; escaping.  | 例句：The thieves were fleeing the scene of the crime.  窃贼正在逃离犯罪现场。

The soldiers were fleeing from the battlefield. 士兵们正在逃离战场。


近义词：escaping, running away, absconding | 反义词：staying, remaining, approaching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is fleeing the country. 他正在逃离这个国家。
They are fleeing from the fire. 他们正在逃离火灾。


一般过去时:
She fled the country after the revolution. 革命后她逃离了这个国家。
They fled from the burning building. 他们从燃烧的建筑物中逃了出来。


现在完成时:
He has fled the city. 他已经逃离了这座城市。
They have fled to the mountains. 他们已经逃到了山里。


过去完成时:
The prisoners had fled before the guards arrived. 狱警到达之前，囚犯们已经逃跑了。
She had fled her home years ago. 她几年前就离开了家。


将来时:
They will flee the city if the volcano erupts. 如果火山爆发，他们将逃离这座城市。
He will flee to another country. 他将逃往另一个国家。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would flee immediately. 如果我是你，我会立刻逃跑。
I wish I could flee this terrible situation. 我希望我能逃离这个可怕的境地。


被动语态:
The city was fled by many residents. 许多居民逃离了这座城市。（被动语态使用较少）
The building was fled by the occupants. 建筑物里的人们都逃出来了。（被动语态使用较少）


疑问句:
Is he fleeing from the police? 他是在逃避警方吗？
Are they fleeing the war-torn country? 他们是在逃离饱受战争摧残的国家吗？


否定句:
He did not flee the scene. 他没有逃离现场。
They did not flee even when the building caught fire.  即使建筑物着火了，他们也没有逃跑。


PHRASE->
flee the scene  逃离现场
flee for one's life  拼命逃命
flee to safety  逃往安全地带
flee justice  逃避法律制裁
flee from danger  逃离危险


词源:
Old English *fleon, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch vliegen and German fliegen ‘to fly’.  The sense development is from ‘fly’ to ‘run away swiftly’.  The modern meaning is primarily associated with escaping from danger or persecution.
