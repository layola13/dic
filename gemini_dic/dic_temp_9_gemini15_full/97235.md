inflection:/ɪnˈflɛkʃən/ | n. | 词形变化；弯曲；音调变化 | The change in the form of a word to express a grammatical function or attribute such as tense, mood, person, number, case, or gender.  It also refers to a change in pitch or tone of voice. |  The inflection of the verb "to be" is complex.  动词“to be”的词形变化很复杂。

The inflection of her voice suggested sadness. 她声音的音调变化暗示了悲伤。|  flexion, modulation |  straightness (反义词，指没有弯曲或词形变化)| 名词
