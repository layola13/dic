nonsynthetically:/ˌnɒnˌsɪnˈθɛtɪkli/ | adv. |非合成地，非人工合成地 |  Not produced synthetically; naturally occurring.  This adverb describes something that is not created artificially through chemical synthesis but rather occurs naturally or is produced through non-synthetic means.  | 例句：The company prides itself on using nonsynthetically sourced ingredients in its products. = 这家公司自豪地在其产品中使用非人工合成来源的成分。

The drug is derived nonsynthetically from plant extracts. = 这种药物是从植物提取物中非人工合成地提取的。


近义词：naturally, organically


反义词：synthetically, artificially


词性:副词
