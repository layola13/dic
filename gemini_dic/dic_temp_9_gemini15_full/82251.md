grounded:/ˈɡraʊndɪd/| adj.|扎实的；务实的；接地的；被拘留的|Based in reality; practical and sensible; having a firm foundation; (of a person) calm and sensible;  (of an aircraft) having landed.|例句：His ideas are grounded in practical experience.他的想法基于实践经验。|近义词：earthy, realistic, practical, sensible|反义词：unrealistic, impractical, flighty, airy, ungrounded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is grounded in his beliefs. = 他对自己的信仰很坚定。
She is grounded in her principles. = 她对自己的原则很坚定。


一般过去时:
The plane was grounded due to bad weather. = 飞机因恶劣天气而停飞。
The child was grounded for misbehaving. = 孩子因为行为不端而被禁足。


现在完成时:
The project has been grounded in sound research. = 该项目以扎实的调查研究为基础。
She has been grounded for a week now. = 她已经被禁足一个星期了。


过去完成时:
The flight had been grounded before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，航班就已经停飞了。
He had been grounded for a month before his parents finally forgave him. = 他被禁足了一个月后，父母才最终原谅了他。


将来时:
The flight will be grounded if the fog continues. = 如果雾持续下去，航班将停飞。
If he misbehaves again, he will be grounded. = 如果他再次行为不端，他将被禁足。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were better, the plane wouldn't be grounded. = 如果天气更好，飞机就不会停飞了。
If I had known he would be grounded, I wouldn't have invited him. = 如果我知道他会禁足，我就不会邀请他了。


被动语态:
The aircraft was grounded by the air traffic controller. = 飞机被空中交通管制员停飞了。
The children were grounded for breaking curfew. = 孩子们因为违反宵禁而被禁足了。


疑问句:
Is the flight grounded? = 航班停飞了吗？
Is he grounded again? = 他又被禁足了吗？


否定句:
The plane is not grounded. = 飞机没有停飞。
He is not grounded; he's just being stubborn. = 他没有被禁足；他只是在耍脾气。



PHRASE->
grounded in = 基于；以……为基础
well-grounded = 根基牢固的；基础扎实的
emotionally grounded = 情绪稳定的
spiritually grounded = 精神充实的
grounded theory = 扎根理论
