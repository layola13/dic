resigners:/rɪˈzaɪnərz/| n. |辞职者，离职者 |People who have resigned from their jobs or positions.  Those who have formally left a job or position. |The number of resigners has increased significantly this year. 今年辞职的人数显著增加。  Many resigners cited burnout as their reason for leaving. 许多辞职者将倦怠列为他们离职的原因。|quitters, leavers |employees, stayers
