egressor:/ɪˈɡresər/| n. |侵略者，攻击者，肇事者|One who initiates aggression or violence; an attacker.  The term often implies a deliberate and unprovoked act of aggression.|例句：The egressor was arrested and charged with assault. 侵略者被逮捕并被控袭击罪。|例句：The egressor nation faced international condemnation for its actions. 侵略国因其行为而面临国际谴责。|近义词：aggressor, attacker, assailant, offender|反义词：victim, defender, peacemaker|名词
