loyaler:/lɔɪˈɑːlər/| n. |忠诚者，拥护者|A loyaler is someone who demonstrates strong loyalty and support, typically towards a person, cause, or group.  They are steadfast in their commitment and dedication.|例句：He was a loyaler to the king throughout the rebellion. 他在整个叛乱中都是国王的忠实拥护者。|近义词：supporter, devotee, adherent |反义词：traitor, deserter
