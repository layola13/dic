reaccumulating: /ˌriːəˈkjuːmjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新积累，再次累积 | The process of gathering or acquiring something again after it has been lost or dispersed.  It implies a previous accumulation that was subsequently lost or reduced. | Example: The company is reaccumulating its market share after a period of decline. 公司在经历一段时间的衰退后正在重新积累市场份额。


The snow is reaccumulating after the thaw.  融化之后，雪又重新堆积起来。|近义词：recollecting, reacquiring, regrouping |反义词：dispersing, dissipating, depleting |动词
