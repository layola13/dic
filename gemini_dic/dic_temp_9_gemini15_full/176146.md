/hwɑːt/|pron.|什么，什么东西|What is your name?|what, which|nothing|pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished this year? = 她今年取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the dinner party? = 晚宴前他们吃了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do. = 我真希望知道该做什么。


被动语态:
What is being discussed? = 正在讨论什么？
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
He didn't say what he meant. = 他没有说出他的意思。


PHRASE->
what's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
what if = 如果…怎么样
what for = 为什么
what about = …怎么样
no matter what = 无论如何
