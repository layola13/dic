scrupulousness:/ˈskruːpjʊləsnəs/| n. |一丝不苟；细心；谨慎 |The quality of being scrupulous; carefulness; meticulousness; conscientiousness. It implies a strong moral sense and attention to detail.  |例句：Her scrupulousness in her work is admirable.  她的工作一丝不苟，令人钦佩。

例句：The meticulous scrupulousness of the scientist ensured the accuracy of the experiment. 科学家一丝不苟的态度确保了实验的准确性。


近义词：meticulousness, conscientiousness, thoroughness, precision, accuracy

反义词：carelessness, negligence, sloppiness, laxity
