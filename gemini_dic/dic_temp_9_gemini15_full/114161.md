mahoe:/ˈmeɪhoʊ/| n. |木槿属植物；火烧树|A tree of the genus *Hibiscus*, especially *Hibiscus tiliaceus*,  a tropical tree with large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that turn reddish-orange.  Also known as sea hibiscus or beach hibiscus. It's valued for its strong fiber and is used in various applications.|例句：The mahoe tree provided shade on the beach.  木槿属植物在海滩上提供了树荫。|例句：They used the strong fibers from the mahoe to make rope. 他们用木槿的强韧纤维来制作绳索。|近义词：sea hibiscus, beach hibiscus|反义词：无
