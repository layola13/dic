overreacting: /ˌoʊvərɪˈæktɪŋ/ | v. |反应过度，过激反应 |  To react in an excessive or disproportionate way to something.  It implies a response that is stronger or more dramatic than the situation warrants.  | 例句：He's overreacting to the criticism; it's not that bad. 他对批评反应过度；没那么糟糕。


She was overreacting to a minor inconvenience. 她对一件小事反应过度了。


近义词：overresponding, exaggerating, making a fuss, blowing things out of proportion, being dramatic | 反义词：underreacting, remaining calm, staying composed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often overreacts to small problems. = 他经常对小问题反应过度。
They overreact whenever someone criticizes their work. = 每当有人批评他们的工作时，他们都会反应过度。


一般过去时:
She overreacted to the news. = 她对这个消息反应过度。
He overreacted and shouted at his colleague. = 他反应过度，朝同事大吼。


现在完成时:
I've overreacted several times this week. = 我这周已经好几次反应过度了。
They have overreacted to every minor setback. = 他们对每一次小小的挫折都反应过度。


过去完成时:
She realized she had overreacted to his joke. = 她意识到她对他的玩笑反应过度了。
I understood that I had overreacted to the situation. = 我明白我对这种情况反应过度了。


将来时:
I think he will overreact if you tell him the truth. = 我认为如果你告诉他真相，他会反应过度的。
She might overreact when she hears the news. = 当她听到这个消息时，她可能会反应过度。


虚拟语气:
If I were him, I wouldn't overreact. = 如果我是他，我不会反应过度。
I wish I hadn't overreacted yesterday. = 我真希望我昨天没有反应过度。


被动语态:
The situation was overreacted to by the media. = 媒体对这种情况反应过度了。(较少用被动语态)
His concerns were overreacted to by the management. = 管理层对他的担忧反应过度了。(较少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Is he overreacting, or is there something more to it? = 他是在反应过度，还是另有隐情？
Are you overreacting to this minor issue? = 你对这个问题反应过度了吗？


否定句:
He didn't overreact; he handled the situation calmly. = 他没有反应过度；他冷静地处理了这种情况。
She didn't overreact to the bad news. = 她对坏消息没有反应过度。
