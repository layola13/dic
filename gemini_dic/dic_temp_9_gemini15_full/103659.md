jollier:/ˈdʒɒliər/| adj.|更快乐的；更愉快的|More cheerful or merry.  It's often used to compare levels of happiness, suggesting a greater degree of jollity than a simple "jolly".| The party was jollier than I expected.  派对比我预期的更热闹。 The children were jollier after receiving their presents.  孩子们收到礼物后更开心了。|jolly, merry, cheerful|sad, gloomy, morose|形容词
