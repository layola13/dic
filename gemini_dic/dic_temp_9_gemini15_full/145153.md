overassessing:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɛsɪŋ/ | v. | 过度评估；高估 | To place too high a value on something; to overestimate.  |例句：The company is overassessing the market potential of their new product.  这家公司高估了其新产品的市场潜力。  |例句：Overassessing the risks involved led to a poor investment decision. 过度评估风险导致了糟糕的投资决策。|近义词：overestimate, overvalue, exaggerate |反义词：underestimate, undervalue
