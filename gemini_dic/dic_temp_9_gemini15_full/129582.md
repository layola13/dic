non-cognitive:/ˌnɒnˈkɒɡnɪtɪv/| adj. |非认知的，非认知能力的| Relating to mental processes not involving conscious intellectual activity;  not related to or involving intellectual processes.  This term often refers to aspects of personality and behavior that influence learning and performance, but aren't directly related to intellectual ability. | Example:  Emotional regulation is a key non-cognitive skill for success in school and life.  非认知技能如情绪管理对学业和人生成功至关重要。 |近义词： affective, emotional,  behavioral |反义词： cognitive, intellectual
