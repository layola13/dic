flickered: /ˈflɪkərd/ | v. | 闪烁；摇曳；忽明忽暗 | To shine with an unsteady light; to burn unsteadily; to appear and disappear intermittently.  |例句：The candle flickered in the draft.  烛光在风中摇曳。|The screen flickered before going blank. 屏幕闪烁了一下然后变黑了。|近义词：fluttered, shimmered, glimmered |反义词：burned steadily, shone brightly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The candle flickers in the wind. = 蜡烛在风中闪烁。
The light flickers intermittently. = 灯光间歇性闪烁。


一般过去时:
The screen flickered and then died. = 屏幕闪烁了一下然后就灭了。
The lights flickered during the storm. = 暴风雨期间灯光闪烁。


现在完成时:
The bulb has flickered all night. = 灯泡整夜都在闪烁。
The image has flickered several times. = 图像已经闪烁了好几次。


过去完成时:
The lights had flickered before going out completely. = 灯光在完全熄灭之前闪烁过。
The candle had flickered and died before I could make a wish. = 蜡烛在我许愿之前就闪烁了一下然后熄灭了。


将来时:
The lights will flicker if the power surges. = 如果电源波动，灯光就会闪烁。
I think the screen will flicker again soon. = 我想屏幕很快就会再次闪烁。


虚拟语气:
If the lights flickered again, I would call an electrician. = 如果灯再次闪烁，我会打电话给电工。
I wish the screen wouldn't flicker so much. = 我希望屏幕不要闪烁那么多。


被动语态:
The image was flickered by a faulty connection. = 图像由于连接故障而闪烁。
The lights were flickered by the strong wind. = 强风导致灯光闪烁。


疑问句:
Did the lights flicker during the presentation? = 演示期间灯光闪烁了吗？
Is the screen flickering again? = 屏幕又在闪烁了吗？


否定句:
The lights didn't flicker at all during the party. = 派对期间灯光根本没有闪烁。
The candle didn't flicker; it burned steadily. = 蜡烛没有闪烁；它稳定地燃烧着。
