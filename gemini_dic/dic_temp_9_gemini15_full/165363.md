prejudical:/ˌpredʒuˈdɪʃəl/ | adj. | 有害的；有偏见的；不利的 | Harmful; detrimental; biased; causing damage or injury.  |The judge ruled that the evidence was prejudicial to the defendant.  法官裁定证据对被告不利。| detrimental, damaging, harmful, injurious | beneficial, advantageous, helpful | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The biased reporting is prejudicial to the ongoing investigation. = 偏颇的报道对正在进行的调查有害。
This decision is prejudicial to the company's future. = 这个决定对公司的未来不利。


一般过去时:
The witness's testimony was prejudicial to the case. = 证人的证词对案件不利。
His comments were highly prejudicial to her reputation. = 他的评论严重损害了她的名誉。


现在完成时:
The media coverage has been prejudicial to the victim's family. = 媒体报道一直对受害者家属不利。
The constant negativity has been prejudicial to his mental health. = 持续的负面情绪一直损害着他的心理健康。


过去完成时:
The lawyer argued that the earlier statements had been prejudicial to his client's defense. = 律师辩称之前的陈述对他的当事人的辩护不利。
The judge determined that the information presented earlier had been prejudicial to the jury. = 法官认定之前提供的信息对陪审团造成了偏见。


将来时:
The proposed changes will be prejudicial to small businesses. = 拟议中的变更将对小型企业不利。
Continued inaction will be prejudicial to the environment. = 持续的无所作为将损害环境。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence had been less prejudicial, the outcome might have been different. = 如果证据不那么不利，结果可能会有所不同。
If the judge had not allowed the prejudicial testimony, the trial would have been fairer. = 如果法官没有允许有偏见的证词，审判就会更公平。


被动语态:
The defendant felt he was prejudiced by the media coverage. = 被告认为自己受到了媒体报道的不利影响。
The jury was prejudiced against the defendant from the start. = 陪审团从一开始就对被告有偏见。


疑问句:
Is this evidence prejudicial to the case? = 这证据对案件不利吗？
Could the judge's comments be construed as prejudicial? = 法官的评论能否被解释为有偏见的？


否定句:
The judge made sure the trial was not prejudicial to either party. = 法官确保审判对任何一方都不利。
The evidence presented was not prejudicial to the defendant. = 出示的证据对被告没有不利影响。


PHRASE->
prejudicial to one's interests = 对某人的利益有害
prejudicial to the public interest = 损害公众利益
prejudicial to the administration of justice = 妨碍司法公正
highly prejudicial = 极度有害的
potentially prejudicial = 潜在有害的
