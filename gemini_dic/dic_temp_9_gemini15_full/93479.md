illustration:/ˌɪləˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 插图；例证；说明 | A picture, diagram, or other visual representation accompanying a text.  It can also refer to an example or instance that clarifies or explains something. |例句：The textbook contained many helpful illustrations. 这本教科书包含许多有用的插图。

例句：Her experience serves as a powerful illustration of the challenges faced by single mothers. 她的经历很好地说明了单亲母亲面临的挑战。

近义词：example, instance, depiction, representation, diagram, figure

反义词：obscuration, concealment
