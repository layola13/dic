hatcher:/ˈhætʃər/| n. |孵化器；孵卵者；孵化场| A person or thing that hatches eggs; a device or place used for hatching eggs.  |Example: The hen is a good hatcher. 母鸡是很好的孵卵者。The farmer uses an incubator as a hatcher for his chicks. 农民用孵化器孵化小鸡。|近义词：incubator, breeder|反义词：N/A
