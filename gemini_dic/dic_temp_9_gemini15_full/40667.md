convers:/ˈkɒnvɜːs/| /kənˈvɜːrs/ |v.|谈话，交谈|to have a conversation; to talk|例句：We conversed about our families. 我们谈论了我们的家人。
例句：They conversed for hours. 他们聊了好几个小时。|近义词：chat, talk, speak|反义词：silence, quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They converse easily in English. = 他们用英语轻松地交谈。
We converse regularly about current events. = 我们定期讨论时事。

一般过去时:
They conversed politely at the dinner party. = 他们在晚宴上礼貌地交谈。
The two friends conversed for hours about their childhood memories. = 这两个朋友聊了几个小时关于他们童年的回忆。

现在完成时:
They have conversed on several occasions about this topic. = 他们已经就这个话题谈论过几次了。
We have conversed extensively about the project's challenges. = 我们已经广泛地讨论了这个项目面临的挑战。

过去完成时:
They had conversed before about the possibility of collaboration. = 他们之前就合作的可能性进行过交谈。
Before the meeting, they had conversed briefly about the agenda. = 会议之前，他们简短地讨论了议程。


将来时:
They will converse with the client tomorrow. = 他们明天将与客户交谈。
We will converse further about this matter at a later date. = 我们稍后将进一步讨论此事。


虚拟语气:
If they conversed more openly, they would understand each other better. = 如果他们更坦诚地交谈，他们会更好地理解彼此。
I wish they had conversed more about their feelings. = 我希望他们能更多地谈谈他们的感受。


被动语态:
The topic was conversed about extensively in the meeting. = 会议上广泛地讨论了这个话题。(注意：converse本身很少用被动语态)
The matter was conversed upon during the negotiations. = 在谈判期间讨论了此事。(注意：converse本身很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Do they converse often? = 他们经常交谈吗？
Did they converse about the project? = 他们讨论过这个项目吗？


否定句:
They did not converse much at the party. = 他们在聚会上没有怎么交谈。
We don't converse frequently. = 我们不经常交谈。



PHRASE->
converse with someone = 与某人交谈
engage in conversation = 开始交谈
have a conversation = 进行谈话
carry on a conversation = 继续谈话
hold a conversation = 保持谈话
