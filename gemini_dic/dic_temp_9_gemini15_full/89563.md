honourably: /ˈɒnərəbli/ /ˈɑːnərəbli/ | adj. | 光荣地；荣誉地；正直地 | In a way that brings honour or is deserving of honour; respectably.  |例句：He served his country honourably. 他光荣地为国家服务。

He was honourably discharged from the army. 他光荣地退役了。


近义词：respectfully, respectably, creditably, with honour | 反义词：dishonourably, shamefully, disgracefully | 词性：形容词
