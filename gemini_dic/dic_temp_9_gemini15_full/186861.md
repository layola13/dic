rings:/rɪŋz/| n., v.|环，戒指；鸣响，打电话|Rings can be made of various materials, such as gold, silver, or platinum.戒指可以由各种材料制成，例如黄金、白银或铂金。The phone rings incessantly.电话不停地响着。|近义词：circles, sounds, calls|反义词:silence, quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bell rings at 8 o'clock. = 钟在8点钟响。
She rings her mother every Sunday. = 她每个星期天都给她母亲打电话。


一般过去时:
The phone rang while I was sleeping. = 我睡觉的时候电话响了。
He rang the doorbell but nobody answered. = 他按了门铃，但没有人回应。


现在完成时:
The phone has rung several times this morning. = 今早电话响了好几次。
She has rung me three times today. = 她今天给我打了三次电话。


过去完成时:
The bell had already rung before I got to the classroom. = 我到教室之前铃声已经响了。
They had rung the alarm before the fire spread. = 火灾蔓延之前，他们已经拉响了警报。


将来时:
The alarm will ring at 6 a.m. tomorrow. = 明天早上6点闹钟会响。
I will ring you later this evening. = 我今晚晚些时候会给你打电话。


虚拟语气:
If the phone rang, I would answer it. = 如果电话响了，我会接。
I wish the bell would ring soon. = 我希望铃声快点响。


被动语态:
The bell is rung by the teacher. = 铃声是由老师按响的。
The alarm was rung too early. = 警报响得太早了。


疑问句:
Does the phone ring often? = 电话经常响吗？
Did the bell ring? = 铃响了吗？


否定句:
The phone doesn't ring very often. = 电话不常响。
The bell didn't ring on time. = 铃声没有准时响。
