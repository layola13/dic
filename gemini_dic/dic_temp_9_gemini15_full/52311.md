dickerer:/ˈdɪkərər/| n. |讨价还价者，漫天要价者 |A dickerer is someone who engages in protracted and often aggressive bargaining, especially over price. They are known for their persistence and willingness to haggle relentlessly.  |例句：The car salesman was a notorious dickerer, always trying to get the highest possible price.  这名汽车销售员是个臭名昭著的讨价还价者，总是试图获得尽可能高的价格。

例句：He's a tough dickerer, so be prepared to negotiate firmly. 他是个强硬的讨价还价者，所以准备好坚定地谈判。


近义词：bargainer, haggler, trader, wheeler-dealer |反义词：acceptor (in the context of readily accepting an offered price)


词源：源自动词“dicker”，意为“讨价还价”，其词源追溯到中古英语“dikken”，意义相近。
