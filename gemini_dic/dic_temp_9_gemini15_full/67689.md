exudation:/ˌɛksjuˈdeɪʃən/| n. |渗出；分泌；溢出 |The oozing out of a substance from a plant or body part.  Exudation is a process of slow discharge of fluids. It can refer to the substance itself or the act of oozing.  |Example: The exudation of sap from the wounded tree was evident. 树木伤口处树液的渗出是显而易见的。

The exudation of pus from the wound indicated an infection. 伤口流出的脓液表明有感染。|近义词：oozing, seepage, secretion, discharge |反义词：absorption, intake
