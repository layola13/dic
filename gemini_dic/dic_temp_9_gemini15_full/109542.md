lessens:/ˈlɛsən/| v.|减少；减轻；降低 |To make or become less; to diminish in intensity, amount, or degree.|例句：The teacher lessened the workload for the students.  老师减少了学生的作业量。|例句：The pain lessened after I took the medicine.  服药后疼痛减轻了。|近义词：reduce, decrease, diminish, mitigate|反义词：increase, augment, enhance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun's intensity lessens as the day ends. = 随着一天结束，太阳的强度减弱了。
Regular exercise lessens the risk of heart disease. = 定期锻炼减少患心脏病的风险。

一般过去时:
The storm lessened in intensity as it moved inland. = 暴风雨向内陆移动时强度减弱了。
His anxiety lessened after he spoke to his friend. = 他和朋友谈话后焦虑减轻了。

现在完成时:
The impact of the news has lessened over time. = 新闻的影响随着时间的推移而减弱了。
The medication has lessened her pain significantly. = 药物已大大减轻了她的疼痛。

过去完成时:
The tension had lessened considerably before the meeting began. = 会议开始前紧张气氛已大大缓解。
His fear had lessened after he received the good news. = 他在收到好消息后恐惧感减轻了。

将来时:
The effects of the drug will lessen over the next few days. = 这种药物的效果在接下来的几天里会减弱。
Hopefully, the traffic will lessen as rush hour ends. = 希望随着高峰时段结束，交通状况会好转。

虚拟语气:
If the government lessened taxes, the economy might improve. = 如果政府减税，经济可能会好转。
I wish the noise would lessen so I could concentrate. = 我希望噪音能小一点，这样我就能集中注意力了。

被动语态:
The workload was lessened for the students. = 学生的作业量减少了。
The symptoms were lessened by the new treatment. = 新疗法减轻了症状。

疑问句:
Does regular exercise lessen the risk of heart disease? = 定期锻炼是否能降低患心脏病的风险？
Will this medicine lessen my headache? = 这药能缓解我的头痛吗？

否定句:
The rain didn't lessen the flooding. = 雨水并没有减轻洪水。
The new policy hasn't lessened the burden on small businesses. = 新政策并没有减轻小型企业的负担。
