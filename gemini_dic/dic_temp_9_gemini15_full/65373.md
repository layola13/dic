eunuchism:/ˈjuːnəkɪzəm/| n. |阉割；宦官制度；去势；（对性压抑的）极端禁欲|The practice of castrating males, often to serve in harems or palaces, or the social system in which eunuchs held significant power.  It also refers to a state of extreme sexual abstinence or repression. |例句：The prevalence of eunuchism in ancient China is well-documented.  中国古代宦官制度盛行有据可查。  The novel explores themes of eunuchism and power. 这部小说探讨了阉割和权力等主题。|近义词：castration, emasculation, celibacy|反义词：sexual activity, virility
