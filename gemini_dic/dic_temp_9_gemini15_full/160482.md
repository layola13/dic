porcelainist: /pɔːrsələnɪst/ | n. |瓷器专家，瓷器制造者 | A porcelainist is a person who makes, repairs, collects, or studies porcelain.  They possess expertise in the creation, restoration, or appreciation of porcelain items. |例句：The renowned porcelainist meticulously restored the antique vase.  这位著名的瓷器专家一丝不苟地修复了这件古董花瓶。


The local museum hired a porcelainist to authenticate the newly discovered Ming Dynasty porcelain. 当地博物馆聘请了一位瓷器专家来鉴定新发现的明代瓷器。


近义词：ceramist (陶艺家), potter (陶工)


反义词：无


词性：名词
