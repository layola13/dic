reassuring: /ˌriːəˈʃʊərɪŋ/ | adj. |令人放心的；使人安心的 | Giving comfort or reassurance; inspiring confidence.  |例句：Her reassuring smile calmed my nerves.  她的令人安心的微笑使我平静下来。
The doctor's reassuring words eased my anxiety. 医生的安慰话语减轻了我的焦虑。|近义词：comforting, encouraging, soothing, calming |反义词：alarming, disturbing, unsettling, worrying
