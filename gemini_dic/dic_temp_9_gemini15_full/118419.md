merrymake:/ˈmɛriˌmeɪk/| v.|欢庆，寻欢作乐 |To celebrate festively; make merry.  It often implies a boisterous or jovial celebration.|例句：The villagers merrymaked all night long after the harvest.  村民们丰收后通宵达旦地欢庆。 The children merrymaked in the park, running and playing. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏玩耍，玩得很开心。|近义词：celebrate, revel, feast, carouse|反义词：mourn, grieve, lament
