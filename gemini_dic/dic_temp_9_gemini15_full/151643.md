particularization: /pərˌtɪkjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 特殊化；详细说明；个别化 | The act of giving details or specific information; the process of making something specific or distinct.  It involves focusing on individual aspects rather than general concepts. | 例句：The particularization of the plan left no room for doubt about its implementation.  计划的详细说明消除了对其实施的任何疑问。

The particularization of the historical data allowed for a more nuanced understanding of the events.  历史数据的个别化使人们对事件有了更细致的理解。| 近义词：specification, detailing, explication, individualization | 反义词：generalization, abstraction
