sacrificed:/ˈsækrɪfaɪst/| v.|牺牲，献祭|To give up something valuable or desirable for the sake of something else, especially something considered more important or worthy.|例句：She sacrificed her career to raise her children. 她为了抚养孩子牺牲了自己的事业。|例句：He sacrificed a goat to the gods. 他向神献祭了一只山羊。|近义词：offered, surrendered, yielded|反义词：gained, acquired, kept


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sacrifices his time for his family. = 他为家人牺牲自己的时间。
She sacrifices her comfort for her work. = 她为了工作牺牲自己的舒适。


一般过去时:
They sacrificed their belongings to help the victims. = 他们牺牲了自己的财物来帮助受害者。
He sacrificed his dream to support his family. = 他为了养家牺牲了自己的梦想。


现在完成时:
She has sacrificed a lot for this project. = 为了这个项目，她已经牺牲了很多。
He has sacrificed his health for his ambition. = 他为了自己的野心牺牲了自己的健康。


过去完成时:
They had sacrificed everything they owned before they escaped. = 他们逃亡前已经牺牲了一切财产。
She had sacrificed her happiness for many years before she finally decided to leave. = 在她最终决定离开之前，她已经牺牲了自己的幸福多年。


将来时:
We will sacrifice our comfort to achieve our goal. = 我们将牺牲我们的舒适来实现我们的目标。
They will sacrifice their free time to study for the exam. = 他们将牺牲他们的空闲时间来备考。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the consequences, I wouldn't have sacrificed so much. = 如果我知道后果，我不会牺牲那么多。
I wish I hadn't sacrificed my health for that job. = 我真希望我没有为了那份工作牺牲我的健康。


被动语态:
Many lives were sacrificed in the war. = 战争中许多生命被牺牲了。
His time was sacrificed for the sake of his career. = 他的时间为了他的事业而被牺牲了。


疑问句:
What did they sacrifice to get here? = 为了到达这里，他们牺牲了什么？
What will you sacrifice for your children? = 你会为了你的孩子牺牲什么？


否定句:
He didn't sacrifice his principles for money. = 他没有为了钱而牺牲自己的原则。
She wouldn't sacrifice her happiness for anyone. = 她不会为了任何人牺牲自己的幸福。


PHRASE->
