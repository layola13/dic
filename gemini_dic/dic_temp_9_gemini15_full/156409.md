phytogeographical:/ˌfaɪtoʊˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪkəl/| adj.|植物地理学的|Relating to the geographical distribution of plants.  The study of the distribution of plants across the Earth's surface, considering factors such as climate, topography, and soil. |Example:  The phytogeographical regions of the Amazon are incredibly diverse. 亚马逊的植物地理区域极其多样化。|近义词：geobotanical|反义词：N/A
