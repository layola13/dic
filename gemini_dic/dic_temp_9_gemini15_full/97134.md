infiltration:/ˌɪnfɪlˈtreɪʃən/ | n. |渗透；渗入；潜入 | The slow infiltration of water caused the dam to collapse.  水的缓慢渗透导致大坝坍塌。|近义词：penetration, seepage, permeation |反义词：exfiltration, expulsion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The enemy is infiltrating our defenses. = 敌人正在渗透我们的防线。
Water infiltration is a problem in this area. = 渗水在这个地区是个问题。


一般过去时:
The spies infiltrated the enemy camp. = 间谍潜入了敌营。
Rainwater infiltrated the basement. = 雨水渗入了地下室。


现在完成时:
Terrorists have infiltrated the city. = 恐怖分子已经渗透到这座城市。
The water has infiltrated the soil. = 水已经渗透到土壤中。


过去完成时:
The rebels had infiltrated the government before the coup. = 在政变之前，叛乱分子已经渗透到政府内部。
The damage had been caused by water infiltration. = 损坏是由渗水造成的。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
