play-by-play: /ˈpleɪ baɪ ˈpleɪ/ | n. | 实况报道，逐一描述 | A description of an event, especially a sports game, as it happens, giving a detailed account of each action or moment.  | Example:  The radio gave a play-by-play account of the football game.  广播对足球比赛进行了实况报道。


The fans watched the game on TV, following the play-by-play commentary.  球迷们在电视上观看比赛，同时收听实况解说。


近义词: running commentary, blow-by-blow account | 反义词: summary, overview | 词性: noun
