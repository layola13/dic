pro-Muslim: /ˌproʊˈmʌzlɪm/ | adj. | 亲穆斯林的，支持穆斯林的 | Supporting or favorable toward Muslims and Islam.  This term describes individuals, groups, or policies that advocate for or show support towards Muslims and their interests. It can be used neutrally or with positive connotations, although the interpretation can depend on context. |例句：The pro-Muslim stance of the politician gained her significant support among Muslim voters.  这位政治家的亲穆斯林立场赢得了穆斯林选民的大力支持。


例句：Many organizations work to promote pro-Muslim policies to combat Islamophobia.  许多组织致力于推广亲穆斯林政策以打击伊斯兰恐惧症。


近义词：Islam-friendly, Muslim-supportive, pro-Islamic


反义词：anti-Muslim, Islamophobic, prejudiced against Muslims


词性:形容词
