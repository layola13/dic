defensor:/dɪˈfɛnsər/ | n. |辩护者，保护者，卫冕者 |A defensor is a person or thing that defends someone or something.  They act as a protector or advocate, often in a legal or figurative sense.  |例句：The lawyer acted as a strong defensor for his client.  律师为他的当事人做了强有力的辩护。

The city's ancient walls served as a defensor against invaders.  这座城市的古城墙曾是抵御侵略者的屏障。


近义词：advocate, protector, champion, guardian |反义词：attacker, assailant, aggressor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lawyer defends his client. = 律师为他的当事人辩护。
She defends her beliefs passionately. = 她热情地捍卫自己的信仰。

一般过去时:
He defended his actions vigorously. = 他大力为自己行为辩护。
They defended the castle against the siege. = 他们保卫城堡抵御围攻。

现在完成时:
He has defended the innocent for many years. = 他多年来一直为无辜者辩护。
The company has defended its position against criticism. = 公司一直为其立场抗辩。

过去完成时:
The knight had defended the kingdom valiantly. = 骑士英勇地保卫了王国。
She had defended her thesis successfully. = 她成功地完成了论文答辩。

将来时:
He will defend himself in court. = 他将在法庭上为自己辩护。
They will defend their territory fiercely. = 他们将坚决保卫他们的领土。

虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would defend us. = 如果他在这里，他会保护我们。
I wish I could defend myself better. = 我希望我能更好地保护自己。

被动语态:
The city was defended bravely. = 这座城市得到了英勇的保卫。
His actions were defended by his supporters. = 他的行为得到了支持者的辩护。

疑问句:
Who will defend the accused? = 谁将为被告辩护？
How will you defend your ideas? = 你将如何捍卫你的想法？

否定句:
He did not defend his friend. = 他没有为他的朋友辩护。
She could not defend herself against the attack. = 她无法自卫。


PHRASE->
chief defensor = 首席辩护人
public defensor = 公设辩护人
defensor of the faith = 信仰的捍卫者
defensor of the realm = 王国的守护者
defensor pro deo =  上帝的辩护者 (指为穷人免费辩护的律师)


词源:
From Latin *defensor, from defendere "to ward off, protect".  The word ultimately derives from the prefix *de- ("away, off") and the verb *fendere ("to strike, cleave").  The original meaning therefore referred to striking away or warding off an attack.
