puppet:/ˈpʌpɪt/| n.|木偶；傀儡；受操纵的人|A puppet is a doll, typically one made of wood or other material, that is moved by strings or by a person's hand.  It can also refer to a person who is controlled by another person or a force. |例句：The children were fascinated by the puppet show.孩子们被木偶戏迷住了。

例句：He felt like a puppet in the hands of fate. 他感觉自己像命运手中的木偶。|近义词：marionette, doll, figurehead|反义词：master, controller
