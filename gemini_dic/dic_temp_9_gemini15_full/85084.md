hardback: /ˈhɑːdbæk/ | n. |精装书|A book bound in a stiff cover, typically a hard cover.|例句：I prefer to buy hardbacks because they last longer.  我更喜欢买精装书，因为它们更耐用。|近义词：cased book|反义词：paperback
