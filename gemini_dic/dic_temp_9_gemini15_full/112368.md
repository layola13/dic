lose:/luːz/ | /luːs/ | vt.  | 失去；丢失；输掉 | To no longer possess, have, or retain something.  | I lost my keys.  我丢了钥匙。  | He lost the game. 他输了比赛。 | find, gain, win | keep, retain, win |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I lose my temper easily. = 我很容易发脾气。
We lose the game every time. = 我们每次都输掉比赛。


一般过去时:
She lost her wallet yesterday. = 她昨天丢了钱包。
They lost their way in the forest. = 他们在森林里迷路了。


现在完成时:
I have lost my appetite. = 我已经失去了食欲。
He has lost a lot of weight recently. = 他最近瘦了很多。


过去完成时:
I had lost all hope before he arrived. = 在他到来之前我已经失去了所有希望。
She had lost her job before she found a new one. = 在找到新工作之前她已经失业了。


将来时:
I will lose my patience soon. = 我很快就会失去耐心。
We will lose the election if we don't work harder. = 如果我们不更加努力，我们将输掉选举。


虚拟语气:
If I lost my job, I would be devastated. = 如果我丢了工作，我会非常沮丧。
I wish I hadn't lost my phone. = 我真希望我没有丢了手机。


被动语态:
The game was lost by a single point. = 比赛以一分之差输掉了。
Many lives were lost in the earthquake. = 地震中很多人丧生。


疑问句:
Did you lose your ticket? = 你把票弄丢了吗？
Have you ever lost a close friend? = 你曾经失去过亲密的朋友吗？


否定句:
I didn't lose my way. = 我没有迷路。
Don't lose heart; try again. = 不要灰心；再试一次。


PHRASE->
lose oneself in = 沉浸于……
lose one's way = 迷路
lose face = 丢脸
lose touch with = 与……失去联系
lose control of = 失去对……的控制
