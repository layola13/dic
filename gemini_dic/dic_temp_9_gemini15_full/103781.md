journeying:/ˈdʒɜːrniɪŋ/ | v. | 旅行；旅程；奔波 | The act of traveling; a long or arduous journey.  It implies a sense of progress or purpose in the travel. |例句：We are journeying across the country to visit family. = 我们正在横跨全国去探望家人。  She is journeying through life with courage and grace. = 她正勇敢优雅地走过人生旅程。|近义词：traveling, traveling, trekking, voyaging |反义词：staying, remaining


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are journeying through Europe. = 他们正在欧洲旅行。
We are journeying to find a better future. = 我们正在为了寻找更好的未来而奔波。

一般过去时:
They journeyed across the desert. = 他们穿越了沙漠。
We journeyed for many days to reach the summit. = 我们跋涉多日才到达山顶。

现在完成时:
She has journeyed extensively throughout Asia. = 她已经广泛地游历了亚洲。
They have journeyed far to be here today. = 他们为了今天来到这里已经走了很远的路。

过去完成时:
He had journeyed for weeks before he finally reached his destination. = 他跋涉了几周才最终到达目的地。
They had journeyed through many hardships before finding success. = 他们在获得成功之前经历了许多磨难。

将来时:
We will be journeying to the mountains next week. = 我们下周要去山上旅行。
They will journey to the distant land in search of adventure. = 他们将前往遥远的国度寻找冒险。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would journey to the Himalayas. = 如果我有时间，我会去喜马拉雅山旅行。
I wish I were journeying to a tropical island right now. = 我真希望我现在正在去一个热带岛屿旅行。

被动语态:
The goods were journeyed across the ocean. = 货物被运送过大洋。 (Note:  Passive voice is less common with "journey" as it's usually an active process)
The story is journeyed through different times and places. = 故事在不同的时间和地点穿梭。(Note: This is a more figurative use of the passive voice)

疑问句:
Are they journeying by train or plane? = 他们坐火车还是飞机旅行？
Where are you journeying to? = 你要去哪里旅行？

否定句:
They are not journeying today. = 他们今天不旅行。
We did not journey as far as we had planned. = 我们没有走得像我们计划的那样远。
