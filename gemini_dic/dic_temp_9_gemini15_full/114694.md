malingers:/məˈlɪŋɡər/| v.|装病；装病逃避工作或责任|To feign illness in order to avoid work or duty.  |例句：He malingered for weeks to avoid the upcoming exam. 他装病几周以逃避即将到来的考试。

例句：She was suspected of malingering to escape her responsibilities at work.  她被怀疑装病以逃避工作责任。|近义词：shirk, feign illness, sham, pretend illness|反义词：work diligently, be conscientious
