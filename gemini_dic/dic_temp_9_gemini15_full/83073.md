gurning:/ˈɡɜːrnɪŋ/| v.|做鬼脸，扮怪相|The act of making a grimace or contorted face.  It often expresses disgust, displeasure, or amusement.|例句：The children were gurning at each other, making silly faces. 孩子们互相做鬼脸，做着滑稽的表情。|例句：He sat there gurning, clearly unhappy with the situation. 他坐在那里做着鬼脸，显然对这种情况很不高兴。|近义词：grimacing, scowling, mugging|反义词：smiling, grinning
