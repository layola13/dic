passed-over:/pɑːstˈoʊvər/ | adj. v. | 被忽视的；未被考虑的；被越过 |  To be disregarded or overlooked; to be bypassed or omitted.  | 例句：He felt passed over for promotion. 他感觉自己被忽视了晋升机会。  | 例句：The old bridge was passed over in favor of the new one. 旧桥被弃用，取而代之的是新桥。 | 近义词：ignored, neglected, overlooked, bypassed | 反义词：considered, chosen, selected |  动词和形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They often pass over minor details. = 他们经常忽略细节。
She passes over the difficult questions. = 她回避难题。

一般过去时:
The committee passed over his application. = 委员会没有采纳他的申请。
They passed over the opportunity to invest. = 他们错过了投资的机会。

现在完成时:
He has been passed over for promotion several times. = 他已经被忽视了几次晋升机会。
They have passed over many qualified candidates. = 他们已经忽视了许多合格的候选人。

过去完成时:
The manager had passed over his suggestion before the meeting. = 经理在会议前已经否决了他的建议。
She realized that she had been passed over for a promotion. = 她意识到自己被忽视了晋升机会。

将来时:
They will likely pass over his request for a raise. = 他们很可能会驳回他的加薪请求。
I fear they will pass over my application again. = 我担心他们会再次忽视我的申请。

虚拟语气:
If they hadn't passed over his application, he would have got the job. = 如果他们没有忽视他的申请，他就会得到这份工作。
I wish they hadn't passed over my ideas. = 我真希望他们没有忽视我的想法。

被动语态:
His application was passed over. = 他的申请被忽视了。

疑问句:

否定句:
