responded:/rɪˈspɒndɪd/ | v. |回应，答复，反应 | To answer or reply to someone or something.  |例句：He responded to my email promptly. 他迅速回复了我的邮件。|例句：The patient responded well to the treatment. 病人对治疗反应良好。|近义词：answered, replied, reacted |反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He responds to emails quickly. = 他很快回复邮件。
She responds positively to criticism. = 她对批评反应积极。


一般过去时:
I responded to his question immediately. = 我立刻回答了他的问题。
They responded with enthusiasm to the proposal. = 他们热情地回应了这个提议。


现在完成时:
He has responded to all the inquiries. = 他已经回复了所有的询问。
She has not yet responded to my invitation. = 她还没有回复我的邀请。


过去完成时:
I had already responded before he called. = 我在他打电话之前就已经回复了。
They had responded negatively to the initial offer. = 他们对最初的提议作出了否定的回应。


将来时:
I will respond to your letter tomorrow. = 我明天会回复你的信。
She will likely respond favorably to the request. = 她可能会对这个请求给予积极的回应。


虚拟语气:
If he had responded sooner, we could have avoided the problem. = 如果他早点回应，我们就能避免这个问题。
I wish he had responded to my concerns. = 我希望他回应我的担忧。


被动语态:
The email was responded to promptly. = 邮件得到了及时的回复。
His request was not responded to. = 他的请求没有得到回应。


疑问句:
Did he respond to your message? = 他回复你的信息了吗？
How did she respond to the news? = 她对这个消息作何反应？


否定句:
He didn't respond to my call. = 他没有回应我的电话。
She hasn't responded to my invitation yet. = 她还没有回应我的邀请。


PHRASE->
respond to = 回答；回应
respond in kind = 以牙还牙
respond favorably = 积极回应
respond negatively = 消极回应
respond with = 以...回应
