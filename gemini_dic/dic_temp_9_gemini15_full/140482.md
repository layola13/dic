old-womanish: /ˌoʊldˈwʊmənɪʃ/ | adj. | 老太婆似的；像老妇人的；唠叨的 |

英文解析:  Suggesting the characteristics of an old woman, often implying negativity such as being fussy, gossipy, or overly concerned with trivialities.  It can also describe something that is outdated or old-fashioned.

例句:

Her old-womanish complaints about the noise were getting on everyone's nerves.  她的关于噪音的老太婆似的抱怨让每个人都心烦。

The décor in the hotel was rather old-womanish and desperately needed updating.  酒店的装饰相当过时，急需更新。


近义词:  fussy, gossipy,  cantankerous,  prissy,  senile (in some contexts),  quaint (when referring to outdated style)

反义词:  youthful, vibrant, energetic, modern, stylish


词性: adjective
