reads:/riːdz/| /ridz/ | v. | 阅读；读懂；显示；听起来；读作 | to pronounce the words of (something written); to understand the meaning of (something written) | 例句：He reads a book every night. 他每天晚上都读一本书。

例句：The sign reads "No Parking".  这个标志写着“禁止停车”。


近义词：peruses, scans, interprets | 反义词：writes, ignores


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reads the newspaper every morning. = 他每天早上读报纸。
She reads very quickly. = 她阅读速度很快。


一般过去时:
I read that book last week. = 我上周读了那本书。
They read the letter aloud. = 他们大声朗读了这封信。


现在完成时:
I have read that book twice. = 我读过那本书两次了。
She has read many novels this year. = 今年她读了很多小说。


过去完成时:
I had read the instructions before I started. = 我在开始之前已经读过说明书了。
They had read the report before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经读过报告了。


将来时:
I will read that new novel tomorrow. = 我明天将阅读那本新小说。
She will read to the children tonight. = 她今晚将给孩子们读书。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would read more books. = 如果我有更多时间，我会读更多书。
I wish I could read faster. = 我希望我能读得更快。


被动语态:
The book was read by many people. = 这本书被很多人读过。
The announcement was read aloud at the meeting. = 会议上大声宣读了公告。


疑问句:
Do you read a lot? = 你读很多书吗？
What are you reading? = 你在读什么？


否定句:
He doesn't read much. = 他不太读书。
She didn't read the email. = 她没有读那封邮件。


PHRASE->
read between the lines = 读懂字里行间的意思
read into something = 过度解读
read up on something = 仔细研究
read out = 大声朗读
read aloud = 大声朗读
