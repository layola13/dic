rescale: /ˌriːˈskeɪl/ | vt. |重新调整比例，重新缩放| To change the scale of something, especially an image or map, so that it is larger or smaller.  |例句：We need to rescale the image to fit the page. 我们需要重新调整图像大小以适合页面。

例句：The architect rescaled the blueprint to show more detail. 建筑师重新调整了蓝图的比例以显示更多细节。


近义词：resize, re-size, scale, adjust


反义词：None directly opposite, but the opposite action would be to leave the scale unchanged.


词性：动词

词源:
The word "rescale" is a combination of the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "anew") and the verb "scale," which in this context means "to adjust the size or proportions of something."  The root of "scale" ultimately traces back to Old French "escale," and ultimately to Latin "scala" (ladder, stairs), reflecting the idea of a graduated series or proportions.
