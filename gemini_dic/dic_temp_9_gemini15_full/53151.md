diplopod:/ˈdɪpləpɒd/| n. |倍足纲动物，千足虫|A diplopod is an arthropod belonging to the class Diplopoda, characterized by having two pairs of legs per body segment.  They are commonly known as millipedes. |Example:  The child shrieked at the sight of the diplopod crawling across the path.  孩子看到一条千足虫在小路上爬，吓得尖叫起来。|近义词：millipede |反义词：None specifically;  perhaps  insect (in the sense of contrasting with a single pair of legs per segment)|词性:名词
