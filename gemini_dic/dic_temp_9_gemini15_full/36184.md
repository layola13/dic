cofinanced:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnænst/| adj. |共同资助的，联名资助的 | jointly financed; funded by more than one source.  The project was cofinanced by the government and private investors.  这个项目由政府和私人投资者共同资助。|近义词：jointly funded, jointly financed |反义词：solely funded, individually financed


USAGE->

The government cofinances many infrastructure projects. = 政府共同资助许多基础设施项目。
Several organizations cofinance this research initiative. = 几家机构共同资助这项研究计划。

The museum's expansion was cofinanced by donations and grants. = 博物馆的扩建由捐款和拨款共同资助。
