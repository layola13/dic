remarry:/ˌriːˈmæri/| v.|再婚|To marry again after the death or divorce of a spouse.  |例句：She remarried a year after her husband died. 她丈夫去世一年后又结婚了。 |例句： After a difficult divorce, he decided to remarry.  经历了一场艰难的离婚后，他决定再婚。|近义词：wed again, marry again|反义词：remain single, stay single


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remarries every few years. = 他每隔几年就再婚。
They remarry after a long separation. = 他们经过长时间的分离后再次结婚。

一般过去时:
She remarried last year. = 她去年再婚了。
He remarried quickly after his divorce. = 他离婚后很快又结婚了。

现在完成时:
They have remarried three times. = 他们已经再婚三次了。
She has remarried and is very happy. = 她已经再婚了，并且非常幸福。

过去完成时:
He had remarried before she even met him. = 在她认识他之前他就已经再婚了。
They had remarried several times before finding lasting happiness. = 在找到持久的幸福之前，他们已经再婚好几次了。

将来时:
I think she will remarry someday. = 我认为她总有一天会再婚。
He says he will not remarry. = 他说他不会再婚。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it requires a willing participant.)


疑问句:
Will he remarry soon? = 他会很快再婚吗？
Has she remarried since her divorce? = 她离婚后再婚了吗？


否定句:
She did not remarry after her divorce. = 她离婚后没有再婚。
He will not remarry.  = 他不会再婚。
