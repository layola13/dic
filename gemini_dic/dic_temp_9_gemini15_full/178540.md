reconvictions:/ˌriːkənˈvɪkʃənz/| n.|再定罪，再次判罪|The act of convicting someone again for the same crime, often after an appeal or a retrial.  This usually implies that the initial conviction was overturned or deemed invalid.|例句：His reconvictions highlight the flaws in the initial judicial process. 他的再次定罪凸显了最初司法程序中的缺陷。
例句：The number of reconvictions for drug-related offenses has increased recently. 近期与毒品相关的再次定罪案件数量有所增加。|近义词：re-convictions, repeat convictions|反义词：acquittals, exonerations|名词
