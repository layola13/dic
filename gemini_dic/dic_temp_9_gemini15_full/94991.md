in-the-way:/ɪn ðə weɪ/| adj.|挡道的；妨碍的；碍事的|Something or someone that is in the way is obstructing or hindering progress or movement.  It can refer to physical objects or abstract obstacles.|例句：The boxes were in the way, so I moved them. 箱子挡道，所以我把它们挪开了。

例句：His negativity was in the way of his success. 他的消极态度妨碍了他的成功。

近义词：obstructing, hindering, impeding, blocking|反义词：out of the way, unobstructed, clear
