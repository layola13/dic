observations:/ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃənz/ /ˌɑːbzərˈveɪʃənz/ | n. |观察；观察结果；备注|The act of carefully watching someone or something.  The things you notice; remarks.|例句：His observations were insightful and helpful. 他的观察深刻而有益。

例句：The scientist recorded his observations meticulously in his notebook. 科学家一丝不苟地将他的观察记录在笔记本上。


近义词：notes, remarks, comments, findings, scrutinies|反义词：inferences, deductions, assumptions|词性：名词
