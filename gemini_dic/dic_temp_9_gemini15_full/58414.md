duboisine:/ˌduːbɔɪˈziːn/| n. |颠茄碱|A tropane alkaloid found in various plants of the Solanaceae family, including Duboisia species. It has similar effects to atropine, causing dilation of pupils and inhibiting parasympathetic nerve function.  |例句：Duboisine is used in ophthalmology to dilate pupils for examinations.  颠茄碱用于眼科扩张瞳孔进行检查。|近义词：atropine, hyoscyamine|反义词：无直接反义词，但其作用可以被抗胆碱酯酶药物拮抗。|名词
