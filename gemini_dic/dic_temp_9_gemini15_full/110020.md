librettists:/lɪˈbrɛtɪsts/ | n. | 剧本作家；歌词创作人 | A librettist is a person who writes the text for an opera, musical, or other musical work.  They craft the story, dialogue, and lyrics that are then set to music. | Example: The librettists of the new opera collaborated closely with the composer.  新的歌剧的剧本作家与作曲家密切合作。 | 近义词:  lyricist, scriptwriter, text writer | 反义词: composer (反义词并非完全对立，而是指创作不同部分的人)
