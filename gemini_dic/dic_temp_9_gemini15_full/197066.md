shamble:/ˈʃæmbəl/| v. n.|蹒跚而行；杂乱无章的状态；|to walk awkwardly, with a shuffling gait; a disordered or disorganized state |例句：He shambled into the room, looking tired and dishevelled. 他疲惫不堪，衣衫不整地蹒跚走进房间。  The old house was a complete shamble.  这座老房子完全是一团糟。|近义词：stumble, shuffle, hobble, mess|反义词：stride, march, strut, order
