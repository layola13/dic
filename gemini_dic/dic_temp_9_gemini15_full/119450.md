micromanage:/ˈmaɪkrəʊˌmænɪdʒ/| v.| 微观管理，事无巨细地管理 | To manage something or someone with excessive control and attention to minor details.  |例句：The CEO micromanaged every aspect of the project, stifling creativity and slowing down progress.  首席执行官事无巨细地管理项目的各个方面，扼杀了创造力并减缓了进度。

例句：He tends to micromanage his employees, which leads to low morale. 他倾向于对员工进行微观管理，这导致士气低落。


近义词：oversee, supervise, control, direct, oversee minutely

反义词：delegate, empower, trust

词源:
The word "micromanage" is a relatively recent coinage, a blend of "micro" (meaning small or tiny) and "manage."  The "micro" prefix emphasizes the extremely detailed and often excessively controlling nature of the management style.  It emerged in the late 20th century to describe a specific type of managerial approach.
