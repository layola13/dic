phytochemical:/ˌfaɪtoʊˈkɛmɪkəl/| n. |植物化学物质|A chemical compound produced by a plant.  Phytochemicals are not considered essential nutrients, but many have biological activity and may provide health benefits.  |Example:  Many fruits and vegetables contain phytochemicals that act as antioxidants. 许多水果和蔬菜含有作为抗氧化剂的植物化学物质。 |近义词：plant chemical, botanical compound |反义词：无
