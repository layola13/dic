headphone:/ˈhɛdfoʊn/ /ˈhedfəʊn/| n. |耳机|A device that you wear over your ears to listen to music or other audio.  Headphones typically consist of two small speakers, one for each ear, connected by a band that fits over the head. They allow for private listening without disturbing others.| I listen to music with my headphones every morning. 我每天早上都戴着耳机听音乐。 I need a new pair of headphones; these ones are broken. 我需要一副新的耳机；这副坏了。|earphones, headset|speaker(反义词，并非完全反义，但功能相反)|名词
