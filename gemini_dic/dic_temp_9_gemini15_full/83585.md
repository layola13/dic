haematocrystallin: /ˌhiːmætəˈkrɪstəlɪn/ | n. | 血晶体蛋白 |  A protein found in the lens of the eye, particularly in certain animals.  It is involved in maintaining the transparency and refractive index of the lens.  The term combines "haemato-" (relating to blood) and "-crystallin" (referring to crystalline proteins found in the lens).  |  Research on haematocrystallin focuses on its role in lens transparency and the potential for understanding eye diseases.  | 近义词: lens protein, crystallin (general term)| 反义词: N/A
