ornamentalize:/ɔːrnəˈmɛntəlaɪz/ | vt. | 装饰；使…显得华丽 | To decorate or adorn something excessively; to make something ostentatious or showy.  | The architect ornamentalized the building with excessive carvings and gilding. 建筑师用过多的雕刻和镀金来装饰这座建筑物。  | embellish, decorate, adorn | simplify, strip, plain | 动词


USAGE->

He ornamentalizes his garden with colorful flowers. = 他用色彩鲜艳的花朵装饰他的花园。
She ornamentalizes her writing with unnecessary adjectives. = 她用不必要的形容词来修饰她的写作。
