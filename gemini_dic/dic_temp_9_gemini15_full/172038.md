purism:/ˈpjʊərɪzəm/| n.|纯粹主义，纯洁主义|The belief in and practice of maintaining the purity of a language or style by avoiding foreign borrowings and adhering strictly to established rules and forms. It often involves resisting changes and innovations, seeking to preserve a perceived ideal form.|例句：His purism led him to reject all modern slang and colloquialisms. 他的纯粹主义使他拒绝所有现代俚语和口语。|近义词：purist, perfectionism|反义词：eclecticism, innovation
