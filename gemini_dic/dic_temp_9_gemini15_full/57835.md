dressers:/ˈdrɛsərz/| n. |梳妆台；衣柜；(尤指旧式)碗柜|A dresser is a piece of furniture with drawers, typically used for storing clothes or other items.  It can also refer to a piece of furniture used in a dining room to store dishes and silverware.|例句：She keeps her jewelry in the top drawer of her dresser. 她把首饰放在梳妆台的抽屉里。|例句：The antique dresser was a family heirloom. 这件古董梳妆台是传家宝。|近义词：chest of drawers, bureau, wardrobe (if used for clothes)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with open shelving or a closet.
