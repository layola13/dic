hippy:/ˈhɪpi/| n. adj.|嬉皮士；嬉皮士的；|A hippy is a person, especially young, who rejects the established social norms and values, often adopting a counterculture lifestyle characterized by peace, love, and freedom.  The term often carries connotations of drug use, unconventional clothing, and communal living. |例句：The hippies of the 1960s championed peace and love.  六十年代的嬉皮士们倡导和平与爱。

例句：She wore brightly colored clothes, typical of a hippy. 她穿着色彩鲜艳的衣服，这是嬉皮士的典型装扮。|近义词：free spirit, bohemian, flower child |反义词：conformist, traditionalist, establishment
