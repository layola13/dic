connect-with: /kəˈnɛkt wɪð/ | v. | 联系；与…建立联系；与…产生共鸣 | To establish a relationship or connection with someone or something; to feel a sense of understanding or empathy with someone or something. |例句：I hope to connect with my audience through my speech.我希望通过我的演讲与听众建立联系。  I really connected with the character in the book. 我真的很能理解书中的人物。|近义词：relate to, engage with, empathize with | 反义词：disconnect from, alienate from


USAGE->

She connects with her roots through her art. = 她通过艺术与自己的根源产生联系。
