plantsmen:/ˈplæntsmen/ | n. | 植物专家，园艺家 | A plantsman is a person with expert knowledge of plants, especially cultivated plants.  They are often deeply involved in the cultivation, propagation, and study of plants.  Their expertise goes beyond mere gardening; it involves a profound understanding of plant taxonomy, horticulture, and botany. |  He's a renowned plantsman, specializing in rare orchids. 他是一位著名的植物专家，专门研究稀有兰花。  The plantsman carefully selected the specimens for his display garden.这位植物专家仔细挑选了用于展示花园的标本。|  horticulturist, botanist, gardener |  (no direct antonym)
