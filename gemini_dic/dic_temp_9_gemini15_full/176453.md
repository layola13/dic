re-maps:/ˌriːˈmæps/| vt.|重新映射；重新规划|To create a new map or plan of something, typically a geographical area or a computer system.|例句：The software re-maps the keyboard layout to accommodate different languages.  软件重新映射键盘布局以适应不同的语言。|例句：The cartographers re-mapped the coastline after the earthquake. 地理学家在地震后重新绘制了海岸线。|近义词：remap, redraw, replan|反义词：map (without re-)
