homeroom:/ˈhoʊmruːm/ | n. | 辅导课；班主任办公室；(美国)班主任的课 | A homeroom is a classroom where a class of students meets at the beginning of the school day for announcements, attendance taking, and sometimes brief instruction.  It's often the same classroom used by the students' main teacher for other subjects. In some schools, it may be a designated room for a specific class or grade level. | Example: My homeroom teacher is Mr. Jones. 我的班主任是琼斯先生。  |  classroom, advisory period |  --- | noun
