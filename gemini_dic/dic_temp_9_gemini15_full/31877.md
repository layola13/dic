cheerless:/ˈtʃɪərləs/| adj. |无精打采的；令人沮丧的；毫无生气| lacking in cheer; depressing; gloomy.  |The cheerless room felt cold and uninviting.  这间毫无生气的房间感觉又冷又不好客。The weather was cheerless and rainy. 天气阴沉多雨。| gloomy, depressing, dismal, bleak | cheerful, bright, lively, vibrant
