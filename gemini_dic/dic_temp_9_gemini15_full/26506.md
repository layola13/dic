cabanas:/kəˈbɑːnəz/| n. |简易小屋；小木屋；凉亭|A cabana is a small, often open-air structure, typically found on beaches or near swimming pools, providing shade and sometimes other amenities.|例句：We rented a cabana by the pool for the day. 我们租了一天泳池边的简易小屋。|例句：The resort offers luxurious cabanas with private butler service. 度假村提供带私人管家服务的豪华简易小屋。|近义词：hut, shelter, kiosk|反义词:mansion, palace|名词

词源:
The word "cabana" originates from the Spanish word "cabaña," meaning "hut" or "cabin."  The Spanish word itself likely derives from the Latin word "capanna," also meaning "hut" or "shelter."
