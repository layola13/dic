gulf:/ɡʌlf/| n.|海湾，深渊，鸿沟|A gulf is a large area of sea that is partly enclosed by land.  It can also refer to a large difference between two things or people.|例句：The Persian Gulf is a vital waterway. 波斯湾是一条重要的水道。|例句：There's a gulf between the rich and the poor. 富人和穷人之间存在着巨大的差距。|近义词：bay, chasm, abyss, divide|反义词：bridge, connection, unity|名词
