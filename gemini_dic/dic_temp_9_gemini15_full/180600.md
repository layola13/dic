registrations:/ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃənz/| n. |注册；登记；注册人数|The act of registering or the state of being registered;  the number of people or things registered.  It often refers to a collection or list of registered items or individuals. |例句：The conference registrations are high this year.今年会议的注册人数很高。|例句：Online registrations for the marathon closed last week. 马拉松赛的在线注册上周关闭了。|近义词：enrollments, entries, sign-ups |反义词：deregistrations, cancellations
