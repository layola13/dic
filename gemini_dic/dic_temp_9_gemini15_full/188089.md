roundedness: /ˈraʊndɪdnəs/ | n. | 圆润；圆满；完整性 | The quality of being round or having a full, smooth shape; completeness or wholeness.  It can also refer to a well-developed, harmonious, or complete quality in something. | 例句：The sculptor admired the roundedness of the statue's curves.  雕塑家欣赏雕像曲线圆润的形态。

The roundedness of her personality made her a joy to be around.  她性格的圆满使她成为一个令人愉快的人。| 近义词：fullness, completeness, roundness, smoothness | 反义词：angularity, jaggedness, incompleteness
