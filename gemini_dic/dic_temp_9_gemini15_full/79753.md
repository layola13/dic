glumpy:/ˈɡlʌmpi/| adj.|脾气暴躁的；阴沉的；不高兴的|bad-tempered and irritable; sullen|例句：He was glumpy all morning after losing the game.  他输了比赛后，整个上午都闷闷不乐。|例句：Don't be so glumpy; it's a beautiful day! 不要这么愁眉苦脸的；今天天气很好！|近义词：grumpy, sullen, grumpy, surly, morose|反义词：cheerful, happy, jovial, merry


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is always glumpy in the morning. = 他早上总是脾气暴躁。
She is glumpy because it's raining. = 她因为下雨而闷闷不乐。

一般过去时:
He was glumpy yesterday after the argument. = 争吵后，他昨天脾气很坏。
She was glumpy because she failed the test. = 她因为考试不及格而闷闷不乐。

现在完成时:
He has been glumpy all week. = 他整个星期都脾气暴躁。
She has been glumpy ever since she lost her job. = 自从失业以来，她一直闷闷不乐。

过去完成时:
He had been glumpy for days before he finally told us what was wrong. = 在他最终告诉我们出了什么事之前，他已经闷闷不乐了好几天了。
She had been glumpy all afternoon before the good news arrived. = 好消息传来之前，她整个下午都闷闷不乐。

将来时:
He will be glumpy if the team loses again. = 如果球队再次输掉比赛，他会很生气。
She will probably be glumpy tomorrow if she doesn't get enough sleep tonight. = 如果今晚睡不好，她明天可能会很生气。

虚拟语气:
If he weren't so glumpy, he would have more friends. = 如果他不那么脾气暴躁，他就会有更多朋友。
I wish she weren't so glumpy all the time. = 我希望她不要总是那么闷闷不乐。

被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice.)  *His mood was described as glumpy.* = 他的心情被形容为暴躁的。

疑问句:
Is he always so glumpy? = 他总是这么脾气暴躁吗？
Was she glumpy because of the weather? = 她是因为天气不好才闷闷不乐的吗？


否定句:
He wasn't glumpy at all; he was just tired. = 他一点也不生气；他只是累了。
She wasn't glumpy; she was just concentrating. = 她不是生气；她只是在专心致志。
