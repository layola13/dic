self-unworthiness: /ˌsɛlf ʌnˈwɜːðɪnəs/ | n. | 自卑感，自我价值感低 | A feeling of being inadequate or undeserving of good things.  It is a deep-seated belief that one is not worthy of love, happiness, success, or any positive experience.  This feeling often stems from past experiences, negative self-talk, or societal pressures. |  Her self-unworthiness prevented her from pursuing her dreams. 她自卑感让她无法追求自己的梦想。 He struggled with feelings of self-unworthiness after losing his job.  失去工作后，他饱受自卑感的折磨。|近义词：low self-esteem, inferiority complex, inadequacy, self-doubt |反义词：self-worth, self-esteem, confidence, self-acceptance | 名词
