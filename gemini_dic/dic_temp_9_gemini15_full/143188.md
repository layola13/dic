outcompete:/ˌaʊt kəmˈpiːt/| vt.|胜过，竞争获胜|To surpass or defeat in competition.|The smaller company managed to outcompete its larger rivals.这家较小的公司设法击败了其较大的竞争对手。|surpass, exceed, outdo|fail to compete, lose to|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outcompete their rivals in the market. = 他们在市场上胜过竞争对手。
Our company outcompetes others through innovation. = 我们公司通过创新胜过其他公司。


一般过去时:
He outcompeted all other candidates for the job. = 他在求职竞争中胜过了所有其他候选人。
The local bakery outcompeted the chain stores. = 当地的面包店战胜了连锁店。


现在完成时:
They have outcompeted all their competitors in the industry. = 他们在业界已战胜所有竞争对手。
Our team has outcompeted the others in several races. = 我们的团队在几场比赛中都胜过了其他队伍。


过去完成时:
She had outcompeted her brother in every academic competition. = 她曾在每场学术竞赛中都胜过她的哥哥。
The small startup had outcompeted the established players in the market. = 这家小型初创公司已在市场上胜过了老牌企业。


将来时:
We believe we will outcompete our competitors with our new technology. = 我们相信凭借新技术，我们将胜过竞争对手。
They predict they will outcompete other bidders for the contract. = 他们预测他们将在竞标中胜过其他投标者。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, we might have outcompeted them. = 如果我们投资更多，我们可能会胜过他们。
If they worked harder, they could have outcompeted their rivals. = 如果他们更努力工作，他们本可以胜过竞争对手。


被动语态:
The smaller firm was outcompeted by the larger corporation. = 这家较小的公司被更大的公司击败了。
Our strategy was outcompeted by their aggressive marketing. = 我们的策略被他们积极的营销策略击败了。


疑问句:
Can we really outcompete the established players in this market? = 我们真的能在市场上击败老牌企业吗？
Will our new product outcompete existing options? = 我们的新产品会胜过现有产品吗？


否定句:
We failed to outcompete the established companies in the market. = 我们未能在这个市场上击败老牌公司。
The new product did not outcompete the existing ones. = 新产品没有胜过现有产品。


PHRASE->
outcompete the market = 占据市场主导地位
outcompete rivals = 击败竞争对手
outcompete in the industry = 在行业中胜出
outcompete on price = 价格竞争获胜
outcompete through innovation = 通过创新获胜
