recital:/rɪˈsaɪtl/ | n. |独奏会；朗诵会；背诵；叙述 | A public performance of music or poetry, especially one given by a single performer.  |例句：The pianist gave a brilliant recital of Chopin's works.  钢琴家精彩地演奏了肖邦的作品。 |例句：She gave a moving recital of her grandmother's life. 她动情地讲述了她祖母的一生。|近义词：performance, presentation, reading |反义词：N/A


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The pianist gives a recital every month. = 钢琴家每月都举行一次独奏会。
He recites poetry at the recital. = 他在朗诵会上朗诵诗歌。

一般过去时:
She gave a beautiful piano recital last night. = 她昨晚举行了一场精彩的钢琴独奏会。
They recited Shakespeare in the recital. = 他们在朗诵会上背诵莎士比亚的作品。

现在完成时:
The student has given several recitals this semester. = 这个学生这学期已经举办了几场独奏会。
She has recited that poem many times. = 她已经多次朗诵过那首诗。

过去完成时:
He had already finished his recital before the audience arrived. = 在观众到达之前，他已经完成了他的独奏。
They had memorized the poems before the recital. = 他们在朗诵会之前已经记住了那些诗歌。

将来时:
She will give a recital next week. = 她下周将举行一场独奏会。
The students will recite their poems at the school assembly. = 学生们将在学校集会上朗诵他们的诗歌。

虚拟语气:
If I were a better musician, I would give a recital. = 如果我是一位更好的音乐家，我会举行一场独奏会。
If she had practiced more, her recital would have been better. = 如果她多练习一些，她的独奏会就会更好。

被动语态:
The recital was well-received by the audience. = 这场独奏会受到了观众的好评。
The poems were recited beautifully. = 诗歌被优美地朗诵出来。

疑问句:
When is the recital? = 独奏会是什么时候？
Will you attend the poetry recital? = 你会参加诗歌朗诵会吗？

否定句:
He did not give a recital last year. = 他去年没有举行独奏会。
She did not recite the poem correctly. = 她没有正确地朗诵那首诗。
