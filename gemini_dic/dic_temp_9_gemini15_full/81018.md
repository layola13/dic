gradualists:/ˈɡrædʒuəlɪsts/| n.|渐变论者，渐进主义者|Advocates of gradualism, a theory or policy of gradual change rather than sudden revolution.  They believe in slow, incremental progress toward a goal.|例句：The gradualists argued for a phased approach to economic reform, avoiding radical change. 渐变论者主张采取分阶段的方式进行经济改革，避免激进的变革。|例句：Many environmentalists disagree with the gradualists' approach to climate change, arguing for immediate and drastic action.许多环保主义者不同意渐进主义者应对气候变化的方法，他们主张立即采取严厉措施。|近义词：incrementalists, evolutionists|反义词：revolutionaries, radicals|名词
