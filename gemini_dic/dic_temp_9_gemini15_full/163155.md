precommitting: /ˌpriːkəˈmɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先承诺；预先承担义务 |  The act of making a commitment in advance.  It often involves binding oneself to a future action or decision before the necessary information or circumstances are fully known. This can be risky but sometimes necessary for strategic reasons. |


例句：
The company is precommitting to investing in renewable energy sources. = 公司预先承诺投资可再生能源。

The general precommitted his troops to a risky offensive. = 将军预先承诺他的部队进行一次冒险的进攻。


近义词：pledging, committing beforehand, promising in advance


反义词：postponing, delaying, hesitating


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He precommits to finishing the project by Friday. = 他预先承诺在周五之前完成项目。
They precommit resources to the new initiative. = 他们预先承诺将资源投入新的计划。

一般过去时:
She precommitted herself to donating a significant amount of money. = 她预先承诺捐赠一大笔钱。
The team precommitted to a specific strategy. = 团队预先承诺采用特定策略。

现在完成时:
We have precommitted to supporting the local community. = 我们已经预先承诺支持当地社区。
They have precommitted to a long-term partnership. = 他们已经预先承诺建立长期伙伴关系。

过去完成时:
He had precommitted to attending the conference, so he couldn't cancel. = 他已经预先承诺参加会议，所以他无法取消。
The company had precommitted to a major expansion, even before the market downturn. =  即使在市场低迷之前，公司就已经预先承诺进行大规模扩张。


将来时:
They will precommit to delivering the goods within two weeks. = 他们将预先承诺在两周内交付货物。
She will precommit to a specific course of action. = 她将预先承诺采取具体的行动方案。

虚拟语气:
If he precommitted to the deal, he would have a better negotiating position. = 如果他预先承诺达成交易，他将拥有更好的谈判地位。
I wish I had precommitted to a healthier lifestyle earlier. = 我希望我早些时候就预先承诺过更健康的生活方式。

被动语态:
The funds were precommitted to the research project. = 资金已预先承诺用于该研究项目。
The resources have been precommitted to the emergency response. = 资源已预先承诺用于紧急响应。

疑问句:
Has the government precommitted to any specific policies? = 政府是否已经预先承诺任何具体的政策？
Will they precommit to funding the project in full? = 他们会预先承诺全额资助该项目吗？


否定句:
The company did not precommit to a specific timeline. = 公司没有预先承诺具体的期限。
They have not precommitted to any further investments. = 他们尚未预先承诺任何进一步的投资。
