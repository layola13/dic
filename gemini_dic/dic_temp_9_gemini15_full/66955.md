experiential:/ˌɛkspɪriˈɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 体验的；经验的；根据经验的 | Relating to or based on experience.  Experiential learning emphasizes learning through doing and reflection on that experience. |  例句：The course offers an experiential learning approach, focusing on hands-on projects. 这门课程提供了一种体验式学习方法，重点是动手项目。

例句：Her experiential knowledge of the industry proved invaluable to the team. 她丰富的行业经验对团队来说非常宝贵。


近义词：empirical, practical, lived


反义词：theoretical, abstract, hypothetical


词性:形容词
