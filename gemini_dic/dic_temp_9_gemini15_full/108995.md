left-winger:/ˈlɛftwɪŋər/| n. |左翼分子，左倾人士|A person who holds left-wing political views, typically advocating for social equality and often supporting government intervention in the economy.|例句：Many left-wingers advocate for universal healthcare. 许多左翼分子倡导全民医疗。|例句：The left-wingers protested against the government's austerity measures. 左翼分子抗议政府的紧缩措施。|近义词：radical, socialist, progressive |反义词：right-winger, conservative
