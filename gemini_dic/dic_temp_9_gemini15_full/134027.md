nonhunter:/ˈnɑːnˌhʌntər/| n. |非猎人；不打猎的人|A person who does not hunt.  This term describes someone who does not participate in hunting activities, either by choice or circumstance. It's a descriptive term rather than a formal category. |Example: The nonhunter watched the hunters return with their deer.  非猎人看着猎人带着鹿回来。 The survey revealed a growing number of nonhunters in the rural community.  调查显示农村社区中不打猎的人越来越多。|近义词：non-hunter, nonparticipant (in hunting) |反义词：hunter
