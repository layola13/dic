federal:/ˈfɛdərəl/| adj.|联邦的；联邦政府的；联盟的|Relating to or denoting the central government of a federation.  It also describes things that are done or organized by the federal government.|例句：The federal government is responsible for national defense. 联邦政府负责国防。

例句：He works for a federal agency. 他在联邦机构工作。


近义词：national, central, governmental|反义词：state, local, regional|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *federal, borrowed from Late Latin *foederālis, from Latin foedus (“treaty, agreement, covenant”), related to foedus (“wicked, foul”).  The sense development is from “treaty” to “league, federation” to “relating to a federation.”
