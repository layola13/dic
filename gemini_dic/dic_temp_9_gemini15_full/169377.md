pronator:/ˈproʊneɪtər/| n. |旋前肌，使旋前者|A muscle that causes pronation (rotation of the forearm so that the palm faces downward).  It refers specifically to the muscles responsible for turning the hand and forearm inward. |The pronator teres is a major muscle involved in pronation. 旋前圆肌是参与旋前的主要肌肉之一。|近义词：rotator (in this context)|反义词：supinator
