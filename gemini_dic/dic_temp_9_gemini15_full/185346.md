retro-futurist: /ˌrɛtroʊˈfjuːtʃərɪst/ | adj. |复古未来主义的|  Retro-futurist describes a style or aesthetic that combines elements of the past with futuristic or science fiction themes. It often evokes a nostalgic vision of the future as imagined in earlier eras, typically the mid-20th century.  This style frequently features retro technology, clothing, and design blended with futuristic concepts. |例句：The retro-futurist artwork depicted flying cars and spaceships in a 1950s setting.  复古未来主义的艺术作品描绘了在20世纪50年代的背景下飞行的汽车和宇宙飞船。

The retro-futurist fashion collection showcased sleek, streamlined silhouettes reminiscent of the 1960s space age, but with modern materials and technology.  这个复古未来主义的时装系列展示了让人联想起20世纪60年代太空时代的简洁流畅的轮廓，但使用了现代材料和技术。

词源:
The term "retro-futurist" is a portmanteau, combining "retro" (from Latin *retro, meaning "backward") and "futurist" (referring to Futurism, an early 20th-century artistic and social movement that celebrated technology and speed).  The prefix "retro-" indicates a looking back to a past vision of the future.
