self-abnegations:/ˌsɛlf æbˈneɪɡeɪʃənz/| n. |自我否定；自我克制；自我牺牲|The act of denying oneself something, especially desires or pleasures.  It often implies a renunciation for a higher purpose or ideal.  The plural form indicates multiple instances of this behavior.|例句：Her self-abnegations were evident in her simple lifestyle and dedication to charity.  她的简朴生活方式和对慈善事业的奉献体现了她的自我牺牲。

例句：His years of self-abnegations in the pursuit of scientific knowledge finally bore fruit. 他多年来为了追求科学知识而做出的自我牺牲最终结出了硕果。


近义词：self-denial, self-sacrifice, renunciation, austerity, mortification


反义词：self-indulgence, self-gratification, hedonism


词性：名词
