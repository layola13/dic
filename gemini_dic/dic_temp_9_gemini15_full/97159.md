infinitude:/ɪnˈfɪnɪtuːd/| n. |无限，无穷大 |The infinitude of the universe is a concept that has fascinated humans for centuries.  宇宙的无限性是几个世纪以来一直吸引着人类的概念。|近义词：boundlessness, immensity, eternity |反义词：finiteness, limitation


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, grammatical tenses such as past, present, future, etc., are not applicable.  The word itself expresses a state of being.
