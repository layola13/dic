no-hopers:/ˈnoʊˌhoʊpərz/| n. |不可救药的人，没希望的人 |People who are considered unlikely to succeed or improve.  They are often seen as lazy, incompetent, or lacking the necessary skills or motivation. |例句：He wrote them off as no-hopers. 他把他们看作是没救的人。

They considered the project a no-hoper from the start. 他们从一开始就认为这个项目没有希望。


近义词：failures, lost causes, dead ends, hopeless cases |反义词：high-fliers, achievers, successes
