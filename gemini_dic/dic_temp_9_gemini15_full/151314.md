parings:/ˈpɛərɪŋz/| n.|削皮;配对;成对的东西|The act of paring or the state of being pared;  something that is pared, especially the peelings of fruit or vegetables.  It can also refer to a pair or pairs of things. |例句：The apple parings were composted. 苹果皮被堆肥了。|例句：The parings of the potatoes were discarded. 土豆削下来的皮被丢弃了。|近义词：peelings, pairings off, matches|反义词：wholes, unpaired


USAGE->

He pares apples every morning. 他每天早上削苹果。
She pairs socks before washing them. 她洗袜子之前先把袜子配对。
