pyrologist:/paɪˈrɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 火学家 | A scientist who studies fire and its effects.  A pyrologist specializes in the chemical and physical properties of combustion, fire prevention, and fire investigation. | The pyrologist determined the cause of the fire to be arson. 火学家认定火灾原因是纵火。|  fire expert, fire scientist |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
