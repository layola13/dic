congruent:/ˈkɒŋɡruənt/ /ˈkɑːŋɡruənt/ | adj. |全等的；一致的；和谐的 |Having the same size and shape; corresponding in character or kind.  |例句：The two triangles are congruent. 这两个三角形全等。

例句：His actions were congruent with his beliefs. 他的行为与他的信仰一致。


近义词：identical, consistent, harmonious | 反义词：dissimilar, inconsistent, discordant
