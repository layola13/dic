faint-heartedly:/feɪntˈhɑːtɪdli/| adv. | 胆怯地；懦弱地；缺乏勇气地 |  The adverb "faint-heartedly" describes an action or behavior performed with a lack of courage, confidence, or determination. It suggests hesitation, timidity, and a tendency to avoid challenges. |例句：He faint-heartedly approached the task, constantly doubting his abilities. 他胆怯地着手这项任务，不断怀疑自己的能力。
She faint-heartedly agreed to the proposal, fearing the consequences of refusal. 她懦弱地同意了这个提议，害怕拒绝的后果。| 近义词：timidly, cowardly, weakly, fearfully | 反义词：boldly, bravely, courageously, confidently |  无
