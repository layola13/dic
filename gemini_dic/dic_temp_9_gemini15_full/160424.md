pops:/pɑːps/| n., v.|砰的一声；轻微爆炸声；突然出现；（尤指父母）|A sudden, short burst of sound, like a small explosion; to appear suddenly; (especially of parents) to come and go unexpectedly.|例句：The champagne cork popped. 香槟酒塞砰的一声开了。 The singer popped onto the stage. 歌手突然出现在舞台上。|近义词：bang, crackle, burst, appear |反义词：disappear, vanish


USAGE->
