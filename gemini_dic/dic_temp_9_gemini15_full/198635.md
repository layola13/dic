shushes:/ʃʌʃɪz/| v.|嘘，使…安静|To make someone be quiet by saying "shush".  It can also be used as a noun referring to the sound of shushing.|例句：She shushed the children to quiet them down. 她嘘孩子们让他们安静下来。

例句：The librarian shushed the noisy students. 图书管理员嘘了那些吵闹的学生。


近义词：hush, quiet, silence|反义词：encourage, excite

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shushes the baby. = 他嘘着婴儿。
She shushes the noisy crowd. = 她让吵闹的人群安静下来。

一般过去时:
He shushed the baby to sleep. = 他嘘着婴儿睡觉。
The teacher shushed the class. = 老师让全班安静下来。

现在完成时:
She has shushed the children several times. = 她已经好几次嘘孩子们了。
He has shushed the noisy neighbours. = 他已经让吵闹的邻居安静下来了。

过去完成时:
The teacher had shushed the class before the principal arrived. = 校长来之前老师已经让全班安静下来了。
She had shushed the baby many times before it finally fell asleep. = 在婴儿最终睡着之前，她已经嘘过婴儿很多次了。

将来时:
I will shush the children if they are too noisy. = 如果孩子们太吵闹，我会嘘他们。
She will shush him if he interrupts again. = 如果他再次打断，她会嘘他。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would shush them. = 如果我是你，我会嘘他们。
I wish I could shush the whole world. = 我真希望我能让全世界安静下来。

被动语态:
The children were shushed by their mother. = 孩子们被他们的母亲嘘了。
The audience was shushed by the usher. = 观众被引座员嘘了。

疑问句:
Will you shush the baby? = 你会嘘婴儿吗？
Should I shush him? = 我应该嘘他吗？

否定句:
I didn't shush him because he wasn't being disruptive. = 我没有嘘他，因为他并没有捣乱。
She didn't shush the children; she simply ignored them. = 她没有嘘孩子们；她只是无视他们。


PHRASE->
