fowl:/faʊl/ | n. |家禽；禽类；鸟；猎鸟 | Any bird, especially a domesticated bird raised for meat or eggs.  It can also refer to birds hunted for sport or food. | 例句：We had roast fowl for dinner. = 我们晚餐吃了烤鸡。

The farmer raises various kinds of fowl. = 这位农民饲养各种家禽。


近义词：poultry, bird, game bird | 反义词：mammal


词源:  From Old English *fugol, from Proto-Germanic *fuglaz, from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂w- ("to fly").  The modern English word retains the older broader sense of "bird" in some contexts while specialized to domesticated birds in others.
