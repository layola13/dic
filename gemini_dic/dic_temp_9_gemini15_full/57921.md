drills:/drɪlz/| n. vt.|钻，练习，操练|A drill is a tool used to make holes, or a repetitive exercise to improve a skill.|例句：The carpenter used a drill to make holes in the wood. 木匠用钻头在木头上钻孔。|例句：The soldiers practiced their drills every morning. 士兵们每天早上进行操练。|近义词：exercises, practices, repetitions|反义词：relaxation, rest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drills holes in the wall. = 他在墙上钻孔。
She drills the students regularly. = 她定期给学生们进行练习。

一般过去时:
They drilled a well for water. = 他们打了一口井取水。
The teacher drilled the vocabulary with her students. = 老师和学生们一起练习词汇。

现在完成时:
We have drilled several holes already. = 我们已经钻了好几个孔了。
They have drilled the team hard for the upcoming game. = 他们为了即将到来的比赛而对球队进行了严格的训练。

过去完成时:
They had drilled the well before the drought. = 干旱之前他们已经挖好了井。
She had drilled the piano piece many times before the concert. = 音乐会之前她已经练习过很多次这首钢琴曲了。

将来时:
We will drill a new well next week. = 我们下周将要打一口新井。
He will drill his presentation until he feels confident. = 他会一直练习他的演示文稿，直到他感觉自信为止。

虚拟语气:
If I had a drill, I would fix the shelf. = 如果我有一把钻头，我会修好架子。
If she had drilled more, she would have performed better. = 如果她练习得更多，她的表现会更好。

被动语态:
The holes were drilled by a machine. = 孔是用机器钻的。
The students were drilled on grammar rules. = 学生们接受了语法规则的训练。

疑问句:
What kind of drill do you use? = 你用什么类型的钻头？
How often do you drill your employees? = 你多久对你的员工进行一次培训？

否定句:
He didn't drill the hole deep enough. = 他没有把洞钻得足够深。
She didn't drill the team sufficiently for the competition. = 她没有对球队进行足够的训练来参加比赛。
