gazebo:/ɡəˈzeɪboʊ/| n.|凉亭；六角凉亭|An open structure, usually with a roof and often a decorative design, used as a shelter or resting place in a garden or park.  Gazebos are often hexagonal or octagonal in shape.|例句：We sat in the gazebo and enjoyed the beautiful sunset. 我们坐在凉亭里，欣赏美丽的日落。

例句：The park features a charming gazebo nestled amongst the flowers. 公园里有一个迷人的凉亭，坐落在鲜花之中。


近义词：pavilion, kiosk, summerhouse|反义词：None. |词性:名词
