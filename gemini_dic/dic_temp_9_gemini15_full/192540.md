scuddle:/ˈskʌdl/| v.|疾走，飞奔，匆忙行动|To move quickly and hurriedly, often in a sideways or shuffling manner.|The children scuddled across the playground to avoid the rain. 孩子们为了躲雨，飞快地跑过操场。The crab scuddled sideways into its hole.  螃蟹侧着身子迅速钻进洞里。|dash, hurry, scamper|walk slowly, stroll|动词
