horticultures:/ˈhɔːrtɪkʌltʃərz/ | n. | 园艺学（复数） | The science and art of cultivating fruits, vegetables, flowers, or ornamental plants.  Horticultures encompass a wide range of practices, from seed selection to pest control. | 例句：Modern horticultures utilize advanced technologies to improve crop yields and quality. 现代园艺学利用先进技术提高作物产量和质量。


The study of horticultures in different climates reveals fascinating adaptations of plants.  对不同气候下园艺学的学习揭示了植物引人入胜的适应性。


近义词：gardening (as a practice), plant cultivation, agriculture (broader term) | 反义词：N/A (no direct antonym) | 词性:名词
