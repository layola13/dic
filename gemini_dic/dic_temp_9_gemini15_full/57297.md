downsizes:/ˈdaʊnsaɪz/| vt.|缩减规模；裁员|To reduce the size or scope of something, especially a company or organization, often by reducing the number of employees.|例句：The company downsized its workforce by 10%.  公司裁员10%。|例句：The project was downsized due to budget constraints.  由于预算限制，项目规模缩减了。|近义词：reduce, shrink, cut back|反义词：expand, enlarge, increase|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company downsizes its operations every year. = 公司每年都会缩减运营规模。
He downsizes his expenses to save money. = 他缩减开支以省钱。

一般过去时:
They downsized their staff last quarter. = 上个季度他们裁员了。
The government downsized the project due to lack of funding. = 由于资金不足，政府缩减了项目规模。

现在完成时:
The company has downsized significantly in recent years. = 近年来，该公司已大幅缩减规模。
She has downsized her belongings after moving to a smaller house. = 她搬到小房子后就减少了她的行李。

过去完成时:
They had downsized the department before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经缩减了部门规模。
The company had downsized its marketing team before the product launch. = 在产品发布之前，公司已经缩减了其营销团队。

将来时:
They will downsize their workforce next year. = 他们明年将裁员。
The company will downsize its operations to improve efficiency. = 公司将缩减运营规模以提高效率。

虚拟语气:
If the economy worsened, they would downsize. = 如果经济恶化，他们就会裁员。
I wish they hadn't downsized the team. = 我真希望他们没有裁员。

被动语态:
The department was downsized last month. = 上个月部门被缩减了。
The project is being downsized due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，项目正在缩减规模。

疑问句:
Will the company downsize again this year? = 今年公司还会裁员吗？
Has the company downsized its marketing budget? = 公司是否缩减了营销预算？

否定句:
The company didn't downsize its workforce. = 公司没有裁员。
They haven't downsized their operations yet. = 他们还没有缩减运营规模。
