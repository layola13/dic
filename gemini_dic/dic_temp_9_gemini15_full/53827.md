It often refers to a lack of connection between people, ideas, or systems. |例句：The disconnectiveness between the government and the people led to widespread unrest.  政府与人民之间的脱节导致了广泛的不满。

The disconnectiveness in his speech made it difficult to follow his argument. 他演讲中的不连贯性使得难以理解他的论点。|近义词：disconnect, separation, fragmentation, discontinuity, disunity |反义词：connection, coherence, unity, integration, continuity
