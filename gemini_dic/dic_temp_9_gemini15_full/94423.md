impliedly:/ɪmˈplaɪdli/| adv.|暗含地；暗示地|In a way that is suggested but not directly expressed.  It suggests something without stating it explicitly.|例句：He impliedly criticized the government's policy. 他含蓄地批评了政府的政策。

例句：She impliedly agreed to the proposal by her silence. 她沉默不语，默认了这个提议。


近义词：implicitly, tacitly, indirectly, by implication


反义词：explicitly, directly, openly


词性:副词
