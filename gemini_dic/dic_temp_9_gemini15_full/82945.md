guna:/ˈɡuːnə/| n.|品质,属性,本性|In Sanskrit philosophy, a guna refers to a fundamental quality or attribute of reality.  There are three gunas: sattva (goodness), rajas (passion), and tamas (ignorance).|例句：The three gunas—sattva, rajas, and tamas—influence all aspects of existence. 三种属性——善性、热情和无明——影响存在的一切方面。|近义词：quality, attribute, characteristic |反义词：N/A
