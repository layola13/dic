hijacks:/ˈhaɪdʒæks/| vt.|劫持（飞机等），擅自利用（资源等）|To seize control of (a vehicle, especially an aircraft) during a journey, typically using force.|The terrorists hijacked the plane and demanded a ransom. 恐怖分子劫持了飞机并索要赎金。The company was accused of hijacking the technology. 公司被指控盗用这项技术。|seize, commandeer, usurp|release, surrender|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hijacks the conversation every time. 他每次都劫持谈话。
They hijack resources from other departments. 他们从其他部门窃取资源。

一般过去时:
The pirates hijacked the ship. 海盗劫持了那艘船。
She hijacked his idea for her presentation. 她剽窃了他的想法用于她的演示。

现在完成时:
Terrorists have hijacked many planes in the past. 恐怖分子过去劫持过许多飞机。
They have hijacked the public debate on this issue. 他们操纵了关于这个问题的公众辩论。

过去完成时:
The police had already arrived before the hijackers had escaped. 在劫机犯逃脱之前，警察已经到达了。
He had hijacked the project before anyone realized it. 他在任何人意识到之前就窃取了这个项目。

将来时:
They will hijack the election with their propaganda. 他们将用他们的宣传来操纵选举。
He will hijack the meeting to promote his own ideas. 他将劫持会议来推销他自己的想法。

虚拟语气:
If he hijacked the plane, he would be arrested. 如果他劫持飞机，他会遭到逮捕。
I wish I hadn't hijacked their project. 我真希望我没有窃取他们的项目。

被动语态:
The plane was hijacked by terrorists. 这架飞机被恐怖分子劫持了。
The project was hijacked by a rival company. 这个项目被一家竞争公司窃取了。

疑问句:
Was the train hijacked? 火车被劫持了吗？
Have they hijacked the funds? 他们是否挪用了资金？

否定句:
He did not hijack the car. 他没有劫持汽车。
They haven't hijacked the server. 他们没有劫持服务器。
