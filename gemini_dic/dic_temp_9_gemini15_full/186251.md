rhinos:/ˈraɪnoʊz/ | n. |犀牛 |The plural of rhinoceros.  Rhinos are large, herbivorous mammals with thick skin and one or two horns on their noses. They are native to Africa and Asia. |Example: We saw a herd of rhinos on our safari.  我们在大草原探险时看到一群犀牛。  There are several species of rhinos. 有几种犀牛。|近义词：rhinoceroses |反义词：无
