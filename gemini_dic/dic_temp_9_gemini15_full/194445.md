self-renounced: /ˌsɛlf rɪˈnaʊnst/ | adj. | 自我否定的；放弃自我的； त्याग किया हुआ |

英文解析: Describing someone who has rejected or given up their own desires, ambitions, or worldly possessions, often for religious or spiritual reasons.  It implies a deliberate and conscious act of renunciation.

例句:

He led a self-renounced life, devoting himself entirely to charitable work.  他过着自我否定的生活，完全致力于慈善事业。

The monk's self-renounced existence was a testament to his faith.  这位僧侣自我放弃的存在证明了他的信仰。


近义词: self-denying, ascetic, austere, abstemious, renunciative

反义词: self-indulgent, hedonistic, materialistic, self-seeking


词源:

* **self-**:  Prefix meaning "of or relating to oneself."  源自古英语 *self*.

* **renounced**: Past participle of the verb "renounce," meaning "to give up or reject (something valued)."  源自古法语 *renoncier*,  最终源自拉丁语 *renuntiare* ("to refuse, deny").  *re-* (again) + *nuntiare* (to announce).  The implication is a formal or public rejection.
