generation:/ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |一代；产生；生成 | A generation is a group of people born and living during the same period.  It can also refer to the act of producing or creating something. | Example: My generation grew up with the internet.  (我这一代是在互联网时代长大的。)  The power plant's generation of electricity has increased. (发电厂的发电量增加了。) |近义词：cohort, age group, creation, production | 反义词：destruction, annihilation |


USAGE->

The factory generates a lot of waste. = 这家工厂产生大量的废物。
