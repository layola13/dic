roughcasting:/ˈrʌfˌkæstɪŋ/| n. |粗略的抹灰；粗灰浆；|The application of a first coat of plaster or render to a wall, typically a rough and uneven layer that provides a base for subsequent finishing coats.|例句：The roughcasting on the exterior walls needs attention before painting. 外墙的粗抹灰需要在粉刷前进行处理。|近义词：rendering, plastering, pargetting |反义词：finishing, smoothing
