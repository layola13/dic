rapid:/ˈræpɪd/| adj.|快速的，迅速的|Moving or capable of moving at a high speed.  Characterized by speed or quickness.|例句：The river flowed with rapid currents. 河水奔流湍急。

例句：He made a rapid recovery from his illness. 他很快从病中恢复了。


近义词：swift, fast, quick, speedy, accelerated|反义词：slow, gradual, leisurely


词性:形容词
