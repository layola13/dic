manhunter:/ˈmænˌhʌntər/| n. | 人猎手；追捕犯人的警探 | A manhunter is someone, typically a police officer, who actively pursues and captures criminals.  They are often depicted as relentless and skilled in their pursuit of justice. | Example: The relentless manhunter finally cornered the notorious bank robber after a year-long chase.  这位不懈的追捕犯人的警探在长达一年的追捕后终于将臭名昭著的银行抢劫犯逼到了绝境。|近义词：crime fighter, detective, investigator, tracker|反义词：criminal, offender
