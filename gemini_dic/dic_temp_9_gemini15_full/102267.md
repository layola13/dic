itinerants:/aɪˈtɪnərənts/ | n. | 流动者，巡回者，旅行者 | People who travel from place to place, usually working temporarily at different locations.  Itinerants often lack a permanent home and move frequently in search of work or opportunity. | Example:  Many itinerant musicians travel the country, playing gigs in different cities. 许多巡回音乐家在全国各地旅行，在不同的城市演出。


The itinerant workers followed the harvest, moving from farm to farm. 流动工人跟着收割季节走，从一个农场搬到另一个农场。 |近义词：wanderers, travelers, nomads, rovers |反义词：residents, settlers, permanent residents |名词
