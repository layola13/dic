catechetic:/ˌkætɪˈkɛtɪk/| adj.|教理问答的；施以教理问答的|relating to or characteristic of catechesis (religious instruction, especially for children, by means of question and answer).  It emphasizes systematic instruction and the transmission of religious doctrine.|The catechetic method is still used in some religious schools to teach children the basic tenets of their faith. 教理问答法仍在一些宗教学校中使用，以教导孩子们他们信仰的基本原则。|近义词：didactic, instructional|反义词：unstructured, informal
