presubmits:/ˌpriːsəbˈmɪts/| n. |预提交；预先提交的文件|Documents or information submitted before the official deadline or formal submission.  This is typically used in contexts where a preliminary review or approval is needed before final submission.|例句：The presubmits for the grant application are due next week. = 资助申请的预提交文件下周到期。

The presubmits showed some minor issues that needed addressing before the final submission. = 预提交文件显示了一些需要在最终提交之前解决的小问题。


近义词：preliminary submissions, advance submissions|反义词：final submissions, resubmissions|词性:名词
