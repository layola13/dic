rosiness:/ˈroʊzinəs/| n. |玫瑰色；红润；健康；乐观 |The quality or state of being rosy; a rosy appearance or complexion.  It often suggests health, happiness, and optimism. |例句：Her cheeks glowed with rosiness after her run.  她跑步后脸颊红润。

例句：The rosiness of the sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues. 夕阳的玫瑰色将天空染成鲜艳的色彩。


近义词：ruddy, bloom, blush, redness, optimism

反义词：paleness, pallor, gloominess, pessimism

词性：名词
