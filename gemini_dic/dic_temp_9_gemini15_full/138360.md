notifier:/noʊˈtɪfaɪər/ | n. | 通知者，报警器 | A person or thing that notifies; something that gives notice or warning.  | Example: The system's notifier alerted us to the server failure.  这个系统的通知器提醒我们服务器故障了。 |近义词：informer, announcer, alerter | 反义词：None specifically, but words implying lack of notification could be considered antonyms, e.g., concealer, hider. | 名词
