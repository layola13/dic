reelection: /ˌriːɪˈlɛkʃən/ | n. | 再选举，连任 | The act of electing someone to the same office for another term.  It refers to the process of voting someone back into a position they previously held. |例句：The president's reelection campaign was highly contested.  总统的竞选连任活动竞争非常激烈。

The governor's reelection is expected to be a close race. 州长的连任选举预计将是一场激烈的竞争。


近义词：re-election, second term (as a result of reelection) | 反义词：defeat, ousting


词性：名词
