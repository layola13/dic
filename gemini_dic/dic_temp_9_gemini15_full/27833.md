canoeing:/kəˈnuɪŋ/| n. |独木舟运动；划独木舟|The activity of traveling in a canoe.  Canoeing is a popular recreational activity enjoyed by many people.|例句：We went canoeing on the lake yesterday. = 我们昨天在湖上划独木舟。

例句：They are planning a canoeing trip down the river. = 他们正计划沿着这条河进行一次独木舟旅行。


近义词：kayaking (皮划艇), boating (泛舟)

反义词：None (无明确反义词)

词性：名词
