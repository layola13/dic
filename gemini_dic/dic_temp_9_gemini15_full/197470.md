sheered:/ʃɪrd/ | v. | 偏离；突然转向；剪；剥夺；(使)碎裂 | To move suddenly to one side; to deviate sharply from a course; to cut or clip; to deprive; to cause to break into pieces. |例句：The car sheered off the road and crashed into a tree.  汽车突然偏离道路撞上了一棵树。

The wind sheered the sails.  风把帆撕裂了。


近义词：swerve, veer, deviate, cut, clip, deprive, shatter | 反义词：straighten, align, consolidate
