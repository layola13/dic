needlepoint:/ˈniːdlpɔɪnt/| n. |刺绣；针绣 |A type of embroidery done with yarn on canvas using a blunt needle.  It creates a textured, raised surface. |例句：She is skilled in needlepoint and creates beautiful tapestries. 她擅长针绣，创作出美丽的挂毯。|例句：The needlepoint pillow added a touch of elegance to the sofa. 这针绣的枕头为沙发增添了一丝优雅。|近义词：embroidery, tapestry |反义词：N/A
