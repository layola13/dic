retaliates:/rɪˈtælieɪts/| v.|报复；反击|To retaliate means to take revenge or act against someone who has harmed you.  It implies a direct response to a previous action.|例句：The country retaliated against the attack by launching its own missiles.  这个国家通过发射自己的导弹来报复袭击。

例句：He retaliated by refusing to cooperate. 他以拒绝合作的方式进行报复。


近义词：revenge, reciprocate, counter, avenge|反义词：forgive, pardon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retaliates against his enemies. 他报复他的敌人。
She retaliates with insults. 她以侮辱进行报复。


一般过去时:
They retaliated swiftly and decisively. 他们迅速而果断地进行了报复。
The company retaliated by lowering its prices. 公司通过降低价格进行反击。


现在完成时:
He has retaliated for the injustice he suffered. 他已经报复了他遭受的不公正待遇。
The government has retaliated against the trade sanctions. 政府已经对贸易制裁采取了报复措施。


过去完成时:
They had retaliated before the police arrived. 在警察到达之前，他们已经进行了报复。
She had retaliated by spreading rumors. 她通过散布谣言进行了报复。


将来时:
He will retaliate if provoked. 如果被激怒，他会进行报复。
They will retaliate against any further aggression. 他们将报复任何进一步的侵略。


虚拟语气:
If he were to retaliate, it would only make things worse. 如果他进行报复，只会使情况恶化。
I wish he hadn't retaliated so violently. 我真希望他没有如此暴力地进行报复。


被动语态:
His actions were retaliated against. 他的行为受到了报复。
The attack was retaliated against immediately. 袭击立即受到了报复。


疑问句:
Will they retaliate? 他们会报复吗？
How will he retaliate? 他将如何报复？


否定句:
He did not retaliate against their insults. 他没有报复他们的侮辱。
The company chose not to retaliate. 公司选择不进行报复。



PHRASE->
retaliate against  对...进行报复
retaliate in kind  以牙还牙
retaliate with  以...进行报复
retaliatory action 报复行动
retaliatory strike 报复性打击
