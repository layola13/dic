empathized:/ˈɛmpəθaɪzd/| v.| 感同身受，同情 |To understand and share the feelings of another.|例句：I empathized with her grief after she lost her pet.  我为她失去宠物而感到悲伤。|近义词：sympathized, understood, related to |反义词：ignored, disregarded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He empathizes with the homeless. = 他同情无家可归的人。
She empathizes with her friend's struggles. = 她同情她朋友的困境。

一般过去时:
I empathized with his situation. = 我同情他的处境。
They empathized with the victims of the accident. = 他们同情事故的受害者。

现在完成时:
She has empathized with many people in her life. = 她一生中同情过很多人。
He has empathized with the suffering of others. = 他同情过别人的痛苦。

过去完成时:
I had empathized with her before she even spoke. = 在她开口之前我就已经同情她了。
We had empathized with their loss long before the funeral. = 早在葬礼之前，我们就同情他们的损失了。

将来时:
I will empathize with you when you tell me your story. = 当你告诉我你的故事时，我会同情你。
They will empathize with the challenges facing the community. = 他们会同情社区面临的挑战。

虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I would empathize with her more. = 如果我处在她的位置，我会更同情她。
I wish I could empathize with his pain better. = 我希望我能更好地理解他的痛苦。

被动语态:
His pain was empathized with by many. = 许多人都同情他的痛苦。
The suffering of the refugees was deeply empathized with by the public. = 公众对难民的苦难深表同情。

疑问句:
Do you empathize with the characters in the book? = 你同情书中的人物吗？
Does he empathize with the plight of the poor? = 他同情穷人的困境吗？

否定句:
I didn't empathize with his anger. = 我不同情他的愤怒。
She couldn't empathize with his feelings. = 她无法理解他的感受。
