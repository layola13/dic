reoxygenation:/ˌriːˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 再氧合；复氧 | The process of supplying oxygen again to tissues or organs that have become hypoxic or anoxic.  It is a crucial step in the treatment of various medical conditions, especially those related to ischemia-reperfusion injury. | Example:  Reoxygenation of the ischemic tissue is essential to prevent further damage. 再氧合缺血组织对于防止进一步损伤至关重要。


The reoxygenation of the heart muscle after a heart attack is critical. 心肌梗死后心肌的再氧合至关重要。|近义词：reperfusion, oxygenation |反义词：deoxygenation, hypoxia
