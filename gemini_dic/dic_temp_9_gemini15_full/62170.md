encoding:/ɪnˈkoʊdɪŋ/ | n. |编码，编码方式 |The process of converting information or data into a code.  This code can be used to store, transmit, or process the information.  Encoding schemes vary depending on the type of data and the application. |Example: The image was encoded using JPEG compression. 这张图片使用JPEG压缩进行了编码。|近义词：coding, encryption |反义词：decoding, decryption
