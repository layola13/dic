postdiluvial: /ˌpoʊstdaɪˈluːviəl/ | adj. | 洪水后的；大洪水以后的 | Relating to or denoting the period after the biblical Great Flood.  The term often implies a simplistic or outdated view of geological history. | Example:  Postdiluvial sediments were studied to understand the geological changes after the flood.  洪水后的沉积物被研究以了解洪水后的地质变化。| 近义词: post-Flood, after the deluge | 反义词: antediluvian
