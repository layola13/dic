ranking:/ˈræŋkɪŋ/ | n. |排名，等级 |The act of assigning a rank or position to something or someone, or the result of this act.  It often refers to a list ordered by some measure of importance, value, or performance. |例句：The ranking of universities is based on various factors. 大学排名基于各种因素。
The company's ranking improved significantly this year.  今年公司排名显著提高。 |近义词：rating, classification, order, placement |反义词：unranking (not a common word, but implies a reversal of ranking) |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ranking of these athletes is based on their scores. = 这些运动员的排名是根据他们的分数确定的。
His current ranking in the world is number 10. = 他目前的世界排名是第十名。

一般过去时:
The ranking of the teams was announced after the final game. = 决赛结束后公布了各队的排名。
Her ranking dropped after her last competition. = 她上次比赛后排名下降了。

现在完成时:
The committee has completed the ranking of the proposals. = 委员会已经完成了提案的排名。
She has maintained a high ranking throughout her career. = 她在整个职业生涯中都保持着较高的排名。

过去完成时:
Before the announcement, we had already predicted his ranking. = 在公布之前，我们已经预测了他的排名。
By the end of the tournament, they had secured a top ranking. = 到锦标赛结束时，他们已经获得了顶级排名。

将来时:
The judges will announce the ranking tomorrow. = 裁判员明天将公布排名。
We expect his ranking to improve next year. = 我们预计他的排名明年将会提高。

虚拟语气:
If the judging were fairer, his ranking would be higher. = 如果评判更公平，他的排名会更高。
I wish my ranking were better in this competition. = 我希望我在这次比赛中的排名更好。

被动语态:
The athletes were ranked according to their performance. = 运动员们根据他们的表现进行了排名。
The results were ranked by a panel of experts. = 结果由专家组进行排名。

疑问句:
What is your ranking in the class? = 你在班里的排名是多少？
How does this ranking compare to last year's? = 这个排名与去年的排名相比如何？

否定句:
His performance wasn't good enough to improve his ranking. = 他的表现不足以提高他的排名。
The team's ranking didn't change after the last match. = 比赛结束后，该队的排名没有变化。
