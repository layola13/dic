raku:/ˈrɑːkuː/| n. | 乐烧，乐烧陶器 | Raku is a type of Japanese pottery known for its low-fire, often crackled glaze, and spontaneous, unpredictable results. It's characterized by its vibrant colors and unique textures.  | Example: The artist fired the pottery using the raku technique.  这位艺术家用乐烧技术烧制陶器。|近义词：Japanese pottery, low-fire pottery |反义词：high-fire pottery
