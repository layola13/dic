semipacifists: /ˌsemiˈpæsɪfɪsts/ | adj. | 半和平主义者；温和的和平主义者 |  Someone who supports pacifism to a certain extent but may not always adhere to it strictly. They might believe in non-violent solutions in most situations but may condone violence in certain circumstances, such as self-defense or in response to extreme aggression.  |  Many semipacifists believe in peaceful resolutions but will resort to force if necessary.  | 近义词：qualified pacifist, conditional pacifist | 反义词：militant, warmonger
