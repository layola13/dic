certification:/ˌsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |证明；证书；认证 | The process of officially recognizing someone or something as meeting specific standards or having specific qualifications.  It can refer to the document proving this recognition or the act of granting it.  | 例句：He received his certification in software engineering after completing the rigorous training program. 他在完成严格的培训计划后获得了软件工程认证。


例句：The company's certification to ISO 9001 demonstrates its commitment to quality.  该公司获得ISO 9001认证证明了其对质量的承诺。


近义词：attestation, accreditation, validation, authorization


反义词：disqualification, invalidation


词性：名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company receives many certifications each year. = 公司每年获得许多认证。
This certification proves his skills. = 这张证书证明了他的技能。


一般过去时:
He received his professional certification last year. = 他去年获得了专业资格认证。
She obtained her teaching certification in 2020. = 她于2020年获得了教师资格证。


现在完成时:
They have completed their certification process. = 他们已经完成了认证流程。
She has received several industry certifications. = 她已经获得了几个行业的认证。


过去完成时:
He had already earned his certification before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前就已经获得了认证。
Before starting the job, she had obtained all the necessary certifications. = 在开始工作之前，她已经获得了所有必要的证书。


将来时:
He will receive his certification next month. = 他下个月将获得认证。

虚拟语气:
