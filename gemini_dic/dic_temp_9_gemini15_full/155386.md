pharyngotomy:/ˌfærɪŋˈɡɒtəmi/ | n. |咽切开术|Surgical incision into the pharynx (throat).  The procedure is typically performed to access or treat conditions within the pharynx, such as tumors, abscesses, or foreign bodies. |Example: The surgeon performed a pharyngotomy to remove the impacted food bolus.  外科医生进行了咽切开术以去除卡住的食物团。  The patient required a pharyngotomy to address a cancerous growth in their throat. 病人需要进行咽切开术来治疗咽喉的癌性生长。|近义词：pharyngeal incision, throat incision |反义词：pharyngoplasty (surgical repair of the pharynx)
