initiatives:/ɪˈnɪʃətɪvz/ | n. |主动性；倡议；主动采取的行动 | An initiative is a new plan or program that is designed to achieve a particular aim.  It often involves taking the first step or showing leadership in a particular area. |例句：The government has launched a new initiative to reduce unemployment.  政府启动了一项新的计划以减少失业。

The company's initiatives in renewable energy have been praised. 这家公司在可再生能源方面的倡议受到了好评。|近义词：plans, programs, projects, endeavors, undertakings |反义词：passivity, inaction, inertia |名词
