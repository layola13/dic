fanatical:/fəˈnætɪkəl/| adj.|狂热的；盲信的；极端的|Extremely enthusiastic or zealous;  characterized by excessive enthusiasm or zeal.  Often implies a lack of reason or objectivity.|例句：He was fanatical about cleanliness. 他对清洁非常狂热。

例句：Her fanatical devotion to her work left her with little time for her family. 她对工作的狂热投入让她几乎没有时间陪伴家人。


近义词：zealous, fervent, passionate, obsessed, extreme, bigoted


反义词：indifferent, apathetic, calm, rational, moderate


词性：形容词
