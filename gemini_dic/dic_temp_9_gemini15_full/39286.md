congruous:/ˈkɒŋɡruəs/ /ˈkɑːŋɡruəs/| adj. |一致的；协调的；合适的|In agreement or harmony; consistent.  Congruous things fit together well and are appropriate to each other.  |例句：Her flamboyant dress was incongruous with the formal setting.  她的艳丽服装与正式场合格格不入。(反义例句)|例句：His actions were entirely congruous with his beliefs. 他的行为与他的信仰完全一致。|近义词：consistent, harmonious, compatible, accordant |反义词：incongruous, discordant, incompatible, contradictory
