interventionary: /ˌɪntərˈvɛnʃənəri/ | adj. | 干预的；介入的 | Relating to or characterized by intervention.  It describes something that involves or is characterized by intervention, especially in a political or social context. |


例句：

The interventionary actions of the United Nations were crucial in preventing further conflict. 联合国的干预行动对于阻止进一步的冲突至关重要。

His interventionary approach to the problem proved effective. 他对这个问题的干预方法被证明是有效的。


近义词：interfering, meddlesome, intrusive


反义词：non-interventionist, passive, laissez-faire


词性:  形容词
