handoffs:/ˈhændɔːfs/| n.| 交接；移交；传递 | The act of handing something over to someone else; the transfer of responsibility or control.  It can refer to a physical object, a task, or a project. | 例句：The handoff of the project to the new team went smoothly.  项目顺利移交给新团队。

The quarterback executed a perfect handoff to the running back. 四分卫完美地将球传给了跑卫。


近义词：transfer, handover, transmission, delivery | 反义词：retention, withholding
