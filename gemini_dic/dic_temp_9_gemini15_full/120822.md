misattributing: /ˌmɪsæˈtrɪbjuːtɪŋ/ | v. |错误归因，误归于| To attribute something to the wrong cause, person, or source.  |例句：He was misattributing the decline in sales to the new marketing campaign, when actually it was due to a broader economic downturn. 他错误地将销售额下降归咎于新的营销活动，而实际上这是由于更广泛的经济衰退造成的。


例句：The historian misattributed the painting to a lesser-known artist instead of the master himself.  历史学家将这幅画错误地归于一位鲜为人知的艺术家，而不是大师本人。


近义词：misascribe, miscredit, wrongly attribute | 反义词：correctly attribute, accurately ascribe
