preexhibits:/ˌpriːˈɛɡzɪbɪts/ | n. | 预展品，预先展出的物品 | Items displayed before a formal exhibition or showing.  Preexhibits allow potential buyers or viewers a preview of the items that will be featured in the main exhibition. | The art gallery held preexhibits of the upcoming modern art collection. = 艺术画廊举办了即将到来的现代艺术收藏的预展。

The museum curated preexhibits of the dinosaur fossils before the grand opening. = 博物馆在盛大开幕前策划了恐龙化石的预展。| 近义词：preview, sneak peek | 反义词：main exhibition, final display | 名词
