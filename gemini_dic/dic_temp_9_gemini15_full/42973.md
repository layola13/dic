coverture:/ˈkʌvərtʃər/| n. | 妇女的依附身份；已婚妇女的法律地位 | The legal status of a married woman, considered to be under the protection and control of her husband.  This term historically referred to the legal doctrine that a wife's legal identity was subsumed under that of her husband upon marriage. | Example:  Under the doctrine of coverture, a married woman could not own property in her own name.  = 根据妇女依附身份的教义，已婚妇女不能以自己的名义拥有财产。|近义词：subjection, dependence |反义词：independence, autonomy | 名词
