multisite:/ˈmʌltiˌsaɪt/| adj. n.|多站点的，多地点的；多站点网络|Relating to or involving multiple sites or locations, especially in the context of computer networks or websites.  A multisite network allows for the management of multiple websites from a single control panel.|例句：Our company uses a multisite WordPress installation to manage our various blogs. = 我们公司使用多站点WordPress安装来管理我们不同的博客。

例句：The research project involved fieldwork at multiple sites across the country. = 该研究项目涉及在全国多个地点进行实地考察。


近义词：multi-location, distributed, networked


反义词：single-site, centralized


词性: adjective, noun
