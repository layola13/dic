longbowman:/ˈlɒŋˌboʊmən/ | n. |长弓手 | A soldier who uses a longbow.  Longbowmen were particularly important in the English armies of the medieval period. | Example: The English longbowmen played a crucial role in the Battle of Agincourt.  英格兰长弓手在阿金库尔战役中发挥了至关重要的作用。  The longbowmen rained arrows down upon the enemy ranks. 长弓手向敌军阵列倾泻箭雨。 | 近义词：archer (更通用的术语) | 反义词：无  |  词性：名词
