diminishes: /dɪˈmɪnɪʃɪz/ | v. | 减少；降低；贬低 | To make or become less; to reduce in size, extent, or importance. |

例句：

The intensity of the sunlight diminishes as the day ends. =  随着一天结束，阳光的强度减弱了。

His confidence diminished after the failure of his project. = 他的项目失败后，他的信心减弱了。


近义词：decrease, reduce, lessen, impair, belittle

反义词：increase, augment, enhance, magnify


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun diminishes in the evening. = 太阳在傍晚变小。
His power diminishes with age. = 他的权力随着年龄增长而减弱。

一般过去时:
The problem diminished after we found a solution. = 我们找到解决方案后，问题就减少了。
Her influence diminished after the scandal. = 丑闻之后，她的影响力减弱了。

现在完成时:
The value of the property has diminished over the years. = 这处房产的价值多年来一直在下降。
His reputation has diminished considerably. = 他的名声大大受损。

过去完成时:
The army had diminished considerably by the time they reached the border. = 当他们到达边境时，军队已经大大减少了。
His enthusiasm had diminished after several setbacks. = 经历几次挫折后，他的热情减退了。

将来时:
The effects of the medication will diminish over time. = 药物的作用会随着时间的推移而减弱。
The company's profits are expected to diminish next quarter. = 预计该公司下季度的利润将会减少。

虚拟语气:
If the noise diminished, we could work more effectively. = 如果噪音减小，我们可以更有效地工作。
I wish the pain would diminish. = 我希望疼痛能减轻。

被动语态:
The power of the king was diminished by the rebellion. = 国王的权力被叛乱削弱了。
His authority was diminished by his mistakes. = 他的权威因他的错误而降低了。

疑问句:
Does the intensity of the light diminish with distance? = 光的强度随着距离的增加而减弱吗？
Will the symptoms diminish after taking the medicine? = 服药后症状会减轻吗？

否定句:
The value of the painting did not diminish over time. = 这幅画的价值并没有随着时间的推移而贬值。
His efforts did not diminish despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，他的努力并没有减少。


PHRASE->

diminish in size = 尺寸减小
diminish in importance = 重要性降低
diminish in value = 价值下降
diminish in number = 数量减少
diminish one's power = 削弱某人的权力

词源:

diminishes 来自于古法语 *diminuer，最终源于拉丁语 *minutus，意思是“小的”。  前缀 “di-” 表示“分开”或“彻底”，因此diminish 的本意是“使变小”。
