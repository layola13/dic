murmurous:/ˈmɜːrmərəs/ | adj. | 低语的；沙沙作响的；嗡嗡作响的 |  Having or producing a low, indistinct sound like that of murmuring; suggestive of a murmur.  | The murmurous sound of the waves lulled her to sleep. 海浪的低语声使她睡着了。 The forest was filled with a murmurous stillness. 森林里充满了低语般的寂静。 |  whispering, rustling, humming | loud, clear, silent | 形容词
