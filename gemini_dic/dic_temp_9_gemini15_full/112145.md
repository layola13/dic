look:/lʊk/ /lʊk/| v. n.|看，注视，外观|To direct one's gaze toward something or someone; to appear or seem a certain way.|例句：Look at that beautiful bird! 看那只美丽的鸟！|例句：The house looks old. 这房子看起来很旧。|近义词：see, gaze, glance, appear, seem|反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I look at the sunset every evening. = 我每天晚上都看日落。
He looks tired today. = 他今天看起来很累。


一般过去时:
I looked at the painting for a long time. = 我看了那幅画很久。
She looked happy at the party. = 她在派对上看起来很开心。


现在完成时:
I have looked for my keys everywhere. = 我到处找我的钥匙了。
She has looked much better since she started exercising. = 自从她开始锻炼以来，她看起来好多了。


过去完成时:
I had looked at the schedule before I left. = 我离开之前看过日程表了。
They had looked forward to the vacation for months. = 他们几个月来一直期待着这次假期。


将来时:
I will look for a new job next year. = 我明年将找一份新工作。
We will look back on this day with fondness. = 我们将怀着美好的回忆回顾这一天。


虚拟语气:
If I looked closer, I might have seen the problem. = 如果我看得仔细些，我可能会发现问题。
I wish I looked younger. = 我希望我看起来更年轻。


被动语态:
The painting is looked at by many visitors every day. = 这幅画每天都被许多游客观看。
His proposal was looked over by the committee. = 他的提案被委员会审查了。


疑问句:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
Where did you look for your wallet? = 你在哪里找你的钱包？


否定句:
I didn't look at the map. = 我没有看地图。
He didn't look impressed by the news. = 他看起来对这个消息并不印象深刻。


PHRASE->
look at = 看；查看
look after = 照顾；照料
look for = 寻找
look forward to = 期待
look out = 当心；注意
