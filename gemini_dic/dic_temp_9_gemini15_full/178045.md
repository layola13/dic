reclamation:/ˌrɛkləˈmeɪʃən/| n.| 开垦；改造；收回；索回 | The act of reclaiming or recovering something.  It often refers to the process of making land usable, such as converting wasteland into farmland or recovering land from the sea. It can also refer to the act of regaining possession of something that was lost or taken. |例句：The reclamation of the marshes created new farmland.  沼泽地的开垦创造了新的农田。

例句：The government is pursuing the reclamation of stolen assets. 政府正在追回被盗资产。|近义词：recovery, restoration, redemption |反义词：abandonment, relinquishment |名词

The government reclaims land from the sea. = 政府从海里填海造地。
Land reclamation projects are underway. = 填海造地项目正在进行中。


一般过去时:

The company reclaimed its stolen property. = 公司收回了被盗的财产。


现在完成时:
They have reclaimed a significant area of land. = 他们已经开垦了一大片土地。
The city has reclaimed many derelict buildings. = 这个城市已经修复了许多废弃的建筑物。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
