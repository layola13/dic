pouches: /ˈpaʊtʃɪz/ | n. |小袋；口袋；囊 |  A small bag or sack, typically made of flexible material and used for carrying small items.  |例句：She kept her money in a small leather pouch. 她把钱放在一个小皮袋里。

例句：The kangaroo carries its young in a pouch. 袋鼠把幼崽放在育儿袋里。


近义词：bag, sack, pocket, satchel

反义词：container (large), receptacle (large)

词性：名词
