practiced:/ˈpræktɪst/ | adj. v. | 熟练的；练习过的；实践过的 |  Experienced and skilled in a particular activity or field through practice.  It can also describe something that has been done or performed repeatedly. |

例句：

She is a practiced pianist. = 她是一位技艺娴熟的钢琴家。

He delivered a practiced speech. = 他发表了一篇熟练的演讲。


近义词：skilled, expert, proficient, adept

反义词：inexperienced, unskilled, amateur


词性：形容词（adj.）, 动词过去式（v.）


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He practices the piano every day. = 他每天练习钢琴。
They practice yoga regularly. = 他们定期练习瑜伽。

一般过去时:
She practiced the speech many times. = 她练习了很多次演讲。
He practiced medicine for over twenty years. = 他行医超过二十年。

现在完成时:
I have practiced playing the guitar for five years. = 我练习弹吉他五年了。
They have practiced hard for the competition. = 他们为比赛刻苦训练。

过去完成时:
She had practiced her lines before the play. = 在演出前，她已经练习过她的台词了。
He had practiced law before entering politics. = 他在从政之前做过律师。

将来时:
I will practice my presentation tomorrow. = 我明天将练习我的演示。
They will practice their dance routine next week. = 他们下周将练习他们的舞蹈套路。

虚拟语气:
If I had practiced more, I would have done better. = 如果我练习更多，我会做得更好。
I wish I had practiced harder for the exam. = 我希望我为考试更努力地练习过。

被动语态:
The technique was practiced by many athletes. = 许多运动员都练习过这项技术。
The music was practiced by the band for weeks. = 乐队练习了这首音乐好几个星期。

疑问句:
Do you practice any sports? = 你练习什么运动吗？
Has he practiced enough for the test? = 他为考试练习够了吗？

否定句:
I didn't practice enough for the test. = 我没有为考试练习足够。
They don't practice their English regularly. = 他们不定期练习他们的英语。
