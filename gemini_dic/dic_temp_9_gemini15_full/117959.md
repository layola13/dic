memento's:/məˈmɛntoʊz/| n.|纪念品（复数）|A memento is an object kept as a reminder or souvenir of a person or event.  It is something that serves as a reminder of a past experience or person.|例句：He kept the old coin as a memento of his trip to Rome. 他把那枚古钱币作为罗马旅行的纪念品保存着。  She gave me a seashell as a memento of our vacation. 她给了我一个贝壳作为我们假期的纪念品。|近义词：souvenir, keepsake, reminder|反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion
