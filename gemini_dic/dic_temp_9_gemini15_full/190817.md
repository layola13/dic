sawboneses: /ˈsɔːboʊnzɪz/ | n. |外科医生们，庸医们| A plural form of "sawbones," a somewhat informal and often derogatory term for a surgeon or doctor.  It implies a lack of skill or professionalism, sometimes suggesting quackery.
