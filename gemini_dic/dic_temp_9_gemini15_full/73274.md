forebeam:/ˈfɔːrbiːm/| n.|前梁，横梁|A horizontal beam, especially one supporting a load at the front of a structure, such as a bridge or a loom.|The forebeam of the old bridge was severely damaged in the storm.  古老桥梁的前梁在暴风雨中严重受损。|The weaver adjusted the forebeam on the loom to begin weaving. 织工调整织布机上的前梁开始织布。|supporting beam, crossbeam| (no clear antonym)|名词
