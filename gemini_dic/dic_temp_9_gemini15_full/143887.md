outpopulating:/ˌaʊtˈpɑːpjuleɪtɪŋ/| v.|超过…的数量；数量超过|The process of exceeding in number or quantity.  It implies a surpassing in population or a growth rate that leads to numerical dominance.|Example:  The rabbits are outpopulating the local predators. 兔子数量超过当地的捕食者。

The invasive species is outpopulating the native plants.  入侵物种的数量超过了本地植物。|近义词：outnumber, surpass, exceed|反义词：be outnumbered, be surpassed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The rabbits outpopulate the local predators. = 兔子数量超过当地的捕食者。
This invasive species is outpopulating the native plants. = 这种入侵物种的数量超过了本地植物。


一般过去时:
The weeds outpopulated the crops last year. = 去年杂草的数量超过了庄稼。
The immigrant population outpopulated the native population in that area. = 在那个地区，移民人口的数量超过了本地人口。


现在完成时:
The city has outpopulated the surrounding towns. = 这个城市的人口已经超过了周围的城镇。
The invasive species has outpopulated the native flora. = 入侵物种已经超过了当地的植物群。


过去完成时:
By the end of the decade, the city had outpopulated its neighboring counties. = 到十年结束时，这个城市的人口已经超过了它附近的几个县。
Before the environmental changes, the native birds had outpopulated the introduced species. = 在环境变化之前，本地鸟类的数量超过了引进物种。


将来时:
Experts predict that the city will outpopulate the neighboring region within the next decade. = 专家预测，这座城市的人口将在未来十年内超过邻近地区。
It is likely that the invasive plant will outpopulate the native wildflowers next year. = 野花明年很可能被入侵植物的数量所超过。


虚拟语气:
If the birth rate continued to rise, the population would outpopulate available resources. = 如果出生率继续上升，人口就会超过可用资源。
If the invasive species had not been controlled, it would have outpopulated all other plants. = 如果没有控制入侵物种，它就会超过所有其他植物。


被动语态:
The native species was outpopulated by the invasive plants. = 本地物种被入侵植物的数量所超过。
The small town was outpopulated by the nearby city. = 小镇的人口被附近的城市所超过。


疑问句:
Will the human population outpopulate the planet's resources? = 人口会超过地球的资源吗？
Does the current growth rate indicate that species X will outpopulate species Y? = 当前的增长率是否表明物种X将超过物种Y？


否定句:
The native birds did not outpopulate the introduced species. = 本地鸟类的数量没有超过引进物种。
The small town has not been outpopulated by the city yet. = 小镇的人口还没有被城市所超过。
