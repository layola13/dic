outshrills:/ˌaʊtˈʃrɪlz/| v.|大声喧哗超过；盖过…的声音|to surpass in loudness or intensity of sound; to shout or scream louder than|例句：The band outshrilled the cheering crowd. 乐队的演奏声盖过了欢呼的人群。
The children outshrilled each other in excitement. 孩子们兴奋地互相大声喊叫。|近义词：overshadow, outdo, surpass|反义词：subdue, quiet, hush
