inquest:/ˈɪŋkwest/| n.|死因调查，审讯|A formal inquiry, usually by a coroner, into the cause and manner of someone's death.|The inquest into the death of the young woman revealed that she had been murdered.|调查，探究|investigation, inquiry|trial|n.
