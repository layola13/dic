mockingly: /ˈmɒkɪŋli/ | /ˈmɑːkɪŋli/ | adv. | 嘲弄地；戏弄地；模仿地 | In a way that is intended to make fun of someone or something.  It suggests a derisive or scornful imitation. | 例句：He mockingly imitated the teacher's accent. 他嘲弄地模仿老师的口音。

He spoke mockingly of her efforts. 他嘲弄地谈论她的努力。


近义词：derisively, scornfully, sarcastically, jeeringly, sneeringly | 反义词：seriously, sincerely, respectfully, earnestly
