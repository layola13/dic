This term often refers to the transition period into adulthood, career development, and personal growth after college.  | Example:  Her postcolleges years were marked by travel and career exploration. 她大学毕业后的几年都在旅行和探索职业中度过。 |近义词: postgraduation, post-university,  |反义词:college years, undergraduate years |名词
