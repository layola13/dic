robotically:/ˌroʊˈbɑːtɪkli/ | adv. | 机械地，机器人般地 |  In a mechanical or automatic way, without feeling or expression.  |  例句：He moved robotically, his eyes glazed over. 他机械地移动着，眼睛呆滞。


例句：She answered the questions robotically, giving only the bare facts. 她机械地回答了问题，只提供了基本事实。


近义词：automatically, mechanically, unfeelingly, emotionlessly


反义词：feelingly, expressively, animatedly, enthusiastically


词性: 副词
