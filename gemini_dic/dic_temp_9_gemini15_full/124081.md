morris:/ˈmɒrɪs/| /ˈmɔːrɪs/| n.|莫里斯（男子名）；莫里斯舞；图案；|A surname of English origin, also used as a given name.  It can also refer to a type of traditional English folk dance, often accompanied by music and morris dancing sticks.  Additionally, it can describe a particular type of decorative pattern or design.|例句：His name is Morris. 他的名字是莫里斯。
