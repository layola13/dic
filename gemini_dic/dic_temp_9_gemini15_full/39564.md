conscious: /ˈkɒnʃəs/ /ˈkɑːnʃəs/ | adj. | 有意识的；清醒的；自觉的 | Aware of and responding to one's surroundings; awake.  |例句：She became conscious of someone watching her. 她意识到有人在看着她。

例句：He was conscious of his own shortcomings. 他意识到自己的缺点。


近义词：aware, mindful, sentient | 反义词：unconscious, insensible, unaware


词源:  From Old French *conscient, from Latin *conscient-, present participle of *conscire "to be aware," from com- "with, together" + scire "to know."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is conscious of his responsibilities. 他意识到自己的责任。
She is conscious that she made a mistake. 她意识到自己犯了一个错误。


一般过去时:
I was conscious of a strange noise. 我意识到一种奇怪的声音。
The patient was unconscious after the accident. 事故后病人失去了知觉。


现在完成时:
He has been conscious of the problem for a long time. 他很长时间以来一直意识到这个问题。
She has become conscious of the need for change. 她已经意识到改变的必要性。


过去完成时:
I had been conscious of his presence before he spoke.  在他说话之前我就已经意识到他的存在了。
She had already become conscious of the danger. 她已经意识到危险了。


将来时:
I will be conscious of my words. 我会注意我的言辞。
He will become conscious of his mistake eventually. 他最终会意识到自己的错误。


虚拟语气:
If I were conscious of the risk, I wouldn't have done it. 如果我意识到风险，我就不会那样做了。
I wish I had been more conscious of the consequences. 我真希望我当时更清楚地意识到后果。


被动语态:
His mistake was consciously overlooked. 他的错误被有意地忽略了。 (Note:  "conscious" is rarely used in the passive voice directly.  The example uses "consciously" as an adverb.)
The need for change is being consciously addressed.  人们正在有意识地解决改变的需求。


疑问句:
Are you conscious of your surroundings? 你意识到你周围的环境吗？
Is he conscious of the implications? 他意识到这些含义吗？


否定句:
He was unconscious of the danger. 他没有意识到危险。
She was not conscious of being watched. 她没有意识到自己被监视。


PHRASE->
conscious effort  有意识的努力

conscious mind  意识
self-conscious  害羞的，不自然的
lose consciousness  失去知觉
