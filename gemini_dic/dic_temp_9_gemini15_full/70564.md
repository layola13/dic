fill-up:/ˈfɪl ʌp/| vt. n.| 加满；填充；填补 | To fill something completely.  |例句：I need to fill up my gas tank. 我需要把油箱加满。|例句：The form requires a fill-up of all the blanks. 表格需要填写所有空格。 |近义词：refill, top up, complete |反义词：empty, drain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fills up his water bottle every morning. = 他每天早上都会装满他的水瓶。
They fill up the swimming pool every week. = 他们每周都会把游泳池加满。

一般过去时:
She filled up the car with petrol yesterday. = 她昨天加满了汽车油箱。
We filled up the forms with all the necessary information. = 我们用所有必要的信息填写了表格。

现在完成时:
I have filled up the bathtub. = 我已经放满了浴缸。
They have filled up all the empty seats. = 他们已经填满了所有空位。

过去完成时:
He had filled up the tank before the long journey. = 长途旅行之前，他已经加满了油箱。
She had filled up all the application forms before the deadline. = 截止日期之前，她已经填好了所有申请表。

将来时:
I will fill up the car with gas before we leave. = 我们出发前我会把车加满油。
They will fill up the balloons with helium for the party. = 他们会为派对用氦气把气球吹满。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would fill up the application thoroughly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会仔细填写申请表。
If she had known, she would have filled up her gas tank earlier. = 如果她知道的话，她会早点加满油箱。

被动语态:
The tank was filled up by the mechanic. = 油箱是由机械师加满的。
The forms were filled up by the applicants. = 表格由申请人填写。

疑问句:

Did he fill up the car with diesel? = 他用柴油加满了车吗？

否定句:

They didn't fill up the forms completely. = 他们没有完整地填写表格。
