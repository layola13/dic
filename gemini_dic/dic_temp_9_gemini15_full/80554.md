goodwill:/ˈɡʊdˌwɪl/| n.|商誉；善意；好意|Intangible assets such as a company's reputation, customer loyalty, and brand recognition.  It's an asset that contributes to a company's profitability. It can also refer to friendly or benevolent feelings towards someone or something.|例句：The acquisition included the goodwill of the established brand. 这项收购包括了已建立品牌的商誉。|例句：She showed great goodwill towards the new neighbors. 她对新邻居表现出极大的善意。|近义词：favor, benevolence, reputation, brand equity|反义词：ill will, animosity, hostility|名词
