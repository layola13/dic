hermaphroditish:/ˌhɜːrmæˈfrɒdaɪtɪʃ/| adj.|雌雄同体的；两性类的|Possessing both male and female reproductive organs.  The term often refers to organisms that have both male and female reproductive structures, but can also describe situations or characteristics exhibiting a blend of typically masculine and feminine traits.|例句：Some species of snails are hermaphroditish, meaning each individual can produce both eggs and sperm.  一些蜗牛物种是雌雄同体的，这意味着每个个体都能产生卵子和精子。
