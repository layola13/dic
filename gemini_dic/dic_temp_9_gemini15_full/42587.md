counterpointing:/ˈkaʊntəpɔɪntɪŋ/| v. | 对位法；对比；对照 | The act of combining or contrasting two or more melodic lines or ideas simultaneously.  It often involves creating a sense of interplay or tension between the different elements. | Example: The composer skillfully used counterpointing melodies to create a rich and complex texture.  作曲家巧妙地运用对位旋律创造出丰富复杂的织体。  The author counterpointed the character's optimism with descriptions of a bleak and hopeless environment. 作者将人物的乐观情绪与阴郁绝望的环境描写进行了对比。 |近义词：contrasting, juxtaposing, comparing |反义词：harmonizing, unifying

一般现在时:
The composer counterpoints the melody with a bass line. = 作曲家用低音线与旋律形成对位。
She counterpoints her arguments with evidence. = 她用证据来反驳她的论点。


一般过去时:

The artist counterpointed the bright colors with dark shadows. = 艺术家用深色阴影与鲜艳的色彩形成对比。

疑问句:
