frontiersmen:/ˈfrʌntɪrzmən/ | n. |拓荒者，边疆人 |A frontiersman is a person who lives in or explores the frontier, especially in the history of the American West. They were often skilled in hunting, trapping, and survival skills.  |Example:  The frontiersmen relied on their hunting skills to survive in the wilderness.  拓荒者依靠他们的狩猎技巧在荒野中生存。  The frontiersmen faced many challenges, including hostile Native American tribes and harsh weather conditions. 拓荒者面临着许多挑战，包括敌对的印第安部落和恶劣的天气条件。|近义词：pioneer, settler, explorer |反义词：city dweller, urbanite
