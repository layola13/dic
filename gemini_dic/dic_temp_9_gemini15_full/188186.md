roving:/ˈroʊvɪŋ/ | adj. | 漫游的；巡游的；漂泊的；流动的 | Wandering or traveling from place to place without a fixed pattern.  It often implies a sense of freedom or exploration. | 例句：The roving reporter traveled across the country, interviewing people from all walks of life.  这位漫游的记者走遍全国各地，采访各行各业的人。

例句：The roving band of musicians played in different squares each night.  这支四处游走的乐队每晚都在不同的广场演奏。


近义词：wandering, traveling, itinerant, nomadic, rambling

反义词：stationary, settled, sedentary


词性: Adjective
