leanr:/lɜːrn/|v.|学习，学会|to gain knowledge or skill in a specific subject or activity.|He learned to play the piano when he was five. 他五岁的时候学会了弹钢琴。|study, acquire, master|forget, ignore, unlearn|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I learn English every day. = 我每天学习英语。
They learn new skills at work. = 他们在工作中学习新技能。

一般过去时:
I learned to swim last summer. = 我去年夏天学会了游泳。
She learned a lot from her mistakes. = 她从自己的错误中学到了很多。

现在完成时:
I have learned a lot about history. = 我已经学到了很多关于历史的知识。
He has learned to drive a car. = 他已经学会了开车。

过去完成时:
I had learned the basics before the test. = 我在考试前已经学习了基础知识。
She had learned French before moving to Paris. = 她搬到巴黎之前已经学过法语了。


将来时:
I will learn to code next year. = 我明年将学习编程。
They will learn about the new project tomorrow. = 他们明天将了解新的项目。

虚拟语气:
If I learned harder, I would pass the exam. = 如果我学习更努力，我就能通过考试。
I wish I had learned more about finance. = 我真希望我以前学习更多关于金融的知识。

被动语态:
This lesson is learned by many students. = 这节课被很多学生学习。
New technologies are learned quickly by young people. = 年轻人很快就能学会新技术。

疑问句:
Do you learn a new language? = 你学习一门外语吗？
What do you learn in school? = 你在学校学习什么？

否定句:
I don't learn Spanish. = 我不学习西班牙语。
He didn't learn to ride a bike until he was ten. = 他直到十岁才学会骑自行车。


PHRASE->
learn by heart = 死记硬背
learn from experience = 从经验中学习
learn the ropes = 熟悉情况
learn to live with = 学会与…相处
learn something new = 学习新事物
