prebreathing:/ˈpriːbriːðɪŋ/ | n. | 预吸气，预先呼吸 | The act of taking a breath before an action, often to prepare for exertion or vocalization.  It is commonly used in diving, singing, and public speaking. |Example:  The singer took a deep prebreathing before launching into the powerful aria. 歌手在开始演唱那首强有力的咏叹调之前深吸了一口气。


The diver performed a prebreathing maneuver to equalize the pressure in his ears.  潜水员进行了预吸气动作以平衡耳内压力。|近义词：deep breath, inhalation, inspiration |反义词：exhalation, expiration |名词
