quavered:/ˈkwɛvərd/| v.|颤抖着说，颤抖|To speak in a trembling voice, typically because of nervousness or fear.|例句：Her voice quavered as she told the story.  她讲故事时声音颤抖。|例句：He quavered a response, unable to meet her gaze. 他颤抖着回应，不敢看她的眼睛。|近义词：trembled, shook, vibrated|反义词：spoke firmly, boomed
