chimaeroid:/kaɪˈmɪərɔɪd/| n. |嵌合体鱼，银鲛|A chimaeroid is a cartilaginous fish belonging to the order Chimaeriformes, also known as ghost sharks or ratfish.  They are characterized by their cartilaginous skeletons,  elongated bodies, and often a long, whip-like tail.  |Example:  The deep-sea chimaeroid was a fascinating specimen for the marine biologists.|近义词: ghost shark, ratfish |反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it refers to a specific type of fish)|名词
