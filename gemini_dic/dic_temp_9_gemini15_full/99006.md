interaction: /ˌɪntərˈækʃən/ | n. |相互作用，互动 |The interplay between two or more objects, people, or forces.  It can refer to a reciprocal action or influence. |例句：The interaction between the teacher and students was positive and productive. 教师和学生之间的互动积极有效。

例句：There was little interaction between the two groups. 两组之间几乎没有互动。


近义词：interplay, reciprocity, exchange, communication,  response,  relationship

反义词：isolation, separation, detachment, independence


词性：名词
