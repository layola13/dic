compatriots:/kəmˈpeɪtriəts/| n. |同胞，同国人 |Fellow citizens of a country.  The term emphasizes shared nationality and often implies a sense of unity or common identity.| 例句：The expatriates longed to be reunited with their compatriots.  流亡海外的人渴望与他们的同胞团聚。

例句：We must stand together with our compatriots in this time of crisis.在这个危急时刻，我们必须与同胞们站在一起。


近义词：fellow countrymen, fellow citizens, nationals | 反义词：foreigners, aliens
