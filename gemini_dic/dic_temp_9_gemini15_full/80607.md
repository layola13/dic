goon:/ɡuːn/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜，暴徒|A stupid or foolish person; a thug or gangster.  |例句：He's such a goon! 他真是个笨蛋！
例句：The goons beat him up and stole his money. 暴徒殴打了他并偷了他的钱。|近义词：idiot, fool, thug, lout|反义词：genius, intellectual
