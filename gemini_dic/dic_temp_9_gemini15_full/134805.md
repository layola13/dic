nonmigration:/ˌnɒnmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/| n. |非迁移，不迁移|The absence of migration; the state or fact of not migrating.  It often refers to a population or species that does not undertake seasonal or other regular movements. |Example: The nonmigration of the local bird population was attributed to the abundance of food sources in the area.  当地鸟类种群的不迁移被认为是由于该地区食物来源丰富造成的。|近义词：sedentariness, immobility|反义词：migration
