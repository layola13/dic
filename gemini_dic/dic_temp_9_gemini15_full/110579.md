limited-access-highways: /ˈlɪmɪtɪd ˈækses ˈhaɪweɪz/ | n. | 高速公路；受限通行高速公路 |  Limited-access highways are roads designed to minimize intersections and allow for high-speed travel. They typically feature controlled access points, such as interchanges, and are separated from local roads. |  例句：Driving on limited-access highways is generally safer and faster than driving on regular roads.  在高速公路上驾驶通常比在普通道路上驾驶更安全、更快。

例句：Many limited-access highways cross state lines, connecting major cities. 许多高速公路跨越州界，连接主要城市。


近义词：expressways, freeways, motorways | 反义词：local roads, surface streets | 词性: n.
