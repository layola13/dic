myrtlewood:/ˈmɜːrtlˌwʊd/| n. |桃金娘木，桃金娘木制品|The term "myrtlewood" refers to the wood of various myrtle trees, primarily those in the genus *Myrtus*, but also sometimes encompassing wood from other plants with similar properties.  It's prized for its beautiful grain, often with a distinctive figure, and is used in fine woodworking, particularly for decorative items.  |The table was made of beautiful myrtlewood.|近义词：myrtle,  (depending on specific species) other hardwood names.|反义词：无|名词
