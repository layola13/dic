drafting:/ˈdræftɪŋ/ | n. v. |起草；制图；设计；选拔 |The act of preparing a preliminary version of something, such as a document, plan, or drawing.  |例句：The drafting of the contract took several weeks. 起草合同花了几个星期。

The team is responsible for drafting the marketing materials. 团队负责起草营销材料。|近义词：drawing up, composing, sketching, designing |反义词：finalizing, completing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drafts a letter every day. 他每天都起草一封信。
They are drafting a new policy. 他们正在起草一项新政策。

一般过去时:
She drafted the report last night. 她昨晚起草了报告。
The architect drafted the building plans. 建筑师绘制了建筑图纸。

现在完成时:
He has drafted several proposals this month. 这个月他已经起草了几份提案。
They have drafted the first version of the document. 他们已经起草了文件的第一个版本。

过去完成时:
She had drafted the speech before the meeting started. 会议开始之前，她已经起草好了演讲稿。
They had drafted the contract before the negotiations began. 在谈判开始之前，他们已经起草好了合同。

将来时:
He will draft a response to the complaint tomorrow. 他明天将起草一份对投诉的回复。
They will be drafting the budget for next year. 他们将起草明年的预算。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would draft a more detailed report. 如果我有更多的时间，我会起草一份更详细的报告。
I wish I had drafted the email earlier. 我真希望我早点起草这封邮件。

被动语态:
The contract was drafted by a lawyer. 合同是由律师起草的。
The plans are being drafted by the engineering team. 计划正在由工程团队起草。

疑问句:
Who drafted the document? 谁起草了这份文件？
When will the proposal be drafted? 提案什么时候起草？

否定句:
He did not draft the letter himself. 他没有自己起草这封信。
They haven't drafted the final version yet. 他们还没有起草最终版本。


PHRASE->
draft a letter: 起草一封信
draft a plan: 制定计划
draft a budget: 编制预算
draft a proposal: 起草提案
draft a design: 设计草图

词源:
The word "drafting" comes from the Old English word "draht," meaning "drawn" or "pulled." This reflects the original sense of drawing something, such as a plan or design.  Over time, the meaning expanded to include the preparation of written documents.
