oversupplied: /ˌoʊvər səˈplaɪd/ | adj. | 供过于求的，供应过剩的 |  Having more of something than is needed or can be used.  This word describes a situation where the supply of a particular good or service exceeds the demand.  | 例句：The market is oversupplied with cheap smartphones. = 智能手机市场供过于求。

The current grain harvest has left the country oversupplied with wheat. = 今年的谷物收成导致国家小麦供应过剩。


近义词：surplus, excessive, abundant, plentiful

反义词：undersupplied, deficient, scarce, short


词性：形容词
