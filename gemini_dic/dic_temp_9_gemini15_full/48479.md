deep-six:/ˌdiːpˈsɪks/| vt.|彻底抛弃；放弃；暗中处理；消灭|To dispose of something secretly or permanently, often by discarding it in a way that makes it difficult to recover.|例句：They deep-sixed the evidence to avoid prosecution.他们销毁了证据以避免起诉。|例句：The company deep-sixed the failing project. 公司彻底放弃了那个失败的项目。|近义词：discard, scrap, eliminate, destroy|反义词：preserve, save, retain|动词
