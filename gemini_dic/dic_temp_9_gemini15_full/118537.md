/ˈmɛzoʊl/ | n. |中层；中段；中叶|A mesole is a middle part or layer; specifically, in geology, a layer of rock between two other layers.  It can also refer to a middle stage or phase in a process or development.|例句：The mesole of the sedimentary rock formation contained fossils from a previously unknown species. = 沉积岩层的中间层包含来自先前未知物种的化石。|近义词：midsection, middle, intermediate |反义词：extreme, periphery, beginning, end |名词
