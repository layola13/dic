monetize:/ˈmʌnɪtaɪz/|/ˈmɑːnɪtaɪz/| vt.|货币化；使获利|To make money from something.|The company is looking for ways to monetize its new app.  这家公司正在寻找使他们的新应用程序获利的方法。|  They monetized their blog through advertising. 他们通过广告使他们的博客获利。| commercialize, capitalize on |  de-monetize,  devalue| 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They monetize their content through subscriptions. = 他们通过订阅货币化他们的内容。
We monetize our social media presence by selling products. = 我们通过销售产品来货币化我们的社交媒体影响力。


一般过去时:
The musician monetized his music by selling it online. =这位音乐家通过在线销售他的音乐来获利。
The artist monetized her artwork by selling prints. =这位艺术家通过出售印刷品来使她的艺术作品获利。


现在完成时:
They have monetized their blog successfully. =他们已经成功地将他们的博客货币化了。

过去完成时:
