in search:/ɪn sɜːrtʃ/| prep.|在寻找中，寻求|英文解析：This phrase indicates the process of looking for something or someone. It's used to describe an activity or state of being.  |例句：He is in search of a new job. 他正在寻找一份新工作。|近义词：seeking, looking for, searching for |反义词：finding, discovering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are in search of a better life. = 他们在寻找更好的生活。
She is in search of her lost cat. = 她正在寻找她丢失的猫。

一般过去时:
He was in search of adventure. = 他那时正在寻找冒险。
They were in search of a hidden treasure. = 他们当时正在寻找隐藏的宝藏。

现在完成时:
I have been in search of knowledge for years. = 我多年来一直在寻求知识。
We have been in search of the perfect house. = 我们一直在寻找完美的房子。

过去完成时:
She had been in search of her roots for a long time before she found them. = 在找到她的根之前，她已经寻找了很长时间。
They had been in search of the lost city for decades. = 他们寻找失落的城市已经几十年了。

将来时:
I will be in search of a new apartment next month. = 我下个月将要寻找新公寓。
They will be in search of a solution to the problem. = 他们将寻求解决这个问题的办法。

虚拟语气:
If I were in search of a new car, I would check online reviews. = 如果我要买新车，我会查看网上评论。
I wish I were in search of a more challenging job. = 我希望我在寻找更有挑战性的工作。

被动语态:  (The phrase "in search of" is rarely used passively.)  The passive voice is not a natural fit for this prepositional phrase.

疑问句:
Is she in search of a new career? = 她正在寻找新的职业吗？
Are they in search of a quiet place to live? = 他们正在寻找一个安静的居住地吗？

否定句:
He is not in search of fame and fortune. = 他并没有追求名利。
They are not in search of trouble. = 他们并没有在找麻烦。
