nonreprehensibilities:/ˌnɒnˌrɛprɪhɛnsɪˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |无可非议的行为；无过失；无谴责之处 | The absence of anything blameworthy or reprehensible;  the state of being free from blame or censure.  This word is extremely rare and is essentially the plural form of "nonreprehensibility". It describes actions or characteristics that are completely free from fault or criticism.  |例句：The committee found the nonreprehensibilities of the actions taken during the emergency. 委员会认为在紧急情况下采取的行动无可非议。 The report highlighted the nonreprehensibilities of the company's handling of the situation. 报告强调了公司处理此事无可非议。 |近义词：innocence, blamelessness, faultlessness, impeccability |反义词：culpability, blameworthiness, reprehensibility |名词
