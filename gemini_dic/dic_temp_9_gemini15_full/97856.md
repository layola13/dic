inlet:/ˈɪnlet/ | n. | 入口；水湾；小河 | A narrow passage of water leading into a larger body of water, such as a lake or sea; an opening or entrance.  | 例句：The boat sailed into the narrow inlet.  这条船驶入了狭窄的海湾。

The inlet to the harbor was blocked by ice. 港口的入口被冰块堵住了。


近义词：entrance, bay, creek, estuary | 反义词：outlet
