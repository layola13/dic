root-bound: /ˈruːtˌbaʊnd/ | adj. | 根系受限的，根系缠绕的 |  Describes a plant whose roots have become so crowded and intertwined within their container that they are restricted from further growth. This can lead to stunted growth, yellowing leaves, and wilting.  | Example: The tomato plant was root-bound in its small pot, so I repotted it into a larger one.  番茄植株在小盆里根系受限，所以我把它移栽到一个更大的盆里。| 近义词：pot-bound | 反义词：well-rooted, unrestricted


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and thus doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  The usage is descriptive and focuses on the state of the plant.


PHRASE->
root-bound plant: 根系受限的植物
become root-bound: 根系受限
extremely root-bound:  严重根系受限
severely root-bound: 严重根系受限
prevent root-bound: 预防根系受限
