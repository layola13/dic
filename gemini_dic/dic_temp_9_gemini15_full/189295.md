sadomasochists:/ˌsædoʊˈmæzəkɪsts/| n. |施虐受虐狂；性虐待狂|People who derive sexual pleasure from inflicting or receiving pain.  They engage in sadomasochistic practices.  The term encompasses a wide range of behaviors and preferences. |例句：The study examined the psychological profiles of sadomasochists. 这项研究调查了施虐受虐狂的心理特征。

例句：Some researchers argue that sadomasochism is a form of consensual power play. 一些研究人员认为施虐受虐狂是一种双方同意的权力游戏。


近义词：sexual deviants (性变态), masochists (受虐狂), sadists (施虐狂)


反义词：None directly, but terms implying healthy sexual practices could be considered antonyms. (没有直接的反义词，但暗示健康性行为的术语可以被认为是反义词。)


词性：名词
