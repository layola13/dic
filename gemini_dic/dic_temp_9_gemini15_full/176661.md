reaching: /ˈriːtʃɪŋ/ | v. | 到达；伸出；达到；影响 |  The act of arriving at a place or goal, or extending to a point.  It can also refer to the act of making contact or achieving something. |

例句：

The train is reaching its destination. = 火车正在到达目的地。

He reached out to grab the falling book. = 他伸手去抓快要掉下来的书。


近义词：arriving, attaining, achieving, extending

反义词：missing, failing, falling short


USAGE->

一般现在时:

He reaches for the apple. = 他伸手去拿苹果。

She reaches the top of the mountain. = 她到达了山顶。


一般过去时:

They reached the summit yesterday. = 他们昨天到达了山顶。

The rescue team reached the survivors. = 救援队找到了幸存者。


现在完成时:

He has reached a high level of proficiency. = 他已经达到了很高的熟练程度。

We have reached an agreement. = 我们达成了协议。


过去完成时:

They had reached the city before dark. = 他们在天黑前到达了城市。

She had reached her breaking point. = 她已经到了崩溃的边缘。


将来时:

We will reach London tomorrow. = 我们明天将到达伦敦。

The project will reach completion next month. = 项目将于下个月完成。


虚拟语气:

If I could reach him, I would ask for his help. = 如果我能联系上他，我会请求他的帮助。

I wish I could reach a better understanding. = 我希望我能更好地理解。


被动语态:

The agreement was reached after long negotiations. = 在长时间的谈判后达成了协议。

The conclusion was reached through careful analysis. = 通过仔细分析得出结论。


疑问句:

Have you reached a decision yet? = 你已经做出决定了吗？

Did they reach the finish line? = 他们到达终点线了吗？


否定句:

He didn't reach the goal. = 他没有达到目标。

They haven't reached a consensus yet. = 他们还没有达成共识。


PHRASE->

within reach:  触手可及
beyond reach:  够不着；无法达到
reaching out:  伸出援手；联系
reaching for:  伸手去拿
reaching a compromise: 达成妥协
