exterminate:/ɪkˈstɜːrmɪneɪt/ | vt. |消灭；根除；灭绝 | To kill or destroy completely.  |例句：The government launched a campaign to exterminate rats in the city.  政府发起了一场消灭城市老鼠的运动。 |例句：They tried to exterminate the entire population. 他们试图消灭整个人口。|近义词：annihilate, eradicate, eliminate, destroy |反义词：preserve, conserve, protect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government exterminates pests annually. = 政府每年消灭害虫。
We exterminate cockroaches regularly. = 我们定期消灭蟑螂。

一般过去时:
The farmer exterminated the weeds in his field. = 农民除掉了田里的杂草。
They exterminated the entire colony of ants. = 他们消灭了整个蚂蚁群落。

现在完成时:
The pest control company has exterminated the mice infestation. = 灭虫公司已经消灭了老鼠的侵扰。
They have exterminated all the unwanted insects. = 他们已经消灭了所有不需要的昆虫。

过去完成时:
The farmer had exterminated the pests before the harvest. = 农民在收割前已经消灭了害虫。
They had exterminated the disease-carrying mosquitoes. = 他们已经消灭了携带疾病的蚊子。

将来时:
We will exterminate the termites in the house. = 我们将消灭房子里的白蚁。
The government will exterminate the invasive species. = 政府将消灭入侵物种。

虚拟语气:
If we exterminated all the mosquitoes, we could prevent malaria. = 如果我们消灭了所有的蚊子，我们就能预防疟疾。
I wish we could exterminate poverty. = 我希望我们能够消灭贫困。

被动语态:
The rats were exterminated by the poison. = 老鼠被毒药消灭了。
The pests will be exterminated soon. = 害虫很快就会被消灭。

疑问句:
Did they exterminate the snakes? = 他们消灭了蛇吗？
Will the company exterminate the ants for us? = 公司会为我们消灭蚂蚁吗？

否定句:
We did not exterminate the rodents. = 我们没有消灭啮齿动物。
They cannot exterminate the problem entirely. = 他们无法完全消灭这个问题。
