grinch:/ɡrɪntʃ/| n. |吝啬鬼；讨厌鬼；脾气坏的人 |A Grinch is a person who is mean, grumpy, and hates the things that other people enjoy, especially Christmas.  The word is derived from the Dr. Seuss book "How the Grinch Stole Christmas!".|例句：He's such a grinch; he never buys anyone gifts. 他真是个吝啬鬼；他从来不给任何人买礼物。
He acted like a real grinch by ruining everyone's fun. 他像个十足的讨厌鬼，毁了大家的乐趣。|近义词：miser, curmudgeon, Scrooge |反义词：Santa Claus, generous person
