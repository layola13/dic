empassionate:/ɪmˈpæʃənət/ | adj. | 热情的；充满激情的 | Filled with or showing strong feeling; passionate.  |例句：He delivered an empassioned speech about the injustice. 他发表了一篇关于不公正的热情洋溢的演讲。|近义词：passionate, fervent, ardent, zealous |反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unconcerned, detached
