remonstrantly:/rɪˈmɒnstreɪntli/| adv.|抗议地；反对地；坚决地|In a way that shows protest or disapproval.  It suggests strong objection or disagreement, often expressed forcefully but not necessarily aggressively.|He remonstrated with his friend, saying, "I cannot believe you did that!" 他抗议地对朋友说：“我真不敢相信你做了那样的事！”|protestingly, objectingly, vehemently|submissively, acquiescently|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She remonstrates with her boss about the unfair treatment. = 她向老板抗议不公平的待遇。
He remonstrates against the new policy. = 他反对这项新政策。


一般过去时:
They remonstrated against the decision. = 他们反对这个决定。
She remonstrated loudly about the noise. = 她大声抗议噪音。


现在完成时:
They have remonstrated repeatedly but to no avail. = 他们一再抗议，但毫无结果。
He has remonstrated against the injustices he witnessed. = 他一直抗议他所目睹的不公正行为。


过去完成时:
She had remonstrated several times before they finally listened. = 在他们最终听取她的意见之前，她已经抗议过好几次了。
They had remonstrated vehemently, but their voices were ignored. = 他们曾强烈抗议，但他们的声音被忽视了。


将来时:
We will remonstrate if they don't change their plans. = 如果他们不改变计划，我们将抗议。
They will remonstrate at the meeting. = 他们将在会议上抗议。


虚拟语气:
If I had the courage, I would remonstrate against the authorities. = 如果我有勇气，我会反抗当局。
I wish I had remonstrated earlier. = 我真希望我早点抗议。


被动语态:
The unfair ruling was remonstrated against by many. = 许多人反对这项不公平的裁决。
The new policy was remonstrated against by the public. = 公众反对这项新政策。


疑问句:
Will they remonstrate against the proposed changes? = 他们会反对拟议的变动吗？
Did anyone remonstrate against the injustice? = 有人反对这种不公正行为吗？


否定句:
They did not remonstrate against the decision. = 他们没有反对这个决定。
She did not remonstrate, choosing silence instead. = 她没有抗议，而是选择了沉默。


PHRASE->
remonstrate against = 反对
remonstrate with = 向...抗议
remonstrate vehemently = 强烈抗议
remonstrate loudly = 大声抗议
remonstrate fruitlessly = 无效地抗议
