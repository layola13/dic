/hɪmzɛlf/ | pron. | 他自己 | himself 指的是“他自己”，是反身代词，用于指代主语，表示动作由主语自身发出或承受。它通常在句中作宾语、表语或同位语。| He hurt himself playing football. 他踢足球时伤到了自己。| himself | oneself, himself |  |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He teaches himself English. = 他自学英语。
She dresses herself every morning. = 她每天早上自己穿衣服。


一般过去时:
He blamed himself for the accident. = 他为那次事故责备自己。
She hurt herself while skiing. = 她滑雪时伤到了自己。


现在完成时:
He has taught himself to play the guitar. = 他自学了吉他。
She has dressed herself in a beautiful gown. = 她穿上了美丽的礼服。


过去完成时:
He had already prepared himself for the exam. = 他已经为考试做好了准备。
She had dressed herself before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经打扮好了自己。


将来时:
He will reward himself with a vacation. = 他将用假期奖励自己。
She will prepare herself for the interview.  = 她将为面试做好准备。


虚拟语气:
If he were to hurt himself, he would regret it. = 如果他伤到自己，他会后悔的。
I wish she had not hurt herself. = 我希望她没有伤到自己。


被动语态:
(Himself cannot be the subject of a passive sentence.)  反身代词不能作被动句的主语。


疑问句:
Did he hurt himself badly? = 他伤得重吗？
Does she dress herself efficiently? = 她自己穿衣服有效率吗？


否定句:
He didn't hurt himself. = 他没有伤到自己。
She didn't prepare herself adequately. = 她没有充分准备。


PHRASE->
by himself = 独自地
for himself = 为他自己
to himself = 自言自语
in himself = 本身
of himself = 自发地
