lobes:/loʊbz/| n.|叶，瓣；脑叶|A lobe is a rounded projection, division, or part of an organ.  In anatomy, it often refers to sections of the brain, liver, or lungs.|例句：The doctor examined the patient's brain lobes to determine the extent of the damage. 医生检查了病人的脑叶以确定损伤程度。The lungs are divided into lobes. 肺部分为几叶。|近义词：section, division, segment|反义词：N/A
