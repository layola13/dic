notchiest:/ˈnɑːtʃiɪst/| adj.|最凹凸不平的，最缺口的|Most deeply notched; having the most notches.  This word is a superlative form, meaning it represents the highest degree of "notched". It describes something that has more notches or indentations than anything else being compared.|例句：The notchiest surface was almost impossible to grip. 最凹凸不平的表面几乎无法抓握。The notchiest old axe head was the one chosen for the demonstration. 最凹凸不平的旧斧头被选中用于演示。|近义词：most jagged, most uneven, most irregular|反义词：smoothest, flattest
