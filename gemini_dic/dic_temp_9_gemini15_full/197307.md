shattered:/ˈʃætərd/| adj., v.|破碎的；崩溃的；极度震惊的；|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "shattered" means broken into many pieces, or completely destroyed.  It can also describe someone who is emotionally devastated or extremely shocked. As a verb, it means to break something into many pieces, or to completely destroy something, both physically and emotionally.

例句:
The vase was shattered when it fell to the floor.  = 花瓶掉到地上摔碎了。

The news shattered her hopes. = 这个消息粉碎了她的希望。


近义词:
broken, smashed, crushed, destroyed, devastated, wrecked, ruined

反义词:
intact, whole, undamaged, unbroken, complete, sound


词性:
adj., v.
