granade:/ɡrəˈneɪd/| n. |手榴弹|A small bomb that can be thrown by hand.  It is typically filled with explosives and designed to detonate after a short delay or upon impact.|例句：The soldier threw a grenade at the enemy bunker.  士兵向敌人的掩体投掷了一枚手榴弹。
The explosion of the grenade caused significant damage. 手榴弹爆炸造成了重大损失。|近义词：hand grenade, bomb|反义词：N/A
