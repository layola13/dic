nonliquidation: /ˌnɒnˌlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 非清算，不清理 | The act or process of not liquidating; the state of not being liquidated.  It often refers to a business or financial situation where assets are not sold off to pay debts. | Example: The company avoided nonliquidation by securing a new round of funding.  这家公司通过获得新一轮融资避免了清算。 The court ruled in favor of nonliquidation, allowing the company to restructure. 法院裁定支持不进行清算，允许该公司进行重组。 | 近义词：preservation, continuance, survival | 反义词：liquidation, dissolution, winding-up | 名词
