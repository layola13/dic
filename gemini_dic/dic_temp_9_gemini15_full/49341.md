delineate:/dɪˈlɪnieɪt/| vt.|描绘，描画，清晰地勾勒出|To describe or portray (something) precisely or in detail.  To clearly mark the boundaries of something.|The architect delineated the plans for the new building.|近义词：describe, portray, outline, depict|反义词：obscure, confuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist delineates the landscape with great precision. =艺术家精确地描绘了风景。
She delineates her ideas clearly in her essay. =她在论文中清晰地阐述了自己的观点。


一般过去时:
He delineated the problem in detail during the meeting. =他在会议上详细地阐述了这个问题。
The map delineated the boundaries of the property. =地图勾勒出了房产的界限。


现在完成时:
The author has delineated the characters' personalities throughout the novel. =作者在小说中勾勒出了人物的性格。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
