laxations:/ˌlækˈseɪʃənz/| n. |放松；宽松；缓解；通便|The term "laxations" refers to the act of loosening, relaxing, or relieving something, often used in the context of easing restrictions or regulations, or in a medical sense to refer to bowel movements.  It is a less common word, and its usage is often more formal or technical.|例句：The government announced laxations in trade restrictions. 政府宣布放松贸易限制。The doctor prescribed laxations to alleviate constipation. 医生开了通便药来缓解便秘。|近义词：relaxations, easements, loosenings, reliefs|反义词： tightenings, restrictions, constraints|名词
