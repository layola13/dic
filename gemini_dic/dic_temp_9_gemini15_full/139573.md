obverse:/ˈɒbvɜːrs/ /ˈɑːbvɜːrs/| n. |正面，正面图；反面；相对面 |The side of something that is visible or presented first; the opposite of the reverse.  It often refers to the front of a coin or medal, as opposed to the reverse. |例句：The obverse of the coin depicts the queen's head. 这枚硬币的正面描绘着女王的头像。

The obverse of his argument is equally compelling. 他论点的反面同样令人信服。|近义词：front, face, exterior |反义词：reverse
