ecotourist:/ˈekoʊˌtʊərɪst/| n. |生态旅游者|A person who travels to natural areas, especially to observe wildlife or scenery, in a way that minimizes environmental impact.|例句：Many ecotourists visit the Amazon rainforest to experience its unique biodiversity.  许多生态旅游者访问亚马逊雨林以体验其独特的生物多样性。  Many ecotourists are concerned about sustainability. 许多生态旅游者关注可持续性。|近义词：environmentalist, nature lover, green traveler |反义词：polluter, environmental destroyer
