gaiting:/ˈɡeɪtɪŋ/| n. |步态；步法；行走方式|The manner of walking, especially as a characteristic of a person, animal, or disease.  Gaiting analysis is used in various fields to assess movement and detect abnormalities.|例句：Her gaiting suggested she was in pain.  她的步态表明她很疼。

例句：The horse's gaiting was smooth and effortless. 这匹马的步态流畅而轻松。


近义词：walk, stride, tread, locomotion | 反义词：None


词性：名词
