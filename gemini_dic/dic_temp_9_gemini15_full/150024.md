palpable:/ˈpælpəbəl/| adj.|可感知的；可触知的；明显的；真实的 |Easily perceived; readily noticeable; tangible.  It refers to something that is so strong or obvious that it can almost be felt or touched, even if it is not physically tangible.  |例句：The tension in the room was palpable.  房间里的紧张气氛非常明显。

例句：Her disappointment was palpable. 她的失望显而易见。


近义词：tangible, perceptible, noticeable, evident, obvious, clear

反义词：intangible, imperceptible, unnoticeable, subtle, obscure


词源：From Latin *palpare "to touch, stroke, feel," from PIE *pel- "to feel."  The suffix -able means "capable of being."
