immunities:/ɪˈmjuːnɪtiz/ | n. | 免疫力；豁免权；特权 |  The state of being immune to a particular disease or the condition of being exempt from something, typically a legal obligation or penalty.  | 例句：The vaccine provides immunity against the flu.  疫苗提供对流感的免疫力。

例句：  He claimed diplomatic immunity from prosecution. 他声称享有外交豁免权，不受起诉。


近义词：exemptions, privileges, protections | 反义词：vulnerability, susceptibility, liability
