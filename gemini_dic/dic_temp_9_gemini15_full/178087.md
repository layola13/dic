reclothed:/ˌriːˈkloʊðd/| v. |重新穿衣；更换衣物 |To clothe again; to put new clothes on.  The word implies a change of clothing, often after a previous state of undress or a change of attire. |例句：After his swim, he reclothed himself quickly.  游泳后，他迅速地重新穿上了衣服。

例句：The statue, reclothed in fresh paint, looked as good as new.  重新粉刷过的雕像看起来焕然一新。


近义词：redress, re-dress, clothe anew | 反义词：undress, disrobe | 词性：verb
