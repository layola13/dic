词源:

The word "hyperpromiscuous" is a combination of "hyper-" (meaning excessive or above normal) and "promiscuous" (meaning having many sexual partners).  "Promiscuous" itself originates from the Latin word "promiscuus," meaning "common," "mixed," or "indiscriminate."
