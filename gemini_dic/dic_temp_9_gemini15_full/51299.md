detail:/ˈdiːteɪl/| n.|细节，详情|a single piece of information or fact about something.|The report provided details of the accident. 报告提供了事故的细节。|近义词：particular, specific, item|反义词：summary, overview


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The architect details every aspect of the building. = 建筑师详细地描述了建筑物的各个方面。
This plan details the project's timeline. = 这个计划详细说明了项目的进度安排。

一般过去时:
She detailed her experiences in her diary. = 她详细地记录了她的经历在日记里。
He detailed the steps involved in the process. = 他详细说明了该过程涉及的步骤。


现在完成时:
The artist has detailed the scene with incredible accuracy. = 艺术家已以令人难以置信的精确度描绘了场景。
They have detailed all the necessary information in the report. = 他们在报告中详细说明了所有必要信息。


过去完成时:
She had detailed her concerns to her supervisor before the meeting. = 在会议之前，她已向她的主管详细说明了她的担忧。
He had detailed the plan before presenting it. = 他在展示计划之前已经详细说明了计划。


将来时:
She will detail her findings in the upcoming presentation. = 她将在即将举行的演示中详细说明她的发现。
The report will detail the company's financial performance. = 该报告将详细说明公司的财务业绩。


虚拟语气:
If she had detailed the problem earlier, it could have been avoided. = 如果她早些时候详细说明了这个问题，它本来是可以避免的。
I wish he had detailed his reasoning more clearly. = 我希望他更清楚地说明了他的理由。


被动语态:
The plan was detailed in the meeting. = 计划在会议上进行了详细说明。
Every aspect of the project will be detailed in the final report. = 项目的各个方面都将在最终报告中详细说明。


疑问句:
Can you detail the steps involved? = 你能详细说明所涉及的步骤吗？
Does the report detail the causes of the problem? = 该报告是否详细说明了问题的起因？


否定句:
The report did not detail the financial implications. = 该报告没有详细说明财务影响。
He didn't detail his reasons for leaving. = 他没有详细说明他离职的原因。
