in-filled:/ɪnˈfɪld/ | adj. |填充的，填满的|Filled in or completed.  This adjective describes something that has had gaps or spaces filled. |例句：The in-filled form was submitted on time. 填好的表格按时提交了。 The in-filled cracks in the pavement were barely noticeable. 人行道上填补的裂缝几乎看不出来。|近义词：filled, completed, full|反义词：empty, unfilled, vacant|
