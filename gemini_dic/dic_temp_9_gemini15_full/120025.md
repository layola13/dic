millionairesses:/ˌmɪljəˈnɛrɛsɪz/| n.|女百万富翁们|The plural form of "millionairess," referring to more than one woman possessing a net worth of at least one million dollars.  It emphasizes the female gender.  |例句：The millionairesses donated generously to the charity. 女百万富翁们慷慨地捐赠给了慈善机构。

例句： Several millionairesses attended the gala.  好几位女百万富翁出席了这次盛会。


近义词：wealthy women, women of means, female tycoons


反义词：pauper women, indigent women


词性：Noun (plural)
