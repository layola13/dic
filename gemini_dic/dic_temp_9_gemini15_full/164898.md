pregalvanize: /ˌpriːˈɡæl vəˌnaɪz/ | vt. | 预先镀锌；预先进行镀锌处理 | To coat (metal) with zinc before further processing or use.  This is typically done to protect the metal from corrosion. | 例句：The steel components were pregalvanized to enhance their durability.  预先对钢材部件进行了镀锌处理以提高其耐用性。  The factory pregalvanizes all its steel sheets before painting them.  这家工厂在给所有钢板喷漆之前先进行镀锌处理。 | 近义词：zinc-coat, galvanize in advance | 反义词：None directly opposite, but "leave uncoated" could be considered an antonym.
