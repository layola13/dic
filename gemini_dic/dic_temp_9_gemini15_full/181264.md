reinstituted:/ˌriːˈɪnstɪtuːt/| v. |重新实行；恢复；重建 | To establish or restore something again, especially a law, system, or practice that had been abolished or discontinued.  |例句：The government reinstituted the death penalty.  政府重新实行死刑。

例句：After a period of suspension, the school reinstituted its music program.  停办一段时间后，学校恢复了音乐课程。


近义词：restore, revive, reinstate, re-establish

反义词：abolish, discontinue, eliminate
