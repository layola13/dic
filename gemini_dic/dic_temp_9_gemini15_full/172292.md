put-on-bold-face:/pʊt ɒn bəʊld feɪs/| vt.|加粗；使醒目|To make text or an image appear in bold typeface.  It can also refer to a deliberate and often exaggerated display of confidence or bravado.|例句：Please put the title in bold face. 请将标题加粗。|例句：He put on a bold face and walked into the room, despite his nervousness. 尽管很紧张，他还是故作镇定地走进了房间。|近义词：emphasize, highlight, embolden|反义词：italicize, dim, downplay|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He puts the important words in bold face. = 他把重要的词加粗。
They put all the headings in bold face. = 他们把所有标题都加粗了。


一般过去时:
She put the address in bold face. = 她把地址加粗了。
We put the key points in bold face for emphasis. = 我们为了强调把要点加粗了。


现在完成时:
I have put all the names in bold face. = 我已经把所有名字都加粗了。
They have put the figures in bold face to make them stand out. = 他们已将数字加粗以使它们脱颖而出。


过去完成时:
