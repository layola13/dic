hemachrome:/ˈhiːməˌkroʊm/| n. |血色素沉着；血色素沉积|A condition characterized by an excessive accumulation of hemosiderin in the tissues, often due to iron overload.  It can manifest in various organs and lead to organ damage.|Example:  Hemochromatosis is a serious condition that requires medical attention. 血色素沉着症是一种需要医疗护理的严重疾病。|近义词：iron overload, hemosiderosis|反义词：iron deficiency|名词
