cowritten:/koʊˈrɪtən/| adj.| 合著的，共同创作的 | jointly written by two or more people.  |例句：The book was cowritten by two renowned authors. 这本书是由两位著名作家合著的。|近义词：collaboratively written, jointly authored |反义词：solely written, single-authored |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The screenplay is cowritten by two talented writers. = 剧本是由两位才华横溢的作家合著的。
This song is cowritten by a famous musician and a newcomer. = 这首歌是由一位著名音乐家和一位新人合著的。

一般过去时:
The novel was cowritten by a husband and wife team. = 这部小说是由一对夫妻合著的。
The article was cowritten by three researchers from different universities. = 这篇文章是由来自不同大学的三位研究人员合著的。

现在完成时:
They have cowritten several successful books together. = 他们已经一起合著了几本成功的书。
She has cowritten numerous articles for prestigious journals. = 她已经为著名的期刊合著了许多文章。

过去完成时:

By the time the project ended, they had cowritten a comprehensive report. = 在项目结束时，他们已经合著了一份全面的报告。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

Is the screenplay being cowritten by a team? = 剧本是由一个团队合著的吗？

否定句:

The report was not cowritten by any external consultants. = 这份报告没有由任何外部顾问合著。
