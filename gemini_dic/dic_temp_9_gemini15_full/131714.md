nonconductor:/ˌnɒnkənˈdʌktə(r)/| n. |非导体，绝缘体 |A substance that does not readily transmit heat or electricity.  It resists the flow of electric current and the transfer of heat. |Examples:  Rubber is a good nonconductor of electricity. 橡胶是良好的绝缘体。  Wood is a poor conductor, thus a nonconductor of heat. 木头是导热不良的物质，因此是绝缘体。|近义词：insulator, dielectric |反义词：conductor
