carabine:/ˈkærəbiːn/|/ˈkærebain/| n.|卡宾枪，短步枪|A carbine is a short-barreled rifle, typically lighter and shorter than a standard rifle, designed for ease of handling and portability.  It's often used by cavalry, mounted infantry, or other troops requiring a compact firearm.|Example: The soldier carried a carbine for quick and easy access in close-quarters combat.  士兵携带卡宾枪以便在近距离作战中快速方便地使用。|近义词：rifle, short rifle|反义词：long rifle
