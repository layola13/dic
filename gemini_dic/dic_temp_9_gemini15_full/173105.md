quash:/kwɒʃ/| /kwɑːʃ/ | vt. |压制；取消；使无效；镇压|To suppress or extinguish something, especially a rebellion or a legal proceeding.  To invalidate or annul something, especially a legal proceeding. |例句：The judge quashed the conviction because of insufficient evidence. = 由于证据不足，法官撤销了定罪。

The police quickly quashed the riot. = 警方迅速平息了暴乱。

|近义词：suppress, annul, overturn, invalidate, crush |反义词：uphold, confirm, validate, ratify |动词
