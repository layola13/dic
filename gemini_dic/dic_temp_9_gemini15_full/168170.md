pro-Catholic: /ˌproʊ ˈkæθəlɪk/ | adj. | 亲天主教的，支持天主教的 |  Supporting or favoring the Catholic Church and its doctrines.  | The pro-Catholic sentiment in the region was strong, influencing local politics.  这个地区的亲天主教情绪强烈，影响着当地政治。 | pro-religious, Catholic-supporting | anti-Catholic, secular
