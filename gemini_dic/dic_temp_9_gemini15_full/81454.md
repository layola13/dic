gratins:/ˈɡræˈtæ̃z/| n.| gratin, 烤菜, gratinéed菜肴|Gratins are dishes typically consisting of vegetables, pasta, or cheese baked until browned and bubbly, often with a crispy topping.  They are usually prepared in a shallow, oven-proof dish.|例句：The creamy potato gratin was a perfect side dish for the roast chicken.  奶油土豆烤菜是烤鸡的完美配菜。
例句：We ordered the seafood gratin, which was full of flavour. 我们点了海鲜烤菜，味道浓郁。|近义词：au gratin, scalloped, baked|反义词：无
