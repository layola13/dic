hatred:/ˈheɪtrɪd/| n. |憎恨，仇恨 | intense dislike; animosity.  Hatred is a strong feeling of dislike or ill will. It often involves a desire to harm or see harm come to the object of hatred. |例句：His hatred for his brother was evident in his every action. = 他对兄弟的憎恨在他的一举一动中都显而易见。

例句：The speech was filled with hatred and prejudice. = 这篇演讲充满了仇恨和偏见。


近义词：animosity, loathing, abhorrence, detestation, antipathy |反义词：love, affection, fondness, adoration
