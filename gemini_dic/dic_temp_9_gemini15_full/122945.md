moments:/ˈmoʊmənts/| n.|瞬间，片刻，时刻|A short period of time.  It can refer to a specific point in time, a brief experience, or a significant event.|例句：I'll be with you in a few moments.  一会儿我就来。
例句：Those were happy moments in my life. 那些是我生命中快乐的时光。|近义词：instant, second, minute, period|反义词:eternity, ages
