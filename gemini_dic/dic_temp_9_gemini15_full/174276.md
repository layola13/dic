questioning:/ˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ/| adj. n. |发问的；疑问的；质问的| Expressing or characterized by questioning or doubt.  The act of questioning.|例句：His questioning expression showed his doubt. 他质疑的表情显示了他的怀疑。

His questioning of the witness was thorough. 他对证人的质询非常彻底。|近义词：inquisitive, inquiring, interrogative, doubtful |反义词：certain, unquestioning, accepting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is questioning the suspect. = 他正在盘问嫌疑犯。
She is questioning the validity of the results. = 她正在质疑结果的有效性。


一般过去时:
The police questioned him for hours. = 警方盘问了他几个小时。
They questioned her motives. = 他们质疑她的动机。


现在完成时:
I have been questioning my decisions lately. = 我最近一直在质疑我的决定。
She has questioned the government's policies. = 她一直质疑政府的政策。


过去完成时:
They had questioned the witness before the trial. = 他们在审判前已经盘问过证人。
He had questioned the accuracy of the data. = 他曾质疑数据的准确性。


将来时:
The lawyer will question the defendant tomorrow. = 律师明天将盘问被告。
She will question him about his whereabouts. = 她将询问他的下落。


虚拟语气:
If I questioned him, he might confess. = 如果我盘问他，他可能会坦白。
I wish I had questioned him more thoroughly. = 我真希望我当时更彻底地盘问了他。


被动语态:
The witness was questioned by the police. = 证人被警方盘问了。
His motives are being questioned by many. = 许多人都在质疑他的动机。


疑问句:
Is he questioning your authority? = 他在质疑你的权威吗？
Are they questioning the new policy? = 他们正在质疑这项新政策吗？


否定句:
He wasn't questioning my honesty. = 他并没有质疑我的诚实。
They didn't question her alibi. = 他们没有质疑她的不在场证明。


PHRASE->
under questioning = 在审问中
in question = 正在讨论中的
beyond question = 无可置疑的
open to question = 有疑问的
raise questions = 提出疑问
