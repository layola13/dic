misintend:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛnd/| vt.|误解，曲解|To understand wrongly; misinterpret.|He misintended my meaning and became angry. 他误解了我的意思，生气了。|understand, misconstrue|understand correctly, interpret correctly|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I misinterpret your words. = 我误解了你的话。
He misintends my intentions. = 他误解了我的意图。


一般过去时:
They misintended the instructions. = 他们误解了指示。
She misintended the message. = 她误解了信息。


现在完成时:
I have misintended his motives. = 我一直误解了他的动机。
We have misintended the situation. = 我们一直误解了情况。


过去完成时:
They had misintended the agreement. = 他们先前误解了协议。
He had misintended her silence. = 他先前误解了她的沉默。


将来时:
I will misintend his actions. = 我将会误解他的行为。
They will misintend the proposal. = 他们将会误解这个提议。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't misintended you, I wouldn't have reacted so angrily. = 如果我没有误解你，我不会反应那么生气。
If she had not misintended his words, they would not have quarreled. = 如果她没有误解他的话，他们就不会吵架了。


被动语态:
His words were misintended by many. = 他的话被许多人误解了。
The instructions were misintended by the workers. = 指示被工人误解了。


疑问句:
Did I misintend his intentions? = 我是否误解了他的意图？
Did she misintend your email? = 她是否误解了你的邮件？


否定句:
I did not misintend your comments. = 我没有误解你的评论。
He did not misintend her explanation. = 他没有误解她的解释。


PHRASE->
misintend completely = 完全误解
misintend badly = 严重误解
misintend deliberately = 故意曲解
misintend constantly = 反复误解
misintend someone's actions = 误解某人的行为
