modeling:/ˈmɒdəlɪŋ/ /ˈmɑːdəlɪŋ/| n. v.| 建模；造型；模仿；塑造；款式；模特行业|The process of creating a model, often a simplified representation of a system or process.  It can refer to the creation of physical models, mathematical models, or computer models.  As a verb, it means to create or work with such models.  In fashion, it refers to the profession of a model.|例句：The architect presented a 3D modeling of the new building. 建筑师展示了新建筑的3D模型。

The fashion designer used computer modeling to create intricate designs.时装设计师使用电脑建模来创建复杂的设计。


近义词：simulation, representation, emulation, design, modeling (as a noun)|反义词：reality (in the sense of a contrast between a model and reality)
