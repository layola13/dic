empathises:/ˈɛmpəθaɪzɪz/| v.| 同情；共鸣；理解|To share and understand the feelings of another.|She empathised with his grief after the loss of his dog. 她对失去爱犬后的他的悲痛表示同情。|sympathise, understand, relate to|indifference, apathy|动词


USAGE->

She empathizes with her friend's situation. = 她同情她朋友的处境。
