envisaging:/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|设想，想象，展望|To form a mental picture of something not yet existing or experienced.|The architect was envisaging a building that would be both functional and aesthetically pleasing. 建筑师设想着一座既实用又美观的建筑物。|近义词：imagining, conceiving, visualizing|反义词：ignoring, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He envisages a bright future for his company. 他设想公司拥有光明的前景。
They are envisaging a new marketing strategy. 他们正在设想一个新的营销策略。

一般过去时:
She envisaged a life of luxury and ease. 她设想过一种奢侈舒适的生活。
The developers envisaged a thriving community. 开发商设想了一个繁荣的社区。

现在完成时:
We have envisaged several possible scenarios. 我们已经设想了几种可能的情况。
They have envisaged the completion of the project by next year. 他们已经设想项目明年完成。

过去完成时:
They had envisaged many difficulties before starting the project. 他们在项目开始前就已经预料到许多困难。
She had envisaged a different outcome. 她原本设想过不同的结果。

将来时:
I will envisage a better future for my children. 我将为我的孩子设想一个更美好的未来。
We will envisage the challenges ahead. 我们将设想未来的挑战。

虚拟语气:
If I had envisaged this problem, I would have prepared for it. 如果我预料到这个问题，我就会做好准备。
I wish I had envisaged the consequences. 我真希望我当时预料到了后果。

被动语态:
A bright future is envisaged for the company. 公司被设想拥有光明的前景。
Several solutions were envisaged by the team. 团队设想了几个解决方案。

疑问句:
What do you envisage happening next? 你设想接下来会发生什么？
What are they envisaging for the future? 他们设想未来会怎样？

否定句:
I don't envisage any problems arising. 我预料不会出现任何问题。
They did not envisage such a dramatic change. 他们没有预料到如此剧烈的变化。
