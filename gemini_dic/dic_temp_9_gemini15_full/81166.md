granddaughters: /ˈɡrændɔːtəz/ | n. | 孙女们 |  The word "granddaughters" refers to the daughters of one's children. It is a plural noun.  | My granddaughters are visiting this weekend.  我的孙女们这周末要来拜访。  | nieces,  | sons (antonym applies to the relationship, not the word itself)|名词
