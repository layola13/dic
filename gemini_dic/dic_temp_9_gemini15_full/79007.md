/ˈɡiːtɑː/|/ˈɡiːtə/ | n. |《薄伽梵歌》 | The Gita is a 700-verse Hindu scripture that is part of the epic Mahabharata.  It is a dialogue between Arjuna, a warrior, and his guide Krishna, a god in human form. The Gita explores themes of dharma (duty), karma (action), and moksha (liberation).|  The Gita offers profound insights into the nature of reality and the path to spiritual liberation. |  近义词: Bhagavad Gita (full name)| 反义词: N/A
