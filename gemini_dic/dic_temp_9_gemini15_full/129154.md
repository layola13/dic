no matter:/ˌnoʊ ˈmætər/ | conj. | 无论如何，不管怎样 |  This phrase is a conjunction used to introduce a clause that expresses a condition that does not affect the main clause. It emphasizes that the result or action in the main clause will happen regardless of the condition.  | I will finish this project, no matter what happens. = 无论发生什么，我都会完成这个项目。

I'll go, no matter how late it is. = 无论多晚，我都会去。| no matter how, regardless of, irrespective of |  matter (n.) |  no matter what, no matter how, no matter when |
