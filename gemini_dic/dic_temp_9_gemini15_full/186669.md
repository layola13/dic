right-clicking:/ˈraɪtˈklɪkɪŋ/| v. |右键点击|The act of pressing the right button on a computer mouse to access a context menu.|例句：Right-clicking on the icon brings up a menu of options. 右键点击图标会弹出一个选项菜单。


例句：I right-clicked on the file and selected "Delete". 我右键点击了文件并选择了“删除”。

近义词：context-menu access, secondary click

反义词：left-clicking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He right-clicks the icon every time. 他每次都右键点击图标。
She right-clicks to open the menu. 她右键点击以打开菜单。

一般过去时:
I right-clicked the file and deleted it. 我右键点击了文件并删除了它。
They right-clicked the image to save it. 他们右键点击图片将其保存。

现在完成时:
I have right-clicked many times today. 我今天已经右键点击了很多次了。
She has right-clicked to open that before. 她之前已经右键点击过打开那个了。

过去完成时:
I had right-clicked the file before realizing it was important. 我在意识到文件很重要之前已经右键点击了它。
They had right-clicked numerous times before finding the option. 他们在找到选项之前已经右键点击了很多次。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
