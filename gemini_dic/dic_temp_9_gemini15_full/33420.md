churchwarden:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːdn/|/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdn/| n. |教堂管理人；（英）教区议员|A churchwarden is an elected member of the governing body of a Church of England parish.  They are responsible for the church building, its finances, and often for some aspects of the parish's social life.  In some cases, they also assist the clergy. |The churchwardens organized a fundraising dinner to repair the church roof. 教堂管理人组织了一场募捐晚宴来修理教堂的屋顶。

The churchwardens are responsible for maintaining the church grounds. 教区议员负责维护教堂的场地。|lay reader, vestry member|—|
