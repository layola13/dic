leas:/liːz/| n.|租赁，租约，租期|A lease is a contract granting use or occupation of property during a specified time for a specified payment.|例句：We signed a lease for a year. 我们签了一年的租约。|例句：The lease on my apartment is up next month. 我的公寓租约下个月到期。|近义词：rental, tenancy, letting|反义词：ownership


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leases a car every year. 他每年租一辆车。
She leases an apartment downtown. 她租了一套市中心的公寓。

一般过去时:
They leased the land for farming. 他们租赁了这块土地用于耕种。
We leased a car for our vacation. 我们租了一辆车用于度假。

现在完成时:
They have leased the property for five years. 他们已经租赁了这处房产五年了。
She has leased several apartments in her life. 她一生中租过几套公寓。

过去完成时:
He had leased the building before the company went bankrupt. 公司破产前他已经租赁了那栋楼。
They had leased the equipment for the project. 他们已经为这个项目租赁了设备。

将来时:
We will lease a new office next year. 我们明年将租赁一个新的办公室。
They will lease the property to a new tenant. 他们将把房产租赁给一个新的租户。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would lease a bigger house. 如果我有更多钱，我会租一栋更大的房子。
I wish I could lease a car for the summer. 我希望我能租一辆车过夏天。

被动语态:
The property was leased to a new company. 该房产被租赁给了一家新公司。
The car is being leased by a customer. 这辆车正在被一位顾客租赁。

疑问句:
Does he lease a car? 他租车吗？
How long will they lease the apartment? 他们将租赁公寓多久？

否定句:
They don't lease any property. 他们没有租赁任何房产。
She didn't lease a car last year. 她去年没有租车。


PHRASE->
lease agreement: 租赁协议
lease term: 租期
lease payment: 租金支付
break a lease: 违反租赁协议
lease option: 租赁期权
