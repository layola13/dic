enlighteningly: /ɪnˈlaɪtnɪŋli/ | adv. | 富有启发性地；具有启发意义地 | In a way that provides enlightenment or understanding.  It describes something that is informative and insightful, leading to a greater understanding. |


例句：

The professor explained the complex theory enlighteningly, making it accessible to all students.  教授富有启发性地解释了这个复杂的理论，使所有学生都能理解。

His enlighteningly honest account of the events shed new light on the situation. 他富有启发意义地、诚实地叙述了事件经过，为局势带来了新的光明。


近义词：instructively, illuminatingly, informatively, insightfully


反义词：confusingly, obscurely, unclearly


词性：副词
