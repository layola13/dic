hellenist:/ˈhɛlənɪst/| n. |希腊学者；希腊文化研究者；希腊化时期的人|A hellenist is a scholar or student of Greek language, literature, and culture.  The term can also refer to a person living during the Hellenistic period (roughly 323-31 BC), a time when Greek culture spread throughout the Mediterranean world. |Example:  Many prominent hellenists contributed significantly to the understanding of ancient Greek philosophy.  许多著名的希腊学者为理解古希腊哲学做出了重大贡献。|近义词：classicist, Grecian, Greek scholar |反义词：无
