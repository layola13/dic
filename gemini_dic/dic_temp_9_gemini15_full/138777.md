numerously:/ˈnjuːmərəsli/ | adv. |多次地；许多地；大量地 | Many times; in large numbers.  |例句：The benefits of exercise have been documented numerous times. 运动的好处已经被多次记录。

例句：The problems were numerous and complex. 问题很多而且很复杂。


近义词：frequently, repeatedly, abundantly, copiously, plentifully |反义词：scarcely, rarely, seldom, infrequently


词源:  From numerous + -ly.  "Numerous" itself derives from the Latin word "numerus," meaning "number." The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "numerous" into an adverb.
