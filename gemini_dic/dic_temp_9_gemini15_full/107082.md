laddo:/ˈlædoʊ/| n. |拉杜（一种印度甜食）|A laddo is a sweet ball-shaped confectionery from the Indian subcontinent. It is made from ingredients such as flour, sugar, ghee (clarified butter), and nuts.  Different regions and communities have their own variations and recipes. |Example:  My grandmother makes the best coconut ladoos. 我奶奶做的椰子拉杜最好吃。|近义词：sweet, confection, ball (in this context)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with savory snacks.|名词
