explaining:/ɪkˈspleɪnɪŋ/ | v.ing | 解释；说明 |  The act of making something clear or understandable.  It involves providing information or reasons to clarify something. | Example:  His explaining the complex theory made it easier to understand.  他的解释使这个复杂的理论更容易理解。|近义词：clarifying, elucidating, illustrating | 反义词：obscuring, confusing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is explaining the rules. = 他正在解释规则。
She explains the process clearly. = 她清晰地解释了这个过程。


一般过去时:
The teacher explained the lesson yesterday. = 老师昨天解释了这节课。
He explained his absence to his boss. = 他向老板解释了他的缺席。


现在完成时:
She has explained the situation to everyone. = 她已经向每个人解释了情况。
They have explained their decision multiple times. = 他们已经多次解释了他们的决定。


过去完成时:
After he had explained the problem, we understood. = 在他解释了问题之后，我们就明白了。
They had explained the rules before the game started. = 比赛开始前他们已经解释了规则。


将来时:
He will explain the details tomorrow. = 他明天将解释细节。
She will explain the project to the team next week. = 她下周将向团队解释这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If he explained it more clearly, I would understand. = 如果他解释得更清楚一些，我就会明白。
I wish he had explained the instructions better. = 我希望他之前能把说明解释得更好一些。


被动语态:
The rules were explained to the students. = 规则向学生们解释了。
The situation is being explained by the spokesperson. = 情况正在由发言人解释。


疑问句:
Can you explain this to me? = 你能向我解释一下这个吗？
Will you explain how it works? = 你会解释一下它是如何工作的吗？


否定句:
He didn't explain the reason for his actions. = 他没有解释他行为的原因。
She couldn't explain the complicated formula. = 她无法解释这个复杂的公式。


PHRASE->
explaining away = 试图解释或掩盖(不好的事情)
explaining oneself = 为自己的行为辩护
explaining in detail = 详细解释
explaining the reasons = 解释原因
explaining the situation = 解释情况
