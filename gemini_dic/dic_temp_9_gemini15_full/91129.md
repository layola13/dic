hybridising:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 杂交；混合；使杂交 | The process of combining different varieties or species of organisms to create a hybrid. This often involves combining the genetic material of two different organisms. |例句：Scientists are hybridising different types of rice to create a strain resistant to drought.  科学家们正在将不同类型的稻米杂交，以培育出抗旱的品种。

例句：The artist is hybridising various artistic styles in their latest work.  这位艺术家在其最新作品中融合了各种艺术风格。


近义词：crossbreeding, interbreeding, mixing, blending | 反义词：purifying, separating, isolating
