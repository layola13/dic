epopee:/ˈɛpəpiː/| n. |史诗；长篇叙事诗 |A long narrative poem celebrating the achievements of a hero, often of national or historical significance.  It typically involves gods or supernatural beings and covers a vast scope of time and space. |Example: The *Iliad* and the *Odyssey* are classic examples of epopees.  《伊利亚特》和《奥德赛》是史诗的经典例子。|近义词：epic poem, epic |反义词：short story, anecdote
