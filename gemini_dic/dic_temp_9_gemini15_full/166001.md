prepend:/prɪˈpɛnd/| vt.|  前置，添加在前面 | To place something at the beginning of something else.  |例句：Please prepend the date to the filename. 请在文件名之前添加日期。|例句：The editor prepended a new chapter to the book. 编辑在书的开头添加了一个新章节。|近义词：add, prefix, insert at the beginning |反义词：append, suffix


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prepends a greeting to every email. = 他在每封电子邮件的开头添加问候语。
They prepend the company logo to all their documents. = 他们将公司标志添加到所有文件的开头。

一般过去时:
She prepended a disclaimer to the article. = 她在一篇文章的开头添加了免责声明。
We prepended the subject line to the email body. = 我们将主题行添加到邮件正文的前面。


现在完成时:
He has prepended the necessary information to the report. = 他已将必要信息添加到报告的开头。
They have prepended the chapter title to each chapter. = 他们已经将章节标题添加到每个章节的前面。


过去完成时:
She had prepended a note to the document before sending it. = 在发送文件之前，她已在文件开头添加了一则说明。
They had prepended the introduction to the presentation. = 他们在演示文稿的开头添加了引言。


将来时:
I will prepend the address to the shipping label. = 我将在发货标签上添加地址。
They will prepend a warning message to the software update. = 他们将在软件更新中添加警告消息。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would prepend a summary to the report. = 如果我是你，我会在报告开头添加一个摘要。
It would be better if they prepended a table of contents to the book. = 如果他们在书的开头添加目录会更好。


被动语态:
The date was prepended to the file name. = 日期被添加到文件名之前。
A new section was prepended to the document. = 一個新的章节被添加到文档的前面。


疑问句:
Did they prepend the correct information? = 他们添加了正确的信息吗？
Will you prepend the author's name to the article? = 你会在文章的开头添加作者的名字吗？


否定句:
He did not prepend the address to the package. = 他没有在包裹上添加地址。
They did not prepend the necessary information to the form. = 他们没有在表格开头添加必要信息。


PHRASE->

prepend a list = 添加一个列表
prepend a value = 添加一个值
prepend a prefix = 添加一个前缀
