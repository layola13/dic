madeleine:/ˌmædˈlɛn/| n. |马德琳蛋糕|A small, shell-shaped sponge cake, often made with butter and lemon.  It is typically baked in a special mold that gives it its distinctive shape.|例句：She baked a batch of delicious madeleines for the tea party. 她为茶话会烤了一批美味的马德琳蛋糕。

例句：The aroma of freshly baked madeleines filled the kitchen. 新鲜出炉的马德琳蛋糕的香味充满了厨房。


近义词：small cake, sponge cake|反义词：无
