ranched:/ræntʃt/| v. |经营牧场；在牧场上放牧|To manage or operate a ranch; to graze livestock on a ranch. |He ranched cattle in Texas for many years. 他在德克萨斯州经营养牛场多年。| farmed, grazed | abandoned, deserted | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ranches cattle in Montana. 他在蒙大拿州放牧牛群。
They ranch sheep on a large scale. 他们大规模地放牧绵羊。

一般过去时:
My grandfather ranched horses on this land. 我的祖父在这片土地上养马。
They ranched sheep in the mountains last summer. 他们去年夏天在山里放牧绵羊。

现在完成时:
He has ranched in this area for over thirty years. 他在这个地区经营牧场超过三十年了。
They have ranched cattle successfully for generations. 他们世世代代成功地经营养牛场。

过去完成时:
Before he retired, he had ranched for fifty years. 在他退休之前，他已经经营牧场五十年了。
They had ranched in the valley before moving to the plains. 他们在搬到平原之前曾在山谷里放牧。

将来时:
She will ranch horses on her new property. 她将在她的新房产上养马。
They will ranch sheep in the foothills next year. 他们明年将在山脚下放牧绵羊。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would ranch. 如果我有钱，我会经营牧场。
If he ranched, he would be much happier. 如果他经营牧场，他会快乐得多。

被动语态:
The land was ranched for generations. 这片土地世世代代都用来放牧。
The cattle were ranched carefully to ensure their health. 牛群被精心放牧以确保它们的健康。

疑问句:
Does he ranch cattle or sheep? 他是养牛还是养羊？
Do they still ranch on that property? 他们还在那块土地上放牧吗？

否定句:
He doesn't ranch anymore. 他不再经营牧场了。
They didn't ranch in that area last year. 他们去年没有在那个地区放牧。
