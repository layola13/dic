northeast:/ˌnɔːrθˈiːst/ | adj., n., adv. | 东北；东北方的 |  Northeast refers to the direction or region that is both north and east. As an adjective, it describes something located in or relating to the northeast. As a noun, it refers to the northeast direction or region itself. As an adverb, it indicates movement or location towards the northeast. | Examples: The wind is blowing from the northeast. (adv.)  The northeast region of the country is known for its beautiful scenery. (n.) We're going to travel northeast. (adv.)  He lives in the northeast. (adj. & n.)|近义词：north-eastern | 反义词：southwest, southeast, northwest
