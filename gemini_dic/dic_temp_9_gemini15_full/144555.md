outwaits:/ˌaʊtˈweɪts/| v. |胜过（在等待方面）;比…等待的时间长|To wait longer than (someone or something).  It implies a successful waiting, often resulting in a desired outcome. |例句：He outwaited the other bidders and secured the property. 他比其他竞标者等的时间长，最终获得了房产。

例句：The persistent salesman outwaited the reluctant customer, finally securing the sale. 坚持不懈的推销员比犹豫不决的顾客等待的时间更长，最终成功完成了销售。


近义词：outlast,  survive, persevere


反义词：give up, surrender, quit
