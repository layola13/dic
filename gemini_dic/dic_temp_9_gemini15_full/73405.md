foreintend:/ˌfɔːrɪnˈtɛnd/| vt.|预先打算，预先计划|To plan or intend beforehand; to premeditate.|例句：He foreintended to visit his parents during the holiday. 他打算在假期去看望父母。

例句：The general foreintended a surprise attack on the enemy. 将军预先计划对敌人进行突然袭击。


近义词：premeditate, plan, intend, purpose|反义词：forget, neglect, overlook|动词
