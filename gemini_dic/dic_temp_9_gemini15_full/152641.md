peacockism:/ˈpiːkɒkɪzəm/| n. |夸夸其谈；卖弄；炫耀；自负；自吹自擂|The act of showing off ostentatiously; excessive vanity or boastfulness.  It often implies a superficial display of accomplishments or possessions to impress others.  The term derives from the peacock's habit of displaying its impressive plumage.|例句：His peacockism at the conference annoyed several colleagues. 他在会议上的夸夸其谈惹恼了几位同事。

例句：Her Instagram feed is pure peacockism; every photo is carefully staged to showcase her luxurious lifestyle. 她的Instagram主页纯粹是炫耀；每张照片都经过精心策划，以展示她奢华的生活方式。|近义词：ostentation, braggadocio, showing off, vanity, boastfulness|反义词：humility, modesty, self-effacement|名词
