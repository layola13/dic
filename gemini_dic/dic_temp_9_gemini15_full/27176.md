calligrapher:/kəˈlɪɡrəfər/ | n. | 书法家 | A calligrapher is a person who practices calligraphy, the art of beautiful handwriting.  They are skilled in the use of various writing tools and techniques to create aesthetically pleasing lettering. | Example:  The calligrapher carefully inscribed the wedding invitation in elegant script.  这位书法家用优雅的笔迹仔细地书写了婚礼请柬。|近义词：scribe, penman |反义词：None directly applicable.
