reguaranteeing: /ˌriːɡærənˈtiːɪŋ/ | v. | 再担保；重新担保 |  The act of guaranteeing something again, often after a previous guarantee has lapsed or been deemed insufficient.  This involves reaffirming a promise or undertaking to fulfill an obligation. |  例句：The bank is reguaranteeing the loan after the initial guarantor's financial difficulties. = 银行在最初担保人出现财务困难后，再次担保贷款。

The company is reguaranteeing its products to regain customer confidence. = 公司正在重新担保其产品以重新获得客户的信任。


近义词：reassuring, reaffirming, reconfirming | 反义词：disavowing, repudiating | 词性：动词
