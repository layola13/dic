numismatologist:/ˌnuːmɪˈzˈmætəlɒdʒɪst/| n. |钱币学家|A numismatologist is an expert in numismatics, the study of coins, medals, paper money, and related objects. They study the history, design, production, and value of these items.  |Example: The numismatologist carefully examined the ancient Roman coin, identifying its maker and approximate date.  钱币学家仔细检查了这枚古罗马硬币，确定了它的制造者和大致年代。|近义词：coin expert, medal expert |反义词：无
