minimalism:/ˈmɪnɪməlɪzəm/| n. |极简主义；最小化 |The philosophy of minimalism emphasizes simplicity and essentialism, rejecting unnecessary complexities. 极简主义的哲学强调简单和本质，拒绝不必要的复杂性。|例句：Her apartment is a testament to minimalism; it's sparsely furnished but beautifully organized. 她的公寓是极简主义的典范；它家具很少，但布置得非常漂亮。|近义词：simplicity, essentialism |反义词：maximalism, extravagance
