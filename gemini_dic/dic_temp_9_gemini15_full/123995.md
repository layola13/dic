morning-room:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ ruːm/| n.|晨室，会客室|A room, typically in a large house, used for informal socialising in the mornings.  It's often furnished comfortably and may be used for reading, writing, or light conversation.|例句：The morning-room was filled with the scent of freshly brewed coffee and the murmur of conversation. 晨室里弥漫着 freshly brewed coffee 的香味和轻声细语的交谈声。|例句：After breakfast, the family gathered in the morning-room to read the newspapers. 早餐后，家人聚集在晨室里阅读报纸。|近义词：sitting-room, drawing-room, parlor|反义词：bedroom, study
