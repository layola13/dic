inter-clouded: /ˌɪntərˈklaʊdəd/ | adj. | 交织的，混杂的，朦胧的 |  The word "inter-clouded" describes a state where things are mixed together or obscured, like clouds overlapping and obscuring the sky. It suggests a lack of clarity or distinctness due to overlapping or interwoven elements.  The "inter-" prefix indicates "between" or "among," emphasizing the interwoven nature of the elements involved. | 例句：The future seemed inter-clouded with uncertainty and doubt. 未来似乎充满了不确定性和怀疑。 The artist's vision was inter-clouded with symbolism and abstraction. 艺术家的视野融合了象征主义和抽象主义。 | 近义词：confused, obscured, mingled, blended, interwoven | 反义词：clear, distinct, transparent, lucid |  词性：形容词


USAGE->
