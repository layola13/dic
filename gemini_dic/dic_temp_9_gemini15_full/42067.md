coterie:/ˈkoʊtəri/ | n. |小圈子，派系 | A small group of people who have the same interests and who do not readily allow others to join them.  It often implies exclusivity and a sense of shared secrets or goals. |例句：The art world is full of exclusive coteries. 艺术界充满了排外的派系。

例句：He was part of a coterie of influential writers. 他是具有影响力的作家小圈子中的一员。


近义词：clique, circle, set, group, gang


反义词：large group, public, the masses


词性：名词
