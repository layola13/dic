hack-around:/ˈhæk əˈraʊnd/| v.|临时拼凑，粗略地解决，应付|To create or fix something in a quick, makeshift, and often inelegant way.  It implies a lack of thoroughness or elegance, prioritizing functionality over perfection.|例句：I hacked around a solution to the problem, but it's not very efficient. 我临时拼凑了一个解决方案，但这效率不高。


例句：We hacked around a quick website for the event. 我们为活动临时拼凑了一个网站。


近义词：improvise, makeshift, jury-rig, cobble together


反义词：refine, perfect, meticulously craft


词性:动词
