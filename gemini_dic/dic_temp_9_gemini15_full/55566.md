disturbance:/dɪˈstɜːrbəns/ | n. |扰乱；干扰；骚乱 | a state of disorder; a disruption; a commotion | 例句：The construction work caused a significant disturbance.  施工造成了很大的干扰。

The unexpected noise was a disturbance to her concentration.  突如其来的噪音扰乱了她的注意力。


近义词：disruption, interruption, commotion, turmoil, unrest | 反义词：calm, peace, tranquility, order


词源:  The word "disturbance" originates from the Old French word "desturbance," which itself comes from the verb "desturber," meaning "to disturb."  This verb ultimately derives from the Latin word "disturbare," a compound of "dis-" (apart) and "turbare" (to confuse, disturb).
