readmit:/ˌriːædˈmɪt/| vt.|重新接纳；重新录取；准予再次进入|To admit again; to allow someone to enter or join again after they have been expelled, dismissed, or suspended.|例句：The university decided to readmit the student after he apologized for his misconduct.  大学决定在他为自己的不当行为道歉后重新录取这名学生。

例句：The hospital readmitted the patient after his condition worsened. 医院在他病情恶化后再次收治了这位病人。


近义词：re-admit, reinstate, accept back |反义词：expel, dismiss, reject, exclude |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school readmits students who have improved their academic performance. 学校重新接纳那些学习成绩有所提高的学生。
They readmit members who have paid their dues. 他们重新接纳那些已缴纳会费的会员。

The judge readmitted the evidence after further investigation.  法官在进一步调查后重新接纳了证据。
