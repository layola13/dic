oper:/ˈɒpər/ /ˈɑːpər/ | n. |歌剧|A dramatic work in one or more acts, typically including singing and orchestral accompaniment, and presented theatrically.  |Example:  The opera was a stunning visual and auditory experience. 这场歌剧是一场令人惊艳的视觉和听觉盛宴。|近义词：dramatic work, musical play |反义词：none
