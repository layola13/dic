prefinish:/ˌpriːˈfɪnɪʃ/ | v. | 预先精加工；预先完成 | To finish (something) in advance or partially.  It often refers to a preparatory finishing process before a final finishing step.  | Example:  The wood was prefinished before being assembled into the furniture.  木材在组装成家具之前进行了预先精加工。 | 近义词：prepare, prime, precoat | 反义词：finish (final), complete (final)

We prefinish the components to save time later. = 我们预先精加工组件以节省以后的时间。
