pyrometry:/paɪˈrɒmətri/| n.|高温测定法；高温计量学|The science and technology of measuring high temperatures.  Pyrometry involves using instruments to measure temperatures that are too high for conventional thermometers.|Example:  Pyrometry is crucial in many industrial processes, such as steelmaking. 高温测定法在许多工业过程中至关重要，例如钢铁制造。|近义词：thermometry (更广义的温度测量)|反义词：无|名词
