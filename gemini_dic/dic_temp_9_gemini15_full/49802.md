demonology:/ˌdiːməˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |恶魔学，魔鬼学 |The study of demons, their nature, and their influence.  It often involves beliefs and practices associated with summoning, exorcism, and other interactions with demonic entities. |例句：He was fascinated by demonology and spent years studying ancient grimoires. 他对恶魔学很着迷，并花了数年时间研究古代魔法书。

例句：The professor's lecture covered various aspects of demonology, including different types of demons and their associated rituals. 教授的讲座涵盖了恶魔学的各个方面，包括不同类型的恶魔及其相关的仪式。


近义词：daemonology (archaic) | 反义词：angelology (the study of angels) | 词性:名词
