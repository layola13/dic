roughen:/ˈrʌfən/| vt.|使粗糙；使粗暴；使粗砺|To make something rough or less smooth.|The sandpaper roughened the wood. 砂纸使木材变粗糙了。The wind roughened the sea. 风使大海变得波涛汹涌。|近义词：coarsen, ruggedize|反义词：smooth, polish|动词
