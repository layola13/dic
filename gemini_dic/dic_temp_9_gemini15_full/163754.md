predelayed:/ˌpriːdɪˈleɪd/| adj. |预先延迟的；提前延迟的|Predelayed refers to something that has been delayed before its scheduled time or before it was expected to happen.  It implies a proactive or planned delay, rather than an unforeseen one. |The predelayed flight caused significant inconvenience to passengers.  预先延迟的航班给乘客带来了很大的不便。 The project was predelayed to allow for more thorough testing.  项目被提前延迟以进行更彻底的测试。|delayed, postponed|on time, prompt|形容词
