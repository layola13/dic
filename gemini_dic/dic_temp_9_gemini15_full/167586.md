preworking:/ˈpriːwɜːrkɪŋ/| adj.| 预先工作的；准备工作的 |Relating to work done in preparation for a main task or project.  This word is not a standard dictionary entry and is likely a newly coined or less common term. It combines "pre-" (meaning before) and "working" (referring to work).|例句：The preworking phase involved extensive research and planning. 预先工作阶段包括广泛的研究和规划。  The preworking meeting helped us to clarify roles and responsibilities. 预先工作会议帮助我们明确了角色和责任。|近义词：preparatory, preliminary, antecedent |反义词：post-working, follow-up
