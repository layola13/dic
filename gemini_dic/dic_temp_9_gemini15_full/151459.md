parostosis: /ˌpærəˈstoʊsɪs/ | n. |骨旁增生，骨旁骨质增生| A condition characterized by the formation of abnormal bone tissue adjacent to existing bone.  This often presents as bony overgrowths or exostoses near the joint. It can be caused by various factors, including trauma, infection, and certain genetic conditions.|例句：The radiograph revealed parostosis of the distal femur.  X光片显示股骨远端有骨旁增生。|近义词：exostosis, osteophyte|反义词：bone resorption
