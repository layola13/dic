preenlist:/ˌpriːˈɛnlɪst/ | v. |预先征募；提前征兵 |To enlist beforehand; to recruit in advance.  |例句：The army decided to preenlist potential recruits before the war broke out.  军队决定在战争爆发前预先征募潜在的士兵。  |近义词：recruit, enlist, conscript |反义词：discharge, demobilize
