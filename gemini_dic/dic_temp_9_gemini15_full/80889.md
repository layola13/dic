governing:/ˈɡʌvərniŋ/| adj.|统治的；管理的；支配的 |  exercising control or authority; directing or managing.  |例句：The governing body of the school made a decision. 学校的管理机构做出了一个决定。|例句：The governing principle is fairness. 支配原则公平。|近义词：ruling, controlling, regulating |反义词：subordinate, subject


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is governing the country well. = 政府治理国家良好。
This principle governs our actions. = 这个原则指导我们的行动。


一般过去时:
The king governed the kingdom wisely. = 国王英明地统治着王国。
The rules governed our behavior. = 规则规范了我们的行为。


现在完成时:
The committee has governed the club for many years. = 委员会管理这个俱乐部多年了。
This law has governed the industry for decades. = 这项法律几十年来一直管辖着这个行业。


过去完成时:
They had governed the territory before the war. = 战争前他们统治过这片领土。
The old laws had governed the land for centuries. = 古老的法律统治这片土地几个世纪了。


将来时:
The new council will govern the city next year. = 新议会明年将管理这座城市。
The new regulations will govern future projects. = 新规章将管理未来的项目。


虚拟语气:
If he governed justly, he would be loved by his people. = 如果他公正地统治，他将受到人民的爱戴。
If these rules governed our lives, we would be happier. = 如果这些规则指导我们的生活，我们会更快乐。


被动语态:
The country is governed by a parliament. = 这个国家由议会统治。
The company's operations are governed by strict regulations. = 公司的运营受到严格规章的管辖。


疑问句:
Who is governing this country? = 谁在统治这个国家？
What rules govern this process? = 什么规则支配这个过程？


否定句:
The old system did not govern effectively. = 旧制度管理效率不高。
His actions were not governed by reason. = 他的行为并非出于理性。
