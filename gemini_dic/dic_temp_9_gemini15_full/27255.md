calorimeters:/ˌkæləˈrɪmɪtərz/| n. |量热器（复数）|An instrument used to measure the amount of heat produced or absorbed during a chemical or physical process.|例句：Scientists use calorimeters to determine the heat of reaction.  科学家使用量热器来确定反应热。|近义词：heat meter|反义词:None.
