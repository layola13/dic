signposted:/ˈsaɪnpəʊstɪd/ | adj. | 标有路标的；有明确指示的 |  Having clear signs indicating the way or giving directions.  |The signposted route was easy to follow.  标有路标的路线很容易跟随。| well-marked, clearly indicated | unclear, unmarked


USAGE->

The path is signposted clearly. = 这条路标示得很清楚。
The museum is well signposted from the train station. = 从火车站到博物馆的路标示得很清楚。
