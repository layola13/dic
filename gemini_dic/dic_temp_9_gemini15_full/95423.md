incidents:/ˈɪnsɪdənts/| n. |事件；事变；冲突|An incident is a single event, often of some importance, especially one that is unusual or undesirable.  It can range from a minor mishap to a serious crime. |例句：The police are investigating the incident.  警方正在调查这起事件。

例句：There were several minor incidents during the protest. 抗议期间发生了几起小事件。


近义词：event, occurrence, happening, episode, affair, case|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|词性:名词
