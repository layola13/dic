glowered:/ˈɡlaʊərd/| v.|怒视，瞪眼|To look at someone or something in an angry or sullen way.|He glowered at me from across the room. 他从房间的另一边怒视着我。|近义词：stare, glare, scowl|反义词：smile, beam


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He glowers at the television. = 他怒视着电视。
She glowers at her homework. = 她怒视着她的作业。

一般过去时:
He glowered at the waiter. = 他怒视着服务员。
She glowered at the driver who cut her off. = 她怒视着那个加塞的司机。

现在完成时:
He has glowered at me all morning. = 他整个上午都在怒视着我。
She has glowered at her reflection in the mirror. = 她一直在对着镜子里的自己怒视。

过去完成时:
He had glowered at her before she even spoke. = 她还没开口，他就已经怒视着对方了。
She had glowered at the painting all day. = 她整天都在怒视那幅画。


将来时:
He will glower at anyone who disagrees with him. = 他会怒视任何和他意见相左的人。
She will glower at the test until she finishes it. = 她会一直怒视着试卷，直到完成它。


虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would glower at us. = 如果他在的话，他会怒视我们的。
I wish he wouldn't glower at me so much. = 我希望他不要总是那样怒视着我。

被动语态:  (Glowered is rarely used passively)
*The painting was glowered at by the art critic.* =  这幅画被艺术评论家怒视着。(不太自然)
*His anger was shown by the way he glowered.* = 他怒视的表情表现了他的愤怒。(更自然)

疑问句:
Does he often glower at people? = 他经常怒视别人吗？
Why is she glowering at me? = 她为什么怒视着我？

否定句:
He didn't glower at me; he just looked serious. = 他没有怒视我；他只是看起来很严肃。
She didn't glower, she simply frowned. = 她没有怒视，只是皱了皱眉。


PHRASE->
glower at someone = 怒视某人
glower with anger = 怒目而视
glower in disapproval = 不赞成地怒视
glower silently = 静静地怒视
glower menacingly = 凶狠地怒视
