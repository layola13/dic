evicted:/ɪˈvɪktɪd/| v.|驱逐，赶走|To force (someone) to leave a place, especially a house or other property.  |例句：The landlord evicted the tenants for non-payment of rent. 房东因房客不付房租而将其驱逐。|例句：They were evicted from their apartment after a dispute with the building manager. 他们与大楼管理员发生争执后被赶出了公寓。|近义词：expel, oust, eject|反义词：allow to stay, welcome


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The landlord evicts tenants who don't pay rent. = 房东驱逐不付房租的租户。
He evicts troublesome guests from his bar regularly. = 他定期将他酒吧里惹麻烦的客人赶走。


一般过去时:
The family was evicted from their home last week. = 这个家庭上周被赶出了家门。
She evicted her roommate after a series of arguments. = 经过一系列争吵后，她赶走了她的室友。


现在完成时:
The city has evicted numerous homeless people from the park. = 该市已将许多无家可归者从公园驱逐出去。
They have been evicted twice in the past year. = 他们在过去的一年里已经被驱逐了两次。


过去完成时:
They had been evicted before they even realized they were behind on rent. = 他们在甚至意识到自己拖欠房租之前就已经被驱逐了。
The family had already been evicted by the time the social worker arrived. = 当社工到达时，这个家庭已经被驱逐了。


将来时:
The court will evict the squatters next month. = 法院下个月将驱逐非法居住者。
If they don't pay, they will be evicted. = 如果他们不付款，他们将被驱逐。


虚拟语气:
If the rent was lower, they wouldn't have been evicted. = 如果租金更低，他们就不会被驱逐了。
I wish they hadn't been evicted from their home. = 我真希望他们没有被赶出家门。


被动语态:
The tenants were evicted for violating the lease agreement. = 租客因违反租赁协议而被驱逐。
Many families are being evicted due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济低迷，许多家庭正在被驱逐。


疑问句:
Were they evicted because of unpaid rent? = 他们是因为欠租而被驱逐的吗？
Has anyone ever been evicted from this building? = 有人曾经被赶出这栋楼吗？


否定句:
The landlord did not evict the tenants despite their late payments. = 尽管房客付款迟到，房东也没有驱逐他们。
They were not evicted, even though they broke several rules. = 即使他们违反了几条规定，他们也没有被驱逐。


PHRASE->

be evicted from = 从...被驱逐
evict someone for = 因为...驱逐某人
face eviction = 面临驱逐
avoid eviction = 避免驱逐
threaten eviction = 威胁驱逐
