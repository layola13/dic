felspar:/ˈfɛlspɑːr/| n. |长石|A group of rock-forming minerals that consist of aluminosilicates of potassium, sodium, and calcium.  They are common constituents of igneous, metamorphic, and sedimentary rocks.  Felspars are characterized by their cleavage and often exhibit a pearly or vitreous luster.|例句：The granite contained a high proportion of felspar.  这块花岗岩含有大量的长石。|近义词：feldspar|反义词:N/A
