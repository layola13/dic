ethnicities:/ˌeθˈnɪsətiz/| n. |种族；民族；族裔|The plural form of "ethnicity," referring to the different ethnic groups or races of people.  It encompasses shared cultural traits, ancestry, language, and often religion.  The study of ethnicities helps us understand the diversity of human populations and the complex relationships between different groups.|例句：The school celebrates the diverse ethnicities of its student body.  学校庆祝其学生群体的多元种族。

例句：Understanding different ethnicities is crucial for building a harmonious society. 理解不同的种族对于构建和谐社会至关重要。


近义词：races, nationalities, peoples|反义词：homogeneity (lack of diversity)|词性：名词
