dandily:/ˈdændɪli/| adv.|潇洒地；花俏地；时髦地 |In a showy or stylish manner; smartly.|He dressed dandily for the party. 他为了派对而穿着潇洒。|近义词：stylishly, smartly, fashionably |反义词：shabbily, sloppily, untidily


USAGE->

She walks dandily down the street. = 她潇洒地走在街上。
