dugouts:/ˈdʌɡaʊts/ | n. |  dugout（战壕，掩体；（棒球场的）教练席） | A dugout is a shelter, typically dug into the ground, used for protection, often in military contexts or as a shelter for players in a sports game.  It can also refer to a shallow boat. | 例句：The soldiers sought refuge in the dugout during the shelling.  士兵们在炮击中寻求掩体。

The baseball team huddled in their dugout between innings.棒球队在局间休息时挤在他们的教练席里。


近义词：shelter, trench, foxhole (for military contexts);  bench (for baseball context) | 反义词：open, exposed
