relitigate:/ˌriːˈlɪtɪɡeɪt/| vt.|重新审理；再次诉讼|To bring a legal case to court again after it has already been decided.  This usually involves raising new evidence or arguments that were not previously considered.|例句：The company attempted to relitigate the case, claiming new evidence had been discovered. 公司试图重新审理此案，声称发现了新的证据。
例句：They relitigated the divorce settlement, causing further distress to both parties.  他们重新诉讼离婚协议，给双方造成了进一步的痛苦。|近义词：rehear, retry, reconsider|反义词：settle, finalize

现在完成时:

They have relitigated the case in a higher court. = 他们在更高的法院重新审理了此案。

过去完成时:
