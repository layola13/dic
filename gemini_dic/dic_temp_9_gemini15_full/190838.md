saws:/sɔːz/| n. v.|锯子；锯（动词）|A saw is a tool with a toothed blade used for cutting wood or other materials.  As a verb, it means to cut with a saw.|例句：The carpenter used a handsaw to cut the wood. 木匠用一把手锯来锯木头。 He sawed the branch off the tree. 他把树枝锯掉了。|近义词：blade, cutter (n); sever, cut (v)|反义词：join, connect (v)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saws wood every morning. = 他每天早上锯木头。
She saws through the metal with ease. = 她轻松地锯穿了金属。


一般过去时:
He sawed the board in half. = 他把木板锯成两半。
They sawed down the old tree. = 他们把那棵老树锯倒了。

She has sawed through the thickest part of the log. = 她已经锯过了原木最厚的部分。

They had sawed through most of the tree trunk before the saw broke. = 锯子断之前他们已经锯掉了大部分树干。


将来时:
