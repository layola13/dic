feeling:/ˈfiːlɪŋ/| n.|感觉，知觉；情绪；同情；触觉|The word "feeling" refers to an emotional state or a physical sensation.  It can describe a range of emotions, from joy and happiness to sadness and anger, or it can refer to the sense of touch. |例句：I have a bad feeling about this. 我对此感到不好的预感。

I'm feeling much better today. 我今天感觉好多了。


近义词：sensation, emotion, sentiment, sense |反义词：unfeeling, apathy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am feeling happy today. = 我今天感觉很快乐。
She is feeling unwell. = 她感觉不舒服。


一般过去时:
I felt sad yesterday. = 我昨天感到悲伤。
He felt a sharp pain in his leg. = 他感到腿部一阵剧痛。


现在完成时:
I have been feeling tired lately. = 最近我一直感到疲倦。
She has felt lonely since her move. = 自从搬家后，她一直感到孤独。


过去完成时:
I had been feeling anxious all day. = 我整天都感到焦虑。
They had felt relieved after the meeting. = 会议结束后他们感到如释重负。


将来时:
I will be feeling better after a good night's sleep. = 睡个好觉后我会感觉好些。
She will feel proud of her achievement. = 她会为自己的成就感到骄傲。


虚拟语气:
If I were feeling better, I would go to the party. = 如果我感觉好些，我会去参加派对。
I wish I were not feeling so stressed. = 我希望我不那么紧张。


被动语态:
The fabric feels soft to the touch. = 这布料摸起来很柔软。(感觉是被动的接受)
His anxieties were felt by everyone in the room. = 房间里每个人都能感受到他的焦虑。(焦虑是被感受到的)


疑问句:
How are you feeling today? = 你今天感觉如何？
What are you feeling? = 你有什么感觉？


否定句:
I am not feeling well. = 我感觉不舒服。
She doesn't feel comfortable in that dress. = 她穿着那件衣服感觉不舒服。


PHRASE->
have a feeling: 有预感
inner feeling: 内心的感觉
strong feeling: 强烈的感情
mixed feeling: 复杂的心情
gut feeling: 直觉
