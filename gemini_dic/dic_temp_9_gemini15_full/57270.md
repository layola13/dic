downpour:/ˈdaʊnpɔːr/|/ˈdaʊnˌpɔːr/| n. |倾盆大雨；骤雨 |A heavy fall of rain.  |例句：The downpour forced us to cancel the picnic. 倾盆大雨迫使我们取消了野餐。

例句：We were caught in a sudden downpour on our way home.  回家的路上我们遭遇了突如其来的暴雨。

近义词：deluge, torrent, cloudburst |反义词：drought, dry spell |词性:名词

词源:
The word "downpour" is a compound word.  "Down" indicates a downward movement, and "pour" refers to the act of pouring liquid.  Therefore, "downpour" literally means rain pouring down heavily.  It originated in the late 19th century.
