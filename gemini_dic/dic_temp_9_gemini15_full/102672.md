jambs:/dʒæmz/| n.| 门框；窗框；书橱侧板 |The vertical side pieces of a doorway or window frame.  They support the lintel and sill.  Also refers to the side pieces of a bookcase.|例句：The painter carefully cleaned the jambs before repainting the door.  油漆工在重新粉刷门之前仔细地清洁了门框。|The old house had rotten window jambs. 那栋老房子窗户的门框都腐烂了。|近义词：frame, casing, side piece |反义词：None specifically.
