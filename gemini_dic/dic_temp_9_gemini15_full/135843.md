nonproprietors:/ˌnɒnprəˈpraɪətərz/ | n. | 非业主；非所有者 | People or entities that do not own or have a proprietary interest in something.  This term often refers to individuals or groups who use or benefit from a resource or system without holding ownership rights.  For example, in the context of a business, nonproprietors could be employees, customers, or even competitors. | Example: The nonproprietors were unhappy with the changes to the park's access policy.  非业主对公园通行政策的改变感到不满。  |近义词: non-owners, outsiders | 反义词: proprietors, owners | 名词
