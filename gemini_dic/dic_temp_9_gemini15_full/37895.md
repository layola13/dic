complaisant:/kəmˈpleɪznt/| adj.| 讨好的；顺从的；彬彬有礼的 | Willing to please others; obliging; agreeable.  It suggests a tendency to be overly eager to please, sometimes at the expense of one's own convictions or interests. | 例句：He was always complaisant towards his superiors, eager to win their favor. 他总是对上司很顺从，渴望得到他们的欢心。

例句：Her complaisant nature made her a popular member of the team.  她讨好的性格使她成为团队中受欢迎的一员。


近义词：obliging, amenable, accommodating, agreeable, affable

反义词：disagreeable, uncooperative, obstinate, intractable, defiant


词性：形容词
