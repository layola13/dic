nonelectiveness:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktɪvnɪs/ | n. |非选择性；必然性 |The lack of choice or option; the state of being inevitable.  It refers to a situation where there is only one possibility and no alternative choices are available. |Example: The nonelectiveness of the outcome was clear to everyone involved; the result was predetermined.  结果的必然性对所有参与者来说都很明显；结果是预先确定的。|近义词：inevitability, certainty, unavoidability |反义词：choice, option, selection |名词
