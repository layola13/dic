self-assuming:/ˌsɛlf əˈsjuːmɪŋ/| adj.|自以为是的，自负的|Someone who is self-assuming believes they are more important or intelligent than they actually are. They often act in a way that is arrogant and presumptuous.  This characteristic can be off-putting to others and hinder their relationships and professional success.|例句：His self-assuming manner alienated many of his colleagues. 他自以为是的态度疏远了他的许多同事。

例句：She made a self-assuming speech, boasting about her accomplishments. 她发表了一篇自以为是的演讲，夸耀自己的成就。


近义词：arrogant, presumptuous, conceited, supercilious, haughty|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, self-effacing
