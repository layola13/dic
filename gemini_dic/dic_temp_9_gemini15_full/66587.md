exhorting: /ɪɡˈzɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 劝告；力劝；激励 |  Urging strongly; advising earnestly.  |例句：The coach was exhorting his team to greater efforts. 教练正在力劝他的队员更加努力。

例句：The preacher exhorted the congregation to live a more virtuous life.  牧师劝告会众过更有道德的生活。


近义词：urge, encourage, implore, entreat

反义词：discourage, dissuade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exhorts his students to study hard. 他劝告他的学生努力学习。
She exhorts her children to be kind. 她劝告她的孩子们要善良。


一般过去时:
The general exhorted his troops to fight bravely. 将军劝告他的军队勇敢作战。
The teacher exhorted the students to finish their homework. 老师劝告学生完成作业。


现在完成时:
He has exhorted us to be more responsible. 他一直劝告我们要更负责任。
She has exhorted her followers to donate to charity. 她一直劝告她的追随者捐款给慈善机构。


过去完成时:
The captain had exhorted his crew to abandon ship before the storm hit. 船长在暴风雨来临之前就劝告船员弃船。
They had exhorted him to reconsider his decision. 他们曾劝告他重新考虑他的决定。


将来时:
The president will exhort the nation to unity. 总统将劝告全国人民团结一致。
She will exhort her colleagues to support the new project. 她将劝告她的同事支持这个新项目。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would exhort him to seek professional help. 如果我是你，我会劝他寻求专业帮助。
I wish I had exhorted him to be more careful. 我真希望我当时劝告他更小心一些。


被动语态:
The workers were exhorted to improve their productivity. 工人们受到劝告要提高生产力。
The audience was exhorted to donate generously. 观众受到劝告要慷慨捐款。


疑问句:
Was he exhorting the crowd to violence? 他是在劝说人群暴力吗？
Are they exhorting their employees to work overtime? 他们是在劝说员工加班吗？


否定句:
He did not exhort them to take any risks. 他没有劝他们冒险。
She didn't exhort her son to apply for that difficult job. 她没有劝说她的儿子申请那份困难的工作。


PHRASE->
exhort to action  劝说采取行动
exhort someone to do something  劝说某人做某事
exhort with passion  激情地劝说
exhort someone to change  劝说某人改变
exhort someone to repent  劝说某人悔过


词源:
源自于拉丁语“exhortari”，意思是“鼓励，激励”。
