polarimeter:/ˌpoʊləˈrɪmɪtər/ | n. |旋光仪 |An instrument used to measure the rotation of the plane of polarization of light that is passed through an optically active substance.  It is used to determine the concentration of optically active substances or to identify them. |The chemist used a polarimeter to analyze the sample's optical activity. 化学家用旋光仪分析样品的旋光性。  The polarimeter reading indicated a high concentration of sugar. 旋光仪读数表明糖浓度很高。|spectropolarimeter, saccharimeter |(no direct antonym)|名词
