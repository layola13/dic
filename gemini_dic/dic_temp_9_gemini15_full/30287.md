cemetery:/ˈsɛmɪtɛri/ | n. | 墓地，公墓 | A cemetery is a place where people are buried after they die.  It often contains many graves and monuments. | Example:  My grandfather is buried in the cemetery on the hill.  我的祖父被埋葬在山上的墓地。

The family visited the cemetery to place flowers on their loved one's grave.  家人去墓地为他们所爱的人的坟墓献花。


近义词：graveyard, burial ground, necropolis
反义词：None (antonyms are not directly applicable, but one could contrast it with "living place" or "place of celebration")

词性:名词

词源:
The word "cemetery" comes from the Greek word "koimeterion," which means "sleeping place" or "dormitory."  This reflects the ancient understanding of death as a kind of sleep.  The word entered English through Latin and French.
