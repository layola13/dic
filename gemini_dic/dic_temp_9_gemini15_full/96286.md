indifferent:/ɪnˈdɪfərənt/| adj.|冷漠的；漠不关心的；不重要的|Unconcerned; not caring; having no particular liking for or dislike of something.|He was indifferent to the suffering of others. 他对别人的痛苦漠不关心。|近义词：apathetic, unconcerned, unconvinced|反义词：passionate, caring, interested


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is indifferent to politics. = 他对政治漠不关心。
She remains indifferent to his advances. = 她对他示好的举动依然漠不关心。


一般过去时:
He was indifferent to my pleas. = 他对我的恳求无动于衷。
They were indifferent to the plight of the refugees. = 他们对难民的困境漠不关心。


现在完成时:
She has remained indifferent to his criticism. = 她一直对他批评无动于衷。
He has been indifferent to his health for years. = 多年来他一直对自己的健康漠不关心。


过去完成时:
He had been indifferent to the warning signs. = 他对警告信号一直漠不关心。
They had remained indifferent to the problem for too long. = 他们对这个问题漠视太久了。


将来时:
He will likely remain indifferent to our requests. = 他很可能仍会对我们的请求漠不关心。
She will probably be indifferent to the outcome. = 她可能对结果漠不关心。


虚拟语气:
If he were indifferent to her feelings, he wouldn't have acted that way. = 如果他对她的感情漠不关心，他就不会那样做了。
I wish I were indifferent to his opinions. = 我希望我对他的意见漠不关心。


被动语态:
His pleas were treated indifferently. = 他的恳求被漠视了。
The matter was treated with indifference. = 这件事被漠视了。


疑问句:
Are you indifferent to the news? = 你对这个消息漠不关心吗？
Is he always indifferent to other people's feelings? = 他总是对别人的感受漠不关心吗？


否定句:
He is not indifferent to her suffering. = 他并非对她所受的苦难漠不关心。
She is not indifferent to the needs of the poor. = 她并非对穷人的需求漠不关心。


PHRASE->
be indifferent to 对…漠不关心
feel indifferent 感到冷漠
indifferent attitude 冷漠的态度
show indifference 表示冷漠
express indifference 表达冷漠
