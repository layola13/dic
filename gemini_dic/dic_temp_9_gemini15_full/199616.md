sinks:/sɪŋks/| n. v.|水槽；下沉；使下沉|The kitchen sinks are made of stainless steel. 厨房水槽是的不锈钢制成的。The ship is sinking fast. 船正在快速下沉。He sank the ball into the hole. 他把球击入洞中。|近义词：basin, washbasin, submerge, descend|反义词：float, rise, ascend|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water sinks slowly into the ground. = 水慢慢渗入地下。
The ship sinks beneath the waves. = 船沉入波浪之下。


一般过去时:
The Titanic sank in 1912. = 泰坦尼克号沉没于1912年。
He sank the winning shot. = 他投进了制胜球。


现在完成时:
The ship has sunk. = 船已经沉没了。
The price of oil has sunk dramatically. = 油价暴跌。


过去完成时:
The boat had sunk before we could reach it. = 我们到达之前船就已经沉了。
The sun had sunk below the horizon. = 太阳已经落山了。


将来时:
The island will sink beneath the rising sea. = 岛屿将沉入上升的海平面之下。
The economy will likely sink further. = 经济可能进一步下滑。


虚拟语气:
If the ship had not sunk, many lives would have been saved. = 如果船没有沉没，许多生命就能得救了。
If only the government had acted sooner, the economic crisis might not have sunk so low. = 如果政府早点采取行动就好了，经济危机可能不会这么严重。


被动语态:
The submarine was sunk by enemy fire. = 潜艇被敌人的炮火击沉了。
The project was sunk due to lack of funding. = 项目因缺乏资金而搁浅了。


疑问句:
Is the boat sinking? = 船正在下沉吗？
Does the price sink below the cost? = 价格低于成本吗？


否定句:
The ship did not sink. = 船没有沉没。
The plan did not sink. = 计划没有失败。


PHRASE->
kitchen sink: 厨房水槽
sink or swim: 沉浮；成败自负
sink in: 渗透；被理解
sink one's teeth into:  全力以赴；埋头苦干
go down the drain/sink:  付诸东流；浪费
