scarabs:/ˈskærəbz/| n.|圣甲虫；甲虫饰品|A scarab is a beetle, especially the sacred scarab beetle of ancient Egypt, often depicted in art and used as a symbol of rebirth and renewal.  The term can also refer to jewelry or other objects shaped like scarab beetles.|例句：The ancient Egyptians revered the scarab beetle. 古埃及人崇拜圣甲虫。

The museum displayed a collection of scarab amulets. 博物馆展出了一系列圣甲虫护身符。|近义词：beetle, amulet (when referring to jewelry)|反义词：无
