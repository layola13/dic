outfalls:/ˈaʊtfɔːlz/| n.|排污口；出水口 |Places where wastewater or other fluids are discharged into a body of water.  Outfalls are often pipes or channels that carry the effluent away from a treatment plant or industrial facility.|例句：The city's wastewater outfalls are located several miles offshore.  城市的污水排放口位于离岸几英里处。

The factory's outfalls discharge treated wastewater into the river.  工厂的出水口将处理后的污水排放到河流中。|近义词：discharge points, effluents, outlets|反义词：inlets, intakes|名词
