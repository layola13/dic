chess's:/ˈtʃɛsɪz/| n.|国际象棋的；属于国际象棋的|The possessive form of "chess," indicating ownership or relationship.  It refers to something belonging to or associated with chess.|Example: Chess's history spans centuries. 国际象棋的历史跨越了几个世纪。|近义词:of chess|反义词:None


USAGE->
This word is not used in any standard grammatical tense because it's a possessive form.  It's a noun phrase, not a verb that conjugates.  It functions similarly to "the king's pawn" or "the queen's gambit."
