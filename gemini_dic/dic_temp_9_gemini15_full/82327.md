groveler:/ˈɡrɑːvələr/ | n. | 卑躬屈膝者；奴颜婢膝者 | A person who acts in a servile manner, showing excessive deference and humility.  |例句：He became a groveler to gain favor with the boss. 他为了讨好老板而变得卑躬屈膝。|例句：She refused to be a groveler, even when facing powerful adversaries. 即使面对强大的对手，她也不愿卑躬屈膝。|近义词：flatterer, sycophant, fawner, crawler |反义词：rebel, defiant, independent

一般现在时:
He grovels to his superiors. = 他对上司卑躬屈膝。
She grovels for forgiveness. = 她卑躬屈膝地请求原谅。

一般过去时:
He groveled at the feet of the king. = 他跪在国王脚下。
She groveled to avoid punishment. = 她卑躬屈膝以避免惩罚。

过去完成时:

She had groveled for years, finally breaking free. = 她卑躬屈膝多年，最终解脱了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
