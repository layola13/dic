leany:/ˈliːni/ | adj. | 瘦长的；倾斜的；易倾倒的 |  Leaning describes something that is thin and tall, or that is sloping or inclined. It can also suggest something that is unstable and likely to fall.  | 例句：The leany tree swayed in the wind.  这棵瘦长的树在风中摇曳。  The leany tower seemed on the verge of collapse.  那座倾斜的塔似乎快要倒塌了。 | 近义词：slender, thin, sloping, inclined, unsteady | 反义词： stout, thick, upright, stable, sturdy
