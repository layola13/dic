cafeterias:/ˌkæfɪˈtɪəriəz/ | n. |自助餐厅，食堂 |A cafeteria is a self-service restaurant, typically found in schools, colleges, hospitals, or businesses, where customers choose their food from a counter and pay for it before eating.  |例句：The school cafeteria serves a variety of lunch options. 学校自助餐厅提供各种午餐选择。|例句：Many office buildings have cafeterias for their employees. 许多办公大楼都设有员工食堂。|近义词：lunchroom, canteen, dining hall |反义词：fine-dining restaurant
