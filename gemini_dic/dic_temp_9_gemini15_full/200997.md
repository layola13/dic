sloe:/sluː/| n.|黑刺李，黑刺李果|The sloe is a small, dark blue or purplish-black fruit of the blackthorn bush.  It's tart and astringent when raw, but often used in jams, jellies, and liqueurs.|The hedgerows were full of ripe sloes.  灌木丛里长满了成熟的黑刺李。|The sloe gin was a delicious, dark purple colour. 黑刺李杜松子酒颜色深紫，十分美味。|plum, berry|sweet fruit|名词
