polysemous:/pɑːliˈsɛməs/| adj.| 多义的 | Having more than one meaning; ambiguous.  A polysemous word has multiple related meanings that have evolved from a single, earlier meaning.  The different meanings are connected through a semantic relationship, unlike homonyms which are words with the same spelling but unrelated meanings. | 例句：The word "bank" is polysemous, referring to both a financial institution and the land alongside a river.  The word "bright" is polysemous; it can describe light, intelligence, or color. | 近义词：ambiguous, equivocal, multifaceted, multivalent | 反义词：unambiguous, univocal, single-meaning
