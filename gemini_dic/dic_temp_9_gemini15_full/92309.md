hypnosis:/ɪpˈnoʊsɪs/|/ˈhɪpnoʊsɪs/| n. |催眠；催眠状态 |The process of inducing a state of consciousness in which a person loses the power of voluntary action and is highly susceptible to suggestion.  It is often used therapeutically to treat anxiety, phobias, and other psychological conditions. |例句：The therapist used hypnosis to help the patient overcome their fear of flying. 治疗师用催眠帮助病人克服对飞行的恐惧。|例句：She underwent hypnosis to try and quit smoking. 她接受了催眠治疗，试图戒烟。|近义词：trance, mesmerism |反义词：wakefulness, consciousness
