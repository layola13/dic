septum:/ˈsɛptəm/| n.|隔膜；中隔；鼻中隔|A septum is a partition separating two chambers or spaces.  It can refer to various anatomical structures, most commonly the nasal septum.|The nasal septum separates the nostrils.  鼻中隔隔开两个鼻孔。|The heart has septa between its chambers. 心脏的心室之间有隔膜。|partition, divider|whole, open (in the context of a missing septum)|名词
