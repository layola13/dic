dirigible:/ˈdɪrɪdʒəbəl/| n. |飞艇；可操纵飞船|A dirigible is a type of airship that is steerable, unlike a free balloon.  It is propelled by engines and controlled by a rudder and other control surfaces.  |例句：The Hindenburg was a famous, but ill-fated, dirigible.  兴登堡号是一艘著名的，但命运多舛的飞艇。

The Graf Zeppelin was a successful passenger dirigible.  齐柏林伯爵号是一艘成功的客运飞艇。|近义词：airship, zeppelin|反义词：balloon (non-steerable)|词性:n.
