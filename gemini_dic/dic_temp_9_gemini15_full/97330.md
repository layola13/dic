informatory:/ɪnˈfɔːrmətəri/ | adj. | 提供信息的；告知的 | Giving information; serving to inform.  | The informatory pamphlet explained the new regulations. 这份介绍性小册子解释了新的规章制度。 | informative, instructive | unclear, confusing | 形容词


例句：

The meeting had an informatory purpose, designed to keep everyone up to date.  会议的目的是为了提供信息，让大家了解最新情况。

The professor gave an informatory lecture on the history of the Roman Empire. 教授做了一场关于罗马帝国历史的介绍性讲座。


词源:

From "inform" + "-atory," a suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to," "of the nature of," or "tending to."  源于“inform” + “-atory”，一个构成形容词的后缀，意思是“与……相关的”、“具有……性质的”或“倾向于……”。


近义词：

informative, instructive, explanatory, enlightening


反义词：

unclear, confusing, misleading, obscure
