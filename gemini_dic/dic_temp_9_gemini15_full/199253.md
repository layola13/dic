silting:/ˈsɪltɪŋ/| n.|淤塞；淤积；泥沙淤积|The process of silt accumulating in a river, harbor, or other body of water, often reducing its depth and navigability.  It refers to the deposition of silt, a type of sediment consisting of fine sand, clay, and other particles.|例句：The silting of the harbor is a major problem for the shipping industry. 港口淤塞是航运业的主要问题。
The continuous silting reduced the river's depth significantly.  持续的淤积显著降低了河流的深度。|近义词：sedimentation, deposition, accumulation|反义词：dredging, excavation|名词
