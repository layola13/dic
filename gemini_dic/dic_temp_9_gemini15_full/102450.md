jackhammered:/ˈdʒækˌhæmər/| adj.|像用破路锤敲打过的；支离破碎的；剧烈震动的|  Describes something that has been severely damaged or forcefully impacted, often resembling the effect of a jackhammer.  It can also describe a sensation of intense vibration.|例句：The road was jackhammered after the earthquake. 地震后，道路被震得支离破碎。 The building felt jackhammered by the explosions. 建筑物在爆炸中剧烈震动。|近义词：shattered, pulverized, pounded, battered|反义词：intact, undamaged, smooth
