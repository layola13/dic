procures:/prəˈkjʊərz/| vt.|获得，取得，购买|To obtain or acquire something, especially with care or effort.  It often implies a degree of difficulty or planning in the acquisition.|例句：The company procures its raw materials from overseas suppliers. 这家公司从海外供应商那里采购原材料。|例句：She managed to procure a rare first edition of the book. 她设法买到了一本珍贵的该书初版。|近义词：obtain, acquire, secure, get, purchase|反义词：lose, relinquish, forfeit, abandon|动词
