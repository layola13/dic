jowliness:/ˈdʒaʊlinɪs/| n. |松弛的下颚; 腮帮子下垂|The appearance of having loose, fleshy jowls, often associated with age or weight gain.  It suggests a sagging or pendulous quality to the flesh of the cheeks and lower face.|例句：The dog's jowliness was accentuated by its droopy ears. 这只狗松弛的下颚被它下垂的耳朵衬托得更加明显。  His jowliness made him look older than his actual age. 他松弛的下颚使他看起来比实际年龄大。|近义词：sagging, flabbiness, pendulousness |反义词：firmness, tautness
