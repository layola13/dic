esteeming: /ɪˈstiːmɪŋ/ | v. | 尊重；敬重；重视 | Considering or regarding someone or something with respect; valuing highly. |例句：He is esteemed by his colleagues for his hard work. 他因其辛勤工作而受到同事们的尊重。 |近义词：respecting, valuing, admiring, honoring |反义词：despising, scorning, contemning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is esteeming her teacher's guidance. = 她很尊重老师的指导。
They are esteeming the importance of education. = 他们重视教育的重要性。


一般过去时:
He esteemed her opinion above all others. = 他最尊重她的意见。
The community esteemed his contributions to the town. = 社区很重视他对小镇的贡献。


现在完成时:
I have esteemed my parents' sacrifices all my life. = 我一生都尊重父母的牺牲。
They have esteemed the value of hard work. = 他们一直重视努力工作的价值。


过去完成时:
She had esteemed his integrity before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，她一直很尊重他的正直。
We had esteemed the project's potential for success. = 我们曾经很看好这个项目的成功潜力。


将来时:
I will esteem his efforts no matter the outcome. = 不管结果如何，我都会尊重他的努力。
They will esteem the advice of the experienced consultant. = 他们将会重视经验丰富的顾问的建议。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would esteem their expertise. = 如果我是你，我会尊重他们的专业知识。
I wish I had esteemed the warning signs earlier. = 我真希望我早点重视那些警告信号。


被动语态:
His contributions to science are esteemed worldwide. = 他对科学的贡献在全世界都受到尊重。
The importance of family is esteemed in many cultures. = 家庭的重要性在许多文化中都受到重视。


疑问句:
Do you esteem his opinion? = 你尊重他的意见吗？
Does she esteem the value of teamwork? = 她重视团队合作的价值吗？


否定句:
He did not esteem her judgment in the matter. = 他并不尊重她在这件事上的判断。
They did not esteem the risks involved. = 他们没有重视所涉及的风险。
