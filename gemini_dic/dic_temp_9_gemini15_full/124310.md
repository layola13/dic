mothering:/ˈmʌðəriŋ/ | n. |母亲的照料；抚养；母性 | The act of caring for and nurturing children.  It can refer to the physical and emotional care provided by a mother, or more broadly, anyone providing parental care. |例句：Her mothering instincts kicked in when she saw the injured bird.  她的母性本能在她看到受伤的小鸟时被激发出来了。

例句：The book explores the challenges and rewards of mothering in modern society. 这本书探讨了现代社会中为人母的挑战和回报。


近义词：parenting, nurturing, childcare, rearing | 反义词：neglect, abandonment
