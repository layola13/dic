/ˈdʒɛtər/| n. v.|喷射器；喷射装置；(用喷射器)清洗|A jetter is a device used to clean pipes, sewers, or other channels by using high-pressure water jets.|例句：The plumber used a jetter to clear the clogged drain.  水管工用喷射器疏通了堵塞的下水道。|例句：They jettered the sewer line to remove the blockage. 他们用喷射器冲洗污水管道以清除堵塞物。|近义词：cleaner, flusher|反义词：clogger
