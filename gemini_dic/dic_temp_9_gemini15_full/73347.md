forefinger:/ˈfɔːrfɪŋɡər/| n. |食指|The index finger; the finger next to the thumb.  It is often used to point or indicate something.|例句：She pointed with her forefinger at the map. 她用食指指着地图。|例句：He used his forefinger to trace the outline of the drawing. 他用食指描绘图画的轮廓。|近义词：index finger, pointing finger|反义词:None.
