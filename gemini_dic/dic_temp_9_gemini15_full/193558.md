self-afflicting: /ˌsɛlf əˈflɪktɪŋ/ | adj. | 自虐的；自我折磨的 |  Self-afflicting describes something that causes suffering or harm to oneself. It implies an intentional act of inflicting pain or hardship upon oneself, often stemming from psychological distress or a desire for punishment.  | Example: His self-afflicting behavior worried his friends and family.  他的自虐行为让他的朋友和家人担心。 | 近义词：self-destructive, masochistic, self-harming | 反义词：self-caring, self-loving, self-nurturing
