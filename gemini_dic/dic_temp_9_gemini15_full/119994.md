milligram:/ˈmɪlɪɡræm/| n. |毫克|A milligram (mg) is a unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a gram.  It's a very small unit of weight often used to measure very small amounts of substances, such as medicine dosages or the weight of very small objects.|The doctor prescribed 5 milligrams of the medication. 医生开了5毫克的药。|The diamond weighed only a few milligrams. 这颗钻石只有几毫克重。|gram, microgram|kilogram
