diagnosis:/ˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊsɪs/ | n. |诊断；判断 |The process of identifying a disease, illness, or problem by examining the symptoms.  It involves a systematic investigation to determine the nature of a condition. |例句：The doctor made a diagnosis of pneumonia.  医生诊断为肺炎。

例句：After a series of tests, the diagnosis was finally confirmed.  经过一系列检查后，诊断最终得到确认。


近义词：identification, determination, assessment | 反义词：treatment, cure


词源:  From Late Latin *diagnosis, from Greek διάγνωσις (diágnōsis), from διαγιγνώσκειν (diagignóskein) "to distinguish, discern," from διά (diá) "through, across" + γνώσις (gnōsis) "knowledge."
