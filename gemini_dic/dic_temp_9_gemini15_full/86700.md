helluva:/hɛlˈʌvə/| adj.|非常，极好的，了不起的 |  A colloquial intensifier meaning "a hell of a," expressing a large degree or amount.  It's an informal and often emphatic way to describe something. | That was a helluva party!  = 那真是一个很棒的派对！  | terrific, amazing, incredible | terrible, awful, mediocre | 形容词
