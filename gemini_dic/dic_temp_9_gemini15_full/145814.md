overdeepen:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːpən/| vt.|加深过度；过度挖掘|To deepen excessively or beyond a desirable or safe limit.  This typically refers to actions that go beyond what is necessary or prudent, often resulting in negative consequences.  The term is often used in contexts related to geology, engineering, and finance.|例句：The river was overdeepened by the unexpected floods.  洪水意外地加深了河道。

The mining company overdeepened the shaft, leading to a structural collapse.  矿业公司过度挖掘矿井，导致结构坍塌。|近义词：exaggerate, overdo, overexcavate|反义词：shallow, insufficiently deep
