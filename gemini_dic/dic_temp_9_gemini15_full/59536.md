eater:/ˈiːtər/| n. |食客，食者，吃东西的人|A person or animal that eats something.  It can also refer to a machine or device that consumes something.|例句：The restaurant was full of hungry eaters.  这家餐馆挤满了饥饿的食客。

例句：The wood eater destroyed the old fence.  这个蛀虫毁坏了旧栅栏。


近义词：consumer, devourer, predator|反义词：producer


词源：源自古英语 *ētan,  意思是“to eat”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a big eater. = 他是一个大胃王。
The machine is a constant eater of resources. = 这台机器不断消耗资源。


一般过去时:
She was a fast eater in her youth. = 她年轻时吃得很快。
The old building was an eater of funds. = 那栋旧楼耗资巨大。


现在完成时:
The insect has been a voracious eater of crops. = 这种昆虫一直是庄稼的 voracious 食客。
The project has been a huge eater of time. = 这个项目耗费了大量的时间。


过去完成时:
He had been a picky eater as a child. = 他小时候是个挑食的人。
The company had been a steady eater of market share. = 这家公司一直在稳步蚕食市场份额。


将来时:
She will be a competitive eater in the contest. = 她将在比赛中成为一名竞技性食客。
The new software will be a significant eater of memory. = 新软件将消耗大量的内存。


虚拟语气:
If he were a better eater, he'd enjoy more foods. = 如果他食量更大些，他会享受更多食物。
If the machine were a less efficient eater of energy, the project would fail. = 如果机器的能源效率更低，项目就会失败。


被动语态:
The cake was quickly eaten by the hungry eaters. = 蛋糕很快被饥饿的食客吃光了。
The resources are being constantly eaten by the system. = 资源正在被系统不断消耗。


疑问句:
Is he a slow eater? = 他吃东西慢吗？
Is this machine an energy eater? = 这台机器是耗能的吗？


否定句:
He is not a picky eater. = 他不是个挑食的人。
That system is not a resource eater. = 那个系统不是资源消耗型。
