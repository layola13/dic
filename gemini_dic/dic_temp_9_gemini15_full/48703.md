defibrillations:/ˌdiːfɪbrɪˈleɪʃənz/| n. |除颤(复数)|The act of using a defibrillator to deliver an electric shock to the heart to restore a normal heartbeat.  It is a life-saving procedure used to treat cardiac arrest. |例句：The paramedics performed several defibrillations before the patient's heart started beating again. 急救人员在病人心脏重新跳动之前进行了几次除颤。


例句：Repeated defibrillations were necessary to stabilize the patient's heart rhythm.  需要重复进行除颤才能稳定病人的心律。


近义词：cardioversion (if the heart is still beating irregularly), resuscitation


反义词：None  (Not a concept with a direct opposite)


词性:名词
