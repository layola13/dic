gamblers:/ˈɡæmblərz/| n.|赌徒，冒险者|People who habitually gamble, especially those who do so excessively or professionally.  They risk money or other things of value in games of chance or on uncertain events. |例句：The casino was filled with gamblers hoping to win big. 赌场里挤满了希望大赚一笔的赌徒。

例句：Many gamblers lose more money than they win in the long run. 长期来看，许多赌徒输的钱比赢的钱多。


近义词：bettors, risk-takers, speculators

反义词：savers, cautious people


词性:名词
