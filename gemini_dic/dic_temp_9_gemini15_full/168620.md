proclassical:/ˌproʊˈklæsɪkəl/| adj. |新古典主义的，准古典主义的|  Referring to a style or movement that emulates or revives aspects of classical art, literature, or music, often with a modern twist or adaptation.  It suggests a continuation or evolution of classical traditions rather than a strict adherence to them. |Example: The composer's style is described as proclassical, blending traditional forms with contemporary harmonies.  作曲家的风格被描述为新古典主义的，它将传统形式与当代和声融合在一起。|近义词：neoclassical, classicizing|反义词：modernist, avant-garde
