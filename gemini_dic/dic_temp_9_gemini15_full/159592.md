pollenless:/ˈpɑːlənˌləs/| adj.|不产花粉的；无花粉的|Lacking pollen; not producing pollen.|The pollenless variety of this plant is popular because it doesn't cause allergies.  这种植物的不产花粉品种很受欢迎，因为它不会引起过敏。|近义词：pollen-free|反义词：pollen-bearing
