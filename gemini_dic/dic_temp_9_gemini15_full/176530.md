re-return:/ˌriːrɪˈtɜːrn/|v.|重新返回，退回|To return something or someone again; to send something back.|例句：The package was re-returned to the sender because of an incorrect address.包裹因为地址错误被重新退回寄件人。
例句：After the initial rejection, she re-returned the manuscript with revisions.在最初被拒稿后，她修改后重新提交了稿件。|近义词：return, send back|反义词：keep, retain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-returns the book to the library every week. 他每周都把书还回图书馆。
They re-return the faulty products to the manufacturer. 他们把有缺陷的产品退回给制造商。


一般过去时:
She re-returned the call after a few hours.  几个小时后她回拨了电话。
The company re-returned the wrongly delivered goods. 公司退回了送错的货物。


现在完成时:
He has re-returned all the borrowed items. 他已经把所有借来的东西都还回去了。
They have re-returned the survey forms to the research team. 他们已经把调查表退回给研究团队了。


过去完成时:
She had re-returned the dress before the store closed. 商店关门前她已经把衣服退回去了。
They had re-returned the damaged goods before the deadline. 截止日期前他们已经退回了损坏的货物。


将来时:
He will re-return the equipment after the project is completed. 项目完成后，他会归还设备。
They will re-return the documents as soon as possible. 他们会尽快归还文件。


虚拟语气:
If he had known the address, he would have re-returned the letter. 如果他知道地址，他就会把信寄回去了。
I wish I had re-returned the book before it was due. 我真希望我在到期前就把书还回去了。


被动语态:
The package was re-returned to the sender. 包裹被退回寄件人。
The faulty items were re-returned to the supplier. 有缺陷的物品被退回给供应商。


疑问句:
Will you re-return the tools tomorrow? 你明天会把工具还回来吗？
Has she re-returned the borrowed money yet? 她已经把借的钱还了吗？


否定句:
He did not re-return the phone to the store. 他没有把手机退回商店。
They haven't re-returned the signed contract. 他们还没有退回签署的合同。


PHRASE->
re-return policy: 退货政策
re-return shipping label: 退货运送标签
re-return authorization: 退货授权
re-return address: 退货地址
re-return the favor: 偿还人情 (Though not literally a "re-return", it implies a return of a favor.)
