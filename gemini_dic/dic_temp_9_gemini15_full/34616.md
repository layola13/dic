It is also used more generally to refer to a hooked tool or implement.|例句：He used a cleek to retrieve the ball from the rough. 他用一个挖起杆从粗糙的地面取出球。

词源:
The word "cleek" ultimately derives from the Middle English word "clik," meaning "hook" or "claw,"  which further traces back to Old Norse and possibly Proto-Germanic roots.  Its use in golf developed specifically to describe a type of club with a hooked head.
