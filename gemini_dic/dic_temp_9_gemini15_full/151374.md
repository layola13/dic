parlays:/ˈpɑːrˌleɪz/| n. v.| 谈判；夸夸其谈；(赌博中)连续下注 | To engage in a discussion or negotiation, often in an attempt to persuade or achieve a particular outcome.  Also, a series of bets in gambling. |例句：The diplomats parlayed for hours, trying to reach a peace agreement. = 外交官们谈判了数小时，试图达成和平协议。

The gambler parlayed his winnings into a larger bet. = 赌徒将他赢得的钱连续下注，押了更大的赌注。|近义词：negotiate, discuss, confer, wager |反义词：fight, battle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He parleys with the enemy. = 他与敌人谈判。
They parley daily to resolve the issue. = 他们每天进行谈判以解决问题。

一般过去时:
The two leaders parlayed for hours. = 两位领导人谈判了数小时。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

Before the final decision, they had parlayed extensively. = 在做出最终决定之前，他们进行了广泛的谈判。
