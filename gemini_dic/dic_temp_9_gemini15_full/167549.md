prewarms:/priːˈwɔːrmz/| vt.|预热；预先加热|To heat something beforehand, typically to a lower temperature than will be used later.|例句：The chef prewarms the plates before serving the food.  厨师在端上菜之前先把盘子预热。|例句：We prewarmed the oven to 350 degrees before baking the cake. 我们在烤蛋糕之前先把烤箱预热到350度。|近义词：heat, warm up|反义词：cool down
