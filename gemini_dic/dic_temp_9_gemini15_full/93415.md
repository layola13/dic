illogical:/ɪˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. |不合逻辑的，荒谬的 | Not conforming to the rules of logic; unreasonable.  | Example: His argument was illogical and unconvincing. 他的论点不合逻辑且缺乏说服力。 |近义词:unreasonable, absurd, irrational, nonsensical | 反义词:logical, rational, reasonable
