/ˈneɪzəlaɪzɪŋ/| v. |鼻音化；带鼻音发音|The act of producing a nasal sound, or modifying a sound to include nasal resonance.  This involves lowering the velum (soft palate) to allow air to escape through the nose as well as the mouth.| Example:  He nasalized the vowels in his French pronunciation. 他在法语发音中鼻化了元音。  The singer nasalized the final syllable of the song. 歌手将歌曲的最后一个音节鼻化了。|近义词：nasalize |反义词：oralize
