dissentions:/dɪˈsenʃənz/| n. |不和；争执；意见分歧 |Disagreement or conflict of opinion between people.  Dissensions often arise from differing viewpoints, beliefs, or interests, and can range from mild disagreements to serious disputes. |例句：The team was plagued by internal dissensions, hindering its progress.  团队内部充满了不和，阻碍了它的进步。

例句：Despite their initial dissensions, they eventually reached a compromise. 尽管他们最初意见不合，但最终还是达成了妥协。


近义词：discord, strife, contention, conflict, disagreement

反义词：harmony, accord, agreement, concord, unity
