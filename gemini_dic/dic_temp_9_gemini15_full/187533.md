romancer:/rəʊˈmænsə(r)/| n. |浪漫主义者；浪漫小说作家；编造浪漫故事的人|A romancer is someone who writes or tells romantic stories, often exaggerating or inventing details to create a more exciting or appealing narrative. They may focus on love, adventure, or idealized versions of reality.  |例句：He is a seasoned romancer, weaving tales of daring adventures and impossible loves.|近义词：storyteller, novelist, fabulist|反义词：realist, pragmatist|名词
