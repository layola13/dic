imprecise:/ɪmˈpraɪsɪs/ | adj. | 不精确的，不准确的 | Not exact or accurate; vague.  | The map was imprecise, making navigation difficult. 这张地图不精确，使导航变得困难。 | precise, inexact, inaccurate, vague | accurate, exact, precise

Her instructions were imprecise. = 她的指示不精确。
