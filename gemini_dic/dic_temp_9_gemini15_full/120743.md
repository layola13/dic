misanthrope:/ˈmɪzænθroʊp/| n. |厌世者，遁世者 |A person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society.  |He became a misanthrope after a series of betrayals. 他经历了一系列背叛后变成了一个厌世者。| cynic, hermit | philanthropist, altruist | 名词
