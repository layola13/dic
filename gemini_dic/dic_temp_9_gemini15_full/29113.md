cascader:/kæˈskeɪdər/| n. |层叠式组件，级联选择器|A cascading component, often used in user interfaces to allow users to select items from a hierarchical structure.  It typically presents a series of nested dropdown lists or menus where the options in each level depend on the selection made in the previous level.|Example: The website uses a cascader to allow users to select their country, state, and city.  网站使用级联选择器允许用户选择他们的国家、州和城市。|近义词：hierarchical selector, nested dropdown|反义词：single-selection component|名词
