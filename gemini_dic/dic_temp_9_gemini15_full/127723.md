negligent:/ˈneɡlɪdʒənt/| adj.| 疏忽的；粗心的；玩忽职守的 | failing to take proper care in doing something |The driver was negligent in failing to stop at the red light. 司机没有在红灯前停车，这是他的疏忽。|近义词：careless, reckless, remiss, inattentive |反义词：careful, attentive, diligent

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is negligent in his work. 他工作很粗心。
She is often negligent about her appearance. 她经常不在意自己的外表。


一般过去时:
He was negligent in his duties. 他玩忽职守。
The company was negligent in its safety procedures. 公司的安全程序疏忽大意。


现在完成时:
They have been negligent in their responsibilities. 他们一直没有尽到责任。
The government has been negligent in addressing the issue. 政府在处理这个问题上一直很疏忽。


过去完成时:
He had been negligent for years before the accident happened. 事故发生前，他多年来一直疏忽大意。
The company had been negligent in its maintenance checks. 公司在维护检查方面一直疏忽大意。


将来时:
They will be negligent if they ignore the warning signs. 如果他们忽视警告标志，他们将要承担疏忽的责任。
He will likely be negligent in his studies if he continues to party every night. 如果他继续每晚狂欢，他很可能会在学习上疏忽大意。


虚拟语气:
If he had not been negligent, the accident would not have happened. 如果他当时不疏忽大意，事故就不会发生了。
If she were not so negligent, she would have finished the project on time. 如果她不那么粗心大意，她就能按时完成项目了。


被动语态:
His negligence was noted by his supervisor. 他的疏忽被他的主管注意到了。
The company was found to have been negligent in its handling of the matter. 公司被发现对其处理该事项的方式疏忽大意。


疑问句:
Was he negligent in his actions? 他在行动中是否疏忽大意？
Were they negligent in their duties? 他们是否玩忽职守？


否定句:
He was not negligent in his preparation for the exam. 他在考试准备方面并不疏忽。
She was not negligent in her work.  她工作中并不粗心。


PHRASE->

negligent misstatement 过失不实陈述
negligent infliction of emotional distress 因疏忽造成的精神痛苦
gross negligence 重大过失
