revictimizing:/ˌriːˈvɪktɪmaɪzɪŋ/| v. |再次使受害；再次使受苦 |The act of subjecting someone who has already been a victim of a crime or trauma to further victimization.  It often involves a repetition of the original harm or a new harm that is related to the original trauma.  |例句：The insensitive questioning by the police revictimized the witness.  警方的不敏感提问再次使证人受害。

The constant media attention revictimized the survivors of the accident.  持续的媒体关注再次使事故幸存者受害。|近义词：traumatizing, hurting again, inflicting further suffering on|反义词：comforting, supporting, healing
