hidalgo:/ɪˈdælɡoʊ/| n. |西班牙贵族；高贵的绅士；|A Spanish nobleman, especially one of the landed gentry.  The term often implies a chivalrous and honorable man, although sometimes with a touch of romantic idealism or impracticality. |Example:  Don Quixote, the famous literary character, is a quintessential hidalgo.  唐吉坷德，这个著名的文学人物，是一个典型的西班牙贵族。|近义词：gentleman, nobleman, cavalier |反义词：peasant, commoner
