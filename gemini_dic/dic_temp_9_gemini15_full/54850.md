displant:/dɪsˈplænt/ | vt. |移栽；取代；驱逐；移植|To remove a plant from its place of growth and plant it elsewhere; to replace something or someone; to displace or remove someone from their home or position.  |例句：The gardener carefully displanted the seedlings. 园丁小心翼翼地移栽了幼苗。

The new CEO displanted the old management team. 新任首席执行官取代了旧的管理团队。|近义词：transplant, replace, remove, displace |反义词：plant, establish, install


USAGE->

He displants flowers every spring. 他每年春天都移栽花朵。
The company displants outdated equipment regularly.  这家公司定期淘汰过时的设备。


一般过去时:
They displanted the old oak tree to make way for the new building. 他们移栽了那棵老橡树，为新建筑腾出空间。
