episodical:/ˌɛpɪˈsɑːdɪkəl/| adj.|情节的；偶然发生的；间歇性的|Occurring in separate episodes or parts; intermittent; sporadic.|例句：The patient experienced episodic memory loss.  病人经历了间歇性的记忆丧失。
例句：His migraines were episodic, occurring only a few times a year.他的偏头痛是间歇性的，一年只发生几次。|近义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional|反义词：continuous, constant, regular
