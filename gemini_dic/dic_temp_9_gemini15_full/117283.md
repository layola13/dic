mechanograph:/ˈmɛkənəɡræf/ | n. |机械描记器，自动记录器 | A mechanograph is a device that automatically records movements or other mechanical actions.  It might be used to record the movements of a machine or a person's typing. | Example: The researchers used a mechanograph to study the fine motor skills of pianists.  研究人员使用机械描记器来研究钢琴家的精细动作技能。|近义词：recorder, instrument,  chart recorder |反义词：manual recorder (手动记录器) |名词
