dissolubility: /ˌdɪsˌɒljʊˈbɪləti/ | n. |溶解度，可溶性 | The ability of a substance to dissolve in a solvent.  This property is influenced by factors such as temperature, pressure, and the nature of both the solute and the solvent. | Example: The dissoluability of salt in water is high. 盐在水中的溶解度很高。 The dissoluability of this medication is crucial for its effectiveness.  这种药物的溶解度对其疗效至关重要。|近义词：solubility |反义词：insolubility
