legionaries:/ˈliːdʒənɛriːz/| n.|军团士兵；罗马军团士兵|  Roman soldiers who served in the legions.  The term often evokes images of disciplined, highly trained soldiers known for their military prowess and conquests.  |例句：The legionaries marched relentlessly across the desert.  = 军团士兵们毫不疲倦地穿越沙漠。

The legionaries' discipline was legendary. = 军团士兵的纪律是传奇的。

近义词：soldiers, troops, warriors|反义词：civilians
