/ˈprɒpə(r)/|/ˈprɑːpər/ | adj. | 适当的；正确的；合适的；正派的；体面的 | Proper means conforming to standards; correct; appropriate. It can also refer to something genuine or authentic, or to behavior that is socially acceptable and respectable. | Examples:  It's proper to thank someone for a gift. (礼貌上应该感谢别人的礼物。)  That's not the proper way to do it. (那不是正确的方法。) |近义词：suitable, appropriate, correct, decent, respectable |反义词：improper, unsuitable, wrong, indecent, disrespectful
