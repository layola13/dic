nonurgently:/ˌnɒnˈɜːrdʒəntli/| adv.|非紧急地，不紧急地|The adverb "nonurgently" means in a way that is not urgent or pressing. It indicates a lack of immediacy or need for swift action.  The word is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adverb "urgently."|例句：The package was delivered nonurgently, arriving several days later than expected.  包裹非紧急地送达，比预期晚了几天。

The task can be handled nonurgently; there's no immediate deadline. 这项任务可以不紧急处理；没有立即的截止日期。|近义词：leisurely, casually, slowly, deliberately|反义词：urgently, immediately, rapidly, quickly|词性：副词
