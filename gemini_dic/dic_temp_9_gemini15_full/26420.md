bylander:/ˈbaɪlændər/| n.|驳船，平底船|A bylander is a type of flat-bottomed sailing barge, typically used for carrying cargo on shallow rivers and canals.  They are characterized by their broad beam and shallow draft, allowing them to navigate in areas inaccessible to deeper-draft vessels.|例句：The bylander slowly navigated the shallow river, carrying its cargo of timber.|近义词：barge, lighter, sloop|反义词：无
