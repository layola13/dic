descendingly:/dɪˈsɛndɪŋli/ | adv. | 下降地；递减地 |  In a descending manner; gradually decreasing.  It describes a process or sequence that goes from a higher value to a lower value. | Example: The numbers were arranged descendingly, from 100 to 1.  数字按从大到小的顺序排列，从100到1。 The temperature has been descendingly these past few days.  这几天气温一直在下降。|近义词：downwardly, progressively, gradually |反义词：ascendingly, increasingly
