demimonde:/ˈdɛmiˌmɒnd/| n. |半社会，交际花圈，上流社会的边缘人群 |The demimonde refers to a class of people, often women, who live on the fringes of respectable society, typically involved in entertainment or prostitution.  They are often associated with wealth and glamour but lack full social acceptance.  The term often carries a connotation of moral ambiguity and social transgression.|例句：The novel explores the lives of women in the demimonde of 19th-century Paris. 这部小说探究了19世纪巴黎半社会女性的生活。|近义词：underworld, demi-monde,  marginalized group |反义词：high society, elite, upper class
