sibylles:/ˈsɪbɪlz/| n.|女预言家，女先知|The Sibylles were priestesses in ancient Greece and Rome who were believed to possess prophetic powers.  They were often associated with oracles and were consulted for their predictions about the future. The term is often used in a more general sense to refer to any woman with exceptional prophetic abilities.  |例句：The Sibylles' pronouncements were considered to be divinely inspired.  女预言家的预言被认为是神圣启示。

The legend of the Sibyl of Cumae is particularly well-known. 库迈女预言家的传说尤其著名。|近义词：prophetess, seer, oracle, clairvoyant|反义词：N/A
