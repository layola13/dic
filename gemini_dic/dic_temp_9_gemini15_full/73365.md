The foregoers of the environmental movement fought tirelessly for conservation. =  环保运动的先行者为环保事业不懈奋斗。|近义词：predecessors, pioneers, forerunners |反义词：successors, followers

词源:
The word "foregoers" is formed from "fore" (meaning "before" or "in front of") and "goer" (a person who goes).  It's a relatively straightforward compound word reflecting the meaning of those who have gone before.
