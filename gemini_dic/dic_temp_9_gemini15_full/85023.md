harasses:/həˈræsəs/ | vt. |骚扰，困扰，使烦恼 |To annoy or trouble someone persistently.  |例句：He continually harasses his colleagues with unwanted attention. 他不断地用不受欢迎的关注骚扰他的同事。
例句：The bullying continually harassed the child, affecting their mental health.  霸凌不断骚扰孩子，影响了他们的心理健康。|近义词：pester, molest, badger, torment |反义词：soothe, comfort, appease |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harasses his classmates every day. = 他每天都骚扰他的同学们。
She harasses her employees with unreasonable demands. = 她用不合理的要求骚扰她的员工。

一般过去时:
The manager harassed his staff with excessive workload. = 经理用过多的工作量骚扰了他的员工。
They harassed the witnesses to change their testimonies. = 他们骚扰证人以改变他们的证词。

现在完成时:
He has harassed her for months. = 他已经骚扰她好几个月了。
The company has been harassed by numerous lawsuits. = 这家公司受到了无数诉讼的骚扰。

过去完成时:
She had harassed him for years before he finally reported it. = 在他最终举报之前，她已经骚扰了他好几年了。
They had harassed the protesters before the march even began. = 游行开始之前，他们就已经骚扰了抗议者。

将来时:
He will harass her again if she doesn't report him. = 如果她不举报他，他会再次骚扰她。
They will continue to harass the neighborhood if the noise doesn't stop. = 如果噪音不停，他们将继续骚扰邻里。

虚拟语气:
If he harassed her again, I would call the police. = 如果他再次骚扰她，我会报警。
I wish he hadn't harassed those innocent people. = 我真希望他没有骚扰那些无辜的人。

被动语态:
He was harassed by a group of strangers. = 他被一群陌生人骚扰了。
The protesters were harassed by the police. = 抗议者受到了警察的骚扰。

疑问句:
Was she harassed by anyone at the party? = 她在派对上被任何人骚扰了吗？
Are you being harassed at work? = 你在工作中受到骚扰吗？

否定句:
He did not harass her. = 他没有骚扰她。
They have not been harassed by anyone. = 他们没有被任何人骚扰。
