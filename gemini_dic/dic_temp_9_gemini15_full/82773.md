guillotining: /ˌɡɪləˈtiːnɪŋ/ | n. | 断头台处决；迅速而彻底地砍掉；突然中止 | The act of beheading with a guillotine; the process of quickly and completely removing or ending something. |


例句：

The swift guillotining of the bill surprised many.  = 这项法案的迅速否决令许多人感到惊讶。

The company's guillotining of its marketing budget led to a decline in sales. = 公司迅速削减营销预算导致销售额下降。


近义词：beheading, execution, termination, abolishment, cancellation


反义词：preservation, continuation, establishment, implementation


词性：名词


词源：源于法国医生Joseph-Ignace Guillotin的名字，他提议使用这种执行死刑的工具。  The word originates from the name of the French physician Joseph-Ignace Guillotin, who proposed the use of this execution device.
