engraft:/ɪnˈɡræft/ | vt. |嫁接；植入；灌输|To insert (a shoot, bud, or scion) into a slit in the stem or bark of a plant so that it unites with the stock and continues its growth.  To implant or introduce (something) into something else.|例句：The gardener skillfully engrafted the rosebud onto the stem. 园丁熟练地将玫瑰花蕾嫁接到茎上。

例句：They tried to engraft new ideas into the company culture. 他们试图将新思想灌输到公司文化中。


近义词：graft, implant, insert, inoculate

反义词：remove, excise, delete
