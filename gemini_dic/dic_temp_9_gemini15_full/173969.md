quasi-spiritedly: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈspɪrɪtɪdli/ | adv. |  几乎精神焕发地；有几分精神十足地 |  Describes an action or state that shows a degree of spiritedness, but not fully or genuinely so. It suggests a semblance of energy or enthusiasm, often with a hint of pretense or superficiality. |


例句：

She approached the task quasi-spiritedly, more out of obligation than genuine interest.  = 她几乎是精神焕发地开始这项任务，更多的是出于责任而不是真正的兴趣。

He gave a quasi-spirited speech, though his heart wasn't really in it. = 他发表了一个几乎是精神十足的演讲，尽管他的内心并不真正投入其中。


近义词： halfheartedly, unenthusiastically, with seeming enthusiasm


反义词： energetically, enthusiastically, passionately, wholeheartedly


词性：副词
