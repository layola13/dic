caretaker:/ˈkeərˌteɪkər/ | n. |看护人，管理人，临时负责人 |A caretaker is a person who takes care of something or someone, especially temporarily.  This can range from a person looking after a child or elderly person to someone managing a property. |例句：The caretaker looked after the building while the owner was away.  看房人在他不在的时候照看房子。  The caretaker of the museum was very knowledgeable. 博物馆的管理员非常博学。 |近义词： custodian, guardian, manager, supervisor |反义词： owner, proprietor
