hyphenated:/ˈhaɪfəneɪtɪd/| adj. | 带连字符的；用连字符连接的 | Having a hyphen or hyphens.  A hyphenated word or phrase is one that is formed by joining two or more words with a hyphen. | Example: The word "well-being" is hyphenated.  这个词“well-being”是用连字符连接的。|近义词：connected, joined, combined |反义词：unhyphenated, separate
