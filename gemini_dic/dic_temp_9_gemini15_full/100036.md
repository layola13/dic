interpilaster:/ˌɪntərˈpɪlæstər/| n. | 中柱, 中间小柱 | An architectural element, a small pilaster placed between larger pilasters or columns.  It is often decorative and less substantial than the main pilasters. | The building featured interpilasters between the main columns, adding visual interest to the facade.  这座建筑在主柱之间装饰了中柱，增加了立面的视觉趣味。| pilaster, column |  (反义词较难直接对应，可考虑与“没有中柱”相关的描述)| 名词
