feedable: /ˈfiːdəbl/ | adj. | 可喂养的，可饲养的 | Capable of being fed; suitable for feeding.  |例句：The baby bird is feedable now that it's hatched.  现在小鸟孵出来了，可以喂养了。

The young puppies are feedable with puppy formula.  这些幼犬可以用幼犬奶粉喂养。


近义词：eatable, edible, nurturable | 反义词：inedible, uneatable


词源:  Derived from the verb "feed" + "-able" (suffix indicating capability or suitability).  The root "feed" traces back to Old English "fēdan," meaning "to nourish" or "to give food to."
