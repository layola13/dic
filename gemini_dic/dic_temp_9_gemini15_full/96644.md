indusium:/ɪnˈduːʒiəm/| n. |  盖膜；包被；（蕨类植物）囊群盖 | A thin membrane or covering, especially one found in certain plants or animals.  It often refers to the protective covering of a fern sorus or the membrane covering the corpus callosum in the brain. | Example: The indusium of the fern protected the developing sporangia.  蕨类的囊群盖保护着正在发育的孢子囊。  The indusium of the brain plays a crucial role in interhemispheric communication. 大脑的盖膜在半球间通讯中起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词：covering, membrane, veil | 反义词：exposure
