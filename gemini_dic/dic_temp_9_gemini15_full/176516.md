re-provision:/ˌriːprəˈvɪʒən/| vt.|重新配置，再次供应|To provide something again or anew, especially resources or services.|例句：The IT department had to re-provision the server after the security breach.  IT 部门在安全漏洞发生后不得不重新配置服务器。

例句：The company decided to re-provision its cloud infrastructure to improve scalability. 公司决定重新配置其云基础设施以提高可扩展性。


近义词：reconfigure, reallocate, resupply|反义词：decommission, dismantle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-provision the servers every month. = 他们每月重新配置服务器。
The company re-provisions its cloud resources based on demand. = 公司根据需求重新配置其云资源。


一般过去时:
The system administrator re-provisioned the database after the outage. = 系统管理员在中断后重新配置了数据库。
