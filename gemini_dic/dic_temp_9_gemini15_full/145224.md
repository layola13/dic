overbeating:/ˌoʊvərˈbiːtɪŋ/ | n. |过度敲打；过度搅拌|The act of beating something too much, resulting in undesirable consequences. This can apply to various contexts, such as cooking (e.g., overbeating egg whites) or physical actions (e.g., overbeating a drum).|例句：Overbeating the egg whites resulted in a tough meringue.  过度搅拌蛋白导致蛋白酥皮变硬。

The constant overbeating of the drum annoyed the audience.  持续过度敲打鼓声惹恼了观众。|近义词：overmixing, excessive beating |反义词：underbeating
