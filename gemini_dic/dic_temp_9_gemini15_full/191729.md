scicomm:/ˈsaɪkɒm/| n.|科学传播|Science communication is the practice of communicating scientific information and ideas to a wider audience.  It aims to make complex scientific concepts understandable and engaging for non-scientists.  This can involve various methods, such as writing articles, creating videos, giving presentations, or using social media.| 例句：Effective scicomm is crucial for fostering public understanding of science and informing policy decisions.  有效的科学传播对于促进公众对科学的理解和为政策决策提供信息至关重要。|近义词：science outreach, science writing, public understanding of science|反义词：scientific secrecy, obfuscation|名词
