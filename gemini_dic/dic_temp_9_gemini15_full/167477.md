/prɪˈvɛnʃən/| n. |预防措施；阻止；防止|The act of preventing something from happening or being done.  It refers to actions taken to avoid or reduce the risk of an undesirable outcome.|Regular exercise is a good prevention against heart disease. 定期锻炼是预防心脏病的好方法。|近义词：avoidance, prevention, safeguard |反义词：promotion, encouragement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Prevention is better than cure. = 预防胜于治疗。
What prevention measures are you taking? = 你正在采取什么预防措施？


一般过去时:
The government implemented new prevention strategies last year. = 政府去年实施了新的预防战略。
What prevention methods did they use? = 他们使用了什么预防方法？


现在完成时:
Significant progress has been made in disease prevention. = 在疾病预防方面取得了重大进展。
Have you taken any steps for fire prevention? = 你采取过任何防火措施吗？


过去完成时:

What prevention steps had they taken before the accident? = 事故发生前他们采取了什么预防措施？


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If we had taken better prevention measures, the accident could have been avoided. = 如果我们采取更好的预防措施，事故本来是可以避免的。
I wish they had implemented more effective prevention strategies. = 我希望他们已经实施了更有效的预防战略。


被动语态:
Effective prevention measures are being developed. = 正在开发有效的预防措施。
Prevention methods were discussed at the meeting. = 会议上讨论了预防方法。


疑问句:

否定句:
