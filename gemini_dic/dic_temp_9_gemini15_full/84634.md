hand-made:/ˌhændˈmeɪd/| adj.|手工制作的|Made by hand, not by machine.  This implies a greater level of craftsmanship and often a higher price. |例句：I bought a hand-made sweater from a local artisan. 我从当地一位工匠那里买了一件手工编织的毛衣。
例句：This hand-made pottery is a beautiful example of traditional craft. 这件手工陶器是传统工艺的精美典范。|近义词：handmade, handcrafted, artisanal|反义词：mass-produced, machine-made|词性：形容词
