multigenic:/ˌmʌltiˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|多基因的，多基因遗传的|relating to or determined by multiple genes.  Many complex traits, such as height and weight, are multigenic.  |例句：The inheritance of human height is a multigenic trait.|近义词：polygenic|反义词：monogenic
