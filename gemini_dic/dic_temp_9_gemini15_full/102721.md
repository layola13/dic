jangling: /ˈdʒæŋɡlɪŋ/ | adj. | 叮当作响的；刺耳的；令人不安的 | Making a repeated, sharp, ringing sound, like that of metal objects striking together; jarring or discordant. |  例句：The jangling of keys irritated him.  钥匙的叮当声让他很烦躁。
The jangling bells announced the arrival of the circus. 叮当作响的铃声宣告马戏团的到来。| 近义词：clattering, tinkling, jarring, discordant | 反义词：silent, harmonious, melodious |
