conspire:/kənˈspaɪər/| v.|共谋；密谋；图谋|to plan secretly with someone else to do something bad or illegal.  |例句：They conspired to overthrow the government.他们密谋推翻政府。

例句：The evidence suggests that they conspired to defraud the company.证据表明他们合谋欺诈公司。


近义词：plot, scheme, intrigue, collude|反义词：cooperate, collaborate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They conspire against their rivals. = 他们密谋对付他们的对手。
We often conspire to surprise our friends. = 我们经常密谋给朋友们惊喜。


一般过去时:
The two men conspired to commit the robbery. = 这两个人密谋犯下抢劫案。
She conspired with her brother to steal the money. = 她与她的兄弟合谋偷钱。


现在完成时:
They have conspired against the company for years. = 他们多年来一直密谋对付这家公司。
The evidence shows that they have conspired to hide the truth. = 证据表明他们一直在密谋隐瞒真相。


过去完成时:
It turned out that they had conspired to kill the witness. = 原来他们密谋杀害了证人。
The investigation revealed that they had conspired to defraud the bank. = 调查显示他们曾密谋诈骗银行。


将来时:
They will conspire to take control of the company. = 他们将密谋控制公司。
I suspect they will conspire to ruin his reputation. = 我怀疑他们将密谋毁掉他的名誉。


虚拟语气:
If they had conspired openly, they would have been arrested sooner. = 如果他们公开密谋，他们会被更早逮捕。
I wish they hadn't conspired to deceive me. = 我真希望他们没有密谋欺骗我。


被动语态:
The plot was believed to have been conspired by several key figures. = 人们相信这个阴谋是由几个关键人物策划的。
It was rumored that a coup was being conspired against the president. = 有传言说正在密谋针对总统的政变。


疑问句:
Did they conspire to commit the crime? = 他们是否密谋犯下罪行？
Who conspired with him to achieve this goal? = 谁和他一起密谋达到这个目标？


否定句:
They did not conspire to harm anyone. = 他们没有密谋伤害任何人。
She denied conspiring with the terrorists. = 她否认与恐怖分子同谋。


PHRASE->
conspire against = 阴谋反对
conspire to do something = 密谋做某事
conspire with someone = 与某人共谋
conspire in silence = 默契地合作
conspire together = 共同密谋

词源：
From Old French *conspirer, from Latin conspirare "to breathe together, agree," from com- "together" + spirare "to breathe."  The sense evolved from "agree" to "plot together."
