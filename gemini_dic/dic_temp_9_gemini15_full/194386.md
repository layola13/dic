self-projections:/ˌsɛlf prəˈdʒɛkʃənz/ | n. |自我投射；自我映射|The act of attributing one's own thoughts, feelings, or characteristics to another person or object.  It often involves unconsciously projecting one's own unresolved conflicts or emotions onto others.  Self-projections can significantly impact interpersonal relationships and self-perception. |Example: Her constant criticism of others could be a self-projection of her own insecurities.  她的不断批评别人可能是她自身不安全感的投射。

His anger towards his boss might be a self-projection of his own dissatisfaction with his life. 他对他老板的愤怒可能是他对自身生活不满的投射。


近义词：projection, attribution, transference | 反义词：self-awareness, objectivity
