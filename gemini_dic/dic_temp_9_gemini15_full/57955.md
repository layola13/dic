dripper:/ˈdrɪpər/| n.|滴管；滴漏；点滴器|A dripper is a device that allows liquid to fall drop by drop.  It can refer to a medical device for administering fluids intravenously, a coffee maker that uses a slow drip method, or a simple device with a small hole for controlled liquid dispensing.|例句：The nurse carefully adjusted the IV dripper to control the flow rate. 护士小心地调整静脉滴注器以控制流速。

例句：He used a dripper to water his delicate orchids. 他用滴管浇灌他珍贵的兰花。

近义词：dropper, dispenser, irrigator|反义词：pourer (in the context of rapid liquid dispensing)|词性:名词
