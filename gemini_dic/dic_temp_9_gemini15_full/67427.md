extincting:/ɪkˈstɪŋktɪŋ/ | v. | 灭绝；使灭绝 |  The process of a species, family, or larger group being reduced to zero, resulting in its complete disappearance from Earth.  This often involves a decline in population numbers over time, leading to the ultimate extinction event. | The current rate of extinction is alarming, with many species facing habitat loss and climate change. = 目前灭绝的速度令人担忧，许多物种面临栖息地丧失和气候变化。

Many plant and animal species are currently extincting due to human activity. = 许多动植物物种目前正由于人类活动而灭绝。


近义词：dying out, becoming extinct, perishing

反义词：flourishing, thriving, proliferating
