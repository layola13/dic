putties:/ˈpʌtiz/| n.|腻子，油灰|A putty is a soft, pliable material used for filling cracks, sealing joints, and glazing. It is typically made from a mixture of oil and pigment, and can be applied using a putty knife or similar tool.  |例句：He used putty to fill the cracks in the window frame. 他用腻子填补了窗框的裂缝。

例句：The glazier used putty to secure the window panes.  玻璃工用油灰固定窗玻璃。


近义词：filler, sealant, mastic|反义词：None specifically.  |词性:名词
