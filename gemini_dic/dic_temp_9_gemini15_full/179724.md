reenumeration:/ˌriːɪˌnuːməˈreɪʃən/| n. |重新编号；重新枚举 |The act of enumerating again; a second or fresh enumeration.  It often involves revising or correcting a previous numbering system. |例句：The reenumeration of the files was necessary after the system crash. 系统崩溃后，需要对文件重新编号。|例句：The reenumeration of the library's collection took several months. 图书馆馆藏的重新编号花费了几个月的时间。|近义词：renumbering, relisting |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be leaving the numbers unchanged.
