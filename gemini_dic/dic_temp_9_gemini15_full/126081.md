/ˈmʌsnt/| modal verb | 不可以，禁止 | Mustn't is a contraction of "must not," indicating a strong prohibition or obligation not to do something.  It expresses a stronger sense of prohibition than "should not" or "ought not." | You mustn't smoke in here. = 你不可以在这里吸烟。

You mustn't tell anyone about this. = 你一定不要告诉任何人这件事。


近义词：may not, should not, ought not, cannot | 反义词：must, should, ought to |  modal verb
