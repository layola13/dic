disciple:/dɪˈsaɪpəl/| n. |信徒；弟子；门徒 | A follower of a person, especially a religious leader.  A person who is a student or follower of a teacher or philosopher. |例句：He was a devoted disciple of Gandhi. 他是甘地的忠实信徒。

例句：Many disciples gathered to hear the master's teachings. 许多弟子聚集在一起聆听大师的教诲。


近义词：follower, student, adherent, follower, pupil

反义词：teacher, master, instructor


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a disciple of Buddha. = 他是佛的信徒。
She is a devoted disciple of yoga. = 她是瑜伽的虔诚信徒。


一般过去时:
He was a disciple of Confucius. = 他是孔子的弟子。
They were disciples of Socrates. = 他们是苏格拉底的弟子。


现在完成时:
She has been a disciple of that teacher for many years. = 她跟随那位老师多年了。
He has remained a faithful disciple throughout his life. = 他一生都保持着忠实的信徒身份。


过去完成时:
He had been a disciple of the guru for over a decade. = 他跟随这位大师超过十年了。
They had been disciples of the same master before they parted ways. = 在他们分道扬鑣之前，他们曾是同一个大师的弟子。


将来时:
She will become a disciple of the new spiritual leader. = 她将成为这位新的精神领袖的信徒。
He will remain a loyal disciple until his death. = 他将忠于他的信仰直到去世。


虚拟语气:
If I were a disciple, I would follow his teachings diligently. = 如果我是他的信徒，我会认真遵循他的教诲。
I wish I had been a disciple of Leonardo da Vinci. = 我真希望我曾是达芬奇的弟子。


被动语态:
He was considered a disciple of great importance. = 他被认为是一位非常重要的弟子。
Many great works were created by his disciples. = 许多伟大的作品都是由他的弟子们创作的。


疑问句:
Are you a disciple of any particular philosophy? = 你是哪个特定哲学的信徒吗？
Was he a disciple of any famous teacher? = 他是哪位名师的弟子？


否定句:
He is not a disciple of any organized religion. = 他不是任何有组织宗教的信徒。
She was never a disciple of that harsh teacher. = 她从未是那位严厉老师的弟子。


PHRASE->
a devoted disciple = 忠实的信徒
a close disciple = 亲密的弟子
a leading disciple = 主要的弟子
a true disciple = 真正的信徒
former disciple = 前弟子
