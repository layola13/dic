diurnalness:/daɪˈɜːrnəl nəs/ | n. |昼行性；白昼性；每日的特性 |The quality or state of being diurnal; the characteristic of being active during the day.  It refers to the regular pattern of activity associated with daytime.  |例句：The diurnalness of the cicadas was a constant sound throughout the summer.  蝉的昼行性是整个夏天持续不断的声响。

The diurnalness of her routine was comforting. 她日常生活的规律性令人感到安慰。|近义词：dailyness, regularity (in relation to daily activity) |反义词：nocturnality
