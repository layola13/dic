lurk:/lɜːk/| v.|潜伏；潜藏；埋伏|to be or remain hidden, so as to wait in ambush for someone or something, or to spy on someone.|例句：The thief lurked in the shadows, waiting for his opportunity. 小偷潜伏在阴影中，等待机会。|例句：He lurked around the corner, watching the house. 他躲在拐角处，观察着房子。|近义词：hide, sneak, conceal|反义词：reveal, expose, show


USAGE->

They lurk online, spreading misinformation. = 他们在网上潜伏，传播虚假信息。
