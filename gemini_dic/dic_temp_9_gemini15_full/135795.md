nonproletarians:/ˌnɒnprəʊlɪˈteəriənz/ | n. |非无产阶级人士；资产阶级 |People who are not members of the working class;  the bourgeoisie.  The term often implies a contrast with the proletariat, highlighting differences in socioeconomic status, ownership of the means of production, and political power.  | Example: The policies favored the nonproletarians, widening the gap between the rich and the poor.  非无产阶级人士的政策，加剧了贫富差距。|近义词:bourgeoisie, capitalists, elites |反义词:proletariat, working class
