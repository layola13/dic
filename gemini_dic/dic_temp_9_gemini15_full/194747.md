selfworth: /ˈsɛlfwɜːrθ/ | n. | 自我价值，自我尊重 |  A feeling of being worthy of respect and appreciation; confidence and satisfaction in oneself.  | Example:  Building self-worth requires self-acceptance and positive self-talk.  建立自我价值需要自我接纳和积极的自我对话。


Example:  She struggled with low self-worth after the breakup.  分手后，她一直与低自尊作斗争。| 近义词: self-esteem, self-respect, confidence | 反义词: self-doubt, low self-esteem, insecurity | 名词


USAGE->

He has high self-worth. = 他有很高的自我价值。
She values her self-worth. = 她珍视她的自我价值。

He lacked self-worth in his younger years. = 他年轻时缺乏自我价值感。
