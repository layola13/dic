larghetto:/lɑːrˈɡetoʊ/| adj.| 小快板 |  A musical direction indicating a tempo somewhat slower than allegro but faster than andante. It suggests a graceful and flowing style of playing.  | Example: The second movement of the symphony is marked larghetto, creating a serene and contemplative atmosphere.  第二乐章标注为小快板，营造出宁静沉思的氛围。 | 近义词：andantino, moderato | 反义词：presto, allegro
