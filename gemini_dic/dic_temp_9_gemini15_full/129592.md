non-committally:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪtəlɪ/| adv. |不承担责任地；含糊其辞地；不置可否地 |  In a way that does not express a definite opinion or decision.  It suggests a reluctance to take a position or make a commitment. |例句：He answered non-committally, avoiding a direct response. 他含糊其辞地回答，避免了直接回应。

例句：She nodded non-committally, giving away nothing. 她不置可否地點了點頭，什麼也沒透露。

近义词：vaguely, ambiguously, evasively, hesitantly

反义词：directly, decisively, explicitly, frankly


词性:副词
