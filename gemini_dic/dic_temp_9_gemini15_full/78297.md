germinators:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪtərz/| n.|发芽者；萌芽者；促使发芽者|A germinator is something that causes something else to begin to grow or develop.  It can refer to a person, a process, or a thing.|例句：The warm spring rain acted as a germinator for the seeds. 春雨温暖，促使种子发芽。|例句：He is a germinator of new ideas in the company. 他是公司新思想的催生者。|近义词：incubator, catalyst, instigator|反义词：inhibitor, suppressor, destroyer|名词
