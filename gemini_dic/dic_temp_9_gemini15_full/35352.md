co-founder:/ˈkoʊˌfaʊndər/| n. |共同创始人 |A co-founder is one of the people who jointly establish a company or organization.  They share the initial responsibility and risk associated with creating and launching the venture.| Example:  She is a co-founder of a successful tech startup. 她是一家成功的科技初创公司的共同创始人。|近义词：joint founder, fellow founder|反义词：sole founder, single founder
