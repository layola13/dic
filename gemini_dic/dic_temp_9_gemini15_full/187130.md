/ɑːrɛnˈɛs/ | n. | 意识；认识；清醒；感觉；知觉 | The awareness of one's surroundings is crucial for safety.  对周围环境的意识对于安全至关重要。 |  consciousness, perception, cognizance | unconsciousness, unawareness | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Awareness is important for personal growth. = 意识对个人成长很重要。
His awareness of the problem is limited. = 他对这个问题的认识有限。


一般过去时:
Her awareness of the danger increased. = 她对危险的意识增强了。
There was a growing awareness of the environmental crisis. = 对环境危机的认识日益增强。


现在完成时:
Public awareness of the issue has grown significantly. = 公众对这个问题的认识已显著提高。
We have raised awareness about the importance of education. = 我们提高了人们对教育重要性的认识。


过去完成时:
By the time the accident happened, his awareness of the risk had faded. = 事故发生时，他对风险的意识已经减弱了。
She realized that her awareness of the situation had been incomplete. = 她意识到她对局势的认识是不完整的。


将来时:
Increased awareness will lead to better solutions. = 提高认识将带来更好的解决方案。
We anticipate that awareness of the problem will increase in the future. = 我们预计未来人们对这个问题的认识将会提高。


虚拟语气:
If I had been more aware, I would have acted differently. = 如果我当时更警觉，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had been more aware of the consequences. = 我真希望我当时更清楚后果。


被动语态:
Awareness of the problem was raised by the media. = 这个问题的意识是由媒体提高的。
The issue was brought to public awareness through various campaigns. = 通过各种宣传活动，这个问题引起了公众的关注。


疑问句:

否定句:
I was unaware of the changes. = 我没有意识到这些变化。
They were completely unaware of the danger. = 他们完全没有意识到危险。
