sideroad:/ˈsaɪdˌroʊd/ | n. |辅路，支路 |A sideroad is a minor road running parallel to and usually less important than a main road.  It often provides access to properties or smaller settlements. |例句：We took the sideroad to avoid the traffic on the highway. 我们走辅路以避开高速公路上的交通拥堵。|近义词：byroad, side street, lane |反义词：highway, main road, freeway
