foamer:/ˈfoʊmər/ | n. |发泡剂，泡沫发生器 | A substance or device that produces foam.  |Example: The barista used a milk foamer to create a creamy latte.  咖啡师用奶泡器制作了一杯奶油拉花拿铁。|近义词：frother, foam maker |反义词：None specifically, but words implying a lack of foam could be considered antonyms, such as "flattener".
