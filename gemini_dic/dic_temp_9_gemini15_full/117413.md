medicining: /ˈmɛdəsɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 药物治疗；医疗行为 | The act or process of administering medicine or providing medical care.  It often refers to the practice of medicine as a whole, or to a specific course of treatment. | 例句：The medicining of the injured soldier was swift and efficient.  受伤士兵的治疗迅速而有效。

The ongoing medicining of the patient requires careful monitoring.  病人的持续治疗需要仔细监测。| 近义词：treatment, medication, therapy | 反义词：neglect, abandonment
