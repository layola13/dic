charity's:/ˈtʃærətiz/| n. |慈善机构的；慈善的|The possessive form of "charity," indicating belonging to or relating to a charity.  It is used to show ownership or connection. For example, "charity's work" refers to the work done by a charity. |例句：The charity's annual fundraising gala was a huge success.  慈善机构的年度筹款晚会非常成功。

The charity's mission is to help the homeless. 慈善机构的使命是帮助无家可归者。


近义词：charitable organization's, philanthropic institution's|反义词：N/A|词性：名词所有格
