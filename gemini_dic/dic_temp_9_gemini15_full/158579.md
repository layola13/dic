pleurobranch: /ˈplʊərəˌbræŋk/ | n. |侧鳃类动物|A marine gastropod mollusk of the order Pleurobranchida, characterized by a flattened body and often brightly colored.  They lack a shell in the adult stage.  Many species have a prominent parapodial fold along their sides. |例句：The pleurobranch gracefully glided across the seabed. 侧鳃类动物优雅地滑过海底。
