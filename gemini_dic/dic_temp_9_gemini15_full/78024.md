gentrified:/ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪd/| adj.|士绅化的；中产阶级化的|Improved by the influx of wealthier residents, often leading to displacement of lower-income residents.|例句：The neighborhood has become gentrified, with trendy cafes and boutiques replacing older businesses.|近义词：upscale, improved, upgraded|反义词：degraded, deteriorated
