overreduction: /ˌoʊvərɪˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 过度简化；还原过度 | The simplification of a complex phenomenon to an overly simplistic explanation, often omitting crucial details or nuances.  This can lead to inaccurate or incomplete understanding. | Example: The overreduction of climate change to a simple issue of carbon emissions ignores the complex interplay of economic and social factors.  过度简化气候变化为简单的碳排放问题忽略了经济和社会因素之间的复杂相互作用。| 近义词: simplification, oversimplification, reductionism | 反义词:  detailed analysis, nuanced understanding | 名词
