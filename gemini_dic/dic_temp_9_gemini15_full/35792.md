cockcrowing: /ˈkɒkˌkroʊɪŋ/ | n. | 鸡叫声；报晓 | The sound made by a rooster crowing, especially at dawn.  It often symbolizes the beginning of a new day or a new beginning. | Example: The cockcrowing woke me before sunrise.  鸡叫声在日出前把我吵醒了。  The distant cockcrowing signaled the start of another day in the countryside.  远处传来的鸡叫声标志着乡村又一天的开始。| 近义词：rooster crow, crowing | 反义词：silence, quiet
