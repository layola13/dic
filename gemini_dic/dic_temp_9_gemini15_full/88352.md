hippophagous:/ˌhɪpəˈfæɡəs/| adj.|食河马的；以河马为食的|Feeding on hippopotamuses.  The term describes animals or organisms that consume hippopotamuses as a primary or secondary food source.  |Example:  While rare, some large crocodiles might exhibit hippophagous behavior.  = 虽然罕见，但一些大型鳄鱼可能会表现出食河马的行为。  |近义词: hippopotamus-eating |反义词: herbivorous (if referring to an animal that could be either carnivorous or herbivorous)


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "eat," "ate," etc.  The usage focuses on describing an animal's diet.
