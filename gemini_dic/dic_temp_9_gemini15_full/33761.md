circulate:/ˈsɜːrkjuleɪt/|/ˈsɝːkjuleɪt/| vt.& vi.|循环；流通；传播|To move or cause to move continuously along a circular or other closed path.|The blood circulates throughout the body.血液在全身循环。|The rumour circulated quickly through the town.谣言迅速传遍了小镇。|diffuse, spread, disseminate|stagnate, stop|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blood circulates throughout the body. = 血液在全身循环。
News circulates rapidly in the digital age. = 在数字时代，新闻传播迅速。


一般过去时:
The rumour circulated quickly after the accident. = 事故发生后，谣言迅速传播开来。
They circulated a petition to save the park. = 他们散发请愿书以拯救公园。


现在完成时:
The news has circulated widely. = 这个消息已经广泛传播。
The rumour has circulated for weeks. = 这个谣言已经流传了好几周了。


过去完成时:
The story had circulated before the meeting. = 在会议之前，这个故事就已经传开了。
The information had already circulated among the employees. = 信息已经在员工中流传开了。


将来时:
The company will circulate a new memo tomorrow. = 公司明天将发布一份新的备忘录。
The details will circulate once the investigation is complete. = 一旦调查完成，细节就会公布。


虚拟语气:
If the information circulated more widely, more people would be aware of it. = 如果信息传播得更广泛，更多的人就会知道它。
I wish the news hadn't circulated so quickly. = 我希望这个消息没有传播得这么快。


被动语态:
The memo was circulated to all staff. = 备忘录已分发给所有员工。
The information is being circulated among the team members. = 信息正在团队成员之间传播。


疑问句:
Does the magazine circulate nationally? = 这本杂志在全国发行吗？
How is the newsletter circulated? = 这份简报是如何发行的？


否定句:
The rumour did not circulate widely. = 这个谣言并没有广泛传播。
The document hasn't circulated yet. = 文件还没有发行。


PHRASE->
circulate a rumour = 散布谣言

circulate a petition = 发起请愿
