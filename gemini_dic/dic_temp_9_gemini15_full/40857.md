cooing:/ˈkuːɪŋ/| n. v.|咕咕叫声；轻声低语|The soft sounds of cooing doves filled the air.  鸽子温柔的咕咕声充满了空气。|近义词：murmuring, purring, murmuring, billing and cooing |反义词：shouting, screaming

词源:
The word "cooing" originates from the Old French word "couer," meaning "to croon" or "to murmur."  This word further derives from the imitative sound of a dove's call.  The English word evolved through Middle English and gradually took on its current meaning.
