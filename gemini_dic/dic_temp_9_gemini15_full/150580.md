paper:/ˈpeɪpər/ | n. | 纸；论文；报纸 |  A thin material made from wood pulp or other fibers, used for writing, printing, or drawing.  | Example: I need some paper to write on. 我需要一些纸来写字。  | Example: She wrote a research paper on climate change. 她写了一篇关于气候变化的研究论文。|近义词：document, article, newspaper | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The printer is running out of paper. = 打印机快没纸了。
This paper describes a new method. = 这篇论文描述了一种新方法。

一般过去时:
I wrote my essay on recycled paper. = 我用再生纸写我的论文。
He read the newspaper from cover to cover. = 他把报纸从头到尾读了一遍。

现在完成时:
I have already printed the document.  = 我已经打印好了文件。
They have published several papers on this topic. = 他们已经发表了几篇关于这个主题的论文。

过去完成时:
She had already read the paper before the meeting. = 会议前她已经读过这篇论文了。
We had run out of paper, so we couldn't print. = 我们用完了纸，所以无法打印。

将来时:
I will buy some more paper tomorrow. = 我明天会买一些纸。
The journal will publish his paper next month. = 该期刊将于下个月发表他的论文。

虚拟语气:
If I had more paper, I would print the report. = 如果我有更多纸，我会打印报告。
I wish I had submitted my paper earlier. = 我真希望我早点提交我的论文。

被动语态:
The paper was written by a renowned expert. = 这篇论文是一位知名专家撰写的。
The newspaper is printed daily. = 这份报纸每天印刷。

疑问句:
What kind of paper do you need? = 你需要什么类型的纸？
What is the title of your paper? = 你论文的标题是什么？

否定句:
I don't have enough paper to print this. = 我没有足够的纸来打印这个。
He didn't submit his paper on time. = 他没有按时提交论文。
