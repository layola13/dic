imperfectibility:/ɪmˌpɜːrfɛktəˈbɪləti/| n. |不完美性，不可完善性 |The inherent inability to achieve perfection; the state or quality of being imperfect.  This concept often arises in philosophical discussions about human nature and the limitations of progress.  |例句：The inherent imperfectibility of human nature is a recurring theme in literature. 人性的固有不完美性是文学中反复出现的主题。

The pursuit of knowledge highlights the imperfectibility of human understanding.  对知识的追求突出了人类理解的不完美性。|近义词：imperfection, flaw, defect |反义词：perfectibility, flawlessness
