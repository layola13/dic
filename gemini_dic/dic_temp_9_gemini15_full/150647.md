paplike:/ˈpæplʌɪk/| adj.| 乳状的；糊状的；柔软的 | Resembling pap; soft and pulpy.  The word evokes the texture of a soft, pulpy substance, similar to porridge or gruel.  | 例句：The cooked potatoes were paplike and lacked flavor.  煮熟的土豆像糊一样，而且没有味道。

The tumor was described as a paplike mass. 肿瘤被描述成一个糊状的肿块。


近义词：pulpy, mushy, soft, creamy

反义词：firm, solid, hard, dry

词性：形容词
