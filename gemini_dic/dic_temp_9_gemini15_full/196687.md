severances:/ˈsɛvərənsɪz/| n.|遣散费；解雇；断绝；切断|The act of severing; the state of being severed; specifically, compensation paid to an employee upon termination of employment.|例句：The company offered generous severances to its laid-off employees. 公司为被解雇的员工提供了丰厚的遣散费。|例句：The severances between the two countries were complete after the war. 战争结束后，两国之间的关系完全断绝了。|近义词：dismissal, termination, separation, severance pay|反义词：retention, employment, continuation
