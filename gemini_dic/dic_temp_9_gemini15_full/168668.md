proconsultation:/ˌproʊkɒnsəlˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 预先咨询；事先协商 |  A consultation that takes place before a decision is made or an action is taken.  It aims to gather information and opinions to inform the decision-making process. | Example: The proconsultation with the stakeholders ensured that everyone's concerns were addressed before the project commenced.  预先与利益相关者进行协商确保项目开始前所有人的顾虑都得到了解决。 | 近义词:preliminary consultation, prior consultation, advance consultation | 反义词:post-consultation, retrospective consultation | 名词
