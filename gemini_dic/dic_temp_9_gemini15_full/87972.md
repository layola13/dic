high-handed:/ˈhaɪˈhændɪd/| adj.|专横的；傲慢的；武断的 | Acting in a domineering or arrogant manner; disregarding the feelings or rights of others.  |例句：He dismissed their concerns with a high-handed wave of his hand. 他傲慢地挥挥手，不屑一顾地打发了他们的担忧。|例句：The manager's high-handed treatment of his employees led to a strike. 经理对员工的专横态度导致了罢工。|近义词：autocratic, dictatorial, imperious, overbearing |反义词：considerate, humble, respectful, deferential


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He acts in a high-handed manner. = 他以专横的态度行事。
She is always high-handed with her subordinates. = 她总是对下属颐指气使。


一般过去时:
The boss acted high-handed in firing the employee without warning. = 老板在没有警告的情况下解雇员工，做法专横。
They handled the situation in a high-handed way. = 他们专横地处理了这件事。


现在完成时:
He has consistently been high-handed in his dealings with others. = 他一直以来对别人都是盛气凌人。
The company has been high-handed in its response to customer complaints. = 公司对客户投诉的回应一直都是傲慢无礼的。


过去完成时:
She had been high-handed throughout the negotiations. = 在整个谈判过程中，她一直都是盛气凌人的。
They had acted high-handed before realizing the consequences. = 他们在意识到后果之前一直表现得很专横。


将来时:
He will likely be high-handed in his approach to the problem. = 他很可能会以专横的态度处理这个问题。
The government will probably be high-handed in enforcing the new regulations. = 政府很可能会专横地执行新的规章制度。


虚拟语气:
If he were not so high-handed, people would respect him more. = 如果他不那么专横，人们会更尊重他。
I wish she wouldn't be so high-handed with her staff. = 我希望她不要再对她的员工那么专横了。


被动语态:
Their concerns were dismissed in a high-handed manner. = 他们的担忧被傲慢地驳回了。
The situation was handled in a high-handed way by the authorities. = 当局以专横的方式处理了这种情况。


疑问句:
Is he always so high-handed? = 他总是这么专横吗？
Was her response high-handed? = 她的回应是专横的吗？


否定句:
He was not high-handed in his approach. = 他处理事情的方式并不专横。
She did not act in a high-handed manner. = 她没有以专横的态度行事。
