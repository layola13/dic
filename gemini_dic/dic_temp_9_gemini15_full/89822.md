hornel:/ˈhɔːrnəl/| n. |号角；角笛；（尤指旧时）号角手 |A hornel is a horn, typically a large one used for signaling or in hunting.  It can also refer to a person who blows a horn, especially historically. |The huntsman blew his hornel to signal the start of the chase.  = 猎人在号角上吹响了号角，标志着追捕的开始。The old paintings depicted several hornels announcing the king's arrival. =  古老的画作描绘了几个吹号角的人宣布国王的到来。|  horn, trumpet |  (no direct antonym)
