compotes:/kɒmˈpoʊts/ | n. |果酱，蜜饯|A compote is a sweet dessert made from fruit that has been cooked in a syrup or other sweet liquid.  It is often served warm or cold and can be made with a variety of fruits, such as apples, pears, peaches, or berries.  |Example: The restaurant's menu featured a delicious plum compote served over vanilla ice cream. 餐厅的菜单上有道美味的李子蜜饯配香草冰淇淋。|近义词：preserve, fruit sauce, conserve |反义词：None
