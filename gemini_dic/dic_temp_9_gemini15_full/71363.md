flag-waving: /ˈflæɡˌweɪvɪŋ/ | n. |挥舞旗帜；爱国主义的炫耀；盲目支持 | The act of waving a flag, often to show support for a cause, country, or team.  It can also refer to overly enthusiastic or uncritical support. |例句：The flag-waving at the rally was intense.  集会上的挥舞旗帜非常激烈。

The excessive flag-waving during the election campaign seemed insincere. 选举期间过度的挥舞旗帜显得不真诚。|近义词：patriotism, jingoism, chauvinism |反义词：apathy, indifference, dissent |名词
