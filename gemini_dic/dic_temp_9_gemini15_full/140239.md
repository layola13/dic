offload:/ˈɒfˌləʊd/ /ˈɔːfˌloʊd/| vt. n.|卸载，卸货，转移负担|To remove or unload (a cargo, etc.) from a vehicle or vessel; to transfer (a task, responsibility, etc.) to someone else.|例句：The workers offloaded the containers from the ship. 工人们从船上卸下了集装箱。例句：She offloaded her worries onto her friend. 她向朋友倾诉了她的烦恼。|近义词：unload, transfer, delegate, shift|反义词：load, burden, shoulder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They offload the goods every morning. = 他们每天早上卸货。
The company offloads its data to the cloud. = 公司将数据卸载到云端。

一般过去时:
He offloaded the heavy boxes onto the trolley. = 他把沉重的箱子卸到手推车上。
We offloaded the responsibility to the new manager. = 我们把责任转移给了新经理。

现在完成时:
They have offloaded all the cargo. = 他们已经卸完了所有的货物。
She has offloaded her emotional burden to her therapist. = 她已经向她的治疗师倾诉了她的情感负担。

过去完成时:
The truck had offloaded its contents before the accident. = 卡车在事故发生前已经卸下了货物。
He had offloaded his work to his assistant before leaving for vacation. = 他在休假前已将工作转移给了他的助手。

将来时:
They will offload the containers tomorrow morning. = 他们明天早上将卸下集装箱。
The team will offload the project to a subcontractor. = 该团队将把项目外包给分包商。

虚拟语气:
If we offloaded some tasks, we could finish the project earlier. = 如果我们卸掉一些任务，我们可以早点完成项目。
I wish I could offload this stress to someone else. = 我希望我能把这个压力转移给别人。

被动语态:
The goods were offloaded quickly and efficiently. = 货物快速有效地被卸下。
The responsibility was offloaded to a more experienced team member. = 责任被转移给了更有经验的团队成员。

疑问句:

Have you offloaded the data yet? = 你已经卸载数据了吗？

否定句:
They didn't offload the packages on time. = 他们没有按时卸下包裹。
We couldn't offload the extra work because of the deadline. = 由于最后期限的缘故，我们无法转移额外的工作。
