reprovisioned:/ˌriːprəˈvɪʒənd/ | v. | 重新供应；再次提供；重新配置 | To supply or provide something again; to reconfigure or reallocate resources.  | The server was reprovisioned after the outage.  服务器在中断后重新配置了。 |  replenished, resupplied, reconfigured | depleted, exhausted | 动词
