globalise:/ˈɡləʊbəlaɪz/ | /ˈɡloʊbəlaɪz/ | vt. |全球化；使全球化 |To make something global in scope or application; to integrate the world's economies and societies through trade, technology, and information exchange.  |Example: The company is globalising its operations to reach a wider market.  这家公司正在将其业务全球化，以覆盖更广阔的市场。 The internet has globalised communication.  互联网使沟通全球化了。|近义词：internationalize, universalize |反义词：localize, regionalize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company globalises its marketing strategy. = 这家公司将其营销策略全球化。
We globalise our products to reach a wider audience. = 我们将产品全球化以接触更广泛的受众。


一般过去时:
The firm globalised its operations last year. = 该公司去年将其业务全球化了。
