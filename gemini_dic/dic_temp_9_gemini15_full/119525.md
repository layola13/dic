microscale:/ˈmaɪkroʊskeɪl/| adj.|微观的，微尺度的|Relating to or denoting processes or structures on a scale of a millionth of a metre or less.  Microscale processes are typically studied using microscopes or other specialized tools to visualize and analyze phenomena at this small scale.|例句：The experiment was conducted at a microscale to conserve resources.  该实验在微观尺度上进行以节省资源。

例句：Microscale techniques are used in chemistry to synthesize new materials. 微观技术用于化学合成新材料。


近义词：minute, tiny, submicroscopic|反义词：macroscopic, large-scale|词性：adj.


词源：来自micro- (表示“小的”) + scale (表示“尺度”)。  “micro-”源自希腊语 mikrós，意思是“小的”。
