recommissions:/ˌriːkəˈmɪʃən/| vt.|重新委托；再次启用|To commission again; to put back into service.|The navy recommissioned the historic battleship.  海军重新启用这艘历史悠久的战列舰。|The museum recommissioned the artist to create a new sculpture. 博物馆再次委托这位艺术家创作一件新的雕塑。| decommission, retire | commission, activate | v.


USAGE->

The company recommissions its software annually. = 公司每年重新启用其软件。
