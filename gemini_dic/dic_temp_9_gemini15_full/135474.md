nonpharmaceutic: /ˌnɒnˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪk/ | adj. | 非药物的；非药学的 | Not related to or involving pharmaceuticals or drugs.  These methods are designed to prevent illness and injury without the use of medication or drugs.  | 例句：The study focused on nonpharmaceutic interventions for chronic pain.  这项研究侧重于慢性疼痛的非药物干预措施。  | 例句：Public health campaigns promote nonpharmaceutic approaches to managing stress. 公共卫生运动推广管理压力的非药物方法。 | 近义词：non-medical, drug-free, alternative | 反义词：pharmaceutic, medicinal
