green-lighting: /ˈɡriːnˌlaɪtɪŋ/ | v. |批准，通过，给予绿灯 | To give approval or authorization for a project, plan, or idea to proceed.  It originates from the traffic signal "green light," signifying "go." | 例句：The board green-lighted the new marketing campaign. 董事会批准了新的营销活动。

The studio green-lighted the film after reviewing the script.  制片厂在审查剧本后批准了这部电影。


近义词：approve, authorize, sanction, okay, permit

反义词：reject, veto, deny, disapprove, prohibit
