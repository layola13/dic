chorusing:/ˈkɔːrəsɪŋ/| v. |齐声歌唱；合唱；异口同声地说|Singing or speaking in unison or chorus.  It implies a group action with unified sound.|例句：The children were chorusing a Christmas carol.孩子们正在齐声唱圣诞颂歌。
The crowd chorused their approval.人群异口同声地表示赞同。|近义词：singing together, chanting, unison, in chorus |反义词：soloing, individual voices
