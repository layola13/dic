consignors:/kənˈsaɪnərz/ | n. | 托运人，寄货人 | A consignor is a person or entity that sends goods to another party (the consignee) for sale or other purposes.  They typically retain ownership of the goods until they are sold. | The consignors shipped their artwork to the gallery for an upcoming exhibition.  托运人将他们的艺术品运送到画廊用于即将举行的展览。| shipper, sender, vendor | receiver, consignee | 名词
