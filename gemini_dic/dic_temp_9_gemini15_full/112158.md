look-ins:/ˈlʊkɪnz/| n.|窥视；短暂的拜访；察看|A look-in is a brief visit or a quick glance.  It can also refer to an opportunity to participate or be included.|例句：I only got a quick look-in at the party before I had to leave. 我只在派对上短暂地看了一眼就不得不离开了。
例句：  She's hoping for a look-in at the competition. 她希望有机会参加比赛。|近义词：glimpse, peek, visit, chance|反义词：exclusion, denial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often gets look-ins at his friend's house. = 他经常去朋友家短暂拜访。
They always give us a look-in at their meetings. = 他们总是让我们参与他们的会议。


一般过去时:
I had a look-in at the new shop yesterday. = 我昨天去新店看了一眼。
They gave me a look-in on the project last week. = 他们上周让我参与了这个项目。


现在完成时:
She has had several look-ins at the museum recently. = 她最近几次去过博物馆。
We haven't had a look-in at the new software yet. = 我们还没来得及看一看新的软件。


过去完成时:
He had already had a look-in at the report before the meeting. = 会议前他已经看过报告了。
They had never had a look-in at such a grand event before. = 他们以前从未参加过如此盛大的活动。


将来时:
I will have a look-in at the library tomorrow. = 我明天要去图书馆看看。
We will give you a look-in on the next project. = 我们将在下一个项目中给你一个机会。


虚拟语气:
If I had a look-in at the documents, I might understand better. = 如果我看一下文件，我可能会更好地理解。
I wish I had a look-in at the exhibition before it closed. = 我真希望展览结束前我能去看一下。


被动语态:
The new employees were given a look-in at the company's strategy. = 新员工们了解了公司的战略。
A look-in at the problem was provided by the expert. = 专家对这个问题进行了考察。


疑问句:

Will we have a look-in at the final decision? = 我们能参与最终决定吗？


否定句:
I didn't get a look-in at the meeting. = 我没有参加会议。
They didn't give us a look-in at the project. = 他们没有让我们参与这个项目。


PHRASE->
