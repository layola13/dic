quickset:/ˈkwɪkˌsɛt/| n. |快速生长的树篱；枸骨；速生树篱 |A quickset is a fence or hedge made of living plants, especially hawthorn, that grow quickly and densely.  It can also refer to the plants themselves. |例句：The farmer planted a quickset hedge around his field. 农民在他的田地周围种了一排速生树篱。
例句：The quickset grew rapidly, forming a dense barrier. 速生树篱生长迅速，形成一道致密的屏障。|近义词：hedge, fence, thicket |反义词：slowly growing plants

词源:

The word "quickset" is a compound word.  "Quick" refers to its rapid growth, and "set" refers to the act of planting or establishing something.  It ultimately derives from the Old English "cwic" (alive, living) and "settan" (to set, to plant).
