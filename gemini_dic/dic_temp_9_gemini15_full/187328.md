rockfish:/ˈrɑːkfɪʃ/| n. |岩鱼，石鱼|A rockfish is any of numerous species of marine fish of the family Scorpaenidae, typically living on or near the seabed. They are often characterized by their spiny fins and sometimes venomous spines.|例句：Many types of rockfish are popular seafood. 许多种类的岩鱼都是受欢迎的海鲜。|例句：The diver carefully avoided the venomous spines of the rockfish. 潜水员小心翼翼地避开了岩鱼有毒的刺。|近义词：sea bass, scorpionfish|反义词：无
