immunizing:/ˈɪmjunaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使免疫；使免于；预防接种 | The process of making someone or something immune to a disease or harmful substance, typically by vaccination. |  例句：The doctor is immunizing the children against measles. 医生正在给孩子们接种麻疹疫苗。

例句：Regular handwashing helps immunize against many common illnesses.  经常洗手有助于预防许多常见疾病。


近义词：vaccinating, inoculating, protecting


反义词：infecting, exposing, endangering


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is immunizing the population against the flu. = 政府正在对人口进行流感免疫接种。
They immunize their dogs annually against rabies. = 他们每年都给他们的狗接种狂犬病疫苗。


一般过去时:
The nurse immunized the baby against polio. = 护士给婴儿接种了小儿麻痹症疫苗。
Scientists immunized mice to test the new vaccine. = 科学家给老鼠接种疫苗以测试新疫苗。


现在完成时:
She has immunized her children against several diseases. = 她已经为她的孩子们接种了多种疾病的疫苗。
They have immunized their livestock against foot-and-mouth disease. = 他们已经为他们的牲畜接种了口蹄疫疫苗。


过去完成时:
The doctor had immunized the patient before the surgery. = 医生在手术前已经为病人接种了疫苗。
The researchers had immunized the monkeys with a weakened virus. = 研究人员已经用减毒病毒为猴子接种了疫苗。


将来时:
The clinic will immunize all the school children next week. = 诊所下周将为所有学童接种疫苗。
They will immunize the entire herd against brucellosis. = 他们将为整个兽群接种布鲁氏菌病疫苗。


虚拟语气:
If they had immunized the population earlier, fewer people would have gotten sick. = 如果他们早些为人口接种疫苗，生病的人就会少一些。
I wish I had immunized myself against the flu. = 我真希望我给自己接种了流感疫苗。


被动语态:
The children were immunized against diphtheria. = 孩子们接种了白喉疫苗。
The population is being immunized against the new virus. = 人口正在接种针对新型病毒的疫苗。


疑问句:
Has the dog been immunized against parvovirus? = 这只狗接种过细小病毒疫苗了吗？
Were the employees immunized before starting work? = 员工在开始工作前接种过疫苗吗？


否定句:
The farmer didn't immunize his cattle this year. = 农民今年没有为他的牛接种疫苗。
They hadn't immunized the horses before the outbreak. = 疫情爆发前他们没有为马接种疫苗。


PHRASE->
immunize against = 预防…
immunize from = 使免于…
immunize oneself = 自己接种疫苗
be immunized to = 对…免疫
immunizing agent = 免疫剂

词源:
免疫（immune） + 化（-izing）  来源于拉丁语 *immunis，意思是“免除的”， “豁免的”。  “-izing”是动词后缀，表示“使…成为”。
