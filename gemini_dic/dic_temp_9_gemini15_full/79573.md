gloomiest:/ˈɡluːmiəst/| adj.|最阴暗的，最忧郁的，最沮丧的 | Most dark and depressing; feeling or causing a feeling of great sadness.  |例句：The gloomiest day of the year was when the news broke.  今年最阴暗的一天是新闻发布的那一天。
The gloomiest prediction for the economy is a deep recession. 对经济最悲观的预测是大萧条。

近义词：darkest, dreariest, most depressing, most dismal, most somber
反义词：brightest, sunniest, cheerfullest, happiest
