grounder:/ˈɡraʊndər/| n. |击地球手，落地球|A grounder is a batted ball in baseball that hits the ground before reaching a fielder.  It is also used to describe a player who hits many ground balls.|例句：The batter hit a hard grounder to shortstop.  击球手击出一个强劲的落地球给游击手。

The shortstop fielded a grounder and threw to first for the out. 游击手接住一个落地球，然后传到一垒造成出局。|近义词：ground ball|反义词：fly ball|词性:名词
