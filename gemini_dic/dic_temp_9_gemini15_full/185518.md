revaccinates:/ˌriːˈvæksɪneɪts/| v.|再次接种疫苗；重新接种疫苗|To vaccinate again; to give a further dose of vaccine.|例句：The doctor revaccinated the child against measles. 医生再次给孩子接种了麻疹疫苗。

例句：Public health officials are urging people to revaccinate against the flu. 公共卫生官员敦促人们再次接种流感疫苗。


近义词：reimmunize, reinoculate


反义词：deimmunize (无确切的反义词，因为“取消疫苗接种”的表达方式不同)


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revaccinates his patients annually. = 他每年都为他的病人接种疫苗。
She revaccinates her dog against rabies. = 她每年都为她的狗接种狂犬病疫苗。


一般过去时:
The clinic revaccinated hundreds of people last week. = 上周诊所为数百人接种了疫苗。
They revaccinated the entire community after the outbreak. = 爆发后，他们为整个社区进行了重新接种疫苗。


现在完成时:
The hospital has revaccinated all its staff. = 医院已经为所有员工重新接种了疫苗。
She has revaccinated her child twice this year. = 她今年已经为她的孩子接种了两次疫苗。


过去完成时:
They had revaccinated the troops before deploying them. = 部署部队之前，他们已经为部队进行了重新接种疫苗。
The doctor had revaccinated the patient before the surgery. = 医生在手术前已经为病人进行了重新接种疫苗。


将来时:
The government will revaccinate the entire population next year. = 政府明年将为全国人口接种疫苗。
They will revaccinate all the children in the school. = 他们将为学校里所有的孩子接种疫苗。


被动语态:
The children were revaccinated against polio. = 孩子们接种了脊髓灰质炎疫苗。
The population will be revaccinated next spring. = 人口将在明年春天再次接种疫苗。


疑问句:
Will they revaccinate the entire city? = 他们会为整个城市接种疫苗吗？
Has she been revaccinated recently? = 她最近接种过疫苗了吗？


否定句:
They did not revaccinate the patient because of an allergic reaction. = 由于过敏反应，他们没有为病人接种疫苗。
The program failed to revaccinate enough people. = 该计划未能为足够多的人接种疫苗。


PHRASE->

revaccinate with = 使用…疫苗接种
