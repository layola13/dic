drawboy:/ˈdrɔːbɔɪ/| n. |绘图男孩；（旧时）剧院勤杂工|A drawboy was a young boy employed in a theatre to perform various tasks, often including pulling curtains and shifting scenery.|In the old theatres, the drawboy was responsible for pulling the curtains.  在旧剧院里，绘图男孩负责拉窗帘。|The young boy worked as a drawboy in the local theatre.  这个小男孩在当地剧院做绘图男孩。|stagehand, assistant| (no direct antonym)|名词
