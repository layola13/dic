rss:/ˌɑːrɛsˈɛs/ | n. | 网址聚合技术，RSS 订阅 | Rich Site Summary (or Really Simple Syndication) is a web feed that allows users to access updates to online content from different websites in a centralized location. It's a way to subscribe to blogs, news sites, and other online publications and receive their latest content automatically. | Example: I subscribe to several RSS feeds to keep up with the latest news in technology. 我订阅了几个 RSS 订阅源来了解最新的科技新闻。 | 近义词：feed, syndication | 反义词：无
