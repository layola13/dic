pocketing:/ˈpɒkɪtɪŋ/|/ˈpɑːkɪtɪŋ/| vt.| 塞入口袋；暗中侵吞；偷偷地藏匿 |To put something into a pocket or pockets.  It can also refer to secretly taking something for oneself, especially money. |例句：He was pocketing the money without anyone noticing. 他在偷偷地把钱塞进口袋，没有人注意到。

He pocketed the coins and walked away. 他把硬币塞进口袋然后走开了。


近义词：secreting, concealing, appropriating, embezzling |反义词：revealing, disclosing, donating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pockets the change every day. = 他每天都把零钱塞进口袋。
She pockets her phone before entering the meeting. = 她在进入会议前把手机塞进口袋。


一般过去时:
The thief pocketed the jewels and escaped. = 小偷把珠宝塞进口袋然后逃跑了。
He pocketed the letter without reading it. = 他把信塞进口袋没有读。


现在完成时:
They have pocketed all the profits. = 他们已经把所有的利润都中饱私囊了。
She has pocketed the evidence.  = 她已经偷偷藏匿了证据。


过去完成时:
He had pocketed the money before the audit. = 审计之前他已经把钱偷偷藏起来了。
She had pocketed the key and forgotten where she put it. = 她已经把钥匙塞进口袋了，却忘了放在哪里。


将来时:
He will pocket the winnings after the race. = 比赛结束后他会把奖金收起来。
She will pocket the report and deal with it later. = 她会把报告收起来，稍后再处理。


虚拟语气:
If he pocketed the money, he would be in trouble. = 如果他中饱私囊，他会惹上麻烦。
I wish I hadn’t pocketed that cookie without asking. = 我真希望我没有不问自取地拿了那块饼干。


被动语态:
The money was pocketed by the dishonest employee. = 钱被那个不诚实的员工偷偷藏起来了。
The evidence was quickly pocketed and hidden. = 证据很快就被偷偷藏了起来。


疑问句:
Did he pocket the money? = 他把钱塞进口袋了吗？
Where did she pocket the key? = 她把钥匙塞到哪个口袋里了？


否定句:
He didn’t pocket the money; he left it on the table. = 他没有把钱塞进口袋；他把它放在桌上了。
She didn't pocket the evidence; she handed it to the police. = 她没有偷偷藏匿证据；她把它交给了警察。
