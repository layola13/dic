illnesses:/ˈɪlnɪsɪz/| n. |疾病，疾病（复数）|The plural form of "illness," referring to multiple instances of sickness or disease.  It encompasses a wide range of conditions, from minor ailments to serious health problems. |Examples:  Many illnesses are preventable through vaccination.  Many illnesses are caused by viruses. |近义词：diseases, ailments, sicknesses |反义词：health, wellness
