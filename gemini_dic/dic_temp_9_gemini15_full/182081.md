remigration:/ˌriːmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/| n. |重新移民；回迁 |The act of migrating back to one's native country or region after having lived elsewhere.  It often implies a return after a period of emigration.  |Example:  The remigration of skilled workers to their home countries is a growing concern for many nations.  重新移民到自己祖国或地区的熟练工人日益成为许多国家的担忧。|近义词：repatriation, return migration |反义词：emigration, immigration |名词
