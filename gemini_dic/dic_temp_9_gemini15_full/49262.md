deli:/ˈdɛli/ | n. |熟食店，熟食柜台 |A deli is a shop or counter that sells prepared foods, such as sandwiches, salads, and cheeses.  | Example: I grabbed a quick lunch from the deli downtown. 我在市中心的熟食店匆匆吃了个午饭。 |近义词：delicatessen, food shop |反义词：无
