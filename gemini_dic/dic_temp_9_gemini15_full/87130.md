herbarist:/ˈhɜːrbərɪst/ | n. |植物学家；药草学家 | A person who studies, collects, or cultivates herbs, especially for medicinal purposes.  | The herbarist identified several rare medicinal plants during the expedition.  这位药草学家在探险中鉴定了几种稀有的药用植物。 | botanist, herbalist |  (无明确反义词，可考虑non-specialist) | 名词

The herbarist identifies various plant species. = 植物学家鉴定各种植物物种。
