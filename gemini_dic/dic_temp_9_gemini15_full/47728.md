decaffeination:/ˌdiːkæfiˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 脱咖啡因；除咖啡因 | The process of removing caffeine from coffee beans or tea leaves.  This process can be achieved through various methods, including water processing, solvent extraction, and supercritical carbon dioxide extraction. |例句：The decaffeination process can affect the flavor and aroma of coffee.  脱咖啡因过程会影响咖啡的风味和香气。

The decaffeination of coffee is a common practice. 咖啡的脱咖啡因是一种常见的做法。
