photonic:/foʊˈtɑːnɪk/|/ˈfoʊtɒnɪk/| adj.|光子的；光学的|Relating to or employing photons.  Photonic technologies utilize light for information processing and transmission.|Example: Photonic integrated circuits are becoming increasingly important in high-speed data communication. 光子集成电路在高速数据通信中变得越来越重要。|近义词：optical|反义词：electronic


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb or noun forms that follow typical tense conjugations.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.  Photonic is used to describe things, not to perform actions.

Example sentences demonstrating adjective usage:

Attributive Adjective:
Photonic devices are revolutionizing data transmission. 光子器件正在彻底改变数据传输方式。
The research focuses on photonic crystals. 这项研究侧重于光子晶体。


Predicative Adjective:
The technology is highly photonic. 这项技术高度依赖光子技术。
The system's components are photonic. 这个系统组件是光子器件。
