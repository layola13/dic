/ˌɪntərˈtɔːkɪŋ/ | adj. | 交谈的；互相谈话的 | Intertalking refers to the act or process of multiple people talking simultaneously or in a way that overlaps.  It can also describe a system or situation where multiple conversations are happening concurrently. |  The intertalking in the crowded marketplace made it difficult to understand any single conversation.  | 近义词：overlapping, simultaneous, concurrent | 反义词：silent, quiet, monologic
