jaunce:/dʒɔːns/| n. v.|颠簸的旅程；漫游|A jaunt is a short journey, usually for pleasure.  It can also refer to the act of making such a journey.|例句：We took a jaunt to the coast last weekend.  我们上周末去海岸边短途旅行了一趟。


例句：The car bounced along on the rough road, making for a bumpy jaunt. 这辆车在崎岖的道路上颠簸前行，造成了颠簸的旅程。


近义词：excursion, trip, outing, ramble|反义词：stationary journey, immobile travel.


词性：名词，动词
