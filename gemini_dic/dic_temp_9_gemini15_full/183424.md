reprove:/rɪˈpruːv/| vt.|责备；谴责；训斥|To express disapproval of; rebuke. To show to be wrong or in error.|例句：The teacher reproved the student for his bad behavior.老师因他的不良行为而责备学生。

例句：He reproved her gently for her carelessness. 他温和地责备她的粗心大意。


近义词：rebuke, reprimand, chide, scold|反义词：praise, commend, approve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reproves his son for lying. = 他因说谎而责备他的儿子。
She reproves her employees for their lateness. = 她因迟到而责备她的员工。

一般过去时:
The judge reproved the lawyer for his unprofessional conduct. = 法官因其不专业的行为而责备律师。
My father reproved me for breaking the vase. = 我父亲因我打破花瓶而责备我。

现在完成时:
He has reproved her several times for the same mistake. = 他已经多次因同样的错误而责备她了。
The teacher has reproved the students for their disruptive behavior. = 老师因他们的扰乱行为而责备了学生们。

过去完成时:
She had reproved him before, but he continued to make the same mistakes. = 她以前就责备过他，但他还是继续犯同样的错误。
The manager had reproved the team for missing the deadline. = 经理因错过最后期限而责备了团队。

将来时:
The principal will reprove the students who are caught cheating. = 校长将责备那些被抓到作弊的学生。
I will reprove him if he continues to be disrespectful. = 如果他继续不尊重人，我会责备他。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reprove him for his rudeness. = 如果我是你，我会因他的粗鲁而责备他。
I wish I had reproved him sooner. = 我希望我早点责备他。

被动语态:
He was reproved for his actions. = 他因他的行为受到了责备。
The students were reproved for their lack of discipline. = 学生们因缺乏纪律而受到责备。

疑问句:
Will you reprove him for his mistake? = 你会因他的错误而责备他吗？
Did she reprove the child for his tantrum? = 她因孩子的发脾气而责备孩子了吗？

否定句:
The teacher did not reprove the student for his honest mistake. = 老师没有因学生的诚实错误而责备他。
He didn't reprove her, but he was clearly displeased. = 他没有责备她，但他显然很不高兴。


PHRASE->
reprove sharply = 严厉地责备
reprove gently = 温和地责备
reprove someone for doing something = 因做了某事而责备某人
reprove with a frown = 皱眉责备
reprove severely = 严厉地责备


词源:
reprove 源自古法语 reprover，来自拉丁语 reprobare，意思是“证明…是错误的；拒绝；谴责”。  re- 表示“再次”或“反”，probare 表示“测试；证明”。  因此，reprove 的字面意思是“再次证明…是错误的”，引申为“责备；谴责”。
