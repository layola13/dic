slept:/slept/| v.|睡觉(sleep的过去式和过去分词)|Slept is the past tense and past participle of the verb "sleep," meaning to be in a state of rest characterized by unconsciousness.|I slept well last night. 我昨晚睡得很香。|She slept through the entire movie. 她睡着了，错过了整部电影。|  sleep, slumber, doze|awake, wake, be awake|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I sleep eight hours a day. = 我每天睡八个小时。
He sleeps soundly every night. = 他每晚睡得很香。


一般过去时:
I slept for ten hours yesterday. = 我昨天睡了十个小时。
They slept in late this morning. = 他们今天早上睡得很晚。


现在完成时:
I have slept better since I started exercising. =自从我开始锻炼以来，我睡得更好了。
She has slept only a few hours. = 她只睡了几个小时。


过去完成时:
I had slept poorly for weeks before I saw the doctor. =在我去看医生之前，我已经连续几周睡得很差了。
They had slept through the alarm. =他们错过了闹钟。


将来时:
I will sleep in tomorrow. = 我明天要睡懒觉。
We will sleep under the stars tonight. = 我们今晚将在星空下睡觉。


虚拟语气:
If I had slept more, I wouldn't be so tired. =如果我睡得多一点，我就不会这么累了。
I wish I had slept better last night. =我希望我昨晚睡得更好。


被动语态: (无被动语态)  The verb "sleep" is generally not used in the passive voice.


疑问句:
Did you sleep well? = 你睡得好吗？
How long did she sleep? = 她睡了多久？


否定句:
I didn't sleep last night. = 我昨晚没睡。
He didn't sleep a wink. = 他一点觉也没睡。


PHRASE->
sleep tight = 睡个好觉
sleep soundly = 睡得很香
sleep in = 睡懒觉
go to sleep = 入睡
fall asleep = 睡着
