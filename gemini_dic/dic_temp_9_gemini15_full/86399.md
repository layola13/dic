hedonic:/hɪˈdɒnɪk/ | /hɪˈdɑːnɪk/ | adj. |快乐的；享乐主义的 | Relating to or characterized by pleasure or gratification, especially of the senses.  Hedonic experiences are those that focus on pleasure and enjoyment.  The term often implies a focus on immediate gratification rather than long-term consequences. | 例句：The hedonic pursuit of pleasure can sometimes lead to negative consequences.享乐的追求有时会导致负面后果。

The resort offers a truly hedonic experience, with luxurious amenities and stunning views. 这个度假村提供真正享乐的体验，拥有奢华的设施和令人惊叹的景色。


近义词：pleasurable, delightful, sensual, luxurious

反义词：ascetic, austere, unpleasant, painful

词性:形容词
