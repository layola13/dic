glob:/ɡlɒb/| n. |球状物；团；大量；整体|A glob is a small, round mass of a viscous substance, such as mud or paint.  It can also refer to a large quantity or amount of something.|例句：A glob of mud splattered on his trousers. 一团泥巴溅到了他的裤子上。|例句：He ate a glob of ice cream. 他吃了一大块冰淇淋。|近义词：lump, mass, blob |反义词：fragment, piece
