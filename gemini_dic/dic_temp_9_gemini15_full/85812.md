hazed:/heɪzd/| adj.| 模糊的；朦胧的；烟雾弥漫的；困惑的 | Hazed describes something obscured by haze, smoke, or fog, or a mental state of confusion or bewilderment.  It can also refer to someone who is under the influence of drugs. |例句：The mountains were hazed in the distance.  远处的山峦被雾气笼罩着。The student felt hazed after the difficult exam.  考试后学生感到困惑。|近义词：foggy, misty, blurry, confused, bewildered |反义词：clear, sharp, lucid, focused


USAGE->

The air is hazed with pollution. = 空气中弥漫着污染物。
The view is hazed by the fog. =  景色被雾气遮挡得模糊不清。

He felt hazed after the long drive. = 长途驾驶后他感到头昏脑胀。
