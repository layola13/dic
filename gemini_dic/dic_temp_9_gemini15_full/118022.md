memorys:/ˈmeməriːz/| n.|记忆，回忆|The plural form of "memory," referring to multiple instances of remembering or things remembered.  It often refers to cherished or significant past events or experiences. |例句：My memorys of childhood are filled with sunshine and laughter. 我的童年记忆充满了阳光和欢笑。

例句：She shared her happy memorys of her time in France. 她分享了她法国时光的美好回忆。


近义词：recollections, reminiscences, recollections
反义词：forgetfulness, amnesia


词性:名词
