deprecator:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪtər/| n. |贬低者；反对者；批评者|A deprecator is someone who expresses disapproval of something, often in a mild or indirect way. They may criticize, belittle, or express regret about a particular action, idea, or situation.  |例句：The deprecator of the new policy voiced their concerns in a letter to the editor.  贬低这项新政策的人在给编辑的信中表达了他们的担忧。|近义词：criticizer, disapprover, objector|反义词：supporter,approver,advocate|名词
