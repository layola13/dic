incubation:/ˌɪŋkjuˈbeɪʃən/ | n. |孵化；潜伏期；酝酿 | The process of keeping eggs warm so that they hatch, or the period of time this takes;  a period of time when something is developing or being planned. |例句：The incubation period for chicken eggs is around 21 days.  鸡卵的孵化期大约是21天。

例句：The incubation period for the flu is typically 1-4 days. 流感的潜伏期通常为1-4天。


近义词：hatching, brooding, development, gestation | 反义词：hatching (in the sense of the completion of incubation)


词源：From Latin *incubāre "to lie upon, brood over," from *in- "in, on" + *cubāre "to lie."  The original meaning referred to the act of birds lying on their eggs to keep them warm.  The extended meaning of "development" or "planning" came later.
