moorings:/ˈmɔːrɪŋz/| n.|系泊设备；系泊处；停泊处|The fixed points, such as anchors or buoys, to which a vessel is moored.  Also refers to the ropes, chains, etc., used for mooring.|例句：The boat was secure in its moorings. 这艘船安全地系泊在它的系泊处。

例句：The yacht swung gently at its moorings.  游艇轻轻地摇晃着，系在它的系泊处。


近义词：anchorages, berths, dockings|反义词：None specifically, but implies the opposite of being adrift or unmoored.|词性:名词
