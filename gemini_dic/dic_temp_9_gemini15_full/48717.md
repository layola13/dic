defied:/dɪˈfaɪd/| v.|反抗，违抗，蔑视|To openly resist or refuse to obey.  It implies a bold challenge to authority or convention.|例句：The rebels defied the government's orders. 反叛者无视政府的命令。

例句：She defied all expectations and won the race. 她战胜了所有人的预期，赢得了比赛。


近义词：disobeyed, resisted, challenged, flouted, contemned|反义词：obeyed, complied with, conformed to|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defies authority. = 他反抗权威。
They defy the odds. = 他们挑战命运。


一般过去时:
She defied her parents' wishes. = 她违抗了父母的意愿。
The soldiers defied the enemy's attack. = 士兵们抵抗了敌人的进攻。


现在完成时:
He has defied expectations throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都超出了预期。
They have defied the law for years. = 他们多年来一直在违反法律。


过去完成时:
She had defied her doctor's orders for weeks. = 她几周来一直无视医生的嘱咐。
The country had defied international sanctions. = 这个国家多年来一直无视国际制裁。


将来时:
They will defy the prediction. = 他们将挑战预测。
He will defy all attempts to silence him. = 他将抵抗所有让他沉默的企图。


虚拟语气:
If I dared, I would defy the system. = 如果我敢，我会反抗这个制度。
I wish I could defy gravity. = 我希望我能克服地心引力。


被动语态:
The law was defied by many. = 这项法律被许多人所违抗。
The order was defied. = 命令被违抗了。


疑问句:

否定句:
