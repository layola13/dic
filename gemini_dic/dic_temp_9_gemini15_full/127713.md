neglectingly:/nɪˈɡlektɪŋli/ | adv. | 疏忽地；粗心大意地 | In a manner characterized by neglect or carelessness.  It implies a lack of attention or care that results in something being overlooked or improperly handled. | 例句：He neglectingly left the door unlocked. 他粗心地没锁门。

例句：She neglectingly forgot to water the plants, causing them to wilt. 她粗心大意地忘了浇花，导致花都枯萎了。


近义词：carelessly, thoughtlessly, heedlessly, rashly

反义词：carefully, attentively, diligently, meticulously

词性：副词
