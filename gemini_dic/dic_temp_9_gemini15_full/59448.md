earthy:/ˈɜːrθi/| adj.|泥土的；朴实的；粗俗的；现实的|Having the qualities or appearance of earth; unrefined; coarse; down-to-earth; realistic. |例句：The pottery has an earthy smell. 这陶器有一种泥土的气味。

例句：Her paintings have an earthy quality, reflecting the rural landscape. 她的画作有一种泥土气息，反映了农村的景观。


近义词：grounded, natural, rustic, coarse, unrefined

反义词：refined, sophisticated, polished, artificial

Her style has become more earthy and less polished over time. = 她的风格随着时间的推移变得更加质朴，不那么精致了。

虚拟语气:
