cantillate:/ˈkæn.tɪ.leɪt/| vt.|吟唱，单调地诵读|To chant or sing in a monotonous tone, often in a religious context.  The term often implies a slow, rhythmical, and somewhat melodic recitation, distinct from simply speaking or reading aloud.|例句：The monks cantillated the ancient prayers for hours.  僧侣们吟唱古老的祈祷文长达数小时。

The choir cantillated the Gregorian chant with beautiful precision. 合唱团以优美的精准度吟唱格里高利圣咏。|近义词：chant, intone, recite, drone|反义词：shout, yell, bellow|动词
