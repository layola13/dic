ooephyte:  /oʊˈɛfaɪt/ | n. |  卵生植物 |  A plant that produces its seeds in an ovule that is not enclosed within an ovary;  a plant that bears its seeds uncovered. This is in contrast to angiosperms (flowering plants) which have their seeds enclosed within an ovary.  | Example:  Ferns are examples of ooephytes.  蕨类植物是卵生植物的例子。 | 近义词:gymnosperm (more commonly used)| 反义词:angiosperm
