congress:/ˈkɒŋɡres/ /ˈkɑːŋɡrəs/| n.|国会，代表大会|A formal meeting of representatives or delegates, typically one with a specific purpose.  It can refer to a legislative body, like the United States Congress, or a gathering for a specific purpose, like a medical congress.|例句：The US Congress passed a new law. 美国国会通过了一项新法律。|例句：The medical congress attracted experts from around the world.  医学大会吸引了来自世界各地的专家。|近义词：assembly, convention, parliament, legislature|反义词：none specifically, but antonyms might relate to the opposite of the action taken by a congress (e.g., if a congress approves something, the antonym would relate to disapproval or rejection).|名词
