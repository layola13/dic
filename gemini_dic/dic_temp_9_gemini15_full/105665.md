It's not a widely used word and lacks a strong established meaning. The usage often depends heavily on context.|例句：The little boy, a veritable kingling in his own eyes, demanded the largest slice of cake.  这个小男孩，在他自己眼里是个名副其实的小国王，要求最大的那块蛋糕。|近义词：princeling, little one, youngster, imp|反义词：monarch, adult, giant
