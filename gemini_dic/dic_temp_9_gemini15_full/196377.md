sermonised:/ˈsɜːrmənaɪzd/| v.|说教；教训；冗长地训斥|To deliver a sermon or preach; to lecture someone in a long, tedious, and often moralistic way.|例句：The priest sermonised on the importance of charity.  牧师就慈善的重要性进行了布道。

例句：He sermonised at length about the dangers of excessive drinking. 他冗长地训斥过度饮酒的危害。


近义词：preached, lectured, harangued, admonished

反义词：praised, complimented, encouraged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sermonises every Sunday. 他每周日都布道。
She sermonises to her children about good manners. 她向孩子们讲说良好礼仪的重要性。


一般过去时:
The minister sermonised on the evils of greed. 牧师就贪婪的罪恶进行了布道。
He sermonised his son for breaking the vase. 他因儿子打破花瓶而训斥了他。


现在完成时:
He has sermonised against war for many years. 多年来他一直在反对战争。
She has sermonised to her class about the importance of hard work. 她一直在向她的班级讲授努力工作的重要性。


过去完成时:
He had sermonised on the topic many times before. 他之前就这个话题布道过很多次。
She had sermonised to him about his irresponsible behavior. 她曾就他的不负责任的行为对他进行过训斥。


将来时:
The pastor will sermonise on forgiveness next Sunday. 牧师下星期日将就宽恕进行布道。
He will sermonise to his employees about punctuality. 他将向他的员工讲授准时的重要性。


虚拟语气:
If he sermonised less, people might listen more. 如果他少说教，人们可能会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't sermonise so much. 我希望他不要再说教了。


被动语态:
The congregation was sermonised on the importance of faith. 会众被教导了信仰的重要性。
His behaviour was sermonised against by his teacher. 他的老师谴责了他的行为。


疑问句:
Does he often sermonise? 他经常布道吗？
Has she ever sermonised on this subject before? 她以前是否就这个话题进行过布道？


否定句:
He didn't sermonise at the meeting. 他在会议上没有说教。
She didn't sermonise to the children about their behaviour. 她没有就孩子们的行为对他们进行说教。



PHRASE->
sermonise on a topic  就某个话题说教
sermonise endlessly  没完没了地说教
sermonise against something  反对某事说教
sermonise to someone  对某人说教
sermonise with someone  与某人一起说教
