clack:/klæk/| v. n.|卡嗒声，发出卡嗒声 |To make a short, sharp sound, like the sound of something hard hitting something else.  The sound of rapid clicking.|例句：The old door clacked shut.  旧门卡嗒一声关上了。 The typewriter keys clacked away. 打字机键盘啪啪作响。|近义词：click, snap, rattle|反义词：silence, hush


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine clacks constantly. = 机器不断发出卡嗒声。
The old woman clacks her knitting needles. = 老妇人咔嗒咔嗒地织毛衣。

一般过去时:
The gate clacked shut behind us. = 门在我们身后咔嗒一声关上了。
The hammer clacked against the metal. = 锤子敲击金属发出卡嗒声。

现在完成时:
The machine has clacked all day. = 这台机器整天都在发出卡嗒声。
The birds have clacked their beaks incessantly. = 鸟儿们一直在不停地啄着它们的喙。

过去完成时:
The train had clacked along the tracks for hours. = 火车已经在铁轨上行驶了好几个小时，发出咔嗒咔嗒的声音。
The clock had clacked twelve times before he woke up. = 他醒来之前，钟已经敲响了十二下。

将来时:
The printer will clack as it prints the document. = 打印机打印文件时会发出卡嗒声。
The wheels will clack rhythmically as the cart rolls down the hill. = 手推车下山时，车轮会发出有节奏的卡嗒声。


虚拟语气:
If the machine clacked louder, we'd know something was wrong. = 如果机器发出更大的卡嗒声，我们就知道出问题了。
I wish the clock wouldn't clack so loudly at night. = 我希望钟表晚上不要发出这么大的卡嗒声。

被动语态:
The sound was clacked by the old wood. =  这声音是由老木头发出的（卡嗒声）。
The rhythm was clacked out by the tireless machine. =  这节奏是由不知疲倦的机器敲击出来的（卡嗒声）。

疑问句:
Does the old clock still clack? = 旧钟表还会发出卡嗒声吗？
What is clacking in the attic? = 阁楼里发出什么卡嗒声？


否定句:
The machine doesn't clack anymore. = 机器不再发出卡嗒声了。
The door didn't clack when it closed. = 门关上时没有发出卡嗒声。
