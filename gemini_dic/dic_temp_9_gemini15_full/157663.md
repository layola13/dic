pity:/ˈpɪti/| n.|怜悯，同情；可惜的事|  Feeling sorrow and compassion for someone's suffering; something regrettable. |例句：I felt a pang of pity for the homeless man. 我对那个无家可归的人感到一阵怜悯。

例句：It's a pity that it's raining. 真可惜下雨了。|近义词：compassion, sympathy, commiseration |反义词：joy, happiness, indifference


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pity the poor. = 我可怜穷人。
She pities the animals in the zoo. = 她可怜动物园里的动物。


一般过去时:
I pitied him because of his misfortune. = 我因为他不幸的遭遇而同情他。
They pitied the lost child. = 他们同情那个迷路的孩子。


现在完成时:
I have pitied him since I heard his story. = 从我听到他的故事以来，我一直同情他。
She has pitied the stray cat for weeks. = 她好几个星期都可怜那只流浪猫。


过去完成时:
I had pitied her before I knew the whole truth. = 在我了解全部真相之前，我就已经同情她了。
They had pitied the victims before the rescue operation. = 在救援行动之前，他们就已经同情受害者了。


将来时:
I will pity him if he loses his job. = 如果他丢了工作，我会同情他的。
They will pity her if she fails the exam. = 如果她考试不及格，他们会同情她的。


虚拟语气:
I would pity him more if I knew his background. = 如果我知道他的背景，我会更同情他。
If I were in her shoes, I would pity myself. = 如果我是她，我会自怜的。


被动语态:
The poor are pitied by many people. = 穷人受到许多人的同情。
The victims were pitied by the whole nation. = 受害者受到了全国人民的同情。


疑问句:
Do you pity the suffering? = 你同情受苦的人吗？
Does she pity the homeless? = 她同情无家可归的人吗？


否定句:
I don't pity him; he brought it on himself. = 我不同情他；这是他自己造成的。
She didn't pity the thief; he deserved it. = 她不怜悯那个贼；他罪有应得。


PHRASE->

a pity that = 很可惜
