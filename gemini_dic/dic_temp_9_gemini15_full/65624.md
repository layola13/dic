evangelised:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzd/ | v. | 传教；布道；使皈依 | To spread the Christian gospel; to convert someone to Christianity.  | The missionaries evangelised the local tribes. 传教士向当地部落传教。  The new religious movement attempted to evangelise the youth. 新兴宗教运动试图使年轻人皈依。 | proselytize, preach, convert | persecute, suppress | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He evangelises in the streets every Sunday. 他每个星期天都在街上布道。
She evangelises through her writing and speeches. 她通过写作和演讲传教。


一般过去时:
The early Christians evangelised throughout the Roman Empire. 早期基督徒在整个罗马帝国传教。
He evangelised his beliefs to anyone who would listen. 他向任何愿意听的人宣扬他的信仰。


现在完成时:
They have evangelised in several countries across Asia. 他们已经在亚洲的几个国家传教。
She has evangelised her message of hope to thousands. 她已经向成千上万的人传达了她希望的信息。


过去完成时:
Before the arrival of the missionaries, they had never been evangelised. 在传教士到来之前，他们从未受过传教。
He had evangelised for years before he found his calling. 他传教多年后才找到了自己的使命。


将来时:
They will evangelise in new communities next year. 他们明年将在新的社区传教。
She will evangelise her message of peace across the globe. 她将在全球传播她的和平信息。


虚拟语气:
If I were to evangelise, I would focus on compassion. 如果我要传教，我会专注于慈悲。
I wish I could evangelise more effectively. 我希望我能更有效地传教。


被动语态:
The local population was evangelised by the Spanish conquistadors. 当地居民被西班牙征服者传教。
His ideas were evangelised through the media. 他的思想通过媒体传播。


疑问句:
How long have you been evangelising? 你传教多久了？
Who are you evangelising to? 你向谁传教？


否定句:
They did not evangelise forcefully. 他们没有强行传教。
She does not evangelise without compassion. 她不会没有慈悲地传教。
