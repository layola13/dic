microfilm:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlm/| n. |缩微胶卷，显微胶卷 |A microfilm is a miniaturized photographic film containing a large number of images. It's used to store documents and other information in a compact format. |Example: The library archives are stored on microfilm.  图书馆的档案保存在缩微胶卷上。|近义词：microfiche|反义词：None
