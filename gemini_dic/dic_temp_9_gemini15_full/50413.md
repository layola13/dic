depot's:/ˈdiːpoʊz/| n.|仓库；车站；兵站|A depot is a place where goods are stored before being sent out to shops or customers, or where buses or trains start and finish their journeys.  It can also refer to a military supply base. |例句：The train departed from the central depot. 这列火车从中央车站出发。

例句：The company's main depot is located in the industrial zone.  这家公司的主要仓库位于工业区。


近义词：warehouse, station, terminal,  supply base

反义词：None (no direct antonym)

词性:名词(复数形式，表示depot的所有格)
