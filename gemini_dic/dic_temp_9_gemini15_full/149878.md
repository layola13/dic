palindromic:/ˌpælɪnˈdroʊmɪk/| adj. |回文的，对称的 |Reading the word or phrase backward or forward yields the same sequence of letters.  A palindromic word or phrase reads the same backward as forward. |Example: "Madam" is a palindromic word; "A man, a plan, a canal: Panama" is a palindromic sentence.|近义词：reversible, symmetrical |反义词：asymmetrical, nonsymmetrical
