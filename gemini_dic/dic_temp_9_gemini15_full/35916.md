cocreator:/ˌkoʊkrɪˈeɪtər/| n. |共同创造者，合作创造者 |A cocreator is a person who works together with others to create something.  It implies a collaborative effort where the contributions of each individual are significant and integral to the final product. | Example:  The film's director and the screenwriter were cocreators of the story's compelling narrative.  这部电影的导演和编剧是引人入胜故事情节的共同创造者。|近义词：collaborator, partner, joint creator |反义词：sole creator, single creator |名词
