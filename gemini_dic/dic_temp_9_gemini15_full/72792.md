foister:/ˈfɔɪstər/| vt.|强迫接受；偷偷塞给；欺骗；伪造|To force (something unwanted or unacceptable) on someone; to impose fraudulently or deceptively; to palm off (something spurious or inferior); to forge or counterfeit.|例句：They foisted a worthless painting on the unsuspecting collector.  他们把一幅毫无价值的画强加给毫无戒心的收藏家。

例句：He tried to foist his responsibilities onto his colleagues. 他试图把他的责任推卸给同事。


近义词：impose, palm off, thrust, pass off, counterfeit|反义词：reject, refuse, decline|动词
