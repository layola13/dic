contradicter:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktə(r)/| n. | 反驳者，反对者 | A person who contradicts or opposes.  Someone who habitually disagrees or offers counterarguments. |  He's a known contradicter, always finding fault with everything. 他是个出了名的反对者，总是对任何事都挑毛病。  |近义词：opponent, objector, gainsayer |反义词：supporter, conformist
