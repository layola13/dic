severely:/sɪˈvɪəli/| adj.|严重的；严厉地；严格地 |  Extremely bad or serious.  It implies a high degree of intensity or seriousness, often suggesting harmful consequences. |例句：He was severely injured in the accident. 他在事故中受了重伤。

例句：The judge severely criticized the defendant's actions. 法官严厉批评了被告的行为。


近义词：seriously, intensely, harshly, rigorously, drastically

反义词：mildly, gently, slightly, leniently
