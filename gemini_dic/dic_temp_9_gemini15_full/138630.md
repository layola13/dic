nucleole:/ˈnjuːkliəʊl/ | n. | 核仁 | A small, dense, spherical structure in the nucleus of a cell, composed of RNA and proteins; it is the site of ribosome biogenesis.  | The nucleolus is a crucial organelle involved in the production of ribosomes.  |近义词：nuclear body (less specific) | 反义词：无
