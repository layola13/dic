sarcomas:/sɑːrˈkoʊməz/ | n. | 肉瘤 | Malignant tumors of connective tissue origin, such as muscle, bone, cartilage, or fat.  Sarcomas are relatively rare compared to carcinomas (cancers of epithelial tissue). They can occur in various parts of the body and are often aggressive. | Example:  The doctor confirmed that the lump was a sarcoma and recommended immediate surgery.  医生确认肿块是肉瘤，并建议立即手术。  The patient was diagnosed with a soft tissue sarcoma in his leg. 患者被诊断出腿部患有软组织肉瘤。|近义词：cancer, tumor, malignancy |反义词：benign tumor
