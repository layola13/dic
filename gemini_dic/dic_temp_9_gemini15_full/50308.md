depleting: /dɪˈpliːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人精疲力竭的；耗尽的；使减少的 |  Describes something that causes exhaustion or reduces something to a lower level.  | 例句：The endless meetings were depleting.  =  没完没了的会议令人精疲力竭。The drought is depleting the water supply. = 干旱正在耗尽水资源。| 近义词：exhausting, draining, weakening, reducing | 反义词：invigorating, replenishing, enriching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The long journey is depleting my energy. = 漫长的旅程正在消耗我的精力。
This constant stress is depleting my mental strength. =  持续的压力正在消耗我的精神力量。

一般过去时:
The war severely depleted the country's resources. = 战争严重耗尽了该国的资源。
The heavy rain depleted the soil's nutrients. = 大雨耗尽了土壤的养分。

现在完成时:
Years of overfishing have depleted fish stocks. = 多年的过度捕捞已经耗尽了鱼类资源。
The prolonged drought has depleted the reservoir's water level. = 长时间的干旱已经耗尽了水库的水位。

过去完成时:
They had depleted their savings by the time they finished the project. =  他们在完成项目时已经花光了积蓄。
The company had depleted its funds before launching the new product. = 公司在推出新产品之前已经耗尽了资金。


将来时:
Continued use will deplete the battery. = 持续使用会耗尽电池电量。
Without conservation, the forest will be depleted in a few years. =  如果不进行保护，森林将在几年内被耗尽。

虚拟语气:
If we continue at this rate, we would deplete our resources quickly. = 如果我们继续以这种速度下去，我们将很快耗尽资源。
I wish we hadn't depleted the ozone layer so much. =  我希望我们没有过度消耗臭氧层。

被动语态:
The ozone layer is being depleted by pollutants. = 臭氧层正在被污染物破坏。
The forest is being depleted by illegal logging. = 森林正在被非法伐木破坏。

疑问句:
Is this work depleting your enthusiasm? = 这项工作是否正在消磨你的热情？
Are our resources being depleted too rapidly? = 我们的资源是否正在被过快地消耗？

否定句:
The new policy will not deplete the national budget. = 新政策不会耗尽国家预算。
The improved farming techniques will not deplete the soil’s fertility. = 改进的耕作技术不会耗尽土壤的肥力。
