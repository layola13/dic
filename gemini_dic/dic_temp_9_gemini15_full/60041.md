educational:/ˌedʒuˈkeɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 教育的；有教育意义的 | Relating to education; designed to educate.  | Example: The museum offers many educational programs for children.  博物馆为孩子们提供许多教育项目。 |近义词：instructive, informative, pedagogical | 反义词：uninformative, unenlightening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This program is educational. = 这个节目很有教育意义。
That museum offers educational tours. = 那家博物馆提供教育性的旅游。

一般过去时:
The workshop was very educational. = 这场研讨会非常有教育意义。

现在完成时:
I have found this book to be very educational. = 我发现这本书非常有教育意义。
The course has been incredibly educational for me. = 这门课程对我来说非常有教育意义。

过去完成时:

将来时:
The new curriculum will be more educational. = 新的课程将会更有教育意义。
