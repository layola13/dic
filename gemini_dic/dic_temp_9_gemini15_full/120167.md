mindblowers:/ˈmaɪndˌbloʊərz/| n.|令人震惊的事物；令人难以置信的事物|Extremely surprising or impressive things; things that are so amazing they shock or astound you.  |例句：The magician's performance was a series of mindblowers. 魔术师的表演是一系列令人震惊的表演。

The new technology is a real mindblower. 这项新技术真是令人难以置信。|近义词：astonishers, stunners, marvels, wonders|反义词：disappointments, bores, trivialities|名词
