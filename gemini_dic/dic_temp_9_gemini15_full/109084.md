legations:/lɛˈɡeɪʃənz/| n.|公使馆；使团；代表团 |A legation is a diplomatic mission, typically less formal than an embassy, headed by a minister plenipotentiary rather than an ambassador.  It represents a country's interests in a foreign country. |例句：The American legation in Tangier was a significant presence during the early 20th century. 美国在丹吉尔的公使馆在20世纪初期是一个重要的存在。
The legation worked tirelessly to secure the release of the hostages. 使团不懈努力以确保人质获释。|近义词：embassy, mission, delegation |反义词：N/A
