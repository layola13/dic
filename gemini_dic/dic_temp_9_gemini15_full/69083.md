faunist:/ˈfɔːnɪst/| n. |牧神崇拜者；沉醉于自然的人|A faunist is someone who is devoted to the worship of Faunus, the Roman god of the woods and fields, or someone who exhibits a strong love for nature and the countryside, often with a romanticized or idealized view.  They may be characterized by a fondness for rustic life, wildness, and the natural world.|例句：He was a faunist at heart, preferring the company of trees and animals to that of people. 他内心深处是个牧神崇拜者，更喜欢与树木和动物为伍，而不是与人相处。|近义词：nature lover, pastoralist, rustic|反义词：urbanite, city dweller
