nivose:/ˈnɪvoʊz/| n. |霜月 (法语共和历的月份)|Nivose is the fourth month of the French Republican Calendar, running from December 21 to January 19.  It is named after the Latin word "nivosus," meaning "snowy." The month is associated with snow and winter.  |The French Republican Calendar was used during the French Revolution. Nivose was a month in that calendar. |近义词：December, January (partially) |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to months associated with summer.
