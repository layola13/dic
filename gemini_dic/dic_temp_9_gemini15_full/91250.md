hydroelectricities:/ˌhaɪdroʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɪsətiz/ | n. | 水力发电（复数形式） | The term "hydroelectricities" is the plural form of "hydroelectricity," referring to multiple instances or types of electricity generated from water power.  It is less common than using "hydroelectric power sources" or simply "hydroelectricity" in the plural context. | Example:  Different regions utilize various hydroelectricities based on their unique geographical features and water resources.  不同的地区根据其独特的地理特征和水资源利用各种水力发电。 | 近义词: hydroelectric power sources, water power (plural contexts) | 反义词:  fossil fuel power, nuclear power
