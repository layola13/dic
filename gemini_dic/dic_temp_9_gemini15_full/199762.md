Sirius: /ˈsɪriəs/ | n. | 天狼星；明亮的；严重的 |

英文解析:Sirius is the brightest star in the night sky, located in the constellation Canis Major.  It is also used to describe something bright, shining, or brilliant, and can also mean serious or grave.

例句:

The star Sirius shone brightly in the clear night sky.  天狼星在晴朗的夜空中闪耀。

His expression was serious; he knew the situation was serious.  他的表情严肃；他知道情况很严重。


近义词: (depending on the context) brilliant, bright, shining, intense, severe, grave, critical

反义词: (depending on the context) dim, faint, dull, mild, trivial, insignificant


词源:From Latin *Sīrius, from Greek Σείριος (Seirios), possibly from a word meaning "glowing" or "scorching."  The name is associated with the star's intense brightness and heat.
