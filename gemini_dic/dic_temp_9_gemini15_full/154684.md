personificator:/pɜːrsɒnɪfɪˈkeɪtə(r)/| n. |拟人化者；赋予人格者 | A personificator is someone who personifies things, giving human qualities or characteristics to inanimate objects, animals, or abstract ideas.  They might do this through writing, art, or other creative mediums. | Example: The author was a master personificator, bringing even the most mundane objects to life on the page.  作者是一位精通拟人化的大师，即使是最普通的物体也能在他的笔下栩栩如生。|近义词：animator, embodiment, representative |反义词：dehumanizer, depersonalizer | 名词
