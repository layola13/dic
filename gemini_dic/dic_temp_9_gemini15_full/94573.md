impoverished:/ɪmˈpɑːvərɪʃt/ | adj. | 贫困的；贫乏的；枯竭的 |  Describes a person, group, or area that is very poor and lacks resources. It can also describe something that has been depleted or weakened. |

例句：
The earthquake left many families impoverished.  地震使许多家庭陷入贫困。

The impoverished soil yielded a poor harvest.  贫瘠的土壤收成很差。


近义词：poor, destitute, needy, deprived, bankrupt (if referring to financial state)

反义词：wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous


词源:

From the verb "impoverish," which comes from the Old French word "empovrir," meaning "to make poor."  The prefix "im-" is a variant of "in-," meaning "in" or "into," and "poverish" is derived from the Old French word "povre," meaning "poor."
