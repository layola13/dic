/ˈoʊpɛk/| n. |石油输出国组织|The Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC) is an intergovernmental organization of 13 countries.  These countries produce and export a large percentage of the world's petroleum.  The organization's stated objective is to coordinate and unify the petroleum policies of its member countries and ensure the stabilization of oil markets in order to secure an efficient, economic and regular supply of petroleum to consumers, a steady income to producers, and a fair return on capital for those investing in the petroleum industry.| OPEC's influence on global oil prices is significant. The recent meeting of OPEC+ resulted in a production cut, which sent oil prices soaring.|近义词：oil cartel|反义词：无
