overloading:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊdɪŋ/ | n. |过载，超载 |The act of placing excessive load on something; assigning multiple meanings to a single symbol or name.  |例句：The power grid experienced overloading during the heatwave.  电力网在热浪期间经历了过载。|例句：The function overloading in C++ allows a single function name to perform multiple operations based on input. C++中的函数重载允许单个函数名根据输入执行多个操作。 |近义词：overburden, overtax |反义词：underloading, underutilization
