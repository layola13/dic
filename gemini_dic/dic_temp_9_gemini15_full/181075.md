reindicate:/ˌriːˈɪndɪkeɪt/| vt.|重新指示；再次表明；重新标示|To indicate again or anew; to show or point out again.|例句：The map needs to be reindicated to show the new road. 地图需要重新标示以显示新路。|例句：The study reindicated a strong correlation between smoking and lung cancer. 研究再次表明吸烟与肺癌之间存在很强的相关性。|近义词：reinstate, reiterate, reaffirm|反义词：obscure, conceal|动词


USAGE->

He reindicates the location on the map. = 他在地图上重新标示了位置。
They reindicate their support for the project. = 他们再次表示他们支持这个项目。
