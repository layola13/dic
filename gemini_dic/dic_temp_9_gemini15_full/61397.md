emblematize:/ˈɛmbləmaɪz/| vt.| 象征；代表；作为…的象征 | To represent or be a symbol of something.  The dove emblematizes peace.  | The white flag emblematizes surrender. 白旗象征着投降。 | symbolize, represent, typify |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境选择如misrepresent, distort等) | 动词
