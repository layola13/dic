elementarity:/ˌɛləmɛnˈtærɪti/| n. |基本性；简单性；元素性 |The state or quality of being elementary; simplicity; fundamental nature.  It often refers to the basic principles or foundational aspects of something.  For example, in mathematics, elementarity might refer to the simplicity of a proof or the fundamental nature of a concept. In physics, it could relate to the basic constituents of matter. |The elementarity of the problem made it easy to solve.  这个问题的基本性使得它很容易解决。|The professor explained the elementarity of the chemical reaction. 教授解释了化学反应的基本原理。|simplicity, fundamentality, basicness |complexity, sophistication, intricacy |名词
