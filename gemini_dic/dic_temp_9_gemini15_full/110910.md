lipidic:/lɪˈpɪdɪk/| adj.|类脂的，脂质的| Relating to or composed of lipids. Lipids are a broad group of naturally occurring molecules that include fats, waxes, sterols, fat-soluble vitamins (such as vitamins A, D, E, and K), monoglycerides, diglycerides, triglycerides, phospholipids, and others.  They are important biological molecules that perform a variety of functions in the body, including energy storage, cell membrane structure, and hormone production.|The cell membrane has a lipidic bilayer.  细胞膜具有脂质双层结构。| fatty, oily, greasy | non-lipidic
