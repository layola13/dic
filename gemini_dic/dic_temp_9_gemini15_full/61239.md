emanant:/ˈɛmənənt/| adj.|散发出的；发出的；流露的| emanating from or proceeding from a source; issuing.|The emanant scent of roses filled the air.  散发出的玫瑰香味充满了空气。|The emanant heat from the fire was intense. 从火中散发出的热量非常强烈。|近义词：exuding, radiating, issuing, proceeding|反义词：incoming, absorbed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The aroma emanates from the bakery. = 香味从面包店散发出来。
The light emanates from the sun. = 光芒来自太阳。


一般过去时:
A strange odor emanated from the basement. = 一种奇怪的气味从地下室散发出来。
Heat emanated from the engine. = 热量从发动机散发出来。


现在完成时:
A sense of peace has emanated from her since she started yoga. = 自从她开始练习瑜伽以来，她身上散发出一股平静的气息。
Confidence has emanated from the team after their recent win. = 团队最近获胜后，他们散发出了自信。


过去完成时:
A feeling of unease had emanated from him before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，他已经流露出了不安的情绪。
An aura of mystery had emanated from the old house for years. = 多年来，这座老房子一直散发着神秘的气息。


将来时:
A new energy will emanate from the company after the restructuring. = 重组后，公司将焕发出新的活力。
Positive vibes will emanate from the concert. = 音乐会将散发积极的氛围。


虚拟语气:
If only joy emanated from every corner of the world. = 如果世界上每个角落都能散发快乐就好了。
I wish peace would emanate from the conflict zone. = 我希望冲突地区能够散发和平的气息。


被动语态:
The fragrance was emanated throughout the room by the flowers. = 花香弥漫在整个房间。 (Note: Passive voice is less common with "emanate")
The warmth was emanated by the fireplace. = 温暖是从壁炉散发出来的。(Note: Passive voice is less common with "emanate")


疑问句:
What emanates from that strange device? = 那台奇怪的装置散发着什么？
Does a sense of danger emanate from this situation? =  这种情况散发着危险的气息吗？


否定句:
No scent emanated from the empty bottle. = 空瓶子里没有散发任何气味。
No warmth emanated from the cold stone. = 冷冰冰的石头没有散发任何温暖。


PHRASE->
emanate from = 源于；来自
emanate energy = 散发能量
emanate light = 发光
emanate confidence = 散发自信
emanate warmth = 散发温暖
