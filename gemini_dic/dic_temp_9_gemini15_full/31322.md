chaptering:/ˈtʃæptərɪŋ/| v.|章节划分；分章节|The process of dividing a book, document, or other work into chapters.  It also can refer to the act of creating or writing chapters.|例句：The editor is currently chaptering the manuscript. 编辑目前正在将手稿分成章节。|例句：She spent weeks chaptering her novel, carefully considering the flow of the narrative. 她花了数周时间给她的小说分章节，仔细考虑了叙事的流程。|近义词：sectioning, dividing|反义词：combining, unifying|动词
