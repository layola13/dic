photorealism:/ˈfoʊtoʊˈriːəlɪzəm/| n. |摄影写实主义|A style of painting and other visual arts that aims for a highly realistic representation of objects, often indistinguishable from a photograph.  It emphasizes precise detail, accurate lighting, and a meticulous rendering of textures and surfaces. |The artist's photorealism is so convincing that it's hard to believe they're not photographs.  这位艺术家的摄影写实主义如此逼真，以至于很难相信它们不是照片。| hyperrealism, realism | abstract art, impressionism |名词
