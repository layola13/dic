obstructionisms:/əbˈstrʌkʃənɪzəm/| n. |阻挠行为；阻碍主义|The actions or practices of obstructing or hindering something, often with deliberate intent.  It often implies a systematic or organized effort to impede progress or create difficulties.  The term carries a negative connotation.|例句：The government's obstructionisms delayed the much-needed infrastructure projects. 政府的阻挠行为延误了急需的基础设施项目。

The constant obstructionisms from the opposition party prevented the bill from passing. 反对党的持续阻挠阻止了该法案的通过。|近义词：obstruction, hindrance, impediment, sabotage, interference|反义词：cooperation, facilitation, assistance
