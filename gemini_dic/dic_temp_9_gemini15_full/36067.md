coedits:/ˈkoʊˌɛdɪts/| n.|合作编辑；共同编辑|The process of multiple individuals editing a document or other work simultaneously or collaboratively.  It often involves using collaborative software or platforms.|例句：The students engaged in coedits of their research paper to ensure accuracy and consistency. 学生们参与了他们研究论文的合作编辑，以确保准确性和一致性。|近义词：collaborative editing, joint editing|反义词：solo editing, individual editing
