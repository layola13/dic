headlamps:/ˈhɛdlæmpz/| n.|前照灯，车前灯|  Headlamps are powerful lights at the front of a vehicle used to illuminate the road ahead at night or in low-light conditions. They improve visibility and safety for the driver and other road users.|The car's headlamps were on full beam.|近义词：headlights, front lights|反义词：taillights, rear lights
