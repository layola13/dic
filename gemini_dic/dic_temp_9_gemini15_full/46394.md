daemon:/ˈdiːmən/ |/ˈdemən/ | n. |守护神；守护进程；精灵；恶魔 |A daemon is a background process in a computer system that runs continuously, performing tasks such as managing resources or handling network connections.  It can also refer to a spirit or supernatural being, often malevolent. In classical mythology, it was a spirit, either benevolent or malevolent. | Example: The system daemon crashed, causing network problems.  系统守护进程崩溃，导致网络问题。 The story featured a mischievous daemon who played tricks on the villagers. 这个故事讲述了一个顽皮的精灵捉弄村民的故事。|近义词：spirit, process, program, demon |反义词：angel, saint
