declaim:/dɪˈkleɪm/ | vt. |朗诵；慷慨激昂地演讲；强烈反对 | to speak or deliver (something, such as a speech or poem) in a formal or impassioned way; to express strong disapproval of something. | 例句：The actor declaimed the sonnet with great feeling.  演员饱含感情地朗诵十四行诗。

The politician declaimed against the new tax law.  这位政治家强烈反对新的税法。| 近义词：recite, pronounce, denounce, protest | 反义词：whisper, murmur, accept
