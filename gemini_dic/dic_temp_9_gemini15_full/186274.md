rhizophora:/ˌraɪˈzɒfərə/| n. |红树属植物|A genus of tropical mangrove trees and shrubs, characterized by their aerial roots and ability to thrive in salty or brackish water.  They are crucial to coastal ecosystems.|Example:  Many species of *Rhizophora* are found in the intertidal zones of tropical and subtropical regions.  许多种类的红树属植物生长在热带和亚热带地区的潮间带。|近义词：mangrove (general term)|反义词：无
