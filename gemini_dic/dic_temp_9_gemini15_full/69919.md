fervency:/ˈfɜːrvənsi/ | n. |热情；热烈； fervor |The intense heat of her passion; a state of being fervent.  It describes the quality of being fervent, characterized by intense and passionate feeling.  |例句：Her fervency in her beliefs was inspiring. 她对信仰的热情令人鼓舞。

例句：The fervency of his plea for mercy moved the judge. 他恳求怜悯的热情打动了法官。


近义词：passion, ardor, zeal, enthusiasm |反义词：apathy, indifference, coolness
