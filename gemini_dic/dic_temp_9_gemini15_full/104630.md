karaism:/ˈkærəɪzəm/| n. |神道教中的神道|A Japanese religious movement emphasizing the importance of the kami (spirits or gods) and rejecting Buddhist influence.  It is characterized by a focus on ritual purity and a rejection of Buddhist doctrines.|Karaism is a significant religious movement in Japan, influencing many aspects of its culture.|近义词：Shinto (though Karaism is a distinct movement within Shinto)|反义词：Buddhism (in the context of Karaism's rejection of Buddhist influence)
