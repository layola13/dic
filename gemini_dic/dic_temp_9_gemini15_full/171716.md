pummel:/ˈpʌməl/ | vt. |猛击；痛打；连续猛击 | To strike repeatedly with the fists; to beat severely.  |例句：The boxer pummeled his opponent until he fell to the ground. 拳击手猛击对手，直到他倒在地上。

例句：The waves pummeled the coastline, causing significant erosion. 海浪猛烈地冲击海岸线，造成了严重的侵蚀。


近义词：beat, batter, thrash, pound, bash | 反义词：caress, soothe

词源:
The word "pummel" likely originates from the Middle English word "pommeler," meaning "to beat," which in turn possibly derives from the Old French word "pommeler," having similar meaning.  The connection to the idea of repeated blows is clear throughout its etymology.
