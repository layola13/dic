meets:/miːts/| v.|满足；遇见；开会；符合|To satisfy a requirement, expectation, or need; to come together with someone or something; to assemble for a meeting or conference; to be in accordance with.|例句：The building meets all safety regulations. 这座建筑物符合所有安全条例。|例句：I met my friend at the cafe. 我在咖啡馆遇见了我的朋友。|近义词：satisfies, encounters, assembles, conforms|反义词：fails, misses, disagrees, conflicts


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team meets every Monday. = 团队每周一开会。
He meets all the requirements for the job. = 他满足这份工作的所有要求。

一般过去时:
We met at the library yesterday. = 我们昨天在图书馆见面了。
The project met its deadline. = 项目按时完成了。

现在完成时:
I have met many interesting people this year. = 今年我结识了许多有趣的人。
The company has met its sales targets. = 公司已达到其销售目标。

过去完成时:
They had met before, so they were comfortable talking. = 他们以前见过面，所以他们谈话很轻松。
She had already met the deadline when she submitted her work. = 她提交作业时已经完成了最后期限。

将来时:
We will meet again next week. = 我们下周再见。
The committee will meet to discuss the proposal. = 委员会将开会讨论该提案。

虚拟语气:
If I met the president, I would ask him about his policies. = 如果我见到总统，我会问他关于他的政策。
I wish I had met him sooner. = 我真希望我早点认识他。

被动语态:
The requirements were met by the candidate. = 候选人满足了要求。
The needs of the community are met by various organizations. = 社区的需求由各个组织满足。

疑问句:
Where do you meet your friends? = 你在哪里见你的朋友？
Does this proposal meet your expectations? = 这个提议符合你的期望吗？

否定句:
The project did not meet its goals. = 项目没有达到其目标。
He doesn't meet the criteria for admission. = 他不符合入学标准。


PHRASE->

meet with = 会见；会面
meet one's expectations = 达到某人的期望
