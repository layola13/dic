lexicography:/ˌlɛksɪˈkɑːɡrəfi/| n. |词典编纂学；词典编纂|The science or practice of compiling dictionaries.  It involves the selection, definition, and arrangement of words, as well as the study of etymology and usage. |Example: Lexicography is a meticulous and time-consuming process requiring extensive research and linguistic expertise.|近义词：dictionary making, lexicology (partially)|反义词：N/A
