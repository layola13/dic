fail-safes:/ˈfeɪlˌseɪfs/| n.|安全装置，故障保护措施|Measures or devices designed to prevent a system from failing or to mitigate the consequences of failure.  They are built-in safeguards that automatically activate when a primary system fails.|例句：The software includes several fail-safes to prevent data loss. 该软件包含多个安全装置以防止数据丢失。
The airplane's fail-safes prevented a catastrophic accident.  飞机的安全装置防止了一场灾难性事故。|近义词：backup, safeguard, contingency plan|反义词：risk, vulnerability
