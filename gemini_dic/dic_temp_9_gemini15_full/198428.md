shredders:/ʃredərz/| n.|碎纸机；粉碎机|A machine that cuts paper or other material into small pieces.|The office uses shredders to destroy confidential documents.|近义词：paper shredder, document destroyer|反义词：None.


USAGE->

The shredder shreds paper quickly. = 碎纸机快速地碎纸。
We use shredders to dispose of sensitive documents. = 我们使用碎纸机来处理敏感文件。
