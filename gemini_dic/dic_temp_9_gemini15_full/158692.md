This can refer to multiple instances of plotlessness. | Example: The movie's plotlessnesses left the audience bewildered and frustrated.  这部电影情节的缺乏让观众感到困惑和沮丧。The novel's multiple plotlessnesses made it difficult to follow. 这部小说的多处情节缺失使其难以理解。|近义词: lack of plot, incoherence, discontinuity |反义词: plot, coherence, continuity
