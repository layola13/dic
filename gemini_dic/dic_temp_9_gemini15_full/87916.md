hierogrammatist:/ˌhaɪəˈroʊɡræməṭɪst/| n.|象形文字学家|A person who studies or is an expert in hierograms (ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs).  A hierogrammatist deciphers and interprets hieroglyphic inscriptions.  They possess a deep understanding of the language, symbols, and cultural context associated with these ancient writings.|Example: The hierogrammatist spent years painstakingly translating the ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs found in the tomb.  这位象形文字学家花费数年时间辛辛苦苦地翻译在墓穴中发现的古埃及象形文字。 The renowned hierogrammatist presented her findings at the international conference.  这位著名的象形文字学家在国际会议上展示了她的研究成果。|近义词：hieroglyphist, Egyptologist|反义词: None readily available.
