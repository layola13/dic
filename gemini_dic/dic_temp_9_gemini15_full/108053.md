latecomer:/ˈleɪtˌkʌmər/| n. |迟到者，后来者 | A person who arrives late or enters a field or activity later than others.  |例句：He was a latecomer to the party, arriving an hour after everyone else. 他是聚会上的迟到者，比其他人晚到一个小时。

例句：She is a latecomer to the field of artificial intelligence, but she's quickly making a name for herself. 她是人工智能领域的后来者，但她很快就成名了。


近义词： straggler, newcomer


反义词：early bird, pioneer
