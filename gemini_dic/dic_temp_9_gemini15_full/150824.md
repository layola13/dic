paragenesis:/ˌpærəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |共生;伴生;矿物共生|The simultaneous or sequential formation of different minerals in the same geological environment.  Paragenesis studies examine the order and conditions under which minerals crystallize together.  This helps geologists understand the history of rock formation and the processes that shaped it.| Example: The paragenesis of the ore deposit shows a clear sequence of mineral formation, starting with quartz and ending with galena.|近义词：association, co-occurrence|反义词：separation, isolation|
