quasi-criminal:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈkrɪmɪnəl/| adj.|准犯罪的，类似犯罪的|Something that resembles a crime but doesn't quite meet the legal definition.  It suggests an act that is morally reprehensible and has some criminal characteristics, but lacks the specific elements to be legally classified as a crime.|例句：His actions, while not strictly illegal, were considered quasi-criminal in nature. 他的行为虽然并非严格意义上的违法，但却被认为具有准犯罪性质。|例句：The company's deceptive marketing practices, though not technically illegal, were deemed quasi-criminal.  公司的虚假营销行为，虽然并非技术上违法，但却被认为是准犯罪行为。|近义词：delinquent,  offensive,  improper|反义词：legal, lawful, legitimate|形容词
