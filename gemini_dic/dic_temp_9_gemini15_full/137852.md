nonvillainous:/ˌnɒnˈvɪlənəs/ | adj. |非邪恶的，善良的 | Not villainous; not characteristic of a villain; benevolent or kind.  This word describes someone or something that lacks the qualities of a villain; it suggests a lack of malice, cruelty, or wickedness. | Example: The story features a nonvillainous antagonist who is driven by misunderstandings rather than evil intent.  这个故事中有一个非邪恶的反派，他的行为源于误解而不是恶意。  |近义词：benevolent, kind, good-natured, harmless, benign |反义词：villainous, evil, wicked, malicious, malevolent
