sextillion:/sekˈstɪljən/| n.| 十亿的十亿的十亿|A sextillion is a number equal to 10 to the power of 21 (1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000).  It's a very large number, often used in astronomy or when discussing extremely large quantities.| The estimated number of stars in the observable universe is in the sextillions.|近义词：quintillion (although smaller),  a vast number|反义词：None (it's a quantity, not a quality with an inherent opposite).|名词
