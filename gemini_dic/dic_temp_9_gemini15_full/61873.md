empowerments:/ɪmˈpaʊərmənts/| n.|授权；赋权；授权行为|The act of giving someone power or authority.  It refers to the process of becoming stronger and more confident, especially in controlling one's life and claiming one's rights.|例句：The women's empowerment movement has made significant strides in recent years. 近年来，妇女赋权运动取得了长足的进步。|例句：  The workshops focused on providing empowerments to marginalized communities.  这些研讨会专注于赋予边缘化社区权力。|近义词：authorization, enablement, authorization|反义词：disenfranchisement, disempowerment|名词
