dehumanized:/ˌdiːˈhjuːmənaɪzd/| adj.|非人道的；没有人性的；被剥夺人性的|Deprived of human qualities or attributes; treated as less than human.  The inhumane conditions in the factory dehumanized the workers.|近义词：inhuman, brutalized, objectified|反义词：humanized, compassionate, empathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory conditions dehumanize the workers. = 工厂条件使工人失去了人性。
This policy dehumanizes the refugees. = 这项政策使难民失去了人性。


一般过去时:
The war dehumanized the soldiers. = 战争使士兵们失去了人性。
Slavery dehumanized millions of people. = 奴隶制使数百万人民失去了人性。


现在完成时:
Years of oppression have dehumanized the population. = 多年的压迫使人民失去了人性。
The system has dehumanized many individuals. = 这个制度使许多人失去了人性。


过去完成时:
The propaganda had dehumanized the enemy in the minds of the soldiers. = 在士兵们的观念中，宣传已经把敌人妖魔化了。
The years of abuse had dehumanized the child. = 多年的虐待使这个孩子失去了人性。


将来时:
This new technology will dehumanize the workplace. = 这项新技术将使工作场所失去人性。
The increasing automation will dehumanize many jobs. = 日益增长的自动化将使许多工作失去人性。


虚拟语气:
