reannouncement:/ˌriːəˈnaʊnsmənt/| n. |重新宣布；再次宣布 |The act of announcing something again; a second announcement of something previously announced.  This often involves emphasizing or clarifying details from the initial announcement, or addressing new information. |例句：The reannouncement of the product launch was met with renewed excitement. = 产品发布的重新宣布受到了新的兴奋。


例句：The company issued a reannouncement of its merger plans, clarifying some details. = 公司再次宣布其合并计划，澄清了一些细节。


近义词：reiteration, reaffirmation, repetition


反义词：cancellation, withdrawal


词性：名词
