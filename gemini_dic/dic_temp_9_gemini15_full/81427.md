gratefullest:/ˈɡreɪtfʊləst/| adj.| 最感激的，极其感激的 | Most grateful; expressing the greatest gratitude.  It is the superlative form of "grateful." |例句：She felt the gratefullest towards her rescuers. 她对她的救命恩人感到无比感激。
例句：Expressing his gratefullest emotions, he embraced his savior.|表达了他最感激的情绪，他拥抱了他的救星。|近义词：most thankful, deeply appreciative |反义词：ungrateful, unappreciative |词性：形容词
