sclerenchyma:/skləˈrɛŋkɪmə/| n. | 厚壁组织 |  Sclerenchyma is a type of plant tissue composed of cells with thick, lignified cell walls that provide structural support and protection. These cells are often dead at maturity and their rigid walls contribute to the plant's strength and rigidity.  | Example: The sclerenchyma cells in the stem provide support for the plant.  厚壁组织细胞在茎中为植物提供支撑。|  collenchyma (but with key differences in cell wall composition and maturity) | parenchyma (which is softer and less rigid) | 名词
