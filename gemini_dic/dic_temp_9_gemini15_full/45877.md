cuteness:/ˈkjuːtnəs/ | n. |可爱，讨人喜欢|The quality or state of being cute; loveliness; charm.  It often refers to something or someone that evokes feelings of fondness and affection due to their small size, appealing features, or endearing behavior. |例句：The kittens' cuteness was irresistible.  小猫的可爱令人无法抗拒。


例句：Her cuteness charmed everyone in the room. 她的可爱迷住了房间里的每个人。


近义词：sweetness, charm, loveliness, adorableness, appeal


反义词：ugliness, repulsiveness, unpleasantness
