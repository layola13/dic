savely: /ˈseɪvli/ | adv. | 安全地，平安地 | Safely means in a way that avoids danger or risk.  It describes the manner in which something is done. | 例句：The pilot landed the plane safely. = 飞行员安全地降落了飞机。

He drove safely home in the heavy rain. = 他在大雨中安全地开车回家了。


近义词：securely, soundly, reliably | 反义词：dangerously, recklessly, haphazardly | 词性：副词
