monarchists:/ˈmɒnəkɪsts/| n. |君主主义者，拥护君主制的人|Supporters of a monarchy or monarchical system of government.  They believe in the hereditary rule of a monarch (king, queen, emperor, etc.).|Many monarchists believe that a monarch provides stability and continuity to a nation.|近义词：royalists, pro-monarchy advocates|反义词：republicans, anti-monarchists
