philologies:/fɪˈlɒlədʒiz/| n. |语言学(学科)；语文学|The study of language in its historical and comparative aspects.  Philologies encompasses the historical development of languages, their structures, and their relationships to each other. It also includes the study of literature and writing systems. |Example:  The professor delivered a fascinating lecture on the philologies of ancient Greece.  教授做了一个关于古希腊语文学的精彩讲座。|近义词：linguistics, comparative linguistics|反义词：None
