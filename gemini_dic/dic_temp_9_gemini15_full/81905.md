grift:/ɡrɪft/| n., v.|欺诈；骗局；行骗；诈骗|

英文解析:A grift is a swindle or a confidence trick.  It can also refer to the act of performing such a trick. The word often implies a degree of skill or cunning in the deception.

例句:
He made a living by grifting tourists. = 他靠欺骗游客为生。

The whole thing was a clever grift. = 整件事都是一个巧妙的骗局。


近义词:fraud, swindle, scam, con, cheat, hoax

反义词:honesty, integrity, fairness


词性:名词，动词
