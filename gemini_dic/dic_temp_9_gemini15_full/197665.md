sherwani:/ʃərˈvɑːni/ | n. | 谢尔瓦尼（男士长袍） | A long, coat-like garment traditionally worn by men in South Asia, typically reaching the ankles and featuring long sleeves, a high collar, and often embroidery or embellishments.  | He wore a beautiful sherwani to the wedding. 他穿着漂亮的谢尔瓦尼参加婚礼。 |  long coat, kurta (loose-fitting shirt), achkan (close-fitting coat) |  (No direct antonym, as it describes a specific type of garment) | 名词
