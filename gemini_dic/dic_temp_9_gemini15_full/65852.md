eviternity:/ˌiːvɪˈtɜːrnɪti/ | n. |永恒；不朽|A state or period of timelessness; eternity.  The word is a relatively recent coinage, combining "ever" and "eternity" to emphasize the unending nature of the concept.  It suggests a more intense or profound sense of eternity than the word "eternity" alone. |例句：The love they shared seemed to transcend time, an eviternity of devotion.  他们之间共享的爱似乎超越了时间，一种永恒的奉献。
