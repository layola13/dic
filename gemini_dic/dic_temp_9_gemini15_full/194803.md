semanticism:/sɪˈmænṭɪsɪzəm/| n. |语义学；意义论|The study of meaning in language.  It explores how words, phrases, sentences, and larger units of text convey meaning. Semanticists investigate the relationship between linguistic forms and their meanings, as well as the ways in which meaning is constructed and interpreted. |The semanticist analyzed the nuances of meaning in the poem. 语义学家分析了这首诗中意义的细微差别。|semantics, signification|nonsensical, meaningless|名词
