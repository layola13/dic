metrication:/ˌmɛtrɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|公制化；度量衡制改革|The process of converting measurements to the metric system.  It involves changing units of measurement from traditional systems (like imperial units in the US and UK) to the metric system (based on meters, kilograms, and liters).| Example: The metrication of the country's infrastructure was a massive undertaking.|近义词：metricization|反义词：imperialization
