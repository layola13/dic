morse:/mɔːrs/| n. |摩尔斯电码|Morse code is a method of transmitting text information as a series of on-off tones, lights, or clicks.  It is named after Samuel Morse, one of its inventors.  Each letter or number is represented by a unique sequence of dots and dashes.|Example:  The message was sent in Morse code.  这条消息是用摩尔斯电码发送的。|近义词：telegraph code|反义词：None
