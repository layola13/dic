geste:/ʒɛst/| n.|姿态；手势；行动；壮举|A gesture, especially one made with the hands, to express something.  A significant or impressive act or deed.|例句：He made a gesture of dismissal. 他做了个打发人的手势。

例句：Her heroic rescue was a geste of great courage. 她的英勇救援是一项伟大的壮举。

近义词：gesture, action, feat, deed|反义词：inaction, stillness
