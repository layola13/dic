oedipal:/ˈiːdɪpəl/| adj.|俄狄浦斯的；恋母（或恋父）情结的| relating to or characteristic of the Oedipus complex.  The term describes a complex of emotions and desires stemming from a child's unconscious sexual feelings for the parent of the opposite sex and rivalry with the parent of the same sex.  This is often manifested in various ways, including jealousy, rivalry, and even aggression.|例句：His intense attachment to his mother was seen as an oedipal manifestation.  他的强烈依恋母亲被视为俄狄浦斯情结的体现。

例句：The play explores the oedipal themes of rivalry and desire. 这部戏剧探讨了俄狄浦斯情结中竞争和欲望的主题。


近义词：mother-fixated (恋母的), father-fixated (恋父的), complexed (有情结的)


反义词：N/A  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用缺乏特定情结的描述来表达反义)


词性：形容词
