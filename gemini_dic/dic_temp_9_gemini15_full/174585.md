quirkier:/ˈkwɜːrkiər/| adj.|古怪的，奇特的，异常的 |More eccentric or strange than usual; odd.  It describes something or someone that is unusual, peculiar, or unconventional in a charming or interesting way. |例句：His quirky sense of humor made him a popular friend. 他古怪的幽默感使他成为一个受欢迎的朋友。

例句：The house had a lot of quirky features, like a spiral staircase that led to nowhere. 这房子有很多古怪的特点，比如一个通向无处可去的螺旋楼梯。


近义词：eccentric, odd, peculiar, strange, unusual, whimsical

反义词：normal, ordinary, conventional, typical


词性：形容词


词源：源自于单词“quirk”，意为“怪癖，奇特之处”。 “-ier”是比较级后缀，表示“更……的”。  The word "quirk" itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to Old French words associated with twisting or turning.  This suggests a connection to the idea of something being unusual or unexpected in its behavior or form.
