sapiens:/ˈseɪpiənz/| n. |智人；聪明人|Wise humans; humans belonging to the species *Homo sapiens*.  The word refers to anatomically modern humans,  distinguished by their capacity for complex thought, language, and culture. |Example:  *Homo sapiens* is the only surviving species of the genus *Homo*.  智人属中唯一幸存的物种是智人。|近义词：humans, humankind, mankind |反义词：brute, savage (in the context of lacking intellect or rationality)
