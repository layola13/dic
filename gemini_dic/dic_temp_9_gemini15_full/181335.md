reintrenching:/ˌriːɪnˈtrentʃɪŋ/| v.| 紧缩；退守；巩固防御工事 | To reduce one's commitments or activities in order to save money or resources; to strengthen one's position defensively.  |例句：The company is reintrenching in the face of declining profits. 公司正在面对利润下降而采取紧缩措施。

例句：The army is reintrenching its position on the hill.  军队正在山上巩固防御工事。

近义词：retrench, consolidate, fortify, economize

反义词：expand, extend, proliferate

The company is reintrenching its operations. = 公司正在紧缩运营。
They are reintrenching their defenses against the enemy. = 他们在加强防御工事以抵抗敌人。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
