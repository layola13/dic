lacerates:/ˈlæsəreɪts/| vt.|撕裂；使…伤痕累累；深深刺痛|To tear or cut roughly; to wound deeply, either physically or emotionally.  The sharp rocks lacerated his skin.  The criticism lacerated his pride.|近义词：tear, rip, gash, wound|反义词：heal, mend, soothe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rough fabric lacerates the skin. =粗糙的布料会磨破皮肤。
The criticism lacerates his self-esteem. = 批评伤害了他的自尊心。


一般过去时:
The barbed wire lacerated his leg. = 带刺的铁丝割伤了他的腿。
Her words lacerated his feelings. = 她说的话深深刺痛了他的感情。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
