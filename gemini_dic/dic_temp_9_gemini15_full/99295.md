intercorrelates:/ˌɪntərˈkɔːrəleɪts/| v.|相互关联，互相联系|To show a mutual relationship or connection between two or more things.|The study intercorrelates various factors contributing to climate change. 这项研究将导致气候变化的各种因素相互关联起来。|近义词：correlates, connects, relates|反义词：disconnects, separates
