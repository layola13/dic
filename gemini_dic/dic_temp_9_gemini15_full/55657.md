如果你的意思是 "dictionary"，则解析如下：

dictionary: /ˈdɪkʃənəri/ | n. |字典，词典 | A book or electronic resource that lists the words of a language in alphabetical order and gives their meaning, or that provides a translation of words from one language into another. |  例句：I looked up the word in the dictionary. 我在字典里查了这个词。|近义词：lexicon, glossary, thesaurus | 反义词：无
