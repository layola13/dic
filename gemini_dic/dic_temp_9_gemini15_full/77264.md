gastropub:/ˈɡæstrəʊpʌb/ | n. |高级酒吧；美食酒吧| A gastropub is a pub that serves high-quality food, often with a focus on locally sourced ingredients and innovative dishes, in addition to traditional pub fare and drinks.  They aim for a more upscale dining experience than a typical pub.|例句：Many gastropubs now offer tasting menus alongside their regular menu.|近义词：upscale pub, gourmet pub |反义词：dive bar, typical pub
