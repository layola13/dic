methodist:/ˈmɛθədɪst/| n. adj.|卫理公会教徒；卫理公会的|A member of a Protestant Christian denomination originating in the 18th century with John Wesley, emphasizing salvation through faith and good works, and characterized by a structured and methodical approach to religious practice.  A Methodist church or its adherents.|例句：My grandmother was a devout Methodist and attended church every Sunday.  我的祖母是一位虔诚的卫理公会教徒，每个星期天都去教堂。


例句：The Methodist church in our town is hosting a charity event next week. 我们镇上的卫理公会教堂下周将举办一场慈善活动。
