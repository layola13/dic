dumdums: /ˈdʌmˌdʌmz/ | n. |笨蛋，傻瓜，蠢货 | A dumdum is a derogatory term for a stupid or foolish person.  The term is considered offensive and should be avoided.  It can also refer to a type of bullet. | 例句：He's such a dumdum; he fell for that obvious scam. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然上当受骗了。


例句：Don't be such a dumdum, think before you act.  别傻了，行动前先想想。


近义词：idiot, fool, moron, imbecile


反义词：genius, brilliant, intelligent, clever


词性：名词
