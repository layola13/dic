/ˈdoʊtəri/ | n. |嫁妆；彩礼 | A dowry is property or money brought by a bride to her husband on their marriage.  The term often implies a transfer of wealth from the bride's family to the groom's family. | Example:  In some cultures, a substantial dowry is expected from the bride's family.  在某些文化中，新娘的家庭需要支付大量的嫁妆。 |近义词：bride price, dower |反义词：无
