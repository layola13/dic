pluripresence:/ˌpluːrɪˈprezns/| n. |多重存在；多重显现|The term "pluripresence" refers to the simultaneous presence of a single entity in multiple locations or states of being.  It suggests a multiplicity of existence, often in a virtual or technologically mediated context.  This concept is explored in fields like virtual reality, telepresence, and distributed systems. |Example: The holographic projection allowed for the pluripresence of the CEO at multiple company branches simultaneously.  CEO的多重全息投影使得他能够同时出现在公司的多个分支机构。|近义词：multiplicity, ubiquity, omnipresence|反义词：singularity, absence, uniqueness|名词
