keened:/kiːnd/| adj.|热切的；渴望的；敏锐的；激烈的|Having or showing eagerness or enthusiasm.  Showing a strong interest in something.|例句：She was keen to get started on the project. 她渴望开始这个项目。

例句：The detective had a keen eye for detail.  这位侦探对细节观察敏锐。


近义词：eager, enthusiastic, ardent, sharp, intense|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, dull, obtuse|词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is keen to learn new skills. = 他渴望学习新技能。
They are keen on playing football. = 他们热衷于踢足球。


一般过去时:
She was keen to help. = 她很乐意帮忙。
He was keen on his studies. = 他学习很认真。


现在完成时:
I have been keen to visit Japan for years. = 多年来我一直渴望去日本。
They have been keen supporters of the charity. = 他们一直是该慈善机构的忠实支持者。


过去完成时:
She had been keen to finish the work before the deadline. = 她渴望在截止日期前完成工作。
They had been keen to see the results of the experiment. = 他们渴望看到实验的结果。


将来时:
He will be keen to hear the news. = 他将渴望听到这个消息。
We will be keen to see you soon. = 我们很快就会很想见到你。


虚拟语气:
If I were keen, I would have applied for the job. = 如果我很渴望，我就会申请这份工作。
I wish I were keen on sports. = 我希望我热衷于运动。


被动语态:
The opportunity was keenly sought after. = 这个机会被人争相追逐。
The plan was keenly debated. = 这个计划被热烈讨论。


疑问句:
Are you keen to go out tonight? = 你今晚想出去吗？
Is she keen on that idea? = 她对那个主意感兴趣吗？


否定句:
He wasn't keen on the idea. = 他对这个主意不感兴趣。
They weren't keen to participate. = 他们不太愿意参与。


PHRASE->
keen interest =浓厚的兴趣
keen competition =激烈的竞争
keen observer =敏锐的观察者
keen sense of smell =灵敏的嗅觉
keen eyesight =敏锐的视力

词源:
源自古英语 *cēne，意思是“敏锐的，勇敢的”。  这个词与“ken”（知识，理解范围）有关。
