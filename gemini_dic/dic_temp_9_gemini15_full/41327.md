coresident:/ˌkoʊˈrezɪdənt/| n. |同住者，同住人 |A person who lives in the same place as another.  It often implies a shared living space,  such as roommates or those sharing a household. |Example:  My coresident and I split the rent and utilities.  我的同住者和我平摊房租和水电费。|近义词：roommate, housemate, flatmate |反义词：stranger, outsider
