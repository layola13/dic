refigure:/ˌriːˈfɪɡər/| vt.|重新计算；重新考虑；重新塑造；重新构想|To calculate or consider something again; to reshape or reconceive something.|例句：We need to refigure the budget to accommodate the unexpected expenses. 我们需要重新计算预算以适应意外开支。

例句：The artist decided to refigure the sculpture, giving it a more dynamic pose.  艺术家决定重新塑造雕塑，赋予它更动感的姿态。


近义词：recalculate, reconsider, reshape, rethink, redesign

反义词：finalize, confirm
