shaggily:/ˈʃæɡɪli/| adj.|蓬乱地；毛茸茸地；粗糙地 |  Describes something that is covered in long, unkempt hair or fur, or something rough and uneven in texture.  It suggests a lack of neatness or smoothness. | 例句：The dog's fur was shaggily matted.  那条狗的毛蓬乱地打结了。

The sheep were shaggily coated in thick wool. 那些羊披着厚厚的蓬乱的羊毛。| 近义词：unkempt, untidy, shaggy, rough, tousled | 反义词：smooth, neat, tidy, sleek
