decontrol:/ˌdiːkənˈtrəʊl/ | v. |解除管制；放松控制 | To remove or reduce control over something, especially a price, industry, or economy.  |例句：The government decided to decontrol the price of gasoline. 政府决定取消汽油价格管制。|例句：The company's decontrol of its marketing strategy led to increased sales. 公司放松营销策略的控制导致销售额增加。|近义词：deregulate, liberalize |反义词：control, regulate

USAGE->

The government decontrols the sugar market. = 政府取消了糖市场的管制。
They decontrol the prices of essential goods. = 他们取消了生活必需品的价格管制。
