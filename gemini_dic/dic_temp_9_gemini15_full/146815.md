overimport:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɔːrt/ | v. | 过度进口 | To import more of a good or service than is needed or desirable, often leading to negative consequences such as harming domestic industries or creating trade imbalances.  |  The country's overimport of cheap textiles has severely damaged its domestic textile industry.  |  excessive import, surplus import |  underimport, restricted import | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overimport steel from China. = 他们过度进口中国的钢铁。
The company overimports electronic components. = 这家公司过度进口电子元件。

一般过去时:
The government overimported grain last year. = 政府去年过度进口了粮食。
The retailer overimported toys during the holiday season. = 零售商在节日期间过度进口了玩具。

现在完成时:
They have overimported so much that prices have plummeted. = 他们过度进口太多，以至于价格暴跌。
The country has overimported foreign cars for years. = 这个国家多年来过度进口外国汽车。

过去完成时:
Before the crisis, they had overimported agricultural products. = 在危机之前，他们过度进口了农产品。
The company realized they had overimported components after the sales slowed. = 在销售放缓后，公司意识到他们过度进口了组件。

将来时:
They will overimport energy resources if prices continue to rise. = 如果价格继续上涨，他们将过度进口能源资源。
The market analysts predict that the country will overimport textiles next year. = 市场分析师预测，该国明年将过度进口纺织品。

虚拟语气:
If they had not overimported, the domestic industry would not have suffered. = 如果他们没有过度进口，国内产业就不会遭受损失。
It would be better if they didn't overimport so many cheap goods. = 如果他们不进口这么多廉价商品就好了。

被动语态:
Too much rice was overimported last year. = 去年过度进口了太多的稻米。
Cheap clothing is being overimported, harming local producers. = 廉价服装被过度进口，损害了当地生产商的利益。

疑问句:
Is the country overimporting oil? = 这个国家是否过度进口石油？
Are they overimporting electronics to the detriment of domestic manufacturers? = 他们是否过度进口电子产品以损害国内制造商的利益？

否定句:
The government is not overimporting wheat this year. = 政府今年没有过度进口小麦。
They did not overimport goods last quarter. = 他们上个季度没有过度进口商品。
