immenseness: /ɪˈmɛnsnɪs/ | n. |无限；广大；巨大 | The vastness or immensity of something, especially in size, extent, or degree.  It refers to something so large or great that it's difficult to comprehend fully. | 例句：The immensess of the ocean filled her with awe.  海洋的广阔让她充满了敬畏。

例句：The immensess of the task ahead was daunting.  面前任务的艰巨性令人望而却步。


近义词：vastness, enormity, magnitude, hugeness, bigness

反义词：littleness, smallness, insignificance, triviality


词性：名词


词源：源自于形容词 immense，其词根是拉丁语的 immensus，由 in- (not) + mensus (measured) 构成，意为“无法衡量”。因此，immenseness 指的是无法衡量的大小或程度。
