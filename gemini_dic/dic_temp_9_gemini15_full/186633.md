riflemanship:/ˈraɪflmənʃɪp/| n. |步枪射击技术；枪法|The skill and art of using a rifle effectively and accurately.  It encompasses proper stance, aiming, breathing techniques, trigger control, and follow-through.|例句：His years of practice had honed his riflemanship to a high degree.  他的多年练习使他的步枪射击技术达到了很高的水平。

例句：The instructor emphasized the importance of proper riflemanship for safety and accuracy. 教官强调了正确的步枪射击技术对于安全和准确性的重要性。


近义词：marksmanship, shooting skill

反义词：inaccuracy, poor marksmanship


词性：名词
