词源:
Heaviness derives from the Old English word *hefignes*, meaning "weight," which itself is from *hefig*, meaning "heavy."  The suffix *-ness* indicates a state or quality.  The evolution of the word reflects the persistent association of physical weight with emotional or spiritual burden.
