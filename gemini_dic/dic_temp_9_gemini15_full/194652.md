self-tuition:/ˌsɛlf tuˈɪʃən/ | n. | 自学，自费学习 |  The act of teaching oneself;  learning without a formal instructor, often implying self-financing.  | Example:  He achieved fluency in Spanish through self-tuition. 他通过自学掌握了西班牙语流利程度。|近义词：self-education, independent study | 反义词：formal instruction, classroom learning | 名词
