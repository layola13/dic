reprosecuted:/ˌriːˈprɑːsɪkjuːtɪd/| v. |再起诉，重新起诉 |To prosecute (someone) again for the same offense.  This typically happens after a previous prosecution has failed or been overturned. |例句：He was acquitted of the charges, but the prosecution decided to reprosecute him. 他被判无罪，但检方决定重新起诉他。

例句：After the initial trial resulted in a hung jury, the defendant was reprosecuted. 初审陪审团意见不一后，被告被重新起诉。


近义词：re-indict, retry


反义词：acquit, dismiss


词性:动词
