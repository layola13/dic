postcerebral: /ˌpoʊstˈsɛrɪbrəl/ | adj. |大脑后部的；大脑后方的| Relating to the part of the brain behind the cerebrum.  This term is primarily used in anatomical contexts to describe structures or locations situated behind the cerebrum, the largest part of the brain. | Example:  The postcerebral region showed signs of inflammation. 大脑后部区域显示出炎症迹象。| 近义词：posterior cerebral,  retrocerebral | 反义词：precerebral, anterior cerebral |  形容词
