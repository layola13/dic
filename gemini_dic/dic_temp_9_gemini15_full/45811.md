custom-built:/ˈkʌstəm bɪlt/| adj.|定制的，量身定制的 |Specifically designed and constructed to meet a particular customer's requirements or specifications.  It implies a high degree of personalization and often higher quality.|例句：We ordered a custom-built computer to meet our specific needs. 我们订购了一台定制电脑以满足我们的特定需求。|例句：The house was custom-built for the previous owner, incorporating many unique features. 这栋房子是为前任屋主定制建造的，融合了许多独特的特色。|近义词：made-to-order, bespoke, tailored, personalized|反义词：mass-produced, standard, off-the-shelf|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company custom-builds high-end audio systems. = 这家公司定制高端音响系统。
They custom-build furniture for individual clients. = 他们为个人客户定制家具。


一般过去时:
They custom-built a racing car for the upcoming competition. = 他们为即将到来的比赛定制了一辆赛车。
She custom-built her dream kitchen last year. = 她去年定制了她梦想中的厨房。


现在完成时:
We have custom-built several software solutions for our clients. = 我们已经为客户定制开发了几种软件解决方案。
The architect has custom-built many unique homes in the area. = 建筑师在这个区域定制建造了许多独特的房屋。


过去完成时:
The client had custom-built the entire house before moving in.  = 客户在搬入前已经定制建造了整栋房子。
The team had custom-built the engine prior to the test run. = 车队在测试运行前已经定制了发动机。


将来时:
We will custom-build a website for your business. = 我们将为您的企业定制一个网站。
They will custom-build a new extension to their home next year. = 他们明年将为他们的家定制一个新的扩建部分。


虚拟语气:
If we had more budget, we would custom-build a new facility. = 如果我们有更多预算，我们会定制建造一个新的设施。
I wish I could custom-build my own car. = 我希望我能定制我的车。


被动语态:
The software was custom-built to our exact specifications. = 软件是根据我们的精确规格定制的。
A new house is being custom-built on the corner lot. = 街角地块正在建造一栋新定制的房子。


疑问句:
Will the car be custom-built or mass-produced? = 这辆车是定制的还是批量生产的？
Is this furniture custom-built or from a catalogue? = 这家具是定制的还是从目录上订购的？


否定句:
They did not custom-build the computers; they bought them off-the-shelf. = 他们没有定制电脑；他们是从货架上买的。
The company doesn't custom-build small projects; they only take on large contracts. = 这家公司不做小型定制项目；他们只承接大型合同。
