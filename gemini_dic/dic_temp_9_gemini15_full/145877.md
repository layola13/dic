overdesires:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪərz/ | v. |过度渴望，极度渴求 | To desire something excessively or to an unhealthy degree.  It implies a craving that goes beyond normal or reasonable wants. | 例句：He overdesires wealth and power, neglecting his family and health. 他过度渴望财富和权力，忽视了他的家庭和健康。

He overdesires the approval of others, constantly seeking validation. 他过度渴望别人的认可，不断寻求肯定。


近义词：crave, yearn for, long for, covet, lust after | 反义词：be indifferent to, disdain, despise


词性：动词
