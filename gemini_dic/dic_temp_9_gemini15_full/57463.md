drafter:/ˈdræftər/| n. |起草人；制图员；绘图员 |A person who drafts documents, plans, or drawings.  |例句：The drafter carefully reviewed the contract before submitting it. 起草人仔细审查了合同后才提交。 |例句：The experienced drafter created detailed architectural drawings. 经验丰富的绘图员制作了详细的建筑图纸。|近义词：designer, writer, architect (depending on context)|反义词：reviewer, approver


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The drafter prepares the documents. = 起草人准备文件。
He drafts the blueprints every day. = 他每天绘制蓝图。


一般过去时:
The drafter prepared the documents yesterday. = 起草人昨天准备了文件。
She drafted the contract last week. = 她上周起草了合同。


现在完成时:
The drafter has prepared all the documents. = 起草人已经准备好了所有文件。
He has drafted several versions of the proposal.  = 他已经起草了提案的几个版本。


过去完成时:
The drafter had prepared the documents before the meeting. = 在会议之前，起草人已经准备好了文件。
She had drafted the report before submitting it. = 她提交报告之前已经起草好了报告。


将来时:
The drafter will prepare the documents tomorrow. = 起草人明天将准备文件。
He will draft the final version next week. = 他下周将起草最终版本。


虚拟语气:
If the drafter were here, he would help us. = 如果起草人在这里，他会帮助我们。
I wish the drafter had finished the drawing earlier. = 我希望起草人早点完成图纸。


被动语态:
The documents were prepared by the drafter. = 文件是由起草人准备的。
The contract was drafted last week. = 合同是上周起草的。


疑问句:
Who is the drafter of this document? = 这份文件的起草人是谁？
Will the drafter be able to finish on time? = 起草人能按时完成吗？


否定句:
The drafter did not prepare the documents. = 起草人没有准备文件。
He did not draft the contract yet. = 他还没有起草合同。
