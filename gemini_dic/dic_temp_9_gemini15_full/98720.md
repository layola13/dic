insurer:/ɪnˈʃʊərər/ | n. |保险公司；保险人 | A person or company that undertakes to compensate someone who suffers a particular loss or injury.  | Example: The insurer paid out the claim quickly and efficiently. 保险公司迅速有效地支付了索赔。 |近义词：underwriter, guarantor |反义词：insured (被保险人)
