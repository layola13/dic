mis-cite:/mɪsˈsaɪt/| vt.| 引用错误；误引 | To quote (a passage, book, author, etc.) incorrectly or inaccurately.  |例句：He mis-cited the source in his paper. 他在他的论文中错误地引用了来源。

例句：The student mis-cited the statistic, leading to a flawed conclusion.  学生误引了统计数据，导致结论有误。


近义词：misquote, misrepresent, paraphrase incorrectly, cite incorrectly

反义词：cite accurately, quote correctly
