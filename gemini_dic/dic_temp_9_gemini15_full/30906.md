chai:/tʃaɪ/ | n. | 茶；柴 | A beverage made by steeping tea leaves in hot water;  also refers to a spiced tea drink, particularly popular in South Asia. |  例句：I enjoy a cup of chai every morning.  我每天早上都喝一杯奶茶。| 例句：The chai at that cafe is incredibly flavorful. 那家咖啡馆的奶茶非常美味。|近义词：tea, beverage |反义词：无 | 名词
