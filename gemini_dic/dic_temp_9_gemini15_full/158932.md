pluralization: /ˌplʊrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 名词复数化；复数形式 | The process of making a word plural.  It involves adding suffixes, changing vowels, or employing other grammatical rules to indicate more than one.  This process varies across languages. | Example: The pluralization of "cat" is "cats".  The pluralization of "child" is "children", showing irregular pluralization. | 近义词: plural formation, making plural | 反义词: singularization
