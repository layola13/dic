reinitializing: /ˌriːɪˈnɪʃəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |重新初始化；重新启动| The act of restarting or resetting something to its original state, often involving clearing existing data and settings.  This is typically done in computing and software but can apply to other contexts. |例句：The system is currently reinitializing; please wait. 系统正在重新初始化，请稍候。

The database is being reinitialized after the power outage. 数据库在断电后正在重新初始化。


近义词：rebooting, resetting, restarting | 反义词：finalizing, completing
