defended:/dɪˈfɛndɪd/| v.|保卫，辩护|To protect someone or something from attack or criticism; to argue in favor of something.|例句：The soldiers defended the city bravely.  士兵们英勇地保卫了这座城市。|例句：The lawyer defended her client against the charges. 律师为她的当事人辩护，反驳了指控。|近义词：protected, shielded, upheld, championed|反义词：attacked, assailed, surrendered, betrayed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defends his beliefs strongly. = 他坚定地捍卫自己的信仰。
She defends the rights of the vulnerable. = 她捍卫弱势群体的权利。

一般过去时:
The army defended the border against invasion. = 军队保卫边境免受入侵。
He defended his actions in court. = 他在法庭上为自己辩护。

现在完成时:
They have defended their title for three years. = 他们已经三年蝉联冠军了。
She has defended her research against criticism. = 她一直为自己的研究成果反驳批评。

过去完成时:
He had defended his position before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经捍卫了自己的立场。
The lawyer had defended many similar cases. = 这名律师代理过许多类似的案件。

将来时:
The government will defend its policies. = 政府将捍卫其政策。
We will defend our country against any threat. = 我们将保卫我们的国家免受任何威胁。

虚拟语气:
If he defended himself better, he might have won the case. = 如果他更好地为自己辩护，他可能就赢了这场官司。
I wish I had defended my ideas more forcefully. = 我希望我当时更强有力地捍卫自己的想法。

被动语态:
The city was defended by the brave soldiers. = 这座城市由英勇的士兵保卫。
His actions were defended by his lawyer. = 他的行为得到了他律师的辩护。

疑问句:
Did he defend his actions? = 他为自己辩护了吗？
How will you defend your position? = 你将如何捍卫你的立场？

否定句:
He did not defend himself against the accusations. = 他没有为自己反驳这些指控。
She didn't defend her friend's actions. = 她没有为朋友的行为辩护。
