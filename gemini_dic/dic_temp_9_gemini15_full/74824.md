frenemies:/ˈfriːˌɛniz/| n. |亦敌亦友；表面朋友 |A frenemy is someone who is both a friend and an enemy; a person with whom one has a love-hate relationship.  They might act like a friend but harbor negative feelings or competitive intentions. |Example:  She's a frenemy; we're always fighting but also spend a lot of time together. 她是个亦敌亦友的人；我们总是吵架，但也经常在一起。|近义词：rival, adversary, competitor, acquaintance (with negative undertones)|反义词：best friend, true friend, confidant|名词
