cocker:/ˈkɒkə(r)/| n. v.|斗鸡;溺爱;梳理(头发);|A cocker is a type of small dog, specifically a Cocker Spaniel.  As a verb, it means to pamper excessively or to comb or style (hair).|例句：He's a spoiled child, his parents cocker him terribly. 他是个被宠坏的孩子，他的父母对他百依百顺。|近义词：pamper, spoil, indulge |反义词：neglect, ignore
