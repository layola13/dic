equivalents:/ɪˈkwɪvələnts/ | n. |等价物；对应物；等值；对等物 |Things that are equivalent are equal in value, amount, meaning, or importance.  They can be used to replace each other because they have the same effect. |The dollar and the euro are not always exact equivalents. 美元和欧元并不总是完全等值。|  The two plans are functionally equivalent. 这两个计划在功能上是等效的。| synonyms: equals, counterparts, parallels, matches | antonyms: opposites, differences |  n.


USAGE->

What are the equivalents of these words in French? = 这些词的法语等价词是什么？
