preaccordance:/ˌpriːəˈkɔːrdəns/| n.|预先协议，预先一致|A preaccordance is a preliminary agreement reached before a formal agreement is signed. It outlines the key terms and conditions that both parties intend to include in the final contract.  This stage allows for clarification and negotiation before the commitment of a legally binding document.|例句：The preaccordance between the two companies paved the way for a successful merger.  两家公司之间的预先协议为成功合并铺平了道路。|近义词：preliminary agreement, preliminary understanding, tentative agreement|反义词：final agreement, concluded agreement|名词
