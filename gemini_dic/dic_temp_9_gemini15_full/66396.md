executor:/ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtər/ | n. | 遗嘱执行人；执行者 |  A person or institution appointed by a testator to carry out the terms of a will.  An executor is responsible for managing the deceased's assets, paying debts, and distributing the remaining property according to the will's instructions. |  The executor of his will distributed his assets fairly among his heirs.  这位遗嘱执行人公平地将他的资产分给了他的继承人。 | administrator, trustee |  heir (in some contexts) |  n.
