pluralize: /ˈplʊərəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使复数化；变为复数 | To make (a noun or pronoun) plural.  This verb refers to the grammatical process of changing a singular word into its plural form. It can be applied to both regular and irregular pluralization. |

例句：
The software automatically pluralizes nouns. = 这个软件会自动将名词复数化。

He needs to pluralize the data before analysis. = 他需要在分析之前将数据复数化。


近义词：make plural, pluralise (British spelling)


反义词：singularize


词性:动词
