crystallizabilities:/ˌkrɪstəlaɪzəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |结晶能力，可结晶性 |The ability of a substance to form crystals.  This property is influenced by factors such as temperature, solvent, and the presence of impurities.  |例句：The crystallizabilities of different polymorphs of a drug can significantly impact its bioavailability.  不同的药物多晶型的结晶能力会显著影响其生物利用度。

The high crystallizabilities of these compounds make them suitable for single-crystal X-ray diffraction studies. 这些化合物的结晶能力很高，因此适合进行单晶X射线衍射研究。|近义词：crystallization tendency, ability to crystallize |反义词：amorphousness, non-crystallinity |名词
