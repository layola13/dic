inattentive:/ˌɪnəˈtɛntɪv/| adj. |不注意的；疏忽的；粗心的 |Not paying attention; careless; neglectful.  |例句：The inattentive student failed the exam.  这个粗心的学生考试不及格。

例句：Her inattentive driving led to a near-miss accident. 她的粗心驾驶导致了一起差点发生事故。


近义词：neglectful, careless, heedless, thoughtless, unmindful |反义词：attentive, mindful, careful, heedful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inattentive in class. 他上课不专心。
She is always inattentive to details. 她总是忽略细节。


一般过去时:
He was inattentive during the lecture. 他在讲座期间心不在焉。
She was inattentive to his warnings. 她对他的警告置之不理。


现在完成时:
He has been inattentive to his health lately. 他最近对自己的健康不太在意。
She has been inattentive to her studies. 她一直学习不认真。


过去完成时:
He had been inattentive all semester, so he failed. 他整个学期都不专心，所以不及格。
She had been inattentive to the instructions, resulting in an error. 她对指示不注意，导致了错误。


将来时:
He will be inattentive if you don't engage him. 如果你不吸引他的注意力，他会心不在焉。
She will likely be inattentive during the long meeting. 在漫长的会议期间，她可能会心不在焉。


虚拟语气:
If he were more attentive, he would succeed. 如果他更专心，他就会成功。
I wish she had been more attentive to my requests. 我希望她能更注意我的要求。


被动语态:
His needs were often overlooked due to the inattentive staff.  由于工作人员的疏忽，他的需求经常被忽视。
The problem was caused by inattentive handling of the equipment. 这个问题是由对设备的粗心处理造成的。


疑问句:
Is he being inattentive on purpose? 他是不是故意不专心？
Was she inattentive because she was tired? 她是不是因为累了所以心不在焉？


否定句:
He is not inattentive; he's just preoccupied. 他并非不专心，只是心事重重。
She wasn't inattentive; she simply misunderstood. 她并非不注意，只是误解了。

词源:
The word "inattentive" comes from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the word "attentive," which derives from the Latin word "attendere," meaning "to stretch towards," "to pay attention to."  Therefore, "inattentive" literally means "not paying attention."
