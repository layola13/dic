sleepiness:/ˈsliːpinəs/ | n. |困倦；嗜睡 | A feeling of tiredness and a strong desire to sleep.  It can be caused by lack of sleep, illness, or medication. |例句：The sleepiness made it difficult to concentrate on the lecture. 困倦使他难以集中精力听讲座。

例句：Overwork and stress often lead to excessive sleepiness during the day.  过度工作和压力常常导致白天过度嗜睡。


近义词：drowsiness, somnolence, lethargy |反义词：wakefulness, alertness


词源:  Derived from the noun "sleep" + the suffix "-iness," which indicates a state or condition.  The word's development reflects the natural grammatical process of adding suffixes to create new nouns describing states or qualities.
