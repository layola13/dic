overbroadens:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɒdnz/| v.|过于宽泛，过分概括|To make something too general or inclusive; to extend beyond reasonable limits.|The law overbroadens the definition of terrorism, potentially criminalizing legitimate dissent.  法律对恐怖主义的定义过于宽泛，可能将合法的异见定为犯罪。|近义词：generalizes excessively, overextends, exaggerates|反义词：specifies, limits, narrows

USAGE->

The government overbroadens its policies on immigration. = 政府在移民政策上过于宽泛。
This rule overbroadens the scope of the project. = 这条规则使项目的范围过于宽泛。
