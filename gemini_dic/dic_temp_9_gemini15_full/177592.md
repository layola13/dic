rebuckling: /ˌriˈbʌklɪŋ/ | v. |重新扣紧；再次扣紧 |The act of buckling something again, typically after it has become unbuckled or loosened.  It implies a process of fastening or securing something that was previously unsecured or improperly secured. |例句：The hiker had to rebuckle his backpack after a sudden gust of wind loosened the straps. 登山者在突然一阵大风吹松了背包带后不得不重新扣紧背包。

The engineer rebuckled the damaged support beam to ensure structural integrity. 工程师重新扣紧受损的支撑梁以确保结构完整性。


近义词：refasten, resecure, tighten, fasten again |反义词：unbuckle, loosen
