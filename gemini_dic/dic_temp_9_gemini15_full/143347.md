outexecute:/ˌaʊtɪɡˈzɛkjuːt/| vt.|胜过；超过；执行得更好|To surpass or exceed in performance or execution.  It implies a significant degree of superiority.|例句：Our team outexecuted the opponents in every aspect of the game. = 我们团队在比赛的各个方面都胜过了对手。
例句：The new software outexecutes the old version in terms of speed and efficiency. = 新软件在速度和效率方面都超过了旧版本。|近义词：outperform, surpass, exceed, outdo|反义词：underperform, fail, underachieve|


USAGE->

The new strategy outexecutes the old one in terms of effectiveness. = 新战略在有效性方面胜过旧战略。

词源:
The word "outexecute" is a combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent than") and the verb "execute" (meaning "to carry out or perform").  Therefore, it literally means "to execute to a greater extent than."
