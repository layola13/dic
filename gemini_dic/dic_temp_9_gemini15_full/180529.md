regexs:/ˈrɛɡɛks/| n.|正则表达式|Regular expressions (regex or regexp) are patterns used to match character combinations in strings. They are used in many programming languages and text editors for tasks like search and replace, data validation, and text parsing.  Regexs provide a concise and powerful way to describe complex patterns.|Example: The regex `\b\w{5}\b` will match any five-letter word.|近义词：regular expression, pattern|反义词：无
