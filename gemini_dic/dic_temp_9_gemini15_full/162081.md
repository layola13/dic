pre-collection: /ˌpriːkəˈlɛkʃən/ | n. | 预售系列，提前发布系列 | A pre-collection is a smaller collection of clothing or other products released before the main collection, often showcasing upcoming trends or styles.  It serves as a preview and can generate excitement for the main launch. | Example: The brand released a pre-collection of its spring line to gauge customer response.  这家品牌发布了春季系列的预售系列以评估客户的反应。|近义词：preview collection, advance collection |反义词：main collection, final collection
