sequent:/ˈsiːkwənt/ | adj. | 接着的；随之发生的；连续的 | Following as a consequence or result; subsequent.  | Example: The sequent events proved his theory correct.  随后发生的事情证明了他的理论是正确的。|近义词：subsequent, following, ensuing | 反义词：preceding, prior


The word "sequent" is derived from the Latin word "sequens," the present participle of "sequi," meaning "to follow."  It retains this core meaning of following in time or order.
