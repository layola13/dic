overstores:/ˌoʊvərˈstɔrz/ | n. |过度储备；库存过多 |The phenomenon where retailers hold excessive inventory levels beyond what's necessary to meet customer demand. This can lead to increased storage costs, potential obsolescence, and ultimately, reduced profitability.  |Example: The company suffered significant losses due to overstores of seasonal products.  公司由于季节性产品的库存过多而遭受了重大损失。|近义词：excess inventory, surplus stock |反义词：understocking, shortage |名词
