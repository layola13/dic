recitations:/ˌresɪˈteɪʃənz/| n. |朗诵；背诵；背诵表演|The act of reciting something, such as a poem or a speech, often from memory.  It can also refer to the performance itself.|The student's recitations were clear and expressive.  这位学生的朗诵清晰而富有表现力。

The teacher praised the students for their excellent recitations of Shakespeare.  老师称赞学生们精彩的莎士比亚朗诵表演。|readings, performances|mumbling, whispering|名词
