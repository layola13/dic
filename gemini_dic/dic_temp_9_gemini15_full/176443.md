re-keyboarding:/ˌriːˈkiːbɔːrdɪŋ/ | v. |重新键入|The act of typing something again, often to correct errors or to transfer data from one format to another.  It implies a complete retyping, not just minor corrections.|例句：The document needed re-keyboarding after the formatting errors were discovered.  文件在发现格式错误后需要重新键入。
The secretary spent the afternoon re-keyboarding the entire report. 秘书整个下午都在重新键入整个报告。|近义词：retyping, re-entering|反义词：proofreading (as a contrasting action)
