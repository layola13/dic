edemata:/ˌɛdɪˈmeɪtə/| n. |水肿；浮肿 |Swelling of soft tissues due to the accumulation of fluid.  Edema is often a symptom of an underlying medical condition.  The plural form is edemata.|例句：The patient presented with significant peripheral edema in both legs.  这位病人双腿出现明显的周围性水肿。

例句：  The doctor diagnosed the edema as a result of heart failure. 医生诊断水肿是心力衰竭引起的。


近义词：swelling, puffiness, turgor


反义词：dehydration


词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The edema persists despite treatment. = 水肿尽管治疗仍然持续存在。
Edema often indicates a serious medical problem. = 水肿通常表明存在严重的医疗问题。


一般过去时:
The edema subsided after the medication was administered. = 服药后水肿消退了。

现在完成时:
The patient has experienced significant edema for several weeks. = 病人几周来一直有严重的水肿。
She has had recurring episodes of edema since her surgery. = 自从手术以来，她反复出现水肿。


过去完成时:

PHRASE->

词源:  From Late Latin *œdēma, from Greek οἴδημα (oídēma) "swelling", from οἰδέω (oidéō) "to swell".
