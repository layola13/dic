self-reproofs:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈpruːfs/| n.|自我责备；自责| Acts of criticizing or blaming oneself.  Self-reproofs can range from mild feelings of guilt to intense self-condemnation. They often stem from perceived failures or shortcomings.|例句：Her self-reproofs were constant, a nagging voice in her head after every mistake.  她的自责是持续不断的，每次犯错后，她的脑海里都会出现一个唠叨的声音。

例句：Overwhelmed by self-reproofs, he decided to seek professional help.  不堪忍受自我责备，他决定寻求专业帮助。

近义词：self-criticism, self-condemnation, remorse, repentance|反义词：self-congratulation, self-satisfaction
