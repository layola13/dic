catastrophic:/ˌkætəˈstrɒfɪk/|/ˌkætəˈstrɑːfɪk/| adj. |灾难性的，灾难的 |extremely bad or unlucky; causing great damage or suffering.  |The earthquake had catastrophic consequences for the region. 地震给该地区带来了灾难性的后果。The fire caused catastrophic damage to the building.  火灾对建筑物造成了灾难性的破坏。| disastrous, devastating, ruinous |minor, insignificant, trivial|形容词
