no-goods:/ˈnoʊˌɡʊdz/| n.|无用之人，坏蛋，废物 |A no-good is a person considered to be worthless or bad.  It is typically used informally and carries a strong negative connotation. |例句：He's a complete no-good; he never keeps a job and always lets people down. 他是个十足的废物；他从来不守工作，总是让人失望。  He called them no-goods and worse. 他称他们为废物，甚至更糟。|近义词：worthless person, scoundrel, good-for-nothing, bum, loser|反义词：saint, hero, paragon
