omelet:/ˈɑːmlət/| n. |煎蛋卷，蛋卷|A dish made from eggs beaten and fried or baked, often with added ingredients such as cheese, vegetables, or meat.|Example: I had a delicious cheese omelet for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个美味的奶酪煎蛋卷。|近义词：fritatta, tortilla (in some contexts)|反义词：无
