resellers:/ˌriːˈsɛlərz/| n. |经销商，转售商 |A reseller buys products at wholesale prices and sells them to consumers or other businesses at a higher price to make a profit.  They act as an intermediary between the manufacturer and the end-user. |Example: Many online retailers are resellers of products manufactured by other companies.  许多在线零售商都是其他公司生产产品的经销商。

Many small businesses operate as resellers, purchasing goods in bulk and then selling them individually. 许多小型企业都是以经销商的身份运营，他们批量购买商品，然后单独出售。|近义词：dealers, distributors, retailers |反义词：manufacturers, producers
