resentful: /rɪˈzɛntfəl/ | adj. | 愤恨的；不满的 | Feeling or expressing bitterness or indignation at having been treated unfairly.  |例句：He was resentful of his brother's success. 他对他兄弟的成功感到愤恨。

例句：She felt resentful at being overlooked for promotion. 她因为晋升被忽视而感到愤恨。


近义词：bitter, indignant, angry, aggrieved, offended | 反义词：content, pleased, happy, satisfied


词源:  The word "resentful" is derived from the verb "resent," which comes from the Old French word "resentir," meaning "to feel again," "to feel keenly," or "to perceive."  The prefix "re-" indicates a repetition or renewal of feeling.  The "-ful" suffix transforms the verb into an adjective, indicating a state of being.


USAGE->

He is resentful of her constant criticism. 他对她不断的批评感到不满。
They are resentful about the unfair treatment they received. 他们对受到的不公平待遇感到愤恨。


一般过去时:
She was resentful that he didn't invite her to the party. 她对他没有邀请她参加聚会感到不满。
He was resentful of the long hours he had to work. 他对他不得不长时间工作感到不满。


现在完成时:
I have been resentful of his behavior for years. 我多年来一直对他这种行为感到不满。
They have been resentful towards the management since the layoffs. 自从裁员以来，他们一直对管理层怀恨在心。


过去完成时:
She had been resentful of her parents' expectations her whole life. 她一生都对她父母的期望感到不满。
He had been resentful of his boss's favoritism for years. 多年来，他一直对他老板的偏袒感到不满。


将来时:

They will likely be resentful if their concerns are ignored. 如果他们的担忧被忽视，他们可能会感到不满。


虚拟语气:

I wish I weren't so resentful of his success. 我真希望我不那么嫉妒他的成功。


被动语态:  (The adjective "resentful" is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:

否定句:
