cuddled:/ˈkʌdld/| v.|拥抱，依偎|To hold close and comfort, typically for warmth or affection.|例句：The mother cuddled her baby close. 母亲紧紧地抱着她的婴儿。|例句：He cuddled up on the sofa with a good book. 他蜷缩在沙发上看一本好书。|近义词：hugged, embraced, nestled|反义词：pushed away, ignored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She cuddles her cat every night. = 她每晚都抱她的猫。
They cuddle on the couch and watch TV. = 他们依偎在沙发上看电视。

一般过去时:
I cuddled my dog after a long day. = 漫长的一天后，我抱了抱我的狗。
We cuddled by the fireplace last night. = 我们昨晚依偎在壁炉旁。

过去完成时:
