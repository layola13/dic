scathless:/ˈskæθləs/| adj.|无伤的；未受损的；完好无损的|Free from harm or injury; undamaged.  The word implies a previous potential for harm or damage.|例句：The car emerged from the accident scathless. 这辆车毫发无损地从事故中逃脱了。

例句：After the storm, surprisingly, the house remained scathless.  暴风雨过后，令人惊讶的是，房子完好无损。


近义词：unharmed, undamaged, uninjured, intact, safe, sound


反义词：damaged, injured, harmed, wounded, impaired


词性：形容词
