creating:/kriˈeɪtɪŋ/| v. |创造；创作；引起| The act of bringing something new into existence.  It can refer to physical objects, ideas, works of art, or situations. |例句：She is creating a beautiful painting. 她正在创作一幅美丽的画作。

例句：The new policy is creating a lot of controversy.  这项新政策引起了很多争议。|近义词：making, producing, generating, inventing |反义词：destroying, ruining, annihilating |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are creating a new app. = 他们在开发一个新的应用程序。
We are creating a better future. = 我们正在创造一个更美好的未来。

一般过去时:
He created a masterpiece. = 他创作了一部杰作。
They created a lot of problems. = 他们制造了很多问题。

现在完成时:
She has created many successful businesses. = 她已经创办了许多成功的企业。
We have created a new strategy. = 我们制定了一个新的策略。

过去完成时:
They had created a detailed plan before starting the project. = 他们在开始项目之前已经制定了一个详细的计划。
She had created a lot of debt before she got the job. = 在她找到工作之前，她已经欠下了很多债务。

将来时:
They will create a new department next year. = 他们明年将成立一个新的部门。
We will create a more sustainable world. = 我们将创造一个更可持续发展的世界。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would create a novel. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会创作一部小说。
I wish I could create a time machine. = 我希望我能发明一台时光机。

被动语态:
The new product was created by a team of engineers. = 这款新产品是由一个工程师团队开发的。
The problem was created by a misunderstanding. = 这个问题是由误解造成的。

疑问句:
What are you creating? = 你正在创作什么？
What created this mess? = 是什么造成了这场混乱？

否定句:
He didn't create anything new. = 他没有创造任何新事物。
They didn't create a good impression. = 他们没有留下好印象。
