flat-screen: /ˈflætˌskriːn/ | adj. |平板的；平面屏幕的 | Having a flat screen, especially a television or computer monitor.  A flat-screen display is characterized by its thin profile and lack of significant curvature. | 例句：He bought a new flat-screen TV. 他买了一台新的平板电视。

例句：The modern office boasts several large flat-screen monitors. 现代办公室拥有几台大型平板显示器。


近义词：planar, flat-panel | 反义词：curved, bulky
