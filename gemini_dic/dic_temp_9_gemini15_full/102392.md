jabbers:/ˈdʒæbərz/| v.|喋喋不休地说话，胡说八道|To talk rapidly and incessantly, often incoherently or nonsensically.  |例句：He jabbered on about his trip to the zoo. 他喋喋不休地谈论着他去动物园的旅行。|例句：The child jabbered excitedly about his new toy.  孩子兴奋地喋喋不休地谈论着他的新玩具。|近义词：babble, chatter, prate, blather|反义词：be silent, be quiet|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jabbers constantly. = 他不停地喋喋不休。
She jabbers in her sleep. = 她睡梦中说胡话。

一般过去时:
He jabbered about politics all evening. = 他整个晚上都在喋喋不休地谈论政治。
She jabbered something unintelligible. = 她胡乱说了一通让人听不懂的话。

现在完成时:
He has jabbered away for hours. = 他已经喋喋不休地说了好几个小时了。
She has jabbered so much that I have a headache. = 她说了那么多，我都头疼了。

过去完成时:
He had jabbered for so long that everyone was tired. = 他喋喋不休说了那么久，大家都累了。
She had jabbered the whole story before I could interrupt. = 我还没来得及打断，她就喋喋不休地把整个故事都讲完了。

将来时:
He will jabber incessantly at the meeting. = 他将在会议上喋喋不休。
She will jabber until she is blue in the face. = 她会一直喋喋不休地说，直到脸都憋紫了。

虚拟语气:
If he jabbered less, people would listen more. = 如果他少说些话，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't jabber so much. = 我真希望他别那么多话。

被动语态:
His ideas were often jabbered away before they could be fully understood. = 他的想法常常在他能完全理解之前就被喋喋不休地带过去了。
The message was jabbered out so quickly that it was hard to understand. = 信息说得太快了，很难理解。

疑问句:
Does he always jabber like that? = 他总是那样喋喋不休吗？
Why is she jabbering so much today? = 她今天为什么说那么多话？

否定句:
He didn't jabber during the presentation. = 他在演示过程中没有喋喋不休。
She didn't jabber about it at all. = 她根本没对此喋喋不休。
