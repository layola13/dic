lactic:/ˈlæktɪk/ | adj. | 乳酸的；乳汁的 | Relating to or derived from milk or lactic acid.  Lactic acid is a byproduct of anaerobic respiration, often found in sour milk and muscles after strenuous exercise. | Example: Lactic acid fermentation is used to produce yogurt. 乳酸发酵用于生产酸奶。  The athlete felt the burn of lactic acid in his muscles after the race. 运动员赛后感到肌肉里乳酸的灼烧感。|近义词：dairy, milky | 反义词：None specifically, but words implying the absence of lactic acid could be considered antonyms in context.
