esurient:/ˈɛzjuəriənt/| adj.| 渴望的；贪婪的；食欲旺盛的 | Extremely hungry; ravenous; voracious.  It also implies a strong desire or craving for something beyond just food, such as power or knowledge. |例句：The esurient child devoured the entire cake in minutes.  这个贪吃的孩子几分钟内就吃掉了整个蛋糕。

例句：Driven by an esurient ambition, she climbed the corporate ladder relentlessly.  受着强烈的野心的驱使，她毫不留情地攀登着公司阶梯。


近义词：ravenous, voracious, greedy, famished, insatiable, rapacious


反义词：satiated, satisfied, content, unambitious


词源：From Latin *esuriēns, present participle of esūriō "to be hungry," from esūris "hungry" (from Proto-Italic *ēsu-rī-, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ews- "to burn" ; thus, figuratively, "to crave").
