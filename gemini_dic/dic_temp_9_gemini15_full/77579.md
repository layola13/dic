geckoes:/ˈɡɛkoʊz/ | n. |壁虎（复数） | Geckos are small lizards characterized by their adhesive toes. They are found in warm climates around the world.  | Example:  Many species of geckoes are nocturnal.  许多种壁虎是夜行动物。

The word "gecko" is thought to originate from the Malay word "gekoq"  or a similar Javanese word,  representing the sound these lizards make. The plural form is simply adding the standard English plural 's'.
