qual:/kwɑːl/| adj.|合格的，胜任的，有资格的|Of the appropriate quality; possessing the necessary skills, knowledge, or qualifications.|例句：He is qual for the job. 他胜任这份工作。|近义词：competent, eligible, qualified|反义词：ineligible, unqualified


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is qualified for the position. = 他有资格担任这个职位。
She is competent in her field. = 她在她的领域很称职。


一般过去时:
They were qualified to vote. = 他们有资格投票。
He was competent enough to handle the project. = 他足够胜任处理这个项目。


现在完成时:
She has qualified for the Olympics. = 她已经获得奥运会资格。
He has proven himself competent time and again. = 他一次又一次地证明了自己的能力。


过去完成时:
He had qualified for the next round before the final match. = 在决赛之前他已经获得了下一轮的资格。
They had already proven themselves competent before the project began. = 在项目开始之前他们已经证明了自己的能力。


将来时:
She will qualify for the scholarship. = 她将获得奖学金资格。
He will be competent enough to lead the team.  = 他将有能力领导团队。


虚拟语气:
If he were qualified, he would apply for the job. = 如果他有资格，他会申请这份工作。
If she were competent, she would have finished the work on time. = 如果她有能力，她就会按时完成工作。


被动语态:
He was qualified by the committee. = 他被委员会认定合格。
The candidates were deemed competent for the role. = 候选人被认为胜任该角色。


疑问句:
Is he qualified to do this work? = 他有资格做这项工作吗？
Are they competent enough to handle this project? = 他们有能力处理这个项目吗？


否定句:
He is not qualified for the job. = 他不胜任这份工作。
She is not competent to make such decisions. = 她没有能力做出这样的决定。


PHRASE->
qualify for = 获得...资格
highly qualified = 高度胜任的
well qualified = 资格很好的
meet the qualifications = 符合资格
lack of qualifications = 缺乏资格
