lash: /læʃ/ | n., v. | 鞭打；睫毛；猛烈抨击 |

英文解析:As a noun, "lash" refers to a whip or a stroke with a whip. It can also refer to an eyelash or a part of something that resembles a whip. As a verb, "lash" means to whip or strike with a whip, to move or swing violently, or to criticize harshly.


例句:

n. (名词)
The trainer used a lash to guide the horse.  = 驯马师用鞭子引导马匹。

The wind lashed against the windows. = 风猛烈地拍打着窗户。


v. (动词)
He lashed out at his opponent. = 他猛烈抨击了他的对手。

She lashed the horse to keep it under control. = 她鞭打马匹以控制它。


近义词: (Noun)  whip, stroke, eyelash; (Verb) whip, strike, flog, criticize, condemn, berate

反义词: (Verb) praise, commend, applaud


词性:  Noun, Verb
