pseudocatholically: /ˌsuːdəʊkæˈθɒlɪkli/ | adv. | 假天主教徒地，貌似天主教徒地 | In a manner characteristic of someone who superficially or falsely claims to be Catholic.  This word suggests a pretense or imitation of Catholic beliefs and practices, lacking genuine faith or commitment. | 例句：He lived pseudocatholically, attending mass only on holidays and ignoring the core tenets of the faith. 他过着假天主教徒的生活，只在节假日参加弥撒，并且无视信仰的核心原则。


例句：She acted pseudocatholically, attending church services but showing no real devotion. 她伪装成天主教徒的样子，参加教堂礼拜，但却没有任何真正的虔诚。


近义词：hypocritically (regarding religion), superficially, nominally


反义词：genuinely, sincerely, devoutly


词性：副词
