cemeteries:/ˈsɛməˌtɛriz/| n.|墓地，公墓|Places where dead people are buried.  Cemeteries are often places of quiet reflection and remembrance.|例句：Many people visit cemeteries on Memorial Day. 许多人在阵亡将士纪念日去墓地祭奠。|例句：The old cemetery was overgrown with weeds.  古老的墓地杂草丛生。|近义词：graveyards, burial grounds|反义词：None.
