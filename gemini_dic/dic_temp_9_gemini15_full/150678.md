papyri:/pəˈpaɪraɪ/| n.|莎草纸；纸莎草纸|The plural of papyrus.  Refers to multiple sheets of papyrus.  Ancient Egyptians used papyri to write on.|例句：The museum displayed ancient papyri containing hieroglyphs.  博物馆展出了包含象形文字的古代莎草纸。

例句：Scholars spent years deciphering the fragmented papyri.  学者们花了数年时间破译这些碎片状的莎草纸。|近义词：scrolls, manuscripts|反义词：无
