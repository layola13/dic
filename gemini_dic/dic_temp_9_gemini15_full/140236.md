offish:/ˈɒfɪʃ/| adj.|冷淡的，疏远的 |Unfriendly and distant; not inclined to talk or engage in conversation.|例句：He seemed offish and uninterested in our conversation. 他看起来很冷淡，对我们的谈话不感兴趣。|近义词：distant, aloof, unfriendly, reserved |反义词：friendly, warm, approachable, gregarious
