case-worker: /ˈkeɪsˌwɜːrkər/ | n. |个案工作者，社会工作者 | A caseworker is a professional who works with individuals, families, or groups to address social, emotional, or economic challenges.  They assess needs, develop plans, and provide support and resources.  | Example: The caseworker helped the family find affordable housing and access to food assistance.  个案工作者帮助这个家庭找到了经济适用房和食品援助。 |近义词：social worker, welfare worker |反义词：N/A
