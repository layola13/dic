non-compliance:/ˌnɒn kəmˈplaɪəns/ | n. |不遵守，违规 |Failure to comply with a rule, law, or order.  It often refers to a failure to meet a specific requirement or standard. |The company faces fines for non-compliance with safety regulations.  这家公司因不遵守安全条例而面临罚款。  |compliance, disobedience |compliance, obedience |名词
