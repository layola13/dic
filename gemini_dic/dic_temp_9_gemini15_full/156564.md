picketee:/ˌpɪkəˈtiː/| n. |纠察队员，巡逻员|A person who patrols a picket line to prevent non-union workers from entering a workplace during a strike.  They often carry signs or banners and try to persuade others to join the strike. |例句：The picketeers were shouting slogans and carrying signs. 纠察队员们喊着口号，拿着标语。

例句：Several picketeers were arrested for blocking the entrance to the factory. 有几名纠察队员因封锁工厂入口而被捕。


近义词：picket, demonstrator, protester |反义词：N/A |词性：名词
