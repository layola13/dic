caulicle:/ˈkɔːlɪkl/| n. |胚根；幼根|The caulicle is the part of a germinating seed that develops into the stem. It's the embryonic stem connecting the radicle (embryonic root) to the cotyledons (seed leaves).|例句：The caulicle emerged from the seed, signaling the beginning of germination. 胚根从种子中长出，标志着发芽的开始。 The length of the caulicle varies depending on the plant species. 胚根的长度因植物种类而异。|近义词：embryonic stem, plumule (partially)|反义词：radicle (in terms of function and position)|名词
