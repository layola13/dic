caretakers:/ˈkerˌteɪkərz/| n.|看护人，管理人，临时负责人|People who take care of something or someone, especially temporarily.  They are responsible for the upkeep and maintenance of a property, a person, or a situation.|例句：The caretakers of the building were responsible for maintaining the cleanliness and security.  大楼的看护人负责维护清洁和安全。

例句：The caretakers of the elderly woman helped her with her daily needs.  这位老妇人的看护人帮助她处理日常需求。


近义词：guardians, custodians, keepers, supervisors|反义词：neglectors, abandoners|名词
