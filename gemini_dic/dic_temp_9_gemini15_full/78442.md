get-with-it: /ɡɛtˈwɪðɪt/ | phrasal verb | 赶上潮流；适应形势；理解；明白 | To understand and adapt to current trends or situations; to become modern or relevant. | 例句：You need to get with it, the rules have changed.  你需要赶上潮流了，规则变了。


例句：The company needs to get with it and update its technology. 公司需要赶上潮流，更新技术。


近义词：catch up, adapt, modernize, understand, grasp


反义词：lag behind, be out of touch, be behind the times


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gets with it quickly. = 他很快就能适应形势。
They get with it and finish the work on time. = 他们适应了形势并在规定时间内完成了工作。


一般过去时:
She got with it after a few weeks. = 她几周后就适应了形势。
We got with it and launched a new marketing campaign. = 我们适应了形势并启动了一个新的营销活动。


现在完成时:
He has gotten with it and learned the new software. = 他已经适应了形势并学会了新的软件。
They have gotten with it and improved their efficiency. = 他们已经适应了形势并提高了效率。


过去完成时:
She had gotten with it before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前就适应了形势。
We had gotten with it by the time the project started. = 项目开始时，我们已经适应了形势。


将来时:
He will get with it soon. = 他很快就会适应形势。
They will get with it once they understand the new system. = 一旦他们理解了新系统，他们就会适应形势。


虚拟语气:
If he got with it, he would be more successful. = 如果他能适应形势，他会更成功。
I wish she would get with it and finish her work. = 我希望她能适应形势并完成她的工作。


被动语态: (This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)  这个短语动词很少用被动语态。


疑问句:

否定句:
