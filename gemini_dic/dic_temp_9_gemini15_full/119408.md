microfiching:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪʃɪŋ/ | n. |微缩胶卷制作；缩微胶片制作 | The process of reducing documents to miniature size and storing them on microfilm.  | Example: The library uses microfiching to preserve its vast collection of historical documents. = 图书馆使用微缩胶卷来保存其大量的历史文献。  The company outsourced its microfiching to a specialized firm. = 公司将其微缩胶卷制作外包给了一家专业公司。|近义词：microfilming |反义词：enlarging, expanding | 名词
