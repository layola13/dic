retest:/riːˈtɛst/ | vt. n. |复试；再测试；重新测试 |To test again; to subject to a second test.  |例句：The doctor ordered a retest of the blood sample. 医生下令对血液样本进行复检。

例句：The software failed the initial test, so we need to retest it.  软件第一次测试失败，所以我们需要重新测试它。


近义词：re-examine, re-evaluate, check again


反义词：first test, initial test


词性:动词，名词
