disproving: /ˌdɪsˈpruːvɪŋ/ | v. | 反驳；证明…是错误的 |  The act of showing something to be false or wrong.  It involves presenting evidence or arguments that contradict or invalidate a claim, theory, or belief. |

例句：

The experiment yielded results disproving the initial hypothesis. = 实验结果反驳了最初的假设。

His testimony disproved the defendant's alibi. = 他的证词证明了被告的不在场证明是错误的。


近义词：refuting, contradicting, invalidating, refelling

反义词：proving, confirming, verifying, substantiating

词性：动词
