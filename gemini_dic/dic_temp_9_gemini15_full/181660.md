released:/rɪˈliːst/ | vt. | 释放；发布；发行；公开；解雇 | To set free from confinement; to make something available to the public; to let something go; to discharge someone from employment. | 例句：The prisoners were released from jail. 囚犯们被释放出狱了。  The company released a new product last week. 公司上周发布了一款新产品。 | 近义词： liberate, publish, launch, discharge, dismiss | 反义词： imprison, detain, withhold, retain, employ


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He releases a new album every year. 他每年发行一张新专辑。
The company releases its financial reports quarterly.  公司每季度发布财务报告。

一般过去时:
The police released the suspect after questioning. 警方在讯问后释放了嫌疑人。
They released the film to critical acclaim. 他们发行的电影获得了好评。

现在完成时:
The government has released a statement on the situation. 政府已经就此事发表声明。
She has finally released her debut novel. 她终于出版了她的处女作。

过去完成时:
They had released the hostages before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前释放了人质。
The news had already been released by the time I heard it. 我听到这个消息时，它已经被发布了。

将来时:
The band will release their new single next month. 乐队下个月将发行他们的新单曲。
The company will release a statement later today. 公司今天晚些时候将发表声明。

虚拟语气:
If they released the information earlier, the damage would have been less. 如果他们早点发布信息，损失就会小一些。
I wish they had released the movie sooner. 我希望他们早点发行这部电影。

被动语态:
The prisoners were released. 囚犯们被释放了。
The new software will be released next week. 新软件将于下周发布。

疑问句:
When will the new game be released? 新游戏什么时候发行？
Has the report been released yet? 报告发布了吗？

否定句:
The information was not released to the public. 信息没有向公众发布。
They haven't released the results of the election yet. 他们还没有公布选举结果。
