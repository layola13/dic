holstein:/ˈhɔːlstaɪn/| n.|荷斯坦牛；荷斯坦弗里斯兰牛|A breed of dairy cattle originally from the Netherlands, known for its high milk production.  Holstein cattle are typically black and white. |例句：The farmer's herd consisted mainly of Holstein cows.  这位农民的牛群主要由荷斯坦奶牛组成。|例句：Holstein Friesian cattle are renowned for their prolific milk yield. 荷斯坦弗里斯兰牛以其丰产的牛奶产量而闻名。|近义词：Friesian|反义词：无
