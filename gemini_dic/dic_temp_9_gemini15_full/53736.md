discoing:/dɪˈskoʊɪŋ/| v.|迪斯科舞会；跳迪斯科舞 |The act of dancing to disco music, attending disco parties, or engaging in activities associated with disco culture.  |例句：They spent the weekend discoing at the beach resort.  他们周末在海滩度假村跳迪斯科舞。  |例句：The vibrant discoing scene of the 70s is remembered fondly by many.  70 年代充满活力的迪斯科舞会场景被许多人怀念。|近义词：dancing, partying, clubbing |反义词：resting, relaxing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is discoing at the club tonight. = 他今晚在俱乐部跳舞。
They are discoing to the beat of the music. = 他们随着音乐节奏跳舞。


一般过去时:
We discoed all night long. = 我们整夜跳舞。
She discoed with her friends at the party. = 她在派对上和朋友们一起跳舞。


现在完成时:
I have discoed many times in my life. = 我一生中跳过很多次舞。
They have discoed to that song countless times. = 他们无数次跳过那首歌的舞。


过去完成时:
Before the party started, they had discoed to warm up. = 派对开始前，他们跳过舞热身。
We had discoed for hours before we felt tired. = 我们跳了几个小时的舞才感到疲倦。


将来时:
We will disco at the new club next weekend. = 我们下周末将在新俱乐部跳舞。
They will disco until the morning. = 他们会跳到早上。


虚拟语气:
If I could disco, I would join them. = 如果我会跳舞，我会加入他们。
I wish I could disco as well as they do. = 我希望我能像他们一样跳舞。


被动语态:
(Discoing is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be needed to express a passive meaning.)


疑问句:
Are you discoing tonight? = 你今晚跳舞吗？
Were they discoing when you arrived? = 你到达时他们在跳舞吗？


否定句:
I don't disco often. = 我不常跳舞。
They didn't disco at the wedding. = 他们在婚礼上没有跳舞。
