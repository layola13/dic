mischannels:/ˌmɪsˈtʃænəl/| vt.|错误引导；误导；滥用；|To divert or use wrongly; to send or direct something to the wrong place or person, often resulting in negative consequences.|例句：The company mischanneled funds into a risky investment.  公司将资金错误地导向了一项高风险投资。|例句：He mischanneled his anger by yelling at his colleagues. 他通过对同事大喊大叫来错误地发泄他的怒火。|近义词：misdirect, divert, misuse, misapply|反义词：channel correctly, guide properly, utilize effectively|动词
