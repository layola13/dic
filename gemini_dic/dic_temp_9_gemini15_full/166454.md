prereview:/ˈpriːvjuː/| n.| 预览；预审 | A preliminary review or examination of something, typically before its official release or presentation.  |例句：The film received positive prereviews before its release. 电影在上映前获得了正面评价。|例句：The committee conducted a prereview of the project proposal. 委员会对项目提案进行了预审。|近义词：preview, pre-screening, advance screening |反义词：post-review


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company conducts a prereview of all marketing materials. = 公司对所有营销材料进行预审。
She always prereviews her presentations before giving them. = 她总是在演讲之前进行预演。


一般过去时:
They prereviewed the document last week. = 他们上周预先审查了文件。
The editor prereviewed the manuscript before sending it to the printer. = 编辑在送交印刷厂之前预先审查了稿件。


现在完成时:
Have you prereviewed the contract yet? = 你已经预先审查过合同了吗？
She has prereviewed the data for accuracy. = 她已经预先审查了数据的准确性。


过去完成时:
They had prereviewed the design before presenting it to the client. = 他们在向客户展示设计之前已经预先审查过了。
The team had prereviewed the report several times before submitting it. = 团队在提交报告之前已经预先审查了好几次。


将来时:
We will prereview the budget tomorrow. = 我们明天将预审预算。
They will prereview the script before the final rehearsal. = 他们将在最终彩排之前预先审查剧本。


虚拟语气:
If they had prereviewed the software, they might have found the bug. = 如果他们预先审查了软件，他们可能会发现那个错误。
I wish I had prereviewed my speech more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地预先审查过我的演讲。


被动语态:
The proposal was prereviewed by the board. = 该提案已由董事会预先审查。
The manuscript is being prereviewed by the editor. = 手稿正在由编辑预先审查。


疑问句:
Will the article be prereviewed before publication? = 文章在发表前会预先审查吗？
Has the script been prereviewed by the director? = 剧本已经被导演预先审查了吗？


否定句:
The company did not prereview the advertisement before airing it. = 公司在播出广告之前没有预先审查。
They haven't prereviewed the documents yet. = 他们还没有预先审查文件。
