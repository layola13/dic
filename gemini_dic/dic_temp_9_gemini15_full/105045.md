kerasin:/ˈkɛrəsɪn/| n. |煤油，火油|A clear, colorless to yellowish, flammable liquid hydrocarbon mixture obtained by distilling petroleum, used as a fuel and solvent.|例句：Kerosene is used as a fuel in some lamps and stoves. 煤油被用作一些灯具和炉灶的燃料。|例句：The plane's engine ran on kerosene. 这架飞机的引擎使用煤油运行。|近义词：paraffin, jet fuel|反义词:无
