capes:/keɪps/| n.|海角；披风；岬|A cape is a large piece of land that sticks out into the sea.  It can also refer to a sleeveless garment worn over the shoulders and upper body. |例句：The rugged cape jutted dramatically into the turbulent sea.  那崎岖的海角戏剧性地伸入波涛汹涌的大海。

The actress wore a beautiful red cape to the premiere. 女演员在首映式上穿着一件美丽的红色披风。|近义词：promontory, headland, cloak, shawl|反义词：inlet, bay
