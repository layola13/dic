labor:/ˈleɪbər/| n. v.|劳动，劳力，工作；分娩| hard physical work. The process of giving birth.|例句：The labor was intense and exhausting.  这工作又累又苦。|例句：She's in labor. 她正在分娩。|近义词：work, toil, effort, childbirth|反义词：leisure, rest, idleness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He labors in the fields every day. = 他每天都在田里劳动。
They labor tirelessly to achieve their goals. = 他们为了实现目标而不知疲倦地工作。


一般过去时:
She labored for hours to finish the project. = 她辛苦工作了好几个小时才完成项目。
The workers labored under difficult conditions. = 工人们在艰苦的条件下工作。


现在完成时:
They have labored long and hard to build this company. = 他们为创建这家公司辛勤工作了很久。
We have labored to make this a success. = 我们一直努力使这件事成功。


过去完成时:
They had labored for years before they achieved their dream. = 在实现梦想之前，他们辛勤工作了很多年。
She had labored diligently on the manuscript before submitting it. = 在提交手稿之前，她已经辛勤地工作了。


将来时:
He will labor on this project until it's complete. = 他将继续从事这个项目直到完成。
They will labor to ensure the project's success. = 他们将努力确保项目的成功。


虚拟语气:
If he labored harder, he would achieve more. = 如果他更努力工作，他会取得更大的成就。
I wish I had labored less and rested more. = 我希望我工作少一点，休息多一点。


被动语态:
The land was labored extensively by the farmers. = 这块土地被农民们耕种得很彻底。
The project was labored over for months. = 这个项目花了几个月的时间才完成。


疑问句:
How long did you labor on that assignment? = 你花了多长时间做那个作业？
Does he labor under a lot of pressure? = 他工作压力大吗？


否定句:
He didn't labor in vain; his efforts paid off.  = 他没有白费力气；他的努力得到了回报。
They did not labor under any illusions about the task's difficulty. = 他们对这项任务的难度没有任何幻想。
