go-over-the-wall:/ˌɡoʊˈoʊvərðəwɔːl/| phrasal verb|项目移交，项目阶段结束|This phrase refers to the point in a project where one team or department hands off their work to another.  It often implies a lack of communication or collaboration between the teams, leading to potential problems.  The "wall" represents the barrier between teams.|Example: The marketing team needs to ensure a smooth go-over-the-wall process to the sales team for seamless product launch.  市场团队需要确保与销售团队顺利移交项目，以实现产品无缝发布。|近义词：handover, transfer, pass on|反义词：collaboration, integration|词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The design team regularly goes over the wall with completed mockups. = 设计团队定期将完成的模型移交给下一个阶段。
We go over the wall with the project documentation at the end of each sprint. = 我们在每个冲刺结束时移交项目文档。

一般过去时:
The developers went over the wall with the code to the testers last Friday. = 开发人员上周五将代码移交给测试人员。
They went over the wall with the project proposal to the client yesterday. = 他们昨天将项目提案移交给客户。

现在完成时:
We have gone over the wall with all the necessary documents. = 我们已经移交了所有必要的文档。
The team has gone over the wall with the alpha version of the software. = 该团队已经移交了软件的 alpha 版本。

过去完成时:
They had gone over the wall with the initial designs before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，他们已经移交了初步设计。
The project manager had gone over the wall with the budget before the final review. = 项目经理在最终审查之前已经移交了预算。

将来时:
We will go over the wall with the finished product next week. = 我们下周将移交成品。
The team will go over the wall with the report after the final analysis. = 该团队将在最终分析后移交报告。

虚拟语气:
If we went over the wall earlier, the project would be finished on time. = 如果我们早点移交，项目就能按时完成。
I wish we had gone over the wall with the specifications more clearly. = 我希望我们能更清楚地移交规格说明。

被动语态:
The project was gone over the wall without proper documentation. = 项目在没有适当文件的情况下被移交。
The designs were gone over the wall to the production team yesterday. = 设计图昨天被移交给生产团队。

疑问句:
When will the project be gone over the wall? = 项目什么时候移交？
Have they gone over the wall with the marketing materials yet? = 他们已经移交营销材料了吗？

否定句:
The team didn't go over the wall with the necessary information. = 团队没有移交必要的信息。
We haven't gone over the wall with the final report yet. = 我们还没有移交最终报告。
