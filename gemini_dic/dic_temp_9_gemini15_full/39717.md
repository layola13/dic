consigning:/kənˈsaɪnɪŋ/| v.|托运；委托；交付；(尤指)将(货物)托运|To consign something means to send it somewhere, especially by a carrier such as a shipping company or postal service.  It often implies a transfer of responsibility or ownership.  The term also carries the sense of entrusting or committing something to someone else's care.|例句：The company consigned the goods to a freight forwarder.  这家公司将货物托付给货运代理商。

例句：We consigned the paintings to an auction house. 我们把画托付给拍卖行。


近义词：ship, send, entrust, commit, deliver|反义词：receive, retain, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They consign goods to various destinations. = 他们将货物运往不同的目的地。
The artist consigns his paintings to the gallery. = 艺术家将他的画作委托给画廊。


一般过去时:
She consigned her belongings to storage. = 她把她的东西寄存在仓库里。
He consigned the package to his friend. = 他把包裹寄给了他的朋友。


现在完成时:
The company has consigned thousands of packages this year. = 今年该公司已托运了数千个包裹。
We have consigned all the documents to the lawyer. = 我们已将所有文件委托给律师。


过去完成时:
They had consigned the furniture before the move. = 他们在搬家之前已经托运了家具。
She had consigned the manuscript to a publisher. = 她已将手稿委托给出版商。


将来时:
We will consign the shipment next week. = 我们下周将托运货物。
They will consign the artwork to the museum. = 他们将把艺术品运送到博物馆。


虚拟语气:
If we consigned the goods earlier, we would have received them by now. = 如果我们早点托运货物，现在就收到了。
I wish I had consigned the package with insurance. = 我真希望我当时给包裹买了保险再托运。


被动语态:
The goods were consigned to the wrong address. = 货物被运送到错误的地址。
The paintings are being consigned to the auction. = 画作正在被委托拍卖。


疑问句:
Where were the goods consigned? = 货物运往何处？
Have the documents been consigned yet? = 文件已经托运了吗？


否定句:
They did not consign the package on time. = 他们没有按时托运包裹。
We have not yet consigned the shipment. = 我们尚未托运货物。
