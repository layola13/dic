pinniform:/ˈpɪnɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 羽状的；羽形的 | Resembling a feather; having the form of a feather.  The term describes structures that are arranged like the barbs of a feather, branching out from a central axis.  | Example: The pinniform leaves of the fern unfurled in the spring breeze.  春风中，蕨类的羽状叶子舒展开来。|近义词：feather-shaped, plume-like |反义词：unbranched, simple
