prometheanism:/ˌprɒmɪˈθiːənɪzəm/|/ˈprɑːmɪθiːənɪzəm/| n. |普罗米修斯主义；反抗权威；为人类利益而盗取天火的精神|The belief in the right to challenge authority and seize knowledge or power for the benefit of humanity, often at great personal risk or cost, drawing inspiration from the myth of Prometheus.  This often involves a willingness to defy established norms and social structures for a greater good, potentially leading to conflict and consequences. |The scientist's prometheanism led him to defy government regulations in his pursuit of a cure for cancer.  这位科学家的普罗米修斯主义精神促使他在追求癌症治疗的过程中无视政府法规。  | 近义词：rebellion, defiance, innovation, transgression | 反义词：conformity, obedience, passivity | 名词
