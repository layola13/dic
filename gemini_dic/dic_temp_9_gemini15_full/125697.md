munerate:/ˈmjuːnəreɪt/| vt.| 叙述；列举；细数；点名 | To mention or list individually; to enumerate.  | 例句：The teacher enumerated the rules of the classroom. 老师列举了教室的规章制度。

例句：He enumerated the reasons for his resignation. 他逐条列举了他辞职的原因。

近义词：list, recount, specify, detail

反义词：summarize, condense


词源:  From Medieval Latin *munerare "to perform a service, discharge a duty," from Latin munus "duty, service, gift." The sense shifted to "to mention, list" in the 17th century.
