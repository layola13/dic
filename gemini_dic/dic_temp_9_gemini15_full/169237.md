prolonge:/prəˈlɒŋ/|/prəˈlɔːŋ/| v.|延长，拖延，拉长|To extend the duration of something; to make something last longer.|例句：They prolonged the meeting until late evening.他们把会议延长到了深夜。|例句：The doctor prescribed medication to prolong his life.医生开了药来延长他的寿命。|近义词：extend, lengthen, prolongate|反义词：shorten, curtail, abbreviate
