dryers:/ˈdraɪərz/| n.|烘干机，干燥器|Machines or devices used to dry something, such as clothes, hair, or food.|例句：We need a new clothes dryer; this one is too old. 我们需要一台新的烘干机；这台太旧了。|例句：The industrial dryers are used to dry the grains. 工业干燥机用于干燥谷物。|近义词：dehydrators, desiccators|反义词：humidifiers, wetters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dryer spins the clothes to remove excess water. = 烘干机旋转衣物以去除多余水分。
My hair dryer works perfectly. = 我的吹风机工作得很好。


一般过去时:
I used the dryer to dry my clothes yesterday. = 我昨天用烘干机烘干了我的衣服。

现在完成时:
We have used this dryer for five years. = 我们已经使用了这台烘干机五年了。
She has already dried her hair. = 她已经吹干了头发。

将来时:
