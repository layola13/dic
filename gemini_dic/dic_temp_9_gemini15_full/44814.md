cruse:/kruːz/| n.|巡航，航行；航程；（尤指）乘船游览；|A cruise is a journey on a ship or boat, especially for pleasure.|例句：We're going on a cruise to the Caribbean. 我们要去加勒比海游轮旅行。|近义词：voyage, trip, journey, sailing|反义词：land travel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ship cruises the Mediterranean Sea. = 这艘船在地中海巡航。
He cruises the city in his new car. = 他开着他的新车在城市里兜风。


一般过去时:
They cruised around the island last summer. = 他们去年夏天在岛屿周围巡航。
The captain cruised the ship into the harbor. = 船长驾驶着船驶入港口。


现在完成时:
We have cruised the coast many times. = 我们多次沿海岸巡航。
She has cruised through life with ease. = 她轻松地度过了人生。


过去完成时:
They had cruised to several islands before reaching their destination. = 他们在到达目的地之前已经巡航了几个岛屿。
He had cruised the streets for hours looking for a parking spot. = 他在街上兜兜转转几个小时寻找停车位。


将来时:
We will cruise the Alaskan coast next year. = 我们明年将沿着阿拉斯加海岸巡航。
They will cruise down the river tomorrow. = 他们明天将沿着河向下游巡航。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would cruise the Pacific Ocean. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会去太平洋巡航。
I wish I could cruise the world in a luxury yacht. = 我希望我能在豪华游艇上环游世界。


被动语态:
