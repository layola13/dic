pratfalls:/ˈprætˌfɔːlz/| n.|滑稽的跌倒；出丑；失败|A pratfall is a comedic fall, typically involving a clumsy or unexpected tumble.  It's often used to describe a humorous and slightly embarrassing mishap. The term can also be used metaphorically to refer to a public failure or humiliation.|例句：The comedian's pratfalls had the audience roaring with laughter.  喜剧演员的滑稽摔跤逗得观众哈哈大笑。

The politician's scandalous affair was a major pratfall in his career.  这位政治家的婚外情是他职业生涯中的一次重大失败。|近义词：stumble, tumble, flop, blunder, fiasco|反义词：success, triumph, achievement
