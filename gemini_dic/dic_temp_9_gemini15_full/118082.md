mends:/mendz/| vt. n.|修理，缝补；恢复；改进|To repair something that is broken or damaged.  |例句：He mended the broken chair. 他修好了坏椅子。|例句：She mended the tear in her dress. 她缝补了她衣服上的破洞。|近义词：repair, fix, patch, restore |反义词：break, damage, destroy|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mends his clothes regularly. = 他定期缝补衣服。
She mends the broken vase carefully. = 她小心地修理破花瓶。


一般过去时:
The mechanic mended my car yesterday. = 修理工昨天修好了我的车。
I mended the fence last weekend. = 我上周末修好了篱笆。


现在完成时:
She has mended all the holes in her socks. = 她已经补好了袜子上的所有洞。
He has mended his ways since the accident. = 事故发生后，他改过自新了。


过去完成时:
The tailor had mended the suit before the wedding. = 裁缝在婚礼前已经修好了西装。
They had mended the roof before the storm arrived. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经修好了屋顶。


将来时:
I will mend the broken toy for my son. = 我会为儿子修好坏掉的玩具。
The plumber will mend the leaky pipe tomorrow. = 水管工明天会修理漏水的管道。


虚拟语气:
If I had a needle and thread, I would mend the rip. = 如果我有针线，我会缝补这个裂口。
I wish I had mended the relationship sooner. = 我真希望我早点修复这段关系。


被动语态:
The road was mended quickly after the accident. = 事故发生后，这条路很快就被修好了。
The broken window is being mended now. = 破窗户现在正在修理。


疑问句:
Can you mend this shirt? = 你能缝补这件衬衫吗？
Will he mend the fence himself? = 他会自己修篱笆吗？


否定句:
He couldn't mend the broken vase. = 他无法修好破花瓶。
I didn't mend the tear in my trousers. = 我没有缝补裤子上的破洞。
