hasten:/ˈheɪsən/ | vt. | 加速；催促；赶紧 | To move or act quickly; to speed up the progress of something; to urge someone to hurry. | 例句：We hastened our steps to catch the bus. = 我们加快脚步去赶公共汽车。

例句：He hastened to assure her that he would be there on time. = 他赶紧向她保证他会准时到。


近义词：accelerate, expedite, hurry, rush, spur | 反义词：delay, postpone, dawdle, procrastinate | 词性：动词


USAGE->

词源:
