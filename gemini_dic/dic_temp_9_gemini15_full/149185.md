oxygen:/ˈɒksɪdʒən/ /ˈɑːksɪdʒən/| n. |氧气|A colorless, odorless, tasteless gas that is the chemical element of atomic number 8 and the most abundant element in the earth's crust, forming about 20% of the air. It is essential for respiration in most living organisms.|例句：Oxygen is essential for life. 氧气对生命至关重要。|例句：Plants produce oxygen during photosynthesis. 植物在光合作用过程中产生氧气。|近义词：O2|反义词:None.
