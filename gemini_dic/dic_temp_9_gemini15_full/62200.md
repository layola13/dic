encounterer:/ɪnˈkaʊntərər/| n.|遭遇者，邂逅者|A person who encounters something or someone.  Someone who experiences an event or person.|例句：The encounterer of the bear was badly shaken.  与熊遭遇的人受到了很大的惊吓。|例句：She's an experienced encounterer of diverse cultures. 她是一位经验丰富的，接触过各种不同文化的人。|近义词：experiencer, witness|反义词：avoider
