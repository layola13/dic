/aʊz/| v.| 浏览；仔细察看；随意吃草 | To browse means to look through a collection of things, such as books or websites, in a casual way, or to graze on plants. | 例句：I like to browse the bookstore for new releases.  我喜欢浏览书店寻找新书。

例句：The sheep were browsing in the meadow.  羊群在草地上吃草。|近义词：peruse, graze, scan |反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They browse the internet for news every day. = 他们每天浏览互联网获取新闻。
He browses through the library's collection. = 他浏览图书馆的藏书。


一般过去时:
She browsed the shops for a birthday present. = 她逛商店为生日礼物挑选。
We browsed through the antique store. = 我们浏览了古董店。


现在完成时:
I have browsed several websites looking for information. = 我已经浏览了几个网站寻找信息。
They have browsed the entire catalog. = 他们已经浏览了整个目录。


过去完成时:
He had browsed the menu before the waiter arrived. = 服务员到达之前他已经浏览了菜单。
She had browsed many books before finding the right one. = 她浏览了很多书才找到合适的。


将来时:
I will browse the online store for clothes tomorrow. = 我明天将浏览网店购买衣服。
We will browse the museum's exhibits. = 我们将浏览博物馆的展品。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would browse the art gallery. = 如果我有更多时间，我会浏览艺术馆。
I wish I could browse the bookshop for hours. = 我希望我能花几个小时在书店浏览。


被动语态:
The website is browsed by millions of people daily. = 这个网站每天被数百万人在浏览。
The books were browsed by many students. = 这些书被许多学生浏览过。


疑问句:
Do you often browse the internet? = 你经常浏览互联网吗？
Will you browse the market for fresh produce? = 你会在市场上浏览寻找新鲜农产品吗？


否定句:
I don't browse social media often. = 我不经常浏览社交媒体。
He didn't browse the newspaper this morning. = 他今天早上没有浏览报纸。
