mulling:/ˈmʌlɪŋ/| v.|仔细考虑，沉思|To think deeply and carefully about something for a period of time.|例句：I'm still mulling over your offer. 我还在仔细考虑你的提议。|例句：She spent the evening mulling over her options. 她花了一晚上仔细考虑她的选择。|近义词：pondering, considering, contemplating, reflecting|反义词：deciding, rushing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is mulling over the problem. = 他正在仔细考虑这个问题。
They are mulling their options. = 他们在仔细考虑他们的选择。

一般过去时:
I mulled over the decision for days. = 我考虑了这个决定好几天。
She mulled the idea in her mind. = 她反复思考着这个主意。

现在完成时:
I have been mulling this over for weeks. = 我已经考虑这个好几个星期了。
They have mulled over the proposal. = 他们已经仔细考虑了这个提案。

过去完成时:
He had mulled over the situation before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经仔细考虑过这种情况了。
She had been mulling it over for months before she finally decided. = 在她最终决定之前，她已经考虑了几个月了。

将来时:
I will be mulling it over tonight. = 我今晚会仔细考虑这件事。
They will mull the matter over before responding. = 他们会在回应之前仔细考虑这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would mull it over more carefully. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更仔细地考虑它。
I wish I had more time to mull over the proposal. = 我希望我有更多时间来仔细考虑这个提案。

被动语态:  (Mull doesn't typically get used in the passive voice because it implies active thinking.)


疑问句:
Are you still mulling over the contract? = 你还在考虑合同吗？
What are they mulling over now? = 他们现在正在考虑什么？


否定句:
He wasn't mulling over the details, he was ignoring them. = 他没有仔细考虑细节，而是忽略了它们。
She didn't mull over the consequences; she acted impulsively. = 她没有考虑后果，而是冲动行事。


PHRASE->
mull over: 仔细考虑
mull something through:  彻底考虑
mull something around:  在脑中反复思考
mull things through:  认真地思考
mulling it all over: 全盘考虑
