forelend:/ˈfɔːrlɛnd/| n.| 岬，海角 | A foreland is a headland or promontory; a point of land extending into the sea.  | Example: The ship rounded the foreland and entered the sheltered bay.  这艘船绕过海角驶入平静的海湾。 | 近义词：headland, promontory, cape | 反义词：inland
