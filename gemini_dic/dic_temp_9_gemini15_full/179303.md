redness:/ˈrɛdnɪs/ | n. | 红色；红润；发红 | The state or quality of being red.  It can refer to the color itself, or to a condition causing something to appear red, such as inflammation. | 例句：The redness of her cheeks suggested she was embarrassed. 她脸颊的红润暗示她很尴尬。

The redness of the sunset was breathtaking.  日落的红色令人叹为观止。


近义词：ruddiness, blush, inflammation | 反义词：paleness, whiteness | 词性：名词
