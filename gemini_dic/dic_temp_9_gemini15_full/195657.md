It often refers to methods, arguments, or systems that are mostly but not entirely precise or formally correct.  | Example: The paper presented a semirigorous proof, relying on some intuitive leaps rather than fully formal steps.  这篇论文提供了一个半严谨的证明，依赖于一些直觉的飞跃，而不是完全正式的步骤。| 近义词：impreciseness, inexactness, looseness | 反义词：rigor, precision, exactness | 名词
