The powers must be non-negative integers.  For example, 3x² + 2x - 5 is a polynomial.| Example: The solution to this equation requires understanding of polynomial factorization.  这个方程的解需要理解多项式的因式分解。|近义词: multinomial (in some contexts)|反义词: monomial (single-term polynomial)
