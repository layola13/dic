re-notify:/ˌriːˈnoʊtɪfaɪ/| vt.|重新通知；再次通知|To notify again; to inform someone again of something.|例句：We need to re-notify all customers of the price increase.我们需要再次通知所有客户价格上涨的消息。|例句：The supplier re-notified us of the delayed shipment. 供应商再次通知我们货物延误。|近义词：reinform, remind, advise again|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-notify customers of any changes. = 他们会再次通知客户任何更改。
The company re-notifies its clients regularly. = 公司定期再次通知其客户。

一般过去时:
The bank re-notified me about the overdue payment. = 银行再次通知我关于逾期付款的事宜。
He re-notified her of the meeting time. = 他再次通知她会议时间。

现在完成时:
They have re-notified all participants of the schedule change. = 他们已经再次通知所有参与者日程安排的更改。
We have re-notified the relevant authorities. = 我们已经再次通知了相关部门。

过去完成时:
They had re-notified the clients before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经再次通知了客户。
She had re-notified him several times, but he still didn't respond. = 她已经再次通知了他好几次，但他仍然没有回应。

将来时:
We will re-notify you as soon as we have more information. = 我们一旦有更多信息就会再次通知您。
The company will re-notify customers about the product recall. = 公司将再次通知客户关于产品召回的事宜。

虚拟语气:
If they re-notified us earlier, we could have avoided the problem. = 如果他们早点再次通知我们，我们就能避免这个问题。
It would be helpful if they re-notified us of any updates. = 如果他们能再次通知我们任何更新，那将很有帮助。

被动语态:
The customers were re-notified about the delay. = 客户再次收到了关于延误的通知。
The participants were re-notified via email. = 参与者通过电子邮件再次收到了通知。

疑问句:
Have they re-notified the stakeholders? = 他们是否再次通知了利益相关者？
Will you re-notify me if there are any changes? = 如果有任何变化，你会再次通知我吗？

否定句:
They did not re-notify the customers about the shipping delay. = 他们没有再次通知客户关于发货延误的事宜。
We have not yet re-notified the relevant parties. = 我们尚未再次通知相关方。
