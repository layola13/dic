helmswoman: /ˈhɛlmzwʊmən/ | n. | 女舵手，女掌舵人 | A woman who steers a ship or aircraft.  It is a compound word combining "helm" (the steering apparatus of a ship or aircraft) and "woman". | Example:  The helmswoman skillfully navigated the ship through the storm.  女舵手熟练地驾驶着船只穿过暴风雨。  | 近义词：female pilot (for aircraft), woman captain (depending on context)| 反义词：helmsman
