over-literal: /ˌoʊvərˈlɪtərəl/ | adj. | 过于字面化的；死板的 |  Taking words too literally; lacking flexibility in interpretation.  It implies a rigid and inflexible understanding of language, often missing the implied meaning or context. | 例句：His over-literal interpretation of the poem missed its subtle humor.  他的诗歌解读过于字面化，错过了其微妙的幽默感。  His over-literal approach to the instructions led to a misunderstanding. 他对指令过于死板的理解导致了误解。 | 近义词：literal-minded, pedantic, inflexible, rigid | 反义词：figurative, nuanced, flexible, imaginative |
