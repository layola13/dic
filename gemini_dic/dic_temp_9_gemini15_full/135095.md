nonobservance:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːvəns/ | n. |不遵守；未遵守；忽视 |The failure to observe or comply with a law, rule, or custom.  It refers to the act of not following or paying attention to something that should be observed or followed. |例句：The nonobservance of safety regulations led to the accident.  不遵守安全规章导致了事故。  The nonobservance of the deadline resulted in a penalty.  未遵守最后期限导致了处罚。 |近义词： disregard, neglect, violation, breach |反义词： observance, compliance, adherence
