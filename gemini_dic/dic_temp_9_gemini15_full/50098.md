dentigerous:/dɛnˈtɪdʒərəs/ | adj. |含牙的；长牙的|Containing or associated with a tooth, especially an unerupted tooth.  The term often refers to a dentigerous cyst, which is a fluid-filled sac that surrounds the crown of an unerupted tooth.|Example: The radiograph revealed a dentigerous cyst around the impacted third molar.  X光片显示第三磨牙周围有一个含牙囊肿。  The patient presented with a dentigerous cyst causing facial swelling. 病人出现含牙囊肿导致面部肿胀。|近义词：containing a tooth, tooth-bearing|反义词：toothless, edentulous
