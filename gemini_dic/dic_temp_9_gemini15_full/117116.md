mealy-mouthedness: /ˈmiːliˌmaʊθtədnəs/ | n. | 吞吞吐吐；说话含糊其辞；委婉说法 |  The act of speaking evasively or indirectly, especially to avoid causing offense or expressing unpleasant truths; characterized by insincerity or lack of candor.  | 例句：His mealy-mouthedness prevented him from telling her the bad news directly.  他的吞吞吐吐让他无法直接告诉她坏消息。


例句：The politician's mealy-mouthed response to the scandal failed to satisfy the public.  这位政治家对丑闻含糊其辞的回应未能令公众满意。


近义词：euphemism, equivocation, circumlocution, prevarication, obfuscation


反义词：candor, frankness, directness, outspokenness


词性: 名词
