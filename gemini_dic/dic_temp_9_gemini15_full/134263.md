noninflationary:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfleɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 非通货膨胀的，抑制通货膨胀的 | Not causing or characterized by inflation.  It refers to economic policies or conditions that do not lead to a general increase in the price level of goods and services. | Example: The government implemented noninflationary fiscal policies to stabilize the economy.  政府实施了抑制通货膨胀的财政政策以稳定经济。 The central bank adopted a noninflationary monetary policy to control inflation. 中央银行采取了非通货膨胀的货币政策来控制通货膨胀。|近义词：anti-inflationary, deflationary (in some contexts)|反义词：inflationary
