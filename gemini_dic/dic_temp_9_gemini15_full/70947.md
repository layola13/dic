firefighting: /ˈfaɪərˌfaɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 消防；灭火 | The process of extinguishing fires.  It involves the coordinated efforts of firefighters to control and suppress fires using various techniques and equipment. | 例句：The firefighting team worked tirelessly to contain the wildfire.  消防队夜以继日地努力控制野火。

例句：Effective firefighting requires training and proper equipment.  有效的灭火需要训练和合适的设备。


近义词：fire suppression, fire control | 反义词：arson (纵火)


词性: 名词
