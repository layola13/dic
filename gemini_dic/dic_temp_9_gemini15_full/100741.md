intransitive: /ɪnˈtrænzɪtɪv/ | adj. | 不及物的 |  An intransitive verb is a verb that does not take a direct object.  In other words, the action of the verb does not act upon something or someone else. |

例句：
The baby sleeps soundly.  婴儿睡得很香。

The bird sings beautifully.  鸟儿歌唱得真美。


近义词：non-transitive


反义词：transitive


词性: adjective
