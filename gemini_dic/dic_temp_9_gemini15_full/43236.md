crackled:/ˈkrækəld/| v.|爆裂声，劈啪作响|To make a series of short, sharp cracking sounds.|The fire crackled merrily in the hearth.壁炉里的火发出愉快的噼啪声。 The old wooden chair crackled as he sat down.他坐下时，旧木椅发出噼啪声。|creaked, snapped, popped|silent, smooth|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wood crackles in the fireplace. = 木头在壁炉里噼啪作响。
The radio crackles with static. = 收音机发出静电噼啪声。

The branch crackled and broke. = 树枝噼啪一声断了。
