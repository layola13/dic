fishbowl:/ˈfɪʃboʊl/| n. |鱼缸；鱼钵|A transparent bowl, usually made of glass, used to keep fish.  It can also refer to a situation or environment that is highly visible and lacks privacy.|例句：The goldfish swam lazily in its fishbowl. 金鱼懒洋洋地在鱼缸里游动。

The small town felt like a fishbowl, with everyone knowing everyone else's business.  这个小镇感觉像个鱼缸，每个人都知道其他人的事。|近义词：aquarium, tank |反义词：privacy, seclusion
