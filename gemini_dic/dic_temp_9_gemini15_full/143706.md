outlie:/ˈaʊtlaɪ/| v.|概述；略述；位于…之外|To give a brief account of something; to be situated outside of something.|The professor outlined the main points of the lecture. 教授概述了讲座的主要内容。  The small village lies outlined against the mountain. 小村庄位于山外。|summarize, sketch, abstract|—|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outlines his plan every morning. 他每天早上概述他的计划。
She outlines the key features of the product. 她概述了产品的关键特性。


一般过去时:
The author outlined the plot of the novel. 作者概述了小说的情节。
They outlined their concerns to the manager. 他们向经理概述了他们的担忧。


现在完成时:
She has outlined her research findings in the report. 她已在报告中概述了她的研究结果。
We have outlined the steps needed to complete the project. 我们已经概述了完成项目所需的步骤。


过去完成时:
He had outlined his argument before the debate began. 在辩论开始之前，他已经概述了他的论点。
They had outlined the risks involved before making the decision. 他们在做出决定之前已经概述了所涉及的风险。


将来时:
She will outline her strategy at the next meeting. 她将在下次会议上概述她的战略。
He will outline the new policy to the employees. 他将向员工概述这项新政策。


虚拟语气:
If I were to outline my ideas, it would take hours. 如果我要概述我的想法，那将需要几个小时。
I wish I could outline the problem more clearly. 我希望我能更清晰地概述这个问题。


被动语态:
The main points were outlined in the introduction. 主要要点在引言中概述。
The plan was outlined by the team leader. 该计划由团队领导概述。


疑问句:
How will you outline your presentation? 你将如何概述你的演示文稿？
Can you outline the benefits of this approach? 你能概述这种方法的好处吗？


否定句:
He did not outline the details of the agreement. 他没有概述协议的细节。
She failed to outline her reasons for leaving. 她未能概述她离职的原因。


PHRASE->
