flame-proof:/ˈfleɪm pruːf/| adj.| 防火的，耐火的 | Resistant to damage or destruction by fire.  The material is treated to be flame-proof, making it suitable for use in high-risk environments.  |近义词：fireproof, fire-resistant | 反义词：flammable, combustible


例句:

This flame-proof suit protects firefighters from intense heat. 这个防火服保护消防员免受高温。

The building's structure is flame-proof, ensuring safety in case of fire. 建筑结构是防火的，确保火灾时的安全。
