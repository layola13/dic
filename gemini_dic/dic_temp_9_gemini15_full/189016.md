The term can also describe something related to the Sabbath or its observance.|例句：Many sabbatarians abstain from work and recreational activities on Sundays. 许多遵守安息日的人在星期天避免工作和娱乐活动。

The town is known for its large population of sabbatarians. 这个城镇以其众多遵守安息日的人而闻名。|近义词：Sabbath observer, Sabbath keeper|反义词：non-Sabbatarian
