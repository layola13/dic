manhandle:/ˈmænˌhændl/| vt.|粗暴地对待；用力搬动|To handle roughly or violently; to move (something heavy) with difficulty. |例句：He manhandled the heavy box into the truck. 他粗暴地把沉重的箱子搬进了卡车。
例句：The protesters were manhandled by the police.  抗议者遭到警察粗暴对待。|近义词：roughhandle, mishandle, maltreat|反义词：handle gently, treat delicately|动词
