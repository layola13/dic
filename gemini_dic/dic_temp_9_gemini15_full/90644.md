humanizers:/ˈhjuːmənˌaɪzərz/ | n. |使人性化的人或事物 |Things or people that make something or someone more human, less mechanical or impersonal.  They emphasize the human element in a situation or process. |例句：The company introduced humanizers into their customer service to improve client relationships. 公司在客户服务中引入了人性化元素来改善客户关系。


例句：The use of storytelling in the presentation acted as humanizers, connecting with the audience on an emotional level.  在演示文稿中使用故事讲述起到了人性化的作用，在情感层面与观众建立了联系。


近义词：humanizing agents, empathizers, personalizers


反义词：dehumanizers, depersonalizers, mechanizers


词性:名词
