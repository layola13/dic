holidayed:/ˈhɒlədeɪd/ /ˈhɑːlədeɪd/| v.|度假；休假 |To spend a holiday or holidays.  It is the past tense of the verb "holiday".|例句：We holidayed in Spain last summer. 我们去年夏天在西班牙度假了。

例句：They holidayed in the mountains for a week. 他们在山里度假了一周。


近义词：vacationed, took a holiday, went on holiday


反义词：worked, toiled


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I holiday in Greece every year. = 我每年都在希腊度假。
She holidays in Bali with her family. = 她和家人在巴厘岛度假。

一般过去时:
We holidayed in Italy last year. = 我们去年在意大利度假了。
They holidayed in the Caribbean for two weeks. = 他们在加勒比海度假了两周。

现在完成时:
I have holidayed in many countries. = 我去过很多国家度假。
She has holidayed in France several times. = 她去过法国几次度假。

过去完成时:
We had holidayed in Mexico before we visited Costa Rica. = 我们在去哥斯达黎加之前去过墨西哥度假。
They had holidayed in Hawaii twice before this trip. = 在这次旅行之前，他们去过夏威夷两次度假。

将来时:
We will holiday in Japan next year. = 我们明年将在日本度假。
They will holiday in Scotland during the summer. = 他们将在夏天去苏格兰度假。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would holiday in the Maldives. = 如果我有更多钱，我会去马尔代夫度假。
I wish I could holiday in a tropical paradise. = 我希望我能去热带天堂度假。


被动语态:
(The verb "holiday" is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Where did you holiday last year? = 你去年去哪里度假了？
Where will you holiday this summer? = 今年夏天你打算去哪里度假？


否定句:
We didn't holiday this year. = 我们今年没有度假。
They haven't holidayed abroad for many years. = 他们很多年没有出国度过假期了。
