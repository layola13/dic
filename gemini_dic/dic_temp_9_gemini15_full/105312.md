kibitz:/ˈkɪbɪts/| v.|插嘴，旁观者评论|To offer unsolicited advice or comments, especially while watching a game or other activity.|例句：Don't kibitz while I'm trying to concentrate!  别在我试图集中注意力的时候插嘴！|例句：The kibitzers were offering helpful (and unhelpful) suggestions.  旁观者们提供了有帮助的（和没帮助的）建议。|近义词：comment, interject, butt in, chip in|反义词：remain silent, keep quiet
