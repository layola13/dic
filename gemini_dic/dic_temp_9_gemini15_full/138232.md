nosophobia:/ˌnoʊsəˈfoʊbiə/| n. |对疾病的恐惧症；疾病恐惧症|An irrational fear of disease or illness.  It often manifests as excessive worry about contracting a specific disease or illness, leading to avoidance behaviors and anxiety.|Example: Her nosophobia prevented her from going to crowded places, fearing she might catch a contagious disease.  她患有疾病恐惧症，因此避免去人多的地方，害怕感染传染病。|近义词：pathophobia, germophobia|反义词：None (it's a phobia, not a positive state)|名词
