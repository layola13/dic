slimnastics: /ˌslɪmˈnæstɪks/ | n. |纤体操，瘦身操 | A form of exercise designed to improve body shape and reduce body fat, often involving a combination of low-impact aerobic exercises, strength training, and flexibility exercises.  It's a blend of "slim" and "gymnastics," suggesting exercises that aim for a slimmer physique. | Example:  She's been attending slimnastics classes twice a week to tone her muscles and lose weight. 她每周去纤体操班两次以锻炼肌肉和减肥。|近义词：body sculpting, fitness training, weight loss exercises |反义词：weight gain exercises, bodybuilding (in the sense of maximizing muscle mass)
