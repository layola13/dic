notations:/noʊˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 符号；注释；记号 |  Symbols, characters, or marks used to represent something, especially in a system of writing, mathematics, or music.  They are also brief notes or explanations added to a text. | Example: The musical notations were difficult to decipher.  乐谱上的记号很难辨认。

The mathematical notations in the equation were quite complex. 方程式中的数学符号非常复杂。


近义词：symbols, marks, signs, comments, annotations

反义词：None directly opposite, but "absence of notations" could be considered an antonym.


词源:  From Middle French *notation, from Latin *notatio, from notare "to mark," from nota "mark, note."
