pomelo:/ˈpɒmɛloʊ/ /ˈpɑːmeloʊ/| n. |柚子|A large citrus fruit with a thick rind and a pale yellow to pinkish flesh, similar to a grapefruit but larger and sweeter.|例句：I bought a pomelo at the Asian market. 我在亚洲市场买了一个柚子。|例句：The pomelo salad was a refreshing start to the meal.  柚子沙拉是这顿饭令人耳目一新的开端。|近义词：grapefruit, shaddock|反义词：无
