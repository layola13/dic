jacketing:/ˈdʒækɪtɪŋ/ | n. |  外套；护套；包皮；(电缆等的)护层 | The protective covering or casing of something.  This can refer to a physical jacket, a protective layer around a cable, or even a figurative layer of protection. |例句：The building required extensive jacketing to improve its insulation.  建筑物需要大规模的包层以改善其隔热性能。

例句：The electrical cables were encased in a fire-resistant jacketing. 电缆被包裹在耐火护套中。


近义词：covering, casing, sheath, lining, encasement | 反义词：exposure, unveiling


词源：源自于单词"jacket"，加上后缀"-ing"构成名词，表示"外套"或"包层"的动作或结果。  The word ultimately derives from the Old French "jaque," a type of close-fitting coat.
