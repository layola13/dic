disavaunce:/dɪsədˈvɑːns/| v.| 预付；垫付；提前支付 | To pay or give something in advance.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. It implies a payment made before something is due or received.| Example:  The landlord disavanced the tenant's rent for the next quarter. = 房东预付了房客下个季度的房租。 | 近义词：advance, prepay, pay in advance | 反义词：postpay, delay payment | 动词
