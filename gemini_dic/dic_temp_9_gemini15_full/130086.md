non-priority: /ˌnɒnpraɪˈɒrəti/ | adj. | 非优先级，次要的 |  Describes something that is not considered important or urgent and should be dealt with after more important matters.  | Example:  We'll address the non-priority issues after completing the urgent tasks.我们将完成紧急任务后再处理非优先级问题。 | 近义词：secondary, less important, unimportant, minor | 反义词：priority, crucial, essential, vital


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses.  Its usage is primarily attributive (placed before the noun it modifies).

Example 1:  The non-priority project was postponed.  非优先级项目被推迟了。
Example 2:  We are dealing with non-priority requests today. 我们今天正在处理非优先级请求。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "non-priority". However, phrases incorporating the concept of non-priority include:

1. lower priority:  较低的优先级
2. less important tasks:  不太重要的任务
3. secondary concerns: 次要问题
4. can wait: 可以等待
5. put on the back burner: 把…搁置
