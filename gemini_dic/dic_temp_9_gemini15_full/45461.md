curatively:/ˈkjʊrətɪvli/ | adv. |治疗地；治癒地 |In a way that cures or heals.  It describes the manner in which something is done, focusing on its curative effect. |Example: The doctor treated the wound curatively, aiming for complete healing.  医生治疗伤口的方式是着眼于完全治愈。 The disease was treated curatively, resulting in a full recovery.  这种疾病得到了治愈性的治疗，最终完全康复了。|近义词：therapeutically, remedially |反义词：palliatively (缓解性地)
