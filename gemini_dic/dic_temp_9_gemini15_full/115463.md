manufacturabilities:/ˌmænjuˌfæktʃərəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可制造性，可生产性 |The degree to which a product or system can be easily and economically manufactured.  It considers factors like design, materials, processes, and tooling.  High manufacturability leads to efficient production and lower costs.|例句：The engineers focused on improving the manufacturability of the new design to reduce production costs. 工程师们专注于提高新设计的可制造性以降低生产成本。

The company prioritized manufacturability during the product development phase.  这家公司在产品开发阶段优先考虑了可制造性。|近义词：producibility, feasibility, workability |反义词：unmanufacturability, infeasibility
