logistically:/ləˈdʒɪstɪkli/| adv.|  从后勤角度；在后勤方面 | In a way that relates to logistics; from the perspective of logistics.  It describes how something is done in terms of organization, planning, and resources needed for transportation, supply, etc. | Example: Logistically, the project is a challenge, requiring coordination across multiple countries. 从后勤角度来看，这个项目是一个挑战，需要跨多个国家进行协调。|近义词：operationally, strategically | 反义词：chaotically, haphazardly
