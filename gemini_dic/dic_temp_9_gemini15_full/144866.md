over-ran:/ˌoʊvərˈræn/ | v. |超过；泛滥；蔓延；控制不住 | To extend beyond the limits of; to spread or grow rapidly and uncontrollably; to exceed (a limit or boundary); to overwhelm or overrun. |例句：The weeds overran the garden.  杂草蔓延了整个花园。

The army overran the enemy's territory.  军队占领了敌人的领土。


近义词：invaded, flooded, overwhelmed, surpassed | 反义词：underestimated, controlled, contained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weeds overrun the garden every year. = 杂草每年都蔓延到花园里。
The project overruns its budget. = 这个项目超支了。


一般过去时:
The flood overran the village. = 洪水淹没了村庄。
The rebels overran the government forces. = 反叛军击败了政府军。


现在完成时:
The rumors have overran the town. = 谣言在镇上迅速传开。
The disease has overran the region. = 疾病蔓延了整个地区。


过去完成时:
The fire had overran the forest before firefighters arrived. = 消防员到达之前，大火已经蔓延到了森林里。
The army had overran the city before the reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，军队已经占领了城市。


将来时:
The river will overrun its banks if it rains heavily. = 如果下大雨，河水将会泛滥。
If we don't act now, the problem will overrun our resources. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，这个问题将会耗尽我们的资源。


虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
