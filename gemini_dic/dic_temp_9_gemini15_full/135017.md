nonnegotiabilities:/ˌnɒn.nɪˌɡoʊʃiəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |不可协商的事项，非议价项目 |The things that cannot be negotiated or changed.  This term often refers to crucial aspects of a contract, agreement, or principle that are not open to discussion or alteration. |Example:  The deadlines for the project are nonnegotiabilities; we must meet them.  项目截止日期是不可协商的；我们必须按时完成。|近义词：inalienables, essentials, fixed terms |反义词：negotiables, flexibilities
