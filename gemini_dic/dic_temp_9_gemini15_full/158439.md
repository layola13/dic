plebes:/ˈpliːbz/| n.|平民；新生；下层阶级| A pleb is a commoner, especially a member of the lower social classes.  The term is often used informally and can be considered derogatory. In the context of military academies, it refers to first-year students. | Example:  He considered himself above the plebes. 他认为自己高于平民/新生。 The plebes were put through rigorous training. 新生们接受了严格的训练。|近义词：commoner, peasant, freshman (in military context) |反义词：aristocrat, elite, upper class
