fragrancy:/ˈfreɪɡrənsi/| n. |香味，芬芳|The pleasant smell of something, especially a flower or perfume.  It refers to a strong and delightful scent.|The fragrancy of the roses filled the garden. 玫瑰的芬芳充满了花园。 The air was filled with the fragrancy of pine and damp earth. 空气中弥漫着松树和潮湿泥土的芬芳。|aroma, scent, perfume|stench, odor (in a negative context)|名词
