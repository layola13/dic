sik:/sɪk/| adj.| 恶心的；有病的；令人厌恶的 |Sick refers to a state of illness or feeling unwell,  or something that causes disgust or revulsion.|例句：I'm feeling sick. 我感觉不舒服。|例句：That smell is sick!  那个气味真恶心！|近义词：ill, unwell, diseased, nauseous, disgusting |反义词：healthy, well, pleasant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is sick today. = 他今天病了。
The food smells sick. = 这食物闻起来很恶心。

一般过去时:
She was sick last week. = 她上周病了。
I felt sick after eating that. = 吃了那个之后我感觉恶心。

现在完成时:
He has been sick for three days. = 他已经病了三天了。
I have been feeling sick all morning. = 我整个上午都感觉不舒服。

过去完成时:
She had been sick for a week before she went to the doctor. = 她去看医生之前已经病了一个星期了。
I had felt sick before I threw up. = 我呕吐之前就感觉恶心了。

将来时:
I think I will be sick. = 我想我会吐的。
She will probably be sick if she eats that. = 如果她吃那个，她可能会生病。

虚拟语气:
If I were sick, I would stay home. = 如果我病了，我会待在家里。
I wish I weren't sick. = 我希望我没有生病。

被动语态:
The patient was declared sick. = 病人被宣布患病。
The food was considered sick. = 这食物被认为令人作呕。

疑问句:
Are you sick? = 你生病了吗？
Is the food sick? = 这食物令人作呕吗？

否定句:
I am not sick. = 我没有生病。
The food is not sick. = 这食物不令人作呕。
