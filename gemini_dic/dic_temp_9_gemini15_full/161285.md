postnuclear: /ˌpoʊstˈnuːkliər/ | adj. |  后核时代的；核后期的；战后核时代的 | Relating to the period following a nuclear war or major nuclear accident.  It often implies the lasting effects and consequences of such events. |

例句：

The postnuclear landscape was desolate and scarred. = 后核时代的景象荒凉而满目疮痍。

Postnuclear anxieties about radiation contamination lingered for decades. = 对核辐射污染的担忧在战后持续了几十年。


近义词：post-atomic,  after-nuclear (less common)


反义词：prenuclear, pre-atomic


词性：形容词
