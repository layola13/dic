oligarchize:/ˈɒlɪɡɑːkaɪz/| vt.|使寡头统治；使成为寡头政治|To establish or maintain the rule of a small group of powerful people.|The country was oligarchized after the coup, with power concentrated in the hands of a few military leaders.  这个国家在政变后被寡头统治，权力掌握在少数几名军事领导手中。|近义词：monopolize, dominate, control|反义词：democratize
