ministrant: /ˈmɪnɪstrənt/ | n. | 助理；服务人员；侍奉者 | A person who attends to or serves another, especially in a religious context.  It often implies a subordinate role in assisting a higher authority or performing tasks in support of a larger operation. |例句：The ministrant helped the priest prepare for the ceremony.  = 助理帮助牧师准备仪式。


The ministrants diligently cleaned the church before the service. = 服务人员在仪式前认真打扫教堂。


近义词：attendant, assistant, server, helper | 反义词：principal, superior, leader
