obbligato:/ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/| /ˌɑːblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/| adj. n.|  义务的；必须的；(音乐)  obbligato |  a musical passage that is obligatory; a necessary part; a required element | 例句：The oboe part in this concerto is an obbligato. 这首协奏曲中的 oboe 部分是 obbligato。  The obbligato section requires a skilled performer.  这段 obbligato 部分需要一位技艺精湛的演奏者。|近义词：required, necessary, mandatory |反义词：optional, voluntary
