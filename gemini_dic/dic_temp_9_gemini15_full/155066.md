petrify: /ˈpɛtrɪfaɪ/ | vt.  adj. | 石化；使惊呆；使僵硬 | to turn into stone; to shock or horrify someone so much that they are unable to move or react | 近义词：terrify, paralyze, stun | 反义词：calm, soothe, excite

英文解析:
As a verb, "petrify" means to turn something into stone or to make someone so frightened or shocked that they are unable to move or react. As an adjective, it means having been turned to stone or being extremely frightened or shocked.  The verb can also describe the process of becoming emotionally numb or unresponsive.

例句:
The ancient city was petrified by a volcanic eruption.  = 古老的城市被火山爆发石化了。

The horrifying news petrified her. = 令人恐惧的消息使她惊呆了。
