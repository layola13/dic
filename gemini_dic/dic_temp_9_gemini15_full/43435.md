crappy:/ˈkræpi/ | adj. |糟糕的，劣质的 |  Describes something of poor quality, unpleasant, or unsatisfactory. It is an informal and often emphatic term. |例句：The food at the restaurant was crappy.  这家餐馆的食物很糟糕。

例句：This crappy weather is ruining my vacation.  这糟糕的天气毁了我的假期。


近义词：awful, terrible, lousy, poor, substandard | 反义词：excellent, superb, fantastic, great, wonderful

词源:
The word "crappy" is a slang term.  Its origin is uncertain, but it likely evolved from "crap," which itself has a long and varied history as a vulgar term referring to excrement. The "-y" suffix adds an intensifying and adjectival quality.
