one:/wʌn/| /wən/ | num. pron. adj.|一，一个；一个… |The word "one" is a numeral, pronoun, and adjective used to represent the number or quantity of a single unit. It can function as a noun, replacing a previously mentioned noun to avoid repetition, or as an adjective modifying a noun. | I have one apple. 我有一个苹果。|  There is only one solution. 只有一种解决办法。| two, single | many, zero | numeral, pronoun, adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have one apple. = 我有一个苹果。
There is one cat in the garden. = 花园里有一只猫。


一般过去时:
I ate one cookie. = 我吃了一块饼干。

现在完成时:

He has written one song. = 他已经写了一首歌。


过去完成时:
I had already read one book before she arrived. = 她来之前我已经读完了一本书。

将来时:
I will buy one car next year. = 我明年会买一辆车。

虚拟语气:

I wish I had one more chance. = 我希望我还有另一个机会。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
