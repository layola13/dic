rima:/ˈriːmə/| n. |韵，押韵；裂缝，裂隙|A rima is a pair of rhyming lines in a poem.  It can also refer to a crack or fissure in a surface. |例句：The poem's beauty lies in its intricate rimas. 这首诗的美在于其复杂的韵律。|例句：A deep rima appeared in the ancient wall. 古老的墙上出现了一道深深的裂缝。|近义词：rhyme, verse, crack, fissure |反义词：prose (in the poetic sense)
