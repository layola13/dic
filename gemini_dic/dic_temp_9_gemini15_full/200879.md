slinkier:/ˈslɪŋkiər/| adj. |更柔软的；更蛇形的；更偷偷摸摸的 | More flexible, more sinuous, more stealthy.  It describes something that is more slender, graceful, and subtly alluring than something else.  This word often implies a suggestive movement or a quality of being sly or secretive. |例句：The slinkier dress accentuated her figure. 这件更柔软的裙子突显了她的身材。

The slinkier cat crept through the tall grass. 那只更偷偷摸摸的猫在高高的草丛中潜行。


近义词：sleeker, more supple, more serpentine, subtler

反义词：stiff, rigid, clumsy, blatant

词性:形容词

词源:
