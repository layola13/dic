secessionist: /səˈseʃənɪst/ | n. | 分裂主义者，分离主义者 | A person who advocates or supports secession, especially from a political union.  A secessionist believes that a group (e.g., a state, region, or people) should separate from a larger entity. | Example:  The secessionist movement gained momentum after the government imposed new taxes. 分裂主义运动在政府征收新税后势头增强。 |近义词：separatist, autonomist | 反义词：unionist, integrationist | 名词
