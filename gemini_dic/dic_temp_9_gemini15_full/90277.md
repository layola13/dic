housekeeping:/ˈhaʊskiːpɪŋ/ | n. |家务管理；内部事务管理 |The management of a household; the work of keeping a house clean and tidy; the administrative tasks of an organization.|例句：Daily housekeeping chores include cleaning, cooking, and laundry. 日常家务包括清洁、烹饪和洗衣。  |例句：The hotel offers excellent housekeeping services. 这家酒店提供优质的客房服务。|近义词：homemaking, domestic work, administration |反义词：neglect, disorder

词源:
Housekeeping comes from the combination of "house" and "keeping".  "House" refers to a dwelling, and "keeping" means maintaining or managing.  Therefore, housekeeping literally means "maintaining a house." The word's evolution reflects the historical importance of domestic management and upkeep.
