in-range:/ˌɪnˈreɪndʒ/| adj.| 范围内的，在范围内 |Within a specified range or limits.  Something that is in-range is within the acceptable or operational parameters. |例句：The target is in range of our artillery.我们的炮兵可以攻击到目标。

The temperature is in range for optimal plant growth.  温度在植物最佳生长范围内。


近义词：within range, acceptable, suitable |反义词：out of range, unacceptable, unsuitable |词性：adjective
