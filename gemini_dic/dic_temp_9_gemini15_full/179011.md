redelegation: /ˌriːˌdɛləˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 再委派，重新委派 | The act of delegating a task or responsibility again, often to a different person or group.  This usually happens when the initial delegation was unsuccessful or inappropriate. | Example: The project manager's initial delegation proved ineffective, necessitating a redelegation of tasks.  项目经理最初的委派被证明无效，需要重新委派任务。|近义词:reassignment, reassignment of duties|反义词:centralization,  direct management|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company redelegates tasks regularly to improve efficiency. = 公司定期重新委派任务以提高效率。
Redelegation often occurs when deadlines are missed. = 当错过最后期限时，经常会发生重新委派。


一般过去时:
The manager redelegated the project to a more experienced team. = 经理将项目重新委派给了一个更有经验的团队。
They redelegated the responsibility after realizing the initial assignee was overloaded. = 他们意识到最初的被委派者工作量过大后重新委派了责任。


现在完成时:
The board has redelegated several key responsibilities this year. = 董事会今年已重新委派了几项关键职责。
Management has redelegated the decision-making power to the lower levels. = 管理层已将决策权重新委派给下级。


过去完成时:
They had redelegated the project before the crisis hit. = 危机发生前，他们已经重新委派了项目。
The CEO had redelegated authority before going on leave. = 首席执行官在休假前已重新委派了权力。


将来时:
The team will redelegate the tasks if they cannot meet the deadline. = 如果团队无法按时完成任务，他们将重新委派任务。
We will redelegate the project to a different department next week. = 我们下周将把项目重新委派给不同的部门。


虚拟语气:
If the project failed, they would redelegate it immediately. = 如果项目失败，他们会立即重新委派。
I wish they had redelegated the task earlier. = 我希望他们早点重新委派任务。


被动语态:
The tasks were redelegated due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，任务被重新委派。
The responsibility was redelegated to ensure timely completion. = 为了确保及时完成，责任被重新委派。


疑问句:
Will the tasks be redelegated to another team? = 任务会被重新委派给另一个团队吗？
Was the project redelegated after the initial failure? = 项目在第一次失败后是否被重新委派？


否定句:
The project was not redelegated, despite the initial difficulties. = 尽管最初遇到困难，项目也没有被重新委派。
They did not redelegate the responsibility, leading to further delays. = 他们没有重新委派责任，导致进一步延误。
