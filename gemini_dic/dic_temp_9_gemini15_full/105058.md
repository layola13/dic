keratophyte:/ˈkɛrətəfaɪt/| n. |角质植物|A keratophyte is a plant that has a hardened, horn-like, or keratinous structure.  This term is most often used in reference to certain lichens and algae that produce a tough, protective layer.  The term is less common and sometimes considered outdated in modern botanical classifications.|The researchers found several species of keratophyte growing on the exposed rocks.|近义词: horn-like plant (informal)|反义词:  None specifically, but could be contrasted with plants lacking hardened structures.
