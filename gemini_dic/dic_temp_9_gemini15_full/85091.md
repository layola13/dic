hardballs: /ˈhɑːrdbɔːlz/ | n. | 硬球；严厉的批评；棘手的问题 |  A hardball is a baseball that is harder than usual, or figuratively, a difficult or unpleasant situation or challenge.  It can also refer to aggressive or ruthless tactics in negotiations or politics. |

例句：

He threw a hardball right at the batter. = 他朝击球手猛投了一个硬球。

The negotiations turned into a hardball game. = 谈判变成了你死我活的斗争。


近义词：tough questions, challenges, difficulties, aggressive tactics


反义词：softballs, easy questions, compromises


词性：名词
