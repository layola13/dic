cocomposers:/ˌkoʊkəmˈpoʊzərz/| n. |共同作曲者 |Those who compose music together are called cocomposers. They share the credit and responsibility for the musical work.  |Example: The two musicians were cocomposers of the award-winning symphony.  两位音乐家是获奖交响乐的共同作曲者。|近义词：collaborative composers, joint composers |反义词：sole composer, single composer
