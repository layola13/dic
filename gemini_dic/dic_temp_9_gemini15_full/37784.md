compete:/kəmˈpiːt/ | v. |竞争，对抗 |To strive to surpass another or others, to win something |例句：Many companies compete for the same customers. 许多公司都在争夺相同的客户。  |例句：The two athletes competed fiercely for the gold medal. 两位运动员为金牌激烈竞争。 |近义词：rival, contend, vie |反义词：cooperate, collaborate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They compete in the market every day. = 他们每天都在市场上竞争。
We compete for resources. = 我们为了资源而竞争。


一般过去时:
She competed in the Olympics. = 她参加了奥运会比赛。
He competed against his brother in the race. = 他在比赛中与他的兄弟竞争。


现在完成时:
They have competed in many tournaments. = 他们已经参加过许多比赛。
She has competed successfully many times. = 她已经成功地竞争过很多次。


过去完成时:
They had competed before, so they knew each other well. = 他们以前就竞争过，所以他们彼此很了解。
She had competed for years before winning the championship. = 在赢得冠军之前，她已经竞争了很多年。


将来时:
They will compete in the next game. = 他们将在下一场比赛中竞争。
He will compete for a scholarship. = 他将竞争奖学金。


虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I would compete. = 如果我更强壮，我会去竞争。
I wish I could compete in the marathon. = 我希望我能参加马拉松比赛。


被动语态:
The competition was won by a newcomer. = 比赛由一个新人赢得。
The race was keenly competed. = 这场比赛竞争激烈。


疑问句:
Will you compete? = 你会竞争吗？
Who will compete against them? = 谁将与他们竞争？


否定句:
They do not compete fairly. = 他们竞争不公平。
I did not compete in the last race. = 我没有参加上一场比赛。



PHRASE->
compete against = 与...竞争
compete for = 为...而竞争
compete with = 与...竞争
compete in = 参加...比赛
to compete successfully = 成功竞争
