prefers:/prɪˈfɜːrz/| vt.|更喜欢，偏爱|To choose something or someone over another or others.  It expresses a stronger preference than "like."|例句：She prefers tea to coffee. 她更喜欢茶而不是咖啡。|例句：He prefers working independently. 他更喜欢独立工作。|近义词：favors, likes better, chooses, opts for|反义词：dislikes, abhors, detests


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prefers coffee to tea. = 他更喜欢咖啡而不是茶。
She prefers walking to driving. = 她更喜欢步行而不是开车。

一般过去时:
He preferred to stay home. = 他宁愿待在家里。
She preferred the red dress to the blue one. = 她更喜欢那件红色的裙子而不是蓝色的。

现在完成时:
I have always preferred classical music. = 我一直更喜欢古典音乐。
She has preferred to work from home since the pandemic. = 自疫情以来，她一直更喜欢在家工作。

过去完成时:
He had preferred to go to the beach, but it rained. = 他本来想去海滩，但是下雨了。
She had preferred a quiet evening, but her friends insisted on going out. = 她本来想安静地度过一个晚上，但是她的朋友坚持要出去。

将来时:
I will prefer to travel by train. = 我将更喜欢乘火车旅行。
They will prefer to stay at a hotel near the beach. = 他们将更喜欢住在海滩附近的酒店。

虚拟语气:
I would prefer to have a quiet evening. = 我宁愿过一个安静的夜晚。
She would have preferred to go to the concert, but she was ill. = 她本来想去音乐会，但她病了。

被动语态:
Tea is preferred by many people. = 茶被很多人喜欢。  (Note: Passive voice is less common with "prefer".)
This solution is preferred over others. = 这个解决方案优于其他解决方案。(Note: Passive voice is less common with "prefer".)

疑问句:
Do you prefer tea or coffee? = 你喜欢茶还是咖啡？
Would you prefer to stay or go? = 你是愿意留下来还是走？

否定句:
I don't prefer spicy food. = 我不喜欢辛辣的食物。
He doesn't prefer working late. = 他不喜欢加班。


PHRASE->
prefer to do something = 宁愿做某事
prefer doing something to doing something else = 更喜欢做某事而不是做另一件事
would prefer to = 宁愿
prefer it if =  更希望
much prefer = 更喜欢


词源:
From Old French *preferer, from Latin prāeferre "to carry before, prefer," from prae- "before" + ferre "to carry, bear."
