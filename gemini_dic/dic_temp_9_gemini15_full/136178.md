nonreinstatements:/ˌnɒnˌriːɪnˈsteɪtmənts/ | n. |  非恢复；未恢复；未重新启用 | The absence of reinstatement; the failure to reinstate something or someone.  This word refers to instances where something (like a policy, a right, or a person's position) has not been restored to its previous state.  It emphasizes the lack of action to reinstate. | Example: The nonreinstatements of the dismissed employees led to widespread protests.  员工未被恢复职位导致了广泛的抗议。 |近义词:lack of reinstatement, failure to reinstate, nonrestoration |反义词:reinstatements
