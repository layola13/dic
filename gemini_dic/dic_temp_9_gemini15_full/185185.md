retorts:/rɪˈtɔːrt/| n. v.| 反驳；顶嘴；尖刻的回答；蒸馏器 | a quick and angry reply to a comment or accusation.  A retort can also refer to a vessel used in distillation. |例句：He retorted angrily to the accusation. 他愤怒地反驳了这一指控。

He retorted with a sarcastic remark. 他以一句尖刻的评论进行反驳。


近义词：reply, answer, rejoinder, comeback |反义词：agreement, acceptance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retorts with a witty comment. = 他以一句俏皮话进行反驳。
She retorts sharply to any criticism. = 她对任何批评都尖锐地反驳。


一般过去时:
He retorted that he was innocent. = 他反驳说自己是无辜的。
She retorted sarcastically, "Oh really?" = 她讽刺地反驳道：“哦，真的吗？”


现在完成时:
He has retorted to every accusation leveled against him. = 他已经反驳了针对他的每一项指控。
She has retorted with such force that no one dares to challenge her again. = 她反驳得如此有力，以至于没有人敢再挑战她。


过去完成时:
He had retorted before I could even speak. = 我还没来得及说话，他就已经反驳了。
She had retorted so vehemently that the argument ended abruptly. = 她反驳得如此激烈，以至于争论突然结束了。


将来时:
He will retort with a prepared statement. = 他将用一份准备好的声明来反驳。
She will retort decisively when faced with such accusations. = 面对这样的指控，她将果断地反驳。


虚拟语气:
If he retorted, I would have lost my temper. = 如果他反驳，我会发脾气的。
I wish she hadn't retorted so aggressively. = 我希望她没有那么咄咄逼人地反驳。


被动语态:
His words were retorted with a raised voice. = 他的话被高声反驳了。
Her statement was retorted with evidence to the contrary. = 她的陈述被相反的证据反驳了。


疑问句:
How did he retort to your question? = 他如何反驳你的问题？
Will she retort to the accusations? = 她会反驳这些指控吗？


否定句:
He didn't retort to the insult. = 他没有反驳侮辱。
She didn't retort to his challenge. = 她没有回应他的挑战。


PHRASE->
a sharp retort = 一句尖刻的反驳
a witty retort = 一句诙谐的反驳
a sarcastic retort = 一句讽刺的反驳
a quick retort = 一个迅速的反驳
to retort angrily = 愤怒地反驳

词源:
From Old French *retortir, from Latin retortus, past participle of retorquere "to twist back," from re- "back" + torquere "to twist."  The sense development is from "twisting back" to "turning back an argument".  The meaning of "a vessel" emerged later.
