helming: /ˈhɛlmɪŋ/ | v. |掌舵；领导；管理 | The act of steering or controlling a ship or other vehicle, or of leading or managing something.  |例句：He was helming the company through a difficult period. 他在带领公司度过艰难时期。 |近义词：steering, guiding, leading, managing, directing |反义词：following, obeying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is helming the project. = 他正在领导这个项目。
She helms the marketing department. = 她掌管市场部。


一般过去时:
He helmed the ship through the storm. = 他驾驶着船只度过了暴风雨。
They helmed the company to success. = 他们带领公司走向成功。


现在完成时:
She has helmed several successful campaigns. = 她已经领导过几次成功的活动。
He has been helming the organization for ten years. = 他已经领导这个组织十年了。


过去完成时:
Before the crisis, he had helmed the company for five years. = 在危机之前，他领导公司五年了。
They had helmed the project to completion before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了项目。


将来时:
She will helm the new initiative. = 她将领导新的计划。
He will be helming the department next year. = 他明年将领导这个部门。


虚拟语气:
If he helmed the project, it would be more successful. = 如果他领导这个项目，它会更成功。
I wish she had helmed the company earlier. = 我希望她早点领导公司。


被动语态:
The ship was helmed by an experienced captain. = 这艘船由一位经验丰富的船长掌舵。
The project is being helmed by a new team. = 该项目由一个新的团队负责。


疑问句:
Who is helming the investigation? = 谁在领导这项调查？
Who helmed the ship during the emergency? = 紧急情况下是谁在掌舵？


否定句:
He didn't helm the company during that time. = 那段时间他并没有领导公司。
She wasn't helming the project when it failed. = 项目失败时她并没有领导。


PHRASE->
helm a company = 领导一家公司
helm a project = 领导一个项目
helm a ship = 掌舵一艘船
take the helm = 掌舵；接管
at the helm = 掌舵；领导


词源:
The word "helming" derives from the Old English word "helm," meaning "rudder" or "steering apparatus" of a ship.  The verb form evolved naturally from the noun's function.
