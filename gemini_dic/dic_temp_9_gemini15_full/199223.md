sill:/sɪl/| n.|窗台；门槛；基石|A horizontal piece of wood or stone forming the base of a window or doorway, or the bottom of a fireplace.  It can also refer to a foundation or base, both literally and figuratively. |例句：The cat sat on the window sill. 猫坐在窗台上。 The old house had a worn stone sill. 那座老房子有一个磨损的石制门槛。|近义词：threshold, lintel, base|反义词：top, apex
