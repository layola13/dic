moniment:/ˈmɒnjʊmənt/| n.|纪念碑，纪念物|A monument is a structure or object built to commemorate a person, event, or idea.  It often serves as a lasting tribute or reminder.|例句：The Lincoln Memorial is a magnificent monument in Washington, D.C. 林肯纪念堂是华盛顿特区一座宏伟的纪念碑。|例句：They erected a monument to the fallen soldiers. 他们为阵亡士兵树立了一座纪念碑。|近义词：memorial, landmark, tribute|反义词：destruction, oblivion
