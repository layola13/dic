garner:/ˈɡɑːrnər/| v. n. |收集，积累；谷仓 |to gather and store something, especially grain; a storehouse for grain |例句：The farmers garnered the wheat before the storm.  农民在暴风雨来临之前收割了小麦。

例句：The museum has a large garner of historical artifacts. 这个博物馆收藏了大量的历史文物。


近义词：collect, gather, accumulate, amass | 反义词：scatter, disperse, squander


词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He garners information from various sources. = 他从各种来源收集信息。
They garner support for their cause. = 他们为他们的事业争取支持。


一般过去时:
She garnered much praise for her performance. = 她因其表演获得了许多赞誉。
The team garnered a significant victory. = 该队取得了重大胜利。


现在完成时:
He has garnered considerable experience in his field. = 他在他的领域积累了相当多的经验。
The company has garnered a large market share. = 这家公司获得了很大的市场份额。


过去完成时:
They had garnered enough evidence to convict him. = 他们已经收集了足够的证据来定他的罪。
She had garnered a reputation for honesty. = 她已经赢得了诚实的声誉。


将来时:
The project will garner significant attention. = 该项目将引起广泛关注。
We expect the campaign to garner many votes. = 我们预计这场运动将赢得许多选票。


虚拟语气:
If she had garnered more votes, she would have won the election. = 如果她获得更多选票，她就赢得了选举。
I wish I had garnered more information before making the decision. = 我希望在做决定之前我已经收集了更多信息。


被动语态:
The information was garnered from confidential sources. = 信息是从机密来源收集的。
The award was garnered by a talented young artist. = 奖项由一位有才华的年轻艺术家获得。


疑问句:
How did they garner so much support? = 他们是如何获得如此多的支持的？
Where did he garner the information? = 他从哪里收集到的信息？


否定句:
He did not garner enough support to win the election. = 他没有获得足够的支持来赢得选举。
The company failed to garner any new customers. = 该公司未能赢得任何新客户。


PHRASE->
garner support  = 获得支持
garner votes = 获得选票
garner attention = 吸引注意
garner praise = 获得赞扬
