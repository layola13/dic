* **-graphy**: From Greek "-graphia," meaning "writing" or "description," often used in medical terms to denote an imaging technique.


Therefore, "mammography" literally means "breast writing" or "breast imaging," referring to the process of creating images of the breast using X-rays.  The 's' adds the possessive case.
