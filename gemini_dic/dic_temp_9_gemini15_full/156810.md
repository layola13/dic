pigeons:/ˈpɪdʒənz/| n. |鸽子|A pigeon is a type of bird, typically grey or bluish-grey in color, known for its ability to find its way home over long distances.  They are often found in urban areas. |Example:  Many pigeons were perched on the statue.  许多鸽子栖息在雕像上。
The pigeons cooed softly in the park. 公园里鸽子轻轻地咕咕叫。|近义词：dove, rock dove|反义词：N/A
