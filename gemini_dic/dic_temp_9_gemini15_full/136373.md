nonresponsible: /ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒn.sə.bl̩/ | adj. | 不负责任的，不承担责任的 | Not taking or showing responsibility.  | The nonresponsible behavior of the company led to many customer complaints.  这家公司的行为不负责任，导致许多客户投诉。 | irresponsible, unaccountable | responsible, accountable | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is nonresponsible with his finances. = 他对自己的财务不负责任。
The company is nonresponsible for the damage. = 公司对损坏不负责任。

一般过去时:
She was nonresponsible in her handling of the project. = 她处理项目时不负责任。
They were nonresponsible for the accident. = 他们对事故不负责任。

现在完成时:
He has been nonresponsible throughout this whole affair. = 在整个事件中，他一直不负责任。
The government has been nonresponsible in addressing the issue. = 政府在处理这个问题上一直不负责任。

过去完成时:
They had been nonresponsible in their previous roles. = 他们在之前的角色中一直不负责任。
The company had been nonresponsible leading up to the scandal. = 在丑闻发生之前，该公司一直不负责任。

将来时:
He will be nonresponsible if he continues this behavior. = 如果他继续这种行为，他将是不负责任的。
The new management will likely be more responsible,  not nonresponsible. = 新管理层可能会更负责任，而不是不负责任。


虚拟语气:
If he were more responsible, he wouldn't be nonresponsible. = 如果他更负责任，他就不会不负责任。
I wish they weren't so nonresponsible. = 我希望他们不要那么不负责任。

被动语态:
His actions were considered nonresponsible by many. = 许多人认为他的行为不负责任。
The decision was deemed nonresponsible and reckless. = 该决定被认为是不负责任和鲁莽的。

疑问句:
Is his behavior nonresponsible? = 他的行为不负责任吗？
Are they being nonresponsible by ignoring the problem? = 他们忽视这个问题是不负责任的吗？

否定句:
His actions were not nonresponsible; he acted appropriately. = 他的行为并不缺乏责任感；他的行为是适当的。
The company is not nonresponsible; they are actively trying to rectify the situation.  = 公司并非不负责任；他们正在积极努力纠正这种情况。
