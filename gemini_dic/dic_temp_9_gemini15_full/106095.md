knifeboard:/ˈnaɪfˌbɔːrd/| n. |刀板；切菜板|A knifeboard is a flat surface, typically made of wood or plastic, used as a stable base for cutting food with a knife.  It protects the work surface from damage and provides a safe and stable platform for food preparation.|Example: She placed the onions on the knifeboard and began to chop them finely. 她把洋葱放在菜板上开始细细地切碎。|近义词：cutting board, chopping board|反义词：无
