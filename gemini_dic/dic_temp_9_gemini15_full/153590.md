pentandrian: /ˌpentænˈdriən/ | adj. | 五雄蕊的；具有五个雄蕊的 | Having five stamens.  The term refers to flowers with five stamens, a key characteristic used in plant classification.  | Example: The pentandrian flower was easily identified by its five prominent stamens.  五雄蕊的花朵很容易通过其五个突出的雄蕊来识别。| 近义词: pentamerous (referring to five parts in general)| 反义词:  None specifically, but terms describing flowers with different stamen counts (e.g., tetrandrous, hexandrous) could be considered opposites.
