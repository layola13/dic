lingua-francas: /ˈlɪŋɡwə ˈfræŋkəz/ | n. | 通用语；桥梁语言 | A language that is adopted as a common language between speakers whose native languages are different.  It is often used in international contexts or situations where people from diverse linguistic backgrounds need to communicate.  The term derives from the historical use of the French language (Franca) in various Mediterranean areas. | Example: English is a lingua franca for international business.  英语是国际商务的通用语。  |近义词：common language, bridge language, intermediary language | 反义词：native language, mother tongue


USAGE->

English serves as a lingua franca in many international organizations. = 英语在许多国际组织中充当通用语。
This region uses a creole as its lingua franca. = 这个地区使用一种克里奥尔语作为通用语。
