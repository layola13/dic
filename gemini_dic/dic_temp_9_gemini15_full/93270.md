ileus:/ˈɪliəs/| n. |肠梗阻，肠麻痹| A condition in which the normal movement of the bowels is slowed or stopped.  This can be caused by various factors, including surgery, medications, or underlying medical conditions.  The lack of bowel movement can lead to a buildup of gas and fluid in the intestines, causing abdominal pain, distention, and vomiting.|Example: The patient developed postoperative ileus after abdominal surgery.  病人腹部手术后出现了术后肠梗阻。  The doctor suspected paralytic ileus as the cause of the patient's abdominal pain and distention.  医生怀疑麻痹性肠梗阻是导致病人腹痛和腹胀的原因。|近义词：bowel obstruction, intestinal obstruction, paralytic ileus|反义词：normal bowel function
