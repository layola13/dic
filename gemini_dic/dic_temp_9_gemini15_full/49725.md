democratization: /ˌdɛməkrətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 民主化 | The process of making a country, organization, or system more democratic.  The spread of democratic ideas and practices. | 例句：The democratization of information through the internet has been revolutionary.  互联网使信息民主化，这是一场革命。

例句：The democratization of access to education is a key goal of many developing countries.  许多发展中国家将普及教育作为主要目标。


近义词：democratisation, popularization, liberalization

反义词：authoritarianism, totalitarianism, autocracy

词性：名词
