self-obsessions:/ˌsɛlf əbˈsɛʃənz/| n.|自我迷恋，沉迷于自我|An excessive preoccupation with oneself;  a state of being overly focused on one's own thoughts, feelings, and experiences, often to the exclusion of others and reality.|例句：Her self-obsessions prevented her from forming meaningful relationships.  她的自我迷恋阻止了她建立有意义的关系。

例句：His self-obsessions were so consuming that he neglected his work and his family.  他的自我迷恋如此强烈，以至于他忽略了他的工作和家庭。|近义词：narcissism, egotism, self-centeredness, self-absorption|反义词：selflessness, altruism, humility|名词
