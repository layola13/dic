hydrokinetic:/ˌhaɪdroʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|水动力学的；利用水流发电的|Relating to or using the energy of moving water to generate power.  Hydro refers to water and kinetic refers to motion or energy of motion.|例：Hydrokinetic energy is a renewable energy source. 水动力能是一种可再生能源。
The hydrokinetic turbine is designed for tidal currents.  这个水动力涡轮机是为潮汐流设计的。|近义词：hydroelectric (for electricity generation)|反义词：static
