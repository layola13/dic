reirrigates:/ˌriːˈɪrɪɡeɪts/ | v. | 再灌溉；重新灌溉 | To irrigate again; to water (land) a second time.  | The farmer reirrigates his fields after a period of dry weather.  农民在一段干旱天气后再次灌溉他的田地。 | irrigate, water |  dehydrate, drain |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer reirrigates his crops every week. = 农民每周都给他的庄稼重新灌溉。
They reirrigate the parched land to ensure a good harvest. = 他们再次灌溉干旱的土地以确保丰收。

一般过去时:
The gardener reirrigated the flowerbeds after the hot spell. = 园丁在酷暑过后再次灌溉了花坛。
He reirrigated the lawn because it was too dry. = 他再次给草坪浇水，因为它太干了。

现在完成时:
They have reirrigated the fields twice this month. = 这个月他们已经给田地重新灌溉了两次。
The farmer has reirrigated his crops to compensate for the lack of rainfall. = 农民已经重新灌溉了他的庄稼以弥补降雨不足。

过去完成时:
The plants had already been reirrigated before the drought. = 在干旱之前，植物已经被重新灌溉过了。
They had reirrigated the orchard before the heatwave struck. = 在热浪来袭之前，他们已经重新灌溉了果园。

将来时:
The farmers will reirrigate their fields next week. = 农民们下周将再次灌溉他们的田地。
We will reirrigate the garden after the upcoming heatwave. = 我们将在即将到来的热浪过后再次灌溉花园。

虚拟语气:
If it didn't rain, we would have to reirrigate the garden. = 如果不下雨，我们就得再次灌溉花园。
If the crops were drier, we would reirrigate them immediately. = 如果庄稼更干旱，我们会立即重新灌溉它们。

被动语态:
The fields were reirrigated yesterday. = 田地昨天被重新灌溉了。
The parched land needs to be reirrigated urgently. = 干旱的土地需要紧急重新灌溉。

疑问句:
Will the fields be reirrigated tomorrow? = 田地明天会被重新灌溉吗？
Have the crops been reirrigated yet? = 庄稼已经重新灌溉了吗？


否定句:
The farmer didn't reirrigate his crops today. = 农民今天没有重新灌溉他的庄稼。
They haven't reirrigated the garden this week. = 他们本周还没有重新灌溉花园。
