catamaran:/ˌkætəməˈræn/| n. |双体船|A catamaran is a type of boat that has two parallel hulls (bodies) connected by a deck.  They are known for their stability and speed. |例句：We sailed on a luxurious catamaran in the Caribbean. 我们在加勒比海乘坐了一艘豪华的双体船。|近义词：double-hulled boat|反义词：monohull
