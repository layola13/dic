cures:/kjʊərz/ | n. |治愈；疗法；解决方法 |The cures for many diseases are still unknown. 许多疾病的治疗方法仍然未知。|近义词： remedies, treatments, solutions |反义词： diseases, illnesses

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This medicine cures many illnesses. = 这药能治许多病。
The new policy cures the economic problem. = 新政策解决了经济问题。

一般过去时:
The doctor cured his patient of the disease. = 医生治好了他病人的病。
The new software cured the computer bug. = 新软件修复了电脑的错误。

现在完成时:
They have cured many patients this year. = 今年他们已经治好了许多病人。
The problem has been cured by the new technology. = 新技术解决了这个问题。

过去完成时:
He had cured many patients before he retired. = 他退休前已经治好了许多病人。
The issue had been cured before the meeting. = 会议之前这个问题就已经解决了。

将来时:
This new drug will cure many diseases. = 这种新药将治愈许多疾病。
We will cure the problem soon. = 我们很快就会解决这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If only there were a cure for cancer! = 但愿有治疗癌症的方法！
I wish this medicine could cure my headache. = 我希望这药能治好我的头痛。

被动语态:
Many diseases are cured by modern medicine. = 许多疾病都被现代医学治愈了。
The problem was cured quickly. = 这个问题很快就被解决了。

疑问句:
What cures this disease? = 什么能治这种病？
What will cure this problem? = 什么能解决这个问题？

否定句:
This medicine doesn't cure all illnesses. = 这药不能治所有病。
The new policy didn't cure the problem. = 新政策没有解决这个问题。


PHRASE->
a cure for = ...的疗法
a miracle cure = 奇效药
