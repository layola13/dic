homophones:/ˈhɒməfoʊnz/ |/ˈhɑːməˌfoʊnz/| n. |同音词 |Words that are pronounced the same but have different meanings and spellings.  They can cause confusion in writing and speaking because their sounds are identical.  |Example:  The words "to", "too", and "two" are homophones.  |近义词：同音异义词 |反义词：无
