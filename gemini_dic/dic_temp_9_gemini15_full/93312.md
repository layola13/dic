ill-fitting:/ˌɪlˈfɪtɪŋ/| adj.|不合身的，不合适的|Not fitting properly; unsuitable or inappropriate.|The ill-fitting shoes caused blisters on her feet.  不合身的鞋子磨起了她的脚泡。|The ill-fitting narrative left many plot holes. 不合逻辑的叙述留下了许多情节漏洞。|well-fitting, suitable|well-fitting, appropriate|
