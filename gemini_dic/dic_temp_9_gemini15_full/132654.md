nondiscussible:/ˌnɒndɪˈskʌsəbl/ | adj. | 不可讨论的，不宜讨论的 | Not to be discussed; forbidden from discussion.  This term implies a prohibition, often due to sensitivity, confidentiality, or the potential for negative consequences. | Example: The terms of the settlement are nondiscussible.  和解条款不可讨论。 |近义词:undiscussable, taboo, confidential, secret | 反义词:discussable, open, public

词源:
The word is a combination of "non-" (meaning "not") and "discussible" (meaning "capable of being discussed").  "Discussible" itself derives from the verb "discuss," ultimately from the Latin "discutere," meaning "to shake apart," "to examine thoroughly," or "to debate."  Therefore, "nondiscussible" literally means "not capable of being shaken apart, examined thoroughly, or debated."
