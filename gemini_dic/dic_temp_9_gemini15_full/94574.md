impoverisher: /ɪmˈpɑːvərɪʃər/ | n. | 使贫困者；使贫瘠者 | Someone or something that makes people or a place poor or unproductive.  | The drought was a major impoverisher of the region, leading to widespread famine.  干旱是该地区的主要贫困原因，导致了大范围的饥荒。 The factory's closure acted as an impoverisher for the entire town. 工厂倒闭使整个城镇都陷入贫困。 | impoverisher, pauperizer | enricher, benefactor | 名词
