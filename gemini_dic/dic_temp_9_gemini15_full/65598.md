evaluation:/ɪˌvæljuˈeɪʃən/ | n. |评估；评价；估价 |The process of judging or determining the significance, worth, or quality of something.  It involves examining something carefully and making a judgment about its value or effectiveness. |例句：The teacher gave a thorough evaluation of my essay. 老师对我的论文进行了彻底的评估。|例句：The company conducted a market evaluation before launching the new product. 公司在推出新产品之前进行了市场评估。|近义词：assessment, appraisal, judgment, review |反义词：neglect, disregard

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The manager evaluates employee performance annually. = 经理每年评估员工绩效。
She evaluates the risks involved before making a decision. = 她会在做决定之前评估相关的风险。

一般过去时:
He evaluated the evidence carefully. = 他仔细地评估了证据。
They evaluated the impact of the new policy. = 他们评估了新政策的影响。

现在完成时:
We have evaluated several options. = 我们已经评估了几个方案。
She has evaluated the situation and made a plan. = 她已经评估了形势并制定了计划。

过去完成时:
They had evaluated the project before submitting the report. = 他们在提交报告之前已经评估了项目。
She had evaluated the data prior to making her presentation. = 她在做演示之前已经评估了数据。

将来时:
We will evaluate the results next week. = 我们下周将评估结果。
The committee will evaluate the proposals in the coming months. = 委员会将在未来几个月内评估这些提案。

虚拟语气:
If we had evaluated the risks more carefully, we might have avoided the problem. = 如果我们更仔细地评估风险，我们本可以避免这个问题。
It is essential that the project be evaluated objectively. =  至关重要的是要客观地评估该项目。

被动语态:
The proposal was evaluated by the board of directors. = 该提案由董事会进行了评估。
The students' work will be evaluated by the professor. = 学生的作品将由教授进行评估。

疑问句:
How will the effectiveness of the program be evaluated? = 如何评估该项目的有效性？
What criteria will be used to evaluate the candidates? = 将使用什么标准来评估候选人？

否定句:
The committee did not evaluate the application thoroughly. = 委员会没有彻底评估申请。
They have not yet evaluated the long-term effects. = 他们尚未评估长期影响。


PHRASE->
performance evaluation = 绩效评估
cost evaluation = 成本评估
risk evaluation = 风险评估
needs evaluation = 需求评估
impact evaluation = 影响评估
