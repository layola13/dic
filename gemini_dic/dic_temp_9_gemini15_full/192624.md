scurries:/ˈskʌriz/| v.|疾跑；匆匆忙忙地跑|To move quickly and hurriedly, typically with short steps.|The mouse scurried across the floor.  老鼠迅速跑过地板。|The children scurried to their rooms before their mother could scold them.孩子们在母亲责备他们之前赶紧跑到自己的房间。|rushes, dashes, darts|walks leisurely, ambles


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He scurries to catch the bus every morning. 他每天早上都匆匆忙忙地赶公共汽车。
The ants scurry across the picnic blanket. 蚂蚁在野餐布上匆匆忙忙地爬行。

一般过去时:
She scurried away when she saw me. 她看到我后匆匆离开了。
The rabbit scurried into its burrow. 兔子迅速钻进了它的洞穴。

现在完成时:
The cat has scurried under the bed to hide. 猫已经跑到床底下躲起来了。
They have scurried to complete the project before the deadline. 他们已经匆匆忙忙地赶在截止日期前完成了项目。

过去完成时:
He had scurried to the kitchen to grab a snack before the meeting. 他在会议前匆匆忙忙地跑到厨房拿零食。
The birds had scurried to their nests before the storm hit. 鸟儿们在暴风雨来临之前已经匆匆忙忙地回到了自己的巢穴。

将来时:
She will scurry to finish her homework tonight. 她今晚会匆匆忙忙地完成作业。
The mice will scurry away if they see a cat. 老鼠如果看到猫就会迅速逃走。

虚拟语气:
If I were late, I would scurry to get there on time. 如果我迟到了，我会匆匆忙忙地赶到那里。
I wish I hadn't scurried so much; I made a mistake. 我希望我当时不要这么匆忙；我犯了一个错误。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The task was scurried through, resulting in many errors. 任务被匆匆忙忙地完成，导致许多错误。 (This is awkward and less common)


疑问句:
Does the mouse scurry away when it sees danger? 老鼠看到危险时是否会迅速逃走？
Why did he scurry so quickly? 他为什么跑得这么快？

否定句:
He didn't scurry; he walked calmly. 他没有跑；他平静地走着。
The children didn't scurry; they walked slowly and carefully. 孩子们没有跑；他们慢慢地、小心地走着。
