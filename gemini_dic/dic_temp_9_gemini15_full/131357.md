nonchromatic:/ˌnɒnkrəˈmætɪk/| adj.|非彩色的，非色调的|Not having, or relating to, color; colorless.  It often refers to sounds or light that lack the characteristic of hue or color.  In music, it refers to music that does not use the notes of a specific key or scale. |例句：The nonchromatic light source emitted a pure white light.  = 非彩色的光源发出了纯白色的光。

The painting was surprisingly nonchromatic, relying solely on shades of gray. = 这幅画出人意料地是非彩色的，仅依靠灰色调。

近义词：achromatic, colorless, monochrome|反义词：chromatic, colorful
