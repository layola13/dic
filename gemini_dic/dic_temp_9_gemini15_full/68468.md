familiarizes:/fəˈmɪliəraɪz/ | vt. |使熟悉；使了解 |To make someone or something familiar; to make someone or something known to someone.  |例句：The tour familiarized us with the city's history.  这次旅行让我们熟悉了这座城市的历史。

例句：The training program familiarizes employees with new safety regulations. 培训项目使员工熟悉新的安全规章。


近义词：acquaint, introduce, accustom | 反义词：estrange, alienate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He familiarizes himself with the new software every day. 他每天都熟悉新的软件。
The teacher familiarizes the students with the new grammar rules. 老师让学生熟悉新的语法规则。

一般过去时:
She familiarized herself with the local customs before her trip.  她在旅行前熟悉了当地的风俗习惯。
The museum curator familiarized the visitors with the ancient artifacts.  博物馆馆长让参观者熟悉了古代文物。

现在完成时:
I have familiarized myself with the project's requirements. 我已经熟悉了项目的各项要求。
They have familiarized the public with the new policy through various media. 他们通过各种媒体向公众宣传了这项新政策。

过去完成时:
He had familiarized himself with the subject matter before writing the report. 他在写报告之前已经熟悉了主题内容。
The team had familiarized themselves with the software before the launch.  团队在发布之前已经熟悉了软件。
