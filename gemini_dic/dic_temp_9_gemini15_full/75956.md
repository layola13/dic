funk:/fʌŋk/| n., v.|  令人不快的事物；忧郁；沮丧；跳舞音乐风格；（俚语）粗制滥造；使（某物）变得无用；（俚语）失败；|A feeling of depression or unease; a style of music characterized by a strong rhythmic bass line and often improvisation; to malfunction. |例句：The funk music is very rhythmic and lively.  放克音乐节奏感很强，而且很活泼。
The old car started to funk after only a few months. 这辆旧车只用了几个月就出毛病了。|近义词：groove,  depression, slump,  dysfunction |反义词：elation, joy,  smooth operation
