fusibility:/ˌfjuːzɪˈbɪləti/| n. |熔化性，可熔性，易熔性 |The ability of a substance to be melted or fused.  It refers to the ease with which a material can be melted. This property is crucial in various applications, including welding, soldering, and glassmaking. |Example: The high fusibility of the alloy made it ideal for casting.  这种合金的高熔化性使其非常适合铸造。 The low fusibility of this material makes it difficult to weld.  这种材料的低熔化性使得焊接它很困难。|近义词：meltability, fusibility, liquefiability |反义词：infusibility, nonfusibility |名词
