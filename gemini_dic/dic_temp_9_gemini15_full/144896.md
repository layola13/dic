over-shortens: /ˌoʊvərˈʃɔːrtn̩z/ | v. | 过度缩短；过分简化 | To shorten something excessively, often to the point of losing important information or detail.  It implies a negative consequence due to the excessive shortening. | 例句：The editor over-shortened the article, losing much of its nuance and impact.  编辑过度缩短了这篇文章，使其失去了许多细微之处和影响力。


The teacher over-shortened the explanation, leaving the students confused. 老师过分简化了讲解，导致学生们感到困惑。


近义词：abbreviate excessively, truncate excessively, condense excessively, oversimplify
