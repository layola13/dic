shopful: /ˈʃɒpfʊl/ | adj. | 商店装满的；商店货品很多的 |  Full of things from a shop; filled with goods from a shop.  The word implies a large quantity or variety of goods, often suggesting a well-stocked shop. |例句：The shopful of Christmas decorations was dazzling. 圣诞装饰品塞满了商店，令人眼花缭乱。


The shopful of toys delighted the children.  玩具塞满了商店，让孩子们欣喜若狂。| 近义词：well-stocked, replete, brimming | 反义词：empty, bare, depleted | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  There's no tense involved.  Its use is descriptive.
