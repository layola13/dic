sinology:/ˌsaɪˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |汉学；中国研究 |The academic study of China, its languages, history, culture, and so on.  It encompasses a wide range of disciplines, including history, literature, philosophy, art, and political science. |例句：His expertise lies in sinology, particularly the Tang Dynasty.  他的专长是汉学，特别是唐代史。  |近义词：Chinese studies, studies of China |反义词：无
