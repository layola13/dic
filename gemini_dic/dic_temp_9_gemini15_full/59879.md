eczemas:/ˈɛksɪməz/| n. |湿疹（复数）|Eczemas are inflammatory skin conditions characterized by redness, itching, and scaling.  They are not contagious and can be caused by various factors, including allergies, irritants, and genetics.  Treatment focuses on managing symptoms and preventing flare-ups.|Example:  The doctor diagnosed her with eczemas on her hands and face. 医生诊断她患有手部和面部的湿疹。

The patient's eczemas were significantly improved after starting a new topical steroid cream.  在开始使用新的局部类固醇霜后，患者的湿疹得到了显著改善。|近义词：dermatitis, skin inflammation |反义词：None.
