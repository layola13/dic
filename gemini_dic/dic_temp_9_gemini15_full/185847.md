reviver:/rɪˈvaɪvər/| n. |复兴者；恢复者；起死回生的人或物 |A reviver is a person or thing that brings something back to life or activity.  It can refer to a person who rescues someone from a near-death experience, a drink that refreshes, or something that revitalizes a declining industry or trend.  |例句：The new CEO was a reviver of the company's fortunes.  这家公司的新任CEO是其命运的复兴者。

The cold drink was a reviver after the long hike. 长途跋涉之后，这杯冷饮令人精神一振。|近义词：restorer, revitalizer, regenerator |反义词：destroyer, ruiner
