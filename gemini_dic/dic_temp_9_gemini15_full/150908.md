parallel-with:/ˌpærəlel wɪð/| prep.|与…平行；与…同时；与…相比|This phrase indicates a relationship of similarity, simultaneity, or correspondence between two things.  It suggests that two things are happening at the same time, or that they share similar characteristics or features.|例句：The two lines are parallel with each other. 这两条线彼此平行。|例句：The economic crisis ran parallel with a rise in unemployment. 经济危机与失业率上升同时发生。|近义词：alongside, concurrently, simultaneously, in line with|反义词：at odds with, perpendicular to, contrasting with|词性:介词
