douc: /daʊk/ | n. | 懒猴 | A douc langur is a type of Old World monkey known for its distinctive colorful fur. They are arboreal primates found in Southeast Asia, particularly in Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia.  Doucs are characterized by their long, slender limbs, relatively long tails, and striking facial markings.  They are primarily herbivores, feeding on leaves, fruits, and flowers. They are considered vulnerable or endangered, facing threats from habitat loss and hunting. |  The douc langur is a fascinating primate known for its vibrant colors. | 近义词：langur (a more general term) | 反义词：无
