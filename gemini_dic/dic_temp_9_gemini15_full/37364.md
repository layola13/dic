commence:/kəˈmɛns/ | v. | 开始，着手 | To begin; to start.  | The meeting will commence at 2 p.m.  会议将于下午2点开始。| begin, start, initiate | cease, end, finish | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The class commences at 9 a.m. = 课程在上午9点开始。
Work commences on Monday. = 工作从星期一开始。


一般过去时:
The ceremony commenced with a prayer. = 典礼以祈祷开始。
The rain commenced suddenly. = 雨突然下起来了。


现在完成时:
The project has commenced, and we're making good progress. = 项目已经开始，我们正在取得良好的进展。
They have commenced their journey. = 他们已经开始了他们的旅程。


过去完成时:
The play had commenced before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，戏已经开始了。
The construction had commenced months ago. = 建筑工程几个月前就已经开始了。


将来时:
The concert will commence at 8 p.m. = 音乐会将于晚上8点开始。
The trial will commence next week. = 审判将于下周开始。


虚拟语气:
If the rain commenced, we would have to postpone the picnic. = 如果下雨了，我们就得推迟野餐。
I wish the meeting would commence soon. = 我希望会议尽快开始。


被动语态:
The meeting was commenced with a welcome speech. = 会议以欢迎词开始。
The project will be commenced next month. = 项目将于下个月开始。


疑问句:
When does the performance commence? = 演出什么时候开始？
At what time does the sale commence? = 销售什么时候开始？


否定句:
The game did not commence on time. = 比赛没有按时开始。
The meeting did not commence until 3 p.m. = 会议直到下午3点才开始。


PHRASE->
