generically:/dʒəˈnɛrɪkli/ | adv. | 一般地；笼统地；从属种类的角度 | In a general way; without specific details.  It refers to something considered as a member of a general category rather than a specific instance. | 例句：The term "fruit" is used generically to refer to apples, oranges, and bananas.  “水果”这一术语泛指苹果、橙子和香蕉。

例句：He generically referred to all the cars as "vehicles". 他笼统地把所有汽车都称为“车辆”。


近义词：generally, broadly, typically, in general, overall

反义词：specifically, particularly, precisely

词性: 副词
