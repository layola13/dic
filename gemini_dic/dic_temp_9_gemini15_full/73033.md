foothill:/ˈfʊtˌhɪl/| n. |山麓，山脚 |The foothills are lower slopes of a mountain range, typically covered in vegetation and less steep than the higher elevations.  They often represent a transition zone between the plains or valleys and the main mountain mass.| 例句：We hiked through the beautiful foothills before reaching the summit. 我们穿过美丽的山麓后才到达山顶。

The wildflowers bloomed profusely in the foothills. 山麓野花盛开。| 近义词：outskirts, base, lower slopes | 反义词：summit, peak, top
