palm:/pɑːm/ /pæləm/| n. v.|手掌；棕榈树；掌状物|The flat inner surface of the hand between the wrist and fingers; a tropical tree with a crown of large leaves and a tall, usually unbranched trunk; something resembling a palm in shape or function.|例句：He opened his palm to show me the coin. 他摊开手掌给我看硬币。  例句：The resort is surrounded by palm trees. 度假村周围环绕着棕榈树。|近义词：hand, frond,  date palm|反义词：back of hand
