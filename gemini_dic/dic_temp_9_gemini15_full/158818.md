plumbaginous:/ˌplʌmˈbædʒɪnəs/| adj.|铅笔状的；深蓝色的；深紫色的|Lead-colored or dark bluish-purple; resembling lead.  The term often refers to the color of plumbago (graphite) or certain plants.|The artist used plumbaginous paints to create a dramatic effect.  艺术家用铅笔状颜料创造出戏剧性的效果。

The deep plumbaginous sky hinted at an approaching storm. 深蓝色的天空暗示着暴风雨即将来临。|近义词：lead-colored, dark blue, purplish-black|反义词：bright, light-colored


Etymology: From plumbago + -inous.  Plumbago itself derives from Latin *plumbum ("lead") because of the mineral's resemblance to lead.  The suffix "-inous" indicates a resemblance or quality.
