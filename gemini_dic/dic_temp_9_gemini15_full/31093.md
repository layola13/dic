champignon:/ʃæmˈpiɲɒn/| n.|蘑菇，香菇|A champignon is a type of edible mushroom, often cultivated.  The term is often used generically to refer to cultivated mushrooms, especially the white button mushroom.|例句：The chef prepared a delicious dish with fresh champignons. 厨师用新鲜香菇做了一道美味的菜肴。|近义词：mushroom, fungus|反义词:None.
