cringingly:/ˈkrɪndʒɪŋli/| adv.|令人尴尬地，令人难受地|In a way that causes or expresses embarrassment or discomfort.|His cringingly bad jokes made everyone uncomfortable.  他那些令人尴尬的烂笑话让每个人都感到不舒服。|awkwardly, embarrassingly, shamefully| confidently, proudly| 副词
