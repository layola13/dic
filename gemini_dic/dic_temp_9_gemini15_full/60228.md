effortless:/ˈɛfərtlɪs/ | adj. |毫不费力的，轻松的 |Without effort; easy.  It implies a lack of difficulty or strain. |例句：She made the difficult task look effortless. 她让艰难的任务看起来毫不费力。
例句：The dancer's movements were effortless and graceful. 舞者的动作轻松优雅。|近义词：easy, simple, smooth, facile |反义词：difficult, strenuous, laborious, arduous
