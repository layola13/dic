humming:/ˈhʌmɪŋ/ | v. | 哼唱；嗡嗡作响 |  To produce a low, continuous sound, like that of a bee or a machine, or to sing a tune with your lips closed. | 例句：The bees were humming in the garden. 花园里的蜜蜂嗡嗡作响。

The engine was humming quietly. 发动机发出轻微的嗡嗡声。


例句：She was humming a cheerful tune. 她哼着快乐的曲子。

The refrigerator was humming all night. 冰箱整夜嗡嗡作响。


近义词： buzzing, droning, murmuring | 反义词：  silent, quiet
