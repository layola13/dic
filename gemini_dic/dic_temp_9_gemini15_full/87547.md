heteromera:/ˌhetərəˈmɪərə/| n.|异形类昆虫|  A heteromeran is a type of beetle belonging to the infraorder Heteromera, characterized by five-segmented tarsi on the front and middle legs, but four-segmented tarsi on the hind legs.  This is a  taxonomic classification within the Coleoptera (beetles). They exhibit significant diversity in morphology and ecology.  | Example: Many species of darkling beetles belong to the Heteromera infraorder.  许多黑暗甲虫属于异形类昆虫下目。|近义词: tenebrionoid (a related group within Heteromera)|反义词: N/A (no direct antonym, as it's a taxonomic grouping)|名词
