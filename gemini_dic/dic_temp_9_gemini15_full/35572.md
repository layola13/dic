coalminer:/ˈkoʊlˌmaɪnər/ | n. | 煤矿工人 | A person who works in a coal mine, extracting coal from the earth. | Example: My grandfather was a coalminer for over forty years.  我的祖父当了四十多年的煤矿工人。|近义词：miner, collier | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with office worker, etc.
