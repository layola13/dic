sawed:/sɔːd/| v.|锯；锯开|To cut (something) with a saw.  The act of cutting something with a saw.|例句：He sawed the wood into pieces. 他把木头锯成几块。

例句：The carpenter sawed through the thick plank. 木匠锯开了厚厚的木板。

近义词：cut, severed

反义词：joined, connected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saws wood every morning. 他每天早上锯木头。
She saws the bread for breakfast. 她早餐锯面包。


一般过去时:
I sawed the board in half yesterday. 我昨天把木板锯成两半。
They sawed down the old tree. 他们锯倒了那棵老树。


现在完成时:
He has sawed all the wood for the project. 他已经锯好了这个项目所需的所有木材。
She has sawed through the metal pipe. 她已经锯穿了金属管。


过去完成时:
They had sawed the log before the rain started.  在下雨之前他们已经锯好了原木。
We had sawed the branches off the tree before the storm.暴风雨来临之前，我们已经把树枝锯掉了。


将来时:
I will saw the wood tomorrow. 我明天会锯木头。
She will saw the meat for the barbecue. 她将为烧烤锯肉。


虚拟语气:
If I had a saw, I would saw the log. 如果我有一把锯子，我会把原木锯开。
I wish I had sawed the wood earlier. 我真希望我早点把木头锯了。


被动语态:
The wood was sawed into planks. 木头被锯成木板。
The tree was sawed down last week. 那棵树上周被锯倒了。


疑问句:
Did you saw the branch? 你锯了树枝吗？
Has he sawed the wood yet? 他锯木头了吗？


否定句:
I didn't saw the wood properly. 我没有把木头锯好。
She hasn't sawed the board yet. 她还没锯木板。


PHRASE->
sawed-off shotgun：截短的猎枪
sawed-off  (adj.):  shortened, especially illegally
sawed-in-half:锯成两半
sawed-off  (adj.):  (informal)  rude, abrupt, and unfriendly
saw something through:完成某事


词源:
源自古英语 *sagōn,  与saw (n.) 同源，意思为“锯子”。  动词sawed 是saw 的过去式和过去分词。
