iconoclasts:/ˈaɪkənəˌklæsts/ | n. | 破坏偶像者；反传统者 | A person who attacks cherished beliefs or institutions.  |  Example:  Many artists throughout history have been iconoclasts, challenging the established norms of their time. 许多历史上艺术家都是反传统者，挑战他们那个时代的既定规范。


近义词：rebel, revolutionary, dissident, nonconformist

反义词：conformist, traditionalist, conservative
