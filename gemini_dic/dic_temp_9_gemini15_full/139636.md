occlude:/əˈkluːd/| vt.|闭塞；阻塞；遮蔽|To close or block something.|The blood clot occluded the artery, causing a heart attack. 血凝块阻塞了动脉，导致心脏病发作。|近义词：block, obstruct, close|反义词：open, unblock

USAGE->

He occludes his ears with his fingers. = 他用手指捂住耳朵。
