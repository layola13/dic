ozone-friendly:/ˈoʊzoʊn ˈfrɛndli/| adj.| 臭氧层友好的，对臭氧层无害的 |  Describes substances or products that do not harm the ozone layer.  This typically refers to products that do not contain ozone-depleting substances (ODS) such as chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs).|  This refrigerator is ozone-friendly.  这个冰箱对臭氧层无害。

The company is committed to producing ozone-friendly products. 这家公司致力于生产对臭氧层友好的产品。|近义词：environmentally friendly, ozone-safe |反义词：ozone-depleting, ozone-damaging |

词源:
The term "ozone-friendly" is a compound word.  "Ozone" comes from the Greek word "ozein," meaning "to smell," referring to the characteristic odor of ozone. "Friendly" is an adjective meaning causing no harm or damage.  Therefore, "ozone-friendly" means not harmful to the ozone layer.
