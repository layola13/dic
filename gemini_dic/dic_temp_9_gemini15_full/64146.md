equalitarian:/ɪˌkwɒlɪˈteəriən/| adj.| 平等主义的；主张平等的 | Relating to or believing in the principle of equality, especially of all people.  An equalitarian society is one that values fairness and justice for everyone, regardless of their background or status. | Example:  The country adopted an equalitarian approach to land ownership, distributing it fairly among its citizens.  = 这个国家采取了平等主义的土地所有制方法，公平地将其分配给公民。  The equalitarian principles of the French Revolution inspired many other movements for social justice. = 法国大革命的平等主义原则启发了其他许多社会正义运动。|近义词：egalitarian, democratic |反义词：inegalitarian, elitist, hierarchical
