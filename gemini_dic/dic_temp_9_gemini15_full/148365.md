overspeeding:/ˌoʊvərˈspiːdɪŋ/ | v. |超速行驶|The act of driving or traveling faster than the legally permitted speed limit.  Overspeeding is a common cause of traffic accidents.|例句：He was fined for overspeeding on the highway. 他因在高速公路上超速行驶而被罚款。


例句：The driver was caught overspeeding and lost his license. 司机因超速驾驶而被抓，并吊销了驾照。


近义词：speeding, exceeding the speed limit


反义词：underspeeding, driving slowly


词性: 动词
