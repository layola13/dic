rambles:/ˈræmbəlz/| v. n.|闲逛；漫步；漫谈；胡言乱语|To walk or travel without a definite purpose or direction; to talk in a rambling, incoherent way.|例句：He rambled through the countryside. 他在乡间漫步。  He rambled on about his childhood. 他絮絮叨叨地谈论起他的童年。|近义词：wander, stroll, roam, meander, ramble on|反义词：hurry, rush, sprint


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rambles through the park every morning. = 他每天早上在公园里漫步。
She rambles on about her day at work. = 她絮絮叨叨地说着她一天的工作。


一般过去时:
They rambled through the forest yesterday. = 他们昨天在森林里漫步。
He rambled on for hours about his travels. = 他滔滔不绝地讲了他旅行的经历好几个小时。


现在完成时:
We have rambled along this path many times. = 我们已经在这条小路上漫步过很多次了。
She has rambled on about that topic for too long. = 她对那个话题唠叨了太久了。


过去完成时:
They had rambled for hours before they found the village. = 他们在找到村庄之前已经走了好几个小时了。
He had rambled on about his problems before I could interrupt. = 我还没来得及打断他，他就已经絮絮叨叨地说了很久他的问题了。


将来时:
We will ramble through the fields tomorrow. = 我们明天将在田野里漫步。
She will ramble on about her new hobby at the party. = 她将在派对上喋喋不休地谈论她的新爱好。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ramble through the mountains. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去山里漫步。
I wish he wouldn't ramble on so much during meetings. = 我希望他在会议上不要说得太多。


被动语态:
The countryside was rambled through by tourists. = 乡村被游客们漫步穿过。
The topic was rambled on about endlessly. = 这个话题被没完没了地谈论着。


疑问句:
Do you often ramble in the woods? = 你经常在树林里漫步吗？
Will he ramble on again at the next meeting? = 他在下次会议上还会絮絮叨叨吗？


否定句:
He didn't ramble far from the house. = 他没有离家走得很远。
She didn't ramble on about her problems this time. = 她这次没有絮絮叨叨地谈论她的问题。


PHRASE->

a rambling house = 一座结构凌乱的房子
ramble around = 到处闲逛
