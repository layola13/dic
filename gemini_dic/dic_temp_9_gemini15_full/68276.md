faking:/ˈfeɪkɪŋ/| v.|伪造；假装；模仿|To fake something means to create a false impression of something or to pretend to have a quality or feeling that you do not possess. It can also refer to the act of creating something counterfeit or imitation.|例句：He was faking his illness to avoid work. 他假装生病以逃避工作。
例句：She was faking a smile, but I knew she was upset. 她强颜欢笑，但我看出她不高兴了。|近义词：pretending, simulating, sham, feigning|反义词：being genuine, being authentic


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is faking a cough. = 他假装咳嗽。
They are faking their enthusiasm. = 他们在假装热情。

一般过去时:
She faked her signature on the check. = 她在支票上伪造了签名。
He faked his way through the interview. = 他在面试中蒙混过关。

现在完成时:
They have faked the evidence. = 他们伪造了证据。
She has been faking her emotions all day. = 她整天都在伪装情绪。

过去完成时:
He had faked his credentials before applying for the job. = 他在申请工作之前就伪造了证书。
They had faked their deaths to escape their pursuers. = 他们为了逃避追捕而伪造了自己的死亡。

将来时:
He will fake an injury to avoid playing. = 他将假装受伤以避免比赛。
They will fake their surprise when they receive the gift. = 他们收到礼物时将假装惊讶。

虚拟语气:
If I were to fake a smile, it wouldn't be convincing. = 如果我要假装微笑，那不会令人信服。
I wish I hadn't faked my illness; now everyone suspects me. = 我真希望我没有假装生病；现在大家都怀疑我了。

被动语态:
The painting was faked by a notorious forger. = 这幅画是由一个臭名昭著的伪造者伪造的。
The results of the experiment were faked to support the hypothesis. = 实验结果被伪造以支持假设。

疑问句:
Is he faking his illness? = 他是在假装生病吗？
Are they faking their happiness? = 他们在假装快乐吗？

否定句:
He wasn't faking his concern; he was genuinely worried. = 他并非假装关心；他是真的担心。
They didn't fake the data; it was all accurate. = 他们没有伪造数据；所有数据都是准确的。
