disown:/dɪsˈoʊn/| vt.| 否认；与…断绝关系；拒绝承认|To refuse to acknowledge or accept as one's own; to renounce; to repudiate.  |例句：He disowned his son after the scandal.  他在这场丑闻后与儿子断绝了关系。

例句：She disowned the painting, claiming it was a forgery. 她否认这幅画是自己的，声称它是赝品。


近义词：renounce, repudiate, reject, abjure

反义词：acknowledge, claim, own, accept


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disowns his past mistakes. 他否认他过去的错误。
They disown their responsibility.  他们否认他们的责任。


一般过去时:
She disowned her illegitimate child. 她否认了她私生子。
He disowned the letter, saying it was a fake. 他否认这封信，说它是假的。


现在完成时:
They have disowned their former leader. 他们已经与他们之前的领导人断绝了关系。
She has disowned the controversial statement. 她已经否认了那条有争议的声明。


过去完成时:
He had disowned his family before he left the country. 他在离开国家之前已经与家人断绝了关系。
The company had disowned the faulty product.  该公司已经否认了有缺陷的产品。


将来时:
I will disown you if you continue this behavior. 如果你继续这种行为，我会与你断绝关系。
The government will disown the treaty if it's violated. 如果条约被违反，政府将否认该条约。


虚拟语气:
If I were him, I would disown my son. 如果我是他，我会与我的儿子断绝关系。
I wish I had disowned him sooner.  我希望我早点就和他断绝关系。


被动语态:
His son was disowned by him.  他的儿子被他抛弃了。
The statement was disowned by the company. 该声明被公司否认了。


疑问句:
Will you disown your family for money? 你会为了钱而与你的家人断绝关系吗？
Did he disown his inheritance? 他否认了他的遗产吗？


否定句:
He did not disown his daughter, even after her arrest. 即使在他女儿被捕后，他也没有与她断绝关系。
She wouldn't disown her beliefs, no matter what. 无论如何，她都不会放弃自己的信仰。
