crosslegged:/ˈkrɒsleɡɪd/| adj.|盘腿的，交叠双腿的|Having legs crossed at the ankles or knees.  The adjective describes a posture where one leg is crossed over the other at either the ankles or knees.|例句：She sat crosslegged on the floor. 她盘腿坐在地板上。


例句：The yogi sat crosslegged in a meditative pose.  瑜伽士盘腿打坐。


近义词：legs crossed, seated with crossed legs


反义词：legs apart, legs outstretched


词性:形容词
