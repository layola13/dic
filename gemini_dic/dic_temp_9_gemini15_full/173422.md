It suggests a hesitant or lukewarm level of interest or commitment. | He listened to her pitch quasi-enthusiastically, his mind already wandering. 他不怎么热心地听着她的推销，心思已经飘到别处去了。| unenthusiastically, halfheartedly, apathetically | enthusiastically, passionately, zealously | 副词
