out-cue:/ˈaʊtˌkjuː/| n.| 出场提示；播出信号；（广播或电视节目的）结束信号 | A signal or instruction indicating the end of a broadcast or performance, or the start of a new segment.  It often involves a specific sound or visual cue that signals the transition. |例句：The director gave the out-cue for the commercial break.  导演发出了广告时段的结束信号。

The sound engineer missed the out-cue and the music continued playing. 音响工程师错过了结束信号，音乐继续播放着。|近义词：signal, cue, prompt |反义词：in-cue, start signal
