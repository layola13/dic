kyoto:/ˈkjoʊtoʊ/ | n. | 京都 | Kyoto is the former imperial capital of Japan, known for its rich history, traditional architecture, and numerous temples and shrines.  It's a significant cultural center and a popular tourist destination. | Kyoto is famous for its beautiful temples and gardens.  京都以其美丽的寺庙和花园而闻名。|  Osaka, Nara |  (No direct antonym,  but one could contrast it with modern, less traditional cities) | 名词
