nondeclaratory:/ˌnɒndɪˈklærəˌtɔːri/| adj. |非陈述的，非声明的 | Not stating a fact or opinion; not making a declaration.  It refers to sentences or utterances that do not make assertions or statements.  This includes questions, commands, exclamations, and other sentence types that don't assert something to be true or false. |The judge's nondeclaratory remarks were carefully worded to avoid bias.|近义词：interrogative, imperative, exclamatory |反义词：declarative
