hanoverian:/ˌhænəˈvɛəriən/| adj. n.| 汉诺威的；汉诺威王朝的；汉诺威人 | Of or relating to Hanover, Germany, or its dynasty.  The term often refers to the British monarchs from the House of Hanover (1714-1837).|
