god-fearing: /ˈɡɑːdˌfɪərɪŋ/ | adj. |敬畏上帝的，虔诚的 |

英文解析:  Describes someone who is deeply religious and lives according to their religious beliefs, showing great respect and fear of God.  It implies a strong moral compass guided by faith.

例句:

He was a god-fearing man who always tried to do what was right.  他是一个敬畏上帝的人，总是努力去做正确的事情。

The god-fearing community rallied together to help those in need.  这个敬畏上帝的社区团结起来帮助那些需要帮助的人。


近义词: pious, devout, religious, righteous, holy

反义词: godless, irreligious, impious, wicked, sinful


词性: adjective
