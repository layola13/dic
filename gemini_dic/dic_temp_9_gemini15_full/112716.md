loyal:/ˈlɔɪəl/ | adj. |忠诚的，忠实的 | Faithful to one's country, a person, or a cause.  It implies a strong feeling of devotion and adherence to duty or commitment. |例句：He was loyal to his friends throughout his life. 他一生都忠于他的朋友们。
She remained loyal to the company even after the merger. 即使在合并之后，她也仍然忠于这家公司。|近义词：faithful, devoted, true, steadfast |反义词：treacherous, disloyal, faithless, unfaithful |词性:形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is loyal to his principles. 他忠于自己的原则。
She remains loyal to her beliefs. 她始终忠于自己的信仰。


一般过去时:
He was loyal to the king. 他忠于国王。
They were loyal supporters of the cause. 他们是该事业的忠实支持者。


现在完成时:
She has always been loyal to her family. 她一直忠于她的家人。
He has remained loyal to his friends through thick and thin. 他在朋友患难与共时一直忠于他们。


过去完成时:
He had been loyal to the company for twenty years. 他已经忠于这家公司二十年了。
They had always been loyal to their country. 他们一直忠于他们的国家。


将来时:
I will always be loyal to my country. 我将永远忠于我的国家。
She will remain loyal to her principles, no matter what. 无论发生什么，她都会坚持自己的原则。


虚拟语气:
If he were loyal, he wouldn't have betrayed us. 如果他忠诚的话，他就不会背叛我们。
I wish she were more loyal to her commitments. 我希望她能更忠于她的承诺。


被动语态:
His loyalty was questioned by many. 他的忠诚受到了许多人的质疑。
The soldiers were expected to be loyal to their commander. 士兵们应该忠于他们的指挥官。


疑问句:
Are you loyal to your friends? 你忠于你的朋友吗？
Was he loyal to his country? 他忠于他的国家吗？


否定句:
He was not loyal to his employer. 他对他的雇主不忠诚。
She is not loyal to her promises. 她不忠于她的承诺。
