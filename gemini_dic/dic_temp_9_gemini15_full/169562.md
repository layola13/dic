prophase:/ˈproʊfeɪz/| n. |前期（细胞分裂）|The first phase of mitosis or meiosis, characterized by the condensation of chromosomes and the disappearance of the nuclear membrane.| 例句：During prophase, the chromosomes condense and become visible under a microscope.  在前期，染色体浓缩并在显微镜下可见。

During prophase, the nuclear envelope breaks down. 在前期，核膜破裂。

近义词：early stage, initial phase | 反义词：anaphase, telophase


词源:  From Ancient Greek πρόφασις (prófasis), from προ- (pro-) "before" + φαίνεσθαι (phainesthai) "to appear".  The term refers to the stage before the chromosomes become clearly visible.
