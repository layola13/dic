in-the-wind: /ɪn ðə wɪnd/ | adj. | 盛传的；即将发生的；不确定的 |  Something that is "in the wind" is rumored or expected to happen, but not yet confirmed. It suggests an atmosphere of anticipation or uncertainty. | 例句：There's a feeling that changes are in the wind.  =  人们感觉变革即将到来。

There are rumors of a merger in the wind. =  有传言说即将合并。


近义词：rumored, anticipated, impending, brewing, forthcoming | 反义词：certain, definite, confirmed


词性：形容词
