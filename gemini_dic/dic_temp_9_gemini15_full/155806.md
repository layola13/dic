/ˈfoʊnəʊtaɪpɪst/ | n. |语音录入员，语音打字员 |A phonotypist is a person who transcribes spoken language into written text, often using specialized software or equipment.  They are skilled in recognizing and accurately representing phonetic variations.  |Example: The court appointed a skilled phonotypist to transcribe the witness's testimony.  法院指定了一位熟练的语音录入员来记录证人的证词。|近义词：stenographer, transcriptionist |反义词：speaker (in the context of the role)
