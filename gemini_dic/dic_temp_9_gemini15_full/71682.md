flaunters: /ˈflɔːntərz/ | n. |炫耀者；夸耀者 | Those who flaunt their wealth and status often lack genuine substance. 炫耀财富和地位的人往往缺乏真正的内涵。 |近义词：showoffs, braggarts |反义词：humblers, modest people |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flaunts his new car every chance he gets. = 他抓住一切机会炫耀他的新车。
They flaunt their success to everyone they meet. = 他们向遇到的每一个人炫耀他们的成功。


一般过去时:
She flaunted her diamond necklace at the party. = 她在派对上炫耀她的钻石项链。
He flaunted his knowledge of art history to impress her. = 他炫耀他对艺术史的了解以打动她。


现在完成时:
He has flaunted his wealth throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都炫耀他的财富。
They have flaunted their connections to gain an advantage. = 他们炫耀他们的关系以获得优势。


过去完成时:
She had flaunted her engagement ring for months before the wedding. = 婚礼前几个月她一直在炫耀她的订婚戒指。
They had flaunted their achievements to secure the promotion. = 他们炫耀他们的成就以确保晋升。


将来时:
He will flaunt his new promotion at the office tomorrow. = 他明天会在办公室炫耀他的新晋升。
She will flaunt her skills during the interview. = 她将在面试中炫耀她的技能。


虚拟语气:
If he were less insecure, he wouldn't flaunt his possessions so much. = 如果他不那么缺乏安全感，他就不会如此炫耀他的财产。
I wish they wouldn't flaunt their superior knowledge all the time. = 我希望他们不要总是炫耀他们高人一等的知识。


被动语态:
His wealth was flaunted throughout the media. = 他的财富在媒体上被大肆炫耀。
Their success was flaunted to intimidate their rivals. = 他们的成功被炫耀以恐吓他们的对手。


疑问句:
Why does he flaunt his wealth so openly? = 他为什么如此公开地炫耀他的财富？
Are they flaunting their achievements to impress us? = 他们是在炫耀他们的成就以打动我们吗？


否定句:
He didn't flaunt his connections to get the job. = 他没有炫耀他的关系来获得这份工作。
She doesn't flaunt her intelligence; she lets her actions speak for themselves. = 她不炫耀她的聪明才智；她让她的行动为自己说话。
