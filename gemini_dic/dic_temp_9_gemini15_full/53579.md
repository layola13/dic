dislocation:/ˌdɪsˌloʊˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |脱位；错位；位移；错乱 |The displacement of a bone from its joint.  Dislocation refers to the displacement of a bone from its normal position within a joint. It often results from trauma or injury, causing pain, swelling, and limited movement.  |例句：The accident resulted in a shoulder dislocation.  事故导致肩关节脱位。

The patient suffered a hip dislocation. 病人遭受了髋关节脱位。


近义词：displacement, misalignment |反义词：alignment, reduction


词源：dis- (否定前缀) + location (位置)
