cluttery:/ˈklʌtəri/| adj.|凌乱的，杂乱的 | full of clutter; messy and disorganized.  |例句：The room was cluttery with toys and clothes strewn everywhere.  房间里乱七八糟的，玩具和衣服到处乱扔。|近义词：messy, untidy, disorganized, cluttered |反义词：tidy, organized, neat, orderly
