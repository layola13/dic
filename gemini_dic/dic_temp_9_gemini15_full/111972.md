lompish:/ˈlɒmpɪʃ/| adj.|迟钝的，懒散的，行动迟缓的|Sluggish and apathetic; lacking energy or enthusiasm.  It often implies a dullness or heaviness of movement or mind.|例句：The lompish dog lay in the sun, unwilling to move.  那条懒洋洋的狗躺在阳光下，不愿动弹。

例句：After a long day's work, he felt lompish and went straight to bed.  一天辛苦的工作后，他感到疲惫不堪，直接上床睡觉了。


近义词：sluggish, lethargic, languid, heavy, apathetic, listless

反义词：energetic, lively, vigorous, sprightly, dynamic


词源:The word "lompish" is of uncertain origin.  It likely emerged in the 16th century as a dialectal or informal variant, possibly related to words suggesting heaviness or clumsiness.  Its etymology is not definitively traceable to a single root.
