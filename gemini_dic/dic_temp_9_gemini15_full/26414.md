byers:/ˈbaɪərz/| n.|买方，购买者|A buyer is a person or organization that buys something.  The term is often used in the context of commercial transactions, such as real estate or stock markets.|例句：The buyers were impressed by the quality of the products. 买家对产品的质量印象深刻。|例句：We need to find more buyers for our new product. 我们需要为我们的新产品找到更多买家。|近义词：purchaser, customer, consumer|反义词：seller, vendor
