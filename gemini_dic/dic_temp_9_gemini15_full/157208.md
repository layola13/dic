pink-slipped:/pɪŋkˈslɪpt/| adj. v.|被解雇的；解雇|To be pink-slipped means to be dismissed from one's job, often in a sudden and unexpected way.  The term originates from the practice of using pink slips of paper to inform employees of their termination.|例句：He was pink-slipped last week due to company downsizing. 他上周因为公司裁员而被解雇了。
She received her pink slip this morning, leaving her quite distraught. 她今天早上收到了解雇通知，让她非常沮丧。|近义词：fired, dismissed, laid off, terminated|反义词：hired, employed
