rekindle: /ˌriːˈkɪndl/ | vt. |重新点燃；复燃；使复兴 | To reawaken or revive something, especially a feeling, relationship, or activity.  |例句：The couple rekindled their romance after years apart.  这对夫妇在分开多年后重燃爱火。

例句：The success of the new album rekindled interest in the band. 这张新专辑的成功重新燃起了人们对该乐队的兴趣。


近义词：revive, reignite, renew, reawaken | 反义词：extinguish, quench, suppress


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rekindles the fire every morning. 他每天早上都重新点燃炉火。
She rekindles her passion for painting. 她重新燃起了她对绘画的热情。

一般过去时:
They rekindled their friendship after a long quarrel. 他们经过长时间的争吵后重修旧好。
The spark rekindled the dry grass. 火星点燃了干草。

现在完成时:
We have rekindled our love after many years.  我们多年后重燃爱火。
The project has rekindled hope in the community.  这个项目重新燃起了社区的希望。

过去完成时:
They had rekindled their romance before the wedding.  他们在婚礼前重燃爱火。
She had rekindled her interest in music before starting lessons.  她在开始上课之前重新燃起了对音乐的兴趣。

将来时:
I will rekindle the fire when it gets cold. 天冷的时候我会重新点燃火。
They will rekindle their friendship next year.  他们明年将重修旧好。

虚拟语气:
If I had the chance, I would rekindle the old friendship. 如果我有机会，我会重修旧好。
I wish I could rekindle the passion of our early years. 我希望我能重燃我们早年间的激情。

被动语态:
The flame was rekindled by a passing spark. 火焰被飞过的火星重新点燃。

疑问句:

否定句:
