pseudo-Germans:/ˈsuːdoʊˈdʒɜːrmənz/ | n. |假冒的德国人，冒充德国人的人 |Those who falsely claim German heritage or affiliation.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying deception or fraud.  |Example:  The investigation revealed that the so-called "pseudo-Germans" had fabricated their ancestry to gain access to certain benefits.  = 调查显示，那些所谓的“假冒德国人”伪造了他们的血统以获得某些好处。|近义词：imposters, frauds, pretenders |反义词：genuine Germans, authentic Germans |名词


USAGE->
This word, being a noun, doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses or aspects like the example word "what".  The usage focuses on its application in sentences.

Example 1:  The authorities exposed the pseudo-Germans and their scheme. = 当局揭露了假冒德国人和他们的阴谋。
Example 2:  Many pseudo-Germans were found to be seeking financial gain. = 许多假冒德国人被发现是为了寻求经济利益。


PHRASE->
This word doesn't readily form common phrases in the same way as "what".  Its usage is primarily standalone.  However, it can be used in phrases like:

Example 1:  A ring of pseudo-Germans = 一群假冒德国人
Example 2:  The case of the pseudo-Germans = 假冒德国人的案子
Example 3:  Identifying pseudo-Germans = 识别假冒德国人
Example 4:  Exposing the pseudo-Germans' lies = 揭露假冒德国人的谎言
Example 5:  The actions of the pseudo-Germans = 假冒德国人的行为


词源:

The word is a compound of "pseudo-" (from Greek ψευδής, meaning "false" or "sham") and "Germans" (referring to people of German nationality or origin).  Therefore, it literally means "false Germans" or "sham Germans".
