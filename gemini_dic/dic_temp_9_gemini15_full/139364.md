obscures: /əbˈskjʊərz/ | vt. | 使模糊；遮掩；使难以理解 | to make something difficult to see, hear, or understand |

例句：
The fog obscured the landscape. = 雾遮蔽了景色。

The dense foliage obscured the path. = 茂密的树叶遮挡了小路。


近义词：conceals, hides, veils, masks, clouds


反义词：clarifies, illuminates, reveals, exposes


词源：

From Old French *obscurer, from Latin obscurus "dark, obscure," from ob- "against" + scurus "dark."  The Latin root is related to the Proto-Indo-European root *skwē- "to cover," which also gives rise to words like "screen" and "shadow."
