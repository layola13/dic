dine out:/daɪn aʊt/| v. |外出就餐，在外面吃饭 |To eat a meal at a restaurant or other place outside of one's home.  |例句：We dined out at a fancy Italian restaurant last night. 昨晚我们在一家高档意大利餐厅吃饭。

例句：Let's dine out tonight; I'm too tired to cook. 今晚我们出去吃饭吧，我太累了，不想做饭。


近义词：eat out, have dinner out, go out to eat | 反义词：dine in, eat at home | 词性:Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We dine out every Friday. = 我们每个星期五都出去吃饭。
They often dine out at expensive restaurants. = 他们经常在昂贵的餐厅吃饭。


一般过去时:
We dined out at a seafood restaurant last weekend. = 上周末我们在一家海鲜餐厅吃饭。
They dined out to celebrate their anniversary. = 他们出去吃饭庆祝结婚周年纪念日。


现在完成时:
We have dined out at that restaurant several times. = 我们在那家餐厅吃过几次饭了。
They have never dined out at a Michelin-starred restaurant. = 他们从未在米其林星级餐厅吃过饭。


过去完成时:
We had dined out before we went to the movie. = 我们在去看电影之前已经吃过饭了。
They had dined out twice that week before they decided to cook at home. = 他们在那周已经出去吃过两次饭了，然后才决定在家做饭。


将来时:
We will dine out at a new restaurant next week. = 我们下周会在一家新餐厅吃饭。
They will dine out to celebrate their graduation. = 他们将出去吃饭庆祝毕业。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would dine out more often. = 如果我们有钱些，我们会更经常出去吃饭。
I wish we could dine out tonight, but I have to work late. = 我希望我们今晚能出去吃饭，但我得加班。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
Dinner was dined out on by the family last night.  = 昨晚这顿饭是全家人在外面吃的。(This sentence is awkward and unnatural.)
A special meal was dined out on to celebrate the occasion. = 特别准备的一顿饭在外面享用以庆祝这个场合。（This is slightly more natural but still uncommon.）


疑问句:
Do you want to dine out tonight? = 你今晚想出去吃饭吗？
Where will you dine out this weekend? = 你这个周末会在哪里吃饭？


否定句:
We don't dine out very often. = 我们不经常出去吃饭。
They didn't dine out last night because they were tired. = 他们昨晚没有出去吃饭，因为他们累了。
