phycoerythrin: /ˌfaɪkoʊˈɛrɪθrɪn/ | n. |藻红蛋白|A red pigment found in red algae and some cyanobacteria, which absorbs light energy for photosynthesis.  It is a type of accessory pigment, meaning it helps chlorophyll absorb a wider range of light wavelengths. Phycoerythrin is also used in some applications as a fluorescent marker.|  Phycoerythrin is a crucial pigment in many marine algae, enabling them to thrive in deeper waters where less sunlight penetrates.|  近义词：phycobilin (更广义的术语)| 反义词：无
