endemicities:/ˌɛnˈdɛmɪsətiːz/ | n. | 地方病流行率；地方性疾病的发生率 | The prevalence of diseases specific to a particular region or population.  It refers to the extent or frequency of endemic diseases within a defined area.  | Example:  The study focused on the endemicities of malaria in the rural areas. 这项研究的重点是农村地区疟疾的流行率。  The high endemicities of certain infectious diseases in developing countries are a major concern.  发展中国家某些传染病的高发病率是主要关注点。 | 近义词: prevalence, incidence, rate | 反义词: absence (of endemic diseases)
