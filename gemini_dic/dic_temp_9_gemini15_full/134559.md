nonlitigation:/ˌnɑːnˌlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |非诉讼|The process of resolving disputes outside of court.  It encompasses various methods such as mediation, arbitration, and negotiation.  Nonlitigation aims to find mutually agreeable solutions, avoiding the costs, time, and adversarial nature of formal legal proceedings.|The company prefers nonlitigation methods to resolve customer complaints.|近义词：alternative dispute resolution (ADR), dispute resolution, settlement|反义词：litigation
