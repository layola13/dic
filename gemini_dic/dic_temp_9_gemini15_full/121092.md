miscounting:/ˌmɪsˈkaʊntɪŋ/| n. |误数；错误计数 |The act of counting incorrectly; an instance of making a mistake in counting.  |例句：The miscounting of votes led to a recount. 投票错误计数导致了重新计票。|例句：His miscounting of the inventory resulted in a shortage of supplies. 他对库存的错误计数导致了物资短缺。|近义词：incorrect counting, erroneous calculation, miscalculation |反义词：accurate counting, correct calculation
