resoldering:/ˌriːˈsɒldərɪŋ/ | v. | 重新焊接 | The act of soldering again; the process of applying solder to a previously soldered joint to repair or strengthen it.  This often involves removing the old solder first. | Example: The technician performed a resoldering operation on the circuit board to fix the faulty connection.  = 技师对电路板进行了重新焊接操作以修复故障连接。

The resoldering of the loose wire restored power to the device. = 重新焊接松动的电线恢复了设备的电源。|近义词：resolder, repair, reflow |反义词：unsoldering, disconnecting
