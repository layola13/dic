dictionaries:/ˈdɪkʃənɛriz/ | n. | 词典；字典 | A book or electronic resource containing a selection of the words of a language, giving their meanings, pronunciations, and often etymologies.  | Example: I looked up the word in several dictionaries to be sure of its meaning. 我查了几本词典以确保其含义。 |近义词:lexicons, wordbooks |反义词: N/A
