creature comfort:/ˈkriːtʃə ˈkʌmfərt/ | n. |生活上的舒适；物质享受 |Material comforts that contribute to a person's ease and well-being.  These comforts are often related to physical needs and desires, providing a sense of ease and relaxation. |例句：The hotel offered every creature comfort imaginable. 这家酒店提供了你能想到的一切舒适设施。

例句：After a long journey, she enjoyed the creature comforts of a warm bath and a soft bed. 长途旅行后，她享受着温暖的浴缸和柔软的床带来的舒适。


近义词：luxury, comfort, ease, convenience

反义词：hardship, discomfort, privation, austerity

词性：名词
