misconstruer:/ˌmɪskənˈstruːər/| n. |误解者，曲解者 |One who misconstrues; one who interprets wrongly.  A person who misunderstands or misinterprets something. |例句：He was a misconstruer of facts, always twisting things to fit his own narrative. 他是个曲解事实的人，总是歪曲事实以符合他自己的说法。|例句：The misconstruer of the agreement caused a significant dispute between the parties.  对协议的误解导致了双方之间严重的争议。|近义词：misinterpreter, misreader |反义词：interpreter (accurate), clarifier
