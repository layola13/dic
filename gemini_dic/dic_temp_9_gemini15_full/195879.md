sensation:/senˈseɪʃən/| n.|感觉，知觉；轰动，轰动事件 |a feeling that is experienced through the senses or a physical feeling that is caused by illness or injury; a feeling of excitement and interest felt by many people |例句：The sensation of the sun on my skin was warm and pleasant.  阳光照在皮肤上的感觉温暖而舒适。  The new movie is creating quite a sensation.这部新电影引起了很大的轰动。|近义词：feeling, perception, excitement, stir |反义词：numbness, apathy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cold wind produces a sensation of chill. = 冷风让人感到寒冷。
This new technology creates a sensation in the market. = 这项新技术在市场上引起轰动。

一般过去时:
I felt a strange sensation in my foot. = 我感到我的脚有奇怪的感觉。
The singer's performance caused a sensation. = 歌手的表演引起了轰动。

现在完成时:
She has experienced many sensations throughout her life. = 她一生中经历过许多感觉。
This book has become a sensation since its publication. = 这本书自出版以来就成为轰动之作。

过去完成时:
Before the accident, he had never felt such a strong sensation of pain. = 事故发生前，他从未感受过如此强烈的疼痛感。
By the end of the concert, the band had created a sensation. = 音乐会结束时，乐队已经引起轰动。

将来时:
I think this new product will create a sensation. = 我认为这款新产品将会引起轰动。
The new drug is expected to produce a sensation in the medical field. = 预计这种新药会在医学领域引起轰动。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't touched the hot stove, I wouldn't have had that burning sensation. = 如果我没有碰到热炉子，我就不会有那种灼烧感。
If this artist released an album, it would certainly be a sensation. = 如果这位艺术家发行专辑，那肯定会有轰动效应。

被动语态:
A tingling sensation was felt in her fingers. = 她的手指感到一阵刺痛。
A sensation of fear was produced by the loud noise. = 巨响引起了恐惧感。

疑问句:
What sensation did you experience? = 你感觉到了什么？
What kind of sensation is this new perfume creating? = 这款新香水会产生什么样的感觉？

否定句:
I didn't feel any sensation at all. = 我什么感觉也没有。
The play didn't create much of a sensation. = 这出戏并没有引起很大的轰动。
