peplum:/ˈpɛpləm/| n. |褶边；束腰；(服装)褶裥上衣 | A short, gathered, or pleated fabric band worn over the waist, typically as part of a dress or top.  It's often used as a decorative element to add volume and shape to a garment. |例句：The dress featured a stylish peplum that cinched her waist. 这件连衣裙有一个别致的褶边，收紧了她的腰部。

The peplum top added a touch of elegance to her outfit.  这件褶边上衣为她的装扮增添了一丝优雅。|近义词：ruffle, flounce, basque |反义词：None directly applicable.

词源:

The word "peplum" comes from the Latin word "peplos," which referred to a type of long, loose garment worn by ancient Greek women.  Over time, the meaning evolved to specifically refer to the gathered or pleated band at the waist.
