geeking:/ˈɡiːkɪŋ/| v.|沉迷于；痴迷于（某事物）|Engaging intensely in a hobby or interest, often to the point of obsession.  It often implies a deep and detailed knowledge of the subject.|例句：He's been geeking out on vintage computers all weekend. 他整个周末都在痴迷于老式电脑。

例句：She spent hours geeking out over the intricate details of the fantasy novel. 她花了数小时痴迷于奇幻小说中错综复杂的情节。


近义词：nerding out, obsessing over, immersing oneself in


反义词：ignoring, neglecting, disregarding
