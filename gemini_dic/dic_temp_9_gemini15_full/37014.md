column's:/ˈkɑːləmz/| n. |专栏的；柱子的|The possessive form of "column," indicating belonging to or relationship with a column.  It's used to describe something related to a column, such as a column's content, a column's author, or a column's width. |例句：The column's author is a renowned journalist.  这个专栏的作者是一位著名的记者。

例句：We measured the column's height before constructing the building. 我们在建造建筑物之前测量了柱子的高度。


近义词：pillar's, post's (in some contexts)
反义词：None (it's a possessive form, not a descriptive word with inherent opposites)

词性:名词性词尾
