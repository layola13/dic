fraudcast:/ˈfrɔːdkæst/| n. |欺诈性广播；虚假报道|A fraudcast is a broadcast, typically online, that intentionally spreads misinformation or disinformation, often for malicious purposes such as financial gain or political manipulation.  It's a portmanteau of "fraud" and "broadcast". |Example: The website was accused of publishing a fraudcast about the election results.  这个网站被指控发布了关于选举结果的虚假报道。|近义词：misinformation campaign, disinformation campaign, hoax, fake news|反义词：truth, factual reporting, accurate reporting|名词
