nonmeeting:/ˈnɑːnˌmiːtɪŋ/| n. |非会议；未举行会议|A nonmeeting is an event or period where a scheduled meeting does not take place, often due to cancellation or postponement.  It can also refer to a situation where people avoid a formal meeting but still communicate informally. |Example: Due to unforeseen circumstances, the nonmeeting of the board left many crucial decisions unresolved.  由于不可预见的情况，董事会的未举行会议使许多关键决策悬而未决。|近义词: cancellation, postponement, informal communication|反义词: meeting, assembly, conference|名词
