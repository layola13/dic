de-ices:/diːˈaɪs/ | vt. | 除冰 | To remove ice from something, typically a surface such as a road or aircraft.  |  例句：The airport workers de-iced the runways before the flight. 机场工作人员在航班起飞前为跑道除冰。 |  例句：We need to de-ice the car before we drive. 我们需要在开车之前为汽车除冰。 | 近义词：clear of ice, thaw, defrost | 反义词：ice up, freeze


USAGE->

The airport regularly de-ices planes before takeoff. = 机场定期在飞机起飞前为其除冰。
