counterconvention:/ˌkaʊntər kənˈvɛnʃən/ | n. |违反惯例；反传统 |A counterconvention is a practice, belief, or behavior that goes against established norms, customs, or traditions.  It often implies a deliberate and conscious rejection of conventional ways of doing things. |Example: The artist's unconventional use of color was a counterconvention in the art world.  这位艺术家的非常规色彩运用在艺术界是一种反传统行为。|近义词：innovation, unconventionality, nonconformity |反义词：convention, tradition, conformity |
