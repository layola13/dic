caresser:/kəˈres/| vt. n.|爱抚，轻抚|To gently touch or stroke someone or something with affection or tenderness.|例句：She caressed the soft fur of the kitten. 她温柔地抚摸着小猫柔软的毛发。例句：His caress was gentle and reassuring. 他的爱抚轻柔而令人安心。|近义词：stroke, fondle, pet|反义词：strike, hit, slap|动词


USAGE->

She caresses her child's hair. = 她抚摸着孩子的头发。
