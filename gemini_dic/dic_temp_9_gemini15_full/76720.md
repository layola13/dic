gambling:/ˈɡæmblɪŋ/| n.|赌博，投机|The act of risking money or other valuables on an event with an uncertain outcome, often with the hope of winning more.  It can involve games of chance, skill, or a combination of both. |例句：Gambling can be addictive and lead to financial ruin. 赌博会上瘾并导致经济破产。|例句：He lost a lot of money gambling in Las Vegas. 他在拉斯维加斯赌博输了很多钱。|近义词：betting, wagering, speculation |反义词：saving, investing
