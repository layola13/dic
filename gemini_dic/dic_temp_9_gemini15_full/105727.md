kiondo:/kiˈɔːndəʊ/| n. |基翁多（一种非洲篮子）|A kiondo is a type of traditional basket typically made by the Makonde people of East Africa.  They are often intricately woven from sisal or other natural fibers and are used for carrying various items. |The woman carried her harvest in a beautiful kiondo.  这位妇女用一个漂亮的基翁多篮子装着她的收成。|  handbag, bag, container |  (无明显反义词) |名词
