rides:/raɪdz/| n. v.|乘坐；旅程；游乐设施；骑乘|The word "rides" can function as both a noun and a verb.  As a noun, it refers to an instance of traveling in or on a vehicle, or an amusement park attraction. As a verb, it means to travel on or in a vehicle, or to sit on and control an animal such as a horse. |例句：He enjoys rides on his motorcycle. 他喜欢骑摩托车。The children loved the rides at the amusement park. 孩子们喜欢游乐园里的游乐设施。|近义词：journeys, trips, travels (n.); drives, travels (v.) |反义词：walks (n.&v.)(in contrast to motorized rides)|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rides his bike to school every day. = 他每天骑自行车上学。
She rides the bus to work. = 她坐公共汽车上班。

一般过去时:
I rode my horse through the forest. = 我骑马穿过森林。
They rode the roller coaster yesterday. = 他们昨天坐了过山车。

现在完成时:
He has ridden that horse many times. = 他骑过那匹马很多次了。
She has ridden the subway to the city center. = 她坐地铁去过市中心。

过去完成时:
I had ridden my bike for hours before I arrived. = 我到达之前已经骑自行车骑了好几个小时了。
They had ridden the Ferris wheel twice before the power went out. = 电力中断之前，他们已经坐了两次摩天轮。

将来时:
We will ride the train to London tomorrow. = 我们明天将乘火车去伦敦。
She will ride her scooter to the park. = 她将骑滑板车去公园。

虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would ride to the beach. = 如果我有一辆车，我会开车去海滩。
I wish I could ride a horse. = 我希望我能骑马。

被动语态:

The new roller coaster is being ridden by many people. = 许多人正在乘坐新的过山车。

疑问句:
Do you ride a bike? = 你骑自行车吗？

否定句:
