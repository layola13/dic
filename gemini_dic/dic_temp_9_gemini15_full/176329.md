re-enjoy:/ˌriːɪnˈdʒɔɪ/| vt.|再次享受，重新享受|To experience the pleasure of something again.  It implies a previous enjoyment that is being repeated or renewed.|例句：After a long break, I'm re-enjoying my hobby of painting.  = 经过长时间的休息后，我又开始享受我的绘画爱好。

例句：We re-enjoyed the movie on DVD at home. = 我们在家用DVD重新欣赏了这部电影。


近义词：enjoy again, relish anew, savor again|反义词：dislike, abhor
词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I re-enjoy the quiet evenings. = 我再次享受宁静的夜晚。
They re-enjoy the music from their youth. = 他们再次享受他们年轻时的音乐。

一般过去时:
She re-enjoyed the taste of her childhood. = 她再次回味童年时的味道。
We re-enjoyed the beautiful scenery during our second trip. = 我们在第二次旅行中再次欣赏了美丽的风景。


现在完成时:
I have re-enjoyed this book several times. = 我已经多次重读这本书，并从中再次获得乐趣。
He has re-enjoyed the benefits of his hard work. = 他再次享受了他努力工作的成果。


过去完成时:
She had re-enjoyed the peace and quiet before the phone rang. = 电话响之前，她已经再次享受到了宁静与安详。
They had re-enjoyed the success of their project before the new challenges came. = 在新的挑战到来之前，他们已经再次享受了项目成功的喜悦。


将来时:
We will re-enjoy the summer vacation next year. = 我们明年将再次享受暑假。
She will re-enjoy the delicious meal at the restaurant. = 她将在餐厅再次享用美味佳肴。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would re-enjoy my favorite video game. = 如果我有更多时间，我会再次玩我最喜欢的电子游戏。
I wish I could re-enjoy those carefree days of my youth. = 我真希望能够再次享受我年轻时无忧无虑的日子。


被动语态:
The old song was re-enjoyed by a new generation. = 这首老歌被新一代人再次欣赏。
The classic movie is being re-enjoyed by many viewers. = 这部经典电影正在被许多观众重新欣赏。


疑问句:
Will you re-enjoy this opportunity? = 你会再次抓住这个机会吗？
Do you re-enjoy this activity? = 你还喜欢这项活动吗？


否定句:
I didn't re-enjoy the concert as much as the first time. = 我这次的音乐会没有第一次那么享受。
They did not re-enjoy the same feeling of victory. = 他们没有再次体验到胜利的喜悦。
