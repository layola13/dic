lollingly:/ˈlɒlɪŋli/ | adv. | 懒洋洋地；无精打采地；松弛地 |  In a lolling or languid manner; lazily.  It describes an action or state characterized by relaxation, limpness, or a lack of energy. | 例句：The cat sat lollingly in the sun.  猫懒洋洋地躺在阳光下。  He spoke lollingly, his words dragging. 他懒洋洋地说话，吐字不清。 | 近义词：lazily, languidly, sluggishly, listlessly | 反义词：energetically, vigorously, briskly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It modifies verbs describing actions performed in a relaxed or languid way.

Example Sentences (Illustrating the adverb's use with various verbs):

He sat lollingly by the pool. 他懒洋洋地坐在泳池边。
She watched lollingly as the clouds drifted by. 她懒洋洋地看着云朵飘过。
They talked lollingly for hours. 他们懒洋洋地聊了好几个小时。
The dog lay lollingly in the shade. 狗懒洋洋地躺在树荫下。
The flowers drooped lollingly in the heat. 花朵在酷暑中无精打采地低垂着。


PHRASE->
There aren't common set phrases directly using "lollingly". Its usage is more about modifying a verb to describe a manner of action.  Examples of phrases using similar words:

Lying around lazily: 懒洋洋地躺着
Hanging around languidly: 无精打采地闲逛
Moving sluggishly: 缓慢地移动
Speaking listlessly: 无精打采地说话
Acting lethargically: 行动迟缓地
