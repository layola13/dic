overneglecting: /ˌoʊvər nɪɡˈlɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 过度忽视 |  The act of neglecting something to an excessive degree.  It implies a failure to give sufficient attention or care to something, to a point where negative consequences are likely to result.  This is not simply a lack of attention, but a significant and harmful disregard. |


例句：

Overneglecting your health can lead to serious problems.  过度忽视你的健康会导致严重的问题。

They were accused of overneglecting the safety regulations, resulting in the accident. 他们被指控过度忽视安全条例，导致了事故。



近义词：

neglecting excessively, seriously neglecting, chronically ignoring, grossly disregarding


反义词：

paying close attention to, meticulously caring for, diligently attending to


词性：动词 (verb)
