dressing's:/ˈdrɛsɪŋz/| n. |调味汁；敷料；打扮；装饰|Dressings are sauces or liquids used to add flavor and moisture to food.  They can be used on salads, meats, or vegetables.  The term can also refer to the act of applying a dressing to a wound or the process of adorning something.|例句：She made a delicious vinaigrette dressing for the salad. 她为沙拉做了一种美味的香醋调味汁。

例句：The nurse applied a sterile dressing to the wound. 护士在伤口上敷上无菌敷料。

近义词：sauce, condiment, garnish, adornment

反义词：bareness, undressing
