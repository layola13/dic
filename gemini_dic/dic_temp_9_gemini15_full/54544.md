dislikes:/dɪsˈlaɪks/| v.|不喜欢，厌恶 |To have a feeling of not liking something or someone.  |例句：He dislikes spicy food. 他不喜欢辛辣的食物。|例句：She dislikes the noise of the city. 她不喜欢城市里的噪音。|近义词：disapproves of, abhors, detests |反义词：likes, loves, enjoys |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dislikes loud music. = 他不喜欢大声的音乐。
She dislikes her job. = 她不喜欢她的工作。


一般过去时:
I disliked the movie. = 我不喜欢那部电影。
They disliked the weather. = 他们不喜欢那个天气。


现在完成时:
He has disliked broccoli since he was a child. = 他从小就不喜欢西兰花。
She has disliked him for years. = 她多年来一直不喜欢他。


过去完成时:
I had disliked him before I met his wife. = 在我遇到他妻子之前，我就已经不喜欢他了。
They had disliked the proposal before the meeting. = 在会议之前，他们就已经不喜欢这个提案了。


将来时:
I will dislike this task if it's too difficult. = 如果这项任务太难，我将不喜欢它。
She will dislike the new rules if they are too strict. = 如果新规太严格，她会不喜欢的。


虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't dislike my boss so much. = 我希望我不那么讨厌我的老板。
If I disliked him less, I would talk to him more. = 如果我不那么讨厌他，我会和他多聊聊。


被动语态: (Dislikes is rarely used passively)
N/A


疑问句:
Does he dislike his new apartment? = 他不喜欢他的新公寓吗？
Do you dislike working overtime? = 你讨厌加班吗？


否定句:
I don't dislike her, I just don't understand her. = 我不喜欢她，我只是不理解她。
He doesn't dislike cats, he's just allergic to them. = 他不讨厌猫，他只是对猫过敏。


PHRASE->
dislike doing something: 不喜欢做某事
dislike of something/someone: 对某事/某人的厌恶
have a dislike for: 对…有反感
strong dislike: 强烈的厌恶
take a dislike to: 开始讨厌
