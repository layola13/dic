indo-germanist:/ˌɪndoʊˈdʒɜːrmənɪst/| n. |印欧语言学家|A scholar specializing in Indo-European languages and their history.  An indo-germanist studies the relationships between the various Indo-European languages, reconstructs their common ancestor (Proto-Indo-European), and analyzes their historical development.|Example: The renowned indo-germanist presented a compelling theory on the origins of the Germanic languages.  著名的印欧语言学家提出了一个关于日耳曼语起源的令人信服的理论。|近义词：comparative linguist, etymologist|反义词：N/A
