sketches:/ˈskɛtʃɪz/| n.|素描；草图；梗概；简略描述|A sketch is a quick, rough drawing that is not intended to be a finished work of art.  It can also refer to a brief outline or summary of something.|例句：The artist made a quick sketch of the landscape.  这位艺术家快速地画了一幅风景素描。

例句：He gave a brief sketch of the events leading up to the war. 他简要概述了导致战争爆发的事件。|近义词：drawing, draft, outline, summary|反义词：masterpiece, finished product, detailed description|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sketches every day. = 他每天都画素描。
She sketches beautiful landscapes. = 她画美丽的风景素描。

一般过去时:
I sketched a portrait of my friend. = 我为我的朋友画了一幅肖像速写。
They sketched the building before demolition. = 在拆除建筑物之前，他们为其画了草图。

现在完成时:
He has sketched many famous people. = 他已经为许多名人画过素描。
She has sketched the design for the new house. = 她已经为新房设计了草图。

过去完成时:
I had sketched the plan before the meeting. = 我在会议之前就已经完成了计划草图。
They had sketched several versions before deciding on one. = 他们在决定一个版本之前已经画了几个版本。

将来时:
I will sketch the scene tomorrow. = 我明天将要画下这个场景。
She will sketch the dress design for the fashion show. = 她将为时装秀绘制服装设计草图。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would sketch more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地画素描。
I wish I could sketch as well as he does. = 我希望我能像他一样画得好。

被动语态:
The building was sketched by a renowned architect. = 这座建筑是由一位著名的建筑师绘制的。
The design was sketched quickly and roughly. = 设计草图绘制得又快又粗糙。

疑问句:
Did you sketch the scene? = 你画了这个场景吗？
Have you ever sketched a person from life? = 你曾经画过真人速写吗？

否定句:
He didn't sketch the details. = 他没有画细节。
She doesn't sketch very often. = 她不经常画素描。
