disburden:/dɪsˈbɜːrdən/ | vt. |卸下负担；摆脱；吐露(心事) | To relieve of a burden; to unload; to unburden oneself. |例句：He disburdened himself of his worries to his friend. 他向朋友倾诉了他的烦恼。

例句：She disburdened the heavy box from the car. 她从车里卸下了沉重的箱子。


近义词：relieve, unload, unburden, free


反义词：burden, load, oppress


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disburdens his heart to his diary every night. 他每天晚上都向日记倾诉心事。
She disburdens the truck of its cargo. 她卸下了卡车上的货物。


一般过去时:
He disburdened himself of his secret. 他吐露了他的秘密。
The workers disburdened the ship of its containers. 工人们卸下了船上的集装箱。


现在完成时:
She has disburdened herself of a heavy responsibility. 她卸下了沉重的责任。
He has disburdened his mind of all anxieties. 他摆脱了所有的焦虑。


过去完成时:
He had disburdened his conscience by confessing his crime. 他通过认罪卸下了良心的重担。
She had disburdened the shelves of all the old books. 她已经把架子上的旧书都清理掉了。


将来时:
He will disburden himself of his worries after the exam. 考试结束后，他会卸下他的担忧。
They will disburden the plane of its passengers. 他们将把飞机上的乘客卸下。


虚拟语气:
If he disburdened his heart, he would feel better. 如果他倾诉心事，他会感觉好些。
If she had disburdened herself sooner, she wouldn't be so stressed. 如果她早点卸下负担，她就不会这么有压力了。


被动语态:
The truck was disburdened of its load quickly. 卡车很快就被卸下了货物。
His worries were disburdened by talking to a therapist. 通过与治疗师交谈，他的担忧得到了缓解。


疑问句:
Will he disburden his secrets to the police? 他会向警方吐露他的秘密吗？
Has she disburdened herself of her guilt? 她卸下了她的罪恶感了吗？


否定句:
He did not disburden himself of his problems. 他没有摆脱他的问题。
She couldn't disburden the heavy suitcase by herself. 她自己无法卸下那个沉重的行李箱。


PHRASE->
disburden oneself of  摆脱…；卸下…
disburden one's mind 吐露心声
disburden one's heart 倾诉衷肠
disburden the ship 卸货
disburden the truck 卸货


词源:
The word "disburden" comes from the Old French word "desburdener," which itself is a combination of the prefix "des-" (meaning "to remove" or "from") and "burden."  The meaning has remained consistent through its development.
