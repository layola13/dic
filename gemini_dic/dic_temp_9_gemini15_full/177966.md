reciprocalness: /ˌrɛsɪˈprɒkələnəs/ | n. |相互性，互惠性 | The quality or state of being reciprocal; mutual exchange or action.  It implies a two-way relationship where actions or feelings are returned or mirrored. | 例句：The reciprocalness of their relationship was evident in their constant support for each other.  他们的关系中的相互性体现在他们彼此之间持续不断的支持中。


The success of the project depended on the reciprocalness of effort from all team members.  项目的成功取决于所有团队成员共同努力的相互性。


近义词：mutuality, interdependence, reciprocity, interaction | 反义词：one-sidedness, unilateralism


词源:  Derived from the adjective "reciprocal," which comes from the Latin word "reciprocus," meaning "returning," "interchangeable," or "mutual."  The suffix "-ness" is added to create a noun signifying the state or quality of being reciprocal.
