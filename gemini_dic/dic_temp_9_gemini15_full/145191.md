overawing: /ˌoʊvərˈɔːɪŋ/ | adj. |令人敬畏的，令人印象深刻的 | Extremely impressive in size, power, or skill; inspiring awe.  |例句：The sheer scale of the project was overawing.  这项工程的规模之大令人敬畏。

The natural beauty of the Grand Canyon was overawing.  大峡谷的自然美景令人叹为观止。

近义词：awesome, impressive, awe-inspiring, daunting, magnificent

反义词：unimpressive, underwhelming, insignificant, commonplace


词源:  The word "overawing" comes from the verb "overawe," which combines the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively" or "to a great degree") with the verb "awe" (meaning "to inspire with reverence or fear").  Therefore, "overawing" describes something that inspires such a great degree of awe that it overwhelms or surpasses the observer.
