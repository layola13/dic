e-signature: /ˈiː ˈsɪɡnətʃər/ | n. | 电子签名 | An electronic signature is a digital representation of a handwritten signature, used to authenticate a document or transaction electronically. It can take various forms, such as a scanned image of a handwritten signature, a biometric signature, or a digital signature created using a digital certificate.  | Example:  The contract required an e-signature for completion.  这份合同需要电子签名才能完成。

|  The company uses e-signatures to streamline its document approval process. 这家公司使用电子签名来简化其文件审批流程。 | digital signature, electronic signature, online signature |  handwritten signature (反义词不直接对应，但意思相反) | 名词
