disintegration:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɪɡreɪʃən/| n. |解体；瓦解；崩解 |The process of separating into parts or losing cohesion; the state of being separated into parts or losing cohesion.  It can refer to physical objects breaking down, or to abstract concepts like social structures or systems falling apart. |例句：The disintegration of the empire led to widespread chaos.  帝国的解体导致了广泛的混乱。

例句：The rapid disintegration of the building after the earthquake was shocking.  地震后建筑物的迅速解体令人震惊。


近义词：decomposition, breakdown, collapse, fragmentation, dissolution

反义词：integration, unification, cohesion, consolidation

词性:名词
