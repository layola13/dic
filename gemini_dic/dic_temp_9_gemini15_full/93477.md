illustrates:/ˈɪləstreɪts/| vt.|阐明；说明；图解|To explain or make something clear by giving examples, etc.|The teacher illustrates the point with a diagram.老师用图表来说明这一点。|explains, clarifies, demonstrates|obscures, confuses|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This example illustrates the rule. = 这个例子说明了这个规则。
The chart illustrates the company's growth. = 这张图表说明了公司的增长。


一般过去时:
The professor illustrated his lecture with slides. = 教授用幻灯片来说明他的讲座。
The author illustrated his point with a compelling anecdote. = 作者用一个引人入胜的轶事来说明他的观点。


现在完成时:
Her work has illustrated the importance of early intervention. = 她的工作已经说明了早期干预的重要性。
Many studies have illustrated the link between diet and health. = 许多研究已经说明了饮食与健康之间的联系。


过去完成时:
The experiment had illustrated the flaw in the theory. = 实验已经说明了理论中的缺陷。
Historical events had illustrated the consequences of unchecked power. = 历史事件已经说明了不受约束的权力带来的后果。


将来时:
The upcoming report will illustrate the impact of climate change. = 即将来临的报告将说明气候变化的影响。
Further research will illustrate the effectiveness of the new treatment. = 进一步的研究将说明这种新疗法的有效性。


虚拟语气:
If the data illustrated the hypothesis, we would publish the results. = 如果数据说明了假设，我们将发表结果。
I wish the article illustrated its arguments more clearly. = 我希望这篇文章能更清晰地说明其论点。


被动语态:
The principle was illustrated by several examples. = 这个原理由几个例子来说明。
The effects of pollution are illustrated in the report. = 报告中说明了污染的影响。


疑问句:
Does this graph illustrate the data correctly? = 这张图是否正确地说明了数据？
How does the author illustrate his main argument? = 作者如何阐明他的主要论点？


否定句:
The data does not illustrate the hypothesis adequately. = 数据不足以说明这个假设。
The study failed to illustrate a clear connection between the two variables. = 这项研究未能说明这两个变量之间的明确联系。
