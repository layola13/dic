cross-leggedly:/ˈkrɔːsˌlɛɡɪdli/| adv.|盘腿地，交叠双腿地|In a manner with one's legs crossed at the ankles or knees.  This adverb describes the posture of sitting with one leg crossed over the other.|例句：She sat cross-leggedly on the floor. 她盘腿坐在地板上。

例句：He meditated cross-leggedly for an hour. 他盘腿打坐了一个小时。


近义词：with legs crossed, with crossed legs|反义词：with legs straight, with legs apart
