sighing:/ˈsaɪɪŋ/| v.|叹息，叹气|The act of sighing; a long, deep breath expressing sadness, weariness, or relief.  It often involves a sound.|例句：She sat sighing sadly after receiving the bad news. 她收到坏消息后悲伤地叹了口气。

例句：He let out a long, weary sighing after finishing the marathon. 他跑完马拉松后长长地叹了一口气。


近义词：groaning, breathing heavily, exhaling deeply|反义词：laughing, cheering, rejoicing|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sighs every time he thinks about her. 他每次想起她都会叹气。
She sighs deeply when she's tired. 她累的时候会深深地叹气。


一般过去时:

She sighed heavily and went to bed. 她重重地叹了一口气然后上床睡觉了。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
