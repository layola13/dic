corroborators:/kəˈrɑːbəreɪtərz/| n. |证实者，支持者|Those who provide evidence or support to confirm something.|The investigators interviewed several corroborators to verify the suspect's alibi.  调查人员采访了几位证实者来验证嫌疑人的不在场证明。|The witnesses' testimonies served as corroborators for the victim's account. 证人的证词为受害者的说法提供了佐证。|supporters, confirmers, witnesses|contradictors, deniers|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He corroborates the story with evidence. 他用证据证实了这个故事。
They corroborate each other's claims. 他们互相证实了彼此的主张。


一般过去时:
She corroborated her husband's statement. 她证实了她丈夫的陈述。
The documents corroborated the historical account. 文件证实了历史记载。


现在完成时:
The new findings have corroborated the earlier research. 新的发现证实了早期的研究。
Scientists have corroborated the theory with new data. 科学家们用新的数据证实了这个理论。


过去完成时:
The witness had corroborated the defendant's alibi before the trial. 证人在审判前证实了被告的不在场证明。
The evidence had corroborated the initial suspicions. 证据已经证实了最初的怀疑。


将来时:
Further investigation will corroborate these claims. 进一步的调查将证实这些说法。
The expert will corroborate the findings of the report. 专家将证实报告的调查结果。


虚拟语气:
If more evidence were found, it would corroborate the hypothesis. 如果找到更多证据，它将证实这一假设。
I wish the results corroborated my theory. 我希望结果能证实我的理论。


被动语态:
The theory was corroborated by recent studies. 近期研究证实了该理论。
His alibi was corroborated by several witnesses. 他的不在场证明得到了几名证人的证实。


疑问句:
Does this evidence corroborate your theory? 这证据能证实你的理论吗？
Will the new data corroborate the existing findings? 新数据会证实现有发现吗？


否定句:
The witness's testimony did not corroborate the defendant's story. 证人的证词并没有证实被告的说法。
The results failed to corroborate the initial hypothesis. 结果未能证实最初的假设。
