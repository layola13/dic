mitigated:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 减轻的；缓和的 | Reduced in severity, intensity, or pain.  |例句：The medication mitigated his pain.  药物减轻了他的疼痛。|近义词：alleviated, lessened, reduced |反义词：aggravated, exacerbated, intensified |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new policy mitigates the risk of fraud. = 新政策减轻了欺诈的风险。
This treatment mitigates the symptoms of the disease. = 这种疗法减轻了疾病的症状。

一般过去时:
The firefighters mitigated the damage caused by the fire. = 消防员减轻了火灾造成的损失。
The government mitigated the impact of the economic crisis. = 政府减轻了经济危机的冲击。

现在完成时:
The measures implemented have mitigated the environmental impact. = 实施的措施已经减轻了环境影响。
We have mitigated the risks associated with the project. = 我们已经减轻了与项目相关的风险。

过去完成时:
They had mitigated the potential problems before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经减轻了潜在的问题。
The company had mitigated the losses by diversifying its investments. = 公司通过多元化投资减轻了损失。

将来时:
The new technology will mitigate the effects of climate change. = 新技术将减轻气候变化的影响。
We will mitigate the risks by implementing a robust security system. = 我们将通过实施强大的安全系统来减轻风险。

虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have mitigated the damage. = 如果我们早点采取行动，我们就能减轻损失。
It is important that we mitigate the potential harm. = 重要的是我们必须减轻潜在的危害。

被动语态:
The damage was mitigated by the quick response of the emergency services. = 紧急服务的快速反应减轻了损失。
The risks are being mitigated through careful planning. = 通过仔细的计划正在减轻风险。

疑问句:
How can we mitigate the negative effects of this policy? = 我们如何才能减轻这项政策的负面影响？
What steps are being taken to mitigate the spread of the virus? = 正在采取什么措施来减轻病毒的传播？

否定句:
The measures taken did not mitigate the crisis. = 采取的措施并没有减轻危机。
The company failed to mitigate the risks associated with the new product. = 公司未能减轻与新产品相关的风险。

词源:
From Latin *mitigāre, from mītis ("gentle, mild") + -āre (suffix forming verbs).  The word ultimately traces back to Proto-Indo-European *mei- ("to change, exchange").
