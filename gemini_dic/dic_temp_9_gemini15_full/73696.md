forgives:/fərˈɡɪvz/| vt.|宽恕；原谅；赦免|to stop feeling angry or resentful towards someone for something they have done wrong.|例句：He forgave his brother for breaking his toy. 他原谅了他弟弟弄坏了他的玩具。

例句：She forgave him, despite everything he had done.  尽管他做了那么多错事，她还是原谅了他。


近义词：pardon, excuse, absolve|反义词：condemn, punish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forgives his friends easily. 他很容易原谅他的朋友们。
She forgives others' mistakes. 她原谅别人的错误。


一般过去时:
I forgave him for his lie. 我原谅了他的谎言。
They forgave her for her transgression. 他们原谅了她的过错。


现在完成时:
He has forgiven her many times. 他已经原谅了她很多次。
She has forgiven her parents for their mistakes. 她已经原谅了她父母的错误。


过去完成时:
I had forgiven him before he apologized.  在他道歉之前我就已经原谅他了。
They had forgiven her long ago. 他们很久以前就原谅了她。


将来时:
I will forgive you if you apologize sincerely. 如果你真诚道歉，我会原谅你。
She will forgive him eventually. 她最终会原谅他的。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would forgive him. 如果我是你，我会原谅他。
I wish I could forgive her, but I can't. 我希望我能原谅她，但我不能。


被动语态:
His mistakes were forgiven by his parents. 他的错误被他的父母原谅了。
Her actions were forgiven, but not forgotten. 她的行为被原谅了，但没有被遗忘。


疑问句:
Will you forgive me? 你会原谅我吗？
Does he forgive her easily? 他容易原谅她吗？


否定句:
I cannot forgive his betrayal. 我无法原谅他的背叛。
She will not forgive him easily. 她不会轻易原谅他。


PHRASE->
forgive and forget  既往不咎
forgive me for... 请原谅我...
ask for forgiveness 请求原谅
forgive oneself 原谅自己
to be forgiven 被原谅
