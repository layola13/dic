reacknowledge:/ˌriːækˈnɒlɪdʒ/|/ˈriːækˈnɑːlɪdʒ/| vt.|重新承认；再次确认|To acknowledge again; to reaffirm.  This implies a previous acknowledgment that may have been forgotten, doubted, or challenged.|例句：The company decided to reacknowledge its commitment to environmental sustainability after facing criticism. 公司在面临批评后决定重新确认其对环境可持续性的承诺。

例句：The court ordered the defendant to reacknowledge his guilt. 法院命令被告重新承认他的罪行。


近义词：reaffirm, confirm, reiterate|反义词：deny, refute, disavow|动词
