encrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/ | vt. | 覆盖；结壳；镶嵌 | To cover or coat with a hard substance, often a crust or layer of something.  |The ancient pottery was encrusted with layers of dirt. 古代陶器上覆盖着厚厚的泥土。 The shell was encrusted with barnacles. 贝壳上附着着藤壶。 | coat, cover, overlay | clean, remove | verb


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The stalactites encrust the cave ceiling.  钟乳石覆盖着洞穴顶部。
He encrusts his cakes with nuts. 他在蛋糕上镶嵌坚果。


一般过去时:
The old well was encrusted with minerals.  旧井被矿物质覆盖。
She encrusted the brooch with jewels. 她用珠宝镶嵌胸针。


现在完成时:
Salt has encrusted the rim of the glass.  盐结晶在玻璃杯的边缘。
The years have encrusted the old building with grime. 岁月使这座古老的建筑蒙上了一层污垢。


过去完成时:
The archaeologists found the artifact encrusted with mud after it had been buried for centuries. 考古学家发现文物被泥土覆盖，它已经埋藏了几个世纪。
Before the restoration, the statue had been encrusted with lichen. 修复前，雕像上长满了地衣。


将来时:
The effects of the salt spray will eventually encrust the ship's hull.  盐雾的影响最终会使船体结壳。
They will encrust the cake with marzipan. 他们会在蛋糕上覆盖杏仁膏。


虚拟语气:
If we left it outside, the rain would encrust it with dirt. 如果我们把它放在外面，雨水会把它弄脏。
I wish I hadn't encrusted the cake with so much sugar.  我希望我没有在蛋糕上放那么多糖。


被动语态:
The surface of the metal was encrusted with rust. 金属表面锈迹斑斑。
The ancient tomb was encrusted with precious stones. 古墓镶嵌着珍贵的宝石。


疑问句:

否定句:
