coworker:/ˈkoʊwɜːrkər/| n. |同事，合作者|A coworker is a person who works with you at the same company or organization.  |Example: I get along well with most of my coworkers. 我和我的大多数同事相处得很好。|近义词：colleague, teammate|反义词：supervisor, boss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My coworker helps me with my projects. = 我的同事帮我做项目。
She often talks to her coworkers during lunch break. = 她经常在午休时间和同事聊天。

一般过去时:
My coworker helped me finish the report. = 我的同事帮我完成了报告。
They discussed the project with their coworkers yesterday. = 他们昨天和同事讨论了项目。

现在完成时:
I have worked with this coworker for five years. = 我和这位同事共事五年了。
She has collaborated with many coworkers on successful projects. = 她曾与许多同事合作完成成功的项目。

过去完成时:
I had already spoken to my coworker before the meeting. = 我在会议前已经和我的同事谈过了。
They had completed the task before their coworkers arrived. = 他们在同事到达之前就完成了任务。

将来时:
I will ask my coworker for help tomorrow. = 我明天会向我的同事寻求帮助。
She will meet her coworkers for a team-building activity next week. = 她下周将与同事参加团队建设活动。

虚拟语气:
If I had a helpful coworker, I would finish the project sooner. = 如果我有一个乐于助人的同事，我会更快完成项目。
I wish I had a coworker who could speak Spanish. = 我希望我有一个会说西班牙语的同事。

被动语态:
The project was completed by the coworkers. = 项目由同事们完成了。
The problem was discussed among the coworkers. = 这个问题在同事之间得到了讨论。

疑问句:
Do you get along with your coworkers? = 你和你的同事相处得好吗？
Have you introduced yourself to your new coworkers? = 你向你的新同事介绍自己了吗？

否定句:
I don't know all of my coworkers' names. = 我不知道所有同事的名字。
She doesn't usually socialize with her coworkers after work. = 她通常下班后不和同事来往。


PHRASE->
get along with coworkers = 与同事相处融洽
work with coworkers = 与同事一起工作
collaborate with coworkers = 与同事合作
support coworkers = 支持同事
conflict with coworkers = 与同事发生冲突
