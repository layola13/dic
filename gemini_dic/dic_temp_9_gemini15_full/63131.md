ensnared:/ɪnˈsneərd/| vt.| 诱捕；使落入圈套；使陷入困境 |To trap or catch in or as if in a snare.  To involve someone in a difficult situation from which it is hard to escape. |例句：The bird was ensnared in a hunter's net.  这只鸟被猎人网住了。

The unsuspecting tourist was ensnared by a clever con artist.  毫无戒心的游客被一个狡猾的骗子骗了。|近义词：trap, catch, ensnare, snare, entrap |反义词：free, liberate, release
