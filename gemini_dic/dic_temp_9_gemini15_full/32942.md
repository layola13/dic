choppered:/ˈtʃɒpərd/ | adj. | 切碎的；剁碎的 |  Describes something that has been cut into small pieces, typically with a chopping motion.  | The choppered vegetables were perfect for the stir-fry.  切碎的蔬菜非常适合做炒菜。| minced, diced, shredded | whole, uncut | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef chops the onions. = 厨师正在切洋葱。
He meticulously chops the herbs. = 他细致地切碎香草。


一般过去时:
She chopped the carrots finely. = 她把胡萝卜切得很细。
They chopped down the old oak tree. = 他们砍倒了那棵古老的橡树。


现在完成时:
I have chopped all the vegetables for the soup. = 我已经把做汤的所有蔬菜都切好了。
She has chopped the wood for the fire. = 她已经把柴火砍好了。


过去完成时:
He had chopped the vegetables before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经把蔬菜切好了。
They had chopped down the tree before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经砍倒了那棵树。


将来时:
I will chop the tomatoes for the salad tomorrow. = 我明天会把西红柿切碎做沙拉。
She will chop the meat for the stew later. = 她稍后会把肉切碎做炖菜。


虚拟语气:
If I had a sharper knife, I would chop the vegetables more quickly. = 如果我有一把更锋利的刀，我会更快地切碎蔬菜。
I wish I had chopped the onions more finely. = 我真希望我把洋葱切得更细一些。


被动语态:

疑问句:
