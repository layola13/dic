reassertion:/ˌriːəˈsɜːrʃən/| n. |重申；再次断言；坚定主张|The act of asserting something again; a renewed or emphatic statement of a claim or belief.  It implies a stronger, more forceful statement than a simple assertion. |例句：The government's reassertion of its commitment to economic reform calmed the markets. 政府重申其致力于经济改革的承诺，平息了市场。|例句：Her reassertion of her innocence was met with skepticism. 她再次声明自己的清白，却遭到怀疑。|近义词：reaffirmation, reiteration, insistence|反义词：retraction, denial, withdrawal|名词
