/ˌpriːdɪˈpliːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 预先耗尽；提前消耗 |  Describes the action of depleting something before it is normally depleted, often to prevent a future shortage or to control a process. It implies a proactive measure taken to avoid a larger problem later on. | The company engaged in predepleting its inventory to avoid supply chain disruptions.  公司提前消耗库存以避免供应链中断。 |  preemptive depletion, anticipatory exhaustion | replenishing, restoring
