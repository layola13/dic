shahanshah:/ʃɑːhænʃɑː/| n. | شاهنشاه |King of Kings; Emperor; supreme ruler.|Shahanshah is a Persian word meaning "king of kings" or "emperor."  It was used to denote the supreme ruler of the Persian Empire, a title held by several powerful monarchs throughout history, most notably those of the Achaemenid, Parthian, and Sasanian empires.|The Shahanshah ruled over a vast empire, commanding legions of soldiers and controlling immense wealth.|近义词：emperor, sovereign, monarch|反义词：subject, commoner
