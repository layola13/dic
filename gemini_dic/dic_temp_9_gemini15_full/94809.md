impulsiveness:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪvnəs/ | n. |冲动性，易冲动 |The tendency to act on sudden urges or feelings without considering the consequences.  It often involves a lack of forethought and planning. |Her impulsiveness led her to quit her job without having another one lined up.  她的冲动让她在没有找到另一份工作的情况下就辞职了。| rashness, spontaneity, precipitateness | deliberation, carefulness, thoughtfulness | 名词
