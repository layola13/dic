overfurnished:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrnɪʃt/ | adj. | 陈设过多的；摆设过多的 | Having too much furniture; excessively furnished.  The apartment was overfurnished, making it feel cramped and cluttered. |近义词：cluttered, overcrowded, congested |反义词：sparsely furnished, minimally furnished
