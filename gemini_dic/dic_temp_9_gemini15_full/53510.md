disassociate:/dɪsˌoʊʃieɪt/|/dɪsəˈsəʊʃieɪt/| vt.|使分离；使脱离关系；使分崩离析|To separate or disconnect (something) from something else; to sever a connection or association.  The verb implies a deliberate act of separating or disconnecting.|例句：He tried to disassociate himself from the scandal. 他试图与丑闻撇清关系。

例句：The company is trying to disassociate its brand from negative publicity. 这家公司正试图将自己的品牌与负面宣传区分开来。


近义词：separate, disconnect, detach, dissociate, sever, alienate


反义词：associate, connect, unite, link


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disassociates himself from the group. 他与那个团体脱离关系。
The company disassociates its products from harmful practices. 这家公司将产品与有害行为区分开来。


一般过去时:
She disassociated herself from the controversial project. 她与这个有争议的项目撇清了关系。
They disassociated the two concepts in their research. 他们在研究中将这两个概念区分开来。


现在完成时:
He has disassociated himself from his former business partner. 他已经与他之前的商业伙伴断绝了关系。
The organization has disassociated itself from the extremist group. 该组织已经与极端组织撇清了关系。


过去完成时:
She had disassociated herself from the company before the scandal broke. 在丑闻爆发之前，她已经与该公司脱离了关系。
They had disassociated the two ideas before presenting their findings. 他们在提出研究结果之前已经将这两个想法区分开来。


将来时:
He will disassociate himself from the political party. 他将与该政党脱离关系。
The company will disassociate its products from child labor. 这家公司将把其产品与童工剥削区分开来。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would disassociate myself from that situation. 如果我是你，我会让自己远离那种情况。
I wish I had disassociated myself from that project sooner. 我真希望我早点与那个项目撇清关系。


被动语态:
He was disassociated from the project due to his unethical behavior. 由于他的不道德行为，他被排除了该项目。
The brand was disassociated from the celebrity endorsement after the scandal. 丑闻之后，该品牌与名人代言脱钩了。


疑问句:
Will he disassociate himself from the group? 他会与那个团体脱离关系吗？
Should the company disassociate itself from the controversial figure? 这家公司是否应该与这位有争议的人物撇清关系？


否定句:
He did not disassociate himself from the scandal. 他并没有与丑闻撇清关系。
The company will not disassociate itself from its controversial practices. 这家公司不会与它有争议的做法撇清关系。



PHRASE->
disassociate oneself from:  与…脱离关系
disassociate ideas: 区分想法
disassociate a concept:  将一个概念分离
disassociate a sound:  分离一个声音
disassociate from reality: 脱离现实
