duties:/ˈdjuːtiz/ | n. |职责；义务；税款 | duties refer to tasks or actions that someone is required or expected to perform, often as part of a job, role, or moral obligation.  It can also refer to taxes or customs imposed by a government. |例句：My duties include answering phones and scheduling appointments. 我的职责包括接听电话和安排约会。

例句：The company has a duty of care to its employees. 公司有责任照顾其员工。


近义词：responsibilities, obligations, tasks, charges | 反义词：privileges, liberties
