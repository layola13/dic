histonomy:/hɪˈstɑːnəmi/ | n. |组织学；组织结构学 |The study of the structure, organization, and function of tissues.  Histonomy is a less common term, often used interchangeably with histology, but with a possible emphasis on the overall organization of tissues rather than just their microscopic structure. |例句：The histonomy of the heart is complex, involving multiple tissue types working in concert. 心脏的组织结构非常复杂，涉及多种组织类型协同工作。


The study of the connective tissues revealed fascinating aspects of histonomy.  对结缔组织的研究揭示了组织结构学的迷人方面。|近义词：histology, tissue anatomy |反义词：暂无  |名词
