preliminary:/prɪˈlɪmɪnəri/ | adj. |初步的；预备的；预赛的 | preliminary means coming before something else and forming a necessary prelude or introduction. It suggests something that is done in preparation for something more important or complete.  |例句：The preliminary results are encouraging.初步结果令人鼓舞。

The preliminary meeting was held to discuss the plan. 预备会议是为了讨论计划而举行的。

|近义词：preparatory, introductory, initial, prior |反义词：final, conclusive, ultimate |形容词
