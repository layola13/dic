renavigated:/ˌriːˈnævɪɡeɪtɪd/| v. |重新导航；重新规划航线 | To navigate again; to plan a new course or route, often after encountering difficulties or changes.  |例句：After the storm, the captain renavigated the ship to a safer port.  暴风雨过后，船长将船重新导航到一个更安全的港口。

例句：The project was renavigated after the initial budget was exceeded.  在最初的预算超支后，项目被重新规划。


近义词：reroute, redirect, replan, rechart


反义词：stay the course, continue on the same path


词性:动词
