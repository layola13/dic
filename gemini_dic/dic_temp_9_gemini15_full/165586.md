prememorandums: /ˌpriːˈmɛməˈrændəmz/ | n. |备忘录（复数） | Preliminary notes or drafts of memorandums;  plural form of prememorandum.  These are often internal communications used for planning or discussion before a formal memorandum is issued. | Example: The prememorandums outlined the key points to be addressed in the upcoming board meeting.  备忘录草稿概述了即将召开的董事会上要讨论的要点。 |近义词：draft memorandums, preliminary notes, initial memos |反义词：final memorandums, official documents |名词
