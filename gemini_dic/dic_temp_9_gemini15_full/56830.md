dormouse:/ˈdɔːrmaʊs/| n. |睡鼠|A small rodent with a bushy tail, large eyes, and a habit of sleeping for long periods, especially during winter.|例句：The dormouse is a nocturnal creature, rarely seen during the day. 睡鼠是夜行动物，白天很少见到。  The dormouse hibernates throughout the winter months. 睡鼠在整个冬季都会冬眠。|近义词：mouse, rodent |反义词:None
