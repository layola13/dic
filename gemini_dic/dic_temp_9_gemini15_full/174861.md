racist:/ˈreɪsɪst/ | adj. | 种族主义的；种族歧视的 | Relating to or characterized by discrimination or prejudice based on race.  A racist person believes that their race is superior to others and often acts in discriminatory ways towards people of other races. | Example: His racist remarks were offensive and unacceptable. 他的种族主义言论令人反感且不可接受。  Example: The company was accused of having racist hiring practices. 这家公司被指控有种族歧视的招聘行为。|近义词：bigoted, prejudiced, discriminatory |反义词：egalitarian, unprejudiced, tolerant |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is racist towards minorities. 他对少数民族有种族歧视。
They are racist and often use slurs. 他们有种族歧视倾向，经常使用侮辱性语言。


一般过去时:
He was racist in his youth, but has since changed. 他年轻时有种族歧视，但后来改变了。
She was a racist who believed in white supremacy. 她是一个相信白人至上主义的种族主义者。


现在完成时:
He has been racist his entire life. 他一生都持有种族主义观点。
They have been accused of racist behavior multiple times. 他们多次被指控种族歧视行为。


过去完成时:
It turned out he had been racist all along. 原来他一直都是种族主义者。
We discovered she had been racist towards her colleagues. 我们发现她一直歧视她的同事。


将来时:
He will likely remain racist despite our efforts. 尽管我们努力了，他很可能仍然抱有种族主义观点。
They will continue to face consequences for their racist actions. 他们将继续为其种族主义行为承担后果。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't racist, he wouldn't have said that. 如果他没有种族歧视，他就不会那样说了。
I wish he weren't so racist. 我希望他不那么种族歧视。


被动语态:
His actions were deemed racist by many. 他的行为被许多人认为是种族主义的。
The policy was criticized for being racist and unfair. 该政策因种族主义和不公平而受到批评。


疑问句:
Is he racist? 他是种族主义者吗？
Are their actions truly racist? 他们的行为真的是种族主义的吗？


否定句:
He is not racist; he simply misunderstands cultural differences. 他不是种族主义者；他只是误解了文化差异。
She is not racist and actively fights against prejudice. 她不是种族主义者，并积极反对偏见。
