intrafoliaceous:/ˌɪntrəfəʊliˈeɪʃəs/| adj. |叶内的；叶片内的| Situated or occurring within a leaf.  The term refers to structures or processes that are found inside the leaf tissue, such as veins or certain types of fungal infections. |Example: The intrafoliaceous fungus caused significant damage to the plant's leaves. 叶内真菌对植物的叶子造成了严重的损害。|近义词:intralaminar|反义词:extrafoliaceous
