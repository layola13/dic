rearers: /ˈrɪərərz/ | n. |饲养者，养殖者 |Those who raise animals or plants.  People or organizations involved in breeding and raising livestock, poultry, or crops. |例句：The rearers of these prize-winning pigs are very proud of their achievements. 这些获奖猪的饲养者对他们的成就感到非常自豪。


例句：Experienced rearers know how to identify and address potential health problems in their animals.  经验丰富的饲养者知道如何识别和解决动物潜在的健康问题。


近义词：breeders, cultivators, farmers, ranchers


反义词：consumers, slaughterers (in the context of animal rearing)


词性: 名词
