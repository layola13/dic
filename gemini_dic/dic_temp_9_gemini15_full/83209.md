guzzlers:/ˈɡʌzələr/| n. |狂饮者；耗油量大的车；贪婪的人|A guzzler is a person or thing that consumes something, especially fuel or drink, in large quantities.  It often implies excessive or wasteful consumption.|例句：That car is a gas guzzler; it only gets 10 miles per gallon.  那辆车很耗油；每加仑只能跑10英里。|近义词：glutton, consumer, hog, wastrel|反义词：miser, conserver, frugal person
