reprimer:/rɪˈpraɪmər/ | n. | 责备者；训斥者；压制物 | A person who reprimands or criticizes severely; something that suppresses or restrains.  | The teacher acted as a reprimander to the unruly students.  老师对不守规矩的学生进行了训斥。| 近义词：rebuker, censurer, corrector | 反义词：praiser, encourager | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher reprimands disruptive students. = 老师训斥扰乱课堂秩序的学生。
He reprimands his employees for poor performance. = 他因业绩不佳而训斥他的员工。


一般过去时:
She reprimanded him for his lateness. = 她因他迟到而责备他。
The manager reprimanded the team for missed deadlines. = 经理因错过最后期限而训斥了团队。


现在完成时:
He has reprimanded his son several times this week. = 本周他已经训斥了他的儿子好几次。
The principal has reprimanded the students for their behavior. = 校长因他们的行为而训斥了学生们。


过去完成时:
She had reprimanded him before, but he continued to misbehave. = 她以前就训斥过他，但他继续不规矩。
The coach had reprimanded the player for his poor sportsmanship. = 教练因其缺乏体育精神而训斥了该球员。


将来时:
The boss will reprimand him if he makes the same mistake again. = 如果他再犯同样的错误，老板将会训斥他。
She will reprimand her daughter for breaking the vase. = 她将因为女儿打破花瓶而训斥她。


虚拟语气:
If he were to misbehave again, I would reprimand him severely. = 如果他再犯错，我会严厉地训斥他。
I wish I hadn't reprimanded him so harshly. = 我真希望我没有那么严厉地训斥他。


被动语态:
He was reprimanded for his inappropriate comments. = 他因不当言论而受到训斥。
The students were reprimanded for cheating on the exam. = 学生们因考试作弊而受到训斥。


疑问句:
Will the teacher reprimand the student? = 老师会训斥那个学生吗？
Have you ever been reprimanded by your boss? = 你有没有被老板训斥过？


否定句:
The teacher did not reprimand the student this time. = 老师这次没有训斥那个学生。
She didn't reprimand him, she just gave him a warning. = 她没有训斥他，只是给了他一个警告。


PHRASE->
reprimand severely = 严厉训斥
receive a reprimand = 受到训斥
issue a reprimand = 发出训斥
deserve a reprimand = 应该受到训斥
a formal reprimand = 正式训斥

词源:
From Old French *reprimander, from reprendre "to take back, retake, reprove," from re- "again" + prendre "to take."  The sense evolution is from "take back" (words) to "reprove."
