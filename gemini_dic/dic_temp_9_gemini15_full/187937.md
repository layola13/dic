rotfl: /ˈrɒtˌɛfˈɛl/ | interj. | 笑到肚子疼，笑到在地上打滚 | Rolling on the floor laughing; an expression of uncontrollable laughter.  It's used in online communication to convey extreme amusement.  | Example:  That joke was hilarious! ROTFL!  那个笑话太搞笑了！笑到肚子疼！ | 近义词: LMAO, LOL, ROFLMAO | 反义词:  N/A


USAGE->
This word is primarily an interjection, and therefore doesn't conjugate in the same way as verbs.  It doesn't have tenses or voices.  The usage is limited to expressing extreme amusement in informal written communication.

Example 1:  I just saw the funniest cat video ever! ROTFL! 我刚刚看到有史以来最好笑的猫视频！笑死了！

Example 2:  His attempt to sing was so bad, it made me ROTFL. 他的唱歌尝试太糟糕了，让我笑到在地上打滚。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "ROTFL". It stands alone as an expression of laughter.
