importunated:/ɪmˈpɔːrtʃəneɪtɪd/ | v. | 纠缠；强求；恳求 | To persistently request or demand something, often in an annoying or bothersome way.  |例句：He importuned his boss for a raise. 他纠缠他的老板要求加薪。 |例句：She importuned her mother to let her go to the party. 她苦苦哀求她母亲让她去参加聚会。|近义词：beseech, implore, dun, badger |反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->

He importunes his friends for money. = 他向朋友们要钱纠缠不休。
She importunes her parents to buy her a new car. = 她纠缠父母给她买一辆新车。

He importuned his teacher for a better grade. = 他向老师恳求更高的分数。
