elks:/ɛlks/| n.|麋鹿|The plural of elk.  A large deer with large antlers, native to North America and East Asia.|Example: We saw a herd of elks grazing in the meadow. 我们看到一群麋鹿在草地上吃草。|近义词：deer, wapiti|反义词：无
