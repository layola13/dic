glutinosity:/ˌɡluːtɪˈnɒsəti/| n. |胶粘性，粘性；胶状；粘稠度 | The stickiness or gumminess of a substance.  It refers to the quality of being gluey or sticky. |Example: The high glutinosity of the jam made it difficult to spread.  这果酱的高粘性使得它很难涂抹。  The artist appreciated the glutinosity of the paint for its textural effect.  艺术家欣赏这种颜料的粘性，因为它具有肌理效果。|近义词：stickiness, viscosity, gumminess, adhesiveness |反义词：dryness, crispness
