gargling:/ˈɡɑːrɡlɪŋ/ | v. | 漱口， gargling (n.) 漱口声 |  To rinse the mouth and throat with a liquid, typically to relieve a sore throat.  |例句：I gargled with warm salt water to soothe my sore throat. 我用温盐水漱口以缓解喉咙痛。  |近义词：rinsing, swishing |反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "gargle" ultimately derives from the Old French word "gargouiller," meaning "to gurgle" or "to make a gurgling sound."  This in turn is of imitative origin, reflecting the sound of the action.
