flayer:/ˈfleɪər/| n. |传单；剥皮工；（飞机的）襟翼|A flayer is a leaflet or pamphlet, often advertising something.  It can also refer to someone who flayers (skins) animals, or a hinged aerodynamic surface on an aircraft. |例句：The company distributed flayers advertising their new product. 这家公司分发了宣传其新产品的传单。
The butcher was a skilled flayer.  那个屠夫是一个熟练的剥皮工。


近义词：leaflet, pamphlet, handbill (for leaflet meaning); skinner (for skinner meaning)
反义词：N/A (depending on context)
