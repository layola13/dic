diverged:/daɪˈvɜːdʒd/| v.| 偏离；分歧；分叉 | To move or extend in different directions from a common point; to differ in opinion, form, or character. |例句：The two roads diverged at the crossroads.  两条路在十字路口分叉。

The scientists diverged on the best course of action.  科学家们在最佳行动方案上存在分歧。


近义词：differ, deviate, branch, separate, diverge from

反义词：converge, unite, merge, coincide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The paths diverge at the river. = 小路在河边分叉。
Their opinions diverge on this issue. = 他们在这个问题上的意见相左。


一般过去时:
The river diverged into two streams. = 河流分成了两股水流。
Their views diverged sharply during the debate. = 在辩论中，他们的观点截然不同。


现在完成时:
Our paths have diverged since we graduated. = 自从我们毕业后，我们的道路就分开了。
Their opinions have diverged significantly over the years. = 多年来，他们的意见差异显著。


过去完成时:
The roads had diverged long before we realized it. = 我们意识到之前很久，路就已经分开了。
Their arguments had diverged so much that reconciliation seemed impossible. = 他们的争论已经如此激烈，和解似乎不可能。


将来时:
The trails will diverge near the mountain summit. = 小路将在山顶附近分叉。
Their political stances will likely diverge further in the coming months. = 在接下来的几个月里，他们的政治立场可能会进一步分歧。


虚拟语气:
If the roads hadn't diverged, we would have met sooner. = 如果道路没有分叉，我们就会早点见面。
If their opinions had not diverged so drastically, the project might have succeeded. = 如果他们的意见没有如此大的分歧，项目也许会成功。


被动语态:
The river was diverged by a large rock. = 河流被一块大石头分流了。
The research findings were diverged from the initial hypothesis.  研究结果与最初的假设相悖。


疑问句:
Do the roads diverge here? = 路在这里分叉吗？
Do their interests diverge significantly? = 他们的兴趣差异很大吗？


否定句:
The paths do not diverge until the next village. = 小路直到下一个村庄才分叉。
Their opinions did not diverge on the matter. = 他们在这个问题上意见一致。
