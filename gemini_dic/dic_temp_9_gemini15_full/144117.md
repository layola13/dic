outscored:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːrd/| vt.|胜过；得分超过|To score more points than (someone or something) in a competition or game. |例句：The home team outscored the visitors by 3-1.  主队以3比1的比分胜过客队。
例句：Our team outscored their team by ten points.  我们的球队比他们的球队多得了十分。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outpoint|反义词：underscored
