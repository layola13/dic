effeminately:/ˈɛfəməneɪtli/ | adv. | 柔弱地；女人气地；娘娘腔地 |  In a way that suggests a lack of masculinity;  showing characteristics considered typical of women.  It often carries a negative connotation, implying weakness or a lack of manliness. | 例句：He spoke effeminately, his voice high and slightly breathy. 他说话柔弱无力，声音尖细而略带气喘。

He dressed effeminately, preferring silks and satins to coarser fabrics. 他打扮得女人气十足，喜欢丝绸和缎子胜过粗糙的布料。


近义词：delicately, weakly, girlishly, unmanly | 反义词：manfully, strongly, virility


词源：源自effeminate (adj.)，  其词根是拉丁语的 *femina (woman).  "-ately" 是一个副词后缀。
