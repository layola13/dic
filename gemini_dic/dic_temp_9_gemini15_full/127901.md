neoliberals:/ˈniːoʊˌlɪbərəlz/| n. |新自由主义者|Supporters of neoliberalism, an economic philosophy emphasizing free markets, deregulation, and privatization.|例句：Neoliberals advocate for policies that reduce government intervention in the economy. 新自由主义者主张减少政府对经济的干预。|近义词：free-market advocates, economic liberals |反义词：socialists, interventionists
