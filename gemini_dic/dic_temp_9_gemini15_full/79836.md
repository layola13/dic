glyph:/ɡlɪf/| n. |刻符；符号；象形文字|A glyph is a symbol that is used to represent a letter, number, or other character.  Glyphs are often used in writing systems, such as hieroglyphs or runes, and can also be used in computer fonts to represent characters.|Example: The ancient Egyptians used many different glyphs in their writing system.  古代埃及人在他们的文字系统中使用了许多不同的象形文字。 The font uses unique glyphs for each letter.  这种字体为每个字母使用独特的符号。|近义词：symbol, character, sign|反义词：无|名词
