loosestrife:/ˈluːsstriːf/ | n. | loosestrife (植物)柳叶菜属植物 |  Loosestrife refers to plants of the genus Lythrum, known for their vibrant flowers and often found in wet habitats.  They are sometimes considered invasive species.  | Example: Purple loosestrife is a beautiful but invasive plant.  紫松叶牡丹是一种美丽但具有入侵性的植物。  The wetlands are choked with loosestrife. 湿地被柳叶菜属植物堵塞了。 | 近义词:  Lythrum (genus name) | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it's a plant genus) | 名词
