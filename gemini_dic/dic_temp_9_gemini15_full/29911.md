cautious: /ˈkɔːʃəs/| adj. |谨慎的；小心的；慎重的 |Careful to avoid potential danger, risk, or harm.  Showing or having caution. |例句：He was cautious in his approach to the problem. 他谨慎地处理这个问题。|例句：She gave a cautious smile. 她小心地笑了笑。|近义词：careful, wary, prudent, circumspect |反义词：reckless, rash, impetuous, careless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is cautious about investing in the stock market. 他对投资股市很谨慎。
She is cautious with her words.  她说话很谨慎。


一般过去时:
He was cautious when crossing the busy street. 他过马路时很小心。
She was cautious in her dealings with the unknown man. 她与那个陌生人交往时很谨慎。


现在完成时:
They have been cautious about sharing personal information online. 他们一直对在网上分享个人信息很谨慎。
She has been cautious throughout her career.  她一直以来在职业生涯中都很谨慎。


过去完成时:
He had been cautious all his life, so he avoided the scam. 他一生都很谨慎，所以避免了这次骗局。
She had been cautious about making promises, so she didn't overcommit. 她一直以来都很谨慎地许诺，所以没有过度承诺。


将来时:
He will be cautious when he travels alone. 他独自旅行时会很谨慎。
She will be cautious in her negotiations. 她会在谈判中保持谨慎。


虚拟语气:
If he were more cautious, he wouldn't have lost so much money. 如果他更谨慎一点，他就不会损失这么多钱了。
I wish I had been more cautious in my choice of friends. 我希望我在选择朋友时更谨慎一些。


被动语态:
His approach to the project was cautiously planned.  他的项目方法是经过谨慎计划的。
The decision was made cautiously, after careful consideration.  这个决定是在仔细考虑后谨慎做出的。


疑问句:
Are you cautious about the risks involved? 你对所涉及的风险谨慎吗？
Wasn't he cautious enough in that situation?  在那样的情况下，他不够谨慎吗？


否定句:
He wasn't cautious enough and made a serious mistake. 他不够谨慎，犯了一个严重的错误。
She is not cautious by nature. 她天生就不是一个谨慎的人。


PHRASE->
cautious approach: 谨慎的方法
cautious optimism: 谨慎的乐观
cautious spending: 节俭的支出
proceed cautiously: 谨慎行事
a cautious reply: 谨慎的回答

词源:
From Middle French *cautieux, from Latin *cautus ("cautious, careful"), past participle of *cavere ("to beware, be cautious").  The ultimate origin is Proto-Indo-European *kau- ("to see, observe").
