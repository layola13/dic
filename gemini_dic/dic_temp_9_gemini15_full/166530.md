/ˈprɛzbɪtər/| n. |长老；牧师|An elder in certain Christian churches, especially in Presbyterian churches.  A presbyter is a member of the clergy, often responsible for pastoral care and leadership within a congregation.  The term derives from the Greek word "presbyteros," meaning "elder."|The presbyter delivered a moving sermon on forgiveness.  长老发表了一篇关于宽恕的感人布道。|minister, pastor, cleric|layperson, deacon (in some contexts)|名词
