commemorates:/kəˈmɛməreɪts/| vt.|纪念，庆祝|To remember and celebrate officially; to mark or observe (an event, anniversary, etc.) with ceremonies or other expressions of respect.|例句：The city commemorates the anniversary of its founding every year.  =  这座城市每年都会纪念建城周年纪念日。|例句：We commemorated the victims of the war with a moment of silence. = 我们以默哀的方式纪念战争的受害者。|近义词：celebrate, remember, observe|反义词：forget, ignore
