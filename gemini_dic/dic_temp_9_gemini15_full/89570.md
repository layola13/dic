honours:/ˈɒnərz/ /ˈɑːnərz/| n.|荣誉；优等成绩；尊敬；给…以荣誉|Honours refer to awards, distinctions, or high academic achievements.  It can also mean showing respect or giving someone a position of honour.|例句：She received many honours for her work. 她因其工作获得了许多荣誉。
例句：He graduated with honours. 他以优等成绩毕业。

近义词：awards, distinctions, accolades, respects, tributes|反义词：dishonour, disgrace, shame

词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The university confers honours on distinguished alumni. = 大学授予杰出校友荣誉称号。
His work brings him great honours. = 他的工作给他带来了极大的荣誉。


一般过去时:
She was honoured for her bravery. = 她因勇敢而受到表彰。
They honoured their commitments. = 他们履行了承诺。


现在完成时:
He has received many honours throughout his career. = 他在职业生涯中获得过许多荣誉。
The company has honoured its contractual obligations. = 公司履行了合同义务。


过去完成时:
They had already honoured their promise before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们就已经履行了承诺。
The award had honoured many deserving individuals before him. = 在他之前，该奖项已经表彰了许多值得表彰的个人。


将来时:
The school will honour the top students. = 学校将表彰优秀学生。
The government will honour its pledge to reduce taxes. = 政府将履行减税的承诺。


虚拟语气:
If they were to honour my request, I would be grateful. = 如果他们能满足我的请求，我将不胜感激。
I wish they would honour their word. = 我希望他们能信守诺言。


被动语态:
Honours were bestowed upon the veteran. = 荣誉授予了这位老兵。
His contributions were duly honoured. = 他的贡献得到了应有的认可。


疑问句:
What honours has he achieved? = 他取得了什么荣誉？
Will the company honour the contract? = 公司会履行合同吗？


否定句:
They did not honour their agreement. = 他们没有遵守协议。
The company failed to honour its commitments. = 公司未能履行承诺。
