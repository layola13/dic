frivolity:/frɪˈvɒləti/| n. |轻浮；轻率；琐事；嬉戏 |Lightheartedness, especially in a playful or slightly inappropriate way.  It often implies a lack of seriousness or a disregard for important matters. |例句：Her frivolity was a stark contrast to her sister's seriousness.  她的轻浮与她姐姐的严肃形成了鲜明对比。|例句：The frivolity of the party quickly faded when the news arrived.  当消息传来时，聚会的嬉闹很快就消失了。|近义词：levity, silliness, flippancy, triviality |反义词：seriousness, gravity, solemnity
