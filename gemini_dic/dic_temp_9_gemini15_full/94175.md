imper: /ˈɪmpər/ | adj. | 命令的；强制的；紧急的 |  Imperative means expressing a command, urging, or strong suggestion. It often describes something that is urgent or necessary.  | Example: The imperious general demanded immediate obedience.  将军以专横的态度要求立即服从。 | 近义词：urgent, mandatory, compulsory | 反义词：optional, voluntary, permissive
