misalign:/ˌmɪs əˈlaɪn/ | vt. |使错位；使未对准；校准错误 |To put (something) out of alignment; to arrange (something) incorrectly.  |例句：The wheels were misaligned, causing the car to pull to one side. 车轮错位，导致汽车向一侧偏。

例句：The printer misaligned the text, making it difficult to read. 打印机文本错位，难以阅读。


近义词：disalign, malalign |反义词：align, straighten
