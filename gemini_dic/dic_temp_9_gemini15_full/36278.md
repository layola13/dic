cohabits:/koʊˈhæbɪts/| v.|同居，共栖|To live together and share a residence, especially without being married.|例句：The couple cohabited for five years before getting married.  这对夫妇结婚前同居了五年。|例句：Many birds cohabit in large flocks during the winter months. 许多鸟类在冬季月份成群结队地一起栖息。|近义词：live together, dwell together|反义词：live separately, reside apart
