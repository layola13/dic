prebargain:/ˌpriːˈbɑːrɡən/| n.|预先谈判；预先协商|A prebargain is a negotiation that takes place before the main bargaining process begins. It often involves informal discussions to explore possibilities and build a foundation for more formal negotiations.  |Example: The two companies engaged in a prebargain to determine the feasibility of a merger before entering formal negotiations.|近义词： preliminary negotiation, prior discussion |反义词：final negotiation, concluding talks|名词
