skintight:/ˈskɪntaɪt/| adj.|紧身的，贴身的|Extremely tight-fitting; clinging closely to the body.  The adjective describes clothing that fits very closely to the body, often emphasizing the body's shape.|例句：She wore a skintight dress to the party. 她穿了一件紧身连衣裙去参加派对。|例句：His skintight jeans showed off his muscular legs. 他的紧身牛仔裤突显了他肌肉发达的腿。|近义词：tight-fitting, close-fitting, form-fitting|反义词：loose, baggy, oversized
