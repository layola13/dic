pinyon:/ˈpɪnjən/| n.|松子, 矮松,  |A pinyon is a pine tree of the genus Pinus, typically a low-growing species found in arid regions of North America.  The term also refers to the edible seeds (pine nuts) of these trees.|例句：We gathered pinyon nuts from the trees on the mountain. 我们从山上的树上采集松子。|例句：The pinyon pine is a characteristic tree of the southwestern United States.  矮松是美国西南部的一种特有树木。|近义词：pine nut (seed), nut pine|反义词：无
