metered:/ˈmiːtərd/ | adj. | 计量的；按表计费的；有节奏的 | Measured or regulated in amount or quantity;  using a meter to measure or control something; having a regular rhythm or beat. | 例句：The apartment has metered electricity. = 这套公寓的电费是按表计费的。  The poem has a metered rhythm. = 这首诗有节奏感。 | 近义词：measured, regulated, rhythmic | 反义词：unmeasured, unregulated, arrhythmic

He meters his words carefully. = 他谨慎地选择措辞。

The poet metered the lines with precision. = 诗人精确地为诗行安排了节奏。
