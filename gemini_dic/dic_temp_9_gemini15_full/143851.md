outplay:/ˈaʊtpleɪ/| vt.|胜过，技高一筹，比…表现更好|To surpass or exceed in skill or performance.  It implies a clear superiority in ability or execution.|例句：The young player outplayed his opponent with superior speed and agility.  年轻球员凭借其更快的速度和更灵活的技巧击败了对手。

例句：Our team completely outplayed the opposing team, winning by a landslide. 我们的球队完全压制了对方，以压倒性优势获胜。


近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass, excel, outmaneuver|反义词：underperform, be outdone, be surpassed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outplays his opponents every game. 他每场比赛都胜过对手。
The experienced chef outplays the newcomer in the cooking competition.  经验丰富的厨师在烹饪比赛中胜过了新手。

现在完成时:

Our company has outplayed its rivals in the market. 我们的公司在市场上胜过了竞争对手。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
