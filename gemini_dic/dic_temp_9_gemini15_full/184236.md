resolutory:/ˈrezəluːtəri/| adj.| 解除的；解决的；取消的 |  Relating to the dissolution or termination of a contract or agreement; capable of being rescinded or annulled.  It implies a condition that, if not met, will automatically void the agreement. |


例句：

The contract contains a resolutory condition that it will be void if payment is not received within 30 days.  = 合同包含一个解除条件，如果30天内未收到付款，则合同无效。

The resolutory clause in the lease agreement allowed the landlord to terminate the tenancy if the tenant failed to pay rent. = 租赁协议中的解除条款允许房东在租客未支付租金的情况下终止租赁。


近义词：terminating, annulling, voiding, rescinding


反义词：affirmative, confirming, ratifying


词性：形容词
