cream-of-the-crop:/kriːm əv ðə krɑːp/| n.|精华，佼佼者|The best of a group; the elite.  This phrase originates from the top layer of cream that rises to the surface of milk. This top layer is considered the best part. |例句：He's the cream of the crop in his class. 他是班上最优秀的学生。

例句：The company hired the cream of the crop from top universities. 公司从顶尖大学招聘了最优秀的人才。


近义词：best, elite, top-notch, select, superior

反义词：worst, dregs, bottom of the barrel, inferior


词性：名词
