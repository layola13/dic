outweighed:/ˌaʊtˈweɪd/| vt.|超过，胜过，比…重要|To be greater in weight, importance, or influence than something else.|The benefits of the project outweighed the costs.  这项计划的益处超过了成本。|The advantages of this plan far outweigh the disadvantages.  这个计划的优点远大于缺点。|近义词：surpass, exceed, eclipse |反义词：underweigh


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The advantages outweigh the disadvantages. = 优点超过缺点。
His enthusiasm outweighs his lack of experience. = 他的热情弥补了他缺乏经验的不足。

一般过去时:
The risks outweighed the potential rewards. = 风险超过了潜在的回报。
Her desire for independence outweighed her fear of failure. = 她对独立的渴望超过了她对失败的恐惧。

现在完成时:
The positive aspects have outweighed the negative ones. =积极方面已经超过了消极方面。
The benefits of this decision have clearly outweighed the drawbacks. = 这个决定的好处已经明显超过了坏处。

过去完成时:
By the time she finished, the benefits had outweighed the effort. = 等她完成时，好处已经超过了努力。
He realized that his ambition had outweighed his common sense. = 他意识到他的野心已经超过了他的常识。

将来时:
The long-term benefits will outweigh the initial investment. = 长期利益将超过初始投资。
The potential risks will likely outweigh the rewards. = 潜在的风险可能会超过回报。

虚拟语气:
If the benefits outweighed the risks, we would proceed. = 如果好处超过风险，我们将继续进行。
I wish the advantages had outweighed the disadvantages. = 我希望优点能超过缺点。

被动语态:
The disadvantages were outweighed by the advantages. = 缺点被优点超过了。
The potential problems were outweighed by the opportunities. = 潜在的问题被机会超过了。

疑问句:
Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages? = 优点是否超过缺点？
Does the potential reward outweigh the risk? = 潜在的回报是否超过风险？

否定句:
The disadvantages did not outweigh the advantages. = 缺点没有超过优点。
The cost of the project did not outweigh its benefits. = 项目的成本没有超过它的效益。


PHRASE->

outweigh the cost=超过成本
outweigh the importance=超过重要性
heavily outweigh =大大超过
