nonsecretory: /ˌnɒnˈsiːkrɪtəri/ | adj. |非分泌的|Not secreting or involved in secretion.  This term describes cells, tissues, or organs that do not produce and release substances into the bloodstream or other bodily fluids. | Example: The nonsecretory cells of the pancreas do not produce digestive enzymes.  胰腺的非分泌细胞不产生消化酶。|近义词:asecretory, non-producing|反义词:secretory
