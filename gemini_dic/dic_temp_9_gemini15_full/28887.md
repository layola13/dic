carpophagous:/kɑːrpˈɒfəɡəs/| adj.| 食果实的；以果实为食的 |  Relating to or denoting animals that feed on fruit.  | Example:  Many tropical birds are carpophagous. 许多热带鸟类是食果实的。  |近义词: frugivorous | 反义词: carnivorous, insectivorous

Etymology:

The word "carpophagous" is derived from the Greek words:

* **karpos (καρπός):** meaning "fruit"
* **phagein (φαγεῖν):** meaning "to eat"

Therefore, carpophagous literally means "fruit-eating".
