partisanly:/ˈpɑːrtɪzənli/ | adv. | 党派地；偏袒地；偏向地 | In a manner showing strong support for a particular party, cause, or person.  It implies a biased or prejudiced approach, favoring one side over others without impartiality. | The judge ruled partisanly in favor of the defendant, ignoring crucial evidence presented by the prosecution.  法官偏袒被告，忽略了检方提出的关键证据。 |  biasedly, one-sidedly, unfairly | impartially, objectively, fairly | 副词
