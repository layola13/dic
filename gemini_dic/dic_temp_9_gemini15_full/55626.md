disyoke:/dɪsˈjoʊk/| vt.| 解开；使分离；使脱离 | To separate or disconnect something from something else, especially a yoke.  |例句：The farmer disyoked the oxen after a long day of plowing.  农夫在犁地一天后解开了牛的轭。

例句：The two countries disyoked themselves from the economic alliance. 这两个国家脱离了经济联盟。


近义词：unyoke, disconnect, separate, detach

反义词：yoke, connect, join, unite

词性：动词
