economics:/ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks/ /ˌɛkəˈnɑːmɪks/| n. |经济学|The social science that studies the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services.  It analyzes how societies allocate scarce resources among competing uses.|例句：She is majoring in economics at university. 她大学主修经济学。|例句：The economics of the situation are complex.  这种情况的经济因素很复杂。|近义词：economy, finance, political economy|反义词：N/A
