footlights:/ˈfʊtˌlaɪts/| n.|脚光灯，舞台灯|The lights located at the front of a stage, near the floor, which illuminate the actors' faces and feet.  These lights are crucial for creating the atmosphere and visibility of a performance.|例句：The actors waited in the wings, bathed in the soft glow of the footlights.  演员们在侧幕等待着，沐浴在柔和的脚光灯的光辉中。


例句：The dramatic lighting, including the strategically placed footlights, enhanced the emotional impact of the scene.  包括精心布置的脚光灯在内的戏剧性灯光增强了场景的情感冲击力。


近义词：stage lights, stage lighting


反义词：None directly applicable.  (One could argue "darkness" as an antonym in the sense of the absence of footlights.)
