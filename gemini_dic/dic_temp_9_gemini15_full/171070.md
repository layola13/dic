psilomelane:/ˈsaɪləməˌleɪn/| n.|水锰矿|A black or dark-gray manganese oxide mineral, usually occurring as a massive, botryoidal, or stalactitic aggregate.  It's a significant manganese ore. |The psilomelane sample showed high manganese content, making it a valuable ore.  水锰矿样本显示锰含量高，使其成为宝贵的矿石。|pyrolusite, wad|  (no direct antonyms, but minerals with lower manganese content could be considered contrastive)|名词
