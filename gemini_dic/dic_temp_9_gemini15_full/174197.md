/kwɛɪnt/ | adj. | 奇特的；古怪的；雅致的；离奇的 |  Something quaint is attractively unusual or old-fashioned. It often suggests charm and a slightly odd or peculiar quality.  It can refer to objects, places, or people. | The quaint little village charmed all the visitors with its narrow streets and old houses.  那个古色古香的小村庄以其狭窄的街道和古老的房屋吸引了所有游客。| picturesque, odd, peculiar, whimsical | commonplace, ordinary, modern
