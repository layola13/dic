demagogues:/ˈdɛməɡɑːɡz/| n.|煽动者，蛊惑民心的政客|A demagogue is a political leader who seeks support by appealing to the desires and prejudices of ordinary people rather than by using rational argument.  They often use emotionally charged language and promises to gain power. |Example:  The demagogue used fear and anger to manipulate the voters.  煽动者利用恐惧和愤怒来操纵选民。  The rise of demagogues often signals a weakening of democratic institutions. 煽动者兴起往往预示着民主制度的衰弱。|近义词：agitator, rabble-rouser, firebrand|反义词：statesman, leader (in a positive sense)
