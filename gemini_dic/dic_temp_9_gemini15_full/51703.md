devis:/dɪˈvaɪz/| vt.|设计；发明；想出|To plan or invent something new, especially a plan or system.|The engineers devised a new system for controlling traffic.|设计；策划；构思|plan, invent, create, contrive|destroy, demolish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They devise new marketing strategies every year. = 他们每年都会设计新的营销策略。
The scientists devise experiments to test their hypotheses. = 科学家们设计实验来检验他们的假设。

一般过去时:
She devised a clever solution to the problem. = 她想出了一个巧妙的解决问题的办法。
He devised a plan to escape from prison. = 他策划了一个越狱计划。

现在完成时:
They have devised a new method for teaching English. = 他们已经设计了一种新的英语教学方法。
The team has devised a winning strategy for the competition. = 团队已经设计了一个赢得比赛的策略。

过去完成时:
They had devised a plan before they started the project. = 他们在开始项目之前就已经制定了一个计划。
She had devised a way to overcome the obstacle. = 她已经想出了克服障碍的方法。

将来时:
We will devise a new system for managing our resources. = 我们将设计一个新的资源管理系统。
The company will devise a marketing campaign to launch the new product. = 公司将设计一个营销活动来推出新产品。


虚拟语气:
If I had devised a better plan, the outcome would have been different. = 如果我制定了一个更好的计划，结果就会不一样。
It's essential that they devise a solution quickly. = 至关重要的是他们要迅速想出一个解决方案。

被动语态:
A new system was devised to improve efficiency. = 设计了一个新的系统来提高效率。
A comprehensive plan was devised to address the crisis. = 制定了一个全面的计划来应对危机。


疑问句:
Have they devised a plan yet? = 他们已经制定计划了吗？
Can you devise a way to solve this problem? = 你能想出一个解决这个问题的方法吗？

否定句:
They did not devise a suitable plan. = 他们没有制定合适的计划。
We could not devise a solution to the problem. = 我们无法想出解决这个问题的办法。


PHRASE->
