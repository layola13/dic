devotionalism:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. |虔诚主义；献身精神 |The excessive or zealous pursuit of religious devotion, often characterized by intense emotional expression and a focus on personal piety. It can also refer to intense dedication to a cause or ideal.|例句：The rise of devotionalism in the 17th century led to new forms of religious expression.  17世纪虔诚主义的兴起导致了新的宗教表达形式。

The fervent devotionalism of the monks was evident in their daily rituals.  僧侣们热烈的虔诚主义在他们的日常仪式中显而易见。|近义词：piety, religious fervor, dedication, commitment |反义词：apathy, indifference, secularism |词性：名词
