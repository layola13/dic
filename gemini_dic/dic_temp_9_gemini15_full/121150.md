misdescribes:/ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪbz/| vt.|错误地描述，误述|To give a wrong or inaccurate description of something or someone.|The article misdescribes the situation and ignores key facts. 这篇文章错误地描述了情况，忽略了关键事实。|近义词：misrepresent, misstate, distort|反义词：accurately describe, correctly portray|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misdescribes the painting's style. 他错误地描述了这幅画的风格。
She misdescribes her qualifications on her resume. 她在简历上错误地描述了自己的资格。


一般过去时:
The witness misdescribed the suspect's appearance. 目击证人错误地描述了嫌疑人的外貌。
The reporter misdescribed the events leading up to the accident. 记者错误地描述了导致事故发生的事件。


现在完成时:
The company has misdescribed its products for years. 这家公司多年来一直在错误地描述其产品。
He has misdescribed his role in the project. 他错误地描述了他在项目中的角色。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
The situation was misdescribed in the media. 媒体对情况进行了错误的描述。

疑问句:

否定句:
