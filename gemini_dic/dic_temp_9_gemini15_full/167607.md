prewrapping: /ˌpriːˈræpɪŋ/ | n. | 预先包装 | The act of wrapping something before it is ready for sale or distribution.  It often involves packaging items individually before they are bundled together.  This process can improve efficiency and protect products from damage. | Example: The factory line performs prewrapping of individual cookies before they are boxed for shipment. = 工厂生产线在饼干装箱发货前进行预先包装。 The prewrapping of the gifts ensured they arrived undamaged. = 礼品预先包装确保它们完好无损地到达。 | 近义词：prepackaging, advance wrapping | 反义词：post-wrapping, unwrapping |  名词
