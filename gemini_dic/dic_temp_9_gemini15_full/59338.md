earliest:/ˈɜːrliɪst/ | adj. |最早的，最先的 |  Earliest refers to something that happened or existed before anything else. It is the superlative form of "early".  | Example:  The earliest records of the city date back to the 12th century. = 这座城市最早的记录可以追溯到12世纪。


The earliest birds sang at dawn. = 最早的鸟儿在黎明时分歌唱。

| 近义词: first, initial, primal | 反义词: latest, final, last | 词性: adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The earliest train leaves at 6 am. = 最早的火车早上6点出发。
His earliest memories are of his childhood home. = 他最早的记忆是关于他童年的家。


一般过去时:
The earliest settlers built their homes near the river. = 最早的定居者在河边建造房屋。
She arrived earlier than anyone else, making it the earliest arrival. = 她比任何人都早到，这使得她成为最早到达的人。


现在完成时:
The earliest I have ever woken up is 5 am. = 我醒得最早的时间是凌晨5点。
They've completed the earliest phase of the project. = 他们已经完成了项目的早期阶段。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, the earliest bus had already left. = 当我们到达时，最早的一班车已经离开了。
The earliest he had ever seen snow was during his childhood. = 他最早看到雪是在童年时期。


将来时:
The earliest we will be ready is next week. = 我们最早下周准备好。
The earliest flight departs at dawn tomorrow. = 最早的航班将于明天黎明出发。


虚拟语气:
If I had known earlier, I would have arrived at the earliest possible time. = 如果我早点知道，我会尽早到达。
I wish I had started the project at the earliest opportunity. = 我希望我尽早开始这个项目。


被动语态:
The earliest date for the meeting was decided last week. = 会议最早的日期是上周决定的。
The earliest submissions will be given priority. = 最早提交的作品将被优先考虑。


疑问句:
What is the earliest you can arrive? = 你最早什么时候能到？
What was the earliest recorded instance of this phenomenon? = 这种现象最早记录的实例是什么时候？


否定句:
It wasn't the earliest opportunity, but it was the best we had. = 这不是最早的机会，但这是我们拥有的最好的机会。
The train wasn't the earliest one, but it was still quite early. = 这趟火车不是最早的，但仍然很早。
