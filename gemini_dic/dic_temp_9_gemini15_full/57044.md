doubt:/daʊt/ | n./v.|怀疑，疑惑；不相信|Uncertainty or lack of conviction; to feel or express uncertainty about. |例句：I have serious doubts about his honesty.  我严重怀疑他的诚实。

例句：I doubt whether he will come. 我怀疑他是否会来。|近义词：suspicion, uncertainty, disbelief|反义词：certainty, confidence, conviction|名词/动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I doubt his ability. = 我怀疑他的能力。
She doubts the truth of the story. = 她怀疑故事的真实性。


一般过去时:
I doubted his word. = 我怀疑他的话。
They doubted the wisdom of the decision. = 他们怀疑这个决定的明智性。


现在完成时:
I have doubted his motives for years. = 多年来我一直怀疑他的动机。
She has doubted her own judgment lately. = 她最近怀疑自己的判断。


过去完成时:
I had doubted his intentions before he confessed. = 他坦白之前我就怀疑他的意图。
They had doubted the feasibility of the project from the start. = 从一开始他们就怀疑这个项目的可行性。


将来时:
I will doubt his claims until I see the evidence. = 在我看到证据之前，我会怀疑他的说法。
They will doubt her sincerity unless she provides proof. = 除非她提供证据，否则他们会怀疑她的真诚。


虚拟语气:
I doubt if he would tell the truth even if he knew it. = 我怀疑即使他知道真相，他也不会说实话。
I doubt that she would have acted differently if she had known. = 我怀疑如果她知道的话，她会采取不同的行动。


被动语态:
His honesty was doubted by many. = 许多人怀疑他的诚实。
The feasibility of the plan was doubted by the experts. = 专家们怀疑该计划的可行性。


疑问句:
Do you doubt his sincerity? = 你怀疑他的真诚吗？
Does she doubt my competence? = 她怀疑我的能力吗？


否定句:
I don't doubt his intelligence. = 我不怀疑他的智力。
She doesn't doubt the accuracy of the report. = 她不怀疑报告的准确性。


PHRASE->
no doubt = 无疑地
beyond a doubt = 毫无疑问
in doubt = 怀疑中
cast doubt on = 对…表示怀疑
without a doubt = 毫无疑问
