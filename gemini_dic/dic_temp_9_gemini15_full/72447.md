flukeworm: /ˈfluːkˌwɜːrm/ | n. | 吸虫 | A flukeworm is a type of flatworm belonging to the class Trematoda, parasitic flukes that infect various animals, including humans.  They typically have a complex life cycle involving multiple hosts. | Example:  The flukeworm infection caused significant liver damage.  肝吸虫感染导致严重的肝损伤。 The researchers studied the flukeworm's lifecycle in snails. 研究人员研究了吸虫在蜗牛中的生命周期。 | 近义词: trematode, flatworm | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑healthy organism)
