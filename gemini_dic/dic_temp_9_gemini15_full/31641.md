chatroom:/ˈtʃætˌruːm/| n. |聊天室 |A chatroom is an online service that allows users to communicate in real time with each other through text messages.  It's a virtual space for online conversations. |Example:  I met many new friends in the online chatroom. 我在网络聊天室认识了很多新朋友。|近义词：online forum, discussion forum, message board |反义词：None
