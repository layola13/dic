protend:/prəˈtɛnd/| v.|假装，伪装|To pretend is to behave as if something is true when it is not, or to make believe.|例句：He pretended to be asleep. 他假装睡着了。

例句：She pretended she didn't see me. 她假装没看见我。


近义词：feign, simulate, affect, dissemble|反义词：be honest, be truthful|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pretends to be a doctor. = 他假装是医生。
They pretend they don't know. = 他们假装不知道。


一般过去时:
She pretended not to hear me. = 她假装没听见我。
He pretended he was busy. = 他假装很忙。


现在完成时:
I have pretended to be brave. = 我一直假装勇敢。
They have pretended to like it. = 他们一直假装喜欢它。


过去完成时:
She had pretended to be ill. = 她之前一直假装生病。
He had pretended he knew nothing. = 他之前一直假装什么都不知道。


将来时:
He will pretend he didn't see it. = 他将会假装没看见。
They will pretend to be surprised. = 他们将会假装很惊讶。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pretend to be sick. = 如果我是你，我会假装生病。
I wish I could pretend to know the answer. = 我希望我能假装知道答案。


被动语态:
It was pretended that nothing happened. = 人们假装什么都没发生。
The illness was pretended to be cured. = 那疾病被假装治愈了。


疑问句:
Does he pretend to be rich? = 他假装富有吗？
Are they pretending to be happy? = 他们假装快乐吗？


否定句:
He didn't pretend to understand. = 他没有假装理解。
She doesn't pretend to be perfect. = 她不假装完美。


PHRASE->
pretend to be = 假装是...
pretend to do = 假装做...
pretend that = 假装...
pretend sleep = 假装睡觉
make-believe and pretend = 假装和虚构


词源:
The word "pretend" comes from the Old French word "pretendre," which means "to claim, allege, or intend."  The meaning shifted over time to its current sense of feigning or simulating.
