USAGE->
This word is a noun, and therefore doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs do.  The usage changes based on the sentence structure, not the verb tense of the word itself.

例句2:  He commented on the fragrantness of the spices in the curry. 他评论了咖喱里香料的芬芳。
