keller:/ˈkɛlər/| n.|地下室，酒窖|A cellar is an underground room or rooms, typically under a house, used for storage, especially of food or wine.|例句：We keep our wine in the cellar. 我们把酒放在地窖里。|例句：The cellar was damp and dark. 地窖潮湿而黑暗。|近义词：basement, vault|反义词：attic, loft


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cellar holds a lot of old wine. = 地窖存放着许多陈年葡萄酒。
They store potatoes in the cellar. = 他们在地窖里储存土豆。

一般过去时:
We found some old jars in the cellar. = 我们在地窖里发现了一些旧罐子。
He cleaned out the cellar last weekend. = 他上周末清理了地窖。

现在完成时:
They have renovated the cellar. = 他们已经装修了地窖。
We have stored our winter supplies in the cellar. = 我们已将冬季用品储存在地窖中。

过去完成时:
The rats had already eaten all the cheese before we checked the cellar. = 在我们检查地窖之前，老鼠已经吃掉了所有的奶酪。
She had forgotten about the wine she'd stored in the cellar. = 她忘记了她储存在地窖里的酒。

将来时:
We will store our furniture in the cellar over winter. = 我们将在冬天把家具储存在地窖里。
They will inspect the cellar for structural damage. = 他们将检查地窖的结构损坏情况。

虚拟语气:
If we had a cellar, we could store more food. = 如果我们有地窖，我们就能储存更多食物。
I wish the cellar were drier. = 我希望地窖更干燥一些。

被动语态:
The cellar was flooded during the storm. = 地窖在暴风雨中被淹了。
The cellar is being renovated at the moment. = 地窖目前正在装修。

疑问句:
Is there anything in the cellar? = 地窖里有什么东西吗？
Have you ever been in a wine cellar? = 你曾经去过酒窖吗？

否定句:
There is nothing in the cellar. = 地窖里什么也没有。
We didn't find anything interesting in the cellar. = 我们在地窖里没有发现任何有趣的东西。


PHRASE->
