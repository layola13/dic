coanchor:/ˈkoʊæŋkər/| n.|联合主持人，共同主播|A coanchor is a person who presents a news program or other broadcast with another person. They share the responsibility of presenting the program.|Example:  She's the coanchor of the evening news.  她是晚间新闻的联合主持人。|近义词：joint anchor, partner anchor|反义词：solo anchor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The coanchor presents the news with her partner. = 联合主持人和她的搭档一起播报新闻。
He coanchors the morning show every weekday. = 他每天工作日都与人共同主持早间节目。


一般过去时:
They coanchored the debate last night. = 他们昨晚共同主持了这场辩论。
She coanchored the show for five years. = 她主持这个节目五年了。


现在完成时:
They have coanchored many successful programs. = 他们共同主持过许多成功的节目。
She has coanchored with him for over a decade. = 她和他共同主持节目超过十年了。


过去完成时:
Before the new season, they had coanchored the show for three years. = 在新一季开始前，他们已经共同主持这个节目三年了。
He had coanchored several news programs before this one. = 在这个节目之前，他已经共同主持过几个新闻节目了。


将来时:
They will coanchor the special election coverage. = 他们将共同主持特别选举报道。
She will coanchor the show next week. = 她下周将共同主持这个节目。


虚拟语气:
If she were the coanchor, the show would be more engaging. = 如果她是联合主持人，节目会更吸引人。
I wish I could coanchor a national news program. = 我希望我能共同主持一个全国性的新闻节目。


被动语态:
The news program was coanchored by two experienced journalists. = 这个新闻节目由两位经验丰富的记者共同主持。
The debate will be coanchored by a male and a female presenter. = 辩论将由一位男性和一位女性主持人共同主持。


疑问句:
Who will coanchor the show tonight? = 今晚谁将共同主持这个节目？
Does he still coanchor with her? = 他还和她一起主持吗？


否定句:
They do not coanchor any other shows. = 他们不共同主持任何其他节目。
She didn't coanchor the show last night. = 她昨晚没有共同主持这个节目。


PHRASE->
coanchor a news program = 共同主持一个新闻节目
coanchor a debate = 共同主持一场辩论
coanchor a television show = 共同主持一个电视节目
coanchor with someone = 与某人共同主持
coanchor a live broadcast = 共同主持一个现场直播
