recessing:/rɪˈsɛsɪŋ/ | v. |凹进；后退；衰退；暂停 |  To move back or inward; to become less active or prominent; to make a recess in.  |例句：The coastline is gradually recessing.  海岸线正在逐渐后退。

The economy is recessing. 经济正在衰退。|近义词：retreating, withdrawing, declining, subsiding |反义词：advancing, progressing, expanding, flourishing |
