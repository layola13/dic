millstream: /ˈmɪlˌstriːm/ | n. | 磨坊溪流；水流湍急的小河 | A stream of water, typically flowing swiftly, that powers a mill.  It often refers to a small river or brook, particularly one used to drive a watermill. | The millstream tumbled over the rocks, creating a picturesque scene. 磨坊溪流飞溅在岩石上，构成了一幅美丽的画面。  The old mill was powered by a strong millstream. 老磨坊是由一条湍急的磨坊溪流驱动的。 | stream, brook, creek |  (no direct antonym, but a still pond could be considered a contrast) | 名词
