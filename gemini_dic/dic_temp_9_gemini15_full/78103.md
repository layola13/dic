geography:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfi/ /dʒiˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |地理学，地理；地貌|The scientific study of the Earth's surface, its features, and the distribution of life on it.  It encompasses physical geography (e.g., landforms, climate, etc.) and human geography (e.g., population, culture, economics, etc.).|例句：He is majoring in geography at university. 他大学主修地理学。

例句：The geography of the region is characterized by rugged mountains and fertile valleys.  该地区的地形特征是崎岖的山脉和肥沃的山谷。
