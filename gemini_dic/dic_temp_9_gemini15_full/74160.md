fountain:/ˈfaʊntən/ | n. |喷泉，源泉 |A structure that spouts water, often decorative.  A source or origin of something. |例句：The Trevi Fountain in Rome is a popular tourist attraction. 罗马的许愿池是一个受欢迎的旅游景点。

The fountain of youth is a legendary spring said to restore youth. 青春之泉是传说中能恢复青春的泉水。


近义词：spring, source, jet, well |反义词：dryness, drought
