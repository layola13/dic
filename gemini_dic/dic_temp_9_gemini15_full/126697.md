name-drops:/ˈneɪmˌdrɑːps/ | vt. |炫耀式地提及(名人或名事)|To casually mention the names of famous people, places, or things to impress someone or to appear sophisticated.|例句：He constantly name-drops celebrities he's supposedly met. 他不断炫耀式地提及他声称认识的名人。|例句：She name-dropped famous designers in an attempt to impress her date. 她为了给她的约会对象留下印象，炫耀式地提到了著名设计师的名字。|近义词：mention, drop (a name), boast, brag|反义词：remain silent, be humble, downplay|动词
