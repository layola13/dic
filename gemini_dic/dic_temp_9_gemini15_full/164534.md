preestimates:/ˌpriːˈɛstɪmeɪts/| n.|预估，预先估计|A preestimate is a preliminary estimation or calculation made before a more precise measurement or assessment is possible.  It often involves making a rough calculation based on available information.|例句：The team's preestimates for the project were significantly lower than the actual costs.  团队对项目的预估远低于实际成本。

例句：Based on the preestimates, we decided to allocate more resources to the project. 根据预估，我们决定为该项目分配更多资源。


近义词：forecasts, projections, estimations, predictions|反义词：post-analysis, final figures|词性：名词
