despairful: /dɪsˈpɛərfl/ | adj. | 绝望的；令人绝望的 | feeling or causing despair; hopeless. |


英文解析:The word "despairful" describes something or someone filled with or causing despair, a complete loss of hope.  It emphasizes the feeling of hopelessness and often implies a sense of overwhelming sadness and dejection.


例句:

His despairful eyes reflected the crushing weight of his loss.  他的绝望的眼神反映了他巨大的损失。

The despairful situation in the war-torn country left many feeling helpless.  战乱国家的绝望局势让许多人感到无助。


近义词: hopeless, despondent, dejected, disheartened, pessimistic


反义词: hopeful, optimistic, cheerful, buoyant, encouraged


词性: adjective
