orientates:/ˈɔːriɛnteɪts/| vt.|使适应；使熟悉；定向；确定方向|To familiarize someone with a new situation, environment, or subject.  To adjust or align something with a particular direction or standard.|例句：The course orientates students to the challenges of modern business. 这门课程使学生们熟悉现代商业的挑战。

例句：The compass helped orientate the hikers in the dense forest. 指南针帮助徒步旅行者在茂密的森林中确定方向。


近义词：accustom, familiarize, adapt, adjust, align


反义词：disorient, confuse, mislead


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher orientates the new students to the school rules. = 老师让新生熟悉学校规章制度。
The map orientates travellers to the local landmarks. = 地图帮助旅行者了解当地的地标。


一般过去时:
The guide orientated us to the historical significance of the site. = 导游让我们了解了该遗址的历史意义。
She orientated herself to the new job quickly. = 她很快适应了新的工作。


现在完成时:
The company has orientated its marketing strategy towards a younger demographic. = 公司已将其营销策略转向更年轻的人群。
He has orientated himself to the new culture successfully. = 他成功地适应了新的文化。


过去完成时:
They had orientated themselves to the difficult terrain before starting the climb. = 他们在开始攀登之前已经适应了崎岖的地形。
She had orientated her research towards a specific area of study. = 她已将她的研究方向导向一个具体的学习领域。


将来时:
The program will orientate participants to the latest research findings. = 该项目将使参与者了解最新的研究成果。
We will orientate the new employees to company policies next week. = 我们下周将让新员工熟悉公司政策。


虚拟语气:
If the company had orientated its products towards a wider market, it would have been more successful. = 如果公司将产品面向更广阔的市场，它会更成功。
I wish I had orientated myself better before the meeting. = 我希望我在会议前能更好地做好准备。


被动语态:
The new recruits were orientated to the company's values. = 新员工们熟悉了公司的价值观。
The students were orientated to the use of the new software. = 学生们学习了如何使用新的软件。


疑问句:
How can we best orientate the audience to the main points of the presentation? = 我们如何才能最好地引导观众了解演示文稿的要点？
Is the map properly orientated? = 地图方向正确吗？


否定句:
The instructions didn't orientate me to the correct procedure. = 说明书并没有引导我进行正确的操作。
He failed to orientate himself in the unfamiliar city. = 他没能在这个陌生的城市中找到方向。
