pita:/ˈpiːtə/|/ˈpɪtɑː/| n. |皮塔饼|A pita is a type of flatbread, typically made from wheat flour, that is baked until puffy.  It's often used as a pocket for fillings in Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cuisine.|Example: I had a pita pocket filled with hummus and falafel for lunch.  我午餐吃了一个装有鹰嘴豆泥和鹰嘴豆饼的皮塔饼。|近义词：flatbread, pocket bread|反义词：无
