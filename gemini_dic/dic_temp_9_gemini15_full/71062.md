firstborn: /ˈfɜːrstˌbɔːrn/ | n. adj. | 长子；长女；头生的 |

英文解析:
The first child born to parents.  It can be used as a noun referring to the child itself, or as an adjective describing the child.

例句:
As the firstborn, he felt a strong sense of responsibility towards his younger siblings. = 作为长子，他对弟弟妹妹们有着强烈的责任感。

She is the firstborn daughter in a family of five children. = 她是一个五口之家里的长女。


近义词: eldest child, oldest child


反义词: youngest child, lastborn


词性: noun, adjective
