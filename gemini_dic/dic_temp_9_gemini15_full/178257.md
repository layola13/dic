recomplicates:/ˌriːˈkɒmplɪkeɪt/| vt.|使再次复杂化，使更复杂|To make something complicated again, often after it had been simplified or resolved.  The new regulations recomplicated the already complex tax system.|近义词：intricate, complicate further,  make more complex|反义词：simplify, clarify,  unravel
