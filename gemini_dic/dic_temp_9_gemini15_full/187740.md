rootin': /ˈruːtɪn/ | v. | 挖掘；搜索；翻找；寻根究底 | To search or dig around in something; to thoroughly investigate. |例句：He was rootin' around in the attic for his old photos. 他正在阁楼里翻找他的旧照片。 |近义词：dig, search, rummage, delve | 反义词：ignore, neglect |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He roots around in his pockets for his keys. = 他在口袋里翻找钥匙。
She roots through the drawers looking for her passport. = 她翻箱倒柜地找她的护照。


一般过去时:
They rooted through the trash looking for clues. = 他们翻找垃圾寻找线索。
He rooted around in the garden for his lost ring. = 他在花园里翻找他丢失的戒指。


现在完成时:
I've rooted around in my closet but still can't find my hat. = 我翻遍了我的衣橱，但还是找不到我的帽子。
She has rooted through all her files, but the report is nowhere to be found. = 她已经翻遍了她所有的文件，但是报告却找不到。


过去完成时:
He had rooted around in the shed before he found the tools he needed. = 他在找到他需要的工具之前已经在棚子里翻找过了。
They had rooted through the archives for hours before discovering the relevant document. = 他们在档案室里翻找了几个小时才找到相关的文件。


将来时:
I will root through the boxes tomorrow looking for my childhood toys. = 我明天会翻箱倒柜地找我的童年玩具。
We will root around in the old chest for family heirlooms. = 我们会在旧箱子里翻找传家宝。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would root through the library archives. = 如果我有更多时间，我会翻阅图书馆的档案。
I wish I could root around in that antique shop; it looks fascinating. = 我真希望我能去那家古董店翻翻看；它看起来很有趣。


被动语态:
The attic was rooted through by the investigators. = 阁楼被调查人员翻找过。
The old trunk has been rooted through many times over the years. = 这个旧箱子多年来被翻找过很多次。


疑问句:
Are you rooting around in the garden again? = 你又在花园里翻找东西了吗？
What are you rooting for in that old box? = 你在那只旧箱子里找什么？


否定句:
He didn't root through the drawers; he just glanced at them. = 他没有翻找抽屉；他只是看了一眼。
She didn't root around in the attic; she asked her brother for help. = 她没有在阁楼里翻找；她向她哥哥求助。


PHRASE->
root around: 翻找；搜索
root out: 根除；消灭
root for: 支持；为...加油
root up: 挖起；连根拔起
get to the root of: 找到...的根源
