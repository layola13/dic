peacekeepers:/ˈpiːsˌkiːpərz/ | n. | 维和人员，维持和平者 | People or groups who work to maintain peace, often in conflict zones.  They may be part of a military force, a civilian organization, or a combination of both, and their activities range from monitoring ceasefires to providing humanitarian aid. | Example: UN peacekeepers are deployed to many volatile regions around the world.联合国维和部队部署在世界许多动荡地区。 | 近义词: peacemakers, mediators, arbitrators | 反义词: warmongers, aggressors | 名词
