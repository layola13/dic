rogues:/roʊɡz/| n.|流氓，无赖，恶棍|A rogue is a dishonest or unprincipled person; a scoundrel.  The term often implies a degree of cunning or mischievousness.  It can also refer to an individual who behaves in a disobedient or rebellious manner.|例句：The rogue stole the jewels and escaped unnoticed. 这个无赖偷走了珠宝，然后悄无声息地逃跑了。|例句：That rogue dog keeps digging holes in my garden. 那条淘气的狗老在我的花园里挖洞。|近义词：villain, scoundrel, rascal, miscreant, outlaw|反义词：saint, angel, paragon
