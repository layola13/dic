posthysterical: /ˌpɒst hɪˈstɛrɪkəl/ | adj. | 后歇斯底里的；歇斯底里之后的；冷静的；超然的 |

英文解析:  Describing a state or behavior that comes after a period of hysteria or intense emotional outburst. It often implies a sense of calm, detachment, or even ironic detachment following a period of extreme emotion.  The term can be used to describe a person's emotional state, a reaction, or even a cultural or artistic movement.


例句:

After the initial panic, a posthysterical calm settled over the crowd. = 最初的恐慌过后，一种后歇斯底里的平静笼罩着人群。

Her posthysterical laughter was both unsettling and strangely comforting. = 她后歇斯底里的笑声既令人不安又奇怪地令人安慰。


近义词:  calm, composed, detached, unemotional, apathetic (depending on context)


反义词: hysterical, frantic, agitated, emotional


词性: adjective
