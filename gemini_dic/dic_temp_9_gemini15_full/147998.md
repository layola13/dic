overrighteous:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪtʃəs/| adj. |过于正直的；自以为是的；道貌岸然的 | Excessively or self-righteously virtuous; sanctimonious.  It suggests a pretense of righteousness, often accompanied by judgmentalism and a lack of empathy. |例句：His overrighteous pronouncements irritated everyone.  他过于正直的断言激怒了所有人。

例句：She was so overrighteous that she couldn't see her own flaws. 她过于自以为是，看不到自己的缺点。


近义词：sanctimonious, self-righteous, hypocritical, pious, holier-than-thou


反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, tolerant


词性：形容词
