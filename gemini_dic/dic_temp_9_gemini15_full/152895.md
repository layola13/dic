peddled:/ˈpedld/ | vt. |兜售，贩卖；宣传，鼓吹 | To try to sell something by going from place to place or by repeatedly contacting people.  |例句：He peddled his wares in the market. 他在市场上兜售他的货物。

例句：They peddled a false narrative about the election. 他们散布了关于选举的虚假说法。|近义词：sell, hawk, promote, push |反义词：buy, purchase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peddles illegal drugs. = 他贩卖非法毒品。
She peddles her ideas to anyone who will listen. = 她向任何愿意倾听的人兜售她的想法。

一般过去时:
They peddled their goods door-to-door. = 他们挨家挨户地兜售商品。
He peddled a false story to the media. = 他向媒体兜售了一个虚假的故事。

现在完成时:
He has peddled his wares all over the country. = 他在全国各地兜售他的商品。
She has been peddling that conspiracy theory for years. = 她多年来一直在散布那个阴谋论。

过去完成时:
Before the scandal broke, he had peddled his influence for years. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他多年来一直在利用他的影响力谋利。
They had peddled their story to several newspapers. = 他们已经向几家报纸兜售了他们的故事。

将来时:
They will peddle their new product at the trade show. = 他们将在贸易展上兜售他们的新产品。
He will continue to peddle his lies until he is caught. = 他将继续散布谎言，直到被抓住。

虚拟语气:
If he peddled his wares more effectively, he would make more money. = 如果他更有效地兜售他的商品，他就能赚更多的钱。
I wish he hadn't peddled that false information. = 我真希望他没有散布那些虚假信息。

被动语态:
His ideas were peddled by a group of influential people. = 他的想法被一群有影响力的人兜售。
The rumors were peddled throughout the town. = 谣言在全镇传播。

疑问句:
What are they peddling this time? = 他们这次在兜售什么？
Where did he peddle his paintings? = 他在哪里兜售他的画？

否定句:
He doesn't peddle illegal substances. = 他不贩卖非法物质。
They didn't peddle their story to the press. = 他们没有向媒体兜售他们的故事。


PHRASE->
peddle influence:  运用影响力
peddle rumors: 散布谣言
peddle drugs: 贩卖毒品
peddle lies: 散布谎言
peddle misinformation: 散布错误信息

词源:
The word "peddle" ultimately derives from the Old English word *pettan, meaning "to go."  Over time, the meaning evolved to encompass the act of traveling from place to place selling goods.  The connection to movement is still present in the sense of actively promoting or spreading something.
