/ˈsiːsiːˈtiːviː/| n.|闭路电视；监控录像|Closed-circuit television; a television system in which signals are not transmitted publicly but are received only by specific receivers.  It is often used for security purposes.|例句：The CCTV footage showed the thief breaking into the store. 闭路电视录像显示小偷闯入了商店。|例句：Many businesses use CCTV to deter crime.许多企业使用闭路电视来阻止犯罪。|近义词：surveillance, monitoring, security cameras|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be open broadcasting or lack of surveillance. |名词
