dispatcher:/dɪˈspætʃər/ | n. |调度员，发派员 |A dispatcher is a person who sends out or directs the movement of vehicles, aircraft, or other things.  They coordinate and manage the flow of resources. |例句：The emergency dispatcher sent ambulances to the scene of the accident.  紧急调度员将救护车派往事故现场。

The flight dispatcher checked the weather conditions before authorizing takeoff.  航班调度员在批准起飞前检查了天气状况。

近义词：controller, coordinator, operator |反义词：receiver (in some contexts) |词性:名词
