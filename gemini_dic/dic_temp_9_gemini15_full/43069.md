cowpea:/ˈkaʊpiː/| n. |豇豆，饭豆 |A cowpea is a vine-like plant grown for its edible seeds and pods.  Cowpeas are a type of legume, related to beans and peas, and are a significant food crop in many parts of the world, particularly in Africa and Asia. They are a good source of protein and other nutrients.  | Example:  We planted cowpeas in the garden this year. 今年我们在花园里种了豇豆。 |近义词：black-eyed pea (某些品种), southern pea |反义词：无
