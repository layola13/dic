counsel:/ˈkaʊnsəl/ | n. v. | 劝告；建议；律师；法律顾问 |  To give advice or guidance; a lawyer; legal advice. |例句：He offered some sound counsel on the matter. 他就此事提供了一些合理的建议。|近义词：advice, guidance, recommendation, attorney |反义词：misguidance, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He counsels his clients wisely. = 他明智地劝告他的客户。
She counsels students on career choices. = 她就职业选择向学生提供建议。


一般过去时:
The lawyer counseled the defendant before the trial. = 律师在审判前为被告提供法律咨询。
My friend counseled me through a difficult time. = 我的朋友在我度过难关时给了我劝告。


现在完成时:
She has counseled hundreds of people throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经为数百人提供过咨询。
He has counseled me on many occasions. = 他多次给我提供过建议。


过去完成时:
He had counseled her many times before she finally decided. = 在她最终决定之前，他已经多次劝告过她。
They had counseled with the experts before making the decision. = 他们曾在做决定之前与专家们进行过咨询。


将来时:
She will counsel the grieving family. = 她将为悲伤的家庭提供咨询。
He will counsel the new employees on company policy. = 他将就公司政策为新员工提供咨询。


虚拟语气:
If I had counseled him earlier, he might have made a different decision. = 如果我早点劝告他，他可能会做出不同的决定。
I wish I had counseled her more effectively. = 我希望我当时能更有效地劝告她。


被动语态:
The defendant was counseled by a prominent lawyer. = 被告由一位著名的律师提供法律咨询。
The students were counseled on the importance of education. = 学生们接受了关于教育重要性的指导。


疑问句:
Will you counsel me on this problem? = 你会就这个问题给我建议吗？
Did the therapist counsel the patient effectively? = 治疗师是否有效地为病人进行了咨询？


否定句:
He did not counsel his son against taking the risk. = 他没有劝告他的儿子不要冒险。
They did not counsel the couple before their marriage. = 他们在结婚前没有对这对夫妇进行咨询。


PHRASE->
counsel for the defense =  辩护律师
counsel against something =  劝阻某事
seek counsel =  寻求建议
take counsel with someone =  与某人商量
follow counsel =  听从劝告
