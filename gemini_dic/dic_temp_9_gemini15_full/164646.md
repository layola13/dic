/ˌpriːɪkˈspɛnd/ | v. | 预先支出，预付 | To spend money in advance, before a need arises.  |  The company preexpended funds for the upcoming project. 公司为即将到来的项目预支了资金。  They preexpended on materials to ensure timely completion. 他们预先支付材料费用以确保按时完成。 |  prepay, advance, pay in advance |  postpone, delay  | 动词

They preexpend funds for research annually. = 他们每年预先拨款用于研究。
The company preexpends on marketing campaigns. = 公司预先支付市场营销活动费用。
