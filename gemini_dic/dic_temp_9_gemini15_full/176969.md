really:/ˈriːəli/ | adv. | 真正地；确实；非常 | Truly, genuinely; to a great extent.  |例句：He really loves his job. 他真的很爱他的工作。
例句：It's really cold today. 今天真的很冷。|近义词：truly, genuinely, very, extremely |反义词：falsely, unrealistically, slightly
