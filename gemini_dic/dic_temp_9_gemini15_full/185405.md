retrospectively:/ˌretrəˈspektɪvli/ | adv. |追溯地；回顾地；回想起来|  From a past perspective; in retrospect.  It considers past events or situations. |例句：The decision was retrospectively judged to be a mistake.  这个决定后来被认为是错误的。
例句：The study retrospectively analyzed data from previous years. 这项研究回顾性地分析了往年数据。|近义词：in retrospect, in hindsight, looking back|反义词：prospectively, beforehand
