pamphletary:/ˈpæmf(ə)lətɛri/| adj.|小册子似的；似小册子的；|relating to or characteristic of a pamphlet; resembling a pamphlet in style or content.  It often implies a brevity, simplicity, and sometimes a propagandistic or persuasive tone.|The pamphletary style of the political flyer was easily identifiable.|近义词：leaflety, tract-like|反义词：extensive, comprehensive
