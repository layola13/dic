It aims to reconstruct the original data as accurately as possible, though perfect reconstruction is not always achievable.  | Example: The image underwent postreduction to recover some detail lost during compression.  后期图像处理以恢复压缩过程中丢失的一些细节。|近义词: restoration, reconstruction, recovery | 反义词: reduction, compression, simplification | 名词
