nondenunciation: /ˌnɒnˌdɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 不谴责；未予谴责 | The act or instance of not denouncing or condemning someone or something.  It implies a passive acceptance or tolerance, rather than active support. | The nondenunciation of the regime's human rights abuses was seen by many as tacit approval.  不谴责该政权侵犯人权的行为被许多人视为默许。 |  acceptance, tolerance, acquiescence | condemnation, denunciation, censure | 名词
