recriminalization:/ˌriːkrɪmɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |重新定为犯罪；再次刑事化 |The process of making something that was previously legal or decriminalized illegal again.  This often involves enacting new laws or reinterpreting existing ones to re-establish criminal penalties for a specific act or behavior. |例句：The recriminalization of drug use led to a surge in arrests. 毒品使用的重新刑事化导致逮捕人数激增。|例句：There are concerns that the recriminalization of abortion will disproportionately affect marginalized communities. 人们担心堕胎的重新刑事化将不成比例地影响边缘化社区。|近义词：re-criminalization, re-enactment of criminal penalties |反义词：decriminalization, legalization
