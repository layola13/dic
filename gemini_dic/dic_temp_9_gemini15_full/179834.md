reexpel:/ˌriːɪkˈspɛl/| vt.|重新驱逐，再次开除|To expel again; to drive out a second time.  The word combines the prefix "re-" (again) with the verb "expel".|例句：The student was reexpelled after violating school rules again.  学生再次违反校规，被重新开除。

例句：The country reexpelled the diplomat for espionage.  该国因间谍活动再次驱逐了这位外交官。

近义词：expel, banish, oust, eject

反义词：admit, welcome, accept

词性:动词
