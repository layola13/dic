crocuses: /ˈkroʊkəsəz/ | n. |番红花（复数）|Small, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, often in vibrant colors like purple, yellow, or white.  They are known for their ability to push through snow and cold ground. |The crocuses poked their heads through the melting snow.|近义词：spring flowers, bulb flowers |反义词：无
