half-training: /ˈhæfˌtreɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 半训练；半程训练 | Partial training; training that is incomplete or not fully developed.  This term often refers to a situation where an individual or system is undergoing training but has not yet reached full competency or completion. It can also describe a training program that is shorter or less comprehensive than a full training program. |  例句：The new employees are currently undergoing half-training before being fully integrated into the team. 新员工目前正在接受半训练，然后再完全融入团队。

The athlete completed only a half-training cycle before the competition. 运动员在比赛前只完成了半程训练。| 近义词：partial training, incomplete training, introductory training | 反义词：full training, complete training
