frankincense: /ˈfræŋkɪnsɛns/ | n. | 乳香 | A fragrant resin obtained from certain trees of the genus Boswellia, used as incense and in perfumes.  It has a warm, spicy, and slightly sweet aroma. | Example: The church was filled with the sweet scent of frankincense and myrrh. 教堂里弥漫着乳香和没药的甜香。 |近义词：incense, resin |反义词：N/A
