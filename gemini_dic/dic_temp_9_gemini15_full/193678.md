self-combustion:/ˌsɛlf kəmˈbʌʃən/ | n. |自燃；自行燃烧 |The spontaneous ignition and burning of a substance without the application of external heat.  This process is typically caused by an internal chemical reaction that generates sufficient heat to reach the ignition temperature of the material. |例句：The haystack spontaneously combusted due to the heat generated by the fermenting hay.  干草堆由于发酵的干草产生的热量而自燃。

The pile of rags underwent self-combustion after being soaked in oil.  浸油后的破布堆发生了自燃。|近义词：spontaneous combustion, spontaneous ignition |反义词：extinction, quenching
