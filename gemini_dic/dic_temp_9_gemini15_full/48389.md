dedecoration:/ˌdiːˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən/| n. |去装饰；去除装饰 |The removal of decoration; the act of undecorating.  It often implies a simplification or stripping away of ornamentation.  |Example: The dedecoration of the building revealed its original, austere structure.  建筑物的去装饰揭示了其原始的、朴素的结构。|近义词：unadornment, simplification, stripping|反义词：decoration, ornamentation, embellishment|名词
