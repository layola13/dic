laugh-out-loud: /ˌlæfˈaʊtˈlaʊd/ | interjection | 捧腹大笑；笑得很大声 |  An interjection expressing unrestrained laughter. It's often used in online communication to indicate something is very funny. | 例句：That joke was laugh-out-loud funny! = 那个笑话笑死我了！

The movie was so hilarious, it was a laugh-out-loud experience. = 这部电影太搞笑了，让人忍不住哈哈大笑。


近义词：LOL, ROFL, hysterical, hilarious | 反义词：sad, depressing
