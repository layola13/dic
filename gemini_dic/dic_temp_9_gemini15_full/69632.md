feminization:/ˌfemɪnɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 女性化；使女性化 | The process of becoming or making something feminine.  This can refer to the adoption of feminine characteristics, roles, or behaviors by individuals or groups, or to the societal changes that lead to a greater emphasis on traditionally feminine traits. | Example: The feminization of the workforce is a significant social trend.  工作场所女性化的趋势是一个重要的社会现象。|近义词：effeminization,  womanization (in specific contexts) | 反义词：masculinization
