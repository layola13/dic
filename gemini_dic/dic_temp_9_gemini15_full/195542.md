semipermanent:/ˌsɛmiˈpɜːrmənənt/| adj.| 半永久性的；暂时的 |  lasting for a fairly long time but not permanently;  existing for a period of time that is more than temporary but less than permanent. | 例句：The dye provides semi-permanent color to the hair.  这种染发剂能使头发呈现半永久性的颜色。

The tattoo is semi-permanent, lasting for several years before fading. 这个纹身是半永久性的，能持续几年才会褪色。

| 近义词：temporary, lasting, provisional | 反义词：permanent, everlasting, perpetual | 词性：adj.
