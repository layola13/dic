repairs:/rɪˈpɛərz/| n.|修理，修补|The process of restoring something damaged to working order.  It can refer to the act of repairing, the things that need repairing, or the cost of repairing.|例句：The repairs to the car cost a fortune. 汽车修理费了一大笔钱。

例句：The house is in need of extensive repairs. 这房子需要大修。|近义词：mend, fix, restoration|反义词：destruction, damage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mechanic repairs cars every day. = 机械师每天修理汽车。
He regularly repairs his bicycle. = 他定期修理他的自行车。


一般过去时:
She repaired the broken vase. = 她修好了破花瓶。
They repaired the roof last week. = 他们上周修好了屋顶。


现在完成时:
He has repaired the fence already. = 他已经修好了栅栏。
The plumber has repaired the leaking pipe. = 水管工已经修好了漏水的管道。


过去完成时:
They had repaired the car before the race. = 他们在比赛前已经修好了车。
She had repaired the dress before the party. = 她在派对前已经修好了裙子。


将来时:
I will repair the computer tomorrow. = 我明天会修理电脑。
They will repair the bridge next month. = 他们下个月会修理桥梁。


虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would repair the bike myself. = 如果我有工具，我会自己修理自行车。
I wish I had repaired the roof sooner. = 我希望我早点修好屋顶。


被动语态:
The car is being repaired at the moment. = 汽车正在修理中。
The road was repaired last year. = 这条路去年修好了。


疑问句:
Will the repairs be finished soon? = 修理工作很快就能完成吗？
Are the repairs very expensive? = 修理费用很高吗？


否定句:
