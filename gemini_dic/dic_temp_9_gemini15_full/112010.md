long-distance:/ˌlɒŋˈdɪstəns/ /ˌlɔːŋˈdɪstəns/| adj.|长途的，远距离的|covering or involving a great distance; relating to telephone calls, etc., made over a long distance.|例句：We took a long-distance bus to the city. 我们乘坐长途汽车去了城市。

例句：She made a long-distance call to her parents. 她给父母打了个长途电话。|近义词：far-reaching, extensive, remote|反义词：short, nearby, local|词性：形容词
