gouges:/ɡaʊdʒ/| n. vt.|凿子；挖出；敲诈；欺骗；|A gouge is a tool with a deep, narrow, U-shaped or V-shaped blade used for cutting grooves or removing material.  As a verb, it means to make a groove or hole with a gouge; to force someone to pay too much money; or to cheat or swindle someone. |例句：He used a gouge to carve the wood. 他用凿子雕刻木头。 The company was accused of gouging customers with high prices. 这家公司被指控以高价敲诈顾客。|近义词：chisel, scoop, extort, swindle|反义词：give, help
