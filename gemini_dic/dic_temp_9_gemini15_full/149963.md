palma:/ˈpɑːlmə/ | n. |棕榈树；手掌；棕榈叶；胜利的象征 | The word "palma" refers to a palm tree, its leaves, or its fruit. It can also be used metaphorically to represent victory or triumph, especially in a competition or contest.  | Example: The ancient Romans used palm branches as symbols of victory. 古罗马人用棕榈枝作为胜利的象征。The beach was lined with majestic palm trees. 海滩上排列着雄伟的棕榈树。|近义词：palm tree, date palm, victory|反义词：defeat, failure
