principle:/ˈprɪnsəpəl/ | n. | 原则；原理；准则 | a fundamental truth or proposition that serves as the foundation for a system of belief or behavior, or for a chain of reasoning.  |  The principle of democracy is that everyone has a voice.  民主的原则是一人一票。| guideline, rule, tenet, precept | chaos, disorder | 名词
