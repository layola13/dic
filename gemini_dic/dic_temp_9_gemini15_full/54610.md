dismayful: /dɪsˈmeɪfəl/ | adj. |令人沮丧的；令人失望的；令人灰心的| Feeling or causing dismay; disheartening.  It describes a feeling of discouragement or disappointment, often caused by something unexpected or unpleasant.|

例句：

The news of the accident was dismayful.  = 事故的消息令人沮丧。

His dismayful expression showed his disappointment. = 他沮丧的表情显示了他的失望。


近义词：disheartening, discouraging, depressing, disheartening, dispiriting

反义词：encouraging, uplifting, heartening, inspiring

词性：形容词
