non-recoverable: /ˌnɒn rɪˈkʌvərəbl/ | adj. | 不可恢复的，无法挽回的 |  Describes something that cannot be retrieved, repaired, or restored to its previous state.  It implies a permanent loss or damage. |


例句：

The data loss was non-recoverable due to the hard drive failure. = 由于硬盘故障，数据丢失是不可恢复的。

The damage to the antique vase was deemed non-recoverable. = 古董花瓶的损坏被认为是无法挽回的。


近义词：irreparable, irretrievable, unrecoverable, irreversible

反义词：recoverable, retrievable, repairable


词性：形容词
