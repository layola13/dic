conceptualizing: /kənˈsɛptʃuəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 概念化；构想；设想 | The act of forming a concept or idea.  It involves understanding and creating a mental representation of something. | Example:  She is conceptualizing a new marketing campaign. 她正在构思一个新的营销活动。  The architect is conceptualizing a sustainable building design. 建筑师正在构想一种可持续的建筑设计。| 近义词：envisioning, imagining, formulating, conceiving | 反义词：materializing (in the sense of making concrete), ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conceptualizes new ideas every day. = 他每天都在构思新的想法。
They conceptualize the project before starting. = 他们在开始之前构思这个项目。

一般过去时:
She conceptualized a groundbreaking theory. = 她构思了一个具有开创性的理论。
We conceptualized the design during the meeting. = 我们在会议期间构思了这个设计。

现在完成时:
He has conceptualized several successful products. = 他已经构思了几种成功的产品。
They have conceptualized a new approach to the problem. = 他们已经构思了一种解决这个问题的新方法。

过去完成时:
She had conceptualized the entire project before presenting it. = 在演示之前，她已经构思了整个项目。
We had conceptualized a different strategy, but it failed. = 我们之前构思了一个不同的策略，但是它失败了。

将来时:
They will conceptualize a new marketing plan next month. = 他们下个月将要构思一个新的营销计划。
He will conceptualize the sculpture before beginning to work on it. = 他将在开始制作雕塑之前构思它。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would conceptualize a better solution. = 如果我有时间，我会构思一个更好的解决方案。
I wish I could conceptualize a way to solve this problem. = 我希望我能构思出一个解决这个问题的方法。

被动语态:
The project was conceptualized by a team of experts. = 这个项目是由一个专家团队构思的。
The new design is being conceptualized as we speak. = 新的设计正在构思中。

疑问句:
How did he conceptualize such a complex idea? = 他是如何构思这样一个复杂的想法的？
What factors were considered when conceptualizing the product? = 在构思产品时考虑了哪些因素？

否定句:
They did not conceptualize the implications of their actions. = 他们没有设想他们行为的含义。
He couldn't conceptualize a solution to the problem. = 他无法构思出解决这个问题的方法。
