cannonaded:/ˌkænəˈneɪdɪd/| v.|炮击；连续炮轰|To bombard with cannon fire; to attack with a continuous barrage of cannon fire.|例句：The enemy cannonaded the city for hours. 敌人炮击了该城市数小时。

例句：The fortress was cannonaded relentlessly.  堡垒遭到无情的炮击。


近义词：bombarded, shelled, bombarded, attacked

反义词：defended, protected
