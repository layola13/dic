discodactylous:/ˌdɪskoʊˈdæktɪləs/| adj. | 分趾的，趾分开的 | Having toes or fingers separated to a considerable extent; not webbed.  The term is primarily used to describe the feet of birds and other animals.  | Example:  The discodactylous feet of the bird allowed it to grip the branch securely.  这只鸟的分趾足使它能够牢牢抓住树枝。 |近义词:  web-footed (antonym in a sense)|反义词: syndactylous (趾连生的)
