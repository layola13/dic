desynchronizing: /ˌdiːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使不同步；使失去同步 |  The act of causing something to lose synchronization or to become out of sync with something else. It implies a disruption or a loss of coordinated timing or function between previously synchronized elements. | 例句：The power outage desynchronized the entire network. = 停电使整个网络失去了同步。

The faulty software is desynchronizing the data streams. = 故障的软件正在使数据流不同步。


近义词：misaligning, disrupting, deranging, uncoupling

反义词：synchronizing, coordinating, harmonizing

词性：动词
