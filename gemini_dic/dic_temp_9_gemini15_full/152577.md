peace-loving: /ˈpiːs lʌvɪŋ/ | adj. |爱好和平的，和平主义的 |  Describing someone or something that desires and promotes peace and avoids violence.  |例句：Many peace-loving nations signed the treaty. 许多爱好和平的国家签署了条约。The peace-loving people of the region yearn for an end to the conflict.  该地区爱好和平的人民渴望冲突结束。|近义词：pacifistic, non-violent, amicable |反义词：warlike, aggressive, bellicose
