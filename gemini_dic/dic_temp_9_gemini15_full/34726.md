clicking:/ˈklɪkɪŋ/| n. v.|咔哒声；点击|The act of making a short, sharp sound, or the sound itself; the action of pressing a button or icon on a computer screen.|例句：I heard a clicking sound coming from the engine. 我听到发动机传来咔哒声。|例句：She was clicking through the photos on her phone. 她正在用手机翻看照片。|近义词：snapping, tapping, clicking sound|反义词：thudding, booming


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The mouse clicks smoothly. = 鼠标点击顺畅。
He clicks the link to open the webpage. = 他点击链接打开网页。


一般过去时:
The door clicked shut. = 门咔哒一声关上了。
She clicked the "submit" button. = 她点击了“提交”按钮。


现在完成时:
I have clicked the button multiple times, but nothing happened. = 我已经点击了多次按钮，但没有任何反应。
He has clicked on every ad on the page. = 他点击了页面上的每一个广告。


过去完成时:
After I had clicked “send”, I realized I’d made a mistake. = 我点击“发送”后，才意识到自己犯了个错误。
She had clicked the wrong link before she realized it. = 她意识到之前点击了错误的链接。


将来时:
I will click the button when I'm ready. = 我准备好后会点击按钮。
He will click the link to access the file. = 他将点击链接来访问文件。


虚拟语气:
If I clicked this button, what would happen? = 如果我点击这个按钮，会发生什么？
I wish I hadn't clicked that link; it led to a virus. = 我真希望我没有点击那个链接；它导致了病毒感染。


被动语态:
The link was clicked thousands of times. = 该链接被点击了数千次。
The button should be clicked only once. = 按钮应该只点击一次。


疑问句:
Did you click the link? = 你点击了链接吗？
What did you click on? = 你点击了什么？


否定句:
The button didn’t click properly. = 按钮没有正常点击。
I didn’t click anything. = 我什么都没点击。
