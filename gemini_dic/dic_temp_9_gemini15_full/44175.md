critique:/krɪˈtiːk/| n., v.| 批评；评论；评论文章 |A critique is a detailed analysis and assessment of something, especially a literary, artistic, or musical work.  It can be a written or spoken evaluation, often offering both positive and negative points.  |例句：The professor gave a thorough critique of my essay, highlighting both its strengths and weaknesses. 教授对我的论文进行了彻底的评论，指出了它的优点和缺点。


例句：Her critique of the film was insightful and well-reasoned. 她对这部电影的评论深刻而有理有据。


近义词：review, analysis, evaluation, assessment, judgment


反义词：praise, commendation, endorsement


词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He critiques the latest trends in fashion. = 他评论最新的时尚潮流。
She critiques her students' work regularly. = 她定期评论学生的作业。


一般过去时:
The art critic critiqued the painting harshly. = 艺术评论家严厉地批评了这幅画。
He critiqued the government's policies in his speech. = 他在演讲中批评了政府的政策。


现在完成时:
She has critiqued many films for various publications. = 她已经为各种出版物评论了许多电影。
He has critiqued the proposal and found several flaws. = 他已经评论了该提案并发现了几个缺陷。


过去完成时:
They had critiqued the design before presenting it to the client. = 他们在向客户展示设计之前就已经对其进行了评论。
She had critiqued the manuscript several times before submitting it. = 在提交手稿之前，她已经多次对其进行了评论。


将来时:
He will critique the performance after the play. = 他将在演出结束后评论这场演出。
They will critique the new software before its release. = 他们将在新软件发布之前对其进行评论。


虚拟语气:
If he critiqued the work more thoroughly, he might find more errors. = 如果他更彻底地评论这项工作，他可能会发现更多错误。
I wish she had critiqued my proposal before I submitted it. = 我希望她在提交之前能评论我的提案。


被动语态:
The artwork was critiqued by several experts. = 这件艺术品受到了几位专家的评论。
The report will be critiqued by the committee. = 该报告将由委员会进行评论。


疑问句:
Will you critique my presentation? = 你会评论我的演讲吗？
Has the book been critiqued by any major publications? = 这本书是否受到了任何主要出版物的评论？


否定句:
He did not critique the movie at all. = 他根本没有评论这部电影。
She will not critique the design without proper preparation. = 没有充分的准备，她不会评论这个设计。



PHRASE->
literary critique = 文学评论
film critique = 电影评论
art critique = 艺术评论
constructive critique = 建设性批评
a harsh critique = 严厉的批评


词源:
源自法语“critique”, 最初源于希腊语“kritikos”（批判的），最终源于“krinein”（判断）。  The word evolved through French and ultimately reflects a process of judgment and analysis.
