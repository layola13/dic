piddler:/ˈpɪdlər/| n. |笨手笨脚的人；做事磨蹭的人；小偷小摸的人|A piddler is someone who works slowly and inefficiently, often making a mess or being clumsy.  They might also be someone who engages in petty theft. |例句：He's such a piddler; it took him all day to paint one room. 他真是个笨手笨脚的人，刷一间房就花了他一整天。|近义词：dawdler, trifler, fusser, petty thief |反义词：expert, efficient, professional
