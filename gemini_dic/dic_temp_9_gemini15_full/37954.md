complexify:/kəmˈplɛksɪfaɪ/| vt.|使复杂化；使变得更复杂|To make something more complex or complicated.|The addition of new regulations complexified the process significantly.  增加新的规章制度使这个过程变得复杂得多。|The new software complexified the workflow. 新软件使工作流程复杂化。|simplify, complicate|simplify, clarify|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They complexify the system with unnecessary features. = 他们用不必要的特性使系统复杂化。
The new rules complexify the tax code. = 新规定使税法更加复杂。

一般过去时:
The project complexified unexpectedly. = 项目出乎意料地变得复杂了。
The merger complexified the company structure. = 并购使公司结构复杂化。

现在完成时:
Years of mismanagement have complexified the company's finances. = 多年的管理不善使公司的财务状况更加复杂。
Recent changes have complexified the situation considerably. = 最近的变化使情况变得相当复杂。

过去完成时:
They had complexified the problem by adding unnecessary steps. = 他们通过添加不必要的步骤使问题变得更加复杂。
The delays had complexified the logistics of the project. = 延误使项目的物流变得更加复杂。

将来时:
The new regulations will complexify the application process. = 新规将使申请流程更加复杂。
Further development will complexify the software's architecture. = 进一步的开发将使软件的架构更加复杂。

虚拟语气:
If they added more features, it would complexify the system. = 如果他们添加更多功能，它将使系统复杂化。
I wish they hadn't complexified the design so much. = 我希望他们不要把设计弄得这么复杂。

被动语态:

否定句:
