ideating:/aɪˈdiːeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 构思；创意；进行创意活动 | The act of generating, developing, and exploring ideas. It often involves brainstorming, mind mapping, and other creative thinking techniques.  |  The team spent the morning ideating new marketing strategies.  团队上午花时间构思新的营销策略。| brainstorming, conceptualizing, envisioning |  criticizing, judging, implementing (in the context of the ideation process itself) | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are ideating solutions to the problem. = 我们正在构思解决这个问题的方法。
They ideate new product features regularly. = 他们定期构思新的产品功能。

一般过去时:
The designers ideated several different logo concepts. = 设计师构思了几个不同的标识概念。
We ideated a marketing campaign last week. = 我们上周构思了一个营销活动。

现在完成时:
We have ideated a comprehensive plan for the project. = 我们已经为这个项目构思了一个全面的计划。
They have ideated numerous solutions to the client's needs. = 他们已经为客户的需求构思了许多解决方案。

过去完成时:
They had ideated a plan before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经构思了一个计划。
We had ideated several different approaches before choosing one. = 我们在选择一种方法之前已经构思了几种不同的方法。

将来时:
We will be ideating new product lines next month. = 我们下个月将构思新的产品线。
They will ideate a response to the customer feedback. = 他们将构思对客户反馈的回应。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would ideate more innovative solutions. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会构思更多创新的解决方案。
I wish I had ideated a better solution. = 我希望我曾构思出一个更好的解决方案。

被动语态:
New marketing strategies were ideated by the team. = 新的营销策略是由团队构思的。
A comprehensive plan was ideated for the project launch. = 为项目启动构思了一个全面的计划。

疑问句:
Are they ideating new ways to improve the product? = 他们正在构思改进产品的新方法吗？
Have you ideated a solution to this problem yet? = 你已经构思出解决这个问题的方法了吗？

否定句:
We did not ideate any new solutions today. = 我们今天没有构思任何新的解决方案。
They were not ideating during the meeting. = 他们开会时没有进行构思。
