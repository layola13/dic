imparters:/ɪmˈpɑːrtərz/| n.| 传递者；给予者 |Those who impart something, such as knowledge, information, or skills.  People or things that convey or bestow something.| The teachers were excellent imparters of knowledge.  老师们是知识的优秀传播者。 The book is an imparters of wisdom. 这本书是智慧的传播者。|近义词：conveyers, communicators, dispensers |反义词：receivers, recipients


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teachers impart knowledge to their students. = 老师们把知识传授给学生。
The machine imparts a special coating to the metal. = 机器给金属涂上特殊涂层。

一般过去时:
He imparted his wisdom to his son. = 他把他的智慧传给了他的儿子。
She imparted the secret to her best friend. = 她把秘密告诉了她最好的朋友。

现在完成时:
They have imparted a lot of information to the public. = 他们已经向公众传达了很多信息。
The organization has imparted valuable skills to many people. = 该组织已经向许多人传授了宝贵的技能。

过去完成时:
He had imparted the news to his family before it was made public. = 在消息公开之前，他已经把这个消息告诉了他的家人。
She had imparted her knowledge to her students before retiring. = 在退休之前，她已经把她的知识传授给了她的学生。

将来时:
They will impart their experience to the next generation. = 他们将把他们的经验传授给下一代。
The company will impart the new strategy to its employees. = 公司将把新的战略传达给它的员工。

虚拟语气:
If I were a better teacher, I would impart more knowledge effectively. = 如果我是一个更好的老师，我会更有效地传授知识。
I wish I could impart my passion for art to my students. = 我希望我能把我对艺术的热情传授给我的学生。

被动语态:
Knowledge was imparted to the students through lectures and discussions. = 知识通过讲座和讨论传授给学生。
The new policy will be imparted to all staff members. = 新政策将传达给所有员工。

疑问句:
How will you impart this critical information? = 你将如何传达这个重要的信息？
What methods do you use to impart your knowledge? = 你用什么方法传授你的知识？

否定句:
He did not impart the full story. = 他没有讲述完整的故事。
The manager failed to impart the necessary instructions. = 经理未能传达必要的指示。
