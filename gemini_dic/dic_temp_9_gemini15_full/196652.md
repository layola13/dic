setwall: /ˈsɛtwɔːl/ | n. |隔墙，隔板 | A setwall is a partition, often temporary or movable, used to divide a space.  It's typically used in offices or event spaces to create smaller, more private areas.  | Example: The conference room used setwalls to create smaller breakout spaces for group discussions.  隔间用于会议室，以便为小组讨论创造更小的分组空间。 | 近义词：partition, divider, screen | 反义词：open space | 名词
