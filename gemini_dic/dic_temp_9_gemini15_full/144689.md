ouzo:/ˈuːzoʊ/| n. |乌佐酒 |A strong, anise-flavored aperitif, a type of alcoholic beverage popular in Greece.  It is typically clear but may turn cloudy when diluted with water. |This ouzo is really smooth.  这杯乌佐酒口感很顺滑。|aperitif, anise liqueur|  |名词
