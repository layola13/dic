jostling:/ˈdʒɒslɪŋ/ |/ˈdʒɑːslɪŋ/| v. n.|推挤；争抢；竞争|The act of pushing or shoving roughly against someone or something.  It can also refer to a competitive struggle or rivalry.|例句：The shoppers were jostling for position near the sale rack.  购物者们为了靠近特卖货架而互相推挤。

The crowds jostled their way through the narrow streets. 人群在狭窄的街道上互相推搡着前进。


近义词：shoving, pushing, crowding, struggling, competing|反义词：yielding, giving way, cooperating|动词
