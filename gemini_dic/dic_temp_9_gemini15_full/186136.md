rhapsodist:/ˈræpsədɪst/| n. |吟诵诗人；狂想家 | A rhapsodist is a person who recites or performs rhapsodies, which are typically passionate and emotional poems or speeches.  They often improvise and embellish their performance. | Example: The rhapsodist captivated the audience with his vibrant delivery of the epic poem.  吟诵诗人以其充满活力的史诗诗歌朗诵征服了观众。 |近义词：poet, performer, orator |反义词：listener, critic
