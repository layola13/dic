semi-aquatic:/ˌsemiˈækwætɪk/| adj.|半水生的；两栖的|Partially aquatic; living both on land and in water.  This term describes organisms that spend part of their life cycle in water and part on land.|例句：Otters are semi-aquatic mammals. 水獭是半水生的哺乳动物。

例句：Many amphibians, like frogs, have a semi-aquatic lifestyle. 许多两栖动物，比如青蛙，都过着半水生的生活。


近义词：amphibious, partly aquatic|反义词：terrestrial, fully aquatic|词性：形容词
