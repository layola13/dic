hesitative: /ˈhezɪteɪtɪv/ | adj. | 犹豫的；迟疑的；不确定的 | Showing or feeling hesitancy.  Characterized by or showing hesitation. |

例句：

He gave a hesitant reply. = 他犹豫地回答。

She was hesitant to commit to the project. = 她对参与这个项目犹豫不决。


近义词：uncertain, wavering, irresolute, tentative, doubtful

反义词：certain, decisive, resolute, confident

词源:

The word "hesitative" comes from the verb "hesitate," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "hæsito," meaning "to stick," "to be slow," or "to stammer."  The sense of delay and uncertainty inherent in "hæsito" evolved into the modern meaning of hesitation.
