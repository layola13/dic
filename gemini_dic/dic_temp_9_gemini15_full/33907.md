circumventions:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛnʃənz/| n.|规避；绕过；回避|The act of circumventing something, especially a rule, law, or difficulty.  It implies finding a way around something rather than confronting it directly.|例句：The company was accused of circumventions of environmental regulations.  这家公司被指控违反环境法规。

例句：They found a clever circumvention to the problem. 他们找到了一个巧妙的方法来规避这个问题。


近义词：evasion, avoidance, bypass, workaround, outmaneuvering

反义词：confrontation, compliance, adherence

词性: 名词
