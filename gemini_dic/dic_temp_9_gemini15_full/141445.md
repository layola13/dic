opiates:/ˈoʊpiˌeɪts/| n.|鸦片类药物；阿片类药物|Substances derived from opium or synthetically produced that have similar effects; these drugs act on opioid receptors in the brain and body, producing analgesia (pain relief), euphoria, sedation, and respiratory depression.  They are highly addictive.|例句：The doctor prescribed opiates to manage his severe pain.  医生开了鸦片类药物来控制他剧烈的疼痛。

例句：Opiate addiction is a serious public health problem. 鸦片成瘾是一个严重的公共卫生问题。


近义词：narcotics, opioids, painkillers|反义词：无|词性:名词
