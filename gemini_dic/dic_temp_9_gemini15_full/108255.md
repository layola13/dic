launcher:/ˈlɔːntʃər/ /ˈlɔːntʃɚ/| n.|发射器；启动器；启动程序；发射装置|A launcher is a device or program used to launch or start something, such as a rocket, a game, or an application.|例句：The rocket launcher was aimed at the target.  火箭发射器瞄准了目标。|例句：Click the application launcher to open the program. 点击应用程序启动器以打开程序。|近义词：initiator, starter, activator|反义词：terminator, stopper
