hypochlorhydric:/ˌhaɪpəʊklɔːrˈhaɪdrɪk/| adj. | 次氯酸的，含少量盐酸的 |  Relating to or containing a deficiency of hydrochloric acid, especially in the gastric juice.  This term describes a condition where the stomach produces less hydrochloric acid than normal. | The patient's hypochlorhydric condition contributed to their poor digestion.  患者的次氯酸缺乏症导致其消化不良。|  hypochloremic (低氯血症的), achlorhydric (无盐酸的) | hyperchlorhydric (高氯酸的) | 形容词
