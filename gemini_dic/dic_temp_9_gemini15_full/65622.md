evangelicism:/ɪˌvænˈdʒɛlɪzəm/| n. |福音派；福音主义；福音运动|The belief and practice of spreading the Christian gospel.  It often emphasizes a personal relationship with Jesus Christ, the authority of the Bible, and the importance of evangelism (spreading the Christian message).  Different evangelical denominations and groups hold varying beliefs and practices.|例句：The rise of evangelicism in the United States has had a significant impact on American politics.|近义词：evangelism, Protestant fundamentalism|反义词：secularism, atheism
