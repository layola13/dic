legalization:/ˌliːɡəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |合法化；使合法 | The act of making something legal.  It refers to the process of changing a law or policy to allow something that was previously illegal. | Example: The legalization of marijuana is a controversial issue in many countries. 大麻合法化在许多国家都是一个有争议的问题。 |近义词：authorization, validation, sanction | 反义词：prohibition, banning, outlaw
