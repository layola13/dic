manchet:/ˈmænʃɪt/| n.|面包片，（报纸的）头版新闻|A manchet is a small, flat loaf of bread, or, in modern usage, a piece of bread.  It also refers to the leading article or headline story in a newspaper.|例句：The baker made several manchets for the picnic.  面包师为野餐做了几块小面包。|例句：The manchet of today’s newspaper focuses on the election. 今天的报纸头版新闻关注的是选举。|近义词：loaf, slice, headline|反义词：N/A
