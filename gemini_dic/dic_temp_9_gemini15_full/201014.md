sloop:/sluːp/| n.|单桅纵帆船|A sloop is a type of sailing vessel with one mast and a single headsail (fore-and-aft rigged).  It's characterized by its fore-and-aft rig, meaning the sails are set lengthwise along the boat, unlike square-rigged ships.|例句：The small sloop sailed gracefully across the bay. 这艘小帆船优雅地驶过海湾。The captain skillfully maneuvered the sloop through the narrow channel. 船长熟练地驾驶帆船穿过狭窄的海峡。|近义词：schooner, yawl, ketch|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with larger vessels like frigates or galleons.|名词
