reaction:/riˈækʃən/| n.|反应，回应|The process of responding to a stimulus or event.  It can refer to a physical, chemical, or emotional response.|例句：His reaction to the news was one of shock.  他对这个消息的反应是震惊。

例句：The chemical reaction produced a lot of heat.  化学反应产生了大量的热。


近义词：response, reply, effect, consequence|反义词：action (in some contexts), inaction|词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His reaction to the news is anger. = 他对这个消息的反应是愤怒。
What is your reaction to this proposal? = 你对这个提议有什么反应？


一般过去时:
Her reaction to the criticism was defensive. = 她对批评的反应是防御性的。
What was his reaction to the accident? = 他对事故的反应是什么？


现在完成时:
My reaction to the film has been mixed. = 我对这部电影的反应褒贬不一。
What has been your reaction to the recent changes? = 你对最近的改变有什么反应？


过去完成时:
By the time the police arrived, his reaction had been to flee. = 等警察到达时，他的反应是逃跑了。
What had been her reaction to the earlier events? = 她对早些时候发生的事件有什么反应？


将来时:
His reaction to the announcement will be interesting to see. = 他对公告的反应将会很有趣。
What will your reaction be if they refuse your request? = 如果他们拒绝你的请求，你的反应会是什么？


虚拟语气:
If he had reacted differently, the outcome might have been better. = 如果他的反应不同，结果可能会更好。
I wish my reaction hadn't been so impulsive. = 我希望我的反应不要那么冲动。


被动语态:
The proposal's reaction from the public was largely negative. = 公众对该提案的反应大多是负面的。
How was his reaction to the surprise interpreted? = 他对这个惊喜的反应是如何被解读的？


疑问句:
What is your reaction to the new policy? = 你对新政策有什么反应？
What was their reaction to the news? = 他们对这个消息有什么反应？


否定句:
There was no reaction from the audience. = 观众没有任何反应。
He showed no reaction to my apology. = 他对我的道歉没有任何反应。
