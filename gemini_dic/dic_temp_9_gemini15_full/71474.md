flamingly:/ˈfleɪmɪŋli/| adv.|非常，极其；燃烧地；火焰般地 |  Intensifying adverb indicating a high degree of something, often in a vivid or passionate manner. It can also literally describe something burning intensely. | 例句：He spoke flamingly about the injustice. 他激昂地谈论了这种不公正。  | 例句：The fire was burning flamingly. 火正熊熊燃烧。|近义词：fiercely, intensely, passionately, vividly|反义词：mildly, faintly, calmly
