ran-through: /ræn θruː/ | v. | 跑过；匆匆浏览；贯穿；仔细检查 | To pass quickly through or over something; to examine something quickly and superficially; to extend or penetrate throughout something. |

例句：

The children ran through the park. = 孩子们跑过了公园。

He ran through his notes before the presentation. = 他在演示之前匆匆浏览了他的笔记。


近义词： raced through, skimmed, perused, reviewed


反义词：  carefully examined, thoroughly investigated


词性： 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:

He runs through the park every morning. = 他每天早上都跑过公园。

They run through their lines before the play. = 他们在演出前排练台词。


一般过去时:

She ran through the forest. = 她跑过了森林。

He ran through the documents quickly. = 他快速浏览了文件。


现在完成时:

I have run through all my savings. = 我已经花光了所有的积蓄。

We have run through the entire script. = 我们已经把整个剧本过了一遍。


过去完成时:

They had run through the plan several times. = 他们已经多次演练过这个计划。

She had run through the list before calling. = 她打电话之前已经看过了清单。


将来时:

He will run through the city marathon. = 他将参加城市马拉松比赛。

They will run through the presentation again tomorrow. = 他们明天将再次进行演示。


虚拟语气:

If I ran through the forest, I might get lost. = 如果我跑过森林，我可能会迷路。

I wish I had run through the plan more carefully. = 我希望我当时能更仔细地研究一下计划。


被动语态:

The documents were run through by the secretary. = 文件由秘书过了一遍。

The proposal was run through quickly by the committee. = 提案被委员会快速浏览了一遍。


疑问句:

Did you run through the checklist? = 你检查过清单了吗？

Has she run through all the possibilities? = 她考虑过所有可能性了吗？


否定句:

He didn't run through the instructions carefully. = 他没有仔细阅读说明。

They haven't run through the budget yet. = 他们还没看完预算。
