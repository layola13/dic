disperser:/dɪˈspɜːrsər/| n. |分散器；扩散器|A device or substance that causes something to spread out or scatter.  It can refer to a physical device, like a spray nozzle, or a chemical agent.|例句：The farmer used a disperser to spread fertilizer evenly across the field.  农民用喷洒器将肥料均匀地撒在田里。

例句：The cloud disperser helped clear the fog from the airport runway.  云雾扩散器帮助机场跑道消除了雾霾。


近义词：disseminator, spreader, diffuser|反义词：concentrator, collector|词性:名词
