resource:/rɪˈsɔːrs/| n. |资源，财力|A source or supply from which benefit is produced.|The company's success depends on its human resources.  这家公司的成功取决于其人力资源。|The country lacks natural resources. 这个国家缺乏自然资源。 |wealth, asset, material |poverty, lack, scarcity |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We utilize many resources in our daily lives. = 我们在日常生活中利用许多资源。
This project requires significant financial resources. = 这个项目需要大量的资金。


一般过去时:
They exhausted their resources during the war. = 他们在战争中耗尽了资源。
