overjoyous: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪəs/ | adj. | 狂喜的，欣喜若狂的 | Extremely joyful; filled with overwhelming happiness. |

例句：

The news of her son's recovery filled her with overjoyous feelings.  = 她儿子康复的消息让她欣喜若狂。

His overjoyous laughter echoed through the hall. = 他欣喜若狂的笑声回荡在走廊里。


近义词：ecstatic, overjoyed, jubilant, rapturous


反义词：dejected, despondent, miserable, sorrowful


词性：形容词
