multiculture:/ˈmʌltiˌkʌltʃər/| n. |多元文化；多种文化 |A society or community that contains people from many different cultures.  It emphasizes the coexistence and interaction of diverse cultural groups within a shared space. |Example: The city boasts a vibrant multicultural community, with residents from all corners of the globe.  这座城市拥有一个充满活力的多元文化社区，居民来自世界各地。|近义词：cultural diversity, pluralism |反义词：monoculture, homogeneity
