cloaking:/ˈkloʊkɪŋ/ | n. |隐蔽；掩饰；伪装 |The act of concealing something or someone; the state of being concealed.  It often refers to making something difficult to detect or identify. |例句：The ship employed cloaking devices to avoid detection. 这艘船使用了隐形装置来避免被探测到。

例句：The magician's cloaking technique was impressive. 魔术师的隐身技巧令人印象深刻。


近义词：concealment, masking, camouflage, disguise |反义词：exposure, revelation, unveiling |词性：名词
