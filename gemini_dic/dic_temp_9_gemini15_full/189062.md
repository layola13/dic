saboteur:/ˈsæbəˌtʊr/| n. |破坏分子，捣乱分子 |A saboteur is a person who intentionally damages or destroys something, especially as an act of war or terrorism.  They engage in sabotage.  |例句：The saboteur planted a bomb in the factory. 这个破坏分子在工厂里安放了一枚炸弹。

例句：The enemy saboteurs were caught trying to disable the bridge. 敌人破坏分子企图破坏桥梁时被抓获。


近义词：underminer, wrecker, subversive, terrorist |反义词：constructor, builder, supporter
