heartburn:/ˈhɑːrtbɜːrn/| n. |胃灼热，烧心|A burning sensation in the chest, often caused by acid reflux.|I suffer from heartburn after eating spicy food. 我吃辛辣食物后会胃灼热。|acid reflux, indigestion|None|


USAGE->
