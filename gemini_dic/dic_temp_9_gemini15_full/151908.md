pastels:/ˈpæstəlz/| n.|彩色粉笔，柔和的色彩|Soft, delicate colors, often light and muted.  They are typically used in art supplies like crayons and chalks, or to describe a color scheme. |例句：The artist used pastels to create a dreamy landscape.  艺术家用彩色粉笔创作了一幅梦幻般的风景画。The room was decorated in pastel shades of pink and blue.  房间装饰着粉红色和蓝色的柔和色调。|近义词：soft colors, muted tones, delicate hues|反义词：bright colors, vivid colors, harsh colors|名词
