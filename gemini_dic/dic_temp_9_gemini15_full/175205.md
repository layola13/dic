ragweeds:/ˈræɡˌwiːdz/| n. |豚草属植物；艾蒿|Ragweeds are a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae, known for causing hay fever in susceptible individuals.  They are characterized by their inconspicuous flowers and abundant pollen.| 例句：Many people suffer from allergies due to ragweed pollen in the fall.  秋季许多人因豚草花粉而过敏。| 例句：The field was overgrown with tall weeds, including several varieties of ragweeds. 田里长满了杂草，其中包括几种豚草。|近义词：weeds,  ambrosia (specific type of ragweed)|反义词：无
