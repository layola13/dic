restorationist: /ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 修复主义者；复原论者 | A person who believes in or practices restorationism, especially in the context of religion or art.  Someone who advocates for restoring something to its original state or condition. |例句：The restorationist painstakingly repaired the damaged fresco.  修复主义者一丝不苟地修复了受损的壁画。

The restorationist meticulously cleaned and preserved the antique furniture.  这位修复专家细致地清洁和保存了古董家具。


近义词：restorer, conservator, revivalist | 反义词：destructionist, vandal
