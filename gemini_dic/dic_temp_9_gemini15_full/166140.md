preprofessed:/ˌpriːprəˈfɛst/| adj.| 预先声明的；预先承认的 |  Previously professed or declared; publicly acknowledged beforehand.  This word is rare and largely obsolete. It suggests a formal or official declaration made in advance. | Example:  His preprofessed innocence was quickly disproven by the evidence.  他的预先声明的清白很快就被证据驳倒了。 | 近义词:predeclared, previously stated, beforehand avowed | 反义词:unacknowledged, undisclosed, unannounced |  adj.
