iris's:/ˈaɪrɪsɪz/| n.|虹膜的；爱丽丝的（名词所有格）|The possessive form of "iris," referring to something belonging to Iris or to the iris flower.  It can also be the plural of "iris" (the flower), depending on context.|例句：These are Iris's paintings. 这些是爱丽丝的画作。|例句：The iris's delicate petals unfurled in the sunlight.  虹膜花纤细的花瓣在阳光下舒展开来。|近义词：Iris’ (possessive),  her (if referring to a person named Iris)|反义词：无|名词
