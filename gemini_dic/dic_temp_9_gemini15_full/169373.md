/ˌproʊnæʃənəlɪst/ | adj. | 宣扬民族主义的；民族主义者的 |  An adjective describing someone or something that promotes or advocates for pronationalism, a strong sense of national identity and often involves a belief in the superiority of one's own nation. It often carries a connotation of aggressive nationalism or chauvinism.  | Example:  The pronationalist rhetoric of the politician alienated many voters.  这位政治家的民族主义言论疏远了许多选民。 | 近义词：nationalistic, chauvinistic, jingoistic | 反义词：internationalist, cosmopolitan
