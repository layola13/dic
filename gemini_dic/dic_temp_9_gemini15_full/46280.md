czarowitz:/ˈzɑːrəvɪts/| n. |沙皇太子，沙皇继承人 |The word "czarowitz" refers to the heir apparent to the throne of the Russian Empire.  It is a title derived from the Russian word "tsarevich," meaning "son of the tsar" or "prince." | Example: Alexei Nikolaevich Romanov was the czarowitz of Russia before the Russian Revolution.  阿列克谢·尼古拉耶维奇·罗曼诺夫在俄国革命前是俄国的沙皇太子。|近义词:tsarevich, crown prince|反义词: None
