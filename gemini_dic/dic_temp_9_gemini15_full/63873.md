epileptogenous:/ˌɛpɪlɛpˈtɒdʒənəs/| adj.|致痫的，癫痫发生的|Causing or capable of causing epilepsy or epileptic seizures.  The term refers to factors, conditions, or substances that can trigger or contribute to the onset of seizures in individuals who are susceptible or who have a predisposition to epilepsy.|例句：The study investigated the epileptogenous effects of certain brain injuries. 这项研究调查了某些脑损伤的致痫作用。|例句：  The researchers identified an epileptogenous zone in the patient's brain. 研究人员在病人的大脑中发现了一个致痫区域。|近义词：convulsive, seizure-inducing|反义词：anticonvulsant, seizure-suppressing
