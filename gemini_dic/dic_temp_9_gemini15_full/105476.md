killifish: /ˈkɪliˌfɪʃ/ | n. | 鳉鱼 | A small fish of the family Cyprinodontidae, many species of which are able to survive in waters with low oxygen levels or high salinity.  They are often used in scientific research due to their hardiness and adaptability. | Example:  Many species of killifish are found in temporary pools that dry up seasonally. 许多鳉鱼物种生活在季节性干涸的临时水池中。 | 近义词: cyprinodont (more formal) | 反义词:  (no direct antonym,  but large fish could be considered an opposite in size) |  名词
