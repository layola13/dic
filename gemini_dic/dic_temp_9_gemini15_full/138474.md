novelle:/nɒˈvɛl/| n. |中篇小说，长篇故事|A novella is a work of fiction longer than a short story but shorter than a novel, typically ranging from 17,000 to 40,000 words. It often focuses on a single plotline and a limited number of characters.|Example:  "The Tell-Tale Heart" by Edgar Allan Poe is a famous example of a novella.|近义词：short story, novelette|反义词：novel
