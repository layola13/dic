heartily:/ˈhɑːrtɪli/| adj. |衷心地；热心地；丰盛地 |Warmly and enthusiastically; in a thorough or generous manner.  |例句：He laughed heartily. 他开怀大笑。  例句：They ate heartily. 他们吃得很饱。|近义词：warmly, enthusiastically, sincerely, abundantly |反义词：coldly, unenthusiastically, sparingly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They heartily support the project. = 他们衷心支持这个项目。
She heartily agrees with the decision. = 她完全同意这个决定。


一般过去时:
He heartily thanked his supporters. = 他衷心感谢他的支持者们。
They heartily enjoyed the meal. = 他们尽情享受了这顿饭。


现在完成时:
I have heartily welcomed the new employees. = 我热烈欢迎新员工。
We have heartily approved the proposal. = 我们衷心赞同这项提案。


过去完成时:
She had heartily prepared for the examination. = 她已为考试做了充分的准备。
They had heartily celebrated their victory. = 他们已热烈庆祝他们的胜利。


将来时:
He will heartily endorse the candidate. = 他将衷心支持这位候选人。
We will heartily welcome any suggestions. = 我们将热烈欢迎任何建议。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have heartily congratulated him. = 如果我知道的话，我会衷心祝贺他。
I wish I could have heartily participated in the event. = 我希望我能尽情参与这次活动。


被动语态:
The plan was heartily approved by the committee. = 该计划得到了委员会的衷心赞同。
The suggestion was heartily welcomed by the team. = 这个建议受到了团队的热烈欢迎。


疑问句:

否定句:
