quasi-private:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈpraɪvɪt/|/ˈkwɑːzi ˈpraɪvət/| adj.|准私人的，半私人的 |Approaching or resembling privacy;  partially private;  not completely public or open to all.  The term often describes entities or situations that possess some characteristics of private entities but are not entirely private, usually due to some degree of public involvement or regulation. |例句：The quasi-private nature of the club meant only certain individuals could join.|近义词：semi-private, semi-public, restricted |反义词：public, open
