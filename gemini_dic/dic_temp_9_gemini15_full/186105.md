rhabdoidal:/ˈræbdɔɪdəl/ | adj. | 杆状的；类杆状的 |  Relating to or resembling a rhabdoid; characterized by rod-shaped structures or cells.  The term often describes cells or structures that are elongated and rod-like, but not perfectly cylindrical. They may be slightly irregular or tapered. | Example: The tumor cells exhibited a rhabdoidal morphology, indicating a possible diagnosis of rhabdomyosarcoma.  肿瘤细胞表现出类杆状形态，表明可能是横纹肌肉瘤的诊断。  | 近义词: rod-shaped, rod-like, bacilliform | 反义词: spherical, rounded, coccoid
