non-factual:/ˌnɒnˈfækʧuəl/ | adj. |非事实的，虚构的 | Not based on or conforming to facts; untrue.  It describes something that is not true or accurate, often implying an element of invention or fabrication. | Example:  The story he told was completely non-factual; it was pure fantasy.  他讲的故事完全是虚构的；纯粹是幻想。  |近义词：fictional, imaginary, untrue, fabricated, invented |反义词：factual, true, real, accurate
