fiscally:/ˈfɪskəli/| adj.|财政上地；财政方面地|Relating to the finances or revenue of a government or other organization.|The company is fiscally responsible. 这家公司在财政上是负责任的。The government implemented fiscally conservative policies. 政府实施了财政保守的政策。|近义词：financially, economically|反义词：unfinancially

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government manages its fiscal affairs efficiently. = 政府有效地管理其财政事务。
Our company operates fiscally responsibly. = 我们公司在财政上运作谨慎。


一般过去时:
The country experienced a fiscally challenging year. = 该国经历了财政上充满挑战的一年。
The project was fiscally unsustainable. = 该项目在财政上不可持续。
