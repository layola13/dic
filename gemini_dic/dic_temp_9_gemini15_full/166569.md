prescribed:/prɪˈskraɪbd/| vt. |规定；开处方；指示；嘱咐|to order or recommend the use of something, especially a medicine or treatment; to lay down or dictate;  to order something to be done |例句：The doctor prescribed antibiotics for her infection. 医生为她的感染开了抗生素。|例句：The rules prescribed that only members could vote.  规定只有会员才能投票。|近义词：order, direct, mandate, dictate|反义词：prohibit, forbid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor prescribes medication for his patients. = 医生为他的病人开药。
The school prescribes a strict dress code. = 学校规定了严格的着装规范。

一般过去时:
The physician prescribed bed rest for the patient. = 医生为病人开了卧床休息的处方。
My teacher prescribed extra reading for the upcoming exam. = 我的老师为即将到来的考试布置了额外的阅读任务。

现在完成时:
The committee has prescribed new guidelines for the project. = 委员会已经为该项目制定了新的指导方针。
She has prescribed a strict diet for herself. = 她为自己制定了严格的饮食计划。

过去完成时:
The judge had prescribed a harsh sentence for the criminal. = 法官已对罪犯判处了严厉的刑罚。
The doctor had prescribed the medicine before the patient's arrival. = 在病人到达之前，医生已经开了药。

将来时:
The government will prescribe new regulations for environmental protection. = 政府将制定新的环境保护条例。
They will prescribe a detailed plan for the construction project. = 他们将为建设项目制定详细的计划。

虚拟语气:
If the doctor prescribed a different treatment, the outcome might have been better. = 如果医生采用了不同的治疗方法，结果可能会更好。
I wish the teacher had prescribed less homework. = 我希望老师少布置一些家庭作业。

被动语态:
The medication was prescribed by the doctor. = 药物是由医生开的。
New rules were prescribed by the company. = 公司制定了新的规章制度。

疑问句:
What medication has been prescribed for you? = 给你开了什么药？
What measures were prescribed to address the issue? = 采取了什么措施来解决这个问题？

否定句:
The doctor did not prescribe any strong painkillers. = 医生没有开任何强效止痛药。
The regulations did not prescribe a specific time limit. = 规章制度没有规定具体的期限。
