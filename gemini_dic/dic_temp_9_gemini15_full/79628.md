glossary:/ˈɡlɒsəri/ /ˈɡlɑːsəri/| n. |词汇表，术语表 |A glossary is an alphabetical list of terms and their definitions, often found at the end of a book or document.  It provides explanations of specialized vocabulary. |例句：The textbook included a glossary of technical terms at the back. 这本教科书在后面附有一个技术术语词汇表。|例句：The scientific paper had a comprehensive glossary defining all the specialized jargon. 这篇科学论文附有一个全面的词汇表，定义了所有专业术语。|近义词：lexicon, vocabulary, index |反义词：无
