crooks:/krʊks/ | n. | 骗子，钩子，弯曲物 |  A crook is a dishonest person, especially one who has committed a crime.  It can also refer to a hook or a bent object. |例句：The police caught the crook red-handed.  警方当场抓获了这个骗子。

He used a crook to pull the branches down. 他用钩子把树枝拉下来。|近义词：thief, swindler, rogue, villain, bend, curve |反义词：honest person, upright person, straight
