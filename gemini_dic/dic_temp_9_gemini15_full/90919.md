/ˈhɜːdn/ | n. |重担；负担；沉重的责任 |A burden is something difficult or unpleasant that you have to deal with.  It can be a physical weight or a responsibility. |例句：The extra work was a heavy burden on her.  额外的工作对她来说是个沉重的负担。

例句：Carrying those bags was a real burden. 搬那些包真是个累赘。


近义词：load, weight, onus, responsibility | 反义词：relief, ease


词源：古英语 *burþen, 源自原始日耳曼语 *burþan (负担)，与古高地德语 *burda (负担) 和古诺尔斯语 byrði (负担) 相关。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy backpack is a burden. = 这个沉重的背包是个负担。
This task is a significant burden for me. = 这项任务对我来说是一个很大的负担。

一般过去时:
The news was a terrible burden to bear. = 这个消息是个难以承受的重担。
The extra work proved to be a considerable burden last week. = 上周额外的工作证明是一个相当大的负担。

现在完成时:
The family has borne the burden of his illness. = 这个家庭承受了他生病的负担。
She has carried a heavy burden of responsibility for years. = 多年来她一直承担着沉重的责任。

过去完成时:
They had already shouldered a heavy burden before this new project. = 在这个新项目之前，他们已经承担了沉重的负担。
I had never felt such a burden before. = 我以前从未感到如此沉重的负担。

将来时:
The new regulations will place a greater burden on small businesses. = 新规将给小型企业带来更大的负担。
This decision will be a huge burden for many years to come. = 这个决定将在未来许多年成为一个巨大的负担。

虚拟语气:
If this were not a burden, I would gladly do it. = 如果这不是个负担，我会乐意去做。
I wish this burden were not so heavy. = 我希望这个负担不要这么重。

被动语态:
The burden was shared equally among the team members. = 负担在团队成员之间平均分配。
The extra work was felt as a burden by the staff. = 额外的工作让员工感觉到了负担。

疑问句:
Is this too much of a burden for you? = 这对你来说是不是太大的负担了？
What is the greatest burden you face today? = 你今天面临的最大负担是什么？

否定句:
This is not a burden; it's a privilege. = 这不是负担；这是荣幸。
The task was not as heavy a burden as I had anticipated. = 任务不像我预期的那样沉重。
