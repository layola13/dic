overemulate: /ˌoʊvərˈɛmjuleɪt/ | v. | 过度模仿，过分效仿 | To emulate excessively or to an inappropriate degree.  It suggests copying someone or something too closely, often to the detriment of originality or effectiveness. | 例句：He overemulated his mentor's style, losing his own distinctive voice. 他过度模仿导师的风格，失去了自己独特的嗓音。


He overemulated the successful marketing campaign of his competitor, resulting in a bland and ineffective strategy. 他过度模仿竞争对手成功的营销活动，导致策略平淡无奇且无效。

近义词：imitate excessively, overcopy, ape excessively | 反义词：innovate, originate, be original
