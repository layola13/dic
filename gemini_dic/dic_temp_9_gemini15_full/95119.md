inanitiation: /ɪˌnænɪʃɪˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 开始；启动；入门仪式 | The act of beginning something; the formal admission of someone into a group or organization, often involving a ritual.  | 例句：The initiation of the project was delayed due to funding issues. 项目的启动因资金问题而被推迟。


The initiation ceremony was a significant event for the new members. 入会仪式对新成员来说是一件重要的事情。


近义词：commencement, start, beginning, induction | 反义词：conclusion, end, termination | 词性：名词


词源:  The word "initiation" comes from the Latin word "initiatio,"  a combination of "initium" (beginning) and the suffix "-atio" (action or process).  It reflects the act of beginning something or starting a process.


USAGE->

The company initiates new projects every quarter. = 公司每个季度都会启动新的项目。

The team initiated the attack at dawn. = 队伍在黎明时发起了攻击。
