luncheonettes:/ˌlʌntʃəˈnɛts/| n. |小餐馆；快餐店|A small restaurant, typically one that serves inexpensive meals quickly.|Many luncheonettes offer classic diner fare like burgers and fries.  许多小餐馆提供汉堡和薯条等经典的餐厅美食。

The word "luncheonette" is a diminutive of "luncheon,"  meaning a small or informal luncheon.  The suffix "-ette" is a diminutive suffix that indicates smallness or a lesser version of something.


近义词：diner, cafe, snack bar
反义词：restaurant (in the sense of a formal, larger restaurant)
