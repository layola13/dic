gable-wall:/ˈɡeɪbəl wɔːl/| n.|山墙|A gable wall is the triangular portion of an exterior wall of a building, enclosed by two sloping roof sections that meet at a ridge.  It is typically found in buildings with gabled roofs.  The wall often features a gable window.  |Example: The gable wall of the old house was covered in ivy.  这座古老房屋的山墙上爬满了常春藤。The architect designed a large gable wall to showcase the home's unique architectural style. 建筑师设计了一个大型山墙来展示房屋独特的建筑风格。|近义词：end wall (in some contexts)|反义词：无
