nonsober:/ˈnɒnsoʊbər/| adj. |不清醒的；醉醺醺的；不严肃的 |Not sober; intoxicated; lacking seriousness.  The word combines "non-" (not) with "sober".|例句：He was nonsober and stumbled as he walked. 他喝醉了，走路摇摇晃晃。

例句：Her nonsober remarks offended many guests at the party. 她醉酒后说的那些话冒犯了许多参加派对的客人。


近义词：drunk, intoxicated, inebriated, tipsy, unsteady, irresponsible, frivolous


反义词：sober, clear-headed, serious, responsible, composed


词性:形容词
