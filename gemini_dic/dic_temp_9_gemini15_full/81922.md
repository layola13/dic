grilles:/ɡrɪlz/ | n. | 格栅；护栅；（扬声器等的）金属网罩 |  A grille is a structure consisting of parallel bars or wires, typically used for protection or decoration.  It can be found in various contexts, such as car radiators, building entrances, or speakers.  | 例句：The car's grille was damaged in the accident.  这辆车的格栅在事故中受损了。

The building's entrance featured an ornate grille. 这座建筑物的入口处有一个装饰华丽的格栅。


近义词： grating, screen, mesh | 反义词：opening (in the sense of an unobstructed space) |  词性：名词
