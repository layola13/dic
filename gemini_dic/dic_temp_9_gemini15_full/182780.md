repatriation: /ˌriːˌpætriˈeɪʃən/ | n. |遣返；归还；送回 |The process of returning a person to their own country.  It often refers to returning refugees, prisoners of war, or citizens who have been living abroad. |例句：The government organized the repatriation of refugees after the war.  政府在战争结束后组织了难民的遣返工作。

例句：The repatriation of cultural artifacts is a complex issue. 文化文物的归还是一个复杂的问题。


近义词：repatriamento, return, restoration |反义词：expatriation, deportation

词源:
