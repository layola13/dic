cassada:/kəˈsɑːdə/| n. |木薯|Cassava is a starchy tuberous root native to South America, widely cultivated as a staple food in tropical regions.  It is also known as yuca or manioc. |例句：Cassava is a crucial crop in many developing countries. 木薯是许多发展中国家的重要作物。|例句：We made cassava flour for our bread. 我们用木薯粉做了面包。|近义词：yuca, manioc|反义词：无
