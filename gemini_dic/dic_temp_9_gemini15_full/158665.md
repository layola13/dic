ploddingly:/ˈplɒdɪŋli/| adv.| 沉闷地；单调地；缓慢而吃力地 | In a plodding manner; slowly and steadily, but without much energy or excitement.  |例句：He ploddingly walked through the mud. 他吃力地走在泥泞中。
例句：She ploddingly completed her tedious tasks. 她单调乏味地完成了她的繁琐工作。|近义词：laboriously, tediously, painstakingly, doggedly |反义词：quickly, swiftly, rapidly, energetically
