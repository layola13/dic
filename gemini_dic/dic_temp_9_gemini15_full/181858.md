relook:/ˌriːˈlʊk/ | vt. |重新审视，再看一遍 | To look at or consider something again.  |例句：We need to relook at our marketing strategy. 我们需要重新审视我们的营销策略。 |近义词：review, reconsider, re-examine |反义词：ignore, overlook

We relook our budget monthly. = 我们每月都会重新审视我们的预算。
