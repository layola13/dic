kinks:/kɪŋks/ | n. | 癖好；怪癖；卷曲；扭结；故障 | A kink is a sharp bend or twist in something that is otherwise straight.  It can also refer to a strange habit or unusual behavior. |例句：There's a kink in the hose. = 水管有个弯曲的地方。

There are a few kinks to work out in the system. = 系统中有一些需要解决的问题。


近义词：twist, bend, quirk, oddity | 反义词：straightness, smoothness, normalcy | 词性：名词
