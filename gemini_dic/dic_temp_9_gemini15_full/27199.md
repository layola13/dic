callosan:/kæləˈsæn/| n. |胼胝体; (商品名)卡洛森(一种治疗鸡眼和老茧的药物)|A callosan is a corn or callous remover, often a medicated plaster.  It is also a type of corn plaster that contains salicylic acid to help soften and remove the corn or callous.|Example:  She used a callosan to treat her painful corn. 她用卡洛森治疗她疼痛的鸡眼。|近义词：corn plaster, callous remover |反义词：无
