harks:/hɑːrks/| v.|暗示；预示；提起；追溯|To hark is to mention something briefly or indirectly; to allude to something.  It also means to listen attentively. |例句：The poem harks back to the romantic era. 这首诗追溯到浪漫主义时代。

例句：The speaker harks to the past glories of the nation. 演讲者提到了国家过去的辉煌。


近义词：alludes, refers, mentions, suggests |反义词：ignores, overlooks


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harks back to his childhood memories. = 他追忆起童年的记忆。
The story harks to ancient myths. = 这个故事暗示着古代神话。


一般过去时:
She harked back to her days in college. = 她回忆起大学时光。
The lawyer harked to the previous testimony. = 律师提到了之前的证词。


现在完成时:
The novel has harked back to classic storytelling techniques. = 这部小说借鉴了经典的讲故事技巧。
The artist has harked to the influence of his teachers. = 这位艺术家受到了老师的影响。


过去完成时:
He had harked back to his roots before making the decision. = 他在做决定之前已经追溯到了他的根源。
They had harked to the warnings, but it was too late. = 他们曾注意过警告，但为时已晚。


将来时:
She will hark back to this moment in her memoirs. = 她将在她的回忆录中回顾这一刻。
The film will hark to the silent movie era. = 这部电影将让人想起无声电影时代。


虚拟语气:
If he harked back to his training, he might succeed. = 如果他回顾他的训练，他可能会成功。
I wish she had harked to my advice. = 我希望她能听从我的建议。


被动语态:
The past is often harked back to in times of uncertainty. = 在不确定的时期，人们经常追忆过去。
The legend is harked to by many generations. = 这个传说被许多代人所提及。


疑问句:
Does the painting hark back to any particular artist? = 这幅画是否借鉴了任何特定艺术家的风格？
Does the author hark to any specific historical events? = 作者是否提到了任何具体的史实？


否定句:
The speech did not hark to any controversial topics. = 这篇演讲没有涉及任何有争议的话题。
The painting does not hark back to any known artistic movement. = 这幅画不属于任何已知的艺术流派。


PHRASE->
hark back to = 回忆；追溯到
hark forward to = 展望；期待
hark upon = 详述；仔细考虑
hark at = 仔细听
hark the hounds = 猎犬追逐（通常用于狩猎）
