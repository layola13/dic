insufficience: /ɪnˈsʌfɪʃəns/ | n. | 不足，不够，缺乏 | The insufficiency of funds prevented the project from going ahead.  资金不足导致项目无法进行。| deficiency, inadequacy, shortage | sufficiency, abundance | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The insufficiency of evidence makes it difficult to convict the suspect. = 证据不足使得很难定罪。
His income is insufficient to support his family. = 他的收入不足以养家。


一般过去时:
The insufficiency of rainfall caused a severe drought. = 降雨不足导致了严重的干旱。
The insufficiency of resources hampered the company's growth. = 资源不足阻碍了公司的发展。


现在完成时:
The insufficiency of funding has delayed the completion of the project. = 资金不足已经延误了项目的完成。
The insufficiency of communication has led to misunderstandings. = 沟通不足导致了误解。


过去完成时:
The insufficiency of preparation had contributed to their failure. = 准备不足导致了他们的失败。
The insufficiency of supplies had left the soldiers vulnerable. = 补给不足使士兵们处于脆弱状态。


将来时:
The insufficiency of skilled labor will impact future economic growth. = 熟练劳动力的不足将影响未来的经济增长。
The insufficiency of resources will likely hinder the project's success. = 资源不足很可能阻碍项目的成功。


虚拟语气:
If there were no insufficiency of food, many people would not starve. = 如果没有食物短缺，许多人就不会挨饿。
I wish there were no insufficiency of funds for education. = 我希望教育经费不会短缺。


被动语态:
The failure was attributed to the insufficiency of planning. = 失败归咎于计划不足。
The delay is caused by the insufficiency of materials. = 延误是由材料不足造成的。


疑问句:
Is the insufficiency of resources a major concern? = 资源不足是一个主要问题吗？
What caused the insufficiency of the harvest? = 收成不足是什么原因造成的？


否定句:
There was no insufficiency of courage among the soldiers. = 士兵们并不缺乏勇气。
The quality of the work wasn't due to any insufficiency of skill. = 工作的质量并非由于缺乏技能。
