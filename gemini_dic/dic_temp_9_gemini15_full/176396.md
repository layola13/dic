re-grinds:/ˌriːˈɡraɪndz/| v.|再磨，重新磨碎|To grind again; to subject to a second grinding.  This verb implies a previous grinding process.  The object often refers to materials that need to be refined or reshaped through a second grinding.|例句：The coffee beans were re-ground to a finer consistency.  咖啡豆被重新磨碎到更细的稠度。|例句：The worn-out grinding wheel needs to be re-ground before it can be used again. 磨损的砂轮需要重新研磨才能再次使用。|近义词：regrind, reprocess, refine|反义词：leave as is,  not to grind.
