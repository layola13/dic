encores:/ˈɑːŋkɔːrz/| n.|加演；返场；再演；（尤指音乐会或戏剧表演结束后）观众要求再次演出的呼声|A request from an audience for a performer to give an additional performance, typically after a concert or play has officially ended.  It can also refer to the additional performance itself. |例句：The audience shouted for encores after the concert. 音乐会结束后，观众高呼要求加演。

例句：The band played three encores before finally leaving the stage. 乐队加演了三首歌曲才最终离开舞台。


近义词：repeat performance, encore performance, additional performance

反义词：No further performance, finale
