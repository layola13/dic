disguised:/dɪsˈɡaɪzd/ | adj. |伪装的；假扮的 |隐藏或掩饰真实身份或性质的；伪装的。The word suggests an intention to deceive or mislead.  |例句：The spy was disguised as a businessman. 间谍伪装成商人。

例句：The thief disguised himself to avoid recognition. 小偷乔装打扮以避免被认出。


近义词：camouflaged, masked, concealed, hidden | 反义词：revealed, exposed, unveiled | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is disguised as a waiter. 他伪装成服务员。
The building is disguised to look like a residential house. 这栋建筑伪装成住宅。


一般过去时:
She was disguised as a police officer. 她伪装成警官。
The soldiers were disguised in enemy uniforms. 士兵们穿着敌军的制服伪装自己。


现在完成时:
They have disguised their true intentions. 他们已经隐藏了他们的真实意图。
The painting has been disguised with a layer of varnish. 这幅画已经被一层清漆掩盖了。


过去完成时:
He had disguised himself before entering the building. 他在进入大楼前已经乔装打扮了。
The evidence had been disguised to mislead the investigators. 证据已被伪装以误导调查人员。


将来时:
They will disguise themselves before the mission. 他们将在任务开始前乔装打扮。
The company will disguise its financial losses. 公司将掩盖其财务损失。


虚拟语气:
If he were disguised, nobody would recognize him. 如果他乔装打扮，没有人会认出他。
I wish I had been disguised during the robbery. 我希望在抢劫案发生时我已经乔装打扮了。


被动语态:
His identity was disguised by a fake beard. 他的身份被假胡子掩盖了。
The true meaning of the poem was disguised by the author's cryptic language. 这首诗的真正含义被作者隐晦的语言所掩盖。


疑问句:
Is he disguised as a doctor? 他伪装成医生吗？
Are the criminals disguised? 罪犯们乔装打扮了吗？


否定句:
He was not disguised; everyone recognized him. 他没有伪装；每个人都认出了他。
The changes to the document were not disguised well enough. 文件的更改没有被很好地掩盖。


PHRASE->
highly disguised:高度伪装的
well disguised:伪装得很好
poorly disguised:伪装得很差
cleverly disguised:巧妙地伪装
skillfully disguised:巧妙地伪装


词源:
disguised 来自于动词 disguise，它源于古法语 desguiser，由 des- (表示“去除”、“取消”)和 guiser (表示“外观”、“伪装”)组成。  最终的词源可以追溯到古高地德语 *waisan，意思是“伪装”。
