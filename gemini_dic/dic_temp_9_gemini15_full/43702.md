creditless:/ˈkrɛdɪtlɪs/| adj.|无署名的；未被记入的；无信用额度的|Without credit or acknowledgment; lacking recognition or attribution.  It can also refer to something that does not involve credit or loans.|The film was creditless, meaning no one received screen credit for their work. 这部电影没有署名，意味着没有人获得屏幕署名。 The creditless loan application was denied.  未经信用评估的贷款申请被拒绝了。|近义词：uncredited, anonymous, unacknowledged|反义词：credited, acknowledged
