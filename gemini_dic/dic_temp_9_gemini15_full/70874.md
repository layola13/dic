finishes:/ˈfɪnɪʃɪz/| v.|完成，结束，终止|To bring to an end; complete.|例句：He finishes his work before 6 pm. 他在下午6点前完成工作。|例句：The race finishes at the stadium. 比赛在体育场结束。|近义词：complete, conclude, end|反义词：begin, start, commence|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She finishes her homework every night. = 她每晚都完成作业。
He finishes his work early. = 他很早就完成了工作。

一般过去时:
They finished the project on time. = 他们按时完成了项目。
She finished reading the book yesterday. = 她昨天读完了那本书。

现在完成时:
I have finished my report. = 我已经完成了我的报告。
Have you finished eating? = 你吃完了吗？

过去完成时:
We had finished dinner before they arrived. = 他们在到达之前我们已经吃完了晚饭。
She had finished her studies before she got married. = 她结婚前已经完成了学业。

将来时:
I will finish my work tomorrow. = 我明天将完成我的工作。
They will finish the painting by next week. = 他们下周将完成这幅画。

虚拟语气:
If I finished the project, I would be very happy. = 如果我完成了项目，我会非常高兴。
I wish I had finished my homework earlier. = 我真希望我早点完成作业。

被动语态:
The work was finished on time. = 工作按时完成了。
The report will be finished by Friday. = 报告将在周五完成。

疑问句:
Have you finished your work? = 你完成你的工作了吗？
When will you finish the painting? = 你什么时候完成这幅画？

否定句:
I haven't finished my assignment yet. = 我还没完成我的作业。
He didn't finish the race. = 他没有完成比赛。
