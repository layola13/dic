imported:/ɪmˈpɔːrtɪd/ | adj. | 进口的；引进的 | Brought in from another country or place.  The word describes goods, ideas, or practices that originate from elsewhere and are introduced into a new location.  | Example:  The store sells imported cheeses from France. 这家商店出售法国进口奶酪。

Example:  The company adopted imported management techniques.  这家公司采用了引进的管理技术。


近义词：foreign, introduced, non-native | 反义词：domestic, native, local | 词性: adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop sells imported chocolates. = 这家商店出售进口巧克力。
Imported goods are often more expensive. = 进口商品通常更贵。


一般过去时:
They imported a lot of rice last year. = 他们去年进口了很多大米。
The museum showcased imported artifacts. = 博物馆展出了进口文物。


现在完成时:
We have imported several new technologies recently. = 我们最近引进了几项新技术。
The country has imported significant amounts of oil. = 这个国家已经进口了大量的石油。


过去完成时:
They had imported all the necessary materials before starting construction. = 他们在开始施工前已经进口了所有必要的材料。
The company had imported advanced machinery by then. = 到那时，公司已经引进了先进的机械。


将来时:
We will import more fruits next season. = 我们下个季度将进口更多水果。
They will import new equipment for the factory. = 他们将为工厂进口新设备。


虚拟语气:
If we imported more, we would have lower prices. = 如果我们进口更多，价格就会更低。
I wish we had imported that technology earlier. = 我希望我们早点引进这项技术。


被动语态:
Many products are imported from China. = 许多产品是从中国进口的。
The new software was imported successfully. = 新软件成功地引入了。


疑问句:
Are these shoes imported? = 这些鞋子是进口的吗？
Where are these goods imported from? = 这些商品是从哪里进口的？


否定句:
This wine is not imported; it's locally produced. = 这款酒不是进口的；它是本地生产的。
We don't import that type of machinery anymore. = 我们不再进口那种类型的机械了。
