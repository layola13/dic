pompom: /ˈpɒmpɒm/ | n. |蓬蓬球；啦啦队队员的挥舞棒| A pompom is a fluffy decoration, often made of yarn or synthetic fibers, used for cheering or decoration.  They are typically used by cheerleaders to add visual excitement to performances. |例句：The cheerleaders waved their pompoms enthusiastically. 啦啦队员们热情地挥舞着他们的蓬蓬球。
