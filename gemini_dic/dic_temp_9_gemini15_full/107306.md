lamb:/læm/ | n. |羔羊，小羊；嫩羊肉 |A young sheep, especially one less than a year old; the meat from a young sheep.  |例句：The farmer raised a flock of lambs.  这位农民养了一群羔羊。

例句：We had roast lamb for dinner. 晚饭我们吃了烤羊肉。

近义词：sheep (young), kid (young goat) |反义词：mutton (meat from mature sheep) |词性:名词

The lamb drinks milk.  小羊喝奶。
The butcher sells lamb.  屠夫卖羊肉。


一般过去时:
The lamb played in the field. 小羊在田野里玩耍。

现在完成时:

将来时:
