lycanthrope:/ˈlɪkænθroʊp/| n. |狼人；兽人|A person who is believed to be able to change into a wolf or other wolf-like creature.  The term often refers to a mythical or fictional creature, but can also be used to describe someone with a psychological condition involving delusions of being a wolf.|例句：The legend speaks of a lycanthrope who terrorized the village. 传说中有一只狼人恐吓了整个村庄。

例句：He claimed to be a lycanthrope, but the doctors diagnosed him with a rare mental illness. 他声称自己是一个狼人，但医生诊断他患有一种罕见的精神疾病。


近义词：werewolf, shapeshifter|反义词：human (in the context of transforming)
