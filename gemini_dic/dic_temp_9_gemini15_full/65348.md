eulogises:/ˈjuːlədʒaɪzɪz/| v.|赞美；颂扬；悼词|To praise highly, especially in a speech or writing; to deliver a eulogy.|The speaker eulogised the deceased's kindness and generosity.  演讲者高度赞扬了死者的好心和慷慨。|The mourners eulogised their friend's achievements. 悼念者们赞扬了他们朋友的成就。|praise, commend, celebrate|criticize, condemn, denounce|动词
