hallmarks:/ˈhɔːlmɑːrks/ | n. | 标记，特征，品质证明 | Distinguishing characteristics or features of something, especially of high quality or authenticity; an official mark stamped on articles of gold or silver to indicate their standard of purity.  |  The car's hallmarks are its speed and reliability. 这辆车的显著特点是速度和可靠性。 The silver candlestick bore the hallmarks of a renowned silversmith. 这根银烛台带有著名银匠的品质证明。 | trademarks, characteristics, signs, features | defects, flaws |  名词
