overpoliced:/ˌoʊvərˈpoʊlɪst/ | adj. |过度警务的；警察力量过剩的 |  Describes a situation where a community or area experiences excessive police presence or intervention, often leading to negative consequences.  It suggests that the level of policing is disproportionate to the actual crime rate or the needs of the community. | Example:  The neighborhood felt overpoliced, with officers constantly patrolling the streets and stopping residents for minor infractions.  这个街区感觉警察过多，警察经常巡逻街道，并因轻微违规而拦截居民。|近义词：overpatrolled, heavily policed | 反义词：underpoliced, lightly policed | 形容词
