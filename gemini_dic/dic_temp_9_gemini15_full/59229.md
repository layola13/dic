dysury:/ˈdɪsjʊəri/ | n. |排尿困难，排尿不适 | Painful or difficult urination.  The condition is often associated with urinary tract infections, bladder stones, or other urinary tract disorders. | Example: She experienced dysuria after a long hike in the desert, likely due to dehydration. 她沙漠徒步后经历了排尿困难，可能是脱水造成的。  |近义词：strangury, painful urination |反义词：easy urination, painless urination
