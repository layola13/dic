It is typically found in oxidized copper deposits and is characterized by its dark green to black color and vitreous to resinous luster.|Example:  The sample contained traces of euchite, indicating past copper mineralization.  这个样本含有微量的欧冶矿，表明过去曾有铜矿化作用。|近义词:olivenite, libethenite|反义词:N/A
