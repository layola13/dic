lizard:/ˈlɪzərd/| n.|蜥蜴|A reptile with a long body and tail, four legs, and usually dry scaly skin. Lizards are found in warm climates all over the world.  They vary greatly in size and appearance.|例句：I saw a green lizard sunning itself on a rock.我看到一只绿蜥蜴在岩石上晒太阳。
例句：Many species of lizards are harmless to humans.许多种类的蜥蜴对人类无害。|近义词：reptile, saurian|反义词：N/A
