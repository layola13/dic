forge's: /fɔːrdʒz/ | n. | 锻造厂的；铁匠铺的 |  The possessive form of "forge," referring to something belonging to a forge or blacksmith.  It indicates ownership or relationship. | Example: The forge's bellows were old but still functional.  铁匠铺的风箱很旧，但仍然可以使用。 | 近义词: blacksmith's, smithy's | 反义词: N/A


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It describes possession, not action.  Therefore, tense variations are not applicable.
