leatherneck:/ˈlɛðərnɛk/ | n. |海军陆战队员|A slang term for a United States Marine, referring to the high, stiff leather collars worn by Marines in the early days of the Corps.  The term evokes a sense of toughness and tradition.|The leatherneck charged into battle with unwavering courage.|近义词：Marine, Devil Dog|反义词：无
