extend:/ɪkˈstɛnd/ |/ɪkˈstend/| vt. vi. n.|延长；扩展；伸展；给予；提供|To make something longer or larger; to stretch out; to offer or give something.|例句：The deadline has been extended. 截止日期被延长了。

The road extends for miles. 这条路绵延数英里。|近义词：prolong, expand, lengthen|反义词：shorten, contract, reduce|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They extend the bridge. = 他们延长了桥梁。
We extend our warmest welcome to you. = 我们向您致以最热烈的欢迎。

一般过去时:
He extended his hand in greeting. = 他伸出手打招呼。
The company extended its operations overseas. = 公司将业务扩展到海外。

现在完成时:
They have extended the deadline by a week. = 他们将截止日期延长了一周。
We have extended our support to the victims. = 我们已经向受害者提供了支持。

过去完成时:
They had extended the invitation before the event. = 他们在活动前已经发出了邀请。
She had extended her stay in Paris for another week. = 她已经在巴黎延长了为期一周的逗留。

将来时:
They will extend the railway line. = 他们将延长铁路路线。
We will extend our gratitude to our sponsors. = 我们将向我们的赞助商表示感谢。

虚拟语气:
If they extended the deadline, I could finish the project. = 如果他们延长截止日期，我就能完成项目了。
I wish they had extended the offer to me. = 我希望他们当时把这个机会给了我。

被动语态:
The contract was extended for another year. = 合同延长了一年。
The exhibition is being extended for another month. = 展览将延长一个月。

疑问句:
Will they extend the payment period? = 他们会延长付款期限吗？
Does this road extend all the way to the city? = 这条路一直延伸到城市吗？

否定句:
They did not extend the offer to him. = 他们没有给他提供这个机会。
The shop doesn't extend its opening hours on weekends. = 这家商店周末不延长营业时间。



PHRASE->
extend one's hand = 伸出手
extend an invitation = 发出邀请
extend one's stay = 延长逗留
extend credit = 贷款
extend sympathy = 表示同情


词源:
The word "extend" comes from the Old French word "estendre," which itself comes from the Latin word "extendere," meaning "to stretch out."  The Latin word is a combination of "ex-" (out) and "tendere" (to stretch).
