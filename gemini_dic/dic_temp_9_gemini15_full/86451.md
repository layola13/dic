heftiest:/ˈhɛftist/| adj.|最重的；最大的；最强壮的|The adjective "heftiest" is the superlative form of "heavy," meaning the greatest in weight, size, or importance. It suggests something significantly larger or more powerful than others in its class.  |例句：He lifted the heftiest box with ease. 他轻松地举起了最重的箱子。

例句：Among all the candidates, she had the heftiest campaign budget. 在所有候选人中，她拥有最大的竞选预算。


近义词：largest, biggest, heaviest, most substantial, most significant

反义词：lightest, smallest, least significant

词性:形容词
