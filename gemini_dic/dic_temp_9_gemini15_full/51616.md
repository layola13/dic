developable: /dɪˈvɛləpəbl/ | adj. | 可发展的；可展开的；可延展的 |

英文解析: Capable of being developed or unfolded;  able to be expanded or extended.  This word can refer to both physical objects (like a surface) and abstract concepts (like an idea or a plan).

例句:

This design is developable into a full-scale prototype.  这个设计可以发展成一个全尺寸的原型。

The software has a highly developable architecture.  这个软件具有高度可发展的架构。


近义词: expandable, extensible, unfoldable,  evolvable

反义词:  inextensible, unfurlable, fixed, static
