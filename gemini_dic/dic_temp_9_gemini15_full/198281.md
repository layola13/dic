shotokan:/ʃoʊtoʊkæn/| n. |少林拳|A style of karate emphasizing powerful linear techniques, strong stances, and precise execution.  It is one of the most popular styles of karate worldwide.  It originated in Japan and is characterized by its emphasis on kime (focus and precision of movement) and strong stances. | Example: He practices Shotokan karate three times a week. 他每周练习三次少林拳。 | 近义词：karate, martial art | 反义词：N/A
