dialoguing:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡɪŋ/| v.| 对话；交谈 |The act of engaging in dialogue; conversation or discussion.|例句：The students are dialoguing about the novel's themes. 学生们正在讨论这部小说的主题。|近义词： conversing, discussing, communicating |反义词：monologuing, soliloquizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are dialoguing about the project. = 他们正在讨论这个项目。
We dialogue regularly about our work. = 我们定期就工作进行对话。


一般过去时:
They dialogued extensively before reaching a decision. = 他们在做出决定之前进行了广泛的讨论。
We dialogued about the problem yesterday. = 我们昨天讨论了这个问题。


现在完成时:
They have dialogued with many stakeholders. = 他们已经与许多利益相关者进行了对话。
We have dialogued on this topic before. = 我们之前讨论过这个话题。


过去完成时:
They had dialogued for hours before the agreement. = 协议达成之前，他们已经讨论了数小时。
We had dialogued extensively before the meeting. = 会议之前我们已经进行了广泛的讨论。


将来时:
They will dialogue further tomorrow. = 他们明天将进一步讨论。
We will dialogue with the clients next week. = 我们下周将与客户进行对话。


虚拟语气:
If they dialogued more openly, they might find a solution. = 如果他们更坦诚地对话，他们可能会找到解决办法。
I wish we had dialogued more before making the decision. = 我希望我们在做决定之前能多进行一些讨论。


被动语态:
The issue was dialogued extensively in the meeting. = 会议上对这个问题进行了广泛的讨论。
The matter is being dialogued among the team members. = 这个问题正在团队成员之间讨论。


疑问句:
Are they dialoguing at the moment? = 他们现在正在对话吗？
Have they dialogued about the new policy? = 他们讨论过新政策了吗？


否定句:
They did not dialogue about the sensitive issue. = 他们没有讨论那个敏感的问题。
We haven't dialogued with them yet. = 我们还没和他们进行对话。


PHRASE->
engage in dialoguing = 参与对话

productive dialoguing = 有成效的对话
open dialoguing = 开放式对话
constructive dialoguing = 建设性对话
