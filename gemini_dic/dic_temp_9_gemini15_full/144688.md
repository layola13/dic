ouzel:/ˈuːzəl/| n.|欧歌鸫；乌鸫|A small, dark-colored bird belonging to the thrush family, known for its melodious song.  Several species exist, notably the ring ouzel and the water ouzel.|The ouzel sang a beautiful song in the early morning.|近义词：thrush, blackbird|反义词：无
