foggy:/ˈfɑːɡi/ /ˈfɒɡi/| adj. |雾蒙蒙的，模糊的|Covered or obscured by fog; misty.  The adjective "foggy" describes something that is difficult to see clearly due to fog or a similar obscuring element. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something unclear or uncertain.|例句：The morning was foggy, reducing visibility to near zero.  早晨雾很大，能见度几乎为零。

The future seems foggy to me right now.  我现在对未来感到迷茫。|近义词：misty, hazy, cloudy, unclear, obscure|反义词：clear, bright, transparent, distinct
