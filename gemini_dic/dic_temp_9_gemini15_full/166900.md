/prɪˈstiːm/| adj.| 预热的，预先蒸汽处理的 |  Preheated; treated with steam beforehand.  This term is typically used in the context of food preparation or industrial processes.  | 例句：The presteamed vegetables cooked more quickly. = 预先蒸过的蔬菜煮得更快。

The presteamed buns were soft and fluffy. = 预先蒸过的包子柔软蓬松。

| 近义词：preheated, steamed | 反义词：unsteamed, raw | 形容词
