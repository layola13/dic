head-dress:/ˈhedˌdrɛs/| n.| 头饰 |An ornament or covering worn on the head.  It can range from simple bands to elaborate constructions.|例句：She wore a beautiful flower head-dress to the wedding. 她婚礼上戴了一个漂亮的花饰头饰。|例句：The ancient Egyptian head-dress was a symbol of status. 古埃及的头饰是地位的象征。|近义词：headdress, headgear, hat, crown|反义词：None specifically.

词源:
The word "head-dress" is a compound word formed from "head" and "dress."  "Head" refers to the top part of the body, and "dress" in this context means to adorn or decorate.  The combination therefore literally means something that is used to dress or decorate the head.
