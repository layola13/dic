pickier:/ˈpɪkiər/| adj.| 更加挑剔的；更讲究的 | More demanding or fastidious; showing more preference for particular things.  |例句：She's getting pickier about her food as she gets older. 她年纪越大，对食物就越挑剔。

例句：The restaurant caters to pickier eaters with its wide variety of options. 这家餐厅以其各种各样的选择迎合了更挑剔的食客。


近义词：fussier, more selective, more discerning, more particular

反义词：less demanding, undemanding, unfussy


词源:

源自动词 "pick"  (挑选，选择)，加上后缀 "-ier" 表示比较级，意为"更……的"。  因此，“pickier” 表示比一般的更挑剔。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pickier about his clothes than his brother. = 他比他哥哥更挑剔衣服。
She is pickier with her friends than I am. = 她比我更挑剔朋友。


一般过去时:
He was pickier about the restaurant choices yesterday. = 他昨天对餐厅的选择更挑剔。
She was pickier than usual when choosing a dress for the party. = 她选参加聚会的衣服时比平时更挑剔。


现在完成时:
He has become pickier about his diet recently. = 他最近对饮食越来越挑剔了。
She has always been pickier than her siblings about her appearance. = 她一直以来都比她的兄弟姐妹们更注重外表。


过去完成时:
He had been pickier in the past, but now he's more relaxed. = 他过去比较挑剔，但现在放松多了。
She had already been pickier than expected when we started shopping. = 我们开始购物的时候，她就已经比预期的更挑剔了。


将来时:
He will be pickier with his choices as he gets older. = 他年纪越大，选择就会越挑剔。
She will likely be pickier about the details of the project. = 她很可能会对项目的细节更挑剔。


虚拟语气:
If he were pickier about his work, he would be more successful. = 如果他对工作更挑剔，他会更成功。
If she were pickier with her investments, she wouldn't have lost so much money. = 如果她投资更谨慎，她就不会损失那么多钱。


被动语态: (此词性较少用被动语态)
His preferences are considered pickier than average. = 他的偏好被认为比平均水平更挑剔。
The requirements are considered pickier than what they were. = 要求比以前更严格。


疑问句:
Is he getting pickier in his old age? = 他老了是不是越来越挑剔了？
Are they pickier about their food than usual? = 他们对食物是不是比平时更挑剔？


否定句:
He isn't pickier than most people. = 他没有大多数人那么挑剔。
She wasn't pickier about the details this time. = 她这次对细节并不那么挑剔。


PHRASE->
a pickier eater = 一个挑食的人
become pickier = 变得挑剔
get pickier = 变得挑剔
more pickier = 更挑剔 (虽然语法上更常用 more selective)
much pickier = 非常挑剔
