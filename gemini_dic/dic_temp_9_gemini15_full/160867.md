post-axially: /ˌpoʊstˈæksiəli/ | adj. | 轴后地，轴后位地 |  Describing something positioned or occurring behind or beyond the axis of something.  It often refers to anatomical locations or movements related to a joint or bone. | The injury was post-axially located, affecting the structures behind the joint's axis. = 损伤位于轴后，影响关节轴后的结构。The rotation was post-axially directed. = 旋转方向指向轴后。| 近义词:posterior, behind | 反义词: pre-axially, anterior
