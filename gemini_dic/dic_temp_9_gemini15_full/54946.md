dispost:/dɪˈspoʊst/| adj.|  处理掉的；被丢弃的；倾向于…的 | disposed of; inclined; tending | disposed of  指处理掉某物，通常指废弃或清除；inclined/tending 指倾向于某种行为或状态。|例句：The old furniture was disposed of. 旧家具被处理掉了。  He is disposed to be helpful. 他乐于助人。|近义词：inclined, prone, tending, ready |反义词：undisposed, reluctant, unwilling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disposes of its waste responsibly. = 这家公司负责任地处理废物。
He is disposed to agree with you. = 他倾向于同意你的意见。


一般过去时:
They disposed of the evidence quickly. = 他们迅速处理掉了证据。
She was disposed to believe him at first. = 她起初倾向于相信他。


现在完成时:
We have disposed of all the old documents. = 我们已经处理掉了所有旧文件。
He has been disposed to help others throughout his life. = 他一生都乐于助人。


过去完成时:
They had disposed of the body before the police arrived. = 警方到达之前，他们已经处理掉了尸体。
She had been disposed to doubt his story, but then she changed her mind. = 她曾倾向于怀疑他的说法，但后来她改变了主意。


将来时:
We will dispose of this problem efficiently. = 我们将有效地解决这个问题。
He will be disposed to forgive you, if you apologize sincerely. = 如果你真诚道歉，他会倾向于原谅你。


虚拟语气:
If I were disposed to help, I would. = 如果我乐意帮忙，我会帮忙的。
If he had been disposed to listen, he might have understood. = 如果他愿意听，他可能会理解。


被动语态:
The waste was disposed of improperly. = 废物处理不当。
The matter will be disposed of fairly. = 这件事将会得到公平处理。


疑问句:
How will you dispose of the garbage? = 你打算怎么处理垃圾？
Is he disposed to cooperate with us? = 他愿意与我们合作吗？


否定句:
I am not disposed to argue with you. = 我不想和你争论。
They were not disposed to accept his explanation. = 他们不愿意接受他的解释。


PHRASE->
dispose of: 处理，清除
be disposed to: 倾向于，乐意
at one's disposal: 任凭某人支配
final disposal: 最终处理
proper disposal: 正确的处理方式
