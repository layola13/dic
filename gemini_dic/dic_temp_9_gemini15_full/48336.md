decriminalize:/ˌdiːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪz/| vt.|使非犯罪化；将…除罪|To remove the criminal status of an act or offense.|例句：Many countries have decriminalized the possession of small amounts of marijuana. 许多国家已经将少量持有大麻的行为非罪化。|例句：The government is considering decriminalizing drug use. 政府正在考虑将吸毒行为非罪化。|近义词：legalize, legitimize|反义词：criminalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They decriminalize certain minor offenses. = 他们将某些轻微罪行非罪化。
The city council decriminalizes public drinking. = 市议会将公开饮酒行为非罪化。


一般过去时:
The government decriminalized homosexuality in 1980. = 政府在1980年将同性恋行为非罪化。
They decriminalized prostitution in the red-light district. = 他们在红灯区将卖淫行为非罪化。


现在完成时:
Many states have decriminalized marijuana possession. = 许多州已经将持有大麻的行为非罪化。
The country has decriminalized assisted suicide under certain conditions. = 该国在特定条件下已将协助自杀行为非罪化。


过去完成时:
By the time the new law passed, they had already decriminalized gambling. = 新法律通过时，他们已经将赌博行为非罪化了。
Before the reform, they had not decriminalized same-sex marriage. = 改革之前，他们还没有将同性婚姻非罪化。


将来时:
The legislature will decriminalize certain drug offenses next year. = 立法机关明年将使某些毒品犯罪非罪化。
The government will likely decriminalize small-scale cannabis cultivation. = 政府可能会将小规模种植大麻的行为非罪化。
