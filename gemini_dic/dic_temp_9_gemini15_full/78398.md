get-back-up:/ɡɛt ˈbæk ʌp/ | phrasal verb | 恢复；重新开始；重新站起来 |  This phrasal verb describes the action of recovering from a setback, failure, or a fall, both literally and figuratively. It implies a renewed effort or attempt after an interruption or defeat.  |例句：After the setback, the team got back up and fought for the win. 在遭遇挫折后，球队重新振作起来，为胜利而战。

The company got back up after a period of financial difficulty. 公司在经历一段时间的财务困境后恢复了元气。|近义词：recover, rebound, resume, restart |反义词：give up, surrender, quit |词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gets back up after every fall. = 他每次跌倒后都会重新站起来。
They get back up and try again. = 他们重新站起来，再试一次。


一般过去时:
She got back up and finished the race. = 她重新站起来，完成了比赛。
The business got back up after the recession. = 这家企业在经济衰退后恢复了元气。


现在完成时:
He has gotten back up from many challenges. = 他已经克服了很多挑战。
The economy has gotten back up after the crisis. = 经济危机后经济已经复苏。


过去完成时:
They had gotten back up before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经恢复了。
She had gotten back up many times before finally succeeding. = 在最终成功之前，她已经多次重新站起来。


将来时:
He will get back up and fight again. = 他将会重新站起来，再次战斗。
The country will get back up stronger after this disaster. = 这个国家将会在这个灾难之后更加强大地恢复元气。


虚拟语气:
If he fell, he would get back up. = 如果他跌倒了，他会重新站起来。
If the company had failed, it wouldn't have gotten back up. = 如果公司倒闭了，它就不会恢复元气。


被动语态:
The project was gotten back up after a delay. = 项目在延误后重新启动了。(不太常用，因为get back up 通常是主动行为)
The fallen athlete was helped to get back up. = 摔倒的运动员得到了帮助重新站起来。


疑问句:
Will he get back up after this defeat? = 他会在这次失败后重新站起来吗？
Can the economy get back up quickly? = 经济能够迅速复苏吗？


否定句:
He didn't get back up after the first attempt. = 他第一次尝试后没有重新站起来。
The project wasn't gotten back up after the cancellation. = 项目取消后没有重新启动。


PHRASE->
get back up and running  = 恢复运转
get back up to speed = 恢复正常速度
get back up on your feet = 重新振作起来
get back up the courage = 重新鼓起勇气
get back up to work = 重新开始工作
