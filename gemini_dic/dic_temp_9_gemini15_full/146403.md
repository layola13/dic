overfly:/ˈoʊvərˌflaɪ/| vt.|飞越；凌驾于|To fly over or above (something).  The act of flying over a place or area.|例句：The plane will overfly the mountain range.  飞机将飞越山脉。

The birds overflew the city. 鸟儿飞越了城市。

近义词：fly over, soar over, pass over|反义词：underfly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pilot overflies the city every day. = 飞行员每天都飞越这座城市。
He overflies the target area before bombing. 他在轰炸前飞越目标区域。


一般过去时:
The plane overflew the Atlantic Ocean. = 飞机飞越了大西洋。
They overflew the enemy's territory undetected. 他们未被发现地飞越了敌人的领土。


现在完成时:
The squadron has over flown the designated area three times.  = 中队已经三次飞越了指定区域。
We have over flown many mountains during this journey. 在这次旅程中，我们已经飞越了许多山脉。


过去完成时:
The bird had over flown the forest before landing on the field. = 这只鸟在降落到田野之前已经飞越了森林。
Before the mission began, the pilot had over flown the target area several times. 在任务开始之前，飞行员已经多次飞越目标区域。


将来时:
The helicopter will overfly the accident site. = 直升机将飞越事故现场。
We will overfly the coast before reaching the island. 我们将在到达岛屿之前飞越海岸。


虚拟语气:
If the weather permitted, we would overfly the Grand Canyon. = 如果天气允许，我们将飞越科罗拉多大峡谷。
I wish I could overfly the Amazon rainforest. 我希望我能飞越亚马逊雨林。


被动语态:
The area was over flown by several aircraft. = 该区域被几架飞机飞越。
The mountain range will be over flown by the passenger plane. 山脉将被客机飞越。


疑问句:
Will the plane overfly the capital? = 飞机会飞越首都吗？
Did the drone overfly the restricted zone? 无人机飞越了禁区吗？


否定句:
The plane did not overfly the target area. = 飞机没有飞越目标区域。
The birds did not overfly the mountain today. 鸟儿今天没有飞越山脉。


PHRASE->
overfly the area = 飞越该区域
overfly the target = 飞越目标
overfly the enemy lines = 飞越敌军防线
overfly the terrain = 飞越地形
overfly the route = 飞越航线


词源:
over- (表示“在…之上”) + fly (飞)  组合而成。
