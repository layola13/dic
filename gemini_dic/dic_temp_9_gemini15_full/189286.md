sadistic:/səˈdɪstɪk/| adj.|虐待狂的；残忍的；令人痛苦的| Deriving from the name of the Marquis de Sade, a French aristocrat known for his libertine writings that depicted extreme sexual violence and cruelty, "sadistic" describes deriving pleasure from inflicting pain or suffering on others. It implies a deliberate and often systematic infliction of cruelty, going beyond simple aggression or anger.  |例句：His sadistic tendencies manifested in his cruel treatment of animals. 他的虐待狂倾向表现在他对动物的残酷虐待上。

He displayed sadistic pleasure in watching the suffering of others. 他从观看别人的痛苦中获得了变态的快乐。|近义词：cruel, brutal, vicious, merciless, tyrannical |反义词：kind, compassionate, merciful, benevolent
