depoliticization: /ˌdiːˌpɒlɪtɪˈzeɪʃən/ | /ˌdiːˌpɑːlətɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 去政治化；非政治化 | The process of removing something from political influence or control.  It often involves reducing the role of politics in a particular area or issue, making it less partisan or ideological.  This can happen through various means, including legal reforms, institutional changes, or shifts in public opinion. |

例句：

The depoliticization of the judiciary is crucial for maintaining public trust. = 司法机构的去政治化对于维护公众信任至关重要。

The ongoing depoliticization of the media is a cause for concern for many. = 媒体正在进行的去政治化令许多人担忧。


近义词：neutralization, apoliticism, depersonalization (in some contexts)


反义词：politicization


词性：名词
