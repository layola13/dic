formatives:/ˈfɔːrmətɪvz/ | n. |构成要素；构成部分； formative assessment |构成性评价；形成性评价；塑造性评价 |Formatives are the elements that contribute to the formation or development of something.  Formative assessment is a type of assessment used to monitor student learning to provide ongoing feedback that can be used by instructors to improve their teaching and by students to improve their learning. |例句：The early experiences of a child are crucial formatives in shaping their personality.  儿童早期的经历是塑造其性格的关键构成要素。|例句：The teacher used formative assessment to identify areas where students needed extra help. 老师使用形成性评价来确定学生需要额外帮助的领域。|近义词：components, constituents, elements, building blocks |反义词：results, outcomes (in the context of assessment)
