hold-in:/ˈhoʊldɪn/| n. |禁闭；压制；忍耐|A period of confinement or restriction, often imposed as a punishment or for safety reasons.  It can also refer to the act of suppressing or restraining something.|例句：The prisoners were kept in a prolonged hold-in. 囚犯被长时间禁闭。|例句：She managed a hold-in of her anger during the meeting.她在会议期间成功地抑制了自己的怒火。|近义词：confinement, detention, restraint|反义词：release, freedom
