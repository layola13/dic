gazpacho:/ɡæzˈpætʃoʊ/| n. |西班牙凉菜汤 |A cold Spanish soup made of blended raw vegetables, typically tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers, and onions, often with bread and olive oil.  | Example:  We enjoyed a refreshing bowl of gazpacho on a hot summer day. 我们在炎热的夏日享用了一碗清爽的西班牙凉菜汤。 |近义词：cold soup, vegetable soup |反义词：hot soup

词源:
The word "gazpacho" is derived from the Castilian Spanish word "gazpacho," which in turn may be of Moorish origin, possibly from the Arabic word "kasbāsh," meaning a "soaked bread."  The evolution of the word reflects the historical influences on Spanish cuisine.
