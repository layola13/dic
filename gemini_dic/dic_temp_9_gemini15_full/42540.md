countermemo:/ˈkaʊntərˌmɛmoʊ/| n. |反驳备忘录；反驳意见书|A countermemo is a document written in response to a memo, usually to refute or contradict its claims or arguments.  It often presents a different perspective or opposing viewpoint. |Example:  The defense attorney submitted a countermemo challenging the prosecution's evidence.  辩护律师提交了一份反驳备忘录，对起诉方的证据提出质疑。|近义词：rebuttal, reply, response|反义词：original memo, supporting memo|名词
