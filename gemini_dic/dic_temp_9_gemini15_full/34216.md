clanging:/ˈklæŋɪŋ/| adj. |铿锵作响的，震耳欲聋的 |  Describes a loud, resonant, metallic sound, often repetitive and harsh.  It can also describe something that is visually striking or overwhelming in a similar way to the sound. | 例句：The clanging of the factory bells signaled the end of the shift. 工厂的钟声铿锵作响，预示着轮班结束。

The clanging cymbals filled the concert hall. 震耳欲聋的铙钹声充满了音乐厅。

近义词：ringing, resounding, clangorous, clamorous | 反义词：soft, quiet, muted

词源:
