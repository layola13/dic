granita:/ɡrəˈniːtə/| n. |意大利冰沙|A semi-frozen dessert made of sugar, water, and fruit juice or other flavorings, typically served in a slushy consistency.|例句：The granita was refreshingly cold on a hot summer day. 炎热的夏日里，这冰沙令人神清气爽。|近义词：slush, shaved ice|反义词：hot drink, ice cream
