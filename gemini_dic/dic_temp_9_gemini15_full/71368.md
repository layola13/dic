flagellating: /ˈflædʒəleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |鞭打；痛斥；自我鞭挞 |  The act of whipping or beating severely; to criticize harshly; to inflict self-punishment. | 例句：The monk was flagellating himself as a form of penance.  = 这位僧侣正在用鞭子抽打自己作为一种赎罪的方式。

The protesters were flagellating the government for its inaction. = 抗议者们严厉批评政府的不作为。

近义词：flogging, whipping, scourging, castigating, berating | 反义词：praising, commending, applauding

词源:
