mucker:/ˈmʌkər/| n. |弄脏者；伙伴；朋友；（尤指）矿工|A mucker is someone who works hard, especially in a dirty or unpleasant job.  The word also carries informal connotations of friendship and camaraderie. In some dialects, it can also mean a person who makes a mess or someone who is unpleasant.|例句：The miners were all muckers, working together in the coal mine. 矿工们都是伙伴，一起在煤矿工作。
例句：He's a right mucker, always getting into scrapes. 他是个十足的捣蛋鬼，总是惹麻烦。|近义词：mate, pal, buddy, companion,  colleague (in a work context)|反义词：enemy, foe, opponent, rival
