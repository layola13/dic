evaded:/ɪˈveɪdɪd/ | v. | 逃避；规避；躲避 | To escape from or avoid something, especially cleverly or skillfully.  | He evaded the police by fleeing across the border. 他越境逃亡以躲避警方。 |近义词: avoid, escape, dodge, circumvent | 反义词: confront, face, encounter |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He evades taxes every year. 他每年都逃税。
She evades answering difficult questions. 她回避回答难题。

一般过去时:
The prisoner evaded capture for months. 囚犯几个月都躲避抓捕。
They cleverly evaded the security guards. 他们巧妙地躲过了保安。

现在完成时:
He has evaded responsibility for his actions. 他逃避了他行为的责任。
She has evaded the issue for too long. 她对这个问题回避太久了。

过去完成时:
The thief had evaded detection until now. 小偷直到现在才被发现。
They had evaded the law for years. 他们违法多年未被发现。

将来时:
He will evade the question again. 他还会再次回避这个问题。
She will try to evade paying her debts. 她会试图逃避偿还债务。

虚拟语气:
If he had not evaded the police, he would be in jail now. 如果他没有躲避警察，他现在会在监狱里。
I wish I could evade my responsibilities, but I can't. 我真希望我能逃避责任，但我不能。

被动语态:
The question was evaded by the politician.  这个问题被政客回避了。
The truth was cleverly evaded for many years. 真相被巧妙地掩盖了许多年。

疑问句:
Did he evade the issue? 他是否回避了这个问题？
How did she evade paying her taxes? 她是如何逃税的？

否定句:
He did not evade the question. 他没有回避这个问题。
She could not evade her responsibilities. 她无法逃避她的责任。


PHRASE->
evade capture: 逃避逮捕
evade responsibility: 推卸责任
evade the law: 逃避法律
evade taxes: 逃税
evade a question: 回避问题


词源:
From Old French *evader, from Latin *evadere, from ex- ("out") + vadere ("to go").  The sense development is from "go out" to "escape."
