hydroponicum: /ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪkəm/ | n. | 水培法；水耕栽培 |  A method of growing plants without soil, using mineral nutrient solutions in a water solvent.  Hydroponics is a subset of hydroculture, which encompasses any technique of growing plants without soil. | Example: The farmer uses hydroponicum to grow tomatoes in his greenhouse.  这位农民在他的温室里使用水培法种植西红柿。| 近义词：hydroponics, aquaponics | 反义词：soil-based cultivation
