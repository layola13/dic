gluteal:/ˈɡluːtiəl/| adj. |臀部的；臀肌的|Relating to the buttocks or gluteus muscles.  The word refers to the muscles and the area of the body they occupy.|例句：The gluteal muscles are important for walking and running.  臀部肌肉对于步行和跑步很重要。

例句：She felt a sharp pain in her gluteal region. 她感到臀部剧烈疼痛。


近义词：buttock, ischial


反义词：没有直接的反义词，但可以考虑与“胸部”、“腹部”等相对


词性:形容词
