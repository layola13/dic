kitsch:/kɪtʃ/| n. adj.|俗气；庸俗的艺术；华而不实的东西|Something considered to be of poor quality, showing a lack of good taste, but appealing to many people because it is cheap and showy.  It often refers to art, design, or other objects that are mass-produced and lacking in originality or artistic merit.  The term carries a somewhat condescending tone, implying a lack of refined taste.|例句：The hotel lobby was decorated with kitsch souvenirs and gaudy paintings.  这家酒店的大堂装饰着俗气的纪念品和俗丽的绘画。

The brightly coloured plastic flamingos were a prime example of kitsch.  那些颜色鲜艳的塑料火烈鸟是俗气的典型例子。|近义词：tacky, gaudy, cheap, trashy |反义词：refined, elegant, tasteful, sophisticated |名词，形容词
