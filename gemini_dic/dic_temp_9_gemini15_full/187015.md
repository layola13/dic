ritenuto:/rɪtɛˈnuːtoʊ/ | adj. | 渐慢 |  A musical direction indicating a gradual slowing of the tempo.  It implies a slight, deliberate retardation rather than a sudden stop.  The tempo is slowed gradually and smoothly, usually to emphasize a particular phrase or section. | 例句：The pianist played the final phrase with a ritenuto, emphasizing the emotional weight of the melody.  钢琴家用渐慢的演奏方式演奏了最后的乐句，突出了旋律的情感分量。

The conductor indicated a ritenuto before the final chord. 指挥在最后一个和弦之前指示渐慢。


近义词：rallentando, ritardando | 反义词：accelerando, stringendo | 词性：形容词
