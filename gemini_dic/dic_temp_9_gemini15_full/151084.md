paraquat: /ˈpærəkæt/ | n. |百草枯|Paraquat is a highly toxic herbicide used to kill weeds and other unwanted plants.  It works by inhibiting photosynthesis and causing oxidative stress in plants.  Due to its high toxicity to humans and animals, its use is strictly regulated in many countries.|The farmer sprayed paraquat on the field to control the weeds.|近义词：diquat (but less toxic)|反义词：herbicide antidote (not a single word antonym)|名词
