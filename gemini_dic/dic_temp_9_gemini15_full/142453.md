oscilloscopes:/ɒˈsɪləskoʊps/ | n. |示波器 | An oscilloscope is a type of electronic test instrument that allows observation of varying voltage in a circuit.  It's commonly used to display waveforms, which are visual representations of the voltage changes over time. This allows engineers and technicians to analyze signals, troubleshoot circuits, and measure various electrical properties. | The engineer used an oscilloscope to analyze the signal from the sensor.  工程师用示波器分析传感器发出的信号。


The technician checked the circuit with an oscilloscope to find the fault.  技师用示波器检查电路以查找故障。|voltmeter, waveform monitor|—|
