dorn:/dɔːrn/ | n. | 刺, 棘; 钉子 | A sharp, pointed projection, especially on a plant.  | Example: The rose bush was covered in thorns.  这棵玫瑰丛上长满了刺。| 近义词：spine, prickle, spike | 反义词：smooth, soft | 名词


USAGE->

The fence is made of thorny bushes. = 这道栅栏是用带刺的灌木丛做的。
