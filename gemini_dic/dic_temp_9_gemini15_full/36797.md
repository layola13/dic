colombin:/ˈkɒləmbɪn/| n.|哥伦拜恩（一种植物）,  鸽子般的|A colombin is a plant, specifically a type of columbine (Aquilegia).  The name likely refers to the resemblance of the flower to a dove (columba in Latin).|例句：The garden was full of vibrant colombin flowers. 花园里盛开着鲜艳的哥伦拜恩花朵。The delicate colombin swayed gently in the breeze.  娇弱的哥伦拜恩在微风中轻轻摇曳。|近义词： columbine, aquilegia |反义词:  (无直接反义词)|名词
