quasi-unconscious: /ˈkwɑːziˌʌnˈkɒnʃəs/ | adj. | 准无意识的；似无意识的 |  Describing actions or states that are partially or seemingly unconscious, lacking full awareness but not entirely involuntary.  It suggests a state between full consciousness and complete unconsciousness, often involving automatic or habitual behaviors. | Example:  He drove home in a quasi-unconscious state after the accident. 他在事故后处于一种准无意识的状态下开车回家了。  The repetitive nature of the task allowed for a quasi-unconscious performance. 这项工作的重复性使得他能够以准无意识的方式完成。| 近义词: semi-conscious, subconscious, automatic, involuntary | 反义词: conscious, deliberate, intentional |
