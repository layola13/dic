legroom:/ˈlɛɡruːm/| n. |腿部空间；活动余地|The space for your legs, especially in a vehicle or seat.  It also refers to freedom of action or maneuverability.|例句：The legroom in the economy class was cramped. 经济舱的腿部空间很狭窄。


例句：There's not much legroom to maneuver in this small kitchen.在这个小厨房里，活动空间很小。


近义词：kneeroom, space, room


反义词：cramped space, confinement
