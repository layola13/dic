housewife:/ˈhaʊswaɪf/| n. |家庭主妇|A woman whose main job is taking care of her home and family.  Housewives traditionally manage the household, raise children, and perform domestic tasks. However, this role is increasingly diverse, with some housewives also pursuing careers or other activities.|Example:  She is a stay-at-home housewife who dedicates her life to her children and home. 她是一位全职家庭主妇，她把自己的生活奉献给了她的孩子和家庭。|近义词：homemaker|反义词：career woman
