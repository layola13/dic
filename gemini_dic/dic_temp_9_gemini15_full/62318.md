endeavored:/ɪnˈdevərd/ | v. |努力，试图 | To make an effort to do something.  |He endeavored to finish the project on time. 他努力按时完成项目。|attempt, strive, try |give up, fail |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They endeavor to please their customers. = 他们努力取悦顾客。
We endeavor to maintain high standards. = 我们努力保持高标准。

一般过去时:
She endeavored to explain the situation. = 她试图解释这种情况。
He endeavored to reach a compromise. = 他努力达成妥协。

现在完成时:
They have endeavored to help the victims. = 他们一直努力帮助受害者。
We have endeavored to improve our service. = 我们一直努力改进我们的服务。

过去完成时:
She had endeavored to warn him, but he didn't listen. = 她曾试图警告他，但他没听。
They had endeavored to find a solution, but failed. = 他们曾努力寻找解决方案，但失败了。

将来时:
We will endeavor to complete the task by Friday. = 我们将在周五前努力完成这项任务。
They will endeavor to attend the meeting. = 他们将努力参加会议。

虚拟语气:
If only I had endeavored more diligently. = 如果我当时更努力就好了。
I wish I had endeavored to understand him better. = 我希望我当时更努力地去理解他。

被动语态:
Every effort was endeavored to resolve the issue. = 已经尽了一切努力来解决这个问题。(较为不自然，很少用被动语态)
The project was endeavored to be completed on time. =  项目努力按时完成。(较为不自然，很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Did they endeavor to help? = 他们努力帮忙了吗？
Will you endeavor to understand? = 你会努力理解吗？


否定句:
He did not endeavor to hide his feelings. = 他并没有试图掩饰自己的感情。
They did not endeavor to contact us. = 他们没有努力联系我们。


PHRASE->
endeavor to do something = 努力做某事
make every endeavor = 尽一切努力
a noble endeavor = 高尚的事业
a worthwhile endeavor = 值得的努力
a valiant endeavor = 英勇的努力
