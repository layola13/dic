counter-battery:/ˈkaʊntərˈbætəri/| n. |反炮兵火力|Artillery fire directed at enemy artillery positions to suppress or destroy them.  It aims to neutralize the enemy's ability to fire back.|The counter-battery fire silenced the enemy's guns.  反炮兵火力压制了敌人的炮火。|近义词：counterfire, return fire|反义词：supporting fire

词源:
The word "counter-battery" is a compound word.  "Counter-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "in opposition to,"  and "battery" refers to a group of artillery pieces. Therefore, "counter-battery" literally means "against the battery" (of enemy artillery).
