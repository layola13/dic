glamourizes:/ˈɡlæməraɪzɪz/| vt.|使…具有魅力；美化；渲染；粉饰|To make something seem more attractive, exciting, or glamorous than it really is.|The film glamourizes the life of a spy, ignoring the dangers and moral compromises involved.  这部电影美化了间谍的生活，忽略了其中涉及的危险和道德妥协。|The magazine glamourizes unrealistic body images. 这本杂志美化了不切实际的体型形象。| idealizes, romanticizes, romanticizes| criticizes, exposes, denounces|动词
