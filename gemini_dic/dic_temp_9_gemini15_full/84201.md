half-reluctantly: /ˌhɑːf rɪˈlʌktəntli/ | adv. | 不情不愿地，勉强地 |  Describes an action or behavior performed with only partial willingness or enthusiasm, suggesting some hesitation or resistance. It implies a mixture of reluctance and acceptance. |例句：He half-reluctantly agreed to help with the project. 他不情不愿地同意帮忙做这个项目。

She half-reluctantly handed over the keys. 她勉强地交出了钥匙。| 近义词：grudgingly, unwillingly, hesitantly, reluctantly | 反义词：eagerly, willingly, enthusiastically | 词性：副词
