drowsing:/ˈdraʊzɪŋ/| v.|昏昏欲睡；打瞌睡|The act of becoming sleepy or dozing; feeling sleepy and lethargic.|例句：The warm sun induced a feeling of drowsing.  温暖的阳光让人昏昏欲睡。

例句：He was drowsing in front of the television. 他在电视机前打瞌睡。

近义词：dozing, slumbering, napping|反义词：awake, alert, vigilant|词性:动词
