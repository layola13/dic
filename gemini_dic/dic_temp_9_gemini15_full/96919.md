inexplorable: /ˌɪnɪkˈsplɔːrəbl/ | adj. | 不可探索的，无法探究的 | incapable of being explored or investigated; beyond the range of exploration.  | The deep ocean is largely unexplored and in many ways, inexplorable with current technology. 深海在很大程度上未被探索，并且在许多方面，凭借目前的科技是不可探索的。 |  unexplored, uncharted, impenetrable | explorable, accessible
