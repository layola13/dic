newsworthy: /ˈnjuːzˌwɜːrði/ | adj. | 值得报道的；有新闻价值的 |  Something that is newsworthy is sufficiently interesting or important to be reported in newspapers, on television, etc.  It often involves elements of timeliness, impact, proximity, prominence, conflict, and human interest. |


例句：

The earthquake was a highly newsworthy event.  = 这场地震是一件非常值得报道的事件。

The politician's scandalous affair was deemed newsworthy by many news outlets. = 这位政治家的丑闻被许多新闻媒体认为是值得报道的。


近义词：important, significant, noteworthy, interesting, sensational


反义词：insignificant, unimportant, trivial, dull


词性：形容词
