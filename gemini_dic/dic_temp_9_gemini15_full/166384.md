prerequisite:/ˌpriːˈrɛkwɪzɪt/| n. |先决条件，必备条件 | A prerequisite is something that is required before something else can happen or be done.  It refers to a condition that must be met before something else can occur.  |Example:  A college degree is a prerequisite for many jobs.  许多工作都需要大学文凭作为先决条件。


Example: Passing the entrance exam is a prerequisite for admission to the university. 通过入学考试是进入大学的先决条件。


近义词：requirement, necessity, precondition


反义词：optional, unnecessary

词性：名词
