clavigerous: /klæˈvɪdʒərəs/ | adj. | 持钥匙的；掌管钥匙的 |  Bearing a key; having the power or authority to unlock or access something.  The word evokes images of guardianship, power, and access to hidden or important things. |  The clavigerous angel guarded the gates of heaven.  = 持钥匙的天使守护着天堂之门。The museum curator, the clavigerous custodian of untold treasures, carefully unlocked the vault. = 作为无数宝藏的掌管者，博物馆馆长小心翼翼地打开了保险库。|近义词：key-bearing, key-holding | 反义词：keyless, powerless
