cartload:/ˈkɑːrtloʊd/|/ˈkɑːrtləʊd/| n. |一车之量；满满一车 |A cartload refers to the amount that can be carried in a cart.  It often implies a large quantity. |例句：We brought a cartload of firewood for the winter. 我们带了一车柴火过冬。

例句：The farmer harvested a cartload of apples.  农民收获了一车苹果。


近义词：load, quantity, amount

反义词：少量 (a small amount), handful


词性: 名词
