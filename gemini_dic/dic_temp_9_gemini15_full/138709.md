nullifies:/ˈnʌlɪfaɪz/| vt.|使无效；取消；抵消|To make something legally or officially ineffective or invalid.|The new law nullifies the previous agreement. 新法律使之前的协议无效。|近义词：invalidate, annul, negate, abolish|反义词：validate, confirm, ratify|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new policy nullifies the old regulations. = 新政策使旧规失效。
This contract nullifies any previous agreements. = 此合同使所有先前的协议无效。


一般过去时:
The court nullified the contract due to fraud. = 法院因欺诈行为而宣布合同无效。
The judge nullified the verdict because of a procedural error. = 由于程序错误，法官撤销了判决。


现在完成时:
The recent Supreme Court decision has nullified the state law. = 最近最高法院的裁决已使州法律无效。
The amendment has nullified the previous restrictions. = 修正案使先前的限制无效。


过去完成时:
They discovered that the previous agreement had nullified their current efforts. = 他们发现先前的协议使他们目前的努力无效。
The evidence presented had nullified the prosecution's case. = 出示的证据使检方的案情无效。


将来时:
The upcoming legislation will nullify the existing tax breaks. = 即将来临的立法将取消现有的税收优惠。
The new technology will nullify the need for manual labor. = 新技术将消除对体力劳动的需求。


虚拟语气:
If they signed the contract, it would nullify our agreement. = 如果他们签署了合同，那将使我们的协议无效。
I wish the judge had not nullified the original ruling. = 我真希望法官没有撤销最初的裁决。


被动语态:
The contract was nullified by the court. = 合同被法院宣布无效。
The agreement was nullified because of unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，协议被宣布无效。


疑问句:
Does this clause nullify the previous terms? = 这条款是否使之前的条款无效？
Will the new law nullify our existing permits? = 新法律会使我们现有的许可证无效吗？


否定句:
The amendment did not nullify the existing regulations. = 该修正案并没有使现有的规章失效。
The evidence presented did not nullify the defendant's alibi. = 出示的证据并没有推翻被告的不在场证明。
