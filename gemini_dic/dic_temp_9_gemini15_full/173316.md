quasi-daringly:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈdɛərɪŋli/| adv.|几乎大胆地；有点大胆地|The adverb "quasi-daringly" suggests an action or behavior that exhibits a degree of boldness or daring, but not to a fully audacious or reckless extent. It implies a tentative or hesitant approach to daring behavior.  It combines the prefix "quasi-" (meaning "seemingly," "apparently," or "to a certain extent") with the adverb "daringly."|例句：He quasi-daringly asked her for a dance. 他几乎大胆地邀请她跳舞。
She quasi-daringly challenged the established norms. 她有点大胆地挑战了既定的规范。|近义词：somewhat boldly, rather bravely, timidly daring|反义词：cautiously, timidly, fearfully|副词
