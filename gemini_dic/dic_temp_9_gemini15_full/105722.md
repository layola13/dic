kinsmanship: /ˈkɪnzʃɪp/ | n. | 血缘关系；亲属关系；家族情谊 | The state or quality of being kin; relationship by blood or marriage; family relationship.  |例句：The strong kinship between the two families was evident in their close cooperation.  两个家族之间牢固的亲属关系体现在他们密切的合作中。

例句：He felt a deep kinship with the other veterans, sharing their experiences of war. 他与其他退伍军人之间有着深厚的战友情谊，分享着他们共同的战争经历。


近义词：relationship, kindred, family ties, consanguinity | 反义词：estrangement, alienation
