resettles: /ˌriːˈsɛtəlz/ | vt. |重新安置；使重新定居 | To settle again; to establish or become established in a new place after having been moved or displaced.  |例句：The government is resettling refugees in different parts of the country. 政府正在将难民重新安置到该国的不同地区。

例句：After the flood, many families were resettled in temporary housing.  洪水过后，许多家庭被安置在临时住房里。


近义词：relocate, rehouse, reinstall


反义词：displace, evict, unsettle


词性：动词
