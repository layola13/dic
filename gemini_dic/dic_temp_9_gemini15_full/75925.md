funge:/fʌndʒ/| v.|真菌；菌类；|to grow or spread like a fungus; to thrive in a damp or dark environment.|例句：The fungus was spreading rapidly across the damp wood. 真菌迅速地在潮湿的木材上蔓延。|近义词：mushroom, mold, mildew|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fungus thrives in moist environments. = 真菌在潮湿的环境中茁壮成长。
The mold is growing on the bread. = 面包上长了霉。


一般过去时:
The fungus spread quickly after the rain. = 雨后真菌迅速蔓延。
The mildew appeared on the walls after the flood. = 洪水过后，墙壁上出现了霉菌。


现在完成时:
The fungus has already colonized the entire log. = 真菌已经侵占了整根原木。
The mold has spoiled the food. = 霉菌已经把食物弄坏了。


过去完成时:
The fungus had already destroyed the wood before we noticed it. = 我们注意到之前，真菌已经破坏了木材。
The mildew had spread throughout the house before the repairs began. = 在维修开始之前，霉菌已经蔓延到整个房子。


将来时:
The fungus will likely spread if the humidity remains high. = 如果湿度保持高的话，真菌可能会蔓延。
The mold will continue to grow unless we address the moisture problem. = 除非我们解决潮湿问题，否则霉菌会继续生长。


虚拟语气:
If the conditions were right, the fungus would grow rapidly. = 如果条件合适，真菌会迅速生长。
If we had cleaned the area sooner, the mold wouldn't have spread so much. = 如果我们早点清洁这个区域，霉菌就不会蔓延得这么厉害。


被动语态:
The wood was destroyed by the fungus. = 木材被真菌破坏了。
The bread was spoiled by the mold. = 面包被霉菌弄坏了。


疑问句:
Is this a poisonous fungus? = 这是毒菌吗？
Is the mold harmful to our health? = 霉菌对我们的健康有害吗？


否定句:

The mold was not visible at first. = 霉菌一开始是不可见的。
