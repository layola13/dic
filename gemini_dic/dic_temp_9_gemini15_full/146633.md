overhappy: /ˌoʊvərˈhæpi/ | adj. | 过分快乐的，欣喜若狂的 | Excessively happy;  delighted to an excessive degree.  This word implies a happiness that is perhaps too intense or inappropriate for the situation. | 例句：She was overhappy about the small promotion, almost giddy with excitement. = 她对那次小小的升职感到过分兴奋，几乎高兴得晕头转向。  He felt overhappy to the point of being unable to focus on his work. = 他高兴得无法集中精力工作。| 近义词：ecstatic, overjoyed, elated, exuberant | 反义词：unhappy, sad, dejected, glum |  形容词
