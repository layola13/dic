overinstructs:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌkts/| vt.|过度指导，教导过多|To instruct or teach excessively; to give more instruction than is necessary or helpful.|The teacher overinstructs her students, leaving them feeling overwhelmed and discouraged.  老师对学生指导过度，导致他们感到不知所措和气馁。|近义词：overteach, overload, inundate|反义词：underteach, neglect
