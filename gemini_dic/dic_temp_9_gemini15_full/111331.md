live up: /lɪv ʌp/ | v. | 达到；不辜负；履行；过着...样的生活 | To fulfill or match expectations; to live in a particular way. |例句：He lived up to his promise. 他遵守了他的诺言。|近义词：fulfill, meet, satisfy |反义词：fail, disappoint


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They live up to their responsibilities. = 他们履行他们的职责。
We live up to our ideals. = 我们实践我们的理想。

一般过去时:
She lived up to the challenge. = 她成功地应对挑战。
He lived up to his reputation as a hard worker. = 他不负他努力工作的声誉。

现在完成时:
The team has lived up to expectations this season. = 这个赛季球队不负众望。
The product has lived up to the hype. = 产品不负盛名。

过去完成时:
They had lived up to their agreement before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前履行了他们的协议。
She had lived up to her potential before the accident. = 事故发生前，她已经充分发挥了自己的潜能。


将来时:
I hope the movie will live up to the trailers. = 我希望电影不会辜负预告片。
We believe the new system will live up to our expectations. = 我们相信新系统将符合我们的预期。

虚拟语气:
If only he lived up to his potential. = 如果他能充分发挥自己的潜能就好了。
I wish the team would live up to the expectations. = 我希望球队能够达到预期。

被动语态:
The expectations were lived up to by the team. = 球队不负众望。
His promise was lived up to by him. = 他履行了他的诺言。

疑问句:
Does the restaurant live up to its reviews? = 这家餐厅是否名副其实？
Will this product live up to the claims? = 这个产品能达到宣传的效果吗？

否定句:
The film didn't live up to the hype. = 这部电影名不副实。
The performance didn't live up to expectations. = 表演没有达到预期。
