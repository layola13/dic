centrifugate:/ˈsɛntrfjuɡeɪt/| vt.|离心分离；使离心|To separate substances of different densities by rotation in a centrifuge.|例句：The blood sample was centrifuged to separate the plasma from the red blood cells.  血液样本被离心分离以分离血浆和红细胞。
例句：The technician centrifuged the solution to remove the precipitate. 技术人员离心分离溶液以去除沉淀物。|近义词：spin, whirl|反义词：无|动词
