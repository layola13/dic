haliographer:/ˌheɪliˈɒɡrəfər/| n. |海螺学家 |A haliographer is a scientist who studies marine gastropods, particularly abalones and other large sea snails.  Their research might involve taxonomy, ecology, physiology, or other aspects of these creatures. |例句：The haliographer presented her findings on the reproductive behavior of red abalone.  这位海螺学家介绍了她关于红鲍鱼繁殖行为的研究结果。|近义词：malacologist (更广义的软体动物学家) |反义词：无
