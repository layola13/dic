reexecution:/ˌriːˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃən/| n. |重新执行，再次执行|The act of executing something again; a second or subsequent execution.  It often implies a repetition of a process or task that was previously executed, possibly due to an error, a need for correction, or a change in requirements.|例句：The program requires reexecution after the database update.  程序需要在数据库更新后重新执行。

例句：Due to a system error, the transaction required reexecution. 由于系统错误，交易需要重新执行。


近义词：repetition, rerun, re-run, reiteration|反义词：completion, termination

词性：名词
