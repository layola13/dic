podophthalmic:/ˌpɒdɒpˈθælmɪk/| adj.| 有柄眼的；具柄眼的 |Having stalked eyes; pertaining to or characterized by stalked eyes.  The term refers to animals, typically crustaceans, that possess eyes on stalks.  |Example:  Many crustaceans, like crabs and shrimps, are podophthalmic.  许多甲壳类动物，例如螃蟹和虾，都是有柄眼的。|近义词：stalk-eyed,  ocular-stalked|反义词：sessile-eyed (eyes not on stalks)
