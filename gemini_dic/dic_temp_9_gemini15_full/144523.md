outvalue:/ˌaʊtˈvæljuː/| vt.|超过价值；胜过；比…更有价值 |To surpass in value or worth.|例句：The antique vase outvalues all the other items in the collection. 这件古董花瓶比收藏中的其他所有物品都更有价值。|例句：Her experience outvalues her lack of formal education. 她的经验弥补了她缺乏正规教育的不足。|近义词：exceed, surpass, outweigh|反义词：underestimate, undervalue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The antique vase outvalues all the other items. = 这件古董花瓶比其他所有物品都更有价值。
This strategy outvalues the previous one. = 这个策略比之前的策略更有价值。


一般过去时:
The painting outvalued the house. = 这幅画比房子更有价值。
His contribution outvalued mine. = 他的贡献比我的更有价值。


现在完成时:
The investment has outvalued its initial cost. = 这项投资的价值已经超过了最初的成本。
Her work has outvalued expectations. = 她的工作成果已经超出了预期。


过去完成时:
The land had outvalued the building before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，土地的价值已经超过了建筑物。
The company's assets had outvalued its liabilities. = 公司的资产价值已经超过了负债。


将来时:
This technology will outvalue its competitors. = 这项技术将胜过其竞争对手。
The property is expected to outvalue its current assessment. = 预计该房产的价值将超过目前的评估价。


虚拟语气:
If this project succeeded, it would outvalue all others. = 如果这个项目成功了，它将超过所有其他项目。
If the market improved, the stock would outvalue its current price. = 如果市场好转，这只股票的价值将超过当前价格。


被动语态:
The benefits were outvalued by the risks. = 好处被风险超过了。
The proposed plan was outvalued by the existing one. = 提出的计划的价值不如现有的计划。


疑问句:
Does this diamond outvalue that ruby? = 这颗钻石的价值比那颗红宝石高吗？
Does her experience outvalue his qualifications? = 她的经验比他的资历更有价值吗？


否定句:
The offer didn't outvalue the cost of production. = 报价没有超过生产成本。
The benefits didn't outvalue the risks involved. = 收益没有超过所冒的风险。


PHRASE->
outvalue significantly = 大大超过价值
outvalue in the long run = 从长远来看更有价值
outvalue by a considerable margin = 比…高出相当大的幅度
outvalue in terms of = 就…而言更有价值
outvalue the competition = 胜过竞争对手
