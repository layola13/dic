inefficient:/ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃənt/| adj. |低效的，无效率的 |Not working or producing as well as it could; wasteful.  The inefficient system caused delays. |近义词：unproductive, ineffective, wasteful |反义词：efficient, effective, productive


例句：

The inefficient heating system wasted a lot of energy.  低效的供暖系统浪费了大量的能源。

Her inefficient methods resulted in missed deadlines.  她低效的方法导致了错过截止日期。


词源:

The word "inefficient" is derived from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the word "efficient."  "Efficient" comes from the Late Latin word *efficiens, present participle of *efficere, meaning "to effect, accomplish."  This in turn is from *ex- ("out") + *facere ("to make, do").


用法说明：

inefficient主要用作形容词，修饰名词，描述人、物或系统效率低下。


PHRASE->

inefficient use of resources 资源利用效率低

inefficient worker 效率低的工人

inefficient management 低效的管理

inefficient system 低效的系统

inefficient process 低效的流程
