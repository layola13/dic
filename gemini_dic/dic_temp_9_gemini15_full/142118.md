oriflamme:/ˈɔːrɪflæm/| n. |军旗；战旗；象征|A oriflamme is a richly decorated flag or banner, historically associated with the French monarchy and often used as a rallying point in battle.  It symbolizes power, authority, and national pride.  The term often evokes a sense of romantic heroism and historical grandeur.|The king unfurled the oriflamme, inspiring his troops to fight bravely.  国王展开军旗，激励他的军队英勇作战。|banner, standard, ensign|peace flag, white flag|名词
