requests:/rɪˈkwɛsts/ | n. |请求，要求 |Requests are expressions of a wish or need. They can be formal or informal, and range from simple requests for information to more complex demands.  | Example:  The customer made several requests for changes to the design.  顾客对设计提出了几项修改要求。 |近义词：demands, pleas, applications |反义词：offerings, grants

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He requests a meeting with the manager. = 他请求与经理会面。
She requests more information about the product. = 她要求了解更多关于该产品的信息。

一般过去时:
They requested a refund for the damaged goods. = 他们要求退还损坏商品的款项。
He requested that the meeting be postponed. = 他请求推迟会议。

现在完成时:
She has requested a transfer to a different department. = 她已经要求调到不同的部门。
They have requested that we submit our reports by Friday. = 他们要求我们在周五之前提交报告。

过去完成时:
He had requested a leave of absence before the incident occurred. = 事故发生前，他已经请假了。
They had requested the documents several times before they finally received them. = 他们在最终收到文件之前已经多次索取了。

将来时:
She will request a raise next month. = 她下个月将要求加薪。
They will request assistance from the authorities. = 他们将向当局请求援助。

虚拟语气:
I request that you be present at the meeting. = 我要求你出席会议。(正式)
I wish they had requested my help sooner. = 我希望他们早点请求我的帮助。

被动语态:
The information was requested by the police. = 这些信息是警方要求提供的。
A meeting was requested by the employees. = 员工要求召开会议。

疑问句:
What did he request? = 他要求什么？
What are they requesting from us? = 他们向我们要求什么？

否定句:
He did not request any special treatment. = 他没有要求任何特殊待遇。
They did not request a change of venue. = 他们没有要求更改地点。


PHRASE->
make a request = 提出请求
at the request of = 应…的要求
respond to requests = 响应请求
fulfill requests = 满足要求
handle requests = 处理请求
