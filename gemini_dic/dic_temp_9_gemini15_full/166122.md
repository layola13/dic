prepractical: /ˌpriːˈpræktɪkəl/ | adj. | 预先实践的；实践前的；准备实践的 |  Describes something that is done or considered before a practical application or implementation, often involving preparation or planning. It emphasizes the preparatory stage before actual practice. |  例句：The prepractical training included simulations and theoretical studies. 预先实践的培训包括模拟和理论学习。

The prepractical research phase was crucial for the project's success. 预先实践的研究阶段对项目的成功至关重要。


近义词：preparatory, preliminary, antecedent, prior


反义词：practical (in the sense of already implemented), actual


词性：形容词
