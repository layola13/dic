pleuroperipneumony:/ˌplʊroʊˌpɛrɪpnuˈmoʊni/| n. |胸膜肺炎|Pleuroperipneumony is a serious lung infection that affects both the pleura (the lining of the lungs and chest cavity) and the lungs themselves.  It's characterized by inflammation of both the lungs and the pleura, often resulting in respiratory distress.  The term is a compound word combining elements related to the pleura and pneumonia.|The outbreak of pleuroperipneumony devastated the local cattle herd.|近义词：pleuropneumonia|反义词：无
