interferes:/ˌɪntərˈfɪrz/| v. |干涉；干扰；妨碍 |To come between so as to prevent, hinder, or disrupt.  It also implies unwelcome or unwanted involvement. |例句：The noise interferes with my concentration. 噪音干扰我的注意力。

例句：His constant interruptions interfered with the meeting's progress. 他不断的打断干扰了会议的进程。


近义词：interrupt, impede, obstruct, hinder, disrupt


反义词：aid, assist, facilitate, help


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music interferes with my work. = 音乐干扰我的工作。
His opinions interfere with the decision-making process. = 他的意见干扰了决策过程。


一般过去时:
The storm interfered with our travel plans. = 暴风雨打乱了我们的旅行计划。
His advice interfered with my original idea. = 他的建议干扰了我的最初想法。


现在完成时:
The constant rain has interfered with the harvest. = 持续的雨水已经影响了收成。
Technical problems have interfered with the broadcast. = 技术问题已经干扰了广播。


过去完成时:
A sudden power outage had interfered with the experiment. = 突然的停电已经干扰了实验。
The previous administration had interfered with the project’s development. = 前一届政府已经干预了该项目的开发。


将来时:
Bad weather will interfere with our flight. = 恶劣天气将会干扰我们的航班。
The strike will interfere with production. = 罢工将会干扰生产。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't interfered, the situation would have been better. = 如果他没有干涉，情况会更好。
I wish he hadn't interfered in our affairs. = 我真希望他没有干预我们的事情。


被动语态:
The project was interfered with by unexpected delays. = 该项目因意外延误而受阻。
The meeting was interfered with by a sudden power cut. = 会议因突然停电而被打扰。


疑问句:
Does the noise interfere with your sleep? = 噪音是否干扰你的睡眠？
Does his behavior interfere with the team's work? = 他的行为是否干扰团队的工作？


否定句:
The new system doesn't interfere with the old one. = 新系统不会干扰旧系统。
Nothing interfered with her plan. = 没有什么干扰她的计划。


PHRASE->
interfere with = 干扰…
interfere in = 干涉…
interfere in someone's affairs = 干涉别人的事情
interfere in a dispute = 干预纠纷
interfere with the progress = 妨碍进步
