fogy:/ˈfoʊɡi/ | /ˈfɒɡi/ | n. adj. | 老顽固；守旧者；陈旧的 |

英文解析:A fogy is a person who is old-fashioned and resistant to new ideas.  The word can be used as a noun to refer to such a person, or as an adjective to describe something that is old-fashioned or out of date.

例句:

He's such a fogy; he refuses to use a computer. = 他真是个老顽固；他拒绝使用电脑。

The company's methods are rather fogy for the modern age. = 这家公司的经营方式对于现代来说相当过时。


近义词:old-fashioned, reactionary, conservative, behind the times, antiquated

反义词:modern, progressive, forward-thinking, innovative


词性:Noun, Adjective
