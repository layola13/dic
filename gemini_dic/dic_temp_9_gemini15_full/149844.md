paleozoology:/ˌpeɪliəʊzuˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 古生物学（研究古代动物的学科）| The study of ancient animal life, including their evolution, distribution, and extinction. Paleozoology relies heavily on fossil evidence to reconstruct past ecosystems and understand the history of life on Earth.  |  例句：Paleozoology provides crucial insights into the evolution of vertebrates.  古生物学为脊椎动物的进化提供了重要的见解。


例句：The findings of this paleozoological study challenge previous theories about the extinction of dinosaurs.这项古生物学研究的结果挑战了之前关于恐龙灭绝的理论。

近义词：paleontology (更广义，包含植物)

反义词：暂无直接反义词，可考虑与研究现代动物的学科对比，如zoology
