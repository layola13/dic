introspected:/ˌɪntrəˈspɛktɪd/| v.|内省，反省 |To examine one's own thoughts or feelings.  |例句：After the argument, she introspected on her behavior and realized she'd been too harsh.  争吵过后，她反省了自己的行为，意识到自己太苛刻了。|例句：He introspected deeply about his life choices, leading him to a new path. 他深入反省了自己的人生选择，这让他走上了一条新的道路。|近义词：reflected, contemplated, examined, considered |反义词：ignored, overlooked |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He introspects regularly to maintain emotional balance. = 他定期进行内省以保持情绪平衡。
She introspects on her motivations before making decisions. = 她会在做决定之前反省自己的动机。


一般过去时:
I introspected after the failure, identifying my mistakes. = 我在失败后进行了反省，找到了自己的错误。
They introspected on their teamwork, finding areas for improvement. = 他们反省了团队合作，找到了改进的方面。


现在完成时:
He has introspected deeply this year, leading to personal growth. = 他今年进行了深刻的反省，从而促进了个人成长。
She has introspected many times about her future career. = 她多次反省过自己未来的职业生涯。


过去完成时:
I had introspected for weeks before making the final decision. = 在做出最终决定之前，我已经反省了好几个星期。
They had introspected about the project's failure before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经对项目的失败进行了反省。


将来时:
I will introspect on this experience to learn from it. = 我将对这次经历进行反省以从中吸取教训。
She will introspect more carefully before responding to criticism. = 她会在回应批评之前更仔细地进行反省。


虚拟语气:
If I had introspected more, I might have avoided that mistake. = 如果我当时多反省一些，我可能会避免那个错误。
I wish I had introspected more thoroughly before the presentation. = 我希望在演示之前我能更彻底地反省一下。


被动语态:
His behavior was introspected upon by the therapist. = 他的行为受到了治疗师的反省。
The team's performance was introspected, leading to changes in strategy. = 团队的表现受到了反省，导致战略上的改变。


疑问句:
Do you often introspect about your life? = 你经常反省自己的人生吗？

否定句:
