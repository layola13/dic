kaleidoscope's:/kəˈlaɪdəskoʊp/| n.|万花筒的，万花筒(复数所有格)|A kaleidoscope is an optical toy that produces changing patterns when you look through it.  The word refers to the rapidly changing patterns themselves, as well as the instrument that produces them.|例句：The kaleidoscope's vibrant colors captivated the child. 万花筒鲜艳的色彩吸引了孩子。  The kaleidoscope's images shifted and changed with every slight turn. 万花筒的图像随着每一次轻微的转动而变化。|近义词：none|反义词：none|名词
