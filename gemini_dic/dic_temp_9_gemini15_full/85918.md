headless:/ˈhedləs/ | adj. | 无头的；缺乏领导的；不合理的 | Lacking a head; lacking leadership or direction; illogical or absurd. |例句：The headless horseman galloped through the night.  无头骑士在夜间飞驰而过。  |例句：The company's headless management led to its downfall. 公司缺乏领导的管理导致了它的垮台。 |近义词：leaderless, directionless, illogical, irrational |反义词：headed, led, logical, rational
