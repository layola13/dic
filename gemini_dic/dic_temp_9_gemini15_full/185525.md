revalidating: /ˌriːˈvælɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 使重新生效；重新确认；重新验证 |  The act of making something valid again; confirming or establishing the validity of something once more.  It involves restoring something to its former validity or proving its continued validity. |

例句：

The company is revalidating its safety procedures after the recent accident. = 公司在最近的事故后正在重新验证其安全程序。

The professor is revalidating the research findings before publishing the paper. = 教授在发表论文之前正在重新验证研究结果。


近义词：recertify, reaffirm, reassert, corroborate

反义词：invalidate, discredit, disprove


词性：动词
