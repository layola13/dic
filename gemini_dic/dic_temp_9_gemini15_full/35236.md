clumping:/ˈklʌmpɪŋ/| adj. |成团的；丛生的；结块的|  Describes the action or state of forming into clumps or clusters.  It can refer to things growing together in a dense mass, or materials sticking together in lumps. |例句：The soil was clumping together, making it difficult to plant seeds.  土壤结成块状，很难播种。

The sheep were clumping together for warmth.  羊群聚在一起取暖。|近义词：clustering, bunching, grouping |反义词：scattered, dispersed, separated
