loadstones:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊnz/ | n. | 磁石，吸铁石 | A naturally magnetized piece of iron ore (magnetite), possessing the property of attracting iron.  Loadstones were historically significant for their navigational use in compasses. | Example: The ancient mariners relied on loadstones for navigation.  古代水手依靠磁石进行导航。 |近义词：magnet |反义词：无
