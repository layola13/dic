goosebumps:/ˈɡuːsbʌmps/| n. |鸡皮疙瘩；起鸡皮疙瘩的感觉|Small bumps that appear on a person's skin as a result of cold, fear, or excitement.  They are caused by the contraction of tiny muscles attached to the hair follicles.|The horror movie gave me goosebumps.|近义词：chills, shivers|反义词：smooth skin
