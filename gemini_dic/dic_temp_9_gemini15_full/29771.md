cattiest: /ˈkætɪst/ | adj. | 刻薄的；尖酸刻薄的 |  Characterized by or given to spiteful remarks; malicious gossip.  It describes behavior that is intentionally hurtful and unkind, often through subtle or indirect means. | 例句：Her cattiest comment about my dress made me feel really uncomfortable.  她对我衣服的尖酸刻薄的评论让我感到非常不舒服。

Her cattiest remarks were aimed at undermining her rival's confidence. 她刻薄的言论旨在破坏对手的信心。


近义词：malicious, spiteful, bitchy, mean-spirited, sarcastic, viperish | 反义词：kind, gentle, compassionate, amiable, friendly


词性：形容词
