comb:/koʊm/| n. v.|梳子；梳理|A comb is a tool with teeth used for untangling and styling hair.  It can also be used to remove debris from things like fur or wool.|例句：She used a comb to style her hair. 她用梳子梳理她的头发。

例句：He combed his beard carefully. 他仔细地梳理了他的胡须。|近义词：brush, rake|反义词：tangle, dishevel|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I comb my hair every morning. = 我每天早上都梳头。
She combs her daughter's hair before school. = 她每天早上上学前都给女儿梳头。


一般过去时:
He combed his hair before the interview. = 他面试前梳了头发。
They combed the beach for lost items. = 他们在海滩上搜寻丢失的物品。


现在完成时:
I have combed my hair. = 我已经梳过头发了。
She has combed the wool several times. = 她已经梳理过几次羊毛了。


过去完成时:
He had combed his hair before he went out. = 他出门前已经梳过头发了。
They had combed the forest for hours before finding the lost child. = 他们在森林里搜寻了好几个小时才找到丢失的孩子。


将来时:

She will comb the dog's fur to remove the mats. = 她会梳理狗狗的毛发以去除缠结。


虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
