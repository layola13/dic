financiers:/fɪˈnænʃərz/ | n. |  financiers |  financiers are people who manage or advise on large amounts of money, usually for organizations or wealthy individuals. They are involved in financial markets and investment strategies.  | Example:  The financiers were confident they could secure the necessary funding for the project.  金融家们相信他们能够为该项目获得必要的资金。

|  Example:  Several prominent financiers attended the economic summit.  几位著名的金融家出席了经济峰会。|  近义词: investors, bankers, fund managers, investment bankers | 反义词: debtor, bankrupt (These are not direct opposites, but represent the opposite financial standing.) | 名词
