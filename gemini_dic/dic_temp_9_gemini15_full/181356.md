reinvest:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛst/| vt.| 再投资，重新投资 |To invest again, especially profits or the proceeds of an investment.|例句：She decided to reinvest her profits in the business. 她决定将利润再投资到企业中。|例句：The company reinvested its earnings in new equipment.  公司将其收益再投资于新设备。|近义词：reinvest,  reapply|反义词：withdraw, spend|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinvest their profits every year. = 他们每年都将利润再投资。
We reinvest in our employees through training and development. = 我们通过培训和发展对员工进行再投资。

一般过去时:
He reinvested his bonus in stocks. = 他将奖金再投资于股票。
The company reinvested a significant portion of its revenue last quarter. = 上个季度，公司将其大部分收入进行了再投资。

现在完成时:
She has reinvested all her savings in real estate. = 她已将所有积蓄再投资于房地产。
They have reinvested their profits consistently for the past five years. = 过去五年，他们始终如一地将利润再投资。

过去完成时:
Before the expansion, they had reinvested all their earnings. = 在扩张之前，他们已经将所有收益都进行了再投资。
The business had reinvested its profits in research and development for several years. = 这家公司多年来一直将其利润再投资于研发。

将来时:
We will reinvest the surplus funds in new projects next year. = 我们明年将把剩余资金再投资于新的项目。
The government plans to reinvest tax revenue in infrastructure development. = 政府计划将税收收入再投资于基础设施建设。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would reinvest it in my business. = 如果我有更多钱，我会把它再投资到我的生意中。
It would be wise to reinvest the profits if the market conditions are favorable. = 如果市场条件有利，明智的做法是将利润再投资。

被动语态:
The profits were reinvested in the company's expansion. = 利润被再投资于公司的扩张。
The funds will be reinvested in renewable energy projects. = 这些资金将被再投资于可再生能源项目。

疑问句:
Will they reinvest their earnings this year? = 他们今年会将收益再投资吗？
Where will the company reinvest its profits? = 公司将把利润再投资到哪里？

否定句:
They did not reinvest their profits last year. = 他们去年没有将利润再投资。
The shareholders decided not to reinvest the dividends. = 股东决定不将股息再投资。
