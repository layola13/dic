overtake:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪk/| vt.|超过，赶上，突然袭击|To go past something or someone by being faster or moving ahead.|The bus overtook several cars on the highway. 公共汽车在高速公路上超过了几辆汽车。|近义词：pass, surpass, outstrip, outpace|反义词：be overtaken, lag behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overtakes me on the highway every morning. 他每天早上都在高速公路上超我。
The new product is quickly overtaking its competitors in the market. 新产品正在迅速超越其在市场上的竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She overtook the runner in the final lap. 她在最后一圈超过了那个跑步者。
The storm overtook the ship at sea. 暴风雨在海上袭击了那艘船。

现在完成时:
They have overtaken us in terms of technology. 他们在技术方面已经超过了我们。
The company has overtaken its rivals to become the market leader. 这家公司已经超越竞争对手成为市场领导者。

过去完成时:
The police had overtaken the criminals before they reached the border. 警方在罪犯到达边境之前就将他们抓获了。
By the time we arrived, he had already overtaken us in the race.  当我们到达时，他已经在比赛中超过我们了。

将来时:
We expect to overtake our sales target by the end of the year. 我们预计到年底将超过我们的销售目标。
She believes she will overtake him in the next few miles. 她相信在接下来的几英里内她会超过他。

虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have overtaken the leader. 如果他训练更刻苦，他也许就能超过领先者。
I wish I had overtaken that car earlier. 我真希望我早点超过那辆车。

被动语态:
We were overtaken by a sudden storm. 我们被突如其来的暴风雨袭击了。
He was overtaken by a feeling of sadness. 他被一种悲伤的情绪所笼罩。

疑问句:
Will you overtake that truck ahead of us? 你会超过我们前面的那辆卡车吗？
Did she overtake him in the race? 她在比赛中超过他了吗？

否定句:
He didn't overtake her despite his best efforts. 尽管他尽了最大努力，但他还是没有超过她。
The slower car couldn't overtake the faster one.  较慢的车无法超过较快的车。
