mistranscription: /ˌmɪsˌtrænsˈkrɪpʃən/ | n. | 误抄；误写；笔误 | The act of incorrectly transcribing something, whether written or spoken.  It involves errors in copying or recording information.  | Example:  The mistranscription of the address led to the package being sent to the wrong location.  误抄地址导致包裹寄到了错误的地方。 | 近义词：error, miscopying, misrepresentation | 反义词：accurate transcription, correct recording |
