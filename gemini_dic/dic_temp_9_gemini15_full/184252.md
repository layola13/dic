resolves:/rɪˈzɒlvz/ | vt. n. | 解决；决定；决心；溶解 | To find a solution to a problem; to firmly decide to do something; to separate into constituent parts |  例句：He resolved to quit smoking. 他下定决心戒烟。  The committee resolved the dispute. 委员会解决了争端。 | 近义词：settle, solve, determine, decide | 反义词：create, complicate, worsen | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resolves conflicts peacefully. = 他和平地解决冲突。
She resolves to study harder. = 她下定决心更加努力学习。


一般过去时:
They resolved the issue quickly. = 他们迅速解决了这个问题。
He resolved to travel the world. = 他决定环游世界。


现在完成时:
We have resolved most of the problems. = 我们已经解决了大部分问题。
She has resolved to be more patient. = 她已经下定决心变得更有耐心。


过去完成时:
They had resolved to leave before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经决定离开。
She had resolved the mystery by the end of the book. = 到书的结尾，她已经解开了这个谜团。


将来时:
He will resolve the matter tomorrow. = 他明天将解决此事。
They will resolve to donate to charity. = 他们将决定向慈善机构捐款。


虚拟语气:
If he resolved to try harder, he might succeed. = 如果他下定决心更加努力，他可能会成功。
I wish I had resolved the problem earlier. = 我希望我早点解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The problem was resolved quickly. = 这个问题很快得到了解决。
The matter will be resolved soon. = 这件事很快就会得到解决。


疑问句:
How will you resolve this conflict? = 你将如何解决这场冲突？
Has the committee resolved the issue yet? = 委员会已经解决了这个问题了吗？


否定句:
He didn't resolve to change his ways. = 他没有下定决心改变他的方式。
They couldn't resolve the differences between them. = 他们无法解决他们之间的分歧。


PHRASE->
resolve a conflict = 解决冲突
resolve a problem = 解决问题
resolve an issue = 解决一个问题
resolve a dispute = 解决争端
resolve to do something = 下定决心做某事
