meandering:/miˈændərɪŋ/| adj.|蜿蜒的；曲折的；漫无目的的|winding or rambling, following a winding course.  It can also describe something that is aimless or indirect.|The river meanders through the valley.  这条河蜿蜒穿过山谷。|The conversation meandered from one topic to another.  谈话漫无目的地从一个话题转到另一个话题。|winding, rambling, rambling, tortuous|straight, direct

虚拟语气:
