moorlands:/ˈmʊr.ləndz/| n.|荒野；荒地；沼泽地|Open, uncultivated upland areas characterized by rough, often boggy terrain, typically covered with heather and other low-lying vegetation.|例句：The sheep grazed peacefully on the vast moorlands.  绵羊在广阔的荒野上平静地吃草。
例句：We hiked across the rugged moorlands, enjoying the breathtaking views. 我们徒步穿越崎岖的荒野，欣赏着令人叹为观止的景色。|近义词：heathland, wasteland, moor, bog|反义词：cultivated land, farmland
