dull-wittednesses:/dʌlˈwɪtɪdnɪsəz/| n.|愚笨的行为；愚蠢的表现|The dull-wittednesses of the council led to a series of costly mistakes.|近义词：foolishness, stupidity, ineptitude|反义词：wisdom, intelligence, brilliance


英文解析:  Dull-wittednesses refers to instances or acts demonstrating a lack of intelligence, understanding, or common sense. It highlights multiple occurrences of such behavior.  The word combines "dull-witted," meaning slow-witted or unintelligent, with the plural noun suffix "-nesses," indicating multiple instances of a quality or state.


例句:

The politician's dull-wittednesses in handling the crisis cost him his job. = 这位政治家在处理危机中的愚笨行为让他丢了工作。

His repeated dull-wittednesses during the negotiations damaged the team's credibility. = 他在谈判中反复出现的愚蠢行为损害了团队的信誉。
