immobilised: /ɪˈməʊbaɪld/ | adj. | 固定不動的；靜止的；癱瘓的 | rendered immobile; unable to move.  | The accident left him immobilised.  事故使他动弹不得。 The police immobilised the suspect's car. 警察使嫌疑人的汽车无法移动。|  paralysed, fixed, stationary | mobile, active, moving
