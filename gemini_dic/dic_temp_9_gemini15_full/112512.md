lout:/laʊt/| n.|笨蛋，粗鲁的人|A lout is an uncouth and ill-mannered person.  They are often clumsy and insensitive. |例句：He's such a lout; he spilled his drink all over me. 他真是个笨蛋，他把饮料都洒在我身上了。|近义词：oaf, boor, clod, bumpkin|反义词：gentleman, lady, refined person|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a lout. = 他是个粗鲁的人。
That man acts like a lout. = 那个人举止粗鲁。


一般过去时:
He was a lout in his youth. = 他年轻时是个粗鲁的人。
They behaved like louts at the party. = 他们在派对上行为粗鲁。

She has acted like a lout more than once. = 她不止一次表现得粗鲁无礼。
