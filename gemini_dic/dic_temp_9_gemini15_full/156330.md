physicians:/fɪˈzɪʃənz/| n. |医生们，内科医生们|Physicians are medical doctors who have completed medical school and residency training.  They provide diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of medical conditions.|例句：Many physicians work long hours to care for their patients. 许多医生为了照顾病人而长时间工作。|例句：The physicians at the hospital are highly skilled and experienced. 医院里的医生技术精湛，经验丰富。|近义词：doctors, medical practitioners, healers|反义词：patients
