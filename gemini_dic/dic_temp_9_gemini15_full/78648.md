giddied:/ˈɡɪdid/| adj.|头晕目眩的，兴奋的|Feeling dizzy or lightheaded, often from excitement or happiness.|例句：The roller coaster left me feeling giddied. 过山车让我感到头晕目眩。|例句：She was giddied by the news of her promotion. 她听到晋升的消息后欣喜若狂。|近义词：dizzy, lightheaded, elated, thrilled|反义词：calm, composed, grounded
