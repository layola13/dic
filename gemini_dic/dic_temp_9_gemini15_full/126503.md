myxophyta:/ˌmɪksəˈfaɪtə/| n. |粘菌门|Slime molds; a group of eukaryotic organisms that were once classified as fungi but are now considered to be a separate kingdom. They are characterized by their amoeboid or plasmodial stages and their ability to produce spores. |例句：Myxophyta are fascinating organisms that exhibit unique life cycles. 粘菌是具有独特生命周期的迷人生物。|近义词：Mycetozoa (synonym)|反义词:N/A
