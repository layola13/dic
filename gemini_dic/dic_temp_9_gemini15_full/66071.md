excalibur:/ˌɛksˈkælibər/| n. |亚瑟王的剑，王者之剑 |The legendary sword of King Arthur, often associated with power, justice, and destiny.  It is said to have been given to Arthur by the Lady of the Lake and possesses magical properties.  |例句：The legend of Excalibur, the magic sword of King Arthur, is well-known worldwide.  亚瑟王的神剑——王者之剑——的传说在全世界都很有名。

例句：Many believed that only the rightful king could wield Excalibur. 许多人相信只有真正的国王才能挥舞王者之剑。
