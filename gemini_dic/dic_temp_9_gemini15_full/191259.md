scatteredly:/ˈskætərdli/| adv.|零星地；分散地；散乱地 |  Describes something that is spread out in a haphazard or irregular manner, not concentrated in one place.  It implies a lack of order or pattern. |例句：The toys were scatterededly strewn across the floor.  玩具零散地散落在地板上。

The flowers were scatterededly planted in the garden. 花儿零星地种在花园里。|近义词：sparsely, randomly, haphazardly|反义词：concentratedly, systematically, uniformly
