parsimonious:/ˌpɑːrsɪˈmoʊniəs/ | adj. | 节俭的；吝啬的 | Extremely unwilling to spend money; frugal to an excessive degree.  The adjective implies an unwillingness to spend money even when it's needed or appropriate, suggesting stinginess rather than simple thrift. | He was so parsimonious that he refused to buy his children new shoes, even though their old ones were falling apart.  | frugal, economical, thrifty, miserly | extravagant, lavish, generous, prodigal | 形容词
