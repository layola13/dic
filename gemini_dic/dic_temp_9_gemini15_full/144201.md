outsighing: /ˌaʊtˈsaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 超越；胜过；眺望 | To surpass or exceed; to have a better view than.  |  The mountain's height outsighed all others in the range.  这座山的高度超过了山脉中其他所有山峰。 | surpass, exceed, excel | be surpassed by, be inferior to | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The panoramic view outsighs the one from the valley. = 全景视野胜过山谷中的视野。
His performance outsighs all his competitors. = 他的表现超越了所有竞争对手。


一般过去时:
The castle outsighed all other buildings in the town. = 这座城堡比镇上其他所有建筑物都高。
Her intelligence outsighed all her peers. = 她的智力超过了她所有的同龄人。


现在完成时:
The new skyscraper has outsighed all previous structures in the city. = 这座新的摩天大楼已经超过了城市中所有以前的建筑物。
His contribution has outsighed everyone else's on the project. = 他对项目的贡献已经超过了其他所有人。


过去完成时:
By the time the new bridge was built, it had outsighed the old one significantly. = 新桥建成时，它已经大大超过了旧桥。
Before the invention, his ideas had outsighed his contemporaries' thinking. = 在这项发明之前，他的想法已经超越了他同时代人的思想。


将来时:
The upcoming satellite launch will outsigh all previous attempts in terms of distance. = 即将进行的卫星发射将在距离方面超越所有之前的尝试。
With this new technology, we expect our production to outsigh our competitors'. = 凭借这项新技术，我们预计我们的产量将超过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If his efforts were to outsigh everyone else's, he would win the competition. = 如果他的努力超过了其他所有人，他就会赢得比赛。
I wish my knowledge outsighed the complexity of this problem. = 我希望我的知识能够超过这个问题的复杂性。


被动语态:
The old record was outsighed by the new one. = 旧纪录被新纪录超越了。
Their efforts were outsighed by the unexpected challenges. = 他们的努力被意想不到的挑战所超越。


疑问句:
Does the view from this mountain outsigh the view from that one? = 从这座山看到的景色是否胜过从那座山看到的景色？
Will this new design outsigh the existing models? = 这种新的设计会超越现有的型号吗？


否定句:
His performance did not outsigh his rival's. = 他的表现没有超过他的对手。
The small company's market share did not outsigh its larger competitors'. = 这家小公司的市场份额没有超过它更大的竞争对手。



PHRASE->

outsigh the expectations = 超越预期
