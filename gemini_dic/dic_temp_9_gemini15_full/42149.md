couple:/ˈkʌpəl/| n.|一对，夫妇，少量|A couple refers to two things of the same kind considered together; a pair; two people married to each other.  It can also mean a small number, approximately two.|例句：There are a couple of apples left. 还剩下几个苹果。|例句：The happy couple are planning their honeymoon. 这对幸福的夫妇正在计划他们的蜜月。|近义词：pair, duo, few|反义词：many, numerous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A couple of birds are singing. = 几只鸟在唱歌。
We need a couple of days to finish the project. = 我们需要几天时间来完成这个项目。

一般过去时:
A couple of students left early. = 有几个学生早退了。
They saw a couple of movies last week. = 他们上周看了几部电影。

现在完成时:
I've met a couple of interesting people. = 我遇到了一些有趣的人。
We've had a couple of problems with the car. = 我们这辆车出现过一些问题。

过去完成时:
They had already seen a couple of films before they went to the cinema. = 在他们去电影院之前，他们已经看过几部电影了。
She had finished a couple of chapters before bed. = 她在睡觉前已经完成了几个章节。

将来时:
We will need a couple of hours to get there. = 我们需要几个小时才能到达那里。
I'll have a couple of beers after work. = 我下班后会喝几瓶啤酒。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
A couple of suggestions were made at the meeting. = 会议上提出了一些建议。
A couple of books were borrowed from the library. = 图书馆借出了一些书。

疑问句:

否定句:
