RSVP's:/ˌɑːrsviːˈpiːz/| n. |请回复；回复名单|The abbreviation of "Répondez s'il vous plaît," a French phrase meaning "Please reply."  It's used on invitations to request a response from the recipient indicating whether or not they will attend.|例句：The RSVP's for the wedding are due by next week.  婚礼的回复截止日期是下周。

例句：We received a lot of RSVPs for the party. 我们收到了很多参加派对的回复。

近义词：replies, responses, confirmations
反义词：无
词性:名词
