improvvisatore:/ˌɪmprɒvɪzəˈtɔːreɪ/ | n. |即兴表演者；即兴创作艺术家 | A person who improvises, especially in music or drama.  An improvvisatore is skilled in creating and performing spontaneously, without prior preparation. They often rely on wit, creativity, and quick thinking to deliver engaging performances. |Example: The improvvisatore captivated the audience with his witty and spontaneous storytelling.  即兴表演者以其诙谐而即兴的讲故事技巧迷住了观众。|近义词：improviser, ad-libber |反义词：pre-planner, scriptwriter
