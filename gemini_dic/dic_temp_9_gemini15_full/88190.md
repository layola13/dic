This word is a proper noun and doesn't have typical verb conjugations like "what".  The usage is limited to referring to the Himalayan mountain range.  Grammatical structures involving "Himalayas" would be those typical of proper nouns.

Etymology:


The name "Himalaya" is derived from the Sanskrit word हिमालय (Himālaya), which is a compound word.  "hima" (हिम) means "snow" and "ālaya" (आलय) means "abode" or "dwelling". Therefore, Himalaya literally translates to "abode of snow".
