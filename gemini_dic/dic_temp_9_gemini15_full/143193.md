outcomplimented: /ˌaʊtˈkɒmplɪmɛntɪd/ | v. | 奉承过度；过分赞美 |  To compliment excessively; to surpass in compliments.  It implies that the compliments were excessive or insincere, going beyond what is appropriate or deserved. |


例句：

He felt outcomplimented by the effusive praise from his boss; it made him uncomfortable. = 他被老板过度的赞美弄得浑身不自在；这让他感到不舒服。

She was outcomplimented at the awards ceremony, receiving more accolades than she felt she deserved. = 在颁奖典礼上，她受到了过多的赞美，收到的赞誉比她自认为应得的还要多。


近义词：overpraise, flatter excessively, shower with excessive praise

反义词：underestimate, criticize, belittle

词性：动词
