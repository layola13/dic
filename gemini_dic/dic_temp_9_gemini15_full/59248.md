e-number:/ˈiːˌnʌmbər/| n. |食品添加剂编号 |An E number is a code used to identify food additives that are permitted for use within the European Union.  Each number refers to a specific substance, and the system is designed to ensure consistency and clarity in food labeling across the EU.  The "E" prefix indicates that the additive is approved under European regulations. |Example:  This yogurt contains E330 (citric acid) as a preservative.  这个酸奶含有E330（柠檬酸）作为防腐剂。|近义词：food additive code, additive identifier|反义词：无
