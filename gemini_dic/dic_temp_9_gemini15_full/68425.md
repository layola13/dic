falsifies:/ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪz/ | vt. | 证明为假；伪造；歪曲 | To prove (a statement or theory) to be false; to forge or counterfeit (a document, signature, etc.); to distort or misrepresent the truth. |例句：The evidence falsifies his alibi.  证据证明他的不在场证明是假的。

The scientist's experiment falsifies the existing theory. 科学家的实验证明了现有的理论是错误的。


近义词：disprove, refute, counterfeit, fabricate | 反义词：verify, confirm, corroborate | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He falsifies the data to support his claim. 他伪造数据以支持他的说法。
The government falsifies its economic reports. 政府伪造其经济报告。

一般过去时:
They falsified the documents to commit fraud. 他们伪造文件以进行诈骗。
She falsified her age on the application. 她在申请表上伪造了年龄。

现在完成时:
He has falsified evidence in multiple cases. 他在多个案件中伪造了证据。
The company has falsified its financial records for years. 这家公司多年来一直在伪造财务记录。

过去完成时:
They had falsified the results before the presentation. 他们在演示之前就已经伪造了结果。
The artist had falsified the painting's origin.  这位艺术家伪造了这幅画的来源。

将来时:
He will falsify the test results if caught cheating. 如果被抓到作弊，他会伪造考试结果。
The journalist fears they will falsify the story.  记者担心他们会歪曲事实。

虚拟语气:
If he falsified the documents, he would be in serious trouble. 如果他伪造文件，他会遇到大麻烦。
I wish he hadn't falsified the data. 我真希望他没有伪造数据。

被动语态:
The documents were falsified by a skilled forger. 文件是由一个熟练的伪造者伪造的。
The data was falsified to mislead the public. 数据被伪造以误导公众。

疑问句:
Did he falsify the report? 他是否伪造了报告？
Were the signatures falsified? 签名是伪造的吗？

否定句:
He did not falsify the evidence. 他没有伪造证据。
The witness did not falsify her testimony.  证人没有歪曲她的证词。

词源:
From Old French *falsifier, from Latin falsus ("false") + -ficare ("to make").  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *dʰel- ("to deceive").
