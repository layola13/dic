closure:/ˈkloʊʒər/| n.|结束；结论；闭合；了结；终结|The feeling of having dealt successfully with a problem or task.|The final act of a play provides closure. 这出戏的最后一幕提供了结局。  She found closure after talking to her brother.  她和哥哥谈话后释然了。|conclusion, resolution, settlement|openness, incompletion|名词
