nontenurially: /ˌnɒnˈtɛnjuəriəli/ | adv. | 非终身职位地；非终身任职地 |  Describes something done or existing without the security of tenure;  not having tenure.  It typically refers to employment or positions that are not permanent or guaranteed. | 例句：He worked nontenurially at the university for many years before finally securing a tenured position. 他在大学里非终身任职多年，最终才获得终身职位。

The lecturer held a nontenurially appointment.  讲师的职位是非终身制的。


近义词：temporarily, provisionally, contractually


反义词：tenured, permanently,  indefinitely
