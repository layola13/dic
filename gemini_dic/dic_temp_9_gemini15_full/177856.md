receptiveness:/rɪˈsɛptɪvnəs/ | n. |接受能力；理解力；敏感性 |The state or quality of being receptive; willingness to receive or accept something.  It often implies an openness to new ideas, experiences, or suggestions. |例句：The teacher praised the students for their receptiveness to new learning methods.  老师称赞学生们对新的学习方法的接受能力。

例句：Her receptiveness to criticism helped her grow professionally. 她对批评的接受能力帮助她在职业上取得了进步。


近义词：openness, responsiveness, susceptibility, willingness, acceptance

反义词：resistance, rejection, closed-mindedness, insensitivity, unresponsiveness
