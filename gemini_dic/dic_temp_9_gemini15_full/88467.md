histologists:/ˌhɪˈstɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |组织学家 |Scientists who specialize in the study of tissues. Histologists examine tissue samples under a microscope to diagnose diseases, conduct research, and contribute to our understanding of biological processes.  |Example: Histologists played a crucial role in identifying the cancerous cells. 组织学家在识别癌细胞方面发挥了至关重要的作用。|近义词: tissue specialists, cytopathologists (if focusing on cells)|反义词: None.
