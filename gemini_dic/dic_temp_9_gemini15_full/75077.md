frigidnesses:/ˈfrɪdʒɪdnɪsɪz/| n.|严寒；冷漠；冷淡|The plural form of "frigidness," referring to multiple instances or aspects of coldness, either in temperature or in demeanor.  It emphasizes a repeated or extensive lack of warmth or emotion.|例句：The frigidnesses of the Arctic winter were almost unbearable.  北极冬季的严寒几乎让人难以忍受。The frigidnesses of her personality kept people at a distance. 她性格的冷漠使人们与她保持距离。|近义词：coldnesses, chills, severities, impassiveness|反义词：warmths, genialities, cordialities|名词
