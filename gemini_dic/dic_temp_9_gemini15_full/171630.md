pulpitically: /pʌlˈpɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 教堂讲坛般地；以说教的语气 | In a manner characteristic of a sermon delivered from a pulpit; with a pompous or preachy tone. | 例句：He lectured us pulpitically on the evils of gambling. 他以说教的语气教训我们赌博的恶习。


He spoke pulpitically about the importance of honesty. 他以教堂讲坛般的语气谈论诚实的重要性。


近义词：preachily, sermonizingly, dogmatically, sanctimoniously | 反义词：casually, informally, colloquially

词源：源自单词"pulpit" (讲坛)，加上后缀"-ically" (表示“以…的方式”)，表示“像在讲坛上一样”。  Pulpit itself comes from the Middle English *pulpet*, from Old French *pulpitre*, ultimately from Latin * pulpitum*, a raised platform.
