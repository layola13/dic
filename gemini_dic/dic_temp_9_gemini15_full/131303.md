noncertainty:/ˌnɒnsɜːtnti/| n. |不确定性，不确定的事物 |The lack of certainty; the state of being uncertain.  It refers to a situation or condition where the outcome or result is unknown or unpredictable. |例句：The noncertainty of the future weighed heavily on her mind. 未来充满了不确定性，这让她感到沉重。

例句：There is a great deal of noncertainty surrounding the company's future plans. 公司未来的计划有很多不确定性。


近义词：uncertainty, doubt, ambiguity, precariousness

反义词：certainty, sureness, definiteness
