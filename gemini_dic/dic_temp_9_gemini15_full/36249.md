cognomen:/ˈkɒɡnəʊmɛn/ /ˈkɑːɡnəʊmɛn/| n. |绰号；姓氏；家族名|A cognomen is a surname or family name, often a descriptive nickname that has become hereditary. It is distinct from a nomen, which is the Roman equivalent of a clan name.  |例句：His cognomen, "the Great," reflected his military achievements. 他的绰号“大帝”反映了他的军事成就。

例句：The cognomen Smith is derived from the occupation of a blacksmith.  史密斯这个姓氏源于铁匠的职业。


近义词：surname, family name, nickname, appellation|反义词：given name, forename
