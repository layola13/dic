receipting:/rɪˈsiːptɪŋ/| v.| 收据签收；收据开具；收款；收取|The act of issuing or receiving receipts.  It can refer to the process of giving or getting a receipt for payment, goods received, or services rendered. |例句：The company has a strict receipting policy for all expenses.  这家公司对所有开支都有严格的收据签收政策。

例句：The cashier is busy receipting the payments. 收银员正忙着收款。


近义词：acknowledging, recording, collecting, receiving

反义词：disbursing, paying out
