lodestars:/ˈloʊdˌstɑːrz/| n. | 指路星，向导，目标 | A lodestar is a guiding star, something that guides or inspires.  It can be a literal star used for navigation, or a metaphorical guide in life.  | Example:  The North Star served as a lodestar for early sailors. 北极星是早期水手们的指路星。

The company's mission statement acts as a lodestar for its employees. 公司的使命宣言是员工的指路明灯。 | 近义词：guide, beacon, inspiration, model | 反义词：misdirection, distraction, impediment
