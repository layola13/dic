improvided:/ɪmˈpraʊvaɪdɪd/| adj.|  缺乏准备的；临时的；即兴的 | unprepared; improvised; lacking foresight or planning.  |例句：The impromtu speech was surprisingly impassioned and well-received.  即兴演讲出乎意料地热情洋溢，并受到了好评。

例句：Their imporovided shelter offered little protection from the storm. 他们临时搭建的住所几乎无法抵御暴风雨。


近义词：unprepared, impromptu, extemporaneous, improvised, makeshift, ad-hoc


反义词：prepared, planned, deliberate, premeditated


词源：来自前缀"im-"(表示否定) + "provided"(已准备好的)，表示"没有准备的"。  The word ultimately derives from the verb "provide," meaning to supply or equip.
