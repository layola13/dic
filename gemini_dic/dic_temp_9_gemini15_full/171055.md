pseudovum:/ˈsuːdoʊvʌm/ | n. |假卵，伪卵 |A pseudovum is a structure that resembles an ovum (egg) but is not a functional egg cell. It may be formed by pathological processes or as a result of developmental abnormalities.  Pseudovums are typically non-viable and cannot develop into an embryo. |Example: The researchers identified several pseudovums in the ovarian tissue sample.  研究人员在卵巢组织样本中发现了几个假卵。|近义词:false ovum, abnormal ovum |反义词: ovum, true egg
