jaywalking:/ˈdʒeɪˌwɔːkɪŋ/| n.|乱穿马路，违规穿越马路|The act of crossing a street or road at a place other than a designated crosswalk or pedestrian crossing,  often against traffic signals.|例句：Jaywalking is a common offense in many cities.  许多城市乱穿马路是一种常见违法行为。


例句：He received a ticket for jaywalking. 他因乱穿马路而收到罚单。


近义词：illegal crossing, crossing against the light, disregarding traffic signals


反义词：using a crosswalk, obeying traffic signals


词性：名词
