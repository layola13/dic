coracle:/ˈkɒrəkəl/| n.|轻便小舟，独木舟|A coracle is a small, lightweight boat, traditionally made of a wicker frame covered with animal skin or waterproof canvas.  It is typically used on rivers and lakes.|例句：The fisherman steered his coracle expertly through the rapids.  渔夫熟练地驾驶着他的独木舟穿过急流。
The ancient Britons used coracles to travel across the rivers. 古代不列颠人用独木舟在河上旅行。|近义词：boat, canoe, raft|反义词：large ship, ocean liner
