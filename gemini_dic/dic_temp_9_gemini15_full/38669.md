condemnably:/kənˈdɛmnəbli/| adj. |应受谴责的；该受责备的 |Deserving condemnation; blameworthy.  The actions of the corporation were condemnably irresponsible, leading to widespread environmental damage.  |例句：His behavior was condemnably selfish. 他的行为是应受谴责的自私行为。  His reckless driving was condemnably dangerous. 他的鲁莽驾驶是应受谴责的危险行为。|近义词：blameworthy, reprehensible, culpable, censurable |反义词：laudable, commendable, praiseworthy
