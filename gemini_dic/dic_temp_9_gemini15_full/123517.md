monotonic:/məˈnɒtənɪk/|/ˌmɑːnəˈtɑːnɪk/| adj. | 单调的，无变化的 | Lacking variety or interest; monotonous.  The word implies a consistent, unchanging pattern, often in a negative context suggesting boredom or lack of stimulation. | Example: The monotonic rhythm of the machine was hypnotic.  单调的机器节奏催眠了人。

The monotonic drone of the speaker's voice put the audience to sleep.  演讲者单调的嗡嗡声让听众睡着了。


近义词: monotonous, uniform, unchanging, unvaried, tedious, dull

反义词: varied, diverse, dynamic, exciting, interesting, stimulating

词性: adjective
