peace:/piːs/| n.|和平；平静；安宁；和解|  a state of tranquility or quiet; freedom from disturbance; agreement between people or groups; a period of time without war|例句：The peace treaty ended the war. 和平条约结束了战争。  The birds sang in peaceful harmony. 鸟儿们在平静的和谐中歌唱。|近义词：tranquility, calm, harmony, serenity |反义词：war, conflict, chaos, turmoil


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The world needs peace. = 世界需要和平。
She enjoys the peace of the countryside. = 她享受乡村的宁静。


一般过去时:
The two countries signed a peace agreement. = 这两个国家签署了一项和平协议。
A deep peace settled over the land. = 一种深沉的平静笼罩着这片土地。


现在完成时:
We have achieved peace after years of fighting. = 在多年的战斗之后，我们实现了和平。
They have lived in peace for many years. = 他们和平地生活了很多年。


过去完成时:
After the storm, a profound peace had fallen. = 暴风雨过后，一种深沉的平静降临了。
Before the agreement, there had been no peace. = 在协议达成之前，没有和平。


将来时:
We hope to see lasting peace in the region. = 我们希望在该地区看到持久的和平。
They believe peace will come soon. = 他们相信和平很快就会到来。


虚拟语气:
If only there were peace in the world! = 但愿世界上有和平！
I wish peace would prevail. = 我希望和平能够占上风。


被动语态:
Peace was declared after many years of war. = 多年战争后，宣布了和平。
Peace is often longed for but rarely achieved. = 和平经常被渴望，但很少实现。


疑问句:
Is there peace in the region? = 该地区有和平吗？
Can we achieve lasting peace? = 我们能实现持久的和平吗？


否定句:
There is no peace in that country. = 那个国家没有和平。
They did not achieve peace through negotiation. = 他们没有通过谈判实现和平。
