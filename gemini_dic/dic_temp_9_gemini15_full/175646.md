ransacking:/ˈrænsækɪŋ/| v.|洗劫；彻底搜查|The act of searching a place thoroughly and often violently, typically to steal something.  It implies a chaotic and destructive search.|例句：The thieves ransacked the house, leaving everything in disarray.  窃贼洗劫了这所房子，把一切都弄得乱七八糟。|例句：Police ransacked the suspect's apartment looking for evidence.  警方彻底搜查了嫌疑人的公寓以寻找证据。|近义词：plunder, loot, pillage, search thoroughly|反义词：organize, tidy
