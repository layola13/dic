milking:/ˈmɪlkɪŋ/| n. v.|挤奶；利用；榨取|The act of extracting milk from a mammal, typically a cow.  It can also refer to the act of exploiting or taking unfair advantage of a situation or person.|例句：The farmer is milking the cows. 这位农民正在挤牛奶。 The company is milking its customers for all they're worth. 这家公司正在竭尽全力榨取顾客的钱财。|近义词：extraction, exploitation, squeezing|反义词：giving, supporting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He milks the cows every morning. = 他每天早上都挤牛奶。
She milks the situation for all it's worth. = 她充分利用了这个情况。

一般过去时:
The farmer milked the cows yesterday. = 农民昨天挤了牛奶。
They milked the idea until it was dry. = 他们把这个主意利用到枯竭。

现在完成时:
I have milked three cows this morning. = 我今天早上已经挤了三头奶牛的奶。
They have milked the system for years. = 他们多年来一直在利用这个制度。

过去完成时:
He had milked all the cows before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前，他已经挤完了所有奶牛的奶。
She had milked the opportunity for everything it was worth.  = 她已经充分利用了这个机会。

将来时:
We will milk the goats tomorrow. = 我们明天将挤山羊奶。
They will milk this scandal for all it's worth. = 他们将充分利用这场丑闻。


虚拟语气:
If I had a cow, I would milk it every day. = 如果我有一头奶牛，我每天都会挤奶。
If I were you, I wouldn't milk that situation. = 如果我是你，我不会利用那种情况。

被动语态:
The cows are milked twice a day. = 奶牛每天挤两次奶。
The resources are being milked by the company. = 资源正在被公司榨取。

疑问句:
Do you milk your own cows? = 你自己挤牛奶吗？
Are they milking the situation for publicity? = 他们是在利用这种情况来做宣传吗？

否定句:
He doesn't milk cows; he raises chickens. = 他不挤牛奶，他养鸡。
They didn't milk the opportunity. = 他们没有利用这个机会。
