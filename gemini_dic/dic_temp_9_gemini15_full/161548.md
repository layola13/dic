potoroo:/ˌpɒtəˈruː/| n. |小型袋鼠|A small kangaroo, usually with a short, thick tail and long hind legs, native to Australia.  Potoroos are nocturnal marsupials known for their diet of fungi and other subterranean organisms.  They are considered vulnerable or endangered in many areas due to habitat loss and predation.|The potoroo is a shy and elusive creature, rarely seen by humans.|近义词：bandicoot (though not a true kangaroo)|反义词：无
