preselling: /ˌpriːˈsɛlɪŋ/ | n. |预售，先行销售 |The act of selling goods or services before they are available.  It often involves taking orders and payments in advance.  This strategy generates hype and secures funding before launch. |例句：The concert tickets went on preselling months before the event.  这场音乐会的门票在活动前几个月就开始预售了。

The company's preselling campaign for its new phone was incredibly successful. 这家公司新款手机的预售活动非常成功。


近义词：advance selling, forward selling, early sales


反义词：post-selling (销售后), back-order (延期订单)


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Preselling generates significant revenue for the company. 预售为公司带来了可观的收入。
The artist is preselling limited-edition prints of his artwork.  这位艺术家正在预售他作品的限量版印刷品。


一般过去时:
They pre-sold all the tickets within a week.  他们在一周内预售了所有门票。
The developer pre-sold many units of the new condominium before construction was complete. 开发商在公寓楼建成之前预售了许多单元。


现在完成时:
The company has pre-sold over 10,000 units of its new product.  该公司已经预售了超过10,000台新产品。
We have pre-sold enough tickets to cover the initial production costs. 我们已经预售了足够的票来支付最初的制作成本。


过去完成时:
By the time the product launched, they had pre-sold half of the inventory.  产品发布时，他们已经预售了一半的库存。
Before the official release, they had pre-sold thousands of copies of the book. 在正式发行之前，他们已经预售了数千本该书。


将来时:
They will presell the album digitally before the physical release. 他们将在实体发行之前数字预售专辑。
The company will presell tickets for the conference next month.  该公司将于下个月预售会议门票。


虚拟语气:
If they had pre-sold more tickets, they would have made more profit. 如果他们预售更多门票，他们就能赚到更多利润。
I wish we had pre-sold more merchandise before the concert.  我希望我们在音乐会之前预售更多商品。


被动语态:
The tickets were pre-sold very quickly. 门票很快就被预售一空。
The new game is being pre-sold on several online platforms.  这款新游戏正在多个在线平台上预售。


疑问句:
Are they preselling tickets for the show?  他们正在预售演出的门票吗？
Will the product be pre-sold online? 该产品会在网上预售吗？


否定句:
The tickets were not pre-sold because of poor marketing.  由于营销不力，门票没有预售出去。
They did not presell enough copies of the book to justify a second printing. 他们预售的书籍数量不足以证明再版是合理的。

词源:  The word "preselling" is a combination of the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") and the verb "selling."  It's a relatively modern term reflecting the growth of advance sales in various industries.
