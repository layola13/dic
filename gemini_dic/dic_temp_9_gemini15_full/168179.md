pro-Colombian: /ˌproʊ kəˈlʌmbiən/ | adj. | 亲哥伦比亚的，支持哥伦比亚的 |  Supporting or favoring Colombia or its people.  The term often implies a positive sentiment towards Colombia's culture, politics, or people. | 例句：He holds pro-Colombian views and actively participates in events promoting Colombian culture. 他持有亲哥伦比亚的观点，并积极参与推广哥伦比亚文化的活动。

例句：The pro-Colombian sentiment among the expatriate community is quite strong.  侨民群体中亲哥伦比亚的情绪相当强烈。


近义词：pro-Colombia, Colombian-supportive (less common)


反义词：anti-Colombian


词性:形容词
