judaism:/ˈdʒuːdeɪɪzəm/| n. |犹太教|The monotheistic religion of the Jewish people, characterized by a belief in one God, the observance of the Torah (the first five books of the Hebrew Bible), and adherence to Jewish law and tradition.|例句：Judaism emphasizes ethical conduct and social justice. 犹太教强调道德行为和社会正义。|例句：My friend practices Judaism and observes all the religious holidays. 我的朋友信奉犹太教，并遵守所有的宗教节日。|近义词：Jewish faith, Hebraism|反义词：Christianity, Islam
