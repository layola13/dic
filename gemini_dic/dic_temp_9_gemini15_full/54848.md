displaces:/dɪsˈpleɪs/| vt.|移置；取代；使流离失所|To move something or someone out of their usual or proper place.|The new building displaced several small businesses.|近义词：remove, replace, supplant|反义词：install, reinstate, retain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy rain displaces the river water. = 大雨使河水泛滥。
The new technology displaces traditional methods. = 新技术取代了传统方法。


一般过去时:
The flood displaced thousands of people from their homes. = 洪水使成千上万的人流离失所。
The earthquake displaced the land. = 地震使土地移动。


现在完成时:
The recent storms have displaced many families. = 最近的风暴使许多家庭流离失所。
Automation has displaced many factory workers. = 自动化取代了许多工厂工人。


过去完成时:
The war had displaced millions before the peace treaty was signed. = 在和平条约签署之前，战争已使数百万人流离失所。
Before the dam was built, the river had displaced the village. = 在大坝修建之前，河水已经淹没了村庄。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will displace coastal communities. = 海平面上升将使沿海社区流离失所。
Further automation will displace even more workers in the future. = 未来进一步的自动化将取代更多的工人。


虚拟语气:
If the dam breaks, it will displace the entire town. = 如果大坝决堤，它将使整个城镇流离失所。
If the factory closed, it would displace hundreds of workers. = 如果工厂倒闭，它将使数百名工人失业。


被动语态:
Many people were displaced by the hurricane. = 许多人被飓风迫使流离失所。
The old system was displaced by a more efficient one. = 旧系统被一个更有效的系统取代了。


疑问句:
Will this new policy displace existing regulations? = 这项新政策会取代现有的规章制度吗？
How many people have been displaced by the conflict? = 冲突使多少人流离失所？


否定句:
The new law did not displace the old one. = 新法律并没有取代旧法律。
The company did not displace any employees. = 公司没有裁员。


PHRASE->
