responsion:/rɪˈspɒnʃən/ | n. |回应，回答，响应 | A responsion is an answer or reply, often to a formal request or question.  It can also refer to a responsive action or reaction. | Example: The professor's lecture prompted a lively responsion from the students. 教授的演讲引起了学生们的热烈回应。 The government's policy received a mixed responsion from the public. 政府的政策受到了公众的褒贬不一的回应。 |近义词：response, reply, answer, reaction |反义词：silence, inaction
