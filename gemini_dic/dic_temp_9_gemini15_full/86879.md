hemipode:/ˈhɛmɪpoʊd/| n. |半趾鸟，半足鸟|A bird of the family Hemipodiidae, characterized by having three toes on each foot, with the hind toe either absent or very small.  They are small, ground-dwelling birds found in Africa and Asia. They are often cryptic and difficult to observe.|Example: The painted hemipode is known for its striking plumage.|近义词:ground bird|反义词: N/A
