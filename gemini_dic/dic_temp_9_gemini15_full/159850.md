polyline:/ˈpɒliˌlaɪn/ /ˈpɑːliˌlaɪn/| n. |多段线，折线 |A polyline is a series of connected line segments.  It's a path defined by a sequence of points, where each point is connected to the next by a straight line. Polylines are commonly used in computer graphics, geographic information systems (GIS), and other applications to represent linear features. |Example: The map displayed a polyline representing the route of the hiking trail.  地图上显示了一条代表远足路线的多段线。

The software used a polyline to draw the outline of the building. 该软件使用多段线绘制建筑物的轮廓。
