caliphate:/ˈkæ.lɪ.feɪt/| n. |哈里发统治；哈里发国；伊斯兰教的统治|The political and religious system of the Muslim community, headed by a caliph.  A caliphate is a state ruled by a caliph, who is considered the successor to the Prophet Muhammad.  The term often implies a specific historical period or a specific political entity claiming religious authority. |例句：The Abbasid Caliphate lasted for several centuries. 阿巴斯王朝的哈里发统治持续了几个世纪。

The rise of the Islamic State, which declared a caliphate, caused international concern. 伊斯兰国的兴起，宣布建立哈里发国，引起了国际社会的担忧。|近义词：kingdom, empire, sultanate|反义词：secular state, republic
