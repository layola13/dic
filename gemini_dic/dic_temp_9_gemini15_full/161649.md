poundcake:/ˈpaʊndkeɪk/| n. |磅蛋糕|A rich, dense cake made with a pound each of butter, sugar, eggs, and flour (originally).  It is now often made with less butter and other variations.  The name refers to the original recipe's equal weight of ingredients.|例句：She baked a delicious poundcake for the party. 她为派对烤了一个美味的磅蛋糕。


例句：The poundcake was so moist and flavorful.  这个磅蛋糕如此湿润美味。


近义词：butter cake, sponge cake|反义词：None specifically, but terms describing light or airy cakes could be considered antonyms. |词性:名词
