overbank:/ˈoʊvərˌbæŋk/| n.|河漫滩，泛滥平原|The area of land that is subject to flooding when a river overflows its banks.|例句：The overbank deposits showed evidence of past flooding. 河漫滩沉积物显示了过去洪水的证据。|近义词：floodplain, alluvial plain|反义词：riverbed, channel
