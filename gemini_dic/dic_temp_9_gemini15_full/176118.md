razed:/reɪzd/| vt.|夷为平地，拆毁|To completely destroy a building or town by destroying it: The village was razed to the ground during the war.  战争期间，村庄被夷为平地。|近义词：destroy, demolish, obliterate|反义词：build, construct, erect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bulldozer razes the old building. = 推土机正在夷平旧建筑物。
They raze old houses to make way for new development. = 他们拆除旧房屋以便为新的发展让路。


一般过去时:
The city was razed by the earthquake. = 城市在地震中被夷为平地。
The army razed the enemy's fortifications. = 军队夷平了敌人的防御工事。


现在完成时:
The developers have razed several blocks of houses. = 开发商已经夷平了几栋住宅楼。
They have razed the old factory to make room for a park. = 他们已经拆除了旧工厂，为公园腾出空间。


过去完成时:
The city had been razed before the new settlers arrived. = 新移民到达之前，这座城市已经被夷为平地了。
The invaders had razed the ancient temples before they retreated. = 侵略者撤退前已经夷平了古老的寺庙。


将来时:
They will raze the abandoned hospital next month. = 他们下个月将拆除废弃的医院。
The old buildings will be razed to make way for a new shopping mall. = 旧建筑物将被拆除，为新的购物中心让路。


虚拟语气:
If they had razed the building sooner, the fire wouldn't have spread. = 如果他们早点拆除那栋建筑物，火就不会蔓延了。
I wish they hadn't razed that historical building. = 我真希望他们没有拆除那座历史建筑。


被动语态:
The old town was razed to the ground. = 老城区被夷为平地。
The building is being razed at this moment. = 建筑物正在被拆除。


疑问句:
Was the building razed by fire? = 这栋建筑物是被火烧毁的吗？
Will the old houses be razed soon? = 旧房子很快会被拆除吗？


否定句:
They did not raze the old church. = 他们没有拆除那座古老的教堂。
The city was not razed during the conflict. = 在冲突期间，这座城市没有被夷为平地。


PHRASE->
raze to the ground: 夷为平地
raze a building: 拆除建筑物
raze a city: 摧毁城市
raze a forest: 砍伐森林
raze the opposition: 彻底粉碎反对派


词源:
From Old French *raser (to scrape, shave), from Vulgar Latin *rasāre, from Latin rādēre (to scrape).  The meaning shifted from scraping to destroying.
