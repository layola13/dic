quebracho:/keɪˈbrætʃoʊ/ | n. |南美的一种树； quebracho木；  |A quebracho is a type of tree native to South America, particularly Argentina, Paraguay, and Bolivia.  The wood is extremely hard and dense, making it resistant to decay and insects. It's prized for its use in tanning leather and railway sleepers.  |The quebracho trees are known for their extremely hard wood. |近义词：hardwood, lignum vitae |反义词：softwood
