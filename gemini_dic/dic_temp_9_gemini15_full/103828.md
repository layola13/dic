joyful:/ˈdʒɔɪfəl/ | adj. | 快乐的；令人高兴的 | Full of joy; characterized by or expressing joy.  |例句：The children were joyful at the prospect of a holiday. 孩子们对假期的到来感到非常高兴。

例句：Her joyful laughter filled the room.  她的欢笑声充满了整个房间。

近义词：happy, merry, cheerful, delighted, glad | 反义词：sad, unhappy, sorrowful, miserable, dejected | 词性: adjective


USAGE->

He is joyful about his promotion. = 他对自己的晋升感到高兴。
The atmosphere is joyful and festive. =  气氛快乐而喜庆。


一般过去时:
