purrer:/ˈpɜːrər/| n.|咕噜咕噜叫的动物（尤指猫）, 发出轻柔声音的人或物 |A purrer is an animal, especially a cat, that purrs, or a person or thing that makes a soft, gentle murmuring sound.  |例句：The kitten was a sweet little purrer, constantly rubbing against my legs.  那只小猫是个可爱的小家伙，总是蹭着我的腿。|例句：The old engine was a noisy purrer, but it worked reliably. 这台旧发动机声音很大，但运行可靠。|近义词：hummer, murmurer |反义词：screamer, howler


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The cat purrs contentedly. = 猫舒服地发出咕噜声。
The engine purrs quietly. = 引擎安静地发出嗡嗡声。

一般过去时:
The cat purred loudly after eating. = 猫吃完饭后大声地咕噜叫。
The motor purred smoothly during the drive. = 开车时，发动机平稳地发出嗡嗡声。

现在完成时:
The cat has purred all afternoon. = 猫整个下午都在咕噜咕噜叫。
The machine has purred consistently since its last service. = 这台机器自从上次保养后一直运行平稳。

过去完成时:
The cat had purred itself to sleep. = 猫咕噜咕噜叫着睡着了。
The engine had purred quietly before it suddenly stalled. = 引擎在突然熄火之前一直安静地发出嗡嗡声。

将来时:
The kitten will purr when you pet it. = 当你抚摸小猫时，它会发出咕噜声。
The new car will purr like a dream. = 新车将会像梦一样平稳地运行。

虚拟语气:
If the cat were happy, it would purr. = 如果猫很开心，它就会发出咕噜声。
I wish the engine would purr as quietly as it used to. = 我希望引擎能像以前一样安静地发出嗡嗡声。

被动语态:
(This is less common as "purr" is usually intransitive.)  A soft purring sound was heard. = 听到了轻柔的咕噜声。


疑问句:
Does the cat purr often? = 猫经常发出咕噜声吗？
Is the engine purring smoothly? = 引擎运行平稳吗？

否定句:
The cat didn't purr at all. = 猫一点咕噜声也没发出。
The engine wasn't purring, it was sputtering. = 引擎不是发出嗡嗡声，而是在发出噼里啪啦的声音。
