overclassifies:/ˌoʊvərˈklæsɪfaɪz/ | v. | 过度分类；分类错误 | To classify something too broadly or inaccurately.  This often leads to a loss of important distinctions or details.  | Example: The algorithm overclassifies images, grouping dissimilar objects together.  算法过度分类图像，将不同的物体归为一类。 The simplistic model overclassifies the complex data, losing crucial nuances.  简化的模型对复杂数据进行了过度分类，丢失了关键的细微差别。 |近义词：misclassify, categorize incorrectly, overgeneralize | 反义词：classify accurately, differentiate, distinguish
