mechanisation:/ˌmekənaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |机械化；机械操作|The process of introducing machines or automated systems into an activity, process, or industry, replacing human or animal labor.  It often leads to increased efficiency and productivity, but can also result in job displacement. |例句：The mechanisation of agriculture led to increased food production.  农业机械化导致粮食产量增加。

例句：The factory's mechanisation improved its output significantly. 工厂的机械化显著提高了产量。


近义词：automation, automation, motorization|反义词：manual labor, handcraft

词性：名词
