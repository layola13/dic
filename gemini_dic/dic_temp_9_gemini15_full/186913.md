This term is derived from Washington Irving's story "Rip Van Winkle," in which the protagonist sleeps for twenty years and wakes up to a drastically changed world. | The company's Rip Van Winkleism allowed its competitors to gain a significant market share.  这家公司的长期沉睡让竞争对手获得了大量的市场份额。

The government's Rip Van Winkleism on climate change is alarming. 政府对气候变化的漠不关心令人担忧。| 近义词：apathy, indifference, unawareness, obliviousness, stagnation | 反义词：awareness, attentiveness, vigilance, progressiveness |  词性: 名词
