less:/les/| adj., adv.|更少；较少 |Less means a smaller amount or degree. It can function as both an adjective and an adverb.  |例句：There is less traffic today.  今天车流量较少。|例句：He works less than he used to. 他比以前工作少了。|近义词：fewer, smaller, minor|反义词：more, greater, additional


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is less water in the bottle. = 瓶子里水比较少了。
She eats less than her brother. = 她比她哥哥吃得少。


一般过去时:
There was less noise after the party ended. = 派对结束后噪音小多了。
He drank less coffee yesterday. = 他昨天喝的咖啡少了。


现在完成时:
We have eaten less this week. = 我们这周吃得少了。
She has spent less money than expected. = 她花的钱比预期的少。


过去完成时:
They had done less work than they planned. = 他们做的工作比计划的少。
He had eaten less than usual that day. = 他那天吃得比平时少。


将来时:
I will drink less soda next week. = 下周我会少喝点苏打水。
We will have less homework tomorrow. = 我们明天作业会少一些。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had less work to do. = 我希望我的工作少一些。
It would be better if we had less stress. = 如果我们压力小一些就好了。


被动语态:
Less money was spent on advertising this year. = 今年在广告上的支出减少了。
Less time is needed to complete the task. = 完成这项任务需要的时间更少。


疑问句:
Is there less sugar in this recipe? = 这个食谱里糖少了？
Do you need less help now? = 你现在需要更少的帮助吗？


否定句:
There isn't less pollution in the city. = 城市里的污染并没有减少。
He doesn't eat less than before. = 他并没有比以前少吃。


PHRASE->
less than = 少于
no less than = 不少于
less and less = 越来越少
feel less = 感到轻松一些
make less = 赚的少
