diversifications:/ˌdaɪvɜːrsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |多样化；多元化 |The process of making something more diverse or varied.  It often refers to expanding into different markets, products, or services to reduce risk and increase growth.  |例句：The company's diversifications into renewable energy have proven successful. 公司向可再生能源领域的扩张已证明是成功的。
The government is encouraging diversifications in the agricultural sector. 政府正在鼓励农业部门的多元化发展。|近义词：diversification, variety, expansion|反义词：specialization, concentration
