oxymoron:/ˌɒksɪˈmɔːrɒn/| n. |矛盾修辞法；矛盾说法|A figure of speech in which apparently contradictory terms appear in conjunction.  It's a rhetorical device used to create a striking or humorous effect.  Examples include "jumbo shrimp," "deafening silence," and "organized chaos."|The military's "surgical strike" resulted in many civilian casualties.  =  军方的“外科手术式打击”导致许多平民伤亡。

The band's performance was a deafening silence; no one could hear a thing. = 乐队的表演是一片令人震耳欲聋的寂静；没有人能听到任何东西。


|近义词：paradox, contradiction |反义词：consistency, agreement |名词
