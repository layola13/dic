dorsel:/ˈdɔːrsəl/| adj.|背部的，背面的，背侧的|Relating to the back or posterior part of the body.  It is often used in anatomy and zoology to describe structures or features located on the back or upper surface of an organism.|例句：The dorsal fin of the shark is prominent.  鲨鱼的背鳍很突出。

The dorsal surface of the leaf is darker than the ventral surface. 叶子的背面比腹面颜色深。|近义词：posterior, back, |反义词:ventral, anterior
