geologist:/dʒiˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |地质学家|A geologist is a scientist who studies the Earth, its structure, substance, history, and the processes that act on it.|例句：The geologist examined the rock samples carefully.  这位地质学家仔细检查了岩石样本。|例句：She is a renowned geologist specializing in paleontology. 她是一位著名的古生物地质学家。|近义词：geoscientist|反义词：N/A
