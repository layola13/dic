frazzle:/ˈfræzəl/| v. n. adj.|使筋疲力尽；烦躁不安；疲惫不堪的；|

英文解析:To make someone extremely tired and irritable.  Also, the state of being extremely tired and irritable.


例句:
The long journey completely frazzled me. = 漫长的旅程让我筋疲力尽。

The exam really frazzled her nerves. = 考试真的让她紧张不安。


近义词:exhaust, tire, wear out, irritate, annoy, stress

反义词:relax, refresh, rejuvenate, calm

词性:verb, noun, adjective
