oedema:/ɪˈdiːmə/| n. |水肿；浮肿 |Swelling of soft tissues due to the accumulation of fluid.  It's a condition characterized by excess fluid buildup in body tissues, causing swelling.|例句：The patient presented with peripheral oedema in both legs.  这位病人双腿出现周围性水肿。|例句：  Heart failure can cause oedema in the ankles and feet. 心力衰竭会导致踝部和足部水肿。|近义词：swelling, effusion, dropsy |反义词：dehydration
