reconquesting:/ˌriːˈkɒŋkwestɪŋ/ | v. | 重夺；重新征服 |  The act of regaining something previously lost or taken, especially territory or power.  It implies a forceful regaining, often after a period of loss or defeat. | 例句：The general planned a daring campaign for reconquesting the lost province.  将军计划了一场大胆的战役来收复失落的省份。

The army is currently reconquesting the occupied territories.  军队目前正在收复被占领的领土。


近义词：retaking, regaining, recovering, repossessing | 反义词：losing, surrendering, relinquishing | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reconquesting the lost land. = 他们在收复失地。
The king is reconquesting his former kingdom. = 国王正在收复他以前的王国。


一般过去时:
They reconquered the lost province. = 他们收复了失落的省份。
The empire reconquered its former territories. = 这个帝国收复了它以前的领土。


现在完成时:
They have reconquered their homeland. = 他们已经收复了他们的家园。
The forces have reconquered the city after a long siege. = 在经过长时间的围困后，军队已经收复了这座城市。


过去完成时:
They had reconquered the city before the enemy could reinforce it. = 他们在敌人增援之前已经收复了这座城市。
The king had reconquered most of his kingdom by the time of his death. = 国王去世时已经收复了他大部分的王国。


将来时:
They will reconquer the lost territories. = 他们将收复失地。
The army will reconquer the occupied city next month. = 军队将于下个月收复被占领的城市。


虚拟语气:
If they had a stronger army, they would reconquer the land. = 如果他们有更强大的军队，他们就会收复这片土地。
I wish they could reconquer their lost home. = 我希望他们能够收复他们失去的家园。


被动语态:
The lost territories were reconquered by the army. = 失地被军队收复了。
The city was reconquered after a fierce battle. = 在一场激烈的战斗之后，这座城市被收复了。


疑问句:
Will they reconquer the lost lands? = 他们会收复失地吗？
Have they reconquered the occupied territory? = 他们已经收复被占领的领土了吗？


否定句:
They did not reconquer the lost city. = 他们没有收复失落的城市。
They could not reconquer the land due to lack of resources. = 由于缺乏资源，他们无法收复这片土地。
