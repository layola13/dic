modernized:/ˈmɒdənaɪzd/ /ˈmɑːdərnaɪzd/| v.|使现代化；现代化改造|To make something more modern. This involves updating or improving something to meet current standards or expectations.  It often implies incorporating new technologies, methods, or ideas.|例句：The factory has been modernized with new equipment.  这家工厂用新设备进行了现代化改造。

例句：They modernized the curriculum to better prepare students for the workforce. 他们对课程进行了现代化改革，以更好地为学生就业做准备。


近义词：updated, improved, renovated, advanced

反义词：antiquated, outdated, obsolete


词性:动词
