jointing:/ˈdʒɔɪntɪŋ/| n. v.|连接；接合；接缝；关节|The act of joining two or more things together; a joint or connection.  |例句：The plumber expertly executed the pipe jointing. 水管工熟练地完成了管道连接。

例句：The quality of the stone jointing in the wall was excellent. 墙上的石头接缝质量非常好。


近义词：connection, joining, union, linkage, articulation|反义词：separation, disconnection, disjunction|名词，动词
