sieging:/ˈsiːdʒɪŋ/| v. |围攻；长期攻击|The act of besieging a place, especially a town or fortress, by surrounding it with armed forces and preventing supplies from entering.  It often implies a prolonged and difficult struggle.|例句：The army continued its sieging of the city for weeks.  军队持续数周围攻这座城市。
The rebels were sieging the capital, cutting off all access.  叛军正在围攻首都，切断了所有通道。|近义词：besieging, attacking, assaulting|反义词：relieving, surrendering


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The army is sieging the castle. = 军队正在围攻城堡。
They are sieging the town with heavy artillery. = 他们用重炮围攻该镇。


一般过去时:
The city was sieged for months. = 这座城市被围困了数月。
They sieged the fort until it surrendered. = 他们围攻要塞直到其投降。


现在完成时:
The rebels have sieged the capital for weeks. = 叛军已经围攻首都数周了。
The army has been sieging the fortress since last month. = 自上个月以来，军队一直在围攻要塞。


过去完成时:
The city had been sieged for years before they finally surrendered. = 在他们最终投降之前，这座城市已经被围困多年了。
They had sieged the castle for months before the reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，他们已经围攻城堡数月了。


将来时:
The general plans to siege the enemy stronghold next week. = 将军计划下周围攻敌人的据点。
They will siege the city if negotiations fail. = 如果谈判失败，他们将围攻这座城市。


虚拟语气:
If they sieged the city, they would face fierce resistance. = 如果他们围攻这座城市，他们将面临激烈的抵抗。
I wish they hadn't sieged the town, causing so much damage. = 我真希望他们没有围攻该镇，造成如此大的破坏。


被动语态:
The castle was sieged by the enemy army. = 城堡被敌军围攻。
The city is being sieged, and food is running out. = 城市正在被围攻，食物即将耗尽。


疑问句:
Are they sieging the city center? = 他们正在围攻市中心吗？
Was the fort sieged during the war? = 要塞在战争期间被围攻了吗？


否定句:
The army did not siege the town. = 军队没有围攻该镇。
They haven't yet sieged the enemy's main base. = 他们还没有围攻敌人的主要基地。
