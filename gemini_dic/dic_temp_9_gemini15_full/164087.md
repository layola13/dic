prediscusses:/ˌpriːdɪˈskʌsɪz/| v.|预先讨论，事先讨论|To discuss something beforehand; to have a preliminary discussion.|例句：The committee prediscussed the budget before the formal meeting.  委员会在正式会议前预先讨论了预算。|例句：They prediscussed the project's feasibility before submitting the proposal. 他们在提交提案之前预先讨论了项目的可行性。|近义词：preconsider, prearrange, preplan|反义词：postpone, delay
