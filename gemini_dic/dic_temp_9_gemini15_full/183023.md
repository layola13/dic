replicate:/ˈrɛplɪkeɪt/| vt. n.|复制，重复，模仿|To make an exact copy of something; to repeat something.|例句：The experiment was replicated several times to ensure accuracy.  实验重复进行了数次以确保准确性。|例句：The software allows you to replicate the original file. 该软件允许你复制原始文件。|近义词：duplicate, copy, reproduce, imitate|反义词：destroy, erase, obliterate|动词, 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists replicate experiments to verify results. = 科学家重复实验以验证结果。
She replicates her mother's cooking style. = 她模仿她母亲的烹饪风格。

一般过去时:
He replicated the painting perfectly. = 他完美地复制了这幅画。
The study was replicated by another research team. = 这项研究被另一个研究小组复制了。

现在完成时:
They have replicated the findings of the original study. = 他们已经复制了最初研究的结果。
Researchers have replicated the experiment numerous times. = 研究人员已经多次重复了该实验。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, she had replicated all the necessary charts. = 在演示之前，她已经复制了所有必要的图表。
The team had replicated the process successfully several times. = 该团队已经成功地多次复制了这个过程。

将来时:
We will replicate the design for the new product. = 我们将复制新产品的设想。
The company plans to replicate its success in other markets. = 公司计划将其成功复制到其他市场。

虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could replicate the experiment on a larger scale. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们就能更大规模地复制实验。
I wish I could replicate her success in business. = 我真希望我能复制她在商业上的成功。

被动语态:
The experiment was replicated by a different group of researchers. = 该实验被另一组研究人员复制了。
The results were replicated in subsequent studies. = 结果在后续研究中得到了重复。

疑问句:
Can you replicate this image? = 你能复制这张图像吗？
Did they replicate the experiment using the same method? = 他们使用相同的方法复制了实验吗？

否定句:
We couldn't replicate the results of the first trial. = 我们无法复制第一次试验的结果。
He failed to replicate the success of his previous project. = 他未能复制他之前项目取得的成功。
