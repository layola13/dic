reprimand:/ˈrɛprɪmænd/ | vt. n. | 训斥；谴责 | To reprimand someone is to rebuke them formally for their wrongdoing.  It implies a more serious and official rebuke than a simple scolding. |例句：The teacher reprimanded the student for talking in class.老师因学生在课堂上讲话而训斥了他。

The manager reprimanded the employee for their repeated lateness.经理因员工屡次迟到而训斥了他。|近义词：rebuke, scold, admonish, chastise |反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reprimands his children regularly for bad behavior. 他经常因为孩子的不良行为而训斥他们。
The principal reprimands students who break the rules. 校长训斥违规的学生。


一般过去时:
The boss reprimanded him for his mistake.老板因为他犯的错误而训斥了他。
My teacher reprimanded me for not completing my homework. 我的老师因为我没有完成作业而训斥了我。


现在完成时:
She has reprimanded him several times this week. 本周她已经训斥了他好几次。
The coach has reprimanded the team for their poor performance. 教练因为球队的糟糕表现而训斥了球队。


过去完成时:
He had already been reprimanded for the same offense before. 他之前就因为同样的罪行被训斥过。
The company had reprimanded him for his unethical behaviour. 公司因为他的不道德行为而训斥了他。


将来时:
The teacher will reprimand the students if they continue to misbehave. 如果学生继续行为不端，老师将会训斥他们。
The judge will reprimand the lawyer for his unprofessional conduct. 法官将因律师的不专业行为而训斥他。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reprimand him for his arrogance. 如果我是你，我会因为他傲慢的态度而训斥他。
I wish I had reprimanded him sooner. 我真希望我早点训斥他。


被动语态:
He was reprimanded for his dishonesty. 他因为不诚实而受到训斥。
The employees were reprimanded for their negligence. 员工们因为他们的疏忽而受到训斥。


疑问句:
Will you reprimand him for his actions? 你会因为他的行为而训斥他吗？
Has she ever been reprimanded for her work? 她因为工作而受过训斥吗？


否定句:
The teacher did not reprimand the student this time. 这次老师没有训斥那个学生。
I won't reprimand him unless he does it again. 除非他再犯，否则我不会训斥他。


PHRASE->
a severe reprimand  严厉的训斥
a formal reprimand 正式训斥
receive a reprimand  受到训斥
issue a reprimand  发出训斥
deserve a reprimand 应该受到训斥

词源:
From Middle French *reprémander, from Old French reprendre (to retake, reprove), from re- + prendre (to take).  The sense development is from "to take back" (as in taking back words or actions) to "reprove" or "rebuke".
