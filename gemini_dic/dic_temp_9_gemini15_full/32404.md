chilling-out: /ˈtʃɪlɪŋ aʊt/ | v. |放松，休闲 |  to relax completely; to unwind.  This is a phrasal verb, meaning it's a verb combined with a preposition or adverb to create a new meaning.  The meaning is derived from the sense of "chill" meaning to relax or calm down.


例句：

I'm just chilling out after a long day at work.  = 我下班后正在放松。

Let's chill out and watch a movie. = 我们放松一下，看部电影吧。


近义词：relaxing, unwinding, taking it easy, de-stressing


反义词：stressing, working hard, being busy


词性：动词 (phrasal verb)

They chill out by the pool. = 他们在游泳池边放松。

被动语态: (The phrasal verb "chill out" is rarely used in the passive voice because it is inherently active.)
