reemergent:/ˌriːɪˈmɜːrdʒənt/| adj.|重新出现的；复发的|Emerging or appearing again after a period of being absent or inactive.  The term often implies a resurgence or renewal, suggesting a return to prominence or significance.|例句：The reemergent interest in vinyl records surprised many.  重新兴起的黑胶唱片兴趣让许多人感到惊讶。

例句：After a period of decline, the reemergent  technology is now gaining popularity.  在一段时间的衰落之后，这项重新出现的技术现在正越来越受欢迎。


近义词：resurgent, reappearing, recurring, reviving

反义词：disappearing, extinct, vanishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem is reemergent in many areas. = 这个问题在许多地区重新出现。
This topic is reemergent in current discussions. = 这个话题在当前的讨论中重新出现。


一般过去时:
The disease reemerged in several countries. = 这种疾病在几个国家卷土重来。
The issue reemerged during the debate. = 这个问题在辩论中再次出现。


现在完成时:
The virus has reemerged in recent years. = 近年来这种病毒再次出现。
The old fashion has reemerged in the modern world. = 这种旧式风格在现代世界中重新出现。


过去完成时:
The symptoms had reemerged before the doctor's appointment. = 在看医生之前，症状又复发了。
The problem had reemerged after initial improvement. = 在最初有所改善之后，问题又再次出现。


将来时:
The disease may reemerge in the future. = 未来这种疾病可能会卷土重来。
Experts predict the trend will reemerge next year. = 专家预测这一趋势明年将卷土重来。


虚拟语气:
If the problem reemerged, we would have to act quickly. = 如果问题再次出现，我们将不得不迅速采取行动。
I wish the old habits hadn't reemerged. = 我希望旧习惯没有死灰复燃。


被动语态:
The issue was reemerged in the media. = 这个问题在媒体上再次出现。
The concerns were reemerged by recent events. = 最近的事件使人们再次关注这些担忧。


疑问句:
Has the problem reemerged? = 问题又出现了吗？
Will the trend reemerge? = 这一趋势会卷土重来吗？


否定句:
The problem didn't reemerge after the solution. = 解决问题后，问题没有再次出现。
The symptoms hadn't reemerged at that time. = 那时症状还没有复发。
