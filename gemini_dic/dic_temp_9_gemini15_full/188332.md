rubbishy:/ˈrʌbɪʃi/| adj.|垃圾的；劣质的；毫无价值的 |Of poor quality; worthless.  The film was rubbishy and not worth watching.  |例句：The food was rubbishy and tasted awful. 这食物很糟糕，味道很差。

例句：His argument was rubbishy and unconvincing. 他的论点很糟糕，令人无法信服。


近义词：trashy, junky, shoddy, worthless, inferior


反义词：high-quality, excellent, superior, first-rate


词源:  Derived from "rubbish," meaning waste or garbage. The suffix "-y" adds an adjectival quality, indicating a characteristic of being like rubbish.
