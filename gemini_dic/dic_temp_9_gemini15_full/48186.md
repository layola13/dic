decomposition:/ˌdiːkɒmpəˈzɪʃən/| n. |分解；腐烂；衰变 |The process of something being broken down into simpler parts or substances.  This can refer to chemical processes, biological processes (like decay), or even abstract concepts.  |例句：The decomposition of organic matter is crucial for nutrient cycling. 有机物的分解对于养分循环至关重要。

The decomposition of the old building was surprisingly quick.  旧建筑的倒塌出乎意料地迅速。


|近义词：decay, disintegration, breakdown, rot |反义词：synthesis, composition, formation |名词
