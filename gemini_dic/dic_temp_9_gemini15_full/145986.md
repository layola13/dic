overdogmatic:/ˌoʊvərˌdɒɡˈmætɪk/| adj. | 过于教条的，墨守成规的 | Excessively dogmatic; rigidly adhering to doctrines or principles.  Characterized by an excessive adherence to dogma; inflexible and uncompromising. |例句：His overdogmatic approach to teaching stifled creativity in the classroom. 他过于教条的教学方法扼杀了课堂上的创造力。

His overdogmatic views on politics alienated many of his friends. 他对政治的过分教条的观点疏远了他的许多朋友。

近义词：rigid, inflexible, doctrinaire, authoritarian, uncompromising |反义词：flexible, open-minded, tolerant, liberal, pragmatic
