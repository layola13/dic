metapodium:/ˌmɛtəˈpoʊdiəm/| n. |掌骨；跖骨|The metapodium is the middle part of the foot or hand, consisting of the metacarpal bones in the hand and the metatarsal bones in the foot.  It's the region between the carpus/tarsus and the phalanges.  |Example: The fracture was located in the metapodium of the right foot.|近义词:metatarsus (foot), metacarpus (hand)|反义词:carpus, tarsus, phalanges|名词
