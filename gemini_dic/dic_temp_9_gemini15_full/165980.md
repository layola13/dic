prepartitioned:/ˌpriːpɑːrˈtɪʃənd/| adj.|预先分区的|Previously divided into sections or partitions, typically in a computer system or data storage context.  This adjective describes a state where something, often data or storage space, has already been organized into distinct parts before further use or processing.|例句：The database was prepartitioned to improve query performance.  数据库已预先分区以提高查询性能。 The hard drive arrived prepartitioned into three logical drives. 硬盘到货时已预先分区成三个逻辑驱动器。|近义词：predivided, preallocated|反义词：unpartitioned
