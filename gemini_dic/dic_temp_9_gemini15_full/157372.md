pipette:/pɪˈpɛt/ | n. |移液管；吸量管 | A pipette is a laboratory instrument used to transfer a precise volume of liquid.  It typically consists of a cylindrical tube with a narrow tip and a bulb or piston for drawing up and dispensing the liquid. | Example: The scientist carefully used a pipette to measure 1 milliliter of the solution.  科学家小心地用移液管测量了1毫升溶液。|近义词：dropper, burette (for larger volumes) |反义词：None (no direct antonym)| 名词
