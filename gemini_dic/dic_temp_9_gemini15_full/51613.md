develop:/dɪˈvɛləp/| vt. vi. n.|发展；开发；研制；养成；显影|To grow or cause to grow and become more mature, advanced, or elaborate.|例句：The company is developing a new product. 这家公司正在研发一种新产品。
例句：His ideas developed over time. 他的想法随着时间推移而发展。

近义词：grow, evolve, expand, improve, cultivate|反义词：decline, deteriorate, regress, shrink


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They develop new software every year. = 他们每年都开发新的软件。
The company develops innovative marketing strategies. = 公司制定创新的营销策略。

一般过去时:
The photographer developed the film. = 摄影师冲洗了胶卷。
The country developed rapidly after the war. = 战后，这个国家迅速发展。

现在完成时:
We have developed a new understanding of the problem. = 我们对这个问题有了新的理解。
The scientists have developed a vaccine for the disease. = 科学家们研制出了该疾病的疫苗。

过去完成时:
They had developed a strong relationship before the project started. = 项目开始前，他们已经建立了牢固的关系。
The company had developed its marketing plan before launching the product. = 公司在推出产品之前已经制定了营销计划。


将来时:
They will develop a new app next year. = 他们明年将开发一个新的应用程序。
We will develop our skills through practice. = 我们将通过练习来提高我们的技能。

虚拟语气:
If they developed a better strategy, they would succeed. = 如果他们制定更好的策略，他们就会成功。
I wish I had developed better study habits earlier. = 我真希望我早些养成更好的学习习惯。

被动语态:
The software is being developed by a team of experts. = 该软件由一个专家团队开发。
The plan was developed over several months. = 该计划历时数月才制定出来。

疑问句:
How do you develop your leadership skills? = 你如何培养你的领导才能？
Will the project develop as planned? = 项目会按计划进行吗？

否定句:
The photo didn't develop properly. = 照片冲洗得不理想。
They did not develop a successful marketing campaign. = 他们没有开展成功的营销活动。
