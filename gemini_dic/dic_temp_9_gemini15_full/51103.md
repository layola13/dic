desperation:/ˌdɛspəˈreɪʃən/| n. |绝望；孤注一掷|A state of despair, typically one that leads to rash or desperate actions.  It implies a feeling of hopelessness and a willingness to take extreme measures to escape a difficult situation.|例句：Driven by desperation, he robbed a bank.  =  在绝望的驱使下，他抢劫了一家银行。

例句：Her desperation was evident in her trembling hands. = 她颤抖的双手显露出了她的绝望。


近义词：hopelessness, despair, extremity |反义词：hope, optimism, calmness |词性: 名词
