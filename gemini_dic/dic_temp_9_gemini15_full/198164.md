shored:/ʃɔːrd/ | v. | 支撑；加固；巩固；(使)靠岸 | To support or strengthen something; to provide support or protection to something; to bring (a boat) to shore. |例句：They shored up the crumbling wall with timbers. 他们用木材支撑快要倒塌的墙。

They shored up the failing business with a new marketing campaign.他们通过新的市场营销活动支撑摇摇欲坠的企业。


近义词：support, reinforce, buttress, bolster | 反义词：weaken, undermine, destabilize | 词性：动词
