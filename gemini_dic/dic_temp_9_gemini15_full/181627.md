relaxant:/rɪˈlæksənt/| n. |松弛剂，镇静剂|A relaxant is a substance that causes relaxation of muscles or reduces tension.  It can be a medication or a naturally occurring substance.|Example: The doctor prescribed a muscle relaxant to alleviate his back pain. 医生开了肌肉松弛剂来缓解他的背痛。|近义词：sedative, tranquilizer, muscle relaxer|反义词：stimulant, tonic
