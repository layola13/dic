gunsmithing:/ˈɡʌnˌsmɪθɪŋ/| n. |枪械制造；枪械修理|The craft of making, repairing, and modifying firearms.  It involves skills in metalworking, machining, and woodworking, as well as a deep understanding of firearm mechanics and safety.|例句：He learned gunsmithing from his grandfather. 他从他祖父那里学会了枪械制造。|例句：The gunsmithing business is thriving due to increased interest in firearms. 由于人们对枪支的兴趣日益浓厚，枪械制造业务蓬勃发展。|近义词：firearm repair, arms making|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with activities like jewelry making or carpentry.
