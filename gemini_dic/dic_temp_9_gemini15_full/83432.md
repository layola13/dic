habitable:/ˈhæbɪtəbəl/| adj.|适宜居住的，可居住的|capable of supporting life; suitable for living in.  The word emphasizes the suitability of an environment for living organisms, particularly humans.|Example: Mars is not currently habitable, but future technologies might make it so.  火星目前不适宜居住，但未来的技术可能会使其适宜居住。|近义词：livable, inhabitable|反义词：uninhabitable, uninhabitable, sterile


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The planet is habitable. = 这个星球适宜居住。
This area is habitable for humans. = 这个区域适合人类居住。

一般过去时:
The land was habitable before the flood. = 洪水泛滥之前这块土地适宜居住。
The house was not habitable after the fire. = 火灾之后这房子无法居住。


现在完成时:
The scientists have proven the planet habitable. = 科学家们已经证明这颗行星适宜居住。
They have made the old house habitable again. = 他们使那座老房子再次适宜居住。


过去完成时:
The area had been habitable for centuries before the volcano erupted. = 火山爆发前，该地区已适宜居住数百年。
The building had not been habitable since the earthquake. = 自地震以来，这座建筑物一直无法居住。


将来时:
The new colony will be habitable within a year. = 新殖民地将在一年内适宜居住。
This planet will likely be habitable in the distant future. = 这颗行星很可能在遥远的未来适宜居住。


虚拟语气:
If the climate were different, the planet might be habitable. = 如果气候不同，这颗行星可能是适宜居住的。
If only the land were habitable, we could build a home there. = 如果这块土地适宜居住就好了，我们就能在那里建个家。


被动语态:
The region has been declared habitable. = 该地区已被宣布适宜居住。
The house is being made habitable for the new tenants. = 正在使房子适合新租户居住。


疑问句:
Is the planet habitable? = 这颗行星适宜居住吗？
Is this area habitable for wildlife? = 这个区域适合野生动物居住吗？


否定句:
The planet is not habitable. = 这颗行星不适宜居住。
The conditions were not habitable for humans. = 这些条件不适合人类居住。
