cheering:/ˈtʃɪərɪŋ/| n. |欢呼声；喝彩声|The sound of people shouting in a way that shows their happiness or approval.  It can refer to the act of cheering itself or the sound produced.|例句：The cheering of the crowd was deafening.  人群的欢呼声震耳欲聋。

例句：The team was energized by the cheering from the stands. 球队受到了看台上欢呼声的鼓舞。


近义词：applause, acclamation, shouts of joy |反义词：silence, booing
