resolicit:/ˌriːsəˈlɪsɪt/| vt.|再次请求；再次征求|To request or solicit again.  This often implies a previous request that was unsuccessful or not fully acted upon.|例句：The company decided to resolicit bids after the initial round proved unsatisfactory. 公司在第一轮投标不令人满意后决定再次征求投标。

例句：He resolicitated donations after the initial fundraising campaign fell short of its goal. 他在最初的筹款活动未能达到目标后再次请求捐款。


近义词：re-request, solicit again, ask again, plead again


反义词：withdraw, retract, deny


词性：动词
