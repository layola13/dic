narcotic:/nɑːrˈkɒtɪk/ | n. adj. |麻醉剂；麻醉的；使人昏睡的 |A narcotic is a drug that induces sleep or stupor and that in higher doses causes narcosis.  Narcotics are often used medically to relieve pain or induce anesthesia, but they are also highly addictive and subject to strict regulation. |例句：Heroin is a powerful narcotic. 海洛因是一种强效麻醉剂。

The doctor prescribed a narcotic to relieve his post-operative pain. 医生开了麻醉药来缓解他的术后疼痛。


近义词：drug, opiate, anesthetic, sedative |反义词：stimulant


词性：名词，形容词
