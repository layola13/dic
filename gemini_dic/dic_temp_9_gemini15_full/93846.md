immobile:/ɪˈmoʊbaɪl/ | adj. | 不动的；静止的；不能移动的 | Unable to move; stationary.  | The patient remained immobile after the surgery.  手术后病人一动不动。| stationary, fixed, still | mobile, movable, dynamic | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remains immobile. = 他保持不动。
The car is immobile due to a flat tire. = 汽车因为轮胎没气而无法移动。

一般过去时:
The statue remained immobile for centuries. = 这尊雕像几个世纪以来一直一动不动。
She lay immobile after the accident. = 事故后她一动不动地躺着。

现在完成时:
The car has been immobile since yesterday. = 这辆车从昨天开始就无法移动了。
The project has remained immobile due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，项目进展停滞不前。

过去完成时:

The soldiers had remained immobile throughout the night. = 士兵们整夜都一动不动。

将来时:
The robot will remain immobile during the test. = 机器人在测试期间将保持不动。
The vehicle will be immobile until the repairs are completed. = 车辆将在修理完成之前无法移动。

虚拟语气:
If the car were immobile, we would have to call a tow truck. = 如果车无法移动，我们就得叫拖车。

被动语态:

The patient was kept immobile to prevent further injury. = 病人被固定不动以防止进一步受伤。

疑问句:

Is the statue immobile? = 这尊雕像是静止不动的吗？

否定句:
The object is not immobile; it is moving slightly. = 这个物体并非静止不动；它在轻微移动。
The situation is not immobile; it's changing rapidly. = 情况并非静止不变；它正在迅速变化。
