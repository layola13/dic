rebottle:/ˌriːˈbɒtəl/| vt.|重新装瓶；再次装瓶|To put (a liquid) back into bottles after it has been emptied or transferred.|The winery had to rebottle the wine after discovering a flaw in the initial bottling process.  酒厂在发现最初装瓶过程中存在缺陷后不得不重新装瓶。|They rebottled the leftover juice into smaller containers. 他们把剩下的果汁重新装进更小的容器里。|refill, repackage|discard, empty|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
We rebottle the wine every year. = 我们每年都重新装瓶葡萄酒。
He rebottles the old olive oil into smaller jars. = 他把旧的橄榄油重新装进较小的罐子里。

一般过去时:
They rebottled the soda after the leak. = 泄漏后，他们重新装瓶苏打水。
She rebottled the homemade jam into new jars. = 她把自制的果酱重新装进新的罐子里。

现在完成时:
We have rebottled all the wine for this year's harvest. = 我们已经为今年的收成重新装瓶了所有的葡萄酒。
The company has rebottled thousands of bottles due to a labeling error. = 由于标签错误，该公司已经重新装瓶了数千瓶。

过去完成时:
They had rebottled the entire stock before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经重新装瓶了全部库存。
The factory had rebottled the product before the recall. = 工厂在召回之前已经重新装瓶了产品。

将来时:
We will rebottle the cider next week. = 我们下周将重新装瓶苹果酒。
The brewery will rebottle the contaminated beer. = 啤酒厂将重新装瓶受污染的啤酒。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would rebottle the entire shipment. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新装瓶整批货物。
I wish we had rebottled the wine sooner; it's gone bad. = 我真希望我们早点重新装瓶葡萄酒；它变质了。

被动语态:
The wine was rebottled due to a faulty seal. = 由于密封不良，葡萄酒被重新装瓶。
The juice is being rebottled in a new facility. = 果汁正在新的工厂里重新装瓶。

疑问句:
Will the company rebottle the affected product? = 公司会重新装瓶受影响的产品吗？
Have they rebottled the old wine yet? = 他们已经重新装瓶旧葡萄酒了吗？

否定句:
We did not rebottle the beer; it was too late. = 我们没有重新装瓶啤酒；太晚了。
They will not rebottle the damaged goods. = 他们不会重新装瓶损坏的货物。
