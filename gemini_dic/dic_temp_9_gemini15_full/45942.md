cutworm:/ˈkʌtwɜːrm/| n.| 切根虫，夜盗虫 | a larva of certain moths that cuts off and feeds on the stems of plants, especially young seedlings.  |例句：The cutworms have devastated the farmer's young corn crop. 切根虫摧毁了农民的幼玉米作物。

例句：We found several cutworms munching on the lettuce leaves. 我们发现几只切根虫正在啃食莴苣叶。


近义词：armyworm,  wireworm (depending on the specific species)


反义词：无


词性：名词
