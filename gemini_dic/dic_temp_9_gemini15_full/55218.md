dissever:/dɪˈsɛvər/ | vt. | 切断；分离；使分离 | To sever; to separate; to divide. The word implies a forceful or complete separation, often of something that was previously joined or united. | Example: The surgeon had to dissever the artery to stop the bleeding.  外科医生不得不切断动脉以止血。 The storm dissevered the power lines.暴风雨切断了电力线路。|近义词：sever, separate, divide, part | 反义词：unite, join, connect | 动词
