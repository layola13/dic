nontransitional: /ˌnɒnˌtrænzˈɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非过渡的，非过渡性的 | Not involving or characterized by transition; lacking a transition.  This adjective describes something that doesn't involve a change or shift from one state or condition to another. It often implies a lack of gradual change or a sudden, abrupt shift. |

例句：

The nontransitional nature of the political system hindered reform.  |  这个政治制度的非过渡性阻碍了改革。

The company's nontransitional approach to technology led to its downfall. | 公司在技术方面非过渡性的做法导致了它的垮台。


近义词：abrupt, sudden, discontinuous, direct, immediate


反义词：transitional, gradual, progressive, evolving


词性: adjective
