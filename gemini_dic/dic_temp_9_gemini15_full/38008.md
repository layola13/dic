complimentative: /ˌkɒmplɪˈmɛntəri/ | /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmɛntəri/ | adj. |赞美的；称赞的；褒奖的 |Expressing praise or admiration.  |例句：He received many complimentative remarks about his performance. 他收到了许多关于他表演的赞美之词。

例句：Her speech was filled with complimentative words for the team's efforts. 她的讲话充满了对团队努力的赞扬之词。


近义词：flattering, praising, laudatory, commendatory | 反义词：critical, disparaging, derogatory


词源:  Derived from the verb "compliment," ultimately from the French word "compliment," meaning "to complete" or "to fulfill."  The -ative suffix transforms the verb into an adjective, indicating a quality or characteristic of something.
