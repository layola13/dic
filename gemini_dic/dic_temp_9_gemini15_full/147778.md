overpromotes: /ˌoʊvərprəˈmoʊts/ | vt. | 过度推销；给予过高的评价 | To promote or advertise something too much or to a degree that is excessive or unrealistic; to give someone or something too much praise or importance. | 例句：The company overpromotes its products, leading to disappointed customers. 公司过度推销其产品，导致客户失望。 The media overpromotes minor celebrities, creating unrealistic expectations. 媒体过度宣传一些小有名气的名人，造成了不切实际的期望。 | 近义词：hype, oversell, exaggerate, puff up | 反义词：undersell, downplay, undervalue | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overpromote their new software. = 他们过度推销他们的新软件。
The manager overpromotes his team's achievements. = 经理对团队的成就给予了过高的评价。


一般过去时:
The marketing team overpromoted the product last year. = 营销团队去年过度推销了该产品。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
