fumble:/ˈfʌmbəl/ | vt. vi. n. | 笨手笨脚地摸索；失误； fumble (at something) |to do something clumsily; to drop or mishandle something |例句：He fumbled in his pocket for his keys. 他笨手笨脚地在口袋里摸索钥匙。 He fumbled the ball and lost the game. 他没接住球，输掉了比赛。|近义词：grope, botch, blunder |反义词：master, succeed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fumbles with the lock. = 他笨手笨脚地弄锁。
She fumbles her words during the speech. = 她在演讲时词不达意。


一般过去时:
I fumbled the catch. = 我没接住球。
