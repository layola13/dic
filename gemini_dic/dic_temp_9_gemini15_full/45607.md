curried:/ˈkʌriːd/| adj.|咖喱味的，用咖喱调味的|Seasoned or flavored with curry.  The dish is characterized by its rich, aromatic, and often spicy flavors derived from the curry powder or paste.|例句：We had a delicious curried chicken for dinner.  我们晚餐吃了一道美味的咖喱鸡。
例句：The curried vegetables were a perfect accompaniment to the rice.  咖喱蔬菜是米饭的完美搭配。|近义词：spiced, seasoned|反义词：plain, unseasoned|形容词

词源:
From the word "curry," which itself comes from Tamil kari "sauce, relish," ultimately from Proto-Dravidian *kaṛi-.  The verb use developed later, reflecting the common practice of using curry to flavor food.
