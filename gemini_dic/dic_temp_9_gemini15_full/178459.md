reconstructed:/ˌriːkənˈstrʌktɪd/ | adj. | 重建的；被重建的；经过改建的 |  Describes something that has been built or created again after being damaged or destroyed, or something that has been formed again from separate parts.  It can also refer to something that has been reinterpreted or reformulated based on available evidence. |


例句：
The reconstructed building looks almost identical to the original. = 重建的建筑物看起来几乎与原建筑物一模一样。

The historian offered a reconstructed narrative of the events. = 历史学家对这些事件提供了一个重建的叙述。


近义词：rebuilt, remade, restored, reformed


反义词：destroyed, demolished, ruined


词性：形容词
