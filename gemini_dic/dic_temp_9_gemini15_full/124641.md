mouthguard: /ˈmaʊθɡɑːrd/ | n. | 齿套；护齿；口器 | A mouthguard is a protective device worn over the teeth and gums to protect them from injury.  It is commonly used in sports such as boxing, hockey, and football, as well as by people who grind their teeth at night (bruxism). | Example: He always wears a mouthguard when he plays rugby. 他打橄榄球时总是戴着护齿。  She wears a mouthguard at night to prevent teeth grinding. 她晚上戴护齿以防止磨牙。 | 近义词：dental protector, mouth protector | 反义词：None.
