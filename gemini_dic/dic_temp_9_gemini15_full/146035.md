overdried:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪd/| adj.|过度干燥的，干透的|Excessively dried; dried beyond the desired or optimal point.  The word combines "over-" (meaning excessively) with "dried" (the past participle of the verb "to dry").|例句：The cake was overdried and crumbled easily.  这个蛋糕太干了，很容易碎。

The herbs were overdried, losing much of their flavor.  这些药草晒得太干了，失去了很多香味。|近义词：parched, desiccated, dehydrated|反义词：moist, damp, hydrated
