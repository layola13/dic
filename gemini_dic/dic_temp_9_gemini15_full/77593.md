geek:/ɡiːk/| n. adj.|怪人，书呆子；对某事物极度痴迷的人|A geek is a person who is passionate about a particular subject or activity, often to the point of obsession. They are typically highly knowledgeable and skilled in their area of interest, but may also be perceived as socially awkward or unconventional.  |例句：He's a total computer geek. 他是个十足的电脑迷。 He is a history geek, spending hours in the library. 他是个历史迷，在图书馆一待就是几个小时。|近义词：nerd, dork, aficionado, enthusiast|反义词：social butterfly, popular person
