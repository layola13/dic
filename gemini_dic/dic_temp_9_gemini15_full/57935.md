drinkableness: /ˈdrɪŋkəb(ə)lnəs/ | n. | 可饮用性，适于饮用 | The quality or state of being drinkable; suitability for drinking.  It considers factors such as taste, safety, and palatability. | Example: The drinkableness of the water was questionable after the storm.  暴风雨过后，水的可饮用性令人怀疑。 The drinkableness of the well water is now being tested. 井水的可饮用性正在接受检测。| 近义词：potability, drinkability | 反义词：undrinkability, unsuitability for drinking | 名词
