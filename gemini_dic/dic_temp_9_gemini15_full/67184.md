express:/ɪkˈsprɛs/ |/ɪkˈspres/| vt. n. adj.|表达，快递，快车|to convey a thought, feeling, or idea|例句：She expressed her gratitude in a heartfelt letter. 她在一封充满真情的信中表达了她的感激之情。|例句：The package was sent by express mail.包裹通过快递邮寄。|近义词：convey, communicate, declare|反义词：suppress, conceal, repress|动词，名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expresses his opinions freely. = 他自由地表达自己的观点。
They express their love through actions. = 他们通过行动表达爱意。

一般过去时:
She expressed her concerns to the manager. = 她向经理表达了她的担忧。
He expressed a desire to travel. = 他表达了想要旅行的愿望。

现在完成时:
I have expressed my interest in the job. = 我已经表达了我对这份工作的兴趣。
They have expressed their dissatisfaction with the service. = 他们已经表达了对服务的失望。

过去完成时:
She had already expressed her reservations before the meeting. = 会议之前她已经表达了她的保留意见。
He had expressed his apologies earlier. = 他早些时候已经表达了他的歉意。

将来时:
I will express my appreciation in a thank-you note. = 我将在感谢信中表达我的感激之情。
They will express their views at the conference. = 他们将在会议上表达他们的观点。

虚拟语气:
If I could express myself better, I would. = 如果我能更好地表达自己就好了。
I wish I could express my feelings more clearly. = 我希望我能更清晰地表达我的感受。

被动语态:
His ideas were expressed eloquently. = 他的想法表达得很流畅。
The company's concerns were expressed to the board. = 公司的担忧已向董事会表达。

疑问句:
How can I best express my sympathy? = 我怎样才能最好地表达我的同情？
Does this painting express your feelings? = 这幅画表达了你的感情吗？

否定句:
He couldn't express his emotions. = 他无法表达他的情绪。
She didn't express any interest in the project. = 她对这个项目没有表达任何兴趣。
