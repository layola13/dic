obviates:/ˈɒbvieɪts/ /ˈɑːbvieɪts/| vt.|消除；使不必要；避免|To remove a need or difficulty.|The new system obviates the need for manual data entry. 新系统消除了手动数据录入的需要。|近义词：eliminate, remove, prevent, avoid|反义词：necessitate, require


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This new software obviates the need for extra staff. 这个新软件消除了对额外员工的需求。
The improved design obviates many of the previous problems. 改进后的设计避免了许多以前的问题。


一般过去时:
The invention of the telephone obviated the need for telegrams. 电话的发明使电报的需求消失了。
Her careful planning obviated any potential delays. 她细致的计划避免了任何潜在的延误。


现在完成时:
The automation of the process has obviated many manual tasks. 过程的自动化已经消除了许多手工任务。
Technological advancements have obviated the need for many traditional jobs. 技术进步已经消除了许多传统工作的需求。


过去完成时:
They had obviated the risk by taking precautions beforehand. 他们事先采取了预防措施，从而避免了风险。
The early warning system had obviated a potential disaster. 预警系统避免了一场潜在的灾难。


将来时:
This new technology will obviate the need for frequent maintenance. 这项新技术将消除频繁维护的需要。
Improved infrastructure will obviate many transportation problems.  改进的基础设施将解决许多交通问题。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have obviated the crisis. 如果我们早点行动，就能避免这场危机。
If they had better communication, they could have obviated the misunderstanding. 如果他们沟通更好，就能避免误解。


被动语态:
The problem was obviated by a simple solution.  这个问题被一个简单的解决方案解决了。
The need for extra funding was obviated by increased efficiency.  通过提高效率，消除了对额外资金的需求。


疑问句:
Does this new policy obviate the need for further regulations? 这项新政策是否消除了对进一步规定的需求？
Will this upgrade obviate the current software issues? 此次升级能否解决当前的软件问题？


否定句:
The new regulations did not obviate the need for more stringent controls. 新规章并没有消除对更严格控制的需求。
The proposed solution did not obviate all the problems.  提出的解决方案并没有解决所有问题。
