indamaged:/ɪnˈdæmɪdʒd/ | adj. | 受损的，损坏的 | Damaged or impaired.  This word is not a standard English word; it's likely a misspelling of "damaged".  Therefore, the following analysis refers to "damaged". | The car was damaged in the accident.  这辆车在事故中受损了。 |  broken, impaired, harmed, spoiled | undamaged, repaired, intact, perfect | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm damages the crops every year. = 暴风雨每年都会损害庄稼。
The constant noise damages his hearing. = 持续的噪音损害了他的听力。


一般过去时:
The earthquake damaged many buildings. = 地震损坏了许多建筑物。
The heavy rain damaged the roof of the house. = 大雨损坏了房子的屋顶。


现在完成时:
The hurricane has damaged many coastal towns. = 飓风已经损坏了许多沿海城镇。
The constant use has damaged the machine. = 持续使用已经损坏了机器。


过去完成时:
They had damaged the car before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们就已经损坏了汽车。
The project had been damaged by the lack of funding. = 该项目由于缺乏资金而受到了损害。


将来时:
The strong winds will damage the trees. = 强风会损坏树木。
The new policy may damage the company's reputation. = 新政策可能会损害公司的声誉。


虚拟语气:
If the company had invested more, they wouldn't have damaged their reputation. = 如果公司投资更多，他们就不会损害他们的声誉。
I wish I hadn't damaged the antique vase. = 我真希望我没有损坏那个古董花瓶。


被动语态:
The bridge was damaged during the flood. = 桥梁在洪水中受损。
The reputation of the company was damaged by the scandal. = 该公司的声誉因丑闻而受损。


疑问句:

否定句:
The car wasn't damaged in the accident. = 这辆车在事故中没有受损。
The project wasn't damaged by the delay. = 该项目没有因为延误而受损。
