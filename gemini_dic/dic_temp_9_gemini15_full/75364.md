frothy:/ˈfrɔːθi/| adj. |泡沫的，起泡的，轻浮的 |Containing or covered with a mass of small bubbles; foamy.  Characterized by superficiality or lack of substance.|The frothy cappuccino was delicious. 这杯泡沫丰富的卡布奇诺很好喝。The politician's speech was full of frothy promises. 这位政治家的讲话充满了轻浮的承诺。|foamy, bubbly, effervescent |flat, serious, substantial|形容词
