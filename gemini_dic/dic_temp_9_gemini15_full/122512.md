词源:
The word "mockingbird" comes from the bird's ability to mimic, or "mock," the sounds of other birds and animals.  The "mock" part is derived from Old French *moquer, meaning "to mock" or "to mimic," ultimately from Vulgar Latin *moccari. "Bird" is of course the common word for avian creature.
