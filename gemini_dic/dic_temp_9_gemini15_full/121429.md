misinforms:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmz/| vt.|错误地告知，误导|To give someone false or inaccurate information.|The advertisement misinformed the public about the product's capabilities.  这条广告误导了公众对产品性能的认识。|He deliberately misinformed the police about his whereabouts. 他故意向警方隐瞒了他的下落。|informs, instructs| clarifies, corrects

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misinforms his clients about the contract details. 他向客户提供关于合同细节的错误信息。
The company misinforms the public about its environmental record.  这家公司误导公众对其环保记录的认知。


一般过去时:
The news report misinformed the public about the cause of the accident. 新闻报道误导了公众对事故原因的理解。
She misinformed her boss about the project's progress. 她向老板错误地汇报了项目的进展。


现在完成时:
The media has misinformed the public about the election results. 媒体误导了公众对选举结果的认知。
They have misinformed us about the risks involved.  他们已经向我们提供了关于相关风险的错误信息。


过去完成时:
He realized he had misinformed the committee about the budget. 他意识到他之前向委员会提供了错误的预算信息。
They discovered that they had misinformed the investors about the company's financial situation. 他们发现他们之前向投资者提供了关于公司财务状况的错误信息。


将来时:
The government will misinform the people about the new policy if they are not careful. 如果政府不小心，他们将会在新的政策方面误导人民。
He will likely misinform his colleagues again about the deadline. 他很可能会再次向同事们提供关于截止日期的错误信息。


虚拟语气:
If he had not misinformed the judge, the trial would have been different. 如果他没有误导法官，审判的结果就会有所不同。
I wish I hadn't misinformed my friend about the party. 我真希望我没有向我的朋友提供关于派对的错误信息。


被动语态:
The public was misinformed about the severity of the situation. 公众被误导了局势的严重性。
The shareholders were misinformed about the company's losses. 股东们被误导了公司损失的状况。


疑问句:
Did he misinform you about the meeting? 他是否在会议方面误导了你？
Was the information misinformed intentionally or by accident?  这些信息是被故意误导还是意外造成的？


否定句:
He did not misinform anyone about the details. 他没有向任何人提供关于细节的错误信息。
The company did not intentionally misinform its customers. 这家公司没有故意误导其客户。
