词源:
The word "dissiliency" is derived from the Latin prefix "dis-" (meaning "apart" or "away") and the verb "silire" (meaning "to leap" or "to spring").  Therefore, the core meaning relates to something leaping or springing apart.  The "-ency" suffix indicates a state or quality.
