rousted:/ˈraʊstɪd/| v. | 粗暴地叫醒；驱逐；驱赶；搜查 | To rouse someone from sleep, typically in a forceful or abrupt manner; to forcibly remove someone from a place; to search a place thoroughly. |例句：The police rousted the protesters from the park.  警察粗暴地将抗议者赶出了公园。  The early morning sun rousted me from my sleep.清晨的阳光把我从睡梦中惊醒。|近义词：ejected, expelled, evicted, awakened, roused |反义词：soothed, calmed, lulled
