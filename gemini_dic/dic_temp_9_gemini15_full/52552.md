diffract:/dɪˈfrækt/| vt.|衍射；绕射|To cause (a wave) to bend as it passes an obstacle or through an aperture.|例句：The light diffracted as it passed through the narrow slit. 光线通过狭窄的缝隙时发生了衍射。|例句：The sound waves diffracted around the corner of the building. 声波绕过建筑物的拐角发生了衍射。|近义词：bend, deflect,refract|反义词：focus, concentrate|动词
