coagulation:/koʊæɡjuˈleɪʃən/| n. |凝结；凝固；血液凝固|The process of changing from a liquid to a solid or semi-solid state, especially of blood.  This involves the clumping together of particles.  It is a vital process in blood clotting. |Example: Blood coagulation is essential to stop bleeding. 血液凝固对于止血至关重要。

The rapid coagulation of the spilled milk prevented its use.  溢出的牛奶迅速凝固，无法再使用了。|coalescence, clotting, thickening|liquefaction, thinning|名词
