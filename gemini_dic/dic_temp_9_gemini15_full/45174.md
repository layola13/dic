cuffs:/kʌfs/ | n. |袖口，手铐 |  Cuffs are the ends of sleeves, or a pair of metal rings connected by a chain, used for restraining wrists. Cuffs can refer to the fabric at the end of a sleeve or to a restraining device used by law enforcement. |例句：He turned up his cuffs to avoid getting them dirty. 他卷起了袖口以免弄脏。|例句：The police officer handcuffed the suspect. 警官用手铐铐住了嫌疑犯。|近义词：wristbands, bracelets (for fabric cuffs); manacles (for restraining devices) |反义词：None directly applicable.
