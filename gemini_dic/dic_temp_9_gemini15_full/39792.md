consolation:/ˌkɒnsəˈleɪʃən/| n. |安慰，慰藉 |The act of consoling; comfort given to someone who is suffering.  Consolation is offered to alleviate sorrow or distress.|例句：She found consolation in her faith. 她从信仰中找到了安慰。|例句：His friends offered him consolation after the loss of his job.  他的朋友在他失业后给了他安慰。|近义词：comfort, solace, solace, sympathy |反义词：distress, suffering, misery
