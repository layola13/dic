hexadecimal:/ˌhɛksəˈdɛsɪməl/| adj. |十六进制的，十六进位的|Relating to or denoting a system of numbers that has 16 as its base.  In this system, the digits 0 to 9 represent the values zero to nine, and the letters A to F represent the values ten to fifteen.|Example:  The hexadecimal color code for red is #FF0000. 十六进制颜色代码为红色的是#FF0000.


The color is represented by a hexadecimal code.  这种颜色用十六进制代码表示。


近义词:base-16, sexadecimal|反义词:decimal (十进制), binary (二进制)|词性:形容词
