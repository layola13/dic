reverse:/rɪˈvɜːrs/| /rɪˈvɜːrs/| v. n. adj.|颠倒；反转；逆转；反面|to change the direction, order, position, result, etc. of something to its opposite; the opposite; turned in the opposite direction|例句：The car reversed into the garage.  车子倒车进了车库。

例句：He reversed his decision after considering the consequences. 他考虑了后果后改变了主意。


近义词：invert, overturn,  upend | 反义词：forward, advance, progress


词性：动词，名词，形容词
