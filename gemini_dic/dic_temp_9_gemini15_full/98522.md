instead:/ɪnˈstɛd/| adv.|代替；反而；而不是|In place of; as a substitute; in contrast or opposition.|例句：I didn't go to the party; I stayed home instead. 我没有去参加聚会；我待在家里了。|例句：He offered me chocolate, but I took an apple instead. 他给我巧克力，但我拿了一个苹果。|近义词：in place of, rather, alternatively|反义词：besides, additionally


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eats rice instead of bread. 他吃米饭而不是面包。
She uses a pen instead of a pencil. 她用钢笔而不是铅笔。


一般过去时:
They went to the beach instead of the park. 他们去了海滩而不是公园。
He chose coffee instead of tea. 他选择了咖啡而不是茶。


现在完成时:
I have chosen tea instead of coffee. 我已经选择了茶而不是咖啡。
She has used a computer instead of a typewriter. 她已经使用了电脑而不是打字机。


过去完成时:
He had taken the bus instead of the train. 他已经乘坐了公共汽车而不是火车。
They had eaten chicken instead of fish. 他们已经吃了鸡肉而不是鱼。


将来时:
I will go to the library instead of the cinema. 我将去图书馆而不是电影院。
She will use her bike instead of her car. 她将使用她的自行车而不是她的汽车。


虚拟语气:
I would choose a different path instead. 我会选择一条不同的路。
It would be better to study instead of playing games.  学习比玩游戏更好。


被动语态:
A bus was used instead of a car. 使用了公共汽车而不是汽车。
The task was completed instead of being postponed. 任务完成了而不是被推迟了。


疑问句:
Would you prefer tea instead? 你更喜欢茶吗？
Should we use this method instead? 我们应该使用这种方法吗？


否定句:
He didn't use a knife, he used a spoon instead. 他没有用刀，而是用了勺子。
She didn't buy a dress; she bought a skirt instead. 她没有买连衣裙；她买了一条裙子。


PHRASE->
instead of  代替

as an alternative  作为替代
in preference to  优先于
