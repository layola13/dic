cop-a-plea: /ˌkɒp ə pliː/ | v. |认罪（以求从轻发落）| To plead guilty to a lesser charge in order to receive a reduced sentence.  |  The defendant decided to cop a plea to avoid a lengthy trial.  被告决定认罪以避免漫长的审判。 | plead guilty, admit guilt | deny guilt, fight charges | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cops a plea in most cases. = 他在大多数情况下认罪。
They cop pleas to get shorter sentences. = 他们认罪以获得较短的刑期。


一般过去时:
The criminal coped a plea to manslaughter. = 罪犯对过失杀人认罪。
She coped a plea and avoided a long prison sentence. = 她认罪并避免了漫长的监禁。


现在完成时:
He has coped a plea in this case. = 他在本案中认罪了。
They have coped pleas to multiple charges. = 他们对多项指控认罪了。


过去完成时:
He had coped a plea before the trial began. = 在审判开始之前，他已经认罪了。
She had coped a plea, hoping for leniency. = 她已经认罪，希望得到宽大处理。


将来时:
