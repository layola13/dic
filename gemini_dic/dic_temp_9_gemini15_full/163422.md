preconsult:/ˌpriːkənˈsʌlt/| v.|预先咨询，事先咨询 |To consult beforehand; to seek advice or information in advance.  |例句：We preconsulted with the architect before starting construction. 我们在开工前预先咨询了建筑师。|例句：It's advisable to preconsult your doctor before starting any new medication. 开始服用任何新药之前，最好先咨询你的医生。|近义词：consult beforehand, seek prior advice, pre-advise |反义词：postconsult (not a common word)


USAGE->

We preconsult our clients on project details. = 我们预先咨询我们的客户关于项目细节。
