dratting:/ˈdrætɪŋ/| v.|草拟，起草|The act of preparing a preliminary version of a document, plan, or design.|例句：He spent the morning dratting the contract. 他上午花了时间起草合同。|近义词：drafting, sketching, outlining|反义词：finalizing, completing


USAGE->

He is dratting a new proposal. = 他正在起草一份新的提案。
She drafts reports every week. = 她每周都起草报告。
