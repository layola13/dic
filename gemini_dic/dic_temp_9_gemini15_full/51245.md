destructively:/dɪˈstrʌktɪvli/ | adv. | 破坏性地；毁灭性地 | In a way that causes destruction or damage.  | 例句：The hurricane destructively impacted the coastal communities.  飓风对沿海社区造成了毁灭性的影响。

例句：The earthquake destructively altered the landscape. 地震破坏性地改变了地貌。


近义词：ruinously, devastatingly, damagingly

反义词：constructively, beneficially, helpfully
