enveloped:/ɪnˈvɛləpt/ | vt. | 包裹；包含；笼罩 | To cover or surround completely; to enclose.  |例句：The village was enveloped in mist.  村庄被雾笼罩着。 The letter was enveloped in a brown paper. 这封信用牛皮纸包着。|近义词：covered, surrounded, enclosed, shrouded | 反义词：uncovered, exposed, revealed | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blanket envelops the child. = 毯子裹着孩子。
The fog envelops the mountain. = 雾笼罩着山峰。

一般过去时:
The darkness enveloped the city. = 黑暗笼罩着城市。
She enveloped the gift in wrapping paper. = 她用包装纸把礼物包起来。

现在完成时:
The snow has enveloped the entire landscape. = 雪覆盖了整个景色。
Mystery has enveloped the case. = 这个案件笼罩着神秘。


过去完成时:
The house had been enveloped in flames. = 房子已经被大火吞噬。
A sense of dread had enveloped her. = 一种恐惧感笼罩着她。

将来时:
The storm will envelop the coast. = 暴风雨将席卷海岸。
Darkness will envelop the earth. = 黑暗将笼罩大地。

虚拟语气:
If the fog enveloped the whole city, we couldn’t see anything. = 如果雾笼罩了整个城市，我们就什么也看不见了。
If I had been enveloped by fear, I would have run away. = 如果我被恐惧笼罩，我会逃跑的。

疑问句:

否定句:
The letter wasn't enveloped properly. = 信没有包好。
The truth was not enveloped in mystery. = 真相并没有被神秘所掩盖。
