sandbank:/ˈsændbæŋk/| n.|沙洲，沙滩|A sandbank is a ridge of sand, especially one that is submerged or partly submerged in the sea.  It's formed by the accumulation of sand deposited by tides, currents, and waves.|例句：The ship ran aground on a sandbank.  船搁浅在沙洲上。

例句：We saw many seabirds resting on the sandbank at low tide. 退潮时，我们看到许多海鸟在沙洲上休息。


近义词：sandbar, shoal|反义词：deep water, open sea|词性:名词
