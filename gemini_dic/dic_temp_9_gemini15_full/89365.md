homonymic:/ˌhɒməˈnɪmɪk/ | adj. | 同音异义的 | Relating to or characterized by homonyms; having the same sound but a different meaning.  | Example:  The words "bank" (river bank) and "bank" (financial institution) are homonymic.  同音词“bank”（河岸）和“bank”（金融机构）是同音异义词。|近义词：polysemous (when referring to words with multiple related meanings)|反义词：synonymous
