campanology:/ˌkæmpəˈnɒlədʒi/| n. |钟铃学；钟声学|The scientific study of bells, especially church bells, their ringing, and their history.  It encompasses the art and science of bell ringing, including the tuning, casting, and playing of bells. |The campanology society held a demonstration of change ringing. 钟声学会进行了一场变化鸣钟的示范。  |近义词：bell ringing, bell studies|反义词:无
