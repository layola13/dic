chromotype:/ˈkroʊməˌtaɪp/ | n. | 色素型，色型 | A chromotype is a classification of individuals based on their skin tone, hair color, and eye color.  It's often used in the context of  personal color analysis, which aims to determine the most flattering colors to wear based on one's natural coloring.  The term is also sometimes used in biology, referring to the color pattern of an organism. | Example:  Determining your chromotype can help you choose clothing and makeup that enhance your natural beauty.  | 近义词：color type, skin tone type | 反义词：None specifically.
