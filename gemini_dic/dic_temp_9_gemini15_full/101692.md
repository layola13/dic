irremovable: /ˌɪrɪˈmuːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可移动的；无法去除的 |  Impossible to remove or dislodge.  | The stain was irremovable.  |近义词：fixed, permanent, unmovable | 反义词：removable, movable


The stain was irremovable, despite repeated attempts at cleaning.  = 尽管反复尝试清洗，但污渍仍然无法去除。

The irremovable bolt required specialized tools to loosen. = 不可移动的螺栓需要特殊的工具才能松动。


词源:

* **ir-**:  A prefix meaning "not" or "without."
* **remove**: From Old French *remuer* "to move, stir," from Vulgar Latin *remutare*, from Latin *re-*, intensive prefix + *movere* "to move."
* **-able**: A suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being."
