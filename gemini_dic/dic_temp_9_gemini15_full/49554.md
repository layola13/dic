demeaned:/dɪˈmiːnd/ | vt. |贬低；降低…的地位；使丢脸 | To lower the status or reputation of someone or something; to cause someone to feel shame or humiliation. |例句：His behavior demeaned his family. 他的行为使他的家人蒙羞。  His constant criticism demeaned her efforts. 他不断的批评贬低了她的努力。|近义词：humiliate, degrade, disgrace, belittle |反义词：elevate, exalt, honor, praise |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demeans his colleagues with his constant sarcasm. = 他用不断的讽刺贬低他的同事。
She demeans herself by gossiping about others. = 她通过说别人的闲话贬低自己。


一般过去时:
The media demeaned the athlete's achievement with their negative reporting. = 媒体通过负面报道贬低了这位运动员的成就。
He demeaned himself by lying to the police. = 他对警察撒谎，从而使自己丢脸。


现在完成时:
His actions have demeaned the entire organization. = 他的行为已经使整个组织蒙羞。
She has demeaned herself by accepting the bribe. = 她接受贿赂，从而使自己丢脸。


过去完成时:
He had demeaned himself so much that no one respected him anymore. = 他已经把自己贬低到如此地步，以至于没有人再尊重他了。
They had demeaned the project by cutting corners. = 他们通过偷工减料贬低了这个项目。


将来时:
His arrogance will demean him in the eyes of his superiors. = 他的傲慢会在他的上司眼中贬低他。
This scandal will demean the reputation of the company. = 这场丑闻将会损害公司的声誉。


虚拟语气:
If he had not acted so arrogantly, he would not have demeaned himself. = 如果他不那么傲慢，他就不会使自己丢脸。
I wish he hadn't demeaned her in front of everyone. = 我真希望他没在大家面前贬低她。


被动语态:
He felt demeaned by her criticism. = 他觉得她的批评使他丢脸。
The team was demeaned by the loss. = 球队因失败而蒙羞。


疑问句:
Does his behavior demean the profession? = 他的行为是否贬低了这个职业？
Do you feel demeaned by their actions? = 你觉得他们的行为让你丢脸吗？


否定句:
He did not demean his opponent, even after the victory. = 即使在胜利之后，他也没有贬低他的对手。
She refused to let others demean her accomplishments. = 她拒绝让别人贬低她的成就。
