scurried:/ˈskʌrid/| v.|疾跑，匆匆忙忙地跑|to move quickly and hurriedly, typically because of fear or panic.|例句：The mice scurried away when the cat appeared.  老鼠看到猫出现后就匆匆逃跑了。|例句：She scurried to catch the bus before it left. 她匆匆忙忙赶上公共汽车，以免错过。|近义词：dashed, hurried, rushed, darted|反义词：sauntered, strolled, ambled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scurries to work every morning. = 他每天早上都匆匆忙忙地去上班。
The children scurry around the playground. = 孩子们在操场上跑来跑去。

一般过去时:
The rabbit scurried into its burrow. = 兔子窜进了它的洞穴。
I scurried to finish my work before the deadline. = 我赶紧在截止日期前完成工作。

现在完成时:
The mice have scurried away. = 老鼠已经逃跑了。
She has scurried through her chores. = 她已经匆匆忙忙地完成了家务。

过去完成时:
The thief had scurried away before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经逃跑了。
They had scurried to find shelter before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经匆匆忙忙地寻找庇护所。

将来时:
The students will scurry to their classes after the bell rings. = 铃声响后，学生们将匆匆忙忙地去上课。
He will scurry to the airport to catch his flight. = 他将匆忙赶往机场赶飞机。

虚拟语气:
If I had seen him, I would have scurried away. = 如果我看到他，我会赶紧跑开。
I wish I hadn't scurried so much; I could have been more organized. = 我真希望我没有那么匆忙，我本来可以更井井有条。

被动语态:  (被动语态较少用，通常用主动语态)
The documents were scurried away. = 文件被匆匆藏了起来。(不太自然)
The children were scurried into the house before the rain started. = 孩子们在雨开始下之前被匆匆赶进了屋子。(不太自然，更倾向于"were rushed")


疑问句:
Did the cat scurry away? = 猫跑开了吗？
Why did you scurry off so quickly? = 你为什么跑得那么快？


否定句:
The dog didn't scurry; it walked calmly. = 狗没有跑；它平静地走着。
He didn't scurry, but took his time. = 他没有匆忙，而是不慌不忙。
