prestigiously:/prɛˈstɪdʒəsli/| adj.|享有声望地，声名显赫地，有威望地 |  Having or showing great prestige.  It implies a high level of respect, admiration, and influence often associated with achievement, status, or reputation. |例句：The university is prestigiously known for its excellent research. 这所大学以其优秀的科研而享有盛名。

例句：He prestigiously delivered the keynote speech at the international conference. 他在国际会议上声名显赫地发表了主题演讲。


近义词：reputably, famously, influentially, prominently, respectably

反义词：obscurely, anonymously, insignificantly, humbly


词性:形容词
