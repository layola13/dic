childe:/tʃaɪld/| n. |孩子，年轻人；(诗)少年；|An archaic term for a child, often used in literature to evoke a sense of innocence or vulnerability.  It also had a more general sense of "youth" or "young person".|Example: The childe went forth to seek his fortune.  这个孩子出去寻找他的财富。|近义词：child, youth, youngster |反义词：adult, grown-up
