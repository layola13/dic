reverends:/ˈrevərəndz/| n.|尊敬的牧师们；牧师（复数）|The plural form of "reverend," a title of respect for a Christian minister.  It is used to address or refer to multiple ministers.|例句：The reverends offered prayers for the community. 尊敬的牧师们为社区祈祷。|例句： Several reverends attended the interfaith service.好几位牧师参加了跨宗教仪式。|近义词：clergymen, ministers, pastors|反义词：无
