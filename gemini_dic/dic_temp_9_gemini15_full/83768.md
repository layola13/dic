haircut:/ˈhɛərkʌt/|/ˈheərkʌt/| n. |理发；发型 |A haircut is the styling or cutting of hair.  It can refer to the act of cutting hair or the resulting style.  |例句：I need a haircut; my hair is too long.  我需要理发了；我的头发太长了。

I got a new haircut yesterday. 我昨天理了个新发型。


近义词：hairstyle, trim

反义词：hair growth (反义词较为宽泛，因为没有一个词语直接表示“不理发”的相反概念)

词性:名词
