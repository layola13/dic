salamanders: /ˈsæləˌmændərz/ | n. | 蝾螈；火蜥蜴 |  Amphibians with elongated bodies, short legs, and smooth, moist skin. They are often found near water and play a vital role in the ecosystem.  | Example: Many species of salamanders are threatened by habitat loss.  许多种类的蝾螈都受到栖息地丧失的威胁。


Example:  The children were fascinated by the brightly colored salamanders in the pond. 孩子们对池塘里色彩鲜艳的蝾螈着迷。


近义词: newt (某些种类的蝾螈)


反义词:  None (no direct antonym)


词性: 名词
