presidia:/prɪˈsɪdiə/| n.|总统府；要塞；防御工事；警卫队 |A presidium is a governing body, especially the executive committee of a political party or other organization.  It often implies a small group holding significant power. The term also refers to a fortified place or a military garrison. |例句：The presidium of the communist party controlled all aspects of the government. 共产党主席团控制着政府的方方面面。

The ancient city boasted a strong presidium, protecting it from invaders.  这座古城拥有强大的防御工事，保护它免受侵略者侵害。|近义词：executive committee, governing body, stronghold, fortress, garrison|反义词：subordinate body, undefended area
