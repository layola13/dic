desolatingly:/ˈdɛsəleɪtɪŋli/ | adv. |令人悲痛地，凄凉地 |In a way that causes desolation or misery; bleakly and depressingly.  It describes something that causes great sadness, emptiness, or despair. |例句：The landscape stretched desolatingly before them, a vast expanse of barren land.  这片荒凉的土地在他们面前无边无际地延伸着，令人感到凄凉。

The news of her death arrived desolatingly, leaving us all heartbroken.  她去世的消息令人悲痛欲绝，让我们所有人心碎。


近义词：depressingly, bleakly, miserably, sorrowfully, dishearteningly


反义词：joyfully, happily, cheerfully, comfortingly, encouragingly


词性: 副词
