distortion:/dɪˈstɔːrʃən/| n. |扭曲；变形；失真；曲解 |The alteration of the shape, size, or other characteristic of something.  It can refer to physical objects, sounds, images, or even information. |例句：The funhouse mirrors caused a comical distortion of our bodies.  哈哈镜使我们的身体发生了滑稽的变形。

The loud music caused a distortion of the speaker.  巨大的音乐导致扬声器失真。|近义词：deformation, perversion, misrepresentation |反义词：accuracy, truth, fidelity


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The loud music distorts the sound. = 大声的音乐使声音失真。
This lens distorts the image slightly. = 这个镜头稍微扭曲了图像。


一般过去时:
The earthquake distorted the building's structure. = 地震扭曲了建筑物的结构。
He distorted the facts to fit his narrative. = 他歪曲事实以适应他的叙述。


现在完成时:
The prolonged heat has distorted the wooden door. = 长时间的热浪使木门变形了。
Years of misinformation have distorted public opinion. = 多年的错误信息扭曲了公众舆论。


过去完成时:
The rumour had been distorted before reaching me. = 谣言在我听到之前就已经被歪曲了。
He had distorted the meaning of the contract. = 他曲解了合同的含义。


将来时:
The intense heat will distort the metal. = 剧烈的热量会使金属变形。
His lies will distort the truth of the matter. = 他的谎言会歪曲事情的真相。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't distorted the evidence, the trial would have been fair. = 如果他没有歪曲证据，审判就会公正。
I wish they hadn't distorted my words. = 我希望他们没有曲解我的话。


被动语态:
The image was distorted by the lens. = 图像被镜头扭曲了。
The facts have been distorted by the media. = 事实已被媒体歪曲。


疑问句:
Does this mirror distort the reflection? = 这面镜子会扭曲反射的影像吗？
Has the information been distorted intentionally? = 信息是被故意歪曲的吗？


否定句:
The painting doesn't distort the subject's features. = 这幅画并没有扭曲人物的特征。
The report didn't distort the findings of the study. = 这份报告没有歪曲研究的结果。
