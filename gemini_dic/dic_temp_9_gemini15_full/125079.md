mullahs:/ˈmʌləz/ | n. |毛拉，伊斯兰教神职人员 | A mullah is a Muslim religious leader, teacher, or scholar, often in charge of a mosque or community.  They are usually well-versed in Islamic law and theology and provide religious guidance and instruction to the community. |  The mullah delivered a passionate sermon on the importance of charity.  毛拉发表了一篇热情洋溢的关于慈善重要性的布道。 The village mullah settled the dispute between the two families. 村里的毛拉解决了这两个家庭之间的纠纷。 | imam, cleric, preacher |  layperson, atheist | 名词
