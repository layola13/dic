self-benefitting: /ˌsɛlfˈbɛnɪfɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 自利的，为自身利益的 |  Describes actions or behaviors primarily aimed at benefiting oneself, often at the expense or disregard of others.  | Example:  The self-benefitting actions of the CEO led to the company's downfall.  这家公司的倒闭是由于首席执行官的自私行为造成的。  |近义词：self-serving, egoistic, selfish, narcissistic | 反义词：altruistic, selfless, unselfish | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company's policies are self-benefitting. = 该公司的政策是自利的。
His actions are always self-benefitting. = 他的行为总是自私的。


一般过去时:
Her decision was self-benefitting and unfair. = 她的决定是自私且不公平的。
The project seemed self-benefitting to the manager. = 这个项目对经理来说似乎是自利的。


现在完成时:
His self-benefitting behavior has alienated many colleagues. = 他自私的行为疏远了许多同事。
The company has engaged in several self-benefitting practices. = 该公司参与了一些自利的行为。


过去完成时:
The evidence showed that he had engaged in self-benefitting activities. = 证据表明他曾从事过自利的活动。
It turned out that their previous actions had been self-benefitting. = 原来他们之前的行为是自私的。


将来时:
His future plans seem self-benefitting and shortsighted. = 他的未来计划似乎是自私且目光短浅的。
They will likely engage in self-benefitting behavior if unchecked. = 如果不加以制止，他们很可能会采取自私的行为。


虚拟语气:
If his actions were less self-benefitting, he would have more friends. = 如果他的行为不那么自私，他就会有更多的朋友。
I wish his proposals weren't so self-benefitting. = 我希望他的提议不要那么自私。


被动语态:
The decision was viewed as self-benefitting by many. = 许多人认为这项决定是自私的。
His actions are often seen as self-benefitting. = 他的行为常常被视为自私的。


疑问句:
Are his motives truly self-benefitting? = 他的动机真的是自私的吗？
Is this policy truly self-benefitting for the organization? = 这项政策对组织来说真的自私吗？


否定句:
His actions were not at all self-benefitting; he acted for the good of the team. = 他的行为一点也不自私；他是为了团队的利益而行动的。
The company's new strategy is not self-benefitting; it aims to benefit all stakeholders. = 公司的新战略并非自私；其目标是使所有利益相关者受益。
