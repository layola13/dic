resummoning:/ˌriːˈsʌmənɪŋ/| v. |再次召唤；重新传唤 |The act of summoning again; calling back or recalling.  It implies a previous summoning. |例句：The court ordered the resummoning of the witness. = 法院下令再次传唤证人。

例句：The resummoning of the suspect surprised everyone. = 再次传唤嫌疑人令所有人惊讶。


近义词：recalling, re-summoning, calling back, summoning again

反义词：dismissing, releasing, discharging
