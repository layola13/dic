luged:/luːdʒd/| v.|乘坐雪橇；滑雪橇|To ride on a luge.  Luge is a winter sport where a person rides down a track on a small sled.|例句：He luged down the icy slope with breathtaking speed. 他以惊人的速度在冰坡上滑雪橇。|例句：They went luging at the winter sports resort. 他们在冬季运动胜地玩雪橇。|近义词：sledded, tobogganed|反义词：None.
