lattice:/ˈlætɪs/| n. |格子，点阵，网络|A lattice is a structure consisting of a network of intersecting lines or points.  It can refer to a framework of crossed strips or bars, or a regular arrangement of points in space.  The term is used in various fields, including mathematics, physics, and architecture.|例句：The garden was shaded by a beautiful wooden lattice. 花园被一个美丽的木制格子遮蔽着。

The crystal structure of diamond is a three-dimensional lattice. 钻石的晶体结构是一个三维点阵。|近义词：grid, network, framework|反义词：无
