pianists:/ˈpiænɪsts/ | n. |钢琴家 | A person who plays the piano, especially professionally.  |Lang Lang is a world-renowned pianist.  郎朗是一位世界著名的钢琴家。  Many talented pianists participated in the competition. 许多有天赋的钢琴家参加了比赛。| musicians, performers |  (反义词无直接对应词，可考虑non-pianists)
