non-brahmanical: /ˌnɒnˌbrɑːmənɪkəl/ | adj. | 非婆罗门教的；非婆罗门教徒的 | Not pertaining to or characteristic of Brahmanism or Brahmins; outside the Brahmanical tradition.  The term refers to anything that is not part of the Brahmanical religious and social system of Hinduism. This includes beliefs, practices, social structures, and philosophies that are distinct from or opposed to the dominant Brahmanical ideology. |  The rise of non-brahmanical religious movements in India challenged the established social hierarchy.  印度非婆罗门教宗教运动的兴起挑战了既定的社会等级制度。| 近义词：non-Hindu, heterodox, heretical | 反义词：brahmanical, orthodox
