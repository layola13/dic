reenumerate:/ˌriːɪˈnuːməreɪt/| vt.|重新编号；重新列举|To number or list again; to enumerate again.|例句：We need to reenumerate the items on the list after adding new ones. 我们需要在添加新项目后重新编号列表中的项目。


例句：The professor decided to reenumerate the slides after realizing a mistake in the order. 教授在意识到顺序错误后决定重新编号幻灯片。


近义词：renumber, list again, recount


反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to leave the numbering as it is.


词性:动词
