devastatingly:/ˈdɛvəsteɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 毁灭性地；极度地；令人震惊地 |  The adverb "devastatingly" describes something that causes great destruction or damage, either physically or emotionally. It emphasizes the extent of the negative impact.  | 例句：The hurricane devastated the coastal town devastatingly.  飓风毁灭性地摧毁了沿海城镇。 The news was devastatingly sad.  这个消息令人震惊地悲伤。 | 近义词：crushing, overwhelmingly, incredibly, shockingly | 反义词：gently, mildly, slightly, reassuringly
