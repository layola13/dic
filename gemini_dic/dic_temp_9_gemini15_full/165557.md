premature:/ˈpreməˌtʃʊr/| adj.|过早的；早熟的；未成熟的|Happening, arriving, or existing before the expected, proper, or usual time.  It can refer to things that are underdeveloped or too early in their development.|例句：The baby was born prematurely.  这个婴儿早产了。
例句：His premature death shocked everyone. 他过早的去世震惊了所有人。|近义词：early, untimely, advanced, precocious|反义词：late, delayed, overdue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fruit is premature. = 水果未成熟。
His ideas are premature for this project. = 他的想法对于这个项目来说过于超前。

一般过去时:
The announcement was premature. = 那个公告发布得太早了。
The decision to fire him was premature. = 解雇他的决定过于仓促。
