costage:/ˈkɒstɪdʒ/| n.|邮资，邮费|The cost of sending a letter or package by post.  It includes the price of stamps or other postage materials and any handling fees.|例句：The costage for this parcel is quite high. 这包裹的邮费相当高。|The costage was included in the price. 邮费包含在价格里。|近义词：postage, mailing fee, postage charges|反义词:无
