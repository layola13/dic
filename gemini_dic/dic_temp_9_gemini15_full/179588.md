reemigrates:/ˌriːˈɛmɪɡreɪts/ | v. | 再移民；重新移民 | To migrate again; to return to a country one has previously emigrated from.  |  Example: After living in Canada for ten years, she reemigrated to her native France.  十年在加拿大生活后，她又重新移民回了她的祖国法国。 | 近义词: remigrate, return, repatriate | 反义词: emigrate (for the first time), immigrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reemigrates to his homeland every summer. = 他每年夏天都回故乡。
She reemigrates to the United States after many years abroad. =  她在国外生活多年后，又回到了美国。


一般过去时:
They reemigrated to Ireland after a decade in Australia. = 他们在澳大利亚生活十年后，又回到了爱尔兰。
My grandparents reemigrated to Italy in 1960. = 我的祖父母在1960年重新移民到了意大利。


现在完成时:
He has reemigrated to his country of origin. = 他已经回到他的祖国了。
She has reemigrated twice in her life. = 她一生中重新移民了两次。


过去完成时:
They had reemigrated before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前他们已经回国了。
She had reemigrated to the UK before the new laws were implemented. = 新法律实施前，她已经重新移民到英国了。


将来时:
He will reemigrate next year. = 他明年将重新移民。
They will reemigrate once their children finish school. = 他们的孩子毕业后，他们就会回国。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would reemigrate to my childhood home. = 如果我有钱，我就会搬回我童年时的家。
I wish I could reemigrate, but I have too many responsibilities here. = 我真希望我能回国，但是我这里责任重大。


被动语态:
Few people are reemigrated against their will. = 很少有人被迫重新移民。
It is often difficult to be reemigrated if you are not a citizen.  = 如果你不是公民，重新移民通常很困难。


疑问句:
Will he reemigrate soon? = 他会很快重新移民吗？
Has she ever reemigrated before? = 她以前重新移民过吗？


否定句:
He did not reemigrate after all. = 他最终没有重新移民。
She will not reemigrate until her contract expires. = 直到她的合同到期，她才会重新移民。
