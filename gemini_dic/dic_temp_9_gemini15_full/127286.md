英文解析：Navigableness refers to the quality or state of being navigable; the ease with which a waterway can be navigated by ships or boats.  It implies the absence of obstacles and the suitability of the waterway for navigation.


例句：

The assessment of the river's navigableness is underway.  对河流的可航行性评估正在进行中。

The increased navigableness of the canal has boosted trade. 运河可航行性的提高促进了贸易。


词源：

The word "navigablenesses" is derived from "navigable," which in turn comes from the Latin word "navigable" (capable of being navigated).  The suffix "-ness" is added to create the noun form, indicating a quality or state.  The plural "navigablenesses" is less common and is usually used to refer to multiple aspects or different degrees of navigability.
