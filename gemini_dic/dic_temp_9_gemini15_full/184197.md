resod:/rɪˈsɒd/| n. |重新铺设（草皮）|The act of replacing an existing lawn with new sod.  It involves removing the old turf and installing fresh sod to create a new, healthy lawn.|例句：The garden needed a resod, so we hired a landscaping company. 花园需要重新铺设草皮，所以我们雇了一家园林公司。|例句：After the dog destroyed the lawn, we had to have it resodded. 狗把草坪破坏了之后，我们不得不重新铺设草皮。|近义词：relaying, returfing, sodding|反义词：removing, destroying, uprooting|名词
