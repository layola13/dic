infestuous: /ɪnˈfɛstuəs/ | adj. |有害的；致病的；充满害虫的|  Infestuous refers to something that is harmful, noxious, or teeming with pests. It suggests a state of being overrun or corrupted by something undesirable. The word carries a strong negative connotation, implying danger or disease.  | The infestuous swamp bred disease and discouraged settlement.  沼泽地里滋生疾病，使人们不愿定居。The infestuous rumors spread fear throughout the town.  有害的谣言在镇上散布恐惧。 | noxious, pestilential, unhealthy, corrupt | salubrious, healthy, beneficial | 形容词
