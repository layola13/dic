nauseam:/ˈnɔːziəm/|adv.|令人作呕地；过度地；令人厌烦地|To a sickening or excessive degree; to the point of disgust or boredom.  It is used to describe something repeated to an excessive and tiresome extent.|例句：He repeated the same story ad nauseam. 他一遍遍地重复着同一个故事，令人厌烦至极。|近义词：excessively, excessively, tediously, endlessly, repeatedly|反义词：sparingly, briefly, concisely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repeats that joke ad nauseam. = 他一遍遍地重复那个笑话。
They discuss the same topic ad nauseam. = 他们没完没了地讨论同一个话题。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
