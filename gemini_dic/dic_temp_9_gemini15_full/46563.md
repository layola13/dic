dalmanites:/dælˈmaɪnaɪts/| n. |达尔曼虫属|An extinct genus of trilobites, characterized by their large, semi-circular cephalon and segmented body.  Dalmanites are common fossils found in Paleozoic rocks.  They are important index fossils, helping geologists date rock strata.|The fossil found in the limestone was identified as *Dalmanites limulurus*.  = 在石灰岩中发现的化石被鉴定为*达尔曼虫属的一种*。|Trilobite, Phacops| (no direct antonym, as it refers to a specific genus) |名词
