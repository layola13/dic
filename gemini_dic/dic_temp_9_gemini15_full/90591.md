huller:/ˈhʌlər/| n. v.|去壳者；脱壳机；去除谷壳者|A huller is a person or a machine that removes the hulls from grains, seeds, or nuts.  It can refer to a person who performs this task manually or a machine designed for efficient hulling. |例句：The rice huller separated the rice grains from their outer shells.  这台碾米机将稻米从外壳中分离出来。The farmer acted as a huller, removing the husks from the harvested corn by hand. 农民充当了去壳者，手工去除收获的玉米的壳。|近义词：sheller, dehusker|反义词：huller (反义词需结合语境，例如：如果指“手工去壳”则反义词可以是“机器去壳”)
