manifestable:/ˈmænɪfɛstəbəl/ | adj. | 可证明的；明显的；可显示的 | capable of being shown or demonstrated; evident; readily apparent.  | The manifestable flaws in the system led to its eventual collapse. 系统中明显的缺陷导致了它最终的崩溃。 The manifestable benefits of exercise include improved cardiovascular health. 运动的明显益处包括改善心血管健康。 | apparent, evident, obvious, demonstrable | hidden, concealed, obscure, latent |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The evidence manifests the truth. = 证据证明了真相。
His anger is manifestable in his behaviour. = 他的愤怒在他的行为中显而易见。


一般过去时:
The results manifested the success of the experiment. = 结果证明了实验的成功。
The problem manifested itself in several ways. = 这个问题以几种方式表现出来。


现在完成时:
The company has manifested a commitment to sustainability. = 公司已经展现出对可持续发展的承诺。
His hard work has manifested in his achievements. = 他的努力在他的成就中得到了体现。


过去完成时:
By then, the symptoms had manifested fully. = 到那时，症状已经完全显现出来。
The flaw had manifested itself before the launch. = 缺陷在发布之前就已经显现出来。


将来时:
The effects of the policy will manifest themselves in the coming years. = 政策的影响将在未来几年显现出来。
The results will manifest clearly in the next report. = 结果将在下一份报告中清晰地显现。


虚拟语气:
If the problem manifested itself earlier, we could have avoided it. = 如果问题早些显现出来，我们就可以避免它了。
If the truth manifested itself, it would be shocking. = 如果真相显现出来，那将令人震惊。


被动语态:
The truth was manifested in the documents. = 真相在文件中得到了体现。
The problem is manifested by a series of errors. = 这个问题表现为一系列错误。


疑问句:
How is the problem manifested? = 这个问题是如何表现出来的？
Does the data manifest the expected results? = 数据是否体现了预期的结果？


否定句:
The evidence did not manifest the defendant's guilt. = 证据并没有证明被告有罪。
The benefits of the program did not manifest themselves immediately. = 该项目的益处并没有立即显现出来。
