directorship:/dəˈrɛktəʃɪp/ | n. | 董事职位；领导职务 | The position or tenure of a director, especially of a company or organization.  It implies authority and responsibility for overseeing and managing the affairs of the entity. |例句：He was appointed to the directorship of the museum. 他被任命为博物馆馆长。  She resigned from her directorship after the scandal. 丑闻发生后，她辞去了董事职位。 | 近义词：management, superintendence, administration | 反义词：subordination, followership | 名词
