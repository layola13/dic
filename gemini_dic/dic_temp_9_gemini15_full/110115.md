licorice:/ˈlɪkərɪʃ/| n. |甘草，甘草糖|A plant with a sweet, black root used to flavor candy and medicine.  The root itself is also sometimes eaten.|例句：I love the taste of licorice in my candy. 我喜欢糖果里的甘草味。|例句：The old remedy used licorice root to soothe the throat. 这种古老的疗法用甘草根来缓解喉咙痛。|近义词：liquorice (British spelling)|反义词：None specifically.
