microtomical:/ˌmaɪkrəˈtɒmɪkəl/ | adj. |显微切片的；显微镜切片的 | Relating to or performed with a microtome.  The adjective describes something related to or done using a microtome, a device used to create extremely thin slices of material for microscopic examination. | The microtomical sections revealed the intricate details of the plant tissue. 显微切片显示了植物组织的复杂细节。 | histological, microscopic | macroscopic |
