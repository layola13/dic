interchangeability: /ˌɪntərˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可互换性，可替换性 | The ability of things to be exchanged or substituted for each other without loss of function or value.  This refers to the property of items or components that can be used in place of one another without affecting the overall performance or outcome. | Example: The interchangeability of the parts made mass production possible.  零件的可互换性使得大规模生产成为可能。  The high degree of interchangeability between these two models makes repairs easier. 这两个型号之间高度的可互换性使得维修更容易。 |近义词：replaceability, substitutability, exchangeability | 反义词：uniqueness, distinctness | 名词
