quasi-mighty:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈmaɪti/ | adj. | 准强大的；似强大的；名义上的强大的 |  Almost but not truly mighty; possessing some but not all the qualities of might.  It suggests a semblance of power or strength, but lacking complete authority or effectiveness. |例句：The quasi-mighty empire crumbled under the pressure of internal conflicts.  准强大的帝国在内部冲突的压力下土崩瓦解。

The company, with its quasi-mighty marketing campaign, failed to gain significant market share. 这家公司虽然开展了声势浩大的营销活动，但未能获得显著的市场份额。| 近义词：seemingly powerful, nominally powerful, superficially strong | 反义词：weak, powerless, insignificant |  词性:形容词
