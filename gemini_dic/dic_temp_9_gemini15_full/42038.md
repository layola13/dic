costrel:/ˈkɒstrəl/| n.|皮酒壶；粗俗的人|A costrel is a type of leather bottle, typically used for carrying wine or other liquids.  The word can also be used, less commonly, as a derogatory term for a vulgar or uncouth person. |例句：The traveler carried a costrel of water across the desert.  旅行者带着一皮酒壶水穿越沙漠。|近义词：flask, bottle, blackguard|反义词：gentleman, refined person
