deescalating: /ˌdiːˈɛskəleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 缓和；平息；使降级 |  The act of reducing the intensity or severity of a conflict or potentially harmful situation.  It involves taking steps to lower tensions and prevent escalation. | 例句：The police successfully deescalated the tense situation between the protesters and the officers.  警察成功地平息了抗议者和警官之间的紧张局势。 | 例句：The mediator worked to deescalate the conflict between the two warring factions. 调解员努力缓和两个交战派系之间的冲突。 | 近义词：reduce, lessen, mitigate, calm, soothe | 反义词：escalate, inflame, aggravate, intensify | 动词
