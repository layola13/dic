double-parked:/ˈdʌbəl pɑːrkt/ | adj. | 双排停放的；违章停放的 |  Describes a vehicle parked illegally, typically in a way that obstructs traffic by taking up more than one parking space or blocking the flow of traffic.  | Example: The car was double-parked, causing a traffic jam. 这辆车双排停放，造成了交通堵塞。  | 近义词：illegally parked, improperly parked | 反义词：properly parked, legally parked |  形容词
