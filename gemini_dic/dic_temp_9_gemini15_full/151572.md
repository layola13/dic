partakers:/pɑːrˈteɪkərz/| n.|参与者，共享者|Those who partake in something, sharing in an activity, experience, or something tangible.  Participants in a religious ceremony, a meal, or any shared event.|例句：The partakers of the feast enjoyed the delicious food.  盛宴的参与者们享用了美味的食物。

例句：All the partakers in the project contributed their expertise.  所有参与这个项目的参与者都贡献了他们的专业知识。


近义词：participants, sharers, participants, consumers


反义词：observers, bystanders, abstainers

词性:名词
