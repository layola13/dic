hybrid:/ˈhaɪbrɪd/| adj., n.|混合的；杂交的；混合动力车|A hybrid is something that is made by combining two different things.  It can be a plant or animal that is the offspring of two different species, or it can be a system or thing that combines different elements.  For example, a hybrid car uses both gasoline and electric power.|例句：The scientist created a hybrid plant with improved disease resistance.  这位科学家创造了一种具有增强抗病性的杂交植物。

例句：My new car is a hybrid; it runs on both gasoline and electricity. 我的新车是混合动力车；它既可以用汽油也可以用电力驱动。|近义词：composite, crossbreed, mixed |反义词：purebred, unmixed
