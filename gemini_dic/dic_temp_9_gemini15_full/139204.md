objective:/əbˈdʒektɪv/| n. adj.|目标；客观性；宾语|  a thing aimed at or sought; a goal; purpose; something that one's effort or actions are intended to attain or accomplish;  an object of a verb, preposition, or clause.  Also: relating to or denoting things as perceived without distortion of personal feelings or interpretation.|例句：Her main objective is to graduate with honors.  她的主要目标是以优异成绩毕业。 The objective of the study was to determine the effectiveness of the treatment. 这项研究的目的是确定治疗的有效性。 |近义词：goal, aim, target, purpose, intent|反义词：subjective, biased, prejudiced |名词，形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His objective is to become a doctor. = 他的目标是成为一名医生。
Their objective this year is to increase profits by 10%. = 他们今年的目标是将利润提高10%。

一般过去时:
Her objective last year was to finish her book. = 她去年的目标是完成她的书。
The objective of the meeting was to resolve the conflict. = 会议的目标是解决冲突。


现在完成时:
We have achieved our objective. = 我们已经实现了我们的目标。
They have not yet reached their objective. = 他们尚未达到他们的目标。


过去完成时:
The objective had been to climb the mountain before sunset, but the weather prevented it. = 目标是在日落前爬上山，但天气阻止了。
They had already achieved their objective before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前就已经实现了他们的目标。


将来时:
Our objective will be to expand into new markets. = 我们的目标将是拓展新市场。
Their objective next year will be to improve customer satisfaction. = 他们明年的目标是提高客户满意度。


虚拟语气:
If our objective were to increase sales, we would need a new marketing strategy. = 如果我们的目标是增加销售额，我们就需要一个新的营销策略。
I wish my objective were easier to attain. = 我希望我的目标更容易实现。


被动语态:
The objective was clearly stated in the proposal. = 目标在提案中已明确说明。
The objectives of the project are being reviewed. = 项目的目标正在审查中。


疑问句:
What is the objective of this meeting? = 这个会议的目标是什么？
What are your objectives for the next quarter? = 你下一季度的目标是什么？


否定句:
The project did not achieve its objective. = 该项目没有实现其目标。
Their attempts to reach their objective were unsuccessful. = 他们实现目标的尝试不成功。


PHRASE->
achieve an objective = 实现目标
set an objective = 设定目标
main objective = 主要目标
long-term objective = 长期目标
stated objective = 明确的目标
