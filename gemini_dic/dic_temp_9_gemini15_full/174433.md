quilt:/kwɪlt/| n.|被子，棉被|A quilt is a bed covering made of two layers of fabric with a layer of padding or wadding between them, all stitched together.|例句：My grandmother made me a beautiful patchwork quilt. 我奶奶为我做了一床漂亮的拼布被子。|近义词：comforter, coverlet|反义词:None
