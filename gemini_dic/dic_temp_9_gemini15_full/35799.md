cockerels:/ˈkɒkərəl/| n. |公鸡(尤指幼公鸡)|A cockerel is a young domestic cock, a male chicken.  The word often implies a younger bird than a fully mature rooster. |例句：The cockerel crowed at dawn.  = 公鸡在黎明时分啼叫。

The farmer proudly showed us his prize-winning cockerels. = 农场主自豪地向我们展示了他获奖的公鸡。|近义词：rooster, cock, chicken (male)|反义词：hen, pullet
