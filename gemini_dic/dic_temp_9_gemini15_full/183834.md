resecure:/ˌriːsɪˈkjʊr/| vt.|重新确保；再次固定|To secure again or more firmly; to make safe or secure once more.|例句：The engineers resecured the bridge after the earthquake. 工程师在地震后重新加固了桥梁。
例句：We need to resecure the loose tiles on the roof. 我们需要重新固定屋顶上松动的瓦片。|近义词：reinforce, strengthen, fasten|反义词：loosen, weaken, unfasten|动词
