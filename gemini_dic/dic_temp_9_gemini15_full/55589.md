disunion:/dɪsˈjuːnjən/| n. |分裂；分离；不团结 |The state of being separated or disconnected; lack of unity or agreement.  It often refers to the separation of a country or organization into parts. |例句：The disunion of the empire led to widespread chaos. 帝国的分裂导致了广泛的混乱。|例句：The team's disunion was evident in their poor performance.  该团队的不团结在他们糟糕的表现中显而易见。|近义词：separation, division, discord, schism |反义词：union, unity, harmony, concord |名词
