enchylemma: /ɛnˈkaɪləmə/ | n. |  内胚层；内层膜 |  A thin membrane lining a cavity or organ, especially the innermost layer of the intestinal wall.  It is derived from the Greek words "en" (in) and "chyle" (juice). The term is largely obsolete in modern biology, having been replaced by more precise anatomical terminology.


例句：

The old medical texts referred to the enchylemma as the innermost layer of the digestive tract. =  古老的医学文献将内胚层描述为消化道的最内层。


近义词： tunica intima (in the context of blood vessels), innermost lining, internal membrane


反义词： outer membrane, outermost layer, tunica adventitia (in the context of blood vessels)


词性：名词
