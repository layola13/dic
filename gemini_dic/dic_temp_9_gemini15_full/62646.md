enfranchises:/ɪnˈfrænˌtʃaɪzɪz/ | vt. |赋予公民权；使享有特权；特许经营 | To grant someone the right to vote or to participate in political processes.  It can also refer to granting someone a franchise or the right to operate a business under a particular brand. |The new law enfranchised women, giving them the right to vote. = 新法律赋予妇女选举权。|The company enfranchised its local distributors, granting them exclusive rights to sell its products in their regions. = 这家公司将其当地经销商特许经营，赋予他们在其地区独家销售其产品的权利。|empower, enable |disenfranchise, suppress |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government enfranchises all citizens over 18. = 政府赋予所有18岁以上公民选举权。
This company enfranchises its retailers aggressively. = 这家公司积极地将其零售商特许经营。


一般过去时:
The 19th Amendment enfranchised women in the United States. = 第十九修正案赋予美国妇女选举权。
They enfranchised the local business by giving them a franchise. = 他们通过给予当地企业特许经营权来使之享有特权。


现在完成时:
Many countries have enfranchised women over the past century. = 在过去的一个世纪里，许多国家已经赋予妇女选举权。
The franchisor has enfranchised over 100 stores nationwide. = 特许经营商已经在全国特许经营了100多家商店。


过去完成时:
Before the reform, many people had been disenfranchised. = 改革之前，许多人被剥夺了公民权。
By the end of the year, the company had enfranchised more than 50 new locations. = 到年底，该公司已特许经营了50多个新地点。


将来时:
The new bill will enfranchise millions of voters. = 新法案将赋予数百万选民选举权。
They plan to enfranchise more dealers next year. = 他们计划明年特许经营更多经销商。


虚拟语气:
If the law were passed, it would enfranchise a significant portion of the population. = 如果该法律通过，它将赋予相当一部分人口公民权。
If they had enfranchised more dealers, sales would have been higher. = 如果他们特许经营更多经销商，销售额就会更高。


被动语态:
Many people were enfranchised by the new law. = 许多人因新法律而获得公民权。
The franchise was enfranchised to a local entrepreneur. = 特许经营权被授予了一位当地企业家。


疑问句:
Will this policy enfranchise all eligible voters? = 这项政策会赋予所有符合条件的选民选举权吗？
Has the company enfranchised its distributors in other regions? = 该公司是否已将其经销商特许经营到其他地区？


否定句:
The government did not enfranchise the minority group. = 政府没有赋予少数民族群体公民权。
The company chose not to enfranchise new dealers this year. = 公司选择今年不特许经营新经销商。
