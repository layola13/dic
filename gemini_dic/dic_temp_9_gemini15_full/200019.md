skeining:/ˈskeɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 卷绕；缠绕；(纱线的)卷；混乱 | The act of winding yarn or thread into a skein; a tangled or confused mass.  |例句：The skeining of the yarn took a surprisingly long time.  = 卷绕纱线出乎意料地花费了很长时间。

The intricate skeining of the plot kept the readers guessing. = 情节错综复杂的缠绕让读者们不断猜测。| 近义词：winding, tangling, entanglement | 反义词：untangling, unwinding | 名词
