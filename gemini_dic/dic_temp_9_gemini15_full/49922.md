denationalize:/ˌdiːˈnæʃənəlaɪz/| vt. |使脱离国有；取消国有化；使具有国际色彩|To remove something from national ownership or control; to make something less national in character.|例句：The government plans to denationalize the railway system. 政府计划将铁路系统私有化。  例句：The company has been denationalized and is now privately owned. 这家公司已经被私有化，现在是私营企业。|近义词：privatize, de-nationalise|反义词：nationalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government denationalizes industries. = 政府正在使工业私有化。
They denationalize the airline. = 他们正在使航空公司私有化。

一般过去时:
The company was denationalized last year. = 这家公司去年被私有化了。
The government denationalized the banks in the 1980s. = 政府在20世纪80年代对银行进行了私有化。

现在完成时:
Many state-owned enterprises have been denationalized. = 许多国有企业已经私有化了。
The country has denationalized its telecommunications industry. = 这个国家已经将其电信产业私有化了。

过去完成时:
The factory had been denationalized before the new owner took over. = 在新老板接管之前，工厂已经被私有化了。
They had denationalized the airline before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经将航空公司私有化了。

将来时:
The government will denationalize the postal service next year. = 政府明年将对邮政服务进行私有化。
They will denationalize more industries in the coming years. = 他们将在未来几年对更多行业进行私有化。

虚拟语气:
If the government denationalized the railway, it would improve efficiency. = 如果政府对铁路进行私有化，将会提高效率。
I wish they had denationalized the energy sector earlier. = 我希望他们早点对能源部门进行私有化。

被动语态:
The airline is being denationalized. = 航空公司正在被私有化。
The industry was denationalized rapidly. = 该行业被迅速私有化。

疑问句:
Will the government denationalize the oil industry? = 政府会对石油工业进行私有化吗？
Has the company been denationalized yet? = 这家公司已经私有化了吗？

否定句:
The government did not denationalize the healthcare system. = 政府没有对医疗系统进行私有化。
They will not denationalize the water company. = 他们不会对自来水公司进行私有化。
