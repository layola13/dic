plim:/plɪm/| n.| 运动鞋，胶鞋 |A plimsoll is a type of canvas shoe with a rubber sole.  They are often worn for sports or casual wear.  The term is primarily British English. |例句：He wore plimsolls to the gym. 他穿运动鞋去健身房。

例句：My daughter loves her new red plimsolls. 我女儿喜欢她新的红色运动鞋。


近义词：sneaker, trainer (BrE) , gym shoe

反义词：无


词源:The origin of the word "plimsoll" is uncertain, but it's believed to be related to the name of a manufacturer,  possibly  the  Plimsoll  Brothers, or to the appearance of the shoes resembling a plimsoll line (the line marking the safe load limit on a ship).  It is first recorded in the late 19th century.
