greediness: /ˈɡriːdinəs/ | n. |贪婪；贪心 |  Excessive desire for more than is needed or deserved; avarice.  |例句：His greediness led him to ruin.  他的贪婪导致了他破产。

例句：The company's greediness in exploiting its workers is appalling. 公司残酷剥削工人的贪婪行径令人震惊。


近义词：avarice, covetousness, rapacity, cupidity | 反义词：generosity, altruism, selflessness

词源:
From Middle English *greedinesse, from greed + -nesse.  Ultimately derived from Old English *grēdian ("to weep, cry").  The semantic shift from "weeping" to "greed" is believed to be connected to the idea of intense longing or desire, akin to the uncontrollable crying of a child.
