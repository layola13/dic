punching-bags: /ˈpʌntʃɪŋbæɡz/ | n. |沙袋；出气筒 |  A punching bag is a heavy bag filled with sand or other material, used for practicing boxing or other martial arts. It is also used metaphorically to refer to someone or something that absorbs abuse or frustration. | 例句：He let out his anger on the punching bag. 他对着沙袋发泄怒气。  He uses his job as a punching bag for his boss's frustrations. 他把自己的工作当作老板发泄不满的出气筒。| 近义词：heavy bag,  (metaphorically) scapegoat | 反义词：N/A
