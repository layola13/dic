It often involves seeing patterns or connections where none exist, leading to inaccurate or misleading conclusions. | Example:  The overcausticity in his analysis led him to conclude that the economic downturn was solely caused by government policy, ignoring other contributing factors. |近义词: overdetermination, hypercausality |反义词: underdetermination, acausality
