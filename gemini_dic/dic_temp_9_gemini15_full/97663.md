inhibition:/ˌɪnɪˈbɪʃən/ | n. |抑制；禁止；约束 |The act of inhibiting, restraining, or preventing something.  It can refer to a feeling of self-consciousness that prevents someone from expressing themselves freely, or to the suppression of a natural process or behavior. |例句：The drug caused a significant inhibition of enzyme activity.  这种药物显著抑制了酶的活性。

例句：Shyness is a form of social inhibition. 害羞是一种社会抑制的表现。


近义词：suppression, restraint, restriction, deterrence |反义词：stimulation, encouragement, freedom


词源:  From Middle French *inhibition, from Latin inhibitus, past participle of inhibere "to restrain, hold back," from in- "in, into" + habēre "to have, hold."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His shyness inhibits him from speaking up. = 他的害羞使他不敢大声说话。
The medication inhibits the growth of bacteria. = 这种药物抑制细菌的生长。


一般过去时:
The fear inhibited her from taking the exam. = 恐惧使她不敢参加考试。
The cold weather inhibited plant growth. = 寒冷的天气抑制了植物的生长。


现在完成时:
Fear has inhibited his progress in his career. = 恐惧已经阻碍了他的职业发展。
The policy has inhibited economic growth. = 该政策已经抑制了经济增长。


过去完成时:
His past trauma had inhibited his ability to trust others. = 他过去的创伤已经影响了他信任他人的能力。
The previous drought had inhibited the crop yield. = 前面的干旱已经抑制了农作物的产量。


将来时:
The new regulations will inhibit the company's expansion. = 新规将抑制公司的扩张。
Lack of funding will inhibit the project's completion. = 资金不足将阻碍项目的完成。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't so inhibited, I would have spoken my mind. = 如果我不那么拘谨，我就说出我的想法了。
If the government hadn't inhibited free speech, the situation would be different. = 如果政府没有压制言论自由，情况就会不同。


被动语态:
His creativity was inhibited by his strict upbringing. = 他严格的教养抑制了他的创造力。
Economic growth is being inhibited by high interest rates. = 高利率正在抑制经济增长。


疑问句:
What is inhibiting your progress? = 什么在阻碍你的进步？
What factors are inhibiting the development of the project? = 哪些因素正在阻碍项目的开发？


否定句:
Nothing inhibited her from achieving her goals. = 没有什么能阻止她实现目标。
The lack of resources did not inhibit their success. = 资源匮乏并没有阻碍他们的成功。
