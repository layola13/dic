expectedly:/ɪkˈspektɪdli/ | adv. |不出所料地，正如预期地 | As anticipated; in a way that is expected.  The adverb "expectedly" signifies that something happened or is happening as predicted or anticipated. It emphasizes the conformity to expectations. |例句：Expectedly, the traffic was heavy during rush hour. 出乎意料的是，交通高峰期交通拥堵。

The meeting ended expectedly early. 会议不出所料地提前结束了。|近义词：predictably, understandably, naturally, as expected |反义词：unexpectedly, surprisingly, astonishingly
