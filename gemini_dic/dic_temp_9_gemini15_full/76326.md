gablelike:/ˈɡeɪbəlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像山墙的，类似山墙的|Resembling a gable; having the shape or characteristics of a gable.  A gable is the triangular upper part of a wall, enclosed by a sloping roof.  The word implies a similar triangular or pointed shape.|The house had a gablelike roofline, sloping steeply down from the peak.|近义词：gable-shaped, triangular, peaked|反义词：flat-topped, rectangular
