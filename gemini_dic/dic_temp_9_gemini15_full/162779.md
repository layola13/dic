prebook:/ˈpriːbʊk/| vt.|预订，提前预订|To reserve or book something in advance.  This usually implies booking something before the general public or before a certain deadline. |例句：I prebooked my flight tickets two months in advance. 我提前两个月预订了我的机票。

例句：We prebooked a table at the restaurant for our anniversary dinner. 我们提前预定了餐厅的桌子来庆祝我们的结婚纪念日。


近义词：reserve, book in advance, arrange beforehand, schedule


反义词：cancel, postpone


词性:动词
