fumigation:/ˌfjuːmɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |熏蒸；烟熏消毒|The process of disinfecting an area or object using gaseous pesticides or fumigants.  This is often done to control pests like insects, rodents, or fungi. |例句：The house underwent fumigation to eliminate termites.  这栋房子进行了熏蒸以消灭白蚁。

例句：The farmer used fumigation to protect his crops from pests. 这个农民使用熏蒸来保护他的庄稼免受害虫侵害。


近义词：disinfection, pest control, sterilization|反义词：contamination
