non-migratory:/ˌnɑːnˈmaɪɡrəˌtɔːri/| adj. |非迁徙的，定居的 | Not migrating; remaining in the same place throughout the year.  This term is typically used to describe animals, especially birds, that do not migrate seasonally. |例句：Many species of birds are non-migratory, remaining in one area year-round.  许多鸟类是非迁徙的，一年四季都待在一个地方。

例句：The local population of robins is largely non-migratory. 当地的知更鸟种群大部分是非迁徙的。


近义词：sedentary, resident, stationary
反义词：migratory
词性:形容词
