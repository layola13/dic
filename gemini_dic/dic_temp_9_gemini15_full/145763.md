overcure:/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊr/| vt.|过度固化，过度处理|To cure or process something to an excessive degree, resulting in undesirable effects.  This term is often used in the context of materials science, food processing, and medicine.|例句：Overcuring the resin led to brittleness. 过度固化树脂导致脆性。

The rubber was overcured, making it hard and inflexible. 橡胶过度硫化，使其坚硬且缺乏弹性。|近义词：overprocess, overtreat|反义词：undercure
