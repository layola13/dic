landsturm: /ˈlændʃtʊrm/ | n. | 国民军，地方民兵 | A landsturm is a type of militia composed of older or otherwise ineligible men, called up in times of national emergency.  It's typically a last resort, supplementing the regular army. | Example:  The invasion prompted the government to call up the landsturm. = 入侵促使政府征召国民军。 The landsturm, though poorly equipped, proved a surprisingly effective defensive force. = 尽管装备简陋，国民军却证明是一支令人惊讶的有效防御力量。 | 近义词：militia, levy, reserves | 反义词：standing army (正规军)
