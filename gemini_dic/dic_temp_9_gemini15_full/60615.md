elderish: /ˈɛldərɪʃ/ | adj. |略显年迈的，有点老的 |Slightly but noticeably older than middle-aged; showing some signs of aging.  The word implies a gentle suggestion of age, not extreme old age. |例句：Her mother is quite elderish, but still very active.  她母亲年纪有点大了，但仍然非常活跃。

近义词：aging, elderly, senile (stronger connotation)

词性：形容词
