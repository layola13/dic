column:/ˈkɑːləm/ /ˈkɒləm/| n. |专栏；列；柱；圆柱|A vertical section of a page or screen, often used to organize text or images.  A supporting pillar. |例句：He writes a weekly column for the newspaper. 他每周为报纸撰写专栏文章。

例句：The ancient temple was supported by massive columns. 这座古老的寺庙由巨大的柱子支撑着。|近义词：pillar, section, row |反义词：row (in the sense of a horizontal line)


USAGE->

The table has four columns. = 这张桌子有四列。
This column contains important information. = 这一栏包含重要信息。
