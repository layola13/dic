propellerless:/ˈprɒpɛlələs/ | adj. | 无螺旋桨的 | Lacking a propeller or propellers.  This adjective describes something that operates or moves without the use of a propeller, a rotating device used to propel aircraft or ships.  | Example:  The drone is propellerless, relying instead on jet propulsion.  这个无人机没有螺旋桨，而是依靠喷气推进。  | 近义词：bladeless (if referring to a fan), rotorless (in specific contexts) | 反义词：propelled
