redeveloped:/ˌriːdɪˈvɛləpt/ | v. | 再开发；重建 | To develop again, especially an area of land or a building,  improving it or changing its use.  | Example: The old factory site has been redeveloped into a modern apartment complex.  旧工厂厂址已被重新开发成现代化的公寓楼。 |近义词：rebuild, renovate, regenerate, refurbish | 反义词：demolish, destroy, raze

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redevelop the old town every year. = 他们每年都对老城区进行重新开发。
The company redevelops abandoned properties. = 这家公司对废弃的房产进行重新开发。

一般过去时:
The city redeveloped the waterfront area. = 该城市对滨水区域进行了重新开发。
They redeveloped the old school into a community center. = 他们把旧学校改建成了社区中心。

现在完成时:
They have redeveloped the shopping mall. = 他们已经对购物中心进行了重新开发。
The government has redeveloped several run-down neighborhoods. = 政府已经对几个破旧的街区进行了重新开发。

过去完成时:
They had redeveloped the park before the festival. = 他们在节日之前已经对公园进行了重新开发。
The developers had redeveloped the land before the housing boom. = 开发商在房地产热潮之前已经对这块土地进行了重新开发。

将来时:
They will redevelop the industrial area next year. = 他们明年将对工业区进行重新开发。
The city council will redevelop the public library. = 市议会将对公共图书馆进行重新开发。

虚拟语气:
If they had more funding, they would redevelop the entire city center. = 如果他们有更多资金，他们将会对整个市中心进行重新开发。
It would be great if they redeveloped that abandoned building. = 如果他们能重新开发那座废弃的建筑就好了。

被动语态:
The old building is being redeveloped into a hotel. = 这座旧建筑正在被改建成一家酒店。
The area has been redeveloped extensively. = 该地区得到了大规模的重新开发。

疑问句:
Is the area being redeveloped? = 该地区正在被重新开发吗？
When will the project be redeveloped? = 这个项目什么时候会被重新开发？

否定句:
They did not redevelop the property. = 他们没有对该房产进行重新开发。
The plan to redevelop the park was rejected. = 重新开发公园的计划被否决了。


PHRASE->
redevelop a site: 开发一个场地
redevelop an area: 开发一个区域
redevelop a plan:  重新制定计划
redevelop a strategy:  重新制定策略
redevelop an industry:  使一个产业得到复兴
