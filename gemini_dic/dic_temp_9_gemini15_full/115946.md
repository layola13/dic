marriage: /ˈmærɪdʒ/ | n. | 婚姻，结婚 | The legal or formal union of two people as partners in a relationship.  Marriage is a significant social institution with various cultural and religious customs. |  例句：Their marriage was a happy one. 他们的婚姻很幸福。

例句：They celebrated their 50th wedding anniversary, marking a long and successful marriage. 他们庆祝了他们的结婚50周年纪念日，标志着一段漫长而成功的婚姻。


近义词：wedlock, matrimony, union | 反义词：divorce, separation
