helplessly:/ˈhɛlpləsli/| adv.|无助地，无奈地|In a helpless manner; without the ability to do anything to change a situation.|例句：She watched helplessly as the fire spread. 她无助地看着火势蔓延。

例句：He helplessly looked on as his friend was attacked. 他无助地看着他的朋友遭到袭击。


近义词：powerlessly, hopelessly, fruitlessly, despairingly|反义词：powerfully, helpfully, confidently, purposefully|词性:副词
