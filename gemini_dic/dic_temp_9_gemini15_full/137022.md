nonstandardized:/ˌnɑːnˈstændərdˌaɪzd/| adj.|非标准化的；未标准化的|Not conforming to an established standard or norm.  It implies a lack of uniformity or consistency, often in relation to established rules, practices, or measurements.|例句：The company's processes are nonstandardized, leading to inconsistencies in product quality. 公司的流程是非标准化的，导致产品质量不一致。

例句：His grammar is often nonstandardized, reflecting his informal speech patterns. 他的语法常常是非标准化的，反映了他的非正式说话模式。


近义词：unstandardized, irregular, inconsistent, unconventional

反义词：standardized, normalized, regular, uniform
