imaginability: /ɪˌmædʒɪnəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可想象性，想象力 | The quality or state of being imaginable; the extent to which something can be imagined.  It refers to the capacity of the mind to form images or concepts of things not present to the senses. | Example: The sheer imaginability of the scenario was breathtaking.  这场景的纯粹可想象性令人叹为观止。 The novel explores the limits of human imaginability. 这部小说探索了人类想象力的极限。 | 近义词: conceivability, envisageability | 反义词: inconceivable, unimaginable |
