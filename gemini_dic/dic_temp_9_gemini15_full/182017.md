remediated:/rɪˈmiːdieɪtɪd/ | v. | 修复；改进；补救 | To remedy or set right (a defect, fault, etc.).  | The teacher remediated the student's misunderstanding of the concept. 老师纠正了学生对这个概念的误解。 | correct, rectify, improve, amend | worsen, exacerbate |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school remediates struggling students. = 学校对学习有困难的学生进行补救教学。
He remediates his mistakes regularly. = 他定期纠正自己的错误。


一般过去时:
The engineer remediated the faulty wiring. = 工程师修复了有故障的线路。
She remediated the situation with a quick apology. = 她迅速道歉，从而改善了局势。


现在完成时:
They have remediated the environmental damage. = 他们已经修复了环境破坏。
We have remediated all the known bugs in the software. = 我们已经修复了软件中所有已知的漏洞。


过去完成时:
He had remediated the problem before his boss arrived. = 他在老板到达之前已经解决了这个问题。
The company had remediated the safety hazard before the inspection. = 公司在检查之前已经消除了安全隐患。


将来时:
They will remediate the contaminated soil. = 他们将修复受污染的土壤。
The government will remediate the effects of the storm. = 政府将修复风暴的影响。


虚拟语气:
If they had remediated the issue earlier, the damage would have been less severe. = 如果他们早点解决这个问题，损失就不会那么严重。
It's essential that the leak be remediated immediately. = 必须立即修复泄漏。


被动语态:
The problem was remediated quickly and efficiently. = 问题得到了快速有效的解决。
The contaminated site is being remediated by the environmental agency. = 受污染的地点正在由环保机构进行修复。


疑问句:
Has the damage been remediated? = 损坏已经修复了吗？
How will the situation be remediated? = 情况将如何得到改善？


否定句:
The company did not remediate the problem effectively. = 公司没有有效地解决这个问题。
They failed to remediate the situation. = 他们未能改善局势。


PHRASE->
remediate environmental damage = 修复环境破坏
remediate a problem = 解决问题
remediate soil contamination = 修复土壤污染
remediate a defect = 修补缺陷
remediate a situation = 改善局势
