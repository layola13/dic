demoting: /dɪˈmoʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 降级；贬低；使…失宠 |  The act of lowering someone in rank, position, or status; to reduce someone to a lower position or rank.  | 例句：He was demoting for his repeated mistakes. 他因为反复犯错而被降职。

The company is demoting the inefficient employee. 公司正在降职这位效率低下的员工。


近义词：downgrading, reducing, lowering, demeaning

反义词：promoting, elevating, advancing


词源:  The word "demoting" comes from the verb "demote," which itself is derived from the Latin prefix "de-" (meaning "down" or "away") and the verb "mote" (from the Latin "movere," meaning "to move"). Therefore, "demote" literally means "to move down."
