keynoting:/ˈkiːnoʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 主题演讲；作主题发言 |  The act of delivering a keynote speech; serving as the central theme or idea.  | Example: The professor's keynoting the conference on climate change was well-received. 教授在气候变化会议上的主题演讲很受欢迎。 |近义词：addressing, presenting, headlining | 反义词：None specifically, but antonyms might relate to the content of the keynote, not the act itself.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He keynotes the conference every year. 他每年都在会议上作主题演讲。
She keynotes on the importance of education. 她以教育的重要性为主题发言。


一般过去时:
The CEO keynoted the annual meeting last week. 首席执行官上周在年度会议上作了主题演讲。
He keynoted at the technology summit. 他在技术峰会上作了主题演讲。


现在完成时:
She has keynoted at numerous international events. 她已经在许多国际活动中作过主题演讲。
They have keynoted the benefits of their new product. 他们已经强调了他们新产品的优点。


过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had keynoted the main points in the introduction. 演示之前，他在引言中已强调了要点。
She had keynoted the importance of collaboration in her previous speech.  她在之前的演讲中已经强调了合作的重要性。


将来时:
The mayor will keynote the fundraising gala. 市长将在筹款晚会上作主题演讲。
She will keynote the conference next month. 她下个月将在这个会议上作主题演讲。


虚拟语气:
If he were to keynote, the audience would be thrilled. 如果他来作主题演讲，观众会很兴奋。
I wish she had keynoted the need for change earlier. 我希望她早点强调改变的必要性。


被动语态:
The conference will be keynoted by a renowned scientist.  会议将由一位著名的科学家作主题演讲。
The event was keynoted effectively. 活动的主题演讲很有效。


疑问句:
Who will keynote the upcoming summit? 谁将在即将到来的峰会上作主题演讲？
Will she keynote the conference? 她会在这个会议上作主题演讲吗？


否定句:
He did not keynote the meeting. 他没有在会议上作主题演讲。
She will not keynote the event. 她不会在这个活动中作主题演讲。
