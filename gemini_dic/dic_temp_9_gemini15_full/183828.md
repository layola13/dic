resectable:/rɪˈsɛktəbəl/ | adj. | 可切除的；可手术切除的 |  Able to be surgically removed.  This term is typically used in the context of tumors or other growths.  A resectable tumor is one that can be completely removed by surgery, leaving no cancerous tissue behind. | The tumor was thankfully resectable.  = 幸运的是，肿瘤是可切除的。

The surgeon determined the lesion to be resectable. = 外科医生确定病灶是可切除的。


近义词：removable, operable | 反义词：inoperable, unresectable

词性:形容词
