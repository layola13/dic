gypsography:/ˌdʒɪpˈsɒɡrəfi/| n. |石膏雕塑术；石膏细工|The art or practice of making casts or sculptures in plaster.  It involves the process of creating three-dimensional forms using plaster of Paris or other similar materials. |例句：The museum showcased a remarkable collection of gypsography, featuring intricate figurines and life-size busts.  博物馆展出了非凡的石膏雕塑作品，包括复杂的雕像和小巧的半身像。|近义词：plasterwork, modeling, casting|反义词：carving (in the sense of subtractive sculpting)|名词
