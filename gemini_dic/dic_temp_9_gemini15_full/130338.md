non-traditionalist:/ˌnɒn trəˈdɪʃənəlɪst/| adj. |非传统主义者；反对传统的人|Someone who opposes or rejects traditional beliefs, practices, or institutions.  They often advocate for new or different approaches.| Example:  The non-traditionalist artist challenged the established norms of the art world.  这位非传统主义艺术家挑战了艺术界的既定规范。|近义词：unconventional, unorthodox, radical, avant-garde |反义词：traditionalist, conservative
