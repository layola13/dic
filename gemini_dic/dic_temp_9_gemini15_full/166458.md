prerevise:/ˌpriːrɪˈvaɪz/| v.|预先修改，预先校订|To revise or edit something before it is finalized or published.  It implies a preliminary review and adjustment before the final version.|例句：The author decided to prerevise the manuscript before submitting it to the publisher. 作者决定在向出版商提交手稿之前预先修改一下。|例句：The editor asked for a prerevised draft of the article. 编辑要求提供文章的预先修改稿。|近义词：preedit, review, revise, amend|反义词：finalize, publish
