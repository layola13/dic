impinge:/ɪmˈpɪndʒ/| v. | 撞击；侵犯；影响 | To have an effect or impact on something or someone.  |例句：The noise impinged on my concentration.  噪音影响了我的注意力。
The new law impinges on our freedom of speech. 这项新法律侵犯了我们的言论自由。|近义词：affect, impact, encroach, infringe |反义词：support, enhance, aid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain impinges on the roof. = 雨水冲击着屋顶。
His actions impinge on my personal life. = 他的行为影响着我的私生活。

一般过去时:
The rock impinged on the windshield. = 石头撞击了挡风玻璃。
The decision impinged heavily on the company's profits. = 这个决定严重影响了公司的利润。

现在完成时:
The constant noise has impinged on my ability to sleep. = 持续的噪音已经影响了我睡觉的能力。
The new regulations have impinged upon our business operations. = 新的法规已经影响了我们的业务运营。

过去完成时:
The previous management had impinged upon the company's financial stability. = 之前的管理层已经影响了公司的财务稳定性。
The storm had impinged on the coastal areas before the warning was issued. = 在发出警告之前，暴风雨已经影响了沿海地区。

将来时:
The expansion project will impinge on the local environment. = 扩张项目将影响当地环境。
The new policy will impinge on the rights of citizens. = 新政策将影响公民的权利。

虚拟语气:
If the project went ahead, it would impinge on the natural habitat. = 如果项目继续进行，它将影响自然栖息地。
I wish the noise wouldn't impinge on my work so much. = 我希望噪音不会这么影响我的工作。

被动语态:
Their privacy was impinged upon by the media. = 他们的隐私受到了媒体的侵犯。
The company's reputation was impinged upon by the scandal. = 公司的声誉受到了丑闻的影响。

疑问句:
Does the new building impinge on the view? = 新建筑物是否遮挡了视野？
Does this policy impinge on the rights of minorities? =这项政策是否侵犯了少数群体的权利？

否定句:
The changes did not impinge on the quality of the product. = 这些变化没有影响产品的质量。
The reform did not impinge on the basic principles of the system. = 改革没有影响该系统的基本原则。
