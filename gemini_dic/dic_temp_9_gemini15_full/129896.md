non-Irish: /ˌnɒnˈaɪrɪʃ/ | adj. | 非爱尔兰的；非爱尔兰人的 |  Not of or relating to Ireland or its people.  This adjective is used to describe something or someone that is not from Ireland or does not have Irish origins.


例句：

He's of non-Irish descent. = 他不是爱尔兰血统。

The museum has a collection of non-Irish art. = 这个博物馆收藏了一些非爱尔兰的艺术品。


近义词：foreign (in relation to Ireland), non-national (in the context of Ireland)


反义词：Irish


词性：形容词
