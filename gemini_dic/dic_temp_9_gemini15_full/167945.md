printmaker:/ˈprɪntˌmeɪkər/| n. |版画家，印刷工人|A printmaker is an artist who creates prints, such as etchings, lithographs, or screen prints.  They use various techniques to transfer images onto paper or other materials. |The printmaker meticulously prepared the etching plate before inking it.  这位版画家在给蚀刻版上墨之前一丝不苟地进行了准备。| artist, engraver, lithographer |  (无明确反义词，可考虑 non-artist)|名词
