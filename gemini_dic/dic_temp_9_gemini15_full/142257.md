orphaned:/ˈɔːrfənd/ | adj. | 孤儿的；失去父母的；被遗弃的 |  Describes a person, animal, or thing that has lost its parents or its source of support.  It often implies a sense of loss and vulnerability. | Example: The orphaned kitten was found alone and hungry.  这只失去父母的小猫被发现时又饿又孤单。  The orphaned project lacked funding and support.  这个被遗弃的项目缺乏资金和支持。|近义词：deserted, abandoned, parentless | 反义词：parented, supported


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is orphaned. 他是孤儿。
The project is orphaned. 这个项目被遗弃了。


一般过去时:
She was orphaned at a young age. 她很小的时候就成了孤儿。
The initiative was orphaned after the funding was cut.  在资金被削减后，这项倡议被搁置了。


现在完成时:
He has been orphaned since the accident. 自那次事故以来，他就成了孤儿。
The software has been orphaned since the company went bankrupt. 自从公司破产以来，这个软件就无人维护了。


过去完成时:
She had been orphaned for five years before she was adopted. 在她被收养之前，她已经做了五年孤儿。
The code had been orphaned for months before someone picked it up. 这段代码在有人接手之前已经无人问津好几个月了。


将来时:
If his parents die, he will be orphaned. 如果他的父母去世，他将成为孤儿。
The project will be orphaned unless more funding is secured. 除非能获得更多资金，否则这个项目将被搁置。


虚拟语气:
If he had not been orphaned, his life would have been different. 如果他没有成为孤儿，他的生活将会不同。
If the project hadn't been orphaned, it might have been successful. 如果这个项目没有被遗弃，它也许会成功。


被动语态:
The child was orphaned by the war. 这个孩子在战争中成了孤儿。
The project is being orphaned due to lack of interest.  由于缺乏兴趣，这个项目正在被搁置。


疑问句:
Was he orphaned as a child? 他小时候是孤儿吗？
Is the software orphaned? 这个软件是无人维护的吗？


否定句:
She was not orphaned; she was raised by her grandparents. 她不是孤儿；她是被祖父母抚养长大的。
The project is not orphaned; it is actively being developed.  这个项目并没有被遗弃；它正在积极开发中。
