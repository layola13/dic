nomology:/nəˈmɒlədʒi/ | n. | 命名法；术语学；分类学 | The study of laws or principles, especially in relation to a particular field of knowledge.  It often refers to the system of naming and classifying things within a specific field. | Example: The nomology of botany involves the consistent naming and classification of plant species.  植物学的命名法涉及植物物种的一致命名和分类。 |近义词：taxonomy, classification, systematics | 反义词：chaos, randomness | 名词
