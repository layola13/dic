hinging:/ˈhɪndʒɪŋ/| v.|以铰链连接；取决于；以…为中心|The success of the project is hinging on securing funding.  项目的成功取决于资金的到位。|近义词：depending on, relying on, pivoting on|反义词：independent of, unrelated to


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The door is hinging smoothly. = 门转动得很顺畅。
The whole argument hinges on his testimony. = 整个论点都取决于他的证词。


一般过去时:
The gate hinged loudly in the wind. = 门在风中嘎吱作响地转动。
His decision hinged on the advice he received. = 他的决定取决于他收到的建议。


现在完成时:
The plan has hinged on the cooperation of all parties. = 计划一直以来都依赖于所有方面的合作。
Everything has hinged on this one crucial decision. = 一切都取决于这个关键的决定。


过去完成时:
The success of the mission had hinged on their stealth. = 任务的成功取决于他们的隐秘行动。
The outcome had hinged on a single, unpredictable event. = 结果取决于一个单一的、不可预测的事件。


将来时:
The negotiations will hinge on the willingness of both sides to compromise. = 谈判的成败将取决于双方妥协的意愿。
The future of the company will hinge on the launch of this new product. = 公司的未来将取决于这款新产品的发布。


虚拟语气:
If the success of the plan hinged on me, I would feel the pressure. = 如果计划的成功取决于我，我会感到压力。
If it had hinged on my ability, I would have succeeded. = 如果它取决于我的能力，我会成功的。


被动语态:
The decision was hinged on the results of the survey. = 决定是根据调查结果作出的。
The success of the operation is being hinged on the latest technology. = 手术的成功取决于最新的技术。


疑问句:
Does the success of this project hinge on his support? = 这个项目的成功是否取决于他的支持？
On what does the outcome hinge? = 结果取决于什么？


否定句:
The result doesn't hinge on luck alone. = 结果不仅仅取决于运气。
The decision didn't hinge on any single factor. = 这个决定并不取决于任何单一因素。
