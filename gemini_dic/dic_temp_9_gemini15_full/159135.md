pod:/pɒd/|ˈpɑːd/| n. |豆荚；舱；(一组)；吊舱|A pod is a seed vessel of a plant, typically one that opens along two lines of dehiscence; it can also refer to a group of things, or a compartment or container.|例句：The pea pod was full of ripe peas.  豌豆荚里装满了成熟的豌豆。|例句：The whales swam in a pod. 鲸鱼成群结队地游动。|近义词：capsule, case, compartment, group, cluster |反义词：N/A
