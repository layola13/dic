multiplies:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪz/| v.|乘；繁殖；增加|To increase in number or amount; to perform multiplication.|例句：The rabbit population multiplies rapidly. 兔子数量迅速繁殖。|例句：Two multiplied by three equals six.  二乘以三等于六。|近义词：increases, expands, breeds, procreates|反义词：decreases, diminishes, reduces


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He multiplies the numbers quickly. = 他快速地进行乘法运算。
The bacteria multiplies rapidly in warm conditions. = 细菌在温暖的条件下迅速繁殖。


一般过去时:
She multiplied the figures incorrectly. = 她计算数字时算错了。
