outstagger:/ˌaʊtˈstæɡər/| v.|蹒跚而出，踉踉跄跄地走出来 |to walk or move unsteadily, often in a way that suggests exhaustion or intoxication.  The word combines "out" (away from a place) and "stagger" (to walk unsteadily).|例句：He outstaggered from the bar, clearly drunk. 他摇摇晃晃地从酒吧出来，显然喝醉了。


例句：The exhausted marathon runner outstaggered across the finish line.  精疲力尽的马拉松运动员踉踉跄跄地穿过终点线。


近义词：stumble out, lurch out, reel out


反义词：stride out, march out, walk steadily out


词性：动词
