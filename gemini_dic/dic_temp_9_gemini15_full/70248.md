fictionalize:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. | 将…虚构化；创作…的小说；把…改编成小说 | To represent or portray (something) in a fictional manner; to create a fictionalized version of.  | 例句：He fictionalized his experiences in the war in his memoir. 他在他的回忆录中虚构了他战争中的经历。

例句：The author fictionalized a real-life event for her novel. 作者为她的小说虚构了一个真实事件。


近义词：romanticize, dramatize, novelize, fabulize | 反义词：document, record, chronicle | 词性:动词
