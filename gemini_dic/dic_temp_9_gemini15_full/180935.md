reilluminate:/ˌriːɪˈluːmɪneɪt/| vt.|重新照亮；使…焕发光彩|To illuminate again; to restore brightness or brilliance to something.|例句：The newly installed lights reilluminate the historic building, showcasing its architectural details.  重新安装的灯重新照亮了这座历史建筑，突显了它的建筑细节。

例句：The artist's bold strokes reilluminate the canvas, giving it renewed vibrancy.  艺术家大胆的笔触使画布焕发光彩，赋予其新的活力。


近义词：relight, brighten, revitalize|反义词：darken, extinguish, dim|词性：动词
