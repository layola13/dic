caustic-sodas:/ˈkɔːstɪk ˈsoʊdəz/| n.|苛性苏打, 氢氧化钠溶液|Caustic soda, also known as lye or sodium hydroxide (NaOH), is a highly alkaline chemical compound. It's a strong base that is corrosive to many materials. It's used in various industrial applications, including the production of soap, paper, textiles, and detergents.|例句：The factory uses caustic soda in the manufacturing process. 这家工厂在生产过程中使用苛性苏打。|例句：Accidental contact with caustic soda can cause severe burns. 不小心接触到苛性苏打会造成严重烧伤。|近义词：lye, sodium hydroxide, sodium hydrate|反义词：acid
