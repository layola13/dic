fishermen:/ˈfɪʃərmən/| n.|渔民，渔夫|People who fish for a living or as a hobby.|例句：The fishermen returned with a bountiful catch. 渔民们满载而归。|例句：Many fishermen rely on the health of the ocean for their livelihood. 许多渔民的生计依赖于海洋的健康。|近义词：fishers, anglers|反义词:None.
