impermissible:/ɪmˈpɜːrmɪsəbl/| adj. |不允许的，不可接受的 |Not permissible; forbidden.  Something that is impermissible is not allowed or is forbidden. It's against the rules or regulations. |例句：That action is impermissible under company policy.  公司政策不允许这种行为。


例句：The use of company property for personal gain is impermissible. 使用公司财产谋取私利是不可接受的。


近义词：unacceptable, prohibited, forbidden, disallowed, illegal


反义词：permissible, allowable, acceptable, legal


词性：形容词
