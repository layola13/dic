higher-ups:/ˈhaɪərˌʌps/| n. |高层领导，上级|Those in authority, especially in a company or organization.  People with high positions and power.|例句：The higher-ups decided to implement a new policy. 高层决定实施一项新政策。

例句：The employees tried to appeal to the higher-ups for a raise. 员工试图向高层领导争取加薪。


近义词：superiors, bosses, management, executives, officials


反义词：subordinates, employees, lower-level staff


词性：名词
