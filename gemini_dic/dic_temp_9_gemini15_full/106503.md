例句：He referred to the Germans as "krauts," a remark that many found offensive. 他称德国人为“酸菜”，许多人认为这种说法很冒犯。|近义词：sauerkraut (more formal), fermented cabbage|反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "kraut" comes from the German word "Kraut," meaning "herb" or "vegetable."  Its specific application to fermented cabbage (sauerkraut) emerged later.  The derogatory use toward Germans developed during wartime, reflecting anti-German sentiment.
