out-of-luck:/ˌaʊt əv ˈlʌk/| adj.|运气不好，倒霉的|Unlucky; experiencing misfortune or bad fortune.  This phrase describes a situation where someone is experiencing misfortune due to bad luck, rather than their own actions. |例句：He was out of luck; the last bus had already left. 他真倒霉，最后一班车已经走了。

例句：She was out of luck and didn't win the lottery. 她运气不好，彩票没中奖。


近义词：unlucky, unfortunate, unlucky, ill-starred, jinxed

反义词：lucky, fortunate, blessed


词性:形容词
