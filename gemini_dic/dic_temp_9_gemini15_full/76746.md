gamecock:/ˈɡeɪmˌkɑːk/|/ˈɡeɪmˌkɒk/| n. |斗鸡；好斗的人；南卡罗来纳大学的吉祥物|A gamecock is a rooster bred for cockfighting.  It can also refer to a person who is aggressive and combative.  The term is also used as the mascot of the University of South Carolina.|例句：He fought like a gamecock. 他像斗鸡一样好斗。

The University of South Carolina's mascot is a gamecock. 南卡罗来纳大学的吉祥物是一只斗鸡。|近义词：fighter, warrior, pugilist |反义词：coward, pacifist
