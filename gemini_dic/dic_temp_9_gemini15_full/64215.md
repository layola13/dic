equilibration:/ˌiːkwɪlɪˈbreɪʃən/ | n. |平衡; 均衡; 平衡状态 |The process of achieving equilibrium; the state of being in equilibrium.  It often refers to the process of a system reaching a stable state where opposing forces are balanced.  |例句：The equilibration of temperature between the two liquids took several hours.  两个液体之间的温度平衡花了几个小时。

例句：The equilibration of the ecosystem after the fire was a slow process. 火灾后生态系统的平衡是一个缓慢的过程。


近义词：balance, stabilization, adjustment

反义词：disequilibrium, imbalance, instability
