fusilier:/ˈfjuːzɪliər/| n. |步枪兵；(鱼)笛鯛|A fusilier is a soldier armed with a fusil (a type of light musket).  It also refers to a type of brightly colored fish. |例句：The fusiliers advanced under heavy fire. 步枪兵在猛烈的炮火下前进。  The coral reef teemed with fusiliers. 珊瑚礁上挤满了笛鯛。|近义词：rifleman, musketeer (for the soldier meaning);  |反义词: |词性:noun
