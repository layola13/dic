shoddyism:/ˈʃɒdiɪzəm/ | n. |劣质品泛滥；粗制滥造；劣质做法 |The practice of producing or selling shoddy goods; the prevalence of shoddy goods.  It refers to a system or situation characterized by low quality, poor workmanship, and a disregard for standards. |例句：The shoddyism in the construction industry led to numerous building collapses. 建筑行业的粗制滥造导致了许多建筑物倒塌。

The increasing shoddyism of mass-produced clothing is a growing concern. 大量生产的服装质量越来越差，这是一个日益关注的问题。|近义词：slipshodness,  cheapness,  inferiority |反义词：excellence,  quality,  superiority
