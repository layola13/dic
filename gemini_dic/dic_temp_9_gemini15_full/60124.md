effected:/ɪˈfɛktɪd/| v.|影响，实现，造成，完成|to bring about or accomplish (something); to produce a change in (something or someone)|The new law effected significant changes in the education system.  这条新法律对教育系统产生了重大影响。|The team successfully effected the rescue.  团队成功地完成了救援行动。|achieved, accomplished, produced|unaffected, unchanged


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The new policy effects changes in workplace culture.  新政策影响着职场文化。
He effects repairs on old machinery. 他维修旧机械。


一般过去时:
The storm effected considerable damage to the coastline. 暴风雨对海岸线造成了相当大的破坏。
She effected a dramatic change in her life. 她给自己的生活带来了戏剧性的改变。


现在完成时:
The reforms have effected positive changes in the community. 改革已经给社区带来了积极的变化。
They have effected a reconciliation between the two parties. 他们促成了双方和解。


过去完成时:
The earthquake had effected widespread destruction before help arrived.  在救援到来之前，地震已经造成了大范围的破坏。
Before the meeting, she had effected a compromise. 会议之前，她已经达成妥协。


将来时:
The new technology will effect a revolution in manufacturing. 新技术将给制造业带来一场革命。
We will effect the necessary changes as soon as possible. 我们将尽快进行必要的更改。


虚拟语气:
If they effected a change in policy, it would improve the situation. 如果他们改变政策，情况就会好转。
I wish I had effected a change sooner. 我希望我早点做出改变。


被动语态:
Significant changes were effected by the new legislation. 新法令带来了重大的变化。
The repairs were effected quickly and efficiently. 维修工作快速高效地完成了。


疑问句:
How can we effect a change in the system? 我们怎样才能改变这个系统？
Did the treatment effect any improvement in his condition?  治疗是否改善了他的病情？


否定句:
The medicine did not effect any change in his symptoms. 这药没有改变他的症状。
He failed to effect a compromise between the two sides. 他未能促成双方妥协。
