meandrian:/miˈændriən/| adj.|蜿蜒的，曲折的；迂回的|winding, twisting, or curving in a complex manner, often resembling the course of a river.  The word evokes a sense of complexity and indirectness.|The meandrian path through the forest was both beautiful and confusing. 森林中蜿蜒的小路既美丽又令人困惑。|The river followed a meandrian course through the valley.  河流蜿蜒穿过山谷。|tortuous, serpentine, convoluted|straight, direct|形容词
