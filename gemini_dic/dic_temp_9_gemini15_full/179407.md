redtail: /ˈrɛdˌteɪl/ | n. |红尾（通常指红尾鹰或其他具有红色尾巴的动物） | A redtail is a common name for several birds of prey, most notably the red-tailed hawk, characterized by their reddish-brown tails.  The term can also refer to other animals with red tails. | Example:  We saw a redtail soaring high above the canyon.  我们看到一只红尾鹰在峡谷上空盘旋。  The redtail lizard scurried under a rock.  红尾蜥蜴窜到一块石头底下。 | 近义词：red-tailed hawk (for the bird),  | 反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与其他颜色尾巴的鸟类对比 | 名词
