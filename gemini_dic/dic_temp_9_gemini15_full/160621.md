portals:/ˈpɔːrtlz/| n.|门户网站；入口；通道|A portal is a doorway, gate, or other entrance, especially one that is large or impressive.  It can also refer to a website that serves as a starting point for accessing other websites or online resources.|例句：The ancient castle had many imposing portals.  古老的城堡有许多雄伟的入口。|例句：This website acts as a portal to various online resources. 这个网站充当了访问各种在线资源的门户。|近义词：gateway, entryway, access point|反义词：exit, outlet
