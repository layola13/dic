keening:/ˈkiːnɪŋ/| n. v.| 恸哭，哀号；悲叹 | A high-pitched, wailing cry, especially one expressing grief.  The act of making such a cry. |例句：The keening of the women filled the air.  妇女们的恸哭声充满了空气。

The mourners' keening was heartbreaking. 哀悼者的恸哭令人心碎。

近义词：wailing, lamenting, mourning, crying |反义词：rejoicing, celebrating
