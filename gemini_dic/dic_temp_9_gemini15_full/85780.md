haymaker:/ˈheɪˌmeɪkər/| n. |大力挥拳的人；（尤指）突然而猛烈的攻击；干草工人| A haymaker is a powerful, sweeping punch, especially one thrown suddenly and unexpectedly.  It can also refer to a person who makes hay, or a worker in a hayfield. |例句：He landed a haymaker that sent his opponent reeling. 他挥出一记重拳，把对手打得东倒西歪。

He's a real haymaker in the boxing ring.  他在拳击台上是个狠角色。

近义词：power punch, knockout blow, hay-maker(alternative spelling) |反义词：gentle blow, light tap |词性:名词
