countermanifestoes:/ˌkaʊntərmænɪˈfɛstoʊz/ | n. | 反宣言；反驳宣言 | A countermanifesto is a public declaration that opposes or refutes the ideas presented in a previous manifesto.  It often serves to challenge the dominant narrative or ideology expressed in the original manifesto.  Countermanifestoes are commonly used in political, social, and artistic contexts. | Example:  The artists issued a countermanifesto, rejecting the purely aesthetic principles outlined in the earlier document. = 艺术家们发布了一份反宣言，反对早期文件中概述的纯粹审美原则。 | 近义词：counter-statement, refutation, rebuttal | 反义词：manifesto, declaration of support
