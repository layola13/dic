lumberers:/ˈlʌmbərərz/| n.|伐木工人，木材工人|People who cut down trees for timber.  They work in forests, felling trees and preparing them for transport to sawmills.|The lumberers worked tirelessly, felling giant redwoods.|近义词：loggers, woodmen|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with office workers, etc.|名词
