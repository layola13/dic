revote:/ˌriːˈvoʊt/| v.|重新投票，再次投票 |To vote again on something.  This often happens after an initial vote is deemed invalid or inconclusive, or if there's a significant change in circumstances.|例句：The council decided to revote on the controversial budget. 市议会决定就备受争议的预算案重新投票。

例句：After a recount, they had to revote on the election results. 重新计票后，他们不得不重新投票决定选举结果。


近义词：reballot, repoll|反义词：abstain, invalidate
