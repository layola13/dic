selling-off: /ˈsɛlɪŋ ɔːf/ | v. |抛售，贱卖 | The act of selling something quickly and often at a reduced price, typically to raise cash or get rid of excess inventory.  |例句：The company is selling off its assets to pay off its debts. 这家公司正在抛售资产以偿还债务。


The company is selling off its outdated equipment to make room for new technology. 这家公司正在抛售过时的设备，以便为新技术腾出空间。


近义词：liquidation, clearance sale, fire sale | 反义词：hoarding, accumulating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are selling off their old furniture. = 他们在抛售旧家具。
The shop is selling off its summer stock. = 这家商店正在清仓处理夏季库存。


一般过去时:
They sold off their shares at a loss. = 他们亏本抛售了他们的股票。
The store sold off all its remaining inventory. = 商店清仓处理了所有剩余库存。


现在完成时:
We have sold off most of our possessions. = 我们已经抛售了大部分财产。
The company has sold off its subsidiary. = 公司已经将其子公司出售。


过去完成时:
They had sold off all their assets before the bankruptcy. = 破产前他们已经抛售了所有资产。
The farmer had sold off his entire crop before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，这位农民已经卖掉了全部收成。


将来时:
They will sell off their property next month. = 他们下个月将出售他们的房产。
The auction house will sell off the remaining items tomorrow. = 拍卖行将于明天拍卖剩余的物品。


虚拟语气:
If they sold off their assets, they would be able to pay their debts. = 如果他们抛售资产，他们就能偿还债务。
I wish they had sold off the property sooner. = 我真希望他们早点卖掉那处房产。


被动语态:
The assets were sold off quickly. = 资产被迅速抛售。
The outdated equipment is being sold off. = 过时的设备正在被抛售。


疑问句:
Are they selling off their business? = 他们正在出售他们的生意吗？
Will the company be selling off any more stock? = 公司还会抛售更多股票吗？


否定句:
They did not sell off their house. = 他们没有卖掉他们的房子。
The company is not selling off its most valuable assets. = 公司没有抛售其最有价值的资产。


PHRASE->
selling off assets = 抛售资产
selling off inventory = 清仓处理库存
selling off at a discount = 打折抛售
selling off shares = 抛售股票
selling off property = 出售房产
