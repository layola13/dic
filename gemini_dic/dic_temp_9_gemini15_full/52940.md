dined:/daɪnd/| v.|进餐，用餐|To have dinner or a formal meal.|例句：We dined at a fancy restaurant last night.  我们昨晚在一家高级餐厅用餐。|例句：They dined elegantly on lobster and champagne. 他们优雅地享用龙虾和香槟。|近义词：ate, supped, feasted|反义词：starved, fasted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dines at seven o'clock every night. = 他每天晚上七点吃饭。
We dine out once a week. = 我们每周外出就餐一次。

一般过去时:
They dined at a Michelin-starred restaurant. = 他们在米其林星级餐厅用餐。
I dined alone last night. = 我昨晚独自用餐。

现在完成时:
We have dined here many times before. = 我们以前在这里吃过很多次饭了。
She has never dined at such a luxurious restaurant. = 她从未在如此奢华的餐厅用餐过。

过去完成时:
They had already dined before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前他们已经吃过饭了。
We had dined and were ready for the concert. = 我们已经吃过饭了，准备去听音乐会。

将来时:
We will dine at the new Italian restaurant tomorrow. = 我们明天将在新的意大利餐厅用餐。
They will have dined by the time the movie starts. = 电影开演时他们已经吃过饭了。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would dine at a nicer restaurant. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去一家更好的餐厅吃饭。
I wish I had dined with them. = 我真希望我和他们一起吃饭。

被动语态:
Dinner was dined on quickly. = 晚饭很快就被吃掉了。(This usage is less common and sounds slightly unnatural)
The meal was being dined on when the lights went out. = 灯灭的时候，饭菜正在被享用。(This usage is also less common)


疑问句:
Did you dine well? = 你吃得好吗？
Where did they dine last night? = 他们昨晚在哪里吃饭？


否定句:
We didn't dine out last night. = 我们昨晚没有外出吃饭。
He did not dine with us. = 他没有和我们一起吃饭。


PHRASE->
dine out = 外出就餐
dine in = 在家吃饭
dine with = 与...一起吃饭
have a nice dine = 美好用餐
dine on = 以...为主食
