pitifullest: /ˈpɪtɪfʊləst/ | adj. | 最可怜的，最悲惨的 | Most pitiful;  expressing or evoking great pity.  It is the superlative form of "pitiful". |


例句：

The pitifullest sight was the starving children begging for food.  = 最可怜的景象是那些挨饿的孩子在乞讨食物。

His pitifullest excuse didn't convince anyone. = 他那最可怜的借口没有说服任何人。


近义词：most pathetic, most wretched, most miserable, most deplorable

反义词：most fortunate, most blessed, most joyful, most prosperous


词性：形容词
