overtheorizing: /ˌoʊvərˈθɪəriˌzaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度理论化；过度解释 |  Engaging in excessive theorizing; formulating theories beyond what is necessary or supported by evidence.  It often implies a detachment from practical considerations or empirical data. |


例句：

His overtheorizing led him to miss the obvious practical solution.  他的过度理论化使他错过了显而易见的实用解决方案。

The professor's lecture was criticized for its overtheorizing and lack of concrete examples. 教授的讲座因其过度理论化和缺乏具体例子而受到批评。


近义词：speculating excessively, overinterpreting, intellectualizing, overanalyzing


反义词：practicality, empiricism, pragmatism


词性：动词 (gerund form)
