peed:/spiːd/| n.|速度，速度，快|The speed of light is incredibly fast. 光速非常快。|近义词：velocity, pace, rate|反义词：slowness, sluggishness

peed:/spiːd/| v.|加速，加快速度，飞奔|He sped down the highway. 他在高速公路上飞驰。|近义词：accelerate, hasten, quicken|反义词：slow down, decelerate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car speeds along the highway. = 这辆车沿着高速公路飞驰。
He speeds up when he sees the police. = 当他看到警察时，他加快了速度。


一般过去时:

We have sped through the work. = 我们快速完成了工作。

词源:
The word "speed" comes from the Old English word "sped," meaning "success, prosperity."  Over time, its meaning shifted to emphasize swiftness and rapid movement.  The verb form retains a connection to this older sense of successful completion.
