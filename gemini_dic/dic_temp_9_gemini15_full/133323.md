nonexportable: /ˌnɒnɪkˈspɔːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可出口的 | Not able to be exported; prohibited from being exported.  This adjective describes goods, services, or technologies that are restricted from leaving a country due to legal, economic, or security reasons.  |


例句：

Due to national security concerns, this software is nonexportable. = 由于国家安全考虑，此软件不可出口。

The embargo made many previously exportable goods nonexportable. = 禁运使许多以前可以出口的商品变成了不可出口的商品。


近义词：unexportable, prohibited, restricted, embargoed


反义词：exportable


词性：形容词
