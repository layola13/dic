denay:/dɪˈneɪ/| v.|否认，拒绝|To deny something is to state that it is not true or to refuse to accept it.  It can also mean to refuse to give or allow something.|例句：He denied all knowledge of the crime. 他否认对这起犯罪有任何了解。|例句：They denied him entry to the club. 他们拒绝他进入俱乐部。|近义词：refute, reject, disclaim|反义词：affirm, admit, concede|动词
