siluroid:/ˈsɪljʊrɔɪd/| n. |鲶形目鱼类；鲶鱼|A siluroid is a fish belonging to the order Siluriformes, which includes catfishes.  They are characterized by their typically lack of scales, barbels around the mouth, and often a slippery skin.  Many species are nocturnal and bottom-dwellers.|例句：Many siluroids are important food sources in various parts of the world. 许多鲶形目鱼类是世界各地重要的食物来源。|例句：The Amazon River is home to a wide variety of siluroid species. 亚马逊河是各种鲶形目鱼类物种的家园。|近义词：catfish (more common name for many siluroids)|反义词：None specifically; the antonym would depend on what characteristic of siluroids is being contrasted.  For example, a scaled fish could be considered an antonym in terms of skin texture.|名词
