personalness:/ˈpɜːrsənəl.nəs/| n. |个人特性；个人隐私；私密性 |The degree to which something is personal or private.  It refers to the quality of being personal, intimate, or private; the extent to which something is related to an individual's feelings, experiences, or identity.  |例句：The personalness of the conversation made me uncomfortable.  谈话的私密性让我感到不舒服。

The personalness of her diary entries was striking. 她日记里私人内容的程度令人震惊。|近义词：privacy, intimacy, individuality |反义词：impersonality, publicness, objectivity |词性：名词
