overwrite:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪt/ | vt. |覆盖写入；改写；覆盖；推翻 |To write over something that has already been written, or to replace something with something new.  |例句：I accidentally overwrote my important document. 我不小心覆盖了我的重要文件。
例句：The new software will overwrite the old version. 新软件将覆盖旧版本。|近义词：replace, supersede, erase |反义词：preserve, retain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overwrites his old files regularly. 他定期覆盖他的旧文件。
She overwrites the document with new information. 她用新信息覆盖了文档。

一般过去时:
I accidentally overwrote the file yesterday. 我昨天不小心覆盖了文件。
They overwrote the previous settings. 他们覆盖了之前的设置。

现在完成时:
He has overwritten the entire hard drive. 他已经覆盖了整个硬盘。
She has overwritten the original manuscript. 她已经改写了原始手稿。

过去完成时:
I had overwritten the file before I realized my mistake. 我在意识到我的错误之前已经覆盖了文件。
They had overwritten the data before the backup was completed. 在备份完成之前，他们已经覆盖了数据。

将来时:
I will overwrite the file tomorrow. 我明天将覆盖这个文件。
They will overwrite the existing code. 他们将覆盖现有的代码。

虚拟语气:
If I had known it would overwrite the file, I wouldn't have done it. 如果我知道这会覆盖文件，我就不会这么做了。
It would be a mistake to overwrite the data without a backup.  没有备份就覆盖数据将是一个错误。

被动语态:
The file was overwritten by the new program.  文件被新程序覆盖了。
The old settings were overwritten. 旧设置被覆盖了。

疑问句:
Did you overwrite the file? 你覆盖了文件吗？
Will the update overwrite my current settings? 更新会覆盖我当前的设置吗？

否定句:
I didn't overwrite the file. 我没有覆盖文件。
They did not overwrite the original data. 他们没有覆盖原始数据。

词源:
The word "overwrite" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "above," "beyond," or "again") and the verb "write."  It literally means "to write over" something else.  Its origins trace back to the Old English word "writan," meaning "to write."
