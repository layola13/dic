hallucinogens:/həˈluːsɪnədʒənz/ | n. |致幻剂 |Substances that cause hallucinations, altering perception, thoughts, and feelings.  They can be natural or synthetic and often have significant risks associated with their use. | Example:  LSD and psilocybin are potent hallucinogens.  迷幻药LSD和裸盖菇素是强效致幻剂。|近义词：psychedelics, psychotomimetics |反义词：None directly opposite, but substances with opposite effects could be considered (e.g., sedatives).
