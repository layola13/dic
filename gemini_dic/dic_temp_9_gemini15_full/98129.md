insanely:/ɪnˈseɪnli/ | adv. | 疯狂地；极其；非常 | Extremely; to a great degree; wildly.  |例句：He was insanely jealous of his brother's success. 他疯狂地嫉妒他兄弟的成功。  |例句：The movie was insanely popular. 这部电影极其受欢迎。|近义词：madly, incredibly, extremely, wildly |反义词：calmly, reasonably, moderately
