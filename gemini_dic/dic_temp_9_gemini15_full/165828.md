preoccupate:/ˌpriːˈɒkjʊpeɪt/| vt.|使全神贯注；使操心；抢先占有|To dominate or engross the mind of (someone); to be the main thing someone is thinking about.  It can also mean to occupy something beforehand.|例句：The problem of climate change preoccupies many world leaders.  气候变化问题使许多世界领导人忧心忡忡。

例句：He was so preoccupied with his work that he didn't notice me. 他工作太投入了，没注意到我。|近义词：absorb, engross, occupy, distract|反义词：distract, ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His worries preoccupy him. = 他的担忧使他心烦意乱。
The thought of the exam preoccupies her. = 考试的念头让她心神不宁。

一般过去时:
The news preoccupied her all day. = 这个消息让她整天都心烦意乱。
The upcoming trip preoccupied his thoughts. = 即将来临的旅行占据了他的思绪。

现在完成时:
The project has preoccupied me for months. = 这个项目已经让我忙了好几个月了。
Financial problems have preoccupied her lately. = 最近财务问题一直困扰着她。

过去完成时:
She had been preoccupied with her studies and missed the phone call. = 她一直忙于学习，错过了电话。
He had been preoccupied with his own affairs and hadn't noticed the change. = 他一直忙于自己的事情，没有注意到变化。

将来时:
The meeting will preoccupy us for the entire afternoon. = 会议将占据我们整个下午的时间。
I know that work will preoccupy me this week. = 我知道本周工作会让我很忙碌。


虚拟语气:
If only I weren't so preoccupied, I could help you. = 如果我不那么忙的话，我可以帮你。
I wish I weren't preoccupied with so many things. = 我真希望我不必为这么多事情操心。

被动语态:
He was preoccupied with the upcoming deadline. = 他被即将到来的截止日期弄得心烦意乱。(被动语态较少用)
She was preoccupied by her family matters. = 她被家事缠身。(被动语态较少用)

疑问句:
Is he preoccupied with something? = 他是不是有什么心事？
What preoccupies you so much these days? = 你最近为什么这么心烦意乱？

否定句:
He wasn't preoccupied with the meeting. = 他并没有被会议占用太多精力。
She wasn't preoccupied at all; she was just daydreaming. = 她一点也不心烦意乱；她只是在做白日梦。


PHRASE->
be preoccupied with = 专注于；忙于
become preoccupied = 变得心烦意乱
preoccupied expression = 心事重重的神情
deeply preoccupied = 深陷于...；极度专注于...
preoccupied silence = 沉默不语（因心事重重）
