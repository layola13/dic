nonaction: /ˈnɑːnˈækʃən/ | n. | 不作为；无行动；消极 | The absence of action; inaction.  It can refer to a deliberate choice not to act, or a failure to act. |例句：The government's nonaction on climate change is alarming. 政府对气候变化的不作为令人担忧。

例句：His nonaction in the face of danger cost him dearly. 他面对危险时的不作为让他付出了沉重的代价。


近义词：inaction, passivity, inertia, dormancy, quiescence

反义词：action, activity, intervention, engagement


词性: 名词
