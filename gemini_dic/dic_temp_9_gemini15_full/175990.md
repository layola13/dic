rattlewort: /ˈrætlˌwɔːrt/ | n. | 响铃草 | A plant of the genus *Rhinanthus*, having inflated calyces that rattle when dry.  It is also known as yellow rattle. | Example: The meadow was dotted with rattlewort, its dry seed pods making a clicking sound in the breeze.  =  草地上点缀着响铃草，其干燥的豆荚在微风中发出咔哒声。|近义词：yellow rattle|反义词：无
