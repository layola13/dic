clip-clopping:/klɪpˈklɒpɪŋ/| n.|得得的声音，马蹄声|The sound of a horse's hooves hitting the ground.  It is an onomatopoeia, meaning it imitates the sound it describes. |例句：The clip-clopping of horses' hooves echoed down the lane. 马蹄得得的声音在小巷回响。

例句：We could hear the clip-clopping of the horse and carriage approaching. 我们能听到马车得得的马蹄声越来越近。


近义词：hoofbeats, clatter,  the sound of horses' hooves


反义词：silence, stillness


词性:名词
