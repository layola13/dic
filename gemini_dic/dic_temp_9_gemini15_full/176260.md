re-cooling:/ˌriːˈkuːlɪŋ/| n.|再次冷却；再冷却|The process of cooling something down again after it has already been cooled once.  It often refers to industrial processes or specific systems. |例句：The re-cooling system of the power plant is crucial for its efficient operation. 电厂的再冷却系统对其高效运行至关重要。

The re-cooling process ensures the product maintains its quality. 再冷却过程确保产品保持其质量。|近义词：re-chilling, secondary cooling|反义词：reheating
