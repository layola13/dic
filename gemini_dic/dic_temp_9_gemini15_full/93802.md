immersions:/ɪˈmɜːʒənz/| n. |沉浸；浸没；融入；沉浸式体验 | The act of immersing or the state of being immersed;  complete involvement or engagement.  It can refer to physical immersion in a liquid, or metaphorical immersion in an experience or activity. |例句：The immersion of the metal in acid caused a chemical reaction. 金属浸入酸中引起了化学反应。

His immersion in the world of virtual reality was total. 他完全沉浸在虚拟现实世界中。

近义词：submersion, engulfment, involvement, immersion | 反义词：emergence, extraction, detachment
