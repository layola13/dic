performant: /pərˈfɔːrmənt/ | adj. | 高效的，性能优良的 |  Having high performance; efficient and effective.  This word is relatively new and primarily used in the context of software and technology, describing systems or applications that operate quickly and efficiently. | 例句：The new server is far more performant than the old one. = 新服务器比旧服务器高效得多。


The new software update makes the application significantly more performant. = 新的软件更新使应用程序的性能显著提高。| 近义词：efficient, high-performing, powerful, effective | 反义词：inefficient, slow, underperforming, sluggish | 词性: adjective
