overvalues:/ˌoʊvərˈvæljuːz/| vt.|过高估价，高估|To place a higher value on something than it is actually worth.|The company overvalues its assets on its balance sheet.  这家公司在其资产负债表上高估了其资产。|He overvalues his own abilities. 他高估了自己的能力。| undervalues, underestimates | undervalues, underestimates |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The market overvalues this stock. = 市场高估了这只股票。
She overvalues her contributions to the team. = 她高估了她对团队的贡献。


一般过去时:
They overvalued the property by 20%. = 他们将该房产高估了20%。
He overvalued his chances of success. = 他高估了他成功的几率。


现在完成时:
The collector has overvalued many of his stamps. = 这位收藏家高估了他的许多邮票。
Investors have overvalued tech stocks recently. = 投资者最近高估了科技股。


过去完成时:
