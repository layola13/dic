confiscate:/ˈkɒnfɪskeɪt/ /ˈkɑːnfɪskeɪt/ | vt. |没收；充公 | To seize (property) by or as if by authority of law; to take possession of (something, especially property) for legal reasons.  |例句：The police confiscated the drugs.  警察没收了毒品。

The government confiscated the land for a new highway. 政府没收了土地修建新的高速公路。

近义词：seize, impound, appropriate, expropriate |反义词：return, restore, relinquish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government confiscates illegal goods. = 政府没收非法商品。
Customs officials confiscate contraband regularly. = 海关官员定期没收违禁品。

一般过去时:
The police confiscated his driver's license. = 警察没收了他的驾照。
They confiscated the stolen property. = 他们没收了被盗的财产。

现在完成时:
The authorities have confiscated numerous weapons. = 当局已经没收了大量武器。
The school has confiscated many cell phones this year. = 学校今年没收了很多手机。

过去完成时:
The judge had confiscated the assets before the trial. = 法官在审判前已没收了资产。
The rebels had confiscated all the food supplies. = 反叛者已经没收了所有食物供应。

将来时:
They will confiscate the car if the owner doesn't pay the fines. = 如果车主不缴纳罚款，他们将没收汽车。
The court will confiscate the property as a penalty. = 法院将没收财产作为惩罚。

被动语态:
His property was confiscated by the state. = 他的财产被国家没收了。
The illegal drugs were confiscated by the police. = 这些毒品被警察没收了。

疑问句:
Will the court confiscate his assets? = 法院会没收他的资产吗？
Were the documents confiscated during the raid? = 突袭期间文件是否被没收了？


否定句:
The police did not confiscate any evidence. = 警察没有没收任何证据。
They did not confiscate the letters. = 他们没有没收信件。

词源:
