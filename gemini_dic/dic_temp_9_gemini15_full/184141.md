resistingly:/rɪˈzɪstɪŋli/| adv.|反抗地；抵抗地|In a manner that shows resistance or opposition.  It describes the action of resisting something or someone. |例句：He pulled away resistingly. 他反抗地挣脱了。|例句：The prisoner resisted arrest resistingly. 囚犯反抗地拒绝被捕。|近义词：defyingly, rebelliously, oppositionally|反义词：willingly, obediently, submissively|副词
