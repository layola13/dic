neatly:/ˈniːtli/ | adj. | 整洁地；干净地；漂亮地 |  Adjective describing something that is arranged in a tidy and pleasing manner. It suggests careful organization and a lack of messiness or disorder. | The children neatly arranged their toys after playing. 孩子们玩完后整齐地收拾了玩具。  | tidy, orderly, trim, spick and span | messy, untidy, cluttered, disorganized |
