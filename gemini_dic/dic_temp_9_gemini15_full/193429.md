seizor:/ˈsiːzər/| n.| 夺取者；没收者；扣押者 | One who seizes or takes possession of something, especially by force or legal authority.  A person who seizes property, especially by legal process. |例句：The police acted as the seizor of the contraband goods.  警察充当了没收违禁品的没收者。

The seizor of the stolen car was rewarded for their efforts.  追回被盗汽车的人因其努力而受到奖励。|近义词：confiscator, appropriator, arrester |反义词：restorer, returner
