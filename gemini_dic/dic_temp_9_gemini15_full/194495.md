self-ruin:/ˌsɛlfˈruːɪn/| n.|自我毁灭，自取灭亡|The act of destroying oneself or one's own prospects through one's own actions or behavior.  It often implies a gradual process of decline brought about by one's own choices.|例句：His self-ruin was a slow, agonizing process fueled by addiction.  他的自我毁灭是一个缓慢而痛苦的过程，由瘾头驱使。

例句：Her relentless pursuit of power led to her ultimate self-ruin. 她对权力的无情追求最终导致了她的自我毁灭。|近义词：self-destruction, downfall, ruin, demise|反义词：self-preservation, success, triumph|名词
