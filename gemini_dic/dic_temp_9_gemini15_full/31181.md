changeful:/ˈtʃeɪndʒfəl/| adj.| 易变的；变化无常的；多变的 | characterized by frequent or unexpected changes; fickle; unstable. |例句：The weather in this region is notoriously changeful.  这个地区的天气以变化无常而闻名。

例句：The stock market is a very changeful environment. 股市是一个非常多变的环境。


近义词：fickle, capricious, volatile, mutable, unsteady, variable

反义词：constant, stable, unchanging, steady, immutable
