gleams:/ɡliːmz/ | v. |闪光；闪耀|To shine with a bright, reflected light.|The diamond gleamed on her finger.  钻石在她手指上闪闪发光。|The polished brass gleamed in the sunlight.  擦亮的黄铜在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：shine, shimmer, glitter|反义词：dull, dim


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The star gleams brightly in the night sky. = 星星在夜空中明亮地闪耀。
The polished surface gleams under the lamp. = 抛光后的表面在灯下闪闪发光。

一般过去时:
The candle gleamed softly in the darkness. = 蜡烛在黑暗中柔和地闪耀。
His eyes gleamed with excitement. = 他的眼睛因兴奋而闪闪发光。

现在完成时:
The dewdrop has gleamed on the leaf all morning. = 露珠整个上午都在叶子上闪耀。
The trophy has gleamed proudly on the shelf for years. = 奖杯多年来一直自豪地陈列在架子上闪闪发光。

过去完成时:
The sword had gleamed menacingly before the battle. = 在战斗之前，宝剑曾发出威胁性的闪光。
The diamond had gleamed brilliantly before it was stolen. = 钻石在被偷之前曾闪耀着耀眼的光芒。

将来时:
The newly polished car will gleam beautifully tomorrow. = 新抛光的汽车明天会闪闪发光。
The sun will gleam brightly through the window. = 太阳会透过窗户明亮地闪耀。

虚拟语气:
If the sun were shining, the snow would gleam. = 如果阳光明媚，雪就会闪闪发光。
I wish my new car would gleam like that forever. = 我希望我的新车能永远闪耀那样。

被动语态:
The gems were gleamed under the spotlights. = 宝石在聚光灯下闪闪发光。
The surface is gleamed by the sunlight. = 表面被阳光照射得闪闪发光。(注意：此被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)

疑问句:
Does the metal gleam in the light? = 金属在光线下闪闪发光吗？
Do your eyes gleam with happiness? = 你的眼睛因幸福而闪闪发光吗？

否定句:
The dull metal doesn't gleam. = 黯淡的金属不闪光。
Her eyes didn't gleam with interest. = 她的眼睛没有流露出兴趣。
