rikshaws:/ˈrɪkʃɔːz/| n.|人力车，黄包车|A rickshaw is a two-wheeled or three-wheeled vehicle, typically pulled by a person or powered by a motor, used for transporting passengers.|例句：Many tourists enjoy riding rickshaws through the bustling streets of Old Delhi. 许多游客喜欢乘坐人力车穿梭于旧德里的繁华街道。

例句：The streets were filled with colorful rickshaws, each with its own unique design. 街道上挤满了色彩鲜艳的人力车，每辆车都有其独特的设计。|近义词：cycle rickshaw, auto rickshaw, pedicab|反义词:car, taxi, bus
