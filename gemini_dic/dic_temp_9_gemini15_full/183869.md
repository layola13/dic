reselling:/ˌriːˈsɛlɪŋ/| v. |转售，再售|The act of selling something that you have previously bought, often at a higher price.  It involves buying goods or services with the intention of reselling them for profit.  |例句：He made a profit by reselling the antique furniture. 他通过转售古董家具赚了一笔钱。

例句：She resells clothes she buys at thrift stores. 她转售她在旧货店买的衣服。


近义词：re-selling, trading, dealing in


反义词：buying, purchasing


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resells vintage clothing online. = 他在网上转售复古服装。
She resells her artwork through a gallery. = 她通过画廊转售她的艺术品。

一般过去时:
They resold the car at a higher price. = 他们以更高的价格转售了那辆车。

过去完成时:

They had resold the property before the new regulations were implemented. = 在新规实施之前，他们已经转售了该房产。

虚拟语气:
