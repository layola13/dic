penitences: /ˈpɛnɪtənsɪz/ | n. | 忏悔；悔罪；苦行 |  The act of showing sorrow and regret for having done wrong; repentance.  Penitences are often expressed through acts of self-punishment or atonement. | 例句：He performed acts of penitence to atone for his sins. 他做了忏悔的行为来赎罪。


He expressed deep penitence for his hurtful words. 他为他伤人的话表达了深深的悔恨。


近义词：repentance, contrition, remorse, atonement | 反义词：complacency, self-satisfaction
