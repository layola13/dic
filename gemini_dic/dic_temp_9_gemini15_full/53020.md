dinting:/ˈdɪntɪŋ/| n. |凹痕；压痕；(金属的)压印|The act of making a small dent or indentation in a surface, typically by striking it.  It can also refer to the dent itself.|例句：The car had a dinting on the bumper after the accident.  车祸后保险杠上有一个凹痕。
The metalworker skillfully created a dinting pattern on the plate.  金属工人在金属板上巧妙地制作了压印图案。|近义词：dent, indentation, impression|反义词：smoothness, flatness
