regenerate:/ˌriːˈdʒɛnəreɪt/| vt. vi.|再生；更新；使再生；复兴|To regenerate means to grow or develop new tissue, cells, or organs to replace old, damaged, or lost ones.  It can also refer to the renewal or revival of something.  The verb can be used in both transitive (acting on something else) and intransitive (acting on itself) senses.|例句：The salamander can regenerate a lost limb.  这只蝾螈可以再生失去的肢体。

The city is trying to regenerate its downtown area. 这个城市正在努力重建其市中心区域。

近义词：renew, revitalize, revive, reform|反义词：destroy, ruin, decay, degenerate|动词
