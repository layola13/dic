mountainscape:/ˈmaʊntənskeɪp/| n. |山景，山区景色 |A mountainscape is a landscape dominated by mountains. It often includes details such as valleys, forests, and rivers, but the mountains are the central feature.  The term emphasizes the visual aspect and the overall impression of a mountain range or a mountainous area. | Example: The breathtaking mountainscape unfolded before us, a panorama of peaks and valleys bathed in the golden light of sunset.  The painting depicted a dramatic mountainscape, with jagged peaks piercing a stormy sky. |近义词：mountain scenery, mountain view, highland vista |反义词：seascape, cityscape
