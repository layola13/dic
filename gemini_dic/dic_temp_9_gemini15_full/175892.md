ratfish: /ˈrætfɪʃ/ | n. |鼠鱼，魔鬼鱼 | A type of cartilaginous fish, characterized by a flattened body, large pectoral fins, and a rat-like tail.  They are bottom-dwelling fish found in the deep sea. | Example:  The deep-sea ratfish is a fascinating creature with unique adaptations to its environment. 深海鼠鱼是一种具有独特环境适应能力的迷人生物。 | 近义词：chimaera (a more formal and scientific term) | 反义词：N/A
