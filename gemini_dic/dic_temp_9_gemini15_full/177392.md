reavowed:/ˌriːəˈvaʊd/| v.|重新宣誓；重申；再声明|To declare or affirm again; to renew a vow or pledge.  The word implies a previous declaration that is being reiterated, often with stronger emphasis.|例句：He reavowed his commitment to the cause after a period of doubt. 他在一段时间的怀疑后再次承诺支持这项事业。

例句：After the scandal, she reavowed her innocence.  丑闻之后，她再次声明自己的清白。

近义词：reaffirm, reaffirm, reiterate, restate, reassert|反义词：renounce, recant, retract|词性：动词
