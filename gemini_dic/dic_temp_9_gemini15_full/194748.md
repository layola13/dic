selic:/ˈsɛlɪk/| n.|巴西中央银行利率|The acronym SELIC stands for Sistema Especial de Liquidação e Custódia, which translates to Special System of Settlement and Custody in English.  It refers to the benchmark interest rate set by the Brazilian Central Bank (Banco Central do Brasil). This rate influences other interest rates in the Brazilian economy. |例句：The SELIC rate is currently at 13.75%. = 巴西央行利率目前为13.75%。
The Central Bank uses adjustments to the SELIC rate to control inflation. = 中央银行利用调整SELIC利率来控制通货膨胀。|近义词：benchmark interest rate, policy rate|反义词：N/A|名词
