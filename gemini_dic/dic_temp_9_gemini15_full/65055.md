eternalism:/ɪˈtɜːrnəlɪzəm/| n. |永恒论；永恒主义|A philosophical view that all moments in time are equally real, and that the past, present, and future all exist simultaneously.  This contrasts with presentism, which holds that only the present moment is real.|例句：Many physicists find the concept of eternalism difficult to grasp, preferring a presentism view of time.  许多物理学家发现永恒论的概念难以理解，更倾向于一种唯现在主义的时间观。

例句：The debate between eternalism and presentism continues to fascinate philosophers.  永恒论与唯现在主义之间的争论继续吸引着哲学家们。

近义词：timelessness, perpetuity|反义词：presentism
