overturn:/ˌoʊvərˈtɜːrn/|/ˈəʊvəˈtɜːn/| vt. n. |推翻；颠覆；倾覆|To cause (something) to turn over; to overturn something.  To overthrow or abolish something, typically a system or institution.|The strong winds overturned several trees. 强风吹倒了几棵树。|The court overturned the lower court's decision. 法院推翻了下级法院的判决。|upend, overthrow, capsize|uphold, sustain, affirm|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong wind overturns the small boats.  强风掀翻了小船。
He overturns the table in anger. 他生气地掀翻了桌子。


一般过去时:
The judge overturned the guilty verdict. 法官推翻了有罪判决。
The revolution overturned the monarchy.  革命推翻了君主制。


现在完成时:
Recent events have overturned our carefully laid plans. 最近发生的事件打乱了我们精心制定的计划。
The scandal has overturned his career.  丑闻毁了他的事业。


过去完成时:
The evidence had overturned the initial assumptions of the investigators. 证据推翻了调查人员最初的假设。
By the time the police arrived, the thieves had overturned several cars.  警察到达时，小偷已经推翻了几辆车。


将来时:
The new law will overturn decades of precedent. 新法将推翻数十年的先例。
I predict that this discovery will overturn current scientific theories. 我预测这一发现将推翻目前的科学理论。


虚拟语气:
If the government were to overturn this decision, it would face massive protests. 如果政府要推翻这个决定，将会面临大规模抗议。
I wish they would overturn the unfair ruling. 我希望他们会推翻这个不公平的裁决。


被动语态:
The decision was overturned by the appeals court.  该决定被上诉法院推翻。
The car was overturned in the accident. 车在事故中翻了。


疑问句:
Will the court overturn the verdict? 法院会推翻判决吗？
Has the government overturned its previous policy? 政府是否推翻了其先前的政策？


否定句:
The judge refused to overturn the conviction. 法官拒绝推翻定罪。
The evidence did not overturn the prosecution's case.  证据并未推翻检方的指控。
