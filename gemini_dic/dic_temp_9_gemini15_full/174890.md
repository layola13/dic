racking-up: /ˈrækiŋ ʌp/ | v. | 积累；达到；获得 |  To accumulate or achieve a large amount or number of something.  |例句：The company is racking up record profits this year.  今年该公司利润创纪录。|  例句：He's racking up miles on his new car. 他正用他的新车行驶许多英里。| 近义词：accumulating, amassing, piling up | 反义词：losing, diminishing, depleting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are racking up points in the game. = 他们在比赛中积累分数。
She racks up frequent flyer miles every year. = 她每年都积累很多飞行常客里程。


一般过去时:
The team racked up a significant victory. = 球队取得了一场重要的胜利。
He racked up a huge debt in college. = 他在大学期间欠下了巨额债务。


现在完成时:
We have racked up considerable experience in this field. = 我们在这个领域积累了相当丰富的经验。
She has racked up many awards for her acting. = 她因其精湛的演技获得了许多奖项。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had racked up impressive sales figures. = 到年底，他们已经取得了令人印象深刻的销售业绩。
Before the tournament, he had racked up several wins in smaller competitions. = 在锦标赛之前，他已经在较小的比赛中取得了几场胜利。


将来时:
They will rack up more wins if they continue playing well. = 如果他们继续打得好，他们将赢得更多胜利。
We expect to rack up significant savings with this new system. = 我们预计这个新系统将节省大量资金。


虚拟语气:
If they played better, they would rack up more points. = 如果他们打得更好，他们就能得到更多分数。
I wish I had racked up more savings before retirement. = 我希望我在退休前积累更多储蓄。


被动语态:
A huge debt was racked up by the government. = 政府积累了巨额债务。
Many miles were racked up during the long road trip. = 在漫长的公路旅行中行驶了许多英里。


疑问句:
How many points have they racked up so far? = 到目前为止他们积累了多少分？
How much debt has the company racked up? = 公司积累了多少债务？


否定句:
They didn't rack up as many points as they expected. = 他们没有积累到他们预期的那样多分。
The project didn't rack up the anticipated profits. = 该项目没有获得预期的利润。
