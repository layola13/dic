premedicates:/ˌpriːˈmɛdɪkeɪts/| vt.| 预先用药；预防性用药 | To administer medication to (a patient) before a procedure or event to prevent or reduce the likelihood of a particular effect or complication.  |例句：The surgeon premedicated the patient with an anti-anxiety drug before the operation. 外科医生在手术前给病人服用抗焦虑药物进行预先用药。|例句：The dentist premedicated the child to reduce their fear and pain during the procedure. 牙医给孩子预先用药以减少他们在治疗过程中的恐惧和疼痛。|近义词：prophylactically medicate, prepare, treat beforehand |反义词：post-medicate
