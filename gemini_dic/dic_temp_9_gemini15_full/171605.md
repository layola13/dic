pulmonate:/ˈpʌlmənət/| adj. n.|肺呼吸的；肺螺类动物|Having lungs or lung-like structures for respiration.  Pulmonates are a large group of gastropods (snails and slugs) that breathe air using a lung. |例句：Many pulmonate snails live in moist terrestrial environments. 许多肺螺类蜗牛生活在潮湿的陆地环境中。The pulmonate respiratory system is quite efficient. 肺呼吸系统相当有效率。|近义词：lung-breathing |反义词：aquatic (for some species), gill-breathing
