cookhouse: /ˈkʊkˌhaʊs/ | n. | 伙房；厨房；食堂 | A room or building where food is cooked, especially in a large institution such as a camp, school, or army barracks.  | 例句：The cookhouse was filled with the aroma of freshly baked bread.  伙房里弥漫着新鲜烘焙面包的香味。

The cookhouse served three meals a day to the workers.  食堂每天为工人提供三餐。| 近义词：kitchen, galley, mess hall | 反义词：dining room (in the sense of a more formal eating area)
