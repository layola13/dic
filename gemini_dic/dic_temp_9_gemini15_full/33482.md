chyme:/kaɪm/| n. |食糜，半消化食物 |The semifluid mass of partly digested food that passes from the stomach into the duodenum.  Chyme is a mixture of partially digested food, water, and digestive enzymes. |The stomach churns the food into chyme. 胃将食物搅成食糜。|  gastric juice, bolus |  solid food | 名词
