narrates:/nəˈreɪts/| vt.|叙述；讲述；作…的解说|To tell a story or describe a series of events.|The book narrates the history of the Roman Empire. 这本书叙述了罗马帝国的历史。|The documentary narrates the life of a famous scientist. 这部纪录片讲述了一位著名科学家的生平。|relates, recounts, describes|misrepresents


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He narrates the story with great passion. 他充满激情地讲述着这个故事。
She narrates her experiences in a captivating way. 她以一种引人入胜的方式讲述着她的经历。


一般过去时:
The author narrated the events leading up to the war. 作者叙述了导致战争的事件。
He narrated a funny anecdote about his childhood. 他讲述了一个关于他童年的有趣轶事。


现在完成时:
She has narrated several best-selling novels. 她已经讲述了好几部畅销小说。
He has narrated his life story in his autobiography. 他在他的自传中讲述了他的生活故事。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, he had narrated the whole incident to the police. 在审判之前，他已经向警方讲述了整个事件。
She had narrated the story many times to her grandchildren. 她已经多次向她的孙子孙女讲述了这个故事。


将来时:
He will narrate his experiences in a new book. 他将在新书中讲述他的经历。
She will narrate the story from a different perspective. 她将从不同的角度讲述这个故事。


虚拟语气:
If he narrated the story differently, it would be more interesting. 如果他以不同的方式讲述这个故事，它会更有趣。
I wish he had narrated the events more clearly. 我希望他更清晰地讲述这些事件。


被动语态:
The story was narrated by a famous author. 这个故事是由一位著名作家讲述的。
The events were narrated in chronological order. 这些事件按时间顺序叙述。


疑问句:
Who narrated the story? 谁讲述了这个故事？
Does she narrate the events accurately? 她是否准确地叙述了这些事件？


否定句:
He did not narrate the story correctly. 他没有正确地讲述这个故事。
She doesn't narrate her feelings openly. 她不会公开地表达她的感受。


PHRASE->
narrate a story 讲述一个故事
narrate an event 叙述一个事件
narrate the details 详细叙述
narrate one's experiences 讲述自己的经历
narrate the facts 陈述事实
