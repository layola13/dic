phrenologists:/frɛˈnɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |颅相学家|Those who study phrenology, a pseudoscience that involves examining the shape and size of the skull to determine a person's character and mental abilities.  Phrenologists believed that different areas of the brain corresponded to specific personality traits and that these areas could be assessed by feeling the bumps on the skull.|The phrenologists examined the bumps on the patient's head to determine his personality traits.  颅相学家检查病人头部的隆起以确定他的性格特征。|physiognomists, character analysts| (no direct antonym, as it's a pseudoscience)|名词
