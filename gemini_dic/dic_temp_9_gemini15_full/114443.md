maker:/ˈmeɪkər/ | n. |制造者，创造者，厂商 |A maker is a person or thing that makes something.  It can refer to the person who creates something, the company that produces a product, or even a device that generates something. |例句：He is the maker of this beautiful sculpture. 他是这件精美雕塑的创作者。|例句：That company is a leading maker of smartphones. 那家公司是领先的智能手机制造商。|近义词：creator, manufacturer, producer, constructor |反义词：destroyer, breaker
