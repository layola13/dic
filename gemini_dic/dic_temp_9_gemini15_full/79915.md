gneissosity:/naɪˈsɒsɪti/| n. |片麻状构造，片理|The parallel alignment of minerals in metamorphic rocks, giving them a banded or layered appearance.  This structure is characteristic of gneiss. |The gneissosity of the rock was clearly visible in the outcrop. 这块岩石的片麻状构造在露头上清晰可见。|foliation, banding|massive, homogeneous|名词
