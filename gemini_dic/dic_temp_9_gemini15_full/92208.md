hypertechnicalities:/ˌhaɪpərˌtɛknɪˈkælɪtiz/| n. |技术细节；过于专业的细节|Highly specialized or excessively detailed technical aspects that are often difficult for non-experts to understand.  It often implies an unnecessary level of detail or complexity. |The presentation was bogged down in hypertechnicalities, losing the audience's attention.  演示陷入了过于专业的细节中，失去了观众的注意力。|technicalities, complexities, minutiae|simplicity, clarity|名词
