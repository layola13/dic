jacarandas:/ˌdʒæˈkærændəz/| n. |蓝花楹属植物；蓝花楹树|A jacaranda is a genus of flowering plants in the family Bignoniaceae, native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America and the Caribbean.  They are known for their beautiful, vibrant purple flowers. |例句：The jacarandas were in full bloom, creating a breathtaking purple canopy. 蓝花楹盛开，形成令人叹为观止的紫色树冠。

The jacaranda tree in the park provided much-needed shade on a hot day. 公园里的蓝花楹在炎热的一天提供了急需的阴凉。|近义词：tree (general term), flowering tree|反义词：无
