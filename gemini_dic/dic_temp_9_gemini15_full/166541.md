presbyteries:/ˈprɛzbɪtəriːz/| n. |长老会；长老会议；长老会教堂 |The governing body of a Presbyterian church, usually composed of ministers and elders.  It's responsible for overseeing the spiritual and administrative affairs of the church.  |例句：The presbyteries will meet next week to discuss the budget.  长老会下周将开会讨论预算。 The local presbyteries are known for their community outreach programs. 当地长老会以其社区外展项目而闻名。|近义词：synod, consistory |反义词：N/A
