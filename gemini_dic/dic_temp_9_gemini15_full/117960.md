mementoes:/mɛˈmɛntoʊz/| n.|纪念品，遗物|Objects that serve as reminders of a person, place, or event.  They are often kept as souvenirs or keepsakes.|例句：She brought back several mementoes from her trip to Italy. 她从意大利旅行带回了许多纪念品。

例句：The old photograph served as a memento of happier times. 这张旧照片是幸福时光的纪念品。


近义词：souvenirs, keepsakes, reminders, relics


反义词：None directly opposite, but antonyms could be things that represent forgetting or discarding.


词性:名词

词源:
From the Latin word *memento*, the third-person singular present active imperative of *memorāre* ("to remind").  This imperative form, "remember," became a noun in English, signifying something that serves as a reminder.
