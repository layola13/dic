dependable:/dɪˈpɛndəbl/| adj.|可靠的，可依赖的|Reliable; able to be depended on.  Dependable people or things consistently perform well and can be trusted. |例句：He is a dependable employee, always finishing his work on time. 他是一位可靠的员工，总是按时完成工作。|例句：This car is very dependable; it's never let me down. 这辆车非常可靠；它从未让我失望过。|近义词：reliable, trustworthy, dependable, steady |反义词：unreliable, undependable, untrustworthy
