promenading:/ˌprɒməˈneɪdɪŋ/ | v. | 闲逛；散步；蹓跶 | The act of strolling leisurely, especially in a public place for pleasure.  | Example:  They were promenading along the beach, enjoying the sunset. 他们在海滩上散步，享受着日落。

They spent the afternoon promenading through the park.  他们下午在公园里散步。 | 近义词：strolling, strolling, walking, sauntering | 反义词：rushing, hurrying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They promenade along the beach every evening. = 他们每天晚上都在海滩上散步。
We promenade in the park on Sundays. = 我们星期天在公园里散步。


一般过去时:
We promenaded through the bustling market. = 我们漫步穿过熙熙攘攘的市场。
They promenaded hand-in-hand down the street. = 他们手牵着手沿着街道漫步。


现在完成时:
They have promenaded along this path many times. = 他们沿着这条路散步过很多次了。
She has promenaded through several European cities. = 她已经漫步穿过几个欧洲城市了。


过去完成时:
They had promenaded for hours before they finally stopped for a rest. = 他们散步了好几个小时才停下来休息。
We had promenaded through the gardens before the rain started. = 下雨之前，我们在花园里散步。

词源:
From French *promener* (to walk, to lead), from Vulgar Latin *proambulare*, from Latin *pro* (forward) + *ambulare* (to walk).  The word ultimately derives from the idea of walking forward or taking a stroll.
