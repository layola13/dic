carted:/ˈkɑːtɪd/| v. |用车运送；搬运|To transport something in a cart or other vehicle.  The word implies a relatively simple and perhaps less efficient method of transport compared to more modern means.|例句：The farmers carted their hay to the barn. 农民们用车把他们的干草运到谷仓。|例句：We carted all the furniture to the new house. 我们把所有的家具都搬到了新房子。|近义词：hauled, transported, conveyed|反义词：carried (by hand), left


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cart the goods to the warehouse every day. = 他们每天都用车把货物运送到仓库。
We cart our own luggage. = 我们自己搬运行李。


一般过去时:
The workers carted the bricks to the construction site. = 工人们把砖头运到建筑工地。
He carted away all the rubbish. = 他把所有的垃圾都运走了。


现在完成时:
They have carted all the harvest to the market. = 他们已经把所有的收成都运到市场了。
We have carted away the old furniture. = 我们已经把旧家具搬走了。


过去完成时:
They had carted the supplies before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把物资运送过去了。
She had carted everything out before the fire started. = 起火之前，她已经把所有东西都搬出去了。


将来时:
They will cart the stones to the building site tomorrow. = 他们明天将把石头运到建筑工地。
We will cart the furniture ourselves. = 我们将自己搬运家具。


虚拟语气:
If they had carted the goods earlier, they wouldn't have missed the deadline. = 如果他们早点把货物运走，就不会错过截止日期了。
I wish I had carted the heavy boxes yesterday instead of today. = 我真希望我昨天而不是今天搬运那些重箱子。


被动语态:
The hay was carted to the barn by the farmers. = 干草被农民们运到谷仓。
The furniture is being carted to the new house. = 家具正在被搬到新房子。


疑问句:
Were the goods carted to the right address? = 货物运送到正确的地址了吗？
How will the supplies be carted to the remote area? = 物资将如何运送到偏远地区？


否定句:
They didn't cart the heavy boxes themselves. = 他们没有自己搬运那些重箱子。
The farmer hadn't carted the wheat before the rain started. = 下雨之前，农民还没有把小麦运走。


PHRASE->
cart away = 运走
cart off = 把…运走
cart around = 搬运
cart home = 运回家
cart out = 搬出去
