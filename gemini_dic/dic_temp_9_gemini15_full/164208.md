predividing: /ˌpriːdɪˈvaɪdɪŋ/ | v. | 预先划分，预先分配 |  The act of dividing something beforehand. This often refers to dividing something into smaller parts or groups before a main process or event.  | 例句：The researchers predivided the participants into control and experimental groups before commencing the study.  研究人员在开始研究之前将参与者预先分成对照组和实验组。


The teacher predivided the class into smaller teams for the group project. 老师预先将班级分成较小的团队来进行小组项目。


近义词：pre-allocating, pre-portioning, pre-segmenting | 反义词：post-dividing,  redividing

词性：动词 (verb)
