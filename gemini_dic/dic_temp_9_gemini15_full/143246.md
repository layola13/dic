outdelivering: /ˌaʊtˈdɪlɪvərɪŋ/ | v. | 超越；胜过；交付超出预期 |  Exceeding expectations in delivery or performance.  It implies surpassing a benchmark or competitor in terms of quantity, quality, or speed of delivery. |

例句：

The company's new strategy is outdelivering its competitors in terms of market share. = 公司的新战略在市场份额方面超越了竞争对手。

Our team consistently outdelivers on project deadlines and exceeds client expectations. = 我们的团队始终如一地按时完成项目，并超出客户的期望。


近义词：surpass, exceed, outperform, outstrip, excel


反义词：underperform, underdeliver, fail


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new system outdelivers the old one in efficiency. = 新系统在效率上胜过旧系统。
Our company outdelivers its competitors in customer service. = 我们公司在客户服务方面超越了竞争对手。

一般过去时:
Their marketing campaign outdelivered all expectations. = 他们的营销活动超出了所有预期。
Last quarter, our sales team outdelivered their targets by 15%. = 上个季度，我们的销售团队超额完成了15%的目标。

现在完成时:
The project has outdelivered its initial projections. = 该项目已经超出了最初的预期。
The company has consistently outdelivered its promises to investors. = 公司始终如一地兑现了对投资者的承诺。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the team had outdelivered all previous performance records. = 到年底，团队已经超越了以往所有的业绩记录。
They had outdelivered their competitors in innovation before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经在创新方面超越了竞争对手。

将来时:
We believe our new product will outdeliver the competition in terms of features. = 我们相信我们的新产品在功能方面将超越竞争对手。
The analysts predict that the company will outdeliver its earnings forecasts. = 分析师预测该公司将超额完成盈利预测。

虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, we might have outdelivered our rivals. = 如果我们投入更多资金，我们可能会超越竞争对手。
I wish our marketing campaign had outdelivered the competitor’s. = 我希望我们的营销活动能够超越竞争对手的。

被动语态:
The results were outdelivered by the unexpected success of the campaign. = 结果被该活动出乎意料的成功所超越。
Our expectations were consistently outdelivered by the team's performance. = 团队的表现始终超出我们的预期。

疑问句:
Does our product outdeliver the competitor's in terms of price? = 我们的产品在价格方面是否优于竞争对手？
Will the new software outdeliver the current version in usability? = 新软件在可用性方面是否会优于当前版本？

否定句:
The project did not outdeliver its initial budget. = 该项目没有超出其最初的预算。
Unfortunately, our sales figures did not outdeliver those of our main competitor. = 不幸的是，我们的销售额没有超过我们主要竞争对手的销售额。
