polygala:/pɒlɪˈɡeɪlə/| n. |远志属植物|Polygala is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygalaceae, commonly known as milkworts.  They are characterized by their distinctive flowers and often have medicinal properties.  Many species are used in traditional medicine.|例句：Several species of *Polygala* are used in herbal remedies.  几种远志属植物被用于草药疗法。|近义词：milkwort (a common name for many Polygala species)|反义词：无
