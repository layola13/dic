genus:/ˈdʒiːnəs/ | n. |属（生物分类单位）|A genus is a taxonomic rank used in the biological classification of organisms, below family and above species.  It is a group of closely related species sharing a common ancestor.  |Example:  The genus *Panthera* includes lions, tigers, leopards, and jaguars.|近义词：class, kind, type |反义词：species (in the context of hierarchical classification)
