centerfire:/ˈsɛntərˌfaɪər/ | adj. | 中心发火的；中心击发的 | Relating to or denoting a firearm in which the cartridge is ignited by a firing pin that strikes the primer in the center of the cartridge base.  | Example:  Many modern handguns are centerfire. 许多现代手枪都是中心击发的。 |近义词: center-fire (same meaning)| 反义词: rimfire |  词性: adjective

词源:
The word "centerfire" is a compound word. "Center" refers to the central location of the primer in the cartridge base, and "fire" refers to the ignition process.  The term emerged with the development of centerfire cartridges in the mid-19th century.
