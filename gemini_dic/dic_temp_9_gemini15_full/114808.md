mammalia:/məˈmeɪliə/| n. |哺乳动物|Mammals are warm-blooded vertebrates characterized by the presence of mammary glands in females, which produce milk to nourish their young.  They are also typically characterized by hair or fur and three middle ear bones.|例句：Whales and dolphins are marine mammals. 鲸鱼和海豚是海洋哺乳动物。|例句：The classification of mammals is complex and constantly evolving.  哺乳动物的分类复杂且不断发展。|近义词：mammalian (adj.)|反义词：None (it's a broad taxonomic group)|名词
