overarch:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrtʃ/ | vt. | 拱起；覆盖；支配；凌驾于 | To extend over and above; to dominate or surpass.  |例句：The cathedral's magnificent arches overarch the nave.  大教堂宏伟的拱门覆盖着中殿。


The overarching theme of the conference was climate change. 会议的主题是气候变化。 |近义词：cover, dominate, overshadow, encompass |反义词：support, underpin

词源:
The word "overarch" is a combination of "over" (meaning above or across) and "arch" (meaning a curved structure).  It literally means to form an arch over something.  The figurative sense of dominating or surpassing developed from this literal meaning.
