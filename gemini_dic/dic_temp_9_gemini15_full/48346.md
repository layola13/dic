decry:/dɪˈkraɪ/| vt.| 公开谴责；强烈反对 | To express strong disapproval of; condemn.  |例句：Many critics decry the violence in the film. 许多评论家公开谴责电影中的暴力。

例句：The government's new policies were decried by opposition parties. 政府的新政策遭到反对党的强烈谴责。


近义词：condemn, denounce, censure, criticize, reprove

反义词：praise, commend, applaud, endorse


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They decry the unfair treatment of refugees. = 他们谴责对难民的不公平待遇。
She decries the lack of funding for education. = 她谴责教育经费不足。


一般过去时:
The protesters decried the government's actions. = 抗议者谴责政府的行动。
He decried the injustice of the system. = 他谴责制度的不公正。


现在完成时:
Activists have decried the environmental damage for years. = 环保人士多年来一直谴责环境破坏。
She has consistently decried the use of violence. = 她一直强烈反对使用暴力。


过去完成时:
They had decried the proposed legislation before it was even introduced. = 提案提交之前，他们就已强烈反对该法案。
The public had decried the company's unethical practices for months. = 公众已经谴责这家公司的不道德行为数月了。


将来时:
We will decry any attempt to suppress free speech. = 我们将谴责任何压制言论自由的企图。
They will surely decry the decision to close the hospital. = 他们肯定会谴责关闭医院的决定。


虚拟语气:
If I were in their position, I would decry such injustice. = 如果我处在他们的位置，我会谴责这种不公正。
I wish they would decry the corruption in the government. = 我希望他们能谴责政府的腐败。


被动语态:
The new law was decried by many as unjust. = 新法律被许多人谴责为不公正。
His actions were decried by the media. = 他的行为受到媒体的谴责。


疑问句:
Does she decry the actions of the police? = 她是否谴责警方的行为？
Will they decry the proposed changes to the law? = 他们会谴责拟议中的法律变更吗？


否定句:
They did not decry the decision, but they did express some concerns. = 他们没有谴责这个决定，但他们确实表达了一些担忧。
He did not decry the plan outright, but he voiced reservations. = 他没有直接谴责这个计划，但他表达了保留意见。



PHRASE->
decry loudly = 大声谴责
decry vehemently = 猛烈谴责
decry publicly = 公开谴责
decry strongly = 强烈谴责
decry consistently = 一直谴责

词源:
From Old French *decrier, from Latin *de- + *clariare "to cry out," from *clarus "clear."  The sense development is from "to proclaim loudly" to "to denounce."
