pricky:/ˈprɪki/ | adj. | 刺人的；易怒的；令人不舒服的 |  Having or covered with prickles; causing a stinging or tingling sensation; irritable or touchy. | 例句：The rose bush has pricky stems.  这棵玫瑰丛的茎上有刺。

The criticism was pricky and made him defensive.  这批评尖刻，让他采取防御姿态。


近义词：thorny, prickly,irritable, touchy, testy | 反义词：smooth, calm, placid, even-tempered

词源:  From Middle English *prikke, from Old English *pricca ("prickle"), from Proto-Germanic *prikkō ("point, prick").  The meaning "irritable" developed later.
