overpromising:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 过度承诺；许诺过高 |  Making promises that are unlikely or impossible to keep.  This often leads to disappointment and broken trust. | Example: The company was criticized for overpromising and underdelivering on its new product.  这家公司因其新产品过度承诺而交付不足而受到批评。 | 近义词：exaggerating, promising too much, hyping | 反义词：underpromising, being realistic, being cautious | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often overpromises and underdelivers. = 他经常过度承诺而无法兑现。
They overpromise on their marketing materials. = 他们在营销材料中夸大其词。


一般过去时:
She overpromised on the project timeline. = 她对项目时间表承诺过高。
The sales team overpromised to the client. = 销售团队向客户许诺过高。


现在完成时:
He has overpromised to his clients many times. = 他多次向客户许诺过高。
The government has overpromised on its economic policies. = 政府在其经济政策上许诺过高。


过去完成时:
They had overpromised on the budget before realizing the shortfall. = 他们在意识到资金短缺之前就已经对预算许诺过高了。
She had overpromised her abilities in the interview. = 她在面试中夸大了自己的能力。


将来时:
They will likely overpromise again on the next project. = 他们很可能在下一个项目中再次许诺过高。
The company will probably overpromise to attract investors. = 公司可能会夸大其词以吸引投资者。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't overpromised, they wouldn't be in this situation. = 如果他们没有许诺过高，他们就不会陷入这种境地。
I wish I hadn't overpromised on the deadline. = 我真希望我没有对截止日期许诺过高。


被动语态:
The promises were overpromised and ultimately broken. = 这些承诺被夸大其词，最终被打破。
The project was delayed because the deadlines were overpromised. = 项目被推迟，因为截止日期承诺过高。


疑问句:
Did he overpromise on the delivery date? = 他是否对交货日期许诺过高？
Are they overpromising to secure the deal? = 他们是否为了确保交易而许诺过高？


否定句:
They didn't overpromise on the features of the software. = 他们没有夸大软件的功能。
He made sure not to overpromise during the negotiations. = 他确保在谈判中没有许诺过高。
