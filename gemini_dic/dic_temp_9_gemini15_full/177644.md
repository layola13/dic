rebutting: /rɪˈbʌtɪŋ/ | v. | 反驳；驳斥 | To offer arguments or evidence that contradict (a statement, opinion, or accusation).  | 例句：The lawyer spent hours rebutting the prosecution's case. 律师花了数小时反驳检方的论点。

例句：She skillfully rebutted his criticisms. 她巧妙地反驳了他的批评。


近义词：refute, counter, contradict, oppose, challenge | 反义词：confirm, support, endorse, corroborate | 词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rebuts his opponent's arguments with facts and figures. 他用事实和数据反驳对手的论点。
She rebuts any suggestion of wrongdoing. 她驳斥任何关于不当行为的暗示。


一般过去时:
The defendant rebutted the witness's testimony. 被告反驳了证人的证词。
He rebutted the accusations against him. 他驳斥了针对他的指控。


现在完成时:
She has rebutted all the criticisms leveled against her. 她已经驳斥了所有对她提出的批评。
The scientist has repeatedly rebutted claims of fraud. 这名科学家一再驳斥欺诈的指控。


过去完成时:
He had rebutted similar arguments many times before. 他之前已经多次反驳过类似的论点。
The lawyer had carefully rebutted each point made by the plaintiff. 律师已经仔细地反驳了原告提出的每一个论点。


将来时:
He will rebut the accusations in his upcoming speech. 他将在即将发表的演讲中反驳这些指控。
She will forcefully rebut any attempts to undermine her credibility. 她将有力地反驳任何试图破坏她信誉的企图。


虚拟语气:
If he were to rebut the evidence, he would need strong counter-arguments. 如果他要反驳证据，他需要强有力的反驳论点。
If I had the time, I would rebut every single point in his article. 如果我有时间，我会反驳他文章中的每一个论点。


被动语态:
His arguments were easily rebutted by the opposing team. 他的论点很容易被对方团队反驳。
The claims were rebutted by several independent researchers. 这些说法被几位独立研究人员驳斥。


疑问句:
How will you rebut the accusations against you? 你将如何反驳针对你的指控？
Can she effectively rebut the evidence presented? 她能有效地反驳所提供的证据吗？


否定句:
He did not rebut the charges against him. 他没有反驳对他提出的指控。
She failed to rebut the overwhelming evidence. 她未能反驳压倒性的证据。


PHRASE->
rebut the claim = 反驳说法
rebut the evidence = 反驳证据
rebut an argument = 反驳论点
rebut the allegations = 反驳指控
rebut a theory = 反驳理论
