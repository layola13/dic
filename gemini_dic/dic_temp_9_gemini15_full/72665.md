foals:/foʊlz/| n.|小马驹，马驹|A foal is a young horse, typically less than one year old. Foals are known for their playful nature and rapid growth.|例句：The farmer showed us his new foals in the pasture. 农场主向我们展示了他牧场里新出生的小马驹。

The mare carefully nuzzled her newborn foal. 母马小心翼翼地蹭着它刚出生的小马驹。|近义词：colt (male foal), filly (female foal)|反义词：stallion, mare (adult horses)
