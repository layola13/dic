penetrating:/ˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 尖锐的；敏锐的；穿透的；洞察的 |  Having or showing a sharp and insightful understanding.  It can also refer to something that pierces or goes through something else. | 例句：His penetrating gaze seemed to see right through her. 他的目光锐利，仿佛看穿了她。

例句：The penetrating cold wind chilled us to the bone.  刺骨的寒风让我们冻僵了。


近义词：insightful, sharp, keen, piercing, perceptive | 反义词：superficial, shallow, obtuse, dull


词源：From Middle English *penetratinge, from Old French penetrant, present participle of penetrer "to penetrate," from Latin penetrare "to enter, go into, pierce," from penitus "to the bottom, completely."
