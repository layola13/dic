explications:/ˌɛkspləˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 解释；阐明 | Explanations are detailed descriptions or interpretations that clarify something.  They provide understanding of a concept, event, or process. | Example:  The professor's explications of quantum physics were incredibly insightful. 教授对量子物理的解释非常有见地。 |近义词：interpretations, clarifications, elucidations |反义词：obfuscations, concealments |名词
