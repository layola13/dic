laner:/ˈleɪnər/| n. |分路者；（游戏中的）路线玩家；车道使用者|A laner is a player in a team-based video game, typically a MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) or similar game, who primarily stays in one lane to farm and fight.  They focus on controlling a specific area of the map and usually have a defined role within their team's strategy.  The term can also be used more generally to refer to someone who uses or occupies a lane, such as a driver in a lane of traffic.|例句：He's a skilled laner, consistently winning his lane and contributing to team objectives. 他是一位技术娴熟的分路者，始终能够赢得自己的路线并为团队目标做出贡献。|例句：The top laner carried the team to victory with their exceptional performance. 上路玩家凭借其出色的表现带领队伍取得了胜利。|近义词：player, gamer, competitor|反义词：roamer (in gaming context), team fighter (in a contrasting gaming role)
