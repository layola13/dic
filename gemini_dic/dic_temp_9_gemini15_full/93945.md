immunosuppressants:/ɪˌmjuːnoʊsʌˈprɛsntz/ | n. | 免疫抑制剂 |  Substances that suppress or reduce the activity of the immune system.  They are used to prevent the rejection of transplanted organs and to treat autoimmune diseases. | Example:  Immunosuppressants are often prescribed after organ transplantation to prevent rejection. = 移植器官后，通常会开具免疫抑制剂以防止排斥反应。

The patient's body was rejecting the new kidney, so the doctors increased the dosage of immunosuppressants. = 病人的身体正在排斥新的肾脏，所以医生增加了免疫抑制剂的剂量。


近义词:immunosuppressant drugs, immune suppressants | 反义词:immunostimulants | 词性:名词
