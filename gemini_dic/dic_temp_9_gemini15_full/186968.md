rises:/ˈraɪzɪz/ | v. | 升起；上涨；起身；起立 | To move upward; to increase in amount, level, or intensity; to get out of bed; to stand up. |例句：The sun rises in the east. 太阳从东方升起。| The price of oil is rising. 油价正在上涨。|近义词：ascend, climb, elevate, soar |反义词：fall, descend, drop, sink


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun rises in the east every morning. = 太阳每天早上从东方升起。
The water level rises during the rainy season. = 雨季水位上涨。


一般过去时:
The balloon rose quickly into the sky. = 气球迅速升上天空。
She rose from her chair to greet him. = 她从椅子上站起来迎接他。


现在完成时:
The temperature has risen significantly this week. = 本周气温显著上升。
The river has risen to dangerous levels. = 河水水位已上涨到危险水平。


过去完成时:
The sun had already risen when we woke up. = 我们醒来时太阳已经升起了。
The stock market had risen sharply before the crash. = 在股市崩盘之前，股市已经大幅上涨。


将来时:
The rocket will rise into space tomorrow. = 火箭将于明天升空。
The sea level is expected to rise in the coming decades. = 预计未来几十年海平面将上升。


虚拟语气:
If the water rose any higher, the dam would break. = 如果水位再上涨，水坝就会决堤。
I wish the sun would rise earlier in the winter. = 我希望冬天太阳能早点升起。


被动语态:
The flag was risen to the top of the pole. = 旗帜被升到旗杆顶端。(注意：rise本身很少用被动语态)
The issue was raised in the meeting. (raise的被动语态，意思相近) = 这个问题在会议上被提出。


疑问句:
Does the sun rise in the west? = 太阳是从西方升起的吗？
Will the price of gas rise again? = 油价还会上涨吗？


否定句:
The bread didn't rise properly. = 面包没有烤好（没有膨胀）。
The plane didn't rise above the clouds. = 飞机没有飞到云层之上。


PHRASE->
rise and shine = 起床；精神饱满
rise to the occasion = 迎接挑战；不负重望
rise to the bait = 上钩；中计
rise above = 克服；超越
early riser = 早起的人
