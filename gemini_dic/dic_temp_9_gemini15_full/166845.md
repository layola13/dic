pressingness: /ˈprɛsɪŋnəs/ | n. |紧迫性，急迫性 |The pressingness of the situation demanded immediate action.  情况的紧迫性要求立即采取行动。| urgency, importance | triviality, unimportance | 名词


英文解析:  Pressingness refers to the quality or state of being pressing; the urgency or importance of something that requires immediate attention.  It emphasizes the sense of need for swift action or resolution.


例句：

The pressingness of the deadline forced him to work overtime.  截止日期的紧迫性迫使他加班。

The pressingness of the humanitarian crisis led to international aid efforts. 人道主义危机的紧迫性导致了国际援助工作。


近义词：

urgency, importance, immediacy, seriousness, gravity


反义词：

unimportance, triviality, insignificance, irrelevance
