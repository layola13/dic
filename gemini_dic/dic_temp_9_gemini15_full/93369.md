The government's actions are illegitimating the peace process.  = 政府的行动正在使和平进程失去合法性。

His claims were illegitimated by contradictory evidence. =  他的说法因相互矛盾的证据而被推翻。


近义词：invalidating, discrediting, undermining, delegitimizing


反义词：legitimizing, validating, authorizing, confirming


词性：动词
