overintellectualized: /ˌoʊvərɪnˌtɛləktʃuəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度智化的；过度思考的 |  Excessively intellectualized; characterized by an excessive emphasis on intellectual analysis or interpretation, often to the detriment of practical application or emotional understanding.  The argument was overintellectualized, obscuring the simple, underlying issue. | 近义词: overanalyzed, overthought, overly intellectual | 反义词: simplistic, naive, unreflective
