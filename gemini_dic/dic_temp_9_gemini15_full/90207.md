houndfish: /ˈhaʊndfɪʃ/ | n. |犬鱼| A houndfish is a type of fish belonging to the family  Sphyraenidae, characterized by its elongated body, pointed snout, and large, sharp teeth.  They are predatory fish known for their aggressive hunting behavior. |例句：The fisherman caught a large houndfish while fishing near the reef.  渔民在礁石附近钓鱼时钓到了一条大犬鱼。
