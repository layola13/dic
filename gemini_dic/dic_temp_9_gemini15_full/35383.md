co-oped:/ˈkoʊ.ɑːpt/| v. |合作经营；合作；占用；兼并|To work together on a project, often involving a cooperative arrangement.  It can also refer to the act of taking over or appropriating something for one's own use.|例句：The students co-oped with a local business to gain practical experience. 学生们与当地一家企业合作以获得实践经验。

例句：The company co-oped the smaller firm to expand its market share.  这家公司兼并了那家较小的公司以扩大其市场份额。|近义词：collaborate, cooperate, integrate|反义词：compete, oppose
