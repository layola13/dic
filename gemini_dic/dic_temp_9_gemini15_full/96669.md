industriousness:/ˌɪnˈdʌstriəsnəs/| n. |勤奋；勤劳；努力|The quality of being diligent and hardworking; persistent effort to accomplish something.| Her industriousness led to her promotion.  她的勤奋使她得到了晋升。|  His success is a testament to his industriousness. 他的成功证明了他的勤奋。| diligence, perseverance, assiduity | laziness, indolence, sloth | 名词

词源:

industriousness 源自 industrious (勤奋的) + -ness (名词后缀)。industrious  则来自 industry (勤劳；工业) + -ous (形容词后缀)。  Industry 最初源于拉丁语  industria，意思是“勤奋；努力”。
