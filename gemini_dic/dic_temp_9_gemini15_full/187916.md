rotate:/roʊˈteɪt/| /ˈrəʊteɪt/| vt. vi. n.|旋转；轮换；循环|To turn or cause to turn around a central point, axis, or line.  To cause to assume different positions in regular succession.  The act of rotating or the state of being rotated.|例句：The earth rotates on its axis. 地球绕其轴自转。
The crops are rotated annually.  庄稼每年轮作。|近义词：revolve, spin, turn, cycle|反义词：remain stationary, stay put|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The earth rotates on its axis. = 地球绕其轴自转。
He rotates his crops to maintain soil fertility. = 他轮作庄稼以保持土壤肥力。


一般过去时:
The wheel rotated smoothly. = 轮子顺利地旋转。
They rotated the staff assignments. = 他们轮换了员工的岗位安排。


现在完成时:
The fan has rotated continuously for hours. = 风扇已经连续旋转了好几个小时。
The company has rotated its marketing strategies several times this year. = 今年公司已经几次轮换了营销策略。


过去完成时:
The engine had rotated several times before it sputtered and died. = 引擎旋转了几次后才发出噼啪声然后熄火。
They had rotated the tires before the long road trip. = 他们在长途旅行之前已经轮换了轮胎。


将来时:
The astronaut will rotate the satellite's solar panels. = 宇航员将旋转卫星的太阳能电池板。
The company will rotate its management team next year. = 公司明年将轮换其管理团队。


虚拟语气:
If the earth didn't rotate, we would have only one side facing the sun. = 如果地球不旋转，我们将只有一面朝向太阳。
I wish the washing machine would rotate faster. = 我希望洗衣机转得更快些。


被动语态:
The tires are rotated every 5,000 miles. = 轮胎每行驶5000英里就轮换一次。
The crops were rotated to prevent soil depletion. = 轮作庄稼是为了防止土壤贫瘠。


疑问句:
Does the propeller rotate clockwise or counter-clockwise? = 螺旋桨是顺时针旋转还是逆时针旋转？
How often are the employees rotated? = 员工多久轮换一次？


否定句:
The motor doesn't rotate. = 电机不转动。
They didn't rotate the crops this year. = 他们今年没有轮作庄稼。


PHRASE->
rotate crops = 轮作庄稼
rotate tires = 轮换轮胎
rotate staff = 轮换员工
rotate shifts = 轮换班次
360-degree rotation = 360度旋转


词源:
From Middle French *rotater, from Latin rotare "to wheel, turn round," from rota "wheel."  The meaning "to cause to alternate in regular succession" developed in the 19th century.
