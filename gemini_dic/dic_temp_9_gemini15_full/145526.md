overcomable: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌməbəl/ | adj. | 可克服的，可以战胜的 |  capable of being overcome; surmountable.  This word emphasizes the possibility of success in overcoming a challenge or difficulty. | Example: The challenges are great, but they are overcomable with enough effort and determination.  这些挑战很大，但只要付出足够的努力和决心，就能克服。 |近义词: surmountable, conquerable, vincible | 反义词: insurmountable, unconquerable, invincible
