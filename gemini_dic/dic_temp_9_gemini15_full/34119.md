clacking:/ˈklækɪŋ/| v. |卡嗒卡嗒地响，发出咔哒声 |The sound of something repeatedly hitting something else, making a sharp, clicking sound.  |例句：The old typewriter was clacking away furiously.  老式打字机咔嗒咔嗒地快速敲击着。

例句： I could hear the clacking of the train wheels on the tracks. 我能听到火车轮子在轨道上卡嗒卡嗒的声音。


近义词：clicking, tapping, rattling, clattering

反义词：None (lack of sound would be the opposite concept)

词性：动词
