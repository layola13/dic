furuncular:/fjʊˈrʌŋkjʊlər/| adj.| 痈的；与痈有关的 | Relating to or characteristic of a furuncle (boil).  A furuncle is a painful, pus-filled skin infection caused by bacteria, usually Staphylococcus aureus.  The term often describes the presence or characteristics of boils. | The patient presented with a furuncular lesion on their arm.  这位病人手臂上出现了一个痈性病变。

The doctor diagnosed a furuncular infection requiring antibiotic treatment.医生诊断为痈性感染，需要抗生素治疗。|近义词：boilious, suppurative |反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider terms implying healthy skin.
