plasterly: /ˈplæstərli/ | adj. |  像灰泥的；涂抹灰泥的；笨拙的 |

英文解析:  Resembling plaster;  having the qualities or appearance of plaster; clumsy or awkward in movement.  The word implies a lack of grace or smoothness, similar to the texture of unrefined plaster.


例句:

He moved with a plasterly gait, his limbs stiff and ungainly. = 他步履笨拙，四肢僵硬而笨拙。

The sculpture had a rather plasterly texture, lacking the fine detail of a bronze. = 这座雕塑的纹理相当粗糙，缺乏青铜的精细细节。


近义词:  clumsy, awkward, stiff, ungainly, unrefined


反义词: graceful, elegant, refined, smooth, dexterous


词性: adjective
