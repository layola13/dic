neckcloth:/ˈnekˌklɒθ/ | n. |围巾；领巾 | A neckcloth is a piece of cloth worn around the neck, often for warmth or decoration.  It can be a scarf, cravat, or other similar garment. | Example: He wore a silk neckcloth with his tuxedo. 他穿着丝绸领巾搭配他的燕尾服。 |近义词：scarf, cravat, kerchief |反义词：无
