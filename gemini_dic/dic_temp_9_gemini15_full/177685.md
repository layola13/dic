recaller:/rɪˈkɔːlər/ | n. |回忆者，想起的人| A person who recalls something; one who remembers or brings something back to mind.  |例句：The eyewitness was a key recallar in the case. 目击证人是此案的关键回忆者。|近义词：rememberer, reminiscer |反义词：forgetter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recalls the details of the accident vividly. 他生动地回忆起事故的细节。
She recalls her childhood with fondness. 她怀着喜爱之情回忆她的童年。


一般过去时:
The old man recalled his youth. 老人回忆起他的青年时代。
The witness recalled seeing the suspect flee the scene. 证人回忆起看到嫌疑人逃离现场。


现在完成时:
I have recalled several interesting events from my trip. 我已经回忆起旅行中的一些有趣事件。
She has recalled the name of the book, but not the author. 她想起了书名，但想不起作者。


过去完成时:
He had recalled the meeting before he started his presentation. 他在开始演讲之前已经回忆起了会议内容。
By the time she finished speaking, she had recalled everything she wanted to say.  当她说完话时，她已经回忆起所有想说的话了。


将来时:
I will recall this day for the rest of my life. 我将永远记住这一天。
She will recall the information when she needs it. 她需要的时候会想起这些信息。


虚拟语气:
If I could recall the password, I would access my account. 如果我能想起密码，我就能访问我的帐户。
I wish I could recall what he said. 我真希望我能想起他说的话。


被动语态:
The details of the incident were recalled by the survivors.  幸存者回忆起了事件的细节。
The meeting was recalled due to unforeseen circumstances. 会议由于不可预见的情况而被取消。


疑问句:
Can you recall the name of the restaurant? 你能想起餐馆的名字吗？
Do you recall what happened that night? 你还记得那天晚上发生的事情吗？


否定句:
I cannot recall her name. 我记不起她的名字。
He did not recall seeing the man before. 他不记得以前见过这个人。


PHRASE->
recall to mind  想起
recall something vividly 生动地回忆起某事
recall a memory 回忆起一段记忆
summon up a recall  努力回忆
flashback recall  闪回记忆
