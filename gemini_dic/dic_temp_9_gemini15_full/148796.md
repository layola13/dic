overvaluations:/ˌoʊvərˌvæljuˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 高估；估价过高 | The act of placing a value on something that is higher than its actual worth.  It often refers to assets, stocks, or currencies being priced above their intrinsic value. |  The market's overvaluations led to a significant correction. 市场的高估导致了大幅度回调。 The real estate market is suffering from overvaluations in certain areas.  某些地区的房地产市场正在遭受高估的困扰。 | undervaluations | fair valuations | n.
