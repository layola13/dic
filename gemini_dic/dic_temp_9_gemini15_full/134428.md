英文解析:The word "nonirrational" is a somewhat uncommon word formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "irrational."  It signifies the opposite of irrational, indicating something that is rational, logical, and sensible.  While grammatically correct, it's often preferred to use "rational" directly for clarity and conciseness.
