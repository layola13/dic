USAGE->

(Gucci as a brand name does not have verb conjugations.  The following applies to verbs used in sentences *about* Gucci.)

一般现在时:
Gucci sells luxury goods. = 古驰销售奢侈品。
They buy Gucci products regularly. = 他们定期购买古驰产品。

一般过去时:
Gucci launched a new collection last year. = 古驰去年推出了一个新的系列。

现在完成时:
Gucci has expanded its market share significantly. = 古驰已大幅扩展了其市场份额。
