landfall:/ˈlændˌfɔːl/| n.|陆地；登陆；首次看见陆地|The act of sighting land after a sea voyage; the first land seen after a sea voyage; the land sighted.  It can also refer to the act of making landfall or the place where landfall is made.|例句：The ship made landfall at dawn.  这艘船在黎明时分登陆。

例句：Their landfall was a small, uncharted island. 他们登陆的地方是一个小的、未绘制地图的岛屿。


近义词：arrival, landing, sighting


反义词：departure, setting sail


词性：名词
