impropriatrix: /ˌɪmˈprɒprɪeɪtrɪks/ | n. | 不正当的女主人；不合适的管理者 | A woman who improperly manages or controls something, especially property or resources;  a female who acts in an improper or unseemly manner as a manager or owner.  It implies a lack of proper authority or a misuse of power. | The impropriatrix of the estate squandered the family fortune.  这家庄园的不当女主人挥霍了家族的财产。|  mismanager, maladministrator | proper manager, rightful owner | 名词
