shady:/ˈʃeɪdi/| adj.|阴凉的；可疑的；不正当的|

英文解析:
1. Providing shade or shelter from the sun.  (阴凉的)
2.  Suspicious or dishonest; not above reproach. (可疑的；不正当的)


例句:
We found a shady spot under the tree to have our picnic. = 我们在树下找到一个阴凉的地方野餐。

The shady dealings of the company led to its downfall. = 该公司的可疑交易导致了它的垮台。


近义词:
(阴凉的)：shadowy, sheltered
(可疑的): suspicious, questionable, dubious, suspect, disreputable

反义词:
(阴凉的)：sunny, bright, exposed
(可疑的): aboveboard, honest, reputable, trustworthy
