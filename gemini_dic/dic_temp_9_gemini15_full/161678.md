pours:/pɔːrz/| v.|倾泻，大量涌出|to flow rapidly in a steady stream|The rain poured down all day.  雨下了一整天。|The river poured into the sea. 河水奔流入海。|近义词：streams, flows, gushes|反义词：drizzles, trickles


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain pours down heavily. 雨下得很大。
He pours milk into his coffee. 他把牛奶倒进咖啡里。


一般过去时:
She poured a glass of wine. 她倒了一杯酒。
The volcano poured lava down the mountainside. 火山喷出岩浆流下山坡。


现在完成时:
It has poured all afternoon. 下午一直在下大雨。
She has poured all her energy into this project. 她已将所有精力投入到这个项目中。


过去完成时:
The rain had poured for hours before it finally stopped. 雨下了几个小时才停。
They had poured all the cement before the inspector arrived. 检查员到达之前，他们已经倒好了所有的水泥。


将来时:
The rain will pour tomorrow. 明天会下大雨。
He will pour the tea into the cups. 他会把茶倒进杯子里。


虚拟语气:
If it poured tomorrow, we would stay inside. 如果明天下雨，我们就待在家里。
I wish it poured so that the garden would get watered. 我希望下雨，这样花园就能浇水了。


被动语态:
The concrete was poured into the mold. 混凝土被倒入模具中。
The tea is being poured into the cups. 茶正在被倒入杯子里。


疑问句:
Is the rain pouring outside? 外面在下雨吗？
Will you pour me a glass of water? 你能给我倒一杯水吗？


否定句:
The rain isn't pouring right now.  现在不下雨。
He didn't pour the milk into the cereal. 他没有把牛奶倒进麦片里。
