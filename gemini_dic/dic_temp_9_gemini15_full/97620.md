inharmoniousness: /ˌɪnhɑːrˈmoʊniəsnəs/ | n. | 不和谐，不协调 | The lack of harmony; discordance; incongruity.  It refers to a state or quality of being lacking in harmony, agreement, or compatibility.  This can apply to various aspects, including sounds, colors, ideas, or relationships. |

例句：

The inharmoniousness of the colors in the painting was jarring. = 画中颜色的不和谐令人刺耳。

The inharmoniousness between the two factions prevented any progress. = 两派之间的不和谐阻碍了任何进展。


近义词：discord, dissonance, incongruity, incompatibility, conflict


反义词：harmony, concord, agreement, compatibility


词性：名词
