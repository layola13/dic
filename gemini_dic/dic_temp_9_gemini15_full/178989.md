redefeats:/ˌriːdɪˈfiːts/| v.|再次击败，再次战胜 |To defeat again or repeatedly.  It implies a previous defeat and a subsequent one. |例句：The champion redefeated his rival in the rematch.  冠军在复赛中再次击败了他的对手。|例句：The army redefeated the rebels after a brief respite.  军队在短暂休整后再次击败了叛军。|近义词：defeat again, overcome again, vanquish again |反义词：be defeated by, lose to, succumb to
