cosmonautically:/ˌkɒzməˈnɔːtɪkli/| adv.|宇航员地，航天员地 |In a manner relating to cosmonauts or space travel.|例句：The mission was conducted cosmonautically, with precision and skill.  此次任务以精确和熟练的宇航员方式进行。


The equipment was tested cosmonautically before launch. 设备在发射前经过了宇航测试。


近义词：astronomically (in a way relating to astronomy, but less directly related to human spaceflight)


反义词：terrestrially (on Earth)


词性: 副词
