pull-a-rabbit-out-the-hat: /pʊl ə ˈræbɪt aʊt əv ðə hæt/ | phrasal verb | 变戏法；突然拿出意想不到的东西 | To unexpectedly produce something surprising or impressive, often as if by magic.  It implies a clever trick or unexpected solution to a problem. | 例句：The magician pulled a rabbit out of the hat, surprising the audience. 魔术师从帽子中变出一只兔子，令观众大吃一惊。

The CEO pulled a rabbit out of the hat by securing a last-minute deal with a competitor.  首席执行官通过与竞争对手达成最后一刻的协议而化险为夷。


近义词：produce unexpectedly, conjure up, surprise, amaze, astound | 反义词：expect, predict, anticipate
