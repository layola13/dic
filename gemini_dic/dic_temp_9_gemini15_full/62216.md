increase:/ɪnˈkriːs/| v. n. |增加；增长；提高|To become or make greater in size, amount, number, or degree.|The company's profits increased by 10% last year.  去年公司利润增长了10%。|The population of the city has increased dramatically. 这个城市的人口急剧增加。|decrease,augment, rise|decrease, decline, fall|动词,名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price of gas increases every year. = 天然气价格每年都在上涨。
She increases her exercise routine every month. = 她每个月都会增加锻炼计划。


一般过去时:
The temperature increased sharply yesterday. = 昨天的气温急剧上升。
He increased his efforts to finish the project. = 他加大了努力以完成项目。


现在完成时:
The company has increased its market share significantly. = 公司已经显著提高了市场份额。
Sales have increased by 20% this quarter. = 本季度销售额增长了20%。


过去完成时:
They had increased production before the demand rose. = 他们在需求增加之前就增加了产量。
The number of students had increased significantly before the new school year began. = 在新学年开始之前，学生人数已经显著增加。


将来时:
The government will increase taxes next year. = 政府明年将提高税收。
They will increase the production to meet the demands. = 他们将增加产量以满足需求。


虚拟语气:
If the company increased its investment, it would be more profitable. = 如果公司增加投资，它将更有利可图。
I wish my salary increased faster. = 我希望我的工资涨得更快。


被动语态:
The volume was increased to a comfortable level. = 音量被调高到舒适的水平。
Production will be increased by 15% next quarter. = 下个季度产量将增加15%。


疑问句:
Did the number of participants increase this year? = 今年参与人数增加了么？
Will the company increase its investment in research? = 公司会增加对研究的投资吗？


否定句:
The company did not increase its prices last year. = 公司去年没有涨价。
The population has not increased significantly in recent years. = 近年来人口没有显著增长。


PHRASE->
increase in =  增加
increase by = 增加（具体数值）
a sharp increase = 急剧增加
a gradual increase = 逐渐增加
significant increase = 重大增加
