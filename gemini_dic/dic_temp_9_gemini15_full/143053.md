outbluster:/ˌaʊtˈblʌstər/ | vt. | 大声斥责；咆哮着压制；以大声喧哗压倒 | To overcome or overwhelm with loud and aggressive speech or behavior.  |例句：He tried to outbluster his opponent into submission. 他试图以大声喧哗压制对手使其屈服。

例句：The bully tried to outbluster the smaller children into giving him their lunch money.  那个恶霸试图以大声吓唬那些较小的孩子，让他们交出午餐钱。


近义词：bully, browbeat, overpower, intimidate | 反义词：subdue, appease, pacify | 词性:动词

词源:
The word "outbluster" is a combination of the verb "bluster" (to speak or act in a loud, boastful, or threatening way) and the prefix "out-", which indicates exceeding or surpassing.  Therefore, "outbluster" means to surpass or overwhelm someone by blustering more loudly or aggressively than them.
