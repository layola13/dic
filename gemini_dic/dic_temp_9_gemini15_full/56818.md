dorky:/ˈdɔːrki/| adj.|傻气的，笨拙的，古怪的|Describes someone or something that is awkward, clumsy, or socially inept; often used in a playful or affectionate way.  It suggests a lack of social grace or sophistication, but not necessarily malice or incompetence. |例句：He wore a dorky sweater with mismatched patterns. 他穿着一件图案不搭调的傻气毛衣。

例句：She made a dorky joke that nobody understood. 她讲了一个没人懂的傻气笑话。


近义词：nerdy, geeky, goofy, awkward, clumsy, uncool|反义词：cool, sophisticated, stylish, suave
