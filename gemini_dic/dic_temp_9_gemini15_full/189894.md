samosas:/səˈmoʊsəz/| n. |萨摩萨饼|Fried pastries filled with spiced potatoes and peas, or other savory fillings.  Samosas are a popular snack or appetizer in many parts of the world, particularly in South Asia and the Middle East.|例句：I ordered some samosas and a chai tea for a light lunch. 我点了一些萨摩萨饼和一杯奶茶作为午餐。

例句：The street vendor sold delicious, crispy samosas filled with spiced potatoes. 街头小贩卖的香料土豆馅萨摩萨饼又脆又好吃。


近义词：pastry, appetizer, snack|反义词：无
