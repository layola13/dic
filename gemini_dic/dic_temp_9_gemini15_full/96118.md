indelicacy: /ɪnˈdɛləkəsi/ | n. | 不雅；粗俗；不妥当 |  Lack of delicacy or refinement; something that is indelicate.  It refers to behavior, language, or subject matter that is considered improper, offensive, or tactless in a particular social context. |

例句：

Her jokes, though intended to be funny, bordered on indelicacy.  她的笑话，虽然是想让人发笑，却有点粗俗。

The indelicacy of his remarks embarrassed everyone present. 他话语中的不妥当让在场的所有人都感到尴尬。


近义词： impropriety, vulgarity, crudeness, gaucherie, tactlessness

反义词： delicacy, refinement, politeness, tact, propriety


词性：名词
