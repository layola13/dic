self-experience:/ˌsɛlfɪkˈspɪəriəns/| n. |自身经历，亲身经历 |Personal experience gained through direct participation or involvement.  It emphasizes the individual's direct involvement and the resulting understanding.|例句：His self-experience in the Amazon rainforest profoundly shaped his views on conservation.  他的亚马逊雨林亲身经历深刻地影响了他对保护的看法。

例句：Through self-experience, she learned the importance of perseverance. 通过亲身经历，她明白了坚持的重要性。


近义词：personal experience, firsthand experience, lived experience |反义词：hearsay, secondhand account |词性：名词
