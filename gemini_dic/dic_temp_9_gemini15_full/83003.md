gunpoints:/ˈɡʌnˌpɔɪnts/| n.|枪口；枪口下；在枪口逼迫下|The noun "gunpoints" refers to the muzzles of guns, or more broadly, to a situation where someone is threatened with a gun or guns.  It often implies coercion or duress.|例句：He was forced to sign the confession at gunpoint. 他在枪口下被迫签署了供词。

例句：The robbers held up the bank at gunpoints.  抢劫犯在枪口下抢劫了银行。

近义词：threat of violence, coercion, duress, intimidation

反义词：safety, security, freedom from threat


词性:名词
