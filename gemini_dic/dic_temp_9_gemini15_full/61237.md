emailing: /iˈmeɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 电子邮件发送；用电子邮件发送 | The act of sending messages electronically using email.  | Example: I'm emailing the report to you now. 我现在正在把报告发邮件给你。  | 近义词: emailing, sending an email, dispatching an email | 反义词: receiving an email


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I email my clients every week. = 我每周都给我的客户发邮件。
She emails her boss daily. = 她每天都给老板发邮件。

一般过去时:
I emailed him yesterday. = 我昨天给他发了邮件。
They emailed the proposal last night. = 他们昨晚发了提案的邮件。

现在完成时:
I have emailed the document already. = 我已经发了邮件。
She has emailed him three times this morning. = 今天早上她给他发了三次邮件。

过去完成时:
I had emailed him before the meeting. = 我在会议之前给他发了邮件。
They had emailed the contract before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前发了合同的邮件。

将来时:
I will email you the details tomorrow. = 我明天会把细节发邮件给你。
She will email the invitation later today. = 她今天晚些时候会发出邀请邮件。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would email him immediately. = 如果我有时间，我会立刻给他发邮件。
I wish I had emailed her sooner. = 我真希望我早点给她发邮件。

被动语态:
The report was emailed to the committee. = 报告已经发邮件给委员会了。
The information is being emailed to all staff. = 信息正在发邮件给所有员工。

疑问句:
Have you emailed the client yet? = 你给客户发邮件了吗？
Did she email you back? = 她回复你的邮件了吗？

否定句:
I didn't email him because I forgot. = 我没有给他发邮件，因为我忘了。
She hasn't emailed me yet. = 她还没给我发邮件。

emailing list: 邮件列表
