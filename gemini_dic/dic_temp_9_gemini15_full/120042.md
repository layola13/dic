millisecond's:/ˈmɪlɪˌsɛkəndz/| n.|毫秒的；千分之一秒的|A millisecond is one thousandth of a second.  It's a unit of time.  The possessive 's indicates belonging or possession.|例句：The processor completed the task in a few milliseconds. 处理器在几毫秒内完成了任务。
