pneumatothorax:/ˌnjuːmətoʊˈθɔːræks/ | n. | 气胸 | A collapsed lung resulting from air leaking into the space between the lung and the chest wall.  This can be caused by injury, disease, or a ruptured air blister in the lung. | Example: The patient presented with a sudden onset of chest pain and shortness of breath, suggestive of a pneumothorax.  患者出现突发胸痛和呼吸急促，提示可能为气胸。|近义词：collapsed lung |反义词：inflated lung
