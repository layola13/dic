farandole:/ˌfærənˈdɔːl/| n. |法朗多尔舞（一种法国南部传统的链舞）|A farandole is a lively, traditional Provençal dance, typically performed in a long line or circle, with dancers holding hands or linking arms.  It often involves intricate steps and movements, and is frequently accompanied by music.  The dance is associated with celebrations and festivities. |The villagers celebrated the harvest with a joyous farandole. 村民们载歌载舞地庆祝丰收。
The children danced a farandole around the maypole. 孩子们在五月柱周围跳起了法朗多尔舞。| 近义词：promenade, procession | 反义词：  None directly applicable.
