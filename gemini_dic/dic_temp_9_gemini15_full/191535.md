schnook:/ʃnuːk/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋，易受骗的人|A schnook is a gullible or foolish person, easily tricked or taken advantage of.  They are often portrayed as naive and somewhat clumsy. |He's such a schnook; he fell for that obvious scam. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然上当了那个明显的骗局。|近义词：fool, dupe, sucker, sap, patsy |反义词：sharpie, shrewd person
