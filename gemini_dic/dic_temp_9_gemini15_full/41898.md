cosiness:/ˈkoʊzinəs/| n. |舒适， уют，温馨|A feeling or state of being warm, comfortable, and relaxed.  It often implies a feeling of security and contentment.|例句：The fire created a lovely cosiness in the room.  壁炉营造了房间里舒适温馨的氛围。|例句：She enjoyed the cosiness of her armchair by the fire. 她享受着坐在壁炉旁扶手椅上的舒适惬意。|近义词：comfort, warmth, snugness, coziness|反义词：discomfort, coldness, bleakness
