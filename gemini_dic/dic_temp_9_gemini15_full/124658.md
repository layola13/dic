mov:/mʊv/ | vt.  |移动；搬动；使感动；感动；拍摄；(电影)镜头切换| to cause (something) to move from one place to another; to affect someone emotionally;  to film or record (something)  |例句：He moved the table to the corner. 他把桌子移到了角落。|近义词：shift, transfer, relocate, touch, affect |反义词：stay, remain, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moves the furniture every year. = 他每年都搬动家具。
The story moves me to tears. = 这个故事让我感动得流泪。


一般过去时:
She moved to a new apartment last month. = 她上个月搬到了新公寓。
The movie moved the audience deeply. = 这部电影深深地打动了观众。


现在完成时:
They have moved three times in the past five years. = 过去五年里他们搬了三次家。
The news has moved many people to donate. = 这个消息感动了许多人捐款。


过去完成时:
He had moved out before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他已经搬走了。
The speech had moved the crowd to cheers. = 演讲已经把人群感动得欢呼起来。


将来时:
We will move to the countryside next year. = 我们明年将搬到乡下。
I believe this film will move you. = 我相信这部电影会感动你。


虚拟语气:
If I moved to Paris, I would learn French. = 如果我搬到巴黎，我会学习法语。
I wish the director had moved the camera earlier. = 我希望导演早点移动摄像机。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
The car wouldn't move. = 车子动不了。
The story didn't move me at all. = 这个故事一点也没打动我。
