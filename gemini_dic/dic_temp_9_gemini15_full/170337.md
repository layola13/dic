providing:/prəˈvaɪdɪŋ/ | v. | 提供，供应 | Providing assistance to those in need is important.  提供帮助给需要的人很重要。 | giving, supplying, furnishing | refusing, withholding | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is providing support to his family. = 他正在为他的家人提供支持。
The company provides excellent customer service. = 这家公司提供优质的客户服务。

一般过去时:
She provided all the necessary information. = 她提供了所有必要的信息。
They provided food and shelter to the homeless. = 他们为无家可归者提供食物和住所。

现在完成时:
We have provided funding for the project. = 我们已经为该项目提供了资金。
The government has provided various social programs. = 政府已经提供了各种社会福利项目。

过去完成时:
They had provided a detailed report before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经提供了一份详细的报告。
She had provided evidence to support her claim. = 她已经提供了证据来支持她的说法。

将来时:
We will provide more details later. = 我们稍后会提供更多细节。
The company will be providing training to its employees. = 公司将为其员工提供培训。

虚拟语气:
If they provided more funding, we could finish the project sooner. = 如果他们提供更多资金，我们可以更快地完成项目。
I wish they had provided clearer instructions. = 我希望他们提供了更清晰的指示。

被动语态:
The information was provided by the police. = 信息是由警方提供的。
Financial aid is being provided to the affected areas. = 受灾地区正在获得财政援助。

疑问句:
What services are you providing? = 你提供什么服务？
Is the company providing enough support? = 公司提供足够的支援吗？

否定句:
They did not provide any explanation. = 他们没有提供任何解释。
The school didn't provide enough resources. = 学校没有提供足够的资源。
