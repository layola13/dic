couchettes:/kuʃˈɛts/| n.|卧铺（火车或船上的）|A couchette is a type of sleeping accommodation found on trains or ships, typically consisting of narrow bunks arranged in tiers.  It is a more basic and less expensive option than a cabin or sleeper compartment.|Example: The overnight train to Paris offered couchettes for budget-conscious travelers.  巴黎的夜班火车为精打细算的旅客提供卧铺。|近义词：berth, bunk|反义词：cabin (first-class sleeping accommodation)
