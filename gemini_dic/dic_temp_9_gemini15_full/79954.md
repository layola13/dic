go against:/ɡoʊ əˈɡɛnst/ | v. |违反；反对；不利于 |To oppose or be contrary to something.  It can also mean to act in opposition to someone or something, or to be detrimental to someone or something. |例句：His actions go against the rules.  他的行为违反了规定。
例句：The wind is going against us. 风对我们不利。|近义词：oppose, contradict, defy, counter, clash with |反义词：conform to, comply with, support, agree with |词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The current goes against the boat. = 水流阻碍了船只前进。
His beliefs go against everything he's taught. = 他的信仰违背了他所接受的一切教诲。

一般过去时:
The evidence went against the defendant. = 证据对被告不利。
They went against the doctor's orders. = 他们违背了医生的嘱咐。

现在完成时:
Everything has gone against them lately. = 最近一切对他们都不利。
His actions have gone against the company's policy. = 他的行为违反了公司的政策。

过去完成时:
The tide had gone against the ship before they could reach the shore. = 在他们到达岸边之前，潮水已经开始转向对船不利。
She had gone against her family's wishes to pursue her dream. = 她为了追求梦想而违背了家人的意愿。

将来时:
The wind will go against our journey. = 风会阻碍我们的旅程。
I fear that the decision will go against us. = 我担心这个决定对我们不利。

虚拟语气:
If I went against my principles, I would feel terrible. = 如果我违背我的原则，我会感觉很糟糕。
I wish I hadn't gone against my intuition. = 我真希望我没有违背我的直觉。

被动语态:
The proposal was voted down; it went against the majority. = 这项提案被否决了；它违背了大多数人的意愿。
His arguments were easily refuted; they went against common sense. = 他的论点很容易被驳斥；它们违背了常识。

疑问句:
Does this decision go against company policy? = 这个决定违反公司政策吗？
Does the evidence go against the accused? = 证据对被告不利吗？

否定句:
This plan doesn't go against the law. = 这个计划没有违反法律。
Their actions didn't go against our interests. = 他们的行为没有损害我们的利益。


PHRASE->
go against the grain: 违反习惯；格格不入
go against the tide: 逆流而上；与潮流对抗
go against one's better judgment: 违背自己的最佳判断
go against the odds: 克服困难；战胜逆境
go against someone's wishes: 违背某人的意愿
