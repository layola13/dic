/ˈkreɪdl/| n. |摇篮；发源地；支架 |A cradle is a bed for a baby, typically one that rocks.  It can also refer to the origin or birthplace of something.  As a noun, it can also describe a support or framework. |例句：She gently rocked the baby in its cradle. 她轻轻地摇着婴儿床里的婴儿。 The cradle of civilization is often considered to be Mesopotamia. 文明的发源地通常被认为是美索不达米亚。|近义词： crib, bassinet, birthplace, origin |反义词：grave, end, demise |名词
