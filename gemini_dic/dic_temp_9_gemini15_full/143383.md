outfielder:/ˈaʊtˌfiːldər/ | n. |外野手 | A player in baseball or softball who plays in the outfield.  Outfielders are responsible for catching fly balls hit by the opposing team. | Example: The outfielder made a spectacular catch to end the inning. 外野手做了一个精彩的接球结束了这一局。 |近义词:outfield player |反义词: infielder
