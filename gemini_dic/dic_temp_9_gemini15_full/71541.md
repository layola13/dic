flared:/fleərd/| adj., v.|张开的；突然加剧的；（裤腿等）喇叭形的|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "flared" describes something that spreads outwards, typically in a wide, bell-like shape.  It can refer to clothing (flared trousers, flared skirt), or other things that widen as they extend from a central point.  As a verb, "flared" means to suddenly increase in intensity or become wider.  It can describe emotions (anger flared), events (violence flared), or even a physical object (the nostrils flared).

例句:
Her flared jeans were a fashion statement. = 她的喇叭裤是一种时尚宣言。

The fire flared up suddenly, threatening the nearby houses. = 火突然窜了起来，威胁着附近的房屋。


近义词:
(adj.) widened, spread, bell-shaped, flared up
(v.) intensified, increased, erupted, blazed


反义词:
(adj.) narrow, straight, tapered
(v.) diminished, decreased, subsided


词性:
Adjective, Verb
