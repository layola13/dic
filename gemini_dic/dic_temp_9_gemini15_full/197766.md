shimabara:/ʃɪmɑːˈbɑːrɑː/| n. |岛原|Shimabara refers to a city and peninsula located in Nagasaki Prefecture, Kyushu, Japan.  It is known for its history, particularly the Shimabara Rebellion of 1637-38, a major Christian uprising. The area is also known for its beautiful scenery, including hot springs and Mount Unzen. |例句：We visited Shimabara Castle during our trip to Nagasaki. 我们在去长崎的旅行期间参观了岛原城。|近义词：Nagasaki (prefecture)|反义词：N/A|名词
