possessive: /pəˈzɛsɪv/ | adj. | 所有格的；占有的；独占欲强的 | Relating to, characterized by, or showing possession or a possessive nature.  It can describe grammatical structures indicating possession, or a person's behavior showing excessive possessiveness. |例句：He has a possessive attitude towards his girlfriend. 他对女朋友占有欲很强。  The possessive pronoun "mine" indicates ownership.  所有格代词“我的”表示所有权。|近义词：possessive, proprietary, jealous |反义词：unselfish, generous, detached
