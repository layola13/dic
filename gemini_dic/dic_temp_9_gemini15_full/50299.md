deplaning:/diːˈpleɪnɪŋ/| n. |下飞机，离机|The act of passengers leaving an aircraft.  It refers to the process of disembarking from a plane.|Example: Deplaning was delayed due to a mechanical issue.  下飞机因为机械故障而延误了。  The deplaning process was smooth and efficient.  下飞机的过程顺利而高效。|近义词：disembarking, getting off|反义词：boarding, embarking|名词

Deplaning was difficult because of the crowded terminal. = 因为航站楼拥挤，下飞机很困难。
