by-lined:/ˈbaɪlaɪnd/| adj.|署名的；加旁注的|Having a byline; attributed to a specific writer or author.  The article was by-lined by a renowned journalist.|近义词：attributed, credited|反义词：anonymous, unattributed

This newspaper always by-lines its articles. = 这家报纸总是为文章署名。
