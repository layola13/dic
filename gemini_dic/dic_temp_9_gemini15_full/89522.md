honky-tonked:/ˈhɑːŋkiˌtɑŋkt/| adj.|喧闹的，低俗的，乡村音乐风格的|Characterized by honky-tonk music or atmosphere; loud, raucous, and often suggestive of low morals or a rough-and-tumble lifestyle.  The term often refers to a type of bar or club associated with this music and atmosphere.|例句：The honky-tonked bar was filled with boisterous laughter and the twang of country music. 这家喧闹的酒吧里充满了喧嚣的笑声和乡村音乐的旋律。

The honky-tonked atmosphere of the festival was infectious. 节日的喧闹氛围极具感染力。|近义词：raucous, boisterous, rowdy, bawdy|反义词：quiet, refined, sophisticated, classy
