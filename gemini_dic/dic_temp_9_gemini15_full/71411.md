flailed:/fleɪld/| v.|挥舞；挣扎；失败；|To move wildly; to struggle violently; to fail utterly.|The drowning man flailed his arms in desperation.  溺水的人绝望地挥舞着手臂。

He flailed around in the dark, trying to find the light switch. 他在黑暗中挥舞着胳膊，试图找到电灯开关。


近义词：struggled, thrashed, floundered, failed|反义词：succeeded, triumphed

词源:
The word "flail" comes from the Old English word *flægel*, meaning "a flail," a farming tool used for threshing grain.  The verb sense developed from the image of the flail's wild, swinging motion.
