pseudolegendary: /ˌsuːdoʊˈlɛdʒəndɛri/ | adj. |似传奇的，半传奇的 |  Appearing to be legendary or mythical, but not fully or officially recognized as such; possessing qualities or characteristics reminiscent of a legend, but lacking definitive historical or factual evidence.  | 例句：The story of the lost city is pseudolegendary, based on fragmented accounts and local folklore.  这个失落之城的传说似是而非，基于支离破碎的记载和当地民间传说。


The tale of the sword in the stone is pseudolegendary; while it holds elements of a legend, its historical veracity is debated.  关于石中剑的故事是似传奇的；虽然它包含传奇的元素，但其历史真实性却存在争议。| 近义词：quasi-legendary, semi-mythical, mythical-sounding | 反义词：historical, factual, documented


词源:

* **pseudo-**:  From Greek *pseudo-* meaning "false," "sham," or "pretended."
* **legendary**: From "legend," ultimately from Latin *legenda* meaning "things to be read," referring to stories passed down through generations.
