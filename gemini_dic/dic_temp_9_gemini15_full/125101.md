mullions:/ˈmʌljənz/ | n. |窗棂，窗格 | Vertical bars dividing a window into panes.  Mullions are used in architecture to create decorative and structural support for windows. They can be made from various materials, such as wood, stone, or metal.  |The cathedral's stained-glass windows were divided by intricate stone mullions.| 近义词：transom, muntin | 反义词：None
