/ˈhɛksərz/| n. |巫师；施行魔法的人|People who practice magic, often with malicious intent.  They may use spells, charms, or other supernatural means to harm or control others.  The term often carries a negative connotation.|Example: The villagers feared the hexers who lived in the dark forest.|近义词：sorcerers, witches, warlocks|反义词：healers, saints, clergymen
