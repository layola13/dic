pass up on:/pæs ʌp ˈɒn/|/ˈpæs ʌp ɑːn/| phrasal verb|放弃；错过；拒绝|To decline or forgo an opportunity or something offered.  It implies a conscious decision to reject something potentially beneficial. |例句：I couldn't pass up on the chance to travel to Europe. 我不能错过去欧洲旅行的机会。
I regret passing up on that job offer. 我后悔错过了那份工作机会。|近义词：refuse, reject, decline, forgo|反义词：accept, embrace, seize


USAGE->

She passes up on dessert every night. 她每晚都放弃吃甜点。
