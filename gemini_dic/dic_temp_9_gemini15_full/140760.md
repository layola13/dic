omnivores:/ˈɒmnɪvɔːrz/ /ˈɑːmnɪvɔːrz/ | n. |杂食动物| Animals that eat both plants and animals. Omnivores play a crucial role in their ecosystems by controlling populations of both plants and animals and contributing to nutrient cycling.  | Example: Bears are omnivores; they eat berries and fish. 熊是杂食动物；它们吃浆果和鱼。  Pigs are omnivores, consuming a wide variety of foods. 猪是杂食动物，吃各种各样的食物。|近义词：general feeders|反义词：herbivores, carnivores|名词
