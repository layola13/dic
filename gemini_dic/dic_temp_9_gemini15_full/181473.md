rejoined:/ˌriːˈdʒɔɪnd/| v.|重新加入，重新结合|To join again; to become a member of something again after having left it.  It can also refer to the act of joining two or more things together again after they have been separated.|例句：After a brief hiatus, she rejoined the team.  在她短暂休假后，她重新加入了团队。

例句：The two broken pieces of the vase were rejoined with glue.  花瓶的两块断裂的部分用胶水重新粘合在一起了。


近义词：reunite, recombine, rejoin, reunite

反义词：separate, leave, depart


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rejoins the discussion after a short break. 他在短暂休息后重新加入讨论。
She rejoins her friends at the park every Saturday.  她每个星期六都重新加入她在公园的朋友们。


一般过去时:
He rejoined the army after his injury healed. 他的伤痊愈后，他重新参军。
She rejoined the choir after a year's absence.  她离开一年后又重新加入了合唱团。


现在完成时:
He has rejoined the company after a period of freelancing. 他自由职业一段时间后又重新加入了公司。
She has rejoined the political party she left years ago. 她离开了多年后又重新加入了那个政党。


过去完成时:
He had rejoined the club before the annual meeting.  在年度会议之前，他已经重新加入了俱乐部。
She had rejoined her family before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前，她已经和家人团聚了。


将来时:
He will rejoin his unit next month. 他下个月将重新加入他的部队。
She will rejoin the project team after her vacation. 她休假后将重新加入项目团队。


虚拟语气:
If he rejoined the team, we would win the championship. 如果他重新加入团队，我们将赢得冠军。
I wish she had rejoined the band sooner. 我希望她早点重新加入乐队。


被动语态:
He was rejoined to his family after years of separation.  多年分离后，他和家人团聚了。(注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The broken pottery was rejoined carefully by the expert.  专家小心地将破碎的陶器重新粘合在一起。


疑问句:
Will he rejoin the project? 他会重新加入项目吗？
Did she rejoin the club after the incident?  事件发生后她重新加入俱乐部了吗？


否定句:
He did not rejoin the conversation. 他没有重新加入谈话。
She has not rejoined the society since moving to a new city.  自从搬到新城市后，她就没有重新加入那个社团。


PHRASE->
rejoin the conversation: 重新加入谈话
rejoin the workforce: 重返职场
rejoin the party: 重新加入聚会
rejoin the fight: 重新加入战斗
rejoin the group: 重新加入小组
