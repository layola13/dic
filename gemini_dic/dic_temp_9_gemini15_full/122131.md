mistranslation:/ˌmɪsˌtrænzˈleɪʃən/| n. |误译，曲解|An inaccurate or incorrect translation of words or text from one language to another. It can result from various factors, including a lack of understanding of the source language's nuances, cultural differences, or simply a careless mistake.|例句：The mistranslation of the contract led to a major dispute between the two companies.  误译合同导致两家公司之间发生重大纠纷。
The article contained several mistranslations that altered the author's intended meaning. 这篇文章包含几处误译，改变了作者的本意。|近义词：misinterpretation, misrendering, distortion|反义词：accurate translation, faithful rendering
