divestments:/daɪˈvɛstmənts/| n. |剥离；资产剥离；撤资 |The act of divesting oneself of something, especially an asset or investment.  It often refers to a company selling off a subsidiary, a division, or other assets. |例句：The company announced a series of divestments to improve its financial position.  该公司宣布了一系列资产剥离以改善其财务状况。

例句：The government's divestments in the coal industry have sparked controversy. 政府撤出煤炭行业的投资引发了争议。


近义词：disposals, sales, liquidations |反义词：acquisitions, investments |词性:名词
