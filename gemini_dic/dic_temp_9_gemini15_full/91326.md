hydrophobia:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfoʊbiə/| n.|狂犬病；恐水症|A morbid fear of water, typically a symptom of rabies.  It can also refer to the disease rabies itself, which often manifests with this fear.|例句：The dog bite led to hydrophobia, requiring immediate medical attention. 狗咬伤导致了狂犬病，需要立即就医。
例句：His hydrophobia prevented him from even washing his hands.他的恐水症让他连洗手都做不到。|近义词：rabies, fear of water, aquaphobia|反义词：None specifically, but  a lack of fear of water could be considered an antonym in the context of the phobia.
