retriers:/rɪˈtraɪərz/| n. |尝试者，重试者，取回者|People who attempt something again after a failure or setback; those who retrieve something.  It is a relatively uncommon word and often used in specific contexts like hunting or data recovery.|例句：The determined retriers finally managed to summit the mountain after several failed attempts. 那些意志坚定的尝试者在几次失败尝试后终于成功登顶了。 The data retriers worked tirelessly to recover the lost files. 数据恢复人员夜以继日地工作以恢复丢失的文件。|近义词：retryers, recoverers, retrievers|反义词：quitters, abandoners
