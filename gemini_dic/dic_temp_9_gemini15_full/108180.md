latterly:/ˈlætərli/| adv. |最近；近来； lately |Recently, in recent times.  It emphasizes a period of time closer to the present than simply "recently".|例句：Latterly, the company has seen a significant increase in profits. 最近，该公司利润显著增长。

例句：Latterly, he's been feeling much better. 近来，他感觉好多了。


近义词：recently, lately, of late


反义词：formerly, previously, in the past
