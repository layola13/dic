nonpredictive: /ˌnɒnprɪˈdɪktɪv/ | adj. |非预测性的，不具有预测性的 | Not able to predict or forecast future outcomes or trends.  It implies a lack of ability to anticipate events or results based on available data or past experiences. |

例句：

The model proved to be nonpredictive of the actual market behavior. = 该模型被证明无法预测实际的市场行为。

The study's nonpredictive results highlight the need for further research. = 该研究的非预测性结果突显了进一步研究的必要性。


近义词：unpredictable, unforeseeable, indeterminate


反义词：predictive, foreseeable, determinate


词性：形容词
