channeling:/ˈtʃænəlɪŋ/| v. |引导；疏导；表达；传达|To direct the flow of something, such as energy, emotions, or information, through a specific channel or medium.  It can also refer to expressing or communicating something indirectly. |例句：She channeled her anger into her painting. 她把怒火发泄到绘画上。

例句：The therapist helped the patient channel his grief. 治疗师帮助病人疏导他的悲伤。


近义词：directing, guiding, conveying, expressing, communicating |反义词：suppressing, repressing, blocking, obstructing |词性：动词

词源:
The word "channeling" comes from the noun "channel," ultimately derived from the Old French word "canel," meaning "gutter" or "pipe." The meaning evolved to encompass any conduit or passageway, leading to its figurative use in referring to the direction or guidance of something.
