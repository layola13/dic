polliwog:/ˈpɒliwɒɡ/ /ˈpɑːliwɑːɡ/| n. |蝌蚪|A polliwog is the larval stage of a frog or toad.  It is an aquatic creature with a tail and gills, and it undergoes metamorphosis to become an adult frog or toad.  |Example: The children were fascinated by the polliwogs swimming in the pond. 孩子们被池塘里游动的蝌蚪迷住了。|近义词：tadpole|反义词：frog, toad
