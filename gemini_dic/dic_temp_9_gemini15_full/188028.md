roughest: /ˈrʌfɪst/ | adj. | 最粗糙的，最艰难的 |  The most uneven or harsh in texture or surface; most difficult or unpleasant.  It is the superlative form of "rough". |例句：This is the roughest terrain we've encountered on this hike. 这段路是我们这次徒步旅行中遇到的最崎岖的地段。

The roughest part of the job was dealing with difficult clients. 这份工作最艰难的部分是应付难缠的客户。

|近义词：harshest, toughest, most difficult, most severe |反义词：smoothest, easiest, gentlest, mildest |词性：形容词
