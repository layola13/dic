hatched:/hætʃt/| v. |孵化；策划；(图案)有斜线纹的 |To come out of an egg; to bring forth (young) from an egg; to devise or plan (something); to mark with lines crossing each other diagonally.|例句：The chickens hatched from their eggs. 小鸡从蛋里孵出来了。|例句：They hatched a plan to escape. 他们策划了一个逃跑计划。|近义词：incubate, breed, devise, plot |反义词：destroy, ruin
