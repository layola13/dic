sazon:/səˈzoʊn/| n. |调味料，佐料|A mixture of spices and seasonings used to flavor food, particularly in Latin American cuisine.  It often includes cumin, oregano, chili powder, garlic powder, and other spices. |例句：She added a generous amount of sazón to the rice. 她往米饭里加了大量的佐料。|例句：The sazón gave the dish a rich, complex flavor. 这佐料使这道菜的味道丰富而复杂。|近义词：seasoning, spice blend, condiment|反义词：blandness (缺乏味道)
