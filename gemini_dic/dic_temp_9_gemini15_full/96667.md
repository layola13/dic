industrious:/ɪnˈdʌstriəs/| adj. |勤奋的；勤劳的；努力工作的 |Diligent and hardworking.  An industrious person works hard and is productive. |例句：She is an industrious student and always gets good grades. 她是一位勤奋的学生，成绩总是很好。

例句：The industrious bees were busy collecting nectar.  勤劳的蜜蜂正忙着采集花蜜。


近义词：diligent, hardworking, assiduous, tireless, energetic |反义词：lazy, idle, indolent, slothful
