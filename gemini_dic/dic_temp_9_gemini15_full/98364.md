insists:/ɪnˈsɪsts/| v.|坚持，强调|To state or declare something firmly or forcefully.|He insists that he is innocent. 他坚持说自己是无辜的。|maintains, asserts, affirms|yields, concedes, admits|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He insists on his rights. 他坚持他的权利。
She insists that we should go. 她坚持我们应该去。


一般过去时:
He insisted on seeing the manager. 他坚持要见经理。
She insisted that she was right. 她坚持认为她是对的。


现在完成时:
He has insisted on his innocence from the beginning. 他从一开始就坚持自己的清白。
She has insisted that this is the only solution. 她一直坚持这是唯一的解决方案。


过去完成时:
He had insisted on a thorough investigation. 他曾坚持要进行彻底调查。
She had insisted that the plan was flawed. 她曾经坚持认为这个计划有缺陷。


将来时:
He will insist on his opinion. 他将会坚持他的意见。
She will insist that you attend the meeting. 她会坚持让你参加会议。


虚拟语气:
I wish he wouldn't insist on doing it his way. 我希望他不坚持用他的方式去做。
She insisted that he be present. 她坚持他必须在场。


被动语态:
It was insisted that the meeting be postponed. 人们坚持会议应该推迟。
His innocence was insisted upon by his lawyer. 他的律师坚持他的清白。


疑问句:
Does he insist on going? 他坚持要去吗？
Does she insist that we stay? 她坚持我们要留下来吗？


否定句:
He doesn't insist on a private room. 他并不坚持要一个单间。
She didn't insist that we leave immediately. 她并没有坚持我们立即离开。


PHRASE->
insist on/upon: 坚持
insist that: 坚持说
insist in doing sth: 坚持做某事
cannot/couldn't insist enough: 再怎么强调也不为过
insist on the importance of: 强调…的重要性
