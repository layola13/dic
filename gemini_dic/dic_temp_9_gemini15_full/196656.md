sevak:/ˈsɛvək/| n.|仆人，侍者，服务员|A sevak is a servant or attendant, often used in a religious or spiritual context in South Asia.  It implies a devoted and often humble service.|例句：He worked as a sevak at the temple, dedicating his life to serving the deity. 他在寺庙里做仆人，一生致力于侍奉神明。|近义词：servant, attendant, minister|反义词：master, employer
