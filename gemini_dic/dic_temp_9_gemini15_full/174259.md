quested:/ˈkwɛstɪd/| v.|请求；寻求；探求|The word "quested" is the past tense and past participle of the verb "to quest."  It means to search for something, often something valuable or important, or to seek something earnestly.  It implies a long and possibly arduous journey or search.|例句：He quested for the lost treasure. 他寻找失落的宝藏。|They quested after knowledge. 他们寻求知识。|近义词：searched, sought, hunted, inquired|反义词：ignored, neglected
