disclaimer:/dɪsˈkleɪmər/| n. |免责声明，否认|A statement that denies something, especially responsibility or liability.  A disclaimer typically precedes a potentially controversial statement or action, aiming to protect the speaker or entity from legal or reputational damage.|Example: The company issued a disclaimer stating that it was not responsible for any damages caused by its product. 这家公司发布了一份免责声明，声明其不对其产品造成的任何损害负责。

Example: The website contains a disclaimer about the accuracy of the information provided.  该网站包含关于所提供信息准确性的免责声明。


近义词：denial, repudiation, disavowal
反义词：affirmation, confirmation, endorsement


词性：名词
