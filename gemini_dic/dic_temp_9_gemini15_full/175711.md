rapier:/ˈreɪpiə(r)/| n. |细剑；敏捷；尖刻|A rapier is a slender, light sword, typically with a two-edged blade, used for thrusting.  It's also used figuratively to describe something sharp, witty, or pointed. |例句：He drew his rapier and lunged at his opponent. 他拔出细剑向对手猛刺。  Her wit was as sharp as a rapier. 她的才智如同细剑般锋利。|近义词：foil, sword, dagger (in some contexts)|反义词：blunt weapon, clumsy, dull|名词
