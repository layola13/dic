ipos:/ˈaɪpoʊz/| n.|首次公开募股|Initial Public Offering; the first time a company sells its stock to the public.  IPOs are a major way for companies to raise capital and become publicly traded.|例句：The company's IPO was a huge success, raising billions of dollars. 这家公司的IPO非常成功，筹集了数十亿美元。|例句：Many investors are eagerly awaiting the upcoming IPO of the tech startup. 许多投资者都在热切期待这家科技初创公司的即将到来的IPO。|近义词：stock offering, public offering|反义词：private placement, going private|名词
