immateriality:/ɪˌmætɪriˈælɪti/| n. |非物质性；不重要；无关紧要|The immateriality of the spirit is a central tenet of many religions.  精神的非物质性是许多宗教的核心信条。|近义词：unimportance, irrelevance, insignificance|反义词：materiality, importance, relevance|名词


英文解析:Immateriality refers to the state or quality of being immaterial; lacking physical substance or form.  It can also mean lacking importance or relevance.


例句:

The immateriality of the soul is a topic of ongoing philosophical debate.  灵魂的非物质性是一个持续进行的哲学辩论话题。

His concerns were deemed immaterial to the main issue.  他的担忧被认为与主要问题无关。
