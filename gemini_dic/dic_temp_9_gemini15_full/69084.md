faunlike:/ˈfɔːnlaɪk/| adj.|似山羊的；好色的|Resembling a fawn; suggestive of a fawn in its gentleness or grace; sometimes used to suggest lasciviousness or lustfulness.|例句：Her faunlike eyes held a mischievous glint.  她那像小鹿一样的眼睛里闪烁着淘气的神采。|例句：The painting depicted a faunlike creature with satyr-like features.  这幅画描绘了一个具有萨提尔特征的似山羊的生物。|近义词：doe-eyed,  buck-like (in the sense of resembling a deer)|反义词：wolfish (in the sense of aggressive or predatory)
