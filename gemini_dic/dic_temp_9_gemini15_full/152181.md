patriarchies:/ˈpeɪtriɑːrkiːz/| n.|父权制；族长统治|A social system in which men hold primary power;  it involves the dominance of men in social structures and roles, including family, government, and religion.  It often entails the subordination of women and other marginalized groups.|例句：Many cultures throughout history have been characterized by strong patriarchies.  许多历史上不同的文化都以强大的父权制为特征。|例句：The feminist movement actively challenges patriarchal structures and norms. 女权运动积极挑战父权结构和规范。|近义词：male dominance, father-rule,  sexism (partially)|反义词：matriarchy, egalitarianism
