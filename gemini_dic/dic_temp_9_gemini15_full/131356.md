词源:
The word "noncholeric" is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and "choleric" (meaning "easily angered; bad-tempered").  "Choleric" itself originates from the Greek word "cholē" (χολή), meaning "bile," which in ancient humoral theory was associated with anger and irritability.
