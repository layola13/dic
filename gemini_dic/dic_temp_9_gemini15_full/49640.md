demilitarises:/ˌdiːˌmɪlɪtəraɪzɪz/ | vt. |非军事化；解除武装|To remove military forces or weapons from (a place or area).  The process of removing military forces, weapons, and fortifications from a region or country to make it neutral or peaceful.|The government demilitarized the border region to reduce tensions with its neighbor.  政府将边境地区非军事化以减少与邻国的紧张关系。|近义词：neutralize, disarm |反义词：militarize
