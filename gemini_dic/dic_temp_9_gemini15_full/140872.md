on-the-shelf: /ˌɒn ðə ʃɛlf/ | adj. | 可供选择的；现成的；库存的 |  Ready for immediate use or purchase; available; in stock.  | 例句：The new model is on-the-shelf and ready to ship. 新型号已上市并准备发货。


The new software is on the shelf, meaning customers can buy it now. 新软件已经上市，这意味着客户现在可以购买了。


近义词：available, ready, in stock, stocked


反义词：unavailable, out of stock, discontinued


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The product is on the shelf. = 产品已上架。
These books are on the shelf. = 这些书在书架上。


一般过去时:
The game was on the shelf for a year. = 这款游戏停售了一年。
The product was on the shelf last week. = 上周该产品已上架。


现在完成时:
The items have been on the shelf for months. = 这些商品已经上架几个月了。
The company has had the product on the shelf since last year. = 该公司自去年以来就一直有该产品上架。


过去完成时:
The product had been on the shelf before the recall. = 在召回之前，该产品已上架。
They had put the prototype on the shelf for further development. = 他们已经将原型搁置起来以作进一步开发。


将来时:
The new phone will be on the shelf next month. = 新手机将于下个月上市。
The updated software will be on the shelf by the end of the year. = 更新后的软件将在年底前上市。


虚拟语气:
If the product were on the shelf, we would sell it. = 如果产品已上架，我们会销售它。
I wish the new features were on the shelf already. = 我希望新功能已经上市了。


被动语态:
The product was put on the shelf by the manufacturer. = 该产品由制造商上架。
The old models are being removed from the shelf. = 旧型号正在下架。


疑问句:
Is the new phone on the shelf yet? = 新手机已经上架了吗？
Are all the sizes on the shelf? = 所有尺寸的商品都上架了吗？


否定句:
The product is not on the shelf at this store. = 这家商店没有该产品。
That version of the software isn't on the shelf anymore. = 该软件版本不再上架了。


PHRASE->
put on the shelf:  搁置；暂时不考虑
take off the shelf:  从货架上取下；重新启用
back on the shelf:  重新上架
off the shelf:  现成的；标准的
on the shelf for a while:  暂时搁置一段时间
