/ˈkrɪspər/| n. |CRISPR|基因编辑技术，CRISPR-Cas系统|CRISPR stands for Clustered Regularly Interspaced Short Palindromic Repeats. It's a revolutionary gene-editing technology that allows scientists to precisely modify DNA sequences and gene function.  It utilizes a naturally occurring genome editing system found in bacteria to target and modify specific sections of DNA. |Scientists are using CRISPR to develop new treatments for genetic diseases.|近义词：genome editing technology|反义词：无
