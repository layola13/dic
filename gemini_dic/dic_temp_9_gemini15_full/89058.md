holometabolic:/ˌhɒləʊmɛˈtæbəlɪk/| adj.|完全变态的|  Relating to or characterized by holometabolism, the type of insect development in which the larva undergoes a complete metamorphosis, transforming into a pupa before emerging as an adult.  This is in contrast to incomplete metamorphosis (hemimetabolism), where the young resemble smaller versions of the adults.  Examples of holometabolous insects include butterflies, beetles, and flies.|The butterfly exhibits holometabolic development, undergoing a complete transformation from larva to pupa to adult.|近义词：complete metamorphic|反义词：hemimetabolic
