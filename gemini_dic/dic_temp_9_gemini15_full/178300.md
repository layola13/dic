reconcilable: /ˌrɛkənˈsaɪləbl/ | adj. | 可调和的；可和解的；可协调的 | capable of being reconciled; able to be made compatible or consistent.  | The two opposing viewpoints seemed irreconcilable at first, but after much discussion, a compromise was reached.  两个对立的观点起初似乎无法调和，但在经过多次讨论后，达成了妥协。 | compatible, consistent, agreeable | irreconcilable, incompatible, conflicting

The differences between them are usually reconcilable. = 他们之间的分歧通常是可以调和的。
This matter is reconcilable with our company's principles. = 这件事与我们公司的原则是一致的。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

We have found that their positions are reconcilable. = 我们发现他们的立场是可以调和的。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:

词源:
From Middle English *reconsiliablen, from Old French *reconciliables, from Latin *reconciliābilis, from reconciliāre "to reconcile."  The Latin root is *con- "together" + *ciliāre "to unite, appease."
