krabby:/ˈkræbi/| adj.|脾气暴躁的，易怒的，蟹状的 |  Describes someone who is easily annoyed or angered, or something resembling a crab in appearance.  | Example:  He's been really krabby all day. 他今天一整天都脾气暴躁。 The old ship looked krabby and weathered. 那艘老船看起来破旧不堪，像螃蟹一样。|近义词：grumpy, irritable, crotchety, crabbed |反义词：cheerful, pleasant, amiable, good-natured |形容词
