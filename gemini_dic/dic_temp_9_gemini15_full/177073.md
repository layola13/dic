reapologized:/ˌriːəˈpɑːlədʒaɪzd/ | v. | 再道歉，再次道歉 | To apologize again; to offer an apology a second time.  | He reapologized for his insensitive remarks. 他再次为其不敏感的言论道歉。 | apologize, excuse oneself |  insult, offend | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reapologizes frequently. = 他经常道歉。
She reapologizes if she makes a mistake. = 她犯错后会再次道歉。


一般过去时:
He reapologized for his behavior. = 他为他的行为再次道歉。
She reapologized to her boss for the delay. = 她向老板再次为延误道歉。


现在完成时:
He has reapologized several times already. = 他已经多次道歉了。
She has reapologized, but I still feel hurt. = 她已经再次道歉了，但我仍然感到受伤。


过去完成时:
He had reapologized before the meeting even started. = 会议甚至还没开始，他就已经再次道歉了。
She had reapologized to him earlier that day. = 那天早些时候，她已经向他道歉了。


将来时:
He will reapologize if necessary. = 必要时他会再次道歉。
She will reapologize tomorrow. = 她明天会再次道歉。


虚拟语气:
If he had reapologized sooner, things might have been different. = 如果他早点道歉，情况可能会有所不同。
I wish he had reapologized more sincerely. = 我希望他更真诚地道歉。


被动语态:
His apology was re-offered. = 他的道歉被再次提出。
A re-apology was made to the victim. = 向受害者再次道歉。


疑问句:

Will she reapologize publicly? = 她会公开再次道歉吗？


否定句:
He did not reapologize. = 他没有再次道歉。
She refused to reapologize. = 她拒绝再次道歉。
