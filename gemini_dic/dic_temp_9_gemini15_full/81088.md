gram:/ɡræm/| n.|克，公克；语法；少量 |A unit of mass in the metric system, equal to one thousandth of a kilogram. Also, the study of the way words are used to form sentences.  |例句：The recipe calls for 100 grams of flour. 这道菜谱需要100克面粉。

例句：She is studying English grammar.  她在学习英语语法。|近义词：kilogram(重量), syntax(语法分支)|反义词：N/A|名词
