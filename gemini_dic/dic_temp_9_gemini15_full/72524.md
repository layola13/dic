flushes:/ˈflʌʃɪz/| n. v.|脸红；冲洗；大量涌现|  The act of becoming red in the face, typically due to embarrassment, anger, or exertion;  a sudden rush of something. |例句：Her cheeks flushed with embarrassment.  她的脸颊羞红了。
