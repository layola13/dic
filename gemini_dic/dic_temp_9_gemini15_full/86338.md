hecklers:/ˈhɛklərz/| n.|起哄者，干扰者|A heckler is a person who shouts or makes disruptive noises to interrupt a public speaker or performer.  They often express disapproval or disagreement. |例句：The comedian dealt with the hecklers by making jokes about their comments.  这位喜剧演员通过拿起哄者的评论开玩笑来应对他们。

例句：The hecklers drowned out the speaker's voice with their shouts. 起哄者的叫喊声盖过了演讲者的声音。


近义词：disruptors, interrupters, hecklers

反义词：listeners, supporters, audience


词性: 名词
