coccyges:/ˈkɒksɪdʒiːz/| n.|尾骨(复数)|The coccyges are the bones forming the coccyx, the terminal part of the vertebral column in humans and other vertebrates.  They are small, fused vertebrae.  |例句：The doctor examined the patient's coccyges after the fall.  医生在病人摔倒后检查了他的尾骨。

例句：The x-ray showed a fracture in one of the coccyges. X光显示尾骨其中一块骨头骨折了。


近义词: coccyx bones, caudal vertebrae (more general)|反义词: None. |词性:noun
