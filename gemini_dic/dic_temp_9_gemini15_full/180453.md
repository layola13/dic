regarment:/rɪˈɡɑːrmənt/| n. |再装备，重新武装|The rearming or supplying of troops or forces with new equipment or supplies.  It often implies a process of replenishing after a loss or depletion.|例句：The army underwent a complete reargement after the devastating battle.  军队在惨烈的战斗后进行了全面整补。|例句：The company's regarment of its marketing team involved hiring several new specialists. 公司对其市场营销团队的重新装备包括聘请几位新的专家。|近义词：rearmament, re-equipping, resupply|反义词：disarmament, decommissioning|名词
