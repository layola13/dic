kibbitzes:/ˈkɪbɪtsɪz/| v.|喋喋不休地评论；插嘴；瞎掺和 |To offer unsolicited advice or comments, often interrupting or interfering.  |例句：He constantly kibbitzes during important meetings, disrupting the flow of discussion. 他在重要的会议上不断地插嘴，打断了讨论的流程。
例句：Stop kibbitzing! Let them handle it themselves.  别再瞎掺和了！让他们自己处理。|近义词：butt in, interfere, meddle, interject |反义词：remain silent, stay out of it
