identities:/aɪˈdentɪtiz/| n. |身份；特性；同一性 |The word "identities" refers to the qualities or attributes that distinguish or identify a person or thing.  It can also refer to a person's sense of self and belonging.  The plural form emphasizes multiple aspects of identity or multiple individuals possessing distinct identities.|例句：He explored his multiple identities as a father, a teacher, and an artist. 他探索了他作为父亲、老师和艺术家的多种身份。

例句：The investigation revealed conflicting identities among the suspects.  调查显示嫌疑人之间身份不符。


近义词：personalities, characteristics, natures, selves |反义词：anonymity, incognito


词性：名词
