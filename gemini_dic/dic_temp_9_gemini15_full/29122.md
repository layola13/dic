cascaron:/kɑːsˈkɑːrən/| n.|彩蛋；空心巧克力蛋；硬壳|A cascaron is a hollowed-out egg, often decorated, that is filled with confetti or small toys and used in celebrations, particularly during Easter.  It's a tradition in some Hispanic cultures.|例句：The children excitedly smashed the cascarones at the Easter egg hunt. 孩子们在复活节寻蛋活动中兴奋地砸碎了彩蛋。
例句：She carefully decorated the cascarones with bright colors and glitter. 她小心翼翼地用鲜艳的色彩和亮片装饰彩蛋。|近义词：decorated egg, hollow egg|反义词：solid egg
