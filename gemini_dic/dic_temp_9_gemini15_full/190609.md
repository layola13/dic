saturating:/ˈsætʃəreɪtɪŋ/| v.| 使饱和；浸透；充满 | To fill (something) completely with something else; to soak thoroughly. |例句：The heavy rain saturated the ground. 大雨使地面饱和。|例句：The sponge was saturated with water. 海绵被水浸透了。|近义词：soak, imbue, permeate |反义词：dry, dehydrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain is saturating the soil. = 雨水正在使土壤饱和。
The dye is saturating the fabric. = 染料正在浸透织物。


一般过去时:
The market was saturated with similar products. = 市场已被类似产品饱和。
The intense heat saturated the air with humidity. = 强烈的热量使空气充满了湿度。


现在完成时:
The company has saturated the market with its new product. = 公司的新产品已经使市场饱和。
The prolonged rain has saturated the ground. = 连续的降雨已经使地面饱和。


过去完成时:
The soil had been saturated by the previous day's rain. = 土壤已经被前一天的雨水浸透了。
The sponge had been saturated with cleaning solution. = 海绵已经被清洁剂浸透了。


将来时:
The upcoming marketing campaign will saturate the market. = 即将来临的营销活动将使市场饱和。
We will saturate the wood with preservative. = 我们将用防腐剂浸泡木材。


虚拟语气:
If we saturated the market, we would dominate the industry. = 如果我们使市场饱和，我们将主导行业。
I wish I had saturated the canvas with more paint. = 我希望我用更多的颜料浸透了画布。


被动语态:
The market is being saturated by competing brands. = 市场正被竞争品牌饱和。
The fabric is being saturated with dye. = 织物正在被染料浸透。


疑问句:
Is the solution saturating the material properly? = 溶液是否正在正确地使材料饱和？

否定句:
The fabric isn't saturating properly. = 织物没有被充分浸透。
The company didn't saturate the market with its advertising campaign. = 该公司的广告宣传活动没有使市场饱和。
