nonsoldier:/ˈnɒnˌsouldʒər/| n. |非战斗人员，平民|A nonsoldier is a person who is not a member of the armed forces.  This term often refers to civilians in a military context,  distinguishing them from soldiers, sailors, airmen, and marines. It can also refer to individuals within a military organization who do not hold combat roles.|Example:  The nonsoldiers were evacuated from the war zone.  非战斗人员从战区撤离。 The camp housed both soldiers and nonsoldiers. 营地里既有士兵也有非战斗人员。|近义词：civilian, noncombatant|反义词：soldier, serviceman, military personnel|名词
