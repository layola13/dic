hemicarp:/ˈhɛmɪkɑːrp/| n. |半个果实，半果 |Half of a fruit, especially one of the two halves of a schizocarp.  A hemicarp is one of the two one-seeded portions of a schizocarp that separate at maturity.  |例句：The mature fruit splits into two hemicarps. 成熟的果实裂成两个半果。

The two hemicarps of the plant were easily distinguishable.  这植物的两个半果很容易区分。|近义词：mericarp (in some contexts)|反义词：whole fruit, entire fruit|
