英文解析：Fragileness refers to the quality or state of being fragile; easily broken or damaged; delicate and vulnerable.  It emphasizes the susceptibility to harm or destruction.  The added 's' indicates a possessive form, suggesting something belonging to or characteristic of fragility.  For instance, "the vase's fragileness" means the quality of being fragile that the vase possesses.

例句：

The fragileness of the peace treaty was a major concern for the international community.  和平条约的脆弱性是国际社会的主要担忧。

Her fragileness after the surgery was evident in her pale complexion and weak movements. 她手术后的脆弱性在她苍白的脸色和虚弱的动作中显而易见。


近义词：delicatesse, vulnerability, weakness, brittleness, frailty


反义词：strength, robustness, resilience, durability, toughness
