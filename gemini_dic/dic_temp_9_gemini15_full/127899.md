neoliberal:/ˌniːoʊˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. | 新自由主义的 |  Relating to or denoting a modified form of liberalism tending to favor free-market capitalism.  It emphasizes deregulation, privatization, and reduced government spending.  Neoliberal policies often aim to create a more competitive and efficient economy, but critics argue they can exacerbate inequality and harm social welfare programs. | The neoliberal policies implemented in the 1980s led to increased economic inequality. 新自由主义政策在20世纪80年代的实施导致了经济不平等的加剧。|  free-market, laissez-faire | socialist, interventionist |
