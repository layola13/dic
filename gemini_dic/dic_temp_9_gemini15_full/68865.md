fast-lane:/fæst leɪn/| n. |快车道；成功之路|The fast lane refers to a life characterized by ambition, high achievement, and often, a relentless pursuit of success, often at the expense of personal relationships or well-being.  It can also literally refer to the leftmost lane on a highway designed for faster-moving traffic.|例句：He's living in the fast lane, working long hours and traveling constantly. 他过着快节奏的生活，长时间工作并经常旅行。|例句：She decided to leave the fast lane and pursue a simpler life. 她决定离开快节奏的生活，追求更简单的生活。|近义词：express lane, high-achiever lifestyle|反义词：slow lane, leisurely life|名词
