farl:/fɑːrl/| n. |小圆面包，小饼|A small, flat quick bread, often made from oatmeal or barley.  It is a traditional bread in Scotland and other parts of the British Isles.|例句：We had farls with our breakfast this morning. = 今天早上我们早餐吃了小圆面包。|例句：The baker sells a variety of farls, including oat and barley. = 面包师出售各种小圆面包，包括燕麦和小麦的。|近义词：bannock, scone, biscuit|反义词:loaf(large bread)|名词
