magnets:/ˈmæɡnɪts/| n.|磁铁，磁体|A magnet is a piece of iron or other material that is able to attract iron.  Magnets are used in many everyday applications, from holding notes on refrigerators to powering electric motors.|例句：The refrigerator magnet held the shopping list firmly.  冰箱磁铁牢牢地吸住了购物清单。|例句：The experiment used powerful magnets to levitate the train. 实验利用强大的磁铁使火车悬浮。|近义词：lodestone|反义词：nonmagnetic material
