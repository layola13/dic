fusiliers:/ˈfjuːzɪliərz/| n. |步枪兵；(鱼类)笛鯛科鱼类|Fusiliers are light infantry soldiers armed with fusils (early type of rifle).  The term also refers to a family of brightly colored tropical fish. |例句：The fusiliers were deployed to the front line.  步枪兵被部署到前线。

The fusiliers are known for their vibrant colors. 笛鯛科鱼类以其鲜艳的色彩而闻名。|近义词：riflemen (for the military meaning), soldier |反义词：artillerymen (for the military meaning)
