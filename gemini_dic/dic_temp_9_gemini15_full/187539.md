romanesques:/ˌrɒməˈnesks/| adj.| 罗马式风格的；奇特的，异样的 | Relating to or characteristic of Romanesque architecture or art.  The term can also be used to describe something unusual or bizarre. |例句：The church boasts impressive Romanesque architecture. 这座教堂拥有令人印象深刻的罗马式建筑。

The novel features a series of romanesque characters and plot twists. 这部小说以一系列奇特的人物和情节转折为特色。|近义词：picturesque (picturesque is more about visual appeal), unusual, bizarre, strange |反义词：modern, plain, conventional
