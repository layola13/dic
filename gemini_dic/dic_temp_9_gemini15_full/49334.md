delimited:/dɪˈlɪmɪtɪd/ | adj. | 分隔的；有限制的 |  Marked off or divided by boundaries or limits.  The text is delimited by commas.  |  The data was delimited by tabs.  这个数据是用制表符分隔的。|  separated, bounded, confined | unlimited, unbounded | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The data is delimited by commas. = 数据用逗号分隔。
The field is delimited by semicolons. = 字段用分号分隔。

一般过去时:
The document was delimited by paragraphs. = 文件由段落分隔。
The sections were delimited with clear headings. = 各部分用清晰的标题分隔。

现在完成时:
The project has been delimited into manageable tasks. = 项目已被分解成可管理的任务。
The area has been delimited for construction. = 该区域已被划定用于施工。

过去完成时:
The boundaries had been delimited before the survey began. = 调查开始前边界已经划分好了。
The responsibilities had been clearly delimited before the project started. = 项目开始前责任已明确划分。

将来时:
The software will delimit the text automatically. = 软件将自动分隔文本。
The region will be delimited by a new fence. = 该区域将由新的围栏分隔。

虚拟语气:
If the data were delimited properly, the analysis would be easier. = 如果数据被正确分隔，分析就会更容易。
If the tasks had been better delimited, we would have finished sooner. = 如果任务划分得更好，我们就会早点完成。

被动语态:
The text is delimited by spaces. = 文本由空格分隔。
The results were delimited according to their categories. = 结果根据其类别进行分隔。

疑问句:
How is the data delimited? = 数据是如何分隔的？
Is the information delimited by any specific characters? = 信息是否由任何特定字符分隔？

否定句:
The data is not delimited correctly. = 数据未正确分隔。
The fields were not delimited properly. = 字段未正确分隔。
