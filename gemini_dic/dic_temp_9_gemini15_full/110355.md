lightnings:/ˈlaɪtnɪŋz/| n.|闪电(复数)|The plural form of lightning, referring to multiple flashes of lightning.  It describes the phenomenon of bright flashes of light in the sky caused by electrical discharges.|Example: The lightnings illuminated the stormy night sky.  闪电照亮了暴风雨的夜空。  The frequent lightnings during the thunderstorm caused a power outage.  雷暴期间频繁的闪电导致了停电。|近义词：flashes, bolts|反义词：无|名词
