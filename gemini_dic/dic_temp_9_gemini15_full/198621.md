shunning:/ˈʃʌnɪŋ/| v.| 躲避；避免；摒弃 |The act of avoiding or rejecting someone or something.  It implies a deliberate and often persistent avoidance, often due to disapproval or dislike.|例句：He was shunning his responsibilities. 他在逃避责任。 He is shunning social contact. 他在避免社交接触。|近义词：avoiding, evading, escaping, rejecting |反义词：embracing, accepting, welcoming

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shuns responsibility. = 他逃避责任。
They shun social gatherings. = 他们躲避社交聚会。

一般过去时:
She shunned his advances. = 她躲避了他的示好。
They shunned the limelight. = 他们躲避了聚光灯。

现在完成时:
He has shunned his family for years. = 他多年来一直躲避他的家人。
She has shunned all contact with him since the argument. = 自从争吵后，她就一直躲避和他的一切联系。

过去完成时:
He had shunned his duties for so long that it was impossible to catch up. = 他长期逃避职责，以至于无法补救。
They had shunned the opportunity to invest, and now regret it. = 他们错过了投资机会，现在后悔了。

将来时:
I will shun all contact with him. = 我将躲避和他的一切联系。
She will shun the media. = 她将躲避媒体。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I would shun such a risky investment. = 如果我是他，我会避免这样冒险的投资。
I wish I had shunned that opportunity, it turned out badly. = 我真希望我当时躲避了那个机会，结果很糟糕。

被动语态:
His advice was shunned by many. = 他的建议被许多人所忽视。
The controversial proposal was shunned by the board. = 董事会回避了这个有争议的提案。

疑问句:
Why are you shunning me? = 你为什么躲着我？
Is he shunning his responsibilities? = 他是在逃避责任吗？

否定句:
He doesn't shun hard work. = 他不逃避努力工作。
She doesn't shun challenges. = 她不回避挑战。
