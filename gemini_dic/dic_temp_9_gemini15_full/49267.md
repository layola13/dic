delibation:/ˌdɪlɪˈbeɪʃən/| n. | 熟虑，仔细考虑；商议 | Deliberation is the careful consideration of something.  It often involves weighing different options before making a decision.  The process can be individual or involve a group discussion. | Example: After much deliberation, she decided to accept the job offer.  经过深思熟虑，她决定接受这份工作。|近义词：consideration, reflection, contemplation, discussion, conference |反义词：impulsiveness, rashness |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee is holding a deliberation on the new policy. = 委员会正在就新政策进行讨论。
His deliberation on the matter is quite thorough. = 他对这件事的考虑非常周全。


一般过去时:
The jury deliberated for hours before reaching a verdict. = 陪审团经过数小时的商议才做出裁决。
Extensive deliberation preceded the final decision. = 最终决定之前进行了广泛的讨论。


现在完成时:
They have completed their deliberation and reached a consensus. = 他们已经完成了讨论并达成共识。
The company has been in deliberation over the merger for months. = 公司就合并事宜已经商议了几个月。


过去完成时:
They had deliberated for days before announcing their decision. = 他们在宣布决定之前已经讨论了几天。
After they had deliberated the matter carefully, they decided to proceed. = 在仔细考虑此事后，他们决定继续进行。


将来时:

We will deliberate further before making a commitment. = 我们将在做出承诺之前进一步商议。


虚拟语气:
If we had deliberated more carefully, we might have avoided this problem. = 如果我们当时考虑得更仔细一些，也许就能避免这个问题。

被动语态:

疑问句:
