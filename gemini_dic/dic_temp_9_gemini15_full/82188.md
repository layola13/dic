grossed-out:/ɡroʊst ˈaʊt/| adj.|感到恶心的；极度厌恶的|Feeling extreme disgust or revulsion.  The sight of the rotting food grossed me out.  看到腐烂的食物让我恶心。|近义词：disgusted, repulsed, nauseated|反义词：delighted, pleased
