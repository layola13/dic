hunting:/ˈhʌntɪŋ/ | n. |狩猎；猎取；搜寻 | The act of pursuing and killing wild animals for food or sport; the act of searching for something.  |例句：He enjoys hunting deer in the fall. 他喜欢在秋天打猎鹿。

例句：The police are hunting for the escaped prisoner. 警方正在搜捕逃犯。|近义词： chasing, pursuing, tracking, searching |反义词： sheltering, protecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hunts deer every weekend. = 他每个周末都去打猎鹿。
They hunt for treasures in old houses. = 他们在老房子里寻宝。

一般过去时:
She hunted rabbits in the woods. = 她曾在树林里打兔子。
The detectives hunted down the clues. = 探员们追查线索。

现在完成时:
He has hunted in this forest many times. = 他在这片森林里打猎很多次了。
They have been hunting for the lost key for hours. = 他们已经找丢失的钥匙好几个小时了。

过去完成时:
He had hunted there before, so he knew the terrain well. = 他以前在那里打猎过，所以他很熟悉地形。
They had hunted for days before they finally found the missing child. = 他们搜寻了几天才最终找到失踪的孩子。

将来时:
We will hunt for mushrooms tomorrow. = 我们明天要去采蘑菇。
They will be hunting for a new apartment next month. = 他们下个月将要寻找新的公寓。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would go hunting. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去打猎。
I wish I could go hunting with you. = 我希望我能和你一起去打猎。

被动语态:
The deer were hunted by the hunters. = 鹿被猎人猎杀了。
The lost dog was hunted down by the rescuers. = 失踪的狗被救援人员找回了。

疑问句:
Do you hunt? = 你打猎吗？

否定句:
