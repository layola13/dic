damming:/ˈdæmɪŋ/| adj.| 令人震惊的；有害的；严重的 | Causing serious harm or damage; extremely critical or negative.  |例句：The damming evidence led to his conviction.  令人震惊的证据导致了他的定罪。

例句：His damming criticism of the project caused a stir. 他对该项目的严厉批评引起了一阵骚动。


近义词：crippling, devastating, incriminating, scathing, condemning

反义词：praiseworthy, commendable, complimentary, innocuous
