echo:/ˈɛkoʊ/| n./v.|回声；回响；模仿|The repetition of a sound caused by reflection of sound waves.|例句：The echo of his voice filled the cave.他的声音回荡在山洞里。|近义词：reverb, resonance|反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The empty canyon echoes with the sound of my voice. = 空旷的山谷回荡着我的声音。
The singer's voice echoes beautifully in the concert hall. = 歌手的歌声在音乐厅里优美地回响。


一般过去时:
The gunshot echoed through the night. = 枪声在夜空中回荡。
Her laughter echoed in the hallway. = 她的笑声在走廊里回荡。


现在完成时:
The news of the victory has echoed around the world. = 胜利的消息已传遍世界各地。
His words have echoed in my mind ever since. = 从那以后，他的话一直回响在我的脑海里。


过去完成时:
The sound of the explosion had echoed across the valley before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，爆炸声已经在山谷中回响。
The rumors had echoed throughout the town before the truth was revealed. = 真相大白之前，谣言已经在镇上流传开来。


将来时:
The announcement will echo across the nation. = 这一声明将在全国引起反响。
Her name will echo through history. = 她的名字将载入史册。


虚拟语气:
If I shouted, my voice would echo across the lake. = 如果我喊叫，我的声音会回荡在湖面上。
I wish my words would echo more effectively in the meeting. = 我希望我的话在会议上更有效果。


被动语态:
The announcement was echoed by all major news outlets. = 所有主要新闻媒体都报道了这一消息。
His achievements were echoed by many of his colleagues. = 许多同事都赞扬了他的成就。


疑问句:
Does your voice echo in this room? = 你的声音在这个房间里会回响吗？
Does the sound echo back to the source? = 声音会回荡到声源处吗？


否定句:
My voice did not echo in the small room. = 我的声音在这个小房间里没有回响。
The message did not echo with the intended audience. = 信息没有被目标受众所理解。
