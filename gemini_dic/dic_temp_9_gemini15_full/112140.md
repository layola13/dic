loofa:/ˈluːfə/| n. |丝瓜络，洗澡海绵 |A loofa is a natural sponge made from the fibrous interior of a dried luffa gourd.  It is used for bathing and exfoliating the skin. |例句：I use a loofa to scrub my back in the shower. 我用丝瓜络在淋浴时搓背。  I bought a new loofa at the drugstore. 我在药店买了一个新的丝瓜络。|近义词：sponge, washcloth, bath sponge |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑 smooth scrubbing tool)
