desensitizers:/ˌdiːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzərz/| n. |脱敏剂，减敏剂 |Substances that reduce or eliminate sensitivity to a stimulus, often used in medicine to treat allergies or reduce the effects of pain or anxiety. They work by altering the body's response to the stimulus. |Example:  Antihistamines are common desensitizers used to treat allergic reactions. 抗组胺药是常用的治疗过敏反应的脱敏剂。

The doctor prescribed desensitizers to help manage my seasonal allergies. 医生开了脱敏剂来帮助我控制季节性过敏。 |近义词：anesthetics, analgesics |反义词：sensitizers
