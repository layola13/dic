octopuses:/ˈɑːktəpʊsiːz/ | n. | 章鱼 (复数) | The plural of octopus.  It refers to more than one octopus.  The word "octopi" is also used, but "octopuses" is generally preferred. | There are many octopuses living in the deep ocean.  有很多章鱼生活在深海。|  octopodes (less common) |  --- | noun
