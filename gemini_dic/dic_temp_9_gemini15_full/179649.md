reengaged:/ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒd/| v. |重新投入，重新参与，重新订婚 |To begin to be involved in something again; to become engaged to someone again after a previous engagement has ended.  |例句：After a brief separation, they reengaged in their research project.  重新开始参与他们的研究项目之后，他们短暂地分开了。

例句：He reengaged with his ex-fiancée after a year apart.  一年分离后，他又与他的前未婚妻订婚了。

近义词：recommence, resume, restart, re-engage


反义词：disengage, withdraw, abandon


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reengage in the conversation after a pause. = 他们暂停后重新开始对话。
He reengages with his work after a long break. = 他长时间休息后重新投入工作。


一般过去时:
She reengaged her former colleague on a new project. = 她在一个新项目中重新与她以前的同事合作。
The army reengaged the enemy after a brief respite. = 军队短暂休整后再次与敌人交战。


现在完成时:
They have reengaged their commitment to environmental protection. = 他们已经重新承诺致力于环境保护。
We have reengaged our efforts to find a solution. = 我们已经重新努力寻找解决方案。


过去完成时:
He had reengaged his passion for painting after years of neglect. = 多年忽视后，他又重新燃起了对绘画的热情。
The company had reengaged its marketing team to develop a new strategy. = 公司已经重新启用其市场营销团队来制定新的战略。


将来时:
She will reengage with her studies after her maternity leave. = 她产假结束后将重新学习。
They will reengage the contractor to finish the project. = 他们将重新雇用承包商来完成项目。


虚拟语气:
If they reengaged their efforts, they might succeed. = 如果他们重新努力，他们可能会成功。
I wish he had reengaged with his family sooner. = 我希望他早点和家人重归于好。


被动语态:
The project was reengaged by the board after a review. = 该项目在审查后由董事会重新启动。
The negotiations were reengaged after a period of stalemate. = 在僵持一段时间后，谈判重新开始。


疑问句:

Have they reengaged their commitment to the cause? = 他们是否重新承诺为事业奋斗？


否定句:
They did not reengage in the argument. = 他们没有重新参与争论。
He refused to reengage with his former business partners. = 他拒绝与他以前的商业伙伴重新合作。
