rhapsodizing:/ˈræpsədaɪzɪŋ/| v.|狂想；热情洋溢地谈论；|滔滔不绝地讲述；充满热情地表达；|He spent the evening rhapsodizing about his trip to Italy. 他整个晚上都在热情洋溢地谈论他去意大利的旅行。|近义词：exalting, eulogizing, celebrating|反义词：criticizing, condemning, disparaging|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rhapsodizes about his childhood memories. = 他滔滔不绝地讲述他童年的回忆。
She rhapsodizes over the beauty of the sunset. = 她热情洋溢地赞美日落的美丽。

一般过去时:
He rhapsodized about his love for classical music. = 他热情洋溢地谈论他对古典音乐的热爱。
She rhapsodized about the delicious food she had eaten. = 她兴高采烈地描述她吃到的美味佳肴。

现在完成时:
He has rhapsodized about that experience for years. = 多年来，他一直滔滔不绝地讲述那次经历。
She has rhapsodized to anyone who would listen about her new job. = 她向任何愿意倾听的人热情洋溢地讲述她的新工作。

过去完成时:
He had rhapsodized about the book before he even finished reading it. = 他甚至还没读完那本书就已开始热情洋溢地谈论它了。
She had rhapsodized about her travels before she even started to pack her bags. = 她甚至还没开始收拾行李就开始滔滔不绝地谈论她的旅行了。

将来时:
He will rhapsodize about his success to his friends. = 他将向朋友们热情洋溢地讲述他的成功。
She will rhapsodize about the concert to her family. = 她将向家人兴致勃勃地描述这场音乐会。

虚拟语气:
If he had the chance, he would rhapsodize about it for hours. = 如果有机会，他会滔滔不绝地谈论几个小时。
If she were here, she would rhapsodize about the scenery. = 如果她在，她会热情洋溢地赞美风景。

被动语态:
His travels were rhapsodized about in the local newspaper. = 当地报纸热情洋溢地报道了他的旅行。
The beauty of the artwork was rhapsodized about by the critics. = 评论家们热情洋溢地赞美了这件艺术品的美丽。

疑问句:
Does he often rhapsodize about his past? = 他经常滔滔不绝地谈论他的过去吗？
Will she rhapsodize about her vacation? = 她会热情洋溢地讲述她的假期吗？

否定句:
He didn't rhapsodize about the difficulties he faced. = 他没有滔滔不绝地讲述他面临的困难。
She didn't rhapsodize about the negative aspects of the trip. = 她没有热情洋溢地谈论旅行的负面方面。


PHRASE->
rhapsodize about:  热情洋溢地谈论…
rhapsodize on:  对…进行热情洋溢的描述
rhapsodize over:  对…赞不绝口
rhapsodize in: 以…的方式热情洋溢地表达
rhapsodize with:  与…一起热情洋溢地谈论


词源:
From the noun "rhapsody," ultimately from the Greek word "rhapsōidia," meaning "stitching together of songs,"  referring to the ancient Greek practice of reciting epic poems in sections.  The "-izing" suffix indicates the act of doing something.
