overbearing:/ˌoʊvərˈbɛərɪŋ/ | adj. | 专横的；盛气凌人的；难以忍受的 | Dominating; arrogant; excessively controlling.  It implies a forceful and often unpleasant dominance over others, suppressing their opinions or desires. |


例句：

His overbearing manner alienated many of his colleagues.  他的专横态度使许多同事疏远了他。

Her overbearing mother made all the decisions for her.  她专横的母亲为她做所有决定。


近义词：domineering, bossy, tyrannical, controlling, imperious, dictatorial


反义词：submissive, meek, yielding, compliant, deferential


词性:形容词
