self-supporting:/ˌsɛlf səˈpɔːrtɪŋ/| adj.| 自给自足的，自立的 | Able to support oneself without outside help or assistance.  This term often refers to structures or systems that are stable and do not require external support. |例句：The self-supporting structure of the bridge is impressive. 这座桥的自支撑结构令人印象深刻。

The project is designed to be self-supporting after the initial funding period. 该项目旨在初始资金期结束后实现自给自足。|近义词：independent, autonomous, self-reliant |反义词：dependent, reliant, unsustainable
