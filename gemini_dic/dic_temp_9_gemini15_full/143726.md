outlive:/ˌaʊtˈlɪv/| vt.|比…活得长；比…经久；比…更持久|To live longer than (someone or something); to survive (someone or something).|例句：He outlived all his brothers. 他比他所有的兄弟都活得长。

例句：The old building outlived its usefulness. 这座旧建筑物已经失去了它的实用性。


近义词：survive, outlast

反义词：predecease, die before


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outlives his rivals in business. = 他在生意场上比他的竞争对手活得久。
The company outlives many of its competitors. = 这家公司比许多竞争对手都存活得更久。

一般过去时:
She outlived her husband by ten years. = 她比她丈夫多活了十年。
The empire outlived its founder by several centuries. = 这个帝国比它的创建者多存在了好几个世纪。

现在完成时:
The oak tree has outlived many generations of humans. = 这棵橡树比许多代人都活得长。
This tradition has outlived its original purpose. = 这个传统已经失去了它最初的目的。

过去完成时:
The old castle had outlived countless wars. = 这座古老的城堡经历了无数次的战争。
They had outlived all expectations regarding the project's lifespan. = 他们比项目预期寿命长寿得多。

将来时:
She will outlive her enemies. = 她将会比她的敌人活得更久。
I believe this technology will outlive its current limitations. = 我相信这项技术将会克服其目前的局限性。

虚拟语气:
If he had taken better care of himself, he might have outlived his wife. = 如果他更注意保养身体，他也许就能比妻子活得更久。
If the company had adapted better to the market, it might have outlived the recession. = 如果公司更好地适应市场，它也许就能挺过经济衰退。

被动语态:
The old house was outlived by the new city.  =  这栋老房子被新城市所取代（延续时间更短）。
His reputation will be outlived by his great works of art. =  他的声誉将因他伟大的艺术作品而流传。


疑问句:
Will this building outlive the next century? = 这座建筑物能延续到下一个世纪吗？
Can this product outlive its competitors in the market? = 这个产品能比竞争对手在市场上存在更久吗？

否定句:
The old car didn't outlive its warranty. = 这辆旧车没有超过保修期。
He sadly didn't outlive his illness. = 他不幸没有战胜疾病。


PHRASE->
outlive oneself = 活过自己的预期寿命

outlive one's reputation =  比自己的名声时间更长
outlive the storm =  度过难关
