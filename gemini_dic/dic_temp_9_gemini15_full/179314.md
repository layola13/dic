redoes:/ˌriːˈduːz/| v.|重新做，再做|To do something again.|The architect redid the blueprints after discovering a flaw. 建筑师在发现缺陷后重新绘制了蓝图。|remake, revise, repeat|leave alone, keep as is|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redid his homework. = 他重做了他的家庭作业。
She redid her makeup. = 她补了妆。

They redid the kitchen last year. = 他们去年重新装修了厨房。
I redid the presentation after receiving feedback. = 我在收到反馈后重新做了演示文稿。

She had redone the painting several times before she was satisfied. = 她在她满意之前已经重画了好几次。
