heels:/hiːlz/| n.|鞋跟；脚后跟|The raised part of a shoe or boot at the back, on which the heel rests.|例句：She wore high heels to the party. 她穿着高跟鞋去参加派对。

例句：I tripped and fell on my heels. 我绊倒了，摔在了脚后跟上。|近义词：back of foot, heel piece|反义词：toes
