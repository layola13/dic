petroleums:/pəˈtroʊliəm/| n. |石油（复数）|The plural form of petroleum, referring to different types or sources of petroleum.  It is less common than using "petroleum" to refer to multiple sources.|例句：The analysis showed variations in the properties of the petroleums from different wells.  不同油井的石油性质存在差异。|近义词：crude oil, mineral oil|反义词：无
