/ˈnjʊrəlɪŋkər/| n. |神经连接器；脑机接口|A neuralinker is a hypothetical or real device that allows direct communication between a brain and a computer or other external device.  It often involves implanting electrodes or other sensors into the brain to record and stimulate neural activity.|Example:  Scientists are working on developing a safe and effective neuralinker for treating neurological disorders. 科学家们正在努力开发一种安全有效的用于治疗神经系统疾病的神经连接器。|近义词：brain-computer interface (BCI), neural implant|反义词：None  (反义词缺乏实际意义)|名词
