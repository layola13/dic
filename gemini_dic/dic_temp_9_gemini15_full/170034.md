protegee:/ˈprɒtəʒeɪ/| n. |女被保护人，女门生；受保护者| A young person who is guided and helped by an older and more experienced person, especially in their career.  |She is a talented protegee of the renowned conductor.|近义词：pupil, apprentice, protégé|反义词：mentor, sponsor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is a protegee of a famous artist. = 她是一位著名艺术家的门生。
The company protects its proteges carefully. = 公司小心翼翼地保护着它的受保护者。


一般过去时:
He was a protegee of the former president. = 他是前总统的被保护人。
The young musician was carefully nurtured by her protegee. = 这位年轻的音乐家得到了她导师的精心培养。


现在完成时:
She has become a successful businesswoman thanks to her mentor's guidance as her protegee. = 多亏了她导师作为她的保护人的指导，她已经成为一名成功的商业女性。
The company has supported many proteges over the years. = 多年来，公司已经支持了许多受保护者。


过去完成时:
He had been a protegee of the famous writer before he started his own career. = 在他开始自己的职业生涯之前，他曾是那位著名作家的门生。
Before the scandal, she had been a highly regarded protegee in the industry. = 在丑闻爆发之前，她一直是业内备受推崇的受保护者。


将来时:
She will be a successful architect, given her talent and her mentor's support as her protegee. = 鉴于她的天赋和她导师作为她的保护人的支持，她将成为一名成功的建筑师。
The program will help many young proteges to flourish in their chosen fields. = 该项目将帮助许多年轻的受保护者在他们选择的领域蓬勃发展。


虚拟语气:
