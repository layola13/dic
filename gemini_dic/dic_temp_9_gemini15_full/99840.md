intermittingly: /ˌɪntərˈmɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 间歇地；断断续续地 |  occurring at intervals; not continuously.  | The rain fell intermittently throughout the day.  雨水一整天断断续续地下着。 |  periodically, sporadically, occasionally | continuously, constantly, steadily | 副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works intermittently. = 他间歇性地工作。
The machine runs intermittently. = 机器间歇性地运转。


一般过去时:
The lights flickered intermittently. = 灯光断断续续地闪烁。
The pain came intermittently. = 疼痛间歇性地出现。


现在完成时:
It has been raining intermittently for days. = 好几天了，雨断断续续地下着。
She has worked intermittently since her injury. = 自从受伤以来，她一直断断续续地工作。


过去完成时:
The engine had been running intermittently before it finally stopped. = 发动机在最终停止之前一直断断续续地运转。
They had felt intermittently ill for weeks. = 他们好几个星期都断断续续地感到不舒服。


将来时:
The traffic will likely be intermittently heavy during rush hour. = 在高峰时段，交通可能会间歇性地拥堵。
The project will proceed intermittently due to funding issues. = 由于资金问题，项目将间歇性地进行。


虚拟语气:
If the pain came intermittently, I would ignore it. = 如果疼痛间歇性地出现，我会忽略它。
I wish the rain would stop intermittently, not continuously. = 我希望雨能断断续续地下，而不是连续地下。


被动语态:
The signal was received intermittently. = 信号断断续续地接收到了。
The task was completed intermittently over several weeks. = 任务断断续续地持续了好几个星期才完成。


疑问句:
Does the machine operate intermittently? = 机器是间歇性运行的吗？
Were the symptoms intermittent? = 症状是间歇性的吗？


否定句:
The power supply did not fail intermittently; it failed completely. = 电源供应不是间歇性故障，而是完全故障。
The sun did not shine intermittently; it shone brightly all day. = 太阳不是间歇性地照耀，而是整天都阳光灿烂。


PHRASE->

词源:
From the word "intermittent," which is from Latin *intermittens, present participle of intermittere "to interrupt, cease for a time," from inter- "between, among" + mittere "to send, let go."
