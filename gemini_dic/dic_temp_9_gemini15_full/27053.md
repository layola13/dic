calendographer: /kælənˈdɒɡrəfər/ | n. | 日历编制者，历法学家 | A calendographer is a person who makes or studies calendars.  They are experts in chronology and the systems used to track time.  Their work might involve researching historical calendars, designing new calendar systems, or correcting inaccuracies in existing ones. | Example:  The renowned calendographer meticulously researched ancient Mayan calendar systems.  这位著名的日历编制者一丝不苟地研究了古代玛雅历法。 | 近义词:chronologist, calendar maker | 反义词: (无明确反义词)
