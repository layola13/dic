involver:/ɪnˈvɒlvər/ | n. | 牵涉者；卷入者 | A person or thing that involves someone or something in an activity, situation, or event.  |The main involver in the scandal was the former CEO.  这场丑闻的主要牵涉者是前任首席执行官。| participant, implicated party | uninvolved, outsider | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project involves many people. = 这个项目涉及很多人。
He involves himself in too many activities. = 他参与了太多的活动。


一般过去时:
The accident involved two cars. = 这起事故涉及两辆车。
She involved her family in her business. = 她把家人也卷入了她的生意。


现在完成时:
They have involved themselves in charity work. = 他们一直参与慈善工作。
The investigation has involved several government agencies. = 这项调查已经涉及了几个政府机构。


过去完成时:
He had involved himself in the dispute before I arrived. = 我到达之前，他已经卷入了这场纠纷。
The company had involved itself in a costly lawsuit. = 公司之前已经卷入了一场代价高昂的诉讼。


将来时:
The new policy will involve significant changes. = 新政策将带来重大变化。
We will involve the community in the decision-making process. = 我们将在决策过程中让社区参与进来。


虚拟语气:
If I involved myself, I would be in trouble. = 如果我参与其中，我会惹上麻烦。
I wish I hadn't involved him in this. = 我真希望我没有把他卷进来。


被动语态:
Many people were involved in the accident. = 许多人卷入了那起事故。
The company is involved in a major project. = 这家公司参与了一个大型项目。


疑问句:
Who was involved in the planning? = 谁参与了计划？
What factors are involved in this decision? = 此项决定的相关因素有哪些？


否定句:
I wasn't involved in that decision. = 我没有参与那个决定。
He wasn't involved in the crime. = 他没有参与犯罪。


PHRASE->
be involved in = 参与…
get involved = 卷入
deeply involved = 深度参与
become involved = 参与到…中
involved in a relationship = 处于恋爱关系中
