crocin:/ˈkroʊsɪn/| n.|藏红花素，番红花苷|A carotenoid pigment found in saffron.  It's responsible for the characteristic color and some of the flavor of saffron.  Crocin is a water-soluble glycoside and is believed to possess various biological activities.|例句：Crocin is the main pigment responsible for the yellow-orange color of saffron. 藏红花素是赋予藏红花黄橙色的主要色素。
