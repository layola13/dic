pyroborate:/ˌpaɪroʊˈbɔːreɪt/| n. |焦硼酸盐|A salt of pyroboric acid (H₂B₄O₇).  Pyroborates are compounds containing the [B₄O₇]²⁻ anion, which is formed by the condensation of borate anions. They are used in various applications, including in glassmaking and as flame retardants.| Example:  The researchers synthesized a novel pyroborate compound with enhanced flame-retardant properties.  研究人员合成了一种具有增强阻燃性能的新型焦硼酸盐化合物。|近义词: borate (a broader term)|反义词: N/A
