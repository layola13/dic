diverges:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒ/|/dʌɪˈvɜːrdʒ/| v.| 偏离；分歧；背离 |To move or extend in different directions from a common point; to differ in opinion, form, or character.|The two roads diverge at the crossroads. 这两条路在十字路口分岔。|The scientists diverge on the best approach to the problem. 科学家们在解决这个问题的最佳方法上存在分歧。|converges, unites|converges, agrees


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The river diverges into two streams.  这条河分成了两条溪流。
Their opinions diverge on this issue.  他们在这个问题上的意见相左。


一般过去时:
The paths diverged at the old oak tree.  小路在古老的橡树下分开了。
The two groups diverged sharply in their views.  这两个群体在观点上截然不同。


现在完成时:
Our paths have diverged significantly since college.  自从大学毕业后，我们的道路就大相径庭了。
Their political views have diverged over the years.  他们的政治观点多年来已经分道扬镖了。


过去完成时:
By that point, their opinions had diverged considerably. 到那时，他们的意见已经大相径庭了。
The two companies had diverged in their business strategies. 这两家公司在商业战略上已经分道扬镖了。


将来时:
The roads will diverge further ahead.  道路将在前方进一步分岔。
Their careers will likely diverge in the future. 他们的职业生涯将来可能会分道扬镖。


虚拟语气:
If the roads had not diverged, we would have met sooner. 如果道路没有分岔，我们会早点相遇。
If their opinions had not diverged, the project might have succeeded. 如果他们的意见没有分歧，这个项目也许会成功。


被动语态:
The roads are diverged by a large rock. 道路被一块巨石分开了。 (很少用被动语态)
The opinions were diverged  by the heated debate. (很少用被动语态) 意见因激烈的辩论而分歧。


疑问句:
Do the roads diverge here?  道路在这里分岔吗？
Do their opinions diverge significantly?  他们的意见差异很大吗？


否定句:
The roads do not diverge until the next town. 道路直到下一个镇才分岔。
Their opinions do not diverge on this matter.  他们在这一问题上意见一致。


PHRASE->

diverge from: 偏离于……
diverge into: 分支成……
diverge widely: 大相径庭
diverge sharply: 截然不同
diverge significantly:  显著不同
