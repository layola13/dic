half-yearly:/ˈhæfˈjɪərli/ | adj. |半年一次的；每半年一次的 |Occurring or done every six months.  |The company holds half-yearly meetings to review its progress. 这家公司每半年举行一次会议以审查其进展。  |biannual, semiannual |annual, daily |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company publishes a half-yearly report. = 公司出版半年报。
This plant blooms half-yearly. =  这种植物半年开花一次。


一般过去时:
They had a half-yearly salary review last month. = 他们上个月进行了半年一次的薪资审查。
The school held a half-yearly exam in June. = 学校在六月举行了期中考试。


现在完成时:
We have had three half-yearly assessments this year. = 今年我们已经进行了三次半年评估。
The project has progressed well since the last half-yearly report. = 自上次半年报以来，项目进展顺利。


过去完成时:
They had already completed the half-yearly targets before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经完成了半年度目标。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
