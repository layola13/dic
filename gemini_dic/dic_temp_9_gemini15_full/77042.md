garlic:/ˈɡɑːrlɪk/| n. |大蒜 |A pungent-smelling bulb used as a flavoring in cooking.  It is a plant in the onion family, with a strong, distinctive odor and flavor. |Example:  I added a clove of garlic to the pasta sauce. 我在意大利面酱里加了一瓣蒜。

Example: The recipe calls for two cloves of garlic, minced.  这个食谱需要两瓣切碎的大蒜。


近义词：none (no perfect synonym, but other pungent vegetables could be considered similar in some dishes)
反义词：none (no direct opposite)
词性: noun
