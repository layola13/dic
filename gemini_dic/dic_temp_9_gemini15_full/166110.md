prepostor: /priːˈpɔːstər/ | n. | 预先摆放者；预先安置者 | A person or thing that precedes and prepares the way for something else.  Someone or something that acts as a precursor or forerunner. |例句：The advance team acted as prepostors, setting up the stage for the main event.  预备队充当先锋，为主要活动做好准备。

例句：The diligent prepostor ensured all the equipment was in place before the experiment began. 勤奋的预先准备者确保实验开始前所有设备都已到位。


近义词：forerunner, precursor, harbinger, pioneer | 反义词：follower, successor
