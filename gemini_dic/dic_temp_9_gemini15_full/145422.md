overcharge:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒ/| vt. n.|过高收费；超额收费 |To charge excessively high prices for goods or services.  |例句：The hotel overcharged me for the room.  这家酒店向我收取了过高的房费。

例句：The mechanic overcharged me for the repair.  这个技工修理费收得太高了。


近义词：extort, gouge, fleece, rip off |反义词：undercharge
