saponify:/ˈsæpənɪfaɪ/| vt.|皂化；使皂化|To convert (a fat or oil) into soap by reaction with an alkali.  This process involves breaking down fats and oils into their constituent fatty acids and glycerol through the action of a base (like lye).|Example:  The process of saponifying fats and oils is crucial in soap making.  皂化脂肪和油的过程对制皂至关重要。|近义词：hydrolyze (in the context of fats and oils)|反义词：N/A|动词
