reinoculated:/ˌriːɪˈnɒkjuleɪtɪd/ | v. | 再接种，再次接种 | To inoculate again; to give a second or further dose of a vaccine.  | Example: The doctor reinoculated the patient against influenza after a year.  医生在一年后再次为病人接种了流感疫苗。|近义词：revaccinate, reimmunize | 反义词：deinoculate (not a common word)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinoculate the herd annually. = 他们每年都会给牲畜重新接种疫苗。
The farmer reinoculates his crops with nitrogen-fixing bacteria. = 农民用固氮细菌再次接种他的庄稼。


一般过去时:
The researchers reinoculated the mice with a stronger strain of the virus. = 研究人员用更强的病毒株再次给老鼠接种了疫苗。
