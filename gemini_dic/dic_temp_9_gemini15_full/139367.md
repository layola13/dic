obscuring: /əbˈskjʊərɪŋ/ | v. | 遮蔽；使模糊；掩盖 | To make something difficult to see, hear, or understand.  |例句：The fog was obscuring the landscape.  浓雾遮蔽了风景。

The heavy rain obscured our view of the mountains. 大雨遮挡了我们对山景的视野。


近义词：concealing, hiding, masking, veiling, shrouding | 反义词：revealing, clarifying, illuminating

词源:
From Old French *obscurer, from Latin obscūrāre "to darken," from obscurus "dark, obscure."  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *sek- "to follow, to see."  The sense development is from "dark" to "hidden" to "difficult to understand."
