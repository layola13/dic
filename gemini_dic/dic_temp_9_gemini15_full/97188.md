inflections:/ɪnˈflɛkʃənz/ | n. | 词形变化；屈折变化 | Inflections are changes in the form of a word (typically a noun, verb, adjective, or adverb) that indicate grammatical function.  These changes often involve the addition of prefixes or suffixes. | Example: The plural inflection "-s" is added to nouns like "cat" to form "cats".  The past tense inflection "-ed" is added to verbs like "walk" to form "walked". | 近义词：bendings, variations, modifications | 反义词：None directly applicable.
