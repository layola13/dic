gists:/dʒɪsts/ | n. |要点，要旨，大意 |The gist of the article is that climate change is a serious threat. 这篇文章的要点是气候变化是一个严重的威胁。|近义词：summary, essence, core, main point |反义词：detail, minutiae

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gist of the meeting is to finalize the project plan. = 会议的要点是敲定项目计划。
What's the gist of his argument? = 他的论点要点是什么？


一般过去时:
The gist of his speech was about economic recovery. = 他演讲的要点是关于经济复苏。
I didn't quite catch the gist of what she said. = 我没完全抓住她所说的要点。


现在完成时:
The gist of the investigation has been summarized in the report. = 调查的要点已在报告中总结。
Have you grasped the gist of the situation yet? = 你已经掌握了局势的要点了吗？


过去完成时:
By the end of the meeting, everyone had understood the gist of the proposal. = 到会议结束时，每个人都理解了提案的要点。
I hadn’t grasped the gist of the instructions before I started the work. = 在我开始工作之前，我没有抓住指示的要点。


将来时:
The gist of the upcoming presentation will be the company's new strategy. = 下次演示的要点将是公司的新的战略。
I think the gist of the next meeting will be about budget cuts. = 我认为下次会议的要点将是关于预算削减。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the gist of the matter, I would be able to help. = 如果我知道事情的要点，我就能帮忙。
I wish I'd understood the gist earlier. = 我希望我早点明白要点。


被动语态:
The gist of the news was widely misinterpreted. = 新闻的要点被广泛误解了。
The gist of the report will be summarized for the board. = 报告的要点将被总结给董事会。


疑问句:
What's the gist of this chapter? = 这一章的要点是什么？
Could you give me the gist of the email? = 你能告诉我邮件的要点吗？


否定句:
I didn't get the gist of the conversation. = 我没抓住谈话的要点。
He failed to convey the gist of his ideas. = 他未能传达他的想法的要点。
