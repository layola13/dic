preformulations:/ˌpriːfɔːrmjuˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |制剂前研究；预制剂；配方前期准备 |The process of evaluating the physical and chemical properties of a drug substance to determine the best formulation approach.  This includes selecting appropriate excipients, determining the best manufacturing process, and assessing stability.  |例句：Preformulations studies are crucial for the successful development of a new drug product. 制剂前研究对于成功开发新药至关重要。

例句：Thorough preformulations are essential to ensure the drug's bioavailability and stability. 彻底的制剂前研究对于确保药物的生物利用度和稳定性至关重要。


近义词：preliminary formulation studies, formulation development, drug development


反义词：post-formulation (although not a direct antonym, it represents the opposite stage in the drug development process)


词性:名词
