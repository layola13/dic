misperformed:/ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrmd/|/ˈmɪspərˈfɔːrmd/| adj. | 表演拙劣的；执行不力的 |  Done or performed badly or incorrectly.  |例句：The play was misperformed due to the actors' lack of rehearsal. 这出戏因为演员排练不足而演出得很糟糕。

The orchestra gave a truly misperformed rendition of Beethoven's Fifth.  乐团对贝多芬第五交响曲的演绎非常糟糕。


近义词：poorly executed, badly done, botched, bungled | 反义词：well-performed, expertly executed, flawlessly done


词源:  The word "misperformed" is formed by adding the prefix "mis-" (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") to the past participle "performed" of the verb "perform".  "Perform" itself comes from Old French *parfournir, from *par- (completely) + fournir (to furnish, supply).
