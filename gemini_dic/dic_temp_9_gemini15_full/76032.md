furiouser:/ˈfjʊəriəsər/| adj.|更愤怒的，更加狂怒的|  A comparative form of "furious," indicating a greater degree of anger or rage. It is generally considered non-standard or informal English, as "more furious" is preferred. |例句：He became furiouser and furiouser as the delay continued. 他随着延误的持续变得越来越愤怒。  He was furiouser than I'd ever seen him. 他比我见过的任何时候都更加愤怒。|近义词：more furious, angrier, more enraged |反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil
