dentate:/ˈdɛnteɪt/| adj.|有齿的，齿状的|Having tooth-like projections or points.  The term is often used to describe the margins of leaves, petals, or other structures.|例句：The leaf margin is dentate, showing clearly defined teeth.  这片叶子的边缘是齿状的，清晰地显示出齿状突起。

The serrated leaf has a more finely toothed edge than the dentate leaf.  锯齿状叶子的边缘比齿状叶子的边缘更细密。


近义词：toothed, serrated, crenate|反义词：entire, smooth, undulate


词源: From Latin *dentātus, past participle of dentāre "to furnish with teeth," from dens, dentis "tooth."
