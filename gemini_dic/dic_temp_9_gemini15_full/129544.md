WORD ORIGIN->
The word is a compound word.  It's formed from "non-" (meaning "not") + "Brahmanic," which is derived from "Brahmin," the priestly caste in Hinduism, ultimately tracing back to the Sanskrit word "Brahman" (ब्रह्मन्), referring to the ultimate reality or cosmic principle in Hinduism.  The suffix "-ic" denotes "relating to" or "characteristic of."
