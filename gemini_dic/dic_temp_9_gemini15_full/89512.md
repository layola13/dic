honing:/ˈhoʊnɪŋ/ | v. | 磨练；改进；加强 | To sharpen or improve something, especially a skill or ability, by continued effort. |He spent years honing his skills as a writer. 他花了数年时间磨练他的写作技巧。 | improving, sharpening, refining | dulling, weakening | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is honing his public speaking skills. = 他正在磨练他的公开演讲技巧。
She hones her skills daily through practice. = 她通过每天练习来磨练自己的技能。

一般过去时:
The blacksmith honed the blade to a razor sharpness. = 铁匠把刀刃磨得锋利无比。
They honed their strategies before the big game. = 他们在大型比赛之前磨练了他们的策略。

现在完成时:
He has honed his skills over many years of practice. = 他多年来通过练习磨练了他的技能。
She has honed her craft to perfection. = 她已经把她的技艺磨练到了完美境界。

过去完成时:
He had honed his argument before presenting it to the judge. = 他在向法官陈述他的论点之前已经磨练好了它。
They had honed their techniques for years before winning the competition. = 他们多年磨练技术后才赢得比赛。

将来时:
She will hone her skills further through advanced training. = 她将通过高级培训进一步磨练她的技能。
He will hone his presentation before the conference. = 他将在会议之前磨练他的演示文稿。

虚拟语气:
If he had honed his skills earlier, he would have succeeded sooner. = 如果他早点磨练技能，他就会早点成功。
I wish I had honed my writing skills more diligently. = 我希望我以前更努力地磨练我的写作技巧。

被动语态:
His argument was honed to perfection. = 他的论点被磨练到了完美境界。
The software is being honed to meet customer needs. = 该软件正在被改进以满足客户需求。

疑问句:
How can I hone my communication skills? = 我该如何磨练我的沟通技巧？
What methods are being used to hone the product design? = 正在使用什么方法来改进产品设计？

否定句:
He did not hone his skills enough to win the competition. = 他没有足够磨练他的技能来赢得比赛。
She didn't hone her presentation skills, and it showed. = 她没有磨练她的演讲技巧，结果显而易见。
