crystal-like: /ˈkrɪstəlˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 水晶般的，晶莹剔透的 | Resembling crystal in appearance; clear, transparent, and often sparkling.  |例句：The ice on the lake was crystal-like in its clarity. 湖上的冰晶莹剔透。The chandelier had crystal-like pendants that shimmered in the light. 吊灯上垂挂着水晶般闪闪发光的吊坠。|近义词：crystalline, transparent, clear, sparkling, glassy |反义词：opaque, cloudy, murky
