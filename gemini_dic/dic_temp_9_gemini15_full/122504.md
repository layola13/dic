mocked:/mɒkt/|ˈmɑːkt/| vt.|嘲笑，模仿，戏弄|To make fun of someone or something by copying their behavior or words in a humorous or insulting way.  It can also mean to ridicule or scorn.|例句：The children mocked the teacher's accent.孩子们嘲笑老师的口音。

例句：He mocked her attempts to sing. 他嘲笑她唱歌的尝试。


近义词：ridicule, deride, jeer, scoff, taunt|反义词：praise, compliment, admire


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mock him for his strange clothes. = 他们因为他奇怪的衣服而嘲笑他。
She mocks his singing voice. = 她嘲笑他的歌声。
