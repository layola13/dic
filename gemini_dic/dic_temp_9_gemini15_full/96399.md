indispensable:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbəl/ | adj. | 不可或缺的，必不可少的 | absolutely necessary; essential |

例句：

Water is indispensable to life.  水是生命所必需的。

His experience is indispensable to the success of the project. 他的经验对项目的成功至关重要。


近义词：essential, crucial, vital, necessary, requisite

反义词：unnecessary, dispensable, optional


词源:

* **in-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **dispens-**: from Latin "dispense" meaning "to distribute, deal out"
* **-able**: suffix meaning "capable of being"


因此，indispensable字面意思是“不能被分配出去的”，引申为“不可或缺的”。
