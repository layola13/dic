celebrated:/ˈsɛləˌbreɪtɪd/| adj.|著名的，知名的，庆祝的 | Widely known and admired; having received or deserving to receive acclaim.  |例句：Shakespeare is a celebrated playwright.莎士比亚是一位著名的剧作家。

例句：The city celebrated its 200th anniversary with a grand parade. 这个城市以盛大的游行庆祝它的200周年纪念。


近义词：famous, renowned, acclaimed, distinguished, noted |反义词：obscure, unknown, infamous
