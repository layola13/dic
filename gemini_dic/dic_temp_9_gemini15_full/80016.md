go-slow:/ˈɡoʊsloʊ/| n.|怠工，减速行驶|A go-slow is a deliberate slowing down of work or traffic as a form of protest.  It is often used by workers to express their dissatisfaction or to put pressure on employers.|例句：The workers staged a go-slow to protest against the low wages. 工人们进行怠工以抗议低工资。  The traffic was slowed down to a go-slow due to the accident. 由于事故，交通速度减慢到怠工的速度。|近义词：slowdown, work-to-rule, strike (in some contexts)|反义词：speed-up, acceleration|名词
