horsiness:/ˈhɔːrsinɪs/ | n. |粗俗，粗鲁；马的气味；像马一样的特性|The quality or state of being like a horse; a characteristic of horses. It can refer to a strong, pungent smell associated with horses, or to a crude, uncouth, or boisterous manner. |例句：The stable had a strong horsiness about it. 马厩里有一股浓重的马粪味。The comedian's act was full of crude horsiness.  这位喜剧演员的表演充满了粗俗的马匹特性。|近义词：coarseness, crudeness, vulgarity, rankness |反义词：refinement, elegance, sophistication
