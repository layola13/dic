double-park:/ˈdʌbəl pɑːrk/| v.|双排停车|To park a vehicle in a way that blocks another vehicle from leaving its parking space, typically by parking in front of a car already parked at the curb.|例句：He double-parked his car in front of the store, causing a traffic jam. 他在商店前面双排停车，造成了交通堵塞。
例句：It's illegal to double-park in this area. 在这个区域双排停车是违法的。|近义词：illegally park, obstruct, block|反义词：properly park
