haggard:/ˈhæɡərd/| adj.|憔悴的，消瘦的，疲惫不堪的|Looking gaunt and exhausted, often because of worry, illness, or fatigue.  The adjective describes a person's appearance, indicating a state of depletion and weariness.|例句：After weeks of sleepless nights, she looked haggard and exhausted.  几周的失眠后，她看起来憔悴不堪。

例句：The long journey left him looking haggard and pale. 漫长的旅程使他看起来憔悴苍白。


近义词：gaunt, emaciated, drawn, weary, exhausted, depleted

反义词：robust, healthy, well-rested, vigorous, blooming


词性:形容词
