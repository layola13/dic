fleets:/fliːts/| n.|舰队；车队；一群|A fleet is a group of ships or vehicles traveling together or owned by the same person or organization. It can also refer to a large number of things moving together quickly.|例句：The naval fleet sailed into the harbor.  海军舰队驶入港口。|例句：A fleet of taxis waited outside the airport. 一队出租车在机场外等候。|近义词：squadron, armada, convoy|反义词:single ship, individual vehicle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company owns a large fleet of trucks. = 这家公司拥有大量的卡车车队。
Many birds fly in fleets across the sky. = 许多鸟成群结队地飞过天空。


一般过去时:
The Roman fleet defeated the Carthaginian fleet. = 罗马舰队击败了迦太基舰队。
A fleet of cars sped past us. = 一队汽车飞快地从我们身边驶过。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
