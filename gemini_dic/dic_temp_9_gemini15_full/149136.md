oxidizability: /ˌɒksɪdaɪzəˈbɪləti/ /ˌɑːksɪdaɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可氧化性 | The ability of a substance to be oxidized.  It refers to how easily a substance loses electrons or reacts with an oxidizing agent.  The higher the oxidizability, the more readily the substance undergoes oxidation. | Example: The high oxidizability of iron contributes to its susceptibility to rust.  铁的高氧化性导致其易生锈。  The oxidizability of this material needs to be tested before use in this application.  这种材料的氧化性需要在应用前进行测试。 | 近义词：oxidative capacity, susceptibility to oxidation | 反义词：resistance to oxidation, stability
