preapproving: /ˌpriːəˈpruːvɪŋ/ | v. |预先批准；预先审核|  Giving approval beforehand; granting authorization in advance.  |例句：The bank preapproved her mortgage application. 银行预先批准了她的抵押贷款申请。

例句：The committee preapproved the budget for the project. 委员会预先批准了该项目的预算。


近义词：approving in advance, preauthorizing


反义词：disapproving, rejecting


词性：动词
