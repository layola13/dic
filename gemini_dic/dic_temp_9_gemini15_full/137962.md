nonwool: /ˈnɒnwʊl/ | adj. |非羊毛的；人造羊毛的 | Not made of wool; synthetic fabric resembling wool.  | 例句：This sweater is nonwool, so it's machine washable. 这件毛衣是非羊毛的，所以可以用洗衣机洗。  | 近义词：synthetic, artificial, man-made | 反义词：woolen, wool


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations.  It modifies nouns.
