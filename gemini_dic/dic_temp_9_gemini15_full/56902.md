dot:/dɒt/ /dɑːt/| n. |点；小点；句点|A dot is a small, round mark.  It can be a punctuation mark at the end of a sentence, or a small spot of color or ink.|例句：He put a dot on the map to mark the location. 他在地图上标了一个点来标记位置。

例句：There's a tiny dot of blood on his shirt.  他的衬衫上有一小滴血。|近义词：point, speck, spot |反义词：dash, line
