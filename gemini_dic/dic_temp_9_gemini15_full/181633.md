relaxed:/rɪˈlækst/| adj.|放松的，轻松的|Feeling or appearing calm and free from tension.|例句：She felt relaxed after her yoga class. 她瑜伽课后感觉很放松。|例句：The atmosphere in the room was relaxed and informal. 房间里的气氛轻松随意。|近义词：calm, tranquil, at ease|反义词：tense, stressed, anxious|
