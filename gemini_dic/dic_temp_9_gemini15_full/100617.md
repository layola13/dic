intimidates: /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪts/ | v. | 恐吓；威胁；使胆怯 | To frighten or overawe (someone), especially in order to make them do what one wants. |  例句：His aggressive behavior intimidates many people.  他的侵略性行为吓倒了许多人。

例句：The dark alley intimidated her, so she hurried through it. 那条黑暗的小巷让她害怕，所以她赶紧跑了过去。


近义词：frighten, scare, threaten, bully, daunt | 反义词：encourage, embolden, reassure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intimidates his classmates. = 他恐吓他的同学们。
The large dog intimidates small children. = 那条大狗吓唬小孩子。


一般过去时:
The bully intimidated the smaller boy. = 那个恶霸恐吓那个较小的男孩。
The loud noise intimidated the horse. = 巨大的噪音吓到了马。


现在完成时:
He has intimidated witnesses before. = 他以前恐吓过证人。
Her harsh words have intimidated me. = 她严厉的话语让我害怕。


过去完成时:
She had intimidated him into silence. = 她已经把他吓唬得不敢说话了。
The rumors had intimidated the investors. = 谣言吓倒了投资者们。


将来时:
I think he will intimidate the other players. = 我认为他会吓唬其他队员。
The storm will intimidate even the bravest sailors. = 即使是最勇敢的水手，暴风雨也会吓倒他们。


虚拟语气:
If he intimidated me, I would tell him to stop. = 如果他吓唬我，我会告诉他停止。
I wish he wouldn't intimidate his colleagues. = 我希望他不吓唬他的同事。


被动语态:
Many people are intimidated by his reputation. = 许多人被他名声吓倒。
The witnesses were intimidated into giving false testimony. = 证人们被恐吓作伪证。


疑问句:
Does his size intimidate you? = 他的体型吓到你了吗？
Does the task intimidate you? = 这项任务吓倒你了吗？


否定句:
He doesn't intimidate me. = 他吓不倒我。
The situation didn't intimidate them. = 这种情况并没有吓倒他们。
