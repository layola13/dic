periagua: /ˌpɛriˈɑːɡwɑː/ | n. |独木舟；轻舟 | A type of dugout canoe, typically made from a single tree trunk, used in the Caribbean and other parts of the Americas.  It is often narrow and relatively shallow. | 例句：The fishermen used a periagua to navigate the shallow coastal waters.  渔民们用独木舟在浅海沿岸航行。

The indigenous people crafted their periaguas with remarkable skill. 土著居民以非凡的技艺制作他们的独木舟。| 近义词：canoe, dugout, pirogue | 反义词：large ship, ocean liner | 词性：名词
