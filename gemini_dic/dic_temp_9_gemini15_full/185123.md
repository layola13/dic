/ˈreɪtɪŋər/ | n. | 保持器；保持装置；(牙齿)托槽；(尤指旧式的) 保持器 | A retainer is a device used to hold something in place or to maintain a certain position or condition.  It can refer to a device in various contexts, such as a dental retainer or a mechanical retainer. | Example:  He wore a retainer to keep his teeth straight after his braces were removed. 他在摘掉牙套后戴着保持器以保持牙齿整齐。|近义词：holder, support, brace |反义词：remover, dislodger |名词
