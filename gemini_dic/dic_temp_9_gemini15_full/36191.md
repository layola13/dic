cofounding: /koʊˈfaʊndɪŋ/ | v. | 共同创立；共同创办 |  The act of establishing or starting something, typically a business or organization, together with others.  It emphasizes joint effort and shared responsibility in the creation. | Example:  They cofounded a successful tech startup.  他们共同创办了一家成功的科技初创公司。 | 近义词:  founding, establishing, initiating | 反义词:  dissolving, dismantling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cofound a new company every year. = 他们每年共同创办一家新公司。
We are cofounding a non-profit organization. = 我们正在共同创办一个非营利组织。

一般过去时:
They cofounded the company in 2010. = 他们在2010年共同创办了这家公司。
We cofounded a charity last year. = 我们去年共同创办了一家慈善机构。

现在完成时:
They have cofounded several successful businesses. = 他们已经共同创办了几家成功的企业。
We have cofounded a school for underprivileged children. = 我们已经共同创办了一所为贫困儿童服务的学校。

过去完成时:
They had cofounded three companies before they started this one. = 在他们创办这家公司之前，他们已经共同创办了三家公司。
We had cofounded a research institute before embarking on this project. = 在着手这个项目之前，我们已经共同创办了一个研究所。

将来时:
They will cofound a new research lab next year. = 他们明年将共同创办一个新的研究实验室。
We will cofound a foundation to support the arts. = 我们将共同创办一个基金会来支持艺术事业。

虚拟语气:
If they had cofounded the company earlier, they would be richer now. = 如果他们早些共同创办这家公司，现在就会更富有。
If we cofounded that company, we might be very successful now. = 如果我们共同创办那家公司，现在可能会非常成功。

被动语态:
The company was cofounded by two brilliant minds. = 这家公司是由两位杰出的人才共同创办的。
The project was cofounded by several different organizations. = 这个项目是由几个不同的组织共同资助创办的。

疑问句:
Who cofounded that famous tech company? = 谁共同创办了那家著名的科技公司？
Have they cofounded any other successful ventures? = 他们还共同创办过其他成功的企业吗？

否定句:
They did not cofound the company; they only invested in it. = 他们并没有共同创办这家公司；他们只是投资了它。
We did not cofound the organization; we were simply advisors. = 我们没有共同创办这个组织；我们只是顾问。


PHRASE->
cofound a business = 共同创办企业
cofound a startup = 共同创办初创公司
cofound an organization = 共同创办一个组织
cofound a project = 共同发起一个项目
cofounders of a company = 公司的共同创始人
