jeofail:/ˈdʒiːoʊfeɪl/| n.|不追究；放弃控告|A failure to prosecute or pursue a legal action.  It often refers to a situation where a legal action is dropped or abandoned due to technicalities or lack of evidence.|Example: The case ended in a jeofail due to procedural errors.  这个案子因为程序错误而不了了之。|近义词：nolle prosequi, abandonment, dismissal|反义词：prosecution, pursuit|名词
