despise:/dɪˈspaɪz/| vt.|轻视；鄙视；厌恶|To regard with contempt; scorn; loathe.  |例句：I despise his arrogance. 我鄙视他的傲慢。

例句：Many people despise dishonesty. 许多人鄙视不诚实。


近义词：scorn, contemn, scorn, loathe, abhor


反义词：admire, respect, esteem


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He despises liars. = 他鄙视说谎者。
She despises injustice. = 她厌恶不公正。


一般过去时:
They despised his behavior. = 他们鄙视他的行为。
I despised the way he treated her. = 我鄙视他对待她的方式。


现在完成时:
I have despised his actions for years. = 多年来我一直鄙视他的行为。
She has despised hypocrisy all her life. = 她一生都鄙视虚伪。


过去完成时:
He had despised her for her betrayal. = 他因为她的背叛而一直鄙视她。
We had despised their cruelty since childhood. = 从小我们就鄙视他们的残酷。


将来时:
I will always despise such behavior. = 我将永远鄙视这种行为。
They will despise him for his cowardice. = 他们会因为他的懦弱而鄙视他。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would despise such actions. = 如果我是你，我会鄙视这种行为。
I wish I didn't despise him so much. = 我希望我不那么鄙视他。


被动语态:
His actions were despised by many. = 他的行为被许多人鄙视。
His ideas are despised by the establishment. = 他的想法被当权者所鄙视。


疑问句:
Do you despise him? = 你鄙视他吗？
Does she despise his methods? = 她鄙视他的方法吗？


否定句:
I do not despise everyone who disagrees with me. = 我并不鄙视所有不同意我的人。
She doesn't despise all forms of authority. = 她并不鄙视所有形式的权威。


PHRASE->
despise oneself = 自卑
despise the world = 厌世
despise the enemy = 痛恨敌人
to be despised = 受人鄙视
despise one's own weakness = 鄙视自身的弱点


词源:
Old French *despiser (from Latin despicere "to look down on," from de- "down" + specere "to look").
