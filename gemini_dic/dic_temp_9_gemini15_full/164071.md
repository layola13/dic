prediscourse: /ˈpriːdɪskɔːrs/ | n. |预论述；前论述；前言 | Discourse that precedes a main discussion or text, often setting the stage or providing background information.  It may include introductory remarks, summaries of previous work, or a statement of the problem being addressed. |  The prediscourse outlined the historical context of the debate.  预论述概述了这场辩论的历史背景。|  prologue, introduction, preface, preamble |  conclusion, postscript | 名词
