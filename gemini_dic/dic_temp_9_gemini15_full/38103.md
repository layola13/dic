compoundedness:/kəmˈpaʊndɪdnəs/ | n. | 复合性，复杂性 | The state or quality of being compounded; complexity; intricacy.  It refers to the nature of something being made up of multiple, often interwoven, parts or elements.  This can apply to various contexts, from chemical compounds to complex situations or emotions. | Example: The compoundedness of the problem made it difficult to find a simple solution.  = 问题的复杂性使得难以找到简单的解决方案。 The compoundedness of her grief was evident in her tearful eyes. = 她悲伤的复杂性在她泪汪汪的眼睛里显而易见。 | 近义词：complexity, intricacy, complication | 反义词：simplicity, straightforwardness
