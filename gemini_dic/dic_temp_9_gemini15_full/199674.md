sinusoidal:/ˌsaɪnəˈsɔɪdəl/| adj. |正弦曲线的；正弦波的|Of or relating to a sine curve or wave; having the form of a sine curve.  A sinusoidal function is a mathematical function that describes a smooth, repetitive oscillation.  It's characterized by its smooth, wave-like pattern, which is crucial in various fields like physics, engineering, and signal processing.|例句：The sound wave produced by a tuning fork is approximately sinusoidal.  音叉产生的声波近似于正弦波。

The alternating current in our homes is sinusoidal. 我们家里的交流电是正弦波的。


近义词：wavelike, undulating, oscillatory|反义词：linear, constant, rectilinear
