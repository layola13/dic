precautioning: /prɪˈkɔːʃənɪŋ/ | v. | 预防；采取预防措施 | The act of taking precautions; the process of making something safe.  |例句：The company is precautioning against future economic downturns by diversifying its investments. 公司正在通过多元化投资来预防未来的经济衰退。

例句：Precautioning against fire hazards is crucial for home safety. 预防火灾隐患对家庭安全至关重要。


近义词：guarding against, preventing, safeguarding, protecting


反义词：risking, endangering, jeopardizing


词性：动词
