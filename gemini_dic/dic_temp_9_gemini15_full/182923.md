repetitively:/rɪˈpɛtɪtɪvli/| adv. |重复地，反复地|Repeatedly; in a repetitive manner.  The word emphasizes the act of doing something again and again, often implying boredom or monotony.|例句：He repetitively tapped his fingers on the table. 他反复地敲打着桌子。|例句：She repetitively practiced the piano piece until she mastered it. 她反复练习这首钢琴曲，直到掌握为止。|近义词：repeatedly, again and again, over and over, incessantly|反义词：uniquely, once, sporadically|副词
