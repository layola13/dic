dissipate:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪt/| v.|驱散，消散，浪费 |to cause to spread thinly or vanish; to spend or use wastefully|The sun dissipated the morning mist.  阳光驱散了早晨的薄雾。|The crowd quickly dissipated after the concert ended. 音乐会结束后，人群迅速散去。|近义词： disperse, scatter, squander |反义词：concentrate, gather, save


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dissipates his energy on trivial matters. = 他把精力浪费在琐事上。
The fog dissipates slowly in the morning sun. = 薄雾在早晨的阳光下慢慢消散。


一般过去时:
The storm dissipated overnight. = 暴风雨一夜之间消散了。
He dissipated his inheritance in a few years. = 他几年内就挥霍了他的遗产。


现在完成时:
The tension has dissipated since the agreement was reached. = 达成协议后，紧张气氛已经消散了。
He has dissipated most of his savings on gambling. = 他大部分积蓄都花在了赌博上。


过去完成时:
The anger had dissipated by the time he arrived. = 等他到达的时候，怒气已经消散了。
She had dissipated her fortune before she turned thirty. = 她30岁之前就挥霍掉了自己的财产。


将来时:
The clouds will dissipate by afternoon. = 云彩到下午就会消散。
He will dissipate his fortune if he continues to live this way. = 如果他继续这样生活，他会挥霍掉他的财产。


虚拟语气:
If the clouds dissipated, we could see the stars. = 如果云散了，我们就能看到星星了。
If he had not dissipated his money, he would be rich now. = 如果他没有挥霍他的钱，现在就会很富有。


被动语态:
The mist was dissipated by the rising sun. = 薄雾被升起的太阳驱散了。
His savings were dissipated through reckless spending. = 他的积蓄由于挥霍无度而被花光了。


疑问句:
Will the storm dissipate soon? = 暴风雨很快就会消散吗？

否定句:
The fog did not dissipate easily. = 薄雾不容易消散。
He did not dissipate his inheritance; he invested it wisely. = 他没有挥霍他的遗产；他明智地投资了。
