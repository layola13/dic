decaled:/dɪˈkeɪld/ | adj. |  脱落的；剥落的；已褪色的 |  Describes a surface that has had its top layer removed, often through wear or the removal of a decal.  This usually implies a loss of original appearance or finish. | Example: The decaled paint on the old car showed years of sun exposure.  这辆旧车的油漆脱落了，显示出多年的日晒痕迹。| 近义词: peeled, chipped, faded, worn | 反义词: intact, pristine, new
