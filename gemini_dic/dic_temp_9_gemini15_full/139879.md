ocupate:/ˈɒkjupeɪt/ | v. | 占据，占用；使忙碌 | To take control of a place or to fill or use a space or time.  | 例句：The army occupied the city.  军队占领了这座城市。  | 例句：The meeting occupied the whole afternoon.  会议占据了整个下午。 | 近义词：occupy, seize, engage | 反义词：vacate, abandon, leave

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army occupies the city. = 军队占领了这座城市。
This task occupies my entire day. = 这个任务占据了我一天的时间。

一般过去时:
The soldiers occupied the building. = 士兵们占领了那座建筑。
She occupied herself with knitting. = 她忙着织毛衣。

现在完成时:
The rebels have occupied the capital. = 反叛军已经占领了首都。
He has occupied himself with his hobbies all week. = 他整个星期都忙于他的爱好。

过去完成时:
They had occupied the island before the enemy arrived. = 他们已经在敌人到达之前占领了该岛。
She had occupied herself with reading before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她一直在看书。

将来时:
The troops will occupy the strategic position. = 部队将占据战略要地。
I will occupy myself with writing this report. = 我将忙于撰写这份报告。

虚拟语气:
If they occupied the hill, they would control the valley. = 如果他们占据了山丘，他们就能控制山谷。
I wish I could occupy my time more productively. = 我希望我能更有效地利用我的时间。

被动语态:
The city was occupied by the enemy. = 这座城市被敌人占领了。
His time was occupied by endless meetings. = 他的时间被没完没了的会议占据了。

疑问句:
Does this project occupy much of your time? = 这个项目占用你很多时间吗？
What occupies your thoughts these days? = 这些天你都在想些什么？

否定句:
The invaders did not occupy the entire country. = 入侵者并没有占领整个国家。
The work didn't occupy all of his time. = 这项工作并没有占据他全部的时间。


PHRASE->
occupy a position = 担任职位

occupy one's mind = 占据某人的心思
occupy a seat = 占据一个座位
