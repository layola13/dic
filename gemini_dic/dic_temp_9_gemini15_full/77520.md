gazumps:/ɡəˈzʌmps/| v.| 抢购（尤指房产）|To outbid someone, especially in buying property.| 例句：They gazumped us on the house we'd offered on.  他们抢在我们前面买下了我们看中的房子。|近义词：undercut, outbid |反义词：None specifically.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gazumps every property he sees. = 他看中的每处房产都试图抢购。
They gazump potential buyers regularly. = 他们经常抢购潜在买家的房产。


一般过去时:
We were gazumped on that amazing apartment. = 那套很棒的公寓被别人抢购了。
The seller gazumped the highest bidder. = 卖家抢购了出价最高的竞标者。


现在完成时:
They have gazumped three buyers this month. = 这个月他们已经抢购了三个买家的房产。
We've been gazumped twice already. = 我们已经被抢购了两次了。


过去完成时:
The couple had been gazumped before, so they were extra cautious this time. = 这对夫妇之前被抢购过，所以这次格外谨慎。
They had gazumped several other offers before securing the property. = 在买到这处房产之前，他们已经抢购了好几个其他的出价。


将来时:
I fear they will gazump us again. = 我担心他们会再次抢购我们。
She thinks they will gazump the competition. = 她认为他们会抢购竞争对手。

I wish we hadn't been gazumped. = 我真希望我们没有被抢购。


被动语态:
