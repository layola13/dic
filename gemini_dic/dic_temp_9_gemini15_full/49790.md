demonize:/ˈdiːmənaɪz/| vt.|妖魔化；丑化；将…描绘成恶魔|To portray (someone or something) as evil, wicked, or demonic.|The media often demonizes politicians to gain viewership.媒体经常妖魔化政治家以吸引观众。|近义词：vilify, malign, slander|反义词：glorify, praise, idealize
