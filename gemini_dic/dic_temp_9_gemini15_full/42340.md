counteracting: /ˈkaʊntəræktɪŋ/ | v. | 对抗；抵消；中和 | Acting against something; opposing or preventing the effects of something.  | The medication is designed to counteract the effects of the poison. 这药物旨在对抗毒药的作用。 |近义词：opposing, neutralizing, counterbalancing, offsetting | 反义词：reinforcing, supporting, augmenting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are counteracting the negative effects of climate change. 他们在对抗气候变化的负面影响。
The government is counteracting inflation with new economic policies. 政府正在通过新的经济政策来对抗通货膨胀。


一般过去时:
The team counteracted the opponent's strategy with a clever maneuver.  团队用巧妙的策略对抗了对手的策略。
She counteracted his anger with calm reassurance. 她以平静的保证来对抗他的愤怒。


现在完成时:
Scientists have counteracted many diseases through vaccination. 科学家们通过疫苗接种对抗了许多疾病。
The company has counteracted the negative publicity with a strong PR campaign. 公司通过强有力的公关活动来对抗负面宣传。


过去完成时:
He had counteracted the initial blow by bracing himself. 他通过保护自己来对抗了最初的打击。
They had counteracted the spread of rumors with factual information. 他们用事实信息来对抗谣言的传播。


将来时:
We will counteract the problem by implementing a new system. 我们将通过实施新系统来解决这个问题。
The company will counteract the competitor's new product with an improved version. 公司将通过改进型产品来对抗竞争对手的新产品。


虚拟语气:
If we acted quickly, we could counteract the damage. 如果我们迅速行动，我们就能减轻损失。
I wish we had counteracted the problem sooner. 我希望我们早点解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The effects of the drug were counteracted by an antidote.  药物的作用被解毒剂抵消了。
The negative impact was counteracted by positive developments. 负面影响被积极发展所抵消。


疑问句:
How can we counteract the spread of misinformation? 我们如何才能对抗虚假信息的传播？
What measures are being taken to counteract the effects of pollution? 正在采取什么措施来对抗污染的影响？


否定句:
They did not counteract the effects of the storm. 他们没有对抗暴风雨的影响。
We failed to counteract the negative trends in the market. 我们未能对抗市场上的负面趋势。


PHRASE->
counteract the effects of  对抗…的影响
counteract the impact of 对抗…的影响
counteract the spread of  对抗…的蔓延
counteract a trend 逆转趋势
counteract each other 相互抵消
