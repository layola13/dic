reelect:/ˌriːɪˈlɛkt/| vt.|再次选举，连任|To elect someone to the same office again.|The mayor was reelected by a landslide.|近义词：re-choose, reinstate|反义词：unseat, oust

They reelect the president every four years. = 他们每四年重新选举总统。
