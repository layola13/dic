英文解析:  The word "dumper's" is primarily an adjective describing something related to a dumper (a person who dumps something or someone).  In a more informal context, particularly in relationship discussions, it's used as a slang term to refer to someone who ends a romantic relationship.  As a noun, it can refer to someone who disposes of waste or someone who ends relationships.
