frisette:/friˈsɛt/| n.|卷发；发卷；小卷发|A frisette is a small, tight curl of hair, often created using curling irons or other styling tools.  It can refer to a hairstyle characterized by many such curls. |例句：She styled her hair in a cascade of frisettes. 她把头发做成了层层叠叠的小卷发。|例句：The hairdresser carefully shaped each frisette. 理发师仔细地梳理每一个小卷发。|近义词：curl, ringlet, wave|反义词：straight hair
