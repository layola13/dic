outmarches:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːrtʃɪz/ | v. |超过（在行军速度上）; 胜过 |To march farther or faster than; to surpass in a march or progress.  |例句：The experienced troop outmarched the novices, reaching the summit first.  经验丰富的部队比新手行军速度更快，率先到达山顶。

例句：The ambitious company outmarched its competitors in innovation, securing a dominant market share. 这家雄心勃勃的公司在创新方面超越了竞争对手，获得了主导的市场份额。


近义词：outstrip, outpace, exceed, surpass | 反义词：lag behind, fall short of
