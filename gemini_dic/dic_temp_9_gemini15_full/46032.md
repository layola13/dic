cyborg:/ˈsaɪbɔːrɡ/|/ˈsaɪbɔːrɡ/| n. |半机械人，人机结合体 |A cyborg is a being with both organic and artificial (usually electronic) parts.  Cyborgs are often depicted in science fiction as humans who have integrated technology into their bodies to enhance their abilities. |Example:  The movie featured a cyborg with advanced weaponry integrated into its arm.  这部电影以一个手臂上安装了先进武器的半机械人为主角。|近义词：bionic human, bio-android |反义词：human, natural being
