orchil: /ˈɔːrtʃəl/ | n. |胭脂红染料；紫红色地衣| A purple or red dye obtained from certain lichens, particularly those of the genus *Roccella*.  It was historically important as a source of dye for textiles and other materials.  The color produced varies depending on the lichen species and the processing method. | The ancient Egyptians used orchil to dye fabrics.  |近义词：dye, pigment |反义词：无
