rheumatologist:/ˌruːməˈtɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |风湿病学家 | A rheumatologist is a medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of rheumatic diseases. These are conditions that affect the joints, muscles, and bones.  Rheumatologists often deal with conditions like arthritis, lupus, and fibromyalgia. | Example:  My aunt saw a rheumatologist to get her arthritis treated. = 我的阿姨去看风湿病学家治疗她的关节炎。 She consulted a rheumatologist about her persistent joint pain. = 她就持续的关节疼痛咨询了一位风湿病学家。|近义词：physician (general), specialist (general) |反义词：无
