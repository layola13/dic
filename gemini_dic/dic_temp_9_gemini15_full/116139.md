marzipanned:/ˈmɑːzɪpænd/| adj.|杏仁糖衣的，用杏仁糖装饰的|Covered or decorated with marzipan.  Marzipan is a sweet confection made from almonds and sugar.|例句：The cake was beautifully marzipanned, with intricate floral designs.  那块蛋糕装饰得很漂亮，上面有复杂的鲜花图案。

The marzipanned fruit looked almost too good to eat.  那些杏仁糖衣的水果看起来几乎好得让人不忍心吃。


近义词：almond-coated, sugar-coated|反义词：unadorned, undecorated|词性：形容词
