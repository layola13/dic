males:/meɪlz/| n.|男性，雄性|The word "males" refers to adult men or, more broadly, to the male sex of any species.  It is the plural form of "male".|例句：The males in this species are larger than the females.  这个物种的雄性比雌性大。

例句：There were more males than females at the conference.  会议上男性比女性多。


近义词：men, boys (depending on context), males, menfolk


反义词：females, women


词性:名词
