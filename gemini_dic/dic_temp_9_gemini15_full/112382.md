loss:/lɒs/ /lɔːs/| n.|损失，丧失，失败|The noun "loss" refers to the fact or process of losing something or someone.  It can refer to material possessions, opportunities, loved ones, or abstract things like hope or confidence. |例句：The company reported a significant loss this quarter.  这家公司本季度报告了巨额亏损。

例句：The loss of his job deeply affected him. 他失去工作的事深深地影响了他。


近义词：damage, detriment, defeat, bereavement, deprivation|反义词：gain, profit, success, acquisition|词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team suffers a loss every game. = 这个队每场比赛都输。
His business experiences a continuous loss. = 他的生意持续亏损。


一般过去时:
We experienced a huge loss in the stock market. = 我们在股市遭受了巨大损失。
She felt a great loss after her grandmother passed away. = 奶奶去世后，她感到非常悲伤。


现在完成时:
They have suffered many losses this year. = 他们今年遭受了很多损失。
I have experienced a loss of hearing in my left ear. = 我的左耳听力下降了。


过去完成时:
By the end of the war, they had sustained heavy losses. = 到战争结束时，他们已经遭受了重大损失。
He had already suffered a significant loss before he invested in that company. = 在他投资那家公司之前就已经遭受了重大损失。


将来时:
We will likely experience a loss in revenue next month. = 我们下个月可能会出现收入损失。
The company projects a loss in sales for the next quarter. = 公司预计下一季度的销售额将出现亏损。


虚拟语气:
If we hadn't made that mistake, we wouldn't have suffered such a great loss. = 如果我们没有犯那个错误，就不会遭受这么大的损失。
I wish I hadn't experienced the loss of my pet. = 我真希望我没有失去我的宠物。


被动语态:
Significant losses were reported by several companies. = 几家公司报告了重大损失。
The loss of the election was felt by many supporters. = 许多支持者都感受到了选举失利的打击。


疑问句:
What was the total loss from the fire? = 火灾造成的总损失是多少？
Did you experience any loss during the storm? = 你在暴风雨中遭受了任何损失吗？


否定句:
The company did not report a loss this year. = 公司今年没有报告亏损。
They didn't experience any loss of power during the hurricane. = 飓风期间他们没有断电。


PHRASE->
at a loss:不知所措  例句：I am at a loss to explain this phenomenon. 我无法解释这种现象。
loss leader:亏本出售商品 例句：The supermarket uses loss leaders to attract customers. 超市用亏本商品来吸引顾客。
loss of appetite:食欲不振 例句：He experienced a loss of appetite due to illness. 他因病食欲不振。
loss prevention:损失预防 例句：The company implemented a loss prevention program. 公司实施了一个损失预防计划。
a sense of loss:失落感 例句：The breakup left her with a deep sense of loss. 分手让她感到深深的失落。
