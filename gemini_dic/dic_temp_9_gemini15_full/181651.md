relearned:/ˌriːˈlɜːrnd/| v. |重新学习；再学习|To learn something again, often after forgetting it or having learned it imperfectly before.  It implies a more thorough or successful learning process than a simple review.|例句：I relearned the piano after a twenty-year break. = 我在二十年后重新学习了钢琴。

例句：She relearned the material for the exam, focusing on the areas where she struggled before. = 她为了考试重新学习了材料，重点放在之前她挣扎的领域。


近义词：revise, review, restudy, brush up on|反义词：forget, unlearn|动词
