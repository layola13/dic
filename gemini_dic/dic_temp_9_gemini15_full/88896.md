hokums:/ˈhoʊkəm/| n. |空话；废话；胡说八道 |Nonsense; empty talk; meaningless or insincere talk.|His speech was full of hokum.  他的演讲全是废话。|近义词：nonsense, balderdash, twaddle, drivel|反义词：truth, fact, sense


USAGE->
This word is a noun, and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What".  Therefore, typical verb tense examples are not applicable.  Here are examples demonstrating its use in different sentence structures:


肯定句:
That's just a lot of hokum. = 那只是一堆废话。
His promises turned out to be pure hokum. = 他的承诺结果全是空话。


否定句:
That wasn't hokum; it was sound advice. = 那不是废话；那是合理的建议。
The report contained no hokum, only facts. = 这份报告没有废话，只有事实。


疑问句:
Is all this just hokum? = 这一切都是废话吗？
Was that speech nothing but hokum? = 那次演讲除了废话还有什么？
