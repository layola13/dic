recontend:/ˌriːkənˈtɛnd/| vt.|再次竞争；重新争夺|To contend again; to renew a contest or struggle.|例句：The two teams will recontend for the championship next year.  明年两队将再次争夺冠军。


例句：After losing the first match, they are determined to recontend for the title.  在输掉第一场比赛后，他们决心再次争夺冠军。
