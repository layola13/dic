nutritionists:/nuːˈtrɪʃənɪsts/| n. |营养学家| Experts who are trained in the science of nutrition and provide advice on healthy eating habits and dietary needs.  They help individuals and groups understand the relationship between food and health. |Many nutritionists recommend a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables.  许多营养学家建议均衡饮食，多吃水果和蔬菜。|  dieticians, dietologists |  (反义词无直接对应词，可根据语境用缺乏营养知识的人等表达) |名词
