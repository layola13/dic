rubdown:/ˈrʌbdaʊn/| n. v.|擦身，按摩|A rubdown is the act of rubbing someone's body, often to relax muscles or to treat an injury. It can also refer to the vigorous rubbing of something to clean it.|例句：He gave himself a vigorous rubdown after his workout. 他锻炼后给自己好好擦了擦身。|例句：The horse received a rubdown after the race. 赛马结束后得到了擦拭。|近义词：massage, scrubbing, polishing|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to the opposite of relaxing, like stressing or tightening.|名词,动词
