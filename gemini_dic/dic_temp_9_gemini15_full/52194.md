diary:/ˈdaɪəri/| n.|日记；日记簿|A diary is a book in which a person records their daily activities, thoughts, and feelings.  It can be a private record kept for personal reflection or a more public account intended for others to read.|例句：I keep a diary to record my daily experiences. = 我写日记来记录我的日常生活经历。

例句：She wrote in her diary every night before bed. = 她每天晚上睡觉前都会写日记。

近义词：journal, logbook|反义词：None|词性：名词
