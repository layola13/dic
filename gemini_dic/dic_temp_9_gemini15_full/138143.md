northermost: /ˈnɔːrðərməʊst/ | adj. | 最北的 |  The furthest north; situated at the northernmost point.  This adjective describes the location that is farthest north in a particular region or area. It is formed by combining "north," "er" (comparative suffix), and "most" (superlative suffix).|


例句：

The northernmost point of the island is a dramatic cliff.  = 岛屿的最北端是一个壮观的悬崖。

Alaska is the northernmost state in the United States. = 阿拉斯加州是美国最北部的州。


近义词：farthest north, most northerly


反义词：southernmost, southermost


词性：形容词
