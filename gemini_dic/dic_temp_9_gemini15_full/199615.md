sinklike:/ˈsɪŋklaɪk/| adj.|像水槽的，像水池的|Resembling a sink; having the characteristics of a sink.  The word is not widely used and is often considered informal or nonce.  It describes something that looks or functions like a sink.|The new basin has a sinklike appearance. = 新的水槽看起来像个水池。The hole in the ground had a sinklike quality, collecting rainwater. = 地上的洞像个水池，收集雨水。|近义词：basin-like, sink-shaped|反义词：raised, elevated
