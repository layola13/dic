plate-glass:/ˈpleɪtˌɡlæs/| n. |平板玻璃|  A type of glass made by pouring molten glass onto a flat surface and allowing it to cool slowly, resulting in a smooth, flat sheet. It is typically thicker and more resistant to breakage than other types of glass.  |The skyscraper's facade was made of gleaming plate-glass.  摩天大楼的外墙是用闪亮的平板玻璃制成的。|sheet glass, float glass|  (反义词缺乏直接对应，可考虑用"rough glass"或"textured glass"来表示与其相对的粗糙或有纹理的玻璃)|名词
