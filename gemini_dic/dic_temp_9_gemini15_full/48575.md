defeasanced:/dɪˈfiːzənst/| adj.|已解除抵押的；已解除债务的 |  Having had a defeasance executed; freed from obligation.  The term typically refers to a financial transaction where a borrower prepays a loan and releases the underlying collateral from the lender's claim.  |例句：The mortgage was defeasanced after the borrower made a lump-sum payment. =借款人一次性付款后，抵押贷款已解除。The bond was defeasanced, freeing up the underlying assets. =债券已解除抵押，释放了相关资产。|近义词：released, discharged, liquidated |反义词：encumbered, burdened
