crunches:/krʌntʃɪz/| n. v.| 嘎吱声；（肌肉）锻炼；（尤指经济上的）困境 |  A crunching sound; an exercise for strengthening abdominal muscles; a difficult or unpleasant situation, especially a financial one. |例句：The snow crunched underfoot.  雪在脚下嘎吱作响。|例句：I do crunches every morning to strengthen my abs. 我每天早上做仰卧起坐来锻炼腹肌。|近义词：crunch (n. & v.), crunch time, strain, struggle |反义词：ease, comfort, prosperity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The leaves crunch under my feet. = 叶子在我的脚下嘎吱作响。
He crunches numbers all day. = 他整天都在计算数字。

一般过去时:
The tires crunched on the gravel. = 轮胎在碎石路上嘎吱作响。
She crunched the data to find the answer. = 她仔细分析数据以找到答案。

现在完成时:
The economy has been crunching for months. = 经济已经萎靡了几个月。
He has crunched the numbers and found a solution. = 他已经计算过数字并找到了解决方案。

过去完成时:
The company had been crunching under the weight of debt. = 公司在债务的重压下苦苦挣扎。
They had crunched all the data before making the decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经分析了所有数据。

将来时:
The market will be crunching if interest rates rise. = 如果利率上升，市场将会陷入困境。
She will crunch the numbers next week. = 她下周将计算这些数字。

虚拟语气:
If the economy crunched further, many businesses would fail. = 如果经济进一步恶化，许多企业将会倒闭。
I wish I hadn't crunched my ankle. = 我真希望我没有扭伤脚踝。

被动语态:
The data is being crunched by the computer. = 数据正在由计算机处理。
The numbers were crunched by the accountant. = 数字是由会计处理的。

疑问句:
Does the snow crunch under your boots? = 雪在你的靴子下嘎吱作响吗？
Are you crunching the numbers for the report? = 你正在为报告计算数字吗？

否定句:
The engine didn't crunch. = 发动机没有发出嘎吱声。
He didn't crunch the data properly. = 他没有正确地分析数据。
