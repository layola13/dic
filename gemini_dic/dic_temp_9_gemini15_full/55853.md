diviner:/ˈdaɪvɪnər/| n. |占卜者；预言家；探测器| A diviner is a person who practices divination, the attempt to gain insight into a situation or future event by supernatural means.  They may use various tools and methods, such as interpreting omens, reading tea leaves, or using a dowsing rod. |例句：The village elder, a renowned diviner, predicted a bountiful harvest.  村里的长老，一位著名的占卜者，预言了丰收。

The ancient diviner interpreted the flight of birds to foresee the outcome of the battle. 古代的占卜者通过观察鸟的飞行来预测战争的结果。|近义词：fortune teller, soothsayer, prophet |反义词：skeptic, doubter
