mastered:/ˈmɑːstəd/ /ˈmæstərd/| v. |掌握；精通；征服|To have learned or achieved complete understanding or skill in (something). To gain complete control of.|例句：She has mastered the art of conversation. 她已经掌握了谈话的艺术。

例句：After years of practice, he finally mastered the piano.  经过多年的练习，他最终掌握了钢琴。


近义词：learned, conquered, acquired, accomplished | 反义词：failed, neglected, ignored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He masters new skills quickly. 他很快就能掌握新技能。
She masters the art of persuasion. 她掌握了劝说的技巧。


一般过去时:
He mastered the language in a few months. 他在几个月内就掌握了这门语言。
She mastered the difficult technique. 她掌握了这项困难的技术。


现在完成时:
I have mastered the basics of programming. 我已经掌握了编程的基础知识。
They have mastered the challenges of the project. 他们已经克服了这个项目的挑战。


过去完成时:
She had mastered the technique before the competition. 比赛前她就已掌握了这项技术。
He had mastered several languages before he turned thirty. 他在三十岁之前已经掌握了好几门外语。


将来时:
I will master this skill eventually. 我最终会掌握这项技能。
She will master the new software by next week. 她下周将掌握这个新软件。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have mastered the subject. 如果我学习更努力，我就会掌握这个科目。
I wish I had mastered a foreign language earlier. 我希望我早点掌握一门外语。


被动语态:
The new technique was quickly mastered by the team.  团队很快掌握了这项新技术。
The subject matter was mastered by only a few students. 只有少数学生掌握了这门学科。


疑问句:
Have you mastered the new software yet? 你已经掌握这个新软件了吗？
Did he master the technique in time for the competition? 他及时掌握了这项技术参加比赛了吗？


否定句:
I haven't mastered this skill yet. 我还没掌握这项技能。
He didn't master the language quickly. 他没有很快掌握这门语言。
