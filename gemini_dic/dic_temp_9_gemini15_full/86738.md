helot:/ˈhɛlət/| n. |希洛特人（斯巴达的国有奴隶）|A member of a class of serfs in ancient Sparta, bound to the land and subject to the state.  Helots were not chattel slaves but were tied to the land and subject to the Spartan state. Their status was hereditary and they formed the basis of Spartan agriculture and economy. |The helots revolted against their Spartan masters several times.  希洛特人曾多次反抗他们的斯巴达主人。|serf, slave|free person, citizen|名词
