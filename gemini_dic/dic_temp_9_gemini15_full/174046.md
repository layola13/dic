quasi-vital: /ˈkwɑːzaɪˈvaɪtəl/ | adj. | 准重要的；似重要的；近乎必要的 |  Almost vital; essential but not absolutely necessary.  Describes something important, but not to the point of being absolutely crucial for survival or existence.  | 例句：The quasi-vital role of social media in modern communication is undeniable, even if we can survive without it.  社交媒体在现代沟通中的准重要作用是不可否认的，即使没有它我们也能生存。


例句：Regular exercise plays a quasi-vital role in maintaining good health.  规律的锻炼在保持健康方面起着准重要的作用。


近义词：essential, important, crucial, necessary, significant | 反义词：unimportant, inconsequential, trivial, unnecessary
