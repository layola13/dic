mountainer:/ˈmaʊntɪnər/| n. |山区居民，登山者 |A person who lives in or frequents mountains; a mountaineer.  |例句：The mountaineers were experienced in navigating treacherous terrain.  山区居民们经验丰富，能够在险峻的地形中导航。|例句：He is a skilled mountaineer who has climbed Mount Everest. 他是一位经验丰富的登山者，曾攀登过珠穆朗玛峰。|近义词：climber, hillman, highlander|反义词：plainsman, lowlander|名词
