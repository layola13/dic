goddaughter: /ˈɡɑːdɔːtə(r)/ | n. |教女，干女儿 | A goddaughter is a girl or woman who is sponsored at baptism by a person other than her parent and who, in some religions, is considered spiritually related to the sponsor.  | Example:  She is my goddaughter and I'm very proud of her. 她是我的教女，我非常为她骄傲。  |近义词：spiritual daughter,  |反义词：godson
