oceanic:/oʊʃiˈænɪk/ | adj. | 海洋的；广阔的；巨大的 | Relating to or characteristic of the ocean.  The term can also suggest vastness or immensity. | 例句：The oceanic current carried the debris across the Pacific.  = 洋流把残骸带过了太平洋。

例句：The oceanic depth is truly awe-inspiring. = 海洋的深度真是令人敬畏。


近义词：marine, sea, vast, immense | 反义词：terrestrial, inland, limited, small

词性：形容词
