nonconductive: /ˌnɒnkənˈdʌktɪv/| adj. |非导电的；绝缘的 |Not capable of conducting electricity or heat.  A nonconductive material prevents the flow of electricity or heat. |例句：Rubber is a nonconductive material, making it safe for use around electrical wiring.  橡胶是一种非导电材料，因此在电线周围使用很安全。

The handle of the pan is nonconductive to protect your hand from the heat. 锅柄是非导电的，可以保护你的手免受高温。
