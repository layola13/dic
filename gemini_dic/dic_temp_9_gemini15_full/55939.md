dizzies:/ˈdɪziz/| v.|使头晕目眩；使眼花缭乱；|to make someone feel dizzy; to cause a feeling of spinning or lightheadedness.  |例句：The spinning ride really dizzied me.  旋转的游乐设施真让我头晕。  |例句：The bright lights dizzied her. 耀眼的光线让她眼花缭乱。|近义词：disorient, bewilder, confuse |反义词：clarify, steady
