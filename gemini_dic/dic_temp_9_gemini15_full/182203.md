remonetizing: /ˌriːˈmʌnɪtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 再货币化；重新盈利 | The act of finding new ways to generate revenue from something that was previously considered unprofitable or no longer generating income.  It often involves innovating on existing assets or business models. | Example: The company is remonetizing its old game by adding in-app purchases.  这家公司正在通过添加应用内购买来使他们的旧游戏重新盈利。 | 近义词:monetizing, revitalizing,re-monetizing | 反义词:demonetizing, devaluing
