inductances:/ɪnˈdʌktənsɪz/ | n. | 电感（复数） | The property of an electric circuit by which an electromotive force is induced in it by a variation of the current.  It is measured in henries. | The inductances of the coils were carefully measured before assembly.  这些线圈的电感在组装前经过仔细测量。| reactances, impedances |  capacitances |
