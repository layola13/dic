collars:/ˈkɒləz/ /ˈkɑːlərz/| n.|衣领；项圈；颈圈|A collar is a band of fabric, leather, or other material that forms the upper part of a garment, such as a shirt or coat, fitting around the neck.|例句：My shirt collar is too tight. 我的衬衫衣领太紧了。

例句：The dog wore a spiked collar. 那条狗戴着带刺的项圈。|近义词：band, neckline|反义词：cuff (袖口)


USAGE->

The dog wears a collar. = 这条狗戴着项圈。
He always wears a crisp collar. = 他总是戴着挺括的衣领。

词源:
The word "collar" comes from the Middle English word "coler," which in turn is derived from the Old French word "col," meaning "neck."  The sense of a garment's neckband developed from the earlier meaning of "neck."
