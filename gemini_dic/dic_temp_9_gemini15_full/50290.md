depilation:/dɪpɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |脱发；去毛；除毛|The removal of hair from the body, usually by methods that remove the hair from below the skin's surface, unlike shaving or clipping.  Depilation techniques aim for longer-lasting hair removal than shaving.|Example:  She opted for depilation to remove unwanted hair on her legs. 她选择用脱毛的方法去除腿上的多余毛发。|近义词：hair removal, epilation|反义词：hair growth
