monandria:/mɒnˈændriə/| n. |单雄蕊植物|Botany:  A class of plants having only one stamen in each flower.  This classification system, based on the number of stamens, is largely obsolete in modern botanical taxonomy.  However, it remains relevant in historical contexts and for understanding older botanical literature.|例句：The Linnaean system classified some plants as belonging to the class Monandria. = 林奈系统将某些植物归类为单雄蕊植物类。|近义词：monandrous (adj.)|反义词：polyandria (many stamens)
