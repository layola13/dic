handguns:/ˈhændɡʌnz/| n.|手枪|A handgun is a firearm that is designed to be held and fired with one hand.  They are typically smaller and lighter than other firearms, and are often used for self-defense or concealed carry.  Handguns come in various types, including revolvers and semi-automatic pistols.|例句：He illegally possessed several handguns. 他非法持有数把手枪。

例句：The police confiscated the handguns found at the crime scene.  警方没收了在犯罪现场发现的手枪。


近义词：pistols, sidearms|反义词：long guns, rifles
