fertile:/ˈfɜːrtl/| adj.|肥沃的，富饶的；多产的，富有创造力的|Producing or capable of producing abundant vegetation or crops.|The fertile land produced a bountiful harvest.  这片肥沃的土地产出了丰收。|The artist had a fertile imagination.这位艺术家拥有丰富的想象力。|barren, infertile, sterile|infertile, unproductive


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The land is fertile this year. = 今年这片土地很肥沃。
This area is fertile in natural resources. = 这个地区自然资源丰富。


一般过去时:
The valley was fertile before the drought. = 干旱之前，山谷很肥沃。
The writer's mind was fertile with ideas. = 作家的头脑充满了创意。


现在完成时:
The farm has been fertile for generations. = 这农场几代人都很肥沃。
Her imagination has been fertile throughout her career. = 她的想象力贯穿了她整个职业生涯。


过去完成时:
The land had been fertile until the river changed course. = 直到河道改变之前，这片土地都很肥沃。
The artist had been fertile with ideas before the accident. = 事故发生前，这位艺术家很有创意。


将来时:
The fields will be fertile after the rain. = 雨后田地会很肥沃。
We believe that the collaboration will be fertile in new ideas. = 我们相信这次合作将产生新的创意。


虚拟语气:
If the soil were fertile, we would have a better harvest. = 如果土壤肥沃，我们的收成会更好。
If she had a fertile imagination, she could write a better novel. = 如果她有丰富的想象力，她就能写出一部更好的小说。


被动语态:
The land was made fertile by the river. = 这片土地被河水滋润得肥沃。
Her ideas were described as fertile by her colleagues. = 她的同事们形容她的想法富有创造力。


疑问句:
Is the soil fertile enough for planting? = 土壤够肥沃可以种植吗？
Is her mind fertile enough for this project? = 她的想法对这个项目够有创意吗？


否定句:
The land is not fertile this year. = 今年这片土地不肥沃。
His ideas were not fertile enough to support the project. = 他的想法不足以支持这个项目。
