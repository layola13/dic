estimate:/ˈɛstɪmət/ | n./vt.|估计，估算；评价；判断 | To form an approximate judgment or opinion regarding the worth, amount, size, etc., of something.  |例句：I estimate the cost of the project to be around $10,000. 我估计这个项目的成本大约为1万美元。

例句：The teacher estimated that about half the class would pass the exam. 老师估计大约一半的学生会通过考试。


近义词：guess, calculate, appraise, assess | 反义词：precisely measure, accurately determine | 词性：Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I estimate the project will take three months. = 我估计这个项目需要三个月。
She estimates her annual income to be around $50,000. = 她估计她每年的收入大约为5万美元。


一般过去时:
They estimated the damage to be quite extensive. = 他们估计损失相当大。
He estimated that he had walked about ten miles. = 他估计自己大约走了十英里。


现在完成时:
We have estimated the cost of the repairs. = 我们已经估算了维修费用。
She has estimated the value of her property at $500,000. = 她已经估算出她房产的价值为50万美元。


过去完成时:
They had estimated the time needed, but they were wrong. = 他们已经估算过所需的时间，但他们错了。
She had estimated the number of attendees to be much higher. = 她之前估计出席人数会高得多。


将来时:
I will estimate the cost of the materials tomorrow. = 我明天将估算材料的成本。
They will estimate the impact of the new policy next week. = 他们下周将评估新政策的影响。


虚拟语气:
If I estimated correctly, we'll finish on time. = 如果我的估计正确，我们就能按时完成。
I wish I had estimated the time more accurately. = 我希望我当时能更准确地估计时间。


被动语态:
The cost of the project was estimated to be $10,000. = 该项目的成本估计为1万美元。
The number of participants is estimated at 500. = 参与人数估计为500人。


疑问句:
How do you estimate the value of this painting? = 你如何估算这幅画的价值？
What do you estimate the project will cost? = 你估计这个项目要花多少钱？


否定句:
I cannot accurately estimate the time required. = 我无法准确地估计所需时间。
They did not estimate the risks involved. = 他们没有评估所涉及的风险。
