flog:/flɒɡ/ /flɑːɡ/| vt. n.|鞭打；猛击；兜售；竭力推销|to beat (someone) with a whip or stick; to try to sell (something) aggressively; to work (someone) hard|例句：The farmer flogged his horse.  农夫鞭打了他的马。|例句：They were flogging their new product at the trade show. 他们在贸易展上大力推销他们的新产品。|近义词：whip, lash, beat, promote, push|反义词：reward, praise, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flogs his horse every day. = 他每天鞭打他的马。
They flog their products aggressively. = 他们积极地推销他们的产品。

一般过去时:
The slave was flogged for disobedience. = 奴隶因为不服从而被鞭打。
The salesman flogged the car successfully. = 销售员成功地推销了那辆车。

现在完成时:
The farmer has flogged his horse several times this week. = 这周农夫已经鞭打过他的马好几次了。
The company has flogged its new software with great success. = 公司成功地大力推销了它的新软件。

过去完成时:
He had flogged the horse before the race. = 他在比赛前鞭打了那匹马。
The manager had flogged the team to meet the deadline. = 经理督促团队加班以赶上最后期限。

将来时:
They will flog the horses before the race. = 他们将在比赛前鞭打那些马。
The company will flog its new product to the public next month. = 公司将在下个月向公众大力推销它的新产品。

虚拟语气:
If I flogged that horse, it would run faster. = 如果我鞭打那匹马，它会跑得更快。
If they had flogged the product more aggressively, they might have sold more. = 如果他们更积极地推销产品，他们可能会卖出更多。


被动语态:
The horse was flogged mercilessly. = 那匹马被无情地鞭打。
The new product is being flogged on television. = 新产品正在电视上大力推销。

疑问句:
Did he flog the horse? = 他鞭打了那匹马吗？
Are they flogging the new software effectively? = 他们有效地推销新软件吗？

否定句:
He didn't flog the horse. = 他没有鞭打那匹马。
They didn't flog the product hard enough. = 他们没有足够努力地推销产品。
