pestering: /ˈpɛstərɪŋ/ | v. adj. n.|  纠缠；烦扰；讨厌的；令人厌烦的人或事 |  The act of persistently annoying or bothering someone.  |例句：His constant pestering finally drove her crazy. 他不断的纠缠最终把她逼疯了。

His pestering questions were annoying. 他喋喋不休的问题很烦人。


近义词：annoying, bothering, harassing, nagging | 反义词：soothing, comforting, pleasing


词性：动词和形容词，偶尔用作名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pestering her with phone calls. = 他不停地打电话骚扰她。
They are constantly pestering me for money. = 他们不断地向我索要钱财。

一般过去时:
She pestered him until he agreed. = 她一直缠着他直到他同意。
The children pestered their mother for sweets. = 孩子们一直缠着他们的母亲要糖果。

现在完成时:
He has been pestering me all day. = 他一整天都在烦我。
They have pestered the teacher with questions. = 他们一直在用问题烦老师。

过去完成时:
She had pestered him for weeks before he finally gave in. = 她缠了他几周他才最终让步。
They had pestered their parents until they agreed to go to the concert. = 他们一直缠着父母直到父母同意去音乐会。

将来时:
I know he will pester me about it again tomorrow. = 我知道他明天还会为此烦我。
She will continue pestering him until he gives her what she wants. = 她会一直缠着他直到他给她她想要的东西。

虚拟语气:
If he pestered me any more, I would lose my temper. = 如果他再烦我，我会发脾气的。
I wish he wouldn't pester me so much. = 我希望他不要再这么烦我了。

被动语态:
He was pestered by reporters all day. = 他一整天都被记者纠缠。
She was constantly pestered with unwanted phone calls. = 她不断受到不必要的电话骚扰。

疑问句:
Is he still pestering you? = 他还在烦你吗？
Are they pestering you about the report? = 他们在为报告烦你吗？

否定句:
He didn't pester her again after that. = 那之后他不再烦她了。
They didn't pester me for money this time. = 这次他们没有向我索要钱财。
