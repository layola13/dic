nonsufferance:/ˌnɒnˈsʌfərəns/| n. |不容忍，不宽容|The state or fact of not suffering or tolerating something;  intolerance.  It often implies a strong negative reaction to something considered unacceptable or offensive.|例句：His nonsufferance of dissent led to a toxic work environment.  他的不容忍异见导致了恶劣的工作环境。
The nonsufferance of his behavior was evident in everyone's silence.  他对这种行为的不容忍体现在每个人的沉默中。|近义词：intolerance, aversion, antipathy |反义词：tolerance, acceptance, forbearance
