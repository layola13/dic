proattack:/proʊəˈtæk/| n. |先发制人攻击，主动攻击|A proattack is a preemptive strike or attack launched by one party against another before the other party can attack.  It aims to neutralize or eliminate the threat posed by the other party.|例句：The military launched a proattack to prevent the enemy from launching their missiles.  军队发动先发制人攻击以阻止敌人发射导弹。

The country's proattack strategy was widely criticized. 该国的先发制人战略受到了广泛批评。|近义词：preemptive strike, offensive, first strike|反义词：defense, counterattack, reactive strike|名词
