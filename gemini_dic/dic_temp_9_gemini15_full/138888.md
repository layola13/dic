nut:/nʌt/| n. |坚果；螺母；古怪的人|A nut is the hard shell containing the seed of a plant, such as an acorn or a walnut.  It can also refer to a metal fastening device or a slightly crazy person.|例句：He cracked the nut open with a hammer. 他用锤子砸开了坚果。|例句：The nut on the wheel was loose. 轮子上的螺母松了。|近义词：seed, bolt, eccentric|反义词：bolt (in the sense of a smooth, uninterrupted process)
