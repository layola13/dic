preinsured: /ˌpriːɪnˈʃʊərd/ | adj. | 预先投保的，已投保的 |  Describes something or someone that has already been insured before a particular event or period.  It implies insurance coverage existed before a specific point in time, often to mitigate potential risks. |


例句：

The preinsured property was spared from the worst of the hurricane damage. = 预先投保的房产免受飓风最严重的破坏。

The preinsured shipment arrived safely, despite the rough seas. = 尽管风浪很大，预先投保的货物安全抵达。


近义词：insured, covered


反义词：uninsured, uncovered


词性：形容词
