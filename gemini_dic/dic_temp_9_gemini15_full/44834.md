crustaceologist:/krʌsˈteɪʃəˌɒlədʒɪst/| n. |甲壳类动物学家|A crustaceologist is a scientist who studies crustaceans, a large group of arthropods that includes crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles.  Their research can cover various aspects, from taxonomy and evolution to ecology and physiology.|The crustaceologist presented her findings on the migration patterns of blue crabs.|zoologist, carcinologist|none|名词
