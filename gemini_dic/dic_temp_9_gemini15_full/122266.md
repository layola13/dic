mitigate:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/| vt.|减轻；缓和；减轻（损害）|to make something less harmful, serious, or painful|The government took steps to mitigate the effects of the drought.  政府采取措施以减轻干旱的影响。| alleviate, lessen, reduce, moderate | aggravate, exacerbate, intensify | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We mitigate risks in our investment strategy. = 我们在投资策略中减轻风险。
The company mitigates environmental impact through sustainable practices. = 公司通过可持续的实践来减轻对环境的影响。


一般过去时:
They mitigated the damage caused by the storm. = 他们减轻了暴风雨造成的损害。
The new policy mitigated the negative effects of the previous one. = 新政策减轻了先前政策的负面影响。


现在完成时:
We have mitigated the risks associated with this project. = 我们已经减轻了与这个项目相关的风险。
The company has mitigated its carbon footprint significantly. = 公司已经大幅度减少了碳足迹。


过去完成时:
They had mitigated the potential problems before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经减轻了潜在的问题。
The engineers had mitigated the safety risks by implementing new protocols. = 工程师们通过实施新的规程来减轻安全风险。


将来时:
We will mitigate the effects of climate change through renewable energy. = 我们将通过可再生能源来减轻气候变化的影响。
The government will mitigate the economic downturn with stimulus packages. = 政府将通过经济刺激计划来减轻经济衰退。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have mitigated the damage more effectively. = 如果我们早点采取行动，就能更有效地减轻损失。
It is crucial that we mitigate the risks before it's too late. = 至关重要的是，在我们为时已晚之前减轻风险。


被动语态:
The damage was mitigated by the quick response of the emergency services. = 紧急服务的快速反应减轻了损失。
The negative consequences were mitigated by careful planning. = 负面后果通过周密的计划得到了减轻。


疑问句:
How can we mitigate the spread of misinformation? = 我们如何才能减轻虚假信息的传播？
What measures are being taken to mitigate the pollution? = 正在采取哪些措施来减少污染？


否定句:
They failed to mitigate the impact of the crisis. = 他们未能减轻危机的冲击。
The company did not adequately mitigate the environmental risks. = 公司没有充分减轻环境风险。


PHRASE->
mitigate risk = 减轻风险
mitigate damage = 减轻损害
mitigate effects = 减轻影响
mitigate harm = 减轻损害
mitigate losses = 减轻损失

词源:
From Latin *mitigāre, from mītis (“mild, gentle”) + -āre (“-ate”).
