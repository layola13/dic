nonirritants:/ˌnɒnˈɪrɪtənts/ | n. |非刺激物 |Substances that do not cause irritation or inflammation.  These are generally considered to be mild and safe for contact with skin or other sensitive tissues.  Examples include many lotions, creams, and some types of oils. |Example: The doctor recommended using only nonirritants on the baby's delicate skin.  医生建议只在婴儿娇嫩的皮肤上使用非刺激性物质。|近义词：soothing agents, bland substances |反义词：irritants, irritative substances
