harangue:/həˈræŋ/| n. vt.|长篇抨击性演说；大声训斥|a long, angry speech of criticism or accusation|The politician delivered a harangue against the government's policies.  这位政治家发表了一篇抨击政府政策的长篇演说。|近义词：tirade, diatribe, rant|反义词：praise, commendation|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harangues the crowd every day. 他每天都对人群进行慷慨激昂的演说。
She harangues her children about their chores. 她对孩子们做家务唠唠叨叨。


一般过去时:
The speaker harangued the audience for over an hour. 演讲者对听众进行了长达一个多小时的训斥。
He harangued his employees about their poor performance. 他对员工糟糕的表现大加训斥。


现在完成时:
The professor has harangued his students about plagiarism all semester.  教授整个学期都在对学生们大谈剽窃问题。
She has harangued me about my spending habits for years.  她多年来一直在对我挥霍无度的消费习惯唠叨不休。


过去完成时:
He had harangued the board for months before they finally agreed. 他在董事会几个月前就一直对他们进行训斥，直到他们最终同意。
They had harangued the workers about safety regulations repeatedly. 他们反复地就安全规章对工人进行训斥。


将来时:
He will harangue the crowd at the rally tomorrow. 他将在明天的集会上对人群进行慷慨激昂的演说。
She will harangue her son about his grades when he gets home. 她儿子回家后将会对他成绩不好进行训斥。


虚拟语气:
If he harangued them more politely, they might listen. 如果他更礼貌地训斥他们，他们可能会听。
I wish he hadn't harangued me in front of everyone. 我真希望他没在大家面前训斥我。


被动语态:
The workers were harangued by the manager about their productivity.  工人们受到了经理关于他们生产力的训斥。
The audience was harangued for hours by the speaker.  听众被演讲者训斥了好几个小时。


疑问句:
Will he harangue the crowd again today? 他今天还会对人群进行慷慨激昂的演说吗？
Does she often harangue her employees? 她经常训斥她的员工吗？


否定句:
He didn't harangue the audience, he simply made a statement. 他没有对听众进行训斥，他只是做了一个陈述。
She doesn't usually harangue people, but she was upset. 她通常不会训斥人，但她当时很生气。


PHRASE->
a fiery harangue  充满激情的训斥
a political harangue  政治性的抨击
a lengthy harangue冗长的训斥
a passionate harangue  充满激情的演说
an angry harangue  愤怒的训斥


词源:
源自古法语 *harangue，最终源自古希腊语 *harrang-,  意为“大声说话”。
