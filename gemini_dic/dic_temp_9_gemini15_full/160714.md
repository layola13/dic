portraitist: /ˈpɔːtrətɪst/ | n. | 肖像画家 | A portraitist is an artist who specializes in painting or drawing portraits.  They create likenesses of individuals, often capturing their personality and character as well as their physical appearance.  |  Example:  The renowned portraitist painted many members of the royal family.  这位著名的肖像画家为王室的许多成员画像。 |近义词: portrait painter, likeness artist | 反义词: N/A
