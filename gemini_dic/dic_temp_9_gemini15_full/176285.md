re-dial:/ˌriːˈdaɪəl/| vt.|重拨（电话号码）|To dial a telephone number again, typically one that has just been dialed unsuccessfully.  |例句：I had a bad connection, so I had to re-dial.  我连接不好，所以我不得不重拨。|例句：She re-dialed the number several times before finally getting through. 她重拨了好几次才终于接通。|近义词：redial, call back, try again|反义词：disconnect, hang up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-dials the number every few minutes. = 他每隔几分钟就重拨号码。
They re-dial frequently when they get a busy signal. = 他们遇到忙音时会频繁地重拨。

一般过去时:
I re-dialed the number after the call dropped. = 电话中断后，我重拨了号码。
She re-dialed his number but still couldn't reach him. = 她重拨了他的号码，但仍然联系不上他。

现在完成时:
I have re-dialed that number three times already. = 我已经重拨了那个号码三次了。
He has re-dialed the wrong number several times. = 他已经重拨了错误的号码好几次。

过去完成时:
I had re-dialed the number several times before I finally gave up. = 在我最终放弃之前，我已经重拨了好几次号码。
They had re-dialed the emergency number before the ambulance arrived. = 救护车到达之前，他们已经重拨了紧急号码。

将来时:
I will re-dial the number if I don't get an answer. = 如果我没有得到回应，我将重拨号码。
He will re-dial the number tomorrow morning. = 他明天早上会重拨号码。

虚拟语气:
If I had remembered the number, I would have re-dialed it. = 如果我记得号码，我就会重拨。
I wish I had re-dialed sooner; I might have reached her. = 我真希望我早点重拨；我可能就联系到她了。

被动语态:
The number was re-dialed automatically after the call failed. = 通话失败后，号码会自动重拨。
The number needs to be re-dialed because of the poor connection. = 由于连接不良，需要重拨号码。

疑问句:
Should I re-dial the number? = 我应该重拨号码吗？
Will you re-dial the number if it is busy? = 如果占线，你会重拨号码吗？

否定句:
I didn't re-dial the number because I was tired of waiting. = 我没有重拨号码，因为我厌倦了等待。
She didn't re-dial the number after the first attempt. = 她第一次尝试后没有重拨号码。
