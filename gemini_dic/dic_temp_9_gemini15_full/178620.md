recover:/rɪˈkʌvər/| vt. vi. n.|恢复；痊愈；重新获得；找回|to return to a normal state of health, mind, or strength after an illness, injury, shock, etc.; to get something back that was lost or taken|例句：He's recovering well from his operation. 他手术后恢复得很好。|例句：The police recovered the stolen painting. 警方找回了被盗的画作。|近义词：regain, retrieve, recoup|反义词：lose, worsen, deteriorate|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I recover quickly from illnesses. = 我生病后恢复得很快。
She recovers her composure after a moment. = 她片刻后恢复了镇静。

一般过去时:
He recovered his stolen wallet. = 他找回了被偷的钱包。
The company recovered from the financial crisis. = 公司从金融危机中恢复过来。

现在完成时:
She has recovered from her injury. = 她已经从伤病中恢复过来了。
They have recovered all the lost data. = 他们已经找回了所有丢失的数据。

过去完成时:
He had recovered his strength before the race. = 他在比赛前已经恢复了体力。
The economy had recovered significantly by the end of the year. = 到年底经济已经显著复苏。

将来时:
The doctor believes he will recover soon. = 医生相信他很快就会康复。
We expect the market to recover next year. = 我们预计市场明年会复苏。

虚拟语气:
If I had rested more, I would have recovered faster. = 如果我多休息，我会恢复得更快。
I wish I could recover the lost files. = 我希望我能找回丢失的文件。

被动语态:
The stolen goods were recovered by the police. = 失窃的物品被警方找回。
The economy is expected to be recovered within the next quarter. = 预计经济将在下一个季度复苏。

疑问句:
Will you recover your investment? = 你能收回你的投资吗？
Has she fully recovered yet? = 她完全康复了吗？

否定句:
He didn't recover consciousness for hours. = 他几个小时都没有恢复知觉。
The company hasn't yet recovered its previous market share. = 公司尚未恢复其之前的市场份额。


PHRASE->
recover from = 从……中恢复

recover costs = 挽回损失
