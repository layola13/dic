radiolytic:/ˌreɪdiəˈlɪtɪk/| adj. |辐射分解的；辐射裂解的 | Relating to or resulting from radiolysis.  Radiolytic processes are chemical changes caused by ionizing radiation. | Example: The radiolytic decomposition of water produces hydrogen and hydroxyl radicals. 辐射分解水会产生氢和羟基自由基。|近义词：radiation-induced |反义词：N/A
