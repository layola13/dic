inhalation:/ˌɪnhəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |吸入；吸气 |The act of inhaling or breathing in.  Inhalation can refer to the process of breathing in air, or the act of taking in a substance, such as medicine, through the respiratory system. |例句：Deep inhalation can help to calm your nerves. 深呼吸可以帮助你平静神经。  The doctor prescribed an inhalation treatment for his asthma. 医生为他开了吸入式哮喘治疗。|近义词：inspiration, intake, breathing in |反义词：exhalation, expiration


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inhales deeply before speaking. = 他在说话前深吸一口气。
She inhales the sweet scent of flowers. = 她深深吸入花朵的芬芳。

一般过去时:
He inhaled the toxic fumes. = 他吸入了有毒烟雾。
She inhaled deeply and slowly. = 她深深地慢慢地吸了一口气。

现在完成时:
She has inhaled too much smoke. = 她吸入了太多的烟。
He has inhaled the medicine as directed. = 他按照指示吸入了药物。

过去完成时:
He had inhaled the gas before he collapsed. = 他在倒下之前已经吸入了气体。
She had inhaled deeply to prepare for the song. = 她为了准备唱歌而深深地吸了一口气。

将来时:
He will inhale the air deeply after the run. = 他跑步后会深深地吸入空气。
She will inhale the aroma of freshly brewed coffee. = 她将吸入新鲜冲泡咖啡的香味。

虚拟语气:
If he inhaled the smoke, he would cough. = 如果他吸入烟雾，他会咳嗽。
If she inhaled the perfume, she would feel dizzy. = 如果她吸入香水，她会感到头晕。

被动语态:
The fumes were inhaled by the workers. = 工人们吸入了烟雾。
The medicine is inhaled through a nebulizer. = 药物通过雾化器吸入。

疑问句:
Did he inhale the entire dose? = 他是否吸入了全部剂量？
What did she inhale that caused the allergic reaction? = 她吸入了什么导致了过敏反应？

否定句:
He did not inhale the smoke intentionally. = 他并非故意吸入烟雾。
She did not inhale enough oxygen during the exercise. = 她在运动中没有吸入足够的氧气。
