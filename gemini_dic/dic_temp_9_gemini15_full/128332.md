neutering:/ˈnuːtərɪŋ/| n. v.|阉割；绝育|The act of surgically removing the testicles of a male animal or the ovaries of a female animal to prevent reproduction.  It is also sometimes used to refer to the removal of reproductive organs in humans, though "castration" is more common in this context.  |例句：The dog underwent neutering to prevent unwanted pregnancies.|近义词：castration, sterilization|反义词：breeding
