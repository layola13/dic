shooing:/ˈʃuːɪŋ/| v.|驱赶，哄走|The act of shooing away something or someone, typically using a gesture or sound.|例句：The farmer was shooing the chickens into the coop. 农夫正把鸡赶进鸡舍。
例句：She shooed the flies away from the picnic table. 她把苍蝇从野餐桌赶走了。|近义词：chasing, driving away, scaring away |反义词：welcoming, inviting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shoos the birds away from the garden every morning. 他每天早上都把鸟从花园里赶走。
They shoo away the stray dogs from the park. 他们把流浪狗从公园赶走。


一般过去时:
She shooed the cat out of the house. 她把猫赶出了房子。
The gardener shooed away the children from the flowerbeds. 园丁把孩子们从花坛赶走了。


现在完成时:
I have shooed away several wasps from the kitchen today. 今天我已经把几只黄蜂从厨房赶走了。
The farmer has shooed the sheep into the field. 农夫已经把羊赶进了田里。


过去完成时:
She had shooed the dog away before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，她已经把狗赶走了。
He had shooed away the flies before serving the food. 上菜之前，他已经把苍蝇赶走了。


将来时:
I will shoo the cat out of the room if it continues to disturb me. 如果猫继续打扰我，我会把它赶出房间。
They will shoo away the pigeons from the square. 他们会把广场上的鸽子赶走。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would shoo the dog away immediately. 如果我是你，我会立刻把狗赶走。
I wish I had shooed the bees away earlier. 我真希望我早点把蜜蜂赶走。


被动语态:

The children were shooed away from the dangerous area. 孩子们被从危险区域赶走了。


疑问句:

否定句:
I didn't shoo the dog away. 我没有把狗赶走。
He doesn't shoo the birds away anymore. 他不再赶鸟了。
