indemnifier:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪər/| n. |赔偿人；保证人|A person or entity that agrees to compensate another for potential losses or damages.  An indemnifier assumes the financial responsibility for the potential liabilities of another party. |Example: The insurance company acted as the indemnifier in the lawsuit, covering the costs of the damages.|近义词：guarantor, surety, insurer|反义词：claimant, obligee


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The insurance company indemnifies its clients against losses. = 保险公司赔偿客户的损失。
He indemnifies his business partner against any potential liabilities. = 他赔偿他的商业伙伴任何潜在的责任。


一般过去时:
The contractor indemnified the homeowner for the damage. = 承包商赔偿房主造成的损失。
The company indemnified its shareholders for their investment losses. = 公司赔偿股东的投资损失。


现在完成时:
The government has indemnified the victims of the disaster. = 政府已经赔偿了灾难的受害者。
The company has indemnified all its employees against workplace accidents. = 公司已经赔偿所有员工因工作场所事故造成的损失。


过去完成时:
The court had already indemnified the plaintiff before the appeal. = 法院在申诉之前已经赔偿了原告。
The insurance company had indemnified the driver for the accident before the investigation concluded. = 在调查结束之前，保险公司已经赔偿了司机的交通事故损失。


将来时:
The company will indemnify its customers for any defective products. = 公司将赔偿客户因任何有缺陷的产品造成的损失。
They will indemnify us against any future legal actions. = 他们将赔偿我们未来任何法律诉讼的损失。


虚拟语气:
If the company indemnified us, we would proceed with the project. = 如果公司赔偿我们，我们将继续进行该项目。
I wish the insurance company had indemnified me for the damage to my car. = 我希望保险公司赔偿我汽车的损坏。


被动语态:
The victims were indemnified by the government. = 受害者得到了政府的赔偿。
The contractor was indemnified for the unforeseen expenses. = 承包商获得了意外费用的赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the company indemnify us for the lost profits? = 公司会赔偿我们损失的利润吗？
Who will indemnify the injured party? = 谁将赔偿受伤方？


否定句:
The company did not indemnify the customer for the faulty product. = 公司没有赔偿顾客有缺陷的产品。
The insurance policy did not indemnify him against acts of God. = 保险单没有赔偿他因不可抗力造成的损失。


PHRASE->

indemnify for: 赔偿...
hold harmless and indemnify: 保护免受损害并赔偿
