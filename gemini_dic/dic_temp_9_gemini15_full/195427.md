/ˌsɛmiˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 半国有化 | Partially nationalized;  in a state of transition towards complete nationalization.  It describes a situation where a significant portion, but not all, of an industry or company's ownership and control has been transferred to the government or state. This often involves a mix of public and private ownership and control. | The government implemented seminationalized policies in the energy sector, resulting in a blend of public and private companies operating within the industry.  政府在能源部门实施了半国有化政策，导致国有和私营公司混合经营。| nationalized, privatized | fully privatized |
