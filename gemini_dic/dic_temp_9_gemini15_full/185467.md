reunification: /ˌriːjuːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 再统一；重新统一 | The act or process of uniting again; the state of being united again.  It often refers to the bringing together of previously separated parts of a country or organization. | 例句：The reunification of Germany was a momentous event. 德国的重新统一是一个意义重大的事件。

The reunification of families separated by war is a priority.  因战争而分离的家庭的团聚是当务之急。| 近义词：unification, reunion, consolidation | 反义词：separation, division, fragmentation
