re-forming:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmɪŋ/| v.|重组；改造；再形成|The process of reshaping or restructuring something, often implying a significant change or improvement.  It can refer to the rearrangement of components, the alteration of a structure, or the reformation of a substance.|例句：The company is undergoing re-forming to improve efficiency. 公司正在进行重组以提高效率。

例句：The sculptor was re-forming the clay into a new shape. 雕塑家正在将粘土重新塑造成新的形状。


近义词：restructuring, reorganizing, reshaping, reforming, remodeling

反义词：deteriorating, disintegrating, destroying
