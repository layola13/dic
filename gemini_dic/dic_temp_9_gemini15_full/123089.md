monger:/ˈmʌŋɡər/| n. |贩卖者，传播者|A person who sells a particular type of goods, often in a derogatory way, implying excessive or unscrupulous sales tactics.  It often implies the selling of something negative or harmful. |He was a war monger, always eager to stir up conflict.|近义词：dealer, trader, peddler|反义词：peacemaker, conciliator|名词
