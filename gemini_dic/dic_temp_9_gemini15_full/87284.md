hermeneutics:/ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪks/| n. |解释学；诠释学|The theory and practice of interpretation, especially of the Bible or literary texts.  It involves understanding the meaning and context of a text, taking into account historical, cultural, and linguistic factors.|Example:  Hermeneutics plays a crucial role in understanding ancient philosophical texts. 解释学在理解古代哲学文本方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：exegesis, interpretation, explication|反义词：misinterpretation, misunderstanding|名词
