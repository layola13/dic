carpintero:/ˌkɑːrpɪnˈtɛroʊ/ | n. | 木匠，木工 | A carpenter is a skilled craftsperson who works with wood to construct or repair buildings, furniture, or other objects.  Carpenters typically use a variety of tools, including saws, hammers, chisels, and planes. | 例句：The carpenter built a beautiful wooden table.  这位木匠做了一张漂亮的木头桌子。

例句：He is a skilled carpenter who can build anything from a simple birdhouse to a complex staircase. 他是一位技艺精湛的木匠，可以建造从简单的鸟舍到复杂的楼梯的任何东西。
