coalmen:/ˈkoʊlmən/| n. | 煤矿工人；运煤人 | A coalman is a person who works in a coal mine or delivers coal.  The term is somewhat archaic, reflecting a time when coal delivery was a more common occupation. | Example: The coalman delivered a ton of coal to the old house.  运煤人送了一吨煤到那栋老房子。  |近义词: coal miner, collier (archaic) |反义词:  None directly applicable.
