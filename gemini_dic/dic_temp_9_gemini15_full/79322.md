gleaner:/ˈɡliːnər/| n. |拾穗者；收集者； glean的动词形式 |A gleaner is someone who collects leftover grain after a harvest.  They gather what others have left behind. |例句：The gleaner carefully collected the fallen wheat. 拾穗者仔细地收集掉落的麦子。

The gleaners worked tirelessly in the fields after the harvest. 收获后，拾穗者在田里辛勤地工作。


近义词：gatherer, collector, scavenger |反义词：sower, planter


词源：源自古英语 *glēnian，意为“收集，挑选”。


USAGE->

She gleans insights from her experiences. 她从她的经验中获得见解。

现在完成时:
