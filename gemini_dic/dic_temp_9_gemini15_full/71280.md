fixatives:/ˈfɪksətɪvz/| n.|固定剂，固定物|Substances used to preserve or harden biological specimens, especially tissues for microscopic examination.  They prevent decomposition and maintain the structural integrity of the sample.|例句：Formaldehyde is a common fixative used in histology. 甲醛是组织学中常用的固定剂。

例句：The museum uses special fixatives to preserve its insect collection.  博物馆使用特殊的固定剂来保存其昆虫收藏。


近义词：preservatives, hardening agents

反义词：None directly opposite, but terms implying decomposition or degradation could be considered indirect opposites.


词性:名词
