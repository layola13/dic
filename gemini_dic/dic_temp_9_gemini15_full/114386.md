make-a-hash-of: /ˌmeɪk ə ˈhæʃ əv/ | phrasal verb | 把…弄糟；把…搞乱；使…混乱 | To ruin or spoil something completely; to thoroughly mess something up.  |例句：He made a hash of the interview. 他把面试搞砸了。|近义词：ruin, spoil, mess up, botch |反义词：improve, perfect, succeed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They make a hash of the project every time. = 他们每次都把项目搞砸了。
He makes a hash of his speeches. = 他每次演讲都讲得一塌糊涂。


一般过去时:
She made a hash of the cake recipe. = 她把蛋糕的食谱弄糟了。
The team made a hash of the presentation. = 团队把演示搞砸了。


现在完成时:
They have made a hash of the garden. = 他们把花园弄得乱七八糟。
He has made a hash of his life. = 他把自己的生活搞得一团糟。


过去完成时:
She had made a hash of the painting before she realized it. = 她意识到之前已经把画弄坏了。
They had made a hash of the negotiations. = 他们把谈判搞砸了。


将来时:
He will make a hash of the exam if he doesn't study. = 如果他不学习，他会把考试搞砸的。
They will make a hash of the trip if they don't plan properly. = 如果他们不做好计划，他们会把旅行搞砸的。


虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he wouldn't have made a hash of the exam. = 如果他学习更努力，他就不会把考试搞砸了。
I wish I hadn't made such a hash of the dinner party. = 我真希望我没有把晚宴搞砸。


被动语态:
The project was made a hash of by the inexperienced team. = 项目被缺乏经验的团队搞砸了。
The report was made a hash of due to lack of planning. = 报告由于缺乏计划而被弄得一团糟。


疑问句:
Did he make a hash of the repairs? = 他把修理工作搞砸了吗？
Will they make a hash of the contract negotiations? = 他们会把合同谈判搞砸吗？


否定句:
He didn't make a hash of the cooking. = 他没有把烹饪搞砸。
She didn't make a hash of the presentation. = 她没有把演示搞砸。

make a hash of something = 把…弄糟
