mucronate:/ˈmjuːkrəneɪt/| adj.|具短尖头的，急尖的|Having a short, sharp point; abruptly pointed.  The term is often used to describe the apex (tip) of a leaf or other plant part.|例句：The leaves of this plant are mucronate, ending in a small, sharp point. 这植物的叶子是具短尖头的，末端有一个小的尖点。

例句：The sepals of the flower were mucronate, giving them a slightly prickly appearance.  花萼是急尖的，使它们看起来有点刺。


近义词：pointed, acute, cuspidate|反义词：obtuse, rounded, blunt|词性:形容词
