samurai:/ˈsæmʊraɪ/ | n. |武士，侍|A member of the powerful military caste in feudal Japan.  Samurai were renowned for their skill in swordsmanship, martial arts, and adherence to a strict code of conduct known as Bushido. They served the daimyo (feudal lords) and played a crucial role in Japanese history and culture. |Example: The samurai fought bravely to defend their lord.  武士们英勇地战斗以保卫他们的领主。|近义词：warrior, knight |反义词：peasant, commoner
