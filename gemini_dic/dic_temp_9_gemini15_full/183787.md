rescues:/ˈrɛskjuːz/| n. vt.|营救；援救；解救|The act of rescuing someone or something from a dangerous or difficult situation.  It can also refer to the process of saving something from destruction or loss.|例句：The firefighters performed a daring rescue of the trapped miners. 消防队员对被困矿工进行了大胆的营救。

例句：The charity focuses on rescuing abandoned animals.  该慈善机构专注于营救被遗弃的动物。|近义词：save, liberate, deliver|反义词：abandon, endanger

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rescues stray cats. = 他救助流浪猫。
The lifeguard rescues drowning swimmers. = 救生员救助溺水的游泳者。

一般过去时:
They rescued the child from the burning building. = 他们从燃烧的建筑物中救出了孩子。
The police rescued the hostages. = 警察救出了人质。

现在完成时:
She has rescued several lost dogs. = 她已经救助了几只走失的狗。
The firefighters have rescued many people from fires. = 消防队员已经从火灾中救出了许多人。

过去完成时:
He had rescued the kitten before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他已经救出了那只小猫。
The team had rescued the survivors before nightfall. = 团队在夜幕降临前已经救出了幸存者。

将来时:
The coast guard will rescue the sailors. = 海岸警卫队将营救水手。
We will rescue the stranded hikers. = 我们将营救被困的徒步旅行者。

虚拟语气:
If I had a boat, I would rescue the drowning man. = 如果我有一艘船，我会救那个溺水的人。
I wish I could rescue them from their misery. = 我希望我能把他们从痛苦中解救出来。

被动语态:
The trapped miners were rescued by the firefighters. = 被困的矿工被消防队员救了出来。
The hostages were rescued after a long negotiation. = 人质在长时间的谈判后获救了。

疑问句:
Can you rescue my cat from the tree? = 你能把我的猫从树上救下来吗？
Will the rescuers rescue the injured climbers? = 救援人员会营救受伤的登山者吗？

否定句:
They couldn't rescue the cat from the well. = 他们无法把猫从井里救出来。
The firefighters were unable to rescue everyone in the building. = 消防队员无法营救大楼里的所有人。
