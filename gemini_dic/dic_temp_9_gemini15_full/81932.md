grimace:/ɡrɪˈmeɪs/| v. n.| 面部扭曲；鬼脸 | To make a grimace is to distort one's face, typically to express disgust, disapproval, or pain.  A grimace is the facial expression itself. |例句：He grimaced at the bitter taste. 他对苦味做了个鬼脸。
He wore a grimace of pain. 他痛苦地扭曲着脸。|近义词：wince, frown, scowl|反义词：smile, grin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grimaces at the sight of blood. = 他看到血就做鬼脸。
She grimaces whenever she eats spicy food. = 她每次吃辣的食物都会做鬼脸。

一般过去时:
He grimaced when he bit into the lemon. = 他咬了一口柠檬后做了个鬼脸。
She grimaced in pain after falling. = 她摔倒后痛苦地皱起了脸。

现在完成时:
He has grimaced throughout the entire movie. = 他在整部电影中一直都在做鬼脸。
She has grimaced every time she's had to take that medicine. = 每次她不得不服用那种药时，她都做鬼脸。

过去完成时:
He had grimaced before he finally admitted his mistake. = 在他最终承认错误之前，他已经做了个鬼脸。
She had grimaced several times before she finally spoke. = 在她最终开口说话之前，她已经好几次做鬼脸了。

将来时:
He will grimace when he sees the dentist. = 他看到牙医时会做鬼脸。
She will grimace at the smell of that cheese. = 她闻到那种奶酪的味道会做鬼脸。

虚拟语气:
If he had to eat that again, he would grimace. = 如果他不得不再次吃那个，他会做鬼脸。
I wish I hadn't grimaced so obviously; it was rude. = 我真希望我没有那么明显地做鬼脸；那样很粗鲁。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
