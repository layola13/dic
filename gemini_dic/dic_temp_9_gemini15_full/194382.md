self-procuring: /ˌsɛlf prəˈkjʊərɪŋ/ | adj. | 自给自足的，自行获取的 | Able to obtain or provide what is needed without external assistance.  This adjective describes something or someone that is capable of independently acquiring necessary resources or supplies. |  The project is designed to be self-procuring, minimizing reliance on external funding.  这个项目旨在自给自足，最大限度地减少对外部资金的依赖。 |近义词：self-sufficient, independent, autonomous |反义词：dependent, reliant
