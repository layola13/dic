confederation:/ˌkənˌfedəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |邦联；联盟；同盟 |A confederation is an organization of people, groups, or states that are joined together in an alliance.  It often implies a looser association than a federation, with member states retaining significant autonomy.  |例句：The Articles of Confederation governed the newly independent United States. = 《邦联条例》统治着新独立的美国。

The European Union is sometimes described as a confederation of nations. = 欧洲联盟有时被描述为一个国家联盟。


|近义词：alliance, league, federation, union |反义词：empire, monarchy, single state |名词
