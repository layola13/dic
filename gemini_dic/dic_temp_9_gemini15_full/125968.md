musing:/ˈmjuːzɪŋ/| n. v.|沉思，冥想；意味深长的想法|The act of thinking deeply and thoughtfully about something; a thought or idea that is thoughtful and often slightly mysterious.|例句：She sat by the window, lost in musing. 她坐在窗边，陷入沉思。

例句：His musing on the nature of reality led to a fascinating philosophical discussion. 他对现实本质的沉思引发了一场引人入胜的哲学讨论。


近义词：contemplation, reflection, pondering, meditation|反义词：action, activity


词性:名词和动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is musing about his future. = 他正在思考他的未来。
She muses on the meaning of life. = 她正在思考生命的意义。


一般过去时:
He mused about his childhood. = 他回忆起他的童年。
She mused on the beauty of the sunset. = 她沉思着日落的美丽。


现在完成时:
I have been musing about this problem all day. = 我整天都在思考这个问题。
She has mused on the subject for years. = 她多年来一直在思考这个问题。


过去完成时:
He had been musing on the matter before he spoke. = 他在说话之前就已经思考过这个问题了。
She had mused over the letter before replying. = 她仔细考虑过信件后再回复。


将来时:
I will be musing on this topic for some time. = 我会思考这个话题一段时间。
She will muse on the implications of her decision. = 她会思考她决定的影响。


虚拟语气:
If I were to muse upon it, I would probably agree. = 如果我要考虑一下的话，我可能会同意。
I wish I had mused more before acting. = 我希望我行动前多思考一下。


被动语态:
The problem was being mused upon by the committee. = 委员会正在思考这个问题。 (较为少见)
His words were mused over by the listeners. = 听众们仔细思考了他的话。(较为少见)


疑问句:
What are you musing about? = 你在想什么？
What was she musing on during the meeting? = 会议期间她在思考什么？


否定句:
He didn't muse on the consequences. = 他没有思考后果。
She wasn't musing when I interrupted her. = 我打断她时，她并没有在沉思。


PHRASE->
deep musing = 深思熟虑
lost in musing = 陷入沉思
profound musing = 深奥的沉思
pensive musing = 沉思默想
idle musing = 漫无目的的思考
