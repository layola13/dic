shoutingly:/ˈʃaʊtɪŋli/ | adv. | 大声地，喧闹地 |  In a loud and boisterous manner; with shouts.  The word is formed by adding the adverbial suffix "-ly" to the present participle "shouting". It emphasizes the manner in which an action is performed, highlighting the loudness and possibly the uncontrolled nature of the shouting.  | Example: He spoke shoutingly to the crowd. 他大声地对人群喊话。 | 近义词：loudly, boisterously, clamorously | 反义词：quietly, softly, silently | 副词
