mercies:/ˈmɜːrsiːz/| n. |怜悯；恩惠；上帝的恩典|The plural form of "mercy," referring to instances of compassion, kindness, or forgiveness, or divine grace.  It can also refer to things one is thankful for.|例句：We give thanks for God's many mercies. 我们感谢上帝的诸多恩典。

例句：He showed mercy to his enemies. 他对敌人表现出了怜悯。


近义词：compassion, kindness, grace, favor, blessings |反义词：cruelty, harshness, injustice, wickedness
