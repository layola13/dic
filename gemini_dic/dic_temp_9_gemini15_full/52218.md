diathermometer:/ˌdaɪəθərˈmɒmɪtər/| n. |透热计，测温计|A diathermometer is a medical instrument used to measure the temperature of deep tissues using radio waves.  It's used to measure the internal temperature of the body, particularly during hyperthermia treatments.  |Example: The doctor used a diathermometer to monitor the patient's temperature during the hyperthermia session. 医生在高温治疗期间用透热计监测病人的体温。|近义词：thermometer (for surface temperature)|反义词：无|名词
