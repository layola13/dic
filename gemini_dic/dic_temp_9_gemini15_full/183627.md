reraises:/ˌriːˈreɪzɪz/| v.|再次提出；重新提出；再次提高|To raise something again; to bring up a topic or issue a second time; to increase something again.|例句：The committee reraises the issue of funding every year.  委员会每年都会重新提出资金问题。
The company reraises its prices after the increase in raw materials cost. 公司在原材料成本上涨后再次提高了价格。|近义词：reintroduce, reiterate, raise again|反义词：withdraw, drop, lower|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reraises the point in every meeting. 他在每次会议上都会重新提出这个问题。
The company reraises its prices annually. 公司每年都会重新调整价格。


一般过去时:
She reraised her concerns about the project. 她再次表达了她对该项目的担忧。
The government reraised the tax rates last year. 政府去年再次提高了税率。


现在完成时:
They have reraised the question several times. 他们已经多次重新提出这个问题了。
The board has reraised the budget concerns. 董事会再次提出了预算方面的担忧。


过去完成时:
He had reraised the objection before the vote. 他在投票前已经再次提出异议了。
They had reraised the topic during the previous meeting. 他们在之前的会议上已经重新讨论过这个话题了。


将来时:
She will reraise the matter at the next meeting. 她将在下次会议上再次提出此事。
The CEO will reraise the issue of expansion. 首席执行官将再次提出扩张问题。


虚拟语气:
If he reraised the issue, it would cause a lot of trouble. 如果他再次提出这个问题，将会引起很多麻烦。
I wish he hadn't reraised that old argument. 我真希望他没有再次提起那个陈旧的论点。


被动语态:
The issue was reraised by the opposition party. 这个议题被反对党再次提出。
The price was reraised due to inflation. 价格由于通货膨胀而再次上涨。


疑问句:
Will they reraise the question at the conference? 他们会在会议上再次提出这个问题吗？
Did he reraise the subject after the break? 中断后他是否再次谈及这个话题？


否定句:
He did not reraise the subject during the discussion. 他在讨论中没有再次提起这个话题。
The company did not reraise its prices this quarter. 公司本季度没有再次提高价格。
