crack:/kræk/| n., v.|裂缝；爆裂声；破裂；尝试；破解；打击；|a sudden sharp sound, especially of something breaking; a line of breakage in something; to break or cause to break suddenly with a sharp sound; to solve or decipher (a code, system, etc.); to strike (someone) heavily; |例句：The vase cracked when it fell. 花瓶摔碎了。  The detective cracked the case. 侦探破案了。|近义词：break, split, fracture, solve, decipher |反义词：mend, repair, fix

PHRASE->

crack down on: 对…采取严厉措施  (The police cracked down on drug dealers.) 警方严厉打击毒贩。
crack a smile: 微微一笑 (She cracked a smile at the joke.) 她听到笑话微微一笑。
crack the whip: 严格管理 (The boss cracked the whip to get the project done on time.) 老板严格管理以确保项目按时完成。
crack up: 崩溃；大笑 (He cracked up laughing at the comedian's jokes.) 他听到喜剧演员的笑话笑得前仰后合。
crack open: 打开 (Let's crack open a bottle of champagne.) 让我们打开一瓶香槟酒。
