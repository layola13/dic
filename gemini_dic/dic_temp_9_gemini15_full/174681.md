quorate:/ˈkwɔːreɪt/| adj.|法定人数的；具有法定人数的|Having the minimum number of members required to conduct a meeting legally.  A quorum is the minimum number of members of an assembly or society that must be present at a meeting for the proceedings to be valid.|例句：The meeting could not proceed because it lacked a quorate number of attendees.  会议因缺乏法定人数而无法进行。

例句：The board was quorate and able to make decisions. 董事会具有法定人数，能够做出决定。
