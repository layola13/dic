preconsolidates: /ˌpriːkənˈsɒlɪdeɪts/ | v. | 预先巩固；预先整合 | To strengthen or combine beforehand; to prepare in advance by consolidating.  This word is a relatively rare and less common usage, formed by combining "pre-" and "consolidates". | 例句：The company preconsolidated its resources before launching the new product. = 公司在推出新产品之前预先整合了资源。


The company preconsolidated its market position by acquiring a key competitor. = 公司通过收购主要竞争对手来预先巩固其市场地位。


近义词：prepare, fortify, integrate, unify | 反义词：disperse, fragment, weaken, divide
