environment:/ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt/ | n. |环境，周围状况 |The environment is the surroundings or conditions in which a person, animal, or plant lives or operates.  It encompasses all living and non-living things occurring naturally, without human intervention. |例句：We need to protect the environment from pollution. 我们需要保护环境免受污染。|例句：The harsh environment made survival difficult. 恶劣的环境使生存变得困难。|近义词：surroundings, setting, context |反义词：N/A
