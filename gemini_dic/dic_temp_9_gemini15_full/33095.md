christendoms:/ˈkrɪstəndəm/| n. |基督教世界；基督教信仰；基督教国家|The collective body of Christian churches or countries; the entire Christian world.  It can also refer to the sphere of influence of Christianity. |例句：The news spread rapidly throughout Christendom.  这条消息迅速传遍了整个基督教世界。

例句：The division within Christendom caused much conflict. 基督教内部的分裂导致了许多冲突。


近义词：the Christian world, Christianity (as a concept), the Christian community


反义词：paganism, non-Christian world


词性：名词
