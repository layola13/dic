sinophobe:/ˈsɪnəfoʊb/| n. | 恐华症患者，排华者 | A person who is afraid of, dislikes, or is prejudiced against China, its people, or Chinese culture.  |  He's a known sinophobe, constantly voicing negative opinions about China. 他是一个众所周知的恐华症患者，不断发表对中国的负面言论。 | xenophobe, anti-Chinese |  philocinist (someone who loves China),  sinophile | 名词
