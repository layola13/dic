meta-language:/ˈmɛtəlæŋɡwɪdʒ/ | n. |元语言，超语言| A language used to describe or analyze another language.  It's a system for talking *about* a language, rather than using the language itself.  This often involves describing its grammar, syntax, semantics, or phonology. | Example:  Linguistics uses a meta-language to describe the grammatical structures of different languages.  语言学使用元语言来描述不同语言的语法结构。|近义词：formal language, descriptive language |反义词：object language
