re-carried:/ˌriːˈkæriːd/| v. |重新携带；再次运送 | To carry something again or in a different way.  This word implies a previous instance of carrying, and often suggests a repetition or a change in the method of transport. |例句：The damaged goods were re-carried on a different truck.  损坏的货物被一辆不同的卡车重新运送。

The package was re-carried by air after the initial sea shipment was delayed.包裹在海运延误后通过空运重新运送。|近义词：retransported, reconveyed |反义词：unloaded, discarded


USAGE->

They re-carry the supplies every week. = 他们每周都重新运送补给。
The company re-carries the packages to different locations. = 公司将包裹重新运送到不同的地点。
