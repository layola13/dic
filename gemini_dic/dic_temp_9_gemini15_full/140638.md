olympic:/əˈlɪmpɪk/| adj. n.|奥林匹克的；奥运会的|relating to the Olympic Games.  The adjective form is used to describe things related to the Olympics, such as events, athletes, or venues. The noun form refers to the Olympic Games themselves or, sometimes, to a single Olympic event.|例句：The Olympic stadium is a magnificent structure. 奥林匹克体育场是一座宏伟的建筑。 The Olympic Games are held every four years. 奥运会每四年举行一次。|近义词：Olympian (adj.),Games|反义词：None.
