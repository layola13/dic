shoulder-surf:/ˈʃoʊldərˌsɜːrf/| v.|窥探屏幕信息，偷看（他人屏幕）|To look over someone's shoulder to see what is on their computer screen.  This is often done without their permission or knowledge. |例句：He was shoulder-surfing to see if he could get my password. 他在偷看我的屏幕，想得到我的密码。

例句：I saw someone shoulder-surfing my colleague's computer at the coffee shop. 我看到有人在咖啡店偷看我同事的电脑屏幕。


近义词：peek, glance, eavesdrop (in a digital context)|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be respecting someone's privacy.
