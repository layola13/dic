pushmina:/pʊʃˈmiːnə/| n. |帕什米纳羊绒围巾|A pashmina is a fine, soft shawl or scarf made from the soft undercoat of the Changthangi goat.  It's known for its luxurious feel and warmth.|例句：She wrapped herself in a luxurious cashmere pashmina. 她披上了一条奢华的羊绒帕什米纳围巾。|例句：The pashmina was a beautiful gift from her grandmother. 这条帕什米纳是她祖母送的精美礼物。|近义词：shawl, scarf, wrap|反义词：None specifically.
