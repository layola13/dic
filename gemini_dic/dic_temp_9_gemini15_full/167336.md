pretransported:/ˌpriːtrænsˈpɔːrtɪd/| adj.| 预先运输的；预先搬运的 |  Describes something that has been transported before a particular event or time.  It implies preparation or planning in advance of the main event or process. | Example: The pretransported goods arrived at the warehouse on schedule. 预先运输的货物按时到达仓库。 The pretransported equipment was already set up before the crew arrived.  预先运输的设备在工作人员到达之前就已经安装好了。| 近义词：pre-shipped, advanced-shipped | 反义词：post-transported, subsequently transported |  adj.
