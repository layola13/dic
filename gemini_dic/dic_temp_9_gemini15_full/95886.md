increasings:/ɪnˈkriːsɪŋz/ | adj. | 增加的；增长的 | Increasing numbers of people are using the internet.  越来越多的人正在使用互联网。|  growing, rising, escalating | decreasing, falling, declining | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Increasing demand drives up prices. =  不断增长的需求推高了价格。
The company is experiencing increasing profits. = 公司正在经历利润增长。


一般过去时:
Increasing tensions led to the conflict. =  日益紧张的局势导致了冲突。
There were increasing reports of violence. =  暴力事件的报道越来越多。


现在完成时:
There have been increasing concerns about climate change. =  人们对气候变化的担忧日益加剧。
We have seen increasing evidence of its effectiveness. = 我们已经看到了越来越多的证据证明其有效性。


过去完成时:
Increasing pressure had already forced them to make a decision. =  日益增长的压力已经迫使他们做出决定。
The increasing cost had made the project unsustainable. =  不断增加的成本使该项目难以为继。


将来时:
Increasing automation will displace many workers. =  日益增长的自动化将取代许多工人。
We anticipate increasing demand for our products next year. = 我们预计明年对我们产品的需求将增加。


虚拟语气:
If there were increasing opportunities, more people would relocate. = 如果机会越来越多，更多的人就会搬迁。
I wish there were increasing investment in renewable energy. = 我希望可再生能源的投资能够越来越多。


被动语态:
Increasing numbers of jobs are being created in the tech sector. = 科技行业正在创造越来越多的就业机会。
The problem is being exacerbated by increasing pollution. =  这个问题因污染加剧而变得更加严重。


疑问句:
Are there increasing signs of economic recovery? =  经济复苏的迹象越来越明显吗？
Is the company experiencing increasing sales? = 公司的销售额是否正在增长？


否定句:
There are not increasing sales this quarter. = 本季度销售额没有增长。
The company is not experiencing increasing market share. = 公司的市场份额没有增长。



PHRASE->
increasingly important = 日益重要

increasingly difficult = 越来越困难
at an increasing rate = 以越来越快的速度
increasingly complex = 越来越复杂
