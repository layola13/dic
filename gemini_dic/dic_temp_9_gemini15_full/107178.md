lagger:/ˈlæɡər/ | n. |落后者，慢吞吞的人，拖拉的人 | A person who falls behind others; someone who is slow or sluggish. |例句：He's a lagger and always finishes his work last. 他是个落后者，总是最后一个完成工作。|例句：Don't be a lagger; we need to hurry up.别拖拉，我们需要快点。|近义词：straggler, slowpoke, dawdler |反义词：leader, pacesetter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lags behind the others in the race. = 他在比赛中落后于其他人。
She lags in her studies. = 她的学习落后了。

一般过去时:
The project lagged due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，项目进展缓慢。
He lagged behind his classmates in math. = 他在数学方面落后于他的同学。


现在完成时:
The team has lagged behind in their fundraising efforts. = 该团队的筹款工作一直落后。
They have lagged in their response to the crisis. = 他们对危机的回应迟缓。


过去完成时:
The company had lagged in adopting new technologies. = 公司在采用新技术方面一直落后。
He had lagged behind in his training, so he wasn't ready for the competition. = 他的训练落后了，所以他还没准备好参加比赛。


将来时:
If we continue at this pace, we will lag behind our competitors. = 如果我们继续以这种速度，我们将落后于竞争对手。
Production is expected to lag next quarter. = 预计下个季度的产量将下降。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't lagged, he would have won the race. = 如果他不拖拉，他就能赢得比赛。
I wish I hadn't lagged in my studies. = 我真希望我的学习没有落后。


被动语态:
The project was lagged by unforeseen delays. = 项目因不可预见的延误而滞后。
The progress of the negotiations was lagged by disagreements. = 谈判的进展因分歧而受阻。


疑问句:
Is he lagging behind in his work? = 他的工作落后了吗？
Are you lagging behind in your studies? = 你的学习落后了吗？


否定句:
He didn't lag behind in the marathon. = 他在马拉松比赛中没有落后。
She didn't lag in her efforts to help. = 她并没有在帮助别人的努力上懈怠。
