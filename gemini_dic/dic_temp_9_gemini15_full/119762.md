miffed:/mɪft/| adj.|生气，恼怒的，不高兴的|Slightly annoyed; irritated.  The word often implies a mild or temporary state of annoyance. |例句：He was a little miffed that he hadn't been invited. 他有点生气因为他没有被邀请。

例句：She was miffed by his careless remark.  她被他粗心大意的评论弄得很生气。


近义词：annoyed, irritated, peeved, vexed, disgruntled

反义词：pleased, delighted, happy, content


词性：形容词


USAGE->
