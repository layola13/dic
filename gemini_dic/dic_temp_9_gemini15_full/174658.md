quod:/kwɒd/|pron.|什么，哪一个|This word is a Latin pronoun meaning "what" or "that which". It's often found in legal and academic contexts, particularly in phrases like "quod erat demonstrandum" (Q.E.D.).  It serves as a relative pronoun, connecting clauses and introducing a subordinate clause.|例句：Quod erat demonstrandum. (Q.E.D.) = 这正是要证明的。|近义词:which, that|反义词:None.
