pathophysiologically:/ˌpæθoʊˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 病理生理学地；从病理生理学的角度 |  Describes something related to the study of the functional changes in the body that result from disease. It explains how a disease process affects the normal functioning of organs and systems. |  The study explored the pathophysiologically relevant changes in the cardiovascular system during acute myocardial infarction.  这项研究探讨了急性心肌梗死期间心血管系统中病理生理学相关的变化。|  病理学地, 生理学地 |  (无明确反义词, 可用"normally" 或 "physiologically" 表示相反的概念) | 副词
