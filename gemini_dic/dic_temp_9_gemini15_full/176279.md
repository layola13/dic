re-definition:/ˌriːˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən/| n.|重新定义，再定义|The act of defining something again, often in a new or different way; a new or revised definition.|The re-definition of marriage has been a subject of much debate.  重新定义婚姻一直是许多争论的主题。The company's re-definition of its brand image led to increased sales. 公司对其品牌形象的重新定义导致销售额增长。|reinterpretation, reformulation, restatement|misinterpretation, original definition|名词
