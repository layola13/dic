cast lots:/kæst lɒts/| v. |抽签；掷签决定|To cast lots is to use a random method, such as drawing lots from a container, to make a decision or determine something.  This often involves a sense of fate or leaving the outcome to chance. |They cast lots to decide who would go first. 他们抽签决定谁先走。|The sailors cast lots to see who would be left behind. 水手们抽签决定谁留下。|decide, determine, choose|refuse to decide, pre-determine|
