dentals:/ˈdɛntlz/ | n. |齿音| Sounds produced with the tongue touching the teeth.  These sounds include /t/, /d/, /n/, /l/, /s/, /z/, /θ/, and /ð/.  They are categorized by their place of articulation. | Example:  The sounds /t/ and /d/ are dental plosives. 齿音包括舌头接触牙齿发出的声音。这些声音包括/t/、/d/、/n/、/l/、/s/、/z/、/θ/和/ð/。它们按发音部位进行分类。 例如：/t/和/d/是齿龈塞音。|近义词:alveolar (sometimes, depending on the specific sound)|反义词:None (it's a category, not a value with an opposite)|
