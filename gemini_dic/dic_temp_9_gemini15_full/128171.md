networkable: /ˈnɛtwɜːrkəbəl/ | adj. | 可联网的；善于建立人脉的 |  Able to be connected to a network; easily able to establish and maintain professional contacts. |


例句：

This new printer is networkable, allowing seamless printing from multiple devices.  = 这台新打印机可以联网，允许从多个设备无缝打印。

He's a highly networkable individual, always building connections and expanding his professional circle. = 他是一个非常善于建立人脉的人，总是建立联系并扩大他的专业圈子。


近义词：connectable, interconnectable, sociable, well-connected


反义词：isolated, unsociable, reclusive


词性：形容词
