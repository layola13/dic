promenader:/ˌprɒmənˈeɪdə(r)/| n. |散步者，蹓跶者 |A person who promenades, especially for pleasure.  A person who takes a leisurely stroll, often in a public place.|例句：The park was filled with promenaders enjoying the sunshine. 公园里挤满了享受阳光的散步者。The elderly couple were leisurely promenaders, enjoying the scenery.  这对老年夫妇是悠闲的散步者，享受着风景。|近义词：stroller, walker, rambler |反义词：stay-at-home, homebody |名词

He promenades in the park every evening. 他每天晚上都在公园散步。
