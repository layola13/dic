dasheen:/dæˈʃiːn/| n.|芋头|A starchy tuber of a plant (Colocasia esculenta) of the arum family, widely cultivated in tropical regions as a food crop.  It is similar to taro but generally larger and with a coarser texture.|例句：Dasheen is a staple food in many Caribbean islands. 芋头是许多加勒比海岛屿的主食。|例句：We used dasheen in the stew to thicken it. 我们在炖菜里用了芋头来增稠。|近义词：taro, cocoyam|反义词：无
