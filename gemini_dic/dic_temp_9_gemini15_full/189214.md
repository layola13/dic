sacrilege:/ˈsækrɪlɪdʒ/| n. |亵渎；渎神；冒犯 |The act of violating or profaning something sacred; an act of disrespect towards something considered sacred.  |例句：To many, destroying ancient artifacts is considered sacrilege. 对许多人来说，破坏古代文物被认为是亵渎神灵的行为。

例句：The graffiti on the church wall was a shocking sacrilege. 教堂墙壁上的涂鸦是一种令人震惊的亵渎行为。


近义词：blasphemy, desecration, profanation, impiety |反义词：reverence, piety, sanctity |词性:名词
