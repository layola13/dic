flourishing: /ˈflʌrɪʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 繁荣的；兴旺的；茂盛的 | thriving, prosperous, successful, blooming | declining, failing, withering | 形容词

英文解析:  Describes something that is growing rapidly and successfully; thriving or prospering.  It can refer to businesses, economies, plants, or even people.

例句:

The company is flourishing under its new CEO.  这家公司在新的CEO领导下蓬勃发展。

His career is flourishing, with numerous awards and accolades. 他的事业蒸蒸日上，获得了无数奖项和赞誉。


近义词: thriving, prosperous, successful, booming, blossoming, blooming


反义词: declining, failing, withering, stagnant, languishing


词源:  From Middle English *flourisshen, from Old French florir "to blossom, flourish," from Latin florēre "to bloom," from flos (genitive floris) "flower."  The sense of "to thrive" developed later.
