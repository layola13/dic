safranine:/ˈsæfrəniːn/| n.|番红;藏红;番红精|A dye that stains cells red, commonly used in histology and microbiology to counterstain cells after staining with other dyes.  It is particularly useful for visualizing nuclei and other cellular structures.|例句：Safranin is used as a counterstain in Gram staining to color Gram-negative bacteria pink or red. 番红在革兰氏染色中用作复染剂，使革兰氏阴性菌染成粉红色或红色。|近义词：neutral red|反义词：无|名词
