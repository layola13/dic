knaw:/nɔː/| vt.| 啃，咬，慢慢地嚼碎 |To bite or chew on something repeatedly, especially in a persistent or determined way.  |例句：The dog was knawing on the bone.  那条狗正在啃骨头。
The mouse knawed a hole in the wall.老鼠啃穿了墙上的一个洞。|近义词：chew, nibble, gnaw |反义词: swallow, gulp


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog knaws the bone. = 狗在啃骨头。
He knaws at the problem. = 他在苦思冥想这个问题。

一般过去时:
The mouse knawed through the wire. = 老鼠咬断了电线。
She knawed on her lip nervously. = 她紧张地咬着嘴唇。

现在完成时:
The rats have knawed through the floorboards. = 老鼠已经啃穿了地板。
He has knawed at the problem for days. = 他已经苦思冥想这个问题好几天了。

过去完成时:
The dog had knawed the bone clean. = 狗已经把骨头啃干净了。
They had knawed at the agreement for weeks. = 他们已经就协议苦思冥想了好几周了。


将来时:
The puppy will knaw on everything in sight. = 小狗会啃咬它能看到的一切东西。
She will knaw at the idea until she understands it. = 她会反复琢磨这个想法直到理解为止。


虚拟语气:
If the dog knawed at the furniture, I would be angry. = 如果狗啃咬家具，我会很生气。
I wish the rats hadn't knawed through the cables. = 我真希望老鼠没有咬断电缆。

被动语态:
The bone was knawed by the dog. = 骨头被狗啃了。
The problem is being knawed at by the committee. = 委员会正在苦思冥想这个问题。

疑问句:
Is the dog knawing on the shoe? = 狗在啃鞋子吗？
What is he knawing at? = 他在啃什么？

否定句:
The dog didn't knaw the furniture. = 狗没有啃家具。
He didn't knaw at the problem effectively. = 他没有有效地思考这个问题。


PHRASE->
knaw at (something): 苦思冥想，努力解决
knaw on (something):  啃咬，咬
knaw through (something): 啃穿，咬断
knaw away at (something): 逐渐地啃食，逐渐地破坏
knaw one's way through (something):  费力地啃食通过
