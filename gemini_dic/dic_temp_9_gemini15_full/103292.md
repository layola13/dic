jibbers:/ˈdʒɪbərz/| v.|胡言乱语，喋喋不休|To talk rapidly and incoherently, often due to excitement, nervousness, or mental distress.  It implies a lack of sense or meaning in the speech.|例句：He was jibbering about aliens and government conspiracies. 他胡言乱语地谈论外星人以及政府阴谋。

例句：The old man jibbered incoherently, unable to communicate his needs. 老人语无伦次地胡言乱语，无法表达自己的需求。


近义词：babble, mutter, ramble, chatter, splutter|反义词：speak clearly, articulate
