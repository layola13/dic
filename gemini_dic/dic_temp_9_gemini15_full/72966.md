foodstuffs:/ˈfuːdˌstʌfs/ | n. | 食品，食物 | Foodstuffs are any substances used or consumed as food.  This term often refers to a range of items, including processed foods and raw ingredients.  The term is often used in a more formal or commercial context. | Example: The supermarket stocks a wide variety of foodstuffs. 这家超市储存各种各样的食品。The company specializes in the import and export of foodstuffs. 这家公司专门从事食品的进出口业务。|近义词：food, provisions, victuals |反义词：None (It's a broad category, not easily opposed) |名词
