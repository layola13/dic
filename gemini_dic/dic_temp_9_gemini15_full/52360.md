dictatorship:/ˈdɪktətərʃɪp/| n. |独裁统治，专政|The rule of a country by a dictator, or a system of government in which power is held by one person or a small group of people with absolute authority.  A dictatorship is characterized by the suppression of opposition and the lack of individual rights. |Example:  The country suffered under a brutal military dictatorship for decades.  这个国家几十年来一直遭受残酷的军事独裁统治。

The people lived in fear under the dictator's harsh dictatorship.  在独裁者的残酷统治下，人民生活在恐惧之中。|近义词：autocracy, tyranny, totalitarianism |反义词：democracy, republic
