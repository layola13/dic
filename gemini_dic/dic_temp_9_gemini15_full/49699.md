demobilise: /ˌdiːˈməʊbɪlaɪz/ | vt. | 解散（军队）；使复员；使停止运转 | To disband (an army or other armed force); to discharge from military service;  to stop the operation of (a system, process, etc.). |


例句：

The army was demobilised after the war. = 战争结束后，军队被解散了。

The factory demobilised its workforce during the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退期间，工厂遣散了员工。


近义词：disband, discharge, decommission, dismantle


反义词：mobilise, activate


词性：动词

疑问句:

否定句:

PHRASE->

词源:
From de- + mobilise.  The prefix "de-" indicates reversal or removal, while "mobilise" means to assemble or prepare for action.  Therefore, "demobilise" means to reverse the process of mobilisation.
