malays:/məˈleɪz/| n.|马来人；马来语|The word "Malays" refers to the indigenous people of the Malay Peninsula and surrounding regions,  including parts of Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, and the Philippines.  It can also refer to their language, Malay. |例句：The Malays have a rich cultural heritage. 马来人拥有丰富的文化遗产。
The Malay language is spoken by millions. 马来语被数百万人使用。|近义词：Malaysian (referring to citizens of Malaysia)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with other ethnic groups in the region.|名词
