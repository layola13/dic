/ˈɡɪtɪn/| v.|得到；获得；出发；开始|getting is the present participle of the verb "get". It indicates an ongoing action or a state of being in the process of obtaining something.  It has many meanings depending on context.|例句：I'm getting ready for work. 我正在准备上班。 He's getting better. 他正在好转。|近义词：obtaining, acquiring, receiving, commencing, starting|反义词：losing, giving up, ceasing, stopping|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am getting a new car. = 我正在买一辆新车。
She is getting ready for the party. = 她正在为派对做准备。


一般过去时:
I got a letter yesterday. = 我昨天收到了一封信。
They got married last year. = 他们去年结婚了。


现在完成时:
I have gotten used to the noise. = 我已经习惯了噪音。
She has gotten much better at playing the piano. = 她弹钢琴已经好多了。


过去完成时:
I had gotten there before he arrived. = 我在他到达之前就已经到了那里。
They had gotten lost before they found their way. = 他们迷路了才找到回家的路。


将来时:
I will be getting a promotion soon. = 我很快就会升职。
She will be getting her results next week. = 她下周会拿到成绩。


虚拟语气:
If I got the job, I would be very happy. = 如果我得到这份工作，我会很高兴。
I wish I had gotten a better grade on the test. = 我真希望我在考试中能取得更好的成绩。


被动语态:
The car is getting repaired. = 车正在修理中。
The problem is getting solved gradually. = 问题正在逐渐得到解决。


疑问句:
Are you getting enough sleep? = 你睡够了吗？
Is she getting along with her classmates? = 她和同学们相处得好吗？


否定句:
I am not getting paid enough. = 我的薪水不够。
He is not getting the support he needs. = 他没有得到他需要的支持。


PHRASE->
getting along = 相处融洽
getting better = 好转
getting started = 开始
getting there = 抵达；成功
getting old = 变老
