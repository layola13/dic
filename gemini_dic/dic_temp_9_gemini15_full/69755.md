feria:/ˈfɛəriə/| n.|假日，节庆；集市|A feria is a fair, market, or holiday, often associated with religious festivals in Spanish-speaking countries.  It can also refer to a period of exemption from work or other duties.|例句：The town square was bustling with activity during the annual feria.  小镇广场在一年一度的集市期间热闹非凡。
The feria offered a wide variety of goods and entertainment. 集市提供了各种各样的商品和娱乐活动。|近义词：market, fair, festival, holiday|反义词：workday, workday
