frees:/friːz/ | v. |使自由；释放；使免除；解放|To liberate or release from confinement, restraint, or obligation.  It can also refer to making something available or accessible.|例句：The prisoner was freed after serving his sentence.  犯人服刑期满后被释放了。|例句：The new software freed up a lot of time for the team. 新软件为团队节省了很多时间。|近义词：liberate, release, unbind |反义词：imprison, restrain, bind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frees the bird from its cage. 他把鸟从笼子里放了出来。
The new policy frees businesses from unnecessary regulations. 新政策使企业免受不必要的规章制度的约束。


一般过去时:
They freed the hostages after long negotiations.经过长时间的谈判后，他们释放了人质。
The judge freed him from all charges. 法官撤销了他所有的指控。


现在完成时:
The government has freed many political prisoners. 政府已经释放了许多政治犯。
She has freed herself from the constraints of her past. 她已经摆脱了过去的束缚。


过去完成时:
The prisoners had been freed before the dawn. 囚犯们在天亮前就被释放了。
He had freed himself from his financial worries by investing wisely. 他通过精明的投资摆脱了经济上的担忧。


将来时:
They will free the prisoners tomorrow.他们明天将释放囚犯。
The new technology will free us from tedious tasks. 新技术将使我们摆脱繁琐的任务。


虚拟语气:
If only I could free myself from this debt! 要是我能摆脱这笔债务就好了！
I wish I could free my mind from all these worries. 我希望我能摆脱所有这些担忧。


被动语态:
The hostages were freed yesterday. 人质昨天被释放了。
The city was freed from the siege after weeks of fighting.经过几周的战斗后，这座城市被解除了围困。


疑问句:
Will they free the prisoners soon? 他们很快会释放囚犯吗？
How can we free ourselves from this difficult situation? 我们如何才能摆脱这种困境？


否定句:
They did not free the prisoners. 他们没有释放囚犯。
The new law did not free the company from its obligations. 新法律并没有使公司免除其义务。
