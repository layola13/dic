patterer:/ˈpætərər/| n. |喋喋不休的人，闲聊的人 |A patterer is someone who talks rapidly and incessantly, often about trivial matters. They may be engaging in light conversation or trying to persuade someone through rapid-fire speech.  |例句：The salesman was a relentless patterer, barely giving me a chance to speak.  那个推销员是个喋喋不休的人，几乎没给我说话的机会。|近义词：chatterbox, blabbermouth, gabber |反义词：silent person, listener
