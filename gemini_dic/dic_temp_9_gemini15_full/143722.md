outlipping:/ˌaʊtˈlɪpɪŋ/| v.|超越，胜过|To surpass or exceed (something or someone) in amount, extent, or degree.|例句：Our sales outlipped those of our competitors last quarter.  我们的销售额上个季度超过了竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip|反义词：underperform, lag behind
