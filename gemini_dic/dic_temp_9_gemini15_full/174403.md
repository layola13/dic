quietish:/ˈkwaɪɪtɪʃ/| adj.|相当安静的，比较安静的|Relatively quiet; moderately quiet.  It suggests a degree of quietness that is less than complete silence but more than a moderate level of noise.|The library was quietish, only a few students were studying.|近义词：calm, still, hushed|反义词：noisy, loud, boisterous
