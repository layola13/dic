distortions:/dɪˈstɔːrʃənz/| n.|扭曲；变形；歪曲；失真|The act of distorting something or the state of being distorted.  It can refer to physical objects being misshapen, sounds being altered, or information being misrepresented.|例句：The funhouse mirrors created amusing distortions of our faces.  哈哈镜扭曲了我们的脸。|例句：The radio broadcast suffered from significant distortions due to static.  由于静电干扰，广播节目受到了严重的失真。|近义词：deformations, perversions, misrepresentations, falsifications|反义词：accuracy, truth, fidelity, authenticity|
