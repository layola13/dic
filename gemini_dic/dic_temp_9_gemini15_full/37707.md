companywide:/ˌkʌmpəniˈwaɪd/ | adj. | 全公司的，公司范围内的 | Relating to or affecting the whole of a company.  | Example: The companywide restructuring resulted in significant layoffs.  全公司范围内的重组导致了大量的裁员。  The new policy will be implemented companywide next month.  这项新政策将于下个月在全公司范围内实施。|  company-wide, across-the-board |  localized, departmental |  形容词
