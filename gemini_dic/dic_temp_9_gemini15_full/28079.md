capillaire:/ˌkæpɪˈleər/| n.|毛细血管；发状植物；糖浆|A capillaire is a thin hair-like structure, most commonly referring to a capillary blood vessel or a type of fern with fine, hair-like fronds.  It can also refer to a syrup made from this fern.|例句：The blood flowed through the fine capillaires.  血液流经细小的毛细血管。|例句：The capillaire syrup was a popular remedy in the past.  过去，发状植物糖浆是一种流行的疗法。|近义词：capillary (for the blood vessel meaning), maidenhair fern (for the plant meaning)|反义词：N/A
