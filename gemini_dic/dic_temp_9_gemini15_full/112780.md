lubricating: /ˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 润滑的；起润滑作用的 |  Describing something that is used to reduce friction between surfaces.  |  The lubricating oil kept the engine running smoothly.  润滑油使发动机平稳运行。 The lubricating properties of the grease are excellent.  这种润滑脂的润滑性能极佳。|  oily, greasy, smoothing | abrasive, rough |
