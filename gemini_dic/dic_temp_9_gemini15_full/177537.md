rebooting:/ˌriˈbuːtɪŋ/ | v. |重启；重新启动；|The act of restarting a computer or other electronic device to clear its memory and reload its operating system.  This can resolve various software or hardware issues. |例句：I had to reboot my computer after the software crashed. 我软件崩溃后不得不重启电脑。

例句：The server is being rebooted to fix the network problem. 服务器正在重启以修复网络问题。


近义词：restarting, restarting, relaunching | 反义词：shutting down, powering off


USAGE->

I reboot my phone every week. = 我每周都重启我的手机。
We reboot the server regularly. = 我们定期重启服务器。

一般过去时:
