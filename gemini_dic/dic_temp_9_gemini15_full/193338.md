seg: /sɛɡ/ | n. | 段，节；部分 | A portion or section of something larger; a distinct part. |例句：The document was divided into several segments.  这份文件被分成几个部分。|例句：The river flows in segments through the valley. 这条河分段流经山谷。|近义词：section, part, portion, division |反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The text is divided into segments. = 这篇文章被分成几段。
This segment of the highway is under construction. = 这段高速公路正在施工。

一般过去时:
The teacher divided the class into segments for the activity. = 老师把班级分成几组进行活动。
We segmented the market by age group. = 我们按年龄段细分了市场。

现在完成时:
They have segmented the data for analysis. = 他们已经将数据细分为用于分析。
We have completed this segment of the project. = 我们已经完成了项目的这一部分。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, she had already segmented her speech into key points. = 在演示文稿之前，她已经将她的演讲分成了几个要点。
They had segmented the market before launching the product. = 他们在推出产品之前已经细分了市场。

将来时:
We will segment the customer base by their purchasing habits. = 我们将根据客户的购买习惯细分客户群。
The company will segment the market into different demographics. = 公司将把市场细分为不同的群体。

虚拟语气:
If we segmented the data differently, we might get different results. = 如果我们对数据进行不同的分割，我们可能会得到不同的结果。
I wish we had segmented the market more effectively. = 我希望我们更有效地细分了市场。

被动语态:
The data was segmented by the software. = 数据由软件分割。
The market is being segmented by several companies. = 市场正在被几家公司细分。

疑问句:
How many segments are there in the report? = 报告中有多少部分？
Into how many segments will the project be divided? = 项目将被分成多少个部分？

否定句:
The data was not properly segmented. = 数据没有被正确地分割。
The report did not segment the information clearly. = 报告没有清晰地分割信息。
