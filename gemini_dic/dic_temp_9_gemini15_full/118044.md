menageries:/məˈnæʒəriːz/| n. |动物园；(尤指)收藏；杂乱无章的集合 | A menagerie is a collection of wild animals, typically kept in captivity for display or exhibition, often implying a diverse and somewhat chaotic collection.  It can also refer to a diverse collection of things, often suggesting a lack of order. |例句：The circus boasted a fascinating menagerie of exotic animals. 马戏团拥有一个令人着迷的奇异动物集合。

The old house contained a bizarre menagerie of antique furniture and forgotten trinkets.  这座老房子里收藏着各种奇奇怪怪的古董家具和被遗忘的小玩意儿。|近义词：zoo, collection, assortment |反义词：order, organization
