nanotechnologist:/ˌnænoʊˌtɛknɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |纳米技术专家 | A scientist or engineer who specializes in nanotechnology.  Nanotechnologists work at the atomic and molecular level to design and build materials and devices with unique properties. | Example:  Dr. Lee is a renowned nanotechnologist who has made significant contributions to the field of medical nanotechnology.  李博士是一位著名的纳米技术专家，他对医学纳米技术领域做出了重大贡献。|近义词：nanoscientist, nanomaterials engineer | 反义词：无
