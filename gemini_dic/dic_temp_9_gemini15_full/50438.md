deprecatingly:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 贬抑地；轻蔑地；自贬地 |  Expressing disapproval or belittling something.  It often implies a slight self-deprecation or a subtle way of expressing criticism. |

例句：He chuckled deprecatingly, "Oh, I'm just a humble nobody." 他轻蔑地笑了笑，“哦，我只是个默默无闻的小人物。”

例句：She deprecatingly waved away the compliment. 她轻描淡写地挥掉了赞美。


近义词：dismissively, condescendingly, apologetically (in some contexts), self-deprecatingly

反义词：approvingly, admiringly, enthusiastically

词性：副词
