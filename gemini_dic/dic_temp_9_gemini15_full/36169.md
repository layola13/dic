cofferdam:/ˈkɒfədæm/| n. |围堰，挡水坝 |A temporary structure built to keep water out of an area where construction is taking place.  It's typically used in underwater or water-adjacent construction projects. | The engineers built a cofferdam to allow them to repair the bridge pier.  工程师们建造了一个围堰以便他们修复桥墩。

|近义词：dam, dike, enclosure |反义词：None directly applicable.  |名词
