contaminates:/kənˈtæmɪneɪts/| vt.|污染；弄脏|To make something impure or unclean by polluting or poisoning it.|The factory contaminates the river with its waste.|近义词：pollute, pollute, infect|反义词：purify, cleanse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory contaminates the river. = 工厂污染了这条河。
He contaminates the food with his dirty hands. = 他用脏手弄脏了食物。

一般过去时:
The oil spill contaminated the coastline. =漏油污染了海岸线。
The contaminated water made many people sick. = 受污染的水使许多人生病。

现在完成时:
The accident has contaminated the soil. =事故已经污染了土壤。
The river has been contaminated for years. = 这条河已经被污染多年了。

过去完成时:
They discovered that the water had been contaminated before the testing. =他们发现水在检测前就已经被污染了。
The investigation revealed that the food had been contaminated in the factory. =调查显示食物是在工厂里被污染的。

将来时:
The new factory will contaminate the air if not properly managed. =如果管理不当，新工厂将会污染空气。
If they continue this practice, they will contaminate the groundwater. =如果他们继续这种做法，他们将会污染地下水。

虚拟语气:
If the water were not contaminated, we could drink it. =如果水没有被污染，我们就能喝它。
It's a shame that the air is contaminated; otherwise, it would be much cleaner. =空气被污染真是可惜，否则会干净得多。

被动语态:
The water supply was contaminated by the chemical spill. =供水被化学品泄漏污染了。
The soil has been contaminated by industrial waste for decades. =几十年来，土壤一直被工业废料污染。

疑问句:
Is the food contaminated? = 食物被污染了吗？
Has the water supply been contaminated by the flood? =洪水污染了供水吗？

否定句:
The air is not contaminated in this area. = 这个地区的空气没有被污染。
The report showed that the soil was not contaminated. =报告显示土壤没有被污染。
