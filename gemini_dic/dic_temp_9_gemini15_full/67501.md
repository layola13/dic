extracorporeal:/ˌɛkstrəˈkɔːrpəriəl/| adj.|体外的，体外循环的|Outside the body; performed outside the body.|Example: Extracorporeal shock wave lithotripsy is a non-invasive procedure to break up kidney stones.  体外冲击波碎石术是一种无创手术，用于粉碎肾结石。

Example: The patient underwent extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) to support their failing lungs. 病人接受了体外膜肺氧合 (ECMO) 来支持其衰竭的肺部。


近义词：external, outside

反义词：intracorporeal, internal
