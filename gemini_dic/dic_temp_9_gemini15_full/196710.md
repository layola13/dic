sewable: /ˈsoʊəbəl/ | adj. | 可缝的，适于缝纫的 | Capable of being sewn.  Something that is sewable is suitable for sewing, meaning it can be stitched together with a needle and thread.  | 例句：This fabric is very sewable; it drapes nicely and doesn't fray easily.  = 这块布料很容易缝纫；它垂坠性很好，而且不易磨损。

This lightweight material is sewable, making it perfect for summer clothing. = 这轻便的材料易于缝纫，非常适合夏季服装。


近义词：stitchable,  sewn


反义词：unsewn, unsewable


词性：形容词
