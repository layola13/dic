overreachers:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃərz/ | n. |越权者；权限过界者 |Those who exceed their authority or power.  People who try to do more than they are capable of, often resulting in failure. |例句：The overreachers in the company were eventually fired for their misconduct. 公司里的越权者最终因不当行为而被解雇。 The ambitious overreachers failed to achieve their goals because they underestimated the challenges. 野心勃勃的越权者未能实现他们的目标，因为他们低估了挑战。|近义词：transgressors, usurpers, encroachers|反义词：underachievers, conformists |名词
