exacerbated:/ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 加重的；恶化的；激怒的 | To make (a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling) worse.  | The drought exacerbated the existing food shortage. 干旱加剧了现有的粮食短缺。The news exacerbated her anxiety.  这条新闻加剧了她的焦虑。| aggravated, worsened, intensified | alleviated, mitigated, improved |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The stress exacerbates his health problems. = 压力加重了他的健康问题。
This policy exacerbates the inequality in society. =这项政策加剧了社会的不平等。

一般过去时:
The conflict exacerbated the already tense situation. = 冲突加剧了本已紧张的局势。
His harsh words exacerbated the argument. = 他尖刻的话语激化了争论。

现在完成时:
The recent rains have exacerbated the flooding. = 最近的雨水加剧了洪水。
Years of neglect have exacerbated the building's structural problems. = 多年的忽视加剧了建筑物的结构问题。

过去完成时:
The previous drought had exacerbated the water shortage. = 先前的干旱加剧了缺水问题。
Years of mismanagement had exacerbated the company's financial problems. = 多年的管理不善加剧了公司的财务问题。

将来时:
Further cuts in funding will exacerbate the crisis. = 进一步削减资金将加剧这场危机。
The expected heatwave will exacerbate the already high temperatures. = 预计的热浪将加剧本已居高不下的气温。

虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, the situation will only be exacerbated. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况只会恶化。
I wish the government hadn't enacted policies that exacerbated the problem. = 我真希望政府没有颁布加剧这个问题的政策。

被动语态:
The situation was exacerbated by the recent storms. = 最近的风暴加剧了局势。
The problem was exacerbated by a lack of communication. = 缺乏沟通加剧了这个问题。

疑问句:
Has the new law exacerbated the existing problem? = 新法律是否加剧了现有问题？
Will this decision exacerbate the tensions between the two countries? = 这个决定会加剧两国之间的紧张关系吗？

否定句:
The medication did not exacerbate his allergies. = 这药没有加重他的过敏。
The new policy did not exacerbate the existing inequalities. = 新政策并没有加剧现有的不平等。


PHRASE->

exacerbate the situation = 恶化局势
exacerbate feelings of = 加剧…的感觉
exacerbate existing inequalities = 加剧现有不平等
