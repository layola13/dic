rebunker:/rɪˈbʌŋkər/| v. |重新筑堡垒，重建掩体|To rebuild or reinforce a bunker or fortification.  The term often implies a hasty or emergency rebuilding, suggesting damage or destruction that necessitates the work. |例句：The soldiers rebunkered their position after the shelling. =炮击后，士兵们重建了他们的防御工事。|例句：The engineers were tasked with rebunkering the damaged coastal defenses. =工程师的任务是重建受损的沿海防御工事。|近义词：rebuild, reinforce, repair, fortify |反义词：destroy, demolish, raze
