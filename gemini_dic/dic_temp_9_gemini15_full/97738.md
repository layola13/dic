initialism:/ɪˈnɪʃəlɪzəm/ | n. |首字母缩略词|An initialism is an abbreviation formed from the initial letters of a name or phrase, pronounced as a series of letters (e.g.,  BBC,  CIA,  USA). Unlike an acronym, which is pronounced as a word (e.g., NATO,  LASER), an initialism is read letter by letter.  |Example:  The FBI is investigating the case. (The Federal Bureau of Investigation is investigating the case.)|近义词：abbreviation, acronym (but with a crucial distinction)|反义词：full form, complete name|
