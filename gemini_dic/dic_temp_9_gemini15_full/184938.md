retardure: /rɪˈtɑːrdʒər/ | n. | 延迟；阻滞；迟缓 | The slowing or delaying of something's progress or development.  This word is primarily used in scientific or technical contexts, referring to a delay in a process or reaction. It is less common in everyday language and may be considered somewhat archaic. |  The retardure of the chemical reaction was attributed to the low temperature.  化学反应的迟缓归因于低温。 The project suffered from a significant retardure due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，项目受到了严重的延误。 | delay, hindrance, impediment, obstruction | acceleration, advancement, furtherance | 名词
