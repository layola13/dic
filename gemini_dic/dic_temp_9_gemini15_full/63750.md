epicene:/ˈɛpɪsiːn/| adj.|阴阳同体的；雌雄难辨的；中性的| Of or relating to a person, animal, or plant that displays characteristics of both sexes; having characteristics of both male and female.  Also, in grammar, referring to nouns or pronouns that can refer to either sex. | Example:  The flower exhibits epicene characteristics, with both male and female reproductive organs.  The pronoun "they" is often used as an epicene pronoun. |近义词: androgynous, hermaphroditic, neuter|反义词:  unisexual, distinctly male/female|  词性: adjective
