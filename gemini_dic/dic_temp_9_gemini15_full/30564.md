ceramics:/səˈræmɪks/| n. |陶瓷制品；陶瓷学 |Ceramics are objects made from clay or other materials that are hardened by heat.  The study of ceramics and their manufacture is also called ceramics. |例句：She collects antique ceramics. 她收集古董陶瓷制品。|例句：He's studying ceramics at university. 他在大学学习陶瓷学。|近义词：pottery, earthenware, porcelain |反义词：无
