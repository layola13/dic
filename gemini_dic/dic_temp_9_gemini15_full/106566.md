/krʌʃ/ | vt. n. |压碎；碾碎；挫伤；镇压；迷恋 | To break (something) into small pieces by pressing or hitting it hard.  To suppress or defeat (someone or something) completely. A strong feeling of infatuation or love. |例句：He crushed the can with his foot. 他用脚踩扁了易拉罐。The army crushed the rebellion. 军队镇压了叛乱。She had a crush on her teacher. 她迷恋上了她的老师。|近义词：smash, crush, pulverize, defeat, conquer |反义词：build, create, support, encourage
