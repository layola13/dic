code-names:/ˈkoʊd neɪmz/| n. |代号；暗号|Secret names used to identify people, places, or things, especially in espionage or military operations.  Code-names are used to maintain secrecy and avoid revealing sensitive information.  |例句：The mission's code-name was "Operation Nightingale".  这个任务的代号是“夜莺行动”。

例句：The spy used a series of code-names to communicate with his handler.  间谍用一系列代号与他的联络人沟通。


近义词：aliases, pseudonyms, codenames, cover names |反义词：real names, true identities|名词
