intentioned:/ɪnˈtɛnʃənd/| adj.| 有意的；故意的；打算的 | Having a particular purpose or intention; deliberate.  |例句：His actions were well-intentioned, but ultimately unhelpful. 他的行为出发点是好的，但最终并没有什么帮助。
例句：It was an unintentionally hurtful comment.  那是一句无意中伤人的评论。


近义词：deliberate, purposeful, planned, designed | 反义词：accidental, unintentional, unplanned, careless | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is well-intentioned in its efforts to help the community. = 这家公司致力于帮助社区，其出发点是好的。
His intentions are good, but his methods are questionable. = 他的意图是好的，但他的方法值得怀疑。


一般过去时:
Her actions were well-intentioned, but they backfired. = 她的行为出发点是好的，但却适得其反。
He was well-intentioned, but his advice was unhelpful. = 他出发点是好的，但他的建议并没有什么帮助。


现在完成时:
They have been well-intentioned in their approach to the problem. = 他们解决这个问题的方法出发点是好的。
She has always been well-intentioned, even when her actions were flawed. = 她一直都心怀善意，即使她的行为存在缺陷。


过去完成时:
I had been well-intentioned, but I hadn't anticipated the consequences. = 我出发点是好的，但我没有预料到后果。
They had well-intentioned plans, but they were poorly executed. = 他们的计划出发点是好的，但执行得很糟糕。


将来时:
We will be well-intentioned in our future dealings with them. = 我们将来与他们交往时，出发点将会是好的。
He intends to be well-intentioned in his actions. = 他打算在行动中怀有善意。


虚拟语气:
If he were better-intentioned, he wouldn't have said that. = 如果他心肠更好一些，他就不会那样说了。
If I had been better-intentioned, I would have helped her. = 如果我的出发点更好一些，我会帮助她的。


被动语态:
His actions were perceived as well-intentioned. = 他的行为被认为是善意的。
The policy was well-intentioned but poorly implemented. = 这项政策出发点是好的，但执行得很糟糕。


疑问句:

Are you sure your intentions are well-intentioned? = 你确定你的意图是善意的吗？


否定句:
His intentions were not well-intentioned. = 他的意图并非出于好心。
The plan, though well-intentioned, was ultimately unsuccessful. = 尽管计划出发点是好的，但最终还是失败了。
