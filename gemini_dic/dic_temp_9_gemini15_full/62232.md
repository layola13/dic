encroaching:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 侵占的；逐渐蔓延的 | Gradually extending or spreading over (a place or area); intruding gradually | The encroaching darkness made it difficult to see.  逐渐蔓延的黑暗使能见度降低。| The encroaching desert threatens the village. 逐渐蔓延的沙漠威胁着村庄。 | encroaching, advancing, expanding, spreading | receding, withdrawing, contracting |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The desert is encroaching on the farmland. = 沙漠正在侵占农田。
His influence is encroaching on my territory. = 他的影响力正在侵犯我的领域。


一般过去时:
The sea encroached upon the coastline. = 海水侵蚀了海岸线。
The forest encroached slowly but surely on the village. = 森林缓慢但肯定地侵入了村庄。


现在完成时:
The city has encroached significantly on the surrounding countryside. = 城市已经大幅侵占了周围的乡村。
The vines have encroached upon the whole garden. = 藤蔓已经爬满了整个花园。


过去完成时:
The glacier had encroached far beyond its original boundary. = 冰川已经远超其原始边界侵蚀。
Before the dam was built, the river had encroached upon the village several times. = 在建坝之前，河水曾多次侵犯村庄。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will encroach on coastal communities. = 海平面上升将侵占沿海社区。
If we don't act, the weeds will encroach upon the entire field. = 如果我们不采取行动，杂草将蔓延整个田地。


虚拟语气:
If the government didn't act, the desert would encroach upon the entire region. = 如果政府不采取行动，沙漠将会侵占整个地区。
I wish the city hadn't encroached so much upon the natural landscape. = 我希望城市没有如此侵占自然景观。


被动语态:
The farmland was encroached upon by the expanding city. = 农田被扩张的城市侵占了。
The coastal areas are being encroached upon by rising sea levels. = 沿海地区正遭受海平面上升的侵蚀。


疑问句:
Is the development encroaching on the wildlife habitat? = 这项开发项目是否正在侵占野生动物栖息地？
Are the encroaching buildings affecting the view? = 这些逐渐蔓延的建筑物是否影响了视野？


否定句:
The government's policies do not allow the sea to encroach on the land. = 政府的政策不允许海水侵占土地。
The new regulations are designed to prevent the forest from encroaching on the farmland. = 新规旨在防止森林侵占农田。
