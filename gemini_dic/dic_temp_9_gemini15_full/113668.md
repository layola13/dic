machos:/ˈmɑːtʃoʊz/| n.|大男子主义者；强壮的男人|A macho person, especially a man, behaves in a way that is intended to show that they are strong and aggressive.  They often exhibit exaggerated masculinity. |例句：He's a bit of a macho man, always showing off his strength. 他有点大男子主义，总是炫耀他的力量。
例句：Some people find macho behavior unattractive. 有些人觉得大男子主义的行为很没有吸引力。|近义词：he-man, tough guy, bully|反义词：effeminate, weak, timid|名词
