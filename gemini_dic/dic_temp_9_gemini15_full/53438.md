disappointing:/ˌdɪs.əˈpɔɪn.tɪŋ/ | adj. |令人失望的 | causing a feeling of disappointment; not as good as one hoped for.  |例句：The movie was disappointing. = 这部电影令人失望。|例句：The results of the test were disappointing. = 测试结果令人失望。|近义词：unsatisfying, frustrating, disheartening |反义词：pleasing, satisfying, exhilarating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news is disappointing. = 这个消息令人失望。
The weather is disappointing today. = 今天的天气令人失望。


一般过去时:
