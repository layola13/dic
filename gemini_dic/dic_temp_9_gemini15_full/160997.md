postarthritic:/ˌpoʊstɑːrˈθrɪtɪk/| adj.|关节炎后的；关节炎后遗症的|relating to the period or condition after an attack of arthritis.  It describes the state of a joint, or a person, after the acute phase of arthritis has subsided, but potential lingering effects remain.|The patient is undergoing physiotherapy in the postarthritic phase of his recovery.  这位病人正在关节炎康复后的阶段进行理疗。|近义词：post-arthritic,  postinflammatory (if inflammation was a key feature)|反义词：arthritic (during active arthritis)
