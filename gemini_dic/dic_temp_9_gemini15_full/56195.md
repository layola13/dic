dodgems: /ˈdɒdʒəmz/ | n. |碰碰车 | Bumper cars; a type of amusement park ride where cars can bump into each other.  The term is primarily used in British English. | 例句：The children loved riding the dodgems at the fair. 孩子们喜欢在游乐场玩碰碰车。

例句：The dodgems were packed with excited riders. 碰碰车里挤满了兴奋的乘客。


近义词：bumper cars


反义词：无


词性：名词

词源:
The word "dodgems" is a shortened form of "dodge 'em,"  an imperative phrase suggesting that players should dodge (or avoid) collisions with other cars.  The origin reflects the nature of the ride.
