self-scrutinizing: /ˌsɛlfˈskruːtənaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我审查的；自我反省的 |  Engaging in critical examination of one's own actions, thoughts, or motives.  | 例句：His self-scrutinizing nature led him to constantly question his decisions.  他的自我反省的本性导致他不断质疑自己的决定。


例句：The self-scrutinizing process helped her understand her biases. 自我审查的过程帮助她理解了自己的偏见。


近义词：introspective, self-reflective, self-examining


反义词：uncritical, self-satisfied, complacent


词性：形容词
