fisherman:/ˈfɪʃərmən/ | n. | 渔民，钓鱼的人 | A person who catches fish, especially as a job.  | Example: The fisherman cast his net into the sea.  这位渔民把渔网撒入大海。 |近义词：angler, fisher | 反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fisherman catches fish every day. = 渔民每天都捕鱼。
He fishes in the river. = 他在河里钓鱼。


一般过去时:
The fisherman caught a big fish yesterday. = 渔民昨天钓到了一条大鱼。
They fished in the lake last weekend. = 他们上周末在湖里钓鱼。


现在完成时:
The fisherman has caught many fish this week. = 渔民本周已经钓到很多鱼了。
He has fished in this river many times. = 他在这条河里钓过很多次鱼了。


过去完成时:
The fisherman had already caught his limit before noon. = 中午之前，渔民就已经钓到限额了。
They had fished for hours before they caught anything. = 他们钓了几个小时才钓到东西。


将来时:
The fisherman will go fishing tomorrow. = 渔民明天要去钓鱼。
They will fish in the ocean next month. = 他们下个月将在海洋里钓鱼。


虚拟语气:
If I were a fisherman, I would live by the sea. = 如果我是个渔民，我会住在海边。
I wish I could fish as well as he does. = 我真希望我能像他一样钓得好。


被动语态:
The fish were caught by the fisherman. = 鱼被渔民钓到了。
The nets are being mended by the fisherman. = 渔网正在被渔民修理。


疑问句:
Is he a fisherman? = 他是渔民吗？
Where do the fishermen fish? = 渔民在哪里钓鱼？


否定句:
He is not a fisherman. = 他不是渔民。
They didn't fish today. = 他们今天没有钓鱼。


PHRASE->
fisherman's luck = 渔夫的运气 (好坏参半的运气)
fisherman's knot = 渔夫结 (一种牢固的绳结)
commercial fisherman = 商业渔民
fisherman's wharf = 渔民码头
a day's fishing = 一天的钓鱼
