legitimations:/ləˌdʒɪtɪˈmeɪʃənz/| n.|合法化；合法性；正统性|The act of making something legitimate or acceptable; the state of being legitimate.  It refers to the process of justifying or providing a valid reason for something, often related to power, authority, or actions. |例句：The government's legitimations for the war were unconvincing. 政府为战争辩护的理由令人难以信服。

例句：The company's legitimations for its environmental practices are under scrutiny.  这家公司关于其环保措施的论据正在接受审查。


近义词：justifications, validations, authorizations, rationalizations

反义词：invalidations, denials, refutations

词性:名词
