catharsis:/kəˈθɑːrsɪs/| n. |卡塔西斯；情感宣泄；净化 |The purging of emotions or relieving of emotional tensions, especially through certain kinds of art, as tragedy or music.  A release of emotional tension, as after an overwhelming experience.|例句：Watching the tragic play provided a catharsis for the audience.  观看这部悲剧为观众提供了一种情感宣泄。

例句：The intense workout provided a powerful catharsis, relieving stress and tension. 剧烈的运动提供了一种强大的情感宣泄，缓解了压力和紧张。


近义词：purification, purgation, release, relief


反义词：suppression, repression, bottling up


词性：名词
