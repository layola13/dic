non-scientific: /ˌnɑːnˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/ | adj. | 非科学的；不科学的 | Not based on or conforming to the methods or principles of science.  It often implies a lack of rigor, objectivity, or empirical evidence. | 例句：His claims are non-scientific and lack empirical support. 他的说法是非科学的，缺乏实证支持。

例句：The article presented a non-scientific explanation of the phenomenon. 这篇文章对这种现象给出了一个非科学的解释。


近义词：unscientific, unprofessional, empirical, anecdotal, subjective


反义词：scientific, objective, empirical, rigorous


词性：形容词
