pro-Italian: /ˌproʊ ɪˈtæliən/ | adj. | 亲意大利的，支持意大利的 | Favoring or supporting Italy or Italian interests.  The term often implies a positive stance towards Italy, its culture, or its people. | 例句：He held pro-Italian sentiments throughout the war. 他在整个战争期间都怀有亲意大利的感情。  | 近义词：Italo-phile, pro-Italianate | 反义词：anti-Italian


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pro-Italian. = 他亲意大利。
She supports pro-Italian policies. = 她支持亲意大利的政策。

一般过去时:
He was pro-Italian in his youth. = 他年轻时亲意大利。
The newspaper maintained a pro-Italian stance. = 这家报纸一直保持着亲意大利的立场。

现在完成时:
They have always been pro-Italian. = 他们一直都亲意大利。
The government has adopted a more pro-Italian foreign policy. = 政府采取了更加亲意大利的外交政策。

过去完成时:
He had been pro-Italian before changing his views. = 他在改变观点之前一直亲意大利。
The country had a history of being pro-Italian. = 这个国家历史上一直亲意大利。

将来时:
He will remain pro-Italian. = 他将继续亲意大利。
The alliance will likely adopt a more pro-Italian approach. = 这个联盟可能会采取更加亲意大利的方式。

虚拟语气:
If he were pro-Italian, he would support them. = 如果他亲意大利，他会支持他们。

被动语态:

否定句:
