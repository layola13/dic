ponchos:/ˈpɒntʃoʊz/ | n. |蓬巧（一种斗篷） |A poncho is a type of cloak, typically made from a single piece of fabric with a hole in the center for the head.  It's often worn as outerwear for warmth or protection from the elements. |She wore a bright red poncho to keep warm during the chilly evening. 她穿着一件鲜红色的蓬巧来抵御寒冷的夜晚。 |cloak, cape, shawl |(反义词较难定义，因为它并非与其他衣物构成直接对立)
