misdescribing: /ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/ | v. | 错误描述，误述 |  Describing something inaccurately or misleadingly.  It involves giving a false or incomplete account of something. |


例句：

He was misdescribing the situation to avoid responsibility. = 他在歪曲事实以逃避责任。

The witness was misdescribing the suspect's appearance. = 目击证人对嫌疑人的长相描述有误。


近义词：misrepresenting, mischaracterizing, distorting, twisting, falsifying


反义词：accurately describing, truthfully representing


词性：动词 (verb)
