eliminated:/ɪˈlɪmɪneɪtɪd/ | v. | 消除，排除，淘汰 | To remove or get rid of something completely.  | The doctor eliminated the possibility of a heart attack. 医生排除了心脏病发作的可能性。 |近义词: remove, eradicate, exclude, delete | 反义词: include, add, retain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They eliminate waste products daily. = 他们每天清除废物。
The company eliminates inefficient processes. = 公司淘汰低效流程。


一般过去时:
The problem was eliminated quickly. = 问题很快就被解决了。
She eliminated all the errors in her report. = 她消除了报告中的所有错误。


现在完成时:
They have eliminated most of the bugs in the software. = 他们已经消除了软件中的大部分错误。
We have eliminated the risk of infection. = 我们已经消除了感染的风险。


过去完成时:
They had eliminated the threat before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经消除了威胁。
She had eliminated all her competitors before the finals. = 决赛前她已经淘汰了所有竞争对手。


将来时:
We will eliminate all the obstacles. = 我们将消除所有障碍。
The new system will eliminate the need for manual input. = 新系统将消除手动输入的需要。


虚拟语气:
If we eliminated the tariff, trade would increase. = 如果我们取消关税，贸易就会增加。
I wish they had eliminated the unnecessary steps. = 我希望他们已经取消了不必要的步骤。


被动语态:
The virus was eliminated by the new drug. = 新药消除了病毒。
The outdated equipment was eliminated from the factory. = 过时的设备已被工厂淘汰。


疑问句:
Can we eliminate this unnecessary step? = 我们能消除这个不必要的步骤吗？
How can we eliminate the source of the problem? = 我们如何消除问题的根源？


否定句:
They did not eliminate the possibility of a mistake. = 他们没有排除出错的可能性。
The problem has not been eliminated yet. = 问题还没有解决。


PHRASE->
eliminate from = 从…中消除
eliminate the possibility of = 排除…的可能性
eliminate the risk of = 消除…的风险
eliminate competition = 消除竞争
eliminate the threat = 消除威胁
