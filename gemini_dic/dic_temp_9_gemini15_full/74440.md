francophone:/ˈfræŋkəfoʊn/| adj. n.|说法语的人；说法语的；使用法语的|A francophone is a person who speaks French.  It can also refer to things related to the French language or French-speaking cultures.  The term often describes countries, regions, or communities where French is the primary language.|例句：Canada has a large francophone population. 加拿大有大量的法语人口。


例句：Many francophone countries are located in Africa.许多说法语的国家位于非洲。

近义词：French-speaking

反义词：Anglophone (English-speaking)

词性: adjective, noun
