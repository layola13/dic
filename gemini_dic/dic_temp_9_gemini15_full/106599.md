kudu:/ˈkuːduː/| n. |大羚羊|A large African antelope with a distinctive coat of white stripes and spots.  Kudu are known for their impressive spiral horns. They are shy and elusive animals found in woodlands and savannas.|Example: We saw a majestic greater kudu during our safari in South Africa.  我们在南非的狩猎旅行中看到了一只雄伟的大羚羊。|近义词：antelope, gazelle|反义词：N/A
