maximizer:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzər/| n. |最大化者；追求最佳者|A maximizer is someone who always seeks the best possible option, even if it means spending more time and effort.  They strive for perfection and often experience decision paralysis.|例句：He's a maximizer, always agonizing over every little detail before making a decision. 他是一个追求最佳者，在做决定之前总是为每一个小细节而苦恼。She's a maximizer in her career, always striving for promotion and recognition.  她在事业上是一个追求最佳者，总是努力争取晋升和认可。|近义词：perfectionist, optimizer|反义词：satisficer
