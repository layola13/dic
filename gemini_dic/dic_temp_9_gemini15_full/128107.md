nestle:/ˈnesəl/| v. n.|依偎，偎依；安顿；舒适地安放；把…安顿在舒适的地方 |to settle oneself comfortably in a snug or sheltered place; to place something snugly or comfortably|The baby nestled in its mother's arms.  婴儿依偎在母亲的怀里。|The cottage nestled among the trees. 小屋依偎在树林中。|近义词：cuddle, snuggle, settle|反义词：unwind, unsettle
