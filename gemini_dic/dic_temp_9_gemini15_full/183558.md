requere:/rɪˈkwaɪər/| vt.|要求，需要|To need or demand something.  It implies a necessity or obligation. |例句：The job requires a high level of skill. 这份工作需要很高的技能。

例句：The regulations require that all employees wear safety glasses. 规章规定所有员工都必须戴安全眼镜。

近义词：need, demand, necessitate, call for

反义词：provide, supply, offer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The job requires a lot of patience. = 这份工作需要很大的耐心。
This recipe requires three eggs. = 这个食谱需要三个鸡蛋。

一般过去时:
The project required more funding than anticipated. = 这个项目需要的资金比预期的要多。
She required immediate medical attention. = 她需要立即进行医疗救治。

现在完成时:
The situation has required a change in strategy. = 情况需要改变策略。
We have required additional resources for the project. = 我们需要为项目增加额外的资源。

过去完成时:
The task required that I had completed the previous step. = 这项任务要求我已完成上一步。
They had required my presence at the meeting. = 他们要求我出席会议。

将来时:
The new system will require training for all staff. = 新系统将需要对所有员工进行培训。
The trip will require a valid passport. = 旅行需要有效的护照。

虚拟语气:
It would require a miracle for us to win. = 我们要赢需要奇迹。
If the project were to succeed, it would require additional funding. = 如果项目要成功，就需要额外的资金。

被动语态:
Additional staff is required to complete the project on time. = 需要额外的人员才能按时完成项目。
A high level of security is required. = 需要高水平的安全措施。

疑问句:
Does this job require any experience? = 这份工作需要任何经验吗？
What qualifications does this position require? = 这个职位需要什么资格？

否定句:
The task doesn't require any special skills. = 这项任务不需要任何特殊技能。
This application doesn't require personal information. = 此应用程序不需要个人信息。


PHRASE->
require of:  要求(某人)做……
require something from someone: 向某人索取某物
require that: 要求……
require attention: 需要注意
require immediate action: 需要立即采取行动


词源:
From Old French *requerir, from Latin requirere "to seek diligently, search for, demand," from re- "again" + quaerere "to seek."
