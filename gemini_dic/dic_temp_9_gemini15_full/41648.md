correctors:/kəˈrektərz/ | n. | 校正者；修正器 | People or things that correct errors or mistakes.  They can be individuals, software, or devices. | Example: The proofreaders acted as correctors, identifying and fixing errors in the manuscript. 校对员充当校正者，找出并纠正手稿中的错误。  The spell checker acted as a corrector, flagging misspelled words. 拼写检查器充当校正器，标记拼写错误的单词。|近义词：editors, proofreaders, revisers |反义词：error-makers,  distorters| 名词


USAGE->
