geology:/dʒiˈɒlədʒi/| n. |地质学|The science that deals with the earth's physical structure and substance, its history, and the processes that act on it.|例句：She is studying geology at university. 她正在大学学习地质学。|近义词：geophysics, earth science |反义词:N/A

Geology explains the formation of mountains. = 地质学解释了山脉的形成。
He studies geology in his spare time. = 他在业余时间学习地质学。

The geologist examined the rock samples. = 地质学家检查了岩石样本。
