prating:/ˈpreɪtɪŋ/| v.|喋喋不休，夸夸其谈 |  Meaning: To talk at length in a foolish or boastful way; to chatter idly.  | Example: He spent the evening prating about his accomplishments. 他整个晚上都在夸夸其谈他的成就。

Example: The children were prating incessantly about their day at school. 孩子们喋喋不休地谈论他们在学校的一天。| 近义词：babbling, chattering, boasting, garrulous | 反义词：silent, quiet, reserved, taciturn

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prates endlessly about politics. = 他没完没了地谈论政治。
She prates about her trip to Europe. = 她喋喋不休地谈论她去欧洲的旅行。

一般过去时:
He prated on for hours about his new car. = 他滔滔不绝地谈论他的新车好几个小时。
She prated about her problems all afternoon. = 她一下午都在喋喋不休地抱怨她的问题。


现在完成时:
He has prated about his success to everyone he knows. = 他已经向他认识的每个人夸耀他的成功。
She has prated incessantly since morning. = 她从早上就开始喋喋不休了。


过去完成时:
He had prated about his wealth before anyone even knew him. = 在任何人认识他之前，他就已经吹嘘他的财富了。
She had prated for hours before finally falling silent. = 她喋喋不休地说了好几个小时才最终安静下来。


将来时:
He will prate about his achievements at the ceremony. = 他将在仪式上夸耀他的成就。
She will prate endlessly about her new job. = 她将会没完没了地谈论她的新工作。


虚拟语气:
If he prated less, people would listen more. = 如果他少说些话，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't prate so much. = 我希望他不那么喋喋不休。


被动语态:
His accomplishments were prated about by everyone. = 他的成就被每个人都夸耀。
Her problems were prated about endlessly. = 她的问题被没完没了地谈论。


疑问句:
Is he prating again? = 他又在喋喋不休了吗？
Does she often prate about trivial matters? = 她经常喋喋不休地谈论琐事吗？


否定句:
He didn't prate about his failures. = 他没有谈论他的失败。
She didn't prate, but listened attentively. = 她没有喋喋不休，而是专心致志地听着。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "prating" originates from the Middle English word "praten," meaning "to chatter" or "to babble."  This, in turn, is derived from an Old French word with a similar meaning.
