conjoiner:/kənˈdʒɔɪnər/ | n. |连接词，连接器 | A word that connects words, phrases, clauses, or sentences.  It functions to show a relationship between the connected elements. Examples include conjunctions (and, but, or), prepositions (in, on, at), and some adverbs (however, therefore). |The conjoiner "and" links the two nouns in the phrase "bread and butter". |近义词：connector, linker, conjunction |反义词：separator, disconnector
