octodecimo:/ˌɒktəˈdɛsɪməʊ/| adj.|第十八；十八分之一|From Latin *octodecimus, from octodecem "eighteen," from octo "eight" + decem "ten."  It refers to something that is eighteenth in a series or a part of something divided into eighteen equal parts.| Example: The octodecimo edition of the book is highly sought after by collectors. 这本书的第十八版受到收藏家的高度追捧。|近义词：eighteenth|反义词：first, second (and so on)
