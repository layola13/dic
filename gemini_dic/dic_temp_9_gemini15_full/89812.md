hornbills:/ˈhɔːrnˌbɪlz/| n. |犀鸟|Hornbills are a family of birds characterized by a large, often colorful bill, sometimes with a casque. They are found in tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.|例句：The hornbill's impressive bill is used for attracting mates and cracking open nuts.  犀鸟令人印象深刻的喙用于吸引配偶和敲开坚果。
