metronomy:/ˈmɛtrənəmi/ | n. | 节拍器；节拍；节奏 | A device used to mark time in music, usually by producing a regular beat.  It helps musicians keep a steady tempo. | Example: The metronome clicked steadily, guiding the orchestra through the complex piece.  节拍器有节奏地咔哒作响，引导乐队演奏这首复杂的乐曲。|近义词：beat keeper, timekeeper |反义词：None  |名词
