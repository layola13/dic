reexhibits:/ˌriːɪɡˈzɪbɪts/| v.|重新展出，再次展出 | To exhibit again; to display something that has been exhibited before.  |例句：The museum will reexhibit the Van Gogh paintings next year. = 博物馆明年将重新展出梵高的画作。

例句：The artist decided to reexhibit his sculptures at a different gallery. = 艺术家决定在不同的画廊重新展出他的雕塑。


近义词：redisplay, reshow, exhibit again

反义词：remove, take down, conceal
