differentia:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃiə/| n. |本质区别，特性，差异 |The essential difference between two things.  A distinguishing characteristic or feature. |例句：The differentia between a square and a rectangle is that a square has four equal sides. 正方形和长方形的区别在于正方形有四个相等的边。

The differentia of this species is its bright red plumage.  这个物种的显著特征是它鲜红的羽毛。

|近义词：difference, distinction, characteristic, feature |反义词：similarity, resemblance |名词
