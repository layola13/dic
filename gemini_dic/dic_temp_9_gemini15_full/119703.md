midpoint:/ˈmɪd.pɔɪnt/| n.|中点，中位数|The point that is exactly halfway between two other points.  It can also refer to the middle value in a set of numbers.|例句：The midpoint of the line segment is 5.  这条线段的中点是5。

The midpoint of the range of scores was 75. 分数范围的中位数是75。|近义词：middle, center, median|反义词：endpoint, extremity
