level:/ˈlevəl/ | n. adj. | 水平；等级；层次 |  A stage in a process, a scale of measurement, or a position on a scale.  |例句：He's reached a high level of proficiency in English. 他已经达到了很高的英语水平。|例句：The water level in the river is rising. 河里的水位正在上涨。|近义词：stage, grade, rank, standard |反义词：bottom, base

The water level is high. = 水位很高。
His English level is intermediate. = 他的英语水平是中级。
