psychoanalyze:/ˌsaɪkoʊˈænəlaɪz/ | vt. |精神分析，心理分析 | To analyze (someone's) thoughts and feelings, typically by using the methods of psychoanalysis.  |例句：The therapist attempted to psychoanalyze the patient's dreams. 治疗师试图对病人的梦进行精神分析。 |例句：She decided to psychoanalyze her own childhood experiences to understand her current anxieties. 她决定剖析自己童年的经历来理解她目前的焦虑。|近义词：analyze, interpret, explore |反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He psychoanalyzes his patients weekly. = 他每周对他的病人进行精神分析。
The doctor psychoanalyzes the patient's behavior. = 医生分析病人的行为。

一般过去时:
Freud psychoanalyzed many famous people. = 弗洛伊德为许多名人做过精神分析。
