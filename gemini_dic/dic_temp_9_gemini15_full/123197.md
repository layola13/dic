monochlamydeous:/ˌmɒnəklæˈmɪdiəs/| adj.|单被片的；单轮被的|Having only one perianth (floral envelope).  This refers to flowers that possess only one whorl of perianth segments, either sepals or petals, not both.  Many wind-pollinated flowers are monochlamydeous.|例句：Many wind-pollinated flowers, such as grasses, are monochlamydeous.|近义词：haplostemonous (in reference to stamens)|反义词：dichlamydeous
