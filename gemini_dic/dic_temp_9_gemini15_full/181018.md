reimprisonment:/ˌriːɪmˈprɪzənmənt/| n. |再次监禁；重新入狱 |The act of imprisoning someone again after they have been released.  This often happens due to parole violations or new charges.  |例句：His reimprisonment was a result of violating his parole. 他的再次监禁是因为违反了假释规定。
|例句：After a brief period of freedom, she faced reimprisonment for her past crimes. 在短暂的自由后，她因过去的罪行面临再次监禁。|近义词：reincarceration, reconfinement |反义词：release, liberation |词性 n.
