saunter:/ˈsɔːntər/| /ˈsɑːntər/| v. n.|闲逛；漫步|To walk in a leisurely and relaxed way.|例句：He sauntered down the street, enjoying the warm sunshine. 他沿着街道漫步，享受着温暖的阳光。  例句：Her saunter through the park was a welcome break from her busy schedule.她在公园里的漫步是她繁忙日程中令人欢迎的休息。|近义词：stroll, amble, stroll, wander|反义词：hurry, rush, sprint|动词

一般现在时:
He saunters through the park every morning. = 他每天早上在公园里散步。
They saunter along the beach, enjoying the sunset. = 他们沿着海滩漫步，欣赏日落。

一般过去时:
She sauntered into the room and greeted everyone. = 她漫步走进房间，向大家问好。
We sauntered through the market, looking at the stalls. = 我们在市场上闲逛，看着摊位。


现在完成时:
He has sauntered along this path many times. = 他已经沿着这条小路散步过很多次了。
They have sauntered through the town and found a nice café. = 他们已经在镇上闲逛，找到了一家不错的咖啡馆。


过去完成时:
She had sauntered into the garden before the rain started. = 下雨之前，她已经在花园里散步了。
They had sauntered along the riverbank before they decided to have a picnic. = 他们在决定野餐之前，已经在河边漫步了。


将来时:
We will saunter along the coast tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海岸边漫步。
He will saunter through the museum, admiring the artwork. = 他将在博物馆里闲逛，欣赏艺术品。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would saunter through the countryside. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会在乡下漫步。
I wish I could saunter along the beach right now. = 我真希望现在能沿着海滩漫步。


被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
The path is often sauntered along by locals. = 这条小路经常被当地人漫步。 (较为生硬)
The park was seldom sauntered through in the evenings. = 晚上很少有人在公园里闲逛。(较为生硬)


疑问句:
Will you saunter with me this evening? = 你今晚和我一起散步吗？
Does she often saunter along the river? = 她经常沿着河边散步吗？


否定句:
He didn't saunter; he ran. = 他没有散步；他是跑的。
They did not saunter through the city, but took a bus. = 他们没有在城市里闲逛，而是乘公共汽车。


PHRASE->
saunter along: 沿着...漫步
saunter through: 穿过...漫步
saunter down: 走下...漫步
saunter off: 闲逛离开
saunter around: 在...周围闲逛

词源:  The word "saunter" ultimately derives from the Old French word "sainte terre," meaning "holy land."  It's believed that pilgrims to the Holy Land would often take leisurely walks, and the term eventually evolved to describe a relaxed, unhurried stroll.
