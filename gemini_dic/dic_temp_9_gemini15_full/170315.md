proverbs:/ˈprɑːvɜːrbz/|/ˈprɒvɜːbz/| n. |谚语；格言|Short, popular sayings that express a common truth or piece of advice. Proverbs often use figurative language and imagery to make their point memorable.  They are a part of a culture's oral tradition and often reflect its values and beliefs.|Example: "A bird in the hand is worth two in the bush."  (手中的一只鸟胜过树上的两只。)  |近义词：saying, maxim, adage, dictum |反义词：N/A
