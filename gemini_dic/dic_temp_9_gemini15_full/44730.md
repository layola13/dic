cruising:/ˈkruːzɪŋ/ | n., v. |巡航；漫游；闲逛 |


英文解析:
As a noun, "cruising" refers to the act of sailing or traveling at a steady speed, especially on a journey for pleasure.  It can also refer to driving around in a car without a specific destination, or casually searching for something or someone.  As a verb, "cruising" means to sail or travel at a steady speed, often for pleasure or leisure. It also implies a relaxed and unhurried pace.


例句:
They enjoyed a relaxing cruise in the Caribbean. = 他们在加勒比海享受了一次轻松的巡航。

We spent the afternoon cruising around town. = 我们下午在城里四处闲逛。


近义词:
sailing, touring, traveling, driving, meandering, roaming, gliding


反义词:
rushing, hurrying, speeding, racing


词性:
Noun, Verb
