This is often associated with infections, particularly tuberculosis.  |Example: Caseation necrosis is a characteristic feature of tuberculosis. 干酪样坏死是结核病的一个特征。The lung showed extensive caseation.肺部显示广泛的干酪样变性。|近义词：necrosis, degeneration|反义词：regeneration, healing|名词
