rowdiness:/ˈraʊdinis/| n. |喧闹；吵闹；粗鲁 |Noisy and disorderly behavior.  The rowdiness of the crowd made it difficult to hear the speakers.|近义词：turbulence, boisterousness, clamor, racket |反义词：quietness, calmness, peacefulness
