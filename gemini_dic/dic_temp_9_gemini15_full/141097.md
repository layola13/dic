onshore:/ˈɒnʃɔːr/ /ˈɑːnʃɔːr/| adj. adv. n.| 陆上的；近岸的；在岸上的；|Relating to or situated on land; not offshore.  It can also refer to activities or operations that take place on land, as opposed to at sea or offshore.| 例句：The onshore wind farm generates clean energy. 陆上风力发电场产生清洁能源。
The company moved its operations onshore to reduce costs. 公司将业务转移到岸上以降低成本。


近义词：coastal, inland, terrestrial|反义词：offshore, maritime
