demonstrate: /ˈdɛmənstreɪt/ | vt. | 演示；证明；说明；展示 | To show clearly; to explain or make clear by examples or experiments; to show (something) to be true. | 例句：He demonstrated how to use the new software. 他演示了如何使用新软件。 | 例句：The experiment demonstrated the effectiveness of the new drug. 实验证明了新药的有效性。 | 近义词：show, illustrate, prove, exhibit | 反义词：conceal, hide, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demonstrates his skills every day. = 他每天都展示他的技能。
The teacher demonstrates the experiment to the students. = 老师向学生演示实验。

一般过去时:
She demonstrated her point with a clear example. = 她用一个清晰的例子证明了她的观点。
The scientist demonstrated the theory in his presentation. = 科学家在他的演示中论证了该理论。

现在完成时:
They have demonstrated their commitment to the project. = 他们已经证明了他们对项目的承诺。
We have demonstrated the effectiveness of this approach. = 我们已经证明了这种方法的有效性。

过去完成时:
He had demonstrated his talent before the competition. = 他在比赛前就已经展现了他的才华。
Scientists had demonstrated the existence of the particle before the announcement. = 科学家在宣布之前就已经证明了粒子的存在。

将来时:
She will demonstrate the new technique at the conference. = 她将在会议上演示这项新技术。
The professor will demonstrate the principles of physics. = 教授将演示物理学原理。

虚拟语气:
If he demonstrated his skills, he would get the job. = 如果他展示了他的技能，他就能得到这份工作。
I wish I had demonstrated more confidence during the interview. = 我希望我在面试中表现出更多的自信。

被动语态:
The new product was demonstrated to potential customers. = 新产品向潜在客户进行了展示。
The theory has been demonstrated to be correct. = 该理论已被证明是正确的。

疑问句:
Can you demonstrate how this machine works? = 你能演示一下这台机器是如何工作的吗？
Will the company demonstrate its commitment to sustainability? = 公司会展示其对可持续发展的承诺吗？

否定句:
He did not demonstrate sufficient understanding of the problem. = 他没有充分展现对问题的理解。
The data did not demonstrate a significant difference between the two groups. = 数据没有显示两组之间有显著差异。


PHRASE->

demonstrate one's loyalty = 表达忠诚
demonstrate the importance = 证明重要性
demonstrate a skill = 展示技能
demonstrate one's ability = 展示能力
