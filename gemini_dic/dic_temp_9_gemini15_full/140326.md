ogrishly:/ˈoʊɡrɪʃli/| adv.|粗暴地；凶狠地；令人厌恶地|In a manner suggesting the characteristics of an ogre; brutally, cruelly, or repulsively.  It implies a harsh, unpleasant, and often violent demeanor.|例句：He glared ogrishly at his opponent. 他凶狠地瞪着他的对手。


例句：She spoke ogrishly about her ex-husband. 她恶毒地谈论她的前夫。


近义词：brutally, cruelly, savagely, fiercely, menacingly, menacingly, ferociously


反义词：gently, kindly, sweetly, softly, tenderly


词性:副词
