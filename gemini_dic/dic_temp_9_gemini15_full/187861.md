rosined:/ˈrɒzɪnd/| adj.|涂松香的；用松香处理过的|Covered or treated with rosin.  Rosin is a resin obtained from various trees, often used to increase friction on surfaces, such as violin bows.|例句：The violinist rosined his bow before the performance.  这位小提琴家在演出前给琴弓涂了松香。

The dancer's shoes were rosined to improve grip on the floor.  舞者的鞋子涂了松香以增强地板抓地力。|近义词：resin-treated|反义词：unrosined|形容词
