duetting:/ˈdjuːtɪŋ/| v.|二重唱，对唱|The act of singing a duet;  a musical performance where two voices or instruments perform together in harmony or counterpoint.  It often implies a sense of collaboration and interplay between the performers.|例句：The birds were duetting beautifully in the early morning.  清晨鸟儿们正在优美地对唱。|例句：The soprano and tenor gave a breathtaking duetting performance.  女高音和男高音带来了一场令人叹为观止的对唱表演。|近义词：singing together, harmonizing, counterpointing|反义词：soloing, monologuing
