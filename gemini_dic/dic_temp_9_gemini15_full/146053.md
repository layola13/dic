overdub:/ˈoʊvərdʌb/ | vt. |后期配音，叠录 |To record new sound onto an existing recording, typically to add a voiceover, sound effects, or music.  This process is commonly used in filmmaking, music production, and voice acting.  |例句：The director decided to overdub the actor's lines because his pronunciation was unclear.  导演决定为演员的台词进行后期配音，因为他的发音不清楚。

例句：We overdubbed some extra sound effects to enhance the atmosphere of the scene. 我们叠录了一些额外的音效来增强场景的气氛。


近义词：re-record, dub, layer | 反义词：N/A | 词性: verb
