reexpanding:/ˌriːɪkˈspændɪŋ/| v. |重新扩张；再次膨胀 |The act of expanding something again after it has been contracted or reduced.  It implies a return to a previous state or a continuation of an earlier expansion.|例句：The economy is showing signs of reexpanding after the recent recession.  经济在最近的衰退之后显示出重新扩张的迹象。

The company is reexpanding its operations into new markets. 公司正在将其业务重新扩张到新的市场。


近义词：re-expanding, expanding again,  resuming expansion


反义词：contracting, shrinking, reducing


词性:动词
