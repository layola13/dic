multiblade:/ˈmʌltiˌbleɪd/| n. |多叶片的；多刃的 |A multiblade tool or device has more than one blade.  This term is typically used to describe tools or equipment with multiple cutting or rotating blades, such as a multiblade fan or a multiblade knife.|例句：The food processor has a multiblade system for efficient chopping.  这款食品加工机具有多叶片系统，可高效切碎食物。

The lawnmower features a multiblade cutting mechanism for a cleaner cut. 这款割草机采用多叶片切割机构，切割更干净。|近义词：multiple-bladed, many-bladed |反义词：single-bladed
