reaches:/riːtʃɪz/ | v. | 到达；伸手够到；延伸；影响 | To arrive at a place, especially after traveling a long distance. To extend to a particular point or place. To achieve a particular level or standard. To successfully communicate with someone. |


例句：

The train reaches the station at 7:00 p.m.  = 火车晚上七点到达车站。

He reaches for the book on the shelf. = 他伸手去够书架上的书。


近义词：arrives, attains, achieves, extends

反义词：misses, fails to reach


USAGE->

一般现在时:

The river reaches the sea. = 这条河汇入大海。

She reaches for the phone. = 她伸手去拿电话。


一般过去时:

The explorers reached the summit. = 探险家们到达了山顶。

He reached out to help his friend. = 他伸出手来帮助他的朋友。


现在完成时:

The climbers have reached the top of Mount Everest. = 登山队员已经到达了珠穆朗玛峰顶。

She has reached a high level of proficiency in English. = 她在英语方面已达到很高的水平。


过去完成时:

They had reached a decision before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经做出了决定。

By the time I arrived, he had already reached home. = 我到达的时候，他已经到家了。


将来时:

The train will reach the next station in ten minutes. = 火车十分钟后将到达下一站。

We will reach our destination by nightfall. = 我们将在夜幕降临前到达目的地。


虚拟语气:

If I reached out, would you help me? = 如果我伸出手，你会帮助我吗？

I wish I had reached the summit. = 我真希望我到达了山顶。


被动语态:

The agreement was reached after many negotiations. = 经过多次谈判后达成了协议。

The top of the mountain is easily reached by cable car. = 山顶很容易乘缆车到达。


疑问句:

Does the road reach the village? = 这条路通向那个村庄吗？

Have you reached a conclusion yet? = 你得出结论了吗？


否定句:

The letter didn't reach its destination. = 这封信没有到达目的地。

He didn't reach the required standard. = 他没有达到规定的标准。


PHRASE->

within reach: 在触手可及的范围内

out of reach: 够不着；无法达到

reach out to: 伸出援手；联系

reach an agreement: 达成协议

reach a conclusion: 得出结论
