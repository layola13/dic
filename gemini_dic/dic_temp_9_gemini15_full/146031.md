overdraws:/ˈoʊvərˌdrɑːz/| v.|透支；超过限额；|To withdraw more money from a bank account than is available, resulting in a negative balance.|例句：He overdrew his checking account by $500. 他支票账户透支了500美元。

例句：The company overdrew its credit line, leading to financial difficulties. 公司透支了信用额度，导致财务困难。


近义词：overspend, exceed, exceed the limit|反义词：underspend, deposit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overdraws his account regularly. = 他定期透支他的账户。
She overdraws her credit card frequently. = 她经常透支她的信用卡。


一般过去时:
I overdrew my account last month. = 我上个月透支了我的账户。
They overdrew their business account. = 他们透支了他们的公司账户。


现在完成时:
He has overdrawn his account several times this year. = 他今年已经几次透支了他的账户。
She has never overdrawn her savings account. = 她从未透支过她的储蓄账户。


过去完成时:
I realized I had overdrawn my account after I received the statement. = 收到对账单后，我才意识到我透支了账户。
They discovered they had overdrawn their account before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前发现他们透支了账户。


将来时:
If I don't deposit more money, I will overdraw my account next week. = 如果我不存入更多钱，下周我的账户就会透支。
She anticipates she will overdraw her account if she continues spending at this rate. = 如果她继续以这种速度花钱，她预计她的账户会透支。


虚拟语气:
If I had known I was so close to my limit, I wouldn't have overdrawn my account. = 如果我知道我离限额这么近，我不会透支我的账户。
I wish I hadn't overdrawn my account; now I have to pay fees. = 我真希望我没有透支我的账户；现在我必须支付费用。


被动语态:
His account was overdrawn due to insufficient funds. = 由于资金不足，他的账户透支了。
The account was frequently overdrawn, prompting bank action. = 账户经常透支，促使银行采取行动。


疑问句:
Did you overdraw your account? = 你透支你的账户了吗？
Has she ever overdrawn her credit card? = 她曾经透支过信用卡吗？


否定句:
I didn't overdraw my account this month. = 我这个月没有透支我的账户。
They did not overdraw their account despite their high spending. = 尽管他们花费很高，但他们并没有透支账户。
