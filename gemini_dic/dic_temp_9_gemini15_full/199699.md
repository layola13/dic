siphonium:/saɪˈfoʊniəm/| n. |虹吸管；虹吸装置|A siphonium is a tube or other device used to convey liquid upwards and then downwards by air pressure,  allowing liquid to flow from a higher to a lower level even over an intervening elevation.|例句：The siphonium was used to transfer water from the reservoir to the lower fields.  虹吸管被用来将水从水库转移到下面的田地。|近义词：siphon, tube, pipe |反义词：N/A
