monarchical:/məˈnɑːkɪkəl/ | adj. | 君主制的；君主统治的 | Relating to a monarch or monarchy; characteristic of a monarchy.  | Example: The country has a strong monarchical tradition.  这个国家有强大的君主制传统。 |近义词：regal, royal, kingly, queenly |反义词：republican, democratic
