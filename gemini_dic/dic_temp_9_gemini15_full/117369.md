mediates:/ˈmiːdieɪts/| vt.| 调解；斡旋；促成 |To mediate is to bring about an agreement or reconciliation between people or groups, often by acting as a go-between.  It can also refer to the act of transmitting or conveying something. |例句：The UN is mediating in the peace talks.联合国正在调解和平谈判。

The teacher mediated a dispute between two students.老师调解了两个学生之间的纠纷。


近义词：arbitrate, reconcile, negotiate, intercede |反义词：aggravate, exacerbate, inflame |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mediates disputes between neighbors. 他调解邻居间的纠纷。
The government mediates trade agreements. 政府调解贸易协议。


一般过去时:
She mediated the conflict peacefully. 她和平地调解了冲突。
They mediated a settlement between the two companies. 他们促成了两家公司之间的和解。


现在完成时:
The mediator has mediated several successful settlements. 调解员已经成功调解了几起纠纷。
We have mediated this issue for months. 我们已经调解这个问题好几个月了。


过去完成时:
Before the intervention, they had mediated several times without success. 在干预之前，他们已经调解过几次，但没有成功。
The judge had mediated similar cases before. 法官之前曾调解过类似的案件。


将来时:
He will mediate the upcoming negotiations. 他将调解即将到来的谈判。
The organization will mediate the conflict between the two tribes. 该组织将调解两个部落之间的冲突。


虚拟语气:
If he mediated the conflict, it might have ended differently. 如果他调解了冲突，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish someone had mediated the argument before it escalated. 我希望有人在争吵升级之前就进行调解。


被动语态:
The dispute was mediated by a neutral third party. 纠纷由中立的第三方调解。
The peace agreement was mediated by international observers. 和平协议由国际观察员促成。


疑问句:
Will someone mediate the conflict? 会有人调解这场冲突吗？
Who will mediate the talks? 谁将调解会谈？


否定句:
He did not mediate the argument. 他没有调解这场争论。
The government refused to mediate the trade dispute. 政府拒绝调解贸易纠纷。


PHRASE->
mediate a dispute 调解纠纷
mediate a conflict 调解冲突
mediate between  在…之间调解
mediate an agreement 促成协议
mediate a solution 促成解决方案
