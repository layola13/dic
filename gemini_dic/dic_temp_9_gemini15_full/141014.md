onetime:/ˈwʌntaɪm/| adj.|一次性的；仅此一次的；从前的；过去的|  Onetime refers to something that happens or exists only once, or to something that happened or existed in the past.  It can also describe a single payment or event.|例句：This is a onetime offer, so don't miss it.  这是一个一次性的优惠，所以不要错过。

例句：He was a onetime champion, but he's retired now. 他曾经是冠军，但现在已经退役了。

近义词：single, once-only, former, past

反义词：recurring, repeated, ongoing, permanent

词性: adjective
