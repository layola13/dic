creeper:/ˈkriːpər/| n. v.|爬行者；蔓生植物；偷偷摸摸的人；（游戏）潜行玩家|英文解析：A creeper can refer to a plant that grows by creeping along the ground or climbing, a person who moves stealthily or secretly, or a type of player in video games who moves slowly and secretly to attack others.  |例句：The ivy is a fast-growing creeper.  常春藤是一种生长迅速的爬藤植物。

The thief was a sneaky creeper, moving silently through the shadows.  那个贼是个鬼鬼祟祟的家伙，在阴影中悄无声息地移动。

近义词：climber, crawler, stalker, prowler|反义词：sprinter, runner
