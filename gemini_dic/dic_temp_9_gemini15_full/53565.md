disbelieve:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːv/ | vt. |不相信，怀疑 | To refuse to believe something; to doubt the truth of something. |例句：I disbelieve his story completely.  我完全不相信他的故事。|例句：Many people disbelieve the claims made by the politician.  许多人不相信这位政治家提出的说法。|近义词：doubt, mistrust, question, suspect |反义词：believe, accept, trust


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disbelieves everything the media says. = 他不相信媒体说的任何话。
They disbelieve her explanation of the incident. = 他们不相信她对事件的解释。


一般过去时:
I disbelieved her at first, but later I changed my mind. = 我一开始不相信她，但后来我改变了主意。
The jury disbelieved the defendant's testimony. = 陪审团不相信被告的证词。


现在完成时:
I have disbelieved this rumour since it was first spread. = 从这个谣言第一次传播以来，我一直都不相信。
She has disbelieved his promises for years. = 她多年来一直不相信他的承诺。


过去完成时:
I had disbelieved him before, so I wasn't surprised when he lied again. = 我以前就不相信他，所以当他再次撒谎时我并不惊讶。
They had disbelieved the warning signs, and now they were paying the price. = 他们以前不相信警告信号，现在他们为此付出了代价。


将来时:
I will disbelieve any further claims without proof. = 我将不相信任何没有证据的进一步说法。
They will disbelieve him unless he provides concrete evidence. = 除非他提供确凿证据，否则他们将不相信他。


虚拟语气:
If I disbelieved everything I heard, I would be very lonely. = 如果我不相信我听到的一切，我会非常孤独。
I wish I had disbelieved his lies sooner. = 我希望我早点不相信他的谎言。


被动语态:
His story was disbelieved by everyone. = 他的故事不被任何人相信。
Her claims were disbelieved by the scientific community. = 她的说法不被科学界相信。


疑问句:
Do you disbelieve what I'm saying? = 你不相信我说的话吗？
Does he disbelieve the evidence presented? = 他不相信所提供的证据吗？


否定句:
I don't disbelieve everything he says, only some of it. = 我并非不相信他说的每一句话，只是其中一部分。
She doesn't disbelieve in the possibility of alien life. = 她不否认外星生命存在的可能性。


PHRASE->
disbelieve wholeheartedly = 全盘否定
disbelieve completely = 完全不相信
disbelieve implicitly = 绝对不相信
disbelieve utterly = 彻底不相信
disbelieve strongly = 强烈怀疑
