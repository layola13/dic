This term often carries a negative connotation, implying artificiality or an excessive adherence to stereotypes. |例句：Her hyperfeminineness felt contrived and unnatural.  她的超女性化感觉做作而不自然。

例句：The advertisement's portrayal of hyperfeminineness is a tired trope. 这则广告对超女性化的刻画是一个老套的比喻。
