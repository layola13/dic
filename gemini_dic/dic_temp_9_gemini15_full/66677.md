exodus:/ˈɛksədəs/ | n. |大批离去；大规模外流；出埃及记 | A mass departure of people, especially emigrants.  The term often implies a hurried or forced movement.  It can also refer to the biblical story of the Israelites' departure from Egypt. | 例句：The exodus from the city center began after the earthquake. 地震后，市中心开始出现大批民众撤离。

There was a mass exodus of refugees fleeing the war-torn country.  大批难民逃离饱受战争蹂躏的国家。| 近义词：departure, emigration, flight, migration | 反义词：immigration, influx, arrival
