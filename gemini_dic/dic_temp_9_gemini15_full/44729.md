cruises:/ˈkruːzɪz/| n.|航行；巡游；游轮旅行|A cruise is a journey on a ship or boat, usually for pleasure.  It can refer to the journey itself or to a type of vacation involving such a journey.|例句：We went on a Caribbean cruise last year.  去年我们去加勒比海进行了邮轮旅行。|例句：The cruise ship is massive and luxurious. 这艘游轮巨大而奢华。|近义词：voyage, trip, voyage, sailing|反义词：staying home, land travel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cruises the Mediterranean every summer. 他每年夏天都在地中海巡游。
She cruises the city streets in her new car. 她开着她的新车在城市街道上兜风。


一般过去时:
They cruised the coast of California. 他们沿着加利福尼亚海岸航行。
We cruised down the river in a small boat. 我们乘坐小船沿河而下。


现在完成时:
They have cruised to many exotic islands. 他们已经去过许多异国情调的岛屿巡游。
She has cruised the Caribbean Sea several times. 她已经多次游览加勒比海。


过去完成时:
He had cruised the Pacific before settling down. 他在定居之前曾经在太平洋上航行过。
They had cruised through the canals of Venice. 他们曾经游览过威尼斯的运河。


将来时:
We will cruise the fjords of Norway next year. 我们明年将游览挪威峡湾。
They will cruise the Aegean Sea on their honeymoon. 他们将在蜜月期间游览爱琴海。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would cruise the world. 如果我有钱，我会环游世界。
I wish I could cruise to the Bahamas this winter. 我希望今年冬天能去巴哈马群岛巡游。


被动语态:
The ship is cruised by many tourists every year. 这艘船每年都被许多游客乘坐。
The Mediterranean is cruised extensively during the summer months. 地中海在夏季会被广泛地巡游。


疑问句:
Does he cruise often? 他经常巡游吗？
Have you ever cruised on a luxury liner? 你曾经乘坐过豪华邮轮吗？


否定句:
They did not cruise last summer. 他们去年夏天没有巡游。
We will not cruise to Alaska this year. 我们今年不会去阿拉斯加巡游。
