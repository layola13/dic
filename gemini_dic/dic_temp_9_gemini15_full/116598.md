matriculator: /məˈtrɪkjuleɪtər/ | n. |入学注册者；新生；录取者 | A person who has matriculated; a newly enrolled student. |例句：He was a matriculator at Oxford University. 他是牛津大学的新生。|近义词：freshman, newcomer, enrollee |反义词：graduate, alumnus


USAGE->

The matriculator attends orientation. = 入学注册者参加了新生入学指导。
He matriculates at a prestigious university. = 他在一所名牌大学入学注册。

一般过去时:
She matriculated last year. = 她去年入学注册。
