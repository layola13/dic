draftsmanship:/ˈdræftsmənʃɪp/| n. |绘图技巧；制图技术；设计技巧|The skill or art of drawing plans, especially architectural or engineering plans.  It encompasses the ability to create clear, accurate, and aesthetically pleasing drawings.  This involves a deep understanding of technical drawing conventions, perspective, proportion, and the effective use of tools and techniques.|The draftsmanship in the blueprints was impeccable, showcasing the architect's mastery of detail.|近义词：drawing skill, design skill, artistry |反义词：clumsiness, inaccuracy
