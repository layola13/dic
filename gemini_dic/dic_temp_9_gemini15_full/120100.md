mimesis: /ˈmɪmɪsɪs/ | n. | 模仿，模拟，再现 | The imitation of the natural world in art or literature.  The act of representing, or the process of being represented.  It encompasses both the creation of art that imitates reality and the reception of art as an act of imitation. |  The painter's mimesis of nature was remarkably accurate.  这位画家的自然模仿非常精准。  The novel's mimesis of 19th-century London life was convincing. 这部小说的19世纪伦敦生活再现令人信服。 | imitation, representation, simulation |  originality, abstraction | 名词
