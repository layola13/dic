postlicentiate: /ˌpoʊstlaɪˈsɛnʃieɪt/ | n. | 准博士，已通过博士资格考试但尚未完成论文者 | A person who has passed the examinations for a doctorate but has not yet completed the dissertation or thesis required for the degree.  They are at the stage after passing their licensing exams but before officially receiving their doctorate. | Example: He is a postlicentiate in physics, diligently working on his doctoral thesis. 他是物理学准博士，正在努力撰写他的博士论文。| 近义词：doctoral candidate, PhD candidate | 反义词：doctor, PhD graduate
