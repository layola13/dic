psychoanalytic: /ˌsaɪkoʊəˈnælɪtɪk/ | adj. | 心理分析的；精神分析的 | Relating to or characteristic of psychoanalysis.  It involves interpreting thoughts, feelings, and behavior in terms of unconscious motives and conflicts. | Example:  His psychoanalytic interpretation of the painting revealed hidden anxieties. 他对这幅画的心理分析解读揭示了隐藏的焦虑。 | 近义词：psychodynamic, psychological | 反义词：N/A
