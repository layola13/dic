dredgers:/ˈdrɛdʒərz/ | n. |挖泥船；疏浚船 |A dredger is a boat or ship equipped to dredge, meaning to excavate or remove material from the bottom of a body of water.  They are used for various purposes, including deepening channels, creating harbors, and removing sediment. |Example: The port authority used several large dredgers to deepen the harbor entrance. 港口管理局使用了数艘大型挖泥船来加深港口入口。  The river dredgers worked continuously to maintain the navigable channel. 河流挖泥船持续工作以保持航道畅通。|近义词：dredging vessel, excavator (in this context)|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be filling or depositing sediment.
