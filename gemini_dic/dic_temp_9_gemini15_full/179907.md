refashioning:/ˌriːˈfæʃənɪŋ/| n. |改造；重新设计；改头换面 |The act of giving something a new form, appearance, or purpose.  It often implies improvement or modernization. |例句：The refashioning of the old building into a modern art gallery was a success. 老建筑改造成现代美术馆很成功。

例句：The refashioning of the curriculum aims to improve student engagement.  课程的改革旨在提高学生的参与度。


近义词：remodeling, redesigning, renovation, transformation, make-over


反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin


词性: 名词
