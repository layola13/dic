disgruntle:/dɪsˈɡrʌntəl/| vt. |使不高兴；使不满 | to make (someone) dissatisfied and unhappy |The long delay disgruntled the passengers.  长时间的延误使乘客们感到不满。|近义词：displease, annoy, upset |反义词：please, satisfy, delight


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disgruntles his employees with his unfair policies. = 他用不公平的政策使他的员工不满。
The constant noise disgruntles the neighbors. = 持续的噪音让邻居们感到不满。

一般过去时:
The bad news disgruntled her. = 这个坏消息让她很不高兴。
The poor service disgruntled the customers. = 差劲的服务让顾客们很不满。

现在完成时:
His constant complaints have disgruntled everyone in the office. = 他不断的抱怨已经让办公室里每个人都不高兴了。
The recent changes have disgruntled many employees. = 最近的变动让很多员工不满。

过去完成时:
The team had been disgruntled for weeks before they finally voiced their complaints. = 团队在最终表达他们的不满之前已经不高兴好几个星期了。
They had been disgruntled by the management's decision for a long time. = 他们早就对管理层的决定不满。

将来时:
The new rules will likely disgruntle many people. = 新规很可能会让很多人不满。
I fear that the announcement will disgruntle the shareholders. = 我担心这个公告会让股东们不高兴。

虚拟语气:
If the company didn't implement these changes, the workers wouldn't be so disgruntled. = 如果公司不实行这些改变，工人们就不会这么不满。
I wish the management wouldn't disgruntle its employees with such petty issues. = 我希望管理层不要因为这些琐碎的事情让员工们不高兴。

被动语态:
The workers were disgruntled by the unfair treatment. = 工人们因为不公平的待遇而不满。
The staff felt disgruntled by the lack of communication. = 员工们因为缺乏沟通而感到不满。

疑问句:
Does this policy disgruntle the employees? = 这项政策让员工们不满吗？
What disgruntles the customers the most? = 什么最让顾客们不满？

否定句:
The boss tried to avoid actions that would disgruntle his employees. = 老板试图避免那些会让员工不满的行为。
The new system didn't disgruntle the users as much as expected. = 新系统并没有像预期的那样让用户感到不满。
