/ɪˈreɪdieɪt/| vt. |散发；辐射；照射；流露| to send out rays of light, heat, or another form of energy, or to spread something in all directions from a central point |The sun radiates heat and light. 太阳散发热和光。  The city radiated energy and excitement.  这座城市散发着活力和兴奋。| emanate, emit, beam | absorb, collect |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun radiates heat. = 太阳散发热量。
The flowers radiate beauty. = 花儿散发着美丽。


一般过去时:
The star radiated a warm glow. = 那颗星星散发着温暖的光芒。
The speaker radiated confidence. = 演讲者散发着自信。


现在完成时:
The project has radiated positive energy throughout the community. = 该项目已为整个社区带来了积极的能量。
The news has radiated outwards across the country. = 这则新闻已传遍全国。


过去完成时:
The room had radiated warmth before the fire went out. = 在火熄灭之前，房间里散发着温暖。
The painting had radiated a sense of mystery. = 这幅画曾散发着神秘感。


将来时:
The new technology will radiate benefits across many industries. = 新技术将在许多行业中带来好处。
The festival will radiate joy and happiness. = 节日将洋溢着快乐。


虚拟语气:
If the star radiated more energy, we could see it more easily. = 如果那颗星散发更多能量，我们就更容易看到它。
If the painting radiated less darkness, it would be more appealing. = 如果这幅画散发较少的黑暗，它会更吸引人。


被动语态:
Heat is radiated by the sun. = 热量由太阳散发。
Positive energy is radiated by the team. = 团队散发着积极的能量。


疑问句:
Does the lamp radiate enough light? = 这盏灯散发足够的灯光吗？
Does the speaker radiate sincerity? = 演讲者散发真诚吗？


否定句:
The object did not radiate any heat. = 该物体没有散发任何热量。
The performance did not radiate enthusiasm. = 表演没有散发热情。


PHRASE->
radiate outwards = 向外辐射
radiate from = 从...辐射
