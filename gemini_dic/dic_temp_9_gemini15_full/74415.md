franchised:/ˈfrænˌtʃaɪzd/ | adj. |特许经营的；授权的|Granted a franchise; operating under a franchise agreement.  This refers to a business model where a company (franchisor) grants another company (franchisee) the right to operate under its brand name and system. |例句：Many fast-food restaurants are franchised businesses.  许多快餐店都是特许经营企业。

例句：The coffee shop is a franchised location of a larger chain. 这家咖啡店是大型连锁店的加盟店。


近义词：licensed, authorized, permitted

反义词：independent, unlicensed


词性：形容词
