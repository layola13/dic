preballots:/ˈpriːˌbæləts/ | n. | 预选投票；预选选票 |  Pre-ballots are ballots cast before the official election date, often used in primaries or early voting.  They allow voters to cast their votes in advance of the main election day.  | The pre-ballots were counted before the official election results were announced. = 预选投票在正式选举结果公布之前就已经统计完毕。  The high number of pre-ballots indicated a strong voter turnout. =  大量的预选投票表明选民投票率很高。 | early voting, advance voting |  (no direct antonym,  but a contrasting term could be "official ballots") | 名词
