crenulate:/ˈkrɛnjuleɪt/| adj.|波浪形的；锯齿形的|Having a wavy, scalloped, or notched edge or outline.  The word describes something with a series of small, rounded indentations resembling the teeth of a saw but smoother.|例句：The edge of the leaf was crenulate.  叶子的边缘是波浪形的。The coastline was crenulate, with numerous small bays and inlets. 海岸线呈波浪状，有很多小的海湾和入口。|近义词：scalloped, wavy, notched, dentate|反义词：smooth, straight, even
