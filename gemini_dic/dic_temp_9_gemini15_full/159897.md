polymyoid:/ˌpɒliˈmɔɪəd/| adj.|多肌的；多肌炎的|Relating to or characterized by inflammation of multiple muscles.  The term often refers to polymyositis, a chronic inflammatory myopathy affecting multiple skeletal muscles.|例句：The patient presented with symptoms consistent with polymyoid inflammation.  患者表现出与多肌炎一致的症状。  The diagnosis of polymyoid disease requires careful examination and testing. 多肌病的诊断需要仔细检查和测试。|近义词：polymyositis (more specific)|反义词：N/A
