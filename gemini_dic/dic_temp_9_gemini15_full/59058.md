dweller:/ˈdwɛlə(r)/| n. |居住者，居民|A person or animal that lives in a particular place.  A dweller in the city often finds the quiet countryside a welcome change.|城市居民经常发现宁静的乡村是一种受欢迎的变化。|inhabitant, resident, occupant|non-resident, visitor|名词

一般现在时:
The city dweller enjoys the vibrant nightlife. = 城市居民享受着充满活力的夜生活。
She is a dweller in a small mountain village. = 她是一位住在小山村的居民。


一般过去时:
He was a dweller of the forest before he moved to the city. = 在搬到城市之前，他是一位森林居民。
They were dwellers in a small coastal town. = 他们曾经住在一个小海滨小镇。


现在完成时:
She has been a city dweller for over twenty years. = 她已经是一个城市居民超过二十年了。
They have been dwellers of this area since they were children. = 从小他们就一直是这个地区的居民。


过去完成时:

They had been dwellers in that old house for many years before it was demolished. = 在那栋旧房子被拆除之前，他们在那里住了很多年。


将来时:
She will be a dweller of the countryside after her retirement. = 退休后，她将成为乡村居民。
They will be dwellers in a new apartment building next year. = 明年他们将居住在一个新的公寓楼里。


虚拟语气:
If I were a dweller of a small town, I would have more peace and quiet. = 如果我住在一个小镇，我会拥有更多宁静。
I wish I were a dweller of a tropical island. = 我希望我住在一个热带岛屿上。


被动语态:
The abandoned house was rarely visited by any dwellers. = 废弃的房子很少有人居住。
The cave was once a dweller of ancient people. = 这个洞穴曾经是古代人的居住地。


疑问句:

否定句:
He is not a dweller of this town. = 他不是这个镇的居民。
