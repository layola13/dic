recency:/ˈriːsənsi/| n. |最近，近来；新近 |The quality or fact of being recent.  It refers to the relative newness of something.  The more recent something is, the higher its recency.  |例句：The recency of the data makes it particularly relevant to our study.  最近的数据使其与我们的研究特别相关。|例句：The recency effect explains why we remember the last items on a list better than the first.  近期效应解释了为什么我们比第一个项目更好地记住列表中的最后一个项目。|近义词：newness, recentness, novelty |反义词：antiquity, remoteness, age
