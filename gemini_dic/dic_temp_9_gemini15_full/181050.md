reincluding:/ˌriːɪnˈkluːdɪŋ/ | v. |重新包含，再次纳入 |  The act of including something again, after it had been previously excluded or removed.  It implies a prior inclusion followed by exclusion and then reinclusion. |例句：After reviewing the data, we decided to reinclude the omitted results in the final report.  重新审查数据后，我们决定在最终报告中重新纳入被遗漏的结果。


The company reincluded the employee after they apologized for their mistake. 公司在员工道歉后重新接纳了他。|近义词：reintroducing, readmitting, reinstating |反义词：excluding, omitting, removing |动词
