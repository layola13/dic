sheafy:/ˈʃiːfi/| adj.| 束状的；成束的 |  Relating to or resembling a sheaf; gathered or bundled together.  | Example: The sheafy wheat stood tall in the field.  麦子成束地高高地立在田野里。 |近义词：bundled, clustered, grouped |反义词：scattered, dispersed |形容词
