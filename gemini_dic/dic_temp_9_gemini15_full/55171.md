disseminating: /dɪˈsɛmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. |传播；散布；普及|  The act of spreading something, especially information, widely; to spread or disperse something over a wide area. |例句：The government is disseminating information about the new virus. 政府正在传播关于这种新病毒的信息。


例句：The university is disseminating knowledge to students.  大学正在向学生传播知识。


近义词：spreading, distributing, propagating, broadcasting, diffusing


反义词：concealing, suppressing, withholding, concentrating, gathering


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disseminates its products throughout the country. = 这家公司在全国范围内传播其产品。
She disseminates information on social media daily. = 她每天都在社交媒体上传播信息。


一般过去时:
The scientist disseminated his research findings to the scientific community. = 科学家向科学界传播了他的研究成果。
They disseminated flyers announcing the event. = 他们分发了宣传活动的传单。


现在完成时:
The organization has disseminated awareness about climate change. = 该组织已经提高了人们对气候变化的认识。
They have disseminated the news to all employees. = 他们已经将消息传达给了所有员工。


过去完成时:
The rumor had been disseminated throughout the town before the truth emerged. = 真相大白之前，谣言已经在镇上散布开来。
She had disseminated the information before the meeting began. = 会议开始之前，她已经传播了信息。


将来时:
The company will disseminate its new marketing campaign next month. = 该公司将于下个月启动其新的营销活动。
He will disseminate his findings at the conference. = 他将在会议上公布他的研究结果。


虚拟语气:
If they disseminated the information more effectively, more people would be aware. = 如果他们更有效地传播信息，更多的人就会意识到。
I wish they had disseminated the news earlier. = 我希望他们早点发布这个消息。


被动语态:
The information was disseminated through various channels. = 信息通过各种渠道传播。
The news is being disseminated rapidly across the globe. = 新闻正在迅速传遍全球。


疑问句:
How is the company disseminating its new product? = 公司是如何传播其新产品的？
How will the information be disseminated? = 信息将如何传播？


否定句:
The government did not disseminate the information to the public. = 政府没有向公众传播信息。
They failed to disseminate the warning effectively. = 他们未能有效地传播警告。


PHRASE->
disseminate information = 传播信息
disseminate knowledge = 传播知识
widely disseminate = 广泛传播
disseminate a report = 发布报告
disseminate seeds = 散播种子

词源:
From Latin *dissemināre, from dis- + semināre "to sow," from semen "seed."  The meaning evolved from the literal sowing of seeds to the figurative spreading of ideas or information.
