self-dependencies:/self-dɪˈpɛndənsiz/| n. |自依赖，自身依赖 |Refers to the situation where a component, module, or system relies on itself, either directly or indirectly through a chain of dependencies.  This can lead to circular dependencies and other problematic issues in software development and system design.  It often indicates a flaw in the architecture or design. |The software exhibited self-dependencies, causing crashes during runtime.  这个软件存在自身依赖，导致运行时崩溃。|circular dependencies, recursive dependencies |independent components, modularity |名词
