ganache:/ɡəˈnɑːʃ/| n. |巧克力酱，甘纳许|A rich, smooth chocolate mixture made from chocolate and cream, often used as a filling or topping for desserts.  It's characterized by its glossy texture and intensely chocolatey flavor.|Example: The ganache topping on the cake was incredibly decadent.  这蛋糕上的巧克力酱非常浓郁。|近义词：chocolate cream, chocolate frosting|反义词：N/A
