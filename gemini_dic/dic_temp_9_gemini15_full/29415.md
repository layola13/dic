catadioptric:/ˌkætædiˈɒptrɪk/| adj.| 反射折射的；兼用反射和折射的 | Combining or using both reflection and refraction of light. Catadioptric systems are often used in telescopes and other optical instruments to improve image quality and reduce aberrations.  | Example: The new telescope uses a catadioptric lens system.  新型望远镜使用反射折射透镜系统。|近义词：reflective-refractive|反义词：refractive-only, reflective-only
