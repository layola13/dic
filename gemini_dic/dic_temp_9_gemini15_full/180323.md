refuelling: /ˌriːˈfjuːəlɪŋ/ | n. | 加油；补充燃料 | The act of supplying fuel to something, especially a vehicle or aircraft.  |例句：The plane needs refuelling before it can continue its journey. = 这架飞机需要加油才能继续飞行。

例句：The car requires refuelling after a long drive. = 长途驾驶后，汽车需要加油。


近义词：refilling, replenishing | 反义词：depleting, exhausting | 词性：名词

Refuelling is essential for long flights. = 长途飞行需要加油。
The car needs refuelling every 500 miles. = 这辆车每行驶500英里需要加油。

词源:
The word "refuelling" is a combination of "re-" (meaning "again" or "back") and "fuelling" (the act of supplying fuel).  "Fuelling" itself comes from "fuel," which has origins in the Middle English word "fuelle," ultimately derived from Old French.
