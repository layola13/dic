horn-in:/hɔːrn ɪn/ | v. | 插嘴；强行介入；闯入 | To interrupt a conversation or activity rudely; to intrude oneself into a situation or group without invitation. | 例句：He kept horn-in on our conversation. 他一直在插嘴我们的谈话。


例句：They horned in on our party uninvited.  他们不请自来闯入了我们的派对。


近义词：butt in, cut in, barge in, intrude, interject


反义词：withdraw, refrain, abstain


词性：动词
