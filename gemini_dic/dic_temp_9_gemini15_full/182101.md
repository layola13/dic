reminisced:/ˌrɛməˈnɪst/| v. |回忆，追忆|To indulge in enjoyable recollection of past events.|例句：She reminisced about her childhood summers spent at the beach. 她回忆起童年在海滩度过的夏天。|近义词：recalled, remembered, reflected on|反义词：forgot, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reminisces about his college days. = 他回忆起他的大学时光。
She often reminisces with her friends. = 她经常和朋友们一起回忆往事。

一般过去时:
We reminisced about our travels in Europe. = 我们回忆了在欧洲的旅行。
They reminisced for hours at the reunion. = 他们在聚会上回忆了好几个小时。

现在完成时:
I have reminisced about that trip many times. = 我已经多次回忆起那次旅行。
She has reminisced about her youth several times. = 她已经几次回忆起她的青春。

过去完成时:
Before he died, he had reminisced about his life. = 他死前回忆了他的一生。
After the party, they had reminisced about old times. = 派对结束后，他们回忆起了往事。

将来时:
We will reminisce about this day in the future. = 将来我们会回忆起今天。
They will reminisce about their adventures together. = 他们将会一起回忆他们的冒险经历。

虚拟语气:
If I could reminisce, I would remember everything. = 如果我能回忆，我会记住一切。
I wish I could reminisce about my childhood. = 我真希望我能回忆起我的童年。

被动语态:
His stories were reminisced by many. = 许多人回忆起他的故事。
The past events were reminisced with fondness. = 过去的事情被怀念地回忆起来。

疑问句:
Do you often reminisce about your past? = 你经常回忆你的过去吗？
Will they reminisce at the wedding? = 他们会在婚礼上回忆往事吗？

否定句:
He didn't reminisce about his failures. = 他没有回忆起他的失败。
She does not often reminisce about difficult times. = 她不经常回忆艰难的时光。


PHRASE->
reminisce about = 回忆...
reminisce over = 细细回忆...
reminisce fondly = 怀念地回忆
reminisce with = 与...一起回忆
reminisce at length = 长篇大论地回忆
