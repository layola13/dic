leopardesses: /ˈlepərdɛsɪz/ | n. |雌豹；雌美洲豹|  The plural form of leopardess, referring to female leopards.  It describes adult female leopards, differentiating them from male leopards (leopards) and young ones (leopard cubs).|

例句：Several leopardesses were spotted near the watering hole.  几只雌豹在水坑附近被发现。

The researchers observed the hunting behavior of the leopardesses and their cubs. 研究人员观察了雌豹及其幼崽的捕猎行为。


近义词：female leopards


反义词：leopards (male)


词性：noun (名词)
