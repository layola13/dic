expiration:/ˌɛkspəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 期满，终止，失效 | The act or instance of expiring; termination.  The end of the period for which something is valid. |例句：The expiration date on the milk is tomorrow.  牛奶的有效期是明天。

The expiration of the contract means we need to renegotiate. 合同到期意味着我们需要重新谈判。|近义词：termination, expiry, end, conclusion |反义词：beginning, commencement, start


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contract's expiration date is next month. = 合同的到期日是下个月。
His membership expiration is approaching. = 他的会员资格快到期了。


一般过去时:
The warranty expiration happened last week. = 保修期已于上周到期。
The drug's expiration was unnoticed. = 药物的有效期未被注意。


现在完成时:
The magazine subscription has reached its expiration. = 杂志订阅已到期。
My passport has expired. = 我的护照已经过期了。


过去完成时:
The visa had expired before he arrived in the country. = 他到达该国之前，签证就已经过期了。
The license had expired months ago. = 执照几个月前就已经过期了。


将来时:
The offer will expire soon. = 优惠活动即将结束。
My driver's license will expire next year. = 我的驾照明年到期。


虚拟语气:
If the product expiration were sooner, we would have to discard it. = 如果产品有效期更早，我们就必须丢弃它。
I wish the patent's expiration were further in the future. = 我希望专利的到期时间更晚一些。


被动语态:
The product's expiration date is clearly marked on the package. = 产品的有效日期在包装上清楚地标明。
The contract's expiration was announced by the company. = 合同到期由公司宣布。


疑问句:
When is the expiration date of this medicine? = 这药的有效期是什么时候？
What is the expiration date on your credit card? = 你的信用卡有效期是什么时候？


否定句:
The warranty doesn't have an expiration date. = 保修期没有到期日。
The medicine hasn't expired yet. = 药还没过期。


PHRASE->
expiration date: 有效日期
date of expiration: 到期日
expiration period: 有效期
near expiration: 即将到期
after expiration: 到期后

词源:
From Middle French *expiration, from Latin expiratio, from expirare "to breathe out, expire," from ex- "out" + spirare "to breathe."  The sense of "coming to an end" developed from the earlier sense of "breathing out" or "death."
