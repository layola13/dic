/ˈpʊləri/| n. |家禽养殖；家禽饲养场|Poultry farming or raising.  It refers to the raising of domesticated birds, such as chickens, ducks, turkeys, and geese, for meat or eggs. |例句：His family runs a successful pullery, supplying eggs to local supermarkets. 他的家族经营着一个成功的家禽养殖场，向当地超市供应鸡蛋。|近义词：poultry farm, poultry ranch|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with cattle farming or pig farming.
