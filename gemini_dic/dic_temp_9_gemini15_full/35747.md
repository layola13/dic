cochineals:/ˈkɒtʃɪniːlz/| n.|胭脂红，胭脂虫|A cochineal is a small insect that is used to make a red dye.  The dye is made from the dried bodies of the female insects.|例句：Cochineal is a natural dye used to create vibrant red colors.  胭脂红是一种天然染料，用于创造鲜艳的红色。|近义词：carmine, crimson|反义词：None
