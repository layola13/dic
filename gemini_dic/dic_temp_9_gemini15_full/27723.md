canescent:/kəˈnesənt/| adj.|灰白的，白发的|Becoming gray or whitish; hoary.  The term often refers to the graying of hair or other plant parts.  |例句：His canescent beard was a testament to his age.  他的灰白胡须证明了他的年龄。

例句：The canescent leaves of the olive tree hinted at the approaching winter.  橄榄树上灰白的叶子暗示着冬天即将到来。

近义词：gray, hoary, grizzled, silvery

反义词：dark, black, raven
