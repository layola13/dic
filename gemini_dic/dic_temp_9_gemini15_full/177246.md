reassemble:/ˌriːəˈsɛmbəl/| vt.|重新组装；重新集合|To put something together again after it has been taken apart.  It implies a prior disassembly.|例句：We need to reassemble the furniture after the move. 我们需要搬家后重新组装家具。

例句：The mechanic reassembled the engine after repairing it. 技工修理好发动机后又重新组装了起来。


近义词：reconstruct, put back together, reunite

反义词：disassemble, dismantle, take apart

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reassemble the machine every week. 他们每周都重新组装机器。
He reassembles the model car with care. 他仔细地重新组装模型车。


一般过去时:
The team reassembled the equipment after the test. 测试结束后，团队重新组装了设备。
She reassembled the puzzle and found a missing piece. 她重新拼凑拼图时发现了一块缺失的碎片。


现在完成时:
We have reassembled the broken chair. 我们已经重新组装了坏掉的椅子。
They have reassembled the data from multiple sources. 他们已经从多个来源重新组合了数据。


过去完成时:
They had reassembled the engine before the race began. 比赛开始前他们已经重新组装好了发动机。
She had reassembled her thoughts before answering the question. 她回答问题之前已经重新整理好了思绪。


将来时:
We will reassemble the committee next week. 我们下周将重新召集委员会。
He will reassemble the parts according to the instructions. 他将按照说明重新组装零件。


虚拟语气:
If we had the right tools, we could reassemble the machine quickly. 如果我们有合适的工具，就能很快地重新组装机器。
I wish I could reassemble the clock; it's too complicated. 我真希望我能重新组装这个钟，它太复杂了。


被动语态:
The furniture will be reassembled by the movers tomorrow. 家具明天将由搬运工重新组装。
The device was reassembled incorrectly, resulting in malfunction. 设备组装错误，导致故障。


疑问句:
Can you reassemble the bicycle for me? 你能帮我重新组装自行车吗？
Will the parts be easily reassembled? 零件容易重新组装吗？


否定句:
He couldn't reassemble the computer after taking it apart. 他拆开电脑后无法重新组装。
They did not reassemble the exhibit after the closing ceremony. 闭幕式后他们没有重新搭建展品。
