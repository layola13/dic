ovens:/ˈʌvənz/| n.|烤箱，烤炉|An oven is a heated compartment used for baking, roasting, or heating food.|例句：We baked a cake in the new oven. 我们在新烤箱里烤了一个蛋糕。|例句：The pizza oven was incredibly hot. 比萨烤炉非常热。|近义词：kiln, furnace|反义词：refrigerator

Our ovens bake many loaves daily. = 我们的烤箱每天烘烤许多面包。
