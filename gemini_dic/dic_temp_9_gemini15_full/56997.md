double-faulted:/ˈdʌbəl ˈfɔːltɪd/| adj.|双误的，犯双误的|Describes a situation, typically in tennis, where a player commits two consecutive faults, resulting in a point lost to the opponent.  It can also be used metaphorically to describe a situation where two serious mistakes are made in succession.|例句：He double-faulted on match point, losing the game. 他在赛点时犯了双误，输掉了比赛。


例句：The project failed because of double-faulted planning and execution.  这个项目失败是因为计划和执行都犯了严重的错误。|近义词：erroneous, flawed, imperfect|反义词：perfect, flawless, faultless
