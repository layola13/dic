offensible:/əˈfɛnsɪbl/ | adj. | 可攻击的；可冒犯的；易受攻击的 | Capable of giving offense; likely to cause resentment, anger, or displeasure.  Something that is offensible is easily offended or hurt.  It can also refer to something that is vulnerable to attack. | Example:  His comments were offensible to many members of the audience. 他的评论冒犯了许多观众。

The use of "offensible" is relatively rare compared to "offensive."  It focuses more on the capacity to be offended rather than the act of offending.


近义词:  vulnerable, susceptible, sensitive, assailable, liable (to offense)
反义词:  insensitive, invulnerable, unassailable, immune (to offense)


词性:  形容词
