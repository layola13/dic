emphases:/ˈɛmfəsiːz/ | n. |强调，重点，重点之处 |The plural form of "emphasis," referring to instances of stress or importance placed on something.  It highlights particular aspects or elements. |例句：The professor's emphases in the lecture were on the historical context and the ethical implications. 教授讲座的重点是历史背景和伦理意义。

例句：Her emphases throughout the presentation were clear and concise, effectively conveying her main points.  她在整个演示文稿中的重点清晰简洁，有效地传达了她的要点。


近义词：stress, highlight, accent, focus, prominence | 反义词：downplaying, minimizing, ignoring
