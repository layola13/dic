out-shapes:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪps/| vt.|胜过，超过，比…更出色|To surpass or exceed in form, size, or quality.|例句：Her new design out-shapes all the previous models. 她的新设计胜过所有之前的型号。|例句：His performance out-shaped his opponent's. 他的表现超过了他的对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, excel|反义词：underperform, be inferior to|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new building out-shapes the old one. = 新建筑比旧建筑更出色。
This plan out-shapes all the others we've considered. = 这个计划比我们考虑过的其他所有计划都要好。


一般过去时:
His painting out-shaped all the others in the competition. = 他的画在比赛中胜过了其他所有作品。
Last year's sales out-shaped those of the previous year. = 去年销售额超过了前一年。


现在完成时:
The company has out-shaped its rivals in the market. = 这家公司已经超越了市场上的竞争对手。
This technology has out-shaped previous methods. = 这项技术已经超越了以前的方法。


过去完成时:
The team had out-shaped their opponents by the end of the game. = 到比赛结束时，球队已经超过了对手。
By the time he finished his presentation, he had out-shaped all expectations. = 到他完成演示时，他已经超出了所有预期。


将来时:
The new model will out-shape its predecessor in terms of efficiency. = 新型号在效率方面将优于其前身。
We believe that our product will out-shape the competition. = 我们相信我们的产品将胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we could out-shape our competitors. = 如果我们更努力工作，我们就能超越竞争对手。
I wish our team had out-shaped the other team. = 我希望我们的团队能够战胜另一支球队。


被动语态:
The old building was out-shaped by the new one. = 旧建筑被新建筑超越了。
His proposal was out-shaped by a more innovative one. = 他的提案被一个更具创新性的提案所超越。


疑问句:
Does this design out-shape the previous one? = 这个设计比之前的更好吗？
Will the new product out-shape its competitors? = 新产品会超越竞争对手吗？


否定句:
Our product doesn't out-shape theirs in terms of price. = 我们的产品在价格方面并不优于他们的产品。
The team did not out-shape their opponents. = 该队没有战胜他们的对手。



PHRASE->

out-shape the market: 占据市场主导地位
out-shape expectations: 超出预期
out-shape the norm: 超出常态
out-shape the previous record:打破之前的纪录
