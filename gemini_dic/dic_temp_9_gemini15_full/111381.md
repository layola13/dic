livestream:/ˈlaɪvstriːm/| n. v.| 直播，现场直播 | The act of broadcasting or transmitting a live video or audio feed over the internet.  It can refer to the act of broadcasting itself or the broadcast itself. | 例句：They livestreamed the concert to millions of fans worldwide.  他们向全球数百万粉丝直播了这场音乐会。

例句：We're going to livestream the wedding ceremony. 我们将对婚礼仪式进行直播。


近义词：live broadcast, webcast, online broadcast | 反义词：recording, replay


词性: Noun & Verb
