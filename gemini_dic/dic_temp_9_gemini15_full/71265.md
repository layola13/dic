fivesome:/ˈfaɪvˌsəm/| n.|五人一组，五人乐队|A group of five people, especially one assembled for a particular purpose, such as a golfing foursome.  It can also refer to a group of five people engaged in a social activity.|例句：They formed a fivesome to play a round of golf.他们组成了一个五人小组打高尔夫球。

例句：We went out for dinner as a fivesome. 我们五个人一起出去吃晚饭。


近义词：quintet (if referring to a musical group), group of five


反义词：无


词性：名词
