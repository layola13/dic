lamellicorn:/ˌlæməˈlɪkɔːrn/| n. |叶甲虫；片状角虫|A lamellicorn is a beetle belonging to the superfamily Scarabaeoidea, characterized by having lamellae (plate-like structures) on their antennae.  They are a diverse group including dung beetles, June bugs, and rhinoceros beetles. | Example: Many species of lamellicorn beetles are important decomposers in their ecosystems.  许多种类的叶甲虫在其生态系统中是重要的分解者。|近义词：scarabaeid (更精确的术语)|反义词：无
