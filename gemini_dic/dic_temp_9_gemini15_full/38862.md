confederating:/kənˈfɛdəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |结盟；联合；联盟化 |The act of forming a confederation or alliance; uniting into a confederacy.  The process of joining together to form a confederation. |例句：Several states were confederating to resist the federal government.  几个州正在结盟以抵抗联邦政府。


例句：The smaller nations were confederating to increase their collective bargaining power.  那些较小的国家正在联合起来，以增强其集体谈判能力。


近义词：uniting, federating, allying, consolidating


反义词：dividing, separating, disuniting


词性:动词
