mangetout:/ˈmæŋʒətuː/| n. |法国豆，四季豆|A type of edible green bean, typically thin and stringless, often eaten whole.  It is also known as snow pea or sugar snap pea.  The name comes from French. |例句：I added some mangetout to the stir-fry.我把一些法国豆加到了炒菜里。

例句：The salad contained fresh mangetout and cherry tomatoes.沙拉里包含新鲜的法国豆和樱桃番茄。


近义词：snow pea, sugar snap pea, green bean


反义词：无


词性：名词
