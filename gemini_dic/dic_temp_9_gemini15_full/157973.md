planipetalous: /ˌplænɪˈpɛtəlʌs/ | adj. | 平瓣的；花瓣平展的 | Having flat, spreading petals.  The term describes flowers where the petals are not noticeably curved, folded, or otherwise shaped in a complex manner, but lie relatively flat.  |


例句：

The planipetalous blossoms of the daisy contrasted sharply with the curled petals of the rose.  = 菊花平展的花瓣与玫瑰花卷曲的花瓣形成了鲜明的对比。

The simple, planipetalous flowers of the buttercup are easily recognizable. = 毛茛科植物简单、平展的花朵很容易辨认。


近义词：flat-petaled, spreading-petaled


反义词：convolute, contorted, inflexed (referring to petal shape)


词性：形容词
