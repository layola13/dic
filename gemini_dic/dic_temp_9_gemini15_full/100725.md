intramurally:/ˌɪntrəˈmʊrəli/| adv.| 校内地；内部地 | Within the walls or boundaries of an institution, especially a school or college.  |例句：The debate was held intramurally, only students from the university could participate.  这场辩论在校内举行，只有大学的学生才能参加。

例句：The championship game was played intramurally on the college campus. 冠军赛在大学的校园内举行。
