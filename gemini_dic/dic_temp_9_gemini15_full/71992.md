fling:/flɪŋ/| vt. n.|猛扔，抛；猛推；挥动；(短暂的)狂欢；恋爱关系|To throw or cast something with force or abandon.  A brief but intense period of activity or enjoyment. A brief romantic relationship.|例句：He flung the door open angrily. 他生气地把门猛地推开。  They had a fling in the summer. 他们在夏天有过一段短暂的恋情。|近义词：toss, hurl, throw, fling oneself into|反义词：carefully place, gently put

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flings the ball to his friend. = 他把球扔给他的朋友。
She flings herself into her work. = 她全身心地投入工作。


一般过去时:
He flung the stone into the river. = 他把石头扔进了河里。
They had a fling last summer. = 他们去年夏天有过一段短暂的恋情。


现在完成时:
She has flung herself into the project. = 她已经全身心地投入到这个项目中了。
He has flung away his old habits. = 他已经改掉了旧习惯。


过去完成时:
He had flung the letter into the fire before she could read it. = 在她能读到信之前，他已经把信扔进了火里。
They had flung themselves into a wild celebration. = 他们已经投入到狂欢庆祝中了。


将来时:

He will fling the trash away tomorrow. = 他明天会把垃圾扔掉。


虚拟语气:

I wish I could fling my worries away. = 我希望我能抛开我的烦恼。


被动语态:

否定句:
