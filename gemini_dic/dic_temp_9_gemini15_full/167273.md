prethreatens:/ˌpriːθˈretənz/| v.|预先威胁；提前发出威胁 | To threaten beforehand; to issue a threat in advance.  |例句：The general prethreatened the enemy with swift retaliation. 将军预先威胁敌人将迅速报复。

例句：The looming storm prethreatened the coastal communities with flooding.  迫在眉睫的风暴预先威胁着沿海社区将发生洪水。


近义词：forewarn, intimidate beforehand, give prior warning of harm


反义词：assure, reassure, comfort


词性: 动词
