chaw:/tʃɔː/ | vt. | 嚼，咀嚼；用力咬；|To chew vigorously or with difficulty.|He was chawing on a piece of tough meat. 他正在用力嚼一块韧肉。|She chawed the end of her pen nervously. 她紧张地嚼着笔头。|chew, gnaw | swallow |  v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chaws his food thoroughly. = 他细嚼慢咽。
She chaws gum all day. = 她整天嚼口香糖。


一般过去时:
He chawed the steak for a long time. = 他嚼了很久牛排。
The dog chawed on the bone. = 狗啃着骨头。


现在完成时:
He has chawed through the rope. = 他已经咬断了绳子。
She has chawed her nails again. = 她又咬指甲了。


过去完成时:
He had chawed the food before swallowing it. = 他在吞咽之前已经咀嚼了食物。
The dog had chawed the shoe before I came home. = 我回家之前，狗已经把鞋子咬了。


将来时:
He will chaw on this problem all night. = 他将整夜苦思这个问题。
She will chaw the food slowly. = 她会慢慢咀嚼食物。


虚拟语气:
If he had chawed his food properly, he wouldn't have indigestion. = 如果他好好咀嚼食物，就不会消化不良。
I wish I hadn't chawed my nails so much. = 我真希望我没有那么频繁地咬指甲。
