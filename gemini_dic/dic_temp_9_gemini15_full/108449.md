laxly:/ˈlæksli/| adv.|松散地，松懈地，不严格地 | loosely or carelessly; not strictly or firmly.  |例句：The rules were laxly enforced.  规则执行得很松懈。

例句：He laxly managed his finances, leading to debt. 他管理财务很松懈，导致负债累累。


近义词：loosely, carelessly, negligently, slackly

反义词：tightly, strictly, rigorously, precisely


词性:副词
