runes:/ruːnz/| n.|符文，神秘文字|Ancient symbols used by Germanic peoples, often carved into objects.|例句：The Viking runes were carved into the stone. 维京符文被刻在石头上。|近义词：characters, symbols, glyphs|反义词：alphabet (in the sense of a fully developed writing system)
