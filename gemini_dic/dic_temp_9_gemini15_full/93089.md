/ˈɪdɪl/| n. |田园诗；理想地；宁静的乡村生活 |An idyllic scene is a peaceful and charming scene, typically in the countryside.  It often evokes a sense of serenity and beauty. The word suggests a perfect or near-perfect environment, free from the stresses of modern life. |例句：The painting depicts an idyllic scene of shepherds tending their flocks in a sun-drenched meadow. 这幅画描绘了牧羊人在阳光普照的草地上放牧的田园风光。

例句：They spent an idyllic summer holiday in the countryside, far from the hustle and bustle of the city. 他们在远离城市喧嚣的乡下度过了一个宁静祥和的暑假。


|近义词：pastoral, utopian, bucolic, picturesque |反义词：chaotic, turbulent, stressful, hectic |名词
