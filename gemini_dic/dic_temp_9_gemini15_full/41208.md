coquimbite:/koʊˈkwɪmbaɪt/| n. |绿矾矿，黄绿矾|A hydrous iron sulfate mineral, typically yellow-green in color.  It's named after Coquimbo, Chile, where it was first discovered. |Example: Coquimbite is a secondary mineral often found in arid regions.  绿矾矿是一种次生矿物，常在干旱地区发现。|近义词：None directly equivalent, but other iron sulfates like copiapite. |反义词：None directly opposite.
