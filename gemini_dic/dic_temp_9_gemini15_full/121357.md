misgrading:/mɪsˈɡreɪdɪŋ/| n.|误评级，评分错误|The act of assigning an incorrect grade or mark to something, typically a student's work or an exam.  This can happen due to oversight, error, or bias.|例句：The teacher admitted to a misgrading of several students' papers, leading to adjustments in final grades. 教师承认对几名学生的试卷进行了误评，导致期末成绩进行了调整。|The professor apologized for the misgrading of the final exam. 教授为期末考试的评分错误道歉。|近义词：incorrect grading, misjudgment, error in grading|反义词：accurate grading, correct assessment
