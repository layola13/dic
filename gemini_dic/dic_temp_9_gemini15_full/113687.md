mackinaw:/ˈmækɪnɔː/|/ˈmækɪnɔː/| n. |马基诺大衣；马基诺毛毯|A heavy woolen coat, typically checked, originating from Mackinac Island.  It can also refer to a heavy woolen blanket.|Example: He wore his mackinaw to protect himself from the biting wind. 他穿着他的马基诺大衣来抵御刺骨的寒风。|近义词：overcoat, greatcoat|反义词：light jacket|名词
