cacks:/kæks/| v.|咯咯地笑，发出嘎嘎声|To laugh in a loud, harsh way; to make a harsh, cracking sound.|例句：The hens cackled loudly in the farmyard.  母鸡在农场里大声咯咯叫。  The old door cackled as it opened.  旧门打开时发出嘎吱声。|近义词：chuckle, giggle, crow|反义词：sob, weep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hens cackle every morning. = 母鸡每天早上都咯咯叫。
He cackles when he's nervous. = 他紧张的时候会咯咯笑。

一般过去时:
The old woman cackled with delight. = 老妇人高兴地咯咯笑。
The branches cackled as the wind blew. = 风吹过时树枝发出嘎吱声。

现在完成时:
The rooster has cackled all morning. = 公鸡整个早上都在咯咯叫。
The floorboards have cackled underfoot for years. = 地板多年来一直发出嘎吱声。

过去完成时:
The children had cackled with laughter before the teacher arrived. = 老师来之前孩子们已经笑得咯咯叫了。
The wood had cackled ominously before it finally broke. = 木头发出不祥的嘎吱声后最终断裂了。

将来时:
The audience will cackle at the comedian's jokes. = 观众将会对喜剧演员的笑话哈哈大笑。
The old house will probably cackle as the foundations settle. = 老房子地基沉降时可能会发出嘎吱声。

虚拟语气:
If he told that joke, the audience would cackle. = 如果他讲那个笑话，观众会哈哈大笑。
I wish the old chair wouldn't cackle so loudly. = 我希望这把旧椅子不要发出这么大的嘎吱声。

被动语态:
The joke was cackled at by everyone in the room. = 房间里的每个人都嘲笑了这个笑话。(很少用被动语态)
The sound of the cracking wood was heard, it cackled ominously. = 听到了木头断裂的声音，它发出不祥的嘎吱声。(很少用被动语态)

疑问句:
Did the audience cackle at the end of the show? = 节目结束时观众有没有哈哈大笑？
Does the floor cackle when you walk across it? = 你走过地板时它会发出嘎吱声吗？

否定句:
The audience didn't cackle at his jokes. = 观众没有嘲笑他的笑话。
The door didn't cackle when it opened. = 门打开时没有发出嘎吱声。
