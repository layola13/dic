carpet-bombing:/ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbɒmɪŋ/| n. |地毯式轰炸|The indiscriminate bombing of a wide area with bombs, usually from aircraft.|例句：The city was subjected to relentless carpet-bombing during the war.  战争期间，这座城市遭受了无情的毯式轰炸。|例句：Carpet-bombing is a controversial military tactic due to its high civilian casualty rate. 地毯式轰炸是一种有争议的军事策略，因为它造成大量平民伤亡。|近义词：saturation bombing, area bombing|反义词：precision bombing, surgical strike|名词
