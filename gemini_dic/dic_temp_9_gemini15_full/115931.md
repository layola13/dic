marquetry:/mɑːˈkeɪtri/ | n. |镶嵌细工；木镶嵌工艺 |  The art of inlaying woods, ivory, mother-of-pearl, etc., of different colors into a surface, typically of furniture.  It involves creating decorative patterns and images by carefully fitting together pieces of contrasting materials. | The antique table was a masterpiece of marquetry, showcasing intricate floral designs. 这张古董桌子是镶嵌细工的杰作，展示了复杂的 floral图案。


The delicate marquetry on the jewelry box was breathtaking.首饰盒上精美的镶嵌细工令人叹为观止。|近义词：inlay, mosaic |反义词：plain (simple surface)
