preentries:/ˈpriːˌɛntriːz/ | n. | 预先登记，预先条目 | Entries made before the official opening of a competition, event, or system.  These are often subject to verification or acceptance. | Example:  The preentries for the marathon were exceptionally high this year.  今年马拉松赛的预先报名人数异常之高。 |近义词：advance entries, preliminary entries | 反义词：post-entries, late entries | 名词
