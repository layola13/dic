postbiblical: /ˌpoʊstbaɪˈbɪkl/ | adj. |  圣经以后的；后圣经时代的 |  Relating to the period of time after the completion of the biblical canon.  This term typically refers to the historical, theological, and literary developments in Judaism and early Christianity following the closing of the biblical canon. | Example: Postbiblical Jewish literature offers valuable insights into the development of rabbinic Judaism.  圣经以后的犹太文学为拉比犹太教的发展提供了宝贵的见解。| 近义词: post-canonical, later, subsequent | 反义词: biblical, canonical
