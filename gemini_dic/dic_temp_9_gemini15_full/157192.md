pinic:/ˈpɪknɪk/| n.|野餐|A picnic is a meal eaten outdoors, typically in a relaxed and informal setting.|例句：We had a lovely picnic by the lake. 我们在湖边吃了一顿愉快的野餐。|近义词：outing, excursion|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We have a picnic every Sunday. = 我们每周日都去野餐。
They often have picnics in the park. = 他们经常在公园野餐。

一般过去时:
We had a picnic in the woods last weekend. = 上周末我们在树林里野餐了。
They had a wonderful picnic on the beach. = 他们在海滩上吃了一顿美妙的野餐。

现在完成时:
We have had many picnics this summer. = 今年夏天我们已经野餐很多次了。
They have never had a picnic in the mountains. = 他们从未在山上野餐过。

过去完成时:
We had already had a picnic before the rain started. = 下雨之前我们已经野餐过了。
They had finished their picnic before the sun set. = 日落之前他们已经结束了野餐。

将来时:
We will have a picnic next Sunday. = 我们下周日将去野餐。
They will have a picnic by the river tomorrow. = 他们明天将在河边野餐。

虚拟语气:
If the weather were better, we would have a picnic. = 如果天气更好，我们会去野餐。
I wish we could have a picnic this weekend. = 我希望我们这个周末能去野餐。

被动语态:
The picnic was spoiled by the rain. = 野餐被雨水破坏了。
The picnic was prepared carefully by my mother. = 野餐由我母亲精心准备。

疑问句:
Will we have a picnic this weekend? = 这个周末我们会去野餐吗？
Where will you have your picnic? = 你将在哪里野餐？

否定句:
We didn't have a picnic yesterday. = 我们昨天没有去野餐。
They won't have a picnic because of the bad weather. = 因为天气不好，他们不会去野餐。
