rhumbaed:/rʌmˈbɑːd/ | v. | 跳伦巴舞；以伦巴舞的节奏移动 | To dance the rhumba; to move rhythmically in the style of the rhumba. |  They rhumbaed the night away at the club.  他们在俱乐部里跳了一整晚的伦巴舞。| He rhumbaed across the dance floor with effortless grace. 他优雅地横跨舞池跳着伦巴舞。 |  dance, sway, move rhythmically|  (无明确反义词，可考虑stop dancing, stand still等)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rhumba every Saturday night. = 他们每个星期六晚上都跳伦巴舞。
She rhumbas with amazing energy. = 她跳伦巴舞充满活力。

一般过去时:
We rhumbaed until dawn. = 我们跳伦巴舞跳到了黎明。
He rhumbaed with his partner gracefully. = 他和他的舞伴优雅地跳着伦巴舞。

现在完成时:
I have rhumbaed many times in my life. = 我一生中跳过很多次伦巴舞。
They have rhumbaed to many different songs. = 他们跳过很多不同歌曲的伦巴舞。

过去完成时:
Before the party started, they had rhumbaed to practice. = 派对开始前，他们已经跳过伦巴舞练习了。
She had rhumbaed for years before she became a professional dancer. = 在成为职业舞者之前，她已经跳了多年的伦巴舞。

将来时:
We will rhumba at the beach party. = 我们将在海滩派对上跳伦巴舞。
They will rhumba all night long. = 他们会跳一整晚的伦巴舞。

虚拟语气:
If I could dance, I would rhumba. = 如果我会跳舞，我会跳伦巴舞。
I wish I could rhumba like that. = 我真希望我能像那样跳伦巴舞。

被动语态:
The rhumba was rhumbaed beautifully. (This is grammatically correct but unusual and unnatural.) =伦巴舞跳得很漂亮。(这个语法上正确，但不常见且不自然)
The music was played for the rhumba. = 音乐是为伦巴舞而演奏的。

疑问句:
Do you know how to rhumba? = 你知道怎么跳伦巴舞吗？
Will they rhumba tonight? = 他们今晚会跳伦巴舞吗？

否定句:
I don't know how to rhumba. = 我不会跳伦巴舞。
They didn't rhumba at the party. = 他们在派对上没有跳伦巴舞。
