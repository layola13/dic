hegemonic:/ˌhɛdʒəˈmɒnɪk/|/ˌhɛdʒəˈmɑːnɪk/| adj. |支配的；霸权的；统治的 | Relating to or characteristic of hegemony;  dominating or ruling.  Describes a situation where a dominant group exercises power and influence over others, often subtly and indirectly. | The hegemonic power of the United States in the post-Cold War era was undeniable. 美国在冷战后时代的霸权力量是不可否认的。| dominant, prevailing, controlling | subordinate, subservient, weak
