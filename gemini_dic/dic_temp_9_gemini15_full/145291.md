overbragged:/ˌoʊvərˈbræɡd/| v. |吹嘘过度；夸大其词 |To boast or brag excessively; to exaggerate one's accomplishments or qualities. |例句：He overbragged about his achievements at the interview, leaving a bad impression. 他在面试中夸夸其谈自己的成就，留下了不好的印象。

例句：She overbragged about her trip to Europe, making everyone else feel jealous and unimpressed. 她对欧洲之行夸夸其谈，让其他人感到嫉妒和不以为然。


近义词：boast, exaggerate, hyperbolize, embellish


反义词：understate, downplay, minimize


词性:动词
