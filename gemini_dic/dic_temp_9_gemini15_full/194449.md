self-repeating:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈpiːtɪŋ/| adj.| 自重复的，反复出现的 |  Describes something that repeats itself, either in a pattern or randomly. This adjective is used to characterize processes, systems, or patterns that exhibit a recursive or iterative nature. It suggests a cyclical or recurring behavior. | 例句：The self-repeating pattern on the wallpaper was hypnotic.  壁纸上自重复的图案令人着迷。

The self-repeating error message frustrated the user.  反复出现的错误信息让用户感到沮丧。


近义词：recursive, iterative, cyclical, recurrent, repetitive


反义词：unique, singular, non-repetitive, one-off


词性:形容词
