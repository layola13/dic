guardful:/ˈɡɑːrdfəl/ | adj. |警惕的，谨慎的 | Careful to avoid potential danger or risk; cautious.  |例句：He was guardful of strangers and avoided eye contact. 他对陌生人很警惕，避免眼神接触。
例句：She moved guardfully through the dark alley, clutching her purse tightly. 她谨慎地穿过黑暗的小巷，紧紧抓住她的钱包。|近义词：wary, cautious, vigilant, circumspect |反义词：reckless, careless, heedless, incautious |形容词
