predecided:/ˌpriːdɪˈsaɪdɪd/| adj.| 预先决定的，预先商定的 | Already decided in advance; determined beforehand.  |The meeting's agenda was predetermined, with topics already decided.  会议的议程是预先确定的，主题已经预先决定。| predetermined, settled, planned | undecided, impromptu, spontaneous | 形容词
