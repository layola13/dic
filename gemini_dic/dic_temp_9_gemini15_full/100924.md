influenced:/ˈɪnflʊənst/| v.| 影响，左右 | to affect or change (someone or something) in an indirect but usually significant way. |例句：His ideas influenced a generation of artists. 他的思想影响了一代艺术家。|例句：The weather influenced our decision to stay home. 天气影响了我们待在家里的决定。|近义词：affected, impacted, swayed, shaped |反义词：unaffected, unaffected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music influences my mood. = 音乐影响我的心情。
His opinions influence public policy. = 他的观点影响着公共政策。

一般过去时:
The movie influenced my decision to travel. = 这部电影影响了我旅行的决定。
Her speech influenced the voters. = 她的演讲影响了选民。

现在完成时:
His work has influenced countless artists. = 他的作品影响了无数艺术家。
The event has influenced my perspective. = 这件事影响了我的观点。

过去完成时:
The earlier events had influenced the outcome of the war. = 早期事件影响了战争的结果。
His childhood experiences had influenced his later work. = 他童年的经历影响了他后来的作品。

将来时:
The new technology will influence how we live. = 新技术将影响我们的生活方式。
Her upcoming book will influence the debate. = 她即将出版的书将影响这场辩论。

虚拟语气:
If he had influenced the decision, things would be different. = 如果他影响了这个决定，事情就会不一样。
I wish I had influenced him to study harder. = 我希望我曾经影响他更努力学习。

被动语态:
His art was influenced by his travels. = 他的艺术受到了他旅行的影响。
The committee's decision was influenced by lobbyists. = 委员会的决定受到了游说者的影响。

疑问句:
What influenced your decision? = 什么影响了你的决定？
Who influenced your choice of career? = 谁影响了你职业的选择？

否定句:
The weather didn't influence our plans. = 天气并没有影响我们的计划。
His advice didn't influence her decision. = 他的建议并没有影响她的决定。


PHRASE->
be influenced by = 受...的影响
under the influence of = 在...的影响下
heavily influenced = 深受...影响
mutually influenced = 互相影响
have a strong influence on = 对...有很大的影响
