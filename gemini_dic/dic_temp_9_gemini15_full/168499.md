词源:
The word "problematical" comes from the noun "problem," ultimately derived from the Greek word "problema" (πρόβλημα), meaning "something thrown forward," "a question proposed for solution," or "a difficulty."  The suffix "-ical" is used to form adjectives, indicating a relationship to or characteristic of the base word.
