cossacks:/ˈkɒzæks/| n.|哥萨克人|A Cossack is a member of a group of predominantly East Slavic peoples who traditionally inhabited the Pontic-Caspian Steppe and are known for their horsemanship and military skills.  They were often employed as mercenaries and border guards.  Their history is marked by both independence and subjugation under various empires.|The Cossacks fought bravely in many battles.|近义词：cavalry, warrior|反义词：civilian
