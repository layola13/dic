meconium:/məˈkoʊniəm/| n. |胎粪|The dark, sticky, greenish-black fecal material present in the intestines of a newborn infant.  It consists of cells shed from the intestinal lining, bile pigments, and other substances.  |The newborn baby passed its meconium shortly after birth.  新生儿出生后不久排出了胎粪。|  |  |noun
