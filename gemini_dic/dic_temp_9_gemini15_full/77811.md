generalship:/ˈdʒɛnərəlʃɪp/ | n. |将才；统帅才能；指挥才能；领导才能 |The skill and ability of a general in leading and commanding troops;  the qualities of effective leadership.  It encompasses strategic thinking, tactical execution,  motivational skills, and the ability to inspire confidence and loyalty.| 例句：His generalship was instrumental in winning the battle. 他的将才对赢得这场战役至关重要。


例句：The army's success was largely due to the exceptional generalship of its commander.  这支军队的成功很大程度上归功于其指挥官杰出的统帅才能。


近义词：command, leadership, commandership, strategic acumen


反义词：ineptitude, incompetence, mismanagement


词性：名词
