nonpersistency:/ˌnɒnpəˈsɪstənsi/ | n. |非持久性，短暂性 | The lack of persistence; the tendency not to last or continue.  It refers to something that is not permanent or lasting.  For example, a nonpersistent memory is one that is easily forgotten.  | Example: The nonpersistency of the data meant we had to re-enter it every time we started the program.  非持久性数据意味着我们每次启动程序都必须重新输入它。|近义词：transitoriness, ephemerality, impermanence |反义词：permanence, persistence, durability |名词
