ginned:/dʒɪnd/| v.| 剥籽；使…兴奋；|To remove the seeds from (cotton) by a machine.  Also, to make (someone) excited or stimulated.|例句：The cotton was ginned before it was spun into thread. 棉花在纺成纱线之前已经过轧棉机轧棉。 They ginned themselves up for the big game.  他们为了这场重要的比赛而兴奋起来。|近义词：separate, excite, stimulate |反义词：leave alone, calm, sedate

PHRASE->
gin up (something):  to create or increase something (often excitement or interest)  (制造或增加某些东西，通常是兴奋或兴趣)
gin out: to exhaust completely (完全耗尽)
cotton gin: a machine for separating cotton fibers from seeds (轧棉机)
ginning machine: another name for a cotton gin (轧棉机)
ginned up: excited, stimulated (兴奋的，被刺激的)
