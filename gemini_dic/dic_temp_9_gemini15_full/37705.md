companying:/kəmˈpænɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 陪伴的；伴随的 | Accompanying something means happening or existing at the same time, or being present with something else.  | Example:  The accompanying documents are essential for processing your application. 随附的文件对于处理您的申请至关重要。


The accompanying music added to the film's emotional impact.  伴奏音乐增强了电影的情感冲击力。|近义词：attendant, concurrent, simultaneous | 反义词：separate, detached
