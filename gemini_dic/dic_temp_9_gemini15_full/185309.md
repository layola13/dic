retri:  /ˈrɛtri/ | n. | 收回；取回；召回 |  Retrieval; the act of getting something back.  |例句：The company issued a retri of the faulty product. 公司召回了有缺陷的产品。|近义词：recall, recovery, retrieval |反义词：loss, relinquishment |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog retrieves the ball. = 狗把球捡回来了。
She retrieves her lost purse. = 她找回了她丢失的钱包。


一般过去时:
He retrieved the file from the trash. = 他从垃圾桶里找回了文件。
They retrieved the sunken treasure. = 他们打捞回了沉船宝藏。


现在完成时:
I have retrieved all the necessary documents. = 我已经找回了所有必要的文档。
She has retrieved her lost memories. = 她已经找回了她失去的记忆。


过去完成时:
He had retrieved the information before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经找回了信息。
They had retrieved the lost child before nightfall. = 他们天黑前已经找回了丢失的孩子。


将来时:
I will retrieve the package from the post office. = 我将从邮局取回包裹。
They will retrieve the data from the server. = 他们将从服务器检索数据。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would retrieve the book from the library. = 如果我有时间，我会从图书馆把书取回来。
I wish I could retrieve the deleted files. = 我希望我能找回已删除的文件。


被动语态:
The lost data was retrieved successfully. = 丢失的数据成功找回。
The stolen car has been retrieved by the police. = 被盗车辆已被警方找回。


疑问句:
Can you retrieve the email? = 你能找回那封邮件吗？
Where can I retrieve the information? = 我在哪里可以检索到这些信息？


否定句:
I couldn't retrieve the password. = 我无法找回密码。
He didn't retrieve his belongings from the hotel. = 他没有从酒店取回他的行李。


PHRASE->
