circumstance:/ˈsɜːrkəmstæns/| n. |环境；情况；境遇 |The word "circumstance" refers to a fact or condition connected with or relevant to an event or action. It often describes the surrounding conditions or facts that influence a situation.  |例句：The circumstances surrounding the accident are still unclear. 事故发生的原因仍然不明确。
例句：He overcame many difficult circumstances to achieve his goals. 他克服了许多困难的环境才达到他的目标。|近义词：situation, condition, state of affairs |反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The circumstances are favorable. = 环境有利。
His current circumstances are difficult. = 他目前的处境艰难。

一般过去时:
The circumstances were very different then. = 那时的环境大不相同。
The circumstances of his death remain a mystery. = 他死亡的经过仍然是个谜。

现在完成时:
Circumstances have changed dramatically since then. = 自那以后情况发生了巨大的变化。
The circumstances have made it impossible for us to continue. = 情况使我们无法继续下去。

过去完成时:
The circumstances had already been decided before the meeting. = 会议之前情况已经确定了。
The circumstances had worsened considerably by the time help arrived. = 等到救援到来时，情况已经恶化了很多。


将来时:
Circumstances will dictate our next move. = 情况将决定我们的下一步行动。
We will adapt to whatever circumstances arise. = 我们将适应出现的任何情况。


虚拟语气:
If the circumstances were different, I would have acted differently. = 如果情况不同，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish the circumstances were better for everyone involved. = 我希望所有相关人员的情况都能更好。

被动语态:
The decision was influenced by the circumstances. = 这个决定受到了环境的影响。
His actions were dictated by the circumstances. = 他的行为受制于环境。

疑问句:
What are the circumstances? = 情况如何？
Under what circumstances did this happen? = 在什么情况下发生这件事的？

否定句:
The circumstances were not favorable for a quick resolution. = 情况不利于迅速解决问题。
It wasn't under normal circumstances that this happened. = 这不是在正常情况下发生的。
