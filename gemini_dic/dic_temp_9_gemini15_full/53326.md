disaccommodate:/ˌdɪs əˈkɒmədeɪt/ | vt. | 使…不方便；对…造成不便；使…不适 | To cause inconvenience or discomfort to someone or something; to make it difficult for someone or something. |  例句：The new regulations disaccommodated many small businesses. 新规给许多小企业带来了不便。

例句：The cramped seating disaccommodated the passengers. 狭窄的座位让乘客感到不舒服。


近义词：inconvenience, trouble, hinder, impede


反义词：accommodate, help, assist


词性：动词
