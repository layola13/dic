divertimento:/ˌdɪvɜːrˈtɪmentoʊ/ | n. | divertimento |一种轻快活泼的器乐作品，通常由多个乐章组成，风格轻松愉快。| A divertimento is a light-hearted instrumental composition, typically consisting of several movements, characterized by a light and cheerful style.  It often features a playful and virtuosic character, showcasing the instrumentalists' skills. |Example: Mozart composed several delightful divertimenti for various instrumental combinations.|近义词：Serenade, suite, entertainment|反义词：Symphony (in terms of weight and seriousness)
