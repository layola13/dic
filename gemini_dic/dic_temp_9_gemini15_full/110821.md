linocut:/ˈlɪnəʊkʌt/|/ˈlɪnoʊkʌt/| n. |木刻版画；油画木刻|A linocut is a printmaking technique where a design is carved into a linoleum block, which is then inked and printed onto paper or fabric.  It's similar to woodcut but uses a softer material.|The artist created a beautiful linocut depicting a rural landscape.  艺术家创作了一幅描绘乡村风景的美丽的木刻版画。|woodcut, relief print|  |
