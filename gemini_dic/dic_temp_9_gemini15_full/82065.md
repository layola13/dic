gritters:/ˈɡrɪtərz/| n. |撒盐车；（尤指用于融化冰雪的）洒砂车|A gritter is a vehicle used to spread grit or salt on roads, typically to improve traction in icy or snowy conditions.  It's often used in winter maintenance.|例句：The gritters were out all night trying to keep the roads clear. 撒盐车整夜都在努力保持道路畅通。|The council deployed several gritters to tackle the heavy snowfall.  市政部门部署了几辆撒盐车来应对大雪。|近义词：snow plow, spreader, salt truck|反义词：无
