fall:/fɔːl/ /fɔːl/| n., v.|落下，秋天，瀑布，下降，跌倒|

英文解析:
As a noun, "fall" refers to the action of dropping from a higher to a lower level, the season between summer and winter, a waterfall, or a decline in something. As a verb, it means to drop from a higher level, to become lower in amount or value, or to lose one's balance and come to the ground.


例句:
The leaves fall from the trees in autumn. 秋天的树叶从树上落下。

The fall of the Roman Empire was a significant historical event. 罗马帝国的衰落是一个重要的历史事件。

The waterfall created a spectacular fall. 瀑布形成了壮观的瀑布景观。

He fell down the stairs and broke his leg. 他从楼梯上摔下来摔断了腿。


近义词:
(n.) autumn, decline, drop, plunge, tumble, cascade
(v.) drop, descend, plummet, tumble, collapse


反义词:
(n.) rise, ascent, climb, increase
(v.) rise, ascend, climb, increase


词性:
Noun, Verb
