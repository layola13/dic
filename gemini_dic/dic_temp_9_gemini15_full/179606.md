reemployment:/ˌriːɪmˈplɔɪmənt/| n. |再就业，重新就业|The act of employing someone again after a period of unemployment; the state of being employed again.  Reemployment programs aim to help individuals find work after job loss. |例句：After a year of unemployment, he finally found reemployment at a smaller company.  一年失业后，他最终在一家小公司找到了再就业机会。|例句：The government's reemployment initiatives have been successful in reducing unemployment rates. 政府的再就业计划在降低失业率方面取得了成功。|近义词：re-employment, job placement, reinstatement|反义词：unemployment, joblessness
