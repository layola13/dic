diverting:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪŋ/ | adj. | 有趣的；令人愉快的；转移注意力的 |  Amusing and entertaining; capable of distracting attention.  | The play was quite diverting.  这部戏剧相当有趣。 | The comedian's jokes were diverting, even though some were a bit crude.  喜剧演员的笑话很有趣，即使有些有点粗俗。| boring, tedious | serious, dull

The game is diverting. = 这游戏很有趣。

词源:

Diverting comes from the verb "divert," meaning to turn aside or distract.  The "-ing" suffix forms the present participle, which in this case functions as an adjective.  The root is from the Latin "divertere,"  meaning "to turn aside."
