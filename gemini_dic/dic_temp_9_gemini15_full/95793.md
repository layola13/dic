inconveniences:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniənsɪz/| n.|不便之处；麻烦；令人讨厌的事 |The plural form of inconvenience.  It refers to things that cause difficulty or trouble, making things less convenient. |例句：The constant flight delays caused numerous inconveniences for the passengers.  航班持续延误给乘客带来了诸多不便。

例句：We apologize for any inconveniences this may cause.  对此造成的不便，我们深表歉意。


近义词：troubles, difficulties, problems, hassles, annoyances |反义词：conveniences, advantages, comforts |词性:noun
