lifequake:/ˈlaɪfˌkweɪk/| n. |人生巨变，生活大地震|A lifequake is a significant and often sudden event that dramatically alters the course of someone's life.  It can be positive or negative, but it always involves a major shift in perspective or circumstances.  The term blends "life" and "earthquake" to emphasize the magnitude of the change. |例句：Losing my job was a real lifequake, forcing me to re-evaluate my priorities. 失去工作对我来说是一次真正的人生巨变，迫使我重新评估我的优先事项。|例句：Winning the lottery was a lifequake; my whole life changed overnight.  赢得彩票是一场人生巨变；我的整个生活一夜之间就改变了。|近义词：turning point, watershed moment, upheaval, crisis, transformation|反义词：continuity, stagnation, sameness
