flail:/fleɪl/| v.|挥舞；挣扎；无目的地活动|To swing or strike wildly or without control; to thrash about wildly.  |例句：He flailed his arms in the air in frustration. 他沮丧地挥舞着手臂。

例句：The drowning man flailed wildly in the water. 溺水的人在水中拼命挣扎。|近义词：thrash, swing, beat, flounder |反义词：control, manage, direct


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flails his arms when he's angry. = 他生气时挥舞手臂。
She flails around trying to find her keys. = 她到处乱翻找钥匙。

一般过去时:
The bird flailed its wings before taking flight. = 鸟儿起飞前拍打翅膀。
He flailed about helplessly in the deep snow. = 他在厚厚的雪地里无助地挣扎。

现在完成时:
He has flailed at the problem for hours without success. = 他几个小时都在努力解决这个问题，但没有成功。
She has flailed her arms so much that they are tired. = 她挥舞手臂挥舞得太多了，以至于手臂都累了。

过去完成时:
He had flailed at the door for minutes before it finally opened. = 他在门终于打开之前已经敲打了很久。
She had flailed about in the dark before finding the light switch. = 在找到电灯开关之前，她在黑暗中摸索了很久。

将来时:

She will flail around until she finds her glasses. = 她会到处乱翻直到找到她的眼镜。

虚拟语气:
If he flailed his arms like that, he would scare the children. = 如果他那样挥舞手臂，他会吓到孩子们的。
I wish I hadn't flailed so much during the interview. = 我真希望我在面试中不要那么慌乱。

被动语态:
His arms were flailed about wildly during the fight. = 在打斗中，他的手臂被猛烈地挥舞着。
The problem was flailed at by many experts before a solution was found. = 这个问题在找到解决方案之前，已被许多专家尝试解决。

疑问句:

Why did the fish flail on the deck? = 为什么鱼在甲板上挣扎？

否定句:
He didn't flail his arms; he just stood there calmly. = 他没有挥舞手臂；他只是平静地站在那里。
She didn't flail about; she moved with grace and precision. = 她没有慌乱；她行动优雅而精准。
