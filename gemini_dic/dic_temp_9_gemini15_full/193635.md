self-builder:/ˈsɛlfˌbɪldə(r)/| n. |自建房者；自行建造者 |A person who builds their own house or other structure, typically without the assistance of professional builders.  |He's a self-builder, meticulously crafting his dream home from the ground up. 他是一位自建房者，一丝不苟地从头开始建造他的梦想之家。|home builder, DIY enthusiast |professional builder, contractor|


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is a self-builder. = 他是一位自建房者。
She builds her own furniture. = 她自己制作家具。


一般过去时:
They were self-builders for their last project. = 他们上一个项目是自建的。
He built a shed in his backyard last year. = 他去年在后院建了一个棚屋。


现在完成时:
He has self-built three houses. = 他已经自建了三栋房子。
She has completed the framing of her new home. = 她已经完成了新家的框架搭建。


过去完成时:
They had already self-built a garage before they started on the house. = 他们在开始建房子之前就已经自建了一个车库。
She had finished the foundation before the first frost. = 在第一次霜冻来临之前，她已经完成了地基的建造。


将来时:
We will be self-builders for our next home. = 我们下一套房子将自己建造。
He will start building the walls next week. = 他下周将开始建造墙壁。


虚拟语气:
If I were a self-builder, I would save a lot of money. = 如果我是自建房者，我会省很多钱。
I wish I had the skills to be a self-builder. = 我希望我有自建房子的技能。


被动语态:
The house is being self-built by a young couple. = 这栋房子正由一对年轻夫妇自建。
The shed was built by him last summer. = 这个棚屋是去年夏天他建造的。


疑问句:
Are you a self-builder? = 你是自建房者吗？
Is he building the house himself? = 他是自己建房子吗？


否定句:
He is not a self-builder; he hired a contractor. = 他不是自建房者；他雇了一个承包商。
She didn't build the house herself; she had it built. = 她没有自己建房子；她找人建的。
