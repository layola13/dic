scaler:/ˈskeɪlər/| n. |缩放器；比例尺；刻度器；(鱼)鳞片清理器 |A scaler is a tool or device used to adjust scale or size, or to remove scales from something.  It can refer to software for scaling images, a device for measuring or adjusting scale, or a tool for cleaning fish scales. |Example: The image scaler enlarged the photo without losing too much quality.  缩放器放大了照片，而没有损失太多质量。  The chef used a scaler to clean the fish before cooking. 厨师在烹饪前用鳞片清理器清洁鱼。|近义词：magnifier, enlarger, descaler |反义词：reducer, minimizer |名词
