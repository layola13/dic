bylines:/ˈbaɪlaɪnz/| n. |署名行；作者简介；（报刊文章等的）作者姓名栏 |A byline is the line at the beginning of a newspaper article or other piece of writing that gives the author's name.  It also refers to the author's name itself.  |Example: The byline read "Jane Doe, Staff Writer."  署名行上写着“简·多伊，特约撰稿人”。|近义词：author's name, credit line |反义词：anonymity
