It implies a flaw existing prior to any external damage or influence. |Example: The predefective components led to the early failure of the machine. 预先有缺陷的部件导致机器过早失效。 The software contained predefective code that caused unexpected crashes. 该软件包含预先存在的缺陷代码，导致意外崩溃。|近义词：flawed, imperfect, faulty, deficient|反义词：perfect, flawless, impeccable|形容词
