preexaminations:/ˌpriːɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃənz/| n.|预考；预试；入学考试|Preliminary examinations conducted before the main examination.  These are tests or assessments given before the main examination to evaluate a candidate's readiness.|The preexaminations were rigorous, testing candidates on a wide range of subjects.|近义词： preliminary exams, qualifying exams |反义词：final examinations
