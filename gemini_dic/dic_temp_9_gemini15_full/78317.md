gerontocrats:/dʒɛrənˈtɑːkræts/| n.|老年统治者；老年政治家|A gerontocracy is a form of oligarchy where power is held by a small group of old people.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting that the elderly in power are out of touch with the needs and desires of younger generations and that their decisions are based on outdated ideas and perspectives.|Example: The country's gerontocracy was criticized for its unwillingness to embrace technological advancements.  这个国家的老人统治集团因不愿接受技术进步而受到批评。|近义词：oligarchs, elders in power|反义词：youthocracy
