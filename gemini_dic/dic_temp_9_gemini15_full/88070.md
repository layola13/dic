highlevel:/ˈhaɪ levl/| adj.|高级的；高层次的；高水平的|  High-level refers to something that is of a high rank, importance, or complexity. It often describes positions, meetings, or discussions that are significant and involve senior individuals or complex matters.  |例句：The company held a high-level meeting to discuss the merger.  这家公司举行了一次高级别会议来讨论合并事宜。

The high-level managers made the final decision. 高层管理人员做出了最终决定。|近义词：advanced, superior, senior, top-level |反义词：low-level, basic, elementary, primary
