fiche:/fiʃ/| n.|菲什卡；卡片式缩微胶片|A small card or sheet of film containing a photographic reduction of documents.  It is a method of storing large amounts of information in a small space.|例句：The library archives are stored on microfiche. 图书馆档案储存在缩微胶片上。

例句：She consulted the microfiche to find the relevant article. 她查阅缩微胶片来查找相关的文章。

近义词：microfilm, card, sheet|反义词：None|词性:名词
