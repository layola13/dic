effacements:/ɪˈfeɪsmənts/ | n. | 抹去；消除；磨灭 | The gradual effacements of the inscription on the tombstone made it illegible. 墓碑上的铭文逐渐被磨灭，变得无法辨认。 | 近义词：erasures, deletions, obliterations | 反义词：embellishments, enhancements


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist's signature is slowly effacing. = 艺术家的签名正在慢慢消失。
Effacements of memories occur naturally over time. = 随着时间的推移，记忆的磨灭自然发生。


一般过去时:
The rain effaced the footprints in the sand. = 雨水冲走了沙滩上的脚印。
The old painting had been effaced by time and neglect. = 这幅旧画因时间和疏忽而被磨灭了。


现在完成时:
The inscription has been effaced by the elements. = 铭文已被风化。
Years of neglect have effaced the details of the painting. = 多年的忽视已磨灭了绘画的细节。


过去完成时:
The original text had been effaced before the copy was made. = 原文在复制之前已被抹去。
By the time I arrived, the message had been effaced from the board. = 我到达时，信息已被从黑板上擦掉了。


将来时:
The inscription will be effaced by the weather eventually. = 铭文最终会被风化。
The new paint will efface the old markings on the wall. = 新油漆会抹去墙上的旧标记。


虚拟语气:
If the inscription had not been effaced, we would know the date. = 如果铭文没有被磨灭，我们会知道日期。
I wish the memories of that day hadn’t been effaced. = 我希望那天的事情没有被遗忘。


被动语态:
The graffiti was effaced by the city workers. = 涂鸦被城市工人清理掉了。
The old photograph was effaced by the chemicals. = 旧照片被化学药品损坏。


疑问句:
Have the markings on the stone been effaced? = 石头上的标记被磨灭了吗？
Were the details of the contract effaced intentionally? = 合同的细节是被故意抹去吗？


否定句:
The inscription was not effaced by the rain. = 铭文没有被雨水冲刷掉。
The efforts to efface the past were unsuccessful. = 试图抹去过去并未成功。
