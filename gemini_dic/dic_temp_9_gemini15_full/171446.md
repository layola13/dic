puerilities:/ˌpjuəˈrɪlətiz/| n. |幼稚的行为；琐碎的事；无聊的事 |Childish or silly actions or ideas.  The essay was filled with puerilities and lacked substance.  |例句：His speech was full of puerilities and irrelevant anecdotes. 他的演讲充满了幼稚的行为和无关紧要的轶事。

例句：The book is full of puerilities that will bore most readers. 这本书充满了会让大多数读者厌烦的幼稚内容。


近义词：childishness, silliness, trivialities, inanities, follies|反义词：maturity, wisdom, seriousness, significance


词源:  From Latin *puerilis ("childish"), from puer ("boy").  The suffix "-ities" indicates a plural of qualities or states.
