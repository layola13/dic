hypergeometric:/ˌhaɪpərˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|超几何的；超几何分布的|Relating to or characterized by hypergeometric functions or distributions.  A hypergeometric distribution is a probability distribution that describes the probability of getting a certain number of successes in a sequence of draws from a finite population without replacement.|The probability of drawing three red balls from a bag containing five red balls and three blue balls is described by a hypergeometric distribution. |geometric, binomial|none|
