philanthropy:/fɪˈlænθrəpi/ | n. |慈善；慈善事业 |The voluntary giving of help to those in need, especially through donating money to good causes.  It involves charitable giving and actions aimed at improving the well-being of others. |例句：His philanthropy extended to supporting numerous educational and medical charities.  他的慈善事业延伸到支持许多教育和医疗慈善机构。

例句：The museum benefited greatly from her generous philanthropy.  博物馆从她慷慨的慈善事业中受益匪浅。


近义词：charity, beneficence, altruism | 反义词：selfishness, greed, avarice


词源:  From Late Latin *philanthropia, from Greek φιλανθρωπία (philanthropia), from φίλος (philos, "loving") + ἄνθρωπος (anthrōpos, "human being").  The term literally means "love of humanity."
