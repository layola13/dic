overbragging: /ˌoʊvərˈbræɡɪŋ/ | n. | 吹牛，过分夸耀 | The act of boasting excessively or about unimportant things.  It implies a degree of arrogance and a lack of self-awareness. | Example:  His overbragging about his accomplishments alienated his colleagues.  他的夸夸其谈让他与同事疏远了。|近义词：boasting, showing off, bragging, grandstanding |反义词：humility, modesty, self-deprecation |名词
