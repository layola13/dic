extrapolable:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪbəl/ | adj. | 可外推的，可推断的 | Capable of being extrapolated; able to be extended beyond known limits by inference or projection.  It refers to data or trends that can be reasonably extended or projected beyond the observed range. | The data set is extrapolable to future years, allowing us to predict sales growth. 这组数据可以推断到未来几年，使我们能够预测销售增长。 | inferable, projectable | unexrapolable, unprojectable |
