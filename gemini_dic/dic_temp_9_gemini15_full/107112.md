ladrone:/læˈdroʊn/| n.|强盗，盗贼|A ladrón is a thief or robber, especially one who uses violence or threats.  The term is of Spanish origin and often carries a connotation of a more daring or violent criminal than a simple thief.|例句：The ladrón stole all the jewels from the mansion. 这个强盗偷走了豪宅里所有的珠宝。|近义词：thief, robber, bandit, burglar|反义词：police officer, law enforcement
