millering:/ˈmɪlərɪŋ/| n.|磨粉；碾磨；铣削|The process of grinding grain into flour or other powdered substances, or the process of machining metal using a milling machine.|例句：The millering of wheat is a crucial step in bread making.  小麦磨粉是面包制作的关键步骤。|近义词：grinding, milling, machining|反义词：None specifically, but opposite concepts could include "leaving whole" or "unprocessed".|名词
