science:/ˈsaɪəns/| n. |科学，学科|The systematic study of the structure and behavior of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment.  科学，通过观察和实验系统地研究物质世界和自然界的结构和行为。|例句：Science plays a vital role in our daily lives. 科学在我们日常生活中扮演着至关重要的角色。|例句：She dedicated her life to the advancement of science. 她毕生致力于科学进步。|近义词：knowledge, scholarship, learning |反义词：ignorance, superstition

一般现在时:
Science explains many natural phenomena. = 科学解释了许多自然现象。
Scientists conduct experiments to test hypotheses. = 科学家进行实验来检验假设。

一般过去时:
Scientists discovered a new planet. = 科学家发现了一颗新的行星。
The experiment failed because of a flaw in the design. = 实验由于设计缺陷而失败了。


现在完成时:
Scientists have made significant progress in genetic research. = 科学家在基因研究方面取得了重大进展。
Many breakthroughs have occurred in the field of medicine. = 医学领域发生了许多突破。


过去完成时:
They had already completed the research before the funding ran out. = 在资金耗尽之前，他们已经完成了研究。
Scientists had long suspected the existence of dark matter. = 科学家们长期以来一直怀疑暗物质的存在。


将来时:
Scientists will continue to explore the universe. = 科学家们将继续探索宇宙。

被动语态:
The experiment was conducted carefully. = 实验进行得很仔细。

疑问句:
