conveyor:/kənˈveɪər/| n.|传送带，输送机|A conveyor is a mechanical device for moving materials from one place to another, often using a continuously moving belt or chain.|例句：The conveyor belt transported the boxes to the loading dock. 传送带将箱子运送到装货码头。The factory uses a conveyor system to move parts between workstations. 工厂使用输送系统在工作站之间移动零件。|近义词：transporter, carrier, belt|反义词: None.
