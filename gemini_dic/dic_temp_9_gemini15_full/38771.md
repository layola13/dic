conduce:/kənˈduːs/| vt.|有助于，促成|to contribute to a particular result.|例句：Regular exercise can conduce to better health.  规律的锻炼有助于增强体质。

例句：His efforts conduced to the success of the project. 他的努力促成了项目的成功。

近义词：contribute to, lead to, facilitate, promote|反义词：hinder, impede, obstruct|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Regular exercise conduces to better health. = 规律的锻炼有助于增强体质。
A healthy diet conduces to a longer life. = 健康的饮食有助于延长寿命。

一般过去时:
His hard work conduced to his success. = 他的努力促使了他的成功。
The new policy conduced to economic growth. = 新政策促进了经济增长。

现在完成时:
His efforts have conduced to significant improvements. = 他的努力已经带来了显著的改进。
The changes have conduced to a more efficient workflow. = 这些变化已经促成了更高效的工作流程。

过去完成时:
The previous government's policies had conduced to the economic crisis. = 上届政府的政策导致了经济危机。
Years of neglect had conduced to the building's deterioration. = 多年的忽视导致了建筑物的损坏。

将来时:
This new technology will conduce to greater efficiency. = 这项新技术将有助于提高效率。
The proposed reforms will conduce to a fairer society. = 提出的改革将有助于建立一个更公平的社会。

虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, it would conduce to better results. = 如果我们更努力工作，将会取得更好的成果。
If they had cooperated, it might have conduced to a better outcome. = 如果他们合作，可能会带来更好的结果。

被动语态:
Better health is conduced to by regular exercise. = 规律的锻炼有助于增强体质。(被动语态略显不自然，很少使用)
Economic growth was conduced to by the new policies. = 新政策促进了经济增长。(被动语态略显不自然，很少使用)

疑问句:
Does regular sleep conduce to better concentration? = 规律的睡眠是否有助于提高注意力？
Will this strategy conduce to the desired outcome? = 这个策略会带来预期的结果吗？

否定句:
Lack of sleep does not conduce to good health. = 缺乏睡眠无益于健康。
His carelessness did not conduce to a successful outcome. = 他的粗心大意没有带来成功的结果。
