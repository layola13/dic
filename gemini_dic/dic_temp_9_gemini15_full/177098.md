reapplies:/ˌriːəˈplaɪz/| v.|重新申请，再次申请|To apply again; to make a new application.|例句：He reapplied for the job after being rejected the first time. 他第一次被拒绝后又重新申请了这份工作。

例句：The company reapplied for the building permit after addressing the initial concerns. 公司在解决了最初的顾虑后再次申请了建筑许可证。


近义词：renew, reapply for, submit again|反义词：withdraw, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reapplies for the scholarship every year. 他每年都重新申请奖学金。
She reapplies for her passport every ten years. 她每十年重新申请一次护照。


一般过去时:
He reapplied for the job last week. 他上周重新申请了这份工作。
She reapplied for her driver's license after it was lost. 驾照丢了之后，她重新申请了一张。


现在完成时:
He has reapplied for the position three times. 他已经三次重新申请了这个职位。
She has reapplied for her visa since it expired. 自从签证过期后，她已经重新申请了。


过去完成时:
He had reapplied for the grant before the deadline. 他在截止日期之前已经重新申请了拨款。
She had reapplied for the job several times before she finally got it. 在她最终得到这份工作之前，她已经好几次重新申请了。


将来时:
He will reapply for the loan next month. 他下个月将重新申请贷款。
She will reapply for the university after improving her grades. 她成绩提高后会重新申请大学。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reapply for the position immediately. 如果我是你，我会立即重新申请这个职位。
I wish I had reapplied for the scholarship sooner. 我真希望我早点重新申请奖学金。


被动语态:
The application was reapplied for by many candidates. 许多候选人都重新申请了。
The permit was reapplied for after the corrections were made. 在修改之后，许可证被重新申请。


疑问句:
Will he reapply for the job? 他会重新申请这份工作吗？
Has she reapplied for the visa yet? 她已经重新申请签证了吗？


否定句:
He did not reapply for the scholarship this year. 他今年没有重新申请奖学金。
She will not reapply for the job after being rejected twice.  她被拒绝两次后将不会重新申请这份工作。
