cantopop:/ˈkæntoʊpɑːp/ | n. |粤语流行音乐|Cantonese pop music; a genre of popular music sung in Cantonese, predominantly originating from Hong Kong and Macau.  It blends elements of Western pop music with traditional Cantonese musical influences. |Example:  Many young people in Hong Kong enjoy listening to cantopop.  许多香港年轻人喜欢听粤语流行音乐。|近义词：Cantonese pop, Hong Kong pop |反义词：无
