presentee:/ˌprɛzənˈtiː/ | n. | 出席者，到场者 | A person who is present at a particular place or event.  | Example: The presentees at the meeting were all senior managers.  会议出席者都是高级管理人员。 |近义词：attendee, participant | 反义词：absentee


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The presentee signs the register. = 出席者签署登记簿。
Several presentees are discussing the agenda. = 好几位出席者正在讨论议程。


一般过去时:
The presentee left early. = 出席者早退了。
All presentees received a certificate. = 所有出席者都收到了证书。


现在完成时:
Every presentee has received an email. = 每位出席者都收到了电子邮件。
Most presentees have already arrived. = 大多数出席者已经到了。


过去完成时:
The presentee had already spoken before the chairman arrived. = 主席到达之前，这位出席者已经发言了。
All presentees had left by the time the meeting ended. = 会议结束时，所有出席者都已经离开了。


将来时:
The presentee will be recognized for their contribution. = 出席者将因其贡献而获得认可。
All presentees will receive a follow-up email. = 所有出席者都将收到后续电子邮件。


虚拟语气:
If the presentee were here, they would answer your question. = 如果这位出席者在这里，他会回答你的问题。
I wish the presentee had been more engaged. = 我希望这位出席者能更积极一些。


被动语态:
The presentee was thanked for their participation. = 出席者因他们的参与而受到感谢。
The presentees were given a warm welcome. = 出席者受到了热烈的欢迎。


疑问句:
Is the presentee comfortable? = 出席者感觉舒服吗？
Are all the presentees accounted for? = 所有出席者都到齐了吗？


否定句:
The presentee was not present for the entire meeting. = 出席者并未参加整个会议。
Not all presentees completed the survey. = 并非所有出席者都完成了调查问卷。
