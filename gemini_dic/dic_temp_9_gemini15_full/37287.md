commanderin:/kəˈmændərɪn/| n.|指挥官的；指挥官夫人|A commander is a person, especially a military officer, who is in command of others.  The word "commanderin" is less common and primarily functions as an attributive adjective, describing something related to a commander or a commander's wife. |例句：The commander's orders were clear. 指挥官的命令很明确。  The commander-in-chief addressed the troops. 总司令向部队发表讲话。|近义词：commanding officer, leader, superior|反义词：subordinate, follower|名词
