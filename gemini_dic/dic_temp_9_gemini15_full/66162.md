excerptions:/ɪkˈsɜːrpʃənz/ | n. |摘录；节选 | Extracts from a text or speech.  A selection of passages or items from a longer work. |例句：The article included excerpts from his new book. 这篇文章节选了他新书的部分内容。|例句：These excerpts are representative of his writing style.  这些摘录代表了他的写作风格。|近义词： extracts, selections, passages, snippets |反义词： entirety, whole, complete text
