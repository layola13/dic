prior to:/ˈpraɪər tuː/| adj. prep.|在…之前，先于|Prior to means "before" in time or order.  It is used to indicate that something happened or existed before another event or time. |例句：The meeting was canceled prior to the announcement. 会议在公告发布之前就被取消了。
例句：Prior to starting the project, we need to secure funding. 在启动项目之前，我们需要确保资金到位。|近义词：before, preceding, beforehand |反义词：after, following, subsequent |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The event takes place prior to the holiday. = 活动在假期之前举行。
We always check the weather prior to our hike. = 我们徒步旅行前总是检查天气。


一般过去时:
They completed the task prior to the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了任务。
She reviewed the document prior to signing it. = 她在签署文件之前仔细阅读了文件。


现在完成时:
We have completed all preparations prior to the launch. = 我们在发布之前已经完成了所有准备工作。
She had already finished her homework prior to watching TV. = 她在看电视之前已经完成了作业。


过去完成时:
He had already left prior to my arrival. = 在我到达之前他已经离开了。
They had discussed the issue prior to making the decision. = 他们在做出决定之前已经讨论过这个问题。


将来时:
We will finalize the contract prior to the meeting. = 我们将在会议之前敲定合同。
She will send the email prior to the presentation. = 她将在演示文稿之前发送电子邮件。


虚拟语气:
If I had known about the deadline, I would have submitted the report prior to the deadline. = 如果我知道截止日期，我会在截止日期前提交报告。
I wish I had completed the assignment prior to the weekend. = 我希望我在周末之前就完成了作业。


被动语态:
The preparations were completed prior to the event. = 活动前的准备工作已经完成。
The documents were reviewed prior to being signed. = 文件在签署之前都经过了审核。


疑问句:
Did you submit the application prior to the deadline? = 你在截止日期前提交申请了吗？
Was the meeting scheduled prior to the conference? = 会议是在会议之前安排的吗？


否定句:
They did not complete the project prior to the deadline. = 他们没有在截止日期前完成项目。
She did not inform us prior to making the changes. = 她没有在做出更改之前通知我们。


PHRASE->
prior to that = 在那之前
prior to commencing = 在开始之前
prior to departure = 在出发之前
prior to the implementation = 在实施之前
subsequent to and prior to = 在之后和之前
