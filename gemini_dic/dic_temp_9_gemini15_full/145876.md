overdesire:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪzaɪər/| v.|过度渴望，极度想要|To want something very much, excessively, or beyond what is reasonable or healthy.|例句：He overdesired the promotion, neglecting his current responsibilities. 他过度渴望升职，忽略了目前的工作职责。|例句：Her overdesire for wealth led her down a path of questionable ethics. 她对财富的过度渴望让她走上了一条道德有问题的道路。|近义词：crave, yearn for, long for, covet|反义词：disregard, indifference, apathy
