scagliola:/skæˈɡljɔːlə/| n. |斯卡利奥拉（一种装饰性石膏）|A type of decorative plaster that imitates marble or other fine stones. It is made by mixing finely ground marble dust, glue, and pigments, and then applying it to a surface in thin layers.  The resulting surface is highly polished to achieve a lustrous, marble-like finish. |The artisans used scagliola to create stunning columns that looked like real marble. 工匠们用斯卡利奥拉制作了看起来像真大理石的精美柱子。|marbling,  stonework |(无明确反义词，可考虑：plain plaster, unadorned surface) |名词
