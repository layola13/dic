incoherent:/ˌɪnkəʊˈhɪərənt/| adj.| 不连贯的；语无伦次的；不协调的 | lacking logical or meaningful connection; rambling;unintelligible |The speaker's incoherent ramblings were difficult to follow.  演讲者语无伦次的话很难理解。|近义词：disjointed, rambling, confused, illogical |反义词：coherent, logical, consistent, clear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His speech is incoherent. = 他的讲话不连贯。
The argument seems incoherent to me. = 这个论点在我看来不连贯。


一般过去时:
Her explanation was incoherent. = 她的解释语无伦次。
The message he left was completely incoherent. = 他留下的信息完全无法理解。


现在完成时:
His thinking has become increasingly incoherent. = 他的思维越来越混乱。
The evidence presented has been incoherent. = 提出的证据前后矛盾。


过去完成时:
His behavior had become incoherent before the accident. = 事故发生前，他的行为已经变得不正常了。
The story she told had been incoherent from the beginning. = 她从一开始讲的故事就前言不搭后语。


将来时:
His speech will likely be incoherent if he continues drinking. = 如果他继续喝酒，他的讲话很可能会语无伦次。
The plan will be incoherent without further clarification. = 没有进一步的说明，这个计划将会不连贯。


虚拟语气:
If he were more coherent, we could understand him better. = 如果他更连贯一些，我们会更容易理解他。
I wish my thoughts were more coherent. = 我希望我的思路更清晰一些。


被动语态:
His ideas were considered incoherent by the committee. = 他的想法被委员会认为是不连贯的。
The report was found to be incoherent and unreliable. = 报告被发现前后矛盾且不可靠。


疑问句:
Is his argument coherent or incoherent? = 他的论点连贯还是不连贯？
Why is the narrative so incoherent? = 为什么叙述如此混乱？


否定句:
His response was not incoherent; it was simply misunderstood. = 他的回应并非不连贯；只是被误解了。
The instructions were not incoherent, but very detailed. = 说明书并非不连贯，而是非常详细。
