self-reputation:/ˌsɛlfˌrɛpjʊˈteɪʃən/| n. | 自我评价；名誉；名声 |  One's self-reputation is the opinion one holds of oneself, often influencing behavior and self-esteem.  It's distinct from one's reputation among others. | 例句：He carefully cultivated his self-reputation as a brilliant strategist. 他小心翼翼地塑造了他作为一位杰出战略家的自我评价。

例句：Her self-reputation as a hard worker preceded her. 她作为一名勤奋工作者的自我评价先于她本人。


近义词：self-image, self-esteem, self-worth | 反义词：self-deprecation, low self-esteem | 词性：名词
