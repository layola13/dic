pseudoedema's:/ˌsuːdoʊˈiːdɪməz/| n.|假性水肿|Apparent swelling that is not caused by fluid accumulation, but rather by other factors.  It mimics the appearance of edema (true swelling) but lacks the underlying physiological mechanism.  This can be caused by various conditions, including inflammation,  allergic reactions, and certain medications.|Example: The patient presented with pseudoedema's in the legs, but further investigation revealed no fluid retention.|近义词：bogus edema, apparent edema|反义词：edema (true swelling)
