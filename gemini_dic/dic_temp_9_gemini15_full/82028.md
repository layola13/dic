grips:/ɡrɪps/ | n. vt. | 抓住；掌握；理解；困境 |


英文解析:
As a noun, "grip" refers to the act of holding something firmly, a firm hold, or a handle.  As a verb, it means to hold something firmly or to understand something. It can also refer to a difficult situation or a strong influence.


例句:
The climber gripped the rock face tightly.  登山者紧紧抓住岩壁。

He has a firm grip on the situation. 他对局势有很好的掌控。


近义词:
Noun: grasp, hold, clasp, control, handle, power
Verb: grasp, seize, clutch, understand, comprehend


反义词:
Noun: release, loosen, let go
Verb: release, let go, misunderstand


词性:
Noun, Verb
