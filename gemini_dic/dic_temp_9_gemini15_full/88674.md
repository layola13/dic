hoaxed:/hoʊkst/ | v. | 欺骗；戏弄；恶作剧 | To deceive someone, typically by means of a trick or hoax.  |例句：The media hoaxed the public into believing the story was true. 媒体欺骗公众相信这个故事是真的。

例句：He was hoaxed out of his money by a confidence trickster. 他被一个骗子骗走了钱。


近义词：deceived, tricked, fooled, duped, conned | 反义词：enlightened, informed, revealed
