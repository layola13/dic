cabinetry:/ˈkæbɪnɪtri/| n. |橱柜；橱柜制作；细木工|The art and practice of designing, manufacturing, installing, and finishing cabinets.  It encompasses the selection of materials, construction techniques, and aesthetic considerations. |例句：The kitchen cabinetry is made of cherry wood. = 厨房的橱柜是用樱桃木做的。

例句：He's a master of cabinetry, creating exquisite pieces for discerning clients. = 他是橱柜制作大师，为眼光独到的客户创作精美作品。

近义词：cabinetwork, woodwork, joinery|反义词：None|词性：名词
