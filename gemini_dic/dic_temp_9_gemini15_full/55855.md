divines:/dɪˈvaɪnz/| v.|预言，预测；视为神圣|To discover or reveal something through supernatural means, or to declare something sacred or holy.|例句：The fortune teller divined that she would meet her soulmate soon.  算命先生预言她很快会遇到她的灵魂伴侣。

The priest divined the sacred text.  牧师解读了神圣的文本。|prophesy, foresee, predict|profane, unholy|动词
