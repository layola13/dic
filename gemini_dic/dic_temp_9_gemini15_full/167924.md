print:/prɪnt/| /prɪnt/| vt. n.|印刷；打印；出版；发表|To produce (text or images) on paper or other material using a printing press or similar device; to produce a copy of (a document or image) using a printer.|例句：The printer printed the document quickly. 打印机快速打印了文件。
The book was printed in 1990. 这本书出版于1990年。|近义词：publish, reproduce, imprint|反义词：erase, delete|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prints his photos at home. 他在家打印照片。
She prints her documents every day. 她每天打印文件。

一般过去时:
I printed the report last night. 我昨晚打印了报告。
They printed thousands of copies of the book. 他们印制了数千本该书。

现在完成时:
I have printed all the documents. 我已经打印了所有文件。
She has printed the photos for the exhibition. 她已经为展览打印了照片。

过去完成时:
He had printed the invitations before the party. 他在聚会前已经印好了请柬。
They had printed the posters before the announcement. 他们早在宣布之前就印好了海报。

将来时:
I will print the document tomorrow. 我明天将打印文件。
She will print the photos after the editing. 她将在编辑完成后打印照片。

虚拟语气:
If I had a printer, I would print the document myself. 如果我有一台打印机，我会自己打印文件。
I wish I had printed the report earlier. 我真希望我早点打印了报告。

被动语态:
The document was printed yesterday. 文件昨天被打印了。
The photos are being printed now. 照片正在被打印。

疑问句:
Did you print the document? 你打印文件了吗？
Has she printed the photos yet? 她打印照片了吗？

否定句:
I didn't print the report. 我没有打印报告。
She hasn't printed the documents yet. 她还没有打印文件。
