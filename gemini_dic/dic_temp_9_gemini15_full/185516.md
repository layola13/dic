revaccinate:/ˌriːˈvæksɪneɪt/ | vt. |再次接种疫苗；重新接种疫苗 |To vaccinate again, especially after a period of time has elapsed.  This is usually done to boost immunity or to protect against new variants of a disease. |例句：The doctor recommended that we revaccinate our children against the flu.医生建议我们再次给孩子们接种流感疫苗。

例句：It's important to revaccinate against measles, mumps and rubella every few years. 每隔几年接种一次麻疹、腮腺炎和风疹疫苗很重要。


近义词：reinoculate, reimmunize |反义词：deimmunize (though not a direct antonym, it represents the opposite action)|词性：动词
