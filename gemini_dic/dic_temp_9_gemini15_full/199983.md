skateboarding:/ˈskeɪtˌbɔːrdɪŋ/| n.|滑板运动|The activity of riding on a skateboard.|例句：Skateboarding is a popular extreme sport. 滑板运动是一项流行的极限运动。

例句：He enjoys skateboarding in the park every weekend. 他每个周末都喜欢在公园里玩滑板。


词源:  From "skateboard" + "-ing" (present participle suffix). "Skateboard" itself is a combination of "skate" (from Dutch schaats, meaning "ice skate") and "board".  The "-ing" suffix transforms the noun "skateboard" into a gerund, functioning as a noun representing the act of skateboarding.


近义词：skating (if referring to the general act of gliding on a board), board sliding


反义词：无 (没有直接的反义词，只能用与之相对的活动来表示反义，例如：reading, swimming)
