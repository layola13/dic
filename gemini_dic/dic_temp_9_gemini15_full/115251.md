manipulated:/məˈnɪpjuleɪtɪd/ | v. |操纵；控制；篡改 |To control or influence (a person or situation) cleverly or unscrupulously.  |例句：The dictator manipulated the election results. 独裁者操纵了选举结果。|例句：He skillfully manipulated the levers to control the machine. 他熟练地操纵杠杆来控制机器。|近义词：control, influence, manage, exploit |反义词：leave alone, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manipulates the data to support his theory. = 他操纵数据来支持他的理论。
She manipulates the conversation to her advantage. = 她操纵谈话以利于自己。


一般过去时:
The artist manipulated the clay to create a beautiful sculpture. = 艺术家操纵粘土创作了一个美丽的雕塑。
They manipulated the stock market to make a profit. = 他们操纵股市以获利。


现在完成时:
He has manipulated the evidence to hide the truth. = 他已经操纵证据来掩盖真相。
She has manipulated her way to the top of the company. = 她已经通过操纵手段爬到了公司的高层。


过去完成时:
They had manipulated the system before the audit. = 他们在审计之前就操纵了系统。
She had manipulated the situation to her benefit. = 她已经操纵局势以使自己受益。


将来时:

被动语态:
