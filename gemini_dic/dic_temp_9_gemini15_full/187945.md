rotifera:/roʊˈtɪfərə/| n. |轮虫|  Small, mostly aquatic, multicellular animals characterized by a ciliated corona used for feeding and locomotion.  They are found in a wide variety of freshwater and marine habitats, and are important components of many aquatic food webs. | Example:  Rotifers are a crucial food source for many fish larvae.  轮虫是许多鱼类幼虫的重要食物来源。|近义词:microscopic animals, zooplankton|反义词:None
