periscians:/pɛˈrɪskiənz/| n. |周日圈居民，生活在赤道地区的人|People living in the torrid zone, where the sun is always at a relatively high angle in the sky, and thus shadows never fall to the north or south.  The term is based on the observation that at certain times of year, the sun can appear to circle directly overhead.  This is a relatively rare occurrence and the term is largely of historical interest.|The periscians, living near the equator, experienced the sun’s almost vertical path.|近义词：equatorial inhabitants|反义词：暂无
