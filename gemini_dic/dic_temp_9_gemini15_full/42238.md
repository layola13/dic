countable: /ˈkaʊntəbl/ | adj. | 可数的 |  An adjective describing nouns that can be counted, having a singular and plural form.  It refers to things that exist as individual, separate units. | Examples:  There are five countable nouns in this sentence. 这句话中有五个可数名词。  We have many countable items in stock. 我们有很多可数的商品库存。 | 近义词: numerable, quantifiable | 反义词: uncountable, innumerable
