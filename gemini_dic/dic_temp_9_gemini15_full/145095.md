overappraisals:/ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪzəl/| n.|过高估价，高估|The act of placing too high a value on something; an assessment that significantly exceeds the actual worth.|The overappraisal of the property led to a significant loss when it was sold.  房产估价过高，出售时导致了重大损失。|The recent market surge resulted in many overappraisals. 最近市场飙升导致许多估价过高。|underappraisals|fair appraisals
