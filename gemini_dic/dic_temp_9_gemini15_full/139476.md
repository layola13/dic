obstinatenesses:/ˈɒbstɪnətˌnɛsɪz/| n. |顽固；固执；倔强|The plural form of obstinateness, referring to multiple instances or types of stubbornness or resistance to change.  It emphasizes the repeated or varied manifestations of this trait.|例句：His obstinatenesses in the face of overwhelming evidence were truly remarkable.  他的顽固不化，即使面对压倒性的证据也令人惊叹。
His various obstinatenesses made it difficult to work with him. 他各种各样的固执使和他共事变得困难。|近义词：stubbornness, mulishness, inflexibility, intractability|反义词：flexibility, compliance, yielding, amenability
