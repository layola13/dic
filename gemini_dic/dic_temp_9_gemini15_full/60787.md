electrometer:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒmɪtər/ | n. |静电计 | An instrument for measuring static electricity or very small electric currents.  It typically works by measuring the deflection of a lightweight needle or other indicator due to the electrostatic force. | Example: The electrometer was used to measure the charge on the capacitor.  静电计被用来测量电容器上的电荷。  |近义词: electrostatic meter, electroscope |反义词: None.
