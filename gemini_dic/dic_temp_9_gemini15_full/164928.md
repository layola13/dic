pregrades:/ˈpriːɡreɪdz/| n.|预科课程，预备课程|  Pre-grades refer to courses or programs designed to prepare students for a specific academic program or level, often bridging the gap between previous schooling and a higher level of education.  These courses might focus on improving foundational skills or introducing core concepts necessary for success in the subsequent program.|例句：She enrolled in pre-grades to improve her math skills before starting college. 她报读了预科课程来提高她的数学技能以便开始大学学习。|近义词：preparatory courses, foundational courses, bridging programs|反义词：advanced courses, graduate courses
