prophesied:/ˈprɑːfəsaɪzd/|/ˈprɒfəsaɪzd/| v.| 预言；预示 | To declare or predict (a future event) | The fortune teller prophesied a great change in my life.  算命先生预言我的生活将发生巨大的变化。|  The ancient texts prophesied the coming of a great king. 古代文本预言一位伟大的国王即将到来。| foretold, predicted, foreseen | refuted, denied


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He prophesies the end of the world. 他预言世界末日。
She prophesies good fortune for us. 她预言我们将有好运。

一般过去时:
The oracle prophesied victory for the army.  神谕预言军队将取得胜利。
The prophet prophesied famine and drought. 先知预言了饥荒和干旱。

现在完成时:
No one has prophesied such a catastrophic event. 没有人预言过如此灾难性的事件。
They have prophesied a new era of peace. 他们预言了一个新的和平时代。

过去完成时:
The ancient texts had prophesied the fall of the empire. 古代文本曾预言过帝国的衰落。
She had prophesied the storm, but nobody listened. 她曾预言过风暴，但没有人听。

将来时:
They will prophesy the future of the nation. 他们将预言国家的未来。
He will prophesy great changes in the economy. 他将预言经济的巨大变化。

虚拟语气:
If I could prophesy the future, I would be rich. 如果我能预言未来，我就会富有。
I wish I could prophesy the outcome of the election. 我希望我能预言选举的结果。

被动语态:
The future was prophesied by the seer. 未来被预言家预言了。
The coming disaster was prophesied long ago.  即将到来的灾难很久以前就被预言了。

疑问句:
Did the prophet prophesy correctly? 先知预言正确了吗？
What did the sibyl prophesy about the future?  女预言家对未来作了何种预言？

否定句:
The soothsayer did not prophesy any misfortune.  占卜师没有预言任何不幸。
He did not prophesy the success of the venture. 他没有预言这次冒险的成功。
