predeciding:/ˌpriːdɪˈsaɪdɪŋ/| adj.|预先决定的；先决的|Determining or deciding something in advance.  It implies a decision made before other factors or events are considered. This often leads to a lack of flexibility or consideration of alternative options.|例句：The predeciding factor in their choice of location was proximity to family.  他们的选址先决因素是靠近家人。

例句：His predeciding to leave the company before receiving the offer was a mistake. 他在收到offer之前就决定离开公司是一个错误。


近义词：predetermined, prior, antecedent, preliminary


反义词：undecided, impromptu, spontaneous


词性：形容词
