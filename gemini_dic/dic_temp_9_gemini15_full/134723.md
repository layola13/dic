nonmaturity: /ˌnɒnməˈtʃʊərɪti/ | n. |非到期；未到期；非成熟期 | The state or quality of not having reached maturity; the absence of maturity.  It often refers to financial instruments that do not have a fixed maturity date. | Example: The nonmaturity of the investment made it difficult to predict the return.  投资的非到期性使得难以预测回报。

The nonmaturity of the bond means it can be redeemed at any time. 这债券的非到期性意味着它可以随时赎回。| 近义词: immaturity,  lack of maturity | 反义词: maturity, due date
