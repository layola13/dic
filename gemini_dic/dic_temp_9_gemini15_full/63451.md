entrenchment:/ɪnˈtʃɛntʃmənt/| n. |巩固；确立； entrenched position |The strengthening or fortifying of a position or attitude.  The act of digging trenches for defensive purposes; a defensive position formed by digging trenches. |例句：The government's entrenchment of its policies made any reform seem impossible. 政府对其政策的巩固使得任何改革都显得不可能。

例句：The soldiers dug in, creating a strong entrenchment against the enemy. 士兵们挖掘工事，构筑了强大的防御工事以对抗敌人。


近义词：fortification, consolidation, strengthening |反义词：weakening, undermining, erosion |词性：名词
