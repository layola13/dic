redlegs:/ˈrɛdˌlɛɡz/| n. |红腿（多种含义）|Redlegs can refer to several things, most commonly a type of soldier or a species of bird.  As a soldier, it historically referred to British soldiers stationed in tropical climates whose legs turned red from sun exposure and/or  skin conditions.  As a bird, it commonly refers to various species with red legs, often in the rail family.  The term can also be used informally and regionally to describe people with red legs, possibly due to sun exposure or a medical condition.|例句：The redlegs marched through the jungle. 红腿士兵穿过丛林。 The redlegs are a common sight in the wetlands. 红腿鸟在湿地很常见。|近义词： (depending on context) soldier, bird,  person with red legs|反义词： (no direct antonym, depends on context)|名词
