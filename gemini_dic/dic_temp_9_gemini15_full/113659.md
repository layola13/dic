machinima:/məˈʃiːnɪmə/| n. |机器影像，机器动画|Machinema is a filmmaking process that uses real-time 3D computer graphics engines, such as those found in video games, to create movies.  It often involves manipulating game characters and environments to tell stories or create artistic expressions. |Example:  The machinima film used characters from the game "Grand Theft Auto" to create a humorous parody.  机器影像电影使用了“侠盗猎车手”游戏中的角色来创作一部幽默的讽刺作品。|近义词：computer animation, CGI film, video game movie |反义词：live-action film, traditional animation |名词
