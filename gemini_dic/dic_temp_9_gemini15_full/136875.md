nonsmoking:/ˈnɒnˈsmoʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 不准吸烟的；无烟的 |  Relating to or designating a place or area where smoking is prohibited.  | Example: This is a nonsmoking area; please extinguish your cigarette. 这是一个禁烟区；请熄灭你的香烟。  |近义词：smoke-free, smokeless | 反义词：smoking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The restaurant is nonsmoking. = 这家餐馆是不准吸烟的。
This section of the train is nonsmoking. = 火车的这个区域是不准吸烟的。

一般过去时:
The cafe was nonsmoking, so I didn't light my cigarette. = 咖啡馆是不准吸烟的，所以我没有点燃我的香烟。
The policy was changed to make the entire building nonsmoking. = 政策改变了，使得整栋建筑物都不准吸烟。

现在完成时:
The airport has become entirely nonsmoking. = 机场已经完全禁烟了。
The city has implemented a nonsmoking policy in all public spaces. = 该城市已在所有公共场所实施了禁烟政策。

过去完成时:
The law had already been changed to a nonsmoking environment before I moved there. = 我搬到那里之前，法律就已经改为无烟环境了。
They had decided to make the office nonsmoking long before I joined the company. = 我加入公司很久之前，他们就已经决定将办公室设为禁烟区。

将来时:
The new stadium will be entirely nonsmoking. = 新体育场将完全禁烟。
Our company will implement a nonsmoking policy next year. = 我们公司明年将实施禁烟政策。

虚拟语气:
If the restaurant were nonsmoking, I would have gone. = 如果这家餐厅是不准吸烟的，我就去了。
I wish the entire building was nonsmoking. = 我希望整栋建筑物都不准吸烟。

被动语态:
The area was designated as nonsmoking last year. = 该区域去年被指定为禁烟区。
The building is being gradually converted to a nonsmoking environment. = 这栋建筑物正在逐步改造成无烟环境。

疑问句:

Are all public places nonsmoking in this country? = 在这个国家，所有公共场所都是禁烟的吗？

否定句:
The bar wasn't nonsmoking. = 酒吧不是禁烟的。
That area is not designated as nonsmoking. = 那个区域未被指定为禁烟区。
