excitatory: /ɪkˈsaɪtəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 兴奋性的；兴奋的；刺激性的 |  Describing a substance, signal, or process that increases the likelihood of a neuron firing an action potential, or that increases the activity of a target cell or organ.  It refers to something that excites or stimulates a response, often in a biological or neurological context. |


例句：

Glutamate is an excitatory neurotransmitter.  | 谷氨酸是一种兴奋性神经递质。

The excitatory effects of caffeine can keep you awake. | 咖啡因的兴奋作用会让你保持清醒。


近义词：stimulatory, activating, arousing, stimulating

反义词：inhibitory, suppressive, calming, depressant

词性：形容词
