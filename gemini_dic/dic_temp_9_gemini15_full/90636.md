humanitarian: /ˌhjuːmænɪˈteəriən/ | adj., n. |人道主义的；人道主义者 |

英文解析: Relating to or characteristic of humanitarianism; having concern for or seeking to promote human welfare.  A person actively engaged in humanitarian work.


例句:

adj. The humanitarian crisis in the region requires immediate international attention.  该地区的人道主义危机需要国际社会立即关注。

n.  She dedicated her life to being a humanitarian, working tirelessly to alleviate suffering. 她致力于成为人道主义者，不知疲倦地致力于减轻痛苦。


近义词: altruistic, philanthropic, compassionate, benevolent


反义词: cruel, inhumane, selfish, heartless


词性: adjective, noun
