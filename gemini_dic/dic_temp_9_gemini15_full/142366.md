It refers to the condition where the visual axes of the eyes are parallel and aligned, allowing for clear, single vision.  | Example:  The patient demonstrated good orthophoria during the eye exam.  这位病人眼科检查中表现出良好的正视。 | Near synonyms: binocular vision, single vision | Antonyms: heterophoria, strabismus |  词性: 名词
