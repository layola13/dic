diesinking:/ˈdaɪˌsɪŋkɪŋ/| n. | 凹模制造；模具冲压；模具制作 | The process of creating a die (a tool used for shaping or cutting materials) by removing material from a block of metal or other material.  This is typically done using a machine that cuts away the unwanted material, leaving behind the desired shape. | 例句：The diesinking process requires precision and expertise.  凹模制造需要精度和专业知识。

例句：The company specializes in diesinking for automotive parts. 这家公司专门从事汽车零件的模具冲压。


近义词：die making, die cutting, mold making


反义词：die casting (adds material instead of removing it)
