grizzles:/ˈɡrɪzəl/| v.|抱怨，低语，不满地嘟囔|To complain or grumble in a low voice, typically expressing discontent or annoyance.|例句：The children grizzled about having to go to bed early. 孩子们抱怨必须早点睡觉。|例句：He grizzled under his breath about the unfairness of it all. 他低声抱怨这一切的不公平。|近义词：grumble, mutter, whine, complain|反义词：praise, compliment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grizzles about the food. = 他抱怨食物。
They always grizzle when they don't get their way. = 他们总是得不到想要的东西时就抱怨。

一般过去时:
She grizzled about the long wait. = 她抱怨漫长的等待。
The baby grizzled all night. = 婴儿整夜都在哭闹。

现在完成时:
He has grizzled about this problem for weeks. = 他已经抱怨这个问题几周了。
They have grizzled incessantly since the announcement. = 自从宣布以来，他们一直在不停地抱怨。

过去完成时:
She had grizzled for hours before finally falling asleep. = 在最终入睡之前，她已经抱怨了好几个小时。
They had grizzled to their parents about the unfair punishment. = 他们已经向父母抱怨了不公平的惩罚。

将来时:
He will grizzle if he doesn't get a new toy. = 如果他不得到一个新玩具，他就会抱怨。
They will grizzle throughout the entire journey. = 他们在整个旅程中都会抱怨。

虚拟语气:
If he grizzled less, people would listen more. = 如果他少抱怨一些，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't grizzle so much. = 我希望他不那么抱怨。

被动语态:  (Grizzle is rarely used in the passive voice)
His complaints were often grizzled about by his colleagues. = 他的抱怨经常被他的同事们嘟囔。  (This is an awkward construction)
The unfairness of the situation was grizzled at by many. = 许多人对情况的不公平感到不满。（较为勉强）


疑问句:
Does he grizzle often? = 他经常抱怨吗？
Why is she grizzling? = 她为什么在抱怨？

否定句:
He didn't grizzle at all. = 他一点也不抱怨。
They didn't grizzle about the extra work. = 他们没有抱怨额外的工作。


PHRASE->
grizzle about something = 抱怨某事
grizzle and groan = 抱怨和呻吟
grizzle to oneself = 自言自语地抱怨
grizzle with discontent = 不满地抱怨
to grizzle one's complaints = 抱怨
