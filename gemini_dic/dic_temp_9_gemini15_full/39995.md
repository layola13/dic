consultant:/kənˈsʌltənt/ | n. | 顾问，咨询者 | A person who provides expert advice professionally.  | The consultant recommended a new marketing strategy.  这位顾问建议了一项新的营销策略。 | advisor, adviser, expert |  | noun

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She consults with clients regularly. = 她定期与客户会面咨询。
He consults on a wide range of business issues. = 他就各种商业问题提供咨询。

一般过去时:
They consulted a doctor about their illness. = 他们就他们的病情咨询了一位医生。
The company consulted an expert on environmental regulations. = 公司咨询了一位环境法规专家。

现在完成时:
We have consulted with several experts on this project. = 我们在这个项目上已经咨询了几位专家。
She has consulted extensively on matters of international law. = 她广泛地就国际法事务进行了咨询。

过去完成时:
They had consulted the manual before attempting the repair. = 他们尝试修理之前已经查阅了手册。
The team had consulted with stakeholders before making the decision. = 该团队在做出决定之前已经与利益相关者进行了协商。

将来时:
We will consult with the board before making any changes. = 我们将在做出任何更改之前与董事会协商。
He will consult an architect about designing his new house. = 他将咨询一位建筑师关于设计他的新房子的事宜。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would consult a lawyer. = 如果我是你，我会咨询律师。
I wish I had consulted an expert earlier. = 我真希望我早点咨询专家。

被动语态:
The company was consulted on the matter. = 公司就此事被咨询了。
The plan was consulted by the team before implementation. = 该计划在实施前由团队进行了审核。

疑问句:
Have you consulted a financial advisor? = 你咨询过理财顾问吗？
Who did they consult about the project? = 他们咨询了谁关于这个项目？

否定句:
He did not consult his colleagues before making the decision. = 他在做决定之前没有咨询他的同事。
The company didn't consult external experts. = 公司没有咨询外部专家。
