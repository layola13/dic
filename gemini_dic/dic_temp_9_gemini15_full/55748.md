divergence:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒəns/| n.|差异；分歧；偏离；发散|The difference between two or more things; the act of separating or moving away from a central point or line; the process by which different species develop from a common ancestor.|例句：There is a significant divergence of opinion on this issue.  在这个问题上存在很大的意见分歧。|例句：The divergence of the two rivers is clearly visible on the map.  两条河流的分流在地图上清晰可见。|近义词：difference, discrepancy, deviation, variance|反义词：convergence, agreement, conformity
