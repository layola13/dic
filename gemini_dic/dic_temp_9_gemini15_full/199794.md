/sɪs/| n. |轻微的嘶嘶声|A soft hissing sound, often made by a snake or escaping air.|The snake made a soft hiss. |近义词：hiss, whisper|反义词：boom, bang

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The radiator hisses softly. = 暖气片轻轻地嘶嘶作响。
The escaping gas hisses. = 泄漏的气体发出嘶嘶声。

一般过去时:
The snake hissed at me. = 蛇朝我嘶嘶地叫。
The tire hissed as it deflated. = 轮胎放气时发出嘶嘶声。

现在完成时:
The kettle has hissed, the water is boiling. = 水壶已经发出嘶嘶声了，水开了。
The engine has been hissing all morning. = 引擎整个上午都在嘶嘶作响。

过去完成时:
The snake had hissed before I even saw it. = 我还没看到蛇之前，它就已经嘶嘶地叫了。
The old machine had hissed and sputtered before it finally stopped. = 旧机器发出嘶嘶声和噼啪声后才最终停止。

将来时:
The escaping steam will hiss loudly. = 泄漏的蒸汽会发出很大的嘶嘶声。
The pressure cooker will hiss when the pressure is released. = 压力锅在压力释放时会发出嘶嘶声。

虚拟语气:
If the tire hissed, we would have to change it. = 如果轮胎发出嘶嘶声，我们就得换胎。
I wish the escaping air wouldn't hiss so loudly. = 我希望泄漏的空气不要发出这么大的嘶嘶声。

被动语态:
A hiss was heard from the engine. = 从引擎里传来了嘶嘶声。
The problem was solved by fixing the hissing valve. = 通过修理嘶嘶作响的阀门解决了问题。

疑问句:
Did you hear that hiss? = 你听到那个嘶嘶声了吗？
What caused that hissing sound? = 那个嘶嘶声是什么引起的？

否定句:
The snake didn't hiss. = 蛇没有嘶嘶作响。
The machine didn't hiss at all during the test. = 机器在测试期间根本没有发出嘶嘶声。
