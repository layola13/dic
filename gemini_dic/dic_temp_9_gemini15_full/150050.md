palpitates:/ˈpæl.pɪ.teɪts/| v.| 心跳加速；悸动 | To beat rapidly or irregularly, especially because of fear, excitement, or illness.  |例句：My heart palpitated with fear as I waited for the results.  我的心脏因等待结果而恐惧地怦怦直跳。|例句：She could feel her heart palpitating in her chest. 她能感觉到自己的心脏在胸腔里怦怦直跳。|近义词：throb, flutter, pound|反义词：be still, be calm
