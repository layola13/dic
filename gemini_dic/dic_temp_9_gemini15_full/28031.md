USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It describes how an action is performed.  Example sentences will illustrate its use in various contexts.

Example 1: The sensor measures the distance capacitively. = 传感器以电容方式测量距离。
Example 2: The circuit is designed to operate capacitively. = 该电路设计为以电容方式工作。

There are no common idiomatic phrases using "capacitively".  The word is primarily used in technical contexts.
