film-maker:/ˈfɪlmˌmeɪkər/| n. |电影制作人|A film-maker is a person who makes films, including writing, directing, producing, and editing.  They are responsible for the overall creative vision of a film.|The film-maker, known for her innovative techniques, received critical acclaim for her latest work.|director, producer, cinematographer| (no direct antonym)|名词
