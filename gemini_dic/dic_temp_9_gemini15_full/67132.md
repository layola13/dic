exportations:/ˌɛkspɔːrˈteɪʃənz/| n. | 出口，输出 | The act of exporting goods or services from one country to another.  It refers to the total volume of goods or commodities shipped out of a country. |例句：The country's exportations of coffee have increased significantly this year.  今年该国的咖啡出口量显著增加。|例句：High tariffs are impacting the exportations from our company. 高关税正在影响我们公司的出口。|近义词：exports, shipments, outbound goods |反义词：importations, imports
