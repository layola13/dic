cirrus:/ˈsɪrəs/| n.|卷云|Thin, wispy clouds found at high altitudes, composed of ice crystals.  They are often white and feathery in appearance. |Example:  Cirrus clouds often indicate fair weather, but can also precede a storm.|近义词：feather clouds, wispy clouds|反义词：cumulonimbus, nimbostratus
