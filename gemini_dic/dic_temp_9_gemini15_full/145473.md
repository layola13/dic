overclean:/ˌoʊvərˈkliːn/ | adj. |过度清洁的；过于干净的 | Excessively clean; cleaner than necessary or desirable.  It often implies that the cleaning has gone too far, resulting in something sterile or lacking in character. | 例句：The house was overclean, almost sterile.  房子过于干净，几乎不像是人住的。  The overclean environment felt unnatural and unwelcoming. 过度清洁的环境感觉不自然且不友好。 | 近义词：excessively clean, hyper-clean, spotless (in a negative context) | 反义词：dirty, unclean, messy
