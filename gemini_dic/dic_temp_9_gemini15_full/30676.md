cerotype:/ˈsɪrətaɪp/| n. |蜡型；原型；范本|A cerotype is a model or prototype made of wax or a similar substance, often used in the creation of other objects, such as sculptures or jewelry.  It can also refer to a wax recording of sound.|The artist created a cerotype of the sculpture before casting it in bronze.  艺术家在青铜铸造之前制作了雕塑的蜡型。 The museum displayed a rare cerotype of an ancient coin. 博物馆展示了一个古代硬币的珍稀蜡型。|近义词：model, prototype, mold|反义词：final product, finished article
