casseroles:/ˈkæsəroʊlz/| n. |砂锅菜；砂锅；焙盘|A casserole is a dish baked in a casserole dish, typically containing meat, vegetables, and a sauce.  It is often cooked in a single dish and served in the same dish.|Example:  She made a delicious chicken casserole for dinner. 她做了美味的鸡肉砂锅菜当晚餐。

The casserole was bubbling merrily in the oven. 砂锅菜在烤箱里咕嘟咕嘟地冒着泡。|近义词：stew, gratin|反义词：salad (in terms of cooking method and presentation)

词源:
The word "casserole" comes from the French word "casserole," which refers to a type of earthenware pot.  The English word then adopted the meaning of both the pot and the dish cooked within it.
