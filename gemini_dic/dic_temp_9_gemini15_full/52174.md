diapophysis:/ˌdaɪəˈpɒfɪsɪs/| n. |关节突，横突|The diapophysis is a bony projection from a vertebra, specifically referring to the transverse process in some vertebrates. It serves as an attachment point for muscles and ligaments, contributing to the structural integrity and movement of the spine. |Example: The diapophysis of the lumbar vertebrae are particularly robust to support the weight of the upper body.  脊椎骨的关节突，特别是腰椎的关节突，非常粗壮以支撑上半身的重量。|近义词：transverse process, articular process|反义词：无
