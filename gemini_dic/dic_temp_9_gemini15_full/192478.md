scrumming:/ˈskrʌmɪŋ/ | v. |  橄榄球争球；(项目管理中)进行Scrum |  The act of participating in a scrum, either in rugby or in the context of agile project management. In rugby, it refers to the formation and action of a scrum. In project management, it refers to the iterative process of working within a Scrum framework.  | 例句：The team was scrumming daily to review progress and address impediments.  团队每天都在进行Scrum会议以审查进度并解决障碍。


The rugby team scrummed effectively to win the ball.  橄榄球队有效地进行了争球以赢得球权。 | 近义词：scrambling, hustling (in rugby context); iterating, collaborating (in project management context) | 反义词：idling, delaying (in project management context)
