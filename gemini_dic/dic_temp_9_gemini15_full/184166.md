resketched:/ˌriːˈskɛtʃt/| v. |重新勾画，重新描绘|To sketch something again, often with changes or improvements.  It implies a prior sketch existed and is being revised.|例句：The architect resketched the building plans to incorporate the client's feedback. 建筑师重新勾画了建筑图纸，以纳入客户的反馈。

例句：She resketched the landscape, adding more detail to the mountains. 她重新描绘了风景，在山区添加了更多细节。


近义词：redesign, redraw, revise, revamp|反义词：finalize, complete


词性:动词
