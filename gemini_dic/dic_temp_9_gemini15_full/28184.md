cappuccino:/ˌkæpəˈtʃiːnoʊ/| n. |卡布奇诺咖啡|A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that is made with steamed milk foam. It is typically served in a tall, narrow cup and is often topped with a dusting of cocoa powder or cinnamon.  Cappuccinos are known for their rich, creamy texture and balanced flavor profile, combining the strength of espresso with the smoothness of steamed milk and the airy lightness of foamed milk.|例句：I ordered a cappuccino and a croissant for breakfast. 我早餐点了一杯卡布奇诺咖啡和一个牛角面包。


例句：She prefers a cappuccino to a latte. 她更喜欢卡布奇诺而不是拿铁咖啡。

近义词：latte, macchiato|反义词：无|词性：名词
