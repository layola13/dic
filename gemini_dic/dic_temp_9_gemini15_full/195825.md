sendoff: /ˈsɛndɒf/ | n. |欢送会；告别；辞行 | A sendoff is a party or other event held to say goodbye to someone who is leaving.  It can also refer to the act of saying goodbye or the departure itself. |例句：The company threw a big sendoff party for its retiring CEO.  这家公司为即将退休的首席执行官举办了一场盛大的欢送会。

They gave him a warm sendoff at the airport. 他们在机场为他送行。|近义词：farewell, departure, leaving, goodbye |反义词：arrival, welcome

词源:
The word "sendoff" is a compound word formed from the verb "send" and the noun "off."  It originally referred to the act of sending someone away, but over time it evolved to encompass the event or ceremony associated with a departure.
