preoceanic:/ˌpriːoʊˈsiːænɪk/| adj.|前大洋的，大洋之前的| Relating to the time or conditions before the existence of an ocean basin; existing before the formation of an ocean.  The term often refers to geological periods or formations. | Example: Preoceanic sediments were discovered beneath the current seabed. 前大洋沉积物在当前的海底之下被发现。|近义词：antecedent, prior, preceding |反义词：postoceanic
