comforters:/ˈkʌmfərtərz/ | n. |被子；安慰者 | A comforter is a thick, soft bed covering used for warmth and comfort.  It can also refer to a person who provides comfort or consolation. | Example:  The fluffy comforter kept her warm all night.  蓬松的被子让她整夜都暖和。  She was a comforter to her grieving friend. 她是她悲伤朋友的安慰者。 |近义词： quilt, blanket, comforter (person: consoler, solace) |反义词： (person: tormentor, harasser)
