gourdlike:/ˈɡɔːrdlaɪk/| adj.|像葫芦的；葫芦状的|Resembling a gourd in shape or appearance.  The word combines "gourd" (a type of fruit with a hard rind) and the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The vase had a gourdlike shape, round and bulbous at the bottom, tapering towards the neck.  那个花瓶形状像葫芦，底部圆鼓，向上逐渐变细。

The pumpkin is a gourdlike vegetable.  南瓜是一种葫芦状的蔬菜。


近义词：cucurbit-shaped, calabash-shaped, bottle-shaped|反义词：none|词性：adjective
