fountaining:/ˈfaʊntənɪŋ/| v.|喷涌，涌出|The action of a liquid, especially water, gushing or spurting out in a jet or jets, like a fountain.  It can also refer to a sudden and abundant outpouring of something.|例句：The geyser fountained hot water high into the air.  间歇泉将热水喷射到高空中。

The oil well fountained uncontrollably. 油井不受控制地喷涌而出。|近义词：gushing, spouting, erupting|反义词：dribbling, trickling
