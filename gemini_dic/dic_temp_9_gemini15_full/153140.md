pelargonium:/ˌpɛləˈɡoʊniəm/| n. |天竺葵|A pelargonium is a flowering plant in the genus Pelargonium, known for its diverse and often brightly colored flowers. They are popular garden and pot plants, prized for their ease of cultivation and attractive blooms.
