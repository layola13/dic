overprograms:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræmz/ | v. |过度编程，程序编写过多 | To write more code than is necessary or efficient to achieve a particular task.  This can lead to increased complexity, reduced performance, and increased difficulty in maintaining and debugging the software. | Example: The developers overprogrammed the system, resulting in slow response times and frequent crashes.  开发人员过度编程，导致响应速度慢且频繁崩溃。|近义词：overengineer, overcomplicate |反义词：simplify, streamline |动词
