frolicful:/ˈfrɒlɪkfʊl/| adj. |嬉戏的；欢乐的；快活的 |Full of fun and lightheartedness; playful.  The word evokes a sense of carefree merriment and spirited activity. |例句：The children were frolicful in the park, running and laughing. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏玩耍，跑来跑去，欢声笑语。

例句：The frolicful puppies tumbled over each other in a pile of joyful chaos.  嬉戏的小狗们快乐地堆成一堆，互相翻滚打闹。


近义词：playful, merry, joyous, cheerful, sportive, exuberant

反义词：serious, somber, gloomy, glum, morose, sullen


词源:  The word "frolicful" is derived from the Old English word "frolic," which meant "to leap or jump for joy." This Old English word has Germanic roots.  The suffix "-ful" is a common English suffix meaning "full of". Therefore, frolicful literally means "full of frolic."
