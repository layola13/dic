rescissory:/rɪˈsɪsəri/| adj.|解除合同的；撤销的；可撤销的|Relating to the rescission or annulment of a contract or agreement.  It describes something that has the power to rescind or cancel a contract.|The rescissory clause in the contract allowed either party to terminate the agreement under certain conditions.|近义词：revocatory, annulling, voiding|反义词：affirmative, confirming, validating
