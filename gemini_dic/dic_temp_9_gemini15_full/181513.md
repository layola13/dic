rekeyboarded:/ˌriːˈkiːbɔːrdɪd/ | v. |重新键入，重新录入 | To type or enter data again, often to correct errors or update information.  | Example: The document had to be rekeyboarded after the formatting errors were discovered.  文件在发现格式错误后不得不重新键入。 | 近义词：retyped, re-entered | 反义词：None specifically.  The opposite would be to leave the data as it is.
