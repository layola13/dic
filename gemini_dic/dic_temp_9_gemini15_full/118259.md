merchandized:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzd/| vt.|商品化；推销；销售|To treat (something) as a commodity to be bought and sold; to promote and sell (a product or service).|例句：The company merchandized its new product effectively through targeted advertising. 公司通过有针对性的广告有效地推销了其新产品。

例句：They merchandized the band's image to appeal to a wider audience. 他们将乐队的形象商品化以吸引更广泛的受众。


近义词：marketed, promoted, sold, commercialized|反义词：de-commercialized, ignored


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company merchandizes its products globally. = 公司在全球范围内销售其产品。
They merchandize their services through online platforms. = 他们通过在线平台销售他们的服务。


一般过去时:
The store merchandized the holiday items aggressively. = 商店积极推销节日商品。
They merchandized the new album through concerts and interviews. = 他们通过演唱会和采访来推销新专辑。


现在完成时:
The brand has successfully merchandized its image. = 该品牌已成功地将自身形象商品化。
They have merchandized their products in various countries. = 他们已在多个国家销售其产品。


过去完成时:
The company had already merchandized the product before the launch event. = 公司在发布会之前就已经将产品商品化了。
They had merchandized the book through social media before the release. = 在发行之前，他们已经通过社交媒体推销了这本书。


将来时:
The company will merchandize its new line of clothing next year. = 公司将在明年推销其新的服装系列。
They will merchandize the film aggressively to increase box office revenue. = 他们将积极推销这部电影以增加票房收入。


虚拟语气:
If they had merchandized the product differently, they might have sold more. = 如果他们以不同的方式推销产品，他们可能会卖得更多。
I wish they had merchandized the event more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地宣传这个活动。


被动语态:
The products were merchandized effectively. = 产品得到了有效的推销。
The concert was merchandized through various media channels. = 音乐会通过各种媒体渠道进行宣传。


疑问句:
How will they merchandize the new game? = 他们将如何推销这款新游戏？
Was the product adequately merchandized? = 产品是否得到了充分的推销？


否定句:
The company did not merchandize the product effectively. = 公司没有有效地推销该产品。
They didn't merchandize the event properly. = 他们没有妥善地宣传该活动。
