decreases:/dɪˈkriːsɪz/| v.|减少；降低|To become less; to diminish in size, amount, intensity, or degree.|例句：The number of students in the class decreases every year. 每年班上的学生人数都在减少。|例句：The company's profits decreased significantly last quarter. 上季度公司利润大幅下降。|近义词：diminishes, reduces, lessens|反义词：increases, grows, expands|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature decreases as the day goes on. = 白天随着时间的推移，气温下降。
Demand for the product decreases during the off-season. = 淡季对产品的需求减少。

一般过去时:
The population of the town decreased dramatically after the factory closed. = 工厂关闭后，小镇的人口急剧下降。
His weight decreased after he started exercising. = 他开始锻炼后体重减轻了。

现在完成时:
The number of accidents has decreased since the new safety regulations were introduced. = 自从实施新的安全条例以来，事故数量有所减少。
The company's market share has decreased in recent years. = 近年来，公司的市场份额有所下降。

过去完成时:
The river had decreased significantly by the time we reached it. = 等我们到达时，河水已经明显下降了。
The amount of rainfall had decreased considerably before the drought. = 干旱之前，降雨量已经大大减少了。

将来时:
The demand for oil is expected to decrease in the coming years. = 预计未来几年对石油的需求将会下降。
The price of the product will decrease if production increases. = 如果产量增加，产品的价格将会下降。

虚拟语气:
If the government decreased taxes, the economy would improve. = 如果政府降低税收，经济就会好转。
I wish the noise level would decrease. = 我希望噪音能小一些。

被动语态:
The volume of the music was decreased by the DJ. = 音乐的音量被DJ调低了。
The budget was decreased by 10%. = 预算减少了10%。

疑问句:
Does the amount of sunlight decrease in winter? = 冬天阳光照射量会减少吗？
Does your anxiety decrease when you exercise? = 你运动时焦虑会减少吗？

否定句:
The level of pollution hasn't decreased enough. = 污染程度还没有下降足够多。
The number of participants didn't decrease, it actually increased. = 参加人数并没有减少，反而增加了。


PHRASE->
decrease in = 减少
decrease by = 减少了...
a decrease of = 减少了...
rapid decrease = 急剧下降
gradual decrease = 逐渐减少
