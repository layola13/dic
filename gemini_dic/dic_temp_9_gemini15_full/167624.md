prezzie:/ˈprezi/| n. |礼物，小礼物|A present, usually a small one.  It's a shortened, informal version of "present".|例句：He gave me a little prezzie for my birthday. 他给了我一件小礼物作为我的生日礼物。

例句：I got a lovely prezzie from my grandma. 我从奶奶那里得到了一份可爱的礼物。

近义词：gift, present, souvenir

反义词：None directly, but "nothing" could be considered an antonym in the context of receiving something.

词性:名词
