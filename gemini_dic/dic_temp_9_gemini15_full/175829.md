raspier:/ˈræspiər/| adj.|沙哑的，粗糙的|Having a rough, grating quality, like the sound of a rasp.  It can describe a voice or a texture.|His voice was raspier after shouting all day. 他喊了一整天后，声音变得沙哑了。 The sandpaper felt raspier than expected. 砂纸比预期的要粗糙。|近义词：rough, grating, hoarse, scratchy|反义词：smooth, soft, clear
