codium:/ˈkoʊdiəm/| n. |海萝属|A genus of green algae, often found in shallow, rocky coastal areas.  Codium species are characterized by their soft, spongy texture and branching or tubular thalli. They are sometimes used in food, and are being studied for their potential medicinal properties. |Example:  The diver collected a sample of *Codium fragile* for analysis.  潜水员收集了*脆海萝*样本进行分析。|近义词:seaweed, algae|反义词:N/A
