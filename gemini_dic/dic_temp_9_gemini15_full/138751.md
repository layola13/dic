numeral:/ˈnuːmərəl/ | n. |数字，数词 | A numeral is a symbol or group of symbols that represents a number.  It can be a word, a digit, or a combination of both.  Numerals are used to express quantities and values. | Example:  The Roman numeral for 10 is X.  The Arabic numeral for ten is 10. |近义词：digit, figure |反义词：letter, word
