ferricyanate:/ˌfɛriˈsaɪəneɪt/| n. |铁氰化物|A salt of ferricyanic acid, containing the hexacyanoferrate(III) anion [Fe(CN)₆]³⁻.  Ferricyanates are typically reddish-brown in color and are used in various applications, including blueprint making and photography.|Example: Potassium ferricyanate is a common ferricyanate used in photography as a chemical oxidant.|近义词: hexacyanoferrate(III) |反义词: ferrocyanate
