词源:
From Greek *aisthetikos, from aisthesis "feeling, sensation," from aisthanesthai "to perceive."  The word originally referred to the branch of philosophy dealing with the nature of beauty and art.  Over time, it came to also describe things possessing qualities of beauty or good taste.
