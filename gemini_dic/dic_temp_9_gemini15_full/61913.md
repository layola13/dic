The term often refers to substances produced by destructive distillation, such as creosote from wood. |例句：The empyreumatic smell of burning wood filled the air.  焦木的烧焦味充满了空气。The empyreumatic flavor of the coffee was off-putting. 咖啡的焦苦味令人反感。|近义词：burnt, smoky, charred, acrid|反义词：fresh, sweet, fragrant
