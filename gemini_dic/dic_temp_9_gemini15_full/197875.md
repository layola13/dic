shipped:/ʃɪpt/| v. |运送；装运；发送；|To transport goods by ship, train, airplane, or truck; to send something.|The goods were shipped yesterday. 货物昨天已运送。|The package has been shipped to your address.包裹已寄到你的地址。|transported, sent, conveyed|received, unshipped|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They ship goods worldwide. = 他们向全球运送货物。
We ship orders daily. = 我们每天都发货。


一般过去时:
He shipped the package last week. = 他上周寄出了包裹。
The company shipped all the products on time. = 公司按时发货了所有产品。


现在完成时:
They have shipped all the orders. = 他们已经寄出了所有订单。
We have shipped the goods to the client. = 我们已经将货物运送给客户。


过去完成时:
They had shipped the goods before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已将货物运送完毕。
The company had shipped the products before the strike. = 公司在罢工前已将产品发货。


将来时:
We will ship your order tomorrow. = 我们明天将寄出你的订单。
The company will ship the goods next week. = 公司下周将运送货物。


虚拟语气:
If they shipped the goods earlier, we would have received them by now. = 如果他们早点发货，我们现在就收到了。
I wish they had shipped my order sooner. = 我希望他们早点寄出我的订单。


被动语态:
The goods were shipped quickly. = 货物很快就被运送了。
The order is being shipped as we speak. = 订单正在发货中。


疑问句:
Has the package been shipped yet? = 包裹寄出去了吗？
When will the goods be shipped? = 货物什么时候发货？


否定句:
They didn't ship the order on time. = 他们没有按时发货。
The goods haven't been shipped yet. = 货物还没发货。


PHRASE->
ship out = 发货
ship to = 运送到
ship from = 从...发货
ship via = 通过...运输
ready to ship = 准备发货
