ego-trippers:/ˈiːɡoʊˌtrɪpərz/| n.|自大狂，自吹自擂的人|People who are excessively boastful and self-centered, often seeking attention and admiration for their perceived accomplishments or qualities. They are characterized by an inflated sense of self-importance and a need for constant validation.|例句：He's such an ego-tripper; he never stops talking about himself. 他真是个自大狂，他总是喋喋不休地谈论自己。  He surrounded himself with yes-men, fueling his ego-tripping behavior. 他周围都是拍马屁的人，助长了他的自吹自擂的行为。|近义词：showoffs, narcissists, braggarts, blowhards|反义词：humble, modest, self-effacing|名词
