reconfirmation's:/ˌriːkɒnfɜːˈmeɪʃənz/| n.|复数名词，再确认；再次确认的行为|The act of confirming something again.  It implies a previous confirmation has been made, and this is a second verification or assurance.|例句：The reconfirmations of the flight bookings were sent to all passengers.  所有乘客都收到了航班预订的再次确认信息。


例句：The company requested reconfirmations from all attendees before the event. 公司在活动开始前要求所有与会者再次确认。


近义词：reverification, reaffirmation, double-check


反义词：cancellation, denial


词性：名词
