schoolma'ams:/ˈskuːlˌmæmz/| n. |女教师；女校长|A schoolma'am is a female teacher, often implying a somewhat strict or old-fashioned type.  The term is now largely considered outdated and somewhat stereotypical.|例句：She remembered her stern schoolma'am from primary school. 她还记得小学里那位严厉的女教师。|例句：The children were afraid of the new schoolma'am. 孩子们害怕新来的女老师。|近义词：schoolteacher, teacher, instructress |反义词：student, pupil|名词
