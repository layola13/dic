reattach:/ˌriːəˈtætʃ/| vt.|重新连接，重新附着|To attach something again; to fix something back in place.|例句：The surgeon had to reattach his severed finger.  外科医生不得不为他重新接上被割断的手指。

例句：We need to reattach the loose button on his shirt.我们需要把衬衫上松动的纽扣重新缝上。


近义词：refasten, rebind, reconnect|反义词：detach, disconnect, remove|动词
