piste:/piːst/|/pɪst/| n. |雪道，滑道|A prepared track of compacted snow or ice for skiing or snowboarding.  |例句：The piste was icy and difficult to ski on. 这条雪道很滑，很难滑雪。|例句：We spent the whole day skiing down different pistes. 我们一整天都在不同的雪道上滑雪。|近义词：slope, run, trail|反义词：off-piste (unprepared snow)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The piste is icy today. = 雪道今天很滑。
This piste offers stunning views. = 这条雪道风景优美。


一般过去时:
The piste was crowded yesterday. = 昨天雪道很拥挤。
We skied the red piste yesterday afternoon. = 我们昨天下午滑了红雪道。


现在完成时:
They have skied all the pistes in this resort. = 他们已经滑了这个度假村的所有雪道。
I have never seen such a well-groomed piste. = 我从未见过如此平整的雪道。


过去完成时:
They had already skied the difficult piste before lunch. = 他们午餐前就已经滑过了那条难的雪道。
We had never skied such a long piste before. = 我们以前从未滑过这么长的雪道。


将来时:
We will ski the black piste tomorrow. = 我们明天将要滑黑雪道。
They will probably close the piste due to the bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，他们可能会关闭雪道。


虚拟语气:
If the piste were less icy, I would ski it. = 如果雪道不那么滑，我就去滑。
I wish the piste were less crowded. = 我希望雪道不那么拥挤。


被动语态:
The piste is being prepared for the race. = 雪道正在为比赛做准备。
The piste was closed due to an avalanche. = 雪道因雪崩而关闭。


疑问句:
Is the piste open today? = 雪道今天开放吗？
Which piste are you going to ski? = 你要去滑哪条雪道？


否定句:
The piste is not suitable for beginners. = 这条雪道不适合初学者。
We did not ski the most difficult piste. = 我们没有滑最难的那条雪道。
