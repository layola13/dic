roynish:/ˈrɔɪnɪʃ/ | adj. |  有点古怪的；古怪的；忧郁的 |  Characterized by a somewhat melancholic or whimsical temperament; eccentric.  The word suggests a gentle, pensive, and slightly unconventional nature, not necessarily negative. |例句：He had a roynish air about him, often lost in thought. 他身上有一种古怪的气质，经常陷入沉思。  He possessed a roynish charm that was hard to resist. 他有一种难以抗拒的古怪魅力。 |近义词：whimsical, eccentric, quirky, odd, peculiar |反义词：conventional, ordinary, normal, typical |  adj.
