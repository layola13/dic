liposuction:/ˈlaɪpoʊsʌkʃən/| n. |吸脂术|The surgical removal of fat from the body using suction.  Liposuction is a cosmetic procedure used to reshape specific areas of the body by removing excess fat deposits. |例句：She underwent liposuction to reduce the fat on her thighs. 她做了吸脂手术来减少大腿上的脂肪。
例句：The doctor recommended liposuction as an option to improve her body contour. 医生建议她通过吸脂手术来改善体型。|近义词：lipectomy, fat removal |反义词：fat grafting, fat injection
