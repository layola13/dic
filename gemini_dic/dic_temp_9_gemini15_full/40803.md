convoking:/kənˈvoʊkɪŋ/| v.|召集；召开；集合|The act of calling people together, especially to a formal meeting.  It implies a summoning of people to a specific purpose.|例句：The university is convoking a special meeting to discuss the budget.  大学正在召开特别会议讨论预算。

例句：The headmaster convokes a meeting of the teachers every Monday morning. 校长每周一早上都会召集教师开会。


近义词：summoning, convening, assembling, calling together


反义词：dispersing, dismissing, adjourning


USAGE->

The president convokes a meeting every week. 总统每周都会召开会议。
