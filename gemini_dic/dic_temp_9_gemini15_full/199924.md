sizeably:/ˈsaɪzəbli/ | adv. | 相当大地；相当多地 |  Significantly; to a considerable extent.  The company's profits increased sizeably last quarter.  | 近义词：considerably, substantially, significantly | 反义词：slightly, marginally, insignificantly |

例句：

The house is sizeably larger than the one we viewed last week. = 这房子比我们上周看的那栋大得多。

Their investment portfolio increased sizeably due to the booming stock market. = 由于股市繁荣，他们的投资组合大幅增加。
