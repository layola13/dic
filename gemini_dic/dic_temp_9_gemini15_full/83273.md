gymnosperm:/ˈdʒɪmnəˌspɜːrm/| n. |裸子植物|A gymnosperm is a plant that has seeds that are not enclosed within an ovary; instead, the seeds are usually borne on the surface of scales or leaves, often modified to form cones. Gymnosperms typically have needle-like or scale-like leaves and are often wind-pollinated.|Conifers, such as pines and spruces, are examples of gymnosperms.  The seeds of these trees are found within cones.|近义词：conifer (though not all gymnosperms are conifers)|反义词：angiosperm|
