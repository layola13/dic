repurchased:/ˌriːˈpɜːrtʃəst/| v. | 再购买，买回 | To buy something back that one has previously sold or owned.  | Example: The company repurchased its own shares to boost its stock price. 公司回购自身股票以提高股价。|近义词：buy back, reacquire | 反义词：sell, dispose of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company repurchases its own shares regularly. = 公司定期回购自身股票。
He repurchases the same brand of coffee every week. = 他每周都回购同一品牌的咖啡。

一般过去时:
They repurchased the house after it had been sold. = 房屋出售后，他们又把它买回来了。
She repurchased the painting at auction. = 她又在拍卖会上买回了那幅画。

现在完成时:
The government has repurchased a significant amount of debt. = 政府已经回购了大量的债务。
He has repurchased his childhood home. = 他已经买回了他的童年故居。

过去完成时:
They had repurchased the land before the development began. = 开发项目开始前，他们已经买回了那块土地。
She had repurchased the shares before the price dropped. = 价格下跌前，她已经买回了股票。

将来时:
The company will repurchase more shares next quarter. = 公司下个季度将回购更多股票。
He will repurchase the car if he can afford it. = 如果他买得起，他会再买那辆车。

虚拟语气:
If the company had more cash, it would repurchase more shares. = 如果公司有更多现金，它就会回购更多股票。
I wish I had repurchased those stocks last year. = 我真希望去年我回购了那些股票。

被动语态:
Many shares were repurchased by the company. = 公司回购了许多股票。
The property was repurchased by the original owner. = 该房产被原业主买回。

疑问句:
Did the company repurchase any shares this year? = 公司今年回购了任何股票吗？
Will they repurchase the building? = 他们会回购那栋建筑物吗？

否定句:
The company did not repurchase any shares last month. = 公司上个月没有回购任何股票。
He hasn't repurchased the book yet. = 他还没买回那本书。


PHRASE->
stock repurchase = 股票回购
share repurchase = 股份回购
debt repurchase = 债务回购
repurchase agreement = 回购协议
corporate repurchase = 公司回购
