dependents:/dɪˈpendənts/ | n. | 受抚养者，家属 | People who rely on someone else, especially financially.  They are often family members, such as children or spouses, but can also include other individuals who are financially supported by another person. | Example:  He listed his wife and two children as dependents on his tax return. 他在纳税申报单上将妻子和两个孩子列为受抚养人。


Example: The company provides health insurance for its employees and their dependents. 这家公司为其员工及其受抚养人提供健康保险。


近义词：dependants, family, relatives | 反义词：None (no direct antonym)


词性：Noun
