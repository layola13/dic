oculated:/ˈɑːkjʊleɪtɪd/ | vt. | 接种；给…接种疫苗；嫁接 | To inoculate is to introduce a vaccine or other substance into a body to produce immunity to a disease or to introduce a virus, serum, or antitoxin into the body for treatment or prevention of disease. It can also refer to the grafting of a plant. | 例句：The doctor oculated the child against measles. 医生给孩子接种了麻疹疫苗。

The gardener oculated the rose bush with a new variety. 园丁给玫瑰丛嫁接了一个新品种。| 近义词：inoculate, vaccinate, graft | 反义词：N/A
