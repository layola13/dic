overserving: /ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvɪŋ/ | v. |过度供应；供应过多 | To serve (someone or something) with too much of something, especially food or drink.  |例句：The waiter overserved the customers, leading to wasted food.  服务员给顾客上了太多的菜，导致食物浪费。

例句：The bartender overserved the already intoxicated patron, resulting in a DUI arrest. 酒保给已经喝醉的顾客供应了过多的酒，导致其因酒驾被捕。


近义词：overcatering, oversupplying, providing too much


反义词：underserving, underproviding


词性:动词
