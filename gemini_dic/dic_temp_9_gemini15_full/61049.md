elk:/ɛlk/ | n. |麋鹿|A large deer with large antlers, native to North America, Europe, and Asia.|例句：We saw a majestic elk grazing in the meadow. 我们看到一只雄伟的麋鹿在草地上吃草。|近义词：wapiti, stag (male deer)|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The elk grazes peacefully in the field. = 麋鹿平静地吃着田里的草。
Elks roam the forests of the Pacific Northwest. = 麋鹿在太平洋西北部的森林中漫游。


一般过去时:
The elk ran quickly away from the wolf. = 麋鹿快速地从狼身边跑开了。
We saw a herd of elk yesterday near the river. = 昨天我们在河边看到一群麋鹿。

虚拟语气:
