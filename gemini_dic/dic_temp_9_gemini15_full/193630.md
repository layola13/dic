self-blame:/ˈsɛlf bleɪm/| n.|自责，自我谴责|The act of holding oneself responsible for a mistake or failure.  It often involves feelings of guilt, shame, and regret. |例句：Her self-blame after the accident was intense. 事故后，她的自责非常强烈。

例句：He engaged in excessive self-blame for the project's failure.  他因项目失败而过度自责。


近义词：self-reproach, self-criticism, guilt, remorse|反义词：self-congratulation, self-justification|词性:noun


USAGE->

He feels self-blame for his mistakes. = 他为自己的错误感到自责。
She expresses self-blame openly. = 她公开表达自责。


一般过去时:
