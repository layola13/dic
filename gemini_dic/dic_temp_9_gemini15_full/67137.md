exports:/ˈɛkspɔːrts/| n. |出口商品；输出；出口|Goods or services sold to another country.  The act of selling or shipping goods to another country. |例句：China's exports to the US have increased significantly.  中国的对美出口显著增加。|例句：The company's exports account for a large part of its revenue. 这家公司的出口收入占其总收入的很大一部分。|近义词：shipments, sales, commodities|反义词：imports


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory exports textiles to many countries. = 这家工厂向许多国家出口纺织品。
Our company exports high-quality coffee beans. = 我们公司出口高品质的咖啡豆。

一般过去时:
Last year, we exported a record amount of goods. = 去年，我们出口了创纪录数量的商品。
They exported their products to Europe for many years. = 他们多年来一直向欧洲出口产品。


现在完成时:
We have exported over a million units this year. = 今年我们已经出口了超过一百万件产品。
The company has successfully exported its products to new markets. = 公司已成功将其产品出口到新的市场。


过去完成时:
Before the trade war, they had exported much more. = 在贸易战之前，他们的出口量要多得多。
By the end of the year, we had exported all our planned products. = 到年底，我们已经出口了所有计划中的产品。


将来时:
We will export even more next year. = 明年我们将出口更多。
The company will export its new product line to Asia. = 公司将把其新的产品线出口到亚洲。


虚拟语气:
If we had better infrastructure, we would export more. = 如果我们有更好的基础设施，我们将出口更多。
If they exported more, they might earn higher profits. = 如果他们出口更多，他们可能会获得更高的利润。


被动语态:
Many goods are exported from this port. = 许多货物从这个港口出口。
Our products are being exported worldwide. = 我们的产品正在向全世界出口。


疑问句:
What goods does your company export? = 贵公司出口什么商品？
Where are these products exported to? = 这些产品出口到哪里？


否定句:
We don't export to that country anymore. = 我们不再向那个国家出口商品了。
The company did not export any products last quarter. = 上个季度公司没有出口任何产品。
