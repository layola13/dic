mismeasured:/ˌmɪsˈmɛʒərd/| adj.|测量错误的，误测的|Incorrectly measured.  The result is inaccurate because of an error in measurement.|例句：The room's dimensions were mismeasured, leading to problems with the new furniture.  房间尺寸测量错误，导致新家具出现问题。

例句：The scientist's mismeasured sample skewed the results of the experiment. 科学家测量样本错误，导致实验结果偏差。


近义词：incorrectly measured, wrongly gauged, inaccurately assessed

反义词：accurately measured, precisely measured

词性：形容词
