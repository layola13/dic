innovate:/ˈɪnəveɪt/| vt.  |创新，革新 |To introduce something new; to make changes or improvements to something existing.  |例句：The company is constantly innovating new products. 这家公司不断创新新产品。

例句：They innovated a new method for teaching English. 他们创新了一种新的英语教学方法。


近义词：create, invent, develop, reform, pioneer |反义词：stagnate, maintain the status quo


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company innovates constantly. = 这家公司不断创新。
She innovates in her approach to problem-solving. = 她在她解决问题的方法上不断创新。


一般过去时:
Tesla innovated the electric car market. = 特斯拉革新了电动汽车市场。
The artist innovated with new painting techniques. =这位艺术家用新的绘画技巧进行了创新。


现在完成时:
They have innovated significantly in recent years. = 近年来，他们的创新非常显著。
The company has innovated many successful products. = 这家公司已经创新了许多成功的产品。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of the internet, they had innovated in other communication methods. = 在互联网发明之前，他们已经在其他通信方法上进行了创新。
They had innovated the design before launching the product. = 他们在发布产品之前已经对设计进行了改进。


将来时:
We will innovate more in the future. = 我们将来会进行更多创新。
The scientists predict they will innovate a cure for the disease. = 科学家预测他们将创新出治疗这种疾病的方法。


虚拟语气:
If they innovated more, they would be more competitive. = 如果他们进行更多创新，他们将更有竞争力。
I wish they had innovated sooner. = 我希望他们早点进行创新。


被动语态:
New methods are being innovated in the field of medicine. = 医学领域正在创新新的方法。
A new approach was innovated by the researchers. = 研究人员创新了一种新方法。


疑问句:
How can we innovate more effectively? = 我们如何才能更有效地创新？
What are they innovating in their latest project? = 他们在最新的项目中进行什么创新？


否定句:
The company did not innovate enough. = 这家公司创新不足。
They failed to innovate a solution to the problem. = 他们未能创新出解决这个问题的方案。


PHRASE->
innovate on=在...方面进行创新
innovate with=用...进行创新
innovate in=在...领域进行创新
innovate for=为...进行创新
innovate around=围绕...进行创新
