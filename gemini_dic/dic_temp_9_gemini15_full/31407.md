charges:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒɪz/| n. v.|收费；指控；充电；|The word "charges" can function as both a noun and a verb.  As a noun, it refers to fees, accusations, or the process of  electricity flowing. As a verb, it means to demand payment, to accuse formally, or to fill something with electricity.|例句：The hotel charges $100 per night.  这家酒店每晚收费100美元。

The police charged him with murder. 警方指控他犯有谋杀罪。


近义词：fees, costs, accusations, allegations, indictments|反义词：credits, discharges, acquittals|名词，动词
