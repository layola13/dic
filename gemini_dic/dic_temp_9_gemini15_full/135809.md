It often refers to the termination of an agreement or contract without renewal. | Example: The nonprolongation of the contract led to the company's closure.  合同的不续签导致公司倒闭。 The nonprolongation of the visa meant he had to return home. 签证的不延期意味着他不得不回国。|近义词：termination, expiration, discontinuance |反义词：renewal, extension, continuation | 名词
