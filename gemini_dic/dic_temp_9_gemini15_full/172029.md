purifies:/ˈpjʊrɪfaɪz/ | vt. |净化；使纯净 |To remove impurities or contaminants from something; to make pure.  |例句：The filter purifies the water before it's bottled.  过滤器在水装瓶前对其进行净化。


The treatment purifies the wastewater, making it safe for reuse.  该处理方法净化废水，使其可以安全地重复使用。 |近义词：cleanse, refine, purify |反义词：contaminate, pollute
