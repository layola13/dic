contemner:/kənˈtɛmnər/| n. |轻蔑者，蔑视者 |A contemner is one who shows contempt; one who despises or scorns.  They often hold a superior attitude towards those they contemn.  |例句：He was a contemner of all things frivolous and superficial. 他是一个轻蔑所有轻浮和肤浅事物的人。

例句：The contemner of the law often faces serious consequences. 轻蔑法律的人常常面临严重的后果。


近义词：despiser, scorner, scoffer, contemnor


反义词：admirer, admirer, respecter, venerator


词性：名词
