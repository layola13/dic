patriarchical: /ˌpeɪtriˈɑːrkəl/ | adj. | 父权制的；家长统治的 | Relating to or characteristic of a patriarchal society.  A patriarchal society is one in which men hold primary power and predominate in roles of political leadership, moral authority, social privilege, and control of property.  |  例句：Many societies throughout history have been deeply patriarchal. 许多历史上社会的制度都深受父权制的影响。

例句：The company's patriarchal structure prevented women from advancing to senior positions. 这家公司的父权制结构阻碍了女性晋升到高级职位。


近义词：paternalistic, male-dominated, authoritarian | 反义词：matriarchal, egalitarian
