prepolitical: /ˌpriːpəˈlɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 前政治的；政治之前的；政治形成前的 |  Relating to the time or state before the development of formal political systems or structures; existing or occurring before the establishment of government or politics.  It often implies a simpler, more communal, or less structured society. |


例句：

The anthropologist studied the prepolitical social structures of the tribe.  人类学家研究了该部落前政治的社会结构。

Their prepolitical customs involved communal decision-making. 他们前政治的习俗包含集体决策。


近义词：protopolitical, pre-governmental, pre-state


反义词：political, governmental


词性：形容词
