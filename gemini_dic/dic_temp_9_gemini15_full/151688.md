partitive:/ˈpɑːrtɪtɪv/| adj. |部分的，分成的| Relating to or denoting a word or phrase that refers to a part of a whole.  A partitive construction often involves the use of "of" to indicate the whole from which the part is taken. For example, "a piece of cake" is a partitive construction where "piece" is the partitive.  |例句： "A slice of the cake" is a partitive phrase.  这块蛋糕是一个部分短语。  The partitive article "some" indicates an unspecified portion.  部分冠词“一些”表示未指明的部分。|近义词：partial, fractional|反义词：whole, entire|形容词
