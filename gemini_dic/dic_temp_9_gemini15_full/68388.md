fallowing:/ˈfɒləʊɪŋ/ | adj. |接下来的，随后的 | Following the storm, the sun came out.  暴风雨过后，太阳出来了。|近义词：subsequent, succeeding |反义词：preceding, prior |


英文解析：Following as an adjective means coming after something else in time or order. It indicates a sequence or succession.


例句：

The following day, we went to the beach.  第二天，我们去了海滩。

The following chapter discusses the causes of the war.  接下来的章节讨论了战争的原因。


词源:  Derived from the verb "follow," ultimately from Old English *folgian, meaning "to follow."
