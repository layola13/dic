lush:/lʌʃ/ | adj. | 茂盛的；繁茂的；丰富的；奢华的 |  Describing something abundant in growth, especially vegetation;  also describes something rich and luxurious. | 例句：The lush green fields stretched as far as the eye could see.  翠绿的田野绵延到视线尽头。

The novel is full of lush descriptions of the tropical landscape. 这部小说充满了对热带景观的丰富描写。


近义词： abundant, luxuriant, opulent, lavish | 反义词： barren, sparse, meager, austere


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The garden is lush and vibrant. = 花园茂盛而充满活力。
The rainforest is lush with life. = 雨林生机勃勃。

一般过去时:
The garden was lush before the drought. = 干旱之前，花园非常茂盛。
The countryside was lush and green. = 乡村一片葱茏。

现在完成时:
The plants have become lush since we started watering them regularly. = 自从我们开始定期浇水以来，植物变得茂盛了。
The vegetation has grown lush and green this summer. = 今年夏天植被长得又茂盛又翠绿。

过去完成时:
The jungle had been lush and teeming with life before the logging began. = 在伐木开始之前，丛林茂盛，充满生机。
The fields had been lush and fertile before the flood. = 洪水泛滥之前，田地肥沃茂盛。

将来时:
The garden will be lush and colorful next spring. = 明年春天花园将会茂盛多彩。
The plants will become lush and healthy with proper care. = 植物在适当的照顾下会变得茂盛健康。


虚拟语气:
If the climate were warmer, the vegetation would be lusher. = 如果气候更温暖，植被会更加茂盛。
I wish my garden were as lush as my neighbor's. = 我希望我的花园能像邻居的花园一样茂盛。

被动语态:
The landscape is often described as lush and beautiful. = 这片风景常常被描述为茂盛而美丽。
The area was lushly cultivated in the past. = 这个地区过去被耕种得非常茂盛。

疑问句:
Is the vegetation lush in this region? = 这个地区的植被茂盛吗？
Isn't the garden lush and beautiful? = 花园不茂盛美丽吗？

否定句:
The desert is not lush; it is barren. = 沙漠不茂盛，而是贫瘠的。
The garden wasn't lush last year due to the lack of rain. = 由于缺乏降雨，花园去年并不茂盛。
