capacify:/kəˈpæsɪfaɪ/ | vt. |使具有能力；使有能力；使胜任 |To give (someone or something) the capacity or ability to do something.  |例句：The training program is designed to capacify employees to handle complex tasks.  培训项目旨在使员工能够处理复杂的任务。

例句：We need to capacify the system to handle increased data volume. 我们需要增强系统的处理能力以应对不断增长的数据量。


近义词：enable, empower, qualify, equip


反义词：disable, incapacitate, cripple


词性：动词
