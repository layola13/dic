defensible:/dɪˈfɛnsəbəl/ | adj. | 可辩护的；可防御的；站得住脚的 | Justifiable or able to be defended against attack or criticism.  It implies that a position, action, or statement can be supported with reasons or evidence, making it reasonable or acceptable. |


例句：

His argument was entirely defensible. = 他的论点完全站得住脚。

The castle was strategically defensible. = 这座城堡具有战略防御优势。


近义词：justifiable, justifiable, supportable, arguable, tenable


反义词： indefensible, unjustifiable, untenable


词性：形容词
