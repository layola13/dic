outwits:/aʊtˈwɪts/| vt. |智胜，以智取胜 |to defeat or trick someone using intelligence and cleverness. |例句：The detective outwitted the cunning criminal.  侦探智胜了狡猾的罪犯。

例句：The clever fox outwitted the hunter.  狡猾的狐狸智胜了猎人。


近义词：outsmart, outmaneuver, trick, deceive |反义词：be outwitted by, be tricked by |词性:动词
