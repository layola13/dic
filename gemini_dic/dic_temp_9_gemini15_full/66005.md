examine:/ɪɡˈzæmɪn/ | vt. |检查；仔细观察；考试；审查 | To inspect or investigate something thoroughly; to test someone's knowledge or skill. |例句：The doctor examined the patient carefully. 医生仔细检查了病人。 |例句：The students are preparing for their final exams. 学生们正在准备期末考试。 |近义词：inspect, investigate, scrutinize, test |反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The professor examines the student's work carefully. = 教授仔细检查学生的作业。
She examines the evidence before making a decision. = 她做出决定之前会仔细审查证据。


一般过去时:
The mechanic examined the car engine. = 技工检查了汽车发动机。
The police examined the crime scene. = 警察检查了犯罪现场。


现在完成时:
The scientists have examined the data thoroughly. = 科学家们已经彻底检查了数据。
I have examined all the possibilities. = 我已经考虑了所有可能性。


过去完成时:

将来时:
