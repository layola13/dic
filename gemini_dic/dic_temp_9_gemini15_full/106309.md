/kɒb/| n. |圆木；土块；马头；（英）面包；|A cob is a thick, short piece of wood, often used as fuel.  It can also refer to a lump of earth or clay, or in British English, a type of bread. In some dialects, it refers to a horse's head.|例句：The farmer stacked the cobs neatly for the winter.  这位农民整齐地堆放着木柴过冬。|例句：The children played in the mud, making cobs of clay. 孩子们在泥里玩耍，做着泥巴块。|近义词：log, chunk, lump |反义词：N/A
