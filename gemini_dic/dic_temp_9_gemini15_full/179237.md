redisposing: /ˌriːdɪˈspoʊzɪŋ/ | v. |重新处理；重新安排；使…重新倾向于 | To dispose of something again; to rearrange or redistribute something; to make someone more inclined or susceptible to something. |例句：The company is redisposing its assets to focus on its core business. 公司正在重新配置资产，以专注于核心业务。

例句：After the storm, the city crews were busy redisposing of debris. 暴风雨过后，市政工作人员忙着清理残骸。


近义词：rearranging, reallocating, redistributing,  reorienting

反义词：keeping, maintaining, retaining
