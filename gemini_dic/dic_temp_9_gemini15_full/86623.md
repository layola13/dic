heliports:/ˈhɛliˌpɔːrts/| n. |直升机坪|A heliport is a landing area for helicopters, typically smaller than an airport and often located on rooftops or other elevated areas.  It usually lacks the extensive facilities of an airport.|例句：The hotel boasts a rooftop heliport for its VIP guests. 这家酒店拥有一个屋顶直升机坪，供其贵宾使用。

The emergency services landed their helicopter on the temporary heliport. 紧急服务部门将直升机降落在临时直升机坪上。|近义词：helipad, landing pad|反义词：airport (in terms of scale and facilities)
