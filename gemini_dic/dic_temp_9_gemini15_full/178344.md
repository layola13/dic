reconferring:/ˌriːkənˈfɜːrɪŋ/| v. |重新商讨，重新授予|The act of conferring again; reconsidering or discussing something again.  It involves a renewed process of deliberation or consultation.|例句：After reviewing the evidence, the committee decided to reconferring on the proposed budget.  委员会在审查证据后，决定重新商讨拟议的预算。

例句：The university is reconferring the honorary degree after discovering new information about the recipient's past. 大学在发现关于获奖者过去的新信息后，正在重新授予荣誉学位。
