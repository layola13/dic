misinformants:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmənts/| n. |故意提供错误信息的人，散布谣言者|People who deliberately give false or inaccurate information, often with the intention to deceive or mislead. They spread misinformation, which can have serious consequences. |The misinformants spread false rumors about the company's financial situation, causing panic among investors.  故意散布关于公司财务状况的谣言的人，导致投资者恐慌。| rumour-mongers,  liars, deceivers |truth-tellers,  informer (in the sense of honest informer) |名词
