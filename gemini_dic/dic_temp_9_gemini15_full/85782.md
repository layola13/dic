haymow:/ˈheɪmoʊ/| n. | 干草堆；储藏干草的阁楼 | A hayloft or barn where hay is stored.  |例句：The farmer stored his winter hay in the haymow.  农民把冬天的干草储存在干草堆里。

例句：The children played hide-and-seek in the dusty haymow. 孩子们在尘土飞扬的干草堆里玩捉迷藏。


近义词：hayloft, hayrack | 反义词：无
