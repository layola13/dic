chemotherapy:/ˌkiːmoʊˈθerəpi/ | n. |化疗；化学疗法 |The use of drugs to treat cancer. Chemotherapy drugs work by killing cancer cells or slowing their growth.  |例句：She underwent chemotherapy to treat her breast cancer. 她接受化疗治疗乳腺癌。|例句：The side effects of chemotherapy can be quite severe. 化疗的副作用可能相当严重。|近义词：cancer treatment, drug therapy |反义词：None (no direct antonym, but could contrast with surgery or radiation therapy) |名词
