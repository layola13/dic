grazers:/ˈɡreɪzərz/| n.|食草动物；放牧者|Animals that graze, typically herbivores.  They feed on grass and other plants.|例句：Zebras are grazers that roam the African savanna.  斑马是非洲大草原上游荡的食草动物。|例句：The cattle are grazers, spending their days in the pasture. 牛是食草动物，整天在牧场里吃草。|近义词：herbivores, browsers, feeders|反义词：carnivores, predators|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cattle graze in the pasture every day. = 牛每天都在牧场吃草。
Those sheep graze peacefully in the field. = 那些绵羊在田野里平静地吃草。

一般过去时:
The goats grazed on the hillside yesterday. = 山羊昨天在山坡上吃草。
The herd grazed quietly near the river. = 牛群在河边静静地吃草。

现在完成时:
The deer have grazed most of the grass in the meadow. = 鹿已经吃掉了草地上的大部分草。
The cows have grazed here for many years. = 奶牛们在这里吃草很多年了。

过去完成时:
The sheep had grazed the entire field before the farmer arrived. = 在农民到达之前，羊已经吃完了整个田野的草。
The horses had grazed all the available hay. = 马已经吃光了所有的干草。

将来时:
The sheep will graze in the new pasture tomorrow. = 羊明天将在新的牧场吃草。
The cattle will graze freely in the open field. = 牛将在开阔的田野里自由地吃草。

虚拟语气:
If the goats grazed more regularly, the grass would grow better. = 如果山羊更经常地吃草，草就会长得更好。
I wish the deer would graze elsewhere; they've eaten all my flowers! = 我希望鹿能去别的地方吃草；它们吃掉了我所有的花！

被动语态:
The pasture was grazed heavily last week. = 牧场在上周被大量啃食。
The field is being grazed by a large herd of sheep. = 田野正在被一大群羊啃食。

疑问句:
Do the cows graze in this field? = 奶牛在这个田野里吃草吗？
Where do the horses graze? = 马在哪里吃草？

否定句:
The sheep didn't graze in the field today. = 羊今天没有在田野里吃草。
The cattle are not grazing near the river anymore. = 牛不再在河边吃草了。
