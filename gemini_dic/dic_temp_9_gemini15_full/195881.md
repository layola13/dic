sensationalised:/ˌsɛnseɪʃənəlaɪzd/| v. | 耸人听闻地报道；使…显得耸人听闻 | To present (information) in a way that is intended to be exciting or shocking, often at the expense of accuracy.  | The media sensationalised the story, exaggerating the details to increase readership.媒体对这个故事进行了耸人听闻的报道，夸大了细节以增加读者数量。 | dramatized, hyped, exaggerated | downplayed, understated | 动词
