self-dominion: /ˌsɛlfˈdɑːmɪnjən/ | n. | 自我控制；自主权 | The power or right to govern oneself, especially in the context of personal behavior and choices.  It emphasizes the ability to control one's impulses, desires, and actions.  | 例句： Achieving self-dominion requires discipline and self-awareness.  实现自我控制需要自律和自我意识。

He demonstrated remarkable self-dominion in overcoming his addiction. 他在克服瘾症方面表现出了非凡的自我控制力。


近义词：self-control, self-mastery, self-regulation, autonomy  | 反义词：self-indulgence, lack of self-control, impulsiveness | 词性：名词
