catch-up:/kætʃ ʌp/| v.|赶上，弥补，闲聊|To reach the same point or standard as someone or something else; to bring oneself up to date on something.|例句：I need to catch up on my work. 我需要赶上我的工作。  
例句：Let's catch up over coffee sometime.  我们找个时间喝咖啡聊聊吧。|近义词：overtake, make up for,  update|反义词：fall behind, lag


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I catch up with my friends every week. = 我每周都和朋友们聚会。
She catches up on her reading during her commute. = 她在通勤途中阅读。

一般过去时:
He caught up on his sleep after the long journey. = 长途旅行后，他补了一觉。
They caught up with the latest news after being out of town. = 他们出城后了解了最新的消息。

现在完成时:
I have caught up with all my emails. = 我已经回复了所有的邮件。
She has caught up to the rest of the class. = 她已经赶上了班上其他同学。

过去完成时:
He had caught up with the work before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前完成了工作。
By the time I arrived, they had caught up on all the gossip. = 当我到达时，他们已经聊完了所有的八卦。

将来时:
I will catch up with you later. = 我稍后再和你联系。
We will catch up on lost time this weekend. = 我们这个周末会弥补失去的时间。

虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I could have caught up with the others. = 如果我学习更努力，我就能赶上其他人了。
I wish I could catch up with my favorite TV show. = 我希望我能赶上我最喜欢的电视剧。

被动语态:
The lost time was never caught up. = 失去的时间从未被追回。
The students are being caught up to by their peers.  = 学生们正被他们的同龄人赶上。

疑问句:
Can you catch up with the project by Friday? = 你能在周五之前完成项目吗？
Have you caught up with the latest developments? = 你了解了最新的进展吗？

否定句:
I couldn't catch up with the speaker's fast pace. = 我跟不上演讲者快速的语速。
He didn't catch up with his friends at the party. = 他在派对上没有和朋友们叙旧。


PHRASE->
catch up on = 赶上(进度，消息等)
catch up with = 赶上(某人，某事)
catch someone up = 把某人带入最新情况
make up for lost time = 弥补失去的时间
catch up to = 赶上(速度，进度)
