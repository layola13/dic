proletaneous:/ˌprɒləˈteɪniəs/| adj.|  无产阶级的；平民的；下层社会的 | Of or relating to the proletariat; characteristic of the proletariat.  The word suggests a lack of refinement or sophistication, often associated with poverty or hardship. |例句：The proletarian conditions of the factory workers were appalling.  工厂工人的无产阶级条件令人震惊。|例句：He lived a proletarian life, devoid of luxury. 他过着简朴的无产阶级生活，没有奢侈。|近义词：proletarian, working-class, common, plebeian |反义词：bourgeois, aristocratic, elite, upper-class
