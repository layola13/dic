revoyage:/ˌriːˈvɔɪjɑːʒ/ | n. | 再航行，重游 | A return journey or voyage; a second journey to the same place.  | Example: After a successful first voyage, they decided on a revoyage the following year.  一年后，在第一次航行成功后，他们决定再次航行。 | 近义词：return voyage, second journey | 反义词：first voyage, initial journey | 名词
