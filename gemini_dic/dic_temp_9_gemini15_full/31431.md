charisma: /kəˈrɪzmə/ | n. | 魅力，感召力 | A compelling attractiveness or charm that can inspire devotion in others.  It's a personal quality that enables someone to influence and inspire others. This influence is often based on perceived qualities such as confidence, energy, and conviction. |例句：The leader's charisma captivated the audience.  这位领导人的魅力吸引了观众。

The politician's charisma helped him win the election.这位政治家的魅力帮助他赢得了选举。|近义词：charm, magnetism, appeal, allure, fascination |反义词：repulsiveness, unattractiveness, dullness
