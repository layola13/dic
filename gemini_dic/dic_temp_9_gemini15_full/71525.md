flapless:/ˈfleɪpləs/| adj. |无襟翼的；无副翼的|Lacking flaps; specifically referring to an aircraft wing design without flaps.  Flapless wings are typically simpler and lighter but offer less lift control at low speeds.|例句：The experimental aircraft featured flapless wings for enhanced aerodynamic efficiency.  这架实验飞机采用无襟翼机翼以提高空气动力效率。


The design of the flapless wing contributed to its high speed but reduced maneuverability at lower altitudes. 无襟翼机翼的设计使其高速性能提升，但降低了低空机动性。|近义词：plain (in the context of wing design), unflapped|反义词：flapped
