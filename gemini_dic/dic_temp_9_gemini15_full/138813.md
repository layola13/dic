nunatak:/ˈnuːnətæk/| n. |努纳塔克（突出于冰川或冰盖的孤峰）|A nunatak is an isolated peak, ridge, or mountain that rises above the surface of a glacier or ice sheet.  Nunataks are often rocky and barren, and they provide valuable habitat for some species of plants and animals. |Example: The climbers spotted a lone nunatak piercing the vast expanse of the glacier. 登山者发现一座孤峰穿透了广阔的冰川。|近义词：island peak, exposed peak |反义词：None.
