misbestowing:/ˌmɪsbɪˈstoʊɪŋ/| v.|错置；滥用；误用 | To bestow incorrectly or inappropriately; to misuse or misapply.  The word implies a failure to use something in its intended or proper way, often leading to negative consequences. |例句：He accused the government of misbestowing public funds on unnecessary projects. 他指责政府将公共资金错用于不必要的项目。


例句：The company's misbestowing of resources led to its eventual bankruptcy.  公司的资源错置最终导致其破产。


近义词：misapply, misuse, misallocate, squander, waste


反义词：bestow appropriately, utilize effectively, allocate properly


词性：动词
