servicemen:/ˈsɜːvɪsmən/ | n. | 服役人员，军人 |  Men who are serving in the armed forces.  The term often refers to enlisted personnel rather than officers. |例句：Many servicemen were injured in the battle. 许多军人在战斗中受伤了。  The servicemen received a warm welcome upon their return home.  这些军人回家后受到了热烈的欢迎。|近义词：soldiers, military personnel, armed forces personnel |反义词：civilians
