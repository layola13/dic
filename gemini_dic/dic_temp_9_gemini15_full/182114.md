remiped:/ˈrɛmɪpɛd/| n.|桨足类动物|A remiped is a small, blind crustacean that lives in underwater caves.  They are characterized by their numerous, paddle-like appendages used for swimming and capturing prey.  They are considered a primitive group of crustaceans, representing an ancient lineage. |Example:  The discovery of a new remiped species in the underwater cave system expanded our understanding of these unusual creatures.  = 在水下洞穴系统中发现了一种新的桨足类动物，这扩展了我们对这些奇特生物的理解。|近义词: crustacean|反义词: N/A
