half-Scottish: /ˌhɑːfˈskɒtɪʃ/ | adj. | 半苏格兰的，部分苏格兰血统的 |  Describes someone of partial Scottish descent or having some, but not all, Scottish ancestry.  It suggests a blend of Scottish and other heritages.  | Example:  She's half-Scottish, half-Irish. = 她一半苏格兰血统，一半爱尔兰血统。


近义词: partly Scottish, part-Scottish

反义词: fully Scottish, entirely Scottish


词性:形容词
