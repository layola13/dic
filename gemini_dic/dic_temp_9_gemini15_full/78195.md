geotextile:/ˈdʒioʊˌtɛkstaɪl/| n.|土工织物|A permeable textile material used in civil engineering projects to separate, filter, reinforce, protect, or drain soil.|例句：Geotextiles are used to stabilize slopes and prevent erosion. 土工织物用于稳定斜坡和防止侵蚀。 The geotextile layer helped to improve the drainage of the landfill. 土工织物层有助于改善垃圾场的排水。|近义词：geomembrane, filter fabric|反义词:|名词
