plates:/pleɪts/ | n. |盘子，碟子；板块；(解剖)板 | Flat, usually round, dishes used for serving food.  Also refers to tectonic plates in geology or anatomical plates. |例句：She placed the plates on the table. 她把盘子放在桌子上。

例句：The earth's crust is made up of several tectonic plates. 地球的地壳由几大板块组成。


近义词：dishes, trays, slabs | 反义词：无


词源:  The word "plate" comes from the Old French word "platte," meaning "flat thing," ultimately deriving from the Late Latin word "platea," also meaning "flat thing" or "flat place."  The evolution reflects the basic function of the object—a flat surface for serving food.
