knowing:/ˈnoʊɪŋ/ | adj. |知道的；了解的；聪明的 | Possessing knowledge or understanding of something.  Showing awareness or understanding. |例句：He had the knowing look of someone who'd been there before. 他有着过来人的那种了然的神情。

例句：Her knowing smile betrayed her secret. 她心照不宣的微笑泄露了她的秘密。


近义词：aware, cognizant, intelligent, insightful | 反义词：ignorant, unaware, unintelligent
