referenced:/ˈrɛfərənst/| adj. |引用的，参考的|  Referring to something mentioned or described earlier.  |例句：The referenced article provides further details.  引用的文章提供了更多细节。|近义词：cited, mentioned, alluded to|反义词：original, unmentioned|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The referenced study shows a clear correlation. = 提到的研究表明存在明显的相关性。
This referenced document supports our claims. = 这份引用的文件支持我们的说法。


一般过去时:
The referenced law was repealed last year. =  被引用的法律去年被废除了。
The speaker referenced several historical events. = 演讲者提到了几个历史事件。


现在完成时:
Researchers have referenced this theory extensively. = 研究人员已经广泛引用了这一理论。
The committee has referenced multiple sources in its report. = 委员会在其报告中引用了多个来源。


过去完成时:
He had referenced the same data in his previous paper. = 他在之前的论文中已经引用了相同的数据。
The author had referenced several key studies before concluding. = 作者在得出结论之前已经参考了几项关键研究。


将来时:
She will reference this book in her upcoming presentation. = 她将在即将发表的演讲中引用这本书。
They will likely reference this finding in their future work. = 他们很可能会在未来的工作中引用这一发现。


虚拟语气:
If I had referenced that source, my argument would have been stronger. = 如果我引用了那个来源，我的论点就会更有力。
I wish I had referenced the relevant legislation earlier. = 我希望我早点引用相关的法律条文。


被动语态:
The article was referenced multiple times in the subsequent discussion. = 这篇文章在随后的讨论中被多次引用。
The data was referenced extensively in the report. = 数据在报告中被广泛引用。


疑问句:
Which study is referenced in the introduction? = 导言中引用的是哪项研究？
What sources were referenced by the author? = 作者引用了哪些资料？


否定句:
The report did not reference any conflicting evidence. = 该报告未提及任何相互矛盾的证据。
The professor didn't reference the article in his lecture. = 教授在讲座中没有引用这篇文章。


PHRASE->
referenced material = 参考材料
properly referenced = 正确引用的
cross-referenced = 交叉引用的
fully referenced = 全面引用的
self-referenced = 自引用的
