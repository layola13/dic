reestablishes:/ˌriːɪˈstæblɪʃɪz/| vt.|重建；恢复；重新建立|To establish again; to restore to a former state.|The government reestablishes order after the riot. 政府在暴乱后重建秩序。|The company reestablished its market share. 公司恢复了它的市场份额。|establish, reinstate, restore|destroy, abolish, dismantle|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reestablishes his business every year. 他每年都重建他的生意。
The committee reestablishes the rules regularly. 委员会定期重新制定规则。


一般过去时:
They reestablished diplomatic relations last year. 他们去年恢复了外交关系。
She reestablished contact with her old friends. 她重新联系了她的老朋友。


现在完成时:
The government has reestablished control over the region. 政府已经重新控制了该地区。
We have reestablished our position in the market. 我们已经重新确立了我们在市场上的地位。


过去完成时:
Before the peace treaty, they had reestablished communication channels. 在和平条约签订之前，他们已经重新建立了沟通渠道。
He had reestablished his reputation by the time the trial ended. 在审判结束时，他已经恢复了自己的名誉。


将来时:
The company will reestablish its presence in the Asian market. 公司将在亚洲市场重新确立自己的地位。
They will reestablish the old tradition next year. 他们明年将恢复古老的传统。


虚拟语气:
If the government reestablished the old laws, there would be protests. 如果政府恢复旧法，将会引发抗议。
I wish they had reestablished the program sooner. 我希望他们早点恢复这个项目。


被动语态:
Order was reestablished by the police. 秩序由警方恢复。
The bridge was reestablished after the flood. 桥梁在洪水过后得到重建。


疑问句:
Will they reestablish the old system? 他们会恢复旧制度吗？
Has the company reestablished its profitability? 公司已经恢复盈利了吗？


否定句:
They did not reestablish contact after the argument. 争吵后他们没有恢复联系。
The company failed to reestablish its market share. 公司未能恢复其市场份额。
