lampyrine: /ˈlæmpɪraɪn/ | n. |萤火虫素，萤光素|A chemical compound that produces light in fireflies (lightning bugs). It is a luciferin, a substance that produces light through a chemical reaction with oxygen. Lampyrine is responsible for the bioluminescence exhibited by fireflies.|The firefly's glow is due to the oxidation of lampyrine. 萤火虫的光芒是由于萤火虫素的氧化作用。|近义词：luciferin|反义词：无
