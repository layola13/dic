helped-out:/helptˈaʊt/| v.|帮助，协助|To provide assistance or support; to contribute to a task or effort.|例句：He helped out with the dishes after dinner. 他晚饭后帮忙洗碗。  例句：She helped out at the homeless shelter on weekends. 她周末在收容所帮忙。|近义词：assist, aid, support|反义词：hinder, obstruct


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He helps out his neighbors regularly. = 他定期帮助他的邻居。
They help out at the food bank every Saturday. = 他们每个星期六都在食物银行帮忙。


一般过去时:
I helped out my friend with his move. = 我帮助我的朋友搬家。
She helped out at the charity event last night. = 她昨晚参加了慈善活动并提供了帮助。


现在完成时:
He has helped out at the community center many times. = 他多次在社区中心帮忙。
They have helped out numerous families in need. = 他们已经帮助了许多需要帮助的家庭。


过去完成时:
I had helped out at the soup kitchen before, so I knew what to expect. = 我以前曾在施粥所帮忙过，所以我了解情况。
She had helped out her sister with her studies before the exam. = 她在考试前帮助过她姐姐学习。


将来时:
I will help out at the school fair next week. = 我下周将在学校的集市上帮忙。
They will help out with the fundraising event. = 他们将帮助筹款活动。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would help out more often. = 如果我有时间，我会更经常地帮忙。
If she had asked, I would have helped out. = 如果她问的话，我会帮忙的。


被动语态:
The project was helped out by many volunteers. = 许多志愿者帮助了这个项目。
The community was helped out by the generous donation. = 社区得到了慷慨捐赠的帮助。


疑问句:

否定句:
