shelves:/ʃɛlvz/ | n. | 架子；搁板 | Shelves are used for storing things.  架子用于存放物品。|  近义词：racks, racks, shelves | 反义词：floor, ground


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shelves hold many books. = 架子上放着许多书。
Those shelves are made of wood. = 那些架子是木头做的。


一般过去时:
We put the books on the shelves yesterday. = 昨天我们把书放在架子上。
The old shelves collapsed. = 旧书架倒塌了。


现在完成时:
They have arranged the books on the shelves. = 他们已经把书摆放在架子上。
We have finally finished building the shelves. = 我们终于完成了书架的搭建。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
