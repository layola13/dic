chivvy's:/ˈtʃɪviːz/| n.| 责骂；催促；唠叨 | The act of repeatedly urging or nagging someone.  It's often used informally and can imply a slightly aggressive or persistent nature. |例句：Her constant chivvying about the chores was driving him mad.  她不断地唠叨家务活快把他逼疯了。|近义词：nag, badger, harry, goad |反义词：encourage (in a gentle way), soothe, pacify
