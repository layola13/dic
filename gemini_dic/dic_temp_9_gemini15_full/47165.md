dawling: /ˈdɔːlɪŋ/ | v. | 慢慢地移动；磨蹭；闲荡 | To move slowly and idly; to waste time; to linger. |例句：Stop dawdling and get ready for school!  别磨蹭了，赶紧准备上学！

例句：He was dawdling along the beach, enjoying the sunset. 他在海滩上慢慢地走着，欣赏着日落。


近义词：delay, linger, procrastinate, loiter, tarry | 反义词：hurry, rush, hasten

词源:
The word "dawdle" likely originates from the Middle English word "dawdelen," which meant "to act sluggishly."  The exact etymology is uncertain, but it may be related to words suggesting slow or weak movement.
