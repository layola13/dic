deficient:/dɪˈfɪʃənt/ | adj. | 缺乏的；不足的；有缺陷的 | Lacking an essential constituent or element; inadequate.  | The soil is deficient in nitrogen. 这土壤缺乏氮。 |  The child's diet was deficient in essential vitamins. 这个孩子的饮食缺乏必需的维生素。| insufficient, inadequate, lacking | sufficient, adequate, complete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is deficient in vitamin D. = 他缺乏维生素D。
The plan is deficient in several key areas. = 这个计划在几个关键领域存在不足。

一般过去时:
The crop was deficient in yield last year. = 去年农作物产量不足。
The report was deficient in detail. = 报告细节不足。


现在完成时:
The team has been deficient in its performance this season. = 这个赛季球队表现不佳。
The company has been deficient in its customer service. = 公司的客户服务一直存在不足。


过去完成时:
The bridge had been deficient in structural integrity for years. = 这座桥梁的结构完整性多年来一直存在缺陷。
The system had been deficient in security measures. = 该系统一直缺乏安全措施。


将来时:
The project will be deficient in funding unless we secure additional grants. = 除非我们获得额外拨款，否则该项目将资金不足。
The product will be deficient in quality if we rush the manufacturing process. = 如果我们仓促生产，产品质量就会有缺陷。


虚拟语气:
If the soil were deficient in nutrients, the plants would not thrive. = 如果土壤缺乏营养，植物就不会茁壮成长。
I wish my knowledge of the subject were not so deficient. = 我希望我对这个主题的了解不那么匮乏。


被动语态:
The building was found to be deficient in fire safety measures. = 该建筑被发现缺乏消防安全措施。
His application was rejected because it was deficient in several areas. = 他的申请被拒绝，因为它在几个方面存在不足。


疑问句:
Is the equipment deficient in any way? = 设备有任何缺陷吗？
Are the children deficient in any essential nutrients? = 孩子们缺乏任何必需的营养物质吗？


否定句:
The product is not deficient in quality. = 产品质量没有缺陷。
The system is not deficient in security. = 系统安全性能没有缺陷。


PHRASE->
deficient in sth.  = 缺乏某物
nutritionally deficient = 营养不良的
cognitively deficient = 认知能力不足的
immune deficient = 免疫缺陷的
developmentally deficient = 发育迟缓的
