pre-void:/priːˈvɔɪd/ | adj. | 预先无效的，先前无效的 |  Describes something that is made invalid or ineffective before it can take effect.  It suggests a proactive measure to prevent something from becoming valid or active. | The pre-void agreement was never actually implemented.  预先无效的协议从未真正执行。|近义词：null, void, invalid, canceled |反义词：valid, effective
