object:/ˈɒbdʒekt/ /ˈɑːbdʒɛkt/| n. v.|物体，目标，宾语；反对|An object is a thing that can be seen or touched.|例句：The cat sat on the object.  猫坐在那个物体上。|近义词：item, thing, goal, purpose|反义词：subject


object:/əbˈdʒɛkt/| v.|反对，抗议|To object is to express disapproval or opposition.|例句：He objected to the proposal. 他反对这项提议。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird is an object of beauty. = 这只鸟是美丽的物体。
That object represents our hopes. = 那个物体代表着我们的希望。


一般过去时:
They objected to the new rules. = 他们反对新规。
She objected strongly to his behavior. = 她强烈反对他的行为。


现在完成时:
Many have objected to the plan. = 许多人已经反对了这个计划。
He has never objected to my ideas before. = 他以前从未反对过我的想法。


过去完成时:
They had objected to the decision before the meeting. = 他们在会议前就对这个决定表示了反对。
She had already objected when he mentioned it again. = 当他再次提起时，她已经表示反对了。


将来时:
I will object if they try to change the rules. = 如果他们试图改变规则，我会反对。
They will object to the increase in taxes. = 他们会反对增税。


虚拟语气:
I would object if I were in his position. = 如果我处于他的位置，我会反对。
If they knew, they would object. = 如果他们知道，他们会反对。


被动语态:
The plan was objected to by many people. = 许多人反对这项计划。
His proposal was strongly objected to. = 他的提议遭到强烈反对。


疑问句:
What is the object of your affection? = 你爱慕的对象是什么？
Do you object to my proposal? = 你反对我的提议吗？


否定句:
I do not object to your suggestion. = 我不反对你的建议。
They did not object to the changes. = 他们没有反对这些变化。
