preinterpretation:/ˌpriːɪnˌtɜːrpɹɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |预释；预解释；先期诠释 |The act of interpreting something before it is fully understood or before all the facts are known; a preliminary interpretation.  It often involves making assumptions or educated guesses based on limited information. |例句：The preinterpretation of the data suggested a positive outcome, but further analysis was needed. 数据的预释表明结果为正，但需要进一步分析。

例句：His preinterpretation of the poem was based on a superficial reading. 他对这首诗的预解释是基于肤浅的阅读。


近义词：preliminary interpretation, anticipation, speculation, conjecture


反义词：final interpretation, definitive explanation


词性：名词
