ghostliest: /ˈɡoʊstliːst/ | adj. |最幽灵似的，最鬼祟的 | Most resembling a ghost; extremely pale, faint, or indistinct.  It is the superlative form of "ghostly". |例句：The ghostliest figure emerged from the shadows.  最幽灵似的身影从阴影中浮现。

The ghostliest whisper echoed through the empty hall. 最令人毛骨悚然的低语回荡在空旷的大厅里。|近义词：spectral, ethereal, shadowy, pallid, faint |反义词：vivid, bright, clear, robust

词源:
