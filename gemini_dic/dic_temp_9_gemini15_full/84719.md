handiest:/ˈhændiɪst/| adj.|最方便的，最得手的|Most convenient or useful.  It implies that something is readily available and easily used. The word is the superlative form of "handy."|例句：This is the handiest tool in my workshop. 这是我车间里最方便的工具。

例句：That new kitchen gadget is the handiest thing I've ever owned.  那个新的厨房用具是我用过的最方便的东西。


近义词：most convenient, most useful, most practical, most accessible

反义词：most inconvenient, least useful, most impractical, least accessible

词性:形容词
