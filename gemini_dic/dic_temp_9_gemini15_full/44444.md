cross-sectioned: /ˈkrɒs ˈsɛkʃənd/ | adj. | 横截面的；被横截的 |  Describes something that has been cut or viewed in a cross-section, revealing the internal structure.  It implies a cut perpendicular to the main axis or length of an object. |

例句：

The geologist examined the cross-sectioned rock sample to study its layers. = 地质学家检查了横截面的岩石样本以研究其层状结构。

The diagram shows a cross-sectioned view of the engine. = 该图显示了发动机的横截面图。


近义词：transected, dissected (in specific contexts)


反义词：longitudinally sectioned, lengthwise sectioned


词性：形容词
