nonigneous:/ˌnɒnɪˈɡniːəs/ | adj. |非火成的；非岩浆的 | Not formed by the action of fire or volcanoes; referring to rocks formed by processes other than volcanic activity, such as sedimentary or metamorphic processes.  | Example:  The nonigneous rocks in this area are primarily sedimentary.  这个地区的非火成岩主要是沉积岩。|近义词：sedimentary, metamorphic |反义词：igneous
