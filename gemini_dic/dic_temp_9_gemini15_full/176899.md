real-world:/ˌriːəlˈwɜːld/| adj.|现实世界的；真实世界的| relating to or denoting the everyday world as opposed to the world of imagination or theory.  This adjective emphasizes the practical and tangible aspects of situations, experiences, or problems, contrasting them with hypothetical or theoretical scenarios. |例句：The real-world application of this technology is still uncertain.  这项技术的现实应用仍不确定。

The experiment produced impressive results in a simulated environment, but its real-world effectiveness remains to be seen.  实验在模拟环境中产生了令人印象深刻的结果，但其现实世界的有效性还有待观察。|近义词：practical, actual, tangible, concrete|反义词：theoretical, hypothetical, imaginary, simulated
