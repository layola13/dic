eisenhower: /ˈaɪzənhaʊər/ | n. |艾森豪威尔 |Eisenhower refers to Dwight D. Eisenhower, the 34th President of the United States.  He is known for his leadership during World War II and his presidency, which saw the beginning of the Interstate Highway System.  The name is also used as an adjective to describe things related to him or his policies. | Example:  Eisenhower's presidency was a time of relative peace and prosperity.  艾森豪威尔的总统任期是相对和平与繁荣的时期。|近义词: None (as a proper noun)|反义词: None (as a proper noun)|名词
