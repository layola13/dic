semi-elliptical:/ˌsemiːɪˈlɪptɪkəl/| adj.|半椭圆形的；半椭圆的|  Describes a shape that is half of an ellipse.  It is often used to describe arches, springs, or other curved structures. | Example: The bridge featured a semi-elliptical arch. 这座桥有一个半椭圆形的拱门。|近义词：half-elliptical, semi-oval|反义词：elliptical, circular

词源:
The word "semi-elliptical" is a combination of "semi-" (meaning "half") and "elliptical" (relating to an ellipse).  "Ellipse" itself comes from the Greek word  ἐλλειψις (elleipsis), meaning "omission" or "falling short," referring to the way an ellipse is a slightly flattened circle.
