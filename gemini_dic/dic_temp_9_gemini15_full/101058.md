invented:/ɪnˈvɛntɪd/ | v. |发明；虚构；捏造 | To create or produce (something) for the first time.  To fabricate or make up (something). | 例句：Alexander Graham Bell invented the telephone.  亚历山大·格雷厄姆·贝尔发明了电话。

例句：He invented a story about being lost in the woods. 他编造了一个关于在树林里迷路的故事。 |近义词：created, devised, fabricated, concocted |反义词：destroyed, abolished,disproved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He invents new gadgets every year. = 他每年都发明新的玩意儿。
She invents excuses to avoid work. = 她编造借口来逃避工作。

一般过去时:
Edison invented the light bulb. = 爱迪生发明了电灯泡。
They invented a new game to play. = 他们发明了一个新的游戏。

现在完成时:
Scientists have invented many life-saving drugs. = 科学家们发明了许多救命的药物。
She has invented a new way to solve the problem. = 她发明了一种解决这个问题的新方法。

过去完成时:
They had invented the machine before the war began. = 战争开始前他们已经发明了那台机器。
He had invented a false alibi before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前他已经编造了一个虚假的不在场证明。

将来时:
They will invent new technologies in the future. = 他们将来会发明新的技术。
She will invent a new story for her next book. = 她将为她的下一本书编造一个新的故事。

虚拟语气:
If I had invented that, I would be rich. = 如果我发明了那个，我就富有了。
I wish I had invented time travel. = 我真希望我发明了时间旅行。

被动语态:
The telephone was invented by Alexander Graham Bell. = 电话是由亚历山大·格雷厄姆·贝尔发明的。
A new theory has been invented to explain the phenomenon. = 一个新的理论被发明出来解释这种现象。

疑问句:
Who invented the printing press? = 谁发明了印刷机？
Did they invent that story? = 他们编造了那个故事吗？

否定句:
He didn't invent the wheel. = 他没有发明轮子。
She didn't invent that excuse. = 她没有编造那个借口。


PHRASE->
invent a story = 编造故事
invent an excuse = 编造借口
invent a solution = 想出解决方案
invent a new language = 创造一种新语言
newly invented = 新发明的
