rowing:/ˈroʊɪŋ/ | n. v. |划船；赛艇运动 |The act of propelling a boat through water using oars.  It can also refer to the sport of competitive rowing. |例句：He enjoys rowing on the lake. 他喜欢在湖上划船。
例句：The rowing team won the championship. 赛艇队赢得了冠军。|近义词：oaring, paddling |反义词：sailing (in the context of boat propulsion)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They row every morning. = 他们每天早上划船。
He rows a single scull. = 他划单人艇。

一般过去时:
We rowed across the lake yesterday. = 我们昨天划船横渡了湖泊。
She rowed in the regatta. = 她参加了赛艇比赛。

现在完成时:
I have rowed several times this summer. = 今年夏天我已经划过几次船了。
They have rowed many miles. = 他们已经划了好几英里了。

过去完成时:
We had rowed for hours before we reached the island. = 我们到达岛屿之前已经划了好几个小时的船了。
He had rowed the entire length of the river before stopping. = 他在停下来之前已经划完了整条河。

将来时:
We will row to the other side of the lake tomorrow. = 我们明天将划船到湖的另一边。
They will be rowing in the competition next week. = 他们下周将参加比赛划船。

虚拟语气:
If I had a boat, I would row every day. = 如果我有一艘船，我每天都会划船。
I wish I could row better. = 我希望我能划得更好。

被动语态:
The boat was rowed by a skilled oarsman. = 这艘船由一位熟练的桨手划动。
The race was rowed in difficult conditions. = 比赛在恶劣的条件下进行。

疑问句:
Do you row regularly? = 你经常划船吗？
Where do they row? = 他们在哪里划船？

否定句:
I don't row very often. = 我不经常划船。
He didn't row in the race. = 他没有参加比赛划船。

词源:
The word "rowing" comes from the Old English word *rōwan*, meaning "to row."  This word has Germanic roots, connected to words in other Germanic languages with similar meanings.
