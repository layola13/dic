doing:/ˈduːɪŋ/ | v. | 做；进行；从事 |  The present participle of "do." It indicates an action in progress or a habitual action. It can function as a noun, adjective, or part of a verb phrase. |

例句：I am doing my homework. = 我正在做我的作业。

例句：He is always doing that. = 他总是那样做。


近义词：making, performing, accomplishing, undertaking | 反义词：undoing, ceasing, stopping | 词性：动词（现在分词）


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am doing my best. = 我正在尽我所能。
They are doing well in school. = 他们在学校表现很好。

一般过去时:
I was doing the dishes when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候我正在洗碗。
She was doing her hair before going out. = 她出门前正在梳头。

现在完成时:
I have been doing this job for five years. = 我已经做了这份工作五年了。
They have been doing research on that topic. = 他们一直在研究那个话题。

过去完成时:
I had been doing yoga for years before I joined the class. = 在我参加那个班之前，我已经练瑜伽很多年了。
They had been doing their homework all day. = 他们一整天都在做作业。

将来时:
I will be doing the presentation tomorrow. = 我明天将做演示。
They will be doing the experiment next week. = 他们下周将进行实验。

虚拟语气:
If I were doing that, I would be careful. = 如果我在做那件事，我会小心。
I wish I were doing something more productive. = 我希望我正在做更有成效的事情。

被动语态:
The work is being done efficiently. = 工作正在高效地进行。
The house is being done up. = 房子正在装修。

疑问句:
What are you doing? = 你在做什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 对于这个问题正在采取什么措施？

否定句:
I am not doing anything interesting. = 我没有做任何有趣的事情。
They weren't doing their work properly. = 他们没有正确地做他们的工作。


PHRASE->
goings-on:  = 发生的事情；活动
in the doing: = 在进行中；在处理中
the doing of: = 做……的行为；……的实施
worth doing: = 值得做的
make a doing: = 尽力做某事
