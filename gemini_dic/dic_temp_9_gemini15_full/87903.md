hieratic:/ˌhaɪəˈrætɪk/| adj. |神圣的；祭司的；（指文字）圣书体的|  relating to or denoting the cursive form of ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs, used chiefly for religious and literary texts.  It's a simplified form of hieroglyphs, used for everyday writing. | Example: The hieratic script was used by priests and scribes in ancient Egypt.  埃及祭司和抄写员使用圣书体。| 近义词：sacred, priestly, cursive | 反义词：profane, secular, demotic
