risking:/ˈrɪskɪŋ/| v. |冒险；使冒风险 |To expose to danger or loss; to take a chance on.  |例句：He's risking his life by climbing that mountain. 他在冒着生命危险攀登那座山。

例句：She's risking her reputation by associating with him. 她与他交往，冒着毁坏名声的风险。


近义词：gambling, jeopardizing, endangering, hazarding |反义词：safeguarding, protecting, securing |词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is risking his savings on the stock market. 他正在把积蓄押在股市上冒险。
They are risking their health by smoking. 他们吸烟正在危害健康。


一般过去时:
I risked everything to start my own business. 我冒着一切风险创办了自己的公司。
She risked her job by speaking out against the company's policies. 她因公开反对公司政策而冒着丢工作的风险。


现在完成时:
He has risked his life many times for his country. 他为了国家多次冒着生命危险。
They have risked their reputation by making false claims. 他们因做出虚假声明而冒着损害名誉的风险。


过去完成时:
I had risked everything, and it had all been for nothing. 我已经冒了所有风险，结果却什么也没有得到。
She had risked her career by challenging the boss's decision. 她因挑战老板的决定而冒着职业生涯受损的风险。


将来时:
I will be risking my neck if I try that. 如果我尝试的话，我会冒着丢掉性命的危险。
They will be risking a lot of money by investing in that company. 他们投资那家公司将冒着损失大量金钱的风险。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't risk it. 如果我是你，我不会冒险。
I wish I hadn't risked so much money. 我真希望我没有冒那么大的风险。


被动语态:
His reputation was risked by his association with criminals. 他与罪犯的交往使他的名誉受到了损害。
Their lives were risked during the rescue mission. 在救援行动中，他们的生命受到了威胁。


疑问句:
Are you risking your job by doing this? 你这样做是在冒着丢工作的风险吗？
Is he risking his health by working so hard? 他如此努力工作是在冒着损害健康的风险吗？


否定句:
I won't risk my life for you. 我不会为了你冒生命危险。
They didn't risk their money on that investment. 他们没有把钱押在那项投资上冒险。

词源：
The word "risking" comes from the Old North French word "risquer," meaning "to risk," which in turn is derived from the Arabic word "risk," meaning "to gamble."  The evolution of the word reflects the historical association between risk-taking and gambling.
