proliferate:/prəˈlɪfəreɪt/| v.|迅速繁殖；激增；扩散 |To increase rapidly in number; to multiply |The cancer cells proliferated rapidly.  癌细胞迅速增殖。|The rumors proliferated throughout the town. 谣言在镇上迅速传播。|increase, multiply, expand, mushroom|decrease, diminish, dwindle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Cancer cells proliferate rapidly. = 癌细胞迅速增殖。
New businesses proliferate in the city center. =  新的企业在市中心迅速涌现。

一般过去时:
The rabbit population proliferated after the flood. = 洪水过后，兔子数量激增。
The weeds proliferated in the neglected garden. = 杂草在被忽视的花园里疯长。

现在完成时:
The use of smartphones has proliferated in recent years. = 近年来智能手机的使用已迅速普及。
Online shopping has proliferated since the pandemic. = 自疫情以来，网上购物已经激增。

过去完成时:
By the time the doctors arrived, the infection had proliferated throughout his body. = 等医生赶到时，感染已经扩散到他全身。
Before the intervention, the rumors had proliferated widely. = 干预之前，谣言已广泛传播。

将来时:
Experts predict that the use of AI will proliferate in the coming years. = 专家预测人工智能的使用将在未来几年迅速普及。
It is likely that plastic pollution will proliferate if we don't take action. = 如果我们不采取行动，塑料污染很可能会激增。

虚拟语气:
If the bacteria proliferated unchecked, it could be disastrous. = 如果细菌不受控制地增殖，那将是灾难性的。
I wish these weeds wouldn't proliferate so quickly. = 我真希望这些杂草不会长得这么快。

被动语态:
The virus was allowed to proliferate unchecked. = 病毒被允许不受控制地增殖。
The misinformation was deliberately allowed to proliferate online. = 虚假信息被故意允许在网上传播。

疑问句:
Why do these plants proliferate so easily? = 为什么这些植物如此容易繁殖？
How did the rumors proliferate so quickly? = 谣言是如何如此迅速地传播开的？

否定句:
The government tried to prevent the disease from proliferating. = 政府试图阻止疾病蔓延。
Efforts to control the spread of the disease failed to prevent it from proliferating. = 控制疾病传播的努力未能阻止其蔓延。
