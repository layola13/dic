equating:/ɪˈkweɪtɪŋ/| v. |视…为等同；使等同；使相等|The act of considering or representing something as equivalent to something else.  It often implies a comparison or an assertion of similarity, even if the things being equated aren't perfectly identical.|例句：Equating success with wealth is a common misconception.  将成功与财富等同是一种常见的误解。

例句：He was equating his experience with mine, ignoring the vast differences. 他将他的经历与我的等同起来，忽略了巨大的差异。


近义词：equalizing, identifying, associating, paralleling


反义词：differentiating, distinguishing, contrasting


词性：动词
