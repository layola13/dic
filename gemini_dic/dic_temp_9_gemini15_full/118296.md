mercurialization:/ˌmɜːrkjʊriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |水银化；用汞处理| The process of treating something with mercury, or becoming saturated with mercury.  This term is primarily used in scientific and technical contexts, referring to the application of mercury in various processes or the unintended contamination of materials with mercury. |Example: The mercurialization of the gold ore improved its extraction efficiency.  水银化金矿提高了其提取效率。The accidental mercurialization of the soil required extensive remediation efforts. 土壤意外水银化需要大规模的修复工作。|近义词：mercurization, amalgamation (in specific contexts)|反义词：demercurization
