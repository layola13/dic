oafish:/ˈoʊfɪʃ/| adj.|笨拙的；粗鲁的；愚蠢的 | Awkward and clumsy in movement or manner; insensitive or stupid.  |例句：His oafish behavior at the party embarrassed everyone. 他在派对上的笨拙行为让每个人都很尴尬。|例句：He made an oafish remark that offended several guests. 他说了一件粗鲁的话，冒犯了几位客人。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, uncouth, gauche |反义词：graceful, refined, sophisticated, polite
