overpatriotism: /ˌoʊvərˈpeɪtriəˌtɪzəm/ | n. | 极端的爱国主义；过度的爱国心 | Excessive patriotism; nationalism taken to extremes.  It often manifests as blind allegiance to one's country, regardless of its flaws or actions.  This can lead to intolerance of dissent and the suppression of criticism. |


例句：

His overpatriotism blinded him to the country's shortcomings.  他的极端爱国主义使他看不到国家的缺点。

The government's actions were fueled by overpatriotism and a disregard for international law. 政府的行为是由极端的爱国主义和对国际法的漠视所驱使的。


近义词：jingoism, chauvinism, ultranationalism


反义词：cosmopolitanism, internationalism


词性：名词
