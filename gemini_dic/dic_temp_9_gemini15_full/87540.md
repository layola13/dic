heterogeneous:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. | 异质的；多种多样的；不均匀的 | Consisting of dissimilar or diverse ingredients or constituents; composed of different kinds; unlike.  | 例句：The student body is remarkably heterogeneous, representing a wide range of backgrounds and experiences. 学生群体非常多元化，代表着广泛的背景和经验。

The soil sample showed a heterogeneous mixture of clay, sand, and organic matter. 土壤样本显示出粘土、沙子和有机质的混合物。


近义词：diverse, varied, mixed, dissimilar, motley | 反义词：homogeneous, uniform, consistent

词性:形容词
