discontentedly:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪdli/ | adv. | 不满地；不高兴地 |  In a discontented manner; showing dissatisfaction or unhappiness. |例句：He grumbled discontentedly about the long wait. 他不满地抱怨漫长的等待。


He stared discontentedly at the unfinished painting. 他不满地盯着未完成的画作。


近义词：unhappily, grumblingly, resentfully, sullenly | 反义词：happily, contentedly, cheerfully, gladly | 词性:副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He speaks discontentedly about his job. = 他不满地谈论他的工作。
She always looks discontentedly at her reflection. = 她总是很不高兴地看着自己的倒影。


一般过去时:
He ate his dinner discontentedly. = 他很不高兴地吃了晚饭。
She responded discontentedly to my suggestion. = 她对我的建议很不满地回应。


现在完成时:
He has lived discontentedly for many years. = 他多年来一直过得不快乐。
She has worked discontentedly ever since the promotion. = 自从升职以来，她一直工作得不开心。


过去完成时:
He had lived discontentedly before he found his passion. = 在他找到自己的热情之前，他一直过得不快乐。
She had worked discontentedly until she finally quit her job. = 直到最终辞职，她才一直工作得不开心。


将来时:
He will likely continue to live discontentedly if things don't change. = 如果情况没有改变，他很可能会继续过得不快乐。
She will probably work discontentedly until she finds a new job. = 她可能会一直工作得不开心，直到找到一份新工作。


虚拟语气:
If he were happier, he wouldn't speak discontentedly. = 如果他更快乐，他就不会不满地说话了。
If she felt valued, she wouldn't look discontentedly at her work. = 如果她感到被重视，她就不会不满地看待她的工作。


被动语态:
His complaints were voiced discontentedly. = 他的抱怨是不满地表达出来的。
Her dissatisfaction was shown discontentedly. = 她的不满是不满地表现出来的。


疑问句:
Did he complain discontentedly? = 他是否不满地抱怨？
Does she often act discontentedly? = 她是否经常表现得不开心？


否定句:
He did not express his unhappiness discontentedly.  = 他没有不满地表达他的不快乐。
She did not react discontentedly to the news. = 她对这个消息并没有不满地作出反应。

词源:
dis- (否定前缀) + content (满意) + -edly (副词后缀)  It combines the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "contented" (meaning "satisfied"), and adds the adverbial suffix "-edly" to form an adverb.
