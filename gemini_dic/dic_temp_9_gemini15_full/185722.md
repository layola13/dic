reverts:/rɪˈvɜːrts/| v.|回复；恢复；逆转|To return to a previous state, practice, or belief.  It can also mean to reply or respond, especially to a message or communication.|例句：The system automatically reverts to the default settings if an error occurs. 系统发生错误时会自动恢复到默认设置。

The patient reverted to his old habits after leaving the hospital.  病人出院后又恢复了旧习惯。


近义词：return, revert back, revert to, restore, regress, relapse|反义词：progress, advance, improve, develop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reverts to his childhood memories often. 他经常回忆起童年的往事。
The system reverts to the previous version when it detects an error. 系统检测到错误时会恢复到之前的版本。


一般过去时:
The company reverted to its original business plan. 公司恢复了最初的商业计划。
She reverted to her maiden name after the divorce. 离婚后她恢复了娘家姓。


现在完成时:
The software has reverted to its previous version after the update. 软件更新后已恢复到之前的版本。
He has reverted to type and started smoking again. 他故态复萌，又开始吸烟了。


过去完成时:
They had reverted to their old ways before the new rules were implemented. 新规实施之前，他们已经恢复了旧习。
The economy had reverted to a state of recession. 经济已经倒退到衰退状态。


将来时:
The system will revert to its default settings if the problem persists. 如果问题持续存在，系统将恢复到默认设置。
I think he will revert to his old bad habits. 我认为他会故态复萌。


虚拟语气:
If the situation worsened, we would revert to the backup plan. 如果情况恶化，我们将恢复到备用计划。
I wish he hadn't reverted to his old drinking habits. 我真希望他没有恢复他以前的酗酒习惯。


被动语态:
The settings were reverted to the factory defaults. 设置已恢复到出厂默认值。
The decision was later reverted.  该决定后来被撤销了。


疑问句:
Did the system revert to the default settings? 系统是否恢复到默认设置？
Will the company revert to its original strategy? 公司会恢复其最初的战略吗？


否定句:
The software did not revert to the previous version. 软件没有恢复到之前的版本。
The government refused to revert its decision. 政府拒绝撤销其决定。
