chlorinating: /ˈklɔːrɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 氯化；用氯处理 | The process of treating something with chlorine, typically to disinfect or bleach it.  | Example: The city chlorinates its water supply to kill harmful bacteria.  城市用氯气处理自来水以杀死有害细菌。  The pool was chlorinated before the swim meet.  游泳比赛前泳池进行了氯化处理。 |近义词： disinfecting, bleaching | 反义词：dechlorinating
