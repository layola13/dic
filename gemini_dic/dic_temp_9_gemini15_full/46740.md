dandles: /ˈdændlz/ | vt. | 轻轻摇晃；爱抚；玩弄 | To move or handle (something) gently and repeatedly; to playfully toss or bounce a baby or child.  | 例句：She dandled the baby on her knee. 她轻轻地把婴儿抱在膝盖上摇晃。

The mother dandled her child to sleep. 母亲轻轻地摇晃孩子哄其入睡。


近义词：rock, toss, bounce, cuddle, fondle | 反义词：throw, hurl, fling, neglect


词源：源自古英语 *dandal,  意思是“摇晃”，与“dance”（跳舞）同源。
