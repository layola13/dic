hankers:/ˈhæŋkərz/| v.|渴望，热切地想要|To have a strong desire or longing for something.|He hankered after a simpler life. 他渴望过一种更简单的生活。|She hankered for the days of her youth. 她渴望回到年轻的时光。|long for, yearn for, crave|dislike, reject.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hankers after fame. = 他渴望成名。
They hanker for a peaceful life. = 他们渴望平静的生活。

一般过去时:
She hankered after a career in music. = 她曾经渴望在音乐方面发展事业。
He hankered for his home country. = 他渴望回到祖国。

现在完成时:
I have hankered after this book for years. = 我多年来一直渴望得到这本书。
They have hankered after recognition for their work. = 他们一直渴望得到他们工作的认可。

过去完成时:
She had hankered after a promotion for months. = 她几个月来一直渴望升职。
He had hankered for a change of scenery for years. = 他多年来一直渴望改变环境。

将来时:
I will hanker after this opportunity for the rest of my life. = 我将在余生渴望得到这个机会。
She will hanker after a quiet retirement. = 她将渴望一个平静的退休生活。


虚拟语气:
If I had the chance, I would hanker after the same thing. = 如果我有机会，我也会渴望同样的事情。
I wish I didn't hanker after things I cannot have. = 我希望我不渴望我得不到的东西。


被动语态:
The position is hankered after by many. = 许多人都渴望得到这个职位。
Peace is hankered after by all. =  所有人都渴望和平。


疑问句:
Does he hanker after power? = 他渴望权力吗？
What does she hanker after? = 她渴望什么？


否定句:
He does not hanker after material possessions. = 他不渴望物质财富。
She did not hanker after a life of luxury. = 她不渴望奢华的生活。


PHRASE->
hanker after something = 渴望某物
hanker for something = 渴望某物
hanker to do something = 渴望做某事
secretly hanker after = 暗中渴望
openly hanker for = 公开渴望
