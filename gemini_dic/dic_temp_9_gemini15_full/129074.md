nitrohydrochloric: /ˌnaɪtroʊˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːrɪk/ | adj. |硝酸盐酸的|  Nitric acid and hydrochloric acid mixed together.  This is a highly corrosive and reactive mixture used in various chemical processes, historically notable for its use in dissolving noble metals like gold and platinum.|

例句：Aqua regia, a nitrohydrochloric acid solution, is capable of dissolving gold. 王水，一种硝酸盐酸溶液，能够溶解黄金。

The etching process utilized nitrohydrochloric acid to create the intricate design.  蚀刻过程使用硝酸盐酸来创建复杂的设计。


近义词：aqua regia (王水)


反义词：无


词性：形容词
