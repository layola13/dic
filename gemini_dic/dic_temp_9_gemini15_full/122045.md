mistake:/mɪˈsteɪk/ | n. |错误，过失 | An incorrect action, statement, or judgment.  A wrong action or statement resulting from bad judgment or ignorance. |例句：I made a mistake in the calculations.  我计算错了。

例句：That was a serious mistake; it cost the company a lot of money. 那是一个严重的错误；它让公司损失了很多钱。


近义词：error, blunder, fault, flaw, slip, oversight | 反义词：correctness, accuracy


词源：Old English *mis- ‘wrong’ + tác ‘act, deed’


USAGE->

I often make mistakes when I'm tired. = 我累了的时候经常犯错。
He makes a lot of spelling mistakes. = 他犯了很多拼写错误。


一般过去时:
She made a mistake in her answer. = 她的回答错了。
They made a terrible mistake by ignoring the warning. = 他们忽略了警告，犯了一个可怕的错误。


现在完成时:

He has never made such a big mistake before. = 他以前从未犯过这么大的错误。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
