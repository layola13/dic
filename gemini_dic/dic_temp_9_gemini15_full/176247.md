re-classed:/ˌriːˈklæst/| v.|重新分类，重新归类 | To classify or categorize something again, often after a review or change in criteria.  |例句：The students were re-classed after the midterm exams. 期中考试后，学生们被重新分班了。
The library's books were re-classed according to a new Dewey Decimal system. 图书馆的书籍根据新的杜威十进制系统重新分类。|近义词：recategorize, reclassify, re-organize|反义词：classify (initially), group (initially)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-class the students based on their academic performance. 他们根据学生的学习成绩重新分班。
The company re-classes its products annually. 公司每年都会对其产品进行重新分类。


一般过去时:
The teacher re-classed the students after reviewing their progress. 教师在复习了学生的进步后重新分班。
