locusta:/ˈlɒkʌstə/| n. |蝗虫|A locust is a type of grasshopper that often travels in large swarms, causing significant damage to crops.  Locusts are known for their ability to reproduce rapidly and consume vast amounts of vegetation.  They are a significant agricultural pest.|The locust swarm devastated the farmer's fields.|近义词：grasshopper, insect|反义词：无
