overearnest:/ˌoʊvərˈɜːrnɪst/| adj. |过分热心的；过于认真的| excessively earnest or serious; showing too much seriousness or enthusiasm.  It suggests an earnestness that is excessive and possibly inappropriate for the situation. |例句：His overearnest attempts to impress the interviewers backfired. 他试图给面试官留下深刻印象的过分热心的尝试适得其反了。

例句：Her overearnest pronouncements on the subject made everyone uncomfortable. 她关于这个话题过于认真的声明让每个人都感到不舒服。


近义词：excessively serious, overly zealous, too intense,  pretentiously earnest

反义词：indifferent, apathetic, nonchalant, unconcerned


词性：形容词
