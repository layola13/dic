shivers:/ˈʃɪvərz/| n. |颤抖，寒战|a shaking movement of the body produced by cold, fear, or excitement.  Shivers are often accompanied by a feeling of coldness.|例句：I got shivers watching that horror movie.  我看那部恐怖片时浑身发抖。|例句：A shiver ran down her spine.  一阵寒意从她的脊椎滑落。|近义词：trembles, tremors, shakes|反义词：calmness, stillness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shivers in the cold wind. = 他在寒冷的风中颤抖。
The dog shivers when it's scared. = 狗害怕时会发抖。


一般过去时:
She shivered all night long. = 她整夜都在发抖。
The child shivered with fear. = 那孩子吓得直哆嗦。


现在完成时:
I have shivered all day because of the cold. = 我因为寒冷整天都在发抖。
He has shivered so much that his teeth are chattering. = 他抖得牙齿都打颤了。


过去完成时:
She had shivered so much that she had a fever. = 她抖得厉害，以至于发烧了。
They had shivered throughout the long, cold night. = 他们在漫长寒冷的夜晚一直发抖。


将来时:
I think I will shiver if I go outside without a coat. = 我想如果我不穿外套出去的话我会发抖。
We will shiver on the mountain top. = 我们在山顶上会发抖。


虚拟语气:
If I went outside without a coat, I would shiver. = 如果我不穿外套出去，我会发抖。
I wish I hadn't gone outside; I'm shivering now. = 我真希望我没有出去；我现在在发抖。


被动语态:
His body was shivered by the cold. (This usage is less common and sounds unnatural.) = 他的身体被寒冷冻得发抖。(这种用法不太常见，听起来不自然)
The poor dog was shivered into stillness by the rain.  (This usage is less common and sounds unnatural.) = 那可怜的狗被雨淋得瑟瑟发抖，一动不动。(这种用法不太常见，听起来不自然)


疑问句:
Are you shivering? = 你在发抖吗？
Why is he shivering so much? = 他为什么抖得这么厉害？


否定句:
He didn't shiver at all during the scary movie. = 在看那部恐怖电影的时候，他一点也不抖。
She didn't shiver despite the freezing temperature. = 尽管气温很低，她也没有发抖。
