monocellular:/ˌmɒnəʊˈsɛljʊlə(r)/|/ˌmɑːnoʊˈsɛljələr/| adj. |单细胞的；单室的 |Consisting of a single cell; relating to or characteristic of a single cell.  A monocellular organism is an organism that consists of only one cell. |Example:  Amoebas are monocellular organisms.  变形虫是单细胞生物。  The bacteria showed a monocellular structure under the microscope. 在显微镜下，细菌显示出单细胞结构。|近义词：unicellular |反义词：multicellular
