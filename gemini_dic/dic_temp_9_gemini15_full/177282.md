reassimilate:/ˌriːəˈsɪməleɪt/| vt.|重新同化；重新吸收|To absorb or integrate (something) again, especially into a larger whole.|例句：The refugees struggled to reassimilate into their home country after years of exile.  难民们在流亡多年后努力重新融入祖国。|例句：The company tried to reassimilate the newly acquired division's technology into its own systems. 公司试图将其新收购部门的技术重新整合到自身的系统中。|近义词：reintegrate, absorb, incorporate|反义词：segregate, isolate, exclude|动词
