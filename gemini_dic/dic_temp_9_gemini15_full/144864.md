over-processed:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːsɛst/| adj. |过度加工的，加工过度的|Excessively processed; subjected to extensive processing, often to the point of losing nutritional value or desirable qualities.  This term often implies that the processing has negatively impacted the food's quality, taste, or health benefits.|例句：Many processed foods are over-processed, leading to a decline in their nutritional value. 许多加工食品过度加工，导致营养价值下降。

The over-processed snacks lacked flavor and texture.  这些过度加工的零食缺乏风味和口感。|近义词：highly processed, excessively refined, artificially enhanced|反义词：unprocessed, minimally processed, natural
