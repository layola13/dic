dichromatic:/ˌdaɪkrəˈmætɪk/| adj.|二色的，双色的|Having or exhibiting two colors.  The term often refers to vision involving only two types of cone cells in the retina, leading to color blindness.|例句：The dichromatic painting featured vibrant blues and yellows. 这幅双色画以鲜艳的蓝色和黄色为特色。The patient's dichromatic vision made it difficult to distinguish certain shades of green and red. 病人的二色视觉使得区分某些绿色和红色的色调变得困难。|近义词：bicolor, two-toned|反义词：monochromatic, polychromatic
