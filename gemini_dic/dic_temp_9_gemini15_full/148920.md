overwinding: /ˌoʊvərˈwaɪndɪŋ/ | v. | 过度缠绕；过度上弦；过度运转 | To wind (something, especially a spring or clock) too tightly or for too long.  It can also refer to excessive operation or exertion. | 例句：Overwinding the clock mechanism damaged the delicate gears.  过度上弦损坏精密的齿轮。

例句：He was hospitalized due to overwinding himself at work. 他因工作过度劳累而住院。


近义词：overtightening, overstraining, overworking


反义词：underwinding, relaxing


词性：动词
