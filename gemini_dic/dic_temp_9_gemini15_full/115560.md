mara:/ˈmɑːrə/| n.| 马拉（南美洲的一种动物）|A mara is a large rodent native to South America, known for its long legs and powerful hindquarters.  It is a social animal, living in family groups.|例：The mara hopped gracefully across the plains.  马拉优雅地跳过平原。|近义词：cavy (广义上的近义词，指豚鼠科动物)|反义词：无
