nicest:/ˈnaɪsɪst/| adj.|最好的，最令人愉快的|The nicest thing about the job is the people.|这项工作最令人愉悦的是同事们。|近义词：kindest, loveliest, most pleasant|反义词：meanest, nastiest, most unpleasant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is the nicest person I know. = 他是我认识的最友善的人。
The nicest cake is the chocolate one. = 最好吃的蛋糕是巧克力蛋糕。

一般过去时:
That was the nicest gift I ever received. = 那是我收到的最好的礼物。
She was the nicest teacher I ever had. = 她是我曾经遇到的最好的老师。


现在完成时:
That's the nicest thing anyone has ever done for me. = 这是别人为我做过最美好的事。
I've never met anyone nicer than her. = 我从未见过比她更好的人。


过去完成时:
It was the nicest day we had experienced that whole week. = 那周我们度过的最美好的一天。
The nicest thing she had ever done was to help her neighbours. = 她曾经做过最美好的事就是帮助邻居。


将来时:
I hope tomorrow will be the nicest day of the year. = 我希望明天将是今年最美好的一天。
The nicest part of the trip will be the visit to the beach. = 旅行中最美好的部分将是去海滩的游览。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have chosen the nicest option. = 如果我知道的话，我会选择最好的方案。
I wish I had bought the nicest dress. = 我真希望我买了那件最漂亮的裙子。


被动语态:
The nicest cake was eaten first. = 最好吃的蛋糕先被吃掉了。
The nicest room was reserved for the VIP guest. = 最好的房间预留给了贵宾。


疑问句:

What's the nicest thing about your job? = 你工作中最美好的事情是什么？


否定句:

He isn't the nicest person in the world. = 他不是世界上最友善的人。
