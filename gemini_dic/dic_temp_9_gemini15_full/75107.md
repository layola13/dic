fringilline:/ˈfrɪndʒɪlaɪn/| adj.|雀科的；似雀的|Of or relating to the finches (family Fringillidae).  The term often describes birds with characteristics similar to finches, such as their beaks and habits, even if they aren't strictly members of the Fringillidae family.|例句：Many fringilline birds have conical beaks adapted for seed-eating. 许多雀科鸟类都长有适合吃种子的圆锥形喙。|例句：The bird's song and feeding habits suggest a fringilline affinity.  这只鸟的鸣叫和觅食习惯暗示它与雀科鸟类有亲缘关系。|近义词：finch-like|反义词：无
