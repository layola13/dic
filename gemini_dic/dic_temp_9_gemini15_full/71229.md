fits:/fɪts/| v. n.|适合，合身；发作；安装；|To be the right size or shape for someone or something; a sudden attack of an illness;|例句：These shoes fit perfectly. 这双鞋很合脚。  The new sofa fits nicely in the living room. 新沙发很适合放在客厅。|近义词：suits, matches, adapts|反义词：misfits, clashes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dress fits her well. = 这件衣服很合她的身。
This puzzle fits together perfectly. = 这个拼图完美地拼合在一起。

一般过去时:
The shoes didn't fit me. = 这双鞋不合我的脚。
The key fitted perfectly into the lock. = 钥匙完美地插进了锁孔。

现在完成时:
The jacket has fitted perfectly since I bought it. = 自从我买了这件夹克以来，它一直很合身。
The pieces have finally fitted together. = 碎片终于拼合在一起了。

过去完成时:
The costume had fitted him well when he was younger. = 这件戏服在他年轻的时候很合身。
The pieces had all fitted together before he noticed the missing one.  =在他注意到缺少的那块之前，所有的碎片都拼合在一起了。

将来时:
I hope the new dress will fit. = 我希望这件新衣服合身。
The mechanic believes he will fit the new engine in a day. = 机械师相信他能在一天内安装好新发动机。

虚拟语气:
If the shoes fitted, I would buy them. = 如果这双鞋合脚，我就买了。
I wish this puzzle would fit together. = 我希望这个拼图能拼合在一起。

被动语态:
The parts were fitted precisely. = 零件安装得很精确。
The new window will be fitted tomorrow. = 新窗户将在明天安装。

疑问句:
Does this shirt fit me? = 这件衬衫合我身吗？
Does this part fit into the machine? = 这个部件适合安装到机器上吗？

否定句:
The coat doesn't fit him. = 这件外套不合他的身。
The pieces don't fit together. = 这些部件拼合不到一起。
