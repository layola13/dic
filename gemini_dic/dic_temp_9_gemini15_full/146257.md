overextreme:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstriːm/| adj.|极端的，过度的|extremely excessive or intense; beyond the usual or acceptable limits.  This word is not a standard English word and is considered a non-standard or informal combination of "over" and "extreme". It's used to emphasize the extreme nature of something.|例句：The overextreme heat caused the power outage.  极度高温导致了停电。 The overextreme reaction was totally unnecessary. 过激的反应完全没有必要。|近义词：excessive, extreme, ultra, exorbitant|反义词：moderate, mild, average|形容词
