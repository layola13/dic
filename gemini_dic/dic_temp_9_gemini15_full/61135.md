elopement:/ɪˈloʊpmənt/| n. |私奔；潜逃|The act of eloping; running away secretly to get married.  It often implies a secret or hasty marriage, typically against the wishes of parents or other authority figures. |Example: Their elopement was a shock to their families. 他们的私奔令他们的家人震惊。|近义词：flight, runaway, absconding|反义词：formal wedding, arranged marriage|名词
