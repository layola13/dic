reoccurrences:/ˌriːəˈkʌrənsɪz/| n. |复发，再次发生|The plural form of "reoccurrence," referring to multiple instances of something happening again after a pause or interruption.  It implies a repetition of a previous event or condition.|例句：The doctor monitored the patient for any reoccurrences of the infection. 医生监测病人是否有感染复发。

例句：The reoccurrences of the drought caused widespread famine. 干旱的反复发生导致了大范围的饥荒。


近义词：repetitions, recurrences, relapses


反义词：cessations, endings


词性：名词
