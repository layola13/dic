discussions:/dɪˈskʌʃənz/| n. |讨论，商讨 | conversations and debates on a particular topic.  Discussions can range from informal chats to formal meetings. |例句：The discussions were lively and productive.  讨论热烈而富有成效。

例句：We need to have a serious discussion about the future of the company. 我们需要就公司的未来进行一次严肃的讨论。


近义词：talks, debates, deliberations, consultations | 反义词：silence, agreement (in the sense of reaching a consensus without discussion) | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Discussions are happening all around us.  = 讨论正在我们周围发生。
The team holds regular discussions on project progress. = 团队定期就项目进展进行讨论。

一般过去时:
Discussions took place before the final decision. = 在最终决定之前进行了讨论。
They had lengthy discussions about the budget. = 他们就预算进行了冗长的讨论。

现在完成时:
We have had several discussions on this topic. = 我们已经就这个话题进行了多次讨论。
They have completed the discussions for the new policy. = 他们已经完成了关于新政策的讨论。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had already had several discussions. = 会议之前，他们已经进行了多次讨论。
The committee had concluded its discussions before the vote. = 委员会在投票前已经结束了讨论。

将来时:
We will have discussions with the client next week. = 我们下周将与客户进行讨论。
The board will hold discussions on the proposed merger. = 董事会将就拟议中的合并进行讨论。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would have more in-depth discussions. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会进行更深入的讨论。
I wish we had had more time for open discussions. = 我希望我们有更多时间进行公开讨论。

被动语态:
The issues were discussed thoroughly during the meeting. = 会议期间对这些问题进行了彻底的讨论。
The proposal is being discussed by the committee. = 委员会正在讨论这项提案。

疑问句:
What were the main points of discussion? = 讨论的主要内容是什么？
Are there any further discussions planned? = 是否计划进行进一步的讨论？

否定句:
There were no discussions about the controversial topic. = 没有关于有争议的话题的讨论。
They did not engage in any meaningful discussions. = 他们没有进行任何有意义的讨论。


PHRASE->
enter into discussions = 开始讨论
engage in discussions = 参加讨论
have discussions = 进行讨论
hold discussions = 举行讨论
conclude discussions = 结束讨论
