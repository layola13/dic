finite:/ˈfaɪnaɪt/ | adj. | 有限的，有限制的 |  Having limits or bounds; not infinite.  | The Earth's resources are finite. 地球的资源是有限的。| infinite, limited | infinite, boundless | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The universe is finite. = 宇宙是有限的。
This project has a finite budget. = 这个项目有有限的预算。


一般过去时:

将来时:
The supply of oil will be finite in the future. = 未来石油供应将是有限的。

虚拟语气:
If the resources were finite, we would need to conserve them. = 如果资源有限，我们就需要节约它们。
I wish we had known the resources were finite earlier. = 我希望我们早点知道资源是有限的。


被动语态:
