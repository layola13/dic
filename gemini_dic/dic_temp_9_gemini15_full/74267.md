foxtrotter:/ˈfɒkstrɒtə(r)/| n. |狐步舞者；（尤指）熟练的飞行员|A foxtrotter is someone who dances the foxtrot, especially a skilled pilot proficient in handling aircraft.  The term is primarily used in aviation, referencing pilots adept at flying and maneuvering aircraft smoothly and precisely. |Example:  He was a renowned foxtrotter, known for his graceful handling of even the most challenging aircraft.|近义词：skilled pilot, expert aviator|反义词：inexperienced pilot, novice aviator|名词
