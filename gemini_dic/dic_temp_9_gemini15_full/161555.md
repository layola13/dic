potshare:/ˈpɑːtʃɛər/| n.|股份，份额；（尤指）小额股份|A potshare refers to a share, especially a small one, often in a company or investment.  It implies a relatively minor stake compared to larger holdings.  The term often suggests a more informal or less significant investment than the term "share" might imply. |例句：He bought a small potshare in the startup. = 他买下了这家初创公司的一小部分股份。  She inherited a potshare of her grandmother's estate. = 她继承了她祖母遗产中的一小部分股份。|近义词：share, stake, portion|反义词：majority, totality|名词
