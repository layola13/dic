concatenate:/kənˈkætəneɪt/ | vt. |连接，串联|To link things together in a chain or series.  The term often refers to joining strings of text or data. |Example: The programmer concatenated the two strings to create a single, longer string. 程序员将两个字符串连接起来，创建了一个更长的字符串。

Example: We concatenated the different data sets for a more comprehensive analysis. 我们将不同的数据集连接起来，以便进行更全面的分析。


近义词：join, combine, link, unite, append

反义词：separate, disconnect, disjoin, sever
