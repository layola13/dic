sewn:/soʊn/| adj.|缝合的；缝制的|Past participle of sew.  Describes something that has been stitched together with needle and thread.  |例句：The buttons were sewn onto the coat.  纽扣缝在了外套上。
The quilt was beautifully sewn. 这床被子缝得很漂亮。|近义词：stitched, stitched together|反义词：un sewn, unstitched|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tailor sews clothes.  裁缝缝衣服。
She sews every day. 她每天缝纫。


一般过去时:
I sewed a button on my shirt yesterday. 昨天我缝了一个纽扣在我的衬衫上。
They sewed the tear in the fabric. 他们缝补了布料上的裂口。


现在完成时:
She has sewn many quilts in her life. 她一生缝制了许多床被子。
He has sewn all the patches onto the jeans. 他已将所有补丁缝在牛仔裤上了。


过去完成时:
She had sewn the dress before the party started. 派对开始前她就缝好了裙子。
They had sewn the curtains before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，他们已经缝好了窗帘。


将来时:
I will sew a new dress for the wedding. 我将为婚礼缝制一件新裙子。
She will sew the buttons back on tomorrow. 她明天会把纽扣缝回去。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would sew a new cushion cover. 如果我有时间，我会缝一个新的靠垫套。
I wish I had sewn that button on earlier. 我真希望我早点把那个纽扣缝上。


被动语态:
The dress was sewn by a professional seamstress. 这件衣服是由一位专业的裁缝缝制的。
The buttons will be sewn on tomorrow. 纽扣明天会缝上。


疑问句:
Have you sewn this dress yourself? 这件衣服是你自己缝的吗？
Was the quilt sewn by hand? 这床被子是手工缝的吗？


否定句:
I haven't sewn anything today. 我今天什么都没缝。
She didn't sew the button on properly. 她没有把纽扣缝好。


PHRASE->
sewn seams 缝合线
