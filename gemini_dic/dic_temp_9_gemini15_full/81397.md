grassed:/ɡrɑːst/| v.|告发，检举 | To inform the police or other authorities about someone's illegal activities.  |例句：He grassed on his friends to the police. 他向警方告发了朋友。|例句：The informant grassed on the drug dealer. 告密者检举了毒贩。 |近义词：inform on, denounce, betray, squeal |反义词：protect, shield, conceal
