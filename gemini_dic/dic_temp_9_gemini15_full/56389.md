dole:/doʊl/| n. v. |津贴；施舍；分发；忧愁；悲痛 |英文解析：As a noun, it refers to money given regularly by the government to people who are unemployed. As a verb, it means to give something out, often in small amounts. |例句：The government provides unemployment dole. = 政府提供失业救济金。He doled out the candy to the children. = 他把糖果分发给孩子们。|近义词：(n.) allowance, benefit, handout; (v.) distribute, dispense, allot |反义词：(n.) wealth, prosperity; (v.) hoard, withhold |词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government doles out unemployment benefits. = 政府发放失业救济金。
He doles out small portions of food to the needy. = 他少量地把食物分给穷人。


一般过去时:
They doled out food to the refugees. = 他们向难民分发食物。
The company doled out bonuses to its employees. = 公司向员工发放奖金。


现在完成时:
The government has doled out billions of dollars in aid. = 政府已经发放了数十亿美元的援助款项。
She has doled out her wisdom generously throughout her life. = 她一生慷慨地分享她的智慧。


过去完成时:
They had doled out all the supplies before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经分发了所有的物资。
The charity had doled out most of its funds by the end of the year. = 到年底，慈善机构已经分配了大部分资金。


将来时:
The organization will dole out aid to the victims of the earthquake. = 该组织将向地震受害者提供援助。
They will dole out the prizes at the end of the competition. = 他们将在比赛结束后颁发奖品。


虚拟语气:
If the government doled out more money, unemployment would decrease. = 如果政府发放更多资金，失业率将会下降。
I wish they would dole out the information more fairly. = 我希望他们能更公平地分发信息。


被动语态:
The money was doled out fairly to all participants. = 钱公平地分发给了所有参与者。
The food was doled out to the hungry people in the streets. = 食物分发给了街上饥饿的人们。


疑问句:
How is the money doled out? = 钱是怎么分发的？
Will the aid be doled out fairly? = 援助会公平地分配吗？


否定句:
The government did not dole out enough aid. = 政府没有发放足够的援助。
They didn't dole out the work equally among the team members. = 他们没有平均分配团队成员的工作。
