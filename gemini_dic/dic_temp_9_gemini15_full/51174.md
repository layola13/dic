desquamatory:/ˌdɛskwæˈmeɪtəri/| adj. |脱屑的；脱皮的；鳞屑的| Relating to or characterized by desquamation (the shedding of epithelium from a surface).  The term often refers to the peeling or scaling of skin, but can also apply to other epithelial surfaces.| 例句：The patient presented with desquamatory lesions on their hands.  患者手上出现了脱屑性病变。

The medication caused desquamatory effects on the skin. 这种药物导致皮肤脱屑。


近义词：exfoliative, scaling, peeling, shedding| 反义词：intact, whole, uninjured| 词性：形容词
