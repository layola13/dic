psoriasis:/sɔːˈraɪəsɪs/| n.|银屑病，牛皮癣|A chronic autoimmune disease that causes raised, red, scaly patches on the skin.|例句：He suffers from psoriasis, a chronic skin condition. 他患有银屑病，这是一种慢性皮肤病。  例句：The psoriasis on her elbows was particularly bothersome. 她肘部的牛皮癣尤其令人烦恼。|近义词：eczema, dermatitis|反义词：None.
