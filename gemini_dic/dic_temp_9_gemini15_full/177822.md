recensor: /rɪˈsɛnsər/ | n. | 复审者；再审查者 | A person who re-examines or reviews something, especially a piece of writing or a film, to ensure it meets certain standards or to make changes.  | Example: The recensor deemed the manuscript unsuitable for publication due to its offensive language.  复审者认为该手稿因其冒犯性语言而不适合出版。 |近义词:reviewer, editor, censor |反义词:author (in the context of someone creating the content being reviewed)
