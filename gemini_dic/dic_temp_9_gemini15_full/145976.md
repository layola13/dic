overdo:/ˌoʊvərˈduː/ | vt. |做得过分；过度；夸张 | To do something to an excessive degree; to exaggerate. |例句：Don't overdo it at the gym; you'll injure yourself.  别在健身房过度锻炼；你会受伤的。

例句：She overdid the makeup, looking rather artificial. 她化了浓妆，显得有点做作。


近义词：exaggerate, overdo, overact, overemphasize | 反义词：underdo, understate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overdoes his jokes, making them less funny. 他把笑话说得太过火了，反而不好笑了。
They overdo their welcome at parties. 他们在派对上待得太久了，让人不太舒服。


一般过去时:
I overdosed on sleeping pills last night. 我昨晚睡前药吃多了。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

词源:
The word "overdo" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the verb "do."  It's a relatively straightforward formation reflecting the meaning.
