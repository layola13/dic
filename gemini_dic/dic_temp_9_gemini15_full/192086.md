scourer:/ˈskaʊərər/| n. |擦洗器；磨光器；清洁工|A scourer is a tool or substance used for cleaning or scouring surfaces. It can refer to a household cleaning utensil (like a scouring pad), or to a person whose job is to clean.  |例句：She used a metal scourer to clean the burnt pan. 她用金属擦洗器清洗了烧焦的平底锅。


例句：The kitchen scourer had seen better days. 厨房的擦洗器已经破旧不堪了。


近义词：cleaner, scrubber, polisher |反义词：dirtier (as a contrasting concept)|名词
