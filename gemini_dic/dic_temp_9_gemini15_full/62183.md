encomion:/ɛnˈkoʊmiən/| n. |赞美诗；颂词；赞扬|A formal expression of praise, especially a speech or written work; a eulogy.|例句：The encomion delivered at the funeral was both moving and heartfelt. 葬礼上致辞的颂词既感人又发自肺腑。|近义词：eulogy, panegyric, tribute, praise|反义词：criticism, condemnation, censure|
