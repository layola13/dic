misseek:/mɪsˈsiːk/| v.|错误地寻找，误求；追求错误的目标|To search for or seek something incorrectly or unsuccessfully; to pursue the wrong goal or objective. |例句：He misseeked the path and ended up lost in the woods. 他找错了路，结果在树林里迷路了。|例句：She misseeked happiness in material possessions and found only emptiness. 她错误地从物质财富中寻找快乐，结果只找到了空虚。|近义词：misplace, err, mistake, blunder|反义词：find, achieve, succeed
