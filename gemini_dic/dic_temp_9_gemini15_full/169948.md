prostrated:/ˈprɒstreɪtɪd/| adj.| 俯卧的；极度虚弱的；沮丧的 |  lying flat on the ground; completely overcome by weakness or despair. |例句：The earthquake left many people prostrated. 地震使许多人倒地不起。


例句：She was prostrated by grief after her husband's death. 她丈夫死后，她悲痛欲绝。


近义词：exhausted, overwhelmed, dejected, prostrate, supine


反义词：energetic, strong, cheerful, upright


词性：形容词

虚拟语气:

词源:
From Middle English *prostrat, from Old French prosterner, from Latin prosternere, from pro- "forward" + sternere "to spread, strew".  The sense evolution is from "to throw down" to "to lay prostrate" to the figurative sense of being overwhelmed.
