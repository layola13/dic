evictors:/ɪˈvɪktərz/| n.|驱逐者；逐出者|People or entities who evict others, typically from property.|The evictors arrived early in the morning, leaving the family with nowhere to go. 驱逐者一大早就来了，让这家人无处可去。|landlords, dispossessors|tenants, protectors|名词

一般现在时:
The landlord evicts tenants who don't pay rent. = 房东驱逐不付房租的租户。
Evictors often use legal loopholes to remove people from their homes. = 驱逐者经常利用法律漏洞将人们赶出家门。


一般过去时:
The court ordered the evictors to leave the property. = 法院命令驱逐者离开该房产。
They were evicted last week by the new owners.  = 他们上周被新房主赶走了。


现在完成时:
The city has evicted hundreds of families this year. = 今年市政府已经驱逐了数百个家庭。
Several tenants have been evicted for violating building codes. = 几个租户因为违反建筑法规而被驱逐。


过去完成时:
The family had been evicted twice before they found stable housing. = 在找到稳定的住房之前，这个家庭已经被驱逐过两次了。
Before the new law passed, many landlords had already evicted their tenants. = 新法律通过之前，许多房东已经驱逐了他们的租户。


将来时:
They will evict him if he doesn't pay the rent. = 如果他不付房租，他们就会驱逐他。
The city council will likely evict the squatters next month. = 市议会很可能会在下个月驱逐那些非法居住者。


虚拟语气:
If I were the landlord, I wouldn't evict them so quickly. = 如果我是房东，我不会这么快就驱逐他们。
I wish the evictors had shown more compassion. = 我希望驱逐者能表现出更多的同情心。


被动语态:
The tenants were evicted without warning. = 租户们在没有警告的情况下被驱逐了。
Many families are being evicted due to rising housing costs. = 由于住房成本上涨，许多家庭正在被驱逐。


疑问句:
Who are the evictors in this case? = 此案中的驱逐者是谁？

否定句:
The landlord did not evict the tenants despite their late payments. = 尽管租户们拖欠了房租，房东也没有驱逐他们。
The court refused to allow the evictors to proceed with the eviction. = 法院拒绝允许驱逐者继续进行驱逐。
