entirety:/ɪnˈtaɪərəti/ | n. | 全部；整体；完全 | The entirety of the project was a success.  |  近义词： totality, whole, completeness | 反义词： part, fraction


英文解析:The entirety of something refers to its complete amount or extent, including every part. It emphasizes the wholeness and comprehensiveness of something.


例句:

He explained the entirety of the situation. 他解释了事情的全部情况。

The entirety of his work is impressive. 他的全部作品令人印象深刻。


词源:

From Old French *entiereté, from entier "entire, whole," from Latin integer "untouched, whole, complete," from in- "not" + tangere "to touch."
