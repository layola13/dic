deliverer:/dɪˈlɪvərər/| n. |递送者；接生婆；解救者 |A deliverer is a person or thing that delivers something. This could be a person who delivers packages, a messenger, a childbirth attendant (midwife), or even a force that delivers someone from danger or hardship.  |Example: The pizza deliverer arrived just in time for the party.  披萨送货员正好赶上派对。The midwife acted as a skilled deliverer, ensuring a safe birth. 接生婆作为一名熟练的接生员，确保了分娩的安全。|近义词：courier, messenger, savior, rescuer |反义词：receiver (in some contexts) |名词
