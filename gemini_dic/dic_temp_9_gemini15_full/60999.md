eliminate:/ɪˈlɪmɪneɪt/ | vt. | 消除，排除，淘汰 | to remove or get rid of something completely |  We need to eliminate waste from the production process. 我们需要消除生产过程中的浪费。  The team was eliminated from the competition in the first round. 该队在第一轮就被淘汰出局了。 | remove, exclude, eradicate | retain, keep, include | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They eliminate errors in the software. = 他们消除软件中的错误。
The company eliminates inefficient workers. = 公司淘汰效率低的员工。


一般过去时:
The doctor eliminated the possibility of a heart attack. = 医生排除了心脏病发作的可能性。
They eliminated the problem by replacing the faulty part. = 他们通过更换故障部件解决了问题。


现在完成时:
We have eliminated most of the bugs in the system. = 我们已经消除了系统中的大部分错误。
She has eliminated all her competitors. = 她已经淘汰了所有竞争对手。


过去完成时:
They had eliminated the threat before it escalated. = 他们在威胁升级之前就将其消除了。
He had eliminated all other possibilities before reaching a conclusion. = 他在得出结论之前排除了所有其他可能性。


将来时:
We will eliminate the use of single-use plastics. = 我们将消除一次性塑料的使用。
The new software will eliminate the need for manual data entry. = 新软件将消除手动数据录入的需要。


虚拟语气:
If we eliminated the tariffs, trade would increase. = 如果我们取消关税，贸易额就会增加。
I wish we could eliminate poverty completely. = 我希望我们能够彻底消除贫困。


被动语态:
The problem was eliminated by a simple software update. = 该问题通过简单的软件更新得以解决。
All traces of the virus were eliminated by the new medication. = 新药消除了病毒的所有痕迹。


疑问句:
How can we eliminate this obstacle? = 我们如何消除这个障碍？
Can we eliminate the risk of failure completely? = 我们能完全消除失败的风险吗？


否定句:
We cannot eliminate all risks. = 我们无法消除所有风险。
They failed to eliminate the source of the problem. = 他们未能消除问题的根源。


PHRASE->

eliminate from: 从...中消除
eliminate the possibility of: 排除...的可能性
eliminate competition: 消除竞争
eliminate the need for: 消除对...的需求
eliminate risk: 消除风险
