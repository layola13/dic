reactions:/riˈækʃənz/| n.|反应，回应|The reactions to the new policy were mixed.  对这项新政策的反应褒贬不一。|近义词：responses, responses, effects|反义词：actions, causes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His reactions are usually calm. = 他的反应通常很平静。
How do you predict the market's reactions? = 你如何预测市场的反应？


一般过去时:
Her reactions to the news were surprising. = 她对这个消息的反应令人惊讶。
What were the public's reactions to the announcement? = 公众对这项宣布的反应是什么？


现在完成时:
The team has analyzed the patients' reactions to the drug. = 团队已经分析了病人对药物的反应。
What reactions have you observed so far? = 到目前为止你观察到了哪些反应？


过去完成时:
By then, he had already anticipated her reactions. = 到那时，他已经预料到了她的反应。
They had observed similar reactions in previous experiments. = 他们在之前的实验中观察到过类似的反应。


将来时:
We will monitor the patients' reactions closely. = 我们将密切监测病人的反应。
How do you think the audience will react? = 你认为观众会如何反应？


虚拟语气:
If he had known her reactions, he would have acted differently. = 如果他知道她的反应，他会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had predicted his reactions. = 我希望我预测到了他的反应。


被动语态:
The drug's side effects are being carefully studied to understand the patients' reactions. = 正在仔细研究这种药物的副作用，以了解病人的反应。
The reactions to the proposal were carefully evaluated by the committee. = 委员会仔细评估了对该提案的反应。


疑问句:
What are your reactions to this proposal? = 你对这个提案有什么反应？
What were the immediate reactions of the witnesses? = 目击证人的即时反应是什么？


否定句:
There were no immediate reactions to the announcement. = 宣布后没有立即的反应。
He showed no visible reactions to the criticism. = 他对批评没有表现出任何明显的反应。
