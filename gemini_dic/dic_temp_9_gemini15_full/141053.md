only-so-much:/ˈoʊnliˌsoʊmʌtʃ/| adj. phrase |仅此而已；有限的；只能这样|This phrase indicates a limit or restriction on something. It implies that there's a boundary to the amount, capability, or possibility.  It suggests that while something is possible or available, it's not unlimited. |例句：There's only so much we can do with the limited resources. = 我们所能做的，仅仅限于有限的资源。

There's only so much time we have to complete this project. = 我们完成这个项目的时间非常有限。


近义词：limited, restricted, finite, constrained

反义词：unlimited, boundless, infinite, abundant

词性：形容词短语
