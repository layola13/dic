declawing: /dɪˈklɔːɪŋ/ | v. | 去爪，切除猫爪 | The surgical removal of a cat's claws.  This procedure is controversial due to its potential for causing pain and affecting the cat's ability to walk and defend itself. |  Many vets now advise against declawing cats.  许多兽医现在建议不要给猫去爪。

The practice of declawing is illegal in many countries.  在许多国家，去爪的行为是非法的。


近义词：onychectomy (医学术语)
反义词：leaving claws intact (保留爪子)

词源:
The word "declawing" is a combination of "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "off") and "clawing" (referring to the action of a cat using its claws).  The "claw" part itself comes from Old English "clawu", related to words like "clench" and "clutch".  因此，"declawing" 的字面意思是“去除爪子”。
