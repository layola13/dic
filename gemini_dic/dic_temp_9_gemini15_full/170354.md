proving:/ˈpruːvɪŋ/| v. |证明；证实|The act of demonstrating the truth or validity of something.  It involves providing evidence or arguments to support a claim or assertion. |例句：The evidence is proving his innocence.  证据证明他是无辜的。

例句：The experiment is proving to be more difficult than we anticipated.  实验证明比我们预期的要困难。


近义词：demonstrating, establishing, verifying, substantiating| 反义词：disproving, refuting, contradicting| 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is proving his point with facts. = 他正在用事实证明他的观点。
She is proving herself to be a valuable asset to the team. = 她正在证明自己是团队中宝贵的财富。


一般过去时:
They proved their theory correct. = 他们证明了他们的理论是正确的。
The lawyer proved the defendant's alibi. = 律师证明了被告的不在场证明。


现在完成时:
We have proved our commitment to sustainability. = 我们已经证明了我们对可持续发展的承诺。
She has proved her resilience in the face of adversity. = 她已经证明了她在逆境中展现出的韧性。


过去完成时:
They had proved their hypothesis before publishing the results. = 他们在发表结果之前已经证明了他们的假设。
The scientist had proved the existence of the new element. = 科学家已经证明了新元素的存在。


将来时:
The upcoming trial will prove his guilt or innocence. = 即将到来的审判将证明他的罪与非罪。
Further research will prove the effectiveness of the new drug. = 进一步的研究将证明这种新药的有效性。


虚拟语气:
If he proved his innocence, he would be released. = 如果他证明了自己的清白，他就会被释放。
I wish I could prove my theory. = 我希望我能证明我的理论。


被动语态:
His innocence was proved by the evidence. = 他的清白由证据证明。
The theorem was proved by a famous mathematician. = 这个定理由一位著名的数学家证明。


疑问句:
How can we prove his claim? = 我们如何证明他的说法？
Will the results prove the hypothesis? = 结果会证明假设吗？


否定句:
He couldn't prove his allegations. = 他无法证明他的指控。
The study failed to prove the connection between the two variables. = 这项研究未能证明这两个变量之间的联系。


PHRASE->
