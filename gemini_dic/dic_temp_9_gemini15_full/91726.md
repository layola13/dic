hyperdeifying: /ˌhaɪpərˈdiːɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. |过度神化；过分尊崇 |  The act of excessively glorifying or elevating someone or something to a divine or superhuman status.  It implies an exaggeration beyond reasonable praise or reverence. |例句：The media's hyperdeifying of the athlete led to unrealistic expectations and ultimately, disappointment.  媒体对这位运动员的过度神化导致了不切实际的期望，最终导致失望。


例句：The cult's hyperdeifying of their leader bordered on religious fanaticism. 该邪教对他们领导人的过度神化近乎宗教狂热。


近义词：deifying, idolizing, apotheosizing, aggrandizing, glorifying


反义词：denigrating, belittling, criticizing, disparaging, vilifying


词性：动词
