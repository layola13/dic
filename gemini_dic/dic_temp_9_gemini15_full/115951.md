marriages:/ˈmærɪdʒɪz/| n. |婚姻；结婚典礼；婚姻状况 |The state of being married; a marriage ceremony; the condition of being married.  |例句：Their marriages lasted for over fifty years. 他们的婚姻持续了50多年。

近义词：unions, wedlocks, matrimony |反义词：divorces, separations
