/ˈoʊvərˌspaɪs/ | v. | 加过多的香料；过度使用香料 | To add too much spice to something, resulting in an overwhelming or unpleasant flavor.  |  The chef overspiced the curry, making it too hot to eat.  厨师在咖喱里加了过多的香料，辣得让人吃不了。 |  season, flavor | underseason, underflavor | 动词

She overspices the soup, making it too salty. = 她放了太多香料到汤里，导致汤太咸了。
