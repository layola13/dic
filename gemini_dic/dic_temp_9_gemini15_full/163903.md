predetect:/priːdɪˈtɛkt/| vt.|预先探测；预先发现|To detect something before it happens or becomes apparent.|The system can predetect potential problems before they impact users.  该系统可以在问题影响用户之前预先探测到潜在的问题。|近义词：anticipate, foresee, forewarn|反义词：overlook, miss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sensor predetects changes in temperature. = 传感器预先探测到温度变化。
We predetect potential risks in our investment strategy. = 我们在投资策略中预先探测潜在风险。

一般过去时:
The software predetected a virus before it could spread. = 软件在病毒传播之前就预先探测到了它。
Researchers predetected the earthquake's epicenter using seismic data. = 研究人员利用地震数据预先探测到了地震震中。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
