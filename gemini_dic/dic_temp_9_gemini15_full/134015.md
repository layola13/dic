/nɒnˈhaʊsˌhoʊldər/ | n. | 非户主，非住户 | A person who does not own or rent a house or other dwelling; someone who is not a householder. This term often appears in legal and census contexts, referring to individuals who reside in a household but do not hold the primary tenancy or ownership. | Example:  The survey excluded non-householders, focusing solely on the primary residents of each dwelling.  这项调查排除了非户主，只关注每个住宅的主要居民。 | 近义词: non-resident, lodger (in some contexts) | 反义词: householder, homeowner, tenant (in some contexts)
