face-offs:/ˈfeɪsɒfs/ | n. |面对面交锋；对抗；决斗 |A face-off is a direct confrontation or contest, often involving a physical or verbal challenge.  It can refer to a specific event in sports, like hockey, or a more general confrontation. |例句：The two teams had a fierce face-off at the beginning of the game.  两队在比赛开始时进行了激烈的对决。

The political rivals engaged in a series of televised face-offs.  两位政治对手进行了一系列电视辩论。|近义词：confrontation, clash, showdown, duel |反义词：agreement, cooperation, harmony
