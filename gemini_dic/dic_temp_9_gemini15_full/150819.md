parafoveal:/ˌpærəˈfoʊviəl/| adj.|  旁侧视的，周边视觉的 | Relating to the area of the visual field surrounding the fovea centralis (the central area of the retina responsible for sharp, detailed vision).  It refers to vision that occurs slightly outside the direct line of sight. | Example: Parafoveal vision allows us to quickly scan a page of text and identify key words before fixating on them.  周边视觉让我们能够快速扫描一页文本并在注视它们之前识别关键词。 | 近义词：peripheral (in the context of vision) | 反义词：foveal
