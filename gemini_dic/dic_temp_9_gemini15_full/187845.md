roset:/ˈroʊzɛt/| n. |莲座，玫瑰花结|A rosette is a decorative motif shaped like a rose, often used in architecture, design, and jewelry.  It can also refer to a cluster of flowers or leaves arranged in a circular pattern. |例句：The building's facade was adorned with intricate stone rosettes. 这座建筑物的正面装饰着复杂的石头莲座。

The cake was decorated with a delicate sugar rosette. 蛋糕上装饰着一个精致的糖玫瑰花结。|近义词：floral design, ornament, embellishment |反义词：plainness, simplicity
