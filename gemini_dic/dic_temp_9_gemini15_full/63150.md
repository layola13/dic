ensue:/ɪnˈsuː/ | v. | 接着发生；继而发生；结果是 | To happen or occur afterward or as a result.  |  Chaos ensued after the announcement. 混乱在公告之后发生了。|  follow, result, succeed | precede, anticipate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What ensues after the storm? = 暴风雨过后会发生什么？
Does a good harvest always ensue from hard work? = 辛勤劳动总是带来好收成吗？

一般过去时:
A fierce argument ensued. = 随后发生了一场激烈的争论。
What ensued after the accident? = 事故发生后发生了什么？

现在完成时:
What has ensued from their decision? = 他们的决定带来了什么后果？
Significant changes have ensued since the new policy was implemented. = 自从实施新政策以来，发生了重大变化。

过去完成时:
After the king died, a power struggle ensued. = 国王死后，一场权力斗争随之而来。
A long period of peace had ensued before the war began. = 在战争爆发之前，曾有一段漫长的和平时期。

将来时:
What will ensue if we fail to act? = 如果我们不采取行动，将会发生什么？
We will see what will ensue from this experiment. = 我们将看看这个实验会带来什么结果。


虚拟语气:
If we were to ignore the problem, dire consequences would ensue. = 如果我们忽视这个问题，就会造成可怕的后果。
It is imperative that no further violence ensue. = 绝对不能再发生暴力事件。


被动语态:
The ensuing chaos was hard to control. = 接踵而来的混乱难以控制。
A great deal of confusion was ensued by the miscommunication. = 误解导致了极大的混乱。


疑问句:
What will ensue if we don't pay our taxes? = 如果我们不纳税，将会发生什么？
What ensued from the meeting? = 会议的结果是什么？


否定句:
Nothing ensued from their efforts. = 他们的努力没有产生任何结果。
No significant changes ensued after the meeting. = 会议之后没有发生什么重大变化。


PHRASE->
ensue from = 源于
ensue upon = 紧随其后
ensue in = 在...中发生
ensue with = 伴随...发生
ensuing events = 后续事件


词源:
The word "ensue" comes from the Old French word "ensuivre," which means "to follow."  It's ultimately derived from the Latin "in" (in, on) + "sequi" (to follow).
