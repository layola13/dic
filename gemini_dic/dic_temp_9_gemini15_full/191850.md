sclerosal:/sklɪəˈroʊsəl/| adj.|巩膜的；硬化的|Relating to or affecting the sclera (the white of the eye).  The term can also refer to hardening or induration of tissue.|例句：The patient presented with sclerosal thickening in the affected eye.  患者患眼出现巩膜增厚。

例句：Sclerosal changes are often associated with age-related macular degeneration. 巩膜改变常与年龄相关性黄斑变性有关。


近义词：scleral (more common)


反义词：None directly opposite, but terms suggesting the opposite of hardening (e.g., softened, flexible) could be used in contrast.


词性:形容词
