jefferson:/ˈdʒɛfərsən/ | n. |杰弗逊；杰弗逊式风格 |  A surname of English origin, often associated with Thomas Jefferson, the third President of the United States.  The name itself is derived from the place name "Jeferson," and its meaning is uncertain, possibly related to "son of Geoffrey" or "son of Jeffrey." It can also refer to a style of furniture or architecture associated with Thomas Jefferson's neoclassical tastes.  | Example:  The Jefferson Memorial is a beautiful monument. 杰弗逊纪念堂是一座美丽的纪念碑。 |近义词: (as a surname)  None directly comparable |反义词: (as a surname) N/A
