carve:/kɑːrv/| /kɑːrv/ | vt. |雕刻；切；刻；(艰难地)开辟；| to cut into or shape something with a knife or other sharp instrument.  |例句：He carved a bird from a piece of wood. 他用一块木头雕刻了一只鸟。
例句：The wind carved deep furrows in the sand. 风在沙子上刻出了深深的沟壑。|近义词：sculpt, engrave, etch |反义词：build, construct

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He carves beautiful sculptures. = 他雕刻美丽的雕塑。
They carve the turkey for Thanksgiving. = 他们为感恩节雕刻火鸡。

一般过去时:
She carved her initials into the tree. = 她把她的姓名首字母刻在树上。

词源:
The word "carve" comes from the Old English word *ceorfan*, meaning "to cut" or "to shape."  This word has Germanic roots, related to words in other Germanic languages with similar meanings.
