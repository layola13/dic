monorail:/ˈmɒnəreɪl/| n. |单轨铁路 |A monorail is a railway system that uses a single rail, typically elevated above the ground.  It is often used in urban areas as a form of public transportation. |例句：The city is considering building a monorail to alleviate traffic congestion.  这个城市正在考虑建造一条单轨铁路来缓解交通拥堵。

例句：Disneyland utilizes a monorail system to transport visitors between different areas of the park. 迪斯尼乐园利用单轨铁路系统来运送游客往返于公园的不同区域。


近义词：single-rail railway, elevated railway |反义词：multi-rail railway
