obeying:/oʊˈbeɪɪŋ/| v. |服从，遵守|The act of complying with or fulfilling a command, order, or request.  It implies submission to authority or rules. |例句：The children were obeying their mother's instructions. 孩子们正在服从母亲的指示。
例句：Obeying traffic laws is crucial for safety. 遵守交通规则对安全至关重要。|近义词：complying, conforming, following|反义词：disobeying, defying, resisting|动词
