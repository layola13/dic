overplowing:/ˌoʊvərˈplaʊɪŋ/| v.|过度耕作|The act of plowing land too frequently or too deeply, leading to soil degradation.|例句：Overplowing the fields led to soil erosion and reduced crop yields.  过度耕作田地导致土壤侵蚀和减产。|近义词：overcultivation, excessive plowing|反义词：conservation tillage, no-till farming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Farmers overplow their fields every year. = 农民每年都过度耕作他们的田地。
They overplow the land to maximize yield. = 他们过度耕作土地以最大限度地提高产量。

一般过去时:
The farmers overplowed their fields last season. = 农民在上个季度过度耕作了他们的田地。
The drought was worsened by the overplowing of the previous year. = 前一年过度耕作加剧了旱情。

现在完成时:
Intensive farming has overplowed vast areas of land. = 集约化农业已经过度耕作了大片土地。
They have overplowed the land for decades. = 他们几十年来一直在过度耕作土地。

过去完成时:
The soil was already degraded because they had overplowed it for years. = 由于他们多年来过度耕作，土壤已经退化。
The erosion was severe because they had overplowed the land before the heavy rains. = 因为他们在暴雨前过度耕作了土地，所以侵蚀非常严重。

将来时:
If they continue this practice, they will overplow the land again next year. = 如果他们继续这种做法，明年他们将再次过度耕作土地。
We predict that they will overplow the fields unless they adopt conservation techniques. = 我们预测，除非他们采用保护性耕作技术，否则他们将会过度耕作田地。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't overplowed the land, the soil would be healthier. = 如果他们没有过度耕作土地，土壤就会更健康。
It would be better if they didn't overplow their fields every year. = 如果他们每年不过度耕作田地就好了。
