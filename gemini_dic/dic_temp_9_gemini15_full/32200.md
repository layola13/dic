chicagoan:/ˌʃɪˈkɑːɡoʊən/| n. |芝加哥人|A person who lives in or comes from Chicago.  This word is formed by adding the suffix "-an" to the name of the city, Chicago, to indicate someone who is from that place.|She's a Chicagoan, and she's very proud of her city's history. 她是个芝加哥人，她为她城市的悠久历史感到非常自豪。| resident of Chicago, Chicago native |  non-Chicagoan
