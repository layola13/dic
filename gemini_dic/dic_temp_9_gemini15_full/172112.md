purposely:/ˈpɜːrpəsli/ | adv. |故意地，有意地 | done in a way that is intended or planned | He purposely ignored her calls. 他故意不接她的电话。| intentionally, deliberately, designedly | accidentally, unintentionally, casually | 副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She purposely avoids him. = 她故意躲着他。
They purposely choose to live in the countryside. = 他们故意选择住在乡下。

一般过去时:
He purposely broke the vase. = 他故意打碎了花瓶。
She purposely misled the investigators. = 她故意误导了调查人员。


现在完成时:
I have purposely kept this secret for years. = 我故意保守这个秘密多年了。
They have purposely avoided contact with the media. = 他们故意避免与媒体接触。


过去完成时:
He had purposely hidden the evidence. = 他事先故意藏起了证据。
She had purposely planned this meeting. = 她事先故意策划了这次会议。


将来时:
I will purposely arrive late. = 我会故意迟到。
They will purposely ignore his comments. = 他们会故意忽略他的评论。


虚拟语气:
If I wanted to hurt him, I would purposely say something mean. = 如果我想伤害他，我会故意说些刻薄的话。
I wish I hadn't purposely missed the meeting. = 我真希望我没有故意错过那次会议。


被动语态:
The damage was purposely inflicted. = 损害是故意造成的。
The meeting was purposely scheduled at an inconvenient time. = 会议故意安排在一个不方便的时间。


疑问句:
Did you purposely do that? = 你是故意那样做的吗？
Was it purposely done or accidental? = 那是故意的还是意外的？


否定句:
I didn't purposely hurt his feelings. = 我并非故意伤害他的感情。
She didn't purposely exclude him from the invitation list. = 她并非故意将他排除在邀请名单之外。
