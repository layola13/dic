ripply:/ˈrɪpli/| adj.|波纹状的，起波浪的|Having a slightly uneven surface with small waves or ripples.  It describes a surface that is not perfectly smooth but has small, wave-like undulations.|例句：The water in the pond was ripply and calm. 池塘里的水波光粼粼，平静如镜。|例句：The silk scarf had a ripply texture. 这条丝巾的质地呈波纹状。|近义词：undulating, wavy, ruffled|反义词：smooth, flat
