/ˈpoʊstʃoʊ/| n. |演后活动，演出后派对|A postshow is an event that takes place after a performance, often involving the cast, crew, and audience.  It might include a meet-and-greet, a party, or a Q&A session. |Example: The cast enjoyed a lively postshow party after the final curtain.|近义词：after-show, post-performance event|反义词：pre-show, rehearsal
