cuddly:/ˈkʌdli/ | adj. | 舒适的；令人想拥抱的；可爱的 |  Descriptive of something soft, huggable, and appealing to the senses of touch and sight, often associated with feelings of warmth, comfort, and affection.  It suggests a desire to hold or cuddle the object or person described. | Example: The kitten was so cuddly; I couldn't resist holding it.  那只小猫太可爱了，我忍不住抱住了它。 The fluffy blanket felt so cuddly on a cold night.  在一个寒冷的夜晚，毛茸茸的毯子感觉非常舒适。 | 近义词：huggable, snuggly, cozy, soft, lovable | 反义词：uncomfortable, prickly, repulsive, harsh
