pinnulate:/ˈpɪnjuːleɪt/ | adj. | 羽状的；羽裂的 | Having small, wing-like projections or lobes along an edge or margin, resembling a feather.  | Example: The pinnulate leaves of the fern unfurled in the spring rain.  蕨类的羽状叶在春雨中舒展开来。| 近义词：pinnate, feathery | 反义词：entire, unlobed
