kamikaze:/ˈkɑːmɪˌkɑːziː/| n.|神风敢死队；自杀性攻击；|a Japanese pilot in World War II who undertook a suicide mission by crashing their bomb-laden airplane into an enemy target.| 例句：The kamikaze pilots were driven by a fierce sense of loyalty and patriotism.  神风敢死队队员们受到强烈的忠诚和爱国主义精神的驱使。

The kamikaze attacks caused significant damage to the Allied fleet. 神风敢死队的攻击对盟军舰队造成了重大损失。|近义词：suicide attack, suicide bomber, self-sacrifice|反义词：peaceful resolution, surrender, negotiation|名词
