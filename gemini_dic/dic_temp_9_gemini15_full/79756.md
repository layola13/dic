glut:/ɡlʌt/ | n. v. |供应过剩；使吃饱；充满 | An excessive supply; to fill to excess; to satisfy fully |例句：The market is glutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价进口商品。  The festival glutted the town with tourists. 节日使这个城镇挤满了游客。|近义词：surplus, excess, oversupply |反义词：scarcity, shortage |词性:名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The market gluts itself with cheap products. = 市场充斥着廉价产品。
This restaurant gluts its customers with food. = 这家餐馆让顾客吃得饱饱的。

一般过去时:
The news glutted the television channels. = 新闻充斥着电视节目。
He glutted himself on cake. = 他吃掉了大量的蛋糕。


现在完成时:
The government has glutted the market with subsidized goods. = 政府用补贴商品充斥了市场。
We have glutted ourselves on information this week. = 我们这周信息过载了。


过去完成时:
They had glutted the warehouse with unsold inventory. = 他们已经用滞销库存塞满了仓库。
She had glutted herself with sweets before dinner. = 晚餐前她已经吃很多糖果了。


将来时:
The new factory will glut the market with cheap electronics. = 新工厂将用廉价电子产品充斥市场。
They will glut themselves on the buffet. = 他们将在自助餐上吃个痛快。


虚拟语气:
If the company glutted the market, they would lose money. = 如果公司产品供应过剩，他们将会亏损。
I wish they hadn't glutted the market with their product. = 我希望他们没有用他们的产品充斥市场。


被动语态:
The market was glutted with cheap imports. = 市场充斥着廉价进口商品。
The consumers were glutted with advertisements. = 消费者被广告轰炸了。


疑问句:
Is the market glutted with this product? = 这个产品市场供应过剩了吗？
Was the concert glutted with people? = 音乐会人满为患吗？


否定句:
The market is not glutted with high-quality products. = 市场并不充斥着高质量的产品。
He didn't glut himself on the food. = 他并没有吃很多食物。


PHRASE->
a glut of something:大量的…
glut the market: 充斥市场
a glut in the market: 市场供应过剩
glut with: 用…填满
be glutted with: 充满…
