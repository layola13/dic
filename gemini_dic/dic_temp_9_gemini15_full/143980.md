outrail:/ˈaʊtreɪl/| n.|辅助轨道，支线|A secondary or auxiliary rail, typically used for switching or shunting railway cars.|例句：The outrail provided a convenient way to move the freight cars to the loading dock. 辅助轨道提供了一种方便的方法将货车移至装货码头。
例句：The damaged outrail needed immediate repair to ensure safe train operations. 受损的辅助轨道需要立即维修以确保火车安全运行。|近义词：spur, siding, branch line|反义词：main line
