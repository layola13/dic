framing:/ˈfreɪmɪŋ/| n. v.| 框架；构图；陷害 | To create a frame or structure for something; to present something in a particular way; to falsely accuse someone of a crime. |例句：The framing of the painting was exquisite.  这幅画的画框精美绝伦。


The journalist's framing of the story was biased. 这名记者对故事的叙述是有偏见的。|近义词：structuring, presentation, setup, entrapment |反义词：destruction, demolition, release


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He frames his arguments logically. 他以逻辑的方式构建他的论点。
The artist frames her pictures beautifully.  这位艺术家漂亮地为她的画作装裱。


一般过去时:
They framed the suspect for the robbery. 他们陷害嫌疑犯犯了抢劫罪。
She framed the photo in a simple wooden frame. 她用简单的木质相框装裱照片。


现在完成时:
The prosecution has framed its case effectively.  检方已有效地构建了其案件。
They have framed the mirror perfectly in the hallway. 他们在走廊里完美地装裱了镜子。


过去完成时:
The police had framed him before the trial.  警方在审判前就陷害了他。
She had framed the artwork before the exhibition.  她在展览前已经装裱好了艺术品。


将来时:
They will frame the new artwork tomorrow. 他们明天将装裱新的艺术品。
He will frame his argument with evidence. 他将用证据来构建他的论点。


虚拟语气:
If I framed the picture differently, it would look better. 如果我以不同的方式装裱这幅画，它看起来会更好。
If they had framed him differently, the outcome might have been different. 如果他们以不同的方式陷害他，结果可能会有所不同。


被动语态:
The painting was framed beautifully. 这幅画被精美地装裱。
The suspect was framed by the police. 嫌疑人被警方陷害。


疑问句:
How did you frame the photo? 你是怎么装裱照片的？
Was he framed for the crime? 他是被陷害犯下这罪行的吗？


否定句:
He didn't frame the picture correctly. 他没有正确地装裱这幅画。
The police did not frame the innocent man. 警方没有陷害无辜的人。
