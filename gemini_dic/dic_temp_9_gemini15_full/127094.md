nationalised: /ˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/ | v. | 国有化 | To bring under state control; to make something (e.g., an industry) a national enterprise. | The government nationalised the railways in 1948.  政府在1948年将铁路国有化。|  socialise, collectivise | privatised

The government nationalises key industries. = 政府将关键产业国有化。
They nationalise the bank this year. = 他们今年将银行国有化。

一般过去时:
