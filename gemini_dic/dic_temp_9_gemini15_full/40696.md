conversion:/kənˈvɜːrʒən/| n. v.|转变；转换；兑换；改宗|The process of changing from one form, state, or system to another.  It can refer to a change in form, function, or belief. |例句：The conversion of the old factory into a modern apartment complex was a huge undertaking. 老工厂改造成现代公寓大楼是一项巨大的工程。

例句：The conversion of British pounds to US dollars involved a small fee. 英镑兑换美元需要支付少量手续费。|近义词：transformation, change, alteration, transposition|反义词：preservation, maintenance, stagnation|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He converts his savings into stocks every month. 他每月都将储蓄转换为股票。
The company is converting to a new accounting system. 这家公司正在转换到一个新的会计系统。

一般过去时:
She converted her garage into a home office. 她把车库改造成家庭办公室。
They converted to Christianity in the 17th century. 他们在17世纪改信基督教。

现在完成时:
He has converted all his assets into cash. 他已将所有资产兑换成现金。
The old building has been converted into a museum. 这座旧建筑已被改造成博物馆。

过去完成时:
They had converted the barn into living quarters before they moved in. 他们在搬进去之前已经把谷仓改造成居住区了。
The company had already converted to the new software before the deadline. 公司在截止日期之前就已经转换到新的软件了。

将来时:
She will convert her old car into a race car. 她将把她的旧车改装成赛车。
We will convert the data into a more usable format. 我们将把数据转换成更易用的格式。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would convert it into property. 如果我有更多钱，我会把它转换成房产。
I wish I could convert my passion into a profitable business. 我希望我能把我的热情转化成一个盈利的生意。


被动语态:
The old church was converted into a library.  那座旧教堂被改造成图书馆。
The data is being converted into a graph. 数据正在转换成图表。

疑问句:
How do you convert Celsius to Fahrenheit? 你如何将摄氏度转换为华氏度？
Will the building be converted into apartments? 这栋建筑物会被改造成公寓吗？


否定句:
He didn't convert his savings into real estate. 他没有把储蓄转换成房地产。
The company hasn't yet converted to the new system. 公司还没有转换到新系统。
