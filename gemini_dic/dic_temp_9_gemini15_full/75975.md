funneled:/ˈfʌnld/ | v. | 漏斗状地倒入；引导；集中 | To pour or channel something into a narrow opening, like a funnel.  It can also refer to guiding or directing something towards a specific point or result. | 例句：The workers funneled the grain into the silos. 工人们把谷物漏斗般地倒入筒仓。The new policy funneled more resources into education. 新政策将更多资源投入教育。| 近义词：channel, guide, direct, concentrate | 反义词：disperse, scatter, spread
