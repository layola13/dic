phenomenologies:/ˌfɪnɑːˈmɑːnəlɑːdʒiz/| n. |现象学(复数)|The study of structures of consciousness as experienced from a first-person perspective.  Phenomenology focuses on the essence of experience, rather than the objective reality. It's a philosophical approach that emphasizes lived experience and the way things appear to consciousness.|例句：His research explores various phenomenologies of grief, focusing on the subjective experiences of bereavement.|近义词：phenomenal studies, experiential studies|反义词：objective studies, positivism|名词
