evaporated:/ɪˈvæpəreɪtɪd/| v.|蒸发；消失；逐渐减少|To change from a liquid or solid state into a gas.  The process of a liquid changing into a gas, often due to heat.|The water in the puddle evaporated in the sun.  水洼里的水在阳光下蒸发了。

The rain evaporated quickly. 雨很快就蒸发了。


近义词：vaporize, dry up, disappear, vanish
反义词：condense, materialize
