heck:/hɛk/ | int. |哎呀，表示轻微的惊讶、生气或沮丧| An exclamation expressing mild surprise, annoyance, or frustration. It's a milder form of "hell."  | Examples: "Heck, I forgot my keys!"  哎呀，我忘了带钥匙！"   "Heck no, I'm not going!"  才不要，我才不去！|近义词：shoot, darn, well, gosh |反义词：无 |词性：感叹词


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't follow typical verb conjugation.  Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.


PHRASE->
heck of a:  一个了不起的，一个极好的  (e.g., "That's a heck of a story!") 那真是个了不起的故事！
what the heck: 到底是怎么回事？ (e.g., "What the heck is going on here?") 这里到底发生了什么事？
like heck: 绝对不 (e.g., "I'm not going like heck!") 我才不去呢！
to heck with:  去你的，我才不管呢 (e.g., "To heck with it, I'm going home.") 去他的，我回家了。
heck yeah:  当然，没错 (e.g., "Heck yeah, let's do it!")  当然，我们来做吧！
