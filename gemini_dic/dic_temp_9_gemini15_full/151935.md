pastil:/ˈpæstɪl/| n.|锭剂；药丸；香料丸|A pastil is a small, usually medicated, tablet or lozenge, often intended to be dissolved in the mouth.  It can also refer to a small, perfumed cake or confection.|Example: The doctor prescribed a pastil to soothe my sore throat.  医生开了个锭剂来缓解我的喉咙痛。|近义词：tablet, lozenge, troche|反义词：liquid medicine, injection|名词
