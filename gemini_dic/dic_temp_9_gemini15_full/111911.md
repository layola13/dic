loiter:/ˈlɔɪtər/| v.|闲逛；徘徊；磨蹭|To spend time idly or waste time; to linger or delay.  |例句：He was loitering outside the store. 他在商店外面闲逛。

例句：Don't loiter; we're late!  别磨蹭，我们迟到了！


近义词：linger, dawdle, hang around, idle, tarry|反义词：hurry, rush

词源:
The word "loiter" comes from the Middle English word "loteren," which means "to delay" or "to linger."  This word is of uncertain origin, possibly related to the Old French word "loter."
