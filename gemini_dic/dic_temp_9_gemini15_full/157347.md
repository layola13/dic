pipefish:/ˈpaɪpfɪʃ/| n.|海龙|A pipefish is a type of fish that belongs to the Syngnathidae family, which also includes seahorses.  They have long, thin bodies resembling a pipe, hence the name. They are often found in shallow coastal waters and are known for their unique mating habits, where the male carries the eggs in a pouch until they hatch. |例句：The pipefish camouflaged itself perfectly amongst the seaweed. 海龙完美地伪装在海藻丛中。
