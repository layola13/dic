poaches:/poʊtʃ/ | vt. |偷猎；侵占；挖走（员工）|To illegally hunt or catch game on someone else's property; to take something that belongs to someone else; to persuade someone to leave their job for another.|例句：Hunters were poaching deer in the national park.  猎人正在国家公园里偷猎鹿。|近义词：hunt illegally, pilfer, steal, raid |反义词：protect, conserve
