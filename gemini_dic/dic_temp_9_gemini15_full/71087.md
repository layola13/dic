fish's:/fɪʃz/| n.|鱼的；鱼类的|The possessive form of "fish," indicating belonging or possession.|例句：The fish's scales shimmered in the sunlight.  鱼鳞在阳光下闪闪发光。
例句：I cleaned the fish's gills before cooking it. 我在烹饪之前清理了鱼的腮。|近义词:belonging to a fish|反义词:None


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  It only shows possession.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.

PHRASE->

fish's bones: 鱼骨
