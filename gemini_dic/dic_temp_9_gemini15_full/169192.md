prolific:/prəˈlɪfɪk/| adj.|多产的；丰富的；大量的|Producing a large amount of something, especially works of art, academic papers, or children.|The author is prolific, producing a new novel every year.  这位作者多产，每年都出版一本新小说。|abundant, copious, fertile|barren, unproductive, sterile|形容词
