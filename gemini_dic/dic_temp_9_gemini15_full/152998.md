pedometrists:/ˌpɛdəˈmɛtrɪsts/ | n. |步数测量专家，计步器专家 | Professionals who specialize in the study and application of pedometers and related technologies for measuring steps and assessing physical activity.  They may analyze data from pedometers to provide insights into an individual's activity levels, health, and well-being.  | Example: The pedometrists analyzed the data collected from the participants' wearable devices to determine the effectiveness of the exercise program.  步数测量专家分析了参与者可穿戴设备收集的数据，以确定运动计划的有效性。 |近义词: activity trackers specialists, gait analysts (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A
