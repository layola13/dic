compromissorial:/ˌkɒmprəˈmɪsɔːriəl/| adj. |愿意妥协的；调解的；和解的 | Relating to or characterized by compromise; conciliatory.  It suggests a willingness to find a mutually acceptable solution. |The judge's ruling was highly compromissorial, aiming for a fair settlement for both parties.|近义词：conciliatory, mediating, compromising,  reconciling|反义词：uncompromising, inflexible, intransigent
