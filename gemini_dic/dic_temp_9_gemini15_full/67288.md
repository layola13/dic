exsert:/ɪɡˈzɜːrt/| vt.|尽力；发挥；运用；施加；使用力|To exert oneself is to make a great effort.  To exert influence is to use one's power to affect a situation. |例句：She exerted all her strength to open the door. 她使出全身力气打开门。

例句：The government exerted pressure on the company to change its policies. 政府向公司施压，要求其改变政策。


近义词：apply, employ, use, wield, leverage


反义词：neglect, ignore, abandon, relinquish


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exerts himself in his studies. = 他在学习上很努力。
She exerts considerable influence over her employees. = 她对她手下的员工有相当大的影响力。


一般过去时:
They exerted pressure to get the project approved. = 他们施压以争取项目的批准。
He exerted his authority to settle the dispute. = 他运用他的权力来解决纠纷。


现在完成时:
She has exerted herself to achieve her goals. = 她为了达到目标而努力奋斗。
They have exerted considerable effort in this project. = 他们在该项目上付出了巨大的努力。


过去完成时:
He had exerted all his energy before he collapsed. = 他在倒下之前已经耗尽了所有的精力。
The team had exerted considerable pressure on the deadline. = 团队在截止日期前承受了巨大的压力。


将来时:
They will exert all their influence to help him. = 他们将尽其所能帮助他。
She will exert her right to vote. = 她将行使她的投票权。


虚拟语气:
If he exerted more effort, he would succeed. = 如果他付出更多努力，他就会成功。
I wish I had exerted more influence on the decision. = 我真希望我对这个决定有更大的影响力。


被动语态:
Pressure was exerted on the government to reform. = 对政府施加了改革的压力。
Influence is exerted by the media on public opinion. = 媒体对公众舆论施加了影响。


疑问句:
How much effort will you exert to finish this task? = 你将付出多少努力来完成这项任务？
What influence is exerted by this organization? = 这个组织有什么影响力？


否定句:
He didn't exert himself enough to win the race. = 他没有足够努力赢得比赛。
They didn't exert any pressure on the situation. = 他们没有对局势施加任何压力。


PHRASE->

exert influence = 施加影响
exert pressure = 施加压力
exert control = 行使控制
