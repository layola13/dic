re-bracing:/ˌriːˈbreɪsɪŋ/| v.|重新支撑；重新加固 |To brace again; to strengthen or support something again.|例句：The engineers are re-bracing the bridge after the recent earthquake.  工程师们在地震过后正在重新加固桥梁。

例句：The doctor recommended re-bracing the injured leg to ensure proper healing. 医生建议重新加固受伤的腿以确保正确愈合。


近义词：reinforce, strengthen, shore up| 反义词：weaken, undermine| 词性：动词
