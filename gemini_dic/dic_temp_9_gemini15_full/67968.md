facilities:/fəˈsɪlətiz/ | n. |设备；设施；工具 |  Facilities refer to the physical structures, equipment, or services provided for a particular purpose.  They can range from basic amenities to complex systems. |The university boasts excellent research facilities. 这所大学拥有优良的研究设施。|The hotel offers a range of facilities, including a swimming pool and a spa.这家酒店提供一系列设施，包括游泳池和水疗中心。|amenities, resources, equipment |inconvenience, lack  |名词
