doomsayers:/ˈduːmseɪərz/| n.| 灾难预言者；悲观主义者 | People who habitually predict disaster or misfortune. They often focus on the negative aspects of a situation and tend to exaggerate potential problems. |例句：Doomsayers predicted the end of the world, but it never happened.  灾难预言者预言世界末日，但它从未发生。

The doomsayers' warnings about climate change are increasingly being taken seriously. 关于气候变化的灾难预言者的警告正日益受到重视。|近义词：pessimists, prophets of doom, Cassandra |反义词：optimists, idealists
