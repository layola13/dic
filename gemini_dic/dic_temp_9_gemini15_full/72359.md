flowerpots:/ˈflaʊəpɒts/ /ˈflaʊɚpɑːts/| n. |花盆；花钵|Flowerpots are containers used to grow plants, typically made of clay, plastic, or ceramic. They provide a controlled environment for plant roots and allow for easy mobility and arrangement of plants.  |例句：She arranged the colorful flowerpots on the patio. 她把五颜六色的花盆摆放在天井里。

例句：The gardener carefully watered the flowerpots in the greenhouse. 园丁小心翼翼地浇灌温室里的花盆。


近义词：planters, pots


反义词：None (no direct antonym)


词性:名词
