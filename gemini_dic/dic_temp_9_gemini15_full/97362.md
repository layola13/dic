infraction:/ɪnˈfrækʃən/ | n. |违规；违反；违法行为 |The act of breaking a law, rule, or agreement.  A violation or breach of a law, rule, or contract. |例句：Parking infractions can result in fines. 违反停车规定可能导致罚款。

He received a traffic infraction for speeding. 他因超速而收到交通违规罚单。


近义词：violation, breach, transgression, offense | 反义词：compliance, obedience


词源：from Old French *infraccion, from Latin infractio, from infringere "to break in, infringe" (from in- "in" + frangere "to break")
