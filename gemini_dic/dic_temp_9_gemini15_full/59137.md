dynamiting:/ˈdaɪnəmaɪtɪŋ/| v.|爆破；用炸药爆破|The act of using dynamite to blast or destroy something.  It implies a forceful and destructive action. |例句：The old building was dynamited to make way for a new highway.  旧楼被炸毁以腾出地方修建新的高速公路。

例句：They are dynamiting the rock face to create a new road.  他们在爆破岩面以修建一条新路。


近义词：blasting, demolition, explosion


反义词：construction, building, erection


词性:动词
