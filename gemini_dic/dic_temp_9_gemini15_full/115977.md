mars's:/mɑːrzɪz/| n.|火星的；火星所有物|The possessive form of the proper noun "Mars," indicating belonging or ownership.|例句：Mars's atmosphere is very thin. 火星的大气层非常稀薄。|近义词：of Mars|反义词:无


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It functions as an adjective modifying other nouns. There is no usage in terms of tenses as in verbs.

PHRASE->
Mars's surface: 火星表面
Mars's gravity: 火星的重力
Mars's moons: 火星的卫星
Mars's exploration: 火星探测
Mars's mysteries: 火星的谜团
