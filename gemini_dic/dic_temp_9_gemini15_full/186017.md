rewiden:/ˌriːˈwaɪdn/| v.|重新加宽，扩大|To widen again or to a greater extent.  The verb "rewiden" implies a previous widening and a subsequent increase in width.|例句：After the road repairs, they had to rewiden the bridge.  路修好后，他们不得不重新加宽桥面。

例句：The river rewidened after the narrow section. 河流在狭窄河段之后重新变宽。|近义词：widen, broaden, expand|反义词：narrow, constrict, reduce
