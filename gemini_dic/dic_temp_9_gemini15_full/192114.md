scouts:/skaʊts/ | n. | 童子军；侦察员；侦察兵 |  Members of the Scouting movement, an organization that teaches young people outdoor skills, leadership, and citizenship. Scouts are known for their adventurous activities and community service. | Example: The scouts went camping in the woods.  童子军们在树林里露营。  |  Example: The army sent out scouts to investigate the enemy's position.  军队派出侦察兵去侦察敌人的位置。 | explorers, pathfinders, explorers | deserters, enemies | 名词
