cobra: /ˈkoʊbrə/ | n. |眼镜蛇| A venomous snake of the family Elapidae, characterized by its ability to spread its neck ribs to form a hood. Cobras are known for their spitting venom and are found in Africa, Asia, and parts of Australia.  |例句：The cobra raised its hood, preparing to strike. 眼镜蛇竖起了它的颈部，准备攻击。  |例句：The zookeeper carefully handled the cobra. 动物园管理员小心地处理着眼镜蛇。|近义词：viper, asp |反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑无毒蛇 harmless snake)|名词
