protectrix: /prəˈtɛktrɪks/ | n. | 保护者，守护者 | A female protector or guardian.  The word is rarely used in modern English and often found in older texts or specialized contexts. It suggests a strong, active role in protection, often implying a maternal or nurturing aspect.  | Example: The protectrix of the village fiercely defended its inhabitants from the invading army.  村庄的守护者英勇地保卫着村民免受侵略军的袭击。 | 近义词：protector, guardian, defender, shield | 反义词：attacker, aggressor, destroyer
