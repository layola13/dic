The chemical composition of explodents varies greatly. |例句：The bomb squad carefully examined the suspicious package to determine if it contained an explodent.  爆炸物处理小组仔细检查了可疑包裹，以确定它是否含有炸药。

The discovery of new explodents revolutionized warfare. 新型炸药的发现彻底改变了战争的面貌。|近义词：explosive, detonator |反义词：inert substance
