moisturizes:/ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪzɪz/| v.| 使湿润；滋润 | To make something moist or wet; to add moisture to something, especially skin.  | 例句：The cream moisturizes my dry skin.  这款面霜滋润我的干燥肌肤。

例句：The lotion moisturizes and softens the skin. 这款润肤露能滋润和柔化肌肤。


近义词：hydrates, conditions, softens

反义词：dries, dehydrates
