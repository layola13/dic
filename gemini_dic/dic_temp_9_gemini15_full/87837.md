hidden:/ˈhɪdn/ | adj. |隐藏的，秘密的 | concealed or kept secret; not visible or easily noticeable. |例句：The treasure was hidden in a secret cave. 宝藏藏在一个秘密的山洞里。|近义词：concealed, secret, unseen, covert |反义词：obvious, visible, exposed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The toy is hidden under the bed. = 玩具藏在床底下。
The truth is hidden from us. = 真相对我们隐藏着。

一般过去时:
She hid the letter in a drawer. = 她把信藏在抽屉里。
They hid the evidence from the police. = 他们把证据藏了起来，不让警察发现。

现在完成时:
He has hidden his feelings very well. = 他把自己的感情隐藏得很好。
The clues have been hidden for centuries. = 线索已经隐藏了几个世纪。

过去完成时:
She had hidden the money before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，她已经把钱藏起来了。
They had hidden the truth for years. = 他们隐瞒真相多年了。

将来时:
He will hide the present until her birthday. = 他会把礼物藏起来直到她的生日。
They will hide their emotions during the meeting. = 他们在会议期间会隐藏他们的情绪。

虚拟语气:
If I had hidden the evidence, I would have avoided suspicion. = 如果我把证据藏起来，我就不会受到怀疑。
I wish I had hidden my feelings better. = 我希望我以前能更好地隐藏我的感情。

被动语态:
The message was hidden in plain sight. = 信息隐藏在显眼的地方。
The truth is being hidden from the public. = 真相正被隐瞒着大众。

疑问句:
Is the gift hidden somewhere? = 礼物藏在哪里了吗？
Where is the money hidden? = 钱藏在哪里？

否定句:
The problem wasn't hidden. = 问题并没有被隐藏。
The truth was not hidden from him. = 真相没有对他隐瞒。
