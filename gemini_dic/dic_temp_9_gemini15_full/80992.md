grade-schooler:/ˈɡreɪdˌskuːlər/| n. |小学生|A child who attends grade school (elementary school).  This term typically refers to children aged between 5 and 11, although the exact age range can vary depending on the location and school system.|Example: The grade-schooler proudly showed his teacher his drawing.  这位小学生自豪地向老师展示了他的画作。|近义词：elementary school student, primary school student|反义词：high school student, college student
