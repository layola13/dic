excuse:/ɪkˈskjuːz/ | v. n. | 原谅；借口；免除 | To forgive someone for a fault or offense; to pardon; a reason or explanation for something; to release or exempt someone from something. | 例句：Please excuse me for being late.  请原谅我迟到了。| 例句：He used his illness as an excuse for not going to work. 他以生病为借口不去上班。| 近义词：pardon, forgive, apology, justification | 反义词：blame, condemn, accuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I excuse your behavior this time. = 我这次原谅你的行为。
He excuses himself from the meeting. = 他为自己缺席会议找借口。

一般过去时:
She excused him from the test. = 她免除了他的考试。
They excused his lateness. = 他们原谅了他的迟到。

现在完成时:
I have excused myself from the party. = 我已经为自己缺席派对找了借口。
She has excused his rudeness several times. = 她已经原谅了他的粗鲁好几次了。

过去完成时:
He had excused himself before the announcement. = 他在宣布之前就已经离开了。
They had excused her absence from the previous meeting. = 他们已经原谅了她上次会议的缺席。

将来时:
I will excuse your tardiness this once. = 我这次会原谅你的迟到。
She will excuse herself from the conversation. = 她将离开谈话。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would excuse her mistake. = 如果我是你，我会原谅她的错误。
I wish I could excuse myself from this responsibility. = 我希望我能免除这项责任。

被动语态:
His absence was excused by the teacher. = 他的缺席得到了老师的原谅。
Her behavior was excused on account of her youth. = 她的行为由于她年轻而被原谅。

疑问句:
Will you excuse my interruption? = 你能原谅我的打断吗？
Can I be excused from this task? = 我可以免除这项任务吗？

否定句:
I cannot excuse his behavior. = 我不能原谅他的行为。
The teacher will not excuse any late submissions. = 老师不会原谅任何迟交的作业。


PHRASE->
excuse me = 请原谅
excuse my French = 对不起，我失态了
under the excuse of = 以...为借口
make excuses = 找借口
with your excuse = 由于你的借口
