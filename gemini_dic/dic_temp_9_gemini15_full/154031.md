performs:/pərˈfɔːrmz/ | vt. |表演；执行；运作；表现 | to carry out, accomplish, or fulfill an action, task, or function.  |例句：The orchestra performs Beethoven's Fifth Symphony tonight. 今晚乐团将演奏贝多芬第五交响曲。|例句：The computer performs calculations at high speed. 这台电脑以高速进行计算。|近义词：execute, accomplish, achieve, function |反义词：fail, neglect


USAGE->

He performs magic tricks every weekend. = 他每个周末表演魔术。
The machine performs its function flawlessly. = 机器完美地执行其功能。


一般过去时:
