词源:
The word "faultless" is a combination of the prefix "fault-" (meaning "without fault") and the suffix "-less" (meaning "without").  The root word "fault" comes from Old French *faulte and ultimately from Vulgar Latin *falta, meaning "defect, failing, mistake".
