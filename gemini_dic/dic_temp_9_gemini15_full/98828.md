intelligences:/ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənsɪz/ | n. |智力，才智；情报部门；理解力 |The plural form of intelligence, referring to multiple forms or instances of intelligence. It can refer to different types of intelligence (e.g., linguistic, logical-mathematical, spatial), the intelligence capabilities of multiple individuals or entities, or separate pieces of intelligence information. |例句：Multiple intelligences theory suggests that human intelligence is not a single, general ability but rather a collection of distinct abilities. 多元智能理论认为，人的智力不是单一的、普遍的能力，而是一系列不同的能力的集合。  The government agencies pooled their intelligences to solve the complex case. 政府机构汇集了他们的情报来解决这个复杂的案件。 |近义词：abilities, talents, capabilities, insights |反义词：stupidity, ignorance, foolishness
