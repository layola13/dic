registration:/ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən/ | n. |注册，登记，注册手续 |The act or process of registering or being registered.  It involves officially recording someone's or something's name, details, or presence on a list. |例句：Student registration begins next week. 学生注册将于下周开始。

例句：The online registration system is easy to use.  在线注册系统易于使用。


近义词：enrollment, entry, record, listing | 反义词：deregistration, cancellation


词源：源自于14世纪晚期的法语 *registracioun*，由拉丁语 *registratio* 派生而来，其动词形式为 *registrare*，意思是“记录，登记”。

The university handles student registration efficiently. = 这所大学有效率地处理学生注册。
