panini:/pəˈniːni/| n. |帕尼尼（意大利烤三明治）|A panini is a grilled sandwich, typically made with Italian bread and filled with various ingredients such as meats, cheeses, and vegetables.  It is often pressed in a panini press to create a crispy exterior and warm, melty interior.| Example: I ordered a panini with prosciutto, mozzarella, and pesto for lunch. 我午餐点了一个普罗修托、马苏里拉奶酪和罗勒酱的帕尼尼。|近义词：grilled sandwich, pressed sandwich|反义词：salad, soup
