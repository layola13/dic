corer: /ˈkɔːrər/ | n. |去核器；核心部分| A corer is a tool used to remove the core or central part of something, typically fruits or vegetables.  It can also refer to the central, essential part of something. |例句：She used a corer to remove the core from the apples before making applesauce. 她用一个去核器在做苹果酱之前去掉了苹果核。|  例句：The corer of the problem is a lack of communication.  问题的核心是缺乏沟通。|近义词：corer, pitter, remover |反义词：N/A
