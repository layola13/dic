relaxation:/ˌriːlækˈseɪʃən/ | n. |放松；松弛；缓和 | The state of being free from tension and anxiety; a state of calmness.  It can also refer to the process of becoming relaxed or the act of making something less tense or rigid. |例句：After a long day at work, I enjoy the relaxation of a warm bath.  一天辛苦工作后，我喜欢泡个热水澡放松一下。

例句：The relaxation of trade restrictions led to increased economic activity.  贸易限制的放松导致了经济活动的增长。


近义词：rest, repose, leisure, ease | 反义词：tension, stress, strain
