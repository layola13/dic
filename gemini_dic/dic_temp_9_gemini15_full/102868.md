jauntier:/ˈdʒɔːntiər/| adj.|更快乐的；更活泼的；更欢快的 |More cheerful and lively.  It suggests a more spirited and carefree attitude than simply being happy. |例句：She wore a jauntier hat than usual, reflecting her improved mood. 她戴着一顶比平时更俏皮的帽子，反映了她心情的改善。

例句：He walked with a jauntier step after receiving the good news.  收到好消息后，他步伐轻快了许多。


近义词：lively, cheerful, spirited, merry, brisk

反义词：depressed, gloomy, downcast, somber, sluggish


词源:源自jaunty (adj.)，“快乐的，活泼的”，这个词来源于17世纪末的荷兰语“jant”，意思是“小伙子，年轻人”。  The added "-ier" suffix intensifies the adjective, indicating a higher degree of the quality.
