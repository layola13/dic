prespecifically:/ˌpriːspəˈsɪfɪkli/| adv.|预先具体地，预先明确地|Specifically beforehand or in advance; with prior explicit detail.  The term implies a level of detail and planning that goes beyond simply being "specific."|例句：The experiment was designed prespecifically to test the effects of the new drug.  实验是预先专门设计来测试新药的效果的。

The parameters of the study were prespecifically defined before data collection began. 研究参数在数据收集开始之前就已经预先明确地定义了。|近义词：specifically, beforehand, in advance, explicitly, precisely|反义词：generally, vaguely, imprecisely, randomly|无
