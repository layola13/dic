decarbonising: /ˌdiːˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 脱碳；去碳化 | The process of reducing or eliminating carbon dioxide emissions, particularly from the burning of fossil fuels.  This involves transitioning to cleaner energy sources, improving energy efficiency, and adopting carbon capture technologies. | 例句：Many countries are investing heavily in decarbonising their energy sectors. 许多国家都在大力投资其能源部门的脱碳工作。 | 例句：Decarbonising transportation requires a multifaceted approach, including electric vehicles and public transit.  脱碳交通需要多方面的方法，包括电动汽车和公共交通。 | 近义词： decarbonization, de-carbonization | 反义词：carbonizing, carbonation
