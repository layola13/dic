ectoblast:/ˈɛktoʊblæst/| n. |外胚层|The outermost layer of cells or tissue of a developing embryo in animals; it gives rise to the skin, nervous system, and sense organs.|例句：The ectoblast differentiates into the epidermis and nervous system. 外胚层分化成表皮和神经系统。|近义词：germ layer, epiblast|反义词：endoblast, mesoblast
