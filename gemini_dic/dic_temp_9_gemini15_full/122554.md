modem:/ˈmoʊdɛm/| n. |调制解调器 |A modem is a device that modulates an analog carrier signal to encode digital information, and also demodulates such a carrier signal to decode the transmitted information.  It allows computers to communicate over telephone lines or other communication channels. |例句：I need a new modem to connect to the internet. 我需要一个新的调制解调器来连接互联网。
