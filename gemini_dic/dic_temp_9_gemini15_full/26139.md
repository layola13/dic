bustled:/ˈbʌsəld/ | v. | 匆忙移动；喧闹；熙攘 | To move quickly and energetically in a busy way; to be full of activity and noise. |例句：The children bustled around the Christmas tree, eager to open their presents. 孩子们在圣诞树周围忙碌地跑来跑去，急切地想打开礼物。

例句：The market was bustling with shoppers. 市场熙攘着购物的人群。


近义词：hurried, rushed, swarmed, thronged | 反义词：dawdled, idled, lingered


词源:
源自中古英语 *bustlen，意思是“喧闹，骚动”。  最终源自古英语 *bystlian，意思是“使喧闹”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The market bustles with activity every Saturday. = 每个星期六市场都熙熙攘攘。
She bustles around the kitchen preparing dinner. = 她忙着在厨房准备晚餐。


一般过去时:
They bustled through the airport to catch their flight. = 他们匆忙穿过机场赶飞机。
The crowd bustled past, barely noticing us. = 人群匆匆走过，几乎没有注意到我们。


现在完成时:
She has bustled about all day preparing for the party. = 她忙活了一整天为派对做准备。
The family has bustled through the holiday season. = 这家人忙忙碌碌地度过了节日季。


过去完成时:
They had bustled through their work before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经完成了工作。
Before the storm hit, everyone had bustled indoors. = 在暴风雨来临之前，每个人都匆匆忙忙地进了屋。


将来时:
They will bustle about getting ready for the wedding. = 他们将忙着准备婚礼。
The streets will bustle with people during the festival. = 节日期间，街道上将挤满了人。


虚拟语气:
If she bustled less, she would be less stressed. = 如果她不那么忙碌，她就会少一些压力。
I wish the children would bustle less and settle down. = 我希望孩子们能少一些喧闹，安静下来。


被动语态:
The room was bustled by the arrival of many guests. = 许多客人的到来使房间热闹起来。(注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，更常用的表达方式是描述房间的状态，例如“The room was filled with activity” )
The hallways were bustled with excited students.  =走廊里挤满了兴奋的学生。（同样，更自然的表达是：The hallways were filled with excited students）


疑问句:
Does the city always bustle like this? = 这个城市总是这么喧闹吗？
Will the streets bustle with activity tomorrow? = 明天街道上会很热闹吗？


否定句:
The street didn't bustle with people as usual. = 街道上的人不像往常那么多。
The library was unusually quiet; it didn't bustle with students. = 图书馆异常安静；不像往常那样挤满了学生。


PHRASE->
bustle about = 忙忙碌碌
bustle around = 到处奔忙
bustle with activity = 充满活力
bustle through = 匆忙完成
bustle into = 匆匆进入
