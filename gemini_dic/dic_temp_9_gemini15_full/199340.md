similative:/ˈsɪmɪlətɪv/| adj.|比喻的；类似的；具有相似性的|  Descriptive of a word, phrase, or figure of speech that expresses resemblance or analogy;  similar or analogous in nature.  It implies a comparison or likeness, but often without using explicit comparative words like "like" or "as".|例句：The poem employs a similative style, drawing parallels between the human heart and the ocean. 这首诗采用比喻的风格，将人心与海洋作比较。

例句：The author's use of similative language created a vivid and memorable image. 作者使用比喻的语言创造了一个生动难忘的意象。


近义词：analogous, comparative, figurative, metaphorical, similar|反义词：literal, dissimilar, unlike|词性：adj.
