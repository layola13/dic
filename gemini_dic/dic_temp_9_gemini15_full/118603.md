mess with:/mɛs wɪð/| vt.|招惹，惹恼，胡乱摆弄|to bother or annoy someone; to interfere with something; to handle something carelessly|例句：Don't mess with him, he's dangerous.  别招惹他，他很危险。

例句：He was messing with the wires and caused a short circuit. 他乱动电线导致短路。

近义词：bother, annoy, interfere with, tamper with

反义词：ignore, leave alone, respect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mess with each other all the time. = 他们总是互相开玩笑。
I don't mess with people who are dishonest. = 我不和不诚实的人交往。


一般过去时:
He messed with the equipment and broke it. = 他弄坏了设备。
She messed with my things without asking. = 她未经允许乱动我的东西。


现在完成时:
I've messed with my hair too much, it's all frizzy. = 我把头发弄得太乱了，都毛躁了。
They've messed with the schedule again. = 他们又改了日程安排。


过去完成时:
He had messed with the computer before I got there. = 我到那儿之前他已经动过电脑了。
She had messed with the paint, making a terrible mess. = 她把颜料弄得到处都是，搞得一团糟。


将来时:
I won't mess with him again. = 我不会再惹他了。
They're going to mess with the system, and it will crash. = 他们要乱动系统，系统会崩溃的。


虚拟语气:

I wish I hadn't messed with that old machine. = 我真希望我没有乱动那台旧机器。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
