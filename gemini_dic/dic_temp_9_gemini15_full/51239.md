destructibles:/dɪˈstrʌktɪblz/| n. |易毁坏物，易损耗品|Things that are easily destroyed or damaged.  Destructibles are items in a game or other context that can be broken or destroyed, often yielding rewards or triggering events. |Example:  The game features destructible environments, allowing players to smash through walls and obstacles. 游戏中包含可破坏的环境，允许玩家撞破墙壁和障碍物。|近义词：fragiles, breakables |反义词：indestructibles, unbreakable.
