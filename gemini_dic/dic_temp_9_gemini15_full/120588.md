mirror-images: /ˈmɪrər ɪmɪdʒɪz/ | n. |镜像，反射影像 |  Two images that are reversed copies of each other, as if reflected in a mirror.  They are often used to describe things that are similar but opposite, such as left and right hands.  |例句：The two sculptures were mirror images of each other. 这两个雕塑是彼此的镜像。

例句：His reflection in the polished brass was a perfect mirror image. 他在磨光的黄铜中的倒影是完美的镜像。


近义词：reflections, reversed copies, opposites

反义词：None directly, but concepts like "identical copies" or "exact duplicates" could be considered antonyms in a contextual sense.

词性: noun
