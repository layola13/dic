skinful:/ˈskɪnfʊl/| n.|一皮肤(量的酒); 满满一杯|A skinful is an informal term referring to a large amount of alcoholic drink, enough to fill the skin (or a container the size of a skin). It implies a considerable amount of intoxication. |例句：He drank a skinful and stumbled home. 他喝了一大杯酒，跌跌撞撞地回家了。  He had a skinful at the party. 他在派对上喝了很多酒。|近义词：a lot, plenty, a great deal|反义词: a drop, a sip, a little
