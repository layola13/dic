prole:/ˈprəʊl/| n.|无产阶级成员，平民|A member of the working class, especially one regarded as being uneducated or having low social status.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying a lack of intellectual or cultural refinement.|例句：In Orwell's *Nineteen Eighty-Four*, proles are depicted as the largely unorganized masses.  在奥威尔的《一九八四》中，无产阶级被描绘成基本上没有组织的大众。|近义词：commoner, working-class person, proletarian|反义词：elite, aristocrat, bourgeois
