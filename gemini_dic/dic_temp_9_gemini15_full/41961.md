cosponsored:/ˌkoʊˈspɑːnsərd/ | v. |共同发起，联合主办 | To sponsor jointly; to support or promote something together. | 例句：The bill was cosponsored by senators from both parties. 这项法案由两党的参议员共同发起。  | 例句：The event was cosponsored by several local businesses.  这次活动由几家当地企业联合主办。 | 近义词：jointly sponsored, collaborated on, partnered on | 反义词：solely sponsored, independently promoted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cosponsor many events each year. = 他们每年共同发起许多活动。
The two companies cosponsor a new product line. = 这两家公司共同发起一个新的产品线。
