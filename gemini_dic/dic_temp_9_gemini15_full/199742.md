siredon:/ˈsaɪrədɒn/| n.|  钝口螈属（一种两栖动物）| A genus of aquatic salamanders, commonly known as axolotls, characterized by their ability to retain larval features throughout their lives (neoteny). They are known for their remarkable regenerative abilities.|Example:  The Mexican axolotl, *Ambystoma mexicanum*, is a well-known species of *siredon*.  墨西哥钝口螈（Ambystoma mexicanum）是著名的钝口螈属物种。|近义词：axolotl (though axolotl refers to a specific species, not the entire genus)|反义词：无|名词
