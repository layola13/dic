larks:/lɑːk/|ˈlɑːrkz/| n.|云雀；玩笑；嬉戏；|A lark is a small brown bird that sings beautifully, especially at dawn.  It can also refer to an act of fun or mischief; a playful prank.|例句：We heard the lark singing at sunrise. 我们听到云雀在日出时唱歌。


The children were having a lark in the park.孩子们在公园里玩得很开心。|近义词：skylark, frolic, prank, joke|反义词：seriousness, drudgery
