reliefs:/rɪˈliːfs/ | n. | 浮雕；地势起伏；救济；解除；减轻 |  Reliefs are raised or molded designs on a surface, often found in art and architecture.  They can also refer to the variations in land elevation, or the alleviation of suffering or a difficult situation. | 例句：The ancient temple walls were adorned with intricate reliefs depicting scenes from mythology.  古庙的墙壁装饰着描绘神话场景的复杂浮雕。

The dramatic reliefs of the mountain range were breathtaking.  山脉戏剧性的起伏令人叹为观止。


近义词：sculptures, carvings, etchings, aid, assistance, alleviation | 反义词：aggravation, worsening, intensification
