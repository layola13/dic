retaste:/ˌriːˈteɪst/| vt.|再次品尝，重新评价|To taste again; to reconsider or re-evaluate.|例句：I retasted the wine and found it even better than before. 我再次品尝了这款酒，发现它比以前更好。
例句：The committee decided to retaste the proposal and make some changes. 委员会决定重新审议该提案并进行一些修改。|近义词：re-evaluate, reconsider, reassess|反义词：accept without question, ignore, overlook|动词
