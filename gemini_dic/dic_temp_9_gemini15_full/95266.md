incapable:/ɪnˈkeɪpəbl/ | adj. |无能力的，不能胜任的 | Unable to do something.  This word highlights a lack of ability or skill to perform a particular task or function. It often implies a fundamental lack of capacity, rather than just a temporary inability. |例句：He is incapable of understanding complex ideas. 他无法理解复杂的思想。

例句：She felt incapable of coping with the stress of her job. 她感到无法应对工作压力。


近义词：unable, incompetent, powerless, unfit, deficient |反义词：capable, competent, able


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is incapable of driving a car. = 他不会开车。
She is incapable of finishing the project on time. = 她无法按时完成项目。


一般过去时:
He was incapable of speaking after the accident. = 事故后他无法说话。
She was incapable of controlling her emotions. = 她无法控制自己的情绪。


现在完成时:
He has been incapable of working since his illness. = 他生病后一直无法工作。
She has become incapable of walking long distances. = 她已经无法走很长的路了。


过去完成时:
He had been incapable of writing for years before he recovered. = 他在康复之前好几年都无法写作。
She had been incapable of concentrating on her studies due to stress. = 由于压力，她之前无法专注于学习。


将来时:
He will be incapable of attending the meeting due to illness. = 他将因病无法参加会议。
She will probably be incapable of lifting heavy objects after the surgery. = 手术后她可能无法举起重物。


虚拟语气:
If he were capable, he would help us. = 如果他有能力，他会帮助我们。(注意：此处用capable作对比)
I wish I were not incapable of learning a new language. = 我希望我不至于学不会一门外语。


被动语态:
(Incapable is rarely used in the passive voice, as it describes a state of being rather than an action performed on someone.)


疑问句:
Is he incapable of understanding the instructions? = 他无法理解这些指示吗？
Are you incapable of completing the task independently? = 你无法独立完成这项任务吗？


否定句:
He is not incapable of learning; he just needs more time. = 他并非学不会；他只是需要更多时间。
She is not incapable of hard work; she just lacks motivation. = 她并非不能努力工作；她只是缺乏动力。


PHRASE->
be incapable of = 不能...
incapable of doing sth = 不能做某事
utterly incapable = 完全无能的
totally incapable = 完全无能力的
seemingly incapable = 看似无能的
