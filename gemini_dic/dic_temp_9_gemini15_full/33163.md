chromatosphere:/ˈkroʊmətoʊsfɪr/| n. |色球层|The chromosphere is the relatively thin layer of the sun's atmosphere above the photosphere and below the corona. It is characterized by its reddish hue, visible during a total solar eclipse.  |Example:  The chromosphere's reddish glow was briefly visible during the total eclipse. 色球层在日全食期间短暂地显露出红色的光芒。|近义词: solar atmosphere (部分)|反义词:photosphere, corona|名词
