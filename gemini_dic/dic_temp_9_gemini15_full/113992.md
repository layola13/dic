magisterially:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəli/| adj.|权威地；威严地；大师般地|In a manner that shows great authority or power; impressively.  The conductor magisterially raised his baton. 指挥威严地举起了指挥棒。|近义词：authoritatively, imperiously, commandingly, impressively|反义词：submissively, humbly, meekly
