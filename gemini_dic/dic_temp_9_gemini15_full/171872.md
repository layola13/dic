punker:/ˈpʌŋkər/| n. |朋克；朋克摇滚乐迷|A punker is a person who identifies with or participates in the punk subculture, often characterized by a rebellious attitude, nonconformist style, and a preference for punk rock music.  Punkers often have distinctive hairstyles, clothing, and body modifications. |例句：He dressed like a punker with ripped jeans and a studded jacket. 他穿着破洞牛仔裤和带铆钉的夹克，打扮得像个朋克。

例句：Many young punkers attended the concert. 许多年轻的朋克乐迷参加了这场音乐会。|近义词：punk, rebel, nonconformist |反义词：conformist, traditionalist
