skedaddle:/skɛˈdædəl/| v.| 偷偷溜走；逃走 | To leave quickly and secretly, often to avoid trouble or danger.  |例句：The thief skedaddled when he saw the police.  小偷看见警察就溜走了。|例句：Let's skedaddle before the rain starts.  下雨前我们赶紧溜吧。|近义词：abscond, bolt, flee, vamoose |反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skedaddles when he sees trouble. = 他一看到麻烦就溜之大吉。
They skedaddle every time the boss comes around. = 老板一来他们就开溜。


一般过去时:
She skedaddled from the party early. = 她早早地离开了派对。
The kids skedaddled after breaking the vase. = 孩子们打破花瓶后逃跑了。


现在完成时:
He has skedaddled and we can't find him. = 他溜了，我们找不到他了。
They have skedaddled several times this week. = 他们本周已经好几次溜走了。


过去完成时:
They had skedaddled before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前他们已经溜走了。
She had skedaddled so quickly that no one saw her. = 她溜得那么快，没有人看见她。
