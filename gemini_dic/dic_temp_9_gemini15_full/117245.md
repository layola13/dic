meccas:/ˈmɛkəz/| n.|麦加圣地；中心；聚集地|A place regarded as a center for a particular activity or interest.|Mecca is the holiest city for Muslims. 麦加是穆斯林最神圣的城市。|近义词：center, hub, focal point|反义词：periphery, outskirts


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This city is a mecca for tourists. = 这个城市是游客的中心。
That restaurant is a mecca for foodies. = 那家餐厅是美食家的聚集地。


一般过去时:
The town was once a mecca for artists. = 这个小镇曾经是艺术家们的中心。
The conference was a mecca for industry leaders. = 这个会议是行业领导者的聚集地。


现在完成时:
This region has become a mecca for outdoor enthusiasts. = 这个地区已经成为户外爱好者的中心。
The festival has grown into a mecca for music lovers. = 这个节日已经发展成为音乐爱好者的聚集地。


过去完成时:
Before the internet, the library had been a mecca for researchers. = 在互联网出现之前，图书馆一直是研究人员的中心。
The shop had become a mecca for collectors before it closed. = 这家商店在关闭之前已经成为收藏家的聚集地。


将来时:
The new park will be a mecca for families. = 新公园将成为家庭的中心。
The event will become a mecca for entrepreneurs. = 这个活动将成为企业家的聚集地。


虚拟语气:
If this were a mecca for artists, more would come. = 如果这里是艺术家的中心，就会有更多的人来。
I wish this place were a mecca for young people. = 我希望这个地方成为年轻人的聚集地。


被动语态:
The city was made a mecca for shoppers by the new mall. = 新购物中心使这座城市成为了购物者的中心。
The area is being developed into a mecca for technology companies. = 该地区正在被开发成科技公司的聚集地。


疑问句:
Is this place a mecca for hikers? = 这个地方是徒步旅行者的中心吗？
Will the concert become a mecca for pop music fans? = 这场音乐会会成为流行音乐粉丝的聚集地吗？


否定句:
This town is not a mecca for tourists. = 这个小镇不是游客的中心。
The event was not a mecca for investors. = 这个活动不是投资者的聚集地。


PHRASE->
a mecca for... = ...的中心/聚集地
the mecca of... = ...的中心
a cultural mecca = 文化中心
a shopping mecca = 购物中心
a culinary mecca = 美食中心
