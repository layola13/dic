episiotomy:/ɛpɪziˈɒtəmi/ | n. |会阴切开术 | A surgical cut made in the perineum (the area between the vagina and the anus) to enlarge the vaginal opening during childbirth.  This procedure is sometimes performed to prevent tearing of the perineum during delivery. | Example: The doctor recommended an episiotomy to assist with the delivery.  医生建议进行会阴切开术以协助分娩。|近义词：perineal incision |反义词：natural birth (without episiotomy)
