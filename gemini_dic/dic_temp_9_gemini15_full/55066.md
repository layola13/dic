disregarded:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdɪd/| v. |忽略；漠视；不理会|To ignore or treat as unimportant.  It implies a conscious decision to not pay attention to something.|The driver disregarded the stop sign and caused an accident. 司机无视停止标志，导致了一起事故。|He disregarded my warnings and went ahead with his plan. 他无视我的警告，继续执行他的计划。| ignored, overlooked, neglected | heeded, attended to, considered |动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
They disregard the rules. = 他们无视规则。
She disregards his advice. = 她不理会他的建议。

一般过去时:
He disregarded the warning signs. = 他无视警告标志。
We disregarded her pleas for help. = 我们无视她寻求帮助的请求。

现在完成时:
They have disregarded all our efforts. = 他们无视了我们所有的努力。
She has disregarded the doctor's orders. = 她无视了医生的嘱咐。

过去完成时:
He had disregarded the previous warnings. = 他之前无视了警告。
They had disregarded the evidence. = 他们无视了证据。

将来时:
She will disregard the consequences. = 她将无视后果。
They will disregard my instructions. = 他们将无视我的指示。

虚拟语气:
If he disregarded the law, he would be punished. = 如果他违反法律，他将受到惩罚。(这里disregard的虚拟语气用法与一般过去时相似)
I wish he hadn't disregarded my advice. = 我真希望他没有无视我的建议。

被动语态:
The warnings were disregarded. = 警告被忽略了。
His pleas were disregarded. = 他的请求被忽略了。

疑问句:
Did they disregard the instructions? = 他们是否无视了指示？
Will she disregard my feelings? = 她会无视我的感受吗？

否定句:
They did not disregard the evidence. = 他们没有无视证据。
She did not disregard his warnings. = 她没有无视他的警告。

PHRASE->
disregard for the law = 漠视法律
disregard the facts = 忽视事实
show disregard for = 对...漠不关心
with disregard to = 不顾；不理会
complete disregard = 完全无视
