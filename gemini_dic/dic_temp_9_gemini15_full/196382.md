sermonize:/ˈsɜːrmənaɪz/ | vt. | 说教；教训；长篇大论地讲道 | To deliver a sermon or preach; to lecture someone in a tedious or moralizing way. |例句：He sermonized on the evils of greed. 他长篇大论地讲述贪婪的罪恶。
He likes to sermonize about the importance of hard work. 他喜欢喋喋不休地谈论努力工作的重要性。|近义词：preach, lecture, moralize |反义词：listen, understand, empathize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sermonizes every Sunday. = 他每周日都讲道。
She sermonizes to her children about good manners. = 她告诫她的孩子们要讲礼貌。

一般过去时:
The priest sermonized on forgiveness. = 牧师就宽恕的问题进行了布道。
He sermonized at length about the dangers of smoking. = 他冗长地训斥吸烟的危害。

现在完成时:
He has sermonized on this topic many times. = 他已经多次就这个话题进行过说教。
She has sermonized to her class about the importance of education. = 她已经多次向她的班级阐述教育的重要性。

过去完成时:
He had sermonized for an hour before the congregation grew restless. = 他已经讲了一个小时的道理，会众才开始不安分。
She had sermonized repeatedly about the need for discipline, but her children remained unruly. = 她反复告诫孩子们要守纪律，但孩子们仍然不听话。

将来时:
The minister will sermonize on the parable of the Good Samaritan. = 牧师将要讲解好撒玛利亚人的比喻。
He will sermonize to the youth group about responsible social media use. = 他将向青年组成员讲解负责任的社交媒体使用。

虚拟语气:
If he sermonized less, people might listen more. = 如果他少说教一些，人们可能会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't sermonize so much. = 我希望他不要再说教了。

被动语态:
The topic of forgiveness was sermonized upon extensively. = 宽恕的话题被广泛地讲解。
The importance of honesty was sermonized to the students. = 学生们被教导诚实的重要性。

疑问句:
Does he often sermonize? = 他经常说教吗？
Will the speaker sermonize about current events? = 演讲者会就时事发表说教吗？

否定句:
He didn't sermonize; he simply offered advice. = 他没有说教；他只是提出了建议。
She doesn't sermonize; she prefers to lead by example. = 她不说教；她更喜欢以身作则。


PHRASE->
sermonize on/about  就…说教
sermonize endlessly 没完没了地说教
sermonize to sb. 对某人说教
sermonize against sth. 反对…说教
sermonize with a purpose 有目的地说教
