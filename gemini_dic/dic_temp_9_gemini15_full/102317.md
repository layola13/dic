ivory:/ˈaɪvəri/ | n. |象牙；象牙色 |A hard, creamy-white material made from the tusks of elephants, formerly used for making ornaments, piano keys, billiard balls, etc.  Now, its use is heavily restricted due to elephant poaching.  |The carvings were made from ivory.  这些雕刻是用象牙制成的。|  The piano keys were a beautiful shade of ivory.  钢琴键是美丽的象牙色。 | bone, bone china | plastic, wood | 名词
