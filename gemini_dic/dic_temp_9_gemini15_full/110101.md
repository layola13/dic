licker:/ˈlɪkər/| n. |舔的人；舔舐器；（尤指）舔舐动物伤口的人|A person or thing that licks;  especially, someone who licks wounds.  The term often carries negative connotations, implying excessive or inappropriate licking. |例句：The dog was a licker, constantly trying to lick my face. 这只狗是个爱舔的人，总是试图舔我的脸。

例句：The vet used a special licker to clean the cat's wound. 兽医用了一个特殊的舔舐器来清洁猫的伤口。


近义词：licker, lapper (less common and less negative)|反义词：N/A |词性:noun


USAGE->

He licks his wounds. = 他舔舐着他的伤口。
The dog licks its paw. = 狗舔着它的爪子。
