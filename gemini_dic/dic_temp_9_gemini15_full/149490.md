padrone:/pəˈdroʊni/| n.|老板，主人，控制者|The word "padrone" refers to a person who has power or control over others, often in a business or organization.  It can also imply a paternalistic or exploitative relationship.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting dominance and sometimes unfair treatment.|Example: The padrone controlled the lives of his workers, dictating their wages and living conditions.  老板控制着工人们的生活，决定他们的工资和居住条件。|近义词：boss, master, overseer, proprietor|反义词：employee, subordinate, worker|名词
