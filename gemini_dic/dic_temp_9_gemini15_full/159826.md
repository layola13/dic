polygyn:/ˈpɒlɪdʒɪn/| n.|一妻多夫制；多妻制（旧用法）|The term "polygyn" refers to a form of marriage where one woman has multiple husbands.  While historically used, it is less common now, often replaced by "polyandry".|例句：The anthropological study focused on the polygyn practiced in certain Himalayan communities. 人类学研究关注喜马拉雅山区某些社群实行的一妻多夫制。|近义词：polyandry (more commonly used)|反义词：monogamy|名词
