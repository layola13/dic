preobtaining: /ˌpriːəbˈteɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 预先获得，提前取得 |  The act of obtaining something beforehand or in advance.  It implies securing something before it is officially needed or available. | 例句：Preobtaining the necessary permits saved them considerable time and hassle. = 预先取得必要的许可证为他们节省了大量时间和麻烦。


例句：The company's strategy involved preobtaining materials to avoid potential supply chain disruptions. = 该公司的战略包括预先获得材料，以避免潜在的供应链中断。


近义词：securing beforehand, acquiring in advance, getting ahead of time


反义词：postponing, delaying, deferring


词性：动词 (verb)
