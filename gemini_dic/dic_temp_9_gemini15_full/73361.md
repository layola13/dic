foregift:/ˈfɔːrɡɪft/| n.|预付金，预付款|A sum of money paid in advance.  It is often a partial payment for goods or services, securing the transaction or commitment.| Example: The buyer paid a foregift of $10,000 to secure the purchase of the property.  买方支付了10,000美元的预付款以确保房产的购买。|近义词：advance payment, down payment, earnest money|反义词：final payment, balance|名词
