masterclass:/ˈmɑːstərklɑːs/| n. |大师班，高级课程|A masterclass is an intensive course or workshop given by an expert in their field.  It often involves a small group of participants and focuses on advanced techniques and skills.|例句：Gordon Ramsay's masterclass on cooking techniques was highly sought after.  戈登·拉姆齐的烹饪技巧大师班非常抢手。

例句：I attended a masterclass on photography led by a renowned photographer. 我参加了一位著名摄影师主导的摄影大师班。


近义词：workshop, seminar, intensive course, tutorial|反义词：beginner's class, introductory course|词性:名词


词源:  The word is a compound of "master" and "class". "Master" in this context signifies an expert or skilled individual, while "class" refers to a lesson or instructional session.  The combination suggests a high-level instructional experience delivered by a master of their craft.
