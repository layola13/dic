overweighting: /ˌoʊvərˈweɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过分重视；给予过大的权重 | To give something more importance or weight than it deserves.  |例句：Overweighting certain factors in the decision-making process led to an incorrect conclusion.  过分重视决策过程中的某些因素导致了错误的结论。


例句：The study suffered from overweighting the opinions of a small, unrepresentative sample.  这项研究的缺陷在于过分重视一小部分不具有代表性的样本的意见。


近义词：overemphasizing, overvaluing, prioritizing excessively


反义词：underweighting, undervaluing, downplaying


词源:  The word "overweighting" is a combination of "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and "weighting" (the act of assigning weight or importance).  "Weighting" itself derives from "weight," meaning importance or significance, ultimately related to the physical sense of weight.
