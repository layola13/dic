monoliths:/ˈmɒnəˌlɪθs/ | n. |巨石，整体，庞然大物 |A large single upright block of stone, especially one that is shaped into a monument or sculpture.  Often used metaphorically to describe something large, powerful, and indivisible.|例句：The ancient civilization left behind several impressive stone monoliths.  古代文明留下了几块令人印象深刻的巨石。|例句：The company's monolithic structure stifled innovation.  公司单一的结构扼杀了创新。|近义词：monument, megalith, colossus |反义词：fragment, piece, component
