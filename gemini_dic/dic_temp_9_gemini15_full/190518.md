satellitious: /ˌsætəˈlɪʃəs/ | adj. | 卫星的；像卫星一样的 | Relating to or resembling a satellite.  The term often implies a dependence or orbiting relationship, similar to a satellite's orbit around a planet. |

例句：

The newly discovered moon is satellitious to the distant planet. =  新发现的卫星环绕着遥远的行星运行。

His career seemed satellitious to his mentor's success. = 他的事业似乎依赖于他导师的成功。


近义词：satellite-like, orbiting, dependent

反义词：independent, self-sufficient

词性：形容词
