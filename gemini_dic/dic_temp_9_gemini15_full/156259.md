phylactolaemata:/ˌfaɪlækˈtɒliːmətə/| n. |苔藓虫纲|A class of freshwater bryozoans characterized by their statoblasts (resting buds) and typically exhibiting a colonial growth form.  They are distinguished by their lophophore, a crown of tentacles used for feeding, which is enclosed in a protective fold of tissue (the "phylactolaem").|例句：The study focused on the diversity of phylactolaemata in the lake ecosystem. 这项研究侧重于湖泊生态系统中苔藓虫纲的多样性。  The researcher observed several colonies of phylactolaemata attached to submerged vegetation. 研究人员观察到几群苔藓虫纲附着在水下植被上。|近义词：暂无完全近义词，可使用"freshwater bryozoans"  |反义词：暂无|名词
