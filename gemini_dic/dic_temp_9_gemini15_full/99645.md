interlayering: /ˌɪntərˈleɪəriŋ/ | n. |层层叠加，相互层叠 | The process of arranging or placing layers in an overlapping or interwoven manner.  This often refers to materials, data, or structures. |例句：The interlayering of different rock strata created a complex geological formation.  层层叠加的不同岩层形成了一个复杂的地质构造。


The interlayering of marketing strategies resulted in increased brand awareness.  市场策略的层层叠加带来了品牌知名度的提升。


近义词：stratification, layering, interleaving


反义词：separation, segregation


词性：名词


词源:  The word "interlayering" is a combination of "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among") and "layering" (the act of arranging in layers).  "Layering" itself comes from the verb "to lay," meaning to place or put down, which has Germanic roots.
