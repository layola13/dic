flourish:/ˈflʌrɪʃ/| v. n.|繁荣，兴旺；挥舞；装饰性笔画|To grow or develop successfully; to thrive.  To wave or move something around energetically. A decorative flourish.|例句：The business flourished under his leadership.  他的领导下，公司蓬勃发展。
The artist added a flourish to her signature. 艺术家在签名上加了一个装饰性的笔画。|近义词：thrive, prosper, boom |反义词：decline, wither, fail|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The business flourishes in the current market. = 这家公司在目前的市场上蓬勃发展。
The artist flourishes her brush with confidence. = 艺术家自信地挥舞着她的画笔。


一般过去时:
The company flourished during the economic boom. = 在经济繁荣时期，公司生意兴隆。
She flourished her handkerchief dramatically. = 她戏剧性地挥舞着手帕。


现在完成时:
The town has flourished since the new road was built. = 自从修建了新路以来，这个小镇蓬勃发展。
He has flourished his sword in the air many times. = 他多次在空中挥舞他的剑。


过去完成时:
The empire had flourished for centuries before its decline. = 这个帝国在衰落之前已经繁荣了几个世纪。
The conductor had flourished his baton before the orchestra began to play. = 指挥在乐队开始演奏之前挥舞了他的指挥棒。


将来时:
The economy will flourish if the government implements these policies. = 如果政府实施这些政策，经济将会繁荣。
She will flourish the flag proudly during the ceremony. = 她将在仪式上自豪地挥舞旗帜。


虚拟语气:
If the project had been better funded, it might have flourished. = 如果这个项目有更好的资金支持，它可能已经蓬勃发展了。
If he had flourished his sword, he could have defeated his opponent. = 如果他挥舞他的剑，他就能打败他的对手。


被动语态:
The city was flourished by the development of new industries. = 新兴产业的发展使这座城市繁荣起来。(flourished在此处为被动语态，不太常见，通常用被动语态表达的是结果)
The flag was flourished dramatically at the end of the parade. = 在游行结束时，旗帜被戏剧性地挥舞起来。


疑问句:
Does your business flourish in this area? = 你的生意在这个区域蓬勃发展吗？
Does she often flourish her hair? = 她经常挥舞她的头发吗？


否定句:
The plants did not flourish in the poor soil. = 植物在贫瘠的土壤中没有生长茂盛。
He did not flourish his weapon at any point during the fight. = 在战斗中，他没有任何时候挥舞武器。
