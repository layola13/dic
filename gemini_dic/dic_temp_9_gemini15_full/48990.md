deglaze:/diːˈɡleɪz/ | vt. | 去掉（锅底）焦糖；用少量液体（如酒）冲洗锅底，溶解焦糖或残渣 | To remove browned food particles from a pan by adding liquid and scraping.  | 例句：I deglazed the pan with some red wine to make a delicious sauce. 我用一些红酒冲洗锅底，做成美味的酱汁。

例句：She deglazed the roasting pan with chicken broth to create a rich gravy. 她用鸡汤冲洗烤盘，做成浓厚的肉汁。


近义词：dissolve, rinse, loosen


反义词：burn, scorch


词性：动词
