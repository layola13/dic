nonperpetuities:/ˌnɒn pɜːpɛˈtʃuːətiz/ | n. |非永久性财产，非永续物|Things that are not perpetual;  assets or rights that do not continue indefinitely.  The term often appears in legal and financial contexts, referring to assets with a limited lifespan or duration, contrasting with perpetuities (things that last forever).|Example: The trust held nonperpetuities, meaning its assets would eventually be distributed.|近义词：limited-term assets, finite assets|反义词：perpetuities, perpetual assets|名词
