predoom:/priːˈduːm/| vt.|预先注定；预言…的厄运|To predetermine or foretell the doom or misfortune of (someone or something).  The word suggests an inescapable fate.|例句：The ancient prophecy predoomed the kingdom to destruction. 古老的预言预示着王国将被毁灭。

例句：His reckless actions predoomed him to failure. 他的鲁莽行为注定了他会失败。


近义词：foreordain, preordain, doom, destine


反义词：save, rescue, deliver


词性：动词
