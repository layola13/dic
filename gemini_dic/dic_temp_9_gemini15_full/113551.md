macadamize: /məˈkædəmaɪz/ | vt. |铺设碎石路面|To cover (a road) with macadam.  Macadamization is a method of road construction using layers of compacted, interlocking stones, typically bound with tar or asphalt. This process creates a durable and relatively smooth road surface.|例句：They macadamized the road to improve its condition. 他们铺设碎石路面以改善路况。The old road was macadamized last year.  这条旧路去年铺了碎石。|近义词：pave, surface|反义词：depave, roughen|动词
