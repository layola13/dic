pitiful:/ˈpɪtɪfəl/| adj.|可怜的；令人怜悯的；差劲的；微不足道的|Inspiring pity; pathetic; woefully inadequate.  It can refer to something evoking compassion or something of poor quality.|例句：His pitiful attempts at cooking resulted in a burnt dinner.  他的可怜的烹饪尝试导致晚餐烧焦了。

例句：The pitiful state of the abandoned building made me sad.  那座废弃建筑物可怜的状态让我很难过。


近义词：pathetic, lamentable, wretched, meager, paltry, insignificant

反义词：admirable, excellent, magnificent, superb, outstanding
