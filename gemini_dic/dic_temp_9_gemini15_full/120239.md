mingled:/ˈmɪŋɡld/| v.|混合；掺杂；交往；融合 |To mix or combine together.| The scents of spices mingled in the air. 香料的气味在空气中混合在一起。|近义词：blend, combine, mix, intermingle |反义词：separate, divide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The colors mingle beautifully in the painting. = 画中的颜色混合得非常漂亮。
The guests mingle freely at the party. = 客人们在聚会上自由地交流。


一般过去时:
The two rivers mingled at the confluence. = 两条河在汇合处汇流。
She mingled with the crowd, unnoticed. = 她混在人群中，无人注意。


现在完成时:
The different cultures have mingled over centuries. = 几百年来，不同的文化相互融合。
He has mingled with the elite of society. = 他与社会精英交往过。


过去完成时:
The ingredients had mingled thoroughly before baking. = 在烘烤之前，配料已经充分混合。
By the time the party ended, everyone had mingled with everyone else. = 到派对结束时，每个人都和其他人都互相认识了。


将来时:

The flavors will mingle perfectly in the stew. = 炖菜里的各种味道将会完美地融合。


虚拟语气:
If the paints had mingled better, the result would have been more pleasing. = 如果颜料混合得更好，结果会更令人满意。
I wish my ideas had mingled more effectively with the team's. = 我希望我的想法能更有效地与团队的想法融合。


被动语态:

The different musical styles were mingled to create a unique sound. = 不同的音乐风格混合在一起，创造出一种独特的音效。


疑问句:

否定句:
