renavigate:/ˌriːˈnævɪɡeɪt/ | vt. |重新导航；重新规划航线 | To navigate again or differently; to plan a new course of action or direction.  |例句：The ship had to renavigate after encountering unexpected storms.  轮船在遭遇意外风暴后不得不重新规划航线。

例句：After the initial plan failed, the team had to renavigate their strategy.  在最初的计划失败后，团队不得不重新规划他们的策略。


近义词：re-route, replan, redirect | 反义词：maintain course, proceed as planned | 词性：动词
