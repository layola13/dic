pestilentious: /ˌpɛstɪˈlɛnʃəs/ | adj. | 令人讨厌的；有害的；致病的 |

英文解析:  Characterized by or tending to cause pestilence;  spreading disease; harmful or destructive; annoying or troublesome.  It suggests something that is not only unpleasant but also potentially harmful or damaging, often in a subtle or insidious way.


例句:

The pestilentious rumors spread quickly through the town.  = 恶毒的谣言迅速传遍了整个城镇。

His pestilentious behavior made him unpopular with his colleagues. = 他令人讨厌的行为使他在同事中不受欢迎。


近义词: infectious, contagious, noxious, pernicious, harmful, destructive, annoying, troublesome, offensive


反义词: beneficial, harmless, salutary, pleasant, agreeable
