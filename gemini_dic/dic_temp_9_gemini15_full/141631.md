optimal:/ˈɒptɪməl/|ˈɑːptɪməl/| adj. |最佳的，最优的 |Most favorable or advantageous; best.  Optimal solutions are those that achieve the best possible outcome given a set of constraints.|The optimal temperature for this process is 37 degrees Celsius.  这个过程的最佳温度是摄氏37度。|The company implemented optimal strategies to maximize profits. 公司实施了最佳策略以最大化利润。|best, ideal, perfect, optimum |suboptimal, inadequate, inferior
