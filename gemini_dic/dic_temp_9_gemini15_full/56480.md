dom:/dɒm/| n.|宅邸，家；管辖范围；权威；控制|A dom is a house or home, often a large and impressive one. It can also refer to a territory or area under one's control, or to the power or authority itself.  |例句：He inherited a grand dom in the countryside. 他继承了乡下的一座宏伟的宅邸。|例句：The king's dom extended across many miles. 国王的统治范围延伸至数英里。|近义词：residence, mansion, realm, territory, authority, control|反义词：subjection, lack of power, anarchy
