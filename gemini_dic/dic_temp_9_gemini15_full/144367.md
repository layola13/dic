outstepping:/ˌaʊtˈstɛpɪŋ/| v.|超越；胜过；超过 |To surpass or exceed in some way, often in a subtle or indirect manner. It implies going beyond the expected or established limits. |例句：He outstepped his authority by making that decision without consulting his superiors. 他未经上司同意就做出了那个决定，超越了他的职权。

例句：Her innovative design outstepped all the other entries in the competition.  她的创新设计超越了比赛中的所有其他作品。


近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, transcend

反义词：underperform, lag behind, fall short

词性:动词
