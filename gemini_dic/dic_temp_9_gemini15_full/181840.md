reload:/ˌriˈloʊd/ | vt. n. |重新装填；重新加载；|To load again, especially a weapon or a machine with ammunition or fuel.|例句：He quickly reloaded his rifle and fired again. 他迅速重新装填步枪并再次射击。

例句：The website is slow because it's reloading the images. 网站速度慢是因为它正在重新加载图片。


近义词：recharge, refill, refresh | 反义词：unload, discharge | 词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reloads his gun before every shot. 他每次射击前都重新装填枪支。
The computer automatically reloads the page after a crash. 电脑在崩溃后会自动重新加载页面。

一般过去时:
She reloaded the washing machine with more clothes. 她往洗衣机里又加了一些衣服。
The soldiers reloaded their weapons after the battle. 战斗结束后，士兵们重新装填了武器。

现在完成时:
