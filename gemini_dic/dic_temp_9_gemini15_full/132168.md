noncustodial: /ˌnɒnkʌˈstoʊdiəl/ | adj. | 非监护的，非抚养权的 | Not having custody of a child.  Relating to a parent who does not have legal custody of a child. |

例句：

The noncustodial parent is responsible for child support payments. = 非监护权一方父母有责任支付子女抚养费。

He has a noncustodial parenting agreement with his ex-wife. = 他与前妻达成了非监护权的育儿协议。


近义词：non-custodial


反义词：custodial
