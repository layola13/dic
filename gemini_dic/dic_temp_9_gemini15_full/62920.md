enlivens:/ɪnˈlaɪvənz/| v.|使活跃；使生动；使充满活力|To make something more lively or interesting.|The music enlivens the atmosphere. 音乐使气氛活跃起来。 The comedian's jokes enlivens the party. 喜剧演员的笑话使聚会活跃起来。|animate, invigorate, enliven|depress, dull, deaden|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music enlivens the party. = 音乐使派对活跃起来。
The comedian's jokes enliven the show. = 喜剧演员的笑话使演出更加生动。

一般过去时:
The news enlivened her spirits. = 这个消息使她精神振奋。
The rain enlivened the garden. = 雨水使花园生机勃勃。

现在完成时:
The new teacher has enlivened the classroom. = 新老师使教室充满活力。
His speech has enlivened the debate. = 他的演讲使辩论更加激烈。

过去完成时:
The good news had enlivened their mood before the meeting. = 好消息在会议前使他们的情绪振奋。
Having already had a glass of wine, the lively music enlivened her even more.  =  已经喝了一杯酒，轻快的音乐使她更加兴奋。

将来时:
The upcoming festival will enliven the town. = 即将到来的节日将使小镇充满活力。
A new exhibit will enliven the museum. = 一个新的展览会使博物馆更加生动。

虚拟语气:
If the music were louder, it would enliven the party. = 如果音乐再大声一点，它会使派对更热闹。
I wish the story would enliven the presentation. = 我希望这个故事能够使演示更生动。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The quiet atmosphere didn't enliven the meeting. = 安静的气氛并没有使会议活跃起来。
