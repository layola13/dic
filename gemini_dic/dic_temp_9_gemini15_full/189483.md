sajou:/ˈsæʒuː/| n. | 刺绣工人；（尤指）以精细针法做刺绣的人 | A sajou is a person, typically a woman, skilled in embroidery, especially fine needlework.  The term often evokes a sense of artistry and precision. | She is a renowned sajou, known for her intricate floral designs. 她是一位著名的刺绣工人，以其复杂的 floral图案而闻名。| embroiderer, needlewoman |  (no direct antonym)
