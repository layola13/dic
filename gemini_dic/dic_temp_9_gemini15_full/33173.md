chroming:/ˈkroʊmɪŋ/ | n. |镀铬；铬处理 |The process of coating a metal surface with chromium, typically for decorative or protective purposes.  This involves an electrochemical process where chromium ions are deposited onto the surface.  | 例句：The car's bumpers had a beautiful chroming finish. 这辆车的保险杠有着美丽的镀铬饰面。

The antique furniture was restored with careful chroming. 这件古董家具经过细致的镀铬修复。| 近义词：plating, chrome plating | 反义词：dechroming (去除镀铬) | 名词
