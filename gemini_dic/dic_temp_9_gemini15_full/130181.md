non-royal:/ˌnɒnˈrɔɪəl/| adj. |非王室的，平民的|Not belonging to or connected with royalty; common; not royal.  |例句：The non-royal guests were seated separately from the family. 非王室的客人与王室成员分开就座。

例句：He led a non-royal life despite his noble lineage. 尽管出身高贵，他过着平民生活。


近义词：common, plebeian, non-aristocratic


反义词：royal, aristocratic


词性：形容词
