noncognitive: /ˌnɒnˈkɒɡnɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非认知的；非认知能力的 | Relating to or involving mental processes other than cognitive ones, such as emotions, attitudes, and beliefs.  These processes are often considered to influence learning and performance, but are not directly measured by traditional cognitive tests.  | Example:  The study examined the noncognitive factors that contribute to student success, such as motivation and perseverance.  这项研究考察了有助于学生成功的非认知因素，例如动力和毅力。 | 近义词: affective, emotional, attitudinal | 反义词: cognitive
