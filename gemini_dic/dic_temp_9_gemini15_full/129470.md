non-aggression:/ˌnɒn əˈɡreʃən/ | n. | 不侵略；互不侵略 | The policy of non-aggression aims to prevent conflict through peaceful means.  不侵略政策旨在通过和平手段防止冲突。|近义词：pacifism, peaceableness |反义词：aggression, hostility |名词
