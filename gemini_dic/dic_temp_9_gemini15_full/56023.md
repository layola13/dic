do without:/duː wɪˈðaʊt/| v.| 将就; 没有…也行;  |to manage without something; to live or function without something.|例句：We'll have to do without a car for a while. 我们暂时得没有车将就一下。|例句：I can do without dessert tonight. 今晚我不吃甜点也行。|近义词：manage without, dispense with, forgo|反义词：need, require


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We do without sugar in our tea. = 我们喝茶不加糖。
They do without a car because they live near work. = 他们住在离工作地点很近的地方，所以不需要车。


一般过去时:
We did without a holiday last year. = 我们去年没有休假。
She did without lunch to save money. = 她为了省钱没吃午饭。


现在完成时:
We have done without many luxuries during the pandemic. = 在疫情期间，我们省去了许多奢侈品。
They have done without help from their family for years. = 他们多年来没有得到家人的帮助。


过去完成时:
We had done without television for a month before we finally got a new one. = 我们在最终买了一台新电视之前，已经一个月没看电视了。
They had done without internet for a week before the repairman arrived. = 维修人员到来之前，他们已经断网一个星期了。


将来时:
We will do without a vacation this year. = 今年我们将不休假。
They will do without a new computer for now. = 他们现在暂时不用买新电脑。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we wouldn't have to do without. = 如果我们有钱的话，就不必省吃俭用了。
I wish I didn't have to do without my phone. = 我真希望不用没有手机。

疑问句:
Can we do without this extra feature? = 我们能省掉这个额外功能吗？
Do you think they can do without assistance? = 你认为他们能没有帮助吗？


否定句:
We cannot do without your help. = 我们离不开你的帮助。
They couldn't do without their daily coffee. = 他们一天都不能没有咖啡。


PHRASE->
do without luxuries = 没有奢侈品也行
