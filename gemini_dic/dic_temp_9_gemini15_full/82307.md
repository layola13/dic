groused:/ɡraʊzd/| v.|抱怨，牢骚；发牢骚|To complain or grumble, especially in a bad-tempered way.|例句：He groused about the long hours and low pay. 他抱怨工作时间长，薪水低。|例句：She constantly groused about the weather. 她总是抱怨天气。|近义词：complain, grumble, whine, moan|反义词：praise, compliment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often grouses about his job. = 他经常抱怨他的工作。
They grouse about the lack of resources. = 他们抱怨资源匮乏。

一般过去时:
She groused about the noisy neighbors last night. = 她昨晚抱怨吵闹的邻居。
He groused to his friend about the unfair treatment. = 他向朋友抱怨不公平的待遇。

现在完成时:
He has groused about this problem for weeks. = 他已经抱怨这个问题几周了。
They have groused repeatedly about the poor service. = 他们反复抱怨糟糕的服务。

过去完成时:
She had groused about the cold weather before the trip. = 在旅行之前，她就抱怨过寒冷的天气。
They had groused many times about the management’s decisions. = 他们多次抱怨管理层的决定。

将来时:
He will grouse about the traffic again tomorrow. = 他明天还会抱怨交通。
They will grouse if the project is delayed. = 如果项目延期，他们会抱怨。

虚拟语气:
If he knew how to fix it, he wouldn't grouse so much. = 如果他知道怎么修理，他就不会抱怨这么多了。
I wish they wouldn't grouse all the time. = 我希望他们不要总是抱怨。

被动语态:  (Grouse is rarely used passively)
The problem was often groused about by the employees. = 这个问题经常被员工抱怨。


疑问句:
Does he often grouse about his work? = 他经常抱怨他的工作吗？
Why are they always grousing? = 他们为什么总是抱怨？

否定句:
He didn't grouse about the food. = 他没有抱怨食物。
They didn't grouse at all during the meeting. = 他们在会议期间一点也没有抱怨。


PHRASE->
grouse about = 抱怨…
grouse at = 向…抱怨
grouse to = 向…抱怨
let out a grouse = 发牢骚
have a grouse = 有怨言
