habituated:/həˈbɪtʃueɪtɪd/| adj.|习惯的，适应的| accustomed to a particular situation, way of life, etc.|She has become habituated to the noise of the city.|近义词：accustomed, used, adapted|反义词：unaccustomed, unfamiliar


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is habituated to early mornings. = 他习惯了早起。
The animals are habituated to their environment. = 这些动物已经适应了它们的环境。


一般过去时:
She was habituated to a life of luxury. = 她习惯了奢华的生活。
They were habituated to the cold climate. = 他们已经习惯了寒冷的气候。


现在完成时:
I have become habituated to working from home. = 我已经习惯了在家工作。
We have habituated ourselves to the new schedule. = 我们已经适应了新的时间安排。


过去完成时:
She had habituated herself to the long hours before the project started. = 在项目开始之前，她已经习惯了长时间工作。
They had habituated themselves to the rigors of military life. = 在加入军队之前，他们已经习惯了军营生活的严苛。


将来时:
He will be habituated to the new job soon. = 他很快就会适应这份新工作。
They will have habituated themselves to the new rules by next week. = 到下周他们就会适应新的规章制度了。


虚拟语气:
If I were habituated to public speaking, I wouldn't be so nervous. = 如果我习惯了公开演讲，我就不会这么紧张了。
I wish I were habituated to waking up early. = 我希望我习惯了早起。


被动语态:
The students were gradually habituated to the strict rules. = 学生们逐渐适应了严格的规章制度。
The animals are being habituated to their new surroundings. = 这些动物正在适应新的环境。


疑问句:
Are you habituated to spicy food? = 你习惯吃辣的食物吗？
Is he habituated to working long hours? = 他习惯长时间工作吗？


否定句:
I am not habituated to this kind of weather. = 我不习惯这种天气。
She was not habituated to the fast pace of city life. = 她不习惯城市生活的快节奏。


PHRASE->
become habituated to = 习惯于
habituated to the idea of = 习惯于…的想法
habituated to a certain lifestyle = 习惯于某种生活方式
gradually habituated to = 逐渐习惯于
habituated to the demands of = 习惯于…的要求
