kept:/kɛpt/| v.|保持，保存；继续；遵守|To continue to have or possess (something); to maintain possession of something; to retain something. |例句：She kept the secret. 她保守了秘密。
He kept his promise. 他遵守了他的诺言。|近义词：maintained, retained, preserved|反义词：lost, abandoned, released|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He keeps his room clean. 他保持他的房间干净。
She keeps a diary. 她坚持写日记。

一般过去时:
I kept the money safe. 我把钱安全地保存起来了。
They kept working late. 他们继续工作到很晚。

现在完成时:
We have kept in touch since college.  我们大学毕业后一直保持联系。
She has kept the same job for ten years. 她做了十年同样的工作。

过去完成时:
He had kept the news secret for months. 他保守了这个秘密好几个月。
They had kept the promise they made to their friend.  他们遵守了对朋友的承诺。

将来时:
I will keep you updated. 我会让你随时了解情况。
They will keep trying until they succeed. 他们会一直尝试直到成功。

虚拟语气:
If I had kept my promise, she wouldn't be angry. 如果我遵守了我的诺言，她就不会生气。
I wish I had kept my cool in that situation. 我希望当时我能保持冷静。

被动语态:
The secret was kept from him. 这个秘密没有告诉他。
The old house was kept in good condition. 老房子保养得很好。

疑问句:
Did you keep your appointment? 你遵守你的约会了吗？
Will they keep the store open late tonight? 他们今晚会把商店开到很晚吗？

否定句:
I didn't keep my appointment. 我没有遵守我的约会。
She couldn't keep her eyes off him. 她无法将目光从他身上移开。
