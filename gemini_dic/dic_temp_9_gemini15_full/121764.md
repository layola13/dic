misquote:/mɪsˈkwoʊt/| vt.| 引用错误；曲解；误传 | To quote inaccurately or out of context.  |例句：He misquoted the statistics to support his argument. 他曲解了统计数据来支持他的论点。

例句：The newspaper misquoted the politician's statement.  报纸曲解了这位政治家的声明。


近义词：misrepresent, misinterpret, paraphrase incorrectly, distort

反义词：quote accurately, cite correctly


词性：动词
