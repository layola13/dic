precontributes:/ˌpriːkənˈtrɪbjuːts/| v.|预先贡献，提前贡献|To contribute beforehand or in advance; to make a contribution before a specific time or event.|The company precontributes to its employees' retirement funds annually. = 公司每年预先为员工的退休基金捐款。|He precontributes to the charity every month to ensure consistent support. = 他每月预先向慈善机构捐款，以确保持续的支持。|contribute, donate|detract, withdraw|动词
