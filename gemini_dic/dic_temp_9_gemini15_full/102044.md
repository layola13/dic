isolators:/ˈaɪsəleɪtərz/| n.|隔离器，绝缘体|A device or material used to isolate something from its surroundings, preventing the flow of electricity, heat, sound, or other forms of energy.|例句：The engineers installed electrical isolators to prevent short circuits. 工程师安装了电气隔离器以防止短路。
例句：The sound isolators reduced the noise pollution significantly. 隔音器显著降低了噪音污染。|近义词：insulators, separators|反义词：connectors, conductors|名词
