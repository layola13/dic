prem:/prɛm/ | n. | 房租，租金；场所；前提；首要条件 |  A sum of money paid for the use of property, especially land or a building.  It can also refer to a premise or a prerequisite. | 例句：The prem for this apartment is quite high. 这套公寓的房租相当高。

The prem of the argument was flawed.  这个论点的基础是有缺陷的。 | 近义词：rent, lease, premise, prerequisite | 反义词：free, gratis
