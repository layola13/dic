nonreplaceable: /ˌnɒnˌrɪˈpleɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不可替代的，必不可少的 |  Incapable of being replaced; essential.  The word highlights the irreplaceability of something, suggesting its unique value or crucial function.  It emphasizes the lack of suitable substitutes. |

例句：

The CEO's experience and expertise are nonreplaceable assets to the company.  = 首席执行官的经验和专业知识是公司不可替代的资产。

This antique vase is nonreplaceable; it's a family heirloom. = 这只古董花瓶是不可替代的；它是传家宝。


近义词：irreplaceable, essential, indispensable, vital, crucial

反义词：replaceable, substitutable, dispensable


词性：形容词
