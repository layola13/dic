ignorance:/ˈɪɡnərəns/| n. |无知，愚昧|Lack of knowledge or information.  It can refer to a lack of awareness of a particular fact or issue, or a general lack of knowledge or education.|His ignorance of basic geography was surprising.  他的基本地理知识匮乏令人惊讶。  |近义词：unawareness, unawareness, nescience |反义词：knowledge, understanding, wisdom


USAGE->

His ignorance prevents him from understanding the situation. = 他的无知妨碍了他理解形势。
Their ignorance of the law is not an excuse. = 他们不懂法不是借口。


一般过去时:
Her ignorance of the dangers led to her injury. = 她对危险的无知导致了她受伤。
