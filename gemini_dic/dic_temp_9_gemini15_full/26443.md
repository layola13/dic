byroad:/ˈbaɪroʊd/ | n. |小路；偏僻小道 | A byroad is a minor road, typically less important than a main road and often unpaved or poorly maintained. It usually leads to a less frequented destination.  |例句：We took a byroad to avoid the traffic on the highway. = 我们走了一条小路来避开高速公路上的交通。
They discovered a hidden village down a secluded byroad. = 他们在一条僻静的小路上发现了一个隐藏的村庄。|近义词：lane, track, path, side road |反义词：highway, main road, freeway
