dune:/dʒuːn/| n. |沙丘|A dune is a mound or ridge of sand formed by the wind, especially on a desert or coast. Dunes can be various sizes, from small ripples to massive structures that stretch for miles.  They are constantly shifting due to wind action. | Example: The desert landscape was characterized by towering sand dunes.  沙漠景观的特点是高耸的沙丘。The wind sculpted beautiful patterns in the coastal dunes. 风在沿海沙丘上雕刻出美丽的图案。|近义词：sand hill, mound, ridge|反义词：valley, depression|名词
