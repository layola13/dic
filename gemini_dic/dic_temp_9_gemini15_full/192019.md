scorpio:/ˈskɔːpiəʊ/| n. |天蝎座；天蝎座的人；蝎子|The word "Scorpio" refers to the eighth astrological sign in the Zodiac, symbolized by the scorpion, and also to the constellation Scorpio.  It can also refer to a person born under this sign.  People born under this sign are often perceived as passionate, intense, and resourceful.|Example:  She's a Scorpio; she's known for her strong will and determination. 她是一个天蝎座的人；她以意志坚强和决心果断而闻名。|Example: The constellation Scorpio is visible in the autumn sky. 天蝎座星座在秋天的夜空中可见。|近义词: scorpion (as an animal), zodiac sign|反义词:None specifically, but antonyms could relate to personality traits, such as "passive" or "unresourceful" depending on the context.|名词
