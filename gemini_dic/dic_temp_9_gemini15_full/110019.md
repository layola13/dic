librettist:/lɪˈbrɛtɪst/| n. |剧作家，歌词作者|A librettist is a person who writes the text for an opera, musical, or other musical work.  They are responsible for crafting the story, characters, and dialogue that are sung or spoken by the performers.|Example:  The librettist for this opera is known for their evocative and emotionally resonant lyrics.|近义词：text writer, opera writer, wordsmith |反义词：composer (in the context of a musical work)
