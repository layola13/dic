gill-like:/ˈdʒɪlˌlaɪk/| adj. |像鳃的；鳃状的 |Resembling gills in appearance or function.  The term typically refers to structures that are thin, feathery, or lacy and involved in gas exchange or filtering. |例句：The organism had gill-like appendages that filtered food from the water.  这种生物有像鳃一样的附属物，可以过滤水中的食物。

The parasitic worm possessed gill-like structures for respiration.  这种寄生虫拥有用于呼吸的鳃状结构。|近义词：branchial, gill-shaped |反义词：lung-like
