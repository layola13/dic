metagenesis:/ˌmɛtəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |世代交替；异型世代交替|The alternation of generations in the life cycle of a plant or animal, involving a sexual phase and an asexual phase.  Metagenesis refers to the cyclical alteration of sexual and asexual reproductive phases in the life cycle of certain organisms, particularly plants and some invertebrates.  This involves distinct generations with different forms and reproductive strategies.|例句：The life cycle of ferns demonstrates metagenesis, alternating between a diploid sporophyte and a haploid gametophyte.  蕨类的生命周期展示了世代交替，在二倍体的孢子体和单倍体的配子体之间交替。|近义词：alternation of generations|反义词：无|名词
