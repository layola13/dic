reapprehending:/ˌriːæprɪˈhɛndɪŋ/| v. |重新逮捕；重新理解；再次担忧|The act of apprehending again; understanding something again; experiencing apprehension again.|例句：The police are reapprehending the escaped prisoner.  警方正在重新逮捕逃犯。

例句：After reviewing the evidence, the detective was reapprehending the entire case.  重新审查证据后，侦探又开始重新审视整个案件。


近义词：rearrest, reconsider, re-evaluate, re-examine|反义词：releasing, freeing, ignoring
