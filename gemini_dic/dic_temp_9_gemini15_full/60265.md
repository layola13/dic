what:/wɒt/ /wʌt/ | pron. | 什么；什么东西；多么；怎么；为什么 |  An interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, actions, reasons, or qualities. |


例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What time is it? = 现在几点了？


近义词：which, that (in certain contexts)


反义词：无


词性：代词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the concert? = 他们在音乐会之前吃了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气将会怎样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do. = 我真希望我知道该做什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built there? = 那里正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are you thinking about? = 你在想什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
She doesn't understand what I mean. = 她不明白我的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's going on? = 发生了什么事？
What if...? = 如果...怎么样？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？
No matter what = 无论如何
