campsite:/ˈkæmpsaɪt/| n.|营地，露营地|A campsite is a place where people can pitch tents and stay overnight, often in a natural setting like a park or forest.  It may offer facilities like toilets and water taps.|例句：We found a lovely campsite by the lake. 我们在湖边找到一个很棒的营地。|例句：The campsite was fully booked for the weekend.  这个营地的周末预订已满。|近义词：campground, camping area|反义词：hotel, motel
