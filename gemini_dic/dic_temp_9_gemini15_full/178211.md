recommended:/ˌrekəˈmɛndɪd/ | adj. | 推荐的，建议的 |  Something that is recommended is suggested as being good or suitable. It implies a positive suggestion or endorsement. | Example: The doctor recommended bed rest.  医生建议卧床休息。

The travel agent recommended a hotel near the beach. 旅行社推荐了一家靠近海滩的酒店。

| Synonyms: suggested, advised, proposed, endorsed, favored | Antonyms: discouraged, forbidden, prohibited |  adj.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor recommends regular exercise. = 医生建议定期锻炼。
This restaurant recommends its seafood dishes. = 这家餐馆推荐它的海鲜菜肴。

一般过去时:
She recommended a good book to me. = 她向我推荐了一本好书。
He recommended that we leave early. = 他建议我们早点离开。

现在完成时:
They have recommended several candidates for the job. = 他们已经推荐了几位候选人。
The committee has recommended changes to the policy. = 委员会已经建议修改该政策。

过去完成时:
The teacher had recommended the student for a scholarship. = 老师曾推荐这位学生获得奖学金。
She had already recommended that plan before the meeting. = 她已经在会议前推荐了那个计划。

将来时:
The committee will recommend a new budget next week. = 委员会下周将推荐一个新的预算。
I will recommend you for that position if you are interested. = 如果你感兴趣，我会向你推荐那个职位。

虚拟语气:
I recommend that he see a doctor immediately. = 我建议他立即去看医生。(动词原形)
It is recommended that we take precautions. = 建议我们采取预防措施。(动词原形)

被动语态:
This book is recommended by many critics. = 这本书受到许多评论家的好评。
The new policy is highly recommended by the board. = 董事会强烈推荐这项新政策。

疑问句:
Is this product recommended for children? = 这个产品推荐给儿童使用吗？
What hotel did they recommend? = 他们推荐了什么酒店？

否定句:
I don't recommend driving in this weather. = 我不建议在这种天气开车。
The doctor did not recommend surgery. = 医生没有建议手术。
