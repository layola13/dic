hymnic:/ˈhɪmnɪk/| adj. |赞美诗般的；颂歌似的；庄严的；崇高的| Relating to or characteristic of hymns; having the qualities of a hymn.  The word evokes a sense of solemnity, reverence, and praise. |例句：The hymnic music filled the cathedral.  赞美诗般的音乐充满了大教堂。

例句：The choir delivered a hymnic rendition of the ancient text. 合唱团用赞美诗般的演唱方式演绎了古老的文本。


近义词：anthemic, liturgical, devotional, sacred, sublime

反义词：profane, irreverent, blasphemous, secular


词源：源自于英语单词"hymn" (赞美诗),  加上后缀"-ic" (形容词后缀), 表示“与…相关的”或“具有…性质的”。  The root traces back to the Greek word "hymnos" (song of praise).
