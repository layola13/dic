debilitates:/dɪˈbɪlɪteɪts/ | v. |使衰弱；使虚弱；削弱 | to weaken someone or something or to make something less effective | The illness debilitated him, leaving him unable to work.  疾病使他虚弱，无法工作。  The long drought has debilitated the crops. 长期干旱使庄稼枯萎。| weakens, enervates, incapacitates | strengthens, invigorates | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The illness debilitates him. = 这疾病使他虚弱。
Stress debilitates many people. = 压力使许多人虚弱。

一般过去时:
The surgery debilitated her for several weeks. = 手术使她虚弱了好几个星期。
The heat debilitated the athletes. = 炎热使运动员们虚弱。

现在完成时:
The long journey has debilitated me. = 漫长的旅程使我虚弱了。
Years of hard work have debilitated him. = 多年的辛勤工作使他衰弱了。

过去完成时:
The previous illness had debilitated him before the accident. = 在事故发生前，之前的疾病已经使他虚弱了。
She had been debilitated by the flu before she took the exam. =  她在考试前就因为流感而虚弱了。

将来时:
The treatment will debilitate him for a short period. =  治疗会使他在短期内虚弱。
Lack of sleep will debilitate your immune system. = 缺乏睡眠会削弱你的免疫系统。

虚拟语气:
If I had not rested, the hard work would have debilitated me. = 如果我没有休息，繁重的工作会使我虚弱。
If she hadn't eaten properly, the illness would have debilitated her further. = 如果她没有好好吃饭，疾病会使她更加虚弱。

被动语态:
He was debilitated by the disease. = 他被疾病弄虚弱了。
The army was debilitated by the long campaign. =  军队因长期作战而元气大伤。

疑问句:
Does the medication debilitate the patient? = 这种药会使病人虚弱吗？
Will the heat debilitate the performance of the athletes? = 炎热会影响运动员的表现吗？

否定句:
The treatment did not debilitate her as much as expected. =  治疗并没有像预期的那样使她虚弱。
The new policy will not debilitate the economy. = 新政策不会削弱经济。


PHRASE->
be debilitated by = 因…而虚弱
severely debilitated =  严重虚弱
debilitating illness =  使人衰弱的疾病
debilitating effect =  削弱作用
physically debilitated = 身体虚弱
