potteringly: /ˈpɒtərɪŋli/ | adv. | 笨手笨脚地；磨磨蹭蹭地；慢吞吞地 |  Acting in a slow, inefficient, and clumsy manner.  It suggests a lack of purpose or direction in one's actions.  |例句：He potteringly arranged the flowers, taking far longer than necessary. 他笨手笨脚地摆弄花，花的时间比必要的多得多。

He potteringly cleaned his room, pausing frequently to check his phone. 他磨磨蹭蹭地打扫房间，经常停下来查看手机。


近义词：dawdlingly, fussily, inefficiently, sluggishly, slowly | 反义词：efficiently, quickly, swiftly, rapidly


词性: 副词
