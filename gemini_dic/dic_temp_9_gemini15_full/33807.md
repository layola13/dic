circumflection:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈflekʃən/ | n. |抑扬顿挫；弯曲；环绕 | The act of bending or curving; a bending or curving; inflection of the voice.  It also refers to a diacritical mark (^) used in writing. |例句：The circumflection of his voice betrayed his nervousness. 他声音的抑扬顿挫暴露了他的紧张。

例句：The architect incorporated a graceful circumflection in the design of the bridge. 建筑师在桥梁设计中融入了优雅的曲线。


近义词：inflection, modulation, curve, bend | 反义词：straightness, directness | 词性：名词


词源：

From Middle English *circumflectioun, from Old French circumflexion, from Latin circumflexio, from circumflectere "to bend around," from circum- "around" + flectere "to bend."  The modern sense relating to accentuation developed later.
