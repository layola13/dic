resubmits:/ˌriːsəbˈmɪts/| v. |重新提交；再次提交 |To submit something again, especially after it has been rejected or found to be unsatisfactory.| The author resubmitted the manuscript after revising it. 作者在修改后重新提交了稿件。|submit, re-present|reject, withdraw|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resubmits his application every week. 他每周都重新提交他的申请。
They resubmit their project proposal regularly. 他们定期重新提交他们的项目提案。

一般过去时:
She resubmitted the report after correcting the errors. 她纠正错误后重新提交了报告。
The company resubmitted the bid after addressing the concerns. 公司在解决相关问题后重新提交了投标书。

现在完成时:
He has resubmitted his application three times. 他已经重新提交了他的申请三次。
They have resubmitted the proposal following the reviewer's suggestions. 他们在审稿人提出建议后重新提交了提案。


过去完成时:
She had resubmitted the document before the deadline.  她在截止日期前重新提交了文件。
They had resubmitted their application several times before it was finally accepted. 他们的申请在最终被接受之前已经重新提交了好几次。


将来时:
He will resubmit the form tomorrow. 他明天将重新提交表格。
They will resubmit their work after the revisions are complete.  他们将在修改完成后重新提交他们的工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the requirements, I would have resubmitted my application properly. 如果我知道要求，我就会正确地重新提交我的申请。
I wish I had resubmitted my work earlier. 我希望我早点重新提交我的工作。


被动语态:
The application was resubmitted by the student.  申请由学生重新提交。
The proposal was resubmitted after several revisions.  提案在经过多次修改后被重新提交。


疑问句:
Has he resubmitted the assignment yet? 他已经重新提交作业了吗？
Will they resubmit the application before the deadline? 他们会在截止日期前重新提交申请吗？


否定句:
He did not resubmit the documents on time. 他没有按时重新提交文件。
She will not resubmit the flawed proposal. 她不会重新提交有缺陷的提案。
