polemics:/pəˈlɛmɪks/| n.|论战；辩论；争论|The art or practice of engaging in debates or controversies, especially on religious or political issues.  It often involves strong and often aggressive arguments.|例句：The article sparked a fierce polemics on social media. 这篇文章引发了社交媒体上激烈的论战。
例句：His polemics against the government's policies were widely publicized. 他反对政府政策的论战被广泛宣传。|近义词：controversy, debate, dispute, argument|反义词：agreement, harmony, consensus|名词
