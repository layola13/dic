mutandis:/mjuːˈtændɪs/| n.| （拉丁语，需作相应更改）| things that need to be changed;  with the necessary changes; with the appropriate modifications.  It is used as an abbreviation, typically in legal or academic writing, of the Latin phrase "ceteris paribus" (all other things being equal),  but implying that some things *must* be changed. |The argument holds true, mutatis mutandis, for other similar cases.  这个论点，在作必要的修改后，也适用于其他类似的情况。|近义词：with modifications, with appropriate changes, with necessary alterations|反义词：unchanged, unaltered
