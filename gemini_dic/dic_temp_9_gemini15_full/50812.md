described:/dɪˈskraɪbd/| v.|描述的，被描述的|To give a detailed account of; narrate or represent.|例句：The witness described the suspect accurately. 目击者准确地描述了嫌疑人。|例句：The painting described a bustling market scene.  这幅画描绘了一个热闹的市场场景。|近义词：portrayed, depicted, recounted, narrated|反义词：ignored, overlooked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He describes his childhood vividly. 他生动地描述了他的童年。
She describes the process in detail. 她详细地描述了这个过程。

一般过去时:
The book described a journey through the Amazon. 这本书描述了一次亚马逊之旅。
The teacher described the experiment clearly. 老师清楚地解释了实验。

现在完成时:
She has described the situation to the police. 她已经向警方描述了情况。
He has described his feelings in his diary. 他在他的日记里描述了他的感受。

过去完成时:
They had described the problem before the meeting. 他们在会议前已经描述了这个问题。
She had described the accident to her parents. 她已经向父母描述了事故。

将来时:
He will describe his experiences in his new book. 他将在他的新书中描述他的经历。
She will describe the painting to the class. 她将向全班同学描述这幅画。

虚拟语气:
If he described the situation honestly, we might understand. 如果他诚实地描述情况，我们或许就能理解。
I wish he had described the incident more clearly. 我希望他更清楚地描述了这件事。

被动语态:
The event was described in the newspaper.  这件事在报纸上被报道了。
The painting is described as a masterpiece. 这幅画被描述为杰作。

疑问句:
How did she describe the attacker? 她是怎么描述袭击者的？
What did he describe in his report? 他的报告中描述了什么？

否定句:
He didn't describe the scene accurately. 他没有准确地描述这个场景。
She didn't describe what happened. 她没有描述发生的事情。


PHRASE->
