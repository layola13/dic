sceptres:/ˈsɛptərz/| n. |权杖；象征统治的标志|A sceptre is a staff or rod carried by a monarch as a symbol of royal authority.  It is often made of precious materials and ornately decorated.|The king held his sceptre high, a symbol of his power and reign. 国王高举权杖，象征着他的权力和统治。| 近义词：staff, rod, wand | 反义词：None
