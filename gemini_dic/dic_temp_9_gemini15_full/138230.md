nosology:/nəˈzɑːlədʒi/| n. |疾病分类学；病理学分类|The scientific classification of diseases.  Nosology involves the systematic arrangement of diseases into categories based on their characteristics, causes, and symptoms. This system helps in diagnosis, treatment, and research. |Example:  The new nosology includes several previously unrecognized viral infections.  新的疾病分类学包括几种以前未被识别的病毒感染。|近义词：classification of diseases, pathology classification |反义词：无
