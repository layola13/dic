lifeblood:/ˈlaɪfblʌd/| n.|生命线；命脉；活力之源|The essential thing that keeps something alive or enables it to function.  It is a metaphor comparing the vital importance of something to the blood that sustains life.|例句：Tourism is the lifeblood of this island. 旅游业是这个岛屿的命脉。

例句：Innovation is the lifeblood of a successful company. 创新是一个成功公司的生命线。


近义词：essential, mainstay, cornerstone, vital force

反义词：death knell, ruin, downfall, destruction


词性:名词
