oologist:/oʊˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |鸟类学家|A zoologist specializing in the study of birds.|The oologist carefully documented the bird's egg.|ornithologist|  (no direct antonym)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He studies bird eggs. = 他研究鸟蛋。
She identifies different species of birds. = 她鉴别不同种类的鸟。


一般过去时:
He collected bird eggs last year. = 他去年收集了鸟蛋。
She identified a rare species of bird. = 她鉴别出一个稀有鸟类物种。


现在完成时:
He has studied bird eggs for many years. = 他研究鸟蛋多年了。
She has identified over a hundred species. = 她已经鉴别了一百多种鸟类。


过去完成时:
He had already collected several rare eggs before the museum called. = 在博物馆打电话之前，他已经收集了好几枚稀有鸟蛋。
She had never seen such a beautiful bird before. = 她以前从未见过这么美丽的鸟。


将来时:
He will present his findings at the conference. = 他将在会议上展示他的研究成果。
She will continue her research next year. = 她明年将继续她的研究。


虚拟语气:
If he were an oologist, he would know more about bird eggs. = 如果他是一位鸟类学家，他会更了解鸟蛋。
I wish I had the opportunity to study birds in the Amazon. = 我希望我有机会在亚马逊研究鸟类。


被动语态:
Bird eggs were collected for research purposes. = 鸟蛋被收集用于研究目的。
A new species was recently identified by the team. = 最近该团队鉴别出一个新物种。


疑问句:
Is he an oologist? = 他是鸟类学家吗？
What kind of birds does she study? = 她研究什么类型的鸟？


否定句:
He is not an oologist; he is an ornithologist. = 他不是鸟类学家；他是一位鸟类学家。(注意：ornithologist 和 oologist 的细微差别)
She did not find any rare birds during her expedition. = 在她的探险中，她没有发现任何稀有鸟类。


PHRASE->
study bird eggs = 研究鸟蛋
identify bird species = 识别鸟类物种
collect bird specimens = 收集鸟类标本
research avian biology = 研究鸟类生物学
document bird habitats = 记录鸟类栖息地
