offices:/ˈɒfɪsɪz/|/ˈɑːfɪsɪz/| n. |办公室；办事处；职务；职责 |The plural form of "office," referring to multiple places of work or multiple official positions. It can also refer to the duties or functions of a particular position.|例句：The company has offices in several major cities. 这家公司在几个主要城市设有办事处。|例句：The duties of the office include managing staff and finances.  该职位的职责包括管理员工和财务。|近义词：workplaces, bureaus, departments |反义词：homes, residences


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company opens its offices at 9 am every day. = 公司每天早上9点开门营业。
Our offices handle a large volume of calls each day. = 我们的办公室每天处理大量的电话。


一般过去时:
The offices closed early yesterday due to the storm. = 由于暴风雨，办公室昨天提前下班了。

现在完成时:

She has held various offices throughout her career. = 她职业生涯中担任过各种职务。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I had a nicer office. = 我希望我有一个更好的办公室。
