cavalierish:/ˌkævəˈlɪəriʃ/| adj. |漫不经心的；轻率的；傲慢无礼的 |disdainfully or arrogantly unconcerned; offhand.  It suggests a lack of proper consideration or respect for others' feelings or circumstances.  |例句：His cavalierish attitude towards safety regulations led to a serious accident. 他对安全规章的漫不经心态度导致了一起严重事故。

例句：She found his cavalierish dismissal of her concerns deeply offensive. 她发现他对她的担忧不屑一顾，深感冒犯。


近义词： nonchalant, disdainful, contemptuous, supercilious, arrogant, flippant

反义词：respectful, considerate, attentive, deferential, humble
