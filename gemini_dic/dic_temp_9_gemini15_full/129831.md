non-hero:/ˈnɑːnˌhɪəroʊ/| adj. n.|非英雄，普通人|A person or character who lacks heroic qualities or is not a hero.  The term often implies a relatable, flawed, or even anti-heroic character. |例句：The story focuses on the non-hero, a seemingly ordinary individual who unexpectedly faces extraordinary circumstances. 故事的焦点是这个非英雄，一个看似普通的人却意外面临着非凡的境遇。|例句：Many modern narratives feature non-heroes, highlighting the complexities and struggles of everyday life. 许多现代叙事都以非英雄为特色，突出了日常生活中的复杂性和奋斗。|近义词：ordinary person, anti-hero, everyman, underdog|反义词：hero, superhero, champion
