decelerating: /dɪˈsɛləreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 减速的；速度减慢的 |  Describes something slowing down or reducing its speed.  | The car was decelerating as it approached the red light.  这辆车在接近红灯时正在减速。 The economy is decelerating. 经济正在减速。| accelerating, speeding up | accelerating, speeding up | 动词
