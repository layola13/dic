ropedancer:/ˈroʊpˌdænsər/ | n. |走钢丝的人，走绳索的人 | A ropedancer is a performer who walks or performs acrobatic feats on a rope or wire, typically high above the ground.  | 例句：The ropedancer balanced precariously on the high wire. 走钢丝的人在高空钢丝上摇摇晃晃地保持平衡。

The ropedancer's daring act left the audience breathless. 走钢丝演员的大胆表演让观众屏住了呼吸。


近义词：tightrope walker, funambulist | 反义词：无


词源: rope (绳索) + dancer (舞蹈演员)  The word is a straightforward compound, combining the noun "rope" indicating the apparatus used and "dancer" describing the performer's activity.  It directly reflects the profession of performing acrobatic feats on a rope.
