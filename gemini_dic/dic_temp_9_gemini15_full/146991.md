/ˈoʊvərˌkɪks/| v. |猛踢；用力踢；踢过头 |To kick with excessive force or to kick something beyond its intended target.  |例句：He overkicked the ball and it went out of bounds. 他用力过猛踢球，球飞出了界外。
He overkicked the fence trying to get the ball. 他试图踢到球，结果用力过猛踢过了栅栏。|近义词：overstrike, miskick |反义词：underkick (not a common word)
