miaow:/mjaʊ/| n., v.|喵喵叫；猫叫声|The sound a cat makes.  A cat's meow can vary in pitch and intensity depending on its mood.|例句：The cat let out a loud miaow.  猫发出一声响亮的喵喵叫。

The kitten miaowed softly for its mother. 小猫轻轻地喵喵叫着找妈妈。|近义词：mew, purr(purring is a different sound, but often associated with contentment, similar to a soft miaow)|反义词:  N/A (no direct antonym)|名词，动词


USAGE->

The cat miaows all night. 猫整夜喵喵叫。
The kitten miaows for food. 小猫喵喵叫着要食物。

The cat miaowed loudly when it saw the mouse. 猫看到老鼠时大声喵喵叫。

The kitten had miaowed for a long time before its mother finally came. 小猫喵喵叫了很久，它妈妈才最终过来。
