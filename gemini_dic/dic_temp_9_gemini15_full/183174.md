repraise:/riːˈpreɪz/| v.|再赞扬，再次称赞|To praise again; to praise repeatedly.|例句：The teacher repraised the student for their exceptional work.  老师再次称赞学生杰出的工作。|例句：He kept repraising her beauty throughout the evening. 他整个晚上都在不断赞美她的美丽。|近义词：commend, applaud, extol|反义词：criticize, condemn, disparage|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repraises her efforts regularly. = 他定期赞扬她的努力。
They repraise the team's performance every week. = 他们每周都赞扬团队的表现。

一般过去时:
She repraised his courage after the incident. = 事故发生后，她再次赞扬了他的勇气。
The judge repraised the lawyer's skillful argument. = 法官再次称赞了律师技巧娴熟的辩论。

现在完成时:
He has repraised her many times for her dedication. = 他多次赞扬她对工作的奉献。
They have repraised the project's success on several occasions. = 他们多次赞扬了项目的成功。

过去完成时:
They had repraised his work before the award ceremony. = 颁奖典礼之前，他们已经赞扬了他的工作。
She had repraised him for his quick thinking several times that day. = 那天她已经几次赞扬他的机智了。

将来时:
He will repraise her achievements at the meeting. = 他将在会议上再次赞扬她的成就。
The manager will repraise the team's efforts tomorrow. =  经理明天将再次赞扬团队的努力。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
