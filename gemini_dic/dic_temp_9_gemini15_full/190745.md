savanna:/səˈvænə/| n. |热带草原；稀树草原|A savanna is a mixed woodland-grassland ecosystem characterized by the trees being sufficiently widely spaced so that the canopy does not close.  The open canopy allows sufficient light to reach the ground to support an unbroken herbaceous layer consisting primarily of grasses.|The African savanna is home to many diverse animals.  非洲大草原是许多不同动物的家园。|The savanna stretches for miles. 热带草原绵延数英里。|grassland, prairie, pampas|desert, forest
