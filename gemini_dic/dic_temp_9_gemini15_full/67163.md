expository:/ɪkˈspɒzɪtəri/ | adj. | 说明性的；解释性的；阐述性的 | Relating to or of the nature of exposition; explanatory.  It aims to inform, explain, describe, or clarify a topic. | Example: The professor delivered an expository lecture on the causes of the American Civil War.  教授做了一场关于美国内战起因的说明性讲座。|近义词：explanatory, descriptive, informative, illustrative |反义词：fictional, narrative, imaginative
