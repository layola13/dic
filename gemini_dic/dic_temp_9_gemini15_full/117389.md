medical:/ˈmɛdɪkəl/ | adj. |医学的；医疗的；医药的 | Relating to the science or practice of medicine.  |例句：He's studying for a medical degree. 他正在攻读医学学位。 |例句：She needs medical attention immediately. 她需要立即进行医疗护理。|近义词：medicinal, therapeutic |反义词：nonmedical
