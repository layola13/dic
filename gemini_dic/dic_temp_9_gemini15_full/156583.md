pickmire:/ˈpɪkmaɪə(r)/| n.|沼泽，泥潭|A pickmire is a type of bog or mire, a wetland characterized by acidic, waterlogged conditions and often covered with sphagnum moss and other bog vegetation.  The term is primarily used in northern England and Scotland.|Example: The sheep sank deep into the pickmire.  羊陷进了沼泽地。|近义词：bog, mire, quagmire, marsh, fen|反义词：dry land, upland
