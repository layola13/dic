festerment:/ˈfɛstərmənt/| n. |腐烂；溃烂；恶化；酝酿的怨恨|The festerment of resentment within the team led to a complete breakdown in communication.  团队内部怨恨的积累导致沟通完全崩溃。|近义词：decay, deterioration, rancor, bitterness |反义词：healing, improvement, reconciliation


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The resentment festers. = 怨恨在滋长。
The wound festers. = 伤口溃烂。


一般过去时:
The anger festered for years. = 怒火积压多年。
The problem festered until it became unmanageable. = 问题恶化到无法控制的地步。


现在完成时:
The situation has festered for too long. = 情况已经恶化太久了。
The wound has festered and needs immediate attention. = 伤口已经溃烂，需要立即处理。


过去完成时:
The conflict had festered for months before it exploded. = 冲突已经酝酿了几个月才爆发。
The grudge had festered in his heart for years. = 他心中的怨恨已经积压多年。


将来时:
This issue will fester if left unaddressed. = 如果不处理这个问题，它将会恶化。
The wound will fester without proper care. = 伤口如果不加适当护理就会溃烂。


虚拟语气:
If we had addressed the issue earlier, it wouldn't have festered. = 如果我们早点处理这个问题，它就不会恶化了。
I wish this problem hadn't festered to this point. = 我真希望这个问题没有恶化到这种程度。


被动语态:
The wound was allowed to fester. = 伤口被任其溃烂。
The problem was allowed to fester, leading to further complications. = 问题被放任不管，导致了进一步的复杂化。


疑问句:
Is the wound festering? = 伤口溃烂了吗？
Is the anger festering inside you? = 你心里是不是积压着怒火？


否定句:
The wound isn't festering. = 伤口没有溃烂。
The problem didn't fester beyond repair. = 问题并没有恶化到无法修复的地步。


PHRASE->
a festering wound = 溃烂的伤口
a festering sore = 溃烂的疮
a festering problem = 一个恶化的问题
a festering resentment = 积压的怨恨
a festering conflict = 恶化的冲突
