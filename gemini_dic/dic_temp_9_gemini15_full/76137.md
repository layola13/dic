fusain:/ˈfjuːzeɪn/| n. |煤炭；炭质煤|A variety of coal that is dull, black, and very soft, composed of compressed plant material. It is often found in thin seams or lenses within other coal types.  It's lightweight and easily crumbles.|The fusain in the sample was easily identifiable due to its characteristically soft texture.|近义词：charcoal, mineral coal |反义词：anthracite (hard coal)
