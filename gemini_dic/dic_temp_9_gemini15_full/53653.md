dischargers:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒərz/| n.|排放者；卸货者；放电器|A discharger is a person or thing that discharges something, such as a substance, energy, or load.  This can refer to various contexts, from someone releasing pollutants to a device releasing electrical charge.|例句：The factory is a major discharger of pollutants into the river.  这家工厂是向河流排放污染物的主要排放者。

The heavy-duty forklift is a powerful discharger of goods from trucks. 这台重型叉车是强大的卡车货物卸货机。


近义词：emitter, releaser, discharger, unloader|反义词：receiver, loader, accumulator|名词
