oxymoronically:/ˌɒksɪməˈrɒnɪkli/ | adv. |矛盾地，自相矛盾地|  In a manner that is oxymoronic; characterized by or containing oxymorons.  It describes something that is self-contradictory or paradoxical. |例句：He oxymoronically described the deafening silence as a "loud hush". 他矛盾地将震耳欲聋的寂静形容为“大声的寂静”。


例句：The politician oxymoronically promised "radical conservatism."  这位政治家矛盾地承诺了“激进的保守主义”。


近义词：paradoxically, contradictorily, incongruously


反义词：consistently, logically, congruously


词源：源自oxymoron + -ically。Oxymoron来自希腊语ὀξύς (oxys, "sharp") + μωρός (mōros, "foolish")，字面意思是“尖锐的愚蠢”，指结合了矛盾概念的修辞手法。-ically是副词后缀。
