panentheist:/ˌpænɪnˈθiːɪst/| n. |泛神论者|A panentheist believes that God is both immanent in the world and transcendent beyond it.  This contrasts with pantheism (God is everything) and theism (God is separate from the world). Panentheism suggests that the world is part of God, but God is also greater than the world.  |Example:  Many modern theologians are exploring panentheistic ideas. 许多现代神学家正在探索泛神论思想。  |近义词：pantheist (partially),|反义词：atheist, deist
