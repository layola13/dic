dynamite:/ˈdaɪnəmaɪt/| n. |炸药；极具爆炸性的事物|A powerful explosive made of nitroglycerin absorbed in an inert substance such as kieselguhr.  It is used in mining, quarrying, and demolition.  The word also figuratively refers to something extremely powerful or impactful. |例句：The miners used dynamite to blast through the rock.  矿工们用炸药炸开岩石。

例句：His speech was dynamite; it completely changed the course of the debate. 他的演讲极具爆炸性；它彻底改变了辩论的进程。|近义词：explosive, blasting agent|反义词：None specifically, but  "safe," "calm," or "unexplosive" could be considered antonyms in a figurative sense.
