multilamellous: /ˌmʌltiˈlæmələs/ | adj. |多层片的；多层板的 | Having or composed of many lamellae (thin plates or layers).  This term is primarily used in biology and materials science to describe structures with numerous thin, parallel layers. |

例句：

The multilamellous structure of the bone provided exceptional strength and resilience. = 骨骼的多层片结构提供了非凡的强度和韧性。

The onion's multilamellous bulb is a classic example of this type of plant structure. = 洋葱的多层鳞茎是这种植物结构的经典例子。


词源:

* **multi-**:  Latin prefix meaning "many" or "much".
* **lamella**: Latin word meaning "thin plate" or "layer".
* **-ous**:  English suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of" or "having the nature of".


近义词：

stratified, layered, laminated, foliated


反义词：

unilamellar (having a single lamella), homogeneous (uniform in structure)


词性：

形容词
