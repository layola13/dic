goodnight:/ˈɡʊdnaɪt/| int.|晚安，祝你晚安|An expression used to say goodbye to someone at night, wishing them a good night's sleep.|例句：Goodnight, sleep tight. 晚安，睡个好觉。|近义词：good evening, farewell (at night)|反义词:good morning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We say "goodnight" every night. = 我们每晚都说“晚安”。
He usually says goodnight to his children before bed. = 他通常在睡觉前对孩子们说晚安。


一般过去时:
I said goodnight to my parents last night. = 我昨晚向父母道了晚安。
She said goodnight and went to bed. = 她说了晚安就上床睡觉了。


现在完成时:
Have you said goodnight to your sister? = 你跟你姐姐道晚安了吗？
We haven't said goodnight yet. = 我们还没说晚安呢。


过去完成时:
They had already said goodnight before the power went out. = 电力中断之前，他们已经道过晚安了。
Had you said goodnight to your grandmother before leaving? = 你离开之前有没有向你祖母道晚安？


将来时:
I will say goodnight to him before I leave. = 我离开之前会跟他道晚安。
We will all say goodnight after the movie. = 电影结束后我们都会道晚安。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would say goodnight and go to sleep. = 如果我是你，我会道晚安然后去睡觉。
I wish I had said goodnight to her before she left. = 我真希望在她离开之前跟她道过晚安。


被动语态:
Goodnight is often said before bedtime. = 晚安通常在睡前说。
Goodnight was said, and then the lights were turned off. = 晚安说了之后，灯就关掉了。


疑问句:
Have you said goodnight to everyone? = 你跟每个人都道晚安了吗？
Didn't you say goodnight to your mother? = 你难道没有跟你母亲道晚安吗？


否定句:
I didn't say goodnight last night. = 我昨晚没说晚安。
He didn't say goodnight to her before leaving. = 他离开前没有跟她道晚安。
