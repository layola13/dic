pupilage:/ˈpjuːpɪlɪdʒ/ | n. |学生身份；受教育时期；学徒期 |The state or period of being a pupil; apprenticeship.  Pupilage refers to the time spent as a pupil under the tutelage of a teacher or master, often implying a period of learning and development within a formal or informal educational setting. This can range from childhood schooling to apprenticeships in a trade. | Example: Her pupilage under the renowned sculptor shaped her artistic style.  她的学徒生涯在著名雕塑家的指导下塑造了她的艺术风格。 |近义词：apprenticeship, studentship, tutelage |反义词：mastership, teaching
