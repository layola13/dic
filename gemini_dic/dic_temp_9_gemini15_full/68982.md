fatigue:/fəˈtiːɡ/|/fəˈtiːɡ/| n. adj. v.|疲劳；疲惫；使疲劳|Extreme tiredness, typically resulting from mental or physical exertion or illness.|The marathon runner was suffering from fatigue.  马拉松运动员感到疲惫不堪。|She felt a deep fatigue after a long day's work. 她一天工作下来感到非常疲惫。|tiredness, exhaustion, weariness|energy, vigor|noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I feel fatigue after a long journey. = 长途旅行后我感到疲惫。
Fatigue is a common symptom of many illnesses. = 疲劳是许多疾病的常见症状。


一般过去时:
He suffered from fatigue after the intense workout. = 剧烈运动后他感到疲惫。
The soldiers experienced extreme fatigue during the battle. = 士兵们在战斗中经历了极度的疲劳。


现在完成时:
I have experienced fatigue for several weeks now. = 我已经疲惫了好几个星期了。
She has felt persistent fatigue despite getting enough sleep. = 尽管睡得足够，她仍然感到持续的疲劳。


过去完成时:
He had been experiencing fatigue for months before he sought medical help. = 在他寻求医疗帮助之前，他已经疲惫了好几个月了。
They had felt intense fatigue after the long hike. = 长途跋涉后他们感到极度疲劳。


将来时:
I will probably feel fatigue after the upcoming marathon. = 我在即将到来的马拉松比赛后可能会感到疲劳。
She will likely experience fatigue if she doesn't get enough rest. = 如果她休息不足，她可能会感到疲劳。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't worked so hard, I wouldn't feel such fatigue. = 如果我不那么努力工作，我就不会感到这么疲劳。
I wish I didn't feel this fatigue. = 我希望我不感到这么疲劳。


被动语态:
His fatigue was caused by lack of sleep. = 他的疲劳是由睡眠不足引起的。
The athletes were overcome by fatigue at the end of the race. = 运动员们在比赛结束时被疲劳击垮了。


疑问句:
Are you experiencing fatigue? = 你感到疲劳吗？
What causes your fatigue? = 什么导致你的疲劳？


否定句:
I don't feel any fatigue today. = 我今天没有感到任何疲劳。
He didn't show any signs of fatigue during the race. = 他在比赛中没有表现出任何疲劳的迹象。
