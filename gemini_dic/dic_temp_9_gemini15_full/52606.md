digester:/daɪˈdʒɛstər/| n. |消化器；消化装置；分解器| A digester is a device or system used to break down organic matter, often through biological or chemical processes.  It can refer to a part of the body (like the stomach) or an industrial apparatus. For example, in wastewater treatment, a digester breaks down sludge to reduce its volume and produce biogas. |例句：The anaerobic digester efficiently breaks down the organic waste.  厌氧消化器有效地分解了有机废物。

The sewage treatment plant uses a large digester to process sludge. 污水处理厂使用大型消化器处理污泥。


近义词：fermenter, decomposer, reactor |反义词：none.
