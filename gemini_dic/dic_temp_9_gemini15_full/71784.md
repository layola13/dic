flecking:/ˈflekɪŋ/| n. |斑点；点缀；少量 |The act of sprinkling or scattering small particles or spots;  small spots or particles scattered on a surface.  |例句：The painting showed flecking of gold throughout. 这幅画上遍布金色的斑点。|例句：There was a flecking of snow on the ground. 地面上有一些雪花。|近义词：speckling, spotting, mottling |反义词：uniformity, homogeneity

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist is flecking the canvas with gold paint. =艺术家正在用金色颜料点缀画布。
The fabric shows flecking of different colors. = 这块布料显示出不同的颜色斑点。


一般过去时:
He flecked the wall with paint. = 他用油漆在墙上点缀。
She flecked the cake with chocolate. = 她用巧克力点缀蛋糕。


现在完成时:
