comfrey:/ˈkɒmfriː/ /ˈkʌmfriː/| n. |紫草|A genus of coarse herbs of the borage family, having rough leaves and bell-shaped flowers; used medicinally.  Comfrey is known for its purported ability to promote healing of wounds and broken bones.|例句：The herbalist recommended comfrey ointment for his injured knee.  草药师建议他用紫草软膏治疗受伤的膝盖。|例句：Comfrey root has been used traditionally to treat sprains and bruises. 紫草根传统上被用来治疗扭伤和瘀伤。|近义词：symphytum (学名)|反义词:|词性:n.
