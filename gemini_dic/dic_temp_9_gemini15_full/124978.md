muggiest:/ˈmʌɡiɪst/| adj.|最潮湿的，最闷热的 | Most humid or oppressive; extremely hot and humid.  The air is thick, heavy, and unpleasant. |例句：It was the muggiest day of the summer.  / 那是夏天最闷热的一天。

例句：The muggiest weather often precedes a thunderstorm. / 最闷热的天气通常预示着雷雨的到来。


近义词：sultry, sweltering, clammy, humid, oppressive


反义词：dry, crisp, cool, refreshing


词性：形容词
