defacement:/dɪˈfeɪsmənt/| n. |毁损；污损；玷污 |The act of damaging or spoiling the surface of something, especially a building or painting.  It can also refer to damaging someone's reputation or character. |例句：The defacement of the ancient temple was a great loss to cultural heritage. 古庙的毁坏是对文化遗产的巨大损失。

The defacement of his reputation followed the scandal. 丑闻之后，他的名誉受损。|近义词：damage, destruction, marring, disfigurement, vandalism |反义词：restoration, preservation, enhancement
