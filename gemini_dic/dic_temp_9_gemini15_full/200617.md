slavish:/ˈsleɪvɪʃ/| adj.|奴性的；盲从的；缺乏独创性的|Characterized by or exhibiting slavish devotion or obedience; lacking originality or independence of thought.|His slavish imitation of other artists' styles prevented him from developing his own unique voice. 他对其他艺术家风格的盲目模仿阻止了他发展自己独特的风格。| servile, obsequious, unoriginal | independent, original, creative | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He shows a slavish devotion to his boss. = 他对老板表现出奴性的奉献。
Her slavish adherence to the rules is stifling. = 她对规则的盲目遵守令人窒息。


一般过去时:
She followed his instructions slavishly. = 她盲目地遵循了他的指示。
The artist's work was slavishly copied. = 艺术家的作品被照搬复制。


现在完成时:
He has been slavishly devoted to his work for years. = 多年来，他一直对工作非常投入（以至于缺乏独立思考）。
The company has slavishly followed market trends. = 这家公司盲目地追随市场趋势。


过去完成时:
She had slavishly obeyed her parents all her life. = 她一生都盲目地服从父母。
They had slavishly adhered to the outdated methods. = 他们一直坚持过时的老方法。


将来时:
He will slavishly follow whatever the leader says. = 他将盲目地服从领导所说的一切。
The company will likely slavishly imitate its competitor's products. = 这家公司很可能会盲目模仿其竞争对手的产品。


虚拟语气:
If he were not so slavishly devoted, he might have a better life. = 如果他不那么盲目地奉献，他可能会过上更好的生活。
I wish I hadn't been so slavish in my imitation of their style. = 我真希望我没有那么盲目地模仿他们的风格。


被动语态:
His ideas were slavishly copied by many. = 他的想法被许多人盲目地抄袭。
The design was slavishly imitated by competitors. = 该设计被竞争对手盲目地模仿。


疑问句:
Is his approach to the problem too slavish? = 他解决问题的方法是不是太盲目了？
Was the translation slavishly literal? = 翻译是不是过于死板？


否定句:
His work is not slavish; it's quite original. = 他的作品并非盲目模仿，而是相当有创意的。
The adaptation was not slavish; it had its own unique interpretation. = 这部改编作品并非照搬原著，它有自己独特的诠释。


PHRASE->
Slavish imitation = 盲目模仿
Slavish adherence to = 盲目遵守
Slavish devotion to = 盲目奉献
Slavish copying = 盲目抄袭
Slavish translation = 死板的翻译


词源:

源自古英语 *slaf, 指的是“奴隶”。  其形容词形式发展出“奴性的”，“盲从的”等含义。
