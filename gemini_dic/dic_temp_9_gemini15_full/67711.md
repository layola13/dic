exungulate: /ɪkˈsʌŋɡjuleɪt/ | vt. | 除去蹄子；使失去蹄子 | To deprive of hooves; to remove the hooves from.  The word implies a violent or forceful removal. | 例句：The hunters exungulated the deer after killing it. = 猎人杀死鹿后去掉了它的蹄子。  The butcher exungulated the animal before processing it. = 屠夫在加工动物之前去掉了它的蹄子。 | 近义词：hoofless, unhoofed (as adjectives describing the resulting state) | 反义词：hoofed

They exungulate the cattle for easier transport. = 他们为了方便运输而除去牛的蹄子。
