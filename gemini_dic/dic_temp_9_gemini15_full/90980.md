hurt: /hɜːrt/|ˈhɝːt/| v. n. adj.|受伤；痛苦；伤害；使…痛苦| to cause physical pain or injury to someone or something; to cause emotional pain or distress. |近义词：injure, wound, pain, harm|反义词：heal, soothe, comfort


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cut on my finger hurts. = 我手指上的伤口很痛。
This headache hurts badly. = 这个头痛很严重。

一般过去时:
I hurt my knee playing football. = 我踢足球时伤了膝盖。
She hurt his feelings with her words. = 她的话伤了他的感情。

现在完成时:
I've hurt my back lifting that box. = 我搬那个箱子伤了背。
He has hurt her deeply with his actions. = 他的行为深深地伤害了她。

过去完成时:
I had hurt my ankle before the race. = 我比赛前扭伤了脚踝。
She had hurt her pride by failing the exam. = 她考试不及格伤了自尊心。

将来时:
I'm afraid I'll hurt myself if I try that. = 我担心如果我尝试那样我会受伤。
He thinks he will hurt her feelings if he tells her the truth. = 他认为如果告诉她真相会伤她的感情。

虚拟语气:
If I had known it would hurt, I wouldn't have done it. = 如果我知道会疼，我就不会做了。
I wish I hadn't hurt his feelings. = 我真希望我没有伤他的感情。

被动语态:
His feelings were hurt by her careless words. = 他被她粗心的话语伤害了感情。
My leg was hurt in the accident. = 我的腿在事故中受伤了。

疑问句:
Does this cut hurt? = 这个伤口疼吗？
Did that comment hurt your feelings? = 那句话伤你的感情了吗？

否定句:
This doesn't hurt at all. = 这完全不疼。
His words didn't hurt me as much as I thought they would. = 他的话并没有像我想象的那样伤到我。


PHRASE->

hurt like hell = 痛得要命
