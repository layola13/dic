ect:/ɛkt/|abbr.| etcetera|等等，诸如此类|et cetera is an abbreviation of the Latin phrase "et cetera," meaning "and the rest" or "and so on." It's used to indicate that a list of items is not exhaustive.|Examples:  We need apples, bananas, oranges, ect.我们需要苹果，香蕉，橘子等等。  He studied math, science, history, ect. 他学习了数学、科学、历史等等。|近义词：and so on, and others, and the like|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The list includes apples, oranges, and ect. = 列表包括苹果，橘子等等。
He studies history, geography, and ect. = 他学习历史，地理等等。


一般过去时:
She bought milk, bread, and ect. = 她买了牛奶，面包等等。
They discussed politics, economics, and ect. = 他们讨论了政治，经济等等。


现在完成时:
He has collected stamps, coins, and ect. = 他收集了邮票，硬币等等。
She has visited Paris, London, and ect. = 她参观了巴黎，伦敦等等。


过去完成时:
They had eaten pizza, pasta, and ect. = 他们已经吃了披萨，意大利面等等。
She had read novels, poems, and ect. = 她已经读过小说，诗歌等等。


将来时:
We will visit Rome, Florence, and ect. = 我们将参观罗马，佛罗伦萨等等。
He will study physics, chemistry, and ect. = 他将学习物理，化学等等。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would study art, music, and ect. = 如果我有更多时间，我会学习艺术，音乐等等。
I wish I knew more about history, literature, and ect. = 我希望我对历史，文学等等了解更多。


被动语态:
The report covered politics, economics, and ect. = 报告涵盖了政治，经济等等。
The course included math, science, and ect. = 这门课程包括数学，科学等等。


疑问句:
What else did you buy besides milk, bread, and ect.? = 除了牛奶、面包等等，你还买了什么？
What subjects did he study besides math, science, and ect.? = 除了数学、科学等等，他还学习了什么科目？


否定句:
The list does not include grapes, pears, and ect. = 列表不包括葡萄，梨等等。
He did not study French, Spanish, and ect. = 他没有学习法语，西班牙语等等。


PHRASE->
ect. and so on = 等等以及其他
and ect. = 以及等等
etc. and the rest = 等等和其余
amongst ect. = 在……之中等等
with ect. = 与等等
