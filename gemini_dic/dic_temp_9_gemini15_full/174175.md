queens:/kwɪnz/ | n. | 女王们；王后们 |  The word "queens" refers to multiple female monarchs or rulers.  It can also refer to a group of women who are considered queens in a metaphorical sense (e.g., "the queens of pop"). | Example 1: The queens of England have a long and fascinating history.  英格兰的女王们拥有悠久而迷人的历史。| Example 2:  She and her friends considered themselves the queens of the dance floor. 她和她的朋友们认为自己是舞池的女王们。 |近义词: princesses, sovereign women | 反义词:  kings (in the context of rulers)
