revisions:/rɪˈvɪʒənz/ | n. |修订，修改，校订 | Changes or modifications made to something.  The process of making such changes. |例句：The author made several revisions to the manuscript before publication. 作者在出版前对稿件做了几次修改。|例句：The revisions to the contract were significant. 合同的修改很重要。|近义词：amendments, corrections, alterations |反义词：originals, first drafts


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes revisions to his work daily. = 他每天都修改他的作品。
She regularly revises her lesson plans. = 她定期修改她的教学计划。


一般过去时:
They revised the document before submitting it. = 他们提交文件之前修改了文件。
The editor revised the article to improve clarity. = 编辑修改了文章以提高清晰度。


现在完成时:
I have revised the essay three times. = 我已经修改了这篇文章三次。
She has revised her opinion on the matter. = 她已经修改了她对这件事的看法。


过去完成时:
He had revised the chapter before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经修改了那一章。
They had revised the strategy several times before implementing it. = 他们在实施策略之前已经修改了策略好几次。


将来时:
We will revise the budget next week. = 我们下周将修改预算。
She will revise her presentation before giving it. = 她将在演讲之前修改她的演示文稿。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would revise my paper again. = 如果我有更多时间，我会再次修改我的论文。
It would be helpful if they revised their approach. = 如果他们修改他们的方法，将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The document was revised by the committee. = 文件由委员会修改。
The plan is being revised at the moment. = 计划正在修改中。


疑问句:
Have you revised your work yet? = 你修改你的作品了吗？
Will the proposal be revised before the meeting? = 提案在会议前会修改吗？


否定句:
He did not revise his essay before handing it in. = 他在交卷之前没有修改他的论文。
They haven't revised the policy yet. = 他们还没有修改政策。


PHRASE->
major revisions = 重大修改
minor revisions = 小修改
under revision = 正在修改中
subject to revision = 可修改的
call for revisions = 要求修改
