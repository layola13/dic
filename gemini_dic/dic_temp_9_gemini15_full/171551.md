pull-myself-together: /pʊl maɪˈsɛlf təˈɡɛðər/ | v. | 振作起来；恢复镇静 | To regain control of one's emotions and composure after a shock, disappointment, or other upsetting experience.  | 例句：She took a deep breath and tried to pull herself together. 她深吸一口气，努力让自己平静下来。


例句：After hearing the bad news, he needed some time to pull himself together before he could speak. 听到坏消息后，他需要一些时间来让自己平静下来才能说话。


近义词：compose oneself, regain composure, recover one's composure, pull oneself together, get a grip, steady oneself


反义词：lose control, fall apart, break down, panic


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pull myself together every morning before work. = 我每天早上上班前都会让自己振作起来。
She pulls herself together quickly after a setback. = 她遇到挫折后能很快振作起来。


一般过去时:
He pulled himself together and delivered a powerful speech. = 他振作精神，发表了一篇精彩的演讲。
She pulled herself together after the accident. = 事故发生后，她很快振作了起来。


现在完成时:
I have pulled myself together after that difficult period. = 我在那段艰难时期后已经振作起来了。
She has pulled herself together and is ready to face the challenge. = 她已经振作起来，准备迎接挑战。


过去完成时:
He had pulled himself together by the time the meeting started. = 到会议开始时，他已经振作起来了。
She had pulled herself together before she spoke to the press. = 在她对媒体发表讲话之前，她已经镇定下来了。


将来时:
