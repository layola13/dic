non-solvency:/ˌnɒnˈsɒlvənsi/| n. |无力偿债；资不抵债|The inability of a company or individual to pay their debts.  This often leads to bankruptcy proceedings. |例句：The company's non-solvency forced it to file for bankruptcy.  这家公司的无力偿债迫使其申请破产。|例句：The farmer's non-solvency was due to a series of poor harvests.  这位农民的资不抵债是由于连续几季歉收造成的。|近义词：insolvency, bankruptcy, default|反义词：solvency
