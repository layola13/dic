reinquire:/ˌriːɪnˈkwaɪər/| vt.|再次询问，重新查询|To inquire again; to make a further inquiry.|例句：I reinquired about the status of my order. 我再次询问了我的订单状态。|例句：He decided to reinquire with the bank about the loan. 他决定向银行再次询问贷款事宜。|近义词：reask, inquire again, check again|反义词：ignore, disregard
