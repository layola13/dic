mooters:/ˈmuːtərz/| n.|辩论者，讨论者|Those who engage in moot court or similar debating exercises.  Mooters are often law students who participate in simulated court proceedings.|例句：The mooters presented compelling arguments during the mock trial.  辩论者在模拟审判中提出了令人信服的论点。

例句：The team of mooters prepared meticulously for the upcoming competition. 这支辩论队为即将到来的比赛做了精心的准备。


近义词：debaters, arguers, discussants
反义词：listeners, observers
词性:名词
