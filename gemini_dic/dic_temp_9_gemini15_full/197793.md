例句：He got a shiner in the fight. 他在打斗中被打出了黑眼圈。


近义词：sparkler, black eye, bruise,  excellent, outstanding|反义词：dull, mediocre, poor


词源:源自shine的派生词，表示闪光或发亮的东西。  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Germanic *skeinan, meaning "to shine."
