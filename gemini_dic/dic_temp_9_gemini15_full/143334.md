outedging:/ˌaʊtˈɛdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 超越，胜过 | To surpass or exceed in some quality, achievement, or performance.  | 例句：The new software outedges its predecessor in speed and efficiency.  新软件在速度和效率方面超越了其前身。 | 例句：Our team outedged the competition with a stunning display of teamwork. 我们的团队以令人惊艳的团队合作表现胜过竞争对手。 | 近义词：outperform, surpass, exceed, outstrip | 反义词：underperform, fall short of, lag behind | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new model outedges the old one in fuel efficiency. = 新车型在燃油效率方面胜过旧车型。
This strategy outedges all others in terms of profitability. = 就盈利能力而言，此策略胜过所有其他策略。

一般过去时:
Last year's sales figures outedged our projections. = 去年的销售额超过了我们的预期。
Their performance outedged all expectations. = 他们的表现超出了所有预期。

现在完成时:
Our company has outedged its competitors in market share. = 我们公司在市场份额方面已超越竞争对手。
The team has outedged all previous records. = 该团队已经超越了所有之前的记录。

过去完成时:

They had outedged their opponents in every aspect of the game. = 他们在比赛的各个方面都超过了对手。

将来时:
We expect our new product to outedge the competition. = 我们预计我们的新产品将胜过竞争对手。
With this new technology, we will outedge our rivals. = 凭借这项新技术，我们将超越我们的竞争对手。

虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, we might have outedged our competitors. = 如果我们投资更多，我们可能会超越竞争对手。
I wish our team had outedged the others in the final match. = 我希望我们的团队在决赛中能够胜过其他队伍。

被动语态:
The previous record was outedged by a young athlete. = 之前的记录被一位年轻运动员打破了。
Our previous design was outedged by the latest model. = 我们之前的设计被最新型号超越了。

否定句:

Their efforts did not outedge the challenges they faced. = 他们的努力并没有克服他们面临的挑战。
