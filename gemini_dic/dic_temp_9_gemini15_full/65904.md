ewallet:/ˈiːwɒlɪt/ | n. |电子钱包| An e-wallet is a digital wallet that allows users to make online transactions.  It typically stores payment information, such as credit card details or bank account information, allowing for quick and easy online purchases.  Some e-wallets also offer additional features, such as loyalty programs and peer-to-peer payment options. |例句：I paid for the groceries using my e-wallet. 我用电子钱包支付了杂货费用。

例句：Many online retailers now accept payments via e-wallets. 许多在线零售商现在接受电子钱包支付。


近义词：digital wallet, online wallet, mobile payment system


反义词：physical wallet, cash


词性:名词
