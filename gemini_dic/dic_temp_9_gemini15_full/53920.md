discourse:/ˈdɪskɔːrs/| n. |论述；谈话；演讲|A formal discussion or debate.  It can also refer to written or spoken communication or conversation.|例句：The professor's discourse on the French Revolution was fascinating. 教授关于法国大革命的论述非常引人入胜。

例句：Their discourse covered a wide range of topics. 他们的谈话涵盖了广泛的主题。

近义词：conversation, discussion, speech, lecture, address|反义词：silence, muteness

词源：From Old French *discours, from Latin discursus, past participle of discurrere "to run to and fro," from dis- "apart" + currere "to run."  The original sense implied a running back and forth in thought or argument.
