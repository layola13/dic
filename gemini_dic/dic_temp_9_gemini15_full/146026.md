overdramatizing:/ˌoʊvərˈdræməˌtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |夸大其词，小题大做 | Exaggerating the significance or seriousness of something.  |例句：He's overdramatizing the situation; it's not as bad as he's making it out to be. 他在夸大事态；情况并没有他说的那么糟糕。

例句：Stop overdramatizing your minor inconvenience; many people face much worse problems. 别把你的小麻烦说得那么严重；很多人面临着更糟糕的问题。


近义词：exaggerating, overstating, sensationalizing, inflating


反义词：downplaying, understating, minimizing


词性：动词

词源:
The word "overdramatizing" is a combination of "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and "dramatizing" (from "drama," ultimately from the Greek word "drama," meaning "action" or "deed").  Therefore, it literally means "to dramatize excessively."
