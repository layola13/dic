calliope:/ˈkæliˌoʊpi/| n. |卡里奥佩（一种乐器）,缪斯女神|A calliope is a musical instrument that uses steam-powered whistles to produce sound.  It is often associated with steam-powered fairground rides and circuses. The name comes from the Greek Muse of epic poetry, Calliope.|例句：The calliope's cheerful melodies filled the air at the carnival. 卡里奥佩欢快的旋律充满了嘉年华的空气。

The calliope's shrill whistles announced the arrival of the circus parade. 卡里奥佩尖锐的汽笛声宣告了马戏游行的到来。|近义词：steam organ, band organ|反义词：无|名词
