disbelieving:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvɪŋ/| adj. |不相信的，怀疑的 |Expressing or showing disbelief; skeptical.|He gave her a disbelieving stare. 他给了她一个怀疑的目光。  She listened with a disbelieving expression on her face. 她带着不相信的表情听着。|近义词：skeptical, incredulous, doubtful |反义词：believing, trusting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is disbelieving everything she says. 他不相信她说的任何话。
They are disbelieving the evidence presented. 他们不相信所提供的证据。


一般过去时:
I was disbelieving when I heard the news. 我听到这个消息时很怀疑。
She was disbelieving of his sudden wealth. 她不相信他突然暴富。


现在完成时:
He has been disbelieving her stories for years. 多年来，他一直不相信她的故事。
We have been disbelieving the government's claims. 我们一直不相信政府的说法。


过去完成时:
I had been disbelieving his promises before he broke them. 在他食言之前，我一直不相信他的承诺。
They had been disbelieving the rumors for months. 他们几个月来一直不相信这些谣言。


将来时:
She will be disbelieving the results until she sees them herself. 在她亲眼看到结果之前，她会不相信。
They will be disbelieving the forecast unless it rains. 除非下雨，否则他们会不相信预报。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I'd be disbelieving his excuses. 如果我是你，我会不相信他的借口。
I wish I weren't so disbelieving of his intentions. 我希望我不那么怀疑他的意图。


被动语态:
His claims were disbelieved by many. 他的说法被许多人所不相信。
The theory is disbelieved by the scientific community. 科学界不相信这个理论。


疑问句:
Are you disbelieving what I'm saying? 你不相信我说的话吗？
Is he disbelieving the evidence? 他不相信证据吗？


否定句:
I am not disbelieving everything you say. 我不是不相信你说的所有话。
She is not disbelieving the possibility. 她不否定这种可能性。
