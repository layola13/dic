onsite:/ˈɑːnsaɪt/ | adj. |现场的；在现场的 | located or occurring at the place where something else is happening or being done.  |例句：The onsite training was very effective. 现场培训非常有效。

例句：We need an onsite engineer to fix the server. 我们需要一名现场工程师来修理服务器。


近义词：on-site, in situ, on the premises | 反义词：offsite, remote
