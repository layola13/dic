plaything:/ˈpleɪθɪŋ/| n. |玩具；玩物；消遣|A plaything is something, typically a toy, that is used for amusement or play, especially by a child. It can also refer to a person or thing that is treated as insignificant or easily manipulated.  |例句：The child's favorite plaything was a stuffed bear.  孩子最喜欢的玩具是一只毛绒熊。|例句：He saw her as a mere plaything, someone to be used and discarded. 他把她视为一个玩物，一个可以利用和抛弃的人。|近义词：toy, trifle, amusement, bauble|反义词：serious matter, responsibility
