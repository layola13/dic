nah:/nɑː/| int.| 不，别，才不 |An informal way of saying "no" or expressing disagreement.  It's often used in casual conversation. |例句： "Want to go out tonight?" "Nah, I'm tired."  今晚想出去吗？“不，我累了。”|近义词：no, nope|反义词：yes, yeah


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't conjugate like verbs or nouns.  It doesn't have tenses or grammatical forms.  It simply expresses a negative sentiment.  Therefore, the grammatical categories below are not applicable.


PHRASE->
Nah, man. = 不，伙计。
Nah, I'm good. = 不，我很好。(拒绝好意)
Nah, I'm busy. = 不，我很忙。(拒绝邀请)
Nah, forget it. = 不，算了吧。
Nah, I don't think so. = 不，我不这么认为。
