scentlessness:/ˈsɛntləsnəs/| n. |无气味；无香味；没有香味|The absence of scent or smell; lack of fragrance.  It describes something that doesn't have a discernible odor.  |例句：The scentlessness of the air was unsettling. 空气的无味令人不安。

The scentlessness of the room suggested a lack of recent activity.  房间里没有气味，这表明最近没有人活动。|近义词：odorlessness, fragrance-free, unscented|反义词：fragrance, aroma, scent
