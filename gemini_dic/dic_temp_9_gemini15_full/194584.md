self-subsistence:/ˌsɛlf səbˈsɪstəns/ | n. |自给自足；自立 | The ability to provide for one's own needs without external assistance.  It implies independence and self-reliance in meeting essential requirements like food, shelter, and clothing. | Example: The remote village achieved self-subsistence through sustainable farming practices.  这个偏远村庄通过可持续的农业实践实现了自给自足。 The family strived for self-subsistence, growing their own vegetables and raising chickens.  这家人努力争取自给自足，自己种植蔬菜，饲养鸡。|近义词：self-sufficiency, independence, autonomy |反义词：dependence, reliance

词源:
self- :  来自古英语 *self,  意思是"自己"
subsistence: 来自古法语 *subsistence,  来自拉丁语 *subsistere,  意思是"存在"，由 *sub- (在下面) + *sistere (站立)构成。  因此，subsistence 的本意是"维持生存"。
