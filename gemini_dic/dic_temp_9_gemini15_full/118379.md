merle:/ˈmɜːrl/| adj.|  杂色；斑驳的 |  Describing a pattern of coloration in animals, particularly in dogs and birds, characterized by a mottled or irregular mixture of colors, typically black, gray, and brown.  |  The dog had a beautiful merle coat.  这只狗有一件美丽的杂色外套。  The bird's feathers showed a striking merle pattern.  这只鸟的羽毛呈现出醒目的杂色图案。 | dappled, mottled, piebald | solid-colored | 形容词
