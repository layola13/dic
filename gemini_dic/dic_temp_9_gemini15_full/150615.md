papeterie:/ˌpæpəˈtɛəri/| n. |文具店；精美的文具|A shop selling stationery, especially high-quality or elegant stationery.  It often implies a more upscale selection than a typical stationery store. |The boutique papeterie offered a stunning array of personalized notecards and exquisite writing paper.这家精品文具店提供种类繁多的个性化贺卡和精美的信纸。|stationery store, writing supplies shop|  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with a generic "store")|名词

词源:
The word "papeterie" is derived from the French word "papier," meaning "paper."  The suffix "-erie" indicates a place where something is made, sold, or kept.  Therefore, "papeterie" literally translates to "paper place" or "place of paper."
