hermitical:/hərˈmɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 隐居的；遁世的；孤寂的 | Relating to or characteristic of a hermit; solitary; secluded.  | 例句：He led a hermitical life in the mountains, far from any human contact. 他在远离任何人类接触的山区过着隐居的生活。

He preferred a hermitical existence to the hustle and bustle of city life. 他更喜欢隐居的生活，而不是城市喧嚣的生活。


近义词：reclusive, solitary, secluded, isolated | 反义词：gregarious, sociable, outgoing


词源:  From Middle English *hermytical, from Old French hermitique, from Late Latin hermiticus, from Greek hermētikós, from Hermēs, the Greek god associated with magic and mystery, later used to refer to hermits who often practiced alchemy and other mystical practices.  The association stemmed from the secretive and solitary nature of their lives.
