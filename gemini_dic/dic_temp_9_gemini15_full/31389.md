charge-with:/tʃɑːrdʒ wɪð/ | vt. |指控；使承担；充电；装满| to accuse someone formally of committing a crime; to give someone a task or responsibility; to fill something with electricity; to fill something completely| 例句：The police charged him with murder. 警方指控他犯有谋杀罪。 They charged me with organizing the event. 他们让我负责组织这次活动。  |近义词：accuse, indict, entrust, load, fill |反义词：acquit, exonerate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police charge him with theft every year. = 警方每年都指控他犯有盗窃罪。
They charge her with the responsibility of managing the team. = 他们让她负责管理团队。


一般过去时:
