orpheus:/ˈɔːrfiːəs/| n.|俄耳甫斯，奥菲斯|The name Orpheus refers to a legendary musician and poet in Greek mythology, renowned for his ability to charm animals and even move inanimate objects with his music. He is best known for his attempt to retrieve his wife Eurydice from the Underworld.  His story is a powerful symbol of love, loss, and the power of art.|例句：The opera is based on the myth of Orpheus and Eurydice. 这部歌剧是根据俄耳甫斯和欧律狄刻的神话改编的。|近义词：musician, poet, singer|反义词: N/A
