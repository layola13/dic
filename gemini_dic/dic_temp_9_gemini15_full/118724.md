metagenetic:/ˌmɛtədʒəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|世代交替的；间代遗传的 | Relating to or characterized by metagenesis, a type of life cycle in which an organism alternates between sexual and asexual reproduction.  It often refers to the alternation of generations in plants, where a diploid sporophyte generation alternates with a haploid gametophyte generation. | Example: The fern exhibits a metagenetic life cycle, alternating between sporophyte and gametophyte phases.  蕨类植物表现出世代交替的生命周期，在孢子体和配子体阶段之间交替。|近义词：alternating, cyclical |反义词：monogenetic
