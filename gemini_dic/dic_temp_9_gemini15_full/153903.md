percussive: /pərˈkʌsɪv/ | adj. | 打击乐的；撞击的；有冲击力的 | Relating to or produced by percussion; characterized by sharp, short, and usually repeated sounds.  | 例句：The music had a strong percussive beat. 这音乐有强烈的打击乐节奏。

例句：The percussive sounds of the thunderstorm echoed through the valley. 雷雨的阵阵冲击声回荡在山谷中。


近义词：rhythmic, striking, pounding, resonant


反义词：gentle, soft, subdued, quiet


词性：形容词
