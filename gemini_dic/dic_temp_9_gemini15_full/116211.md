masochistic:/ˌmæsəˈkɪstɪk/| adj.| 受虐狂的；自虐的 | Enjoying activities that cause pain or discomfort.  Characterized by or deriving gratification from the infliction of pain on oneself. |例句：His masochistic tendencies led him to seek out increasingly painful experiences.  他的受虐倾向使他寻求越来越痛苦的经历。

例句：She found a strange, masochistic pleasure in cleaning the house all day. 她发现整天打扫房子有一种奇怪的、自虐式的乐趣。


近义词：self-punishing, self-flagellating, self-destructive


反义词：sadistic


词源:The term is derived from the name of Leopold von Sacher-Masoch (1836-1895), an Austrian novelist whose works featured characters who derived pleasure from suffering.  The term was coined in the late 19th century.
