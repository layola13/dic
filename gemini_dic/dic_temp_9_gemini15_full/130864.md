The term often implies a lack of structure, organization, or design, suggesting something haphazard or unplanned. |  The novel's plot felt nonarchitectonic, a rambling collection of loosely connected events.  The nonarchitectonic arrangement of the furniture made the room feel cluttered. |近义词: unstructured, disorganized, formless, chaotic |反义词: architectonic, structured, organized, well-designed
