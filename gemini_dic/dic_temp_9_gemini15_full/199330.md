simians:/ˈsɪmiənz/| n.|类人猿；猿猴|A simian is an ape or monkey.  Simians are characterized by their relatively large brains, five-fingered hands, and five-toed feet, and generally arboreal lifestyles. The term encompasses a wide range of species, including both Old World and New World monkeys, apes, and humans.|例句：Orangutans, chimpanzees, and gorillas are all simians.  = 猩猩、黑猩猩和大猩猩都是类人猿。


Many different simian species inhabit the rainforests of the Amazon. = 许多不同的类人猿物种栖息在亚马逊雨林中。|近义词：apes, monkeys, primates|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with other mammals.
