noblewoman:/ˈnoʊblˌwʊmən/| n. |贵族女子，贵妇|A noblewoman is a woman of noble birth or rank, typically possessing a title of nobility.  She often enjoys privileges and social standing associated with her family's aristocratic lineage.|例句：The noblewoman hosted a lavish ball in her grand estate.  这位贵族女子在她的庄园里举办了一场奢华的舞会。|例句：She was a noblewoman known for her philanthropy and kindness. 她是一位以慈善和仁爱而闻名的贵族女子。|近义词：lady of title, aristocrat (female), peeress|反义词：commoner, peasant
