geminations:/ˌdʒɛmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 双音节词；重叠；成对出现 | The repetition of a sound, syllable, or word.  It is often used in linguistics to describe the repetition of a phoneme, especially a consonant, immediately adjacent to itself within a word.  | Example: The word "bookkeeper" contains geminations of the /k/ sound.  书记员这个词包含/k/音的双音节词。|近义词：reduplication, repetition | 反义词：None (反义词不适用) | 名词
