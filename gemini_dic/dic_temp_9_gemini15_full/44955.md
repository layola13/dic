cryptovolcanic:/ˌkrɪptoʊvɒlˈkænɪk/| adj.|隐火山(的);隐爆火山(的)|Relating to or denoting volcanic activity that occurs beneath the surface of the earth, often resulting in uplift or deformation of the ground without eruption.  It describes geological processes where magma interacts with the crust without reaching the surface as a visible volcanic eruption.|例句：The researchers attributed the unusual geological formations to cryptovolcanic activity. 研究人员将这些不同寻常的地质构造归因于隐火山活动。

The slow uplift of the landmass was likely caused by a series of cryptovolcanic intrusions.  陆地缓慢隆起很可能是由一系列隐火山侵入造成的。|近义词：subvolcanic, subterranean volcanic|反义词：extrusive volcanic, effusive volcanic|
