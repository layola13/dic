plagiotropic:/ˌplæʒiˈɒtrɒpɪk/| adj.|偏上地生的；横生的；斜向生长的|Growing or extending obliquely or horizontally, not vertically.  The term is often used to describe the growth habit of certain plants, particularly in response to environmental factors like light or gravity.|例句：The branches of this tree exhibit a plagiotropic growth habit, spreading horizontally rather than growing upwards. 这棵树的树枝表现出偏上地生的生长习性，水平扩展而不是向上生长。


例句：The roots of this plant show a strong plagiotropic tendency, growing parallel to the surface.  这株植物的根系表现出很强的横向生长趋势，平行于地表生长。


近义词：horizontal, oblique, decumbent |反义词：orthotropic, erect, vertical
