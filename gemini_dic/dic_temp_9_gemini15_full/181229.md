reinsisted:/ˌriːɪnˈsɪstɪd/| v.|再坚持，再次坚持|To insist again or repeatedly; to reiterate one's insistence.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning again) to the verb "insisted."|例句：He reinsisted on his innocence despite the overwhelming evidence against him.  他尽管面对压倒性的证据，还是再次坚持自己的清白。


He reinsisted that the meeting be postponed. 他再次坚持会议延期。

近义词：persist, reiterate, reaffirm|反义词：concede, yield, acquiesce|动词
