centralizations: /ˌsɛntrəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 集中化；中央集权化 | The process of concentrating power or authority in a central location or authority.  It often refers to the consolidation of functions or resources under a single administration. | 例句：The centralizations of government power led to a more efficient bureaucracy.  政府权力的集中导致了更高效的官僚机构。

例句：The company's recent centralizations of its operations resulted in significant cost savings.  公司最近对其业务的集中化带来了显著的成本节约。


近义词：consolidation, concentration, unification


反义词：decentralization, dispersal, fragmentation


词性：名词
