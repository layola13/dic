chinatown:/ˈtʃaɪnəˌtaʊn/| n. |唐人街|A district in a city, especially in Western countries, where a large number of Chinese people live and work.  It often features Chinese businesses, restaurants, and cultural institutions.|例句：We spent the afternoon exploring Chinatown, sampling delicious dumplings and browsing unique shops. 我们下午花时间探索了唐人街，品尝了美味的饺子，浏览了独特的商店。|例句：The Chinatown in San Francisco is one of the oldest and most famous in North America. 旧金山的唐人街是北美最古老、最著名的唐人街之一。|近义词：Chinese quarter|反义词：无
