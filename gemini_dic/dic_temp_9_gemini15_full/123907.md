more:/mɔːr/| adj., pron., adv.|更多的；更加；此外|  More information is needed.  需要更多信息。  |近义词：additional, further, extra |反义词：less, fewer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are more apples than oranges. = 苹果比橘子多。
He needs more time to finish the project. = 他需要更多时间来完成项目。

一般过去时:
There were more people at the party last night. = 昨晚派对上有更多的人。
She wanted more cake after dinner. = 她晚饭后想要更多蛋糕。

现在完成时:
We have had more rain this year than last year. = 今年的雨比去年多。
She has earned more money this month. = 她这个月赚的钱更多了。

过去完成时:
They had read more books before the exam. = 考试前他们读过更多的书。
He had spent more money than he had planned. = 他花了比计划更多的钱。

将来时:
We will have more free time next year. = 明年我们将有更多空闲时间。
They will need more help with the project.  他们将需要更多帮助来完成项目。

虚拟语气:
I wish I had more time. = 我希望我有更多时间。
If I had more money, I would travel more. = 如果我有更多钱，我会多旅行。

被动语态:
More attention should be paid to environmental protection. = 应该更多地关注环境保护。
More details will be provided later.  稍后将提供更多细节。

疑问句:
Do you want more coffee? = 你想再来点咖啡吗？
Is there more to this story? = 这个故事还有更多吗？

否定句:
I don't want any more. = 我不想再要了。
There aren't more seats available. = 没有更多的座位了。


PHRASE->
more or less = 或多或少
more than = 多于
no more = 不再
once more = 再一次
the more...the more... = 越…越…
