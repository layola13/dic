ready:/ˈredi/ | adj. |准备好的，愿意的，随时可以的 |Ready means prepared and willing to do something. It can also describe something that is in a state of being prepared for use or action.  |例句：Are you ready to go? 你准备好了吗？

The cake is ready to be served. 蛋糕准备好了可以上桌了。|近义词：prepared, willing, set |反义词：unprepared, unwilling, reluctant |形容词
