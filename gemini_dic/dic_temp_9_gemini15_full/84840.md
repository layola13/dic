hang-over:/ˈhæŋˌoʊvər/| n.|宿醉；后遗症|The unpleasant physical effects experienced after drinking too much alcohol, such as headache, nausea, and dizziness.|I had a terrible hangover after the party last night.  昨晚派对后我宿醉得很厉害。|The project left a hangover of unfinished work. 这个项目留下了一堆未完成的工作。| headache, aftereffects | sober, recovered | 名词

词源:
The word "hangover" originally referred to something that remains after something else has ended.  The alcoholic meaning developed later.  The etymology is linked to the verb "hang" and the sense of something "hanging around" or lingering.
