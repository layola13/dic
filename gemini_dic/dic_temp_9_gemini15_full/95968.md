inculpatory: /ɪnˈkʌlpəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 归罪的；可归罪的 | tending to inculpate or accuse; implying guilt.  | The inculpatory evidence was enough to convict him.  证据足以定他的罪。 | incriminating, accusatory, condemnatory | exculpatory, exonerating | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence seems inculpatory. = 证据看起来是定罪的。
This statement is inculpatory to the suspect. = 这份声明对嫌疑人来说是具有指控性的。


一般过去时:
The lawyer presented inculpatory testimony. = 律师提供了具有指控性的证词。
The judge found the evidence inculpatory. = 法官认定证据具有指控性。


现在完成时:
The investigation has yielded inculpatory findings. = 调查已经产生了定罪的发现。
The prosecution has presented much inculpatory material. = 控方已经提交了许多具有指控性的材料。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If the evidence were more inculpatory, the defendant would be convicted. = 如果证据更有指控性，被告就会被定罪。

被动语态:
