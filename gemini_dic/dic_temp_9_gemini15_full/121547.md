mismeasure:/ˌmɪsˈmɛʒər/| vt. n.| 测量错误；误判；错误估计 | To measure incorrectly; to judge or assess wrongly.  |例句：The architect mismeasured the room, leading to design flaws. 建筑师测量房间时出错，导致设计缺陷。  The government's mismeasure of public opinion led to a surprising election result. 政府对公众舆论的错误判断导致了令人惊讶的选举结果。|近义词：miscalculate, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate |反义词：accurately measure, correctly assess
