hinds:/haɪndz/| n.|母鹿；鹿群；后面部分；臀部|The plural of hind.  Refers to female deer, a group of deer, or the rear part of something.|例句：The hunter spotted a herd of hinds grazing peacefully in the meadow.  猎人看见一群母鹿在草地上安静地吃草。

例句：The car's rear hinds were damaged in the accident.  汽车的后部在事故中受损。|近义词：doe (for female deer), rear, buttocks|反义词：fore, front
