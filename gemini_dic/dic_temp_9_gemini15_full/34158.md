clambake:/ˈklæmˌbeɪk/| n. |蛤蜊派对；海滩野餐|A clambake is a social gathering, typically outdoors, where clams are cooked over an open fire, often with other seafood and vegetables.  It's a traditional New England event, often associated with summer and celebrations.|例句：We're having a clambake on the beach this weekend.  我们这个周末在海滩上举行蛤蜊派对。


例句：The family reunion included a lively clambake with music and dancing.  家庭聚会包括一个热闹的蛤蜊派对，有音乐和舞蹈。|近义词：beach party, barbecue, picnic|反义词：indoor party, formal dinner|名词
