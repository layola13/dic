nondirigible:/ˌnɒnˈdɪrɪdʒəbəl/ | adj. | 不可操纵的；非飞艇的 | Not capable of being steered or directed; not a dirigible.  This word typically refers to aircraft that cannot be steered, especially in contrast to dirigibles (airships). | Example: The small, experimental aircraft was nondirigible, relying entirely on wind currents.  这架小型实验飞机是不可操纵的，完全依靠风力。 | 近义词: unsteerable, uncontrollable | 反义词: dirigible, steerable
