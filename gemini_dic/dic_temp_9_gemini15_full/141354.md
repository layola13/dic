operated:/ˈɒpəreɪtɪd/ /ˈɑːpəreɪtɪd/| v.|操作；运转；经营；动手术|To control the functioning of a machine or process.|The engineer operated the complex machinery with ease. 工程师轻松地操作了复杂的机器。|The factory operated around the clock. 工厂昼夜不停地运转。|近义词：run, manage, function, perform|反义词：idle, cease, stop|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine operates smoothly. 这台机器运转平稳。
He operates a successful business. 他经营着一家成功的企业。


一般过去时:
They operated on the patient yesterday. 他们昨天为病人做了手术。
The company operated at a loss last year. 公司去年亏损经营。


现在完成时:
We have operated this system for five years. 我们已经运行这个系统五年了。
She has operated on hundreds of patients. 她已经为数百名病人做过手术。


过去完成时:
The factory had operated for decades before it closed.  这家工厂关闭前已经运营了几十年。
The surgeon had already operated when the emergency occurred.  紧急情况发生时，外科医生已经动手术了。


将来时:
They will operate the new equipment next week. 他们下周将操作新设备。
The hospital will operate a new wing next year. 医院明年将启用新的病房。


虚拟语气:
If I operated the machine correctly, it would work. 如果我正确操作机器，它就会工作。
I wish I had operated more carefully. 我真希望我操作更仔细一些。


被动语态:
The machine was operated by a skilled technician. 这台机器由一名熟练的技术员操作。
The company is operated by a family. 这家公司由一个家族经营。


疑问句:
Does this machine operate automatically? 这台机器是自动操作的吗？
Who operated the crane? 谁操作的起重机？


否定句:
The machine didn't operate correctly. 机器没有正确运转。
He didn't operate the equipment properly. 他没有正确操作设备。


PHRASE->
operate on (动手术): The doctor operated on the patient.医生给病人动手术了。
operate a business (经营企业): He operates a successful business. 他经营一家成功的企业。
operate a machine (操作机器):  She operates the heavy machinery skillfully. 她熟练地操作重型机械。
operate under (在...下运作): The company operates under strict guidelines. 公司在严格的指导方针下运作。
operate in (在...活动/运作): The company operates in multiple countries. 这家公司在多个国家开展业务。
