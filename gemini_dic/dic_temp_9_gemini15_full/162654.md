preascertain:/ˌpriːəˈsɜːrtn/| vt.|预先确定；预先查明|To find out beforehand; to ascertain in advance.|例句：The committee will preascertain the feasibility of the project before making a final decision. 委员会将在做出最终决定之前预先确定项目的可行性。

例句：The detective tried to preascertain the killer's motive before arresting him.  侦探试图在逮捕凶手之前预先查明其作案动机。


近义词：determine beforehand, predetermine, ascertain in advance


反义词：neglect to ascertain, overlook


词性：动词
