non-proprietary: /ˌnɑːn prəˈpraɪəˌteri/ | adj. | 非专有的；公用的 | Not owned or controlled by a private person or company; available for general use.  |  The software is non-proprietary and can be freely distributed.  该软件是非专有的，可以自由分发。|近义词：public, open-source, common, generic |反义词：proprietary, patented, exclusive


USAGE->

This software is non-proprietary. = 这个软件是非专有的。
The recipe is non-proprietary information. = 这个食谱是非专有的信息。


一般过去时:
The formula was non-proprietary until the patent was granted. = 在专利授予之前，该配方是非专有的。

现在完成时:
The company has made its operating system non-proprietary. = 该公司已将其操作系统设置为非专有的。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
