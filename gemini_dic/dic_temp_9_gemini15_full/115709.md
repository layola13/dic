margining: /ˈmɑːrdʒɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 保证金交易；保证金；保证金业务 | The act of providing or maintaining a margin, especially in financial transactions.  It refers to the process of securing a transaction, often involving borrowing money to purchase assets.  | 例句：The broker requires margining for all leveraged trades. = 经纪人要求所有杠杆交易都需提供保证金。


The bank is investigating irregularities in its margining practices. = 银行正在调查其保证金业务中的违规行为。


近义词：hedging, securing, collateralizing | 反义词：unsecured, uncovered


词性:动词
