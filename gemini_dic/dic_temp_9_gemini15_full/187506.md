rolloff:/ˈroʊlɔːf/ | n. |滚落；逐渐减少；（录音等的）衰减 | A gradual decrease or decline; the process of something gradually decreasing or disappearing.  It can also refer to the point at which a signal or effect diminishes to a negligible level. |例句：The rolloff of the bass frequencies was noticeable. 低频的衰减很明显。


The rolloff of support for the candidate was dramatic after the scandal.  丑闻之后，对这位候选人的支持急剧下降。|近义词：decline, decrease, fade-out, attenuation |反义词：increase, rise, surge
