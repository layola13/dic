finding's:/ˈfaɪndɪŋz/| n.|发现；调查结果；调查结果|The plural form of "finding," referring to the results of an inquiry, investigation, or research.  It often refers to conclusions or discoveries made after a process of study or analysis. |例句：The finding's of the study showed a strong correlation between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究的结果显示吸烟与肺癌之间有很强的相关性。

例句：The jury's finding's were released to the public yesterday. 陪审团的调查结果昨天公布了。
