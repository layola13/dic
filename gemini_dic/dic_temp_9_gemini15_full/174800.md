racecard:/ˈreɪskɑːrd/| n. |种族牌；种族歧视的借口|A racecard is an accusation that someone is using someone's race to their advantage, or is prejudiced against them because of their race.  It's often used to dismiss or discredit someone's argument or actions by suggesting their views are driven by racism, either real or perceived.  The term implies a manipulative or cynical use of racial issues.|Example: He played the race card to deflect criticism of his policies. 他打出种族牌来转移对其政策的批评。|近义词：racial slur, prejudice, discrimination|反义词：racial equality, fairness, unbiased opinion|名词
