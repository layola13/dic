persuaded:/pərˈsweɪdɪd/| v.| 说服；劝服 | To have convinced someone to do something or believe something. | 例句：He persuaded me to go to the party. 他说服我去参加派对。

例句：She persuaded her parents to let her go to the concert. 她劝说父母让她去听音乐会。


近义词：convinced, influenced, induced | 反义词：dissuaded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He persuades her easily. 他很容易说服她。
She persuades many customers every day. 她每天说服许多顾客。

一般过去时:
They persuaded him to change his mind. 他们说服他改变主意。
I persuaded my friend to join the club. 我说服我的朋友加入俱乐部。

现在完成时:
I have persuaded him to accept the offer. 我已经说服他接受这个提议。
She has persuaded many people to donate. 她已经说服许多人捐款。

过去完成时:
She had persuaded him to invest before the market crashed.  她在市场崩盘前已经说服他投资。
We had persuaded them to stay, but they left anyway. 我们已经说服他们留下来，但他们还是离开了。


将来时:
I will persuade him to cooperate. 我将会说服他合作。
We will persuade the authorities to change the policy. 我们将说服当局改变政策。

虚拟语气:
If I could persuade him, I would. 如果我能说服他，我会的。
I wish I could persuade her to stay. 我希望我能说服她留下。

被动语态:
He was persuaded to join the army. 他被说服参军。
The audience was persuaded by her speech. 听众被她的演讲所打动。

疑问句:
Can you persuade her to come? 你能说服她来吗？
Will they be persuaded to participate? 他们会被说服参与吗？

否定句:
I couldn't persuade him to help. 我无法说服他帮忙。
They didn't persuade the manager to approve the project. 他们没有说服经理批准这个项目。


PHRASE->
persuade someone to do something  说服某人做某事
persuade someone of something  使某人相信某事
persuade someone into doing something  诱导某人做某事
be persuaded to do something 被说服做某事
persuade someone out of something 说服某人放弃某事

词源:
From Old French persuader, from Latin persuadere "to persuade," from per- "through, thoroughly" + suadere "to advise, urge."
