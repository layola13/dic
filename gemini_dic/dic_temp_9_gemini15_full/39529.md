conquerors:/ˈkɒŋkərərz/|/ˈkɑːŋkərərz/| n. |征服者，胜利者|People or groups who have successfully taken control of a place or people by force.  Conquerors often impose their own culture, laws, and systems of governance on the conquered. |例句：The Roman conquerors built roads and aqueducts throughout their empire.  罗马征服者在他们的帝国各地修建了道路和渡槽。
The Mongol conquerors spread their influence across a vast territory.  蒙古征服者将他们的影响力扩展到广阔的领土上。|近义词：victors, vanquishers, subjugators |反义词：conquered, defeated, vanquished
