paddocked:/ˈpædəkt/| v.|  围在围场里；把…关进围场 | to confine or keep (an animal) in a paddock  |例句：The horses were paddocked for the night.  马儿们晚上被关进了围场。 |例句：The farmer paddocked his sheep before the storm.  暴风雨来临前，农夫把羊关进了围场。|近义词：enclose, confine, pen|反义词：release, free


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The farmer paddocks his sheep every evening. = 农夫每天晚上都把羊关进围场。
They paddock their horses before the race. = 他们在比赛前把马关进围场。


一般过去时:
He paddocked the cattle after milking them. = 他挤完奶后把牛关进了围场。
We paddocked the dog in the garden. = 我们把狗关在了花园里的围场里。


现在完成时:
They have paddocked the horses since yesterday. = 他们从昨天起就把马关进了围场。
The farmer has paddocked all his animals for the night. = 农夫已经把所有的动物关进围场过夜了。


过去完成时:
They had paddocked the sheep before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把羊关进了围场。
The horses had been paddocked for hours before the vet arrived. = 兽医到达之前，马匹已经在围场里待了好几个小时了。


将来时:
He will paddock the cows tomorrow morning. = 他明天早上会把奶牛关进围场。
We will paddock the goats in the new enclosure. = 我们会把山羊关进新的围场。


虚拟语气:
If I had a paddock, I would paddock my chickens. = 如果我有一个围场，我会把我的鸡关进去。
I wish I could paddock the noisy dogs next door. = 我真希望我能把隔壁那些吵闹的狗关进围场。


被动语态:
The sheep were paddocked for their safety. = 为了安全起见，羊被关进了围场。
The horses are being paddocked until the race begins. = 马匹在比赛开始前一直被关在围场里。


疑问句:
Are the horses paddocked yet? = 马匹已经被关进围场了吗？
Were the cows paddocked before dark? = 奶牛在黑暗来临之前就被关进围场了吗？


否定句:
The farmer didn't paddock the pigs last night. = 农夫昨晚没有把猪关进围场。
They haven't paddocked the sheep yet. = 他们还没有把羊关进围场。
