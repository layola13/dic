flaunt:/flɔːnt/| /flɑːnt/| vt.|炫耀，夸示|To show off something in a proud or boastful way.  |例句：He flaunted his wealth by driving a expensive sports car. 他开着一辆昂贵的跑车炫耀他的财富。|例句：She flaunted her new diamond ring to everyone. 她向每个人炫耀她新的钻戒。|近义词：show off, display, boast, parade|反义词：hide, conceal, suppress
