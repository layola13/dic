idiolects:/ˈɪdiəlekt/| n.|个人语言习惯，个人方言|The way a person speaks, including their vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation, which is unique to them.  It's influenced by factors like region, social group, and personal experiences.  |例句：Linguists are interested in studying the idiolects of different individuals to understand language variation.  语言学家们对研究不同个人的个人语言习惯以了解语言变异很感兴趣。|近义词：personal speech, individual dialect|反义词：standard language, common dialect
