pant:/pænt/| /pɑːnt/| v. n.|喘气，气喘吁吁；（裤子）|To breathe rapidly and heavily, usually because of exertion or excitement;  a type of trousers.|例句：He panted after running a mile. 他跑了一英里后气喘吁吁。|例句：She wore elegant pants to the party. 她穿着优雅的裤子去参加派对。|近义词：gasp, puff, breathe heavily| trousers, breeches|反义词：breathe easily


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pants heavily after exercise. = 他锻炼后呼吸急促。
They pant with excitement. = 他们兴奋地喘着粗气。


一般过去时:
The dog panted in the heat. = 狗在热天里喘着粗气。
She panted as she climbed the stairs. = 她爬楼梯时气喘吁吁。


现在完成时:
I have panted all the way up the hill. = 我一路爬到山顶都气喘吁吁。
He has panted for breath since the race. = 自从比赛以来，他一直喘不过气来。


过去完成时:
He had panted for hours before he finally reached the summit. = 他在最终到达山顶之前已经喘了好几个小时了。
They had panted all day in the hot sun. = 他们在炎热的阳光下喘了一整天。


将来时:
I will pant if I run that far. = 如果我跑那么远，我会喘不过气。
She will pant with exhaustion after the marathon. = 马拉松过后，她会累得气喘吁吁。


虚拟语气:
If I ran a marathon, I would pant. = 如果我跑马拉松，我会气喘吁吁。
I wish I hadn't run so fast; I wouldn't pant so much now. = 我真希望我没有跑那么快；我现在就不会喘得这么厉害了。


被动语态:
(The verb "pant" is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Are you panting? = 你喘气吗？
Why is the dog panting so much? = 为什么狗喘得这么厉害？


否定句:
He didn't pant during the walk. = 他散步时没有喘气。
She didn't pant at all, despite the exertion. = 尽管很费力，她一点也没喘。
