hutchunsonian: /ˌhʌtʃənˈsoʊniən/ | adj. | 哈奇森式的 | Relating to or characteristic of Hutchinsonian views, particularly in biology and ecology.  Specifically referring to the ecological ideas of G. Evelyn Hutchinson, often concerning niche theory and the diversity of life. | The hutchunsonian niche concept emphasizes the multidimensional nature of an organism's environmental requirements.  | 近义词: ecological, niche-based | 反义词:  N/A (Not a directly opposite concept, but opposing theories could be mentioned like those emphasizing competition over niche partitioning) |  adj.
