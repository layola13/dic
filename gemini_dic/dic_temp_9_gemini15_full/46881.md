daring:/ˈdɛərɪŋ/| adj.| 大胆的；勇敢的；冒险的 |  Showing willingness to take risks; adventurous.  It implies a certain excitement and a disregard for potential danger or disapproval. |例句：He made a daring escape from prison. 他大胆地越狱了。

例句：Her daring fashion choices always turn heads. 她大胆的时尚选择总是引人注目。


近义词：bold, brave, audacious, adventurous, intrepid | 反义词：timid, cautious, cowardly, fearful


词源:  The word "daring" comes from the Old English word "darian," meaning "to dare."  This, in turn, has roots in Proto-Germanic and beyond, connecting to the concept of challenging or defying.
