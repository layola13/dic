nontaxability: /ˌnɒnˌtæksəˈbɪləti/ | n. |非课税性，免税性 | The state or quality of being exempt from taxation.  It refers to the characteristic of something not being subject to tax.  This can apply to income, goods, services, or other items. | Example: The nontaxability of municipal bonds is a major advantage for investors.  市政债券的免税性对投资者来说是一个主要优势。 |近义词：tax exemption, immunity from taxation | 反义词：taxability, liability to taxation
