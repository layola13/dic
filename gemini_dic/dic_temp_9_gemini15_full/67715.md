exuperate:/ˈɛksjuːpəreɪt/| vt. |使…升华；使…纯净；使…精炼|To purify, refine, or exalt something, often metaphorically.  The process often implies a removal of impurities or imperfections, leading to a higher state or more refined form. |例句：The artist sought to exuperate his craft through years of dedicated practice. = 艺术家试图通过多年的刻苦练习来提升他的技艺。

The sculptor exuperated the rough stone into a masterpiece. = 雕塑家将粗糙的石头升华为杰作。


近义词：refine, purify, elevate, sublimate|反义词：debase, degrade, corrupt, taint|动词
