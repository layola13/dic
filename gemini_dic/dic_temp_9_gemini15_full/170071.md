protestation:/ˌprɒtɛsˈteɪʃən/ | n. |抗议；声明；断言 |A strong expression of disagreement or disapproval.  A solemn declaration or assertion. |例句：His protestation of innocence was unconvincing.  他的无辜声明令人难以信服。

例句：The workers staged a protestation against the unfair dismissal. 工人们举行抗议活动，反对不公平的解雇。


近义词：objection, complaint, denial, affirmation, assertion

反义词：acceptance, approval, confirmation


词源:  From Middle French *protestation, from Latin protestatio, from protestari "to bear witness," from pro- "forth" + testari "to bear witness," from testis "witness."
