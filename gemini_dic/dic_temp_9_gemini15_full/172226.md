pushers:/ˈpʊʃərz/| n.| 推销员；贩毒者；推动器 |A person who pushes something, especially drugs or a particular product aggressively.  It can refer to someone who promotes or sells illegal drugs, or someone who aggressively promotes a product or idea.|例句：The police arrested several drug pushers in the raid.  警方在突击搜查中逮捕了几名贩毒者。

例句：He's a pushers of new technology, always eager to adopt the latest gadgets. 他是新技术的推销员，总是渴望采用最新的电子产品。


近义词：dealer, trafficker, promoter, salesman

反义词：consumer, buyer
