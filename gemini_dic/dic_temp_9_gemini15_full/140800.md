on-board:/ˌɒnˈbɔːd/ /ˌɑːnˈbɔːrd/| adj.|在船上的；在飞机上的；被雇用的；|已安装的；已加入的|On-board means something is present or happening within a vehicle, such as a ship, plane, or train. It can also refer to someone who has been hired or something that has been installed or integrated. |例句：The on-board computer system malfunctioned.  机载电脑系统出现故障。

The on-board catering service was excellent.  机上餐饮服务很棒。

|近义词：aboard, installed, employed, integrated|反义词：off-board, disembarked, uninstalled|形容词
