slogan:/ˈsloʊɡən/| n. |标语，口号 |A slogan is a short, memorable phrase used in advertising or politics to promote a product, idea, or cause.  It aims to be catchy and easily remembered. |Example: Nike's slogan is "Just Do It." 耐克的口号是“只管去做”。|近义词：mottos, catchphrases, taglines |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company uses a new slogan this year. = 今年公司使用了一个新的口号。
What is the slogan of your school? = 你学校的校训是什么？


一般过去时:
Their slogan was very effective. = 他们的口号非常有效。
The company changed its slogan last month. = 公司上个月更换了口号。


现在完成时:
They have used that slogan for many years. = 他们已经使用那个口号很多年了。
The campaign has had a successful slogan. = 这场运动有一个成功的口号。


过去完成时:
They had already chosen a slogan before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经选好了口号。
The company had used a different slogan previously. = 公司以前用过不同的口号。


将来时:
We will launch a new slogan next week. = 我们下周将推出一个新的口号。
What slogan will they use for the election? = 他们将在选举中使用什么口号？


虚拟语气:
If they had a better slogan, they would sell more products. = 如果他们有一个更好的口号，他们就能卖出更多产品。
I wish they would choose a more inspiring slogan. = 我希望他们能选择一个更鼓舞人心的口号。


被动语态:
The slogan was designed by a famous advertising agency. = 这个口号是由一家著名的广告公司设计的。
A new slogan is being considered by the marketing team. = 营销团队正在考虑一个新的口号。


疑问句:
What is their campaign slogan? = 他们的竞选口号是什么？
Does the slogan accurately reflect the product? = 这个口号是否准确地反映了产品？


否定句:

The company doesn't want to use a negative slogan. = 公司不想使用负面的口号。
