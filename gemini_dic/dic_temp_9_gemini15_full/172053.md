puritanlike: /ˈpjʊərɪtənlaɪk/ | adj. | 像清教徒一样的；严谨的；禁欲的 | Resembling or characteristic of the Puritans; austere and strict in moral and religious matters.  |  例句：His puritanlike lifestyle was marked by abstinence from alcohol and other pleasures. 他像清教徒一样的生活方式以戒酒和其他享乐为特征。

例句：The puritanlike atmosphere of the school discouraged any form of fun or entertainment. 学校清教徒般的气氛使任何形式的娱乐活动都受到抑制。


近义词：ascetic, austere, strict, rigid, moralistic

反义词：lax, permissive, indulgent, hedonistic
