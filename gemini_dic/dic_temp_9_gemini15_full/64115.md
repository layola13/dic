eps:/ɛps/| n.|每股收益|Earnings Per Share, a company's profit divided by the number of outstanding shares.  It's a key indicator of a company's profitability.|Example: The company's EPS increased by 10% last quarter.  这家公司的每股收益在上个季度增长了10%。|近义词：earnings per share, profit per share|反义词：N/A|名词
