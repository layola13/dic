non-literate:/ˌnɑːnˈlɪtərət/| adj. | 不识字的，文盲的 | Unable to read or write.  This term refers to individuals lacking the ability to read and write, often due to lack of education or opportunity.  It is important to use this term sensitively, acknowledging the complex social and economic factors that contribute to illiteracy. | Example:  The non-literate population in that region is surprisingly high.  那个地区的文盲人口出奇地高。 The program aims to help non-literate adults learn to read and write. 该项目旨在帮助不识字的成年人学习阅读和写作。| 近义词：illiterate, unlettered | 反义词：literate
