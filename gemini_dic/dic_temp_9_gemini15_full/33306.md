chucking:/ˈtʃʌkɪŋ/| v.|抛掷；扔；丢弃；放弃；解雇 | To throw something carelessly or forcefully.  It can also refer to abandoning something or someone, or dismissing someone from a job. | 例句：He was chucking stones at the birds. 他正朝鸟儿扔石头。 | 例句：She chucked her job to travel the world. 她辞掉工作去环游世界。| 近义词：throwing, tossing, flinging, discarding, dismissing | 反义词：catching, keeping, retaining, employing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He chucks the ball to his friend. = 他把球扔给他的朋友。
She chucks her old clothes away. = 她把旧衣服扔掉了。

一般过去时:
He chucked the rubbish in the bin. = 他把垃圾扔进了垃圾桶。
She chucked her boyfriend for another guy. = 她甩了她的男朋友，和另一个男人在一起了。

现在完成时:
I have chucked all my old textbooks. = 我已经把所有旧教科书都扔掉了。
They have chucked the project due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，他们已经放弃了这个项目。

过去完成时:
He had chucked the letter before realizing it was important. = 他在意识到信很重要之前就把信扔了。
She had chucked her dreams of becoming a doctor years ago.  = 多年前她就放弃了成为医生的梦想。

将来时:
I will chuck this broken chair away tomorrow. = 我明天要把这把坏椅子扔掉。
He will chuck his old car once he buys a new one. = 他买了一辆新车后就会把旧车扔掉。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would chuck that broken vase. = 如果我是你，我会把那个破花瓶扔掉。
I wish I had chucked that old computer sooner. = 我真希望我早点把那台旧电脑扔掉。

被动语态:
The old furniture was chucked out last week. = 旧家具上周被扔掉了。
The project was chucked because of budget cuts. = 由于预算削减，该项目被取消了。

疑问句:
Are you chucking that away? = 你要扔掉那个吗？
Will he be chucking his job soon? = 他很快会辞职吗？

否定句:
I didn't chuck the letter; I put it in my bag. = 我没有扔掉那封信；我把它放进我的包里了。
She wouldn't chuck her loyal dog. = 她不会抛弃她忠实的狗。


PHRASE->
Chuck it! = 算了吧！/别管它了！
Chuck a wobbly = 发脾气；大发雷霆
Chuck someone out = 把某人赶出去
Chuck something in = 随便塞进；扔进去
To chuck something down = 狼吞虎咽地吃
