hokeday:/ˈhoʊkdeɪ/ | n. |愚人节|  A day of celebration, typically April 1st, on which people play tricks or make jokes on others. It is believed to have origins in various folk traditions and celebrations.  | Example:  Many people play pranks on each other on Hokeday. 许多人在愚人节互相开玩笑。 |近义词：April Fool's Day |反义词：None  |名词
