precalculated:/ˌpriːˈkælkjuleɪtɪd/| adj. | 预先计算的，预先算好的 | Already calculated or computed in advance.  This adjective describes something that has been determined beforehand using mathematical or computational methods. | Example: The precalculated values were used to speed up the simulation.  预先计算出的值被用来加快模拟速度。 The software uses precalculated tables to improve performance. 该软件使用预先计算的表格来提高性能。|近义词：computed beforehand, predetermined, pre-computed | 反义词：uncalculated, impromptu, improvised |
