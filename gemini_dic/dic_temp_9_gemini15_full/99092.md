interbrigade: /ˌɪntərˈbraɪɡeɪd/ | n. |国际旅，国际纵队 |A brigade composed of people from different countries, often formed for a specific purpose, typically during wartime or for humanitarian aid.  The term is often associated with the International Brigades who fought in the Spanish Civil War. | Example: The International Brigades fought bravely against fascism in Spain. 国际纵队在西班牙英勇地对抗法西斯主义。 |近义词:international unit, multinational force |反义词:national brigade, domestic force |名词
