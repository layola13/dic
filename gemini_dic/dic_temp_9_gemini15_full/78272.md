germanization:/ˌdʒɜːrmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |日耳曼化；使具有日耳曼特征|The process of making something German in character or culture. This can refer to the adoption of German language, customs, or institutions, or the imposition of German influence on another culture or territory.  |例句：The germanization of Alsace-Lorraine after the Franco-Prussian War led to significant cultural changes.  二战后阿尔萨斯-洛林的日耳曼化导致了重大的文化变迁。 The forced germanization of conquered territories was a key aspect of Nazi ideology.  对被征服领土的强制日耳曼化是纳粹意识形态的关键方面。|近义词：germanization,  Teutonization |反义词：de-germanization,  denazification|名词
