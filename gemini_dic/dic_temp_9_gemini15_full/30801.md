cetyl: /ˈsɪtɪl/ | n. |十六烷基；鲸蜡基|  A saturated fatty alcohol with the formula CH3(CH2)15OH. It is a waxy, white solid that is found in many natural waxes, such as spermaceti (from sperm whales) and cetyl palmitate (from palm oil). It is used in cosmetics and other products as an emollient and surfactant. | Example: Cetyl alcohol is a common ingredient in lotions and creams.  十六烷醇是乳液和面霜中常见的成分。| 近义词: hexadecanol | 反义词:  N/A
