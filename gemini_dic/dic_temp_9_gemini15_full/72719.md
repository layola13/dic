foddered:/ˈfɒdəd/| /ˈfɑːdɚd/| adj.|用饲料喂养的；供应饲料的|Provided with fodder; supplied with feed.  The sheep were well foddered for the winter.  |The farmer foddered his cattle with hay.|  近义词：fed, supplied| 反义词：starved, deprived


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer fodders his animals every morning. = 农民每天早上都喂他的动物。
They fodder the horses with oats and hay. = 他们用燕麦和干草喂马。


一般过去时:
He foddered the chickens before he left for work. = 他上班前喂了鸡。
The animals were well foddered throughout the harsh winter. = 在严酷的冬天，动物们得到了很好的喂养。


现在完成时:
The farmer has foddered the sheep this week. = 农民本周已经喂了羊。
They have foddered the cattle regularly throughout the drought. = 他们在干旱期间定期喂养牲畜。


过去完成时:
The animals had been well foddered before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，动物们已经得到了很好的喂养。
The farmer had foddered the horses before the long journey. = 农民在长途旅行之前已经喂了马。


将来时:
The farmer will fodder the cows tomorrow morning. = 农民明天早上将要喂奶牛。
They will fodder the pigs with leftover vegetables. = 他们将用剩下的蔬菜喂猪。


虚拟语气:
If I had foddered the animals properly, they wouldn't be so thin. = 如果我当初好好喂养动物，它们就不会这么瘦了。
I wish I had foddered the horses better before the race. = 我真希望比赛前我更好地喂养了马匹。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

They haven't foddered the sheep this morning. = 他们今天早上没有喂羊。
