embitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/| vt.|使痛苦；使怀恨；使难受|To cause (someone) to feel bitter; to make (something) unpleasant or disagreeable.|The loss of his job embittered him.  失去工作使他痛苦不堪。|He was embittered by years of hardship.  多年的苦难使他心怀怨恨。|近义词：sour, exasperate, gall|反义词：sweeten, soothe, delight|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant criticism embitters her. =  不断的批评使她痛苦。
This experience embitters many people. = 这段经历使许多人痛苦。


一般过去时:
The news embittered him deeply. = 这个消息使他深受打击。
His betrayal embittered her relationship with him. = 他的背叛使她与他的关系变得痛苦。


现在完成时:
Years of disappointment have embittered him. =  多年的失望使他痛苦。
The harsh treatment has embittered her towards authority. = 严厉的待遇使她对权威心怀怨恨。


过去完成时:
He had been embittered by previous failures. = 他之前经历的失败使他痛苦。
She had been embittered by a lifetime of injustice. = 她一生遭受的不公正待遇使她痛苦。


将来时:
This decision will embitter many people. =  这个决定将会使许多人痛苦。
Continued hardship will embitter the population. =  持续的苦难将会使人民痛苦。


虚拟语气:
If you continue to behave like this, it will embitter your relationships. = 如果你继续这样行为，它会使你的关系变得痛苦。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
