overpleased:/ˌoʊvərˈpliːzd/| adj. |过分高兴的，欣喜若狂的 |Excessively pleased; delighted beyond what is reasonable or appropriate.  It implies a level of pleasure that might be considered excessive or even slightly inappropriate given the circumstances. |例句：He was overpleased with his own cleverness. 他对自己聪明才智过分得意。

She was overpleased to receive such a generous gift. 她收到这么慷慨的礼物，高兴极了。(This example highlights the excessive nature of the pleasure.) |近义词：thrilled, delighted, overjoyed, ecstatic |反义词：disappointed, displeased, unhappy
