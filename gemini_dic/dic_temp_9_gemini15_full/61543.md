embryotroph:/ˈɛmbrɪoʊtroʊf/| n. |胚胎滋养细胞，胚胎营养物质|A substance that nourishes an embryo.  It refers to the nutritive material supplied to the developing embryo, either from the mother or from the yolk sac.  This term is often used in the context of developmental biology and reproductive biology.| Example: The embryo receives essential nutrients from the embryotroph.|近义词: embryonal nutrient, placental nutrient|反义词:  None directly applicable; antonyms would relate to things that harm or starve an embryo.
