scrapheap:/ˈskræpˌhiːp/| n. |废料堆；垃圾堆；弃置场 |A scrapheap is a place where discarded items, especially metal scraps, are piled up. It often represents obsolescence, uselessness, and the end of something's life cycle.  |Example:  The old factory was reduced to a scrapheap.  这家旧工厂变成了废料堆。

The car was destined for the scrapheap. 这辆车注定要被送进废料场。|近义词：junkyard, dump, landfill |反义词：treasure trove, valuable collection
