gyppers:/ˈɡɪpərz/| n.|骗子，江湖骗子，流浪者|A derogatory term for someone who is dishonest or deceitful, often implying a nomadic or itinerant lifestyle.  The term is considered offensive and should be avoided.|例句：He accused the traveling salesman of being a gypper and trying to cheat him. 他指责那个旅行推销员是骗子，试图欺骗他。


例句：Some people considered the travelling tinkers to be gyppers, taking advantage of unsuspecting folk.有些人认为旅行的修补匠是骗子，利用毫无戒心的民众。


近义词：con man, swindler, sharper, rogue, trickster|反义词：honest person, trustworthy person, upright person|名词
