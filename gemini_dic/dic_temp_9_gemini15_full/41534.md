coroner:/ˈkɔːrənər/|/ˈkɔːnər/| n. |验尸官|A coroner is a public official who investigates violent, sudden, or suspicious deaths.  They determine the cause and manner of death. |The coroner ruled the death a suicide.  验尸官裁定死因是自杀。|The coroner's inquest revealed the cause of death.  验尸官的调查揭示了死因。|forensic pathologist, medical examiner|—|
