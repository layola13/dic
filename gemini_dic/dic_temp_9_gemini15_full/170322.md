provided:/prəˈvaɪdɪd/ | adj. v. | 被提供的；假如；以…为条件 |  Given; on condition that; supplying. |  例句：The provided information was insufficient.  提供的资料不足。

例句：Provided that the weather is good, we'll have a picnic. 天气好的话，我们就去野餐。


近义词：given, supplied, conditional, assuming


反义词：lacking, absent, unconditional


词性：adjective, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company provides excellent customer service. = 公司提供优质的客户服务。
He provides for his family. = 他养家糊口。

一般过去时:
They provided us with all the necessary equipment. = 他们为我们提供了所有必要的设备。
She provided a delicious meal for her guests. = 她为她的客人准备了一顿美味的饭菜。

现在完成时:
They have provided ample evidence to support their claims. = 他们已经提供了充分的证据来支持他们的说法。
She has provided financial assistance to many charities. = 她已经为许多慈善机构提供了经济援助。

过去完成时:
They had provided all the materials before the construction began. = 在施工开始之前，他们已经准备好了所有材料。
She had provided a detailed report before the meeting. = 会议之前，她已经提交了一份详细的报告。

将来时:
The government will provide more funding for education. = 政府将为教育提供更多资金。
He will provide transportation to the airport. = 他将提供机场交通。

虚拟语气:
Provided that you work hard, you will succeed. = 如果你努力工作，你就会成功。
If they had provided more information, we could have made a better decision. = 如果他们提供了更多信息，我们就能做出更好的决定。

被动语态:
The necessary documents were provided to the committee. = 必要的文档已提供给委员会。
Funding was provided by a private foundation. = 资金由一个私人基金会提供。

疑问句:
Did they provide everything you needed? = 他们提供你所需的一切了吗？
What services does the company provide? = 公司提供什么服务？

否定句:
They did not provide any explanation for their actions. = 他们没有对他们的行为做出任何解释。
The school didn't provide enough resources for the students. = 学校没有为学生提供足够的资源。


PHRASE->
provided that = 假如；条件是
provided for = 为…做准备；供养
make provision for = 为…作准备
provided with = 装备有；供应有
with the proviso that = 只要
