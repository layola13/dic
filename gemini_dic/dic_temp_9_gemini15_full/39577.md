conscripting:/kənˈskrɪptɪŋ/| v.|征兵；强制征用|The act of forcing people to join the military or to perform some other duty.|例句：The government is conscripting young men into the army. 政府正在强征年轻男子入伍。|例句：The company is conscripting resources from its other divisions. 公司正在从其他部门征用资源。|近义词：drafting, enlisting, mobilizing|反义词：releasing, discharging
