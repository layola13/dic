plonker:/ˈplɒŋkə(r)/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A plonker is a British slang term for a foolish or incompetent person.  It's a relatively mild insult, often used playfully but can be offensive depending on context and tone.|例句：He's such a plonker; he forgot his own birthday. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然忘了自己的生日。 He's a right plonker for losing his keys again. 他真是个笨蛋，又把钥匙弄丢了。|近义词：idiot, fool, idiot, twit, dope|反义词：genius, brilliant, clever|名词
