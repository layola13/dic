detection: /dɪˈtɛkʃən/ | n. | 检测；探测；发现 | The act of detecting or the state of being detected; the process of identifying something hidden, concealed, or disguised.  | The early detection of cancer can significantly improve the chances of survival. 早期癌症检测可以显著提高生存几率。  The police are conducting a thorough detection of the crime scene. 警方正在对犯罪现场进行彻底的侦查。 | discovery, identification, recognition | ignorance, unawareness | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system detects intruders. = 系统检测入侵者。
She detects a change in his behavior. = 她察觉到他行为的变化。

一般过去时:
The doctor detected a problem during the examination. = 医生在检查中发现了一个问题。
They detected a faint signal from the satellite. = 他们探测到卫星发出的微弱信号。

现在完成时:
Scientists have detected a new strain of the virus. = 科学家们已经检测到一种新的病毒株。
We have detected no errors in the software. = 我们没有检测到软件中的任何错误。

过去完成时:
The police had detected the suspect's movements before he fled. = 警方在他逃跑前已经侦测到嫌疑人的行动。
They had detected the leak before it caused significant damage. = 他们在泄漏造成重大损害之前就发现了它。


将来时:
The sensors will detect any changes in temperature. = 传感器将检测到任何温度变化。
We will detect the presence of harmful substances in the water. = 我们将检测出水中是否有有害物质。


虚拟语气:
If we had better equipment, we could detect even smaller particles. = 如果我们有更好的设备，就能检测到更小的粒子。
I wish I had detected the problem sooner. = 我真希望我早点发现这个问题。

被动语态:
The fault was detected during testing. = 故障在测试期间被检测到。
The presence of toxins was detected in the water sample. = 在水样中检测到毒素的存在。

疑问句:
Can the device detect radiation? = 该装置能探测辐射吗？
Did the security system detect any unauthorized access? = 安全系统检测到任何未经授权的访问吗？

否定句:
The test did not detect any abnormalities. = 测试没有检测到任何异常。
The sensors failed to detect the approaching storm. = 传感器未能检测到正在逼近的风暴。
