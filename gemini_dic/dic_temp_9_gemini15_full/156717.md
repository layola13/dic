pieces:/piːsiz/| n.|碎片；部分；作品|Pieces are fragments or parts of something larger.  The word can refer to physical objects broken into smaller parts, or to sections of a larger work, like a musical piece or a written piece.|例句：The vase shattered into many pieces.  这只花瓶碎成了许多碎片。
The artist created several pieces of abstract art.这位艺术家创作了几件抽象艺术作品。|近义词：fragments, parts, sections|反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The vase breaks into many pieces. = 花瓶碎成了许多碎片。
He writes several pieces of music each year. = 他每年创作几首乐曲。


一般过去时:
