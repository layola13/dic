misjudger:/mɪsˈdʒʌdʒər/| n. |误判者，判断错误的人 | A person who makes incorrect judgments.  Someone who consistently misjudges situations or people. | Example: He's a misjudger of character; he often mistakes kindness for weakness. 他是个判断性格错误的人；他经常把善良误认为软弱。|近义词：erroneous judge, poor judge, bad judge |反义词：wise judge, discerning judge
