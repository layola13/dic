e-toll:/ˈiːtoʊl/| n. |电子收费系统|An electronic tolling system that automatically charges drivers for using a toll road or bridge without requiring them to stop at a toll booth.  It typically uses transponders or license plate recognition technology. |Example:  Many highways now utilize e-toll systems for faster and more efficient traffic flow.  许多高速公路现在都使用电子收费系统，以实现更快、更高效的交通流量。|近义词：electronic toll collection, automatic toll payment |反义词：manual toll collection, toll booth
