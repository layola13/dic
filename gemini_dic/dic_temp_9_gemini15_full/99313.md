intercrop:/ˈɪntərkrap/| n. |间作，套种|The practice of cultivating two or more crops in the same field at the same time.  Intercropping can increase yields, improve soil health, and reduce pest and disease problems.|例句：Farmers often use intercropping to maximize land use and crop diversity.  农民经常使用间作来最大限度地利用土地和作物多样性。

例句：The intercropping of legumes with cereals improves soil fertility. 豆类与谷物间作提高了土壤肥力。


近义词：mixed cropping, multiple cropping|反义词：monoculture|词性:noun
