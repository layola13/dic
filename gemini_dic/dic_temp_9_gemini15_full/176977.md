realpolitik:/ˈreːəlˈpɒlɪtɪk/|/ˈriːəlˈpɑːlətɪk/| n. |现实政治；务实政治|A system of politics or principles based on practical considerations rather than ideology.|例句：The government adopted a policy of realpolitik, prioritizing national interests over moral concerns.  政府采取了现实政治政策，优先考虑国家利益而不是道德考量。

例句：Their foreign policy is driven by realpolitik, not idealism. 他们的外交政策是由现实政治而非理想主义驱动的。


近义词： pragmatism, expediency, power politics | 反义词： idealism, utopianism
