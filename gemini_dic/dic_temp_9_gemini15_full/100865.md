introverted:/ˈɪntrəvɜːrtɪd/| adj.| 内向的 |  Describing someone who tends to be shy, quiet, and reserved, and who prefers spending time alone or with a small group of close friends rather than in large groups.  |  She's a very introverted person and doesn't enjoy large social gatherings. 她是一个非常内向的人，不喜欢参加大型社交聚会。|  reserved, shy, withdrawn | extroverted, outgoing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is introverted and prefers reading to partying. 他很内向，更喜欢看书而不是参加派对。
She is introverted by nature and finds social situations draining. 她天生内向，觉得社交场合很累人。


一般过去时:
He was introverted as a child, but became more outgoing as he got older. 他小时候很内向，但随着年龄的增长变得越来越外向。
She was introverted at the meeting and didn't speak much. 她在会议上很内向，没有怎么说话。


现在完成时:
He has always been introverted and rarely attends social events. 他一直很内向，很少参加社交活动。
She has become more introverted since the accident. 自从那次事故后，她变得更加内向。


过去完成时:
He had been introverted for years before he decided to seek therapy. 在决定寻求治疗之前，他已经内向了很多年。
She had always been introverted, so the large party overwhelmed her. 她一直很内向，所以大型派对让她不知所措。


将来时:
He will likely remain introverted throughout his life. 他很可能一生都会保持内向。
She will probably be introverted at the conference, as she usually is. 她很可能在会议上会很内向，就像她平时一样。


虚拟语气:
If I were more extroverted, I would attend more social events. 如果我更外向一些，我会参加更多社交活动。
I wish I weren't so introverted; it makes it hard to make friends. 我希望我不那么内向，这让我很难交朋友。


被动语态:
His introverted nature was often misunderstood by his colleagues. 他的内向性格经常被同事误解。
Her introversion was a key factor in her decision to work from home. 她的内向是她在家里工作的关键因素。


疑问句:
Is he introverted or extroverted? 他是内向还是外向？
Are you introverted, or do you enjoy large gatherings? 你是内向的，还是喜欢大型聚会？


否定句:
He is not introverted; he's quite outgoing and sociable. 他不是内向的；他非常外向和善于交际。
She is not introverted at all; she loves meeting new people. 她一点也不内向；她喜欢结识新朋友。
