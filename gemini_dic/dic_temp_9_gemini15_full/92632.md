iban:/ˈaɪbæn/| n. |国际银行账户号码|International Bank Account Number (IBAN) is an internationally standardized bank account numbering system.  It is used to identify bank accounts across international borders.  It is designed to enable efficient and reliable international funds transfers. The IBAN usually contains the country code, a check digit, and the bank account number.|Example: My IBAN is DE89 3704 0044 0532 0130 00. 我的IBAN是DE89 3704 0044 0532 0130 00。|近义词：bank account number, account number|反义词：无
