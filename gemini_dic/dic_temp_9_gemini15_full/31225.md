chansonnette:/ʃɑːnsəˈnet/| n. |小歌曲；短歌；小调|A short, light song, often of a playful or sentimental nature.  It is typically characterized by its brevity and lyrical quality.|例句：She hummed a sweet chansonnette as she walked through the garden. = 她漫步花园时哼着一首甜美的短歌。
The opera included several charming chansonnettes between the main arias. = 这部歌剧在主要咏叹调之间穿插了几首迷人的小歌曲。|近义词：song, ditty, air|反义词：epic, symphony|名词
