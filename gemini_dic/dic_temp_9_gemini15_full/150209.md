pancreatography:/ˌpæŋkriəˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 胰管造影术 |  A radiographic examination of the pancreatic ducts after injection of a contrast medium. This procedure is used to visualize the pancreatic ducts and detect abnormalities such as stones, tumors, or inflammation. | The pancreatography revealed a blockage in the pancreatic duct. 胰管造影显示胰管阻塞。  The doctor recommended pancreatography to investigate the patient's abdominal pain. 医生建议进行胰管造影以检查患者的腹痛。 |  |  |  n.
