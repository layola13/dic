continuator:/kənˈtɪnjuˌeɪtər/| n. |继续者，继承者 |A continuator is someone or something that continues something, such as a process, a tradition, or a lineage.  It often implies carrying on a work or legacy. | Example:  He is considered the continuator of his father's artistic legacy. 他被认为是他父亲艺术遗产的继承者。|近义词：successor, follower, inheritor |反义词：terminator, ender, disrupter
