lisped:/lɪspt/ | v. | 说/吐字含糊不清(常因口齿不清) | To speak with a lisp; to pronounce sounds, especially /s/ and /z/, as /θ/ and /ð/.  | He lisped his way through the poem. 他含糊不清地背诵了这首诗。 | stammer, slur |  speak clearly | v.
