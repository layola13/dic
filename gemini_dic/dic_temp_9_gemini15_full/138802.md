nummulites:/ˈnʌmjʊlaɪts/| n. |货币虫属，钱币虫 |Extinct genus of large, lenticular foraminifera, the shells of which are often found as fossils and resemble coins.  These single-celled organisms flourished in warm, shallow seas during the Eocene epoch. |The limestone of the Eocene period is often full of nummulites.  |近义词：foraminifera (a broader term)|反义词：无
