metronomes:/ˈmɛtrənˌoʊmz/| n. |节拍器|A metronome is a device that produces a regular beat or clicking sound to help musicians keep time while playing music.  It typically has a pendulum that swings back and forth, and the speed of the swing can be adjusted to set the tempo.| 例句：The musician used a metronome to practice playing the piece at the correct tempo.  这位音乐家用节拍器练习以正确的速度演奏乐曲。

The composer set the tempo of the symphony using a metronome. 作曲家用节拍器设定交响乐的节奏。|近义词：timekeeper, beat-keeper|反义词：None specifically.
