demobilises:/diːˈməʊbəlaɪz/ | vt. |复员；遣散；使…停止运转| To demobilize means to disband or discharge (soldiers, etc.) from military service; to cease military operations; or to dismantle or stop the operation of something.  |例句：The army was demobilised after the war ended.  战争结束后，军队被遣散了。|例句：The company demobilised its production line after the product failed to sell. 产品滞销后，公司停产了生产线。|近义词：discharge, disband, decommission |反义词：mobilize, activate
