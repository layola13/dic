lay-it-on:/ˈleɪ ɪt ˈɑːn/| phrasal verb|道出；倾诉；全力以赴；猛烈攻击|To tell someone something, especially something unpleasant or difficult.  It can also mean to apply something forcefully or to give one's best effort.|例句：Just lay it on me, I can handle it.  把事情告诉我吧，我能应付。

例句：He decided to lay it on thick with the compliments. 他决定用尽赞美之词。


近义词：reveal, disclose, unleash, exert, apply


反义词：conceal, withhold, restrain, hold back


词性:phrasal verb
