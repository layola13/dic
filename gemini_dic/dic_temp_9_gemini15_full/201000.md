slog:/slɒɡ/ /slɑːɡ/| v. n.|苦干，辛劳工作；沉重地走；|to work hard and continuously at a task that is difficult or unpleasant; to walk heavily or slowly|例句：He had to slog through the mud. 他不得不艰难地跋涉在泥泞中。
例句：She slogged away at her studies all weekend. 她整个周末都在努力学习。|近义词：toil, drudge, trudge|反义词：rest, relax|动词, 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slogs every day to meet the deadline. = 他每天都努力工作以赶上截止日期。
They slog through the paperwork every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在处理文件。

一般过去时:
I slogged for hours to finish the report. = 我花了几个小时努力完成报告。
We slogged through the snow to reach the summit. = 我们艰难地跋涉在雪地里到达山顶。

现在完成时:
He has slogged all year to achieve this success. = 他为了取得这个成功已经努力工作了一整年。
She has slogged tirelessly to support her family. = 她为了养家一直不知疲倦地努力工作。

过去完成时:
They had slogged for days before they finally reached their destination. = 他们努力工作了好几天才最终到达目的地。
We had slogged through the entire book before the test. = 我们在考试前已经努力学习了整本书。

将来时:
I will slog through this project no matter what. = 无论如何我都会努力完成这个项目。
She will slog to finish the assignment before the deadline. = 她会努力在截止日期前完成作业。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it would be this hard, I wouldn't have slogged so much. = 如果我知道会这么难，我不会那么努力。
I wish I hadn't slogged so hard; I could have taken a break. = 我希望我不要那么努力工作；我可以休息一下。

被动语态: (很少用于slog)
The work was slogged through by the team. = 这项工作被团队艰难地完成了。(不常用)
The project was slogged over for weeks. = 这个项目拖延了好几周才完成。(不常用)


疑问句:
Does he always slog like that? = 他总是这么努力工作吗？
How long will you slog on this project? = 你在这个项目上还要努力工作多久？


否定句:
He doesn't slog as much as he used to. = 他不像以前那样努力工作了。
I didn't slog today; I took a day off. = 我今天没有努力工作；我休假了。


PHRASE->
slog away: 努力工作
slog it out: 坚持到底
slog through: 艰难地完成
slog on: 继续努力工作
a hard slog: 一场艰苦的努力
