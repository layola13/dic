missionaire:/ˌmɪʃəˈnɛər/| n.|传教士|A person sent on a mission, especially a religious missionary.|The missionary dedicated his life to spreading Christianity in remote villages.  这位传教士将毕生精力奉献给了在偏远村庄传播基督教。|近义词：missionary, evangelist|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The missionary teaches the local children. = 传教士教当地孩子。
He spreads the Gospel every Sunday. = 他每周日传播福音。


一般过去时:
The missionary traveled to Africa last year. = 传教士去年去了非洲。
She helped the villagers build a school. = 她帮助村民们建造了一所学校。


现在完成时:
The missionary has worked in this region for ten years. = 这名传教士在这个地区工作了十年。
He has converted many people to Christianity. = 他已经将许多人改信基督教。


过去完成时:
The missionary had already learned the local language before arriving. = 传教士到达之前已经学会了当地语言。
They had built a church before the government intervened. = 政府干预之前他们已经建了一座教堂。


将来时:
The missionary will visit a new village next week. = 传教士下周将访问一个新的村庄。
She will continue her work for many years to come. = 她将在未来多年继续她的工作。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
