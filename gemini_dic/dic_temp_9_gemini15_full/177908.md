recharges:/ˌriˈtʃɑːrdʒɪz/| v.|充电；重新充电；补充能量|To restore energy to (a battery or other device) by passing an electric current through it.  To replenish or revitalize. |例句：I need to recharge my phone. 我需要给我的手机充电。

例句：The team recharged their batteries after a long day of work with a relaxing evening. 团队在辛苦工作了一天后，通过一个轻松的夜晚来恢复精力。

近义词：refuel, replenish, revitalize

反义词：discharge, deplete
