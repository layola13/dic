interdisciplinarity:/ˌɪntərˌdɪsɪplɪˈnærɪti/| n. |跨学科性，多学科性|The nature of involving or combining several academic disciplines or fields of study.|例句：The project demonstrates the benefits of interdisciplinarity in tackling complex problems.  这个项目展示了跨学科方法在解决复杂问题方面的益处。

例句：The research highlights the importance of interdisciplinarity for innovative solutions. 这项研究强调了跨学科性对于创新解决方案的重要性。


近义词：multidisciplinarity, transdisciplinarity|反义词：disciplinarity, specialization|词性：名词


词源:

* **inter-**:  prefix meaning "between," "among," or "amongst." (来自拉丁语，意为“在…之间”)
* **disciplin-**: from "discipline," referring to a branch of knowledge or study. (来自拉丁语 "disciplina", 意为“学习，训练，学科”)
* **-arity**: suffix forming nouns denoting a quality or state. (表示性质或状态的名词后缀)


因此，"interdisciplinarity" 字面意思是“学科之间”（的性质或状态）。
