replowing: /riˈplaʊɪŋ/ | v. | 再耕, 翻耕 |  To plow (land) again; to cultivate (land) a second time.  It implies a further cultivation or preparation of the land after an initial plowing.  | Example: The farmer decided to replow the field after the heavy rain, as the soil had become compacted. = 暴雨过后，农民决定重新翻耕田地，因为土壤变得很紧实。 |近义词：retilling, recultivating | 反义词：harvesting, leaving fallow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer replows the field every spring. = 农民每年春天都重新翻耕田地。
They replow the land to improve drainage. = 他们重新翻耕土地以改善排水。

一般过去时:
He replowed the section of the field that was too dry. = 他重新翻耕了过于干燥的那部分田地。
We replowed the garden after removing the weeds. = 除草后，我们重新翻耕了菜园。

现在完成时:
The farmer has replowed the entire field this year. = 农民今年已经重新翻耕了整个田地。
They have replowed the land several times this season. = 这个季节他们已经重新翻耕了几次土地。

过去完成时:
The farmer had replowed the field before the seeds were planted. = 种子播种前，农民已经重新翻耕了田地。
They had replowed the land twice before the harvest. = 收获之前，他们已经重新翻耕了两次土地。

将来时:
The farmer will replow the field next week. = 农民下周将重新翻耕田地。
They will replow the land after the frost. = 霜冻过后，他们将重新翻耕土地。

虚拟语气:
If it rained, the farmer would replow the field. = 如果下雨，农民就会重新翻耕田地。
I wish I had replowed the garden earlier. = 我希望我早点重新翻耕菜园。

被动语态:
The field was replowed after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，田地被重新翻耕了。
The land is being replowed in preparation for planting. = 为了播种，土地正在被重新翻耕。


疑问句:
Will they replow the field before planting? = 他们会在播种前重新翻耕田地吗？
Have they replowed the land yet? = 他们已经重新翻耕土地了吗？

否定句:
The farmer did not replow the field this year. = 农民今年没有重新翻耕田地。
They will not replow the land until next spring. = 直到明年春天他们才会重新翻耕土地。
