roller-skates:/ˈroʊlərˌskeɪts/ | n. |旱冰鞋 |Roller skates are a type of footwear with wheels attached to the soles, allowing the wearer to glide across surfaces. They are popular for recreational use and in some competitive sports.  |例句：She loves to roller-skate in the park. = 她喜欢在公园里滑旱冰。 |例句：My kids got new roller-skates for Christmas. = 我的孩子们圣诞节收到了新的旱冰鞋。|近义词：skates, inline skates|反义词：None |名词
