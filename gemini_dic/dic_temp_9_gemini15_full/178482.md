recontacted:/ˌriːˈkɒntæktɪd/| v.|再次联系，重新联系|To contact someone again after a period of time.|例句：I recontacted my old college roommate after many years.  我多年后再次联系了我的老大学室友。|例句：The company recontacted me about the job interview. 公司再次就面试与我联系。|近义词： contacted again, reached out again, got back in touch with |反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They recontact clients regularly. = 他们定期与客户再次联系。
He recontacts his former colleagues occasionally. = 他偶尔会与以前的同事再次联系。


一般过去时:
She recontacted her old friend last week. = 她上周再次联系了她的老朋友。
The company recontacted him after a month. = 公司一个月后再次联系了他。


现在完成时:
I have recontacted several potential investors. = 我已经联系了几位潜在投资者。
They have recontacted all the participants in the survey. = 他们已经再次联系了调查中的所有参与者。


过去完成时:
He had recontacted his former boss before accepting the new job. = 他在接受新工作之前已经再次联系了他的前老板。
She had recontacted her agent before launching her new project. = 在启动她的新项目之前，她已经再次联系了她的经纪人。


将来时:
We will recontact you within 24 hours. = 我们将在24小时内再次与您联系。
They will recontact the supplier next week. = 他们下周将再次联系供应商。


虚拟语气:
If I needed help, I would recontact him immediately. = 如果我需要帮助，我会立即再次联系他。
I wish I had recontacted her sooner. = 我真希望我早点再次联系她。


被动语态:
The customers were recontacted by the company. = 客户被公司再次联系了。

否定句:
