lich:/lɪtʃ/| n. |亡灵；幽灵；恶灵|A reanimated corpse, especially one that is malevolent and undead.  The term often evokes images from fantasy literature and games.|例句：The lich king raised his skeletal hand and commanded his undead army.  = 巫妖王抬起他骨骼般的手，指挥他的不死军队。|例句：Legends told of a powerful lich guarding a hidden treasure. = 传说中有一位强大的巫妖守护着隐藏的宝藏。|近义词：specter, ghost, wraith, ghoul |反义词：living being, the living
