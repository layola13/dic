pre-define:/ˌpriːdɪˈfaɪn/| adj.|预定义的，预先定义的|Already defined or determined beforehand; established in advance.|The function uses pre-defined variables.  这个函数使用预定义的变量。|近义词：predetermined, preset, established|反义词：undefined, unspecified
