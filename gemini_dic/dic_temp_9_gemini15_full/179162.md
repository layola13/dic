redigests:/ˌriːˈdaɪdʒɛsts/| vt.|重新消化；重新考虑；再编辑|To digest again; to reconsider or re-examine something, often with a view to improving or clarifying it; to edit or revise something again.|例句：The committee redigested the proposal before submitting it. 委员会在提交提案之前对其进行了重新审议。
例句：The author redigested the manuscript to improve its clarity. 作者重新编辑了手稿以提高其清晰度。|近义词：revise, re-examine, reconsider, re-edit|反义词：ignore, neglect|动词
