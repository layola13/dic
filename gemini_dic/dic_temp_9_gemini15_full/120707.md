misaligned:/ˌmɪs.əˈlaɪnd/| adj.|未对准的；未协调的；错位的 | Not properly aligned; out of alignment.  The word describes a situation where things that should be in line or agreement are not.  This can refer to physical objects, plans, or even perspectives. | 例句：The wheels of the car were misaligned after the accident.  车祸后，汽车的轮子错位了。  The company's marketing and sales teams were misaligned, leading to poor performance. 公司的市场营销和销售团队脱节，导致业绩不佳。|近义词：unaligned, out of line, skewed, askew |反义词：aligned, straight, in line
