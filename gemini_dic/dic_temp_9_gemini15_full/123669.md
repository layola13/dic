mood-altering: /ˈmuːdˌɔːltərɪŋ/ | adj. |改变情绪的，影响情绪的 | Affecting or capable of affecting someone's mood.  This adjective describes substances, experiences, or activities that can change a person's emotional state. |例句：Many people find that listening to music has a mood-altering effect. 许多人发现听音乐会改变情绪。

例句：The medication had a powerful mood-altering effect, leaving her feeling unusually calm.  这种药物有强大的改变情绪的作用，让她感到异常平静。


近义词：emotion-altering, mind-altering, psyche-altering


反义词：mood-stabilizing


词性：形容词
