monest:  /ˈmɒnɪst/ | adj. | 独一无二的；唯一的；极简主义的 |  Relating to or characterized by monism; believing in or characterized by the doctrine that only one supreme being exists.  Often used in philosophical contexts to describe a belief system that reduces all phenomena to a single ultimate substance or principle.  It can also describe something as unique or single. | 例句：His monest approach to design resulted in a clean and uncluttered aesthetic. 他的极简主义设计方法产生了简洁明了的审美效果。
