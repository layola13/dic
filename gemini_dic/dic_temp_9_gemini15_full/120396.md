ministerial:/ˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl/| adj.| 部长的；内阁的；政府的；大臣的 | Relating to a minister or ministers, especially government ministers.  It often implies an official or governmental capacity. |例句：The ministerial car waited outside the building. 部长的汽车在楼外等候。

例句：He held a ministerial portfolio in the previous government. 他在上一届政府中担任部长职务。


近义词：governmental, official, governmental, administrative

反义词：private, unofficial, independent


词性:形容词
