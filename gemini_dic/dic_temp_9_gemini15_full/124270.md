motel:/moʊˈtɛl/ | n. |汽车旅馆|A motel is a roadside hotel designed for motorists, typically offering simple accommodation and often including parking directly outside the rooms.  Motels are usually less expensive and less luxurious than traditional hotels.| We stayed at a motel near the highway. 我们住在一间靠近高速公路的汽车旅馆。 We booked a motel room for the night. 我们预订了一间汽车旅馆房间过夜。| hotel, inn | (none explicitly) | 名词
