incredulous:/ɪnˈkrɛdjʊləs/| adj.|难以置信的，不相信的|Expressing or showing disbelief.|He looked at me with an incredulous stare. 他用难以置信的眼神看着我。|skeptical, doubtful, disbelieving|believing, trusting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is incredulous about the news. = 他对这个消息难以置信。
She is incredulous that he lied. = 她不相信他撒谎。


一般过去时:
I was incredulous when I heard the story. = 我听到这个故事时感到难以置信。
They were incredulous at her sudden departure. = 他们对她突然离开感到难以置信。


现在完成时:
I have been incredulous ever since I saw it. = 从我看到它以来，我一直难以置信。
She has remained incredulous despite the evidence. = 尽管有证据，她仍然难以置信。


过去完成时:
I had been incredulous until I saw the proof. = 直到我看到证据，我才相信。
They had been incredulous about the rumor for weeks. = 他们对这个谣言怀疑了好几个星期。


将来时:
I will be incredulous if he actually wins. = 如果他真的赢了，我会感到难以置信。
They will be incredulous until they see it with their own eyes. = 直到他们亲眼看到，他们才会相信。


虚拟语气:
I would be incredulous if I didn't see it myself. = 如果我没有亲眼看到，我会感到难以置信。
She would have been incredulous if she had known the truth. = 如果她知道真相，她会感到难以置信。


被动语态:
His claims were met with incredulous reactions. = 他的说法受到了难以置信的反应。
The story was viewed as incredulous by many. = 许多人认为这个故事难以置信。


疑问句:
Are you incredulous about the possibility? = 你对这种可能性难以置信吗？
Is he still incredulous after all this? = 经历了这一切后，他仍然难以置信吗？


否定句:
I'm not incredulous; I just need more evidence. = 我不是不相信；我只是需要更多证据。
She wasn't incredulous; she was simply surprised. = 她不是不相信；她只是很惊讶。


PHRASE->
an incredulous look = 一副难以置信的表情
an incredulous gasp = 一声难以置信的倒吸气
incredulous laughter = 难以置信的笑声
incredulous stare = 难以置信的凝视
incredulous disbelief = 难以置信的不信
