slobbier:/ˈslɒbiər/| adj.|更邋遢的，更懒散的 | More slovenly or untidy; lacking neatness or care.  It implies a greater degree of sloppiness than "sloppy".|例句：His room was slobbier than I'd ever seen it before. 他的房间比我以前见过的还要邋遢。

例句：The slobbier the dog, the happier it seems to be.  狗越邋遢，看起来就越开心。


近义词：sloppy, messy, untidy, careless, slovenly

反义词：neat, tidy, orderly, clean, meticulous
