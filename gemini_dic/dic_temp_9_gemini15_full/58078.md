drooler:/ˈdruːlər/| n.|流口水的人，傻瓜|A drooler is someone who drools, often used informally and sometimes pejoratively to describe someone considered foolish or incompetent.  It can also refer to a baby or young child who drools frequently.|例句：He's such a drooler; he's always messing things up.|近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton|反义词：genius, mastermind
