meister:/ˈmaɪstər/| n.|大师，能手|A meister is a master or expert in a particular field, often implying a high level of skill and craftsmanship, particularly in traditional crafts or the arts.|例句：He is a meister of carpentry, crafting exquisite furniture. 他是一位木工大师，制作精美的家具。|近义词：master, expert, virtuoso, proficient|反义词：novice, amateur, beginner
