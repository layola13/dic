crepuscular:/krɪˈpʌskjʊlər/| adj.|暮光的；黄昏的；薄明的| Relating to, or occurring in the twilight.  The word often describes animals that are active during twilight hours.|The crepuscular rays of the setting sun painted the sky in vibrant hues.|近义词：twilight, dusk, gloaming|反义词：diurnal
