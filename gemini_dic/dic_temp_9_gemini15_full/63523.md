enunciation:/ɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 发音，清晰度 | The act of pronouncing words or a particular word; the manner of pronouncing words.  It refers to the clarity and distinctness of speech.  Good enunciation is essential for effective communication. | Example:  Her enunciation was so clear that everyone in the large hall could understand her. = 她的发音非常清晰，大厅里的每个人都能听懂她的话。

His enunciation improved significantly after taking speech therapy. = 他在进行言语治疗后，发音有了显著的提高。


近义词：articulation, pronunciation, delivery | 反义词：mumbling, slurring
