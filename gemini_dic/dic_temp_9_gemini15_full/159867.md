polymerises:/pəˈlɪməraɪzɪz/ | v. | 聚合；聚合反应 | To combine (simple molecules) to form a polymer.  The process involves the joining of many small molecules (monomers) to form a much larger molecule (polymer). This is a key process in the creation of many synthetic materials and is also found in nature in the formation of biological macromolecules like proteins and nucleic acids. | Example:  The monomers polymerise to form a long chain. 单体聚合形成长链。

The styrene monomers polymerise to form polystyrene. 苯乙烯单体聚合形成聚苯乙烯。


近义词:  polymerize (同义词，美式拼写)


反义词: depolymerize (分解聚合物)


词性: 动词
