maximizing:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzɪŋ/| v. |使最大化；最大限度地利用|To make the most of; to increase to the greatest possible extent.|The company is maximizing its profits by cutting costs. 公司正在通过削减成本使其利润最大化。|optimize, amplify|minimizing, reducing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are maximizing efficiency in our workflow. = 我们正在最大限度地提高工作流程的效率。
They maximize their profits through aggressive marketing. = 他们通过积极的营销来最大化利润。

一般过去时:
The engineer maximized the engine's power output. = 工程师使发动机的功率输出最大化。
She maximized her study time by eliminating distractions. = 她通过消除干扰来最大限度地利用学习时间。

现在完成时:
We have maximized our use of renewable energy sources. = 我们已经最大限度地利用了可再生能源。
The company has maximized its market share this year. = 公司今年已经将其市场份额最大化。

过去完成时:
They had maximized their production capacity before the order arrived. = 订单到达之前，他们已经将其生产能力最大化。
She had maximized her savings before taking a sabbatical. = 休假之前，她已经最大限度地储蓄了资金。

将来时:
We will maximize our investment returns by diversifying our portfolio. = 我们将通过多元化投资组合来最大化投资回报。
They will maximize their brand visibility through social media marketing. = 他们将通过社交媒体营销来最大限度地提高品牌知名度。

虚拟语气:
If we maximized our resources, we could achieve more. = 如果我们最大限度地利用资源，我们可以取得更多成就。
I wish I had maximized my potential earlier in my career. = 我希望在我职业生涯的早期就能够最大限度地发挥我的潜力。

被动语态:
The system is being maximized for performance. = 该系统正在被优化以提高性能。
Profits were maximized through strategic partnerships. = 通过战略合作伙伴关系使利润最大化。

疑问句:
How can we maximize the impact of our campaign? = 我们如何才能最大限度地提高我们活动的的影响？
Are they maximizing their opportunities in the new market? = 他们在新的市场上是否正在最大限度地利用机会？

否定句:
They did not maximize the potential of the new technology. = 他们没有最大限度地发挥新技术的潜力。
The company failed to maximize its profits last quarter. = 公司上个季度未能使其利润最大化。
