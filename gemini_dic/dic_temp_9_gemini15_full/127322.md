naysayer:/ˈneɪseɪər/| n. |反对者；唱反调的人|A person who habitually expresses negativity or opposition.  Someone who denies or opposes something. |例句：He's become a naysayer, constantly criticizing every new idea. 他变成了一个反对者，不断地批评每一个新想法。


例句：Despite the naysayers, the project went ahead and was a success.  尽管有反对者，项目还是继续进行并取得了成功。


近义词：critic, doubter, pessimist, cynic, skeptic


反义词：optimist, supporter, advocate, believer


词性：名词
