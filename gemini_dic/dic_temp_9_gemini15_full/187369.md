rodents:/ˈroʊdnts/| n. |啮齿动物 |Rodents are mammals characterized by a single pair of continuously growing incisors in each jaw.  They are a diverse group, including mice, rats, squirrels, and beavers.  |例句：Mice, rats, and squirrels are all examples of rodents.  老鼠、大鼠和松鼠都是啮齿动物的例子。

例句：The farmer set traps to control the rodent population in his fields. 农民设置陷阱来控制田里的啮齿动物数量。
