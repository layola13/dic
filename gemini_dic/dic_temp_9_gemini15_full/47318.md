de-mobbing:/diːˈmɒbɪŋ/ | n. |复员；遣散；(军队)解散；|The process of discharging or dismissing soldiers from military service, or employees from a company, often in a large group.  It often implies a sudden or unexpected ending. |例句：The demobbing of troops after the war was a chaotic affair.  战后军队复员是一件混乱的事情。|例句：The company announced a sudden demobbing of its workforce due to financial difficulties.  由于经济困难，该公司宣布突然遣散员工。|近义词：discharge, dismissal, decommissioning, redundancy|反义词：mobilization, recruitment, enlistment |词性：名词
