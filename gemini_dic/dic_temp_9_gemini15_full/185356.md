retrocede:/ˌrɛtrəˈsiːd/| v.|后退；倒退；放弃；让步|To go back or return to a previous state or condition; to yield or relinquish something.|例句：The army retroceded to its former positions after the failed attack.  军队在进攻失败后退回了原来的阵地。 The government retroceded control of the industry to private hands.  政府将对该行业的控制权交还给了私人。|近义词：recede, retreat, revert, relinquish|反义词：advance, progress, proceed
