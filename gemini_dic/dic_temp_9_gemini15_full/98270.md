inserts:/ɪnˈsɜːrts/ | v. n. |插入；嵌入物；插入物 |to put something into something else|例句：He inserts a new paragraph into the document. 他在文档中插入了一个新段落。|近义词：insert, embed, integrate |反义词：remove, delete, extract| 动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inserts the key into the lock. = 他把钥匙插入锁孔。
She inserts a picture into her presentation. = 她把一张图片插入她的演示文稿。

一般过去时:
They inserted a new chapter into the book. = 他们把新的一章插入书中。
He inserted the coin into the vending machine. = 他把硬币投入自动售货机。

现在完成时:
She has inserted several new features into the software. = 她已经在软件中插入了一些新功能。
They have inserted a new clause into the contract. = 他们已在合同中插入了一个新条款。

过去完成时:
Before publishing, they had inserted a disclaimer. = 出版前，他们已插入免责声明。
He had inserted the card before the machine malfunctioned. = 机器出现故障之前，他已经插入了卡片。

将来时:
I will insert the data into the spreadsheet tomorrow. = 我明天将把数据插入电子表格。
They will insert a new scene into the movie. = 他们将在电影中插入一个新场景。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would insert more details. = 如果我有时间，我会插入更多细节。
I wish I had inserted a better picture. = 我希望我插入了一张更好的图片。

被动语态:
The image was inserted into the report. = 图片被插入报告中。
A new paragraph will be inserted by the editor. = 编辑将插入一段新文字。

疑问句:
Where should I insert this picture? = 我应该把这张图片插入哪里？
When was the amendment inserted into the law? = 该修正案是什么时候被插入法律中的？

否定句:
I didn't insert the correct password. = 我没有输入正确的密码。
He did not insert the CD into the player. = 他没有把CD放入播放器。
