This term is commonly used in finance and credit risk analysis. | Example: The bank reported a low rate of nondefaultings among its mortgage borrowers.  这家银行报告称，其抵押贷款借款人的非违约率很低。 |近义词：non-defaulters,  payers, creditworthy individuals |反义词：defaulters, delinquents
