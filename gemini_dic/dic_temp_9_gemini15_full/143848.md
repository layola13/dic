outplanned:/ˌaʊtˈplænd/| v. | 巧妙计划，智胜，预先计划好| To plan or scheme more effectively than someone else; to surpass in planning.  It implies a degree of cleverness or strategic thinking that results in a successful outcome. |例句：The opposing team completely outplanned us; their strategy was superior.  对手机队完全智胜了我们；他们的策略更胜一筹。


The general manager outplanned his rivals by securing the lucrative contract. 总经理通过获得这份利润丰厚的合同，巧妙地计划胜过了他的竞争对手。


近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit,  strategize better than |反义词：be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted, be outwitted
