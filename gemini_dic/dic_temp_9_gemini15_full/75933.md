fungiform:/ˈfʌŋdʒɪfɔːrm/| adj.|蕈状的；菌状的|Resembling a fungus in shape; mushroom-shaped.  The term is often used to describe the shape of certain papillae on the tongue.|例句：The fungiform papillae on the tongue are responsible for taste. 舌头上的蕈状乳头负责味觉。|近义词：mushroom-shaped, fungus-like|反义词：filiform (thread-like)
