draw in:/drɔː ɪn/ | vt. |吸引；使卷入；逐渐变暗；(车辆)靠边停车| To pull or attract something or someone towards a particular place or situation.  Also, to become gradually darker or less bright.  For vehicles, it means to pull over to the side of the road. |例句：The city's vibrant culture drew in many tourists.  城市的活力文化吸引了许多游客。

The long winter evenings drew in early. 长长的冬夜很快就降临了。


近义词：attract, lure, entice, pull in, absorb|反义词：repel, push away, disperse
