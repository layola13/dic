nonaffecting: /ˌnɒnəˈfɛktɪŋ/ | adj. |不影响的，无关紧要的 | Not having any effect or influence.  This word is a combination of "non-" (meaning "not") and "affecting" (meaning "having an influence on"). It describes something that does not produce any change or impact. |  The nonaffecting policy change had little impact on the company's operations.  这项不影响的政策变更对公司的运营几乎没有影响。


近义词：irrelevant, inconsequential, immaterial, unimportant, insignificant
反义词：influential, impactful, significant, important, consequential
