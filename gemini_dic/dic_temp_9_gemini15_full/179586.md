reemigrate:/ˌriːˈeɪmɪɡreɪt/| v.|再次移民；重新移居|To migrate back to a former country of residence.|例句：Many refugees reemigrated to their home countries after the war ended. 许多难民在战争结束后重新移居到他们的祖国。  例句：After living abroad for ten years, she decided to reemigrate to her native land. 在国外生活了十年后，她决定重新移居到她的祖国。|近义词：return, migrate back|反义词：emigrate, immigrate

一般现在时:
He reemigrates to his homeland every summer. 他每年夏天都重新移居到他的祖国。
They reemigrate to escape the harsh winter. 他们为了躲避严酷的冬天而再次移民。

一般过去时:
She reemigrated to Canada after living in the US for five years.  她在美国住了五年后重新移居加拿大。
My grandparents reemigrated to Europe after World War II. 我的祖父母在二战后重新移居欧洲。
