miscategorize:/ˌmɪsˈkætəɡəraɪz/ | vt. |错误分类，归类错误|To classify incorrectly.  This verb implies a mistake in assigning something to a category.|例句：The librarian miscategorized the book, placing it in the wrong section. 图书管理员把书归错了类，放在了错误的区域。

例句：He miscategorized his expenses, leading to inaccurate accounting. 他把他的支出归类错误，导致会计核算不准确。


近义词：misclassify, misfile, wrongly categorize | 反义词：categorize correctly, classify accurately | 词性: verb
