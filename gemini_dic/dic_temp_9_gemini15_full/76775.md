gamete:/ˈɡæmiːt/ | n. | 配子，生殖细胞 | A gamete is a reproductive cell (sex cell) that fuses with another gamete during fertilization (conception) in organisms that sexually reproduce.  Gametes are haploid, meaning they contain only one set of chromosomes.  | Example:  The sperm and egg are both gametes. 精子和卵子都是配子。 |近义词：sex cell, reproductive cell |反义词：zygote (受精卵)
