intercept:/ˈɪntərsɛpt/ | v., n. |截取；拦截；截断 | To interrupt or stop something or someone from continuing to a destination.  |例句：The police intercepted a shipment of illegal drugs.  警方截获了一批非法毒品。

例句：The missile intercepted the incoming projectile.  导弹拦截了来袭的炮弹。


近义词：interrupt, block, seize, halt | 反义词：continue, allow, permit | 词性: Verb, Noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The spy intercepts enemy communications. = 间谍拦截敌方通讯。
She intercepts emails sent to her competitors. = 她拦截发送给竞争对手的邮件。


一般过去时:
The police intercepted the stolen car. = 警方拦截了被盗车辆。
He intercepted a pass intended for the other team. = 他拦截了传给对方球队的球。


现在完成时:
The government has intercepted several terrorist plots. = 政府已经挫败了几起恐怖袭击阴谋。
She has intercepted numerous phishing attempts. = 她已经拦截了多次网络钓鱼企图。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had intercepted the enemy's message before the attack. = 士兵们在袭击之前截获了敌人的信息。
They had intercepted the package before it reached its destination. = 他们在包裹到达目的地之前将其拦截了。


将来时:
The security system will intercept any unauthorized access attempts. = 安全系统将拦截任何未经授权的访问尝试。
We will intercept the signal as soon as it's transmitted. = 我们将在信号传输后立即拦截它。


虚拟语气:
If they intercepted the message, they would have prevented the disaster. = 如果他们截获了信息，他们就能阻止这场灾难。
I wish I had intercepted that email before it was sent. = 我真希望我在邮件发送之前就拦截了它。


被动语态:
The package was intercepted at the border. = 包裹在边境被拦截了。
All incoming calls are intercepted and monitored. = 所有来电都被拦截和监控。


疑问句:
Did the security system intercept the intrusion? = 安全系统拦截了入侵吗？
Will the satellite intercept the signal from the spacecraft? = 卫星会拦截来自宇宙飞船的信号吗？


否定句:
The security guard didn't intercept the thief. = 保安没有拦截小偷。
The system failed to intercept the virus. = 系统未能拦截病毒。


PHRASE->
intercept a message = 截获信息
intercept a call = 拦截电话
intercept a ball = 拦截球
intercept a transmission = 拦截传输
intercept a flight = 拦截航班


词源:
From Middle French *intercepter, from Latin intercipere, from inter- ("between") + capere ("to take").  The word reflects the action of "taking something between" two points or parties.
