sculptor:/ˈskʌlptər/| n.|雕刻家，塑像家|A person who practices the art of sculpture.  Sculptors work with a variety of materials, including stone, wood, metal, and clay, to create three-dimensional works of art.|例句：Michelangelo was a renowned sculptor.  米开朗基罗是一位著名的雕刻家。

The sculptor carefully chiseled the marble.  这位雕刻家仔细地凿着大理石。|近义词：artist, carver, modeller|反义词：None
