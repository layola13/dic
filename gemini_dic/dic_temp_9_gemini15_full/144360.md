outsteals:/ˌaʊtˈstiːlz/| v.| 偷窃，抢先获得 |To acquire or obtain something before someone else, often through cunning or skillful maneuvering.  This word is a relatively uncommon neologism, blending "out" (meaning to surpass or exceed) and "steals". It implies a more strategic or skillful theft than a simple act of stealing. |例句：The rival company outsteals our market share with a clever marketing campaign.  竞争对手通过巧妙的营销活动抢占了我们的市场份额。


The team outsteals the opposition with a series of brilliant plays.  球队通过一系列精彩的比赛抢先获得优势。|近义词：preempt, snatch, secure,  outmaneuver|反义词：lose, yield, surrender
