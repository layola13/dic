looser:/ˈluːsər/ | adj., n. | 松散的；失败者；不严格的人 |

英文解析:As an adjective, "looser" describes something that is not tightly fastened or packed; lacking firmness or tightness. As a noun, it refers to someone who is unsuccessful, a loser, or someone who is lax in their morals or standards.  The spelling "loser" is more common in modern usage.

例句:

(Adjective)
The screws were loose; the entire structure felt looser than it should. = 螺丝松了；整个结构感觉比应该的更松散。

The fabric is looser than I expected. = 这布料比我预期的更松。


(Noun)
He felt like a looser after the competition. = 比赛结束后他感觉自己像个失败者。

Don't be such a looser; try harder! = 别那么没用；再努力点！


近义词: (adj.) slack, loose, relaxed,  (n.) loser, failure, underachiever

反义词: (adj.) tight, firm, strict, (n.) winner, champion, achiever


词性: adjective, noun
