seldomly:/ˈsɛldəmli/ | adv. |很少地，难得地 | Rarely or infrequently.  The adverbial form of "seldom". It emphasizes the infrequency of an action or event. |例句：She seldomly visits her grandparents. 她很少去看望她的祖父母。
He seldomly attends meetings. 他很少参加会议。|近义词：rarely, infrequently, scarcely |反义词：frequently, often, usually |无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They seldomly eat out. = 他们很少在外吃饭。
We seldomly go to the movies. = 我们很少去看电影。


一般过去时:
She seldomly called her mother last year. = 去年她很少给她妈妈打电话。
He seldomly went to church in his youth. = 他年轻时很少去教堂。


现在完成时:
They have seldomly travelled abroad. = 他们很少出国旅行。
We have seldomly seen such a beautiful sunset. = 我们很少看到这么美丽的日落。


过去完成时:
I had seldomly encountered such a problem before. = 我以前很少遇到过这样的问题。
She had seldomly been to the city center before that day. = 在那一天之前，她很少去过市中心。


将来时:
They will seldomly have time for leisure activities. = 他们将来很少有时间进行休闲活动。
We will seldomly be able to afford such expensive vacations. = 我们将来很少能负担得起如此昂贵的假期。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would seldomly watch television. = 如果我有更多时间，我就会很少看电视。
I wish I seldomly had to work overtime. = 我希望我很少需要加班。


被动语态:
The museum is seldomly visited by tourists. = 这个博物馆很少被游客参观。
Such events are seldomly reported in the media. = 这样的事件很少在媒体上报道。


疑问句:
Do they seldomly go shopping? = 他们很少去购物吗？
Does he seldomly use the computer? = 他很少用电脑吗？


否定句:
They don't seldomly go to the park. (This is grammatically awkward; it's better to say "They frequently go to the park.") = 他们经常去公园。(这句话语法上很别扭；最好说“他们经常去公园”)
He doesn't seldomly read books. (Similarly awkward; use "He often reads books.") = 他经常看书。(同样别扭；用“他经常看书”)
