conferring:/kənˈfɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 授予；给予；商讨；协商 | To grant or bestow something, typically a title, degree, or honor; to discuss something with someone in order to exchange ideas or reach an agreement. |例句：The university is conferring honorary degrees on several distinguished guests.  大学正在授予几位杰出的客人荣誉学位。

The committee spent hours conferring about the best course of action. 委员会花了数小时讨论最佳行动方案。|近义词：bestowing, granting, awarding, discussing, consulting |反义词：withholding, denying, rejecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are conferring the award to the winner. = 他们正在将奖项授予获胜者。
We are conferring about the project's next steps. = 我们正在讨论项目的下一步计划。

一般过去时:
The president conferred a medal upon the soldier. = 总统授予士兵一枚奖章。
They conferred for several hours before reaching a decision. = 他们在达成决定之前商讨了几个小时。

现在完成时:
The university has conferred many honorary degrees this year. = 大学今年已经授予了许多荣誉学位。
They have conferred on the matter extensively. = 他们已经广泛地讨论了这件事。

过去完成时:
They had conferred before the meeting, so the discussion was brief. = 他们在会议前已经商讨过，所以讨论很简短。
She had conferred with her colleagues before making a decision. = 在做决定之前，她已经与同事们协商过。

将来时:
The school will confer the scholarship on a deserving student. = 学校将把奖学金授予一位应得的学生。
We will confer tomorrow morning to discuss the proposal. = 我们明天上午将商讨该提案。

虚拟语气:
If they conferred more often, they might reach a better solution. = 如果他们更频繁地商讨，他们可能会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish they had conferred with me before making that decision. = 我希望他们在做那个决定之前能与我协商。

被动语态:
The award was conferred upon her last night. = 奖项昨晚授予了她。
The matter is being conferred upon by the committee. = 委员会正在讨论此事。

疑问句:
Will the board be conferring this afternoon? = 董事会今天下午会开会商讨吗？
Are they conferring about the budget? = 他们正在讨论预算吗？

否定句:
The committee did not confer before making a decision. = 委员会在做决定之前没有进行商讨。
The university will not confer the degree without a proper application. = 大学不会在没有适当申请的情况下授予学位。


PHRASE->
confer with someone = 与某人商讨
confer a title = 授予头衔
confer an honor = 授予荣誉
confer authority = 授予权力
confer benefits = 带来好处

词源:
源自古法语 *conferrer，来自拉丁语 conferre，意为“带来一起，聚集”。  其动词词根ferre的意思是“to bring, to carry”。
