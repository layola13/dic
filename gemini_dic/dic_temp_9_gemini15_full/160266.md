pony:/ˈpoʊni/| n. |小马，矮马|A pony is a small horse, typically less than 14.2 hands high (58 inches or 147 cm).  Ponies are often known for their strength, hardiness, and suitability for riding by children.|例句：She rode a pony at the fair. 她曾在集市上骑过一匹小马。|例句：My daughter loves to ride her pony. 我的女儿喜欢骑她的那匹小马。|近义词：horse (small horse), steed (poetic)|反义词：stallion (adult male horse)
