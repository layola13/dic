khimar:/ˈkiːmɑːr/| n. |希玛尔（穆斯林妇女穿的长袍）|A khimar is a headscarf or veil worn by Muslim women, often as part of modest dress.  It typically covers the head and neck, and sometimes extends to the chest. The specific style and length can vary depending on cultural and personal preferences.|She wore a flowing khimar in a rich, deep blue.|近义词：hijab, headscarf, veil|反义词：None.
