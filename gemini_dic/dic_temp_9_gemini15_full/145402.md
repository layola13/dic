overcautiousness: /ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəsnəs/ | n. |过度谨慎；过分小心 | Excessive caution; undue care.  It can lead to missed opportunities or inefficient actions due to an overemphasis on avoiding risk. |

例句：

His overcautiousness prevented him from taking the necessary risks to advance his career.  他的过度谨慎阻止了他为了提升事业而冒险。

The company's overcautiousness in adopting new technologies cost them a significant market share.  该公司在采用新技术方面的过度谨慎让他们损失了大量的市场份额。


近义词：

timidity, apprehension, hesitancy, scrupulousness, wariness


反义词：

recklessness, rashness, boldness, daring, impulsiveness


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating "too much" or "excessive"
* **cautious**: adjective meaning "careful," "wary," or "avoiding risks"
* **-ness**: suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
