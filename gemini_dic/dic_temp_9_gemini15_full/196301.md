serfship:/ˈsɜːrfʃɪp/| n. |农奴身份；农奴制|The state or condition of being a serf; the system of serfdom.  It refers to the social and economic position of serfs, bound to the land and subject to the will of their lord. |例句：The serfship of the medieval period was characterized by harsh conditions and limited freedoms. 中世纪时期的农奴身份以严酷的条件和有限的自由为特征。|例句：The abolition of serfship marked a significant turning point in European history.  废除农奴制是欧洲历史上一个重要的转折点。|近义词：serfdom, bondage, servitude, vassalage|反义词：freedom, liberty, independence|名词
