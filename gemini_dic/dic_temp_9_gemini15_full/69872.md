ferrules:/ˈfɛrʊlz/| n.|金属套管；套环；(笔杆等的)金属笔帽|A ferrule is a metal ring or cap, often used to strengthen or protect the end of something, such as a tool handle, a cane, or a paintbrush.  They are also used to secure parts together. |The ferrule on my walking stick is loose.  我的拐杖上的金属套环松了。 The artist carefully replaced the worn ferrule on his paintbrush. 艺术家仔细地更换了他画笔磨损的金属笔帽。| 近义词：ring, cap, band, collar | 反义词: (无明确反义词)
