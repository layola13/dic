escapists:/ɪˈskeɪpɪsts/| n. |逃避现实者；逃避主义者 |People who seek escape from reality, often through activities such as fantasy, daydreaming, or addiction.  They tend to avoid dealing with their problems directly. |Example:  Many of the characters in the novel are escapists, finding solace in their imaginary worlds. 许多小说中的人物都是逃避现实者，在他们虚构的世界中找到了慰藉。  The escapists in the group refused to confront their issues.  小组里的逃避现实者拒绝面对他们的问题。|近义词：dreamers, fantasists, daydreamers |反义词：realists, pragmatists
