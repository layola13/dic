redisciplines:/ˌriːˈdɪsəplɪnz/| v.|再训练；重新训练；重新规范|To train or discipline someone again; to reorganize or restructure a system or field of study.|例句：The company redisciplined its sales team after a period of poor performance. 公司在业绩低迷一段时间后对销售团队进行了再培训。
例句：The university is redisciplining its curriculum to better meet the needs of students. 这所大学正在重新调整课程以更好地满足学生的需求。|近义词：retrain, restructure, reorganize|反义词：neglect, abandon.
