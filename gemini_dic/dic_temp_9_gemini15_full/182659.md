reorienting:/ˌriːˈɔːriɛntɪŋ/ | v. |重新定向；重新定位；使重新定向 | The act of changing someone's or something's direction or focus.  It implies a shift in perspective, understanding, or approach. | Example:  The company is reorienting its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司正在调整其营销策略以瞄准更年轻的人群。

The navigation system needs reorienting after the unexpected turn. 导航系统在意外转弯后需要重新定向。|近义词：redirect, refocus, realign |反义词：misdirect, disorient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reorienting their business model. = 他们正在调整他们的商业模式。
We reorient our goals every quarter. = 我们每个季度都会重新调整我们的目标。


一般过去时:
The pilot reoriented the plane to avoid the storm. = 飞行员重新调整了飞机方向以避开风暴。
She reoriented herself to the new environment. = 她使自己适应了新的环境。


现在完成时:
The team has reoriented its priorities since the merger. = 自从合并以来，团队已经重新调整了其优先事项。
He has reoriented his career path toward sustainability. = 他已经将他的职业道路重新定向到可持续发展方向。


过去完成时:
They had reoriented their strategy before launching the product. = 他们在推出产品之前已经重新调整了他们的策略。
She had reoriented her thinking after the feedback. = 在收到反馈后，她已经重新调整了自己的想法。


将来时:
We will reorient the curriculum to meet the new standards. = 我们将调整课程以满足新的标准。
The company will reorient its efforts towards innovation. = 公司将把精力重新转向创新。


虚拟语气:
If we reoriented our resources, we could achieve more. = 如果我们重新调整资源，我们可以取得更多成就。
I wish I had reoriented my priorities sooner. = 我希望我早点重新调整我的优先级。


被动语态:
The map was reoriented to show the correct north. = 地图被重新定向以显示正确的北方。
Their focus was reoriented by the unexpected crisis. = 他们的注意力被突发的危机转移了。


疑问句:
Are they reorienting their investment strategy? = 他们正在调整他们的投资策略吗？
How will the company reorient itself in the changing market? = 公司将在变化的市场中如何调整自身？


否定句:
The company did not reorient its marketing approach. = 公司没有调整其营销方法。
He refused to reorient his perspective. = 他拒绝改变他的观点。
