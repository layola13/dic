gloomier:/ˈɡluːmiər/| adj.|更阴沉的，更忧郁的 |More gloomy; characterized by or expressive of gloom. It is the comparative form of the adjective "gloomy."|例句：The weather was gloomier than yesterday.  天气比昨天更阴沉。

The future looks gloomier for small businesses.  小型企业的未来看起来更加黯淡。


近义词：more depressing, more somber, more melancholy|反义词：brighter, cheerier, sunnier |词性：形容词
