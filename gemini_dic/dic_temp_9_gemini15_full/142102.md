orientational:/ˌɔːriənˈteɪʃənəl/ | adj. |方位性的；定向的；方向性的 | Relating to orientation or the determination of orientation.  This adjective describes something that pertains to, or is concerned with, the position, direction, or alignment of something.  | Example: The orientational properties of the molecule influence its reactivity. 分子的方向特性影响其反应活性。 The company provides orientational training for new employees. 公司为新员工提供定向培训。 |近义词：directional, positional, spatial | 反义词：disorienting, disorganizing
