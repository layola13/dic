preobtrude:/ˌpriːəbˈtruːd/| vt.|突出地强加；冒昧地提出|To thrust or push forward presumptuously or obtrusively; to impose oneself or one's opinions upon others.  |例句：He preobtruded his opinions into the conversation, much to the annoyance of others. 他在谈话中冒昧地提出自己的意见，这让其他人很恼火。|例句：Don't preobtrude your unwelcome advice on me. 不要把你不受欢迎的建议强加于我。|近义词：obtrude, impose, thrust, intrude|反义词：restrain, withdraw, suppress|动词
