ladder: /ˈlædər/ | n. |梯子，阶梯，等级 | A structure consisting of two parallel side pieces joined by rungs, used for climbing up or down.  |例句：He climbed the ladder to reach the roof. 他爬上梯子到达屋顶。

例句：She used a ladder to clean the gutters. 她用梯子清洗排水沟。


近义词：staircase, steps | 反义词：N/A


词源:  The word "ladder" comes from Old English *hlæddre,  ultimately of Germanic origin, related to Old High German *leitar, meaning "ladder".  The word's development reflects a consistent evolution within the Germanic language family.
