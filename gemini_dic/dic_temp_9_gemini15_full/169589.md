prophetism:/ˈprɒfɪtɪzəm/| n. |预言；预言行为；预言家气质|The practice or profession of a prophet; the communication of divine revelations.  It often involves predicting future events, interpreting divine will, and acting as an intermediary between the divine and humanity.  The nature and validity of prophetism are subject to diverse interpretations across different religious and cultural contexts.|例句：The ancient civilization placed great emphasis on prophetism, seeking guidance from oracles and seers.  古代文明非常重视预言，寻求神谕和先知的指引。
