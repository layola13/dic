pseudoemotional:/ˌsuːdoʊɪˈmoʊʃənəl/ | adj. | 假情感的；伪装情感的 |  Seeming or pretending to have emotions; insincere or artificial in the expression of emotion.  It describes behavior or expressions that mimic genuine emotions but lack genuine feeling. | 例句：His pseudoemotional apology lacked sincerity.  他那假惺惺的道歉缺乏真诚。

例句：Her pseudoemotional outburst was unconvincing. 她的假装情绪激动令人难以信服。


近义词：affected, artificial, feigned, insincere, contrived, theatrical


反义词：genuine, authentic, sincere, heartfelt, true


词性：形容词
