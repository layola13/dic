incarcerated:/ɪnˈkɑːrsəreɪtɪd/| adj.|监禁的；被囚禁的|Imprisoned; confined in jail or prison.|The incarcerated man hoped for parole.  被监禁的男子希望获得假释。|Many people are incarcerated for non-violent crimes. 许多人因非暴力犯罪而被监禁。|imprisoned, confined, jailed|freed, released, liberated|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is incarcerated for theft. 他因盗窃而被监禁。
The prisoner is incarcerated in a maximum-security prison. 囚犯被关押在最高安全级别的监狱里。


一般过去时:
He was incarcerated last year. 他去年被监禁。
She was incarcerated for her involvement in the robbery. 她因参与抢劫而被监禁。


现在完成时:
He has been incarcerated for five years. 他已被监禁五年了。
She has been incarcerated since the trial. 自审判以来，她一直被监禁。


过去完成时:
He had been incarcerated before for similar offenses. 他以前曾因类似罪行被监禁过。
They had been incarcerated for many months before their release. 在获释之前，他们已被监禁了几个月。


将来时:
He will be incarcerated if found guilty. 如果罪名成立，他将被监禁。
She will likely be incarcerated for a considerable amount of time. 她很可能会被监禁相当长的时间。


虚拟语气:
If he were incarcerated, his family would suffer greatly. 如果他被监禁，他的家人将遭受巨大痛苦。
I wish he were not incarcerated; he deserves a second chance. 我希望他没有被监禁；他应该得到第二次机会。


被动语态:
He was incarcerated without trial. 他未经审判就被监禁。
Many people are incarcerated without proper legal representation. 许多人在没有适当的法律代理的情况下被监禁。


疑问句:
Is he incarcerated? 他被监禁了吗？
Was she incarcerated for a long period? 她被监禁了很长时间吗？


否定句:
He was not incarcerated at that time. 他当时没有被监禁。
She has not been incarcerated before. 她以前从未被监禁过。

be incarcerated for 被监禁于...
