observer:/əbˈzɜːrvər/ | n. |观察者，观测者 |A person who watches or notices something.  Someone or something that observes. |例句：The scientist was a keen observer of animal behavior.  这位科学家是动物行为的敏锐观察者。

例句：The observer noted down all the changes in temperature. 观察员记下了所有温度变化。


近义词：watcher, viewer, onlooker, monitor |反义词：participant, actor


词源：源自古法语 *observeor，来自拉丁语 *observātor，是 *observāre（观察）的施动者。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The observer watches the birds. = 观察者观察鸟类。
She observes the stars every night. = 她每晚都观察星星。


一般过去时:
The observer watched the experiment carefully. = 观察者仔细观察了实验。
He observed a strange phenomenon. = 他观察到了一种奇怪的现象。


现在完成时:
The scientist has observed many interesting patterns. = 科学家已经观察到许多有趣的模式。
She has observed the patient's condition for weeks. = 她已经观察病人的病情几周了。


过去完成时:
They had observed the comet before it disappeared. = 他们在彗星消失之前就已经观察到它了。
The astronomer had observed the star's movement for years. = 天文学家多年来一直在观察这颗恒星的运动。


将来时:
The team will observe the eclipse tomorrow. = 团队明天将观察日食。
He will observe the reaction carefully. = 他将仔细观察反应。


虚拟语气:
If I were an observer, I would record every detail. = 如果我是观察员，我会记录每一个细节。
I wish I had observed the event more closely. = 我希望我当时能更仔细地观察到这件事。


被动语态:
The experiment was observed by many scientists. = 许多科学家观察了这个实验。
The changes in the weather were observed by the meteorologist. = 气象学家观察到了天气变化。


疑问句:
Who is the observer in this experiment? = 这个实验中的观察者是谁？
What did the observer see? = 观察者看到了什么？


否定句:
The observer did not see anything unusual. = 观察者没有看到任何异常情况。
He did not observe any significant changes. = 他没有观察到任何显著的变化。
