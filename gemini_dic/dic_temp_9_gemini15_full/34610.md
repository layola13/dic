cliché:/ˈkliːʃeɪ/ | n. | 陈词滥调，老套 | A cliché is a phrase or opinion that is overused and betrays a lack of original thought.  It's a common expression that has lost its impact due to overuse. | Example:  "He's a diamond in the rough" is a cliché.  他是个璞玉（这是句陈词滥调）。|近义词：platitude, stereotype, banality | 反义词：originality, novelty
