popeless:/ˈpoʊpləs/| adj.|  无教皇的；没有教皇统治的 | Without a pope; lacking papal authority or governance.  The term often implies a period of vacancy in the papacy or a situation where the papacy lacks effective power. | Example:  The city felt popeless during the interregnum.  这座城市在教皇空位期间感觉失去了教皇的统治。


近义词：papaless (less common), without a pope

反义词：with a pope, under papal rule

词性：形容词
