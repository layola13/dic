non-violent:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪələnt/| adj. |非暴力的；和平的| Not involving or characterized by violence.  The adjective "non-violent" describes actions, protests, or individuals that do not use force or aggression. |例句：The protesters staged a non-violent demonstration. 抗议者组织了一场和平示威。

The movement advocated for non-violent resistance to oppression.  该运动提倡以非暴力抵抗压迫。


近义词：peaceful, passive, unarmed, gentle

反义词：violent, aggressive, forceful, brutal
