regranting: /ˌriːˈɡræntɪŋ/ | v. | 再拨款；重新授予 | The act of granting something again, especially funds or rights.  It implies a previous grant that has been renewed or re-allocated. |

例句：

The government is considering regranting funds to the struggling arts organizations. = 政府正在考虑再次拨款给那些苦苦挣扎的艺术组织。

The university is regranting the research funds to a more promising project. = 大学正在将研究经费重新拨给一个更有前景的项目。


近义词：re-awarding, reallocating, redistributing

反义词：revoking, withdrawing, canceling


词性：动词
