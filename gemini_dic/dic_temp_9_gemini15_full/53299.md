dirtyish:/ˈdɜːrtiʃ/| adj.|有点脏的，不太干净的|Slightly dirty; not quite clean.  The word implies a degree of dirtiness that is less than "dirty" but more than "clean".|例句：The dishes were a bit dirtyish after the party. 聚会后盘子有点脏。
The car was dirtyish after the long drive. 长途驾驶后，车有点脏。|近义词：slightly dirty, somewhat unclean, grubby|反义词：clean, spotless
