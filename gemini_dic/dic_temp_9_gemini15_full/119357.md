microclimatically:/ˌmaɪkroʊklaɪˈmætɪkli/| adv.|微气候地，就微气候而言 |  Describes something relating to or affected by microclimates.  It indicates that a phenomenon is influenced by the localized climate conditions of a small area. | 例句：The plants thrived microclimatically due to the sheltered location.  植物由于其隐蔽的位置而微气候地生长茂盛。 The study examined the microclimatically driven variations in insect populations. 这项研究考察了微气候驱动的昆虫种群变化。|近义词：locally, regionally (in a limited context) |反义词：globally, macroscopically|副词
