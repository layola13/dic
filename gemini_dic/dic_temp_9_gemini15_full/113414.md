lymphangitis:/ˌlɪmfænˈdʒaɪtɪs/| n. |淋巴管炎|Inflammation of the lymphatic vessels, often caused by bacterial infection.  The inflammation causes pain, redness, and swelling along the affected lymphatic vessel. |Example: The patient presented with lymphangitis in her left arm, characterized by a red streak extending from her hand towards her armpit.  患者左臂出现淋巴管炎，其特征是从手部到腋窝有一条红线。|近义词：lymphadenitis (if lymph nodes are also involved) |反义词：None specifically, but resolution of inflammation could be considered an antonym.
