paint:/peɪnt/ | n., vt. |油漆；颜料；绘画；涂抹 |To cover a surface with a liquid substance such as paint, or to create a picture using paints.  |例句：He painted the wall blue. 他把墙漆成了蓝色。
He painted a beautiful landscape. 他画了一幅美丽的风景画。|近义词：color, coat, daub |反义词：erase, remove


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He paints landscapes. = 他画风景画。
She paints her nails red. = 她把指甲涂成红色。

一般过去时:
They painted the house last week. = 他们上周粉刷了房子。
He painted a stunning portrait. = 他画了一幅令人惊叹的肖像画。

现在完成时:
I have painted three pictures this month. = 这个月我已经画了三幅画。
She has painted the entire fence. = 她已经把整个栅栏都刷漆了。

过去完成时:
He had painted the door before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经把门漆好了。
They had painted the mural for weeks. = 他们已经创作那幅壁画好几个星期了。

将来时:
I will paint the kitchen tomorrow. = 我明天要粉刷厨房。
She will paint a picture of her dog. = 她将画一幅她狗的画像。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would paint a masterpiece. = 如果我有更多时间，我会创作一幅杰作。
I wish I could paint like Picasso. = 我希望我能像毕加索一样画画。

被动语态:
The house was painted white. = 房子被漆成白色了。
The mural is being painted by a famous artist. = 这幅壁画正在由一位著名的艺术家创作。

疑问句:
Did you paint the fence? = 你粉刷了栅栏吗？
What will you paint next? = 你接下来要画什么？

否定句:
I didn't paint the picture. = 我没有画那幅画。
She hasn't painted anything today. = 她今天什么都没画。

词源:
The word "paint" comes from the Old French word "peindre," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "pingere," meaning "to paint" or "to decorate."  The evolution reflects the spread of the word through Romance languages and into English.
