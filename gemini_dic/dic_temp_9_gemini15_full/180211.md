refoundations: /ˌriːfaʊnˈdeɪʃənz/ | n. |重建的基础；重新奠基 | The act of rebuilding or re-establishing the foundations of something;  a new foundation or basis upon which something is built.  It implies a thorough and fundamental restructuring or rebuilding, often after a period of decline or damage. |例句：The company underwent refoundations after the financial crisis.  公司在金融危机后进行了重建。

The refoundations of the educational system are essential for future success. 教育体系的重建对于未来的成功至关重要。


近义词：rebuilding, restructuring, renovation, reformation, reorganization |反义词：destruction, demolition, collapse
