hypochondrium:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪəm/| n.|季肋部，软肋|The hypochondrium is the region of the abdomen on either side of the epigastrium, below the ribs.  It is often associated with feelings of anxiety and discomfort.  |例句：He felt a sharp pain in his right hypochondrium. 他感到右季肋部一阵剧痛。|例句：The doctor examined the patient's hypochondrium for any signs of inflammation. 医生检查了病人的季肋部是否有炎症迹象。|近义词：flank, side|反义词：N/A
