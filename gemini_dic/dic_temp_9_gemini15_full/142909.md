out-rent:/ˌaʊtˈrɛnt/| v.|出租，转租|To rent out (a property) to someone else.|The landlord decided to out-rent his apartment to a young couple. 房东决定把他的公寓转租给一对年轻夫妇。|sublet, lease out|rent in, occupy|v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He out-rents his spare room every summer. 他每年夏天都把他的空余房间出租。
They out-rent properties to students. 他们把房产租给学生。


一般过去时:
She out-rented her cottage last year. 她去年把她的小屋出租了。
We out-rented the apartment for a good price. 我们以一个好价钱把公寓租了出去。


现在完成时:
He has out-rented that property for five years. 他已经把那处房产出租五年了。
They have out-rented several rooms this semester. 这个学期他们已经出租了好几个房间。


过去完成时:
They had out-rented the house before we arrived.在我们到达之前，他们已经把房子租出去了。
She had out-rented her apartment before finding a new one. 她找到新公寓之前已经把公寓租出去了。


将来时:
We will out-rent our cabin next summer. 我们明年夏天将把我们的木屋出租。
They will out-rent the space to a local business. 他们将把空间租给当地一家企业。


虚拟语气:
If I had a spare room, I would out-rent it. 如果我有一个空余房间，我会把它租出去。
If they out-rented the garage, they could pay off their debt. 如果他们把车库租出去，他们就能还清债务。


被动语态:
The property was out-rented quickly. 这处房产很快就被租出去了。
The rooms are out-rented to tourists during peak season. 在旅游旺季，房间出租给游客。


疑问句:
Do you out-rent any part of your house? 你出租你房子的任何一部分吗？
Will they out-rent the property to a family? 他们会把房产租给一个家庭吗？


否定句:
We don't out-rent our property to smokers. 我们不把房产租给吸烟者。
They didn't out-rent their apartment last year. 他们去年没有把公寓租出去。
