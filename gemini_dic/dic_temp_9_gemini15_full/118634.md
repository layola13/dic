messiest:/ˈmɛsiɪst/| adj.|最脏乱的，最凌乱的 | Most untidy or disordered.  It describes the superlative degree of messiness. |例句：His room is the messiest in the whole house. 他的房间是整个房子里最乱的。
The messiest part of the project was organizing the data.  这个项目最混乱的部分是整理数据。|近义词：dirtiest, most cluttered, most disorganized |反义词：cleanest, tidiest, most organized |词性:形容词

USAGE->

The room is the messiest room I have ever seen. = 这是我见过的最乱的房间。
His desk is always the messiest in the office. = 他的办公桌总是办公室里最乱的。
