silversmith:/ˈsɪlvərsmɪθ/| n. |银匠，银器匠|A silversmith is a craftsperson who makes objects from silver, such as jewelry, tableware, and decorative items.  They typically work with techniques like hammering, soldering, and polishing to shape and finish the silver. |The silversmith crafted a beautiful silver necklace.  这位银匠制作了一条漂亮的银项链。|jeweler, metalsmith|  N/A| noun
