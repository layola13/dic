hemiplegia:/ˈhɛmiˈpliːdʒə/| n. |偏瘫|Paralysis of one side of the body, typically resulting from a stroke or brain injury.  It affects the arm, leg, and trunk on the same side of the body. |Example: The stroke left him with hemiplegia on his right side.  中风导致他的右侧偏瘫。|近义词：paralysis, palsy|反义词：full mobility, unimpaired function|名词
