level-with:/ˈlɛvəl wɪð/| v.|坦诚相待；与…平等；与…齐平|to be honest and frank with someone; to be on the same level as someone or something.|例句：I need to level with you about your performance. 我需要和你坦诚地谈谈你的表现。|例句：The table is level with the floor. 桌子与地板齐平。|近义词：confide in, be frank with, be honest with|反义词：deceive, mislead, lie
