reblended:/ˌriˈblɛndɪd/| adj. |重新混合的，重新调配的 |  Describes something that has been blended again, often implying an improvement or alteration to a previous blend.  This usually refers to liquids, foods, or other materials that have been mixed. | 例句：The winemaker reblended the grapes to create a smoother flavor profile. = 酿酒师重新混合了葡萄以创造更柔和的风味。

The coffee beans were reblended to enhance their aroma. = 咖啡豆经过重新混合以增强其香气。


近义词：remixed, recombined, remastered | 反义词：unblended, original


词性：形容词
