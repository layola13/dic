semimaliciously:/ˌsemiˈmælɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 半恶意地，略带恶意地 |  Slightly maliciously; with a hint of malice.  The word combines "semi-" (half, partly) with "maliciously" (in a malicious manner). It describes an action or behavior that is not entirely malicious but contains an element of mischief or ill will. |  He semimaliciously tripped his brother, causing him to stumble. 他略带恶意地绊倒了他的兄弟，导致他跌倒。  |  maliciously, mischievously, playfully |  kindly, benignly, innocently | 副词
