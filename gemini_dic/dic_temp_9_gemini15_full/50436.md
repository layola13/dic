deprecated:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.| 已弃用的；不推荐使用的 |  Something that is deprecated is no longer recommended for use, often because it has been superseded by a newer, better, or safer alternative.  It's not necessarily broken or unusable, but its use is discouraged. | Example:  The `get` method is deprecated in favor of the newer `fetch` method.  `get`方法已被弃用，推荐使用新的`fetch`方法。|近义词：obsolete, outdated, superseded,discouraged|反义词：recommended, preferred, current, up-to-date|形容词
