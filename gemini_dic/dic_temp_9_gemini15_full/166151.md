prepromoted:/ˌpriːprəˈmoʊtɪd/| adj.|预先提升的；提前晋升的|Already promoted before the official announcement or process.  This term describes someone who has been given a higher position or rank ahead of schedule or the usual procedure.|例句：The prepromoted manager was already leading the team before the official announcement.  提前晋升的经理在正式宣布之前就已经领导团队了。|The newly hired employee was prepromoted to senior analyst due to her exceptional skills.  这位新员工因为其出色的技能而被提前晋升为高级分析师。|近义词：advanced, elevated, upgraded |反义词：demoted, downgraded
