precredited:/ˈpriːˈkredɪtɪd/| adj.| 预先记入贷方的；预先贷记的 |  Precredited refers to something that has been credited or added to an account before the usual time or date. It often involves financial transactions where funds are added to an account in advance.  |Example:  The company precredited my account with the bonus payment.  公司预先将奖金支付记入我的账户。

The system automatically precredited the funds to our account. 系统自动将资金预先记入我们的账户。 |近义词：advanced, pre-approved, pre-authorized | 反义词：postcredited, unpaid
