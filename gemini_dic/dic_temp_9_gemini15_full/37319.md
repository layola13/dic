commemorating:/kəˈmɛməreɪtɪŋ/| v.|纪念，庆祝 | To remember and celebrate a person or event, especially officially. |例句：The ceremony was held to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the company.  这次仪式是为了纪念公司成立50周年。

例句：We are commemorating the victims of the war with a minute of silence. 我们将通过默哀一分钟来纪念战争中的受害者。


近义词：celebrating, remembering, honoring, marking


反义词：forgetting, ignoring, neglecting


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
We commemorate the fallen soldiers every year. = 我们每年都纪念阵亡士兵。
They commemorate important historical events. = 他们纪念重要的历史事件。

一般过去时:
The city commemorated its founding with a festival. = 这个城市用节日来纪念它的建城。

过去完成时:
They had commemorated the event before the new building was erected. = 新楼建成之前，他们已经纪念过这一事件了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
