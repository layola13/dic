/ɪnˈvɜːrtɪbrəts/ | n. | 无脊椎动物 | Invertebrates are animals that lack a backbone or spinal column. They represent the vast majority of animal species on Earth, encompassing a wide range of phyla, including insects, crustaceans, mollusks, arachnids, and many others.  | Example:  Insects, spiders, and crabs are all examples of invertebrates.  昆虫、蜘蛛和螃蟹都是无脊椎动物的例子。 |近义词:  invertebrate animals | 反义词: vertebrates |名词
