overhelping: /ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpiŋ/ | v. | 过度帮助 |  The act of helping someone excessively, often to the point of hindering their independence or self-reliance.  It implies an imbalance where the helper's actions overshadow the recipient's needs and abilities.  | 例句：He was overhelping his son with his homework, preventing him from learning to solve problems independently. 他过度帮助儿子做作业，妨碍了儿子独立解决问题的能力。


例句：Her constant overhelping made him feel incompetent and frustrated. 她不断的过度帮助让他感到无能和沮丧。


近义词：oversupporting, coddling, pampering, interfering


反义词：underhelping, neglecting, ignoring


词性: 动词
