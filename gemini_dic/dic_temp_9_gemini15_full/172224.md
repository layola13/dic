pushed-around: /pʊʃt əˈraʊnd/ | adj. | 被摆布的；受欺凌的 |  Describes someone who is treated badly and without respect, often manipulated or controlled by others.  It implies a lack of power or agency. | 例句：He felt pushed-around and undervalued at work. 他在工作中感到被摆布和不被重视。


例句：She was constantly pushed around by her older siblings. 她总是被她的哥哥姐姐欺负。


近义词：bullied, victimized, manipulated, controlled, oppressed


反义词：empowered, respected, in control, dominant


词性: Adjective (形容词)
