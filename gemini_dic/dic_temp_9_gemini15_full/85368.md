harrows:/ˈhærəʊz/| n. |耙；痛苦经历；折磨| A harrow is an agricultural implement used to break up and level the surface of the soil.  It can also refer to a painful or distressing experience.|例句：The farmer used a harrow to prepare the field for planting. = 农民用耙子准备播种田地。 The experience was a harrowing ordeal. = 这段经历是一场痛苦的折磨。|近义词：rake, plough, torment, distress |反义词：comfort, soothe, ease
