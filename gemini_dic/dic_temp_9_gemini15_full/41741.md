corrosives:/kəˈroʊsɪvz/| n.|腐蚀剂；腐蚀物|Substances that cause irreversible damage to living tissue or other materials by chemical reaction.|例句：Many acids are corrosives and should be handled with care.  许多酸都是腐蚀剂，应小心处理。|例句：The corrosives leaked from the damaged container, causing significant damage. 腐蚀剂从损坏的容器中泄漏出来，造成了严重的损坏。|近义词：caustics, abrasives|反义词：protectives


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The acid corrodes the metal. = 酸腐蚀金属。
These chemicals corrode quickly. = 这些化学物质腐蚀很快。
