long-running: /ˌlɒŋˈrʌnɪŋ/ /ˌlɔːŋˈrʌnɪŋ/ | adj. | 长期运行的；持续时间长的 |  Describes something that continues for a long period of time.  | 例句：The long-running TV series finally ended after 10 seasons.  = 这部长期播出的电视剧在播出10季后最终结束。

The company's long-running success is a testament to its innovative spirit. = 公司的长期成功证明了其创新精神。


近义词：enduring, protracted, extended, sustained, ongoing


反义词：short-lived, brief, fleeting, ephemeral


词性: adjective
