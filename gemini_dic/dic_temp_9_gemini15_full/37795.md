compellatory:/kəmˈpɛlətɔːri/| adj.|强制的；义务的；必须履行的|Compulsory means something is required by a law or rule.  It suggests that something must be done, and failure to comply will result in consequences.  It often implies a lack of choice or freedom. |Example: Attendance at these meetings is compellatory.  这些会议的出席是强制性的。

Example:  The law makes wearing seatbelts compellatory.  法律规定必须系安全带。|近义词：obligatory, mandatory, required, compulsory|反义词：optional, voluntary
