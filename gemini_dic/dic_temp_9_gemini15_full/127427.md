neatify:/ˈniːtɪfaɪ/| vt.|使整洁；使整齐|To make something neat and tidy.  The verb "neatify" implies making something more orderly and aesthetically pleasing. It suggests a process of improving the appearance and organization of something.|例句：He neatified his desk before the meeting. 他在会议前整理了他的桌子。

例句：She neatified the garden by weeding and trimming the bushes. 她通过除草和修剪灌木使花园变得整洁。|近义词：tidy, organize, straighten, arrange|反义词：mess up, disarrange, clutter
