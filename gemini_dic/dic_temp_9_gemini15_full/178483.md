recontacting:/ˌriːˈkɒntæktɪŋ/| v.|再次联系，重新联系 |The act of contacting someone again after a period of time.  It implies a previous contact has already occurred. |例句：I'm recontacting all my old clients to see if they need my services again.  我正在重新联系我所有的老客户，看看他们是否再次需要我的服务。

例句：We are recontacting participants from our previous study. 我们正在重新联系我们之前研究的参与者。


近义词： contacting again, getting back in touch with, reaching out to again, following up with

反义词： ignoring, neglecting, abandoning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is recontacting his former colleagues. 他正在重新联系他以前的同事。
She recontacts clients regularly. 她定期与客户重新联系。


一般过去时:
They recontacted me after a year.  他们一年后又联系了我。
I recontacted the company about the faulty product. 我就那个有缺陷的产品重新联系了该公司。


现在完成时:
I have recontacted him several times. 我已经联系了他几次。
She has recontacted all the potential investors. 她已经重新联系了所有潜在的投资者。


过去完成时:
They had recontacted the supplier before placing a new order. 他们在下新订单之前已经重新联系了供应商。
Before the deadline, she had recontacted all the interviewees. 在截止日期之前，她已经重新联系了所有受访者。


将来时:
I will recontact you next week. 我下周会再联系你。
They will recontact the witnesses for further information. 他们将重新联系证人以获取更多信息。


虚拟语气:
If I needed help, I would recontact you. 如果我需要帮助，我会重新联系你。
I wish I had recontacted her sooner. 我真希望我早点联系她。


被动语态:
The clients were recontacted by the sales team. 客户被销售团队重新联系了。
The participants will be recontacted in a few weeks. 参与者将在几周内被重新联系。


疑问句:
Will you be recontacting the candidates? 你会重新联系候选人吗？
Have they recontacted the author yet? 他们已经重新联系作者了吗？


否定句:
I didn't recontact him. 我没有重新联系他。
They haven't recontacted me yet. 他们还没有联系我。
