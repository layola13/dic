narratives:/ˈnærətɪvz/| n. |叙事，故事，记述 |Accounts or descriptions of events; a story or series of events, especially one that is fictional.  |Example:  The book contains several interwoven narratives. 这本书包含几个交织在一起的故事。  The film's narrative is complex and multifaceted.  这部电影的叙事复杂而多方面。|近义词：stories, accounts, tales, chronicles |反义词：facts (in contrast to fictional narratives)
