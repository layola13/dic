luciferous:/luːˈsɪfərəs/ | adj. | 发光的；光明；带来光明的 | Bringing or giving light; luminous. The word evokes images of light and illumination, often with a connotation of brilliance or even supernatural radiance.  It is less commonly used than synonyms like luminous or radiant. | Example: The luciferous stars shone brightly in the night sky.  夜空中闪耀着明亮的星星。 The luciferous dawn broke through the darkness.  光明破晓驱散了黑暗。| 近义词：luminous, radiant, bright, shining | 反义词：dark, obscure, shadowy
