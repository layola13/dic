levitator: /ˈlɛvɪteɪtər/ | n. | 悬浮者；使…悬浮的装置 | A person or device that levitates.  A levitator uses various methods, often electromagnetic, to lift an object without physical support.  | 例句：The magician amazed the audience with his levitator skills. 魔术师用他的悬浮技巧让观众惊叹不已。 The magnetic levitator lifted the train effortlessly. 磁悬浮列车毫不费力地升了起来。 | 近义词：levitater, lifter (in some contexts) | 反义词：grounder, weigher (in some contexts)
