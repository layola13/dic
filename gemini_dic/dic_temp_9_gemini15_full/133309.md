nonexploitable: /ˌnɒnɪkˈsplɔɪtəbəl/ | adj. |不可利用的，无法剥削的 |  Not able to be exploited or used for unfair gain.  This adjective describes something that cannot be taken advantage of or misused for personal profit or benefit. It emphasizes the inability to manipulate or control something for one's own ends. | The security system is designed to be nonexploitable, preventing hackers from gaining unauthorized access.  这个安全系统设计成不可利用的，防止黑客获得未经授权的访问。| 近义词：inexploitable, unusable, unworkable | 反义词：exploitable, usable, workable |
