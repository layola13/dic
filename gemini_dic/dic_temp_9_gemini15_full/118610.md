messages:/ˈmɛsɪdʒɪz/| n. |消息；信息；启示|A message is a piece of information that is sent from one person or thing to another.  It can be written, spoken, or even implied through actions.  Messages can be personal, formal, or informal, depending on the context.|例句：I left a message on his answering machine. 我在他的答录机上留言了。|例句：The message was clear: we needed to act quickly. 信息很明确：我们需要迅速行动。|近义词：communications, announcements, notifications|反义词：silence, secrecy

We receive many messages daily. = 我们每天收到很多信息。
