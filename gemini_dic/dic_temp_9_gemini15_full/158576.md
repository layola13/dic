pleuritis:/pluəˈraɪtɪs/| n. |胸膜炎|Inflammation of the pleura, the membrane surrounding the lungs.  The inflammation can be caused by various factors, including infections (viral, bacterial, or fungal), autoimmune diseases, or exposure to irritants. Symptoms can include chest pain, cough, shortness of breath, and fever.|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with pleuritis after reviewing her chest X-ray and listening to her lungs.|近义词：pleurisy|反义词:None.
