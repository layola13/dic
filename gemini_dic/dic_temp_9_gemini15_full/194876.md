semi-deity:/ˈsɛmɪˌdeɪəti/| n. |半神，神祇 |A being of intermediate rank between gods and humans, often possessing divine powers but also human weaknesses.  They are often the offspring of a god and a human, or a demigod and a human. |Example: In Greek mythology, Heracles is a famous semi-deity.  希腊神话中，赫拉克勒斯是一位著名的半神。|近义词：demigod, minor deity |反义词：god, mortal
