reballot:/ˌriːˈbælət/| v.|重新投票，再次投票|To vote again on something, typically because the previous vote was inconclusive or invalid.|The council decided to reballot on the controversial proposal. 市议会决定就这一有争议的提案进行重新投票。|近义词：revote, re-vote|反义词：confirm, ratify|动词
