honour:/ˈɒnə(r)/ /ˈɑːnər/| n./vt.|荣誉；尊敬；给予荣誉|The noun "honour" refers to high respect, esteem, or admiration; a source of pride or distinction;  a privilege or distinction conferred upon someone; or a sense of moral or ethical duty. The verb "honour" means to show respect or admiration for; to fulfill (a promise, commitment, etc.); or to accept (a cheque, etc.) as valid.|例句：He received an award in honour of his achievements. 他因其成就而获奖。|She honoured her promise to visit him. 她遵守了去看望他的诺言。|近义词：glory, prestige, respect, dignity|反义词：dishonor, shame, disgrace|名词/动词
