posting:/ˈpoʊstɪŋ/| n.|帖子；公布；邮寄|The act of placing something, typically information or an advertisement, in a public place or online; the act of sending something by mail.|例句：The posting of the job description attracted many applicants.  职位描述的发布吸引了许多申请者。|例句：The delay in posting the letters caused a problem. 邮寄信件的延误造成问题。|近义词：publication, advertisement, dispatch|反义词：removal, deletion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is posting updates on social media. = 他正在社交媒体上发布更新。
She posts pictures of her cat every day. = 她每天都发布她猫的照片。

一般过去时:
They posted the notice on the bulletin board. = 他们把通知贴在公告栏上。
I posted the package yesterday. = 我昨天寄出了包裹。

现在完成时:
She has posted several articles on her blog. = 她已经在她的博客上发表了几篇文章。
He has posted the letter, so we can expect an answer soon. = 他已经寄出信件了，所以我们很快就能收到回复。

过去完成时:
They had posted the results before the meeting. = 他们会在会议前公布结果。
She had posted the letter a week before it arrived. = 她在一周前寄出的信件才到达。

将来时:
I will post the announcement tomorrow. = 我明天将发布公告。
They will post the package next week. = 他们下周将寄出包裹。

虚拟语气:
If I posted the letter earlier, it would have arrived by now. = 如果我早点寄出信件，它现在应该已经到了。
I wish I had posted the application earlier. = 我真希望我早点提交申请。

被动语态:
The news was posted on the company website. = 新闻发布在公司网站上。
The package is being posted today. = 包裹今天正在邮寄。

疑问句:
Where did you post the announcement? = 你在哪里发布了公告？
When will you post the package? = 你什么时候寄包裹？

否定句:
I didn't post the letter yet. = 我还没寄信。
They haven't posted the results online. = 他们还没有在网上公布结果。
