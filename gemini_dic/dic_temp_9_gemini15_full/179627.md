reencourages:/ˌriːɪnˈkʌrɪdʒɪz/| v.|重新鼓励，再次鼓舞|To encourage again; to give renewed encouragement to.|例句：The coach reencouraged the team after their loss. 教练在他们输球后再次鼓励了球队。
例句：Her success story reencouraged many young people to pursue their dreams. 她的成功故事再次鼓励了许多年轻人去追求他们的梦想。|近义词：reinvigorate, inspire anew, hearten again|反义词：discourage, dispirit, depress|动词
