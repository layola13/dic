persist:/pərˈsɪst/| vi.|坚持，持续|To continue to exist or occur; to continue doing something despite difficulties or opposition.|例句：The pain persisted for several days. 疼痛持续了好几天。  例句：Despite the setbacks, she persisted in her efforts. 尽管遭遇挫折，她仍然坚持不懈。|近义词：continue, endure, persevere|反义词：cease, stop, discontinue|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He persists in his belief. = 他坚持他的信仰。
They persist in their efforts. = 他们坚持他们的努力。

一般过去时:
She persisted in her studies despite difficulties. = 尽管困难重重，她仍然坚持学习。
The problem persisted throughout the project. = 这个问题在整个项目中一直存在。

现在完成时:
The rain has persisted all day. = 雨下了一整天。
He has persisted in his denial. = 他一直否认。

过去完成时:
The pain had persisted for weeks before he finally sought medical attention. = 疼痛持续了几周，他才最终寻求医疗救助。
They had persisted in their efforts for years before achieving success. = 他们在取得成功之前努力奋斗了很多年。

将来时:
The drought will likely persist for several more months. = 干旱可能会持续数月。
I will persist in my search for the truth. = 我将坚持寻找真相。

虚拟语气:
If he persisted, he might succeed. = 如果他坚持下去，他可能会成功。
I wish he would persist in his studies. = 我希望他能坚持学习。

被动语态:
The problem was persisted with for too long.  = 这个问题被拖延得太久了。(此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The rumor was persistently spread. = 谣言被持续传播。


疑问句:
Will the symptoms persist? = 症状还会持续吗？
Does the problem persist? = 这个问题仍然存在吗？

否定句:
The pain did not persist. = 疼痛没有持续。
He did not persist in his efforts. = 他没有坚持他的努力。


PHRASE->
persist in = 坚持
persist with = 坚持做...
persistently = 持续地，坚持地
insist on persisting = 坚持要坚持
persist despite = 尽管...仍然坚持
