insurgencies:/ɪnˈsɜːrdʒənsiz/| n. |叛乱；暴动；起义|The act or state of rebelling or resisting against established authority.  Insurgencies are often characterized by armed conflict and a challenge to the existing government or social order.|例句：The government is struggling to suppress the various insurgencies in the region.  政府正在努力镇压该地区的多起叛乱。

例句：The country has been plagued by insurgencies for decades. 这个国家几十年来一直饱受叛乱之苦。


近义词：rebellions, revolts, uprisings, mutinies|反义词：obedience, compliance, submission|词性：名词
