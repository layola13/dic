hiring:/ˈhaɪrɪŋ/| n. |招聘，雇用|The act of employing someone.  It refers to the process of finding, evaluating, and selecting candidates for a job.  This includes advertising the position, reviewing applications, conducting interviews, and making a job offer.|例句：The hiring process is very competitive. 招聘过程竞争非常激烈。|例句：The company is currently undertaking a major hiring drive. 公司目前正在进行大规模招聘。|近义词：employment, recruitment, engagement|反义词：firing, dismissal, layoff


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is hiring new graduates. = 公司正在招聘应届毕业生。
He is hiring a new assistant. = 他正在招聘一位新的助理。


一般过去时:
They hired ten new employees last month. = 他们上个月雇佣了十名新员工。
The factory hired many temporary workers during the busy season. = 工厂在旺季雇佣了许多临时工。


现在完成时:
We have hired three people for the marketing team. = 我们已经为市场团队招聘了三个人。
The company has been hiring aggressively this year. = 今年公司一直在积极招聘。


过去完成时:
They had already hired a replacement before he resigned. = 他辞职之前，他们已经聘请了一名替补人员。
The project manager had hired a consultant before the project started. = 项目经理在项目开始之前已经聘请了一名顾问。


将来时:
We will be hiring for several new positions next quarter. = 我们将在下个季度为几个新的职位招聘。
They will hire someone with experience in data analysis. = 他们将聘请一位具有数据分析经验的人。


虚拟语气:
If they hired more staff, they could finish the project on time. = 如果他们雇佣更多员工，他们就能按时完成项目。
I wish they had hired a more experienced project manager. = 我希望他们当时聘请一位更有经验的项目经理。


被动语态:
Ten new employees were hired last month. = 上个月雇佣了十名新员工。
A new marketing manager is being hired currently. = 目前正在招聘新的市场经理。


疑问句:
Are they still hiring for that position? = 他们还在招聘那个职位吗？
Is the company actively hiring right now? = 公司现在正在积极招聘吗？


否定句:
They are not hiring at the moment. = 他们目前不招聘。
The company did not hire anyone without experience. = 公司没有招聘任何没有经验的人。
