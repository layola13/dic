captivative: /ˈkæptɪveɪtɪv/ | adj. | 迷人的，吸引人的 |  Engaging, enchanting, and holding one's attention completely.  Something captivative is so interesting or attractive that it holds your attention completely. | 例句：The captivating story held the audience spellbound. = 迷人的故事让观众着迷。

The captivating melody lingered in my mind long after the concert ended. = 音乐会结束后很久，动听的旋律还萦绕在我的脑海中。


近义词：enthralling, fascinating, alluring, charming, enchanting | 反义词：boring, dull, uninteresting, repulsive


词性:形容词
