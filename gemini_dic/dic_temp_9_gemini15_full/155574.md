philology:/fɪˈlɒlədʒi/ /fɪˈlɑːlədʒi/ | n. |语言学；文献学|The scientific study of language and its history, including the study of the structure, history, and development of languages, as well as the relationship between languages.|Philology encompasses a wide range of disciplines, from historical linguistics to the study of ancient texts.|Linguistics, comparative linguistics, historical linguistics|None|名词
