conformity:/kənˈfɔːrməti/ | n. | 一致；符合；顺从 | The state or fact of conforming; the action or practice of conforming to a standard.  It refers to the act of matching attitudes, beliefs, and behaviors to group norms.  This can be driven by a desire to fit in, gain approval, or avoid social rejection. |  例句：There is a strong pressure for conformity in many social groups.  在许多社会群体中，存在着强大的从众压力。

例句：His conformity to the company's dress code was unquestionable. 他对公司着装规范的遵守无可置疑。


近义词：compliance, adherence, agreement, obedience | 反义词：nonconformity, defiance, rebellion, individuality
