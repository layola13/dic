shod:/ʃɒd/| /ʃɑːd/ | v. | 穿鞋；给…穿鞋 | To provide (someone or something) with shoes or footwear.  The word often implies a covering or protection, like horseshoes. | Example: The blacksmith shod the horse. = 那个铁匠给马钉了马蹄铁。 The children were shod in sturdy boots for their hike. = 孩子们为了远足穿上了结实的靴子。 | 近义词：fitted, equipped | 反义词：unshod, barefoot

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shoes the horses every morning. = 他每天早上都给马钉蹄铁。
The cobbler shoes many people in the town. = 那个鞋匠为镇上很多人钉鞋。

一般过去时:
The farrier shod the horse quickly and efficiently. = 蹄铁匠快速有效地为马钉上了蹄铁。
She shod her horse before the long ride. = 她在长途骑行前给马穿上了马蹄铁。

现在完成时:
The horses have been shod for the race. = 马匹已经为比赛穿上了蹄铁。
I have shod all the animals in the stable. = 我已经为马厩里的所有动物钉上了蹄铁。

过去完成时:
The horse had been shod before the accident. = 事故发生前，马已经钉上了蹄铁。
They had shod the mules before beginning the journey. = 他们在开始旅程之前已经给骡子钉上了蹄铁。

将来时:
The farrier will shod the horses tomorrow. = 蹄铁匠明天将给马钉蹄铁。
We will shod the animals before the winter storm. = 我们会在暴风雪来临之前为动物们钉上蹄铁。

虚拟语气:
If I had shod the horse properly, it wouldn't have gone lame. = 如果我当初给马钉蹄铁钉得合适，它就不会瘸了。
I wish I had shod my horse before the long journey. = 我真希望在长途旅行前已经给我的马钉了蹄铁。

被动语态:
The horses were shod by the blacksmith. = 马匹是由铁匠钉蹄铁的。
The animals are being shod this afternoon. = 动物们今天下午正在钉蹄铁。

疑问句:
Were the horses shod before the race? = 比赛前马匹钉蹄铁了吗？
Have the oxen been shod yet? = 牛已经钉蹄铁了吗？

否定句:
The horse was not shod properly. = 马的蹄铁没有钉好。
They didn't shod the donkey before the trip. = 他们在旅行前没有给驴子钉蹄铁。

barefoot and unshod = 赤脚的，没穿鞋的
