ethics: /ˈɛθɪks/ | n. |伦理学，道德规范 | The philosophical study of moral principles; a set of moral principles governing a person's behaviour or the conducting of an activity.  | Example:  The ethics of genetic engineering are hotly debated.  基因工程的伦理问题正在激烈争论中。

Example: Medical ethics require doctors to prioritize patient well-being.  医学伦理要求医生优先考虑病人的福祉。


近义词：morals, principles, values, morality | 反义词：immorality, unethical behavior
