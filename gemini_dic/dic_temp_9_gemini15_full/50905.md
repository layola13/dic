deserts:/ˈdɛzərts/ | n. |沙漠；应得的惩罚；抛弃|  A desert is a barren land with little or no vegetation.  It can also refer to the deserved punishment or abandonment of someone or something.| The Sahara Desert is the largest hot desert in the world.  撒哈拉沙漠是世界上最大的热带沙漠。|He deserved the deserts of his actions. 他罪有应得。|近义词：wilderness, wasteland,  penalty |反义词：oasis, paradise, reward


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The desert expands yearly. = 沙漠每年都在扩张。
This area deserves its reputation as a desert. = 这个地区名副其实是个沙漠。


一般过去时:
The explorers crossed the desert. = 探险家们穿越了沙漠。
He deserved punishment for his crime. = 他应该为他的罪行受到惩罚。


现在完成时:
We have crossed many deserts on our journey. = 在我们的旅程中，我们已经穿越了许多沙漠。
She has deserved all the success she's achieved. = 她当之无愧地获得了所有的成功。


过去完成时:
They had already crossed the desert before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临之前就已经穿越了沙漠。
He had deserved a better fate, but his actions led to his downfall. = 他本应该拥有更好的命运，但他的行为导致了他的失败。


将来时:
We will explore the desert next year. = 我们明年将探索沙漠。
They will receive their just deserts. = 他们将受到应得的惩罚。


虚拟语气:
If I were in the desert, I would need lots of water. = 如果我在沙漠里，我会需要很多水。

被动语态:
The desert is being studied by scientists. = 沙漠正在被科学家们研究。
His actions were justly deserved. = 他的行为得到了应有的惩罚。


疑问句:
Is this a desert? = 这是沙漠吗？
