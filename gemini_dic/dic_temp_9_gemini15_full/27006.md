calculating:/ˈkælkjuleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 算计的；精明的；有心机的 |  Characterized by careful and shrewd thought; calculating; scheming.  It implies a deliberate and often self-serving use of intelligence. | 例句：He had a calculating look in his eyes. 他眼中带着精明的目光。 | 例句：Her calculating nature allowed her to manipulate the situation to her advantage. 她精明的本性使她能够操纵局势以利于自己。 | 近义词：scheming, cunning, shrewd, manipulative | 反义词：naive, ingenuous, guileless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is calculating the total cost. = 他正在计算总成本。
She is calculating the risks involved. = 她正在计算所涉及的风险。


一般过去时:
They calculated the distance to the moon. = 他们计算了到月球的距离。
He calculated his chances of success. = 他盘算着自己成功的几率。


现在完成时:
She has calculated the area of the room. = 她已经计算出了房间的面积。
We have calculated the profits for the year. = 我们已经计算出了今年的利润。


过去完成时:
They had calculated the trajectory before launching the rocket. = 他们发射火箭前已经计算好了弹道。
She had calculated the budget before presenting it. = 她在提交预算之前已经计算好了预算。


将来时:
We will calculate the results tomorrow. = 我们明天将计算结果。
They will calculate the impact of the new policy. = 他们将计算新政策的影响。


虚拟语气:
If I had calculated correctly, I would have won the lottery. = 如果我计算正确的话，我就中彩票了。
I wish I had calculated the travel time more accurately. = 我希望我更准确地计算了旅行时间。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
