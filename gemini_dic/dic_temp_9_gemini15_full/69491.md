felicitation:/fəˌlɪsɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |祝贺；庆贺 | Congratulations offered to someone on a happy occasion.  It expresses pleasure at someone's good fortune or success. |例句：The company offered felicitations to its employees on the successful launch of the new product. 公司对员工成功推出新产品表示祝贺。

例句：She received many felicitations on her wedding day.  她结婚那天收到了许多祝贺。


近义词：congratulation, commendation, compliment

反义词：condolence


词性:名词
