overfluent: /ˌoʊvərˈfluːənt/ | adj. |过于流利的，词藻华丽的 | excessively fluent; characterized by an abundance of words; too wordy | The speaker's overfluent style, while impressive, sometimes obscured his message.  演讲者过于流利的风格虽然令人印象深刻，但有时却模糊了他的信息。 | fluent, articulate, verbose | concise, succinct, terse | 形容词
