overground:/ˈoʊvərɡraʊnd/ | adj., n. | 地上的；地面上的；地面铁路；地面交通 | Above ground; relating to or operating on the surface of the ground, as opposed to underground.  |例句：The pipes are overground, not buried.  管道在地面上，而不是埋在地下。

例句：The overground train is a convenient way to travel across the city. 地面火车是穿越城市的一种便捷方式。


近义词：aboveground, surface, elevated


反义词：underground, subterranean


词性: adjective, noun
