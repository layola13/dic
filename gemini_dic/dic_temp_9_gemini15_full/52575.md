diffusil:/dɪˈfjuːzɪl/| adj.|弥散的；扩散的；传播的 |  Relating to or characterized by diffusion; spreading widely or gradually.  The term is less common than its related words "diffusive" and "diffuse". It suggests a process of spreading or scattering that is often gradual and pervasive. | Example: The diffusil nature of the rumors made it difficult to trace their origin.  弥散的谣言使得难以追踪其来源。  |近义词：diffusive, diffuse, dispersive|反义词：concentrated, localized
