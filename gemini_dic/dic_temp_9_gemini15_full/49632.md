demigration:/ˌdɛmɪˈɡreɪʃən/| n. |人口迁移（通常指离开某地）, 衰退|The process of a group of people moving out of a region or country, often implying a decline in population or economic activity.  It often refers to a mass exodus from a place. |Example: The demigration from rural areas to cities has led to a decline in the rural population.  乡村地区向城市的人口迁移导致农村人口减少。|近义词：emigration, exodus, migration (when implying leaving), out-migration |反义词：immigration, in-migration, population growth
