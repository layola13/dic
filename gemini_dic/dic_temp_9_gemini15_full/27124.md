caliphal:/ˈkælɪfəl/| adj.|哈里发的；哈里发统治的；伊斯兰教哈里发制度的|Of or relating to a caliph or the caliphate.  The adjective describes things connected to the caliph, the title of the supreme religious and political leader in Islam after Muhammad's death, or to the system of government under a caliph.|例句：The caliphal court was renowned for its lavishness and power. 哈里发宫廷以其奢华和权力而闻名。 The caliphal armies conquered vast territories. 哈里发军队征服了广阔的领土。|近义词：imperial, sultanic |反义词：republican, democratic
