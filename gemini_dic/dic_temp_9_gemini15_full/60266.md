egad:/ˈeɡæd/| int.|哎呀；我的天哪|An exclamation of surprise, astonishment, or dismay, similar to "good heavens!" or "oh my!"  It is now largely archaic or used humorously to evoke a period feel.|例句：Egad, man, what happened here? 哎呀，伙计，这里发生了什么事？|近义词：good heavens, oh my, my goodness, wow, gosh|反义词:None.


USAGE->
This word is an interjection, so it doesn't have typical verb conjugations like other parts of speech.  It's used as a standalone exclamation.

PHRASE->
Egad! = 哎呀！/我的天哪！
Egad, that's amazing! = 哎呀，太神奇了！
Egad, I'm late! = 我的天，我迟到了！
Egad, what a mess! = 哎呀，真是一团糟！
Egad, you scared me! = 哎呀，你吓到我了！
