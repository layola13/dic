monsters:/ˈmɒnstəz/ /ˈmɑːnstərz/| n. |怪物，妖怪；可怕的人或事物 |Creatures of myth or legend, often grotesque or frightening in appearance and possessing supernatural powers.  They can also refer to people or things that are exceptionally unpleasant or frightening.|例句：The children were terrified by the monsters in the movie.孩子们被电影里的怪物吓坏了。

例句：He's a monster; he treats his employees terribly. 他是个可怕的人；他对待员工非常糟糕。


近义词：beast, creature, fiend, ogre, demon |反义词：angel, saint, hero
