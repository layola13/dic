parks:/pɑːrks/| n.|公园，园地|An area of land, typically in a town or city, that is set aside for recreational use and often contains trees, grass, and other natural features.  It can also refer to a specific area within a larger space. |例句：We spent the afternoon relaxing in the city parks. 我们在城市公园里度过了轻松的下午。

例句：The national parks are home to a variety of wildlife. 国家公园是各种野生动物的家园。


近义词：garden, reserve, common, grounds|反义词：built-up area, urban sprawl
