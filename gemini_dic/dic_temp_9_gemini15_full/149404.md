packagers:/ˈpækɪdʒərz/| n. |包装商；打包程序|People or companies that package goods for distribution or software packages for installation.  They prepare products for sale or use by putting them in containers or creating installation files.|例句：The packagers ensured the products arrived undamaged. 包装商确保产品完好无损地到达。|例句：Software packagers often include helpful documentation. 软件打包程序通常包含有用的文档。|近义词：packers, distributors, installers|反义词：unpackers, disassemblers
