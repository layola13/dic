disposability:/ˌdɪˌspoʊzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可丢弃性；一次性；易处理性 | The quality of being disposable; the ease with which something can be discarded or disposed of.  It often refers to products designed for single use and then discarded, but can also refer to the ease of getting rid of something unwanted. | Example: The high disposability of plastic products contributes to environmental pollution.  一次性塑料制品的高可丢弃性导致了环境污染。 The disposability of the evidence made it difficult for the police to solve the case.  证据的易处理性使得警方难以破案。| 近义词：discardability, expendability | 反义词：durability, reusability, permanence
