microbicide:/ˈmaɪkrəʊbaɪsaɪd/| n.|杀微生物剂，杀菌剂|A substance that kills microorganisms, such as bacteria, viruses, or fungi.|Example:  Researchers are developing new microbicides to combat sexually transmitted infections.  研究人员正在开发新的杀微生物剂来对抗性传播感染。|近义词：germicide, antimicrobial, antiseptic|反义词：None specifically, but could be considered the opposite of a microorganism-promoting substance.
