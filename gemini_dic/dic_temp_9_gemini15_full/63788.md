epicyclic:/ˌɛpɪˈsaɪklɪk/| adj.| 周转的；本轮的；偏心轮的 | Relating to or denoting a system of circular motion in which a point moves on a circle whose center itself moves on another circle.  This is often used to describe gears or planetary systems. | Example: The epicyclic gear system is efficient for transmitting power at variable speeds.  这个周转齿轮系统能够高效地以可变速度传递动力。|近义词：planetary (in the context of gears)|反义词：无
