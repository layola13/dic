reuniting: /ˌriːˈjuːnɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 团聚；重聚 | The act of bringing people or things together again after a period of separation.  |例句：The family is reuniting for the holidays.  家人正在为假期团聚。

例句：After years apart, the old friends were finally reuniting.  多年分离后，老朋友们终于重聚了。


近义词：rejoining, reassembling, reconvening


反义词：separating, parting, dividing


词性：动词 (Verb)
