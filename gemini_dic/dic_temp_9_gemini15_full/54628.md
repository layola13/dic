dismissal's:/dɪsˈmɪsəlz/| n.|解雇；开除；驳回|The plural possessive form of "dismissal," referring to something belonging to or associated with dismissals.  It indicates ownership or relationship. For example, "the dismissal's impact" refers to the effect of one or more dismissals.|例句：The dismissal's impact on morale was significant.  这次解雇对士气的影响很大。|近义词：discharge's, firing's, sacking's|反义词：appointment's, hiring's,  retention's|名词
