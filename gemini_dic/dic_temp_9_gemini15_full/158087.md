plastic:/ˈplæstɪk/ | n. adj. |塑料；塑料的；可塑的；人工的 |A synthetic material made from a wide range of organic polymers, often flexible and moldable when heated.  It is used extensively in a variety of applications, from packaging to construction. |例句：The bag is made of plastic. 这个袋子是塑料做的。

The artist used plasticine to sculpt the figure.  艺术家用橡皮泥塑造了这个雕像。

近义词：synthetic, artificial, pliable, flexible |反义词：rigid, inflexible, natural
