inuendo:/ɪˈnjuːændəʊ/ | n. | 暗示；影射；弦外之音 | A hint, suggestion, or insinuation about something that is usually considered improper or taboo.  It often implies something more than what is explicitly stated. | Example: Her comments contained a strong inuendo of disapproval.  她的评论中含有强烈的不满暗示。 |近义词：insinuation, implication, suggestion, hint |反义词：explicit statement, direct statement |词性:名词
