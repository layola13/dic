disappointingly:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪŋli/ | adv. |令人失望地，令人沮丧地 |  The adverb "disappointingly" means in a way that causes disappointment or sadness. It describes how something happened or was done, emphasizing the negative outcome. |例句：Disappointingly, the results of the experiment were negative. =令人失望的是，实验结果是负面的。

The weather was disappointingly cold for a summer day. =对于夏日来说，天气令人失望地寒冷。|近义词：depressingly, frustratingly, sadly, unfortunately |反义词：pleasingly, delightfully, happily, encouragingly
