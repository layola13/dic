powered-down: /ˈpaʊərd daʊn/ | adj. | 关闭的；断电的 |  Describes a device or system that has been turned off, typically intentionally.  It implies a deliberate action to switch something off, rather than a simple failure. |


例句：

The server was powered down for maintenance. = 服务器已关闭以进行维护。

My laptop is powered down for the night. = 我的笔记本电脑已关机过夜。


近义词：off, shut down, deactivated, switched off

反义词：powered on, on, active, running

词性：形容词
