douses:/ˈdaʊzɪz/| vt.|浇湿；泼水；使…熄灭|To wet thoroughly; to extinguish (a fire) by pouring water or other liquid onto it.  |例句：The fireman doused the flames with water. 消防员用水浇灭了火焰。
例句：He doused himself with cold water to cool down. 他用冷水浇自己来降温。|近义词：soak, saturate, quench|反义词：dry, ignite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He douses the plants every morning. = 他每天早上都给植物浇水。
She douses the campfire before leaving. = 她离开前把营火浇灭了。

一般过去时:
The firefighter doused the burning building. = 消防员浇灭了燃烧的建筑物。
He doused his face with cold water. = 他用冷水洗了脸。

现在完成时:
The rain has doused the entire field. = 雨水已经浇湿了整个田地。
They have doused the bonfire and cleaned up the area. = 他们已经把篝火浇灭并清理了该区域。

过去完成时:
They had doused the flames before the fire department arrived. = 消防队到达之前，他们已经把火焰浇灭了。
She had doused the towels in cold water before applying them to her face. = 她用冷水浸湿毛巾后才敷在脸上。

将来时:
He will douse the flowers with water tomorrow. = 他明天将给花浇水。
The gardener will douse the plants with insecticide. = 园丁将用杀虫剂喷洒植物。

虚拟语气:
If I had a fire extinguisher, I would douse the fire quickly. = 如果我有一个灭火器，我会很快把火扑灭。
I wish I could douse the rumours spreading about me. = 我希望我能扑灭关于我的谣言。


被动语态:
The fire was doused by the firefighters. = 火被消防员扑灭了。
The field was doused by a heavy downpour. = 田地被一场大暴雨浇透了。


疑问句:
Did he douse the candles before leaving the room? = 他离开房间前把蜡烛熄灭了吗？
Will you douse the burning log with water? = 你会用水浇灭燃烧的木头吗？


否定句:
He didn't douse the campfire completely. = 他没有完全把营火浇灭。
She didn't douse the plants because it rained. = 因为下雨了，她没有给植物浇水。
