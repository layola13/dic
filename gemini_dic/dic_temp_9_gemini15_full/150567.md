/ˈpɑːpɑːz/ | n. |  土豆;爸爸们（复数）|  The word "papas" is the plural of "papa," a term of endearment for "father" or "dad," especially used by children.  It can also refer to potatoes, particularly in Spanish-speaking cultures. | 例句：The children called their father papas.孩子们叫他们的父亲爸爸。 The papas were delicious, roasted with herbs.烤土豆配香草很美味。| 近义词：(as father) dad, daddy, father; (as potatoes) potatoes, spuds | 反义词：无
