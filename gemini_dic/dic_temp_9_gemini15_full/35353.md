co-foundress:/ˈkoʊfaʊndrəs/ | n. |女联合创始人 | A woman who co-founded a company or organization.  It implies a shared role in establishing the entity. |She is a co-foundress of a successful tech startup.|近义词：female co-founder, joint founder (female)|反义词：sole founder
