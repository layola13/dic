dia:/ˈdaɪə/| n.|直径， диаметр|A straight line passing from side to side through the center of a body or figure, especially a circle or sphere.|例句：The diameter of the circle is 10cm.  圆的直径是10厘米。|近义词：width, breadth|反义词：radius,半径
