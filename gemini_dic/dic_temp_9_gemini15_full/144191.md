It often involves a sense of not belonging, being different, or lacking acceptance. |例句：His outsiderness made him feel isolated and lonely.他的局外人地位让他感到孤立和孤独。

例句：The novel explores the themes of outsiderness and alienation in modern society. 这部小说探讨了现代社会中局外人地位和疏离感等主题。


近义词：alienation, isolation, exclusion, marginality, estrangement
