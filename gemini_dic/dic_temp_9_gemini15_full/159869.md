polymerism:/pɒˈlɪmərɪzəm/| n. |聚合现象，多形性|The existence of several forms of the same chemical compound.  This can be due to different arrangements of atoms within the molecule (structural isomerism), different spatial arrangements of atoms (stereoisomerism), or different ways the molecules are linked together (polymerization).|例句：The phenomenon of polymerism is crucial in understanding the diversity of macromolecules.  聚合现象对于理解大分子的多样性至关重要。|例句：The study of polymerism in crystals revealed interesting properties. 晶体多形性研究揭示了有趣的特性。|近义词：polymorphism, isomerism|反义词：monomorphism (单形性)
