overresponded:/ˌoʊvərɪˈspɑːndɪd/ | v. |反应过度|To react or respond in a way that is excessive or disproportionate to the stimulus or situation.  It implies a stronger, more intense response than necessary or appropriate.|The audience overresponded to the comedian's jokes, laughing hysterically at even the mildest ones. 观众对喜剧演员的笑话反应过度，即使是最温和的笑话也笑得歇斯底里。|responded, reacted|underresponded|动词
