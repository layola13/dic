devoured:/dɪˈvaʊərd/ | vt. |吞食；狼吞虎咽；吞噬；全神贯注地阅读| to eat (something) eagerly and in large amounts; to completely consume or destroy something. |例句：The hungry children devoured the pizza in minutes.  饥饿的孩子们几分钟内就吃掉了披萨。

例句：She devoured the book in a single sitting. 她一口气读完了那本书。


近义词：gobble, gulp, consume, inhale, engulf


反义词：starve, nibble


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He devours his food every day. 他每天都狼吞虎咽地吃饭。
The fire devours the forest. 大火吞噬着森林。


一般过去时:
She devoured the entire cake. 她吃掉了整个蛋糕。
The flames devoured the building.  火焰吞噬了建筑物。


现在完成时:
The army has devoured the enemy's supplies.  军队已经消耗了敌人的补给。
He has devoured three books this week.  他这周已经读完了三本书。


过去完成时:
They had devoured all the food before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，他们已经吃掉了所有的食物。
The insect had devoured the leaf completely.  昆虫已经完全吃掉了叶子。


将来时:
The monster will devour the village.  怪物将会吞噬村庄。
I will devour this entire novel tonight. 我今晚会读完这本小说。


虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would devour that whole pie. 如果我饿了，我会把整个馅饼都吃掉。
If he had devoured the book sooner, he would have finished the assignment. 如果他早点读完那本书，他就能完成作业了。


被动语态:
The entire meal was devoured in under an hour. 整顿饭不到一小时就被吃光了。
The village was devoured by the fire. 村庄被大火吞噬了。


疑问句:
Has the dog devoured the bone already? 狗已经把骨头吃掉了吗？
Will the fire devour the entire forest? 大火会吞噬整个森林吗？


否定句:
The lion did not devour the zebra. 狮子没有吃斑马。
She did not devour the book as quickly as she had hoped. 她没有像希望的那样快速读完那本书。



PHRASE->
devour with one's eyes  贪婪地注视
devour a book  如饥似渴地读书
devour the scenery  欣赏风景
devour one's time  占用时间
devour resources  耗费资源


词源:
源自古法语 *devorer，来自拉丁语 *devorare，由 *de-（向下）和 *vorare（吞噬）构成。
