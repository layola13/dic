overweight:/ˌoʊvərˈweɪt/ | adj. |超重的，体重超标的 | Excessively heavy; weighing more than is considered normal or healthy.  |例句：He is overweight and needs to lose some weight. 他超重，需要减肥。
例句：The package is overweight and will incur extra charges.包裹超重，将产生额外费用。|近义词：obese, plump, heavy |反义词：underweight, slender, thin |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overweight. = 他超重。
She is overweight by 20 pounds. = 她超重20磅。


一般过去时:
He was overweight last year. = 他去年超重。
The package was overweight, so they charged extra. = 包裹超重，所以他们额外收费了。


现在完成时:
She has been overweight for many years. = 她多年来一直超重。
He has become overweight since he stopped exercising. = 自从他不运动以来，他就变胖了。


过去完成时:
She had been overweight for years before she started dieting. = 在她开始节食之前，她已经超重多年了。
The doctor said he had been overweight his entire life. = 医生说他一生都超重。


将来时:
He will be overweight if he continues to eat junk food. = 如果他继续吃垃圾食品，他会超重。
She will likely be overweight if she doesn't change her lifestyle. = 如果她不改变生活方式，她很可能会超重。


虚拟语气:
If I were overweight, I would try to lose weight. = 如果我超重，我会尝试减肥。
I wish I weren't overweight. = 我希望我不超重。


被动语态:
(Overweight is rarely used passively)  The athlete was deemed overweight for the competition. 运动员被认为超重，无法参加比赛。
The child was found to be overweight during a routine check-up. 在例行检查中发现孩子超重。


疑问句:
Is he overweight? = 他超重吗？
Are you overweight? = 你超重吗？


否定句:
He is not overweight. = 他不超重。
She is not overweight; she is just muscular. = 她不超重，她只是肌肉发达。


PHRASE->
overweight baggage = 超重行李
seriously overweight = 严重超重
become overweight = 变胖
dangerously overweight = 危险性超重
slightly overweight = 轻微超重

词源:
The word "overweight" combines the prefix "over-" (meaning "above" or "beyond") with the noun "weight."  It emerged naturally as a descriptive term to indicate exceeding a normal or healthy weight.  The word’s development reflects the increasing concern about weight and health in modern society.
