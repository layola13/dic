heights:/haɪts/ | n. | 高度；高处；顶点；极点 | The word "heights" refers to the measurement of something from base to top,  a high place or level, or a peak of achievement. It can be used literally to describe physical elevation, or figuratively to represent the peak of something abstract like success or power. |例句：The mountain's heights were breathtaking.  这座山的高度令人叹为观止。

The plane reached great heights.  飞机达到了很高的飞行高度。


近义词：elevation, altitude, peak, summit | 反义词：depths, lows, bottoms | 词性：名词
