refineries:/rɪˈfaɪnəriːz/| n. |炼油厂；精炼厂|A refinery is an industrial plant where crude oil is processed and refined into usable products such as gasoline, diesel fuel, and kerosene.  It involves complex chemical processes to separate and purify different components of crude oil.|例句：The oil refineries on the coast are crucial to the nation's economy.  沿海的炼油厂对国家的经济至关重要。

例句：Many refineries are located near major ports to facilitate the import and export of crude oil and refined products. 许多炼油厂位于主要港口附近，以方便原油和精炼产品的进出口。
