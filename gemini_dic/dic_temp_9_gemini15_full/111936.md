lolled:/lɒld/ | v. |懒洋洋地坐或躺; 慢吞吞地移动 | To sit, lie, or hang in a relaxed, lazy, or idle manner; to move slowly and languidly.  |例句：He lolled in a deck chair, reading a book. 他懒洋洋地躺在躺椅上看书。

例句：The old dog lolled its head on the porch. 老狗懒洋洋地把头搁在门廊上。


近义词： lounged, sprawled, slumped, sauntered | 反义词：hustled, rushed
