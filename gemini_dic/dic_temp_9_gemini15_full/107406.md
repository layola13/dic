laming:/ˈleɪmɪŋ/| v.|使跛；使衰弱；削弱；损害 |To make someone or something weaker or less effective.|例句：The injury lamed him for life.  这伤让他终身残疾。 |例句：The constant criticism lamed his efforts.  持续的批评削弱了他的努力。|近义词：crippling, weakening, impairing |反义词：strengthening, empowering


USAGE->

This policy lames the economic growth. =这项政策削弱了经济增长。
