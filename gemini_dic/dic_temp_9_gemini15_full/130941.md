nonassertions:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːʃənz/| n. |非断言；非陈述|Statements that do not make a claim or assertion; they may be questions, commands, exclamations, or other types of utterances that do not express a proposition.  They lack truth value. |Example: "Close the door!" is a nonassertion; it's a command.  "What a beautiful day!" is a nonassertion; it's an exclamation.|近义词:non-statements,  interjections,  questions, commands|反义词: assertions, statements, propositions|名词
