lassa-fevers:/ˈlæsə ˈfiːvərz/| n. |拉沙热 | Lassa fever is a viral hemorrhagic fever caused by the Lassa virus.  It is endemic in West Africa. Symptoms range from mild to severe, and in severe cases, it can be fatal. The virus is transmitted to humans via contact with food or household items contaminated with rodent urine or feces. |Example: The outbreak of Lassa fever in the region caused widespread panic and concern. 拉沙热在该地区的爆发引起了广泛的恐慌和担忧。|近义词:viral hemorrhagic fever, arenavirus infection|反义词: N/A
