shoestring: /ˈʃuːstrɪŋ/ | n. |鞋带；少量资金|

英文解析:A shoestring is a thin string used to tie a shoe.  Figuratively, it refers to a very small amount of money, often barely enough to start or continue something.

例句:

They started the business on a shoestring. = 他们用很少的钱启动了这家公司。

He lived on a shoestring budget. = 他过着非常节俭的生活。


近义词:minimal amount, small sum, pittance, trifle


反义词:abundance, fortune, plenty, wealth


词性:Noun
