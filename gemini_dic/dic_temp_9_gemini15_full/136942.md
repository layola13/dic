nonspeaking: /ˈnɒnˈspiːkɪŋ/ | adj. | 不说话的，不会说话的 |  Describes a person or animal who does not speak, often due to a disability or choice.  This can refer to inability to speak verbally or a refusal to speak. | 例句：The nonspeaking child communicated through gestures.  这个不会说话的孩子通过手势交流。


The nonspeaking patient needed alternative communication methods.  这位不会说话的病人需要替代性交流方法。


近义词：mute, speechless, voiceless, silent | 反义词：speaking, vocal, articulate | 词性: adjective
