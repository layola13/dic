mojoco:/moʊˈdʒoʊkoʊ/| n.|莫霍科（MuJoCo物理引擎）|MuJoCo (Multi-Joint Dynamics with Contact) is a physics engine for simulating articulated rigid body dynamics. It is known for its accuracy, speed, and ease of use in various applications, including robotics research, animation, and gaming.  |Example: Researchers are using MuJoCo to simulate robot locomotion and control strategies.|近义词：physics engine, simulator|反义词：N/A
