ciderkin:/ˈsaɪdərkɪn/| n. |苹果酒渣；苹果酒的残渣|Weak, thin cider made from the second pressing of apples, often used as an ingredient in other drinks or for feeding livestock. Ciderkin is essentially the leftover juice from the first pressing of cider apples, often containing less sugar and alcohol.|例句：The ciderkin was used to feed the pigs.|近义词：apple pomace, apple waste|反义词：hard cider, strong cider
