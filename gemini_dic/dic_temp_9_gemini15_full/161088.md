postdevelopmental:/ˌpoʊstˌdɛvəlˈɒpməntəl/ | adj. | 后发育的，发育后的 | Relating to the period of life after the completion of major developmental processes.  This term is often used in developmental psychology and biology to describe the stages of life following the primary growth and maturation periods.  The specific meaning can vary depending on the context, referring to physical, cognitive, or social aspects. | Example: Postdevelopmental changes in brain structure are often subtle but significant.  发育后大脑结构的变化通常是微妙但重要的。


|近义词: post-maturational, later-stage, subsequent  |反义词: developmental, pre-developmental |词性: adjective
