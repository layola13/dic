camping:/ˈkæmpɪŋ/| n. |露营，野营|The activity of spending a holiday living in a tent or other temporary shelter.  Camping is a popular outdoor activity.  |例句：We went camping in the mountains last weekend. = 我们上周末去山里露营了。

例句：They are planning a camping trip to Yellowstone National Park. = 他们正计划去黄石国家公园露营。


近义词：campout, bivouacking |反义词：indoors, staying home


词性:名词
