predrying:/ˈpriːˌdraɪɪŋ/| v. |预干燥，初步干燥|The process of partially drying something before a final drying stage.  It is often used to remove a significant portion of moisture to facilitate the subsequent drying process, making it more efficient and effective. |例句：Predrying the wood before painting prevents warping. = 涂漆前对木材进行预干燥可以防止翘曲。

The predrying step reduced the overall drying time by 20%. = 预干燥步骤将总干燥时间缩短了20%。


近义词：partial drying, preliminary drying |反义词：None (it's a process, not a state) |动词
