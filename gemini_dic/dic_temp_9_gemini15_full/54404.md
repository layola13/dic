disincarcerate:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkɑːrsəreɪt/| vt.|使脱离监禁；释放；使解脱|To release from imprisonment or confinement; to free from constraint or restriction.  The word implies a more formal or figurative release than simply "releasing" someone.|例句：The prisoner was finally disincarcerated after serving his full sentence. = 犯人在服满刑期后终于被释放了。

例句：She felt disincarcerated from the burden of her responsibilities after handing in her resignation. = 她辞职后感觉摆脱了责任的重担。

近义词：release, liberate, free, emancipate

反义词：incarcerate, imprison, confine, restrain

词性：动词
