quibbles:/ˈkwɪbəlz/| n.|吹毛求疵；细小的异议；琐碎的抱怨| minor complaints or objections;  petty arguments. |例句：He always quibbles over the smallest details. 他总是对最细微的细节吹毛求疵。

例句：She had a few quibbles about the contract. 她对合同有一些小小的异议。


近义词：complaints, objections, cavils, criticisms

反义词：approvals, agreements, acceptances


词性：名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often quibbles about the price. 他经常对价格吹毛求疵。
They quibble over insignificant matters. 他们为一些无关紧要的事情争吵。


一般过去时:
She quibbled about the quality of the food. 她对食物的质量表示不满。
We quibbled for hours over the details. 我们就细节争论了几个小时。


现在完成时:
He has quibbled about this issue for weeks. 他就这个问题争论了好几个星期。
They have quibbled over the terms of the agreement.  他们就协议条款争论不休。


过去完成时:
She had quibbled with her boss before the meeting.  她在会议前就和老板争论过。
They had quibbled all afternoon about the budget. 他们整个下午都在为预算问题争吵。


将来时:
He will quibble about the bill, I'm sure. 我肯定他会对账单吹毛求疵。
They will probably quibble about the deadline. 他们可能会对截止日期吹毛求疵。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't quibble so much, we'd finish the project sooner. 如果他不那么吹毛求疵，我们就能更快地完成项目。
I wish he wouldn't quibble over every little thing. 我希望他不要对每件小事都吹毛求疵。


被动语态:
The details were quibbled over endlessly.  细节被无休止地争论。
His suggestions were quibbled about by the team. 他的建议受到了团队的质疑。


疑问句:
Does he often quibble about his work? 他经常对工作吹毛求疵吗？
Will they quibble about the payment? 他们会对付款吹毛求疵吗？


否定句:
He didn't quibble about the price this time. 这次他没有对价格吹毛求疵。
They didn't quibble over the minor details. 他们没有对细枝末节吹毛求疵。


PHRASE->
to quibble over details  对细节吹毛求疵
to quibble about something 对某事吹毛求疵
to quibble with someone 与某人争吵
don't quibble 不要斤斤计较
petty quibbles  琐碎的抱怨


词源:  The word "quibble" comes from the Middle English word "quibbelen," meaning "to evade or equivocate,"  ultimately deriving from a word of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old French words associated with dodging or evading.
