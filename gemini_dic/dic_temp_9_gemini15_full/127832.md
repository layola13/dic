nematoidean:/ˌnɛməˈtɔɪdiən/| n. |线虫的；线虫类动物|A nematoidean is any member of the phylum Nematoda, a large group of roundworms.  They are found in virtually all environments, from soil and water to the bodies of plants and animals.  Many are free-living, while others are parasitic. |Example: The soil sample contained a high concentration of nematoidean species.  土壤样本中含有高浓度的线虫物种。 The farmer is concerned about the nematoidean infestation in his crops. 农民担心他的庄稼感染了线虫。|近义词：roundworm, nematode|反义词：无
