byline:/ˈbaɪlaɪn/| n. |作者署名行；（报刊文章等的）作者姓名栏 |The line at the beginning or end of an article giving the author's name.  It is often found in newspapers, magazines, and other publications. |Example: The byline of the article read "Jane Doe, Staff Writer." 这篇文章的作者署名行写着“简·多伊，特约撰稿人”。|近义词：author's name, credit line |反义词：无
