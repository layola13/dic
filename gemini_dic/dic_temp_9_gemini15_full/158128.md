plateaux:/ˈplætoʊz/| n.|高原；平台；稳定时期|The word "plateaux" is the plural of "plateau," referring to a highland area of relatively level terrain.  It can also refer to a period of little or no change or progress. | 例句：The Andes Mountains are characterized by high plateaux. 安第斯山脉的特点是高耸的山原。The company experienced a plateau in sales growth last quarter. 公司上个季度的销售增长停滞不前。|近义词：highlands, plains, tablelands, standstill|反义词：valley, decline, drop
