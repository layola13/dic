chucklehead:/ˈtʃʌkəlˌhed/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋，蠢货 | A chucklehead is a foolish or stupid person.  The term is informal and often used playfully or affectionately, though it can also be insulting depending on context. | Example:  He's such a chucklehead; he fell for that obvious scam. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然上当受骗了。 |近义词：fool, idiot, dunce, simpleton |反义词：genius, intellect, mastermind |名词
