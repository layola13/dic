preloaded:/ˌpriːˈloʊdəd/ | adj. | 预先加载的；预先装载的 | Already loaded or installed before the main use or operation begins.  This adjective describes something that is already in place and ready to go, eliminating the need for additional loading or installation. |Example: The software came with preloaded games.  这个软件预装了一些游戏。

The operating system had several preloaded applications. 操作系统预装了几个应用程序。


近义词：preinstalled, pre-installed, ready-to-use


反义词：unloaded, empty, blank

词性：形容词
