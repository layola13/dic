excoriate:/ɪkˈskɔːrieɪt/| vt.|痛斥；严厉批评；剥皮；擦伤|To criticize severely; to denounce; to strip the skin from; to abrade.|例句：The critics excoriated the author's new novel. 批评家痛斥了作者的新小说。
例句：The harsh sun excoriated his skin. 烈日灼伤了他的皮肤。|近义词：castigate, rebuke, lambaste, flay|反义词：praise, commend, applaud
