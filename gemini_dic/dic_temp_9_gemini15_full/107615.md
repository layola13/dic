lane:/leɪn/| n.|车道；巷道；泳道；领域|A lane is a narrow road, especially in a rural area, or a path or track, typically one that is part of a larger road.|例句：We drove down a narrow country lane. 我们沿着一条狭窄的乡间小路行驶。|例句：The swimming lane is marked with buoys.  游泳道用浮标标示。|近义词：road, path, track, way|反义词：highway, avenue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cars are driving in their lanes. = 车辆在各自的车道行驶。
He walks along the garden lane every morning. = 他每天早上沿着花园小路散步。


一般过去时:
The cyclists raced in separate lanes. = 自行车手在不同的车道比赛。
She strolled down the lane, enjoying the quiet. = 她沿着小路漫步，享受着宁静。
