fancier:/ˈfænsiər/ | n. |爱慕虚荣的人，爱好者，讲究的人 |A fancier is someone who is enthusiastically interested in and knowledgeable about a particular thing, often a breed of animal or a type of plant.  They often breed or cultivate these things, aiming for high quality. |Example: He's a bird fancier and has a large collection of rare parrots. 他是个养鸟爱好者，收藏了许多珍稀鹦鹉。|近义词：enthusiast, connoisseur, aficionado |反义词：amateur (in the sense of lacking expertise), novice
