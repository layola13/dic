roughages:/ˈrʌfɪdʒɪz/| n. |粗饲料；粗纤维食物|  Roughages are coarse, fibrous feeds, typically plant matter, that are high in fiber and low in digestible nutrients. They are an important part of the diet for many herbivores, aiding in digestion and providing bulk.  | Example:  The farmer fed his cows a diet rich in roughages, such as hay and straw.  农民用丰富的粗饲料喂养他的奶牛，例如干草和稻草。|近义词：coarse fodder, fibrous feed, bulk feed |反义词：concentrates, refined feed
