quoif:/kwɔɪf/| n.|头巾；发罩|A close-fitting cap or hood, especially one worn by women.  Historically, it was a type of headdress worn by women, often made of linen or other fine material and designed to cover the hair.  In modern usage, it can refer to any close-fitting head covering.|例句：She wore a white quoif to church. 她戴着白色头巾去教堂。|例句：The medieval painting depicted a woman wearing a linen quoif. 中世纪的绘画描绘了一位妇女戴着亚麻头巾。|近义词：coif, cap, hood, headscarf|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting uncovered hair could be considered antonyms.|名词
