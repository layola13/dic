ineptical: /ɪˈnɛptɪkəl/ | adj. |无能的；笨拙的；不合适的 | Ineptical refers to someone or something that is clumsy, awkward, or incompetent. It suggests a lack of skill or ability, often leading to mistakes or failures.  |例句：His ineptical handling of the situation only worsened the crisis. 他笨拙的处理方式只会加剧危机。  |近义词：incompetent, clumsy, awkward, inept, unskilled |反义词：competent, skillful, adept, proficient
