re-enact:/ˌriːˈækt/ | vt. | 再现；重演；重新制定 | To perform or act out again; to reproduce or recreate an event or situation.  | 例句：The historical society plans to re-enact the famous battle. 历史学会计划重现那场著名的战役。

例句：The actors will re-enact the key scenes from the play.  演员们将重演剧中的关键场景。


近义词：reproduce, recreate, replay, perform again | 反义词：destroy, abolish, erase | 词性：动词
