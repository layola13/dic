gestate:/ˈdʒɛsteɪt/| vt.|孕育；酝酿；构思|To develop or mature gradually.  It can refer to the development of a plan, idea, or even a physical organism in the womb.|例句：The artist gestated the idea for months before starting the painting. = 这名艺术家在开始绘画前酝酿了这个想法几个月。

例句：The hen gestated her eggs for three weeks. = 母鸡孵蛋三周。|近义词：incubate, develop, mature, brood|反义词：abort, destroy, hinder
