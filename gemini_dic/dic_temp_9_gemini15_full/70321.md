field-marshal: /ˈfiːld ˈmɑːʃəl/ | n. | 陆军元帅 | A field marshal is the highest rank in many armies,  a five-star general officer.  The rank is usually reserved for wartime commanders of exceptional military accomplishment. | Example:  General Montgomery was promoted to Field Marshal after the victory in North Africa.  蒙哥马利将军在北非胜利后被晋升为陆军元帅。

| Example:  The Field Marshal commanded the entire Allied forces during the decisive battle.  这位陆军元帅指挥了决定性战役中的所有盟军部队。| 近义词: Generalissimo (最高统帅), General (将军) | 反义词: Private (列兵), Recruit (新兵) | 名词
