pourer: /ˈpɔːrər/ | n. |倾倒者；浇注者 | A person or thing that pours.  |The pourer carefully filled each glass. 倾倒者小心地注满了每个杯子。| dispenser, filler | stopper, blocker | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pourer carefully fills each glass. = 倾倒者小心地注满每个杯子。
The machine pours the liquid automatically. = 机器自动倾倒液体。

一般过去时:
The pourer filled all the glasses quickly. = 倾倒者很快地注满了所有的杯子。
He poured the milk into the cereal. = 他把牛奶倒进麦片里。


现在完成时:
She has poured all the wine. = 她已经倒完了所有的酒。
The chef has poured the sauce over the pasta. = 厨师已经把酱汁浇在意大利面上。


过去完成时:
He had poured the tea before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经倒好了茶。
They had poured the concrete before the rain started. = 下雨之前，他们已经浇好了混凝土。


将来时:
The bartender will pour you a drink. = 酒保会给你倒一杯酒。
She will pour the water into the vase. = 她会把水倒进花瓶里。


虚拟语气:
If he were a better pourer, the drink wouldn't be spilled. = 如果他是一个更好的倾倒者，饮料就不会洒了。
I wish the pourer had been more careful. = 我希望倾倒者更小心一些。


被动语态:
The drinks were poured by the bartender. = 饮料是由酒保倒的。
The concrete was poured early in the morning. = 混凝土是在清晨浇筑的。


疑问句:
Who is the pourer? = 谁是倾倒者？
Is the pourer using the right container? = 倾倒者使用的是正确的容器吗？


否定句:
He did not pour the milk. = 他没有倒牛奶。
The machine wasn't pouring the liquid properly. = 机器没有正确地倾倒液体。


PHRASE->
