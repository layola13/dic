filmographies:/fɪlˈmɒɡrəfiz/ | n. | 电影作品目录，电影作品列表 | A list of films made by a particular director, actor, or studio.  It details the films' titles, release dates, and sometimes other information like roles played or the director's contribution. |  The director's filmography includes several award-winning documentaries.  这位导演的电影作品目录包括几部获奖纪录片。|  bibliography, catalog |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：absence of filmography) | 名词
