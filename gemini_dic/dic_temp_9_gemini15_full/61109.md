elocutive:/ɪˈlɒkjʊtɪv/| adj.|雄辩的；口才好的；表达能力强的| eloquent or persuasive in speaking or writing.  The elocutive skills of the lawyer were evident in his closing argument. |近义词：articulate, eloquent, fluent, expressive|反义词：inarticulate, ineloquent, speechless


例句：

His elocutive speech captivated the audience.  他的雄辩演讲迷住了听众。

The professor's elocutive style made complex ideas easily understandable. 教授雄辩的风格使复杂的想法易于理解。


词源:

From Late Latin *elocutivus, from Latin elocutus, past participle of eloqui "to speak out," from e- "out" + loqui "to speak."
