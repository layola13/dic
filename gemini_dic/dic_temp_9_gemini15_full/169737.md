propulsions:/prəˈpʌlʃən/ | n. | 推进；推进力；推进系统 | The force that propels something forward.  It refers to the act of propelling or the means by which something is propelled. This can include the force itself, the system used to generate the force, or the process of movement.  | Example: The rocket's propulsion system malfunctioned. 火箭的推进系统发生故障了。The propulsion of the ship was powered by steam. 这艘船的推进动力是蒸汽。|近义词：thrust, drive, impetus | 反义词：inhibition, restraint
