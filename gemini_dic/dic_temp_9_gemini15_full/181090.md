reindorsement:/ˌriːɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/| n.|再背书，再次认可|The act of endorsing something again; a second endorsement.  It often refers to the re-endorsement of a check or other negotiable instrument after it has been previously endorsed.|Example: The bank refused the check due to the irregular reindorsement.  银行拒绝了这张支票，因为再次背书不规范。|近义词：second endorsement, renewed endorsement|反义词：cancellation, revocation|名词
