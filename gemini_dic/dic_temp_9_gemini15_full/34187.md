clamouring: /ˈklæmərɪŋ/ | v. |喧闹，吵闹，强烈要求 |  To make a loud and confused noise; to demand something loudly and insistently. |

例句：

The crowd was clamouring for tickets to the concert. = 人群吵着要演唱会的门票。

The children were clamouring for attention. = 孩子们吵着要人注意他们。


近义词：shouting, yelling, demanding, protesting, clamoring

反义词：whispering, murmuring, quiet, silent


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The protesters are clamouring for change. = 抗议者强烈要求改变。
The audience is clamouring for an encore. = 观众强烈要求再来一首。

一般过去时:
The students clamoured for a longer break. = 学生们吵着要更长的休息时间。
The crowd clamoured for justice. = 人群强烈要求正义。

现在完成时:
They have been clamouring for weeks for a response. = 他们已经喊了几周了，要求得到回应。
The public has clamoured for stricter regulations. = 公众强烈要求更严格的法规。


过去完成时:
They had clamoured for years before they finally got what they wanted. = 他们在多年强烈要求之后终于得到了他们想要的东西。
The people had clamoured for reform for decades. = 人们几十年来一直强烈要求改革。


将来时:
The workers will clamour for better wages. = 工人们将强烈要求更高的工资。
The fans will clamour for their favourite team to win. = 粉丝们将强烈要求他们最喜欢的球队获胜。


虚拟语气:
If they clamoured louder, maybe they would have been heard. = 如果他们喊得更大声，也许他们会被听到。
I wish they wouldn't clamour so much. = 我希望他们不要吵得这么厉害。


被动语态:
Changes were clamoured for by the public. = 公众强烈要求改变。
Better working conditions were clamoured for by the employees. = 员工们强烈要求改善工作条件。


疑问句:
Were they clamouring for anything in particular? = 他们强烈要求什么特别的东西吗？
Is the media clamouring for his resignation? = 媒体是否强烈要求他辞职？


否定句:
The people did not clamour for war. = 人们没有强烈要求战争。
The students didn't clamour for more homework. = 学生们没有强烈要求更多的家庭作业。



PHRASE->

clamour for sth:  强烈要求某事
clamour against sth:  强烈反对某事
a clamour of voices: 一片喧闹声
the public clamour: 公众的强烈要求

词源:

The word "clamouring" comes from the Old French word "clamer," meaning "to shout," "to cry out," or "to proclaim."  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "clamare," with the same meaning.
