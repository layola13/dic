roist:/rɔɪst/| v.|喧闹，狂欢；痛饮|To make a loud, boisterous noise; to revel noisily; to drink heavily.  |例句：The students roistered in the streets after the game. 学生们在比赛后在街上喧闹狂欢。
The sailors roistered in the tavern all night. 水手们整夜在酒馆里喧闹痛饮。|近义词：revel, carouse, brawl, make merry |反义词：be quiet, be subdued, be somber
