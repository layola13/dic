escorting:/ɪˈskɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. |护送，陪同 | The act of accompanying someone or something, especially for protection or guidance.  | 例句：The police escorted the witness to the courthouse.  警察护送证人到法院。 | 例句：He was escorting his daughter to her first dance. 他陪女儿去参加她的第一次舞会。| 近义词： accompanying, guiding, attending, convoying | 反义词： abandoning, deserting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He escorts her to the theatre every Friday. 他每个星期五都护送她去剧院。
The security guard escorts visitors through the building. 保安护送访客穿过大楼。

一般过去时:
