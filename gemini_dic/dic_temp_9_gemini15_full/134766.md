nonmental: /ˌnɒnˈmɛntəl/ | adj. |非精神的，非智力的|Not relating to or affecting the mind; relating to the body rather than the mind.  It often refers to physical or bodily functions as opposed to mental or cognitive ones.|例句：The doctor diagnosed a nonmental illness, attributing the patient's symptoms to a physical ailment. 医生诊断出的是非精神疾病，将患者的症状归因于身体疾病。|近义词：physical, bodily, somatic|反义词：mental, psychological, cognitive
