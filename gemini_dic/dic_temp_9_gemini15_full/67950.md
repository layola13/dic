facie:/ˈfeɪʃi/| n.|面貌，外观；（地质）层面|A facie is a surface or aspect, especially the outward appearance or look of something.  In geology, it refers to a distinct layer or bed in a rock formation.|例句：Her facie was pale with worry.  她的脸色苍白，带着担忧。|例句：The geologist studied the facie of the sedimentary rock. 地质学家研究了沉积岩的层面。|近义词：appearance, aspect, look, countenance (for face)|反义词：interior, inside, essence
