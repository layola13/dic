nonmalicious: /ˌnɒn məˈlɪʃəs/ | adj. |非恶意的；良性的；无害的 | Not intended to cause harm or damage.  It describes actions, intentions, or software that are not harmful or malicious. |


例句：

The hacker's actions, though unauthorized, were deemed nonmalicious. = 黑客的行为虽然未经授权，但被认为是非恶意的。

His comments, while critical, were ultimately nonmalicious and intended to be constructive. = 他的评论虽然很尖锐，但最终是善意的，目的是为了建设性地提出意见。


近义词：benign, harmless, innocent, well-meaning

反义词：malicious, harmful, malevolent, hostile


词性:形容词
