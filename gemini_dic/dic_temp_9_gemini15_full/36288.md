cohelper:/ˈkoʊhɛlpər/| n. | 协作者，辅助者 | A cohelper is someone who assists or works together with another person to achieve a common goal.  It's a less common word, often used in specific contexts, like collaborative research or teamwork. | Example:  The lead researcher and her cohelper published their findings jointly.  首席研究员和她的助手共同发表了他们的研究成果。 |近义词：assistant, collaborator, aide, partner |反义词：opponent, adversary
