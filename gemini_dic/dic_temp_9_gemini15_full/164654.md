preexperience: /ˌpriːɪkˈspɪəriəns/ | n. | 预先经验；先前经验 | Prior experience; experience gained before a particular event or time.  |  例句：His preexperience in the military helped him in his new role. (他之前的军队经历帮助了他胜任新的角色。)

例句：The candidate's preexperience in finance was a key factor in his selection. (这位候选人在金融领域的先前经验是其被选中的关键因素。)


近义词：prior experience, previous experience, antecedent experience | 反义词：inexperience, naivety | 词性: 名词
