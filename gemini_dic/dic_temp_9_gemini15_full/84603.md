hamstring: /ˈhæmstrɪŋ/ | n. vt. | 腘绳肌；（尤指）腿筋；阻碍；使…难以进行 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "hamstring" refers to any of the tendons at the back of the knee.  As a verb, it means to cripple someone by cutting their hamstring tendons, or figuratively, to severely hinder or impede progress or effectiveness.

例句:
He pulled a hamstring during the race. = 他在比赛中拉伤了腿筋。

She injured her hamstring while playing basketball. = 她打篮球时拉伤了腿筋。


The new regulations hamstring the company's ability to expand. = 新的法规严重阻碍了公司扩张的能力。

The constant interruptions hamstrung her efforts to finish the project on time. = 持续不断的干扰阻碍了她按时完成项目的工作。


近义词:
(noun) tendon, muscle
(verb) cripple, impede, hinder, obstruct, disable, thwart

反义词:
(verb) aid, assist, facilitate, enable


词性:
Noun, Verb
