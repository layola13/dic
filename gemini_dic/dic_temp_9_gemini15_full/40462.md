contrecoup: /ˈkɒntrəkuːp/ | n. |反冲，反作用力|A contrecoup injury is an injury to the brain occurring on the opposite side of the head from where the impact occurred. This is because the brain is suspended within the skull, and when one side is impacted, the opposite side can also be injured due to the brain's movement within the skull.|例句：The boxer suffered a contrecoup injury after a blow to the head. 拳击手头部受到重击后，遭受了反冲性损伤。  The car accident caused a contrecoup injury to his brain. 车祸导致他的大脑受到了反冲性损伤。|近义词：rebound effect, secondary injury |反义词：direct impact
