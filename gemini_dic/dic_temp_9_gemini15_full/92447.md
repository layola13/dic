hypophyllous:/ˌhaɪpəˈfɪləs/| adj.|叶下的，着生于叶下面的|  Growing or situated on the lower surface of a leaf.  The term is primarily used in botany to describe the position of structures such as flowers, buds, or appendages on a leaf.  | Example: The small, delicate flowers were hypophyllous, nestled beneath the broad leaves.|近义词：subfoliaceous|反义词：epiphyllous
