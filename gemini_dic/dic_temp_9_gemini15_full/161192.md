postilion:/ˈpɒstɪliən/| n. |邮差；骑马送信的人|A postilion is a person who rides one of the leading horses of a carriage or other vehicle.  Historically, postilions were common in Europe, particularly before the widespread adoption of automobiles.|Example: The postilion expertly guided the horses through the narrow cobblestone streets.  邮差熟练地驾驭着马匹穿过狭窄的鹅卵石街道。 The postilion blew his horn to announce the arrival of the mail. 邮差吹响号角，宣布邮件到达。|近义词：courier, messenger|反义词：N/A
