sauté:/ˈsoʊteɪ/| vt.|  翻炒；爆炒 |To cook (food, especially meat or vegetables) quickly in a little hot fat.  |例句：I sautéed the mushrooms with garlic and butter. 我用黄油和蒜翻炒了蘑菇。|例句：She sautéed the chicken breast until it was golden brown. 她把鸡胸肉翻炒至金黄。|近义词：fry, stir-fry, pan-fry |反义词：boil, stew


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sautés vegetables every morning. 他每天早上都翻炒蔬菜。
She sautés the onions until they are translucent. 她把洋葱翻炒至透明。


一般过去时:
I sautéed the shrimp with some white wine. 我用白葡萄酒翻炒虾仁。
They sautéed the garlic before adding the meat. 他们在加入肉类之前翻炒了大蒜。

过去完成时:
