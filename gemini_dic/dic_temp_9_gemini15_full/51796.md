devouter:/dəˈvaʊtər/| n. |虔诚的信徒，献身者 |A devouter is a person who is deeply religious and dedicated to their faith. They often show this through prayer, worship, and service to others.  | Example:  She was a devouter of the ancient faith, spending hours each day in prayer and meditation. 她是一位古代信仰的虔诚信徒，每天花数小时祈祷和冥想。|近义词：worshipper, devotee, adherent |反义词：apostate, heretic, unbeliever |名词
