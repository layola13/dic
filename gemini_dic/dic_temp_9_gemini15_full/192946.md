secant:/ˈsiːkənt/| n. adj.|割线；割线的；正割|A straight line that intersects a curve at two or more points.  In trigonometry, the secant is the reciprocal of the cosine.|The secant line intersects the circle at points A and B.  这条割线与圆相交于A点和B点。The secant function is used in various mathematical calculations. 正割函数用于各种数学计算。|tangent, chord|  --|n.
