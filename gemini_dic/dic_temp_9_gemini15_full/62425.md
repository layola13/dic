endoneurium:/ˌɛndoʊˈnʊəriəm/| n. |内神经膜|A delicate connective tissue sheath that surrounds each individual nerve fiber (axon) within a peripheral nerve.  It provides support and insulation to the nerve fibers.  |Example: The endoneurium plays a crucial role in maintaining the health and function of individual axons within a nerve bundle.  内神经膜在维持神经束内单个轴突的健康和功能方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词： perineurium (surrounds fascicles), epineurium (surrounds the entire nerve)|反义词：无|
