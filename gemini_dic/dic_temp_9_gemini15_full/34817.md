clincher:/ˈklɪntʃər/| n.|决定性因素；最终论据；（拳击）抱摔；钉书钉|A clincher is something that settles a matter conclusively, often a final argument or piece of evidence. It can also refer to a wrestling hold or a type of fastener.|例句：His testimony was the clincher in the case. 他的证词是此案的决定性证据。

The final goal was the clincher, securing their victory.  最后进球是决定性的一球，确保了他们的胜利。|近义词：decider, closer, finisher|反义词：inconclusive evidence, refutation
