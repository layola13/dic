dealings:/ˈdiːlɪŋz/| n.|交易；交往；行为| dealings refers to the way in which someone conducts business or interacts with others. It can refer to business transactions, personal interactions, or even the actions of a government.  |例句：Their dealings with the company were always above board. 他们与这家公司的交易一直都是光明正大的。|例句：His dealings with his neighbors were always friendly and helpful. 他与邻居的交往总是友好而乐于助人。|近义词：transactions, interactions, activities, conduct|反义词：inaction, inactivity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Their dealings with customers are always professional. = 他们与客户的交易总是专业的。
Our daily dealings involve a lot of communication. = 我们的日常交往涉及大量的沟通。


一般过去时:
His dealings with the previous firm were problematic. = 他与之前的公司的交易是有问题的。
The company's dealings in the past were quite shady. = 这家公司过去的交易相当可疑。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
Before the scandal, their dealings had always seemed legitimate. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们的交易似乎一直都是合法的。
By that time, his dealings with the underworld had become widespread. = 到那时，他与黑社会的交往已经变得很普遍了。


将来时:

We will evaluate all potential dealings before making a decision. = 我们将在做出决定之前评估所有潜在的交易。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
