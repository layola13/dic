renown:/rɪˈnaʊn/| n.|名声，声誉|fame and admiration that someone or something receives for being important, famous, or excellent|例句：The city enjoys a renown for its beautiful architecture.  这座城市以其美丽的建筑而闻名。|例句：Her renown as a writer spread throughout the country.|她作为作家的名声传遍全国。|近义词：fame, reputation, prestige|反义词：obscurity, anonymity, disrepute
