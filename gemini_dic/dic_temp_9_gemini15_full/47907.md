decentralises:/ˌdiːˈsentrəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 分散；权力下放 | To distribute the administrative powers of a central authority over a less concentrated area.  | The company is decentralizing its operations to improve efficiency. 公司正在分散其业务以提高效率。 | centralize, distribute | centralize, concentrate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government decentralises power to local authorities. = 政府将权力下放给地方政府。
The company decentralises its manufacturing process. = 公司将其制造流程分散化。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
