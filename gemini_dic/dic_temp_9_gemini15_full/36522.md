colitis:/kəˈlaɪtɪs/| n. |结肠炎|Inflammation of the colon, the large intestine.  Symptoms can vary depending on the cause and severity, but often include abdominal pain, cramping, diarrhea, and sometimes blood in the stool.  Different types of colitis exist, such as ulcerative colitis and Crohn's disease.|例句：He was diagnosed with colitis after experiencing persistent abdominal pain and bloody diarrhea. 他在经历持续的腹痛和血便后被诊断出患有结肠炎。|例句：The doctor prescribed medication to help manage her colitis symptoms. 医生开了药来帮助控制她的结肠炎症状。|近义词：enterocolitis, proctitis (if affecting the rectum)|反义词：None specifically, but a healthy colon would be the opposite state.|名词
