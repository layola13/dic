gyro:/ˈdʒaɪroʊ/ | n. |陀螺；烤肉卷饼 | A gyro is a type of food consisting of meat (usually lamb, chicken, or pork) cooked on a vertical rotisserie, sliced, and served in a pita bread with vegetables and sauce.  | Example: I had a delicious gyro for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的烤肉卷饼。|近义词：souvlaki (similar Greek dish), döner kebab (similar Turkish dish) |反义词：无
