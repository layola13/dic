milks:/mɪlks/| v.|挤奶，榨取|To extract milk from (an animal), typically a cow or goat.  It can also refer to the act of repeatedly exploiting or taking advantage of someone or something.|例句：The farmer milks his cows every morning.  农夫每天早上都挤牛奶。|例句：The company milked the success of its first product for years. 这家公司多年来一直利用其首款产品的成功。|近义词：extract, exploit|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to provide or nurture.|词性:动词
