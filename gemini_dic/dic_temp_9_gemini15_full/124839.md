muckraker:/ˈmʌkˌreɪkər/| n. |揭露丑闻的记者；揭发者|A muckraker is a journalist who investigates and exposes scandals, corruption, or other wrongdoing, often in a sensationalist manner.  They are known for their aggressive investigative journalism and willingness to challenge powerful institutions.|Example:  The muckraker published a series of articles detailing the mayor's corrupt dealings.  这个揭露丑闻的记者发表了一系列文章，详细介绍了市长的腐败交易。|近义词：investigative journalist, whistleblower, exposé writer|反义词：apologist, whitewasher|名词
