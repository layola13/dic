quadrupedalism:/kwɑːˈdruːpədælɪzəm/ | n. |四足动物行走；四足运动 | The state or habit of moving on four legs.  It is a common form of locomotion in many animal species.  |例句：Many mammals exhibit quadrupedalism as their primary mode of locomotion. 许多哺乳动物以四足行走为主要运动方式。

例句：The study focused on the evolution of quadrupedalism in early reptiles. 这项研究关注早期爬行动物四足运动的进化。


近义词：tetrapodalism


反义词：bipedalism (two-legged locomotion)
