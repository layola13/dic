on-even-keel:/ˌɒnˈiːvənkiːl/| adj. | 平稳；稳定；平静 | In a stable and steady condition; calm and balanced.  The phrase originates from nautical terminology, referring to a ship sailing with its keel (the central structural member along the bottom of a hull) level in the water, indicating stability. |例句：After the initial shock, she managed to stay on an even keel.  在最初的震惊过后，她设法保持了平静。

The company is sailing on an even keel despite the economic downturn.  尽管经济低迷，公司仍然保持稳定。|近义词：stable, steady, balanced, calm, composed |反义词：unstable, unsteady, turbulent, chaotic, agitated
