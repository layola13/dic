preescape:/ˌpriːɪˈskeɪp/| v.|预先逃逸，提前逃脱|To escape or avoid something before it happens; to preemptively escape a situation.|The prisoner attempted to preescape before the guards could apprehend him.  囚犯试图在守卫抓捕他之前提前逃跑。|近义词：avoid, evade, circumvent|反义词：confront, face

He preescapes danger by being vigilant. 他通过保持警惕来预先躲避危险。
They preescape difficult conversations by ignoring calls. 他们通过不接电话来避免困难的谈话。
