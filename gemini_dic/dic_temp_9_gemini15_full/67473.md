extorters:/ɪkˈstɔːrtərz/ | n. |敲诈者，勒索者 | People who extort money or other things from others by threats or force.  They use coercion to obtain something of value. |The extorters demanded a large sum of money or they would release damaging information. 敲诈者勒索一大笔钱，否则他们就会泄露有害信息。 |blackmailers, racketeers | benefactors, philanthropists | 名词
