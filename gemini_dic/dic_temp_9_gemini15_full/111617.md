localhost:/ˈloʊkəlˌhoʊst/| n. |本地主机|The term "localhost" refers to the computer you are currently using. It is a hostname that is used to refer to the local machine.  It is typically used in networking, programming, and web development to address the local computer.  |Example: I accessed the database on localhost. 我访问了本地主机的数据库。|近义词：local machine, local server |反义词：remote host, external server
