ironing:/ˈaɪərnɪŋ/| n. |熨烫；熨衣；熨好的衣服|The act of smoothing fabrics with a heated iron.  It can refer to the process itself or the clothes that have been ironed.|例句：I hate ironing, it's such a chore. 我讨厌熨衣服，真是一件苦差事。
例句：The ironing is piled high on the chair. 熨好的衣服堆在椅子上。|近义词：pressing, smoothing|反义词：wrinkling, creasing|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I iron my shirts every Sunday. = 我每个星期天都熨烫衬衫。
She irons her clothes before going to work. = 她上班前熨烫衣服。

一般过去时:
I ironed my dress yesterday. = 我昨天熨烫了我的连衣裙。

现在完成时:
I have ironed all the shirts. = 我已经把所有的衬衫都熨烫好了。
She has ironed the tablecloth for the dinner party. = 她已经为晚宴熨烫了桌布。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
