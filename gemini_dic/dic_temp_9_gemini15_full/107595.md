landskip:/ˈlændskeɪp/| n. |风景，景色；地貌；景观；(绘画)风景画|A landscape is a view of the countryside, especially one that is beautiful or picturesque.  It can also refer to the physical features of a region, such as mountains, valleys, and rivers, or to a particular style of painting. |例句：The landscape was breathtaking, with rolling hills and a clear blue sky.  这片风景令人叹为观止，有连绵起伏的丘陵和湛蓝的天空。|例句：The artist painted a beautiful landscape of the Tuscan countryside.这位艺术家画了一幅美丽的托斯卡纳乡村风景画。|近义词：scenery, vista, view, panorama|反义词：portrait (in the context of painting)|词性:名词
