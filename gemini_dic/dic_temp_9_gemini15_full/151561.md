part-timer:/ˈpɑːrtˌtaɪmər/|/ˈpɑːrtˌtaɪmə(r)/| n. |兼职者，兼职人员 |A part-timer is someone who works part-time.  They typically work fewer hours per week than a full-time employee. |She's a part-timer at the coffee shop, working only weekends.  她是咖啡店的一名兼职人员，只在周末工作。|He is a part-timer who teaches English online. 他是一位在线教英语的兼职教师。|full-timer, full-time employee|full-time employee


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a part-timer at a local library. 他是当地图书馆的兼职人员。
She works as a part-timer during the summer. 她在夏天做兼职工作。

一般过去时:
He was a part-timer at that company last year.  去年他那家公司做兼职。
She worked as a part-timer during her college years. 她大学期间做过兼职。

现在完成时:
She has been a part-timer for five years. 她已经兼职五年了。
He has worked as a part-timer since graduation. 他毕业后一直做兼职。

过去完成时:
She had been a part-timer before she got her full-time job. 在她找到全职工作之前，她做过兼职。
He had worked as a part-timer for two years before he quit. 他辞职前做过两年兼职。

将来时:
She will be a part-timer next semester. 下个学期她将成为一名兼职人员。
He will work as a part-timer during the holidays. 假期期间他将做兼职。

虚拟语气:
If I were a part-timer, I would have more free time. 如果我是兼职人员，我就会有更多空闲时间。
I wish I were a part-timer so I could travel more. 我希望我是兼职人员，这样我就可以多旅行了。

被动语态:
Part-timers are often paid less than full-time employees. 兼职人员的工资通常低于全职员工。
Many tasks are assigned to part-timers. 许多任务都分配给兼职人员。

疑问句:
Is he a part-timer or a full-timer? 他是兼职人员还是全职人员？
Are you looking for a part-timer for your business? 你在为你的生意寻找兼职人员吗？

否定句:
She is not a part-timer; she's a full-time employee. 她不是兼职人员；她是全职员工。
They don't hire part-timers during the off-season. 他们在淡季不雇佣兼职人员。
