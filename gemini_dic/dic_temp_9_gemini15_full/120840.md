The misawarding of the prize caused considerable controversy.  奖项的错误授予引起了相当大的争议。| 近义词：misallocation, maldistribution, improper allocation | 反义词：proper awarding, just allocation | 动词

词源:

The word "misawarding" is formed by combining the prefix "mis-" (meaning "badly," "wrongly," or "incorrectly") with the verb "awarding" (the act of giving or bestowing something).  Therefore, it directly describes the act of giving something incorrectly or unfairly.
