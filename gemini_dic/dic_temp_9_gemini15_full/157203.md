pinions:/ˈpɪnjən/| n.|羽翼；羽状物；小齿轮；意见；看法|The bird spread its pinions and soared into the sky.  这只鸟展开翅膀飞向天空。|近义词：wings, feathers, opinions|反义词：N/A


英文解析：
1.  **As a noun:**
    *   **Ornithology:**  Refers to the wings of a bird, especially the large flight feathers.
    *   **Mechanics:**  A small gear wheel, typically one of a set of gears.
    *   **Figurative:** Can refer to opinions or judgments, often implying a collection of diverse viewpoints.


例句：

The eagle's powerful pinions carried it high above the mountains.  雄鹰有力的翅膀把它带到了高山之上。

The clockwork mechanism was intricate, with dozens of tiny pinions meshing together.  钟表机构非常复杂，由数十个小型齿轮啮合在一起。


词源:
From Middle English *pinyoun*, from Old French *pignon*, from Vulgar Latin *pinnōnem*, accusative of *pinnōn-,* diminutive of Latin *pinna* "feather, wing, fin".  The mechanical sense developed later, likely from the resemblance of the gear to a feather.
