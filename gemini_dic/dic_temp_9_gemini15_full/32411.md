chillout:/tʃɪlˈaʊt/| v. |放松；冷静；休息|To relax completely; to calm down.|例句：Let's chill out and watch a movie.  让我们放松一下，看场电影。|例句：Just chill out, everything will be alright. 放松点，一切都会好起来的。|近义词：relax, unwind, take it easy|反义词：stress, tense, agitate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I chill out by listening to music. = 我通过听音乐来放松。
We chill out at the beach every weekend. = 我们每个周末都在海滩上放松。

一般过去时:
I chilled out after a long day at work. = 我在工作了一整天后放松了下来。
They chilled out by the pool yesterday. = 他们昨天在泳池边放松了。

现在完成时:
I have chilled out a lot since starting yoga. = 自从开始练习瑜伽以来，我放松了很多。
She has chilled out considerably after her vacation. = 她度假后放松了很多。

过去完成时:
I had chilled out before the important meeting. = 在重要的会议之前，我已经放松了下来。
They had chilled out completely by the time the party started. = 到派对开始的时候，他们已经完全放松下来了。

将来时:
I will chill out at home tonight. = 我今晚会在家里放松。
We will chill out by the fire after dinner. = 晚饭后我们会在火炉旁放松。

虚拟语气:

I wish I could chill out more easily. = 我希望我能更容易地放松。
