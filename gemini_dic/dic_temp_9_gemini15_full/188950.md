/ɑːrˈviː/| n. |房车；休闲车；活动房屋|A recreational vehicle (RV) is a motor vehicle or trailer designed for recreational purposes, such as camping or travel.  They are typically equipped with living accommodations, such as sleeping areas, cooking facilities, and bathrooms.|例句：We rented an RV for our cross-country road trip. 我们租了一辆房车进行跨国公路旅行。|例句：Many families enjoy camping in their RVs during the summer. 许多家庭喜欢在夏天用他们的房车露营。|近义词：camper, motorhome, caravan|反义词：sedan, car
