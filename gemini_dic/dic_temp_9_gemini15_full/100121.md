interpreter's: /ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtərz/ | n. | 解释者的；口译员的 | Relating to or characteristic of an interpreter.  The possessive form of "interpreter."  It indicates something belongs to or is associated with an interpreter. | Example: The interpreter's booth was located near the stage. 解释员的隔间位于舞台附近。|近义词: translator's, paraphraser's | 反义词: N/A


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It describes something related to an interpreter.  Therefore, tense and voice are not applicable.

Example 1: The interpreter's translation was accurate and clear. 解释员的翻译准确清晰。
Example 2: We appreciated the interpreter's patience and professionalism. 我们感谢解释员的耐心和专业精神。
