scoop:/skuːp/| n. v.|勺子；铲子；独家新闻；一勺；舀；铲；抢先报道|a small tool with a deep bowl-shaped container used for lifting and moving substances such as liquids, powders, or soft foods; a piece of news reported by one news organization before others; the act of scooping something; to lift or move something with a scoop|例句：She scooped the ice cream into a bowl. 她用勺子把冰淇淋舀进碗里。|例句：The newspaper got the scoop on the scandal. 这家报纸抢先报道了这起丑闻。|近义词：ladle, shovel, exclusive|反义词：N/A|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scoops the ice cream. = 他舀冰淇淋。
She scoops up the spilled milk. = 她把洒了的牛奶舀起来。


一般过去时:
I scooped the snow from the driveway. = 我把车道上的雪铲掉了。
They scooped up all the toys. = 他们把所有的玩具都捡起来了。


现在完成时:
She has scooped the winning lottery ticket. = 她已经中了彩票。
He has scooped up a lot of information. = 他已经收集了很多信息。


过去完成时:
They had scooped the gold before the others arrived. = 他们在其他人到达之前已经挖到了金子。
She had scooped the information from a confidential source. = 她已经从一个秘密来源获得了信息。


将来时:
I will scoop the cat litter tomorrow. = 我明天会铲猫砂。
He will scoop up the profits. = 他将赚取利润。


虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger scoop, I would scoop all the sand. = 如果我有一个更大的铲子，我会把所有的沙子都铲起来。
If she had known, she would have scooped the news earlier. = 如果她知道的话，她会更早地报道这条新闻。


被动语态:
The ice cream was scooped into bowls. = 冰淇淋被舀进碗里。
The news was scooped by a rival newspaper. = 这条新闻被竞争对手的报纸抢先报道了。


疑问句:
Will you scoop the dog's food? = 你会铲狗粮吗？
Did he scoop the lottery? = 他中了彩票吗？


否定句:
He didn't scoop the snow. = 他没有铲雪。
She didn't scoop the details from her report. = 她没有从她的报告中提取细节。


PHRASE->

scoop out: 挖出；掏空
