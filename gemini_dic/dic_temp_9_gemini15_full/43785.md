cremations:/krɪˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. |火葬（复数）|The act of burning a dead body.  It is a common practice in many cultures as a method of disposing of the deceased.|例句：The cremations were conducted according to family wishes.  火葬是按照家人的意愿进行的。|例句：Ancient cremations have been discovered in the archaeological dig. 考古挖掘中发现了古代的火葬遗迹。|近义词：incinerations, burnings|反义词：burials
