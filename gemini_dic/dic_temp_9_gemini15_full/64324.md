/ər/ | suffix | …的人；…的东西；…者 |  A suffix forming nouns from verbs or adjectives, denoting the doer of an action, the thing that performs an action, or something that is related to the action or quality described by the root word. It often indicates an agent, instrument, or result.  |  The worker finished the job. (the one who works) |  worker, player, teacher |  unemployed, inactive | noun suffix


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is a teacher. = 他是一位老师。
She is a singer. = 她是一位歌手。


一般过去时:
He was a writer. = 他是一位作家。
She was a dancer. = 她是一位舞蹈演员。


现在完成时:
He has been a doctor for ten years. = 他当医生已经十年了。
She has been a lawyer since graduation. = 她毕业后就当律师了。


过去完成时:
He had been a soldier before the war. = 战争前他当过兵。
She had been a student at Oxford. = 她曾是牛津大学的学生。


将来时:
He will be a pilot. = 他将成为一名飞行员。
She will be a manager next year. = 明年她将成为一名经理。


虚拟语气:
If I were a bird, I would fly. = 如果我是一只鸟，我会飞。
I wish I were a painter. = 我希望我是一位画家。


被动语态:
The problem was solved by the engineer. = 这个问题由工程师解决了。(The engineer is the agent)
The song was written by a famous composer. = 这首歌是一位著名的作曲家写的。(The composer is the agent)


疑问句:
Is he a doctor? = 他是医生吗？
Is she a teacher? = 她是一位老师吗？


否定句:
He is not a student. = 他不是学生。
She is not a singer. = 她不是歌手。
