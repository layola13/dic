cottoning:/ˈkɒtənɪŋ/| v.|熟悉，习惯于；同意，赞成|To become accustomed to something; to agree with or approve of something.|例句：I'm still cottoning on to the new software. 我还在适应新的软件。|例句：They quickly cottoned on to our plan. 他们很快认同了我们的计划。|近义词：acclimating, adapting, agreeing, approving |反义词：disagreeing, disapproving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is cottoning on to the new system. = 他正在适应新系统。
They cotton on to new trends quickly. = 他们很快就能适应新的潮流。


一般过去时:
She cottoned on to the joke immediately. = 她立刻明白了那个笑话。
We cottoned on to their secret plan. = 我们明白了他们的秘密计划。


现在完成时:
I have cottoned on to the meaning of this poem. = 我已经理解了这首诗的意思。
They have cottoned on to the fact that we are lying. = 他们已经发现我们在说谎。


过去完成时:
He had cottoned on to the deception before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，他已经识破了骗局。
We had cottoned on to their scheme by the time they revealed it. = 在他们揭露计划的时候，我们就已经识破了他们的阴谋。


将来时:
She will cotton on to the truth eventually. = 她最终会明白真相的。
They will cotton on to our strategy soon enough. = 他们很快就会明白我们的策略。


虚拟语气:
If he cottoned on to the clues, he could solve the mystery. = 如果他能注意到这些线索，他就能解开这个谜团。
I wish they had cottoned on to our problems earlier. = 我希望他们早点发现我们的问题。


被动语态:
The new rules were quickly cottoned on to by the staff. = 新规很快就被员工们接受了。
The changes were not easily cottoned on to by the older generation. = 老一代人不容易接受这些变化。


疑问句:
Are they cottoning on to what we're doing? = 他们明白我们在做什么吗？
Did he cotton on to the hidden message? = 他明白隐藏的信息了吗？


否定句:
He didn't cotton on to the sarcasm in her voice. = 他没有听出她语气中的讽刺意味。
They haven't cottoned on to our plan yet. = 他们还没有发现我们的计划。


PHRASE->
cotton on to something = 理解，明白
cotton to someone = 喜欢，亲近
cotton on to the fact that = 明白...的事实
cottoning to the idea = 赞同这个想法
cottoning up to someone = 讨好某人
