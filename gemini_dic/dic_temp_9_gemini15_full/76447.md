gains:/ɡeɪnz/ | n. | 收益，增长，进步 |  Gains refer to an increase in something, typically wealth, profit, or knowledge.  It can also refer to advantages or improvements. | The company reported significant gains in the last quarter.  这家公司报告说上一季度获得了可观的收益。

The investment yielded substantial gains. 这项投资带来了可观的收益。


近义词：profits, increases, advancements, improvements | 反义词：losses, decreases, setbacks


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gains weight easily. 他很容易发胖。
She gains experience through her work. 她通过工作获得经验。


一般过去时:
The team gained a decisive victory.  队伍取得了决定性的胜利。
He gained a lot of knowledge from his travels. 他从旅行中学到了很多知识。


现在完成时:
The company has gained a strong reputation.  公司赢得了良好的声誉。
She has gained considerable weight recently. 她最近体重增加了许多。


过去完成时:
They had gained a significant lead before the final lap. 在最后一圈之前，他们已经取得了显著的领先优势。
She had gained confidence after completing the project. 完成项目后，她增强了信心。


将来时:
He will gain admission to a prestigious university. 他将被一所名牌大学录取。
The company expects to gain market share next year. 公司预计明年将获得市场份额。


虚拟语气:
If I studied harder, I would gain better grades. 如果我学习更努力，我会取得更好的成绩。
I wish I had gained more experience before taking this job. 我希望在做这份工作之前能积累更多经验。


被动语态:
Much knowledge can be gained from reading.  从阅读中可以获得很多知识。
A lot of weight was gained during the holiday season. 假期期间体重增加了许多。


疑问句:
What did you gain from this experience? 你从这次经历中获得了什么？
How much weight have you gained? 你增重多少了？


否定句:
The team didn't gain any ground in the game. 球队在比赛中没有取得任何进展。
He didn't gain any profit from that investment. 他从那项投资中没有获得任何利润。


PHRASE->
gain control 获得控制
gain access 获得进入
gain momentum 获得动力

gain an advantage 获得优势

词源:
The word "gains" originates from the Old French word "gainer," meaning "to earn" or "to win," which ultimately derives from the Germanic root "gewinnan," with the same meaning.  The noun form evolved from the verb's usage describing the results of earning or winning.
