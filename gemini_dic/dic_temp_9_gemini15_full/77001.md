gardenship:/ˈɡɑːrdənʃɪp/ | n. | 花园船；（旧时）训练船 | A gardenship is a ship, typically a sailing vessel, used for training purposes, often equipped with gardens or other recreational facilities.  Historically, gardenships were used by navies to train young sailors. The term also refers to a ship with extensive onboard gardens, often used for leisure or scientific purposes. |  The old gardenship was a popular training ground for young sailors.  这艘旧花园船是年轻水手们受欢迎的训练场所。 The research gardenship sailed the seas, conducting experiments in marine botany. 这艘研究花园船航行在大海上，进行海洋植物学的实验。 | training ship, training vessel | warship, battleships  |名词
