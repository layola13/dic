interjugular: /ˌɪntərˈdʒuːɡjʊlər/ | adj. | 颈间的，锁骨间的 | Relating to the space or region between the collarbones, specifically the area between the jugular veins.  | Example: The interjugular space is often examined during a physical examination to assess for swelling or masses. 颈间隙在体格检查中经常被检查以评估肿胀或肿块。 | 近义词: clavicular, suprasternal | 反义词:  None readily available.  The antonym would describe a location *not* between the collarbones.


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  Therefore, tense and voice examples are not applicable.
