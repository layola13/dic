pupate:/ˈpjuːpeɪt/| v.|化蛹；进入蛹期|To form a pupa; to enter the pupal stage of development.  The process of transformation from larva to adult in insects involving a pupal stage. |例句：The caterpillar will pupate in a cocoon.  毛毛虫会在茧中化蛹。

The butterfly pupated under a leaf. 蝴蝶在树叶下化蛹。


近义词：chrysalise, cocoon (intransitive uses)|反义词：emerge (from pupa), metamorphose (into adult)
