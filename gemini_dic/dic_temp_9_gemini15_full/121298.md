misfit:/ˈmɪsfɪt/| n., adj.| 不合群的人；不合适的东西；不协调 | A person who is not accepted or does not fit in with a particular group or situation; something that is not suitable or does not fit properly.  |例句：He felt like a misfit at the party. 他在派对上感觉格格不入。  He felt like a misfit in his new school. 他在新学校感觉格格不入。|近义词：outsider, oddball, misfit, anomaly |反义词：insider, well-adjusted, perfect fit |名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a misfit in this company. 他在这家公司格格不入。
That piece is a misfit; it doesn't match the others. 那块部件不合适，它和其他的部件不匹配。

一般过去时:
She was a misfit in her family. 她在她家里格格不入。
The puzzle piece was a misfit and wouldn't fit into the puzzle. 那个拼图块不合适，无法放入拼图中。

现在完成时:
He has always been a misfit. 他一直都是个不合群的人。
The new software has proved to be a misfit with our system. 新软件已被证明与我们的系统不兼容。

过去完成时:
She had always felt like a misfit before moving to the new city. 在搬到新城市之前，她一直感觉自己格格不入。
They realized the part was a misfit after they had already assembled the machine.  他们在组装好机器后才意识到零件不合适。

将来时:
He will likely remain a misfit wherever he goes. 不管他去哪里，他很可能仍然会格格不入。
The new design will likely be a misfit with the existing architecture. 新设计很可能与现有架构不兼容。

虚拟语气:
If he weren't such a misfit, he'd have more friends. 如果他不是那么不合群，他就会有更多朋友。
If this part were a perfect fit, the machine would work properly. 如果这个零件完全合适，机器就能正常工作。

被动语态:
He was considered a misfit by his classmates. 他被同学认为是个不合群的人。
The old furniture was found to be a misfit in the modern house.  人们发现旧家具不适合现代化的房子。

疑问句:
Is he a misfit in his social circle? 他在他的社交圈子里格格不入吗？
Is this part a misfit for this machine? 这个零件不适合这台机器吗？

否定句:
He is not a misfit; he is just shy. 他不是不合群；他只是害羞。
This component is not a misfit; it fits perfectly.  这个组件很合适；它完美地契合。
