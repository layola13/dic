non-interchangeable: /ˌnɒnɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/ | adj. | 不可互换的；不能互换的 |  Not able to be exchanged or substituted for one another.  Things that are non-interchangeable are distinct and cannot be used in place of each other. |


例句：

These parts are non-interchangeable; make sure you use the correct one.  这些零件不可互换；确保你使用正确的零件。

The two roles are non-interchangeable; each requires a unique skill set. 这两个角色是不可互换的；每个角色都需要独特的技能。


近义词：indispensable, irreplaceable, unique, distinct

反义词：interchangeable, substitutable, replaceable


词性：形容词
