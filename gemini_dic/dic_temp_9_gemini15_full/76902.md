gannets:/ˈɡænɪts/| n.|塘鹅|A gannet is a large seabird with a long, pointed bill and long wings.  They are known for their spectacular diving ability, plunging from great heights to catch fish.|Example: We saw several gannets diving for fish off the coast of Ireland. 我们在爱尔兰海岸看到几只塘鹅在捕鱼。  The gannet's plunge into the water was breathtaking. 塘鹅俯冲入水的景象令人叹为观止。|近义词：booby (某些种类)|反义词：无
