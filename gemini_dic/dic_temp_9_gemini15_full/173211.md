quasi-clerical:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈklɛrɪkəl/| adj.|准教务的；类似教务的|Relating to or resembling the duties or functions of a cleric, but not officially or fully so.  It often implies a role that combines aspects of clerical work with other responsibilities.|The office manager had a quasi-clerical role, handling both administrative tasks and some pastoral duties.|近义词：semi-clerical,  clerical-like|反义词：lay, secular
