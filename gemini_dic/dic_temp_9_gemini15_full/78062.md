geocentrical:/ˌdʒioʊˈsɛntrɪkəl/| adj. |地心说的；以地球为中心的| relating to or denoting a system of astronomy that regards the earth as the centre of the universe.  This term is primarily used in a historical context, referring to the geocentric model of the universe that predates the heliocentric model.  |例句：The geocentrical view of the universe was widely accepted for centuries. 地心宇宙观被广泛接受了几个世纪。 The Ptolemaic system is a classic example of a geocentrical model. 托勒密体系是地心模型的经典例子。|近义词：earth-centered|反义词：heliocentric
