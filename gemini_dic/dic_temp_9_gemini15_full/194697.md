self-weights:/ˈsɛlfˌweɪts/| n.|自重，自身重量|The inherent weight of an object or structure, excluding any external loads.|例句：The self-weights of the bridge components were carefully calculated. 桥梁部件的自重经过仔细计算。|近义词：intrinsic weight, inherent weight|反义词：external load, added weight|名词

We calculate the self-weight of the building before construction. = 我们在施工前计算建筑物的自重。
