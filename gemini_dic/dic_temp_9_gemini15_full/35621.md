coat:/koʊt/| n. |外套，涂层|A coat is an outer garment worn to protect the body from cold or wet weather.  It can be made of various materials and styles.|例句：He put on his winter coat before going outside. 他出门前穿上了他的冬衣。|例句：The car needs a new coat of paint. 这辆车需要重新喷漆。|近义词：jacket, overcoat, covering |反义词：underwear, lining
