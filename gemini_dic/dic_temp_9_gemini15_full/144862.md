over-practices: /ˈoʊvərˈpræktɪsɪz/ | v. |过度练习|Excessive or unnecessary practice, often leading to negative consequences such as burnout, injury, or decreased performance due to fatigue or mental exhaustion.  It implies practicing beyond the optimal point of improvement. |例句：He over-practiced his piano piece and now his fingers are aching. 他钢琴曲练得太多了，现在手指都疼了。


例句：The team over-practiced for the game, resulting in several players suffering injuries. 球队为比赛进行了过度练习，导致几名球员受伤。


近义词：overtraining, overexertion, excessive practice |反义词：under-practice, insufficient practice |词性：动词
