forepurpose:/fɔːrˈpɜːrpəs/| n. |预定目的；预先计划；预谋|A forepurpose is a plan or intention formed in advance.  It implies a deliberate and premeditated action, often with a sense of premeditation or scheming.  It is less common than "premeditated" but carries a similar meaning.|例句：He acted with forepurpose, carefully planning each step of his revenge. 他蓄意行动，精心策划着他复仇的每一步。  The arson was committed with clear forepurpose.  纵火案是蓄意犯下的。|近义词：premeditation, intention, design, plan, scheme|反义词：accident, chance, spontaneity|名词
