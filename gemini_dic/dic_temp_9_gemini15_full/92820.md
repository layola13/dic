iconographist:/ˌaɪkənˈɒɡrəfɪst/ | n. |图像学家，图标学家 | A person who studies or creates iconography.  An iconographist analyzes the symbolic meaning of images and their cultural context. | Example: The iconographist meticulously examined the painting, noting the symbolic significance of each element.  图像学家仔细检查了这幅画，注意到每个元素的象征意义。|近义词：iconologist, image analyst |反义词：N/A
