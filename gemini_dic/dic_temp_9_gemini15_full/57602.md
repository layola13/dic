drapers:/ˈdreɪpərz/| n. |布商；纺织品商；（旧时）杂货店|A draper is a person who sells cloth and clothing.  The term is now largely archaic, with "clothier" or "haberdasher" being more commonly used.|例句：In the past, drapers were common in high streets.  过去，布商在商业街上很常见。|例句：My grandmother used to work in a draper's shop. 我祖母过去在一家布店工作。|近义词：clothier, haberdasher|反义词：customer (in this context)|词性:名词
