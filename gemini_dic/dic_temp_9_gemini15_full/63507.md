enumerates:/ɪˈnjuːməreɪts/ | vt. |列举；枚举；细数 |To mention (a number of things) one by one; to list.  |The report enumerates the problems facing the company. 这份报告列举了公司面临的问题。 The contract enumerates the responsibilities of each party. 合同列举了各方的责任。| lists, details, specifies | omits, ignores | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enumerates the benefits of the program. 他列举了该项目的益处。
She enumerates the ingredients on the label. 她列举了标签上的成分。


一般过去时:
The teacher enumerated the rules of the classroom. 老师列举了教室的规则。
The speaker enumerated the key points of his presentation. 演讲者列举了他的演讲要点。


现在完成时:
She has enumerated all the possible solutions. 她已经列举了所有可能的解决方案。
He has enumerated the reasons for his decision. 他已经列举了他做出决定的原因。


过去完成时:
They had enumerated the casualties before reporting to headquarters. 他们在向总部报告之前已经统计了伤亡人数。
She had enumerated the steps before starting the experiment.  在她开始实验之前，她已经列举了步骤。


将来时:
The professor will enumerate the historical events in his lecture. 教授将在他的讲座中列举历史事件。
The manager will enumerate the company's achievements in the annual meeting. 经理将在年度会议上列举公司的成就。


虚拟语气:
If he enumerated all the facts, the truth would be clear. 如果他列举所有事实，真相就会大白。
I wish she had enumerated the risks involved before proceeding. 我希望她在继续之前列举了相关的风险。


被动语态:
The items were enumerated in the inventory. 清单中列举了这些物品。
The advantages of the new system were enumerated in the brochure. 小册子中列举了新系统的优点。


疑问句:
Does the document enumerate all the necessary steps?  这份文件是否列举了所有必要的步骤？
Does the list enumerate all the participants?  这份名单是否列举了所有参与者？


否定句:
The report does not enumerate all the possible causes. 报告没有列举所有可能的原因。
He did not enumerate the specific details of the incident. 他没有列举该事件的具体细节。


PHRASE->
enumerate the reasons  列举原因
enumerate the possibilities 列举可能性
enumerate the advantages 列举优点
enumerate the disadvantages 列举缺点
enumerate the steps 列举步骤
