hotpots:/ˈhɑːtpɑːts/| n. |火锅，热锅|A hotpot is a type of cooking and eating method where ingredients are cooked at the table in a simmering pot of broth.  It's often a social activity, enjoyed with family and friends.|Example: We had a delicious hotpot for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了一顿美味的火锅。|近义词：fondue, stew|反义词：None
