dynasty: /ˈdaɪnəsti/ | n. |王朝，朝代 | A succession of rulers of the same family or line; a powerful family or group that maintains power for a considerable time. |例句：The Tang Dynasty was a golden age in Chinese history.  唐朝是中国历史上一个黄金时代。|例句：The Qing Dynasty ruled China for over two centuries. 清朝统治中国超过两个世纪。|近义词：reign, regime, rule |反义词：anarchy, chaos

The Ming Dynasty lasts for nearly 300 years. = 明朝持续了近300年。
This dynasty rules the country with an iron fist. = 这个王朝铁腕统治国家。

一般过去时:
The Han Dynasty fell after several rebellions. = 汉朝在几次叛乱后灭亡了。
