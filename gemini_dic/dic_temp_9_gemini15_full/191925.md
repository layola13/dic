scooper:/ˈskuːpər/| n. |勺子；铲子；（新闻等的）爆料者；挖土机|A scooper is a tool used for scooping things, such as ice cream, or a person who reveals secrets.|例句：He used a large ice cream scooper to serve the dessert. 他用一个大冰淇淋勺来盛甜点。|近义词：spoon, shovel, ladle, digger|反义词:pourer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The worker scoops the grain into sacks. = 工人把谷物铲进麻袋里。
She scoops ice cream into bowls. = 她把冰淇淋舀进碗里。

一般过去时:
He scooped up the spilled sugar. = 他把洒出来的糖铲了起来。
They scooped the snow off the driveway. = 他们把车道上的雪铲掉了。

现在完成时:
She has scooped all the ice cream. = 她已经把所有的冰淇淋都舀完了。
He has scooped the sand from the bucket. = 他已经把桶里的沙子铲出来了。

过去完成时:
They had scooped the snow before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经铲掉了雪。
She had scooped the ice cream before the party started. = 派对开始前，她已经把冰淇淋舀好了。

将来时:
He will scoop the dirt into a wheelbarrow. = 他将把土铲进独轮车上。
They will scoop the sand into the truck. = 他们将把沙子铲进卡车里。

虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger scooper, I would finish faster. = 如果我有一个更大的勺子，我会更快完成。
I wish I had scooped the ice cream earlier. = 我真希望我早点把冰淇淋舀出来。

被动语态:
The ice cream was scooped into cones. = 冰淇淋被舀进甜筒里。
