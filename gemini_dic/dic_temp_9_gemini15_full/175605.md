ranger:/ˈreɪndʒər/| n. |护林员，公园管理员，游骑兵| A ranger is a person who protects and manages a natural area, such as a forest or national park.  They often patrol the area, provide information to visitors, and enforce regulations. |The park ranger gave us a guided tour. 公园管理员带我们参观了。| park guard, warden, gamekeeper |  poacher, vandal | 名词
