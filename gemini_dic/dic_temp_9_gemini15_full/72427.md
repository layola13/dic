fluid:/ˈfluːɪd/| adj., n.|流动的；流体；液体|easily flowing or moving; a substance that flows and adapts its shape to its container; a liquid or gas |The lava flowed like a fluid.  熔岩像液体一样流动。|The blood is a vital fluid.  血液是重要的体液。|liquid, liquid substance, gas|solid, rigid


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water flows smoothly. = 水流得很顺畅。
The liquid is flowing steadily. = 液体稳定地流动着。

一般过去时:
The honey flowed slowly from the jar. = 蜂蜜慢慢地从罐子里流出来。
The oil flowed out of the container. = 油从容器里流出来了。

现在完成时:
The river has flowed through the valley for centuries. = 这条河已经流经山谷几个世纪了。
The molten metal has flowed into the mold. = 熔化的金属已经流进了模具。

过去完成时:
The dam had already broken before the floodwaters flowed into the town. = 大坝在洪水流进城镇之前就已经决堤了。
The lava had flowed down the mountainside before the eruption stopped. = 火山爆发停止前，熔岩已经流下山坡了。

将来时:
The water will flow down the drain. = 水会流到下水道里。
The syrup will flow easily once it's heated. = 糖浆加热后会很容易流动。

虚拟语气:
If the liquid were less viscous, it would flow more easily. = 如果液体粘度更低，它就会更容易流动。
I wish the paint would flow more smoothly. = 我希望油漆能更流畅地流动。

被动语态:
The liquid is being poured into the container. = 液体正在被倒入容器中。
The fluid was carefully transferred to the new vessel. = 液体被小心地转移到新的容器中。

疑问句:
Does this fluid flow easily? = 这种液体容易流动吗？
Is the lava still flowing? = 熔岩还在流动吗？


否定句:
The engine oil is not flowing properly. = 发动机油流动不畅。
The honey didn't flow easily because it was too thick. = 蜂蜜因为太稠而不容易流动。
