lopsided:/ˈlɒpsaɪdɪd/| adj. | 倾斜的；不平衡的；一边倒的 |  Unevenly balanced or developed; leaning to one side.  |The lopsided victory was due to the opposing team's poor performance.  这场一边倒的胜利是由于对方球队表现不佳造成的。 The tree grew lopsided after the storm.  暴风雨过后，这棵树长得歪歪扭扭的。| unbalanced, uneven, asymmetrical | balanced, symmetrical, even |
