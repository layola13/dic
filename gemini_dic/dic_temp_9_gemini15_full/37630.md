communities:/kəˈmjuːnɪtiz/ | n. | 社区，团体 | A group of people living in the same place or having a particular characteristic in common.  |例句：The local communities are working together to improve the park. 当地社区正在合作改善公园。

例句：Strong communities are essential for a healthy society. 健全的社区对于健康的社会至关重要。


近义词：groups, societies, associations | 反义词：individuals, solitudes


词源:  From Middle English *comunités, from Old French communauté, from Latin communitas "commonness, community," from communis "common."
