skylight:/ˈskaɪlaɪt/| n. |天窗，天窗灯|A skylight is a window set into a roof or ceiling to allow light to enter a building.  It is often used in areas where there are few or no other windows.  Skylights can be fixed or operable, allowing for ventilation. |The skylight in the attic let in plenty of natural light.  阁楼的天窗带来了充足的自然光。

The skylight was opened to let in some fresh air.  天窗被打开以让一些新鲜空气进来。|roof window,rooflight|  solid roof, solid ceiling|n.
