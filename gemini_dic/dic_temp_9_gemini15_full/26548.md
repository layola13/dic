cabinets:/ˈkæbɪnəts/| n. |橱柜；内阁；(一组)储藏柜 |A cabinet is a piece of furniture with shelves or drawers for storing things.  It can also refer to a group of ministers who advise a head of state. |例句：The kitchen cabinets are full of dishes. 厨房的橱柜里装满了餐具。

例句：The President's cabinet met to discuss the crisis. 总统内阁开会讨论危机。


近义词：cupboard, locker, closet |反义词：None.
