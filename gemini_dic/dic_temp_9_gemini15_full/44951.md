cryptopunks:/ˈkrɪptoʊpʌŋks/| n. |加密朋克|A collection of 10,000 unique digitally generated, algorithmically created cartoon-like characters that exist as non-fungible tokens (NFTs) on the Ethereum blockchain.  Each CryptoPunk is unique and has different attributes, making them collectible and valuable. |Example:  He invested a significant amount of money in buying a rare CryptoPunk. 他投资了一大笔钱购买一个稀有的加密朋克。|近义词：NFT collectibles, digital art, blockchain art|反义词：physical art, traditional art
