runnet:/ˈrʌnɪt/| n. |凝乳酶；干酪素|Rennet is an enzyme preparation traditionally obtained from the fourth stomach of unweaned ruminant mammals, such as calves, lambs, and kids. It is used in cheesemaking to coagulate milk.  |例句：The cheesemaker added rennet to the milk to start the coagulation process. 制奶酪的人往牛奶里加了凝乳酶来开始凝固过程。|近义词：chymosin, rennase|反义词：无
