cullet:/ˈkʌlət/| n. |碎玻璃；玻璃渣 |Broken or waste glass;  fragments of glass. Cullet is often recycled and used in the manufacturing of new glass products.  |Example: The glass factory uses cullet to reduce its manufacturing costs and environmental impact.  玻璃厂使用碎玻璃来降低制造成本和环境影响。|近义词：broken glass, glass fragments, waste glass |反义词：whole glass, intact glass

词源:
The word "cullet" ultimately derives from the Old French word "coulée," meaning "a running or flowing," referring to the molten glass flowing.  Over time, the meaning shifted to refer specifically to the waste or broken pieces of glass.
