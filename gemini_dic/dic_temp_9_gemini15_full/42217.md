counseling: /ˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/ | n. |咨询；辅导；顾问服务 | The act of giving advice or guidance to someone.  It often involves helping individuals cope with personal problems, emotional distress, or career challenges. |例句：She sought counseling after her divorce. 她离婚后寻求了咨询服务。|例句：The school offers career counseling to students. 学校为学生提供职业咨询。|近义词：advising, guidance, therapy, mentoring |反义词：neglect, abandonment
