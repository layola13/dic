potentialities:/ˌpoʊtɛnʃiˈælətiz/ | n. |潜能；可能性；潜在能力 | The inherent capacity or ability of something to develop or achieve a certain result.  It refers to the possibilities that exist, whether or not they are realized. |例句：The potentialities of the new technology are vast. 这项新技术的潜力巨大。

例句：He explored the potentialities of his new invention. 他探索了他新发明的潜力。


近义词： possibilities, capabilities, prospects, potential, chances

反义词： impossibilities, limitations, constraints


词性：名词
