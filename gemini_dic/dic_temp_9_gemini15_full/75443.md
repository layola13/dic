fruitiest:/ˈfruːtiɪst/| adj.| 最多汁的；最果香的；最成功的 | Most full of fruit; having the most intense fruity flavor or aroma; exceptionally good or successful.  |例句：This is the fruitiest wine I've ever tasted. 这是我喝过最香醇的葡萄酒。

例句：Her latest novel is her fruitiest yet, full of vibrant characters and exciting plot twists.  她最新的小说是她迄今为止最成功的一部，充满了充满活力的人物和令人兴奋的情节转折。


近义词：most flavorful, most aromatic, most delicious, most successful
