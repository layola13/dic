delights:/dɪˈlaɪts/| n. v.|使高兴；乐事；乐趣|The act of giving great pleasure; something that gives great pleasure.|例句：The children delighted in playing in the snow. 孩子们喜欢在雪地里玩耍。  The garden was a delight to the eye. 花园赏心悦目。|近义词：pleasures, joys, thrills|反义词：disappointments, sorrows, miseries|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music delights the audience. = 音乐使观众高兴。
His jokes delight everyone. = 他的笑话使每个人都高兴。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I could delight in simpler pleasures. = 我希望我能享受更简单的快乐。


被动语态:

疑问句:

Does gardening delight you? = 你喜欢园艺吗？


否定句:
