denuder:/dɪˈnuːdər/ | n. | 除去剂，净化器 | A device or process that removes unwanted substances from a gas or liquid stream.  It is often used in environmental monitoring and industrial processes to remove pollutants or contaminants before analysis or release.  | Example: The denuder efficiently removed the sulfur dioxide from the air sample.  除去剂有效地从空气样本中去除了二氧化硫。 |近义词：scrubber, purifier, cleaner | 反义词：polluter, contaminator
