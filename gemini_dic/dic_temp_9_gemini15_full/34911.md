clitellus:/klɪˈtɛləs/| n.|环带，生殖带|A clitellum is a thickened band of segments in some annelid worms, such as earthworms, that secretes a cocoon for eggs.  It's a reproductive structure.|例句：The earthworm's clitellum is visible as a swollen band around its body.  蚯蚓的环带在其身体周围可见为一条肿胀的带状物。

The clitellus secretes mucus that helps in mating and cocoon formation. 环带分泌粘液，有助于交配和茧的形成。|近义词：girdle (in this context)|反义词：无|词性:名词
