chuntering:/ˈtʃʌntərɪŋ/ | v. |小声抱怨；嘟囔；喋喋不休 |  The act of muttering or grumbling in a low, indistinct tone.  It often implies discontent or annoyance. |例句：He was chuntering to himself about the unfairness of it all. 他自言自语地抱怨着这一切的不公平。

例句：The children were chuntering about having to go to bed. 孩子们嘟囔着必须上床睡觉。


近义词： grumbling, muttering, grumbling, murmuring, complaining | 反义词：praising, complimenting
