pummelling: /ˈpʌməlɪŋ/ | v. | 猛击；痛打；连续猛击 |  To strike repeatedly with the fists or a similar object; to beat severely. |  例句：The boxer pummelled his opponent relentlessly. 拳击手无情地猛击对手。

例句：The waves were pummelling the coastline. 海浪猛烈地冲击着海岸线。


近义词：batter, thrash, beat, pound, maul | 反义词：caress, soothe
