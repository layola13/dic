pitchforks:/ˈpɪtʃfɔːrks/| n.|干草叉；煽动暴乱的人|A pitchfork is a long-handled tool with multiple tines (usually three or four), used primarily for pitching (tossing) hay or other loose materials.  Figuratively, it can refer to a group of angry people ready to cause trouble.|例句：The farmer used a pitchfork to move the hay.  农夫用干草叉搬运干草。

例句：The social media posts ignited a pitchfork mob against the company. 社交媒体上的帖子激怒了一群人，他们对该公司群起而攻之。|近义词：fork (in some contexts), hayfork|反义词：N/A
