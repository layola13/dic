marketplace:/ˈmɑːrkɪtpleɪs/| n. |市场；集市；市场份额；竞争环境 |A marketplace is a location where goods and services are exchanged, either physically or virtually.  It can refer to a physical market, such as a farmer's market, or a virtual market, such as an online store.  The term can also be used more broadly to describe the competitive environment in which businesses operate. |例句：The farmer's market is a bustling marketplace every Saturday.  这个农贸市场每个星期六都很热闹。|例句：Amazon is a dominant player in the online marketplace. 亚马逊是线上市场的主要参与者。|近义词：market, exchange, bazaar, store, online store |反义词：monopoly
