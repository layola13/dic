rhinoplastic:/ˌraɪnoʊˈplæstɪk/ | adj., n. | 鼻整形术的；鼻整形术 | Relating to or denoting plastic surgery of the nose.  A surgical procedure to reshape the nose. |  The rhinoplastic surgery was successful and improved her breathing.  鼻整形手术很成功，改善了她的呼吸。 |  nasal surgery, nose job |  (No direct antonym, but could be described as the opposite of leaving the nose unchanged)
