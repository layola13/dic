merchandise:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪs/| n.|商品，货物|Goods or wares that are bought and sold.  Merchandise can refer to a wide range of products, from clothing and electronics to food and household items.|例句：The store offers a wide variety of merchandise. 这家商店提供各种各样的商品。|例句：The company's merchandise is sold worldwide. 该公司的商品在全球销售。|近义词：goods, wares, products, commodities|反义词：services
