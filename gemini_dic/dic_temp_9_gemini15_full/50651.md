derivable:/dɪˈraɪvəbl/ | adj. | 可推导的，可派生的 | Able to be derived; obtained or deduced from a source.  | Example:  The theorem is derivable from Euclid's postulates.  这个定理可以从欧几里得的公理推导出来。  The conclusion is derivable from the given premises.  结论可以从给定的前提推导出来。 |近义词：deducible, inferable, attainable | 反义词：indeducible, unobtainable
