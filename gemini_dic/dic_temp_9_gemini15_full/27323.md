calyx:/ˈkeɪlɪks/ | n. |花萼；萼片；(某些植物的)杯状结构 | The cup-shaped structure at the base of a flower, enclosing the reproductive organs. It's usually green and leaf-like.  |The rose's calyx was a vibrant green.  玫瑰的花萼是鲜艳的绿色。 The botanist carefully examined the calyx of the rare orchid. 植物学家仔细检查了这种稀有兰花的花萼。|sepals|petal (反义词，相对概念)|名词
