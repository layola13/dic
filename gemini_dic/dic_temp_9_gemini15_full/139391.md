observant:/əbˈzɜːrvənt/| adj.|观察仔细的；善于观察的|Paying close attention to one's surroundings and details.|He is an observant man, noticing even the smallest details. 他是一个观察仔细的人，即使是最小的细节也能注意到。|perceptive, watchful, attentive|inattentive, oblivious, unobserving


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is observant of his surroundings. 他观察周围环境很仔细。
She is observant of people's behavior. 她善于观察人们的行为。


一般过去时:
He was observant during the meeting. 他在会议期间观察得很仔细。
She was observant of the changes in the market. 她观察到了市场上的变化。


现在完成时:
He has been observant throughout his career. 他在整个职业生涯中都观察仔细。
She has been observant of the political climate. 她一直密切关注政治气候。


过去完成时:
He had been observant of her behavior for weeks. 他几周来一直密切关注她的行为。
She had been observant of the clues, leading to the solution. 她一直观察线索，最终找到了解决办法。


将来时:
He will be observant of the children's activities. 他会密切关注孩子们活动。
She will be observant of any suspicious activity. 她会留意任何可疑活动。


虚拟语气:
If he were more observant, he would have noticed the mistake. 如果他更细心一些，他就会注意到这个错误。
If she had been more observant, she would have prevented the accident. 如果她更细心一些，她就能避免事故发生。


被动语态:
The details were observed by the observant detective.  细致的细节被那位敏锐的侦探观察到。
His behavior was observed by an observant bystander.  他的行为被一个细心的旁观者观察到。


疑问句:
Is he observant enough to notice the subtle difference? 他观察力够敏锐到注意到细微差别吗？
Was she observant enough to catch the thief? 她观察力够敏锐到抓住小偷吗？


否定句:
He was not observant enough to see the danger. 他观察力不够敏锐，没有看到危险。
She was not observant of the warning signs. 她没有注意到警告信号。


PHRASE->

a highly observant person: 一个非常敏锐的人
an observant eye: 一双善于观察的眼睛
observant of detail: 注意细节
remarkably observant: 非常敏锐的
be observant of: 注意到；观察到
