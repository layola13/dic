saturant:/ˈsætʃərənt/| n.|饱和剂，溶剂|A substance that saturates something else, typically by dissolving in or absorbing it.  It often refers to a substance that is used to fully soak or impregnate another material.|例句：The dye acted as a saturant, deeply coloring the fabric. 染料作为饱和剂，使织物着色很深。|例句：The soil was treated with a saturant to improve its water retention.土壤用饱和剂处理以提高其保水性。|近义词：impregnant, solvent,  drenching agent |反义词：desaturant (less common)
