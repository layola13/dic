sclerenchyme: /skləˈrɛŋkaɪm/ | n. | 厚壁组织 |  A type of plant tissue composed of cells with thick, lignified walls that provide structural support.  The cells are typically dead at maturity.  | Example:  The sclerenchyme in the stem provides strength and rigidity.  厚壁组织赋予茎干强度和硬度。| 近义词： supporting tissue, strengthening tissue | 反义词： parenchyma (薄壁组织) |
