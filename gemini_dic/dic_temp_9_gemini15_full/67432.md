extinguish:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/| vt.|熄灭；使破灭；消灭|to put out a fire or light; to bring to an end|例句：The firefighters quickly extinguished the blaze. 消防员迅速扑灭了大火。
例句：His ambition was extinguished by repeated failures. 他屡遭失败，雄心壮志也随之破灭。|近义词：quench, put out, suppress|反义词：ignite, kindle, start
