cranes:/kreɪnz/| n.|鹤；起重机；(船的)起重臂|A crane is a large bird with long legs and a long neck.  A crane is also a machine used for lifting and moving heavy objects.|例句：The cranes are migrating south for the winter.  鹤正在向南迁徙过冬。

The construction workers used a crane to lift the steel beams. 建筑工人用起重机吊起钢梁。|近义词：bird (for the bird), hoist, derrick |反义词：None that directly oppose the meaning.
