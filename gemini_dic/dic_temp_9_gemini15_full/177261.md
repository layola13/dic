reassertion's:/ˌriːæˈsɜːrʃənz/| n.|再断言；重申；再次肯定|The act of asserting something again; a renewed or repeated assertion.  It often implies a stronger or more forceful statement than the original assertion.|例句：His reassertion of his innocence was met with skepticism.  他的再次声明清白受到了怀疑。

例句：The reassertion of traditional values is a significant trend in society.  传统价值观的再次强调是社会中的一个重要趋势。


近义词：reaffirmation, reiteration, confirmation

反义词：retraction, denial, repudiation

词性:名词
