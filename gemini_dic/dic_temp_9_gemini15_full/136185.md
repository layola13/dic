nonrelational:/ˌnɒnˌreɪˈleɪʃənəl/| adj. |非关系型数据库的；非关系型的|Not based on the relational model of data; referring to databases that do not use tables with rows and columns to store data.  Instead, they use various other data models such as key-value stores, document databases, graph databases, or wide-column stores.| Example:  "NoSQL databases are examples of nonrelational database systems."  "The application uses a nonrelational database for its flexible data structure."| 近义词:NoSQL, non-relational database| 反义词:relational, relational database
