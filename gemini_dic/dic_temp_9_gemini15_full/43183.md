crawled:/krɔːld/ | v. | 爬行；缓慢移动 | To move on the hands and knees, or by dragging the body along the ground.  It can also describe slow or difficult movement. |例句：The baby crawled across the floor.  婴儿在地上爬行。

The injured man crawled to safety. 受伤的男人艰难地爬到安全地带。

近义词：crept, inched, slithered | 反义词：walked, ran


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The snail crawls slowly.  蜗牛缓慢地爬行。
He crawls on his hands and knees. 他用手和膝盖爬行。


一般过去时:
The snake crawled away. 蛇爬走了。
She crawled into bed. 她爬上床。


现在完成时:
The baby has crawled across the room. 婴儿已经爬过了房间。
The dog has crawled under the table. 狗已经爬到桌子下面了。


过去完成时:
The thief had crawled through the window before the police arrived.  警察到达之前，小偷已经从窗户爬进来了。
After the earthquake, survivors had crawled from the rubble. 地震后，幸存者们从瓦砾中爬了出来。


将来时:
The caterpillar will crawl up the plant. 毛毛虫将会爬上植物。
He will crawl to me for help. 他会向我求助。


虚拟语气:
If I were a snake, I would crawl silently. 如果我是一条蛇，我会悄悄地爬行。
I wish I could crawl faster. 我希望我能爬得更快。


被动语态:
The toy car can be crawled across the floor by the child. 玩具车可以被孩子在地上推着爬行。 (This use is less common and sounds unnatural)


疑问句:
Does the insect crawl or fly?  这只昆虫是爬行还是飞行？
How does a caterpillar crawl? 毛毛虫是怎么爬行的？


否定句:
The injured bird could not crawl. 受伤的鸟儿不能爬行。
The robot did not crawl; it rolled. 机器人没有爬行；它是滚动的。
