reexamining:/ˌriːɪɡˈzæmɪnɪŋ/| v. |重新审查；再检查；重新审视 |To examine again; to reconsider.  It implies a more thorough or critical review than a simple re-examination.|例句：The committee is reexamining the evidence before making a decision. 委员会在做出决定之前正在重新审查证据。

例句：The professor is reexamining his theories in light of new research. 教授根据新的研究成果正在重新审视他的理论。


近义词：review, reconsider, reassess, re-evaluate | 反义词：accept (without further examination), ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reexamining the budget. = 他们正在重新审查预算。
We reexamine the data every quarter. = 我们每季度都会重新检查数据。


一般过去时:
She reexamined the document carefully. = 她仔细地重新检查了文件。
The judge reexamined the witness. = 法官再次传唤证人作证。


现在完成时:
They have reexamined the evidence several times. = 他们已经多次重新审查了证据。
We have reexamined our strategies. = 我们已经重新审视了我们的策略。


过去完成时:
They had reexamined the accounts before the audit. = 审计之前，他们已经重新审查了账目。
She had reexamined her life choices. = 她已经重新审视了她的人生选择。


将来时:
We will reexamine the proposal next week. = 我们下周将重新审查该提案。
They will reexamine the patient tomorrow. = 他们明天将对病人进行复查。


虚拟语气:
If they reexamined the evidence, they might reach a different conclusion. = 如果他们重新审查证据，可能会得出不同的结论。
I wish I had reexamined my assumptions earlier. = 我希望我早点重新审视我的假设。


被动语态:
The case was reexamined by the Supreme Court. = 此案被最高法院重新审理。
The data will be reexamined by an independent team. = 数据将由一个独立小组重新检查。


疑问句:
Are they reexamining the policy? = 他们正在重新审查这项政策吗？
Will the committee reexamine the proposal? = 委员会会重新审查该提案吗？


否定句:
They did not reexamine the evidence. = 他们没有重新审查证据。
She will not reexamine her decision. = 她不会重新考虑她的决定。


PHRASE->
reexamining the facts = 重新审查事实
reexamining the evidence = 重新审查证据
reexamining the assumptions = 重新审视假设
reexamining the implications = 重新审视影响
reexamining the options = 重新考虑选择
