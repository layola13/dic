foreknowingly:/fɔːrˈnoʊɪŋli/|/fɔːrˈnəʊɪŋli/| adj.|预知地；预先知道地 |Knowing something beforehand; with prior knowledge.  It implies a deliberate act or decision made with the knowledge of future consequences. |例句：He foreknowingly misled the investigators, knowing his actions would hinder the case. 他预知地误导了调查人员，知道他的行为会妨碍案件。

例句：She foreknowingly chose to remain silent, anticipating the backlash. 她预先知道地选择保持沉默，预料到会遭到反弹。


近义词： knowingly, intentionally, deliberately, purposefully

反义词： unknowingly, unintentionally, accidentally, inadvertently
