lampreys:/ˈlæmpriːz/| n. |七鳃鳗；八目鳗| Lampreys are jawless fish that are parasitic on other fish. They have a sucker-like mouth with sharp teeth. |例句：The lampreys attached themselves to the salmon and sucked its blood.  七鳃鳗吸附在鲑鱼身上吸食它的血液。|例句：Scientists are studying the lamprey's unique physiology. 科学家们正在研究七鳃鳗独特的生理机能。|近义词：hagfish (although hagfish are not true lampreys)|反义词：无|名词
