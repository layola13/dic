dwarfisms:/ˈdwɔːrfɪzəm/| n. |侏儒症；矮小症 | A group of conditions that cause a person to be much shorter than average.  It can be caused by genetic factors, hormonal imbalances, or other medical conditions.  Dwarfism affects many aspects of a person's life, including physical appearance, mobility, and potential health issues. |例句：The child was diagnosed with a form of dwarfism affecting bone growth.  这个孩子被诊断为一种影响骨骼生长的侏儒症。

例句：Several types of dwarfism exist, each with unique characteristics and causes.  存在几种类型的侏儒症，每种都有其独特的特征和原因。


近义词：short stature, nanism | 反义词：gigantism
