prereports:/ˌpriːˈrɪpɔːrts/| n. |预先报告，预报|Preliminary reports issued before the full findings are available.  These reports often contain initial observations and findings, but may lack the depth of analysis present in the final report.|The prereports indicated a potential problem with the software.|近义词：preliminary reports, advance reports, preliminary findings|反义词：final reports, conclusive reports
