dictograph:/ˈdɪktoɡræf/| n. |窃听器，录音机 |A dictograph is a device used for secretly recording conversations or for listening to conversations in a clandestine manner.  It's often associated with espionage and investigations. |The detective used a dictograph to gather evidence against the suspect.  侦探用窃听器收集证据以对付嫌疑人。  The courtroom drama featured a hidden dictograph as a crucial plot point. 法庭戏剧中隐藏的窃听器是关键的情节点。| wiretap, bug, listening device |  (近义词没有完全的反义词，因为“不窃听”本身不是一个设备) | 名词
