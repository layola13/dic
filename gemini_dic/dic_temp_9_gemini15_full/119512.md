microportal: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈpɔːrtl/ | n. | 微型门户网站，微门户 | A small, specialized website or web application that provides access to a specific set of information or services.  It often focuses on a niche topic or community. | Example:  The company launched a microportal for its employees to access internal documents and communication. 这家公司为员工启动了一个微型门户网站，用于访问内部文档和沟通。| 近义词：mini-portal, niche website, specialized website | 反义词：macroportal (this word is not commonly used)

词源:

The word "microportal" is a compound word.  "Micro-" is a prefix meaning "small" or "minute," derived from the Greek word "mikros" (μικρός). "Portal" comes from the Latin word "portalis," relating to a gate or entrance, later evolving to mean a gateway or access point, particularly in the context of the internet.  Therefore, "microportal" literally means a small gateway or access point on the internet.
