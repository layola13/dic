dibber:/ˈdɪbər/| n. v.|  播种器；用播种器播种 |A dibber is a tool used for making holes in the ground for planting seeds or seedlings, especially small ones.  It is often a pointed stick or rod. As a verb, it means to plant (seeds or seedlings) using a dibber.|例句：The gardener used a dibber to plant the tiny seeds. 园丁用播种器播种了微小的种子。  He dibbed the seedlings into the prepared soil. 他用播种器将幼苗种入准备好的土壤中。|近义词：planter, seed drill|反义词：harvester|名词，动词
