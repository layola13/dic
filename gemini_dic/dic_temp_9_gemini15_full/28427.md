carbonizes:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/ | v. |使碳化；使变成焦炭 | To convert (something) into carbon or charcoal; to char.  The process involves the removal of volatile constituents from organic materials, leaving behind a primarily carbon-based residue. |例句：The intense heat carbonized the wood.  高温使木材碳化。
