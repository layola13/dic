conjecturalist:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərəlɪst/| n. |推测者，臆测者 |A conjecturalist is someone who engages in conjecture, forming opinions or theories based on incomplete information or speculation. They are often willing to put forward hypotheses even with limited evidence.  |Example: The historian, though a brilliant conjecturalist, often strayed into unfounded speculation.  这位历史学家虽然是一位杰出的推测者，但他常常陷入毫无根据的推测中。|近义词：speculator, theorist, guesser |反义词：empiricist, pragmatist |名词
