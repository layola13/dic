instep's:/ˈɪnˌstɛp/| n.| 同步；协调一致 |  The possessive form of "instep," referring to the arched part of the foot between the toes and the ankle. It's primarily used in phrases describing movement or coordination.  |例句：The dancers moved in perfect instep's rhythm. 舞者们以完美的步伐节奏同步移动。  |近义词：synchronization, harmony, unison |反义词：discord, disharmony, asynchrony |名词


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense considerations are irrelevant.  The usage focuses on its use in phrases.


PHRASE->
in step: 步调一致, 同步
out of step: 步调不一致, 脱节
in step with: 与...同步, 与...一致
fall into step: 齐步前进, 步调一致起来
keep in step: 保持同步, 保持一致
