equalized:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzd/| v.| 使平等；使均衡；使相等 | To make something equal or uniform.  The process of making things equal. |例句：The team equalized the score in the final minutes.  球队在最后几分钟扳平了比分。

The government implemented policies to equalize income distribution. 政府实施了旨在均衡收入分配的政策。

近义词：balanced, leveled, equated, standardized |反义词：differentiated, unequalized, disparate

词源:
The word "equalized" is derived from the verb "equalize," which in turn comes from the adjective "equal" + the suffix "-ize," meaning "to make or become."  The root "equal" traces back to Old French "egal" and ultimately to Latin "aequalis," meaning "equal."
