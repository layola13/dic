regretting:/rɪˈɡrɛtɪŋ/| v. |后悔；遗憾 | expressing sorrow over something done or not done. It's a present participle form of the verb "regret".|例句：I'm regretting not studying harder for the exam. = 我后悔考试没有努力学习。

例句：She's regretting her decision to quit her job. = 她后悔辞职了。


近义词：repenting, rueing, lamenting | 反义词：rejoicing, celebrating | 词性：动词(现在分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am regretting my hasty words. = 我正在后悔我轻率的话语。
He is regretting missing the concert. = 他后悔错过了音乐会。


一般过去时:
I regretted my actions immediately. = 我立刻后悔了自己的行为。
She regretted not telling him the truth. = 她后悔没有告诉他真相。


现在完成时:
I have been regretting my decision ever since. = 从那以后我一直后悔我的决定。
They have regretted their investment for years. = 他们多年来一直后悔他们的投资。


过去完成时:
I had regretted my decision before I even left. = 我甚至在离开之前就后悔了我的决定。
She had regretted her impulsive purchase long before the item arrived. = 在商品到达很久之前，她就后悔冲动购物了。


将来时:
I will regret this later. = 我以后会后悔的。
We will regret not taking this opportunity. = 我们会后悔没有抓住这个机会的。


虚拟语气:
I would regret it if I didn't try. = 如果我不尝试的话我会后悔的。
She wished she hadn't regretted her choice so much. = 她希望自己不要那么后悔自己的选择。


被动语态:
The decision was later regretted by many. = 许多人后来后悔了这个决定。(很少用被动语态)
His words were regretted by everyone present. = 在场的人都后悔他的话。(很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Are you regretting your choice? = 你后悔你的选择吗？
Does he regret what he did? = 他后悔自己所做的事情吗？


否定句:
I don't regret anything. = 我不后悔任何事。
He didn't regret his decision at all. = 他一点也不后悔他的决定。


PHRASE->
regret to inform you = 遗憾地通知你
regrettably = 遗憾地
deep regret = 深深的遗憾
with regret = 遗憾地
feel regret = 感到后悔
