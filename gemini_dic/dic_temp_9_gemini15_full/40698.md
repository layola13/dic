conversionist: /kənˈvɜːrʒənɪst/ | adj. n. | 皈依者；改宗者；主张改宗者 | A person who seeks to convert others to a particular religion or belief system.  Someone actively involved in the process of conversion, often religious. |  例句：He was a fervent conversionist, traveling the world to spread his faith. 他是一位狂热的皈依者，周游世界传播他的信仰。

例句：The conversionist's passionate sermons attracted many new followers.  这位皈依者的热情布道吸引了许多新的信徒。


近义词：proselytizer, missionary, evangelist | 反义词：apostate (in a religious context)


词性: adjective, noun
