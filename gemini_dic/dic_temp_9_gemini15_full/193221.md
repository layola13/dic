seductresses:/sɪˈdʌktrəsɪz/| n. | 妖妇；尤物；诱惑者 |  A seductress is a woman who is skilled at tempting or attracting people, especially sexually.  The word often implies a manipulative or deceitful nature.  | Example: The novel featured a cunning seductress who used her charms to achieve her goals.  小说中出现了一个狡猾的妖妇，她用自己的魅力来实现自己的目标。 |近义词：temptress, siren, charmer | 反义词：prude, innocent
