forages:/ˈfɒrɪdʒ/|/ˈfɔːrɪdʒ/| v.|觅食；搜索；搜寻 |To search for food or provisions.|例句：The birds forage for seeds in the fields.  鸟儿在田野里觅食种子。

例句：She foraged through the drawers looking for her keys. 她翻箱倒柜地找钥匙。


近义词：search, hunt, seek, rummage| 反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could depend on context (e.g., "provide" if referring to providing food).|动词

词源:
From Old French *forrager ("to forage"), from Old North French forre ("fodder, forage"). Ultimately from Frankish *fodrum ("fodder").  The word's meaning has broadened over time from specifically referring to collecting fodder for animals to the broader sense of searching for food or supplies.
