cabazite:/ˈkæbəzaɪt/| n. |方沸石|A hydrated calcium aluminosilicate mineral, a member of the zeolite group.  It is usually white or colorless, but can also be yellow, brown, or red.  It forms in cavities in volcanic rocks. |例句：Cabazite is a relatively rare zeolite mineral found in cavities of volcanic rocks. 方沸石是一种相对稀有的沸石矿物，存在于火山岩的空洞中。|近义词：chabazite (often used interchangeably)|反义词：无|名词
