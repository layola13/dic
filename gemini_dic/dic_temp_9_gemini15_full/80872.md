gouty:/ˈɡaʊti/ | adj. |痛风的；患痛风的；像痛风一样的|  Characterized by or affected with gout.  The adjective "gouty" describes something related to or suffering from gout, a form of inflammatory arthritis. It can refer to the condition itself, the symptoms, or even something that resembles the swollen, inflamed appearance of a gout-affected joint. |例句：He suffered from gouty arthritis in his big toe. 他的大脚趾患有痛风性关节炎。

He had gouty swelling in his knee. 他的膝盖肿胀，像痛风一样。|近义词：arthritic, inflamed |反义词：healthy, uninflamed
