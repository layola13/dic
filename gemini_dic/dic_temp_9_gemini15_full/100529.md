intervisited:/ˌɪntərˈvɪzɪtɪd/ | v. |相互探访；互相拜访 |  The word "intervisited" is a relatively rare and informal word formed by combining "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among") and "visited" (the past tense of "visit"). It implies a reciprocal act of visiting, where multiple parties visit each other.  |例句：The two families intervisited frequently throughout the summer.  两个家庭整个夏天都频繁地互相拜访。
The neighboring countries intervisited to strengthen diplomatic ties.  邻国互相访问以加强外交关系。|近义词：mutually visited, visited each other |反义词：remained apart, stayed isolated
