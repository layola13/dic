cantine:/kænˈtiːn/| n. |食堂，餐厅|A canteen is a place where food and drinks are served, especially in a workplace, school, or military base. It can be a simple room with tables and chairs or a more elaborate cafeteria-style facility.  |例句：The factory canteen provides lunch for all employees.  工厂食堂为所有员工提供午餐。|例句：The school canteen offered a variety of healthy options. 学校食堂提供各种健康食品。|近义词：cafeteria, refectory, mess hall|反义词：N/A
