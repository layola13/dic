megastar:/ˈmɛɡəˌstɑːr/ | n. |巨星，超级明星 | A megastar is a celebrity of extremely high status and popularity, typically in entertainment such as film, music, or sports.  They are known for their immense fame, significant influence, and often, enormous wealth. | Example: Beyoncé is a megastar whose influence extends beyond music.  碧昂丝是一位巨星，她的影响力超越了音乐领域。  She's a global megastar known for her captivating performances. 她是一位以迷人表演而闻名的全球巨星。| 近义词：superstar, celebrity, icon | 反义词：nobody, unknown
