This word is a combination of "hyper-" (excessive), "chlor-" (referring to chlorine or chloride), and "-hydric" (referring to hydrogen or containing hydrogen).  Therefore, it literally means "excessively acidic due to chloride" or more precisely, "having an excessive amount of hydrochloric acid."
