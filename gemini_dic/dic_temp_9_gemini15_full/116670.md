mattoid:/ˈmætɔɪd/| n.|类疯子，古怪的人|A person exhibiting eccentric or mildly insane behavior.  The term suggests a less severe form of madness than "maniac" or "lunatic," often implying harmless oddity or eccentricity. |Example: He's a bit of a mattoid, always talking to squirrels.|近义词：eccentric, oddball, crank, weirdo|反义词：sane, normal, rational|名词
