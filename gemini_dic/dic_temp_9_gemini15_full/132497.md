nondesistance: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈsɪs.təns/ | n. | 不放弃；坚持；不屈服 |  The act or state of not ceasing; persistence; continued effort or action.  It implies a steadfast refusal to give up or yield. | Example:  The nondesistance of the workers in their strike finally forced the company to negotiate. 工人们罢工的坚持最终迫使公司进行谈判。 | 近义词：persistence, perseverance, steadfastness | 反义词：abandonment, surrender, cessation
