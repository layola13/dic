nonclarified:/ˌnɒnˈklærɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 未阐明的，未澄清的 | Not made clear or explained; ambiguous.  |例句：The reasons for the company's sudden closure remain nonclarified. 公司突然倒闭的原因仍然不明。 |近义词：unclear, unexplained, ambiguous, obscure |反义词：clear, clarified, explicit
