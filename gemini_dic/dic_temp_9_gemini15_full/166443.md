prerevelations: /ˌpriːˌrevəˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 预言；先兆；预示 |  Things that reveal something before it happens; early indications or signs of something to come.  The word combines "pre" (before) and "revelations" (the act of revealing something previously unknown).  It's a relatively uncommon word, often used in a literary or theological context.  | The ancient prophecies served as prerevelations of the coming apocalypse.  古代预言是即将到来的世界末日的预兆。 | foreshadowing, premonition, portent |  (反义词较难直接对应，因为其含义本身是“预示”，反义词需要根据语境选择，例如：concealment, mystery) | noun
