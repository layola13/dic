misemploy:/ˌmɪsɪmˈplɔɪ/| vt.|滥用；误用；错用|To use wrongly or inefficiently; to misuse.  It implies a failure to utilize something to its full potential or in a way that is intended or appropriate.|例句：He misemployed his talents by working as a simple clerk. 他滥用他的才能，只做了一个小小的职员。

The company misemployed its resources on a failing project.  这家公司把资源错用在一个失败的项目上。|近义词：misuse, misapply, waste|反义词：utilize, employ effectively
