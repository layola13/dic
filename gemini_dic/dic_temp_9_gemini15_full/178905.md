redcurrant:/ˈredˌkʌrənt/| n. |红醋栗|A small, red berry that grows in clusters on a shrub.  Red currants are tart and often used in jams, jellies, and sauces.|例句：We made a delicious redcurrant jam from the berries we picked in the garden. 我们用花园里摘的浆果做了一罐美味的红醋栗果酱。|例句：The tartness of the redcurrants complemented the sweetness of the cake. 红醋栗的酸味与蛋糕的甜味相得益彰。|近义词：red currant berry|反义词：无
