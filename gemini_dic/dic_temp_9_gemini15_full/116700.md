maturity: /məˈtʃʊrɪti/ | n. | 成熟；到期；完备 | The state or process of becoming mature; the state of being fully developed.  |例句：He showed remarkable maturity for his age. 他表现出与年龄不相符的成熟。

例句：The maturity of the bond is in ten years.  这债券的到期日是十年后。


近义词：ripeness, adulthood, completeness, development | 反义词：immaturity, youth, infancy


词源:  From Middle English maturite, from Old French maturité, from Latin maturitās, from matūrus "ripe, mature".
