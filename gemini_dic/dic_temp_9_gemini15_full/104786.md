词源:
The name "Kazakhstan" is derived from the Kazakh word "Qazaq", meaning "free", "independent", or "wanderer".  The suffix "-stan" is a Persian suffix meaning "land" or "place". Therefore, "Kazakhstan" literally translates to "land of the Kazakhs" or "land of the free".
