reexhibiting:/ˌriːɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪŋ/| v. |重新展出，再次展出 |The act of exhibiting something again, after a period of time.  It implies a previous exhibition. |例句：The museum is reexhibiting the Van Gogh paintings after a decade-long restoration.  博物馆在十年修复后重新展出梵高画作。

例句：The artist is reexhibiting his award-winning sculptures in a new gallery.  艺术家在一个新的画廊重新展出他屡获殊荣的雕塑。


近义词：re-showing, displaying again, presenting again

反义词：removing, withdrawing, taking down

The museum reexhibits its collection annually. = 博物馆每年重新展出其收藏品。
They are reexhibiting the dinosaur fossils this summer. = 他们今年夏天正在重新展出恐龙化石。
