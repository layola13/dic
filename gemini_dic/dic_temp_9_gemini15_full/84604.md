hamstringed:/ˈhæmstɹɪŋd/ | adj. v. | 严重削弱；使…难以发挥作用；使…跛足 | Severely weakened or hampered; crippled.  | The company's expansion plans were hamstrung by a lack of funding. 公司的扩张计划由于缺乏资金而受到严重阻碍。  | hindered, impeded, crippled, disabled | empowered, enabled, strengthened |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lack of resources hamstrings our ability to innovate. = 资源匮乏严重阻碍了我们的创新能力。
This new regulation hamstrings small businesses. = 这项新规严重束缚了小型企业的发展。

一般过去时:
The storm hamstrung the rescue efforts. = 暴风雨严重阻碍了救援工作。
His injured leg hamstrung his athletic performance. = 他受伤的腿严重影响了他的运动成绩。


现在完成时:
Years of budget cuts have hamstrung the public education system. = 多年的预算削减已经严重削弱了公共教育体系。
The project has been hamstrung by delays. = 该项目因延误而严重受阻。


过去完成时:
The team had been hamstrung by internal disagreements before the competition. = 在比赛之前，团队内部的分歧已经严重影响了他们的发挥。
The company had been hamstrung by poor management for years. = 多年来，这家公司一直受到糟糕的管理的严重拖累。


将来时:
A prolonged strike will hamstring production. = 长期罢工将严重阻碍生产。
Further restrictions will hamstring economic growth. = 进一步的限制将严重阻碍经济增长。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't interfered, the project wouldn't have been hamstrung. = 如果政府没有干预，这个项目就不会被严重阻碍了。
If they had more funding, they wouldn't be hamstrung by budget constraints. = 如果他们有更多资金，就不会受到预算限制的严重影响了。


被动语态:
The negotiations were hamstrung by mistrust between the parties. = 双方之间的不信任严重阻碍了谈判。
Our plans were hamstrung by unforeseen circumstances. = 我们的计划由于不可预见的情况而严重受阻。


疑问句:
Was the project hamstrung by a lack of resources? = 项目是否由于资源匮乏而严重受阻？
Are our efforts hamstrung by bureaucratic red tape? = 我们的努力是否被官僚主义的繁文缛节严重阻碍？


否定句:
The team wasn't hamstrung by a lack of talent. = 这个团队并不缺乏人才。
The company wasn't hamstrung by the new regulations. = 这家公司并没有受到新规的严重影响。


PHRASE->
be hamstrung by = 受…的严重阻碍
hamstrung with =  充满（负面因素，通常指问题或障碍）
severely hamstrung = 严重受阻
totally hamstrung = 完全受阻
effectively hamstrung = 事实上受阻
