regale:/rɪˈɡeɪl/| vt.|款待；使快乐；讲述；|to entertain someone with stories or amusing anecdotes|He regaled us with tales of his adventures in the Amazon. 他给我们讲述了他亚马逊探险的故事。|delight, entertain, amuse|bore, depress|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regales his friends with funny stories every weekend. 他每个周末都给朋友们讲笑话。
She regales her audience with captivating tales of faraway lands. 她给观众讲述了关于遥远土地的引人入胜的故事。


一般过去时:
The grandfather regaled the children with stories of his youth. 爷爷给孩子们讲了他年轻时的故事。
The storyteller regaled the listeners with a captivating narrative.  讲故事的人给听众讲述了一个引人入胜的故事。


现在完成时:
He has regaled us with so many interesting stories this evening. 今晚他已经给我们讲了很多有趣的故事了。
She has regaled the children with bedtime stories for years. 多年来，她一直给孩子们讲睡前故事。


过去完成时:
They had regaled themselves with delicious food and wine before the performance. 演出前，他们已经用美味的食物和葡萄酒款待了自己。
She had regaled her guests with anecdotes of her travels before they left. 客人离开前，她已经向他们讲述了她旅行中的趣闻轶事。


将来时:
He will regale us with his experiences in Japan next week. 他下周将向我们讲述他在日本的经历。
The comedian will regale the audience with hilarious jokes. 喜剧演员将用滑稽的笑话逗乐观众。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would regale you with stories of my childhood. 如果我有更多时间，我会给你讲讲我童年的故事。
I wish I could regale you with a more exciting adventure story. 我真希望我能给你讲一个更刺激的冒险故事。


被动语态:
The guests were regaled with a sumptuous feast. 客人们享用了丰盛的盛宴。
The audience was regaled with captivating music and dance. 观众欣赏了引人入胜的音乐和舞蹈。


疑问句:
Will he regale us with another story? 他还会给我们讲另一个故事吗？
Did she regale the children with a fairy tale? 她给孩子们讲了童话故事吗？


否定句:
He didn't regale us with any interesting stories. 他没有给我们讲任何有趣的故事。
She didn't regale her guests with her usual wit. 她没有像往常一样用她的机智来招待她的客人。
