despiting:/dɪˈspaɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 不顾；尽管；无视 |  The present participle of the verb "despite". It functions as a preposition or adverb, indicating something happens in spite of an obstacle or difficulty. | 例句：Despite the rain, we went for a walk. = 尽管下雨，我们还是去散步了。  Despite his efforts, he failed the exam. = 尽管他努力了，考试还是不及格。| 近义词：in spite of, notwithstanding, regardless of | 反义词：because of, owing to, due to |
