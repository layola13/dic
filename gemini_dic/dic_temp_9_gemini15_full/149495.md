paeanism:/ˈpiːənɪzəm/| n. |赞美诗歌; 颂歌习俗 |The practice or system of writing or reciting paeans (songs of praise or triumph).  It refers to the enthusiastic expression of praise or admiration, often in a stylized or ritualistic manner.  |例句：The ancient Greeks practiced paeanism as a way to honor their gods and heroes.  希腊人通过颂歌来歌颂他们的神和英雄。|近义词：eulogism, hymnody, laudation |反义词：denunciation, condemnation, vilification
