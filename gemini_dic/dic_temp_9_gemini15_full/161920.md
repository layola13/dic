prairies:/ˈpreəriːz/| n.|大草原，牧场|Extensive grasslands, especially of North America.|The prairies of North America are home to a variety of wildlife.  北美的大草原是各种野生动物的家园。|The vast prairies stretched as far as the eye could see.  广阔的大草原一直延伸到视线所能及的地方。|plains, grasslands|forests, mountains|名词
