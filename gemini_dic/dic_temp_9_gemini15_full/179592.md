reemissions:/ˌriːɪˈmɪʃənz/ | n. | 再排放；二次排放 | The act of emitting something again, especially pollutants or greenhouse gases that have been previously captured or reduced.  This term often refers to the release of greenhouse gases that were previously stored or sequestered.  | Example: The reemissions from deforestation significantly impact climate change.  再造林的二次排放严重影响气候变化。  |近义词：secondary emissions, re-releases | 反义词：sequestration, absorption | 名词
