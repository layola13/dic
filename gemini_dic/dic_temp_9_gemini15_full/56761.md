doozy's:/ˈduːziːz/| n. |极好的人或事物；了不起的事；令人震惊的事|Something extraordinarily good or impressive; something remarkable or astonishing.  A doozy is something exceptional, often in a positive but sometimes negative sense, depending on context. |例句：That cake was a real doozy! = 那蛋糕真是太棒了！

That car accident was a doozy. = 那次车祸真是惨烈。|近义词：marvel, wonder, stunner, knockout, humdinger|反义词：dud, failure, disappointment
