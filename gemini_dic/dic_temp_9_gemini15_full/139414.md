observing:/əbˈzɜːrvɪŋ/ | v. | 观察；遵守；庆祝 | To watch, look at, or pay attention to someone or something carefully.  |例句：The scientist was observing the behavior of the animals.  科学家正在观察动物的行为。

例句：We observed a minute's silence to remember the victims.  我们默哀一分钟来悼念遇难者。


近义词：watching, noticing, monitoring, heeding, celebrating | 反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He observes the birds every morning. = 他每天早上都观察鸟类。
She observes the speed limit. = 她遵守限速。


一般过去时:
I observed a strange phenomenon yesterday. = 我昨天观察到了一种奇怪的现象。
They observed the national holiday with a parade. = 他们以游行的方式庆祝国庆日。


现在完成时:
We have observed a significant increase in sales. = 我们观察到销售额显著增长。
She has observed all the rules. = 她遵守了所有规则。


过去完成时:
He had observed the pattern before making his prediction. = 他在做出预测之前已经观察到了这种模式。
They had observed the tradition for generations. = 他们世世代代都遵循着这个传统。


将来时:
We will observe the eclipse tomorrow. = 我们明天将观察日食。
They will observe the anniversary next week. = 他们下周将庆祝周年纪念日。


虚拟语气:
If I had observed the warning signs, I would have avoided the accident. = 如果我注意到了警告标志，我就能避免事故。
I wish I had observed the details more carefully. = 我希望我当时能更仔细地观察细节。


被动语态:
The experiment was observed by several scientists. = 实验由几位科学家观察。
The rules must be observed by everyone. = 每个人都必须遵守规则。


疑问句:
Did you observe anything unusual? = 你观察到什么异常情况了吗？
What is being observed in this experiment? = 在这个实验中观察的是什么？


否定句:
He didn't observe any changes in the patient's condition. = 他没有观察到病人病情有任何变化。
They did not observe the deadline. = 他们没有遵守最后期限。
