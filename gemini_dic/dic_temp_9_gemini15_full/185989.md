rewaters:/riːˈwɔːtərz/| n. |重新浇水者；再次浇水的人|A rewaterer is someone who waters something again, especially plants or crops that need additional watering.  It's not a common word and is usually understood from context.|例句：The farmer, acting as a rewaterer,  carefully checked the moisture levels before deciding whether to irrigate again.  农民作为再次浇水的人，仔细检查了湿度水平，然后再决定是否再次灌溉。|近义词：irrigator, waterer|反义词：dehydrator (反义词较为宽泛，并非直接相反)|名词
