demonstrating:/ˈdɛmənstreɪtɪŋ/| v. |证明；展示；示范；表达|To clearly show the existence or truth of something by giving proof or evidence; to show how something works or is done.|例句：The experiment demonstrated the effectiveness of the new drug.  实验证明了新药的有效性。

例句：He demonstrated how to use the software. 他演示了如何使用该软件。


近义词：showing, exhibiting, illustrating, proving, showcasing| 反义词：concealing, hiding, obscuring| 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demonstrates his skills daily. = 他每天都展示他的技能。
She demonstrates her point with facts. = 她用事实来证明她的观点。


一般过去时:
They demonstrated against the war. = 他们举行示威抗议战争。
The scientist demonstrated a new theory. = 科学家证明了一个新的理论。


现在完成时:
She has demonstrated her commitment to the cause. = 她已经证明了她对事业的投入。
He has demonstrated his talent many times. = 他已经多次展示了他的天赋。


过去完成时:
They had demonstrated their loyalty before the crisis. = 危机发生前，他们已经证明了他们的忠诚。
She had demonstrated the technique perfectly in practice. = 她已经在实践中完美地展示了这项技术。


将来时:
He will demonstrate the new product at the conference. = 他将在会议上展示新产品。
We will demonstrate our support for the project. = 我们将展示我们对该项目的支持。


虚拟语气:
If he demonstrated more effort, he would succeed. = 如果他付出更多努力，他就会成功。
I wish he had demonstrated more respect. = 我希望他表现出更多尊重。


被动语态:
The theorem was demonstrated by the mathematician. = 这个定理是由数学家证明的。
The new technology is being demonstrated at the trade show. = 新技术正在贸易展会上展示。


疑问句:
How will you demonstrate your competence? = 你将如何证明你的能力？
Does this experiment demonstrate the principle? = 这个实验能证明这个原理吗？


否定句:
He did not demonstrate any remorse. = 他没有表现出任何悔恨。
The evidence failed to demonstrate his guilt. = 证据未能证明他的罪行。


PHRASE->
demonstrate against = 反对…进行示威
demonstrate convincingly = 有说服力地证明
demonstrate clearly = 清晰地展示
demonstrate the importance of = 证明…的重要性
demonstrate one's ability = 展示某人的能力
