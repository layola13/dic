overpolitical: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːlətɪkəl/ | adj. | 过度政治化的；政治色彩过浓的 | Excessively or inappropriately concerned with politics.  The debate became overpolitical, straying from the issue at hand. | 近义词：politicized, partisan, biased | 反义词：apolitical, unbiased, objective

英文解析:
The adjective "overpolitical" describes something that is excessively focused on politics or has an excessive amount of political influence or motivation. It implies that the subject is overly concerned with political aspects to the detriment of other considerations or that political considerations are improperly dominating a situation.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting that the focus on politics is disproportionate, unnecessary, or even manipulative.

例句:

The media coverage of the event was criticized for being overpolitical, ignoring the social and economic aspects.
媒体对该事件的报道因过度政治化而受到批评，忽略了社会和经济方面。


The internal discussions within the company became overpolitical, hindering productivity and decision-making.
公司内部的讨论变得过于政治化，阻碍了生产力和决策。
