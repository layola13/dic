postmark:/ˈpoʊstˌmɑːrk/| n.|邮戳；邮资已付标志|A postmark is an impression made on a letter or package by a post office to show the date and place it was mailed.  It usually consists of a date stamp and the name of the post office.|例句：The postmark on the letter indicated it was sent from London. 这封信上的邮戳表明它是从伦敦寄出的。

The package had a clear postmark showing it had been sent from New York.  包裹上清晰地盖着纽约寄出的邮戳。|近义词：stamp, cancellation mark|反义词:无
