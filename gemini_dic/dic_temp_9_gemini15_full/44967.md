crystallizing: /ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 结晶；明确化；具体化 | To form crystals; to become clear or definite; to take a definite form. | 例句：The sugar solution is crystallizing in the jar. 糖溶液正在罐子里结晶。  The committee is crystallizing its ideas into a concrete proposal. 委员会正在将其想法具体化为具体的提案。 | 近义词：solidifying, clarifying, defining | 反义词：liquefying, blurring, obscuring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The liquid is crystallizing slowly. = 液体正在慢慢结晶。
His ideas are crystallizing into a coherent theory. = 他的想法正在形成一个连贯的理论。

一般过去时:
The scientist crystallized the chemical compound. = 科学家使化学化合物结晶。
The plan crystallized after many discussions. = 经过多次讨论后，计划明确了。


现在完成时:
The solution has crystallized overnight. = 溶液一夜之间结晶了。
The debate has crystallized public opinion. = 这场辩论已经明确了公众舆论。


过去完成时:
The sugar had crystallized before we could analyze it. = 在我们分析之前，糖已经结晶了。
The concept had crystallized in his mind during the long journey. = 在漫长的旅途中，这个概念在他的脑海中变得清晰了。


将来时:
The mixture will crystallize if we leave it undisturbed. = 如果我们让它 undisturbed，混合物就会结晶。
The situation will likely crystallize after the election. = 选后局势可能会明朗。


虚拟语气:
If the conditions were right, the substance would crystallize. = 如果条件合适，物质就会结晶。
If we had more time, our ideas would have crystallized better. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们的想法就会更清晰。


被动语态:
The substance was crystallized by the chemist. = 化合物是由化学家结晶的。
The policy was crystallized after much debate. = 政策经过多次辩论后才最终确定。


疑问句:
Is the solution crystallizing properly? = 溶液结晶是否正常？
Have your thoughts on the matter crystallized yet? = 你对这件事的看法已经明确了吗？


否定句:
The solution did not crystallize as expected. = 溶液没有按预期结晶。
His ideas haven't crystallized yet. = 他的想法还没有明确。


PHRASE->
crystallize out =析出结晶
crystallize into = 发展成；具体化成
crystallize around =  围绕...形成；集中在...
crystallization of thought = 思想的明确化
slow crystallization = 缓慢结晶
