middleweights:/ˈmɪdlˌweɪts/ | n. | 中量级拳击手；中量级选手；中等重量级的人或物 |  A middleweight is a person or thing of medium weight, especially a boxer competing in a weight class between welterweight and light heavyweight.  The term is also used more broadly to refer to something of medium size, importance, or influence. | 例句：He's a middleweight boxer with a powerful punch. 他是一位拥有强大拳力的中量级拳击手。


He's a middleweight contender in the upcoming election. 他是即将到来的选举中的一个中等实力的竞争者。


近义词：mediumweights, average weights | 反义词：heavyweights, lightweights
