lombardeer:/ˌlɒmbɑːˈdɪə(r)/| n. |伦巴第人；放债者| A Lombard, especially one who lent money at high interest rates.  The term originally referred to a member of the Lombardy region of Italy, known for their banking activities in medieval Europe.|例句：Many Lombard bankers held great influence in the medieval courts. 许多伦巴第银行家在中世纪的宫廷中拥有很大的影响力。|近义词：moneylender, usurer|反义词：borrower
