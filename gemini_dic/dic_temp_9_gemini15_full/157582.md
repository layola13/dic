pitchout:/pɪtʃˈaʊt/ | vt. | 投出，抛出；（棒球）故意四坏球保送 | To throw something forcefully.  In baseball, to intentionally walk a batter by throwing four balls outside the strike zone. |例句：He pitched out the ball to the catcher. 他把球投给了捕手。 |例句：The pitcher pitched out the batter to avoid a home run. 投手故意四坏球保送击球手以避免对方打出全垒打。|近义词：throw, hurl, toss, walk (baseball)|反义词：catch, receive


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He pitches out the ball every day. = 他每天都投球。
She pitches out the garbage regularly. = 她定期扔垃圾。

一般过去时:
He pitched out a fastball. = 他投出了一个快速球。
They pitched out the old furniture. = 他们把旧家具扔掉了。

现在完成时:
He has pitched out many balls in his career. = 他职业生涯中投出了许多球。
She has pitched out all the rotten apples. = 她把所有烂苹果都扔掉了。

过去完成时:
He had pitched out several balls before he got tired. = 他在感到疲倦之前已经投出了好几个球。
They had pitched out the broken vase before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经把破花瓶扔掉了。

将来时:
He will pitch out the ball with all his might. = 他将用尽全力投球。
She will pitch out the trash tomorrow. = 她明天会扔垃圾。

虚拟语气:
If he pitched out the ball harder, he might have struck him out. = 如果他投球更用力，他可能就会三振他了。
If she had pitched out the old coat sooner, it wouldn't be so smelly. = 如果她早点扔掉那件旧外套，它就不会这么臭了。

被动语态:
The ball was pitched out by the pitcher. = 球被投手投出去了。
The trash was pitched out yesterday. = 垃圾昨天被扔掉了。

疑问句:
Did he pitch out the ball intentionally? = 他是故意把球投出去的吗？
Will they pitch out the broken chair? = 他们会把坏椅子扔掉吗？

否定句:
He didn't pitch out the ball accurately. = 他没有准确地把球投出去。
She didn't pitch out the letter. = 她没有把信扔掉。
