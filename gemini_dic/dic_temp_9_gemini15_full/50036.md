denotational:/ˌdiːnoʊˈteɪʃənəl/| adj. |指称的；语义的| Relating to denotation, the literal or primary meaning of a word or expression.  It focuses on the relationship between a symbol and its referent,  emphasizing the direct, unambiguous meaning. | The denotational meaning of "dog" is a canine animal.  The denotational semantics of programming languages are concerned with the precise mathematical meaning of programs. |近义词：literal, explicit, semantic (in some contexts)|反义词：connotative, figurative, implicit|
