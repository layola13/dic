chancellor:/ˈtʃænsələr/| n.|大臣；校长；总理|A chancellor is a high-ranking official, the meaning of which varies depending on the context.  It can refer to a head of state, a university administrator, or a high-ranking government official.|例句：The German Chancellor addressed the nation. 德国总理向全国发表讲话。|例句：The university chancellor announced the new academic year.大学校长宣布新学年开始。|近义词：minister, president, rector, headmaster|反义词：None specifically applicable across all meanings.
