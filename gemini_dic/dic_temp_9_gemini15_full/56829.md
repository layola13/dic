dormor:/ˈdɔːrmər/ | n. |老虎窗，屋顶窗 | A dormer is a vertical window that projects from a sloping roof.  It's typically set within a small structure that protrudes from the roofline, adding headroom to an attic or upper storey. | Example: The attic had two charming dormer windows that let in plenty of light.  阁楼有两个迷人的老虎窗，让充足的光线照进来。The house had several dormers, indicating a spacious attic. 这栋房子有好几个老虎窗，表明阁楼很宽敞。| 近义词：roof window, attic window | 反义词：None directly applicable.
