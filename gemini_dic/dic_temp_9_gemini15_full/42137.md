cottonwoods:/ˈkɑːtənˌwʊdz/ | n. |棉白杨树；棉白杨树林 |  Cottonwoods are trees of the genus Populus, known for their cottony seeds. They are fast-growing and often found near rivers and streams. |The cottonwoods along the river bank were swaying gently in the breeze. 河岸边的棉白杨树在微风中轻轻摇曳。  |poplars, willows|  (无明确反义词)| 名词
