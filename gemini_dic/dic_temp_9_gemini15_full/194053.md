self-guards:/ˈsɛlf ɡɑːrdz/| n.|自我保护措施，自我防卫手段|Self-guards are mechanisms or actions taken to protect oneself from harm or danger, either physically or emotionally.  They can be proactive measures to prevent harm or reactive measures taken after an incident. Examples include setting boundaries, seeking support, practicing self-care, and developing coping mechanisms. |例句：The company implemented new self-guards to protect its data from cyberattacks.  这家公司实施了新的自我保护措施来保护其数据免受网络攻击。

例句：He learned to employ better self-guards after experiencing a difficult period in his life. 他在经历人生中一段艰难时期后，学会了运用更好的自我保护措施。|近义词：protective measures, safeguards, defenses, precautions|反义词：vulnerability, exposure, defenselessness|名词
