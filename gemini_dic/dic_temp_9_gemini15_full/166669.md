preservationism:/ˌprɛzərˈveɪʃənɪzəm/| n. |保护主义；保存主义|The philosophy and practice of preserving natural resources and historic sites.  It emphasizes maintaining the status quo and preventing change, often prioritizing the protection of existing environments and structures over development or alteration.  |例句：The debate over the new highway pitted preservationism against the need for improved infrastructure.  保护主义与改善基础设施的需求在新建高速公路的辩论中针锋相对。|近义词：conservationism, historic preservation |反义词：development, exploitation
