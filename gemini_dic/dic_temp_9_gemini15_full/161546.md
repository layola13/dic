potman:/ˈpɒtmən/| n. |卖茶小贩；（尤指过去）酒吧服务员|A potman is a person who sells tea, or, especially in the past, a barman in a pub.  The term is largely obsolete now. |Example: In the old days, the potman would bring around the tea in a large pot.  = 过去，卖茶小贩会用大壶送茶。  The potman at the pub knew everyone's name. = 酒吧的服务员认识每个人的名字。|近义词：barman, bartender, server|反义词：customer, patron


USAGE->
This word is largely obsolete and doesn't readily lend itself to grammatical tense variations in modern English.  The examples below use the word in its historical context.


一般现在时:
The potman serves drinks. = 服务员供应饮料。
The potman brings the tea. = 服务员端来茶。


一般过去时:
The potman served many customers. = 服务员服务了很多顾客。
The potman cleaned the glasses. = 服务员擦拭杯子。


现在完成时:
The potman has served countless pints. = 服务员已经供应了无数品脱的啤酒。
The potman has worked here for years. = 服务员在这里工作了很多年。


过去完成时:
The potman had already closed up before the last customer arrived. = 最后一位顾客到达之前，服务员已经打烊了。
The potman had spilled a drink earlier that evening. = 那天晚上早些时候，服务员洒了一杯饮料。


将来时:
The potman will be busy tonight. = 服务员今晚会很忙。
The potman will open the pub tomorrow morning. = 服务员明天早上会开门营业。


虚拟语气:
If the potman were here, he would help us. = 如果服务员在这儿，他会帮助我们。
I wish the potman hadn't spilled my drink. = 我希望服务员没有洒我的酒。


被动语态:
The drinks were served by the potman. = 饮料是由服务员供应的。
The glasses were cleaned by the potman. = 杯子是由服务员擦拭的。


疑问句:
Was the potman busy? = 服务员忙吗？
Is the potman still working? = 服务员还在工作吗？


否定句:
The potman wasn't available. = 服务员没空。
The potman didn't see me. = 服务员没看见我。
