partner:/ˈpɑːrtnər/| n. |伙伴，合伙人，配偶|A person who takes part in an undertaking with another or others.  A partner in a business shares the profits and losses.|例句：He is my business partner. 他是我的商业伙伴。

例句：She is my life partner. 她是我的终身伴侣。


近义词：colleague, associate, spouse, mate|反义词：opponent, enemy, rival|词性：名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is my business partner. = 他是我的商业伙伴。
They are partners in a law firm. = 他们是律师事务所的合伙人。

一般过去时:
She was his partner in crime. = 她是他的同伙。
They were partners in the dance competition. = 他们在舞蹈比赛中是搭档。

现在完成时:
We have been partners for ten years. = 我们已经合作十年了。
They have been business partners since 2000. = 自2000年以来，他们一直是商业伙伴。

过去完成时:
They had been partners before starting their own company. = 在他们创办自己的公司之前，他们就已经是合作伙伴了。
She had been his partner in several projects. = 她曾是他在几个项目中的合作者。

将来时:
We will be partners in this new venture. = 我们将成为这个新项目的合伙人。
They will remain partners despite their disagreements. = 尽管他们意见不合，他们仍将保持合作伙伴关系。

虚拟语气:
If I had a partner, I would start a business. = 如果我有一个合伙人，我会开始创业。
I wish I had a reliable partner for this project. = 我希望在这个项目中有一个可靠的合作伙伴。

被动语态:
He was chosen as her partner in the dance. = 他被选为她的舞伴。
The company was formed by two equal partners. = 这家公司是由两位平等的合伙人组建的。

疑问句:
Who is your partner in this project? = 你在这个项目中的合伙人是谁？
Will you be my partner for the tango? = 你愿意做我的探戈舞伴吗？

否定句:
He is not my business partner. = 他不是我的商业伙伴。
She doesn't want to be his partner anymore. = 她不想再做他的伙伴了。
