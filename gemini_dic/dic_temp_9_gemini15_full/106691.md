kwela:/ˈkwɛlə/| n.|夸莱拉舞（一种南非舞蹈）|A kwela is a South African musical genre and dance characterized by its lively, upbeat tempo and the use of pennywhistles.  It originated in the townships of South Africa during the apartheid era. |Example: The band played a lively kwela tune, and everyone started dancing.|近义词：jive, mbaqanga|反义词:None specifically.
