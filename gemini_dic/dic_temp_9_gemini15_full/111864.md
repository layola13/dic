logistics:/ləˈdʒɪstɪks/ | n. | 物流；后勤学 | The detailed coordination of a complex operation, in particular, the movement of soldiers or equipment to a war zone.  It involves the planning, implementation, and control of the efficient flow and storage of goods, services, and related information between the point of origin and the point of consumption. | 例句：The company's logistics system is highly efficient. 这家公司的物流系统非常高效。

例句：Efficient logistics are crucial for the success of any large-scale project.  有效的物流对于任何大型项目的成功至关重要。


近义词：supply chain management, distribution, transportation | 反义词：disorganization, chaos | 词性：名词
