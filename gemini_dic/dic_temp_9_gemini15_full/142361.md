orthopedist:/ɔːrθəˈpiːdɪst/ | n. |骨科医生|A physician who specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of musculoskeletal disorders.  Orthopedists deal with conditions affecting bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles.|  I have an appointment with my orthopedist next week to discuss my knee pain. = 我下周约了我的骨科医生来讨论我的膝盖疼痛。| He consulted an orthopedist about his recurring back pain. = 他咨询了一位骨科医生关于他反复出现的背痛。|近义词：orthopaedic surgeon, bone doctor |反义词：无
