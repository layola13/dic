journe: /ˈdʒɜːni/ | n. |旅程，行程 | A journey is an act of traveling from one place to another.  It can refer to a long or short trip, and often implies a sense of purpose or adventure. |例句：The journey to the mountain top was long and arduous.  通往山顶的路途漫长而艰辛。 |例句：Our journey across the country took three days.  我们横跨全国的旅程花了三天时间。|近义词：trip, voyage, travel, excursion |反义词：stay, residence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The journey takes about six hours. = 这段旅程大约需要六个小时。
He journeys to the city every week. = 他每周都去城里。


一般过去时:
We journeyed through the forest. = 我们穿越了森林。
They journeyed to a faraway land. = 他们去遥远的地方旅行了。


现在完成时:
She has journeyed extensively throughout Europe. = 她游历了欧洲各地。
He has journeyed to many different countries. = 他去过许多不同的国家。


过去完成时:
They had journeyed for days before they reached their destination. = 他们在到达目的地之前已经旅行了几天。
She had journeyed far from home. = 她离家很远。


将来时:
We will journey to the coast next summer. = 我们明年夏天将去海边旅行。
They will journey across the desert. = 他们将穿越沙漠。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would journey to the Himalayas. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去喜马拉雅山旅行。
I wish I could journey to the moon. = 我希望我能去月球旅行。


被动语态:
The journey was completed successfully. = 旅程圆满完成。
The route was carefully planned for the journey. = 旅途的路线经过仔细规划。


疑问句:
How long will the journey take? = 旅程要多久？
Where are you journeying to? = 你要去哪里？


否定句:
They did not journey far that day. = 他们那天没有走很远。
We will not journey without proper preparation. = 我们不会没有做好充分准备就出发。
