dumptruck: /ˈdʌmptrʌk/ | n. |自卸卡车 |A heavy-duty truck designed to transport loose materials such as sand, gravel, or dirt, and to easily unload them by tilting its body.  |例句：The construction workers used a dumptruck to haul away the rubble. 建筑工人用自卸卡车运走了瓦砾。

例句：That dumptruck is carrying a huge load of gravel. 那辆自卸卡车装载着大量的碎石。


近义词：tipper, dumper  |反义词：无


词性：名词
