radiotelephone:/ˈreɪdioʊˌtɛləfoʊn/|/ˈreɪdiəʊˌtɛləfoʊn/| n. |无线电话；无线电电话 |A radiotelephone is a device that uses radio waves to transmit and receive voice communications over long distances.  It's essentially a telephone that works without wires, relying on radio frequencies for transmission.  |Example: The captain used the radiotelephone to contact the coast guard. 船长用无线电话联系了海岸警卫队。The ship was equipped with a sophisticated radiotelephone system. 这艘船配备了先进的无线电话系统。|近义词：wireless telephone, radio phone|反义词：landline telephone|名词
