Example:  The maisonette offers stunning views from its upper-floor balcony. 这套复式公寓从其顶层阳台提供了令人惊叹的景色。


近义词：duplex, apartment (in some contexts) | 反义词：single-story apartment, studio apartment | 词性：名词
