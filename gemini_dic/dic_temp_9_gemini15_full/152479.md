payback:/ˈpeɪbæk/ | n. v. | 报复；回报；偿还 |

英文解析：
As a noun, "payback" refers to an act of revenge or retribution for a previous wrong. It can also refer to the return of something previously given, especially financial returns on an investment. As a verb, "payback" means to take revenge on someone, or to repay someone or something.

例句：
He sought payback for the insult.  = 他寻求报复之前的侮辱。

The investment finally gave a good payback. = 这项投资最终带来了丰厚的回报。


近义词：
revenge, retribution, recompense, repayment, return, retaliation

反义词：
forgiveness, pardon, absolution


词性：
Noun, Verb
