perverseness:/ˈpɜːrvərsnəs/| n. | 乖张；任性；故意作对；邪恶 | The deliberate and stubborn resistance to what is considered proper, reasonable, or expected; a quality of being contrary or obstinate.  It often implies a malicious or mischievous intent. |例句：Her perverseness in refusing to cooperate made the project even more difficult.  她的故意不合作使项目更加困难。

例句：The child's perverseness was a constant source of frustration for his parents.  孩子的任性是父母持续的挫败来源。


近义词：obstinacy, contrariness, perversity, waywardness, mulishness


反义词：obedience, compliance, docility, amenability


词源：源自于中古英语 *perversnesse,  源于古法语 perversité, 来自于拉丁语 perversus (歪曲的，反常的)，由 per- (完全地) + versus (转动) 构成。
