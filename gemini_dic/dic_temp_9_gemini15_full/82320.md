grouting:/ˈɡraʊtɪŋ/| n. v.|灌浆；填缝；|The act of filling cracks or spaces with grout.  Grouting is a crucial step in many construction projects to ensure stability and water resistance.|例句：The workers are grouting the tiles in the bathroom. 工人们正在给浴室瓷砖灌浆。


The grout needs to be thoroughly mixed before application.  灌浆材料需要在使用前充分混合。|近义词：filling, sealing |反义词：removing, opening
