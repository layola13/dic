polymerous:/pɒˈlɪmərəs/| adj.|多聚体的；多体的；多部分的|Having many parts or segments; composed of many similar units.  This term is frequently used in biology and chemistry to describe structures composed of many repeating units.|例句：The flower has a polymerous corolla, meaning it has many petals.  这朵花具有多瓣花冠，这意味着它有很多花瓣。|例句：Some proteins are polymerous, consisting of multiple polypeptide chains.  一些蛋白质是多聚体的，由多个多肽链组成。|近义词：multipartite, multifaceted, multinomial|反义词：monomerous, simple, single-part
