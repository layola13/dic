overthrowing:/ˌoʊvərˈθroʊɪŋ/ | v. | 推翻；颠覆；打倒 | The act of violently removing a person or group from power.  It implies a forceful and often violent removal, frequently associated with revolutions or coups. |例句：The rebels were planning to overthrow the government. = 反叛者计划推翻政府。
The dictator was overthrown by his own army. = 独裁者被自己的军队推翻了。|近义词：overturn, subvert, depose |反义词：establish, uphold, support |动词

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
