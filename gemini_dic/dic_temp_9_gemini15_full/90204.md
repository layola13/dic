hounded:/ˈhaʊndɪd/| adj. v.| 受迫害的；追捕；纠缠 |  Continuously harassed or pursued.  |例句：The journalist was hounded by the paparazzi.  记者被狗仔队纠缠不休。

例句：He was hounded out of his job after the scandal broke. 丑闻爆发后，他被逼离职。

近义词：persecuted, pursued, harassed, chased

反义词：protected, shielded, ignored


词性: Verb (过去分词作形容词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hound him relentlessly. = 他们无情地追逐他。
The press hounds the celebrity. =  媒体紧追不舍地报道这位名流。


一般过去时:
The police hounded the suspect. = 警方追捕嫌疑犯。
They hounded her until she confessed. = 他们纠缠她直到她坦白。


现在完成时:
The media has hounded him for years. = 媒体多年来一直追逐他。
She has been hounded by debt collectors. = 她一直被讨债人纠缠。


过去完成时:
The police had hounded the criminal for months before they caught him. = 警方在抓捕罪犯前已经追捕了数月。
They had hounded him into submission. = 他们一直纠缠他直到他屈服。


将来时:
They will hound him until he gives up. = 他们会一直追逐他直到他放弃。
The media will hound her every move. = 媒体会密切关注她的每一个举动。


虚拟语气:
If they hounded him any harder, he would break. = 如果他们更用力地追逐他，他会崩溃的。
I wish they wouldn't hound me so much. = 我希望他们不要这么纠缠我。


被动语态:
He was hounded by the press. = 他被媒体追逐。
She was hounded out of town. = 她被赶出了城镇。


疑问句:
Are they hounding him again? = 他们又在追逐他了吗？
Was he hounded by his creditors? = 他被债权人纠缠了吗？


否定句:
They did not hound him. = 他们没有追逐他。
The police did not hound the innocent man. = 警方没有追捕无辜的人。


PHRASE->
be hounded by = 被…纠缠
hound someone into = 逼迫某人做…
hound someone out = 把某人赶走
hound someone down = 追捕某人
hound after = 追赶


词源:
源自古英语 *hund, 指的是猎犬。  逐渐演变为指代用猎犬追捕，再引申到比喻意义上的追捕、纠缠。
