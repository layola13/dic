erysipelatoid:/ˌɛrɪsɪˈpɛlətɔɪd/| adj.| 猩红热样的，似丹毒的 | Resembling erysipelas; characterized by a red, inflamed, and often swollen appearance similar to that seen in erysipelas.  The term is often used to describe skin conditions that mimic the appearance of erysipelas, but may not be caused by the same streptococcal infection. |例句：The rash presented with an erysipelatoid appearance, prompting further investigation.  疹子呈现出丹毒样的外观，促使进一步调查。

The patient's leg exhibited an erysipelatoid lesion, characterized by its redness, swelling, and sharp demarcation.  病人的腿上出现了一个丹毒样的病灶，其特征是发红、肿胀和明显的界限。


近义词：erythematous, inflamed, rubescent | 反义词：pale, uninflamed

词源:
The word "erysipelatoid" is derived from "erysipelas" + "-oid".

* **Erysipelas:**  From the Late Latin *erysipelas*, from Greek *ἐρυσίπελας* (*erysipēlas*), literally "red skin," from *ἐρυθρός* (*erythrós*) "red" + *πέλας* (*pélas*) "skin, surface."

* **-oid:**  A suffix meaning "resembling," "like," or "having the form of."  It is derived from the Greek *-ειδής* (*-eidēs*).


Therefore, "erysipelatoid" means "resembling erysipelas" or "like erysipelas" in appearance.
