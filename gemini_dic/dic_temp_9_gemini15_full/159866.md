polymerised:/ˈpɒlɪməraɪzd/ /ˈpɑːlɪməraɪzd/ | v. | 聚合的；聚合作用 | To combine (simple molecules) to form a polymer.  The process involves the joining together of many small molecules (monomers) to form a large chain-like or network structure. | Example: The monomers polymerised to form a long chain of polyethylene.  单体聚合形成一条很长的聚乙烯链。| 近义词: polymerize | 反义词: depolymerize
