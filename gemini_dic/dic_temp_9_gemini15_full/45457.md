curating:/kjʊˈreɪtɪŋ/| v.|策展；挑选；整理|The act of selecting, organizing, and presenting objects or works of art in a museum or exhibition, or in a more general sense, carefully choosing and presenting a collection of things.|例句：The museum is curating a new exhibition of modern art. = 博物馆正在策划一个现代艺术的新展览。
例句：She spent months curating her Instagram feed to reflect her personal brand. = 她花了几个月的时间来策划她的Instagram动态，以反映她的个人品牌。|近义词：selecting, organizing, arranging, assembling|反义词：neglecting, ignoring, discarding


USAGE->

The gallery curator curates the upcoming exhibition. = 画廊馆长策划即将举行的展览。
