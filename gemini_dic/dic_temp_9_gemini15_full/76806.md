The chef expertly prepared the gammoning for the holiday feast.  厨师熟练地准备了节日盛宴的腌制猪肉。


例句：His gammoning about his supposed wealth was easily seen through. 他吹嘘自己所谓的财富很容易就被识破了。


He was full of gammoning about his accomplishments. 他夸夸其谈自己的成就。
