filiality:/ˌfɪliˈæləti/| n. |孝顺；孝敬；子女对父母的孝顺|The quality or feeling of filial piety; devotion to parents.|Her unwavering filiality was admired by all.  她的孝顺赢得了所有人的赞赏。|近义词：piety, devotion, reverence|反义词：disrespect, disobedience|名词

词源:

From Latin *filiālis (“of or pertaining to a son or daughter”), from filius (“son”) + -ālis (“-al”).  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *wi- (“man, male person”).
