butternuts: /ˈbʌtərˌnʌts/ | n. | 核桃，胡桃 |  A type of squash with a hard shell and sweet, edible flesh.  It is also sometimes used to refer to the edible nuts of the butternut tree (Juglans cinerea), although this is less common. | Example:  We roasted the butternuts for a delicious side dish. 我们烤了核桃南瓜作为美味的配菜。  |  近义词：squash, winter squash | 反义词：None specifically.
