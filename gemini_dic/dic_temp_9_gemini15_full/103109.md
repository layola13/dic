jerk:/dʒɜːrk/| n., v.| 笨蛋；急促地拉动；抽搐；猛地一推 | A sudden, sharp movement; a stupid or unpleasant person; to move or pull something suddenly and roughly. |例句：He gave the rope a jerk. 他猛地拉了一下绳子。He's such a jerk! 他真是个笨蛋！|近义词：idiot, fool, lout, spasm, tug|反义词：genius, saint, smoothly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jerks the door open. = 他猛地拉开了门。
The engine jerks violently. = 发动机剧烈地抖动。

一般过去时:
She jerked her head up. = 她猛地抬起了头。
He jerked the lever. = 他猛地拉动操纵杆。

现在完成时:
I have jerked my back. = 我闪到腰了。(此处jerk指突然的肌肉抽动)
He has jerked the handle repeatedly. = 他反复猛拉把手。

过去完成时:
He had jerked the steering wheel before the crash. = 事故发生前他猛打方向盘。
She had jerked her hand away from the hot stove. = 她迅速把手从热炉子上缩了回来。

将来时:
He will jerk the line to catch the fish. = 他将猛地一拉鱼线来钓到鱼。
The car will jerk when it changes gears. = 汽车换挡时会抖动。

虚拟语气:
If he jerked the rope harder, it might break. = 如果他更用力地拉绳子，它可能会断。
I wish I hadn't jerked my knee so badly. = 我真希望我没有把膝盖弄得那么严重。(此处jerk指突然的肌肉拉伤)

被动语态:
The rope was jerked violently. = 绳子被猛地拉了一下。
The car was jerked to a stop. = 汽车猛地停了下来。

疑问句:
Did he jerk the door open? = 他是不是猛地拉开了门？
Why did the car jerk? = 为什么汽车会抖动？

否定句:
He didn't jerk the handle. = 他没有拉把手。
The car didn't jerk at all. = 汽车根本没有抖动。


PHRASE->
jerk around: 愚弄，戏弄
jerk off: 自慰
jerk someone around: 随意对待某人
jerk reaction: 反射动作，下意识反应
a real jerk:  一个真正的笨蛋
