mechanics: /məˈkænɪks/ | n. |力学；机械学；技巧；机制 | The branch of physics concerned with the motion and equilibrium of bodies and the action of forces.  It also refers to the skill in carrying out a task. |例句：He has a good understanding of mechanics. 他对力学有很好的理解。
例句：The mechanics of the engine are quite complex. 这台发动机的机械结构相当复杂。

近义词：physics (in the context of physical mechanics), technique, skill, system

反义词：无

词源:
From Middle French *mécanique, from Late Latin *mechanica, from Greek μηχανική (mēkhanikḗ), from μηχανικός (mēkhanikós), "of or pertaining to machines," from μῆχος (mêkhos), "contrivance, machine."  The meaning evolved from the study of machines to the broader principles of motion and forces.
