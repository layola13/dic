glow:/ɡloʊ/| n., v.|发光，光辉，兴旺|A soft, warm light; to shine with a soft light; to be filled with warmth or happiness.|例句：The fire cast a warm glow on the room.  火光给房间投下温暖的光芒。|例句：Her face glowed with happiness. 她的脸上洋溢着幸福的光芒。|近义词：shine, radiance, brightness|反义词：darkness, dullness|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The embers glow softly. = 余烬轻轻地发光。
The city glows at night. = 城市在夜晚闪耀。

一般过去时:
The candle glowed brightly. = 蜡烛明亮地燃烧着。
His cheeks glowed with exertion. = 他因用力而脸颊通红。

现在完成时:

She has glowed with pride since her son's success. = 自从儿子成功以来，她一直为之骄傲。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
