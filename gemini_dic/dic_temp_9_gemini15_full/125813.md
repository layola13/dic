murmurer:/ˈmɜːrmərər/| n. |低声说话者，喃喃自语者 |A murmurer is someone who speaks in a low, indistinct voice, often muttering to themselves.  They may be expressing discontent, secrets, or simply thinking aloud. | Example: The old man sat alone, a constant murmurer of unintelligible words.  这位老人独自坐着，不停地喃喃自语着一些听不懂的话。|近义词：mumbler, whisperer |反义词：shouter, bellower


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He murmurs complaints under his breath. = 他低声抱怨。
She murmurs sweet nothings in his ear. = 她在他耳边轻声细语甜言蜜语。

一般过去时:
He murmured his agreement. = 他低声表示同意。
