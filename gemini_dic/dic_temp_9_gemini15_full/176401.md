re-handles:/ˌriːˈhændəlz/| v. |重新处理，重新操控|To handle something again, often after a problem or failure.  It implies a corrective or improved approach.|例句：The software developers re-handled the code after discovering a critical bug. = 软件开发者在发现一个严重错误后重新处理了代码。


例句：The airline re-handled the baggage after it was mishandled during the initial transfer. = 航空公司在行李在最初的转运过程中被错误处理后重新处理了它。


近义词：reprocess, rework, re-evaluate, revise


反义词：ignore, neglect


词性：动词
