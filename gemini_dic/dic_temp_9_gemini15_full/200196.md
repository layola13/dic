skink:/skɪŋk/| n.|石龙子；蜥蜴；一种汤|A skink is a type of lizard.  They are often slender and have smooth scales.  Many species are found worldwide.|例句：The garden skink darted under the rock. 花园里的石龙子窜到石头底下去了。|例句：We had skink soup for dinner. 晚饭我们吃了石龙子汤。|近义词：lizard, reptile|反义词：无
