froggy:/ˈfrɒɡi/| adj.|青蛙似的；潮湿的；阴冷的| resembling a frog; damp and cold; characterized by dampness and cold.|例句：The froggy pond was covered in lily pads.  青蛙池塘里长满了睡莲。|例句：The basement had a froggy smell. 地下室里有一股潮湿的气味。|近义词：damp, moist, chilly, dampish|反义词：dry, arid, warm, sunny
