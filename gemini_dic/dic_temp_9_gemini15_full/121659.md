mispictured: /ˌmɪsˈpɪktʃərd/ | adj. | 歪曲的，被误绘的 |  Describes something that has been portrayed inaccurately or falsely, often in a way that distorts the truth or reality.  It implies a deliberate or unintentional misrepresentation. |

例句：The article mispictured the senator's stance on the issue, leading to public confusion.  这篇文章歪曲了参议员在这个问题上的立场，导致公众困惑。

The painting mispictured the landscape, making the mountains appear much taller than they actually are. 这幅画歪曲了风景，使山看起来比实际高得多。


近义词：misrepresented, distorted, falsified, wrongly depicted, inaccurately portrayed

反义词：accurately depicted, truthfully represented, faithfully portrayed


词性：形容词
