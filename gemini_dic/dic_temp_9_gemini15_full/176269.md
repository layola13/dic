re-covered:/ˌriːˈkʌvərd/| v. |重新覆盖；恢复；找回 |To cover something again; to regain something lost or damaged; to recover from illness or injury.|例句：The roof was re-covered with new tiles. 屋顶重新铺上了新瓦片。|例句：She has re-covered from her illness. 她已从疾病中恢复过来。|近义词：re-roofed, restored, regained |反义词：uncovered, lost, damaged
