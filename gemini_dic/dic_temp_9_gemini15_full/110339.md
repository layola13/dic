lighthearted:/ˈlaɪtˈhɑːtɪd/| adj. |轻松愉快的，无忧无虑的 |Lighthearted describes something or someone that is cheerful and carefree, lacking seriousness.  It implies a playful and happy disposition. |例句：The party had a lighthearted atmosphere.  派对的气氛轻松愉快。

例句：She told a lighthearted joke to break the tension. 她讲了一个轻松愉快的笑话来缓解紧张气氛。


近义词：carefree, cheerful, jovial, merry, playful, happy-go-lucky

反义词：serious, solemn, grave, somber, gloomy


词源:  The word "lighthearted" is a compound word.  "Light" here means "not heavy," referring to a lack of seriousness or worry. "Hearted" refers to the heart, the center of emotions.  Therefore, "lighthearted" literally means having a heart that is not burdened by serious concerns.
