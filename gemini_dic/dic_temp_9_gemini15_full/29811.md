caught:/kɔːt/| v.|抓住；感染；被困住|The past tense and past participle of "catch".  It implies the successful apprehension or capture of something or someone. It can also refer to being unexpectedly affected by something, like an illness. |例句：The police caught the thief red-handed. 警察当场抓住小偷。

The dog caught the ball. 狗接住了球。


近义词：seized, apprehended, trapped, infected|反义词：missed, escaped, released|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
I catch the bus every morning. = 我每天早上都乘公交车。
He catches butterflies in the park. = 他在公园里捉蝴蝶。

一般过去时:
I caught a cold last week. = 我上周感冒了。
She caught the thief stealing her purse. = 她抓到小偷正在偷她的钱包。

现在完成时:
I have caught three fish today. = 我今天钓到三条鱼了。
She has caught the flu. = 她得了流感。

过去完成时:
I had caught the train before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前我已经赶上了火车。
They had caught the burglar before he could escape. = 他们在窃贼逃跑之前就抓住了他。

将来时:

We will catch you later. = 我们稍后再联系你。

虚拟语气:
If I had caught the bus, I wouldn't be late. = 如果我赶上了公交车，我就不会迟到了。
I wish I had caught that falling star. = 我真希望我抓住了那颗流星。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
