convenors:/kənˈviːnərz/| n.|召集人，组织者|People who convene or organize a meeting or event.  They are responsible for arranging and managing the event, often including inviting attendees, setting the agenda, and facilitating discussions.|例句：The convenors of the conference sent out invitations to all the registered participants.  会议召集人向所有注册参与者发出了邀请函。

The convenors ensured that the meeting ran smoothly and efficiently.  召集人确保会议顺利有效地进行。|近义词：organizers, coordinators, chairmen, chairpersons|反义词：attendees, participants
