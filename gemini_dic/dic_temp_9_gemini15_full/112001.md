loners:/ˈloʊnərz/| n.|独行者，孤独者|A loner is a person who prefers to spend time alone and avoids social interaction.  They often enjoy solitude and may find social situations draining or uncomfortable.|例句：He's always been a loner, preferring his own company to crowds. 他一直是个独行者，更喜欢独处而不是人群。|例句：Many loners find fulfillment in their solitary pursuits. 许多独行者在孤独的追求中找到了满足感。|近义词：recluse, hermit, introvert|反义词：extrovert, social butterfly
