oversoak:/ˌoʊvərˈsoʊk/ | vt. | 浸泡过度；过度湿透 | To soak something for too long, resulting in it becoming excessively wet or damaged. | 例句：Don't oversoak the beans, or they'll become mushy.  别把豆子泡太久，否则会烂掉。

例句：The heavy rain oversoaked the ground, leading to flooding. 大雨将地面浸透，导致洪水泛滥。


近义词：drench, saturate, waterlog

反义词：dehydrate, dry out

词性:动词
