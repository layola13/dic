graphemically:/ɡræˈfɛmɪkli/ | adv. |  图形地；从书写形式上 | Adverb describing something related to the writing system or visual representation of language. It focuses on the graphemes (letters, symbols) themselves rather than their pronunciation or meaning.  | Example:  The word "though" is graphemically similar to "through", but they have different pronunciations.  这个词“though”在书写形式上与“through”相似，但它们的读音不同。| 近义词： orthographically, visually | 反义词：phonetically, semantically | 副词
