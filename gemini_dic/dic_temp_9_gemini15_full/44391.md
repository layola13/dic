cross-fertilize:/ˌkrɒsˈfɜːrtɪlaɪz/| vt.|异花授粉；互相借鉴；相互影响|To fertilize (a plant) with pollen from a different variety or species.  To exchange ideas or methods between different groups or fields. |例句：The researchers cross-fertilized their ideas to create a more comprehensive theory. 研究人员互相交流思想，创建了一个更全面的理论。


例句：The farmer cross-fertilized his apple trees to improve the fruit's quality.  这位农民给他的苹果树进行了异花授粉以提高果实的品质。


近义词：pollinate, hybridize, interbreed, influence, inspire


反义词：self-fertilize, isolate
