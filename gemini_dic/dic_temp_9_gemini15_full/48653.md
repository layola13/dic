defenser: /dɪˈfɛndər/ | n. | 防御者；辩护者；卫冕者 |

英文解析: A defender is someone who defends someone or something. This could be a person who defends someone in a legal case, a player who defends their team's goal in sports, or someone who protects something from harm.  The term can also refer to something that provides protection, like a defensive system or a type of armor.

例句：

The lawyer acted as the defendant's defender in court.  律师在法庭上担任被告的辩护人。

The team's strong defender prevented the opposing team from scoring. 球队的强力后卫阻止了对方球队得分。


近义词：protector, advocate, champion, guardian

反义词：attacker, assailant, aggressor


词性：名词


词源:  The word "defender" comes from the Old French word "defendour," which itself derives from the verb "defendre," meaning "to defend."  The Latin root is "defendere," which combines "de-" (away, off) and "fendere" (to strike, cleave).  The original sense implied warding off a blow or attack.
