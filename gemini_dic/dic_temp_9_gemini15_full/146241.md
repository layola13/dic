overexpressively:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛsɪvli/ | adv. | 过度表达地，夸张地 |  Describes an action or manner of expression that is excessively demonstrative or emphatic. It suggests an overabundance of feeling or emphasis in the way something is conveyed. | Example: He overexpressively gestured towards the door, emphasizing his impatience. 他夸张地向门口示意，强调了他的不耐烦。|近义词：excessively, dramatically, theatrically | 反义词：understatedly, subtly, mildly
