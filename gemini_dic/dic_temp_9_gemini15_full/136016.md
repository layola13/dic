nonreactors:/ˌnɒnriˈæktərz/| n. |非反应堆；不反应者|Those materials that do not undergo fission or sustain a chain reaction in a nuclear reactor are called nonreactors.  在核反应堆中不会发生裂变或维持链式反应的材料被称为非反应堆材料。|近义词：inert materials, non-fissionable materials|反义词：reactors
