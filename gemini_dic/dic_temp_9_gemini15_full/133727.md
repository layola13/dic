nonfugitive:/ˌnɒnˈfjuːdʒɪtɪv/| adj.|非逃犯的，不逃亡的|Not tending to flee or escape; not fugitive.  The term often applies to individuals or assets that are not likely to disappear or be hidden from legal authorities.|例句：The nonfugitive assets were easily seized by the authorities.  非逃亡资产很容易被当局查封。 The court determined that he was a nonfugitive defendant and released him on bail. 法院认定他是非逃犯被告，并将其保释释放。|近义词：settled, established, non-absconding|反义词：fugitive, absconding, runaway
