It often involves reviewing the original audit's procedures and findings, and potentially gathering additional evidence. | The company underwent a reaudit after inconsistencies were discovered in their financial statements. = 在财务报表中发现不一致之处后，该公司进行了重新审计。  The external auditor requested a reaudit of the internal controls. = 外部审计员要求对内部控制进行重新审计。 | second audit, review audit, verification audit | initial audit |  n.
