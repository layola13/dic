gasteropoda:/ˌɡæstrɒˈpɒdə/|/n.|腹足类动物|Gastropoda is a large taxonomic class within the phylum Mollusca, encompassing snails and slugs.  They are characterized by a single, usually spiraled shell (in most species) and a muscular foot used for locomotion.|Example:  The garden snail is a common example of a gastropod. 花园蜗牛是腹足类动物的常见例子。  Many gastropods are found in the intertidal zone. 许多腹足类动物都生活在潮间带。|近义词:mollusk, shellfish|反义词:None specifically.  Could consider "cephalopod" (e.g., octopus, squid) as a contrasting group within mollusks.
