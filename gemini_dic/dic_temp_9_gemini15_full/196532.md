servos:/ˈsɜːrvoʊz/ | n. |伺服机构；伺服系统 |  A servo, or servo mechanism, is a system for maintaining a desired position, velocity, or acceleration.  It uses feedback to control its action.  Servos are used in many applications where precise control is required, such as robotics, industrial automation, and model airplanes. |例句：The robot arm's precise movements are controlled by a sophisticated servo system.  = 机器臂的精确运动由复杂的伺服系统控制。

The drone uses servos to control its flight. = 无人机使用伺服系统控制它的飞行。|近义词：actuator, controller |反义词：manual control
