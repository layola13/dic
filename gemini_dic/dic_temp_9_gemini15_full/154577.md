persecuted:/ˈpɜːrsɪkjuːtɪd/| v. |迫害，使受苦 |To subject to hostility and ill-treatment, usually based on religion, race, or political beliefs.  |The religious minority were persecuted for their beliefs.  宗教少数派因其信仰而受迫害。|oppressed, harassed, victimized |protected, aided, supported |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They persecute religious minorities. = 他们迫害宗教少数群体。
He persecutes his employees relentlessly. = 他无情地迫害他的员工。


一般过去时:
The government persecuted dissidents during the war. = 战争期间，政府迫害异议人士。
They persecuted her for her political views. = 他们因为她的政治观点而迫害她。


现在完成时:
They have persecuted this group for many years. = 他们多年来一直迫害这个群体。
She has been persecuted for speaking out against injustice. = 她因为公开反对不公正而受到迫害。


过去完成时:
The community had been persecuted for generations. = 这个社群几代人都受到迫害。
Before the revolution, they had persecuted anyone who disagreed. = 在革命之前，他们迫害任何持不同意见的人。


将来时:
They will persecute anyone who opposes their rule. = 他们将迫害任何反对他们统治的人。
The regime will continue to persecute its opponents. = 该政权将继续迫害其反对者。


虚拟语气:
If they had not persecuted him, he might still be alive. = 如果他们没有迫害他，他可能还活着。
I wish they would stop persecuting innocent people. = 我希望他们能停止迫害无辜的人。


被动语态:
Many people were persecuted for their beliefs. = 许多人因为他们的信仰而受到迫害。
He was persecuted for his political activities. = 他因为政治活动而受到迫害。


疑问句:
Were they persecuted for their faith? = 他们因为信仰而受到迫害吗？
Why are they being persecuted? = 为什么他们受到迫害？


否定句:
They did not persecute him because of his race. = 他们并没有因为他的种族而迫害他。
The government has not persecuted anyone unjustly. = 政府没有不公正地迫害任何人。
