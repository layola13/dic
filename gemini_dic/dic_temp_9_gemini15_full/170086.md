prothallium:/proʊˈθæliəm/| n. |原叶体，配子体|The prothallium is the small, flat, green, gametophyte generation of a fern or other pteridophyte.  It develops from a spore and produces the sex organs (antheridia and archegonia).|Example: The prothallium of a fern is usually heart-shaped and only a few millimeters in size.|近义词：gametophyte|反义词：sporophyte
