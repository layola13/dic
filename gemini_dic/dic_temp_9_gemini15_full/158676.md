plonker:/ˈplɒŋkə(r)/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜 | A plonker is a British slang term for a foolish or incompetent person.  It's generally considered mildly offensive, similar to "idiot" or "fool," but less harsh. |例句：He's such a plonker; he forgot his own birthday. 他真是个笨蛋，竟然忘了自己的生日。

例句：Don't be such a plonker, read the instructions!  别那么傻，看看说明书！


近义词：idiot, fool, idiot, dope, twit, buffoon

反义词：genius, mastermind, intellect


词性：名词
