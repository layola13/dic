scammed:/skæmd/| v. |被骗，受骗|To have been tricked or defrauded.  The victim of a scam has been deceived into giving money or something else of value to a perpetrator.|例句：I was scammed out of $1000 by an online seller.  我被一个网上的卖家骗走了1000美元。
I feel scammed because the product I received was not as advertised. 我感觉被骗了，因为我收到的产品和广告宣传的不一样。|近义词：cheated, defrauded, conned, swindled|反义词：honestly gained, fairly obtained|词性:动词
