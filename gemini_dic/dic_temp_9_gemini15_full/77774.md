general:/ˈdʒenərəl/| adj. n.|一般的；普遍的；将军|relating to or affecting the whole; general in nature.  A general statement or principle applies broadly, not to specific cases.  As a noun, it refers to a high-ranking military officer.|例句：The general opinion is that the plan is flawed.  普遍的观点是这个计划有缺陷。

The general manager announced the company's new strategy. 总经理宣布了公司的新战略。|近义词：common, usual, overall, widespread, average |反义词：specific, particular, detailed, individual, special|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a general understanding of the topic. 他对这个话题有一个大致的了解。
General knowledge is important for everyone. 常识对每个人都很重要。

一般过去时:

The general feeling was one of disappointment.  普遍的感觉是失望。


现在完成时:
She has gained a general understanding of the subject. 她已经对这个主题有了一定的了解。
We have covered the general principles in this chapter. 我们在本章中已经介绍了基本原则。


过去完成时:
They had already discussed the general plan before the meeting. 他们在会议之前已经讨论了总体计划。
I had a general idea of what was going on. 我对当时发生的事情有一个大致的了解。


将来时:
The company will implement a general restructuring next year. 公司将在明年实施全面重组。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
