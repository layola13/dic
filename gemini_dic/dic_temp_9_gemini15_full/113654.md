machinery:/məˈʃiːnəri/ | n. | 机械；机器；机械装置 | The machinery in the factory is very old. 工厂里的机器很旧。|近义词：equipment, apparatus, mechanism |反义词：manpower, handwork


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machinery runs smoothly. = 机器运转顺利。
This machinery requires regular maintenance. = 这台机器需要定期维护。


一般过去时:
The machinery broke down yesterday. = 机器昨天坏了。
They repaired the old machinery last week. = 他们上周修理了旧机器。


现在完成时:
The company has invested heavily in new machinery. = 公司已大量投资新机器。
They have modernized their machinery recently. = 他们最近现代化了他们的机器。


过去完成时:
The machinery had already stopped before the power cut. = 断电前机器已经停止运转了。
They had overhauled the machinery before starting production. = 他们在开始生产之前已经检修了机器。


将来时:
They will install new machinery next month. = 他们下个月将安装新机器。
The factory will need more machinery to increase production. = 工厂将需要更多机器来增加产量。


虚拟语气:
If we had better machinery, we could produce more. = 如果我们有更好的机器，我们就能生产更多。
I wish we had more modern machinery. = 我希望我们有更多现代化的机器。


被动语态:
The machinery is regularly inspected. = 机器定期检查。
The old machinery was replaced last year. = 旧机器去年被更换了。


疑问句:
What kind of machinery do you need? = 你需要什么样的机器？
Is this machinery efficient enough? = 这台机器效率够高吗？


否定句:
