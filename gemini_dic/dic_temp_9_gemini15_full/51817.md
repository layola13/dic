dewdrops:/ˈduːdrɒps/ | n. |露珠 |Tiny drops of water that form on cool surfaces at night, especially on plants, from condensation of atmospheric water vapor.| 例句：The dewdrops sparkled on the spiderweb.  露珠在蜘蛛网上闪闪发光。

The grass was covered in glistening dewdrops.  草地上覆盖着闪闪发光的露珠。


近义词：water droplets, condensation


反义词：无


词性：名词
