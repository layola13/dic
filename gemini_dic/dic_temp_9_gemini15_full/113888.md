madrasi:/mæˈdrɑːsi/| n. |马德拉斯人；马德拉斯纱布|A person from Madras (now Chennai), India.  The term is now considered outdated and potentially offensive due to its colonial origins and association with stereotypes.  It can also refer to a type of lightweight cotton fabric originating from Madras.|例句：He claimed to be a Madrasi, though his accent suggested otherwise. 他声称自己是马德拉斯人，尽管他的口音表明并非如此。|近义词：chennaite (preferred term), South Indian|反义词：无
