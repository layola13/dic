invitations:/ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |邀请函；邀请 |  The plural form of "invitation," referring to multiple instances of inviting someone to an event.  It implies a formal or semi-formal request. | Example:  We sent out 100 invitations to the wedding. 我们寄出了100张婚礼请柬。  | 近义词：requests, summonses | 反义词：rejections, refusals | 名词


USAGE->

The company sends out many invitations to its events. = 公司向其活动发出许多邀请函。
She sends invitations to all her friends. = 她向所有朋友发出邀请。
