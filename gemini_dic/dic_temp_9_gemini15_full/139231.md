oblation:/ɒˈbleɪʃən/| n. |祭品；供奉；奉献 |An offering, especially one made to a deity.  The act of offering something as a religious sacrifice. |例句：The priest received the oblation of bread and wine.  祭司接受了面包和葡萄酒的祭品。

例句：The villagers made an oblation to appease the gods. 村民们献祭以平息众神。


近义词：offering, sacrifice, tribute, donation |反义词：taking, seizure, plunder


词源:  From Latin *oblatio, from offerre "to offer".  The word reflects a long history of religious and ceremonial offerings.
