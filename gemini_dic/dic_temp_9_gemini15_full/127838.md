nemertid:/ˈnɛmərtɪd/| n. |纽形动物|A nemertid is a worm belonging to the phylum Nemertea, also known as ribbon worms.  They are characterized by a long, ribbon-like body, a complete digestive system, and a proboscis used for capturing prey.
