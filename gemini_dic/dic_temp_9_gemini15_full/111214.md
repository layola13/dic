lithophyte:/ˈlɪθəfaɪt/| n. |石生植物|A plant that grows on rocks or stones.  Lithophytes are often found in harsh environments where soil is scarce. They typically have specialized adaptations for obtaining water and nutrients from the rock surface.  |例句：Many lichens are lithophytes, thriving on exposed rock faces. 许多地衣是石生植物，在裸露的岩石表面繁盛生长。

Many alpine plants are lithophytes, adapted to the cold and rocky terrain. 许多高山植物是石生植物，适应寒冷多岩石的地形。|近义词：rock plant, epilithic plant|反义词：soil plant
