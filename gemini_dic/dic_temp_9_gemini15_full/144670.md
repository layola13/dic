outwriting:/ˌaʊtˈraɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 超越写作；写作能力的超越 | The act of surpassing someone else in writing, often in terms of skill, speed, or quantity.  It can also refer to a piece of writing that significantly surpasses others in quality. | 例句：Her outwriting of her peers was undeniable.  她的写作能力远超同龄人，这是不可否认的。


例句：The novel demonstrated an outwriting of contemporary fiction. 这部小说展现了对当代小说的超越。


近义词：surpassing, excelling, outperforming


反义词：underwriting, inferior writing


词性：名词
