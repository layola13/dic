haemogram:/ˈhiːməɡræm/| n. |血常规检查报告；血象|A blood test that measures the different components of blood, including red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets.  It provides information about a person's overall health and can help diagnose various medical conditions.|例句：The haemogram showed a low red blood cell count, indicating anemia.  血常规检查显示红细胞数量低，表明患有贫血。

例句：Her doctor ordered a haemogram to investigate her fatigue.她的医生为了调查她的疲劳情况，给她开了血常规检查。


近义词：complete blood count (CBC), blood profile, blood test|反义词：无|词性：名词

词源:  The word "haemogram" is a combination of "haemo-" (from Greek "haima," meaning blood) and "-gram" (from Greek "gramma," meaning something written or drawn).  Therefore, it literally means "a written or drawn record of blood."
