closed-captioned:/ˌkloʊzdˈkæpʃənd/ | adj. | 配有隐藏字幕的 | Having subtitles that can be displayed on a screen for viewers who are deaf or hard of hearing, or who prefer to read along.  These subtitles are usually activated by the viewer through a device setting. | Example: The movie was closed-captioned, making it accessible to a wider audience.  这部电影配有隐藏字幕，使更多观众能够观看。 |近义词：subtitled (if the subtitles are visible by default) | 反义词：open-captioned (subtitles are always visible)
