europa:/juˈrɔːpə/| n. |欧洲；木星的卫星|Europa is the smallest of the four Galilean moons orbiting Jupiter.  Europa is one of Jupiter's four largest moons. |近义词：Europe (continent)|反义词:无


英文解析:
Europa can refer to two distinct things:

1. **The continent of Europe:**  This is the most common usage, referring to the large landmass in the Northern Hemisphere.  It's a proper noun, always capitalized.

2. **One of Jupiter's moons:** This is a proper noun, also always capitalized, referring to a Galilean moon known for its smooth, icy surface and potential for subsurface oceans.  Scientific studies suggest that Europa may harbor life.
