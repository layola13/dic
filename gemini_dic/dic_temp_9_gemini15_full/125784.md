muriate:/ˈmjʊəriˌeɪt/| n. |盐酸盐；氯化物|A muriate is a salt of muriatic acid (hydrochloric acid).  It refers to a compound formed by the combination of a metal or other cation with the chloride anion (Cl−).|Example: Sodium chloride (NaCl) is a common muriate. 氯化钠（NaCl）是一种常见的盐酸盐。|近义词：chloride, salt|反义词：无
