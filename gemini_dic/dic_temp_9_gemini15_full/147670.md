overpost:/ˈoʊvərpoʊst/| vt.|过度发布，发布过多|To post (something) excessively or too frequently, often to the point of annoyance or being overwhelming.  This usually refers to online platforms. |例句：He overposted on Instagram about his vacation, annoying his followers. 他在Instagram上过度发布关于他假期的内容，惹恼了他的粉丝。|近义词：spam, deluge, flood|反义词：underpost


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He overposts on social media. = 他在社交媒体上过度发帖。
She overposts pictures of her cat. = 她过度发布她猫的照片。

一般过去时:
They overposted about the concert last night. = 他们昨晚过度发布了关于音乐会的帖子。
I overposted my feelings on Facebook. = 我在Facebook上过度表达了我的感受。

现在完成时:
He has overposted so much this week. = 他这周过度发帖太多了。
She has overposted on every platform she uses. = 她在她使用的每个平台上都过度发帖了。

过去完成时:
I realized I had overposted before I saw the negative comments. = 我在看到负面评论之前才意识到我发帖太多了。
They had overposted and then deleted most of their posts. = 他们过度发帖后又删除了大部分帖子。

将来时:
I will not overpost on my new blog. = 我不会在我的新博客上过度发帖。
She will probably overpost again during the holidays. = 她假期期间可能会再次过度发帖。

虚拟语气:
If I knew it would cause such a reaction, I wouldn't have overposted. = 如果我知道这会引起这样的反应，我就不会过度发帖了。
I wish I hadn't overposted that embarrassing picture. = 我真希望我没有过度发布那张令人尴尬的照片。

被动语态:
His posts were overposted by a bot. = 他的帖子被机器人过度发布了。(较少用，因为通常是主动行为)
The information was overposted across multiple channels. = 信息在多个渠道上被过度发布了。(较少用，通常指信息本身被重复发布)


疑问句:
Did he overpost again? = 他又过度发帖了吗？
Are you worried about overposting? = 你担心过度发帖吗？

否定句:
I didn't overpost anything today. = 我今天没有过度发帖。
She doesn't usually overpost; this is unusual. = 她通常不会过度发帖；这很不寻常。
