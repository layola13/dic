dredge:/drɛdʒ/ | n., vt. |挖掘；疏浚；拖网捕捞；|to remove mud, weeds, etc. from the bottom of a river, lake, or canal; to search for something; to bring something up from the bottom of a body of water|例句：They are dredging the harbour to make it deeper. 他们正在疏浚港口以使其更深。They dredged the river for the lost body. 他们搜寻河底寻找失蹤的尸体。|近义词：excavate, dredge up, drag|反义词：fill, bury


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dredge the canal regularly. = 他们定期疏浚运河。
The ship dredges for shellfish. = 这艘船正在拖网捕捞贝类。


一般过去时:
They dredged the harbour last year. = 他们去年疏浚了港口。
The fishermen dredged the seabed for oysters. = 渔民们在海底拖网捕捞牡蛎。


现在完成时:
They have dredged the river several times this year. = 今年他们已经疏浚了这条河好几次了。
The archaeologists have dredged up some interesting artifacts. = 考古学家们挖掘出了一些有趣的文物。


过去完成时:
They had dredged the channel before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经疏浚了航道。
The police had dredged the lake for the missing car. = 警方已经在湖中搜寻失踪的汽车。


将来时:
They will dredge the port next month. = 他们下个月将疏浚港口。
The company will dredge the seabed for minerals. = 公司将疏浚海底寻找矿物。


虚拟语气:
If they dredged the river, they might find the lost treasure. = 如果他们疏浚这条河，他们可能会找到丢失的宝藏。
I wish they had dredged the lake sooner. = 我希望他们早点疏浚湖泊。


被动语态:
The harbour is dredged annually. = 港口每年都会被疏浚。
The riverbed was dredged to remove the debris. = 河床被疏浚以清除碎石。


疑问句:
Will they dredge the canal this year? = 他们今年会疏浚运河吗？
Have they dredged the lake for the missing boat? = 他们是否已经搜寻湖泊寻找失踪的船只？


否定句:
They did not dredge the river last week. = 他们上周没有疏浚这条河。
The company will not dredge the area without a permit. = 没有许可证，公司不会疏浚该区域。
