competentness:/ˈkɒmpɪtənts/ /ˈkɑːmpɪtənts/| n. |能力；胜任；称职|The ability to do something successfully or efficiently.  It refers to the possession of the necessary skills, knowledge, and experience to perform a task or job effectively. |例句：Her competentness in handling complex projects is well-known.  她的处理复杂项目的能力是出了名的。

例句：The company values competentness above all else in its employees. 公司最看重员工的能力。


近义词：capability, ability, proficiency, expertise |反义词：incompetence, incapability, inadequacy


词性：名词
