refit:/ˌriːˈfɪt/| vt. n.|改装；修理；重新装备|To repair and improve something, especially a ship or aircraft, by replacing parts or adding new equipment.|例句：The ship was refitted with new engines. 这艘船重新安装了新发动机。|例句：They gave the old house a complete refit. 他们对那栋旧房子进行了全面翻修。|近义词：renovate, overhaul, repair|反义词：demolish, destroy|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They refit the engine every year. = 他们每年都改装发动机。
He refits old cars for a living. = 他靠改装旧汽车为生。

一般过去时:
The mechanic refitted the car's brakes. = 技工修理了汽车的刹车。
We refitted the kitchen with new appliances. = 我们用新的家用电器重新装修了厨房。

现在完成时:
They have refitted the entire ship. = 他们已经改装了整艘船。
She has refitted her wardrobe with new clothes. = 她用新衣服重新整理了她的衣橱。

过去完成时:
The plane had been refitted before the flight. = 飞机在飞行前已经过改装。
They had refitted the laboratory with new equipment. = 他们已经用新设备重新装修了实验室。

将来时:
We will refit the bathroom next month. = 我们下个月将重新装修浴室。
The company will refit the factory with new machinery. = 公司将用新机器重新装备工厂。

虚拟语气:
If they refitted the engine, the car would run better. = 如果他们改装发动机，汽车就会跑得更好。
I wish they had refitted the kitchen before we moved in. = 我希望他们在我们搬进来之前就重新装修了厨房。

被动语态:
The engine was refitted by a skilled mechanic. = 发动机是由一位熟练的技工改装的。
The old building is being refitted for a new purpose. = 这座旧建筑正在被改装以用于新的用途。

疑问句:
Will they refit the boat before the race? = 他们会在比赛前改装船吗？
Have they refitted the computer system yet? = 他们已经改装了电脑系统了吗？

否定句:
They did not refit the aircraft on time. = 他们没有按时改装飞机。
We will not refit the office until next year. = 我们直到明年才会重新装修办公室。
