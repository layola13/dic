labrum:/ˈleɪbrəm/| n.|唇，(昆虫的)上唇；(解剖学)唇状结构|A labrum is an anatomical structure resembling a lip.  In insects, it is the upper lip, part of the mouthparts. In other animals, it can refer to various lip-like structures. For example, in the human knee, the labrum is a ring of cartilage that helps stabilize the joint.|例句：The insect's labrum was damaged. 这只昆虫的上唇受损了。|例句：The surgeon repaired the torn labrum in his shoulder. 外科医生修复了他肩部撕裂的唇状软骨。|近义词：lip (in some contexts), rim |反义词：None directly applicable.
