half-bleached:/ˌhæfˈbliːtʃt/| adj. | 半漂白的 | Partially bleached; having undergone a bleaching process but not completely.  The term suggests a less intense or thorough bleaching than "fully bleached" or "completely bleached".  It implies a faded or partially lightened appearance. | Example: The half-bleached denim jacket had a unique, vintage look.  半漂白的牛仔外套有一种独特的复古外观。 The half-bleached hair showed streaks of blonde and brown. 半漂白的头发呈现出金色和棕色的条纹。 | 近义词: partially bleached, lightly bleached, faded | 反义词: fully bleached, unbleached
