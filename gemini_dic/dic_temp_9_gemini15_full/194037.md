self-fruitions:/self-fruːˈʃənz/ | n. |自我实现，自我满足 |The act of achieving self-fulfillment or realizing one's full potential; the state of experiencing the positive results of one's own efforts.  It emphasizes the internal satisfaction derived from personal accomplishments rather than external validation. |例句：Her paintings were a source of self-fruition, bringing her immense personal satisfaction.  她的绘画作品是她自我实现的源泉，带给她巨大的个人满足感。

The culmination of years of hard work brought about a feeling of profound self-fruition.  多年的辛勤工作最终带来了深刻的自我实现感。|近义词：self-actualization, self-realization, fulfillment, satisfaction |反义词：frustration, disappointment, failure |名词
