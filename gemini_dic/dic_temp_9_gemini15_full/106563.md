krunch:/krʌntʃ/| v. n.|嘎吱嘎吱地响，用力咀嚼|To make a crushing or crunching sound; to chew something noisily and forcefully.|例句：The leaves crunched underfoot. 叶子在我的脚下嘎吱作响。
例句：He crunched the apple noisily. 他大声地咀嚼着苹果。|近义词：crush, crunch, munch, chew|反义词：whisper, murmur.
