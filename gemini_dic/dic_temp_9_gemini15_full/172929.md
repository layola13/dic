qualification:/ˌkwɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |资格，资历；合格；限定；条件|A qualification is something such as a skill, examination result, or experience that makes someone suitable for a particular job or course of study. It can also refer to a condition or limitation. |例句：He lacks the necessary qualifications for the job. 他缺乏这份工作所需的资格。

例句：Her qualifications include a degree in engineering and five years' experience.  她的资历包括一个工程学位和五年的工作经验。


近义词：credential, attainment, eligibility, requirement |反义词：disqualification, ineligibility


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What qualifications do you have? = 你有什么资格？
What qualifications are required for this position? = 这个职位需要什么资格？


一般过去时:
What qualifications did he have? = 他有什么资格？
What qualifications were required for the previous position? = 之前的职位需要什么资格？


现在完成时:
What qualifications have you gained? = 你获得了什么资格？
What qualifications has she acquired so far? = 到目前为止她获得了什么资格？


过去完成时:
What qualifications had he acquired before applying? = 他在申请之前获得了什么资格？
What qualifications had they considered before making the decision? = 他们在做决定之前考虑过什么资格？


将来时:
What qualifications will you need for your future career? = 你未来的职业需要什么资格？
What qualifications will be required for the new role? = 新职位需要什么资格？


虚拟语气:
If I had the necessary qualifications, I would apply for the job. = 如果我有必要的资格，我会申请这份工作。
I wish I had the qualifications to be a doctor. = 我希望我有当医生的资格。


被动语态:
What qualifications are needed for this job? = 这份工作需要什么资格？
What qualifications are being considered for the selection process? = 在筛选过程中正在考虑什么资格？


疑问句:
What are your qualifications? = 你的资格是什么？
What qualifications does the candidate possess? = 候选人拥有哪些资格？


否定句:
He doesn't have the qualifications for the job. = 他没有这份工作所需的资格。
She lacked the necessary qualifications for admission. = 她缺乏入学所需的必要资格。
