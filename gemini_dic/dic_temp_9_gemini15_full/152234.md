patrolman:/pəˈtroʊlmən/ | n. |巡警，警员 |A patrolman is a police officer who patrols a specific area on foot or in a vehicle.  They are responsible for maintaining order, responding to calls, and preventing crime. |例句：The patrolman stopped the speeding car.  巡警拦下了超速行驶的汽车。
The patrolman apprehended the suspect. 巡警逮捕了嫌疑犯。|近义词：police officer, constable, policeman |反义词：criminal, offender
