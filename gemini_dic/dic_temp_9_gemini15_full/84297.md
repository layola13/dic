half-vellums: /ˈhæf ˈvɛləmz/ | n. | 半皮革装订 |  Half-vellum refers to a bookbinding technique where the cover is made of half vellum (a fine parchment made from calfskin) and half another material, usually cloth or leather.  This provides a balance between the durability of leather or cloth and the aesthetic appeal of vellum.  It's less expensive than a full vellum binding. |  The antique book was bound in half-vellums, with the spine and corners in vellum and the rest in cloth.  这本古书采用半皮革装订，书脊和书角为羊皮纸，其余部分为布料。 | 近义词：part-vellum binding | 反义词：full-vellum binding, full-cloth binding | 名词
