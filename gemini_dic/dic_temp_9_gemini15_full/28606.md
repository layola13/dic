cares:/kɛrz/ | v. |关心；在乎；介意 |To feel or show concern or worry about someone or something.  |She cares deeply about her family. 她非常关心她的家人。 | worries, concerns, minds |indifference, apathy |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He cares about his friends. 他关心他的朋友们。
She cares for her sick mother. 她照顾她生病的母亲。


一般过去时:
I cared for him very much. 我非常关心他。
They cared little for their possessions. 他们不太在乎他们的财产。


现在完成时:
He has cared for his grandmother for years. 他多年来一直照顾他的祖母。
She has cared about environmental issues since she was young. 她从小就关心环境问题。


过去完成时:
I had cared for my dog until he passed away. 我一直照顾我的狗直到它去世。
She had cared about his feelings, but he never noticed. 她一直关心他的感受，但他从未注意到。


将来时:
I will care for my parents when they are old. 我老了会照顾我的父母。
She will care about the result of the exam. 她会关心考试的结果。


虚拟语气:
If I cared more, I would have helped him. 如果我更关心的话，我会帮助他的。
I wish I cared less about what people think. 我希望我不那么在乎别人怎么想。


被动语态:
His feelings are cared for by his family. 他的感受受到了家人的关心。
The environment should be cared for by everyone. 环境应该受到每个人的关心。


疑问句:
Do you care what others think? 你在乎别人怎么想吗？
Does she care about her grades? 她关心她的成绩吗？


否定句:
He doesn't care about money. 他不在乎钱。
She didn't care what happened to him. 她不在乎他发生了什么。


PHRASE->

take care = 保重；小心
care for = 喜欢；照顾
care about = 关心；在乎
with care = 小心地；谨慎地
in care of = 转交；收件人


词源:

The word "care" originates from Old English *caru, meaning "sorrow, grief, anxiety,"  related to Old High German *kara "sorrow," and Old Norse kar "sorrow, anxiety."  The sense evolved to include concern and worry, and later, to the more positive sense of looking after someone or something.
