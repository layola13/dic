praising:/ˈpreɪzɪŋ/| v. |赞扬；称赞 |The act of expressing approval or admiration; commendation.  It often involves highlighting positive qualities or achievements. |例句：He received praising for his outstanding work. 他因杰出的工作而受到赞扬。

例句：The teacher gave praising comments on her essay. 老师对她的论文给予了赞扬的评价。


近义词：commending, applauding, extolling, lauding, complimenting |反义词：criticizing, condemning, blaming, censuring |词性：动词
