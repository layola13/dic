mesogaster:/ˌmɛsoʊˈɡæstər/| n. |中肠；中胃|The mesogaster is the middle section of the alimentary canal in insects and some other invertebrates.  It's responsible for much of the digestion and absorption of nutrients. |Example: The mesogaster of the grasshopper is particularly well-developed for processing plant material.  蝗虫的中肠特别发达，可以处理植物材料。|近义词：midgut (although not a perfect synonym, as "mesogaster" is more specific to certain invertebrates)|反义词：foregut, hindgut
