nonverifiable: /ˌnɒnˈvɛrɪfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 不可验证的，无法证实的 |  Impossible to verify or confirm; unable to be proven true.  This term describes something that lacks sufficient evidence or means to be checked for accuracy or authenticity. |

例句：The claim is nonverifiable due to a lack of supporting evidence.  这项说法由于缺乏 supporting evidence 而无法验证。

例句：His story about meeting aliens is completely nonverifiable. 他关于遇到外星人的故事完全无法证实。


近义词：unprovable, unconfirmable, unsubstantiated, undocumented

反义词：verifiable, provable, confirmable


词性：形容词
