overshadowers:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædoʊərz/| n.|遮蔽者；使黯然失色的人或事物|Those who or that which overshadows something else, making it less important or noticeable.  They can be people, events, or even ideas.|例句：The success of her brother's career overshadowed her own achievements. 她哥哥事业的成功使她自己的成就黯然失色。|例句：The towering skyscrapers overshadowed the smaller buildings in the neighborhood. 高耸的摩天大楼使附近较小的建筑物黯然失色。|近义词：obscurers, eclipsers, suppressors|反义词：illuminators, highlighters|名词
