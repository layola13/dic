muscosity:/mjuːˈskɒsəti/| n. |粘性，粘度|The property of being viscous; resistance to flow.  Muscosity is a measure of a fluid's resistance to deformation under shear stress.  It describes how "thick" or "sticky" a fluid is.|例句：The muscosity of honey is much higher than that of water.蜂蜜的粘度比水的粘度高得多。

The high muscosity of the paint made it difficult to apply.  油漆的高粘度使其难以涂抹。|近义词：viscosity, stickiness, thickness|反义词：fluidity, thinness|名词
