esophagus:/ɪˈsɒfəɡəs/ | n. | 食道 | The esophagus is the muscular tube that connects the pharynx to the stomach.  It transports food and liquids from the mouth to the stomach through a process called peristalsis. | Example:  The swallowed food moves down the esophagus into the stomach.  吞咽的食物通过食道进入胃部。 |近义词：gullet |反义词：无
