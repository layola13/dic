nonclinical:/ˌnɒnˈklɪnɪkl/| adj.|非临床的，临床外的| relating to or involving studies or tests that are not carried out on humans or animals in a clinical setting.  Nonclinical studies are often used in drug development to assess the safety of a compound before human clinical trials.|Example:  Nonclinical toxicology studies are crucial for evaluating drug safety before human trials. 非临床毒理学研究对于在人体试验前评估药物安全性至关重要。|近义词：preclinical, laboratory|反义词：clinical
