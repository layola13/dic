re-codify:/ˌriːˈkoʊdəˌfaɪ/| vt.|重新编纂；重新制定法典|To revise and reorganize a system of laws or rules, often with the aim of simplification or modernization.  The process involves creating a new, improved version of existing codes.|例句：The government decided to re-codify the tax laws to make them clearer and more efficient. 政府决定重新编纂税法，使其更清晰、更高效。|例句：The company re-codified its internal policies to reflect changes in best practices. 公司重新制定了内部政策，以反映最佳实践的变化。|近义词：revise, reformulate, rewrite, reorganize|反义词：leave unchanged, maintain status quo|动词
