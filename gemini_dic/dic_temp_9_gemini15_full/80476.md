gonorrhea:/ˌɡɒnəˈriːə/| n. |淋病 |A sexually transmitted infection (STI) caused by the bacterium Neisseria gonorrhoeae. It can infect the genitals, rectum, and throat.  Symptoms may include pain during urination, discharge from the penis or vagina, and abdominal pain.  However, many people with gonorrhea have no symptoms.  Early diagnosis and treatment with antibiotics are crucial to prevent complications. |例句：The doctor confirmed that he had contracted gonorrhea. 医生证实他感染了淋病。|例句：Untreated gonorrhea can lead to serious health problems. 不治疗淋病会导致严重的健康问题。|近义词：gonococcal infection |反义词：N/A
