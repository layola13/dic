grackle:/ˈɡrækl/| n.|椋鸟，八哥|A grackle is a type of bird, typically black with iridescent feathers, known for its intelligence and sometimes considered a pest due to its large flocks.|Example:  A large flock of grackles descended upon the park, feasting on the fallen berries.  一大群椋鸟飞落公园，啄食掉落的浆果。|近义词：blackbird, crow|反义词：无
