ictus:/ˈɪktəs/ | n. | 冲击；重音；强音节 | The stressed syllable in a metrical foot.  It refers to the beat or emphasis in a rhythmic pattern, particularly in poetry or music.  | Example: The ictus in the word "beautiful" falls on the first syllable.  美丽这个词的重音落在第一个音节上。|近义词：stress, emphasis, beat | 反义词：arsis (in metrics)
