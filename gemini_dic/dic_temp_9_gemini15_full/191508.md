schmancy:/ˈʃmænsi/| adj.|时髦的；花哨的；讲究的|Stylish and sophisticated, often in a showy or pretentious way.  It implies a certain level of elegance but can also suggest ostentation.|例句：Her schmancy handbag cost a fortune.  她的名牌手袋价值不菲。

例句：The restaurant had a schmancy atmosphere, with white tablecloths and candlelight. 这家餐厅氛围高雅，铺着白色桌布，烛光摇曳。


近义词：fancy, posh, stylish, elegant, upscale, ritzy, flashy

反义词：cheap, tacky, dowdy, unstylish, drab


词源:The etymology of "schmancy" is uncertain, but it's believed to be of Yiddish origin, possibly a variant or alteration of a word related to "smart" or "showy."  The precise origin remains elusive, and several theories exist, but no definitive source has been established.
