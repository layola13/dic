farmers-markets:/ˈfɑːrmərz ˈmɑːrkɪts/| n. |农贸市场|A farmers' market is a public market where farmers and other food producers sell their products directly to consumers.  These markets often feature fresh produce, baked goods, meats, cheeses, and other locally sourced foods. |例句：We buy our vegetables at the farmers' market every Saturday. 我们每个星期六都在农贸市场买蔬菜。|例句：The local farmers' market is a great place to find seasonal fruits and vegetables. 当地农贸市场是寻找应季水果和蔬菜的好地方。|近义词：farmers market, produce market, market, farm stand|反义词：supermarket, grocery store
