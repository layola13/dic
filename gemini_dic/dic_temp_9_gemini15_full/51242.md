destructionist: /dɪˈstrʌkʃənɪst/ | adj., n. | 破坏主义者；破坏论者 |  One who advocates or practices destruction, especially of established institutions or traditions; relating to or characterized by destruction.  A destructionist may be someone who actively seeks to destroy something, or someone whose theories or actions lead to destruction.  The term can carry a strong negative connotation, implying malice or disregard for consequences.


例句：

The protesters were accused of being destructionists, intent on damaging public property.  = 抗议者被指控为破坏分子，意图破坏公共财产。

His policies, critics argue, are inherently destructionist, leading to the erosion of social safety nets. = 批评人士认为，他的政策本质上是破坏性的，导致社会保障体系遭到破坏。


近义词：saboteur, vandal, nihilist, demolitionist

反义词：preservationist, conservationist, constructionist, builder
