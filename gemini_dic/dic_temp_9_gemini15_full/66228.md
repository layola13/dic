excitingest:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋɪst/| adj.|最令人兴奋的，最刺激的|This word is a superlative form of "exciting," formed by adding "-est" directly.  It's considered informal and non-standard in formal writing, though common in informal speech, especially among children and adolescents.  The standard superlative is "most exciting." |例句：That rollercoaster was the excitingest ride I've ever been on! = 那个过山车是我玩过的最刺激的游乐设施！ That was the excitingest part of the whole trip. = 那次旅行中最令人兴奋的部分是那个。|近义词：thrilling, exhilarating, stimulating|反义词：boring, dull, tedious|形容词
