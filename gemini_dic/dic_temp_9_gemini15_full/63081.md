enschedule:/ɪnˈskɛdʒuːl/ | vt. | 安排；预定；排定时间 | To arrange or schedule something in advance.  | 例句：We need to enschedule the meeting for next week.我们需要为下周安排会议。

近义词：schedule, arrange, plan, timetable | 反义词：postpone, delay, cancel | 词性：动词
