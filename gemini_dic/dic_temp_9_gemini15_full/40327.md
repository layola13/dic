contra-indication:/ˌkɒntrəɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |禁忌症，禁忌|A contraindication is a specific situation or condition in which a particular treatment should not be used because it may be harmful to the patient.  It's a reason to avoid a particular treatment. |Example:  The patient's allergy to penicillin is a contraindication to using that antibiotic.  这位病人对青霉素过敏，这是使用该抗生素的禁忌症。

Example:  A history of heart problems is a contraindication for strenuous exercise.  有心脏问题的病史是剧烈运动的禁忌症。


近义词: contravention, prohibition, warning
