quasi-consuming: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ kənˈsuːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 近似消费的，似消费的 |  Describes something that resembles or has the characteristics of consumption, but not in a complete or fully realized sense. It suggests an incomplete or partial act of consumption, or a process that leads towards, but doesn't fully achieve, consumption. | The quasi-consuming nature of the online experience, where users browse endlessly without making purchases, is a challenge for e-commerce businesses.  | 近义词: partial consumption, near-consumption, incipient consumption | 反义词: complete consumption, full consumption |  形容词
