fluorescent:/fluəˈrɛsnt/ | adj. | 荧光的，发出荧光的 |  Emitting or reflecting light of a particular wavelength when exposed to ultraviolet radiation or other forms of electromagnetic radiation.  Fluorescent materials absorb light of a shorter wavelength and re-emit light of a longer wavelength. This process is known as fluorescence. | The poster is decorated with fluorescent colors. 这张海报装饰着荧光色。 The fluorescent lights hummed overhead.  头顶上的荧光灯嗡嗡作响。 | phosphorescent, luminous | opaque, dark |

The neon sign fluoresces brightly at night. 夜晚霓虹灯发出明亮的荧光。
That mineral fluoresces under ultraviolet light.  那种矿物在紫外线下发出荧光。

Scientists have discovered many substances that fluoresce. 科学家们已经发现了许多发出荧光的物质。

虚拟语气:
If the solution fluoresced, we would know the reaction was complete. 如果溶液发出荧光，我们就知道了反应完成了。
I wish the paint fluoresced more vividly in daylight. 我希望这种颜料在日光下能发出更鲜艳的荧光。


被动语态:
The sample was fluoresced under UV light. 样品在紫外线下被照射发出荧光。（注：fluoresce本身为不及物动词，此处为被动语态的特殊用法，强调样品受紫外线照射）
The results were carefully analyzed to determine if the substance fluoresced. 结果被仔细分析以确定该物质是否发出荧光。


疑问句:

否定句:
