desolate:/ˈdɛsələt/ | adj. |荒凉的；凄凉的；孤独的 |  Emphasizes emptiness, loneliness, and lack of life or activity.  It can describe a place, a feeling, or a person's state of mind. |例句：The desolate landscape stretched before them, devoid of life.  那片荒凉的景象展现在他们面前，毫无生机。

The desolate feeling of grief overwhelmed her after the loss of her pet.  失去宠物后，她被压抑的悲伤所淹没。|近义词：barren, empty, lonely, bleak, forlorn |反义词：populated, thriving, cheerful, joyful, lively
