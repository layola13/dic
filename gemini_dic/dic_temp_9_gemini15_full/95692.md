incondensable: /ˌɪnkənˈdɛnsəbəl/ | adj. | 不可冷凝的，不凝结的 | Not capable of being condensed into a liquid or solid state; remaining as a gas.  | Example:  Water vapor is condensable, but many gases, such as helium and nitrogen, are incondensable under normal atmospheric conditions.  水蒸气是可以冷凝的，但许多气体，如氦气和氮气，在正常大气条件下是不可冷凝的。|近义词：non-condensable | 反义词：condensable
