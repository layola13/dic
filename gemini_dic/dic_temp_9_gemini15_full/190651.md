saucers:/ˈsɔːsər/| n.|碟子，茶托|A small, shallow, dish-shaped vessel, typically used to place a cup on.  It can also refer to a flying saucer.|例句：She placed her teacup on the saucer. 她把茶杯放在茶托上。

例句：Many people believe in the existence of UFOs, or flying saucers. 许多人相信不明飞行物，也就是飞碟的存在。


近义词：dish, plate (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically|词性:Noun
