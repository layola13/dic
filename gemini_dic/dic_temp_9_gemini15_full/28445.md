carbuncled:/ˈkɑːrbʌŋkld/ | adj. | 患痈的；布满痈的 | Covered with or resembling carbuncles (severe skin infections).  The word evokes a sense of roughness, redness, and inflammation. |例句：The patient presented with a carbuncled neck, requiring immediate medical attention. 患者脖子患有痈，需要立即就医。

The old leather chair was carbuncled with age and wear.  那把旧皮椅因年代久远和磨损而布满疙瘩。


近义词：blotchy, inflamed, infected, pustular | 反义词：smooth, unblemished, healthy


词源:  From carbuncle + -ed.  "Carbuncle" itself comes from Late Latin *carbunculus, a diminutive of Latin carbo "coal," referring to the dark, inflamed appearance of the infection.
