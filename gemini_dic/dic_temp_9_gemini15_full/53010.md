dinosaurian:/ˌdaɪnəˈsɔːriən/| adj.|恐龙的；似恐龙的|Of or relating to dinosaurs.  The word evokes the characteristics or features associated with dinosaurs, including their size, appearance, and behavior.|例句：Dinosaurian fossils are found in many parts of the world.  = 恐龙化石在世界许多地方都有发现。

例句：The museum boasts a remarkable collection of dinosaurian remains. =  博物馆拥有令人瞩目的恐龙遗骸收藏。


近义词：dinosaur-like, reptilian (in some contexts)


反义词：mammalian (in the context of contrasting with dinosaurs)


词性：形容词
