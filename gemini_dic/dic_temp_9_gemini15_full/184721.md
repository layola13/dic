resubstantiate:/ˌriːsəbˈstænʃieɪt/| vt.|  使…恢复实质；重新证实；为…提供实质基础|To give substance or support to (something); to re-establish or confirm the truth or validity of (something).  It implies restoring something to its former strength or importance after it has been weakened or questioned.|


例句：
The lawyer tried to resubstantiate his client's alibi. = 律师试图重新证实其当事人的不在场证明。

The new evidence helped resubstantiate the theory. = 新证据有助于重新证实这一理论。


近义词：reinforce, corroborate, substantiate, reaffirm, validate


反义词：refute, invalidate, contradict, debunk


词性:动词
