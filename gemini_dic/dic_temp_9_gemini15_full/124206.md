mosher:/ˈmɒʃər/| n. |狂热舞者；激烈摇滚乐迷|A mosher is someone who participates in moshing, a type of energetic dancing typically seen at punk rock, metal, or hardcore concerts.  They often push and shove other people in the crowd. |例句：The mosher in the front row was really getting into the music.  前排的那个狂热舞者真的沉醉在音乐中了。|近义词：punk rocker, metalhead, hardcore kid |反义词：spectator, observer
