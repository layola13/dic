sensitised:/ˈsɛnsɪtaɪzd/ | adj. | 敏感的；过敏的；感光了的 | Made sensitive or more sensitive to a particular stimulus.  Sensitised  describes something that has been made more responsive or reactive to something else, often light or a particular substance. | 例句：The film was sensitised to capture even the faintest light.  胶卷经过感光处理，即使是最微弱的光也能捕捉到。 The patient is sensitised to penicillin and should avoid it. 病人对青霉素过敏，应避免使用。 | 近义词：sensitive, reactive, responsive, allergic | 反义词：insensitive, unresponsive, immune
