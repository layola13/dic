extol:/ɪkˈstoʊl/ | vt. | 赞扬；颂扬；吹捧 | To praise enthusiastically; glorify.  | 例句：The critic extolled the young writer's talent. 批评家高度赞扬了这位年轻作家的才华。 | 例句：She extolled the virtues of hard work. 她赞扬了努力工作的益处。 | 近义词：praise, commend, laud, celebrate | 反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They extol the benefits of exercise. = 他们赞扬运动的好处。
We extol the virtues of honesty. = 我们赞扬诚实的优点。


一般过去时:
The speaker extolled the achievements of the team. = 演讲者赞扬了团队的成就。
The article extolled the beauty of the countryside. = 这篇文章赞扬了乡村的美景。


现在完成时:
She has extolled the importance of education for years. = 多年来她一直强调教育的重要性。
They have extolled the benefits of this new technology. = 他们一直赞扬这项新技术的益处。


过去完成时:
He had extolled the virtues of patience before the challenge began. = 在挑战开始之前，他已经赞扬了耐心的美德。
The teacher had extolled the students' hard work throughout the semester. = 老师在整个学期都赞扬了学生们的努力工作。


将来时:
The mayor will extol the city's progress in his speech. = 市长将在他的演讲中赞扬城市的发展。
We will extol the bravery of our heroes. = 我们将赞扬我们英雄的勇气。


虚拟语气:
If I were to write a review, I would extol the film's innovative techniques. = 如果我要写一篇影评，我会赞扬这部电影的创新技术。
I wish they would extol the positive aspects of the situation. = 我希望他们能赞扬情况的积极方面。


被动语态:
His contributions were extolled by many. = 他的贡献受到了许多人的赞扬。
The benefits of the program are widely extolled. = 该项目的益处得到了广泛的赞扬。


疑问句:
Does the article extol the virtues of simplicity? = 这篇文章是否赞扬了简单朴素的优点？
Will the speaker extol the achievements of the company? = 演讲者会赞扬公司的成就吗？


否定句:
The review did not extol the film's acting. = 这篇评论并没有赞扬这部电影的表演。
He did not extol his own accomplishments. = 他并没有夸耀自己的成就。


PHRASE->
extol the virtues of = 赞扬……的优点
extol the merits of = 赞扬……的优点
extol someone's praises = 赞扬某人
extol someone to the skies = 极力赞扬某人
extol the greatness of = 赞扬……的伟大


词源:
From Old French *extoller, from Latin extollere "to raise up, lift up," from ex- "up, out" + tollere "to lift, raise."
