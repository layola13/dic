flyovers:/ˈflaɪˌoʊvərz/| n.|立交桥，高架桥|An elevated roadway that crosses over another road or railway, typically at an intersection.|例句：The new flyovers significantly reduced traffic congestion. 新建的立交桥大大缓解了交通拥堵。

例句：We took the flyover to avoid the traffic jam below. 我们走高架桥以避开下面的交通堵塞。


近义词：overpass, viaduct, elevated roadway


反义词：underpass, subway


词性:名词
