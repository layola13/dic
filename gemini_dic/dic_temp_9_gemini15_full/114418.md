make sense:/meɪk sɛns/| v. |讲得通；有意义；合乎情理|To be logical, understandable, or reasonable.|The explanation makes sense to me now.  现在我理解这个解释了。|His actions don't make sense. 他的行为毫无道理。|understand, be logical, be coherent|be nonsensical, be illogical, be absurd|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His arguments make sense. = 他的论点很有道理。
This plan makes perfect sense to me. = 这个计划对我来说非常合理。

一般过去时:
Her story didn't make sense at first. = 她的故事起初听起来毫无道理。
It made sense to leave early. = 早点离开是明智之举。

现在完成时:
It has finally made sense to me. = 我终于明白了。
Everything has made sense since I read the book. = 自从我读了那本书之后，一切都变得清晰明了。

过去完成时:
It hadn't made sense to him before the meeting. = 会议之前他还没弄明白。
After much thought, it had made sense to her. = 经过深思熟虑之后，她明白了。


将来时:
It will make sense once you understand the context. = 一旦你理解了上下文，就会明白。
This decision will make more sense in the long run. = 从长远来看，这个决定会更有意义。

虚拟语气:
If you read the instructions carefully, it would make more sense. = 如果你仔细阅读说明，就会更容易理解。
It would make sense to consult a doctor. = 看医生是明智的。


被动语态:
The instructions need to be rewritten; they don't make sense. = 说明需要重写；它们讲不通。
The solution is easily understood, if it makes sense. = 如果说得通，那么这个解决方案很容易理解。

疑问句:
Does this make sense to you? = 你觉得这有道理吗？
Does what he said make any sense? = 他说的话有道理吗？

否定句:
That doesn't make sense at all. = 那完全没有道理。
His explanation didn't make any sense to me. = 他的解释我一点也不懂。


PHRASE->
It makes sense to… = 做……是明智的
That makes sense. = 这样说有道理。
Does that make sense? = 你明白了吗？
This doesn't make sense to me. = 我不明白这个。
