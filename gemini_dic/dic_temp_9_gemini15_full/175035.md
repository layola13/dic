radioecologist:/ˌreɪdiouɪˈkɒlədʒɪst/| n. |放射生态学家|A scientist who studies the effects of radiation on ecosystems and organisms.  Radioecologists investigate how radioactive materials move through the environment, affecting different species and the overall health of ecosystems. They often work with government agencies, research institutions, and environmental consulting firms.|例句：The radioecologist analyzed the soil samples to determine the extent of radioactive contamination. 放射生态学家分析土壤样本以确定放射性污染的程度。 The team of radioecologists studied the impact of the Chernobyl disaster on the surrounding wildlife. 放射生态学家团队研究了切尔诺贝利灾难对周边野生动物的影响。|近义词：environmental scientist, radiation biologist|反义词：N/A
