/ˈdoʊsɪz/ | n. |剂量，剂；(药物的)一剂|A dose is a measured amount of something, especially medicine.  It refers to the quantity administered at one time.|例句：The doctor prescribed a dose of antibiotics twice a day.  医生开了每天两次抗生素的处方。|例句：She gave the plant a dose of fertilizer. 她给植物施了一剂肥料。|近义词：portion, quantity, amount |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor gives the patient a dose of medicine every day. = 医生每天给病人服用一剂药。
A single dose of this drug is usually enough. = 通常一剂这种药就足够了。
