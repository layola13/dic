reinjected:/ˌriːɪnˈdʒektɪd/| v.|重新注射；再次注入；重新引入 | To inject again; to introduce or add something again.  |例句：The doctor reinjected the patient with antibiotics. 医生再次给病人注射了抗生素。

例句：The government reinjected funds into the failing project. 政府再次向失败的项目注资。


近义词：reintroduce, reapply, inject anew


反义词：withdraw, remove, extract


词性：动词

词源:
The word "reinjected" is formed from the prefix "re-" (again, anew) + "injected," which comes from the verb "inject" (to force a fluid into something).  "Inject" itself has Latin roots, combining "in-" (into) and "iacere" (to throw). Therefore, "reinjected" literally means "thrown into again."
