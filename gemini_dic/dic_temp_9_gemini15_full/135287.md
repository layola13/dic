nonparticulate: /ˌnɒn.pɑːˈtɪk.jʊ.lət/ | adj. |非颗粒状的，非微粒的 | Not consisting of or relating to particles; continuous or unbroken.  This term is often used in scientific contexts, particularly in relation to matter in various states. |Example: The nonparticulate nature of the substance made it difficult to filter.  非颗粒状物质的特性使得过滤它变得困难。The experiment demonstrated the nonparticulate emission of radiation.  实验证明了辐射的非颗粒状发射。| 近义词: continuous, unbroken, homogeneous | 反义词: particulate, granular


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What is your name?".  The usage focuses on describing the nature of a substance or phenomenon.  Examples will illustrate its use in different sentence structures.

Affirmative Sentence:
The sample exhibited a nonparticulate structure. = 样本显示出非颗粒结构。
The fluid was entirely nonparticulate in its composition. = 该流体在其组成上完全是非颗粒状的。


Negative Sentence:
The substance was not nonparticulate; it was clearly granular. = 该物质并非非颗粒状；它显然是颗粒状的。

Question:
