scanning:/ˈskænɪŋ/ | n./v. |扫描；浏览；快速检查 |The act of examining something quickly, often visually, to get a general idea or to look for something specific.  It can refer to both the action and the process.  As a noun, it refers to the act itself; as a verb, it refers to the process of performing the act. |例句：The doctor performed a scanning of the patient's body. 医生对病人的身体进行了扫描。

The student quickly scanned the text for the answer. 学生快速浏览文本寻找答案。


近义词：scanning, skimming, surveying, perusing, reviewing | 反义词： scrutinizing, studying meticulously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scans the newspaper every morning. 他每天早上浏览报纸。
She scans the barcode at the checkout. 她在收银台扫描条形码。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
