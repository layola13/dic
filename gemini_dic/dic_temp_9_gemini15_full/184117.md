resinoid:/ˈrezɪnɔɪd/ | n. |树脂状物；类树脂物质 |A resinoid is a synthetic resin that imitates or resembles natural resins in its properties.  It's often used in various applications where natural resins might be too expensive or unavailable.  Resinoids are typically made from various raw materials, such as rosin, and processed to enhance their desired qualities.| The furniture was coated with a durable resinoid finish. 这件家具涂上了耐用的树脂状涂层。The laboratory tested the resinoid for its adhesive properties. 实验室测试了该类树脂物质的粘合性能。|synthetic resin, resin-like substance |  natural resin (反义词较为宽泛，取决于具体语境)
