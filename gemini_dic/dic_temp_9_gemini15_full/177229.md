reasoned:/ˈriːzənd/| adj.|合理的，有根据的，经过深思熟虑的|Based on or using reason; logical.|His reasoned arguments convinced the jury.  他的有理有据的论证说服了陪审团。|logical, rational, sensible|unreasonable, illogical, irrational|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His decision is always reasoned. = 他的决定总是很合理。
Her response is reasoned and thoughtful. = 她的回应是经过深思熟虑的，合情合理的。


一般过去时:
The judge's ruling was well-reasoned. = 法官的裁决非常合理。
They reasoned that the best course of action was to wait. = 他们认为最好的做法是等待。


现在完成时:
He has reasoned out the solution to the problem. = 他已经想出了解决这个问题的方法。
She has presented a reasoned argument against the proposal.  = 她提出了反对该提案的合理论据。


过去完成时:
They had reasoned that the evidence was insufficient. = 他们之前认为证据不足。
Having reasoned through the options, she made her decision. = 在仔细权衡了各种选择之后，她做出了决定。


将来时:
We will reason out the best approach before we start. = 我们将在开始之前想出最佳方案。
I believe she will present a reasoned case for her position. = 我相信她会为自己的立场提出合理的论据。


虚拟语气:
If he had reasoned more carefully, he would have avoided the mistake. = 如果他更仔细地思考一下，他就能避免这个错误。
I wish I had reasoned with him before it escalated. = 我希望在我之前能和他好好沟通。


被动语态:
The decision was reasoned out by the committee. = 委员会经过深思熟虑后做出了决定。
The solution was carefully reasoned and explained. = 解决方案经过仔细推敲并做了解释。


疑问句:
Was his decision reasoned and fair? = 他的决定合理且公平吗？
Is the argument sufficiently reasoned to be persuasive? = 这个论证是否足够合理以具有说服力？


否定句:
His argument was not reasoned. = 他的论证没有道理。
The proposal wasn't well-reasoned. = 这份提案缺乏理性。
