dinghies:/ˈdɪŋiːz/| n.|小划艇，小艇 |A small boat, typically used for sailing or rowing, often carried aboard a larger vessel.  |例句：We lowered the dinghy into the water. 我们把小艇放入水中。

例句：The children enjoyed sailing in the dinghy. 孩子们喜欢在小艇上航行。


近义词：small boat, tender, rowboat


反义词：large ship, yacht, ocean liner


词性:名词

词源:
The word "dinghy" is believed to have originated from the Hindi word "dingi," referring to a small boat.  The word's journey into English is likely through the influence of British sailors in India.  Over time, its spelling and pronunciation evolved to the current form.
