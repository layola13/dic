noncontingent:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɪndʒənt/| adj.|非偶然的，必然的，独立的|Not dependent on or subject to chance or contingency; certain to happen; inevitable.  It implies a lack of dependence on external factors or conditions. |例句：The laws of physics are noncontingent; they are not dependent on chance. 物理定律是非偶然的；它们不依赖于偶然性。

The outcome of the experiment was noncontingent upon the initial conditions. 实验的结果与初始条件无关。|近义词：necessary, inevitable, certain, unavoidable|反义词：contingent, accidental, chance, fortuitous
