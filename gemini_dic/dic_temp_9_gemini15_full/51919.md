dhurries:/ˈdʒʊriːz/| n.| 地毯，毛毯 | A dhurrie is a type of flat-woven carpet or rug, typically made in India. They are known for their simple designs and durable construction.  | Example:  The living room was adorned with a beautiful hand-woven dhurrie. 客厅里装饰着一块漂亮的手工编织地毯。  |近义词：rug, carpet, mat|反义词：None (反义词不适用)|名词
