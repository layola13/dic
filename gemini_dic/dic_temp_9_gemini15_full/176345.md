re-exchange:/ˌriːɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ/ | v. | 再兑换；重新交换 | To exchange again; to exchange something for a second time.  | 例句：He had to re-exchange the faulty goods for a new set. 他不得不把有问题的商品再换成一套新的。

例句：The bank agreed to re-exchange the traveler's checks after he explained the situation.  银行在他解释情况后同意重新兑换旅行支票。

近义词：re-convert,  redeem, replace

反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-exchange currencies regularly. = 他们定期兑换货币。
He re-exchanges the books with his friend. = 他和朋友重新交换书籍。


一般过去时:
She re-exchanged the dress for a smaller size. = 她把那件衣服换成了更小的尺寸。
We re-exchanged the gifts because we both got the wrong things. = 我们重新交换了礼物，因为我们俩都拿错了东西。


现在完成时:
They have re-exchanged the information several times. = 他们已经多次交换信息了。
I have re-exchanged the faulty product twice already. = 我已经两次重新兑换了有缺陷的产品。


过去完成时:
He had re-exchanged the tickets before the show started. = 在演出开始之前，他已经重新兑换了票。
She had re-exchanged the dollars for euros before her trip. = 在旅行之前，她已经把美元重新兑换成欧元了。


将来时:
They will re-exchange the documents tomorrow. = 他们明天将重新交换文件。
We will re-exchange the phone numbers after the meeting. = 会议结束后我们将重新交换电话号码。


虚拟语气:
If I needed to, I would re-exchange the item. = 如果需要的话，我会重新兑换商品。
If he had known, he would have re-exchanged the gift earlier. = 如果他知道的话，他会早点重新交换礼物。


被动语态:
The currency was re-exchanged at a better rate. = 货币以更好的汇率重新兑换了。
The items were re-exchanged due to a manufacturing defect. = 由于制造缺陷，物品被重新兑换了。


疑问句:

Did they re-exchange the items successfully? = 他们成功地重新交换了物品吗？


否定句:
They did not re-exchange the faulty equipment. = 他们没有重新兑换有故障的设备。
I will not re-exchange the tickets after the deadline. = 截止日期后我不会重新兑换门票。
