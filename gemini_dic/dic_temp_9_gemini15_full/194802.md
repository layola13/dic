semanticians:/sɪˈmænṭɪʃənz/| n. |语义学家，语义学者 |Those who study semantics, the branch of linguistics and logic concerned with meaning. Semanticians analyze how meaning is created, interpreted, and conveyed through language.  |Example:  Many semanticians are interested in the relationship between words and their meanings. 许多语义学家都对词语及其含义之间的关系很感兴趣。  |近义词：linguists (if focusing on the linguistic aspect),  semioticians (if considering broader systems of signs) |反义词：无
