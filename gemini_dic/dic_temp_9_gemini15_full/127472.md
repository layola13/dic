necessitarianism:/nəˈsɛsətəriənɪzəm/| n. | 必然论；决定论 | The philosophical doctrine that all events are causally determined and inevitable.  It asserts that free will is an illusion, and that every action is the necessary consequence of prior events.  | Example:  Necessitarianism is a controversial philosophical position that challenges our intuitive sense of free will. = 必然论是一个有争议的哲学立场，它挑战了我们对自由意志的直觉。

Example:  Some scientists believe that necessitarianism is supported by the laws of physics. = 一些科学家认为物理定律支持必然论。

近义词：determinism, fatalism | 反义词：libertarianism, indeterminism | 词性：名词
