extracranial:/ˌɛkstrəkˈreɪniəl/| adj.|颅外的，颅腔外的|Situated or occurring outside the cranium (skull).  It refers to structures, processes, or conditions that are external to the skull.|例句：The extracranial arteries supply blood to the face and scalp. 颅外动脉向面部和头皮供血。

例句：The surgeon successfully repaired the extracranial injury. 外科医生成功修复了颅外损伤。


近义词：outside the skull, extracerebral (if specifically referring to the brain)|反义词：intracranial


词性：形容词
