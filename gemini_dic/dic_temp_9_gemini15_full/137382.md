nontemporizing: /ˌnɒnˈtɛmpəraɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 不拖延的；当机立断的 | Not delaying or procrastinating; decisive and prompt in action.  | The general's nontemporizing response to the enemy attack prevented further losses. 将军对敌人攻击的果断反应阻止了进一步的损失。 | 近义词：decisive, prompt, immediate, expeditious | 反义词：procrastinating, dilatory, hesitant, irresolute


词源:

* **non-**:  Prefix meaning "not". (否定前缀)
* **temporizing**: Verb "to temporize" meaning "to compromise; to avoid making a decision; to delay". (妥协；避免做决定；拖延)  This word itself derives from "temper," meaning to moderate or adjust.  (缓和或调整)


用法说明:

"Nontemporizing" is primarily used to describe actions, decisions, or individuals that are characterized by a lack of delay or procrastination. It emphasizes immediate and decisive action.  It is often used in formal contexts to highlight the importance of swift and effective responses in situations demanding prompt action.  While not as frequently used as some other synonyms, its precision makes it a useful word when emphasizing the absence of delay.
