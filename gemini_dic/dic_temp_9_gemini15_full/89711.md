hope:/hoʊp/| n. vt.|希望，期望|To wish for something to happen or to be true.|例句：I hope it doesn't rain. 我希望不下雨。|例句：I hope to see you soon. 我希望很快见到你。|近义词：wish, desire, expect|反义词：despair, dread


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hope it snows tomorrow. = 我希望明天会下雪。
She hopes to win the prize. = 她希望赢得奖品。


一般过去时:
I hoped for a better result. = 我希望得到更好的结果。
They hoped to finish early. = 他们希望早点完成。


现在完成时:
I have hoped for this day for years. = 我盼望这一天已经很多年了。
She has hoped to travel to Europe. = 她一直希望去欧洲旅行。


过去完成时:
I had hoped to see you earlier. = 我本希望早点见到你。
They had hoped the weather would be better. = 他们原本希望天气会更好。


将来时:
I will hope for the best. = 我将抱最好的希望。
We will hope to hear from you soon. = 我们希望很快收到你的来信。


虚拟语气:
I hope I could fly. = 我希望我能飞。
I hope it were true. = 我希望这是真的。


被动语态:
(Hope is rarely used in the passive voice.  A similar construction might use "It is hoped...")
It is hoped that the project will be completed on time. = 人们希望该项目能按时完成。
It was hoped that the rain would stop. = 人们希望雨能停。


疑问句:
What do you hope for? = 你希望什么？
Do you hope to succeed? = 你希望成功吗？


否定句:
I hope it doesn't rain. = 我希望不下雨。
I don't hope for much. = 我不抱太大希望。


PHRASE->
hope for:  盼望
have hope:  怀有希望
give someone hope: 给某人希望
lose hope:  失去希望
full of hope:  充满希望
