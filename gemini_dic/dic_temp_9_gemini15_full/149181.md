oxychloric:/ˌɒksɪˈklɔːrɪk/| adj.|含氧氯的；次氯酸的|Containing or relating to oxychlorine, a compound containing oxygen and chlorine.  Oxychloric acid is a hypothetical acid, HClO₄,  containing chlorine in its highest oxidation state (+7).  The term is often used in the context of oxychlorine compounds, which are often strong oxidizing agents.|The oxychloric acid is highly reactive and unstable.|近义词：perchloric (more common and accurate for HClO₄)|反义词：N/A
