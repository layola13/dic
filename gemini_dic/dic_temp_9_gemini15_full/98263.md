inserters:/ɪnˈsɜːrtərz/| n.|插入器，插入物|A device or person that inserts something.|例句：The surgeon used specialized inserters to place the stents.  外科医生使用专业的插入器放置支架。|近义词：inserts, implants, introducers|反义词：removers, extractors


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine inserts the labels automatically. = 机器自动插入标签。
He inserts a coin into the vending machine. = 他往自动售货机里投币。

一般过去时:
She inserted the key into the lock. = 她把钥匙插进锁孔。
They inserted a new paragraph into the document. = 他们在文档中插入了一个新段落。

现在完成时:
They have inserted the data into the database. = 他们已将数据插入数据库。
She has inserted a picture into her presentation. = 她已在演示文稿中插入了一张图片。

过去完成时:
He had already inserted the SIM card before the phone started. = 手机启动前他已经插入了SIM卡。
They had inserted the wrong information into the system. = 他们已经将错误的信息插入系统。

将来时:
We will insert the new module into the software next week. = 我们下周将把新模块插入软件。
She will insert a humorous anecdote into her speech. = 她将在演讲中插入一个幽默的轶事。

虚拟语气:
If I inserted the correct code, the program would run. = 如果我输入正确的代码，程序就会运行。
I wish I had inserted a footnote explaining that point. = 我真希望我插入了一个脚注来解释这一点。

被动语态:
The data was inserted into the spreadsheet successfully. = 数据已成功插入电子表格。
The new chapter will be inserted into the book before publication. = 新章节将在出版前插入书中。

疑问句:
Where did you insert the file? = 你把文件插入到哪里了？
Have they inserted the correct information? = 他们插入的信息正确吗？

否定句:
He didn't insert the USB drive properly. = 他没有正确插入U盘。
She failed to insert the necessary plugins. = 她未能插入必要的插件。
