prearrange:/ˌpriːəˈreɪndʒ/ | vt. |预先安排；预先约定 | To arrange (something) beforehand.  |例句：We prearranged a meeting for next week. 我们预先安排了下周的会议。

例句：They prearranged to meet at the library. 他们预先约定在图书馆见面。


近义词：arrange beforehand, plan in advance, schedule, organize in advance | 反义词：improvise, wing it


词性：动词
