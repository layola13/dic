endure:/ɪnˈdʊr/| vt. vi. n.|忍受；持久；持续|to suffer something difficult, unpleasant, or painful|The pain was almost unbearable, but she endured.  疼痛几乎难以忍受，但她坚持了下来。|She endured years of hardship. 她忍受了多年的苦难。|近义词：tolerate, withstand, suffer|反义词:give up, quit, surrender


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endures the harsh weather. = 他忍受着恶劣的天气。
They endure long working hours. = 他们忍受着漫长的工作时间。

一般过去时:
She endured the pain bravely. = 她勇敢地忍受了疼痛。
We endured a long and boring journey. = 我们忍受了一次漫长而无聊的旅程。

现在完成时:
They have endured many challenges. = 他们已经经受了许多挑战。
She has endured much suffering in her life. = 她一生经历了很多苦难。

过去完成时:
He had endured years of poverty before he found success. = 在他获得成功之前，他已经忍受了多年的贫困。
They had endured countless hardships during the war. = 在战争期间，他们经历了无数的苦难。

将来时:
We will endure whatever comes our way. = 我们将忍受任何降临在我们身上的事情。
They will endure the test of time.  = 他们将经受时间的考验。

虚拟语气:
If I had known what I was getting into, I would not have endured it. = 如果我知道自己会遇到什么，我就不 会忍受它了。
I wish I didn't have to endure this anymore. = 我希望我不必再忍受这一切了。

被动语态:
The hardship was endured by the people. = 这种苦难是由人民承受的。
The long journey was endured patiently. = 漫长的旅程被耐心地忍受了。

疑问句:
How long can you endure the pain? = 你能忍受多久的疼痛？
Can we endure this situation any longer? = 我们还能忍受这种情况多久？

否定句:
He could not endure the pressure any longer. = 他再也无法忍受压力了。
She would not endure such injustice. = 她不会忍受这种不公正的待遇。
