guesswork:/ˈɡɛsˌwɜːrk/| n. |猜测；臆测；推测|Work based on guesses rather than facts or knowledge.  It often implies a lack of certainty or reliable information. |The project timeline is pure guesswork at this stage.  这个项目的进度安排在这个阶段纯粹是猜测。|conjecture, speculation, surmise|fact, certainty|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His success is largely guesswork. = 他的成功很大程度上是靠猜测。
Their plans are based on pure guesswork. = 他们的计划完全是基于猜测。

一般过去时:
The detective's conclusion was pure guesswork. = 侦探的结论纯粹是猜测。
We made a decision based on guesswork. = 我们做出的决定是基于猜测的。

现在完成时:
Much of what we know has been guesswork. = 我们所知道的大部分都是猜测。

过去完成时:
