heartlessly:/ˈhɑːrtləsli/ | adj. | 无情地；冷酷地；残忍地 | Showing or feeling no pity or compassion.  |例句：He heartlessly abandoned his family. 他无情地抛弃了他的家人。

例句：She heartlessly ignored his pleas for help. 她冷酷地无视了他求助的恳求。


近义词：cruelly, ruthlessly, pitilessly, unfeelingly, callously

反义词：compassionately, kindly, mercifully, sympathetically, tenderly


词源:

* **heart:**  Old English *heorte*, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *hart* and German *Herz*.  The original meaning related to the core or center of something.  Its association with emotion developed later.

* **-less:** Old English *lēas*, meaning "without," "lacking."  A common suffix forming adjectives.

* **-ly:** Old English *līċe*, meaning "like," forming adverbs.
