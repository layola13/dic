institutions:/ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənz/ | n. | 机构；制度；习俗 |  Established organizations or systems of social, political, or economic life.  Institutions can refer to formal organizations like schools and governments, or informal systems like traditions and customs. | 例句：Educational institutions play a vital role in shaping society.  教育机构在塑造社会方面发挥着至关重要的作用。


例句：Many ancient institutions are still relevant today.  许多古老的制度至今仍然适用。


近义词：organizations, establishments, systems, conventions


反义词：chaos, disorder, anarchy


词性：名词
