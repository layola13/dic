sledging:/ˈslɛdʒɪŋ/| n. |雪橇运动；严厉批评；(板球)故意击打对方球员|The act of riding on a sled;  the act of verbally attacking someone aggressively and persistently; in cricket, deliberately hitting the batsman on the body to discourage them.|例句：The children enjoyed sledging down the snowy hill.孩子们喜欢在雪坡上玩雪橇。|例句：The opposition party engaged in relentless sledging of the prime minister.反对党对首相进行了无情的攻击。|近义词：criticism, verbal abuse, taunting|反义词：praise, compliment
