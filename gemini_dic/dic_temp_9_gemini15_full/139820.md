octogamy:/ɒkˈtɒɡəmi/ | n. |八婚制 |The practice or custom of having eight spouses simultaneously.  This term is rarely used and is primarily found in academic discussions of unusual marriage systems.  It is often used in a theoretical or hypothetical context rather than describing a real-world practice. | Example:  The anthropological study explored the hypothetical societal structures that might arise under a system of octogamy. = 人类学研究探讨了在八婚制制度下可能出现的假设性社会结构。|近义词:polygamy (more general term for multiple spouses)|反义词:monogamy
