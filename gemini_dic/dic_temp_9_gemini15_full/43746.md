creepered:/ˈkriːpərd/| adj.| 爬行的；缓慢移动的；潜行的 |  Describes something that moves slowly and stealthily, often on the ground.  It often implies a creeping or crawling motion, and can be used to describe both animate and inanimate things.  The word is less common than "creeping" but shares a similar meaning. | 例句：The creeper plant slowly made its way up the wall.  这株爬藤植物缓慢地向上攀爬。

The creeper moved silently through the undergrowth.  潜行者悄无声息地穿过灌木丛。


近义词：creeping, crawling, stealthy, insidious | 反义词：swift, rapid, rushing, blatant
