modulated:/ˈmɑːdʒuleɪtɪd/ /ˈmɒdjuleɪtɪd/| adj. v.| 调制的；调整的；变调的 |  Adjusted or altered in some way;  having had its characteristics changed or refined.  It can describe sounds, signals, or even behavior or expression. |例句：The singer modulated her voice to a higher pitch. 歌手把她的声音调高了音调。 The radio signal was modulated to carry the audio information. 无线电信号被调制以携带音频信息。|近义词：varied, adjusted, regulated, tempered|反义词：unmodified, unchanged, constant
