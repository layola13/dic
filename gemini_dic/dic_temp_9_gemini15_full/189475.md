saints:/ˈseɪnts/ | n. |圣徒，圣人 |People who are considered to be holy or virtuous, especially those officially recognized by a church.  |例句：Many saints are revered for their miracles and selfless acts. 许多圣徒因其奇迹和无私的行为而受到尊敬。|例句：The cathedral houses the relics of several saints. 这座大教堂收藏着几位圣徒的遗物。|近义词：holy people, blessed ones, virtuous individuals |反义词：sinners, wicked, unholy
