monographers:/məˈnɒɡrəfəz/| n. |专著作者；撰写专著的人|A monographer is a person who writes monographs, which are scholarly works on a single subject or a specific aspect of a larger topic.  They often involve extensive research and in-depth analysis. |Example: Many renowned historians are monographers, dedicating years to researching and writing detailed studies of specific historical periods or figures.  许多著名的历史学家都是专著作者，他们致力于研究和撰写特定历史时期或人物的详细研究。|近义词：scholar, researcher, specialist, author (of monographs)|反义词：non-specialist, generalist|名词
