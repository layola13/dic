headhunt:/ˈhɛdˌhʌnt/| vt.|猎头；挖角|To recruit (someone, especially a person with skills and experience highly valued by others) by actively searching for suitable candidates, often persuading them to leave their current job.|例句：The company headhunted her from a rival firm.  这家公司从竞争对手公司挖走了她。|例句：They headhunted him for his expertise in artificial intelligence. 他们因为他在人工智能方面的专业知识而挖角他。|近义词：recruit, poach, entice|反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company headhunts top talent from all over the world. = 这家公司在全球范围内猎取顶尖人才。
She headhunts for a leading technology firm. = 她为一家领先的科技公司猎头。


一般过去时:
They headhunted him from a smaller company. = 他们把他从一家小公司挖了过来。
The startup headhunted several experienced engineers. = 这家初创公司挖走了几名经验丰富的工程师。


现在完成时:
They have headhunted several key executives this year. = 他们今年已经挖走了几名关键高管。
The firm has headhunted aggressively to expand its team. = 这家公司积极地猎头以扩大其团队。


过去完成时:
Before joining our team, she had been headhunted by several other companies. = 在加入我们团队之前，她已被其他几家公司挖过角。
The CEO had already been headhunted by a competitor before he made his decision. = 在做出决定之前，首席执行官已经被竞争对手挖过角了。


将来时:
They will headhunt new candidates for the open position next month. = 下个月他们将为空缺职位物色新的候选人。
We will likely headhunt from our competitor's team. = 我们可能会从竞争对手的团队中挖角。


虚拟语气:
If they headhunted me, I would consider it. = 如果他们挖我，我会考虑的。
I wish they had headhunted me sooner. = 我希望他们早点挖我。


被动语态:
He was headhunted by a large multinational corporation. = 他被一家大型跨国公司挖走了。
Many talented individuals are headhunted every year. = 每年都有许多人才被挖走。


疑问句:
Were you headhunted for your current role? = 你是为你的现任职位被挖走的吗？
Has she ever been headhunted before? = 她以前被挖过角吗？


否定句:
They didn't headhunt anyone from that company. = 他们没有从那家公司挖任何人。
The company hasn't headhunted anyone recently. = 公司最近没有挖任何人。
