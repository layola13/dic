semeiography:/ˌsɛmiːaɪˈɒɡrəfi/| n. | 符号学; 记号学 | The study of signs and symbols and their use or interpretation.  It focuses on the relationship between signs, their meanings, and the users of those signs.  Semeiography explores how meaning is constructed and communicated through various semiotic systems, including language, images, and gestures. | Example:  Semeiography is a crucial field for understanding how meaning is created and conveyed in different cultures.  符号学是理解不同文化中意义是如何创造和传达的关键领域。 | 近义词：semiotics, semiology | 反义词：无
