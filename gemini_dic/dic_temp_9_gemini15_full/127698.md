negators:/nɪˈɡeɪtərz/ | n. |否定词；否定者 |Words or phrases that express negation or denial.  They reverse or cancel the meaning of a statement. Examples include "not," "no," "never," "neither," and "none."|The negators in the sentence made it difficult to understand the true meaning.  否定句中的否定词使句子难以理解其真实含义。|近义词：deniers, contradictors |反义词：affirmers, confirmers |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He does not like broccoli. = 他不喜欢西兰花。
They are not going to the party. = 他们不去参加派对。


一般过去时:
She did not finish her homework. = 她没有完成作业。
We were not at home yesterday. = 我们昨天不在家。


现在完成时:
I have not seen him recently. = 我最近没见过他。
They haven't finished the project yet. = 他们还没完成项目。


过去完成时:
He had not eaten all day. = 他一整天都没吃东西。
She hadn't realized her mistake. = 她还没意识到自己的错误。


将来时:
We will not be able to attend. = 我们将无法参加。
They won't go to the concert. = 他们不会去音乐会。


虚拟语气:
If I were not tired, I would go out. = 如果我不累，我会出去。
I wish I had not said that. = 我真希望我没有说过那样的话。


被动语态:
The problem was not solved. = 问题没有解决。
The work is not being done properly. = 工作没有做好。


疑问句:
Isn't it beautiful? = 它不漂亮吗？
Doesn't he know the answer? = 他不知道答案吗？


否定句:
I don't understand. = 我不明白。
She didn't come to class. = 她没来上课。
