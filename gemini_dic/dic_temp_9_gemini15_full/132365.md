nondelicately: /ˌnɒnˈdɛlɪkətli/ | adv. | 不微妙地；粗鲁地；不客气地 |  The word "nondelicately" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adverb "delicately," which means "in a delicate manner." Therefore, "nondelicately" signifies a lack of delicacy or finesse. It suggests actions or words that are blunt, insensitive, or tactless. | 例句：He nondelicately pointed out her grammatical errors in front of everyone. 他当着所有人的面不客气地指出她的语法错误。 He handled the situation nondelicately, causing further offense. 他处理事情的方式很粗鲁，导致进一步的冒犯。| 近义词：crudely, bluntly, roughly, tactlessly, insensitively | 反义词：delicately, subtly, tactfully, sensitively |  词性：副词
