downscale:/ˈdaʊnˌskeɪl/| vt. adj.|缩小规模；降低档次；简化；|To reduce the scale or size of something; to lower the quality or grade of something.|例句：The company had to downscale its operations due to the economic downturn. 公司由于经济衰退不得不缩减规模。 The hotel downscaled its services to cut costs.  酒店简化了服务以降低成本。|近义词：reduce, scale down, simplify, downgrade|反义词：upscale, enlarge, upgrade|动词，形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They downscale their marketing campaign. = 他们缩小了他们的市场营销活动规模。
The restaurant downscales its menu. = 这家餐厅简化了菜单。

一般过去时:
The factory downscaled production last year. = 工厂去年缩减了产量。
She downscaled her life after retirement. = 她退休后简化了生活。

现在完成时:
They have downscaled their operations significantly. = 他们已经大幅度缩减了业务规模。
The company has downscaled its workforce. = 公司已经裁员了。

过去完成时:
The project had been downscaled before the funding was cut. = 在资金被削减之前，项目规模已经被缩小了。
They had downscaled their expectations. = 他们已经降低了预期。

将来时:
We will downscale the software for mobile devices. = 我们将为移动设备简化软件。
The government will downscale its spending on infrastructure. = 政府将削减基础设施建设支出。

虚拟语气:
If the market conditions worsen, we would downscale production. = 如果市场状况恶化，我们将缩减产量。
I wish they had not downscaled the project so drastically. = 我希望他们没有那么大幅度地缩减项目。

被动语态:
The plan was downscaled due to budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，计划被缩小了。
The image was downscaled for faster loading. = 图片为了更快加载而被缩小了。

疑问句:
Will they downscale the project? = 他们会缩减项目吗？
Has the company downscaled its operations? = 公司是否已经缩减了业务规模？

否定句:
We did not downscale our marketing efforts. = 我们没有缩减我们的营销工作。
They will not downscale the product line. = 他们不会缩减产品线。
