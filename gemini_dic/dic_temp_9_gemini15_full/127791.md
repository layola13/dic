neighbouress:/ˈneɪbəres/| n.|女邻居|A female neighbor.  A woman who lives near another.|例句：The neighbouress next door baked us a delicious cake.  隔壁的女邻居给我们烤了一个美味的蛋糕。

例句：I borrowed a cup of sugar from the neighbouress across the street. 我从街对面的女邻居那里借了一杯糖。


近义词：female neighbor, woman next door|反义词：None|词性：名词

词源: neighbour (邻近的人) + -ess (表示女性的后缀)  The suffix "-ess" is a common way to create feminine forms of nouns in English.
