re-designation:/ˌriːˌdeziɡˈneɪʃən/| n. |重新指定；改名；改称 |The act of assigning a new designation or name to something.  It involves changing the existing classification, title, or role of a person, place, thing, or concept.  |Example: The re-designation of the park as a wildlife sanctuary was met with mixed reactions. 公园重新指定为野生动物保护区，引起了各种不同的反应。|近义词：reassignment, renaming, reclassification |反义词：original designation, initial naming

The company is undergoing a re-designation of its departments. = 公司正在对其部门进行重新划分。
This project requires a re-designation of resources. = 这个项目需要重新分配资源。
