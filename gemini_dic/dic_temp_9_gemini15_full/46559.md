dally:/ˈdæli/ | v. | 浪费时间；磨蹭；闲荡 | To waste time; to dawdle; to spend time idly. |例句：He dallied over his breakfast. 他慢慢悠悠地吃着早餐。  |例句：Don't dally; we're already late. 别磨蹭了，我们已经迟了。 |近义词：dawdle, linger, loiter, procrastinate | 反义词：hurry, rush

They dally in the park every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在公园里闲逛。

She dallied on her way home. = 她回家路上磨蹭。
We dallied too long at the beach. = 我们在海滩上逗留太久了。

They had dallied with several plans before deciding on this one. = 他们在决定这个计划之前考虑过几个计划。
