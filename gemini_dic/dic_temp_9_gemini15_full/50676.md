derma:/ˈdɜːrmə/| n. |皮肤；真皮|The outermost layer of skin, the epidermis, protects the underlying dermis.  真皮是皮肤最外层，表皮保护着下面的真皮。|近义词：skin, cutis |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.  The usage is primarily descriptive.

Example 1: The dermatologist examined the patient's derma for signs of infection. 皮肤科医生检查了病人的皮肤是否有感染迹象。

Example 2:  The new cream is designed to improve derma elasticity.  这种新霜旨在改善皮肤弹性。
