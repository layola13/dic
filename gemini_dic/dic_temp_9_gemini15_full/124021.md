morosaurus:/ˌmɔːrəˈsɔːrəs/ | n. |迷惑龙|A genus of large, quadrupedal, herbivorous dinosaurs from the Late Jurassic period.  Morosaurs were characterized by their long necks and tails, relatively small heads, and robust bodies. They were among the largest animals to ever walk the Earth. |Example:  The Morosaurus grazed peacefully on the ferns and cycads. 迷惑龙平静地吃着蕨类植物和苏铁。|近义词:  (other sauropods, e.g.,  Brachiosaurus, Apatosaurus) |反义词: N/A |词性:n.
