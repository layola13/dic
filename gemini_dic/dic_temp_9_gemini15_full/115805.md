mark:/mɑːrk/| /mɑːk/| n., v.| 标记，记号；做标记；得分；显著，突出 | To make a mark or sign on something; to notice or remember something; a symbol or sign; a grade or score; a noticeable or significant characteristic. |例句：Please mark your name on the paper. 请在纸上写上你的名字。 He marked the important points in the book. 他在书中标注了重点。 He got a high mark in the exam. 他考试得了高分.  His kindness marked him as a true gentleman. 他的善良使他成为一个真正的绅士。|近义词：sign, label, score, note, denote |反义词：ignore, overlook, erase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He marks his work carefully. 他仔细地批改作业。
She marks the important dates on her calendar. 她把重要的日期标在日历上。

一般过去时:
I marked the package fragile. 我在包裹上标明易碎。
The teacher marked the students' tests. 老师批改了学生的试卷。

现在完成时:
He has marked all the important passages in the book. 他已经标注了书中所有重要的段落。
She has marked a significant improvement in her work. 她的工作有了显著的改进。

过去完成时:
I had marked the map before we set off. 我们出发前我已经标注了地图。
They had marked the answer sheets before the students submitted them. 学生提交试卷之前，他们已经批改了答案纸。


将来时:
I will mark the papers tomorrow. 我明天将批改试卷。
She will mark the important points with a highlighter. 她将用荧光笔标注重点。


虚拟语气:
If I had marked the package correctly, it wouldn't have been lost. 如果我正确地标注了包裹，它就不会丢失了。
I wish I had marked the exam questions more carefully. 我真希望我更仔细地标注考试题目。


被动语态:
The papers are marked by the teacher. 试卷由老师批改。
The important points were marked in red. 重要之处用红色标注。


疑问句:
How do you mark the answers? 你如何批改答案？
Where did he mark the location? 他在哪里标注了位置？


否定句:
He didn't mark the essay. 他没有批改这篇论文。
She didn't mark the answer correct. 她没有把答案标为正确。


PHRASE->
mark down: 打折扣；记下
mark time: 踏步；原地踏步
mark up: 加价；在…上做记号
make one's mark: 出名；成功
trade mark: 商标
