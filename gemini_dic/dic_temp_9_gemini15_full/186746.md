They are characterized by their inflexibility and uncompromising adherence to standards.|The professor was a rigorist when it came to grading, giving no partial credit for incomplete answers.  这位教授在评分方面非常严格，不给不完整的答案任何部分分数。|近义词：puritan, ascetic, disciplinarian|反义词：lenient, lax, permissive|名词
