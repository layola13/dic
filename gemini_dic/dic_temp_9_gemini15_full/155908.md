photochronography:/ˌfoʊtoʊkrɒˈnɒɡrəfi/ | n. |摄影连续摄影术，光影连续摄影术 | The process of recording a series of photographs to show movement or change over time.  This technique is often used to study motion and create animations. | Example: Eadweard Muybridge's pioneering work in photochronography revolutionized the understanding of animal locomotion.  爱德华·迈布里奇在摄影连续摄影术方面的开创性工作彻底改变了人们对动物运动的理解。|近义词：time-lapse photography, chronophotography | 反义词：无
