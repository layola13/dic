muscule:/ˈmʌs(k)əl/| n.|肌肉|A band of fibrous tissue in a human or animal body that has the ability to contract, producing movement in or maintaining the position of parts of the body.|例句：He flexed his muscles to show his strength. 他弯曲他的肌肉以展示他的力量。|近义词：muscle|反义词:None.
