clione:/ˈklaɪoʊn/| n. |海天使，裸海蝶|A clione is a small, free-swimming pelagic sea slug.  They are also known as sea angels and are characterized by their delicate, transparent bodies and wing-like parapodia used for locomotion.  They are carnivorous, feeding primarily on other sea slugs. | Example:  The researchers observed a large swarm of cliones in the Arctic waters.  研究人员在北极水域观察到一大群海天使。  |近义词:sea angel, gymnosome (scientific name)|反义词: N/A
