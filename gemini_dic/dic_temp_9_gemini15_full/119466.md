micrometer:/maɪˈkrɒmɪtər/|/ˈmaɪkroʊmiːtər/| n. |微米；千分尺|A micrometer is a device for measuring very small distances, typically in micrometers (millionths of a meter).  It can also refer to a unit of length equal to one millionth of a meter.|例句：The thickness of the wire was measured with a micrometer.  这根线的厚度是用千分尺测量的。 The bacteria were only a few micrometers in length. 细菌只有几微米长。|近义词：micron, micrōn|反义词：kilometer, mile
