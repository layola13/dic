drupel:/ˈdruːpəl/| n. |核果，单果|A drupe is a type of fruit in which an outer fleshy part (exocarp and mesocarp) surrounds a hard shell (endocarp) that contains a seed.  The hard shell is often called a pit or stone.  Examples include peaches, plums, cherries, and olives.| Examples:Peaches are a delicious example of a drupe.  桃子是核果的一个美味例子。The olive is a type of drupe that is widely used in cooking. 橄榄是一种广泛用于烹饪的核果。|近义词：stone fruit, drupaceous fruit|反义词： pome (apple, pear), berry
