interconnect:/ˌɪntərˈkənɛkt/ | vt.  | 使互连；使相互关联 | To connect two or more things together.  | The new highway system interconnects all the major cities. 新的高速公路系统连接了所有主要城市。|近义词：link, connect, network | 反义词：disconnect, separate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fiber optic cables interconnect the servers.  光纤电缆连接着服务器。
The railway lines interconnect various cities across the country.  铁路连接着全国各地的城市。

一般过去时:
The engineers interconnected the two systems last week. 工程师们上周将这两个系统互连起来。
The company interconnected its various departments through a new intranet. 公司通过新的内联网将各个部门互联起来。

现在完成时:
They have interconnected the entire network successfully. 他们已经成功地互联了整个网络。
We have interconnected all the buildings in the campus with high-speed internet. 我们已经用高速互联网将校园里的所有建筑物互连起来。

过去完成时:
Before the upgrade, they had interconnected the systems using older technology.  升级之前，他们使用旧技术将系统互连起来。
The team had interconnected the databases before the merging of the two companies. 两家公司合并之前，团队已经将数据库互连起来。

将来时:
We will interconnect the new building to the existing network next month.  下个月我们将把新建筑连接到现有网络。
The company plans to interconnect all its branches by the end of the year.  公司计划在年底前将其所有分支机构互联起来。

虚拟语气:
If we interconnected the systems, we could share data more efficiently. 如果我们将系统互连起来，我们可以更高效地共享数据。
It would be beneficial if we interconnected the two departments. 如果我们将这两个部门互连起来将大有裨益。

被动语态:
The systems are interconnected by high-speed fiber optic cables. 系统通过高速光纤电缆互连。
The new software will be interconnected with the existing database. 新软件将与现有数据库互连。

疑问句:
Are the two buildings interconnected? 这两座建筑物互连了吗？
How are the various components interconnected?  各个组件是如何互连的？

否定句:
The two networks are not interconnected. 这两个网络没有互连。
The old system was not interconnected with the new one. 旧系统没有与新系统互连。
