/ˈmædʒɪstri/| n. |权威；统治；炼金术；精湛技艺|The supreme power or authority; the art or process of transmuting base metals into gold;  a high degree of skill or artistry.|例句：The magistery of the alchemist was legendary.  炼金术士的权威是传奇般的。

例句：Her magistery in painting was undeniable.  她在绘画方面的精湛技艺是无可否认的。


近义词：authority, mastery, skill, expertise | 反义词：incompetence, ineptitude


词源:  From Medieval Latin *magisterium, from Latin magister "master, teacher".  The sense of "supreme authority" developed from the use of "magisterium" to refer to the teaching authority of the Church. The alchemical sense evolved from the idea of the master alchemist possessing the skill to achieve transmutation.
