gruffs:/ɡrʌfs/| adj.|粗暴的；粗鲁的；严厉的 |gruff means abrupt and rough in manner. It often describes a person's voice or behavior.  A gruff person is not necessarily unkind, but they may come across as unfriendly or unapproachable due to their harsh manner.|例句：He had a gruff voice and a surly expression. 他声音粗暴，表情不耐烦。

例句：The old man's gruff exterior hid a kind heart.  这位老人的粗鲁外表掩盖了一颗善良的心。|近义词：rough, harsh, blunt, surly, curt|反义词：gentle, kind, polite, soft, mild|
