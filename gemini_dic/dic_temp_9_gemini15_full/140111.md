off-puttingly:/ˌɒfˈpʊtɪŋli/ | adv. |令人厌恶地，令人反感地 |  Describes something that is unpleasant or repulsive and causes someone to lose interest or be disgusted. It emphasizes the effect of something causing a negative reaction. |  The strong smell of fish was off-puttingly strong.  那股强烈的鱼腥味令人作呕。

The persistently loud music was off-puttingly jarring.  那持续不断的吵闹音乐令人极其烦躁。|  disgustingly, repulsively, unpleasantly | pleasingly, attractively |  副词
