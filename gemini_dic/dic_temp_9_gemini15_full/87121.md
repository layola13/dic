herbalist:/ˈɜːrbəlɪst/| n. |草药医生，草药学家|A person who practices herbalism; someone skilled in the use of herbs for medicinal purposes.  |例句：The herbalist recommended a tea made from chamomile to soothe my anxiety.  草药医生建议我喝一杯用甘菊制成的茶来缓解焦虑。|例句：She studied to become a certified herbalist, specializing in traditional Chinese medicine. 她学习成为一名认证的草药师，专门从事中医。|近义词：herbal practitioner, herbologist |反义词:allopathic physician (conventional medicine doctor)
