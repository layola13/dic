overcomer:/ˈoʊvərˌkʌmər/ | n. |克服困难者，战胜者 |A person who has successfully overcome a difficulty or challenge.  Someone who triumphs over adversity. |She is a true overcomer, having battled cancer and emerged stronger. 她是一位真正的战胜者，她战胜了癌症，变得更加强大。| victor, conqueror, survivor |defeated, vanquished
