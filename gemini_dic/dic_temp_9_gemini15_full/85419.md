harvestless: /ˈhɑːvɪstləs/ | adj. | 歉收的；无收获的 |  Lacking a harvest; unproductive.  Describes a situation where there is no crop or yield from farming or other productive activities. |

例句：
The farmer faced a harvestless year due to the drought. = 由于干旱，这位农民遭遇了歉收的一年。

The harvestless fields were a stark reminder of the harsh winter. = 毫无收成的田地残酷地提醒着人们严酷的冬天。


近义词：barren, unproductive, fruitless, sterile

反义词：bountiful, fruitful, productive, abundant


词性:形容词
