sacking:/ˈsækɪŋ/| n. v.|解雇；麻布；编织；|The act of dismissing someone from their job; coarse fabric made from jute or similar fibers.  |例句：His sacking from the company came as a shock. 他被公司解雇的消息令人震惊。|例句：The old building was covered in sacking. 那座旧建筑物被麻布覆盖着。|近义词：dismissal, firing, discharge (解雇); burlap (麻布)|反义词：hiring, employment, appointment (解雇的反义词)|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are sacking employees due to economic downturn. = 由于经济衰退，他们正在解雇员工。
He sacks many workers every year. = 他每年解雇许多工人。


一般过去时:
The company sacked him for misconduct. = 公司因行为不端而解雇了他。
They sacked the entire marketing team. = 他们解雇了整个市场营销团队。


现在完成时:
They have sacked several managers this year. = 今年他们已经解雇了几位经理。
She has been sacked twice in her career. = 她在她职业生涯中被解雇过两次。


过去完成时:
He had been sacked before he started his own business. = 在他开始自己的生意之前，他已经失业了。
The company had sacked many workers before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经解雇了许多工人。


将来时:
They will sack him if he continues to perform poorly. = 如果他继续表现不佳，他们将会解雇他。
The company will probably sack more workers next quarter. = 公司下个季度可能会解雇更多工人。


虚拟语气:
If they sacked him, the project would fail. = 如果他们解雇了他，项目就会失败。
I wish they hadn't sacked him; he was a valuable employee. = 我真希望他们没有解雇他；他是一位宝贵的员工。


被动语态:
He was sacked for his incompetence. = 他因不称职而被解雇。
Many workers were sacked during the restructuring. = 在重组期间，许多工人被解雇。


疑问句:
Will they be sacking anyone else? = 他们还会解雇其他人吗？
Have they sacked the project manager yet? = 他们解雇项目经理了吗？


否定句:
They didn't sack him, despite his mistakes. = 尽管他犯了错误，他们也没有解雇他。
She wasn't sacked; she resigned. = 她没有被解雇；她是辞职的。
