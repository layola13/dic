emerges:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒ/| vi.|出现；浮现；显露；兴起|To come out of or into view; to become apparent or prominent.|例句：The sun emerged from behind the clouds. 太阳从云后出现。  The truth eventually emerged. 真相最终大白。|近义词：appear, arise, surface, develop|反义词：disappear, vanish, subside


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun emerges from behind the clouds every morning. = 太阳每天早上从云层后面出现。
A new problem emerges every day. = 每天都会出现新的问题。


一般过去时:
The seedlings emerged from the soil after a week. = 幼苗一周后从土壤中冒了出来。
A new leader emerged from the chaos. = 一个新的领导者从混乱中脱颖而出。


现在完成时:
Several new trends have emerged in the past few years. = 在过去的几年里出现了几种新的趋势。
The truth has finally emerged after years of investigation. = 多年调查之后，真相终于大白。


过去完成时:
After the storm had subsided, the city slowly emerged from the darkness. = 暴风雨过后，城市慢慢地从黑暗中浮现出来。
It emerged that he had been lying all along. = 结果发现他一直在撒谎。


将来时:
New technologies will emerge in the next decade. = 未来十年将出现新技术。
The results of the experiment will emerge next week. = 实验结果将在下周公布。


虚拟语气:
If a new leader emerged, the situation might improve. = 如果出现一位新的领导者，情况可能会好转。
I wish a solution would emerge soon. = 我希望很快能找到解决办法。


被动语态:
The issue was not fully emerged until the meeting. = 直到会议上，这个问题才被充分展现出来。（此处被动语态较为生硬，主动语态更常用）
The details of the scandal are slowly being emerged. = 丑闻的细节正在慢慢浮出水面。（此处被动语态较为生硬，主动语态更常用）


疑问句:
When will the winner emerge? = 冠军何时产生？
Will a solution emerge to this problem? = 会出现解决这个问题的方案吗？


否定句:
No new evidence emerged during the trial. = 审判期间没有出现新的证据。
The truth did not emerge until years later. = 真相直到数年后才真相大白。


PHRASE->
emerge from = 从…出现
emerge victorious = 胜利出现
emerge unscathed = 安然无恙
emerge as = 成为
emerge onto = 走向
