outdistanced:/ˌaʊtˈdɪstənst/| v.|超越，超过，抛在后面|To go faster than and leave behind (someone or something).  It implies a significant lead gained.|例句：The marathon runner outdistanced all his competitors.  这位马拉松运动员超过了所有竞争对手。

例句：Our sales figures have significantly outdistanced those of our competitors this quarter.  本季度我们的销售额已大幅超过竞争对手。


近义词：surpass, exceed, overtake, leave behind, eclipse

反义词：lag behind, trail, fall behind

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outdistances his rivals in the race. 他在比赛中超过了他的对手。
The company outdistances its competitors in innovation. 这家公司在创新方面超越了竞争对手。


一般过去时:
She outdistanced her opponents in the debate.  她在辩论中胜过了对手。
The new technology outdistanced the old methods. 新技术超越了旧方法。


现在完成时:
The team has outdistanced all expectations.  这个团队已经超出了所有预期。
Our progress has outdistanced our initial projections. 我们的进展已经超过了我们最初的预测。


过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outdistanced all the other runners.  比赛结束时，他已经超过了所有其他跑步者。
The company had outdistanced its rivals in market share before the merger. 这家公司在合并之前就已经在市场份额上超过了竞争对手。


将来时:
They will outdistance their opponents in the next election.  他们将在下次选举中超过对手。
We believe that our new product will outdistance the competition. 我们相信我们的新产品将胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would outdistance his rivals. 如果他训练更努力，他就能超过对手。
If we had invested more, we could have outdistanced our competitors. 如果我们投资更多，我们本可以超越竞争对手。


被动语态:
He was outdistanced by the other runners in the final lap. 在最后一圈，他被其他跑步者超过了。
Our plans were outdistanced by unforeseen circumstances. 我们的计划被不可预见的情况超越了。


疑问句:
Did he outdistance the others in the race? 他在比赛中超过其他人了吗？
Will our company outdistance the competition in the long run? 从长远来看，我们的公司会超越竞争对手吗？


否定句:
He didn't outdistance his opponent. 他没有超过他的对手。
The team failed to outdistance their rivals.  这个团队未能超越对手。
