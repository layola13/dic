russets:/ˈrʌsɪts/| n. adj.|红褐色，带红褐色的；（尤指苹果）红褐色的|Reddish-brown in color, especially of apples.  The word can refer to a type of apple or the color itself.|例句：The russets on the trees were ripe for picking.  树上的红褐色苹果成熟可以采摘了。|例句：Her hair was a beautiful shade of russet. 她的头发是美丽的红褐色。|近义词：reddish-brown, auburn, brownish-red|反义词：pale, light
