demonising: /ˈdiːmənʌɪzɪŋ/ | v. |妖魔化；诽谤；丑化 | To represent someone or something as evil or wicked, especially in a way that is unfair or untrue.  | 例句：The media is demonising the protesters, portraying them as violent extremists.  媒体正在妖魔化抗议者，将他们描绘成暴力极端分子。


例句：The politician's opponents are trying to demonise him by spreading false rumours.  这位政客的对手试图通过散布谣言来妖魔化他。


近义词：vilify, denigrate, slander, smear, blacken


反义词：glorify, praise, extol, commend
