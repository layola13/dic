revolutionize: /ˌrevəˈluːʃənaɪz/ | vt. |彻底改变；革新；使发生革命性变化 | To change something completely, often in a way that improves it. |例句：The invention of the internet revolutionized communication.  互联网的发明彻底改变了通讯方式。|例句：The new technology has revolutionized the manufacturing process. 新技术彻底改变了制造流程。|近义词：transform, reform, modernize, innovate |反义词：maintain, preserve, stagnate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new software revolutionizes the workflow. = 新软件彻底改变了工作流程。
This discovery revolutionizes our understanding of the universe. = 这一发现彻底改变了我们对宇宙的理解。


一般过去时:
The industrial revolution revolutionized society. = 工业革命彻底改变了社会。
The invention of the printing press revolutionized the spread of information. = 印刷术的发明彻底改变了信息的传播方式。


现在完成时:
The internet has revolutionized how we access information. = 互联网已经彻底改变了我们获取信息的方式。
Recent advances in medicine have revolutionized the treatment of many diseases. = 近年来医学的进步彻底改变了许多疾病的治疗方法。


过去完成时:
By the time the war ended, the invention of the airplane had revolutionized warfare. = 到战争结束时，飞机的发明已经彻底改变了战争模式。
Before the reforms, the education system had not been revolutionized for decades. = 在改革之前，教育体系几十年来都没有发生革命性的变化。


将来时:
Artificial intelligence will revolutionize many industries in the coming decades. = 在未来几十年里，人工智能将会彻底改变许多行业。
This new technology promises to revolutionize transportation. = 这项新技术有望彻底改变交通运输。


虚拟语气:
If we adopted this new approach, it would revolutionize our productivity. = 如果我们采用这种新方法，它将彻底改变我们的生产力。
I wish they would revolutionize the outdated system. = 我希望他们能彻底改革过时的系统。


被动语态:
The industry was revolutionized by the introduction of automation. = 该行业因引进自动化而发生了彻底变革。
The educational system is being revolutionized by technology. = 教育体系正在被技术所彻底改变。


疑问句:
Will this new policy revolutionize the healthcare system? = 这项新政策会彻底改变医疗体系吗？
How can we revolutionize the way we approach this problem? = 我们如何才能彻底改变我们解决这个问题的方式？


否定句:
The proposed changes did not revolutionize the company's performance. = 提出的变更并没有彻底改变公司的业绩。
The new product failed to revolutionize the market. = 新产品未能彻底改变市场。


PHRASE->
revolutionize the way = 彻底改变…的方式
revolutionize the industry = 彻底改变行业
revolutionize the process = 彻底改变流程
revolutionize education = 彻底改变教育
revolutionize healthcare = 彻底改变医疗保健


词源:
revolution + -ize  (动词后缀)

revolution 来自于拉丁语 *revolution-,  revolutionis (旋转，循环)，源于 revolvere (旋转，翻转)。
-ize 是一个动词后缀，表示"使...化"，"使成为..."。
因此，revolutionize 字面意思是“使…发生革命性的变化”。
