pyritize:/ˈpaɪrɪtaɪz/ | vt. | 使黄铁矿化；用黄铁矿处理 | To convert (something) into pyrite or a pyritic substance.  The process often involves the impregnation of a substance with iron sulfide. |例句：The process pyritizes the rock, making it harder and more resistant to weathering.  这个过程使岩石黄铁矿化，使其更坚硬，更耐风化。

The ancient mine shafts were pyritised by the ingress of groundwater. 古代矿井因地下水渗入而发生黄铁矿化。

近义词：sulfurize, mineralize | 反义词：de-pyritize  | 词性：动词
