plea-bargains:/ˈpliː ˈbɑːrɡɪnz/ | n. |认罪协议；认罪协商 | An agreement in a criminal case in which a defendant agrees to plead guilty to a lesser charge or to a reduced sentence in exchange for avoiding a trial.  | Example: The defendant accepted a plea bargain to avoid a lengthy trial.  被告接受了认罪协议以避免冗长的审判。  The prosecutor offered a plea bargain to the defendant, reducing the charges in exchange for a guilty plea.  检察官向被告提供了认罪协议，以换取认罪而减轻指控。 |近义词：negotiated settlement, compromise |反义词：trial, full sentence
