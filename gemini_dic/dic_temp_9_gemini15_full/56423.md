dollars:/ˈdɑːlərz/| n. |美元；金钱|The basic unit of currency in the United States and several other countries.  It is often used informally to refer to money in general. |例句：The car cost me ten thousand dollars. 这辆车花了我一万美元。

例句：I need some dollars to buy groceries. 我需要一些美元买杂货。


近义词：money, funds, cash


反义词：debt, owing


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He earns a lot of dollars. = 他赚很多钱。
How many dollars do you have? = 你有多少美元？


一般过去时:
She spent all her dollars on clothes. = 她把所有的钱都花在了衣服上。
They gave me ten dollars for my help. = 他们给了我十美元作为报酬。


现在完成时:
I have earned a lot of dollars this year. = 今年我赚了很多钱。
She has saved several thousand dollars. = 她已经存了几千美元。


过去完成时:
He had already spent all his dollars before he arrived home. = 他回家之前已经把所有的钱都花光了。
They had earned enough dollars to buy a house. = 他们已经赚够钱买房子了。


将来时:
I will earn more dollars next year. = 明年我会赚更多钱。
She will spend her dollars wisely. = 她会明智地花她的钱。


虚拟语气:
If I had more dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我有更多钱，我会环游世界。
I wish I had enough dollars to buy that car. = 我真希望我有足够的钱买那辆车。


被动语态:
The price was quoted in dollars. = 价格以美元报出。
The goods were paid for in dollars. = 货物是用美元支付的。


疑问句:
How many dollars did you pay? = 你付了多少钱？
What is the price in dollars? = 价格是多少美元？


否定句:
I don't have enough dollars to buy that. = 我没有足够的钱买那个。
He didn't spend his dollars carelessly. = 他没有随意花钱。
