commiserations:/kəˌmɪzəˈreɪʃənz/| n.|慰问；同情；怜悯|Expressions of sympathy and understanding towards someone who is suffering or has experienced misfortune.  It often implies a shared feeling of sorrow or regret.|例句：My commiserations on your failed exam.  = 你考试不及格，我很同情。

例句：Please accept my commiserations on the loss of your pet. = 请接受我对您失去宠物的慰问。


近义词：sympathy, condolences, empathy, pity

反义词：celebration, rejoicing, gloating

词性:名词
