pet:/pet/| n. v.|宠物；宠爱；轻抚|A domestic or tamed animal, kept for companionship or pleasure; to treat someone with excessive indulgence.|例句：My cat is my beloved pet.  我的猫是我的心爱宠物。
例句：She pets her dog every evening. 她每天晚上都抚摸她的狗。|近义词：animal, darling, pamper|反义词：neglect, ignore|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pets his cat every day. 他每天都抚摸他的猫。
She keeps a pet hamster. 她养了一只宠物仓鼠。


一般过去时:
I petted the dog gently. 我轻轻地抚摸那只狗。
They kept a pet bird in a cage. 他们在一个笼子里养了一只宠物鸟。


现在完成时:
I have petted that dog many times. 我已经抚摸过那只狗很多次了。
She has had a pet snake for five years. 她养了一条宠物蛇五年了。


过去完成时:
I had petted the cat before it ran away. 在它跑掉之前我抚摸过那只猫。
They had kept a pet parrot for ten years before it died. 在它死之前，他们养了一只宠物鹦鹉十年了。


将来时:
I will pet the rabbit tomorrow. 我明天会摸摸兔子。
She will get a pet fish for her birthday. 她生日会得到一条宠物鱼。
