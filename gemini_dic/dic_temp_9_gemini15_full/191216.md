scarpering:/ˈskɑːpərɪŋ/| v.|逃跑，溜走，迅速离开 |To run away quickly; to flee.  It often implies a hurried or secretive departure.|例句：The thieves scarpered when they saw the police. 小偷看到警察就逃跑了。

例句：The children scarpered off to play as soon as school was over. 放学后孩子们就跑出去玩了。


近义词：running away, fleeing, bolting, making a run for it, absconding

反义词：staying, remaining, lingering
