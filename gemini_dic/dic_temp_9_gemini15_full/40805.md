convoluted:/ˈkɒnvəluːtɪd/ /ˈkɑːnvəluːtɪd/ | adj. | 复杂的；费解的；盘绕的 | extremely complex and difficult to follow;  involved; intricate.  The plot of the novel was so convoluted that I lost track of the characters. |  intricate, complex, tangled, involved | simple, straightforward, clear, easy


例句：

The instructions were so convoluted that I couldn't understand them.  = 说明书太复杂了，我无法理解。

The lawyer's arguments were convoluted and difficult to follow. = 律师的论点复杂难懂。


词源：

From Middle English *convoluten, from Latin convolūtus, past participle of convolvere "to roll together," from com- "together" + volvere "to roll."  The sense evolved from the literal meaning of "rolled up" or "twisted" to the figurative meaning of "complex and difficult to understand."


近义词：

intricate, complex, tangled, involved, tortuous, labyrinthine


反义词：

simple, straightforward, clear, easy, uncomplicated, direct
