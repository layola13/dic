caddie:/ˈkædi/ | n. |球童；球车；行李员 | A caddie is a person who carries a golfer's clubs during a round of golf.  They may also provide other assistance, such as cleaning clubs or offering advice on club selection. | Example:  He hired a caddie to carry his clubs around the course. 他雇了一个球童来为他搬运球杆。  |近义词： assistant, helper | 反义词：N/A

He often advises on club selection. = 他经常就球杆选择提出建议。
