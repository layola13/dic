paramagnetic:/ˌpæræˌmæɡˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|顺磁性的|Exhibiting paramagnetism; possessing a small positive magnetic susceptibility.  A paramagnetic substance is weakly attracted to a magnetic field. The magnetism is caused by the unpaired electrons in the atoms of the material.  This effect is temporary and disappears when the external magnetic field is removed.|例句：Oxygen is a paramagnetic gas. 氧气是一种顺磁性气体。

The element manganese exhibits paramagnetic properties at room temperature. 锰元素在室温下表现出顺磁性。


近义词：weakly magnetic|反义词：diamagnetic, ferromagnetic
