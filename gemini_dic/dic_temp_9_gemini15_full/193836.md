self-devised:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈvaɪzd/| adj.|自行设计的；自己想出的|Designed or created by oneself, without external assistance or guidance.  It implies originality and independence in the creation process.|例句：He used a self-devised method to solve the problem. 他用自己想出的方法解决了这个问题。

例句：Her self-devised training regimen helped her achieve her fitness goals. 她自行设计的训练计划帮助她实现了健身目标。


近义词：homemade, homegrown, original, independent, self-created


反义词：prefabricated, conventional, standard, commercially-produced


词性:形容词
