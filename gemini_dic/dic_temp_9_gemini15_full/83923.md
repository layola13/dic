half-buried: /ˈhɑːf ˈbɛriːd/ | adj. | 半埋的，半掩埋的 | Partially covered or hidden in the ground or another substance.  |例句：The ancient city was half-buried in the sand.  那座古城一半埋在沙子里。

The treasure chest lay half-buried in the mud. 宝箱半埋在泥里。| 近义词：partially buried, partly submerged, concealed, hidden | 反义词：fully exposed, unearthed, uncovered | 词性:形容词
