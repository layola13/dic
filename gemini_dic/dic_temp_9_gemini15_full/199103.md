sikh:/sɪk/| n. |锡克教徒；锡克教信徒|A member of a monotheistic religion originating in the Punjab region of India.  Sikhs follow the teachings of the ten Sikh Gurus and believe in one God. |Example:  Many Sikhs live in Punjab. 许多锡克教徒居住在旁遮普邦。  My neighbor is a Sikh. 我的邻居是锡克教徒。|近义词：Sikh devotee, Sikh follower|反义词：无|名词
