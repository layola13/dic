pentose:/ˈpɛntoʊs/| n.|戊糖|A pentose is a monosaccharide (simple sugar) with five carbon atoms.  It's a type of carbohydrate found in various biological molecules,  including DNA and RNA.  Pentose sugars play crucial roles in cellular processes and metabolism.|例句：Ribose and deoxyribose are important pentoses found in RNA and DNA respectively. 核糖和脱氧核糖分别是RNA和DNA中重要的戊糖。  The metabolism of pentoses is vital for energy production in cells. 戊糖的代谢对于细胞的能量产生至关重要。|近义词：monosaccharide, carbohydrate|反义词：hexose (六碳糖)
