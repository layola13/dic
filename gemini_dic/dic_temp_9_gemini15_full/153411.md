penitentials:/ˌpɛnɪˈtɛnʃəlz/| n.|忏悔书；悔罪仪式|Books containing sets of canonical penances for various sins.  They were widely used in the early medieval Church to guide priests in assigning appropriate penances to penitents.|Example:  The early medieval Church relied heavily on penitentials to structure the process of confession and absolution.|近义词：manuals of penance|反义词：None specifically.
