censor:/ˈsɛnsər/| n., v.|审查员；审查；删改|To remove or suppress parts of a book, film, etc. that are considered objectionable.|例句：The government censored the news report. 政府审查了新闻报道。  The film was heavily censored before release.这部电影在发行前进行了大量的删改。|近义词：examiner, editor, expurgate|反义词：approve, permit


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government censors the news. = 政府审查新闻。
He censors his emails before sending them. = 他在发送邮件之前会审查邮件内容。


一般过去时:
They censored the controversial scenes from the movie. = 他们删掉了电影中那些有争议的场景。
The book was censored for its offensive language. = 这本书因为语言冒犯而被审查。


现在完成时:
The authorities have censored many dissident voices. = 当局已经审查了许多持不同政见者的声音。
They have censored the article for publication. = 他们已经审查了这篇文章以便发表。


过去完成时:
The editor had censored the manuscript before submitting it. = 编辑在提交手稿之前已经审查过它了。
The playwright had censored several lines of dialogue before the premiere. = 剧作家在首映前删掉了几句台词。


将来时:
They will censor the broadcast if it contains inappropriate content. = 如果广播包含不当内容，他们将会审查它。
The committee will censor the student's paper before grading it. = 委员会将在给学生的论文打分之前对其进行审查。


虚拟语气:
If they censored the article, nobody would know the truth. = 如果他们审查了这篇文章，没有人会知道真相。
I wish they hadn't censored my speech. = 我希望他们没有审查我的演讲。


被动语态:
The article was censored by the government. = 这篇文章被政府审查了。
His speech was censored for its political content. = 他的演讲因其政治内容而被审查。


疑问句:
Was the book censored? = 这本书被审查了吗？
Will the movie be censored? = 这部电影会被审查吗？


否定句:
They did not censor the article. = 他们没有审查这篇文章。
The government did not censor the news report. = 政府没有审查新闻报道。
