qualitatively:/ˈkwɒlɪteɪtɪvli/ | adv. |定性地，质的方面|Describes something in terms of its quality or characteristics, rather than quantity or numerical measurement. It focuses on the nature, type, or kind of something.|例句：The study examined the qualitatively different experiences of patients with chronic pain. 这项研究定性地考察了慢性疼痛患者的不同经历。 |例句：The results were assessed qualitatively, focusing on the themes that emerged from the interviews. 结果进行了定性评估，重点是访谈中出现的主题。|近义词：substantively, essentially|反义词：quantitatively
