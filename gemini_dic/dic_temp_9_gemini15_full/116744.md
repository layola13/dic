maundering:/ˈmɔːndərɪŋ/| v. |唠叨；含糊不清地说；漫无目的地闲逛 |To talk in a rambling and inconsequential way; to wander aimlessly.  |例句：He spent the afternoon maundering on about his childhood. 他一下午都在唠叨他的童年往事。

例句：She was maundering along the beach, lost in thought. 她漫无目的地沿着海滩走着，陷入沉思。

近义词： rambling, rambling, wandering, meandering, droning

反义词： concise, succinct, focused


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maunders on endlessly about nothing. = 他没完没了地唠叨着一些无关紧要的事。
She maunders through the park every morning. = 她每天早上都在公园里漫无目的地闲逛。


一般过去时:
He maundered about his lost youth for hours. = 他唠叨了他失去的青春好几个小时。
They maundered through the countryside, enjoying the scenery. = 他们漫步在乡间，欣赏着风景。


现在完成时:
He has been maundering about politics all evening. = 他整个晚上都在唠叨政治。
She has maundered through life without a clear goal. = 她漫无目的地度过一生，没有明确的目标。


过去完成时:
He had maundered for so long that everyone was tired of listening. = 他唠叨了这么久，以至于每个人都听烦了。
She had maundered through the forest before realizing she was lost. = 她漫无目的地穿过森林后才意识到自己迷路了。


将来时:
He will maunder on about his accomplishments again tomorrow. = 他明天还会再次唠叨他的成就。
They will maunder along the coast all day. = 他们会一整天沿着海岸漫无目的地闲逛。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't maundered so much, we could have finished the project earlier. = 如果他不那么唠叨，我们就能早点完成项目。
I wish he wouldn't maunder on about such trivial matters. = 我希望他不要再唠叨这些琐事了。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His stories were often maundered over by the listeners. = 听众经常对他的故事感到乏味。  (This is a less common usage)


疑问句:
Is he maundering again about his vacation? = 他又在唠叨他的假期了吗？
Was she maundering to herself as she walked? = 她走路的时候是不是在自言自语？


否定句:
He didn't maunder; he spoke clearly and concisely. = 他没有唠叨；他说话清晰简洁。
She didn't maunder through the task; she completed it efficiently. = 她没有漫无目的地完成任务；她高效地完成了任务。
