reinflating:/ˌriːɪnˈfleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再充气；重新膨胀；使复苏 | The process of filling something with air or gas again, or of reviving something that has declined.  It can refer to literal inflation (like a tire) or metaphorical inflation (like an economy). | Example: The mechanics are reinflating the flat tire. 机械师正在给瘪了的轮胎充气。

The company is trying to reinflate its struggling stock prices. 公司正试图提振其低迷的股价。 | 近义词：reflate, revive, inflate (again) | 反义词：deflate, depress
