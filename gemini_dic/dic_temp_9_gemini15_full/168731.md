英文解析:  The word "procurator's" is the possessive form of "procurator," referring to something belonging to or associated with a procurator. A procurator is a person authorized to act on behalf of another, particularly in legal matters.  In some legal systems, especially in civil law jurisdictions, a procurator might be a public prosecutor or a representative acting for someone else in court.  The term's meaning can vary depending on context and jurisdiction.
