posting's:/ˈpoʊstɪŋz/| n. |帖子；张贴物；公布；邮件|The plural form of "posting," referring to multiple instances of something being posted, such as online posts, job postings, or physical notices.|例句：The company's job postings attracted many applicants.  公司的招聘信息吸引了许多求职者。|例句：There were several interesting postings on the forum.  论坛上有几篇有趣的帖子。|近义词：uploads, announcements, publications|反义词：removal, deletion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company posts job openings regularly. = 公司定期发布职位空缺。
He posts updates on his blog daily. = 他每天都在博客上更新信息。


一般过去时:
She posted the letter yesterday. = 她昨天寄了信。
They posted the announcement on the bulletin board. = 他们把公告贴在布告栏上。


现在完成时:
I have posted all the photos online. = 我已经把所有照片都上传到网上了。
She has posted several articles on the topic. = 她已经发表了几篇关于这个主题的文章。


过去完成时:
He had posted the package before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前寄出了包裹。
They had posted the flyers around the neighborhood. = 他们已经在附近张贴了传单。


将来时:
We will post the results tomorrow. = 我们明天将公布结果。
She will post a picture of her trip. = 她将发布她旅行的照片。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have posted it earlier. = 如果我知道的话，我就会早点发布它。
I wish I had posted the comment sooner. = 我希望我早点发表评论。


被动语态:
The news was posted on the website. = 新闻发布在网站上。
The notices are being posted throughout the building. = 通知正在整个建筑物内张贴。


疑问句:
Have you posted the package yet? = 你寄包裹了吗？
Where was the announcement posted? = 公告贴在哪里？


否定句:
I didn't post the comment because I was busy. = 我因为忙而没有发表评论。
She hasn't posted anything on social media lately. = 她最近没有在社交媒体上发布任何东西。
