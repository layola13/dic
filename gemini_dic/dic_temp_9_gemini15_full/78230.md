gerbil:/ˈdʒɜːrbəl/ | n. |沙鼠| A small rodent, typically with a long tail and large ears, often kept as a pet.  Gerbils are known for their sociable nature and active behavior. They are native to Africa and Asia. |例句：My daughter really enjoys playing with her gerbil. 我女儿很喜欢玩她的沙鼠。|例句：We bought a new cage for our gerbils. 我们为我们的沙鼠买了一个新笼子。|近义词： rodent, hamster (less accurate)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with larger pets like cats or dogs. |名词
