sindhi: /ˈsɪndiː/ | n. |信德语；信德人 |Sindhi is a language spoken primarily in Sindh, Pakistan.  It is also spoken in parts of India and other countries. It belongs to the Indo-Aryan branch of the Indo-European language family. |例句：She speaks Sindhi fluently. 她流利地说信德语。

例句：Many immigrants from Sindh maintain their Sindhi culture and language. 许多来自信德的移民保持着他们的信德文化和语言。


近义词：无直接近义词，可根据语境用其他南亚语言替代


反义词：无


词性：名词 (也可作形容词，指与信德相关的)
