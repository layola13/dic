letterhead:/ˈlɛtərhɛd/| n. |信头，抬头|Printed heading at the top of a sheet of stationery, typically containing the sender's name and address.|Example:  The company letterhead featured its logo and contact information. 公司抬头印有其标识和联系方式。|近义词：heading, stationery header|反义词：body (of a letter)
