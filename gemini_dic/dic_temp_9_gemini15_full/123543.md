monoxenous:/mɒˈnɒksɪnəs/| adj.|单宿主性的，单寄生的|Relating to or denoting a parasite that completes its life cycle in a single host.  A monoxenous parasite only requires one host to complete its life cycle. This is in contrast to a heteroxenous parasite, which requires multiple hosts.|Example: The parasite is monoxenous, completing its life cycle entirely within the human host.|近义词：unihost|反义词：heteroxenous
