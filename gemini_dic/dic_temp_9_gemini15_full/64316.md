equivocators:/ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪtərz/| n.|模棱两可的人，含糊其辞的人|People who use ambiguous language to conceal their true intentions or avoid committing to a clear position. They deliberately use vague or misleading statements to deceive or confuse others.  |Example:  The politician's equivocators during the press conference only fueled speculation about his true motives.  这位政客在新闻发布会上的含糊其辞只会加剧人们对其真实动机的猜测。|近义词：prevaricators, obfuscators, tergiversators|反义词：truth-tellers, straightforward people|名词
