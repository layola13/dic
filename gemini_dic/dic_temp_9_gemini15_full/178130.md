recognition's:/ˌrekəɡˈnɪʃənz/| n.| 承认；认出；识别；认可 | The act of recognizing or being recognized; acknowledgment or appreciation.  It's often used in the possessive form, indicating something belonging to or associated with recognition. |例句：The company's recognition of its employees' hard work boosted morale. 公司对员工辛勤工作的认可提高了士气。

例句：His recognition of the problem was crucial to finding a solution. 他对问题的认识对于找到解决方案至关重要。


近义词：acknowledgment, identification, appreciation, discernment | 反义词：ignorance, disregard, oversight | 词性：名词
