chalybeous:/kæˈlɪbiəs/| adj.|含铁的，钢质的|Containing iron, especially in the form of iron salts; steel-like.  The term often refers to substances containing iron, particularly in mineral waters.|例句：The chalybeous spring water is said to have health benefits.  这含铁的泉水据说有益健康。The blacksmith worked with chalybeous metal to create strong tools.  铁匠用含铁金属打造坚固的工具。|近义词：ferruginous, iron-containing|反义词：iron-free
