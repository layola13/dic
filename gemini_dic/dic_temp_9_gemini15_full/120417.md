minitour:/ˈmɪniˌtʊr/| n. |小型旅游，短途旅行 | A minitour is a short trip, usually for leisure, often lasting only a few days.  It's a smaller-scale version of a longer tour.  |Example: We took a minitour to the nearby coastal town for the weekend.  我们周末去了附近沿海小镇进行了一次短途旅行。|近义词：short trip, brief excursion, getaway |反义词：grand tour, extensive journey |名词
