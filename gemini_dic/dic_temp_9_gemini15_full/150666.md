papular:/ˈpɑːpjələr/| adj.|丘疹的；丘疹状的| Relating to or characterized by papules (small, raised bumps on the skin).  The term is often used in medical contexts to describe skin conditions. |Example: The patient presented with a papular rash on their arms.  病人手臂上出现了丘疹性皮疹。

The doctor noted several small, papular lesions on the patient's back.  医生注意到病人背上有一些小的丘疹样病变。


近义词：nodular, pustular (depending on the context)
反义词：smooth, flat (describing skin texture)
词性：形容词
