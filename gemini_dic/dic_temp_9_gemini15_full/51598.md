devastate:/ˈdɛvəsteɪt/| vt.|毁坏；使震惊；使极度悲伤|To damage something very badly, or to severely upset someone.|The hurricane devastated the coastal town.  飓风摧毁了沿海城镇。 The news devastated her.  这个消息使她非常震惊。|destroy, ruin, wreck, shatter|repair, rebuild, console, comfort|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The earthquake devastates the city. = 地震摧毁了这座城市。
He devastates his opponents in debate. = 他在辩论中击败了他的对手。


一般过去时:
The fire devastated the forest. = 大火摧毁了森林。
The news devastated him. = 这个消息使他非常震惊。


现在完成时:
The drought has devastated the crops. = 干旱已经摧毁了庄稼。
The loss of her job has devastated her. = 失去工作让她非常沮丧。


过去完成时:
The army had devastated the countryside before the peace talks began. = 和谈开始前，军队已经摧毁了农村地区。
She had been devastated by the news of her father's death. = 她父亲去世的消息让她非常悲痛。


将来时:
The storm will devastate the region. = 暴风雨将摧毁该地区。
The scandal will devastate his career. = 这场丑闻将毁掉他的事业。


虚拟语气:
If the war had continued, it would have devastated the country. = 如果战争继续下去，它将摧毁这个国家。
I wish the news hadn't devastated her so much. = 我希望这个消息没有让她如此伤心。


被动语态:
The city was devastated by the bombing. = 这个城市被轰炸摧毁了。
He was devastated by the criticism. = 他受到了批评而非常沮丧。


疑问句:
Has the storm devastated the coastal areas? = 暴风雨是否摧毁了沿海地区？
Was she devastated by the breakup? = 她被分手打击了吗？


否定句:
The attack did not devastate the enemy forces. = 这次袭击并没有摧毁敌军。
The news did not devastate him as much as we expected. = 这个消息并没有像我们预期的那样让他沮丧。


PHRASE->
be devastated by = 被…摧毁/打击
devastate the environment = 破坏环境
devastate a landscape = 摧毁景观
devastate the economy = 摧毁经济
devastate one's hopes = 粉碎某人的希望
