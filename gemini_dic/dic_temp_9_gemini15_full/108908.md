ledgier:/ˈlɛdʒər/| n.|分类账，总账|A ledger is a book or other collection of accounts in which commercial transactions are recorded.  It's a principal book of accounts in which entries are made systematically. |例句：The accountant meticulously updated the ledger with the day's transactions. 会计仔细地用当天发生的交易更新了分类账。|例句：The company's ledger showed a significant profit for the quarter. 公司的总账显示本季度盈利显著。|近义词：account book, register, record|反义词：None.
