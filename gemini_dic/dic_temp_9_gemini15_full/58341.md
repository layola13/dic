/ˈdiːɛslɑːr/|/ˈdiːɛsɛlɑːr/ | n. | 单反相机 | Digital Single-Lens Reflex camera.  A type of camera that uses a single lens for both viewing and taking the picture, and a digital sensor to record the image instead of film.  | Example: I bought a new DSLR for landscape photography. 我买了一台新的单反相机来拍风景照。  I'm learning to use my new DSLR to take better portraits. 我正在学习使用我的新单反相机来拍摄更好的肖像照。| 近义词：SLR (Single-lens reflex camera) | 反义词：Point-and-shoot camera | 名词
