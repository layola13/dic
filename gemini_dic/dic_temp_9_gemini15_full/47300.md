de-integrate:/ˌdiːˈɪntɪɡreɪt/| vt.|使…不再整合；使…分离；取消…的种族隔离|To separate or cause to separate from an integrated system or group;  to desegregate.  The verb implies a reversal of integration, often in a social or political context, particularly concerning racial segregation.|例句：The school board voted to de-integrate the schools. 学校董事会投票决定取消学校的种族隔离。

例句：The company decided to de-integrate its marketing and sales departments. 公司决定将其市场营销和销售部门分开。|近义词：disintegrate, separate, disband, desegregate|反义词：integrate, unify, combine, consolidate|动词
