eatt:/iːt/| v.|吃，食用|To put food into your mouth and swallow it.|I eat breakfast every morning. 我每天早上吃早餐。|He ate a big lunch. 他吃了一顿丰盛的午餐。|consume, devour, ingest|starve, fast


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I eat breakfast every day. = 我每天吃早餐。
She eats a lot of vegetables. = 她吃很多蔬菜。

一般过去时:
I ate a hamburger yesterday. = 我昨天吃了一个汉堡包。
They ate dinner at seven o'clock. = 他们七点钟吃的晚饭。

现在完成时:
I have eaten all the cookies. = 我已经把所有的饼干都吃掉了。
She has eaten lunch already. = 她已经吃过午饭了。

过去完成时:
I had eaten before they arrived. = 他们到达之前我已经吃过了。
He had eaten all the cake before we got there. = 我们到那里之前他已经把蛋糕都吃掉了。

将来时:
I will eat dinner with my family tonight. = 我今晚将和家人一起吃晚饭。
We will eat at the new restaurant tomorrow. = 我们明天将在新餐馆吃饭。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would eat something. = 如果我是你，我会吃点东西。
I wish I had eaten less at the party. = 我真希望在派对上少吃点。

被动语态:
The cake was eaten quickly. = 蛋糕很快就被吃掉了。
All the food was eaten at the party. = 派对上的所有食物都被吃掉了。


疑问句:
What did you eat for lunch? = 你午餐吃了什么？
Do you eat meat? = 你吃肉吗？

否定句:
I don't eat seafood. = 我不吃海鲜。
He didn't eat anything for breakfast. = 他早餐什么也没吃。


PHRASE->
eat up = 吃光
eat out = 外出吃饭
eat in = 在家吃饭
eat away at = 逐渐侵蚀
eat one's words = 收回前言
