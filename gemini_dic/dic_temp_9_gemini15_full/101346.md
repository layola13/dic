iodyrite:/ˈaɪədaɪraɪt/| n. |碘银矿|Iodyrite is a mineral consisting of silver iodide (AgI). It is a rare mineral found in some hydrothermal vein deposits and in some volcanic environments.  It is usually found as a secondary mineral formed from the alteration of other silver minerals.  The color varies from yellow to greenish-yellow to brownish-yellow.
