nondeciduate:/ˌnɒnˈdɛsɪdʒuət/| adj.|非落叶的；常绿的|Not shedding leaves annually; evergreen.  The term refers to plants that retain their leaves throughout the year, unlike deciduous plants which lose their leaves seasonally.|Example:  Many conifers are nondeciduate. 许多针叶树都是常绿的。|近义词：evergreen, perennial|反义词：deciduous
