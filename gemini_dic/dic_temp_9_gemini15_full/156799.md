pigeon:/ˈpɪdʒən/| n. |鸽子|A pigeon is a type of dove, a bird known for its plump body, short legs, and cooing sound. They are commonly found in urban areas and are known for their homing instinct.  |Example:  A flock of pigeons landed on the park fountain.  一群鸽子落在公园喷泉上。

The pigeons were feeding on the breadcrumbs. 鸽子正在吃面包屑。|近义词：dove, rock dove|反义词：无|名词
