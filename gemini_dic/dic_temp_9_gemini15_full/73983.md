fortnights:/ˈfɔːrtnaɪts/| n. |两星期；十四天|A fortnight is a period of fourteen days or two weeks.  It's often used in contexts where a longer period than a week is being discussed but a month is too long. |例句：We'll be on holiday for a fortnight. = 我们将休假两周。

例句：The project is expected to take a fortnight to complete. = 预计该项目需要两周时间才能完成。


近义词：two weeks, fourteen days|反义词：a day, a week


词源:
The word "fortnight" comes from the Old English phrase "fourteen nights".  It literally means "fourteen nights," reflecting the original way of counting time periods.  The "fort-" part is derived from the Old French "quatorze" meaning "fourteen".
