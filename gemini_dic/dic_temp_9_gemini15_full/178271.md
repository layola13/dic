recomputes:/ˌriːkəmˈpjuːts/| v.|重新计算 |To compute again; to recalculate.  This typically involves using the same data or algorithm but potentially with corrections or updated inputs. |例句：The software recomputes the trajectory after each sensor reading.  软件在每次传感器读数后重新计算轨迹。

The program recomputed the results after detecting an error. 程序在检测到错误后重新计算了结果。|近义词：recalculate, recalibrate, re-evaluate |反义词：compute (initially), finalize
