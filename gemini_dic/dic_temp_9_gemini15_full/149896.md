palisades: /ˌpælɪˈseɪdz/ | n. |栅栏；防御工事；峭壁 | A fence of stakes or posts, especially one forming a defensive barrier.  Often refers to a line of steep cliffs or a row of trees. |例句：The pioneers built palisades around their settlement for protection.  拓荒者在他们的定居点周围建造了栅栏以保护自己。

The Hudson River Palisades are a spectacular sight. 哈德逊河峭壁是一道壮丽的景色。|近义词：fence, barricade, rampart, cliffs |反义词：open space, unprotected area
