landings:/ˈlændɪŋz/| n.|着陆；平台；楼梯平台|The act of an aircraft, etc., touching down on the ground or water; a place where aircraft land; a level part of a staircase.  |例句：The pilot executed a smooth landing.  飞行员进行了平稳着陆。


例句：The landing was bumpy due to strong winds. 由于强风，着陆颠簸。


近义词：arrival, touchdown, disembarkation|反义词：takeoff, departure|名词
