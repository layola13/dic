rich:/rɪtʃ/ | adj. | 富有的；丰富的；浓厚的 | Having a great deal of money or assets; abundant in supply or quantity; intense in flavor, color, or other characteristic. |例句：He is a rich man. 他是个富人。 |例句：The land is rich in minerals. 这块土地矿产丰富。 |近义词：wealthy, affluent, abundant, plentiful |反义词：poor, impoverished, scarce, meager


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is rich. = 他很有钱。
The soup is rich in flavor. = 这汤味道很浓郁。

一般过去时:
They were rich before the economic crisis. = 经济危机前他们很富有。
The soil was rich and fertile. = 土壤肥沃且富饶。

现在完成时:
She has become rich through hard work. = 她通过努力工作变得富有。
The garden has yielded a rich harvest. = 花园获得了丰收。

过去完成时:
They had been rich, but lost everything in the war. = 他们曾经富有，但在战争中失去了一切。
The painting had been rich in vibrant colors, but time had faded them. = 这幅画曾经色彩鲜艳，但时间已使其褪色。

将来时:
He will be rich one day. = 他总有一天会富有。
The land will be rich with crops after the rain. = 雨后土地将五谷丰登。

虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would travel the world. = 如果我有钱，我会环游世界。
I wish I were rich enough to buy that car. = 我希望我有足够的钱买那辆车。

被动语态:
The cake is richly decorated. = 蛋糕装饰得很华丽。
The soil is richly endowed with nutrients. = 土壤富含营养物质。

疑问句:
Is he rich? = 他富有吗？
Is the color rich enough? = 颜色够浓郁吗？

否定句:
He is not rich. = 他不富有。
The flavor is not rich enough. = 味道不够浓郁。
