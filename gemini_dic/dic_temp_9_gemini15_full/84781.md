handset:/ˈhændˌsɛt/| n. |手持电话；听筒|A handset is the part of a telephone that you hold to your ear to speak and listen.  It can refer to a mobile phone or a traditional telephone's receiver.|Example: I dropped my handset and cracked the screen.  我摔坏了我的手机屏幕。|Example: He carefully placed the handset back on the cradle. 他小心翼翼地把听筒放回话机座上。|近义词：mobile phone, cell phone, receiver|反义词：base (of a phone)|词性:名词
