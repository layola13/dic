lawyered:/ˈlɔːjərd/| v.|请律师，寻求法律帮助|To seek the advice or services of a lawyer; to obtain legal representation.|例句：He lawyered up after the accident.  他事故后请了律师。|例句：She decided to lawyer up before signing the contract. 她决定在签合同之前请律师。|近义词：consulted a lawyer, sought legal counsel|反义词：represented oneself, self-represented
