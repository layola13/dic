occupying:/ˈɑːkjupaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 占据，占用，使忙碌 |  The act of taking or holding possession of something; to fill or use up (space, time, etc.) | The army is occupying the city.  军队正在占领这座城市。  The meeting occupied the entire afternoon.  会议占据了整个下午。 | possessing, holding, engaging | vacating, relinquishing, freeing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are occupying the building. = 他们在占据那栋建筑物。
He is occupying his time with reading. = 他正忙着阅读。


一般过去时:
The soldiers occupied the town. = 士兵占领了那个城镇。
She occupied herself with knitting. = 她忙着织毛衣。


现在完成时:
They have occupied the land for centuries. = 他们已经占领那片土地几个世纪了。
We have occupied ourselves with preparations all day. = 我们一整天都在忙着准备。


过去完成时:
The army had occupied the capital before the negotiations began. = 在谈判开始之前，军队已经占领了首都。
She had occupied herself with gardening before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她一直在忙着园艺。


将来时:
They will occupy the new office next week. = 他们下周将占据新的办公室。
He will occupy his time with volunteer work. = 他将把时间用在志愿工作上。


虚拟语气:
If they occupied the building, they would control the city. = 如果他们占领了那栋建筑物，他们就能控制这座城市。
I wish I could occupy my time more productively. = 我希望我能更有效率地利用我的时间。


被动语态:
The building is being occupied by the protestors. = 这栋建筑物正在被抗议者占据。
His time is occupied by work and family responsibilities. = 他的时间被工作和家庭责任占据。


疑问句:
Are they occupying the house? = 他们正在占据房子吗？
What is occupying your time these days? = 这些天是什么占据了你的时间？


否定句:
They are not occupying the land illegally. = 他们并没有非法占据土地。
He wasn't occupied with anything important. = 他并没有忙于任何重要的事情。



PHRASE->
occupy oneself with = 忙于…
occupy a position = 担任职位
occupy space = 占据空间
occupy one's mind = 占据某人的心思
occupy a territory = 占据领土
