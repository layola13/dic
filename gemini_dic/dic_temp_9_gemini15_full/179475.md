redwood:/ˈrɛdˌwʊd/| n. |红杉，红木|A redwood is a large evergreen coniferous tree of the genus Sequoia, particularly Sequoia sempervirens (coast redwood) and Sequoia giganteum (giant sequoia). They are known for their massive size and longevity.|Example: The redwood forest was breathtaking in its scale and beauty.  红杉森林的规模和美丽令人叹为观止。|近义词：sequoia, conifer|反义词：无
