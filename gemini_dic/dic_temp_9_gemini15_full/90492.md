huckleberries:/ˈhʌkəlˌbɛriːz/| n. |越橘；蓝莓|Small, dark-colored berries, similar to blueberries but often with a slightly tart flavor. They are commonly found in North America.  Huckleberries are often used in pies, jams, and other desserts. |Example: We went hiking in the mountains and found a patch of wild huckleberries.|近义词：blueberries, bilberries|反义词：无
