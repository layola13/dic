scimitar:/ˈsɪmɪtɑːr/|/ˈsɪmɪtər/| n. |弯刀，马刀|A curved sword with a single-edged blade that broadens toward the point.  It is typically used in the Middle East and parts of Asia.|例句：The warrior brandished his scimitar menacingly.  战士凶狠地挥舞着他的弯刀。

例句：The museum displayed a collection of antique scimitars from various cultures. 博物馆展出了来自不同文化的古代弯刀收藏。


近义词：curved sword, saber, falchion|反义词：straight sword


词源：The word "scimitar" comes from the Italian *scimetro*, ultimately derived from the Arabic *saif* (سيف), meaning "sword".  The word entered English in the 16th century.
