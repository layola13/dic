overmanage:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒ/| vt.|过度管理，管理过度|To manage something excessively or to an unnecessary degree, often leading to negative consequences.  This can involve micromanagement, excessive control, or unnecessary intervention.|例句：The project was overmanaged, leading to delays and increased costs.  这个项目管理过度，导致延误和成本增加。

The team felt overmanaged and stifled their creativity. 团队感觉管理过度，扼杀了他们的创造力。|近义词：micromanage, overcontrol,  supervise excessively|反义词：undermanage, delegate, trust


USAGE->

They overmanage the staff. = 他们过度管理员工。
