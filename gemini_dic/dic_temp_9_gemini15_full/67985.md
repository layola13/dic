factchecking:/ˈfæktˌtʃɛkɪŋ/ | n. |事实核查；查证事实 | The process of verifying the accuracy of information, especially in news reports and social media.  Fact-checking involves investigating sources, comparing claims to evidence, and assessing the credibility of information. | Example:  The article underwent rigorous fact-checking before publication. 这篇文章在发表前经过了严格的事实核查。

The organization is dedicated to fact-checking political claims. 这个组织致力于核实政治言论的事实。 | 近义词：verification, confirmation, validation | 反义词：misinformation, disinformation, fabrication |  词性：名词
