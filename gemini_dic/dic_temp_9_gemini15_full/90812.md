hums:/hʌmz/| v.|哼唱，发出嗡嗡声|To make a low, continuous sound, like a bee or a machine, or to sing a tune in a low voice without words.|例句：The bees were humming in the garden. 花园里的蜜蜂嗡嗡作响。

例句：She hummed a happy tune while she worked. 她工作时哼着快乐的曲子。|近义词：buzz, drone, murmur|反义词：scream, shout


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hums a tune every morning. 他每天早上都哼着歌。
The engine hums quietly. 引擎安静地嗡嗡作响。

一般过去时:
She hummed a lullaby to her baby. 她给宝宝哼唱催眠曲。
The refrigerator hummed all night. 冰箱整夜嗡嗡作响。

现在完成时:
He has hummed that song all day. 他整天都在哼唱那首歌。
The machine has been humming for hours. 机器已经嗡嗡作响了好几个小时了。

过去完成时:
She had hummed the melody before she even realised it.  在她意识到之前就已经哼起了旋律。
The old car had been humming strangely before it broke down.  那辆旧车在抛锚之前就发出奇怪的嗡嗡声。

将来时:
