garbled:/ˈɡɑːrbəld/| adj.|含糊不清的，混乱的|Confused or distorted sound or speech; unclear or unintelligible.|The message was garbled by static.|近义词：jumbled, confused, distorted, unintelligible|反义词：clear, understandable, coherent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The radio signal is garbled. = 收音机信号不清楚。
His speech is often garbled. = 他的讲话常常含糊不清。

一般过去时:
The recording was garbled due to poor connection. = 由于连接不良，录音变得含糊不清。
The message she received was garbled. = 她收到的信息是混乱的。

现在完成时:
The transmission has been garbled by interference. = 传输因干扰而变得模糊不清。
My understanding of the situation has been garbled by misinformation. = 我对情况的理解因错误信息而变得混乱。

过去完成时:
The message had been garbled before it reached its destination. = 信息在到达目的地之前就已经变得模糊不清了。
By the time we deciphered it, the message had been thoroughly garbled. = 等我们破译时，信息已经完全混乱了。

将来时:
