dishonouring: /dɪsˈɒnərɪŋ/ | v. | 羞辱；败坏；使蒙受耻辱 | To bring shame or disgrace upon; to violate or fail to honor. |  The politician's actions dishonoured his family and his party.  这位政治家的行为使他的家人和政党蒙羞。 |  disgrace, shame, humiliate | honour, glorify, exalt | 动词
