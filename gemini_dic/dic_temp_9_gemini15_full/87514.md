heterodont:/ˈhɛtərədɒnt/| adj.|异齿的；具有不同类型的牙齿的|Having teeth of different kinds, as opposed to homodont.  This is characteristic of mammals, who typically possess incisors, canines, premolars, and molars, each adapted for different functions such as biting, tearing, and grinding.|例句：Humans are heterodont, possessing incisors for cutting, canines for tearing, and molars for grinding.|近义词：differently toothed|反义词：homodont
