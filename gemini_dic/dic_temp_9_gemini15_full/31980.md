chemoreceptor:/ˌkiːmoʊrɪˈsɛptər/ | n. | 化学感受器 | A sensory receptor that transduces a chemical signal into an electrical signal.  Chemoreceptors are found throughout the body and are responsible for detecting a wide range of chemicals, including odorants, taste stimuli, oxygen, carbon dioxide, and various hormones. | Example: The olfactory chemoreceptors in the nose detect odor molecules and transmit signals to the brain, allowing us to perceive smells.  嗅觉化学感受器位于鼻腔内，能够检测气味分子并将信号传递给大脑，使我们能够感知气味。


Example:  The carotid body contains chemoreceptors that monitor blood oxygen levels.  颈动脉体含有化学感受器，可以监测血液中的氧气水平。


近义词: sensory receptor,  receptor


反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其功能相反的结构，例如：mechanoreceptor(机械感受器))


词性: 名词
