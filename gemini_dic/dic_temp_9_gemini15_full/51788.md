devourers:/dɪˈvaʊərərz/| n.|吞噬者，毁灭者|Those who consume or destroy something voraciously.  The word refers to entities that consume something entirely and often with great speed or intensity. This could be literal consumption (like animals eating prey) or figurative consumption (like a fire devouring a building).|例句：The locusts were voracious devourers, leaving nothing but barren fields in their wake.  蝗虫是贪婪的吞噬者，所过之处寸草不生。


The army were devourers of resources, consuming vast quantities of supplies. 这支军队是资源的吞噬者，消耗了大量的物资。|近义词：consumers, destroyers, predators, gluttons|反义词：preservers, protectors, savers
