inquietude:/ˌɪŋˈkwaɪəˌtjuːd/| n. |不安；焦虑；忧虑 |A state of being troubled or restless; anxiety.  It often involves a feeling of unease or worry about something uncertain or unknown. |例句：Her inquietude grew as the night wore on.  她的不安随着夜晚的推移而加剧。

例句：The long silence filled him with inquietude. 长久的沉默让他感到不安。


近义词：anxiety, apprehension, disquiet, unease, restlessness, worry |反义词：calmness, peace, tranquility, serenity


词源:From Middle French *inquiétude, from Latin inquietudo, from inquietus "uneasy," from in- "not" + quietus "quiet, at rest."
