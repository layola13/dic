rescuers:/ˈreskjuːərz/| n. |救援人员，施救者 |People who rescue others from danger or difficult situations.  They often work in teams and use specialized equipment and training. |Example: The rescuers worked tirelessly to free the trapped miners.  救援人员夜以继日地工作以解救被困的矿工。
The rescuers bravely entered the burning building to save the children. 救援人员勇敢地冲进燃烧的建筑物去救孩子。|近义词：lifesavers, saviors, helpers |反义词： None directly applicable, but perhaps oppressors, tormentors  |名词

一般现在时:
Rescuers search for survivors after a disaster. 救援人员在灾难发生后搜寻幸存者。
The rescuers use specialized equipment. 救援人员使用专业设备。


一般过去时:
Rescuers found several survivors trapped under the rubble. 救援人员发现几名幸存者被困在瓦砾下。
