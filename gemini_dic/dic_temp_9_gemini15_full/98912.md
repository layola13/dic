intensiveness:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪvnəs/ | n. | 强度；剧烈；紧张 | The degree or quality of being intense; the state or quality of being concentrated or forceful.  It refers to the strength, power, or forcefulness of something.  |例句：The intensiveness of the training program left the athletes exhausted.  训练项目的强度让运动员们筋疲力尽。

The intensiveness of the heatwave caused widespread power outages.  热浪的强度导致了大范围的停电。


近义词：intensity, strength, force, power, vigour

反义词：weakness, mildness, gentleness, leniency


词性: 名词
