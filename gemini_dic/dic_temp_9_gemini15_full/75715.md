full-grown:/ˌfʊlˈɡroʊn/| adj.| 成熟的；发育完全的 | Fully developed and mature.  |例句：The full-grown dog weighed over 100 pounds.  这条成年狗重达100多磅。
The full-grown oak tree provided shade for the entire park. 这棵长成的橡树为整个公园提供了阴凉。|近义词：mature, adult, developed |反义词：immature, young, undeveloped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant is full-grown. = 这株植物已经成熟了。
The horse is full-grown and ready for work. = 这匹马已经成年了，可以工作了。


一般过去时:
The tomato was full-grown when we picked it. = 我们采摘西红柿时，它已经成熟了。

The tree has finally full-grown after many years. = 这棵树经过多年终于长大了。


过去完成时:

These seedlings will be full-grown by next summer. = 这些幼苗到明年夏天就会长大了。


虚拟语气:
