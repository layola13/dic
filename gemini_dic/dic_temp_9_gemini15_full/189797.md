salvadoran:/ˌsæl vəˈdɔːrən/| adj.|萨尔瓦多人的；萨尔瓦多的|Of or relating to El Salvador, its people, or its culture.  The word refers to both the nationality and the culture of El Salvador.|例句：She is Salvadoran. = 她是萨尔瓦多人。

例句：He enjoys Salvadoran cuisine. = 他喜欢萨尔瓦多菜系。


近义词：El Salvadorean (less common)


反义词：None (It's an adjective describing a specific nationality)
