encircling:/ɪnˈsɜːrkəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 包围的；环绕的 |  Surrounding something completely; forming a circle around something. | 例句：The encircling mountains created a breathtaking view.  环绕的山峦构成了一幅令人叹为观止的景象。

The encircling army cut off the enemy's escape route. 包围的军队切断了敌人的退路。|近义词：surrounding, encompassing, enclosing | 反义词：unbounded, open, free
