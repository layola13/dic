double-checked:/ˈdʌbəlˌtʃɛkt/ | adj. v. |仔细检查过的，反复核实的 |  Having been checked twice or more to ensure accuracy or correctness.  This word is formed by combining "double" and "checked," indicating a repetition of the checking process. | 例句：I double-checked my work before submitting it. 我提交之前仔细检查了我的工作。

例句：She double-checked the figures to make sure they were correct. 她反复核对数字以确保其正确性。


近义词：verified, confirmed, re-examined, reviewed

反义词：unchecked, unverified


词性: Adjective (形容词), Verb (动词)
