It can also refer to the act of doing something with great accuracy and precision.|例句：His sharpshooting skills won the team the championship.  他的精准射击技巧为球队赢得了冠军。

His sharpshooting analysis revealed the flaw in the opponent's strategy. 他精准的分析揭示了对手策略中的缺陷。|近义词：accurate shooting, marksmanship, precision shooting|反义词：inaccurate shooting, poor aim
