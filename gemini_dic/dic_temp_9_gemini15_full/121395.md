mishmashes:/ˈmɪʃmæʃɪz/| n. |混杂物；大杂烩；一团糟 |A mishmash is a confused mixture of different things.  It implies a lack of order or coherence. |例句：The garden was a mishmash of different flowers and plants. 花园里各种花草杂乱无章。|例句：His essay was a mishmash of unrelated ideas. 他的论文是一堆毫不相关的想法的堆砌。|近义词：hodgepodge, jumble, medley, mixture |反义词：order, organization, coherence
