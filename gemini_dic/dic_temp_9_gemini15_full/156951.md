pilgrimage:/ˈpɪlɡrɪmɪdʒ/ | n. |朝圣，朝圣之旅 | A journey to a sacred place for religious reasons.  |例句：Many people undertake a pilgrimage to Mecca. 许多人前往麦加朝圣。|例句：Her trip to the Himalayas was a spiritual pilgrimage. 她去喜马拉雅山的旅行是一次心灵的朝圣之旅。|近义词：journey, expedition, voyage |反义词：None  |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pilgrim makes a pilgrimage every year. = 朝圣者每年都进行一次朝圣。
She undertakes a pilgrimage to the holy city. = 她前往圣城进行朝圣。


一般过去时:
They made a pilgrimage to the shrine last month. = 他们上个月去神社朝圣了。
He completed a challenging pilgrimage across the desert. = 他完成了穿越沙漠的艰苦朝圣之旅。


现在完成时:
We have completed our pilgrimage. = 我们完成了我们的朝圣之旅。
She has made several pilgrimages to Lourdes. = 她已经多次去卢尔德朝圣了。


过去完成时:
They had already begun their pilgrimage before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经开始朝圣了。
She had never made a pilgrimage before this year. = 在今年之前，她从未进行过朝圣。


将来时:
We will make a pilgrimage to Rome next year. = 我们明年将前往罗马朝圣。
They will undertake a long and arduous pilgrimage across the mountains. = 他们将进行一次漫长而艰巨的跨越山脉的朝圣之旅。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would make a pilgrimage to Jerusalem. = 如果我有时间，我会去耶路撒冷朝圣。
I wish I could make a pilgrimage to Santiago de Compostela. = 我希望我能去圣地亚哥德孔波斯特拉朝圣。


被动语态:
The pilgrimage was organized by a local religious group. = 朝圣之旅是由当地的宗教团体组织的。
The pilgrimage is being documented by a film crew. = 朝圣之旅正在被摄制组记录。


疑问句:
Have you ever been on a pilgrimage? = 你去过朝圣吗？
When will the pilgrimage to the temple take place? = 朝圣寺庙的朝圣活动什么时候举行？


否定句:
They did not make the pilgrimage this year. = 他们今年没有去朝圣。
She has never made a pilgrimage before. = 她以前从未去过朝圣。


PHRASE->
religious pilgrimage = 宗教朝圣
pilgrimage site = 朝圣地
pilgrimage route = 朝圣路线
pilgrimage journey = 朝圣之旅
spiritual pilgrimage = 心灵朝圣


词源:
The word "pilgrimage" comes from the Old French "pelerinage," which in turn derives from the Latin "peregrinationem," meaning "a journey to a foreign land."  The connection to religion solidified over time, as pilgrimages became strongly associated with journeys to holy sites.
