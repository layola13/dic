counterplot:/ˈkaʊntə(r)plɒt/| n.| 反阴谋；对抗阴谋 | A secret plan to oppose or defeat another plan.  It often involves deception and intrigue. | The rebels devised a counterplot to overthrow the tyrant's regime. 反叛者策划了一个反阴谋来推翻暴君的政权。|近义词：countermeasure, counter-scheme, riposte|反义词：plot, scheme


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He counterplots against his rivals. = 他密谋对抗他的竞争对手。
The spy agency counterplots enemy operations. = 间谍机构对抗敌人的行动。


一般过去时:
They counterplotted the assassination attempt. = 他们挫败了暗杀企图。
She counterplotted her opponent's strategy. = 她对抗了对手的策略。


现在完成时:
The government has counterplotted several terrorist attacks. = 政府已经挫败了几次恐怖袭击。
He has counterplotted their every move. = 他已经挫败了他们每一次行动。


过去完成时:
They had counterplotted the enemy's advance before they arrived. = 他们在敌人到来之前就挫败了敌人的进攻。
She had counterplotted his plan long before he put it into action. = 在他付诸行动之前很久，她就挫败了他的计划。


将来时:
We will counterplot their attempt to seize power. = 我们将挫败他们夺取政权的企图。
The military will counterplot any invasion. = 军方将挫败任何入侵。


虚拟语气:
If they had known about the counterplot, they would have acted differently. = 如果他们知道这个反阴谋，他们就会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had been part of the counterplot. = 我真希望我能参与到这个反阴谋中。


被动语态:
The counterplot was discovered by the authorities. = 这个反阴谋被当局发现了。
The enemy's plans were counterplotted effectively. = 敌人的计划被有效地挫败了。


疑问句:
Is there a counterplot against the current leader? = 有针对现任领导人的反阴谋吗？
What is the counterplot they are planning? = 他们正在计划什么反阴谋？


否定句:
There was no counterplot to the king's ambitious plan. = 国王雄心勃勃的计划没有遭到任何反阴谋。
They did not successfully counterplot the coup. = 他们没有成功地挫败政变。


PHRASE->
devise a counterplot = 设计一个反阴谋
uncover a counterplot = 揭露一个反阴谋
execute a counterplot = 实施一个反阴谋
foil a counterplot = 挫败一个反阴谋
a successful counterplot = 一个成功的反阴谋
