gases:/ˈɡæsɪz/| n.|气体|Gases are substances that are in a gaseous state at room temperature. They are characterized by their ability to expand to fill any container they occupy.|例句：The gases in the atmosphere protect us from the sun's harmful rays.  大气中的气体保护我们免受太阳的有害射线。|例句：Noble gases are inert and do not readily react with other elements. 惰性气体是惰性的，不会轻易与其他元素反应。|近义词：vapors, fumes|反义词：solids, liquids|名词
