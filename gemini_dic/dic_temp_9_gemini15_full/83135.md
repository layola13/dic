gutlessly:/ˈɡʌtləsli/| adv.|懦弱地，胆怯地|lacking courage or spirit; cowardly.|例句：He acted gutlessly, refusing to stand up for what he believed in. 他表现得很懦弱，拒绝为自己的信仰挺身而出。 He gutlessly abandoned his post during the battle. 他在战斗中怯懦地放弃了自己的岗位。|近义词：cowardly, cravenly, timidly|反义词：bravely, courageously, valiantly
