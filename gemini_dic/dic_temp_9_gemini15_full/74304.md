fractions:/ˈfrækʃənz/| n.|分数；部分；片段|Parts of a whole, especially numbers expressed as a numerator over a denominator.  Fractions represent a portion of a whole.  They can also represent ratios or divisions.|例句：He understood fractions in math class. 他在数学课上理解了分数。

例句：The cake was cut into fractions for everyone. 蛋糕被切成几块分给大家。


近义词：parts, portions, segments, pieces|反义词：wholes, totalities


词源:  From Old French *fraction, from Latin *fractus, past participle of frangere "to break".  The sense development is from "a broken piece" to "a numerical part".
