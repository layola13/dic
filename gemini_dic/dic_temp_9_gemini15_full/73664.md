forger:/ˈfɔːrdʒər/| n. v.|伪造者；伪造者；锻造者 | A forger is someone who makes illegal copies of documents, money, etc., or someone who creates something false.  A forger can also be someone who shapes metal by heating and hammering. |例句：The police arrested the forger who had been counterfeiting hundred-dollar bills.  警方逮捕了伪造百元钞票的伪造者。

The blacksmith was a skilled forger, known for his strong and durable tools. 这位铁匠是一位熟练的锻造者，以其坚固耐用的工具而闻名。|近义词：counterfeiter, falsifier, fabricator |反义词：authenticator, verifier|名词，动词
