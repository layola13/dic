rueing:/ˈruːɪŋ/| v.|后悔，惋惜|To feel sorrow or regret about something.  It often implies a sense of loss or disappointment.|例句：He spent the evening rueing his hasty decision. 他整个晚上都在后悔自己草率的决定。
例句：She was rueing the day she’d ever met him. 她后悔那天遇见了他。|近义词：regretting, repenting, lamenting|反义词：rejoicing, celebrating|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am rueing my decision to quit my job. = 我正在后悔我辞职的决定。
They are rueing their lost opportunity. = 他们正在后悔失去的机会。

一般过去时:
He rueed his impulsive actions. = 他后悔自己的冲动行为。
She rueed the missed chance to travel. = 她后悔错过了旅行的机会。

现在完成时:
I have been rueing my mistake ever since. = 从那以后我一直在后悔我的错误。
They have rueed their hasty words. = 他们一直在后悔自己说过的话。

过去完成时:
He had rueed his silence for years. = 他多年来一直后悔自己的沉默。
She had rueed her decision long before the consequences became apparent. = 在后果显现之前很久，她就后悔自己的决定了。


将来时:
I will rue this day for the rest of my life. = 我余生都会后悔这一天。
They will rue their inaction. = 他们将会后悔自己的不作为。


虚拟语气:
I would rue the day if I hadn't taken that chance. = 如果我没有抓住那个机会，我会后悔这一天的。
If I had listened to him, I wouldn't be rueing this now. = 如果我听了他的话，我现在就不会后悔了。


被动语态:
His actions were ruefully regretted by everyone. = 他的行为受到了所有人的深深后悔。
The lost opportunity is being ruefully remembered by them. = 他们正在悔恨失去的机会。


疑问句:
Are you rueing your choice now? = 你现在后悔你的选择了吗？
Does he ever rue his past mistakes? = 他是否后悔自己过去的错误？


否定句:
I do not rue my decision. = 我不后悔我的决定。
They did not rue their actions. = 他们不后悔自己的行为。


PHRASE->
rue the day: 后悔某一天

rue one's actions: 后悔自己的行为
rue the consequences: 后悔后果
bitterly rue: 痛苦地后悔
