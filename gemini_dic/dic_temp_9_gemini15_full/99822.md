intermingle:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡəl/ | v. |混合；交错；交往 | To mix or mingle together.  |例句：The two groups intermingled at the party.  两个团体在派对上混杂在一起。|例句：The sounds of the city intermingled with the music from the nearby bar. 城市的喧嚣声与附近酒吧的音乐交织在一起。|近义词：mix, blend, combine, commingle |反义词：separate, segregate
