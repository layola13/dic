lake:/leɪk/ | n. |湖泊，湖 |A large body of water surrounded by land.  |例句：The children played by the lake.孩子们在湖边玩耍。|例句：We went fishing at the lake last weekend.  上周末我们去湖里钓鱼了。|近义词：pond, loch, pool |反义词：ocean, sea
