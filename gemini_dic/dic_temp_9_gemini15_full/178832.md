recussion:/rɪˈkʌʃən/ | n. | 反响，冲击波，后果 | The reverberation or repercussions of a sound or event;  a consequence, typically an unwelcome one. |例句：The repercussions of the scandal were felt throughout the company.  这场丑闻的反响在整个公司都能感受到。|近义词：consequence, effect, aftermath, fallout |反义词：cause, origin, source |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The decision has repercussions for the entire team. = 这个决定对整个团队都有影响。
His actions have repercussions that extend far beyond his personal life. = 他的行为带来的影响远远超出了他的个人生活。


一般过去时:
The earthquake had severe repercussions across the region. = 地震给该地区带来了严重的灾难性后果。
The political scandal had significant repercussions for the government. = 这场政治丑闻给政府带来了重大的影响。


现在完成时:
The company has already felt the repercussions of its poor decisions. = 公司已经感受到了其糟糕决策带来的后果。
The reforms have had unforeseen repercussions on the economy. = 这些改革对经济产生了意想不到的影响。


过去完成时:
By the time the crisis hit, the company had already experienced the repercussions of its previous mistakes. = 当危机来临时，公司已经经历了之前错误带来的后果。
They had not anticipated the negative repercussions of their actions. = 他们没有预料到他们行为的负面影响。


将来时:
The changes will have significant repercussions for the industry. = 这些变化将对该行业产生重大影响。
We need to consider the potential repercussions before making a decision. = 我们需要在做决定之前考虑潜在的影响。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, the repercussions would have been less severe. = 如果我们早点采取行动，后果就不会那么严重了。
I wish the repercussions of my actions were less damaging. = 我希望我的行为带来的影响不那么严重。


被动语态:
The repercussions of the war were felt across the globe. = 战争的影响遍及全球。
The negative repercussions were widely discussed in the media. = 负面影响在媒体上被广泛讨论。


疑问句:
What are the repercussions of this policy change? = 这一政策变化会带来什么影响？
What repercussions can we expect from this decision? = 我们能从这个决定中期待什么影响？


否定句:
The reforms had no negative repercussions on the economy. = 这些改革对经济没有产生负面影响。
There were no discernible repercussions from the incident. = 这件事没有明显的后续影响。


PHRASE->
long-term repercussions = 长期影响
serious repercussions = 严重后果
negative repercussions = 负面影响
unintended repercussions = 非预期后果
immediate repercussions = 立即影响
