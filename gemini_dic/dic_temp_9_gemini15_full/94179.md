imperative:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪv/| adj., n.|命令的；必要的；祈使的；必须的 |  An imperative is something that is absolutely necessary or extremely important.  It can also refer to a grammatical mood expressing a command or request. |

例句：
It is imperative that you finish this report by tomorrow. = 你必须在明天之前完成这份报告。(adj. 必须的)

The imperative mood is used to give commands. = 祈使语气用于发出命令。(n. 祈使的)


近义词：necessary, essential, crucial, mandatory, urgent

反义词：optional, unnecessary, unimportant


词性: adjective, noun
