protatic:/proʊˈtætɪk/ | adj. | 辅助的，附加的；从属的 | Relating to or of the nature of a protasis; introductory or prefatory.  A protatic clause is a subordinate clause that introduces a main clause, often expressing a condition or supposition. | Example:  In the sentence "If it rains, we will stay inside," "if it rains" is the protatic clause.  在句子“如果下雨，我们就待在里面”中，“如果下雨”是从句。| 近义词： introductory, preliminary, subordinate, dependent | 反义词： principal, main, independent
