shiftless:/ˈʃɪftləs/| adj.|懒惰的；无目标的；无计划的 | Lacking ambition, energy, or initiative; lazy and inefficient.  |例句：He was described as a shiftless and irresponsible young man. 他被形容为一个懒惰且不负责任的年轻人。

例句：The shiftless farmer let his crops wither in the field.  那个懒惰的农民让他的庄稼在田里枯萎了。


近义词：lazy, idle, indolent, careless, ineffective, purposeless, aimless

反义词：industrious, diligent, energetic, ambitious, motivated, purposeful
