groupie:/ˈɡruːpiː/| n. |追星族，乐队迷|A groupie is an enthusiastic fan, especially of a pop music group, who follows the group around and tries to get to know the members personally.  They are often associated with a particular band or artist and may attend many of their concerts and events.  The term can sometimes carry negative connotations, implying obsessive behavior or a pursuit of relationships with the musicians. |Example:  Many groupies followed the band on their tour.  许多追星族在巡演中跟随乐队。

She was a devoted groupie of the Rolling Stones. 她是一位滚石乐队的忠实粉丝。|近义词：fan, follower, admirer |反义词：critic, hater
