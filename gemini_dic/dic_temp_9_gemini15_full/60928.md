/ˈɛləveɪt/ | vt. | 提升；举起；提高；使兴奋 | To raise or lift to a higher position or level; to improve or enhance something; to increase the level or intensity of something. | 例句：They elevated the platform to make it easier to access. = 他们抬高了平台，以便更容易通行。

The good news elevated her spirits. = 好消息使她精神振奋。


近义词：raise, lift, hoist, enhance, improve | 反义词：lower, depress, decrease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He elevates the weights. = 他举起杠铃。
The new policy elevates the standard of living. = 新政策提高了生活水平。


一般过去时:
She elevated the painting to the wall. = 她把画挂到墙上。
The doctor elevated his blood pressure with medication. = 医生用药物提高了他的血压。


现在完成时:
They have elevated the bridge to accommodate larger ships. = 他们已经提高了桥梁以适应更大的船只。
This experience has elevated my understanding of the subject. = 这段经历提高了我对这个主题的理解。


过去完成时:
He had elevated the platform before the speech began. = 演讲开始前，他已经把讲台抬高了。
The company had elevated its profits through effective marketing. = 公司通过有效的营销提高了利润。


将来时:
They will elevate the statue to a prominent position in the park. = 他们将把雕像抬高到公园显眼的位置。
The government will elevate the minimum wage next year. = 政府明年将提高最低工资。


虚拟语气:
If they elevated the bridge, more ships could pass. = 如果他们把桥抬高，更多的船就能通过。
I wish they had elevated the concerns of the workers. = 我希望他们重视工人们的担忧。


被动语态:
The statue was elevated to a new height. = 雕像被抬到了新的高度。
The mood was elevated by the music. = 音乐提升了气氛。


疑问句:
Will they elevate the prices? = 他们会涨价吗？
How can we elevate our performance? = 我们如何才能提高我们的表现？


否定句:
They did not elevate the building. = 他们没有建造那栋楼。（此处'elevate'用作建造，而非提升高度）
The news did not elevate her spirits. = 这个消息并没有让她振奋。


PHRASE->
elevate one's spirits = 振奋精神
elevate oneself = 自我提升
elevate to a higher position = 提升到更高的职位
elevate the debate = 提升辩论水平
elevate the standard = 提高标准
