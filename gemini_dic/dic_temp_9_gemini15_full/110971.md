liquidambar:/ˈlɪkwɪdəmˌbɑːr/|/ˈlɪkwɪdəmˌbɑːr/| n. |枫香树；美洲枫香树|Liquidambar refers to a genus of deciduous trees belonging to the family Altingiaceae, known for their fragrant, star-shaped leaves and distinctive spiky seed pods.  They are native to North America, Central America, and East Asia. |The liquidambar trees in the park turn vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall.|近义词：sweetgum (informal)|反义词：无
