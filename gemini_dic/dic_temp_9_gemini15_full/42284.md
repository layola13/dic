counter-extend: /ˌkaʊntərɪkˈstɛnd/ | v. | 反驳；反驳…的论点；驳斥 | To oppose or refute an argument or claim; to extend in the opposite direction. | 例句：He tried to counter-extend her argument by citing several contradictory examples. 他试图通过列举几个相互矛盾的例子来反驳她的论点。

例句：The defense attorney counter-extended the prosecution's theory by presenting alternative explanations.  辩护律师通过提出替代性解释来反驳检方的理论。


近义词：refute, contradict, oppose, rebut | 反义词：support, confirm, endorse | 词性：动词

词源:  The word is a combination of "counter-" (meaning "against" or "opposite") and "extend" (meaning "to prolong" or "to offer").  The meaning evolved to describe the act of opposing or prolonging an argument in an opposing direction.
