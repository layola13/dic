defectious:/dɪˈfɛktɪv/| adj.|有缺陷的，不完美的|Having defects; imperfect or faulty.|The software has several infectious defects.  这个软件有几个缺陷。|近义词：faulty, flawed, imperfect, deficient|反义词：perfect, flawless, impeccable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is defective. = 这台机器有缺陷。
This product is defective and needs to be replaced. = 这个产品有缺陷，需要更换。


一般过去时:
The car was defective when I bought it. = 我买这辆车的时候它就有缺陷。
The bridge was found to be defective after the inspection. = 检查后发现桥梁有缺陷。


现在完成时:
The company has admitted that the product is defective. = 公司承认该产品有缺陷。
Many customers have complained about defective products. = 许多顾客抱怨产品有缺陷。


过去完成时:
We discovered that the engine had been defective for months. = 我们发现发动机几个月前就有缺陷了。
The inspector reported that the building had been defective since its construction. = 检查员报告说该建筑物自建成以来就有缺陷。


将来时:
They will replace the defective part tomorrow. = 他们明天将更换有缺陷的部件。
The engineer predicts that the system will become defective soon. = 工程师预测该系统很快就会出现缺陷。


虚拟语气:
If the product were defective, we would refund your money. = 如果产品有缺陷，我们会退还您的钱。
I wish I had known the software was defective before I purchased it. = 我希望在我购买之前就知道软件有缺陷。


被动语态:
The defective item was returned to the manufacturer. = 有缺陷的物品被退回给制造商。
The problem was caused by a defective component. = 问题是由有缺陷的组件引起的。


疑问句:
Is the product defective? = 产品有缺陷吗？
Are there any defective parts in this shipment? = 这批货物中是否有任何有缺陷的部件？


否定句:
The product is not defective. = 产品没有缺陷。
The investigation revealed that the equipment was not defective. = 调查显示设备没有缺陷。
