orientness:/ˈɔːriɛntnəs/ | n. |方位感；方向感；定向性 | The sense of direction or position;  the state of being oriented.  It can also refer to the characteristic of something that is oriented in a particular way.  |例句：His orientness was impeccable; he never got lost. 他的方向感极好；他从未迷路。  The orientness of the building's design maximizes sunlight. 这座建筑的设计方向最大限度地利用了阳光。 |近义词：directionality, orientation, bearing |反义词：disorientation, confusion
