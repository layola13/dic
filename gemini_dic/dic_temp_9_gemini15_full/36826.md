It's often promoted as a way to detoxify the body, though scientific evidence supporting this claim is limited. | 例句：Some people believe that colonics can improve their digestive health.  一些人认为结肠冲洗疗法可以改善他们的消化健康。


She underwent a series of colonics to address her constipation. 她进行了一系列结肠冲洗疗法来解决便秘问题。|近义词：colonic irrigation, colonic lavage, bowel cleansing |反义词：constipation (反义词并非直接对应，而是指结肠冲洗疗法所针对的症状)
