codifiers:/ˈkoʊdɪfaɪərz/| n.|编码器；规范者；编纂者|People or things that create or enforce a system of codes or rules.  They establish standards or conventions. |The international organization acts as codifiers of best practices in the industry. 国际组织充当行业最佳实践的规范者。The new software includes several advanced codifiers for data security. 新软件包含若干先进的数据安全编码器。|近义词：standard-setters, regulators, codifiers |反义词：non-conformists, rule-breakers
