over-righteous:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪtʃəs/| adj. |过于正直的；自以为是的；假道学；道貌岸然的 | Excessively or ostentatiously righteous; self-righteously moralistic.  It implies a pretense of righteousness, often accompanied by a judgmental attitude towards others. |例句：His over-righteous pronouncements on morality alienated many people. 他关于道德的过于正直的声明疏远了许多人。

例句：She was so over-righteous that she criticized everyone for their minor flaws. 她过于自以为是，总是批评别人的小缺点。
