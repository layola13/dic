mizzen:/ˈmɪzən/| n.|后帆， mizzenmast 后桅|A mizzen is a sail on the mizzenmast, a mast located aft (towards the stern) of the mainmast on a three-masted sailing ship.  It's typically smaller than the mainsail and foresail.|例句：The captain ordered the mizzen to be furled before the storm hit. 船长下令在暴风雨来临之前收起后帆。

The mizzen played a crucial role in steering and maneuvering the ship. 后帆在操纵和控制船舶方面发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词：aftersail |反义词：None.
