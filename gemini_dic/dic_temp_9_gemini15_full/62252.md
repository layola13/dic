encumbered:/ɪnˈkʌmbərd/ | adj. |  burdened; burdened with a load; impeded |  burdened; impeded; hampered; loaded down |  The encumbered hiker struggled up the steep mountain path.  负重的徒步旅行者艰难地攀登陡峭的山路。|  burdened, hampered, impeded, weighed down | free, unburdened, unimpeded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy boxes encumber the room. = 沉重的箱子占据了房间。
His many responsibilities encumber him. = 他众多的责任让他不堪重负。


一般过去时:
The old car was encumbered with rust. = 那辆旧车锈迹斑斑。
The project was encumbered by delays. = 项目因延误而受阻。


现在完成时:
The business has been encumbered by debt for years. = 这家企业多年来一直负债累累。
They have been encumbered by legal battles. = 他们一直被法律纠纷所困扰。


过去完成时:
The ship had been encumbered by heavy cargo. = 船只之前装载了大量的货物。
The negotiations had been encumbered by mistrust. = 谈判因不信任而受阻。


将来时:
The new regulations will encumber small businesses. = 新规将给小型企业带来负担。
The construction project will be encumbered by bad weather. = 建设项目将因恶劣天气而受阻。


虚拟语气:
If the company were not encumbered by debt, it would expand. = 如果公司没有债务负担，它就会扩张。
I wish I were not encumbered by so many commitments. = 我希望我没有那么多承诺。


被动语态:
The progress of the research was encumbered by a lack of funding. = 研究的进展因缺乏资金而受阻。
The athlete was encumbered by an injury. = 运动员因受伤而受阻。


疑问句:
Is the process encumbered by unnecessary regulations? = 这个过程是否受到不必要的规章制度的阻碍？
Are they encumbered by their past mistakes? = 他们是否被过去的错误所困扰？


否定句:
The path was not encumbered by obstacles. = 道路没有障碍物。
She was not encumbered by convention. = 她不受习俗的约束。


PHRASE->
encumbered with debt = 负债累累
encumbered by regulations = 受规章制度的约束
encumbered by problems = 问题重重
encumbered with responsibilities = 责任重大
legally encumbered = 法律上受限


词源:
From Middle English encumbren, from Old French encombrer, from combrer "to obstruct," from Old French combre "heap, obstacle," of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old High German chumbor "bundle, heap."
