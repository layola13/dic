pontons:/ˈpɒntɒnz/| n.|浮桥；浮筒；驳船|A ponton is a flat-bottomed boat or structure that floats on the water and is used as part of a temporary bridge or floating dock.  They are often made of metal or concrete and can be connected together to create larger structures.|Example: The army built a ponton bridge across the river.  军队在河上架起了一座浮桥。

The engineers used several pontons to create a stable platform for the construction work.工程师们使用了几个浮筒来为建筑工作创建一个稳定的平台。|近义词：float, pontoon, barge|反义词：bridge(permanent)
