crossposts:/ˈkrɔːsˌpoʊsts/| n.|交叉发布，转载|The act of posting the same content to multiple online platforms or forums.  It's a common strategy to reach a wider audience.|例句：She crossposted her blog article to several social media sites to maximize reach. 她将她的博客文章交叉发布到几个社交媒体网站以最大限度地扩大影响范围。|例句：The announcement was crossposted on the company's website and internal forum.  该公告已在公司网站和内部论坛上交叉发布。|近义词：reposts, shares, distributes|反义词：exclusive posting, single platform posting|名词
