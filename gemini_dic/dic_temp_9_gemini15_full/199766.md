sirname:/ˈsɜːrneɪm/| n. |姓氏|A surname is a family name; the name shared by members of a family.  It is inherited and passed down through generations.  It's also known as a last name or family name.|例句：My surname is Smith. = 我的姓是史密斯。|例句：What is your full name, including your surname? = 你的全名是什么，包括你的姓？|近义词：family name, last name, family surname|反义词：given name, first name
