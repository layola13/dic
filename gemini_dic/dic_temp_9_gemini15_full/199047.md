significance:/sɪɡˈnɪfɪkəns/| n. |重要性；意义；意思|The importance, meaning, or value of something.|The significance of the discovery was immediately apparent.  这个发现的重要性是显而易见的。|The study highlights the significance of early intervention. 这项研究强调了早期干预的重要性。|importance, meaning, weight|insignificance, triviality, unimportance|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The significance of this event is undeniable. = 这件事的重要性是不容否认的。
His work holds great significance in the field of medicine. = 他的工作在医学领域具有重大意义。


一般过去时:
The significance of his contribution was not fully understood at the time. = 当时人们并没有完全理解他的贡献的重要性。
The meeting held little significance for me. = 那次会议对我来说意义不大。


现在完成时:
Scientists have recognized the significance of climate change. = 科学家们已经认识到气候变化的重要性。
The full significance of her actions has yet to be determined. = 她行为的全部意义还有待确定。


过去完成时:
By then, the significance of the warning had become clear. = 到那时，警告的重要性已经变得清晰了。
They hadn't grasped the significance of the data until later. = 直到后来他们才意识到数据的意义。


将来时:
The significance of this research will become apparent in the future. = 这项研究的意义将在未来变得显而易见。
We will understand the significance of this event in time. = 我们最终会理解这一事件的意义。


虚拟语气:
If the significance of this problem were understood, action would have been taken sooner. = 如果人们理解这个问题的重要性，就会早点采取行动。
I wish I had understood the significance of his words earlier. = 我真希望我早点理解他话语的意义。


被动语态:
The significance of the findings was highlighted in the report. = 报告中强调了研究结果的重要性。
The true significance of the event is being debated. = 该事件的真正意义正在被争论。


疑问句:
What is the significance of this change? = 这种变化的意义是什么？
What significance do you attach to these results? = 你如何看待这些结果的意义？


否定句:
The study did not fully explore the significance of social factors. = 这项研究没有完全探索社会因素的重要性。
His contribution was of little or no significance to the project. = 他的贡献对这个项目几乎没有意义。
