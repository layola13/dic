pico:/ˈpiːkoʊ/| n. |皮克，微微|A prefix meaning one trillionth (10^-12).  It is used in the metric system to denote a unit one trillionth of a base unit. For example, a picometer is one trillionth of a meter.|例句：The size of an atom is measured in picometers. 原子的尺寸是用皮米来测量的。|近义词：None (unique prefix)|反义词：None (unique prefix)|名词
