paleology:/ˌpeɪliˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |古生物学 |The study of ancient life, including fossils and other evidence of past organisms.  Paleontology encompasses the study of fossils to infer the forms of past life, evolution and the relationships between organisms. |例句：Paleology provides valuable insights into the history of life on Earth. 古生物学为地球生命史提供了宝贵的见解。|例句：The paleology department at the university is renowned for its research on dinosaurs.  大学的古生物学系以其恐龙研究而闻名。|近义词：paleozoology, palaeontology |反义词：N/A
