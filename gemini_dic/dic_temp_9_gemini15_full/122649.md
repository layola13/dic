modish:/ˈmoʊdɪʃ/| adj.|时髦的，流行的，新潮的|Stylish and fashionable;  conforming to or reflecting the current fashion.|The modish young woman wore the latest designer dress.|近义词：stylish, fashionable, trendy, chic|反义词：old-fashioned, outdated, passé


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Her style is always modish. = 她的风格总是很时髦。
That color is quite modish this season. = 这种颜色在这个季节非常流行。

一般过去时:
Her dress was very modish last year. = 她的裙子去年非常时髦。
The hat was quite modish in the 1920s. = 这顶帽子在20世纪20年代非常流行。


现在完成时:
Her designs have become increasingly modish recently. = 她的设计最近越来越时髦了。
The shop has always stocked modish clothing. = 这家店一直都有时尚的服装。


过去完成时:
The painting style had already become modish before he started to paint it. = 在他开始画这幅画之前，这种绘画风格就已经流行了。
They had already adopted a modish hairstyle before the trend became popular. = 在这种发型流行之前，他们就已经采用了这种时髦的发型。


将来时:
This style will be modish next year. = 这种风格明年将会很流行。
Those colors are predicted to be modish in the coming season. = 预测这些颜色将在下一个季度流行。
