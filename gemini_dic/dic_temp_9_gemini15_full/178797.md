recure:/rɪˈkjʊr/| v.|复发，重现，治愈|To occur again after a period of inactivity; to heal or cure (a disease or injury).|例句：His illness recurred after a period of remission.  他的病在缓解期后复发了。|例句：The doctor managed to recure the patient's broken leg. 医生成功治愈了病人的骨折。|近义词：reappear, relapse, heal, cure|反义词：cure (in the sense of preventing recurrence), resolve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem recurs frequently. = 这个问题经常复发。
The pain recurs every few hours. = 疼痛每隔几个小时就会复发。

一般过去时:
The symptoms recurred after a week. =  一周后症状复发了。
The infection recurred despite treatment. = 尽管接受了治疗，感染还是复发了。


现在完成时:
The rash has recurred several times. = 皮疹已经复发了好几次。
The headache has recurred since yesterday. = 自从昨天开始头痛就一直复发。


过去完成时:
The cough had recurred before the doctor's visit. = 在看医生之前咳嗽已经复发了。
The fever had recurred several times that month. = 那一个月发烧已经复发了好几次。


将来时:
The doctor fears the disease will recur. = 医生担心这种疾病会复发。
I'm afraid the problem will recur next year. = 我担心这个问题明年会再次发生。


虚拟语气:
If the infection recurred, I would seek immediate medical attention. = 如果感染复发，我会立即寻求医疗救助。
If the pain recurred, he would take another pill. = 如果疼痛复发，他会再吃一颗药。


被动语态:
The infection was recurred despite the treatment. = 尽管进行了治疗，感染还是复发了。(注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常用主动语态)
The problem is recurred every year. = 这个问题每年都会复发。(注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Will the disease recur? = 这种病会复发吗？
Does the pain recur often? = 疼痛经常复发吗？


否定句:
The illness did not recur. = 病没有复发。
The problem hasn't recurred since the repair. = 自从维修后，问题就没有再发生。


PHRASE->
recur frequently = 经常复发
recur repeatedly = 反复复发
recur suddenly = 突然复发
recur unexpectedly = 出乎意料地复发
recur with alarming regularity = 以惊人的规律性复发
