semi-hoop:/ˈsemiːhuːp/| n.|半环，半圆形物|A semi-hoop is a structure or object that is roughly half of a hoop or circle.  It may be a partial ring, a semicircle, or a similar shape.|例句：The artist used a semi-hoop of willow branches to create the sculpture.  艺术家用柳条编织的半环来制作雕塑。


例句：The earring was a delicate semi-hoop of gold. 这枚耳环是一个精致的金半环。


近义词：half-circle, semicircle, partial ring


反义词：full hoop, complete circle

词性:名词
