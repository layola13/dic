nonstimulating: /ˌnɒnˈstɪmjʊleɪtɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈstɪmjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 无刺激的；乏味的 | Not stimulating; dull and unexciting.  The lecture was nonstimulating and many people fell asleep. | 近义词：dull, boring, tedious, uninspiring, monotonous | 反义词：stimulating, exciting, interesting, engaging


例句:

The nonstimulating work environment led to low morale among employees. = 无聊的工作环境导致员工士气低落。

The movie was nonstimulating and lacked any suspense or action. = 这部电影很无聊，缺乏任何悬念或动作。


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "without".
* **stimulating**:  adjective derived from the verb "stimulate", meaning to encourage or arouse interest or excitement.  "Stimulate" itself comes from Latin *stimulāre* ("to prick, goad").
