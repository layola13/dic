discernible:/dɪˈsɜːrnəbl/|/ˈdɪsɜːrnəbəl/| adj. |可辨别的；可识别的；可觉察的 |easily seen, heard, understood, or recognized.  Something that is discernible is noticeable or clear enough to be seen, heard, or understood. |The difference between the two products is barely discernible.  两种产品的区别几乎难以察觉。

The faint sound was barely discernible. 微弱的声音几乎难以辨别。


近义词：perceptible, noticeable, detectable, apparent, obvious


反义词：imperceptible, indistinguishable, unnoticeable, obscure


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The signature is clearly discernible. 签名清晰可见。
The pattern is easily discernible.  图案很容易辨认。


一般过去时:
The message was discernible only with a microscope.  只有用显微镜才能辨别出信息。
The details were discernible in the photograph. 照片中的细节清晰可见。


现在完成时:
The results have been discernible since the beginning of the experiment.  自从实验开始以来，结果就一直很明显。
The improvement has been discernible over the past few months. 过去几个月，进步已经很明显了。


过去完成时:
The symptoms had become discernible several weeks before the diagnosis.  在诊断前的几周，症状就已经可以辨别出来了。
The error had been discernible in the early stages of the project.  错误在项目的早期阶段就已经可以察觉了。


将来时:
The effects of the medication will be discernible within a few hours.  药物的效果将在几小时内显现出来。
The results of the study will be discernible by the end of the year.  研究结果将在年底可见。


虚拟语气:
If the problem were discernible, it would be easy to solve. 如果问题可以辨别出来，就很容易解决。
I wish the cause of the failure were more discernible. 我希望失败的原因能更容易辨别出来。


被动语态:
The subtle differences were discernible only to experts. 细微的差异只有专家才能辨别出来。
The change in temperature was not discernible by the naked eye. 温度的变化肉眼无法察觉。


疑问句:
Is the difference in color discernible? 颜色上的差异是否可以辨别？
Are the details discernible in this image? 这张图片中的细节是否清晰可见？


否定句:
The handwriting was not discernible. 字迹难以辨认。
The signal was not discernible above the noise. 信号在噪声中无法辨别。


PHRASE->
barely discernible: 几乎无法辨别的
easily discernible: 易于辨别的
clearly discernible: 清晰可见的
hardly discernible: 几乎无法察觉的
just discernible: 勉强可以辨别的

词源：
discernible源自于动词discern，其拉丁语词根为discernere，由dis- (分开)和cernere (区分，分辨)组成。因此，discern的意思是“辨别，区分”，而discernible则表示“可以被辨别或区分的”。
