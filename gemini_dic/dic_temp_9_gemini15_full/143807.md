outpacing:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪsɪŋ/ | v. |超越，超过，赶超 |To move or progress faster than; surpass in speed or rate of progress. |例句：The company's growth is outpacing its competitors.  这家公司的增长速度超过了它的竞争对手。|例句：Inflation is outpacing wage increases.通货膨胀的速度超过了工资增长的速度。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, outrun |反义词：lag behind, fall short of

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The technology sector is outpacing other industries in innovation. = 科技行业在创新方面超过其他行业。
She consistently outpaces her colleagues in sales. = 她在销售方面总是超过她的同事。


一般过去时:
Last year, our sales outpaced our projections. = 去年，我们的销售额超过了我们的预期。
The runner outpaced all his competitors in the final sprint. = 在最后的冲刺中，这位跑步运动员超过了所有竞争对手。


现在完成时:
Technological advancements have outpaced our ability to regulate them. = 技术进步已经超过了我们监管它们的能力。
The company has outpaced its rivals in market share. = 这家公司在市场份额上已经超过了它的竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outpaced every other participant. = 到比赛结束时，他已经超过了其他所有参赛者。
The project had outpaced its original timeline. = 项目已经超过了最初的时间表。


将来时:
Demand is expected to outpace supply in the coming months. = 预计未来几个月需求将超过供应。
Experts predict that renewable energy will outpace fossil fuels in the next decade. = 专家预测，可再生能源将在未来十年超过化石燃料。


虚拟语气:
If the company continues this trend, it will outpace its competitors within a year. = 如果公司继续保持这种趋势，它将在一年内超过竞争对手。
I wish I could outpace the challenges I'm facing. = 我希望我能克服我面临的挑战。


被动语态:
The growth of the city was outpaced by the influx of new residents. = 城市的增长速度被大量涌入的新居民超过了。
His record was outpaced by a younger athlete. = 他的纪录被一位更年轻的运动员超越了。


疑问句:
Is the demand for electric vehicles outpacing the supply? = 电动汽车的需求是否超过了供应？
Does the country's population growth outpace its economic growth? = 该国的经济增长速度是否超过了人口增长速度？


否定句:
The team's performance did not outpace their opponents. = 该队的表现没有超过对手。
Our efforts to improve efficiency have not outpaced the rising costs. = 我们提高效率的努力并没有超过不断上涨的成本。
