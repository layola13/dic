nocturne:/ˈnɒktɜːn/ /ˈnɑːktɜːrn/| n. |夜曲；夜景|A nocturne is a piece of music, typically for piano, that is characterized by a dreamy, romantic, and often melancholic mood, evoking the feeling of night. It often features flowing melodies, soft harmonies, and a generally tranquil atmosphere.  |Example: Chopin's nocturnes are renowned for their beauty and emotional depth.  肖邦的夜曲以其美丽和情感深度而闻名。|近义词： serenade, ballad, reverie |反义词：diurnal piece,  daytime composition|名词
