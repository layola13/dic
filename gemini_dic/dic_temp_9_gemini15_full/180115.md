reflux:/ˈriːflʌks/| n. v.|反流；倒流；回流|The backward flow of fluid.  This can refer to various bodily fluids, like stomach acid or blood.|例句：Acid reflux is a common digestive problem.  胃酸反流是一种常见的消化问题。
例句：The reflux of blood into the heart caused a murmur.  血液回流到心脏导致了杂音。|近义词：backflow, regurgitation|反义词：forward flow, efflux|名词，动词
