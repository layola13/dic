kwashiorkor:/ˌkwɑːʃiˈɔːrkɔːr/| n. | 夸氏病 | A severe form of malnutrition characterized by edema, irritability, and growth retardation, primarily affecting young children.  It is caused by a deficiency of protein in the diet, often accompanied by adequate calorie intake. | Example:  The child suffered from kwashiorkor due to a lack of protein in their diet.  夸氏病儿童由于饮食中缺乏蛋白质而患病。|近义词：protein-energy malnutrition, severe malnutrition |反义词：None (it's a disease state, not a condition with a direct opposite)|名词
