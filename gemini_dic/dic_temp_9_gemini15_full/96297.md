indigeneity:/ˌɪndɪdʒəˈnɛɪti/| n. |本土性；土著性；原住民身份|The state of being indigenous; the quality of belonging originally to a place. It encompasses cultural practices, social structures, and a deep connection to the land.  The concept often includes historical injustices and ongoing struggles for self-determination.|例句：The museum's exhibit showcased the rich indigeneity of the Amazonian tribes.  博物馆的展览展示了亚马逊部落丰富的本土性。|例句：  Recognition of indigeneity is crucial for addressing historical injustices and promoting reconciliation.  承认原住民身份对于解决历史不公和促进和解至关重要。|近义词：nativity, aboriginality, indigenism|反义词：foreignness, alienness
