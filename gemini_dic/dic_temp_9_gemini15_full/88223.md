hindermost:/ˈhɪndərməʊst/| adj.|最远的，最末的，最偏僻的 | Furthest or farthest back; most remote.  This word is a superlative adjective, implying comparison among multiple items.  It typically refers to position, especially in a series or sequence. |例句：The hindermost carriage of the train derailed. 火车的最后一节车厢脱轨了。

The hindermost house on the street was dilapidated. 街上最里面的一座房子破败不堪了。


近义词：farthest, furthest, last, rearmost | 反义词：foremost, frontmost


词性：形容词
