interparental:/ˌɪntərˈpærənṭəl/| adj. |父母间的；父母双方的| Relating to or involving both parents.  The term typically refers to the relationship, interaction, or dynamics between two parents, especially concerning their child or children. |例句：Interparental conflict can negatively impact a child's development. 父母间的冲突会对孩子的发育产生负面影响。

例句：The study examined interparental agreement on child-rearing practices.  这项研究考察了父母双方在养育子女方面的意见一致性。


近义词：biparental (强调两个父母), parental (更广义的父母相关的)

反义词：unilateral (单方面的), single-parent (单亲的)

词性：形容词
