oversteadiness: /ˌoʊvərˈstedinəs/ | n. | 过于稳定；过度稳定 |


英文解析：The state or quality of being excessively steady or stable;  going beyond a desirable level of steadiness, often implying rigidity or inflexibility.  It suggests a lack of dynamism, adaptability, or responsiveness.


例句：

The company's oversteadiness in its marketing strategies led to a decline in sales as it failed to adapt to changing consumer preferences.  公司的营销策略过于稳定，导致销售额下降，因为它未能适应不断变化的消费者偏好。

His oversteadiness in his beliefs prevented him from considering alternative viewpoints. 他过于固执己见，妨碍了他考虑其他观点。


近义词：rigidity, inflexibility, immobility, stagnation,  unresponsiveness


反义词：adaptability, flexibility, dynamism, fluidity, responsiveness


词性：名词
