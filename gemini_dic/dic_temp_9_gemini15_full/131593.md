noncompetitively:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvli/ | adv. |非竞争性地，非竞争性地|  In a manner that does not involve competition or rivalry.  It describes an action or process that occurs without competing against others.|

The drug was administered noncompetitively.  药物是非竞争性给药的。

The two companies worked noncompetitively on the project. 这两家公司在项目上非竞争性地合作。


近义词：uncompetitively, cooperatively

反义词：competitively
