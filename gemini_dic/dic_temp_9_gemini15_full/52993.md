dinned:/dɪnd/| vt.|重复灌输；喧闹地重复；使厌烦|To repeat something persistently and forcefully, often to the point of annoyance.  The sound was loud and persistent.|例句：The teacher dinned the rules into the students' heads. 老师把规则反复灌输给学生。

The advertisement dinned its message into our consciousness. 广告不断地向我们灌输它的信息。|近义词：hammer, reiterate, repeat, drone on|反义词：whisper, murmur
