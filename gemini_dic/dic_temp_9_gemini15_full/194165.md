self-interpretations:/ˌsɛlf ɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 自我解释；个人理解 |  The act of interpreting something based on one's own understanding and perspective, often without external input or validation.  It can refer to the process of making sense of one's own experiences, thoughts, feelings, or actions.  | 例句：Her self-interpretations of the poem were quite different from the critic's analysis.  她的对这首诗的个人理解与评论家的分析大相径庭。

Her self-interpretations of her childhood trauma significantly impacted her adult relationships. 她对自己童年创伤的个人解读极大地影响了她成年后的关系。| 近义词：self-analysis, personal understanding, subjective interpretation | 反义词：objective interpretation, external analysis | 名词
