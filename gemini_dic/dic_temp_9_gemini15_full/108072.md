laterality:/ˌlætəˈræləti/| n. |侧向性，偏侧性 |The preference for using one hand or foot over the other, or a similar preference for one side of the body over the other.  It refers to the functional asymmetry of the brain and body. |例句：Research on handedness is a major area of study within the field of laterality.  研究左右手偏好是侧向性研究领域中的一个主要课题。|例句：The study explored the relationship between language laterality and musical aptitude. 这项研究探讨了语言侧向性和音乐天赋之间的关系。|近义词：sidedness, asymmetry |反义词：bilaterality
