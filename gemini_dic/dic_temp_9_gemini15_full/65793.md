eviction:/ɪˈvɪkʃən/ | n. |驱逐，逐出 |The act of forcing someone to leave a property, typically a tenant from rented accommodation.  |例句：The eviction of the tenants caused a lot of hardship.  驱逐房客造成了很多困难。|例句：The landlord threatened eviction if the rent wasn't paid. 房东威胁说如果房租不付就驱逐。|近义词：expulsion, ousting, removal |反义词：acceptance, admission


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The landlord evicts tenants who don't pay rent. = 房东驱逐不付房租的租客。
Eviction often leads to homelessness. = 驱逐往往导致无家可归。


一般过去时:
They were evicted from their apartment last month. = 他们上个月被赶出了公寓。
The court ordered the eviction of the squatters. = 法院下令驱逐非法占用者。


现在完成时:
The family has been evicted twice this year. = 这个家庭今年已经被驱逐了两次。
Many people have experienced eviction due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济低迷，许多人经历了驱逐。


过去完成时:
They had been evicted before they even had a chance to appeal. = 甚至在他们有机会上诉之前，他们就被驱逐了。
The tenants had already been evicted when the police arrived. = 当警察到达时，租户已经被驱逐了。


将来时:
The tenants will be evicted if they don't pay the rent by Friday. = 如果租客在星期五之前不付房租，他们将被驱逐。
He fears he will be evicted soon. = 他担心自己很快就会被驱逐。


虚拟语气:
If the rent was lower, they wouldn't have faced eviction. = 如果房租低一些，他们就不会面临驱逐。
I wish I hadn't ignored the eviction notice. = 我真希望我没有忽视驱逐通知。


被动语态:
The tenants were evicted without warning. = 租客们在没有任何警告的情况下被驱逐了。
He was evicted for violating the lease agreement. = 他因为违反租赁协议而被驱逐。


疑问句:
Will they face eviction if they are late with the rent? = 如果他们房租迟交，他们会面临驱逐吗？
Has the eviction notice been served yet? = 驱逐通知已经送达了吗？


否定句:
The tenants were not evicted, despite their late rent payments. = 尽管租客们房租迟交，但他们没有被驱逐。
The landlord did not initiate the eviction process. = 房东没有启动驱逐程序。
