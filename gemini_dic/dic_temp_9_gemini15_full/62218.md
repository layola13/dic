encrinal: /ɪnˈkraɪnəl/ | adj. | 海百合的；海百合状的 | Relating to or resembling encrinites (sea lilies).  The term refers to the fossilized remains of crinoids, which are marine animals.  | Example: The encrinal limestone contained numerous fossilized sea lily stems.  海百合石灰岩包含许多海百合茎的化石。 | 近义词: crinoidal | 反义词:  None readily available.  (The antonym would describe something *not* resembling sea lilies.)
