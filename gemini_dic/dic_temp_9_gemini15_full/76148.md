fuselages:/ˈfjuːzəlɑːʒz/| n. |机身，机体|The main body of an aircraft, to which the wings and tail are attached.|例句：The engineers inspected the fuselage for any damage after the emergency landing.  工程师在紧急着陆后检查了机身是否有任何损坏。

例句：The airline grounded all its planes after discovering a crack in the fuselage of one aircraft. 航空公司在一架飞机的机身上发现裂缝后，将所有飞机停飞。


近义词：airframe, body (of an aircraft)


反义词：无


词性：名词


词源：源自法语 *fuselage，最终源自拉丁语 *fusilis，意思是“易熔的”，指早期飞机机身是用可熔化的材料制成的。
