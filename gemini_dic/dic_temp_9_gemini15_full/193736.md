self-contempts:/ˈsɛlf kənˈtɛmpts/ | n. | 自卑；自我厌恶 |  A feeling of intense dislike or dissatisfaction with oneself.  It often stems from perceived failings or inadequacies.  | Example:  Her self-contempts prevented her from pursuing her dreams.  她的自卑感阻止了她追求梦想。  |近义词: self-loathing, self-hatred, low self-esteem | 反义词: self-love, self-acceptance, high self-esteem | 名词
