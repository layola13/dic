monos:/ˈmɒnəʊz/ /ˈmɑːnoʊz/| n.| 单数；单音节词 | Monos refers to a single unit or element, often used in linguistic contexts to describe words with a single syllable or single sound.  | Example:  The word "cat" is a monosyllable.  这个词“猫”是一个单音节词。|近义词：single, one, unit |反义词：plural, multiple, polysyllabic
