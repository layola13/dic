insulins:/ˈɪnsəlɪnz/| n. |胰岛素|A hormone produced in the pancreas by the islets of Langerhans that regulates the amount of glucose in the blood.  The lack of insulin causes a form of diabetes. |例句：The doctor prescribed insulin injections to manage his diabetes.  医生开了胰岛素注射剂来控制他的糖尿病。|例句：  Regular monitoring of blood glucose levels is crucial for patients on insulin therapy. 定期监测血糖水平对于使用胰岛素治疗的患者至关重要。|近义词：hormone (in this context)|反义词：无|名词
