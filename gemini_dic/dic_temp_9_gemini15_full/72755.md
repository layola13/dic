foggier:/ˈfɒɡiər/| adj.| 更加雾蒙蒙的，更加模糊的 | More foggy; less clear.  It describes a state of increased fog or a lack of clarity, often metaphorical. |例句：The foggier the weather, the more difficult it is to drive. 天气越雾蒙蒙，开车越困难。

例句：The details of the plan are still rather foggier than we'd like. 计划的细节仍然比我们希望的模糊得多。

近义词：mistier, hazier, cloudier

反义词：clearer, brighter


词性:形容词
