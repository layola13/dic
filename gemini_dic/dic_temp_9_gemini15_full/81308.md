grap:/ɡræp/| n.| 抓，抓住；葡萄；钩；图表|The word "grap" refers to the act of seizing something firmly with one's hand or claws, or to the result of such an action. It can also refer to a cluster of grapes, a hook or a chart or graph. |例句：He grap the rope and pulled himself up. 他抓住绳子，把自己拉了上来。|近义词：clutch, seize, snatch, grasp|反义词：release, let go


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grasps the concept quickly. 他很快理解了这个概念。
She grasps the opportunity. 她抓住了机会。

一般过去时:
I grasped the handle of the door. 我抓住了门把手。
The monkey grasped the banana. 猴子抓住了香蕉。

现在完成时:
He has grasped the main points of the lecture. 他已经掌握了讲座的主要内容。
She has grasped the chance to study abroad. 她抓住了出国留学的机会。

过去完成时:
I had grasped the meaning before he finished speaking.  在他说完话之前我已经理解了意思。
They had grasped the opportunity to invest. 他们抓住了投资的机会。

将来时:
I will grasp the chance to travel. 我将抓住旅行的机会。
She will grasp the meaning soon. 她很快就会明白意思。

虚拟语气:
If I had grasped the opportunity, I would have succeeded. 如果我抓住了机会，我就会成功。
I wish I had grasped the importance of that moment. 我真希望当时我已经意识到了那一刻的重要性。

被动语态:
The rope was grasped firmly. 绳子被牢牢地抓住了。
The opportunity was grasped by her. 机会被她抓住了。

疑问句:
Did he grasp the situation? 他是否理解了情况？
Can you grasp the meaning of this sentence? 你能理解这句话的意思吗？

否定句:
He didn't grasp the significance of the event. 他没有理解事件的重要性。
She didn't grasp the opportunity. 她没有抓住机会。
