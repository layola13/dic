mezzo-soprani:/ˈmɛtsoʊ soʊˈprɑːni/| n. |女中音歌唱家|A mezzo-soprano is a female singer whose vocal range lies between that of a soprano and an alto.  They possess a rich, warm tone and often sing roles requiring both lyrical beauty and dramatic power.|  Many famous operas feature prominent mezzo-soprano roles.  The renowned mezzo-soprano Renée Fleming is known for her expressive performances.|近义词：contralto (though contralto is generally lower)|反义词：tenor, bass, soprano
