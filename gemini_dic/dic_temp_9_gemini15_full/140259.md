offshore:/ˈɔːfʃɔːr/ /ˈɒfʃɔːr/| adj., adv., n.|近海的；海外的；离岸的|Relating to or located in the sea at some distance from the shore.  It can also refer to activities or businesses based outside a country to avoid taxes or regulations.|例句：The offshore wind farm is a significant source of renewable energy.  近海风力发电场是可再生能源的重要来源。

The company moved its operations offshore to reduce its tax burden.  该公司将业务转移到海外以减少税收负担。


近义词：overseas, foreign, coastal (for location), extraterritorial (for activities)
反义词：onshore, inland, domestic
