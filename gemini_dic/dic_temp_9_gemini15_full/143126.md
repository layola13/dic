outcarol: /ˌaʊtˈkærəl/ | v. |  大声歌唱，超过…的歌声 | To sing louder or more skillfully than someone else; to surpass in singing. |  例句：The choir outcaroled the soloist. 合唱团的歌声盖过了独唱演员。 | 近义词：outsing, outvoice | 反义词：be outsung by, be outvoiced by | 动词
