chaomancy:/ˈkeɪməmænsi/| n. | 占卜术，风水术 | Divination by means of interpreting the movements and configurations of the earth and its features, especially mountains and rivers.  It often involves the use of maps and geomantic figures. |  The geomancer used chaomancy to determine the auspicious location for the new temple.  风水师用占卜术来确定新庙宇的吉利位置。|  geomancy, divination |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：rational planning, scientific analysis) | 名词
