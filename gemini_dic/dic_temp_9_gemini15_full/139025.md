oafs:/oʊfs/| n.|笨蛋，粗人|A person who is considered to be stupid and clumsy.  An oaf is someone who is considered insensitive, awkward, and generally unpleasant. They often lack social grace and may be physically clumsy.|例句：He's such an oaf; he tripped over the rug again. 他真是个笨蛋，他又绊倒在地毯上了。|例句：Don't be such an oaf!  Be more considerate of others.  别那么粗鲁！多为别人考虑一下。|近义词：clod, lout, buffoon, dolt, goon|反义词：gentleman, lady, refined person, sophisticated person|名词
