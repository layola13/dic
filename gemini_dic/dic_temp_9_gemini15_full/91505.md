hymnographer:/haɪˈmɒnəɡræfər/ | n. |圣歌作者，赞美诗作者 | A hymnographer is a composer or writer of hymns.  They create religious songs, often for use in church services. |  The renowned hymnographer composed a moving piece for the cathedral's centennial.  这位著名的圣歌作者为大教堂的百年庆典创作了一首感人的作品。| composer, lyricist |  profaner (antonym is less direct, as it refers to someone who desecrates sacred things) | 名词
