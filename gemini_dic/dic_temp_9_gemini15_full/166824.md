press-agents: /ˈprɛsˌeɪdʒənts/ | n. |新闻代理人，宣传人员 | People or companies that create and manage publicity for a client.  They work to shape public opinion and create a positive image for their clients through various media channels. | Example: The press agents worked tirelessly to promote the new film.  新闻代理人夜以继日地宣传这部新电影。  |近义词：publicists, publicists, spin doctors | 反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑与“批评家”或“揭露者”形成对比。 | 名词
