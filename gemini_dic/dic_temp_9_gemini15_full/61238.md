emails:/ˈiːmeɪlz/| n.|电子邮件|Electronic messages sent and received via the internet.|I received several emails this morning.  今天早上我收到几封电子邮件。|letters, messages|snail mail|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I send emails every day. = 我每天都发电子邮件。
He checks his emails several times a day. = 他一天查看几次电子邮件。

一般过去时:
She sent an email last night. = 她昨晚发了一封邮件。
They emailed me about the meeting. = 他们就会议给我发了邮件。

现在完成时:
I have sent three emails today. = 我今天已经发了三封邮件。
She has received many emails from her clients. = 她收到了很多来自客户的邮件。

过去完成时:
I had already sent the email before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经发了邮件。
They had emailed the documents before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经发了文件。

将来时:
I will send you an email tomorrow. = 我明天会给你发邮件。
He will email the report as soon as it's finished. = 报告一完成，他就会发邮件。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would email you. = 如果我有时间，我会给你发邮件。
I wish I had emailed her sooner. = 我真希望我早点给她发邮件。

被动语态:
The email was sent yesterday. = 邮件是昨天发的。
Many emails are received daily. = 每天收到很多邮件。

疑问句:
Have you received my email? = 你收到我的邮件了吗？
Did he send the email? = 他发了邮件吗？

否定句:
I didn't send the email. = 我没有发邮件。
She hasn't received my email yet. = 她还没有收到我的邮件。
