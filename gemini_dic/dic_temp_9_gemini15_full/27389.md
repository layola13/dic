camellias:/kəˈmɛliəz/ | n. |茶花属植物；山茶花 |Camellias are a genus of flowering plants in the family Theaceae.  They are evergreen shrubs or small trees with showy flowers. Many species and cultivars are grown as ornamentals. |例句：The garden was ablaze with the vibrant colours of numerous camellias. 花园里盛开着许多颜色鲜艳的山茶花，一片绚丽。

The camellias in her garden were her pride and joy. 她花园里的山茶花是她引以为傲的珍宝。|近义词：tea rose (部分品种), azalea (部分品种) |反义词：无
