replaster:/ˌriːˈplæstər/ | vt. | 重新粉刷；重新抹灰 | To apply a fresh coat of plaster to (a wall, ceiling, etc.).  | Example: We need to replaster the bathroom walls after the leak. 我们需要在漏水后重新粉刷浴室的墙壁。 |近义词:recoat, repair, renovate |反义词:destroy, demolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He replasters walls for a living. = 他以粉刷墙壁为生。
She replasters the damaged section of the ceiling. = 她重新粉刷了天花板受损的部分。

一般过去时:
The builders replastered the entire hallway. = 建筑工人重新粉刷了整个走廊。
They replastered the kitchen after the flood. = 洪水过后，他们重新粉刷了厨房。

现在完成时:
He has replastered the damaged area. = 他已经重新粉刷了受损的区域。
They have replastered several rooms in the house. = 他们已经重新粉刷了房子里的几个房间。

过去完成时:
Before the painting, they had replastered the walls. = 在粉刷之前，他们已经重新粉刷了墙壁。
The contractor had replastered the ceiling before installing the new light fixture. = 承包商在安装新的灯具之前已经重新粉刷了天花板。

将来时:
They will replaster the damaged wall tomorrow. = 他们明天将重新粉刷受损的墙壁。
We will replaster the entire house next month. = 我们下个月将重新粉刷整栋房子。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would replaster the whole house. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新粉刷整栋房子。
I wish I had replastered the walls sooner. = 我希望我早点重新粉刷墙壁。

被动语态:
The walls were replastered last week. = 墙壁上周重新粉刷了。
The ceiling is being replastered at the moment. = 天花板正在重新粉刷。

疑问句:
Will the contractor replaster the damaged areas? = 承包商会重新粉刷受损的区域吗？
Has the plasterer replastered the entire building yet? = 泥瓦匠已经重新粉刷了整栋建筑物了吗？

否定句:
They did not replaster the bathroom this year. = 他们今年没有重新粉刷浴室。
We won't replaster the walls until next spring. = 我们直到明年春天才会重新粉刷墙壁。
