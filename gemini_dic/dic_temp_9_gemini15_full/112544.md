loved-up: /lʌvd ʌp/ | adj. | 热恋中的，充满爱意的 |  Describes a couple or person who is very much in love and showing it openly.  It implies a state of intense affection and happiness. |

例句：

They were so loved-up, constantly holding hands and gazing into each other's eyes. = 他们热恋中，不停地手牵着手，深情对视着。

The loved-up couple couldn't stop smiling. = 这对热恋中的情侣笑个不停。


近义词：in love, smitten, lovestruck, amorous

反义词：heartbroken, unloved, estranged


词性:形容词
