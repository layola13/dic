reinvaded:/ˌriːɪnˈveɪd/| v.|再次入侵，重新侵略|To invade again; to attack and occupy a place a second time.|The enemy reinvaded the territory after a brief truce. 敌人短暂休战后再次入侵该领土。|近义词：reoccupy, reconquer|反义词：evacuate, withdraw


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The army reinvades the country every year.  军队每年都入侵该国。
They reinvade the market with a new product. 他们用新产品再次进军市场。

一般过去时:
The barbarians reinvaded the Roman Empire.  野蛮人再次入侵罗马帝国。
The company reinvaded the market after a period of decline. 公司在一段时间的衰退后再次进军市场。


现在完成时:
The insects have reinvaded the house.  昆虫再次入侵了房子。
The virus has reinvaded the computer system. 病毒再次入侵了电脑系统。


过去完成时:
The army had reinvaded the city before the reinforcements arrived. 增援部队到达之前，军队已经再次入侵了这座城市。
The disease had reinvaded the population after a period of remission.  在一段时间的缓解期后，疾病再次侵袭了人口。


将来时:
The enemy will reinvade the border region next spring. 敌人将在明年春天再次入侵边境地区。
The competitor will reinvade the market with a new strategy. 竞争对手将采取新战略再次进军市场。


虚拟语气:
If the enemy reinvaded, we would be prepared. 如果敌人再次入侵，我们将做好准备。
I wish they hadn't reinvaded our privacy. 我真希望他们没有再次侵犯我们的隐私。


被动语态:
The country was reinvaded by a foreign power. 该国遭到外国势力的再次入侵。
The market was reinvaded by a cheaper competitor. 市场被一个更便宜的竞争对手再次入侵。


疑问句:
Will the army reinvade the territory? 军队会再次入侵该领土吗？

否定句:
The enemy did not reinvade the country. 敌人没有再次入侵该国。
The company chose not to reinvade the market. 公司选择不再次进军市场。
