rostellum:/rɒˈstɛləm/| n. |喙状物；小喙；(兰花)蕊柱附属物|A small beak-like appendage, especially the beak-like projection on the column of an orchid.  It often plays a role in pollination. |Example: The rostellum of the orchid deflected the pollen onto the bee's back.  兰花的蕊柱附属物将花粉弹到蜜蜂的背上。|近义词：beak, projection, appendage |反义词：无
