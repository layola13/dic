pedestals:/ˈpɛdəstəlz/ | n. |基座，底座；支架 |A pedestal is a support or stand, typically for a statue or vase.  It can also refer to a position of high regard or importance. |例句：The ancient statue stood proudly on its marble pedestal. 古代雕像骄傲地矗立在它的白色大理石基座上。

The singer was placed on a pedestal by her adoring fans.  这位歌手被她崇拜的歌迷捧上了神坛。|近义词：base, stand, support, plinth |反义词：top, summit


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The vase sits on a pedestal. = 花瓶放在基座上。
These statues are on pedestals. = 这些雕像都在基座上。


一般过去时:
The artist placed the sculpture on a pedestal. = 艺术家把雕塑放在底座上。

We have placed all the trophies on their pedestals. = 我们已经把所有的奖杯都放在它们的底座上了。

过去完成时:
