mediaevals:/ˌmiːdiˈiːvəlz/| adj.|中世纪的；中古时代的|Relating to the Middle Ages in Europe (roughly from the 5th to the 15th century).  The term often evokes a sense of chivalry, feudalism, and religious influence.  |例句：The castle is a magnificent example of mediaeval architecture. 这座城堡是中世纪建筑的杰出范例。
The novel depicts a mediaeval romance. 这部小说描绘了一段中世纪的爱情故事。|近义词：medieval|反义词：modern, contemporary
