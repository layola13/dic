plodded:/ˈplɑːdɪd/ | v. | 沉重地走；艰难地进行；|To walk slowly and heavily; to make slow, heavy progress.|例句：He plodded through the mud. 他在泥泞中艰难地行走。

例句：She plodded on with her work, despite feeling tired.  她尽管感到疲倦，但还是继续埋头工作。


近义词：trudged, lumbered, struggled, toiled| 反义词：dashed, sped, raced | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plods along the road every day. = 他每天在路上慢吞吞地走。
They plod through their work. = 他们艰难地完成工作。


一般过去时:
We plodded home in the rain. = 我们在雨中艰难地走回家。
The hiker plodded up the steep mountain path. = 徒步旅行者吃力地沿着陡峭的山路向上走。


现在完成时:
He has plodded through many hardships in his life. = 他一生经历过许多磨难。
She has plodded along, patiently waiting for her chance. = 她一直默默地等待机会。


过去完成时:
They had plodded for hours before reaching the village. = 他们走了几个小时才到达村庄。
He had plodded through the entire book before he finally understood it. = 他通读了整本书才最终理解。


将来时:
I will plod on, regardless of the obstacles. = 无论遇到什么障碍，我都会继续前进。
They will plod through the next few weeks of work. = 他们将艰难地度过接下来的几周工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it would be so difficult, I wouldn't have plodded through. = 如果我知道这么难，我就不会坚持下去了。
I wish I hadn't plodded along that difficult path. = 我真希望我没有走那条艰难的路。


被动语态:
The work was plodded through by the team. = 这项工作被团队艰难地完成了。
The journey was plodded through slowly and laboriously. = 这段旅程缓慢而艰难地走完了。


疑问句:
Did he plod through the forest? = 他是否在森林里艰难地跋涉？
Will she plod along, even when she is tired? = 即使她累了，她还会继续前进吗？


否定句:
He did not plod through the task; he completed it quickly. = 他没有艰难地完成任务；他很快完成了。
They did not plod along aimlessly; they had a clear plan. = 他们没有漫无目的地缓慢前进；他们有一个明确的计划。
