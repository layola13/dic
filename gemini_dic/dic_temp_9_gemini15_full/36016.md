codifies:/ˈkoʊdəˌfaɪz/ | vt. |将…编成法典；将…系统化；把…列入法典 | To arrange (something) into a system or code; to put into a code or system.  |例句：The new law codifies existing regulations. 这部新法律将现有的规章制度编纂成法典。|例句：The company codified its internal procedures. 这家公司将其内部程序系统化。|近义词：systematize, organize, formalize |反义词：disorganize, randomize |动词
