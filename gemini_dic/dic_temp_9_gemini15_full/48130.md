decodes:/diːˈkoʊdz/ | vt. |解码，译解；破译 | To convert (a coded message) into intelligible language.  It also refers to the process of understanding or interpreting something complex or cryptic. |例句：The spy decoded the enemy's secret message.  间谍破译了敌人的秘密信息。


The computer program decoded the encrypted file. 电脑程序解码了加密文件。

近义词： decipher, decrypt, interpret, translate | 反义词：encode, encrypt
