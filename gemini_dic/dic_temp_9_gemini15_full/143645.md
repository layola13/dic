outkilled:/ˌaʊtˈkɪld/| v.| 杀死了…更多的人；超过…杀死的人数 |To surpass someone in the number of people killed.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "out-" to the verb "killed," indicating exceeding or surpassing in the action of killing. |例句：The allied forces outkilled the enemy, resulting in a decisive victory. 盟军杀死的敌人数量超过了敌军，从而取得了决定性的胜利。 The ruthless dictator outkilled all his rivals in the power struggle. 残酷的独裁者在权力斗争中杀死了所有竞争对手。|近义词：surpassed, exceeded, outmatched|反义词：underkilled (not a common word)
