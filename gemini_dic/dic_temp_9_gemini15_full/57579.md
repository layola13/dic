/ˈdræməˌtɜːrdʒ/|/ˈdræməˌtɜːʒ/| n. |剧作家；戏剧理论家|A dramaturge is a literary or theatre professional who specializes in the dramatic arts.  They may be involved in the creation, analysis, or criticism of plays. Their roles can vary widely, from collaborating with playwrights to adapting existing works for the stage. |The dramaturge worked closely with the playwright to refine the script. 剧作家与编剧密切合作，改进了剧本。| playwright, scriptwriter, literary critic |  |  n.
