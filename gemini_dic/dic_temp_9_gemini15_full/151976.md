pasttime:/ˈpɑːstaɪm/ | n. |消遣；娱乐；业余爱好 | A pasttime is an activity that someone does regularly for pleasure, especially during their free time.  It is something done to relax, enjoy oneself, or relieve stress. | Example:  Gardening is a relaxing pasttime for many people.  许多人把园艺当作一种轻松的消遣。


Reading is my favorite pasttime. 阅读是我最喜欢的消遣。


近义词：hobby, leisure activity, recreation, amusement


反义词：work, chore, duty, obligation
