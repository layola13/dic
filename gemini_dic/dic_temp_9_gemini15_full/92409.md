hypogene:/ˈhaɪpədʒiːn/| adj.|内生的；地下成因的| formed or occurring beneath the surface of the earth; relating to processes that occur beneath the Earth's surface.  The term is primarily used in geology and mineralogy. |例句：The ore deposits are hypogene in origin, formed by hydrothermal fluids circulating deep within the Earth.  这些矿床是内生成因的，由在地球深处循环的热液形成。|近义词：endogenetic, endogenous |反义词：epigenetic, exogenetic|
