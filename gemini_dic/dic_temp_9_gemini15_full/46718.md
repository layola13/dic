dancing:/ˈdænsɪŋ/ | v.ing |跳舞，舞蹈 | The act of moving rhythmically to music, typically with the feet and legs. It can be a form of expression, recreation, or performance. | 例句：She loves dancing to salsa music. = 她喜欢跳萨尔萨舞。

The children were dancing joyfully at the party. = 孩子们在派对上快乐地跳舞。


近义词：moving rhythmically, twirling, swaying | 反义词：standing still, being immobile


词性：动名词
