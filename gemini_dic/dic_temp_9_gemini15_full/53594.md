disbursing:/dɪsˈbɜːrsɪŋ/ | v. |支付；支出；发放 | The act of paying out money; distributing funds.  |例句：The company is disbursing bonuses to its employees. 公司正在向员工发放奖金。|例句：The government is disbursing funds for disaster relief. 政府正在拨款用于救灾。|近义词：paying out, distributing, dispensing, allotting |反义词：receiving, collecting, accumulating |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The treasurer disburses the funds every month. = 出纳员每月支付资金。
She disburses the money to the needy. = 她把钱发放给穷人。


一般过去时:
The company disbursed a large sum of money last year. = 公司去年支付了一大笔钱。
They disbursed the donations to various charities. = 他们将捐款分配给各个慈善机构。


现在完成时:
The organization has disbursed millions of dollars in aid. = 该组织已拨款数百万美元用于援助。
He has disbursed all the funds allocated to the project. = 他已支付了分配给该项目的所有资金。


过去完成时:
Before the project started, they had already disbursed the initial funding. = 项目启动前，他们已经支付了初始资金。
The bank had disbursed the loan before the deadline. = 银行在截止日期前已发放贷款。


将来时:
The government will disburse the emergency relief funds next week. = 政府将于下周发放紧急救济金。
The company will disburse the profits among its shareholders. = 公司将把利润分配给股东。


虚拟语气:
If the bank disbursed the loan quickly, we could start the project immediately. = 如果银行迅速发放贷款，我们就能立即启动项目。
I wish they had disbursed the funds earlier. = 我希望他们早点发放资金。


被动语态:
The funds were disbursed according to the agreed schedule. = 资金按照商定的时间表支付。
The money is being disbursed to the victims of the storm. = 资金正在发放给风暴的受害者。


疑问句:
When will the company disburse the salaries? = 公司什么时候发放工资？
How is the organization disbursing the donations? = 该组织如何发放捐款？


否定句:
The company did not disburse the bonuses this year. = 公司今年没有发放奖金。
They haven't yet disbursed the funds for the research project. = 他们还没有为研究项目拨款。



PHRASE->

disburse among = 分配给
disburse to = 发放给
