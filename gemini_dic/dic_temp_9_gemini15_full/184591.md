restored:/rɪˈstɔːrd/| vt. |修复；恢复；重建；归还|To bring back to a former or original state.  It implies a return to a previous condition after damage, decay, or loss.|例句：The antique clock was beautifully restored. 这座古董钟表被精美地修复了。

例句：They restored the old house to its former glory. 他们把老房子恢复了昔日的辉煌。


近义词：rehabilitate, renovate, repair, recover|反义词：destroy, ruin, damage, demolish |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He restores antique furniture. = 他修复古董家具。
The government restores historical buildings. = 政府修复历史建筑。


一般过去时:
The artist restored the damaged painting. = 艺术家修复了受损的绘画。
They restored the old car to its original condition. = 他们把旧车恢复到了原来的状态。


现在完成时:
She has restored several old houses in the neighborhood. = 她已经修复了附近几栋老房子。
The museum has restored the ancient artifacts. = 博物馆已经修复了古代文物。


过去完成时:
They had restored the church before the anniversary. = 他们在周年纪念日之前修复了教堂。
The engineer had restored the power supply before the deadline. = 工程师在截止日期前恢复了电力供应。


将来时:
We will restore the damaged bridge next year. = 我们明年将修复受损的桥梁。
They will restore the old garden to its former beauty. = 他们将把旧花园恢复到以前的美丽。


虚拟语气:
If they had more money, they would restore the entire building. = 如果他们有更多的钱，他们就会修复整栋建筑。
I wish they had restored the old park. = 我希望他们已经修复了旧公园。


被动语态:
The painting is being restored by a famous artist. = 这幅画正在由一位著名的艺术家修复。
The damaged statue was restored last year. = 受损的雕像去年被修复了。


疑问句:
Will the damaged car be restored? = 受损的汽车会被修复吗？
Have they restored the old library yet? = 他们已经修复旧图书馆了吗？


否定句:
They did not restore the old building. = 他们没有修复旧建筑。
She could not restore the damaged vase. = 她无法修复受损的花瓶。
