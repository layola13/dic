oped:/ˈɒpɛd/| n.|观点文章；专栏文章|An op-ed is a usually opinionated newspaper or magazine article that is written by a person who is not a staff member of the publication.  It is typically placed on the page opposite the editorial page.|Example:  She wrote an op-ed piece about the importance of early childhood education. 她写了一篇关于幼儿教育重要性的观点文章。|近义词：column, commentary, editorial (though op-eds are distinct from editorials)|反义词：news report, factual article|名词
