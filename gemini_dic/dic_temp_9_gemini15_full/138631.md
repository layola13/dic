nucleolus:/ˈnjuːkliəʊləs/| n. |核仁|The nucleolus is a small, dense, spherical structure in the nucleus of a cell, which is responsible for producing ribosomes. It is composed primarily of RNA and proteins.  The nucleolus is not membrane-bound, but rather a region within the nucleus where ribosome biogenesis takes place.|Example: The nucleolus plays a crucial role in protein synthesis within the cell. 核仁在细胞内的蛋白质合成中起着至关重要的作用。 The electron micrograph clearly showed the nucleolus within the cell's nucleus. 电子显微照片清楚地显示了细胞核内的核仁。|近义词：nuclear body (less specific)|反义词：None|名词
