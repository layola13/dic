nonpathologically:/ˌnɒnˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. |非病理地；非致病性地 |  Describes something that is not caused by or related to disease. It indicates a normal or healthy condition, in contrast to something that is pathological. | Example: The changes in the brain were determined to be nonpathologically related to aging.  大脑的变化被确定与衰老的非病理因素有关。| 近义词：normally, naturally, healthily | 反义词：pathologically, diseasedly
