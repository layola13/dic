crags:/kræɡz/| n.|峭壁，岩崖|Rough, steep, and irregular rocks, especially on a mountainside.  The word often implies a rugged and possibly dangerous terrain.|The climbers faced treacherous crags during their ascent.|近义词：cliffs, rocks, precipices|反义词：plains, lowlands

The crags loom large over the valley. = 峭壁高耸在山谷之上。
These crags are home to rare birds. = 这些峭壁是稀有鸟类的栖息地。
