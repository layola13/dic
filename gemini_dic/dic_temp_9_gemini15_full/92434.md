hyponym: /ˈhaɪpənɪm/ | n. |下义词；属概念词 | A word or phrase that is a more specific instance of a more general word or phrase (its hypernym).  For example, "dog" is a hyponym of "animal," and "red" is a hyponym of "color".  It represents a subordinate term within a hierarchical taxonomic structure. |  "Dog" is a hyponym of "mammal."  The word "car" is a hyponym of "vehicle". |近义词：subordinate term, specific instance |反义词：hypernym, superordinate term |
