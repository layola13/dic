reorientates:/ˌriːɔːriənˈteɪts/| v.|重新定向；使重新定位；使改变方向 |To change the direction or focus of something or someone; to adjust or realign something.|例句：The company reorientates its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司调整了其营销策略，以瞄准更年轻的人群。|例句：The navigator reorientates the ship towards the safe harbor. 导航员将船重新定向到安全港。|近义词：redirect, readjust, realign|反义词：misdirect, disorient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reorientates its marketing strategy every year. = 公司每年都会调整其营销策略。
He reorientates his life after the accident. = 事故后他重新规划了自己的生活。

一般过去时:
They reorientated the furniture to maximize space. = 他们重新摆放家具以最大限度地利用空间。
The government reorientated its economic policy. = 政府调整了其经济政策。

现在完成时:
She has reorientated her career towards environmental conservation. = 她已经将自己的职业生涯转向了环境保护。
The company has reorientated itself to focus on sustainable practices. = 公司已经调整自身方向，专注于可持续实践。

过去完成时:
They had reorientated the building's layout before the renovation. = 他们在装修前已经重新设计了建筑物的布局。
The team had reorientated their approach after the initial failure. = 在第一次失败后，团队已经调整了他们的方法。

将来时:
We will reorientate the project's goals to meet the new requirements. = 我们将重新调整项目的目标以满足新的要求。
The organization will reorientate its training programs to improve efficiency. = 该组织将重新调整其培训计划以提高效率。

虚拟语气:
If they reorientated their thinking, they would find a better solution. = 如果他们改变思维方式，他们会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish I had reorientated my priorities sooner. = 我希望我早点调整我的优先级。

被动语态:
The map was reorientated to show the new highway. = 地图被重新定向以显示新的高速公路。
The company's strategy is being reorientated to improve customer satisfaction. = 公司的策略正在被调整以提高客户满意度。

疑问句:

否定句:
