reinviting:/ˌriːɪnˈvaɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 再邀请的；重新邀请的 |  Inviting again; suggesting or implying a renewed invitation.  This word describes something that is inviting again, suggesting a second or repeated invitation, often implying a more welcoming or appealing nature than the original invitation. | Example: The reinviting atmosphere of the newly decorated restaurant drew in more customers.  重新装修的餐厅营造出再次吸引人的氛围，吸引了更多顾客。  |近义词:  attractive, appealing, alluring | 反义词:  repulsive, uninviting, off-putting |
