/aɪz/ | /aɪz/ |后缀| 使…成为；使…化；变成…|  表示“使成为……；使……化”的意思，通常加在名词或形容词后面构成动词。它源于希腊语后缀“-ίζειν (-izein)”，表示“使成为”。|


英文解析:The suffix "-ize" is used to form verbs from nouns or adjectives, meaning "to make or become."  It's a productive suffix, meaning it's frequently used to create new words.  The suffix ultimately derives from the Greek suffix "-ίζειν (-izein)," which had a similar meaning.

例句:
To modernize a building = 使建筑现代化
To organize a meeting = 组织会议


近义词:  -ify, -ate (depending on the context)


反义词:  There isn't a direct antonym for "-ize," as it depends entirely on the word it's attached to.  The antonym would be the opposite action of the verb created.  For example, the antonym of "modernize" might be "antiquate."


词性: 后缀 (suffix)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He organizes his day carefully. = 他仔细地安排他的一天。
They modernize their homes. = 他们使他们的家现代化。


一般过去时:
She modernized her kitchen last year. = 她去年把厨房现代化了。
He computerized the company's records. = 他将公司的记录电脑化了。


现在完成时:
They have computerized many of their operations. = 他们已经将许多业务电脑化了。
We have hospitalized the injured patient. = 我们已将受伤的病人送往医院。


过去完成时:
They had already privatized the company before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经将公司私有化了。
She had alphabetized the files before the meeting. = 会议前她已将文件按字母顺序排列。


将来时:
We will hospitalize him immediately. = 我们将立即把他送往医院。
They will finalize the contract tomorrow. = 他们明天将完成合同。


虚拟语气:
If they privatized the industry, it would create more jobs. = 如果他们将该行业私有化，将会创造更多就业机会。
If she hospitalized him earlier, he might have recovered faster. = 如果她早点把他送医院，他可能恢复得更快。


被动语态:
The building was modernized last year. = 这栋建筑物去年进行了现代化改造。
The files were alphabetized by the assistant. = 文件由助理按字母顺序排列。


疑问句:
Did they hospitalize the victim? = 他们是否将受害者送往医院？
Have you computerized your accounting system yet? = 你已经将你的会计系统电脑化了吗？


否定句:
They did not privatize the company. = 他们没有将公司私有化。
We haven't finalized the deal yet. = 我们还没有完成这笔交易。


PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases directly using "-ize" as a standalone element. Its usage is always within a larger word.  Examples would depend on the specific words created using "-ize."  For instance:

* **To finalize a deal:** 完成交易
* **To summarize a report:** 总结报告
* **To categorize data:** 分类数据
* **To symbolize something:** 象征某物
* **To harmonize voices:** 使声音和谐
