distantness: /ˈdɪstəntnəs/ | n. |疏远；冷漠；遥远 | The feeling of being far away, either physically or emotionally.  It can refer to a lack of closeness or connection between people, or to a great physical distance. |例句：The distantness between the two families was palpable.  两家人之间的疏远显而易见。

例句：The vast distantness of the desert overwhelmed her.  沙漠的广袤无垠让她感到震惊。


近义词：remoteness, aloofness, coldness, estrangement, separation


反义词：closeness, intimacy, nearness, warmth, familiarity


词性：名词
