quasi-original:/ˈkwɑːzi ˈɒrɪdʒənəl/| adj. | 准原创的；似原创的 |  Approaching originality; having some but not all of the characteristics of an original work.  It suggests a derivative work that incorporates enough unique elements to be considered somewhat original.  |例句：The film, while borrowing heavily from other sources, had a quasi-original plot that kept audiences engaged.|近义词：derivative, original-ish, semi-original |反义词：completely derivative, unoriginal
