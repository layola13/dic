ivory-like:/ˈaɪvəri laɪk/| adj.|象牙似的，象牙色的|Resembling ivory in color or texture; having a creamy white or yellowish-white color and a smooth, slightly glossy surface.|例句：The antique vase had an ivory-like sheen.  这古董花瓶有着象牙般的光泽。
例句：Her skin had an ivory-like pallor.  她的皮肤呈现出象牙般的苍白。|近义词：creamy, off-white, pearly, bone-colored|反义词：dark, black, colorful
