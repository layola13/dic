multichanneled:/ˌmʌltiˈtʃænld/| adj. |多渠道的，多通道的 |Having or using multiple channels or pathways.  This adjective describes systems or processes that utilize several channels for communication, distribution, or operation.  |例句：The company uses a multichanneled marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. 这家公司使用多渠道营销策略来接触更广泛的受众。

The company's multichanneled distribution network ensures products reach consumers quickly. 这家公司多渠道的分销网络确保产品能够快速到达消费者手中。|近义词：multifaceted, omnichannel, polychannel |反义词：single-channeled, unichanneled |形容词

词源:
The word "multichanneled" is a combination of "multi-" (meaning many or multiple) and "channeled" (referring to a channel or pathway).  "Channel" originates from the Old French "canel,"  ultimately from the Latin "canalis" meaning "pipe, channel, gutter."
