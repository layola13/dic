confrontative:/kənˈfrʌntətɪv/| adj.|对抗性的，好争论的，挑衅的|  Relating to or characterized by confrontation; tending to confront.  This adjective describes someone or something that is aggressively challenging or argumentative.|

例句：His confrontative attitude made it difficult to negotiate.  他的对抗性态度使得谈判难以进行。

例句：The article presented a confrontative view of the government's policies. 这篇文章对政府的政策提出了对抗性的观点。


近义词：aggressive, challenging, antagonistic, assertive, combative

反义词：accommodating, conciliatory, cooperative, submissive, yielding
