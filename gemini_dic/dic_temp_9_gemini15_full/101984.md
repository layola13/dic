isochimenal: /ˌaɪsoʊkaɪˈminəl/ | adj. | 等温线的；等值线的 | Relating to or denoting lines on a map connecting points of equal winter temperature.  Isochimenal lines are used in climatology to represent the distribution of winter temperatures across a region.
