outrivalling: /ˌaʊtˈraɪvəlɪŋ/ | v. | 超越；胜过 | surpassing or exceeding in excellence or achievement.  |例句：His latest novel is outrivalling all his previous works in terms of critical acclaim.  他的最新小说在评论界的好评方面超越了他以前的所有作品。
His performance outrivalled all expectations.他的表现超出了所有人的预期。|近义词：surpassing, excelling, exceeding, outdoing |反义词：underperforming, lagging behind

一般现在时:
His skills outrival those of his peers. 他的技能胜过他的同龄人。
This product outrivals all competitors in the market.  这款产品在市场上超越了所有竞争对手。

一般过去时:
Her painting outrivalled all others in the competition.  她的画在比赛中胜过其他所有作品。
The team outrivalled their opponents in the final match.  这支队伍在决赛中胜过了对手。

现在完成时:
She has outrivalled all her rivals in the business world. 她已经超越了她在商界的所有竞争对手。
This company has outrivalled its competitors in innovation. 这家公司在创新方面已经超越了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outrivalled all the other runners.  比赛结束时，他已经超越了所有其他跑步者。
The company had outrivalled its competitors in market share before the merger.  在合并之前，该公司在市场份额方面已经超越了其竞争对手。


将来时:
The new technology will outrival existing systems in efficiency.  这项新技术将在效率方面超越现有系统。
We expect that our product will outrival the competition in the coming year. 我们预计我们的产品将在来年超越竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he might outrival the world champion. 如果他训练更努力，他可能会超越世界冠军。
I wish I could outrival my brother in this game. 我希望我能在游戏中胜过我的哥哥。


被动语态:
His work was outrivalled by a younger artist. 他的作品被一位年轻的艺术家超越了。
The previous record was outrivalled by the new athlete.  之前的纪录被这位新的运动员打破了。


疑问句:
Does this technology outrival its predecessors?  这项技术是否超越了其前辈？
Will his new book outrival his previous bestsellers?  他的新书会超越他之前的畅销书吗？


否定句:
His attempt to outrival his colleagues failed. 他试图超越同事的尝试失败了。
The team did not outrival their opponents in the tournament. 这支队伍在锦标赛中没有战胜对手。
