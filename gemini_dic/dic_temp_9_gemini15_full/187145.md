roadblock:/ˈroʊdˌblɑːk/|/ˈrəʊdˌblɒk/| n. |路障；障碍；阻碍 |A roadblock is an obstacle that prevents progress or movement, either literally (like a physical barrier on a road) or figuratively (like a problem preventing the completion of a task).|例句：The police set up a roadblock to stop the fleeing suspects.  警察设置路障以阻止逃犯。

The lack of funding proved to be a major roadblock to the project. 资金短缺证明是该项目的主要障碍。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, barrier, hurdle |反义词：open road, clear path, solution


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The roadblock prevents traffic flow. = 路障阻止了交通流动。
That problem is a significant roadblock. = 那个问题是一个重大的障碍。


一般过去时:
