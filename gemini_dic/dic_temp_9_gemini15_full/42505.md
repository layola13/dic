counterinstance:/ˈkaʊntərɪnstəns/| n. |反例，反证 | An example that contradicts a statement or theory.  A counterinstance disproves a general statement by showing a specific case where the statement is false. |The claim that all swans are white is refuted by the counterinstance of black swans.  |反例：counterexample, exception |无明确反义词，但可以认为是“支持性例子”或“符合例证”的相对概念。|名词
