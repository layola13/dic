playday:/ˈpleɪdeɪ/| n. |游乐日，游戏日 |A day set aside for play and recreation, often for children.|例句：The school organized a playday with games and activities for the students. 学校为学生们组织了一个有游戏和活动的游乐日。  The children were looking forward to the annual playday at the park. 孩子们期待着在公园举行的年度游乐日。|近义词：holiday, festive day|反义词：workday
