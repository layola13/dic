pourability: /ˌpɔːrəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可倾倒性，易倾倒性 | The ease with which a liquid can be poured.  It often refers to the viscosity and fluidity of a liquid.  A liquid with high pourability flows easily. | 例句：The high pourability of the paint made it easy to apply.  这油漆的可倾倒性很高，很容易涂抹。


The pourability of the honey was affected by its temperature.  蜂蜜的可倾倒性受温度影响。| 近义词：flowability, fluidity | 反义词：thickness, viscosity
