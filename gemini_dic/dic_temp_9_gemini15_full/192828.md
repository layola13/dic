searchability:/ˌsɜːrtʃəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可搜索性 | The ease with which something can be found using a search engine or other search method.  It refers to how easily information or data can be retrieved.  A high searchability indicates that the information is easily accessible and discoverable. | Example:  The website's improved searchability has led to a significant increase in user engagement.  网站可搜索性的提高导致用户参与度显著增加。 |近义词：findability, accessibility | 反义词：inaccessibility, obscurity |  词性：名词
