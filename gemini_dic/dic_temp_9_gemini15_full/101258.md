invoke:/ɪnˈvoʊk/ | vt. | 祈求；恳求；调用；实施；引起 | To call upon or invoke (a deity, spirit, etc.) with prayers or incantations; to call forth (a spirit, etc.); to put into effect; to cite as an authority; to mention; to cause or bring about. | 例句：The shaman invoked the spirits to heal the sick.  巫师祈求神灵治愈病人。

例句：The program invokes a library function to perform calculations. 该程序调用库函数进行计算。


近义词：evoke, summon, implement, cite, cause, elicit

反义词：repress, suppress, ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He invokes the name of God in his prayers. 他在祈祷中祈求上帝的名号。
The software invokes a specific routine each time the user clicks the button. 软件每次用户点击按钮时都会调用特定例程。


一般过去时:
She invoked her rights under the contract. 她援引合同中的权利。
The judge invoked a precedent to support his ruling. 法官援引先例来支持他的裁决。


现在完成时:
They have invoked the emergency powers. 他们已经动用了紧急权力。
The programmer has invoked the debugging tools. 程序员已经调用了调试工具。


过去完成时:
He had invoked the help of his friends before he attempted the project. 在尝试这个项目之前，他已经求助于他的朋友们。
The system had invoked the backup routine before the crash. 系统在崩溃之前已经调用了备份程序。


将来时:
They will invoke the relevant clause in the agreement. 他们将援引协议中的相关条款。
The scientist will invoke a new theory to explain the phenomenon. 科学家将引用一种新的理论来解释这种现象。


虚拟语气:
If I were to invoke this law, the outcome would be different. 如果我要援引这项法律，结果将会不同。
I wish I could invoke some supernatural power to fix this. 我希望我能调用某种超自然力量来解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The rule was invoked to settle the dispute.  该规则被援引来解决争端。
The function is invoked automatically when the data changes.  当数据发生变化时，该函数会自动调用。


疑问句:
Will the company invoke its right to terminate the contract? 公司会行使终止合同的权利吗？
What law did he invoke to support his argument? 他引用什么法律来支持他的论点？


否定句:
He did not invoke the emergency clause. 他没有援引紧急条款。
The system did not invoke the error handling routine. 系统没有调用错误处理例程。



PHRASE->
invoke a law 援引法律
invoke a rule 援引规则
invoke a spirit 召唤灵魂
invoke a function 调用函数
invoke a method 调用方法


词源:
From Old French invoker, from Latin invocare, from in- ("in, into") + vocare ("to call").  The word's history shows its connection to the act of calling upon something or someone, be it a deity, a power, or a function in a program.
