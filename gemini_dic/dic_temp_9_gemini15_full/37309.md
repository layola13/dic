commat:/ˈkɒmæt/| n. |逗号|A punctuation mark (,) used to separate items in a series or to indicate a pause in a sentence.|例句：Remember to use commas in your writing.  记住在写作中使用逗号。|例句：There are three items on the list: apples, bananas, and oranges. 列表上有三样东西：苹果、香蕉和橘子。|近义词：separator|反义词:period, full stop
