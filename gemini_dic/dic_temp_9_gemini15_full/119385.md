microdensitometry:/ˌmaɪkroʊdɛnˈsɪtəmi/| n. |显微密度测定法|The measurement of the optical density of small areas of a photographic film or plate, or other similar material, using a microdensitometer. This technique is used in various fields, including astronomy, medicine, and materials science, to analyze the density variations within a sample.  |Example: Microdensitometry was used to analyze the variations in density on the photographic plate of the nebula.|近义词：densitometry|反义词：None
