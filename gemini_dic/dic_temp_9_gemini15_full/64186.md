equery:/ˈɛkwəri/ | n. |询问；质询；查问| An inquiry, especially a formal one.  A request for information. |例句：The police made an equery about the missing painting. 警方就失窃的画作进行了询问。

例句：He sent an equery to the library concerning the availability of certain books. 他向图书馆询问某些书籍是否有货。


近义词：inquiry, question, interrogation, query | 反义词：answer, response

词性:名词
