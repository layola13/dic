sect:/sekt/| n.|派别；宗派；部分；章节|A sect is a group of people with somewhat different religious beliefs from the main group to which they belong.  It can also refer to a division or faction within a larger group.|例句：The religious sect held a private ceremony.  这个宗教派别举行了一场私人仪式。|例句：The second sect of the book deals with the war.  这本书的第二部分论述了战争。|近义词：faction, group, division, chapter|反义词：whole, entirety
