recaptured:/ˌriːˈkæptʃərd/| v.|重新捕获；夺回；重获|To capture again; to regain possession of something that was lost or taken.|The police recaptured the escaped prisoner.  警察重新抓获了逃犯。|The army recaptured the lost territory.  军队收复了失地。|recovered, regained|lost, surrendered|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recaptured the feeling of childhood. 他重拾了童年的感觉。
They recapture the lost market share. 他们夺回了失去的市场份额。


一般过去时:
The soldiers recaptured the city after a long battle. 士兵们经过长时间的战斗后收复了这座城市。
She recaptured her composure after the initial shock. 她从最初的震惊中恢复了镇静。


现在完成时:
We have recaptured the original design. 我们已经恢复了最初的设计。
The company has recaptured its leading position in the market. 这家公司已经重新夺回了市场领先地位。


过去完成时:
The police had recaptured the suspect before he could escape the country.  警方在他逃离该国之前已经将他抓获。
They had recaptured their enthusiasm for the project after a period of doubt. 在一段时间的怀疑之后，他们重燃了对项目的热情。


将来时:
We will recapture the beauty of the past in our new design. 我们将在新的设计中重现过去的美丽。
The team hopes to recapture the championship title next year. 该队希望明年夺回冠军头衔。


虚拟语气:
If only we could recapture the magic of that summer. 如果我们能重温那个夏天的美好时光就好了。
I wish I could recapture my youth.  我希望我能重返青春。


被动语态:
The stolen painting was recaptured by the police.  失窃的画作被警方追回。
The lost memories were recaptured through therapy. 通过治疗，失落的记忆被找回。


疑问句:
Can we recapture the excitement of our first meeting? 我们能重温第一次见面的兴奋吗？
Will they be able to recapture their previous success? 他们能够重现过去的成功吗？


否定句:
They failed to recapture the public's imagination. 他们未能重新引起公众的兴趣。
We haven't been able to recapture the original flavor. 我们一直未能恢复原来的味道。
