peones:/pɪˈoʊnz/| n.|  步兵， 农民， 缺乏经验的人 |  The word "peones" refers to unskilled laborers, often agricultural workers, typically in a hierarchical system. It can also refer to pawns in a game of chess, highlighting their low status and lack of agency.  Historically, the term carried connotations of exploitation and subjugation. |  The peones worked tirelessly in the fields from sunrise to sunset.  那些农民从日出到日落都在田里辛勤工作。  The general treated his peones with disdain.将军轻蔑地对待他的步兵。 | 近义词：laborers, farmhands, peasants, pawns | 反义词：masters, bosses, elites
