dialysis:/daɪˈælɪsɪs/| n. |透析，渗析|The process of separating substances in solution by means of their different rates of diffusion through a semipermeable membrane.|例句：He needs dialysis three times a week. 他每周需要透析三次。|近义词：filtration,separation|反义词:synthesis,combination
