psychologist: /saɪˈkɒlədʒɪst/ /saɪˈkɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. |心理学家| A psychologist is a professional who studies the mind and behavior of humans and animals. They use scientific methods to understand and explain mental processes, emotions, and behaviors. Psychologists work in various settings, including hospitals, schools, universities, and private practices. They may conduct research, provide therapy, or both. |  Dr. Lee is a renowned psychologist specializing in child development. 李博士是一位专门研究儿童发展著名的临床心理学家。

The psychologist conducted a study on the effects of stress on memory.  这位心理学家进行了一项关于压力对记忆影响的研究。


|近义词:therapist, psychiatrist (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A |n.|
