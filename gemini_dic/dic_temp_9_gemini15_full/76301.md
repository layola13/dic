gabbler:/ˈɡæblər/| n.|喋喋不休的人，说话含糊不清的人|A gabbler is someone who talks rapidly and indistinctly, often in a rambling or incoherent manner.  They may be difficult to understand due to their speed or unclear pronunciation.|例句：He was a nervous gabbler, barely able to string a sentence together. 他是个紧张的喋喋不休的人，几乎无法连贯地说一句话。

例句：The politician was a notorious gabbler, known for his long, rambling speeches.  这位政客以冗长、散漫的演讲而闻名，是个臭名昭著的喋喋不休的人。


近义词：babbler, chatterbox, blabbermouth, voluble speaker|反义词：quiet person, concise speaker


词源：源自动词gabble，意为“快速而含糊不清地说话”，其词源可能与古法语单词"gabber"有关，意为"to chatter"  (喋喋不休)。
