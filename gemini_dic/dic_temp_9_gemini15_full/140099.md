off-licences:/ˈɒf ˈlaɪsnsɪz/ | n. |持照酒类零售商；（英式英语）酒类零售执照 | A business licensed to sell alcoholic beverages for off-premises consumption.  Off-licences are common in the UK and some other countries. They are distinct from pubs or bars, where alcohol is consumed on the premises. |  Many off-licences also sell snacks and mixers. 许多持照酒类零售商也出售零食和调味品。|The local off-licence sells a wide range of wines. 当地的持照酒类零售商出售各种各样的葡萄酒。|liquor store, wine shop | pub, bar |
