potassa:/pəˈtæsə/| n. |钾碱；碳酸钾|Potassa, also known as potash, is a potassium-rich plant ash.  It's a  mixture of potassium carbonate (K₂CO₃) and other potassium compounds. Historically, it was obtained by leaching wood ash. Today, it is primarily produced industrially.|The farmer used potassa to improve the soil's fertility.|近义词：potash|反义词：无
