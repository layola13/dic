re-braced:/ˌriːˈbreɪst/| v.|重新支撑；重新加固|To brace again; to strengthen or support something again.|例句：The engineer re-braced the weakened bridge support. 工程师重新加固了受损的桥梁支撑。


例句：After the storm, the homeowner re-braced the damaged fence. 暴风雨过后，房主重新加固了受损的围栏。


近义词：reinforce, strengthen, support|反义词：weaken, undermine


词性：动词

词源:
The word "re-braced" is formed from the prefix "re-" (meaning "again") and the verb "brace," which ultimately comes from the Old French word "bracer" (to arm, to make ready).  The sense of "brace" evolving to mean "to support" or "to strengthen" is a later development.
