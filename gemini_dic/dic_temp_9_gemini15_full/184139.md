resistibly:/rɪˈzɪstəbli/ | adv. | 不可抗拒地，难以抵挡地 | Irresistibly means in a way that is impossible to resist or oppose. It describes something so attractive, tempting, or compelling that one cannot help but be affected by it. | The aroma of freshly baked bread was irresistibly tempting.  新鲜出炉的面包香味令人难以抗拒。

The delicious cake was irresistibly appealing to the children.  美味的蛋糕对孩子们有着不可抗拒的吸引力。


近义词：irresistibly, compellingly, overwhelmingly, unavoidably | 反义词：resistably
