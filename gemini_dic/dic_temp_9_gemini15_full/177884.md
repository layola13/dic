rechabite:/ˈrɛkəbaɪt/| n.|禁酒会成员；节制者|A member of an ancient Arabian tribe, traditionally known for their abstinence from wine.  The term is often used more broadly to refer to anyone who abstains from alcohol.|例句：He considered himself a Rechabite, abstaining completely from alcohol. 他认为自己是禁酒会成员，完全不喝酒。

例句：The Rechabites were known for their strict adherence to their vows of temperance. 禁酒会成员以严格遵守他们的节制誓言而闻名。


近义词：teetotaler, abstainer, prohibitionist

反义词：drunkard, alcoholic


词性：名词
