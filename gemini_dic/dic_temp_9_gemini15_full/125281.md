multifibrous: /ˌmʌltiˈfaɪbrəs/ | adj. | 多纤维的，多纤维状的 | Composed of or having many fibers.  This adjective describes materials or structures containing numerous fibers. | Example: The multifibrous tissue showed significant damage after the injury.  多纤维组织在受伤后显示出明显的损伤。 The multifibrous structure of the tendon provided strength and flexibility. 肌腱的多纤维结构提供了强度和灵活性。|近义词：fibrous, multistranded | 反义词：unifibrous, nonfibrous
