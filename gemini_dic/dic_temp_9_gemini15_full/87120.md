herbalism: /ˈɜːrbəlɪzəm/ | n. | 草药学；草药疗法 | The study and practice of using herbs for medicinal purposes.  Herbalism encompasses the knowledge of identifying, harvesting, preparing, and administering herbal remedies.  It's a holistic approach to healthcare, often incorporating traditional practices and beliefs. | Example:  She studied herbalism for many years and now runs a successful clinic. 她学习草药学多年，现在经营着一家成功的诊所。


近义词：phytotherapy, herbology

反义词：allopathy (conventional medicine)
