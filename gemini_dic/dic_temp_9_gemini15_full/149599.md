pained:/peɪnd/| adj.|痛苦的，悲痛的| feeling or showing suffering or distress, either physical or mental.  The word describes a state of emotional or physical discomfort.|例句：He looked pained when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息时看起来很痛苦。


例句：Her pained expression showed her sorrow. 她痛苦的表情显示了她的悲伤。


近义词：hurt, aching, sore, distressed, sorrowful, grieved|反义词：joyful, happy, delighted, relieved, comfortable|词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pained by the loss of his friend. = 他因为失去朋友而感到痛苦。
The situation pains her deeply. = 这种情况让她非常痛苦。


一般过去时:
I was pained to hear of your accident. = 听到你发生意外，我很难过。
She was pained by his unkind words. = 她被他无情的话语伤透了心。


现在完成时:
I have been pained by their constant criticism. = 我一直被他们的不断批评所困扰。
She has been pained by the betrayal of her trust. = 她一直为背叛她的信任而痛苦。


过去完成时:
He had been pained by the news before he even received the phone call. = 他甚至在接到电话之前就已经被这个消息弄得很痛苦了。
She had been pained by the thought of leaving home. = 她一直为离开家而感到痛苦。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
