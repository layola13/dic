downhill:/ˌdaʊnˈhɪl/| adj., adv., n.|下坡的；走下坡路；下坡|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "downhill" describes something going or situated down a slope. As an adverb, it means in a downward direction.  As a noun, it refers to a downward slope or a decline in something.

例句:
The downhill slope was steep and dangerous. = 下坡很陡峭，很危险。

The car rolled downhill uncontrollably. = 汽车不受控制地向下坡滚去。


近义词:
descending, downward, declining


反义词:
uphill, upward, ascending


词性:
adjective, adverb, noun
