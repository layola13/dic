The company accused its competitors of predealing with key suppliers to secure scarce resources. 公司指控其竞争对手与主要供应商预先交易以确保稀缺资源。| 近义词：prearrangement, advance preparation, manipulation | 反义词：fair dealing, open dealing | 词性: verb
