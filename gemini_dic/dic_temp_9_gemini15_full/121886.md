miss out:/mɪs aʊt/| v.|错过，遗漏，失去|To fail to take advantage of an opportunity or experience.  |例句：I'm going to miss out on the party because I have to work. 我因为要工作而将错过派对。|例句：Don't miss out on this amazing offer! 不要错过这个绝佳的优惠！|近义词：lose, forfeit, omit|反义词：gain, acquire, obtain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misses out on a lot of fun because he studies too hard. 他因为学习太努力而错过了很多乐趣。
They miss out on important information if they don't read the instructions. 他们如果不阅读说明就会错过重要信息。


一般过去时:
I missed out on the sale because I was late. 我因为迟到而错过了促销。
We missed out on seeing the sunset because of the clouds. 因为云层遮挡，我们错过了日落。


现在完成时:
She has missed out on many opportunities because of her shyness. 她因为害羞错过了许多机会。
They have missed out on some great experiences during their travels. 他们在旅行中错过了一些很棒的体验。


过去完成时:
I realised I had missed out on a fantastic job opportunity. 我意识到我错过了一个绝好的工作机会。
He was disappointed that he had missed out on the concert. 他很失望，因为他错过了音乐会。


将来时:
I will miss out on the meeting if I don't leave now. 如果我现在不走，我就会错过会议。
You will miss out on a lot if you don't go to college. 如果你不上大学，你会错过很多东西。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been sick, I wouldn't have missed out on the trip. 如果我不生病，我就不会错过这次旅行。
I wish I hadn't missed out on that opportunity. 我真希望我没有错过那个机会。


被动语态:
He felt he was missed out on by the team. 他感觉自己被团队忽略了。(被动语态在此处略显牵强，更常见的表达是主动语态)
The information was missed out in the report. 报告中遗漏了该信息。


疑问句:
Did you miss out on anything interesting at the conference? 你在会议上错过了什么有趣的事情吗？
Will I miss out if I don't register early? 如果我不提前注册，我会错过什么吗？


否定句:
I made sure I didn't miss out on any details. 我确保我没有错过任何细节。
He tried his best not to miss out on the chance. 他尽力不让这个机会从手中溜走。
