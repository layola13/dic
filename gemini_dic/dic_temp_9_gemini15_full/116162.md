mascotte:/mæsˈkɒt/| n.|吉祥物，护身符|A mascot is a person, animal, or object believed to bring good luck, especially to a sports team or organization.  It is often a symbol that represents a group's identity and spirit. |Example: The team's mascot, a fierce-looking tiger, energized the crowd.  这个队的吉祥物，一只看起来很凶猛的老虎，激励了人群。|近义词：charm, talisman, lucky charm|反义词：jinx, ill omen
