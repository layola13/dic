immobilizers: /ɪˈməʊbɪlaɪzəz/ | n. | 防盗器，防盗装置 | A device that prevents a vehicle from being started without a specific key or code.  It is a crucial security feature designed to deter theft. |  例句：Many modern cars are equipped with immobilizers to prevent theft. 许多现代汽车都配备了防盗器以防止被盗。

例句：The mechanic installed a new immobilizer in my car after it was broken into.  技工在我车被撬后安装了一个新的防盗器。


近义词：theft deterrent, anti-theft device

反义词：ignition starter (in the context of facilitating starting, not preventing it)


词性: 名词
