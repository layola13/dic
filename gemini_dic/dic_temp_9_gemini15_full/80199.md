godlikenesses: /ˈɡɒdlaɪknəsɪz/ | n. |神性；神一般的特性；超凡脱俗的品质 |  The qualities or characteristics that resemble those of a god; attributes suggesting divine power, wisdom, or perfection.  It often implies exceptional abilities or qualities exceeding those of ordinary humans.


例句：

The artist's godlikenesses were evident in his breathtaking masterpieces.  艺术家的神性在他的令人叹为观止的杰作中显而易见。

His godlikenesses, particularly his unwavering courage in the face of danger, inspired awe in his followers. 他的神性，特别是面对危险时坚定不移的勇气，激起了追随者的敬畏。



近义词：divinity, godhood, superhumanity, magnificence, excellence


反义词：ordinariness, mediocrity, imperfection, insignificance


词性: 名词


词源:  The word is derived from "godlike" (adjective) + "-nesses" (suffix denoting plurality or abstract qualities).  "Godlike" itself comes from "god" + "-like" (suffix indicating resemblance).  The root "god" has origins in Proto-Germanic and Proto-Indo-European roots referring to deities or powerful figures.
