connoisseur:/ˌkɒnəˈsɜːr/ /ˌkɑːnəˈsɜːr/| n. |鉴赏家，行家 |A connoisseur has expert knowledge and keen appreciation of a particular field, especially in the fine arts, food, or drink.  They are able to discriminate between different qualities and appreciate subtleties. |He is a connoisseur of fine wines. 他是一位葡萄酒鉴赏家。|expert, aficionado, expert, specialist |layman, amateur, novice |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a connoisseur of art. = 他是一位艺术鉴赏家。
She is a connoisseur of fine dining. = 她是一位美食鉴赏家。

一般过去时:
He was a connoisseur of classical music. = 他是一位古典音乐鉴赏家。
She was a connoisseur of antique furniture. = 她是一位古董家具鉴赏家。

现在完成时:
He has become a connoisseur of whisky. = 他已经成为一位威士忌鉴赏家。
She has cultivated her palate as a connoisseur of cheese. = 她已经培养出了作为奶酪鉴赏家的味觉。

过去完成时:
He had already established himself as a connoisseur of paintings before he turned 30. = 他在30岁之前就已经成为一位著名的绘画鉴赏家了。
She had been a connoisseur of tea for many years. = 她多年来一直是位茶叶鉴赏家。

将来时:
He will be a renowned connoisseur of ceramics in the future. = 将来他将成为一位著名的陶瓷鉴赏家。
She will become a connoisseur of rare books. = 她将成为一位珍本书籍鉴赏家。

虚拟语气:
If I were a connoisseur of wine, I would know the difference. = 如果我是个葡萄酒鉴赏家，我会知道其中的区别。
I wish I were a connoisseur of opera. = 我真希望我是个歌剧鉴赏家。

被动语态:
He was considered a connoisseur of jazz music. = 他被认为是一位爵士乐鉴赏家。
Her expertise was recognized; she was regarded as a connoisseur of ancient artifacts. = 她的专业知识得到了认可；她被认为是古代文物的鉴赏家。

疑问句:
Is he a connoisseur of French cuisine? = 他是法国菜的鉴赏家吗？
Are you a connoisseur of modern art? = 你是现代艺术的鉴赏家吗？

否定句:
He is not a connoisseur of classical literature. = 他不是古典文学的鉴赏家。
She is not yet a connoisseur of single malt Scotch. = 她还不是单一麦芽苏格兰威士忌的鉴赏家。

词源:
From French *connoisseur*, from Old French *connoistre* "to know," from Vulgar Latin *cognoscere* "to know," from Latin *cognoscere*.  The word's evolution reflects the connoisseur's deep understanding and familiarity with their chosen field.
