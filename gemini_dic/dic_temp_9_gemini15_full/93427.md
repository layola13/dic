illuminance:/ɪˈluːmɪnəns/| n. |照度|The luminous flux incident on a surface per unit area.  It is a measure of how much light is falling on a surface.  The SI unit of illuminance is the lux (lx).|The illuminance in the room was low, making it difficult to read.|lux, brightness|darkness, shadow|名词

词源:
illuminance源于illumination (照明)，后者来自拉丁语illuminare（照亮），由illuminare的过去分词illuminatus派生而来。  “-ance”是名词后缀，表示状态或性质。
