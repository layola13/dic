self-rectifying: /ˌsɛlfˈrɛktɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 自校正的，自我修正的 |

英文解析：Describing a system or process that automatically corrects errors or deviations from a desired state without external intervention.  It implies an inherent capability to identify and compensate for problems.


例句：

The new software features a self-rectifying algorithm that automatically detects and fixes minor bugs.  = 新软件具有一个自校正算法，可以自动检测和修复小错误。

This self-rectifying circuit is designed to maintain a stable output voltage despite fluctuations in the input power. =  这个自校正电路的设计目的是为了在输入电压波动的情况下保持稳定的输出电压。


近义词：self-correcting, self-adjusting, automatic error correction


反义词：error-prone, requiring manual correction


词性：形容词
