shucking:/ʃʌkɪŋ/| v. |去壳；剥壳；欺骗；抛弃|The act of removing the shell or husk from something, especially corn or oysters.  It can also refer to a deceptive or dishonest act, or to abandoning something.|例句：He spent the afternoon shucking corn. 他下午都在剥玉米。|例句：The salesman was shucking us with his promises. 那个推销员用他的承诺欺骗我们。|近义词：husking, shelling, peeling, deceiving, discarding|反义词：shelling (in the sense of adding a shell), retaining, keeping
