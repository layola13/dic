eruption:/ɪˈrʌpʃən/ | n. |喷发；爆发；出疹 |The sudden or violent throwing out of something, especially lava from a volcano, or something similar.  It can also refer to a sudden outbreak of something, such as a disease or violence. |例句：The volcanic eruption destroyed the entire village. 火山喷发摧毁了整个村庄。

The eruption of violence shocked the nation.  暴力的爆发震惊了全国。|近义词：outburst, explosion, outbreak |反义词：subsidence, calm, quiescence
