reenjoys:/ˌriːɪnˈdʒɔɪz/ | v. |再次享受；重新享受 |To enjoy something again.  This word is a relatively uncommon neologism, formed by adding the prefix "re-" to "enjoys". It implies a renewed experience of pleasure or satisfaction. |例句：After a long break, she reenjoys the thrill of mountain climbing.  在她长时间休息后，她再次享受登山的刺激。

例句：He reenjoys his childhood memories by revisiting his hometown. 他通过重访家乡来重温童年的回忆。


近义词：relives, rediscovers, experiences again | 反义词：dislikes, abhors


词性:动词
