hurry:/ˈhʌri/| /ˈhʌri/| n./vi./vt.|匆忙，赶快| to move or act quickly |例句：There's no need to hurry.  = 没必要着急。

例句：He hurried to catch the train. = 他匆忙赶火车。|近义词：rush, hasten, expedite|反义词：delay, dawdle, procrastinate|动词/名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hurry to work every morning. = 我每天早上都匆匆忙忙去上班。
They hurry through their meals. = 他们匆匆忙忙地吃饭。

一般过去时:
He hurried home after work. = 他下班后匆匆忙忙地回家了。
We hurried to the airport to catch our flight. = 我们匆忙赶到机场赶飞机。

现在完成时:
I have hurried through this project to meet the deadline. = 我为了赶截止日期匆匆忙忙地完成了这个项目。
She has hurried all day. = 她一整天都匆匆忙忙的。

过去完成时:
I had hurried to finish my work before the meeting. = 我在开会前匆忙完成了我的工作。
They had hurried to get there on time. = 他们为了准时到达那里而匆匆忙忙。

将来时:
I will hurry to finish this task. = 我将尽快完成这项任务。
We will hurry to the station. = 我们将赶往车站。

虚拟语气:
I would hurry if I were you. = 如果我是你，我会赶快。
If I had hurried, I would have caught the bus. = 如果我早点赶，我就赶上公共汽车了。

被动语态:
The work was hurried, so there were many mistakes. = 工作很仓促，所以有很多错误。
The project was hurried to completion. = 该项目仓促完成。

疑问句:
Why are you hurrying so much? = 你为什么这么匆忙？
Should we hurry? = 我们应该快点吗？

否定句:
Don't hurry; we have plenty of time. = 不要着急，我们有很多时间。
They didn't hurry, so they missed the bus. = 他们不着急，所以错过了公共汽车。
