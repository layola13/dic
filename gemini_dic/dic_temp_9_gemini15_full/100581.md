intestinal:/ɪnˈtɛstɪnəl/| adj.|肠的；肠内的|Relating to or affecting the intestines.  The adjective refers to the intestines, the long tube in the body where digestion takes place.|例句：He experienced severe intestinal pain. 他经历了剧烈的肠痛。|例句：Intestinal bacteria play a crucial role in digestion. 肠道细菌在消化过程中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：enteric, visceral|反义词：extraintestinal
