hardtop:/ˈhɑːrtɒp/| n. |硬顶敞篷车；硬顶车；硬顶 |A hardtop is a car with a fixed metal roof, as opposed to a convertible.  It's a type of coupe or sedan with a non-removable roof. |例句：He bought a stylish new hardtop for his birthday. 他买了辆时尚的新硬顶车作为生日礼物。|例句：The hardtop offered better protection from the elements than a convertible. 硬顶车比敞篷车提供了更好的防风雨保护。|近义词：coupe, sedan (depending on the body style)|反义词：convertible, cabriolet
