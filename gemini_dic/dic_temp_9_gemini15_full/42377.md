counterbalancing: /ˈkaʊntərˌbælənsɪŋ/ | v. |平衡；抵消；使平衡|The act of balancing opposing forces or influences;  to arrange or design a study so that the effects of extraneous variables are minimized by distributing them equally across conditions. |例句：The experiment used counterbalancing to control for order effects.  实验采用平衡法来控制顺序效应。


The study employed counterbalancing to minimize bias.  这项研究采用平衡法来尽量减少偏差。


近义词：balancing, neutralizing, offsetting | 反义词：unbalancing, exacerbating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They counterbalance the risks and benefits. = 他们权衡风险和收益。
Researchers counterbalance the experimental conditions. = 研究人员平衡实验条件。


一般过去时:
The scientist counterbalanced the variables. = 科学家平衡了变量。
The team counterbalanced the design to minimize bias. = 团队平衡设计以尽量减少偏差。


现在完成时:
We have counterbalanced the effects of fatigue. = 我们已经平衡了疲劳的影响。
They have counterbalanced the experimental groups. = 他们已经平衡了实验组。


过去完成时:
She had counterbalanced the factors before the analysis. = 在分析之前，她已经平衡了这些因素。
The researchers had counterbalanced the order of the tasks. = 研究人员已经平衡了任务的顺序。


将来时:
We will counterbalance the workload. = 我们将平衡工作量。
They will counterbalance the experimental design. = 他们将平衡实验设计。


虚拟语气:
If we counterbalanced the data, the results would be more reliable. = 如果我们平衡数据，结果会更可靠。
It would be better if they counterbalanced the effects of practice. = 如果他们平衡练习的影响会更好。


被动语态:
The experimental effects were counterbalanced. = 实验效应得到了平衡。
The design was counterbalanced to control for order effects. = 设计被平衡以控制顺序效应。


疑问句:
How did they counterbalance the confounding variables? = 他们如何平衡混杂变量？
Have you counterbalanced the conditions in your study? = 你在研究中是否平衡了条件？


否定句:
The study did not adequately counterbalance the groups. = 该研究没有充分平衡各组。
They failed to counterbalance the effects of the intervention. = 他们未能平衡干预的影响。


PHRASE->
counterbalancing design = 平衡设计
counterbalancing technique = 平衡技术
counterbalancing method = 平衡方法
counterbalancing effects = 平衡效应
counterbalancing procedure = 平衡程序

词源:
Counter- (表示“反对”、“对抗”) + balancing (表示“平衡”)。  字面意思是“对抗性平衡”，引申为在实验设计中通过各种方法来平衡不同因素的影响，以避免偏差。
