relies:/rɪˈlaɪz/ | v. |依赖；依靠；信赖 | To depend on someone or something for support, help, or something else.  | 例句：The success of the project relies on the team's cooperation.  这个项目的成功取决于团队的合作。| 例句：He relies heavily on his parents for financial support. 他非常依赖父母的经济支持。|近义词：depends on, counts on, rests on | 反义词：is independent of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relies on his friends for support. = 他依靠朋友的支持。
She relies on public transport to get to work. = 她依靠公共交通工具上班。

一般过去时:
They relied on outdated technology. = 他们依赖过时的技术。
He relied on his instincts. = 他依靠他的直觉。

现在完成时:
We have relied on him for years. = 我们多年来一直依赖他。
The company has relied on innovation for its success. = 这家公司依靠创新取得了成功。

过去完成时:
They had relied on a faulty system. = 他们依赖了一个有缺陷的系统。
She had relied on her memory, which failed her. = 她依赖她的记忆，但记忆却让她失望了。

将来时:
I will rely on your expertise. = 我将依赖你的专业知识。
The project will rely heavily on funding. = 这个项目将严重依赖资金。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't rely on him. = 如果我是你，我不会依赖他。
I wish I hadn't relied on his promises. = 我真希望我没有依赖他的承诺。

被动语态:
The decision was relied upon by the committee. = 委员会依赖这个决定。
The system can be relied upon for accuracy. = 这个系统可以依赖其准确性。

疑问句:
Can you rely on him? = 你能依靠他吗？
Does the system rely on external data? = 这个系统依赖外部数据吗？

否定句:
I don't rely on hearsay. = 我不依赖道听途说。
The machine doesn't rely on electricity. = 这台机器不依赖电力。


PHRASE->
rely on/upon = 依赖，依靠
rely heavily on = 严重依赖
rely entirely on = 完全依赖
to rely on oneself = 自力更生
rely for support = 依靠…的支持


词源:
The word "rely" comes from the Old French word "relier," meaning "to bind back" or "to unite." This reflects the sense of dependence and connection implied by the word.  The suffix "-ies" indicates the third-person singular present tense of a verb.
