floppy disk:/ˈflɒpi dɪsk/ | n. |软盘 |A floppy disk is a type of magnetic disk storage that was commonly used to store and transfer computer data before the widespread adoption of USB flash drives and other storage methods.  It's characterized by its flexible, thin magnetic disk enclosed in a square or rectangular plastic casing. | I saved my document on a floppy disk. 我把我的文档保存到软盘上了。  My old computer only used floppy disks. 我的旧电脑只使用软盘。| USB flash drive, hard drive | hard drive, solid-state drive | 名词
