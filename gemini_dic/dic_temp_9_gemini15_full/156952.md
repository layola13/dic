pilgrimaged:/ˈpɪlɡrɪmɪdʒd/ | v. |朝圣；长途跋涉 | To make a pilgrimage; to travel to a sacred place. |例句：They pilgrimaged to Mecca. 他们前往麦加朝圣。|例句：We pilgrimaged across the country to see our family. 我们长途跋涉穿越全国去看望家人。 |近义词：journeyed, traveled, trekked |反义词：stayed, remained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pilgrimage to the shrine every year. = 他们每年都去神社朝圣。
He pilgrimages to different temples to seek enlightenment. = 他去不同的寺庙朝圣以寻求启蒙。

一般过去时:
We pilgrimaged to Santiago de Compostela last summer. = 我们去年夏天去圣地亚哥德孔波斯特拉朝圣。
She pilgrimaged to the holy mountain to pray for her family. = 她去圣山朝圣为家人祈祷。

现在完成时:
They have pilgrimaged to Lourdes several times. = 他们已经去卢尔德朝圣好几次了。
She has never pilgrimaged to any sacred sites before. = 她以前从未去过任何圣地朝圣。

过去完成时:
They had pilgrimaged to Varanasi before visiting the Taj Mahal. = 他们在参观泰姬陵之前已经去过瓦拉纳西朝圣了。
She had already pilgrimaged to Jerusalem when she decided to visit Bethlehem. = 她决定去伯利恒之前就已经去耶路撒冷朝圣了。

将来时:
We will pilgrimage to the Himalayas next year. = 我们明年将去喜马拉雅山朝圣。
He will pilgrimage to Rome to see the Pope. = 他将去罗马朝圣，去见教皇。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would pilgrimage to the ancient ruins. = 如果我有时间，我会去古代遗址朝圣。
I wish I could pilgrimage to Mecca someday. = 我希望有一天能去麦加朝圣。

被动语态:
The ancient temple was pilgrimaged to by thousands of people annually. = 这座古老的寺庙每年都被成千上万的人朝圣。
The holy city is being pilgrimaged to by people from all over the world. = 来自世界各地的人们正在朝圣这座圣城。

疑问句:
Have you ever pilgrimaged to any sacred sites? = 你曾经去过任何圣地朝圣吗？
Where will they pilgrimage next year? = 他们明年要去哪里朝圣？

否定句:
They did not pilgrimage to the temple this year. = 他们今年没有去寺庙朝圣。
She has never pilgrimaged to that mountain before. = 她以前从未去过那座山朝圣。
