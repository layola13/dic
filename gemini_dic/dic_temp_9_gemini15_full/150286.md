paneless:/ˈpænəlləs/| adj.|无板的，无镶板的 |Without panels; lacking panels.  A paneless window is one without panes of glass.  The term often describes a simple, often rustic or minimalist design.|例句：The paneless door was made of solid wood. 这扇无镶板的门是用实木做的。

The old barn had paneless windows, letting in the wind and rain.  旧谷仓的窗户没有玻璃窗格，风和雨都灌了进来。|近义词：frameless, unpaneled |反义词：paneled
