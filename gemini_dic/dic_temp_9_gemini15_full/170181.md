protoplastic: /ˌproʊtəˈplæstɪk/ | adj. | 原生质的；细胞质的 | Relating to or characteristic of protoplasm.  The term often refers to the living substance of a cell, excluding the cell wall. | The protoplastic membrane controls the movement of substances into and out of the cell. 原生质膜控制物质进出细胞。The protoplastic streaming in the cell was clearly visible under the microscope. 在显微镜下，细胞内的原生质流动清晰可见。|近义词：cytoplasmic, cellular | 反义词：non-living
