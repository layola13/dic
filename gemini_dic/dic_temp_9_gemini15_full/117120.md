meander:/miˈændər/| v. n. |蜿蜒；漫步；闲逛 |to follow a winding course |The river meanders through the valley. 这条河蜿蜒流经山谷。|近义词：wander, ramble, wind |反义词：straighten, direct


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river meanders through the valley. = 这条河蜿蜒流经山谷。
The hikers meander along the trail. = 徒步旅行者沿着小路漫步。

一般过去时:
The path meandered through the woods. = 小路蜿蜒穿过树林。
We meandered through the streets of the old town. = 我们漫步在老城区的街道上。

现在完成时:
The river has meandered for miles. = 这条河已经蜿蜒了几英里。
We have meandered through many different towns. = 我们已经漫游过许多不同的城镇。

过去完成时:
The river had meandered through the mountains before reaching the plain. = 这条河在到达平原之前已经蜿蜒穿过群山。
They had meandered for hours before finding their way. = 他们已经漫步了几个小时才找到路。

将来时:
The hikers will meander along the coast tomorrow. = 徒步旅行者明天将在沿海漫步。
The river will meander further into the desert. = 这条河将进一步蜿蜒进入沙漠。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would meander through the art gallery. = 如果我有更多时间，我会在美术馆闲逛。
I wish I could meander through the countryside. = 我希望我能漫游乡村。

被动语态:
The path is not easily meandered. = 这条路不容易蜿蜒而行。 (This is less common in passive voice)
The river could be meandered by boat. = 这条河可以乘船蜿蜒而下。(This is less common in passive voice)


疑问句:
Does the river meander to the sea? = 这条河蜿蜒流向大海吗？
Will you meander through the park after dinner? = 晚饭后你会在公园里闲逛吗？

否定句:
The road doesn't meander; it's straight. = 这条路不蜿蜒；它是笔直的。
We didn't meander; we walked directly to our destination. = 我们没有闲逛；我们直接走到目的地。


PHRASE->
meander along = 沿着...蜿蜒
meander through = 穿过...蜿蜒
meander down = 沿...向下蜿蜒
meandering stream = 蜿蜒的小溪
meandering path = 蜿蜒的小路
