dog-leg:/ˈdɒɡˌlɛɡ/ | n. |狗腿；（道路、河流等的）急转弯 | A sharp bend, especially in a road, river, or pipeline, resembling a dog's leg.  | Example: The road took a sharp dog-leg to the left.  这条路向左急转弯。  The river made a dog-leg around the hill. 河水绕山丘急转弯。 |近义词：bend, curve, turn |反义词：straight
