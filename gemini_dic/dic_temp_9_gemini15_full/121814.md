misregistered:/ˌmɪsˈrɛdʒɪstərd/| adj.|登记错误的，注册错误的|Incorrectly or wrongly registered.  This term implies an error in the official record of registration. |例句：The voter was misregistered in the wrong precinct. 选民登记在了错误的选区。

例句：His name was misregistered on the guest list, causing confusion at the event. 他的名字在宾客名单上登记错误，导致活动现场一片混乱。


近义词：incorrectly registered, wrongly enrolled, improperly recorded


反义词：correctly registered, properly enrolled, accurately recorded


词性：形容词
