custody:/ˈkʌstədi/ | n. | 监护权；拘留；保管 | The legal right to care for a child.  The state of being kept in prison or in official care. The protection or care of something. | 例句：The court awarded custody of the child to the mother. 法院判决孩子的监护权归母亲。 The police took the suspect into custody. 警方将嫌疑人拘留。 He was given custody of the valuable painting. 他被委托保管这幅珍贵的画作。 | 近义词：care, guardianship, keeping, confinement, detention | 反义词：freedom, release, abandonment


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The museum has custody of many priceless artifacts. = 博物馆保管着许多无价的文物。
She holds custody of her grandchildren. = 她拥有她孙辈的监护权。

一般过去时:
Police took him into custody after the robbery. = 抢劫案发生后，警方将他拘留。
The parents lost custody of their child due to neglect. = 由于疏忽，父母失去了孩子的监护权。

现在完成时:
The family has had custody of the antique clock for generations. = 这个家族世世代代保管着这台古董钟。
She has been in police custody for three days. = 她已经被警方拘留三天了。

过去完成时:
He had been in custody for a year before his trial. = 在审判之前，他已经被拘留了一年。
They had lost custody of their children before the court hearing. = 在法庭听证会之前，他们已经失去了孩子的监护权。

将来时:
The court will decide who will have custody of the twins. = 法院将决定谁将拥有双胞胎的监护权。
He will remain in custody until his bail hearing. = 他将在保释听证会之前继续被拘留。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been in custody, he would have attended the wedding. = 如果他没有被拘留，他就会参加婚礼。
I wish I had custody of my dog again. = 我希望我能再次拥有我的狗的监护权。

被动语态:
The suspect was taken into custody without incident. = 嫌疑人被平静地拘留。
The documents were kept in safe custody. = 文件被安全保管。

疑问句:
Who has custody of the children? = 谁拥有孩子的监护权？
Is the prisoner still in custody? = 囚犯还在拘留中吗？

否定句:
The police did not take him into custody. = 警方没有将他拘留。
She does not have custody of her son. = 她没有儿子的监护权。
