festers:/ˈfɛstərz/| v.|腐烂；恶化；溃烂 |To decay and generate pus; to become worse over time.  |例句：The wound festered and became infected.  伤口溃烂并感染了。

The anger festered within him for years.  多年的怒火在他心中积聚。|近义词：rot, decay, worsen, aggravate |反义词：heal, improve, mend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wound festers. = 伤口溃烂。
The problem festers. = 问题恶化。

一般过去时:
The wound festered. = 伤口溃烂了。
The resentment festered for years. = 怨恨持续多年。

现在完成时:
The wound has festered for days. = 伤口溃烂好几天了。
The situation has festered since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，情况一直在恶化。

过去完成时:
The wound had festered before he sought medical attention. = 他就医前伤口就已经溃烂了。
The conflict had festered for decades before it erupted into war. = 这场冲突在爆发战争之前已经持续了几十年。

将来时:
The wound will fester if left untreated. = 如果不治疗，伤口将会溃烂。
The issue will fester unless addressed promptly. = 除非立即解决，否则这个问题将会恶化。

虚拟语气:
If the wound were left untreated, it would fester. = 如果伤口不治疗，它就会溃烂。
If he had addressed the issue earlier, it wouldn't have festered. = 如果他早点处理这个问题，就不会恶化了。

被动语态:
The wound was allowed to fester. = 伤口被任其溃烂。
The problem is being allowed to fester. = 这个问题被允许恶化。

疑问句:
Is the wound festering? = 伤口溃烂了吗？
Is the situation festering? = 情况在恶化吗？

否定句:
The wound isn't festering. = 伤口没有溃烂。
The resentment didn't fester. = 怨恨没有恶化。
