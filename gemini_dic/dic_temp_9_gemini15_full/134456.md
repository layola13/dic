nonjoinder:/nɒnˈdʒɔɪndə(r)/| n. |未参加；未加入；未列名|The failure to include all necessary parties in a lawsuit.  This can lead to dismissal or other legal complications.  It refers to the omission of a necessary party who should have been included in the legal proceedings.|Example: The court dismissed the case due to nonjoinder of indispensable parties. 法院因未加入不可或缺的当事人而驳回了此案。|近义词：omission, exclusion |反义词：joinder, inclusion
