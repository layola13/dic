feeler:/ˈfiːlər/| n. |触角；试探者；试探；感觉器|A feeler is a slender, mobile appendage on the head of an invertebrate, especially an insect or crustacean, used as a sensory organ.  It can also refer to a person who tries to find out information secretly or a tentative suggestion or inquiry. |例句：The insect used its feelers to explore its surroundings.  这只昆虫用触角探测周围环境。

例句：He sent out a feeler to gauge the public's reaction to his proposal. 他试探性地探测公众对他提议的反应。
