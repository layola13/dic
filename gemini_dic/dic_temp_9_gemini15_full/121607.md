misorganizations:/ˌmɪsˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 组织混乱；管理不善；不良组织 |  A state of disorganization or poor management, often leading to inefficiency or chaos.  It can also refer to organizations that are poorly structured or have unethical practices.  | Example: The company's misorganizations led to significant financial losses. 公司的管理混乱导致了严重的经济损失。  |近义词：disorganization, mismanagement, chaos, dysfunction |反义词：organization, efficiency, order
