recolonizes:/ˌriːˈkɑːlənaɪzɪz/| v.|重新殖民；再次殖民|To establish control over (a territory or people) again after a period of not being in control.|The empire attempted to recolonize the lost territories.  帝国试图重新控制失去的领土。

The government plans to recolonize the abandoned mining town. 政府计划重新开发被废弃的矿业小镇。|近义词：reoccupy, resettle|反义词：decolonize, liberate
