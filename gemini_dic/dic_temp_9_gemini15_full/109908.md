lib:/lɪb/| n.|图书馆；文库；程序库|A library is a place where books and other materials are available for people to read or borrow.  A library can also refer to a collection of files or programs used by a computer. |例句：The university library has a vast collection of books. 大学图书馆藏书丰富。

例句：The software uses a dynamic link library to handle graphics. 该软件使用动态链接库来处理图形。|近义词：archive, collection |反义词:None
