recoating:/ˌriːˈkoʊtɪŋ/| n.|再涂层；重新涂覆|The act of applying a new coat of paint, sealant, or other material to a surface that has already been coated.  It aims to restore, protect, or enhance the surface. |例句：The recoating of the bridge will require a significant road closure.  桥梁的重新涂层将需要大规模的道路封闭。

例句：The recoating of the car's paint job made it look brand new. 汽车油漆的重新涂层使它看起来焕然一新。


近义词：repainting, refinishing, resurfacing|反义词：stripping (removing a coating)|词性:名词
