hunkers:/ˈhʌŋkərz/| v.|蹲下；蜷缩；|to crouch or squat down, often for warmth or protection.|例句：The hikers hunkered down beside the fire to escape the biting wind.  徒步旅行者们蜷缩在火堆旁躲避刺骨的寒风。|例句：She hunkers down in her favorite chair every evening to read. 她每天晚上都蜷缩在她最喜欢的椅子上看书。|近义词：crouch, squat, huddle, snuggle|反义词：stand, stretch, straighten
