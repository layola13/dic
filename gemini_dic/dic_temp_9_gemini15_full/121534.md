mismanaging:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|管理不善；处理不当|The act of managing something poorly or ineffectively, leading to negative consequences.  It implies a lack of skill, care, or attention in handling responsibilities.|例句：The company's mismanaging of funds led to its bankruptcy.  公司的资金管理不善导致其破产。

例句：His mismanaging of the project resulted in delays and cost overruns. 他对项目的管理不善导致延误和成本超支。


近义词：misadministering, mishandling, botching, bungling|反义词：managing well, administering effectively


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are mismanaging the resources. = 他们正在滥用资源。
The government is mismanaging the economy. = 政府正在搞砸经济。


一般过去时:
He mismanaged his finances last year. = 他去年理财不当。
The company mismanaged the crisis. = 公司处理危机不当。


现在完成时:
They have mismanaged the project from the start. = 他们从一开始就管理不当这个项目。
The team has mismanaged its time effectively. = 团队有效地浪费了时间。


过去完成时:
They had mismanaged the funds before the audit. = 审计之前，他们就已经滥用资金了。
She had mismanaged her career for years. = 她多年来一直对自己的职业生涯处理不当。


将来时:
They will mismanage the situation if they don't change their approach. = 如果他们不改变方法，他们会把情况搞砸。
The company will mismanage the launch if they don't test the product thoroughly. = 如果他们不彻底测试产品，公司将会搞砸发布。


虚拟语气:
If they had managed the project properly, they wouldn't have failed. = 如果他们当时妥善管理项目，就不会失败了。
If she hadn't mismanaged her health, she would be feeling better now. = 如果她没有不注意自己的健康，现在会感觉好得多。


被动语态:
The resources were mismanaged by the team. = 资源被团队管理不善。
The crisis was mismanaged by the authorities. = 危机被当局处理不当。


疑问句:
Are they mismanaging the company's finances? = 他们是否在滥用公司的资金？
Was the project mismanaged by the inexperienced team? = 这个项目是不是由缺乏经验的团队管理不善的？


否定句:
They did not mismanage the situation. = 他们没有处理不当这种情况。
The company didn't mismanage the funds.  = 公司没有挪用资金。
