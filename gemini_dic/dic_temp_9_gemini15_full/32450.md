chimney-sweep:/ˈtʃɪmni swiːp/| n. |扫烟囱的人，清扫烟囱工|A chimney sweep is a person whose job is to clean chimneys.  Chimney sweeps remove soot and creosote buildup from chimneys to prevent chimney fires and ensure efficient airflow.|The chimney sweep came to clean our chimney every year. 扫烟囱的人每年都来清理我们的烟囱。|   The chimney sweep arrived promptly at 9 a.m. 扫烟囱的人准时在上午9点到达。 |  |  |n.
