progesterones: /proˈdʒɛstəroʊnz/| n. |孕酮（类激素）| A group of steroid hormones produced primarily in the ovaries, placenta, and adrenal glands.  Progesterones are involved in the menstrual cycle, pregnancy, and other reproductive processes. They prepare the uterus for pregnancy and help maintain it.  Different types of progesterones exist, with varying effects and applications.| Example:  High levels of progesterones are associated with pregnancy.|近义词: progestogens |反义词:  (no direct antonym, but effects can be contrasted with those of estrogens)|名词
