reincurring:/ˌriːɪnˈkɜːrɪŋ/| adj.| 再度发生的；反复发生的 | Recurring again; happening repeatedly. | The reincurring costs of maintaining the old equipment were substantial.  维持旧设备的再次发生的成本相当可观。|近义词：recurring, repeated, recurrent|反义词：one-time, single, infrequent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem is reincurring frequently. = 这个问题频繁地再次发生。
Reincurring costs are a major concern for the project. = 再度发生的成本是该项目的主要担忧。


一般过去时:
The company reincurred the same mistake last year. = 公司去年再次犯了同样的错误。
We reincurred significant debt after the unexpected expenses. =  在意外支出之后，我们再次背负了巨额债务。


现在完成时:
They have reincurred losses three times this year. = 他们今年已经三次再次遭受损失。

过去完成时:

将来时:

They will likely reincur the same problems unless they change their approach. = 除非他们改变方法，否则他们很可能会再次遇到同样的问题。


虚拟语气:

否定句:
