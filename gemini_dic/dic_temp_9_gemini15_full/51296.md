detaching: /dɪˈtætʃɪŋ/ | v. | 分离；拆卸；使分离；使脱离 | The act of separating something from something else; removing something from its attachment.  |例句：The surgeon carefully detached the tumor from the surrounding tissue. 外科医生小心地将肿瘤与周围组织分离。

例句：She detached herself from the group and walked away. 她与人群分开，独自走开了。


近义词：separating, disconnecting, disengaging, unfastening | 反义词：attaching, connecting, joining, uniting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is detaching the trailer from the car. = 他正在把拖车从车上卸下来。
She detaches herself from emotional situations. = 她让自己远离情绪化的境况。


一般过去时:
The mechanic detached the wheel from the car. = 机械师把车轮从车上卸了下来。
I detached the coupon from the magazine. = 我把优惠券从杂志上撕了下来。


现在完成时:
They have detached the old pipes. = 他们已经拆除了旧管道。
She has detached herself from the drama. = 她已经让自己远离了这场闹剧。


过去完成时:
He had detached the wires before starting the repair. = 他在开始修理之前已经拆除了电线。
We had detached ourselves from the negativity. = 我们已经让自己摆脱了消极情绪。


将来时:
They will detach the satellite from the rocket. = 他们将把卫星从火箭上分离。
She will detach herself from the company. = 她将离开这家公司。


虚拟语气:
If I detached the battery, the device would shut down. = 如果我把电池拆下来，设备就会关机。
I wish I had detached myself from that project earlier. = 我真希望我早点退出那个项目。


被动语态:
The wheels were detached from the train. = 车轮被从火车上拆卸下来了。
The report was detached from the email. = 报告已从电子邮件中分离出来。


疑问句:
Are you detaching the engine? = 你正在拆卸发动机吗？
Has she detached herself from the situation? = 她已经让自己脱离困境了吗？


否定句:
He didn't detach the hose. = 他没有拆下软管。
She hasn't detached herself from the problem yet. = 她还没有让自己摆脱这个问题。


PHRASE->
detach from: 脱离；与……分开
detach oneself from: 使自己与……分开
detach a file: 分离文件
detach retina: 视网膜脱离
detach troops: 分遣部队

词源:
From Old French *destacher, from des- (from Latin dis-, "apart") + atacher ("to attach").  Ultimately derived from Latin *ad-tangere ("to touch").
