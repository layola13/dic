outsatisfies:/ˌaʊtˈsætɪsfaɪz/| v.|胜过，超过（在满足程度上）|To surpass in satisfaction or fulfillment.  This is not a standard dictionary word; it's a newly coined word combining "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent") and "satisfies." It implies exceeding expectations or providing a greater level of satisfaction than something else.|例句：The new design outsatisfies the old one in terms of user experience. 新设计在用户体验方面胜过旧设计。


The new product completely outsatisfies the customer's expectations. 新产品完全超出了客户的预期。|近义词：exceeds, surpasses, outperforms, betters |反义词：disappoints, underwhelms
