milkweed:/ˈmɪlkwid/| n.|乳草, 萝藦|A flowering plant with milky sap, many species of which are food sources for monarch butterfly larvae.  Many species are poisonous to humans and animals.|The monarch butterflies laid their eggs on the milkweed plants.|近义词：asclepias|反义词：无

词源:
The name "milkweed" comes from the milky, latex-like sap that exudes from the plant when it's broken or cut.  The scientific genus name, *Asclepias*, is derived from Asclepius, the Greek god of healing, reflecting the plant's historical use in medicine.
