lorcha:/ˈlɔːrtʃə/| n. |中式帆船；鸦片战争时期中国的一种商船|A lorcha was a type of Chinese trading vessel, typically a hybrid design combining Western and Chinese shipbuilding techniques.  They were common in the 19th century, particularly during the Opium Wars.  |Example: The British navy encountered numerous lorchas during their operations in the South China Sea.  英国海军在南海行动中遭遇了许多中式帆船。|近义词：junk (a more general term for a Chinese sailing vessel)|反义词：N/A
