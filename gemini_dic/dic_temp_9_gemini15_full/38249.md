computing:/kəmˈpjuːtɪŋ/ | n. | 计算；计算机；计算技术 | The act of calculating or computing; the science or art of computation; the use of computers.  It encompasses the design, development, and application of computer systems and software. | Example:  Modern computing relies heavily on integrated circuits.  现代计算技术很大程度上依赖于集成电路。


Example: Cloud computing is transforming how businesses operate. 云计算正在改变企业的运作方式。


近义词：calculation, computation, data processing | 反义词：None (antonyms are context-dependent)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Computing is essential for modern life. = 计算对于现代生活至关重要。
He is computing the results of the experiment. = 他正在计算实验结果。


一般过去时:
They computed the trajectory of the rocket. = 他们计算了火箭的轨迹。
We computed the area of the triangle. = 我们计算了三角形的面积。


现在完成时:
Scientists have computed the distance to the star. = 科学家们已经计算出了到那颗恒星的距离。
She has been computing data all day. = 她一整天都在计算数据。


过去完成时:
They had computed the figures before the meeting. = 他们会议前就已计算出这些数据。
The engineers had computed the stress on the bridge. = 工程师们已经计算出了桥梁的压力。


将来时:
We will compute the total cost later. = 我们稍后会计算总成本。
They will be computing the average score tomorrow. = 他们明天将计算平均分。


虚拟语气:
If I had a more powerful computer, I would compute the model faster. = 如果我有一台更强大的电脑，我会更快地计算模型。
It would be helpful if we could compute the risks more accurately. = 如果我们能更准确地计算风险，那就有帮助了。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
