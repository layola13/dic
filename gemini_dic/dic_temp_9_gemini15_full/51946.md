diabolify:/ˈdaɪæbəlaɪfɪ/| vt.|妖魔化；使魔鬼化|To represent or portray someone or something as evil or demonic.|例句：The media diabolified the politician, portraying him as a ruthless tyrant. 媒体妖魔化了这位政治家，把他描绘成一个冷酷的暴君。例句：The novel diabolified the villain, making him a truly terrifying figure. 这部小说妖魔化了反派，使他成为一个真正可怕的人物。|近义词：demonize, vilify, malign|反义词：deify, sanctify, glorify|
