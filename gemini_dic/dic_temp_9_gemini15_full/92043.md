hyperphalangy:/ˌhaɪpərˈfælən(d)ʒi/| n. |多指（畸形）|The presence of more than the normal number of phalanges (bones of the fingers or toes).  This condition can affect one or more digits and can be inherited or caused by a developmental abnormality.|例句：The radiograph revealed hyperphalangy in the patient's left thumb.  X光片显示患者左拇指有多指畸形。|The patient presented with polydactyly, a form of hyperphalangy.|该患者表现为多指畸形，一种多指畸形。|近义词：polydactyly (if involving extra digits),  extra digit|反义词：oligodactyly ( fewer than the normal number of digits)
