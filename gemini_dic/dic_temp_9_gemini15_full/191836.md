sclerite:/ˈsklɪraɪt/| n. |几丁质硬片；骨片；硬化板 |A sclerite is a hardened plate forming part of the exoskeleton of an invertebrate animal, such as an arthropod.  These plates are often interconnected and provide protection and support. |The beetle's exoskeleton is composed of numerous sclerites. 这只甲虫的外骨骼由许多几丁质硬片组成。|plate, segment|soft tissue|名词
