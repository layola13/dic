hackles:/ˈhæklz/| n. |颈背竖起的毛；怒气；激动|The raised hairs on the back of the neck of an animal, especially a dog, typically as a sign of anger or fear.  Also refers to a feeling of anger or irritation.|例句：The dog's hackles rose when it saw the stranger.  这只狗看到陌生人时，毛都竖起来了。

例句：His hackles were raised by the unfair accusation. 不公平的指控激怒了他。


近义词：anger, irritation, fury, indignation|反义词：calmness, tranquility, placidity|名词
