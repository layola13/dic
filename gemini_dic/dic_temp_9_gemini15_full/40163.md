contendingly:/kənˈtɛndɪŋli/ | adv. | 争论地；竞争性地；坚决地 | In a manner that shows contention or argument; in a competitive manner; resolutely.  |例句：He spoke contendingly about the unfairness of the situation. 他争论着这种处境的不公平。

例句：They contendedly fought for the top spot in the race. 他们为了比赛中的第一名而激烈竞争。


近义词：argumentatively, competitively, resolutely, stubbornly

反义词：submissively, peacefully, cooperatively
