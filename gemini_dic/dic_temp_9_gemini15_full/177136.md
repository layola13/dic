reapproval:/ˌriːəˈpruːvəl/ | n. | 再批准，重新批准 | The act of approving something again; a second approval.  It often implies a previous approval that was perhaps revoked or requires reaffirmation. | Example: The project received reapproval after a thorough review of the revised plans.  重新审查修改后的计划后，该项目获得了重新批准。 The reapproval of the building permit was delayed due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，建筑许可证的重新批准被推迟了。 | 近义词：reauthorization, reaffirmation, confirmation | 反义词：rejection, disapproval
