responsal:/rɪˈspɒnsəl/| n.|回应；应答；答复|A responsal is a short response, typically in a religious service.  It often involves a chanted or sung response to a reading or prayer.|例句：The choir sang a responsal after the priest's reading.  唱诗班在牧师朗读后唱了一段回应歌。|近义词：response, reply, answer|反义词：question, query
