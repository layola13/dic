self-taught:/ˌselfˈtɔːt/ | adj. | 自学的；自修的 |  Someone who is self-taught has learned a skill or subject by themselves, without the help of a teacher.  | He's a self-taught musician. 他是一位自学成才的音乐家。 |  self-educated, autodidactic |  professionally trained  


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is self-taught in programming. = 他是自学编程的。
She is self-taught in painting. = 她是自学绘画的。

一般过去时:
He was self-taught in carpentry. = 他以前是自学木工的。
She was self-taught in playing the guitar. = 她以前是自学吉他的。

现在完成时:
He has been self-taught in several languages. = 他自学了几门外语。
She has been self-taught in photography for years. = 她自学摄影多年了。

过去完成时:
He had been self-taught in mathematics before entering university. = 他在大学之前就自学了数学。
She had been self-taught in cooking before she started her culinary career. =  在她开始烹饪生涯之前，她就自学了烹饪。


将来时:
He will be self-taught in astrophysics by next year. = 到明年他将自学天体物理学。
She will be self-taught in graphic design by the end of the course. = 到课程结束时，她将自学平面设计。


虚拟语气:
If I were self-taught in coding, I would create my own app. = 如果我是自学编程的，我就会创建自己的应用程序。
I wish I were self-taught in playing the piano. = 我希望我是自学钢琴的。


被动语态:
The skills were self-taught. = 这些技能是自学的。
The knowledge was self-taught over many years. = 这些知识是多年自学的。


疑问句:
Is he self-taught in web development? = 他是自学网页开发的吗？
Are they self-taught in music composition? = 他们自学作曲的吗？


否定句:
He is not self-taught in medicine. = 他不是自学医术的。
She is not self-taught in law. = 她不是自学法律的。



PHRASE->
self-taught programmer = 自学成才的程序员
self-taught artist = 自学成才的艺术家
