lowliest:/ˈloʊliəst/| adj.| 最卑微的，最低下的 | The adjective "lowliest" means occupying the lowest position or rank; humblest; most insignificant.  It is the superlative form of "lowly."  It often implies a sense of humility or degradation. | Example:  He was the lowliest servant in the king's court. 他是国王宫廷里地位最低下的仆人。  She lived in the lowliest quarters of the city. 她住在城市里最贫民窟的地方。|近义词： humblest, meanest, most humble,  most insignificant | 反义词： highest, noblest, greatest
