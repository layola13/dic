multiform:/ˈmʌltɪfɔːrm/| adj.|多样的；多种形式的；多形的|Having or assuming many forms; of many forms.  The word describes something that exists in, or can be expressed in, many different shapes, types, or ways.|例句：The artist's work is multiform, ranging from sculpture to painting to performance art.  这位艺术家的作品多种多样，从雕塑到绘画再到行为艺术。|例句：The virus has a multiform presentation, making diagnosis difficult.  这种病毒有多种表现形式，这使得诊断变得困难。|近义词：varied, diverse, manifold, multifaceted|反义词：uniform, singular, monolithic
