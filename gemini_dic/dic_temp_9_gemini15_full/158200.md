platt:/plæt/| n.|平台；高地；平地|A relatively level expanse of land, often raised above surrounding areas.  It can refer to a raised structure or surface.|例句：The farmer built a platt for his crops. 农民为他的庄稼建了一个平台。|例句：We enjoyed the view from the platt overlooking the valley. 我们从俯瞰山谷的高地上欣赏风景。|近义词：platform, terrace, level ground|反义词：pit, ravine, precipice|名词
