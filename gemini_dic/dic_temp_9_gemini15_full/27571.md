canalizes:/ˈkænəlaɪz/| vt.|引导，规范，使…定向 |To direct or restrict the flow of something, especially ideas or feelings, into a particular channel or course.|The government canalized its efforts to improve the infrastructure. 政府将其努力集中于改善基础设施。|近义词：channel, direct, guide, constrain|反义词：diversify, disperse
