overmortgaged:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrɡɪdʒd/| adj. | 抵押过度；负债累累的 | Excessively mortgaged; burdened with excessive debt secured by mortgages.  This term describes a situation where someone has borrowed significantly more money through mortgages than the value of their assets, leaving them in a precarious financial position. | The family was overmortgaged and faced foreclosure.  这家负债累累，面临着被取消抵押品赎回权的风险。| heavily indebted, deeply in debt | solvent, debt-free
