double-parking:/ˈdʌbəl ˈpɑːrkɪŋ/ | n. |双排停车，违章停车 |The act of parking a vehicle in a way that blocks another vehicle from leaving its parking space.  This is generally illegal and considered inconsiderate. |例句：Double-parking is illegal and can result in a ticket. 双排停车是违法的，可能会导致罚单。

例句：He received a hefty fine for double-parking in front of the hospital. 他因为在医院前面双排停车而收到了一张巨额罚单。|近义词：illegal parking, obstructing traffic |反义词：proper parking, legal parking
