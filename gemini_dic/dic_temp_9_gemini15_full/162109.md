pre-cuts:/ˌpriːˈkʌts/| n.|预切好的(材料)|Pre-cuts are pieces of material that have already been cut to a specific size or shape, often for convenience or to save time.  They are commonly used in crafting, sewing, and cooking.|例句：The craft store sells pre-cuts of fabric in various colors and patterns.  这家工艺品店出售各种颜色和图案的预切布料。

The bakery offers pre-cuts of cookies for easy snacking. 这家面包店提供预切饼干，方便零食食用。|近义词：pre-cut pieces, ready-cut, cut-to-size|反义词：whole, uncut
