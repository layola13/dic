flirtatiousness:/ˌflɜːrteɪʃəsnəs/| n. |轻浮；调情；卖弄风情|The quality or state of being flirtatious;  behavior intended to attract romantic attention, often in a playful or suggestive way.  It can be charming or annoying depending on the context and the individuals involved.|例句：Her flirtatiousness was evident in her playful glances and suggestive remarks.  她的轻浮从她嬉戏的目光和暗示性的话语中显而易见。

例句：His blatant flirtatiousness made several women uncomfortable. 他公然的调情让几个女人感到不舒服。


近义词：coquetry,  amorousness,  suggestiveness,  playfulness


反义词：seriousness,  modesty,  reserve,  unapproachability


词源：From "flirt" + "-atious" (forming adjectives) + "-ness" (forming nouns).  The word "flirt" itself has an uncertain etymology, possibly related to a Dutch word meaning "to snap" or "flick."
