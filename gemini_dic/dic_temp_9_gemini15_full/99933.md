internist:/ɪnˈtɜːrnɪst/| n. |内科医生|A physician specializing in the diagnosis and nonsurgical treatment of diseases and disorders of the internal organs.|The internist diagnosed the patient's condition as pneumonia.|近义词：physician, doctor|反义词：surgeon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The internist examines patients daily. = 内科医生每天检查病人。
She treats a wide range of internal diseases. = 她治疗各种各样的内科疾病。

一般过去时:
The internist diagnosed the patient's illness. = 内科医生诊断了病人的疾病。
He prescribed medication for the patient's symptoms. = 他为病人的症状开了药。

现在完成时:
The internist has treated many patients with similar symptoms. =这位内科医生已经治疗过许多有类似症状的病人。
She has successfully managed numerous complex cases. = 她成功地处理了许多复杂的病例。

过去完成时:

Before the consultation, he had reviewed the patient's medical history. = 会诊前，他已复习了病人的病史。
