handsets:/ˈhændˌsɛts/| n. |手持电话，手机|A handset is a portable telephone that can be held in the hand, typically connected to a network via a base station.  Handsets are now largely synonymous with mobile phones.|Example:  Modern handsets offer a wide range of features beyond just making calls. 现代手机除了打电话之外，还提供各种各样的功能。|The mobile phone, cell phone, mobile| landline phone.
