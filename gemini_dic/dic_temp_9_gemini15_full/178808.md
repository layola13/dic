recursant:/rɪˈkɜːrsənt/| adj.|反复出现的；再发的；循环的 |Recurring or repeated; happening again and again.  The recursant theme of betrayal runs throughout the novel.|近义词：recurring, repeated, recurrent, cyclical |反义词：unique, single, infrequent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem recurs constantly. = 这个问题不断重复出现。
The theme recurs throughout the book. = 这个主题贯穿全书。


一般过去时:
The symptoms recurred after a few days. = 几天后症状又复发了。
The error recurred in the next calculation. = 下一次计算中又出现了错误。


现在完成时:
The problem has recurred several times. = 这个问题已经反复出现好几次了。
The pain has recurred since the operation. = 手术后疼痛一直反复出现。


过去完成时:
The headache had recurred before he consulted the doctor. = 他去看医生之前头痛已经复发了。
The issue had recurred many times in previous meetings. = 这个问题在之前的会议上已经多次出现。


将来时:
The disease will likely recur in the future. = 这疾病很可能会复发。
I fear the problem will recur next year. = 我担心这个问题明年还会出现。


虚拟语气:
If the symptoms recurred, he would seek medical attention immediately. = 如果症状复发，他会立即就医。
If the error recurred, the program would halt. = 如果错误再次出现，程序将停止运行。


被动语态:
The issue was recurred in the discussion. = 这个问题在讨论中再次被提及。
The pattern was recurred in the design. = 该图案在设计中被重复使用。


疑问句:
Does the pain recur frequently? = 疼痛经常复发吗？
Will the problem recur? = 这个问题还会再次出现吗？


否定句:
The problem did not recur. = 问题没有再次出现。
The symptoms have not recurred. = 症状没有复发。
