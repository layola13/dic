restamps:/riːˈstæmp/| v.|重新盖印章；重新贴邮票|To stamp again; to affix a stamp again.|例句：He had to restamp the envelope because the original stamp was invalid. 他不得不重新盖印信封，因为原来的邮票无效了。例句：The company restamps all returned goods with a new date. 公司将所有退回的商品重新盖上新的日期章。|近义词：re-mark, re-label|反义词：remove stamp
