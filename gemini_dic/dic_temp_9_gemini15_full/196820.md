sexualities:/ˌsɛkʃuˈælətiz/ | n. |性取向；性行为 |The plural of sexuality, referring to the diverse range of human sexual orientations, behaviors, and expressions.  It encompasses a spectrum beyond the traditional binary of heterosexual and homosexual. |例句：Understanding different sexualities is crucial for creating an inclusive society. 理解不同的性取向对于创造一个包容的社会至关重要。

例句：The study explored the diverse sexualities represented in contemporary literature. 这项研究探讨了当代文学中展现出的多样化性取向。


近义词：sexual orientations, sexual preferences | 反义词：无


词源:  Derived from the word "sexuality," which itself originates from the Latin word "sexualis," meaning "of or pertaining to sex." The suffix "-ities" indicates a plural form, referring to multiple sexual orientations or expressions.
