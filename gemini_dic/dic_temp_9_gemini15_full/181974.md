remasticates:/ˌriːˈmæstɪkeɪts/| v.|重新咀嚼；反复咀嚼|To chew (something) again.  This word implies a more thorough or repeated chewing than simply chewing.|例句：The cow remasticates its cud. = 这头牛反刍了它的食物。

例句：He remasticated the tough piece of meat before swallowing. = 他在吞下那块硬肉之前又嚼了一遍。


近义词：rechew, chew again, masticate again

反义词：swallow, gulp
