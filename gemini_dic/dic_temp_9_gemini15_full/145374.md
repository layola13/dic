overcapable:/ˌoʊvərˈkeɪpəbəl/ | adj. |能力过剩的，能力过强的 | Excessively capable; having more ability or skill than is needed or required.  |The project was overcapable of handling the small workload. 这个项目的能力过剩，应付这个小工作绰绰有余。|近义词：highly capable, supremely skilled, exceptionally talented |反义词：incapable, unskilled, incompetent |

She is overcapable in her current position. 她在她目前的职位上能力过剩。
