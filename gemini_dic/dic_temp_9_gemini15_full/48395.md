dedesignating: /ˌdiːˈdɛzɪɡneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 取消指定；撤销指定 |  The act of removing a designation or purpose previously assigned to something. It implies a reversal of a prior decision or action.  | 例句：The city is dedesignating the parkland for commercial development. = 城市正在取消公园用地用于商业开发的指定用途。

The government is dedesignating the protected area, opening it up to logging. = 政府正在取消该保护区的指定地位，将其开放用于伐木。


近义词：revoking, rescinding, withdrawing, canceling


反义词：designating, assigning, appointing


词性：动词
