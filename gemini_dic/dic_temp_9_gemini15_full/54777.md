dispense:/dɪˈspɛns/| vt.|分配，分发，免除，调剂(药方)|to distribute or give out something; to grant an exemption from something; to prepare and give out medicine|例句：The teacher dispensed the test papers to the students. =老师把试卷分发给学生。

The pharmacist dispensed the prescription. = 药剂师配药。


近义词：distribute, allot, administer, exempt|反义词：retain, withhold, keep
