mooing:/ˈmuːɪŋ/| n. |哞哞叫声；牛叫声 |The sound a cow makes.  |例句：The mooing of the cows filled the air. 牛的哞哞叫声充满了空气。  The farmer heard the gentle mooing from his pasture. 农民听到了牧场上传来的轻柔的牛叫声。|近义词：lowing, bellow |反义词： 无 |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cow is mooing loudly. = 这头牛大声地哞哞叫。
They are mooing constantly. = 它们一直在哞哞叫。

一般过去时:
The calf mooed softly last night. = 小牛昨晚轻轻地叫了一声。
The cows were mooing all through the night. = 牛整夜都在哞哞叫。

现在完成时:
The cows have been mooing for hours. = 牛已经哞哞叫了几个小时了。
She has heard the mooing since dawn. = 她从黎明开始就听到牛叫声了。

过去完成时:
The farmer had heard the mooing before he went to bed. = 农民睡觉前就听到了牛叫声。
They had been mooing for days before the farmer noticed. = 在农民注意到之前，它们已经哞哞叫了几天了。

将来时:
The cows will be mooing again tomorrow morning. = 牛明天早上还会哞哞叫。
I think the calf will moo soon. = 我想小牛很快就会叫了。

虚拟语气:
If the cows were mooing, I would know they were hungry. = 如果牛在哞哞叫，我就知道它们饿了。
I wish the cows would stop mooing; it's disturbing my sleep. = 我希望牛能停止哞哞叫；这扰乱了我的睡眠。

被动语态:
(This is not naturally used in passive voice. The mooing itself can't be acted upon.)

疑问句:
Are the cows mooing? = 牛在哞哞叫吗？
Why is the calf mooing so much? = 为什么小牛叫得这么厉害？

否定句:
The cows aren't mooing now. = 牛现在没有哞哞叫。
I didn't hear any mooing last night. = 我昨晚没有听到任何牛叫声。
