rosmarine:/ˈrəʊzməri/ | n. |迷迭香|Rosemary is an aromatic evergreen herb with needle-like leaves and small white, pink, purple, or blue flowers. It is used in cooking and as a medicinal herb.|例句：She added a sprig of rosemary to the roast chicken. 她往烤鸡里加了一小枝迷迭香。|近义词：sage, thyme, lavender |反义词：无
