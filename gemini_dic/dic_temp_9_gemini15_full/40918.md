coolibah:/ˈkuːlɪbɑː/| n.|库利巴树（澳洲的一种桉树）|A type of eucalyptus tree native to Australia, known for its ability to survive in arid conditions.  Coolibahs are often found near water sources or in areas with relatively high water tables.|The coolibah tree provided shade for the weary travelers.|近义词：eucalyptus (general term)|反义词：无
