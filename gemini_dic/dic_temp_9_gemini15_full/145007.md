overadjustment:/ˌoʊvərəˈdʒʌstmənt/| n. |过度调整，矫枉过正|The act of adjusting something too much, often resulting in a worse outcome than the initial problem.  It implies an excessive or inappropriate level of correction.|例句：The overadjustment of the brakes caused the car to skid.  过度调整刹车导致汽车侧滑。

例句：His overadjustment of the camera settings ruined the photos. 他过度调整相机设置毁了照片。


近义词：overcorrection, excessive adjustment, overcompensation|反义词：underadjustment, insufficient adjustment|词性: noun
