hispanicizing: /ˌhɪspænɪˈsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使具有西班牙裔特征；使西班牙化 |  The act of making something or someone more Hispanic in character or culture.  This can involve adopting Hispanic customs, language, or values.  The term often implies a process of assimilation or adaptation to Hispanic culture, which can be voluntary or imposed. |
