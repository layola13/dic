fouling:/ˈfaʊlɪŋ/| n. v.|弄脏；犯规；污垢|The act of making something dirty or impure; an infringement of rules; a source of obstruction or impediment.|例句：The fouling of the river by industrial waste is a serious problem. 工业废物污染河流是一个严重的问题。
The player received a penalty for fouling his opponent.  球员因犯规而受到处罚。|近义词：soiling, polluting, contamination, infringement, obstruction|反义词：cleaning, purification
