rekeyboards:/ˌriːˈkiːbɔːrdz/ | n. | 重新键入；重新打字；(对键盘的)重新配置 |  The process of retyping text, often after a system failure or to correct errors, or the act of reconfiguring a keyboard's layout or settings.  It can also refer to replacing a keyboard. |例句：After the power outage, we had to rekeyboard the entire report.  停电后，我们不得不重新键入整份报告。  The IT department rekeyed the database after discovering a security breach. IT部门在发现安全漏洞后重新录入了数据库。|近义词：re-enter, retype, re-input |反义词：N/A
