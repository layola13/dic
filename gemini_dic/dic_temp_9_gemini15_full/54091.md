disembed:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɛd/ | vt. |  取出；使脱离；解放 | To remove something that has been embedded or fixed in something else.  |例句：The surgeon carefully disembedded the bullet from the patient's arm. 外科医生小心地从病人的手臂中取出子弹。
The archaeologist carefully disembedded the ancient artifact from the surrounding earth. 考古学家小心翼翼地将古代文物从周围的泥土中取出。| 近义词：extract, remove, release | 反义词：embed, implant, insert | 动词
