diaconal:/daɪˈæknəl/| adj.|执事的；副祭的；教区执事的|Relating to deacons or their office; pertaining to the diaconate.  The word often refers to the work or ministry of deacons within a church or religious organization. |例句：The diaconal ministry focuses on service and outreach to the community.  执事部致力于服务和社区外展。

例句：She received training in diaconal work before her ordination. 她在她被任命之前接受了执事工作的培训。


近义词：ministerial, pastoral (in a religious context)


反义词：hierarchical (in a contrasting organizational structure)


词性:形容词
