pensative: /ˈpɛnsətɪv/ | adj. |沉思的；忧郁的 | Thoughtful;dreamily thoughtful;meditative;musing. Characterized by or given to pensive thought.  | 例句：She sat by the window, lost in pensive thought. 她坐在窗边，陷入沉思。

例句：He wore a pensive expression on his face. 他脸上带着沉思的表情。


近义词：reflective, thoughtful, meditative, contemplative, dreamy, wistful

反义词：cheerful, joyous, carefree, lighthearted

词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pensive today. = 他今天很忧郁。
She is always pensive when she reads poetry. = 她读诗的时候总是很沉思。


一般过去时:
He was pensive after receiving the bad news. = 他收到坏消息后很忧郁。
She was pensive throughout the entire ceremony. = 她在整个仪式上都很沉思。


现在完成时:
He has been pensive since his grandfather passed away. = 自从他祖父去世后，他就一直很忧郁。
She has been pensive about her future career path. = 她一直在思考自己未来的职业道路。


过去完成时:
He had been pensive for weeks before making the decision. = 他在做决定之前已经忧郁了好几个星期。
She had been pensive all day, lost in her thoughts. = 她整天都忧郁地沉浸在自己的思绪中。


将来时:
He will be pensive if he doesn't get the job. = 如果他得不到这份工作，他会很忧郁。
She will likely be pensive during the long journey. = 在漫长的旅途中，她可能会很沉思。


虚拟语气:
If I were less pensive, I would enjoy the party more. = 如果我不那么忧郁，我会更享受派对。
I wish I weren't so pensive all the time. = 我希望我不总是那么忧郁。


被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice.)
His pensive nature was evident. = 他沉思的本性是显而易见的。
His face was pensive. = 他脸上露出了沉思的表情。(This is a more acceptable passive use.)


疑问句:
Is he pensive about the upcoming exam? = 他在为即将到来的考试而忧虑吗？
Does she often appear pensive? = 她经常看起来很忧郁吗？


否定句:
He is not pensive today; he seems quite happy. = 他今天不忧郁；他看起来很高兴。
She was not pensive at all during the meeting. = 她在会议期间一点也不忧郁。



PHRASE->
a pensive mood = 沉思的心情
a pensive gaze = 沉思的目光
a pensive silence = 沉思的沉默
a pensive expression = 沉思的表情
a pensive walk = 沉思的散步
