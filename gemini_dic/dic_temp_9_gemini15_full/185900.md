revolutionised: /ˌrevəˈluːʃənaɪzd/ | vt. |彻底改变；革新 | To change something completely, often in a way that is sudden and dramatic.  | 例句：The internet revolutionised communication.  互联网彻底改变了通讯方式。 | 例句：The invention of the printing press revolutionised the spread of information. 印刷术的发明彻底改变了信息的传播方式。| 近义词：transformed, changed drastically, modernized, reformed | 反义词：maintained, preserved, left unchanged | 动词

一般过去时:
