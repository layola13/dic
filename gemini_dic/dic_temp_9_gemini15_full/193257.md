seedless:/ˈsiːdləs/| adj.|无籽的，不结果实的|Lacking seeds; without seeds.|例句：Seedless grapes are convenient to eat. 无籽葡萄吃起来很方便。

例句：This variety of watermelon is seedless.  这种西瓜是无籽的。


近义词：seedfree, coreless (for fruits like apples)


反义词：seeded


词源:  The word "seedless" is a combination of "seed" and the suffix "-less," which indicates the absence of something. "Seed" comes from Old English *sæd, from Proto-Germanic *sæþaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *set- "to sow."  The suffix "-less" is also of Old English origin, meaning "without".
