changeover: /ˈtʃeɪndʒˌoʊvər/ | n. | 变换；转换；切换；交接 | The process of changing from one thing to another, or the period of time during which this happens.  It can refer to a shift in operation, personnel, or systems. |

例句：

The changeover to the new system was surprisingly smooth.  =  向新系统的转换出奇地顺利。

The night shift changeover happened at midnight. = 夜班交接发生在午夜。


近义词：transition, shift, switch, transfer, handover

反义词：continuation, permanence, stability


词性: 名词
