one-shots:/ˈwʌnʃɒts/ | n. | 单篇故事，一次性作品 | A one-shot is a self-contained story, often short, that doesn't belong to a larger series or continuity.  It tells a complete narrative within its own boundaries. | Example:  I wrote a one-shot for fun, exploring a different character in my established universe.  我写了一个短篇故事作为消遣，探索了我既定世界观中不同的角色。|近义词：short story, single story, standalone story |反义词：series, serial, saga
