The patient complained of scrotal pain and swelling, suggesting a possible varicocele. 病人抱怨阴囊疼痛和肿胀，这表明可能是精索静脉曲张。


近义词：varicocele (more common term) | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词


词源:  From Latin *cirso- (from Greek kirsos, “varix,” a dilated vein) + -cele (from Greek -kēlē, “swelling,” “tumor”).  The term combines the concept of a dilated vein (cirso-) with the general term for a swelling or hernia (-cele).
