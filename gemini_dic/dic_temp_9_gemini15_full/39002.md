confirming: /kənˈfɜːrmɪŋ/ | v.ing | 确认；证实；批准 | The act of verifying or establishing the truth or accuracy of something.  It implies a process of making certain.  |例句：I'm confirming my flight reservation. = 我正在确认我的航班预订。

例句：He is confirming the details of the contract. = 他正在确认合同的细节。


近义词： verifying, validating, ratifying, assuring | 反义词：denying, refuting, contradicting | 词性：动词现在分词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are confirming the order details. = 他们正在确认订单详情。
We are confirming your appointment for tomorrow. = 我们正在确认您明天的预约。


一般过去时:
The manager confirmed the meeting time. = 经理确认了会议时间。
She confirmed that she would attend the conference. = 她确认她将参加会议。


现在完成时:
They have confirmed the delivery date. = 他们已经确认了交货日期。
I have confirmed my participation in the project. = 我已经确认了我参与这个项目。


过去完成时:
He had already confirmed his travel plans before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，他已经确认了他的旅行计划。
She had confirmed the reservation a week in advance. = 她在一周前就确认了预订。


将来时:
They will confirm the results tomorrow. = 他们明天将确认结果。
We will confirm your booking once we receive payment. = 我们收到付款后将确认您的预订。


虚拟语气:
If they confirmed the deal, we would start production. = 如果他们确认了这笔交易，我们将开始生产。
I wish they had confirmed the information sooner. = 我希望他们早点确认信息。


被动语态:
The booking was confirmed yesterday. = 预订昨天已确认。
The information will be confirmed shortly. = 信息将很快得到确认。


疑问句:
Have they confirmed the shipment yet? = 他们已经确认发货了吗？
Will you confirm your attendance by Friday? = 你会在星期五之前确认你的出席吗？


否定句:
He did not confirm the rumors. = 他没有证实那些谣言。
They haven't confirmed the date of the wedding yet. = 他们还没有确认婚礼的日期。
