non-cooperation:/ˌnɒnˌkəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃən/|/ˌnɑːnˌkoʊˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən/| n. |不合作；抵制；拒不合作|The refusal to cooperate or comply with something.  It often implies a deliberate and active resistance, rather than simply a lack of cooperation.|例句：The government faced widespread non-cooperation from the public during the protests.  政府在抗议期间面临公众的普遍不合作。

例句：Their non-cooperation with the investigation hampered the police's efforts. 他们拒绝对调查进行合作妨碍了警方的努力。


近义词：resistance, defiance, obstruction, recalcitrance, insubordination|反义词：cooperation, compliance, collaboration, assistance
