lipogrammatic:/ˌlɪpəɡræˈmætɪk/| adj.|省略某字母的；避免使用某字母的|Relating to or characteristic of a lipogram, a piece of writing that omits a particular letter or letters of the alphabet.  It describes a writing style or text that deliberately avoids using a specific letter or letters.  |例句：The author's lipogrammatic novel was a challenging but fascinating read.  这位作者的省略某字母的小说读起来很有挑战性，但也引人入胜。  The lipogrammatic poem was surprisingly successful despite the self-imposed constraint. 这首省略某字母的诗歌尽管受到自我设定的限制，但出奇地成功。|近义词：letterless (in a limited sense)|反义词：complete, comprehensive
