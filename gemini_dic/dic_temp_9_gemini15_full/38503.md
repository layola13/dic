conciliar:/kənˈsɪliər/| adj.|会议的；宗教会议的；调解的| Relating to a council, especially an ecclesiastical council.  The word often implies a spirit of compromise and reconciliation among differing viewpoints.|The conciliar decisions were aimed at promoting unity within the church.  教会议决旨在促进教会内部的团结。|ecclesiastical, synodal|divisive, contentious|形容词
