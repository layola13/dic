replotted:/ˌriːˈplɑːtɪd/| v. |重新绘制；重新描绘；重新策划|To plot or plan again; to redraw a graph or chart with revised data.|例句：The engineer replotted the data after discovering an error. 工程师在发现错误后重新绘制了数据。

例句：The marketing team replotted their strategy after the initial campaign failed.  市场团队在最初的宣传活动失败后重新制定了策略。


近义词：revise, redraw, replan, restructure

反义词：None directly opposite, but could be considered the opposite of  initial plotting or planning.

词性：动词
