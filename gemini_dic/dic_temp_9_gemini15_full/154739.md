persuade:/pərˈsweɪd/| vt.|劝说；说服|To convince someone to do something or to believe something.|例句：I tried to persuade him to change his mind, but he refused. 我试图说服他改变主意，但他拒绝了。

例句：She persuaded me to go to the party even though I didn't want to. 她劝我说服我去参加派对，尽管我不想去。


近义词：convince, influence, induce, coax|反义词：dissuade

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He persuades many people to donate money every year. 他每年说服很多人捐款。
She persuades her children to eat vegetables. 她劝说她的孩子吃蔬菜。

一般过去时:
The salesman persuaded me to buy a new car. 销售员说服我买了一辆新车。
They persuaded the judge to reconsider the sentence. 他们说服法官重新考虑判决。

现在完成时:
I have persuaded him to reconsider his decision. 我已经说服他重新考虑他的决定。
She has persuaded many people to join her cause. 她已经说服很多人加入她的事业。

过去完成时:
He had persuaded her to leave before the storm hit. 他在暴风雨来临之前已经说服她离开了。
They had persuaded the committee to approve the plan. 他们已经说服委员会批准了该计划。

将来时:
I will persuade him to come to the meeting. 我将说服他来参加会议。
We will persuade them to support our project. 我们将说服他们支持我们的项目。

虚拟语气:
If I could persuade her, I would. 如果我能说服她，我会的。
I wish I could persuade him to change his mind. 我希望我能说服他改变主意。

被动语态:
He was persuaded to give up smoking. 他被说服戒烟了。
The audience was persuaded by her speech. 观众被她的演讲所打动。

疑问句:
Can you persuade him to help us? 你能说服他帮助我们吗？
Will you be able to persuade them to agree? 你能说服他们同意吗？

否定句:
I couldn't persuade her to go. 我无法说服她去。
They failed to persuade the board of directors. 他们未能说服董事会。


PHRASE->
persuade someone to do something  说服某人做某事
persuade someone of something 使某人相信某事
persuade someone into doing something 劝说某人做某事
persuade someone out of doing something 劝说某人不要做某事
be persuaded to do something 被说服做某事


词源:
From Old French *persuader, from Latin persuadēre "to persuade," from per- "thoroughly" + suādēre "to advise, urge, entreat."  The word's journey reflects its meaning: a thorough urging or advising that leads to conviction.
