misprize:/mɪsˈpraɪz/ | vt. |轻视；贬低；误解|To undervalue or scorn; to misunderstand or misjudge.  |例句：He misprized her talents, failing to recognize her potential. 他轻视了她的才能，没有认识到她的潜力。

例句：The critics misprized the novel, failing to appreciate its subtle nuances. 批评家们误解了这部小说，没有欣赏到它细微的差别。


近义词：undervalue, scorn, slight, belittle, underestimate, disparage


反义词：appreciate, value, respect, esteem, admire


词源：源于古英语 *misprisen，由 mis-（错误地）和 prisen（评价）组成。
