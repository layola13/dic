clear-outs: /ˈklɪrˌaʊts/ | n. | 清理；清除；大扫除 | The act of removing unwanted or unnecessary items from a place; a thorough cleaning or tidying.  |例句：The spring clear-out revealed many forgotten treasures. 春季大扫除发现了许多被遗忘的珍宝。

例句：We need a major clear-out in the garage. 我们需要彻底清理一下车库。


近义词：cleanup, purge, clearance, spring cleaning | 反义词：accumulation, hoarding | 词性:名词
