retotaled:/ˌriːˈtoʊtəld/ | v. |重新计算，重新统计 | To calculate or count again.  This often implies a correction or revision of a previous calculation. |例句：The accountant retotaled the figures after discovering an error. 会计发现错误后重新计算了数字。
The company retotaled its inventory after the stocktake. 公司盘点后重新清点了库存。|近义词：recalculate, recount, reassess |反义词：None specifically, but implies the opposite of a final, accurate calculation.
