colonization:/ˌkɑːlənaɪˈzeɪʃən/|/ˌkɒlənaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |殖民；殖民地化；拓殖|The establishment, exploitation, and settlement of a colony in a new territory.  It often involves the imposition of the colonizer's culture, language, and political systems upon the indigenous population. |例句：The colonization of America had a devastating impact on the Native American population.  美洲的殖民对美洲原住民人口造成了毁灭性影响。

例句：European colonization of Africa led to significant political and economic changes. 欧洲对非洲的殖民导致了重大的政治和经济变化。


近义词：settlement, occupation, conquest | 反义词：decolonization, independence
