misbelief:/ˌmɪsˈbiːf/| n. |错误的信仰，误解|A false belief; an incorrect understanding of something.  It often stems from misinformation, misunderstanding, or a lack of knowledge.|例句：His disbelief in climate change is based on misbeliefs perpetuated by certain media outlets.  他的气候变化怀疑论是基于某些媒体散布的错误信仰。|His misbelief about the company's financial status led to a poor investment decision. 他对公司财务状况的误解导致了糟糕的投资决策。|近义词：delusion, misconception, fallacy|反义词：belief, truth, understanding
