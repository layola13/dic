词源:

The word "inodorate" is derived from the Latin prefix "in-" (meaning "not" or "without") and the Latin word "odor" (meaning "smell" or "scent").  The suffix "-ate" is used to form adjectives.  Therefore, "inodorate" literally means "without odor."
