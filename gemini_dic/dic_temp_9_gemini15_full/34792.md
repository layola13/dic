climax:/ˈklaɪmæks/| n. |高潮；顶点；结局|The most intense, exciting, or important point of something; a culmination or peak.|例句：The climax of the story was when the detective finally caught the murderer. 故事的高潮是侦探最终抓住了凶手。|例句：The concert reached its climax with a breathtaking performance by the lead singer. 音乐会的最高潮是主唱令人惊叹的表演。|近义词：peak, culmination, apex, high point|反义词：anticlimax, denouement, resolution|名词
