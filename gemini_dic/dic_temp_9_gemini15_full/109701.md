levanter:/ləˈvæntər/| n. |东风，热风；（尤指地中海地区的）东风； |A strong east wind, especially one in the Mediterranean.  The term often implies a warm, dry wind.| 例句：The levanter blew strongly throughout the day, making sailing difficult.  东风整天强劲地吹着，使航行变得困难。  The hot levanter whipped up dust devils across the desert. 炎热的东风在沙漠上卷起了尘卷风。|近义词：easterly, sirocco (specific to the Mediterranean)|反义词：westerly
