offerings:/ˈɔːfərɪŋz/| n. |祭品；供奉；产品；服务|Things offered, especially to a god or as a gift; a range of products or services offered for sale or use.|The temple received many offerings from its devotees.  神庙收到了许多信徒的供奉。|The company's new offerings include a range of sustainable products.  这家公司的新产品包括一系列可持续产品。|gifts, presents, sacrifices, services, products|refusals, denials|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company offers a wide range of services. = 这家公司提供各种各样的服务。
Our store offers fresh produce daily. = 我们商店每天提供新鲜农产品。


一般过去时:
She offered me a cup of tea. = 她给我一杯茶。
They offered a generous donation to the charity. = 他们向慈善机构慷慨捐赠。


现在完成时:
They have offered several solutions to the problem. = 他们已经为这个问题提出了几个解决方案。
The company has offered excellent customer service. = 该公司提供了优质的客户服务。


过去完成时:
He had already offered his resignation before the meeting. = 会议之前他已经递交了辞呈。
She had offered to help before I even asked. = 我还没开口她就主动提出帮忙了。


将来时:
They will offer a special discount for the holiday season. = 他们将在节日期间提供特别折扣。
The restaurant will offer a new menu next month. = 餐厅下个月将推出新的菜单。


虚拟语气:
If they offered me the job, I would accept it. = 如果他们给我这份工作，我会接受。
I wish they had offered me more support. = 我希望他们能给我更多支持。


被动语态:
Many sacrifices were offered to the gods. = 许多祭品被献给神明。
New products are being offered to customers. = 新产品正在提供给顾客。


疑问句:
What offerings are available at the temple? = 寺庙里有什么供奉？
What offerings are included in the package? = 套餐里包含哪些服务？


否定句:
They did not offer any explanation for their actions. = 他们没有对他们的行为做出任何解释。
The company does not offer refunds on sale items. = 公司不退还打折商品的货款。
