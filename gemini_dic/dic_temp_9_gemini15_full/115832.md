marketers:/ˈmɑːrkɪtərz/| n. |营销人员，市场营销者 |People involved in the planning and execution of marketing activities to promote and sell products or services.|Example:  The marketers developed a new campaign to reach a younger demographic.  营销人员开发了一个新的活动以接触更年轻的人群。|近义词：marketers, sales representatives, advertisers |反义词：consumers, buyers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The marketers analyze consumer behavior. = 营销人员分析消费者行为。
Marketers create targeted advertising campaigns. = 营销人员创建有针对性的广告活动。

一般过去时:
The marketers launched a successful product launch. = 营销人员成功地推出了产品。
Marketers studied the effectiveness of their previous campaigns. = 营销人员研究了他们先前活动的有效性。

现在完成时:
The marketers have improved their strategies. = 营销人员改进了他们的策略。
Marketers have reached their sales targets. = 营销人员达到了他们的销售目标。

过去完成时:
The marketers had already prepared the presentation before the meeting. = 会议之前，营销人员已经准备好了演示文稿。
Marketers had completed the market research before launching the product. = 在推出产品之前，营销人员已经完成了市场调查。

将来时:
The marketers will implement a new marketing plan next quarter. = 营销人员将在下个季度实施新的营销计划。
Marketers will use social media to promote the brand. = 营销人员将使用社交媒体来推广品牌。

虚拟语气:
If the marketers had more budget, they would run a larger campaign. = 如果营销人员有更多预算，他们将开展更大规模的活动。
I wish the marketers had considered customer feedback earlier. = 我希望营销人员早些考虑客户反馈。

被动语态:
The new marketing strategy was developed by the marketers. = 新的营销策略是由营销人员制定的。
The campaign is being managed by a team of experienced marketers. = 该活动由一支经验丰富的营销人员团队管理。

疑问句:
Who are the target marketers for this product? = 这个产品的目标营销人员是谁？
What marketing strategies are the marketers using? = 营销人员正在使用什么营销策略？

否定句:
The marketers did not achieve their sales goals last year. = 营销人员去年没有实现他们的销售目标。
The company's marketers haven't launched a new product this year. = 该公司的营销人员今年还没有推出新产品。
