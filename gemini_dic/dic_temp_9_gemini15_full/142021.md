organized:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ /ˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/| adj.| 有组织的；井井有条的；有系统的 |  Characterized by good order and planning; systematic.  It implies a methodical and efficient arrangement of things or activities. |


例句：He has an organized approach to his work.  他工作有条不紊。

例句：Her desk is always so organized. 她的桌子总是井井有条。


近义词：systematic, methodical, efficient, orderly, structured

反义词：chaotic, disorganized, messy, haphazard, cluttered

词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team is organized and efficient. = 这个团队组织有序且效率高。
His schedule is always well organized. = 他的日程安排总是井井有条。


一般过去时:
The event was well organized. = 活动组织得很好。
The files were organized alphabetically. = 文件按字母顺序排列。


现在完成时:
They have organized several successful fundraising events. = 他们已经组织了几次成功的筹款活动。
She has organized her thoughts for the presentation. = 她已经整理好思路准备做演讲。


过去完成时:
He had organized everything before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经把一切都安排好了。
The committee had organized a detailed plan. = 委员会已经制定了一个详细的计划。


将来时:
We will organize a trip to the beach next month. = 我们下个月将组织一次海滩旅行。
They will organize a meeting to discuss the proposal. = 他们将组织一次会议来讨论这项提案。


虚拟语气:
If we were better organized, we would finish on time. = 如果我们更有条理，就能按时完成。
I wish I had been more organized for the exam. = 我真希望考试时我能更有条理一些。


被动语态:
The conference was organized by the university. = 会议由大学组织。
The documents are being organized for the audit. = 文件正在整理以备审计。


疑问句:
Is the project well organized? = 这个项目组织得好吗？
Are you organized enough to handle this task? = 你有条理到足以处理这项任务吗？


否定句:
The meeting wasn't well organized. = 会议组织得不好。
His thoughts are not organized. = 他的思路不清晰。


PHRASE->
highly organized = 高度组织化的
well organized = 组织良好的
poorly organized = 组织不良的
get organized = 整理好；有条理起来
stay organized = 保持井井有条
