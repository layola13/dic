huntsman:/ˈhʌnts.mən/| n. |猎手；狩猎者；（某些蜘蛛的）雄蛛|A huntsman is a person who hunts animals, especially for sport or food.  The word can also refer to a type of spider, specifically the male of certain species.|例句：The huntsman skillfully tracked the deer through the forest. 猎手熟练地追踪着穿过森林的鹿。|例句：The huntsman returned home with a large bag of game. 猎手带着满满一袋猎物回家了。|近义词：hunter, sportsman, tracker|反义词：prey

The huntsman hunts deer every autumn. = 猎人在每个秋季都狩猎鹿。
He hunts rabbits in the fields near his home. = 他在家附近的田野里狩猎兔子。

一般过去时:
The huntsman hunted a fox yesterday. = 猎手昨天狩猎了一只狐狸。
They hunted ducks in the marsh. = 他们在沼泽地里狩猎鸭子。

现在完成时:
The huntsman has hunted in these woods for many years. = 这位猎人在这些树林里狩猎多年了。
She has hunted several different species of animals. = 她已经猎杀过几种不同的动物。

过去完成时:

They had hunted for hours before they found any game. = 他们在找到任何猎物之前已经狩猎了好几个小时。
