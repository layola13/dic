nostalgia:/nɒˈstældʒə/ /nɑːˈstældʒə/| n. |怀旧，乡愁 |A sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past, typically for a period or place with happy personal associations.| 例句：He felt a pang of nostalgia for his childhood home. 他对童年家园感到一阵怀旧之情。

例句：The old photograph evoked a rush of nostalgia. 这张旧照片唤起了强烈的怀旧之情。


近义词：sentimentality, longing, yearning | 反义词：present, contemporary


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He feels nostalgia for his college days. = 他怀念他的大学时光。
She often experiences nostalgia when listening to old songs. = 她听老歌时经常会感到怀旧。


一般过去时:
I felt a deep nostalgia for my hometown after moving abroad. = 我搬到国外后，对家乡感到深深的怀旧。
They recalled their childhood with a sense of nostalgia. = 他们怀着怀旧的心情回忆童年。


现在完成时:
He has felt nostalgia for his childhood home many times since moving away. = 自从搬走后，他多次怀念他的童年家园。
She has never experienced such intense nostalgia before. = 她以前从未有过如此强烈的怀旧之情。


过去完成时:
Before the trip, she had already felt nostalgia for her family. = 旅行之前，她就已经开始怀念家人了。
By the time he returned, he had developed a strong nostalgia for his old life. = 等他回来的时候，他已经对过去的生活产生了强烈的怀旧之情。


将来时:
I think I will feel nostalgia for this place when I leave. = 我想离开这里的时候会怀念这个地方。
They believe they will experience more nostalgia as they grow older. = 他们相信随着年龄的增长，他们会体验到更多的怀旧之情。


虚拟语气:
If I could go back in time, I wouldn't feel this nostalgia. = 如果我能回到过去，我就不会有这种怀旧之情了。
I wish I hadn't lost those photos; now I have such strong nostalgia. = 我真希望我没有丢失那些照片；现在我如此怀旧。


被动语态:
Nostalgia is often triggered by sensory experiences. = 怀旧之情常常由感官体验引发。
His feelings of nostalgia were evoked by the old music. = 他对过去的怀念之情是由老歌引起的。


疑问句:
Does he often feel nostalgia for his past? = 他经常怀念过去吗？
What triggers your nostalgia? = 什么会引发你的怀旧之情？


否定句:
He doesn't feel any nostalgia for that period of his life. = 他对人生的那段时期没有任何怀旧之情。
She didn't experience much nostalgia during her visit to her old school. = 她回到母校参观时并没有感到太多的怀旧。
