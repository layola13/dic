poplin:/ˈpɒplɪn/| n.|涤纶;府绸|A fine, closely woven fabric, typically of cotton or cotton and silk, with a slightly glossy surface.  It's known for its smooth texture and crispness.|The shirt is made of a lightweight poplin.|近义词：broadcloth, calico, muslin|反义词：无

词源:
The word "poplin" derives from the French word "papelyn," which in turn is believed to come from Avignon, a city in France, historically renowned for its textile production.  The term likely refers to the specific type of fabric produced there.
