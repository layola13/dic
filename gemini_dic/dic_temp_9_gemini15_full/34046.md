citylike:/ˈsɪtiliːk/| adj.|城市似的，像城市的|Resembling a city; having the characteristics of a city.  The term often implies a high density of population, buildings, and infrastructure, and sometimes a bustling or impersonal atmosphere.|例句：The sprawling suburb had a surprisingly citylike atmosphere.  这个广阔的郊区有着令人惊讶的城市氛围。

例句：The new development, with its towering buildings and busy streets, felt very citylike. 这处新开发项目，高耸的建筑和繁忙的街道，感觉非常像城市。


近义词：urban, metropolitan, urbane

反义词：rural, countrified, rustic

词性:形容词
