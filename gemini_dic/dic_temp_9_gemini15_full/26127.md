buster's:/ˈbʌstərz/| n. |巴斯特的；破坏者；（尤指）顽皮的孩子|The possessive form of "buster,"  referring to something belonging to or associated with a person named Buster.  It can also be used informally as a term for a mischievous child or someone who destroys things. |例句：That's Buster's bike.那是巴斯特的自行车。|近义词： rascal's, scamp's, troublemaker's|反义词： N/A
