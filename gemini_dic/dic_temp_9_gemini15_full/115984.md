marseillaise:/ˌmɑːrsɛˈleɪz/| n. |马赛曲|The Marseillaise is the national anthem of France. It is a powerful and stirring song that evokes feelings of patriotism and revolution.  The song was written during the French Revolution and quickly became a symbol of French national identity and revolutionary fervor.|The Marseillaise was adopted as the French national anthem in 1795.  The song is played at official ceremonies and sporting events. |近义词：national anthem, patriotic song|反义词：无|名词
