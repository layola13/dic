dreadnought:/ˈdrɛdnɔːt/| n. |无畏号战列舰；（尤指）无畏级战列舰；令人敬畏的人或事物|A dreadnought is a type of battleship, particularly referring to the HMS Dreadnought and subsequent ships of its class, which revolutionized naval warfare with its all-big-gun armament.  It can also refer to something or someone that inspires awe or fear. |例句：The HMS Dreadnought marked a significant leap in naval technology.  无畏号战列舰标志着海军技术的重大飞跃。

The new CEO was a true dreadnought in the business world.这位新任首席执行官是商界一位真正令人敬畏的人物。|近义词：battleship, giant, powerhouse|反义词：weakling, insignificant
