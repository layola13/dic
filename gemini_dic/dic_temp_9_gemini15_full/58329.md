dryroast:/ˈdraɪroʊst/ | vt. | 干烤，焙炒 | To roast (food) without added liquid or fat.  This cooking method is often used for nuts, seeds, and spices to enhance their flavor and texture. | Example: I dry-roasted the pumpkin seeds until they were golden brown and crispy. 我把南瓜子干烤到金黄酥脆。|近义词：roast, parch, toast | 反义词：boil, stew
