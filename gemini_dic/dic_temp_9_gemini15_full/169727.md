pterygium:/ptɛˈrɪdʒiəm/| n. |翼状胬肉|A pterygium is a triangular thickening of the conjunctiva that grows onto the cornea from the nasal side. It is a common condition, particularly in sunny or windy climates.  The growth is usually slow and painless, but it can affect vision if it grows large enough to cover the pupil.  Treatment options include surgery to remove the pterygium. |Example: The ophthalmologist examined the patient's eye and confirmed the presence of a pterygium. 眼科医生检查了病人的眼睛，并确认存在翼状胬肉。 The growing pterygium was affecting his vision.  不断生长的翼状胬肉影响了他的视力。|近义词:conjunctival growth, corneal pinguecula (although pinguecula is a different condition)|反义词: N/A |名词
