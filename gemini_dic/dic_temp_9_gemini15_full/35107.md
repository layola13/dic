clotting:/ˈklɒtɪŋ/| /ˈklɑːtɪŋ/ | n. |凝结；凝固；血液凝固 |The process by which blood changes from a liquid to a semi-solid gel, forming a blood clot.  This is a crucial process for stopping bleeding. |例句：The clotting time was abnormally long. 凝血时间异常的长。

The rapid clotting of the blood prevented excessive bleeding. 血液快速凝固阻止了大量出血。|近义词：coagulation, thickening, congealing |反义词：liquefaction, thinning, bleeding
