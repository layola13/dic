perinatal:/ˌpɛəriˈneɪtəl/| adj.|围产期的|Relating to the time around childbirth, typically taken as extending from the 28th week of pregnancy to the end of the neonatal period (about 28 days after birth).  This period encompasses significant developmental changes in the fetus and newborn, and is critical for long-term health. |Example:  Perinatal care is crucial for both the mother and the baby. 围产期护理对母亲和婴儿都至关重要。 The study focused on perinatal mortality rates. 这项研究侧重于围产期死亡率。|近义词：postnatal, antenatal (partially), neonatal (partially)|反义词：prenatal (in some contexts)
