octanoic: /ˌɒkˈteɪnoʊɪk/ /ˌɑːkˈteɪnoʊɪk/ | adj. | 辛酸的；辛酸味的 |  Octanoic refers to a saturated fatty acid with eight carbon atoms.  It is also known as caprylic acid.  It is found in coconut oil and palm kernel oil, and has various applications in the food and cosmetic industries. | Example: Coconut oil contains a significant amount of octanoic acid. 椰子油含有大量的辛酸。|近义词: caprylic | 反义词: N/A
