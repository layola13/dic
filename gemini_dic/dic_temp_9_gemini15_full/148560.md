oversystematize:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪstəməˌtaɪz/| v. |过度系统化；使过于系统化 |To organize or systematize something to an excessive or unnecessary degree, often resulting in rigidity and inefficiency.  |The company oversystematized its workflow, leading to delays and frustration among employees. 公司过度系统化了工作流程，导致员工延误和沮丧。|  systematize, overcomplicate | simplify, streamline | 动词
