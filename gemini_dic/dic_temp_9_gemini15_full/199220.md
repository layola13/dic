silkworm:/ˈsɪlkwɔːrm/| n. |蚕|A silk-producing insect, especially the larva of a moth of the family Bombycidae, that feeds on mulberry leaves and spins a cocoon of silk.  |例句：The silkworm spins a cocoon of silk. 蚕吐丝结茧。|例句：The life cycle of a silkworm is fascinating. 蚕的生命周期令人着迷。|近义词：silk moth larva|反义词：无
