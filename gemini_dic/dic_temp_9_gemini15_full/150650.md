/pæp/| n.|糊状食物，婴儿食品|pap is a soft, easily digestible food, typically made from boiled cereal, often given to babies or invalids.| 例句：The baby was fed a bowl of rice pap. 婴儿吃了一碗米糊。| 例句：The sick child ate only pap for a week.  生病的孩子一周只吃糊状食物。|近义词：porridge, gruel|反义词：solid food
