greetingless: /ˈɡriːtɪŋləs/ | adj. | 无问候的，没有问候的 | Lacking greetings or the usual courtesies.  It describes a situation or interaction where greetings are absent or omitted. | 例句：The meeting started in a greetingless manner, straight into the agenda.  会议一开始就直奔主题，毫无问候之意。

The atmosphere was greetingless and somewhat unfriendly.  气氛冷冰冰的，有些不友好。


近义词：unwelcoming, unsociable, formal (in a negative context) | 反义词：welcoming, cordial, friendly
