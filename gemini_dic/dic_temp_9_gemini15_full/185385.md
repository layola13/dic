retrogradist:/ˈrɛtrəɡreɪdɪst/| adj., n.| 反动者；反对进步的人；倒退的 | A person or thing opposed to progress or reform;  someone who favors a return to older, often worse, ways.  |例句：His views are considered retrogradist by many. 许多人认为他的观点是反动的。

例句：The retrogradist policies stifled economic growth.  那些反动的政策扼杀了经济增长。


近义词：reactionary, conservative, traditionalist


反义词：progressive, forward-thinking, innovator


词性:  adjective, noun


词源:  From  Latin *retrogradus, from retro- ("backward") + gradi ("to step").  The word reflects a movement backward, against progress.
