mesalliance:/ˌmɛsæˈliɑːns/| n. |不门当户对的婚姻；不相称的结合 |An unsuitable or socially unacceptable marriage, typically one between people of different social classes or backgrounds.  It often implies a marriage where one partner is considered inferior in social standing, wealth, or family reputation. |例句：Their marriage was considered a mesalliance by her family, who disapproved of his humble origins.  他们的婚姻被她的家人视为不门当户对，因为他们不赞成他的卑微出身。|近义词：misalliance, ill-assorted marriage, unequal match |反义词：suitable marriage, advantageous match
