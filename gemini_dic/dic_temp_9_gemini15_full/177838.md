recentrifuged: /ˌriːsɛnˈtrɪfjuːdʒd/ | adj. | 再次离心的；重新离心的 |  Having been centrifuged again.  This term describes a substance or sample that has undergone centrifugation a second time, or multiple times after an initial centrifugation. | 例句：The recentrifuged sample showed a clearer separation of components. = 再次离心的样品显示出更清晰的组分分离。

The recentrifuged blood yielded a more concentrated serum. = 再次离心的血液产生了更浓缩的血清。


近义词：re-spun, re-centrifugated | 反义词：uncentrifuged | 词性：形容词
