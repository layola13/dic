dissolvable: /dɪˈsɒlvəbl/ | adj. | 可溶解的，可溶的 | Capable of being dissolved; soluble.  | Example:  Sugar is dissolvable in water. 糖溶于水。  The tablet is dissolvable under the tongue. 药片可在舌下溶解。 | 近义词：soluble, soluble in water | 反义词：insoluble, undissolvable

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This sugar is dissolvable in hot water. = 这糖在热水中可溶解。
That tablet is dissolvable under the tongue. = 那个药片可在舌下溶解。

一般过去时:
The salt was dissolvable in the water. = 盐溶于水中。
The medicine was easily dissolvable. = 这药很容易溶解。

现在完成时:
The ice has become dissolvable in the warm sun. = 冰在温暖的阳光下已经融化了。
The snow has become dissolvable due to the heat. = 雪由于高温已经融化了。

过去完成时:
The pill had been dissolvable before it reached the stomach. = 药片在到达胃部之前已经溶解了。
The sugar had already been dissolvable in the tea. = 糖已经在茶里溶解了。

词源:
