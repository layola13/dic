scamp:/skæmp/| n. v.| 小淘气；恶作剧；逃脱；飞奔 | a mischievous child; to playfully misbehave; to run or move quickly and playfully; to escape | The scamp hid behind the tree.  这个小淘气藏在树后面。 | He scampered away. 他飞快地跑开了。| rascal, rogue, sprite | angel, saint | noun, verb


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He scamps around the house all day. 他整天在房子里跑来跑去。
That child is a real scamp.  那个孩子真是个小淘气。


一般过去时:
The dog scampered away when it saw me. 那只狗看到我就跑开了。
He scamped his homework and got a bad grade. 他匆匆忙忙地完成了作业，结果得了低分。


现在完成时:
He has scamped through his exams. 他草率地应付了考试。
She has scamped out of trouble many times. 她多次逃脱了麻烦。


过去完成时:
He had scamped his way through life, never settling down to any one thing. 他一路玩耍着度过人生，从未安定下来做任何一件事。
They had scamped the project and it was full of errors. 他们草率地完成了项目，里面充满了错误。


将来时:
He will scamp away as soon as he sees the police. 他一看到警察就会跑掉。
She will scamp through her work to finish on time. 她将草率地完成工作以按时完成。


虚拟语气:
If he were a scamp, he would have done it. 如果他是个小淘气，他会这么做的。
I wish he hadn't scamped his responsibilities. 我真希望他没有逃避责任。


被动语态:
The assignment was scamped. 作业完成得很粗糙。

疑问句:

否定句:
