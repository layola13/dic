graciousness:/ˈɡreɪʃəsnəs/ | n. |优雅；亲切；宽厚；恩惠 |The quality of being courteous, kind, and pleasant.  It often implies a refined elegance and generosity of spirit. |例句：Her graciousness and charm won over everyone in the room.  她的优雅和魅力赢得了房间里所有人的好感。

例句：The graciousness of the host made everyone feel welcome. 主人的热情好客让每个人都感到宾至如归。


近义词：courtesy, politeness, kindness, generosity, elegance, charm

反义词：rudeness, discourtesy, unkindness, ungraciousness, harshness


词源：源自古法语 *gracious,  最终源自拉丁语 *gratia,  意思是"感激, 恩惠, 喜悦"。
