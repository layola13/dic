expressionismus:/ɛkspˈrɛʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 表现主义 | Expressionism is a modernist movement, primarily in the visual arts, performing arts, and literature, that emphasizes subjective experience and emotional expression over objective representation.  It often features distortion, exaggeration, and symbolic representation to convey inner feelings and states of mind.  | Example:  The painting exemplifies the bold colors and distorted forms characteristic of Expressionism. 这幅画体现了表现主义特有的大胆色彩和扭曲形式。 | 近义词:  modernism, avant-garde | 反义词:  realism, classicism
