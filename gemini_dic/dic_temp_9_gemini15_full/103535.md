jog-on:/dʒɒɡ ˈɒn/| phrasal verb|滚开，走开，离开|To leave or go away, often in a slightly rude or dismissive way.  It implies a suggestion to move along and stop bothering someone.|例句：Just jog on, will you?  你走吧，好吗？

例句：He told the pesky kids to jog on and leave him alone. 他让那些讨厌的孩子滚开，别烦他。


近义词：clear off, get lost, piss off (informal),  go away, move along


反义词：stay, remain, linger


词性：phrasal verb
