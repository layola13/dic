nocturnes:/ˈnɒktɜːrnz/ | n. |夜曲，夜想曲 | Nocturnes are musical compositions, typically for piano, that evoke the atmosphere of night. They often feature a melancholic or dreamy quality.  | Example: Chopin's nocturnes are renowned for their beauty and emotional depth.  肖邦的夜曲以其美丽和情感深度而闻名。  |近义词：night piece, serenade |反义词：diurnal piece
