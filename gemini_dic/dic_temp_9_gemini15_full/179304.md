redo:/ˌriːˈduː/ | vt. |重做，再做|To do something again, often because it was not done correctly the first time.  |例句：We had to redo the entire project because of a critical error. 我们不得不因为一个严重的错误而重做整个项目。
例句：The teacher asked me to redo my essay because of the grammar mistakes. 老师让我因为语法错误重做我的论文。|近义词：repeat, revise, remake |反义词：complete, finish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redoes his work every time he finds a mistake. = 他每次发现错误都会重做他的工作。
They redo the presentation before every important meeting. = 他们在每次重要会议之前都会重做演示文稿。


一般过去时:
She redid the painting after receiving criticism. = 她收到批评后重做了这幅画。
We redid the experiment to ensure accuracy. = 我们重做了实验以确保准确性。


现在完成时:
He has redone the assignment three times already. = 他已经重做了三次作业了。
They have redone the website to improve its user experience. = 他们已经重做了网站以改善用户体验。


过去完成时:
She had redone the calculations before submitting her report. = 她提交报告之前已经重做了计算。
They had redone the entire scene after realizing the mistake in lighting. = 他们意识到灯光错误后重做了整个场景。


将来时:
I will redo my homework after dinner. = 晚饭后我将重做我的家庭作业。
We will redo the test next week. = 我们下周将重做测试。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would redo the whole thing. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重做整个事情。
It would be better if we redid the presentation with more visuals. = 如果我们用更多视觉效果重做演示文稿会更好。


被动语态:
The presentation was redone due to the poor feedback. = 演示文稿由于反馈不佳而被重做。
The entire section will be redone by the end of the week. = 整节内容将在本周末之前重做。


疑问句:
Do you need to redo the exercise? = 你需要重做练习吗？
Should we redo the experiment? = 我们应该重做实验吗？


否定句:
I don't need to redo my work; it's perfect. = 我不需要重做我的工作；它很完美。
They didn't redo the project, despite the initial flaws. = 尽管最初存在缺陷，他们也没有重做项目。
