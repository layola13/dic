self-sufficientness: /ˌsɛlf səˈfɪʃəntnəs/ | n. |自给自足；自立；独立自主 | The state or quality of being self-sufficient; the ability to provide for one's own needs without external assistance.  |例句：The farm achieved self-sufficientness in food production. 这个农场实现了粮食生产的自给自足。

例句：Her self-sufficientness allowed her to live comfortably in a remote area.  她的自给自足使她能够舒适地生活在偏远地区。


近义词：independence, autonomy, self-reliance, self-sustenance

反义词：dependence, reliance, neediness
