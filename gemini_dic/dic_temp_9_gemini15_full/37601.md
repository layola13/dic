communicates:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪts/ | v. | 传达；沟通；交流 | To convey information or express oneself successfully. |例句：He communicates his ideas clearly and effectively. 他清晰有效地表达了他的想法。|近义词：conveys, expresses, transmits |反义词：conceals, hides, obscures |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She communicates effectively with her team. = 她与她的团队有效沟通。
He communicates his needs clearly. = 他清楚地表达了他的需求。


一般过去时:
The speaker communicated his message powerfully. = 演讲者有力地传达了他的信息。
They communicated their concerns to the manager. = 他们向经理表达了他们的担忧。


现在完成时:
She has communicated her decision to the board. = 她已将她的决定告知董事会。
We have communicated frequently throughout the project. = 在整个项目中，我们经常沟通。


过去完成时:
He had communicated his intentions before leaving. = 他在离开之前已经表达了他的意图。
They had communicated their dissatisfaction several times. = 他们已经多次表达了他们的不满。


将来时:
He will communicate the results of the study next week. = 他下周将公布研究结果。
She will communicate with the client shortly. = 她很快就会与客户联系。


虚拟语气:
If he communicated more clearly, he would be better understood. = 如果他沟通更清晰，他会更容易被理解。
I wish she communicated her feelings more openly. = 我希望她能更坦诚地表达自己的感受。


被动语态:
The message was communicated effectively to the audience. = 信息有效地传达给了观众。
The decision was communicated to the employees yesterday. = 决定昨天已经传达给了员工。


疑问句:
How does he communicate with his colleagues? = 他如何与同事沟通？
Does she communicate regularly with her family? = 她经常和家人联系吗？


否定句:
He didn't communicate his concerns properly. = 他没有恰当地表达他的担忧。
She doesn't communicate well under pressure. = 她在压力下沟通不好。


PHRASE->
communicate with = 与...沟通
communicate effectively = 有效沟通
communicate a message = 传达信息
communicate ideas = 传达想法
communicate clearly = 清晰地沟通
