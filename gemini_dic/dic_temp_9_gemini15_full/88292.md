hip's: /hɪps/ | n. |臀部（复数）|The hips are the area of the body where the legs join the trunk.  It refers to the two hip bones.|例句：She has very narrow hips. = 她的臀部很窄。

例句：He injured his hips during the fall. = 他在跌倒时伤到了臀部。


近义词：buttocks, haunches


反义词：无


词性: 名词 (复数形式)


词源:  The word "hip" ultimately derives from the Old English word "hippe," meaning "hip, haunch."  This, in turn, has connections to Proto-Germanic and even further back to Proto-Indo-European roots related to the concept of the body's flank or side.  The plural form "hips" simply indicates that it refers to both hip bones.
