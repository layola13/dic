disentomb:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtuːm/ | vt. | 掘出；发掘出（尤指坟墓中的人或物） | To remove (something, especially a body) from a tomb; unearth.  |例句：Archaeologists disentombed the ancient artifacts. 考古学家挖掘出了古代文物。

例句：They disentombed the king's remains for further study. 他们挖掘出国王的遗骸以便进一步研究。


近义词：unearth, exhume, uncover | 反义词：bury, inter, entomb | 词性：动词
