guarder:/ˈɡɑːrdər/| n.|守护者，看守者，保管人|A person or thing that guards or protects something.|The museum guarder kept a watchful eye on the priceless artifacts.  博物馆的守护者密切注视着无价的文物。|The dog acted as a guarder of the house. 这只狗充当了房子的守护者。|protector, custodian, keeper|attacker, destroyer, vandal|名词
