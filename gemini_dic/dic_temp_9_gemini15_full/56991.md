double-decker:/ˈdʌbəlˌdɛkər/| adj. n.|双层（的）|Having or consisting of two decks or levels, especially a double-decker bus.  A double-decker bus is a bus with two levels for passengers.|例句：We rode on a double-decker bus to see the city. 我们乘坐双层巴士观光城市。  The hotel offers double-decker rooms with stunning views. 这家酒店提供带迷人景色的双层客房。|近义词：two-story, two-tiered|反义词：single-decker|形容词，名词
