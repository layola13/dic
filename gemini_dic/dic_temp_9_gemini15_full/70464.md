figurine: /ˌfɪɡəˈriːn/ | n. | 小雕像，小塑像 | A small statuette, typically of a person, animal, or mythological creature.  |例句：She collected porcelain figurines of animals. 她收集动物瓷器小雕像。


例句：The antique shop was filled with beautiful figurines. 这家古董店里摆满了漂亮的小雕像。


近义词：statuette, figure, model


反义词：无


词性:名词

词源:
