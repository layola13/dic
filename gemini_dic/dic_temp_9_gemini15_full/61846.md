employed:/ɪmˈplɔɪd/ | adj. | 受雇的；被雇佣的；使用的 | Having a job; working for an employer.  |例句：She is employed as a teacher. 她受雇于一家学校当老师。|例句：The new technique employed in this project is innovative.  这个项目中采用的新技术很创新。 |近义词：hired, engaged, utilized |反义词：unemployed, jobless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is employed at a local bank. = 他在当地一家银行工作。
She is employed full-time. = 她全职工作。

一般过去时:
He was employed at the factory for ten years. = 他在那家工厂工作了十年。
She was employed by a large corporation. = 她受雇于一家大公司。

现在完成时:
He has been employed in the same company for five years. = 他在这家公司工作了五年。
She has been employed by several firms. = 她曾在几家公司工作过。

过去完成时:
He had been employed there before the company closed. = 在公司倒闭之前，他曾在那里工作过。
She had been employed as a consultant for many years. = 她做过多年的顾问。

将来时:
He will be employed by a new company next month. = 他下个月将受雇于一家新公司。
She will be employed in a different department. = 她将被调到不同的部门工作。

虚拟语气:
If I were employed, I would have more money. = 如果我有工作，我就会有更多钱。
I wish I were employed somewhere I enjoyed. = 我希望我能找到一份我喜欢的工作。


被动语态:
The new method was employed successfully. = 新方法成功地得到了应用。
Many resources have been employed in this research. = 这项研究投入了大量的资源。

疑问句:
Is she employed? = 她有工作吗？
Are you currently employed? = 你目前有工作吗？

否定句:
He is not employed at the moment. = 他目前没有工作。
She was not employed by that company. = 她没有在那家公司工作。


PHRASE->
employed in = 从事…；被用在…
employed by = 受雇于…
fully employed =  充分就业
employed as = 担任…；被用作…
long-term employed = 长期雇员
