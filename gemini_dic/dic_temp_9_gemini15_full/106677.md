kvetcher:/ˈkvɛtʃər/| v.|抱怨，唠叨 | To complain constantly and in a whining manner.  |例句：He's always kvetchering about something. 他总是抱怨一些事情。  |近义词：complain, grumble, whine, moan |反义词：praise, compliment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kvetches about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
She kvetches about her job constantly. = 她不断地抱怨她的工作。

一般过去时:
He kvetched about the long lines at the store. = 他抱怨商店的长队。
She kvetched to her friend about her boyfriend. = 她向她的朋友抱怨她的男朋友。


现在完成时:
He has kvetched about this problem for weeks. = 他已经抱怨这个问题好几个星期了。
She has kvetched to everyone she knows. = 她已经向她认识的每个人抱怨了。


过去完成时:
He had kvetched about the delay before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始他就已经抱怨延误了。
She had kvetched to her boss numerous times before she quit. = 在她辞职之前，她已经多次向老板抱怨了。


将来时:
He will kvetcher about the traffic again tomorrow. = 他明天又会抱怨交通。
She will kvetcher about the heat all summer long. = 整个夏天她都会抱怨炎热。


虚拟语气:
If he kvetched less, people would like him more. = 如果他少抱怨一些，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she wouldn't kvetcher so much. = 我希望她不要抱怨那么多。


被动语态:
His complaints are often kvetched about by others. = 他的抱怨经常被别人抱怨。 (This example is less common as "kvetcher" is typically used in the active voice.)


疑问句:
Does he kvetcher often? = 他经常抱怨吗？
Why does she kvetcher so much? = 她为什么抱怨那么多？


否定句:
He doesn't kvetcher as much as he used to. = 他不像以前那样经常抱怨了。
She didn't kvetcher at all during the meeting. = 会议期间她根本没有抱怨。
