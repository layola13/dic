corporeality: /ˌkɔːrpəˈriːælɪti/ | n. | 肉体性；物质性；实体性 | The quality or state of being corporeal; physical existence or reality.  It refers to the tangible, physical aspect of existence, as opposed to the spiritual or intangible.  The concept often explores the relationship between the body and the mind, or the body and the soul. | Example: The artist's work explores the themes of corporeality and mortality. 这名艺术家的作品探讨了肉体性和死亡的主题。  The philosopher argued for the importance of corporeality in understanding human experience.  这位哲学家论证了肉体性在理解人类经验中的重要性。 | 近义词：materiality, physicality, tangibility | 反义词：immateriality, spirituality, incorporeality |
