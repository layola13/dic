painter:/ˈpeɪntər/ | n. |画家，油漆工 | A person who paints, especially as an art form or profession.  | Example:  He is a renowned landscape painter. 他是一位著名的风景画家。  |近义词：artist, decorator | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painter paints a beautiful picture every day. = 画家每天都画一幅美丽的画。
She paints houses for a living. = 她以粉刷房屋为生。


一般过去时:
The painter painted a stunning mural last week. = 画家上周画了一幅令人惊叹的壁画。
He painted the entire house in one weekend. = 他在一个周末内粉刷了整栋房子。


现在完成时:
The artist has painted over a hundred portraits. =这位艺术家已经画了超过一百幅肖像。
They have painted the kitchen a cheerful yellow. = 他们把厨房漆成了明快的黄色。


过去完成时:
The painter had already finished the canvas before the exhibition. = 在展览之前，画家已经完成了画布。
Before they moved in, they had painted all the rooms. = 在他们搬进去之前，他们已经粉刷了所有的房间。


将来时:
The painter will paint a new series of abstract works next year. = 画家明年将创作一系列新的抽象作品。
They will paint the fence tomorrow. = 他们明天将粉刷栅栏。


虚拟语气:
If I were a painter, I would paint landscapes. = 如果我是一个画家，我会画风景画。
I wish I could paint like Picasso. = 我希望我能像毕加索一样画画。


被动语态:
The painting was painted by a famous artist. = 这幅画是由一位著名的艺术家创作的。
The house is being painted at the moment. = 房子正在被粉刷。


疑问句:
Who painted this masterpiece? = 谁画了这幅杰作？
Is the painter using oil paints? = 画家是用油彩作画吗？


否定句:
He is not a painter; he's a sculptor. = 他不是画家，他是雕塑家。
She didn't paint the whole wall. = 她没有粉刷整面墙。
