negrohood:/ˈniːɡroʊhʊd/| n. |黑人居住区，黑人社区 |A residential area primarily inhabited by Black people.  The term is now widely considered offensive due to its historical association with racism and segregation. |例句：The study focused on the living conditions in several negrohoods in the South.  这项研究关注南方几个黑人居住区的居住条件。|近义词：Black neighborhood, African American community |反义词： (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with predominantly white neighborhoods) |名词
