constitutionalists:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəlɪsts/|/ˈkɑːnstɪˌtuːʃənəlɪsts/| n. |宪法主义者|Advocates of constitutional government; those who believe in and support a system of government based on a constitution.  They emphasize the rule of law, separation of powers, and protection of individual rights as enshrined in the constitution.|The constitutionalists argued for a stronger judiciary to uphold the principles of the constitution.  宪法主义者主张加强司法系统以维护宪法的原则。|  supporters of a constitution, constitutional advocates | opponents of constitutional government (depending on context)| 名词
