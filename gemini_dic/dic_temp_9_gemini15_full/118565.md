mesorchium:/ˌmɛzˈɔːrkiəm/| n.| 中肾中隔 |The mesorchium is a fold of peritoneum that connects the testis to the body wall in males.  It is part of the mesentery, a double layer of peritoneum that supports the abdominal organs. The mesorchium contains blood vessels, nerves, and lymphatic vessels that supply the testis. In some species, the mesorchium may also contain the epididymis.  The mesorchium is a structure that is found in both humans and animals.  |The mesorchium is a crucial structure in the development and function of the male reproductive system.| 近义词：mesentery (更广义)| 反义词：无
