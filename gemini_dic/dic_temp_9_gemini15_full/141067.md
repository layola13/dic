onomatechny: /ˌɒnəʊməˈtɛkni/ | n. |拟声技术；摹声技术 | The creation of words or phrases that imitate the sounds they describe.  It involves the use of language to represent sounds, often natural sounds, like animal cries or the sounds of machinery.  | 例句：The author's onomatechny was masterful, perfectly capturing the clatter of the train. 作者的拟声技巧十分高超，完美地捕捉到了火车隆隆作响的声音。


例句：The use of onomatechny in poetry can add a layer of realism and vividness.  在诗歌中运用拟声技巧可以增添一层现实感和生动性。


近义词：sound imitation, phonetic symbolism, mimetic language


反义词：abstract language, literal description


词性: noun
