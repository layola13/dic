mothiest: /ˈmɒθiɪst/ | adj. | 最多蛾的；最像蛾的 |  The word "mothiest" is the superlative form of "mothy," meaning resembling or full of moths. It describes something that is extremely moth-eaten, covered in moths, or having the characteristics of moths to the greatest degree.  There is no standard dictionary definition for "mothiest" as it's not a frequently used word; it's a less common superlative form created by adding "-iest" to "mothy." However, its meaning is clearly derived from the base word. | 例句：The mothiest sweater I've ever seen was practically disintegrating.  = 我见过的最破旧的毛衣几乎要散架了。 The mothiest area of the attic was near the old chests. = 阁楼里最满是飞蛾的地方靠近旧箱子。 | 近义词：most moth-eaten, most infested with moths | 反义词：moth-free, clean, pristine |  词性：形容词
