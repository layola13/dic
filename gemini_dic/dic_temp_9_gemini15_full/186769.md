riling:/ˈraɪlɪŋ/| n. adj.|激怒；使恼火；令人恼火的 |  The act of irritating or provoking someone; something that irritates or provokes.  |例句：His constant complaining was riling me.  他的不断抱怨让我很生气。

例句：The riling effect of the news was evident in the tense atmosphere. 新闻的激怒效果在紧张的气氛中显而易见。


近义词：annoying, infuriating, irritating, provoking, exasperating | 反义词：soothing, calming, pacifying


词性: 名词，形容词
