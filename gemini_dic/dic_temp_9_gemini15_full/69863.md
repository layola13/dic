ferroso: /fɛˈroʊsoʊ/ | adj. | 亚铁的，二价铁的 | Relating to or denoting ferrous iron (iron in the +2 oxidation state).  Ferrous compounds are typically green or pale green in color. | 例句：Ferrous sulfate is a common mineral supplement.  硫酸亚铁是一种常见的矿物补充剂。

The ferrous ion is an important part of hemoglobin.  亚铁离子是血红蛋白的重要组成部分。


近义词：ferric (when contrasting with ferric, which refers to iron in the +3 oxidation state)  | 反义词：ferric | 词性：形容词


词源：From Latin *ferrōsus, from ferrum ("iron").  The suffix "-ous" indicates an adjective relating to a noun.
