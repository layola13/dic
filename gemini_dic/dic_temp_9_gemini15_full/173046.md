quartation:/kwɔːrˈteɪʃən/ | n. |四分之一；合金精炼法 |The process of refining gold or silver by repeatedly melting it with lead to remove impurities.  This process involves creating a mixture of one part gold or silver and three parts lead, hence the name "quartation" (one quarter).|例句：The goldsmith used quartation to purify the gold, removing base metals through the process of alloying and melting.|近义词：refining, purification |反义词：contamination, adulteration
