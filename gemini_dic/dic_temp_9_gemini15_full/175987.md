rattletrap: /ˈrætltræp/ | n. adj. | 摇摇晃晃的破车；破旧不堪的东西 | A rattletrap is a vehicle, especially an old car, that is in very bad condition and makes a lot of rattling noises.  It can also be used figuratively to describe something that is old, worn-out, and unreliable. |例句：His old car was a real rattletrap, constantly breaking down. 他的旧车真是辆破车，总是抛锚。 The old house was a rattletrap, full of drafts and squeaks.  这栋旧房子摇摇晃晃的，到处漏风吱呀作响。|近义词：jalopy, wreck, heap, dinosaur |反义词：new car, well-maintained vehicle, reliable machine
