channel-hopper: /ˈtʃænəl ˌhɑːpər/ | n. | 频道跳跃者；转台者 | A person who frequently changes television channels.  Someone who switches rapidly between different television channels, often without settling on any one program for very long. | Example:  He's a real channel-hopper; he never watches a show all the way through. 他是个真正的转台者；他从来不完整地看完一个节目。|近义词：zapper (informal)|反义词：dedicated viewer
