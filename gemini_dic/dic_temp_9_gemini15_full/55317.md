dissolves:/dɪˈzɒlvz/ | v. |溶解；解散；消失；瓦解|To become or cause to become liquid; to break up or be broken up into parts; to fade away; to disappear.|例句：Sugar dissolves in water. 糖溶于水。|例句：The parliament dissolved after the election. 选举后议会解散了。|近义词：melt, disintegrate, disperse, vanish|反义词：solidify, coalesce, form, appear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sugar dissolves quickly in hot water.  糖很快溶解在热水中。
Salt dissolves easily in water. 盐很容易溶解在水中。


一般过去时:
The snow dissolved in the spring sunshine.  春天的阳光融化了积雪。
The company dissolved after years of financial struggles.  这家公司在经历了多年的财务困境后解散了。


现在完成时:
The ice has dissolved completely. 冰已经完全融化了。
The tension between the two countries has dissolved since the peace agreement.  自从和平协议达成以来，两国之间的紧张关系已经消解了。


过去完成时:
The evidence had dissolved before the police arrived.  警察到达之前证据已经消失了。
The committee had dissolved before they could reach a decision.  委员会在他们能做出决定之前就解散了。


将来时:
The ice will dissolve as the temperature rises.  随着温度升高，冰会融化。
The government will likely dissolve parliament before the next election.  政府很可能会在下一次选举前解散议会。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
