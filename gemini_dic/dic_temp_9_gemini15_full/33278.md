chrysopa:/krɪˈsɒpə/| n. |草蛉|A common name for insects of the family Chrysopidae, characterized by their delicate, lace-like wings and often green bodies.  They are beneficial insects, as their larvae are voracious predators of aphids and other small insects. |例句：The gardener released chrysopas into the greenhouse to control the aphid infestation. 园丁将草蛉释放到温室中以控制蚜虫的侵扰。|近义词：lacewing|反义词：N/A
