ounces:/ˈaʊnsɪz/| n. |盎司|A unit of weight, equal to 1/16 of a pound (avoirdupois) or 1/12 of a pound (troy).  It's also used as a unit of fluid volume, especially in the US, for liquids such as beer.|The cake weighs 12 ounces.  这蛋糕重12盎司。|  He drank a 16-ounce bottle of water. 他喝了一瓶16盎司的水。|pounds, grams|milligrams, kilograms|noun
