republisher:/ˌriːˈpʌblɪʃər/ | n. | 再版者；重新发行者 | A person or organization that republishes something.  | Example: The small press acted as a republisher, bringing lesser-known works to a wider audience.  小型出版社扮演着再版者的角色，将鲜为人知的作品带给更广泛的读者。 |近义词:reissuer, reprint publisher |反义词:original publisher


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company republishes classic novels every year. = 这家公司每年都再版经典小说。
He republishes old maps for collectors. = 他为收藏家再版旧地图。


一般过去时:
They republished his first book after his death. = 他去世后，他们再版了他的第一本书。
She republished the article in her own blog. = 她在她自己的博客上重新发表了这篇文章。


现在完成时:
They have republished several of his works in recent years. = 近年来，他们已经再版了他的几部作品。
The publishing house has republished the book three times. = 这家出版社已经三次再版这本书了。


过去完成时:
They had republished the book before the author's death. = 在作者去世之前，他们已经再版了这本书。
The editor had republished the corrected version. = 编辑已经重新发布了更正后的版本。


将来时:
They will republish the book next year. = 他们明年将再版这本书。
The company will republish the magazine with updated content. = 公司将以更新的内容重新出版杂志。


虚拟语气:
If they republished his work, it would sell well. = 如果他们再版他的作品，它将会卖得很好。
I wish they would republish that old science fiction novel. = 我希望他们能再版那本旧的科幻小说。


被动语态:
The book was republished last month. = 这本书上个月再版了。
His poems are being republished by a university press. = 他的诗歌正在由一家大学出版社再版。


疑问句:
Will the book be republished? = 这本书会再版吗？
Has the magazine been republished yet? = 杂志已经再版了吗？


否定句:
They did not republish the book. = 他们没有再版这本书。
The publisher won't republish the controversial essay. = 出版商不会再版这篇有争议的文章。
