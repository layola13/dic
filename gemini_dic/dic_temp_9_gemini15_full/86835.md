/ˌhiːmətoʊˈθɜːrməl/ | adj. | 热液的；热液生成的 | Relating to or denoting processes or formations associated with hot water, especially in geological contexts.  The term often refers to hydrothermal vents on the ocean floor, where heated water rich in minerals emerges from the Earth's crust. | Example: The researchers discovered a rich array of hematothermal deposits near the mid-ocean ridge.  研究人员在洋中脊附近发现了一系列丰富的热液矿床。  | 近义词: hydrothermal, geothermic | 反义词:  cryogenic, glacial


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective, so verb conjugations are not applicable.  The usage centers on describing geological formations and processes.
