doom-laden: /ˈduːmˌleɪdn/ | adj. | 厄运缠身的；充满灾难预兆的 | Filled with or suggestive of doom; foreboding.  The atmosphere was doom-laden, heavy with the unspoken fear of war. | 近义词：ominous, threatening, foreboding, portentous | 反义词：auspicious, hopeful, promising


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sky looks doom-laden. = 天空看起来厄运缠身。
The news report is doom-laden. = 新闻报道充满了不祥之兆。

疑问句:
