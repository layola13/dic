embarrassing:/ɪmˈbærəsɪŋ/| adj. |令人尴尬的，难堪的 | causing a feeling of awkwardness, shame, or embarrassment.  It suggests a situation that makes someone feel uncomfortable or foolish. | 例句：Spilling coffee all over my boss was incredibly embarrassing.  我把咖啡洒了我老板一身，真是太尴尬了。

例句：His clumsy dance moves were quite embarrassing to watch. 他的笨拙舞姿看着实在尴尬。


近义词：awkward, shameful, mortifying, humiliating, uncomfortable

反义词：comfortable, pleasant, agreeable
