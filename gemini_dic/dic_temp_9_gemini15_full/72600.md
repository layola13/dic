fly-out: /ˈflaɪ aʊt/ | n. v. | 逃亡；飞出；弹出式菜单 |

英文解析:  A fly-out can refer to a quick escape or departure, often sudden or unexpected.  As a noun, it often describes a menu that appears when you click on something. As a verb, it describes the action of flying out or something being ejected.

例句:

n.(弹出式菜单):
The software has a handy fly-out menu for quick access to settings. = 这个软件有一个方便的弹出式菜单，可以快速访问设置。

v.(飞出):
The bird flew out of its cage. = 鸟飞出了笼子。


近义词: (depending on context)  escape, departure, pop-up, eject, outburst


反义词: (depending on context)  arrival, entrance, entry, retraction


词性: noun, verb
