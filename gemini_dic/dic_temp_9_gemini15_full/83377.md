gyrose:/ˈdʒaɪroʊz/ | n. |旋转烤肉，肉卷|A gyrose is a type of sandwich that contains meat (usually lamb, pork, chicken, or beef) that has been cooked on a vertical rotisserie.  It is typically served in pita bread with vegetables such as tomatoes, onions, and lettuce, and often with a sauce like tzatziki. |Example: I ordered a gyrose with extra tzatziki sauce. 我点了一个加了额外酸奶酱的肉卷。  |近义词：gyro, Greek sandwich|反义词：无
