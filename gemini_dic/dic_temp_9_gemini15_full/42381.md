counterblast:/ˈkaʊntəblæst/| n. v.|反击；对抗；反驳|A strong attack or reply made in response to an attack or criticism.  It can refer to a strong response to something negative.|例句：The company launched a counterblast against its critics. 这家公司对批评者发起了反击。|例句：The government's new policy prompted a counterblast from the opposition. 政府的新政策引发了反对派的强烈反驳。|近义词：retaliation, counterattack, rejoinder|反义词：surrender, concession, acquiescence|名词，动词
