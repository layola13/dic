license:/ˈlaɪs(ə)ns/| n. v.|许可证；执照；许可；授权 |A license is an official document that gives you permission to do something.  It can refer to a permit to drive a car, practice a profession, or use copyrighted material. |例句：He has a driver's license. 他有驾照。

She needs a license to practice law. 她需要执照才能执业律师。|近义词：permit, authorization, permit, certificate |反义词：prohibition, ban


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He licenses his software to many companies. = 他将他的软件授权给许多公司。
The city licenses street vendors. = 这个城市向街头小贩颁发许可证。


一般过去时:
They licensed the song for use in the film. = 他们获得了这首歌在电影中使用的许可。
The government licensed the new drug. = 政府批准了这种新药。


现在完成时:
She has licensed her invention to a major corporation. = 她已将她的发明授权给一家大公司。
The company has licensed several new technologies. = 公司已经获得了多项新技术的许可。


过去完成时:
They had licensed the technology before the product launch. = 他们在产品发布之前就获得了这项技术的许可。
The band had licensed their music for several commercials. = 乐队已将他们的音乐授权用于多个广告。


将来时:
We will license our software to international markets. = 我们将把我们的软件授权给国际市场。
The artist will license her paintings to a gallery. = 艺术家将把她的画作授权给一家画廊。


虚拟语气:
If they licensed the technology, they would have a competitive advantage. = 如果他们获得了这项技术的许可，他们将具有竞争优势。
I wish I had licensed my invention earlier. = 我真希望我早点把我的发明授权出去。


被动语态:
The software is licensed to users worldwide. = 该软件已授权给全球用户。
The new drug was licensed by the FDA. = 这种新药已获得美国食品药品监督管理局的批准。


疑问句:
Is this software licensed for commercial use? = 这款软件是否已授权用于商业用途？
Are you licensed to operate this equipment? = 你有操作这台设备的许可证吗？


否定句:
The software is not licensed for redistribution. = 该软件未授权重新分发。
He is not licensed to drive a truck. = 他没有驾驶卡车的执照。


PHRASE->

marriage license = 结婚证


词源:
The word "license" comes from the Old French word "licence," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "licentia," meaning "permission," "liberty," or "freedom."  The Latin word is formed from "licere," meaning "to be permitted" or "to be allowed."
