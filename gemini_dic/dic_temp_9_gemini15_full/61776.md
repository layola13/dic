empower:/ɪmˈpaʊər/| vt.|授权；使能够；赋予权力|To give someone more control over their own life or work.|The program aims to empower women and girls.|近义词：authorize, enable, strengthen|反义词：disable, weaken, disempower|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company empowers its employees to make decisions. = 公司授权员工做决定。
This training empowers you to handle difficult situations. = 这项培训使你能够处理困难的情况。


一般过去时:
The revolution empowered the people. = 革命赋予了人民权力。
The new software empowered the team to work more efficiently. = 新软件使团队能够更高效地工作。


现在完成时:
The movement has empowered many marginalized communities. =  这场运动已经赋予许多边缘化社群权力。
This experience has empowered me to overcome challenges. = 这段经历使我能够克服挑战。


过去完成时:
She had empowered herself through years of hard work. = 她通过多年的努力使自己变得更有能力。
The reforms had empowered local governments to make their own decisions. = 改革已经赋予地方政府自行决策的权力。


将来时:
This technology will empower future generations. = 这项技术将赋予后代力量。
The new policy will empower small businesses to thrive. = 新政策将使小型企业蓬勃发展。


虚拟语气:
If we empowered the workers, productivity would increase. = 如果我们授权员工，生产力就会提高。
I wish the government would empower local communities to address their own issues. = 我希望政府能够授权地方社区解决自身问题。


被动语态:
Employees are empowered to take initiative. = 员工被授权采取主动。
The community was empowered by the new resources. = 社区因新的资源而得到了赋权。


疑问句:
How can we empower young people to become leaders? = 我们如何才能使年轻人成为领导者？
Does this program empower women in rural areas? = 这个项目是否赋予农村妇女权力？


否定句:
The system did not empower the employees to make changes. = 这个系统并没有授权员工进行更改。
The dictator refused to empower any opposition groups. = 独裁者拒绝赋予任何反对派权力。
