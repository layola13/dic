predetention: /ˌpriːdɪˈtɛnʃən/ | n. | 预先拘留，拘留前的阶段 | The period of time before someone is formally arrested and detained.  It often involves questioning, investigation, and potentially observation.  This term is less common than "pre-arrest" but is used in specific legal contexts. | Example: The police conducted a predetention interview with the suspect.  警方对嫌疑人进行了拘留前的问话。


The suspect's predetention was marked by a series of intense interrogations. 嫌疑人的预先拘留期间经历了一系列严厉的审问。|近义词：pre-arrest, detention before arrest |反义词：release, freedom
