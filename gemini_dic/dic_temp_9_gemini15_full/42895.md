cousin:/ˈkʌzən/| n. |堂兄弟姐妹，表兄弟姐妹|A cousin is a child of your aunt or uncle.  Cousins can be related through your mother's or father's side of the family.|My cousin is a doctor. 我的表哥是医生。|sibling, nephew, niece|parent, child
