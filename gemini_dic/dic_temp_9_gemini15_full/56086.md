dockyards:/ˈdɒkˌjɑːrdz/ | n. |船坞，船厂 |A dockyard is a place where ships are built, repaired, and maintained.  It typically includes dry docks, workshops, and storage facilities. |The Royal Navy's dockyards were crucial to Britain's naval power.  英国皇家海军的船坞对英国的海军实力至关重要。  Many skilled workers were employed at the Portsmouth dockyards.  朴茨茅斯船坞雇佣了许多熟练工人。| shipyards, naval bases |  (无明确反义词，可考虑与“unused land”, “inactive port”等词语形成对比)|名词
