gnats:/næts/| n.|小蚊子，蠓|A gnat is a very small flying insect, similar to a mosquito but usually not biting.|例句：The gnats were buzzing around my head.  小蚊子在我头上嗡嗡叫。|例句：We were plagued by gnats all evening.  整个晚上我们都被小蚊子困扰着。|近义词：midge, mosquito|反义词：None
