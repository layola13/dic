gravities:/ˈɡrævətiz/ | n. | 重力，引力，严肃性 | The force that attracts a body towards the center of the earth, or towards any other physical body having mass.  It is also used figuratively to refer to seriousness or solemnity. | 例句：The moon's gravity affects the tides. 月球的引力影响着潮汐。 The gravity of the situation was clear to everyone.  局势的严重性对每个人都很明显。 | 近义词：gravitation, seriousness, solemnity | 反义词：lightness, levity
