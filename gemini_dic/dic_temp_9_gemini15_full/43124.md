coying:/ˈkɔɪɪŋ/| v.|矫揉造作，做作，卖弄风情|To behave in an affectedly coy or flirtatious manner; to act in a way that is intended to attract attention in a playful or flirtatious way.|例句：She was coying with the handsome stranger at the bar. 她正在酒吧里跟那个英俊的陌生人卖弄风情。
例句：Stop coying and tell me what you really think. 别扭扭捏捏的了，告诉我你真正是怎么想的。|近义词：flirt, affect, feign, simper|反义词：genuine, sincere, forthright
