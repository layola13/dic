reannounced:/ˌriːəˈnaʊnst/| v.|重新宣布，再次宣布|To announce something again, often with more emphasis or to a wider audience.|例句：The company reannounced its commitment to environmental sustainability.  公司再次宣布其对环境可持续性的承诺。|例句：The government reannounced the new tax policies after the initial announcement was poorly received. 政府在最初的公告不受欢迎后再次宣布了新的税收政策。|近义词：reiterate, reaffirm, restate|反义词：withdraw, retract|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reannounce their plans every year. = 他们每年都会重新宣布他们的计划。
The company reannounces its product line regularly. = 公司定期重新发布其产品线。

一般过去时:
The mayor reannounced the city's budget cuts last week. = 市长上周再次宣布了城市的预算削减。
She reannounced her candidacy after a brief hiatus. = 在短暂中断后，她再次宣布竞选。

现在完成时:
The school has reannounced its reopening date. = 学校已经重新宣布了开学日期。
They have reannounced their support for the project. = 他们已经再次表示支持该项目。

过去完成时:
He had reannounced his intentions before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经再次宣布了他的意图。
The company had reannounced the product launch several times. = 该公司已经多次重新宣布产品发布。

将来时:
The president will reannounce the new policy next month. = 总统下个月将再次宣布这项新政策。
They will reannounce the concert dates soon. = 他们很快就会再次宣布音乐会日期。

虚拟语气:
If they reannounced the sale, more people would attend. = 如果他们再次宣布促销活动，将会有更多人参加。
I wish they had reannounced the concert earlier. = 我希望他们早点再次宣布音乐会。

被动语态:
The decision was reannounced to the public. = 该决定再次向公众宣布。
The new rules were reannounced by the authorities. = 新规被当局再次宣布。

疑问句:
Will the company reannounce its earnings report? = 公司会再次公布其收益报告吗？
Have they reannounced the changes to the schedule? = 他们是否已经重新宣布了时间表的变更？

否定句:
The company did not reannounce the layoffs. = 公司没有再次宣布裁员。
They haven't reannounced their plans for expansion yet. = 他们还没有重新宣布他们的扩张计划。
