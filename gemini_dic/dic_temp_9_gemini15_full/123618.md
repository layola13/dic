month:/mʌnθ/| /mʌnθ/ | n. |月份；一个月的时间|A month is a period of time containing approximately 30 days.  月份是一个大约包含30天的时间段。|The project will take several months to complete. 这个项目需要几个月才能完成。|year, period | day | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
This month is very busy. = 这个月非常忙碌。
January is the first month of the year. = 一月是一年的第一个月。


一般过去时:
Last month, I went on vacation. = 上个月，我去了度假。
We finished the project last month. = 我们上个月完成了项目。


现在完成时:
I haven't seen him this month. = 这个月我还没见过他。
She has worked hard this month. = 这个月她工作很努力。


过去完成时:
By the end of last month, we had finished the report. = 到上个月底，我们已经完成了报告。
They had already left by the time the month ended. = 到这个月结束时，他们已经离开了。


将来时:
Next month, I will travel to Europe. = 下个月，我将去欧洲旅行。
We will start the new project next month. = 我们下个月将启动新的项目。


虚拟语气:
If it were next month, I would have more time. = 如果是下个月，我会有很多时间。
I wish this month were over already. = 我希望这个月快点结束。


被动语态:
The meeting was scheduled for next month. = 会议安排在下个月举行。
The report will be completed by the end of the month. = 报告将在月底完成。


疑问句:
What month is it? = 现在是几月份？
Which month is your birthday in? = 你的生日是几月份？


否定句:
It wasn't a good month for business. = 这对生意来说不是一个好月份。
We didn't have much free time this month. = 这个月我们没有太多空闲时间。
