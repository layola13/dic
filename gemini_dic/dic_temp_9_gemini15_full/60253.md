effusions:/ɪˈfjuːʒənz/ | n. |感情流露；溢出；涌出 |The unrestrained outpouring of emotions or words; a sudden, copious flow of something.  |例句：Her effusions of gratitude were heartfelt.  她的感激之情发自肺腑。

例句：The volcano produced a massive effusion of lava.  火山喷出了大量的熔岩。

近义词：outpouring, overflow, outburst, gush

反义词：restraint, repression, suppression


词性：名词
