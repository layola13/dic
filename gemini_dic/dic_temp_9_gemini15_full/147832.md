overpunish: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃ/ | vt. |过度惩罚；严惩| To punish someone more severely than is necessary or fair.  |例句：The judge was criticized for overpunishing the young offender.  法官因过度惩罚年轻罪犯而受到批评。


The school's zero-tolerance policy led to many students being overpunished for minor infractions. 学校的零容忍政策导致许多学生因轻微违规而受到过度惩罚。|近义词：punish excessively, overdiscipline, excessively penalize |反义词：underpunish, pardon, forgive |动词
