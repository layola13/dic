injury:/ˈɪndʒəri/| n. |伤害，损伤 |Physical harm or damage to someone's body.  Injuries can range from minor bruises to severe fractures. |例句：He sustained a serious injury to his leg in the accident. 他在事故中腿部受了重伤。|例句：The fall caused a minor injury to her arm.  摔倒导致她手臂轻微受伤。|近义词：harm, damage, hurt, wound |反义词：healing, recovery


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Injuries often occur during sports. = 损伤经常发生在运动中。
The wound causes him constant injury. = 伤口给他造成持续的伤害。


一般过去时:
He suffered a serious injury last year. = 他去年受了重伤。
The accident caused multiple injuries. = 那次事故造成了多处损伤。


现在完成时:
She has recovered from her injuries. = 她已经从伤病中恢复过来了。
He has sustained several injuries throughout his career. = 他在职业生涯中遭受过多次伤病。


过去完成时:

将来时:
He will likely require surgery for his injury. = 他很可能需要手术治疗他的伤。
The doctors believe his injury will heal within weeks. = 医生相信他的伤会在几周内痊愈。


虚拟语气:
