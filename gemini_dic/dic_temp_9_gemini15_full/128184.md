neuralgias:/nʊˈrældʒəz/ | n. |神经痛 | Neuralgias are intense, sharp pains that arise from the nerves.  They can occur in various parts of the body and are often caused by nerve damage or irritation.  | Example:  She suffered from chronic neuralgias in her face. 她患有慢性面部神经痛。

The patient complained of severe neuralgias in his leg after the accident. 事故后，病人抱怨腿部剧烈的神经痛。|近义词：nerve pain, neuralgic pain | 反义词：numbness, analgesia |  名词
