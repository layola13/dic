incisors: /ɪnˈsaɪzərz/ | n. | 门齿 |  The incisors are the four front teeth in each jaw, used for cutting food.  They are characterized by their sharp, chisel-like edges. | Example:  Rabbits have very prominent incisors that grow continuously.  兔子有非常突出的门齿，它们会不断生长。

The child lost one of his incisors while playing.  孩子玩耍时掉了一颗门齿。|  近义词: front teeth, cutting teeth | 反义词: molars, premolars
