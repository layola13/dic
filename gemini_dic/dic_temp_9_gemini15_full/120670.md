misadapting: /ˌmɪsæˈdæptɪŋ/ | v. | 适应不良；错误适应 |  The process of adapting in a way that is harmful or ineffective; failing to adapt successfully to a new environment or situation.  | 例句：The plant species is misadapting to the changing climate, showing signs of stress and reduced growth.  这种植物物种正在不适应变化的气候，表现出压力和生长减少的迹象。


The company's misadapting to the market shifts resulted in significant financial losses. 公司未能适应市场变化导致了巨大的经济损失。| 近义词：maladapting, failing to adapt | 反义词：adapting, adjusting, thriving
