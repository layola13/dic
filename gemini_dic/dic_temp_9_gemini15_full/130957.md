nonassociation: /ˌnɒnəˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 非关联；不相关 | The lack of connection or association between things.  It refers to a state or condition where there is no link, relationship, or connection between entities or concepts.  This can be applied to various fields, including statistics, psychology, and sociology.  | Example: The study demonstrated a nonassociation between smoking and the disease.  这项研究表明吸烟与该疾病之间没有关联。 |近义词:dissociation, disconnect, unrelatedness | 反义词:association, correlation, connection |  名词
