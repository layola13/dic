puddliest:/ˈpʌdlist/| adj.|最泥泞的，最水洼的|Most full of puddles; extremely muddy.  The word is the superlative form of "puddly".|例句：After the storm, the streets were the puddliest I've ever seen.  暴风雨过后，街道是我见过的最泥泞的。The puddliest path led to the farmer's house. 最泥泞的小路通向农民的房子。|近义词：muddiest, sloppiest, boggiest|反义词：driest, cleanest
