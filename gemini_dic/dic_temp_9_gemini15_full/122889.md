mollycoddle:/ˈmɒlikɒdl/| /ˈmɑːlikoʊdl/| vt.|娇惯；溺爱；过分关怀|To treat someone in an excessively indulgent or pampering way.  |例句：Don't mollycoddle him; he needs to learn to stand on his own two feet.  别太娇惯他；他需要学会独立。
例句：She mollycoddled her son, and he grew up spoiled and entitled. 她过分溺爱儿子，结果他长大后变得骄纵自私。|近义词：pamper, spoil, coddle, indulge|反义词：neglect, ignore, discipline|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mollycoddle their children. = 他们娇惯他们的孩子。
He mollycoddles his employees. = 他过分迁就他的员工。

一般过去时:
She mollycoddled her grandchild. = 她娇惯她的孙子。
My parents mollycoddled me when I was young. = 我小时候父母很溺爱我。

现在完成时:
They have mollycoddled their son for too long. = 他们娇惯儿子太久了。
She has mollycoddled him since he was a baby. = 她从他还是婴儿的时候就开始溺爱他了。

过去完成时:
