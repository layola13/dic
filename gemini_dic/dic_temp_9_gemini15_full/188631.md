ruminatingly:/ˈruːmɪneɪtɪŋli/| adv.|沉思地，反刍地 |  In a thoughtful and often pensive manner; as if deeply considering something.  The word implies a slow, deliberate process of thought, often involving going over things repeatedly. | 例句：He stared ruminatingly out the window, lost in thought. 他沉思地望着窗外，陷入沉思。

例句：She sat ruminatingly by the fire, reviewing the events of the day. 她坐在火炉旁，沉思地回顾着当天发生的事情。


近义词：thoughtfully, contemplatively, reflectively, pensively

反义词：rashly, impulsively, hastily

词性：副词
