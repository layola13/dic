looks:/lʊks/ |/lʊks/| v. n.| 看起来；外观；相貌 | To appear to be a certain way; the appearance of something or someone. | 例句：He looks tired. 他看起来很累。| 例句：The house looks beautiful. 这房子看起来很漂亮。|近义词：appears, seems, resembles|反义词: N/A |动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He looks happy today. = 他今天看起来很开心。
The flowers look beautiful in the garden. = 花园里的花看起来很漂亮。


一般过去时:
She looked sad yesterday. = 她昨天看起来很悲伤。
The building looked old and dilapidated. = 那栋建筑看起来又旧又破败。


现在完成时:
He has looked unwell for several days. = 他好几天看起来都不舒服。
The situation has looked grim since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，情况看起来很严峻。


过去完成时:
She had looked much younger before the illness. = 她生病之前看起来年轻得多。
The evidence had looked promising before the new information emerged. = 在新信息出现之前，证据看起来很有希望。


将来时:
The project will look promising once we secure funding. = 一旦我们获得资金，项目的前景就会一片光明。
He will look different after the surgery. = 手术后他会看起来不一样。


虚拟语气:
If he looked healthier, he would feel better. = 如果他看起来更健康，他会感觉更好。
I wish my apartment looked more modern. = 我希望我的公寓看起来更现代化。


被动语态:
The painting was looked at by many people. = 这幅画被很多人观赏过。
The problem is looked into by the experts. = 专家正在调查这个问题。


疑问句:
How does she look today? = 她今天看起来怎么样？
Does he look familiar to you? = 他看起来你眼熟吗？


否定句:
He doesn't look well. = 他看起来不太好。
The report doesn't look convincing. = 这份报告看起来不令人信服。


PHRASE->
look after = 照顾
