contravene:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːn/ | vt. |违反，违背；与…相抵触 | To contravene something means to act in a way that breaks a law, rule, or agreement.  | 例句：His actions contravened company policy. = 他的行为违反了公司政策。

His actions contravened the law. = 他的行为违反了法律。


近义词：violate, infringe, breach, defy, transgress | 反义词：obey, comply, conform


词源：来自拉丁语 *contravenire，由 contra- ("against") 和 venire ("to come") 组成，字面意思是“来反对”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company's actions contravene international law. = 该公司的行为违反了国际法。
His behavior contravenes the school's code of conduct. = 他的行为违反了学校的行为准则。

一般过去时:
The driver contravened the traffic regulations. = 司机违反了交通规则。
They contravened the terms of the contract. = 他们违反了合同条款。

现在完成时:
He has contravened the rules on several occasions. = 他已经多次违反了规定。
The government has contravened the agreement reached last year. = 政府违反了去年达成的协议。

过去完成时:
They had contravened the agreement before the dispute arose. = 在争议出现之前，他们就已经违反了协议。
She had contravened company policy numerous times. = 她已经多次违反了公司政策。

将来时:
He will contravene the law if he continues this behavior. = 如果他继续这种行为，他将违反法律。
The new policy may contravene existing regulations. = 新政策可能会违反现行法规。

虚拟语气:
If he were to contravene the rules, he would face disciplinary action. = 如果他违反规则，他将面临纪律处分。
It is vital that he does not contravene the terms of the contract. = 至关重要的是，他不应违反合同条款。

被动语态:
Company policy was contravened by his actions. = 他的行为违反了公司政策。
The law has been contravened by many individuals. = 许多个人都违反了法律。

疑问句:
Does this action contravene any existing laws? = 这一行为是否违反了任何现行法律？
Does his behaviour contravene company policy? = 他的行为是否违反了公司政策？

否定句:
His actions did not contravene any regulations. = 他的行为没有违反任何规章。
The company claims its actions did not contravene the agreement. = 公司声称其行为没有违反协议。
