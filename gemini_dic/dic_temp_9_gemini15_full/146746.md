overhumanity: /ˌoʊvərhjuːˈmænɪti/ | n. | 超人类性，超越人类的特性 |  The state or quality of being beyond human capabilities or limitations; often refers to advanced technological or intellectual capabilities exceeding human norms.  It can also imply a loss of essential human qualities in pursuit of these advancements. | 例句：The pursuit of artificial intelligence raises concerns about overhumanity and the potential displacement of human agency.  超越人类能力的人工智能追求引发了对超人类性和潜在的人为代理置换的担忧。


The relentless pursuit of technological advancement sometimes leads to an unsettling sense of overhumanity, where human connection is sacrificed for efficiency.  对技术进步的无情追求有时会导致一种令人不安的超人类感，在这种感觉中，人际关系被牺牲以提高效率。


近义词：transhumanism, superhumanity, posthumanism | 反义词：humanity, subhumanity
