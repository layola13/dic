nonprogrammatic: /ˌnɒnˌproʊɡræˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非程序的，非编程的 | Not relating to or involving computer programming or a systematic plan or procedure.  It implies a lack of structure, spontaneity, or improvisation. | 例句：The artist's approach to the project was nonprogrammatic, allowing for improvisation and creative exploration.  艺术家对这个项目的方法是非程序化的，允许即兴发挥和创造性探索。

The discovery was completely nonprogrammatic; it happened by chance.  这项发现完全是非程序化的；它是偶然发生的。


近义词：unprogrammed, unscripted, impromptu, spontaneous, unplanned, ad hoc | 反义词：programmed, planned, systematic, structured
