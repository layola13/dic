pledgery: /ˈplɛdʒəri/ | n. | 抵押；保证；誓言 | The act of pledging or giving a pledge; something pledged.  It refers to the act of making a solemn promise or commitment, often involving a pledge of something of value as security.  | Example: The pledgery of the land secured the loan.  抵押土地担保了贷款。 |近义词：promise, guarantee, assurance, vow |反义词：breach, violation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes a pledgery of support to the charity. = 公司承诺支持该慈善机构。
He gives a pledgery of allegiance to the king. = 他向国王宣誓效忠。


一般过去时:
They made a pledgery to donate a significant sum. = 他们承诺捐赠一大笔款项。
She gave a pledgery of secrecy.  = 她保证保守秘密。


现在完成时:
The government has made a pledgery to reduce emissions. = 政府已承诺减少排放。
He has given a pledgery to repay the debt. = 他已保证偿还债务。


过去完成时:
They had made a pledgery to finish the project by the deadline. = 他们之前已经承诺在截止日期前完成项目。
She had given a pledgery to keep the information confidential. = 她之前已经保证将信息保密。


将来时:
The organization will make a pledgery to help the victims. = 该组织将承诺帮助受害者。
He will give a pledgery to complete the task. = 他将保证完成任务。


虚拟语气:
If he had made a pledgery, he would have kept it. = 如果他做出了承诺，他就会遵守。
I wish I had given a more firm pledgery. = 我希望我当初能做出更坚定的承诺。


被动语态:
A pledgery of support was made by the community. = 社区做出了支持的承诺。
A pledgery of confidentiality was given to the witness. = 向证人保证了保密。


疑问句:
Was a pledgery made to the injured party? = 是否对受伤方做出了承诺？
Has he given a pledgery of his loyalty? = 他是否保证过他的忠诚？


否定句:
He didn't make a pledgery to attend the meeting. = 他没有承诺参加会议。
She didn't give a pledgery to reveal the truth. = 她没有保证会说出真相。
