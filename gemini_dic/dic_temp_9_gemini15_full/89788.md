It refers to the orientation of something being parallel to the horizon, often used in geology, surveying, and other fields dealing with spatial orientation. | Example: The horizontality of the sedimentary layers indicated a period of quiet deposition.  沉积层的水平性表明了一段平静的沉积时期。 The horizontality of the table top was crucial for the stability of the experiment. 桌面的水平性对于实验的稳定性至关重要。|近义词：levelness, flatness | 反义词：verticality, inclination
