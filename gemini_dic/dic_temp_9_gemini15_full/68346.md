fall-to:/fɔːl tuː/| phrasal verb|开始做;动手;突然开始|To begin doing something suddenly or unexpectedly, often with enthusiasm.  It implies a spontaneous start, often after a period of inactivity or hesitation.|例句：After a long break, he finally fell to the task of writing his novel.  长时间休息后，他终于开始着手写他的小说了。

例句：The children fell to their toys the moment they entered the playroom. 孩子们一进游戏室就玩起了玩具。


近义词：begin, commence, start, embark on|反义词：cease, stop, discontinue|词性: phrasal verb
