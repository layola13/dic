crenelle:/krəˈnɛl/ | n. | 雉堞；垛口 | A battlement, usually of a castle wall, with regularly spaced openings or indentations.  It is a defensive architectural feature designed to provide cover for archers and other defenders while allowing them to fire upon attackers.  | Example: The medieval castle was protected by high walls with crenellations. 中世纪城堡由带有雉堞的高墙保护着。 | 近义词：battlement, embrasure | 反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be a smooth, unbroken wall. | 名词
