piggy-back:/ˈpɪɡiˌbæk/| n. v.| piggyback; 搭便车；背负；|英文解析：To ride on someone's back; to carry someone on one's back.  Also used figuratively to describe adding something onto something else that already exists. |例句：The child piggybacked on his father's shoulders.  孩子骑在父亲的肩膀上。 The new feature was piggybacked onto the existing software. 新功能被添加到现有的软件中。|近义词：carry, hitchhike (for the riding aspect), add on (for the figurative aspect) |反义词：walk alone, remove
