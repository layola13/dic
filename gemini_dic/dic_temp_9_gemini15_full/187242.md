robinia:/rəˈbɪniə/| n. |刺槐属植物；刺槐|A genus of deciduous trees and shrubs in the pea family, Fabaceae, known for their fragrant flowers and thorny branches.  They are native to North America and are widely cultivated for their ornamental value and timber. |The robinia tree is known for its beautiful flowers. 刺槐树以其美丽的花朵而闻名。|  The locust tree is a type of robinia.  刺槐树是一种刺槐属植物。| acacia, locust tree |  (No direct antonym, as it refers to a genus)| 名词
