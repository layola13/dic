earbuds:/ˈɪərbʌdz/ | n. | 耳塞；耳塞式耳机 | Small speakers that fit inside or over the ears, typically used with portable electronic devices.  | Example: I listen to music on my earbuds every morning. 我每天早上都戴着耳机听音乐。 |近义词: earphones, earpieces | 反义词:  headphones (generally larger)


USAGE->

I use earbuds to listen to podcasts. = 我用耳塞听播客。
He wears earbuds while commuting. = 他通勤时戴着耳机。

一般过去时:
She lost her earbuds yesterday. = 她昨天丢了耳机。

现在完成时:
