scanted:/ˈskæntɪd/| adj.| 吝啬的；不足的；缺乏的 |  Giving or receiving less than is needed or deserved; meager; insufficient.  |例句：The workers felt scanted in their wages. 工人们觉得他们的工资太低了。

例句：He gave a scanted explanation of the accident. 他对事故的解释语焉不详。


近义词： meager, insufficient, paltry, skimpy, short, inadequate | 反义词： abundant, plentiful, ample, sufficient


词源:源自古英语 *sċēansian “减少，削减”，源自原始日耳曼语 *skaun- “斜切，倾斜”，源自原始印欧语根 *skeu- “斜切”。
