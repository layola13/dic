schooler:/ˈskuːlər/| n. |小学生；学生；上学的人|A schooler is a pupil attending school, especially a young child.  The term often implies a child in primary or elementary school.|Example:  The schooler excitedly showed his mother his drawing.|近义词：pupil, student, scholar|反义词：graduate, adult
