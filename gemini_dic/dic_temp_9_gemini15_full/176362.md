re-filtering:/ˌriːˈfɪltərɪŋ/| v. |重新过滤；再次过滤 |The process of filtering something again, typically to remove further impurities or unwanted elements.  It often implies a prior filtering process already took place. |例句：The water needs re-filtering after passing through the initial filter. 这水经过最初的过滤后还需要再次过滤。

例句：We decided on re-filtering the coffee to remove the sediment. 我们决定再次过滤咖啡以去除沉淀物。


近义词：refining, purifying, sieving, straining


反义词：contaminating, polluting


词性：动词
