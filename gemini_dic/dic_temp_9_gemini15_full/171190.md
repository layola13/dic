psychometry:/saɪˈkɒmətri/ | n. |心灵测量学；心灵感应；超心理学 | The study of alleged psychic ability to gain information about an object or person by touching it.  It involves the purported ability to perceive information about an object or person by touching it. This information may include details about the object's history, its previous owners, or events associated with it. Psychometry is considered a pseudoscience, lacking empirical evidence to support its claims. | Example:  The psychometrist claimed to have discovered the identity of the murderer by psychometrically examining a bloody knife. 心灵测量师声称通过心灵测量检查一把带血的刀子发现了凶手的身份。 |近义词：psychic ability, extrasensory perception (ESP) | 反义词：N/A
