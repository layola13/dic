miss-paces:/ˌmɪsˈpeɪsɪz/| v.|步调不一致，行动失误|To make mistakes in one's actions or plans; to misjudge the pace or timing of something.|例句：He miss-paced his entire campaign, alienating key supporters early on. 他在整个竞选中都步调不一致，早期就疏远了关键支持者。|例句：The company miss-paced its product launch, resulting in disappointing sales. 公司产品发布的时机把握不准，导致销售业绩令人失望。|近义词：miscalculate, misjudge, blunder, err|反义词：time perfectly, plan effectively
