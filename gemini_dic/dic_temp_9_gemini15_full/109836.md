lexicologically:/ˌlɛksɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 词汇学上地；从词汇学的角度 |  Adverb describing something done or considered from the perspective of lexicology (the study of the vocabulary of a language). It indicates an approach or analysis that focuses on the words themselves, their meanings, origins, and relationships within a language. |  The word was analyzed lexicologically to trace its etymology.  这个词从词汇学的角度被分析以追溯其词源。 |  semantically, etymologically |  (no direct antonym, but opposites would be approaches like syntactically or pragmatically) | adv.
