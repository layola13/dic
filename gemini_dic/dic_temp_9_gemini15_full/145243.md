overblamed:/ˌoʊvərˈbleɪmd/| adj.| 被过度责备的 |  The word "overblamed" describes someone or something that has received more blame than is deserved or warranted. It implies an unfair or excessive amount of criticism or responsibility. | Example: He felt overblamed for the project's failure, as others also contributed to the shortcomings. 他觉得自己为项目的失败承担了过多的责任，因为其他人也为此负有责任。 | 近义词：unfairly blamed, unjustly accused, scapegoated | 反义词：underblamed, exonerated
