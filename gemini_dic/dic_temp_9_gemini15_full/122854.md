mollah:/ˈmɒlə/| n.|毛拉，伊斯兰教神职人员|A mollah is a Muslim religious leader, usually a learned scholar of Islamic law and theology, often with authority in a local community.  They may lead prayers, give religious instruction, and offer legal opinions based on Islamic law (Sharia).|例句：The village mollah gave a sermon on the importance of charity. 村里的毛拉作了关于慈善重要性的布道。|例句：Many mollahs opposed the modernization of the country. 许多毛拉反对国家的现代化。|近义词：imam, cleric, mullah (alternative spelling)|反义词：layperson
