cameras:/ˈkæmərəz/ | n. |照相机，摄像机 |A camera is a device for recording visual images, either as still photographs or as moving images (video).  Cameras work by using a lens to focus light onto a light-sensitive surface (film or a digital sensor).| 例句：The photographer used a professional camera to capture the stunning landscape. 摄影师用专业的相机捕捉了令人惊叹的风景。

例句：Security cameras are installed throughout the building to monitor activity.  整个建筑物都安装了安全摄像头来监控活动。


近义词：photographic equipment, recorders, video cameras | 反义词：N/A
