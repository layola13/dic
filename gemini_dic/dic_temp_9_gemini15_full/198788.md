sicko:/ˈsɪkoʊ/| n.|精神病患者，变态狂|A sicko is a slang term for someone considered to be mentally disturbed or perverse, often exhibiting cruel or unusual behavior.  The term carries a strong negative connotation and is often used informally and judgmentally.|例句：He's a real sicko for doing that to the animals. 他对动物做出那样的事，真是个变态。|近义词：psychopath, pervert, maniac, weirdo|反义词：sane, normal, well-adjusted|名词
