saliva:/səˈlaɪvə/| n. |唾液|A clear, viscous fluid secreted into the mouth by glands, aiding in the digestion of food.  Saliva lubricates the mouth and helps to prevent tooth decay.|例句：The dog drooled saliva all over the floor. 这条狗把唾液流了一地。|例句：Excessive saliva production can be a symptom of certain medical conditions. 过度分泌唾液可能是某些疾病的症状。|近义词：spittle|反义词：dryness
