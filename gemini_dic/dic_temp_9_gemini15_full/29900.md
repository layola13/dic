cauterizing:/ˈkɔːtəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 烧灼；止血 | The act of burning tissue to destroy it, typically to stop bleeding or prevent infection.  | Example: The surgeon cauterized the wound to stop the bleeding. 外科医生烧灼伤口以止血。 |近义词：searing, burning, scarifying | 反义词：healing
