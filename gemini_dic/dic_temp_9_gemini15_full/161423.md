posturist: /ˈpɒstʃʊrɪst/ | n. | 姿势矫正师，姿势专家 | A posturist is a healthcare professional who specializes in posture assessment, correction, and improvement. They work to identify and address postural imbalances that can lead to pain, discomfort, and other health problems.  | Example: The posturist examined her posture and recommended exercises to strengthen her core muscles. 姿势矫正师检查了她的姿势，并建议进行核心肌群强化练习。 | 近义词：posture therapist, posture specialist | 反义词：无 |  词性：名词
