internal: /ɪnˈtɜːrnəl/ | adj. | 内部的；内部的；内在的 | Relating to or situated on the inside of something; inner.  | 例句：The internal structure of the building is very complex. 这座建筑物的内部结构非常复杂。

例句：He suffered internal injuries in the accident. 他在事故中受了内伤。


近义词：inner, inward, intrinsic | 反义词：external, outer, extrinsic | 词性: adjective
