dominates:/ˈdɒmɪneɪts/ /ˈdɑːmɪneɪts/| vt.|支配；统治；控制；主导；突出；占据显著地位|To have control over a place or person.|The company dominates the market. 这家公司主导市场。|近义词：control, rule, govern, lead, overshadow|反义词：submit to, yield to, obey


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dominates the conversation. = 他主导着谈话。
The mountain dominates the landscape. = 这座山在景观中占据主导地位。

一般过去时:
She dominated the chess game. = 她赢得了国际象棋比赛。
The empire dominated the region for centuries. = 这个帝国统治了该地区几个世纪。

现在完成时:
This company has dominated the industry for years. = 这家公司多年来一直主导着这个行业。
His ambition has dominated his life. = 他的野心主宰了他的生活。

过去完成时:
The team had dominated the game until the last minute. = 直到最后一刻，球队一直占据主导地位。
Before the merger, the smaller company had dominated the local market. = 在合并之前，这家规模较小的公司主导着当地市场。

将来时:
They will dominate the market with their new product. = 他们将凭借他们的新产品主导市场。
It is predicted that AI will dominate many aspects of our lives in the future. = 预计人工智能将在未来主导我们生活的许多方面。

虚拟语气:
If he were to dominate the field, he would have to work much harder. = 如果他要主导这个领域，他就必须更加努力地工作。
I wish I could dominate my fears. = 我希望我能战胜我的恐惧。

被动语态:
The game was dominated by the visiting team. = 比赛被客队控制。
The discussion was dominated by a few outspoken individuals. = 讨论被少数直言不讳的人主导。

疑问句:
Does one country dominate the global economy? = 一个国家是否主导全球经济？
Which team dominated the match? = 哪支球队在比赛中占据主导地位？

否定句:
He doesn't dominate the conversation anymore. = 他不再主导谈话了。
Smaller businesses are not able to dominate the market against large corporations. = 小型企业无法与大型企业竞争以主导市场。


PHRASE->
dominate the conversation = 主导谈话

dominate the news = 成为新闻焦点
dominate someone's life = 左右某人的生活
