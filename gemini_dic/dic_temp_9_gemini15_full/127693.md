negatives:/ˈneɡətɪvz/ | n. |否定词；负数；底片；负面因素 |Negatives are words or phrases that express the opposite of something. They can be used to express disagreement, refusal, or the absence of something.  Negatives can also refer to photographic negatives or negative numbers in mathematics. |例句：He answered my question with a negative. 他用否定的回答来回答我的问题。  |例句：The negatives of the film were damaged. 胶卷的底片被损坏了。|近义词：opposites, denials, negatives (photographic) |反义词：positives, affirmations
