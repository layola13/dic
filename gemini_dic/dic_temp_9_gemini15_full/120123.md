mimosa:/mɪˈmoʊzə/| n.|含羞草；含羞草属植物；金合欢；米莫萨酒|A mimosa is a genus of flowering plants in the pea family, known for their sensitive leaves that fold up when touched.  The name is also used for a cocktail made with champagne and orange juice.|例句：The mimosa tree’s delicate pink blossoms were a beautiful sight. 含羞草树上娇嫩的粉色花朵美极了。

The mimosa cocktail is a popular brunch drink.  米莫萨鸡尾酒是一种受欢迎的早午餐饮品。|近义词：sensitive plant (for the plant), champagne cocktail (for the drink)|反义词：N/A|词性 n.
