misdefining:/ˌmɪsdiˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | v. |错误定义，曲解 |To give a wrong or inaccurate definition of something.  It involves presenting a concept, term, or idea in a way that is misleading or untrue. |The professor was criticized for misdefining key terms in his lecture. 教授因在讲座中错误定义关键术语而受到批评。|Defining, explaining, clarifying |Correctly defining, accurately explaining |动词
