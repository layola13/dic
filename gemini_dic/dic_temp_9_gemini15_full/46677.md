dampers:/ˈdæmpərz/| n.|减震器；阻尼器；抑制器|A damper is a device that reduces or prevents the transmission of sound, vibration, or other forms of energy.  It can also refer to something that suppresses or restrains something else.|例句：The car's shock dampers were worn out. 这车的减震器磨损了。

The government introduced new dampers on economic growth. 政府出台了抑制经济增长的措施。|近义词：suppressor, restrictor, check|反义词：accelerator, stimulator|名词
