palpation:/pælˈpeɪʃən/| n.|触诊，触察|The act of examining something by touch, especially a part of the body.  It's often used in medical examinations to assess the size, texture, and consistency of organs or tissues.|例句：The doctor performed a palpation of the abdomen to check for any abnormalities.医生进行了腹部触诊以检查是否有任何异常。

例句：Through careful palpation, the nurse detected a lump in the patient's breast. 通过仔细的触诊，护士在病人的乳房中发现了一个肿块。

近义词：examination, feeling, touching|反义词：observation (visual examination)|词性：名词
