refamiliarizes:/ˌriːfəˈmɪljəriˌzaɪz/| vt.|使重新熟悉；使再熟悉|To make someone or something familiar again; to reintroduce someone or something to a previously known situation or context.|例句：The museum's new exhibit refamiliarizes visitors with the artist's early works.  博物馆的新展览使参观者重新熟悉了这位艺术家的早期作品。

例句：The teacher refamiliarized the students with the grammatical rules before the test. 老师在考试前让学生们重新熟悉语法规则。

近义词： reacquaint, reintroduce, remind


反义词： estrange, alienate


词性:动词
