mesmerizability: /ˌmɛzmərɪˈzeɪbɪləti/ | n. | 可催眠性，易受催眠的程度 | The capacity or susceptibility to being hypnotized.  It refers to the degree to which an individual is responsive to hypnotic suggestion.  This can vary greatly from person to person. | Example:  The study assessed the participants' mesmerizability before beginning the hypnotic induction. 这项研究在开始催眠诱导之前评估了参与者的可催眠性。


近义词: hypnotizability, suggestibility | 反义词:  resistance to hypnosis,  insusceptibility to hypnosis | 词性: 名词
