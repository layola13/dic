outstealing: /ˌaʊtˈstiːlɪŋ/ | v. | 偷窃；擅自拿走 | The act of stealing something from someone else, especially in a sneaky or underhanded way.  It often implies a more deliberate and planned act than simple theft. |例句：The company accused its former employee of outstealing trade secrets.  这家公司指控其前雇员盗窃商业机密。


例句：He was caught outstealing supplies from the warehouse. 他被抓到从仓库偷东西。


近义词：theft, larceny, pilfering, embezzlement, purloining


反义词：giving, donating, contributing


词性：动词
