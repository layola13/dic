ingot:/ˈɪŋɡət/| n. |金块，锭；铸块 | A mass of metal, typically a precious metal, cast into a bar.  Ingots are typically used as a convenient form for storing, transporting, and processing metals. |例句：The gold ingots were carefully stacked in the vault. 金条小心地堆放在金库里。|例句：The refinery produces aluminum ingots for various industrial applications.  炼油厂生产用于各种工业用途的铝锭。|近义词：bar, lump, nugget |反义词:powder, dust
