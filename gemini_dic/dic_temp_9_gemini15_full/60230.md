effortlessness:/ˈɛfərtləsnəs/ | n. |轻松，毫不费力 |The state or quality of being effortless; ease; facility.  It suggests a lack of apparent effort or difficulty in accomplishing something. |例句：She played the piano with effortless grace. 她以轻松优雅的姿态弹钢琴。

例句：The dancer moved with effortless fluidity.  这位舞者动作流畅自如。


近义词：ease, fluency, smoothness, facility, grace |反义词：difficulty, struggle, labor, toil, strain
