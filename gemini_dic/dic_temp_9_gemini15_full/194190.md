self-killing:/ˈsɛlfˌkɪlɪŋ/ | n. |自杀；自我毁灭 |The act of intentionally taking one's own life.  It often refers to suicide but can also describe actions that lead to the destruction of something. | Example:  His self-killing behavior was a symptom of his deep depression.  他的自杀行为是他严重抑郁的症状。 The company's self-killing marketing strategy led to its downfall. 公司自我毁灭的营销策略导致了它的垮台。|近义词：suicide, self-destruction, self-murder |反义词：self-preservation, survival |名词
