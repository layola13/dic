catty-cornered:/ˈkætiˈkɔːrnərd/| adj.|斜对角的，成对角线的|Located diagonally across from something else.  The term often refers to buildings or streets positioned at an angle rather than directly opposite each other.|例句：The library is catty-cornered from the post office. 图书馆与邮局斜对着。
The houses are situated catty-cornered to each other.  这些房子斜对着彼此。|近义词：diagonal, oblique, askew|反义词：directly opposite, adjacent
