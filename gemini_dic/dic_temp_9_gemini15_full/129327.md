noise's:/nɔɪzɪz/| n.|噪音的；噪声的 |The possessive form of "noise," indicating ownership or relationship.  It shows that something belongs to or is associated with noise. |Example: The noise's volume was unbearable.  噪音的音量令人难以忍受。|近义词: sound's, clamor's |反义词: silence's


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It follows the rules of possessive nouns.  The usage examples below demonstrate the possessive case.

Possessive Case:
The noise's source was traced to the construction site. = 噪音的来源被追溯到建筑工地。
The intensity of the noise's impact was significant. = 噪音影响的强度是显著的。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "noise's".  The possessive form is used within phrases describing the characteristics or origin of noise, as seen in the examples above.  For example:  "the noise's effect on hearing", "the source of the noise's origin", etc.
