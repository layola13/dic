disfavouring: /dɪsˈfeɪvərɪŋ/ | v. | 不赞成；反对；不利于 |  To regard with disfavor; to oppose or be against something; to be unfavorable to something. |  The government's policies are disfavouring small businesses.  政府的政策不利于小型企业。 The committee is disfavouring the proposal. 委员会反对这项提案。 |近义词：opposing, disapproving, hindering | 反义词：favouring, supporting, promoting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is disfavouring the new marketing strategy. = 公司不赞成新的营销策略。
He is disfavouring the use of chemical fertilizers. = 他反对使用化肥。


一般过去时:
The judge disfavoured the defendant's argument. = 法官不赞成被告的辩论。
They disfavoured the idea of expanding the business. = 他们反对扩大业务的想法。


现在完成时:
The public has disfavoured the politician's recent actions. = 公众对这位政治家最近的行为表示不满。
The board has disfavoured the proposed merger. = 董事会否决了拟议中的合并。


过去完成时:
The committee had disfavoured the project before the funding was even considered. = 在考虑资金之前，委员会就已经否决了该项目。
They had disfavoured the plan due to its high cost. = 由于成本高昂，他们之前就反对了这个计划。


将来时:
The council will disfavour any proposal that increases taxes. = 理事会将反对任何增加税收的提案。
We believe they will disfavour our application. = 我们相信他们会反对我们的申请。


虚拟语气:
If the government disfavoured this policy, there would be public outcry. = 如果政府反对这项政策，将会引起公众的强烈抗议。
I wish they hadn't disfavoured my suggestion. = 我真希望他们没有反对我的建议。


被动语态:
The proposal was disfavoured by the board. = 该提案遭到董事会的反对。
His ideas were disfavoured by the majority. = 他的想法遭到大多数人的反对。


疑问句:
Does the committee disfavour the amendment? = 委员会是否反对这项修正案？
Why is this approach disfavoured by scientists? = 为什么科学家反对这种方法？


否定句:
The director did not disfavour the initiative. = 主任并不反对这项倡议。
They did not disfavour the candidate's qualifications. = 他们并不反对这位候选人的资格。
