reconnected:/ˌriːkəˈnɛktɪd/| v. adj.|重新连接，重新联系|  The past participle of "reconnect," indicating a renewed connection or relationship. It can describe the restoration of a physical connection (like a wire) or a re-establishment of a personal relationship.|例句：After years of silence, they reconnected at a reunion. 多年沉默后，他们在重聚时重新联系了。

例句：The technician reconnected the internet cable, restoring service. 技术人员重新连接了互联网电缆，恢复了服务。


近义词：rejoined, re-established, reunited|反义词：disconnected, severed, estranged|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reconnect regularly. = 他们定期重新联系。
We reconnect the wires after each test. = 我们每次测试后都会重新连接电线。


一般过去时:
They reconnected after many years. = 他们多年后重新联系了。
The engineer reconnected the power supply. = 工程师重新连接了电源。


现在完成时:
We have reconnected with old friends through social media. = 我们通过社交媒体与老朋友重新联系了。
They have reconnected the server to the network.  = 他们已将服务器重新连接到网络。


过去完成时:
I had reconnected the phone line before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经重新连接了电话线。
She had reconnected with her childhood friend before the trip. = 她旅行前已经和童年朋友重新联系了。


将来时:
We will reconnect the equipment tomorrow. = 我们明天将重新连接设备。
They will reconnect after the conference. = 会议结束后他们将重新联系。


虚拟语气:
If only we could reconnect with our lost youth. = 如果我们能找回逝去的青春就好了。
If we reconnected the system now, it would work properly. = 如果我们现在重新连接系统，它就能正常工作了。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
