grubbers:/ˈɡrʌbərz/| n.|贪婪的人；(尤指)寻食者；(非正式)笨拙的人|A grubber is someone who is greedy, especially someone who searches for food, often in a clumsy or unrefined way.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying a lack of refinement or sophistication.|例句：The grubbers fought over the last scraps of food.  那些贪婪的人为最后一点食物争吵。  He's a real grubber when it comes to money.  说到钱，他真是个贪婪的人。|近义词：scrounger, scavenger, miser, cheapskate|反义词：generous, altruistic, refined
