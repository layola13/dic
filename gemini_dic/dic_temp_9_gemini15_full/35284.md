cluttered:/ˈklʌtərd/| adj. |凌乱的，杂乱的 | filled with too many things, making it look untidy and disorganized.  |The cluttered room made it difficult to find anything.  这间凌乱的房间很难找到任何东西。|messy, untidy, jumbled, congested |orderly, neat, tidy, organized |形容词
