jonas:/ˈdʒoʊnəs/| n. |乔纳斯（男子名）|A Hebrew name meaning "dove" or "pigeon," often used as a given name.  It's a biblical name, associated with the prophet Jonah.|My friend Jonas is a talented musician. 我的朋友乔纳斯是一位有才华的音乐家。|近义词：Jonah (similar biblical name)|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is a proper noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what" does.  Therefore, grammatical tense analysis is not applicable.  It functions solely as a name.

Example 1: Jonas went to the store. 乔纳斯去了商店。
Example 2:  Is Jonas coming to the party? 乔纳斯会来参加聚会吗？


PHRASE->
Because "Jonas" is a proper noun,  it doesn't form common phrases in the same way as common words.  Phrases involving "Jonas" would depend on the context and would be specific to the individual named Jonas. For example:

Jonas's book (乔纳斯的书)
Jonas and his friends (乔纳斯和他的朋友们)
The story of Jonas (乔纳斯的故事)
Waiting for Jonas (等待乔纳斯)
Remember Jonas (记住乔纳斯)
