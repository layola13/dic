quaintest:/kwɛɪntɪst/| adj.|最奇特的，最古雅的，最古怪可爱的|Most charmingly unusual or old-fashioned.  It is the superlative form of "quaint".|例句：The quaintest little village nestled in the valley. 山谷里依偎着一个最奇特的村庄。

例句：Her quaintest habit was collecting antique buttons. 她最古怪的习惯是收集古董纽扣。

近义词：picturesque, charming, odd, peculiar, whimsical

反义词：ordinary, commonplace, modern, typical

词性：形容词
