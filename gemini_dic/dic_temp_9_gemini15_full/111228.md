lithotripsy:/lɪˈθɒtrɪpsi/ /lɪˈθɑːtrɪpsi/| n. | 结石碎裂术；体外冲击波碎石术 | The non-invasive medical procedure that uses shock waves to break up stones in the kidneys, gallbladder, or urinary tract.  |  The doctor recommended lithotripsy to remove the kidney stones.  医生建议进行碎石手术以去除肾结石。|  extracorporeal shock wave lithotripsy (ESWL) |  (无直接反义词，可考虑描述其相反过程，如手术切除) | 名词
