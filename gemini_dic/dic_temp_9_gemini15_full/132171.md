noncustomer:/ˌnɑːnˈkʌstəmə(r)/| n. |非顾客，非客户 |A person or entity that is not a customer of a particular business or organization.  It often refers to someone who interacts with a company without being a buyer, such as a potential customer, a competitor's customer, or someone making an inquiry. |Example: The marketing team focused on converting noncustomers into paying clients.  市场营销团队专注于将非客户转化为付费客户。|近义词：prospect, potential customer, outsider |反义词：customer, client
