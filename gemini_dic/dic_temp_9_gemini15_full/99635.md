interlamellar:/ˌɪntərˈlæmələr/| adj.|层间的，层状的|Situated or occurring between lamellae (thin layers or plates).  The term is primarily used in scientific contexts, particularly in materials science and biology, to describe the space or location between layered structures.|例句：Interlamellar spacing affects the material's overall strength.  层间距影响材料的整体强度。|近义词：interlaminar, interfoliate|反义词：intra-lamellar
