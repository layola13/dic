dialing-up: /ˈdaɪəlɪŋ ʌp/ | v. |拨号上网| The act of establishing a connection to the internet using a modem by dialing a phone number. This method is significantly slower than broadband connections.  | Example: I spent hours dialing-up to download that song. 我花了几个小时拨号下载那首歌。 | 近义词: connecting (to the internet via modem), accessing (the internet via dial-up)| 反义词: broadband, high-speed internet
