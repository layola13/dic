hyperresponsively:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈspɒnsɪvli/ | adv. |反应过度地，过敏地 |  Describes a response that is excessively or abnormally sensitive.  It indicates an overreaction to a stimulus. | Example: The patient reacted hyperresponsively to the medication, experiencing severe side effects. 患者对药物反应过度，出现了严重的副作用。|近义词：overreactively, excessively sensitively |反义词：hyporesponsively, underreactively
