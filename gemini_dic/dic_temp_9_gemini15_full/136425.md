nonretrievable: /ˌnɒnrɪˈtriːvəbəl/ | adj. |不可恢复的，无法取回的 |  Impossible to retrieve or recover.  Something that is nonretrievable is lost permanently and cannot be brought back. | Example: The deleted files were nonretrievable.  已删除的文件无法恢复。  The lost data was nonretrievable due to the hard drive failure. 由于硬盘故障，丢失的数据无法恢复。 | 近义词：irrecoverable, unrecoverable, irretrievable | 反义词：retrievable, recoverable
