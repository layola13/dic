competitor:/kəmˈpɛtɪtər/| n. |竞争者，对手|A competitor is a person, company, or country that is competing with others.  A competitor tries to gain something that others are also trying to gain. | 例句：Our main competitor is a large multinational company.  我们的主要竞争对手是一家大型跨国公司。
| 例句：He's a strong competitor in the race. 他是这场比赛中强劲的竞争者。|近义词：rival, opponent, contender|反义词：collaborator, partner|名词

一般现在时:
The competitor runs a successful marketing campaign. = 这个竞争者正在开展一个成功的营销活动。
Many competitors enter the market each year. = 每年都有许多竞争者进入市场。


一般过去时:
The competitor launched a new product last year. = 竞争对手去年推出了一款新产品。
Our competitor lost a major client last month. = 我们的竞争对手上个月失去了一位重要客户。


现在完成时:
The competitor has significantly increased its market share. = 竞争对手已经显著提高了市场份额。
Our competitor has dominated the market for years. = 我们的竞争对手多年来一直主导着市场。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, the competitor had already filed for bankruptcy. = 在合并之前，竞争对手已经申请破产了。
By the time we released our product, the competitor had already captured a significant market share. = 当我们发布产品时，竞争对手已经占据了相当大的市场份额。


将来时:
The competitor will likely introduce a new technology next year. = 竞争对手很可能明年推出新技术。
We expect our competitor will face challenges in the coming quarter. = 我们预计我们的竞争对手在下一个季度将面临挑战。


虚拟语气:
If the competitor were to lower its prices, we would respond accordingly. = 如果竞争对手降价，我们会相应地做出回应。
I wish the competitor hadn't copied our design. = 我希望竞争对手没有抄袭我们的设计。


被动语态:
The market was dominated by a single competitor for many years. = 多年来，市场一直被一家竞争对手主导。
Our new product is being aggressively marketed to compete with the competitor. = 我们的新产品正在积极推向市场以与竞争对手竞争。


疑问句:
Who is our biggest competitor? = 谁是我们最大的竞争对手？
What strategies are our competitors using? = 我们的竞争对手正在使用什么策略？


否定句:
Our company is not a major competitor in that market. = 我们的公司不是那个市场的主要竞争对手。
They are not a serious competitor in the long term. = 从长远来看，他们不是一个严重的竞争对手。
