foetuses: /ˈfiːtəsɪz/ | n. | 胎儿 (复数) |  The plural of "foetus," referring to unborn offspring.  It is a medical term used to describe developing embryos after the embryonic stage.  The word is generally used in a scientific or medical context. | Example:  Studies on foetuses have revealed much about human development.  研究胎儿揭示了许多关于人类发育的知识。 The doctor examined the foetuses using ultrasound. 医生用超声波检查胎儿。|  embryos, unborn babies |  (No direct antonym, but "born babies" or "infants" could be considered contrasting terms) | 名词
