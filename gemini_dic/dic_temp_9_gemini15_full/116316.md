mastercrafted:/ˈmɑːstəkræftɪd/| adj.|大师级制作的，精工细作的 |  Describes something made with exceptional skill and artistry, often implying a high level of craftsmanship and attention to detail. It suggests a superior quality exceeding that of mass-produced items. | Example: The mastercrafted violin produced a rich, resonant tone. 这把大师级制作的小提琴发出丰富、共鸣的音色。


近义词: expertly crafted, finely made, exquisitely made, handcrafted, artisan-made

反义词: poorly made, shoddy, mass-produced, factory-made

词性:形容词
