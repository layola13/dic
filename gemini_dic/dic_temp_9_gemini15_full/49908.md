demystify:/ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪ/| vt.|使…清晰易懂；解释；阐明|to make something easier to understand; to remove the mystery from something. |例句：The professor's lecture demystified the complex scientific concepts. 教授的讲座使复杂的科学概念易于理解。

例句：The book demystifies the process of starting a business. 这本书阐明了创业的过程。


近义词：clarify, explain, elucidate, simplify, illuminate

反义词：obscure, mystify, complicate


词性:动词

USAGE->

词源:
The word "demystify" is a combination of the prefix "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "to reverse") and the word "mystify" (meaning "to make mysterious" or "to confuse").  Therefore, "demystify" literally means "to remove the mystery."
