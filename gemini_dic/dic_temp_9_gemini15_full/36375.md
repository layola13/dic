coinhabitant:/koʊɪnˈhæbɪtənt/ | n. | 同住者，同居者 | A person or animal that lives in the same place or habitat as another.  This term often implies a shared living space, but not necessarily a cooperative relationship. | Example:  The coinhabitants of the apartment building were a diverse group.  这栋公寓楼的住户来自不同的背景。

The squirrels and the birds are coinhabitants of the park.  松鼠和鸟儿是公园里的同住者。


近义词: roommate, housemate, fellow occupant, inhabitant


反义词: stranger, outsider, solitary inhabitant


词性:名词

词源:
The word "coinhabitant" is a combination of "co-" (meaning "together" or "jointly") and "inhabitant" (meaning "a person or animal that lives in a particular place").  Therefore, it literally means "one who inhabits a place together with others".
