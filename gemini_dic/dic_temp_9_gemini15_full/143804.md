outpace:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪs/| vt.|超过，胜过，比…发展快|To move or progress faster than (someone or something).|例句：The company's growth outpaced its competitors. 公司的增长速度超过了竞争对手。

例句：The demand for electric cars is outpacing the supply. 对电动汽车的需求超过了供应。


近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, outrun

反义词：lag behind, fall behind, trail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The economy outpaces inflation this year. 今年经济增速超过通货膨胀。
He consistently outpaces his colleagues in sales. 他在销售方面总是超过他的同事。


一般过去时:
Our team outpaced the rivals in the final race. 我们队在决赛中超过了对手。
Last year, sales outpaced expectations. 去年，销售额超过了预期。


现在完成时:
Technological advancements have outpaced our ability to regulate them. 技术进步已经超过了我们监管它们的能力。
The company has outpaced its initial growth projections. 公司已经超过了最初的增长预测。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, the other team had already outpaced us.  在我们到达之前，另一队已经超过我们了。
The runner had outpaced all his competitors before the final lap.  在最后一圈之前，跑步者已经超过了他所有的竞争对手。


将来时:
Experts predict that renewable energy will outpace fossil fuels in the next decade. 专家预测，未来十年可再生能源将超过化石燃料。
We anticipate that demand will outpace supply in the coming months. 我们预计未来几个月需求将超过供应。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we could outpace our competitors. 如果我们更努力工作，就能超过我们的竞争对手。
I wish our progress could outpace the challenges we face. 我希望我们的进步能够超过我们面临的挑战。


被动语态:
The small company was outpaced by the larger corporations. 这家小公司被更大的公司超过了。
Our efforts were outpaced by the rapidly changing market conditions. 我们的努力被快速变化的市场环境所超越。


疑问句:
Can our company outpace the market leader? 我们的公司能够超过市场领导者吗？
Does technological innovation always outpace regulation? 技术创新总是超过监管吗？


否定句:
Our sales didn't outpace the industry average. 我们的销售额没有超过行业平均水平。
The project didn't outpace the original timeline. 项目没有超过最初的时间表。
