outwish:/ˌaʊtˈwɪʃ/ | vt. |超过…的愿望；胜过…的愿望 | To surpass in wishing; to exceed in desire or longing.  |例句：He outwished his brother for the new toy. 他比他弟弟更想要那个新玩具。  |近义词：outshine, outstrip, surpass |反义词：underestimate, undervalue |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outwishes his sister for the candy. = 他比他妹妹更想要那块糖果。
They outwish their friends for the promotion. = 他们比他们的朋友更渴望晋升。


一般过去时:
She outwished him for the scholarship. = 她比他更渴望获得奖学金。
They outwished their rivals for the contract. = 他们比竞争对手更渴望得到合同。


现在完成时:
He has outwished everyone for the top position. = 他一直都比其他人更渴望获得最高职位。
She has outwished her colleagues for years. = 多年来，她一直都比她的同事们更渴望成功。


过去完成时:
They had outwished the other team for the championship. = 他们之前就比另一支队伍更渴望赢得冠军。
She had outwished him for the lead role in the play. = 她之前就比他更渴望在戏剧中扮演主角。


将来时:
He will outwish them all for the prize. = 他将比他们所有人更渴望获得奖品。
They will outwish their competitors for the market share. = 他们将比竞争对手更渴望获得市场份额。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could outwish my anxieties. = 我希望我能战胜我的焦虑。
If only I could outwish my fear of failure. = 但愿我能战胜我对失败的恐惧。


被动语态:
(Outwish is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be needed.)  The prize was desired more by her than by him. = 她比他更想要奖品。(被动语态的替代表达)
The position was coveted more by them than by us. = 他们比我们更渴望这个职位。(被动语态的替代表达)


疑问句:
Does he outwish his brother for the inheritance? = 他比他兄弟更渴望继承权吗？
Do they outwish their opponents for victory? = 他们比对手更渴望胜利吗？


否定句:
He didn't outwish his friend for the job. = 他并没有比他的朋友更渴望那份工作。
She didn't outwish him for the attention. = 她并没有比他更渴望得到关注。


PHRASE->
outwish someone for something (渴望某物胜过某人)
outwish one's desires (超过自己的愿望)
outwish one's fears (战胜恐惧)
outwish a rival (胜过竞争对手的愿望)
outwish oneself (过分渴望)
