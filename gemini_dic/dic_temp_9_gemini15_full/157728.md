pjs:/ˈpiːdʒeɪz/ | n. |睡衣| Pajamas, often shortened to PJs, are a type of loose-fitting clothing worn for sleeping. They typically consist of a top and bottom, often made of soft and comfortable materials like cotton or flannel. |Example: I changed into my PJs after a long day. 我长途跋涉一天后换上了睡衣。|近义词：sleepwear, nightclothes |反义词:day clothes, outerwear|名词
