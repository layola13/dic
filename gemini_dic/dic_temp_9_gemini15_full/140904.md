onboard:/ɒnˈbɔːd/ /ˈɑːnbɔːrd/| adj., adv., v.|在船(车、飞机)上的；上船(车、飞机)；雇用；|English Explanation:  Onboard refers to something or someone that is on a vehicle such as a ship, plane, or train.  It can be used as an adjective, adverb, or verb. As an adjective, it describes something located or happening within a vehicle. As an adverb, it indicates the location or process of getting on board. As a verb, it means to take someone on as a member of a team or organization.

|例句：The onboard computer malfunctioned.  船载电脑失灵了。

The new employee was quickly onboard with the project. 新员工很快适应了这个项目。


|近义词：aboard, on, employed, engaged|反义词：offboard, ashore, disembarked|词性：adj., adv., v.
