phonier:/ˈfoʊniər/ | adj. | 假的；虚假的；不真诚的 |  Something that is phonier is less genuine or authentic than it appears; it is artificial, counterfeit, or insincere.  It suggests a deliberate attempt to deceive or impress. | Example: His apology felt phonier than a three-dollar bill.  他的道歉听起来比三美元的假钞还假。

The story he told was phonier than a politician's promise. 他讲的故事比政客的承诺还要虚假。|近义词：fake, artificial, sham, bogus, insincere, counterfeit |反义词：genuine, authentic, real, sincere, truthful |
