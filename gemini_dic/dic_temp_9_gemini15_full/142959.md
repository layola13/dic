outages:/ˈaʊteɪdʒɪz/| n.|中断，停电，服务中断|The noun "outage" refers to a period of time when a service, such as electricity, a telephone line, or a computer system, is not working.|Power outages are common during severe storms.  停电在暴风雨期间很常见。
The internet outage lasted for several hours.  互联网中断持续了几个小时。|blackouts, disruptions, interruptions|restorations, uptime|名词
