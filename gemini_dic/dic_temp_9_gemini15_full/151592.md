parthenogeny:/ˌpɑːrθəˈnɒdʒəni/|/ˌpɑːrθɛˈnɒdʒəni/| n. |孤雌生殖；单性生殖|The process of reproduction in which an egg develops into a new individual without fertilization.  It's a form of asexual reproduction. |Example:  Many species of insects reproduce through parthenogeny. 许多昆虫物种通过孤雌生殖进行繁殖。

Many lizards reproduce via parthenogeny. 许多蜥蜴通过孤雌生殖繁殖。|近义词：asexual reproduction, virgin birth|反义词：sexual reproduction
