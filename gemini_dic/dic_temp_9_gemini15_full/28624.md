caretakings:/ˈkerˌteɪkiŋz/| n. |照管；管理；保管 |The act of taking care of something or someone; the responsibility for looking after something or someone.  This word often refers to the tasks involved in maintaining a property or looking after a person or animal.|例句：The caretakings of the elderly parents fell to the eldest son.  长子的责任是照顾年迈的父母。 The building's caretakings are handled by a professional management company. 这栋楼的管理由一家专业的管理公司负责。|近义词：maintenance, upkeep, supervision, guardianship |反义词：neglect, abandonment
