privately: /ˈpraɪvətli/ | adv. | 私下地，秘密地 | In a private manner; secretly or confidentially.  |例句：They discussed the matter privately. 他们私下讨论了这件事。 |They decided to keep the news privately for a while. 他们决定暂时私下保留这个消息。|近义词：secretly, confidentially, quietly |反义词：publicly, openly
