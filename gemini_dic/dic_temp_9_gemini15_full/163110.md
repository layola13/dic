precollectors: /ˌpriːkəˈlɛktərz/ | n. |预收集者，预先收集者 | Individuals or systems that gather data or items before a main collection process.  They act as a preliminary step in a larger data gathering or item collection operation. For example, in a large-scale survey, precollectors might initially gather data from a smaller, representative sample to refine the survey questions before the main collection phase.  | Example: The precollectors verified the accuracy of the addresses before the main delivery team started their routes.  预收集员在主要派送团队开始路线之前验证了地址的准确性。|近义词：data gatherers, advance collectors, preliminary collectors |反义词：final collectors, post-collectors |名词
