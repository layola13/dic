remagnetize:/ˌriːˈmæɡnətaɪz/ | vt. | 重新磁化 | To magnetize again; to restore the magnetism of (something).  |例句：The old speaker needed to be remagnetized to improve its sound quality.  老式扬声器需要重新磁化以提高音质。

例句：We had to remagnetize the hard drive after it lost its data.  在硬盘丢失数据后，我们不得不重新磁化它。


近义词：re-magnetise (British spelling),  re-energize (in a broader sense) | 反义词：demagnetize | 词性：动词
