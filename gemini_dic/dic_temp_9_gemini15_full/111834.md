logging:/ˈlɒɡɪŋ/| /ˈlɔːɡɪŋ/| n. v.|伐木；记录；登录|The act of cutting down trees for timber or clearing land.  Also refers to the process of recording events or data. |例句：Illegal logging is destroying the rainforest.  非法伐木正在破坏雨林。

例句：The system automatically logs all user activity. 系统会自动记录所有用户活动。

近义词：recording, registering, felling (for trees)

反义词：planting (reforestation), preserving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They log the data daily. = 他们每天记录数据。
We log in to the system using our usernames and passwords. = 我们使用用户名和密码登录系统。

一般过去时:
The company logged a significant profit last quarter. = 公司上个季度获得了可观的利润。(记录利润)
The lumberjacks logged the entire forest. = 伐木工人砍伐了整片森林。(伐木)

现在完成时:
They have logged over 1000 hours of flight time. = 他们已经记录了超过1000小时的飞行时间。
We have logged into the system already. = 我们已经登录系统了。

过去完成时:
They had logged all the data before the system crashed. = 系统崩溃之前，他们已经记录了所有数据。
The loggers had logged most of the trees before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，伐木工人已经砍伐了大部分树木。


将来时:
We will log the results of the experiment tomorrow. = 我们明天将记录实验结果。
They will log in remotely to access the files. = 他们将远程登录以访问文件。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had logged my workout earlier. = 我真希望我早点记录我的锻炼情况。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
