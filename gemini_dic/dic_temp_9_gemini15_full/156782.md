piety:/ˈpaɪəti/| n.|虔诚；孝顺；敬畏|The quality of being religious or reverent.  It often involves a deep sense of devotion to God or a higher power, and can also manifest as dutiful respect for parents or elders. |例句：Her piety was evident in her daily prayers and charitable works.  她的虔诚体现在她每天的祈祷和慈善事业中。

例句：He showed great piety in caring for his aging parents. 他在照顾年迈的父母方面表现出极大的孝顺。


近义词：devotion, reverence, godliness, holiness, religiousness|反义词：impiety, irreverence, wickedness, ungodliness


词源：源自古法语 pieté，最终源自拉丁语 pietas，意为“虔诚，孝顺”。
