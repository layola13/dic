re-arrest:/ˌriːəˈrɛst/| vt.|再次逮捕|To arrest someone again, typically after they have been released.|The suspect was re-arrested after new evidence emerged.  嫌疑犯在出现新的证据后再次被捕。|The man was re-arrested for violating his parole. 这名男子因违反假释而被再次逮捕。|arrest, apprehend|release, free

一般现在时:
They re-arrest suspects frequently. = 他们经常再次逮捕嫌疑犯。
The police re-arrest criminals who violate parole. = 警方再次逮捕违反假释的罪犯。

一般过去时:
The police re-arrested him after finding new evidence. = 警方在找到新证据后再次逮捕了他。
They re-arrested the escaped prisoner. = 他们再次逮捕了逃犯。

现在完成时:
The police have re-arrested the suspect several times. = 警方已经多次再次逮捕了嫌疑犯。
Authorities have re-arrested him on multiple occasions. = 当局已经多次再次逮捕了他。

过去完成时:
The judge had re-arrested the defendant before the trial. = 在审判之前，法官已经再次逮捕了被告。
The police had re-arrested the suspect before the investigation ended. = 在调查结束之前，警方已经再次逮捕了嫌疑犯。

将来时:
They will re-arrest the fugitive if they find him. = 如果他们找到逃犯，他们将再次逮捕他。
The police will re-arrest him should he break his parole. = 如果他违反假释，警方将会再次逮捕他。

虚拟语气:
If they had more evidence, they would re-arrest him. = 如果他们有更多证据，他们就会再次逮捕他。
If new evidence emerged, they might re-arrest the suspect. = 如果出现新的证据，他们可能会再次逮捕嫌疑犯。

被动语态:
He was re-arrested yesterday. = 他昨天被再次逮捕了。
The suspect was re-arrested on charges of obstruction of justice. = 嫌疑犯因妨碍司法公正而被再次逮捕。

疑问句:
Will they re-arrest the suspect? = 他们会再次逮捕嫌疑犯吗？
Was he re-arrested after his release? = 他释放后被再次逮捕了吗？

否定句:
They did not re-arrest the man despite the new information. = 尽管有新的信息，他们也没有再次逮捕那个人。
The police did not re-arrest her because the evidence was insufficient.  警方没有再次逮捕她，因为证据不足。
