reauthentication:/ˌriːɔːθɛnˈtɪkeɪʃən/| n. |重新验证，再次认证 |The act of verifying or authenticating something again.  This often involves confirming identity or credentials a second time.  It is frequently used in security contexts to ensure continued access or prevent unauthorized use.|例句：The system requires reauthentication every hour for security reasons. =出于安全原因，系统每小时都需要重新验证。


The website prompted reauthentication after detecting unusual login activity. =在检测到异常登录活动后，网站提示重新验证。|近义词：reverification, revalidation, confirmation|反义词：deauthentication

He reauthenticates his account regularly to ensure security. = 他定期重新验证他的帐户以确保安全。
