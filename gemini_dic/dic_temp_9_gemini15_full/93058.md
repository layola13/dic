idolatry:/ˈaɪˈdɒlətri/| n. |偶像崇拜；迷信；盲目崇拜 |The worship of idols or idols as gods; excessive or blind adoration or reverence for someone or something.  |例句：His devotion to the company bordered on idolatry.  他的对公司的忠诚近乎盲目崇拜。

例句：The ancient civilization practiced idolatry, offering sacrifices to numerous gods.  古代文明实行偶像崇拜，向许多神灵献祭。


近义词：worship, veneration, adoration, devotion, fetichism

反义词：iconoclasm, atheism, rationalism


词性:名词
