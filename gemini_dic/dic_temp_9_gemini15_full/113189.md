lurgies:/ˈlɜːrdʒiz/ | n. | 恶心，呕吐感 | A feeling of nausea and sickness, often accompanied by a need to vomit.  The term is informal and chiefly used in British English. |  I woke up with the lurgies this morning.  今天早上我醒来感觉恶心想吐。  | He had a bad case of the lurgies after eating the shellfish.  他吃了海鲜后严重恶心。 | nausea, sickness, queasiness |  health, wellness |  名词
