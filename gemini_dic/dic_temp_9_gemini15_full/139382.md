observabilities:/ˌɒbzɜːvəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可观察性 | The degree to which internal states of a system can be inferred from its outputs.  Observability is a crucial concept in system design and monitoring, ensuring that the system's behavior can be understood and issues can be diagnosed effectively. | Example:  Improved observabilities allow engineers to quickly identify and resolve performance bottlenecks.  改进的可观察性允许工程师快速识别和解决性能瓶颈。|近义词：monitorability, visibility, traceability |反义词：inobservability, opacity
