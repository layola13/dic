re-educate:/ˌriːˈɛdʒukeɪt/| vt.|再教育；重新教育|To educate someone again, especially to change their beliefs or political opinions.|例句：The government attempted to re-educate the population about the dangers of smoking. 政府试图对民众进行关于吸烟危害的再教育。|例句：The program aims to re-educate former criminals and help them reintegrate into society. 该项目旨在对前罪犯进行再教育，并帮助他们重新融入社会。|近义词：retrain, rehabilitate, re-orient|反义词：mislead, indoctrinate|动词
