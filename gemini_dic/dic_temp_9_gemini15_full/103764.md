journaling:/ˈdʒɜːrnəlɪŋ/| n.|日记写作；记录；日志记录|The act of writing in a journal; the process of recording thoughts, feelings, experiences, or events in a diary or log.|例句：Journaling helps me process my emotions. 日记写作帮助我处理我的情绪。|例句：Regular journaling can improve mental well-being. 定期记录日记可以改善心理健康。|近义词：diary writing, logging, recording|反义词：forgetting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I journal every morning. = 我每天早上写日记。
She journals her experiences in a travel blog. = 她在一个旅游博客上记录她的经历。

一般过去时:
He journaled his thoughts after the meeting. = 会议后，他记录了他的想法。
They journaled their progress throughout the project. = 他们在整个项目过程中记录了他们的进展。

现在完成时:
I have journaled consistently for five years. = 我已经坚持写日记五年了。
She has journaled about her struggles with anxiety. = 她写日记记录了她与焦虑症的斗争。

过去完成时:
He had journaled extensively before writing his memoir. = 在写回忆录之前，他已经做了大量的日记记录。
They had journaled their observations for months before publishing their findings. = 在发表他们的研究结果之前，他们已经记录了几个月的观察结果。

将来时:
I will journal about my trip to Europe. = 我将记录我欧洲旅行的经历。
She will journal daily during her backpacking adventure. = 在她背包旅行冒险期间，她将每天写日记。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would journal more frequently. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更频繁地写日记。
I wish I had journaled my childhood memories. = 我希望我曾经记录过我的童年记忆。

被动语态:
The events were journaled meticulously by the researcher. = 这些事件被研究人员一丝不苟地记录下来。
The patient's progress is journaled regularly by the doctor. = 病人的病情进展由医生定期记录。

疑问句:
Do you journal regularly? = 你经常写日记吗？
Does she journal her dreams? = 她会记录她的梦吗？

否定句:
I don't journal every day. = 我不是每天都写日记。
He didn't journal his feelings that day. = 他那天没有记录他的感受。
