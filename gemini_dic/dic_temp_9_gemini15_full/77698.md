gemini:/ˈdʒɛmɪnaɪ/ | n. |双子座，双胞胎| The word "gemini" refers to the third astrological sign in the Zodiac, symbolized by twins, and also to twins or a pair of similar things.  It derives from the Latin word "gemini," meaning "twins."| Example:  She's a Gemini, so she's known for her duality. 她是一个双子座，所以她以她的两面性而闻名。  My twin brother and I are very different, despite being Geminis.  尽管我们是双胞胎，我和我哥哥却大相径庭。|近义词：twins, double, pair |反义词：singleton, single
