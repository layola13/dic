muhtar:/muχˈtɑːr/ | n. | 村长，乡长 | A muhtar is an elected head of a village or neighborhood, especially in Turkey and some other parts of the Middle East.  They often act as a liaison between the community and local government. | Example: The muhtar organized a meeting to discuss the new road project.  村长组织了一次会议来讨论新的道路项目。 |近义词: village headman, headman, mayor (in a larger context) |反义词:  None directly applicable;  the antonym would depend on the context, perhaps "citizen" or "resident". | 词性: 名词
