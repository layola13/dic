extemporizes:/ɪkˈstɛmpəraɪzɪz/| v.|即兴创作，临时发挥|To speak or perform without preperation or notes; to improvise.|The comedian extemporized for ten minutes after forgetting his prepared jokes.  喜剧演员忘记了准备好的笑话后即兴表演了十分钟。|She extemporized a beautiful melody on the piano. 她即兴演奏了一段优美的钢琴曲。|improvise, ad-lib|preplan, rehearse
