job-sharing: /ˈdʒɒbˌʃɛərɪŋ/ | n. | 工作分享，分担工作 |  An arrangement where two or more people share the responsibilities and hours of a single job. This allows for flexibility in work schedules and can benefit both the employees and the employer. |  Example:  They implemented a job-sharing arrangement, with each employee working part-time. 他们实施了工作分享安排，每个员工都兼职工作。


Example: The two colleagues decided on job-sharing to better balance work and family life.  两位同事决定分担工作，以便更好地平衡工作和家庭生活。


近义词:  work-sharing, shared employment


反义词: sole responsibility, full-time employment


词性: 名词
