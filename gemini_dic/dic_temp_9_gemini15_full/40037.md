consumings:/kənˈsuːmɪŋz/| adj.| 耗尽的；极度消耗的；极度费心的 | Extremely demanding or absorbing; requiring or using a large amount of time, effort, or resources.  |例句：The consumings task of raising a family kept her busy.  抚养一个家庭的繁重任务让她很忙。
例句：He was consumed by a consuming passion for painting. 他被对绘画的强烈热情吞噬了。|近义词：exhausting, demanding, absorbing, engrossing |反义词：easy, effortless, simple


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is consuming a lot of resources. = 这个项目消耗了大量的资源。
His job is consuming all his free time. = 他的工作耗费了他所有空闲时间。

一般过去时:
The war consumed the entire nation. = 那场战争吞噬了整个国家。
The fire consumed the entire building. = 大火烧毁了整栋建筑物。

现在完成时:
The disease has consumed him completely. = 这种疾病完全吞噬了他。
The project has consumed more time than we anticipated. = 这个项目耗费的时间比我们预期的要多。

过去完成时:

将来时:
The project will consume all our resources. = 这个项目将耗尽我们所有的资源。
This task will consume a significant amount of time. = 这项任务将耗费大量时间。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I wouldn't be so consumed by work. = 如果我有更多时间，我就不会被工作弄得这么焦头烂额。
If he hadn't been so consumed by grief, he might have recovered sooner. = 如果他没有被悲伤吞噬，他可能会恢复得更快。
