shouldn't: /ˈʃʊdn̩t/ | v. |不应该，不应当 | Contraction of "should not".  It expresses a mild obligation, advice, or expectation that something should not happen or be done. It often implies a suggestion or warning. |


例句：
You shouldn't smoke. = 你不应该吸烟。

You shouldn't eat so much candy. = 你不应该吃这么多糖果。


近义词：ought not to, must not (stronger), shouldn't have (past), had better not


反义词：should, must (obligation), can (permission)


词性：动词 (缩略形式)
