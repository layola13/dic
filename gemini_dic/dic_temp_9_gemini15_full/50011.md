denoiser:/ˈdiːnɔɪzər/| n. |降噪器，去噪器 |A denoiser is a device or algorithm that reduces or eliminates noise from a signal, image, or audio.  It works by identifying and removing unwanted elements that interfere with the intended signal. |Example:  The software includes a powerful denoiser to improve image quality.  该软件包含一个强大的降噪器来提高图像质量。

The advanced denoiser significantly reduced the background hiss in the recording.  先进的降噪器显著降低了录音中的背景嘶嘶声。|近义词：noise reducer, filter |反义词：noise enhancer, amplifier |名词
