endeavor:/ɪnˈdɛvər/ | n., vt. |努力；尝试；尽力 |努力，尽力尝试；（尤指长期而认真地）试图做某事 |例句：She endeavored to keep the children quiet. 她努力让孩子们安静下来。|例句：Despite their best endeavors, they failed to reach the summit. 尽管他们竭尽全力，但还是没能到达山顶。|近义词：attempt, strive, try, struggle |反义词：neglect, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endeavors to improve his English. = 他努力提高他的英语水平。
She endeavors to please everyone. = 她努力取悦每一个人。

一般过去时:
He endeavored to explain the situation. = 他努力解释了情况。
They endeavored to find a solution. = 他们努力寻找解决方案。

现在完成时:
She has endeavored to help the needy. = 她一直努力帮助穷人。
He has endeavored to finish the project on time. = 他一直努力按时完成项目。

过去完成时:
They had endeavored to warn him, but he didn't listen. = 他们曾试图警告他，但他没有听。
She had endeavored to contact him, but without success. = 她曾试图联系他，但没有成功。

将来时:
He will endeavor to complete the task by Friday. = 他将努力在星期五前完成任务。
They will endeavor to make it to the meeting on time. = 他们将努力按时参加会议。

虚拟语气:
If he endeavored more, he would succeed. = 如果他更加努力，他就会成功。
I wish I had endeavored harder in my studies. = 我希望我学习时更努力一些。

被动语态:
Every effort was endeavored to save the company. = 已经尽了一切努力来挽救公司。
All possible solutions were endeavored to be implemented. = 所有可能的解决方案都试图被实施。


疑问句:
Did he endeavor to help? = 他努力帮忙了吗？
Will she endeavor to finish the work today? = 她今天会努力完成工作吗？

否定句:
He did not endeavor to hide his feelings. = 他并没有努力隐藏他的感情。
They did not endeavor to contact us. = 他们没有试图联系我们。
