petitioners:/pəˈtɪʃənərz/ | n. |请愿人，申请人 |Those who sign or present a petition.  People who formally request something from an authority. |例句：The petitioners presented their case to the judge. 请愿人向法官陈述了他们的案情。  The petitioners demanded immediate action on climate change.  请愿者要求立即采取行动应对气候变化。|近义词：supplicants, applicants, pleaders |反义词：respondents, defendants
