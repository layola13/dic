enuresis:/ˌɛnjʊˈriːsɪs/ | n. |遗尿症，夜尿症 |Involuntary urination, especially by children at night, after the age at which bladder control is normally attained.| 例句：The doctor diagnosed the child with enuresis.  医生诊断孩子患有遗尿症。|例句：He suffers from nocturnal enuresis. 他患有夜间遗尿症。|近义词：bed-wetting, nocturnal incontinence |反义词：bladder control, continence | 名词
