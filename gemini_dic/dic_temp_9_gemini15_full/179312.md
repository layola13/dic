redocumenting:/ˌriːˈdɑːkjʊmɛntɪŋ/| v.|重新记录，重新编写文档|The act of creating new documentation or revising existing documentation.  It often involves updating, correcting, or expanding upon previous materials.|例句：We are redocumenting the entire software system to improve clarity. 我们正在重新编写整个软件系统的文档以提高清晰度。  The company is redocumenting its processes after the recent merger.  公司在最近的合并后正在重新记录其流程。|近义词：revising, updating, rewriting|反义词：discarding, deleting
