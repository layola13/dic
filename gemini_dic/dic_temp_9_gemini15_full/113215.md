lusern:/ˈluːzɜːrn/ | n. |紫花苜蓿| Alfalfa, a leguminous plant cultivated as fodder.  It is a valuable forage crop, known for its high protein content and ability to improve soil fertility. |例句：The farmer planted a field of lusern for his cattle. = 农民种了一片紫花苜蓿喂他的牛。|例句：Lusern hay is a nutritious feed for horses. = 紫花苜蓿干草是马的营养饲料。|近义词：alfalfa, lucerne|反义词：无|名词
