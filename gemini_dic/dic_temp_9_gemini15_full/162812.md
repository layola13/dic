precalculate:/ˌpriːˈkælkjuleɪt/| vt.|预先计算，预估|To calculate or estimate something in advance.  This often involves making assumptions or using approximate values to get a rough idea before a more precise calculation is possible.|例句：We precalculated the project's costs to ensure we had sufficient funding. 我们预先计算了项目的成本以确保我们有足够的资金。

例句：The engineers precalculated the stress on the bridge before construction began.工程师们在桥梁施工开始前预先计算了桥梁的压力。


近义词：estimate, forecast, project, compute beforehand


反义词：postcalculate (not a common word)


词性：动词
