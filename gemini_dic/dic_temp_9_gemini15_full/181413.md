reiterate:/ˌriˈɪtəreɪt/| vt.| 重申，反复地说 | To say or do something again or repeatedly.  |The teacher reiterated the importance of studying hard.老师反复强调努力学习的重要性。| repeat, restate, emphasize |  understate, downplay | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reiterates his commitment to the project. 他重申了他对项目的承诺。
She reiterates the instructions clearly. 她清晰地重复了指示。


一般过去时:
The speaker reiterated his main points at the end. 演讲者在最后重申了他的要点。
The government reiterated its position on the issue. 政府重申了其在该问题上的立场。


现在完成时:
They have reiterated their support for the cause many times. 他们已经多次重申了他们对这项事业的支持。
He has reiterated his apology for the mistake. 他已经为这个错误反复道歉了。


过去完成时:
She had reiterated her concerns before the meeting. 会议前她已经重申了她的担忧。
They had reiterated their agreement to the terms. 他们已经重申了他们对条款的同意。


将来时:
He will reiterate his arguments in the debate. 他将在辩论中重申他的论点。
The company will reiterate its commitment to customer satisfaction. 公司将重申其对客户满意度的承诺。


虚拟语气:
If he reiterated his request, they would consider it. 如果他再次提出请求，他们会考虑。
I wish he would reiterate his offer. 我希望他会重申他的提议。


被动语态:
The rules were reiterated throughout the training session. 在整个培训课程中，规则被反复强调。
The policy has been reiterated in several official statements. 该政策已在几份官方声明中得到重申。


疑问句:
Will the president reiterate his stance on immigration? 总统会重申他对移民的立场吗？
Did she reiterate her concerns to the manager? 她是否向经理重申了她的担忧？


否定句:
He did not reiterate his concerns at the meeting. 他在会议上没有重申他的担忧。
They will not reiterate their previous statements. 他们不会重申他们之前的声明。


PHRASE->

reiterate an offer 重申一个提议
