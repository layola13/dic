fixation:/ˌfɪkˈseɪʃən/| n. |固定；痴迷；注视；（精神分析）恋物癖|The state of being fixed or attached; an intense preoccupation or obsession with something.  It can refer to a physical attachment, a mental preoccupation, or a psychoanalytic concept.|例句：His fixation on detail slowed down the project.  他的对细节的执着减缓了项目的进度。

例句：Her fixation on her ex-boyfriend was unhealthy. 她对前男友的痴迷是不健康的。

近义词：obsession, preoccupation, attachment, fascination

反义词：indifference, detachment, apathy


词源:  From Old French *fixation, from Latin fixatio, from fixare "to fix," from fixus, past participle of figere "to fix, fasten."
