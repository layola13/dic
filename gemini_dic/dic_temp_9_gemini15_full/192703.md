scythes:/saɪðz/| n.|镰刀；收割机|A scythe is a long curved blade, traditionally used for cutting grass or grain by hand.  It consists of a long handle and a curved blade. Modern scythes are often used for landscaping.|例句：The farmer used a scythe to cut the tall grass.  这位农民用镰刀割高高的草。
The ancient scythe was a powerful symbol of the harvest. 古代的镰刀是丰收的有力象征。|近义词：sickle, reaping hook|反义词：N/A
