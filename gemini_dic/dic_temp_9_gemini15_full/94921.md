in light of: /ɪn laɪt əv/ | prep. |鉴于；考虑到；由于| Considering; taking into account; because of. |例句：In light of the new evidence, the case will be reopened. 鉴于新的证据，此案将被重新审理。

例句：In light of recent events, we've decided to postpone the launch. 鉴于最近发生的事件，我们决定推迟发布。


近义词：considering, given, because of, owing to, due to


反义词：ignoring, disregarding, despite
