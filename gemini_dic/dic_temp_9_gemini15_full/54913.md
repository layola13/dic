dispose:/dɪˈspoʊz/ | /dɪˈspəʊz/ | vt. |处理；处置；丢弃；安排；布置 | to get rid of something, especially by throwing it away; to deal with something; to arrange or organize something |

例句：
We need to dispose of this old furniture. = 我们需要处理掉这些旧家具。

We disposed the troops strategically. = 我们战略性地部署了军队。


近义词：discard, get rid of, arrange, settle


反义词：keep, retain, acquire, obtain


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disposes of his waste responsibly. = 他负责任地处理他的垃圾。
She disposes her time efficiently. = 她有效率地安排她的时间。


一般过去时:
They disposed of the evidence quickly. = 他们迅速处理了证据。
He disposed his belongings before moving. = 他在搬家前处理了他的所有物。


现在完成时:
I have disposed of all my old clothes. = 我已经处理掉了所有旧衣服。
She has disposed her garden beautifully. = 她已经把她的花园布置得很漂亮。


过去完成时:
They had disposed of the problem before the meeting. = 他们会在会议前解决这个问题。
She had disposed of her old car before buying a new one. = 她在买新车之前已经处理掉了她的旧车。


将来时:
We will dispose of the hazardous waste properly. = 我们将妥善处理危险废物。
They will dispose the furniture in the recycling center. = 他们将把家具送到回收中心处理。


虚拟语气:
If I had more space, I would dispose of less things. = 如果我有更多空间，我会扔掉较少的东西。
If she had planned better, she would have disposed of the tasks more efficiently. = 如果她计划得更好，她就能更高效地处理任务。


被动语态:
The waste was disposed of incorrectly. = 废物处理不当。
The troops were disposed along the riverbank. = 部队沿河岸部署。


疑问句:
How do you dispose of your empty cans? = 你怎么处理你的空罐子？
How will the furniture be disposed of? = 家具将如何处理？


否定句:
They did not dispose of the documents properly. = 他们没有妥善处理文件。
She didn't dispose of her old belongings efficiently. = 她没有有效率地处理她的旧物品。


PHRASE->
dispose of: 处理掉
dispose to: 使倾向于
be disposed to: 倾向于
at one's disposal: 任凭某人支配
at the disposal of: 受...支配


词源:
源自古法语 *desposer，由拉丁语 dis-（分开）+ ponere（放置）构成。最初含义是“放置、安排”，后演变为“处理、处置”。
