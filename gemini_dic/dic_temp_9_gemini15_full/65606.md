evanescence:/ˌɛvəˈnɛsəns/ | n. | 消失；逐渐消逝；短暂；易逝 | The fleeting nature of beauty, its evanescence, is a recurring theme in poetry.  美丽的短暂性，它的易逝性，是诗歌中反复出现的主题。|近义词：disappearance, fading, transience | 反义词：permanence, durability |

The scent of flowers shows its evanescence. = 花的香味显示了它的短暂性。
His anger displays a quick evanescence. = 他的怒气很快消散。

The evanescence of the dream surprised him. = 梦的消失让他很惊讶。

过去完成时:

词源:
From Middle French *évanescence, from Latin evanescere "to vanish, disappear," from e- "out, from" + vanescere "to become empty, vanish."  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *gʷeh₁- "to go, to come".
