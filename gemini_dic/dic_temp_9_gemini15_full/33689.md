cinnoline:/ˈsɪnəlaɪn/| n. |噌啉|A heterocyclic aromatic organic compound with the formula C8H6N2. It is isomeric with quinoline and isoquinoline.  It is a bicyclic compound containing a benzene ring fused to a diazine ring. Cinnoline is a weak base and has found some use in the synthesis of other compounds.|Cinnoline is a fascinating molecule with potential applications in medicinal chemistry. |近义词：quinoline, isoquinoline (isomers)|反义词：无
