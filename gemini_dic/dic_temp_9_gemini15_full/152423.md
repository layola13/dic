pawer:/ˈpɔː.wər/| vt.|猛力击打；用力拍打|to strike or beat forcefully with a paw or paws.|例句：The cat pawed at the door, trying to get in. 猫用爪子猛挠门，想进来。|例句：The dog pawed the ground excitedly. 狗兴奋地用爪子刨地。|近义词：strike, beat, slap|反义词：gently touch, caress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat paws at the mouse. = 猫用爪子拍打老鼠。
He paws the ground impatiently. = 他不耐烦地用脚掌拍打地面。

一般过去时:
The dog pawed at my leg yesterday. = 昨天狗用爪子拍打我的腿。
She pawed the dirt looking for a lost earring. = 她扒拉着泥土寻找丢失的耳环。

现在完成时:
The kitten has pawed at the ball of yarn. = 小猫已经用爪子拍打毛线球了。
He has pawed the floor until it is muddy. = 他已经把地板扒拉得泥泞不堪了。

过去完成时:
The bear had pawed at the honey pot before I arrived. = 我到达之前，熊已经用爪子拍打蜂蜜罐子了。
She had pawed through the papers before she found the letter. = 在找到信之前，她已经翻阅了所有的文件。

将来时:
The lion will paw at its prey. = 狮子将会用爪子拍打它的猎物。
I think the puppy will paw at the door to be let in. = 我想小狗会挠门想进来。

虚拟语气:
If the dog pawed at the furniture, I would scold him. = 如果狗抓坏家具，我会责备它。
I wish the cat wouldn't paw at the curtains. = 我希望猫别再抓窗帘了。

被动语态:
The door was pawed at by the cat. = 门被猫抓了。
The ground was pawed by the dog. = 地面被狗刨了。

疑问句:
Did the bear paw at the picnic basket? = 熊有没有拍打野餐篮？
Has the cat pawed at your new shoes? = 猫有没有抓你的新鞋？

否定句:
The dog didn't paw at the stranger. = 狗没有拍打陌生人。
The cat hasn't pawed at the sofa today. = 猫今天没有抓沙发。
