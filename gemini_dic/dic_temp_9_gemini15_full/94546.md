impostor:/ɪmˈpɒstər/| n. |冒名顶替者；骗子；伪君子 | A person who pretends to be someone else in order to deceive others.  An impostor often assumes a false identity to gain an advantage or to avoid responsibility. |例句：He was exposed as an impostor after years of pretending to be a doctor. 他冒充医生多年后，身份被揭穿了。|例句：The impostor skillfully imitated the CEO's signature. 那个冒名顶替者巧妙地模仿了首席执行官的签名。|近义词：fraud, charlatan, deceiver, sham|反义词：legitimate person, authentic person|名词
