permanganic:/ˌpɜːrmæŋˈɡeɪnɪk/| adj.|高锰酸的；紫红色的|Pertaining to or containing permanganate, a salt of permanganic acid.  The term often describes the deep purple color characteristic of permanganate solutions.|The permanganic acid solution is a strong oxidizing agent.  高锰酸溶液是一种强氧化剂。|The deep permanganic color of the potassium permanganate solution indicated a high concentration.  高锰酸钾溶液的深紫色表明浓度很高。| manganic, purplish | colorless (in the absence of manganese)
