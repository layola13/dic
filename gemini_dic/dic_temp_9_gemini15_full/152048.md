paten:/ˈpeɪtən/| n.| 专利；特许状| A patent is an exclusive right granted for an invention, which allows the patent holder to exclude others from making, using, or selling the invention.  It provides legal protection for the inventor's creation for a specific period.| Example:  He applied for a patent on his new invention. 他为他的新发明申请了专利。|近义词：copyright, license |反义词：public domain
