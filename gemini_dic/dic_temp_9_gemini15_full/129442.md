non-abstract:/ˌnɒnˈæbstrækt/ | adj. | 非抽象的，具体的 | Not abstract; relating to or dealing with concrete realities.  It emphasizes tangible objects, events, or experiences rather than theoretical concepts or ideas.  | Example:  The artist preferred non-abstract art, focusing on realistic portrayals of landscapes.  这位艺术家更喜欢非抽象艺术，专注于对风景的真实描绘。 The report provided non-abstract evidence to support its claims.  报告提供了具体的证据来支持其说法。| 近义词：concrete, specific, tangible, realistic | 反义词：abstract, theoretical, conceptual |
