rearing:/ˈrɪərɪŋ/| v. n.|抚养，饲养；培养；竖起；兴起|The act of raising or bringing up children or animals.  It can also refer to the act of something rising or standing upright.|例句：She is rearing her children alone. 她独自抚养她的孩子。

例句：The farmer is rearing cattle for meat. 这个农民正在饲养牛来获取牛肉。


近义词：raising, breeding, nurturing, fostering|反义词：neglecting, abandoning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rearing three children. = 他们正在抚养三个孩子。
The farmer rears chickens and pigs. = 这个农民饲养鸡和猪。


一般过去时:
My grandparents reared me after my parents died. = 我父母去世后，我的祖父母抚养我长大。
They reared a large family. = 他们养育了一个大家庭。


现在完成时:
She has reared five children successfully. = 她成功地抚养了五个孩子。
We have reared several generations of our family dogs. = 我们已经饲养了几代我们家的狗。


过去完成时:
They had reared all their children before they retired. = 他们退休前已经把所有孩子都抚养大了。
The family had reared horses for many years on their land. = 这个家庭多年来一直在他们的土地上饲养马匹。


将来时:
We will be rearing a litter of puppies next spring. = 明年春天我们将饲养一窝小狗。
They will rear more sheep to increase their income. = 他们将饲养更多绵羊以增加收入。


虚拟语气:
If I had the resources, I would rear a flock of sheep. = 如果我有资源的话，我会饲养一群羊。
If they had reared more chickens, they would have made more money. = 如果他们养更多的鸡，他们就会赚更多的钱。


被动语态:
The children were reared in a loving home. = 这些孩子在一个充满爱的家庭中长大。
Many animals are reared on farms for food. = 许多动物在农场被饲养作为食物。


疑问句:
Are they rearing any animals on their farm? = 他们在农场饲养任何动物吗？
How many children are you rearing? = 你养育了多少个孩子？


否定句:
They are not rearing children at the moment. = 他们目前没有抚养孩子。
She did not rear her children alone. = 她没有独自抚养她的孩子。



PHRASE->
rear a family = 养育一个家庭
rear cattle = 饲养牛
rear children = 抚养孩子
rear a horse = 饲养一匹马
rear up = 竖起来 (指动物)

词源:
Old English *reran, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch rijzen and German reisen ‘to raise’.  The noun sense developed later.
