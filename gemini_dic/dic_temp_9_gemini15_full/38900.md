confess:/kənˈfɛs/| vt. n.|承认；坦白；忏悔|to admit that one has done something wrong or has a particular feeling;  to make a confession.|例句：He confessed to the crime. 他承认了罪行。

She confessed her love for him. 她承认爱着他。


近义词：admit, acknowledge, own up|反义词：deny, conceal, hide|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confesses his mistakes every day. 他每天都承认自己的错误。
She confesses her feelings to her friend. 她向朋友倾诉她的感情。


一般过去时:
He confessed his guilt to the police. 他向警方承认了自己的罪行。
She confessed that she had lied. 她承认自己撒了谎。


现在完成时:
He has confessed to stealing the money. 他已经承认偷了钱。
She has confessed her sins to the priest. 她已经向牧师忏悔了她的罪过。


过去完成时:
He had confessed his wrongdoing before the trial began. 在审判开始之前，他已经承认了自己的错误。
She had confessed her secret to her diary. 她已经把她的秘密告诉了她的日记。


将来时:
He will confess his crime to the authorities. 他将向当局承认自己的罪行。
She will confess her love to him tomorrow. 她明天将向他表白爱意。


虚拟语气:
If he confessed, he would feel better. 如果他坦白了，他会感觉好些。
I wish I had confessed sooner. 我真希望我早点坦白。


被动语态:
The crime was confessed by the suspect. 罪行被嫌疑人承认了。
His love was confessed by her. 他的爱被她承认了。


疑问句:
Did he confess to the murder? 他承认谋杀了么？
Will she confess her feelings? 她会坦白她的感情吗？


否定句:
He didn't confess his involvement in the accident. 他没有承认自己参与了事故。
She refused to confess her mistake. 她拒绝承认自己的错误。
