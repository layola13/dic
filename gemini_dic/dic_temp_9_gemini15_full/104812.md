kebab:/kəˈbæb/| n. |烤肉串；肉串 |A kebab is a dish of small pieces of meat (usually lamb, chicken, or beef), vegetables, or fish that are grilled or roasted on a skewer.  It's a popular street food in many countries. |例句：I ordered a chicken kebab for lunch. 我午餐点了一份鸡肉烤肉串。  He bought a delicious lamb kebab from the street vendor. 他从街边小贩那里买了一份美味的羊肉烤肉串。|近义词：shish kebab, skewer, spit |反义词:  None  |名词
