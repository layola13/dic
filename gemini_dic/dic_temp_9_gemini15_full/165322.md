preinvestigates:/ˌpriːɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪts/| v.|预先调查；事先调查 |To investigate something before it happens or before a decision is made.|The committee preinvestigates all proposed projects before granting funds.  委员会在拨款之前会预先调查所有拟议的项目。|近义词：pre-examine,  probe beforehand |反义词：post-investigate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preinvestigate all potential risks before launching a new product. = 他们在推出新产品之前会预先调查所有潜在的风险。
The company preinvestigates the market before releasing a new advertising campaign. = 公司在发布新的广告宣传活动之前会预先调查市场。

一般过去时:
The police preinvestigated the suspect's background before the arrest. = 警方在逮捕之前预先调查了嫌疑人的背景。
She preinvestigated the historical records before writing her dissertation. = 在撰写论文之前，她预先调查了历史记录。

现在完成时:
The team has preinvestigated several options and chosen the best one. = 团队已经预先调查了几个方案，并选择了最佳方案。
He has preinvestigated the matter thoroughly and found no evidence of wrongdoing. = 他已经彻底预先调查了此事，没有发现任何不当行为的证据。

过去完成时:
They had preinvestigated the site before construction began. = 在施工开始之前，他们已经预先调查了现场。
The investigators had preinvestigated the witnesses' statements before the trial. = 调查人员在审判前已预先调查了证人的证词。

将来时:
We will preinvestigate the feasibility of the plan before making a final decision. = 我们将在做出最终决定之前预先调查该计划的可行性。
The government will preinvestigate the environmental impact before approving the project. = 政府将在批准该项目之前预先调查其对环境的影响。

虚拟语气:
If they had preinvestigated the situation, they would have avoided the crisis. = 如果他们事先调查了情况，他们就能避免这场危机。
I wish we had preinvestigated the market trends before investing. = 我希望我们在投资之前预先调查了市场趋势。

被动语态:
All potential risks were preinvestigated before the project commenced. = 在项目开始之前，所有潜在的风险都已预先调查。
The witnesses' statements were preinvestigated by the police before the trial. = 证人的证词在审判前已由警方预先调查。

疑问句:
Did they preinvestigate the safety protocols before the experiment? = 他们在实验前是否预先调查了安全规程？
Had the company preinvestigated the consumer preferences before launching the product? = 公司在推出产品之前是否预先调查了消费者的偏好？


否定句:
They did not preinvestigate the potential problems adequately. = 他们没有充分地预先调查潜在的问题。
The company didn't preinvestigate the market thoroughly enough. = 公司没有充分地预先调查市场。

preinvestigate systematically = 系统地预先调查
