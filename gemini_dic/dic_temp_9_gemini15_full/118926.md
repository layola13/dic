metempsychosis:/ˌmɛtɛmpsɪˈkoʊsɪs/| n. |转世；轮回；灵魂迁移|The belief that the soul after death enters a new body.  This concept is central to many Eastern religions and philosophies.|Example:  Many ancient cultures believed in metempsychosis, the transmigration of souls into new bodies.|近义词：reincarnation, transmigration|反义词：annihilation, extinction|
