dreamingly:/ˈdriːmɪŋli/| adv.|梦幻般地，沉思地，做梦似地|In a dreamy or fanciful manner; as if lost in thought or a dream.|例句：She looked dreamingly out of the window at the falling snow. 她梦幻般地望着窗外飘落的雪花。
例句：He spoke dreamingly of his childhood adventures. 他沉思地讲述着童年冒险经历。|近义词：dreamily, wistfully, pensively|反义词：realistically, practically, alertly
