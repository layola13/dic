redeeding:/ˌriːˈdiːdɪŋ/| v.|再授予；重新赠与；重新交付；|The act of granting or conveying something again;  re-granting something, especially land or property.|The king redeeded the land to the rightful heir. 国王将土地重新授予了合法继承人。|近义词：re-granting, reconveying|反义词：revoking, reclaiming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redeed the property to the original owner. = 他们将房产重新授予了原业主。
He redeeds the land to his son every year. = 他每年都将土地重新授予他的儿子。


一般过去时:
The company redeeded the building to the city. = 公司将大楼重新授予了城市。
She redeeded the farm to her brother after his illness. = 她在她哥哥生病后将农场重新授予了他。


现在完成时:
The government has redeeded the forest to the local community. = 政府已将森林重新授予当地社区。
He has redeeded the shares to his daughter. = 他已将股份重新授予了他的女儿。


过去完成时:
They had redeeded the property before the lawsuit began. = 在诉讼开始之前，他们已经将房产重新授予了。
The family had redeeded the land to the tribe many years ago. = 多年前，这个家庭已经将土地重新授予了部落。


将来时:
The court will redeed the assets to the rightful owners. = 法院将把资产重新授予合法所有者。
They will redeed the house after the renovations are complete. = 装修完成后，他们将重新授予房屋。


虚拟语气:
If the judge were to redeed the land, there would be a great celebration. = 如果法官要重新授予土地，将会举行盛大的庆祝活动。
I wish the council had redeeded the park to the people earlier. = 我希望市议会早点把公园重新授予人民。


被动语态:
The land was redeeded to the original owners after a long legal battle. =经过漫长的法律诉讼后，土地被重新授予了原业主。
The property is being redeeded to the trust. = 该房产正在被重新授予信托。


疑问句:

否定句:
The court did not redeed the property to the claimant. = 法院没有将房产重新授予原告。
They refused to redeed the land to the developers. = 他们拒绝将土地重新授予开发商。
