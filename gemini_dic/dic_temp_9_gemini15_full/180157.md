reformability: /ˌriːˌfɔːrməˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可改革性，可改良性 | The capacity or potential for being reformed or improved.  It refers to the extent to which something can be changed or improved to become better or more efficient.  This could apply to institutions, systems, processes, or even individuals. |


例句：

The reformability of the healthcare system is a subject of ongoing debate.  = 医疗系统的可改革性是一个持续争论的话题。

The high reformability of the software allows for quick adaptation to new requirements. = 该软件高度的可改革性允许快速适应新的需求。


近义词：malleability, adaptability, modifiability, amendability

反义词：inflexibility, rigidity, immutability


词性: 名词
