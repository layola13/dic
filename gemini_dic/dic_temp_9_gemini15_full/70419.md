fighter-bomber: /ˈfaɪtərˌbɒmbər/ | n. |战斗轰炸机| A military aircraft designed for both air-to-air combat and ground attack.  It combines the capabilities of a fighter jet (speed, maneuverability, and weaponry for air combat) with the ability to carry and deliver a substantial bomb load for ground attacks. |例句：The A-10 Thunderbolt II is a well-known example of a fighter-bomber.  A-10 雷霆二世是著名的战斗轰炸机的一个例子。

The F-111 Aardvark was a supersonic fighter-bomber used extensively during the Vietnam War. F-111土豚是一种超音速战斗轰炸机，在越南战争中广泛使用。


近义词：attack aircraft, strike aircraft |反义词：transport aircraft, reconnaissance aircraft
