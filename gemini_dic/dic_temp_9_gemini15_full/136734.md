nonsensitively:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnsɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 不敏感地，迟钝地 |  Acting or behaving in a way that shows a lack of understanding or consideration for the feelings of others.  | 例句：He responded nonsensitively to her grief. 他对她的悲伤反应迟钝。 | 例句：She handled the delicate situation nonsensitively, causing further offense. 她处理棘手的情况很不敏感，导致进一步的冒犯。 | 近义词：insensibly, thoughtlessly, crudely, insensitively | 反义词：sensitively, considerately, tactfully
