dukeship:/ˈdjuːkʃɪp/| n. |公爵地位；公爵领地|The state or rank of a duke; the territory or domain ruled by a duke.  It also refers to the period of time someone holds the title of Duke.|例句：He inherited his father's dukeship and vast estates. 他继承了他父亲的公爵爵位和广阔的庄园。

例句：The dukeship passed to his eldest son upon his death.  在他死后，公爵爵位传给了他的长子。


近义词：dukedom (more common synonym)|反义词：None specifically, as it describes a status.


词性: 名词
