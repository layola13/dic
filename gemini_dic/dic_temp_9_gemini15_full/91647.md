hyperborean:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɔːriən/| adj.|极北的；北极的；严寒的；|Relating to the extreme north, especially the lands and peoples beyond the north wind.  The term often evokes a sense of remoteness, mystery, and often harsh, cold conditions.|例句：The hyperborean winds howled across the icy plains. = 极北的寒风呼啸着穿过冰原。The legend speaks of a hyperborean civilization existing in a land of perpetual twilight. = 传说讲述了一个存在于永恒暮光之地的极北文明。|近义词：arctic, boreal, polar, frigid|反义词：tropical, equatorial, meridional
