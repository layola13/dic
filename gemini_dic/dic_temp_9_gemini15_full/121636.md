misspell:/ˌmɪsˈpɛl/ | vt. | 拼写错误 | To spell a word incorrectly.  |例句：I misspelled your name on the form.  我在表格上拼错了你的名字。| 近义词：miswrite, misspelt | 反义词：spell correctly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misspells many words. = 他经常拼错很多单词。
She misspells names frequently. = 她经常拼错名字。


一般过去时:
I misspelled your address last time. = 我上次拼错了你的地址。
He misspelled the word "definitely". = 他把“definitely”这个词拼错了。


现在完成时:
I have misspelled his name twice. = 我已经两次拼错他的名字了。
She has misspelled the word "accommodate" several times. = 她已经几次拼错“accommodate”这个词了。


过去完成时:
I had misspelled the word before I checked the dictionary. = 我在查字典之前已经拼错了那个词。
They had misspelled the company name on the invoice. = 他们已经在发票上拼错了公司名称。


将来时:
I will misspell it again if I don't look it up. = 如果我不查字典，我还会再拼错它。
He will likely misspell the word again tomorrow. = 他明天很可能会再次拼错这个单词。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the correct spelling, I wouldn't have misspelled it. = 如果我知道正确的拼写，我就不会拼错了。
I wish I hadn't misspelled your name in the letter. = 我真希望我没有在信中拼错你的名字。


被动语态:
The word was misspelled in the article. = 这篇文章中这个词拼错了。
His name was misspelled on the invitation. = 请柬上他的名字拼错了。


疑问句:

否定句:
I didn't misspell the word; I just wrote it quickly. = 我没有拼错这个词；我只是写得很快。
She didn't misspell any words in her essay. = 她的论文中没有任何单词拼写错误。
