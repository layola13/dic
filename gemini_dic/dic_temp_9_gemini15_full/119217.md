miaoued:/mjaʊd/| v.|喵喵叫(过去式)|The verb "miaoued" is the past tense of "miaow,"  an onomatopoeia representing the sound a cat makes. It describes the act of a cat meowing in the past.  |The cat miaoued loudly when it saw the mouse.  猫看到老鼠后大声喵喵叫。|  purred, meowed, chirped | barked (反义词不够贴切，仅供参考)|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat miaows every morning. = 猫每天早上都喵喵叫。
My cat miaows when she's hungry. = 我的猫饿了的时候会喵喵叫。


一般过去时:
The cat miaoued all night. = 猫整夜都在喵喵叫。
The kitten miaoued softly for its mother. = 小猫轻轻地喵喵叫着找妈妈。


现在完成时:
The cat has miaowed several times already. = 猫已经喵喵叫了好几次了。
She hasn't miaowed since this morning. = 自今早以来，她就没有喵喵叫过。


过去完成时:
The cat had miaowed before I even woke up. = 我还没醒猫就已经喵喵叫了。
The dog had barked before the cat miaowed. = 狗叫之前，猫就已经喵喵叫了。


将来时:
The cat will miaow when it sees food. = 猫看到食物就会喵喵叫。
I think the cat will miaow again soon. = 我想猫很快还会再喵喵叫。


虚拟语气:
If the cat miaowed, I would wake up. = 如果猫喵喵叫，我就会醒来。
I wish the cat hadn't miaowed so loudly last night. = 我希望猫昨晚不要叫那么大声。


被动语态: (此词较少用被动语态)
(This tense is rarely used for this word)
A miaow was heard from the backyard. = 从后院传来一阵猫叫声。(被动语态，但并非miaowed的被动)
The sound of a cat miaowing was recorded. = 猫叫的声音被录下来了。(被动语态，但并非miaowed的被动)


疑问句:
Did the cat miaow? = 猫叫了吗？
When did the cat miaow? = 猫什么时候叫的？


否定句:
The cat didn't miaow last night. = 猫昨晚没叫。
The cat hasn't miaowed yet. = 猫还没叫呢。
