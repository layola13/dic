klutzy:/ˈklʌtsi/| adj.|笨手笨脚的，笨拙的| Awkward in movement or handling things.  Someone who is klutzy is clumsy and often bumps into things or drops things. |例句：He's a bit klutzy, always tripping over his own feet. 他有点笨手笨脚，总是绊倒自己。

例句：She's so klutzy; she dropped the vase and broke it. 她真是笨手笨脚，把花瓶打碎了。


近义词：clumsy, awkward, ungainly, clumsy, maladroit|反义词：graceful, dexterous, skillful, nimble
