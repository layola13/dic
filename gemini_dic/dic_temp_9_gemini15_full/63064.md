enrollee:/ɛnˈroʊliː/ | n. |注册者，入学人员，参保人员 | A person who has enrolled in a program, course, or organization.  |Example:  The enrollee count for the fall semester is higher than last year.  秋季学期的注册人数比去年高。|近义词：registrant, participant, subscriber |反义词：none


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The enrollee pays the tuition fees. = 注册者支付学费。
She is an enrollee in the online course. = 她是这门在线课程的注册者。


一般过去时:
He was an enrollee in the military academy. = 他曾是军事学院的学生。
The enrollee completed the registration process. = 注册者完成了注册流程。


现在完成时:
She has been an enrollee in the program for three years. = 她已经参加这个项目三年了。
Many enrollees have already submitted their applications. = 许多注册者已经提交了他们的申请。
