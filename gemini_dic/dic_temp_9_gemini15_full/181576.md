relatable:/rɪˈleɪtəbl/| adj.| 可理解的；感同身受的；引起共鸣的 | Easily understood and empathized with; evoking a feeling of shared experience.  |Example: Her struggles with anxiety were relatable to many young adults.  她的焦虑斗争对许多年轻人来说是感同身受的。  |近义词：understandable, comprehensible,empathetic,resonant |反义词：unrelatable, incomprehensible, alienating
