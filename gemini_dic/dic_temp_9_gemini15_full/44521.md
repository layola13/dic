crossplatform: /ˌkrɒsˈplætfɔːrm/ | adj. |跨平台的|  Supporting multiple operating systems or computing platforms.  Software or applications that are cross-platform can run on different operating systems without modification or with minimal changes. For example, a cross-platform game can be played on Windows, macOS, and Linux. | 例句：This game is cross-platform, so you can play with your friends regardless of what operating system they use.  这个游戏是跨平台的，所以无论你的朋友使用什么操作系统，你都可以和他们一起玩。

The popularity of cross-platform mobile development frameworks has increased dramatically in recent years. 近年来，跨平台移动开发框架的普及率急剧增加。| 近义词：multiplatform, platform-independent | 反义词：platform-specific
