rasher:/ˈræʃər/| n.|熏肉片，一片熏肉|A rasher is a thin slice of bacon or other cured meat, typically fried or grilled.  It's often served as part of a breakfast.|例句：I had two rashers of bacon with my eggs. 我吃了两片熏肉配我的鸡蛋。  I like my rashers crispy. 我喜欢我的熏肉片脆脆的。|近义词：slice, strip|反义词：无
