miscible:/ˈmɪsəbl/ | adj. | 可混溶的，可混合的 | Capable of being mixed; especially capable of being mixed in any proportion without separation into layers.  This term usually refers to liquids. | Example: Water and alcohol are miscible in all proportions.  水和酒精可以任意比例混合。

Example: Oil and water are not miscible; they separate into distinct layers. 油和水不可混溶；它们会分成不同的层。


近义词：mixable, blendable, soluble (in some contexts)

反义词：immiscible


词源：From Latin *miscere "to mix" + -ible "capable of".  The suffix "-ible" indicates capability or possibility.
