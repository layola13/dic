This term is often used in the context of food and wine descriptions. | 例句：The wine possessed delightful fruit flavors with a complete absence of nonastringencies. 这款葡萄酒拥有令人愉悦的果香，完全没有涩味。


The ripe mangoes showed noticeable nonastringencies, indicating their sweetness and readiness to eat.  成熟的芒果表现出明显的无涩味，表明它们很甜，可以食用了。|近义词：softness, smoothness |反义词：astringency, puckery, dryness
