nondegeneration: /ˌnɒndɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən/ | adj. | 非退化；不退化 | Lack of degeneration; the state of not degenerating.  It refers to a system or process that maintains its structure and function without deterioration or decline.  This term is often used in mathematical and scientific contexts. |

例句：

The nondegeneration of the energy levels is crucial for the stability of the system. = 能级的非退化对于系统的稳定性至关重要。

The model assumes nondegeneration of the vibrational modes. = 该模型假设振动模式不退化。


近义词：stability, persistence, integrity

反义词：degeneration, decay, deterioration

词性: adjective
