holds:/hoʊldz/ | v. | 保持；握住；容纳；持有；举行 | To have something in your hand or arms; to contain something; to support something; to maintain possession of something; to organize and be responsible for an event. | 例句：He holds the baby gently. 他轻轻地抱着婴儿。  She holds a strong belief in her abilities. 她对自己的能力深信不疑。 | 近义词：grasp, contain, possess, organize, conduct | 反义词：release, relinquish, abandon, drop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He holds the door open for her. = 他为她扶着门。
The museum holds a vast collection of art. = 博物馆收藏了大量的艺术品。

一般过去时:
She held my hand during the movie. = 看电影的时候她握着我的手。
They held a party to celebrate their anniversary. = 他们举办派对庆祝结婚周年纪念。

现在完成时:
I have held this job for five years. = 我已经做了这份工作五年了。
The company has held its market share despite the competition. = 尽管有竞争，这家公司仍然保持着它的市场份额。

过去完成时:
He had held the position for ten years before he retired. = 他退休前已经担任这个职位十年了。
They had held a meeting before making the decision. = 他们做出决定之前已经开过会了。

将来时:
She will hold the baby while you cook dinner. = 你做晚饭的时候她会抱着婴儿。
The company will hold its annual meeting next month. = 公司下个月将举行年度会议。

虚拟语气:
If I held a higher position, I would have more influence. = 如果我职位更高，我的影响力就会更大。
I wish I held the winning lottery ticket. = 我真希望我持有中奖彩票。

被动语态:
The meeting was held in the conference room. = 会议在会议室举行。
The information was held confidential. = 信息被保密。

疑问句:
Does she hold any grudges? = 她心存怨恨吗？
How long have you held this belief? = 你相信这个多久了？

否定句:
He doesn't hold any resentment towards her. = 他对她没有任何怨恨。
The bridge couldn't hold the weight of the vehicles. = 这座桥承受不了车辆的重量。
