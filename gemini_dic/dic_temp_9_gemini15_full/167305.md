pretrained:/ˌpriːˈtreɪnd/| adj.|预训练的|Previously trained; referring to a model or algorithm that has already been trained on a large dataset before being used for a specific task.  This allows for faster and often more accurate performance on the new task.|例句：The pretrained model significantly reduced the training time for our image recognition system. 预训练模型大大缩短了我们图像识别系统的训练时间。

例句：Using a pretrained language model improved the accuracy of our sentiment analysis. 使用预训练的语言模型提高了我们情感分析的准确性。


近义词：pre-trained, pre-trained model


反义词：untrained, newly trained


词性：形容词
