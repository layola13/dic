rescind:/rɪˈsɪnd/| vt.|撤销；废除；取消|To make (something previously stated or done) legally null and void; to repeal or cancel.|The company rescinded its offer of employment after discovering the applicant's falsified credentials. 公司在发现申请人伪造了证书后撤回了录用通知。|近义词：revoke, annul, repeal, cancel|反义词：ratify, confirm, endorse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The court rescinds the unfair contract. = 法院撤销了不公平的合同。
The government rescinds the new regulation. = 政府撤销了新的规章。

一般过去时:
The judge rescinded the previous ruling. = 法官撤销了之前的裁决。
They rescinded their membership in the club. = 他们取消了在俱乐部的会员资格。

现在完成时:
The company has rescinded its offer of employment. = 公司已经撤回了录用通知。
The school has rescinded the ban on cell phones. = 学校已经取消了手机禁令。

过去完成时:
The president had rescinded the order before the public outcry. = 总统在公众抗议之前已经撤销了命令。
They had rescinded their agreement before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经取消了协议。

将来时:
The university will rescind his scholarship if his grades don't improve. = 如果他的成绩没有提高，大学将会取消他的奖学金。
The company will rescind the contract if the terms are not met. = 如果没有满足条款，公司将取消合同。

虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, the court would rescind the verdict. = 如果证据更充分，法院就会撤销判决。
I wish they had rescinded the policy sooner. = 我希望他们早点取消这项政策。

被动语态:
The contract was rescinded by the court. = 合同被法院撤销了。
The decision was rescinded after further review. = 在进一步审查后，该决定被撤销了。

疑问句:
Will the order be rescinded? = 命令会被撤销吗？
Has the contract been rescinded? = 合同已经被撤销了吗？

否定句:
The court did not rescind the ruling. = 法院没有撤销裁决。
The company will not rescind the contract. = 公司不会取消合同。


PHRASE->

rescind an order = 撤销命令
rescind a decision = 撤销决定
rescind a policy = 取消政策
