disrespecting:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktɪŋ/| v.|不尊重，轻视 |The act of showing a lack of respect.  It involves treating someone or something in a way that is considered rude, impolite, or insulting. |例句：He was accused of disrespecting his elders by talking back to them. 他因顶撞长辈而被指责不尊重长者。

例句：Disrespecting the law can lead to serious consequences.  蔑视法律会导致严重的后果。


近义词：insulting, slighting, disregarding, ignoring, belittling, demeaning, offending


反义词：respecting, honoring, revering, admiring, esteeming


词性：动词 (present participle of disrespect)
