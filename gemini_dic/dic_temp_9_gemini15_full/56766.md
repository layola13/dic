dopaminergic:/ˌdoʊpəˈmɜːrdʒɪk/|/ˌdəʊpəˈmɜːdʒɪnɪk/| adj. |多巴胺能的；多巴胺相关的|Relating to dopamine or its effects.  Dopaminergic neurons produce, release, and/or are sensitive to dopamine. The term often refers to systems or pathways in the brain that utilize dopamine as a neurotransmitter. | Example:  The study focused on the dopaminergic pathways in the brain. 这项研究集中在脑中的多巴胺通路。


The reward system in the brain is largely dopaminergic.  大脑的奖赏系统很大程度上是多巴胺能的。

近义词: dopaminergic, dopaminergic system

反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym, but one could consider terms related to neurotransmitters with opposite effects, such as GABAergic)


词性:形容词
