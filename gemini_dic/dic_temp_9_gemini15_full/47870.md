deceiving: /dɪˈsiːvɪŋ/ | adj. | 欺骗的；迷惑的；虚伪的 |  Giving a false impression; misleading.  Something that is deceiving appears to be one thing but is actually something else.  |

例句：

The advertisement was deceiving; the product was not as good as it claimed.  = 这则广告是具有欺骗性的；产品不如其宣传的那样好。

His friendly manner was deceiving; he was actually very ruthless. = 他友好的态度具有欺骗性；实际上他非常残酷。


近义词：misleading, deceptive, illusory, fallacious

反义词：truthful, honest, genuine, straightforward
