outward:/ˈaʊtwərd/ | adj. |向外的；外面的；公开的 |  Directed or moving towards the outside; external; overt.  |例句：The outward appearance of the building was deceptive. 这座建筑物的外部外观具有欺骗性。

例句：He showed no outward signs of nervousness. 他没有表现出任何紧张的迹象。


近义词：external, exterior, outward-bound, visible, apparent | 反义词：inward, internal, inner, hidden, secret | 词性：形容词

词源:
The word "outward" originates from Old English "utweard," composed of "ut" (out) and "weard" (direction, guard).  It reflects a directional sense of movement or orientation towards the exterior.
