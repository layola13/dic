inequivalvular: /ˌɪnˌiːkwɪˈvælvjʊlər/ | adj. |不等瓣的；瓣膜不等的 | Having valves of unequal size or function.  This term is primarily used in the context of the heart or other organs with valves.  It describes a condition where the valves are not symmetrical or do not function uniformly. |

Example:  The patient's inequivalvular heart condition required immediate surgical intervention.  这位病人的不等瓣心脏病需要立即进行手术干预。


近义词:  asymmetrical (in the context of valve structure), uneven (in the context of valve function)

反义词: equivalvular (having valves of equal size and function)


词性: adjective
