skeptic:/ˈskɛptɪk/ | n. |怀疑论者，持怀疑态度的人 | A person inclined to question or doubt accepted opinions.  |He's a skeptic about the effectiveness of the new drug. 他对这种新药的疗效持怀疑态度。| doubter, questioner, cynic | believer, optimist | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a skeptic about climate change. = 他对气候变化持怀疑态度。
Many people are skeptics regarding the government's claims. = 许多人对政府的说法表示怀疑。


一般过去时:
She was a skeptic until she saw the evidence. = 在她看到证据之前，她一直是个怀疑论者。
The scientist remained skeptical even after the experiment. = 即使实验结束后，这位科学家仍然持怀疑态度。


现在完成时:
Scientists have been skeptics about the existence of aliens. = 科学家们一直对是否存在外星人持怀疑态度。
I have become a skeptic about promises made by politicians. = 我对政客的承诺变得怀疑起来。


过去完成时:
He had been a skeptic for years before he changed his mind. = 在他改变主意之前，他已经是个怀疑论者好多年了。
They had been skeptics about the project's feasibility before the success. = 在项目成功之前，他们一直对项目的可行性持怀疑态度。


将来时:
She will remain a skeptic until more proof is presented. = 在提供更多证据之前，她将继续保持怀疑态度。
I think he will be a skeptic about the new technology. = 我认为他对这项新技术将会持怀疑态度。


虚拟语气:
If I were a skeptic, I would demand more evidence. = 如果我是个怀疑论者，我会要求更多证据。
I wish I weren't such a skeptic about human nature. = 我希望我不要对人性如此怀疑。


被动语态:
His ideas were treated with skepticism by many experts. = 他的想法受到了许多专家的质疑。
The theory was met with skepticism from the scientific community. = 这一理论遭到了科学界的质疑。


疑问句:
Are you a skeptic about the new policy? = 你对这项新政策持怀疑态度吗？
Is he always such a skeptic about everything? = 他总是对所有事情都如此怀疑吗？


否定句:
He is not a skeptic; he's simply cautious. = 他不是怀疑论者；他只是谨慎。
She's not a skeptic about progress, but she's aware of its downsides. = 她对进步并不怀疑，但她意识到进步的缺点。


PHRASE->
a confirmed skeptic = 一个坚定的怀疑论者
a healthy skepticism = 健康的怀疑态度
skeptical attitude = 怀疑的态度
a skeptic's view = 怀疑论者的观点
express skepticism = 表达怀疑
