hoped-for: /ˌhoʊpt fɔːr/ | adj. | 期盼的，盼望的，期待的 |  Describes something that is desired or expected. It suggests a positive anticipation.  |例句：The hoped-for improvement in sales didn't materialize.  = 期盼的销售额增长并没有实现。

The company finally achieved its hoped-for results. = 公司最终实现了它期盼的结果。


近义词：expected, anticipated, desired, wished-for | 反义词：dreaded, feared, unwanted | 词性：形容词
