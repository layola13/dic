hung:/hʌŋ/| v.|悬挂；吊；绞死|The picture hung on the wall. 图片挂在墙上。The criminal was hung. 罪犯被绞死了。|近义词：suspend, hang|反义词：detach, remove|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hangs the laundry every Monday. 他每周一都晾衣服。
The painting hangs in the gallery. 这幅画挂在画廊里。

一般过去时:
She hung the coat in the closet. 她把外套挂在衣橱里。
The thief was hung for his crime. 窃贼因其罪行被绞死。

现在完成时:
They have hung the decorations for the party. 他们已经挂好了派对的装饰品。
He has hung the clothes to dry. 他把衣服晾起来晒干了。


过去完成时:
She had hung the washing before it started to rain.  在下雨之前，她已经把衣服晾好了。
The judge had already hung the verdict before the jury reached it.  法官在陪审团得出结论之前就已经宣判了。

将来时:
I will hang the picture in the living room. 我会把画挂在客厅里。
They will hang the new banner tomorrow. 他们明天会挂上新的横幅。

虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would hang the shelf myself. 如果我有工具，我会自己安装架子。(would have hung更符合虚拟语气)
I wish I had hung the painting higher. 我希望我把画挂得更高一点。

被动语态:
The portrait was hung in the hall. 那幅肖像画挂在大厅里。
The decorations are being hung at the moment. 装饰品正在悬挂中。

疑问句:
Where is the poster hung? 海报挂在哪里？
Has the washing been hung out yet? 洗好的衣服晾了吗？

否定句:
He didn't hang the picture straight. 他没有把画挂直。
The sign wasn't hung properly.  标志没有正确地悬挂。


PHRASE->

hang around:闲逛
hang in there:坚持下去
hang on:稍等
hang up:挂断电话
hang out:出去玩
