prealarms:/ˌpriːˈɑːrmz/| n.|预警，预警信号|A prealarm is an early warning signal indicating a potential problem or emergency before it fully develops.  It's a preparatory alert, often used in technical systems or security contexts.|例句：The fire alarm system emitted a series of prealarms before the full alarm sounded.  消防报警系统发出了一系列预警信号，然后才发出全面警报。

The system sent prealarms indicating a possible power surge. 系统发出了预警信号，表明可能出现电源浪涌。|近义词：warning, alert, forewarning |反义词：post-alarm (not a standard word, but conveys the opposite meaning)|名词
