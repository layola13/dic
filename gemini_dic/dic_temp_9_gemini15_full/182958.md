replacements:/rɪˈpleɪsmənts/ | n. |替代物，替换；更换 |Things or people that replace others.  The process of replacing something. |例句：We need replacements for the broken chairs. 我们需要替换坏掉的椅子。 |例句：The replacements for the old equipment arrived today.  新设备今天到了，替换旧设备。|近义词：substitutions, substitutes, replacements  |反义词：originals


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company needs replacements for its aging workforce. = 公司需要替换日益老龄化的员工。
These parts are replacements for the damaged ones. = 这些零件是用来替换损坏零件的。

一般过去时:
They made replacements for the lost documents. = 他们替换了丢失的文件。
The old engine was replaced with a new one; the replacements ran smoothly. = 旧发动机被换成了新的；替换的发动机运行平稳。

现在完成时:
We have made several replacements to the team this season. = 这个赛季我们已经对球队进行了多次人员调整。
The company has implemented many replacements of its outdated software. = 公司已经实施了很多过时软件的替换。

过去完成时:
They had already found replacements before the employees resigned. = 在员工辞职之前，他们就已经找到了替代者。
Before the meeting, they had prepared all the replacements for the broken equipment. = 会议之前，他们已经准备好了所有损坏设备的替换品。

将来时:
We will need replacements for the damaged components next week. = 下周我们将需要损坏部件的替代品。
The factory will install replacements for the faulty machines soon. = 工厂很快就会安装故障机器的替代品。

虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, we could purchase replacements for all the outdated equipment. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们就可以购买所有过时设备的替代品。
I wish we had better replacements for these worn-out tools. = 我希望我们有更好的替代品来替换这些磨损的工具。

被动语态:
The damaged parts were replaced by new ones; the replacements were immediately installed. = 损坏的零件被新的零件替换了；替换件立即安装。
The old system is being replaced by a new one; the replacements will improve efficiency. = 旧系统正在被新系统取代；替换系统将提高效率。

疑问句:
What are the replacements for this outdated software? = 这个过时软件的替代品是什么？
Do we have enough replacements for the broken tools? = 我们有足够的损坏工具的替代品吗？

否定句:
We don't have any replacements for the missing parts. = 我们没有丢失零件的替代品。
The company didn't find suitable replacements for its key employees. = 公司没有为其关键员工找到合适的替代者。
