dispels:/dɪˈspɛlz/ | vt. |驱散；消除；打消 |To drive away or cause to vanish; to dispel doubts or fears. |例句：The rising sun dispelled the fog.  旭日驱散了雾霾。
The evidence dispelled any lingering doubts about his guilt.  证据消除了对他罪行的任何 lingering 怀疑。|近义词：disperse, dissipate, banish |反义词：gather, accumulate, foster


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun dispels the darkness. = 太阳驱散黑暗。
Warm weather dispels the chill. = 温暖的天气驱散了寒意。

一般过去时:
The wind dispelled the clouds. = 风吹散了云彩。
The explanation dispelled her fears. = 解释打消了她的恐惧。

现在完成时:
The evidence has dispelled all doubts. = 证据已经消除了所有疑虑。
The good news has dispelled our anxieties. = 好消息消除了我们的焦虑。

过去完成时:
The announcement had dispelled any rumors. = 公告已经打消了任何谣言。
The doctor's reassurances had dispelled her apprehension. = 医生的保证消除了她的忧虑。

将来时:
The rain will dispel the heat. = 雨会驱散酷热。
Hopefully, this meeting will dispel any misunderstandings. = 希望这次会议能消除任何误解。

虚拟语气:
If the wind blew harder, it would dispel the smoke. = 如果风再大一些，它就会驱散烟雾。
I wish the sunshine would dispel this gloom. = 我希望阳光能驱散这种阴郁。

被动语态:
The fog was dispelled by the morning sun. = 雾被清晨的阳光驱散了。
Her doubts were dispelled by the new evidence. = 她的疑虑被新的证据消除了。

疑问句:
Will the warm weather dispel the cold? = 温暖的天气会驱散寒冷吗？
Can this explanation dispel your concerns? = 这个解释能打消你的顾虑吗？

否定句:
The explanation did not dispel her suspicions. = 解释并没有打消她的怀疑。
Nothing could dispel the feeling of unease. = 什么也无法驱散这种不安的感觉。


PHRASE->
dispels doubt = 消除疑虑

词源:
dis- (away, from) + pellere (to drive, push)  来自拉丁语，表示“驱散，驱逐”。
