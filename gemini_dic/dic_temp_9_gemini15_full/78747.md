gigue:/ʒiːɡ/| n. | 吉格舞；急促的乐曲 | A gigue is a lively, fast-paced dance and musical form, typically in 6/8 or 3/4 time, characterized by its quick, energetic tempo and often intricate rhythmic patterns.  It's frequently found in Baroque music, particularly in suites. | Example: The final movement of the suite was a spirited gigue.  这组曲的最后一乐章是一首充满活力的吉格舞。|近义词：jig, lively dance, quick step |反义词：slow dance, ballad
