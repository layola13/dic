houseroom:/ˈhaʊsrʊm/ | n. | 住处；住所；容身之所 |  Accommodation or shelter; a place to live.  The term is now largely archaic or dialectal. |例句：They found houseroom in a small cottage. =他们在一个小村舍里找到了容身之所。

例句：After losing their home, they struggled to find houseroom for the winter. = 在失去家园后，他们努力寻找过冬的住所。


近义词：lodging, shelter, accommodation, room and board


反义词： homelessness


词性：名词
