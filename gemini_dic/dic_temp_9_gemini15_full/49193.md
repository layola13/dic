This can involve using alternative dispute resolution methods, simplifying legal documents, or employing technology to automate legal tasks. | Example:  The company implemented a delawyering strategy to streamline its contract negotiation process.  公司实施了去律师化战略以简化其合同谈判流程。 |近义词：legal simplification, streamlining, disintermediation | 反义词：lawyer-centric, over-legalization | 词性：名词
