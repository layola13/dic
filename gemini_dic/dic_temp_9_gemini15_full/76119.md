furtive:/ˈfɜːrtɪv/ | adj. | 鬼鬼祟祟的；偷偷摸摸的；秘密的 | secretive, sly, and trying to avoid being noticed.  It suggests an attempt to conceal something, often something wrong or embarrassing. |

例句：He cast a furtive glance at the clock. 他偷偷地看了一眼钟。

例句：There was a furtive movement in the shadows.  阴影中有一个鬼鬼祟祟的动作。


近义词：stealthy, clandestine, surreptitious, sneaky

反义词：open, aboveboard, overt, frank


词性: 形容词

词源:
