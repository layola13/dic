circumference: /sərˈkʌmfərəns/ | n. | 周长，圆周 | The distance around a circle or any curved figure.  It refers to the linear measurement of the boundary of a closed curve. | The circumference of the Earth is approximately 40,075 kilometers. 地球的周长大约是40075公里。


The circumference of the wheel is too small for this car. 这个轮子的周长对于这辆车来说太小了。


近义词：perimeter, periphery | 反义词：diameter (直径)


词源:  From Middle English *circumference, borrowed from Old French *circonference, from Latin circumferentia, from circumferre "to carry around," from circum- "around" + ferre "to carry."
