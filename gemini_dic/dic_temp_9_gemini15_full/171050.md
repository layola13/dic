pseudouracil:/ˌsuːdoʊˈjʊərəsɪl/| n. |假尿嘧啶|Pseudouracil is a naturally occurring modified nucleotide base found in various RNA molecules.  It is an isomer of uracil, differing only in the position of the carbonyl group.  Its presence often influences the structure and function of RNA. |Example:  The presence of pseudouracil in tRNA is crucial for its function in protein synthesis.  假尿嘧啶在tRNA中的存在对其蛋白质合成功能至关重要。|近义词:5-ribosyluracil|反义词:uracil
