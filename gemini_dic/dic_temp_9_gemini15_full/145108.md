overapprehensive:/ˌoʊvəræprɪˈhɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 过度焦虑的，杞人忧天的 | excessively apprehensive; worrying too much.  | Example:  He was overapprehensive about the upcoming exam, studying for days without rest. 他对即将到来的考试过度焦虑，连续几天不眠不休地学习。  |近义词: overly anxious, excessively worried, hyper-vigilant | 反义词: unconcerned, calm, relaxed |


USAGE->

She is overapprehensive about her health. = 她对自己的健康过度焦虑。
