muskiness:/ˈmʌskɪnəs/| n.|麝香味， 麝香气味 |The strong muskiness of the perfume filled the room.  香水强烈的麝香味充满了房间。|近义词：musky, mustiness, aroma|反义词：clean scent, freshness


英文解析：Muskiness refers to the strong, often pungent and sometimes sweet odor characteristic of musk.  Musk is a substance with a distinctive scent secreted by the male musk deer and used in perfumes.  Muskiness can also describe a similar smell in other substances.

例句：

The muskiness of the old chest was overpowering.  旧箱子的麝香味令人难以忍受。

The air was thick with the muskiness of the damp earth.  空气中弥漫着潮湿泥土的麝香味。


词源:  Derived from "musk," ultimately from Old French *musc, from Arabic مسك (mask), from Persian مُشک (musk).


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, grammatical tenses (present, past, future, etc.) are not applicable.  However, it can be used in different sentence structures:

肯定句:
The room was filled with a pleasant muskiness. = 房间里充满了令人愉悦的麝香味。
His cologne had a distinct muskiness. = 他的古龙水有一种独特的麝香味。


否定句:
The soap didn't have any muskiness. = 这肥皂没有任何麝香味。
There was no muskiness in the air. = 空气中没有麝香味。


疑问句:
Do you detect any muskiness in this fragrance? = 你在这款香氛中闻到任何麝香味吗？
Is that muskiness coming from the candles? = 那麝香味是蜡烛散发出来的吗？
