demean:/dɪˈmiːn/| vt.|贬低；降低；使丢脸|To lower in status, dignity, or estimation; to degrade.|His behavior demeaned his position as a leader.他的行为贬低了他作为领导的地位。|近义词：humiliate, degrade, disgrace|反义词：elevate, exalt, honor|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demeans himself with his rude remarks. 他用粗鲁的言辞贬低自己。
She constantly demeans her colleagues. 她不断贬低她的同事。


一般过去时:
The scandal demeaned the reputation of the company. 丑闻损害了公司的声誉。
He demeaned himself by lying. 他撒谎使自己丢脸。


现在完成时:
His actions have demeaned the entire family. 他的行为已经使整个家庭蒙羞。
She has demeaned herself in public many times. 她多次在公众面前丢脸。


过去完成时:
He had demeaned himself so much that no one respected him. 他已经把自己贬低到没有人尊重他的地步。
They had demeaned the importance of the project. 他们已经贬低了这个项目的重要性。


将来时:
His actions will demean him in the eyes of the public. 他的行为将会在公众面前使他丢脸。
This decision will demean the value of our work. 这个决定将贬低我们工作的价值。


虚拟语气:
If he had not behaved so badly, he would not have demeaned himself. 如果他没有表现得那么糟糕，他就不会使自己丢脸。
If I were you, I wouldn't demean my friends like that. 如果我是你，我不会那样贬低我的朋友。


被动语态:
He was demeaned by the harsh criticism. 他受到了严厉批评的贬低。
The team felt demeaned by the manager's comments. 团队成员感觉受到了经理评论的贬低。


疑问句:
Does his behavior demean his office? 他的行为是否贬低了他的职位？
Does this action demean the company's image? 这个行为是否损害了公司的形象？


否定句:
He did not demean himself in the meeting. 他在会议上没有贬低自己。
The company did not demean its employees. 公司没有贬低其员工。


PHRASE->
demean oneself:自贬身份
demean one's office: 降低职位的尊严
demean one's position: 降低自身地位
demean one's character: 败坏自己的品格
demean one's dignity: 降低自身尊严
