premonopoly:/ˌpriːməˈnɑːpəli/| adj.|垄断前的；垄断之前的|Existing or occurring before a monopoly is established.  Describes a period or state before a single entity gains control over a market or industry.|Example:  The premonopoly era saw fierce competition among several smaller firms.|近义词：pre-monopolistic, ante-monopoly|反义词：monopolistic, post-monopoly
