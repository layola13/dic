lumin:/ˈluːmɪn/ | n. |流明，光通量 | A unit of luminous flux, equal to the amount of light emitted per second in all directions by a source with a luminous intensity of one candela.  |The lamp emits 1000 lumens. 这盏灯发出1000流明的光。|近义词：luminous flux |反义词：无
