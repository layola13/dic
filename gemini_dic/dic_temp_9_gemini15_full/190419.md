sarsaparilla:/ˌsɑːrsəpəˈrɪlə/| n. |萨萨帕里拉（一种植物及其根茎的饮料）|A sarsaparilla is a sweet, carbonated beverage traditionally flavored with extracts from the sarsaparilla plant.  It is often associated with old-fashioned soda fountains.| Example: He ordered a sarsaparilla at the old-fashioned drugstore. 他在老式药店点了一杯萨萨帕里拉。|近义词：root beer (有时), soda, soft drink |反义词：无
