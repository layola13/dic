de-litter:/ˌdiːˈlɪtər/| vt.|清除垃圾，清理杂物|To remove litter or rubbish from a place.  The process involves cleaning up discarded waste materials.|例句：We need to de-litter the park before the picnic. 我们需要在野餐前清理一下公园的垃圾。

例句：The volunteers spent the morning de-littering the beach. 志愿者们上午花时间清理海滩垃圾。


近义词：clean up, clear up, tidy up
反义词：litter, mess, pollute
