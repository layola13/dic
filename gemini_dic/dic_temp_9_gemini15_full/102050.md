isomere:/ˈaɪsəmər/| n. |异构体|A molecule with the same molecular formula as another molecule, but with a different arrangement of atoms.|Example: Glucose and fructose are isomers.  葡萄糖和果糖是异构体。|近义词:isomorph|反义词:无


USAGE->
(由于isomere是名词，没有动词的各种时态变化，以下列举名词的用法)

单数名词:
This molecule is an isomer.  这个分子是一个异构体。

复数名词:
There are many isomers of this compound.  这种化合物有很多异构体。

名词作主语:
Isomers have the same molecular formula but different structures. 异构体具有相同的分子式但结构不同。

名词作宾语:
Scientists study isomers to understand their properties. 科学家研究异构体以了解它们的性质。

名词作定语:
Isomer analysis is crucial in organic chemistry. 异构体分析在有机化学中至关重要。

名词与介词搭配：
The difference between the isomers lies in their spatial arrangement. 异构体之间的区别在于它们的立体排列。
