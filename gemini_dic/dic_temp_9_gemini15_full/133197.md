nonevolutional: /ˌnɒnˌɛvəˈluːʃənəl/ | adj. | 非进化的；非演化的 | Not relating to or resulting from evolution.  This term describes something that does not involve or result from the process of biological evolution over time. It emphasizes the lack of evolutionary change or influence.  | Example: The design of the machine is nonevolutional; it shows no adaptation over time.  这个机器的设计是非演化的；它没有随着时间推移而体现出任何适应性。 |近义词：non-evolving, static, unchanging | 反义词：evolutionary, adaptive, developing
