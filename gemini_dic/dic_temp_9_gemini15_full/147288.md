overmodernizations: /ˌoʊvərˌmɒdərnɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |过度现代化 |The excessive or inappropriate modernization of something, often resulting in negative consequences.  This term highlights the potential drawbacks of modernization efforts that go too far or neglect important aspects of tradition or context.  It suggests a process that is not only excessive but also potentially damaging.

例句：

The overmodernizations of the city center destroyed many historic buildings. = 城市中心的过度现代化破坏了许多历史建筑。

The rapid overmodernizations of the agricultural sector led to soil erosion and water pollution. = 农业部门的快速过度现代化导致了水土流失和水污染。


近义词：hypermodernization, excessive modernization, inappropriate modernization

反义词：preservation, conservation, traditionalism


词性：名词 (plural)
