chapeau:/ʃæˈpoʊ/| n. |帽子；(戏曲中的)帽子；（表示赞赏的）致敬；精彩表演|A chapeau is a hat, especially a man's hat.  It can also refer to a metaphorical hat representing an achievement or a concluding flourish. In theatre, it specifically refers to a hat worn by a character.|例句：He tipped his chapeau in greeting. 他点头致意。|近义词：hat, bonnet, toque|反义词：bare head
