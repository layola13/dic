outgate:/ˈaʊtˌɡeɪt/| n. |出入口，出口 |A passage or gate providing an exit.  An outgate is a way out, often from a confined space or area.  |例句：The outgate was blocked by a fallen tree. 出口被一棵倒下的树挡住了。
The emergency outgate was clearly marked.  紧急出口标志清晰可见。|近义词：exit, outlet, egress |反义词：entrance, ingress
