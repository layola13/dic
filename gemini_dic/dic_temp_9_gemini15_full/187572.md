romanticism:/rəʊˈmæn.tɪ.sɪ.zəm/| n. |浪漫主义；浪漫情怀|Romanticism is a movement in the arts and literature that emphasized emotion, imagination, and individualism. It reacted against the Enlightenment's emphasis on reason and order.  Romanticism celebrated nature, the sublime, and the power of the human spirit. |The Romantic poets, like Wordsworth and Shelley, greatly influenced the development of English literature. |Idealism, sentimentalism |Realism, Classicism |名词
