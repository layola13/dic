rended:/ˈrɛndɪd/ | vt. |撕裂；使…痛苦；呈…状态|to tear (something) violently; to cause (someone or something) to suffer greatly; to be in a particular state|例句：The fabric was rendered useless by the sharp rocks.  布料被锋利的岩石弄得无法使用了。

The storm rendered many homes uninhabitable. 暴风雨使得许多房屋无法居住。


近义词：tore, split, ripped, shattered, devastated |反义词：mended, repaired, healed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dog renders the bone. = 狗啃骨头。
He renders assistance to the needy. = 他向穷人提供帮助。


一般过去时:
The storm rendered the bridge unusable. = 暴风雨使桥梁无法使用。
She rendered her verdict after careful consideration. = 她经过仔细考虑后作出了判决。


现在完成时:
The constant rain has rendered the ground muddy. =  持续的雨水使地面泥泞不堪。
His efforts have rendered the project successful. = 他的努力使项目取得了成功。


过去完成时:
The previous owner had rendered the house uninhabitable. =  前任房主已将房屋弄得无法居住。
They had rendered their final decision before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前做出了最终决定。


将来时:
The new policy will render many existing regulations obsolete. = 新政策将使许多现行规章制度过时。
The technology will render the old system inefficient. = 这项技术将使旧系统效率低下。


虚拟语气:
If the machine were rendered useless, we would have to buy a new one. = 如果机器变得无法使用，我们就必须买一台新的。
I wish the situation hadn't been rendered so complicated. = 我真希望情况没有变得如此复杂。


被动语态:
The building was rendered unsafe by the earthquake. = 建筑物在地震中变得不安全。
The document was rendered illegible by the rain. = 文件被雨淋得无法辨认。


疑问句:
Was the contract rendered void? = 合同是否被宣布无效？
Has the evidence rendered the suspect guilty? = 证据是否证明了嫌疑人的罪行？


否定句:
The efforts did not render the situation better. = 努力并没有使情况好转。
The medicine didn't render him any relief. = 这药对他没有任何缓解作用。


PHRASE->

render aid = 提供援助
render assistance = 提供帮助
render a decision = 作出决定
render a service = 提供服务
render homage = 致敬


词源:

源自古法语 *rendir 和拉丁语 reddere，意思是“归还，交还”。  它的含义演变为“给予，提供”，最终发展到“使成为，使处于某种状态”。
