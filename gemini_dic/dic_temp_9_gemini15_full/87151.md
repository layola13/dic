herbiferous: /ˌhɜːrˈbɪfərəs/ | adj. | 食草的；草食性的 |  Herbivorous means feeding on plants. It describes animals whose diet consists primarily of plants, including grasses, leaves, fruits, and other vegetation.  | Example: Cows are herbiferous animals.  奶牛是食草动物。  | 近义词: herbivorous, plant-eating | 反义词: carnivorous, insectivorous
