evolutionary:/ˌɛvəˈluːʃənəri/ | adj. | 进化的；演变的；发展论的 | Relating to or involving evolution.  The word describes processes or characteristics that are related to the gradual development of something over time, typically through natural selection.  | Example:  The evolutionary process is slow and incremental. =  进化过程缓慢而渐进。

The evolutionary history of the horse is well documented. = 马的进化史有据可查。


近义词: developmental, progressive, transformative | 反义词: static, unchanging, regressive

词源:

The word "evolutionary" is derived from the word "evolution," which comes from the Latin word "evolvere," meaning "to unroll" or "to unfold."  This reflects the gradual and unfolding nature of the evolutionary process.
