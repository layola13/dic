extra-metrical:/ˌɛkstrəˈmɛtrɪkəl/ | adj. |超出韵律的；非韵律的 |  Beyond the metrical pattern; not conforming to the regular metrical structure of a verse or poem.  It refers to syllables or words that are added to a line of verse without affecting the basic metrical pattern.  These extra syllables are often unstressed and may be considered as an embellishment or a deviation from the strict metrical form. | Example:  The extra-metrical syllable in that line of poetry doesn't disrupt the overall rhythm.  那行诗歌中超出韵律的音节并没有破坏整体节奏。  | 近义词: unmetrical, irregular | 反义词: metrical, regular
