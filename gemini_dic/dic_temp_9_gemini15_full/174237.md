querist: /ˈkwɪrɪst/ | n. |提问者，询问者 | A person who asks questions, especially one who makes inquiries formally or officially.  | Example: The querist’s question was insightful and challenged the speaker's assumptions. 提问者的提问富有洞察力，并对演讲者的假设提出了质疑。 |近义词：inquirer, questioner, enquirer | 反义词：answerer, respondent | 名词
