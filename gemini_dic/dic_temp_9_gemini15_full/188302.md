rubbed:/rʌbd/ | vt. |摩擦；擦；揉；涂抹|To move something over the surface of another thing, applying pressure.|The cat rubbed against my leg.  猫蹭我的腿。|He rubbed the dirt off his shoes. 他擦掉了鞋上的泥土。|近义词：scrub, wipe, smear |反义词：polish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rubs his eyes every morning. 他每天早上都揉眼睛。
She rubs the lotion onto her skin. 她把乳液涂抹在皮肤上。


一般过去时:
I rubbed the stain until it disappeared. 我用力擦拭污渍直到它消失。
They rubbed their hands together to warm them. 他们搓搓手来取暖。


现在完成时:
She has rubbed the paint off the wall. 她把墙上的油漆擦掉了。
He has rubbed the surface until it's smooth. 他一直摩擦表面直到它变得光滑。


过去完成时:
I had rubbed the candle wax off the table before they arrived. 他们在来之前我已经把桌上的蜡烛油擦掉了。
She had rubbed her eyes until they were red. 她揉眼睛揉到眼睛都红了。


将来时:
I will rub the lotion on my sunburned skin. 我会把乳液涂在我的晒伤的皮肤上。
He will rub the salt into the meat. 他会把盐搓进肉里。


虚拟语气:
If I rubbed the lamp, a genie might appear. 如果我擦了神灯，可能会出现一个精灵。
I wish I had rubbed the dirt off my clothes earlier. 我真希望我早点把衣服上的泥土擦掉。


被动语态:
The table was rubbed until it shone.  桌子被擦拭得闪闪发光。
The paint was rubbed off the wall by accident. 油漆意外地被擦掉了。


疑问句:
Did you rub the cream into your skin? 你把乳霜涂到皮肤上了吗？
Have you rubbed the car clean yet? 你把车擦干净了吗？


否定句:
He didn't rub the dirt off his shoes. 他没有擦掉他鞋子上的泥土。
She doesn't rub her eyes in the morning. 她早上不揉眼睛。


PHRASE->

rub it in:  旧事重提，雪上加霜
rub along:  勉强过活，凑合着过
rub up against: 摩擦；偶然相遇
rub someone the wrong way:  使某人不快，惹恼某人
