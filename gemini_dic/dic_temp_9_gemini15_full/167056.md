presumes:/prɪˈzjuːmz/ | v. | 假定；推测；认为；擅自行动 | To accept something to be true without proof; to suppose.  | The defendant presumed the witness was lying.  被告假定证人说谎了。| assume, suppose, postulate | deny, refute, disprove | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He presumes his friend will help him. = 他假定他的朋友会帮助他。
She presumes to know everything. = 她自以为什么都懂。


一般过去时:
They presumed the package was lost. = 他们推测包裹丢了。
I presumed you knew about the meeting. = 我以为你知道会议的事。


现在完成时:
He has presumed too much on their kindness. = 他对他们的好意过于依赖了。
We have presumed his innocence until proven guilty. = 在证明他有罪之前，我们一直假定他是无罪的。


过去完成时:
I had presumed that he would be there. = 我原以为他会在那儿。
She had presumed on their hospitality. = 她滥用了他们的好客。


将来时:
They will presume that I am late. = 他们会以为我迟到了。
We will presume that the project will be successful. = 我们预计该项目将会成功。


虚拟语气:
If I presumed to criticize his work, he might be offended. = 如果我冒昧批评他的作品，他可能会生气。
I wish I hadn't presumed on your generosity. = 我真希望我没有滥用你的慷慨。


被动语态:
His guilt was presumed by the judge. = 法官认定他有罪。
Her innocence was presumed until proven otherwise. = 在证明她有罪之前，她被认为是无罪的。


疑问句:
Do you presume to question my authority? = 你敢质疑我的权威吗？
Does he presume to know all the answers? = 他自以为知道所有答案吗？


否定句:
I do not presume to know the answer. = 我不敢说我知道答案。
She did not presume to interfere in their affairs. = 她没有擅自干涉他们的私事。


PHRASE->
presume upon/on: 滥用；冒昧
presume to: 敢于；冒昧
presumed innocent: 被假定为无罪
on the presumption that: 基于…的假设
presumed dead: 推定死亡
