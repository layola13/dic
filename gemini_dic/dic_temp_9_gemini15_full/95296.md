incarnated:/ɪnˈkɑːrneɪtɪd/ | adj. |化身的，具象化的 | Embodied in flesh; given a bodily form.  It refers to a spirit or deity assuming human form, or a quality or idea being represented in a physical or tangible way. |例句：The goddess appeared incarnated as a beautiful woman.  女神以一位美丽女子的形象出现。

例句：His anger was incarnated in a furious expression. 他的愤怒化为暴怒的表情。


近义词：embodied, embodied, personified, exemplified | 反义词：spiritual, abstract, intangible | 词性：形容词
