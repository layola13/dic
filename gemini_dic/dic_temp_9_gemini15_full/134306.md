noninsects: /ˈnɑːnɪnˌsɛkts/ | n. |非昆虫 |  Animals that are not insects.  They belong to other arthropod groups or even entirely different phyla. The term is used broadly to encompass all arthropods and other invertebrates that are not classified as insects.  | Example:  Spiders, crustaceans, and myriapods are examples of noninsects.  蜘蛛、甲壳类动物和多足类动物是非昆虫的例子。|近义词: other arthropods, non-insect invertebrates | 反义词: insects |  词性:名词
