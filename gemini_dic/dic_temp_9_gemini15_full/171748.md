puna:/ˈpuːnə/|/ˈpuːnjə/| n.|普纳（秘鲁高地的一种植物群落）, 高山草原|A high-altitude grassland ecosystem, typically found in the Andes Mountains of South America, characterized by low-growing vegetation adapted to harsh conditions.  |Example: The puna ecosystem is home to unique flora and fauna adapted to high altitudes and extreme weather. 普纳生态系统是适应高海拔和极端天气的独特动植物的家园。|近义词：paramo (similar high-altitude ecosystem)|反义词：无|名词
