gloms:/ɡlɒmz/| v.|粘合；紧抓；迅速抓住；突然袭击|To cling or stick to something; to grab or seize something quickly; to suddenly attack.|例句：The kitten glommed onto my leg. 小猫紧紧抱住我的腿。


例句：The detective glommed onto the suspect. 侦探迅速抓住了嫌疑犯。


近义词：cling, stick, grab, seize, snatch|反义词：detach, release, let go
