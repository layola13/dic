peekaboo:/ˌpiːkəˈbuː/| int. n.| 躲猫猫；捉迷藏；突然出现|An exclamation or game in which someone hides their face and then suddenly reveals it, often to a child.  It can also refer to the act of briefly appearing and disappearing.|例句：The children played peekaboo with their father.孩子们和他们的父亲玩起了躲猫猫。

例句：She peeked through the curtains, then quickly ducked back. 她透过窗帘偷看了一下，然后迅速躲了回去。（This example uses the verb form of "peek", related to peekaboo but not the same word class.）|近义词：hide-and-seek, hide,  boo|反义词:  N/A (Not applicable; it's an interjection and a noun describing a game, not an adjective or adverb that would have a direct opposite.)|词性: int. n.
