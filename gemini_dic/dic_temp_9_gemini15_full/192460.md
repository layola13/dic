scruffier:/ˈskrʌfiər/| adj.|更邋遢的，更不整洁的|More scruffy;  less neat or tidy.  It implies a slightly greater degree of untidiness or dishevelment than "scruffy".|The scruffier the dog, the more affection it seemed to possess.|近义词：shabbier, messier, more dishevelled|反义词：neater, tidier, cleaner
