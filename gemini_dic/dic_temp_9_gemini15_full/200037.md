skelp:/skɛlp/| vt. n.|轻拍；掴；斥责；(俚语)偷窃；|to strike lightly; to slap; to scold sharply; (slang) to steal|例句：He skelped the child for misbehaving. 他因为孩子行为不端而轻拍了他。


例句：The teacher skelped the student for talking in class. 老师因为学生在课堂上讲话而斥责了他。


近义词：slap, smack, rap,  thrash(更强烈的责骂)


反义词：praise, commend, reward


词性:动词(vt.)，名词(n.)
