hummocks:/ˈhʌməks/| n.|土堆；小丘；隆起|A hummock is a small hill or mound, especially one on a relatively flat surface, often formed by the action of ice, wind, or water.  They are typically rounded or irregular in shape and can vary in size from a few centimeters to several meters in height.|例句：The tundra landscape was dotted with numerous hummocks of frozen earth.  苔原景观上点缀着许多冻土堆。

The windswept beach was covered in small hummocks of sand.  风吹拂的海滩上覆盖着许多小沙丘。|近义词：knoll, mound, hillock|反义词：depression, hollow, valley
