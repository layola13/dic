mid-winter:/mɪdˈwɪntər/| n. adj.|仲冬，隆冬；隆冬的 |The period in the middle of winter, typically the coldest part.|例句：The mid-winter freeze caused widespread power outages. 仲冬的冰冻导致了大范围的停电。The mid-winter sun was weak and pale. 隆冬的太阳又弱又苍白。|近义词：deep winter, heart of winter|反义词：midsummer
