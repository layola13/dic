countermark:/ˈkaʊntərmɑːrk/| n. v.|反印记，副印记；加盖副印|A countermark is a mark or stamp added to something, often a coin or medal, usually to indicate a change in value or ownership.|The coin had a countermark indicating it had been re-valued.|近义词：overstamp, secondary mark|反义词：original mark
