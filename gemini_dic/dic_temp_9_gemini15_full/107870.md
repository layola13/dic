largess:/ˈlɑːrdʒes/| n.|慷慨的赠与；施舍；大量赠送|Generous giving; the bestowing of gifts; a generous gift.  It often implies a gift given in a somewhat condescending or patronizing manner.|The king showed his largess by distributing gold coins to the cheering crowds. 国王慷慨地向欢呼的人群分发金币。|The company's largess to its employees included a generous bonus and extended vacation time. 公司对员工的慷慨包括丰厚的奖金和延长的假期。|generosity, bounty, munificence|stinginess, miserliness, parsimony|名词
