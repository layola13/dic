overdefiantness: /ˌoʊvərdiˈfaɪəntnəs/ | n. | 过度反抗；过分蔑视；傲慢不驯 | Excessive defiance;  an extreme display of rebelliousness or contempt.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered overly formal or even neologistic (newly coined).


英文解析:Overdefiantness combines the prefix "over-" (meaning excessively or to a great extent) with "defiantness," which is the noun form of "defiant" (showing defiance or resistance). Therefore, it describes a level of defiance that goes beyond what is considered normal or acceptable.


例句:

His overdefiantness towards authority ultimately led to his dismissal. = 他对权威的过度反抗最终导致了他的解雇。

The child's overdefiantness made it difficult for the teachers to manage him in class. = 孩子过度的反抗使得老师难以在课堂上管教他。


近义词:insubordination, recalcitrance, contumacy, rebelliousness,  contempt


反义词:obedience, compliance, submissiveness, docility


词性:noun
