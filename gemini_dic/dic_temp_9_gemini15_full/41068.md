coppel:/ˈkɒpəl/| n. |铜精矿；（炼铜用的）熔炉|A coppel is a porous cupel, usually made of bone ash, used in assaying to separate precious metals from base metals.  The process involves heating the mixture in the coppel, allowing the base metals to oxidize and be absorbed by the coppel, leaving the precious metals behind. |例句：The assayer carefully placed the ore sample into the coppel for refining.  化验员小心地将矿石样品放入铜精矿炉中进行提炼。|近义词：cupel|反义词：无
