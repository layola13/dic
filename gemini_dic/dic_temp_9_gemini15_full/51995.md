diagnosing:/ˈdaɪəɡnoʊzɪŋ/| v.|诊断，判断|The act of identifying a disease or problem by examining its symptoms.|例句：The doctor is diagnosing the patient's illness. 医生正在诊断病人的病情。|例句：The engineer is diagnosing the problem with the engine. 工程师正在诊断发动机的问题。|近义词：identifying, determining, assessing|反义词：treating, curing


USAGE->

The doctor is diagnosing the patient's condition. = 医生正在诊断病人的病情。
She diagnoses illnesses with great accuracy. = 她能非常准确地诊断疾病。
