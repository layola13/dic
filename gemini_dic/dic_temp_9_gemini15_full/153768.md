peptide:/ˈpɛptaɪd/| n. |肽|A peptide is a short chain of amino acids, typically containing 2 to 50 amino acid residues, linked by peptide bonds.  Peptides are an important component of proteins and have various biological functions. |例句：Insulin is a peptide hormone that regulates blood sugar levels.  胰岛素是一种调节血糖水平的多肽激素。

例句：Many peptides are being studied for their potential therapeutic applications.  许多肽正在被研究以探索其潜在的治疗应用。


近义词：polypeptide (longer chains) |反义词：None  |词性:n.
