contendent:/kənˈtɛndənt/ | n. |竞争者，对手 | A person who competes or is a rival.  A contender for the championship is someone who is competing for it. |Example:  She is a strong contender in the race. 她在这场比赛中是一位强有力的竞争者。

Example: The two contenders faced each other in the final round.  两位竞争者在决赛中相遇。|近义词：competitor, rival, applicant |反义词：winner (in a specific context)
