megawatt:/ˈmɛɡəwɒt/| n.|兆瓦|A megawatt (MW) is a unit of power equal to one million watts.  It's commonly used to measure the output of large power plants, generators, or electrical systems.  |例句：The new power plant generates 1000 megawatts of electricity. 这座新的发电厂发电量为1000兆瓦。|近义词：kilowatt (less powerful)|反义词：milliwatt (much less powerful), microwatt (extremely less powerful)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The power plant generates 500 megawatts. = 这座电厂发电500兆瓦。
This generator produces 10 megawatts of power. = 这台发电机产生10兆瓦的电力。


一般过去时:
The factory consumed 20 megawatts yesterday. = 昨天工厂消耗了20兆瓦电能。
The wind turbine produced only 5 megawatts during the storm. = 暴风雨期间，风力涡轮机只产生了5兆瓦电力。


现在完成时:
The dam has generated millions of megawatt-hours this year. = 这座水坝今年已发电数百万兆瓦时。
We have installed a new system that can handle up to 100 megawatts. = 我们安装了一个新的系统，它可以处理高达100兆瓦的电力。


过去完成时:
Before the upgrade, the system had only produced 50 megawatts. = 在升级之前，该系统只产生了50兆瓦电力。
The old plant had consumed far more megawatts than the new one. = 旧工厂消耗的兆瓦数远多于新工厂。


将来时:

They will need a larger transformer to handle the increased megawattage. = 他们需要一个更大的变压器来处理增加的兆瓦数。


虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
