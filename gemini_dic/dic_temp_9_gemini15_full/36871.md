colorants:/ˈkʌlərənts/ | n. |着色剂，染料 | Substances that are used to add color to something.  They are used in various industries, including food, textiles, and paints. | Example:  Food colorants are often used to enhance the appearance of food products. 食品着色剂常用于增强食品的外观。  The artist used vibrant colorants to create a striking painting.艺术家使用鲜艳的着色剂创作了一幅引人注目的绘画。 |近义词：dyes, pigments, coloring agents | 反义词：decolorants (脱色剂)
