predigested:/ˌpriːdaɪˈdʒɛstɪd/| adj.| 预先消化的；已消化的 | Already digested; prepared in a form easily digested.  This usually refers to food that has undergone partial digestion before consumption,  making it easier for the body to process. | Example:  The baby food is predigested to aid in digestion.  婴儿食品经过预先消化，以帮助消化。  The predigested formula is easier on sensitive stomachs.  预先消化的配方更易于敏感的胃部吸收。|近义词：processed, pre-cooked |反义词：undigested, raw |
