officiates:/əˈfɪʃieɪts/ | v. |主持，担任司仪，执行公务 |To perform the duties of an official, especially at a ceremony or event.  |The priest officiated at the wedding.  牧师主持了婚礼。|The judge officiated over the trial.  法官主持了审判。| preside, administer, conduct | obstruct, hinder |
