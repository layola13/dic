picturegraph:/ˈpɪktʃəɡræf/| n. |象形图；图画统计表|A picturegraph is a type of graph that uses pictures or symbols to represent data.  It is often used to represent data in a way that is easy for children or those unfamiliar with graphs to understand.  Each picture or symbol represents a certain number of items.|The picturegraph showed that apples were the most popular fruit.  这个象形图显示苹果是最受欢迎的水果。 The teacher used a picturegraph to show the class the number of students who chose each activity.|chart, diagram|graph, bar chart|名词
