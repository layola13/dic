petal:/ˈpetl/ | n. | 花瓣 | A petal is one of the often brightly colored parts of a flower that are arranged in a circle around the center.  Petals are usually soft and delicate, and they attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. | Example: The rose petals were a deep, velvety crimson.  玫瑰花瓣是深邃的天鹅绒般的深红色。 The delicate petals of the cherry blossoms fluttered in the breeze.  樱花的娇嫩花瓣在微风中飘动。|近义词：blossom, leaf (in some contexts) | 反义词：stem, sepal
