corrival:/ˈkɔːraɪvəl/| n. |竞争对手，对手 |A corrival is a rival or competitor, someone who is competing for the same thing.  It often implies a strong sense of competition and a similar level of ability or resources. |Example:  The two companies are cor rivals in the market for electric vehicles. 这两家公司是电动汽车市场的竞争对手。

|近义词：rival, competitor, antagonist |反义词：ally, partner, collaborator |名词
