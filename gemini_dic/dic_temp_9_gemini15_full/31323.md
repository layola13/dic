chapters:/ˈtʃæptərz/ | n. |章节，篇章 | A book is divided into chapters.  一本书被分成章节。|The final chapter of the book was particularly moving. 书的最后一章特别感人。|sections, parts, divisions |  | n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The book has ten chapters. = 这本书有十章。
This chapter explains the theory. = 这一章解释了这个理论。


一般过去时:
We read three chapters yesterday. = 我们昨天读了三章。
She finished the chapter before dinner. = 她在晚餐前读完了那一章。


现在完成时:
I have read all the chapters. = 我已经读完了所有章节。
They have completed the first chapter. = 他们已经完成了第一章。


过去完成时:
I had read five chapters before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经读了五章。
We had finished the chapter before the quiz. = 测验前我们已经完成了那一章。


将来时:
I will read the next chapter tomorrow. = 我明天将阅读下一章。
We will discuss the chapter in the next class. = 我们将在下一节课讨论这一章。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would read more chapters. = 如果我有更多时间，我会阅读更多章节。
I wish I had read this chapter earlier. = 我真希望我早点读了这一章。


被动语态:
The chapters were written by a famous author. = 这些章节是由一位著名的作家写的。
The chapter is being discussed in class now. = 现在课堂上正在讨论这一章。


疑问句:
How many chapters are there in this book? = 这本书有多少章？
Which chapter are we on now? = 我们现在读到哪一章了？


否定句:
The book doesn't have many chapters. = 这本书没有很多章节。
I haven't read that chapter yet. = 我还没读那一章。


PHRASE->
chapter and verse = 詳細說明 (详细解释)
a new chapter = 新篇章 (新的开始)
the opening chapter = 开篇 (序章)
a pivotal chapter = 关键章节 (关键时刻)
the final chapter = 结局 (最终篇章)
