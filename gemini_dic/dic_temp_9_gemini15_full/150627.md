papilloma:/ˌpæpɪˈloʊmə/| n. |乳头瘤；赘生物|A papilloma is a benign (non-cancerous) tumor that grows on the skin or mucous membranes.  It is caused by infection with human papillomavirus (HPV). Papillomas can vary in size and appearance depending on their location and the type of HPV involved.  They are often wart-like in appearance.|例句：The doctor examined the suspicious papilloma on her neck.医生检查了她脖子上可疑的乳头瘤。

例句：The patient presented with multiple genital papillomas. 病人出现了多发性生殖器乳头瘤。|近义词：wart, polyp, tumor|反义词：None specifically, but the antonym would relate to the absence of a papilloma, or a healthy, unaffected area of skin or mucous membrane.
