It refers to a specific scoring situation where one player takes all the tricks after a certain point in the game.  It's a significant advantage.|例句：He won the codille and significantly increased his score. 他赢得了科迪尔，并显著提高了他的分数。  |近义词：piquet point (in the context of piquet)|反义词：None specifically|名词
