gravelish: /ˈɡrævəlɪʃ/ | adj. |略带沙砾的；像砾石的 |  Resembling gravel; consisting of or containing gravel.  The adjective describes something that has the characteristics of gravel, such as a rough, gritty texture or a gravelly appearance. |

例句：The path was gravelish underfoot. = 小路底下踩上去感觉有点像砾石。

The texture of the paint was strangely gravelish. = 油漆的质地奇怪地有点像砾石。


近义词：gritty, gravelly, stony

反义词：smooth, polished


词性: 形容词
