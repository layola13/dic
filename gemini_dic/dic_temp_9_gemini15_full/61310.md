embarrass:/ɪmˈbærəs/ | vt. |使尴尬；使窘迫 |To cause (someone) to feel awkward, ashamed, or uneasy.  |例句：His clumsy remarks embarrassed her.  他的笨拙言辞让她感到尴尬。|例句：The unexpected question embarrassed the speaker.  这个意外的问题让演讲者感到窘迫。|近义词：humiliate, shame, mortify |反义词：embolden, encourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embarrasses his friends with his jokes. = 他用他的笑话让他的朋友们尴尬。
The situation embarrasses me. =  这种情况让我尴尬。


一般过去时:
The singer embarrassed himself on stage. = 歌手在舞台上出丑了。
Her mistake embarrassed her deeply. = 她的错误让她深感尴尬。


现在完成时:
I have embarrassed myself many times in public. = 我在公众场合多次出丑。
This incident has embarrassed the whole company. = 这件事让整个公司都感到难堪。


过去完成时:
He had embarrassed himself before the interview even started. = 采访甚至还没开始，他就已经让自己出丑了。
She realized she had embarrassed her parents with her behavior. = 她意识到她的行为已经让她父母感到难堪了。


将来时:
I'm afraid that will embarrass you. = 我担心那会让你难堪。
His actions will embarrass the whole team. = 他的行为将会让整个团队难堪。


虚拟语气:
I would be embarrassed if I did that. = 如果我那样做我会很尴尬。
If I were you, I wouldn't do that; it would embarrass you. = 如果我是你，我不会那样做；那会让你难堪的。


被动语态:
He was embarrassed by her comments. = 他被她的评论弄得很尴尬。
The team was embarrassed by their loss. = 球队因为他们的失败而感到难堪。


疑问句:
Does this situation embarrass you? = 这种情况让你尴尬吗？
Would it embarrass you if I came to your house? = 如果我去你家你会尴尬吗？


否定句:
Don't embarrass yourself in front of your boss. = 不要在你老板面前出丑。
I didn't mean to embarrass you. = 我不是故意要让你难堪的。


PHRASE->
embarrass oneself = 自讨没趣
embarrass sb. greatly = 使某人非常尴尬
be embarrassed to do sth. = 做某事感到尴尬
feel embarrassed = 感到尴尬
embarrassingly enough = 令人尴尬的是
