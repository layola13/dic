cigarettes:/ˌsɪɡəˈrɛts/ | n. |香烟，纸烟 |A small roll of finely cut tobacco wrapped in paper for smoking.  Cigarettes are a major cause of preventable deaths worldwide. |例句：He lit a cigarette and took a long drag. 他点燃了一支香烟，深深地吸了一口。

例句：Smoking cigarettes is a dangerous habit. 吸烟是一种危险的习惯。


近义词：smokes, fags (BrE, informal) | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词
