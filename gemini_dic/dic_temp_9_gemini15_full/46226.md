cystitises:/sɪsˈtaɪtɪsiːz/| n. |膀胱炎（复数）|The plural form of cystitis,  inflammation of the urinary bladder.  It refers to multiple instances of bladder infection.|例句： Several cases of cystitises were reported in the hospital last week.  上周医院报告了几起膀胱炎病例。
