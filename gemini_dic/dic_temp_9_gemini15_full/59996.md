editorialises: /ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəlaɪz/ | vt. |发表社论；表达意见；对…发表评论|To express opinions or judgments in a way that resembles the style of a newspaper editorial.  It often implies a strong or authoritative opinion.|

例句：

The columnist editorialised against the government's new policy. = 专栏作家对政府的新政策发表了评论。

The professor editorialised on the failings of the current education system. = 教授对现行教育制度的缺陷发表了评论。


近义词：comment, opine, pontificate, expound

反义词：remain silent, stay neutral


词性：动词

The newspaper editorialises against the proposed tax increase. = 这家报纸对拟议的增税发表社论评论。

现在完成时:

They have editorialised several times on the need for reform. = 他们已经多次就改革的必要性发表评论。


过去完成时:
He had editorialised frequently about the need for change before the election. = 大选前，他经常就变革的必要性发表评论。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
