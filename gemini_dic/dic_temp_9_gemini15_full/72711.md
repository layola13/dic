focusers:/ˈfoʊkəsɝz/| n. |焦点者，集中者|People or things that draw attention to a particular point or issue.  They help to direct attention and highlight key aspects.|例句：The media reports acted as focusers, drawing public attention to the environmental crisis.  媒体报道充当了焦点，将公众的注意力吸引到环境危机上。|近义词：centers, highlights, emphases|反义词：distractors, diversions
