semicultivated:/ˌsɛmɪˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/| adj.|半栽培的，半驯化的|Partially cultivated; referring to plants or animals that are not fully domesticated but show some adaptation to human influence.  The term often implies a transitional state between wild and fully domesticated forms.|例句：The semicustivated  plants showed increased yield compared to their wild counterparts.  半栽培植物的产量比野生植物高。
The semicustivated sheep were more docile than wild ones, but still required more management. 半驯化的羊比野生羊温顺，但仍需要更多管理。|近义词：partially domesticated, partly cultivated|反义词：wild, fully domesticated
