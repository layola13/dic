nomadize:/ˈnoʊmədaɪz/ | v. |过游牧生活；游牧 |To live as a nomad; to travel from place to place without a permanent home.  |例句：Many tribes nomadize across the vast plains of Africa.  许多部落在非洲广阔的平原上过着游牧生活。|例句：The family decided to nomadize for a year, exploring different parts of the country. 这家人决定游牧一年，探索该国的不同地区。|近义词：roam, wander, migrate, travel |反义词：settle, reside, stay

They nomadize across the desert. = 他们在沙漠中游牧。
We nomadize from season to season. = 我们随着季节游牧。

The Mongols nomadized throughout Central Asia. = 蒙古人曾在中亚游牧。
