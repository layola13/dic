grosses:/ˈɡroʊsɪz/| v. |总收入，总计|To earn or receive a specified amount of money as gross income.  The verb "grosses" is the third-person singular present tense of the verb "gross." It indicates the total amount of money earned before deductions.|例句：The movie grosses $100 million worldwide. 这部电影在全球总收入达1亿美元。
The new restaurant grosses $5,000 a week. 这家新餐厅每周总收入5000美元。|近义词：earns, takes in, rakes in |反义词：nets (refers to income after deductions)
