cerebropathy:/ˌsɛrəˈbrɒpəθi/ | n. |脑病|A general term for any disease affecting the brain.  It encompasses a wide range of conditions that can impair brain function, leading to various neurological symptoms.  The specific symptoms depend on the underlying cause and the area of the brain affected.|例句：The patient presented with symptoms consistent with cerebropathy, requiring further investigation. 患者表现出与脑病一致的症状，需要进一步检查。|近义词：encephalopathy, encephalopathy, brain disease|反义词：cerebral health
