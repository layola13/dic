lifechanging: /ˈlaɪfˌtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 改变人生的，具有重大影响的 |  Describes something that has a profound and lasting impact on someone's life, often in a positive way, but can also be negative.  |例句：Her experience volunteering in Africa was a life-changing event.  她的非洲志愿者经历是一次改变人生的事件。

例句：The diagnosis was life-changing, forcing him to re-evaluate his priorities.  诊断结果改变了他的生活，迫使他重新评估自己的优先事项。


近义词：transformative, revolutionary, momentous, pivotal, significant


反义词：insignificant, inconsequential, trivial, unimportant


词性：形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
This job is life-changing. = 这份工作改变人生。
That experience is life-changing for many people. = 那段经历对许多人来说是改变人生的。


一般过去时:
The trip was life-changing. = 那次旅行改变了人生。
His decision proved to be life-changing. = 他的决定证明是改变人生的。


现在完成时:
The program has been life-changing for many participants. = 这个项目对许多参与者来说已经改变了人生。
Volunteering has been a life-changing experience for me. = 志愿服务对我来说是一次改变人生的经历。


过去完成时:
I didn't realize how life-changing that decision had been until years later. = 直到多年以后，我才意识到那个决定是多么地改变人生。
She hadn't anticipated that the move would be so life-changing. = 她没有预料到搬家会如此改变人生。


将来时:
This new therapy will be life-changing for many patients. = 这种新疗法将改变许多患者的人生。
I believe this opportunity will be life-changing for you. = 我相信这次机会将会改变你的人生。


虚拟语气:
If I had taken that chance, it might have been life-changing. = 如果我抓住那个机会，那可能会改变人生。
I wish I had known then how life-changing that moment would be. = 我真希望当时知道那一刻会如此改变人生。


被动语态:
His life was changed by the accident. = 他的生活被事故改变了。(Although not directly using "life-changing", this demonstrates the passive form conveying the same meaning)
The situation was described as life-changing by many witnesses. = 许多目击者将这种情况描述为改变人生的。


疑问句:
Will this new treatment be life-changing? = 这种新的治疗方法会改变人生吗？
Was the experience truly life-changing? = 那次经历真的改变人生了吗？


否定句:
The experience wasn't life-changing, but it was certainly memorable. = 那次经历并没有改变人生，但它无疑是令人难忘的。
The job, unfortunately, wasn't life-changing as I had hoped. = 不幸的是，这份工作并没有像我期望的那样改变人生。
