pseudocharitable:/ˌsuːdəʊˈtʃærɪtəbl/| adj. |伪慈善的，假慈悲的 | Seemingly charitable but actually motivated by selfish interests.  It describes organizations or individuals who present a charitable facade while primarily pursuing personal gain. | Example:  The pseudocharitable organization used most of its donations for administrative costs, leaving little for the actual cause.  伪慈善组织将大部分捐款用于行政费用，几乎没有用于实际的事业。| 近义词：hypocritical, self-serving, disingenuous | 反义词：genuinely charitable, altruistic |


词源:

* **pseudo-**:  From Greek *pseudo-* meaning "false," "sham," or "pretended."

* **charitable**: From Old French *charitable*, ultimately from Latin *caritas* meaning "charity," "love," or "dearness."
