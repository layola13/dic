manifestos:/ˌmænɪˈfɛstoʊz/| n. |宣言；纲领 |A public declaration of intentions, motives, or views.  A manifesto typically outlines the goals and principles of a political party, movement, or individual. |例句：The Communist Manifesto is a foundational text of Marxist theory.  《共产党宣言》是马克思主义理论的奠基性文本。

例句：The artist released a manifesto explaining her new artistic direction.  这位艺术家发布了一份宣言，解释了她新的艺术方向。

近义词：declaration, proclamation, statement, program, platform

反义词：concealment, secrecy
