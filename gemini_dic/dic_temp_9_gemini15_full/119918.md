milked:/mɪlkt/ | v. |挤奶；榨取；利用；|to extract milk from (an animal), especially a cow; to exploit or take unfair advantage of someone or something.|例句：The farmer milked the cows every morning.  农夫每天早上都挤牛奶。|例句：They milked the situation for all it was worth. 他们充分利用了这种情况。|近义词：squeezed, exploited, tapped|反义词：none

She milks the opportunity for all it's worth. = 她充分利用这个机会。
