shipmates:/ˈʃɪpmeɪts/| n. |船员；同船水手|Fellow sailors or crew members on the same ship.  Shipmates share a close bond due to their shared experiences and reliance on each other in a potentially dangerous environment. |例句：The shipmates shared stories and songs during their long voyage.  船员们在漫长的航行中分享故事和歌曲。
