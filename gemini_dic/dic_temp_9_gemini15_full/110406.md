likable:/ˈlaɪkəbəl/| adj. |讨人喜欢的，可爱的|Pleasing and attractive in character or manner; easy to like.  This word describes someone or something that is easy to like and enjoy being around. |例句：He's a very likable guy; everyone enjoys his company. 他是个很讨人喜欢的小伙子；每个人都喜欢和他在一起。

例句：The new CEO has a likable personality, making her easy to work with. 新任CEO性格讨喜，让她易于共事。


近义词：lovable, amiable, agreeable, pleasant, charming|反义词：unlikable, disagreeable, unpleasant, repulsive|词性：形容词
