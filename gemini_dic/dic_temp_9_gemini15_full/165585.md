prememorandum:/ˌpriːˈmɛməˈrændəm/| n. |备忘录（正式会议前的）；预备备忘录 |A prememorandum is a preliminary document prepared before a formal meeting or negotiation, outlining key points and issues to be discussed. It serves as a preparation document for a more formal memorandum.  |Example: The prememorandum for the upcoming trade negotiations detailed the potential sticking points and proposed compromises.  =  即将进行的贸易谈判的预备备忘录详细说明了潜在的症结点和提出的妥协方案。|近义词： preliminary note, draft memorandum |反义词：final memorandum, post-meeting summary|名词
