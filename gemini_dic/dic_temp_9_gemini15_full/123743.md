moonwalk:/ˈmuːnwɔːk/|/ˈmuːnˌwɔːk/| n. v.|月球漫步；太空步|A dance move in which the dancer appears to be gliding backward while his feet move in a short, shuffling manner.  It is famously associated with Michael Jackson.|例句：He learned to do the moonwalk after watching Michael Jackson's videos. 他在看了迈克尔·杰克逊的视频后学会了太空步。

例句：Michael Jackson's moonwalk became an iconic dance move. 迈克尔·杰克逊的太空步成为了一个标志性的舞蹈动作。
