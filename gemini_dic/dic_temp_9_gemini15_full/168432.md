proauction:/prəˈdʌkʃən/| n.|生产；产量；制作；产品|The act of producing something; the process of making, manufacturing, or creating something. It can refer to the creation of goods, services, art, or other outputs.|例句：The factory's production of cars increased last year.  = 去年这家工厂的汽车产量增加了。|例句：The production of the play was excellent. = 这部戏的制作非常出色。|近义词：manufacture, creation, output, generation|反义词：consumption, destruction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory produces thousands of cars every month. = 工厂每月生产数千辆汽车。
Our company produces high-quality software. = 我们公司生产高质量的软件。


一般过去时:
They produced a successful film last year. = 他们去年制作了一部成功的电影。
The company produced a new model of phone. = 公司生产了一款新型手机。


现在完成时:
We have produced three reports this week. = 我们本周已经制作了三份报告。
The artist has produced many famous paintings. =这位艺术家创作了许多名画。


过去完成时:
They had produced several prototypes before launching the product. = 他们在推出产品之前已经制作了几个原型。
The team had produced a comprehensive plan before the meeting. = 团队在会议前已经制定了一个全面的计划。


将来时:
We will produce a new line of clothing next season. = 我们将在下个季度生产新的服装系列。
The company will produce more electric cars in the future. = 公司将来会生产更多电动汽车。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could produce more goods. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们就能生产更多商品。
I wish we had produced a better marketing campaign. = 我希望我们能策划一个更好的营销活动。


被动语态:
Thousands of cars are produced by the factory every month. = 工厂每月生产数千辆汽车。
The film was produced by a well-known director. = 这部电影是由一位著名的导演制作的。


疑问句:
How many cars are produced each day? = 每天生产多少辆汽车？
What is being produced in that factory? = 那个工厂在生产什么？


否定句:
The company did not produce enough goods to meet demand. = 公司生产的商品不足以满足需求。
We haven't produced the final report yet. = 我们还没有制作最终报告。
