permeate:/ˈpɜːmieɪt/| v.|渗透，弥漫，遍布|To spread throughout (something); to penetrate or pervade.|The aroma of freshly brewed coffee permeated the air. 新鲜冲泡的咖啡香气弥漫在空气中。The new ideas gradually permeated the organization. 新思想逐渐渗透到整个组织中。|diffuse, infiltrate, penetrate, imbue|isolate, separate, exclude|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The aroma permeates the room. = 香气弥漫房间。
His influence permeates every aspect of the company. = 他的影响渗透到公司的各个方面。


一般过去时:
The rain permeated the ground. = 雨水渗透了地面。
The sadness permeated her heart. = 悲伤充满了她的心。


现在完成时:
The idea has permeated the public consciousness. = 这个想法已经深入人心。
His influence has permeated the entire industry. = 他的影响已经渗透到整个行业。


过去完成时:
The scent had permeated the entire house before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，香味已经弥漫了整个房子。
The fear had permeated the town before the news broke. = 在消息传出之前，恐惧已经弥漫了整个城镇。


将来时:
The new technology will permeate all aspects of our lives. = 新技术将渗透到我们生活的方方面面。
The effects of climate change will permeate the entire ecosystem. = 气候变化的影响将渗透到整个生态系统。


虚拟语气:
If the dye permeated the fabric evenly, the result would be perfect. = 如果染料均匀地渗透到织物中，结果就会完美。
I wish the message had permeated the entire team. = 我希望这条信息已经传达给了整个团队。


被动语态:
The building was permeated by a strange odor. = 这栋建筑物弥漫着一种奇怪的气味。
The culture was permeated by a sense of optimism. = 这文化弥漫着乐观主义。


疑问句:
Does the water permeate the soil easily? = 水容易渗透土壤吗？
Does the fragrance permeate the entire house? = 香味弥漫整个房子了吗？


否定句:
The water didn't permeate the concrete. = 水没有渗透混凝土。
The message didn't permeate the group effectively. = 信息没有有效地传达到小组中。


PHRASE->
