continue:/kənˈtɪnjuː/ | v. |继续，持续 |To go on; to proceed without interruption.  |例句：The meeting will continue after lunch.  会议将在午餐后继续。|例句：The rain continued throughout the night. 雨持续了一整夜。|近义词：prolong, resume, persevere |反义词：stop, cease, discontinue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He continues to work hard. = 他继续努力工作。
She continues her studies. = 她继续她的学业。

一般过去时:
The music continued for another hour. = 音乐又持续了一个小时。
He continued his speech despite the interruptions. = 尽管被打扰，他还是继续他的演讲。


现在完成时:
We have continued to support this project. = 我们一直继续支持这个项目。
She has continued to improve her skills. = 她一直在提高她的技能。


过去完成时:
They had continued to live there for ten years. = 他们在那里已经住了十年了。
The rain had continued all night. = 雨下了一整夜。


将来时:
I will continue my work tomorrow. = 我明天将继续我的工作。
The show will continue next week. = 节目将在下周继续。


虚拟语气:
If he continued working, he would succeed. = 如果他继续工作，他会成功的。
I wish she continued her education. = 我希望她继续她的教育。


被动语态:
The meeting was continued after a short break. = 会议在短暂休息后继续进行。
The project will be continued next year. = 该项目将在明年继续。


疑问句:
Will you continue the experiment? = 你会继续实验吗？
Does the story continue in the next chapter? = 故事在下章还会继续吗？


否定句:
He did not continue his studies. = 他没有继续他的学业。
She will not continue with the plan. = 她不会继续执行这个计划。


PHRASE->
continue to do sth = 继续做某事
continue with sth = 继续做某事
continue doing sth = 继续做某事
continue on = 继续前进
without continue = 不停地
