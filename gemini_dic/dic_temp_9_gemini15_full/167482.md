preventives:/prɪˈvɛntɪvz/| n. |预防措施，预防性措施 |Things done to prevent something from happening.  Preventive measures are crucial in mitigating risks. |Example:  Regular exercise and a balanced diet are important preventives against heart disease.  定期锻炼和均衡饮食是预防心脏病的重要措施。  |近义词： safeguards, precautions, protections |反义词：accelerants, catalysts
