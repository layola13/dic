catmints: /ˈkætˌmɪnts/ | n. |猫薄荷属植物|A genus of aromatic herbs in the mint family, known for their attractive flowers and the euphoric effect they have on cats.  The plants are characterized by their square stems, opposite leaves, and often lavender or purple flowers. They are popular in gardens and are sometimes used in herbal remedies. |例句：The cat went wild when it smelled the catmints. 猫闻到猫薄荷的味道后就发狂了。

The cat rolled around in the fragrant catmints. 猫在芬芳的猫薄荷里打滚。|近义词：catnip (a specific species of catmint)|反义词：无|名词
