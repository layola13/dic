/ˈkæntoʊz/| n.| cantos | 歌曲；篇章；诗节 |  A canto is a section or division of a long poem, similar to a chapter in a book.  The term is frequently used in reference to epic poems.  | The poem was divided into three cantos. 这首诗被分成三个章节。  | chapter, section, stanza |  (无明显反义词) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The poem consists of many cantos. = 这首诗由许多章节组成。
Each canto tells a different story. = 每个章节讲述一个不同的故事。

一般过去时:
He read the first canto aloud. = 他大声朗读了第一章。
The audience listened intently to each canto. = 观众专心致志地听着每一章节。

现在完成时:
She has finished reading all the cantos. = 她已经读完了所有章节。
We have studied the cantos in detail. = 我们已经详细研究了这些章节。

过去完成时:
They had already read the first three cantos before the lecture. = 他们在讲座前已经读完了前三个章节。
I had memorized several cantos by heart before the competition. = 我在比赛前已经背诵了好几个章节。

将来时:

We will analyze each canto separately. = 我们将分别分析每个章节。
