commissars:/ˈkɒmɪsɑːz/| n.|委员；专员；政治委员|A commissar is a member of a communist party appointed to supervise or control a particular area or activity.|例句：The commissars oversaw the implementation of the five-year plan.  政治委员监督五年计划的实施。|例句：The military commissar ensured troop morale remained high.  军事委员确保部队士气高昂。|近义词：commissioner, supervisor, official|反义词：subordinate
