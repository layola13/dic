paleographer:/ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfər/| n. |古文字学家|A paleographer is a scholar who studies ancient handwriting and writing systems.  They analyze the physical characteristics of historical documents, such as script, ink, and materials, to understand the history and context of the writing.| The paleographer meticulously examined the faded script, deciphering the ancient text.|近义词：archivist, epigrapher|反义词：None
