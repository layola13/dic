litigation:/ˌlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |诉讼，诉讼程序 |The process of taking legal action.  It involves a dispute between two or more parties that is resolved through the court system.  |例句：The litigation lasted for five years and cost the company millions of dollars. 这场诉讼持续了五年，并使公司损失了数百万美元。

例句：They are involved in extensive litigation over the patent.  他们在专利权上卷入了旷日持久的诉讼。


近义词：lawsuit, legal action, court case, trial

反义词：settlement, agreement, compromise


词性:名词
