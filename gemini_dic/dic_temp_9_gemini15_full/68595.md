fantasies:/ˈfæntəsiːz/ | n. |幻想；幻想的事物 | Imaginary situations or events that are considered pleasant or exciting.  Fantasies often involve wish fulfillment or escapism. |例句：He spends hours lost in fantasies of wealth and power. 他花几个小时沉浸在财富和权力的幻想中。

例句：Her childhood fantasies often involved flying and talking to animals. 她童年的幻想经常包括飞翔和与动物交谈。


近义词：dreams, daydreams, illusions, imaginings |反义词：realities, facts
