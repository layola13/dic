glows:/ɡloʊz/| v.|发光；发热；充满活力|To shine with a soft light; to emit light or heat; to be filled with energy or excitement.|例句：The fire glows warmly in the hearth.壁炉里的火温暖地燃烧着。|例句：Her face glowed with happiness.她的脸上洋溢着幸福。|近义词：shines, radiates, beams|反义词：darkens, dims, fades


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun glows brightly today. = 太阳今天明亮地照耀着。
The embers glow faintly in the darkness. = 余烬在黑暗中微弱地发光。

一般过去时:
The city glowed with a million lights. = 城市闪烁着百万盏灯光。
His cheeks glowed after the run. = 他跑步后脸颊红润。

现在完成时:
The fire has glowed all night. = 火整夜都在燃烧。
She has glowed with confidence since her promotion. = 自从升职以来，她一直充满自信。

过去完成时:
The sunset had glowed brilliantly before fading. = 日落之前辉煌地燃烧着。
The coals had glowed red hot before we added more wood. = 我们添加更多木柴之前，煤炭已经烧得通红。

将来时:
The stars will glow in the night sky. = 星星将在夜空中闪耀。
The lamp will glow softly in the room. = 灯将在房间里柔和地发光。

虚拟语气:
If the sun glowed brighter, we could see better. = 如果太阳更亮，我们就能看得更清楚。
I wish my artwork glowed with more vibrancy. = 我希望我的作品更鲜艳。

被动语态:
The room was glowed with warm light. = 房间里充满了温暖的光线。(此用法较少见，通常用被动语态表达"被照亮")
The painting is glowed with vibrant colors. = 这幅画充满了鲜艳的色彩。(此用法较少见，通常用被动语态表达"被涂上")


疑问句:
Does the lava glow intensely? = 岩浆剧烈燃烧吗？
Does the gemstone glow in the dark? = 宝石在黑暗中发光吗？

否定句:
The screen doesn't glow anymore. = 屏幕不再发光了。
The fire didn't glow as brightly as it should have. = 火没有像应该的那样明亮地燃烧。
