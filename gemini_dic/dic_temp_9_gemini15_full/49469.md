deluged:/ˈdɛlʌdʒd/| v.|淹没；使应接不暇|To overwhelm with a large quantity of something, especially water or information.|The city was deluged by the heavy rain.  这座城市被大雨淹没了。|The company was deluged with applications for the job.  公司收到了大量的求职申请。|inundated, overwhelmed, swamped|drained, dried|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain deluges the streets every afternoon. = 下午雨水经常淹没街道。
He deluges his friends with emails. = 他用邮件淹没他的朋友们。


一般过去时:
The town was deluged by the flood. = 城镇被洪水淹没了。
They were deluged with phone calls after the announcement. = 公布之后，他们接到了大量的电话。


现在完成时:
The coastal areas have been deluged by the storm. = 沿海地区已经被风暴淹没了。
We have been deluged with orders since the new product launched. = 新产品发布以来，我们收到了大量的订单。


过去完成时:
The basement had been deluged before the pump was repaired. = 水泵修理之前，地下室已经被淹了。
The inbox had been deluged with spam emails before he cleared them. = 他清理之前，收件箱已经被垃圾邮件淹没了。


将来时:
The low-lying areas will be deluged if the river bursts its banks. = 如果河水决堤，低洼地区将被淹没。
We expect to be deluged with calls when the sale starts. = 我们预计促销开始时将接到大量的电话。


虚拟语气:
If the dam broke, the valley would be deluged. = 如果大坝决堤，山谷将被淹没。
If I had known they would deluge me with questions, I would have prepared better. = 如果我知道他们会用问题淹没我，我会准备得更好。


被动语态:
The fields were deluged with water. = 田地被水淹没了。
The office was deluged with complaints. = 办公室收到了大量的投诉。


疑问句:
Was the village deluged by the recent storm? = 最近的风暴是否淹没了村庄？
Will they be deluged with orders after the television advertisement? = 电视广告播出后，他们会接到大量的订单吗？


否定句:
The crops were not deluged by the light rain. = 轻微的雨水并没有淹没庄稼。
He wasn't deluged with work that day. = 那天他的工作并不繁重。
