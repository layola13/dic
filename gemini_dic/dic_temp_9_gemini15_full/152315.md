pattys:/ˈpætiz/| n.|肉饼；馅饼|Small, flat cakes of ground meat, often served in a hamburger.  Patty is the singular form.|例句：She made delicious beef pattys for the barbecue. 她为烧烤做了美味的牛肉饼。

例句：I ordered two veggie pattys on my burger. 我在我的汉堡里点了两个素食肉饼。


近义词：burgers, patties, meat cakes|反义词：None.
