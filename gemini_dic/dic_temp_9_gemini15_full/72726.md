foe:/foʊ/| n. |敌人，仇敌 |A foe is an enemy or opponent.  The term often suggests a personal or long-standing antagonism, rather than simply a rival. |例句：He considered his business rival a bitter foe. 他认为他的商业对手是他的死敌。|例句：The two countries were sworn foes for centuries. 这两个国家几个世纪以来一直是世仇。|近义词：enemy, adversary, opponent |反义词：friend, ally, comrade
