meliorism:/ˈmiːliərɪzəm/| n. |改良主义；向善主义|The belief that the world can be made better by human effort.  This philosophy emphasizes the importance of continuous improvement and progress through human action, often focusing on social and political reform.|Example:  The meliorism of the Progressive Era led to significant social and political changes in the United States.  进步时代的改良主义导致了美国社会和政治的重大变革。|近义词：optimism, progressivism, reformism|反义词：pessimism, nihilism, fatalism|名词
