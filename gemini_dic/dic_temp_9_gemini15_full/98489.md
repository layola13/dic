instalment:/ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ | n. |分期付款；一部分；（广播、电视节目的）一集 | A payment of a sum of money at regular intervals.  It can also refer to one part of a larger whole, such as a book or series. | 例句：The new television series will be shown in weekly instalments. 这部新电视剧将分周播出。

She paid for the car in monthly instalments. 她分期付款买车。


近义词：payment, part, episode, installment (US spelling) | 反义词：lump sum, entirety


词源：源自法语“installer” (to install),  其名词形式演变为“instalment”。  The -ment suffix indicates a result or action.
