hypermodernist:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɒdənɪst/ | adj. | 超现代主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of hypermodernism, an artistic or intellectual movement that reacts against or transcends modernism.  It often emphasizes irony, pastiche, and a self-conscious awareness of artistic conventions.  | Example: The artist's hypermodernist sculptures challenged traditional notions of form and function.  这位艺术家的超现代主义雕塑挑战了形式和功能的传统观念。 | 近义词：postmodern, contemporary, avant-garde | 反义词：traditional, classical, modernist
