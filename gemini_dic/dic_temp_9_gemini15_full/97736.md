initialises:/ɪˈnɪʃəlaɪz/ | vt. |初始化；启动；赋初值 | To set something to its initial state or value, often at the start of a process or program.  This involves assigning initial values to variables, configuring settings, or preparing a system for operation. | The program initialises the memory before it starts running. = 程序在运行之前初始化内存。  The computer initialises the hard drive during the boot process. = 电脑在启动过程中初始化硬盘。 | initialise, initiate, start, begin |  terminate, end, stop | 动词
