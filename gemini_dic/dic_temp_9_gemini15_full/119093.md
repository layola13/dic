metrics'/ˈmɛtrɪks/| n.|指标，度量标准|A system or set of principles used to measure something.|The company uses various metrics to track its progress. 这家公司使用各种指标来跟踪其进度。|standards, measurements, indicators|qualitative aspects.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
What metrics are used to assess performance? = 使用哪些指标来评估绩效？
The company uses sales metrics to determine its success. = 公司使用销售指标来衡量其成功。

What metrics did they use in the last quarter? = 上个季度他们使用了哪些指标？
