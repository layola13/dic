mitigating:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪtɪŋ/| v. |减轻；缓和；减轻（负面影响）|To make something less harmful, serious, or painful.  |The government is taking steps to mitigate the effects of the drought. 政府正在采取措施以减轻干旱的影响。| alleviate, lessen, reduce, moderate | aggravate, exacerbate, worsen | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are mitigating the risks of the project. = 他们正在减轻该项目的风险。
We mitigate potential problems before they arise. = 我们在问题出现之前就减轻潜在的风险。


一般过去时:
The company mitigated the damage caused by the accident. = 公司减轻了事故造成的损失。
He mitigated his sentence by pleading guilty. = 他通过认罪来减轻了他的刑罚。


现在完成时:
They have mitigated the impact of the crisis. = 他们已经减轻了危机的冲击。
We have mitigated the environmental damage.  = 我们已经减轻了环境破坏。


过去完成时:
They had mitigated the risks before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经减轻了风险。
She had mitigated the situation before her boss arrived. = 在老板到达之前，她已经缓和了局面。


将来时:
They will mitigate the effects of climate change. = 他们将减轻气候变化的影响。
We will mitigate the potential damage to the environment. = 我们将减轻对环境的潜在损害。


虚拟语气:
If they had mitigated the risks earlier, the outcome would have been better. = 如果他们早些减轻风险，结果会更好。
I wish they had mitigated the damage more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地减轻损失。


被动语态:
The risks were mitigated by careful planning. = 通过仔细的计划，风险得到了减轻。
The impact of the storm was mitigated by the emergency services. = 暴风雨的影响被紧急服务部门减轻了。


疑问句:
How can we mitigate the negative consequences? = 我们如何才能减轻负面影响？
What measures are being taken to mitigate the pollution? = 正在采取什么措施来减轻污染？


否定句:
They did not mitigate the situation effectively. = 他们没有有效地缓解局势。
The company failed to mitigate the risks. = 公司未能减轻风险。


PHRASE->
mitigate damage = 减轻损失
mitigate risk = 降低风险
mitigate harm = 减轻损害
mitigate the effects of = 减轻…的影响
mitigating circumstances = 减轻处罚的情况
