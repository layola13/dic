goggling:/ˈɡɒɡlɪŋ/ | v. | 瞪眼；眩晕；| To stare with one's eyes wide open, often in surprise, amazement, or fear.  |例句：The child goggled at the enormous dinosaur skeleton. 孩子瞪大眼睛看着巨大的恐龙骨架。|例句：The bright light made me goggle.  刺眼的光让我眼睛睁得很大。|近义词：staring, glaring, gawking |反义词：squinting, blinking
