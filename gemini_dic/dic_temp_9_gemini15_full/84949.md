haphazardly:/ˌhæpˈhæzərdli/ | adv. | 随意地；偶然地；杂乱无章地 | In a haphazard manner; without plan, order, or care.  | The garden was haphazardly planted, with flowers blooming wherever they chose. 花园里种满了花，随意地开着。 | carelessly, randomly, aimlessly | methodically, systematically, deliberately
