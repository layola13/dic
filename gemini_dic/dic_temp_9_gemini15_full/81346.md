graphites:/ˈɡræfaɪts/| n.|石墨|A crystalline form of carbon, a greyish-black, soft, and flaky mineral that conducts electricity and is used in pencils, lubricants, and electrodes.|例句：The pencil lead is actually made of graphite.  这支铅笔芯实际上是由石墨制成的。|例句：Graphite is used in many industrial applications. 石墨在许多工业应用中都有使用。|近义词：carbon (in some contexts)|反义词：diamond (in terms of hardness and structure)
