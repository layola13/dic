revisited:/ˌriːˈvɪzɪtɪd/| v. | 再访，重新审视，重新讨论 | To visit again; to consider or examine again.  |例句：We revisited the old house where we spent our childhood. 我们重访了我们童年时住过的老房子。

例句：The committee revisited the proposal after receiving new data.  委员会在收到新的数据后重新审议了该提案。


近义词：re-examined, reviewed, reconsidered, revisited, re-evaluated | 反义词：ignored, neglected

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They revisit their favorite restaurant every week. = 他们每周都重访他们最喜欢的餐馆。
He revisits the past in his memoirs. = 他在回忆录中回顾过去。


一般过去时:
She revisited her decision after careful consideration. = 她经过仔细考虑后重新考虑了她的决定。
We revisited the museum after many years. = 多年后，我们又参观了那家博物馆。


现在完成时:
They have revisited the topic several times. = 他们已经多次重新讨论过这个话题。
I have revisited that memory many times. = 我已经多次回忆起那段记忆。


过去完成时:
He had revisited the scene of the accident before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经重返了事故现场。
She had revisited her childhood home numerous times. = 她已经多次重返她的童年故居。


将来时:
We will revisit this issue in the next meeting. = 我们将在下次会议上再次讨论这个问题。
They will revisit the project plan next week. = 他们下周将重新审视项目计划。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would revisit my old university. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重返我的旧大学。
I wish I could revisit that magical summer. = 我真希望我能重温那个神奇的夏天。


被动语态:
The proposal was revisited by the board. = 该提案被董事会重新审议。
The old house was revisited by its former owners. = 这栋老房子被以前的主人再次拜访。


疑问句:
Have you ever revisited your hometown? = 你曾经回过家乡吗？
Will they revisit the agreement before signing? = 他们在签字之前会重新审阅协议吗？


否定句:
He did not revisit the subject during the discussion. = 他在讨论中没有再次提及这个话题。
They have not revisited their earlier conclusions. = 他们没有重新审视他们早期的结论。
