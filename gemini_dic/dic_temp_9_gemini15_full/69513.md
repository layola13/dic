fell-out: /felˈaʊt/ | v. | 争吵；脱落；（头发）脱落；（牙齿）脱落 | To have a serious argument or disagreement; to become detached or separated from something; (of hair or teeth) to come out. |  例句：They had a terrible fell-out and haven't spoken since. 他们发生了激烈的争吵，从那以后就没说过话了。  |近义词：quarrel, dispute, argument,  shed, drop | 反义词：reconcile, agree,  attach, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They often fell out over trivial matters. = 他们经常为一些琐事争吵。
My hair falls out a lot in the spring. = 我春天掉很多头发。


一般过去时:
We fell out after a heated debate. = 在一场激烈的辩论后，我们吵了一架。
Several of his teeth fell out during the accident. = 在事故中，他的几颗牙齿掉了出来。


现在完成时:
We haven't fallen out since that incident. = 从那件事以来，我们再也没有吵过架了。
A significant amount of his hair has fallen out due to stress. = 由于压力，他掉了大量的头发。


过去完成时:
They had fallen out several times before finally separating. = 在最终分开之前，他们已经吵过几次架了。
Before the surgery, several of her teeth had fallen out. = 手术前，她的几颗牙齿已经掉了。


将来时:
I think we will fall out if he continues to behave like this. = 我认为如果他继续这样表现，我们会吵架的。
With age, more of your hair will fall out. = 随着年龄增长，你的头发会掉得更多。


虚拟语气:
If we had fallen out, I would have been devastated. = 如果我们吵架了，我会非常沮丧。
If all my teeth fell out, I would go to the dentist immediately. = 如果我的牙齿都掉了，我会立即去看牙医。


被动语态:
The argument was eventually resolved before they fell out completely. = 在他们彻底闹翻之前，这场争论最终得到了解决。(This is a less common passive usage;  "fell out" is generally active.)
The leaves were falling out of the trees. = 树叶从树上掉下来了。（这个是被动语态更自然的例子）


疑问句:
Have you and your brother ever fallen out? = 你和你哥哥曾经吵过架吗？
Is your hair falling out more than usual? = 你的头发脱落比平时多吗？


否定句:
They haven't fallen out in years. = 他们多年来没有吵过架了。
My teeth haven't fallen out, thankfully. = 谢天谢地，我的牙齿没有脱落。


PHRASE->
fall out of love = 失去爱情
fall out with someone = 与某人争吵
fall out of favor = 失宠
fall out of use = 废弃不用
fall out of line = 脱离队伍
