outwit:/ˌaʊtˈwɪt/| vt.|智胜，以智取胜|to defeat or trick someone using intelligence and cleverness|The cunning fox outwitted the hunter by leading him on a chase through the forest.狡猾的狐狸通过引诱猎人穿过森林进行追逐而智胜了他。|outmaneuver, outsmart, trick|be outsmarted by, be defeated by

She outwits the security system with ease. = 她轻松地智胜了安全系统。


一般过去时:
