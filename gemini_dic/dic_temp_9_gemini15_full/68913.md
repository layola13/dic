fastuous:/fæˈstuːəs/| adj.|炫耀的；浮夸的；铺张的| ostentatious or vain; excessively proud of oneself; given to boastfulness.  It suggests a display of wealth, importance, or talent intended to impress others, often in a way that is considered excessive or tasteless. |The fastuous display of wealth at the party was off-putting to many guests.|近义词：ostentatious, flamboyant, showy, pretentious|反义词：modest, humble, unassuming
