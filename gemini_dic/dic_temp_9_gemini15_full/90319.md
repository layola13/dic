housewives:/ˈhaʊsˌwaɪvz/| n.|家庭主妇|A housewife is a woman whose main job is taking care of her home and family.  This term is now often considered outdated and potentially sexist, with many preferring terms like "homemaker" or simply "stay-at-home mom/parent".|例句：Many housewives in the 1950s dedicated themselves to raising families and maintaining homes.  五十年代许多家庭主妇都致力于养育家庭和维持家务。|近义词：homemakers, stay-at-home mothers/parents|反义词：career woman, working woman


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She manages the household budget. = 她管理家里的预算。
He cooks dinner for the family. = 他为家人做饭。


一般过去时:
She baked a cake for the children's party. = 她为孩子们的派对烤了一个蛋糕。
He cleaned the house thoroughly. = 他彻底打扫了房子。


现在完成时:
She has raised three children. = 她养育了三个孩子。
He has maintained the garden beautifully. = 他把花园打理得非常漂亮。


过去完成时:
She had already prepared dinner before they arrived. = 他们到达之前，她已经准备好晚餐了。
He had finished all the chores before going out. = 他出门之前已经完成了所有的家务。


将来时:
She will organize a family gathering next weekend. = 她下周末将组织一次家庭聚会。
He will renovate the kitchen next month. = 他下个月将装修厨房。


虚拟语气:
If she had more time, she would take up painting. = 如果她有更多时间，她会开始绘画。
He wished he could spend more time with his family. = 他希望他能花更多时间和家人在一起。


被动语态:
The house was cleaned regularly. = 房子定期打扫。
Dinner is prepared every evening. = 晚餐每天晚上都准备。


疑问句:

Have they finished their household chores? = 他们完成家务了吗？


否定句:
She doesn't work outside the home. = 她不在家外面工作。
He didn't have time to do the laundry. = 他没有时间洗衣服。
