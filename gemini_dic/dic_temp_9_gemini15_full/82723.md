guided:/ˈɡaɪdɪd/ | adj. |引导的；指导的；受引导的 |  Having or showing guidance; directed or controlled.  |例句：The guided tour of the museum was very informative.  博物馆的导游讲解非常丰富。|例句：The guided meditation helped me relax.  引导冥想帮助我放松。|近义词：directed, led, instructed |反义词：unsupervised, unguided, uncontrolled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher guided the students through the lesson. = 老师引导学生们学习这节课。
He guides his team to success. = 他引导他的团队走向成功。

一般过去时:
She guided me to the nearest station. = 她引导我到最近的车站。
They guided the lost hikers back to safety. = 他们引导迷路的徒步旅行者们安全返回。

现在完成时:
The government has guided the country through economic crisis. = 政府引导国家渡过经济危机。
We have guided many students to their dream schools. = 我们已经引导许多学生进入他们梦想的学校。

过去完成时:
She had guided him before, so he knew what to expect. = 她之前曾引导过他，所以他知道会发生什么。
They had guided the project to a successful conclusion. = 他们已经将项目引导到圆满结束。

将来时:
He will guide us through the process step-by-step. = 他将一步一步地引导我们完成这个过程。
They will guide the new employees during their training. = 他们会在培训期间指导新员工。

虚拟语气:
If I had been guided properly, I would have succeeded. = 如果我被正确引导，我就会成功。
I wish I had been guided earlier in my career. = 我希望在我职业生涯的早期就能得到指导。

被动语态:
The students were guided through the experiment. = 学生们在实验中得到了指导。
The project was guided by a team of experts. = 该项目由专家团队指导。

疑问句:
Are the visitors guided by a tour leader? = 游客们是由导游引导的吗？
Was the ship guided by radar? = 这艘船是由雷达引导的吗？

否定句:
He wasn't guided by his conscience. 他没有遵循自己的良心行事。
The lost hikers were not guided to safety. 迷路的徒步旅行者们没有得到引导而安全返回。
