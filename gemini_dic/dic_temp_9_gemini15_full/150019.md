The term can also be used as an adjective to describe the color of a horse or other things.|例句：He owns a beautiful palomino stallion. 他拥有一匹漂亮的帕洛米诺种公马。

例句：The palomino mare was a stunning sight. 这匹帕洛米诺母马真是令人惊艳。


近义词：golden, light cream, flaxen (for the coat color) |反义词：dark, black, brown (for the coat color) |词性:n. adj.
