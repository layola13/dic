nonabiding:/ˌnɒnəˈbaɪdɪŋ/| adj. |不遵守的；不服从的；不固定的|Not abiding; not adhering to; not permanent.  This word suggests a lack of adherence to rules, laws, or agreements, or a lack of permanence or stability. |Example:  The nonabiding nature of the truce led to renewed fighting.  不遵守停战协定的性质导致了战斗的再次爆发。The nonabiding spirit of the youth led him into trouble.  年轻人不守规矩的精神给他带来了麻烦。|近义词：disobedient, unruly, transient, impermanent|反义词：obedient, compliant, permanent, abiding
