idf:/ˈaɪdiːɛf/|abbr.|逆文档频率|Inverse Document Frequency,  a measure of how much information the word provides.  It is the inverse of the document frequency.  A high IDF indicates that the word is rare and contains more information.  It's commonly used in TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency), a weighting scheme used in information retrieval and text mining.|例句：The IDF score for the word "the" is very low because it appears in almost every document.  逆文档频率分数对于单词“the”非常低，因为它出现在几乎每篇文档中。|近义词：Rarity measure,  Inverse term frequency|反义词：Term Frequency (TF)
