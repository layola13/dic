directly:/daɪˈrektli/ | adv. |直接地，径直地 | Directly means in a straight line, without anything in between, or immediately. It can also mean frankly or openly. |例句：He walked directly to the door. 他径直走向门。

例句：She directly addressed the issue. 她直接解决了这个问题。


近义词：immediately, straight, frankly, openly | 反义词：indirectly, circuitously


词源:  The word "directly" comes from the Old French word "directement," which itself derives from the Latin word "directus," meaning "straight," "right," or "correct."  The suffix "-ly" is an adverbial suffix indicating manner.
