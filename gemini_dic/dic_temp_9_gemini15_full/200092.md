ski-plane: /ˈskiːpleɪn/ | n. |水上飞机 | A type of aircraft equipped with skis instead of wheels, allowing it to land and take off on snow or ice.  | Example: The ski-plane landed smoothly on the frozen lake. 水上飞机平稳地降落在冰冻的湖面上。 |近义词：snowplane,  |反义词：无
