non-provocative: /ˌnɑːn prəˈvɑːkətɪv/ | adj. | 不激怒的，不挑衅的 | Not likely to provoke a reaction, especially anger or offense.  It describes something that is calm, inoffensive, and unlikely to cause a dispute or argument. |


例句：

The doctor used a non-provocative approach to examine the patient.  医生采用了一种不激怒病人的方法来检查病人。

He delivered a non-provocative speech, aiming for unity rather than division. 他发表了一篇不具挑衅性的演讲，目的是为了团结而不是分裂。


近义词：

inoffensive, harmless, unprovocative, calming, soothing, peaceful


反义词：

provocative, inflammatory, aggressive, antagonizing, irritating, offensive


词性：形容词
