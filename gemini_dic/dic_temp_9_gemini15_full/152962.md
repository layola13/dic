pedicurist:/ˈpɛdɪkjʊrɪst/| n. |修脚师|A pedicurist is a professional who provides pedicures, which are cosmetic treatments for the feet and toenails.  They typically clean, trim, and shape toenails, remove calluses and dead skin, and may apply nail polish or other treatments. |例句：I have an appointment with my pedicurist next week.  下周我和我的修脚师预约了。|例句：The pedicurist used a special tool to remove the ingrown toenail. 修脚师用特殊的工具去除嵌甲。|近义词：nail technician, foot care specialist |反义词：无
