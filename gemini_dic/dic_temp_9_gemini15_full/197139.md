shanghaier:/ˌʃæŋhaɪˈər/| n. |上海人；上海风格的事物|A person from Shanghai, or something characteristic of Shanghai.  The term can refer to a person, a style, or a product originating from Shanghai.|例句：He's a Shanghaier, born and raised in the city. 他是上海人，在上海土生土长。  That restaurant serves authentic Shanghaier cuisine. 那家餐馆供应正宗的上海菜。|近义词：Shanghai native, Shanghai resident |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "Beijinger" (北京人) or other regional identifiers.
