preemploying:/ˌpriːɪmˈplɔɪɪŋ/| v.| 预先雇佣；提前雇用 |  The act of hiring someone before they are actually needed, often for a specific project or task.  This can involve training or onboarding activities in advance. |例句：The company is preemploying several engineers for the upcoming project.  这家公司正在为即将到来的项目预先雇佣几名工程师。

The construction company is preemploying skilled laborers in anticipation of the spring building boom.  这家建筑公司正在预先雇用熟练工人，以应对春季建筑业的繁荣。


近义词：pre-hiring, advance hiring,  提前招聘


反义词：post-hiring,  事后雇佣


词性：动词
