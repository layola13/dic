hierar:/ˈhaɪərɑːrki/| n. |等级制度；层次结构|A system or organization in which people or groups are ranked one above the other according to status or authority.  It often involves a clear chain of command and defined roles within the system.|例句：The company has a strict hierarchy, with the CEO at the top.  这家公司等级森严，CEO 位居顶端。|例句：Social hierarchies exist in many animal species as well as in human societies.  许多动物物种以及人类社会中都存在社会等级制度。|近义词：gradation, ranking, stratification|反义词：equality, egalitarianism|名词
