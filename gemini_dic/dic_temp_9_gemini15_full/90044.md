hospitalised: /ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzd/ /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪzd/ | v. | 住院治疗 |  To be admitted to a hospital for treatment.  The verb is typically used in the passive voice. | 例句：He was hospitalised after the car accident. 他在车祸后住院治疗了。

The patient was hospitalised for observation.  病人住院观察。


近义词：admitted to hospital, hospitalized (US spelling) | 反义词：discharged from hospital, released from hospital | 词性: Verb
