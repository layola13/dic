moondog:/ˈmuːndɔːɡ/|/ˈmuːnˌdɒɡ/| n. |流浪汉；疯子；（尤指）古怪的人|A moondog is a homeless person, often associated with eccentric or unusual behavior.  The term carries a slightly derogatory connotation, suggesting someone who is out of touch with societal norms.|Example: He lived like a moondog, sleeping under bridges and scavenging for food. 他像个流浪汉一样生活，睡在桥下，捡垃圾吃。|近义词：vagrant, tramp, bum, eccentric, oddball|反义词：homeowner, respectable citizen

The etymology of "moondog" is uncertain but likely refers to the erratic, unpredictable behavior often associated with homelessness and mental illness, likened to the unpredictable nature of the moon and its phases.
