deprecations:/ˌdeprɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |弃用，废弃；贬低；非难 | The act of deprecating something; the state of being deprecated.  It often refers to features or functionalities in software that are outdated or considered harmful and are scheduled for removal in future versions.  It can also refer to the act of expressing disapproval of something. | Example: The deprecations in the new software update mean we need to rewrite some of our code.  弃用新软件更新中的功能意味着我们需要重写部分代码。  The company issued a public statement filled with deprecations of its competitor's product. 公司发表了一份公开声明，其中充满了对竞争对手产品的贬低。 | 近义词：disapproval, condemnation, criticism, obsolescence | 反义词：approval, praise, commendation

USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company deprecates outdated software. = 公司不再使用过时的软件。
Many programmers deprecate the use of this function. = 许多程序员不再使用此函数。


一般过去时:
They deprecated the old system last year. = 他们去年弃用了旧系统。
The developer deprecated the feature due to security concerns. = 开发人员由于安全问题而弃用了该功能。


现在完成时:
The team has deprecated several modules in this release. = 该团队在本版本中已弃用多个模块。
They have deprecated the old library in favor of a newer one. = 他们已弃用旧库，转而使用较新的库。


过去完成时:
Before the update, they had deprecated the legacy code. = 更新之前，他们已弃用旧代码。
The company had deprecated the product before releasing a new version. = 公司在发布新版本之前已弃用了该产品。


将来时:
They will deprecate this function in the next update. = 他们将在下一个更新中弃用此函数。
The library will be deprecated next year. = 该库将于明年被弃用。


虚拟语气:
If they deprecated that feature, it would solve many problems. = 如果他们弃用该功能，许多问题就能得到解决。
I wish they had deprecated this buggy system earlier. = 我希望他们早点弃用这个有问题的系统。


被动语态:
The feature was deprecated due to low usage. = 由于使用率低，该功能已被弃用。
The old API is being deprecated in favor of a newer one. = 旧的 API 正在被弃用，取而代之的是新的 API。


疑问句:
Has the feature been deprecated yet? = 该功能已被弃用了吗？
Will this library be deprecated soon? = 这个库很快就会被弃用吗？


否定句:
They haven't deprecated the old system yet. = 他们还没有弃用旧系统。
The company does not plan to deprecate this feature anytime soon. = 公司近期不打算弃用此功能。
