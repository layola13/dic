halloween:/ˌhæləʊˈiːn/| n. |万圣节|All Hallows' Eve, the night before All Saints' Day (November 1), celebrated especially by children who wear costumes and ask for candy.  It's a time associated with ghosts, goblins, and spooky decorations.|例句：The children went trick-or-treating on Halloween.孩子们在万圣节晚上挨家挨户要糖果。|例句：We carved pumpkins and decorated our house for Halloween. 我们雕刻了南瓜并装饰了我们的房子来庆祝万圣节。|近义词：All Hallows' Eve, All Saints' Eve|反义词：Christmas (a contrasting holiday)
