buster:/ˈbʌstər/| n.|破坏者；家伙；（尤指）孩子；|A buster is a person who destroys things, especially a child.  It can also be used as an informal term of address for a person, typically a male child or young man. |例句：He's a real buster, always breaking things. 他是个真正的破坏狂，总是把东西弄坏。

例句：That little buster got into my cookie jar again.  那个小家伙又偷吃了我的饼干。


近义词：destroyer, kid, lad, chap, fellow

反义词：creator, builder, repairer
