cayenne pepper:/kaɪˈen pɛpər/| n. |卡宴辣椒|A variety of chili pepper, *Capsicum frutescens*, known for its intense heat.  It is typically long, thin, and bright red or orange when ripe. Cayenne pepper is used as a spice in many cuisines, adding a fiery kick to dishes.  It's also used in some medications and topical treatments.|例句：The chef added a pinch of cayenne pepper to the stew for extra flavor.  厨师在炖菜中加了一撮卡宴辣椒粉来增加风味。

The dish had a delightful kick of cayenne pepper. 这道菜有令人愉快的卡宴辣椒的辣味。|近义词：chili pepper, red pepper, paprika (less heat)|反义词：无（反义词应为描述其味道的反义词，如mild pepper, sweet pepper等，但没有一个完全对应的单一词语)|名词
