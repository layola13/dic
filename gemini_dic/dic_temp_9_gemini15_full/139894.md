oddest:/ˈɒdəst/| adj.|最奇怪的，最古怪的 |Most strange or unusual.  It implies being exceptionally unusual or unexpected, often in a way that is slightly unsettling or amusing. |例句：That's the oddest thing I've ever seen! = 那是我看过的最奇怪的事情！

The oddest noise woke me up in the middle of the night. = 半夜一个最奇怪的声音把我吵醒了。|近义词：strangest, queerest, weirdest, most peculiar |反义词：most normal, most ordinary, most typical
