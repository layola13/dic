series:/ˈsɪəriːz/| n.|系列，连续，丛书|A series is a number of things, events, or people of a similar kind or related in some way, occurring one after another.|例句：The museum has a series of exhibitions on ancient civilizations.  博物馆有一系列关于古代文明的展览。|例句：A new series of books has just been published. 一套新书刚刚出版。|近义词：sequence, succession, string|反义词：singularity, individuality
