insurgence:/ɪnˈsɜːrdʒəns/| n. |暴动；叛乱；起义|The sudden insurgence caught the government by surprise.  这场突然的暴动让政府措手不及。|近义词：rebellion, uprising, revolt |反义词：submission, compliance

The people are experiencing a growing insurgence against the current regime. = 人民正在经历一场针对现任政权日益增长的暴动。
