nonsacred:/ˈnɒnˈseɪkrɪd/| adj.|非神圣的，世俗的|Not sacred; profane; secular.  The term refers to things or concepts that are not considered holy, divine, or consecrated.  It often contrasts with the sacred, implying a lack of religious or spiritual significance.|例句：The museum displays both sacred and nonsacred artifacts.  博物馆展出神圣和非神圣的文物。

The nonsacred nature of the meeting frustrated the religious leader.  会议的世俗性质让宗教领袖感到沮丧。|近义词：profane, secular, worldly, earthly, temporal|反义词：sacred, holy, consecrated, divine
