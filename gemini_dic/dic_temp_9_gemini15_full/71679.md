flaunt:/flɔːnt/| /flɑːnt/ | vt.  |炫耀；夸示 |To show off something in a very noticeable and often ostentatious way.  |He flaunted his wealth by driving a new sports car every week. 他每周开一辆新的跑车来炫耀他的财富。| show off, boast, parade | hide, conceal, downplay | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flaunts his success. = 他炫耀他的成功。
She flaunts her new jewelry. = 她炫耀她的新珠宝。

一般过去时:
He flaunted his wealth at the party. = 他在派对上炫耀他的财富。
She flaunted her knowledge of art. = 她炫耀她对艺术的知识。

现在完成时:
She has flaunted her achievements all year. = 她整年都在炫耀她的成就。
He has flaunted his connections to the press. = 他一直在向媒体炫耀他的关系。

过去完成时:
They had flaunted their power before the revolution. = 革命前他们曾炫耀过他们的权力。
She had flaunted her independence to her parents. = 她曾向父母炫耀过她的独立性。

将来时:
He will flaunt his new car at the race track. = 他将在赛道上炫耀他的新车。
She will flaunt her designs at the fashion show. = 她将在时装秀上炫耀她的设计。

虚拟语气:
If he were rich, he would flaunt his wealth. = 如果他富有，他会炫耀他的财富。
I wish I could flaunt my talents like that. = 我真希望我能像那样炫耀我的才能。

被动语态:
His success was flaunted by him. = 他的成功被他炫耀了。(较为少见，主动语态更常用)
Her beauty was flaunted in the magazine. = 她的美貌在杂志上被夸耀了。

疑问句:
Does he flaunt his intelligence? = 他炫耀他的智力吗？
Will she flaunt her new dress at the party? = 她会在派对上炫耀她的新裙子吗？

否定句:
He doesn't flaunt his connections. = 他不炫耀他的关系。
She didn't flaunt her knowledge. = 她没有炫耀她的知识。


PHRASE->
flaunt one's wealth = 炫耀财富
flaunt one's power = 炫耀权力
flaunt one's success = 炫耀成功
flaunt one's knowledge = 炫耀知识
flaunt one's beauty = 炫耀美貌

词源:
From Middle English flaunten "to wave, flutter," perhaps from Old French flaunter "to wave, flourish."  The modern sense of "show off" developed later.
