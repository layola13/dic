ripened:/ˈraɪpənd/| adj.|成熟的；完美的|The word "ripened" is the past participle of the verb "ripen," meaning to become ripe or mature. It describes something that has reached a state of full development or perfection, often used to describe fruit, but can also apply to other things, such as ideas, plans, or relationships. |例句：The ripened mangoes were sweet and juicy.  成熟的芒果又甜又多汁。The ripened plan was finally put into action.  完善的计划终于付诸实施了。|近义词：mature, fully developed, perfected|反义词：unripe, immature, undeveloped
