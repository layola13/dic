landholdings: /ˈlændˌhoʊldɪŋz/ | n. | 土地所有权；土地持有量 |  The collective term for land owned by a person, organization, or entity. It refers to the total amount of land under their possession and control, including farms, estates, or other properties.  |  例句：The family's landholdings spanned several counties.  这家人的土地所有权跨越了好几个县。

例句：The company's vast landholdings are a valuable asset.  这家公司广阔的土地所有权是一项宝贵的资产。


近义词：estates, properties, holdings, acreage


反义词：landlessness (lack of land ownership)


词性: 名词
