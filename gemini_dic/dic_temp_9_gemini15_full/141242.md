op-arts:/ˈɒp ɑːrts/| n.|欧普艺术|Optical art, also known as Op Art, is a style of visual art that makes use of optical illusions.  Op art typically uses geometric patterns, repetition, and contrasting colors to create the illusion of movement, vibration, or depth. The aim is to stimulate the viewer's visual system and create a dynamic visual experience. Op art pieces often appear to change or move when viewed from different angles or distances.|The painting was a stunning example of op-arts, its swirling patterns creating a mesmerizing effect.|近义词：optical illusion, kinetic art|反义词：representational art, realistic art
