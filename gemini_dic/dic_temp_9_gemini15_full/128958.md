ninnyish: /ˈnɪniʃ/ | adj. | 傻气的；愚蠢的；笨拙的 |  Characterized by foolishness or silliness; simple-minded.  It suggests a lack of intelligence or common sense, often in a harmless or endearing way. | 例句：His ninnyish attempts at humor fell flat.  他傻里傻气的幽默尝试完全失败了。

His ninnyish grin made everyone uncomfortable. 他傻乎乎的傻笑让每个人都感到不舒服。


近义词：foolish, silly, simple-minded, goofy, daft, imbecilic


反义词：clever, intelligent, wise, shrewd, astute


词性：形容词
