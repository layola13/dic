co-proprietor: /ˌkoʊproʊˈpraɪətər/ | n. |共有业主，共同所有人 |

英文解析:A co-proprietor is someone who jointly owns a business or property with one or more other people.  They share the responsibilities, profits, and liabilities associated with the ownership.

例句:

They are co-proprietors of a successful restaurant in the city. = 他们是城里一家成功餐厅的共有业主。

The building is owned by three co-proprietors. = 这栋楼由三个共有业主所有。


近义词: joint owner, partner, shareholder (in certain contexts)

反义词: sole proprietor, single owner


词性: noun
