innov:/ˈɪnɒv/| v.|创新，革新|to introduce something new|例句：The company is constantly innovating new products. 这家公司不断创新新产品。|近义词：create, invent, develop|反义词: stagnate, maintain status quo.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They innovate constantly to stay ahead of the competition. = 他们不断创新以保持竞争优势。
The company innovates in sustainable technologies. = 这家公司在可持续技术方面进行创新。


一般过去时:
They innovated a new marketing strategy last year. = 他们去年创新了一种新的营销策略。
The inventor innovated a revolutionary device. = 发明家发明了一种革命性的装置。


现在完成时:
They have innovated significantly in recent years. = 近年来，他们的创新非常显著。
The company has innovated its manufacturing process. = 这家公司对其制造流程进行了创新。


过去完成时:
They had innovated several products before launching this one. = 在推出这款产品之前，他们已经创新了几款产品。
The team had innovated a solution before the deadline. = 团队在截止日期前已经创新了一个解决方案。


将来时:
They will innovate new ways to reduce carbon emissions. = 他们将创新新的方法来减少碳排放。
The company will innovate in artificial intelligence. = 公司将在人工智能领域进行创新。


虚拟语气:
If they innovated more, they would be more successful. = 如果他们更有创新精神，他们会更成功。
I wish they innovated faster in this area. = 我希望他们在这一领域能更快地创新。


被动语态:
New methods were innovated to improve efficiency. = 创新了新的方法来提高效率。
A new approach is being innovated by the research team. = 研究团队正在创新一种新的方法。


疑问句:
How do they innovate so effectively? = 他们如何如此有效地进行创新？
What areas are they innovating in? = 他们在哪些领域进行创新？


否定句:
They did not innovate enough to meet the demands. = 他们的创新不足以满足需求。
The company hasn't innovated significantly in this sector. = 这家公司在这个领域没有进行重大创新。



PHRASE->
innovate in = 在...方面创新
innovate with = 用...创新
innovate upon = 在...的基础上创新
innovate for = 为...而创新
innovate through = 通过...创新
