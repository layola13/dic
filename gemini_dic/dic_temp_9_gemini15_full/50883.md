desertedly:/dəˈzɜːtɪdli/| adv.|荒凉地，无人地|Emphasizes the state of being deserted;  completely or strikingly empty of people.  It suggests a sense of loneliness and isolation.|例句：The old house stood desertedly on the hill.  那栋老房子孤零零地矗立在山上。
例句：The streets were desertedly quiet after the storm.  暴风雨过后，街道异常安静。|近义词：emptily, forlornly, bleakly|反义词：bustlingly, lively, crowdedly
