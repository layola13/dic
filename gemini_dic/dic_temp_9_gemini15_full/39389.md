conjuration:/kənˈdʒʊəreɪʃən/ | n. |咒语；祈祷；召唤；变戏法 |The act of conjuring; the use of magic or spells.  It can also refer to a summoning or calling forth, or even a strong appeal or entreaty. |例句：The magician's conjuration brought forth a dazzling display of lights. 魔术师的咒语带来了炫目的灯光表演。

The ancient ritual involved a complex conjuration to appease the gods.  古老的仪式包含一个复杂的咒语来安抚神灵。


近义词：incantation, enchantment, invocation, spell, summoning |反义词：disenchantment, banishment

词源:
From Middle English *conjuracioun, from Old French conjuration, from Latin coniūrātiōn-, from coniūrāre "to swear together," from con- "together" + iūrāre "to swear."  The meaning evolved from a sworn agreement to a magical incantation or summoning.
