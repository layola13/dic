reinfiltrates:/ˌriːˈɪnflɪtreɪts/ | v. | 重新渗透；再次渗入 | To infiltrate again; to penetrate or permeate again.  |例句：The enemy forces reinfiltrated the border after the ceasefire.  敌人停火后再次渗透边境。

例句：After the floodwaters receded, the contaminated water reinfiltrated the soil. 洪水退去后，受污染的水再次渗入土壤。


近义词：re-enter, seep back into, permeate again | 反义词：evacuate, withdraw, extract
