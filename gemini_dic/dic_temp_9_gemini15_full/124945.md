muffineer:/ˌmʌfɪˈnɪər/| n. |松饼师傅，松饼制作人 |A muffineer is a person who makes or sells muffins.  The term is informal and rarely used in formal contexts. It emphasizes the skill and dedication of someone specializing in muffin production.| Example:  "He wasn't just a baker, he was a true muffineer, his muffins legendary in the town." = 他不仅仅是一个面包师，他是一个真正的松饼师傅，他的松饼在镇上很有名。|近义词：muffin baker, pastry chef (more formal)|反义词：无
