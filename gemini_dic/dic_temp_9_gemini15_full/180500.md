regenerates:/rɪˈdʒɛnəreɪts/| v.|再生；恢复；重建|To regenerate means to grow or develop new tissue, cells, or organs to replace those that have been lost or damaged. It can also refer to the process of renewing or revitalizing something. |例句：The lizard regenerates its tail if it is lost.  壁虎失去尾巴后可以再生。

The forest is slowly regenerating after the fire. 森林在大火之后正在慢慢地再生。

近义词：renew, revive, restore, rebuild|反义词：destroy, ruin, devastate|词性:动词
