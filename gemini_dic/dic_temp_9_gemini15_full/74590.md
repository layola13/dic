freaked:/friːkt/| v.|吓坏了，惊慌失措|to become extremely frightened or upset; to make someone extremely frightened or upset |例句：I freaked out when I saw the spider. 我看到蜘蛛时吓坏了。

例句：The loud noise freaked her out completely.  巨大的噪音把她完全吓坏了。


近义词：scared, terrified, panicked, freaked out|反义词：calm, relaxed, composed|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He freaks out easily. = 他很容易吓到。
The loud music freaks me out. = 吵闹的音乐让我害怕。


一般过去时:
I freaked out when I saw the accident. = 我看到事故时吓坏了。
She freaked out at the news. = 她听到这个消息吓坏了。


现在完成时:
I've freaked out several times this week. = 我这周已经好几次吓坏了。
He's never freaked out before. = 他以前从未吓坏过。


过去完成时:
I had freaked out before he even started speaking. = 他还没开始说话我就吓坏了。
She had freaked out earlier that day. = 她那天早些时候就吓坏了。


将来时:
I think I'll freak out if I fail the exam. = 我想如果考试不及格我会吓坏的。
They will freak out when they see the bill. = 他们看到账单时会吓坏的。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a ghost, I would freak out. = 如果我看到鬼，我会吓坏的。
I wish I hadn't freaked out so much. = 我希望我当时没有那么害怕。


被动语态:
He was freaked out by the sudden noise. = 他被突如其来的噪音吓坏了。
The audience was freaked out by the horror movie. = 观众被恐怖电影吓坏了。


疑问句:
Did the movie freak you out? = 电影吓到你了么？
Does loud thunder freak you out? = 响雷会吓到你吗？


否定句:
The situation didn't freak me out. = 这种情况并没有吓到我。
I wasn't freaked out by the scary movie. = 我没有被那部恐怖片吓到。


PHRASE->
freak out about = 对...感到极度焦虑
freak someone out = 吓到某人
freak someone out completely = 把某人彻底吓坏
freak out over = 对...过度反应
to freak out = 吓坏，惊慌失措
