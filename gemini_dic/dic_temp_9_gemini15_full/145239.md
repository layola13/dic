overbitterly: /ˌoʊvərˈbɪtəli/ | adv. |极其痛苦地，极度悲痛地 |Extremely or excessively bitterly.  It implies a degree of bitterness that is beyond the usual or expected. |例句：She wept overbitterly at the news of her father's death. 她听到父亲去世的消息后，极其悲痛地哭泣。
He spoke overbitterly about his lost opportunities. 他极其痛苦地谈论着他失去的机会。|近义词：bitterly, intensely, profoundly, exceedingly |反义词：joyfully, happily, cheerfully |词性：副词
