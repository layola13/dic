restructure:/ˌriːˈstrʌktʃər/| vt.| 重组；改组；改建 | To reorganize something, typically a business or system, in order to improve its efficiency or effectiveness.  |例句：The company is restructuring its operations to reduce costs. 公司正在重组运营以降低成本。|例句：The government announced plans to restructure the healthcare system. 政府宣布了重组医疗体系的计划。|近义词：reorganize, revamp, reform |反义词：disorganize, dismantle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company restructures its departments annually. = 公司每年都会重组部门。
They restructure the curriculum to meet new standards. = 他们重组课程以满足新的标准。

一般过去时:
The company restructured its operations last year. = 公司去年重组了运营。
We restructured the sentence to make it clearer. = 我们改写了句子使其更清晰。

现在完成时:
The government has restructured the education system. = 政府已经重组了教育体系。
They have restructured their debt to improve their financial situation. = 他们已经重组了债务以改善财务状况。

过去完成时:
They had restructured the team before the new project started. = 新项目启动前，他们已经重组了团队。
The company had restructured its finances before the merger. = 公司在合并之前已经重组了财务。

将来时:
The company will restructure its marketing department next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度重组其市场部门。
We will restructure the organization to improve efficiency. = 我们将重组组织以提高效率。

虚拟语气:
If they restructured the company, it might be more profitable. = 如果他们重组公司，它可能会更赚钱。
It would be better if we restructured the report. = 如果我们重组报告会更好。

被动语态:
The company's operations were restructured last year. = 公司的运营去年被重组了。
The curriculum is being restructured to improve student learning. = 课程正在被重组以改善学生的学习。

疑问句:
Will the company restructure its sales team? = 公司会重组其销售团队吗？
Has the organization been restructured? = 组织已经被重组了吗？

否定句:
The company did not restructure its management team. = 公司没有重组其管理团队。
They haven't yet restructured their business model. = 他们还没有重组他们的商业模式。


PHRASE->
restructure a business = 重组企业
restructure a sentence = 改写句子
restructure a loan = 贷款重组
restructure a debt = 重组债务
restructure a team = 重组团队

词源:
re- (prefix indicating repetition or doing again) + structure (from Latin *struere, "to build")  表示“重新构建”
