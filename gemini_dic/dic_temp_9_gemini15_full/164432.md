preengineering: /ˌpriːˌɛndʒɪˈnɪərɪŋ/ | n. | 预备工程；工程预备课程 |  The study of foundational concepts and principles in engineering, typically taken by students before they specialize in a specific engineering discipline.  It often includes introductory courses in mathematics, physics, and computer science. |  例句：He took preengineering courses in college to prepare for his mechanical engineering major. 他在大学里修读了预备工程课程，为他的机械工程专业做准备。


例句：The preengineering program provides a solid foundation for students pursuing various engineering fields. 预备工程项目为学生攻读各个工程领域提供了坚实的基础。


近义词：engineering preparation, foundational engineering studies | 反义词：advanced engineering, specialized engineering
