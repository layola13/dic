nonreusable: /ˌnɒnˌjuːzəbəl/ | adj. |不可重复使用的，一次性的|Not capable of being used again; disposable.  The term emphasizes the inability to reuse the item, often implying a single use or a limited lifespan before becoming unusable.| Example:  The plastic cups are nonreusable and should be discarded after use.  这些塑料杯是不可重复使用的，用后应丢弃。|近义词：disposable, single-use, expendable|反义词：reusable, recyclable
