recipe:/ˈresəpi/ | n. |食谱，配方 |A set of instructions for preparing a particular dish, including a list of the ingredients.| 例句：I followed the recipe exactly, but the cake still didn't rise. 我严格按照食谱做了，但蛋糕还是没发起来。

例句：She has a secret recipe for her famous chocolate chip cookies. 她有她那著名的巧克力曲奇饼的秘方。|近义词：formula, method, instruction |反义词：无

This recipe makes four servings. = 这个食谱能做四份。
