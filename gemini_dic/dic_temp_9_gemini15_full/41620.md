corrasion:/kəˈreɪʒən/ | n. | 磨损；侵蚀 | The erosion or wearing away of a surface by friction.  Corrasion is a geological process that refers to the wearing away of rock surfaces by the abrasive action of other rocks and sediment carried by water, ice, or wind.  | Example: The corrasion of the riverbed was evident in the smooth, rounded stones.  河床的磨损在光滑的圆形石头上很明显。 |近义词：abrasion, erosion, attrition | 反义词：accretion, deposition | 名词
