reshouldering: /ˌriːˈʃoʊldəriŋ/ | v. |重新肩负；重新承担责任 |  The act of taking on a responsibility or task again, often after a period of inactivity or delegation.  It implies a renewed commitment or a resumption of a previous burden. | 例句：After a long break, she's reshouldering the project. = 经过长时间的休息后，她又重新承担了这个项目。  The company is reshouldering its marketing campaign after a disappointing quarter. = 公司在经历了一个令人失望的季度后，正在重新肩负起它的营销活动。 | 近义词：reassuming, retaking, shouldering again | 反义词：abandoning, relinquishing, delegating | 动词
