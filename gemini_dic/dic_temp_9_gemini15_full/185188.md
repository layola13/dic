retotal:/ˌriːˈtoʊtəl/ | vt. |重新计算，再计算 |To calculate the total of something again.  This often implies a correction or a more thorough calculation than the initial one. |例句：We had to retotal the invoice after discovering a mistake. 我们在发现错误后不得不重新计算发票总额。|例句：The accountant retotaled the figures to ensure accuracy. 会计重新计算了数字以确保准确性。 |近义词：recalculate, recalculate, recompute |反义词：N/A
