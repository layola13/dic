empathic:/ˈɛmˈpæθɪk/| adj.|有同理心的，感同身受的|Showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of another.|The empathic therapist helped her client process their trauma.  这位富有同理心的治疗师帮助她的客户处理他们的创伤。|近义词：sympathetic, compassionate, understanding|反义词：apathetic, unsympathetic, indifferent
