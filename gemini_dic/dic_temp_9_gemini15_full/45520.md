curettement:/ˌkjʊrɪtˈmɑːnt/| n. |刮除术；子宫刮除术 |The surgical removal of material from a cavity or surface, especially the uterus.  This procedure is often performed to obtain a tissue sample for diagnosis or to remove abnormal tissue. |Example: The doctor recommended a curettement to remove the uterine polyps. 医生建议进行子宫刮除术以去除子宫息肉。|近义词：scraping, cleansing, evacuation |反义词：implantation
