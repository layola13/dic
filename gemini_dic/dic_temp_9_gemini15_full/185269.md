retransmitting:/ˌriːtrænˈzmɪtɪŋ/| v. |重新发送，再发送 |The act of sending a signal or data again.  This often happens when a transmission fails or is incomplete.  It's a crucial part of ensuring reliable communication in systems like networks and satellite communication. |例句：The router is retransmitting the lost data packets. 路由器正在重新发送丢失的数据包。

The server is continuously retransmitting the signal to maintain a stable connection. 服务器不断重新发送信号以保持稳定的连接。|近义词：resend, rebroadcast, relay |反义词：receive, initially transmit
