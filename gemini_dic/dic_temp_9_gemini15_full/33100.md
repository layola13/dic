christenings:/ˈkrɪsənɪŋz/| n. |洗礼仪式；命名仪式 |The act of christening someone, or the ceremony itself.  It often refers to the Christian religious ceremony of baptism, but can also be used more generally to refer to any naming ceremony. |例句：The church was filled with family and friends for the christenings. 教堂里挤满了参加洗礼仪式的家人和朋友。


The christenings of the twins were held separately. 双胞胎的洗礼仪式分别举行。|近义词：baptisms, namings |反义词：None.
