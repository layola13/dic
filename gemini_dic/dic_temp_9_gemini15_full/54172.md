disentrance:/dɪsɪnˈtræns/| vt.|使…失去魅力；使…不再着迷；使…扫兴|To deprive of charm or enchantment; to disenchant; to disillusion.|例句：The mundane reality of daily life disentranced her from the romantic ideals she once held.  日常生活的平凡现实让她对曾经抱有的浪漫理想不再着迷。

例句：The magician's trick, once revealed, disentranced the audience. 魔术师的把戏一旦揭露，就让观众失去了兴趣。


近义词：disenchant, disillusion, disenthrall|反义词：entrance, enchant, fascinate|词性:动词
