salon:/səˈlɒn/|/ˈsælən/| n. |沙龙；客厅；美容院；展览室 |A salon is a social gathering, typically of intellectuals or artists, at which ideas are discussed.  It can also refer to a hairdressing or beauty salon, or a room in a house used for social gatherings. |例句：They held a salon in their elegant drawing-room. = 他们在他们优雅的客厅里举办了一个沙龙。 |例句：I'm going to the salon to get my hair cut. = 我要去美容院剪头发。|近义词：gathering, meeting, parlor, beauty parlor |反义词：None specifically applicable.
