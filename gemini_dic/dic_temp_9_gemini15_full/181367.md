reinvests:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛsts/ | vt. | 再投资；重新投资 | To invest (profits or capital) again, especially in the same business or type of business.  |例句：The company reinvests a significant portion of its profits each year.  公司每年将大部分利润再投资。|例句：He reinvested his earnings from the sale of his house into his new business. 他把他卖房的收入重新投资到他的新生意中。|近义词：reinvest, recapitalize, plough back |反义词：withdraw, divest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reinvests its profits. = 公司将利润再投资。
She reinvests her savings regularly. = 她定期将储蓄再投资。


一般过去时:
They reinvested the bonus in new equipment. = 他们将奖金再投资于新设备。
He reinvested his winnings from the lottery. = 他将他彩票中奖的钱再投资了。


现在完成时:
We have reinvested all our earnings this year. = 今年我们已经将所有收入再投资了。
She has reinvested her inheritance wisely. = 她已明智地将继承的遗产再投资了。


过去完成时:
They had reinvested their profits before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘前，他们已将利润再投资了。
The company had reinvested heavily in research and development. = 公司已大力投资研发。


将来时:
We will reinvest the surplus funds in expanding our operations. = 我们将把剩余资金再投资于扩大业务。
They will reinvest their profits in a new venture. = 他们将把利润再投资于一个新的企业。


虚拟语气:
If they reinvested their money, they would have more capital. = 如果他们将钱再投资，他们就会有更多资金。
I wish I had reinvested my money instead of spending it. = 我真希望我把钱再投资而不是花掉它。


被动语态:
The profits were reinvested in the business. = 利润被再投资于业务中。
A large sum of money was reinvested in the project. = 一大笔钱被再投资到该项目中。


疑问句:
Will the company reinvest its profits this year? = 公司今年会将利润再投资吗？
Does she reinvest her earnings regularly? = 她定期将收入再投资吗？


否定句:
The company did not reinvest its profits last year. = 公司去年没有将利润再投资。
He hasn't reinvested his savings yet. = 他还没将储蓄再投资。
