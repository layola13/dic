lynx:/lɪŋks/| n.|猞猁|A lynx is a medium-sized wild cat with tufted ears and a short tail.  They are known for their excellent eyesight and hunting skills.|例句：The lynx stalked its prey silently through the snow.  猞猁悄无声息地穿过雪地跟踪猎物。
The mountain lynx is a rare and elusive creature.  山猫是一种稀有而难以捉摸的生物。|近义词：wildcat, bobcat|反义词：domestic cat
