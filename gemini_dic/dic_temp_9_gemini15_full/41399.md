corncobs: /ˈkɔːrnkɒbz/ | n. |玉米棒子| The dried core of an ear of maize, to which the kernels are attached.  It is often used as animal feed or discarded after eating the kernels. |例句：The chickens enjoyed pecking at the leftover corncobs.  鸡很喜欢啄食剩下的玉米棒子。

例句：We used the corncobs to start a bonfire. 我们用玉米棒子生了篝火。


近义词：maize cob, cob


反义词：None (no direct antonym)


词性：名词
