expugn:/ɪkˈspʌn/| vt.|攻克；征服；驳斥|To overcome or conquer (a place or people) by force; to refute or disprove (an argument or theory).|例句：The army attempted to expugn the heavily fortified city.  军队试图攻克那座设防严密的城市。

例句：He tried to expugn the arguments presented by his opponent. 他试图驳斥对手提出的论点。


近义词：conquer, vanquish, refute, disprove|反义词：yield, surrender, concede, accept


词源：  From Latin *expugnare, from ex- "out" + pugnare "to fight".  The word's history reflects its meaning of forceful overcoming.
