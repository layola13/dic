rendered:/ˈrɛndərd/ | vt. | 使成为；翻译；表演；呈现；烘烤；熬制| To cause (something) to be in a specified state or condition; to translate (something, especially from one language into another); to perform (a piece of music, a role, etc.); to represent (something) artistically or in a particular way; to render fat by melting it down; to render down. |例句：The artist rendered the scene beautifully.  艺术家精美地描绘了这幅场景。

例句：The translator rendered the novel into English. 翻译将小说翻译成英文。


近义词：made, translated, presented, depicted, portrayed, interpreted | 反义词：destroyed, ruined, obscured, concealed
