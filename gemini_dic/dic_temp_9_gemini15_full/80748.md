gospel:/ˈɡɑːspəl/ /ˈɡɒspəl/| n.|福音，好消息，信条|The good news or message of salvation;  a set of principles or beliefs; a style of religious music.|例句：The gospel of Jesus Christ is central to Christianity. 基督教的核心是耶稣基督的福音。

例句：The gospel singer's voice filled the church. 福音歌手的歌声充满了教堂。


近义词：good news, message, creed, doctrine|反义词：bad news, ill tidings
