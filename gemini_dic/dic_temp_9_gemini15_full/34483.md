clayiest:/ˈkleɪiəst/| adj.|最粘的，最泥泞的|Most clay-like in texture; extremely clayey.  The clayiest soil is difficult to work with.|例句：The clayiest parts of the field were almost impossible to plow.  这块田里最粘的泥土几乎无法耕种。

The clayiest areas of the garden need better drainage. 花园里最粘的区域需要更好的排水。|近义词：muddiest, stickiest, gooiest |反义词：driest, sandiest
