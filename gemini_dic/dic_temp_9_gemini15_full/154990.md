petaloid:/ˈpɛtəlɔɪd/ | adj. |花瓣状的，似花瓣的 | Resembling a petal; having the form or appearance of a petal.  The term often describes structures in plants that have a petal-like appearance but are not true petals, such as modified leaves or sepals. | The orchid's staminodes were petaloid, adding to its delicate beauty.  兰花的退化雄蕊呈花瓣状，增加了它的精致美感。The petaloid sepals of the tulip mimicked the petals.郁金香的花瓣状萼片模仿了花瓣。 | petal-like, flower-like | non-petaloid (反义词相对较少使用，通常用描述性词语来表达非花瓣状的特征)
