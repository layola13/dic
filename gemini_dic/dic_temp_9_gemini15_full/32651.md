chit-chat:/tʃɪtʃæt/| n. v.|闲聊，聊天|Light, informal conversation; to engage in such conversation.|例句：We spent the afternoon in chit-chat. 我们一下午都在闲聊。
They were chit-chatting about their weekend plans. 他们在闲聊他们的周末计划。|近义词：gossip, small talk, banter|反义词：serious discussion, debate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chit-chat every day. = 他们每天都聊天。
We chit-chat during our coffee break. = 我们在咖啡休息时间聊天。

一般过去时:
They chit-chatted for hours. = 他们聊了好几个小时。
We chit-chatted about the latest movie. = 我们聊了最新的电影。

现在完成时:
They have chit-chatted all morning. = 他们一上午都在聊天。
We have chit-chatted about this topic before. = 我们之前讨论过这个话题。

过去完成时:
They had chit-chatted before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经聊过了。
We had chit-chatted for a while before realizing the time. = 我们聊了一会儿才意识到时间。

将来时:
They will chit-chat later. = 他们稍后会聊天。
We will chit-chat after the party. = 聚会后我们会聊天。

虚拟语气:
If they had more time, they would chit-chat longer. = 如果他们有更多时间，他们会聊更久。
I wish we could chit-chat more often. = 我希望我们能更经常地聊天。

被动语态: (Chit-chat is rarely used in the passive voice.)  The passive voice is not naturally used with this verb.

疑问句:
Do they chit-chat often? = 他们经常聊天吗？
Were they chit-chatting when you arrived? = 你到的时候他们在聊天吗？

否定句:
They don't chit-chat much. = 他们不太聊天。
We didn't chit-chat yesterday. = 我们昨天没有聊天。
