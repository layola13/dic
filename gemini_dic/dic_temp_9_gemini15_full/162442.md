preadmits:/ˌpriːədˈmɪts/| vt.|预先录取；提前录取|To admit or accept someone or something beforehand or in advance.|例句：The university preadmits students based on their high school grades.  大学根据高中成绩预先录取学生。|例句：The hospital preadmits patients for scheduled surgeries. 医院提前录取安排手术的病人。|近义词：admit in advance, accept beforehand|反义词：reject, refuse

He preadmits his mistakes readily. = 他欣然承认自己的错误。
