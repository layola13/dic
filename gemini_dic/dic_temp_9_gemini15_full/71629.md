flatlines:/ˈflætˌlaɪnz/| v.|生命体征平直；停滞不前；|To cease to show any vital signs; to become stagnant or inactive.|例句：His heart flatlined during surgery. 手术过程中他的心跳停止了。|例句：The project flatlined after the funding was cut.  资金被削减后，项目进展停滞了。|近义词：cease, stop, stagnate|反义词：improve, progress, thrive

USAGE->

The patient's heart flatlines. = 病人的心跳停止了。
The economy flatlines this year. = 今年经济停滞不前。
