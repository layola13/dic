by-elections:/ˈbaɪ ɪˈlɛkʃənz/ | n. | 补选 | Elections held to fill a vacancy that has occurred in a legislative body between general elections.  By-elections are typically held for a single seat, unlike general elections which cover all seats. | Example: The by-election in the constituency was triggered by the resignation of the sitting MP.  这次选区的补选是由现任国会议员辞职引发的。  The by-election saw a surprisingly high turnout.  这次补选的投票率出奇地高。| 近义词: supplementary elections, special elections | 反义词: general elections
