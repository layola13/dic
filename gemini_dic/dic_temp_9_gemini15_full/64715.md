eschatologist:/ˌɛskəˈtɒlədʒɪst/| n. |末世论者，研究末世学的人|A person who studies eschatology, the part of theology concerned with death, judgment, and the final destiny of the soul and of humankind.  |Example: The eschatologist delivered a lecture on the various interpretations of the Book of Revelation. 末世论者就《启示录》的不同解释做了一场讲座。|近义词：theologian (if focusing on theological aspects), prophet (if making predictions)|反义词：N/A
