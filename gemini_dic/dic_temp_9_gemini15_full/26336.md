buzzards:/ˈbʌzərdz/| n.|秃鹰；[俚]讨厌的人|A buzzard is a type of large bird of prey, known for its scavenging habits.  They are often seen circling overhead, looking for carrion.  The term can also be used informally to refer to a unpleasant or disliked person.|例句：Several buzzards circled the carcass of the dead deer. 几只秃鹰盘旋在死鹿的尸体上空。

例句：He's a real buzzard; always complaining and never helping. 他真是个讨厌鬼；总是抱怨，从不帮忙。


近义词：vulture, carrion bird, scavenger|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting helpfulness or pleasantness could be considered antonyms in the informal sense.|词性:名词
