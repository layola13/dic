czaristic:/zɑːˈrɪstɪk/| adj.|沙皇的；沙皇制度的；专制的|Relating to or characteristic of a czar or the czardom; autocratic or despotic.  The word evokes images of absolute rule and often implies oppression.|例句：The czaristic regime suppressed all opposition. 沙皇政权压制了所有的反对势力。  The czaristic system was characterized by its rigid social hierarchy. 沙皇制度的特点是其僵化的社会等级制度。|近义词：autocratic, despotic, tyrannical, authoritarian|反义词：democratic, liberal, egalitarian
