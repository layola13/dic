It is used primarily in scientific and technical contexts to form compound words related to fish or fish-like structures.  |Example: Ichthyoplankton refers to the fish eggs and larvae that drift in the water column.  鱼卵和幼鱼在水柱中漂浮。|近义词:piscine|反义词: (no direct antonym, context dependent)|名词
