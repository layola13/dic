elaborating:/ɪˈlæbəreɪtɪŋ/| v. |详述；详细阐述；精心制作|To explain or describe something in detail; to develop or improve something by adding details or features.|The professor elaborating on the theory of relativity.  教授正在详细阐述相对论。|He was elaborating on his travel experiences. 他正在详细讲述他的旅行经历。|simplifying, summarizing|concise, brief|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is elaborating his ideas in the meeting. 他正在会议上详细阐述他的想法。
She elaborates on the details of the project every week. 她每周都会详细说明项目的细节。


一般过去时:
The author elaborated on the historical context of the novel. 作者详细阐述了这部小说的历史背景。
She elaborated her point with numerous examples. 她用许多例子来详细阐述她的观点。


现在完成时:
He has elaborated a comprehensive plan for the future. 他已经制定了一个全面的未来计划。
The architect has elaborated on the design, adding more details. 建筑师已经详细阐述了设计，并添加了更多细节。


过去完成时:
They had elaborated a strategy before the negotiation began. 他们在谈判开始之前已经制定了一套策略。
She had elaborated her argument before presenting it to the committee.  她在向委员会提交论点之前已经详细阐述了她的论点。


将来时:

She will elaborate on the proposal in the upcoming meeting. 她将在即将召开的会议上详细说明该提案。


虚拟语气:
If he elaborated more clearly, everyone would understand. 如果他更清楚地阐述，每个人都会理解。
I wish he had elaborated on the implications of his findings. 我希望他能详细阐述其发现的含义。


被动语态:
The plan was elaborated by a team of experts.  该计划是由专家团队制定的。

疑问句:

否定句:
He did not elaborate further on the topic. 他没有进一步详细阐述这个话题。
She chose not to elaborate on the sensitive issue. 她选择不详细说明这个敏感问题。
