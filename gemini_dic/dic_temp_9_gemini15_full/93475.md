illustrate:/ˈɪləstreɪt/ | vt. | 说明；阐明；图解；例证 | To explain or make something clear by giving examples, charts, pictures, etc.; to serve as an example of something. |例句：The teacher illustrated the grammar rule with several examples. 老师用几个例子来说明语法规则。


The charts illustrate the rise in unemployment.图表显示了失业率的上升。


近义词：explain, exemplify, demonstrate, clarify | 反义词：obscure, confuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He illustrates his points with anecdotes. 他用轶事来说明他的观点。
The data illustrates the problem clearly. 数据清楚地说明了这个问题。


一般过去时:
She illustrated her lecture with slides. 她用幻灯片来说明她的讲座。
The author illustrated the story with vivid descriptions. 作者用生动的描写来阐述故事。


现在完成时:
His work has illustrated the importance of the subject. 他的工作已经说明了这个主题的重要性。
Many studies have illustrated the link between diet and health. 许多研究已经说明了饮食与健康之间的联系。


过去完成时:
The previous speaker had illustrated the same point. 前一位发言人已经说明了相同的观点。
They had illustrated the concept with multiple diagrams. 他们已经用多个图表来说明这个概念。


将来时:
She will illustrate her ideas with a presentation. 她将用演示文稿来说明她的想法。
The report will illustrate the impact of climate change. 报告将说明气候变化的影响。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would illustrate this point further. 如果我有更多的时间，我会进一步说明这一点。
I wish I could illustrate this idea more effectively. 我希望我能更有效地说明这个想法。


被动语态:
The theory was illustrated with a simple experiment. 这个理论是用一个简单的实验来说明的。
The book is illustrated with beautiful photographs. 这本书配有精美的照片。


疑问句:
How will you illustrate your argument? 你将如何说明你的论点？
Does this example illustrate the point? 这个例子说明了这一点吗？


否定句:
The report failed to illustrate the key findings. 报告未能说明关键发现。
He didn't illustrate his argument sufficiently. 他没有充分说明他的论点。


PHRASE->
illustrate with = 用…来说明
illustrate the point = 说明要点
illustrated edition = 图解版
fully illustrated = 全图解的
illustrated book = 图书


词源:
illustrate源于拉丁语“illustrare”，意思是“使明亮，使清晰”。  前缀"il-"是加强语气， "lustrare" 意思是"to purify, to brighten"  (净化，使明亮)。  因此，其本义是“使…明亮清晰”，引申为“说明，阐明”。
