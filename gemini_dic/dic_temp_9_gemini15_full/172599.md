pyrophyllite:/ˌpaɪrəˈfaɪlaɪt/| n. |叶蜡石|A hydrous phyllosilicate mineral of aluminum and silicon, with a pearly luster and a soft, soapy feel. It is used as a filler in paper, paint, and plastics, and also as a lubricant. |例句：Pyrophyllite is a soft mineral often used as a filler in various industrial applications. 叶蜡石是一种软矿物，常被用作各种工业应用中的填料。|例句：The smooth, soapy feel of pyrophyllite makes it suitable for use as a lubricant. 叶蜡石光滑，有肥皂感，因此适合用作润滑剂。|近义词：talc (滑石)|反义词：N/A
