overwarily:/ˌoʊvərˈwerəli/| adv. |过分谨慎地，过于小心地 |excessively or unnecessarily cautious.  It implies a degree of caution that is excessive or hinders progress. |例句：He overwarily checked the door lock three times before leaving. 他离开前过于谨慎地检查了三次门锁。


例句：She approached the situation overwarily, missing a golden opportunity because of her excessive caution. 她过于谨慎地处理这种情况，错失了一个绝佳的机会。


近义词：cautiously, carefully, prudently, circumspectly


反义词：recklessly, carelessly, rashly


词性：副词
