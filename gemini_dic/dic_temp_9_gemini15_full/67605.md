extravagation:/ˌɛkstrævəˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|挥霍；奢侈；过度；放纵|The excessive expenditure of money or resources.|例句：His extravagant lifestyle led to financial ruin.  他的奢侈生活导致了经济破产。


例句：The extravagance of the wedding was astonishing. 婚礼的奢华令人震惊。


近义词：profligacy, lavishness, excess, exuberance|反义词：frugality, thrift, economy, restraint|词性：名词
