nonsystematically:/ˌnɒnˌsɪstəˈmætɪkli/| adj. |非系统地；无规律地；不系统地 |  Not done or occurring according to a system or plan; haphazardly.  It implies a lack of organization, method, or consistency. |例句：The data was collected nonsystematically, making it difficult to draw reliable conclusions. 数据收集是非系统性的，这使得难以得出可靠的结论。

The samples were selected nonsystematically, leading to biased results. 样本是非系统性选择的，导致结果偏差。|近义词：randomly, haphazardly, irregularly, chaotically |反义词：systematically, methodically, orderly
