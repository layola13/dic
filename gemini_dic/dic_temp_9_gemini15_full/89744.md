hoppers:/ˈhɑːpərz/| n.|蝗虫；跳跃者；蚱蜢；（尤指）谷仓跳虫；跳跃机；（某些昆虫的）幼虫|A hopper is an insect that hops, especially a grasshopper or locust.  The term can also refer to a container for holding and feeding grain or other materials to a machine, or to a machine that performs a hopping action.|例句：A swarm of hoppers descended upon the fields, devouring everything in their path.  一群蝗虫飞落田间，吞噬着所有的一切。

例句：The farmer used a hopper to feed grain into the mill. 农民用漏斗将谷物送入磨坊。


近义词：grasshopper, locust, cricket|反义词：None specifically.
