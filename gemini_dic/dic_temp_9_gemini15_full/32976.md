choree:/tʃɔːr/| n.|杂务，家务|A chore is a small job that is boring but necessary.  It is usually a household task.|例句：Taking out the trash is a daily chore.  倒垃圾是一项日常杂务。|例句：Mowing the lawn is a chore I dislike.  割草是我不喜欢做的一项杂务。|近义词：task, job, errand|反义词：pleasure, fun, recreation|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Doing the chores takes a lot of time. = 做家务需要很多时间。
This chore is easy to complete. = 这项家务很容易完成。


一般过去时:
I did all my chores yesterday. = 我昨天完成了所有家务。
She finished her chores before dinner. = 她在晚餐前完成了家务。


现在完成时:
I have finished all my chores for the week. = 我已经完成了本周所有的家务。
She has already done her chores. = 她已经做完家务了。


过去完成时:
I had already done my chores when he arrived. = 他来的时候我已经做完家务了。
She had finished her chores before going out. = 她出门前已经做完家务了。


将来时:
I will do my chores tomorrow. = 我明天会做家务。

虚拟语气:

I wish I didn't have so many chores to do. = 我希望我没有那么多家务要做。


被动语态:
The chores were completed quickly. = 家务很快就完成了。

疑问句:

否定句:
