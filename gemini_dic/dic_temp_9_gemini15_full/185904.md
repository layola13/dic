revolutionist: /ˌrevəˈluːʃənɪst/ | n. |革命者，革命分子 | A person who advocates or engages in revolution.  A revolutionist is someone who actively participates in or supports a revolution, aiming to overthrow an existing system or government. They often believe the current system is unjust or oppressive and seek to replace it with a new one.  | Example: Many revolutionists risked their lives fighting for independence. 许多革命者冒着生命危险为独立而战。 |近义词：rebel, insurgent, revolutionary | 反义词：conservative, loyalist, traditionalist
