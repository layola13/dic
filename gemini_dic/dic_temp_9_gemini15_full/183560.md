requested:/rɪˈkwɛstɪd/ | adj. | 被请求的，要求的 |  Something that is requested has been asked for.  |  Example: I received a requested document. 我收到了一份被请求的文件。|近义词：required, demanded, asked for | 反义词：unrequested, unwanted | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The requested item is on its way. = 被请求的物品正在路上。
He is requested to attend the meeting. = 他被要求参加会议。


一般过去时:
The requested information was provided yesterday. = 被请求的信息昨天提供了。
She was requested to leave the premises. = 她被要求离开场地。


现在完成时:
The requested changes have been implemented. = 被请求的更改已经实施。
The documents have been requested and are expected soon. = 文件已经被请求，预计很快就会收到。


过去完成时:
The requested data had already been collected before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，被请求的数据已经被收集了。
It had been requested that he resign from his position. = 他已被要求辞去他的职务。


将来时:
The requested materials will be delivered tomorrow. = 被请求的材料将于明天送达。
He will be requested to give a presentation next week.  = 他将被要求下周做一个演示。


虚拟语气:
If the requested funds were available, the project would proceed. = 如果有足够的资金，项目就会继续进行。
It was requested that he be given another chance.  = 人们要求给他另一次机会。


被动语态:
The requested book was borrowed by someone. = 那本被请求的书被别人借走了。
A report was requested by the manager. = 经理请求了一份报告。


疑问句:
Has the requested information been sent? = 被请求的信息是否已经发送？
Were the requested materials delivered on time? = 被请求的材料是否准时送达？


否定句:
The requested file was not found. = 未找到被请求的文件。
The supplies were not requested in time. = 供应品没有及时被请求。


PHRASE->
as requested = 正如所要求的
highly requested = 高度需求的
frequently requested = 经常被请求的
the requested information = 被请求的信息
time requested = 要求的时间
