sebaceous:/sɪˈbeɪʃəs/| adj.|皮脂的，油脂分泌的| Relating to or secreting sebum (an oily substance secreted by sebaceous glands).  Sebaceous glands are found in the skin and secrete an oily or waxy matter called sebum, which lubricates and protects the skin and hair.| 例句：The sebaceous glands produce an oily secretion that keeps the skin supple. 皮脂腺产生油性分泌物，使皮肤保持柔软。

The sebaceous cyst on his face was quite large. 他脸上的皮脂囊肿相当大。|近义词：oily, greasy, fatty|反义词：dry
