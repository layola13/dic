overcompressed:/ˌoʊvər kəmˈprɛst/ | adj. |过度压缩的|  Excessively compressed; compressed beyond a desirable or optimal level. This often leads to a loss of quality or detail in the compressed data or material.  | Example: The overcompressed image resulted in a significant loss of detail and appeared pixelated.  过度压缩的图像导致细节大量丢失，看起来像素化了。 |近义词：highly compressed, excessively compressed, squeezed |反义词：uncompressed, decompressed
