effervesce:/ˌɛfərˈvɛs/|/ɪˈfɜːvɛs/| v. | 冒泡；兴奋；充满活力 | To bubble, fizz, or foam; to show liveliness or excitement. | The champagne effervesced in the glass. 香槟酒在杯中冒泡。The city effervesced with excitement before the festival. 节日前夕，城市里充满了兴奋之情。 | bubble, fizz, foam, sparkle | still, flat, dull | 动词
