inhibitingly: /ɪnˈhɪbɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 抑制地；阻碍地 |  In a way that inhibits or restrains.  This adverb describes something that hinders, restricts, or prevents something else from happening or developing. |


例句：

The new regulations are working inhibitingly on the growth of small businesses.  = 新的法规正在抑制小型企业的发展。


The extremely cold weather was inhibitingly affecting the harvest. = 极度寒冷的天气正在抑制收成。


近义词：restrictively, repressively, suppressingly


反义词：stimulatingly, encouragingly, promotingly


词性：副词
