一般现在时:
The horse's hooves beat rhythmically against the pavement. = 马蹄有节奏地敲打着路面。
The cow's hooves are muddy. = 母牛的蹄子很脏。

一般过去时:
The hooves of the cattle left prints in the mud. = 牛的蹄印留在泥土里。
The deer's hooves pounded the forest floor. = 鹿的蹄子重重地敲打着森林地面。

现在完成时:
The hooves have worn down from years of hard work. = 蹄子因为多年的辛勤工作而磨损了。
The farmer has cleaned the mud from the cow's hooves. = 农民已经清理了母牛蹄子上的泥巴。

过去完成时:

The horse's hooves had been shod before the race. = 在比赛前，马蹄已经被钉上了铁掌。
