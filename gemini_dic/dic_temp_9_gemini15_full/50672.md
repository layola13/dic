deriving:/dɪˈraɪvɪŋ/| v. |获得；推断；起源于 | Obtaining something from a source; concluding something through reasoning; originating from.  |例句：He derives great pleasure from his work. 他从工作中获得极大的乐趣。

例句：We can derive the formula from these equations. 我们可以从这些方程式中推导出公式。


近义词：obtaining, deducing, originating, stemming,  extracting

反义词：giving, providing, losing, forfeiting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He derives his income from investments. = 他从投资中获得收入。
She derives satisfaction from helping others. = 她从帮助他人中获得满足感。

一般过去时:
They derived the answer from the clues. = 他们从线索中推导出答案。
I derived much knowledge from that book. = 我从那本书中学到了很多知识。

现在完成时:
We have derived significant benefits from this project. = 我们已经从这个项目中获得了显著的益处。
She has derived her inspiration from nature. = 她从大自然中获得了灵感。

过去完成时:
He had derived his conclusion from insufficient evidence. = 他从不足的证据中得出了结论。
They had derived their wealth from real estate. = 他们从房地产中获得了财富。

将来时:
We will derive the equation in the next chapter. = 我们将在下一章推导出这个方程式。
She will derive great joy from her grandchild. = 她将从她的孙子孙女那里获得极大的快乐。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would derive more pleasure from painting. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会从绘画中获得更多乐趣。
I wish I could derive more meaning from my life. = 我希望我能够从我的生活中获得更多意义。

被动语态:
The results were derived from a large sample size. = 结果来自一个大型样本。
The conclusion was derived from statistical analysis. = 结论是通过统计分析得出的。

疑问句:
What do you derive from this experience? = 你从这次经历中得到了什么？
Where did they derive their information? = 他们从哪里获得的信息？

否定句:
He did not derive any benefit from the deal. = 他从这笔交易中没有得到任何好处。
She could not derive any meaning from the abstract painting. = 她无法从抽象画中理解任何意义。


PHRASE->
derive from = 源于，来自
derive benefit from = 从...中获益
derive pleasure from = 从...中获得快乐
derive satisfaction from = 从...中获得满足
derive inspiration from = 从...中获得灵感
