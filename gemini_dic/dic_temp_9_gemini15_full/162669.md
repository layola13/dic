preassigning:/ˌpriːəˈsaɪnɪŋ/| v.|预先分配，预先指定 |The act of assigning something beforehand.  It implies allocating resources, tasks, or roles before they are actually needed or used. |例句：Preassigning tasks to team members ensures everyone knows their responsibilities. 预先分配任务给团队成员确保每个人都知道他们的责任。

例句：The company's preassigning of project budgets improved efficiency. 公司预先分配项目预算提高了效率。


近义词：allocating beforehand, predetermining, assigning in advance
反义词：assigning later, improvising, allocating afterwards
词性:动词
