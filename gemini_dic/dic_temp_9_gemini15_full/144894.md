over-serves:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvz/ | vt. |过度服务；提供过多服务 | To provide more service than is needed or wanted, often to the point of being excessive or intrusive.  | 例句：The waiter over-served the customers, leading to complaints. = 服务员过度服务顾客，导致顾客投诉。  | 例句：The company over-served the market with its new product, resulting in low sales. = 公司的新产品过度供应市场，导致销量低迷。 | 近义词：overwhelm, inundate, overload | 反义词：underserve, neglect
