deperdition:/ˌdɛpərˈdɪʃən/| n. |毁灭；丧失；损失 |The complete loss or destruction of something; ruin.  It often refers to a gradual decline or decay.  |例句：The deperdition of their savings was a devastating blow.  他们的积蓄损失对他们来说是毁灭性的打击。

The deperdition of the ancient city was a slow process, caused by neglect and natural disasters. 古城的毁灭是一个缓慢的过程，是由忽视和自然灾害造成的。


近义词：destruction, ruin, loss, decay, waste

反义词：preservation, salvation, recovery, gain, increase

词性:名词


词源:  From Old French *deperdicion, from Latin *deperditio, from deperdere "to lose utterly," from de- "away, from" + perdere "to lose."  The word reflects a sense of complete and irretrievable loss.
