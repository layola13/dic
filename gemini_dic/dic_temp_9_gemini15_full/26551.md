cabins:/ˈkæbɪnz/| n.|小木屋，船舱|A small, simple dwelling, typically of wood, used as a shelter or lodging.  Also, a small room on a ship or aircraft.|例句：We rented a cozy cabin in the mountains for a weekend getaway. 我们租了一间舒适的山间小屋度周末。
例句：The passengers were assigned to their cabins before the ship set sail.  乘客在船启航前被分配到各自的船舱。|近义词：hut, cottage, berth|反义词：mansion, palace
