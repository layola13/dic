refigures:/ˌriːˈfɪɡərz/ | v. |重新计算；重新考虑；重新估算 | To calculate or consider something again; to re-evaluate.  |例句：The accountant refigured the total after discovering an error. 会计发现错误后重新计算了总数。 |例句：We need to refigure our strategy in light of the new information. 鉴于新的信息，我们需要重新考虑我们的策略。|近义词：recalculate, reconsider, reassess |反义词：finalize, confirm
