go-down-the-drain:/ɡoʊ daʊn ðə dreɪn/| phrasal verb|付诸东流；浪费；失败|To be wasted or lost completely; to fail completely.|例句：All our hard work went down the drain when the project was cancelled.  我们的辛勤工作因为项目取消而付诸东流了。

例句：His savings went down the drain after he invested in that failing company. 他的积蓄在他投资那家倒闭的公司后就打了水漂。


近义词：be wasted, be lost, fail completely, come to nothing


反义词：succeed, thrive, prosper


词性:phrasal verb
