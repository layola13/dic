notarize:/ˈnoʊtəraɪz/|/ˈnəʊtəraɪz/ | vt. |使公证；认证|To notarize a document is to have a notary public officially witness and certify its signature(s).  This process verifies the signer's identity and the authenticity of the document.| 例句：I need to notarize this document before I can submit it. 我需要在提交这份文件之前对其进行公证。


例句：The lawyer notarized the contract.  律师对合同进行了公证。


近义词：certify, authenticate, validate


反义词：invalidate, disavow
