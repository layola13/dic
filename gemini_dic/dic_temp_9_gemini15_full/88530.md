hit-lists:/ˈhɪt lɪsts/| n.|热门名单，热门歌曲排行榜，目标名单|A hit list is a list of popular items, such as songs or products, or a list of targets, such as people to be arrested or assassinated.|例句：The band's new single topped the hit lists. 这支乐队的最新单曲登上了热门歌曲排行榜。|近义词：top ten, best-sellers, target list|反义词：flop list, unpopular list
