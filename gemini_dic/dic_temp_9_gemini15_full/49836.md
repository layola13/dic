demonstratorship:/dɪˈmɒnstreɪtəʃɪp/| n. |助教职位；示范职位|The position of a demonstrator, typically a graduate student who assists in teaching practical classes or laboratory work.  A demonstratorship is often a stepping stone to a more permanent academic position.  |Example: He secured a demonstratorship in the physics department, gaining valuable teaching experience. 他在物理系获得了一个助教职位，获得了宝贵的教学经验。|近义词：assistantship, instructorship |反义词：professorship (implies a more senior position)
