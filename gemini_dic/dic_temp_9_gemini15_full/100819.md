introduce:/ˌɪntrəˈduːs/ |/ˌɪntrəˈduːs/| vt. |介绍，引进，采用，提出|To make someone or something known to someone else, or to bring something into use or existence.|例句：Let me introduce you to my friend, John.  让我向你介绍我的朋友约翰。|例句：The company introduced a new product last year. 公司去年推出了一款新产品。|近义词：present, acquaint, launch|反义词：conceal, hide, withdraw|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He introduces himself to the class. = 他向全班同学做了自我介绍。
She introduces new ideas to the team. = 她向团队引进了新的想法。


一般过去时:
They introduced me to their parents. = 他们把我介绍给了他们的父母。
The teacher introduced the new topic. = 老师介绍了新的主题。


现在完成时:
I have introduced myself several times. = 我已经自我介绍了好几次了。
They have introduced many changes to the system. = 他们已经对系统做了很多修改。


过去完成时:
She had introduced the project before the meeting. = 她在会议之前已经介绍了这个项目。
He had introduced himself to the boss previously. = 他之前已经向老板做过自我介绍了。


将来时:
We will introduce a new policy next month. = 我们下个月将推出一个新的政策。
They will introduce him to the client tomorrow. = 他们明天将把他介绍给客户。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would introduce myself confidently. = 如果我是你，我会自信地自我介绍。
I wish I had introduced myself earlier. = 我希望我早点自我介绍。


被动语态:
The new technology was introduced to the market last year. = 这种新技术去年被引进了市场。
He was introduced to the president. = 他被介绍给了总统。


疑问句:
Will you introduce me to your colleagues? = 你会把我介绍给你的同事吗？
Have they introduced the new software yet? = 他们已经引进新的软件了吗？


否定句:
I didn't introduce myself at the party. = 我在派对上没有自我介绍。
They didn't introduce the changes soon enough. = 他们没有及时推出这些改变。



PHRASE->
introduce oneself = 自我介绍
introduce a bill = 提出一项法案
introduce a new concept = 引进一个新概念
introduce a speaker = 介绍演讲者
introduce a resolution = 提出决议
