familiarized:/fəˈmɪliəraɪzd/| v. |使熟悉；使了解 |To make someone or something familiar or well-known; to become familiar with something.  |例句：He familiarized himself with the software before starting the project. 他在开始项目之前熟悉了软件。|例句：The teacher familiarized the students with the new rules.老师让学生们熟悉了新的规则。|近义词：acquainted, introduced, accustomed |反义词：estranged, alienated, unfamiliar |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She familiarizes herself with new vocabulary every day. = 她每天都学习新的词汇。
They familiarize their employees with company policies. = 他们让员工熟悉公司政策。

一般过去时:
I familiarized myself with the city map before my trip. = 我在旅行前熟悉了城市地图。
The professor familiarized the students with the research methods. = 教授让学生们熟悉了研究方法。

现在完成时:
She has familiarized herself with the local customs. = 她已经熟悉了当地的习俗。
He has familiarized himself with the company's financial reports. = 他已经熟悉了公司的财务报表。

过去完成时:
I had familiarized myself with the instrument before the concert. = 我在音乐会之前已经熟悉了乐器。
They had familiarized themselves with the procedure before they began. = 他们在开始之前已经熟悉了流程。

将来时:
We will familiarize ourselves with the new system next week. = 我们下周将熟悉新系统。
The company will familiarize its clients with the updated terms of service. = 公司将让客户熟悉更新的服务条款。

虚拟语气:
If I had familiarized myself with the instructions, I wouldn't have made so many mistakes. = 如果我熟悉了说明书，我就不会犯这么多错误。
It's important that everyone familiarize themselves with the emergency procedures. = 每个人都熟悉紧急程序非常重要。

被动语态:

疑问句:
