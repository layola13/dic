disarray:/dɪsərˈeɪ/| n., v.|混乱；杂乱；使混乱 | A state of disorder or confusion.  |例句：The room was in a state of disarray after the party.  派对后房间一片混乱。

The sudden news threw the whole office into disarray.  突如其来的消息使整个办公室陷入混乱。


近义词：chaos, disorder, confusion, disorganization | 反义词：order, organization, neatness


词性: 名词和动词


词源:  Comes from the Old French "desarraier," meaning "to disarm,"  "to disarrange," ultimately from "arraier," meaning "to array" (to put in order). The prefix "dis-" indicates negation or reversal.


用法说明:

作为名词 (noun):

一般现在时:
The papers are in a state of disarray on his desk. = 他的办公桌上文件凌乱不堪。
Her hair is in complete disarray. = 她的头发乱糟糟的。


一般过去时:
The battlefield was in disarray after the battle. = 战斗结束后，战场一片混乱。
His thoughts were in disarray after the accident. = 事故后，他的思绪一片混乱。


现在完成时:
The company has been in disarray since the CEO resigned. = 自从首席执行官辞职以来，公司一直处于混乱状态。
The evidence has been left in disarray. = 证据已经被弄得乱七八糟。


过去完成时:
The army had been in disarray before the enemy's attack. = 在敌人进攻之前，军队就已经处于混乱状态。
The room had been in disarray for days. = 房间已经乱了好几天了。


将来时:
The market will be in disarray if the new regulations are implemented. = 如果实施新的规章制度，市场将会陷入混乱。
The team will be in disarray without their leader. = 没有领导，团队将会陷入混乱。


虚拟语气:
If the government were in disarray, the country would be at risk. = 如果政府陷入混乱，国家将面临危险。
If the files were in disarray, we would have trouble finding the information. = 如果文件凌乱不堪，我们将难以找到信息。


被动语态:
The room was left in disarray. = 房间被弄得乱七八糟。
The schedule was thrown into disarray by the unexpected delay. = 由于意外的延误，日程安排被打乱了。


疑问句:
Is the situation in disarray? = 情况混乱吗？
Is the garden in disarray after the storm? = 暴风雨过后花园一片狼藉吗？


否定句:
The office wasn't in disarray; it was meticulously organized. = 办公室并不混乱；它井井有条。
The documents weren't in disarray; they were neatly stacked. = 文件并没有凌乱；它们整齐地堆放着。



作为动词 (verb):

一般现在时:
The strong winds disarray the garden. = 强风吹乱了花园。
The news disarrays his carefully planned schedule. = 这个消息打乱了他精心安排的日程。


一般过去时:
The unexpected rain disarrayed the picnic. = 突如其来的雨打乱了野餐。
The scandal disarrayed the political party. = 丑闻使政党陷入混乱。


现在完成时:
The constant changes have disarrayed the team. = 持续不断的变化使团队陷入混乱。
The rumors have disarrayed the market. = 谣言扰乱了市场。


过去完成时:
The previous administration had disarrayed the economy. = 前一届政府已经搞乱了经济。
The heavy snow had disarrayed the transportation system. = 大雪打乱了交通系统。


将来时:
The upcoming election will disarray the political landscape. = 即将到来的选举将扰乱政治格局。
The new policy will disarray the current system. = 新政策将扰乱现行制度。


虚拟语气:
If the information were disarrayed, we couldn't analyze it. = 如果信息凌乱不堪，我们就无法分析它。
If the army were disarrayed, they would be vulnerable to attack. = 如果军队陷入混乱，他们将容易受到攻击。


被动语态:
The army was disarrayed by the surprise attack. = 军队被突然袭击打乱了阵脚。
The schedule was disarrayed by the unexpected events. = 日程安排被意外事件打乱了。


疑问句:
Was the army disarrayed during the battle? = 战斗中军队是否陷入混乱？
Was the market disarrayed by the news? = 市场是否被新闻扰乱？


否定句:
The attack didn't disarray the enemy forces. = 袭击并没有打乱敌军。
The changes didn't disarray the company's operations. = 这些变化并没有扰乱公司的运营。



PHRASE->
in disarray: 处于混乱状态

a state of disarray: 一片混乱
bring into disarray: 使陷入混乱
fall into disarray: 陷入混乱
