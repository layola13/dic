lintiest: /ˈlɪntiɪst/ | adj. | 最有绒毛的；最起球的 |  Most covered in lint; most prone to producing lint.  The word is a superlative form of "linty," meaning covered in lint.  It describes something that has the most amount of lint or is most likely to create lint. | Example:  "Her new sweater was the lintiest garment in her wardrobe." = 她新毛衣是她衣橱里最容易起球的衣服。| 近义词: fluffiest,  most fuzzy | 反义词:  smooth, lint-free |  There is no verb or noun form of "lintiest".
