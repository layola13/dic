overlords:/ˈoʊvərlɔːrdz/| n.|统治者，最高统治者|Those who hold supreme authority over others, often implying tyranny or oppression.  The term often refers to a ruler who controls multiple subordinate rulers or entities.|例句：The empire was ruled by a cruel overlord who demanded absolute obedience.  这个帝国由一个残酷的暴君统治，他要求绝对服从。

例句：The alien overlords imposed their will upon the conquered planet.  外星统治者将他们的意志强加于被征服的星球。


近义词：tyrant, sovereign, supreme ruler, master


反义词：subject, subordinate, vassal


词性：名词
