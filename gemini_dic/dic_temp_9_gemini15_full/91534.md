hypanthium:/haɪˈpænθiəm/| n. |花托筒；花托杯|A hypanthium is a floral structure formed by the fusion of the bases of sepals, petals, and stamens. It often forms a cup-like or tubular structure surrounding the ovary.  The hypanthium can be various shapes and sizes, and its presence or absence is a key characteristic used in plant taxonomy.|The rose's hypanthium is prominent and cup-shaped. 玫瑰的花托筒很明显，呈杯状。| floral cup, receptacle tube |  (无明显反义词，因为是描述性词语)|名词
