reprocesses:/ˌriːˈprɑːsɛsɪz/| vt.|再加工；重新处理|To process something again.|The factory reprocesses waste materials into new products.  这家工厂将废料再加工成新产品。|The company reprocesses used cooking oil into biodiesel. 这家公司将用过的食用油再加工成生物柴油。|process, recycle|dispose of, waste|动词
