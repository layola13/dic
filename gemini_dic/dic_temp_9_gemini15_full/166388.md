prerequisites:/ˌpriːˈrɛkwɪzɪts/ | n. |先决条件，必备条件 |Something that is required as a prior condition for something else to happen or exist.  It typically refers to a requirement that must be fulfilled before something else can begin. |This course has certain prerequisites; you must have completed introductory physics. 这门课程有一些先决条件；你必须修完入门物理。
The job requires a college degree and several years of experience as prerequisites. 这份工作需要大学学位和几年的工作经验作为先决条件。|requirements, essentials, necessities |non-prerequisites|名词
