romanticists: /rəʊˈmæntɪsɪsts/ | n. | 浪漫主义者 |  People who follow the principles of Romanticism, especially in art, literature, or music. They emphasize emotion, imagination, and individualism over reason and logic.  | The romanticists of the 19th century celebrated nature and the power of human emotion.  浪漫主义的19世纪庆祝了大自然和人类情感的力量。|  idealists, visionaries | realists, classicists | 名词
