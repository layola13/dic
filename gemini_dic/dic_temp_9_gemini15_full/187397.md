roedeer:/ˈrɔːdɪər/| n. |狍子 |A roe deer is a small, deer-like animal with a reddish-brown coat. They are known for their graceful movements and are often found in forests and woodlands.  |Example: We saw a herd of roe deer grazing peacefully in the meadow. 我们看到一群狍子正在草地上安静地吃草。|近义词：deer (general term) |反义词：无
