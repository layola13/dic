realpolitiker:/ˌreːəlˈpɒlɪtɪkə(r)/| n. |现实政治家；务实政治家 | A realpolitiker is a politician who is primarily concerned with practical considerations and expediency rather than with ideology or ethics. They prioritize national interests and power above all else, often employing pragmatic and sometimes ruthless methods to achieve their goals.  |Example:  The chancellor, a seasoned realpolitiker, prioritized economic stability over idealistic social reforms.  这位总理是一位经验丰富的现实政治家，他优先考虑经济稳定而不是理想化的社会改革。

|近义词： pragmatist, power politician, strategist |反义词：idealist, utopian |
