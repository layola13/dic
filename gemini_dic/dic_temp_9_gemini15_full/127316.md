naxalite:/ˈnæksəlaɪt/| n. |纳萨尔派成员；纳萨尔派支持者|A Naxalite is a member of a communist revolutionary movement in India, particularly active in the states of Andhra Pradesh, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha and West Bengal.  Naxalites are known for their Maoist ideology and use of guerrilla warfare against the Indian government.  The term derives from the village of Naxalbari in West Bengal where the movement originated.|The Naxalite movement has been a significant source of conflict in India for decades.|近义词：Maoist, communist revolutionary|反义词：government supporter, establishment figure
