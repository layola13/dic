play:/pleɪ/ | n./v.| 玩；戏剧；比赛；演奏 | To engage in an activity for enjoyment or recreation; a dramatic performance; a contest or game; to perform music on an instrument. |例句：The children are playing in the park.孩子们在公园里玩耍。  The play was a great success. 这出戏非常成功。|近义词：game, sport, performance, act |反义词：work, labor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They play football every Saturday. = 他们每个星期六都踢足球。
She plays the piano beautifully. = 她钢琴弹得很好。

一般过去时:
We played hide-and-seek yesterday. = 我们昨天玩捉迷藏。
He played the guitar at the party. = 他在派对上弹吉他。

现在完成时:
I have played the violin for ten years. = 我拉小提琴十年了。
They have played three matches this season. = 这个赛季他们已经打了三场比赛。

过去完成时:
We had played the game before the rain started. = 下雨前我们已经玩过这场比赛了。
She had played the song many times before the concert. = 音乐会之前她已经演奏过这首歌很多次了。

将来时:
They will play tennis tomorrow. = 他们明天将打网球。
We will play a new game next week. = 我们下周将玩一个新的游戏。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would play the guitar more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常弹吉他。
I wish I could play the piano. = 我希望我能弹钢琴。

被动语态:
The game was played fairly. = 这场比赛进行得很公平。
The song was played on the radio. = 这首歌在广播里播放了。

疑问句:
Do you play any musical instruments? = 你会演奏任何乐器吗？
What games do they play? = 他们玩什么游戏？

否定句:
