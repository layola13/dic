cashing:/ˈkæʃɪŋ/| v.|兑现；套现；利用|The act of converting something into cash.  This can refer to exchanging a check or other negotiable instrument for cash, or to the process of realizing the value of an asset.|例句：He's cashing in his savings bonds. 他正在兑现他的储蓄债券。

例句：The company is cashing in on the popularity of its new product. 这家公司正在利用其新产品的流行来获利。


近义词：converting, realizing, liquidating|反义词：investing, hoarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cashes his paycheck every Friday. = 他每个星期五都兑现他的工资支票。
She cashes in her frequent flyer miles for a free ticket. = 她用她的飞行常客里程兑换免费机票。


一般过去时:
I cashed a check at the bank yesterday. = 我昨天在银行兑现了一张支票。
They cashed out their investments before the market crashed. = 他们在市场崩盘之前套现了他们的投资。


现在完成时:
He has cashed in all his stocks. = 他已经兑现了他所有的股票。
She has cashed out of the business. = 她已经退出这家公司并套现了。


过去完成时:
They had cashed their winning lottery tickets before celebrating. = 他们在庆祝之前已经兑现了他们中奖的彩票。
She had cashed out her retirement fund to buy a house. = 她已经兑现了她的退休基金来买房子。


将来时:
I will cash my check tomorrow. = 我明天将兑现我的支票。
They will cash out their investments next month. = 他们下个月将套现他们的投资。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would cash in my savings bonds. = 如果我有更多时间，我会兑现我的储蓄债券。
I wish I had cashed out my stocks before the market fell. = 我希望我在市场下跌之前已经套现了我的股票。


被动语态:
The check was cashed at the store. = 支票在商店被兑现了。
The investment will be cashed out soon. = 投资很快就会被套现。


疑问句:
Where can I cash this check? = 我在哪里可以兑现这张支票？
Will you cash out your shares? = 你会套现你的股份吗？


否定句:
I couldn't cash the check because it was expired. = 我不能兑现这张支票，因为它过期了。
They didn't cash in on the opportunity. = 他们没有抓住这个机会获利。
