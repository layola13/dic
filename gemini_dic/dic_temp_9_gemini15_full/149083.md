These compounds often appear as derivatives of oxalic acid.  | Oxamic acid is used in some industrial processes.  The oxamic acid derivative, ethyl oxamate, shows interesting properties. | 近义词:  (No direct synonyms;  related terms would include oxalic, amide, carboxamide) | 反义词: (No direct antonyms)
