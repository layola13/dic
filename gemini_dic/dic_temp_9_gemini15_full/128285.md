neuropter:/ˈnʊərɒptər/| n. |脉翅目昆虫|Neuroptera is an order of insects, commonly known as net-winged insects.  They are characterized by their delicate, net-like wings with numerous veins. Many species are predatory, both as larvae and adults.  |Example: Lacewings and antlions are examples of neuropteran insects.|近义词:net-winged insect|反义词:N/A
