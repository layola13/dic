sidekicks:/ˈsaɪdkɪks/| n.|助手，伙伴，帮手|A sidekick is a person who assists or accompanies another person, especially a more important or powerful one.  Often used informally and implies a close relationship, sometimes with a sense of loyalty and camaraderie.|例句：Robin was Batman's loyal sidekick. 罗宾是蝙蝠侠忠诚的助手。|例句：The comedian's sidekick provided hilarious banter throughout the show.  喜剧演员的助手在整场演出中提供了搞笑的玩笑。|近义词：assistant, accomplice, companion, associate|反义词：antagonist, opponent, enemy
