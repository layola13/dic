rattlesnake: /ˈrætlsneɪk/ | n. |响尾蛇| A venomous snake with a rattle at the end of its tail.  Rattlesnakes are found in the Americas and are known for their distinctive warning sound produced by shaking their rattle. |例句：The hiker froze when he saw a rattlesnake coiled on the trail.  徒步旅行者看到一条响尾蛇盘踞在小路上时吓呆了。

例句：We heard the distinctive rattle of a rattlesnake in the bushes. 我们听到灌木丛中响尾蛇特有的响声。
