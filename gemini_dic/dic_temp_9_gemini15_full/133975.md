nonhesitant:/ˌnɒnˈhezɪtənt/ | adj. | 不犹豫的，果断的 |  Not hesitating; ready and willing to act.  It describes someone who acts quickly and decisively without delay or doubt. | Example: She gave a nonhesitant "yes" to the proposal. 她毫不犹豫地答应了这个提议。  |近义词：unhesitating, prompt, decisive, immediate | 反义词： hesitant, reluctant, wavering, irresolute |
