grovellers:/ˈɡrɑːvələrz/ | n. | 谄媚者，卑躬屈膝者 |  A groveller is someone who behaves in an excessively submissive or obsequious way in order to gain favor. They act with exaggerated humility and deference, often flattering those in power.  | Example: He was a groveller, always seeking approval from his superiors. 他是个谄媚者，总是寻求上级的认可。  |近义词: sycophant, flatterer, fawner, yes-man | 反义词: independent, assertive, self-respecting | 名词
