protoxide:/ˈproʊtɒksaɪd/ | n. |一氧化物|A chemical compound containing one atom of oxygen combined with one or more atoms of another element, typically a metal.  It often refers to the lowest oxide of a metal.  |Example: Cuprous oxide is a protoxide of copper.  氧化亚铜是铜的一氧化物。|近义词:monoxide|反义词:N/A
