It refers to a method of inquiry or analysis that avoids making assumptions about the subject matter before investigation.  |Example:  A truly presuppositionless approach to the study of history is difficult to achieve.  一个真正不预设任何前提的历史研究方法很难实现。  |近义词：assumption-free, unbiased, impartial |反义词：biased, prejudiced, preconceived
