pull-over:/ˈpʊlˌoʊvər/| n. v.|套衫；(使)靠边停车|A pullover is a type of sweater that you pull on over your head.  It can also refer to the act of pulling a vehicle to the side of the road.|例句：I bought a new wool pullover for the winter. = 我买了一件新的羊毛套衫过冬。

He pulled over to the side of the road to let the ambulance pass. = 他把车停靠在路边让救护车过去。|近义词：sweater, jumper (BrE),  (for the verb)  stop, park|反义词：button-up (for the noun) , continue (for the verb)|名词，动词


USAGE->

I pull over when I see a police car. = 我看到警车就靠边停车。
She pulls on her pullover every morning. = 她每天早上都穿上套衫。
