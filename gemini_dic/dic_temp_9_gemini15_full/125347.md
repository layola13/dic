multilingual:/ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwəl/| adj. |多语言的；使用多种语言的 |Able to speak, read, or write in several languages.  This adjective describes people, texts, or environments that involve multiple languages. |例句：She is a multilingual person, fluent in English, French, and Spanish. 她是一个多语言的人，英语、法语和西班牙语都流利。|例句：The conference was multilingual, with simultaneous translation provided in several languages.  这次会议是多语言的，并提供了几种语言的同声传译。|近义词：polyglot, plurilingual |反义词：monolingual
