roomlet:/ˈruːmlɛt/| n. |小房间；小套房 |A small room, especially a small rented room.  It often implies a less luxurious or smaller space than a typical room. |Example:  The roomlet was cozy but cramped.  这个小房间很舒适但很狭窄。  The student found a cheap roomlet near the campus. 这个学生在校园附近找到一个便宜的小房间。|近义词：small room, cubicle, alcove |反义词：large room, suite, mansion
