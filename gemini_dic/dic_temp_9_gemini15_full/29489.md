catapuce:/ˈkætəpjuːs/| n.|飞燕草，毛茛科植物|A catapuce is a plant of the genus Euphorbia, often with poisonous milky sap.  The term is sometimes used more broadly to refer to various spurge plants.|例句：The gardener carefully avoided touching the catapuce, knowing its sap was poisonous. 园丁小心地避免触摸飞燕草，知道它的汁液有毒。|近义词：spurge, euphorbia|反义词：无
