perceptibility:/ˌpɜːsɛptəˈbɪləti/| n. |可感知性，可觉察性 |The ability to be perceived or noticed; the extent to which something can be perceived.  It refers to how easily something can be detected by the senses or the mind.  |Example: The perceptibility of the faint sound was low due to the background noise.  微弱的声音由于背景噪音而难以察觉。 The perceptibility of the change in temperature was gradual. 温度变化的感知是逐渐的。|近义词：detectability, discernibility, visibility |反义词：imperceptibility, undetectability |名词
