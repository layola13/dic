misorganizes:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪz/| vt.|组织混乱；安排不当|To arrange or organize badly or inefficiently; to throw into disorder.|The company misorganized its resources, leading to project delays. 公司资源安排混乱，导致项目延误。|近义词：disorganize, disarrange, jumble|反义词：organize, arrange, systematize


USAGE->

She misorganizes her time terribly. = 她非常糟糕地安排自己的时间。
