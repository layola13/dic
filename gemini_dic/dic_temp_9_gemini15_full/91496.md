hymned:/hɪmd/| v.|赞美，歌颂|To praise or celebrate someone or something in a hymn or song.|The choir hymned the birth of the savior.|近义词：praised, celebrated, extolled|反义词：denounced, condemned, criticized


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hymn the virtues of hard work. = 他们歌颂勤劳的美德。
We hymn the beauty of nature. = 我们赞美大自然的美丽。

一般过去时:
The crowd hymned their victory. = 人群歌颂了他们的胜利。
The poet hymned the love of his country. = 诗人歌颂了他对祖国的爱。

现在完成时:
They have hymned the achievements of the past. = 他们已经歌颂了过去的成就。
She has hymned the power of faith. = 她已经歌颂了信仰的力量。

过去完成时:
The people had hymned the king's return before he arrived. = 国王到来之前，人们已经歌颂了他的回归。
They had hymned the greatness of the nation before the war. = 战争爆发前，他们已经歌颂了国家的伟大。

将来时:
We will hymn the coming of spring. = 我们将歌颂春天的到来。
They will hymn the new leader's policies. = 他们将歌颂新领导人的政策。

虚拟语气:
If we had time, we would hymn the beauty of the sunset. = 如果我们有时间，我们将歌颂日落的美丽。
I wish I could hymn the joys of freedom. = 我希望我能歌颂自由的喜悦。

被动语态:
The hero's bravery was hymned by the people. = 英雄的勇敢精神受到了人们的歌颂。
The team's victory was hymned throughout the city. = 球队的胜利在全城得到了歌颂。

疑问句:
Will they hymn the achievements of the scientists? = 他们会歌颂科学家的成就吗？
Have they hymned the importance of education? = 他们已经歌颂了教育的重要性吗？

否定句:
They did not hymn the actions of the villain. = 他们没有歌颂恶棍的行为。
She did not hymn the oppression of the people. = 她没有歌颂人民的压迫。
