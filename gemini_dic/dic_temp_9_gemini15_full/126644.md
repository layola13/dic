naivete:/näɪˈiːvtˌeɪ/|/n.|天真；幼稚；单纯|The quality of being naive; lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment.  It often implies a charming or endearing lack of worldliness.|例句：Her naivete was endearing, but also concerning in such a dangerous situation.  她的天真可爱，但在如此危险的情况下也令人担忧。

例句：His naivete about the business world led to some costly mistakes. 他对商界的幼稚导致了一些代价高昂的错误。


近义词：innocence, simplicity, artlessness, ingenuousness

反义词：sophistication, worldliness, cynicism, shrewdness

词性:名词
