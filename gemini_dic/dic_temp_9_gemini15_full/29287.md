cast-out: /kæstˈaʊt/ | vt. |逐出，驱逐，抛弃 | To expel or reject someone or something.  |例句：The evil spirit was cast out of the possessed man.  被附身的男子身上的邪灵被赶走了。

例句：He was cast out from his family for his disgraceful behavior. 因为他可耻的行为，他被逐出家门。


近义词：expel, banish, eject, dismiss, reject | 反义词：accept, welcome, include


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cast out the wicked from the city. = 他们把恶人逐出城外。
The farmer casts out the diseased plants. = 农民把患病的植物扔掉了。

一般过去时:
The villagers cast out the stranger. = 村民们把陌生人赶走了。
She cast out her old clothes. = 她扔掉了旧衣服。

现在完成时:
The company has cast out several employees this year. = 今年公司已经解雇了几名员工。
They have cast out all their doubts. = 他们已经打消了所有的疑虑。

过去完成时:
He had cast out his old shoes before buying new ones. = 他在买新鞋之前已经把旧鞋扔掉了。
The king had cast out the rebels before the war started. = 国王在战争开始之前已经驱逐了叛乱分子。

将来时:
They will cast out the intruders. = 他们将把入侵者赶出去。
She will cast out the old furniture. = 她将把旧家具扔掉。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would cast out those negative thoughts. = 如果我是你，我会摒弃那些消极的想法。
I wish I had cast out that bad habit years ago. = 我真希望几年前就改掉那个坏习惯。

被动语态:
The evil spirits were cast out by the priest. = 邪灵被牧师赶走了。
The old furniture was cast out to the curb. = 旧家具被扔到了路边。

疑问句:
Were the trespassers cast out? = 那些闯入者被赶走了吗？
Will the faulty products be cast out? = 有缺陷的产品会被淘汰吗？

否定句:
They did not cast out the evidence. = 他们没有销毁证据。
She wouldn't cast out her loyal friend. = 她不会抛弃她忠诚的朋友。



PHRASE->
cast out demons = 驱魔
cast out doubts = 打消疑虑
cast out a line = 钓鱼
cast out from grace = 失宠
cast out of paradise = 被逐出天堂

词源:
The verb "cast out" originates from the Old English verb *cēastān, meaning "to throw".  The "out" component simply indicates direction.  The meaning evolved over time to encompass the figurative sense of expulsion or rejection.
