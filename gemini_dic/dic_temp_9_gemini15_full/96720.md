inefficacious:/ˌɪnɪˈfeɪkeɪʃəs/| adj.|无效的，不起作用的|Not producing any significant or desired effect.|The treatment proved inefficacious in relieving the symptoms.|近义词：ineffective, useless, fruitless, futile|反义词：effective, efficacious, successful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medicine is inefficacious for this disease. = 这药对这种病无效。
His efforts are inefficacious in solving the problem. = 他的努力对解决这个问题无效。


一般过去时:
The old method was inefficacious. = 旧方法无效。
Her attempts to persuade him were inefficacious. = 她试图说服他，但没有成功。


现在完成时:
The campaign has been inefficacious in raising awareness. = 这场运动在提高公众意识方面没有起到作用。
Their strategies have proved inefficacious. = 他们的策略已被证明是无效的。


过去完成时:
The previous treatment had been inefficacious, so they tried a new one. = 之前的治疗无效，所以他们尝试了一种新的治疗方法。
His earlier attempts had been inefficacious in preventing the accident. = 他早先的尝试未能阻止事故发生。


将来时:
The new policy will likely be inefficacious. = 新政策很可能无效。
Without more resources, the project will be inefficacious. = 没有更多资源，这个项目将是无效的。


虚拟语气:
If the drug were inefficacious, we would need to find an alternative. = 如果药物无效，我们就需要寻找替代药物。
I wish the policy hadn't been inefficacious.  = 我真希望这项政策没有无效。


被动语态:
The proposed solution was deemed inefficacious. = 提出的解决方案被认为是无效的。
The new drug was found to be inefficacious in clinical trials. = 新药在临床试验中被发现无效。


疑问句:
Is the current approach inefficacious? = 目前的做法无效吗？
Are these measures really inefficacious? = 这些措施真的无效吗？


否定句:
The treatment is not inefficacious; it's just slow to show results. = 治疗并非无效；只是见效慢。
The results were not inefficacious, they were simply inconclusive. = 结果并非无效，只是没有定论。
