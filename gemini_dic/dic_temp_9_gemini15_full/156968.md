piling:/ˈpaɪlɪŋ/| n. v.|打桩；桩；桩基|The act or process of driving piles into the ground to form a foundation.  It can also refer to the piles themselves, or a mass or accumulation of things.|例句：The piling of the bridge was completed last week.  桥梁的打桩工作上周完成了。 The contractor is responsible for the piling. 承包商负责打桩。|近义词：piling up, driving, ramming, foundation, piling work|反义词：removing, demolition, dismantling

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are piling the wood neatly. = 他们整齐地堆放木材。
We pile the books on the shelf. = 我们把书堆在书架上。

一般过去时:
They piled the sandbags against the wall. = 他们把沙袋堆在墙边。
The workers piled the stones carefully. = 工人们小心地堆放石头。

现在完成时:
They have piled all the debris into a large heap. = 他们已经把所有碎石堆成一大堆。
She has piled her clothes on the chair. = 她已把衣服堆在椅子上。

过去完成时:
They had piled the logs before the rain started. = 下雨前他们已经堆好了木头。
She had piled up a lot of work before her vacation. = 她休假前积压了很多工作。

将来时:
They will pile the earth to make a mound. = 他们将堆起泥土做一个土堆。
We will pile the luggage in the car. = 我们将把行李堆放在车里。

虚拟语气:
If they piled the books more carefully, they wouldn’t have fallen. = 如果他们更小心地堆放书本，书本就不会掉下来。
I wish they had piled the wood properly. = 我希望他们把木头堆放整齐。

被动语态:
The sand was piled high against the door. = 沙子被高高地堆在门边。
The books are being piled onto the shelves. = 书本正在被堆放在书架上。

疑问句:
Are they piling the stones correctly? = 他们堆放石头正确吗？
Have they piled all the materials? = 他们已经把所有材料都堆好了吗？

否定句:
They didn't pile the wood properly. = 他们没有把木头堆放整齐。
She didn't pile the dishes in the sink. = 她没有把盘子堆在水槽里。


PHRASE->
pile up: 堆积；积累
pile on:  大量增加；猛烈攻击
pile into: 拥挤进入
pile high: 堆得很高
drive piles: 打桩


词源:
From Middle English *pilen, from Old French *piler ("to drive in stakes"), from Vulgar Latin *pilare ("to drive in a stake, pile"), from Latin pila ("pile, stake").  The meaning evolved to include the act of accumulating things in a heap.
