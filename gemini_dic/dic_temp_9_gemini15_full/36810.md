colonia:/kəˈloʊniə/|/ˈkɒləʊniə/| n. |殖民地；群体；菌落 |A colonia is a territory under the immediate political control of a distant country.  It can also refer to a group of people living together, or a colony of microorganisms.|例句：The Spanish established numerous colonias in the Americas. 西班牙人在美洲建立了许多殖民地。|例句： A large colonia of ants invaded the kitchen.  一大群蚂蚁入侵了厨房。|近义词：settlement, colony, community |反义词：mother country, metropolis
