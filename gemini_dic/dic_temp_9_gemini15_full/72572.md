flux:/flʌks/ | n. v. | 变迁；流量；通量；熔剂 |  A continuous change or movement; a flow or discharge.  |例句：The constant flux of information on the internet can be overwhelming.  互联网上不断变化的信息可能会让人不知所措。


The economic situation is in a state of flux. 经济形势动荡不定。|近义词：flow, change, shift, fluctuation |反义词：stasis, stability, constancy |名词，动词
