outmouthed:/ˌaʊtˈmaʊθt/| vt.|大声说过的；超过…的音量|To speak louder or more forcefully than (someone); to overpower with loudness of speech.|例句：He completely outmouthed his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中完全盖过了对手的声音。 |例句：The speaker outmouthed the hecklers, making his point clear despite the interruptions. 演讲者声音盖过了捣乱者的声音，尽管被打扰，但他还是清晰地表达了自己的观点。|近义词：outtalk, overpower, drown out|反义词：be silenced, be subdued
