raised:/reɪzd/ | v. adj.| 举起；抚养；提高；筹集；引起；提出 | To lift something to a higher position. To bring up and care for a child. To increase the amount or level of something. To collect money for a cause. To bring something to someone's attention. |例句：He raised his hand to ask a question. 他举手提问。  She raised three children on her own. 她独自抚养了三个孩子。近义词：lifted, reared, elevated, increased, brought up |反义词：lowered, dropped, decreased


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He raises his voice when he's angry. = 他生气的时候会提高嗓门。
They raise livestock on their farm. = 他们在农场饲养牲畜。

一般过去时:
She raised her eyebrows in surprise. = 她惊讶地扬起了眉毛。
The company raised prices last month. = 公司上个月提高了价格。

现在完成时:
They have raised a lot of money for charity. = 他们为慈善机构筹集了很多钱。
She has raised three children successfully. = 她成功地抚养了三个孩子。

过去完成时:
He had raised the issue at the previous meeting. = 他在之前的会议上已经提出了这个问题。
They had raised their concerns before the project started. = 项目开始前他们已经表达了他们的担忧。

将来时:
The government will raise taxes next year. = 政府明年将提高税收。
We will raise the flag at sunrise. = 我们将在日出时升起国旗。

虚拟语气:
If he had raised his hand, the teacher would have seen him. = 如果他举手了，老师就会看到他。
I wish I had raised my concerns earlier. = 我希望我早点提出我的担忧。

被动语态:
The issue was raised by several members of the committee. = 这个问题由委员会的几名成员提出。
The price was raised due to inflation. = 价格因通货膨胀而上涨。

疑问句:
Did they raise enough money for the trip? = 他们为这次旅行筹集到足够的钱了吗？
Was the question raised during the meeting? = 会议期间是否提出了这个问题？

否定句:
He didn't raise his hand. = 他没有举手。
They didn't raise any objections. = 他们没有提出任何异议。
