commune:/ˈkɒmjuːn/ /kəˈmjuːn/| n. vi.|公社；社区；分享；交流 | A commune is a group of people living together and sharing possessions and responsibilities.  It can also refer to a feeling of shared experience or a quiet, secluded place. |例句：They established a commune based on socialist principles. 他们建立了一个基于社会主义原则的公社。

例句：The artist found inspiration in the quiet commune with nature.  这位艺术家在与大自然的静谧相处中找到了灵感。


近义词：community, collective, fellowship | 反义词：isolation, solitude


词性：名词(n.),不及物动词(vi.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They commune with nature daily. = 他们每天与自然交流。
The community communes on important decisions. = 社区就重要决定进行协商。


一般过去时:
The group communed in prayer. = 该小组一起祈祷。
The artists communed through their shared love of art. = 艺术家们通过对艺术的共同热爱而彼此沟通。


现在完成时:
We have communed with our ancestors through these rituals. = 我们通过这些仪式与我们的祖先沟通。
The villagers have communed peacefully for generations. = 村民们世世代代和平相处。


过去完成时:
They had communed for hours before reaching a decision. = 他们商讨了数小时才做出决定。
The monks had communed in silence before the sunrise. = 僧侣们在日出前静默地进行祈祷。


将来时:
We will commune with the spirits during the ceremony. = 我们将在仪式期间与灵魂沟通。
The group will commune together to plan the festival. = 该小组将一起商讨节日计划。


虚拟语气:
If they communed more openly, they might resolve their differences. = 如果他们更坦诚地交流，他们可能会解决分歧。
I wish we could commune more often. = 我希望我们能更经常地交流。


被动语态:
(Commune is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Do they commune regularly? = 他们经常交流吗？
How often do you commune with nature? = 你多久与自然交流一次？


否定句:
They did not commune with the outside world. = 他们与外界没有交流。
She refused to commune with her feelings. = 她拒绝与自己的情感沟通。


PHRASE->
commune with nature: 与自然交流
rural commune: 农村公社
commune in silence: 静默地相处/祈祷
intentional community: 共享社区
commune of artists: 艺术家社区

词源:
The word "commune" comes from the Old French word "commune," meaning "common" or "shared."  This in turn derives from the Latin word "communis," also meaning "common," "shared," or "public."  The sense of a group living together evolved later.
