catechist:/ˈkætɪkɪst/| n. |宗教教师，教理问答教师 |A catechist is a person who instructs others in the principles of religion, especially Christianity, often through the use of catechisms (a book of religious instruction).  |Example: The catechist patiently explained the Lord's Prayer to the children.  宗教教师耐心地向孩子们解释了主祷文。|近义词：instructor, teacher, religious educator |反义词：student, pupil
