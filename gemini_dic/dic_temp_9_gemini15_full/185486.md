reunion:/ˌriːˈjuːnjən/| n. |团聚，重聚 |The act of coming together again after a period of separation; a gathering of people who have not seen each other for a long time.  |例句：The family reunion was a joyous occasion.  家庭团聚是一个快乐的时刻。|例句：We had a reunion after twenty years apart.  我们分别二十年后举办了一次重聚。|近义词：reassembly, gathering, meeting |反义词：separation, parting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A family reunion is planned for next summer. = 下个夏天计划举行一次家庭聚会。
The alumni reunion takes place every five years. = 校友聚会每五年举行一次。


一般过去时:
We had a wonderful reunion last year. = 我们去年举行了一次很棒的聚会。
They attended their high school reunion. = 他们参加了他们的高中同学会。


现在完成时:
We have not had a family reunion in many years. = 我们很多年没有举行过家庭聚会了。
They have finally had their long-awaited reunion. = 他们终于举行了他们期待已久的重聚。


过去完成时:
By the time the reunion started, many people had already arrived. = 当聚会开始时，许多人已经到了。
Before the reunion, they had not seen each other for decades. = 聚会之前，他们几十年没见过面了。


将来时:

They will be attending the class reunion. = 他们将参加班级聚会。
