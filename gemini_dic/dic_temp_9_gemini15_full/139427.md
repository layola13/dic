obsessive:/əbˈsɛsɪv/ | adj. | 痴迷的；强迫性的 | characterized by excessive preoccupation with a particular idea or activity; unable to stop thinking about something.  |例句：He has an obsessive need for control. 他有一种强迫性的控制欲。

例句：Her obsessive interest in antiques led her to open her own shop. 她对古董的痴迷让她开了自己的店。

近义词：compulsive, fixated, preoccupied

反义词：indifferent, unconcerned, detached

词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is obsessive about cleanliness. = 他对清洁很痴迷。
She is obsessive about her work. = 她对工作很痴迷。

一般过去时:
He was obsessive about his diet. = 他过去对饮食很痴迷。
She was obsessive about her appearance. = 她过去对自己的外表很痴迷。

现在完成时:
He has been obsessive about collecting stamps for years. = 他多年来一直痴迷于集邮。
She has been obsessive about fitness since she was young. = 她从小就痴迷于健身。

过去完成时:
He had been obsessive about his studies before the exam. = 考试前他对学习非常痴迷。
She had been obsessive about finding a solution to the problem. = 她一直痴迷于找到解决问题的办法。

将来时:
He will be obsessive about his new project. = 他将会痴迷于他的新项目。
She will be obsessive about achieving her goals. = 她将会痴迷于实现她的目标。

虚拟语气:
If he were not so obsessive, he would be happier. = 如果他不那么痴迷，他会更快乐。
If she weren't so obsessive, she would have more friends. = 如果她不那么痴迷，她就会有更多朋友。

被动语态:
His behavior is considered obsessive by many. = 许多人认为他的行为是痴迷的。
Her actions were described as obsessive by the therapist. = 治疗师将她的行为描述为痴迷的。

疑问句:
Is he obsessive about anything in particular? = 他对什么特别痴迷吗？
Is she obsessive about her health? = 她对健康很痴迷吗？

否定句:
He is not obsessive about fashion. = 他并不痴迷于时尚。
She is not obsessive about social media. = 她并不痴迷于社交媒体。
