leveeing: /lɛˈviːɪŋ/ | v. |筑堤；建造堤防|The act of constructing a levee or embankment, typically to prevent flooding.  |例句：The engineers are overseeing the leveeing of the riverbank to protect the town from future floods. 工程师们正在监督河岸的筑堤工程，以保护城镇免受未来的洪水侵害。|近义词：embanking, diking |反义词：breaching (a levee)
