garnet:/ˈɡɑːrnɪt/ | n. |石榴石|A garnet is a hard, reddish gemstone.  Garnets come in various colors, including red, orange, yellow, green, and purple.  The color is usually due to the presence of iron, manganese, or chromium. They are often used in jewelry.|例句：Her necklace featured a beautiful, deep red garnet.  她的项链上有一颗美丽的深红色石榴石。


The ring contained a rare green garnet. 这枚戒指里有一颗稀有的绿色石榴石。|近义词：ruby (for red garnets), gem, jewel |反义词：无

词源:
The word "garnet" comes from the Middle English word "gernet," which itself derives from the Old French word "grenat." This, in turn, is thought to be connected to the Latin word "granatum," meaning "grain" or "seed," referring to the resemblance of the gemstone to the seeds of a pomegranate.
