criticizes:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 批评；批判；挑剔 | To express disapproval of someone or something.  |例句：The critics criticizes his latest movie for its lack of originality. 批评家们批评他最新的电影缺乏原创性。|例句：She criticizes her children's bad habits. 她批评她孩子们的坏习惯。|近义词：condemn, censure, rebuke, fault |反义词：praise, commend, applaud |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He criticizes her work every day. = 他每天都批评她的工作。
The professor criticizes the students' essays. = 教授批评学生的论文。

一般过去时:
She criticized his performance harshly. = 她严厉地批评了他的表现。
They criticized the government's policies. = 他们批评了政府的政策。

现在完成时:
He has criticized the company's actions repeatedly. = 他一再批评公司的行为。
The media has criticized the mayor's handling of the crisis. = 媒体批评了市长处理危机的办法。

过去完成时:
She had criticized his ideas before he even presented them. = 他甚至还没提出自己的想法，她就批评过了。
The committee had criticized the proposal several times. = 委员会已经多次批评了这项提案。

将来时:
They will criticize his new book. = 他们将会批评他的新书。
The reviewers will criticize her latest novel. = 评论家们将批评她的最新小说。

虚拟语气:
If he criticized her, she would be upset. = 如果他批评她，她会不高兴。
I wish he hadn't criticized my work so harshly. = 我真希望他不要那么严厉地批评我的工作。

被动语态:
His work was criticized for being too simplistic. = 他的作品因过于简单而受到批评。
The plan was criticized by the public. = 该计划受到了公众的批评。

疑问句:
Does he often criticize his colleagues? = 他经常批评他的同事吗？
Why did they criticize the project? = 他们为什么批评这个项目？

否定句:
He doesn't criticize others without good reason. = 他不是没有充分理由就批评别人。
They didn't criticize her work, actually they praised it. = 他们并没有批评她的工作，事实上他们表扬了她。


PHRASE->
criticize severely = 严厉批评
criticize unfairly = 不公平地批评
criticize constructively = 建设性地批评
criticize openly = 公开批评
self-criticize = 自我批评
