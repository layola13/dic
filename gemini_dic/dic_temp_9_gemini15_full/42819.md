couranto:/kʊˈrɑːntoʊ/| n.|快板舞曲；快板；新闻简报|A couranto is a lively dance in triple time, popular in the 17th and 18th centuries.  It was also a musical piece written for this dance. The term can also refer to a brief news report or summary. |例句：The musicians played a lively couranto. 音乐家演奏了一首轻快的快板舞曲。

The newspaper published a couranto of the day's events. 报纸刊登了当天事件的新闻简报。|近义词：jig, galliard, news bulletin|反义词：slow dance, ballad
