schizorhinal:/ˌskɪzəˈraɪnəl/| adj. | 裂鼻的，鼻裂的 | Relating to or characterized by a cleft or fissure in the nose.  The term is primarily used in a biological or medical context, often describing a congenital condition. | Example:  The newborn presented with a schizorhinal deformity requiring surgical intervention. = 新生儿出现鼻裂畸形，需要进行手术干预。 |近义词: cleft-nosed |反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be described as "having an intact nose")
