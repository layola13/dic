hedgehogs:/ˈhɛdʒhɒɡz/| n.|刺猬|A small nocturnal mammal with a spiny covering.  Hedgehogs are known for their ability to curl into a ball when threatened.|例句：The hedgehog rolled into a tight ball to protect itself from the fox.  刺猬紧紧地蜷成一团以保护自己免受狐狸的攻击。

例句：We saw a hedgehog crossing the road last night.  昨晚我们看到一只刺猬过马路。


近义词：porcupine (豪猪，但刺猬的刺更短更密)|反义词：None (没有合适的反义词)|词性：名词
