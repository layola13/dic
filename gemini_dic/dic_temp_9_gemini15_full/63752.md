epicenisms:/ˈɛpɪsɪnɪzəm/| n. |(指称男女两性的)中性词；通称词 |Words or names that can refer to both male and female.  They are gender-neutral terms.  Epicenisms avoid gendered language. |Example: The word "student" is an epicenism because it can refer to both male and female students.  The term "chairperson" is another example, acting as a gender-neutral alternative to "chairman".|近义词：unisex terms, gender-neutral terms |反义词：masculine terms, feminine terms
