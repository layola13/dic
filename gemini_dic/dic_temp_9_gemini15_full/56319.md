dogma:/ˈdɒɡmə/ /ˈdɑːɡmə/| n. |教条；信条；教义|A dogma is a principle or set of principles laid down by an authority as incontrovertibly true.  It often implies a rigid adherence to belief, even in the face of contradictory evidence.  Dogmas can be religious, political, or philosophical in nature.|例句：The church's dogma on abortion is strictly against it. 教会的堕胎教条是严格反对的。

The scientist rejected the dogma of spontaneous generation.  这位科学家否定了自然发生论的教条。|近义词：doctrine, tenet, creed, belief|反义词：fact, truth(when dogma is false), skepticism
