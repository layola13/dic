drawing:/ˈdrɔːɪŋ/| n. v.|绘画，图画；绘制，绘画|The act of producing a picture with a pencil, crayon, pen, etc., or the picture so produced.  It can also refer to the act of pulling or dragging something.|例句：Her drawing of the horse was incredibly lifelike.  她的马的素描栩栩如生。

例句：The architect is drawing up plans for a new building. 建筑师正在绘制新建筑的图纸。|近义词：sketch, illustration, design, picture|反义词：erasing, obliterating

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He draws a beautiful landscape every week. 他每周都画一幅美丽的风景画。
She is drawing a self-portrait.  她在画一幅自画像。

一般过去时:
I drew a picture of my cat yesterday. 我昨天画了一幅我猫的画。
They drew a map to show us the way. 他们画了一张地图给我们指路。

现在完成时:
She has drawn many beautiful pictures in her life. 她一生中画过许多美丽的画。
He has drawn a conclusion from the data. 他从数据中得出结论。

过去完成时:
I had drawn the sketch before the meeting started. 会议开始前我已经画好了草图。
They had drawn up the contract before we arrived. 我们到达之前他们已经起草好了合同。

将来时:
I will draw a picture for you tomorrow. 我明天会为你画一幅画。
She will be drawing a mural for the school. 她将为学校绘制一幅壁画。

虚拟语气:
If I could draw, I would illustrate my own books. 如果我会画画，我会为自己的书配图。
I wish I had drawn a better picture. 我希望我画得更好一些。

被动语态:
The picture was drawn by a famous artist. 这幅画是一位著名艺术家画的。
The conclusion was drawn from the experiment results. 结论是从实验结果中得出的。

疑问句:
What are you drawing? 你在画什么？
Who drew this amazing portrait? 谁画了这幅令人惊叹的肖像？

否定句:
He didn't draw the picture. 他没有画那幅画。
I couldn't draw when I was a child. 我小时候不会画画。
