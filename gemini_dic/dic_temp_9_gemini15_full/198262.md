shortwing:/ˈʃɔːrtwɪŋ/| n.|短翅鸟|A small bird with short wings, typically belonging to the family Brachypteridae.|例句：The shortwing flitted between the low branches, its short wings beating rapidly.  短翅鸟在低矮的树枝间飞舞，它那短小的翅膀快速地拍打着。|近义词：babbler (泛指)|反义词：longwing (无此词，可理解为翅膀长的鸟类)|名词
