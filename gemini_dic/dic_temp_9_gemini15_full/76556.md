galleot:/ˈɡæliət/| n.|盖轮船，轻型帆船|A galleot was a type of sailing ship, smaller than a galleon but larger than a caravel, typically having two or three masts and a relatively shallow draft.  They were used for various purposes, including trade, warfare, and exploration.|例句：The galleot sailed smoothly through the narrow channel. 这艘盖轮船顺利地驶过狭窄的海峡。 The Spanish galleot was heavily armed for coastal defense. 这艘西班牙盖轮船装备精良，用于沿海防御。|近义词：caravel, brigantine, xebec|反义词：galleon (larger)|名词
