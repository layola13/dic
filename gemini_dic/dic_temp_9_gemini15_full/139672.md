occur:/əˈkɜːr/| /əˈkɝː/| vi.|发生，出现；存在；想起|To happen; to take place; to be found or met with; to come to mind.|例句：The accident occurred at 3 p.m. 事故发生在下午三点。

例句：It occurred to me that I had forgotten my keys. 我突然想起我忘了带钥匙。

近义词：happen, take place, arise, befall| 反义词：cease, end

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting occurs every Tuesday. = 会议每周二举行。
Problems occasionally occur. = 问题偶尔发生。


一般过去时:
The earthquake occurred at dawn. = 地震发生在黎明。
A strange thing occurred last night. = 昨晚发生了一件奇怪的事情。


现在完成时:
Many changes have occurred since I last saw you. = 自从我上次见到你以来，发生了很多变化。
Several accidents have occurred on this road. = 这条路上发生过几次事故。


过去完成时:
The accident had occurred before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，事故已经发生了。
It had occurred to me that I should have studied harder. = 我突然想起我本应该更努力学习。


将来时:
The event will occur next month. = 活动将在下个月举行。
I think a problem will occur if we don't plan carefully. = 我认为如果我们不仔细计划，就会出现问题。


虚拟语气:
If the opportunity occurred, I would seize it. = 如果有机会出现，我会抓住它。
I wish it hadn't occurred to me to mention it.  = 我真希望我没有提起那件事。


被动语态:
The incident was reported to have occurred yesterday. = 据报道，事件发生在昨天。
An error occurred in the system; it is being investigated. = 系统中发生了一个错误；正在调查中。


疑问句:
When did the problem occur? = 问题是什么时候发生的？
Where did the accident occur? = 事故发生在哪里？


否定句:
The problem didn't occur to me until later. = 直到后来我才想到这个问题。
The meeting didn't occur as planned. = 会议没有按计划进行。


PHRASE->
occur to: 想起，想到
occurred to me: 我突然想起
it occurs to me that: 我突然想到
as it occurs: 随着...发生
frequently occur: 经常发生
