connector:/kəˈnɛktər/ | n. |连接器，连接物 | A device or substance that connects two or more things.  A connector can be physical, like a cable, or conceptual, like a transitional phrase. | Examples: The electrical connector was faulty. (这个电源连接器坏了。) The bridge acts as a connector between the two towns. (这座桥充当了两个城镇之间的连接点。) |近义词：link, joint, coupler, interface |反义词：separator, divider
