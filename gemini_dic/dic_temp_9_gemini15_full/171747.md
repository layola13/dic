pun:/pʌn/| n. |双关语，俏皮话 |A pun is a form of word play that exploits multiple meanings of a term, or of similar-sounding words, for an intended humorous or rhetorical effect.  |例句：He made a pun about the weight of the situation, saying it was a "heavy" topic. 他用一个关于情况重量的双关语，说这是一个“沉重”的话题。|例句：The comedian's act was full of puns, some clever, others groan-inducing.  这位喜剧演员的表演充满了双关语，有些很巧妙，有些则令人痛苦。|近义词：wordplay, play on words |反义词：literal statement
