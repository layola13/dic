The word suggests an increase in energy or speed.|例句：The brisk morning air enquickened her spirits. 清爽的晨风使她精神振奋。|例句：The news enquickened the pace of preparations.  这个消息加快了准备工作的速度。|近义词：animate, invigorate, accelerate, quicken|反义词：depress, slow, retard, dampen|动词
