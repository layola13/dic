resolute:/ˈrezəluːt/| adj.|坚决的；果断的；坚定不移的|firm in purpose or conviction; determined; unwavering.|The general was resolute in his decision to attack.|近义词：determined, firm, steadfast, unwavering|反义词：wavering, irresolute, hesitant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is resolute in his commitment to the project. = 他对这个项目的承诺是坚决的。
She remains resolute despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，她仍然坚定不移。


一般过去时:

She remained resolute even in the face of adversity. = 即使面对逆境，她也依然坚定不移。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
