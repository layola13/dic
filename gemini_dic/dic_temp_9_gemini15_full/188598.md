rumba:/ˈrʌmbə/| n. v.|伦巴舞；伦巴节奏|A lively dance of Cuban origin, characterized by its rhythmic hip movements and typically performed in 4/4 time.|例句：She learned to dance the rumba in Cuba. 她曾在古巴学习跳伦巴舞。|例句：The band played a rumba rhythm. 乐队演奏了伦巴舞曲节奏。|近义词：salsa, mambo|反义词：无
