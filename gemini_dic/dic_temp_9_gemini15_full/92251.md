hyperurban:/ˌhaɪpərˈɜːrbən/ | adj. |高度城市化的；超都市的 | Excessively urban; characterized by extreme urbanization and its associated problems.  The term often implies negative consequences such as overcrowding, pollution, and social issues. |例句：The hyperurban landscape of Mumbai is a stark contrast to the rural villages just outside the city.  孟买的超都市景观与城郊的农村形成了鲜明对比。

The hyperurban sprawl has led to significant environmental degradation.  超都市蔓延导致了严重的環境退化。


近义词：overurbanized, highly urbanized | 反义词：rural, suburban, sparsely populated
