phenanthridine:/fɛˈnænθrɪdiːn/| n. |菲咯啉|A heterocyclic organic compound with the formula C₁₃H₉N. It is a structural isomer of acridine. Phenanthridine is a colorless crystalline solid that is sparingly soluble in water but soluble in organic solvents. It is used as an intermediate in the synthesis of various pharmaceuticals and dyes.  |Example: Researchers are studying the use of phenanthridine derivatives in the development of new anticancer drugs. 研究人员正在研究菲咯啉衍生物在新抗癌药物开发中的应用。|近义词：acridine (structural isomer)|反义词：无
