reinjection:/ˌriːɪnˈdʒɛkʃən/| n. |再注射；重新注入；再投入|The act of injecting something again; the process of reintroducing something that has been removed or stopped.  It can refer to injecting a substance (like medicine or a chemical) or reintroducing something abstract (like capital into a business).|例句：The doctor ordered a reinjection of the painkiller. 医生下令再次注射止痛药。

例句：The government announced a reinjection of funds into the failing project. 政府宣布向失败的项目再次注入资金。

近义词：reintroduction, reapplication, resumption|反义词：withdrawal, removal, cessation|词性:名词
