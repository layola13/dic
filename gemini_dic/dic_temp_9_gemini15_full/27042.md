calendar-years:/ˈkælɪndər jɪrz/| n. |日历年；历年|A calendar year is a period of 12 months as defined by a calendar system, usually starting on January 1st and ending on December 31st.  It's used for accounting, scheduling, and other purposes that require a consistent yearly timeframe. |The company's profits increased significantly over the past three calendar years. 这家公司过去三年的利润显著增长。 The project is scheduled to be completed within two calendar years.  该项目计划在两年内完成。|fiscal years, financial years |lunar years |n.
