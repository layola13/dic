emitters:/ɪˈmɪtərz/| n.|发射器，排放物 | A device or substance that emits something, such as light, heat, or radiation.  Something that gives off or produces something. |Example:  The sun is a powerful emitter of light and heat. 太阳是光和热的强大发射源。

The factory is a major emitter of pollutants. 这家工厂是主要的污染物排放源。|近义词：radiators, sources, dischargers |反义词：absorbers, receivers


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun emits light and heat.  太阳发出光和热。
These devices emit radio waves.  这些设备发射无线电波。


一般过去时:
The volcano emitted a large cloud of ash. 火山喷发出大量的火山灰。
The factory emitted harmful chemicals into the river. 工厂向河里排放有害化学物质。


现在完成时:
The factory has emitted tons of pollutants this year.  这家工厂今年已经排放了数吨污染物。
The star has emitted a powerful burst of energy. 这颗恒星已经释放出一股强大的能量。


过去完成时:
The reactor had emitted radiation before the accident was discovered.  事故被发现之前，反应堆已经释放出辐射。
The machine had emitted a strange noise before it broke down. 机器在故障前发出过奇怪的噪音。


将来时:
The satellite will emit signals back to Earth.  卫星将向地球发射信号。
The device will emit a warning sound if the temperature gets too high. 如果温度过高，设备会发出警告声。


虚拟语气:
If the device emitted a stronger signal, we would receive it. 如果设备发出更强的信号，我们就能收到。
I wish the plant emitted less pollution. 我希望工厂排放更少的污染物。


被动语态:
Radio waves are emitted by the antenna.  无线电波是由天线发射的。
Harmful gases were emitted by the burning cars.  燃烧的汽车排放出有害气体。


疑问句:
What kind of radiation is emitted by this substance?  这种物质会发出什么类型的辐射？
What pollutants are being emitted by the power plant?  发电厂正在排放哪些污染物？


否定句:
The device did not emit any signal. 设备没有发出任何信号。
The new technology doesn't emit harmful radiation. 新技术不会发出有害辐射。
