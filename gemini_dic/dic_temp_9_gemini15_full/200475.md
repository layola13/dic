slammer:/ˈslæmər/| n. v.|监狱；猛击；砰的一声关上|A slammer is an informal term for a prison or jail.  It can also refer to the act of slamming something shut, or the sound of something slamming. |例句：He ended up in the slammer for grand theft auto. 他因盗窃汽车而进了监狱。|近义词：jail, prison, clink, can, bang|反义词：freedom, liberty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mechanic slams the car door shut. = 技工砰地一声关上了车门。
He slams the book on the table. = 他把书砰的一声摔在桌子上。


一般过去时:
The prisoner slammed the cell door in frustration. = 囚犯愤怒地砰地一声关上了牢房门。
She slammed the phone down after a heated argument. = 她在激烈的争吵后把电话摔了。


现在完成时:
The police have slammed the suspect into the patrol car. = 警察已将嫌疑犯塞进警车。
He has slammed the door several times in anger. = 他生气地多次猛关房门。


过去完成时:
The thief had slammed the window shut before the police arrived. = 警察到达前，小偷已砰地一声关上了窗户。
She had slammed the door before I could even speak. = 我还没来得及说话，她就砰地一声关上了门。


将来时:
He will slam the door in my face if I try to talk to him. = 如果我试图和他说话，他会把门摔在我脸上。
They will slam the brakes if they see the accident. = 如果他们看到事故，他们会猛踩刹车。


虚拟语气:
If he were to slam the door again, I'd be furious. = 如果他再砰地一声关门，我会非常生气。
I wish he hadn't slammed the door so hard. = 我真希望他不要把门关得那么用力。


被动语态:
The door was slammed shut by the wind. = 门被风砰地关上了。
The suspect was slammed against the wall by the officer. = 嫌疑犯被警官按在墙上。


疑问句:
Did he slam the door? = 他把门摔了吗？
Why did she slam the book on the table? = 她为什么把书摔在桌子上？


否定句:
He didn't slam the door; he closed it gently. = 他没有把门摔上；他轻轻地关上了门。
She didn't slam the phone down, she hung up calmly. = 她没有把电话摔掉，她平静地挂断了电话。
