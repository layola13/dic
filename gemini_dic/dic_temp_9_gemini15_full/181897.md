remain:/rɪˈmeɪn/| vi.| 留下；保持；依然；剩余 |To continue to exist, especially after other things have gone or ended.  |例句：Despite the storm, the house remained intact. 尽管暴风雨来袭，房子依然完好无损。

例句：Only a few soldiers remained after the battle.  战斗结束后，只剩下少数士兵。


近义词：stay, persist, endure, continue | 反义词：depart, leave, vanish, disappear | 词性：不及物动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remains optimistic despite the setbacks. 他尽管遇到挫折，仍然保持乐观。
The problem remains unsolved. 问题仍然未解决。


一般过去时:
She remained silent throughout the meeting.  她在整个会议中保持沉默。
The building remained empty for years. 这座建筑空置多年。


现在完成时:
The mystery remains unsolved to this day. 这个谜团至今未解。
He has remained loyal to his friends. 他一直忠于他的朋友。


过去完成时:
After everyone else left, he remained.  其他人离开后，他留了下来。
The situation remained unchanged until the next day.  情况直到第二天仍未改变。


将来时:
The question remains to be answered.  这个问题还有待解答。
He will remain in charge until his successor is found. 他将继续负责，直到找到继任者。


虚拟语气:
If he remained silent, they might not notice. 如果他保持沉默，他们可能不会注意到。
It is vital that the evidence remain confidential.  证据必须保密至关重要。


被动语态:
The matter remains to be discussed.  此事尚待讨论。
The building was left, and remained, deserted. 这栋建筑被遗弃了，并且一直荒废着。


疑问句:
Does the problem remain? 问题仍然存在吗？
How long will this situation remain? 这种情况还会持续多久？


否定句:
The suspect did not remain at the scene of the crime. 嫌疑犯没有留在犯罪现场。
He didn't remain in his job for long. 他没有在这个工作岗位上待多久。
