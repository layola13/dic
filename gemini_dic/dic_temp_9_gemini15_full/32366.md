children:/ˈtʃɪldrən/| n. |孩子们，儿童 |The plural of "child".  Refers to young human beings, typically from birth to adolescence.  |例句：The children are playing in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩耍。

例句：We need to provide better education for our children. 我们需要为孩子们提供更好的教育。


近义词：kids, youngsters, minors |反义词：adults


词源:  From Old English *cildru, plural of *cild "child".  The "-ren" ending is a remnant of an older plural formation.


用法说明:


一般现在时:
The children play in the garden every day. = 孩子们每天都在花园里玩耍。
The children always listen to their teacher. = 孩子们总是听老师的话。


一般过去时:
The children played hide-and-seek yesterday. = 孩子们昨天玩捉迷藏。
The children went to the zoo last week. = 孩子们上周去了动物园。


现在完成时:
The children have finished their homework. = 孩子们已经完成了他们的家庭作业。
The children have been to the beach many times. = 孩子们去过海滩很多次了。


过去完成时:
The children had eaten all the cookies before I arrived. = 我到达之前孩子们已经吃掉了所有的饼干。
The children had already left when their parents called. = 当他们的父母打电话时，孩子们已经离开了。


将来时:
The children will visit their grandparents next month. = 孩子们下个月将去看望他们的祖父母。
The children will be going to school soon. = 孩子们很快就要上学了。


虚拟语气:
If the children were older, they could go on the rollercoaster. = 如果孩子们再大一点，他们就可以坐过山车了。
I wish the children were behaving better. = 我希望孩子们表现得更好。


被动语态:
The children are being watched by their parents. = 孩子们正在被他们的父母看着。
The children were given presents for their birthday. = 孩子们收到了生日礼物。


疑问句:
Are the children ready for bed? = 孩子们准备好睡觉了吗？

否定句:
