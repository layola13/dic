pott:/pɒt/| n. |罐，壶；少量；贫民窟|A pot is a container, typically round and usually made of metal, clay, or plastic, used for cooking or storing things.  It can also refer to a small amount of something, or a slum. |例句：She put the stew in a large pot on the stove. 她把炖菜放在炉子上一个大锅里。  He only had a pot of money left. 他只剩下了一点钱。|近义词：jar, container, vessel, kettle |反义词：abundance, plenty
