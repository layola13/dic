monstruosity:/mɒnˈstruːəsɪti/| /mɑːnˈstruːɑːsəti/| n. |畸形；怪诞的事物；极端丑陋的事物 |A monstrosity is something that is extremely ugly, unnatural, or shocking. It can refer to a physical object, a creation, or an event.  The term often carries a strong sense of disapproval or disgust. |例句：The new building is a monstrosity; its architecture is jarring and offensive. 新建筑是个畸形建筑；它的建筑风格刺眼且令人反感。

The film depicted a monstrous monstrosity, a creature of unimaginable horror. 这部电影描绘了一个极其恐怖的怪物，一个难以想象的恐怖生物。|近义词：abomination, atrocity, horror, perversion |反义词：beauty, masterpiece, marvel
