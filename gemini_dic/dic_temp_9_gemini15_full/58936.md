dusks:/dʌsks/ | n. |黄昏，傍晚 | The period of twilight at the end of the day, between sunset and complete darkness.  |例句：The birds sang their evening songs as dusk settled.  傍晚时分，鸟儿们唱起了它们的夜曲。
例句：We walked home together under the dusky sky. 我们在昏暗的天空下一起回家。|近义词：twilight, evening, nightfall |反义词：dawn, sunrise

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dusk settles quickly in winter. 冬天黄昏来临得很快。
Dusk often brings a sense of peace. 黄昏常常带来一种宁静的感觉。

一般过去时:
Dusk fell before we reached the summit. 我们到达山顶之前天就黑了。
The village was bathed in the soft light of dusk. 村庄沐浴在黄昏柔和的光线下。

现在完成时:
Dusk has already fallen. 天已经黑了。
Dusk has brought a chill to the air. 黄昏带来了阵阵寒意。

过去完成时:
Dusk had already fallen when they arrived. 他们到达时天已经黑了。
By the time we left, dusk had settled over the mountains. 我们离开的时候，黄昏已经笼罩了群山。

将来时:
Dusk will fall soon. 天很快就要黑了。
We will watch the sunset as dusk approaches. 我们将在黄昏来临之际观看日落。

虚拟语气:
If dusk had fallen earlier, we would have reached home before dark. 如果黄昏来得早一些，我们就能在天黑前到家了。
I wish dusk would linger longer in summer. 我希望夏天黄昏能持续久一些。

被动语态:
(Dusk cannot be easily put into the passive voice.  The verb "to dusk" is not commonly used in the passive.) 黄昏无法轻易用被动语态表达。"黄昏"这个动词通常不用于被动语态。
The landscape was shrouded in the approaching dusk.  风景被即将到来的黄昏笼罩了。


疑问句:
Has dusk fallen yet? 天黑了吗？
When will dusk begin to fall? 什么时候天开始黑？

否定句:
Dusk had not yet fallen. 天还没黑。
It wasn't quite dusk yet. 现在还不是黄昏。


PHRASE->

during dusk: 在黄昏期间
as dusk falls: 当黄昏来临时
into the dusk: 进入黄昏
