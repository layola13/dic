footwears:/ˈfʊtweərz/| n.|鞋类，鞋袜|Footwear refers to coverings for the feet, encompassing a wide range of items such as shoes, boots, sandals, slippers, and socks.  It is a collective noun referring to various types of footwear.|Example:  She has a wide collection of footwear for all occasions. 她有一大堆适合各种场合的鞋类。|近义词：shoes, boots, footwear, foot covering|反义词：bare feet
