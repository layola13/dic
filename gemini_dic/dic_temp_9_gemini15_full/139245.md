obligations:/ˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. |义务，责任 |The duties or responsibilities that you have because of your job, your relationship with somebody etc.  |例句：He has many obligations as a father. 他作为父亲有很多责任。|例句：The company fulfilled all of its obligations under the contract. 公司履行了合同规定的所有义务。|近义词：responsibilities, duties, commitments |反义词：privileges, rights


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My obligations include paying taxes. = 我的义务包括纳税。
What are your obligations to your family? = 你对家庭有什么义务？

一般过去时:
His obligations prevented him from attending the party. = 他的责任阻止了他参加聚会。
We fulfilled all our obligations on time. = 我们按时履行了所有义务。


现在完成时:
I have met all my obligations for this project. = 我已经完成了这个项目的所有义务。
She has neglected her obligations to her employees. = 她忽略了她对员工的义务。


过去完成时:
He had already fulfilled his obligations before the deadline. = 他在截止日期之前已经履行了他的义务。
They had neglected their obligations for years. = 他们多年来一直忽视他们的义务。


将来时:
I will meet all my obligations promptly. = 我将及时履行我的所有义务。
What obligations will you have next year? = 你明年将有什么义务？


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have these obligations, I would travel the world. = 如果我没有这些义务，我会周游世界。
I wish I didn't have so many obligations. = 我希望我没有那么多义务。


被动语态:
The obligations were clearly stated in the contract. = 合同中明确规定了这些义务。
All obligations are to be met by the end of the month. = 所有义务必须在本月底之前完成。


疑问句:
What are your obligations in this matter? = 在这件事上你的义务是什么？
Do you understand your obligations? = 你明白你的义务吗？


否定句:
He failed to meet his obligations. = 他没有履行他的义务。
She was not aware of her obligations. = 她没有意识到她的义务。
