gurgle:/ˈɡɜːrɡəl/|/ˈɡɜːrɡl/| v. |咕噜咕噜地响，汩汩流；发出咕噜声 |To make a series of bubbling sounds, like water flowing through a narrow space or air bubbling through a liquid.  |例句：The stream gurgled merrily over the smooth stones. 小溪快乐地哗哗流过光滑的石头。

The water gurgled down the drain. 水咕噜咕噜地流进下水道。

|近义词：bubble, gurgle, splash, purl|反义词：silence, hush
