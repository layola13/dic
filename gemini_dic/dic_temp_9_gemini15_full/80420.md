gomuti:/ɡoˈmuːti/ | n. | 砂糖椰子树；砂糖椰子的纤维； | A type of palm tree ( *Borassus flabellifer*) known for its strong fibers used in making various items, and its sap which can be processed into sugar.  | The gomuti palm is a source of strong fibers used for making ropes and mats. 砂糖椰子树是制造绳索和垫子的坚韧纤维的来源。 |  Arenga pinnata |  (no clear antonym) | 名词
