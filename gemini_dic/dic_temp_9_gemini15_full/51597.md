devast:/ˈdɛvəsteɪt/| vt.|毁坏；破坏；使震惊；使极度悲伤|to destroy or severely damage something:|The earthquake devastated the city. 地震摧毁了这座城市。|近义词：destroy, ruin, wreck, shatter|反义词：build, construct, create, repair

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm devastates coastal areas every year. = 暴风雨每年都摧毁沿海地区。
He devastates his opponents in every debate. = 他在每一次辩论中都击败了他的对手。


一般过去时:
The fire devastated the entire forest. = 大火摧毁了整片森林。
The news devastated her. = 这个消息使她非常震惊。


现在完成时:
The war has devastated the country's economy. = 战争已经摧毁了该国的经济。
The scandal has devastated his reputation. = 这场丑闻毁了他的名誉。


过去完成时:
The drought had devastated the crops before the rains came. = 在雨水到来之前，干旱已经摧毁了庄稼。
She had already been devastated by the loss of her job before receiving the news of her father's death. = 在收到她父亲去世的消息之前，她就已经因为失业而感到非常悲痛了。


将来时:
The hurricane is expected to devastate the island. = 预计飓风将摧毁该岛屿。
If he continues this way, his behavior will devastate his family. = 如果他继续这样下去，他的行为将会摧毁他的家庭。


虚拟语气:
If the flood had come earlier, it would have devastated the entire town. = 如果洪水早些到来，它就会摧毁整个城镇。
I wish the news hadn't devastated her so much. = 我希望这个消息没有让她如此伤心。


被动语态:
The town was devastated by the tornado. = 这个城镇被龙卷风摧毁了。
His reputation has been devastated by the allegations. = 他的名誉因这些指控而受损。


疑问句:
What will devastate the ecosystem in the future? = 未来什么会破坏生态系统？
Has the recent economic downturn devastated your business? = 最近的经济衰退是否摧毁了你的生意？


否定句:
The storm did not devastate the city as much as expected. = 暴风雨并没有像预期的那样摧毁这座城市。
The news, thankfully, did not devastate her completely. = 谢天谢地，这个消息并没有让她完全崩溃。


PHRASE->
devastate a country = 摧毁一个国家
devastate an area = 摧毁一个地区
be devastated by = 被…摧毁
devastate someone emotionally = 使某人情绪崩溃
devastate someone's hopes = 粉碎某人的希望
