pikes:/paɪks/| n.|梭镖；长矛；鱼叉；尖峰|A pike is a long, pointed weapon, similar to a spear, used for thrusting.  It can also refer to a type of fish or a pointed peak. |例句：The knight carried a pike into battle.  骑士带着长矛上战场。

例句：The mountain range is characterized by its sharp pikes.  山脉的特点是其尖锐的山峰。


近义词：spear, lance, javelin, spike


反义词：无


词源：源自古英语 *pīc,  意为“尖头武器”。
