recollate:/ˌriːˈkɒleɪt/ | /ˌriːˈkɑːleɪt/| vt.|重新整理；校对；重新编排|To arrange or organize (something) again, typically in a more systematic way; to verify or check the accuracy of something, often by comparing it to a source document. |例句：The librarian had to recollate the scattered pages of the ancient manuscript. 图书管理员不得不重新整理古代手稿散落的页码。


例句：The team leader asked me to recollate the data to ensure accuracy before submitting the report. 团队领导让我在提交报告之前重新校对数据以确保准确性。


近义词：rearrange, reorganize, collate, verify, check


反义词：disorganize, scatter, jumble


词性:动词
