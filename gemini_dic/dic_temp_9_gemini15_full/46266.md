czar's:/zɑːrz/| n. |沙皇的；沙皇的财产|The possessive form of "czar," referring to something belonging to or associated with a czar (a Russian emperor).  It's used as an adjective or to show possession.|Example: The czar's palace was magnificent. 沙皇的宫殿非常宏伟。|近义词：emperor's, sovereign's, monarch's|反义词：subject's, citizen's


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and therefore doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  It's primarily used as an adjectival possessive.

Example 1:  The czar's decree was immediately enforced. 沙皇的法令立即得到执行。
Example 2: We examined the czar's extensive art collection. 我们检查了沙皇庞大的艺术收藏。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "czar's" as it is a possessive form. However, phrases could be constructed around it such as:

Example 1: the czar's winter palace 沙皇的冬宫
Example 2: the czar's secret police 沙皇的秘密警察
