simulate:/ˈsɪmjuleɪt/ | vt. | 模拟，模仿，假装 | To imitate the appearance or function of (something); to create a model of (a system or process) to study its behavior. |例句：The software simulates real-world conditions.  该软件模拟现实环境。

例句：Scientists used computer models to simulate the effects of climate change. 科学家们使用计算机模型来模拟气候变化的影响。


近义词：imitate, mimic, feign, model, represent | 反义词：None (antonyms depend on context; e.g., if simulating reality, the antonym might be "to experience directly") | 词性: Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The program simulates flight conditions. = 该程序模拟飞行条件。
They simulate a disaster scenario for training. = 他们模拟灾难场景进行训练。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
