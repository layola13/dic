endears:/ɪnˈdɪrz/| vt.|使受喜爱；使受疼爱；|to make someone like or love you|Her kindness endeared her to everyone.  她的善良使她深受大家的喜爱。|His witty remarks endeared him to the audience. 他诙谐的谈吐博得了观众的喜爱。|近义词：charm, attract, win over|反义词：alienate, repel
