resting:/ˈrɛstɪŋ/ | adj. | 静止的；休息的；安息的 |  Describes something that is in a state of inactivity, repose, or stillness;  also refers to something providing rest or relaxation. | 例句：The resting heart rate of a healthy adult is typically between 60 and 100 beats per minute.  健康成年人的静息心率通常在每分钟 60 到 100 次之间。

The resting position of the lever is horizontal.  杠杆的静止位置是水平的。| 近义词：inactive, still, quiescent, dormant | 反义词：active, moving, dynamic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird is resting on the branch. = 鸟儿栖息在树枝上。
The car is resting in the garage. = 车停放在车库里。


一般过去时:
He rested after a long day of work. = 他在一天辛苦的工作后休息了。
She rested her head on the pillow. = 她把头枕在枕头上。


现在完成时:
I have been resting all afternoon. = 我整个下午都在休息。
They have rested sufficiently for the journey. = 他们已经充分休息好了，准备旅行。


过去完成时:
I had rested for an hour before he arrived. = 他来之前我已经休息了一个小时了。
She had rested well, so she felt refreshed. = 她休息得很好，所以感觉很清爽。


将来时:
I will rest when I get home. = 我回家后就要休息。
We will rest at the campsite tonight. = 我们今晚将在营地休息。


虚拟语气:
If I had rested more, I wouldn't be so tired. = 如果我休息更多的话，就不会这么累了。
I wish I had rested yesterday. = 我希望我昨天休息了。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
