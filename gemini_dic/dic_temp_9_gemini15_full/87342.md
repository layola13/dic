heroes:/ˈhɪərəʊz/| n. |英雄们，勇士们 |People who are admired for their courage, outstanding achievements, or noble qualities.  Heroes can be real people or fictional characters.  |例句：The firefighters were hailed as heroes for saving the children from the burning building. 消防队员们因从燃烧的建筑物中救出孩子们而被誉为英雄。

例句：Many children idolize superheroes from comic books and movies. 许多孩子崇拜漫画书和电影中的超级英雄。


近义词：champions, legends, saviors, idols |反义词：villains, antagonists, cowards
