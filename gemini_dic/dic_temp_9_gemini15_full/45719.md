curves:/kɜːrvz/| n.|曲线，弯曲；曲线图；曲线美|A curve is a line that gradually bends.  It can refer to a physical shape or a graphical representation of data.  The word also implies a graceful, elegant shape, often associated with a woman's body.|例句：The road has many sharp curves. 这条路有很多急转弯。

The graph shows a smooth curve.  图表显示一条平滑的曲线。|近义词：bend, arc, line, contour|反义词：straight line, straightness|名词
