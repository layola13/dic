fizzer:/ˈfɪzə(r)/| n.|失败的人或事；不起作用的东西；（汽水等）嘶嘶作响的声音 |A fizzer is something that fails to have the desired effect or is a complete failure. It can also refer to a hissing sound, like that of a fizzy drink.  |例句：The party was a complete fizzer.  派对完全失败了。

The fireworks display was a bit of a fizzer; most of them didn't go off. 烟火表演有点失败；大多数都没放起来。

近义词：failure, flop, dud, disappointment |反义词：success, triumph, hit
