loafers:/ˈloʊfərz/ | n. |懒汉鞋，便鞋 |Soft, comfortable shoes, typically without laces, often made of leather or suede.  They are typically worn casually. |例句：He wore loafers with his chinos. 他穿着休闲裤搭配懒汉鞋。 |例句：Those loafers are really stylish. 那些懒汉鞋真的很时尚。|近义词：slip-ons, moccasins |反义词：boots, high heels |名词
