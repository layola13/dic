highmark:/ˈhaɪmɑːrk/| n.|高水准，高点，最佳成绩|A highmark represents a peak achievement or a superior quality.  It can refer to a top score, a significant milestone, or an exceptional example of something. |例句：Her performance was a highmark in her career.  她的表演是她职业生涯中的巅峰之作。

例句：The new design is a highmark of modern architecture.  这个新设计是现代建筑的典范。|近义词：peak, pinnacle, apex, zenith|反义词：low point, nadir, bottom
