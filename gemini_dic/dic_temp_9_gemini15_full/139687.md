oceanarium:/ˌoʊʃəˈneəriəm/| n. |海洋馆|A large building with tanks of seawater where many kinds of sea animals are kept and shown to the public.  It's a place where you can see various marine life up close. |例句：The Monterey Bay Aquarium is a world-renowned oceanarium.  蒙特雷湾水族馆是一个世界闻名的海洋馆。|例句： We spent the afternoon at the city's new oceanarium, marveling at the colorful fish and playful seals. 我们在城市新建的海洋馆度过了下午，惊叹于色彩斑斓的鱼类和顽皮的海豹。|近义词：aquarium (though usually smaller and may not exclusively feature marine life)|反义词：None.
