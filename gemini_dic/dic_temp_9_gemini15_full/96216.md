indexing:/ˈɪndɛksɪŋ/| n. v.|索引，编制索引|The process of creating an index, typically for a database or a book.  It involves assigning unique identifiers or keywords to data elements for efficient retrieval.|例句：The database indexing improved search speed significantly. 数据库索引显著提高了搜索速度。

例句：We need to improve the indexing of our library catalog. 我们需要改进图书馆目录的索引。


近义词：cataloging, cataloguing,  registering|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be removing index entries or not indexing at all.|词性：Noun, Verb
