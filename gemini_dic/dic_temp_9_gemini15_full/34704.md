cleverest: /ˈklɛvərɪst/ | adj. | 最聪明的，最机灵的 |  The superlative form of "clever," indicating the highest degree of intelligence, skill, or ingenuity.  It describes someone or something that is exceptionally intelligent and resourceful. | 例句：She is the cleverest student in her class. = 她是班上最聪明的学生。 He devised the cleverest solution to the problem. = 他想出了解决这个问题的最巧妙的办法。 | 近义词：most intelligent, most ingenious, most resourceful, brightest, sharpest | 反义词：most foolish, dullest, stupidest, unintelligent
