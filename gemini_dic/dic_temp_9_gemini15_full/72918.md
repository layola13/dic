fondant:/ˈfɑːndənt/ /ˈfɒndænt/| n. |糖霜，软糖 |A type of candy made from sugar, water, and sometimes other ingredients, that is smooth, creamy, and pliable. It is often used to cover cakes or make decorative elements. |The cake was covered in a smooth, white fondant. 这蛋糕覆盖着一层光滑的白色糖霜。The confectioner skillfully sculpted flowers from fondant.  糖果师熟练地用糖霜雕刻出花朵。| icing, marzipan | hard candy | 名词
