semisavages:/ˈsemiˌsævɪdʒɪz/| adj.|半野蛮的，未开化的|Partially or somewhat savage; not fully civilized.  The term often carries a judgmental connotation, implying a lack of refinement or sophistication compared to a perceived civilized standard.  It's important to note that this term is often considered outdated and potentially offensive due to its ethnocentric bias.|例句：The explorer described the indigenous people as semisavages, a view now considered highly problematic. 探险家将土著居民描述为半野蛮人，这种观点现在被认为极具问题性。


例句：Some Victorian-era writers portrayed non-European cultures as semisavages, reflecting the prejudices of their time.  一些维多利亚时代的作家将非欧洲文化描绘成半野蛮的，反映了他们那个时代的偏见。


近义词：uncivilized, barbarian, primitive, underdeveloped


反义词：civilized, cultured, refined, sophisticated
