divides:/dɪˈvaɪdz/ | vt. | 分配；分开；划分；使分裂 | To separate into parts; to share out among several people; to cause to be separate or distinct. |例句：The river divides the city into two parts. 这条河将城市分为两部分。|例句：The cake was divided equally among the children. 蛋糕被孩子们平均分了。|近义词：separates, splits, shares, distributes |反义词：unites, joins, combines |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river divides the land. = 这条河分割了这片土地。
She divides her time between work and family. = 她把时间分给了工作和家庭。


一般过去时:
They divided the spoils of war. = 他们瓜分了战利品。
He divided the class into four groups. = 他把班级分成了四个小组。


现在完成时:
We have divided the tasks fairly. = 我们已经公平地分配了任务。
She has divided her inheritance among her children. = 她已经把遗产分给了她的孩子们。


过去完成时:
They had divided the cake before I arrived. = 我到之前他们已经把蛋糕分好了。
He had divided his assets before his death. = 他死前已经分配了他的资产。


将来时:
We will divide the work equally. = 我们将平均分配工作。
They will divide the profits among the partners. = 他们将把利润分给合伙人。


虚拟语气:
If we divided the resources fairly, everyone would benefit. = 如果我们公平地分配资源，每个人都会受益。
I wish they had divided the workload more evenly. = 我希望他们更平均地分配工作量。


被动语态:
The land is divided by the river. = 这片土地被河流分割。
The profits will be divided among the shareholders. = 利润将被分配给股东。


疑问句:
How will you divide the money? = 你将如何分配这笔钱？
How is the work divided among the team members? = 工作是如何在团队成员之间分配的？


否定句:
They did not divide the property fairly. = 他们没有公平地分配财产。
The class was not divided into groups. = 班级没有分成小组。


PHRASE->
divide and conquer = 分而治之
divide by = 除以
divide into = 分成
divide up = 分配
sharply divided = 严重分裂


词源:
源自古法语 *departir  (to depart, to divide)  and Latin *partire (to divide, to share).  The prefix "di-" indicates separation or division.
