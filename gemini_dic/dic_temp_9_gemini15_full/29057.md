caruncle:/ˈkærʌŋkəl/| n. |小肉瘤，肉阜，(眼睑的)小肉赘|A small fleshy outgrowth, especially one on the eye.  It can also refer to other small, fleshy protuberances on the body.|例句：The veterinarian noticed a caruncle on the chicken's wattle.  兽医注意到鸡的肉垂上有一个小肉瘤。|例句：She had a small caruncle on her eyelid. 她的眼睑上长了一个小肉赘。|近义词：excrescence, nodule, protuberance |反义词：无
