scrimps:/skrɪmps/ | v. | 吝啬；节省；勉强维持 | To be unwilling to spend money; to live very economically, often to the point of hardship.  |  He scrimped and saved for years to buy a house. 他多年来省吃俭用买了一套房子。  |  save, economize, pinch | squander, splurge | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrimps on everything. = 他对所有东西都吝啬。
She scrimps to make ends meet. = 她省吃俭用以维持生计。

一般过去时:
They scrimped and saved for their children's education. = 他们为了孩子的教育省吃俭用。
He scrimped during his college years. = 他在大学期间省吃俭用。

现在完成时:
We have scrimped and saved for a down payment on a house. = 我们省吃俭用攒首付买房子。
She has scrimped all her life. = 她一辈子都省吃俭用。

过去完成时:
They had scrimped for months before they could afford the trip. = 他们在几个月前就开始省吃俭用，才能负担得起这次旅行。
She had scrimped and saved her whole life for that moment. = 她为那一刻省吃俭用了一辈子。

将来时:
We will scrimp and save to send our son to university. = 我们将省吃俭用送儿子上大学。
They will scrimp to buy a new car. = 他们将省吃俭用买一辆新车。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I wouldn't have to scrimp. = 如果我有更多钱，我就不用省吃俭用了。
I wish I didn't have to scrimp so much. = 我希望我不必如此省吃俭用。

被动语态:  (Scrimp is rarely used in the passive voice.)
Money should not be scrimped on when it comes to education. = 教育方面不应该吝啬金钱。(被动语态，但强调“金钱”而非“省钱”的动作)
Resources were scrimped on during the project. = 项目期间资源受到了节约。(被动语态，但强调资源受到影响)


疑问句:
Does he scrimp on food? = 他在食物上吝啬吗？
Do they scrimp to save for retirement? = 他们为了退休储蓄而省吃俭用吗？

否定句:
They don't scrimp on quality. = 他们不吝啬质量。
He doesn't scrimp when it comes to his family. = 当涉及到他的家人时，他不吝啬。


PHRASE->
scrimp and save = 省吃俭用
scrimp on something = 在...方面吝啬
scrimp and scrape = 勉强维持生计
to scrimp and save for something = 为了...省吃俭用
scrimping and saving = 省吃俭用 (动名词短语)
