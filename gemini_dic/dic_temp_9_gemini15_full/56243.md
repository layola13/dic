dog-house:/ˈdɒɡhaʊs/ /ˈdɔːɡhaʊs/ | n. |狗窝，狗舍 |A small structure, typically made of wood, for a dog to sleep in.  It provides shelter and protection from the elements.|例句：The dog happily curled up in its new dog-house.  那条狗高兴地蜷缩在新狗窝里。

例句：We built a dog-house for our new puppy. 我们为新来的小狗建了一个狗窝。


近义词：kennel, dog shelter | 反义词：None.
