delusion:/dəˈluʒən/| n. |妄想；错觉；谬见 |A delusion is a false belief that is strongly held despite evidence to the contrary. It is often a symptom of a mental illness.  |Example: He suffered from the delusion that he was being followed by spies. 他患有被间谍跟踪的妄想症。|近义词：fantasy, hallucination, illusion|反义词：reality, truth


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He has a delusion that he is wealthy. = 他有一种自己是富人的妄想。
Her delusion causes her to act strangely. = 她的妄想导致她行为古怪。


一般过去时:
He held a delusion about his abilities. = 他对自己的能力抱有幻想。
The delusion affected his judgment. = 妄想影响了他的判断。


现在完成时:
She has harbored this delusion for years. = 她多年来一直抱有这种妄想。
His delusions have worsened recently. = 他最近的妄想加重了。


过去完成时:
He had held that delusion since childhood. = 他从小就抱有那种妄想。
Before the treatment, she had experienced severe delusions. = 在治疗之前，她经历了严重的妄想。


将来时:
He will likely continue to suffer from delusions. = 他很可能会继续遭受妄想的困扰。
His delusions will probably worsen without treatment. = 他的妄想如果不治疗可能会恶化。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't have this delusion, he would be happier. = 如果他没有这种妄想，他会更快乐。
I wish he could overcome his delusion. = 我希望他能克服他的妄想。


被动语态:
His delusion was diagnosed as schizophrenia. = 他的妄想被诊断为精神分裂症。
The delusion was effectively treated with medication. = 妄想通过药物有效地得到治疗。


疑问句:
What is the root cause of his delusion? = 他妄想的根本原因是什么？
Does she understand the nature of her delusion? = 她明白她妄想的性质吗？


否定句:
He doesn't have any delusions, as far as we know. = 据我们所知，他没有妄想。
She doesn't realize her beliefs are delusions. = 她没有意识到她的信念是妄想。
