gallery:/ˈɡæləri/| n.|画廊；美术馆；陈列室|A gallery is a room or building for the display or sale of works of art, or a collection of such works.|例句：The art gallery showcased many famous paintings. 这家美术馆展出了许多名画。|例句：The museum's gallery featured a stunning collection of sculptures. 博物馆的画廊展出了一系列令人惊叹的雕塑。|近义词：museum, exhibition, showroom|反义词：N/A
