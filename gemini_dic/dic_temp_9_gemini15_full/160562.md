porosities:/ˌpɔːrˈɒsɪtiz/ | n. | 孔隙度，多孔性 | The interconnectedness of pores within a material.  It's a measure of the void spaces in a material, often expressed as a percentage.  High porosity materials have many interconnected pores, while low porosity materials have few or small pores. | Example: The high porosities of the sandstone allowed for significant groundwater storage.  高砂岩的孔隙度允许大量的地下水储存。 The rock's porosities affect its ability to absorb water.  岩石的孔隙度影响其吸水能力。|近义词：porousness, permeability | 反义词：density, compactness |  名词
