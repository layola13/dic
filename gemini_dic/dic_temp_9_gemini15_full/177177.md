rearm:/riːˈɑːrm/| vt.|重新武装；再次装备|To equip (someone or something) again with arms or weapons; to provide with arms or weapons again.|例句：The army was rearmed with the latest weapons.  军队重新装备了最新的武器。|例句：The rebels rearmed themselves after the ceasefire.  停火后，叛军重新武装自己。|近义词：re-equip, refit, supply|反义词：disarm, decommission|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The country rearms its military every year. = 这个国家每年都为军队重新配备武器。
They rearm their troops before the battle. = 他们在战斗前为军队重新配备武器。


一般过去时:
The government rearmed the army after the war. = 战争结束后，政府为军队重新配备了武器。
The soldiers rearmed themselves quickly. = 士兵们迅速地重新武装了自己。
