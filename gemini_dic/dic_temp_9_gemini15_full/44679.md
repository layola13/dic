cruciform:/ˈkruːsɪfɔːrm/| adj.|十字形的，十字交叉形的|Cross-shaped; having the form of a cross.  The term derives from the Latin word "crux," meaning "cross."  It is often used to describe objects or structures with a cross-like shape, particularly in architecture and biology.|例句：The church features a cruciform design. 这座教堂采用十字形设计。

The stained-glass window is cruciform in shape. 彩绘玻璃窗是十字形的。|近义词：cross-shaped, crosslike|反义词：circular, square
