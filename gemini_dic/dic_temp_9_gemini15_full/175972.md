rattier:/ˈrætiər/| adj.|更像老鼠的；更肮脏的；更破旧的 |More like a rat; dirtier; shabbier.  The rattier parts of town are best avoided at night.  |近义词：filthier, dirtier, shabbier, more dilapidated |反义词：cleaner, neater, more pristine


例句:

The rattier the building, the cheaper the rent. = 建筑物越破旧，租金越便宜。

His rattier clothes spoke volumes about his financial situation. = 他破旧的衣服充分说明了他的经济状况。
