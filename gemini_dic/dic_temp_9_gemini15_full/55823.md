dividant:/ˈdɪvɪdænd/| n. |股息，红利|A dividend is a payment made by a corporation to its shareholders, usually as a distribution of profits.  It represents a share of the company's earnings that is paid out to investors.|Example: The company announced a generous dividend payout to its shareholders.  这家公司宣布向股东支付丰厚的股息。|近义词：profit distribution, payout|反义词：loss, deficit|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company pays a dividend every quarter. = 公司每季度支付一次股息。
Investors receive dividends based on their shareholding. = 投资者根据其持股量获得股息。


一般过去时:
The company paid a large dividend last year. = 公司去年支付了巨额股息。
They received a dividend check in the mail. = 他们收到了一张邮寄的股息支票。


现在完成时:
The company has paid out significant dividends this year. = 公司今年已支付了可观的股息。
We have received our dividends for the past three quarters. = 我们已经收到了过去三个季度的股息。


过去完成时:
The shareholders had already received their dividends before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，股东们已经收到了他们的股息。
The company had not paid a dividend in several years. = 这家公司好几年没有支付股息了。


将来时:
The company will pay a dividend next month. = 公司下个月将支付股息。
They expect to receive a higher dividend next year. = 他们预计明年将获得更高的股息。


虚拟语气:
If the company were profitable, it would pay a dividend. = 如果公司盈利，它就会支付股息。
I wish the company had paid a larger dividend. = 我希望公司支付更高的股息。


被动语态:
Dividends are paid out quarterly. = 股息按季度支付。
A dividend was declared by the board of directors. = 董事会宣布派发股息。


疑问句:
Will the company pay a dividend this year? = 公司今年会支付股息吗？
What is the dividend yield of this stock? = 这只股票的股息收益率是多少？


否定句:
The company did not pay a dividend this quarter. = 公司本季度没有支付股息。
They will not receive a dividend this year. = 他们今年将不会收到股息。


PHRASE->
