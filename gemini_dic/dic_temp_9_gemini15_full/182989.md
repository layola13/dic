repledged:/ˌriːˈplɛdʒd/| v. | 再抵押；重新承诺 | To pledge something again; to reaffirm a commitment.  |例句：The company repledged its assets to secure a new loan. 公司再次抵押其资产以获得新贷款。

例句：He repledged his support for the project after initial doubts.  在最初的怀疑之后，他再次承诺支持该项目。

近义词：rededicate, reaffirm, recommit | 反义词：revoke, renege, retract | 词性:动词
