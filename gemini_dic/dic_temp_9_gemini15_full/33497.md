ciao:/ˈtʃaʊ/| int.|再见，你好|An informal Italian greeting used for both "hello" and "goodbye".  It's a versatile word expressing casual friendliness. |例句：Ciao, how are you?  你好，你好吗？
例句：Ciao for now! 再见！|近义词：hello, goodbye, bye, hi|反义词:None


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't have verb conjugations like "to be" or "to do".  It's used as a complete utterance in itself.  Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.


PHRASE->
Ciao a presto! = See you soon!
Ciao bella! = Hello beautiful! (typically used to address a woman)
Ciao ragazzi! = Hi guys!
A presto, ciao! = See you soon, goodbye!
Ciao for now! = See you later!
