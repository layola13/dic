reestimated:/ˌriːˈɛstɪmeɪtɪd/| v. |重新估算；重新估计|The project was reestimated after the initial cost proved too low.  (项目在最初的成本证明过低后被重新估算。)  The damages were reestimated to be significantly higher than initially thought. (损失被重新评估，远高于最初的估计。) |estimate, reassess| underestimate, overestimate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reestimate the project cost regularly. = 他们定期重新估算项目成本。
We reestimate the budget every quarter. = 我们每个季度都重新评估预算。

一般过去时:
The contractor reestimated the job after finding unforeseen issues. = 承包商在发现不可预见的问题后重新估算了工作。
The committee reestimated the population after the census. = 人口普查后，委员会重新估算人口。

现在完成时:
They have reestimated the project's timeline several times. = 他们已经多次重新评估了项目的进度。
The government has reestimated the economic growth for this year. = 政府已经重新评估了今年的经济增长。

过去完成时:
They had reestimated the budget before the final decision. = 他们在最终决定之前已经重新估算了预算。
The team had reestimated the risk factors before proceeding. = 团队在继续之前已经重新评估了风险因素。

将来时:
They will reestimate the damage after the storm passes. = 风暴过后，他们将重新评估损失。
We will reestimate the cost of materials next week. = 我们下周将重新估算材料成本。

虚拟语气:
If they had reestimated the costs earlier, they would have avoided the shortfall. = 如果他们早点重新估算成本，他们就能避免资金短缺。
I wish they had reestimated the time needed for the project. = 我真希望他们能重新评估项目所需的时间。

被动语态:
The project's budget was reestimated by the management team. = 项目预算由管理团队重新估算。
The damage was reestimated to be much higher than initially reported.  = 损失被重新评估，远高于最初报告的数字。


疑问句:
Has the value of the property been reestimated recently? = 房地产的价值最近是否被重新估算过？
Will the project timeline be reestimated after the delays? = 项目进度在延误后是否会被重新估算？

否定句:
The company did not reestimate the project costs, resulting in a budget deficit. = 公司没有重新估算项目成本，导致预算赤字。
They haven't reestimated the risk yet. = 他们还没有重新评估风险。
