cozier:/ˈkoʊziər/| adj.|更舒适的，更惬意的 | More comfortable and pleasant.  |例句：The new sofa made the living room much cozier. 新沙发让客厅舒适多了。|例句：The fireplace made the cabin cozier on a cold night.壁炉让小屋在寒冷的夜晚更加舒适。|近义词：more comfortable, snugger, more snug, warmer|反义词：uncomfortable, cold, drafty
