prologist:/ˈproʊlədʒɪst/| n. |序言撰写者；开场白撰写者|A person who writes prologues.  Someone who composes or delivers a prologue, often for a play or a book.| The prologist for the play delivered a captivating introduction that set the stage for the performance.  序言撰写者为该剧作了一个引人入胜的开场白，为演出奠定了基础。|近义词：introducer, preface writer|反义词：epilogist
