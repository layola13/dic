nictate:/ˈnɪkteɪt/| v.| 眨眼；(动物)瞬膜闭合 |To close and open the eyes quickly.  Also refers to the action of a nictitating membrane in some animals. |例句：The old man nictitated slowly, his eyes tired.  老人缓慢地眨了眨眼，眼睛很疲倦。

例句：The bird nictitated its third eyelid across its eye.  那只鸟用它的瞬膜遮住了眼睛。


近义词：blink, wink,  (for animals) close (the nictitating membrane)


反义词：stare, keep eyes open


词性:动词
