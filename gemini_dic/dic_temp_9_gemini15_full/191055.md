scandalmongering:/ˈskændəlˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ/| n. |造谣中伤；散布丑闻；搬弄是非 |The act of spreading scandalous rumors or information.  It involves the deliberate creation and dissemination of gossip and false accusations to damage someone's reputation. |例句：The constant scandalmongering in the media is damaging to the reputation of the company.媒体持续不断的造谣中伤正在损害公司的声誉。

The opposition party engaged in relentless scandalmongering to discredit the government. 反对党为了诋毁政府而不断地搬弄是非。


近义词：slander, defamation, muckraking, character assassination, gossiping |反义词：truth-telling, honesty, integrity
