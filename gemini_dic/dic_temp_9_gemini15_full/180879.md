rehospitalizes: /ˌriːˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 再住院；二次住院 | To admit (a patient) to a hospital again after a previous discharge.  |例句：The patient was rehospitalized due to complications from the surgery.  病人因手术并发症而再次住院。 The high readmission rate indicates a need for improved post-discharge care to prevent rehospitalizations. 高复诊率表明需要改进出院后的护理以防止再次住院。| 近义词：readmit, return to hospital | 反义词：discharge, release from hospital |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hospital rehospitalizes many patients each year. = 医院每年都会让许多病人再次住院。
They rehospitalize patients with serious complications. = 他们让有严重并发症的病人再次住院。

一般过去时:
The doctor rehospitalized the patient after observing his condition. = 医生观察了病人的病情后，让他再次住院。
She was rehospitalized last week due to a sudden relapse. = 她因为突然复发而上周再次住院。


现在完成时:
The hospital has rehospitalized him three times this year. = 医院今年已经让他三次再次住院了。
They have rehospitalized numerous patients suffering from chronic illness. = 他们已经让许多患有慢性病的病人再次住院了。


过去完成时:
He had been rehospitalized twice before his final recovery. = 在他最终康复之前，他已经两次再次住院了。
The surgeons had rehospitalized her after discovering a post-operative infection. = 外科医生在发现术后感染后，让她再次住院了。


将来时:
The doctors believe he will be rehospitalized if his condition worsens. = 医生认为如果他的病情恶化，他将再次住院。
They predict that she will likely be rehospitalized next month. = 他们预测她下个月可能会再次住院。


虚拟语气:
If his condition hadn't improved, he would have been rehospitalized. = 如果他的病情没有好转，他就会再次住院了。
I wish they hadn't rehospitalized her so quickly. = 我希望他们没有那么快让她再次住院。


被动语态:
He was rehospitalized against his will. = 他被迫再次住院。
Many patients are rehospitalized due to preventable errors. = 许多病人因可避免的错误而再次住院。


疑问句:

Will she be rehospitalized soon? = 她很快会再次住院吗？


否定句:
They did not rehospitalize the patient after the initial assessment. = 他们在初步评估后没有让病人再次住院。
The patient was not rehospitalized despite the concerns. = 尽管存在担忧，病人并没有再次住院。
