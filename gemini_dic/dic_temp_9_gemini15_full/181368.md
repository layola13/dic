reinvigorate:/ˌriːɪnˈvɪɡəreɪt/ | vt. |使恢复活力；使重新充满活力 | To give new energy or strength to something or someone.  |例句：The holiday reinvigorated him.  假期使他恢复了活力。  The new manager reinvigorated the team. 新经理使团队重获活力。|近义词：revitalize, refresh, rejuvenate, energize |反义词：exhaust, deplete, weaken


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain reinvigorates the plants. = 雨水使植物恢复了活力。
He reinvigorates himself with a cup of coffee every morning. = 他每天早上都用一杯咖啡来提神。

一般过去时:
The coach's speech reinvigorated the team before the game. = 教练的讲话在比赛前激励了球队。
A week at the beach reinvigorated her. = 在海滩上待了一周使她恢复了活力。

现在完成时:
The new CEO has reinvigorated the company's image. = 新任CEO已经重塑了公司的形象，使其焕发了活力。
The project has reinvigorated my interest in history. = 这个项目重新燃起了我对历史的兴趣。

过去完成时:
They had reinvigorated the organization before the merger. = 他们在合并之前已经使组织恢复了活力。
She had reinvigorated her career by taking up a new challenge. = 她通过接受新的挑战使自己的事业焕发了生机。


将来时:
The new marketing campaign will reinvigorate the brand. = 新的营销活动将使品牌重获活力。
This new training program will reinvigorate our workforce. = 这项新的培训计划将使我们的员工重获活力。

虚拟语气:
If we reinvigorated our marketing strategy, we would see better results. = 如果我们改进营销策略，就能取得更好的成果。
I wish the government would reinvigorate the economy. = 我希望政府能够振兴经济。

被动语态:
The team was reinvigorated by the unexpected victory. = 球队因意外的胜利而士气大振。
The economy needs to be reinvigorated through investment. = 经济需要通过投资来复苏。

疑问句:
Will this new policy reinvigorate the tourism industry? = 这项新政策会使旅游业恢复活力吗？
What can we do to reinvigorate our local community? = 我们能做些什么来振兴我们的当地社区？

否定句:
The long hours did not reinvigorate her, instead, they left her exhausted. = 漫长的工作时间并没有使她恢复活力，反而让她筋疲力尽。
His attempts to reinvigorate the business failed. = 他试图重振企业，但失败了。
