laid-by:/leɪdˈbaɪ/| adj. n.|储备的；储藏的；预备的；|Something that is laid by is saved or stored for future use.  It refers to something put aside, often for a specific purpose, like food or money.|例句：We have a substantial amount of food laid by for the winter. 我们为冬天储备了大量的食物。


例句：She's been diligently laying by money for a down payment on a house. 她一直在努力攒钱付房子的首付。


近义词：reserved, stored, saved, stocked


反义词：spent, consumed, wasted


词性：形容词，名词
