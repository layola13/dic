dabb:/dæb/| v.|轻触；轻蘸；少量尝试|To touch or dip lightly; to try something in a small way. |例句：She dabbed a little paint onto the canvas. 她轻轻地把一点颜料涂在画布上。|例句：He dabbled in politics for a while. 他涉足政界一段时间。|近义词：touch, dip, dabble|反义词：soak, immerse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dabs paint onto the canvas. = 他把颜料轻轻涂在画布上。
She dabs her eyes with a tissue. = 她用纸巾轻轻擦拭眼睛。

一般过去时:
He dabbed the wound with antiseptic. = 他用消毒剂轻轻擦拭伤口。
She dabbed at her tears. = 她轻轻擦拭眼泪。

现在完成时:
She has dabbed a little perfume behind her ears. = 她已经在她耳朵后面轻轻地涂了一点香水。
He has dabbed at the spill with a cloth. = 他已经用布轻轻擦拭了溢出的液体。

过去完成时:
She had dabbed on some lipstick before leaving the house. = 她出门前已经涂了一点口红。
He had dabbed at the stain, but it remained. = 他已经试着擦拭污渍了，但污渍依然存在。

将来时:
He will dab the paint carefully onto the delicate surface. = 他会小心翼翼地将颜料涂抹在精致的表面上。
She will dab some cream on her face before going to bed. = 她睡前会在脸上涂些面霜。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would dab a little more color onto the painting. = 如果我是你，我会在画上再涂一点颜色。
I wish I had dabbed more sunscreen on my nose. = 我真希望我多在鼻子上涂些防晒霜。

被动语态:
The paint was dabbed onto the canvas with a delicate touch. = 颜料是用轻柔的触感涂抹在画布上的。
The wound was dabbed with antiseptic. = 伤口用消毒剂擦拭过。

疑问句:
Did you dab the sauce on the pasta? = 你把酱汁涂在意大利面上了吗？
Will she dab some perfume on her wrist? = 她会在手腕上涂些香水吗？

否定句:
He didn't dab the paint evenly. = 他没有均匀地涂抹颜料。
She didn't dab at the spill quickly enough. = 她没有及时擦拭溢出的液体。

PHRASE->

dab off: 轻擦掉
dab hand: 熟练的人
