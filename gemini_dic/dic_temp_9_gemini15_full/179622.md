reencounters:/ˌriːˈkaʊntərz/ | n. |再次相遇，重逢 |The act of meeting again after a period of time; renewed contact or interaction.  |例句：Their reencounter after twenty years was filled with emotion.  他们二十年后的再次相遇充满了情感。|例句：The reencounter of old friends sparked joyful reminiscences. 老朋友们的重逢引发了快乐的回忆。|近义词：reunions, meetings, encounters |反义词：partings, separations


USAGE->

He reencounters his former colleague at the conference. = 他在会议上再次遇到他以前的同事。


一般过去时:
They reencountered after many years apart. = 他们多年分离后再次相遇。
She reencountered her childhood sweetheart at the market. = 她在市场上再次遇到了她的童年恋人。


现在完成时:
I have reencountered him several times since our last meeting. = 自从上次见面以来，我已经几次遇到他了。
They have reencountered many challenges in their business. = 他们在生意上遇到过许多挑战。(This example uses a slightly different meaning, focusing on facing challenges again)


过去完成时:
Before the final scene, they had reencountered many obstacles. = 在最后一幕之前，他们已经遇到了许多障碍。(Similar to the above, focusing on challenges)

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
