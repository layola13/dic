overstaffed:/ˌoʊvərˈstæft/ | adj. |人员过多的，人手过剩的 |Having more staff than is needed.  The company is overstaffed and needs to reduce its workforce. 公司人手过剩，需要裁员。|近义词：overmanned, overcrowded |反义词：understaffed, short-staffed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The department is overstaffed. = 这个部门人手过剩。
The company is currently overstaffed in the marketing department. = 公司市场部目前人员过剩。


一般过去时:
The factory was overstaffed last year. = 去年工厂人手过剩。
The project was overstaffed, leading to inefficiencies. = 项目人员过剩，导致效率低下。


现在完成时:
The company has been overstaffed for several months. = 公司已经人手过剩好几个月了。
The team has become overstaffed since the merger. = 自从合并以来，团队人员变得过剩。


过去完成时:
They had been overstaffed before the layoffs. = 在裁员之前，他们一直人手过剩。
The department had been overstaffed for years before the restructuring. = 在重组之前，该部门多年来一直人员过剩。


将来时:
The department will be overstaffed if we hire more people. = 如果我们再雇佣更多人，部门就会人手过剩。
The company will likely be overstaffed after the acquisition. = 收购后，公司很可能会人员过剩。


虚拟语气:
If the company were not overstaffed, it would be more profitable. = 如果公司不人手过剩，就会更有利可图。
I wish the team were not overstaffed; it's affecting productivity. = 我希望团队不要人手过剩；这正在影响生产力。


被动语态:
The issue of overstaffing was discussed at the meeting. = 会议上讨论了人员过剩的问题。
The problem of being overstaffed is being addressed by management. = 管理层正在解决人员过剩的问题。


疑问句:
Is the company overstaffed? = 公司人手过剩吗？
Are we overstaffed in this department? = 我们这个部门人手过剩吗？


否定句:
The company is not overstaffed; it's actually understaffed. = 公司并不人手过剩；实际上人手不足。
The team is not overstaffed; we have the right number of people. = 团队人员并不过剩；我们的人数正好。
