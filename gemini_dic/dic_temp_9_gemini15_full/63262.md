entertains:/ˌɛntərˈteɪnz/ | vt. |娱乐；使快乐；招待；考虑 | To provide amusement or enjoyment for; to hold the attention of; to consider or contemplate. |例句：The clown entertained the children at the party. 小丑在派对上逗孩子们开心。|例句：She entertained the idea of moving to the countryside. 她考虑搬到乡下。|近义词：amuse, delight,  divert,  host |反义词：bore, depress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He entertains his guests with stories. = 他用故事招待他的客人。
The comedian entertains the audience with jokes. = 喜剧演员用笑话逗乐观众。

一般过去时:
We entertained friends last night. = 我们昨晚招待了朋友。
The magician entertained the children with tricks. = 魔术师用戏法逗孩子们开心。

现在完成时:
She has entertained many guests in her home. = 她在家招待过许多客人。
The band has entertained crowds all over the world. = 乐队在世界各地为人群表演过。

过去完成时:
They had entertained the thought of quitting their jobs. = 他们曾考虑过辞职。
He had entertained many different ideas before making his decision. = 他在做决定之前考虑过许多不同的想法。

将来时:
We will entertain our relatives this weekend. = 我们这个周末将招待我们的亲戚。
The singer will entertain the audience with her beautiful voice. = 歌手将用她优美的歌声来娱乐观众。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would entertain the offer. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会考虑这个提议。
I wish I could entertain the possibility of traveling abroad. = 我希望我能考虑出国的可能性。

被动语态:
The audience was entertained by the performance. = 观众被演出所吸引。
The children were entertained by the storyteller. = 孩子们被讲故事的人逗乐了。

疑问句:
Does she often entertain guests? = 她经常招待客人吗？
Will the show entertain the children? = 这场演出会让孩子们开心吗？

否定句:
The movie didn't entertain me at all. = 这部电影一点也没让我开心。
He refused to entertain the suggestion. = 他拒绝考虑这个建议。
