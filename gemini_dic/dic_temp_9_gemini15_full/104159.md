junglist:/ˈdʒʌŋɡlɪst/| n. |丛林舞曲爱好者，丛林音乐制作人|A junglist is a fan or follower of jungle music, a fast-paced and complex subgenre of electronic dance music characterized by breakbeats and often incorporating elements of reggae, hip hop, and other genres.  They are often associated with the rave culture.|例句：He's a dedicated junglist, attending every jungle rave he can.|近义词：jungle music fan, rave goer|反义词：classical music enthusiast
