by far:/baɪ fɑːr/| adv. |到目前为止；…得多；非常|By far, this is the best solution.|到目前为止，这是最好的解决方案。|近义词：definitely, certainly, undoubtedly |反义词：by no means, not at all

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is by far the tallest student in the class. = 他是班里个子最高的。
This is by far the most delicious cake I've ever tasted. = 这绝对是我吃过最好吃的蛋糕。


一般过去时:
That was by far the most exciting game I've ever watched. = 那是我看过的最激动人心的比赛。
She was by far the most popular girl in school. = 她绝对是学校里最受欢迎的女孩。


现在完成时:
This is by far the best movie I have seen this year. = 这是我今年看过的最好的电影。
He has by far the greatest influence in this company. = 到目前为止，他在公司里最有影响力。


过去完成时:
That was by far the worst decision they had ever made. = 那绝对是他们做过的最糟糕的决定。
By far the most challenging project they had completed was the bridge construction. = 到目前为止，他们完成的最具挑战性的项目是桥梁建设。


将来时:

By far the most difficult part will be the fundraising. = 到目前为止，最困难的部分将是筹款。


虚拟语气:
That would by far be the best option, if we had more resources. = 如果我们有更多资源，那将是最好的选择。
It would by far be the easiest way, if we just followed the instructions. = 如果我们只是按照说明去做，那将是最简单的方法。


被动语态:

This design was by far the most praised by the client. = 这种设计是客户最称赞的。


疑问句:

否定句:

It was by no means the easiest task. = 这绝对不是最简单的任务。
