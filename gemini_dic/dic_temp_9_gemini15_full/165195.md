preinhere: /ˌpriːɪnˈhɪr/ | v. | 内在存在；内含；固有 | To exist within; to be inherent; to be contained within something else.  |The problem preinhabits the very structure of the argument.  这个问题内在于论证的结构本身。|近义词：inhere, reside, be inherent in |反义词：be external to, be extrinsic to |动词
