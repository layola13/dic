contemporaneously:/kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsli/| adv.|同时地，当代地| happening at the same time; existing or occurring in the same period of time.  |例句：The two events happened contemporaneously. 这两件事同时发生。|近义词：simultaneously, concurrently, at the same time|反义词：sequentially, successively

词源:
The word "contemporaneously" is derived from the Latin words "con" (together), "tempus" (time), and the suffix "-aneously" (in a manner of).  It essentially means "at the same time."
