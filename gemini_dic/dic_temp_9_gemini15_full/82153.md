groomsman:/ˈɡruːmzˌmæn/ | n. |伴郎| A male friend or relative of the groom who attends him at his wedding.  A groomsman typically helps the groom with preparations before the ceremony and participates in wedding events. |Example:  He asked his best friend to be his groomsman.|近义词：best man, usher|反义词：bridemaid

He is a groomsman at his brother's wedding. = 他是他哥哥婚礼上的伴郎。
The groomsman helps the groom get ready. = 伴郎帮助新郎准备。
