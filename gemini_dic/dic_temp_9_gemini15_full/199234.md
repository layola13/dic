silllike:/ˈsɪlˌlaɪk/| adj.|窗台似的；笨重的；呆板的|Resembling a sill;  dull, heavy, or stodgy.  The term often implies a lack of grace or elegance, suggesting something broad, flat, and possibly immovable.|例句：The large, heavy furniture gave the room a silllike, uninspired feel.  这笨重的家具让房间显得呆板，缺乏生气。  The silllike quality of his prose made it difficult to read. 他散文的呆板风格使它难以阅读。|近义词：stodgy, heavy, clumsy, ungainly, uninspired|反义词：graceful, elegant, light, airy
