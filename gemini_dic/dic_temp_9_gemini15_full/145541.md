overcommercializes:/ˌoʊvərˌkəˈmɜːrʃəlˌaɪzɪz/ | vt. |过度商业化|To make something excessively commercial; to exploit something for profit to an excessive degree.  This verb implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the focus on profit has overshadowed other important aspects.|例句：The festival has been criticized for overcommercializing its traditional arts and crafts.  节日因过度商业化其传统工艺美术而受到批评。The government is accused of overcommercializing the national parks. 政府被指控过度商业化国家公园。|近义词：commercialize excessively, exploit commercially, monetize excessively |反义词：decommercialize, preserve, protect
