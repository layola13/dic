handsomer:/ˈhændsəmər/ | adj. |更英俊的，更漂亮的 | More handsome; possessing greater beauty or attractiveness.  |He's handsomer than his brother. 他比他哥哥更英俊。|  The painting is handsomer than the photograph. 这幅画比照片更漂亮。|  handsome, attractive, beautiful | ugly, unattractive, plain

She is handsomer in that dress. = 她穿那件衣服更漂亮。
