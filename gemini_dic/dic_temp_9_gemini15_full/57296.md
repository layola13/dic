downsized:/ˈdaʊnˌsaɪzd/| vt.|裁员；缩小规模；简化|To reduce the size, amount, or extent of something, often by reducing staff.|例句：The company downsized its workforce by 10%. 公司裁员10%。|例句：They downsized their operations to cut costs. 他们缩减业务规模以降低成本。|近义词：reduce, shrink, cut back|反义词：expand, enlarge, increase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company downsizes its workforce every year. = 公司每年都会裁员。
He downsizes his possessions regularly. = 他定期精简自己的财产。


一般过去时:
The factory downsized last year due to low demand. = 由于需求低迷，这家工厂去年进行了裁员。
They downsized their marketing campaign after poor results. = 营销活动效果不佳后，他们缩减了营销规模。


现在完成时:
The company has downsized three times in the last decade. = 这家公司在过去十年中已经裁员三次了。
She has downsized her life considerably since retiring. = 她退休后，生活规模大大缩小了。


过去完成时:
The company had downsized before the merger was announced. = 在合并宣布之前，公司已经进行了裁员。
They had downsized their expectations after the initial setbacks. = 在最初的挫折之后，他们已经降低了预期。


将来时:
The company will downsize next quarter if sales don't improve. = 如果销售额没有改善，公司将在下个季度裁员。
We will downsize our apartment once the children move out. = 孩子们搬出去后，我们将搬到小一点的公寓。


虚拟语气:
If the company hadn't downsized, it might still be in business. = 如果公司没有裁员，它可能仍然还在运营。
I wish we hadn't downsized our house so quickly. = 我真希望我们没有那么快就搬到小房子。


被动语态:
The workforce was downsized due to economic downturn. = 由于经济衰退，员工队伍被裁减了。
The project was downsized to meet the budget constraints. = 项目规模被缩小以满足预算限制。


疑问句:
Will the company downsize again this year? = 公司今年还会裁员吗？
Has the government downsized its bureaucracy recently? = 政府最近是否精简了官僚机构？


否定句:
The company didn't downsize its operations despite the losses. = 尽管亏损，公司并没有缩减业务规模。
They did not downsize their ambitions even after the failure. = 即使失败了，他们也没有降低自己的雄心壮志。


PHRASE->

downsize one's life = 简化生活
downsize a project = 缩小项目规模
downsize a budget = 削减预算
