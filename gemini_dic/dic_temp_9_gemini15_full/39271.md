congressionally:/kənˈɡreʃənəli/ | adv. | 国会地；经国会批准地 |  By action of Congress; with the approval of Congress.  It describes something done or approved by the United States Congress. |例句：The bill was congressionally approved last week.  上周该法案获得了国会的批准。|例句：The budget was congressionally mandated. 预算由国会强制规定。|近义词：legislatively, officially |反义词：unofficially, privately
