nonpsychologically:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkli/| adv. |非心理地，非心理学地 |Not in a way that relates to or is affected by psychology or the mental processes.|The experiment was conducted nonpsychologically, focusing solely on observable behaviors.  实验是非心理地进行的，只关注可观察到的行为。|近义词：nonmentally, objectively |反义词：psychologically


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses or voice.  Its usage depends on the sentence structure it modifies.

Example Sentences:

The study was conducted nonpsychologically, focusing on physiological responses.  这项研究是非心理地进行的，关注的是生理反应。

He reacted nonpsychologically to the upsetting news, showing no visible emotional response. 他对令人不安的消息反应非心理化，没有表现出任何可见的情绪反应。
