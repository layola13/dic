millets:/ˈmɪləts/| n.|小米；各种小米的统称|Several types of small-seeded grasses cultivated for their grain.  Millets are important crops in many parts of the world, particularly in arid and semi-arid regions.| 例句：Pearl millet is a drought-resistant crop, making it suitable for cultivation in dry areas.  珍珠小米是一种抗旱作物，使其适合在干旱地区种植。

Millets are a staple food in many African countries. 小米是许多非洲国家的粮食主食。|近义词：grains, cereals|反义词：无
