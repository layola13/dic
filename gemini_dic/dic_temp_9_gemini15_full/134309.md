noninsistence:/ˌnɒnɪnˈsɪstəns/| n. |不坚持；不勉强；默许|The absence of insistence; a willingness to accept a situation or decision without protest or pressure.  It implies a passive acceptance rather than active agreement. |例句：Her noninsistence on the issue allowed the meeting to progress smoothly.  她的不坚持使得会议顺利进行。

例句：His noninsistence on immediate payment was a sign of his trust in me. 他不坚持立即付款表明了他对我的信任。


近义词：acquiescence, acceptance, tolerance, forbearance


反义词：insistence, insistence, persistence, pertinacity


词性: 名词
