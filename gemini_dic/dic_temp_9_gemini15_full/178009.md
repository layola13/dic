recivilization:/ˌriːˌsɪvɪləˈzeɪʃən/| n. |再文明化；重新文明化；复兴文明|The process of restoring or reviving civilization after a period of decline or destruction.  It often involves rebuilding infrastructure, restoring social order, and promoting cultural and intellectual growth.  The concept often implies a return to or a refinement of previous cultural values and norms, but it can also involve the creation of new societal structures and ways of life.|例句：The post-war recivilization of Europe was a long and arduous process.  战后欧洲的重建文明是一个漫长而艰巨的过程。

The recivilization of the devastated city took decades.  被摧毁的城市的重新文明化花了数十年时间。|近义词：reconstruction, regeneration, revitalization|反义词：decivilization, barbarization, decline|名词
