greetings:/ˈɡriːtɪŋz/ | n. |问候；祝贺；致意 |  Expressions of goodwill or friendliness, often upon meeting or parting. Greetings can range from simple verbal exchanges to more elaborate ceremonies.  |Example:  He offered his greetings to the assembled guests. 他向聚集的宾客致意。|  Example:  The card contained warm greetings for the new year. 卡片里包含了新年温暖的问候。 |近义词：salutations, welcomes, regards |反义词：farewells, goodbyes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team exchanges greetings every morning. = 团队每天早上互相问候。
She sends greetings to her family regularly. = 她定期向家人问候。


一般过去时:
They exchanged greetings before the meeting. = 他们在会议前互相问候。
He received greetings from his friends. = 他收到了朋友们的问候。


现在完成时:
I have sent greetings to my relatives. = 我已经向我的亲戚们问候了。
They have received many greetings on their wedding anniversary. = 他们结婚纪念日收到了很多祝福。


过去完成时:
Before the performance, they had exchanged greetings with the audience. = 演出前，他们已向观众致意。
She had sent greetings to her boss before leaving for vacation. = 她休假前已向老板问候。


将来时:
We will send greetings to our clients next week. = 我们下周将向客户问候。
He will offer greetings to the new employee. = 他将向新员工表示问候。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would offer my greetings. = 如果我见到他，我会向他问候。
I wish I could have sent greetings to her sooner. = 我希望我能早点向她问候。


被动语态:
The greetings were warmly received. = 问候受到了热烈的欢迎。
His greetings were ignored. = 他的问候被忽视了。


疑问句:
Have you sent your greetings? = 你问候了吗？
Did they receive your greetings? = 他们收到你的问候了吗？


否定句:
He did not offer greetings. = 他没有问候。
She didn't receive any greetings. = 她没有收到任何问候。
