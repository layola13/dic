dogie:/ˈdoʊɡi/| n.|小牛犊，瘦弱的小牛|A young, usually small and thin calf.  The term often implies a weak or undernourished animal.|例句：The rancher carefully tended to the sickly dogie.  牧场主仔细照料那头瘦弱的小牛犊。|例句：Several dogies were separated from the herd.  几头小牛犊与牛群走散了。|近义词：calf, yearling|反义词：bull (adult male cow), cow (adult female cow)
