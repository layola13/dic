missis:/ˈmɪsɪz/| n. |太太，夫人|A title used before the surname of a married woman.  It is a shortened form of "mistress".| Mrs. Smith is a kind teacher. 史密斯太太是一位善良的老师。|近义词：Mrs., Madam, wife|反义词：Mr., Miss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is Mrs. Jones. = 她是琼斯太太。
Mrs. Brown teaches English. = 布朗太太教英语。


一般过去时:
Mrs. Davis lived next door. = 戴维斯太太住在隔壁。
She was Mrs. Green before she remarried. = 她在再婚前是格林太太。


现在完成时:
Mrs. Lee has lived here for ten years. = 李太太在这里住了十年了。
They have known Mrs. Johnson for many years. = 他们认识约翰逊太太很多年了。


过去完成时:
She had been Mrs. Thompson for twenty years. = 她做过二十年的汤普森太太。
Before the accident, she was already Mrs. Smith. = 事故发生前，她已经是史密斯太太了。


将来时:
Mrs. Garcia will be here tomorrow. = 加西亚太太明天会来。
She will become Mrs. Miller next month. = 下个月她将成为米勒太太。


虚拟语气:
If she were Mrs. Davis, she would know. = 如果她是戴维斯太太，她就会知道。
I wish I were Mrs. Smith. = 我希望我是史密斯太太。


被动语态:
Mrs. Brown was invited to the party. = 布朗太太被邀请参加聚会。
The letter was addressed to Mrs. Jones. = 这封信是寄给琼斯太太的。


疑问句:
Is she Mrs. Smith? = 她是史密斯太太吗？
Who is Mrs. Brown? = 布朗太太是谁？


否定句:
She is not Mrs. Davis. = 她不是戴维斯太太。
That's not Mrs. Lee's car. = 那不是李太太的车。


PHRASE->
Mrs. + surname:  (e.g., Mrs. Smith, Mrs. Jones)  太太 + 姓氏
Call me Mrs. [Surname]: 请叫我[姓氏]太太
My Mrs.: 我的妻子（非正式）
Dear Mrs. [Surname]: 尊敬的[姓氏]太太
Address to Mrs. [Surname]: 寄给[姓氏]太太
