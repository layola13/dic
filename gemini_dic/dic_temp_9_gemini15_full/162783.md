preborn: /ˈpriːbɔːrn/ | adj. | 未出生的；胎儿的 |  Relating to or occurring before birth.  The term is often used in discussions about abortion and fetal rights, and is sometimes considered controversial due to its implications. | 例句：Preborn babies are vulnerable to environmental toxins.  未出生的婴儿容易受到环境毒素的影响。  The preborn child's heartbeat was detected.  检测到了未出生婴儿的心跳。 | 近义词：unborn, prenatal | 反义词：born, postnatal
