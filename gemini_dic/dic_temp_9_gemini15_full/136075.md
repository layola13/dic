nonreciprocally:/ˌnɒnˌrɛsɪˈprɒkəli/| adv. |非相互地，单向地|Not reciprocally; in a one-sided way.  It describes a situation or action where something is given or done without a return or equivalent. |例句：The favor was given nonreciprocally, without expectation of anything in return.  这份恩情是单方面给予的，没有期待任何回报。

The company's policy of nonreciprocally sharing information with its competitors proved to be a disadvantage. 公司单方面与竞争对手共享信息的政策最终被证明是一个劣势。


近义词：unilaterally, one-sidedly, one-way

反义词：reciprocally, mutually, two-sidedly


词源:

* **non-**:  Prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".
* **reciprocally**: Adverb meaning "mutually; in a way that is exchanged or shared between two or more people or things".  It comes from the Latin word "reciprocus," meaning "returning" or "interchanging".

USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs or decline like nouns. Its usage is primarily descriptive, modifying verbs or entire clauses.  There's no tense or voice to consider.


PHRASE->
There aren't common set phrases directly using "nonreciprocally".  Its usage is more integrated within sentences describing actions or relationships.  Examples of phrases where it could be used include:

* act nonreciprocally
* respond nonreciprocally
* interact nonreciprocally
* behave nonreciprocally
* relate nonreciprocally
