ohs:/oʊz/| int.|哦，啊(表示惊讶、痛苦或理解)|An interjection expressing surprise, pain, or understanding.  It's a shortened or informal version of "oh".|例句：Ohs! That hurt! 哎哟！疼死我了！
例句：Ohs, I understand now. 哦，我现在明白了。|近义词：oh, wow, ouch|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't follow typical verb tenses.  It expresses a feeling or reaction rather than describing an action.  Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.


PHRASE->
Oh my god! = 天哪！
Oh dear! = 天哪！
Oh well! = 算了！
Oh, I see! = 哦，我明白了！
Oh no! = 哦不！
