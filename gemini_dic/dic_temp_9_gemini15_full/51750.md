devolving:/dɪˈvɒlviŋ/| v.|转移；衰退；退化|The act of devolving power or responsibility from a central authority to a lower level.  It can also refer to a decline or deterioration in quality, strength, or character.|例句：The company is devolving more responsibility to its regional offices. 这家公司正在将其更多责任下放给地区办事处。

例句：The quality of the product has devolved over time.  产品的质量随着时间的推移而下降。|近义词：transferring, delegating, declining, deteriorating|反义词：centralizing, improving, enhancing|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is devolving more power to local authorities. = 政府正在将更多权力下放给地方当局。
They are devolving their responsibilities to their team members. = 他们正在将其职责下放给团队成员。


一般过去时:
The empire devolved into several smaller kingdoms. = 帝国分裂成几个较小的王国。
The company devolved its manufacturing operations to a subcontractor. = 公司将其制造业务委托给了分包商。


现在完成时:
Power has devolved significantly since the revolution. = 自从革命以来，权力已经发生了显著的转移。
The situation has devolved into chaos. = 局势已经演变成混乱。


过去完成时:
The company had devolved most of its production before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经将其大部分生产业务下放了。
The political system had devolved into a state of anarchy. = 政治体制已经演变成无政府状态。


将来时:

They will devolve the project to a junior team. = 他们将把项目下放给一个初级团队。


虚拟语气:

More resources are being devolved to the research department. = 更多的资源正在下放给研究部门。
