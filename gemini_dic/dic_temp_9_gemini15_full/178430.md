reconsideration: /ˌriːkənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |重新考虑，再考虑 | The act of thinking again about a decision or opinion.  It involves reviewing previous thoughts and possibly changing one's mind. |例句：After careful reconsideration, she decided to accept the job offer.  经过仔细的重新考虑，她决定接受这份工作。

例句：The committee is undergoing a reconsideration of its earlier decision. 委员会正在重新考虑其先前的决定。


近义词：re-evaluation, review, rethinking, second thought | 反义词：confirmation, affirmation, persistence


词性：名词
