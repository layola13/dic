outbragged:/ˌaʊtˈbræɡd/| v.|吹嘘过甚，夸大其词|To surpass someone in bragging; to boast more than someone else.|He outbragged everyone at the party with his tales of adventure. 他在派对上用他的冒险故事吹嘘得比任何人都厉害。|outboast, overshadow|understate, downplay|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outbrags everyone in the office. 他在办公室里比任何人都爱吹牛。
They outbrag each other constantly. 他们不断地互相吹嘘。

一般过去时:
She outbragged him about her accomplishments. 她夸耀自己的成就，比他还厉害。
He outbragged his friends about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车，比他的朋友们还厉害。

现在完成时:
He has outbragged everyone he's ever met. 他遇到过的每个人都被他吹嘘过。
She has outbragged her colleagues on numerous occasions. 她多次在同事面前夸大其词。

过去完成时:
He had outbragged everyone by the time the party ended. 派对结束时，他已经把每个人都吹嘘过了。
She had outbragged her sister about her trip before anyone else could speak. 在其他人说话之前，她就已经夸耀她的旅行比她姐姐的更精彩了。

将来时:
He will outbrag his opponent in the debate. 他将在辩论中比他的对手更能说会道。
She will outbrag everyone at the conference with her achievements. 她将在会议上用她的成就吹嘘得比任何人都厉害。

虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he would have outbragged his classmates. 如果他学习更努力，他就能比他的同学们更能吹嘘了。
If she had more experience, she would outbrag him easily. 如果她更有经验，她就能轻松地比他更能吹嘘了。

被动语态:
His achievements were outbragged by his rivals. 他的成就被他的竞争对手夸大其词了。
Their claims were outbragged by the opposition. 他们的说法被反对者夸大了。


疑问句:
Did he outbrag his brother about his new job? 他是否因为他的新工作而吹嘘得比他哥哥厉害？
Has she ever outbragged anyone before? 她以前有没有吹嘘得比任何人都厉害？


否定句:
He didn't outbrag anyone at the meeting. 他在会议上没有吹嘘得比任何人厉害。
She didn't outbrag her friends about her vacation. 她没有夸耀她的假期比她朋友的好。
