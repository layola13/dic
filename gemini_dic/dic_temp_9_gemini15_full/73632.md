forfeiture: /ˈfɔːrfɪtʃər/ | n. | 没收；丧失；罚没 | The loss or giving up of something as a penalty for wrongdoing or failure to fulfill an obligation.  It often involves property or rights. | Example: The forfeiture of his land was a harsh punishment.  他的土地被没收是种严厉的惩罚。  The company faced forfeiture of its license. 这家公司面临吊销执照的风险。 | 近义词：confiscation, relinquishment, surrender | 反义词：acquisition, retention, preservation
