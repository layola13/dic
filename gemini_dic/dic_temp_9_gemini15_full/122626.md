modif:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪ/ /ˈmɑːdəfaɪ/| v.|修改，更改，修饰|To change something slightly, especially in order to improve it or make it more suitable for a particular purpose.  |例句：We need to modify our plans slightly.我们需要稍微修改一下我们的计划。
例句：The architect modified the design to incorporate the client's suggestions. 建筑师修改了设计以包含客户的建议。|近义词：alter, adjust, amend, change|反义词：maintain, preserve, keep


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They modify their behavior depending on the situation. = 他们根据情况修改自己的行为。
I modify my recipes frequently. = 我经常修改我的食谱。


一般过去时:
She modified the dress to fit better. = 她修改了这件衣服使其更合身。
He modified his statement after reviewing the evidence. = 他在审查证据后修改了他的陈述。


现在完成时:
They have modified the software several times. = 他们已经修改了该软件几次。
I have modified my approach to the problem. = 我已经修改了我解决这个问题的方法。


过去完成时:
She had modified the document before submitting it. = 她提交文件之前已经修改了它。
They had modified their strategy after the initial setback. = 他们经过最初的挫折后已经修改了他们的策略。


将来时:
He will modify the design according to the feedback. = 他将根据反馈修改设计。
We will modify the schedule if necessary. = 如果必要，我们将修改日程安排。


虚拟语气:
If I modified the code, it might work. = 如果我修改了代码，它可能会运行。
I wish I had modified the plan earlier. = 我希望我早点修改了计划。


被动语态:
The document was modified by the editor. = 文件由编辑修改。
The system will be modified next week. = 系统将在下周修改。


疑问句:
Will you modify the report? = 你会修改报告吗？
Have they modified the software yet? = 他们已经修改软件了吗？


否定句:
He did not modify the contract. = 他没有修改合同。
They will not modify their position. = 他们不会改变立场。
