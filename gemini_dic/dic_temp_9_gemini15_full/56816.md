dorking:/ˈdɔːkɪŋ/| n.|多金鸡|A breed of chicken known for its distinctive appearance and docile nature.  Dorkings are large, heavy birds with five toes on each foot (instead of the usual four). They are valued for their meat and are popular among poultry enthusiasts.|例句：He raises a flock of prize-winning Dorking chickens. 他饲养着一群获奖的多金鸡。|例句：The Dorking chicken is known for its plump meat and docile temperament. 多金鸡以其丰满的肉质和温顺的性情而闻名。|近义词：chicken (general term)|反义词：无
