don't:/doʊnt/| v. |不，没有|Contraction of "do not".  It is used to form the negative of verbs in the simple present tense (for I, you, we, they) and in the simple past tense (for I, you, we, they).|


例句：I don't like broccoli. = 我不喜欢西兰花。

例句：They don't understand the instructions. = 他们不明白这些指示。


近义词：do not, haven't, hasn't, didn't, won't


反义词：do, did


词性：助动词
