hither-and-thither:/ˈhɪðər ænd ˈðɪðər/| adv.|到处，往返不定地|Moving or going from one place to another in a haphazard way; back and forth.|The children ran hither and thither in the playground.|近义词：here and there, to and fro, back and forth|反义词：nowhere, still, stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ball rolls hither and thither across the floor. = 球在房间的地板上到处滚动。
Birds fly hither and thither in the sky. = 鸟儿在空中四处飞翔。


一般过去时:
The dog ran hither and thither, searching for its bone. = 狗到处跑，寻找它的骨头。
The lost child wandered hither and thither, crying for his mother. = 迷路的孩子到处乱跑，哭着喊妈妈。


现在完成时:
The wind has blown the leaves hither and thither all morning. =  整个上午风把树叶吹得四处飞舞。
The cat has darted hither and thither, chasing a mouse. = 猫一直在到处窜，追赶一只老鼠。


过去完成时:
The guests had wandered hither and thither before the party started. = 派对开始前，客人们已经在四处走动。
The soldiers had moved hither and thither throughout the night. = 士兵们整夜都在四处移动。


将来时:
The boats will sail hither and thither across the lake. = 船只将在湖上四处航行。
The performers will move hither and thither across the stage. = 表演者将在舞台上四处走动。


虚拟语气:
If the wind blew hither and thither, the boat would be tossed about. = 如果风四处吹，船就会被抛来抛去。
If the children ran hither and thither, they might get lost. = 如果孩子们到处乱跑，他们可能会迷路。


被动语态:
The papers were scattered hither and thither by the wind. = 文件被风吹得四处散落。
The toys were thrown hither and thither by the playful children. = 玩具被顽皮的孩子们扔得四处都是。


疑问句:
Did the children run hither and thither in the park? = 孩子们在公园里到处乱跑了吗？
Will the dancers move hither and thither during the performance? = 舞蹈演员在表演过程中会到处走动吗？


否定句:
The bird did not fly hither and thither; it stayed in one place. = 那只鸟没有到处飞，它一直待在一个地方。
The soldiers did not move hither and thither; they remained in formation. = 士兵们没有到处移动；他们保持着队形。
