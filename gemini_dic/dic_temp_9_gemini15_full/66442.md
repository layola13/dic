USAGE->
This word is an adjective,  so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  It modifies nouns.  Examples illustrate its use in various sentence structures.

Positive Sentence:
His behaviour was exemplarly.  = 他的行为堪称典范。
The project was completed exemplarly. = 项目完成得非常出色。


Negative Sentence:
The work was not exemplarly. = 工作做得不够出色。
His performance was not exemplarly. = 他的表现不尽如人意。


Question Sentence:
Was her response exemplarly? = 她的回应堪称典范吗？
Is this approach exemplarly? = 这种方法堪称典范吗？


Passive Voice:
His actions were deemed exemplarly. = 他的行为被认为是典范。
The solution was considered exemplarly. = 该方案被认为是典范的。



PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "exemplarly".  The word is used to directly describe a noun.
