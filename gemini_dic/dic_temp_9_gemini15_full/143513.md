outgoinger:/ˌaʊtˈɡoʊɪŋər/ | n. | 外向的人 | An outgoing person is friendly and enjoys meeting and talking to new people.  This word is less common than "extrovert". | She's a very outgoing person and makes friends easily. 她是一个非常外向的人，很容易交朋友。 He is an outgoing person; he likes to attend parties. 他是一个外向的人；他喜欢参加聚会。| extrovert, sociable | introvert, shy, reserved | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an outgoing person. = 他是一个外向的人。
They are outgoing and friendly. = 他们外向且友好。

一般过去时:
She was a very outgoing child. = 她小时候非常外向。
He wasn't as outgoing as his brother. = 他不如他哥哥外向。

现在完成时:
He has become more outgoing since he joined the club. = 自从加入俱乐部以来，他变得更外向了。
She has always been outgoing and confident. = 她一直都很外向自信。

过去完成时:
I had been outgoing, but the recent setbacks made me withdrawn. = 我以前很外向，但最近的挫折让我变得内向。
Before the accident, she had always been outgoing and cheerful. = 事故发生前，她一直都外向开朗。

将来时:
I think she will be more outgoing in the future. = 我认为她将来会更外向。
He will be outgoing once he gets over his shyness. = 他克服了害羞后就会变得外向了。

虚拟语气:
If I were more outgoing, I would have more friends. = 如果我更外向一些，我会拥有更多朋友。
I wish I were as outgoing as my sister. = 我希望我能像我姐姐一样外向。

被动语态:
(This word is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes a personality trait.)

否定句:
