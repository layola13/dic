reanalyzes:/ˌriːˈænəlaɪzɪz/| v.|重新分析，再分析 |To analyze something again, often with a different approach or new information.  |例句：The researchers reanalyzed the data to account for the new variable. 研究人员重新分析了数据以考虑新的变量。

例句：After reviewing the initial findings, the team decided to reanalyze the data using a different statistical method. 在审查了初步调查结果后，团队决定使用不同的统计方法重新分析数据。


近义词：re-examine, review, reassess, reconsider


反义词：analyze (initially), accept (without further analysis)


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reanalyzes the data every week. 他每周都重新分析数据。
She reanalyzes the market trends regularly. 她定期重新分析市场趋势。

一般过去时:
They reanalyzed the evidence and found new clues. 他们重新分析了证据，发现了新的线索。
He reanalyzed his investment strategy after the market crash. 市场崩盘后，他重新分析了他的投资策略。

现在完成时:
She has reanalyzed the patient's medical history multiple times. 她已经多次重新分析了病人的病史。
They have reanalyzed the data and published new findings. 他们已经重新分析了数据并发表了新的研究结果。

过去完成时:
We had reanalyzed the project before presenting the final report. 我们在提交最终报告之前已经重新分析了项目。
The scientists had reanalyzed the samples when a new technique became available. 当一种新技术出现时，科学家们已经重新分析了样本。

将来时:
They will reanalyze the results once the new software is installed. 安装新软件后，他们将重新分析结果。
She will reanalyze the problem from a different perspective. 她将从不同的角度重新分析这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would reanalyze the data more thoroughly. 如果我们有更多时间，我们会更彻底地重新分析数据。
I wish I had reanalyzed the situation before making the decision. 我希望在做决定之前我已经重新分析了情况。

否定句:
They did not reanalyze the data due to time constraints. 由于时间限制，他们没有重新分析数据。
