mourningly:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋli/ | adv. | 悲哀地；哀伤地 | In a mournful manner; sorrowfully.  It describes an action or state performed or existing in a way that shows grief or sadness. |例句：She looked mourningly at the wilting flowers. 她悲伤地望着枯萎的花朵。

例句：He spoke mourningly of his lost friend. 他悲哀地谈论着他失去的朋友。


近义词：sadly, sorrowfully, dolefully, grievously, dejectedly


反义词：joyfully, happily, cheerfully, merrily


词性：副词
