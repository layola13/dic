capricorn:/ˈkæprɪkɔːrn/|/ˈkæprɪkɔːrn/| n. |摩羯座；摩羯座的人；山羊|The word "Capricorn" refers to the tenth astrological sign in the zodiac, symbolized by a sea-goat. It is also used to describe a person born under this sign, typically associated with characteristics like ambition, discipline, and practicality.  |My birthday is in December, so I am a Capricorn.|近义词：Capricornus (astronomical term)|反义词：无
