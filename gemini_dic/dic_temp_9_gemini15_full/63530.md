enure:/ɪˈnjʊər/| vt.| 保持；继续存在；使习惯于；任命；授予（职位）| to continue in existence; to accustom or habituate; to invest with an office or right| The company's policy of enuring its employees to long hours has led to high burnout rates. 公司长期让员工加班的政策导致了高比例的倦怠。  The treaty ensured peace for decades. 这项条约确保了几十年的和平。|近义词：maintain, continue, habituate, invest|反义词：terminate, end, discontinue


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company enures its employees to long working hours. = 公司让员工习惯长时间工作。
He enures his dog to loud noises. = 他让他的狗习惯大声噪音。


一般过去时:
The king enured his son to the throne. = 国王让他的儿子继承王位。
The teacher enured the students to the difficult curriculum. = 老师让学生习惯于困难的课程。


现在完成时:
The policy has enured the employees to a high-pressure work environment. = 这项政策使员工习惯了高压的工作环境。
They have enured themselves to the harsh climate. = 他们已经习惯了恶劣的气候。


过去完成时:
The previous administration had enured the city to corruption. = 上届政府使这个城市习惯了腐败。
She had enured herself to the loneliness of living abroad. = 她已经习惯了独自居住在国外的寂寞。


将来时:
The new management will enure the company to a more efficient system. = 新管理层将使公司习惯于更高效的系统。
They will enure the children to responsibility. = 他们将使孩子们习惯于责任。


虚拟语气:
If the company enured its employees to flexible working hours, productivity might increase. = 如果公司让员工习惯弹性工作时间，生产力可能会提高。
I wish they had enured me to the challenges beforehand. = 我希望他们事先让我习惯了这些挑战。


被动语态:
The employees were enured to long working hours by the company. = 员工们被公司迫使习惯长时间工作。
The new recruits are being enured to the rigors of military life. = 新兵们正在适应军队生活的严酷。


疑问句:
How long will it take to enure the dog to its new home? = 让狗适应新家需要多长时间？
How did they enure themselves to the extreme heat? = 他们是如何适应极度高温的？


否定句:
The company did not enure its employees to the new software. = 公司没有让员工习惯新的软件。
We cannot enure ourselves to such injustice. = 我们不能容忍这种不公正。
