predestroys:/ˌpriːdɪˈstrɔɪz/| vt.|预先破坏；先发制人地摧毁|To destroy something before it can cause harm or damage.  This is typically done proactively to prevent future problems.|例句：The government predestroyed the enemy's ammunition depot to prevent a potential attack. 政府预先摧毁了敌人的弹药库以防止潜在的袭击。

例句：The firefighters predestroyed the surrounding brush to create a firebreak. 消防员预先烧毁了周围的灌木丛以创造防火带。|近义词：preempt, anticipate, prevent|反义词：preserve, protect, conserve|词性:动词
