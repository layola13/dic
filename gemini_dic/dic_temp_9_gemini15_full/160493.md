porch:/pɔːrtʃ/|ˈpɔːrtʃ/| n.|门廊，走廊|A covered shelter projecting in front of the entrance of a building.|例句：We sat on the porch and watched the sunset. 我们坐在门廊上看日落。|例句：The porch was decorated with flowers. 门廊装饰着鲜花。|近义词：veranda, stoa, gallery|反义词：interior
