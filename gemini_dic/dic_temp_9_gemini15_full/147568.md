/ˌoʊvərˈpɛrəmptəri/| adj. | 过于专横的；极其武断的 | excessively peremptory; extremely arbitrary and dictatorial.  The judge's overperemptory manner alienated the jury.  | 近义词：autocratic, dictatorial, imperious, domineering | 反义词：deferential, conciliatory, diplomatic


例句：

His overperemptory dismissal of her concerns was deeply offensive.  他的过于专横地驳回她的担忧非常令人反感。

The overperemptory tone of the letter left the recipient feeling insulted.  信中过于专横的语气让收信人感到受辱。


词源：

* **over-**:  prefix indicating excess or to a great degree. (表示过度或极度)
* **peremptory**: adj. leaving no opportunity for denial or refusal; imperative. (不容拒绝的；专横的；命令式的)  It derives from Latin *peremptorius*, from *peremptor* "one who brings to an end," from *perimere* "to destroy, kill." (源于拉丁语 *peremptorius*，来自 *peremptor* “结束者”，来自 *perimere* “毁灭，杀死”)


用法说明：

`overperemptory` is an adjective used to describe someone's behavior or manner as excessively demanding, domineering, and leaving no room for discussion or dissent. It emphasizes the excessive nature of the peremptoriness.  ( `overperemptory` 是一个形容词，用来形容某人的行为或态度过于强势、专横，不容置疑。它强调了专横的过度性质。)
