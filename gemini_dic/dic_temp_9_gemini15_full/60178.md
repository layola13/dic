effete:/ɪˈfiːt/| adj.|衰弱的；柔弱的；无力的；精致颓废的；缺乏活力的 | Exhausted of energy; barren; decadent.  The word suggests a decline from a former state of strength or vitality, often associated with intellectual or artistic sterility. It can refer to things, people, or societies.  |例句：The once-powerful empire became effete and easily conquered.  曾经强大的帝国变得衰弱无力，很容易被征服。

The effete aristocracy lived a life of luxury and leisure, detached from the concerns of the common people.  腐朽的贵族过着奢侈懒散的生活，与普通民众的担忧脱节。

近义词：decadent, degenerate, weak, feeble, sterile, exhausted

反义词：strong, vigorous, powerful, fertile, productive
