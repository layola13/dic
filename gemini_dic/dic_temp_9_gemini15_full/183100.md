reportedly:/rɪˈpɔːtɪdli/| adv. |据报道；据称|The adverb "reportedly" means according to what has been reported or said. It indicates that the information being presented is based on a report or statement, but it doesn't necessarily mean the information is true or verified.  It suggests a degree of uncertainty or lack of firsthand knowledge.|例句：He reportedly resigned from his position.  据报道，他已辞职。|例句：The company is reportedly facing financial difficulties. 据报道，该公司正面临财务困难。|近义词：allegedly, supposedly, it is said that|反义词：certainly, undeniably, verifiably|词性:副词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is reportedly raining in London. = 据报道伦敦正在下雨。
They reportedly plan to expand their business. = 据报道他们计划扩大业务。

一般过去时:
He reportedly left the country last week. = 据报道他上周离开了这个国家。
The meeting reportedly ended without resolution. = 据报道会议没有达成共识而结束。

现在完成时:
The project has reportedly been completed ahead of schedule. = 据报道该项目已提前完成。
She has reportedly won several awards for her work. = 据报道她因其作品赢得了多个奖项。

过去完成时:
They had reportedly already left before the police arrived. = 据报道警察到达之前他们已经离开了。
The building had reportedly been damaged by the storm. = 据报道建筑物已被暴风雨损坏。

将来时:
They will reportedly launch a new product next month. = 据报道他们下个月将发布一款新产品。
The price is reportedly going to increase soon. = 据报道价格很快就会上涨。

虚拟语气:
If it reportedly snowed tomorrow, we would cancel the trip. = 如果据报道明天会下雪，我们将取消旅行。(This example is less common with "reportedly")
It is reportedly said that if he had known, he would have acted differently. = 据报道说，如果他知道的话，他会采取不同的行动。

被动语态:
It was reportedly said that he was innocent. = 据报道说他是无辜的。
The decision was reportedly made without consulting the employees. = 据报道，这项决定是在没有征求员工意见的情况下做出的。

疑问句:
Is it reportedly true that he is leaving? = 据报道说他将要离开是真的吗？
Was it reportedly a successful event? = 据报道那是一次成功的活动吗？

否定句:
It reportedly isn't true that he is quitting. = 据报道他辞职的消息不属实。
The rumors reportedly aren't accurate. = 据报道谣言并不准确。
