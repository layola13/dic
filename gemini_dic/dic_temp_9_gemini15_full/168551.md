proceeding:/prəˈsiːdɪŋ/ | n. v. | 诉讼程序；会议记录；继续进行；前进 |

英文解析：
As a noun, "proceeding" refers to a series of actions or events that follow a particular course.  It can also denote the official record of a meeting, conference, or legal case. As a verb, "proceeding" means to continue or move forward after a pause or interruption.

例句：
The court proceedings were delayed due to a lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，庭审程序被推迟了。

The minutes of the meeting are included in the proceedings. = 会议记录包含在会议记录中。

After a brief interruption, the concert proceedings continued. = 短暂中断后，音乐会继续进行。

The army proceeded cautiously into enemy territory. = 军队谨慎地进入敌方领地。


近义词：
(n.)  process, transaction, record, minutes, event, action
(v.) continue, advance, progress, go on, move forward


反义词：
(v.) stop, cease, halt, pause, regress


词性：
Noun, Verb
