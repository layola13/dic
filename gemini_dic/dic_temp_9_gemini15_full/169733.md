propugner:/prəˈpʌɡnər/| n. |支持者，拥护者，辩护者 |A propugner is a person who argues in favor of or supports a cause, belief, or idea. They actively defend and promote it.  |Example: He was a staunch propugner of free speech and religious tolerance. 他是言论自由和宗教宽容的坚定支持者。|近义词：advocate, champion, defender, supporter |反义词：opponent, adversary, critic
