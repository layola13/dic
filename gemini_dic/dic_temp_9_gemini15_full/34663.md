clergyman:/ˈklɜːrdʒɪmən/| n. |牧师，神职人员 |A clergyman is a male member of the clergy,  a person ordained for religious duties.  The term is traditionally used for Christian clergy, but can sometimes be used more broadly. |例句：The clergyman gave a moving sermon about forgiveness.  这位牧师做了一场关于宽恕的感人布道。

例句：Many clergymen are involved in community service. 许多牧师都参与社区服务。


近义词：priest, minister, pastor, reverend |反义词：layperson, layman


词性: 名词
