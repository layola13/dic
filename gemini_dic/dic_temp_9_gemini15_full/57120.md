dousing:/ˈdaʊzɪŋ/| vt.|浇水；泼水；使…浸湿|To wet thoroughly, especially by pouring liquid over something or someone.|The firefighters doused the flames with water. 消防队员用水浇灭了火焰。He doused himself with cold water to cool down. 他用冷水冲洗自己以降温。|soak, saturate, inundate|dry, dehydrate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He douses the plants every morning. = 他每天早上都给植物浇水。
She douses her face with cold water. = 她用冷水拍打脸部。

一般过去时:
The gardener doused the flowers with water. = 园丁给花浇了水。
They doused the bonfire with buckets of water. = 他们用桶里的水浇灭了篝火。

现在完成时:
He has doused the fire with a fire extinguisher. = 他用灭火器扑灭了火。
The rain has doused the entire field. = 雨水浸湿了整个田地。

过去完成时:
They had doused the burning building before the fire spread. = 在大火蔓延之前，他们已经用水浇灭了燃烧的建筑物。
She had doused the curtains with water to prevent the fire. = 她用水浇湿窗帘以防止火灾。

将来时:
The farmer will douse the crops with pesticides. = 农民将用杀虫剂喷洒庄稼。
We will douse the campfire before we leave. = 我们离开前会把营火浇灭。

虚拟语气:
If it were raining, it would douse the dust. = 如果下雨的话，它会冲走灰尘。
I wish it would douse the fire quickly. = 我希望它能迅速扑灭大火。

被动语态:
The car was doused in gasoline. = 这辆车被汽油浇湿了。
The field was doused with pesticides. = 田地被杀虫剂喷洒了。

疑问句:
Did they douse the fire with water? = 他们用水浇灭了火吗？
Will the rain douse the dust? = 雨会冲走灰尘吗？

否定句:
He didn't douse the flames quickly enough. = 他没有足够快地浇灭火焰。
They did not douse the fire before leaving. = 他们在离开之前没有浇灭火。
