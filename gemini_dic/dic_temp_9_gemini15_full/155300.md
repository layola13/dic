Phantasmagorists were popular entertainers in the 19th century, using magic lanterns to project moving images that created eerie and often frightening effects. |The phantasmagorist skillfully manipulated the light and shadows, creating a terrifying spectacle for the audience.  幻影制造者巧妙地操纵光影，为观众创造了一个恐怖的景象。|近义词：illusionist, magician, showman |反义词：realist
