goldsmith:/ˈɡoʊldˌsmɪθ/ | n. | 金匠；金银匠 | A goldsmith is a craftsperson who makes and repairs objects from gold, silver, and other precious metals.  They often work with precious stones as well. |  The goldsmith carefully crafted the intricate necklace.  这位金匠细致地制作了这条复杂的项链。 | jeweler, silversmith |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
