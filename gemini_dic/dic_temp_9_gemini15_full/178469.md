reconstructive:/ˌriːkənˈstrʌktɪv/| adj.|重建的；修复的；再造的 | Relating to or involving the reconstruction of something, especially a damaged part of the body.  This adjective describes something that aims to rebuild or restore something to its original state or a functional state.  It is often used in the context of surgery or engineering. |例句：The patient underwent reconstructive surgery after the accident.  事故后，病人接受了重建手术。

例句：The architect drew up reconstructive plans for the damaged building. 建筑师为受损建筑物制定了重建计划。

近义词：restorative, reparative, rehabilitative

反义词：destructive, demolishing, dismantling

词性：形容词
