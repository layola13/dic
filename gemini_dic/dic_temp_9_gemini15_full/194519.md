self-scourging: /ˈsɛlf ˈskaʊrdʒɪŋ/ | n. | 自我鞭挞；自我谴责 |

英文解析:The act of punishing oneself, typically by whipping or other forms of self-inflicted pain, often as an expression of religious penance or extreme self-reproach.  It implies a severe and often excessive criticism or punishment of oneself.

例句:

His self-scourging was a desperate attempt to atone for his sins.  他的自我鞭挞是试图弥补罪孽的绝望尝试。

The artist's self-scourging was evident in his relentless self-criticism.  这位艺术家的自我鞭挞在他无情的自我批评中显而易见。


近义词: self-flagellation, self-chastisement, self-reproach, self-condemnation, mortification

反义词: self-indulgence, self-complacency, self-acceptance


词性: Noun
