eulogization:/ˌjuːlədʒaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |颂扬；赞美；悼词 |The act of praising someone or something highly, especially in a formal speech or writing, often at a funeral.  It involves expressing admiration and respect for the deceased's qualities and achievements. |Example: The eulogization of the fallen soldier highlighted his bravery and sacrifice.  颂扬这位阵亡士兵的悼词突出了他的勇敢和牺牲。|近义词：praise, commendation, tribute, panegyric |反义词：criticism, condemnation, denunciation |名词
