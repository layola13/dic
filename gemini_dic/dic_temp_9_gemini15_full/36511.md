colic:/ˈkɒlɪk/ /ˈkɑːlɪk/| n.| 肠绞痛；剧烈腹痛|Severe abdominal pain, especially in infants, characterized by crying and drawing up of the legs.|The baby was crying inconsolably; I think it might be colic.  婴儿哭个不停；我觉得可能是肠绞痛。|The horse suffered from severe colic after eating spoiled hay. 这匹马吃了腐烂的干草后，患上了严重的肠绞痛。|cramps, spasms| (反义词缺乏明确的反义词，因为这是一种症状而非状态)|名词
