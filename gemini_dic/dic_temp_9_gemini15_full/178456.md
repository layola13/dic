reconstitutions:/ˌriːkɒnstɪˈtʃuːʃənz/ | n. |重建；复原；恢复；(物质的)溶解；复原物 | The act of reconstituting something, or the state of being reconstituted.  This can refer to rebuilding something that has been destroyed or damaged, restoring something to its original state, or dissolving a substance in a liquid.  |例句：The archaeological team worked diligently on the reconstitution of the ancient city. 考古队勤奋地致力于古城的重建工作。

The reconstitution of the dried soup was simple, just add hot water.  干汤的复原很简单，只需加热水即可。


近义词：reconstruction, restoration, rehabilitation, revival | 反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin
