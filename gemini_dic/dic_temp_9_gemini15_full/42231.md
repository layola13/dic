count for:/kaʊnt fɔːr/|/ˈkaʊnt fɔːr/| phrasal verb|重要；有价值；算数；对…有影响|To be important or significant; to matter.  It also refers to having a specific value or influence on a result.|


例句：

Her experience counts for a lot in this job. = 在这份工作中，她的经验很重要。

Your opinion counts for something in this decision. = 你的意见在这个决定中很重要。


近义词：matter,  be important,  be significant

反义词：be irrelevant,  be insignificant,  not matter

词性:phrasal verb
