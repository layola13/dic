convoy:/ˈkɒnvɔɪ/ /ˈkɑːnˌvɔɪ/| n. v.|护送（队）；车队；护航|A convoy is a group of vehicles, ships, or aircraft travelling together, especially for protection.  It can also refer to the act of escorting or protecting a group of vehicles, ships, or aircraft.|例句：A convoy of trucks carrying supplies crossed the desert.  一队运送物资的卡车穿过沙漠。

例句：The navy provided a convoy for the merchant ships. 海军为商船提供了护航。


近义词：escort, fleet, caravan, procession|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be unguarded travel.|词性:Noun, Verb

The navy convoys merchant ships across the ocean. 海军护送商船横渡海洋。
