impanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/| vt.|编入陪审团；挑选(陪审团)|To select and swear in (a jury) for a trial.  The judge impaneled the jury before the trial began.|近义词：select, choose, enlist|反义词：dismiss, discharge|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The judge impanels the jury each morning. = 法官每天早上都挑选陪审团。
They impanel a new jury for each case. = 他们为每个案件都挑选一个新的陪审团。


一般过去时:
The court impaneled a jury of twelve. = 法庭挑选了一个由十二人组成的陪审团。
