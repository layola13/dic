prelogical:/ˌpriːˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|前逻辑的，非逻辑的|Relating to or characteristic of a stage of cognitive development preceding the development of logical thought; often characterized by magical thinking and egocentrism.|例句：The child's explanation of the event was prelogical, full of fantasy and lacking in causal connections.  孩子的事件解释是非逻辑的，充满幻想且缺乏因果关系。  例句：His prelogical reasoning led him to believe in superstitions. 他非逻辑的推理使他相信迷信。|近义词：illogical, irrational, nonsensical|反义词：logical, rational
