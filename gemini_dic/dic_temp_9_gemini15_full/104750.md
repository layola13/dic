kawaii:/kaˈwaɪi/| adj.|可爱的，卡哇伊的|  A Japanese word describing something cute, lovely, or charming, often used to describe things that are small, delicate, or childlike.  It evokes a feeling of fondness and affection. |例句：That kitten is so kawaii! 那只小猫真可爱！

例句：The kawaii stationery made her happy.  那些可爱的文具让她很开心。


近义词：cute, adorable, charming, lovely, sweet

反义词：ugly, frightening, scary, repulsive

词性：形容词
