cyclings:/ˈsaɪklɪŋz/| n.|自行车运动；骑车次数|The act of riding a bicycle, often repeated or as a sport or hobby.  It can also refer to multiple instances of cycling.|例句：His weekend cyclings often take him through scenic countryside.  他的周末骑车活动经常带他穿过风景优美的乡村。
例句：The number of cyclings increased significantly after the new bike path was built. 新自行车道建成后，骑车次数显著增加。|近义词：bicycling, biking|反义词：walking, driving
