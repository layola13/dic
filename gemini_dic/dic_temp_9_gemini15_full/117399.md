medicated:/ˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 药物治疗的；含有药物的 | Containing medicine; treated with medicine.  |例句：He took a medicated cough syrup. 他喝了药用止咳糖浆。

例句：The medicated cream helped soothe the rash.  这种药膏有助于缓解皮疹。


近义词：drugged, dosed, treated

反义词：untreated, unmedicated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor medicates the patient with antibiotics. = 医生用抗生素治疗病人。
This cream is medicated to relieve itching. = 这种药膏可以缓解瘙痒。


一般过去时:
The nurse medicated the wound. = 护士对伤口进行了药物处理。
She medicated her headache with aspirin. = 她用阿司匹林治疗头痛。


现在完成时:
He has medicated himself for several days. = 他自己服药已经好几天了。
The patient has been medicated regularly since the surgery. = 自手术以来，病人一直定期服药。


过去完成时:
The doctor had medicated her before the surgery. = 医生在手术前给她服过药。
They had medicated the horses before the race. = 他们在比赛前给马服了药。


将来时:
The doctor will medicate the patient with a new drug. = 医生将用一种新药治疗病人。
I will medicate myself before bedtime to help me sleep. = 我将在睡前服药以帮助睡眠。


虚拟语气:
If I were in pain, I would medicate myself. = 如果我疼痛，我会自己服药。
I wish I had medicated the wound sooner. = 我希望我早点处理伤口。


被动语态:
The patient was medicated with painkillers. = 病人服用了止痛药。
The wound was medicated and bandaged. = 伤口经过药物处理并包扎。


疑问句:
Is the lotion medicated? = 这种乳液是药用的吗？
Has the child been medicated for his fever? = 孩子因为发烧服药了吗？


否定句:
The cream is not medicated. = 这种药膏不是药用的。
He did not medicate his son without a doctor's order. = 他没有在没有医嘱的情况下给儿子服药。

词源:
From the verb "medicate" + "-ed" (past participle suffix), ultimately deriving from Latin "medicare" (to heal, cure).
