epitomized:/ɪˈpɪtəmaɪzd/| v.| 成为…的典型；是…的缩影；完美地体现 |To be a perfect example of; to embody or represent in a concentrated form. |例句：Her work epitomized the spirit of the times. 她的作品完美地体现了那个时代的精神。|例句：The building epitomized the city's architectural style. 这座建筑是这座城市建筑风格的缩影。|近义词：embody, exemplify, represent, symbolize |反义词：misrepresent, contradict


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He epitomizes success. = 他是成功的典范。
Her paintings epitomize modern art. = 她的画作是现代艺术的缩影。

一般过去时:
The film epitomized the era's anxieties. = 这部电影体现了那个时代人们的焦虑。
His speech epitomized the party's platform. = 他的演讲完美地体现了该党的纲领。

现在完成时:
His career has epitomized dedication and hard work. = 他的职业生涯体现了奉献和努力。
The project has epitomized innovative thinking. = 这个项目体现了创新思维。

过去完成时:
The novel had epitomized the author's life experiences. = 这部小说完美地体现了作者的人生经历。
The painting had epitomized the artist's unique style. = 这幅画体现了艺术家独特的风格。

将来时:
His new book will epitomize his lifelong research. = 他的新书将体现他毕生的研究成果。
The exhibition will epitomize the artist's career. = 这个展览将体现艺术家的职业生涯。

虚拟语气:
If his work epitomized perfection, it would be widely acclaimed. = 如果他的作品完美无缺，它将会广受赞誉。
I wish his actions epitomized honesty. = 我希望他的行为能体现诚实。

被动语态:
The era was epitomized by its technological advancements. = 那个时代以其科技进步为特征。
His leadership was epitomized by his decisive actions. = 他的领导才能体现在他果断的行动中。

疑问句:
Does this design epitomize modern architecture? = 这个设计体现了现代建筑吗？
Does his attitude epitomize professionalism? = 他的态度体现了专业精神吗？

否定句:
His behavior did not epitomize good sportsmanship. = 他的行为没有体现良好的体育精神。
The product design does not epitomize elegance. = 产品设计并不体现优雅。
