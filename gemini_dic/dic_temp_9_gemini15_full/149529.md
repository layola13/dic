paganization:/ˌpeɪɡənɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |异教化；非基督教化 |The process of adopting or imposing pagan beliefs and practices.  This often involves the suppression or rejection of existing religious or cultural norms in favor of pagan traditions.  It can refer to a societal shift or an individual's personal transformation.|例句：The romanization and paganization of the Celtic tribes led to a blending of religious practices. 罗马化和凯尔特部落的异教化导致了宗教习俗的融合。
The paganization of the festival involved incorporating nature worship and ancient rituals.  这个节日的异教化包括融入自然崇拜和古代仪式。|近义词：heathenization,  dechristianization |反义词：Christianization
