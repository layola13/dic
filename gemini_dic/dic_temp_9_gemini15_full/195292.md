semihistorical:/ˌsɛmihɪˈstɔːrɪkəl/| adj.|半历史的；部分基于历史的|Partly based on historical facts but also containing fictional elements.  It refers to something that blends historical accuracy with imaginative elements, often for the purpose of storytelling or artistic expression.|The novel presents a semihistorical account of the French Revolution, blending factual events with invented characters and scenarios.|近义词：partly historical, historically inspired|反义词：entirely fictional, purely factual
