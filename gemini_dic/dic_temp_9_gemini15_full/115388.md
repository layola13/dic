mansions:/ˈmænʃənz/| n.|大厦，豪宅|A large, impressive house.  |例句：They live in a magnificent mansion overlooking the ocean. 他们住在一座俯瞰大海的宏伟豪宅里。|例句：The old mansion was rumored to be haunted.  人们谣传那座古老的豪宅闹鬼。|近义词：palace, manor, hall|反义词：hut, shack, cottage|名词
