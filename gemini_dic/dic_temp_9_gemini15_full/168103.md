prizing:/ˈpraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |珍视；重视；估价；奖励|To value highly; to regard as important; to estimate the worth of; to award a prize to. |例句：She prized her friendship with him above all else. 她珍视与他的友谊胜过一切。  |例句：The judges prized her painting for its originality. 评委们因其独创性而珍视她的绘画作品。|近义词：valuing, cherishing, appreciating, rewarding |反义词：disregarding, neglecting, devaluing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They prize their privacy. = 他们珍视他们的隐私。
He prizes honesty above all else. = 他最看重诚实。


一般过去时:
The judges prized her entry in the competition. = 评委们奖励了她比赛中的参赛作品。
She prized her old family photos. = 她珍视她家里的旧照片。


现在完成时:
We have prized this antique clock for generations. = 我们世世代代都珍视这台古董钟。
She has prized her education more than anything. = 她比任何事都珍视她的教育。


过去完成时:
They had prized the artifact before it was stolen. = 在文物被盗之前，他们就一直珍视它。
He had prized his friendship with her for many years. = 多年来，他一直珍视与她的友谊。


将来时:
They will prize the winning essay. = 他们将奖励获奖的论文。
We will prize our time together.  = 我们将珍惜我们在一起的时光。


虚拟语气:
If I had known its value, I would have prized it more. = 如果我知道它的价值，我会更珍惜它。
I wish I had prized my time better when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻时能更好地珍惜我的时间。


被动语态:
Her work was prized by many critics. = 她的作品受到许多评论家的赞赏。
The winning entry will be prized at the ceremony. = 获奖作品将在仪式上被奖励。


疑问句:
Do you prize your freedom? = 你珍视你的自由吗？
What qualities do you prize most in a friend? = 你最看重朋友的哪些品质？


否定句:
He didn't prize material possessions. = 他不重视物质财富。
They did not prize her contribution to the project. = 他们没有重视她对项目的贡献。
