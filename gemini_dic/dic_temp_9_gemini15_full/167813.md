primitive's: /ˈprɪmətɪvz/ | n. | 原始事物；原始人；原始艺术品 |  Primitives are things or people belonging to an early stage of development or civilization.  The term often refers to art created by non-Western cultures or by artists who consciously reject modern techniques. | 例句：The museum showcased a collection of primitive artifacts from the Amazon. 这个博物馆展出了亚马逊地区的一批原始文物。

The artist's work is described as primitive, characterized by its bold colors and simple forms.  这位艺术家的作品被描述为原始的，其特点是色彩大胆，形式简单。


近义词：rudimentary, undeveloped, unsophisticated, aboriginal, indigenous | 反义词：sophisticated, advanced, modern, civilized
