pluralities:/pluːˈrælɪtiz/ | n. |多数；复数；多种；多种情况 |The existence of multiple possibilities or interpretations.  It refers to the fact that there are several different options or answers.  It can also refer to the state of having more than one thing.  |例句：The election results showed a clear plurality for the incumbent.  选举结果显示现任者获得了明显的多数票。

The survey revealed a plurality of opinions on the issue. 调查显示在这个问题上存在多种意见。


近义词：majorities, multiplicities, varieties | 反义词：unanimity, singularity | 词性：名词
