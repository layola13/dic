skilfully:/ˈskɪlfəli/ | adv. | 熟练地；巧妙地 |  Expertly and efficiently.  It implies a high level of competence and dexterity. |例句：She skilfully navigated the crowded streets. = 她熟练地穿梭于拥挤的街道。

例句：He skilfully repaired the damaged engine. = 他熟练地修理了损坏的发动机。


近义词：expertly, adroitly, deftly, proficiently, masterfully

反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly


词源：skill (n.) + -fully (adv. suffix)  The word "skill" originates from Old English *scill, meaning "understanding, knowledge, wisdom."  The suffix "-fully" indicates "in a… manner" or "to a great extent."
