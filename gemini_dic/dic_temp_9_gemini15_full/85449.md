hashimoto:/ˌhɑːʃɪˈmoʊtoʊ/| n. |桥本氏病(甲状腺炎)| Hashimoto's thyroiditis is an autoimmune disease where the body's immune system attacks the thyroid gland.  This leads to hypothyroidism, characterized by a deficiency in thyroid hormones. | Example:  She was diagnosed with Hashimoto's disease and now takes medication to manage her hypothyroidism. 她被诊断出患有桥本氏病，现在服用药物来控制她的甲状腺功能减退症。|近义词: autoimmune thyroiditis, chronic lymphocytic thyroiditis |反义词: hyperthyroidism (甲状腺功能亢进)
