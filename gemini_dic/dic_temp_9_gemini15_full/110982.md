liquidises: /ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzɪz/ | v. | 液化；使…清算；使…变为液体 | To make or become liquid.  |  The blender liquidises the fruit easily.  搅拌机很容易将水果液化。 The company was forced to liquidise its assets to pay off its debts. 公司被迫清算资产以偿还债务。 |  melt, dissolve, liquefy | solidify, freeze | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He liquidises the soup with a blender. 他用搅拌机把汤打成液体。
The company liquidises its assets annually. 这家公司每年清算其资产。
