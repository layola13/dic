endocarp:/ˈɛndoʊkɑːrp/| n.|内果皮|The inner layer of the pericarp of a fruit, surrounding the seed(s).  It can be hard (like in a peach pit), fleshy (like in a plum), or membranous (like in a citrus fruit).|例句：The endocarp of a peach is the hard shell that surrounds the seed. 桃子的内果皮是围绕种子的硬壳。


The endocarp of this mango is quite thin and soft. 这个芒果的内果皮很薄很软。|近义词：inner layer, seed coat (sometimes, depending on context)|反义词：exocarp, mesocarp
