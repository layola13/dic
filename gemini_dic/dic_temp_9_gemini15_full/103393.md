jinking:/ˈdʒɪŋkɪŋ/| v.|左右摇摆，急转弯；闪躲|To move with quick, irregular changes of direction; to dodge or swerve.|例句：The car jinked to avoid hitting the pedestrian.  汽车急转弯以避免撞到行人。|例句：The boxer jinked away from his opponent's punch. 拳击手闪躲对手的拳击。|近义词：dodging, weaving, swerving, darting|反义词：straight, direct
