pin-in:/pɪn ɪn/| vt.|别住，钉住|To fasten or secure something with a pin or pins.|例句：I pinned in the fabric to the pattern.我把布料别在了图案上。|例句：He pinned in the notice to the board. 他把通知钉在了木板上。|近义词：fasten, attach, secure|反义词：unpin, detach, release|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pins in the papers every morning. 他每天早上都用图钉把文件钉起来。
She pins in her hair accessories carefully. 她仔细地别上她的发饰。


一般过去时:
I pinned in the map to the wall yesterday. 昨天我把地图钉在了墙上。
They pinned in the fabric before sewing. 他们在缝纫前把布料别好。


现在完成时:
She has pinned in all the decorations for the party. 她已经把派对的所有装饰都别好了。
He has pinned in the important documents to the file. 他已将重要文件别在档案中了。


过去完成时:
I had pinned in the notice before the meeting started. 会议开始前我已经把通知钉好了。
They had pinned in the artwork to the display board. 他们已经把艺术品别在展示板上。


将来时:
I will pin in the curtains tomorrow. 我明天要把窗帘别上。
She will pin in the name tag to her bag. 她会把名牌别在包上。


虚拟语气:
If I had a pin, I would pin in the notice. 如果我有一根别针，我会把通知别上。
I wish I had pinned in the fabric earlier. 我真希望我早点把布料别好。


被动语态:
The notice was pinned in the bulletin board. 通知被钉在公告栏上了。
The fabric is pinned in place ready for sewing. 布料已经别好，准备缝纫。


疑问句:
Have you pinned in the papers? 你把文件别好了吗？
Is the artwork pinned in securely? 艺术品别牢了吗？


否定句:
I didn't pin in the map. 我没有把地图钉上。
She didn't pin in the fabric properly. 她没有把布料别好。


PHRASE->
