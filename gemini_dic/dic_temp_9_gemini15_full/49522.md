demandingness: /dɪˈmændɪŋnəs/ | n. | 苛求；要求高；费力 | The state or quality of being demanding; the act of making excessive or difficult demands.  It often implies a high level of expectation or a need for significant effort. | 例句：The demandingness of the job led to high employee turnover. 这份工作的苛刻要求导致员工流失率很高。  The demandingness of the course discouraged some students.  这门课程的要求之高让一些学生望而却步。 | 近义词：exactitude, stringency, rigor, strenuousness | 反义词：leniency, tolerance, easiness
