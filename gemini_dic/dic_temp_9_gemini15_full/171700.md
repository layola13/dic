puma:/ˈpuːmə/| n.|美洲狮，美洲豹|A puma is a large, cat-like mammal found in the Americas.  It is also known as a cougar, mountain lion, or panther.|例句：The puma silently stalked its prey through the tall grass. 美洲狮悄无声息地穿过高高的草地追捕猎物。|例句：We saw a puma track in the mud near the river. 我们在河边的泥土里发现了一只美洲狮的足迹。|近义词：cougar, mountain lion, panther|反义词：无

词源:
The word "puma" comes from the Quechua word "puma," the language of the Inca people of South America.  The Quechua word itself may be onomatopoeic, imitating the animal's cry.
