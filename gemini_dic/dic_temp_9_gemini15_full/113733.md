macroeconomics:/ˌmækroʊˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks/ | n. |宏观经济学 |The branch of economics that studies the behavior and performance of an economy as a whole.  It focuses on aggregate indicators such as inflation, unemployment, economic growth, and gross domestic product (GDP).| Example:  Macroeconomics examines the factors that influence national income and employment. 宏观经济学研究影响国民收入和就业的因素。|近义词：national economics |反义词：microeconomics

The government uses macroeconomic policies to manage the economy. = 政府利用宏观经济政策来管理经济。
