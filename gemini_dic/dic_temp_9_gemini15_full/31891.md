cheeseburger: /ˈtʃiːzbɜːrɡər/ | n. |芝士汉堡| A hamburger with cheese on it.  A cheeseburger is a type of sandwich consisting of a patty of ground beef (or other meat substitute) placed inside a sliced bun, and topped with cheese.  |Example: I ordered a cheeseburger with fries.  我点了一个芝士汉堡配薯条。

I ate a delicious cheeseburger for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的芝士汉堡。|近义词：hamburger, burger |反义词：salad (as a contrasting meal option)

词源:
The word "cheeseburger" is a combination of "cheese" and "hamburger."  "Hamburger" itself originated from Hamburg, Germany, referring to a type of minced beef patty. The addition of "cheese" simply specifies the inclusion of this topping.
