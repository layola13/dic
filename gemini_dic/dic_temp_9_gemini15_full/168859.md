prof:/prɒf/ /prɑːf/| n.|教授，专家|A professor; an expert in a particular field.|Professor Smith is a renowned expert in astrophysics. 史密斯教授是天体物理学的知名专家。|近义词：teacher, expert, specialist|反义词：student, novice


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a professor of history.  = 他是一位历史学教授。
She is a leading professor in her field. = 她是她所在领域的领先教授。


一般过去时:
He was a professor at Oxford University. = 他曾是牛津大学的教授。
She was a respected professor for many years. = 她多年来一直是一位受人尊敬的教授。


现在完成时:
He has been a professor for over twenty years. = 他当教授已超过二十年了。
She has taught as a professor at several universities. = 她曾在几所大学担任教授。


过去完成时:
He had been a professor before he retired. = 他退休前曾是教授。
She had already been a professor for ten years when she received the award. = 她获得奖项时，已经当了十年教授了。


将来时:
He will be a professor at the new university. = 他将在新大学担任教授。
She will continue to be a professor for many years to come. = 她将在未来许多年继续担任教授。


虚拟语气:
If I were a professor, I would teach astrophysics. = 如果我是教授，我会教授天体物理学。
I wish I were a professor and could conduct my own research. = 我希望我是教授，可以进行自己的研究。


被动语态:
He was appointed as a professor last year. = 他去年被任命为教授。
She is highly regarded as a professor by her students. = 她的学生非常尊重她作为教授。


疑问句:
Is he a professor? = 他是教授吗？
Are you a professor at this university? = 你是这所大学的教授吗？


否定句:
He is not a professor; he is a lecturer. = 他不是教授，他是讲师。
She was not a professor at the time of the incident. = 事件发生时她还不是教授。


PHRASE->
Professor of... = ...教授
Associate Professor = 副教授
Full Professor = 正教授
Visiting Professor = 客座教授
Professor Emeritus = 名誉教授
