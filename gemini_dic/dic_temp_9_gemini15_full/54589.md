dismantle:/dɪsˈmæntl/ | vt. | 拆卸；取消；使瓦解 | To take something apart; to end the existence or operation of something.  | 例句：The old factory was dismantled brick by brick.  旧工厂被一块砖一块砖地拆除了。  | 例句：The government is dismantling the social welfare system. 政府正在取消社会福利制度。 | 近义词：disassemble, demolish, abolish, dismantle | 反义词：assemble, construct, establish, build


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dismantle the machine every year for maintenance. = 他们每年拆卸机器进行维护。
We dismantle old furniture for recycling. = 我们拆卸旧家具进行回收。


一般过去时:
The workers dismantled the bridge after the flood. = 洪水过后，工人们拆除了桥梁。
The team dismantled the opponent's strategy effectively. = 该团队有效地瓦解了对手的策略。


现在完成时:
They have dismantled the old building. = 他们已经拆除了旧建筑。
The company has dismantled its overseas operations. = 公司已经取消了其海外业务。


过去完成时:
They had dismantled the tent before the storm arrived. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经拆除了帐篷。
The government had dismantled the program before the public outcry. = 公众抗议之前，政府已经取消了该项目。


将来时:
They will dismantle the statue next week. = 他们下周将拆除雕像。
The company will dismantle its unprofitable divisions. = 公司将取消其无利可图的部门。


虚拟语气:
If they dismantled the system, it would cause chaos. = 如果他们拆除了系统，将会造成混乱。
I wish they had dismantled the old equipment sooner. = 我希望他们早点拆除旧设备。


被动语态:
The machine was dismantled by the technicians. = 机器由技师拆卸。
The old building is being dismantled. = 旧建筑正在被拆除。


疑问句:
Will they dismantle the power plant? = 他们会拆除发电厂吗？
Is the old car being dismantled? = 旧车正在被拆卸吗？


否定句:
They didn't dismantle the engine. = 他们没有拆卸发动机。
The government will not dismantle the healthcare system. = 政府不会取消医疗体系。
