erasure:/ɪˈreɪʒər/ | n. | 擦除；抹去；删除 | The act of erasing something; the state of being erased.  It can refer to the removal of writing, marks, or data, or to the removal of something from memory or existence. | Example: The erasure of the hard drive was complete. 硬盘的擦除是彻底的。  The erasure of her memory was a mystery. 她记忆的消失是一个谜。 |近义词：deletion, removal, obliteration | 反义词：creation, preservation, retention | 名词
