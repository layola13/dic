nonpunishment: /ˌnɒnˈpʌnɪʃmənt/ | n. |非惩罚；不惩罚 | The absence of punishment; a policy or system that avoids punishment.  This term often refers to approaches in education, child-rearing, or criminal justice that focus on restorative justice or other non-coercive methods. | Example:  The school adopted a policy of nonpunishment, focusing instead on positive reinforcement and conflict resolution. 学校采取了不惩罚的政策，而是专注于积极强化和冲突解决。


近义词：leniency, forbearance, pardon, reprieve | 反义词：punishment, penalty, sanction, retribution
