magenta:/məˈdʒɛntə/| adj. n.|紫红色，洋红|A purplish-red color.|The walls were painted a vibrant magenta.|近义词：crimson, fuchsia, purplish-red|反义词：greenish-blue


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and noun describing a color.  Therefore, grammatical tenses and voice don't directly apply in the same way as with verbs.  Instead, we will show how "magenta" is used in different sentence structures.


Declarative Sentences:
The dress is a beautiful magenta. = 这件衣服是美丽的紫红色。

The artist used magenta paint for the flowers. = 艺术家用紫红色的颜料来画花。


Interrogative Sentences:
Is this shirt magenta or pink? = 这件衬衫是紫红色的还是粉红色的？

Do you prefer magenta or purple? = 你更喜欢紫红色还是紫色？


Exclamatory Sentences:
What a vibrant magenta! = 多么鲜艳的紫红色！

That magenta is stunning! = 那紫红色真令人惊叹！


PHRASE->
magenta ink = 紫红色墨水
magenta dye = 紫红色染料
magenta pigment = 紫红色颜料
electric magenta = 电光紫
shocking magenta = 炫目紫
