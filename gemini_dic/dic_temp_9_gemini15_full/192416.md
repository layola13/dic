USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrolls through his phone. = 他在浏览手机。
She scrolls down the webpage. = 她向下滚动网页。


一般过去时:
I scrolled through the document. = 我浏览了这份文件。
They scrolled back to the top of the page. = 他们滚动回到页面的顶部。


现在完成时:
She has scrolled through countless articles. = 她已经浏览了无数篇文章。
He has scrolled past the relevant information. = 他已经滚动过了相关信息。


过去完成时:
I had scrolled to the bottom before I realized my mistake. = 我在意识到错误之前已经滚动到了底部。
They had scrolled through the entire list. = 他们已经浏览了整个列表。


将来时:
I will scroll down to find the answer. = 我将向下滚动以查找答案。
She will scroll through the images. = 她将浏览图片。


虚拟语气:
If I had scrolled further, I might have found it. = 如果我再滚动一下，我可能会找到它。
I wish I had scrolled back to check. = 我希望我回滚检查一下。


被动语态:
The document was scrolled through by the manager. = 文件被经理浏览了。

疑问句:

否定句:
