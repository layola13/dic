semi-intelligent:/ˈsɛmiɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt/ | adj. | 半智能的，不太聪明的 |  Possessing or showing a moderate level of intelligence; not fully intelligent.  It suggests a level of intelligence above simple or rudimentary, but below truly intelligent or highly capable. | Example: The semi-intelligent robot could perform basic tasks but struggled with complex problems.  半智能机器人可以执行基本任务，但难以处理复杂问题。|近义词：moderately intelligent, somewhat intelligent | 反义词：highly intelligent, brilliant, unintelligent
