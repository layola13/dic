footwork:/ˈfʊtwɜːrk/| n.|步法；脚法；技巧|The skillful use of one's feet, especially in sports, dance, or combat.  It refers to the agility, precision, and efficiency of movement using one's feet.|例句：Her footwork was impeccable during the tango.  她的探戈舞步完美无瑕。

例句：The boxer's lightning-fast footwork gave him an advantage. 拳击手的闪电般的脚步让他占据了优势。


近义词：foot skills, agility, dexterity, maneuverability

反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, ungainliness
