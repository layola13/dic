municipality:/ˌmjuːnɪsɪˈpæləti/| n. |市政当局；自治市；市；区 |A municipality is a city or town that has its own local government.  It refers to the area governed by a municipal corporation or council, and also can refer to the governing body itself.  |例句：The municipality is responsible for collecting garbage.  市政当局负责收集垃圾。

The municipality decided to build a new park. 市政当局决定建造一个新的公园。

|近义词：city, town, borough, county |反义词：rural area, countryside |名词
