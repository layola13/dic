jumpers:/ˈdʒʌmpərz/ | n. | 套头衫，毛衣，跳线衫 | A jumper is a knitted garment worn on the upper body, typically without buttons or a zip.  It's often made of wool or a wool blend.  In British English, it is a more common term for what is called a sweater in American English. | 例句：She wore a warm wool jumper to protect herself from the cold. = 她穿着一件保暖的羊毛套头衫来御寒。

She knitted a lovely jumper for her grandson. = 她为她的孙子织了一件漂亮的毛衣。


近义词：sweater, pullover, knitwear | 反义词：None (antonyms are not directly applicable to clothing items in this context) | 词性:名词
