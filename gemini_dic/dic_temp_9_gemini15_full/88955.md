holes:/hoʊlz/| n.|洞，孔，裂缝|A hole is an opening in a solid object.  Holes can be of any size or shape and are often created by drilling, digging, or other processes. They can be found in various materials, from wood and metal to cloth and the ground.|例句：There are several holes in my socks. 我的袜子上有一些洞。

There's a hole in the wall. 墙上有个洞。|近义词：opening, gap, cavity|反义词：solid, whole


USAGE->

一般现在时:
There are holes in the roof. = 屋顶上有洞。
He finds holes in my argument. = 他在我的论点中找出漏洞。


一般过去时:
The bomb created a large hole in the ground. = 炸弹在地面上炸出一个大洞。
They dug holes to plant trees. = 他们挖坑种树。


现在完成时:
We have patched all the holes in the boat. = 我们已经修补了船上的所有洞。
The rats have made holes in the walls. = 老鼠在墙上打洞了。


过去完成时:
They had already filled the holes before the rain started. = 下雨之前他们已经填好了洞。
The thieves had dug a hole under the fence. = 小偷已经在围栏下挖了一个洞。


将来时:
