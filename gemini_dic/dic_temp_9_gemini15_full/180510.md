regent: /ˈriːdʒənt/ | n. |摄政王；理事；校董 | A regent is a person who governs a kingdom, especially during the minority, absence, or incapacity of the sovereign.  It can also refer to a member of a governing board, particularly of a university or college. |例句：The regent ruled the country in the king's absence. 摄政王在国王缺席期间统治国家。|例句：She is a regent of the university. 她是这所大学的校董。|近义词：governor, ruler, trustee, director |反义词：subject, subordinate
