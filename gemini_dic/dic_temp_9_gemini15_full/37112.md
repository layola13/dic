combusted:/kəmˈbʌstɪd/ | v. |燃烧；氧化；激动 | To burn completely; to be consumed by fire; to undergo rapid oxidation with the evolution of heat and light.  |例句：The car combusted after the collision.  碰撞后汽车燃烧殆尽。The engine combusted violently. 引擎剧烈燃烧。|近义词：burned, ignited, incinerated |反义词：extinguished, quenched


USAGE->

The candle combusts slowly. = 蜡烛慢慢燃烧。
