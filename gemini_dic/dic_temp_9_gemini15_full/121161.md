misdiagnose:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪəɡnoʊz/ | vt. |误诊|To diagnose incorrectly; to make a wrong diagnosis of (a disease or condition).|例句：The doctor misdiagnosed his condition, leading to a delay in treatment. 医生误诊了他的病情，导致治疗延误。

例句：The patient's symptoms were misleading, resulting in a misdiagnosis of the illness. 病人的症状具有误导性，导致疾病被误诊。


近义词：misinterpret, mistake,  err in diagnosing


反义词：diagnose correctly, accurately diagnose


词性:动词
