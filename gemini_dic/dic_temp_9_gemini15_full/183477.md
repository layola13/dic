repugnable:/rɪˈpʌɡnəbl/| adj.|令人厌恶的；令人反感的；令人作呕的|Extremely distasteful; repulsive.|The smell of rotting garbage was repugnant.|近义词：abhorrent, disgusting, repulsive, offensive|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, appealing


USAGE->

He finds the idea repugnant. = 他觉得这个主意令人厌恶。
The behavior of some politicians is repugnant to many voters. = 一些政客的行为让许多选民感到厌恶。
