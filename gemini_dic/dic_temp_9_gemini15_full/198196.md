short-sighted:/ʃɔːrtˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 近视的；目光短浅的；缺乏远见的 | Lacking foresight or intellectual insight; Myopic.  It can refer to literal eyesight problems or figurative lack of foresight. |例句：He's short-sighted and only thinks about the immediate benefits. 他目光短浅，只考虑眼前的利益。

例句：She's short-sighted and needs glasses. 她近视，需要戴眼镜。


近义词：nearsighted, myopic, short-term, unimaginative, unperceptive

反义词：farsighted, long-sighted, visionary, prescient
