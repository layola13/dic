petalism:/ˈpɛtəlɪzəm/| n. |花瓣现象；花瓣状结构|The botanical term for the arrangement of petals in a flower. It refers to the number, shape, and arrangement of petals, which can vary widely across different plant species.  The study of petalism helps botanists classify and understand plant evolution.  |Example: The petalism of the rose is characterized by its numerous, overlapping petals. 玫瑰的花瓣现象以其众多重叠的花瓣为特征。|近义词：floral arrangement, petal structure|反义词：N/A
