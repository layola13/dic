reformulating:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新表述；改写；重新制定 | Expressing something in a new or different way; restructuring or rephrasing something.  |Example:  The teacher reformulated the question to make it easier for the students to understand. 老师重新表述了这个问题，以便学生更容易理解。


The scientist reformulated the hypothesis after receiving new data. 科学家在获得新的数据后重新制定了假设。|近义词：rephrasing, restating, rewording, revising |反义词：leaving unchanged, maintaining the original form
