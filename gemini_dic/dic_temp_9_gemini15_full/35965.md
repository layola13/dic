codefendant:/ˌkoʊdɪˈfɛndənt/| n. |共同被告，共同受诉人 |A codefendant is a person who is charged with a crime along with another person or persons.  They are jointly accused in the same legal proceeding.  |Example: The codefendant testified against the main accused, hoping for a lighter sentence.  共同被告为了减轻刑罚而作证指控主犯。

The codefendants were tried together. 共同被告一起受审。|近义词：joint defendant, fellow defendant |反义词：plaintiff, complainant
