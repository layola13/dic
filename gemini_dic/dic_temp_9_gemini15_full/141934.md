ordination:/ˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 授圣职；任命；排序；协调 | The act of ordaining or the state of being ordained, especially the conferring of holy orders.  It can also refer to the act of arranging things in a particular order or sequence. | 例句：His ordination as a priest was a joyous occasion.  他的神父任命仪式是一个令人高兴的场合。

His ordination to the priesthood marked a turning point in his life. 他被任命为神父标志着他人生的转折点。


近义词：consecration, appointment, installation, arrangement | 反义词：removal, dismissal
