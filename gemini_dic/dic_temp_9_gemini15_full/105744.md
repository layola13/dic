kippering:/ˈkɪpərɪŋ/| n. |腌制鲱鱼；腌制（鱼）法|The process of preserving fish, typically herring, by salting and drying or smoking.  Kippering involves curing the fish in salt, sometimes with additional spices, before drying or smoking it. This process preserves the fish and gives it a distinctive smoky or salty flavor.|例句：The kippering process gives the herring a unique taste. 腌制过程使鲱鱼具有独特的风味。|例句：He learned the traditional method of kippering from his grandfather. 他从祖父那里学习了传统的腌制方法。|近义词：smoking, curing, salting |反义词：fresh (as opposed to preserved)
