lustiest:/ˈlʌstiɪst/| adj.|最强壮的；最精力充沛的；最健康的|Most strong and healthy; full of vigor and vitality.|例句：The lushest, lustiest, most vigorous plants. 最茂盛、最强壮、最旺盛的植物。

例句：He was the lustiest man in the whole village. 他是全村最强壮的男人。


近义词：strongest, most vigorous, most robust, most healthy|反义词：weakest, frailest, sickliest, most feeble


词性:形容词
