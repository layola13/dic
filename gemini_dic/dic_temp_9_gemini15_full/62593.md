enfeeblers:/ɪnˈfiːblərz/ | n. |使衰弱者，削弱因素 |Things or people that weaken or debilitate.  Enfeeblers can be physical illnesses, emotional distress, or even societal structures. |例句：Constant stress and lack of sleep are major enfeeblers.  持续的压力和睡眠不足是主要的削弱因素。|例句：The oppressive regime was an enfeebler of the people's spirit.  压迫性政权削弱了人民的精神。|近义词：weakeners, debilitators, impairers |反义词：strengtheners, invigorators
