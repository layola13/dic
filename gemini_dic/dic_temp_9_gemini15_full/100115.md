interpretations:/ˌɪntɜːrprɪˈteɪʃənz/| n. |解释，诠释，理解|The multiple interpretations of the poem led to a lively debate.  这首诗有多种解释，引发了热烈的讨论。|近义词：explanations, readings, analyses |反义词：misunderstandings, misconstructions


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The professor gives many interpretations of the text. = 教授对文本做了很多解释。
His interpretations of the data are often controversial. = 他对数据的解释往往存在争议。


一般过去时:
She offered several interpretations of the ambiguous statement. = 她对含糊不清的陈述提供了几种解释。
The court considered various interpretations of the law. = 法院考虑了法律的各种解释。


现在完成时:
Scholars have offered many different interpretations of Shakespeare's plays. = 学者们对莎士比亚的戏剧提出了许多不同的解释。
We have received several interpretations of the contract clauses. = 我们已经收到了对合同条款的几种解释。


过去完成时:
By the end of the seminar, we had explored several interpretations of the theory. = 研讨会结束时，我们已经探讨了该理论的几种解释。
Before the trial, the lawyers had prepared various interpretations of the evidence. = 审判前，律师们已经准备了各种证据解释。


将来时:
Future generations will offer their own interpretations of our current events. = 后代将对我们当前的事件给出他们自己的解释。
The new research will likely lead to revised interpretations of the historical data. = 新的研究很可能会导致对历史数据的重新解读。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better understanding of the context, my interpretations would be more accurate. = 如果我对语境有更好的理解，我的解释会更准确。
I wish my interpretations of his actions were less cynical. = 我希望我对他的行为的解释不那么愤世嫉俗。


被动语态:
The painting's interpretations are often debated by art critics. = 这幅画的解释经常被艺术评论家争论。
Different interpretations of the poem have been published over the years. = 多年来，这首诗的不同解释已被发表。


疑问句:
What are your interpretations of the latest scientific findings? = 你对最新的科学发现有什么解释？
What interpretations did the jury give to the witness testimony? = 陪审团对证人证词作何解释？


否定句:
There are no simple interpretations for this complex situation. = 对于这种情况，没有简单的解释。
His interpretations of the facts did not convince the judge. = 他对事实的解释没有说服法官。
