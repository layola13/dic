domesticant: /dəˈmɛstɪkənt/ | n. | 家养动物 | An animal that has been domesticated and is kept as a pet or for work.  It's a relatively rare and newly coined word, combining "domestic" and "animal" (or implying it).  It emphasizes the act of domestication and the resulting state of the animal. | Example:  The cat is a successful domesticant, having adapted well to human companionship.  这只猫是成功的家养动物，它已经很好地适应了与人类的陪伴。 | 近义词: domesticated animal, pet, house pet | 反义词: wild animal, feral animal |
