hyalography:/ˌhaɪəˈlɒɡrəfi/ | n. |玻璃体摄影术|The photographic recording of the vitreous humor of the eye.  It is a specialized medical imaging technique used to visualize the vitreous body, the gel-like substance that fills the space between the lens and the retina of the eye.  |Example: Hyalography can help detect abnormalities within the vitreous humor, such as hemorrhages or opacities. 玻璃体摄影术可以帮助检测玻璃体内的异常，例如出血或混浊。|近义词:vitreous imaging, ocular imaging|反义词: N/A
