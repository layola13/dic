luging:/ˈluːdʒɪŋ/ | n. |雪橇运动 |A winter sport in which a person rides a small sled, called a luge, down a track of ice.  It involves lying face down on the sled. |Example:  My brother is a competitive luger and he trains every day. 我的哥哥是一名竞技雪橇运动员，他每天都训练。


The Olympic luge competition was thrilling to watch. 奥运雪橇比赛观看起来令人激动。|近义词：sledding (though sledding is a broader term and includes other types of sleds)|反义词：N/A|名词
