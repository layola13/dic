slim:/slɪm/| adj. n. v.|苗条的；纤细的；微小的；减少；精简|thin or slender of body; small in amount, size, or degree; to make or become thinner or smaller|例句：She has a slim figure. 她身材苗条。  The chances of success are slim. 成功的机会很渺茫。|近义词：slender, thin, slight, meager|反义词：fat, obese, stout, ample
