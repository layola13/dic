dhotis:/ˈdoʊtiːz/| n. |多提(印度男式裹身布)|A dhoti is a traditional garment worn by men in the Indian subcontinent. It's a long piece of unstitched cloth wrapped around the waist and legs.  The style of draping varies regionally. |The man wore a white dhoti for the ceremony.  这位男士在仪式上穿了一条白色的多提。He tied his dhoti neatly before leaving the house. 他出门前把多提系得整整齐齐。|lungi, loincloth|trousers, pants|名词
