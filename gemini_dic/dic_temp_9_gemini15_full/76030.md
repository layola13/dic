furioso:/fʊriˈoʊsoʊ/|/ˌfjʊəriˈoʊsoʊ/| adj. |狂怒的；暴怒的；激烈的 |extremely angry; furious; passionate and unrestrained in expression.  |例句：He was furioso at the injustice. 他对这种不公正感到暴怒。  |例句：The conductor delivered a furioso interpretation of the symphony. 指挥家对这首交响乐作了激情四溢的演绎。|近义词：enraged, furious, enraged, wrathful, indignant |反义词：calm, placid, serene

He is furioso about the situation. = 他对这种情况非常愤怒。
She is always furioso when she is late. = 她迟到的时候总是很生气。
