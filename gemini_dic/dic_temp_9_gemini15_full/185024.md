retexturing: /ˌriːˈtekstʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 重构纹理；重新纹理化 | The process of altering or changing the texture of something, often in a digital or artistic context.  This can involve modifying the surface appearance of a material, image, or model to create a new or different texture. | Example: The artist used digital retexturing to give the old photograph a more modern look.  艺术家使用数字重构纹理使旧照片看起来更现代。  The architect experimented with retexturing the building's facade to improve its visual appeal. 建筑师尝试重新纹理化建筑物的外墙以改善其视觉吸引力。|近义词：remodeling, resurfacing, surface modification |反义词：smoothing (in some contexts) |名词
