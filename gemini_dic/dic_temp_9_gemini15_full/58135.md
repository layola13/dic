drosera:/ˈdrɒsərə/| n. |茅膏菜属植物|A genus of carnivorous plants that trap insects using sticky glands on their leaves.  They are commonly known as sundews.|Example:  Many species of *Drosera* thrive in boggy environments.  许多茅膏菜属植物在沼泽环境中茁壮成长。|近义词:sundew (指茅膏菜植物本身，而非属名)|反义词: None specifically.
