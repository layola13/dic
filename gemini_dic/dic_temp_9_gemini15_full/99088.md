interbred: /ˌɪntərˈbred/ | v. | 杂交，混血 | To breed (animals or plants of different breeds or varieties) together.  |例句：The farmer interbred his prize-winning horses to produce a stronger breed.  这个农民将他的获奖马匹杂交以培育更强壮的品种。

例句：Scientists interbred different strains of rice to create a more drought-resistant variety. 科学家们将不同品种的水稻杂交，培育出了更耐旱的品种。


近义词：crossbreed, hybridize | 反义词：inbreed, purebreed | 词性：动词
