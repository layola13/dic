namaskar:/nəməˈskɑːr/| n. int.|问候语，你好|A respectful greeting in Hindi and other Indian languages, meaning "I bow to you" or "I salute you".  It is a common greeting used in many situations, from formal to informal settings.|Example: Namaskar, how are you today?  你好，你今天好吗？|近义词：hello, greetings,  salutations|反义词：farewell, goodbye


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I say namaskar to my elders. = 我向长辈问好。
We offer namaskar as a sign of respect. = 我们行 namaskar 礼表示尊敬。


一般过去时:
She gave a namaskar to the priest. = 她向祭司行礼问好。
They exchanged namaskars before the ceremony. = 仪式前他们互相问好。


现在完成时:
I have given many namaskars today. = 我今天已经问候很多人了。
She has received numerous namaskars since her arrival. = 自她到达以来，她收到了很多问候。


过去完成时:
He had already given a namaskar before I entered the room. = 我进房间前他已经问候过了。
They had exchanged namaskars before the meeting began. = 会议开始前他们已经互相问候过了。


将来时:
I will give a namaskar when I see him. = 我见到他时会向他问好。
We will exchange namaskars at the beginning of the class. = 我们将在课堂开始时互相问好。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would give him a namaskar. = 如果我看到他，我会向他问好。
I wish I had given her a namaskar before she left. = 我真希望在她离开前向她问过好。


被动语态:
Namaskar is often given with hands pressed together. = Namaskar 问候通常双手合十进行。
A namaskar was given to the guest upon arrival. = 来宾一到就受到了问候。


疑问句:
Did you give a namaskar? = 你问候了吗？
Should I give a namaskar to this person? = 我应该向这个人问候吗？


否定句:
I didn't give a namaskar. = 我没有问候。
They did not exchange namaskars. = 他们没有互相问候。



PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "namaskar" in the way "what" is used in English.  The word itself functions as a complete greeting.
