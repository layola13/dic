citing:/ˈsaɪtɪŋ/| v.| 引用；提起；表彰 | To mention something as a source of information or as evidence to support a point.  It involves referencing a specific work or author to validate a claim or add credibility to an argument. |例句：The essay cites several prominent historians. 这篇文章引用了几位著名的历史学家。|例句：She cited her own experience to illustrate the point. 她引用了自己的经历来说明这一点。|近义词：quoting, referencing, mentioning |反义词：ignoring, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cites several examples in his report. = 他在他的报告中引用了几个例子。
They cite the latest research findings in their paper. = 他们在论文中引用了最新的研究成果。

一般过去时:
She cited a relevant study to support her argument. = 她引用了一项相关的研究来支持她的论点。
The lawyer cited previous case law during the trial. = 律师在审判期间引用了之前的案例法。

现在完成时:
Researchers have cited this article numerous times. = 研究人员多次引用了这篇文章。
The author has cited various sources throughout the book. = 作者在整本书中引用了各种来源。

过去完成时:
The professor had cited that article in his previous lecture. = 教授在他之前的讲座中引用过那篇文章。
They had cited several historical events to illustrate their point. = 他们引用了几个历史事件来说明他们的观点。

将来时:
He will cite this statistic in his upcoming presentation. = 他将在即将到来的演示文稿中引用这个统计数据。
She will cite relevant legislation in her legal brief. = 她将在她的法律简报中引用相关的立法。

虚拟语气:
If I were writing that paper, I would cite that study. = 如果我写那篇论文，我会引用那项研究。
I wish I had cited more sources in my essay. = 我希望我在我的论文中引用更多来源。

被动语态:
The article was cited in numerous publications. = 这篇文章在许多出版物中被引用。
His work has been widely cited by other scholars. = 他的作品已被其他学者广泛引用。

疑问句:
Where did you cite that information? = 你在哪里引用了那个信息？
Which sources did they cite in their report? = 他们在报告中引用了哪些来源？

否定句:
The student did not cite any sources for his research. = 这个学生没有为他的研究引用任何来源。
The article failed to cite the original author's work. = 这篇文章没有引用原作者的作品。
