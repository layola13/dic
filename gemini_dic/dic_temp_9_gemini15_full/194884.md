semi-dome:/ˈsɛmiˌdoʊm/| n. |半圆形屋顶；半圆顶 |A semi-dome is a structure that is half of a dome, typically found in architecture.  It often forms part of a larger architectural design, such as a niche or apse.  |例句：The cathedral features a magnificent semi-dome over the altar.  这座大教堂在祭坛上方有一个宏伟的半圆顶。The building's entrance is framed by an elegant semi-dome. 这座建筑的入口由一个优雅的半圆顶构成。|近义词：half-dome, partial dome |反义词：full dome, complete dome
