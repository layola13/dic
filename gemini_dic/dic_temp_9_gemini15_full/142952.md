outact:/aʊtˈækt/| vt.|胜过，超过；表现得比…好|to surpass or exceed someone or something in action or performance; to act more effectively or impressively than.|例句：He outacted all the other actors in the play. 他在剧中胜过所有其他演员。

例句：Her performance outacted even the most experienced professionals. 她的表演甚至胜过了经验最丰富的专业人士。


近义词：outdo, outperform, surpass, excel|反义词：underperform, be outdone


词性：动词
