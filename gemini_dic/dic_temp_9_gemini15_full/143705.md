outlets:/ˈaʊtlets/| n.|出口，商店，(感情等的)发泄途径|A place where something is sold, especially a small shop selling a particular type of goods, or a place where a product or service is available.  It can also refer to a way of expressing or releasing emotion. |例句：The mall has many clothing outlets.  这家商场有很多服装店。

例句：She used painting as an outlet for her anger. 她把绘画作为发泄愤怒的途径。

近义词：store, shop, vent, release

反义词：intake (反义词相对而言较弱，因为outlet并非总有明确的反义词)

词性：名词
