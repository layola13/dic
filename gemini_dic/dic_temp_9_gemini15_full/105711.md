quinone:/ˈkwɪnoʊn/| n.|醌|Quinones are a class of organic compounds that are characterized by the presence of a cyclic diketone functional group.  They are widely distributed in nature and play important roles in various biological processes.  Many quinones have vibrant colors and are used as dyes and pigments. |例句：Ubiquinone, also known as coenzyme Q10, is a vital quinone involved in cellular respiration.  泛醌，也称为辅酶Q10，是一种参与细胞呼吸的重要醌。|近义词：benzoquinone, naphthoquinone |反义词：无
