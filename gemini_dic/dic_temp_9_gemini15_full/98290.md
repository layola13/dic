inshrine:/ɪnˈʃraɪn/| vt.|奉为神圣；珍藏；铭记|To enclose in a shrine; to cherish as sacred; to preserve carefully. |例句：The museum inshrines the nation's history.  博物馆珍藏着国家的历史。
The Constitution inshrines the rights of citizens. 宪法保障公民的权利。|近义词：treasure, enshrine, revere, venerate |反义词：desecrate, profane, violate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They enshrine their ancestors' memory. = 他们奉他们的祖先为神圣。
The company enshrines customer satisfaction. = 这家公司奉客户满意为圭臬。


一般过去时:
The king enshrined the new law. = 国王将新法律奉为神圣。
She enshrined her mother’s memory in her heart. = 她将母亲的记忆珍藏在心里。


现在完成时:
They have enshrined the principles of democracy. = 他们已经将民主原则奉为圭臬。
We have enshrined this moment in our memory. = 我们已经将这一刻铭记在心。


过去完成时:
They had enshrined the treaty before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前，他们已经将条约奉为神圣。
She had enshrined that memory for years. = 她多年来一直珍藏着那段记忆。


将来时:
We will enshrine their names in the hall of fame. = 我们将把他们的名字刻在名人堂里。
The museum will enshrine the newly discovered artifacts. = 博物馆将珍藏新发现的文物。


虚拟语气:
If we enshrined these values, society would be better. = 如果我们奉行这些价值观，社会将会更好。
I wish they had enshrined the agreement in writing. = 我希望他们当时已将协议写入文件。


被动语态:
The treaty was enshrined in international law. = 该条约已载入国际法。
His achievements will be enshrined in history. = 他的成就将载入史册。


疑问句:
Will this decision be enshrined in law? = 这个决定会被载入法律吗？
What values does this society enshrine? = 这个社会奉行什么价值观？


否定句:
They did not enshrine the agreement. = 他们没有将协议奉为神圣。
The museum does not enshrine weapons. = 博物馆不收藏武器。
