donating:/ˈdoʊneɪtɪŋ/| v. |捐赠；捐献|The act of giving something, especially money or goods, to a person or cause.  It implies a voluntary and generous contribution, often to a charity or other organization.|例句：He is donating blood today. 他今天在献血。

例句：Many people are donating to the earthquake relief fund. 许多人正在向地震救济基金捐款。


近义词：contributing, giving, bestowing

反义词：receiving, taking, withholding

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She donates regularly to the local animal shelter. = 她定期向当地动物收容所捐款。
They donate blood every three months. = 他们每三个月献一次血。


一般过去时:
He donated a large sum of money to the hospital. = 他向医院捐赠了一大笔钱。
We donated our old clothes to charity. = 我们把旧衣服捐给了慈善机构。


现在完成时:
She has donated over $10,000 to various charities this year. = 今年她已经向各种慈善机构捐款超过一万美元。
They have donated their time to volunteering at the soup kitchen. = 他们已经将时间贡献给了食物银行的志愿工作。


过去完成时:
Before the concert, they had donated all the proceeds to the orphanage. = 音乐会之前，他们已经将所有收益捐给了孤儿院。
He had donated his entire collection of stamps to the museum. = 他已经将他所有的邮票收藏捐赠给了博物馆。


将来时:
I will donate my old books to the library next week. = 我下周将把我的旧书捐给图书馆。
We will be donating our services to the community cleanup project. = 我们将为社区清理项目提供服务。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would donate to that cause. = 如果我有更多钱，我会捐给那个事业。
I wish I could donate more time to helping the homeless. = 我希望我能有更多时间帮助无家可归的人。


被动语态:
A significant amount of money was donated to the research project. = 一大笔钱被捐赠给了该研究项目。
The books are being donated to schools across the country. = 书籍正在被捐赠给全国各地的学校。


疑问句:
Will you be donating to the fundraiser? = 你会捐款给募捐活动吗？
Are they still accepting donations for the victims? = 他们还在接受受害者的捐款吗？


否定句:
He did not donate to the campaign. = 他没有向竞选活动捐款。
They will not be donating any more money this year. = 他们今年不会再捐钱了。
