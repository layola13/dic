insufficientness:/ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃəntnəs/| n. |不足；不够；缺乏 |The insufficiency of funds prevented the project from going ahead.  资金不足导致项目无法进行。|lack, deficiency, shortage |sufficiency, abundance |名词
