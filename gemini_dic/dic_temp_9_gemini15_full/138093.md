normative:/ˈnɔːrmətɪv/| adj.|规范性的；标准的；规定性的| Relating to or based on norms; expressing a value judgment or standard.  It describes something that sets a standard or expectation, often related to behavior, values, or beliefs. |例句：The normative approach to ethics emphasizes rules and principles.  规范的伦理方法强调规则和原则。

例句：This study aims to establish normative standards for evaluating the effectiveness of teaching methods. 这项研究旨在建立评估教学方法有效性的规范标准。


近义词：prescriptive, standard, regulative, typical

反义词：descriptive, empirical, factual
