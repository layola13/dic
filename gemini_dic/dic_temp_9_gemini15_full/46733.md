dandify:/ˈdæn.də.faɪ/| vt.|使变得花哨；使变得时髦；打扮得花枝招展|To make (someone or something) dandy or foppish; to adorn or decorate excessively.|例句：He dandified himself before going to the party. 他在去参加聚会之前把自己打扮得很花哨。

例句：She dandified her apartment with too many cushions and throw pillows. 她用过多的靠垫和抱枕把她的公寓装饰得过于花哨。


近义词：embellish, adorn,  frivolous,  fop


反义词：simplify, plain, austere


词性:动词
