come to light:/kʌm tuː laɪt/ | v. | 暴露；被发现；真相大白 | To become known or revealed, especially after being hidden or secret.  | The scandal came to light after a journalist's investigation.  丑闻在一名记者的调查后被曝光。  The truth will eventually come to light. 真相最终会水落石出。 | emerge, surface, appear, transpire | concealed, hidden, secret | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The truth often comes to light eventually. = 真相最终往往会大白。
New evidence comes to light in the case every day. = 每天都有新的证据在案件中被发现。


一般过去时:
The missing documents came to light last week. = 失踪的文件上周被发现了。
The details of the conspiracy came to light during the trial. = 阴谋的细节在审判期间被曝光。


现在完成时:
Several new facts have come to light since the investigation began. = 自调查开始以来，已经发现了一些新的事实。
More information has come to light about the accident. = 关于事故的更多信息已经浮出水面。


过去完成时:
By the time the police arrived, the truth had come to light. = 等警察到达时，真相已经大白了。
It had come to light that he had been lying all along. = 他一直在说谎的事实已经暴露了。


将来时:
The results of the study will come to light next month. = 研究结果将于下个月公布。
We believe the truth will eventually come to light. = 我们相信真相最终会大白。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence had come to light sooner, the case would have been solved faster. = 如果证据早点被发现，案件就能更快破获。
I wish the truth had come to light earlier. = 我希望真相早点大白。


被动语态:
The information was eventually brought to light by a whistleblower. = 信息最终由一名举报人曝光。
The hidden documents were brought to light during the search. = 隐藏的文件在搜查期间被发现。


疑问句:
When will the truth come to light? = 真相什么时候会大白？
Has any new evidence come to light recently? = 最近有什么新的证据被发现吗？


否定句:
The details of the deal never came to light. = 这笔交易的细节从未被曝光。
The killer's motive has not yet come to light. = 凶手的动机尚未被发现。


PHRASE->
come to light unexpectedly =出乎意料地被发现
bring something to light = 揭露某事
shed light on = 阐明；解释
throw light on = 阐明；解释
come to light gradually =逐渐被发现
