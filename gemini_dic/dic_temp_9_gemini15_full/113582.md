macchiato:/məˈkjɑːtoʊ/| n. |玛奇朵，意式玛奇朵|A macchiato is an espresso coffee drink that is marked by a small amount of foamed milk added on top.  It is Italian in origin and literally translates to "stained" or "marked".|例句：I ordered a caramel macchiato this morning.  今天早上我点了一杯焦糖玛奇朵。

例句：She prefers a macchiato to a latte. 她更喜欢玛奇朵而不是拿铁。


近义词：espresso (with a spot of foam), caffè macchiato |反义词：None  |词性：Noun
