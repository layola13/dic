fittings:/ˈfɪtɪŋz/| n.|配件，装置|The word "fittings" refers to the pieces of equipment or parts that are installed or fixed to something else to make it complete or functional.  It often implies smaller, standardized components rather than large or custom-made parts.|例句：The bathroom fittings are all brand new.  浴室的配件都是全新的。

例句：We need to order some extra pipe fittings for the plumbing project. 我们需要为管道工程订购一些额外的管道配件。


近义词：accessories, attachments, components, fixtures

反义词：None (it's a noun describing a category, not something with a direct opposite)

词性:名词
