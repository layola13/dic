calaverite:/ˌkæləˈvaɪraɪt/| n. | 钙金矿 | Calaverite is a telluride mineral consisting of gold and tellurium.  It is a relatively rare mineral found in hydrothermal veins associated with gold deposits.  Its color ranges from brass yellow to silver-white, and it has a metallic luster. | Example: The prospector discovered a vein rich in calaverite, a telluride of gold.  采矿者发现了一条富含钙金矿（一种金的碲化物）的矿脉。  The analysis revealed the presence of significant amounts of calaverite in the sample. 分析结果显示样品中含有大量的钙金矿。 | 近义词：Gold telluride, sylvanite | 反义词：无
