heavy:/ˈhevi/| adj.|沉重的；大量的；严重的；浓厚的|The box is too heavy for me to lift. 这个箱子太重了，我搬不动。|近义词： weighty, ponderous, substantial |反义词：light, easy, insignificant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bag feels heavy. = 这个包感觉很重。
The traffic is heavy today. = 今天的交通很拥挤。

一般过去时:
The rain was heavy last night. = 昨晚雨下得很大。
The workload was heavy last week. = 上周工作量很大。


现在完成时:
It has been heavy raining all morning. = 一上午都在下大雨。
The company has had a heavy loss this year. = 公司今年损失惨重。


过去完成时:
The snow had been heavy before the storm ended. = 暴雪在暴风雨结束前就下得很大了。
The pressure had been heavy on him before the deadline. = 截止日期前他承受着巨大的压力。


将来时:

I wish the traffic weren't so heavy. = 我希望交通不要这么拥挤。


被动语态:

否定句:
