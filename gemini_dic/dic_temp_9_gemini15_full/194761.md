sell-offs:/ˈsɛlˌɔːfs/| n. |抛售，大规模抛售 |The rapid selling of assets, often driven by panic or a perceived loss in value.  This can apply to stocks, bonds, real estate, or other investments.  |例句：The stock market crash triggered widespread sell-offs.  股票市场崩盘引发了大规模抛售。

例句：The company announced a sell-off of its non-core assets to reduce debt. 公司宣布抛售非核心资产以减少债务。


近义词：liquidation, fire sale, dumping

反义词：accumulation, hoarding

词性:名词
