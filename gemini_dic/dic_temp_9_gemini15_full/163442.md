precontemplated:/ˌpriːkənˈtɛmpleɪtɪd/| adj.|预先考虑的；预先计划的 |  Having been considered or planned in advance.  It implies a thorough consideration before the action or event. |例句：The precontemplated escape route proved invaluable during the fire.  预先计划好的逃生路线在火灾中证明非常宝贵。

The precontemplated strategy was a success. 预先制定的策略获得了成功。


近义词：premeditated, planned, considered, foreseen

反义词：unplanned, impromptu, spontaneous, unexpected


词性：形容词
