liquidate:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪt/| vt. n.| 清算；偿清；消灭；变现 | To settle a debt, usually by selling assets.  To eliminate or destroy something completely.  The noun form refers to the act of liquidating. |例句：The company was forced to liquidate its assets to pay off its debts.  公司被迫变卖资产以偿还债务。


The bank decided to liquidate the failing business.  银行决定清算这家亏损的企业。|近义词：settle, eliminate, destroy, wind up, close down|反义词：accumulate, preserve, maintain, establish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company liquidates its assets every year. = 公司每年清算其资产。
They liquidate their debts by selling properties. = 他们通过出售房产来偿还债务。

一般过去时:
The court ordered the company to liquidate. = 法院命令公司进行清算。
He liquidated his investments to buy a house. = 他变卖投资来买房子。

现在完成时:
They have liquidated all their debts. = 他们已经偿清了所有债务。
The government has liquidated the terrorist group. = 政府已经消灭了恐怖组织。

过去完成时:
The business had liquidated before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，这家企业已经清算完毕。
He had liquidated his shares before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，他已经卖掉了他的股票。

将来时:
They will liquidate the company next month. = 他们下个月将清算公司。
We will liquidate our remaining assets. = 我们将变卖我们剩余的资产。

虚拟语气:
If the company didn't liquidate, it would have gone bankrupt. = 如果公司不进行清算，它就会破产。
If I had liquidated my stocks earlier, I would be richer now. = 如果我早点变卖股票，我现在就会更富有。

被动语态:
The assets were liquidated by the court. = 这些资产被法院变卖了。
The terrorist organization was liquidated by the military. = 恐怖组织被军队消灭了。

疑问句:
Will the company be liquidated soon? = 公司很快就会被清算吗？
Have they liquidated their holdings? = 他们已经变卖了他们的资产吗？

否定句:
The company did not liquidate its assets. = 公司没有变卖其资产。
They haven't yet liquidated their debts. = 他们还没有偿清债务。


PHRASE->
liquidate assets = 变卖资产
liquidate a debt = 偿还债务
liquidate a company = 清算公司
liquidate a problem = 解决问题
orderly liquidation = 有序清算


词源:
The word "liquidate" comes from the Latin word "liquidāre," meaning "to make liquid," or "to dissolve."  This originally referred to the process of turning assets into cash.  Over time, the meaning broadened to include the elimination or destruction of something.
