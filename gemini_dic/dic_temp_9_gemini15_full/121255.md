misestimates:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪt/| vt.| 估计错误，低估或高估 | To make a wrong judgment about the amount, size, cost etc. of something.  |例句：He misestimated the time it would take to complete the project. 他错误估计了完成项目所需的时间。|例句：The company grossly misestimated the demand for its new product. 公司严重低估了其新产品的需求。|近义词：underestimate, overestimate, miscalculate |反义词：accurately estimate


USAGE->

They misestimate the cost of the project. = 他们低估了项目的成本。
We misestimate the difficulty of the task. = 我们低估了任务的难度。

一般过去时:
She misestimated the amount of paint needed. = 她错误估计了所需的油漆量。

过去完成时:
