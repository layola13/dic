gruff:/ɡrʌf/| adj.|粗暴的，粗鲁的；沙哑的|Rough, surly, and unfriendly in manner.  A gruff voice is harsh and low.|例句：He spoke in a gruff voice. 他用粗暴的声音说话。

例句：The old man had a gruff manner but a kind heart.  这位老人态度粗暴，但心地善良。


近义词：surly, harsh, blunt, rough, grumpy

反义词：gentle, kind, polite, soft, sweet


词性:形容词
