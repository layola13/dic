hath:/hæθ/| v. |拥有；持有；曾经|Archaic third-person singular present indicative of *have*.  It is rarely used in modern English except in literary or religious contexts, and mainly in the phrase "He hath."|例句：He hath a good heart. = 他有一颗善良的心。 He hath spoken the truth. = 他说了实话。|近义词：has, possesses, owns|反义词：lacks, wants
