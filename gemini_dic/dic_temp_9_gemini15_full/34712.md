/kliː/| n.| 悬崖,峭壁 | A steep rock face, especially at the edge of the sea. |例句：The climbers scaled the sheer cliff. 登山者攀登了陡峭的悬崖。|例句：The coastal path offered stunning views of the cliffs.  沿海小路提供了令人惊叹的悬崖景色。|近义词： precipice, crag, bluff |反义词：plain, valley
