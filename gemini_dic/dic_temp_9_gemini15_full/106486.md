kraaling: /ˈkrɑːlɪŋ/ | n. |圈养牲畜的围栏；牲畜围栏 |A kraal is an enclosure, typically made of branches, stones, or other readily available materials, used to keep livestock, such as cattle, sheep, or goats, safe at night or during the day.  It is a common feature in many parts of Africa and other regions with pastoralist traditions. |The farmer drove his cattle into the kraal for the night.  = 农夫把牛赶进围栏过夜。 The villagers built a strong kraal to protect their goats from predators. = 村民们建了一个坚固的围栏来保护他们的山羊免受捕食者的侵害。 |enclosure, pen, corral, fold |(无明确反义词，可考虑open field)|名词
