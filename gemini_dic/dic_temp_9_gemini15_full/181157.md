reinfused:/ˌriːɪnˈfjuːzd/| v.|再次注入；重新灌输|To infuse again; to instill or inspire anew.|例句：The artist reinfused the painting with vibrant colors, giving it new life.  艺术家再次为画作注入了鲜艳的色彩，赋予了它新的生命。

例句：The teacher reinfused the students with enthusiasm for the subject by introducing a new, engaging project. 老师通过引入一个新的、引人入胜的项目，重新激发了学生们对这门学科的热情。


近义词：reinvigorate, re-energize, refresh|反义词：exhaust, deplete|动词
