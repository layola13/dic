johnny-come-latelies:/ˈdʒɑːni kʌm ˈleɪtliːz/ | n. |后起之秀；后来者；新手 |Those who arrive late or enter a situation late, often implying a lack of experience or knowledge compared to those already involved.  They are often newcomers who try to assert themselves despite their lack of experience. |例句：The johnny-come-latelies tried to take over the project, but the experienced team members pushed back.  那些后起之秀试图接管项目，但经验丰富的团队成员予以反击。


例句：Don't listen to the johnny-come-latelies; they don't know what they're talking about. 别听那些后来者的，他们不知道自己在说什么。


近义词：newcomer, latecomer, upstart, newcomer


反义词：veteran, old-timer, seasoned professional


词性:名词
