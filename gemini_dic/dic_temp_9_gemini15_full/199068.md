signing:/ˈsaɪnɪŋ/| n. v.|签名；签署；打手势；唱歌|The act of signing a document or making a gesture.  It can also refer to the act of singing.|例句：He put his signature on the contract. 他在合同上签了字。|The singer's signing was a highlight of the concert.  这位歌手的演唱是音乐会的亮点。|近义词：signature, subscription, signing off, singing|反义词：erasing, canceling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He signs the check every month. 他每月签支票。
She signs the song beautifully. 她唱这首歌很美。


一般过去时:
They signed the agreement yesterday. 他们昨天签署了协议。
He signed autographs for his fans. 他为粉丝们签了名。


现在完成时:
She has signed three contracts this week.  她本周已经签署了三份合同。
The band has signed a new record deal. 乐队已经签署了一份新的唱片合约。


过去完成时:
He had signed the document before the meeting. 他在会议之前就已经签署了文件。
She had already signed up for the class. 她已经报名参加了课程。


将来时:
They will sign the contract tomorrow. 他们明天将签署合同。
She will sign the song at the concert. 她将在音乐会上演唱这首歌。


虚拟语气:
If I signed that contract, I would regret it. 如果我签署了那份合同，我会后悔的。
I wish I had signed up for that trip. 我真希望我报了那个旅行团。


被动语态:
The contract was signed last week. 合同是上周签署的。
The song was beautifully signed. 这首歌被优美地演唱了。


疑问句:
Have you signed the petition? 你签署了请愿书吗？
Will you sign the song with me? 你会和我一起唱这首歌吗？


否定句:
He didn't sign the document. 他没有签署文件。
She didn't sign up for the course. 她没有报名参加课程。


PHRASE->
signing bonus 签约奖金
signing off 结束广播；签退
signing ceremony 签字仪式
signing authority 签字权
signing statement 签署声明
