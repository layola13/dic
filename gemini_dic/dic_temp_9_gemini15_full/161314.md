postpone:/pəˈspoʊn/|/pəˈspəʊn/| vt. |推迟，延期|To delay something until a later time.  |例句：We had to postpone the meeting because of the storm. 我们不得不因为暴风雨而推迟会议。|例句：They postponed their vacation until next year.他们把假期推迟到明年。|近义词：delay, defer, adjourn |反义词：expedite, accelerate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They postpone the project regularly. = 他们定期推迟项目。
We postpone our decision until next week. = 我们把决定推迟到下周。

一般过去时:
She postponed her trip due to illness. = 她因病推迟了旅行。
He postponed the announcement until after the election. = 他将公告推迟到选举之后。

现在完成时:
They have postponed the launch several times. = 他们已经几次推迟了发布。
We have postponed making a decision until we have more information. = 我们已经推迟了做决定，直到我们获得更多信息。

过去完成时:
They had postponed the meeting twice before finally canceling it. = 他们在最终取消会议之前已经推迟了两次。
She had postponed her studies for years before finally returning to school. = 她推迟学业多年后才最终回到学校。

将来时:
We will postpone the game if it rains. = 如果下雨，我们将推迟比赛。
They will postpone the wedding until next spring. = 他们将把婚礼推迟到明年春天。

虚拟语气:
I wish I had postponed my vacation. = 我真希望我推迟了我的假期。
If we had postponed the project, we would have avoided many problems. = 如果我们推迟了项目，我们将避免许多问题。

被动语态:
The meeting was postponed due to unforeseen circumstances. = 会议因不可预见的情况而被推迟。
The concert has been postponed until further notice. = 音乐会已被推迟，另行通知。

疑问句:
Will the game be postponed? = 比赛会被推迟吗？
Should we postpone the discussion? = 我们应该推迟讨论吗？

否定句:
We did not postpone the deadline. = 我们没有推迟截止日期。
They will not postpone the launch date. = 他们不会推迟发布日期。


PHRASE->
postpone until = 推迟到...
postpone to = 推迟到...
postpone for = 推迟...
postpone indefinitely = 无限期推迟
postpone the decision = 推迟决定
