damagers:/ˈdæmɪdʒərz/| n.|损害者；造成损害的事物|Those who cause damage or harm.  People or things that inflict damage.|例句：The storm was a major damager to the coastal areas.  暴风雨对沿海地区造成了巨大的破坏。  The vandals were identified as the primary damagers.  破坏者被认定为主要肇事者。|近义词：destroyers, wreckers, offenders|反义词：protectors, preservers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hail damages the crops every year.  冰雹每年都损害庄稼。
The careless driver frequently damages his car.  粗心的司机经常损坏他的车。

一般过去时:
The earthquake damaged many buildings.  地震损坏了许多建筑物。
The falling tree damaged the fence.  倒下的树木损坏了栅栏。

现在完成时:
The flood has damaged many homes.  洪水已经损坏许多房屋。
The heavy rain has damaged the road.  大雨已经损坏了道路。

过去完成时:
The car had already been damaged before the accident.  车祸发生前，汽车就已经受损了。
They had damaged the equipment beyond repair.  他们已经损坏设备到无法修理的地步。

将来时:
The storm will damage the power lines.  暴风雨将损坏电力线。
The construction work will damage the surrounding environment.  建筑工程将破坏周围环境。


虚拟语气:
If the company continued to pollute, it would damage its reputation. 如果公司继续污染，它将会损害自己的声誉。
I wish the careless driver hadn't damaged my car. 我真希望粗心的司机没有损坏我的车。

被动语态:
The building was damaged by the fire.  建筑物被火灾损坏了。
The crops were damaged by the frost.  庄稼被霜冻损坏了。

疑问句:
What damaged the car?  是什么损坏了汽车？
Who damaged the property?  是谁损坏了财产？

否定句:
The accident didn't damage the car.  事故没有损坏汽车。
The storm didn't damage the house.  暴风雨没有损坏房屋。
