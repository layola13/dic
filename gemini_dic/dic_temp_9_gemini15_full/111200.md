lithomancy:/ˈlɪθəʊmænsi/ | n. |占石术，石卜|Divination by means of stones; the interpretation of patterns or markings on stones to foretell the future.  This practice often involved using smooth, naturally occurring stones, or stones with naturally occurring markings. |Example:  The ancient Celtic tribes practiced lithomancy to gain insights into the future.  古代凯尔特部落通过占石术来洞察未来。|近义词：geomancy, divination|反义词：N/A
