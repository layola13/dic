outdelivers:/ˌaʊtˈdɪlɪvərz/ | vt. | 超越；胜过；履行承诺 | To surpass or exceed in performance or achievement; to fulfill or exceed expectations. |例句：The company consistently outdelivers its competitors in terms of innovation.  这家公司在创新方面始终超越竞争对手。|例句：Their performance outdelivered their initial promises. 他们的表现超出了最初的承诺。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outperform, excel |反义词：underperform, fail, disappoint


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The team outdelivers its targets every quarter. = 这个团队每个季度都超额完成目标。
This product consistently outdelivers the competition. = 这个产品始终胜过竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Last year's sales outdelivered our projections. = 去年销售额超出了我们的预期。
The athlete outdelivered expectations in the final race. = 运动员在决赛中超出了预期。


现在完成时:
The company has outdelivered its promises to shareholders. = 公司已经兑现了对股东的承诺（并且超出了承诺）。
Our marketing campaign has outdelivered its KPIs. = 我们的营销活动已经超额完成了关键绩效指标。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, we had outdelivered the initial scope of work. = 到项目结束时，我们已经超出了最初的工作范围。
They had outdelivered all previous records before the final match. = 在决赛之前，他们已经打破了所有之前的记录。


将来时:
We expect the new strategy to outdeliver the previous one. = 我们预计新战略将优于之前的战略。
The improved technology will outdeliver current market offerings. = 改进的技术将超越目前的市场产品。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we could outdeliver our rivals. = 如果我们更努力工作，我们就能超越竞争对手。
I wish our product outdelivered the market leader. = 我希望我们的产品能够超越市场领导者。


被动语态:
The expectations were outdelivered by the team's performance. = 团队的表现超出了预期。
The project was outdelivered by a significant margin. = 项目以显著优势超额完成。


疑问句:
Does this software outdeliver its predecessors? = 这个软件是否优于其前身？
Will the new marketing campaign outdeliver the old one? = 新的营销活动能否胜过旧的营销活动？


否定句:
The project did not outdeliver its budget. = 项目没有超支。
Unfortunately, the results did not outdeliver our expectations. = 不幸的是，结果没有达到我们的预期。


PHRASE->

outdeliver the competition: 超越竞争对手
outdeliver expectations: 超出预期
outdeliver the market: 超越市场
outdeliver the goals: 超额完成目标
outdeliver the previous results: 超过之前的业绩
