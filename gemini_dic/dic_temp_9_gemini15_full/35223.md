clueless:/ˈkluːləs/| adj.|茫然的；一窍不通的；傻的|Lacking knowledge or awareness; uninformed.  It implies a complete lack of understanding or information, often in a humorous or slightly condescending way.|例句：He was completely clueless about the assignment. 他对作业完全一窍不通。

例句：She's so clueless about technology; she can't even send a text message.  她对科技一窍不通，连发短信都不会。


近义词：ignorant, unaware, uninformed, unintelligent, daft

反义词：knowledgeable, informed, aware, intelligent, savvy


词性:形容词
