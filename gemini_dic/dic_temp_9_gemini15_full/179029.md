redelivers:/ˌriːdɪˈlɪvərz/| v.|重新递送，再次发送 | To deliver something again.  This often implies a previous delivery attempt that was unsuccessful or required a second attempt. |例句：The courier redelivered the package after the first attempt failed. = 快递员第一次送货失败后重新送了包裹。

The company redelivers all undelivered orders the next day. = 公司第二天重新发送所有未送达的订单。|近义词：retransmit, resend, re-deliver|反义词：receive, collect
