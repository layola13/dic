/ˌoʊvərˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度组织的，组织过度 | Excessively organized; characterized by an excessive degree of planning, structure, or control, often to the point of being counterproductive or inefficient.  |  His schedule was overorganized, leaving no room for spontaneity.  他的日程安排过于紧凑，没有留下任何空间来应对突发事件。 |  highly organized, meticulously planned | disorganized, chaotic, haphazard | 形容词
