pistillate:/ˈpɪstɪleɪt/ | adj. | 雌蕊的；具有雌蕊的 | Having a pistil or pistils; female.  Pistillate flowers contain only female reproductive organs (pistils), unlike perfect flowers, which have both male and female parts. | Example: The squash plant produced many pistillate flowers, but few male ones, resulting in a poor yield.  这个南瓜植株结了很多雌花，但雄花很少，导致产量低。 |近义词：female, feminine | 反义词：staminate, male
