moisturise:/ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪz/ | vt. | 保湿，滋润 | To make (something, especially skin) moist or wet.  | Example:  I moisturise my face every morning.  我每天早上都给脸部保湿。  She uses a rich cream to moisturise her dry skin. 她用富含油脂的乳霜滋润干燥的皮肤。 |近义词：hydrate, condition, lubricate | 反义词：dry out, dehydrate | 动词
