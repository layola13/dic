loin:/lɔɪn/| n.|腰部，腰肉|The fleshy part of an animal's body on either side of the backbone, especially when used as meat.  It refers to the part of the body between the ribs and the hip. |例句：The butcher cut the loin into steaks.  屠夫把腰肉切成牛排。

例句：We need to protect our lower back and loins. 我们需要保护我们的下背部和腰部。


近义词：back, flank, rump|反义词：无


词源:  From Old French *loin, from Latin lumbus "loin, flank".  The word's history reflects its importance as a cut of meat.
