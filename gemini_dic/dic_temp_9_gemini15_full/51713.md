devises:/dɪˈvaɪzɪz/| v. |设计；发明；想出；策划 |To plan or invent something, typically a scheme or device. |例句：He devised a clever plan to escape. 他设计了一个巧妙的逃跑计划。|例句：The engineers devised a new system for controlling traffic.工程师们设计了一种新的交通控制系统。|近义词：invent, contrive, create, design |反义词：destroy, dismantle, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He devises new strategies every year. = 他每年都设计新的策略。
She devises innovative solutions to complex problems. = 她为复杂的问题设计创新的解决方案。


一般过去时:
They devised a plan to increase sales. = 他们设计了一个增加销量的计划。
The team devised a new marketing campaign. = 团队设计了一个新的营销活动。


现在完成时:
She has devised several successful marketing campaigns. = 她已经设计了几次成功的营销活动。
He has devised a way to improve the efficiency of the system. = 他已经设计了一种提高系统效率的方法。


过去完成时:
They had devised a plan before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经制定了一个计划。
She had devised a strategy to counter her opponent's arguments. = 她已经制定了一个策略来反驳对手的论点。


将来时:
We will devise a new system for managing our resources. = 我们将设计一个新的系统来管理我们的资源。
They will devise a more efficient production process. = 他们将设计一个更高效的生产流程。


虚拟语气:
If I had devised a better plan, the outcome would have been different. = 如果我制定了一个更好的计划，结果就会不一样。
I wish I had devised a way to prevent this problem. = 我真希望我想出一个办法来防止这个问题。


被动语态:
A new system was devised to improve customer service. = 设计了一个新的系统来改善客户服务。
A clever escape plan was devised by the prisoners. = 囚犯们设计了一个巧妙的逃跑计划。


疑问句:
What methods did they devise to solve the problem? = 他们设计了什么方法来解决这个问题？
What strategies have you devised for achieving your goals? = 你为实现目标设计了什么策略？


否定句:
He did not devise a suitable solution to the problem. = 他没有设计出合适的解决方案。
They didn't devise a plan to deal with the crisis. = 他们没有制定应对危机的计划。


PHRASE->
devise a plan = 制定计划
devise a strategy = 制定策略
devise a method = 设计一种方法
devise a system = 设计一个系统
devise a solution = 想出一个解决办法
