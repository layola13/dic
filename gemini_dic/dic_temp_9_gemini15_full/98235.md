insensibility:/ˌɪnsɛnˈsɪbɪləti/| n. |感觉迟钝；麻木；无感觉；冷漠；不省人事|Lack of feeling or sensitivity; unconsciousness.|The insensibility of his fingertips after the accident made it difficult to perform simple tasks. 事故后他的指尖感觉迟钝，使得一些简单的任务都很难完成。| insensitivity, numbness, apathy | sensitivity, feeling, awareness | 名词

词源:

insensibility comes from the prefix "in-" (not) + "sensibility" (the ability to feel).  Sensibility itself derives from the Latin word "sentire" (to feel).
