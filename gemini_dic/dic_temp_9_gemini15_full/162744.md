prebenefit:/ˌpriːˈbɛnɪfɪt/ | n. | 预期利益，预先好处 | A benefit received before a service or product is fully delivered or completed;  an advantage gained in advance.  It often refers to an early or preliminary advantage. | Example: The prebenefit of the new software was increased efficiency in data entry, even before the full rollout.  = 新软件的预先好处是在全面推出之前，数据录入效率有所提高。|近义词：advance benefit, prior advantage |反义词：postbenefit, subsequent disadvantage | 名词
