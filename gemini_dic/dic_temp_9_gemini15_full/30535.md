cephalology:/ˌsɛfəˈlɒlədʒi/ | n. | 头颅学；脑科学 | The study of the head, including its structure, function, and diseases.  It encompasses various aspects of the head, from the skull and brain to the face and neck.  | Example:  Cephalology plays a crucial role in understanding and treating head injuries. 头颅学在理解和治疗头部损伤方面起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: craniology (侧重颅骨), neurology (侧重神经系统) | 反义词:  None directly applicable | 词性: 名词
