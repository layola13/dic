nonglazed:/ˌnɒnˈɡleɪzd/| adj. |未上釉的，无釉的 |Not coated with glaze.  This term is typically used to describe pottery, ceramics, or bricks that lack a vitreous coating.  |Example: The nonglazed terracotta pot absorbed water easily.  未上釉的赤陶罐很容易吸水。|近义词：unglazed |反义词：glazed
