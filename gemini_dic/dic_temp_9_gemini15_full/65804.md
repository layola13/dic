evidently:/ˈɛvɪdəntli/ | adv. | 明显地；显然地 | Clearly or obviously.  It implies that something is easily perceived or understood. |例句：Evidently, he was not paying attention.  显然，他没注意。

例句：The evidence was evidently fabricated.  证据显然是伪造的。


近义词：clearly, obviously, apparently, plainly, seemingly

反义词：obscurely, unclearly, doubtfully
