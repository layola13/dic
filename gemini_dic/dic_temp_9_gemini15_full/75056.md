frighteners:/ˈfraɪtənərz/| n.|令人害怕的事物；恐怖片；恐怖小说|Things that frighten or scare someone;  films or novels intended to frighten.|例句：The old house was full of frighteners.  那座老房子充满了令人害怕的事物。

例句：The movie was a collection of cheap frighteners, relying on jump scares rather than suspense. 那部电影是一堆廉价恐怖片的集合，依靠的是突如其来的惊吓而不是悬念。
