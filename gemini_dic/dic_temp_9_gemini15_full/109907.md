liaison: /liˈeɪzɒn/| n. |联络员；联系；调解；（尤指男女之间的）私通| a person who acts as a link between people or organizations; a connection or link between things; a secret sexual relationship, especially one that is considered immoral. |例句：He acted as a liaison between the two departments. 他担任这两个部门之间的联络员。|近义词：connection, link, intermediary, go-between |反义词：separation, disconnection


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The diplomat serves as a liaison between the two countries. = 这名外交官担任两国之间的联络员。
Our company maintains a close liaison with its suppliers. = 我们公司与供应商保持着密切联系。


一般过去时:
She acted as a liaison during the merger negotiations. = 在并购谈判期间，她担任联络员。
The two teams had a productive liaison to resolve the conflict. = 这两个团队进行了富有成效的沟通以解决冲突。


现在完成时:
They have established a strong liaison with the local community. = 他们已经与当地社区建立了牢固的联系。
The project manager has maintained regular liaison with the stakeholders. = 项目经理一直与利益相关者保持定期联系。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had already established a liaison with the other team. = 会议之前，他们已经与另一个团队建立了联系。
The company had a successful liaison with its new partner.  = 公司与新合作伙伴的合作非常成功。


将来时:
We will need a liaison to coordinate the different teams. = 我们将需要一个联络员来协调不同的团队。
The company plans to establish a liaison office in the new market. = 公司计划在新市场设立联络办事处。


虚拟语气:
If we had a better liaison, the project would have been completed sooner. = 如果我们有一个更好的联络员，项目就会早点完成。
It would be beneficial if we had a stronger liaison with our clients. = 如果我们与客户有更强的联系，那将是有益的。


被动语态:
The information was relayed through a liaison officer. = 信息是通过联络官转达的。
The communication was facilitated by a dedicated liaison team. = 沟通是由一个专门的联络小组促成的。


疑问句:
Who is the liaison for this project? = 这个项目的联络员是谁？
Do you have a dedicated liaison officer? = 你有专门的联络官吗？


否定句:
There was no liaison between the two groups. = 这两组之间没有联系。
The company did not establish a liaison with its competitors. = 公司没有与竞争对手建立联系。


PHRASE->
liaison officer = 联络官
maintain liaison = 保持联系
establish liaison = 建立联系
act as liaison = 担任联络员
in liaison with = 与...联系
