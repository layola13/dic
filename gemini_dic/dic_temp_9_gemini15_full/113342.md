luxuriating: /lʌɡˈʒʊəriˌeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |尽情享受；沉溺于；豪华的 | Indulging in or enjoying great comfort or pleasure.  |例句：She was luxuriating in a long, hot bath. 她正在尽情享受长时间的热水浴。

例句：He luxuriated in the feeling of the sun on his skin. 他沉溺于阳光洒在皮肤上的感觉。


近义词：indulging, reveling, wallowing, enjoying

反义词：suffering, enduring, tolerating

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She luxuriates in the quiet of the countryside. = 她沉醉于乡村的宁静。
They luxuriate in their wealth and privilege. = 他们沉溺于他们的财富和特权。


一般过去时:
We luxuriated in the delicious food and wine. = 我们尽情享受美味的食物和葡萄酒。
He luxuriated in the comfort of his armchair. = 他舒适地坐在扶手椅上享受。


现在完成时:
She has luxuriated in her retirement for years. = 她退休多年来一直过着舒适的生活。
They have luxuriated in the success of their business. = 他们一直沉浸在生意成功的喜悦之中。


过去完成时:
They had luxuriated in the warmth of the fire before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们曾在温暖的炉火旁尽情享受。
She had luxuriated in the silence of the library before the students arrived. = 学生到来之前，她一直在图书馆的寂静中享受。


将来时:
We will luxuriate in the sunshine on our vacation. = 我们将在假期尽情享受阳光。
They will luxuriate in their new home. = 他们将在他们的新家尽情享受。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would luxuriate in a long spa treatment. = 如果我有时间，我会尽情享受一次长时间的水疗护理。
I wish I could luxuriate in a tropical paradise. = 我希望我能尽情享受热带天堂。


被动语态:
The guests were luxuriated in five-star service.  = 客人们享受着五星级的服务。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，很少使用)
Their needs were luxuriated in by the staff. = 员工们满足了他们的需求。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，很少使用)


疑问句:
Does she luxuriate in her solitude? = 她沉醉于她的孤独吗？
Will they luxuriate in their new yacht? = 他们会享受他们的新游艇吗？


否定句:
He didn't luxuriate in the extra work. = 他并不享受额外的工作。
She did not luxuriate in the criticism. = 她并不享受批评。


PHRASE->
luxuriate in the sunshine: 享受阳光
luxuriate in comfort: 享受舒适
luxuriate in silence: 享受宁静
luxuriate in luxury: 沉溺于奢华
luxuriate in idleness: 享受懒散


词源:
源自拉丁语 "luxuria" (奢华，繁荣)，加上了"-ate"动词后缀，表示"使…变得…；从事…"，最终形成了现在分词形式 "luxuriating"。
