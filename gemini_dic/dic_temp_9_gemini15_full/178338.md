reconducting:/ˌriːkənˈdʌktɪŋ/ | v. | 再进行；重新传导；重新引导 |  The act of conducting again;  to conduct something again, especially electricity or heat.  This word is less common and often replaced with more specific terms depending on the context. |  例句：The engineers are re-conducting the electricity to the new wing of the building. 工程师们正在将电力重新输送到大楼的新翼。 The experiment involved re-conducting the heat through a different material. 实验包括通过不同的材料重新传导热量。 | 近义词：re-transmitting, reconducting,redirecting | 反义词：disconnecting, interrupting
