exerciser:/ˈɛksərsaɪzər/ | n. |锻炼者；健身器材 | A person who exercises regularly; a piece of equipment used for exercising.  |例句：He's a keen exerciser and goes to the gym three times a week. 他是一位热衷锻炼的人，每周去健身房三次。  |例句：The new exerciser is designed to strengthen your core muscles. 这台新的健身器材旨在增强你的核心肌肉力量。|近义词：athlete, fitness enthusiast, workout machine, training equipment |反义词：couch potato, sluggard
