responsive:/rɪˈspɑːnsɪv/ | adj. | 反应迅速的；反应灵敏的；易于回应的 | Quickly and positively reacting to something; showing sensitivity to a situation or the needs of others.  |  The customer service team was responsive to my complaint and resolved the issue quickly. 客户服务团队对我的投诉反应迅速，并迅速解决了问题。The responsive suspension system made the car handle well on rough roads.  反应灵敏的悬挂系统使汽车在崎岖的道路上操控良好。| receptive, sensitive, reactive | unresponsive, insensitive, inert | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dog is responsive to its owner's commands.  这只狗对主人的命令反应灵敏。
This software is responsive to user input. 这个软件对用户输入反应迅速。

一般过去时:
The market was responsive to the news of the merger. 市场对并购的消息反应迅速。
He was responsive to her needs during her illness.  在她生病期间，他对她的需求反应灵敏。

现在完成时:
The company has been responsive to customer feedback. 公司一直对客户反馈很重视。
The government has been responsive to the public's concerns about the environment. 政府一直对公众的环境担忧做出回应。

过去完成时:
The team had been responsive to the crisis, preventing further damage.  团队对危机做出了迅速反应，防止了进一步的损害。
She had been responsive to his advances before they fell out. 在他们闹翻之前，她对他的追求曾有过回应。

将来时:
The system will be responsive to changes in market conditions. 系统将对市场环境的变化做出反应。
We expect the government to be responsive to our demands. 我们希望政府能够回应我们的要求。

虚拟语气:
If the government were more responsive, the situation would improve. 如果政府反应更迅速，情况就会改善。
I wish the company was more responsive to customer complaints. 我希望公司能对客户投诉更重视。

被动语态:
The issue was responded to promptly.  这个问题得到了迅速的回应。
Customer concerns are being responded to effectively. 客户的担忧正在得到有效的解决。

疑问句:
Is the system responsive enough? 系统反应够快吗？
Are the authorities responsive to the public's needs? 当局是否对公众的需求做出了回应？

否定句:
The company was not responsive to our concerns. 公司没有理会我们的担忧。
The machine is unresponsive to the control signals. 机器对控制信号没有反应。

quickly responsive 快速反应的
