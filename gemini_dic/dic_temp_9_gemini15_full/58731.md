dunderheads:/ˈdʌndərˌhɛdz/ | n. |笨蛋，傻瓜 |A dunderhead is a stupid or foolish person.  The word is informal and often used humorously or contemptuously. |例句：He acted like a complete dunderhead, spilling his drink all over the table. 他像个十足的笨蛋，把饮料洒了一桌子。

例句：Don't be such a dunderhead; it's easy to understand. 不要这么傻，这很容易理解。

近义词：fool, idiot, moron, nincompoop, simpleton

反义词：genius, intellect, mastermind, sage
