resupinated:/ˌriːˈsuːpɪneɪtɪd/| adj.| 反转的；背腹反转的；倒置的 | Turned or twisted so that the upper side is turned downwards.  The term often refers to biological structures, particularly in botany and zoology, describing an organ or organism that is inverted or turned upside down from its normal position. | Example: The orchid's flower is resupinated, meaning its lip is positioned at the bottom instead of the top.  兰花的 花朵是反转的，这意味着它的唇瓣位于底部而不是顶部。|近义词：inverted, reversed, upside-down |反义词：erect, upright
