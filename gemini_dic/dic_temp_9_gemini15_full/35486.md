coaction:/koʊˈækʃən/| n.|合作行动；协同作用|The joint action of two or more agents or forces.  It often implies a reciprocal or interdependent relationship, where the outcome depends on the combined effort. |例句：The success of the project depended on the coaction of the various teams.  项目的成功取决于各个团队的协同合作。|例句：The coaction of gravity and air resistance determines the trajectory of a falling object. 重力与空气阻力的共同作用决定了落体轨迹。|近义词：collaboration, cooperation, synergy, teamwork|反义词：opposition, antagonism, conflict|名词
