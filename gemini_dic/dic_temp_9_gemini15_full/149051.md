owner-occupiers: /ˈoʊnər ˈɑːkjupaɪərz/ | n. |业主自住者 | 指既是房产所有者又是居住者的人。他们拥有并居住在他们购买的房产中，而不是将其出租给其他人。  | Example:  The government is offering incentives to owner-occupiers to encourage homeownership. 政府正在向业主自住者提供激励措施以鼓励房屋所有制。


The number of owner-occupiers has decreased in recent years. 近年来业主自住者的数量有所减少。


近义词: homeowners, homebuyers (in the context of also residing there)


反义词: landlords, renters, tenants


词性: noun (plural)
