robberies:/ˈrɒbəriːz/ | n. |抢劫案；盗窃案 |The plural form of "robbery," referring to multiple instances of theft by force or threat.  It implies a crime involving the taking of property from a person or place by force or violence, or by threat of force or violence. |例句：The city has seen a recent spike in robberies.  这个城市最近抢劫案激增。

例句：Police are investigating a series of robberies in the downtown area. 警方正在调查市中心发生的系列抢劫案。


近义词：thefts, burglaries, heists


反义词：donations, gifts (these are opposites in the sense of the action, not the noun itself)


词性：名词 (plural)
