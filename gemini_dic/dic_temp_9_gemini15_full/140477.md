old-school:/oʊld skuːl/ | adj. | 老式的；传统派的；过时的 |  Relating to or characteristic of an earlier period; traditional.  It often implies a nostalgic appreciation for methods or styles from the past, but can also suggest something outdated or obsolete. | 例句：His old-school methods of teaching were surprisingly effective. 他的老式教学方法出奇地有效。

His old-school car was a classic model. 他的老式汽车是一款经典车型。


近义词：traditional, classic, conventional, retro, vintage | 反义词：modern, contemporary, new-fangled, avant-garde
