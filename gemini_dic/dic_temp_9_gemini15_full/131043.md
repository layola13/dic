nonavoidableness:/ˌnɒnəˈvɔɪdəbəlnəs/ | n. |不可避免性 | The state or quality of being unavoidable; inevitability.  It refers to a situation or event that cannot be prevented or escaped.  | Example: The nonavoidableness of death is a fundamental truth of human existence.  死亡的不可避免性是人类存在的基本真理。 |近义词: inevitability, inescapability, unavoidability |反义词: avoidability, preventability |名词
