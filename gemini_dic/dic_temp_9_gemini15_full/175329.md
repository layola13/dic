raita:/ˈraɪtɑː/| n. |印度酸奶菜|A yogurt-based Indian condiment or side dish, often containing vegetables, spices, and sometimes fruit. It is typically served as an accompaniment to spicy Indian dishes to cool down the palate. |Example:  The spicy vindaloo was perfectly balanced by the cool, refreshing raita.  辛辣的文达鲁咖喱与清爽的莱塔完美搭配。|近义词：yogurt dip, cooling sauce|反义词：N/A
