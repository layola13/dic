presciently: /ˈprɛsiəntli/ | adv. | 预见地；有先见之明地 |  Showing knowledge of events before they happen; prophetically.  | 例句：He presciently warned against the dangers of unchecked technological advancement. 他预见性地警告了不受控制的技术进步的危险。


例句：The author, presciently, described a future dominated by social media. 作者预见性地描述了一个被社交媒体主导的未来。


近义词：foresightedly, prophetically, insightfully, shrewdly


反义词：blindly, shortsightedly, myopically


词性：副词
