loof:/luːf/| n.|丝瓜络；洗澡海绵|A loof is a sponge-like material, often made from the fibrous interior of a luffa gourd, used for washing or cleaning.  It can also refer to the gourd itself.|例句：I use a loofah to exfoliate my skin.我用丝瓜络去角质。|例句：The gardener used the dried loofah gourd as a pot scrubber. 园丁用干了的丝瓜作锅刷。|近义词：sponge, washcloth|反义词：无
