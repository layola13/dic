nonreasonable: /ˌnɒnˈriːzənəbl/ | adj. | 不合理的，无理的 |  Not reasonable; illogical or absurd.  It implies a lack of justification or sound judgment.  |  例句：His demands were nonreasonable and unacceptable. 他的要求不合理且无法接受。

例句：The company's decision to lay off so many employees was nonreasonable, given the recent increase in profits.  鉴于最近利润的增长，该公司裁员的决定是不合理的。


近义词：unreasonable, irrational, illogical, absurd, senseless, unreasonable, preposterous, nonsensical

反义词：reasonable, rational, logical, sensible, justifiable

词性：形容词
