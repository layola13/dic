non-green:/ˌnɑːnˈɡriːn/ | adj. |非绿色的；非环保的 |Not green in color;  relating to things that are not environmentally friendly.  |例句：The non-green plastic bag ended up in the landfill.  非绿色的塑料袋最终被扔进了垃圾填埋场。


The car's non-green features include a large engine and poor fuel efficiency. 这车的非环保特性包括大排量发动机和低燃油效率。|近义词：ungreen,  un-ecological, environmentally unfriendly |反义词：green, ecological, environmentally friendly |词性：形容词

词源:
The word "non-green" is a compound word formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "green," which in this context refers to environmentally friendly practices and sustainable development.  The usage of "green" in this environmental sense emerged in the late 20th century.
