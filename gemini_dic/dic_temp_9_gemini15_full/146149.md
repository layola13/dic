overengineered:/ˌoʊvərˈɛndʒɪnɪrd/| adj.|设计过度；过度工程化的|Excessively complex or complicated;  designed or built with more features or capabilities than are necessary or desirable.  It often implies inefficiency and unnecessary cost.|例句：The software was overengineered, leading to slow performance and high development costs. 这个软件设计过度，导致性能缓慢和高昂的开发成本。The new car is overengineered; it has features most drivers will never use. 这辆新车设计过度；它有一些大多数司机永远不会用到的功能。|近义词：complex, complicated, elaborate, convoluted|反义词：simple, streamlined, minimalist|词性:形容词
