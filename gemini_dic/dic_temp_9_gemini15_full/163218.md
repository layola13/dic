precompresses:/ˌpriːkəmˈprɛsɪz/ | vt. |预压缩；预先压缩| To compress beforehand; to compress in advance.  The process involves applying pressure to a material before its final use or processing, often to improve its properties or reduce its volume.  |例句：The manufacturer precompresses the springs before assembling them into the car. 制造商在将弹簧组装到汽车中之前对其进行预压缩。


The company precompresses the cotton bales to save shipping space. 公司预先压缩棉花包以节省运费空间。|近义词：compress beforehand, pre-compress|反义词：decompress, expand
