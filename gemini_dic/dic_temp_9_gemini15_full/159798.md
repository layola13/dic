polyglots:/ˈpɒliɡlɒts/ /ˈpɑːliɡlɑːts/ | n. |通晓多种语言的人| A polyglot is a person who speaks, reads, or writes several languages fluently.  Polyglots often have a deep understanding of linguistics and a passion for learning languages. They may use various methods to acquire new languages, such as immersion, formal study, or self-teaching. |She is a polyglot, fluent in English, French, Spanish, and Mandarin. 她是一位通晓多种语言的人，流利地掌握英语、法语、西班牙语和普通话。| multilingual, linguist | monolingual
