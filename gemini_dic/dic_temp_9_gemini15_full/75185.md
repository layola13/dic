frizzier:/ˈfrɪziər/| adj.|更卷曲的，更蓬松的|More curly or fluffy; exhibiting a greater degree of frizz.  The adjective is the comparative form of "frizzy".|例句：Her hair became frizzier in the humid weather.  她的头发在潮湿的天气里变得更卷曲了。

例句：The wool became frizzier after washing. 洗涤后羊毛变得更蓬松了。


近义词：curlier, bushier, fluffier |反义词：straighter, smoother


词性:形容词
