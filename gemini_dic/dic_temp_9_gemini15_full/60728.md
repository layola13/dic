electrobiological:/ˌɪˌlɛktrəʊbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|电生物学的；生物电的| relating to the interaction between electricity and living organisms.  The study of electrobiology explores how electrical signals influence biological processes. |Example: Electrobiological research is crucial for understanding the nervous system. 电生物学研究对于理解神经系统至关重要。|近义词: bioelectrical |反义词:  None specifically;  antonyms would depend on the context of its use.
