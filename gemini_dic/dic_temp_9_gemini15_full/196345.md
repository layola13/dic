series:/ˈsɪəriːz/| n.|系列，连续，丛书|A series is a number of things of the same kind or type, following one after another.  It can refer to a sequence of events, objects, or numbers.|例句：He watched the entire series of documentaries. 他观看了整套纪录片。|例句：The new series of stamps will be released next month. 新系列的邮票将于下个月发行。|近义词：sequence, succession, string|反义词：singularity, single item
