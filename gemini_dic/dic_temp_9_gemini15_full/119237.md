miaul:/mjaʊl/| v.| 猫叫；喵喵叫 |To make the characteristic sound of a cat.  |例句：The cat miauled loudly for food. 猫大声喵喵叫着要食物。
The kitten miauled softly in its sleep. 小猫睡梦中轻轻地喵喵叫。|近义词：meow, purr (purring is a softer sound)|反义词：bark (dog sound), roar (lion sound)

词源:
The word "miaul" is an onomatopoeia, meaning it imitates the sound it describes.  It's likely derived from the sound cats make.  Different languages have similar onomatopoeic words to describe the sound of a cat.
