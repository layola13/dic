cyperus:/saɪˈpɪərəs/| n.|莎草属植物|A genus of mostly perennial, rhizomatous or tuberous herbs in the sedge family Cyperaceae.  They are characterized by their triangular stems and usually grass-like leaves. Many species are found in wet habitats. |例句：Cyperus papyrus, also known as papyrus sedge, was used by ancient Egyptians to make paper.  莎草纸莎草，也被称为莎草，被古埃及人用来造纸。|近义词：sedge, bulrush|反义词:  (无明确反义词)|名词
