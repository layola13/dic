semi-oval:/ˈsɛmiˈoʊvəl/| adj.|半椭圆形的|Half of an oval; resembling an oval but not perfectly symmetrical.|The table had a semi-oval shape. 这张桌子是半椭圆形的。|近义词：partially oval, half-oval|反义词：circular, rectangular
