repromulgated:/ˌriːˈprɒmjəlɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 再颁布的；重新公布的 |  Having been promulgated again; reissued or re-enacted.  This term describes a law, decree, or regulation that has been officially published or announced again, often after a period of time or after some amendment. | The old law was repromulgated after a period of suspension.  旧法在暂停一段时间后被重新颁布。 |近义词：reissued, reenacted, republished | 反义词：abolished, repealed
