dolman:/ˈdɒlmən/| n.|多尔曼式外套，宽松长袍|A dolman is a loose-fitting garment, typically a woman's jacket or coat, with sleeves that extend from the neck to the wrist without a shoulder seam.  It often has a cape-like appearance. |Example: She wore a stylish black dolman over her dress. 她穿着一件时尚的黑色多尔曼外套在她的连衣裙外面。|近义词：cape, loose jacket, coat|反义词：tailored jacket, fitted coat|名词
