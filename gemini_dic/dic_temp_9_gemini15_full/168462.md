probating:/ˈproʊbeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 遗嘱认证；财产检验 | The legal process of proving a will is valid and distributing the assets according to its terms.  This involves verifying the testator's signature, capacity, and the absence of undue influence. |  The family is currently probating their grandmother's will.  这个家族目前正在办理他们祖母的遗嘱认证。 |  validating, administering |  invalidating, contesting | 动词
