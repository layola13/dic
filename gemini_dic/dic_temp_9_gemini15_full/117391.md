medically:/ˈmɛdɪkəli/| adv. |医学上地；医药上地 |In a manner relating to medicine or medical practice.  It indicates that something is done, considered, or happens in relation to medicine or the treatment of illness.|例句：The patient was medically treated for the injury.  病人接受了医疗治疗。|例句：The condition is medically incurable.  这种病在医学上无法治愈。|近义词：therapeutically, clinically|反义词：naturally, non-medically

虚拟语气:
If the treatment were medically sound, it would be widely adopted. 如果治疗方法在医学上是合理的，它将会被广泛采用。

被动语态:
The patient was medically treated for his condition. 病人因其病情接受了医学治疗。
The new procedure is medically being tested in several hospitals. 新疗法正在几家医院接受医学测试。


疑问句:
Is this condition medically treatable?  这种情况在医学上是可以治疗的吗？
Was the surgery medically necessary? 手术在医学上是必要的吗？


否定句:
The symptoms were not medically significant. 症状在医学上并不重要。
The claim was not medically substantiated.  该说法没有得到医学证实。

词源:
From *medical* (adjective) + *ly* (adverbial suffix).  *Medical* originates from Latin *medicus* "physician," related to *mederi* "to heal," "to cure."
