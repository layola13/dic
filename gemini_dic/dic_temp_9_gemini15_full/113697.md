maclurin:/məˈklʊrɪn/| n. |麦克劳林 |Maclaurin is a surname of Scottish origin. It is a locational name from various places named MacLaurin, which are named after the Gaelic personal name MacLaghlin.  The name elements are mac meaning "son of," and Laghlin, a personal name meaning "from the hollow of the lake."  The surname Maclaurin is most prominently associated with Colin Maclaurin, a renowned Scottish mathematician. |例句：Colin Maclaurin was a significant figure in the development of calculus.  科林·麦克劳林是微积分发展中的重要人物。|近义词：MacLauren (variant spelling)|反义词：无
