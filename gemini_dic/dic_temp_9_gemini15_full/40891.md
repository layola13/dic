cookroom:/ˈkʊkrʊm/| n. |厨房|A room or area in a house or other building where food is cooked.  It is a less formal term than "kitchen".|例句：The cookroom smelled delicious with the aroma of freshly baked bread. 厨房里弥漫着新鲜烘焙面包的香味，闻起来很美味。|近义词：kitchen, galley|反义词：dining room, living room
