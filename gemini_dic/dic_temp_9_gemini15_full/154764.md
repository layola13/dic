pertained:/pərˈteɪnd/| v.|相关的；属于；关于|To belong to; relate to; concern.  The documents pertained to the case.|例句：The evidence pertained to the ongoing investigation.  证据与正在进行的调查有关。

例句：His remarks pertained to the subject of the meeting. 他的话与会议主题有关。


近义词：related, concerned, relevant, appertaining|反义词：unrelated, irrelevant

词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The documents pertain to the case. = 这些文件与案件有关。
This information pertains to your health. = 这些信息与你的健康有关。

一般过去时:
The letter pertained to a business deal. = 这封信与一笔生意有关。
The discussion pertained to the upcoming election. = 讨论与即将到来的选举有关。

现在完成时:
The evidence has pertained to the case for many years. = 这些证据多年来一直与本案有关。
All her work has pertained to environmental protection. = 她所有的工作都与环境保护有关。


过去完成时:
The records had pertained to the family's history for generations. = 这些记录世世代代都与家族历史有关。
The evidence had pertained to the suspect before his arrest.  = 在他被捕之前，证据就与嫌疑人有关。


将来时:
The report will pertain to the financial performance of the company. = 这份报告将涉及公司的财务业绩。
These findings will pertain to future research on this topic. = 这些发现将与对该主题的未来研究有关。


虚拟语气:
If the information pertained to national security, it would be classified. = 如果信息与国家安全有关，它将被列为机密。
I wish the report pertained more to practical solutions. = 我希望报告更多地关注实际解决方案。


被动语态:
The matter was pertaining to the court. = 这件事与法庭有关。
The issue is pertaining to the board of directors. = 这个问题与董事会有关。


疑问句:
Does this information pertain to the project? = 这些信息与项目有关吗？
To whom does this document pertain? = 这份文件涉及谁？


否定句:
The findings did not pertain to the original hypothesis. = 这些发现与最初的假设无关。
That statement does not pertain to this discussion. = 那句话与本次讨论无关。


PHRASE->
pertain to: 与…有关
pertain to the matter:  与此事有关
pertain to the subject: 与主题有关
pertain to the case: 与案件有关
pertaining to: 关于；涉及


词源:
源自古法语 *perteignir，意为“属于”，由拉丁语 * pertinēre 派生而来，意为“延伸；属于；到达”。  *pertinēre  由 pre-  （靠近）和 tenere （保持）构成。
