especial:/ɪˈspeʃəl/ | adj. |特别的，特殊的；专门的 |  Especially means to a greater degree than is usual or average;  it emphasizes something as being more important or significant than other things. It can also mean designed for a particular purpose or person. |例句：This is an especial honor. 这是个特别的荣誉。

This cake is especial for your birthday.  这个蛋糕是为你生日特制的。|近义词：special, particular, specific |反义词：ordinary, common, general


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This restaurant offers an especial menu for vegetarians. 这家餐厅为素食者提供一份特别的菜单。
The teacher gives especial attention to students who struggle.老师特别关注那些学习有困难的学生。
