词源:

The word "self-enhancements" is a compound word.

* **self-**:  This prefix indicates something relating to oneself or one's own identity.  It has Old English roots.

* **enhance**: This verb means to improve or increase the quality, value, or attractiveness of something. It comes from Old French "enhancier" (to raise, heighten). The "-ment" suffix turns the verb into a noun, indicating the action or result of enhancing.
