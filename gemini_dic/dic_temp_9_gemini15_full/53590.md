disbursement:/dɪsˈbɜːrsmənt/ | n. |支付；支出；发放 | The act of paying out money; distribution of funds.  |The company's disbursement of funds to its employees was delayed. 公司向员工支付款项被延误了。 | payment, expenditure, distribution | receipt, income | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes regular disbursements to its suppliers. = 公司定期向供应商付款。
Disbursement of funds is handled by the finance department. = 资金的发放由财务部门负责。


一般过去时:
The government made a large disbursement for disaster relief. = 政府为救灾拨付了一大笔款项。
They completed the disbursement of the grant money last week. = 他们上周完成了拨款的支付。


现在完成时:
The organization has already made several disbursements this year. = 该组织今年已经进行了多次付款。
They have completed the disbursement of all the funds. = 他们已经完成了所有资金的发放。


过去完成时:
Before the project started, the company had made a significant disbursement for equipment. = 项目开始前，公司为设备支付了一大笔款项。
The funds had been disbursed before the deadline. = 资金已在截止日期前发放。


将来时:
The company will make a disbursement for the new project next month. = 公司下个月将为新项目支付款项。
Disbursements for the upcoming conference will be made in advance. = 将提前支付即将举行的会议的款项。


虚拟语气:
If the budget allowed, we would make a larger disbursement. = 如果预算允许，我们会支付更多款项。
I wish we could make a quicker disbursement of the funds. = 我希望我们可以更快地发放资金。


被动语态:
The funds were disbursed according to the agreement. = 资金按照协议发放。

否定句:

No disbursement has been made yet. = 尚未支付任何款项。
