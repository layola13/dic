pyramiding:/ˈpɪrəmɪdɪŋ/| n. |金字塔式；递增；积累|The act of increasing something, such as investments or debts, in a way that resembles a pyramid, where each layer builds upon the previous one.  This often involves reinvesting profits or borrowing more money based on previous gains, leading to potentially high rewards but also significant risks.|例句：He employed a pyramiding strategy in his investments, reinvesting profits to buy more stocks. 他在投资中采用了金字塔式策略，将利润再投资以购买更多股票。
例句：The company's debt pyramiding led to its eventual bankruptcy. 该公司的债务累积最终导致其破产。|近义词：escalation, accumulation, compounding|反义词：reduction, decrease, decline|名词
