petrifiers:/ˈpɛtrɪfaɪərz/| n.|石化物；使石化的事物|Things or processes that petrify or turn something to stone.  Petrifiers can refer to both the agents of petrification (like minerals) and the things that are petrified (like wood or bones).| Example: The ancient forest was transformed into a field of petrified wood by the action of mineral-rich water, making the trees petrifiers over time.  这片古老的森林被富含矿物质的水转化成了一片化石林，使树木随着时间的推移变成了石化物。|近义词：fossilizers, mineralizers|反义词：restorers, revivers
