intergenerationally: /ˌɪntərˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəli/ | adv. |跨代地，几代人之间地 |  Relating to or involving several generations.  It describes something that happens between or affects different generations. | 例句：The family's wealth was passed down intergenerationally.  家族的财富是跨代传承的。

例句：The study examined intergenerational trauma within families affected by war. 这项研究考察了受战争影响的家庭中的跨代创伤。


近义词：cross-generationally, transgenerationally


反义词：intragenerationally (within a single generation)


词性：副词
