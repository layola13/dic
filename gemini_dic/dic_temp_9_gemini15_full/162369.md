preacquisitions:/ˌpriːækwɪˈzɪʃənz/| n.| 事先收购；预先收购 |  The activities undertaken before an acquisition is finalized, including due diligence, negotiations, and legal preparations.  Preacquisitions involve a thorough assessment of the target company to minimize risk and maximize value. |例句：The preacquisitions process took six months, during which they conducted extensive due diligence. 事先收购过程耗时六个月，期间他们进行了广泛的尽职调查。 |近义词：due diligence, preliminary investigations, preparatory work |反义词：post-acquisition activities, post-merger integration
