overgarnishing: /ˌoʊvərˈɡɑːrnɪʃɪŋ/ | n. |过度装饰；装饰过度|The act of excessively decorating or ornamenting something.  It implies that the decoration is excessive, detracting from the overall effect rather than enhancing it. |Example: The overgarnishing of the cake made it look cluttered and less appealing.  过度装饰蛋糕使其看起来凌乱且不那么吸引人。|近义词：overdecoration, excessive ornamentation|反义词：understatement, simplicity
