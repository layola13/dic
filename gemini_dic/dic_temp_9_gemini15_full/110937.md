例句：

Her lippiness got her into trouble with the teacher.  她的多嘴惹恼了老师。

His lippiness was a sign of his insecurity. 他的傲慢是其缺乏安全感的表现。


近义词：sassiness, cheekiness, impertinence, boldness, garrulity
