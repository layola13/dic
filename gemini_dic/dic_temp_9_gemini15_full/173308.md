quasi-cunning: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈkʌnɪŋ/ | adj. | 貌似狡猾的；略显狡诈的 |  Apparently cunning; showing a slight degree of cunning.  It suggests a level of cleverness or shrewdness that is not fully developed or overtly malicious.  It might be described as a milder, less aggressive form of cunning. |


例句：

He employed a quasi-cunning strategy, more of a clever trick than a truly deceptive plan. = 他采用了一种貌似狡猾的策略，与其说是真正的欺骗计划，不如说是一个巧妙的诡计。

Her quasi-cunning smile hinted at a secret she wasn't ready to reveal. = 她那略显狡诈的笑容暗示着一个她还没准备好透露的秘密。


近义词：shrewd, sly, artful, wily, canny


反义词：naive, ingenuous, innocent, straightforward


词性：形容词
