/aʊtfeɪmz/ | vt. |胜过…的名声；比…更有名 | to surpass in fame; to exceed in reputation |  His achievements outfame his predecessors. 他的成就胜过他的前辈。 | surpass, eclipse, overshadow | be less famous than, be unknown | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outfames all other artists in the city. = 他比城里其他所有艺术家都更有名。
This new invention outfames all previous attempts. =这项新发明比以前所有的尝试都更有名。


一般过去时:
Her novel outfamed all others that year. = 她的小说在那一年比其他小说都更有名。
The actor's performance outfamed the play itself. = 这位演员的表演比戏剧本身更有名。


现在完成时:
His legacy has outfamed him in death. = 他死后，他的遗产比他本人更有名。
The band's music has outfamed their initial expectations. = 乐队的音乐比他们最初的预期更有名。


过去完成时:
By the time he died, his work had outfamed him. = 他死的时候，他的作品比他本人更有名。
Before the scandal, the company had outfamed its competitors. = 丑闻发生之前，这家公司比它的竞争对手更有名。


将来时:
This discovery will outfame all previous scientific breakthroughs. = 这一发现将比以往所有科学突破都更有名。
His next book is predicted to outfame his previous works. = 他的下一本书预计将比他之前的作品更有名。


虚拟语气:
If his paintings were discovered earlier, they would outfame his sculptures. = 如果他的画作早些被发现，它们就会比他的雕塑更有名。
I wish his music had outfamed his contemporaries during his lifetime. = 我希望他的音乐在他有生之年就比他的同时代人更有名。


被动语态:
He was outfamed by his younger brother. = 他被他的弟弟比下去了（名气不如弟弟）。
The original work was outfamed by its many imitations. = 原作的名气被众多模仿作品盖过了。


疑问句:
Does his work outfame his personality? = 他的作品比他的个性更有名吗？
Will this new technology outfame existing solutions? =这项新技术会比现有的解决方案更有名吗？


否定句:
His latest film did not outfame his previous successes. = 他最新的电影并没有比他之前的作品更成功（有名）。
The singer's new album failed to outfame her earlier hits. = 歌手的新专辑未能超越她之前的热门歌曲（名气不如）。


PHRASE->

outfame someone/something = 比某人/某物更有名
outfame in popularity = 在受欢迎程度上胜过
outfame by a long shot = 远远胜过
outfame across generations =  跨越几代都更有名
outfame the competition = 比竞争对手更有名
