rally's:/ˈræliːz/| n. |集会；群众大会；反弹；恢复；团结；|a large public meeting, especially one held outdoors, for a particular purpose; a sudden increase in the price, value, or amount of something; the act of coming together to support a cause. |例句：The political rally drew a huge crowd.  这场政治集会吸引了大批人群。
The stock market experienced a significant rally after the news.  消息公布后，股市出现了大幅反弹。|近义词：meeting, gathering, demonstration, rebound, recovery |反义词：decline, fall, slump, collapse


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The team rallies together before every game. = 每场比赛前，球队都会团结一心。
She rallies her supporters with a powerful speech. = 她用充满力量的演讲激励支持者。


一般过去时:
The protesters rallied against the new law. = 抗议者们集会反对新法律。
The price of oil rallied sharply after the announcement. = 宣布后，油价急剧上涨。


现在完成时:
The company has rallied from its recent losses. = 公司已经从最近的损失中恢复过来。
The community has rallied around the family in their time of need. = 社区在他们需要的时候团结在那个家庭周围。


过去完成时:
The stock market had rallied before the crash. = 在崩盘之前，股市曾出现过反弹。
They had rallied their troops before the final battle. = 他们在最后的战斗前集结了军队。


将来时:
The supporters will rally behind their candidate. = 支持者们将支持他们的候选人。
We expect the economy to rally in the coming months. = 我们预计经济将在未来几个月复苏。


虚拟语气:
If they rallied their forces, they might have won the battle. = 如果他们集结兵力，他们可能会赢得战斗。
I wish the market had rallied sooner. = 我希望市场能早点反弹。


被动语态:
The troops were rallied by the general. = 士兵们被将军集结起来。
The community was rallied by the disaster. = 社区因灾难而团结起来。


疑问句:
Will they rally enough support to win the election? = 他们能否获得足够的支持赢得选举？
Did the price rally after the announcement? = 公布后价格上涨了吗？


否定句:
The team did not rally enough to win the game. = 球队没有足够团结来赢得比赛。
The economy has not rallied as quickly as expected. = 经济复苏的速度不如预期快。
