/ˈkaʊntərkɹɪtɪsɪzəm/| n. |反驳，批评性回应 |A countercriticism is a criticism made in response to a previous criticism. It's a rebuttal or refutation of an earlier argument or accusation.  |Example:  His initial criticism of the policy was met with a strong countercriticism from the government.  他的最初批评受到了政府强烈的反驳。

Example: The professor's countercriticism of the student's paper was both insightful and constructive. 教授对学生论文的反驳既有见地又富有建设性。|近义词：rebuttal, refutation, rejoinder, answer |反义词：endorsement, approval, agreement |名词
