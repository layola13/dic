colloquies:/ˈkɒləkwiːz/ | n. |对话，谈话；座谈会 | A colloquy is a formal conversation or dialogue, often on a serious or intellectual topic.  It can also refer to a series of dialogues or a discussion. | Example: The philosopher engaged in a lengthy colloquy with his student about the nature of reality.  哲学家与他的学生就现实的本质进行了长时间的对话。The colloquy on climate change brought together leading experts from around the world.  关于气候变化的座谈会汇聚了来自世界各地的 leading experts. |近义词：conversation, dialogue, discussion, conference |反义词：monologue, soliloquy


Etymology: From Medieval Latin *colloquium, from Latin colloqui "to talk together," from com- "together" + loqui "to speak."
