prefashioning:/ˌpriːˈfæʃənɪŋ/| n.|预先制作，预先成型|The act of shaping or preparing something in advance; preliminary shaping or forming.|例句：The prefashioning of the clay was crucial to the success of the sculpture.  预先塑造粘土对于雕塑的成功至关重要。

例句：Careful prefashioning of the metal ensured a smooth weld. 金属的预先成型确保了焊接的平滑。


近义词：prefabrication, preformation, preparation


反义词：improvisation, impromptu
