grandkids:/ˈɡrændˌtʃɪldrən/| n.|孙辈，外孙辈|Grandkids are the children of one's children or grandchildren.  It's a shortened and informal version of "grandchildren".|例句：My grandkids are coming to visit next week. = 我的孙子孙女下周要来访。
例句：I love spending time with my grandkids. = 我喜欢和我的孙子孙女一起度过时光。|近义词：grandchildren, granddaughters, grandsons|反义词：None
