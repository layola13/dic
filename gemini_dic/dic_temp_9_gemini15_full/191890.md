scolecida:/ˌskoʊlɪˈsaɪdə/| n. |杀线虫剂，杀蠕虫剂|A scolecida is a type of pesticide specifically designed to kill nematodes (roundworms), a type of parasitic worm.  These are often used in agriculture and veterinary medicine to control nematode infestations.|例句：The farmer applied a scolecida to his crops to combat the nematode infestation. 农民在他的庄稼上施用了杀线虫剂来防治线虫侵染。|近义词：nematicide, anthelmintic|反义词：None specifically, but  "nematode growth stimulant" could be considered an antonym in a specific context.
