perpetuators: /pərˈpetʃuˌeɪtərz/ | n. | 继续者；使…永久存在者 | Those who perpetuate something, especially something undesirable, by prolonging it or causing it to continue.  They are the ones who keep something going, often something harmful or negative. | 例句：The perpetuators of violence must be brought to justice.  暴力行为的 perpetuation者必须绳之以法。  The media's portrayal perpetuated harmful stereotypes. 媒体的描绘延续了有害的刻板印象。 | 近义词：maintainers, preservers, sustainers | 反义词：terminators, abolishers, destroyers | 名词
