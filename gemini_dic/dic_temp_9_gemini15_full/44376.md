cross-current:/ˈkrɒsˌkʌrənt/|/ˈkrɑːsˌkʌrənt/| n. |交叉流，逆流，对立的意见或趋势 |A cross-current is a current that flows in a different direction from the main current.  It can also refer to conflicting opinions or trends. |例句：The swimmer struggled against the strong cross-current.  游泳者努力对抗强劲的逆流。

The political debate was full of cross-currents, with various factions pulling in different directions.  政治辩论充满了对立的意见，各个派系朝着不同的方向拉扯。|近义词：countercurrent, opposing force, conflicting trend |反义词：mainstream, consensus, uniformity
