clear-headed: /ˌklɪrˈhedɪd/ | adj. |头脑清醒的；思路清晰的 |Having a clear and rational mind; able to think clearly and make sound judgments.  |例句：She remained clear-headed despite the chaos.  她尽管身处混乱之中，仍然保持着清醒的头脑。

例句：He needs to be clear-headed to make such an important decision. 他需要保持头脑清醒才能做出如此重要的决定。


近义词：rational, lucid, sensible, composed, level-headed

反义词：confused, muddle-headed, befuddled, disoriented, dazed


词源:  The word is a compound of "clear" (meaning free from confusion or ambiguity) and "headed" (referring to the head, the seat of intellect and reasoning).  Its meaning is a straightforward combination of the two words.
