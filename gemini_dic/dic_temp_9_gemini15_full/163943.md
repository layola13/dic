predicable:/prɪˈdɪktəbl/ | adj. | 可预测的，可预知的 | Easily foreseen or predicted; expected.  Something that is predictable happens or is likely to happen in a way that you expect. | 例句：The outcome of the election was entirely predictable.  选举的结果完全可以预料。

The weather forecast is usually quite predictable in this region.  这个地区的天气预报通常相当准确。


近义词：foreseeable, expected, anticipated, foreseen | 反义词：unpredictable, unexpected, unforeseen


词源:  The word "predictable" is derived from the verb "predict," which comes from the Latin word "praedicere," meaning "to say beforehand" or "to foretell."  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.
