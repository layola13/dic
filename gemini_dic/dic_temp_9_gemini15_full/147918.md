overregulating: /ˌoʊvərˈreɡjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |过度管制，管制过度 | The act of regulating something excessively, often leading to negative consequences.  | Example: Overregulating the market can stifle innovation and competition.  过度管制市场可能会扼杀创新和竞争。  | 近义词：overcontrol, overmanaging, micromanaging | 反义词：deregulating, underregulating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government overregulates the financial industry. = 政府过度管制金融业。
They overregulate small businesses with unnecessary paperwork. = 他们用不必要的文书工作过度管制小型企业。


一般过去时:
The previous administration overregulated the energy sector. = 前一届政府过度管制能源部门。
They overregulated the food industry, leading to higher prices. = 他们过度管制食品行业，导致价格上涨。


现在完成时:
The authorities have overregulated the transportation system. = 当局已经过度管制了交通系统。
The city council has overregulated building permits, slowing down construction. = 市议会过度管制了建筑许可证，减缓了建设速度。


过去完成时:
The company had overregulated its internal processes, causing inefficiencies. = 公司已经过度管制了其内部流程，导致效率低下。
They had overregulated the market before the economic downturn. = 经济衰退之前，他们已经过度管制了市场。


将来时:
The new regulations will overregulate the tech industry. = 新法规将过度管制科技行业。
They will overregulate the healthcare system, limiting patient choices. = 他们将过度管制医疗系统，限制患者的选择。


虚拟语气:
If they overregulated the market, businesses would suffer. = 如果他们过度管制市场，企业将会遭受损失。
I wish they hadn't overregulated the education system. = 我希望他们没有过度管制教育系统。


被动语态:
The market was overregulated by the government. = 市场受到了政府的过度管制。
The small businesses are being overregulated by excessive bureaucracy. = 小型企业正受到过度官僚主义的过度管制。


疑问句:

否定句:
The government is not overregulating the telecommunications sector. = 政府没有过度管制电信部门。
They did not overregulate the pharmaceutical industry. = 他们没有过度管制制药行业。
