It implies a greater degree of negligence than simply being remiss. |例句：The manager was deemed overremiss in his handling of the safety concerns, leading to a serious accident.  经理在处理安全问题上被认为过于疏忽，导致了一起严重的事故。|近义词：negligent, careless, derelict, defaulting |反义词：diligent, careful, attentive, meticulous
