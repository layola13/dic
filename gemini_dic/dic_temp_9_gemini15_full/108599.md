lead the way:/liːd ðə weɪ/| phrasal verb|带路，引路|To guide someone or something by going in front of them.|例句：Please lead the way to the meeting room. 请带路去会议室。


例句：Could you lead the way to the nearest hospital? 你能带路去最近的医院吗？


近义词：guide, show the way, precede


反义词：follow, trail


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leads the way through the forest. 他带路穿过森林。
She leads the way to a better future. 她引领着走向更美好的未来。


一般过去时:
He led the way to the hidden cave. 他带路去那个隐藏的洞穴。
She led the way through the crowded streets. 她带路穿过拥挤的街道。


现在完成时:
They have led the way in technological innovation. 他们在技术创新方面一直走在前列。
She has led the way in promoting women's rights. 她一直走在促进妇女权利的前列。


过去完成时:
He had led the way before, so he knew the path. 他以前带过路，所以他知道路。
They had led the way for many years before they retired. 他们退休前已经带路很多年了。


将来时:
He will lead the way to victory. 他将带领我们走向胜利。
She will lead the way to a new era of peace. 她将引领一个新的和平时代。


虚拟语气:
If he led the way, we would follow him. 如果他带路，我们会跟着他。
If she had led the way, we wouldn't have gotten lost. 如果她带路，我们就不会迷路了。


被动语态:
The way was led by a local guide. 路是由当地导游带的。
The path was led by the experienced hiker. 路是由经验丰富的徒步旅行者带的。


疑问句:
Who will lead the way? 谁来带路？
Can you lead the way to the station? 你能带路去车站吗？


否定句:
He did not lead the way. 他没有带路。
She couldn't lead the way in the dark. 她黑暗中无法带路。


PHRASE->
lead the way to success  引领走向成功
lead the way in innovation  在创新方面领先
lead the way to a brighter future  引领走向更光明的未来
lead the way in the fight against poverty  在反贫困斗争中带头
lead the way to a better understanding  引领走向更好的理解


词源:
"Lead" comes from the Old English word "lædan," meaning "to guide" or "to conduct."  "The way" is a simple prepositional phrase indicating direction or path. The phrasal verb thus combines the meaning of guiding and the direction being guided towards.
