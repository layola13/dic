/ˈrɪkʃɑːz/| n.|人力车；黄包车|A rickshaw is a two-wheeled or three-wheeled vehicle pulled by a person, typically used for transportation of passengers.|例句：Many tourists enjoy riding in rickshaws through the old city.  许多游客喜欢乘坐人力车游览老城。|例句：The rickshaw driver pulled his cart through the crowded streets.  人力车夫拉着他的车穿过拥挤的街道。|近义词：jinrikisha, cycle rickshaw|反义词：car, taxi
