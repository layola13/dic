non-theocratic:/ˌnɒnθiːəˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 非神权政治的；非宗教统治的 | Not governed by religious leaders or laws.  This term describes a system of government where religious authority does not hold supreme power.  The state and religious institutions are separate. | Example:  Many modern nations are non-theocratic, with a clear separation of church and state.  许多现代国家是非神权政治的，教会和国家之间有着明确的分离。|近义词：secular, lay, civil | 反义词：theocratic
