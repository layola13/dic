clocked-in: /klɑːkt ˈɪn/ | adj. | 已打卡上班的；已登记上班的 |  Describes a person who has recorded their arrival at work using a time clock or similar system.  It implies that they have begun their workday. | Example: "He was clocked-in by 8:00 am sharp." 他八点整准时打卡上班了。|近义词：signed in, checked in, registered | 反义词：clocked-out, off-duty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He clocks in at 8 am every day. = 他每天早上8点打卡上班。
She usually clocks in before her boss. = 她通常比老板先打卡上班。

一般过去时:
I clocked in late yesterday. = 我昨天上班打卡迟到了。
They clocked in before the meeting started. = 他们在会议开始前打卡上班了。

现在完成时:
He has clocked in for the day. = 他今天已经打卡上班了。
She has already clocked in this morning. = 她今天早上已经打卡上班了。

过去完成时:
He had clocked in before the system crashed. = 系统崩溃之前他已经打卡上班了。
They had already clocked in when the fire alarm rang. = 火警响起时他们已经打卡上班了。

将来时:
I will clock in early tomorrow. = 我明天会早点打卡上班。
She will clock in at her usual time. = 她会在她通常的时间打卡上班。

虚拟语气:
If I had clocked in on time, I wouldn't have been late. = 如果我按时打卡上班，我就不会迟到了。
I wish I had clocked in earlier this morning. = 我真希望今天早上早点打卡上班。

被动语态:
The employees are expected to be clocked in by 9 am. = 员工们应该在上午9点之前打卡上班。
The system automatically clocks in employees when they scan their badges. = 当员工扫描他们的徽章时，系统会自动为他们打卡上班。

疑问句:
Has everyone clocked in yet? = 大家都打卡上班了吗？
What time did he clock in this morning? = 他今天早上几点打卡上班的？

否定句:
He didn't clock in this morning. = 他今天早上没有打卡上班。
She hasn't clocked in yet. = 她还没打卡上班。
