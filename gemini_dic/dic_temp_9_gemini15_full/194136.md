self-inflations:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈfleɪʃənz/| n.|自我膨胀；自负；自我夸大|The act of inflating oneself, often referring to an inflated sense of self-importance or self-worth.  It implies an unrealistic or exaggerated view of one's abilities, achievements, or importance.|例句：His self-inflations were evident in his boasting and disregard for others' opinions.  他的自我膨胀在他夸夸其谈和无视他人意见中显而易见。

例句：The company's self-inflations led to unrealistic projections and ultimately, failure.  公司的自我膨胀导致了不切实际的预测，最终导致了失败。


近义词：ego inflation, conceit, narcissism, arrogance, self-aggrandizement|反义词：humility, modesty, self-deprecation|词性:noun
