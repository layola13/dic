overconstructs:/ˌoʊvərˈkənstrʌkts/ | v. |过度构建；过度设计|To create something with excessive complexity or detail, often to the detriment of its functionality or clarity.  It implies an unnecessary or counterproductive elaboration. |例句：The software engineers overconstructed the user interface, making it confusing and difficult to use.  软件工程师过度设计了用户界面，使其混乱且难以使用。

The architect overconstructed the building, resulting in high costs and structural instability. 建筑师过度设计了建筑物，导致高成本和结构不稳定。|近义词：overengineer, overcomplicate, overelaborate |反义词：simplify, streamline, minimize
