participate:/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪt/| /pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪt/| v.|参与，参加|To take part in something.|例句：Many students participated in the school play. 许多学生参加了学校的戏剧演出。

例句：She actively participated in the discussion. 她积极参与了讨论。


近义词：join, take part in, engage in, be involved in| 反义词：withdraw, abstain, refrain from| 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I participate in many extracurricular activities. = 我参加许多课外活动。
They participate in the decision-making process. = 他们参与决策过程。

一般过去时:
He participated in a marathon last year. = 他去年参加了一场马拉松比赛。
We participated in a fundraising event for charity. = 我们参加了一个慈善募捐活动。

现在完成时:
She has participated in several international conferences. = 她已经参加了几次国际会议。
They have participated in the project since its inception. = 自从项目开始以来，他们就参与其中了。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, he had participated in several similar discussions. = 会议之前，他已经参加过几次类似的讨论。
By the time the competition ended, she had participated in every round. = 到比赛结束时，她已经参加了每一轮比赛。

将来时:
I will participate in the upcoming conference. = 我将参加即将举行的会议。
They will participate in the experiment next week. = 他们下周将参与实验。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would participate in the volunteer program. = 如果我有时间，我会参加志愿者项目。
I wish I could participate in the debate. = 我真希望我能参加辩论。

被动语态:
Many volunteers were participated in the relief efforts. = 许多志愿者参与了救灾工作。(注意：participate本身不常用被动语态)
The students were invited to participate in the research. = 学生们被邀请参与研究。(此处是被动语态，强调邀请)


疑问句:
Will you participate in the project? = 你会参与这个项目吗？
Did he participate in the meeting? = 他参加会议了吗？

否定句:
I did not participate in the argument. = 我没有参与这场争论。
She will not participate in the competition. = 她不会参加比赛。


PHRASE->
participate in = 参与…
actively participate = 积极参与
fully participate = 全力参与
meaningfully participate = 有意义地参与
enthusiastically participate = 热情地参与
