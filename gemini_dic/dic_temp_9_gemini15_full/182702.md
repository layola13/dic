repacking:/ˌriːˈpækɪŋ/| n.|重新包装，再包装|The act of packing something again, often after it has been unpacked or partially used.  It often involves reorganizing or rearranging items within a container.|例句：The repacking of the goods took several hours.  重新包装货物花了几个小时。|例句：Repacking the suitcase after the trip was a tedious task. 旅行后重新打包行李是一项繁琐的任务。|近义词：repackaging, repackaging, re-packing|反义词：unpacking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repacks his suitcase every morning. 他每天早上都重新打包行李箱。
They repack the boxes for shipment. 他们重新包装箱子以便运输。


一般过去时:
She repacked the gifts before sending them. 她寄送礼物之前重新包装了一下。
We repacked the boxes after the inspection. 检查之后我们重新包装了箱子。


现在完成时:
