nonhospitalized:/ˌnɒnˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzd/ | adj. |未住院的，非住院的 | Not hospitalized; not receiving treatment in a hospital.  | Example: The study included both hospitalized and nonhospitalized patients. 这项研究包括住院和未住院的患者。 |近义词：outpatient, untreated (in this context) | 反义词：hospitalized, inpatient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The patient is nonhospitalized and recovering at home. = 病人未住院，在家休养。
He remains nonhospitalized despite his recent illness. = 尽管他最近生病了，但他仍然没有住院。


一般过去时:
She was nonhospitalized throughout her illness. = 她在整个生病期间都没有住院。
The child was nonhospitalized after the accident. = 事故后，孩子没有住院。


现在完成时:
He has remained nonhospitalized since his surgery. = 他手术后一直没有住院。
She has been nonhospitalized for the duration of her treatment. = 在她的治疗期间，她一直没有住院。


过去完成时:
They had been nonhospitalized prior to the worsening of their condition. = 在病情恶化之前，他们一直没有住院。
The patient had remained nonhospitalized until the last stages of the disease. = 直到疾病的最后阶段，病人一直没有住院。


将来时:
The doctor expects him to remain nonhospitalized. = 医生预计他不会住院。
She will likely be nonhospitalized after the procedure. = 她手术后很可能不会住院。


虚拟语气:
If he were nonhospitalized, he would be able to recover more quickly. = 如果他没有住院，他就能更快康复。
I wish she were nonhospitalized and could be at home. = 我希望她没有住院，可以待在家里。


被动语态:
It was determined that the patient should be kept nonhospitalized. = 确定病人应该不住院。
The decision was made to keep him nonhospitalized. = 决定让他不住院。


疑问句:
Is the patient currently nonhospitalized? = 病人目前没有住院吗？
Was she nonhospitalized during her recovery? = 她康复期间没有住院吗？


否定句:
The patient is not nonhospitalized; he is currently in the hospital. = 病人没有不住院；他目前正在医院。
She was not nonhospitalized; she required immediate hospitalization. = 她没有不住院；她需要立即住院。
