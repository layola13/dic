resignedness:/rɪˈzaɪndnəs/| n. |顺从；听天由命；认命；甘心 |The state of having accepted something unpleasant that one cannot change.  It implies a quiet acceptance, often with a sense of weariness or defeat. |例句：There was a quiet resignedness in her voice as she spoke about her loss.  她说起她的损失时，声音里带着一种平静的认命。

例句：After many failed attempts, he accepted defeat with a weary resignedness.  经过多次失败的尝试后，他带着疲惫的顺从接受了失败。


近义词：acceptance, submission, acquiescence, passivity

反义词：resistance, defiance, rebellion, protest

词性:名词
