mercaptal:/mərˈkæptəl/| n. |缩硫醛|A mercaptal is an organic compound that contains two sulfur atoms bonded to a single carbon atom, which is also bonded to two other carbon atoms.  It is a sulfur analog of an acetal. Mercaptals are often formed by the reaction of a thioketone with a thiol. |例句：The synthesis of the mercaptal involved the reaction of a thioketone with a thiol.  缩硫醛的合成涉及硫酮与硫醇的反应。|近义词：thioacetal|反义词：N/A
