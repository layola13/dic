guy's:/ɡaɪz/| n.|家伙们；伙计们|The plural possessive form of "guy,"  referring to a group of men or people.  It's informal and often used in spoken English. |例句：That's the guy's car.  那是那家伙的车。
例句：The guys' project is almost finished.  伙计们的项目快完成了。|近义词：fellows, chaps, blokes|反义词: 无
