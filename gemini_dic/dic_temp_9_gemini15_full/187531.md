romance:/rəˈmæns/| n. v. adj.|浪漫；恋爱；传奇故事；虚构；渲染；使浪漫化|

英文解析:
As a noun, "romance" refers to a feeling of excitement and mystery associated with love; a love affair, especially one that is exciting or adventurous;  a long story, especially one set in the past, about exciting events and adventures, often involving love;  a style of literature or art that is characterized by idealized love and adventure. As a verb, it means to have a romantic relationship with someone; to write or tell romantic stories. As an adjective, it means relating to or characteristic of romance; exciting and mysterious.


例句:
Their romance blossomed quickly. = 他们的爱情迅速发展。

Their holiday was full of romance. = 他们的假期充满了浪漫。


近义词:
love affair, love story, adventure, fiction, idealization


反义词:
reality, truth, practicality


词性:
noun, verb, adjective
