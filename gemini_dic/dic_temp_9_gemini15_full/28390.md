carbolic:/kɑːrˈbɒlɪk/|/kɑːrˈbɑːlɪk/| adj. |石炭酸的；苯酚的 | Relating to or denoting carbolic acid (phenol).  Carbolic is used to describe substances containing or derived from phenol, a powerful antiseptic. |The carbolic smell was strong in the operating theatre.  手术室里弥漫着浓烈的石炭酸味。|  phenol, antiseptic |  (无明确反义词，可考虑non-carbolic, but this is rarely used)  |形容词
