pointing:/ˈpɔɪntɪŋ/|/ˈpɔɪntiŋ/| v.|指向，指着；表明；(建筑)砌石灰浆|The act of directing someone's attention to something by extending a finger or other object towards it;  indicating something.  Also refers to the application of mortar in bricklaying.|例句：He pointed to the building across the street. 他指着街对面的建筑物。

例句：The arrow pointing to the north indicates the direction. 指向北方的箭头表明方向。


近义词：indicating, directing, showing, gesturing|反义词：ignoring, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He points at the map. 他指着地图。
She points out the mistakes in my essay. 她指出我论文中的错误。


一般过去时:
He pointed at the thief. 他指着小偷。
She pointed out the flaws in the plan. 她指出了计划中的缺陷。


现在完成时:
He has pointed out the problem many times. 他已经多次指出了这个问题。
She has pointed the way to success. 她已经指明了成功的道路。


过去完成时:
He had pointed out the danger before the accident. 事故发生前他已经指出了危险。
She had pointed to the solution earlier. 她之前已经指出了解决方案。


将来时:
He will point out the error in the report. 他将指出报告中的错误。
She will point the way for the newcomers. 她将为新来者指明方向。


虚拟语气:
If he pointed out the problem earlier, we could have avoided it. 如果他早点指出问题，我们就能避免它了。
I wish she had pointed out the mistake before I submitted it. 我希望她在提交之前指出错误。


被动语态:
The problem was pointed out by the manager. 这个问题是由经理指出的。
The way was pointed to us by a kind stranger. 一个好心陌生人为我们指明了道路。


疑问句:
What are you pointing at? 你指着什么？
Where is she pointing? 她指着哪里？


否定句:
He didn't point out the mistake. 他没有指出错误。
She didn't point the way. 她没有指路。


PHRASE->
point to: 指向…
point out: 指出
point the way: 指明方向
make a point: 提出论点
to the point: 切题的
