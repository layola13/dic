offered:/ˈɔːfəd/ | v. | 提供；出价；建议；献祭 | to present or make available (something) to someone; to state a price for something; to suggest something as a possibility or course of action; to sacrifice something to a deity. |例句：The company offered me a job.  公司给我提供了一份工作。

The shop offered a wide selection of goods. 这家商店提供各种各样的商品。


近义词：provided, presented, suggested, proposed | 反义词：demanded, refused, withheld

词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He offers his help to everyone. 他向每个人提供帮助。
She offers a variety of services. 她提供各种各样的服务。

一般过去时:
They offered me a drink. 他们给我提供了一杯饮料。
He offered a solution to the problem. 他提出了解决这个问题的方法。

现在完成时:
She has offered her resignation. 她已经提出辞职了。
They have offered several suggestions. 他们已经提出了几个建议。

过去完成时:
He had offered to help before I asked.  在我问之前他已经主动提出帮忙了。
They had offered a good price, but we refused. 他们开出了一个好价钱，但是我们拒绝了。

将来时:
She will offer a prayer. 她将祈祷。
They will offer a reward for information.  他们将悬赏提供信息。


被动语态:
The job was offered to me. 这份工作提供给了我。
A reward was offered for the lost dog.  悬赏寻找丢失的狗。


疑问句:
What did they offer you? 他们提供给你什么了？
Will they offer any discounts? 他们会提供任何折扣吗？


否定句:
They didn't offer any explanation. 他们没有提供任何解释。
She offered no resistance. 她没有反抗。


PHRASE->
offer a price 出价
offer a reward 提供奖励
offer an apology 道歉
offer assistance 提供帮助
offer condolences 表示慰问
