hayrack:/ˈheɪræk/| n. |干草架；饲草架 | A rack or framework for storing hay.  Hayracks are often used in barns or stables to feed livestock. | Example: The farmer filled the hayrack with freshly cut alfalfa.  农夫用新割的紫花苜蓿填满了干草架。  |近义词：hay feeder, fodder rack | 反义词：无
