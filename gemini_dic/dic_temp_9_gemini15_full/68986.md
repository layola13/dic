fatiguing:/fəˈtiːɡɪŋ/ | adj. |令人疲惫的，使人疲劳的 | causing a feeling of tiredness; exhausting.  |The long journey was extremely fatiguing. 长途旅行非常令人疲惫。  |exhausting, tiring, wearying, laborious | invigorating, refreshing, energizing | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This work is fatiguing. = 这项工作令人疲惫。
The long meeting is fatiguing. = 漫长的会议令人疲惫。


一般过去时:
The hike was fatiguing yesterday. = 昨天的徒步旅行很累人。
The exam was very fatiguing. = 考试非常令人疲惫。


现在完成时:
The trip has been fatiguing. = 这趟旅程令人筋疲力尽。
This week has been particularly fatiguing. = 这周特别累人。


过去完成时:
I had been fatiguing myself all day. = 我一整天都把自己累坏了。
She had been fatiguing for weeks before the break. = 在休息之前，她已经疲惫了好几个星期了。


将来时:
The climb will be fatiguing. = 这段攀登将会很累人。
The marathon will be extremely fatiguing. = 马拉松比赛将非常令人疲惫。


虚拟语气:
If the work were less fatiguing, I would enjoy it more. = 如果工作不那么累人，我会更享受它。
I wish the journey hadn't been so fatiguing. = 我希望旅途没有那么累人。


被动语态:
He was fatigued by the long day.  = 他被一整天累坏了。(注意：此句是被动语态，但主语是人，形容词是"fatigued"，而非"fatiguing")
She felt fatigued after the strenuous workout. = 剧烈运动后她感到疲惫。(注意：此句是被动语态，但主语是人，形容词是"fatigued"，而非"fatiguing")


疑问句:
Is this task too fatiguing? = 这项任务太累人了吗？
Do you find this job fatiguing? = 你觉得这份工作累人吗？


否定句:
The work wasn't fatiguing at all. = 这项工作一点也不累人。
The journey wasn't as fatiguing as I expected. = 旅途并没有我想象的那么累人。


PHRASE->
a fatiguing journey  = 一段令人疲惫的旅程
a fatiguing day = 令人疲惫的一天
a fatiguing experience = 一次令人疲惫的经历
a fatiguing task = 一项令人疲惫的任务
fatiguing work = 令人疲惫的工作


词源:
From the verb "fatigue", ultimately from Latin "fatigare" meaning "to tire, weary".
