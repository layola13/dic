depersonalises:/ˌdiːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪz/ | v. | 使非个性化；使去个性化；使客观化 | To remove the personal element from something; to make something impersonal or objective.  | The bureaucratic system depersonalises the interaction between citizens and the government.  官僚制度使公民与政府之间的互动变得非人性化。 The repetitive nature of factory work depersonalises the employees. 工厂工作的重复性使员工感到缺乏个性。 | impersonalize, objectify | personalize, humanize | 动词
