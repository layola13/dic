hero-worships:/ˈhɪərəʊ wɜːʃɪps/ | v. | 英雄崇拜；盲目崇拜 | To admire and respect someone greatly, often excessively.  This often involves imitating their actions and beliefs.  |  例句：Many young people hero-worship pop stars. 许多年轻人盲目崇拜流行歌星。

例句：He hero-worships his uncle, a successful businessman. 他盲目崇拜他叔叔，一位成功的商人。


近义词：idolize, idolise, venerate, revere


反义词：despise, scorn, disdain


词性：动词
