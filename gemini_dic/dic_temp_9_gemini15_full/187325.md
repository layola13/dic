rockets:/ˈrɒkɪts/ /ˈrɑːkɪts/| n.|火箭，飞速发展|A rocket is a vehicle, projectile, or spacecraft propelled by a rocket engine. Rockets are used for various purposes, from fireworks to space exploration.|例句：The rocket soared into the sky. 这枚火箭飞上了天空。The company's profits are rocketing. 这家公司的利润正在飞速增长。|近义词：missile, projectile|反义词：None
