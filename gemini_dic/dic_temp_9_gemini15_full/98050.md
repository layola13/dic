inpatient:/ˈɪnˌpeɪʃənt/| n. |住院病人| A patient who is admitted to a hospital for treatment or care.  Inpatients receive 24-hour medical care and supervision. | Example: The hospital has a dedicated ward for inpatient rehabilitation. 这家医院有一个专门的住院康复病房。|近义词：hospitalized patient, resident patient |反义词：outpatient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an inpatient at the local hospital. = 他是当地医院的一名住院病人。
The inpatient receives three meals a day. = 住院病人一天吃三餐。


一般过去时:
She was an inpatient for two weeks after her surgery. = 她手术后住了两周院。
The inpatient recovered quickly from his illness. =这位住院病人很快从疾病中恢复过来。


现在完成时:
He has been an inpatient since last month. = 他从上个月起就一直住院。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
