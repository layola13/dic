monolithic:/ˌmɒnəˈlɪθɪk/| /ˌmɑːnəˈlɪθɪk/ | adj. |整体的；庞大的；单片的；单一构成的 |consisting of a single, large piece of stone.  Figuratively, it describes something large, unified, and indivisible.  It can also imply a lack of diversity or flexibility. |例句：The ancient temple was carved from a single, monolithic block of granite.  古老的寺庙是由一块巨大的整体花岗岩雕刻而成的。

The company's monolithic structure hindered innovation.  公司的单一结构阻碍了创新。

|近义词：unified, integral, undifferentiated, massive |反义词：fragmented, diverse, decentralized, disparate |
