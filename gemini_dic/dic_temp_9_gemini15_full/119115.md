metropole:/ˈmɛtrəˌpoʊl/| n. |大都市；中心城市；宗主国|A large or important city, especially one that is the center of a particular region or country.  It can also refer to a mother country in relation to its colonies. |例句：Paris is a great metropole of fashion and culture. 巴黎是时尚和文化的中心都市。|例句：London was once a powerful metropole, ruling over a vast empire. 伦敦曾经是一个强大的宗主国，统治着一个巨大的帝国。|近义词：capital, metropolis, center |反义词：province, countryside, colony
