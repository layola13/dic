rampe:/ræmp/| n.|斜坡，坡道|A ramp is an inclined plane, a sloping surface connecting two different levels.  It is used for facilitating the movement of people or things between levels, often as an alternative to stairs.|例句：The wheelchair ramp made the building accessible to everyone.  轮椅坡道使建筑物对每个人都可进入。The loading ramp was used to unload the trucks. 装卸坡道用于卸货。|近义词：slope, incline, gradient|反义词：stairs, steps|名词
