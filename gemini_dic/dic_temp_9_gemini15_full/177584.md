rebroadening:/ˌriːˈbrɔːdnɪŋ/ | v. | 重新拓宽；再次扩展；重新广播 | The act of broadening something again;  the act of broadcasting something again.  It often implies a previous broadening or broadcasting event.  | Example: The company is rebroadening its marketing campaign to reach a wider audience. 公司正在重新拓宽其营销活动以覆盖更广泛的受众。  |近义词: re-expanding, extending again, retransmitting | 反义词: narrowing, restricting, silencing |
