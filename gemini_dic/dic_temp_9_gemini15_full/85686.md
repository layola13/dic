haveless:/ˈhævləs/| adj.|缺乏的，没有的，无...的|Lacking something specified; without.  The word is often used to describe a lack of essential resources or possessions.|例句：He felt haveless and alone in the big city. 他在大城市里感到孤单无助。

例句：The haveless children lacked proper nutrition and healthcare. 这些贫困的孩子缺乏足够的营养和医疗保健。


近义词：needy, deprived, destitute, impoverished|反义词：affluent, wealthy, well-off, prosperous|词性：形容词
