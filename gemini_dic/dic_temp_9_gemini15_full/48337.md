decriminalized:/ˌdiːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzd/ | adj. v. | 使非刑事犯罪化；使合法化 | To remove the criminal status of (an act or offense).  The act of making something that was previously a crime no longer a crime. | 例句：Many countries have decriminalized the possession of small amounts of marijuana. 许多国家已经将持有少量大麻的行为非刑事犯罪化了。


The law was decriminalized after years of public pressure. 在多年公众压力之后，该法律被非刑事犯罪化了。


近义词：legalize, legitimize | 反义词：criminalize


词性：动词 (过去分词形式)
