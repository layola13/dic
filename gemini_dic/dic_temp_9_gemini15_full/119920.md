milker:/ˈmɪlkər/ | n. |挤奶工，挤奶器 | A person or machine that milks cows or other animals.  |例句：The farmer is a skilled milker.  这位农民是一位熟练的挤奶工。

例句：The new automatic milker increased the farm's efficiency. 新的自动挤奶器提高了农场的效率。

近义词：dairy worker (针对人), milking machine (针对机器) | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He milks the cows every morning. = 他每天早上都挤牛奶。
She milks goats for their cheese. = 她挤山羊奶来做奶酪。

一般过去时:
The farmer milked the cows before sunset. = 农民在日落前挤了牛奶。
They milked the sheep to get wool. = 他们挤羊奶来获取羊毛。（虽然不常见，但可以说明过去式用法）


现在完成时:
The farmer has milked all the cows. = 农民已经把所有的奶牛都挤完了奶。
He has milked the cow three times today. = 他今天已经给那头奶牛挤了三次奶。


过去完成时:
They had milked the cows before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经挤完了牛奶。
She had milked the goat twice before I arrived. = 我到达之前，她已经给那只山羊挤了两次奶。


将来时:
I will milk the cows tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上将要挤牛奶。
They will milk the goats after lunch. = 他们午饭后将要挤山羊奶。


虚拟语气:
If I were a farmer, I would milk cows. = 如果我是一个农民，我会挤牛奶。
I wish I could milk a cow without making a mess. = 我希望我能不弄脏的情况下挤牛奶。


被动语态:
The cows are milked twice a day. = 奶牛一天挤两次奶。
The goats were milked yesterday afternoon. = 山羊昨天下午被挤了奶。


疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "milker" is derived from the verb "milk," which has Old English roots ("meoluc"). The suffix "-er" is an agent noun suffix, indicating a person or thing that performs the action.  Therefore, "milker" literally means "one who milks."
