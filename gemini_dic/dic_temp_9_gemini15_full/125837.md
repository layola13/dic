murrhine:/ˈmɜːraɪn/| n. adj.| 琉璃的；似琉璃的 |  Relating to or denoting a type of ancient Roman glass or stone, typically translucent and variegated in color, prized for its beauty and often thought to be a type of agate. The exact nature of murrhine remains debated by scholars.|The museum's collection boasts a magnificent example of murrhine glassware.|近义词：glassware, translucent, variegated|反义词：opaque, dull
