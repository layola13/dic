flibbertigibbet: /ˌflɪbərˈtɪdʒɪbɪt/ | n. |轻浮的人；喋喋不休的人；轻率的人 | A flighty, frivolous, or gossipy person.  Someone who talks excessively and often nonsensically. |例句：He's such a flibbertigibbet, always chattering away about nothing. 他真是个轻浮的人，总是喋喋不休地说着一些无关紧要的事情。

例句：Stop being such a flibbertigibbet and focus on your work! 别这么轻浮了，集中精力工作！


近义词：busybody, chatterbox, gossip, scatterbrain, gadfly


反义词：serious person, quiet person, reserved person


词性：名词


词源:  The word's origin is uncertain, but it's believed to be a whimsical coinage, possibly combining elements suggesting flighty or frivolous behavior.  There's no clear etymological root tracing it to older words.  Its playful, nonsensical sound contributes to its meaning.
