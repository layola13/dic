remodernization:/ˌriːˌmɒdərnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 再现代化；重新现代化 | The process of modernizing something that has already been modernized, often to incorporate newer technologies or designs, or to address shortcomings of a previous modernization effort.  It implies a second or subsequent wave of modernization. | Example: The remodernization of the power grid involved upgrading outdated infrastructure and implementing smart grid technologies.  再现代化电网包括升级过时的基础设施和实施智能电网技术。|近义词：renovation, refurbishment, upgrading | 反义词：demodernization, decay, obsolescence |  名词
