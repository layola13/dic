masque:/mɑːsk/| n.|假面；面具；化妆舞会|A mask worn at a ball or entertainment; a dramatic entertainment, especially one of the 16th and 17th centuries, given by amateurs, with music, dancing, and often elaborate scenery and costumes.|例句：The actors wore elaborate masques during the performance.  演员们在表演中戴着精心制作的面具。|例句：The Renaissance masque was a popular form of entertainment. 文艺复兴时期的假面舞会是一种流行的娱乐形式。|近义词：mask, pageant, masquerade|反义词：none|名词
