preobligations:/ˌpriːˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃənz/| n. |预先义务，先期责任|Prior obligations are duties or responsibilities that exist before a particular event or agreement.  They are commitments or requirements that must be fulfilled before other actions can be taken.  These obligations can be legal, contractual, or moral in nature. |Example:  He couldn't attend the meeting due to prior preobligations. 他因为之前的预先义务而无法参加会议。|近义词：prior commitments, prior responsibilities, antecedent duties |反义词：postobligations (not a common word)
