pantheologist:/ˌpænθiˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |泛神论者|A pantheologist is a scholar or expert in pantheism, the philosophical concept that God is everything and everywhere, and that the universe itself is divine.  They study the various aspects of pantheistic thought, its history, its different interpretations, and its implications for theology, philosophy, and religion. |Example: The renowned pantheologist delivered a compelling lecture on the historical development of pantheistic ideas.  著名的泛神论学者就泛神论思想的历史发展作了一个引人入胜的讲座。|近义词：pantheist (泛神论者), theologian (神学家)|反义词：theist (有神论者), atheist (无神论者)
