doubles:/ˈdʌbəlz/| n. v.|双打；双倍；两倍；成双|英文解析：The word "doubles" can function as both a noun and a verb. As a noun, it refers to something that is double or consists of two parts. As a verb, it means to become double or to multiply by two.|例句：As a noun:  The tennis match was doubles. (网球比赛是双打。)  The recipe calls for double the amount of sugar. (食谱需要双倍的糖量。)|近义词：pairs, twins, duplicates, twofold|反义词：singles, halves
