incontinence:/ɪnˈkɒntɪnəns/| n. |大小便失禁；自制力差|The inability to control the bladder or bowels, resulting in unintentional urination or defecation.  It can be caused by various factors, including neurological conditions, muscle weakness, and certain medications.|例句：The elderly woman suffered from urinary incontinence.  这位老年妇女患有尿失禁。|例句：His emotional incontinence led to many regrettable actions. 他的情绪失控导致许多令人后悔的行为。|近义词：uncontrollability, lack of self-control|反义词：continence, self-control|名词
