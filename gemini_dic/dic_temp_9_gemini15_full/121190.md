misdo:/mɪsˈduː/| vt.|做错，干坏事|To do something incorrectly or wrongly; to commit a misdeed.|例句：He misdid the calculations and got the wrong answer. 他计算错误，得到了错误的答案。|例句：They misdid their duty and were punished. 他们失职受罚。|近义词：err, blunder, botch,  |反义词：do correctly, do well


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misdoes his work regularly. = 他经常工作出错。
They misdo their taxes every year. = 他们每年都报税出错。

一般过去时:
She misdid the recipe and the cake was burnt. = 她做菜谱做错了，蛋糕烤焦了。
He misdid his job and lost his position. = 他工作失职，丢了工作。

现在完成时:
I have misdone many things in my life.  = 我一生中做过许多错事。
They have misdone their responsibilities. = 他们没有尽到责任。

过去完成时:
She realized she had misdone the assignment after submitting it. = 她提交作业后才意识到自己做错了。
He regretted that he had misdone his friend. = 他后悔对不起他的朋友。

将来时:
I fear he will misdo the project. = 我担心他会把项目搞砸。
They will misdo the repairs if they are not careful. = 如果他们不小心，就会把修理工作搞砸。


虚拟语气:
If I had not misdone the instructions, the result would have been better. = 如果我没有误解指示，结果会更好。
I wish I hadn't misdone the cooking. = 我真希望我没有把菜做坏。


被动语态:
The task was misdone due to lack of experience. = 由于缺乏经验，这项任务做错了。
The instructions were misdone by the new employee. = 新员工误解了说明。


疑问句:
Did he misdo anything during the meeting? = 他在会议上做错什么了吗？
Has she ever misdone her job before? = 她以前有没有做错过工作？


否定句:
He didn't misdo the experiment. = 他没有做错实验。
They did not misdo the instructions given to them. = 他们没有做错给他们的指示。


PHRASE->
misdo oneself = 虐待自己
misdo one's duty = 玩忽职守
misdo one's part = 没有尽到自己的责任
misdo one's best = 尽力而为(虽然结果可能不好)
misdo something terribly = 把事情搞砸了
