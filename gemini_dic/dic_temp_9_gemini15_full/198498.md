shrined:/ʃraɪnd/| vt.|奉为神圣；珍藏；供奉|To regard or treat (someone or something) as sacred or holy; to enshrine.|例句：His achievements are enshrined in the national history.  他的成就被载入史册。

例句：The memory of their love is enshrined in this song. 他们的爱情记忆被铭刻在这首歌里。


近义词：treasure, revere, enshrine, venerate|反义词：desecrate, profane, dishonor|动词
