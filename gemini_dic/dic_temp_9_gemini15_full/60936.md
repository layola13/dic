elevates:/ˈɛləveɪts/| vt.|提升；举起；提高；使兴奋|to raise something to a higher position; to improve or increase something; to make someone feel happy and excited |例句：The new bridge elevates the city's skyline.  这座新桥提升了城市的天际线。

例句：The good news elevated her spirits.  好消息使她精神振奋。


近义词：raise, lift, hoist, uplift, enhance, improve, exalt

反义词：lower, depress, degrade, diminish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The crane elevates heavy objects. = 起重机提升重物。
The music elevates my mood. = 音乐提升我的心情。

一般过去时:
He elevated the flag to the top of the pole. = 他把旗子升到旗杆顶端。
The victory elevated the team's morale. = 胜利提升了球队的士气。

现在完成时:
The new policy has elevated the company's profile. = 新政策提升了公司的形象。
She has elevated herself to a position of leadership. = 她提升到了领导地位。

过去完成时:
They had elevated the bridge before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经建好了桥。
The artist had elevated his technique through years of practice. = 通过多年的练习，这位艺术家提升了他的技艺。

将来时:
They will elevate the statue to a prominent position. = 他们将把雕像提升到显眼的位置。
The new technology will elevate the standard of living. = 新技术将提高生活水平。

虚拟语气:
If they elevated the prices, they would make more profit. = 如果他们提高价格，他们就能赚取更多利润。
I wish they would elevate the discussion to a more professional level. = 我希望他们能把讨论提升到更专业的层面。

被动语态:

Her status was elevated after she received the award. = 她获奖后，她的地位得到了提升。

疑问句:

What factors elevate the risk of heart disease? = 哪些因素会增加患心脏病的风险？

否定句:
The company did not elevate his position. = 公司没有提升他的职位。
The drug does not elevate mood in all patients. = 这种药物并非对所有患者都有提神作用。
