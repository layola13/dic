phlebotomize:/ˌflɛboʊˈtɑːmaɪz/| vt.|放血；抽血|To perform phlebotomy; to draw blood from a vein.|例句：The nurse had to phlebotomize the patient for a blood test. 护士必须为病人抽血进行血液测试。
The doctor decided to phlebotomize the patient to relieve pressure. 医生决定为病人放血以减轻压力。|近义词：bleed, draw blood|反义词：infuse(输液), transfuse(输血)|动词

词源:
