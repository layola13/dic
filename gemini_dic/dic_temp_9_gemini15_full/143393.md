outfished:/ˌaʊtˈfɪʃt/| vt. |超过…的捕捞量；比…捕捞得多|To catch more fish than (someone or something).  This word implies a competition or comparison in fishing yield.|例句：The small fishing boats were outfished by the larger trawlers. 小渔船的捕捞量被大型拖网渔船超过了。
例句：Last year, the local fishermen were outfished by the visiting fleet. 去年，当地渔民的捕捞量被来访的船队超过了。|近义词：outsurpass, outstrip, exceed|反义词：underfished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The large fishing company outfishes the smaller ones. = 大型渔业公司比小型渔业公司的捕捞量多。
They outfish their rivals every year. = 他们每年都比竞争对手捕捞更多。


一般过去时:
