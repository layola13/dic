gangsters:/ˈɡæŋstərz/| n. |匪徒，歹徒，黑帮成员 |Members of a gang, especially one involved in organized crime.  Gangsters often operate outside the law, using violence and intimidation to achieve their goals. They are typically involved in illegal activities such as drug trafficking, extortion, and gambling.|例句：The gangsters were arrested after a high-speed chase.  黑帮分子在高速追逐后被捕。

例句：The police are investigating the activities of a local gang of gangsters. 警方正在调查当地一个黑帮团伙的活动。


近义词：thugs, criminals, mobsters, racketeers |反义词：law-abiding citizens, peacekeepers
