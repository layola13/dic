manservants:/ˈmænˌsɜːrvənts/ | n. |男仆；男佣人 | Male servants, typically in a household.  The term often implies a degree of personal service beyond that of a general servant. | The wealthy family employed several manservants to tend to their needs.  这个富裕的家庭雇佣了几个男仆来满足他们的需求。 | servants, valets, footmen | mistresses, maids | 名词
