reinductions:/ˌriːɪnˈdʌkʃənz/| n.|重新介绍；再次入职|The process of reintroducing someone to a job, role, or organization, often after a period of absence or change.  It might involve refresher training or updates on new procedures.|例句：The company offered reinductions to employees returning from parental leave. 公司为休完产假的员工提供重新入职培训。|例句：The reinductions covered new software and safety protocols.  重新入职培训涵盖了新的软件和安全规程。|近义词：reorientation, retraining, onboarding (in some contexts)|反义词：dismissal, termination|名词
