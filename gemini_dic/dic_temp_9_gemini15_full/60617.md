elderliness:/ˈɛldərlinɛs/| n.|老年；衰老|The state or quality of being elderly; old age.  It often implies the physical and mental decline associated with aging.|例句：His elderliness prevented him from participating in the marathon. 他的老年阻止了他参加马拉松比赛。

例句：  The increasing elderliness in the population is putting a strain on healthcare resources. 人口老龄化日益严重，给医疗资源带来了压力。


近义词：old age, senility, senescence

反义词：youth, youthfulness
