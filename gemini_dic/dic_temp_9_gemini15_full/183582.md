requirement: /rɪˈkwaɪərmənt/ | n. | 要求；必要条件；规定 |  A thing that is needed or wanted.  A condition or feature that must be present for something to happen or exist. |  例句：The job requirement includes three years of experience. 这份工作的要求包括三年的工作经验。

例句：Meeting the customer's requirements is our top priority. 满足客户的需求是我们的首要任务。


近义词：need, demand, stipulation, prerequisite | 反义词：option, choice

词源:  From Middle English *requirement, from Old French *requerement, from requerir "to require," from re- "again" + querre "to seek, ask".  The noun sense developed from the verb's meaning.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project requires careful planning. = 这个项目需要仔细的计划。
This position requires excellent communication skills. = 这个职位需要优秀的沟通能力。


一般过去时:
The application required a passport photo. = 申请需要一张护照照片。
The job required long hours and hard work. = 这份工作需要长时间工作和努力工作。


现在完成时:
The project has required more resources than anticipated. = 这个项目需要的资源比预期的要多。
She has met all the requirements for graduation. = 她已经满足了毕业的所有要求。


过去完成时:
They had already met the requirements before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前就已经满足了要求。
The company had required extensive testing before launching the product. = 公司在发布产品之前已经进行了广泛的测试。


将来时:
The new regulations will require businesses to adapt quickly. = 新的规章制度将要求企业迅速适应。
The next phase of the project will require additional funding. = 项目的下一阶段将需要额外的资金。


虚拟语气:
If the project required more time, we would ask for an extension. = 如果项目需要更多时间，我们会申请延期。
I wish the job requirements were less demanding. = 我希望工作要求没那么苛刻。


被动语态:
All necessary documents are required for the application process. = 申请过程中需要所有必要的证件。
The changes were required by the government. = 这些变化是政府要求的。


疑问句:
What are the requirements for this course? = 这门课程的要求是什么？
Are there any specific requirements for the job? = 这份工作有什么具体要求吗？


否定句:
The project does not require any additional funding. = 项目不需要任何额外的资金。
The software doesn't require installation. = 该软件不需要安装。
