crower:/ˈkraʊər/| n.|啼叫的动物；报晓的雄鸡|A crower is an animal that crows, especially a rooster.|例句：The crower woke me up at dawn.  报晓的雄鸡在黎明时把我吵醒了。|The early crower caught the worm.  捷足先登。|近义词：rooster, cock|反义词：None specifically.
