reaps:/riːps/| vt.| 收割；获得；收获 | To gather a crop from; harvest.  To obtain or receive (something positive) as a result of one's efforts. |例句：The farmer reaps his wheat in the autumn.  农民在秋天收割小麦。

The company reaped the rewards of its hard work. 这家公司获得了努力工作的回报。|近义词：gather, harvest, glean, collect |反义词：sow, plant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reaps the benefits of his hard work. 他收获了他努力工作的成果。
Farmers reap the rice in the fall. 农民在秋天收割稻米。


一般过去时:
They reaped a bountiful harvest last year. 他们去年获得了丰收。
She reaped the consequences of her actions. 她承担了她行为的后果。


现在完成时:
He has reaped the rewards of his investment. 他已经收获了他投资的回报。
We have reaped what we have sown. 我们已经收获了我们所播种的。


过去完成时:
They had reaped all the wheat before the storm. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经收割了所有的小麦。
She had reaped the benefits of her education. 她已经获得了教育带来的好处。


将来时:
We will reap the benefits of this project next year. 我们明年将受益于这个项目。
They will reap what they have sown. 他们将收获他们所播种的。


虚拟语气:
If he had worked harder, he would have reaped greater rewards. 如果他工作更努力，他就会获得更大的回报。
I wish I had reaped the benefits of that opportunity. 我真希望我抓住了那个机会带来的好处。


被动语态:
The harvest was reaped by the farmers. 收成由农民收割。
The benefits were reaped by the company. 收益由公司获得。


疑问句:

否定句:
