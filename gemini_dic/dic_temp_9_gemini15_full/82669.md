guessingly:/ˈɡɛsɪŋli/| adv. |猜测地，推测地 |In a way that shows or suggests guessing; uncertainly.  It implies a lack of certainty or definite knowledge. |例句：He answered guessingly, unsure of the correct response. 他不确定正确的答案，所以猜测着回答。

例句：She spoke guessingly about the future of the company. 她对公司的未来进行了猜测性的谈论。


近义词：speculatively, conjecturally, tentatively, uncertainly |反义词：certainly, definitely, assuredly, confidently |词性：副词
