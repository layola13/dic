raison:/rɛɪˈzɔːn/|/rɛɪˈzɒn/| n. |理由，原因；理性；道理|Reason is a cause, explanation, or justification for an action or event. It can also refer to the power of reason, or sound judgment.  |例句：The reason for his absence was illness. 他缺席的原因是生病了。|例句：There is no good reason to delay the project. 没有充分理由推迟这个项目。|近义词：reason, cause, motive, justification |反义词：irrationality, absurdity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is the reason for your decision? = 你做决定的理由是什么？
Why do you think this is the best solution? = 你为什么认为这是最佳方案？


一般过去时:
What was the reason for the accident? = 事故的原因是什么？
Why did she quit her job? = 她为什么辞职？


现在完成时:
What reasons have you found to support your claim? = 你找到了哪些理由来支持你的说法？
Why have you changed your mind? = 你为什么改变主意了？


过去完成时:
What reasons had they given before the meeting? = 会议前他们给出了哪些理由？
Why had he already left when we arrived? = 我们到达时他为什么已经离开了？


将来时:
What will be the reason for the delay? = 延误的原因将会是什么？
Why will they choose this option? = 他们为什么选择这个选项？


虚拟语气:
If I knew the reason, I would tell you. = 如果我知道原因，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew why he did that. = 我真希望知道他为什么那样做。


被动语态:
The reason for the failure was not explained. = 失败的原因没有被解释。
Why was the meeting cancelled? = 为什么会议被取消了？


疑问句:
What is the reason? = 原因是什么？
Why is this happening? = 为什么会发生这种情况？


否定句:
There is no reason to worry. = 没有理由担心。
I don't know why he's angry. = 我不知道他为什么生气。


PHRASE->
by reason of = 由于…的原因
for the reason that = 因为…
for what reason = 为什么
with reason = 有理由地
beyond reason = 无法解释
