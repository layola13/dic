perjured:/ˈpɜːdʒərd/| adj.|伪证的；作伪证的|Having committed perjury; false; untrue.  It refers to testimony given under oath that is known to be false.|The witness perjured himself on the stand, leading to the collapse of the prosecution's case.  证人在证人席上作伪证，导致检方案件崩溃。|false, untrue, deceitful|truthful, honest, veracious|形容词
