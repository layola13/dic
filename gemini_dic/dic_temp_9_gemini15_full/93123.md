ifications:/ˌɪdɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.| 鉴定；识别；确认；说明 |The process of identifying or establishing something's identity or nature.  It often involves detailed examination and analysis to verify or clarify information. |例句：The police conducted thorough identifications of the suspects.  警方对嫌疑人进行了彻底的辨认。|例句：The identifications of the plants were made by an expert botanist. 植物的鉴定是由一位专业的植物学家完成的。|近义词：identifications, verifications, confirmations |反义词：misidentification, obfuscation |名词
