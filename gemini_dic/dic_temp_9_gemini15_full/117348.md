mediaevalist:/ˌmiːdiˈiːvəlɪst/| n. |中世纪学者，中世纪史学家|A mediaevalist is a scholar who studies the Middle Ages.  They specialize in the history, literature, art, and culture of the period roughly spanning from the 5th to the 15th centuries. |Example:  Professor Davies is a renowned mediaevalist, specializing in the Arthurian legends.  戴维斯教授是一位著名的中世纪学者，专门研究亚瑟王传说。

The leading mediaevalist presented her findings at the conference.  主要的中古史学家在会议上介绍了她的研究成果。|近义词：medieval historian, medieval scholar|反义词：N/A
