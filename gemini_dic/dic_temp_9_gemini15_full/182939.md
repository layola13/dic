repick:/riːˈpɪk/ | vt. |重新挑选，再挑选 |To pick again; to select again.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered informal or a nonce word (a word created for a specific occasion).  It's essentially a combination of "re-" (again) and "pick" (to select).|例句：He repicked the apples, discarding the bruised ones. 他重新挑选苹果，扔掉了那些烂的。


例句：After the initial selection, the team leader decided to repick some of the candidates. 初选之后，团队领导决定重新挑选一些候选人。


近义词：reselect, choose again, select anew


反义词：discard, reject


词性：动词
