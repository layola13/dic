retreat:/rɪˈtriːt/| n. vi. |撤退；退却；隐退；静修；休养地 |  A retreat is the action of moving back or withdrawing. It can refer to a military withdrawal, a personal withdrawal from society for reflection or rest, or a place where one can retreat to. |例句：The army was forced to retreat after suffering heavy losses.  军队在遭受重大损失后被迫撤退。


The soldiers made a strategic retreat from the enemy. 士兵们从敌人那里进行了战略性撤退。


近义词：withdrawal,  retraction,  recession | 反义词：advance,  attack,  onward


词性: 名词和不及物动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army retreats every evening.  军队每天傍晚都撤退。
He retreats to his cabin for peace and quiet. 他退到自己的小屋寻求平静。


一般过去时:
The troops retreated after the battle.  战斗结束后，部队撤退了。
She retreated into her shell after the argument. 争吵后，她变得沉默寡言。


现在完成时:
The enemy has retreated from the border. 敌人已从边境撤退。
He has retreated from public life since the scandal. 自丑闻发生以来，他已退出公众生活。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had retreated before the reinforcements arrived. 增援部队到达之前，士兵们已经撤退了。
She had retreated to her room before I could talk to her. 我还没来得及和她说话，她就已退回房间了。


将来时:
The army will retreat at dawn. 军队将在黎明撤退。
He will retreat to the mountains for the summer. 他将在夏天撤退到山里。


虚拟语气:
If they had retreated earlier, they would have saved more lives. 如果他们早点撤退，就能挽救更多生命。
I wish I could retreat to a desert island. 我真希望我能退到一个荒岛上。


被动语态:
The city was retreated from by the enemy.  这座城市被敌人放弃了。(此被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态表达)
The position was retreated from quickly.  这个阵地很快就被放弃了。(此被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态表达)


疑问句:
Did the army retreat? 军队撤退了吗？
Will he retreat from the challenge? 他会逃避挑战吗？


否定句:
The army did not retreat. 军队没有撤退。
He will not retreat from his position. 他不会放弃自己的立场。
