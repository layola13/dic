disarraying:/dɪsərˈeɪɪŋ/| v.|使混乱；使杂乱；使散乱 | To throw into disorder; to disrupt; to cause confusion.  |例句：The unexpected news disarrayed their carefully laid plans.  意外的消息打乱了他们精心制定的计划。

例句：The sudden storm disarrayed the carefully arranged flower display. 突如其来的暴风雨打乱了精心布置的花展。


近义词：disorganizing, disrupting, unsettling, confusing

反义词：organizing, ordering, arranging, systematizing
