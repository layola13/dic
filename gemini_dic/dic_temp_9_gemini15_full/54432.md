disinfects:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkts/ | vt. | 消毒；杀菌 | To clean something using a chemical that kills bacteria and other harmful microorganisms.  | 例句：The nurse disinfected the wound before bandaging it. 护士在包扎伤口之前对伤口进行了消毒。 | 例句：We need to disinfect the kitchen surfaces after handling raw meat.  处理生肉后，我们需要对厨房表面进行消毒。 | 近义词：sterilize, sanitize | 反义词：contaminate
