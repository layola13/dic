circuitor:/ˈsɜːkjuːteɪtər/| n. |电路测试仪，巡回者|A circuitor is a device or person that moves in a circuit or performs a circuitous action.  It can refer to an electrical testing instrument or a person who travels a circuit, such as a judge or salesperson. |例句：The electrician used a circuitor to test the wiring. 电工用电路测试仪测试线路。 The travelling judge was known as the circuitor of the region.  巡回法官被称为该地区巡回者。|近义词：inspector, tester, traveller, itinerant|反义词：stationary, sedentary
