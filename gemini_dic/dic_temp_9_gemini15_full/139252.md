oblige:/əˈblaɪdʒ/ | vt. | 使…感到必须；施恩惠于；帮忙；履行（义务） | to make someone legally or morally bound to do something; to do a favor for someone; to fulfill an obligation |

例句：
I am obliged to help him. = 我有义务帮助他。

The law obliges employers to provide a safe working environment. = 法律规定雇主必须提供安全的工作环境。


近义词：compel, force, require, assist, help

反义词：refuse, neglect, ignore


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He obliges his customers with excellent service. = 他以优质的服务使他的顾客满意。
I always oblige my friends when they need help. = 我的朋友需要帮助时，我总是乐于助人。


一般过去时:
She obliged me by lending me her car. = 她借给我她的车，帮了我一个忙。
The judge obliged the defendant to pay a fine. = 法官判决被告支付罚款。


现在完成时:
I have obliged my parents by helping them with their moving. = 我通过帮助父母搬家来报答他们。
He has obliged his promise to donate to charity. = 他履行了向慈善机构捐款的承诺。


过去完成时:
They had obliged to complete the project before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前不得不完成项目。
She had obliged herself to keep the secret. = 她已承诺保守秘密。


将来时:
I will oblige you by giving you a lift. = 我会开车送你，帮你个忙。
The company will oblige to meet the new regulations. = 公司将遵守新的规章制度。


虚拟语气:
If you obliged me, I would be very grateful. = 如果你帮我这个忙，我会非常感激。
I wish he would oblige and finish the work. = 我希望他能帮忙把工作做完。


被动语态:
We were obliged to leave early. = 我们不得不早点离开。
The company is obliged to follow the rules and regulations. = 公司有义务遵守规章制度。


疑问句:
Will you oblige me with a dance? = 你愿意赏脸和我跳支舞吗？
Are you obliged to attend the meeting? = 你必须参加会议吗？


否定句:
I cannot oblige you this time. = 这次我帮不了你。
He refused to oblige and did nothing. = 他拒绝帮忙，什么也没做。


PHRASE->
be obliged to = 不得不；必须
feel obliged to = 感到有义务
under obligation to = 有义务
oblige someone with something = 用……使某人满意
oblige a request = 答应请求

词源:
源自古法语 *obliger，最终源自拉丁语 *obligāre，意思是“to bind”。  这个词根也与英语单词“obligation”和“obligatory”相关。
