fleatrap: /ˈfliːtræp/ | n. |捕蝇器，陷阱|A device or situation designed to trap and kill flies or other insects, or, metaphorically, to entrap someone.  |例句：The sticky flytrap caught dozens of flies.  这个粘蝇器粘住了几十只苍蝇。

The company's new marketing strategy proved to be a fleatrap for unwary investors. 公司新的营销策略证明对毫无戒心的投资者来说是一个陷阱。|近义词：flycatcher, trap, snare |反义词：release, freedom
