femalize:/ˈfiːmeɪlaɪz/ | vt. |使女性化；使具有女性特征 | To make or become more feminine in appearance, behavior, or characteristics.  |例句：He tried to femalize his appearance by wearing makeup and jewelry. 他试图通过化妆和佩戴首饰来使自己的外表女性化。

例句：The advertising campaign attempted to femalize the brand's image to appeal to a wider audience.  这个广告活动试图使品牌的形象女性化，以吸引更广泛的受众。


近义词：effeminate, feminize, womanize


反义词：masculinize, virility


词性：动词
