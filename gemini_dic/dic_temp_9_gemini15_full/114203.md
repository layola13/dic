maidenly: /ˈmeɪdənli/ | adj. | 少女的；娴淑的；温顺的 |  Suitable for or characteristic of a maiden; modest and gentle.  |  She possessed a maidenly shyness that was quite charming. 她有一种非常迷人的少女般的羞涩。  Her maidenly demeanor won her many admirers. 她娴淑的举止赢得了许多人的赞赏。 |  modest, demure, gentle, meek |  bold, brazen, immodest, forward |  形容词
