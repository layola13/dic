rightly:/ˈraɪtli/| adv.|正确地，恰当地|Correctly; in a just or proper manner.|He rightly refused to participate in the dishonest scheme. 他理所当然地拒绝参与这个不诚实的计划。|correctly, properly, justly|wrongly, improperly, unjustly|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She rightly judges the situation. = 她正确地判断了形势。
They rightly refuse to compromise their principles. = 他们理所当然地拒绝妥协他们的原则。


一般过去时:
He rightly guessed the answer. = 他猜对了答案。
She rightly suspected foul play. = 她正确地怀疑有犯规行为。


现在完成时:
They have rightly earned their success. = 他们理所当然地获得了成功。
We have rightly been criticized for our actions. = 我们因为我们的行为而受到批评是理所应当的。


过去完成时:
He had rightly anticipated the problem. = 他正确地预料到了这个问题。
She had rightly feared the consequences. = 她正确地害怕了后果。


将来时:
They will rightly be rewarded for their efforts. = 他们将因他们的努力而得到应有的奖励。
He will rightly face the music for his mistakes. = 他将为他的错误承担应有的责任。


虚拟语气:
If he had acted rightly, the outcome would have been different. = 如果他当时做得正确，结果就会不同。
I wish I had rightly assessed the risks involved. = 我希望我当时正确地评估了所涉及的风险。


被动语态:
The decision was rightly made. = 这个决定是正确的。
His actions were rightly condemned. = 他的行为受到了应有的谴责。


疑问句:
Did he rightly understand the instructions? = 他是否正确地理解了指示？

否定句:
