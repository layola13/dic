conceptualism:/kənˈsɛptʃuəlɪzəm/ | n. |概念主义；概念艺术 | The theory or practice of conceptual art, emphasizing ideas and concepts over traditional aesthetic concerns.  Conceptualism prioritizes the artist's idea or concept over the finished artwork's physical form.  It often involves the use of text, photography, and everyday objects to convey a message or idea. | Example:  The exhibition showcased a variety of conceptual art, including installations that challenged traditional notions of beauty.  这场展览展示了各种概念艺术，包括挑战传统审美观念的装置艺术。 | 近义词：conceptual art, idea art | 反义词：formalism, realism
