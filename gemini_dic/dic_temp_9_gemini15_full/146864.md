overindustrializing: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度工业化 |  The process of excessively developing industries, often leading to negative consequences for the environment and society.  It implies a level of industrialization beyond what is sustainable or beneficial.


例句：

Overindustrializing the region has led to severe air pollution. =  该地区的过度工业化导致了严重的空气污染。

The country is grappling with the consequences of overindustrializing its economy too quickly. =  这个国家正在努力应对其经济发展过度工业化过快的后果。


近义词：overdeveloping, hyperindustrialization

反义词：deindustrializing, underdeveloping


词性：动词
