It rejects the idea that reality can be reduced to a single fundamental substance or principle.  | Example: The philosopher presented a nonmonistic view of consciousness, arguing that it arises from a complex interplay of various factors rather than a single source.  哲学家提出了关于意识的非一元论观点，认为意识源于多种因素的复杂相互作用，而不是单一来源。| 近义词：pluralistic, multi-faceted,多元的 | 反义词：monistic, unitary,一元的 |  形容词
