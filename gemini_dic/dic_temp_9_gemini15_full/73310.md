foreclosable: /ˌfɔːrˈkloʊzəbəl/ | adj. | 可被取消抵押品赎回权的；可止赎的 | Capable of being foreclosed; subject to foreclosure.  This refers to a property or loan that can be legally seized by a lender when the borrower defaults on payments. |


例句：

The bank determined the property was foreclosable after repeated missed mortgage payments. = 在多次错过抵押贷款还款后，银行认定该房产可被取消抵押品赎回权。

The loan agreement clearly stated under what conditions the property would become foreclosable. = 贷款协议明确规定了在什么情况下房产将成为可被取消抵押品赎回权的。


近义词：seizable, repossessable


反义词：unforeclosable, secure


词性：形容词
