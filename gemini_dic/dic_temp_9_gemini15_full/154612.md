persicot:/pɜːrsɪkɒt/| n. |桃子；桃树|A persicot is a type of peach, often referring to a specific variety known for its particularly sweet and fragrant flavour.  The term is less common than "peach" in modern usage.  It may also refer to a peach tree.|例句：The old orchard was renowned for its persicots, prized for their exquisite sweetness.  古老的果园以其桃子而闻名，其桃子以其极佳的甜味而闻名。|近义词：peach, nectarine|反义词:无
