contemporaneity:/ˌkɒntɛmpəˈreɪniəti/| n. |同时代；同时期；当代性 |The state or quality of being contemporary; existence or occurrence at the same time.  It often refers to the shared cultural, social, or historical context of a particular period. |例句：The exhibition explores the contemporaneity of ancient and modern art.  展览探讨了古代和现代艺术的当代性。

例句：The novel's contemporaneity is evident in its exploration of social media's impact. 小说的当代性在其对社交媒体影响的探索中显而易见。


近义词：simultaneity, contemporariness, currentness |反义词：antiquity, pastness
