qualmish:/ˈkwɑːlmɪʃ/ | adj. | 恶心想吐的，感到不适的 | Feeling slightly sick; nauseous.  |Example:  The bumpy bus ride made me feel qualmish.  颠簸的巴士旅程让我感到恶心。

The smell of the fish made her feel qualmish. 鱼腥味让她感到恶心。|近义词：nauseous, queasy, sick, ill |反义词：well, healthy

词源:
The word "qualmish" is derived from the noun "qualm," meaning a sudden feeling of faintness or nausea.  The suffix "-ish" adds a somewhat diminishing or less intense connotation.  Ultimately, "qualm" has roots in Old Norse and Old English words related to oppression or discomfort.
