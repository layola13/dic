octo:/ˈɒktoʊ/| /ˈɑːktoʊ/ |prefix|八，八个|A prefix meaning "eight" or "eighth". It is derived from the Latin word "octo".|The octopus has eight arms.|近义词: octa-, oct-|反义词:None


USAGE->
This word is a prefix, not a standalone word, so it doesn't have verb conjugations or other grammatical structures as shown in the example.  It combines with other words to create new words.


PHRASE->
octogenarian:  an octogenarian is a person who is between 80 and 89 years old.  八旬老人
octopus: an octopus is a sea creature with eight arms. 章鱼
October: October is the eighth month of the Roman year (before the Julian calendar reform). 十月
octagon: an octagon is a polygon with eight sides and eight angles. 八边形
octopod:  an octopod is an animal with eight feet or appendages (typically used for cephalopods like octopuses). 八足动物
