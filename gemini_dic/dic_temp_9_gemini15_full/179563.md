reemanates: /ˌriːˈɛməneɪts/ | v. | 再散发；重新发出；再次流露 | To emanate again; to be emitted or given off anew.  The word is a relatively rare and less common usage, formed by adding the prefix "re-" to "emanate".|


例句：

The flowers reemanated a sweet fragrance after the rain.  雨后鲜花再次散发出阵阵清香。

His anger reemanated throughout the room after he received the news. 他收到消息后，怒气再次弥漫整个房间。


近义词：reeffuse, re-emit, radiate anew


反义词：absorb, suppress, conceal


词性：动词
