pre-eminent: /ˌpriːˈemɪnənt/ | adj. | 卓越的；杰出的；首位的 |

英文解析:Surpassing all others; very distinguished in some way.  Significantly better than others of its kind.


例句:

He is a pre-eminent scholar in his field. 他是该领域里卓越的学者。

Her pre-eminent beauty captivated everyone. 她的卓越美貌迷住了所有人。


近义词: outstanding, exceptional, distinguished, foremost, leading, supreme, superior


反义词: insignificant, inferior, mediocre, ordinary


词性: adjective
