fabricate:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪt/| vt.|捏造；制造；伪造|To fabricate something means to create or make it, often in a skillful way. It can also mean to invent or construct a false story or piece of information. |例句：He fabricated a story to explain his absence. 他编造了一个故事来解释他的缺席。

例句：The company fabricates car parts. 这家公司制造汽车零件。|近义词：construct, manufacture, invent, concoct|反义词：destroy, demolish, deconstruct|动词


USAGE->

The factory fabricates high-quality steel. = 工厂制造高质量的钢材。
