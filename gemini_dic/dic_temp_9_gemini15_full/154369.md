permalink:/ˈpɜːrməlɪŋk/| n. |永久链接|A permalink is a permanent link to a specific webpage or online content.  It remains the same even if the website's structure changes. | Example:  This is the permalink to the article: https://example.com/article/123.  这个是文章的永久链接: https://example.com/article/123.|近义词：permanent link, stable URL|反义词：temporary link
