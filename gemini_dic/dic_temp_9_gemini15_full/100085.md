interpone:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊn/| vt.|插入；介入；调停|To place or insert between; to interpose.|例句：He interposed himself between the two arguing men. 他介入两个争吵的人之间。|例句：She interposed a plea for mercy. 她插入一句求情的恳求。|近义词：interject, insert, intervene|反义词：remove, withdraw, extract|动词
