janizary:/ˈdʒænɪzəri/| n. |耶尼切里|A member of an elite corps of infantry soldiers in the Ottoman army from the 14th to the 19th centuries.  They were known for their military prowess and loyalty, but also for their eventual involvement in political machinations and ultimately their suppression.|例句：The Janissaries were a powerful force in the Ottoman Empire for centuries.  耶尼切里几个世纪以来都是奥斯曼帝国的一支强大的力量。The rebellion of the Janissaries ultimately contributed to the decline of the empire.  耶尼切里的叛乱最终导致了帝国的衰落。|近义词：soldier, mercenary, guardsman |反义词：civilian
