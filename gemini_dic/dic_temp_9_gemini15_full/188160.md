routers:/ˈraʊtərz/| n.|路由器|A router is a networking device that forwards data packets between networks.  It determines the best path for a packet to take to reach its destination. Routers are essential for connecting different networks, such as the internet and a local area network (LAN).|例句：My home network relies on a powerful router to connect all my devices. 我的家庭网络依靠一台强大的路由器来连接我所有的设备。

The company uses multiple routers to manage its large network. 这家公司使用多个路由器来管理其大型网络。|近义词：gateway, switch (in some contexts)|反义词：None|名词
