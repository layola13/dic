misappreciate:/ˌmɪsæˈpriːʃieɪt/| vt.|误解；低估；不欣赏|to fail to understand or appreciate something correctly; to undervalue something|例句：I misappreciate the difficulties he faced. 我误解了他面临的困难。|例句：They misappreciated the value of his contribution. 他们低估了他的贡献价值。|近义词： misunderstand, undervalue, underestimate |反义词： appreciate, understand, value


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misappreciates the complexity of the problem. = 他误解了问题的复杂性。
They misappreciate the efforts of their employees. = 他们不重视员工的努力。


一般过去时:
