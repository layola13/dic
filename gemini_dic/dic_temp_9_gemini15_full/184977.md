retelegraphs:/ˌriːˈtɛləɡræfs/| v.| 再电报；重复发送电报 |To send a telegram again, typically to ensure delivery or to correct an error.  It implies a second transmission of the same message. | Example: The company retelegraphed the urgent order to ensure its prompt attention. 公司再次发电报催促订单以确保其得到及时处理。|近义词： resend, retransmit |反义词： receive, delete

They retelegraph important updates to all branches. = 他们向所有分公司重复发送重要的更新信息。
