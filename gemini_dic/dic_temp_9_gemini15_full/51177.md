desserts:/dəˈzɜːrts/| n. |甜点，甜食 |Sweet dishes served at the end of a meal.  Desserts are often sweet and can include cakes, pies, ice cream, and fruit. |例句：We had chocolate cake for dessert. 我们吃巧克力蛋糕当甜点。
例句：The restaurant offers a wide variety of desserts. 这家餐厅提供各种各样的甜点。|近义词：sweet, afters, sweets |反义词：appetizer, starter
