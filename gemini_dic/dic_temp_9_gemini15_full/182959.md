replacer:/rɪˈpleɪsər/| n. |替换物，替代者 |A replacer is something or someone that replaces something or someone else.  It refers to a thing, person, or process that takes the place of another. | Example:  The old engine was replaced with a new, more efficient replacer.  旧引擎被一个更高效的替代品取代了。|近义词：substitute, replacement, alternative |反义词：original, predecessor
