selenography:/səˌlɪˈnɒɡrəfi/ | n. |月面学，月球地形学 |The scientific study of the physical features of the Moon's surface.  It involves mapping, charting, and analyzing the Moon's craters, mountains, valleys, and other geological formations. |Example: Selenography is a branch of astronomy that focuses on the Moon's surface. 月面学是天文学的一个分支，专注于月球的表面。  |近义词：lunar topography, lunar cartography |反义词：None.
