grains:/ɡreɪnz/| n.|谷物；颗粒；少量|Small, hard pieces of a substance, especially of a foodstuff such as rice, wheat, or sand.  It can also refer to individual pieces of something.|The farmer harvested a plentiful crop of grains this year.  今年农民收成了一大批谷物。

The sand grains were smooth and warm underfoot. 沙粒在脚下光滑温暖。


近义词：kernels, seeds, granules, particles|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
