quell:/kwɛl/| vt.|压制；平息；抑制|to suppress or extinguish, especially a feeling or outburst.|例句：The police quickly quelled the riot.  警察迅速平息了暴乱。|例句：She tried to quell her anger. 她试图压制自己的怒火。|近义词：suppress, subdue, stifle, quell|反义词：incite, provoke, encourage, stimulate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government quells dissent. = 政府压制异见。
He quells his fear with courage. = 他以勇气压制恐惧。

一般过去时:
The firefighters quelled the fire. = 消防员扑灭了大火。
She quelled her anxieties before the speech. = 演讲前她平息了自己的焦虑。

现在完成时:
They have quelled the rebellion. = 他们已经平息了叛乱。
The medication has quelled his cough. = 药物已经止住了他的咳嗽。

过去完成时:
He had quelled his emotions before entering the room. = 他在进房间之前已经平息了自己的情绪。
The army had quelled the uprising before reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，军队已经平息了起义。


将来时:

We hope to quell the rumors soon. = 我们希望尽快平息谣言。

虚拟语气:
If only we could quell the storm! = 但愿我们能平息这场风暴！
I wish I could quell my doubts. = 我希望我能消除我的疑虑。

被动语态:
The riot was quelled by the police. = 暴乱被警察平息了。
His anger was quelled by a calming influence. = 他的怒火被一种平静的影响所压制。

疑问句:

否定句:
