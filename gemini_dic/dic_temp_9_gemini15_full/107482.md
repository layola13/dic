lanced:/lænst/| vt.| 刺破；切开；排脓 | To pierce or cut open (something), especially a boil or abscess, to release pus or other fluid.  | 例句：The doctor lanced the boil to drain the infection. 医生切开了脓肿以排出感染物。

The surgeon lanced the abscess. 外科医生切开了脓肿。


近义词：incise, puncture, cut open | 反义词：seal, close


词源:源自于古法语“lancer”,意思是“投掷，刺”。  最终源于拉丁语“lancea”, 意思是“长矛”。
