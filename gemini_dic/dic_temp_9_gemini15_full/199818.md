sistren:/ˈsɪstrən/| n.|姐妹们|The plural of "sister," referring to a group of sisters.  It is now largely archaic or poetic.|例句：My sistren and I shared a close bond. = 我的姐妹们和我有着紧密的联系。

例句：The sistren gathered for their annual reunion. = 姐妹们聚集在一起参加她们每年的聚会。


近义词：sisters, female siblings


反义词：brothers, male siblings


词性:名词
