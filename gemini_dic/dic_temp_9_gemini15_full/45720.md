curvet:/kɜːrˈvet/ | n. v. | 腾跃；曲线；弯曲 | A curvet is a leap or bound, especially of a horse.  It can also refer to a curve or bend. As a verb, it means to leap or curve. |例句：The horse executed a graceful curvet. 这匹马优雅地腾跃了一下。 The road curved in a gentle curvet.  这条路轻轻地弯曲着。|近义词：leap, bound, curve, bend |反义词：straight line, direct path |
