chamber:/ˈtʃeɪmbər/ | n. |房间；腔；议院；协会 | A chamber is a room, especially a large, formal room in a public building or a private house.  It can also refer to a cavity or hollow space within something, or an assembly or group, such as a legislative chamber. |例句：The king held court in the grand chamber. 国王在宏伟的房间里举行朝会。

The heart has four chambers. 心脏有四个腔。


近义词：room, hall, apartment, cavity,  assembly, house | 反义词：open space, exterior

词源:
