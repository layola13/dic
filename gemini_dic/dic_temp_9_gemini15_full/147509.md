overpaint:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪnt/ | vt. | 重新粉刷；在…上再涂一层漆；掩盖 | To paint over something already painted.  It implies covering a previous layer of paint, often to hide or alter the original appearance. | 例句：He overpainted the faded mural with a fresh coat of white. 他用一层新的白色油漆重新粉刷了褪色的壁画。

They overpainted the graffiti to restore the wall's original look. 他们重新粉刷了涂鸦以恢复墙壁的原貌。


近义词：repaint, coat, cover | 反义词：unpaint (this is less common and often implies removing paint)
