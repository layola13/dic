electrolysis:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒləsɪs/| n.|电解；电解作用|The process of using electricity to produce a chemical change, typically the decomposition of a compound into its elements.|例句：Electrolysis is used to produce chlorine and sodium hydroxide. 电解用于生产氯和氢氧化钠。

例句：The process of electrolysis is used in many industrial applications. 电解过程在许多工业应用中使用。


近义词：electrodecomposition|反义词：无
