precondensing:/ˌpriːkənˈdensɪŋ/| v.|预冷凝，预冷凝作用 |The process of partially condensing a vapor before it enters a main condenser. 预冷凝是蒸汽进入主冷凝器之前部分冷凝的过程。|例句：Precondensing the steam reduces the load on the main condenser. 预冷凝蒸汽减轻了主冷凝器的负荷。|近义词：precooling, preliminary condensation|反义词：post-condensation
