dezincify:/ˌdiːˈzɪŋkɪfaɪ/| vt.|脱锌；除锌|To remove zinc from (a material, especially brass).  This process often occurs due to corrosion. |例句：The brass fittings dezincified over time due to exposure to seawater.  长时间暴露于海水导致黄铜配件脱锌。

例句：Acidic environments can dezincify brass components, leading to structural weakening. 酸性环境会使黄铜部件脱锌，从而导致结构减弱。


近义词：dezincize


反义词：zinc (verb: to coat with zinc)


词性：动词
