enthuse:/ɪnˈθuːz/| vt.|使热情；使兴奋|To fill (someone) with enthusiasm; to make (someone) enthusiastic.|The news enthused the whole team.  这个消息让整个团队都兴奋起来。|inspire, excite|depress, discourage|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enthuses about his new job. = 他对他的新工作充满热情。
The teacher enthuses her students about learning. = 老师激发学生们对学习的热情。


一般过去时:
The concert enthused the audience. = 音乐会让观众们兴奋不已。
Her speech enthused the crowd. = 她的演讲让人群兴奋起来。


现在完成时:
The success of the project has enthused the team. = 项目的成功让团队士气大振。
His passion for art has enthused many young people. = 他对艺术的热情激励了许多年轻人。


过去完成时:
The positive reviews had enthused the author before the book launch. = 在书籍发布之前，积极的评论让作者信心倍增。
She had enthused her friends about the trip before they left. = 在他们出发之前，她曾对朋友们讲述旅行的兴奋。


将来时:
The new technology will enthuse consumers. = 新技术将使消费者兴奋。
I think the movie will enthuse the audience. = 我认为这部电影会让观众兴奋。


虚拟语气:
If the manager enthused the staff, productivity would improve. = 如果经理能够激励员工，生产力就会提高。
I wish the news would enthuse everyone as much as it enthused me. = 我希望这个消息能像激励我一样激励每个人。


被动语态:
The audience was enthused by the performance. = 观众被演出所吸引。
The team was enthused by the positive feedback. = 团队受到了积极反馈的鼓舞。


疑问句:
Does this new product enthuse you? = 这个新产品让你兴奋吗？
What enthuses you about this job? = 你对这份工作的什么方面感到兴奋？


否定句:
The lack of support didn't enthuse the team. = 缺乏支持并没有激励团队。
The dull presentation didn't enthuse the audience. = 乏味的演讲并没有让观众兴奋。


PHRASE->
enthuse about: 对…感到兴奋
enthuse over: 对…感到狂热
be enthused by: 被…所激励
enthuse someone into doing something: 激励某人去做某事
to enthuse someone to do something: 鼓励某人去做某事
