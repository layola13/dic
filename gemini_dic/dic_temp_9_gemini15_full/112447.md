loud-mouthed:/ˌlaʊdˈmaʊθt/ | adj. | 大嘴巴的，夸夸其谈的 |  Characterized by loud and often boastful or offensive talk.  This adjective describes someone who talks loudly and often says things that are considered rude or boastful. | 例句：He's a loud-mouthed bully who always tries to intimidate others. 他是个大嘴巴的恶霸，总是试图恐吓别人。  | 例句：The loud-mouthed politician was eventually discredited.  那个夸夸其谈的政客最终名誉扫地。| 近义词：boastful, vociferous, garrulous, loquacious | 反义词：quiet, reserved, taciturn, reticent |
