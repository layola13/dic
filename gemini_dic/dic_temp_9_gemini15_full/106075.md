knew:/nuː/| v.|知道，了解|To have knowledge or information about something.  It is the past tense of "know".|I knew the answer to the question. 我知道这个问题的答案。|She knew him well. 她很了解他。|understand, comprehend, recognize|forgot, ignored,  misunderstood|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
I know the truth. = 我知道真相。
They know the way. = 他们知道路。

一般过去时:
I knew him in college. = 我在大学里认识他。
We knew it was a risk. = 我们知道这是一个风险。

现在完成时:
I have known her for years. = 我认识她很多年了。
They have known about the problem for a while. = 他们已经知道这个问题有一段时间了。

过去完成时:
I had known him for a long time before he moved. = 在他搬家之前，我认识他很久了。
She had known the secret for months. = 她几个月前就知道这个秘密了。

将来时:
I will know the results tomorrow. = 我明天会知道结果。
They will know what to do. = 他们会知道该怎么做。

虚拟语气:
If I knew his address, I would write to him. = 如果我知道他的地址，我会给他写信。
I wish I knew the answer. = 我真希望我知道答案。

被动语态: (Know doesn't typically use passive voice,  as it's not an action done *to* something)  The information was known to everyone. =  每个人都知道这个信息。(This uses a different verb related to "know")


疑问句:
Do you know the answer? = 你知道答案吗？
Did you know he was leaving? = 你知道他要离开吗？

否定句:
I didn't know the rules. = 我不知道规则。
She didn't know what to say. = 她不知道该说什么。


PHRASE->
know-how: 专业知识,技能
know better than to:  知道不该...
know one's place: 知趣
know the ropes: 熟悉情况
get to know: 逐渐了解
