oasis:/oʊˈeɪsɪs/| n. |绿洲；慰藉；令人欣慰之处 |A fertile or green area in a desert, typically containing water.  An oasis provides a refuge or respite from a harsh or unpleasant environment. |例句：The weary travelers found an oasis in the vast desert.  疲惫的旅行者在广阔的沙漠中发现了一片绿洲。

例句：After a long day of work, a relaxing bath was an oasis of calm.  经过一天漫长的工作之后，一个轻松的澡成了平静的慰藉。


近义词：haven, refuge, sanctuary, respite |反义词：desert, wasteland


词源：From Latin *ōāsīs, from Greek ὄασις (óasis), of Egyptian origin.  The Egyptian word is thought to be related to the word *wḥ3t, meaning "waterplace".
