explanators:/ɪkˈsplænˌeɪtərz/| n. |解释者，说明者|People or things that explain something.  Explanators can be individuals, texts, diagrams, or any other means used to clarify a concept, process, or event.|例句：The professor's lecture served as excellent explanators for the complex theory. 教授的讲座是对这一复杂理论的极好解释。|近义词：explainers, interpreters, commentators |反义词：obscurers, confounders
