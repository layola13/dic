pirogue:/ˈpɪrəɡuː/| n. |独木舟；平底船 |A pirogue is a type of narrow, shallow-bottomed boat, often made from a single tree trunk.  It is commonly used in rivers and swamps. |The fishermen used a pirogue to navigate the shallow river. 渔民们用独木舟航行在浅河上。|canoe, dugout, boat|  |n.
