crevasse:/krəˈvæs/| n. |冰川裂缝；裂隙|A deep crack, especially one in a glacier or the earth's surface.|例句：The climbers carefully avoided the crevasse. 登山者小心地避开了冰川裂缝。

例句：A wide crevasse split the glacier in two. 一条宽阔的裂缝将冰川劈成了两半。

近义词：fissure, chasm, crack, rift|反义词：solid surface, unbroken ground|词性:名词
