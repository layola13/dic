correlativeness:/ˌkɔːrəˈleɪtɪvnəs/| n. |相关性，关联性 |The state or quality of being correlative; mutual relationship.  It refers to the degree to which two or more things tend to vary together.  A high correlativeness suggests a strong relationship, while a low correlativeness indicates a weak or nonexistent relationship.|例句：The study demonstrated a high correlativeness between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究表明吸烟和肺癌之间存在高度相关性。

The researchers found a low correlativeness between the two variables. 研究人员发现这两个变量之间的相关性较低。|近义词：relationship, connection, interdependence, association |反义词：independence, unrelatedness, disassociation |名词
