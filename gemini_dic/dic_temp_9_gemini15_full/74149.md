foundership: /ˈfaʊndərʃɪp/ | n. | 创始；创办；创立；创办人身份 | The act of founding or establishing something, especially an organization or institution; the position or role of a founder.  It also refers to the qualities or characteristics associated with being a founder. |

例句：

Her foundership of the charity was instrumental in its success. = 她创办该慈善机构对它的成功起到了关键作用。

The company's foundership is marked by innovation and risk-taking. = 该公司的创立以创新和冒险为特征。


近义词：foundation, establishment, creation, inception, founding


反义词：dissolution, termination, closure


词性：名词
