head-hunter:/ˈhedˌhʌntər/| n. |猎头，猎头公司|A person or firm whose business is to find people with the right skills and experience to fill senior positions in companies.  They actively seek out suitable candidates, often approaching them even if they are not actively looking for a new job.  |例句：The head-hunter contacted me about a job opportunity at a major tech firm.  猎头联系我，介绍一家大型科技公司的工作机会。

例句：Several head-hunters are vying for the opportunity to recruit the CEO.  几家猎头公司都在争夺招聘首席执行官的机会。


近义词：executive recruiter, talent scout, recruiter |反义词：N/A


词性：名词
