pseudosocial:/ˌsuːdoʊˈsoʊʃəl/ | adj. |伪社会的，貌似社会的 | Apparently social but lacking genuine social interaction or understanding.  It describes behavior or interactions that mimic social behavior but lack genuine social depth or connection.  The term often implies a superficial or artificial quality to the social interaction. | Example: The online community, while seemingly vibrant and pseudosocial, lacked real-world connections among its members.  在线社区虽然看起来很活跃，表面上很社会化，但成员之间缺乏现实世界的联系。 | 近义词：simulated, artificial, superficial, ersatz | 反义词：genuine, authentic, real, true
