nonbenevolent: /ˌnɒnbenˈɛvələnt/ | adj. | 不慈善的；恶意的；不仁慈的 | Not benevolent; unkind; malevolent.  It implies a lack of goodwill, kindness, or compassion, often suggesting an active intention to harm or disregard the well-being of others. |

例句：

The nonbenevolent dictator ruled with an iron fist, suppressing any dissent.  =  这个不仁慈的独裁者铁腕统治，压制任何异议。

His nonbenevolent actions caused widespread suffering and resentment. = 他不慈善的行为造成了广泛的痛苦和怨恨。


近义词：malicious, unkind, cruel, malevolent, spiteful, wicked, harmful

反义词：benevolent, kind, compassionate, charitable, altruistic, humane


词性：形容词
