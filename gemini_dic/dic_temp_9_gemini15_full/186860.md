ringneck:/ˈrɪŋnek/| n.|环颈（动物）|A ringneck is an animal, typically a bird, with a ring of different colored feathers around its neck.  This term is often used to refer to specific species, most commonly the ring-necked pheasant.|例句：The ringneck pheasant is a popular game bird. 环颈雉是一种受欢迎的狩猎鸟类。

例句：We saw a beautiful ringneck dove in the park. 我们在公园里看到一只美丽的环颈鸽。


近义词：ringed bird (泛指)


反义词：无


词性：名词
