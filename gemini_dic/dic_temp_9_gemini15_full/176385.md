refuse's:/rɪˈfjuːzɪz/ | v. | 拒绝（第三人称单数形式） |  To decline or reject (something offered, proposed, or requested).  | He refuses to admit his mistakes. 他拒绝承认自己的错误。 | She refused my offer of help. 她拒绝了我的帮助。 | accept, decline | consent, agree |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refuses to cooperate. = 他拒绝合作。
She refuses all offers of help. = 她拒绝一切帮助。


一般过去时:
They refused to answer my questions. = 他们拒绝回答我的问题。
He refused the job offer. = 他拒绝了这份工作。


现在完成时:
She has refused to see him all week. = 她整个星期都拒绝见他。
He has refused to comment on the allegations. = 他拒绝就这些指控发表评论。


过去完成时:
They had refused to believe him before the evidence surfaced. = 在证据出现之前，他们一直不相信他。
She had refused to go to the party until her friend convinced her. = 在她的朋友说服她之前，她一直拒绝去参加派对。


将来时:
He will refuse to sign the contract. = 他将拒绝签署合同。
They will refuse any further negotiations. = 他们将拒绝进一步谈判。


虚拟语气:
If he refused to help, what would we do? = 如果他拒绝帮忙，我们该怎么办？
I wish he hadn't refused my invitation. = 我真希望他没有拒绝我的邀请。


被动语态:
His request was refused. = 他的请求被拒绝了。
The offer was refused by the board. = 该提议被董事会否决了。


疑问句:
Will he refuse to go? = 他会拒绝去吗？
Does she refuse to answer the questions? = 她拒绝回答问题吗？


否定句:
He doesn't refuse to help. = 他不拒绝帮忙。
She didn't refuse my invitation. = 她没有拒绝我的邀请。


PHRASE->
refuse to comply = 拒绝遵守
refuse point-blank = 断然拒绝
refuse entry = 拒绝入内
refuse treatment = 拒绝治疗
refuse bail = 拒绝保释
