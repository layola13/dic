dormant:/ˈdɔːrmənt/ | adj. | 休眠的；潜伏的；静止的 | inactive, sleeping, or temporarily not showing any activity.  A dormant volcano is one that is not currently erupting but could erupt again.  The seeds remained dormant until the spring rains arrived. | 近义词：inactive, latent, quiescent, sleeping | 反义词：active, awake, vigorous


例句：

The volcano has been dormant for centuries.  这座火山已经休眠了几个世纪。

The company's expansion plans are currently dormant due to the economic downturn.  由于经济低迷，公司的扩张计划目前处于停滞状态。


词源：

源自古法语 *dormant，意思是“睡觉的”，来自拉丁语 *dormiens，是动词dormire 的现在分词，意思是“睡觉”。
