preconstitute:/ˌpriːkənˈstɪtjuːt/| vt.|预先构成；预先组成；预先制定|To constitute beforehand; to form or establish in advance.  The committee was preconstituted before the meeting began.  |例句：The ingredients were preconstituted before the chef began cooking.  = 厨师开始做饭之前，配料就已经预先配制好了。

The company preconstituted a crisis management team to deal with potential problems. = 公司预先组建了一个危机管理团队来处理潜在的问题。|近义词：prearrange, prefabricate, establish beforehand|反义词：improvise, impromptu
