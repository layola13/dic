lorel:/ˈlɔːrəl/| n.|月桂树；桂冠；荣誉| a type of evergreen tree with fragrant leaves, also a symbol of victory or achievement.  |Example:  The poet received a laurel wreath for his outstanding work.  这位诗人因其杰出的作品获得了桂冠。|近义词：bay tree, crown, honor|反义词：disgrace, shame


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The laurel tree provides shade in the summer. 夏季月桂树提供阴凉。
We admire the laurels of the Olympic champions. 我们钦佩奥运冠军的桂冠。


一般过去时:
The athlete received a laurel wreath after winning the race. 运动员赢得比赛后获得了桂冠。
They celebrated their success with a laurel garland.  他们用月桂花环庆祝他们的成功。


现在完成时:
She has earned many laurels throughout her career. 她在整个职业生涯中赢得了许多荣誉。
The team has achieved many laurels this season. 这个赛季球队取得了很多荣誉。


过去完成时:
He had already won several laurels before he retired.  在他退休之前，他已经赢得了几个桂冠。
The company had gained many laurels in the previous decade. 这家公司在之前的十年里获得了许多荣誉。


将来时:
She will surely win more laurels in the future.  她将来肯定会赢得更多荣誉。
The young scientist will receive many laurels for his research.这位年轻的科学家将因他的研究获得许多荣誉。


虚拟语气:
If I were to win a laurel, I would be very proud. 如果我赢得桂冠，我会非常自豪。
I wish I had received more laurels for my contributions. 我希望我的贡献能得到更多认可（荣誉）。


被动语态:
The champion was crowned with a laurel wreath. 冠军被戴上了月桂花冠。
His achievements were lauded with laurels by the critics. 他的成就受到评论家的高度赞扬（用桂冠来比喻）。


疑问句:
Will he be awarded with a laurel for his bravery? 他会因为他的勇敢而获得奖励（荣誉）吗？
Does she deserve the laurels she has received? 她值得她所获得的荣誉吗？


否定句:
He did not receive the laurel he deserved. 他没有得到他应得的荣誉。
Their efforts did not yield the laurels they expected. 他们的努力没有获得他们预期的荣誉。
