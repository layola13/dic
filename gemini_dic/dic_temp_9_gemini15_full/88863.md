hoise:/hɔɪs/| vt. n.| 提升，吊起；(尤指)用绞车吊起 | To lift or raise something, especially using a hoist. |例句：They hoised the sails. 他们升起了帆。|例句：The crane hoised the heavy container onto the ship. 起重机将沉重的集装箱吊到船上。|近义词：lift, raise, hoist up, heave|反义词：lower, drop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hoise the flag every morning. = 他们每天早上升旗。
He hoises the heavy boxes with a crane. = 他用起重机吊起沉重的箱子。


一般过去时:
The sailors hoised the anchor. = 水手们起锚了。
They hoised the injured man to safety. = 他们把受伤的人吊到安全的地方。


现在完成时:
They have hoised the sails and are ready to set sail. = 他们已经升起了帆，准备起航了。
We have hoised the heavy equipment to the top floor. = 我们已经把重型设备吊到了顶楼。


过去完成时:
They had hoised the tent before the rain started. = 下雨前他们已经把帐篷支起来了。
The workers had hoised all the materials before the break. = 工人们在休息前已经把所有材料都吊上去了。


将来时:
They will hoise the cargo onto the ship tomorrow. = 他们明天将把货物吊到船上。
We will hoise the new sign into place next week. = 我们下周将把新的标牌安装到位。


虚拟语气:
If they had a crane, they could hoise the heavy stone easily. = 如果他们有起重机，他们就能轻松地吊起那块巨石。
I wish they would hoise the sails quickly, the wind is picking up. = 我希望他们能尽快升起帆，风越来越大了。


被动语态:
The flag was hoised at sunrise. = 国旗在日出时升起。
The heavy machinery was hoised into position carefully. = 重型机械被小心地吊到了指定位置。


疑问句:
Will they hoise the mast today? = 他们今天会升起桅杆吗？
How do they hoise such a heavy object? = 他们是如何吊起如此沉重的物体的？


否定句:
They didn't hoise the sails because of the storm. = 因为暴风雨，他们没有升起帆。
He couldn't hoise the box alone; it was too heavy. = 他一个人无法吊起箱子；它太重了。
