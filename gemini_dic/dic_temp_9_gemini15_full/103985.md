juggled:/ˈdʒʌɡld/ | vt. | 耍杂技；同时应付；巧妙处理 | To keep several things in motion or in the air at the same time; to manage with skill despite difficulties. | 例句：He juggled three balls in the air. 他抛接三个球。 He juggled work and family responsibilities. 他同时应付工作和家庭责任。 | 近义词：manage, balance, maneuver, handle | 反义词：neglect, ignore

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He juggles three balls every day. = 他每天都玩三个球。
She juggles her career and her family life. = 她同时兼顾事业和家庭生活。

一般过去时:
The clown juggled flaming torches. = 小丑耍着燃烧的火把。
I juggled my schedule to fit in the appointment. = 我调整了我的日程安排以适应预约。

现在完成时:
He has juggled his finances carefully. = 他仔细地处理了他的财务。
She has juggled multiple projects simultaneously for years. = 多年来，她同时处理多个项目。

过去完成时:
He had juggled his responsibilities for months before he finally cracked. = 他在最终崩溃之前已经应付了几个月的责任。
She had juggled several jobs to pay off her student loans. = 她打了几份工来偿还学生贷款。

将来时:
He will juggle the demands of his new job. = 他将应付新工作的要求。
She will juggle her commitments to attend the conference. = 她将权衡她的承诺以参加会议。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would juggle more activities. = 如果我有更多时间，我会做更多的事情。
I wish I could juggle my work and personal life more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地平衡工作和个人生活。

被动语态:
The balls were juggled skillfully. = 球被熟练地抛接。
The responsibilities were juggled effectively by the team. = 团队有效地处理了这些责任。

疑问句:
Does he juggle? = 他会耍杂技吗？
Can she juggle her work and family life successfully? = 她能成功地平衡工作和家庭生活吗？

否定句:
He doesn't juggle well. = 他不擅长耍杂技。
She couldn't juggle all her commitments. = 她无法应付她所有的承诺。
