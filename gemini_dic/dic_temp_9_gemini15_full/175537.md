ranches:/ˈræntʃɪz/| n.|牧场；大农场|Large farms, especially in the western United States, where livestock (cattle, sheep, horses, etc.) are raised.|例句：The cowboys worked hard on the ranches. 牛仔们在牧场上辛勤工作。|例句：Many ranches in Texas raise cattle.德克萨斯州的许多牧场都饲养牛。|近义词：farm, estate, ranchland|反义词：city, town
