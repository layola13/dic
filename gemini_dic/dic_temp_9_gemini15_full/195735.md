semitertian: /ˌsɛmɪˈtɜːrʃən/ | adj. | 半三次的，每隔两次的 | Relating to or denoting a fever that recurs every other day.  It refers to a type of intermittent fever where the fever returns every other day, meaning there is a fever on day 1, no fever on day 2, fever on day 3, and so on.  The term is largely obsolete in modern medicine, having been replaced by more precise classifications of fevers. | Example: The patient presented with a semitertian fever, characterized by its recurrence every other day.  | 近义词:  (In the context of fever patterns)  intermittent, recurring | 反义词:  continuous, constant
