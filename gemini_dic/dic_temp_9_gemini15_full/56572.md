dominoed:/ˈdɒmɪnoʊd/ | v. | 骨牌效应，连锁反应 | To cause (something) to fall or collapse in a chain reaction, like dominoes.  |例句：The bank's collapse dominoed the entire financial system.  银行的倒闭引发了整个金融体系的连锁反应。

例句：One company's bankruptcy dominoed into a series of failures across the industry.  一家公司的破产导致该行业一系列的倒闭。


近义词：cascaded, triggered, set off, initiated | 反义词：stabilized, prevented, halted | 词性:动词
