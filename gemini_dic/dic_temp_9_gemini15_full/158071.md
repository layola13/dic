plasterboard:/ˈplæstəbɔːrd/ | n. | 石膏板，纸面石膏板 | A building material consisting of a core of gypsum plaster sandwiched between two layers of heavy paper. It is used for interior walls and ceilings. |  The walls are made of plasterboard.  墙壁是用石膏板做的。  | drywall, gypsum board |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
