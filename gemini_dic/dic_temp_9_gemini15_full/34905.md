cliquishly:/ˈklɪkɪʃli/| adv.|派系地，小集团地，拉帮结派地|In a manner characteristic of a clique; in a way that suggests exclusivity and a lack of openness.  It implies that interactions are limited to a select group, often excluding others.|例句：They behaved cliquishly, ignoring anyone outside their inner circle.  他们表现得很排外，无视圈子外的人。

例句：The students cliquishly gathered in their separate groups during lunch break. 学生们在午餐休息时间拉帮结派地聚在一起。


近义词：exclusively, factionally, sectarianly

反义词：inclusively, openly, democratically

词性:副词
