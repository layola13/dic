internalized: /ɪnˈtɜːrnəlaɪzd/ | v. | 使内化；使成为习惯；吸收；同化 | To make (something, such as a belief, idea, or value) an accepted part of one's character or way of thinking.  | 例句：He had internalized the values of his upbringing. 他已经将他的成长环境中的价值观内化了。 He has internalized the company culture. 他已经将公司文化内化了。 | 近义词：assimilated, absorbed, integrated, adopted | 反义词：externalized, rejected, resisted | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They internalize the lessons learned. = 他们将学到的教训内化了。
She internalizes her emotions. = 她将自己的情感内化了。

一般过去时:
He internalized the criticisms and improved his work. = 他将批评内化并改进了他的工作。
The community internalized the new rules quickly. = 社区迅速地将新规章制度内化了。

现在完成时:
She has internalized the principles of non-violence. = 她已经将非暴力原则内化了。
They have internalized the company's mission statement. = 他们已经将公司的使命宣言内化了。

过去完成时:
He had internalized so much negativity that he felt depressed. = 他已经内化了如此多的消极情绪以至于感到沮丧。
By the time the project ended, they had internalized the new software. = 到项目结束时，他们已经掌握了新软件。


将来时:
She will internalize the information after she reviews it a few more times. = 她再复习几次之后，就会把信息内化了。
The students will internalize the concepts through practice. = 学生们将通过实践来内化这些概念。

虚拟语气:
If he internalized the feedback, he would have improved significantly. = 如果他能将反馈内化，他就会有显著的进步。
I wish I had internalized the warnings earlier. = 我真希望我早点把警告内化了。

被动语态:
The values were internalized by the young people. = 这些价值观被年轻人内化了。
The new policies are being internalized by the staff. = 新政策正在被员工内化。

疑问句:
Has she internalized the lessons from her mistakes? = 她已经从自己的错误中吸取教训了吗？
Did the team internalize the strategic goals? = 团队是否已经将战略目标内化？

否定句:
He did not internalize the feedback he received. = 他没有将收到的反馈意见内化。
They failed to internalize the importance of teamwork. = 他们未能将团队合作的重要性内化。
