schoolboyishness:/ˈskuːlˌbɔɪɪʃnəs/| n. |孩子气；幼稚；顽皮|The characteristic behavior or qualities of a schoolboy;  immature behavior often characterized by boisterousness, silliness, or mischief.  It suggests a playful, sometimes irresponsible, and often endearing lack of maturity. |例句：His schoolboyishness was endearing, even at his age.  他的孩子气即使在他这个年纪也挺讨人喜欢的。

例句：Despite his professional success, a hint of schoolboyishness remained in his personality. 尽管事业有成，他身上仍保留着一丝孩子气。


近义词：childishness, immaturity, puerility, boyishness


反义词：maturity, adulthood, seriousness, sophistication
