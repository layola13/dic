part-time:/pɑːrt ˈtaɪm/| adj.|兼职的；部分时间的|Partially or less than full-time.  It describes work, study, or other activities that are done for only part of the usual working or studying day or week.|例句：He works part-time at a restaurant. 他在一家餐馆兼职。

例句：She's taking a part-time course in French. 她正在上一个法语兼职课程。

近义词：half-time, occasional, subsidiary|反义词：full-time, whole-time|词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works part-time at the library. = 他在图书馆兼职。
She studies part-time at the university. = 她在大学兼职学习。

一般过去时:
I worked part-time during my college years. = 我大学期间做过兼职。
They studied part-time to balance work and education. = 他们兼职学习以平衡工作和学习。

现在完成时:
She has worked part-time for five years. = 她兼职工作五年了。
He has studied part-time since last year. = 他从去年开始兼职学习。

过去完成时:
I had worked part-time before I got my full-time job. = 在我找到全职工作之前，我做过兼职。
She had studied part-time for two years before she decided to quit. = 在她决定辞职之前，她兼职学习了两年。

将来时:
I will work part-time next summer. = 我明年夏天将做兼职。
She will study part-time after she graduates. = 她毕业后将兼职学习。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would work part-time. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会做兼职。
If she were free, she would study part-time. = 如果她有空，她会兼职学习。

被动语态:
Part-time jobs are often advertised online. = 兼职工作通常在网上发布广告。
Part-time courses are designed for flexibility. = 兼职课程的设计是为了灵活性。

疑问句:
Do you work part-time or full-time? = 你是兼职还是全职？
Is she studying part-time this semester? = 她这个学期是兼职学习吗？

否定句:
He doesn't work part-time anymore. = 他不再兼职了。
She doesn't plan to study part-time next year. = 她不打算明年兼职学习。
