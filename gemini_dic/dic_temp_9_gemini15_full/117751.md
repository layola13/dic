melanotic:/ˌmɛləˈnɒtɪk/| adj.|黑色素的；产生黑色素的|Characterized by or producing melanin.  Melanotic lesions are often dark-colored due to the presence of melanin.|例句：The mole was melanotic, prompting the doctor to order a biopsy.  这个痣是黑色素的，促使医生下令进行活检。

例句：The patient presented with melanotic macules on their skin. 病人皮肤上出现了黑色素斑。


近义词：pigmented (着色的), dark-pigmented (深色素的)
反义词：amelanotic (无黑色素的), hypopigmented (色素减少的)
词性：形容词
