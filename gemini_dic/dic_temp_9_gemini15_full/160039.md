polytheists:/ˈpɒliθiːɪsts/| n. |多神论者；相信多神的人|People who believe in or worship more than one god.  Polytheism is the belief in or worship of more than one god.  The ancient Greeks and Romans were famous polytheists.|例句：Many ancient civilizations were polytheists, each with their own pantheon of gods and goddesses.  许多古代文明都是多神论者，每个文明都有自己的一套神和女神。

Many cultures throughout history have been polytheists, demonstrating diverse belief systems.  历史上许多文化都是多神论者，展示了多种多样的信仰体系。


近义词：pagans, idolaters |反义词：monotheists, atheists
