invariantive: /ɪnˈveriəntiv/ | adj. | 不变的；与变化无关的 |  Relating to or denoting something that is invariant; not subject to change or variation.  It often describes mathematical or physical properties that remain constant under certain transformations. |

例句：

The laws of physics are often described as invariantive under certain transformations. = 物理定律在某些变换下通常被描述为不变的。

The speed of light is an invariantive quantity in special relativity. = 在狭义相对论中，光速是一个不变的量。


近义词：constant, unchanging, invariable, stable

反义词：variable, changeable, fluctuating


词性：形容词
