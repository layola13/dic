disaffiliated: /ˌdɪsæˈfɪlieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 脱离关系的；失去联系的 |  No longer affiliated; separated from an organization or group.  |

例句：

The university was disaffiliated from the athletic conference.  = 这所大学已脱离该体育联盟。

The artist became disaffiliated with the gallery after a dispute. = 在发生争执后，这位艺术家与画廊失去了联系。


近义词：separated, disassociated, detached, unconnected

反义词：affiliated, associated, connected


词性：形容词
