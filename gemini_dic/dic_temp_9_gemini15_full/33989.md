citations:/saɪˈteɪʃənz/| n.|引文；引用；传唤|References or mentions of a source.  The act of citing a source.  In legal contexts, a summons to appear in court.|例句：The essay contains numerous citations from scholarly articles. 这篇文章包含许多学术文章的引用。

例句：The police issued a citation for speeding. 警方因超速开出了传票。


近义词：quotations, references, mentions, summons


反义词：omission
