rationalized:/ˈræʃənəlaɪzd/ | v. | 使合理化；解释；使合理 | To provide a rational explanation for something, often to justify it or make it seem more acceptable.  It can also refer to streamlining a process or system to make it more efficient. | Example:  The company rationalized its operations by closing several unprofitable branches. 公司通过关闭几家无利可图的分支机构来使其运营合理化。

The government rationalized its decision to increase taxes by citing the need for increased public spending. 政府以需要增加公共支出为由，使其增税的决定合理化。|近义词：justify, explain, streamline, optimize |反义词：irrationalize, confound, complicate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rationalize their actions by saying it was for the greater good. = 他们以为了更大的利益为由为自己的行为辩解。
The company rationalizes its processes to improve efficiency. = 公司使其流程合理化以提高效率。


一般过去时:
He rationalized his fear of flying by focusing on statistics. = 他通过关注统计数据来使他对飞行的恐惧合理化。
The factory rationalized production by investing in new machinery. = 工厂通过投资新机器使生产合理化。


现在完成时:
She has rationalized her spending habits after going into debt. = 她负债后已经使其消费习惯合理化。
The company has rationalized its workforce to cut costs. = 公司已经使其员工队伍合理化以削减成本。


过去完成时:
He had rationalized his past mistakes before seeking forgiveness. = 在寻求原谅之前，他已经使其过去的错误合理化。
The government had rationalized its policies before the election. = 政府在大选之前已经使其政策合理化。


将来时:
They will rationalize their investment strategy to minimize risk. = 他们将使其投资策略合理化以最大限度地减少风险。
The organization will rationalize its structure to improve communication. = 该组织将使其结构合理化以改善沟通。


虚拟语气:
If I rationalized my feelings, I might be less anxious. = 如果我使我的感受合理化，我可能会不那么焦虑。
I wish I had rationalized my decision sooner. = 我希望我早点使我的决定合理化。


被动语态:
The decision was rationalized by the board of directors. = 该决定得到了董事会的合理化解释。
The production process is being rationalized to increase output. = 正在使生产过程合理化以提高产量。


疑问句:
How did they rationalize such a drastic measure? = 他们如何使如此激烈的措施合理化？
Has the company rationalized its marketing strategy? = 公司是否使其营销策略合理化？


否定句:
He did not rationalize his behavior; he simply apologized. = 他没有为自己的行为辩解；他只是道歉了。
The company has not yet rationalized its supply chain. = 公司尚未使其供应链合理化。

词源:
From the word "rational," meaning based on reason or logic.  The suffix "-ize" indicates to make or become something.  Therefore, "rationalize" means to make something reasonable or logical, or to explain something in a reasonable way.
