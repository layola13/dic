self-consumption: /ˌsɛlf kənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. | 自用；自耗；自行消耗 | The act of consuming something oneself, rather than selling it or using it for other purposes.  It often refers to the energy generated by a renewable energy system (like solar panels) that is used on-site by the owner, rather than being fed into the grid.  | 例句：The high rate of self-consumption of solar energy reduces reliance on the grid.  太阳能的 高自耗率降低了对电网的依赖。

The farm's self-consumption of its produce meant less was sent to market.  农场自行消费其农产品意味着送往市场的农产品减少了。| 近义词：own use, personal consumption | 反义词：export, sale, grid injection |  词性: noun
