enclosures:/ɪnˈkloʊʒərz/ | n. |附件；围场；附件物 | Items enclosed with a letter or other document; a space surrounded by a barrier.  |例句：Please find the relevant documents in the enclosures. 请在附件中查找相关文件。

例句：The farmer built strong enclosures to keep his sheep safe. 农民建了结实的围栏来保护他的羊。


近义词：attachments, annexes, addenda | 反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept could be "main body" or "unattached items". | 词性：名词
