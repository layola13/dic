lumbering:/ˈlʌmbərɪŋ/| adj.|笨重的；缓慢移动的；笨拙的|Moving in a slow, heavy, clumsy way.  It suggests a lack of grace or agility.|例句：The lumbering elephant moved slowly through the jungle.  笨重的象缓慢地穿过丛林。

例句：He gave a lumbering apology, stumbling over his words. 他笨拙地道歉，结结巴巴地说不出话来。


近义词：clumsy, awkward, ungainly, ponderous, cumbersome

反义词：graceful, agile, nimble, swift, spry

词性：形容词

词源:
The word "lumbering" comes from the Old English word "lombard," which referred to a type of heavy wagon.  The meaning evolved over time to describe the slow and heavy movement associated with such wagons, and then extended to describe the movement of anything large and clumsy.
