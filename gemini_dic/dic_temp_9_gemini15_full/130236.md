non-Slavic: /ˌnɑːnˈslæviːk/ | adj. | 非斯拉夫的 | Not relating to or characteristic of the Slavic peoples or their languages.  This term refers to anything that is not associated with the Slavic ethnic groups, their cultures, or their languages (which include Russian, Polish, Czech, Slovak, Serbian, Croatian, Bulgarian, Slovenian, etc.). | Example:  Many non-Slavic languages are spoken in Europe.  许多非斯拉夫语系语言在欧洲被使用。


近义词: non-Slavonic


反义词: Slavic


词性: 形容词
