generalistic: /ˌdʒɛnərəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 一般性的；通才的；概括性的 | Dealing with or involving general principles or aspects rather than specific details or cases; characteristic of a generalist.  | 例句：His approach to the problem was too generalistic; he needed to focus on specific details.  他的解决问题的方法过于笼统；他需要关注具体的细节。

例句：The generalistic nature of the survey made it difficult to draw precise conclusions.  这项调查的一般性特征使得难以得出精确的结论。


近义词：general, broad, comprehensive, nonspecific, overarching | 反义词：specific, detailed, particular, specialized


词源：源自general (一般的)+ -istic (形容词后缀)  The suffix "-istic" indicates "relating to" or "characteristic of".  Thus, "generalistic" means "relating to or characteristic of a general approach".
