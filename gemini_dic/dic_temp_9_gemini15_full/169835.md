prosecutor:/ˈprɑːsɪkjuːtər/|/ˈprɒsɪkjuːtə(r)/| n. |检察官，公诉人 |A prosecutor is a legal professional who represents the government in criminal cases and is responsible for bringing charges against those accused of crimes.  They work to prove the guilt of the defendant beyond a reasonable doubt. |The prosecutor presented compelling evidence to the jury.  检察官向陪审团提供了令人信服的证据。|The prosecutor's office will decide whether to press charges.  检察官办公室将决定是否提起诉讼。|plaintiff, attorney general|defendant, defense attorney|名词
