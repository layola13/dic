conjunct:/ˈkɒndʒʌŋkt/ /ˈkɑːndʒʌŋkt/| n., adj.|连词，连接词；联合的，共同的|A word that connects words, phrases, clauses, or sentences.  It can be coordinating (e.g., and, but, or) or subordinating (e.g., because, although, if).|例句：The words "and," "but," and "or" are conjuncts.  这些词“和”、“但是”和“或者”是连词。


例句：The conjunct clauses were clearly linked by the use of "therefore". 通过使用“因此”，联合从句清晰地联系在一起。

近义词：connective, conjunction, link, union|反义词：disjunct, separate, independent|名词，形容词
