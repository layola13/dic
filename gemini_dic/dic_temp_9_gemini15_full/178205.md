recommendable: /ˌrekəˈmɛndəbəl/ | adj. |值得推荐的，推荐的 |  Worth recommending; advisable.  It suggests that something is good, useful, or suitable and therefore deserves to be recommended. | 例句：That restaurant is highly recommendable. = 那家餐馆非常值得推荐。

That new movie is highly recommendable for fans of action films. = 那部新电影非常值得动作片影迷推荐。


近义词：advisable, commendable, desirable, suitable, appropriate | 反义词：unadvisable, inadvisable, undesirable


词源：源自动词recommend + -able (形容词后缀)，表示“可以被推荐的”。  The suffix "-able" indicates something is capable of being done or having a certain quality.


USAGE->

The new hotel is recommendable. = 这家新酒店值得推荐。
This book is recommendable for beginners. = 这本书适合初学者阅读。

过去完成时:

The new software will be highly recommendable once it's released. = 新软件一旦发布，将非常值得推荐。
