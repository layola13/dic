sellotaping:/ˈsɛləˌteɪpɪŋ/| v.|用胶带粘贴|The act of sticking something together using cellotape (or Sellotape, a brand name).  This usually implies a quick, informal method of fastening.|例句：I'm sellotaping the poster to the wall. 我正用胶带把海报贴到墙上。

例句：He quickly sellotaped the broken vase back together. 他迅速地用胶带把破花瓶粘了起来。


近义词：taping, sticking, fastening


反义词：untaping, detaching, unfastening


词性:verb
