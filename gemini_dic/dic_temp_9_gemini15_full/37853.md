competitor:/kəmˈpɛtɪtər/| n. |竞争者，对手|A competitor is a person, company, or country that is competing with others.  |例句：Our main competitor is a large multinational corporation. 我们的主要竞争对手是一家大型跨国公司。

例句：Several small businesses are competing with the big chain stores.几家小企业正在与大型连锁店竞争。


近义词：rival, opponent, contender |反义词：collaborator, ally


词源：来自拉丁语“competere”，意思是“to strive together, be suitable”。  这个词根也产生了“competent” (胜任的) 和 “competition” (竞争)等词。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Who are your main competitors? = 你的主要竞争对手是谁？
The company competes aggressively for market share. = 这家公司积极争夺市场份额。


一般过去时:
Our company competed in the national championship last year. = 我们的公司去年参加了全国锦标赛。
Who did you compete against in the race? = 你在比赛中与谁竞争？


现在完成时:
They have competed in many international events. = 他们参加过许多国际赛事。
Our team has competed successfully against the best in the world. = 我们的团队成功地与世界最佳团队竞争。


过去完成时:
Before the final race, he had already competed in several heats. = 在决赛之前，他已经参加了几轮预赛。
The company had competed in the market for over a decade before it was acquired. = 这家公司在被收购之前，已经在市场上竞争了十多年。


将来时:
We will compete in the upcoming Olympics. = 我们将参加即将到来的奥运会。
Will you compete against your former teammate? = 你会和你的前队友竞争吗？


虚拟语气:
If I were a better competitor, I would win more often. = 如果我是一个更好的竞争者，我会更频繁地获胜。
I wish I had competed more aggressively earlier in my career. = 我希望在我的职业生涯早期更有侵略性地竞争。


被动语态:
The company was outcompeted by its rivals. = 这家公司被竞争对手击败。
This product has been successfully competed against in the market. = 该产品在市场上已经成功地进行了竞争。


疑问句:
How many competitors are there in this market? = 这个市场有多少竞争对手？
Is there any real competition in this industry? = 这个行业真的有竞争吗？


否定句:
We don't have many serious competitors at the moment. = 目前我们没有很多严重的竞争对手。
The company didn't compete effectively in the new market. = 这家公司在新市场上没有有效地竞争。


PHRASE->

direct competitor = 直接竞争对手
fierce competitor = 激烈的竞争对手
potential competitor = 潜在竞争对手
key competitor = 关键竞争对手
