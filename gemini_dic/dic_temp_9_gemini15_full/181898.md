remained:/rɪˈmeɪnd/| v.| 留下；保持；依然；剩余 | To continue to exist or remain in a particular state or condition. | 例句：The problem remained unsolved. 问题依然没有解决。  | 例句：Despite the rain, he remained cheerful. 尽管下雨，他仍然很高兴。|近义词：stayed, persisted, endured|反义词：departed, left, changed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remains silent. = 他保持沉默。
The situation remains unchanged. = 情况保持不变。

一般过去时:
The house remained empty for years. = 这房子空置了好几年。
She remained loyal to her friends. = 她对朋友们始终忠诚。

现在完成时:
He has remained single all his life. = 他终身未婚。
The mystery has remained unsolved. = 这个谜团一直没有解开。

过去完成时:
The building had remained undamaged after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，建筑物依然完好无损。
After the war, many things had remained the same. = 战争结束后，许多事情仍然保持不变。

将来时:
The situation will likely remain unchanged. = 情况很可能会保持不变。
He will remain in charge. = 他将继续负责。

虚拟语气:
If he had remained silent, the situation would have been different. = 如果他保持沉默，情况就会不一样了。
I wish he had remained in the company. = 我希望他留在公司。

被动语态:
The question remained unanswered. = 这个问题没有得到解答。(被动语态中"remained"本身就是被动形式)
The matter remained unresolved. = 这件事仍然悬而未决。(被动语态中"remained"本身就是被动形式)

疑问句:
Did the suspect remain in the country? = 嫌疑犯是否还留在国内？
How long did the peace remain? = 和平持续了多久？


否定句:
He did not remain long. = 他没有待多久。
The situation did not remain stable. = 情况没有保持稳定。
