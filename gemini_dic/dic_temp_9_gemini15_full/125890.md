muscles: /ˈmʌsəlz/ | n. |肌肉|Muscles are tissues in the body that contract and relax to produce movement.|例句：He flexed his muscles to show his strength. 他弯曲他的肌肉来展示他的力量。|近义词：musculature, sinews|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His muscles ache after the workout. = 他锻炼后肌肉酸痛。
The muscles in my legs are strong. = 我腿部的肌肉很强壮。


一般过去时:
She strained her muscles lifting the heavy box. = 她搬那个沉重的箱子时拉伤了肌肉。
The athlete warmed up his muscles before the race. = 运动员在比赛前热身他的肌肉。


现在完成时:
I have built up my muscles through regular exercise. = 我通过规律的锻炼增强了肌肉。
He has injured his muscles playing football. = 他踢足球时弄伤了肌肉。


过去完成时:

He had worked his muscles hard all day. = 他一整天都在努力锻炼肌肉。


将来时:
I will strengthen my muscles with weight training. = 我将通过举重来增强肌肉。

虚拟语气:

I wish I had more defined muscles. = 我希望我有更明显的肌肉。

疑问句:
