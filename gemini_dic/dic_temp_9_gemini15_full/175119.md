USAGE->

一般现在时:
He rafflers tickets every weekend to raise money for his daughter's education. 他每个周末都卖彩票为女儿的教育筹款。
The school rafflers a new car every year. 学校每年都会抽奖一辆新车。


一般过去时:
She raffled off her old bicycle to raise money for a good cause. 她拍卖了她的旧自行车，为慈善事业筹款。
They raffled a painting at the art show. 他们在艺术展上抽奖了一幅画。


现在完成时:
He has raffled off many items in the past year. 他在过去一年里已经拍卖了很多东西。
The organization has raffled numerous prizes this year alone. 仅今年，该组织就抽奖了无数奖品。


过去完成时:
Before the event, they had raffled off most of the prizes. 活动开始前，他们已经抽奖送出了大部分奖品。
By the time the auction ended, they had raffled off almost everything. 拍卖结束时，他们几乎把所有东西都抽奖送出去了。


将来时:
We will raffle a signed football jersey at the upcoming event. 我们将在即将举行的活动中抽奖一件签名足球衫。
They will raffle off the grand prize at the end of the night. 他们将在晚上结束时抽取大奖。


虚拟语气:
If I were a better raffler, I would have sold more tickets. 如果我是一个更好的抽奖者，我会卖出更多彩票。
I wish I had known how to raffle off the items more effectively. 我真希望我知道如何更有效地进行抽奖。


被动语态:
