emblazon:/ɪmˈbleɪzən/| vt.|装饰；显著地显示；以醒目方式展示 |to decorate (something) conspicuously; to display (something) prominently or conspicuously |例句：The shield was emblazoned with the family crest.  盾牌上饰有家族纹章。

例句：The company emblazoned its logo on every product. 公司在其每个产品上都印上了醒目的商标。

近义词：decorate, adorn, display, blazon|反义词：obliterate, conceal, hide|动词
