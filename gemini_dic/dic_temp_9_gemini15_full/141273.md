opaque: /oʊˈpeɪk/ | adj. | 不透明的；难以理解的；迟钝的 | Not able to be seen through; not transparent.  Also, difficult to understand; obscure.  Lacking in understanding or intelligence. |

英文解析：The word "opaque" describes something that does not allow light to pass through it. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is difficult to understand or comprehend, or someone who is slow to understand.

例句：

The glass is opaque, so I can't see through it. = 这个玻璃是不透明的，所以我无法看穿它。

His explanation was opaque, and I didn't understand a word. = 他的解释很晦涩，我一个字也没听懂。


近义词：translucent, obscure, impenetrable, unclear, dense, obtuse

反义词：transparent, clear, lucid, translucent, perspicuous


词性：形容词
