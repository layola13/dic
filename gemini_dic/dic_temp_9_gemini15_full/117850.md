mellowing: /ˈmɛloʊɪŋ/ | v. | 变柔和；变醇厚；成熟；缓和 | The process of becoming softer, smoother, or more gentle; the process of ripening or maturing, often applied to things like sounds, flavors, or personalities.  | 例句：The wine is mellowing nicely in the cellar. 这瓶酒在地窖里陈酿得很好。

The music mellowed as the evening progressed.  随着夜晚的推移，音乐变得柔和了。


近义词：softening, ripening, maturing, moderating, tempering | 反义词：sharpening, hardening, intensifying, exacerbating | 词性：动词
