hearts:/hɑːrts/| n. |心；心脏；红心；感情；勇气；核心|The plural of "heart".  Refers to the organ that pumps blood, emotional feelings, courage, or the central part of something.|例句：My heart aches for you. 我的心为你而痛。
She has a heart of gold. 她有一颗金子般的心。|近义词：core, center, soul|反义词：callousness, cruelty

My heart beats fast. = 我的心跳很快。
Their hearts are full of joy. = 他们的心里充满了喜悦。

一般过去时:
