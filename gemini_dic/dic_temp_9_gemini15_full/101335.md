iodize:/ˈaɪədaɪz/| vt.|用碘处理；加碘于|To treat (something, especially salt) with iodine.  This is typically done to prevent iodine deficiency.|例句：The government iodized salt to prevent goiter. 政府给食盐加碘以预防甲状腺肿大。
例句：We iodized the water to make it safe to drink. 我们给水加碘以使其安全饮用。|近义词：treat, add iodine to|反义词：de-iodize
