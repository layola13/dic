kplr:/ˈkɛplər/| n.|开普勒（人名）|Kepler refers to Johannes Kepler (1571-1630), a prominent German astronomer and mathematician known for his laws of planetary motion.|例句：Kepler's laws of planetary motion revolutionized astronomy. 开普勒的行星运动定律彻底改变了天文学。|近义词：astronomer, mathematician|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is primarily a proper noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  Therefore, grammatical tenses, voice, and negation are not applicable in the same way.  The usage is limited to referring to the person Johannes Kepler or things named after him (like the Kepler space telescope).
