pentacrinus:/ˌpentəˈkraɪnəs/| n. |海百合|A pentacrinus is a type of sea lily, a marine invertebrate animal belonging to the Crinoidea class.  They are characterized by their feathery arms and stalked body.  They are often found in deep waters.|The deep-sea expedition discovered several species of pentacrinus.|近义词:sea lily, crinoid|反义词:None
