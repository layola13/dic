seance:/ˈseɪɑːns/| n. | 降神会；灵媒召魂仪式 | A seance is a meeting, especially one held in dim light, at which people attempt to communicate with the spirits of the dead.  |例句：The medium conducted a seance in a darkened room.  灵媒在一个昏暗的房间里举行了一场降神会。

例句：They held a seance hoping to contact their deceased grandfather. 他们举行了一场降神会，希望能与他们已故的祖父取得联系。


近义词：spiritualist meeting, séance, spiritual sitting | 反义词：None directly applicable. | 词性：名词

词源：
The word "seance" comes from the French word "séance," meaning "sitting," "session," or "meeting."  It entered English in the 19th century, coinciding with the rise of spiritualism.
