postconceptual:/ˌpoʊstˈkɒnseptʃuəl/| adj.| 后概念的；后概念主义的 | Postconceptual art is a broad term encompassing artistic practices that engage with and challenge the legacy of conceptual art.  It often involves a return to materiality and the physicality of art-making, while still retaining some of the intellectual rigor of conceptual art. | Example: The exhibition featured several postconceptual sculptures that explored the relationship between form and meaning.  这场展览展出了几件探索形式与意义之间关系的后概念雕塑。| 近义词：postmodern, contemporary | 反义词：conceptual (in a strict sense)

This word is primarily an adjective describing a style or movement in art.  Therefore, grammatical tense conjugation does not apply in the same way as with verbs.  Instead, we examine its usage in different sentence structures.

Attributive Adjective:
The postconceptual installation captivated the audience.  这件后概念装置艺术作品吸引了观众。

Predicative Adjective:
Her work is decidedly postconceptual in its approach.  她的作品在方法上明显是后概念的。

Comparative and Superlative:
This exhibition is more postconceptual than the last one.  这次展览比上次展览更具后概念性。
This artist's work is the most postconceptual I've ever seen.  这位艺术家的作品是我见过的最具后概念性的作品。
