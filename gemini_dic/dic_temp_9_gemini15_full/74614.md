freckling:/ˈfrɛklɪŋ/| n. |雀斑的出现；生雀斑|The appearance of freckles; the process of developing freckles.  It refers to the gradual development of freckles on the skin, often due to sun exposure.|例句：The summer sun caused freckling across her nose. 夏日的阳光让她鼻子上长满了雀斑。|例句：Her skin showed signs of recent freckling. 她的皮肤显示出最近才长雀斑的迹象。|近义词：speckling (with small spots)|反义词：N/A|名词
