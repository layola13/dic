predistributes:/ˌpriːdɪsˈtrɪbjuːts/| vt.|预先分配，预先分发|To distribute beforehand or in advance.|The company predistributes marketing materials to its sales team before the launch.|近义词：allocate beforehand, preallocate, distribute in advance|反义词：centralize, withhold
