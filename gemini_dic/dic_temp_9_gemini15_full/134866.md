nonmonetarists:/ˌnɒnˈmɒnɪtərɪsts/ | n. |非货币主义者 |Those who disagree with or do not fully subscribe to monetarist economic theories.  They believe that factors other than the money supply, such as aggregate supply and demand, significantly influence economic activity. | Example: Nonmonetarists often criticize the simplistic assumptions of monetarism regarding the relationship between money supply and inflation.  非货币主义者经常批评货币主义关于货币供应量与通货膨胀之间关系的简单假设。|近义词：Keynesians (in contrast)|反义词：monetarists |名词
