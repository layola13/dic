homophobia:/ˌhoʊməˈfoʊbiə/ | n. |恐同症，同性恋恐惧症 |An irrational fear of or aversion to homosexuals.  It's often manifested as prejudice, discrimination, or hatred towards LGBTQ+ individuals. | Example:  His homophobia led him to make offensive remarks about gay people. 他对同性恋的恐惧导致他对同性恋者发表了冒犯性言论。 |近义词：homosexual prejudice, anti-gay sentiment |反义词：homophilia, LGBTQ+ acceptance
