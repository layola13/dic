reticketing:/ˌriːˈtɪkɪtɪŋ/| n. |重新出票；改签|The process of issuing a new ticket, often due to a change in travel plans.  This may involve changing dates, times, or destinations, and may incur additional fees.|例句：The airline offered reticketing after the flight was delayed. 航空公司在航班延误后提供了重新出票服务。

例句：Due to unforeseen circumstances, I had to request reticketing for my flight to London. 由于不可预见的情况，我不得不申请更改飞往伦敦的航班机票。


近义词：rebooking, rescheduling|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
