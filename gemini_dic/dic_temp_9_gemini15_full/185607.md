revengeful: /rɪˈvɛndʒfəl/ | adj. | 怀有报复心的；报复性的 |  Characterized by or seeking revenge; vengeful.  It implies a strong desire for retribution and often suggests a prolonged or persistent feeling of anger and a determination to inflict harm. |


例句：

He harbored revengeful feelings towards his former employer. = 他对他的前任雇主怀有报复之心。

Her revengeful actions stemmed from a deep-seated resentment. = 她的报复行为源于根深蒂固的怨恨。


近义词：vengeful, vindictive, retaliatory, unforgiving

反义词：forgiving, merciful, compassionate


词性：形容词
