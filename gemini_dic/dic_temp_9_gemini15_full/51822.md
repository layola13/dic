dewiest: /ˈduːiɪst/ | adj. | 最潮湿的，最湿润的 |  Most wet; saturated with moisture.  The word is the superlative form of " dewy," meaning covered with or resembling dew. | Example: The dewiest morning of the year saw the grass glistening with moisture.  今年最潮湿的早晨，草地上闪耀着露珠。 | Near synonyms: wettest, dampest, soggiest | Antonyms: driest, arid | adjective
