digits:/ˈdɪdʒɪts/ | n. |数字，位数 |The word "digits" refers to the numerals from 0 to 9, which are used to represent numbers in a decimal system.  It can also refer to fingers or toes. |Example: The password requires eight digits. 密码需要八位数字。  My child is learning to count her digits. 我的孩子正在学习数她的手指。|近义词：numerals, figures|反义词：letters, words
