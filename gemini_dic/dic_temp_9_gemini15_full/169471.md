propaedeutics:/ˌproʊpəˈdjuːtɪks/| n. |预备课程；导论；基础知识 |The preliminary studies or instruction that prepares a student for a more advanced course of study. Propaedeutics often involves foundational knowledge and skills necessary for success in a specific field. For example, in medicine, propaedeutics might refer to the introductory courses and clinical experiences that prepare students for medical practice.  |Example:  The medical school's propaedeutics program included anatomy, physiology, and basic clinical skills.  医学预科课程包括解剖学、生理学和基础临床技能。|近义词：introduction, preliminaries, fundamentals, groundwork |反义词：advanced studies, specialization
