consummate:/ˈkɒnsəmeɪt/ /ˈkɑːnsəmeɪt/ | adj. v. |完美的；完成的；达到顶峰的；完成（婚姻）|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "consummate" means perfect or complete in every respect; supremely skilled.  As a verb, it means to make (a marriage or relationship) complete by having sexual intercourse; to bring to completion or perfection.

例句:
adj. The two companies have a consummate partnership.  两家公司有着完美的合作关系。

adj. He is a consummate professional. 他是一位技艺精湛的专业人士。

v. They consummated their marriage after a long engagement. 他们经过长时间的订婚后完成了他们的婚姻。（暗示性行为）

v.  The deal was consummated last week. 这笔交易在上周达成了。


近义词:
adj.: perfect, complete, excellent, supreme, masterful
v.: complete, finalize, perfect, achieve


反义词:
adj.: imperfect, incomplete, flawed, deficient
v.:  begin, initiate, start


词性:
adj., v.
