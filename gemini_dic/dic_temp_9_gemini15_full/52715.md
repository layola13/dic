digue:/daɪɡ/| n.|堤坝；防波堤|A digue is an embankment or a dam, especially one built to protect low-lying land from the sea.|例句：The village was protected from flooding by a strong digue.  这个村庄受到坚固堤坝的保护，免受洪水侵袭。|近义词：embankment, dam, levee|反义词：None specifically
