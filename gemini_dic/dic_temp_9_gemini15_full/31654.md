chatterbox:/ˈtʃætərˌbɑːks/| n. |喋喋不休的人，话匣子 |A chatterbox is a person who talks a lot, often about unimportant things.  |例句：He's such a chatterbox; I can hardly get a word in edgewise. 他是个话匣子，我几乎插不上话。|例句：My aunt is a real chatterbox, always full of gossip. 我的阿姨是个十足的话匣子，总爱说闲话。|近义词：gossipmonger, blabbermouth, talkative person |反义词：quiet person, reserved person
