polytheist:/ˈpɒliθiːɪst/ /ˈpɑːliθiːɪst/| n. |多神论者|A polytheist is a person who believes in or worships more than one god.  Polytheism is the belief in or worship of more than one god.  This is in contrast to monotheism, which is the belief in only one god.|例句：Many ancient civilizations were polytheistic, with complex pantheons of gods and goddesses.  许多古代文明都是多神论的，拥有复杂的众神和女神体系。|例句：  The ancient Greeks were renowned polytheists, their mythology populated by a vast array of deities. 古代希腊人以多神论著称，他们的神话中充满了大量的各种神灵。|近义词：pagan (in some contexts), idolater|反义词：monotheist
