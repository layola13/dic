pinder:/ˈpɪndər/| n.| 矮胖的人；（尤指）矮胖的马；（方言）小贩 | A pinder is a person, especially a short, stout person; or a small, stout horse; or (dialect) a peddler.  |The old pinder was known for his strong legs.  老矮胖的人以腿粗壮而闻名。|  He rode a sturdy pinder across the fields. 他骑着一匹结实的矮胖马穿过田野。|  近义词：stout person, short person,  small horse| 反义词：tall person, slender person, large horse | 名词
