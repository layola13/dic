reforging:/ˌriːˈfɔːrdʒɪŋ/| v.|重铸；改造；重建|The act of forging again; reshaping or improving something, often metaphorically.|例句：The company is reforging its image after a series of scandals. 公司在经历一系列丑闻后正在重塑形象。例句：He is reforging his life after a period of hardship. 他在经历一段艰难时期后正在重建自己的生活。|近义词：remake, reshape, rebuild, revamp|反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reforging their strategy. = 他们在重塑战略。
The blacksmith reforges swords daily. = 铁匠每天重铸宝剑。

一般过去时:
She reforged her career after a long break. = 她休息很久后重塑了她的职业生涯。

现在完成时:

将来时:
