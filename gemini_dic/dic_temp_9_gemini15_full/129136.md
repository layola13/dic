no-balling:/ˈnoʊˌbɔːlɪŋ/| n. |投出坏球（尤指板球）|The act of bowling a ball in cricket that is illegal according to the laws of the game.  This usually involves the bowler's foot crossing the crease line before the ball is released.  It results in a penalty for the bowling team.|Example: The umpire called a no-balling for overstepping the crease.  裁判因为越过投球线而判罚坏球。|近义词：illegal delivery, foul ball (in baseball)|反义词：fair ball (in baseball), legal delivery
