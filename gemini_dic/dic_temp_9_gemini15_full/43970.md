cricks:/krɪks/ | n. | 颈部或背部的剧烈疼痛；（尤指因肌肉痉挛引起的）疼痛；| A sudden sharp pain in the neck or back, especially due to muscle spasm. | Example: I woke up with a crick in my neck.  我醒来时脖子僵硬疼痛。|近义词：pain, ache, spasm | 反义词：relief, comfort |

A crick in my neck prevents me from turning my head. = 我脖子僵硬，无法转头。
He often gets cricks in his back from sitting at the computer for too long. = 他经常因为长时间坐在电脑前而背部僵硬疼痛。
