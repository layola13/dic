overdignify:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪɡnɪfaɪ/| vt.|过分尊崇；抬高身价；赋予过高的尊严|To treat or regard (someone or something) with excessive dignity or importance.  It often implies an artificial or undeserved elevation.| 例句：The critics overdignified the film, praising its minor virtues while ignoring its major flaws. 批评家们过分抬高了这部电影，赞扬其微不足道的优点，却忽略了其主要的缺点。


例句：He overdignified his simple hobby by calling it "philosophical contemplation." 他把自己的简单爱好说得过于高雅，称之为“哲学沉思”。


近义词：exaggerate, inflate, aggrandize, elevate


反义词：belittle, denigrate, demean, downplay


词性:动词
