everlastingly:/ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋli/ | adv. | 永恒地；永远地 |  Forever and always; for all time.  The adverb emphasizes the unending or continuous nature of something. |例句：He loved her everlastingly. 他永远爱着她。
They will be everlastingly grateful for your help. 他们将永远感激你的帮助。|近义词：eternally, perpetually, forever, always |反义词：temporarily, briefly, shortly
