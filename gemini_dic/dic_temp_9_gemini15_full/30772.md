cesspool:/ˈsɛsˌpuːl/| n. |污水坑；污秽场所；堕落的环境 |A cesspool is an underground tank for collecting sewage.  It can also refer to a place or situation that is morally corrupt or unpleasant. |例句：The old well was a cesspool of stagnant water and disease. 这口旧井是一个污水坑，积满了污水，滋生疾病。

The city's political system has become a cesspool of corruption.  这个城市的政治体制已经变成了一个腐败的污水坑。|近义词：sewer, pit, mire, sinkhole |反义词：paradise, utopia
