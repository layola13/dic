calculate:/ˈkælkjuleɪt/ | vt. |计算，推算，估计 |To determine (the amount, number, size, etc.) of something by using mathematical processes; to compute.  |例句：I need to calculate the total cost of the project. 我需要计算项目的总成本。|例句：She calculated the distance between the two cities. 她计算了两个城市之间的距离。|近义词：compute, reckon, estimate |反义词：guess (in the sense of lacking precision)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I calculate the area of the rectangle every day. = 我每天计算矩形的面积。
We calculate the budget carefully before each project. = 我们在每个项目之前都仔细计算预算。

一般过去时:
They calculated the trajectory of the rocket. = 他们计算了火箭的轨迹。
She calculated the risk before making the investment. = 她在投资前计算了风险。

现在完成时:
I have calculated the total expenses for this year. = 我已经计算出今年的总支出。
They have calculated the probability of success. = 他们已经计算出成功的概率。

过去完成时:
He had calculated the amount of materials needed before starting the construction. = 他在开始施工前已经计算出了所需的材料数量。
We had calculated the travel time, but we were still late. = 我们已经计算了旅行时间，但我们仍然迟到了。

将来时:
I will calculate the results tomorrow. = 我明天将计算结果。
They will calculate the impact of the policy changes. = 他们将计算政策变化的影响。

虚拟语气:
If I calculated correctly, the answer should be 10. = 如果我计算正确，答案应该是10。
I wish I had calculated the risk more accurately. = 我希望我当时更准确地计算了风险。

被动语态:
The area was calculated by using a complex formula. = 面积是用一个复杂的公式计算出来的。
The budget was calculated by the finance department. = 预算是由财务部门计算的。

疑问句:
How do you calculate the average speed? = 你如何计算平均速度？
How is the profit margin calculated? = 利润率是如何计算的？

否定句:
I cannot calculate the exact figures without more data. = 没有更多数据，我无法计算出确切的数字。
They did not calculate the potential losses. = 他们没有计算潜在的损失。


PHRASE->

calculate something up:  计算出总数
carefully calculate: 仔细计算
