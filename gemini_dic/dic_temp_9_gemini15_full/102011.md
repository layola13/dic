isodulcite:/ˌaɪsoʊˈdʌlsɪt/| n. |异构糖；松鼠糖|A rare sugar found in some plants, specifically a type of hexose sugar. It is a stereoisomer of other hexoses, meaning it has the same chemical formula but a different arrangement of atoms in space.  It is also known as fucose.  |例句：Isodulcite is a less common sugar compared to glucose or fructose. 异构糖与葡萄糖或果糖相比，是一种不太常见的糖。|近义词：fucose, 6-deoxygalactose |反义词：无|
