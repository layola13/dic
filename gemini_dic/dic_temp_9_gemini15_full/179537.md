reelected:/ˌriːɪˈlɛktɪd/| adj.|再次当选的，连任的|Having been elected again to an office.|例句：The incumbent president was reelected by a landslide.  现任总统以压倒性优势再次当选。

例句：She was reelected to the city council. 她再次当选市议员。


近义词：re-elected, returned (to office)

反义词：defeated, ousted


词性:形容词 (过去分词作形容词)


词源:

* **re-**:  prefix meaning "again" or "back"
* **elect**: verb meaning "to choose or appoint by vote"


The word "reelected" is formed by adding the prefix "re-" to the past participle "elected" of the verb "elect".  It describes someone who has been chosen for a position a second (or more) time.
