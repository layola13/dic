nonsubscription:/ˌnɒn.səbˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. |非订阅；非会员；免费 |The term "nonsubscription" refers to something that does not require a subscription or membership.  It indicates access or availability without the need to pay a recurring fee or sign up for a service. |Example:  The website offers nonsubscription access to its basic features.  这个网站提供其基本功能的免费访问。  The library provides nonsubscription access to its online catalog. 图书馆提供其在线目录的免费访问。|近义词：free access, open access, free trial|反义词：subscription, membership
