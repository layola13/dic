fairy:/ˈfɛəri/| n. |仙女，妖精|A mythical being, typically female, with magical powers; often depicted as having wings and a beautiful appearance.  Fairies are often associated with nature and folklore.  |Example: The children believed in fairies and left offerings for them in the garden. 孩子们相信有仙女，并在花园里留下了供奉给她们的祭品。|近义词：sprite, elf, nymph|反义词：human, mortal
