mechanist:/ˈmɛkənɪst/| n. |机械论者；机械师|A mechanist is someone who believes in mechanism, the philosophical theory that all phenomena, including mental states, can be explained in terms of physical or mechanical principles.  They also may be a skilled worker who repairs or makes machinery. |例句：He is a skilled mechanist, capable of repairing complex machinery. 他是一位熟练的机械师，能够修理复杂的机器。He is a staunch mechanist, believing that consciousness is simply a product of physical processes. 他是一个坚定的机械论者，相信意识仅仅是物理过程的产物。|近义词：mechanic, engineer, materialist |反义词：idealist, vitalist
