incapacities:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsɪtiz/| n.|无能力，无力，残疾|The plural form of "incapacity," referring to multiple instances of lacking the ability to do something.  It can refer to physical, mental, or legal inability. |例句：His incapacities prevented him from working.  他的残疾妨碍了他工作。 |例句：The company's incapacities in marketing led to its failure. 公司在市场营销方面的无能导致了它的失败。|近义词：disabilities, handicaps, impairments, deficiencies |反义词：abilities, capabilities, competences
