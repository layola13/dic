monisms:/ˈmɒnɪzəm/| n. |一元论；唯心论；一元主义|The philosophical view that attributes oneness or unity to the basic principle of reality, holding that all things are manifestations of a single substance, force, or principle.  Monism contrasts with dualism and pluralism.  It can be materialistic (everything is matter), idealistic (everything is mind), or neutral (everything is a neutral substance).|例句：Many Eastern religions embrace a form of monism, believing in a single, underlying reality. 许多东方宗教都信奉某种形式的一元论，相信存在一个单一的、根本的现实。

例句：His philosophical position is a form of monism, reducing all phenomena to physical processes.他的哲学立场是一种一元论，将所有现象都简化为物理过程。


近义词：unitarianism,  idealism (in some contexts), materialism (in some contexts)|反义词：dualism, pluralism|名词
