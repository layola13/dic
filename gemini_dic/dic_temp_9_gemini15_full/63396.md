entozoal:/ˌɛntoʊˈzoʊəl/| adj.|内寄生虫的；内寄生的|Relating to or caused by entozoa (internal parasites).  The term refers to parasites that live within the body of an animal, such as worms, flukes, and protozoa.|例句：The veterinarian diagnosed an entozoal infection in the dog.  兽医诊断出这条狗感染了内寄生虫。

The study focused on the entozoal diseases prevalent in the region. 这项研究的重点是该地区普遍存在的内寄生虫疾病。|近义词：parasitic, endoparasitic|反义词：ectozoal (外寄生的)
