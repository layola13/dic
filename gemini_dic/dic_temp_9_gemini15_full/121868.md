misrewarded:/ˌmɪs.rɪˈwɔːr.dɪd/| adj.| 奖赏不当的，报酬不足的 | Improperly or inadequately rewarded; deserving of better treatment or compensation.  |The misrewarded employee felt undervalued and underappreciated.  这位报酬不公的员工感到自己被低估和不被重视。| underpaid, undercompensated | fairly rewarded, justly compensated | 形容词


USAGE->
