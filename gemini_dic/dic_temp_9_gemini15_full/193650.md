self-catering: /ˌsɛlfˈkeɪtərɪŋ/ | adj. | 自助式餐饮的；自理伙食的 |  Relating to or denoting accommodation where guests provide their own food.  | 例句：We rented a self-catering apartment for our holiday.  我们租了一套自助式公寓度假。

例句：The self-catering cottage was fully equipped with a kitchen and cooking utensils. 这间自助式小屋配备了齐全的厨房用具。


近义词：self-provisioning


反义词：full-board, all-inclusive


词性：形容词
