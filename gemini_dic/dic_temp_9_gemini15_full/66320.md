excreted:/ɪkˈskriːtɪd/| v. |排泄的，分泌的 |To separate and eliminate waste products from the body; to secrete a substance.|例句：The kidneys excreted the toxins from his blood. 肾脏排出了他血液中的毒素。
The glands excreted hormones into the bloodstream. 腺体将荷尔蒙分泌到血液中。|近义词：eliminated, discharged, secreted|反义词：absorbed, retained|动词
