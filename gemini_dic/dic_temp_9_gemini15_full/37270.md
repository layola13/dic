comm:/kɒm/| /kɑːm/ | n. |通讯；交流；委员会；命令；商品|communication, exchange of information, a group of people appointed for a specific function, an order or instruction, goods or merchandise|例句：The comm system failed during the mission. 任务期间通讯系统失效了。  例句：The committee will discuss the matter next week. 委员会下周将讨论此事。|近义词：communication, correspondence, committee, order, merchandise|反义词：silence, secrecy, isolation


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The committee communicates regularly. = 委员会定期沟通。
He commutes to work every day. = 他每天通勤上班。


一般过去时:
The soldiers communicated by radio. = 士兵们通过无线电进行通讯。
The committee decided on a new policy. = 委员会决定了一项新政策。


现在完成时:
They have communicated their concerns to the manager. = 他们已经向经理表达了他们的担忧。
The committee has already made its decision. = 委员会已经做出了决定。


过去完成时:
They had communicated their plans before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经沟通了他们的计划。
The committee had already met several times before the final vote. = 在最终投票之前，委员会已经开过几次会了。


将来时:
We will communicate our findings in the report. = 我们将在报告中说明我们的发现。
The committee will meet next week to discuss the budget. = 委员会下周将开会讨论预算。


虚拟语气:
If they communicated better, they would have avoided the conflict. = 如果他们沟通得更好，他们就能避免冲突。
It is important that the committee reach a consensus. = 重要的是委员会要达成共识。


被动语态:
The information was communicated effectively. = 信息得到了有效的沟通。
The decision was made by the committee. = 决定是由委员会做出的。


疑问句:

When will the committee announce its decision? = 委员会什么时候宣布决定？


否定句:

The committee did not reach a consensus. = 委员会没有达成共识。
