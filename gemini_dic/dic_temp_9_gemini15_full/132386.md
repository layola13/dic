nondemocracies:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈmɒkrəsiːz/| n. |非民主国家；非民主政体|Plural of nondemocracy.  Refers to countries or political systems that are not democratic, lacking features like free and fair elections, protection of civil liberties, and the rule of law.  They may be authoritarian, totalitarian, or otherwise undemocratic. | Example: Many nondemocracies suppress dissent and limit freedom of speech.  许多非民主国家压制异见并限制言论自由。|近义词：authoritarian regimes, dictatorships, totalitarian states |反义词：democracies
