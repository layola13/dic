self-deifying: /ˌsɛlfˈdiːɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我神化；自命不凡的 |  Describing someone or something that elevates themselves to a godlike status, often through arrogance or excessive pride.  It implies an unwarranted sense of self-importance and a disregard for others. | 例句：His self-deifying pronouncements alienated many of his former supporters.  他的自我神化言论疏远了他许多以前的支持者。


例句：The cult leader's self-deifying rhetoric convinced his followers of his divine authority.  邪教领袖自我神化的言辞使他的信徒相信了他的神圣权威。


近义词：narcissistic, egotistical, self-aggrandizing, vainglorious, grandiose


反义词：humble, modest, self-effacing, unassuming
