词源:

The word "reordination" is derived from the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back") and the noun "ordination," which ultimately comes from the Latin word "ordinatio," meaning "arrangement" or "ordering."  Therefore, "reordination" literally means "a re-arranging" or "a re-ordering."
