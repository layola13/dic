proforma:/ˈproʊfɔːrmə/| adj., n.|形式上的；（尤指商业上的）形式发票，估价单|A pro forma invoice is a preliminary invoice sent to a customer before the goods or services are provided. It outlines the terms of sale and the expected cost.  It is not a request for immediate payment.| pro forma invoice, pro forma quotation, pro forma contract |  non-binding invoice | adj.

The company sends out pro forma invoices to clients. = 公司向客户发送形式发票。
This pro forma document is not legally binding. = 这份形式文件不具有法律约束力。
