ingeniously:/ɪnˈdʒiːnjəsli/ | adj. |巧妙地；有独创性地 |  Showing skill and inventiveness.  It suggests cleverness and originality in the way something is done or created. |例句：He ingeniously solved the complex problem. 他巧妙地解决了这个复杂的问题。

例句：The engineer ingeniously designed a system to conserve energy.  工程师巧妙地设计了一个节能系统。


近义词：cleverly, skillfully, cleverly, inventively, resourcefully, brilliantly


反义词：clumsily, ineptly, awkwardly


词源：源自ingenious (adj.) + -ly (adv. suffix)。ingenious  来自拉丁语 *ingēniōsus，由 *ingēnium（才能，天赋）派生而来。
