imaginarily:/ɪˈmædʒɪnərili/ | adv. | 想象地；虚构地 | In a way that is imagined rather than real.  It describes something that exists only in the mind or imagination. |例句：He imaginarily transported himself to a tropical island. 他想象着自己被传送到了一个热带岛屿。

例句：She imaginarily conversed with her deceased grandmother. 她想象着和去世的祖母交谈。


近义词：fancifully, hypothetically, theoretically | 反义词：really, actually, truly | 词性: 副词
