impasto:/ɪmˈpɑːstoʊ/| n.|  厚涂法；浓彩法 |A technique used in painting, where paint is laid on an area of the surface very thickly, usually thick enough that the brush or painting-knife strokes are visible.  It is often used to create texture and depth. |Example: The artist used impasto to create a sense of movement in the painting.  这位艺术家用厚涂法在绘画中营造出动感。 |近义词：thick application, heavy application, textural painting |反义词：thin application, smooth application |名词
