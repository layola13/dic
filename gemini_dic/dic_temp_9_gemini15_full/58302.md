dry-cleaning:/ˈdraɪˌkliːnɪŋ/| n.|干洗|The process of cleaning clothes and fabrics using solvents other than water.|例句：I need to take my suit to the dry cleaner. 我需要把我的西装拿去干洗。|近义词：dry cleaning service, professional cleaning|反义词：laundering, washing


USAGE->

I dry-clean my suits every month. = 我每个月都干洗我的西装。
She dry-cleans her delicate dresses. = 她干洗她精致的连衣裙。
