infighter:/ˈɪnfaɪtər/| n. |内线拳击手；近身搏斗者；好斗的人|A person who fights at close quarters, especially in boxing.  An infighter relies on close-range punches and body blows, often employing aggressive tactics.  They are typically shorter and stockier than outfighters.|例句：He's a relentless infighter, always pressing forward and landing punishing blows. 他是一个不知疲倦的内线拳击手，总是向前逼近并打出惩罚性的打击。

例句：The small but powerful infighter dominated the match with his relentless pressure.  这位身材矮小但强壮的近身搏斗者凭借其不懈的压力主导了比赛。


近义词：brawler, slugger, close-quarters fighter | 反义词：outfighter


词源: in- (表示“在里面”， “进入”) + fighter (拳击手，斗士)
