flips:/flɪps/ | vt. n. |轻弹；翻转；快速移动；筋斗；突然改变| To move or turn something over quickly and lightly.  Also, a quick, sudden movement or change. |例句：He flips pancakes with ease. 他轻松地翻煎饼。

The chef flipped the omelette. 厨师翻动了煎蛋卷。


近义词：turn, toss, invert,  (for sudden change) reverse, switch


反义词： (for turning) stay put, remain unchanged, (for a sudden change) maintain, continue


词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flips the coin every time he makes a decision. = 他每次做决定都抛硬币。
She flips through the magazine quickly. = 她快速地翻阅杂志。


一般过去时:
I flipped the switch and the lights went on. = 我拨动开关，灯亮了。
He flipped the pancake in the air. = 他把煎饼抛向空中。


现在完成时:
She has flipped several houses for profit. = 她已经翻新了几套房子以获利。
He has flipped his hair back many times today. = 他今天已经多次把头发向后甩。


过去完成时:
Before the accident, he had flipped his car several times. = 事故发生前，他的车翻了好几圈。
They had flipped the script before the final rehearsal. = 他们在最后一次彩排前修改了剧本。


将来时:
I will flip the page when I’m ready. = 我准备好后就会翻页。
She will flip the house and sell it next year. = 她明年将翻新这栋房子再出售。


虚拟语气:
If I had flipped the switch, the lights would have gone on. = 如果我拨动开关，灯就会亮。
I wish I had flipped the coin earlier. = 我真希望我早点抛硬币。


被动语态:
The pancake was flipped perfectly. = 煎饼翻得完美无缺。
The switch was flipped by accident. = 开关是不小心拨动的。


疑问句:
Did he flip the coin fairly? = 他抛硬币公平吗？
Will you flip the channel for me? = 你能帮我换频道吗？


否定句:
He didn't flip the pancake properly. = 他没有把煎饼翻好。
She didn't flip the page. = 她没有翻页。


PHRASE->
flip out = 变得疯狂；抓狂
flip through = 快速翻阅
flip side = 反面；另一方面
flip-flop = 改变主意；拖鞋
flip the bird = 给某人竖中指
