negotiating:/nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 谈判；协商；洽谈 | The act of engaging in discussion with another person or group in order to reach an agreement.  It involves compromise and the exchange of proposals to find a mutually acceptable solution. | Example:  They are negotiating a new contract. 他们在协商一份新合同。

They are currently negotiating the terms of the peace treaty. 他们目前正在协商和平条约的条款。


近义词：bargaining, mediating, discussing, conferring, haggling

反义词：dictating, demanding, imposing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is negotiating a better salary. 他正在协商更高的薪水。
They are negotiating the sale of their house.  他们在协商卖房事宜。


一般过去时:
She negotiated a lower price for the car. 她协商到了更低的购车价。
We negotiated a peace agreement with the rebels. 我们与叛军协商达成了和平协议。


现在完成时:
They have negotiated a successful trade deal. 他们已经成功地谈判达成了一项贸易协议。
She has been negotiating with the supplier for weeks. 她已经和供应商协商了几周了。


过去完成时:
He had negotiated the terms before the meeting. 他在会议之前就已经协商好了条款。
They had negotiated a ceasefire before the fighting stopped.  战斗停止之前，他们已经协商停火了。


将来时:
We will be negotiating with the bank for a loan. 我们将与银行协商贷款事宜。
They will negotiate a new contract next week. 他们下周将协商一份新合同。


虚拟语气:
If they negotiated in good faith, they might have reached an agreement. 如果他们真诚地谈判，他们或许就能达成协议。
I wish I had negotiated a better deal. 我真希望我当时能谈个更好的条件。


被动语态:
The contract is being negotiated by both parties. 合同正在由双方协商。
A new trade agreement was negotiated last year. 去年谈判达成了一项新的贸易协定。


疑问句:
Are they still negotiating? 他们还在谈判吗？
What are you negotiating with them about? 你跟他们协商什么？


否定句:
He did not negotiate a good deal. 他没有谈到一个好条件。
They refused to negotiate with the terrorists. 他们拒绝与恐怖分子谈判。


PHRASE->
negotiate a deal: 达成协议
negotiate a price: 协商价格
negotiate a contract: 协商合同
negotiate a settlement: 协商和解
negotiate terms: 协商条款

词源:
From Latin *negotiāri ("to carry on business," "to traffic"), from negotium ("business," "occupation," "trouble").  The word's evolution reflects the often complex and sometimes troublesome nature of business dealings and agreements.
