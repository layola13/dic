cabbages:/ˈkæbɪdʒɪz/| n.|卷心菜，洋白菜|Plural of cabbage.  A leafy, usually green vegetable with a firm, round head.  |例句：We had stewed cabbages for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了炖卷心菜。

例句：The farmer grew many cabbages in his field. 这个农民在他的田里种了很多卷心菜。


近义词：cole, kale (部分情况)


反义词：无


词性:名词(复数)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer grows cabbages. = 农民种植卷心菜。
They sell cabbages at the market. = 他们在市场上卖卷心菜。


一般过去时:
We bought some cabbages yesterday. = 我们昨天买了些卷心菜。
She cooked cabbages with potatoes. = 她用土豆炖了卷心菜。


现在完成时:
I have never eaten so many cabbages before. = 我以前从未吃过这么多卷心菜。
They have grown cabbages for many years. = 他们种植卷心菜已经很多年了。


过去完成时:
They had already harvested the cabbages before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经收割了卷心菜。
We had eaten all the cabbages by the time the guests arrived. = 客人到达时，我们已经把所有的卷心菜都吃掉了。


将来时:

词源:
From Old French *cabel, ultimately from Latin *capitum (plural of caput "head").  The word refers to the head-like shape of the vegetable.
