cottoner:/ˈkɒtənə(r)/|/ˈkɑːtənər/| n. |棉农；棉花种植者；棉纺厂工人 | A cottoner is a person who grows, processes, or works with cotton.  They may be involved in cultivating cotton plants, harvesting the cotton bolls, or working in a cotton mill. | Example:  Many cottoners in the south lost their jobs after the boll weevil infestation.  许多南方棉农在棉铃象甲虫侵害后失业了。 |近义词: cotton farmer, cotton worker, cotton gin operator|反义词: N/A
