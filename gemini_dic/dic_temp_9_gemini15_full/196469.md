servage:/ˈsɜːvɪdʒ/ | n. | 农奴身份；农奴制度；奴役 |  The state of being a serf; the system of serfdom; servitude.  It refers to the condition of being bound to the land and obligated to work for a lord in medieval Europe.  | Example: The peasants lived under brutal servage, bound to the land and their lord.  农奴们在残酷的农奴制度下生活，被束缚在土地和他们的领主身上。 | 近义词：serfdom, bondage, servitude, thraldom | 反义词：freedom, liberty, emancipation |  n.
