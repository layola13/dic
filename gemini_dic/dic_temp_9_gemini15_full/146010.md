overdoubt: /ˌoʊvərˈdaʊt/ | v. | 过分怀疑，过度怀疑 | To doubt excessively or excessively.  To have too many doubts about something. |例句：He overdoubted his own abilities, leading to self-doubt and inaction. 他过分怀疑自己的能力，导致自我怀疑和无所作为。


例句：Don't overdoubt your instincts; sometimes, trusting your gut feeling is the best approach. 不要过分怀疑你的直觉；有时候，相信你的直觉是最好的方法。


近义词：hyperdoubt, overquestion, overscruple


反义词：trust, believe, have faith in


词性：动词
