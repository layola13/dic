raptor:/ˈræptər/| n. |猛禽，掠食性鸟类|A raptor is a bird of prey, typically having a hooked beak and talons.  They are carnivorous and hunt other animals for food.  Examples include eagles, hawks, falcons, and owls.|例句：The golden eagle is a powerful raptor known for its hunting prowess.  金雕是一种以其狩猎能力而闻名的强大的猛禽。|例句：Many raptors migrate long distances to find food and breeding grounds.  许多猛禽会迁徙很远的距离以寻找食物和繁殖地。|近义词：bird of prey, predator|反义词：prey
