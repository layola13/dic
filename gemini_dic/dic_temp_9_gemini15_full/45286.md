culture-shocked: /ˈkʌltʃərʃɒkt/ | adj. | 文化冲击的，文化震惊的 | Feeling disoriented, confused, or anxious when experiencing an unfamiliar culture.  This feeling arises from encountering vastly different customs, beliefs, and social norms than one is used to.  The experience can range from mild discomfort to significant psychological distress. | 例句：She was culture-shocked by the pace of life in Tokyo. 她对东京的生活节奏感到文化冲击。

例句： After spending a year in a remote village, he felt culture-shocked returning to the bustling city.  在偏远村庄生活一年后，回到繁华的城市，他感到文化冲击。


近义词：disoriented, discombobulated, overwhelmed, bewildered, alienated

反义词：comfortable, at ease, familiarized


词性:形容词
