luncheonette:/ˌlʌnʃəˈnɛt/| n. |小餐馆，快餐店 | A small restaurant, typically one that serves simple, inexpensive meals.  |例句：She grabbed a quick lunch at the luncheonette on the corner. = 她在那条街角的小餐馆里匆匆吃了个午饭。|例句：The luncheonette offered a variety of sandwiches and coffee. = 这家小餐馆提供各种三明治和咖啡。|近义词：diner, cafe, snack bar |反义词：restaurant (in the sense of upscale or formal)|词性：名词
