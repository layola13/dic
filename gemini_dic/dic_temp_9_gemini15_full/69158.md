fawned:/fɔːnd/| v.| 奉承；讨好；(动物) 驯服地依偎 |To try to please someone by behaving in a very submissive way.  |例句：She fawned over the movie star, hoping to get an autograph. 她奉承电影明星，希望能得到签名。

例句：The dog fawned on its owner, wagging its tail excitedly.  这只狗依偎着它的主人，兴奋地摇着尾巴。


近义词：flatter,  coax,  ingratiate


反义词：ignore, snub, disdain


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fawns over his boss to get a promotion. = 他奉承老板以求升职。
She constantly fawns on the wealthy. = 她不断地讨好富人。


一般过去时:
The puppy fawned at my feet. = 小狗依偎在我的脚边。
He fawned over the critic to get a good review. = 他讨好评论家以获得好评。


现在完成时:
She has fawned over him for years. = 她多年来一直奉承他。
They have fawned on the new CEO since his arrival. = 自从新任首席执行官到来以来，他们一直奉承他。


过去完成时:
He had fawned on the director for months before getting the part. = 在得到这个角色之前，他几个月来一直在奉承导演。
She had fawned over the celebrity all evening. = 她整个晚上都在奉承那位名人。


将来时:
I think she will fawn over him at the party. = 我认为她会在派对上奉承他。
They will fawn on their new supervisor. = 他们会讨好他们的新主管。


虚拟语气:
If I fawned over him, I might get the job. = 如果我奉承他，我可能会得到这份工作。
I wish I hadn’t fawned over him; it was embarrassing. = 我真希望我没有奉承他；那太尴尬了。


被动语态:
He was fawned over by the entire team. = 他受到整个团队的奉承。
The politician was fawned upon by his supporters. = 这位政治家受到支持者的奉承。


疑问句:
Does she fawn over everyone she meets? = 她会奉承她遇到的每一个人吗？
Did he fawn over the professor to get a better grade? = 他是否奉承教授以获得更好的成绩？


否定句:
He didn’t fawn over the manager; he earned his promotion. = 他没有奉承经理；他是凭自己的努力获得晋升的。
She doesn't fawn over celebrities; she treats everyone equally. = 她不奉承名人；她平等对待每一个人。


PHRASE->
fawn over someone = 奉承某人
fawn upon someone = 讨好某人
fawn on someone = 巴结某人
fawn like a dog = 像狗一样依偎
fawn all over someone = 竭尽全力讨好某人


词源:
The word "fawn" as a verb ultimately derives from the Old French word "fauner," meaning "to act like a fawn" (a young deer).  The image is of a young deer behaving submissively, which is why the verb came to describe obsequious behavior in humans.
