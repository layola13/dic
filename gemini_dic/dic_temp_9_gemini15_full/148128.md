overseeing: /ˌoʊvərˈsiːɪŋ/ | v. | 监督；管理；照管 |  To oversee something means to supervise or manage it, making sure that it is done correctly.  It implies a higher level of responsibility and often involves ensuring that others are completing tasks effectively. |


例句：

The project manager is overseeing the construction of the new building.  = 项目经理正在监督新建筑的施工。

She oversaw the implementation of the new company policy. = 她监督了新公司政策的实施。


近义词：supervising, managing, monitoring, directing, supervising


反义词：neglecting, ignoring, disregarding


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He oversees the daily operations of the company. = 他负责公司的日常运营。
She oversees a team of ten designers. = 她负责一个由十位设计师组成的团队。

一般过去时:
He oversaw the completion of the project. = 他监督了项目的完成。
She oversaw the transition to the new system. = 她监督了向新系统的过渡。

现在完成时:
She has overseen many successful projects in her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经成功地监督了许多项目。
He has overseen the development of the new software. = 他一直负责新软件的开发。

过去完成时:
The manager had overseen three projects before taking on this one. = 在接手这个项目之前，经理已经监督了三个项目。
They had overseen the construction of the bridge for five years. = 他们已经监督了桥梁的建设五年了。

将来时:
She will oversee the merger of the two companies. = 她将监督两家公司的合并。
He will oversee the implementation of the new strategy. = 他将负责实施新战略。

虚拟语气:
If he were to oversee the project, it would be completed on time. = 如果他来监督这个项目，它就能按时完成。
I wish someone experienced would oversee this critical phase. = 我希望有经验的人能监督这个关键阶段。

被动语态:
The project was overseen by a highly experienced manager. = 该项目由一位经验丰富的经理负责监督。
The implementation of the new system is being overseen by a dedicated team. = 新系统的实施由一个专门的团队负责监督。

疑问句:
Who is overseeing the investigation? = 谁在监督调查？

否定句:

The board did not oversee the company's finances effectively. = 董事会没有有效地监督公司的财务。
