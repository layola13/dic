overvalued:/ˌoʊvərˈvæljuːd/ | adj. | 估价过高的；被高估的 |  Something is overvalued when its price or worth is considered higher than its actual value.  It implies an inflated assessment, often due to speculation, market sentiment, or misjudgment. | Example: The stock market is currently overvalued, and a correction is expected soon.  股票市场目前估值过高，预计很快就会进行调整。  The artwork was overvalued at auction. 这件艺术品在拍卖会上估价过高。|近义词：inflated, overpriced, exaggerated, exorbitant |反义词：undervalued, underpriced
