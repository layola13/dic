recompensate:/ˌrekəmˈpɛnseɪt/| vt.|补偿，酬谢|to give someone something, especially money, in return for something they have done or suffered:|The company will compensate employees for the extra hours they worked. 公司将补偿员工加班的额外时间。|近义词：reimburse, repay, reward|反义词：deprive, penalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company compensates its employees fairly. = 公司公平地补偿其员工。
He compensates for his lack of experience with hard work. = 他用努力工作来弥补他经验的不足。


一般过去时:
They compensated me for the damaged goods. = 他们赔偿了我损坏的货物。
She compensated her friend for the lost money. = 她赔偿了朋友丢失的钱。


现在完成时:
The government has compensated the victims of the flood. = 政府已经赔偿了洪灾的受害者。
He has compensated for his mistakes. = 他已经弥补了他的错误。


过去完成时:
They had compensated the workers before the strike. = 罢工前他们已经补偿了工人们。
She had compensated him for his losses before leaving. = 离开之前她已经赔偿了他的损失。


将来时:
The insurance company will compensate you for the damage. = 保险公司将赔偿你的损失。
They will compensate the employees for their overtime. = 他们将补偿员工的加班费。


虚拟语气:
If the company compensated its employees better, they would be more productive. = 如果公司更好地补偿员工，他们将会更有生产力。
I wish they had compensated me for the inconvenience. = 我希望他们已经赔偿了我因为不便所受的损失。


被动语态:
The employees were compensated for their extra work. = 员工因额外工作而得到了补偿。
The victims will be compensated for their losses. = 受害者将获得损失赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the company compensate me for the delay? = 公司会赔偿我因延误造成的损失吗？
How will they compensate for the damage? = 他们将如何弥补损失？


否定句:
The company did not compensate the employees for their overtime. = 公司没有补偿员工的加班费。
They refused to compensate for the damages. = 他们拒绝赔偿损失。


PHRASE->
compensate for = 弥补
compensate somebody for something = 赔偿某人因某事造成的损失
fully compensate = 全额赔偿
adequately compensate = 足够赔偿
compensate financially = 经济补偿
