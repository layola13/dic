compliant: /kəmˈplaɪənt/ | adj. | 顺从的；服从的；符合的 |  Willing to comply with requests or instructions; conforming to rules or standards.  |例句：He was always compliant and willing to help. 他总是很顺从，乐于助人。

例句：The new building is compliant with all safety regulations. 这座新建筑符合所有安全规定。


近义词：obedient, submissive, docile, conforming, agreeable | 反义词：rebellious, defiant, disobedient, intractable, recalcitrant

词源:  From Middle French *complaisant (now complaisant), from Old French complaisant, present participle of complaire "to please," from com- "together" + plaire "to please" (from Latin placere).  The sense shifted from "pleasing" to "compliant" over time.
