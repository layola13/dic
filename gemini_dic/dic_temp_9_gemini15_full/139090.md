obbligatos:/ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtoʊz/| n.|  义务乐段；必奏乐段 | An obbligato is a musical part that is essential to the performance of a piece and must be played.  It's not optional. It is often a melodically prominent part, played by a solo instrument or voice that interacts with the main melody.  | Example: The oboe obbligato in the Mozart concerto is a highlight of the piece.  莫扎特协奏曲中的双簧管义务乐段是该乐曲的亮点。|近义词：essential part, required part |反义词：ad lib, optional part |名词
