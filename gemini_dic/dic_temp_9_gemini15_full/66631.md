existence:/ɪɡˈzɪstəns/ | n. |存在，生存；实存；现实 |The existence of God is a matter of faith.  神的存在是一个信仰问题。|  The book explores the existence of different cultures. 这本书探讨了不同文化的存在。|近义词：being, reality, presence |反义词：nonexistence, nothingness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The universe exists. = 宇宙存在。
My problem exists because of my carelessness. = 我的问题是因为我的粗心造成的。


一般过去时:
Dinosaurs existed millions of years ago. = 恐龙在数百万年前存在。
The company existed for over 50 years before it went bankrupt. = 这家公司破产前存在了50多年。


现在完成时:
Humans have existed for a long time. = 人类已经存在很长时间了。
The problem has existed since the beginning of the project. = 这个问题从项目开始就一直存在。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of the wheel, different modes of transport existed. = 在发明轮子之前，存在着不同的交通方式。
The evidence suggested that the civilization had existed long before the previous estimations. = 证据表明，这个文明的存在时间比之前的估计要长得多。


将来时:
Will humanity still exist in a thousand years? = 一千年后人类还会存在吗？
The company will likely exist for many more years to come. = 这家公司很可能会继续存在很多年。


虚拟语气:
If unicorns existed, the world would be a more magical place. = 如果独角兽存在，世界将会更神奇。
I wish I could change the existence of poverty in our society. = 我希望我能改变我们社会中贫困的存在。


被动语态:
The theory was proven to exist. = 理论被证明存在。
His existence was threatened by the war. = 他的生存受到了战争的威胁。


疑问句:
Does life exist on other planets? = 其他星球上存在生命吗？
Does the problem still exist? = 这个问题还存在吗？


否定句:
Ghosts do not exist. = 鬼魂不存在。
There is no evidence to suggest that the rumor exists. = 没有证据表明谣言存在。


PHRASE->
into existence: 进入存在；产生
out of existence: 不复存在；消失
come into existence: 产生；出现
bring into existence: 使产生；创造
in existence: 现存的；存在的
