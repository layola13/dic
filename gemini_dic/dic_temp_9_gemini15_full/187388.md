rodomontador:/ˌrɒdəmɒnˈtɑːdɔːr/| n.|吹牛者，夸夸其谈者|A rodomontador is a person who boasts or brags excessively and often about things that are not true. They are characterized by their inflated self-importance and tendency to exaggerate their accomplishments.  |例句：He was a notorious rodomontador, always exaggerating his exploits. 他是个臭名昭著的吹牛大王，总是夸大自己的功绩。|近义词：boaster, braggart, swaggerer, show-off|反义词：humble person, modest person

词源:
