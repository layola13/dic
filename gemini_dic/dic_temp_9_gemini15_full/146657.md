overhastening: /ˌoʊvərˈheɪsənɪŋ/ | n. | 过于匆忙；操之过急 | The act of doing something too quickly or prematurely, often resulting in mistakes or inefficiency.  | 例句：The overhastening of the project led to several costly errors.  项目的操之过急导致了一些代价高昂的错误。

例句：His overhastening to finish the report resulted in numerous factual inaccuracies. 他为了赶报告而过于匆忙，导致了许多事实上的不准确之处。


近义词：precipitation, rashness, haste, hurry,  prematureness


反义词： deliberation, carefulness, slowness, patience


词性: 名词
