overtruthful: /ˌoʊvərˈtruːθfəl/ | adj. | 过分诚实的，过于坦率的 | Excessively truthful; more truthful than is necessary or desirable, often to the point of being tactless or indiscreet.  |  He was overtruthful in his criticism, hurting her feelings unintentionally. 他在批评中过于坦率，无意中伤了她的感情。 | 近义词：excessively honest, overly candid, brutally frank | 反义词：tactful, discreet, diplomatic
