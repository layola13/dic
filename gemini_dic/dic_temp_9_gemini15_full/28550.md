cardiologist:/ˌkɑːrdiˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |心脏病专家 | A cardiologist is a physician specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of heart diseases.  |The cardiologist examined the patient's electrocardiogram. 心脏病专家检查了病人的心电图。|physician, heart specialist |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps general practitioner) | 名词

词源:
cardio-  (from Greek καρδία, kardia, "heart") + -logist (from Greek -λογος, -logos, "one who studies")  心脏病学家一词源于希腊语，其中“cardio-”表示心脏，“-logist”表示研究者或专家。
