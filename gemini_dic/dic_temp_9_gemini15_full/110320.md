lightener:/ˈlaɪtnər/| n.|漂白剂，增白剂，使变亮的东西|A substance used to make something lighter in color, especially hair or skin.  It can also refer to something that makes something brighter or clearer.|例句：She used a hair lightener to brighten her dark brown hair. 她用漂白剂使她深棕色的头发变亮。|例句：The photo lightener made the dark picture much clearer. 照片增亮软件使昏暗的照片清晰得多。|近义词：bleach, brightener, whitener|反义词：darkener, duller
