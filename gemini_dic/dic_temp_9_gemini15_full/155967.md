photogrammeter:/ˌfoʊtoʊɡræˈmɛtər/ | n. |摄影测量仪|A photogrammeter is an instrument used in photogrammetry to measure the three-dimensional coordinates of points from two or more photographs of the same scene taken from different positions. It uses the principles of stereoscopy to create a three-dimensional model of the photographed object or area. |The surveyor used a photogrammeter to create a detailed 3D model of the construction site.  摄影测量员使用摄影测量仪创建了施工现场的详细3D模型。|stereoplotter, 3D scanner|  |n.
