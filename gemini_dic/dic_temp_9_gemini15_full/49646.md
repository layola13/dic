demilitarizes: /ˌdiːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzɪz/ | vt. | 非军事化；解除武装 | To remove military forces or weapons from (a place or area).  |例句：The treaty demilitarizes the border region.  该条约使边境地区非军事化。

例句：The government is committed to demilitarizing the islands. 政府致力于使这些岛屿非军事化。


近义词：disarm, neutralize, pacify | 反义词：militarize, arm
