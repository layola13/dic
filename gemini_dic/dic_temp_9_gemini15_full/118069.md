mendicancy: /ˈmɛndɪkənsi/ | n. | 乞讨；乞丐行为 | The act of begging; the state of being a beggar.  It often implies a persistent and possibly shameless dependence on charity. | 例句：The widespread mendicancy in the city shocked the visitors.  城市的普遍乞讨行为震惊了游客们。  | 例句：He lived a life of abject mendicancy after losing his job. 他失业后过着卑微的乞讨生活。 | 近义词：begging, pauperism, vagrancy | 反义词：wealth, affluence, opulence

词源:
