pipping:/ˈpɪpɪŋ/| n. v.| 轻微的尖锐声；发出轻微的尖锐声 | A short, high-pitched sound; to make such a sound.  |例句：The kettle gave a cheerful piping sound as it boiled. 水壶沸腾时发出愉快的尖锐声响。  |例句：The birds were piping their morning song. 鸟儿们正在唱着它们的晨歌。|近义词：chirping, tweeting, whistling, squeaking |反义词： booming, roaring

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird pipes a cheerful tune. = 鸟儿唱着快乐的曲子。
He pipes up with a suggestion. = 他提了个建议。

一般过去时:
The whistle piped loudly. = 口哨声响亮地吹响了。
She piped a melody on her flute. = 她用长笛演奏了一支旋律。
