leninists:/ˈlɛnɪnɪsts/| n.|列宁主义者|Adherents of Leninism, a political ideology based on the theories of Vladimir Lenin.  Leninists typically advocate for a socialist or communist system achieved through a vanguard party and a revolutionary overthrow of the existing order. They emphasize the importance of a strong central government and a planned economy.|例句：Many historians debate the long-term effects of Leninist policies on the Soviet Union. 许多历史学家都在争论列宁主义政策对苏联的长期影响。|例句：The rise of Leninist movements in the early 20th century significantly altered the political landscape of many countries. 20 世纪初列宁主义运动的兴起极大地改变了许多国家的政治格局。|近义词：Marxists, communists, socialists (depending on context)|反义词：capitalists, anarchists, liberal democrats|名词
