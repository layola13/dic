listening:/ˈlɪsənɪŋ/| n.| 倾听；听力；收听 | The act or process of hearing attentively; the ability to hear and understand.  | Example:  Her listening skills are excellent.  她的倾听能力极佳。 |近义词：hearing, attention |反义词： ignoring, disregarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am listening to music. = 我正在听音乐。
He listens attentively in class. = 他上课认真听讲。

一般过去时:
I listened to the radio last night. = 我昨晚听了收音机。
She listened carefully to what he said. = 她仔细听了他说的话。

现在完成时:
I have been listening to this song all day. = 我一整天都在听这首歌。
They have listened to the arguments patiently. = 他们耐心地听取了这些论点。

过去完成时:
I had listened to the entire album before I went to bed. = 我睡觉前已经听完了整张专辑。
She had listened to the news before she called him. = 她打电话给他之前已经听了新闻。

将来时:
I will be listening to a podcast tomorrow. = 我明天将听播客。
We will listen to the judge's decision. = 我们将听取法官的判决。

虚拟语气:
If I had listened to her advice, I wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果我听了她的建议，我就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had listened more carefully to my teacher. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地听老师讲课。

被动语态:
The music is being listened to by everyone. =  这音乐正在被大家收听。
His concerns were listened to by the committee. = 他的担忧得到了委员会的重视。（他的担忧被委员会倾听了）


疑问句:
Are you listening to me? = 你在听我说吗？
What are you listening to? = 你在听什么？

否定句:
I wasn't listening to the conversation. = 我没有在听那段谈话。
He didn't listen to my warning. = 他没有听我的警告。


PHRASE->
active listening 主动倾听
critical listening 批判性倾听
comprehension listening 理解性倾听
selective listening 选择性倾听
listening skills 听力技巧
