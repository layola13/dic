counter-advertising's: /ˈkaʊntərˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋz/ | n. | 反广告宣传 |  Advertising that aims to counteract or negate the effects of other advertising campaigns, often by highlighting misleading or harmful aspects of a competitor's product or message.  It's a form of defensive marketing. | Example: The tobacco company launched a counter-advertising campaign to combat the anti-smoking advertisements.  反烟草公司发起了一场反广告宣传活动来对抗反吸烟广告。 | 近义词：corrective advertising, defensive advertising | 反义词：propaganda, promotion
