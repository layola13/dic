reracks:/riːˈræks/| v.|重新整理；重新排列；|To arrange or stack (something) again, typically in a more organized way.|例句：He reracked the weights after his workout. 他在锻炼后重新整理了杠铃片。|例句：She reracked the books according to their genre. 她按类型重新整理了书籍。|近义词：reorganize, rearrange, restack|反义词：disorganize, scatter
