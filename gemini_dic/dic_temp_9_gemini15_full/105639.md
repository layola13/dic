kingbolt:/ˈkɪŋbəʊlt/ | n. |大螺栓，主螺栓|A large bolt, especially one used to secure something in place or to carry a heavy load.  It is typically a central or main bolt, hence the "king" in its name.  |例句：The kingbolt on the trailer was loose, causing instability. 拖车的킹볼트가 느슨하여 불안정해졌습니다.

The kingbolt secures the front axle to the vehicle's frame.  该主螺栓将前桥固定在车辆的车架上。|近义词：master bolt, main bolt |反义词：small bolt, insignificant bolt
