garbler:/ˈɡɑːrb(ə)lər/| n. |歪曲者；含糊不清的人|A garbler is someone who distorts or misrepresents facts or information.  They often confuse or obscure the meaning of something. |例句：The journalist was accused of being a garbler of facts.  记者被指控歪曲事实。|近义词：distorter, misinterpreter, obfuscator |反义词：clarifier, elucidator
