paragliding:/ˈpærəɡlaɪdɪŋ/| n. |滑翔伞运动|The activity of flying a paraglider.  Paragliding involves using a paraglider, a type of wing made of fabric, to fly through the air. It's a popular recreational activity and sport.|例句：He took a paragliding lesson and loved the experience. 他上了一节滑翔伞课，并且非常喜欢这项体验。|例句：Many people enjoy paragliding for its breathtaking views.  许多人享受滑翔伞运动带来的令人叹为观止的景色。|近义词：hang gliding|反义词：None specifically, but  'ground-based activities' could be considered an antonym in a broad sense.|名词
