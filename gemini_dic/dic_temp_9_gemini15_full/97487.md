ingenuous:/ɪnˈdʒɛnjʊəs/ | adj. | 天真的；单纯的；巧妙的；精巧的 |  Characterized by a lack of worldly experience; innocent and unsuspecting. Also, cleverly or skillfully devised or done. |

例句：

Her ingenuous smile charmed everyone.  = 她天真的笑容迷住了所有人。

The solution to the problem was remarkably ingenuous. =  解决这个问题的方法非常巧妙。


近义词：naive, artless, simple, clever, ingenious

反义词：sophisticated, worldly, cunning, deceitful


词源:

源自于拉丁语 *ingeniosus,  由ingenium (天赋，才能，智力)派生而来。  ingenium 本身来自in- (在…里) + gignere (产生，创造)。  因此，ingenuous 最初的意思与“有天赋的”，“有创造力的”相关，后来才发展出“天真”和“巧妙”的含义。
