ortho:/ˈɔːrθoʊ/| /ˈɔːθəʊ/ | adj. | 正确的；直的；正交的 |  From the Greek word *orthos* meaning "straight," "right," or "correct,"  "ortho" is a combining form used in scientific and technical terms to indicate straightness, correctness, or a standard position. It often refers to something being in the correct or standard position, or a position that is perpendicular or at right angles.  | Example: Orthopedics is the branch of medicine dealing with the correction of deformities of bones or muscles. 正骨医学是处理矫正骨骼或肌肉畸形的医学分支。| 近义词：straight, correct, right, perpendicular | 反义词：wrong, incorrect, oblique, crooked |
