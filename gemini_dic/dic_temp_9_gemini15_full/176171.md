re-acclimated:/ˌriːəˈklaɪmɪteɪtɪd/| v. |重新适应；再次适应|  To become accustomed to a new climate or environment again after a period of adjustment or change.  This implies a previous period of acclimation that was interrupted or followed by a period requiring further adjustment.|例句：After a month in the hospital, she slowly re-acclimated to her normal life.  在一个月的住院治疗后，她慢慢地重新适应了正常的生活。

The bird, initially stressed by captivity, re-acclimated to the wild after its release.  这只鸟最初因被囚禁而感到压力，在获释后重新适应了野外生活。|近义词：readapt, readjust, reaccustom|反义词：maladapt, disorient
