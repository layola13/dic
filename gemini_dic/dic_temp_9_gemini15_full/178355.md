reconfine:/ˌriːkənˈfaɪn/| vt.|重新限制；再次限制 |To confine again; to restrict or limit something once more.  The word combines the prefix "re-" (again) with "confine" (to restrict).|例句：The government decided to reconfine the city after a surge in COVID-19 cases. 政府决定在新冠肺炎病例激增后再次封锁这座城市。

The authorities had to reconfine the suspect to prevent further escape attempts. 当局不得不再次限制嫌疑人的行动以防止其再次逃跑。


近义词：restrict, limit, restrain, contain

反义词：liberate, release, free
