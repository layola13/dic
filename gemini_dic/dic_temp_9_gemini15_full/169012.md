profusion:/prəˈfjuːʒən/ | n. |丰富，充裕，大量 | An abundance or large quantity of something.  |例句：There was a profusion of wildflowers in the meadow.  山谷里野花盛开。|例句：The artist's work is characterized by a profusion of color and detail.  这位艺术家的作品以丰富的色彩和细节为特色。|近义词：abundance, plethora, wealth, copiousness |反义词：scarcity, dearth, paucity
