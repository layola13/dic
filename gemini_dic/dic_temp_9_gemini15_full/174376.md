quiddle:/ˈkwɪdl/ | v. | 吹毛求疵；挑剔；唠叨 | To find fault with; to criticize in a petty or trifling way; to spend time on trifles. | He quiddled over the price of the car for hours. 他就车价吹毛求疵地争论了几个小时。|近义词：niggle, cavil, carp, quibble | 反义词：accept, approve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quiddles over every detail. = 他对每一个细节都吹毛求疵。
She quiddles about the smallest things. = 她对最细微的事情都唠叨个不停。


一般过去时:
He quiddled about the bill for a long time. = 他就账单唠叨了很久。
They quiddled over the wording of the contract. = 他们就合同措辞吹毛求疵。


现在完成时:
She has quiddled about this issue for weeks. = 她就这个问题吹毛求疵了好几周了。
He has quiddled away the whole afternoon. = 他整个下午都在浪费时间。


过去完成时:
They had quiddled over the arrangements for hours before reaching a decision. = 他们在做出决定之前就安排问题争论了好几个小时。
She had quiddled with her brother about the inheritance. = 她和她哥哥为遗产问题争吵过。
