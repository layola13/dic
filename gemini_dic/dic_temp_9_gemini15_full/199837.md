site:/saɪt/| n. |地点；场所；网站；遗址|A place where something is, was, or will be located; a website; the remains of a building or settlement from the past.|例句：The construction site is very busy.  建筑工地非常繁忙。|例句：This website is a great resource for learning English. 这个网站是学习英语的好资源。|近义词：place, location, website, spot |反义词：non-site (非地点，非正式用法)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum is a popular tourist site. = 博物馆是一个受欢迎的旅游景点。
This website provides information on various topics. = 这个网站提供各种主题的信息。

一般过去时:
The ancient city was a significant historical site. = 这座古城是一个重要的历史遗址。
We visited several interesting sites during our trip. = 在旅途中我们参观了几个有趣的地方。

现在完成时:
Archaeologists have discovered a new site. = 考古学家们发现了一个新的遗址。
They have built a new website for their company. = 他们为公司建立了一个新的网站。

过去完成时:
The explorers had already visited the site before the storm. = 探险家们在暴风雨来临之前就已经访问过那个地方了。
The developers had completed the website design before launching it. = 开发人员在启动之前就已经完成了网站设计。

将来时:
We will visit the archaeological site next week. = 我们下周将参观考古遗址。
They will launch their new website next month. = 他们下个月将启动他们的新网站。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would visit that historical site. = 如果我有更多时间，我会参观那个历史遗址。
If they had a better website, they would attract more customers. = 如果他们有一个更好的网站，他们会吸引更多客户。

被动语态:
The site was damaged by the earthquake. = 该遗址在地震中受损。
The website is being updated regularly. = 网站正在定期更新。

疑问句:
What is the name of the site? = 这个地方叫什么名字？
Where is the website located? = 网站在哪里？

否定句:

The website is not working properly. = 网站运行不正常。
