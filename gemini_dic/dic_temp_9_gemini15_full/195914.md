sensibly:/ˈsensəbli/| adv.|明智地；合理地；稳重地|In a sensible manner; reasonably.  It implies acting or thinking in a way that shows good judgment and practicality.|He sensibly decided to wait for the storm to pass before driving.|近义词：reasonably, wisely, prudently|反义词：foolishly, unwisely, recklessly|副词
