nontitularly:/ˌnɒnˈtaɪtʃʊləli/ | adv. |非名义上地，非正式地 |  Not in a titular or official capacity; informally.  This adverb indicates that something is done or exists without holding an official title or position. | 例句：He served nontitularly as the group's advisor. 他非正式地担任该组织的顾问。 | 例句：The company operates nontitularly in several countries. 这家公司在几个国家非正式运营。 | 近义词：unofficially, informally, in an unofficial capacity | 反义词：officially, formally, titularly
