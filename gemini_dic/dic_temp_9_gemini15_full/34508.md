clean-up:/ˈkliːnʌp/ | n., v. |清理；清除；整顿 |The act of cleaning or the process of making something clean and tidy;  to clean or tidy something.|例句：The clean-up after the party took hours. 派对后的清理工作花了几个小时。|例句：We need to do a clean-up of the old files. 我们需要清理一下旧文件。|近义词：cleanup, cleaning, tidying, sprucing up |反义词：messing up, soiling, dirting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They clean up the room every day. = 他们每天都打扫房间。
He cleans up the mess after dinner. = 他晚饭后清理残局。

一般过去时:
She cleaned up the spilled milk. = 她清理了洒了的牛奶。
We cleaned up the garage yesterday. = 我们昨天清理了车库。

现在完成时:
I have cleaned up the kitchen. = 我已经打扫干净了厨房。
They have cleaned up the beach after the storm. = 暴风雨后他们已经清理了海滩。

过去完成时:
He had cleaned up the apartment before his guests arrived. = 他的客人到达之前，他已经打扫干净了公寓。
She had cleaned up the spill before anyone noticed. = 在任何人注意到之前，她已经清理了溢出的东西。

将来时:
We will clean up the park tomorrow. = 我们明天将清理公园。
They will clean up the mess after the festival. = 节日后他们将清理残局。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would clean up the attic. = 如果我有更多时间，我会清理阁楼。
I wish I had cleaned up the garage sooner. = 我真希望我早点清理车库。

被动语态:
The room was cleaned up by the maid. = 房间是由女佣打扫干净的。
The spill was cleaned up quickly. = 溢出的东西很快就被清理干净了。

疑问句:
Did you clean up the mess? = 你清理了烂摊子了吗？
Have they cleaned up the kitchen yet? = 他们已经打扫厨房了吗？

否定句:
I didn't clean up my room. = 我没有打扫我的房间。
He hasn't cleaned up the garage yet. = 他还没清理车库。
