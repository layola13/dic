rackfuls: /ˈrækfʊlz/ | n. | 满架的；大量的 |  A large number or quantity, typically filling several racks.  It implies a significant and possibly overflowing abundance. The word is formed by combining "rack" (a framework or structure with shelves or bars) and the plural suffix "-fuls," indicating multiple fillings of a rack. | 例句：The shelves were filled with rackfuls of books. = 书架上摆满了书。  The market overflowed with rackfuls of fresh produce. = 市场里堆满了大量的鲜货。 | 近义词：loads, heaps, piles, stacks | 反义词：a few, scarcity, shortage | 名词
