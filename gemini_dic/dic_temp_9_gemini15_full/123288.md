monogamous:/məˈnɒɡəməs/| adj.| 一夫一妻制的；专一的 | Relating to or characterized by having only one mate at a time.  |例句：Many bird species are monogamous, forming pair bonds for life. 许多鸟类是一夫一妻制的，终生形成配偶关系。

例句：He's been in a monogamous relationship for five years. 他保持了一段五年的一夫一妻制的恋爱关系。


近义词：faithful, loyal, constant | 反义词：polygamous, unfaithful
