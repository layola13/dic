intenders:/ɪnˈtɛndərz/ | n. |意图者，打算做某事的人 |Those who intend to do something. People with specific plans or goals.  |例句：The intenders of the project are meeting next week.  项目的发起人下周要开会。|例句：Many intenders of the new policy are optimistic about its success.  许多支持这项新政策的人都对其成功持乐观态度。|近义词：planners, proposers, initiators |反义词：obstructors, opponents, resisters |名词
