ex-tends:/ɪkˈstɛndz/| v.|延伸；扩展；给予；提供；持续|To stretch out in length or area; to make something longer or larger; to offer or give something; to continue in time or space.|例句：The park extends to the river.  公园延伸到河边。|例句：She extended her hand in greeting. 她伸出手打招呼。|近义词：prolong, expand, lengthen, offer, provide|反义词：shorten, contract, reduce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The road extends for miles. = 这条路延伸数英里。
He extends a warm welcome to all guests. = 他热情地欢迎所有客人。

一般过去时:
The exhibition extended for a week. = 展览持续了一周。
They extended their stay by a few days. = 他们延长了几天住宿时间。

现在完成时:
The railway has extended to the new town. = 铁路已经延伸到新城镇。
She has extended her help to many people. = 她已经帮助了许多人。

过去完成时:
The bridge had extended across the river before the war. = 大桥在战争爆发前已经横跨了这条河。
He had extended his apologies before the meeting ended. = 会议结束前他已经道过歉了。

将来时:
The company will extend its business to Asia. = 公司将把业务扩展到亚洲。
They will extend the deadline for the project. = 他们将延长项目的截止日期。

虚拟语气:
If the company extended its support, we could succeed. = 如果公司给予支持，我们就能成功。
I wish they extended more time for the assignment. = 我希望他们能给作业多一些时间。

被动语态:
The deadline was extended by the professor. = 截止日期被教授延长了。
The bridge is being extended to accommodate more traffic. = 大桥正在拓宽以容纳更多的交通流量。

疑问句:
Does the contract extend beyond next year? = 合同是否会延续到明年以后？
How far does the forest extend? = 森林延伸到多远？

否定句:
The offer does not extend to everyone. = 这项优惠并非人人适用。
He didn't extend his hand in friendship. = 他没有友好地伸出手。
