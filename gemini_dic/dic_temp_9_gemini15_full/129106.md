nitwittery:/ˈnɪtwɪtəri/| n. |愚蠢的行为；无聊的言论；废话 |Foolish or silly talk or behavior; nonsense.  It often implies a lack of seriousness or intelligence, and can be used to describe frivolous or pointless chatter. |例句：His constant nitwittery on social media is tiresome. 他在社交媒体上不断发表的愚蠢言论令人厌烦。

例句：The meeting was filled with unproductive nitwittery, achieving nothing of substance.  会议充满了无益的废话，什么实质性的事情都没做到。


近义词：silliness, foolishness, nonsense, drivel, twaddle, inanity, buffoonery


反义词：wisdom, intelligence, seriousness, profundity


词性: 名词
