machination:/ˌmækɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 阴谋；诡计；策划 |  A scheming or plotting; an elaborate plot or scheme.  Machinations often involve cunning, deceit, and a hidden agenda. | The villain's machinations were finally exposed, leading to his downfall. 邪恶分子的阴谋最终被揭露，导致了他的垮台。 |  intrigue, plot, scheme, conspiracy | honesty, straightforwardness | 名词
