overutilizes:/ˌoʊvərˈjuːtəlaɪzɪz/ | v. |过度使用，滥用 | To use something excessively or to a point of inefficiency or harm.  |例句：The company overutilizes its resources, leading to burnout among employees. 公司过度使用资源，导致员工筋疲力尽。  |例句：He overutilizes his credit card, resulting in high debt. 他过度使用信用卡，导致债务缠身。 |近义词：misuse, overuse, exploit, deplete |反义词：underuse, conserve, preserve |动词
