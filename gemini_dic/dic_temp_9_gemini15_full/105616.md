kinesis:/kaɪˈniːsɪs/| n. |运动；动势；自动运动|Movement of an organism in response to a stimulus; specifically, undirected movement in response to a stimulus, as opposed to taxis, which is directed movement.  Kinesis is often characterized by changes in speed or rate of turning. |Example: The woodlice showed kinesis in response to changes in humidity, moving faster in dry areas and slower in damp areas.|近义词：movement, locomotion, activity |反义词：stasis, inactivity|名词
