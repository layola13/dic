chafing:/ˈtʃeɪfɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|擦伤，摩擦；恼怒，烦躁；|to make (skin) sore by rubbing; to feel annoyed or impatient|例句：The new shoes chafed my heels. 这双新鞋把我的脚后跟磨破了。  The constant criticism was chafing him.  不断的批评让他感到恼火。|近义词：abrading, irritating, annoying|反义词：soothing, comforting

USAGE->

He chafes at the restrictions. = 他对这些限制感到恼火。
