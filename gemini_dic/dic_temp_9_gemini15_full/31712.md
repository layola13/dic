chawdron: /ˈtʃɔːdrən/ | n. |内脏；动物内脏汤 |  A mixture of the entrails of an animal, especially when used in a stew or soup.  The word often evokes a sense of something unrefined or rustic.  | Example: The cook prepared a hearty chawdron of beef tripe and vegetables.  厨师准备了一锅丰盛的牛肚蔬菜杂碎汤。 | 近义词: offal, entrails, giblets | 反义词:  None specifically, but words suggesting refined food could be considered antonyms. | 名词
