reforecasted:/ˌriːˈfɔːrkæst/ | v. | 再预报，重新预报 | To make a new forecast, often after revising earlier predictions.  It implies a correction or update to a previous forecast. | The weather was reforecasted after the storm hit.  暴风雨过后，天气预报进行了重新预报。 The sales figures were reforecasted to reflect the recent market downturn. 销售数据进行了重新预报以反映最近的市场低迷。 |  reforecast, revise, update |  —  |  verb
