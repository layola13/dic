例句：The rubescence of the sunset painted the sky in fiery hues.  日落时分的红晕给天空涂上了火红的色彩。


近义词：blush, redness, flush, bloom|反义词：pallor, paleness|词性:名词


词源:  From Latin *rubescere "to become red," from ruber "red."
