retractor:/rɪˈtræktər/| n. |牵引器，拉钩| A surgical instrument used to retract or hold back tissue or organs during an operation, providing a clear surgical field.  It can be various shapes and sizes depending on its intended use. |例句：The surgeon used a retractor to keep the abdominal muscles out of the way during the operation.  外科医生在手术过程中用拉钩将腹部肌肉拉开。

例句：The self-retaining retractor helped maintain a clear view of the surgical site. 自持式拉钩有助于保持手术部位的清晰视野。


近义词：tissue holder, retracting device |反义词：None (no direct antonym exists)|词性:noun
