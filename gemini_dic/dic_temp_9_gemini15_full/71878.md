flet:/flet/| n.|箭羽；（箭的）翎毛|A flet is a feather attached to an arrow to stabilize its flight.  It is also sometimes used to describe a small, flat piece of material attached to something for decoration or function.|例句：The archer carefully attached the flet to the arrow.  弓箭手小心地将箭羽绑在箭上。 The decorative flet on the hat added a touch of elegance. 帽子上的装饰性翎毛增添了一丝优雅。|近义词：feather, vane|反义词：无|名词
