plough-out: /plaʊˈaʊt/ | vt. | 犁出；克服；排除；挣脱 | To clear (something, such as snow) from a place by plowing.  Also, to overcome or get rid of a problem or difficulty. | 例句：The snow ploughs are working hard to plough out the roads.  铲雪车正在努力清理道路上的积雪。  They finally ploughed out of debt after years of struggle.  他们经过多年的奋斗终于摆脱了债务。| 近义词：clear, remove, overcome, extricate | 反义词：accumulate, bury, succumb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer ploughs out the field every spring. = 农民每年春天都犁地。
They plough out new ideas for their next project. = 他们为下一个项目挖掘新的想法。


一般过去时:
The team ploughed out the tunnel through the mountain. = 团队挖通了山里的隧道。
She ploughed out the difficult emotions she was feeling. = 她克服了她正在经历的负面情绪。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
