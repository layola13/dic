instinctive:/ɪnˈstɪŋktɪv/| adj.|本能的，天生的|Acting or occurring without conscious thought; based on instinct.|The cat's instinctive reaction was to flee.|近义词：intuitive, innate, natural|反义词：learned, deliberate, conscious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Her instinctive response is to hide. = 她本能的反应是躲起来。
Birds instinctively build nests. = 鸟类本能地筑巢。


一般过去时:
He instinctively ducked as the ball flew past. = 球飞过去时，他本能地躲闪了一下。
She instinctively knew something was wrong. = 她本能地知道有什么不对劲。


现在完成时:
I have instinctively avoided that situation many times. = 我本能地避免过很多次那种情况。
They have instinctively understood the danger. = 他们本能地理解了危险。


过去完成时:
He had instinctively known he should leave. = 他本能地知道他应该离开。
She had instinctively reacted to the sudden noise. = 她本能地对突如其来的噪音做出了反应。


将来时:
He will instinctively reach for his phone. = 他会本能地去拿他的手机。
She will instinctively protect her child. = 她会本能地保护她的孩子。


虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would instinctively chase mice. = 如果我是一只猫，我会本能地追逐老鼠。
If she had been there, she would instinctively have helped. = 如果她在那里，她会本能地提供帮助。


被动语态:
The behavior was instinctively performed. = 这种行为是本能地执行的。
The response is instinctively triggered. = 这种反应是本能地触发的。


疑问句:
Is this an instinctive reaction? = 这是本能反应吗？
Was his behavior instinctive? = 他的行为是本能的吗？


否定句:
The action was not instinctive; it was learned. = 这个动作不是本能的，而是后天习得的。
His response wasn't instinctive, he thought it through. = 他的反应不是本能的，他是经过深思熟虑的。
