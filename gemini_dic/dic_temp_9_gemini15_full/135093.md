It's frequently used in scientific contexts, particularly in fields like quantum physics and economics. | Example: The effects of dark matter are nonobservable directly, but can be inferred from their gravitational influence.  不可直接观察到暗物质的影响，但可以从其引力影响推断出来。|近义词：unobservable, imperceptible, undetectable |反义词：observable, visible, detectable
