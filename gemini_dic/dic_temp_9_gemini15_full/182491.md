renovates:/ˈrɛnəveɪts/| vt.|修复；翻新；革新|To restore something to a good state of repair; to modernize or improve.|例句：They are renovating the old house. 他们正在翻新那栋旧房子。

例句：The company renovates old buildings and sells them at a profit. 这家公司翻新旧建筑并以盈利出售。


近义词：restore, refurbish, modernize, rehabilitate|反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He renovates old cars. 他翻新旧汽车。
She renovates her kitchen every five years. 她每五年翻新一次厨房。


一般过去时:
They renovated the bathroom last year. 他们去年翻修了浴室。
He renovated the entire house himself. 他自己翻修了整栋房子。


现在完成时:
She has renovated three houses this year. 今年她已经翻新了三栋房子。
They have renovated the school's library. 他们已经翻新了学校的图书馆。


过去完成时:
He had renovated the engine before the race. 他在比赛前已经翻修了发动机。
She had renovated the apartment before moving in. 她在搬进去之前已经翻修了公寓。


将来时:
We will renovate the office next month. 我们下个月将翻修办公室。
They will renovate the entire building. 他们将翻修整栋大楼。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would renovate the house. 如果我们有更多钱，我们会翻新房子。
I wish I could renovate my old car. 我希望我能翻新我的旧车。


被动语态:
The house is being renovated. 房子正在被翻修。
The old building was renovated last year. 旧楼去年被翻修了。


疑问句:
Are they renovating the kitchen? 他们正在翻修厨房吗？
When will the building be renovated? 这栋楼什么时候会翻修？


否定句:
They did not renovate the house. 他们没有翻修房子。
He will not renovate his apartment this year. 他今年不会翻修他的公寓。
