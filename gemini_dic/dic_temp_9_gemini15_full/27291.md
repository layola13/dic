calve:/kɑːv/| /kælv/ | v. |产崽（尤指牛）;分裂，脱落|To give birth to a calf (young of a cow, etc.); to split or break off. |例句：The cow is about to calve. 这头牛快要产崽了。
The glacier is calving. 冰川正在崩裂。|近义词：give birth to, part, separate |反义词：fuse, join


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cow calves every year. = 这头牛每年都产崽。
The ice shelf calves regularly. = 冰架定期崩裂。


一般过去时:
The cow calved last week. = 这头牛上周产崽了。
A large piece of ice calved from the glacier. = 一大块冰从冰川上崩裂下来。


现在完成时:
The cow has calved three times this year. = 这头牛今年已经产崽三次了。
The glacier has calved several times this season. = 这个冰川本季已经崩裂好几次了。


过去完成时:
The cow had calved before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，那头牛已经产崽了。
The iceberg had calved from the main glacier many years ago. = 那座冰山多年前就从主要冰川崩裂下来了。


将来时:
The cow will calve next month. = 这头牛下个月将要产崽。
Scientists predict the glacier will calve soon. = 科学家预测冰川很快就会崩裂。


虚拟语气:
If the cow calved today, we would be very happy. = 如果母牛今天产崽，我们会非常高兴。
If the glacier had calved earlier, the shipping lane would be blocked. = 如果冰川早些崩裂，航道就会被堵塞。


被动语态:
No passive voice is typically used with this verb.  该动词通常不用被动语态。


疑问句:
When will the cow calve? = 母牛什么时候产崽？
Has the glacier calved yet? = 冰川已经崩裂了吗？


否定句:
The cow didn't calve this year. = 这头牛今年没有产崽。
The ice shelf hasn't calved recently. = 冰架最近没有崩裂。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrasal verbs with "calve".  "calve" 没有常见的短语动词。
