harming:/ˈhɑːrmɪŋ/| v.| 损害；伤害 | causing physical or emotional injury or damage |  The storm is harming the crops.  暴风雨正在损害庄稼。 | He was harming himself with drugs. 他正在用毒品伤害自己。| benefiting, helping | protecting, aiding |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are harming the environment. = 他们正在损害环境。
The policy is harming small businesses. = 这项政策正在损害小型企业。


一般过去时:
The accident harmed several people. = 那次事故伤害了几个人。
He harmed his reputation with his careless words. = 他因为轻率的言辞损害了自己的名誉。


现在完成时:
Pollution has harmed the local ecosystem. = 污染已经损害了当地生态系统。
His actions have harmed his relationship with his family. = 他的行为已经损害了他与家人的关系。


过去完成时:
They had harmed the animals before anyone noticed. = 他们在任何人注意到之前就已经伤害了那些动物。
The company had harmed its customers' trust through repeated failures. = 公司由于屡次失败而损害了客户的信任。


将来时:
The project will harm the local wildlife. = 该项目将损害当地野生动物。
Ignoring the problem will harm future generations. = 忽视这个问题将会损害子孙后代。


虚拟语气:
If we continue this way, we will harm ourselves. = 如果我们继续这样下去，我们会伤害自己。
I wish I hadn't harmed their feelings. = 我真希望我没有伤害他们的感情。


被动语态:
Many people were harmed by the earthquake. = 许多人在地震中受伤。
The company's reputation was harmed by the scandal. = 该公司的声誉因丑闻而受损。


疑问句:
Is this action harming anyone? = 这个行为会伤害到任何人吗？
Are your words harming your relationships? = 你的话语是否正在损害你的关系？


否定句:
The medication does not harm the body. = 这种药物不会伤害身体。
The changes won't harm the environment. = 这些变化不会损害环境。



PHRASE->
self-harming: 自残
harmful to: 对...有害的
do harm to: 对...造成伤害
come to no harm: 安然无恙
beyond harm: 安然无恙
