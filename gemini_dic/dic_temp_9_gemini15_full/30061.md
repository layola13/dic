/siːsiːpiː/ |abbr.| 中国共产党 |The Communist Party of China (CPC), is the founding and ruling political party of the People's Republic of China.  It is the world's largest Marxist-Leninist party, with over 90 million members. The CCP's ideology is officially Marxism-Leninism with Chinese characteristics. The party controls the state through a complex system of organizations and institutions. |The CCP's policies have significantly impacted China's economic and social development. |近义词: CPC (Communist Party of China)|反义词:  (no direct antonym, but opposing political ideologies could be considered, such as liberalism or democracy)|abbr.
