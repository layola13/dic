seminars:/ˈsɛmɪnɑːrz/ | n. |研讨会，讲习班 | A seminar is a small group meeting for discussion and instruction, typically on a specific academic subject.  It often involves presentations by experts and active participation from attendees. | Example: I attended a seminar on climate change last week.  上周我参加了一个关于气候变化的研讨会。  The professor led a fascinating seminar on 19th-century literature. 教授主持了一个关于19世纪文学的精彩研讨会。|近义词：workshop, conference, lecture series |反义词：N/A
