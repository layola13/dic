disarrayed:/ˌdɪsərˈeɪd/| adj.|混乱的；杂乱的；衣衫不整的|In a state of disorder or confusion; disorganized.  It can also describe someone whose clothing is untidy or dishevelled.|例句：The room was disarrayed after the party.  派对后房间一片狼藉。

The soldiers were disarrayed and demoralized after the battle.  士兵们在战斗后溃不成军，士气低落。

近义词：disorganized, disordered, chaotic, messy, untidy, rumpled

反义词：organized, orderly, neat, tidy, arranged

词性:形容词
