disliking:/dɪsˈlaɪkɪŋ/| n.| 不喜欢；厌恶 |The feeling of not liking someone or something.  This is a noun form expressing the state of disliking. |例句：Her disliking of spiders was quite pronounced.  她的厌恶蜘蛛之情相当明显。
例句：His disliking for loud noises was evident. 他对噪音的厌恶是显而易见的。|近义词：dislike, aversion, antipathy |反义词：liking, fondness, affection |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His disliking of broccoli is well-known. = 他不喜欢西兰花是出了名的。
My disliking for early mornings is intense. = 我非常讨厌早起。


一般过去时:
Her disliking of the movie was apparent. = 她不喜欢那部电影是显而易见的。
Their disliking for the new policy was widespread. = 他们对这项新政策的不满非常普遍。


现在完成时:
My disliking of this job has grown over the years. = 我对这份工作的厌恶感多年来一直在增长。
His disliking of his neighbor has intensified recently. = 他最近对邻居的厌恶加剧了。


过去完成时:
Her disliking of the singer had been long-standing. = 她对这位歌手的不满由来已久。
Their disliking of the project had already been established. = 他们对这个项目的不满早已存在。


将来时:
His disliking of the task will likely increase as the deadline approaches. = 随着截止日期的临近，他对这项任务的厌恶可能会增加。
My disliking for public speaking will probably never disappear. = 我可能永远都无法克服对公开演讲的恐惧。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have a disliking of crowds, I would go to the concert. = 如果我不讨厌人群，我会去参加音乐会的。
I wish I didn't have this disliking for seafood. = 我真希望我不讨厌海鲜。


被动语态:
His disliking of the food was not expressed directly. = 他对食物的不满并没有直接表达出来。  (This is less common as disliking is a feeling and less frequently used passively)


疑问句:
Is your disliking of the plan based on facts? = 你对这个计划的不满是基于事实的吗？
What is the root cause of his disliking of cats? = 他讨厌猫的根本原因是什么？


否定句:
There was no disliking of the proposal from the team. = 团队对该提案没有任何异议。
She showed no disliking towards her colleagues. = 她对同事们没有表现出任何厌恶。



PHRASE->
a strong disliking of = 强烈的厌恶
a growing disliking of = 日益增长的厌恶
a deep-seated disliking of = 根深蒂固的厌恶
a mutual disliking = 互相厌恶
express a disliking of = 表达对…的不满
