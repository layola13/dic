pummace:/ˈpʌmɪs/| n.|果渣，渣滓|The residue remaining after the juice has been pressed from fruit, especially grapes.  It is often used as fertilizer or animal feed. |例句：The pummace from the winemaking process is rich in nutrients.  酿酒过程中产生的果渣富含营养物质。  The farmer used the apple pummace to enrich the soil. 农民用苹果渣来改良土壤。|近义词：residue, refuse, pomace|反义词：juice, extract
