infantilize: /ˈɪnfəntaɪz/ | vt. | 使幼小化；以婴儿的方式对待 | To treat (someone) as if they were a baby, especially by being overly protective or by failing to give them responsibility.  | 例句：He infantilizes his wife by constantly making decisions for her. 他总是替妻子做决定，把她当小孩子一样对待。

例句：The company's policy infantilizes its employees by micromanaging every aspect of their work.  这家公司的政策通过对员工工作的方方面面进行微观管理，使其员工幼稚化。


近义词：baby, coddle, pamper, overprotect


反义词：empower, enable, mature


词性：动词


USAGE->
