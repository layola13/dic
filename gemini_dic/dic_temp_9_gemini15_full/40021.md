It encompasses factors such as ease of use, clarity of information, accessibility, and overall positive user experience. |Example: The company redesigned its website, prioritizing consumer-friendliness to improve user engagement. 公司重新设计了其网站，优先考虑消费者友好度以提高用户参与度。|近义词：user-friendliness, ease of use, accessibility |反义词：user-unfriendliness, complexity, inaccessibility
