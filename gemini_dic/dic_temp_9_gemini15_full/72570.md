fluviograph:/ˈfluːviəɡræf/| n. |河流图，河流剖面图|A fluviograph is a graphical representation of the characteristics of a river or stream, often showing its discharge over time or its cross-sectional profile.  It can be used to analyze hydrological data and understand the river's behavior. |Example: The fluviograph clearly showed the peak discharge during the recent storm.  河流图清楚地显示了近期暴风雨期间的峰值流量。|近义词：hydrograph, river profile|反义词：无
