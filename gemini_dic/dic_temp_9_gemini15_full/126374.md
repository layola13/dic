myristate:/ˈmɪrɪsteɪt/| n. |肉豆蔻酸酯|Myristate is a salt or ester of myristic acid, a saturated fatty acid with 14 carbon atoms. It's commonly found in various natural oils and fats.  Myristates are used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and other applications.|例句：Coconut oil contains a significant amount of myristate. 椰子油含有大量的肉豆蔻酸酯。|近义词：fatty acid ester|反义词：无|名词
