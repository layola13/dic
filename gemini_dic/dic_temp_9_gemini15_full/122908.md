molted:/ˈmoʊltɪd/| adj.|蜕皮的；换毛的；脱落的|Past participle of molt.  Describes something that has shed its skin, feathers, or other outer covering.  |例句：The snake molted its skin. = 蛇蜕皮了。 The bird molted its feathers. = 鸟换毛了。|近义词：shed, cast off |反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The snake molts its skin regularly. = 蛇定期蜕皮。
Birds molt their feathers annually. = 鸟类每年换羽。

一般过去时:
The caterpillar molted before it became a butterfly. = 毛毛虫在变成蝴蝶之前蜕皮了。
The snake molted its skin last week. = 蛇上周蜕皮了。

现在完成时:
The snake has molted twice this year. = 蛇今年已经蜕皮两次了。
The bird has already molted its summer plumage. = 这只鸟已经换掉了它的夏季羽毛。


过去完成时:
The snake had molted several times before it reached adulthood. = 蛇在成年之前已经蜕皮好几次了。
The bird had molted its feathers before the migration. = 鸟在迁徙之前已经换毛了。


将来时:
The snake will molt again next month. = 蛇下个月还会再蜕皮。
The bird will molt its feathers in the autumn. = 鸟会在秋天换毛。


虚拟语气:
If the snake had molted sooner, it would have been healthier. = 如果蛇早点蜕皮，它就会更健康。
If the bird molted earlier, it would have been ready for the migration. = 如果鸟早点换毛，它就能准备好迁徙了。


被动语态:
The skin was molted by the snake.  = 蛇蜕了皮。(This is less common usage)
The feathers are molted annually by the bird. = 鸟的羽毛每年都会脱落。(This is less common usage)


疑问句:
Has the snake molted yet? = 蛇蜕皮了吗？
When will the bird molt? = 鸟什么时候换毛？


否定句:
The snake hasn't molted yet. = 蛇还没蜕皮。
The bird didn't molt on time this year. = 鸟今年没有按时换毛。
