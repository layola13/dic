corner-shop:/ˈkɔːnə(r)ʃɒp/|/ˈkɔːrnərˌʃɑːp/| n. |街角小商店|A small shop, typically in a residential area, located on a street corner.  These shops often sell everyday necessities like groceries, newspapers, and confectionery. |Example: I popped into the corner shop to buy some milk.  我去街角的小商店买了一些牛奶。|近义词：convenience store, local shop|反义词：supermarket, hypermarket
