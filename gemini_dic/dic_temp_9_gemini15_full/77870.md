genetically:/dʒəˈnɛtɪkli/| adj. | 遗传地；基因地 | Relating to genes or heredity.  | Example: Genetically modified foods are a controversial topic.  遗传改良食品是一个有争议的话题。  The disease is genetically linked to a specific chromosome.  这种疾病在遗传上与特定的染色体有关。 |近义词：hereditarily, inherited |反义词：acquired, environmentally


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He genetically modifies crops. 他进行作物基因改造。
This trait is genetically determined.  这个特性是由基因决定的。


一般过去时:
Scientists genetically engineered the bacteria. 科学家对细菌进行了基因工程改造。
The plant was genetically altered.  这种植物经过了基因改造。


现在完成时:
Researchers have genetically sequenced the human genome. 研究人员已经完成了人类基因组的测序。
They have genetically tested the samples. 他们已经对样本进行了基因检测。


过去完成时:
The experiment had genetically modified the mice before the study.  在研究之前，实验已经对老鼠进行了基因改造。
The family had a genetically predisposed history of heart disease. 这个家庭有遗传性心脏病史。


将来时:
Scientists will genetically engineer disease-resistant crops. 科学家将基因工程改造抗病作物。
They will genetically analyze the DNA.  他们将对DNA进行基因分析。


虚拟语气:
If we genetically engineered humans, it would be unethical. 如果我们对人类进行基因工程改造，那将是不道德的。
I wish we understood genetically inherited diseases better. 我希望我们能更好地理解遗传性疾病。


被动语态:
The seeds were genetically modified for higher yields. 种子经过基因改造以提高产量。
The results were genetically interpreted. 结果经过遗传学解释。


疑问句:
Is this condition genetically determined? 这种情况是由基因决定的吗？
Are these plants genetically modified? 这些植物是转基因的吗？


否定句:
The disease is not genetically linked to diet.  这种疾病与饮食无关。
This trait is not genetically inherited.  这个特征不是遗传的。
