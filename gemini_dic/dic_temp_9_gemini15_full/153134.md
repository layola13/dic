pelagics:/pəˈleɪdʒɪks/| n. |远洋生物；远洋的 |Relating to or living in the open ocean, away from the coast.  Pelagic organisms are typically found in the water column, rather than on the seafloor or near the shore. |例句：Many pelagic fish species migrate great distances.  许多远洋鱼类会迁徙很长的距离。

例句：The study focused on the distribution of pelagic invertebrates in the Pacific Ocean. 这项研究的重点是大平洋远洋无脊椎动物的分布。


近义词：oceanic, deep-sea (in some contexts) |反义词：littoral, neritic, benthic


词源:From Late Latin *pelagicus, from Greek pelagikos "of the open sea," from pélagos "sea."
