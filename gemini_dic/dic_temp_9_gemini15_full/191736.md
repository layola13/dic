scientific:/ˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/ | adj. | 科学的；符合科学规律的 | Relating to or using science, or based on the methods of science.  | The scientific method involves observation, hypothesis, and experimentation. 科学方法包括观察、假设和实验。  | empirical, systematic | unscientific, illogical | 形容词
