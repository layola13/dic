perfective:/pərˈfɛktɪv/| adj.| 完成的，完成体的 | Relating to or denoting grammatical aspect expressing the completion of an action or event.  It indicates that an action is viewed as complete and whole, without reference to its internal structure or duration. | 例句：The perfective aspect in Russian verbs shows that the action is completed.  俄语动词的完成体表示动作已经完成。

例句：The perfective aspect highlights the completion of the task, regardless of how long it took. 完成体强调任务的完成，而不管花了多长时间。


近义词：completive, finished, concluded | 反义词：imperfective, incomplete, ongoing | 词性：形容词
