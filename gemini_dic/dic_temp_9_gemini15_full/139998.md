odysseus:/oʊˈdɪsiəs/| n. |奥德修斯|The main character of Homer's epic poem, *The Odyssey*, a cunning and resourceful king of Ithaca who endures many trials and tribulations on his long journey home after the Trojan War.  He is known for his intelligence, his leadership, and his unwavering determination to return to his family. |Example: Odysseus's ten-year journey home is a testament to human resilience. 奥德修斯的十年返乡之旅是对人类韧性的证明。|近义词： Ulysses (alternative name)|反义词：N/A
