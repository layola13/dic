parsley: /ˈpɑːrsli/ /ˈpɑːrsli/ | n. | 欧芹，香芹 | Parsley is a common herb used in cooking to add flavor and garnish dishes. 欧芹是一种常见的香草，用于烹饪中增加风味和装饰菜肴。|  The soup was garnished with fresh parsley.  汤用新鲜欧芹装饰。

The salad was sprinkled with chopped parsley. 色拉撒上切碎的欧芹。


近义词：coriander (芫荽), cilantro (香菜) (Note: While similar in use, these are distinct herbs)


反义词：无


词性:名词


词源:  The word "parsley" comes from the Old French *persil*, which in turn derives from the Latin *petroselinum*.  The Latin word is ultimately of Greek origin, from *petros* ("rock") and *selinon* ("celery"), reflecting its frequent growth in rocky areas and its kinship to celery.
