explanatoriness: /ɪkˌsplænəˈtɔːrɪnəs/ | n. | 解释性，说明性 | The quality of being explanatory; the extent to which something explains something else.  It refers to the ability of a theory, explanation, or statement to make something clear and understandable. |  The explanatoriness of the scientist's theory was impressive, leaving no doubt about the cause of the phenomenon.  科学家的理论具有令人印象深刻的解释性，消除了对现象成因的任何疑问。 |  clarity, comprehensibility, elucidativeness |  obscurity, incomprehensibility, ambiguity | 名词
