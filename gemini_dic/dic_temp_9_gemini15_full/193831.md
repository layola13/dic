self-determination:/ˌsɛlf dɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 自决；自主决定；自我决定 | The right of a people to govern themselves without external interference.  It refers to the ability of individuals or groups to make their own choices and control their own lives without external coercion.  This concept is central to many political and philosophical discussions, particularly in relation to national liberation movements and minority rights. | Example: The people of that nation fought for their self-determination, achieving independence after a long struggle.  这个国家的民众为他们的自决权而战，经过长期斗争后获得了独立。


Example:  The principle of self-determination is enshrined in international law. 自决原则载入国际法。


近义词：autonomy, self-governance, independence, sovereignty | 反义词：coercion, subjugation, oppression, dependence
