distance-education:/ˈdɪstəns ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən/| n. |远程教育|Distance education refers to any form of education where the learning is not face-to-face.  It uses various technologies to deliver educational content and facilitate interaction between instructors and students. This includes online courses, video conferencing, and other methods. |Example: Many universities now offer distance-education programs to students worldwide. 许多大学现在为世界各地的学生提供远程教育课程。|近义词：online learning, e-learning, virtual learning |反义词：traditional education, on-campus learning
