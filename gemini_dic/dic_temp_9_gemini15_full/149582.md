paid-out: /peɪdˈaʊt/ | adj. | 已支付的；已支出 | 已支付的款项或已经发生的支出。通常指已经完成的付款或支出，可以指具体的金额，也可以指一个过程。| 例句：The paid-out amount was significantly higher than expected.  已支付的金额远高于预期。


The paid-out bonuses were a great morale booster for the employees. 已支付的奖金极大地鼓舞了员工的士气。| 近义词：disbursed, expended, disbursed, settled | 反义词：outstanding, unpaid


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company pays out bonuses annually. = 公司每年支付奖金。
He pays out a large sum of money every month. = 他每月支付一大笔钱。

一般过去时:
They paid out all the claims promptly. = 他们迅速支付了所有索赔。
The government paid out disaster relief funds. = 政府支付了救灾款项。

现在完成时:
We have paid out all our invoices this month. = 我们这个月已经支付了所有发票。
She has paid out a considerable amount for her education. = 她为教育已经支付了相当大的一笔钱。

过去完成时:
They had paid out the money before the deadline. = 他们早在截止日期之前就付清了钱。
The company had paid out all the dividends before the announcement. = 公司在公告之前已经支付了所有股息。

将来时:
We will pay out the winners next week. = 我们下周将支付给获胜者。
The insurance company will pay out the claim once the investigation is complete. = 保险公司将在调查结束后支付索赔。

虚拟语气:
If they had paid out the money earlier, the problem would have been avoided. = 如果他们早点付钱，问题就可以避免了。
I wish they had paid out the reimbursement more quickly. = 我希望他们能更快地报销。

被动语态:
The bonuses were paid out last Friday. = 奖金是上周五支付的。
The funds will be paid out in installments. = 资金将分期支付。

疑问句:
Has the money been paid out yet? = 钱已经支付了吗？
When will the dividends be paid out? = 股息什么时候支付？

否定句:
The claims have not yet been paid out. = 索赔尚未支付。
They did not pay out the bonus this year. = 他们今年没有支付奖金。
