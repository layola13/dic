refacing:/riːˈfeɪsɪŋ/| n.|表面翻新；重新装修；更换表面|The act of renewing the surface of something, such as a building or furniture, by applying a new facing material.  It often involves replacing worn or damaged surfaces to improve appearance and functionality.|例句：The refacing of the kitchen cabinets gave the room a completely new look.  厨房橱柜的翻新使房间焕然一新。|例句：The old building underwent extensive refacing to meet modern building codes. 这座旧建筑物进行了大规模的表面翻新以符合现代建筑规范。|近义词：renovation, refurbishment, restoration|反义词：destruction, demolition
