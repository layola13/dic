patwork:/ˈpætˌwɜːrk/| n.|拼缝;拼布;拼凑之物|Work made from pieces of different fabrics sewn together; something resembling this in being made of different parts.  A patchwork quilt is a common example.|The artist created a stunning patchwork of colors and textures in the painting.|近义词：mosaic, collage, medley|反义词：uniformity, homogeneity
