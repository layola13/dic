nonpersonally:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːsənəli/ | adv. |非亲自地，非个人地 |Not in person; indirectly.  This word emphasizes the lack of personal involvement or direct interaction.  |例句：The information was delivered nonpersonally via email.  信息是通过电子邮件非亲自发送的。  |例句：The contract was signed nonpersonally, using electronic signatures. 合同是通过电子签名非亲自签署的。|近义词：indirectly, remotely, impersonally |反义词：personally, directly
