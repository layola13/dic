I'll: /aɪl/ | pron. | 我会，我将 |  Contraction of "I will."  It expresses future intention, willingness, or prediction. |

例句：I'll help you with your homework. = 我会帮你做作业。

例句：I'll be there in five minutes. = 我五分钟后到。


近义词：I will, I shall


反义词：I won't (I will not)


词性：代词 (pronoun)  (It's a contraction, so its grammatical function is that of the pronoun "I" and the auxiliary verb "will")
