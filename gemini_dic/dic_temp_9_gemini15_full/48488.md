deepeners: /ˈdiːpənərz/ | n. |加深者；使加深的事物 |Things or processes that make something deeper or more intense.  The term often appears in specific contexts, such as in the context of  deepening financial problems or deepening relationships. |例句：The recent economic downturn has been a series of deepeners for the already fragile market. 近期经济低迷加剧了本已脆弱的市场。 The couple's shared experiences served as deepeners for their bond. 这对夫妻共同的经历加深了他们的感情。 |近义词：intensifiers, aggravators, exacerbators |反义词：alleviators, mitigators, reducers |名词
