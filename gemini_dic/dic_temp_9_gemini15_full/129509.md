non-Asiatics:/ˌnɑːn eɪˈʒætiks/| n. |非亚洲人|People who are not of Asian descent or origin.  The term is often used in a broad geographical sense, encompassing people from all continents except Asia. However, it can be considered outdated and potentially offensive due to its broad and potentially exclusionary nature.  It’s preferable to use more specific and respectful terminology when referring to people of different ethnic or national backgrounds.|The conference included participants from various countries, both Asians and non-Asiatics.|近义词：non-Asians (less broad, but still potentially problematic), people of other continents|反义词：Asians
