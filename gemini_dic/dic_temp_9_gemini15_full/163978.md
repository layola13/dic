predictiveness:/prɪˈdɪktɪvnəs/| n. |预见性；预测能力|The ability to predict future outcomes or events accurately.  This involves analyzing existing data and patterns to forecast what might happen next.  Predictiveness is crucial in fields like weather forecasting, financial modeling, and medical diagnosis.|例句：The model's high predictiveness allowed for accurate predictions of customer behavior.  该模型的高预见性使得能够准确预测客户行为。

The study demonstrated the strong predictiveness of the early warning system. 这项研究证明了早期预警系统的强大预见性。|近义词：forecasting ability, predictive power, prognostic ability|反义词：unpredictability, randomness, unreliability|名词
