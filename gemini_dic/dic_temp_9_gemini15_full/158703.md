plough-back:/plaʊˈbæk/| vt.| 再投资；重新投入；归还|To reinvest profits or resources back into a business or project.  This term often refers to reinvesting profits to further develop the business or fund future projects. |例句：The company decided to plough back its profits into research and development. 公司决定将其利润再投资于研发。

例句：They ploughed back a significant portion of their earnings into expanding their operations. 他们将大部分收益再投资于扩大经营规模。


近义词：reinvest, reinvestment, put back, return

反义词：withdraw, spend, distribute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company ploughs back its profits annually. = 公司每年将利润再投资。
We plough back a portion of our earnings into employee training. = 我们将部分收益再投资于员工培训。


一般过去时:
They ploughed back all their surplus into new equipment. = 他们将所有盈余都再投资于新设备。
The farmer ploughed back the manure into the field. = 农民将肥料重新施回田地。


现在完成时:
The firm has ploughed back considerable sums into upgrading its facilities. = 该公司已将大量资金再投资于升级其设施。
We have ploughed back most of our profits this year. = 今年我们已经将大部分利润再投资了。


过去完成时:
They had ploughed back all their savings before starting the new project. = 在启动新项目之前，他们已将所有积蓄都再投资了。
The company had ploughed back its profits for five years before expanding into new markets. = 在扩张到新市场之前，该公司已经将利润再投资了五年。


将来时:
We will plough back 50% of the profits into expanding our marketing efforts. = 我们将把50%的利润再投资于扩大我们的营销力度。
The company plans to plough back its earnings into new technology. = 公司计划将其收益再投资于新技术。


虚拟语气:
If they ploughed back their profits, the company would grow faster. = 如果他们将利润再投资，公司会发展得更快。
I wish they had ploughed back more money into marketing. = 我希望他们当初在营销方面投入更多资金。


被动语态:
A large portion of the profits was ploughed back into the business. = 大部分利润被再投资到企业中。
The money will be ploughed back into community projects. = 这笔钱将被再投资到社区项目中。


疑问句:
Will the company plough back its profits this year? = 公司今年会将利润再投资吗？
How much of the revenue will be ploughed back into R&D? = 多少收入将被再投资于研发？


否定句:
The company didn't plough back any of its profits last year. = 公司去年没有将任何利润再投资。
They chose not to plough back their earnings this quarter. = 他们选择在本季度不将收益再投资。
