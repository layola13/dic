pouncing: /ˈpaʊnsɪŋ/ | v. |猛扑，突然袭击 | The act of pouncing involves suddenly leaping or springing upon something or someone.  It often implies aggression, surprise, or swiftness. |例句：The cat pounced on the mouse.  猫猛扑向老鼠。

The cheetah pounced from behind a bush, catching its prey. 猎豹从灌木丛后猛扑出来，抓住了猎物。


近义词：leaping, springing, swooping, diving


反义词：slowly approaching, gently touching


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat pounces on the mouse. = 猫猛扑向老鼠。
He pounces on every opportunity. 他抓住每一个机会。


一般过去时:
The lion pounced on the zebra. = 狮子猛扑向斑马。
She pounced on the mistake in the report. 她发现了报告中的错误。


现在完成时:
The cat has pounced on several mice today. = 猫今天已经扑了好几只老鼠了。
He has pounced on the chance to travel abroad. 他抓住了出国旅行的机会。


过去完成时:
The bird had pounced before I could react. = 我还没反应过来，鸟就扑下来了。
She had pounced on the opportunity before anyone else. 她比任何人都快地抓住了这个机会。


将来时:
The cat will pounce when it sees a mouse. = 猫看到老鼠就会猛扑过去。
He will pounce at the first sign of weakness. 他会在看到第一个弱点时就扑上去。


虚拟语气:
If the cat saw a mouse, it would pounce. = 如果猫看到老鼠，它就会扑过去。
I wish I had pounced on that opportunity. 我真希望我当时抓住了那个机会。


被动语态:
The mouse was pounced upon by the cat. = 老鼠被猫扑住了。
The opportunity was pounced on by a competitor. 机会被竞争对手抢走了。


疑问句:
Did the cat pounce on the mouse? = 猫扑向老鼠了吗？
Will he pounce on the chance? 他会抓住机会吗？


否定句:
The cat did not pounce on the mouse. = 猫没有扑向老鼠。
He didn't pounce on the opportunity. 他没有抓住机会。


PHRASE->
pounce on an opportunity: 抓住机会
pounce on someone: 猛扑向某人
pounce on a mistake: 抓住错误
pounce with excitement: 兴奋地猛扑
pounce on the evidence: 抓住证据


词源:  The word "pounce" ultimately derives from the Old French word "poncer," meaning "to stamp," or "to punch."  This likely refers to the forceful impact of the action.  The sense shifted over time to focus on the act of a predator leaping on its prey.
