monotheists:/ˈmɒnəθiːɪsts/ | n. | 一神论者 | People who believe in the existence of only one god.  Monotheism is a belief system centered around the worship of a single deity, rejecting the concept of multiple gods or goddesses.  This belief has profoundly shaped various cultures and religions throughout history.  | Example: Judaism, Christianity, and Islam are all monotheistic religions.  犹太教、基督教和伊斯兰教都是一神教。  |近义词: unitarians, believers in one God |反义词: polytheists, pagans
