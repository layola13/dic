nonconforming:/ˌnɒnkənˈfɔːrmɪŋ/| adj. |不符合的；不一致的；不服从的 |Not conforming to a standard, rule, or specification.  It implies a failure to meet expectations or requirements. |例句：The product was rejected because it was nonconforming to the specifications.  该产品因不符合规格而被拒绝。

例句：His nonconforming behavior led to disciplinary action. 他的不符合规定的行为导致了纪律处分。


近义词：deviant, aberrant, inconsistent, noncompliant, irregular

反义词：conforming, compliant, consistent, regular
