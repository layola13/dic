peacemaker:/ˈpiːsˌmeɪkər/| n. | 和平缔造者；调解人 | A peacemaker is a person who helps to end conflicts or disputes between people or groups.  They work to create peace and harmony. | Example: The UN acted as a peacemaker in the conflict between the two nations.  联合国在两国冲突中充当了调解人的角色。 |近义词：mediator, arbitrator, conciliator |反义词：agitator, troublemaker
