loons:/luːn/| n.|潜鸟；傻瓜；疯子|A loon is a type of diving bird with a distinctive cry.  It can also be used informally to refer to a foolish or crazy person.|例句：The loon's haunting cry echoed across the lake.  潜鸟凄厉的叫声在湖面上回荡。|例句：He's a loon; he believes the earth is flat. 他是个疯子；他相信地球是平的。|近义词：diver (bird), crazy, insane, idiot|反义词：sane, rational, sensible
