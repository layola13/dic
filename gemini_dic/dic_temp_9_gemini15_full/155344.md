pharmacognosy:/ˌfɑːrməkˈnɒɡnəsi/ | n. |药物鉴定学；生药学 |The study of the physical, chemical, biochemical, and biological properties of drugs, especially those derived from natural sources (plants, animals, and minerals).  It encompasses the identification, isolation, and characterization of bioactive compounds from natural sources and their uses in medicine. |Pharmacognosy is a crucial field in the development of new drugs derived from natural sources.  药物鉴定学是开发源于天然来源的新药的关键领域。|近义词：ethnobotany (if focusing on plant sources), natural products chemistry |反义词: synthetic chemistry (studies drugs made artificially)
