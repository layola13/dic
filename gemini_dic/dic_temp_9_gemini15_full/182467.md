renotification:/ˌriːˌnoʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |重新通知；再次通知 |The act of notifying again; a second notification.  It often implies a previous notification that may have been missed, ignored, or deemed insufficient. |例句：The renotification of the meeting time was necessary due to a scheduling conflict. = 由于时间冲突，需要重新通知会议时间。

例句：The bank sent a renotification of the overdue payment. = 银行再次通知逾期付款。


近义词：reminder, second notice, fresh notification |反义词：initial notification, first notice |词性: noun
