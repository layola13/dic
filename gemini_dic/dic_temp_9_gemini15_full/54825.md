dispersible:/dɪˈspɜːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可分散的，可弥散的 | Able to be dispersed or scattered; capable of being distributed or spread widely.  | The powder is dispersible in water.  这种粉末可溶于水。(This example shows dispersible in the context of solubility, a common usage.)  The dye is dispersible throughout the fabric.  染料可以分散到整个织物中。(This illustrates dispersible in the sense of spreading widely.) |  diffusable, spreadable,  dispersive |  concentrated, localized |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dye is dispersible in water. = 染料可溶于水。
This powder is dispersible in most solvents. = 这种粉末在大多数溶剂中都可以分散。


一般过去时:
The pigment was dispersible in the oil-based paint. = 颜料可分散在油性涂料中。
The medication was easily dispersible throughout the body. = 该药物很容易在全身分散。


现在完成时:
The company has developed a new dispersible tablet formulation. = 公司已经开发了一种新型的可分散片剂配方。
The research has shown that the nanoparticles are highly dispersible in water. = 研究表明，纳米粒子在水中具有高度分散性。


过去完成时:
The engineers had ensured the material was dispersible before the launch. = 工程师们在发布之前已经确保了材料的可分散性。
They had found that the substance was less dispersible at lower temperatures. = 他们发现，该物质在较低温度下分散性较差。


将来时:
The new product will be highly dispersible in various media. = 新产品将在各种介质中具有高度分散性。
Scientists predict that the additive will be easily dispersible in the polymer. = 科学家预测该添加剂将易于分散在聚合物中。


虚拟语气:
If the particles were more dispersible, the mixture would be smoother. = 如果颗粒更易分散，混合物就会更光滑。
I wish the ink was more dispersible on this type of paper. = 我希望墨水在这种纸张上更易分散。


被动语态:

The particles were dispersed evenly by the high-speed mixer. = 通过高速混合器将颗粒均匀地分散。


疑问句:

Are the nanoparticles dispersible without the use of surfactants? = 纳米粒子在不使用表面活性剂的情况下是否可以分散？


否定句:
The mixture is not dispersible in water. = 这种混合物不能溶于水。
The powder was not easily dispersible without the addition of a dispersing agent. = 不添加分散剂，粉末不容易分散。
