extricable:/ˈɛkstrɪkəbl/ | adj. | 可解开的；可救出的；可摆脱的 | Able to be freed or disentangled.  Something that is extricable can be separated or removed from something else. | The two cars were so intertwined that they were almost extricable. 这两辆车纠缠在一起，几乎无法分开。  The situation seemed extricable; there was a way out after all.  情况似乎是可以解决的；毕竟还是有办法的。 |  disentangle, separate, untie, release | inextricable, entangled, trapped, impossible to solve
