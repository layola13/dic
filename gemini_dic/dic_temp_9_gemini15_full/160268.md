pony-trekking:/ˈpəʊniˌtrekɪŋ/| n. |骑矮马旅行；矮马骑乘|The activity of riding ponies, especially on trails or in scenic areas. It is often a leisurely and enjoyable way to explore the countryside.|例句：We went pony-trekking in the mountains and had a wonderful time.  我们在山里骑矮马旅行，玩得很开心。  We booked a pony-trekking tour for the whole family. 我们为全家预订了一次骑矮马旅行。|近义词：horseback riding, trail riding|反义词：None|名词
