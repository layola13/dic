sacker:/ˈsækər/| n. |掠夺者，洗劫者|A sacker is someone who sacks or plunders a place, typically during war or a riot.  They take goods and possessions by force.|例句：The sackers looted the city, leaving behind only destruction. 掠夺者洗劫了这座城市，只留下满目疮痍。The invading army's sackers were merciless. 入侵军队的掠夺者们毫不留情。|近义词：looter, plunderer, raider, pillager |反义词：protector, defender
