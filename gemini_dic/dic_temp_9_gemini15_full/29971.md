caviar:/ˈkævɪɑːr/| n. |鱼子酱|The salted roe of sturgeon, especially that of the beluga, osetra, and sevruga sturgeons, highly prized as a delicacy.  Caviar is typically served as an appetizer or hors d'oeuvre.|Example:  He ordered a small portion of beluga caviar as an appetizer. 他点了一小份白鲸鱼子酱作为开胃菜。|近义词：roe, fish eggs|反义词：N/A
