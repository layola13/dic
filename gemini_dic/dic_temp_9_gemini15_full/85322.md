harping:/ˈhɑːrpɪŋ/| v.|喋喋不休地谈论；反复强调；弹竖琴 | Continuously talking about something, especially in a tiresome way; playing the harp.  |例句：He's been harping on about the same thing all day. 他整天都在喋喋不休地谈论同一件事。
例句：She sat by the window, harping a gentle melody. 她坐在窗边，轻轻地弹奏着竖琴。|近义词：nagging, dwelling on, reiterating|反义词：being quiet, remaining silent

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is harping on about his problems. = 他一直在喋喋不休地抱怨他的问题。
They are harping on the same old tune. = 他们一直在反复强调老一套。


一般过去时:
She harped on the importance of punctuality. = 她反复强调准时的重要性。
He harped on the same subject for hours. = 他就同一个话题唠叨了几个小时。


现在完成时:
He has been harping on about the injustice for weeks. = 他已经抱怨这种不公正的事情好几个星期了。
She has harped on this issue since the beginning of the meeting. = 从会议开始她就一直在强调这个问题。


过去完成时:
He had been harping on about his lost keys for days before he finally found them. = 在他最终找到钥匙之前，他已经抱怨丢失的钥匙好几天了。
They had harped on the need for reform for years before any real changes were made. = 在任何真正的改变发生之前，他们多年来一直在强调改革的必要性。


将来时:
He will be harping on about his promotion all evening. = 他整个晚上都会喋喋不休地谈论他的晋升。
She will harp on the details of the report again tomorrow. = 她明天将再次详细地讲解报告。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't always harping on about his health, he'd be more enjoyable to be around. = 如果他不总是喋喋不休地谈论他的健康，相处起来会更愉快。
I wish she wouldn't harp on the mistakes I made. = 我真希望她不要总是念叨我犯的错误。


被动语态:
The same points were harped on throughout the meeting. = 在整个会议中，都反复强调了同样的要点。
The issue has been harped on relentlessly by the media. = 媒体一直在无情地强调这个问题。


疑问句:
Is he still harping on the same old story? = 他还在喋喋不休地谈论着老一套吗？
What are you harping on about now? = 你现在喋喋不休地谈论什么？


否定句:
He didn't harp on the negative aspects of the project. = 他没有喋喋不休地谈论该项目的负面影响。
She didn't harp on the details; she gave a concise summary. = 她没有详细说明，而是做了简洁的总结。


PHRASE->
harp on about something: 喋喋不休地谈论某事
harp on a theme: 反复强调一个主题
harp on the same string:  反复强调同一件事
harp on somebody's nerves:  使某人烦躁
harp on the negative:  总是强调消极方面
