reenlightened:/ˌriːɪnˈlaɪtnd/| adj.|重新启蒙的；重新教化的；获得新理解的 |Having been enlightened again; experiencing a renewed understanding or awareness, often after a period of darkness or misunderstanding.  |例句：After years of darkness, he felt reenlightened by the philosopher's wisdom.  多年黑暗之后，他感觉受到了这位哲学家智慧的重新启蒙。

例句：The reenlightened perspective allowed her to approach the problem with fresh insight.  重新获得的视角使她能够以新的见解来处理这个问题。


近义词：re-educated, re-inspired, newly enlightened,  illuminated

反义词：benighted, unenlightened, ignorant, confused


词性：形容词
