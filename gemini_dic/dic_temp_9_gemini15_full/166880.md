pressurizers:/ˈprɛʃəraɪzərz/ | n. |增压器，压力器 | A device or system used to increase pressure, typically in a fluid system.  It maintains pressure within a system or vessel.  Examples include those used in nuclear reactors, water systems, and aircraft cabins. |例句：The nuclear reactor's pressurizers maintain the necessary pressure for efficient operation. 核反应堆的增压器维持高效运行所需的压力。

The aircraft's pressurizers keep the cabin at a comfortable altitude. 飞机的增压器使客舱保持舒适的高度。|近义词：pressure regulators, compressors, pumps |反义词：depressurizers, vacuum pumps |名词
