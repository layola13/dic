sjamboked: /ˈʃæmbəkt/ | v. |  痛打；严厉斥责 |  The word "sjamboked" is a South African English slang term derived from the Afrikaans word "sjambok," referring to a type of whip.  Therefore, "sjamboked" means to beat severely with a sjambok. It can also be used figuratively to mean to reprimand or scold harshly. | Example: He was sjamboked for his insolence. 他因为无礼而被狠狠地痛打了一顿。  |近义词：flogged, thrashed, caned, severely reprimanded | 反义词：praised, commended, rewarded | 动词
