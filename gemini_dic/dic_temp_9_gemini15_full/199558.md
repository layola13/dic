singleframe: /ˈsɪŋɡəlfreɪm/ | adj. | 单帧的，单画面 |  A singleframe image or video is one that consists of a single frame, without any sequence or motion.  It's often used in animation or video editing to describe a still image within a larger sequence.  | Example: The animator showed me a singleframe rendering of the character's face.  动画师给我看了一张角色面部的单帧渲染图。 |  近义词：still, static | 反义词：multiframe, animated
