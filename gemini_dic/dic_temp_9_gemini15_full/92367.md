hypochondriacs:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪæks/|/ˈhaɪpəˌkɑːndrɪæks/| n. | 疑病症患者；臆想病人 |A hypochondriac is someone who is excessively worried about their health and often believes they have a serious illness, even when there is no medical evidence to support this belief. They may constantly seek medical attention and interpret normal bodily functions as signs of disease.  | Example:  He's a hypochondriac; he goes to the doctor for every little ache and pain. 他是个疑病症患者；他为每一丁点儿的疼痛都去看医生。


Example:  Many hypochondriacs spend hours researching their symptoms online, often leading to increased anxiety and fear. 许多疑病症患者会花数小时在网上搜索他们的症状，这往往会导致焦虑和恐惧加剧。|近义词：health worrywart, neurotic (in this context) |反义词： stoic, healthy individual (in the absence of illness)
