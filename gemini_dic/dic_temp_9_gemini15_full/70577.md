fillibeg:/ˈfɪlɪbɛɡ/ | n. |苏格兰短裙 | A kilt, especially one worn by men in Scotland.  A fillibeg is a type of kilt, traditionally made of pleated tartan cloth and worn by men in Scotland. It typically reaches to the knees and is often worn with a sporran (a type of pouch) and other traditional Highland dress accessories.  | Example: He wore a fillibeg in the Highland Games. 他在高地运动会上穿了一件苏格兰短裙。 |近义词: kilt, Highland dress |反义词: trousers, pants |名词
