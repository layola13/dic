refinancing:/ˌriːˈfaɪnænsɪŋ/| n. |再融资，重新贷款|The act of obtaining a new loan to pay off an existing one, typically with more favorable terms.  This often involves securing a lower interest rate, changing the loan term, or consolidating multiple debts. |Example:  They refinanced their mortgage to get a lower interest rate.他们进行了房屋抵押贷款再融资以获得更低的利率。

Example: The company refinanced its debt to reduce its monthly payments.  这家公司进行了债务再融资以减少月供。


近义词：renegotiation, restructuring | 反义词：initial financing, original loan


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refinances his car loan every few years. 他每隔几年就对他的汽车贷款进行再融资。
The bank offers various refinancing options.  银行提供各种再融资方案。


一般过去时:
