generalizations:/ˌdʒɛnərəlɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |概括；普遍化；推广 |The act of making a general statement or forming a general opinion from particular instances.  It involves drawing conclusions from specific examples and applying them broadly.  Generalizations can be helpful for simplifying complex situations, but they can also be inaccurate or misleading if not based on sufficient evidence or if applied inappropriately. |例句：His generalizations about women were offensive and inaccurate. 他关于女人的概括性说法令人反感且不准确。

例句：The study's generalizations are based on a limited sample size. 这项研究的概括基于有限的样本量。


近义词：summarizations, summaries, abstractions, generalizations


反义词：specifics, details, particulars


词性：名词
