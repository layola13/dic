overhandles: /ˌoʊvərˈhændəlz/ | v. | 过度处理；处理过度 | To handle something excessively or improperly.  It implies an over-involvement or unnecessary intervention, often leading to negative consequences. | 例句：He overhandled the situation, making it worse. 他过度处理了这件事，反而弄巧成拙。  The company overhandled the crisis, damaging its reputation. 这家公司过度处理了危机，损坏了它的声誉。 | 近义词： mishandle, mismanage, overwork, overprocess | 反义词：underhandle (less common), neglect, ignore | 动词
