roaring:/ˈrɔːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 轰鸣的；咆哮的；喧闹的 |  making a loud, deep sound like a lion; very loud and forceful.  |例句：The roaring engine shook the ground. 轰鸣的引擎震动了地面。

例句：The crowd roared their approval. 人群爆发出赞许的欢呼声。


近义词：bellowing, booming, thunderous, clamorous | 反义词：quiet, silent, hushed


词源：源自古英语 *hrōrian，意思是“咆哮，喧闹”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine is roaring. = 引擎正在轰鸣。
The crowd roars with laughter. = 人群爆发出笑声。


一般过去时:
The lion roared loudly. = 狮子大声咆哮。
The audience roared its approval. = 观众大声喝彩。


现在完成时:
The storm has been roaring all night. = 暴风雨整夜都在咆哮。
The crowd has roared its disapproval. = 人群发出了反对的声音。


过去完成时:
The volcano had been roaring for days before it erupted. = 火山爆发前几天一直在咆哮。
The audience had roared with laughter before the comedian even started. = 喜剧演员还没开始表演，观众就已经笑声如雷了。

The crowd will roar with excitement. = 人群将兴奋地欢呼。


虚拟语气:
