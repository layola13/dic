lepidopterous:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərəs/| adj.|鳞翅目的；像鳞翅目的| relating to or characteristic of the Lepidoptera (butterflies and moths).  The term refers to the insects' characteristic scaly wings. |例句：Many lepidopterous insects are brightly colored. 许多鳞翅目昆虫颜色鲜艳。

例句：The lepidopterous collection at the museum was extensive. 博物馆里鳞翅目昆虫的收藏非常丰富。

反义词：无
