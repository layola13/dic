cable-cars:/ˈkeɪbəl kɑːrz/| n. |缆车 |A cable car is a type of vehicle that is pulled up and down a slope by a cable. They are often used in mountainous areas or to transport people across difficult terrain.  |Example: We rode the cable cars up to the top of the mountain for a breathtaking view. 我们乘坐缆车到达山顶，欣赏了令人叹为观止的景色。|近义词：aerial tramway, funicular |反义词：无
