hacking:/ˈhækɪŋ/| n. v.|黑客攻击；入侵；擅自进入；猛砍；劈；乱砍；|The act of gaining unauthorized access to a computer system or network.  It can also refer to the act of forcefully breaking something. |例句：The company suffered a major hacking incident last week.  上周该公司遭遇了一起重大黑客攻击事件。

例句：He was hacking at the ice with an axe. 他正用斧头猛砍冰块。|近义词：intrusion, breach, cracking, slashing, chopping |反义词：protection, security, safeguarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hacks into secure systems. 他入侵安全系统。
They are hacking the code. 他们在破解代码。

一般过去时:
She hacked the bank's website. 她黑进了银行的网站。
The hackers hacked the server last night. 黑客们昨晚入侵了服务器。

现在完成时:
They have hacked several accounts. 他们已经入侵了几个账户。
The government has acknowledged the hacking attempts. 政府已经承认了这些黑客攻击企图。

过去完成时:
The police discovered that he had hacked the system before the incident. 警方发现他在事件发生前就入侵了系统。
They had hacked the database before we realized it.  在我们意识到之前，他们就已经入侵了数据库。

将来时:

否定句:
