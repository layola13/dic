prerefining:/ˌpriːˈrɪfaɪnɪŋ/| n.|预精炼；预提炼|The process of partially refining a substance before further processing.  This often involves removing impurities or preparing the substance for a more complete refining process later.|例句：The prerefining stage significantly reduced the impurities in the crude oil. 预精炼阶段显著减少了原油中的杂质。

例句：The prerefining of the metal improved its malleability. 金属的预精炼提高了它的延展性。|近义词：preliminary refining, initial purification|反义词：final refining, complete purification|名词
