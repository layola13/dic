reduces:/rɪˈduːsɪz/ | v. | 减少；缩小；简化 | To make or become smaller or less in amount, size, importance, etc.  |  例句：The new policy reduces pollution. 新政策减少了污染。
例句：He reduces his expenses. 他减少了他的开支。|近义词：lessen, decrease, diminish |反义词：increase, enlarge, augment | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government reduces taxes every year. = 政府每年都减税。
Regular exercise reduces the risk of heart disease. = 定期锻炼可以降低患心脏病的风险。


一般过去时:
The company reduced its workforce last year. = 公司去年裁员了。
The teacher reduced the workload for the students. = 老师减少了学生的作业量。


现在完成时:
They have reduced their carbon footprint significantly. = 他们已经大大减少了碳足迹。
We have reduced the price of our products. = 我们已经降低了产品的价格。


过去完成时:
She had reduced her weight before the wedding. = 她在婚礼前减了体重。
They had reduced the number of employees before the merger. = 他们在合并之前已经裁员了。


将来时:
The factory will reduce its emissions next year. = 工厂明年将减少排放。
The company will reduce its operating costs. = 公司将降低运营成本。


虚拟语气:
If they reduced prices, they would sell more goods. = 如果他们降价，他们就会卖出更多商品。
I wish the government reduced taxes more. = 我希望政府能更多地减税。


被动语态:
Taxes are reduced by the government annually. = 税收每年由政府减免。
The workload was reduced for the students. = 学生的作业量减少了。


疑问句:
Does this medicine reduce pain? = 这药能止痛吗？
Will this plan reduce costs? = 这个计划会降低成本吗？


否定句:
The new policy doesn't reduce poverty. = 新政策并没有减少贫困。
He didn't reduce his spending. = 他没有减少开支。


PHRASE->
reduce to: 减少到…
reduce by: 减少了…
reduce the risk of: 降低…的风险
reduce stress: 减压
reduce speed: 减速
