isopodiform:/ˌaɪsoʊˈpɒdɪfɔːrm/| adj.|等足类动物形的；似等足类的|Resembling an isopod in form.  The term refers to the body shape and characteristics of isopods, which are crustaceans with flattened bodies and seven pairs of legs.|例句：The newly discovered species exhibited an isopodiform body plan, with a flattened dorsoventral axis and numerous legs.  这种新发现的物种呈现出等足类动物形的体型，具有扁平的背腹轴和许多腿。|近义词：isopod-like|反义词：elongate, cylindrical
