retheorization:/ˌriːθiːəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 重新理论化，再理论化 | The act of re-examining and reinterpreting existing theories, often leading to a revised or new understanding.  It involves revisiting established ideas, concepts, or frameworks to offer a fresh perspective or accommodate new evidence.  This process often challenges assumptions and leads to a more nuanced or comprehensive understanding. | Example: The retheorization of gender in feminist scholarship has broadened our understanding of societal structures.  重新理论化性别在女权主义学术中的研究拓宽了我们对社会结构的理解。|近义词：reinterpretation, reformulation, revision |反义词：acceptance, affirmation
