overbraces:/ˈoʊvərbreɪsɪz/ | n. | 牙套，矫正器 |  Overbraces are orthodontic appliances used to straighten teeth.  They are typically made of metal or ceramic brackets bonded to the teeth, connected by wires. |  The dentist fitted her with overbraces to correct her overbite.  牙医给她装上了牙套来矫正她的深覆合。| braces, retainers |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps "unaligned teeth") | 名词
