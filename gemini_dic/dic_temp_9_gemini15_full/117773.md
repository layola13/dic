melding:/ˈmɛldɪŋ/| n. |融合；合并；混合|The act of melding or the state of being melded; combining things to form a whole.  It often implies a smooth or seamless integration. |Example: The artist achieved a remarkable melding of different artistic styles in her latest work.  艺术家在她最新的作品中巧妙地融合了不同的艺术风格。|近义词：blending, fusing, combining, merging|反义词：separating, dividing, splitting|名词
