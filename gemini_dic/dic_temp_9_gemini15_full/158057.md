plasmatic:/plæˈzæmætɪk/| adj.|血浆的；等离子体的|Relating to or resembling plasma, especially blood plasma.  The term is used to describe something that is similar in nature or properties to plasma. |例句：The plasmatic membrane surrounds the cell. 血浆膜包围着细胞。

例句：The study focused on the plasmatic components of blood. 这项研究集中在血液的血浆成分上。


近义词：plasmic, serous (in the context of blood plasma)

反义词：cellular (in the context of blood components)

词性:形容词
