pectinated:/ˈpektɪneɪtɪd/| adj.| 梳状的；齿状的 | Having the form or arrangement of a comb; resembling a comb in structure.  The term often describes structures with closely set, parallel elements resembling the teeth of a comb. |例句：The pectinated edges of the leaf were clearly visible. 叶子梳状的边缘清晰可见。
The pectinated antennae of the insect helped it sense its surroundings.  昆虫梳状的触角帮助它感知周围环境。


近义词：comb-like, toothed, serrate (if the teeth are pointed)

反义词：smooth, untoothed


词性：形容词
