parting-with: /ˈpɑːrtɪŋ wɪð/ | v. | 与…分离；放弃；卖掉 | To separate from; to give up; to sell.  |The phrase implies a sense of relinquishing something, often with some reluctance or sadness. It suggests a more deliberate and conscious act than simply "giving up" something. |

例句：

She was heartbroken at parting with her childhood home. = 她与童年故居分离，心都碎了。

He decided to part with his valuable stamp collection. = 他决定卖掉他珍贵的邮票收藏。


近义词：separating from, giving up, relinquishing, letting go of, selling, disposing of

反义词：acquiring, keeping, retaining, holding onto


词性：动词 (verb)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are parting with our old car. = 我们正在卖掉我们的旧车。
They part with their belongings reluctantly. = 他们不情愿地放弃他们的财物。

一般过去时:
I parted with my favorite book. = 我卖掉了我最喜欢的书。
She parted with her old bicycle yesterday. = 她昨天卖掉了她的旧自行车。

现在完成时:
They have parted with most of their possessions. = 他们已经放弃了大部分财产。
He has parted with his shares in the company. = 他已经卖掉了他在公司里的股份。

过去完成时:
I had parted with my old phone before I got a new one. = 在我买新手机之前，我已经卖掉了我的旧手机。
She had parted with her engagement ring before the wedding was called off. = 在婚礼取消之前，她已经摘下了订婚戒指。

将来时:
We will part with our apartment next month. = 我们下个月将搬出我们的公寓。
They will part with their summer house soon.  = 他们很快就会卖掉他们的避暑别墅。

虚拟语气:
If I had to part with one of my children, I don't know what I would do. = 如果我不得不和我的一个孩子分别，我不知道我会做什么。
I wish I hadn't parted with that painting years ago. = 我真希望几年前我没有卖掉那幅画。

被动语态:
The old house was parted with after many years of happy memories. = 那栋旧房子在住了许多幸福的年之后被卖掉了。
The antique furniture will be parted with at auction. = 古董家具将在拍卖会上出售。


疑问句:
Are you parting with your car? = 你要卖掉你的车吗？
Will they be parting with their business? = 他们会卖掉他们的生意吗？

否定句:
He wasn't willing to part with his lucky charm. = 他不愿意放弃他的幸运符。
She wouldn't part with the photograph, no matter what. = 无论如何，她都不会放弃这张照片。

PHRASE->
part with one's life = 牺牲生命
part with money = 花钱；付钱
part with something reluctantly = 不情愿地放弃某物
part with a secret = 透露秘密
part with a friend = 与朋友分离
