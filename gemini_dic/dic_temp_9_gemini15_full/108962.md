leery:/ˈlɪəri/| adj.| 怀疑的；不信任的；警惕的 |Suspicious; wary; distrustful.  Someone who is leery is cautious and distrustful, often because of past negative experiences. They are hesitant to believe or trust something or someone. |例句：I'm a little leery of his promises. 我对他许下的诺言有点怀疑。

例句：She was leery of signing the contract without reading it carefully. 她对在仔细阅读之前就签署合同感到警惕。


近义词：wary, suspicious, distrustful, skeptical, cautious

反义词：trusting, credulous, unsuspecting


词性:形容词
