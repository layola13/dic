sensitize:/ˈsɛnsaɪz/| vt.|使敏感；使过敏；使觉察到|To make someone or something more aware of something, or more likely to react to something.|The campaign aimed to sensitize the public to the issue of homelessness. 这项运动旨在让公众更加关注无家可归者的问题。|  make aware, alert, arouse|desensitize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We sensitize the film to light. = 我们使胶片对光敏感。
The doctor sensitizes the patient to potential allergic reactions. = 医生使病人对潜在的过敏反应敏感。


一般过去时:
The experience sensitized her to the needs of others. = 那次经历使她对别人的需求更加敏感。
They sensitized the public to the dangers of smoking. = 他们使公众意识到吸烟的危害。


现在完成时:
Years of exposure have sensitized my skin to sunlight. = 多年的日晒使我的皮肤对阳光过敏。
The recent events have sensitized many people to social injustice. = 最近发生的事件使许多人更加关注社会不公。


过去完成时:
The previous training had sensitized the team to potential risks. = 此前的培训使团队对潜在风险更加敏感。
She had sensitized herself to the nuances of the language. = 她之前已经让自己对语言的细微之处更加敏感。


将来时:
This program will sensitize students to different cultures. = 这个项目将使学生们对不同的文化更加敏感。
We will sensitize the public to the importance of environmental protection. = 我们将使公众意识到环境保护的重要性。


虚拟语气:
If we sensitized the population earlier, the problem wouldn't be so severe. = 如果我们早点提高公众意识，问题就不会这么严重了。
I wish they had sensitized me to the possible side effects. = 我希望他们当时已经让我了解可能的副作用。


被动语态:
The audience was sensitized to the plight of refugees. = 观众对难民的困境产生了共鸣。
The students are being sensitized to the ethical dilemmas of the profession. = 学生们正在被教育了解该行业的伦理困境。


疑问句:
How can we sensitize people to climate change? = 我们该如何让人们关注气候变化？
Has the campaign successfully sensitized the public? = 这项运动成功地提高了公众意识吗？


否定句:
The campaign failed to sensitize the public to the issue. = 这项运动未能引起公众对这个问题的关注。
He wasn't sensitized to the cultural differences. = 他没有意识到文化差异。


PHRASE->
sensitize to = 使对……敏感
become sensitized to = 对……变得敏感
highly sensitized = 高度敏感的
sensitize oneself to = 使自己对……敏感
be sensitized by = 被……所触动
