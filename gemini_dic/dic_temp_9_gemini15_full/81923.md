grilling:/ˈɡrɪlɪŋ/| n. v. |烧烤；严厉盘问；折磨|

英文解析:
As a noun, "grilling" refers to the process of cooking food over direct, intense heat, typically on a grill.  As a verb, it means to cook food in this way. It can also refer to a vigorous and intense questioning, often in a formal setting, such as an interrogation.  Figuratively, it can describe a harsh or difficult experience.

例句:
The grilling of the steaks was perfect. = 牛排烤得恰到好处。

We enjoyed a relaxing evening of grilling burgers in the backyard. = 我们在后院享受了一个轻松的晚上，烤汉堡。

The police grilling of the suspect lasted for hours. = 警方对嫌疑人的严厉盘问持续了数小时。

The job interview was a real grilling. = 这次工作面试简直就是一场严厉的盘问。

近义词:
(noun) barbecuing, broiling, roasting; (verb) interrogating, questioning, cross-examining,  tormenting

反义词:
(noun) boiling, frying, stewing; (verb) praising, comforting, coddling
