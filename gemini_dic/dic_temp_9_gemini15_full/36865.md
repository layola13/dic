colorable:/ˈkʌlərəbl/ | adj. | 可着色的；貌似有理的；可辩解的 |  Appearing true, reasonable, or fair; able to be colored.  It suggests something that might be plausibly defended or justified, even if not entirely legitimate. |


例句：

His excuse for being late was colorable, but I still wasn't convinced. 他的迟到理由貌似有理，但我仍然不太相信。

The artist found the fabric colorable, easily taking on vibrant hues.  艺术家发现这种布料易于着色，很容易呈现出鲜艳的色彩。


近义词：plausible, credible, defensible, justifiable

反义词：implausible, incredible, indefensible, unjustifiable


词源：源自古法语 *colorable,  由color (颜色) + -able (可…的)构成。最初指“可着色的”，后来引申出“貌似有理的”的含义。
