feudary:/ˈfjuːdəri/| n.|封臣，附庸|A feudary is a person who holds land from a feudal lord in return for military service or other obligations.  The term often refers to a vassal in a feudal system. |例句：Many knights were feudaries of powerful nobles. 许多骑士都是强大贵族的封臣。|例句：The king expected loyalty from all his feudaries. 国王期望所有他的封臣都忠诚。|近义词：vassal, retainer, subject|反义词：overlord, sovereign


USAGE->

He is a feudary of the Duke. = 他是公爵的封臣。
The feudary serves his lord faithfully. = 这个封臣忠实地侍奉他的领主。
