photofits:/ˈfoʊtoʊfɪts/ | n. | 拼合照片，合成照片 | A photofit is a composite image of a person's face created from a description given by a witness.  It is used by law enforcement to help identify suspects. | The police released a photofit of the suspect to the media.  警方向媒体发布了嫌疑人的拼合照片。| composite image, identikit |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：actual photograph, real image) | 名词
