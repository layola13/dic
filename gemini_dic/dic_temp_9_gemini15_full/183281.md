reprioritize:/ˌriːˈpraɪɔːraɪtaɪz/| vt.|重新排序优先级，重新确定优先顺序 |To reconsider the order of priority of tasks or items; to assign a new order of importance.  |例句：We need to reprioritize our tasks to meet the deadline. 我们需要重新安排任务的优先级以赶上最后期限。|例句：The company had to reprioritize its investments after the market downturn. 公司在市场低迷后不得不重新确定投资的优先顺序。|近义词：re-evaluate, rearrange, reorder, reassess |反义词：maintain the priority, leave unchanged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reprioritize their work every Monday. = 他们每周一都会重新安排工作优先级。
We reprioritize our projects based on client needs. = 我们根据客户需求重新安排项目的优先级。

一般过去时:
She reprioritized her to-do list after the emergency. = 紧急情况发生后，她重新安排了待办事项的优先级。
The team reprioritized their goals following the new strategy. = 团队在新策略之后重新确定了目标的优先级。

现在完成时:
They have reprioritized their budget allocation this year. = 他们今年已经重新分配了预算。
The project manager has reprioritized tasks to ensure timely completion. = 项目经理已经重新安排了任务的优先级以确保按时完成。

过去完成时:
They had reprioritized their research focus before the funding cut. = 在资金削减之前，他们已经重新确定了研究重点的优先级。
The company had reprioritized its marketing campaigns after the poor sales results. = 在销售业绩不佳之后，公司已经重新调整了营销活动的优先级。

将来时:
We will reprioritize our efforts once we receive the new data. = 我们一旦收到新数据，就会重新安排我们的工作重点。
The government will reprioritize its spending plans in response to the crisis. = 政府将针对危机重新调整其支出计划。

虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would reprioritize our development goals. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们会重新设定我们的发展目标的优先级。
I wish we could reprioritize our tasks to allow for more collaboration. = 我希望我们可以重新安排任务的优先级，以便进行更多合作。

被动语态:
The tasks were reprioritized by the project manager. = 任务的优先级被项目经理重新安排了。

疑问句:

否定句:

The company will not reprioritize its research and development efforts. = 公司不会重新安排其研发工作的优先级。
