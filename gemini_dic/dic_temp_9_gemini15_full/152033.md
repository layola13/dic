patchworking:/ˈpætʃˌwɜːrkɪŋ/| n. |拼凑；拼缀；修补；(尤指)拼布|The act of creating something by combining different pieces of material, often fabric scraps, in a decorative way.  It often implies a somewhat haphazard or improvisational approach.|例句：Her quilt is a beautiful example of patchworking. 她的被子是拼布的精美例子。

例句：The artist created a striking artwork through clever patchworking of various textures and colors. 艺术家通过巧妙地拼凑各种纹理和颜色创造出一件引人注目的作品。


近义词：piecing, quilting, mending, repairing|反义词：creating from scratch, making something new entirely|词性：名词
