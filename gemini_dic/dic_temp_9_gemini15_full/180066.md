reflexed:/rɪˈflɛkst/ | adj. | 反折的；弯曲的 | Bent or turned backward or inward.  The term often describes plant parts, such as leaves or petals, that curve backward. | Example: The petals of the flower were beautifully reflexed. 这朵花的花瓣向后优雅地弯曲着。 | Near synonyms: recurved, curved backward, bent back | Antonyms: erect, straight, forward-pointing
