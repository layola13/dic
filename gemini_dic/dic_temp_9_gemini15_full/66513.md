exhalation:/ˌɛks.həˈleɪ.ʃən/ | n. |呼气，呼出；散发 | The act of exhaling; the process of breathing out.  It can also refer to the release or emission of something, such as a gas or vapor. |Example:  The deep exhalation calmed her nerves. 深呼吸使她平静下来。

The gentle exhalation of steam from the kettle filled the kitchen. 水壶轻轻地冒出的蒸汽充满了厨房。 |近义词：expiration, emission, release |反义词：inhalation
