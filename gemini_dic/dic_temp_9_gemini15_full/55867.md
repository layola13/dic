divinization:/ˌdɪvɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |神化；奉为神明；神格化 |The process of elevating someone or something to divine status, or attributing divine qualities to them.  It often involves the ascription of supernatural powers, attributes, or characteristics.  | Example: The divinization of emperors in ancient Rome was a common practice. 罗马皇帝的神化在古代是一种普遍的做法。The divinization of national leaders can be a dangerous form of political manipulation.  国家领导人的神化可能是危险的政治操纵形式。 |近义词：deification, apotheosis, elevation |反义词：degradation, debasement, secularization |名词
