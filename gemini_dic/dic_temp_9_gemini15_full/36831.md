colonises:/ˈkɒlənaɪzɪz/ | v. |殖民；占据；迁居 |To establish a colony or colonies in (a place).  It also implies the dominance and often exploitation of the indigenous population. |例句：The British colonised much of India during the 18th and 19th centuries.  英国在18和19世纪殖民了大部分印度。

The European powers colonised vast territories across the globe.  欧洲列强殖民了全球大片领土。


近义词：occupy, settle, conquer | 反义词：decolonize, liberate
