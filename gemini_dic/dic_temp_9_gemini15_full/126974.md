narrowses:/ˈnærəʊzɪz/| v.|变窄；使变窄 |The river narrows as it approaches the sea. |近义词：constricts, contracts, shrinks |反义词：widens, expands


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river narrows at this point. = 河流在这一点变窄。
The road narrows significantly ahead. = 前面的路明显变窄了。


一般过去时:
The passage narrowed considerably. = 通道明显变窄了。
The bridge narrowed the river's flow. = 桥梁使河水的流速变慢了。


现在完成时:
The path has narrowed over the years. = 这条路多年来已经变窄了。
The government has narrowed its focus on education. = 政府已经将重点缩小到教育方面。


过去完成时:
The road had narrowed before the accident. = 事故发生前，道路已经变窄了。
The river had narrowed significantly due to drought. = 由于干旱，河流已经明显变窄了。


将来时:
The valley will narrow as we ascend. = 当我们上升时，山谷会变窄。
The company will narrow its product line. = 公司将缩减其产品线。


虚拟语气:
If the tunnel narrowed further, it would be impassable. = 如果隧道进一步变窄，它将无法通行。
I wish the gap between rich and poor hadn't narrowed so much. = 我希望贫富差距没有缩小这么多。


被动语态:
The road was narrowed for construction. = 道路因施工而被拓宽。
The river's flow was narrowed by the dam. = 河水的流量被水坝限制了。


疑问句:
Does the road narrow further ahead? = 道路前面还会更窄吗？
Does this policy narrow the gap between the rich and the poor? = 这一政策是否缩小了贫富差距？


否定句:
The river doesn't narrow until it reaches the ocean. = 河流直到到达海洋才变窄。
The company will not narrow its marketing efforts. = 公司不会缩减其营销力度。
