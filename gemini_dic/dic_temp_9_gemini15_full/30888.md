chaffinches:/ˈtʃæfɪntʃɪz/| n.|欧亚树麻雀|A small, common Eurasian finch (Fringilla coelebs) with a reddish-brown breast in the male.  The female is duller. Chaffinches are known for their cheerful song.|例句：We saw several chaffinches flitting amongst the branches. 我们看到几只欧亚树麻雀在树枝间飞舞。

例句：The male chaffinch's reddish-brown breast is a distinctive feature. 雄性欧亚树麻雀红棕色的胸部是一个显著特征。


近义词：finch (a more general term)| 反义词：无
