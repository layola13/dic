/ˌhaɪpəˈmeɪniæk/ | n. |躁狂症患者；极度兴奋的人 | A person experiencing a manic episode, characterized by elevated mood, increased energy, and impulsive behavior.  The term often implies a less severe form of mania than seen in bipolar disorder.  | Example:  He exhibited hypermanic behavior, talking rapidly and making grandiose plans. 他表现出躁狂的行为，说话很快，并制定了宏伟的计划。 |近义词：manic, hyperactive |反义词：depressed, lethargic
