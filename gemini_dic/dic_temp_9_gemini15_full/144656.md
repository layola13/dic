outwrest:/ˌaʊtˈrɛst/ | vt. |夺取，强夺，暴力夺取 |to take (something) away from someone by force; to wrest from someone's possession or control | The thief outwrested the purse from the old woman. 小偷从老妇人那里抢走了钱包。  He outwrested control of the company from his rivals. 他从竞争对手手中夺取了公司的控制权。| wrest, seize, snatch | yield, surrender | 动词


USAGE->

He outwrests the ball from his opponent. = 他从对手那里抢走了球。
