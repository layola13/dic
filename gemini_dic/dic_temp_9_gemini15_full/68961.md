fatherly: /ˈfɑːðərli/ | adj. |  父亲般的；慈祥的；像父亲一样的 |  Having the qualities or manner of a father; characterized by paternal care, kindness, or authority.  |例句：He showed a fatherly concern for his employees' well-being. 他对员工的福祉表现出父亲般的关怀。

例句：His fatherly advice guided me through difficult times. 他像父亲一样的建议帮助我度过了难关。


近义词：paternal, fatherlike, protective, caring, solicitous

反义词：uncaring, unfatherly, harsh, cold, indifferent


词性：形容词
