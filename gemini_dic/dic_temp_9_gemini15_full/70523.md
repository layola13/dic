filial:/ˈfɪliəl/| adj.|子女的；孝顺的；|Of or relating to a son or daughter.  Showing respect and affection for parents or family. |例句：He showed great filial piety by caring for his aging parents. 他通过照顾年迈的父母表现出极大的孝顺。  His filial devotion was evident in his constant support of his mother. 他对母亲的孝顺之情在他持续的支持中显而易见。|近义词：pious, dutiful, respectful|反义词：disobedient, disrespectful, impious|
