mythbusting: /ˈmɪθˌbʌstɪŋ/ | n. | 揭穿迷思；辟谣；破除迷信 | The act of debunking myths or false beliefs.  It often involves using evidence and logic to contradict popular misconceptions.  | 例句：The documentary engaged in mythbusting, revealing the truth behind several long-held beliefs. 这部纪录片致力于揭穿迷思，揭示了几个长期存在的错误观念背后的真相。

The recent mythbusting campaign successfully corrected misinformation about vaccines. 最近的辟谣活动成功地纠正了关于疫苗的错误信息。| 近义词：debunking, fact-checking, exposing | 反义词：perpetuating myths, spreading misinformation |  词性：名词
