overcounting:/ˌoʊvərˈkaʊntɪŋ/ | n. | 过度计数，重复计数 | The act of counting something more than once, leading to an inflated total.  It often occurs when items are inadvertently counted multiple times in a counting process. | Example:  Overcounting of votes led to inaccurate election results.  过度计票导致选举结果不准确。 | 近义词：multiple counting, redundant counting | 反义词：accurate counting, precise counting
