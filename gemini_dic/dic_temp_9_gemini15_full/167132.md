presweetened:/ˌpriːˈswiːtn/| adj. | 预先加糖的；预先甜化的 | Already sweetened before packaging or use.  This adjective describes food or drinks that have had sugar added to them during processing, before they are sold or consumed. | Example: The presweetened cereal is convenient but high in sugar.  预先加糖的麦片很方便，但含糖量很高。  The presweetened iced tea was too sugary for my taste. 预先加糖的冰茶对我来说太甜了。|近义词：sweetened, sugared |反义词：unsweetened, plain
