gobbed:/ɡɒbd/| vt.|吐口水；猛塞；狼吞虎咽|To eject saliva forcefully from the mouth; to put something into something else quickly and roughly; to eat something greedily and quickly.|例句：He gobbed a mouthful of food into his mouth. 他把一大口食物猛塞进嘴里。
He gobbed at the rude man. 他朝那个粗鲁的男人吐口水。|近义词：spat, crammed, devoured|反义词：refined, elegant, delicately
