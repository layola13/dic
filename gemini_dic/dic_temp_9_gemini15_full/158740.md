plowable:/ˈplaʊəbəl/| adj.| 可耕的，适于耕作的 | Suitable for plowing; capable of being plowed.  |The farmer assessed the field to determine if it was plowable.  这个农民评估了这块地是否适于耕作。| cultivable, arable | rocky, stony, untillable | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The land is plowable. = 这块地是可耕的。
This field is plowable in spring. = 这块田地春天可以耕种。


一般过去时:
The field was plowable last year. = 这块地去年是可耕的。
The land wasn't plowable after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，这块地无法耕种。


现在完成时:
The land has been plowable since the drainage improvements. = 自从排水设施改进后，这块地一直可以耕种。
The farmer has found that this section of land is finally plowable. = 农民发现这部分土地终于可以耕种了。


过去完成时:
The field had been plowable until the drought hit. = 直到干旱来临之前，这块地都是可以耕种的。
The land hadn't been plowable for years before the irrigation system was installed. = 在灌溉系统安装之前，这块地多年来都不能耕种。


将来时:
The field will be plowable after the snow melts. = 雪融化后，这块地就可以耕种了。
The land will not be plowable until the spring rains arrive. = 直到春雨到来，这块地才能耕种。


虚拟语气:
If the land were plowable, we would plant crops. = 如果这块地可以耕种，我们就种植庄稼。
I wish this field were plowable so I could plant potatoes. = 我希望这块地可以耕种，这样我就可以种植土豆了。


被动语态:
The soil was deemed plowable by the agricultural engineer. = 这块土壤被农业工程师认定为可耕地。
The land is being prepared to be plowable for the next season. = 这块土地正在准备用于下个季节的耕种。


疑问句:
Is the land plowable? = 这块地可以耕种吗？
Is this area plowable after the flood? = 洪水过后，这个地区可以耕种吗？


否定句:
The land is not plowable at present. = 这块地目前不可耕种。
The soil is too rocky to be plowable. = 土壤太石质了，无法耕种。


PHRASE->

plowable land: 可耕地

easily plowable: 易于耕作的
difficult to plowable: 难以耕作的
potentially plowable: 潜在的可耕的

词源:  From "plow" + "-able," a suffix indicating capability.  (犁 + "-able"，表示能力的后缀)
