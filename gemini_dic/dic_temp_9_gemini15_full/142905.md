out-print:/ˌaʊtˈprɪnt/| vt.|超过印刷量，印出…的副本|To print more copies of something than were previously printed, or to surpass in printing.|The company had to out-print its initial order due to unexpected high demand. 公司不得不超过最初的订单印刷量，因为需求意外地高。|近义词：surpass, exceed, overprint|反义词：underprint


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory out-prints its quota every month. = 工厂每月都超额完成印刷配额。
They out-print the competition with their superior technology. = 他们凭借其先进的技术超过了竞争对手的印刷量。


一般过去时:
The newspaper out-printed its previous edition by 10,000 copies. = 报纸比之前的版本多印了10000份。
Last year, they out-printed all other publishers in the region. = 去年，他们在该地区超过了所有其他出版商的印刷量。


现在完成时:
The publisher has out-printed its initial estimate by 20%. = 出版商已超额完成了其最初估计的20%。
They have out-printed their rivals consistently for the past five years. = 在过去的五年里，他们一直超过他们的竞争对手的印刷量。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had out-printed their sales projections. = 到年底，他们的印刷量已经超过了他们的销售预测。
The printer had out-printed the previous record before the new model was even released. = 在新机型发布之前，打印机就已经超过了之前的记录。


将来时:
We will out-print the current edition by at least 15%. = 我们将至少超额印刷当前版本的15%。
They believe they will out-print their competitors in the coming quarter. = 他们相信在下个季度将超过其竞争对手的印刷量。


虚拟语气:
If we had better equipment, we could out-print our competitors easily. = 如果我们有更好的设备，我们可以轻松地超过我们的竞争对手。
I wish we had out-printed the rival company last year. = 我希望去年我们能超过竞争对手公司的印刷量。


被动语态:
The initial order was out-printed due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，最初的订单被超额印刷了。
Their sales figures were out-printed by their rival company last month. = 上个月，他们的销售数据被竞争对手公司超过了。


疑问句:
Did they out-print the expected number of copies? = 他们是否超过了预期的印刷数量？
Will the new press out-print the old one? = 新印刷机能否超过旧印刷机的印刷量？


否定句:
The small publishing house did not out-print its competitors. = 这家小型出版社没有超过其竞争对手。
We couldn't out-print the demand, even with extra shifts. = 我们无法满足需求，即使加班也不行。
