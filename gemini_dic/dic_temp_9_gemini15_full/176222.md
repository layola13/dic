re-booting:/ˌriˈbuːtɪŋ/| v.|重启，重新启动|The act of restarting a computer or other electronic device to restore normal operation.  It can also refer to restarting a process or system.|例句：I had to re-boot my computer after the software update. 我在软件更新后不得不重启我的电脑。

例句：The company is re-booting its marketing strategy after a period of declining sales. 公司在销售下滑一段时间后正在重启其营销策略。


近义词：restart, relaunch, reset|反义词：shutdown, power off


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-boot their server regularly. = 他们定期重启他们的服务器。
We re-boot the system every night. = 我们每晚重启系统。


一般过去时:
I re-booted my phone yesterday. = 我昨天重启了我的手机。
He re-booted the computer after the crash. = 电脑崩溃后，他重启了电脑。


现在完成时:
I have re-booted my computer three times today. = 我今天已经重启了电脑三次。
She has re-booted her business plan several times. = 她已经多次修改了她的商业计划。


过去完成时:
They had re-booted the server before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前已经重启了服务器。
We had re-booted the system twice before we found the problem. = 我们在找到问题之前已经重启了两次系统。


将来时:
I will re-boot my computer later. = 我稍后会重启我的电脑。
We will re-boot the system after maintenance. = 我们将在维护后重启系统。


虚拟语气:
If the system crashed, I would re-boot it immediately. = 如果系统崩溃，我会立即重启它。
It would be better if we re-booted the server now. = 现在重启服务器会更好。


被动语态:
The computer was re-booted by the technician. = 电脑由技术人员重启。
The system is being re-booted to install the update. = 系统正在被重启以安装更新。


疑问句:
Did you re-boot the computer? = 你重启电脑了吗？
Should we re-boot the router now? = 我们现在应该重启路由器吗？


否定句:
I didn't re-boot my computer. = 我没有重启我的电脑。
They didn't re-boot the server after the power outage. = 停电后他们没有重启服务器。
