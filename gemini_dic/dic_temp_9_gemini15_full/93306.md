ill-disposed:/ˌɪl dɪˈspoʊzd/| adj.|怀有恶意的；不友好的；不利的|Having or showing unfriendly or unfavourable feelings; malevolent; adverse.|例句：He was ill-disposed towards his neighbour after their argument.  他与邻居争吵后对邻居怀有恶意。|例句：The ill-disposed weather conditions forced us to postpone the picnic. 不利的恶劣天气迫使我们推迟野餐。|近义词：hostile, unfriendly, antagonistic, malevolent|反义词：well-disposed, friendly, amicable, benevolent|词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is ill-disposed towards his colleagues. = 他对同事怀有恶意。
The current situation is ill-disposed for business growth. = 目前形势不利于业务增长。


一般过去时:
She was ill-disposed towards the idea from the start. = 她从一开始就对这个主意持反对态度。
The judge was ill-disposed to the defendant’s plea. = 法官对被告的申诉持否定态度。


现在完成时:

The prolonged drought has ill-disposed the land for farming. = 长期干旱使得这块土地不再适合耕种。


过去完成时:

The company had been ill-disposed towards innovation for years. = 多年来，该公司一直对创新持反对态度。


将来时:
I fear he will be ill-disposed towards our request. = 我担心他会对我们的请求持反对态度。
The forecast suggests the weather will be ill-disposed for outdoor activities. = 预报显示天气将不利于户外活动。


虚拟语气:
If he were ill-disposed, he would have sabotaged the plan. = 如果他怀有恶意，他就会破坏这个计划。
I wish I hadn't been ill-disposed to their suggestions. = 我真希望我没有对他们的建议持反对态度。


被动语态:

His ideas were ill-disposed to by his superiors. = 他的想法不被他的上司所接受。


疑问句:
Is he ill-disposed towards the new manager? = 他对新经理怀有恶意吗？
Are the economic conditions ill-disposed to investment? = 经济形势不利于投资吗？


否定句:
He is not ill-disposed towards helping others. = 他并不反对帮助别人。
The situation is not ill-disposed to a quick resolution. = 情况并非不利于迅速解决问题。
