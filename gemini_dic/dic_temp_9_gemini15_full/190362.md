sardining:/ˈsɑːrdɪnɪŋ/| v.|沙丁鱼般地挤在一起；拥挤|The act of packing closely together, like sardines in a can.  It often implies a lack of space and a feeling of being cramped.|例句：The concert was so popular that the crowd was sardining in the entrance hall.  这场音乐会太受欢迎了，以至于人群挤满了入口大厅。

例句：During rush hour, the subway cars are sardining with commuters. 在高峰时间，地铁车厢里挤满了通勤者。


近义词：crowding, cramming, packing, thronging|反义词：spreading out, dispersing|词性:动词
