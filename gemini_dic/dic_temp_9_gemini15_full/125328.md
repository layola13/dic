multilaminar: /ˌmʌltiˈlæmɪnər/ | adj. |多层结构的，多层片的 | Having or composed of many layers.  The term refers to a structure composed of numerous thin layers or laminae.  This structure is often found in materials like wood, geological formations, and biological tissues. |例句：The multilaminar structure of the cortex allows for complex information processing.  大脑皮层的多分层结构允许进行复杂的信息处理。

The multilaminar composite material showed enhanced strength and durability. 多层复合材料显示出增强的强度和耐久性。|近义词：stratified, layered, laminated |反义词：unilaminar, monolayer
