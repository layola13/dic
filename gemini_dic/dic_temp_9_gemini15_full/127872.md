neocolonization:/ˌniːoʊkɑːlənaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |新殖民主义；新殖民化 |The practice of using economic, political, cultural, or other pressures to control or influence other countries, often in ways that resemble the historical practices of colonialism, but without direct political rule.  This often involves exploiting resources, manipulating markets, and imposing cultural norms.|例句：The critics argue that certain international trade agreements constitute a form of neocolonization. 批评人士认为某些国际贸易协定构成了新殖民主义的一种形式。

例句：Some scholars believe that the dominance of multinational corporations in developing countries is a manifestation of neocolonization. 一些学者认为跨国公司在发展中国家的主导地位是新殖民主义的表现。


近义词：economic imperialism, cultural imperialism, exploitation, domination

反义词：decolonization, self-determination, independence


词性:名词
