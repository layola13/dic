inferring:/ɪnˈfɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 推断；推论 |  The act of deducing or concluding information from evidence and reasoning. It involves using available facts to reach a logical conclusion.  | Example: From the dark clouds, I inferred that it would rain.  从乌云密布，我推断出要下雨了。 |近义词：deducing, concluding, implying, surmising | 反义词： stating, declaring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inferring the meaning from the context. = 他正在从语境中推断意思。
She infers that the economy will improve. = 她推断经济将会好转。


一般过去时:
I inferred from his silence that he was angry. = 我从他的沉默中推断出他生气了。
They inferred the criminal's motive from the evidence. = 他们根据证据推断出罪犯的动机。


现在完成时:
Scientists have inferred the existence of dark matter. = 科学家们已经推断出暗物质的存在。
We have inferred from the data that the project is failing. = 我们从数据中推断出项目正在失败。


过去完成时:
She had inferred that he was lying before he confessed. = 他坦白之前，她就推断出他在说谎。
The detectives had inferred the killer's identity from the fingerprints. = 探员们根据指纹推断出了凶手的身份。


将来时:
I will infer the answer from the clues provided. = 我将从提供的线索中推断出答案。
Researchers will infer the cause of the disease from the symptoms. = 研究人员将从症状中推断出疾病的原因。


虚拟语气:
If I had more information, I would infer the solution more easily. = 如果我有更多信息，我就能更容易地推断出解决方案。
If he were honest, we would not need to infer his intentions. = 如果他诚实，我们就无需推断他的意图。


被动语态:
The results were inferred from the experimental data. = 结果是从实验数据中推断出来的。
The meaning can be inferred from the context. = 意思可以从上下文中推断出来。


疑问句:
What can you infer from this evidence? = 你能从这个证据中推断出什么？
What did the author infer from the characters' actions? = 作者从人物的行为中推断出了什么？


否定句:
You cannot infer that conclusion from the given facts. = 你不能从给定的事实中推断出那个结论。
I did not infer any malicious intent from his words. = 我并没有从他的话语中推断出任何恶意。


PHRASE->
infer from = 从...推断
safely infer = 可以安全地推断
reasonably infer = 合理地推断
can be inferred = 可以推断
it can be inferred that = 可以推断出
