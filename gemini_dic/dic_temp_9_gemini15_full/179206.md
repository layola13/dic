rediscussed:/ˌriːdɪˈskʌst/| v. |重新讨论，再次讨论 |To discuss something again, often in more detail or with a different perspective.  |例句：We rediscussed the budget after discovering a significant error. 我们在发现一个重大错误后重新讨论了预算。

例句：The committee rediscussed the proposal, incorporating new data. 委员会重新讨论了提案，纳入了新的数据。


近义词：reconsidered, reviewed, revisited, reexamined |反义词：concluded, finalized


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rediscuss the contract every year. = 他们每年都会重新讨论合同。
We rediscuss the project plan regularly. = 我们定期重新讨论项目计划。

一般过去时:
They rediscussed the issue after the meeting. = 会议后他们重新讨论了这个问题。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
