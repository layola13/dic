sledgehammers:/ˈslɛdʒˌhæmər/| n. |大锤，大铁锤 |A sledgehammer is a heavy hammer with a long handle, used for breaking things or driving in stakes.|例句：He used a sledgehammer to break the rock. 他用大锤砸碎了石头。

He swung the sledgehammer with all his might. 他用尽全力挥舞大锤。|近义词：hammer, maul |反义词：small hammer, delicate tool |名词
