downs:/daʊnz/| n.|低地，低谷，下坡，沮丧|Low-lying land, a period of decline or difficulty, a downward slope.  |例句：The sheep grazed in the downs.  羊群在低地上吃草。|例句：The company is experiencing some downs. 公司正经历一些低谷时期。|近义词：lowlands, valleys, depression, slump |反义词：uplands, peaks, heights, prosperity
