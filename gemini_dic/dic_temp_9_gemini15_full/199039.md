signees:/saɪˈniːz/ | n. | 签署人，签字人 | People who have signed a document or agreement.  | Example: The signees of the contract are required to attend the meeting.  签署合同的人必须参加会议。 |近义词: signatories, subscribers | 反义词:  N/A


USAGE->

The signees are reviewing the contract. = 签署人正在审查合同。
Several signees represent the company. = 几个签署人代表公司。
