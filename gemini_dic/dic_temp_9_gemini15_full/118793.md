metalorganic: /ˌmɛtəlɔːrˈɡænɪk/ | adj. |金属有机|Containing or relating to both metal and organic components.  Metalorganic compounds are characterized by the presence of a metal atom directly bonded to a carbon atom of an organic molecule.  These compounds play crucial roles in various fields, including catalysis, material science, and medicine.|例句：Metalorganic chemical vapor deposition (MOCVD) is a common technique for depositing thin films. 金属有机化学气相沉积 (MOCVD) 是沉积薄膜的常用技术。

例句：The study focused on the synthesis and characterization of novel metalorganic frameworks. 该研究侧重于新型金属有机框架的合成和表征。


近义词：organometallic (often used interchangeably, though subtle differences exist in usage)


反义词：inorganic (lacking carbon-metal bonds)


词性：形容词
