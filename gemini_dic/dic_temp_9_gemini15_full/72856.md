folkloristics:/ˌfoʊklɔːˈrɪstɪks/ | n. |民间故事学，民俗学 |The academic study of folklore, encompassing its collection, classification, analysis, and interpretation.  It examines the traditions, beliefs, customs, and narratives of a particular culture or group. |例句：Her doctoral dissertation focused on the folkloristics of the Appalachian region.  她的博士论文重点研究阿巴拉契亚地区的民俗学。|例句：The professor's course in folkloristics covered a wide range of topics, from myths and legends to folk songs and dances. 教授的民俗学课程涵盖了从神话和传说到民歌和舞蹈的广泛主题。|近义词：folklore studies, ethnology, cultural anthropology |反义词：无
