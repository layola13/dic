mollifiers: /ˈmɒlɪfaɪəz/ | n. | 软化剂；缓和剂；调和剂 |  A substance or agent that softens or mitigates something harsh, severe, or unpleasant.  Mollifiers are often used in various contexts, from chemistry to social interactions, to reduce the intensity or harshness of something.  | 例句：In the chemical industry, mollifiers are used to soften hard materials. = 在化工行业中，软化剂用于软化硬质材料。

The use of calming words acted as a mollifier in the tense situation. = 使用平静的话语在紧张的局势中起到了缓和作用。 | 近义词：softener, mitigator, palliative, emollient | 反义词：exacerbator, intensifier, irritant
