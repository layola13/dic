elevate:/ˈɛləveɪt/| vt.  |提升；举起；提高；鼓舞|To raise to a higher level; to lift up; to improve or enhance; to uplift in spirits.|例句：The workers elevated the heavy beam into place. 工人们把沉重的横梁吊到了位。|例句：The good news elevated her spirits. 好消息使她精神振奋。|近义词：raise, lift, hoist, uplift, enhance|反义词：depress, lower, degrade, diminish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He elevates the weight above his head. 他把重物举过头顶。
The company elevates its employees to higher positions. 公司将员工提升到更高的职位。


一般过去时:
