rummily:/ˈrʌməli/| adv.|喧闹地；嘈杂地；混乱地|In a confused or disorderly manner; noisily and confusedly.|例句：The children were playing rummily in the garden.孩子们在花园里吵吵闹闹地玩耍着。

例句：The papers were scattered rummily across the desk. 文件散乱地堆在桌子上。


近义词：noisily, chaotically, disorderly, tumultuously|反义词：quietly, orderly, calmly|词性：副词
