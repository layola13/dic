cushion:/ˈkʊʃən/| n. v.|软垫；缓冲；减轻|A cushion is a soft pillow or pad, often used for comfort or support.  It can also refer to something that softens a blow or reduces impact.|例句：The sofa has soft cushions.  沙发上有柔软的坐垫。

She placed a cushion under her head. 她把一个垫子放在头下。|近义词：pillow, pad, buffer|反义词：hardness, rigidity

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cushions the blow with his hand. 他用手缓冲了一下冲击力。
The couch cushions are very comfortable. 沙发垫很舒服。


一般过去时:
She cushioned the baby's fall with a blanket. 她用毯子缓冲了婴儿的跌倒。
He cushioned the impact of the news. 他缓和了这个消息带来的冲击。


现在完成时:
They have cushioned the chairs with new padding. 他们已经用新的填充物给椅子加上了软垫。
The government has cushioned the impact of the recession. 政府已经减轻了经济衰退的影响。


过去完成时:
She had cushioned the box with straw before shipping it.  她在邮寄包裹前已经用稻草垫好了。
They had cushioned the blow of the bad news beforehand. 他们在之前已经缓冲了坏消息带来的打击。


将来时:
They will cushion the floor with a thick rug. 他们将会用厚地毯来缓冲地板。
The company will cushion its losses with insurance. 公司将用保险来缓冲损失。


虚拟语气:
If I had cushioned the package better, it wouldn't have broken. 如果我包装得更好一些，它就不会坏了。
I wish I had cushioned the impact of my words. 我希望我当时能缓和一下我的措辞。


被动语态:
The impact was cushioned by the airbags. 冲击被安全气囊缓冲了。
The blow was cushioned by a thick layer of foam.  冲击被一层厚厚的泡沫缓冲了。


疑问句:
Are the chairs cushioned? 椅子上有软垫吗？
How can we cushion the effects of this crisis? 我们该如何缓冲这场危机的冲击？


否定句:
The seats weren't cushioned. 座位没有软垫。
The company didn't cushion the workers from the layoffs. 公司没有让员工免受裁员的影响。
