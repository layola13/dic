enrolling:/ɪnˈroʊlɪŋ/ | v. |注册，登记，招收|The act of officially registering or signing up for something, such as a course, program, or membership.  It can also refer to the process of admitting someone to something.|例句：I am enrolling in a French course next semester. = 我下学期要选修一门法语课。

I'm enrolling my daughter in ballet classes. = 我正在为女儿报名参加芭蕾舞课程。


近义词：registering, signing up, subscribing, admitting, inducting | 反义词：withdrawing, unregistering, dropping out


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enrolls in a new gym every year. = 他每年都注册一个新的健身房。
They enroll students from all over the country. = 他们招收来自全国各地的学生。


一般过去时:
She enrolled in the university last fall. = 她去年秋天入学了那所大学。
They enrolled their child in a private school. = 他们把孩子送进了私立学校。


现在完成时:
He has enrolled in three online courses this year. = 他今年已经报了三门在线课程。
She has enrolled all her children in music lessons. = 她已经把她所有的孩子都报了音乐课。


过去完成时:
Before the deadline, she had enrolled in the advanced class. = 在截止日期之前，她已经报了高级班。
By the time the term started, he had enrolled in all the necessary courses. = 学期开始的时候，他已经选修了所有必要的课程。


将来时:
I will enroll in a yoga class next month. = 我下个月将参加瑜伽课程。
They will enroll their son in kindergarten next year. = 他们明年将把儿子送入幼儿园。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would enroll in that painting class. = 如果我有更多时间，我会报那个绘画班。
I wish I had enrolled in that course earlier. = 我真希望我早点报那门课。


被动语态:
Many students are enrolled in the introductory course. = 许多学生选修了入门课程。
New members are enrolled throughout the year. = 全年都在招收新会员。


疑问句:
Are you enrolling in any new classes this term? = 这个学期你报名参加任何新课程吗？
Will he be enrolled in the program? = 他会被录取到这个项目吗？


否定句:
I am not enrolling in any extra classes this semester. = 这个学期我不再报任何额外的课程了。
They did not enroll their daughter in dance lessons. = 他们没有让女儿上舞蹈课。


PHRASE->
enroll in a course = 报名参加课程
enroll as a member = 注册为会员
enroll oneself in = 自己报名参加
enroll for a class = 注册一门课程
