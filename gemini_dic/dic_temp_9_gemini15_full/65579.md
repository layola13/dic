evacuator: /ɪˈvækjuˌeɪtər/ | n. |疏散者；撤离者；排空器 |A person or thing that evacuates.  An evacuator is someone or something that removes people or things from a dangerous place.  It can also refer to a device that removes something, like a pump that removes liquid. |例句：The fire department acted as the evacuator, guiding residents to safety. 消防部门充当疏散者，引导居民安全撤离。

The automated evacuator removed all the water from the flooded basement. 自动排空器将水库中所有积水排出。

近义词：remover, remover, evacuant |反义词：filler, introducer
