postcoup:/ˈpoʊstkuːp/| adj. |政变后的；政变之后的 |Postcoup refers to the period or events following a coup d'état (a sudden, violent, and illegal seizure of power from a government).  It often describes the instability, political changes, and social consequences that occur after such an event. | Example: Postcoup instability led to widespread protests and economic decline.  政变后的不稳定导致了大规模抗议和经济衰退。|近义词：post-coup, after-coup, post-seizure |反义词：pre-coup, before-coup
