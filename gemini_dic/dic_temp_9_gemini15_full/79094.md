glacier:/ˈɡleɪʃər/ | n. |冰川 |A glacier is a large mass of ice that moves slowly over land.  Glaciers are formed from the accumulation and compaction of snow over many years. They are found in high mountain regions and polar areas. |Example: The majestic glacier flowed slowly down the mountainside.  这壮丽的冰川缓缓地流下山坡。  The retreating glacier revealed ancient rock formations.  冰川的消退露出了古老的岩层。|近义词：ice sheet, ice field |反义词：N/A
