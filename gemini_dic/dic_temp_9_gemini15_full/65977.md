exagitation:/ɪɡˌzædʒɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |夸张；夸大；激昂|The exaggeration in his story made it hard to believe.  他的故事里夸张的成分使得它难以置信。|近义词：hyperbole, overstatement |反义词：understatement, minimization |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The politician's speeches are full of exaggeration. = 这位政治家的演讲充满了夸张。
His description of the event is an exaggeration. = 他对事件的描述是夸张的。


一般过去时:
He exaggerated his achievements in the interview. = 他在面试中夸大了自己的成就。
She exaggerated the seriousness of the problem. = 她夸大了问题的严重性。


现在完成时:
He has exaggerated his role in the project. = 他夸大了自己在项目中的作用。
She has exaggerated the difficulties she faced. = 她夸大了她面临的困难。


过去完成时:
He had exaggerated his experience to get the job. = 他为了得到这份工作而夸大了自己的经验。
She had exaggerated the size of the crowd. = 她夸大了人群的大小。


将来时:
He will exaggerate his success to impress his friends. = 他会夸大自己的成功来给朋友们留下深刻印象。
She will exaggerate the danger to scare them. = 她会夸大危险来吓唬他们。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't exaggerated, we would have believed him. = 如果他没有夸张，我们会相信他的。
If she hadn't exaggerated the situation, things wouldn't have gotten so bad. = 如果她没有夸大情况，事情就不会变得这么糟糕。


被动语态:
The dangers were exaggerated by the media. = 危险被媒体夸大了。
His contributions were exaggerated in the report. = 他的贡献在报告中被夸大了。


疑问句:
Is he exaggerating the situation? = 他是在夸大事态吗？
Are you exaggerating the difficulties? = 你是在夸大困难吗？


否定句:
He didn't exaggerate his capabilities. = 他没有夸大自己的能力。
She didn't exaggerate the importance of the meeting. = 她没有夸大会议的重要性。


PHRASE->
degree of exaggeration: 夸张的程度
a gross exaggeration: 严重的夸张
without exaggeration: 毫不夸张地说
to exaggerate matters: 夸大事实
