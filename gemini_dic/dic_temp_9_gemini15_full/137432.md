nonthematically:/ˌnɒnθiːˈmætɪkli/| adj. |非主题地，与主题无关地 |Not related to the main theme or subject; not systematically organized around a central idea.  It describes something that is incidental, digressive, or lacking a coherent thematic structure. |The professor's lecture strayed nonthematically into personal anecdotes.  教授的讲座非主题地偏离到个人轶事上。|近义词：irrelevantly, incidentally, digressively |反义词：thematically, systematically
