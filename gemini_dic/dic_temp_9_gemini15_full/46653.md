damningnesses:/ˈdæmɪŋnəsɪz/| n.| 令人谴责的证据；毁灭性的证据；罪证 |The damningnesses presented in court sealed his fate. |  |  | 名词


英文解析：The plural form of "damningness," referring to the quality or state of being damning;  the overwhelming evidence of guilt or wrongdoing. It emphasizes the weight and impact of the incriminating evidence.


例句：

The damningnesses of the evidence left no room for doubt about his guilt.  | 确凿的证据不容置疑地证明了他的罪行。

The accumulation of damningnesses throughout the investigation led to the suspect's arrest. | 调查中累积的罪证导致了嫌疑人的逮捕。



近义词：incriminating evidence, condemnatory proof,  overwhelming evidence,  irrefutable evidence


反义词：exculpatory evidence, exonerating proof
