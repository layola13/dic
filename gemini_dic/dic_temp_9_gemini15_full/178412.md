reconquers:/ˌriːˈkɒŋkərz/| vt.|重新征服；收复|To conquer again; to regain possession or control of something that was previously lost or taken.|例句：The army reconquered the lost territory.  军队收复了失地。
例句：The king aimed to reconquer his ancestral lands. 国王致力于收复祖先的土地。|近义词：regain, recapture, recover|反义词：lose, surrender, cede|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconquers lost territories. 他重新征服失地。
They reconquer the market share. 他们夺回市场份额。


一般过去时:
The general reconquered the city. 将军收复了这座城市。

将来时:
