homogenize:/ˈhoʊmədʒənaɪz/|/ˈhɒmədʒənaɪz/| vt.|使均匀；使同质化 |To make something uniform or of the same kind throughout.|The milk was homogenized to prevent creaming.  牛奶经过均质处理以防止出现奶油层。|The factory homogenized the raw materials before processing. 工厂在加工前将原材料均质化。|uniformize, standardize|segregate, diversify|动词
