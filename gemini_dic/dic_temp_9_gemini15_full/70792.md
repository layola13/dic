fingering:/ˈfɪŋɡərɪŋ/| n. v.|手指法；指法；拨弦法；触觉|The way a musician presses the strings or keys of an instrument;  the act of using one's fingers in a particular way; a feeling or sensation produced by touching.  |例句：His fingering was impeccable. = 他的指法无可挑剔。
The delicate fingering of the lace showed great skill. = 精致的花边指法展现出高超的技艺。|近义词：touching, manipulation, playing|反义词:ignoring, neglecting
