nascency: /ˈnæsənsi/ | n. | 初期；萌芽时期；发生初期 | The initial stage of existence or development.  It refers to something in its earliest stages of existence or development, often implying potential but also vulnerability. |例句：The project is still in its nascency, and many challenges lie ahead.  这个项目还处于初期阶段，还有许多挑战摆在前面。

例句：The ideas were still in their nascency, requiring further refinement and development. 这些想法还处于萌芽阶段，需要进一步完善和发展。


近义词：inception, beginning, commencement, origin, infancy


反义词：maturity, culmination, completion, decline


词性: 名词
