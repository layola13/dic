gynoecium:/ˌdʒaɪnoʊˈsiːəm/| n. |雌蕊群,雌蕊 |The gynoecium is the female reproductive part of a flower, consisting of one or more carpels.  It typically includes the ovary, style, and stigma.  | Example: The gynoecium of this orchid is unusually large and showy.  这个兰花的雌蕊群异常巨大而醒目。|近义词：pistil (in simpler contexts) |反义词：androecium
