convocation:/ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃən/|/kɑːnvəˈkeɪʃən/| n. |召集；集会；会议；授予学位仪式 |A formal assembly of people, especially one summoned for a particular purpose.  Often refers to a ceremony for graduating students. |例句：The university held a grand convocation to celebrate the graduating class.  大学举行盛大的毕业典礼来庆祝毕业生。

例句：The convocation of scholars discussed the future of research. 学者们的集会讨论了研究的未来。


近义词：assembly, gathering, meeting, ceremony, congregation

反义词：dispersal, separation, adjournment


词性：名词
