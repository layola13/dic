high-fiving:/ˈhaɪˌfaɪvɪŋ/| n. v.|击掌庆祝|The act of two people simultaneously slapping each other's upraised hands in celebration or congratulation. It's a gesture of camaraderie, excitement, and shared success. |例句：They were high-fiving after their team won the game.  他们队赢了比赛后击掌庆祝。
例句：The boss gave him a high-five for closing the deal. 老板因为完成交易而给他击了个掌。|近义词：slapping hands, celebrating, congratulating|反义词：ignoring, snubbing
