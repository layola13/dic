metaphorically:/ˌmɛtəˈfɔːrɪkli/ | adv. | 比喻地；隐喻地 |  Figuratively; using metaphors.  It describes something in a way that is not literal but uses a comparison to something else to express an idea or feeling more vividly. | Example: He metaphorically described his life as a rollercoaster. 他比喻地把自己的生活形容为过山车。 | 近义词：figuratively, symbolically | 反义词：literally
