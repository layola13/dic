dreamlands: /ˈdriːmlændz/ | n. |梦幻之地，梦境世界 |A place or state resembling a dream, often characterized by fantasy, surrealism, or idyllic beauty. It can refer to a literal place in fiction or a metaphorical state of mind.  |例句：The artist's paintings depicted fantastical dreamlands filled with impossible creatures and vibrant colors.  这位艺术家的绘画描绘了充满不可能的生物和鲜艳色彩的梦幻仙境。

The novel transports the reader to a series of exotic dreamlands. 这部小说将读者带入一系列奇异的梦幻之地。|近义词：fantasy land, wonderland, paradise |反义词：reality, nightmare
