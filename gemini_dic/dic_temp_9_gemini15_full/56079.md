docking:/ˈdɒkɪŋ/|/ˈdɑːkɪŋ/| n. v.|对接；码头停泊；(飞机)着陆|The act of connecting two things together, or the place where this happens.  This can refer to spaceships, aircraft, computer systems, or boats. |例句：The spacecraft successfully completed its docking with the space station.  宇宙飞船成功完成了与空间站的对接。

例句：The ship is currently undergoing docking procedures at the port. 这艘船目前正在港口进行码头停泊程序。


近义词：coupling, connection, joining, berthing (for ships)
反义词：disconnecting, separating, uncoupling


USAGE->

The pilot is docking the plane. = 飞行员正在降落飞机。
The software automatically docks the two programs. = 软件自动将这两个程序对接。
