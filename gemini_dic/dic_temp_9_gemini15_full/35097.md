clothing:/ˈkloʊðɪŋ/| n.|衣服，服装|The word "clothing" refers to garments worn to cover the body. It encompasses a wide range of items, from underwear to outerwear, and varies greatly depending on culture, climate, and occasion. |例句：She bought new clothing for her trip. 她为旅行买了新衣服。|例句：The clothing store had a sale on winter coats. 这家服装店正在打折销售冬衣。|近义词：garments, apparel, attire|反义词:nudity
