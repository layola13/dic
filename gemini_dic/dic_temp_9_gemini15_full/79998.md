go-kaput:/ˈɡoʊ ˈkɑːpʊt/|/ˈɡəʊ ˈkæpʊt/| adj. | 坏掉；报废；崩溃；失灵 |  Describes something that has stopped working, usually suddenly and completely. It implies a total and often irreversible failure.  | The washing machine went kaput after only a year. 洗衣机只用了一年就坏了。 My computer went kaput during the presentation. 我的电脑在演示过程中坏了。| broken, defunct, out of order, malfunctioning | working, functional, operational | 形容词
