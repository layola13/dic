rethreshed:/riːˈθrɛʃt/| v. |再脱粒；重新研磨|To thresh again; to subject to a second threshing.  The farmer rethreshed the grain to extract any remaining kernels.|近义词：reprocess, re-examine|反义词：harvest(in the sense of gathering before threshing)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rethreshes the wheat every autumn. = 他每年秋天都重新脱粒小麦。
They rethresh the barley to get more yield. = 他们重新脱粒大麦以获得更高的产量。

一般过去时:
The farmer rethreshed the grain after the first attempt proved insufficient. = 农民在第一次尝试证明不足后重新脱粒谷物。
She rethreshed the spices to release more aroma. = 她重新研磨香料以释放更多香气。

现在完成时:
They have rethreshed the rice several times this year due to heavy rains. = 今年由于暴雨，他们已经几次重新脱粒稻米了。
We have rethreshed the data to ensure accuracy. = 我们已经重新处理数据以确保准确性。

过去完成时:
The miller had rethreshed the flour before baking the bread. = 磨坊主在烘烤面包之前已经重新研磨了面粉。
They had rethreshed the evidence before presenting it to the court. = 他们在向法庭提交证据之前已经重新审查了证据。

将来时:
The farmer will rethresh the oats next week if the weather permits. = 如果天气允许，农民下周将重新脱粒燕麦。
We will rethresh the results to confirm the findings. = 我们将重新处理结果以确认发现。

虚拟语气:
If they had rethreshed the crop earlier, they would have had a better yield. = 如果他们早些重新脱粒庄稼，他们就会有更好的产量。
I wish I had rethreshed the data before submitting the report. = 我希望我在提交报告之前已经重新处理了数据。

被动语态:
The grain was rethreshed using a modern machine. = 谷物是用现代机器重新脱粒的。

疑问句:
Has the wheat been rethreshed yet? = 小麦已经重新脱粒了吗？
Will the coffee beans be rethreshed before roasting? = 咖啡豆在烘焙前会重新研磨吗？

否定句:
They did not rethresh the soybeans this year. = 他们今年没有重新脱粒大豆。
The spices were not rethreshed before being added to the stew. = 香料在添加到炖菜之前没有重新研磨。
