cheddar-cheese:/ˈtʃɛdər tʃiz/ | n. |切达干酪 |A hard, crumbly cheese with a sharp, sometimes nutty flavor, originating in the Cheddar region of Somerset, England.  It is one of the most popular cheeses worldwide and comes in various ages and milk types. |例句：I love the sharp taste of mature cheddar cheese. 我喜欢成熟切达干酪的浓郁味道。

例句：We made grilled cheese sandwiches with cheddar cheese. 我们用切达干酪做了烤奶酪三明治。


近义词：cheddar, hard cheese| 反义词：soft cheese, cream cheese
