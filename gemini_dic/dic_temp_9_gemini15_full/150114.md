pamper:/ˈpæmpə(r)/| vt.| 纵容；溺爱；娇养 | To treat someone very kindly and lovingly, often to an excessive degree.  |例句：She pampers her children. 她溺爱她的孩子。|例句：He pampers himself with long baths and massages. 他用长时间的泡澡和按摩来纵容自己。|近义词：indulge, spoil, coddle |反义词：neglect, ignore, discipline


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She pampers her dog excessively. = 她过分地溺爱她的狗。
They pamper their clients with exceptional service. = 他们用卓越的服务来款待他们的客户。

一般过去时:
He pampered himself with a luxurious vacation. = 他给自己安排了一个奢华的假期来纵容自己。
My grandmother pampered me with delicious homemade cookies. = 我祖母用美味的自制饼干来宠爱我。

现在完成时:
She has pampered herself all week. = 她整个星期都在纵容自己。
They have pampered their customers with special offers. = 他们用特价商品来款待顾客。

过去完成时:
He had pampered himself for months before the big event. = 在大型活动之前，他几个月来一直都在纵容自己。
She had pampered her skin with expensive creams for years. = 她多年来一直用昂贵的护肤霜来护理她的皮肤。

将来时:
I will pamper myself with a spa day next weekend. = 下周末我将给自己安排一个水疗日来放松。
They will pamper their guests with a five-star service. = 他们将用五星级的服务来款待他们的客人。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pamper myself more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常地纵容自己。
I wish I could pamper myself with a trip to the beach. = 我希望我能去海边旅行来放松一下。

被动语态:

疑问句:
