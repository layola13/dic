prestrengthening: /ˌpriːˈstrɛŋθənɪŋ/ | n. | 预先加强；预先增强 | The act of strengthening something beforehand, typically to prepare it for a greater stress or strain.  It often involves applying a treatment or process to increase its resistance or durability. | Example: The prestrengthening of the bridge supports ensured its stability during the earthquake. 预先加强桥梁支撑确保其在地震期间的稳定性。| 近义词：reinforcement, fortifying, hardening | 反义词：weakening, enfeeblement | 名词
