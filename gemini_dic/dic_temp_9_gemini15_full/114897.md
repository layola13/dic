man-hours:/ˈmæn ˌaʊərz/| n.|工时；人时|The total amount of work done by one person in a specified number of hours, or by a number of people in one hour.  It's a measure of labor effort, often used in project planning and costing.|例句：The project required 1000 man-hours to complete.  这个项目需要1000个工时才能完成。|例句：We need to estimate the man-hours needed for the construction. 我们需要估算一下建筑所需的工时。|近义词：labor hours, person-hours|反义词：None  (no direct antonym)|名词
