overspeak:/ˌoʊvərˈspiːk/ | vt. |说得太多；说得过分|To speak for too long or say more than is necessary or appropriate.  |例句：He overspoke during the meeting, revealing confidential information. 他在会议上说了太多话，泄露了机密信息。

例句：She overspoke and offended her boss with her blunt comments. 她说话太直率，冒犯了她的老板。


近义词：ramble, gabble, prattle, blabber, expatiate


反义词：be concise, be brief, understate


词性:动词
