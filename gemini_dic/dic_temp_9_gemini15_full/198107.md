shootee:/ʃuːˈtiː/| n. |被射击者，射击目标|A person who is shot at or targeted for shooting.  It is often used in informal contexts and can also refer to someone who is the target of criticism or ridicule.| 例句：The shootee ducked as the bullet whizzed past.  被射击者在子弹呼啸而过时躲闪。 The politician became the shootee of intense media scrutiny.  这位政治家成了媒体强烈审查的目标。|近义词：target, victim|反义词：shooter
