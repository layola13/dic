leave-in-the-lurch:/liːv ɪn ðə lɜːtʃ/| phrasal verb|抛弃；丢下不管；使陷入困境|To leave someone in the lurch means to abandon them in a difficult situation, leaving them without help or support.  This phrase implies a sudden and unexpected abandonment, often leaving the abandoned person feeling helpless and betrayed.|例句：He left me in the lurch when I needed him most. 他在我最需要他的时候抛弃了我。

例句：The company left its employees in the lurch when it declared bankruptcy.  公司破产时，把员工们都抛弃了。


近义词：abandon, desert, forsake, strand, ditch


反义词：support, assist, help, rescue, aid


PHRASE->
leave someone in the lurch = 抛弃某人
leave sb high and dry = 使某人陷入困境
let sb down = 让某人失望
abandon ship = 临阵脱逃
walk out on sb = 抛弃某人


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They often leave their employees in the lurch. = 他们经常抛弃他们的员工。
He leaves his friends in the lurch whenever he gets into trouble. = 他每当惹上麻烦就抛弃朋友。


一般过去时:
She left him in the lurch during his financial crisis. = 在他经济危机期间，她抛弃了他。
The government left the citizens in the lurch after the natural disaster. = 自然灾害后，政府抛弃了公民。


现在完成时:
They have repeatedly left their customers in the lurch. = 他们反复抛弃他们的顾客。
The company has left its investors in the lurch with its failed projects. = 公司因项目失败而抛弃了投资者。


过去完成时:
He had left his family in the lurch before he disappeared. = 他消失之前抛弃了他的家人。
The bank had left the borrowers in the lurch before the new regulations were announced. = 在新规发布之前，银行抛弃了借款人。


将来时:
They will leave their partners in the lurch if the deal falls through. = 如果交易失败，他们将会抛弃他们的合作伙伴。
He will leave his colleagues in the lurch if he quits without notice. = 如果他不辞而别，他会抛弃他的同事。


虚拟语气:
If he had helped, he wouldn't have left her in the lurch. = 如果他帮忙的话，他就不会抛弃她了。
I wish they hadn't left us in the lurch. = 我希望他们没有抛弃我们。


被动语态:
Many people were left in the lurch by the sudden economic downturn. = 许多人被突如其来的经济衰退抛弃了。
The project was left in the lurch due to lack of funding. = 该项目由于资金不足而被搁置了。


疑问句:
Were they left in the lurch by the company's actions? = 公司的行为是否抛弃了他们？
Will he be left in the lurch if he loses his job? = 如果他丢了工作，他会被人抛弃吗？


否定句:
They did not leave their friends in the lurch during their time of need. = 他们没有在朋友需要的时候抛弃他们。
The organization did not leave the victims in the lurch after the earthquake. = 地震后，该组织没有抛弃受害者。
