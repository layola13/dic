parapatry:/ˈpærəpætəri/| n.|邻域物种形成，同域物种形成|The formation of new species that occurs when populations are in contact along a common border.  This is in contrast to allopatric speciation, where populations are geographically separated.| Example:  Parapatric speciation is often observed in plants that exhibit a gradual change in environmental conditions across their range. 邻域物种形成经常在植物中观察到，这些植物在其分布范围内表现出逐渐变化的环境条件。| sympatry, allopatry| allopatry
