semi-intelligible:/ˌsɛmiɪnˈtɛlədʒəbl/| adj.| 半可理解的，含糊不清的 | Partially understandable; unclear or difficult to understand.  The speaker's mumbling made his speech semi-intelligible.  | 近义词：unclear, obscure, ambiguous, indistinct | 反义词：clear, understandable, lucid, intelligible


例句：

The message was semi-intelligible, but we managed to glean the main points.  信息含糊不清，但我们还是设法了解了要点。

His mumbled words were barely semi-intelligible, even to those sitting closest.  他含糊不清的话语即使对坐在最近的人来说也很难理解。


词源：

* **semi-**:  A prefix meaning "half," "partly," or "partially."  源自拉丁语 "semi-"，意为“半的”、“部分的”。

* **intelligible**:  From Latin *intellegibilis*, from *intellegere* "to understand."  源自拉丁语 *intellegibilis*，来自 *intellegere* “理解”。


用法说明：

semi-intelligible 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，例如 speech, message, statement 等，表示这些事物部分可以理解，但整体不够清晰明了。它强调的是理解上的不完整性，而不是完全的不可理解。
