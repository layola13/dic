gelatins:/ˈdʒɛlətɪnz/| n.|明胶；胶状物|A protein substance obtained from animal tissues, used in food and photography. It's a translucent, colorless, brittle, flavorless solid that dissolves in hot water.|例句：The chef used gelatin to set the dessert.  厨师用明胶来凝固甜点。|例句：The photographic plates were coated with a gelatin emulsion.  照相底片涂有明胶乳剂。|近义词：collagen, jelly|反义词：None
