overbridge:/ˈoʊvərbrɪdʒ/ | n. |天桥，立交桥 | A structure carrying a road, path, railway, etc., over another road, railway, or obstacle.  | Example: We crossed the overbridge to reach the other side of the road. 我们穿过天桥到达马路对面。 |近义词：flyover, viaduct, overpass | 反义词：underpass
