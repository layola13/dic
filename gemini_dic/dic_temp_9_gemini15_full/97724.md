iniquous:/ɪˈnɪkwəs/| adj.|邪恶的；不公平的；不正当的|Wicked; unjust; immoral.  Iniquitous acts are those that are grossly unfair, morally wrong, and often illegal.  The term emphasizes the inherent wickedness or unfairness of the action, rather than simply its illegality.|例句：The iniquitous tax laws disproportionately burdened the poor. = 不公平的税法不成比例地加重了穷人的负担。

The iniquitous treatment of prisoners of war is a violation of international law. = 对战俘的残酷待遇违反了国际法。|近义词：unjust, wicked, unfair, immoral, nefarious, iniquitous|反义词：just, fair, righteous, equitable
