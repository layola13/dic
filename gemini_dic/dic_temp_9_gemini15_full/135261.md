nonparalytic: /ˌnɒnˌpærəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. |非瘫痪的，不瘫痪的 | Not characterized by or causing paralysis.  Describes a condition or illness that does not result in paralysis. |


例句：

The patient experienced a nonparalytic stroke, meaning they did not lose motor function. = 病人经历了一次非瘫痪性中风，这意味着他们没有失去运动功能。


The virus caused a nonparalytic illness with flu-like symptoms. = 这种病毒引起了一种非瘫痪性疾病，伴有类似流感的症状。


近义词：unparalyzed, non-paralytic


反义词：paralytic


词性：形容词
