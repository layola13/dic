overbriefing:/ˌoʊvərˈbriːfɪŋ/ | n. |过度简报；信息过少| The act of providing insufficient information or briefing, often leading to misunderstandings or inadequate preparation.  It's the opposite of over briefing, which implies giving too much information. | Example: The overbriefing before the mission resulted in several critical errors by the team.  过度简报导致团队犯了几个严重的错误。|近义词: under briefing, insufficient briefing, inadequate preparation |反义词: over briefing, comprehensive briefing
