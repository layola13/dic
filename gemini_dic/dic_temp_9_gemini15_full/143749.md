outmanipulates:/ˌaʊt.mənˈɪpjʊleɪts/| vt.|巧妙地操纵；胜过…的操控|To skillfully manipulate or control someone or something to a greater extent than another.|The skilled negotiator outmanipulated his opponent, securing a favorable deal.  熟练的谈判者巧妙地操纵了他的对手，从而达成了一笔有利的交易。|近义词：outwit, outsmart, surpass, exceed|反义词：be manipulated by, be controlled by|动词
