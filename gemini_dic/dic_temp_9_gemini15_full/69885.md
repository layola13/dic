fertigation:/ˌfɜːrtɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. | 灌溉施肥 | The application of fertilizers through an irrigation system. This method combines irrigation and fertilization, delivering nutrients directly to the plant roots through the water.  | Example: Fertigation is a highly efficient method of delivering nutrients to crops, reducing fertilizer waste and improving water use efficiency. 灌溉施肥是一种高效的向作物输送养分的方法，可减少肥料浪费并提高用水效率。| 近义词：fertigation system, nutrient application,  | 反义词：dry fertilization (applying fertilizer without irrigation) | 名词
