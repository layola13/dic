pillage:/ˈpɪlɪdʒ/| v. n.|掠夺；抢劫；洗劫|The act of looting, especially in war; to steal goods from (a place or person), typically using force. |例句：The Vikings pillaged the coastal villages. 维京人洗劫了沿海村庄。

例句：The army pillaged the city, leaving behind only destruction.  军队洗劫了这座城市，只留下满目疮痍。


近义词：plunder, loot, sack, raid, despoil|反义词：restore, rebuild, replenish

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pillage the countryside for supplies. = 他们洗劫乡村以获取补给。
The soldiers pillage villages for food and resources. = 士兵们洗劫村庄以获取食物和资源。

一般过去时:
The invaders pillaged the town and burned the houses. = 侵略者洗劫了城镇并烧毁了房屋。
The pirates pillaged the ship and stole all the treasure. = 海盗洗劫了船只并偷走了所有财宝。

现在完成时:
The rebels have pillaged several towns in the region. = 反叛分子已经洗劫了该地区几个城镇。
The army has pillaged the country's resources for years. = 多年来，军队一直在掠夺该国的资源。

过去完成时:
The villagers had been pillaged many times before the army arrived. = 在军队到达之前，村民们已经被洗劫过很多次了。
The city had been pillaged and left in ruins. = 城市已经被洗劫一空，变成废墟。

将来时:
The invaders will pillage the city if they are not stopped. = 如果不阻止侵略者，他们将洗劫这座城市。
If the conflict continues, the rebels will pillage more villages. = 如果冲突继续，叛乱分子将洗劫更多的村庄。

虚拟语气:
If they had not pillaged the village, the people would still have their homes. = 如果他们没有洗劫村庄，村民们仍然会有他们的家园。
I wish they hadn't pillaged the museum. = 我真希望他们没有洗劫博物馆。

被动语态:
The village was pillaged by bandits last night. = 昨晚村庄被强盗洗劫了。
The ancient city was pillaged centuries ago. = 古城几个世纪前被洗劫了。

疑问句:
Was the town pillaged by the enemy? = 城镇是被敌人洗劫的吗？
Have the rebels pillaged any other villages? = 反叛分子洗劫了其他村庄吗？

否定句:
The army did not pillage the villages. = 军队没有洗劫村庄。
They refused to pillage the innocent civilians. = 他们拒绝洗劫无辜平民。


PHRASE->
pillage a city = 洗劫一座城市
pillage resources = 掠夺资源
pillage and plunder = 掠夺和抢劫
pillage the countryside = 洗劫农村
pillage and burn = 洗劫焚烧

词源:
源自古法语 *piler,  意为"to plunder, loot,"  最终源自拉丁语 *pilare,  意为 "to rob, plunder."
