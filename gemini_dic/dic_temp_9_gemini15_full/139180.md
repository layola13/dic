objected:/əbˈdʒɛktɪd/| v.|反对，不赞成|To express or feel disapproval of or opposition to something.|The committee objected to the proposed changes. 委员会反对拟议的更改。|近义词：protested, opposed, dissented|反义词：approved, agreed, consented|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He objects to the noise. = 他反对噪音。
They object to the new policy. = 他们反对这项新政策。

一般过去时:
She objected to my suggestion. = 她反对我的建议。
We objected strongly to the decision. = 我们强烈反对这个决定。

现在完成时:
I have objected to this many times. = 我已经多次反对过这件事。
They have objected to the plan since the beginning. = 从一开始他们就反对这个计划。

过去完成时:
He had objected to the idea before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就反对过这个主意。
She had already objected when he presented his proposal. = 当他提出建议时，她已经反对了。

将来时:
I will object if they try to change the rules. = 如果他们试图改变规则，我会反对。
They will object to the increased workload. = 他们会反对增加的工作量。

虚拟语气:
I would object if I were in his position. = 如果我处于他的位置，我会反对。
If he had known, he would have objected. = 如果他知道的话，他会反对的。

被动语态:
His proposal was objected to by several members. = 他的提议遭到几名成员的反对。
The changes were strongly objected to. = 这些改变遭到了强烈的反对。

疑问句:
Did anyone object to the plan? = 有人反对这个计划吗？
Does she object to working late? = 她反对加班吗？

否定句:
He didn't object to the idea. = 他不反对这个主意。
They didn't object to the extra work. = 他们不反对额外的工作。


PHRASE->
object to sth = 反对某事
have no objection to sth = 不反对某事
strongly object = 强烈反对
raise an objection = 提出异议
make an objection = 提出异议
