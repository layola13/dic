词源:  The word "mechanurgy" is a blend of "mechanism" (from Greek *mechane* meaning "machine, device, contrivance") and "-urgy" (from Greek *-ergein* meaning "to work").  Therefore, it literally means "the working of machines" or "the science of machines".
