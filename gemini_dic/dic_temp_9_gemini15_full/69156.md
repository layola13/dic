/ˈfɔːknər/| n. |福克纳（姓氏）| Faulkner is a surname of English origin.  It is a variant of the surname Falkner, which itself derives from the Old English personal name "Falco," meaning "falcon," combined with the occupational suffix "-ner," indicating someone who worked with or bred falcons.  The name thus originally signified a falconer. |William Faulkner was a renowned American novelist and short-story writer. |近义词:|无|反义词:|无|
