remotes:/rɪˈmoʊts/ | n. |遥控器，偏远地区 |  A device used to control a machine or appliance from a distance, or something situated far away from populated areas. | Example: I need a new remote for my television. 我需要一个新的电视遥控器。  The remote villages are difficult to access. 偏远的村庄很难到达。|近义词：controls, controllers, dials,  distant areas, outlying regions |反义词：nearby areas, local areas
