shoebill:/ˈʃuːbɪl/| n. |鲸头鹳|A large, flightless bird of eastern tropical Africa with a very large, broad bill resembling a shoe.  It is known for its distinctive appearance and unusual hunting behavior. | The shoebill is a fascinating bird to observe in its natural habitat. 鲸头鹳在其自然栖息地是一种令人着迷的鸟类。|近义词：whalehead (informal)|反义词: N/A
