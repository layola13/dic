outdesigns:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈzaɪnz/| vt.|胜过…的设计；设计得比…好|To surpass in design; to design better than.|The new building outdesigns the old one in both functionality and aesthetics. 新建筑在功能性和美学方面都胜过旧建筑。|outdo, surpass, excel|inferior, worse design|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outdesigns his competitors in every project. = 他在每一个项目中都胜过他的竞争对手。
The architect outdesigns all other proposals with her innovative design. = 建筑师凭借其创新的设计胜过了所有其他方案。


一般过去时:
Their team outdesigned the opponent's strategy in the competition. = 他们的团队在比赛中设计方案胜过了对手。
The artist outdesigned the previous version of the painting with a more vibrant color palette. = 艺术家用更鲜艳的色彩调色板设计出了比以前更好的绘画版本。


现在完成时:
She has outdesigned all previous attempts at solving the engineering challenge. = 她已经胜过了所有先前解决这一工程难题的尝试。
The company has outdesigned its rivals in the market with its sleek new product. = 公司凭借其时尚的新产品在市场上胜过了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
They had outdesigned their previous model by incorporating new technology. = 他们通过融入新技术，设计出了比以前更好的型号。
The designer had outdesigned her initial sketches with a more refined and elegant design. = 设计师用更精致优雅的设计胜过了她最初的草图。


将来时:
We will outdesign the competition with our groundbreaking technology. = 我们将凭借我们突破性的技术在竞争中胜出。
The firm will outdesign the existing infrastructure with a more sustainable approach. = 公司将采用更可持续的方法来设计出比现有基础设施更好的方案。


虚拟语气:
If he had more time, he would outdesign his current work. = 如果他有更多时间，他会设计出比现在更好的作品。
I wish I could outdesign the limitations of the current software. = 我希望我能克服当前软件的限制，设计出更好的方案。


被动语态:
The old bridge was outdesigned by the new one. = 旧桥的设计被新桥超越了。
His proposal was outdesigned by a more practical and cost-effective solution. = 他的方案被一个更实用、更经济的方案超越了。


疑问句:
Can we outdesign the current market leader? = 我们能胜过目前的市场领导者吗？
Does this new design outdesign the previous one? = 这种新的设计比之前的更好吗？


否定句:
They did not outdesign the opposing team's strategy. = 他们没有胜过对方团队的策略。
The company failed to outdesign its competitors in the latest product release. = 公司在最新的产品发布中未能胜过竞争对手。
