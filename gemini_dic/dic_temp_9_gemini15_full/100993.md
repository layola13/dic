invalide:/ɪnˈvælɪd/| adj. |无效的，作废的；体弱多病的| Invalid means not legally or officially acceptable.  It can also refer to someone who is weak or sick. |例句：The contract is invalid because it wasn't properly signed. 这份合同无效，因为它没有被正确签署。

The invalid soldier was cared for by his family.  这位体弱多病的士兵由家人照顾。|近义词：null, void, ineffective, sickly, infirm|反义词：valid, effective, strong, healthy|形容词
