probationship:/proʊˈbeɪʃənʃɪp/| n. |试用期；缓刑期 |A period of time during which someone is tested or observed to see if they are suitable for a particular job or position, or a period of time during which a convicted criminal is supervised in the community.  It often involves a set of conditions that must be met. |Example:  He completed his probationship successfully and was offered a permanent position. 他顺利完成了试用期，并获得了正式职位。  She was placed on probationship after her conviction for drunk driving. 她因醉酒驾车被判缓刑。|近义词：trial period, apprenticeship, parole |反义词：permanent position, full employment
