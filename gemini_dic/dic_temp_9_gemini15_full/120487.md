mintiest:/ˈmɪntɪst/| adj.| 最薄荷味的，最像薄荷的 | Most resembling mint; having the strongest or most intense mint flavor.  |The mintiest ice cream I've ever tasted was the peppermint swirl.|近义词：most minty, intensely minty|反义词：least minty, bland


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The candy is mintiest in the summer. = 夏天糖果薄荷味最浓。
This toothpaste is the mintiest on the market. = 这款牙膏是市面上薄荷味最浓的。

She has never tasted anything mintier than this. = 她从未尝过比这薄荷味更浓的东西。


过去完成时:
