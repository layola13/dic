pollinate:/ˈpɒləneɪt/ /ˈpɑːləneɪt/| vt.|授粉，使受粉|To transfer pollen from the anther of a flower to the stigma of the same or another flower, enabling fertilization.|Bees pollinate flowers. 蜜蜂给花授粉。The farmer pollinated the plants by hand. 农民手工给植物授粉。|fertilize, inseminate|deprive of pollen|动词
