cribbing:/ˈkrɪbɪŋ/| n.|抄袭；偷窃；（马的）食槽；（婴儿的）婴儿床|The act of copying someone else's work without permission;  the act of stealing something; a manger or feeding trough for horses; a child's bed.|例句：The student was caught cribbing during the exam.  学生考试作弊被抓住了。|近义词：plagiarism, theft, copying, crib|反义词：originality, honesty
