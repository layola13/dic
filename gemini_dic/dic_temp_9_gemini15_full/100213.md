interrogated:/ɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪtɪd/| v.|审问，盘问|To question someone intensively, especially in order to obtain information.  |例句：The police interrogated the suspect for hours. 警察审问了嫌疑犯几个小时。|例句：The journalist interrogated the politician about his controversial policies.  记者就这位政治家的有争议的政策盘问了他。|近义词：question, examine, quiz, cross-examine|反义词：ignore, overlook|动词

USAGE->

The police interrogate suspects regularly. = 警察定期审问嫌疑人。
He interrogates witnesses before court. = 他在法庭审判前会盘问证人。
