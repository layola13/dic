quasi-feudally: /ˈkwɑːzaɪˈfjuːd əli/ | adj. | 准封建地；类似封建制度地 |  Describes a system or situation that resembles feudalism in some aspects but lacks its full characteristics.  It suggests a hierarchical structure with elements of power imbalance,  land ownership, and possibly social obligation, but without the rigid legal and social frameworks of a true feudal system. | The land was worked quasi-feudally, with tenants owing labor and produce to the landowner but lacking the formal serfdom of the medieval period.  | 近义词：semi-feudal, feudalistic (in part) | 反义词：democratic, egalitarian
