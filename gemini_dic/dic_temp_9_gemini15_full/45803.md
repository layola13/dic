custodians:/kʌˈstoʊdiənz/ | n. |保管人；监护人；管理者 |Those who have the responsibility for protecting or maintaining something.  Custodians are responsible for the upkeep of a building or property. |例句：The school custodians keep the building clean and safe. 学校的管理员保持建筑物清洁安全。

例句：The museum custodians carefully preserve the artifacts. 博物馆的管理员小心地保存着文物。


近义词：keepers, guardians, caretakers, conservators | 反义词：destroyers, neglecters


词源:  From Middle English *custodye, from Old French *custode, from Latin *custos ("guard, keeper").  The suffix "-ian" indicates a person who performs a specific action or holds a specific role.
