pooches:/ˈpuːtʃɪz/| n.|小狗；（尤指）宠物狗|The plural of "pooch," a colloquial term for a dog, often implying a small or cute dog.  It carries a more affectionate connotation than "dog."|例句：The children loved playing with the pooches in the park.孩子们喜欢在公园里和小狗玩耍。|例句：She took her two adorable pooches for a walk on the beach. 她带她两只可爱的小狗去海滩散步。|近义词：dogs, pups, canines|反义词：N/A
