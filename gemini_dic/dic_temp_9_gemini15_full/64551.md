erratics:/ɪˈrætɪks/| n.|冰川漂砾；古怪的人或事|Large rocks or boulders that have been transported a considerable distance by glacial action and deposited in an area of different geological composition.|例句：The erratics scattered across the valley floor are evidence of past glacial activity.  山谷底部散落的冰川漂砾是过去冰川活动留下的证据。The erratic behavior of the stock market is causing investors concern. 股市的异常波动正引起投资者的担忧。|近义词：boulder, erratic block, glacial erratic|反义词：consistent, predictable
