USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seizes every opportunity. = 他抓住每一个机会。
The company seizes control of the market. = 公司控制了市场。

一般过去时:
The police seized the stolen goods. = 警察查获了赃物。
She seized her chance to speak. = 她抓住了说话的机会。

现在完成时:
The government has seized the assets of the criminal. = 政府已经没收了罪犯的资产。
They have seized power illegally. = 他们非法夺取了政权。

过去完成时:
The army had seized the city before the reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，军队已经占领了这座城市。
He had seized the opportunity to escape. = 他抓住了逃跑的机会。


将来时:
The rebels will seize control of the capital. = 叛军将控制首都。
The authorities will seize the opportunity to reform the system. = 当局将抓住机会改革制度。

虚拟语气:
If the police had seized the suspect earlier, the crime might have been prevented. = 如果警察早点抓住嫌疑人，也许就能阻止犯罪了。
I wish I had seized the opportunity to travel abroad. = 我真希望我抓住了出国旅行的机会。

被动语态:
The property was seized by the government. = 该房产被政府查封了。
The illegal weapons were seized by the police. = 这些非法武器被警方查获了。

疑问句:

否定句:
