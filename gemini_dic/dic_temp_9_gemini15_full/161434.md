postweaning:/ˌpoʊstˈwiːnɪŋ/ | adj. |断奶后的，断奶期后的 | Relating to the period of time after an animal or infant has been weaned.  This term is commonly used in animal science and developmental psychology to describe the period after the cessation of breastfeeding or the equivalent in other mammals.  The postweaning period is often characterized by significant physiological and behavioral changes. | Example: Postweaning growth rates were significantly higher in the experimental group. 断奶后实验组的生长速度明显高于对照组。  The study focused on the postweaning behavior of rats. 该研究的重点是大鼠的断奶后行为。| 近义词: postlactation | 反义词: preweaning
