rebrewed:/ˌriːˈbruːd/ | adj. | 再酿造的；重新酿造的 |  Describes something that has been brewed again, often implying a second brewing or a re-processing of already brewed material. This term is primarily used in the context of beverages, especially beer. | Example: The brewery is known for its rebrewed stouts, using leftover grains for a unique flavor profile.  = 这家酿酒厂以其再酿造的黑啤酒而闻名，它使用剩余的谷物来创造独特的风味。  |近义词：re-brewed, re-fermented (if applicable) | 反义词：newly brewed, first brewed | 词性：形容词
