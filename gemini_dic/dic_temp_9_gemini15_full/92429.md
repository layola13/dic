hyponatraemia:/ˌhaɪpəʊnætˈriːmiə/| n. |低钠血症|Hyponatraemia is a condition in which the level of sodium in the blood is abnormally low. This can lead to a variety of symptoms, from mild to severe, depending on the severity of the sodium deficiency.| 例句：The patient presented with hyponatraemia, causing nausea and confusion.  病人出现低钠血症，导致恶心和意识混乱。| 例句：Severe hyponatraemia can lead to seizures and coma.  严重的低钠血症可导致癫痫发作和昏迷。|近义词：sodium deficiency, low serum sodium|反义词：hypernatremia
