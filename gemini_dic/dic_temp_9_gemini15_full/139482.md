obstreperously:/ɒbˈstrɛpərəsli/ | adj. |喧闹地；吵闹地；粗暴地 | Excessively or boisterously noisy; clamorous.  Characterized by or given to uncontrolled boisterousness; turbulent. |例句：The children were obstreperously playing in the park.孩子们在公园里喧闹地玩耍。

The crowd became obstreperously unruly after the game ended.  比赛结束后，人群变得喧闹而难以控制。|近义词：noisily, clamorously, boisterously, turbulently, vociferously |反义词：quietly, calmly, peacefully, serenely
