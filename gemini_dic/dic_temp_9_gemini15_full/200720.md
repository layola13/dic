sleet:/sliːt/| n.|冻雨，雨夹雪|Frozen rain.  It's a form of precipitation consisting of ice pellets, which are small, translucent ice balls.  These pellets are formed when rain freezes as it falls through a layer of cold air.|例句：The sleet made driving conditions dangerous. 冻雨使驾驶条件变得危险。  The sleet coated the trees with a layer of ice. 冻雨在树上覆盖了一层冰。|近义词：freezing rain, ice pellets|反义词：rain, snow

词源:
The word "sleet" comes from the Old English word "slīt," meaning "smooth, slippery."  This reflects the nature of sleet as small, icy pellets that can make surfaces slippery.
