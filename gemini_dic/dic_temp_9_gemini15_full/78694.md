gigabytes:/ˈɡɪɡəbaɪts/| n. |吉字节，千兆字节 |A gigabyte (GB) is a unit of information or computer storage equal to one billion bytes.  It's commonly used to measure the size of files, hard drives, and other data storage devices.|例句：The game requires 50 gigabytes of hard drive space. 这个游戏需要50吉字节的硬盘空间。

The video file is over 2 gigabytes in size.  这个视频文件超过2吉字节。|近义词：megabytes, terabytes|反义词：bits, bytes
