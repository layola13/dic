habitant:/ˈhæbɪtænt/ | n. |居民，栖息者 | A person or animal that inhabits a particular place.  |The inhabitants of the island were friendly and welcoming.  岛上的居民友好而热情。  The natural habitat of the panda is mountainous regions. 熊猫的自然栖息地是山区。 | resident, dweller, occupant |  non-resident, stranger
