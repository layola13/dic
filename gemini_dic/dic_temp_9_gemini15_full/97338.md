informingly:/ɪnˈfɔːrmɪŋli/ | adv. | 富有信息地，提供信息地 | In a way that provides information or is informative.  |The professor lectured informingly on the subject of quantum physics.  教授富有信息地讲解了量子物理学。| informatively, instructively | uninformatively, vaguely | 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and doesn't follow typical verb conjugation patterns like "what".  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.  The word itself describes *how* something is done.


例句：
The report was written informingly, providing valuable insights into the market trends. 报告写得很有信息量，提供了宝贵的市场趋势见解。

The speaker presented the data informingly, ensuring the audience understood the key takeaways. 演讲者富有信息地展示了数据，确保观众理解了关键要点。


PHRASE->
This word is not typically used in phrases.  It functions as a single adverbial unit.
