perish:/ˈpɛrɪʃ/| v.| 死亡；枯萎；腐烂；毁灭；丧失；|to die, especially in a violent or sudden way; to be destroyed or lost|The plants perished in the frost.  植物在霜冻中枯萎了。|The ancient civilization perished centuries ago.  古代文明在几个世纪前消失了。|die, decay, expire, ruin, destroy|flourish, thrive, survive|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perishes from hunger. 他饿死了。
The flowers perish in the sun. 花儿在太阳下枯萎了。


一般过去时:
Many people perished in the earthquake.  许多人在地震中丧生。
The old building perished in the fire. 这座旧建筑在大火中被毁。


现在完成时:
Many species have perished due to habitat loss. 许多物种由于栖息地丧失而灭绝。
The hope of a peaceful solution has perished.  和平解决的希望破灭了。


过去完成时:
The crops had perished before the rain came.  庄稼在下雨之前就已经枯萎了。
The empire had perished long before the discovery of America.  帝国早在美洲被发现之前就灭亡了。


将来时:
If we don't act now, many more people will perish. 如果我们现在不采取行动，更多的人将会丧生。
Without water, the plants will perish.  没有水，植物将会枯萎。


虚拟语气:
If he had acted sooner, he might not have perished. 如果他早点行动，他可能不会死。
I wish that war had never perished. 我希望战争从未发生过。


被动语态:
Thousands were perished in the war.  数千人在战争中丧生。
The crops were perished by the drought.  庄稼被干旱旱死了。


疑问句:
Will all these flowers perish soon? 这些花很快都会枯萎吗？
Did many people perish in the accident? 事故中很多人丧生了吗？


否定句:
The plants did not perish despite the drought.  尽管干旱，植物却没有枯萎。
He did not perish in the accident. 他在事故中没有丧生。
