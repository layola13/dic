servicing:/ˈsɜːvɪsɪŋ/ | v. |维修，保养，服务 |The act of maintaining, repairing, or providing service for something.  It can refer to the maintenance of machinery, vehicles, or systems, or the provision of services to customers.| 例句：The car needs servicing. = 这辆车需要保养。

The company provides excellent servicing to its clients. = 这家公司为客户提供优质的服务。


近义词：maintenance, repair, upkeep, support | 反义词：neglect, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mechanic services cars every day. = 机械师每天都维修汽车。
Our company services clients globally. = 我们公司为全球客户提供服务。


一般过去时:
I serviced my bicycle last week. = 我上周修理了我的自行车。
They serviced the air conditioning unit yesterday. = 他们昨天维修了空调设备。


现在完成时:
We have serviced over 1000 clients this year. = 今年我们已经为超过1000位客户提供服务了。
The technician has serviced the machine twice this month. = 技术员这个月已经两次维修了这台机器。


过去完成时:
They had serviced the engine before the race. = 他们在比赛前已经修理了发动机。
The building had been serviced regularly, preventing major issues. = 这栋建筑定期进行保养，避免了重大问题。


将来时:
We will service your car next Tuesday. = 我们下周二将维修您的汽车。
The company will service all its branches by the end of the year. = 公司将在年底前为所有分公司提供服务。


虚拟语气:
If the equipment were serviced regularly, it would last longer. = 如果设备定期维护，它可以使用更长时间。
I wish I had serviced my computer sooner; now it's crashed. = 我真希望我早点维修我的电脑，现在它崩溃了。


被动语态:
The car is being serviced at the moment. = 汽车正在维修中。
Our requests were serviced promptly and efficiently. = 我们的请求得到了迅速有效的处理。


疑问句:
Does this machine require regular servicing? = 这台机器需要定期维修吗？
When was the last time the system was serviced? = 系统上次维修是什么时候？


否定句:
The equipment hasn't been serviced in months. = 设备几个月没保养了。
They did not service the request in a timely manner. = 他们没有及时处理请求。
