percentaged: /pərˈsɛntɪdʒd/ | adj. | 百分比化的；按百分比计算的 |  Expressed or calculated as a percentage.  This word is less common and often considered informal. It describes something that has been converted into or represented as a percentage. | 例句：The results were percentaged to show the relative proportions.  结果被转换成百分比以显示相对比例。

The percentaged figures clearly show the company's growth. 百分比化的数据清楚地显示了公司的增长。


近义词：proportioned,  expressed as a percentage


反义词：unpercentaged (This is not a standard word, but it conveys the opposite meaning)


词性：形容词
