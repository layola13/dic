rebalances: /ˌriːˈbælənsɪz/ | v. | 再平衡；重新平衡 | To restore equilibrium or balance; to adjust the proportions or weight of something to achieve a more stable or desirable state.  | 例句：The portfolio manager rebalances the investment portfolio quarterly to minimize risk.  投资组合经理每季度重新平衡投资组合以最大限度地降低风险。

The central bank rebalances the economy by adjusting interest rates. 中央银行通过调整利率来重新平衡经济。


近义词： readjust, re-weight, realign, stabilize | 反义词： destabilize, unbalance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fund manager rebalances the portfolio regularly. = 基金经理定期重新平衡投资组合。
She rebalances her work and personal life carefully. = 她仔细地平衡工作和生活。


一般过去时:
The company rebalanced its product line last year. = 公司去年重新调整了产品线。
He rebalanced his budget after an unexpected expense. = 他在发生意外开支后重新调整了预算。


现在完成时:
They have rebalanced their marketing strategy this year. = 他们今年已经重新调整了营销策略。
The government has rebalanced its fiscal policy to stimulate growth. = 政府已经重新调整了财政政策以刺激经济增长。


过去完成时:
The team had rebalanced their approach before the final presentation. = 团队在最终演示之前已经重新调整了方法。
She had rebalanced her priorities after a period of reflection. = 在一段时间的反思后，她已经重新调整了优先级。


将来时:
The investor will rebalance his holdings next month. = 投资者下个月将重新调整其持股。
We will rebalance the workload to ensure fairness. = 我们将重新平衡工作量以确保公平。


虚拟语气:
If the team rebalanced their resources, they would be more efficient. = 如果团队重新平衡他们的资源，他们会更高效。
I wish I had rebalanced my diet sooner. = 我希望我早点调整饮食。


被动语态:

The budget was rebalanced to address the deficit. = 预算已被重新平衡以解决赤字问题。


疑问句:

否定句:
The investor did not rebalance his portfolio this quarter. = 投资者本季度没有重新平衡他的投资组合。
They haven't rebalanced their team yet. = 他们还没有重新调整他们的团队。
