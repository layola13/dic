leased:/liːst/ | v. |出租，租赁；被出租 |To grant the use of (property) in return for rent; to rent something.  |例句：We leased a car for our vacation. 我们租了一辆车去度假。|例句：The apartment has been leased to a young couple. 这套公寓已经租给了一对年轻夫妇。|近义词：rented, hired |反义词：owned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They lease their property to tourists every summer. = 他们每年夏天都把他们的房产租给游客。
The company leases office space in the city center. = 这家公司在市中心租用办公空间。

一般过去时:
We leased a small cottage by the sea last year. = 我们去年租了一间海边的简易小屋。
The farmer leased his land to a larger agricultural company. = 农民把他的土地租给了更大的农业公司。

现在完成时:
They have leased that building for five years. = 他们已经租用那栋建筑五年了。
We have leased several vehicles for our business operations. = 我们已经为我们的业务运营租赁了几辆车。

过去完成时:
Before moving in, they had leased the apartment for a year. = 在搬进去之前，他们已经租了那套公寓一年了。
The company had leased the equipment before the project started. = 在项目开始之前，公司已经租用了设备。

将来时:
We will lease a larger warehouse next year. = 我们明年将租用一个更大的仓库。
They will lease the property to the highest bidder. = 他们将把房产租给出价最高的人。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would lease a bigger house. = 如果我们有更多的钱，我们会租一栋更大的房子。
I wish we could lease a car for the weekend. = 我希望我们周末能租一辆车。

被动语态:
The car was leased to him for six months. = 这辆车租给他六个月。
The property is being leased by a multinational corporation. = 该房产正在被一家跨国公司租赁。

疑问句:
Have they leased the apartment yet? = 他们已经租到公寓了吗？
Will you lease the equipment for the whole project? = 你会为整个项目租用设备吗？

否定句:
They did not lease the property because of the high rent. = 由于租金高昂，他们没有租用该房产。
We could not lease a car because none were available. = 我们租不到车，因为没有车可用。

lease renewal = 租赁续期
break a lease = 违约终止租赁
