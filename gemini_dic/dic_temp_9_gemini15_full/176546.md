re-seeking:/ˌriːˈsiːkɪŋ/| v.|重新寻求；再次寻找 |The act of seeking something again or anew.  It implies a previous attempt to find or obtain something that was unsuccessful.|例句：After the initial search proved fruitless, they are re-seeking the missing documents.  再次搜寻无果后，他们正在重新寻找丢失的文件。|例句：The company is re-seeking investors after its initial funding round fell short.  在第一轮融资失败后，该公司正在重新寻找投资者。|近义词：re-searching, seeking again, pursuing anew |反义词：giving up, abandoning, ceasing to search|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are re-seeking funding for their project. = 他们正在为他们的项目重新寻求资金。
We are re-seeking a solution to this problem. = 我们正在重新寻找这个问题的解决方案。


一般过去时:
They re-sought funding after their initial application was rejected. = 他们的初始申请被拒绝后，他们重新寻求资金。
She re-sought her lost keys after searching everywhere. = 她到处寻找后，又重新寻找她丢失的钥匙。


现在完成时:
They have re-sought advice from several experts. = 他们已经向几位专家重新寻求建议。
We have re-sought permission to proceed with the project. = 我们已经重新申请了进行该项目的许可。


过去完成时:
They had re-sought the help of their lawyer before the trial. = 在审判之前，他们已经重新寻求了律师的帮助。
She had re-sought employment after being laid off. = 在被解雇后，她已经重新寻找工作。


将来时:
They will re-seek employment opportunities in the new year. = 他们将在新年重新寻找就业机会。
We will re-seek a more suitable location for the event. = 我们将重新寻找一个更合适的活动地点。


虚拟语气:
If they were to re-seek funding, they would need a strong proposal. = 如果他们要重新寻求资金，他们需要一份强有力的提案。
I wish I had re-sought medical advice sooner. = 我希望我早点重新寻求医疗建议。


被动语态:

A new solution is being re-sought to address the ongoing issue. = 正在重新寻找新的解决方案来解决持续存在的问题。


疑问句:

Are they re-seeking a different approach to the problem? = 他们正在尝试解决这个问题的不同方法吗？


否定句:
They did not re-seek employment after being fired. = 他们被解雇后没有重新找工作。
She did not re-seek his forgiveness. = 她没有再次寻求他的原谅。
