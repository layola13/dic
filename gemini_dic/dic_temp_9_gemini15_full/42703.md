countertop:/ˈkaʊntərtɑːp/| n.|台面，工作台面|A countertop is a horizontal surface, typically made of stone, wood, or synthetic materials, that is installed on top of kitchen cabinets or other furniture.|例句：We chose a granite countertop for our new kitchen. 我们为我们的新厨房选择了一个花岗岩台面。|例句：The countertop was stained with spilled coffee. 台面上沾上了洒出的咖啡渍。|近义词：work surface, benchtop|反义词:floor, basement
