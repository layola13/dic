non-use: /ˈnɒnjuːs/ | n. |不用，不使用，闲置| The lack of use;  a state of not being used.  It often refers to something that exists but is not being utilized or has potential but is not being realized. |例句：The non-use of this equipment is costing the company money.  这套设备的闲置正在给公司造成损失。|  例句：The non-use of this valuable land is a shame. 这块宝地闲置不用真是可惜。|近义词：disuse, idleness, inactivity, dormancy|反义词：use, utilization, employment, application|
