engulfing: /ɪnˈɡʌlfɪŋ/ | v. | 吞没；淹没；吞噬 |  To surround and cover completely; overwhelm.  | 例句：The flames engulfed the building.  火焰吞没了整栋大楼。


The rising tide engulfed the coastal village.  涨潮淹没了沿海村庄。


近义词：swallowing, overwhelming, submerging, inundating | 反义词：emerging, freeing, liberating | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river is engulfing the nearby fields. = 河水正在淹没附近的田地。
Fear is engulfing her. = 恐惧正在吞噬着她。


一般过去时:
The tsunami engulfed the entire town. = 海啸吞噬了整个城镇。
The darkness engulfed the forest. = 黑暗吞噬了森林。


现在完成时:
The forest fire has engulfed thousands of acres. = 森林大火已经吞噬了数千英亩土地。
Grief has engulfed him since his wife's death. = 自妻子去世以来，悲伤一直笼罩着他。


过去完成时:
The flood had engulfed the lower levels of the city before the rescue teams arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，洪水已经淹没了城市的低洼地区。
The army had been engulfed by enemy forces before reinforcements could arrive. = 增援部队到达之前，军队已经被敌军包围了。


将来时:
The lava will engulf the village if the volcano erupts. = 如果火山爆发，熔岩将会吞噬村庄。
The darkness will engulf the city as night falls. = 夜幕降临，黑暗将笼罩城市。


虚拟语气:
If the storm were to engulf the island, the inhabitants would be in grave danger. = 如果风暴吞噬了这个岛屿，岛上的居民将面临严重危险。
I wish the sadness wouldn't engulf me so completely. = 我希望悲伤不要如此完全地吞噬我。


被动语态:
The small boat was engulfed by the waves. = 小船被海浪吞噬了。
The city was engulfed in flames. = 城市被大火吞噬了。


疑问句:
Will the rising waters engulf our homes? = 上涨的河水会淹没我们的家园吗？
Is the entire forest being engulfed by the fire? = 整片森林都被大火吞噬了吗？


否定句:
The firefighters prevented the fire from engulfing the entire building. = 消防员阻止了大火吞噬整栋大楼。
The army managed to avoid being engulfed by the enemy. = 军队成功避免被敌人包围。


PHRASE->
be engulfed in = 沉浸于；被…包围
engulfed by = 被…吞没；被…淹没
engulfed in flames = 陷入火海
engulfed in darkness = 陷入黑暗
engulfed by grief = 沉浸在悲伤中
