sewerless:/ˈsuːərləs/| adj.|无污水管道的；不依赖下水道的|Without a sewer system; not reliant on sewers.  The term often describes systems or technologies designed to manage wastewater without the use of traditional sewage systems.|例句：This sewerless composting toilet is environmentally friendly. 这个无污水管道的堆肥厕所很环保。
