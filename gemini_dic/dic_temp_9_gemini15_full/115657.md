mare's: /mɛərz/ | n. | 母马的；母马所属的 |  Belonging to or associated with a mare (a female horse).  |例句：The mare's foal was healthy and strong. 母马的马驹健康而强壮。

The mare's mane was beautifully braided. 母马的鬃毛编得非常漂亮。|近义词：female horse's |反义词：stallion's


USAGE->
This is not applicable to a possessive form like "mare's".  Possessive forms don't conjugate through verb tenses.  The verb tense would apply to a verb used *with* the possessive phrase.  For example:

一般现在时:
The mare's foal drinks milk. = 母马的马驹喝奶。
The mare's coat shines in the sun. = 母马的毛在阳光下闪闪发光。

一般过去时:
The mare's foal drank milk yesterday. = 母马的马驹昨天喝了奶。
The mare's coat shone brightly last week. = 母马的毛上周闪闪发光。

现在完成时:
The mare's foal has drunk all its milk. = 母马的马驹已经喝完了所有的奶。
The farmer has brushed the mare's coat. = 农民已经刷过母马的毛了。

过去完成时:
The mare's foal had drunk most of its milk before I arrived. = 我到达之前，母马的马驹已经喝掉了大部分的奶。
The mare's coat had been groomed before the show. = 演出前母马的毛已经被梳理过了。

将来时:

The groom will clean the mare's stall tomorrow. = 饲养员明天会打扫母马的马厩。

虚拟语气:
If the mare's foal were stronger, it would run faster. = 如果母马的马驹更强壮，它就会跑得更快。
I wish the mare's coat were cleaner. = 我希望母马的毛更干净。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
