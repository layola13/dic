comebacks:/ˈkʌmbæks/| n.|绝地反击；妙语；精彩回应；东山再起|A comeback is a return to a previous level of success or popularity, or a clever or witty reply.|例句：Her comeback to the music scene was spectacular.  她的音乐界复出非常壮观。|例句：His comeback was quick and decisive.他的反击迅速而果断。|近义词：recovery, resurgence, retort, rejoinder|反义词：failure, defeat, downfall
