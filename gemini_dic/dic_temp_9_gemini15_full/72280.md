flote:/floʊt/| v.|漂浮，飘动|to be suspended in or on a liquid or gas, or to move gently in a liquid or gas|The boat floated gently on the water.  船轻轻地漂浮在水上。The balloon floated up into the sky. 气球飘上了天空。|drift, hover, sail|sink|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The leaf floats on the water. 叶子漂浮在水上。
The balloon floats in the air. 气球漂浮在空中。

一般过去时:
The boat floated down the river. 船顺流而下。
The feather floated to the ground. 羽毛飘落到地上。

现在完成时:
The boat has floated out to sea. 船已经漂到海上了。
The balloon has floated away. 气球已经飘走了。

过去完成时:
The boat had floated away before we arrived. 我们到达之前，船已经漂走了。
The leaves had floated down the stream. 叶子已经顺流而下。

将来时:
The boat will float down the river tomorrow. 船明天将顺流而下。
The balloon will float in the air. 气球将会漂浮在空中。

虚拟语气:
If the boat floated, we could go for a ride. 如果船漂浮起来，我们就可以去兜风。
I wish the balloon floated higher. 我希望气球能飘得更高。

被动语态:
The boat was floated down the river. 船被顺流冲下去了。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常用主动语态)
The balloon was floated into the sky. 气球被放飞到空中。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常用主动语态)

疑问句:
Does the boat float? 船会漂浮吗？
Will the balloon float? 气球会漂浮吗？

否定句:
The boat didn't float. 船没有漂浮。
The balloon didn't float properly. 气球没有漂浮好。


PHRASE->
float on:继续漂浮
float away:飘走
float along:漂流
float down:漂流而下
float up:漂浮起来
