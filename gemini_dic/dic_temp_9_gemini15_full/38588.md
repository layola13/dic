concordantly:/kənˈkɔːrdəntli/ | adv. | 一致地；协调地；和谐地 | In agreement or harmony.  The results of the two studies concordantly supported the hypothesis.  | The two teams worked concordantly to achieve their shared goal.  两个团队协同工作以实现共同目标。 | consistently, harmoniously, uniformly | discordantly, inconsistently, disharmoniously | 副词
