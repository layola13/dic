pocketbook:/ˈpɑːkɪtbʊk/ /ˈpɒkɪtbʊk/| n. |钱包；笔记本；袖珍书；小型手提包 |A pocketbook is a small bag or purse carried in the pocket or hand, typically by a woman, to hold money, keys, and other small personal items. It can also refer to a small, inexpensive book, or figuratively, to someone's finances. |例句：She reached into her pocketbook for her keys. = 她把手伸进钱包里拿钥匙。

例句：He bought a pocketbook edition of Shakespeare's plays. = 他买了一本莎士比亚戏剧的袖珍本。

近义词：purse, handbag, wallet, billfold, notebook

反义词：None directly applicable
