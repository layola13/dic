poorhouse: /ˈpʊrˌhaʊs/ | n. |济贫院，贫民院 | A poorhouse is a publicly funded institution providing housing and support for the poor, especially the elderly or infirm.  It is often associated with a time of limited social welfare programs. | Example: Many elderly people ended up in the poorhouse during the Great Depression.  许多老年人在大萧条时期最终住进了济贫院。|近义词：workhouse, almshouse |反义词：mansion, palace
