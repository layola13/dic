mandrake:/ˈmændreɪk/| n.|曼德拉草|A mythical plant, believed to scream when uprooted, with a root resembling a human figure, and possessing magical properties.  It is often associated with witchcraft and sorcery.|例句：In folklore, the mandrake root was believed to possess magical properties and could bring good luck or misfortune depending on its use.  在民间传说中，曼德拉草根被认为具有神奇的功效，可以带来好运或厄运，这取决于它的用途。|近义词：magic root, devil's herb|反义词:|无
