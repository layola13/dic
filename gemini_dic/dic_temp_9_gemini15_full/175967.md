rattan-palms:/ˈrætn pɑːmz/| n. |藤棕榈科植物|Rattan palms refer to climbing palms belonging to the genus *Calamus* and related genera within the Arecaceae family.  They are known for their long, slender stems which are used extensively in furniture making and other crafts.  The term is a compound noun, combining "rattan" (the material) with "palms" (the plant family).| Example:  Many traditional crafts in Southeast Asia utilize rattan-palms for weaving baskets and mats.  许多东南亚的传统工艺都利用藤棕榈来编织篮子和垫子。|近义词：climbing palms, cane palms|反义词：无
