quasi-compliant:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kəmˈplaɪənt/ | adj. | 准符合的；近似符合的 |  Partially or seemingly compliant;  approximating compliance but not fully meeting all requirements or specifications.  It suggests a degree of adherence to rules or standards, but with some shortcomings or deviations. | Example: The software is quasi-compliant with the new regulations, meaning it meets most but not all requirements.  软件几乎符合新规，这意味着它满足大部分但并非所有要求。| 近义词: partially compliant, substantially compliant, near-compliant | 反义词: fully compliant, non-compliant |
