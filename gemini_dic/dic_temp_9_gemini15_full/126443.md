mystificator: /ˌmɪstɪfɪˈkeɪtər/ | n. |神秘化者，使迷惑者 | One who mystifies; one who obscures or confuses.  A person or thing that causes mystification or confusion. |例句：The magician, a master mystificator, amazed the audience with his illusions.  魔术师，一位精通神秘化的人，用他的幻象让观众惊叹不已。

例句：His cryptic remarks made him seem like a mystificator, deliberately avoiding clarity. 他含糊其辞的发言让他看起来像个故意含糊其辞的人。


近义词：enigmatist, obfuscator, deceiver, illusionist

反义词：clarifier, elucidator, truth-teller


词性: 名词
