drainages:/ˈdreɪnɪdʒɪz/ | n. | 排水系统；排水；流域 | The process or system by which something, especially water, is drained away from an area.  It can refer to the natural drainage of land or to artificial systems designed for this purpose. |例句：The poor drainage caused flooding in the basement.  糟糕的排水系统导致地下室被淹。

The new drainage system significantly improved the area's sanitation. 新的排水系统大大改善了该地区的卫生状况。

近义词：waterway, outflow, runoff,  sewerage

反义词：retention, accumulation, damming
