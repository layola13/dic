ill-advisedly:/ˌɪl ədˈvaɪzɪdli/ | adv. | 不明智地，欠考虑地 | done in a way that shows poor judgment or is likely to have bad consequences.  It implies a lack of careful thought or planning before acting. |例句：He ill-advisedly challenged the champion to a duel. 他不明智地向冠军挑战决斗。


例句：She ill-advisedly invested all her savings in a risky venture. 她欠考虑地把所有积蓄都投资到一个高风险的项目中。


近义词： unwisely, foolishly, imprudently, recklessly, rashly


反义词： wisely, prudently, cautiously, thoughtfully
