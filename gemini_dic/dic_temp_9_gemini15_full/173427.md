quasi-equal:/ˈkwɑːziˈiːkwəl/| adj.|准平等的，近乎相等的 |Almost equal; nearly but not exactly equal.  The term implies a similarity or approximation to equality, but not a perfect or strict equivalence.  |例句：The two groups were quasi-equal in terms of size and resources.  两个群体在规模和资源方面几乎相等。

The distribution of wealth was quasi-equal among the villagers. 村民的财富分配几乎平等。|近义词：approximately equal, near-equal, roughly equal |反义词：unequal, disparate, dissimilar
