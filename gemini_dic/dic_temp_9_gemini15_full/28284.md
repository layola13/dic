capturable: /ˈkæptʃərəbl/ | adj. | 可捕获的，可捉住的 | Able to be captured or seized.  This adjective describes something that can be successfully caught, apprehended, or obtained.  |例句：The escaped criminal is highly capturable given his limited resources.  逃犯由于资源有限，很容易被抓到。


例句：The rare bird is capturable only with specialized equipment.  这种珍稀鸟类只有使用专业设备才能捕捉到。


近义词：catchable, seizable, attainable, obtainable


反义词：uncapturable, inescapable, unobtainable


词性：形容词
