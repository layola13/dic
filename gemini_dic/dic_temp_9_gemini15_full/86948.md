hen:/hen/| n.|母鸡，雌鸡|A hen is a female chicken, typically kept for its eggs.|例句：The hen laid a brown egg this morning.  今天早上母鸡下了一个棕色的蛋。|例句：We have several hens in our backyard. 我们后院养了几只母鸡。|近义词：female chicken, pullet|反义词：rooster, cock


USAGE->
