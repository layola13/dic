epithelium:/ˌɛpɪˈθiːliəm/| n. |上皮组织|A sheet-like tissue that covers body surfaces, lines body cavities and forms glands.  Epithelial cells are closely packed together and have a free surface.  They function in protection, secretion, absorption, excretion, filtration, diffusion, and sensory reception.|Example: The skin's outer layer is composed of epithelium. 皮肤的外层由上皮组织构成。  The lining of the digestive tract is a type of epithelium. 消化道的内衬是一种上皮组织。|近义词：lining, covering|反义词：无
