polysilicic: /ˌpɒliˈsɪlɪsɪk/| adj. |多硅酸的|  Relating to or containing several silicon atoms, particularly in the context of silicate minerals or compounds.  It often refers to a type of silica with a complex structure involving multiple silicon-oxygen units. |The research focused on the properties of polysilicic acids in soil formation.| 近义词：multisilicic | 反义词：monosilicic
