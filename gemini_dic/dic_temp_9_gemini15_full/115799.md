maritime:/ˈmærɪtaɪm/| adj.|海事的；海上的；海军的|relating to the sea, especially in relation to navigation, shipping, or commerce.  Maritime law governs activities at sea.|例句：The maritime museum houses a vast collection of ship models.  这个海事博物馆收藏了大量的船模。|例句： He has a long and distinguished career in the maritime industry. 他在海运业拥有漫长而辉煌的职业生涯。|近义词：naval, nautical, seafaring|反义词：terrestrial, inland
