lepidosauria:/ˌlɛpɪdəˈsɔəriə/| n. |鳞龙类|Lepidosauria is a clade of diapsid reptiles that includes lizards, snakes, amphisbaenians, and tuatara.  It is characterized by features such as overlapping scales, a kinetic skull (in many members), and a unique jaw structure.|The Lepidosauria includes a wide variety of species, showcasing remarkable adaptations to diverse environments.|近义词：Squamata (though not a perfect synonym, as it only includes lizards and snakes)|反义词：无
