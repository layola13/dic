kauri:/ˈkaʊri/| n.|贝壳杉，考里树|A kauri is a type of large coniferous tree native to New Zealand, known for its durable timber.  The word also refers to the resin (kauri gum) produced by these trees.|例句：The kauri forest is a breathtaking sight.|近义词：conifer, evergreen|反义词:无
