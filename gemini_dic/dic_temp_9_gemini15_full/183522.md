repurchasing: /ˌriːˈpɜːrtʃeɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 再购买，回购 | The act of buying back something that has already been sold.  This often refers to a company buying back its own stock or a customer returning an item and then repurchasing it. | 例句：The company is engaged in a program of repurchasing its own shares. = 公司正在进行回购自家股票的计划。


例句：Many customers returned the faulty product and subsequently repurchasing a new one. = 许多顾客退回了有缺陷的产品，随后又重新购买了一个新的。


近义词：rebuying, buying back, reacquisition


反义词：selling, disposal


词性：动词
