manticore:/ˈmæntiˌkɔːr/| n. |人面狮身兽|A mythical creature with the body of a lion, the head of a human, and often the wings of a dragon or bat, and a scorpion tail.  It is typically depicted as fierce and dangerous.|Example: In Greek mythology, the manticore is a fearsome beast that terrorizes travelers.  希腊神话中，人面狮身兽是一种令旅行者恐惧的可怕野兽。|近义词：griffin, chimera|反义词：none
