ditcher:/ˈdɪtʃər/| n. |沟渠挖掘者；排水道工人；（非正式）放弃者|A ditcher is a person who digs ditches, often as a job.  The term can also be used informally to refer to someone who abandons something or someone. |例句：The ditchers worked tirelessly to complete the drainage project.  沟渠挖掘者夜以继日地工作以完成排水项目。|例句：He was considered a ditcher by his friends after he abandoned his plans to travel the world. 他的朋友们认为他放弃环游世界的计划后成了个放弃者。|近义词：excavator, ditch digger, deserter (informal)|反义词：perseverer, completer
