propagatrix: /ˈprɒpəɡeɪtrɪks/ | n. | 传播者；繁殖者 | A propagatrix is a person or thing that propagates or spreads something, especially a disease or idea.  It often refers to a female entity involved in propagation. | Example:  She was considered a propagatrix of revolutionary ideas in her time. 她被认为是她那个时代的革命思想的传播者。 | 近义词: disseminator, spreader, promoter | 反义词: suppressor, inhibitor |  词性: noun

The scientist studies how the virus propagates. = 科学家研究病毒是如何传播的。
She propagates plants in her greenhouse. = 她在温室里繁殖植物。

一般过去时:

过去完成时:
