godlessly:/ˈɡɑːdləsli/| adv.|不敬神地，无神地，邪恶地 |Acting without regard for God or religion; impiously; wickedly.  |例句：He lived a godlessly life, ignoring all religious principles. 他过着一种不敬神的生活，无视所有宗教原则。

例句：They behaved godlessly, showing no respect for sacred things. 他们行为邪恶，对神圣的事物毫无尊重。


近义词：impiously, wickedly, ungodly, irreligiously

反义词：piously, religiously, devoutly
