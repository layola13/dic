cowgirl:/ˈkaʊɡɜːrl/|/ˈkaʊɡɜːl/| n. |女牛仔；牧场女工|A cowgirl is a woman who works on a ranch or farm, often riding horses and involved in cattle herding.  They are typically skilled equestrians and comfortable with the demands of a rural lifestyle.|例句：She's a skilled cowgirl, able to rope a calf with ease. 她是一位技艺娴熟的女牛仔，能够轻松地套住小牛。|例句：The cowgirl expertly guided her horse through the narrow canyon.  这位女牛仔熟练地引导她的马穿过狭窄的峡谷。|近义词：ranch hand (woman), cowpuncher (woman), farmhand (woman)|反义词：city girl, urbanite|名词
