candlefish:/ˈkændlfɪʃ/| n. |燭魚|A small, oily fish (genus *Thaleichthys*) of the northern Pacific Ocean, whose body contains so much oil that it can be burned like a candle.  |The candlefish is a valuable food source for many animals, and its oil was historically used by indigenous peoples. |近义词：eulachon (a synonym)|反义词：无
