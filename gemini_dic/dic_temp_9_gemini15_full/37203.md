comes:/kʌmz/| v.|来，来到；发生；源于|To move toward or reach a place from a different place.|例句：The bus comes every ten minutes.  公交车每十分钟来一趟。|例句：Success comes from hard work. 成功源于努力。|近义词：arrives, proceeds, originates|反义词：goes, departs, leaves


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The train comes at 8 am. = 火车早上八点到。
He comes to work by bus. = 他乘公交车上班。

一般过去时:
The package came yesterday. = 包裹昨天到了。
She came to see me last week. = 她上周来看我了。

现在完成时:
My opportunity has finally come. = 我的机会终于来了。
He has come to understand the problem. = 他已经理解了这个问题。

过去完成时:
The rain had come before the game started. = 游戏开始前已经下雨了。
After she had come, we started the meeting. = 她来了之后，我们才开始会议。

将来时:
The next bus will come soon. = 下一趟公交车很快就会来。
Spring will come next month. = 春天将在下个月到来。

虚拟语气:
If he came, I would be happy. = 如果他来，我会很高兴。
I wish he came more often. = 我希望他经常来。

被动语态:
The decision was finally come to. = 决定最终做出了。
This problem must be come to grips with immediately. = 这个问题必须立即得到解决。


疑问句:
When does the next train come? = 下一趟火车什么时候来？
Does he often come to visit? = 他经常来访吗？

否定句:
The train didn't come on time. = 火车没有准时到。
He doesn't come here anymore. = 他不再来这里了。


PHRASE->
as it comes = 顺其自然

come across = 偶然遇见
come along = 进步；一起来
come down with = 染上（疾病）
