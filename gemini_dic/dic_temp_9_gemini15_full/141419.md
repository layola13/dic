ophiuran:/ˈɒfɪjuərən/| n. |蛇尾,海蛇尾|Ophiurans are echinoderms belonging to the class Ophiuroidea.  They are characterized by their distinct, slender arms that are sharply differentiated from a small central disc.  They are often found in various marine habitats, from shallow waters to the deep sea. |例句：The diver observed several brittle stars, a type of ophiuran, clinging to the coral reef.  潜水员观察到几只蛇尾（一种海蛇尾），它们依附在珊瑚礁上。  Many ophiurans play a vital role in the marine ecosystem by helping to recycle nutrients. 许多海蛇尾通过帮助循环营养物质在海洋生态系统中发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词：brittle star, serpent star|反义词：无
