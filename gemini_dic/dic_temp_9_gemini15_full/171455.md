puff-pastries:/pʌf ˈpeɪstriːz/| n. |酥皮糕点|Light, flaky pastries made with layers of dough and butter, creating air pockets when baked.  They are often sweet and served as desserts or with fillings. |例句：These puff pastries are filled with delicious cream. 这些酥皮糕点里充满了美味的奶油。
例句：The bakery sells a variety of puff pastries, from fruit tarts to savory cheese puffs. 这家面包店出售各种酥皮糕点，从水果馅饼到咸味奶酪泡芙。|近义词：pastries, flaky pastries|反义词：dense pastries
