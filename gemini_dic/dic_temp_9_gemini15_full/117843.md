mellon:/ˈmɛlən/| n. |甜瓜，哈密瓜|A melon is a large, round fruit with a sweet, juicy flesh and a hard rind.  Many varieties exist, including cantaloupe, watermelon, and honeydew. |例句：I bought a delicious cantaloupe melon at the farmer's market. 我在农贸市场买了一个美味的哈密瓜。|例句：Watermelons are my favorite type of melon.  西瓜是我最喜欢的甜瓜。|近义词：cantaloupe, watermelon, honeydew, muskmelon |反义词：无
