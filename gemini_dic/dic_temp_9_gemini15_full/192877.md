seasonally:/ˈsiːzənəli/| adj.|季节性地，季节的|Occurring or done during a particular season or seasons; relating to a particular season.|例句：The hotel is seasonally busy. 这家酒店在旅游季节很忙。|近义词：periodically, cyclically|反义词: constantly, perpetually
