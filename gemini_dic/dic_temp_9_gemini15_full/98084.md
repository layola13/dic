inquisitional: /ˌɪnkwɪˈzɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 宗教裁判所式的；审问式的；严厉盘问的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Inquisition, especially its methods of interrogation and punishment.  It implies a rigorous and often unfair questioning process, characterized by suspicion, coercion, and a lack of due process. |

例句：

The inquisitional methods used by the police led to false confessions.  警察使用的审问式方法导致了虚假供词。

His inquisitional tone made the witness uncomfortable. 他严厉盘问的语气让证人感到不舒服。


近义词：interrogative, investigative, prosecutorial,  inquisitive (less formal and less negative)

反义词： lenient, merciful, fair, impartial, open-minded


词性: adjective (形容词)
