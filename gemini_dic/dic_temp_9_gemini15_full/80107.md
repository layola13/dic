gobbles:/ˈɡɑːbəlz/ /ˈɡɒbəlz/| vt. & n.|狼吞虎咽；发出咯咯声|To eat something very quickly and noisily; a series of quick gulps or noises.|例句：He gobbled down his food in a few minutes. 他几分钟就狼吞虎咽地吃完了食物。

例句：The turkey gobbles loudly.  那只火鸡大声地发出咯咯声。

近义词：devour, gulp, scarf down, gobble up|反义词：nibble, sip, savor
