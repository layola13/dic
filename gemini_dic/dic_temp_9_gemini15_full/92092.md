hyperrealizing: /ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 超现实化，过度现实化 |  The act of making something seem more real or intense than it actually is, often through exaggeration or artificial means.  It can refer to a process of amplifying reality or creating a heightened sense of realism, sometimes to the point of being unrealistic or artificial. | 例句：The film's hyperrealizing techniques made the violence seem almost too real. 这部电影的超现实化技巧使暴力看起来几乎过于真实。


The director's hyperrealizing of the characters' emotions made the film emotionally exhausting.  导演对人物情感的过度现实化使得这部电影在情感上令人筋疲力尽。| 近义词：over-dramatizing, intensifying, exaggerating | 反义词：understating, downplaying, minimizing | 动词
