muskets:/ˈmʌskɪts/| n. |火枪|A musket is a muzzle-loading firearm, typically a long gun with a smooth bore, used from the 16th to the 19th centuries.  It was a significant weapon in warfare and played a crucial role in the development of military tactics.| Example:  The soldiers fired their muskets in unison.  士兵们齐声开火。|近义词：firearm, long gun|反义词：无
