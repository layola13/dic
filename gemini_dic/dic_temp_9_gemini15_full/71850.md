flense:/flɛns/| vt.|剥皮；割下鲸鱼的脂肪|To remove the blubber from (a whale or other large marine mammal).  The term often implies a violent or brutal method.|例句：The whalers began to flense the dead whale. 捕鲸者开始剥割死鲸的鲸脂。  The process of flensing was long and arduous. 剥鲸脂的过程漫长而艰巨。|近义词：skin, flay,  strip|反义词：cover, clothe|动词
