luscious:/ˈlʌʃəs/ | adj. | 甘美的；多汁的；性感的；华丽的 |  Deliciously sweet and juicy; appealing to the senses; luxuriously rich and attractive. | 例句：The luscious peaches were ripe and juicy.  甘美的桃子又熟又多汁。

The singer's luscious voice filled the concert hall. 歌手甜美的声音充满了音乐厅。


近义词：delicious, delightful, succulent, tempting, appealing, voluptuous | 反义词：tasteless, bland, unappetizing, dry, austere
