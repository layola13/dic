galanga:/ɡəˈlæŋɡə/| n. |高良姜|A rhizome of plants of the genus Alpinia, used as a spice and in traditional medicine.  Galangal has a pungent, spicy, and slightly citrusy flavor, often described as a blend of ginger and cardamom. It is used in Southeast Asian cuisines and traditional medicine for various purposes.| Example: The fragrant galangal added a unique zest to the Thai curry.  高良姜为泰国咖喱增添了独特的风味。|近义词：ginger (to some extent), cardamom (shares some flavor notes)|反义词：无|名词
