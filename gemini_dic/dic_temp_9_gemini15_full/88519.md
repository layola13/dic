hit:/hɪt/| v., n.|击中；打击；成功；热门歌曲|To strike or impact something forcefully.|The ball hit the window. 球打中了窗户。 He hit the nail on the head. 他说中了要害。|近义词：strike, punch, impact, success|反义词：miss, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hits the ball hard. 他用力击球。
She hits the gym every morning. 她每天早上都去健身房。

一般过去时:
I hit my head on the door. 我撞到了门。
The car hit a tree. 车撞到了一棵树上。


现在完成时:
He has hit a home run. 他打出了全垒打。
She has hit a rough patch in her life. 她的人生经历了一段艰难时期。


过去完成时:
I had hit a wall in my research. 我的研究遇到了瓶颈。
They had hit several roadblocks before they reached their destination. 他们在到达目的地之前遇到了几个障碍。


将来时:
I will hit the books tonight. 我今晚要认真看书。
He will hit the road tomorrow. 他明天就要上路了。


虚拟语气:
If I had hit the jackpot, I would travel the world. 如果我中了彩票，我就会周游世界。
I wish I had hit the target. 我真希望我击中了目标。


被动语态:
The car was hit by a truck. 车被一辆卡车撞了。
The ball was hit far. 球被打得很远。


疑问句:
Did he hit the target? 他击中目标了吗？
What did he hit? 他撞到了什么？


否定句:
He didn't hit the ball. 他没有击中球。
She didn't hit the brakes in time. 她没有及时刹车。
