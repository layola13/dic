need-to-know:/niːd tə ˈnoʊ/| adj.|需要知道的，必须知道的|Essential information that must be known; crucial information.  This term often implies a limited scope of information, focusing only on what is absolutely necessary.|The need-to-know information was kept confidential.|近义词：essential, crucial, vital, important|反义词：unnecessary, irrelevant, trivial
