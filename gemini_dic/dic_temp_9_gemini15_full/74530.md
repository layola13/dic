fraternity:/frəˈtɜːrnəti/ | n. | 兄弟情谊；兄弟会；友爱；协会 | A fraternity is a feeling of friendship and loyalty towards other people, especially those who belong to the same organization or group.  It can also refer to a social organization for male students at a college or university. | Example:  The fraternity of soldiers fighting together forged strong bonds.  士兵们一起战斗的兄弟情谊 forged 牢固的纽带。

The fraternity at his university was known for its charitable work.  他大学里的兄弟会以慈善工作而闻名。


近义词： brotherhood, fellowship, camaraderie, association

反义词：estrangement, animosity, hostility

词源:

The word "fraternity" comes from the Late Latin word *fraternitas*, meaning "brotherhood," derived from the Latin word *frater*, meaning "brother."  The concept of brotherhood and mutual support has been central to the meaning of the word throughout its history.
