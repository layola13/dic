rheumatism:/ˈruːmətɪzəm/| n.|风湿病|A general term for various conditions causing pain and inflammation in the joints, muscles, and connective tissues.|例句：He suffers from rheumatism in his knees. 他膝盖患有风湿病。|近义词：arthritis, gout, fibrositis|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He suffers from rheumatism. = 他患有风湿病。
Rheumatism causes joint pain. = 风湿病引起关节疼痛。

一般过去时:
My grandmother had rheumatism. = 我祖母患有风湿病。
The doctor diagnosed her with rheumatism. = 医生诊断她患有风湿病。

现在完成时:
She has had rheumatism for years. = 她多年来一直患有风湿病。
