cyclostomous: /ˌsaɪkləˈstoʊməs/ | adj. | 圆口类的；无颌的 | Having a circular mouth; referring to the class Cyclostomata, which includes lampreys and hagfish.  These creatures are characterized by their lack of jaws. | Example:  Lampreys are cyclostomous fish.  =七鳃鳗是圆口纲的鱼类。  |近义词：jawless |反义词： gnathostomatous (having jaws)
