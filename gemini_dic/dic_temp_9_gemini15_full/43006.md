Cowardliness can manifest in various ways, from avoiding challenges to failing to stand up for one's beliefs. | Example: His cowardice in the face of danger was evident to everyone. 他在危险面前的懦弱显而易见。|近义词：fearfulness, timidity, pusillanimity |反义词：bravery, courage, boldness | 名词
