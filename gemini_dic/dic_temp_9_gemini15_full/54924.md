disposing: /dɪˈspoʊzɪŋ/ | v. |处理，处置；安排；去掉；使倾向于 |  The act of getting rid of something, typically waste or something unwanted; also, to arrange or settle something; or to incline someone towards a particular action or opinion. |

例句：

The city is disposing of its waste responsibly. = 这个城市正在负责任地处理其垃圾。

They are disposing of their old furniture. = 他们正在处理他们的旧家具。


近义词：getting rid of,  eliminating,  managing, arranging, inclining

反义词：acquiring, keeping, retaining, preserving


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disposes of his trash every week. = 他每周都处理他的垃圾。
She disposes of her old clothes to charity. = 她把旧衣服捐给了慈善机构。

一般过去时:
They disposed of the evidence quickly. = 他们迅速处理了证据。
He disposed of his unwanted belongings. = 他处理掉了不需要的物品。

现在完成时:
We have disposed of all the hazardous waste. = 我们已经处理掉了所有危险废物。
She has disposed of her old car. = 她已经处理掉了她的旧车。

过去完成时:
They had disposed of the body before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经处理掉了尸体。(注意：此例句涉及敏感内容，仅作语法示例)
The company had disposed of its toxic waste illegally. = 这家公司非法处理了其有毒废物。(注意：此例句涉及敏感内容，仅作语法示例)


将来时:
We will dispose of these materials properly. = 我们将妥善处理这些材料。
The factory will dispose of its wastewater according to regulations. = 工厂将按照规定处理其废水。

虚拟语气:
If I had more space, I would dispose of all my old books more easily. = 如果我有更多空间，我就能更容易地处理掉我所有的旧书。
If he disposed of the problem earlier, he wouldn't be in this situation now. = 如果他早点解决这个问题，他现在就不会处于这种境地了。

被动语态:

The old building is being disposed of by the city. = 这座旧建筑正在被城市处理。

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:

The word "disposing" comes from the Old French word "disposer," meaning "to arrange, order, or set in order."  This in turn derives from the Latin "disponere," which combines "dis-" (apart, away) and "ponere" (to place, put).  The sense of "getting rid of" developed later.
