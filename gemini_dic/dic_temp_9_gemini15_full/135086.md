nonobjection:/ˌnɒnəbˈdʒɛkʃən/| n. |无异议，不反对|The absence of objection; agreement or consent.  It implies a passive acceptance rather than an active endorsement.|例句：The proposal passed with nonobjection from the board. =  董事会对该提案没有异议，提案通过了。

例句：There was a general nonobjection to the new policy. = 对这项新政策，大家普遍没有异议。


近义词：acquiescence, assent, consent, approval

反义词：objection, disapproval, dissent, protest


词性: 名词
