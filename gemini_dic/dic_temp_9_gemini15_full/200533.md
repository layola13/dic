slapping:/ˈslæpɪŋ/ | v. | 掴，拍打 | The act of striking with an open hand.  It can be done playfully or aggressively. | 例句：He gave the child a playful slapping on the back. 他轻轻拍了拍孩子的背。


He received a slapping across the face for his rudeness. 他因为粗鲁而挨了一巴掌。| 近义词：patting, striking, cuffing | 反义词：caressing, stroking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is slapping the table angrily. = 他生气地拍着桌子。
She slaps her knee when she laughs. = 她笑的时候拍打着膝盖。

一般过去时:
The teacher slapped the naughty student. = 老师打了那个调皮的学生。
I slapped the mosquito on my arm. = 我拍死了我胳膊上的蚊子。

过去完成时:

She had slapped the mixture before baking it. = 在烘焙之前，她拍打过混合物。

将来时:
