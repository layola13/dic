近义词：affectedly, artificially, insincerely, mawkishly, sentimentally

反义词：authentically, genuinely, sincerely, naturally


词源:  Derived from "cheesy," meaning something of poor quality or lacking taste, often in a sentimental or overly familiar way.  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb, modifying the manner of an action or state.  The original sense of "cheesy" relates to the strong, sometimes overpowering flavor of cheese, extended metaphorically to describe something overly rich, cloying, or sentimental.
