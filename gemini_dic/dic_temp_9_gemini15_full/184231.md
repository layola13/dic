resolution's: /ˌrezəˈluːʃənz/ | n. |  决议；决心；分辨率；解决；清晰度 | The plural possessive form of "resolution."  It refers to the resolutions belonging to someone or something. |例句：The committee's resolutions were published yesterday.  委员会的决议昨天公布了。


The company's resolutions for the next year include expanding into new markets.  公司明年的决议包括拓展新市场。


近义词：determinations, decisions, findings, pronouncements


反义词：indecision, uncertainty, vagueness

词性：名词
