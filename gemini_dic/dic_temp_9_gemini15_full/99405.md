interdisciplinary: /ˌɪntərˈdɪsɪplɪneri/ | adj. | 跨学科的 | Relating to more than one branch of knowledge.  Interdisciplinary studies integrate multiple academic disciplines, drawing upon their perspectives and methodologies to address complex issues. |例句：The research project adopted an interdisciplinary approach, combining insights from sociology, psychology, and economics.  该研究项目采用跨学科方法，结合了社会学、心理学和经济学的见解。

例句：This interdisciplinary program allows students to explore connections between various fields of study.  这个跨学科项目允许学生探索各个学习领域之间的联系。


近义词：multidisciplinary, transdisciplinary


反义词：disciplinary, single-discipline


词源:  The word "interdisciplinary" is formed from the prefix "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among"), the word "discipline" (referring to a branch of knowledge or study), and the suffix "-ary" (indicating a relating to or characterized by).  Thus, it literally means "between disciplines" or "relating to multiple disciplines".
