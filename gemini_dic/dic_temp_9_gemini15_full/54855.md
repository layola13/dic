displayed:/dɪˈspleɪd/ | adj. | 陈列的；显示的；展示的 | Shown or presented to view; made visible.  |例句：The museum displayed a collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆展出了一批古代文物。|例句：The data is displayed on the screen. 数据显示在屏幕上。|近义词：shown, exhibited, presented, revealed |反义词：hidden, concealed, obscured |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The products are displayed in the shop window. = 产品陈列在商店橱窗里。
The information is displayed clearly on the website. = 信息在网站上清晰地显示。

一般过去时:
The artist displayed his paintings at the gallery. = 艺术家在画廊展出了他的画作。
The results were displayed on a large screen. = 结果在大屏幕上显示。

现在完成时:
The company has displayed its commitment to sustainability. = 公司已经展示了其对可持续发展的承诺。
The museum has displayed this exhibit for three months. = 博物馆已经展出这个展览三个月了。

过去完成时:
The evidence had been displayed before the jury. = 证据已呈现在陪审团面前。
The artist had displayed his works in many galleries. = 艺术家曾在许多画廊展出过他的作品。

将来时:
The company will display its new products at the trade show. = 公司将在贸易展上展示其新产品。
The results will be displayed after the experiment is complete. = 实验完成后将显示结果。

虚拟语气:
If the data were displayed differently, it would be easier to understand. = 如果数据以不同的方式显示，就更容易理解。
I wish the images were displayed more clearly. = 我希望图像显示更清晰。

被动语态:
The painting was displayed prominently in the hall. = 这幅画在大厅里醒目地展出。
The information is being displayed on the monitor. = 信息正在显示器上显示。

疑问句:
How are the products displayed in the store? = 商店的产品是如何陈列的？
Where is the information displayed? = 信息在哪里显示？

否定句:
The results were not displayed correctly. = 结果显示不正确。
The museum did not display any modern art. = 博物馆没有展出任何现代艺术。


PHRASE->
