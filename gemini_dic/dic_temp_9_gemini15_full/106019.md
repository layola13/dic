kne:/niː/| n.|膝盖|The kneecap is a small bone that protects the knee joint.|例句：He injured his kneecap during the game. 他在比赛中伤到了膝盖骨。|近义词：knee|反义词:无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My knee hurts. = 我的膝盖疼。
His knees are weak. = 他的膝盖无力。

一般过去时:
I fell and scraped my knee. = 我摔倒了，擦破了膝盖。
She knelt down to pray. = 她跪下祈祷。

现在完成时:
I have hurt my knee several times. = 我多次伤到过膝盖。
He has had knee problems for years. = 他多年来一直有膝盖问题。

过去完成时:
I had already injured my knee before the race. = 我在比赛前就已经伤到膝盖了。
She had knelt for so long that her knees were numb. = 她跪了太久，以至于膝盖麻木了。

将来时:
I will need knee surgery soon. = 我很快需要做膝盖手术。
He will be able to bend his knee after the recovery. = 康复后他就能弯曲膝盖了。
