priesthood:/ˈpriːsthʊd/| n. |神职；僧侣；牧师身份 |The state or position of being a priest; the body of priests in a religion.  It refers to the office, duties, and privileges of a priest, as well as the collective body of priests within a religious organization. |例句：He dedicated his life to the priesthood. 他将一生献给了神职。

例句：The priesthood played a significant role in medieval society.  僧侣在中世纪社会中扮演着重要的角色。


近义词：ministry, clergy, pastorate
反义词：layperson, laity
