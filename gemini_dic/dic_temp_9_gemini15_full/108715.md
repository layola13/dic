leaning-on: /ˈliːnɪŋ ɒn/ | v. |倚靠，依赖，依靠 | To rely on or depend on something or someone for support, either physically or emotionally. | 例句：The old woman was leaning on her cane.  这位老太太拄着拐杖。

例句：He's leaning on his family for financial support. 他依靠家人提供经济支持。


近义词：relying on, depending on, relying upon, counting on | 反义词：independent, self-reliant | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leans on his friends for advice. 他依靠朋友的建议。
She leans on her faith during difficult times.  她在困难时期依靠信仰。


一般过去时:
The tired hiker leaned on a tree to rest.  疲惫的徒步旅行者靠在一棵树上休息。
He leaned on his experience to solve the problem. 他依靠经验来解决这个问题。


现在完成时:
She has leaned on her family for support throughout her illness.  在她生病期间，她一直依靠家人支持。
They have leaned on each other through thick and thin.  他们风雨同舟，互相依靠。


过去完成时:
He had leaned on his parents for financial help before he got his job. 在找到工作之前，他一直依靠父母的经济帮助。
She had leaned on her beliefs to get through the challenging situation. 她依靠自己的信念度过了困境。


将来时:
I will lean on my team for help with this project. 我将依靠我的团队来完成这个项目。
They will lean on their knowledge to make the best decision.  他们将依靠自己的知识做出最佳决策。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would lean on my mentor for guidance. 如果我是你，我会向我的导师寻求指导。
I wish I had leaned on my friends sooner. 我真希望我早点依靠我的朋友们。


被动语态:  (Leaning-on is rarely used in the passive voice because it's an intransitive verb when referring to physical support and requires a prepositional phrase.)


疑问句:
Does he lean on anyone for emotional support? 他依靠任何人寻求情感支持吗？
On whom do you lean for help with your studies? 你依靠谁来帮助你学习？


否定句:
She doesn't lean on others for help; she's very independent. 她不依靠别人帮忙；她非常独立。
He didn't lean on his family for support; he handled it himself.  他没有依靠家人支持；他自己解决了。
