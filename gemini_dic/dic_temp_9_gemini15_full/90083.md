hostess:/ˈhoʊstəs/| n.|女主人，空姐，女招待|A woman who receives guests or attends to their comfort.  A woman employed to serve passengers on an aircraft.|例句：The hostess greeted us warmly at the door. 女主人热情地在门口迎接我们。

The flight hostess offered me a drink.  空姐给我递了一杯饮料。|近义词：host, stewardess, waitress|反义词：guest
