exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | n., v. | 开发；剥削；功绩；武勇事迹 | to make full use of a resource or situation, sometimes in a way that is unfair or unethical; a remarkable achievement | 近义词：develop, utilize, abuse, feat | 反义词：underutilize, neglect

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They exploit the natural resources of their country. = 他们开发利用本国的自然资源。
The company exploits its workers by paying them low wages. = 公司通过支付低工资来剥削工人。


一般过去时:
He exploited a loophole in the system to avoid paying taxes. = 他利用系统中的漏洞来逃避纳税。
The miners exploited the coal seam for many years. = 矿工们多年来一直开采煤层。


现在完成时:
She has exploited her talents to become a successful writer. = 她充分发挥了自己的才能，成为一名成功的作家。
They have exploited the situation to their advantage. = 他们利用了这种情况来为自己谋利。


过去完成时:
The company had exploited the workers before the new laws were enacted. = 在新法律颁布之前，该公司一直在剥削工人。
He had exploited the opportunity before anyone else realized its potential. = 在其他人意识到其潜力之前，他已经抓住了这个机会。


将来时:
They will exploit the new technology to improve their products. = 他们将利用新技术改进他们的产品。
We will exploit every opportunity to achieve our goals. = 我们将抓住每一个机会来实现我们的目标。


虚拟语气:
If they exploited the market more effectively, they would have been more successful. = 如果他们更有效地开发市场，他们会更成功。
I wish they hadn't exploited the workers so ruthlessly. = 我希望他们没有那么残酷地剥削工人。


被动语态:
The resources were exploited for profit. = 资源被用于盈利。
The workers were exploited by the factory owners. = 工人们被工厂主剥削。


疑问句:
How do they plan to exploit this new market? = 他们计划如何开发这个新市场？
Are they exploiting the workers unfairly? = 他们是否在不公平地剥削工人？


否定句:
They did not exploit the situation to their advantage. = 他们没有利用这种情况来为自己谋利。
The company does not exploit its employees. = 公司不会剥削其员工。


PHRASE->
exploit a loophole  利用漏洞
exploit a situation 利用形势
exploit resources 开发资源
exploit the market 开发市场
exploit someone's weakness 利用某人的弱点
