injected:/ɪnˈdʒektɪd/| v. |注射的；注入的|To force (a liquid, gas, or other substance) into something.  It implies a forceful introduction, often using a needle or syringe, but can also refer to the introduction of something abstract.|例句：The doctor injected the patient with the medicine.  医生给病人注射了药。

The scientist injected a new chemical into the solution. 科学家将一种新的化学物质注入溶液中。|近义词：inserted, introduced, infused |反义词：withdrawn, removed
