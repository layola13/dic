fiddled:/ˈfɪdld/| v.|摆弄；乱动；欺骗；拉小提琴|To move or handle something restlessly or nervously; to manipulate dishonestly; to play the fiddle.|例句：He fiddled with the buttons on his coat. 他不安地摆弄着外套上的纽扣。

He fiddled the accounts to hide his losses. 他篡改账目以掩盖损失。


近义词：play, tamper, cheat, manipulate|反义词：leave alone, be honest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fiddles with his pen during meetings. = 他在会议期间摆弄他的钢笔。
She fiddles with her hair when she's nervous. = 她紧张的时候会拨弄她的头发。

一般过去时:
He fiddled with the lock until it broke. = 他摆弄锁直到它坏了。
She fiddled the numbers to make the report look better. = 她篡改数字使报告看起来更好。

现在完成时:
He has fiddled with the computer all morning. = 他整个上午都在摆弄电脑。
She has fiddled away her savings on useless things. = 她把积蓄都花在无用的东西上了。

过去完成时:
He had fiddled with the evidence before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他已经篡改了证据。
She had fiddled the accounts for years before being caught. = 她多年来一直在做假账，直到被抓。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

If she hadn't fiddled the results, she would have been promoted. = 如果她没有篡改结果，她就会被提升。

否定句:
