non-taxonomic:/ˌnɑːnˌtæksəˈnɑːmɪk/| adj.|非分类学的；非系统分类的|Not related to or based on taxonomy (the science of classification of organisms).  It refers to classifications or systems that are not based on evolutionary relationships or shared characteristics used in biological classification.|The study used a non-taxonomic approach, grouping species based on habitat rather than evolutionary lineage.  这项研究采用了一种非分类学方法，根据栖息地而不是进化谱系对物种进行分组。|近义词：uncategorized, unclassified |反义词：taxonomic, systematic
