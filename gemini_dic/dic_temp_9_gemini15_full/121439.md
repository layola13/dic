misintending:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛndɪŋ/| v.|误解，曲解|To understand incorrectly; to misinterpret.|He was misintending my instructions, leading to the project's failure. 他误解了我的指示，导致项目失败。| misunderstanding, misconstruing | understanding, interpreting correctly |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misintends my meaning every time. = 他每次都误解我的意思。
They misintend the rules of the game. = 他们误解了游戏的规则。


一般过去时:
I misintended his intentions yesterday. = 我昨天误解了他的意图。
She misintended the map and got lost. = 她看错地图迷路了。


现在完成时:
I have misintended the instructions several times. = 我已经几次误解了说明。
They have misintended the purpose of the meeting. = 他们误解了会议的目的。


过去完成时:
He had misintended the message before he acted. = 他采取行动之前就已误解了信息。
We had misintended the significance of the event. = 我们之前误解了该事件的重要性。


将来时:
I will misintend his words if he doesn't speak clearly. = 如果他不说清楚，我会误解他的话。
They will misintend the plan if it's not explained properly. = 如果计划解释不清楚，他们会误解计划。


虚拟语气:
If I had not misintended his request, I would have helped him. = 如果我没有误解他的请求，我会帮他的。
I wish I hadn't misintended the signal. = 我真希望我没有误解信号。


被动语态:
His intentions were misintended by the audience. = 他的意图被观众误解了。
The instructions were misintended by the workers. = 说明书被工人们误解了。


疑问句:
Did he misintend my question? = 他是否误解了我的问题？
Were the instructions misintended? = 说明书是否被误解了？


否定句:
I did not misintend his remarks. = 我没有误解他的话。
They did not misintend the contract. = 他们没有误解合同。


PHRASE->
misintend completely = 完全误解
misintend deliberately = 故意曲解
misintend constantly = 总是误解
misintend seriously = 严重误解
misintend badly = 严重误解


词源:
mis- (错误地) + intend (意图)  The prefix "mis-" indicates a wrong or bad action, while "intend" means to have a plan or purpose.  Therefore, "misintend" signifies to have a wrong or incorrect understanding of something.
