denegation:/ˌdɛnɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |否认；否定；拒绝；贬低|The act of denying something; the state of being denied.  It can refer to a denial of a fact, a claim, a right, or even a feeling.  |例句：His denegation of the charges was unconvincing.  他的否认指控的说法令人难以信服。

例句：The court dismissed her denegation of responsibility. 法院驳回了她对责任的否认。


近义词：denial, refutation, repudiation, disavowal, rejection

反义词：affirmation, confirmation, acceptance, admission


词性:名词


词源:  From Latin *denegatio, from denegare "to deny," from de- "away, off" + negare "to deny."  The word's roots trace back to the Proto-Indo-European root *nek- "to say no."
