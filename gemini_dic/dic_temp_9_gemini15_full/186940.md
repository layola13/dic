riposting:/rɪˈpɒstɪŋ/| v.| 反驳；迅速回应；回击 | To reply quickly and sharply, especially in a witty or effective way.  |例句：He riposted with a clever remark that silenced his opponent. 他以一句巧妙的评论迅速反驳了对手。

例句：She riposted to his accusations with a well-reasoned defense. 她以合理的辩护来回应他的指控。


近义词：retorting, replying sharply, answering back, counterattacking

反义词：agreeing, conceding, yielding


词性:动词
