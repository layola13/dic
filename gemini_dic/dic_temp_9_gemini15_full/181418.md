reiterating:/ˌriˈɪtəreɪtɪŋ/| v.|重申；反复地说|Repeating something again and again, often for emphasis or clarification.  |例句：The teacher kept reiterating the importance of studying hard.老师不断重申努力学习的重要性。|例句：She reiterated her commitment to the project. 她重申了她对这个项目的承诺。|近义词：repeating, restating, reemphasizing|反义词：neglecting, ignoring

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reiterates his point every meeting. 他每次会议都重申他的观点。
They reiterate the company's policy to all new employees. 他们向所有新员工重申公司的政策。


一般过去时:
The spokesperson reiterated the government's position on the issue. 发言人重申了政府对这个问题的立场。
She reiterated her apologies for the misunderstanding. 她再次为误解道歉。


现在完成时:
He has reiterated his support for the candidate several times. 他已经多次重申了他对候选人的支持。
The company has reiterated its commitment to environmental sustainability. 公司已经重申了其对环境可持续性的承诺。


过去完成时:
After the accident, he reiterated what had happened. 事故发生后，他重申了发生的事情。
She reiterated what she had already explained to the police. 她重申了她已经向警方解释的事情。


将来时:
The president will reiterate his message during his upcoming speech. 总统将在即将发表的讲话中重申他的信息。
They will reiterate their demands until they are met. 他们将一再重复他们的要求，直到得到满足。


虚拟语气:
If he reiterated his concerns, they might listen. 如果他重申他的担忧，他们可能会听。
I wish he had reiterated his instructions more clearly. 我希望他更清楚地重申他的指示。


被动语态:
The statement was reiterated by the spokesperson. 该声明由发言人重申。
The company's policy has been reiterated many times. 公司的政策已经被多次重申。


疑问句:
Will he reiterate his position on the matter? 他会重申他对这件事的立场吗？
Has she reiterated her request for a raise? 她是否重申了她加薪的要求？


否定句:
He did not reiterate his objections. 他没有重申他的反对意见。
She didn't reiterate the instructions, and we made a mistake. 她没有重申指示，我们犯了一个错误。


PHRASE->
reiterating the importance of  重申……的重要性
reiterating his/her commitment 重申他的/她的承诺
reiterating the need for 重申对……的需求
reiterating the facts 重申事实
reiterating previous statements 重申之前的声明
