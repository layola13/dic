familiarisingly:/fəˈmɪliərizaɪŋli/ | adv. | 熟悉地，亲切地 | In a way that shows familiarity or friendliness.  The adverb implies a comfortable and informal level of interaction.  | He greeted her familiarisingly, as if they were old friends. 他熟悉地向她打招呼，好像他们是老朋友一样。 | affably, intimately, cordially | distantly, formally, coldly | 副词


USAGE->

This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses. The usage is always the same.

Example 1:
She smiled familiarisingly at the stranger. = 她对陌生人亲切地笑了笑。

Example 2:
He spoke familiarisingly to the child. = 他对孩子亲切地说话。


PHRASE->

There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "familiarisingly."  Its usage is generally standalone.  However, we can show phrases that convey similar meanings:

Phrase 1:
on familiar terms = 关系密切

Phrase 2:

Phrase 3:

Phrase 4:

Phrase 5:
