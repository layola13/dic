roam:/roʊm/| /rəʊm/ | v. |漫游，闲逛 |to travel around an area without any definite aim or direction|例句：We roamed the countryside for hours. 我们在乡下漫游了几个小时。

例句：The cattle roamed freely across the fields. 牛群自由自在地在田野里漫游。


近义词：wander, ramble, stray, rove | 反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He roams the streets every night. 他每晚都在街上闲逛。
They roam the forest in search of adventure. 他们在森林里寻找冒险。


一般过去时:
We roamed the beach yesterday afternoon. 昨天 下午我们在海滩上漫步。
She roamed the halls of the museum, captivated by the art. 她漫步在博物馆的大厅里，被艺术所吸引。


现在完成时:
The explorers have roamed across vast deserts. 探险家们已经穿越了广阔的沙漠。
He has roamed the world, seeking new experiences. 他周游世界，寻求新的体验。


过去完成时:
They had roamed the city for days before finding a suitable hotel. 他们在找到合适的酒店之前已经在这个城市里游荡了几天。
Before settling down, she had roamed many countries. 在定居之前，她游历了很多国家。


将来时:
We will roam the mountains next summer. 我们明年夏天将漫游山区。
They will roam the countryside, enjoying the fresh air. 他们将漫游乡村，享受新鲜的空气。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would roam the world. 如果我有时间，我会周游世界。
I wish I could roam the streets without fear. 我希望我能无惧地漫游街头。


被动语态: (roam本身很少用被动语态)
The countryside is rarely roamed by tourists in the winter. 冬天很少有游客在乡间漫游。(被动语态略显生硬)
The ancient ruins were once roamed by emperors. 古代遗址曾经是皇帝们漫游的地方。（此例更自然）


疑问句:
Do you often roam the streets at night? 你经常在晚上在街上闲逛吗？
Will they roam the forest again this year? 他们今年还会去森林里漫游吗？


否定句:
He does not roam the streets anymore. 他不再在街上闲逛了。
They didn't roam the countryside that day. 那天他们没有在乡下漫游。


PHRASE->
roam free 自由漫游
roam the streets 在街上闲逛
roam the countryside 在乡间漫游
roam the world 周游世界
roam at will 随意漫游

词源:
Old English *rūmian  "to make room, go about, wander"
