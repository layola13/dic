milkin:/ˈmɪlkɪn/| n.|挤奶，产奶|The act of milking a cow or other animal.  It can also refer to the process of extracting milk or a milk-like substance.|例句：The farmer was busy with the morning milkin.  这位农民忙着早晨挤奶。  The milkin yielded a surprisingly large quantity of milk.  挤奶获得了令人惊讶的大量牛奶。|近义词：milking, lactation|反义词：None.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The farmer milks the cows every morning. = 这位农民每天早晨都挤牛奶。
She milks the goat twice a day. = 她每天给山羊挤两次奶。


一般过去时:
He milked the cow yesterday. = 他昨天挤了牛奶。
They milked all the goats before sunset. = 他们在日落前挤完了所有山羊的奶。


现在完成时:
I have milked all the cows. = 我已经把所有的奶牛都挤过了。
She has milked the goat three times today. = 她今天已经给山羊挤了三次奶。


过去完成时:
They had milked all the animals before the storm broke. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把所有的动物都挤过了奶。
The farmer had milked the cow twice before he noticed it was ill. = 农民在注意到奶牛生病之前已经给它挤了两次奶。


将来时:
I will milk the cows tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上要挤牛奶。
We will milk the goats after breakfast. = 我们早饭后要挤山羊奶。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would milk the goat myself. = 如果我有更多时间，我会自己挤山羊奶。
I wish I could milk a cow without making a mess. = 我希望我能不弄脏就挤牛奶。


被动语态:
The cows are milked twice a day. = 奶牛每天挤两次奶。
The goat was milked before it was fed. = 山羊在喂食前被挤了奶。


疑问句:
Do you milk the cows yourself? = 你自己挤牛奶吗？
Have you ever milked a goat? = 你曾经挤过山羊奶吗？


否定句:
I didn't milk the cow today. = 我今天没有挤牛奶。
He doesn't milk the goats anymore. = 他不再挤山羊奶了。
