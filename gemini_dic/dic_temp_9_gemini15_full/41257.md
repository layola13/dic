corbie:/ˈkɔːrbi/| n. |渡鸦；乌鸦；（苏格兰）石雕像|A corbie is a type of large black bird, similar to a raven or crow.  In Scotland, it also refers to a grotesque carved stone figure often found on buildings. |例句：The corbie perched on the castle wall. 那只渡鸦栖息在城堡的墙上。

The ancient church was adorned with corbies. 那座古老的教堂装饰着石雕的渡鸦。|近义词：raven, crow|反义词：无
