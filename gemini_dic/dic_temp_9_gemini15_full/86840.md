hemelytrum:/ˈhɛməlɪtrəm/| n. |半鞘翅|The hardened forewing of some insects, particularly in the order Hemiptera (true bugs), that partially covers the hindwing.  It is often leathery or partially hardened, unlike the fully hardened elytra of beetles.|例句：The hemelytra of the assassin bug are partly hardened and partly membranous. 刺杀臭虫的半鞘翅部分坚硬，部分膜质。  The hemelytra protected the delicate hindwings from damage. 半鞘翅保护着娇嫩的后翅免受损伤。|近义词：forewing (in some contexts), tegmen|反义词：hindwing|名词
