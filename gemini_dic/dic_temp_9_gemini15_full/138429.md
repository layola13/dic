nourisher: /ˈnɜːrɪʃər/ | n. | 养育者；滋养物；营养品 | A person or thing that nourishes.  Someone or something that provides nourishment, support, or encouragement. |例句：The fertile soil acted as a nourisher for the plants. 肥沃的土壤是植物的养料。

例句：My mother was a great nourisher, always providing for my needs. 我的母亲是一位伟大的养育者，总是满足我的需求。

近义词：nutrient, sustainer, provider, fosterer

反义词：depleter, destroyer, starver
