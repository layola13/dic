conjugate:/ˈkɒndʒuɡeɪt/ /ˈkɑːndʒuɡeɪt/| vt.| 结合；使结合；(语法)活用；共轭|To combine or unite; in grammar, to inflect a verb through its various forms to show tense, person, number, mood, and voice.  |例句：The chemist conjugated the two chemicals to create a new compound. 化学家将两种化学物质结合起来制造了一种新的化合物。

例句：We need to conjugate the verb "to be" in all its tenses. 我们需要将动词“to be”的所有时态都活用。


词源：From Latin *coniugāre "to yoke together, join in marriage," from iungere "to join."


近义词：combine, unite, link, inflect


反义词：separate, disconnect, disjoin
