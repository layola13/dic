puritanism:/ˈpjʊərɪtənɪzəm/| n. |清教主义；清教徒的生活方式|The strict religious and moral beliefs and practices of the Puritans.  Puritanism emphasized a simple, austere lifestyle, hard work, and strict adherence to religious doctrines. It played a significant role in shaping early American society and culture. |The strict Puritanism of the community led to many social restrictions. 清教徒社区的严格清教主义导致了许多社会限制。  Her upbringing instilled in her a strong sense of Puritanism. 她的成长环境让她养成了强烈的清教主义思想。|piety, asceticism, rigorism |hedonism, laxity, permissiveness|名词
