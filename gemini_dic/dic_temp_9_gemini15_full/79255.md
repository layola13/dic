glassiest:/ˈɡlæsiɪst/| adj.| 最玻璃似的，最光滑的，最闪亮的 | Most like glass; extremely smooth and shiny.  The word intensifies the quality of being glassy. |例句：The glassiest surface reflected the sunset perfectly. 最光滑的表面完美地反射了日落。

The glassiest eyes I've ever seen stared back at me from the dark.  我见过的最闪亮的眼睛从黑暗中盯着我。|近义词：smoothest, shiniest, most polished |反义词：dullest, roughest, most matte
