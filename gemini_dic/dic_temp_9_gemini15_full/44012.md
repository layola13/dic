It implies unfair treatment based on factors like race, gender, religion, or origin.|Example:  The discriminatory hiring practices of the company led to a lawsuit. 这家公司的歧视性招聘行为导致了一场诉讼。|近义词：biased, prejudiced, unjust, unfair|反义词：fair, equitable, impartial, just
