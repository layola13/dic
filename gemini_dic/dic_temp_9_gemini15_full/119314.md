microbenthic:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈbɛnθɪk/| adj.| 微底栖的 | Relating to or denoting the organisms living in the bottom sediments of a body of water, especially those too small to be seen without magnification.  | 例句：Microbenthic communities play a crucial role in nutrient cycling. 微底栖群落对养分循环起着至关重要的作用。 |  例句：The study focused on the distribution of microbenthic invertebrates in the coastal waters. 这项研究集中在沿海水域微底栖无脊椎动物的分布。| 近义词：benthic (but less specific to microscopic organisms) | 反义词：pelagic (relating to the open ocean) |
