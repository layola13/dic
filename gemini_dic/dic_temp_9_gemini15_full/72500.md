fluorides:/ˈflɔːraɪdz/ | n. | 氟化物 | Compounds containing fluorine.  Fluorides are used in toothpaste to prevent tooth decay and in water treatment to reduce dental caries. | Example:  Many communities add fluorides to their drinking water to improve dental health.  许多社区在饮用水中添加氟化物以改善牙齿健康。|近义词：fluorine compounds |反义词：无
