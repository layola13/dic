miniaturizes:/ˌmɪniˈætʃəraɪzɪz/| v. |小型化；使微型化 |To make something smaller; to reduce the size of something significantly.|例句：The company miniaturizes electronic components for use in mobile phones.  这家公司将电子元件小型化，用于手机。


例句：Modern technology has miniaturizes many devices, making them portable and convenient. 现代科技使许多设备小型化，使它们便携且方便。


近义词：reduce, shrink, downsize|反义词：enlarge, magnify, amplify|词性:动词
