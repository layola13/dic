pediatrician:/ˌpiːdiəˈtrɪʃən/| n. |儿科医生|A pediatrician is a physician specializing in the care of infants, children, and adolescents.  They diagnose and treat illnesses and injuries in young patients, providing preventative care and managing chronic conditions.|例句：My daughter sees a pediatrician for her annual checkups. 我的女儿去看儿科医生进行年度体检。|例句：The pediatrician prescribed antibiotics for my son's ear infection. 儿科医生为我儿子的中耳炎开了抗生素。|近义词：child specialist, pediatric physician|反义词：geriatric physician

词源:

The word "pediatrician" is derived from the Greek words "pais" (παις), meaning "child," and "iatros" (ἰατρός), meaning "physician" or "healer."  The suffix "-ician" indicates someone who practices a particular profession or skill.  Therefore, "pediatrician" literally means "child physician."
