nonintersectional: /ˌnɒnɪntərˈsɛkʃənəl/ | adj. | 非交叉的；不考虑交叉性的 | Not considering or addressing intersecting social categorizations such as race, class, and gender.  It implies a failure to acknowledge the interconnected nature of various forms of oppression or discrimination. | Example:  The nonintersectional approach to feminist theory ignored the unique challenges faced by women of color.  非交叉的女性主义理论忽略了有色人种女性面临的独特挑战。| 近义词: single-axis, singular, unidimensional | 反义词: intersectional
