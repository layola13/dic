fortuity:/fɔːˈtʃuːəti/| n.|偶然性；意外事件；幸运|The unexpected good fortune that comes from an unforeseen event.  It often implies a fortunate outcome, but not always.  The word emphasizes the chance or accidental nature of the event.|例句：The fortuitous discovery of the lost manuscript made him famous.  偶然发现失落的稿件使他成名。

The fortuitous meeting led to a successful business partnership.  这次偶然的相遇促成了一次成功的商业合作。|近义词：chance, accident, coincidence, luck|反义词：certainty, inevitability, design, predetermination|名词
