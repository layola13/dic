questingly:/ˈkwɛstɪŋli/| adv.|探究地，疑问地|In a questioning or inquiring manner; seeking information or knowledge.|例句：He looked at me questingly, as if seeking an answer. 他探究地望着我，好像在寻找答案。例句：She spoke questingly, her voice full of uncertainty. 她疑问地说着话，声音里充满了不确定。|近义词：inquiringly, questioningly, interrogatively|反义词：confidently, assuredly
