retransport:/ˌriːˈtrænspɔːrt/| vt.|重新运输，再运输|To transport something again or in a different way.|The damaged goods had to be retransported to the factory for repair.  损坏的货物必须重新运回工厂修理。
The company retransported the shipment after discovering a defect. 公司在发现缺陷后重新运输了货物。|近义词：relocate, reship, convey again|反义词：unload, deliver (in the sense of completing the transportation)|动词

USAGE->

They retransport goods daily. = 他们每天都重新运输货物。
