petiolule:/ˈpɛtijuːl/| n. |小叶柄；小梗 |A small stalk, such as that supporting a leaflet of a compound leaf.  It is a smaller version of a petiole, the stalk that attaches a leaf blade to the stem.|The leaflets of the fern were attached to the rachis by short petiolules.  蕨类的叶片通过短小的叶柄连接到叶轴上。|近义词：leaflet stalk, secondary petiole |反义词：petiole (in the sense of being a larger structure)
