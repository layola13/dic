disqualification:/ˌdɪskwɑːlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |取消资格；取消参赛资格|The act of disqualifying someone or something; the state of being disqualified.  It implies the removal of someone's eligibility or right to participate in a competition, process, or activity. |例句：His disqualification from the race was due to a doping violation.  他的比赛资格被取消是因为服用兴奋剂违规。

例句：The judge's disqualification of the evidence significantly weakened the prosecution's case.  法官取消证据资格大大削弱了检方的案情。


近义词：debarment, ineligibility, disallowance


反义词：qualification, eligibility, acceptance


词性：名词
