/ʒɑ̃b/| n.|腿 (尤指人的腿)|Legs, especially human legs.  The word is primarily used in French.  It refers to the legs from the hips to the feet.|Example:  Elle a de belles jambes. = She has beautiful legs. |近义词:legs, limbs|反义词:None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms like "arms" or "torso".|名词


USAGE->
This word is not commonly used in English grammar exercises, as it is a French word.  The following examples demonstrate simple sentence structures using "jambes" in French.

一般现在时:
Ses jambes sont longues. = Her legs are long.
Il a des jambes fortes. = He has strong legs.


一般过去时:
Ses jambes étaient fatiguées. = Her legs were tired.
Il a couru jusqu'à ce que ses jambes lui fassent mal. = He ran until his legs hurt.


现在完成时:
Elle s'est blessée aux jambes. = She has injured her legs.
Ils ont marché pendant des heures et leurs jambes sont douloureuses. = They walked for hours and their legs are sore.


过去完成时:
Avant la course, ses jambes étaient déjà fatiguées. = Before the race, her legs were already tired.
Ils avaient couru si longtemps que leurs jambes tremblaient. = They had run so long that their legs were shaking.


将来时:
Ses jambes seront reposées après la course. = Her legs will be rested after the race.
Ils marcheront jusqu'à ce que leurs jambes leur fassent mal. = They will walk until their legs hurt.


虚拟语气:
Si seulement j'avais des jambes plus longues ! = If only I had longer legs!
Je voudrais que mes jambes soient plus fortes. = I wish my legs were stronger.


被动语态:
Ses jambes ont été blessées dans l'accident. = Her legs were injured in the accident.
Ses jambes sont massées régulièrement. = Her legs are massaged regularly.


疑问句:
Tes jambes sont-elles fatiguées ? = Are your legs tired?
Ses jambes sont-elles assez fortes pour la randonnée ? = Are her legs strong enough for the hike?


否定句:
Ses jambes ne sont pas assez longues. = Her legs are not long enough.
Il n'a pas blessé ses jambes. = He didn't injure his legs.



PHRASE->
There are no common English phrases using "jambes."  However, in French, you might see phrases like:

avoir les jambes lourdes = to have heavy legs
