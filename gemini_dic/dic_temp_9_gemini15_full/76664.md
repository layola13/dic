galvanising: /ˈɡæl vəˌnaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |镀锌；激励；使兴奋 | The process of coating iron or steel with zinc to protect it from rust.  It also means to excite or inspire someone. |例句：The factory is galvanising steel sheets for export. 工厂正在镀锌钢板用于出口。The speaker's words galvanised the crowd into action. 演讲者的言语激励了人群采取行动。|近义词：zincing, stimulating, exciting, inspiring |反义词：depressing, discouraging, disheartening
