pallet: /ˈpælɪt/ | n. | 托盘；调色板；（尤指艺术家的）调色板；（感知的）色板 |

英文解析:A pallet is a flat structure, typically made of wood or plastic, used for transporting or storing goods.  It can also refer to a board with a range of colors used by an artist to mix paints, or more broadly, to the range of colors used in a particular work or context.

例句:
The workers loaded the pallets onto the forklift.  工人们把托盘装上叉车。

The artist arranged his paints on his pallet. 艺术家把颜料摆在他的调色板上。


近义词:tray, platform, palette (as a color range)

反义词:N/A (No direct antonym)


词源:  The word "pallet" ultimately derives from the Old French word "palette," meaning a small shovel or spade. This evolved to refer to a flat surface for mixing paints, and then expanded to include its current meaning as a platform for goods.
