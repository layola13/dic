meditating:/ˈmɛdɪteɪtɪŋ/| v.|沉思；冥想|The act of meditating involves focusing one's mind on a particular object, thought, or activity to achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm state.  It's often practiced for stress reduction, self-awareness, and spiritual growth.|例句：She was meditating quietly in the garden. 她安静地坐在花园里冥想。

例句：He meditates daily to improve his focus. 他每天冥想以提高专注力。


近义词：contemplating, reflecting, pondering, contemplating, daydreaming|反义词：acting, rushing, distracting

词源:
The word "meditating" comes from the Latin word "meditari," meaning "to think, consider, or plan."  It entered English through Old French.  The modern sense of "meditating" as a spiritual practice developed later.
