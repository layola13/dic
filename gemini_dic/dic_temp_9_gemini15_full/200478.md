slamming:/ˈslæmɪŋ/| v.|猛击；砰地关上；猛烈批评|To forcefully throw or shut something; to criticize harshly.|例句：He slammed the door in anger. 他生气地砰地关上了门。|例句：The critics slammed the new movie. 批评家们猛烈批评了这部新电影。|近义词：banging, crashing, slamming down,  condemning, denouncing|反义词：gently closing, praising, commending|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slams the door every time he leaves. = 他每次离开都砰地关上门。
She slams her fist on the table. = 她重重地把拳头砸在桌子上。


一般过去时:
He slammed the book shut. = 他砰的一声把书合上了。
They slammed the car door. = 他们砰地一声关上了车门。


现在完成时:
He has slammed the door several times today. = 他今天已经砰地关了好几次门了。
She has slammed the government's policies. = 她猛烈抨击了政府的政策。


过去完成时:
He had slammed the door before I could say anything. = 我还没来得及说什么，他就已经砰地关上门了。
They had slammed the brakes before the accident. = 事故发生前他们已经猛踩了刹车。


将来时:
He will slam the door again if you annoy him. = 如果你惹恼了他，他还会砰地关上门。
She will slam the report if she finds mistakes. = 如果她发现错误，她会猛烈批评这份报告。


虚拟语气:
If he slammed the door harder, it would break. = 如果他更用力地关门，门就会坏。
I wish he hadn't slammed the door so loudly. = 我真希望他不要把门关得那么大声。


被动语态:
The door was slammed shut. = 门被砰地关上了。
The new policy was slammed by the opposition. = 新政策遭到反对派的猛烈抨击。


疑问句:
Did he slam the door? = 他是不是砰地关上了门？
Why did she slam the phone down? = 她为什么把电话摔了？


否定句:
He didn't slam the door; he closed it gently. = 他没有砰地关上门；他是轻轻地关上的。
She didn't slam the proposal; she offered constructive criticism. = 她没有猛烈批评这个提议；她提出了建设性的批评。
