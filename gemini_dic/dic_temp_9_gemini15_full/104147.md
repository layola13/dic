Juneteenth:/ˈdʒuːnˈtiːnθ/| n. |六月节|Juneteenth is an annual American holiday that commemorates the emancipation of enslaved African Americans in the United States.  It is observed on June 19th. The holiday marks the day in 1865 when Union General Gordon Granger arrived in Galveston, Texas, with news that the Civil War was over and that the enslaved people were free, more than two years after President Abraham Lincoln issued the Emancipation Proclamation.|例句：Juneteenth celebrations often involve community gatherings, parades, and educational events.|近义词：Emancipation Day, Freedom Day|反义词：Slavery Day (no such holiday exists)
