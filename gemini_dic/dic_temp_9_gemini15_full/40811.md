convolutional: /ˌkɒnvəˈluːʃənəl/ | /ˌkɑːnvəˈluːʃənəl/ | adj. | 卷积的；回旋的 | Relating to or involving convolution, a mathematical operation.  In computer science, especially in image processing and deep learning, it refers to a type of neural network layer that uses convolution to extract features from input data.  | Example: Convolutional neural networks (CNNs) are widely used in image recognition.  卷积神经网络（CNN）广泛应用于图像识别。 |近义词: convolute (v.), folded (adj.) | 反义词: unfolded (adj.), straight (adj.)
