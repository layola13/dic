shout: /ʃaʊt/ | vt. & vi. | 喊叫；大声说 | To utter or cry out loudly and forcefully.  |例句：He shouted at the top of his lungs. 他大声喊叫。|例句：She shouted instructions to her team. 她大声向她的团队下达指示。|近义词：yell, scream, cry out |反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shouts at the TV. = 他对着电视喊叫。
They shout instructions to the workers. = 他们大声向工人喊叫指令。

一般过去时:
She shouted with excitement. = 她兴奋地大喊。
He shouted at the thief. = 他朝小偷大喊。

现在完成时:
I have shouted myself hoarse. = 我喊得嗓子都哑了。
They have shouted for help several times. = 他们已经多次呼救。

过去完成时:
He had shouted warnings before the accident. = 事故发生前他已经发出警告。
She had shouted so much that her throat was sore. = 她喊叫太多，嗓子都痛了。

将来时:
I will shout if I see something dangerous. = 如果我看到危险的事情，我会喊叫。
They will shout their support for the team. = 他们将大声表达对球队的支持。

虚拟语气:
If I shouted, would anyone hear me? = 如果我喊叫，有人会听到吗？
I wish I hadn't shouted at him. = 我真希望我没有对他大喊大叫。

被动语态:
The message was shouted across the field. = 消息在田野里被大声喊了出来。
His name was shouted from the rooftops. = 他的名字被大声喊了出来。

疑问句:
Did she shout at you? = 她对你大喊了吗？
Why did he shout? = 他为什么大喊？

否定句:
He didn't shout, he whispered. = 他没有喊叫，而是低声细语。
She didn't shout at the children. = 她没有对孩子们大喊大叫。


PHRASE->
shout at = 对…大声喊叫
shout down = 斥责；压制
shout out = 大声喊出；高声宣布
shout for joy = 欢呼雀跃
let out a shout = 发出一阵喊叫
