nonreceptivity:/ˌnɒnˌrɛsɛpˈtɪvɪti/| n. |不易接受；拒不接受；无反应性 |The lack of willingness or ability to receive or accept something, such as information, ideas, or emotions.  This can manifest as indifference, resistance, or a closed-off attitude. |例句：His nonreceptivity to new ideas hampered the team's progress.  他的拒不接受新想法阻碍了团队的进步。

例句：The patient's nonreceptivity to treatment was a major concern for the doctors. 病人的拒不接受治疗让医生非常担忧。


近义词：unresponsiveness, resistance, insensitivity, imperviousness, recalcitrance


反义词：receptivity, openness, acceptance, responsiveness


词性:名词
