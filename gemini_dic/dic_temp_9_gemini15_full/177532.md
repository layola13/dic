rebook:/ˌriːˈbʊk/| vt.|重新预订；再订购|To book or order something again.  This usually implies a previous booking or order that has been cancelled, changed, or requires renewal. |例句：I had to rebook my flight after the airline cancelled my original reservation. 我不得不重新预订我的航班，因为航空公司取消了我原来的预订。  I need to rebook my doctor's appointment. 我需要重新预约我的医生。|近义词：re-order, reschedule, re-engage|反义词：cancel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rebooks his flight every year. = 他每年都重新预订他的航班。
She rebooks her favourite restaurant every weekend. = 她每个周末都重新预订她最喜欢的餐馆。

一般过去时:
They rebooked their hotel room after finding a better deal. = 他们在找到更好的交易后重新预订了他们的酒店房间。
He rebooked the tickets because the original ones were lost. = 他重新预订了票，因为原来的票丢了。

现在完成时:
I have rebooked my train ticket three times this month. = 这个月我已经三次重新预订了我的火车票。
She has rebooked her appointment for next week. = 她已经将她的预约改到了下周。

过去完成时:
They had rebooked their holiday before the storm warning was issued. = 暴风雨预警发布之前，他们已经重新预订了他们的假期。
We had rebooked the venue after the original one was unavailable. = 在原来的场地不可用之后，我们已经重新预订了场地。

将来时:
I will rebook my appointment if the doctor is unavailable. = 如果医生没空，我会重新预约。
They will rebook the tickets online. = 他们将在网上重新预订门票。

虚拟语气:
If I had known the flight was cancelled, I would have rebooked it earlier. = 如果我知道航班取消了，我会早点重新预订。
I wish I had rebooked my hotel room sooner; the prices are now higher. = 我真希望我早点重新预订酒店房间；现在价格更高了。

被动语态:
The flight was rebooked for the following day. = 航班重新预订到了第二天。
The tickets were rebooked after a complaint was filed. = 在投诉提出后，门票被重新预订了。

疑问句:
Have you rebooked your appointment yet? = 你已经重新预订你的预约了吗？
Will they rebook the tickets before the deadline? = 他们会在截止日期前重新预订门票吗？

否定句:
I didn't rebook my ticket because the airline offered a refund. = 我没有重新预订我的票，因为航空公司提供了退款。
She didn't rebook the hotel room because she changed her travel plans. = 她没有重新预订酒店房间，因为她改变了旅行计划。
