desulphuration:/ˌdiːsʌlfəˈreɪʃən/| n. |脱硫；除硫|The removal of sulfur from a substance, such as coal or petroleum.  This process is crucial in reducing air pollution and improving the quality of fuels. |例句：The desulphuration of flue gases is essential for environmental protection.  脱硫烟气对于环境保护至关重要。|例句：The refinery uses a sophisticated process for desulphuration of crude oil. 这家炼油厂采用复杂工艺对原油进行脱硫。|近义词：sulfur removal, desulfurization|反义词：sulfurization, sulfuration|名词
