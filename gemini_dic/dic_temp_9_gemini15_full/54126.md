disenclose:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkloʊz/| vt.|使…不再封闭；揭露；泄露|To remove from an enclosure; to reveal or disclose something previously concealed.  |例句：The company disenclosed its new product strategy to its investors. 公司向投资者披露了其新的产品战略。|例句：The journalist disenclosed the politician's secret affair.  记者揭露了这位政治家的秘密恋情。|近义词：unseal, reveal, disclose, unveil|反义词：enclose, conceal, hide|动词
