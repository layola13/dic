intendeds:/ɪnˈtɛndɪdz/| n.|意图，打算|The intendeds are getting married next month.  (意图是下个月结婚。)|近义词：plans, purposes, aims|反义词：unintendeds


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company intends to expand its business. = 公司打算扩大业务。
He intends to travel to Europe next year. = 他打算明年去欧洲旅行。


一般过去时:
She intended to go to the party, but she was sick. = 她本来打算去参加聚会，但她病了。
They intended to finish the project by Friday, but they didn't. = 他们本来打算在周五完成项目，但他们没有。


现在完成时:
I have intended to call her, but I haven't had the time. = 我一直想给她打电话，但我一直没时间。
They have intended to move to a new house for a long time. = 他们很久以前就打算搬到新房子里了。


过去完成时:
He had intended to propose, but she left him. = 他本来打算求婚，但她离开了他。
We had intended to go to the beach, but it rained. = 我们本来打算去海滩，但是下雨了。


将来时:
She will intend to buy a new car next year. = 她打算明年买一辆新车。(This is less common; future intent is usually expressed without "will intend")
They will probably intend to finish the work soon. = 他们可能会很快打算完成这项工作。(This is less common;  future intent is usually expressed without "will intend")


虚拟语气:
If I had intended to hurt you, I would have apologized. = 如果我本来打算伤害你，我会道歉的。
I wish I had intended to study harder for the exam. = 我真希望我本来打算为考试更努力地学习。


被动语态:
The meeting was intended to discuss the budget. = 会议的目的是讨论预算。
The project is intended to be completed by the end of the year. = 项目计划在年底前完成。


疑问句:
What are their intendeds? = 他们的意图是什么？
What was intended by the author? = 作者的意图是什么？


否定句:
He did not intend to cause any trouble. = 他无意造成任何麻烦。
The plan was not intended to last for long. = 这个计划并非打算持续很久。


PHRASE->
intended meaning = 本意
intended audience = 目标受众
intended use = 预期用途
intended effect = 预期效果
intended outcome = 预期结果
