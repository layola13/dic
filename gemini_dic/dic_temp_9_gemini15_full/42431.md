counterculturist:/ˈkaʊntərkʌltʃərɪst/ | n. | 反文化主义者 | A person who actively participates in or advocates for a counterculture.  Someone who rejects mainstream values and norms and embraces alternative lifestyles and beliefs. | Example:  Many young people in the 1960s became counterculturists, rejecting the established social order.  许多20世纪60年代的年轻人成为反文化主义者，拒绝既定的社会秩序。 |近义词：nonconformist, rebel, bohemian | 反义词：conformist, traditionalist | 名词
