carbonizer:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzər/| n. |碳化器；使…碳化的东西|A carbonizer is a device or substance that converts something into carbon or a carbon-containing substance.  It can refer to equipment used in industrial processes or a material that facilitates carbonization. |The high-temperature furnace acted as a carbonizer, turning the wood into charcoal.  高温炉充当碳化器，将木材变成木炭。  The chemical compound served as a powerful carbonizer in the experiment.  该化合物在实验中用作强效碳化剂。|近义词：carbonizing agent, charring agent |反义词：decarbonizer (depending on context)
