deliberated:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪd/| adj. v.|仔细考虑过的；深思熟虑的；经过仔细考虑的|To think about or discuss something carefully, especially before making a decision.  It implies a conscious and thorough process of weighing options.|The committee deliberated for hours before reaching a decision. 委员会经过数小时的仔细考虑后才做出决定。|They deliberated the pros and cons of the proposal before voting. 他们在投票前仔细权衡了提案的利弊。|considered, pondered, contemplated|hasty, impulsive, rash|v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They deliberate on the matter every week. = 他们每周都仔细考虑这件事。
The council deliberates on the budget. = 理事会正在审议预算。


一般过去时:
The jury deliberated for several days. = 陪审团商议了好几天。
We deliberated long and hard before making a choice. = 我们经过长时间的认真考虑后才做出选择。


现在完成时:

They have deliberated over the plan for months. = 他们已经考虑这个计划好几个月了。


过去完成时:
They had deliberated for hours before they announced their verdict. = 他们在宣布判决前已经商议了数小时。
After they had deliberated, the judges reached a consensus. = 经过商议之后，法官们达成了一致意见。


将来时:

We will deliberate carefully before making a final decision. = 我们将在做出最终决定之前仔细考虑。


虚拟语气:
If they had deliberated more carefully, they might have made a better decision. = 如果他们当时考虑得更仔细一些，他们可能会做出更好的决定。
I wish I had deliberated more before accepting the job. = 我希望在接受这份工作之前我考虑得更多一些。


被动语态:

The proposal was deliberated upon for weeks. = 这个提案被讨论了好几个星期。


疑问句:
