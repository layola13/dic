raininess:/ˈreɪnɪnəs/| n. |雨量；多雨；潮湿 |The degree or frequency of rainfall.  It refers to the amount of rain that falls in a particular area over a given period.  |例句：The raininess of the region makes it unsuitable for growing certain crops.  这个地区雨量很大，不适合种植某些作物。

The high raininess this year led to widespread flooding.  今年雨水过多导致了大范围的洪涝灾害。

|近义词：precipitation, wetness, dampness |反义词：dryness, aridity |

词源:
raininess derives from the noun "rain"  + the suffix "-iness", which is used to form nouns meaning "the state or quality of being..."  Therefore, raininess signifies "the state or quality of being rainy".  The word ultimately traces back to Proto-Germanic *regną, meaning "rain".
