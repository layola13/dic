redress:/rɪˈdrɛs/| n. vt.|赔偿；纠正；补偿；修补|To correct or compensate for a wrong or injustice; to remedy a fault or defect;  to set right.|例句：The company offered redress to the customers affected by the faulty product.  该公司为受有缺陷产品影响的客户提供了赔偿。|例句：The government promised to redress the grievances of the people. 政府承诺纠正人民的冤屈。|近义词：remedy, reparation, compensation, rectification|反义词：injustice, wrong, harm, damage|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redresses his wrongs. = 他纠正他的错误。
She redresses her clients' grievances. = 她为她的客户的冤屈提供补偿。

一般过去时:
They redressed the balance of power. = 他们恢复了力量的平衡。
The court ordered the company to redress the damage. = 法院命令该公司赔偿损失。

现在完成时:
The company has redressed the situation. = 公司已经纠正了这种情况。
He has redressed many injustices in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中纠正了许多不公正行为。

过去完成时:
They had redressed the issue before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经解决了这个问题。
She had redressed the imbalance in the budget. = 她已经纠正了预算中的不平衡。

将来时:
The government will redress the situation. = 政府将纠正这种情况。
He will redress the grievances of the affected parties. = 他将为受影响的各方提供补偿。

虚拟语气:
If they redressed the situation, everything would be fine. = 如果他们纠正了这种情况，一切都会好起来的。
I wish the company would redress my complaint. = 我希望公司能处理我的投诉。

被动语态:
The situation was redressed quickly. = 情况很快得到了纠正。
The wrongs will be redressed in due course. = 不公正行为将在适当的时候得到纠正。

疑问句:
Will the company redress the customer's complaint? = 公司会处理客户的投诉吗？
How will the government redress the situation? = 政府将如何纠正这种情况？

否定句:
The company did not redress the customer's complaint. = 公司没有处理客户的投诉。
The government failed to redress the imbalance. = 政府未能纠正不平衡。
