self-scanned:/ˈsɛlfˌskænd/| adj.| 自扫描的，自行扫描的 |  Describes something that has been scanned using a self-service scanner, typically found in supermarkets or libraries.  It implies the process was initiated and completed by the user, without the assistance of a staff member. | Example:  The self-scanned receipt showed a total of $25.  自行扫描的收据显示总计25美元。| 近义词：user-scanned, self-checked | 反义词：staff-scanned, manually-scanned
