protoplasms:/ˈproʊtəˌplæzəm/| n.|原生质；细胞质|The living substance of a cell, including the cytoplasm, nucleus, and other organelles.  It is the fundamental unit of life.|例句：The protoplasm of the cell is responsible for its metabolism and growth.  细胞的原生质负责其新陈代谢和生长。|例句：Damage to the protoplasm can lead to cell death. 原生质损伤会导致细胞死亡。|近义词：cytoplasm, cell substance|反义词：无
