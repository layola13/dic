/kwiː/|Pronoun|什么，哪一个|The word "what" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something.|What is your name?|which, which one|nothing, none|Pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

What do you think of this idea? = 你觉得这个主意怎么样？


一般过去时:
What did you eat for lunch? = 你午餐吃了什么？

What happened at the party? = 晚会上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？

What has she accomplished in her career? = 她在事业上取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？

What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do this weekend? = 你这个周末打算做什么？

What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天的天气将会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？

I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？

What is being built in that area? = 那个地区正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？

What is your address? = 你的地址是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。

He didn't say what he meant. = 他没有说出他的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/你好吗？
What if...? = 如果……将会怎样？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？
No matter what = 无论如何
What about...? = ……怎么样？
