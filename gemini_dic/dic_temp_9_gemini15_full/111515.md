loaning:/ˈloʊnɪŋ/| v. |借出；贷款；给予|The act of lending something, especially money.  It can also refer to the process of providing something temporarily.|例句：The bank is loaning money to small businesses.  这家银行正在向小型企业贷款。|例句：He's loaning me his car for the weekend. 他这周末把他的车借给我。|近义词：lending, advancing, providing|反义词：borrowing, receiving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank is loaning money to farmers. = 银行正在向农民贷款。
They are loaning books to students. = 他们正在向学生借书。


一般过去时:
The company loaned me the equipment. = 公司把设备借给了我。
She loaned him her bicycle. = 她把她的自行车借给了他。


现在完成时:
We have loaned several computers to the school. = 我们已经向学校借出几台电脑了。
The library has loaned out all its copies of that book. = 图书馆已经把那本书的所有副本都借出去了。


过去完成时:
They had loaned him money before, so he was not surprised. = 他们以前借给他钱，所以他不惊讶。
The museum had loaned the painting to another gallery for the exhibition. = 博物馆已经把这幅画借给另一个画廊展出。


将来时:
We will be loaning our car to John next week. = 下周我们将把车借给约翰。
The government will loan more money to developing countries. = 政府将向发展中国家贷款更多资金。


虚拟语气:
If the bank were loaning more money, we could expand our business. = 如果银行贷款更多，我们就能扩大我们的业务。
I wish they were loaning me the money I need. = 我希望他们能借给我我需要的钱。


被动语态:
Money was loaned to the students by the government. = 政府向学生贷款。
The equipment is being loaned to several construction companies. = 设备正在被借给几家建筑公司。


疑问句:
Is the bank loaning money to anyone at this time? = 银行现在向任何人贷款吗？
Are they loaning out the equipment now? = 他们现在正在把设备借出去吗？


否定句:
The bank is not loaning any more money this month. = 银行这个月不再贷款了。
They weren't loaning cars without insurance. = 他们没有向没有保险的人贷款。
