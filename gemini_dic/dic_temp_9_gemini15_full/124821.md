muck:/mʌk/| n. v.|泥土，污物；乱七八糟的东西；弄脏；乱搞|

英文解析:
Noun:  Refers to wet mud, filth, or dung. It can also refer to something messy, unpleasant, or of poor quality.

Verb: To clean out or remove muck; to make a mess of something.


例句:
The pigs were wallowing in the muck. = 猪在泥里打滚。

The room was a complete muck. = 房间里乱七八糟的。


近义词:
dirt, mire, filth, grime, mess, rubbish


反义词:
cleanliness, purity, order


词性:
Noun, Verb
