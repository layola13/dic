precaptivity:/priːkæpˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 囚禁前；被俘前 | The period of time before someone is captured or imprisoned;  the state of being free before captivity.  It often refers to a time of freedom and normalcy before a significant change in one's life, often involving confinement or loss of liberty. | Example:  Her precaptivity days were filled with carefree adventures.  她被囚禁前的日子充满了无忧无虑的冒险。  |近义词：pre-imprisonment, pre-confinement | 反义词：captivity, imprisonment
