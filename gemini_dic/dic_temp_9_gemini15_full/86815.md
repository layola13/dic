hemato: /ˈhɛmətō/ | combining form | 血的；血液的 | Relating to or derived from blood.  | The combining form "hemato" is used to create compound words related to blood.  For example, hematology is the study of blood. |  Hematology is the study of blood and blood-forming tissues. |  近义词: sanguin(ous) | 反义词:  None (combining form doesn't have direct antonyms) | combining form


USAGE->
This is a combining form, not a standalone word, so it doesn't have verb conjugations or tenses.  It's used as a prefix in medical terminology.

Examples of usage in compound words:

hematoma:  /ˌhiːməˈtoʊmə/ A localized collection of blood outside the blood vessels.  血肿
A blow to the head can cause a hematoma.  头部撞击会导致血肿。

hematocrit: /hɪˈmætəkrɪt/ The proportion of blood volume that is occupied by red blood cells. 血细胞比容
The hematocrit test measures the percentage of red blood cells in your blood. 血细胞比容测试测量血液中红细胞的百分比。

hematopoiesis: /ˌhɛmætəpɔɪˈiːsɪs/ The formation of blood cells in the living body.造血作用
Hematopoiesis occurs primarily in the bone marrow. 造血作用主要发生在骨髓中。

hemoglobin: /ˌhiːməˈɡloʊbɪn/ A protein in red blood cells that carries oxygen. 血红蛋白
Hemoglobin levels can indicate anemia. 血红蛋白水平可以指示贫血。

hemorrhage: /ˈhɛmərɪdʒ/ An escape of blood from a ruptured blood vessel, especially when profuse. 出血
The patient experienced a severe hemorrhage after the accident. 事故后病人发生了严重的出血。


PHRASE->
There are no phrases directly using "hemato" as it's a combining form.  Phrases are formed with words containing "hemato". For example:

internal bleeding: 内出血 (related to hemorrhage)
blood transfusion:输血 (related to blood)
bone marrow transplant: 骨髓移植 (related to hematopoiesis)
low red blood cell count: 红细胞数量低 (related to hematocrit)
blood clot: 血凝块 (related to hematoma)
