infrareds:/ˈɪnfrəˌredz/ | n. |红外线 (复数)| Infrareds are invisible electromagnetic radiation with wavelengths longer than those of visible light.  Infrared radiation is felt as heat. |Example:  Infrareds are used in night vision goggles. 红外线被用于夜视镜。 The remote control uses infrareds to communicate with the television. 遥控器使用红外线与电视机通信。|近义词：infrared radiation, heat rays|反义词：ultraviolet rays
