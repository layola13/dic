half-colored:/ˈhæfˌkʌləd/| adj.|混血的；肤色不均匀的|A term used historically to describe people of mixed racial heritage, particularly those of African and European descent.  It is now considered outdated and offensive due to its association with racist ideologies and the inherent implication of inferiority. The term lacks precision and fails to account for the diversity of mixed-race identities. |例句：The term "half-colored" was once used to categorize individuals of mixed race, but it's now deemed offensive. = “混血”一词曾被用来对混血人士进行分类，但现在已被认为是冒犯性的。The painting displayed a half-colored effect, with patches of light and dark hues. = 这幅画呈现出一种半色调的效果，带有深浅不一的色块。|近义词：mixed-race, biracial, multiracial|反义词：purebred, single-race|
