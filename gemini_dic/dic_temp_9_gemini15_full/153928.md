pere:/ˈpɛər/| n.|父亲；祖先|The word "pere" is a French word meaning "father" or "ancestor". It is often used in genealogical contexts or in formal settings.  |例句：In French, "pere" means father. 法语中，“pere”的意思是父亲。|近义词：father, sire, ancestor, progenitor|反义词：child, offspring, descendant
