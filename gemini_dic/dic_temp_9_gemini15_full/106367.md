kombat:/ˈkɒmbæt/ | n. |格斗；战斗 |A fight or battle, especially one involving hand-to-hand combat.  |例句：The two warriors engaged in fierce kombat.  这两个战士进行了激烈的格斗。|例句：The army prepared for kombat.  军队准备战斗。|近义词：battle, fight, struggle, conflict |反义词：peace, harmony


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soldiers combat the enemy. = 士兵们与敌人作战。
We combat prejudice and discrimination. = 我们打击偏见和歧视。


一般过去时:
The knights combated bravely. =  骑士们英勇作战。
They combated the fire with water. = 他们用水扑灭了大火。


现在完成时:
The team has combated many challenges. =  这个团队已经克服了许多挑战。
She has combated illness for years. = 她多年来一直在与疾病作斗争。


过去完成时:
They had combated for hours before reinforcements arrived. = 增援到达之前，他们已经战斗了好几个小时。
He had combated his fear before stepping onto the stage. = 他上台之前已经克服了自己的恐惧。


将来时:
We will combat climate change. = 我们将对抗气候变化。
The army will combat the rebellion. = 军队将平息叛乱。


虚拟语气:

I wish we had combated the spread of misinformation. = 我希望我们能够阻止虚假信息的传播。


被动语态:
