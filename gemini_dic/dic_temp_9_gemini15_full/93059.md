idoling:/ˈaɪdəlɪŋ/| v.|空转，怠速运转|The engine was idling smoothly.|引擎平稳地空转。|近义词：coasting, running idle|反义词：working, functioning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car engine idles quietly. = 汽车引擎静静地空转。
The motor idles at 800 RPM. = 电机以每分钟800转的速度空转。

一般过去时:
The plane idled on the runway. = 飞机在跑道上空转。
The engine idled for several minutes before shutting off. = 引擎空转了几分钟才熄火。

现在完成时:
The engine has been idling for too long. = 引擎空转的时间太长了。
The car has idled since I parked it. = 自从我把车停好后，汽车就一直空转。

过去完成时:
The engine had idled for an hour before it finally stalled. = 引擎空转了一个小时后才最终熄火。
The car had idled for so long that the battery was drained. = 汽车空转了这么久，电池没电了。

将来时:
