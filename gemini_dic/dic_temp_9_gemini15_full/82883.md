gulper:/ˈɡʌlpər/| n. |吞咽者，大口吞食者|A gulper is someone or something that gulps or swallows something quickly and in large amounts.  It can also refer to a type of fish known for its large mouth.|例句：The whale is a massive gulper of krill.  这头鲸鱼是磷虾的大量吞食者。|例句：The child was a gulper, finishing his plate of food in minutes.  这孩子是个大胃王，几分钟就吃完了盘子里的食物。|近义词：devourer, gorger, glutton|反义词：sipper, nibbler
