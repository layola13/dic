drosophila:/drɒˈsɒfɪlə/| n. |果蝇|A genus of small flies, often used in genetic research because of their rapid reproduction and simple genetics.  Drosophila melanogaster is the most common species used.|Example:  Scientists are studying the effects of radiation on Drosophila.|近义词：fruit fly|反义词：无
