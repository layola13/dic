ninther:/ˈnaɪnθər/| n. |九岁的孩子；九先令；九镑|A ninther is informal slang referring to either a nine-year-old child or a sum of nine shillings (pre-decimalization British currency) or nine pounds.  The usage depends heavily on context and the speaker's age and background.|He's just a little ninther, still learning his ABCs. 他只是一个九岁的孩子，还在学习字母表。  |近义词：nine-year-old (for the child meaning)|反义词：无

PHRASE->
