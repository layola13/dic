refugeeisms:/ˌrefjuˈdʒiːɪzəm/| n. |难民习语；难民说法|Words or phrases used by refugees, often reflecting their experiences and cultural background.  These expressions might be unique to refugee communities or adaptations of existing language.  They can include slang, idioms, and grammatical structures not commonly used in the mainstream language.|Example: "The term 'camp life' is a refugeeism that encapsulates the reality of living in a refugee camp."  难民习语“营地生活”概括了难民营生活的现实。|近义词：refugee slang, dialect, jargon |反义词：standard language, formal language
