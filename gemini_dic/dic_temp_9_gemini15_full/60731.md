electrocardiograms:/ˌɪˌlɛktroʊˈkɑːrdɪəˌɡræmz/ | n. |心电图 | Recordings of the electrical activity of the heart.  Electrocardiograms are used to diagnose various heart conditions. | Example: The doctor reviewed the electrocardiograms to assess the patient's heart health. The electrocardiograms showed an irregular heartbeat. |近义词：ECG, EKG | 反义词：无
