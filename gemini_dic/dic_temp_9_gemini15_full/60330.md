egoistic:/ˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 自私的；利己的 |  Characterized by or arising from an excessive preoccupation with oneself and one's own needs; self-centered.  It emphasizes an excessive focus on one's own interests and desires, often at the expense of others. |  He made the egoistic decision to take all the credit for the project. 他自私地决定将项目的全部功劳都归于自己。

His egoistic behavior alienated many of his friends. 他自私的行为疏远了许多朋友。

| 近义词：selfish, self-centered, narcissistic, self-absorbed | 反义词：altruistic, selfless, unselfish |  形容词
