griddled:/ˈɡrɪdld/| vt.|用烤架烤；在烤架上煎|To cook (food) on a griddle.  Griddling is a method of cooking that uses a flat, heated surface, typically a griddle, to cook food. This method results in a browned, slightly crispy exterior and a tender interior.| 例句：I griddled the pancakes until they were golden brown. 我把煎饼烤到金黄色。

例句：She griddled the vegetables before adding them to the stir-fry.  她在把蔬菜添加到炒菜之前先用烤架烤了一下。


近义词：pan-fry, broil, grill|反义词：boil, steam|动词
