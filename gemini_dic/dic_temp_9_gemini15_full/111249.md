litigants:/ˈlɪtɪɡənts/| n. |诉讼当事人，争讼者|People involved in a lawsuit; parties to a lawsuit.  The litigants presented their cases to the judge.|近义词：parties, disputants, contenders|反义词：observers, mediators


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The litigants are presenting their arguments. = 诉讼当事人正在陈述他们的论点。
These litigants are known for their aggressive tactics. = 这些诉讼当事人以其咄咄逼人的策略而闻名。
