folk:/foʊk/|ˈfəʊk/| n. adj.|民间；人们；亲属；民族；|People of a particular region or group, especially those sharing a common culture, language, or history.  Also, family members. |例句：The local folk were very welcoming. 当地的人们非常热情。

例句：My folks are coming to visit next week. 我的家人下周要来探望我。


近义词：people, nation, tribe, kin, relatives |反义词： none


词性:名词，形容词
