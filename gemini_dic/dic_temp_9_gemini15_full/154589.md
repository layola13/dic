persecutrix:/ˈpɜːrsɪkjuːtrɪks/ | n. |迫害者（女性）| A woman who persecutes.  The term is rarely used and often considered archaic or overly formal. It emphasizes the female gender of the perpetrator in acts of persecution. | Example:  The prosecutrix presented compelling evidence of the defendant's cruelty.  迫害者出示了令人信服的证据证明被告的残酷行为。|近义词：persecutor (general term), tormentor, oppressor |反义词：victim, sufferer
