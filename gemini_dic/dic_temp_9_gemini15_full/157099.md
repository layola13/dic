pinakothek:/ˌpɪnəˈkoʊtɛk/| n. |美术馆，画廊|A pinakothek is a gallery or museum, especially one containing a large collection of paintings.|The Alte Pinakothek in Munich houses a vast collection of Old Masters.|近义词：gallery, museum|反义词:None.
