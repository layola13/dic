repackaging:/ˌriːˈpækɪdʒɪŋ/ | n. | 再包装；重新包装 | The act of repackaging goods, often to change their appearance, presentation, or to make them more appealing to consumers.  It involves putting products into new containers or altering existing packaging.  This is commonly done for marketing purposes, to extend shelf life, or to improve logistics. | Example: The company decided on repackaging its product line to attract a younger demographic.  公司决定重新包装其产品线以吸引更年轻的人群。 | 近义词：rebranding, relaunching, restyling | 反义词：original packaging, initial packaging | 名词
