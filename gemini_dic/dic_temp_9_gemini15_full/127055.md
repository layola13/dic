/næt/| n. |天生, 本性, 本质 |The inherent nature of something;  a person's inherent character. |例句：The nat of the beast is to hunt.  兽性就是狩猎。|近义词：nature, essence, character |反义词：artificiality, nurture


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your nature? = 你的天性是什么？
What is the nature of this problem? = 这个问题的本质是什么？

一般过去时:
What was his nature like? = 他的天性是什么样的？
What was the nature of the accident? = 事故的性质是什么？

现在完成时:
What has been the nature of their relationship? = 他们关系的本质是什么？
What has determined the nature of this event? = 什么决定了这次事件的性质？

过去完成时:
What had been the nature of the conflict before the peace talks? = 和谈之前冲突的性质是什么？
What had been his nature before the trauma? = 受伤之前他的天性是什么？

将来时:
What will be the nature of the future? = 未来将会是什么样的？
What will be the nature of the work? = 工作的性质将会是什么？

虚拟语气:
If I knew the nature of his intentions, I would act differently. = 如果我知道他的意图的本质，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I knew the true nature of the situation. = 我希望我知道情况的真相。

被动语态:
The nature of the problem was not fully understood. = 这个问题的性质没有被完全理解。
The nature of the experiment is being carefully considered. = 实验的性质正在被仔细考虑。

疑问句:
What is the nature of your complaint? = 你投诉的性质是什么？
What is the nature of this substance? = 这物质的性质是什么？

否定句:
The nature of the problem is not immediately apparent. = 问题的性质并不立即显而易见。
I don't understand the nature of his anger. = 我不明白他愤怒的本质。
