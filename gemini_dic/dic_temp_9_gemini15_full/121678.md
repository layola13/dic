misplayed:/ˌmɪsˈpleɪd/| v.| 玩弄；误用；打错；处理不当 | To play incorrectly or to handle or use something wrongly.  It implies a failure to use something correctly or effectively, often resulting in a negative outcome. | 例句：He misplayed the crucial hand and lost the game. 他打错了关键牌，输掉了比赛。

He misplayed his cards in the negotiation and lost a valuable contract. 他在谈判中处理不当，失去了一个重要的合同。


近义词： mishandled, misused, mismanaged, botched


反义词：played well, managed well, handled skillfully


USAGE->

She misplays her role in the team. = 她没有很好地发挥她在团队中的作用。

一般过去时:
The company misplayed its response to the crisis. = 公司对危机的回应处理不当。
