interpollinated:/ˌɪntərˈpɒləneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 交配的；异花授粉的 |  Describes plants that have been pollinated by pollen from a different plant of the same species.  This contrasts with self-pollination, where a plant is pollinated by its own pollen. | Example: The farmer ensured a good harvest by planting interpollinated varieties of apples.  农民通过种植异花授粉的苹果品种确保了丰收。 | 近义词：cross-pollinated | 反义词：self-pollinated | 形容词
