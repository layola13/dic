reacidifies:/ˌriːˈæsɪdaɪfɑɪz/ | v. | 再酸化；重新酸化 | To make something acidic again.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (again) to "acidifies," which itself is the third-person singular present tense of the verb "acidify." | The rain reacidifies the soil after a period of drought. 雨后土壤再次酸化。The factory's emissions reacidified the nearby lake. 工厂的排放使附近的湖泊再次酸化。|  acidify, sour | neutralize, alkalize | 动词
