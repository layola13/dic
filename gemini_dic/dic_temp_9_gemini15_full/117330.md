meddlingly:/ˈmɛd(ə)lɪŋli/| adv.| 爱管闲事地；干涉地 | In a meddling or interfering manner.  |例句：He meddled meddingly in his neighbor's affairs. 他爱管闲事地干涉了他邻居的事务。
例句：She meddingly offered unsolicited advice. 她爱管闲事地提供了并非请求的建议。|近义词：interferingly, intrusively, officiously |反义词：passively, unconcernedly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It modifies verbs, describing *how* an action is performed.  It's always used in the same way.

PHRASE->
There aren't common set phrases with "meddlingly".  Its usage is quite straightforward.  It's primarily used to describe the manner of meddling actions.
