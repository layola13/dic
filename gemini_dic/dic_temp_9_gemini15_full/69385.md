feed:/fiːd/ | /fid/| n./vt.|喂养；饲料；信息；进料|to give food to a person or animal;  supply information or data to a system.|例句：The farmer feeds his cows twice a day.  农民每天喂两次牛。  The news feed constantly updates with new information. 新闻推送不断更新信息。|近义词：nourish, supply, provide|反义词：starve, deprive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feeds his dog every morning. = 他每天早上喂他的狗。
The birds feed on seeds and insects. = 鸟类以种子和昆虫为食。

一般过去时:
I fed the cat yesterday. = 我昨天喂了猫。
She fed the baby a bottle of milk. = 她给婴儿喂了一瓶牛奶。

现在完成时:
They have fed the animals. = 他们已经喂了动物。
We have fed the information into the computer. = 我们已经将信息输入电脑。

过去完成时:
The farmer had fed the sheep before it rained. = 下雨前农民已经喂了羊。
She had fed the baby before putting him to bed. = 她哄孩子睡觉前已经喂过他了。

将来时:
I will feed the birds tomorrow. = 我明天将喂鸟。
He will feed the data into the program. = 他将把数据输入程序。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would feed the stray cats. = 如果我有更多时间，我会喂那些流浪猫。
I wish I had fed the dog earlier. = 我真希望我早点喂狗。

被动语态:
The animals are fed twice a day. = 动物每天喂两次。
The information is fed into the system automatically. = 信息自动输入系统。

疑问句:
Do you feed your pets regularly? = 你定期喂养你的宠物吗？
How often do they feed the fish? = 他们多久喂一次鱼？

否定句:
I didn't feed the dog this morning. = 我今天早上没有喂狗。
They haven't fed the chickens yet. = 他们还没喂鸡。


PHRASE->
