self-restoring: /ˌsɛlf rɪˈstɔːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 自修复的，自动恢复的 |  Having the ability to repair or recover itself without external intervention.  This term often describes systems, materials, or processes that can automatically correct errors or damage. |


例句：

The self-restoring software automatically fixed the corrupted files.  = 自修复软件自动修复了损坏的文件。

This material boasts self-restoring properties, making it ideal for high-stress applications. =  这种材料具有自修复特性，使其成为高应力应用的理想选择。


近义词：self-healing, regenerative, self-repairing


反义词：irreparable, non-regenerative,  unrecoverable


词性：形容词
