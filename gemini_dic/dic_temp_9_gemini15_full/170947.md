pseudoorganic:/ˌsuːdoʊˈɔːrɡænɪk/|/ˌsjuːdəʊˈɔːɡænɪk/| adj. | 似有机物的；假有机物的 | Apparently organic, but actually synthesized or derived from inorganic sources.  The term often refers to compounds that mimic the structure or properties of organic molecules but have different origins.  | Example:  Many pseudoorganic pesticides are now being phased out due to environmental concerns.  许多似有机物的杀虫剂由于环境问题正在逐步淘汰。| 近义词:synthetic, artificial, man-made | 反义词:natural, genuine, organic
