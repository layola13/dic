letterheads:/ˈlɛtərhɛdz/ | n. |信头纸；抬头信纸 |Printed stationery with a company's name and address at the top.  Letterheads are used for formal correspondence to give a professional appearance.|  Example:  The company's letterhead featured its logo and contact information. 公司的信头纸印有其标识和联系信息。


The marketing department ordered new letterheads with an updated logo. 市场部订购了印有更新标识的新信头纸。|近义词：stationery, headed paper |反义词：plain paper
