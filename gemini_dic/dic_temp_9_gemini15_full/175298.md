rainproof: /ˈreɪnprʊf/ | adj. | 防水的，防雨的 | Resistant to rain; designed or treated to prevent the passage of water.  | 例句：The jacket is rainproof, so you won't get wet. 这件夹克是防水的，所以你不会淋湿。


The new tent is completely rainproof. 这顶新帐篷完全防水。


近义词：waterproof, watertight | 反义词：permeable, porous | 词性: adjective

词源:
The word "rainproof" is a compound word formed from "rain" and "proof."  "Rain" refers to water falling from the clouds, and "proof" in this context means resistant to or protected from something.  Its creation is a straightforward combination reflecting its meaning.
