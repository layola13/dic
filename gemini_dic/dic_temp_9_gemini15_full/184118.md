resinous:/ˈrezɪnəs/| adj.|树脂的；像树脂的|Having the characteristics of resin; resembling resin in appearance or properties.  The resinous substance was sticky and fragrant.|例句：The pine tree exuded a resinous sap. 这棵松树分泌出树脂状的汁液。

The air in the forest was thick with the resinous scent of pine needles. 森林里的空气中弥漫着浓烈的松针树脂味。

近义词：resinlike, gummy, viscous|反义词：watery, fluid
