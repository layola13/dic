couplets:/ˈkʌplɪts/| n.|对句；双行诗；|A couplet is a pair of successive lines of verse, especially a rhyming pair.  Couplets are often used in poetry and song lyrics to create a sense of closure or emphasis.|例句：Shakespeare's sonnets often end with a rhyming couplet.  莎士比亚的十四行诗结尾通常是一对押韵的诗句。|例句：The poem concluded with a witty couplet. 这首诗以一句诙谐的对句结尾。|近义词：distich, pair, duet|反义词：None
