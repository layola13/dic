outcatch:/aʊtˈkætʃ/| vt.|超过，胜过；捕获（比别人多）|To surpass in catching or obtaining something, especially in a competitive context.|例句：The experienced fisherman outcaught his novice companions, reeling in a far greater number of fish.  经验丰富的渔夫比他的新手同伴捕获了更多的鱼。|例句：The sales team outcaught their rivals, securing a larger share of the market. 销售团队超过了竞争对手，获得了更大的市场份额。|近义词：outsmart, outdo, surpass, exceed|反义词：underperform, fail
