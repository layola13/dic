probable:/ˈprɒbəbl/ /ˈprɑːbəbl/ | adj. | 可能的，大概的 | likely to be true or to happen; having a high probability | The probable cause of the fire was faulty wiring.  火灾的可能原因是线路故障。It is probable that he will arrive late. 他很可能会迟到。| likely, possible, plausible | improbable, unlikely, impossible | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is probable that it will rain today. = 今天可能下雨。
The probable outcome is a draw. = 可能的结果是平局。


一般过去时:
It was probable that he had already left. = 他可能已经离开了。
The probable reason for his absence was illness. = 他缺席的可能原因是生病了。


现在完成时:
It has been probable that she has won the competition. = 她很可能已经赢得了比赛。
It's probable that they have finished the project. = 他们很可能已经完成了项目。


过去完成时:
It had been probable that the storm would hit the coast. = 暴风雨很可能已经袭击了海岸。
It was probable that they had already eaten. = 他们很可能已经吃过了。


将来时:
It will probably rain tomorrow. = 明天可能会下雨。
It is probable that he will succeed. = 他很可能会成功。


虚拟语气:
It would be probable that he would accept the offer. = 他很可能会接受这个提议。（条件句）
It is probable that he would have helped if he had known. = 如果他知道的话，他很可能会帮忙。（与过去事实相反）


被动语态:
It is probable that the problem will be solved soon. = 这个问题很可能会很快得到解决。
The cause of the accident is probable to be determined. = 事故原因很可能会被确定。


疑问句:
Is it probable that he will come? = 他可能会来吗？
Is it probable that the meeting will be cancelled? = 会议可能会取消吗？


否定句:
It is not probable that he will win. = 他不太可能赢。
It is improbable that she will agree. = 她不太可能同意。
