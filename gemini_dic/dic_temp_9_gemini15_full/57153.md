dowagers:/ˈdaʊeɪdʒərz/| n.|  寡妇（尤指有爵位的贵妇）,  老妇人 | A dowager is a widow, especially a wealthy or titled one.  The term often carries a connotation of age, dignity, and perhaps a bit of imperiousness. |例句：The dowager queen continued to exert considerable influence on court affairs.  这位王太后继续对宫廷事务施加相当大的影响力。

例句：She was a dowager of considerable wealth and social standing. 她是一位拥有相当财富和社会地位的寡妇。


近义词：widow, elderly woman, matron


反义词：young woman


词性:名词
