knights:/naɪts/| n. |骑士，武士|A knight is a man who served his sovereign or lord as a mounted warrior in the Middle Ages.  The term also refers to a person who has received a knighthood. |例句：King Arthur and his knights of the Round Table are legendary figures. 亚瑟王和他的圆桌骑士是传奇人物。

例句：He was knighted for his service to the country. 他因对国家的贡献而被封为爵士。

近义词：warrior, champion, sir (in the context of knighthood)
反义词：peasant, commoner
