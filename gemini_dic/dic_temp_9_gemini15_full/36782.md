colobus:/ˈkɒləbəs/| n. |疣猴属动物|A colobus is a type of monkey belonging to the genus *Colobus*, characterized by long limbs, a long tail, and often a distinctive black-and-white coat.  They are primarily arboreal and are found in Africa. |Example: The black-and-white colobus monkey is a common sight in many African forests.  =黑白疣猴是非洲许多森林中常见的景象。|近义词：monkey, primate |反义词：N/A
