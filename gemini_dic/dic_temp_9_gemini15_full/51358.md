deter:/dɪˈtɜːr/| vt.|阻止，劝退，威慑|To discourage (someone) from doing something, typically by making them doubt its desirability or feasibility.|The threat of severe penalties should deter potential offenders.  严厉的惩罚威胁应该可以阻止潜在的罪犯。|The heavy rain deterred many people from attending the concert. 大雨阻止了许多人参加音乐会。| dissuade, discourage | encourage, incite |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The high price deters many customers. = 高价阻止了许多顾客购买。
Harsh punishments deter potential criminals. = 严厉的惩罚震慑潜在的罪犯。


一般过去时:
The storm deterred us from going to the beach. = 暴风雨阻止了我们去海滩。
Fear of failure deterred him from applying for the job. = 害怕失败阻止了他申请这份工作。


现在完成时:
Nothing has deterred her from pursuing her dreams. = 没有什么能阻止她追求梦想。
The warnings have deterred many tourists from visiting the area. = 警告已经阻止了许多游客前往该地区。


过去完成时:
Previous failures had deterred him from trying again. = 之前的失败阻止了他再次尝试。
The bad weather had deterred them from attending the picnic. = 恶劣的天气阻止了他们参加野餐。


将来时:
Higher taxes will deter investment. = 更高的税收将阻止投资。
Increased security measures will deter potential threats. = 加强的安全措施将阻止潜在的威胁。


虚拟语气:
If the penalties were stricter, it would deter more crime. = 如果处罚更严厉，它将阻止更多的犯罪。
I wish the threat of punishment would deter them from doing it again. = 我希望惩罚的威胁能阻止他们再次这样做。


被动语态:
Many people are deterred by the high cost. = 许多人被高昂的成本吓退了。
Potential investors were deterred by the economic uncertainty. = 潜在投资者被经济不确定性吓退了。


疑问句:
Will the new law deter crime effectively? = 新法律能否有效地阻止犯罪？
What can be done to deter young people from smoking? = 可以采取什么措施阻止年轻人吸烟？


否定句:
The lack of security didn't deter the thieves. = 安全措施的缺乏并没有阻止小偷。
Nothing could deter her from achieving her goal. = 没有什么能够阻止她实现自己的目标。


PHRASE->
deter from = 阻止…做…
deter crime = 阻止犯罪
deter violence = 阻止暴力
deter aggression = 阻止侵略
deter corruption = 阻止腐败
