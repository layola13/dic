homonymous:/ˈhɒmənɪməs/ /ˈhɑːmənɪməs/ | adj. | 同音异义词的；同形异义词的 | Having the same sound or spelling but a different meaning.  Words that are homonymous are called homonyms.  | 例句：The words "bank" (financial institution) and "bank" (riverside) are homonymous.  同音异义词“bank”（金融机构）和“bank”（河岸）是同音异义词。

例句：  "Bat" (a flying mammal) and "bat" (a piece of sporting equipment) are homonymous words.  “蝙蝠”（一种飞行动物）和“球棒”（一种运动器材）是同音异义词。


近义词： synonymous (in the sense of sharing a sound or spelling, not necessarily meaning)


反义词：  没有直接的反义词，但可以考虑与“同义词”或“完全不同意义的词”相对比。


词性：形容词
