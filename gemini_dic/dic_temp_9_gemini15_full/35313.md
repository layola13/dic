cnbc:/ˌsiːˌenˈbiːˌsiː/| n. |美国全国广播公司财经频道|CNBC (Cable News Network Business) is an American multinational financial news channel, providing business and financial news coverage around the clock. It's known for its in-depth analysis, market reports, and interviews with leading business figures.|例句：I watch CNBC every morning to get the latest market updates. 我每天早上都看CNBC来获取最新的市场信息。  I learned about the economic downturn from CNBC's reporting. 我是从CNBC的报道中了解到经济衰退的。|近义词：Bloomberg, Fox Business|反义词：无
