postcentral: /ˌpoʊstˈsɛntrəl/ | adj. | 中央后部的；中枢后的 |  Relating to the area behind the central part of something, especially the central sulcus of the brain.  This term is primarily used in anatomy and neurology to describe the location of structures or functions relative to the central sulcus, a major fissure separating the frontal and parietal lobes of the brain. | Example: The postcentral gyrus is located immediately posterior to the central sulcus and is primarily involved in somatosensory processing.  中央后回位于中央沟的后方，主要参与躯体感觉处理。| 近义词: posterior, behind | 反义词: precentral, anterior
