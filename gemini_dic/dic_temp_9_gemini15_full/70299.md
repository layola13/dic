fidgetinesses:/ˈfɪdʒɪtɪnəsiz/ | n. |  烦躁不安的举动；坐立不安的行为 | The plural of fidgetiness, referring to multiple instances or types of restless or nervous behavior.  It describes repeated actions indicative of anxiety or discomfort. |例句：His fidgetinesses, from tapping his foot to constantly adjusting his glasses, betrayed his nervousness during the interview.  他的坐立不安，从敲打脚到不断调整眼镜，都暴露了他面试时的紧张情绪。


例句：The child's fidgetinesses, including constant squirming and whining, made it difficult for the teacher to conduct the lesson.  孩子的各种烦躁不安的行为，包括不停地扭动和哭闹，使得老师难以进行教学。


近义词：restlessnesses, unease, anxieties, nervous habits


反义词：calmness, composure, tranquility


词性：名词 (复数)
