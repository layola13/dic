faxed:/fækst/ | v. | 传真发送 | To send a document or image electronically via fax machine.  | I faxed the contract to the client yesterday.  我昨天把合同传真给了客户。 | emailed, emailed, sent | received, collected | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I fax documents to my colleagues regularly. = 我定期用传真给同事发送文件。
She faxes reports to the head office every Friday. = 她每周五都把报告传真给总部。


一般过去时:
He faxed the order to the supplier this morning. = 他今天早上把订单传真给了供应商。
They faxed the documents before the meeting. = 他们会议前传真了文件。


现在完成时:
I have faxed all the necessary documents. = 我已经传真了所有必要的文档。
She has faxed the report, but hasn't received confirmation. = 她已经传真了报告，但还没有收到确认。


过去完成时:
He had faxed the application before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经传真了申请。
They had faxed the information before I called. = 在我打电话之前他们已经传真了信息。


将来时:
I will fax you the details later today. = 我今天晚些时候会把细节传真给你。
She will fax the documents as soon as possible. = 她会尽快传真文件。


虚拟语气:
If I had a fax machine, I would fax the document immediately. = 如果我有传真机，我会立即传真文件。
I wish I had faxed the documents earlier. = 我希望我早点把文件传真了。


被动语态:
The document was faxed to the wrong number. = 文件传真到了错误的号码。
The report will be faxed to you tomorrow. = 报告明天会传真给你。


疑问句:
Did you fax the application? = 你传真申请了吗？
Has she faxed the report yet? = 她已经传真报告了吗？


否定句:
I didn't fax the document because I didn't have the number. = 我没有传真文件，因为我没有号码。
She hasn't faxed the contract yet. = 她还没有传真合同。


PHRASE->
fax over = 传真过来
fax a copy = 传真一份副本
fax through = 传真发送
fax something to someone = 把某物传真给某人
fax it to me = 传真给我
