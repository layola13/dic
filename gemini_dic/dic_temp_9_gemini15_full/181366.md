reinvestment:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛstmənt/ | n. | 再投资；重新投资 | The act of investing again, typically profits or returns from a previous investment.  It involves using the earnings generated from an investment to acquire more assets.  This strategy aims to generate compound growth. | Example: The company's reinvestment strategy has led to significant growth over the past decade.  这家公司的再投资战略在过去十年中带来了显著增长。  |近义词：reinversion, reapplication of funds|反义词：withdrawal, disinvestment|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reinvests its profits annually. = 公司每年都会将利润进行再投资。
He reinvests his earnings in the stock market. = 他将他的收入再投资到股市。


一般过去时:
They reinvested the dividends in new equipment. = 他们将股息再投资于新设备。
She reinvested her bonus in real estate. = 她将奖金再投资于房地产。


现在完成时:
We have reinvested all our profits this year. = 今年我们已经将所有利润进行了再投资。
They have reinvested a significant portion of their earnings. = 他们已经将很大一部分收入进行了再投资。


过去完成时:
The firm had reinvested its profits before the downturn. = 在经济下滑之前，该公司已经将利润进行了再投资。
Before the acquisition, they had reinvested heavily in R&D. = 在收购之前，他们已大力投资于研发。


将来时:
We will reinvest the surplus funds in the business. = 我们将把剩余资金再投资于企业。
She will reinvest her savings in a new venture. = 她将把积蓄再投资于新的企业。


虚拟语气:
If they had reinvested earlier, they would have made more money. = 如果他们早点进行再投资，他们就会赚到更多钱。
I wish I had reinvested my earnings instead of spending them. = 我真希望我把收入再投资而不是花掉了。


被动语态:
The profits were reinvested in research and development. = 利润被再投资于研发。
A large portion of the returns has been reinvested in infrastructure projects. = 大部分收益已再投资于基础设施项目。


疑问句:
Will the company reinvest its profits this year? = 公司今年会将利润进行再投资吗？
Where will the funds be reinvested? = 资金将被再投资到哪里？


否定句:
They did not reinvest their profits last year. = 他们去年没有将利润进行再投资。
The company chose not to reinvest the surplus funds. = 公司选择不将剩余资金进行再投资。
