rationale:/ˌræʃəˈnæl/| n. |基本原理；根本原因；理由；逻辑依据 |The rationale behind the decision was to minimize costs.  这项决定的基本原理是为了最大限度地降低成本。| reason, justification, grounds, basis | illogical, unreasonable | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rationale for this policy is clear. = 这项政策的基本原理很明确。
His rationale is based on sound evidence. = 他的理由基于可靠的证据。


一般过去时:
The rationale for the project was poorly explained. = 项目的理由解释得很糟糕。
Their rationale was ultimately flawed. = 他们的理由最终是错误的。


现在完成时:
The rationale has been clearly articulated in the report. = 报告中已清楚地阐述了其基本原理。
We have yet to understand the full rationale. = 我们尚未完全理解其基本原理。


过去完成时:
The rationale had already been established before the debate began. = 在辩论开始之前，其基本原理就已经确定了。
It turned out that their rationale had been based on false assumptions. = 事实证明，他们的理由是基于错误的假设。


将来时:
The rationale will be explained in detail later. = 基本原理稍后将详细解释。
We will need a strong rationale to justify the changes. = 我们需要一个强有力的理由来证明这些改变是合理的。


虚拟语气:
If the rationale were more convincing, they would agree. = 如果理由更有说服力，他们就会同意。
I wish the rationale for this decision were clearer. = 我希望这项决定的理由更清晰。


被动语态:
The rationale was questioned by many experts. = 许多专家对这一基本原理提出了质疑。
The rationale is being carefully reviewed. = 基本原理正在被仔细审查。


疑问句:
What is the rationale behind this proposal? = 这个提案背后的基本原理是什么？
Can you explain the rationale for your actions? = 你能解释你行为背后的理由吗？


否定句:
There is no rationale for such harsh measures. = 采取如此严厉措施没有任何理由。
His actions had no clear rationale. = 他的行为没有明确的理由。
