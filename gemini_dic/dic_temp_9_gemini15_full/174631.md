quivers:/ˈkwɪvərz/| n. v.|颤抖；箭袋；|英文解析：A quiver can be a trembling motion, or a case for holding arrows.  As a noun, it refers to a container for arrows; as a verb, it means to tremble or shake. |例句：The frightened child quivered with fear.  = 受惊吓的孩子吓得发抖。

The archer reached for his quiver. = 射手伸手去拿他的箭袋。|近义词：(n.) case, container; (v.) tremble, shake, shiver|反义词：(v.) be still, be steady|词性:n. v.
