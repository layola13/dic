dynamiters:/ˈdaɪnəmaɪtərz/| n. |爆破者，使用炸药的人|People who use dynamite to demolish buildings, clear land, or engage in other activities requiring explosives.  They may be employed in mining, construction, or demolition work. Their skills involve careful handling of explosives and adherence to safety regulations.|例句：The dynamiters carefully placed the charges before initiating the controlled demolition. 爆破人员在引爆受控爆破之前小心地放置了炸药。The illegal dynamiters were apprehended by the police.  非法爆破者被警方逮捕。|近义词：blasters, demolition experts, saboteurs (in a negative context)|反义词：builders (in a contrasting context)
