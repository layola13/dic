micropyle:/ˈmaɪkrəpaɪl/| n.|珠孔，小孔|A micropyle is a small opening in the surface of an ovule, egg, or seed through which the pollen tube penetrates or the sperm enters during fertilization.  It's also a tiny pore in the shell of some insect eggs.|例句：The pollen tube entered the ovule through the micropyle.  花粉管通过珠孔进入胚珠。|The micropyle is crucial for successful fertilization in many plants. 珠孔对许多植物的成功受精至关重要。|近义词：pore, opening, aperture|反义词：None specifically.
