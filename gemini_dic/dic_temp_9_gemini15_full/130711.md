nonalienation: /ˌnɒnˌeɪliˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 非剥夺；不丧失；保有；继续拥有 | The state or condition of not being alienated or estranged; the act of preventing alienation.  It often refers to the continued possession or control of something, especially property or rights. | Example:  The nonalienation clause in the contract ensured that the property would remain with the original owner.  = 合同中的不可转让条款确保了该房产将仍然属于原业主。


近义词：retention, preservation, safeguarding


反义词：alienation, estrangement, dispossession


词性：名词
