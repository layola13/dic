screeches:/skriːtʃɪz/| n. v.|尖锐刺耳的声音，尖声叫喊|The sound made by something rubbing against something else harshly, or a loud, harsh cry.|例句：The brakes screeched as the car came to a sudden stop.  刹车发出尖锐刺耳的声音，汽车突然停了下来。

The child screeched in protest when his toy was taken away.  孩子被拿走玩具时尖叫着抗议。|近义词：shriek, squeal, screech, yell, scream|反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The tires screech on the wet road. 轮胎在湿滑的路上发出刺耳的摩擦声。
Birds screech loudly in the early morning. 鸟儿在清晨大声尖叫。

一般过去时:
The train screeched to a halt. 火车嘎吱一声停了下来。
The woman screeched with laughter.  那个女人尖声大笑。

现在完成时:
The brakes have screeched several times today. 刹车今天已经尖叫了几次了。
The children have been screeching all afternoon. 孩子们整个下午都在尖叫。

过去完成时:
The car had screeched before it crashed.  汽车在撞车前发出刺耳的声响。
The birds had screeched all night. 鸟儿整夜都在尖叫。

将来时:
The tires will screech if you brake too hard. 如果你刹车太猛，轮胎就会发出刺耳的声音。
The baby will screech if he's hungry. 如果宝宝饿了，他会尖叫。

虚拟语气:
If the brakes had screeched earlier, the accident might have been avoided. 如果刹车早点发出尖锐的声音，事故或许可以避免。
If the birds were to screech all night again, I wouldn't be able to sleep. 如果鸟儿再整夜尖叫，我就睡不着了。

被动语态:
A terrifying screech was heard in the night. 夜里听到一声可怕的尖叫声。
A high-pitched screech was made by the owl. 猫头鹰发出一声高亢的尖叫。

疑问句:
Did you hear that screech? 你听到那尖叫声了吗？
What made that screeching sound?  是什么发出了那种尖锐的声音？

否定句:
The car didn't screech when it stopped. 车停下来时没有发出尖锐的声音。
The birds didn't screech this morning.  鸟儿今天早上没有尖叫。
