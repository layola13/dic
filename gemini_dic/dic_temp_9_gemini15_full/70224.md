fibulas:/ˈfɪbjʊləz/| n. |腓骨(复数)|The fibulas are the smaller of the two bones in the lower leg, running parallel to the tibias. They are also known as the calf bones.  |例句：The doctor examined the patient's injured fibulas.  医生检查了病人受伤的腓骨。|例句：  Fractures of the fibulas are relatively common in sports injuries.腓骨骨折在运动损伤中比较常见。|近义词：calf bones|反义词：tibias (胫骨)
