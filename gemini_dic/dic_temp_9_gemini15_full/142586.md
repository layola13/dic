osteodystrophy:/ˌɒstiːoʊˈdɪstrəfi/| n. |骨营养不良；骨发育不良 |A disorder characterized by defective bone development or growth.  It can result from various causes including nutritional deficiencies, hormonal imbalances, and genetic conditions.  The condition often leads to skeletal deformities and impaired bone function.|例句：Renal osteodystrophy is a common complication of chronic kidney disease. 肾性骨营养不良是慢性肾脏病的常见并发症。

The child's short stature was attributed to osteodystrophy.  孩子的矮小身材归因于骨发育不良。|近义词：bone dystrophy, skeletal dysplasia|反义词：optimal bone health, normal bone development|
