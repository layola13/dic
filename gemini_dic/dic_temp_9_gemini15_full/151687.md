partitions: /pɑːrˈtɪʃənz/ | n. | 分区；隔板；分割 |  Divisions or sections of something; a dividing wall or screen.  | Example:  The hard drive is divided into several partitions.  硬盘被分成了几个分区。

The office partitions provide some privacy.  办公室的隔断提供了一些隐私。


近义词：divisions, sections, compartments, subdivisions | 反义词：unification, consolidation, merger


词源:  From Old French *particion, from Latin *partitio, from partire "to divide".


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The software partitions the hard drive automatically. = 软件自动分区硬盘。
This wall partitions the kitchen from the dining room. = 这堵墙把厨房和餐厅隔开了。

过去完成时:
