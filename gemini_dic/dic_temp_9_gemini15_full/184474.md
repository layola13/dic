restages:/riːˈsteɪdʒ/| vt.|重新上演；重新搬演|To perform (a play, etc.) again, often in a new or different production.|例句：The theatre company will restage the classic play next year. 剧团明年将重新上演这部经典剧作。
例句：They decided to restage the historical event for a documentary. 他们决定为纪录片重新演绎这段历史事件。|近义词：revive, reproduce, reenact|反义词：archive, discontinue
