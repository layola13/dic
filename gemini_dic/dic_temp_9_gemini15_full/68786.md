furthermore:/ˈfɜːðərˌmɔːr/| adv.|此外，而且|Furthermore, it is important to note that... 此外，重要的是要注意……|例句：The evidence is compelling; furthermore, the suspect has a history of similar crimes.  证据确凿；此外，嫌疑人还有类似犯罪史。

例句：He is intelligent; furthermore, he is very hardworking. 他很聪明；而且他非常努力。


近义词：moreover, besides, in addition, also|反义词：however, nevertheless, on the other hand|词性：副词
