egos:/ˈiːɡoʊz/| n. |自我，自负，自我中心 |The plural form of "ego," referring to instances of self-importance or inflated self-image.  It can also refer to the sense of self. |例句：His egos clashed during the debate.  他的自负在辩论中冲突了。|例句：Managing multiple egos on a film set is a challenging task. 在电影片场管理多重自我是一项具有挑战性的工作。|近义词：self-importance, vanity, conceit, pride |反义词：humility, modesty, selflessness |名词
