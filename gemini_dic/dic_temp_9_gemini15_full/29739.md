catnaps:/ˈkæt næps/| n.|小睡，打盹儿|a short sleep, especially during the day.|例句：She often takes catnaps in the afternoon. 她经常在下午打个盹儿。|例句：The baby took several catnaps throughout the day. 宝宝一整天都睡了好几次。|近义词：nap, doze, siesta|反义词：insomnia, wakefulness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He takes catnaps every afternoon. = 他每天下午都会小睡一会儿。
She often takes a catnap after lunch. = 她通常午饭后会小睡一会儿。
