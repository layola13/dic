inheres:/ɪnˈhɪrz/| v.|内在于；固有；存在于；|To be an essential or inherent characteristic of something; to belong intrinsically.|The inherent dignity of all human beings inheres in their capacity for reason and moral judgment. 人类的固有尊严在于他们具有理性判断和道德判断的能力。|exist, reside, be intrinsic to|extrinsic, external, alien|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The beauty inheres in the simplicity of the design. = 美在于设计的简洁。
This right inheres in every citizen. = 每个公民都享有这项权利。


一般过去时:
The problem inherent in the plan inhered in its lack of funding. = 该计划中固有的问题在于其缺乏资金。
A certain sadness inhered in her smile. = 她的笑容中蕴含着某种悲伤。


现在完成时:
The strength of their bond has inhered throughout their long marriage. = 他们之间牢固的纽带在其漫长的婚姻生活中一直存在。
This principle has inhered in the legal system for centuries. = 这个原则在法律体系中已经存在了几个世纪。


过去完成时:
Before the scandal, the company’s reputation had inhered in its ethical practices. = 在丑闻爆发之前，公司的声誉一直与其道德行为相符。
It had inhered in his nature to be cautious. = 他天生就谨慎小心。


将来时:
The success of the project will inhere in the team's dedication. = 项目的成功将取决于团队的奉献精神。
This power will inhere in the newly elected president. = 这种权力将属于新当选的总统。


虚拟语气:
If the value inhered only in material wealth, many would be unhappy. = 如果价值只在于物质财富，许多人将会不快乐。
I wish that more integrity inhered in our political system. = 我希望我们的政治体制中能有更多的正直。


被动语态:
The problem was inhered in the faulty design. = 问题在于设计缺陷。
The decision is inhered in the contract. = 该决定已载入合同。


疑问句:
Does the meaning inhere in the words themselves, or in their context? = 意义在于词语本身，还是在于它们的语境？
Where does the authority inhere? = 权力来源于哪里？


否定句:
The problem does not inhere in the design but in the implementation. = 问题不在于设计，而在于实施。
The beauty does not inhere in superficial appearances. = 美不在于表面现象。
