proprioceptive:/ˌproʊpriəˈsɛptɪv/| adj.| 本体感受的，本体感觉的 | Relating to the sense of the position and movement of the body.  This sense is mediated by sensory receptors located in muscles, tendons, and joints.  | Example:  Proprioceptive information helps us maintain balance and coordinate movement. 本体感受信息帮助我们保持平衡和协调运动。


Example:  The dancer relies heavily on proprioceptive feedback to execute precise movements.  这位舞者非常依赖本体感受反馈来执行精确的动作。


近义词：kinesthetic  (动觉的)


反义词：N/A (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与“缺乏本体感受”相关的词语，如 insensitive to body position)


词性:形容词
