flatterers:/ˈflætərərz/| n.| 奉承者，拍马屁者 | People who flatter are insincere in their praise, often for personal gain. They may exaggerate someone's qualities or accomplishments to win favor or achieve their own ends.  | Example:  He surrounded himself with flatterers who told him only what he wanted to hear. 他周围围绕着一些奉承者，他们只告诉他他想听的内容。


He dismissed the flatterers' praise as insincere. 他认为奉承者的赞美不真诚。


近义词:sycophants, fawning, yes-men | 反义词:critics, detractors
