overretention: /ˌoʊvər rɪˈtɛnʃən/ | n. |过度保有；滞留；保留过多 | The continued holding or possession of something beyond a reasonable or necessary period.  It often implies an undesirable or inefficient state. |例句：The company's overretention of obsolete inventory led to significant losses. 公司过度保有过时的库存导致了巨大的损失。

例句：The overretention of water in the soil hampered plant growth. 土壤中水分滞留过多阻碍了植物生长。


近义词：hoarding, accumulation, retention,  stagnation


反义词：release, disposal, depletion, elimination


词性：名词
