leucoscope:/ˈluːkəskoʊp/| n. |白光镜，白光检眼镜|An instrument used to examine the eye, especially to detect opacities in the ocular media.  It uses a bright light source to illuminate the eye and allows the observer to see the structures within the eye.  The term is relatively obsolete, with more modern instruments having replaced it.|Example: The ophthalmologist used a leucoscope to examine the patient's retina.|近义词：ophthalmoscope|反义词：无|名词
