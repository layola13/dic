containing: /kənˈteɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 包含，容纳 |  The verb "containing" is the present participle of the verb "contain," meaning to hold or keep something within. It describes the action of something holding something else inside. | 例句：The box containing the documents is locked.  = 包含文件的箱子锁上了。


The bottle containing the medicine should be kept out of reach of children. =  装有药品的瓶子应放在儿童够不着的地方。


近义词：holding, encompassing, including, comprising | 反义词：excluding, omitting, leaving out | 词性: Verb (present participle)


词源:  The word "containing" comes from the Old French word "contenir," which itself comes from the Latin word "continēre," meaning "to hold together," "to keep," or "to restrain."  The prefix "con-" means "together," and the root "-tenēre" means "to hold."
