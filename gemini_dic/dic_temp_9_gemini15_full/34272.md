clar:/klɑːr/| v.|阐明，澄清|to make something clear or easier to understand; to remove confusion or doubt|例句：Could you clarify that point for me?  你能帮我解释一下那点吗？|例句：The professor clarified the complex theory with simple examples. 教授用简单的例子阐明了复杂的理论。|近义词：explain, elucidate, illuminate|反义词：confuse, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He clarifies his position regularly. 他定期阐明自己的立场。
She clarifies the instructions for the assignment. 她阐明了作业的指示。

一般过去时:
The judge clarified the rules of the trial. 法官澄清了审判的规则。
He clarified his statement after the meeting. 会后，他澄清了他的声明。

现在完成时:
She has clarified her misunderstanding with her friend. 她已经澄清了她与朋友的误会。
They have clarified the details of the contract. 他们已经澄清了合同的细节。

过去完成时:
He had clarified the issue before the conference began. 会议开始前，他已经阐明了这个问题。
She had clarified her doubts before asking questions. 她提出问题之前已经澄清了自己的疑问。

将来时:
He will clarify the situation tomorrow. 他明天将澄清情况。
She will clarify the instructions before the test. 她将在考试前阐明指示。

虚拟语气:
If he clarified his position, there would be less confusion. 如果他阐明了自己的立场，就不会有那么多的困惑。
I wish she had clarified the instructions earlier. 我希望她早点说明指示。

被动语态:
The misunderstanding was clarified by the manager. 误会由经理澄清了。
The rules will be clarified in the next meeting. 规则将在下一次会议上澄清。

疑问句:
Can you clarify the meaning of this word? 你能解释这个词的意思吗？
Will the speaker clarify the point later? 演讲者稍后会阐明这一点吗？

否定句:
He did not clarify his intentions. 他没有说明他的意图。
She failed to clarify the ambiguity in her statement. 她未能澄清她声明中的含糊之处。
