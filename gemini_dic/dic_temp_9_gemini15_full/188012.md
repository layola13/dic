roughcast:/ˈrʌfˌkæst/| n. & v.|粗糙的灰泥；粗略的草稿；粗制滥造|A roughcast is a type of exterior wall finish made of a mixture of cement, sand, and water, applied roughly to a wall.  It can also refer to a preliminary sketch or draft.  As a verb, it means to apply roughcast to (a wall) or to make a rough draft of something.|例句：The old cottage had a roughcast exterior. 这座老房子有着粗糙的外墙。

The architect made a roughcast of the building design before presenting the final plan. 建筑师在提交最终方案之前先制作了建筑设计的草图。|近义词： (n.) plaster, rendering, sketch, draft; (v.) plaster, sketch, draft|反义词： (n.) fine finish, detailed drawing; (v.) refine, polish|Noun & Verb
