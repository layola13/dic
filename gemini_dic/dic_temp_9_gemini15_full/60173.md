effervescent:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsnt/| adj.| 欢腾的；活泼的；充满活力的；（液体）冒泡的 |  Characterized by or producing a bubbling or fizzing action; lively and enthusiastic.  | 例句：The party had an effervescent atmosphere.  派对气氛热烈欢腾。

The champagne was deliciously effervescent. 香槟酒口感清爽，冒着细小的气泡。| 近义词：bubbly, lively, vivacious, spirited | 反义词：flat, dull, lifeless, sluggish
