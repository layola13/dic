froths:/frɒθz/| /froʊθz/| n. v.|泡沫；起泡沫|The noun "froth" refers to a mass of small bubbles in a liquid, often produced by agitation or fermentation.  The verb "froth" means to form or produce froth.|例句：The beer frothed in the glass. 这啤酒在杯子里起泡沫了。

The champagne frothed when it was poured. 香槟酒倒出来时起泡沫了。

近义词：foam, lather, suds|反义词：flat, still|词性:n. v.
