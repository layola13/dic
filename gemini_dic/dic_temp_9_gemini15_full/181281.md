reinsulating: /ˌriːˈɪnsjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 重新隔热 | The process of adding insulation to something that was previously insulated, or improving existing insulation.  This involves adding new insulation materials or repairing and replacing existing ones to enhance thermal performance. | 例句：We are reinsulating the attic to improve energy efficiency. = 我们正在重新隔热阁楼以提高能源效率。

例句：The house needs reinsulating to prevent heat loss during winter. = 这房子需要重新隔热以防止冬季热量流失。


近义词：re-insulating, improving insulation, adding insulation


反义词：removing insulation, exposing (to the elements)


词性：动词
