galileo:/ˌɡælɪˈleɪoʊ/|/ˈɡælɪleɪoʊ/| |n.|伽利略|Galileo Galilei (1564-1642), an Italian astronomer, physicist, and engineer, sometimes called the "father of observational astronomy", the "father of modern physics", the "father of the scientific method", and the "father of modern science". His achievements include improvements to the telescope and consequent astronomical observations, and support for heliocentrism.|Example: Galileo's discoveries revolutionized our understanding of the universe. 伽利略的发现彻底改变了我们对宇宙的理解。|近义词:  astronomer, physicist, scientist|反义词: None.
