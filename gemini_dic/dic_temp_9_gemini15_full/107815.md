lapwing:/ˈlæpwɪŋ/| n.|田凫，田鹨|A lapwing is a medium-sized bird with a long, pointed crest and distinctive cry.  They are known for their breeding behaviour, including distraction displays to protect their nests.|例句：The lapwing's shrill cry echoed across the marsh. 田凫尖锐的叫声在沼泽地上回荡。

The lapwing is a common sight in open fields and wetlands. 田凫在开阔的田野和湿地很常见。|近义词：peewit,  plover|反义词:|
