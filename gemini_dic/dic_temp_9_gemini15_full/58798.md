duodecimal:/ˌduːoʊˈdesɪməl/| adj.|十二进制的；十二进位的|Relating to, or expressed in, a system of numbers based on twelve.|例句：The duodecimal system is used in some cultures for counting and measurement.  十二进制系统在一些文化中用于计数和测量。

例句：Many people prefer the duodecimal system for its divisibility by 2, 3, 4, and 6.许多人更喜欢十二进制系统，因为它可以被2、3、4和6整除。


近义词：dozenal (less common)


反义词：decimal (十进制的)


词性：形容词
