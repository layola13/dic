quasi-maternally:/ˈkwɑːzaɪməˈtɜːrnəli/ | adj. |准母性地，似母性地 |  Describes behavior or feelings that resemble those of a mother, but not fully or officially.  It suggests a caring and protective attitude, but without the legal or biological ties of motherhood. | Example:  The teacher treated her students quasi-maternally, offering comfort and guidance.  老师以准母性的方式对待她的学生，给予安慰和指导。|近义词：motherly, nurturing, protective | 反义词：neglectful, uncaring, indifferent |
