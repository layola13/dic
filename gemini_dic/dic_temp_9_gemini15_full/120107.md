mimetism:/ˈmɪmɪtɪzəm/| n. |拟态；模仿；拟生|The imitation by one organism of the form, structure, or behavior of another organism.  This often serves as a protective mechanism, such as camouflage or mimicry.  Mimetism can also refer to the imitation of one art form by another.|例句：The stick insect exhibits remarkable mimetism, blending seamlessly with its surroundings.  这种竹节虫表现出非凡的拟态能力，能与周围环境完美融合。|例句：The artist's work showed a clear mimetism of Impressionist techniques.  这位艺术家的作品明显模仿了印象派的技术。|近义词：mimicry, imitation, simulation|反义词：uniqueness, originality|名词
