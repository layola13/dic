disfigurement:/dɪsˈfɪɡjərmənt/| n. |毁容；变形；损害 |The permanent alteration of the form or appearance of something, especially the face, often resulting in a loss of beauty or symmetry.  It can refer to physical damage or impairment.  |例句：The accident caused serious disfigurement to his face. 这场事故严重毁了他的容貌。

例句：The bombing caused widespread disfigurement to the city's architecture. 轰炸使城市建筑遭到广泛破坏。


近义词：defacement, deformity, mutilation, scarring


反义词：beautification, enhancement, improvement


词性:名词
