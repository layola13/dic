head-to-head:/ˌhed tu ˈhed/| adj.|面对面的；直接竞争的|directly against each other; in direct competition|The two teams will have a head-to-head matchup in the final.  两队将在决赛中进行直接对决。
The two companies are engaged in a fierce head-to-head competition. 两家公司正在进行激烈的正面竞争。|direct, face-to-face, one-on-one|indirect, indirect competition


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They play head-to-head every week. = 他们每周都进行面对面的比赛。
The two companies are competing head-to-head in the market. = 两家公司正在市场上展开直接竞争。

一般过去时:
We had a head-to-head debate on the issue. = 我们就这个问题进行了面对面的辩论。
Last year, they had a head-to-head battle for the championship. = 去年，他们为争夺冠军进行了直接对抗。

现在完成时:
They have had several head-to-head meetings this year. = 今年他们已经进行了几次面对面的会议。
The company has been competing head-to-head with its rivals for years. = 这家公司多年来一直在与竞争对手进行直接竞争。

过去完成时:
Before the final game, they had already had three head-to-head matches. = 在决赛之前，他们已经进行了三场面对面的比赛。
They had never had a head-to-head encounter before that event. = 在那次活动之前，他们从未有过面对面的遭遇。

将来时:
We will have a head-to-head discussion tomorrow. = 我们明天将进行面对面的讨论。
The two candidates will have a head-to-head debate next week. = 两位候选人下周将进行一场直接辩论。

虚拟语气:
If we had a head-to-head competition, I think we would win. = 如果我们进行直接竞争，我认为我们会赢。
I wish we could have a head-to-head meeting to discuss this. = 我希望我们可以进行一次面对面的会议来讨论此事。

被动语态:
The debate was presented head-to-head. = 辩论是以面对面的方式进行的。
The competition was designed to be head-to-head. = 比赛的设计是直接竞争的。

疑问句:
Will the teams play head-to-head in the playoffs? = 球队在季后赛中会进行直接对决吗？
Are they going to have a head-to-head competition? = 他们打算进行直接竞争吗？

否定句:
They did not have a head-to-head confrontation. = 他们没有进行面对面的对抗。
The companies are not competing head-to-head in this market. = 这些公司在这个市场上并没有进行直接竞争。
