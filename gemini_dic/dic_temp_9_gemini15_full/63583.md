envisage:/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒ/| vt.|想象；展望；设想|To form a mental picture of something; to imagine or conceive of something as being possible.|例句：I can envisage a future where technology solves many of our problems. 我可以想象一个未来，技术将解决我们许多问题。

例句：She couldn't envisage a life without him. 她无法想象没有他的生活。


近义词：imagine, conceive, picture, foresee | 反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They envisage a bright future for the company. = 他们设想公司拥有光明的未来。
I envisage a successful outcome. = 我预想会有一个成功的结果。


一般过去时:
We envisaged a different scenario last year. = 去年我们设想了不同的情景。
She envisaged a peaceful retirement. = 她曾设想过一个平静的退休生活。


现在完成时:
They have envisaged a new marketing strategy. = 他们已经构想出一个新的营销策略。
We have envisaged many possibilities. = 我们已经设想了许多可能性。


过去完成时:
They had envisaged a much simpler project initially. = 他们最初设想过一个简单的项目。
I had envisaged a quicker resolution to the problem. = 我本以为问题能够更快解决。


将来时:
We will envisage a more sustainable approach in the future. = 我们将来会设想一个更可持续的方案。
They will envisage the challenges ahead. = 他们将预见未来的挑战。


虚拟语气:
If I had envisaged the consequences, I would have acted differently. = 如果我预见到后果，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had envisaged the difficulties earlier. = 我真希望早点预见到这些困难。


被动语态:
A bright future is envisaged for the project. = 该项目被设想拥有一个光明的未来。
Many scenarios were envisaged by the team. = 团队设想了许多方案。


疑问句:
Do you envisage any problems with the plan? = 你觉得这个计划有什么问题吗？
What do they envisage happening next? = 他们设想接下来会发生什么？


否定句:
I don't envisage any major obstacles. = 我预想不会遇到什么大的障碍。
They didn't envisage such a negative response. = 他们没有预料到这么负面的回应。


PHRASE->
envisage a future = 展望未来
envisage a scenario = 设想一种情况
envisage a solution = 设想一个解决方案
envisage the consequences = 预见后果
envisage the possibilities = 设想可能性
