simmers:/ˈsɪmərz/| v.|炖，慢煮|To cook (something) slowly and gently in a liquid just below the boiling point.  |例句：The sauce simmers gently on the stove.  酱汁在炉子上慢慢炖着。|例句：I simmered the vegetables until they were tender. 我把蔬菜炖到软烂为止。|近义词：stew, boil gently|反义词：fry, roast


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The soup simmers gently. = 汤在慢慢炖着。
She simmers the vegetables for an hour. = 她把蔬菜炖了一个小时。

一般过去时:
He simmered the sauce until it thickened. = 他把酱汁炖到浓稠为止。
They simmered the stew for three hours. = 他们把炖菜炖了三个小时。

现在完成时:
I have simmered the curry for two hours. = 我已经炖咖喱两个小时了。
She has simmered the broth until it's flavorful. = 她已经把肉汤炖得很鲜美了。


过去完成时:
The vegetables had simmered for an hour before they were added to the soup. = 蔬菜在加入汤之前已经炖了一个小时了。
The chef had simmered the sauce before adding the spices. = 厨师在添加香料之前已经把酱汁炖好了。


将来时:
I will simmer the chicken until it is tender. = 我将把鸡肉炖到软烂。
She will simmer the beans for at least 30 minutes. = 她将把豆子炖至少30分钟。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would simmer the beef for longer. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会把牛肉炖更久。
I wish I had simmered the sauce for a longer time. = 我希望我当初把酱汁炖久一点。


被动语态:
The soup is simmered slowly over low heat. = 汤在小火上慢慢炖着。
The vegetables were simmered until they were soft. = 蔬菜被炖到软烂。


疑问句:
Is the sauce simmering properly? = 酱汁炖得合适吗？
How long should I simmer the rice? = 我应该炖米饭多久？


否定句:
Don't simmer the vegetables for too long. = 不要把蔬菜炖太久。
The stew wasn't simmering; it was boiling. = 炖菜没在炖着；它在沸腾。
