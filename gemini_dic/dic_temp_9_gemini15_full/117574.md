megalith:/ˈmɛɡəlɪθ/| n. |巨石；巨石建筑|A megalith is a large stone that forms part of a structure or monument, especially one dating from prehistory.  Megaliths are often found in structures like dolmens, cromlechs, and stone circles.  They were typically erected by Neolithic or Bronze Age peoples. |例句：Stonehenge is a famous example of a megalithic monument.  巨石阵是一个著名的巨石纪念碑的例子。

The construction of these megaliths remains a mystery, with theories ranging from astronomical alignments to ritualistic purposes. 这些巨石的建造仍然是一个谜，理论从天文排列到仪式目的不等。|近义词：monolith, large stone,巨石|反义词：small stone, pebble, 小石头
