hyped:/haɪpt/ | adj. | 大肆宣传的；兴奋的；过度炒作的 |  Highly publicized; excessively promoted;  extremely excited.  | 例句：The new phone is incredibly hyped. = 这款新手机受到了极大的炒作。

The concert was hyped up by the media. =这场音乐会受到了媒体的大肆宣传。


近义词：promoted, publicized, excited, exhilarated, pumped | 反义词：underpromoted, ignored, calm, subdued


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The media is hyping the new product. = 媒体正在炒作这款新产品。
He is hyped for the upcoming game. = 他对即将到来的比赛非常兴奋。


一般过去时:
They hyped the movie release extensively. = 他们对电影上映进行了大肆宣传。
She was hyped after winning the race. = 赢得比赛后她非常兴奋。


现在完成时:
The company has hyped its new technology for months. = 这家公司已经炒作其新技术数月了。
I have been hyped up on coffee all day. = 我一整天都喝咖啡兴奋着。


过去完成时:
They had hyped the event so much that expectations were unrealistic. = 他们对活动进行了过度的宣传，以至于期望值不切实际。
He had been hyped for the interview, so he was nervous. = 他对面试非常期待，所以他很紧张。


将来时:
They will hype the product launch on social media. = 他们将在社交媒体上炒作产品发布。
She will be hyped if her team wins. = 如果她的球队获胜，她会非常兴奋。


虚拟语气:
If they hyped the product more, they would sell more. = 如果他们更多地宣传该产品，他们就会卖出更多。
I wish I hadn't been so hyped up before the presentation. = 我真希望在演示之前我没有那么兴奋。


被动语态:
The event was hyped by the organizers. = 该活动被组织者大肆宣传。
He was hyped up by the crowd's enthusiasm. = 他被人群的热情激昂了。


疑问句:
Is the new movie hyped? = 这部新电影被炒作了吗？
Are you hyped for the vacation? = 你对假期感到兴奋吗？


否定句:
The product wasn't hyped enough. = 该产品宣传不足。
She wasn't hyped about the party. = 她对派对并不兴奋。


PHRASE->
hyped up = 兴奋的；过度兴奋的
