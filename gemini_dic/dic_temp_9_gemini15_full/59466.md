eased:/iːzd/| v.|使轻松；缓和；减轻；|to make or become less severe, difficult, or painful|The pain eased after I took the medicine.  服药后疼痛减轻了。|The new policy eased tensions between the two countries.  新政策缓和了两国之间的紧张关系。|近义词：alleviate, relieve, mitigate|反义词：aggravate, intensify, worsen|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eases his worries with meditation. 他通过冥想来缓解焦虑。
The warm breeze eases the tension in the air. 暖风使空气中的紧张气氛缓和下来。


一般过去时:
The doctor eased her pain with a shot. 医生用注射缓解了她的疼痛。
The rain eased, and the sun peeked through the clouds. 雨停了，阳光透过云层照射下来。


现在完成时:
The new law has eased traffic congestion in the city. 新法律缓解了该市的交通拥堵。
The situation has eased considerably since the negotiations. 自谈判以来，局势已大大缓和。


过去完成时:
She had eased the child's fears before bedtime. 她在睡前已经减轻了孩子的恐惧。
By the time I arrived, the tension had eased. 当我到达时，紧张气氛已经缓和了。


将来时:
This medicine will ease your symptoms.  这种药会缓解你的症状。
The new bridge will ease traffic congestion. 新桥将缓解交通拥堵。


虚拟语气:
If only the pain would ease!  但愿疼痛能减轻！
I wish the situation would ease soon. 我希望情况尽快好转。


被动语态:
His anxiety was eased by the calming music. 他焦虑的心情被舒缓的音乐抚慰了。
The burden was eased by the help of his friends.  他的朋友们的帮助减轻了他的负担。


疑问句:
Will this treatment ease the symptoms? 这种治疗会缓解症状吗？
Does this medication ease the pain effectively?  这种药物能有效缓解疼痛吗？


否定句:
The medicine didn't ease my headache at all.  药根本没有缓解我的头痛。
The measures taken did not ease the financial crisis.  采取的措施并没有缓解金融危机。


PHRASE->
eased into: 慢慢地适应
eased off: 逐渐减少；减轻
eased back: 放松；退后
eased up: 放松；缓和
eased the way:  铺平道路；使事情更容易
