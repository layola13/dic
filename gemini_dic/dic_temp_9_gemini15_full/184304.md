resows: /ˈriːsoʊz/ | vt. | 再播种；重新播种 | To sow again; to sow (seeds) a second time.  |例句：The farmer resowed the field after the hail storm.  农民在冰雹过后重新播种了田地。  |近义词：replant, sow again |反义词：harvest, reap


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer resows the field every spring. = 农民每年春天都重新播种田地。
She resows her flowerbeds annually. = 她每年都重新播种她的花坛。

一般过去时:
They resowed the damaged crops last week. = 他们上周重新播种了受损的庄稼。
He resowed the seeds after the birds had eaten some. = 鸟儿吃掉了一些种子后，他又重新播种了。

现在完成时:
The gardener has resowed the lettuce after the slugs ate it. = 园丁在蛞蝓吃掉莴苣后重新播种了。
They have resowed the entire field this year. = 他们今年重新播种了整个田地。

过去完成时:
The farmer had resowed the wheat before the heavy rain came. = 暴雨来临之前，农民已经重新播种了小麦。
She had resowed the seeds twice before they finally sprouted. = 种子最终发芽之前，她已经重新播种了两次。


将来时:

The farm will resow the barley next month. = 农场下个月将重新播种大麦。
