fomenter:/fəˈmɛntər/ | n. |煽动者，挑拨者 | One who foments trouble or discord.  A fomenter incites unrest or rebellion. | Example: The fomenter of the riot was finally apprehended.  煽动暴乱的人最终被逮捕了。|近义词：agitator, instigator, troublemaker |反义词：peacemaker, conciliator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He foments discord among his colleagues. 他在同事中挑拨离间。
She foments unrest in the community. 她煽动社区动乱。


一般过去时:
The politician fomented dissent before the election.  这位政治家在大选前煽动异议。
They fomented a rebellion against the government. 他们煽动了一场反对政府的叛乱。


现在完成时:
He has fomented trouble for years. 他多年来一直挑拨事端。
She has fomented dissent within the party. 她一直在党内煽动异议。


过去完成时:
They had fomented a revolution before the king was overthrown. 在国王被推翻之前，他们已经煽动了一场革命。
The rebels had fomented unrest for months before the uprising.  叛乱分子在起义前的几个月里一直在煽动骚乱。


将来时:
They will foment further unrest if their demands are not met. 如果他们的要求得不到满足，他们将进一步煽动骚乱。
He will foment trouble if he doesn't get his way. 如果他不遂心愿，他就会挑拨事端。


虚拟语气:
If he fomented rebellion, he would be imprisoned. 如果他煽动叛乱，他就会被监禁。
I wish he hadn't fomented such discord. 我真希望他没有挑拨这样的纷争。


被动语态:
Discontent was fomented by the opposition party. 不满情绪是由反对党煽动的。
Trouble was fomented by a small group of extremists. 骚乱是由一小撮极端分子挑起的。


疑问句:
Who fomented this unrest? 谁煽动了这场骚乱？
Was the rebellion fomented by foreign powers? 叛乱是由外国势力煽动的吗？


否定句:
He did not foment the riot. 他没有煽动暴乱。
She would not foment trouble even if provoked. 即使被激怒，她也不会挑拨事端。
