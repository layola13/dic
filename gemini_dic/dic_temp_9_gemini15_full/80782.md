goswami:/ɡoʊˈswɑːmi/| n. |戈斯瓦米（印度教僧侣称号）|A Goswami is a title or honorific used in Hinduism, particularly in Vaishnavism, often associated with spiritual teachers or those of high religious standing.  It literally translates to "master of the senses" or "lord of the senses," implying mastery over one's desires and senses.|Example:  Many followers revere Swami Prabhupada as a great Goswami.|近义词：Swami, Maharaj|反义词：None specifically, as it's a title, not a descriptive adjective.
