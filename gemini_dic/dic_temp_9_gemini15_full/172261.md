pussycats: /ˈpʊsiˌkæts/ | n. |小猫；（尤指）可爱的猫咪 |A pussycat is a cat, especially a young or small one, often used affectionately.  The term often implies a gentle and harmless nature. |例句：The kittens are such sweet little pussycats.  那些小猫真是可爱的小猫咪。

例句：She treated her cat like a spoiled pussycat, giving it the best food and a soft bed. 她把她的猫当成一只娇生惯养的小猫，给它最好的食物和柔软的床。


近义词：kitten, cat, kitty


反义词：无  (反义词取决于语境，例如与“凶猛的动物”相对)


词性：名词
