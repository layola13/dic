preaffiliate: /ˌpriːəˈfɪlieɪt/ | n. | 预先关联公司，预先加盟商 | A preaffiliate is a company or individual that is in the process of becoming an affiliate, but has not yet completed the process of formal affiliation.  They may be undergoing a trial period, waiting for approval, or still negotiating the terms of the affiliation.  They often benefit from early access to resources and training in preparation for their full affiliation. | Example:  Several preaffiliates are currently undergoing training in our new marketing platform.  几个预先关联公司目前正在接受我们新营销平台的培训。 | 近义词: prospective affiliate, potential affiliate | 反义词: affiliate
