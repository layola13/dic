craftsmanship:/ˈkræftsmənʃɪp/ | n. |工艺，技艺；技工手艺 |The skill in making something, especially by hand.  It refers to the quality of work done by a skilled craftsperson.  This includes the precision, artistry, and overall excellence of the finished product. |例句：The craftsmanship of the antique furniture is truly remarkable. 这件古董家具的工艺真是非凡。

例句：His exceptional craftsmanship is evident in every detail of his sculptures. 他的雕塑作品的每一个细节都体现了他精湛的技艺。


近义词：skill, artistry, workmanship, technique

反义词：clumsiness, shoddiness
