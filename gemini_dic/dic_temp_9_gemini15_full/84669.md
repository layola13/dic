handcraft:/ˈhændkræft/| n. v. |手工制品；手工艺；手工制作|The skillful creation of something by hand.  Handcraft can refer to the finished product or the act of making it. |例句：The artisan showed off her beautiful handcrafts.  这位工匠展示了她精美的工艺品。

例句：He enjoys handcrafting wooden toys for his grandchildren. 他喜欢为孙子们手工制作木制玩具。


近义词：handmade, handiwork, craftsmanship |反义词：mass-produced, factory-made


词性：名词和动词

They handcraft unique furniture pieces. = 他们手工制作独特的家具。
