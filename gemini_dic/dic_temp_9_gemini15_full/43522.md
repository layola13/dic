cravings:/ˈkreɪvɪŋz/| n.|渴望，热望 |强烈的欲望或需求，通常指食物或某种物质。  |例句：She had intense cravings for chocolate during her pregnancy. 她怀孕期间非常渴望吃巧克力。
例句：He struggled to overcome his cravings for nicotine. 他努力克服对尼古丁的渴望。|近义词：longings, yearnings, desires |反义词：aversion, dislike, indifference |名词
