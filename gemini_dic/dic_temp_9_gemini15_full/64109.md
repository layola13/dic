epoxy:/ˈɛpɑːksi/| n.|环氧树脂|A thermosetting resin formed by the reaction of an epoxide with a curing agent.  Epoxy resins are known for their strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals and heat. They are used in a wide variety of applications, from adhesives and coatings to composites and electronics.|例句：The bridge was reinforced with epoxy resin to increase its durability.  这座桥用环氧树脂加固以提高其耐久性。|近义词：resin, adhesive|反义词:N/A
