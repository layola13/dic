preexcursions:/ˌpriːɪkˈskɜːrʒənz/ | n. | 事先的远足；预先的旅行 |  A preexcursion is a trip or excursion that takes place before a main event or journey. It's often a preparatory or exploratory trip.  | Example: The team took a preexcursion to the mountain range to scout the best climbing route before the main expedition.  团队在主要探险之前进行了一次预先的远足，以侦察最佳攀登路线。 | 近义词: preliminary trip, reconnaissance, advance trip | 反义词: post-excursion, after-trip | 名词
