numerous:/ˈnuːmərəs/| adj. |许多的，大量的，无数的|  Numerous means very many; a large quantity. It emphasizes a large and significant number.  |There are numerous reasons why I can't go.|近义词：many, plentiful, countless, abundant |反义词：few, scarce, limited


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are numerous stars in the sky. = 天上有无数颗星星。
Numerous birds sing in the morning. = 早上有许多鸟在唱歌。

一般过去时:
Numerous problems arose during the project. = 项目期间出现了许多问题。
Numerous guests attended the wedding. = 许多宾客参加了婚礼。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

Numerous challenges had been overcome before reaching the summit. = 在到达山顶之前已经克服了许多挑战。

将来时:
Numerous difficulties will arise in the future. = 未来将会出现许多困难。
Numerous people will attend the concert. = 许多人将会参加音乐会。

虚拟语气:
If there were numerous options, I'd be happier. = 如果有很多选择，我会更快乐。

被动语态:

疑问句:
