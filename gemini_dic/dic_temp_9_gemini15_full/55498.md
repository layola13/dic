distractors:/dɪˈstræktərz/| n. | 干扰因素；转移注意力的事物 | Things that divert attention from something else, especially incorrect options in a test or misleading information.  | Example: The loud music was a major distractor during the exam.  嘈杂的音乐在考试期间是一个主要的干扰因素。 The multiple-choice questions included several plausible distractors. 多项选择题包含几个貌似合理的干扰项。|近义词：interruptions, diversions, distractions |反义词：aids, helps, focuses


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud noises distract me. = 嘈杂的声音分散我的注意力。
Many things distract her from her work. = 许多事情分散了她工作的注意力。

一般过去时:
The bright lights distracted the driver. = 刺眼的光线分散了司机的注意力。
The sudden noise distracted him from his reading. = 突然的噪音分散了他阅读的注意力。

现在完成时:
The constant interruptions have distracted me all day. =  持续不断的打断让我一整天都心不在焉。
The news reports have distracted many people from the real issues. = 新闻报道分散了许多人对真正问题的注意力。

过去完成时:
The ringing phone had distracted her from her important task. = 响铃的电话已经分散了她对重要任务的注意力。
The previous arguments had distracted the team from their goal. = 此前的争论已经分散了团队对目标的注意力。

将来时:
The upcoming event will distract many people from their daily routines. =  即将到来的事件将会分散许多人对日常生活的注意力。
The new project will distract the team from their current work. = 新项目将分散团队对当前工作的注意力。

虚拟语气:
If the music weren't so loud, it wouldn't distract me. = 如果音乐不大声，就不会分散我的注意力。
I wish the constant notifications wouldn't distract me. = 我希望持续不断的通知不会分散我的注意力。

被动语态:
He was distracted by the beautiful scenery. = 他被美丽的景色吸引了注意力。
The students were distracted by the teacher's loud voice. = 学生们被老师的大嗓门分散了注意力。

疑问句:
What distracts you the most during work? = 工作中什么最分散你的注意力？
What distracted you from finishing the project on time? = 什么分散了你的注意力，导致你没有按时完成项目？

否定句:
The quiet room didn't distract her at all. = 安静的房间一点也没有分散她的注意力。
Nothing distracted him from his goal. = 没有什么能分散他实现目标的注意力。
