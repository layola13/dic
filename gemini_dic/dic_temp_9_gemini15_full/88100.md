highlighter:/ˈhaɪˌlaɪtər/| n. |荧光笔，醒目之处|A tool used to highlight text or images, typically with a fluorescent color.  It can also refer to something that emphasizes or makes something stand out.|Example: I used a highlighter to mark the important passages in the textbook. 我用荧光笔标注了教科书中的重要段落。|近义词：marker, marker pen|反义词：dimmer, obscurer|名词
