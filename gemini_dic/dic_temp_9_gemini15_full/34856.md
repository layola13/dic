clinkstone:/ˈklɪŋkˌstoʊn/| n. |响石，石英岩|A hard, sonorous, usually dark-colored rock, often a variety of hornfels or quartzite, that rings when struck.  It is sometimes used for tools or paving.|The archaeologist found several clinkstones used as tools in the ancient burial site.|近义词：sonorous stone, ringing stone|反义词：soft stone, dull stone
