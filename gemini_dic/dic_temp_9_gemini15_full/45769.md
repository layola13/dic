cushion:/ˈkʊʃən/| n.|软垫，靠垫，缓冲物|A cushion is a soft pad, usually filled with soft material, used for sitting, lying on, or as decoration.  It can also refer to something that softens the impact of something else.|例句：I sat on a soft cushion. 我坐在一个柔软的靠垫上。

例句：The car's airbags acted as cushions in the accident.  车上的安全气囊在事故中起到了缓冲作用。

近义词：pillow, pad, buffer

反义词：hard surface, rigid support

词性：名词
