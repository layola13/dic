inaccuracies:/ɪnˈækjʊrəsiːz/ | n. | 不准确之处，错误 |  Plural of inaccuracy.  Refers to instances of being incorrect or not exact.  These errors can be in measurements, statements, accounts, or any other form of representation. | Examples: The report contained several inaccuracies regarding the company's financial performance.  报告中包含几处关于公司财务业绩的不准确之处。 There were many inaccuracies in his historical account. 他的历史记载有很多不准确之处。 |近义词：mistakes, errors, imperfections, faults | 反义词：accuracy, precision, correctness | 名词
