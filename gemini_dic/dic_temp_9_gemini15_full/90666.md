humbled:/ˈhʌmbəld/| adj.|谦逊的；感到羞愧的|Feeling humble; showing humility; reduced in pride or arrogance; feeling shame or humiliation.|例句：I felt humbled by their generosity.  他们的慷慨让我感到谦卑。

例句：She was humbled by the experience of losing her job.  失去工作的那次经历让她感到很羞愧。


近义词：humble, meek, modest, ashamed, chastened|反义词：proud, arrogant, haughty, boastful|形容词

词源:
