porwigle:/ˈpɔːrwɪɡəl/ | n. |水虿（蜻蜓的幼虫）|A porwigle is the aquatic larval stage of a dragonfly or damselfly.  They are characterized by their elongated bodies, six legs, and the ability to breathe underwater using gills.  Porwigles are voracious predators, feeding on other aquatic insects and small creatures. |The pond was teeming with porwigles.  池塘里挤满了水虿。
