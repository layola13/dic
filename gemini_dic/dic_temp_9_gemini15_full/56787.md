dops:/dɒps/| n.|滴剂，剂量|Drops are small amounts of liquid.  The doctor prescribed eye drops for my infection. |近义词：doses, portions|反义词：large amounts


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The doctor prescribes eye drops twice a day. = 医生每天开两次眼药水。
He takes his medicine in drops. = 他用滴剂服用药。


一般过去时:
She dropped the medicine bottle. = 她掉了药瓶。
The nurse gave him the drops. = 护士给他滴了药。


现在完成时:
They have administered the drops successfully. = 他们已经成功地滴了药。
I have used all the drops. = 我已经用完了所有的滴剂。


过去完成时:
He had taken all the drops before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，他已经服用了所有滴剂。
She had dropped the bottle before I could catch it. = 我还没来得及接住，她就掉瓶子了。


将来时:
The doctor will prescribe drops for your condition. = 医生会根据你的病情开滴剂。
I will administer the drops carefully. = 我会小心地滴药。


虚拟语气:
If I had more drops, I would give you some. = 如果我有更多滴剂，我会给你一些。
I wish I had remembered to bring my eye drops. = 我真希望记得带眼药水。


被动语态:
The drops were administered by a nurse. = 滴剂是由护士滴的。
The medicine is given in drops. = 药物以滴剂的形式服用。


疑问句:
How many drops should I take? = 我应该服用多少滴？
Are these drops safe for children? = 这些滴剂对儿童安全吗？


否定句:
I didn't take my drops today. = 我今天没有服用滴剂。
The doctor did not prescribe any drops. = 医生没有开任何滴剂。


PHRASE->
eye drops 眼药水
ear drops 耳药水
nose drops 鼻药水
cough drops 咳嗽药片(虽然是药片，但通常以“drops”称呼)
blood drops 血滴
