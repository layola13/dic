refuseed:/rɪˈfjuːzd/| v.|拒绝，谢绝|To decline or reject (something offered, proposed, or requested).|He refused the offer of a job.|近义词：reject, decline, deny|反义词：accept, agree


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refuses to cooperate. = 他拒绝合作。
They refuse my request. = 他们拒绝我的请求。


一般过去时:
She refused my invitation. = 她拒绝了我的邀请。
I refused to believe it. = 我拒绝相信这件事。


现在完成时:
He has refused to comment on the matter. = 他拒绝就此事发表评论。
They have refused all offers of help. = 他们拒绝了一切援助。


过去完成时:
She had refused to see him before. = 她以前拒绝见过他。
They had refused the deal earlier. = 他们之前已经拒绝了这笔交易。


将来时:
He will refuse to sign the contract. = 他将拒绝签署合同。
They will refuse any further negotiation. = 他们将拒绝任何进一步的谈判。


虚拟语气:
If he refused, what would you do? = 如果他拒绝了，你会怎么办？
I wish he hadn't refused my help. = 我真希望他没有拒绝我的帮助。


被动语态:
His request was refused. = 他的请求被拒绝了。
The proposal was refused by the committee. = 该提案被委员会否决了。


疑问句:
Will they refuse to help us? = 他们会拒绝帮助我们吗？
Did she refuse your offer? = 她拒绝了你的提议吗？


否定句:
He didn't refuse the offer. = 他没有拒绝那个提议。
She wouldn't refuse a helping hand. = 她不会拒绝帮助。


PHRASE->
refuse to comply = 拒绝遵守
refuse point-blank = 断然拒绝
refuse entry = 拒绝入内
refuse treatment = 拒绝治疗
refuse to acknowledge = 拒绝承认
