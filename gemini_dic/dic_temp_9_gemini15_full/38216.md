comput: /kəmˈpjuːt/ | v. | 计算，估算 | To calculate or reckon mathematically.  | Example:  The computer computed the answer in a fraction of a second.  计算机在极短时间内计算出了答案。  | 近义词: calculate, reckon, figure | 反义词: estimate (in the sense of a rough guess, not precise calculation)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He computes the area of the circle. 他计算圆的面积。
They compute the total cost. 他们计算总成本。

一般过去时:
She computed the results accurately. 她准确地计算出了结果。
We computed the distance between two points. 我们计算了两点之间的距离。

现在完成时:
I have computed the final score. 我已经计算出最终得分。
They have computed the average. 他们已经计算出平均值。

过去完成时:
He had computed the trajectory before launch. 他在发射前已经计算出了轨迹。
She had computed the probabilities. 她已经计算了概率。

将来时:
We will compute the statistical significance. 我们将计算统计显著性。
They will compute the new budget. 他们将计算新的预算。

虚拟语气:
If I computed correctly, the answer is 10. 如果我计算正确，答案是10。
I wish I had computed the risk more carefully. 我希望我当时更仔细地计算过风险。

被动语态:
The answer was computed by the program. 答案是由程序计算出来的。
The total cost is being computed. 总成本正在计算中。

疑问句:
How did you compute the value? 你是怎么计算这个值的？
What formula did they use to compute the result? 他们用什么公式计算结果？

否定句:
I could not compute the solution. 我无法计算出解决方案。
They did not compute the interest correctly. 他们没有正确计算利息。
