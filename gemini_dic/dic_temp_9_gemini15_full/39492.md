connotes:/kənˈnoʊt/| vt.|暗示，意味着|To imply or suggest (an idea or feeling) in addition to the literal or primary meaning.  Connotation refers to the associated or secondary meaning of a word or expression in addition to its explicit or primary meaning.|The word "home" connotes warmth and security.  这个词“家”暗示着温暖和安全。|suggests, implies, signifies|denotes, signifies (in a purely literal sense)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The word "autumn" connotes a sense of melancholy. = “秋天”这个词暗示着一种忧郁的感觉。
This image connotes feelings of peace and tranquility. = 这幅图像暗示着和平与宁静的感觉。


一般过去时:
The painting connoted a sense of loss and despair. = 这幅画暗示着一种失落和绝望的感觉。
Her silence connoted disapproval. = 她的沉默暗示着不赞成。


现在完成时:
His actions have connoted a lack of respect for authority. = 他的行为暗示着他缺乏对权威的尊重。
The novel's ending has connoted a sense of ambiguity. = 这部小说的结尾暗示着一种模棱两可的感觉。


过去完成时:
The old house had connoted a sense of mystery before it was renovated. = 这栋旧房子在翻新之前暗示着一种神秘感。
His previous behavior had connoted that he was unreliable. = 他之前的行为暗示着他不可靠。


将来时:
The new marketing campaign will connote a sense of modernity. = 新的营销活动将暗示一种现代感。
This design will connote elegance and sophistication. = 这个设计将暗示优雅和精致。


虚拟语气:
If the poem connoted hope, it would be more uplifting. = 如果这首诗暗示着希望，它就会更令人振奋。
If the advertisement connoted luxury, it might appeal to a wealthier audience. = 如果广告暗示着奢华，它可能会吸引更富裕的观众。


被动语态:
A sense of urgency was connoted by the news report. = 新闻报道暗示了一种紧迫感。
Peace and harmony were connoted by the music. = 音乐暗示着和平与和谐。


疑问句:
What does this symbol connote? = 这个符号暗示着什么？
What feelings does this painting connote? = 这幅画暗示着什么感觉？


否定句:
The word "cheap" doesn't necessarily connote poor quality. = “便宜”这个词并不一定暗示着质量差。
The evidence did not connote guilt. = 证据并没有暗示罪责。


PHRASE->
connotes something = 暗示某事
strongly connotes = 强烈暗示
clearly connotes = 清晰地暗示
implicitly connotes = 隐含地暗示
connotes a sense of = 暗示一种…的感觉
