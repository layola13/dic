guerrero:/ɡɛˈrɛroʊ/| n. |战士，勇士，格雷罗（墨西哥地名）|A guerrero is a warrior or fighter, often skilled in combat.  The word can also refer to a person who is brave and strong.  It's also a state in Mexico.|例句：He fought like a true guerrero. 他像个真正的战士一样战斗。
The state of Guerrero is known for its beautiful beaches. 格雷罗州以其美丽的海滩而闻名。|近义词：warrior, fighter, soldier, combatant |反义词：coward, pacifist
