This term often refers to the capacity of a system (like a language model or a neural network) to produce novel outputs, rather than simply repeating learned patterns.  It implies creativity and innovation. |Example: The generativeness of the new AI model is impressive; it can create realistic images from text descriptions.  这个新的AI模型的生成能力令人印象深刻；它可以根据文本描述生成逼真的图像。|近义词：productivity, creativity, originality, inventiveness |反义词：sterility, unoriginality, repetitiveness |名词
