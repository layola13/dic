jabbing:/ˈdʒæbɪŋ/ | v. | 刺；戳；猛击；(言语)尖刻地批评 | The act of jabbing or thrusting something sharply.  It can refer to a physical action or a verbal attack. | 例句：He was jabbing the needle into the patient's arm. 他正将针刺入病人的手臂。

例句：Her criticism was sharp and jabbing, leaving me feeling quite hurt. 她的批评尖锐而刻薄，让我感到很受伤。


近义词：prodding, poking, thrusting, stabbing, needling


反义词：caressing, soothing, comforting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jabs the air with his finger. = 他用手指戳着空气。
She jabs at the keyboard furiously. = 她愤怒地猛击键盘。


一般过去时:
He jabbed the apple with a fork. = 他用叉子戳苹果。
She jabbed him in the ribs. = 她戳了他的肋骨。


现在完成时:
He has jabbed the ball several times. = 他已经多次猛击球了。
She has jabbed at the problem all day. = 她整天都在努力解决这个问题。


过去完成时:
He had jabbed the painting before he realized it was valuable. = 他在意识到这幅画很值钱之前就戳了它。
She had jabbed at the dough many times before it rose. = 面团发起来之前，她已经多次戳它了。


将来时:
He will jab the button to start the machine. = 他将按下按钮启动机器。
She will jab at the wasp to get it away from her. = 她将戳黄蜂以将其赶走。


虚拟语气:
If he jabbed the hornet's nest, he would be stung. = 如果他捅马蜂窝，他会被蛰。
I wish I hadn't jabbed him so hard. = 我真希望我没有那么用力地戳他。


被动语态:
The apple was jabbed with a fork. = 苹果被叉子戳了。
The painting was jabbed several times. = 画被戳了好几次。


疑问句:
Did he jab the needle into the patient's arm? = 他是否将针刺入病人的手臂？
Was she jabbing at the keyboard furiously? = 她是否愤怒地猛击键盘？


否定句:
He didn't jab the ball hard enough. = 他没有用力地猛击球。
She didn't jab at the problem at all. = 她根本没有努力解决这个问题。


PHRASE->
jab at:  猛击；猛刺；尖刻地批评
jab away:  持续地戳；持续地猛击
jab something into:  将某物猛插进
jab something out:  迅速地画出；粗略地写出
jab someone with:  用某物戳某人
