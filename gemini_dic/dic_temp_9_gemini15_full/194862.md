semi-coke:/ˈsɛmiˌkoʊk/| n. |半焦|A fuel produced by the incomplete combustion of coal, containing more volatile matter than coke. It is a porous, intermediate product between coal and coke, possessing properties of both.  It is used as a fuel and reducing agent in various industrial processes.|例句：Semi-coke is used in the production of iron and steel. 半焦用于钢铁生产。|例句：The plant uses semi-coke as a fuel source for its kilns. 该工厂使用半焦作为其窑炉的燃料来源。|近义词：coal, char|反义词：coke (in the sense of being a more completely processed product)
