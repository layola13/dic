devolutions:/ˌdɛvəˈluːʃənz/| n. |权力下放；移交；权力交接；衰退|The gradual transfer of power or authority from a central authority to a lower level.  It often refers to the process of delegating responsibilities or the decline of power or influence. |例句：The devolution of power to regional governments led to increased local autonomy.权力下放给地方政府导致了地方自治的增强。

例句：The company's devolution of decision-making to its branch offices improved efficiency. 公司将决策权下放给分公司提高了效率。


近义词：transfer, delegation, relinquishment, decline, decay |反义词：centralization, concentration, ascension, rise
