oedemata:/ˌiːdɪˈmeɪtə/| n. |水肿（复数）|Swelling of soft tissues due to fluid accumulation.  Oedemata are a symptom of various medical conditions, often indicating impaired lymphatic drainage or venous return.|Example:  The patient presented with bilateral leg oedemata and shortness of breath.|近义词：swellings, edema (singular)|反义词：None directly opposite, but "normal tissue hydration" could be considered a contrasting state.|
