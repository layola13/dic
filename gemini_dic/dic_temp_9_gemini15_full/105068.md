kerbside:/ˈkɜːrbˌsaɪd/| n. |路边；(英式英语)人行道旁 |The side of a road next to the pavement.  It is primarily used in British English.  In American English, "curbside" is more common.|例句：I parked my car on the kerbside. = 我把车停在了路边。

例句：He waited for the bus on the kerbside. = 他在路边等公交车。


近义词：curbside (AmE), roadside, street side|反义词：mid-road, centre of the road|词性:名词
