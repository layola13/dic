pumped-up:/pʌmpt ʌp/| adj.|兴奋的；充满能量的；夸大的；膨胀的|Excited, full of energy, inflated, exaggerated.  The adjective "pumped-up" describes something or someone filled with excitement and energy, often to an extreme degree. It can also describe something that has been artificially inflated or exaggerated.|例句：He was pumped up before the big game. 他在大型比赛前非常兴奋。
She gave a pumped-up speech. 她发表了一篇慷慨激昂的演讲。|近义词：excited, energized, hyped, inflated, overblown |反义词：depressed, lethargic, deflated, understated
