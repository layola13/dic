secondhand:/ˈsɛkəndhænd/ | adj., n. | 二手的，旧的；二手货 |

英文解析:
Used or owned by someone else before being bought by the current owner; not new.  As a noun, it refers to goods that have been previously owned.

例句:
The car is in good condition for a secondhand vehicle. = 这辆车作为二手车来说状况良好。

I bought a secondhand book for a bargain price. = 我以便宜的价格买了一本二手书。


近义词:used, pre-owned, second-hand (same spelling, used as adjective)

反义词:new, unused, firsthand


词源:
The word combines "second" and "hand."  The "hand" refers to the person who owned the item previously.  It implies a transfer of ownership from one person's possession to another's.
