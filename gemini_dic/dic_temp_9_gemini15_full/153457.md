pennington:/ˈpɛnɪŋtən/| n. |彭宁顿（姓氏）|Pennington is a surname of English origin. It is a locational name from places called Pennington in Lancashire and Cumbria, England. These places derive their names from the Old English personal name *Penne* + *tun*, meaning "Penna's farmstead or settlement".|The Pennington family has a long and distinguished history.  |近义词:  (无直接近义词，因其为姓氏)|反义词: (无)|名词
