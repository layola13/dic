intuit:/ɪnˈtjuːɪt/ | v. |凭直觉知道；领悟；预感 | to understand something instinctively, without the need for conscious reasoning | She intuited that something was wrong. 她凭直觉知道出了问题。| understand, perceive, sense | ignore, disregard | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I intuit the answer. = 我凭直觉知道答案。
She intuits her client's needs. = 她能直觉地了解客户的需求。

一般过去时:
He intuited the solution to the problem. = 他凭直觉找到了问题的解决方案。
I intuited that she was unhappy. = 我预感到她很不开心。

现在完成时:
I have intuited the truth behind the story. = 我已经凭直觉明白了故事背后的真相。
She has intuited the danger ahead. = 她已经预感到危险即将来临。

过去完成时:
He had intuited the consequences of his actions. = 他事先就预感到自己行为的后果。
We had intuited that the project would fail. = 我们预感到这个项目会失败。

将来时:
I will intuit the best course of action. = 我将凭直觉选择最佳行动方案。
She will intuit the hidden meaning of the painting. = 她将领悟到这幅画的隐藏含义。

虚拟语气:
If I intuited his intentions, I would have acted differently. = 如果我预感到他的意图，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had intuited the market crash. = 我真希望我预感到股市崩盘。

被动语态:
The answer was intuited by the detective. = 侦探凭直觉得到了答案。(被动语态使用较少)
The solution could not be intuited. = 解决方案无法凭直觉得到。(被动语态使用较少)

疑问句:
Do you intuit anything unusual? = 你感觉有什么异常吗？
Can she intuit the meaning of his words? = 她能领悟到他话中的意思吗？

否定句:
I couldn't intuit her true feelings. = 我无法感知到她的真实感受。
He didn't intuit the danger. = 他没有预感到危险。


PHRASE->
intuit the truth = 直觉感受到真相

intuit the meaning = 领悟其含义
