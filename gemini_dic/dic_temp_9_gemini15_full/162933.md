prechemical:/ˌpriːˈkɛmɪkəl/| adj.|前化学的；化学之前的|Relating to the period or conditions before the development of chemistry as a science.  Describes processes or substances that exist or occur before chemical reactions or transformations take place.|Example:  The prechemical processes involved in the formation of the planet were complex and largely unknown.  前行星形成过程中涉及的前化学过程复杂且很大程度上未知。|近义词：protochemical, precursory|反义词：postchemical
