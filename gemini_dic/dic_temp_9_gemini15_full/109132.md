legionnaires:/ˈliːdʒənɛrz/| n. |军团士兵；军团成员；（尤指）退伍军人|A large group of soldiers, especially Roman soldiers.  The term often refers to a group of veterans or those who have served together.  It can also be used figuratively.|例句：The legionnaires marched proudly through the streets.  军团士兵们骄傲地穿过街道。
例句：He's a member of the American Legion, a group for legionnaires. 他是美国退伍军人协会的成员，一个退伍军人团体。|近义词：soldiers, veterans, troops|反义词：civilians|名词
