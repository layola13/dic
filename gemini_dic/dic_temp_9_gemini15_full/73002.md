footage:/ˈfʊtɪdʒ/ | n. |镜头画面；影片片段 |Recorded images, especially those taken by a camera.  Footage refers to the recorded material itself, not the final edited product.  It can be raw or unedited. |例句：The documentary included some incredible footage of the wildlife. 这部纪录片包含了一些令人难以置信的野生动物镜头画面。

The police released security footage of the robbery. 警方公布了抢劫案的监控录像。

|近义词：film, video, recording, clips |反义词：N/A |词性：名词
