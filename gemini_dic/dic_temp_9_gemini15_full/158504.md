plenitudinarian:/ˌplɛnɪˈtuːdɪnɛəriən/| n. |过着丰裕生活的人，享乐主义者 |A person who lives in or seeks fullness of life, especially in terms of sensual pleasures.  Someone who enjoys a life of abundance and luxury. |He was a confirmed plenitudinarian, dedicated to the pursuit of pleasure and comfort.|近义词：hedonist, epicurean, sybarite|反义词：ascetic, puritan, minimalist|名词
