collar:/ˈkɒlə(r)/ /ˈkɑːlər/| n. v.|衣领；项圈；颈圈；抓住；逮捕|A collar is a band of fabric or other material that forms the upper part of a garment around the neck.  It can also refer to a similar band around the neck of an animal. As a verb, it means to seize or arrest someone.|例句：He wore a crisp white collar with his shirt. 他穿着衬衫配了一个挺括的白色衣领。|例句：The police collared the suspect near the bank. 警察在银行附近逮捕了嫌疑犯。|近义词：band, neckline, seize, apprehend|反义词：release
