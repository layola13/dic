nuggets:/ˈnʌɡɪts/| n.|金块；块；小块；（尤指炸的）鸡块|A nugget is a small, irregularly shaped lump of a precious metal, especially gold, or a small piece of something, especially food.|例句：He found a gold nugget worth thousands of dollars. 他发现了一块价值数千美元的金块。

例句：We ordered a plate of chicken nuggets for the kids. 我们为孩子们点了一盘鸡块。


近义词：lump, piece, chunk, bit
