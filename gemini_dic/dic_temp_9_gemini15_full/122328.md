mixabilities: /ˌmɪksəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |混合性，可混合性 | The capacity or potential for different things to be mixed together or combined.  This refers to the degree to which substances, materials, or even ideas can be blended or integrated without negative consequences.  It can encompass physical mixing, chemical reactions, or even social and cultural interactions. | Example:  The mixabilities of the paints were tested before they were used in the mural.  = 在壁画中使用颜料之前，对其混合性进行了测试。  The mixabilities of different cultures were a key topic of the conference. = 不同文化的融合性是会议的一个关键主题。 | 近义词：compatibility, blendability, miscibility | 反义词：incompatibility, immiscibility
