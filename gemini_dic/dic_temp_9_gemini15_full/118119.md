menopause:/ˈmɛnəpɔːz/ | n. | 绝经期 | The natural cessation of menstruation in women, typically occurring between the ages of 45 and 55.  It is marked by a decline in ovarian function and hormonal changes. | Example:  She experienced hot flashes and mood swings during menopause. 她绝经期间经历了潮热和情绪波动。|近义词：climacteric | 反义词：menarche
