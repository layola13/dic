paydays:/ˈpeɪdeɪz/| n. |发薪日；支付工资的日子|The days on which employees receive their wages or salaries.  Paydays are often eagerly anticipated by workers.|例句：I'm looking forward to payday so I can finally buy that new game. 我期待着发薪日，这样我就能买到那个新游戏了。


例句：My payday is always the last Friday of the month. 我的发薪日总是每月的最后一个星期五。


近义词：pay day, salary day, salary payment day|反义词：payless day(无薪日)


词性:名词
