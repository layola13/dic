chalets:/ʃæˈleɪz/| n.|木屋；小屋|A chalet is a type of building, typically a wooden house, especially one in a mountainous area, often used as a holiday home.  They are characterized by their sloping roofs, often with overhanging eaves.|例句：We rented a charming chalet in the Swiss Alps. 我们在瑞士阿尔卑斯山租了一间迷人的小屋。|例句：The chalet offered stunning views of the valley. 这间小屋提供了令人惊叹的山谷景色。|近义词：cabin, lodge, cottage|反义词：skyscraper, high-rise
