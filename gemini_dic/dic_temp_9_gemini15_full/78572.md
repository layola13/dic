/dʒiːz/| n. |美国大兵；士兵；（非正式）家伙|American soldiers; soldiers; (informal) guys |例句：The GI's fought bravely in the war.  美国大兵们在战争中英勇作战。|例句：Those GI's were known for their resilience. 那些士兵以其韧性而闻名。|近义词：soldier, serviceman, trooper|反义词：civilian
