nutriceuticals: /ˌnuːtriːuˈtɪkəlz/ | n. |营养保健品，营养药物 | Food or food supplements that provide health benefits beyond basic nutrition.  Nutriceuticals are often marketed as having preventative or therapeutic effects on various health conditions.  They are distinct from pharmaceuticals, which are drugs that are rigorously tested and regulated. | Example:  Many people take nutriceuticals like vitamins and probiotics to improve their overall health. 许多人服用维生素和益生菌等营养保健品来改善整体健康状况。

|近义词：dietary supplements, functional foods, nutraceuticals  |反义词：placebo, harmful substances |
