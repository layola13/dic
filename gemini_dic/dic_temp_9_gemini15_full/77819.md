generating: /ˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 产生；生成；引起 |  Producing or creating something.  |例句：The new factory is generating hundreds of jobs.  这家新工厂正在创造数百个就业机会。 |例句：The storm is generating strong winds.  这场风暴正在产生强风。 |近义词：producing, creating, causing, making |反义词：destroying, consuming, ending


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine is generating power. = 发动机正在产生动力。
She is generating new ideas for the project. = 她正在为这个项目产生新的想法。

一般过去时:
The turbine generated enough electricity for the whole town. = 涡轮机产生了足够全镇使用的电力。
The earthquake generated a tsunami. = 地震引发了海啸。

现在完成时:
We have generated a lot of waste this year. = 今年我们产生了大量的垃圾。
The company has generated significant profits. = 公司已经获得了可观的利润。

过去完成时:
The volcano had generated a large amount of ash before it erupted. = 火山爆发前已经喷发出大量的火山灰。
They had generated a plan before the meeting. = 他们会议前已经制定了一个计划。

将来时:
The solar panels will generate enough energy for our home. = 太阳能电池板将产生足够的能量供我们家庭使用。
This new technology will generate many new opportunities. = 这项新技术将创造许多新的机会。

虚拟语气:
If we invested more, we could generate higher profits. = 如果我们投入更多，我们可以获得更高的利润。
I wish we could generate more interest in the project. = 我希望我们可以对这个项目产生更多兴趣。

被动语态:
Electricity is generated by the power plant. = 电力是由发电厂产生的。
New ideas are generated through brainstorming. = 新的想法是通过头脑风暴产生的。

疑问句:
How does the system generate reports? = 这个系统是如何生成报告的？
What factors generate customer loyalty? = 哪些因素会产生客户忠诚度？

否定句:
The machine is not generating enough heat. = 这台机器产生的热量不足。
The campaign did not generate the expected results. = 这场运动没有产生预期的结果。
