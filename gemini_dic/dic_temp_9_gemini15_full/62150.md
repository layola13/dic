enclose:/ɪnˈkloʊz/| vt.|包围，封闭，装入|To surround or shut in on all sides; to put something in an envelope or container.|例句：The garden was enclosed by a high wall. 花园被一堵高墙围着。

例句：Please enclose a check with your order. 请在订单中附上支票。|近义词：surround, encompass, contain|反义词：open, expose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encloses the letter in an envelope. 他把信装进信封。
The fence encloses the garden. 栅栏围着花园。

一般过去时:
She enclosed a photograph with her application. 她把照片附在申请书里。
They enclosed the area with barbed wire. 他们用铁丝网围起了那个区域。

现在完成时:
He has enclosed all the necessary documents. 他已经附上了所有必要的文档。
The police have enclosed the crime scene. 警方已经封锁了犯罪现场。

过去完成时:
She had enclosed the money in a sealed envelope. 她已将钱装在一个密封的信封里。
They had enclosed the package before shipping it. 他们寄送包裹前已将包裹封好。

将来时:
I will enclose a copy of my resume with my application. 我会在申请材料中附上我的简历副本。
They will enclose the park with a new fence. 他们将用新的栅栏围住公园。

虚拟语气:
If I enclosed the check, they would process my order immediately. 如果我附上支票，他们会立即处理我的订单。
I wish I had enclosed the instructions with the package. 我真希望我把说明书和包裹一起寄出。

被动语态:
The garden is enclosed by a tall fence. 花园被一堵高墙围着。
The package was enclosed in a box. 包裹被装在一个盒子里。

疑问句:
Did you enclose the warranty with the product? 你有把保修单和产品一起寄出吗？
Is the area enclosed by a wall? 这个区域是被墙围起来的吗？

否定句:
He did not enclose the return address on the envelope. 他没有在信封上写上回邮地址。
The document was not enclosed with the package. 文件没有和包裹一起寄出。



PHRASE->
enclose with:  用...包围
enclose in: 装入...
enclose under:  置于...之下并封闭
enclose a check: 附上支票
enclose the area:  包围区域
