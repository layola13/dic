self-disdains:/ˌsɛlf dɪsˈdeɪnz/| v. |自轻自贱，自我鄙视 |To feel contempt or lack of respect for oneself.  It implies a negative self-image and a lack of self-worth.|He self-disdained his lack of achievement.|近义词：self-deprecates, belittles oneself, despises oneself |反义词：self-love, self-respect, self-esteem |动词
