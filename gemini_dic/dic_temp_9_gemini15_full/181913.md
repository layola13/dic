remakers:/riːˈmeɪkərz/| n. |重制者，翻拍者|People or companies that create new versions of existing works, such as films, television shows, or video games.  They often update the original for a modern audience or explore a new perspective.|Example: The remakers of the classic film modernized the special effects while retaining the original story's heart.|近义词：re-creators, revisers |反义词：originators, creators (of the original work)
