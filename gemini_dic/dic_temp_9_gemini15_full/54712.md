disowned:/dɪsˈoʊnd/ | vt. | 否认；与...断绝关系；不承认 | To refuse to acknowledge or accept as one's own; to reject or abandon (a person or thing). | 例句：He disowned his son after he was arrested for theft. 他儿子因盗窃被捕后，他和他断绝了父子关系。 | 例句：The company disowned the product after several reports of malfunction.  在几起产品故障报告之后，公司否认了该产品。 | 近义词：reject, repudiate, renounce, abandon | 反义词：acknowledge, accept, claim, own


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He disowns his past mistakes. 他否认他过去的错误。
She disowns her family's controversial views. 她否认她家人的有争议的观点。


一般过去时:
My father disowned me after I married against his wishes. 我违背了他的意愿结婚后，我父亲与我断绝了关系。
The artist disowned the painting after discovering it was a forgery. 艺术家在发现那幅画是赝品后，否认了那幅画。


现在完成时:
She has disowned her inheritance after learning about its origins.  在她了解到遗产的来源后，她已经放弃了继承权。
The government has disowned the controversial policy. 政府已经否认了这项有争议的政策。


过去完成时:
They had disowned their son long before he contacted them again.  在他再次联系他们很久以前，他们就已经和他断绝了关系。
The company had disowned the faulty product before the lawsuit was filed. 在诉讼提起之前，公司就已经否认了有缺陷的产品。


将来时:
I fear he will disown his daughter if she continues to defy him. 我担心如果他的女儿继续违抗他，他会与她断绝关系。
The company will likely disown the campaign if it backfires. 如果这个活动适得其反，公司很可能会否认它。


虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would disown my irresponsible child. 如果我是他，我会与我那个不负责任的孩子断绝关系。
I wish I had disowned him sooner. 我真希望我早点和他断绝关系。


被动语态:
The painting was disowned by the artist after it was proven to be a fake.  在证明这幅画是赝品之后，艺术家否认了这幅画。
His claim was disowned by the court. 他的说法被法院驳回了。


疑问句:
Would you disown your child if they committed a serious crime? 如果你的孩子犯了重罪，你会和他断绝关系吗？
Will the company disown the product after the recall?  召回后，公司会否认该产品吗？


否定句:
He did not disown his son despite his many flaws.  尽管他儿子有很多缺点，但他并没有和他断绝关系。
The government did not disown the report, despite the criticisms.  尽管受到了批评，政府并没有否认这份报告。
