investment-banking:/ɪnˈvɛstmənt ˈbæŋkɪŋ/| n. |投资银行业务|The business of investment banks, which typically involves underwriting securities and providing financial advisory services to corporations and governments.  Investment banking is a complex field requiring specialized knowledge and skills.|例句：Many investment banking firms are based in major financial centers such as New York and London.  许多投资银行都位于纽约和伦敦等主要金融中心。|例句：The role of investment banking in facilitating mergers and acquisitions is crucial. 投资银行在促进并购中的作用至关重要。|近义词：financial advisory services, securities underwriting|反义词：retail banking
