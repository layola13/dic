quasi-poetic: /ˈkwɑːzaɪˈpoʊɪtɪk/ | adj. | 类似诗歌的；略带诗意的 |  Having qualities resembling poetry; somewhat poetic.  The writing style, though not strictly poetry, possessed a quasi-poetic quality.  | 例句：His prose, while technically accurate, possessed a quasi-poetic grace. 他的散文，虽然在技术上准确无误，却具有诗意的优雅。  |近义词：poetical, lyrical, evocative, figurative | 反义词：prosaic, literal, plain
