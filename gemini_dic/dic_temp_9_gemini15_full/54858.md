displaying: /dɪˈspleɪɪŋ/ | v. | 显示；展示；表现 | The act of showing or presenting something to view.  It implies making something visible or noticeable.  It can refer to visual displays, emotional displays, or displays of skill or talent. |例句：The museum is displaying a collection of ancient artifacts. = 博物馆正在展出一批古代文物。

The child was displaying signs of anxiety. = 这个孩子表现出焦虑的迹象。


近义词：showing, exhibiting, presenting, demonstrating, revealing | 反义词：concealing, hiding, obscuring | 词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is displaying his artwork at the gallery. = 他正在画廊展出他的艺术作品。
The store is displaying new products in the window. = 商店橱窗里展示着新产品。


一般过去时:
She displayed great courage during the crisis. = 在危机期间，她表现出极大的勇气。
They displayed the winning entry prominently. = 他们显眼地展示了获奖作品。


现在完成时:
He has displayed remarkable talent throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都展现了非凡的天赋。
The company has displayed a commitment to sustainability. = 公司一直致力于可持续发展。


过去完成时:
They had displayed the posters before the event. = 他们在活动之前已经张贴了海报。
She had displayed her paintings several times before. = 她以前已经展出过几次她的画作了。


将来时:
The artist will be displaying his latest paintings next month. = 艺术家下个月将展出他的最新画作。
The company will display its new technology at the conference. = 公司将在会议上展示其新技术。


虚拟语气:
If he displayed more confidence, he would succeed. = 如果他表现出更多自信，他就会成功。
I wish he displayed more interest in his studies. = 我希望他对他的学习表现出更多兴趣。


被动语态:
The painting was displayed in a prominent position. = 这幅画被摆放在显眼的位置。
The results were displayed on the screen. = 结果显示在屏幕上。


疑问句:
Is the museum displaying any new exhibits? = 博物馆正在展出任何新的展品吗？
Is he displaying any symptoms of illness? = 他表现出任何疾病症状吗？


否定句:
The shop wasn't displaying any sale items. = 这家商店没有展示任何特卖商品。
He didn't display any emotion. = 他没有表现出任何情绪。
