misplants:/ˈmɪsplænts/ | v. | 误植；种植错误 | To plant something in the wrong place or incorrectly.  This word is a relatively rare and informal usage, more likely to be found in gardening or horticultural contexts.  It combines the prefix "mis-" (wrongly) with "plants." | Example:  The gardener misplanted the tulips, putting them in the shade instead of the sun. 花园工人把郁金香种错了地方，把它们种在了阴凉处而不是阳光下。| 近义词: misplace, wrongly plant | 反义词: correctly plant, plant properly | 动词
