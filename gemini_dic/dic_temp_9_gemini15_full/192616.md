scuppers:/ˈskʌpərz/ | n. |排水孔；(船舷上的)排水沟；毁坏；破坏 |  Channels or openings in the side of a ship or boat to drain away water;  something that ruins or spoils something else. |例句：The scuppers were clogged with debris.  排水孔被碎屑堵住了。

The storm scuppered our plans for a picnic.  暴风雨破坏了我们野餐的计划。|近义词：drains, gutters, spoil, ruin |反义词：repair, preserve
