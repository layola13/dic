jobcentres:/ˈdʒɒbˌsɛntəz/ | n. |就业中心 |Jobcentres are government-funded agencies that provide assistance to job seekers, such as job placement services, training programs, and career advice.  They aim to help unemployed individuals find employment and improve their employment prospects. |Example:  He visited the local jobcentre to look for employment opportunities. 他去当地的就业中心寻找就业机会。|近义词:employment agencies, career services |反义词: N/A
