combing:/ˈkoʊmɪŋ/| v.|梳理；仔细搜寻|The act of passing a comb through (hair) or (something similar) to straighten or arrange it; a thorough search or examination.|例句：She spent hours combing her long hair. 她花了几个小时梳理她长长的头发。

例句：The police combed the area for clues. 警察仔细搜查了该地区寻找线索。

近义词：brushing, searching, scanning|反义词：tangling, neglecting
