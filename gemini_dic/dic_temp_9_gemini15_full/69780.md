fermentation:/ˌfɜːrmɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 发酵；酝酿 | The process by which microorganisms, such as yeast or bacteria, break down an organic substance, such as sugar, in the absence of oxygen, producing carbon dioxide, alcohol, or other by-products. This process is widely used in food production, such as brewing beer and making wine and yogurt.  | Example: The fermentation of grapes produces wine.  葡萄的发酵产生葡萄酒。

The fermentation of dough makes bread rise. 面团的发酵使面包膨胀。|  近义词：decomposition, brewing | 反义词：sterilization, pasteurization |  词性: 名词
