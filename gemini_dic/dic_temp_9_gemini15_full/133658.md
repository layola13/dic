nonfortifiable: /ˌnɒnˈfɔːrtɪfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 不可设防的，无法设防的 |  Incapable of being fortified; unable to be strengthened against attack.  The terrain was deemed nonfortifiable, making the position vulnerable.  | 近义词: undefendable, vulnerable, exposed | 反义词: fortifiable, defensible


例句：

The city's location on the open plain rendered it nonfortifiable.  = 这座城市位于开阔的平原上，使其无法设防。

The enemy considered the position nonfortifiable due to the lack of natural barriers. = 由于缺乏天然屏障，敌人认为该阵地无法设防。


词源:

The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "fortifiable," which itself derives from the verb "fortify" (to strengthen or protect).  "Fortify" ultimately comes from the Latin word "fortis" meaning "strong."
