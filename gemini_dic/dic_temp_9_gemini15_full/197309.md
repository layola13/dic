shatterers: /ˈʃætərərz/ | n. | 粉碎者；破坏者 | Those who shatter or destroy something, either physically or metaphorically.  The word refers to people or things that cause destruction or breakdown. |例句：The earthquake was a shatterer of homes and lives. 地震摧毁了房屋和生命。The scandal proved to be a shatterer of reputations. 这场丑闻毁掉了许多人的名声。|近义词：destroyers, breakers, demolishers, ruiners |反义词：builders, creators, preservers, restorers |名词
