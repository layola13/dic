potency:/ˈpoʊtənsi/| n. |效力；效能；潜能 |The power or capacity to produce an effect.  It refers to the strength or effectiveness of something, often in a medical or pharmacological context, but can also apply to other areas.  |例句：The potency of the drug was evident in its rapid effect.  药物的效力在其快速的作用中显而易见。

The potency of his argument convinced the jury. 他论点的效力说服了陪审团。|近义词：efficacy, strength, power, force |反义词：impotence, weakness, inefficacy

词源:
From Middle English *potence, from Old French potence, from Vulgar Latin *potentĭa, from Latin potens, present participle of posse "to be able."  The word ultimately derives from the Indo-European root *pot-, meaning "to be able."
