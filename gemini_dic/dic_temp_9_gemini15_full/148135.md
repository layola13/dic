oversell:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛl/| vt.|过度推销，吹嘘；|to promote or praise something too much, often to the point of being unconvincing or misleading.|例句：The company oversells its products, making unrealistic promises to customers.  这家公司过度推销其产品，向顾客做出不切实际的承诺。|例句：He oversells his abilities in the interview. 他在面试中夸大了自己的能力。|近义词：hype, exaggerate, puff up|反义词：undersell, downplay


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They oversell their services every day. = 他们每天都过度推销他们的服务。
The salesman oversells his products to unsuspecting customers. = 销售员向毫无戒心的顾客过度推销他的产品。

一般过去时:
The company oversold its new software last quarter. = 公司上个季度过度推销了它的新软件。
He oversold his skills during the job interview. = 他在求职面试中夸大了自己的技能。

现在完成时:
They have oversold the benefits of the new policy. = 他们已经夸大了新政策的好处。
The marketing team has oversold the product's capabilities. = 营销团队已经夸大了产品的性能。

过去完成时:
The investors had oversold their shares before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，投资者已经卖出了过多的股票。(注意：此处oversell指卖出过多，而非过度推销，但仍可用此词)
They had oversold the potential of the project. = 他们之前已经夸大了项目的潜力。


将来时:
They will oversell the tickets for the concert. = 他们将会过度销售演唱会的门票。
The politician will oversell his promises during the campaign. = 这位政治家将在竞选期间夸大其词地许诺。


虚拟语气:
If they oversell the product, they might lose customers. = 如果他们过度推销产品，他们可能会失去顾客。
I wish they hadn't oversold the potential of the project. = 我真希望他们没有夸大项目的潜力。


被动语态:
The product was oversold by the marketing department. = 该产品被营销部门过度推销了。
The benefits of the program were oversold to the public. = 该项目的益处被夸大宣传给了公众。


疑问句:
Do you think they oversell their products? = 你认为他们过度推销他们的产品吗？
Were the advantages of the plan oversold? = 计划的优势是否被夸大了？


否定句:
They don't oversell their services; they are quite honest. = 他们并没有过度推销他们的服务；他们很诚实。
The company didn't oversell its capabilities this year. = 今年公司没有夸大其能力。
