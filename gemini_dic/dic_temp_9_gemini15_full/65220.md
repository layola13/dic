一般现在时:
The etiologist studies the causes of various diseases. = 病因学者研究各种疾病的病因。
She investigates the etiology of cancer. = 她调查癌症的病因。


一般过去时:
The etiologist examined the patient's history to determine the cause of illness. = 病因学者检查了病人的病史以确定疾病的原因。
He researched the etiological factors of the outbreak. = 他研究了疫情爆发的病因因素。


现在完成时:
Researchers have identified several risk factors for the disease. = 研究人员已经确定了该疾病的几种危险因素。
Scientists have made significant progress in understanding the etiology of Alzheimer's disease. = 科学家们在理解阿尔茨海默病的病因方面取得了重大进展。
