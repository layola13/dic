euthanatise: /juːˈθænətaɪz/ | vt. | 安乐死；实施安乐死 | To kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to end suffering.  |例句：The vet had to euthanatise the injured dog. 兽医不得不对受伤的狗实施安乐死。
例句：Some people advocate euthanasia for terminally ill patients. 一些人提倡对绝症患者实施安乐死。| 近义词：put down, kill (mercifully) | 反义词：save, heal, cure | 词性：动词
