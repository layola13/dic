resource:/ˈriːsɔːrs/|/ˈrizɔːrs/| n. |资源；财力；办法 |A resource is something that can be used to help you achieve something, such as money, materials, or information.  It can also refer to a person who is skilled or knowledgeable and can provide help. |The company's greatest resource is its employees. 这家公司最大的财富是它的员工。

The report details the country's natural resources. 这份报告详细介绍了该国的自然资源。


|近义词：supply, asset, material, means |反义词：lack, shortage, deficiency |名词|
