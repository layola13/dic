ourself:/ˌaʊərˈsɛlf/| pron.|我们自己|Reflective pronoun.  Used when the subject of the verb is also the object, indicating the action is done to oneself by oneself.  It is used with plural subjects (we).|We hurt ourselves playing football. 我们在踢足球时弄伤了自己。 We taught ourselves to play the piano. 我们自学了弹钢琴。|ourselves|themselves|pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We praise ourselves for our achievements. = 我们为自己的成就感到自豪。
We reward ourselves with a vacation. = 我们用假期来犒劳自己。


一般过去时:
We hurt ourselves yesterday. = 我们昨天受伤了。
We blamed ourselves for the mistake. = 我们为这个错误责备自己。


现在完成时:
We have challenged ourselves to run a marathon. = 我们已经挑战自己去跑马拉松。
We have convinced ourselves that it's the right decision. = 我们已经说服自己这是正确的决定。


过去完成时:
We had already exhausted ourselves before the race even began. = 比赛开始前我们就已经筋疲力尽了。
We had prepared ourselves for the worst-case scenario. = 我们已经为最坏的情况做好了准备。


将来时:
We will push ourselves to the limit. = 我们将逼近自己的极限。
We will treat ourselves to a nice dinner. = 我们将请自己吃一顿丰盛的晚餐。


虚拟语气:
If we were to blame ourselves, it wouldn't change the outcome. = 如果我们要责备自己，也不会改变结果。
We wished we hadn't pushed ourselves so hard. = 我们希望我们没有那么努力地逼迫自己。


被动语态:  (Ourself is rarely used in passive voice.  Ourselves is usually used instead.)
Ourselves were surprised by the results. = 我们对结果感到惊讶。(This is less common and sounds unnatural.)
The team surprised themselves with their victory. = 球队取得胜利，他们自己也很惊讶。(This is more natural.)


疑问句: (Not typically used in questions directly, contextually implied.)
Did we injure ourselves? = 我们受伤了吗？
Are we going to overexert ourselves? = 我们会过度劳累吗？


否定句:
We didn't hurt ourselves. = 我们没有受伤。
We don't want to deceive ourselves. = 我们不想欺骗自己。


PHRASE->
by ourself = 独自地
to ourself = 对我们自己说
for ourself = 为我们自己
in ourself = 本身
of ourself = 发自内心
