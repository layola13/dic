sabji:/ˈsɑːbdʒi/ | n. |蔬菜，菜肴 |A sabji is a vegetable dish, often curry-based, common in Indian cuisine.  It can be made with a variety of vegetables and spices.  | 例句：My mother makes the best aloo gobi sabji. 我妈妈做的土豆花椰菜最好吃。

The creamy tomato sabji was delicious.  那道奶油番茄菜肴很好吃。|近义词：vegetable dish, curry, side dish |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to meat dishes or desserts. |名词
