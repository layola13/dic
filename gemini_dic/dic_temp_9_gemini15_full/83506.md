hacks:/hæks/ | n. v. | 技巧；窍门；入侵；砍；劈 |  A hack is a clever solution to a problem, often involving a shortcut or unconventional approach.  It can also refer to unauthorized access to a computer system. As a verb, it means to cut or chop something roughly. | 例句：He showed me a few hacks to speed up my workflow. 他给我展示了一些加快工作流程的小技巧。 |近义词：trick, shortcut, cheat, exploit (n.) |反义词：inelegant solution, orthodox method (n.)
