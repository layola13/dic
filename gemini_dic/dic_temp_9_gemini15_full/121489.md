mislead:/mɪsˈliːd/| vt.| 误导，欺骗 |To give someone the wrong idea or impression, causing them to believe something that is not true.  |例句：The misleading advertisement caused many people to buy the faulty product.  误导性的广告导致许多人购买了有缺陷的产品。
例句：Don't mislead him into thinking he'll get the job. 不要误导他让他以为他会得到这份工作。|近义词：deceive, delude, trick |反义词：guide, enlighten, inform |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misleads his customers about the product's quality. 他误导他的顾客关于产品的质量。
They mislead the public with false information. 他们用虚假信息误导公众。

一般过去时:
The map misled us; we took a wrong turn. 地图误导了我们；我们拐错了弯。
The politician misled the voters with empty promises.  这位政客用空洞的承诺误导了选民。

现在完成时:
The company has misled investors for years. 这家公司多年来一直在误导投资者。
She has been misled by her friends' bad advice. 她被朋友的坏建议误导了。

过去完成时:
I realized I had been misled by the instructions. 我意识到我被说明书误导了。
They discovered they had been misled about the project's deadline. 他们发现他们被关于项目截止日期的事情误导了。

将来时:
The advertisement will mislead many consumers. 这则广告将会误导许多消费者。
He will likely mislead her with his charm. 他很可能会用他的魅力误导她。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't misled me, I wouldn't have made that mistake. 如果他没有误导我，我就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I hadn't been misled by his false promises. 我真希望我没有被他虚假的承诺所误导。

被动语态:
Many people were misled by the fake news. 许多人被假新闻误导了。
The investors were misled by the fraudulent report. 投资者被欺诈报告误导了。

疑问句:
Were you misled by the instructions? 你被说明书误导了吗？
Has the advertising campaign misled anyone? 广告宣传活动有没有误导任何人？

否定句:
The teacher did not mislead her students. 老师没有误导她的学生。
He tried to mislead the police, but they weren't fooled. 他试图误导警方，但他们没有上当。


PHRASE->
mislead someone into doing something  误导某人做某事
mislead someone about something  在某事上误导某人
be misled by something 被某事误导
grossly mislead  严重误导
intentionally mislead  故意误导
