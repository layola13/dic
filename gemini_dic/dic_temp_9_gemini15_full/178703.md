recrudesces:/ˌriːkrʊˈdɛsɪz/| v.|复发；恶化；重新变得剧烈|To break out again; to become active or virulent again after a period of inactivity.|The old wound recrudesced after he overexerted himself. 他过度劳累后旧伤复发了。|The symptoms of the disease recrudesced after a period of remission.  疾病的症状在缓解期后再次出现。|improve, abate, subside|alleviate, cure, heal
