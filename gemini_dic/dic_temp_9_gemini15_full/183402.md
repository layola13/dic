reproofing:/ˌriːˈpruːfɪŋ/| n. |再涂层；再防雨处理；重新证明|The process of applying a new layer of waterproofing material to a surface, or the act of proving something again.  It often refers to treating fabrics or materials to make them waterproof or resistant to stains. |例句：The old roof needed reproofing to withstand the harsh winter. 老屋顶需要重新防水以抵御严酷的冬天。

例句：The company is conducting reproofing of its claims to support its legal case. 公司正在对其索赔进行重新证明以支持其法律案件。


近义词：re-waterproofing, waterproofing, reproving| 反义词：damaging, destroying | 词性：名词
