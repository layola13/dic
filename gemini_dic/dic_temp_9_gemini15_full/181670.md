relegates:/ˈrɛləɡeɪt/| vt.|降级；贬低；把…放到次要地位 |To lower someone or something to a lower rank or position.  To assign someone or something to a less important place or position. |例句：The team was relegated to a lower league after a poor season.  这个队因为赛季表现糟糕被降级到低级别联赛。

The company relegated the project to a lower priority. 公司将该项目列为较低优先级。


近义词：demote, downgrade, reduce, banish

反义词：promote, elevate, upgrade, advance


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relegates his work to the weekends. 他把工作安排在周末做。
She relegates minor tasks to her assistant. 她把一些小任务交给她的助理。


一般过去时:
They relegated him to a less important role. 他们把他降职到一个不太重要的职位。
The government relegated the issue to a committee. 政府将这个问题交给了一个委员会。


现在完成时:
They have relegated the old equipment to the storeroom. 他们已将旧设备移到储藏室。
The company has relegated that product line. 公司已经停止生产那条产品线了。


过去完成时:
He had relegated the matter to the back of his mind. 他已经把这件事抛诸脑后了。
The manager had relegated the employee to a lower position. 经理已将该员工降职到较低职位。


将来时:
They will relegate the decision to the board of directors. 他们将把决定权交给董事会。
She will relegate the task to a junior member of staff. 她会把这项任务交给一位初级职员。


虚拟语气:
If they relegated him, he would sue the company. 如果他们降他的职，他会起诉公司。
I wish they hadn't relegated him to such a trivial task. 我真希望他们没让他做这么琐碎的任务。


被动语态:
He was relegated to the reserves. 他被降到替补席。
The proposal was relegated to the bottom of the pile.  该提案被搁置一旁。


疑问句:
Will he be relegated to a lower position? 他会被降职吗？
Was the team relegated last season?  这个队上个赛季被降级了吗？


否定句:
They did not relegate him despite his mistakes. 尽管他犯了错误，他们也没有降他的职。
The company would not relegate the project despite budget constraints. 尽管预算有限，公司也不会放弃这个项目。


PHRASE->
relegate to the background 将…置于次要地位
relegate to oblivion  遗忘；使…湮没无闻
relegate to the dustbin of history  让…成为历史的尘埃
relegate to the sidelines 将…置于次要地位；冷落
relegate to a lower division 降级到低级别联赛
