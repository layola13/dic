engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/| vt.|雕刻，铭刻；深刻地印在（脑海里）|to cut or carve words, designs, etc. into a hard surface, or to impress something on a person's mind|The artist engraved his initials on the bottom of the sculpture. 艺术家在他的雕塑底部刻上了他的姓名首字母。 He had the date of his wedding engraved on his watch. 他把结婚日期刻在了他的手表上。|inscribe, etch, carve|erase, obliterate|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He engraves beautiful designs on wood. 他在木头上雕刻精美的图案。
She engraves personalized messages on jewelry.  她在首饰上刻制个性化信息。


一般过去时:
The craftsman engraved the family crest onto the silver plate. 工匠将家族纹章刻在银盘上。
My grandfather engraved his name on his pocket watch. 我的祖父在他的怀表上刻上了他的名字。


现在完成时:
The artist has engraved many masterpieces throughout his career.这位艺术家在他的职业生涯中雕刻了许多杰作。
She has engraved her initials on all her paintings. 她已在她所有的画作上刻上了她的姓名首字母。


过去完成时:
They had engraved the inscription before the ceremony began. 仪式开始前，他们已经刻好了铭文。
He had engraved the ring before he proposed.  他求婚前已经刻好了戒指。


将来时:
She will engrave a special message on the trophy. 她将在奖杯上刻上特别的信息。
The company will engrave your name on the plaque. 公司将在牌匾上刻上你的名字。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better tool, I would engrave a more intricate design. 如果我有更好的工具，我会雕刻更精细的图案。
I wish I could engrave this sentiment on my heart.  我希望我能把这种情感铭刻在我的心里。


被动语态:
The name was engraved on the monument.  名字被刻在纪念碑上。

疑问句:

否定句:
They did not engrave the message correctly. 他们没有正确地刻上信息。
I cannot engrave the design on this material. 我不能在这个材料上雕刻这个图案。
