panyard:/pænˈjɑːrd/| n. |船上晾帆布的处所；院子；围栏|A panyard is an enclosed area, typically on a ship, used for storing and drying sails and other ship's canvas.  The term can also refer to a yard or enclosed space on land. |例句：The sailors carefully stowed the sails in the panyard after the storm.  暴风雨过后，水手们小心地将帆收进晾帆处。

The old house had a charming panyard where the family grew herbs.  那栋老房子有一个迷人的院子，家人在那里种植药草。|近义词：yard, enclosure, court|反义词：open space, expanse
