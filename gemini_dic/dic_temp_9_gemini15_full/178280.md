reconceive:/ˌriːkənˈsiːv/| vt.|重新构想；重新设想；改变对…的看法|To think about or plan something again, often in a new or different way.|例句：We need to reconceive our approach to urban planning.我们需要重新构想我们对城市规划的方法。

例句：The artist reconceived the painting, adding new elements and changing the composition.艺术家重新构想了这幅画，添加了新的元素并改变了构图。


近义词：reimagine, rethink, reformulate, re-evaluate|反义词：maintain, preserve, adhere to|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reconceive their marketing strategy every year. = 他们每年都会重新构想他们的营销策略。
She reconceives the novel's ending in her adaptation. = 她在她改编的版本中重新构想了小说的结局。

一般过去时:
He reconceived the project after receiving feedback. = 他在收到反馈后重新构想了这个项目。
The company reconceived its logo to appeal to a younger audience. = 公司重新设计了它的标志以吸引更年轻的受众。

现在完成时:
They have reconceived their business model several times. = 他们已经几次重新构想了他们的商业模式。
She has reconceived her role in the company since her promotion. = 自从晋升以来，她已经重新思考她在公司中的角色。

过去完成时:
They had reconceived the plan before presenting it to the board. = 他们在向董事会提交计划之前就已经重新构想了该计划。
She had reconceived her approach to the problem after several failed attempts. = 在几次尝试失败后，她已经重新构想了她解决这个问题的方法。

将来时:
They will reconceive their product line next year. = 他们明年将重新设计他们的产品线。
She will reconceive the play for a modern audience. = 她将为现代观众重新构思这出戏。

虚拟语气:
If they reconceived their strategy, they might succeed. = 如果他们重新构想他们的策略，他们可能会成功。
I wish I had reconceived my answer earlier. = 我希望我早点重新构想我的答案。

被动语态:
The project was reconceived by the team after many discussions. = 该项目在多次讨论后由团队重新构想。
The novel's ending was reconceived for the film adaptation. = 小说的结局被重新构思用于电影改编。

疑问句:
Will they reconceive the design for the new building? = 他们会重新设计新建筑的设计吗？
Have they reconceived their plans for the future? = 他们是否已经重新构想了他们未来的计划？

否定句:
They did not reconceive their initial plans. = 他们没有重新构想他们的最初计划。
She will not reconceive her opinion easily. = 她不会轻易改变她的看法。


PHRASE->
reconceive the future = 重新构想未来
reconceive the role of = 重新定义……的角色
reconceive oneself = 重新认识自己
reconceive the meaning of = 重新理解……的意义
reconceive a problem = 重新审视一个问题


词源:
re- (again) + conceive (to form in the mind)
