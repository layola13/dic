deadwood:/ˈdedˌwʊd/| n.|枯木；无用之人；无用之物|Useless or unproductive things or people;  something that is no longer useful or productive.|The old equipment is just deadwood; we should replace it. 这台旧设备已经成了废物；我们应该更换它。  The company needs to get rid of its deadwood.  这家公司需要清除冗员。|redundant, obsolete, useless|essential, productive, valuable|名词
