protocanonical:/ˌproʊtoʊˈkænənɪkəl/| adj.| 原型的，典型的，标准的 | Relating to or characteristic of a prototype or original model; serving as an archetype or model.  The term often appears in theological or biblical contexts, referring to an original, authoritative text or version. | Example: The protocanonical books of the Bible are those universally accepted as scripture.  原型圣经书籍是那些普遍被接受为圣经的书籍。| 近义词：archetypal, standard, original, typical | 反义词：apocryphal, deuterocanonical
