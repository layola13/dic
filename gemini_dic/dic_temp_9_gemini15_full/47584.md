debasers:/dɪˈbeɪzərz/| n. |贬低者；降低价值者|Those who debase something, lowering its quality, value, or moral standing.  They diminish the worth or integrity of something.|The debasers of the currency were brought to justice. 货币贬值者受到了法律制裁。|近义词：corrupters, degraders, depreciators |反义词：uplifters, enhancers, improvers
