heaves:/hiːvz/ | vt. & vi. |  举起；用力地抛；呕吐；喘息；起伏；剧烈摇晃 | To lift or throw something heavy with great effort; to vomit; to breathe heavily; to rise and fall; to move up and down violently. | 例句：The ship heaved violently in the storm.  这艘船在暴风雨中剧烈摇晃。

The old man heaved a sigh of relief.  老人舒了一口气。


近义词：lift, hoist, toss, vomit, gasp, surge, pitch | 反义词：lower, descend, calm, still | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He heaves the sack of potatoes onto his shoulder. 他把一袋土豆扛到肩上。
The ocean heaves gently in the morning sun.  早晨的阳光下，大海轻轻起伏。


一般过去时:
She heaved the heavy box into the truck. 她费力地把那个沉重的箱子搬进了卡车。
The earthquake caused the ground to heave and crack. 地震导致地面隆起和开裂。


现在完成时:
He has heaved several tons of rock today. 他今天已经搬运了几吨岩石。
The ship has heaved and pitched all night. 这艘船整夜都在剧烈摇晃。


过去完成时:

The ground had heaved and split open after the explosion. 爆炸后，地面隆起并裂开了。


将来时:

The waves will heave against the shore. 海浪将拍打海岸。


虚拟语气:
If he had been stronger, he could have heaved the boulder. 如果他更强壮一些，他就能搬动那块巨石。
If only he hadn’t heaved that heavy box, his back wouldn't hurt so much. 要是他没有搬动那个沉重的箱子就好了，他的背就不会这么疼。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Did he heave the anchor? 他起锚了吗？

否定句:
He couldn't heave the stone. 他搬不动那块石头。
The small boat did not heave in the calm sea. 小船在平静的海面上没有摇晃。
