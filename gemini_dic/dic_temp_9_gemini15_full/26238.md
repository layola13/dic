butternut:/ˈbʌtənʌt/| n. |美洲山核桃，核桃南瓜 |A butternut is a type of winter squash with a sweet, nutty flavor, or the tree that produces the nut.  |例句：The butternut squash soup was creamy and delicious.  核桃南瓜汤又浓稠又美味。

例句：We harvested a lot of butternuts from our garden this year. 今年我们从花园里收获了很多核桃南瓜。


近义词：winter squash (for the squash), hickory nut (for the nut, less common usage) |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with other types of squash or nuts. |词性:noun
