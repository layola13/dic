geomancy:/ˈdʒiːoʊmænsi/| n. |地相术，风水术|The art or practice of divination by means of figures or lines made at random and interpreted as signs; also called geomancie.  Geomancy is an ancient divination method that uses points, lines, and figures to interpret the earth's influence on human affairs.|例句：Many ancient cultures practiced geomancy to predict the future and make important decisions.  许多古代文化都曾利用地相术来预测未来并做出重要决定。

例句：He consulted a geomancer before building his new house. 他在建造新房子之前咨询了一位风水师。|近义词：divination, augury, fortune-telling |反义词：rationality, science, logic


词源:
From Middle English *geomancie, from Old French geomancie, from Late Latin geomancia, from Greek gē “earth” + manteia “divination”.
