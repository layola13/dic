ionizing: /ˈaɪənaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 电离的；使电离的 | Causing or capable of causing ionization; relating to or resulting from ionization.  |  例句：Ionizing radiation can damage DNA. 电离辐射会破坏DNA。
例句：The process involves ionizing the gas molecules. 这个过程包括使气体分子电离。| 近义词：ionogenic, radiogenic | 反义词：non-ionizing
