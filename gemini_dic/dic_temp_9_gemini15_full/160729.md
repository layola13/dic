portrays:/pɔːˈtreɪz/ | vt. | 描绘；描写；扮演 | To represent or describe someone or something in a particular way.  | The author portrays the main character as a complex and flawed individual. 作者将主人公描绘成一个复杂而有缺陷的个体。| depicts, describes, represents | misrepresents, distorts | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist portrays the landscape beautifully. = 艺术家美丽地描绘了这片风景。
He portrays himself as a victim. = 他把自己描绘成受害者。

一般过去时:
The movie portrays the war accurately. = 这部电影准确地描绘了战争。
She portrayed a strong female lead in the play. = 她在剧中扮演了一个坚强的女主角。

现在完成时:
The novel has portrayed the social issues effectively. = 这部小说有效地描绘了社会问题。
The media has portrayed the event differently. = 媒体对这一事件的报道各不相同。

过去完成时:
The painting had portrayed a sense of longing. = 这幅画曾描绘出一种渴望之情。
They had portrayed the king as a cruel tyrant. = 他们曾把国王描绘成一个残酷的暴君。

将来时:
The new biography will portray him in a different light. = 这本新传记将从不同的角度来描写他。
The director will portray the story through flashbacks. = 导演将通过倒叙来讲述这个故事。

虚拟语气:
If the book portrayed the character differently, it would be more appealing. = 如果书中对人物的刻画有所不同，它将会更吸引人。
I wish the play portrayed the historical context better. = 我希望这出戏能更好地展现历史背景。

被动语态:
The situation was portrayed inaccurately in the news report. = 新闻报道中对情况的描述是不准确的。
The character is portrayed as both sympathetic and ruthless. = 这个角色既被描绘成富有同情心的，也被描绘成冷酷无情的。

疑问句:
How does the author portray the protagonist’s internal conflict? = 作者是如何描绘主人公内心冲突的？
Does the painting accurately portray the scene? = 这幅画是否准确地描绘了场景？

否定句:
The film does not portray the historical events truthfully. = 这部电影没有真实地展现历史事件。
The artist did not portray the emotion effectively. = 艺术家没有有效地表达情感。
