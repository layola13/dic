muffs:/mʌfs/| n.|耳罩，暖手筒|A muff is a warm covering, typically made of fur or wool, for the hands.  It's usually cylindrical and has openings at both ends to allow the hands to be inserted.|例句：She wore a fluffy muff to keep her hands warm in the cold winter. 她戴着毛茸茸的暖手筒来保暖。

例句：My grandmother knitted me a beautiful wool muff as a gift. 我祖母织了一副漂亮的羊毛暖手筒送给我。|近义词：mittens, gloves|反义词：None specifically.  Could consider "bare hands" as a contrasting concept.

词源:
The word "muff" in this context originates from the Old French word "mofle," meaning a "muzzle" or "covering for the mouth."  Over time, this evolved to refer to a covering for the hands, likely due to the similar function of protecting a vulnerable area from the elements.
