games:/ɡeɪmz/| n.|游戏，比赛|Activities or competitions involving rules, skill, and often chance, played for recreation or entertainment.|例句：We played board games all afternoon. 我们整个下午都在玩棋盘游戏。|例句：The Olympic Games are held every four years. 奥运会每四年举行一次。|近义词：sports, contests, pastimes|反义词：work, labor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plays video games every day. = 他每天都玩电子游戏。
They play chess in the park. = 他们在公园下棋。


一般过去时:
I played basketball yesterday. = 我昨天打篮球了。
She played the piano beautifully. = 她钢琴弹得很漂亮。


现在完成时:
We have played this game many times. = 我们玩过很多次这个游戏了。
They have won several games this season. = 这个赛季他们赢了好几场比赛。


过去完成时:

They had played three sets before the rain started. = 下雨之前他们已经打了三局。


将来时:

They will participate in the games next week. = 他们下周要参加比赛。


虚拟语气:
