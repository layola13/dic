countersues:/ˈkaʊntɚsuːz/| v.|反诉，反控告|To file a countersuit against someone who has sued you.  It means to bring a lawsuit against the person who initially sued you.|Example: After being sued for breach of contract, the company countersued, alleging fraud on the part of the plaintiff.  在被控违反合同后，该公司反诉，声称原告存在欺诈行为。|近义词：rebut, retaliate against|反义词：sue, file a suit|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He countersues his former employer for wrongful dismissal. = 他反诉他的前雇主不当解雇。
The defendant countersues the plaintiff for defamation. = 被告反诉原告诽谤。


一般过去时:
She countersued her neighbor after he sued her for property damage. = 在她的邻居因为财产损失起诉她之后，她反诉了她的邻居。
The company countersued the government for unfair trade practices. = 公司反诉政府不正当贸易行为。


现在完成时:
They have countersued the original plaintiff for malicious prosecution. = 他们已经反诉最初的原告恶意起诉。
The lawyer has countersued his client for unpaid fees. = 律师已经反诉他的客户未付的费用。


过去完成时:
Before the judge ruled, the defendant had countersued, alleging breach of contract. = 在法官裁决之前，被告已经反诉，声称违反合同。
The plaintiff had countersued before the discovery phase of the trial. = 原告在审判的证据开示阶段之前已经反诉。


将来时:
He will countersue the plaintiff for emotional distress. = 他将反诉原告精神损害。
The corporation will countersue its competitor for patent infringement. = 公司将反诉其竞争对手专利侵权。


虚拟语气:
If he had more evidence, he would countersue. = 如果他有更多证据，他会反诉。
I wish I could countersue, but I lack the resources. = 我希望我能反诉，但我缺乏资源。


被动语态:
The case was countersued by the defendant. = 此案被告反诉了。
The plaintiff's claim was countersued by the defendant for damages. = 原告的索赔被被告反诉索赔。


疑问句:
Will the defendant countersue? = 被告会反诉吗？
Did the company countersue the plaintiff? = 公司反诉原告了吗？


否定句:
He did not countersue, choosing to settle out of court. = 他没有反诉，选择庭外和解。
The defendant decided not to countersue at this time. = 被告决定目前不反诉。


PHRASE->
countersue for damages = 反诉索赔
countersue on grounds of = 以...为由反诉
countersue successfully = 成功反诉
countersue the plaintiff = 反诉原告
file a countersuit = 提出反诉
