reconverts:/ˌriːˈkɒnvɜːrts/| vt.|重新转换，再转换|To convert something back to its original state or to a different state.|The factory reconverts waste materials into usable products.  工厂将废料重新转换为可用产品。|They reconverted the old building into a modern apartment complex. 他们把旧建筑改建成了现代化的公寓楼。| converts, transforms |deconverts, destroys|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reconverts waste materials into energy. = 公司将废料重新转换成能源。
He regularly reconverts his digital photos to print format. = 他定期将他的数码照片转换成打印格式。


一般过去时:
The engineer reconverted the old system to a new one. = 工程师将旧系统重新转换成新系统。
She reconverted her savings from dollars to euros. = 她将她的储蓄从美元兑换成欧元。


现在完成时:
They have reconverted the entire building into a museum. = 他们已将整座建筑改建成博物馆。
We have reconverted our marketing strategy to focus on social media. = 我们已经重新调整了我们的营销策略，重点放在社交媒体上。


过去完成时:
He had reconverted the data before submitting the report. = 他在提交报告之前已经转换了数据。
The team had reconverted the engine to its original specifications. = 该团队已将发动机转换回其原始规格。
