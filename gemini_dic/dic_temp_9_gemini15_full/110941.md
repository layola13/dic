lips:/lɪps/| n.|嘴唇|The fleshy folds that surround the mouth.  They are used for speaking, eating, and kissing. |例句：Her lips curved into a smile.  她的嘴唇弯成一个微笑。|例句：He kissed her softly on the lips. 他轻轻地吻了她的嘴唇。|近义词：mouth (部分含义)|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My lips are dry. = 我的嘴唇很干。
His lips move when he speaks. = 他说话时嘴唇会动。

一般过去时:
Her lips trembled with emotion. = 她的嘴唇因激动而颤抖。
His lips formed the words "I love you". = 他的嘴唇形成了“我爱你”这几个字。

现在完成时:
I've bitten my lip several times today. = 我今天已经咬了好几次嘴唇了。
She's painted her lips a bright red. = 她把嘴唇涂成了鲜红色。

过去完成时:
He had bitten his lip to stop himself from crying. = 他咬着嘴唇才忍住没有哭。
They had pursed their lips in disapproval. = 他们撅着嘴表示反对。

将来时:
I will apply lip balm to my chapped lips. = 我会给干裂的嘴唇涂上润唇膏。
Her lips will surely tremble when she hears the news. = 她听到这个消息时嘴唇肯定会颤抖。

虚拟语气:
If my lips were not so dry, I wouldn't need lip balm. = 如果我的嘴唇不那么干，我就不用润唇膏了。
I wish my lips were fuller. = 我希望我的嘴唇更丰满。

被动语态:
Her lips were kissed gently. = 她的嘴唇被轻轻地吻了。
His lips were chapped by the cold wind. = 他的嘴唇被寒风吹裂了。

疑问句:
Are your lips chapped? = 你的嘴唇干裂了吗？
Why are your lips blue? = 为什么你的嘴唇是蓝色的？

否定句:
My lips are not bleeding. = 我的嘴唇没有流血。
She didn't purse her lips. = 她没有撅嘴。
