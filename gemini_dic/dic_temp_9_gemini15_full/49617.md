demibastion: /ˈdɛmiˌbæʃən/ | n. | 半棱堡，半堡垒 | A demibastion is a type of fortification, a half-bastion, projecting less from the main wall than a full bastion.  It offers less protection than a full bastion but is sometimes used where space is limited or for other tactical reasons. | The architect designed a demibastion to fit the limited space on the clifftop.  = 建筑师设计了一个半棱堡以适应悬崖顶部有限的空间。| 近义词：half-bastion, partial fortification | 反义词：full bastion, complete fortification
