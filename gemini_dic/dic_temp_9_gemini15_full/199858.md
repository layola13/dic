sitting: /ˈsɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 坐着；使…就座 | The act of being seated; the state of resting on one's buttocks.  It can also refer to the placement of something. |例句：He was sitting on the park bench. 他坐在公园的长椅上。

例句：The vase is sitting on the table. 花瓶放在桌子上。


近义词：seated, perched, enthroned | 反义词：standing, upright


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am sitting here. = 我正坐在這裡。
They are sitting at the table. = 他們正坐在桌子旁。

一般过去时:
She sat down quietly. = 她静静地坐了下来。
He sat there for hours. = 他在那儿坐了几个小时。

现在完成时:
I have been sitting here for an hour. = 我已经坐在这里一个小时了。
She has been sitting by the window all afternoon. = 她整个下午都坐在窗边。

过去完成时:
I had been sitting for so long that my legs were numb. = 我坐了太久，腿都麻了。
They had been sitting in the waiting room for an hour before the doctor called them. = 医生叫他们之前，他们已经在候诊室坐了一个小时了。


将来时:
I will be sitting in the front row. = 我将坐在前排。
They will be sitting on the patio later. = 他们稍后将坐在露台上。

虚拟语气:
If I were sitting next to you, I would feel better. = 如果我坐在你旁边，我会感觉好些。
I wish I were sitting on a beach right now. = 我真希望我现在正坐在海滩上。

被动语态:
The chair was sitting against the wall. = 椅子靠墙放着。
The guests were being seated by the staff. = 客人们正由工作人员安排就座。

疑问句:
Are you sitting comfortably? = 你坐得舒服吗？
Where is he sitting? = 他坐在哪里？

否定句:
He isn't sitting still. = 他坐不住。
She wasn't sitting at her desk. = 她没坐在她的办公桌旁。


PHRASE->
sitting pretty:  处境优越；情况很好
sitting duck:  容易攻击的目标
sitting on:  拥有（某物但不使用）
sitting around:  闲坐着
sitting tight:  静观其变
