impact:/ˈɪmpækt/| n., v.|影响；冲击；撞击|The force of a collision, a strong effect or influence.|例句：The impact of the car crash was devastating.  车祸的影响是毁灭性的。 The new policy will have a significant impact on the economy. 新政策将对经济产生重大影响。|近义词：effect, influence, consequence, collision|反义词：negligible effect, no influence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain impacts the crops. = 雨水影响庄稼。
His decision impacts the team's morale. = 他的决定影响团队士气。

一般过去时:
The earthquake impacted the city severely. = 地震严重影响了这座城市。
The news impacted her deeply. = 这个消息深深地影响了她。

现在完成时:
The reforms have impacted the education system positively. = 改革对教育体系产生了积极的影响。
His words have impacted my life significantly. = 他的话极大地影响了我的生活。

过去完成时:
The drought had impacted the harvest before the storm arrived. = 在暴风雨来临之前，干旱已经影响了收成。
The scandal had impacted his reputation before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，丑闻已经影响了他的名誉。

将来时:
The new technology will impact many industries. = 新技术将影响许多行业。
The decision will impact our future plans. = 这个决定将影响我们的未来计划。

虚拟语气:
If the project succeeds, it will impact the company positively. = 如果项目成功，它将对公司产生积极影响。
If he had listened, it would have impacted his life differently. = 如果他听了，那将不同地影响他的生活。

被动语态:
The city was impacted by the hurricane. = 这座城市受到了飓风的冲击。
The company's profits were impacted by the recession. = 公司的利润受到了经济衰退的影响。

疑问句:
How will the changes impact the environment? = 这些变化将如何影响环境？
Will the new law impact small businesses? = 新法会影响小型企业吗？

否定句:
The changes did not impact the results significantly. = 这些变化并没有显著影响结果。
The policy will not impact the population negatively. = 该政策不会对人口产生负面影响。


PHRASE->
have an impact on = 对…有影响
make an impact = 产生影响
the impact of = …的影响
a major impact = 重大影响
significant impact = 重大影响
