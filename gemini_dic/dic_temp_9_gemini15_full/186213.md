rheumy:/ˈruːmi/ | adj. | 泪汪汪的；分泌物多的；潮湿的 |  Characterized by or containing rheum; watery or running;  as, rheumy eyes.  Describes eyes that are watery and often inflamed, typically due to a cold or other ailment.  | 例句：His rheumy eyes suggested a long night of crying. 他的泪汪汪的眼睛暗示了他哭了一整夜。

例句：The old dog's rheumy nose dripped constantly.  这只老狗的湿漉漉的鼻子不停地滴着水。


近义词：watery, moist, damp,  weepy


反义词：dry
