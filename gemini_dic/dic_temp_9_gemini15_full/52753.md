dilatability:/ˌdaɪləˈteɪbɪləti/| n. |扩张性，可扩张性 |The ability of something to be dilated or expanded.  This term is often used in medical contexts to describe the capacity of a blood vessel, pupil, or other structure to widen. |Example: The doctor checked the patient's pupillary dilatability to assess neurological function.  医生检查了病人的瞳孔扩张性以评估神经功能。The high dilatability of the artery contributed to the successful angioplasty.  动脉的高扩张性有助于血管成形术的成功。|近义词：expandability, extensibility |反义词：constrictability, inelasticity |名词
