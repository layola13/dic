psychologically:/ˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. |心理上地；从心理学角度 |In a way that relates to the mind and its functions.  It describes actions, states, or processes that are influenced by or pertain to mental or emotional factors.|例句：He was psychologically damaged by the war. 他在战争中受到了心理创伤。|例句：Psychologically, she's still a child.  心理上，她还是个孩子。|近义词：mentally, emotionally |反义词：physically
