issuers:/ˈɪʃuːərz/| n. |发行人，签发人|Entities that issue securities, such as bonds or stocks.  They are responsible for the creation and distribution of these financial instruments.|Example:  The issuers of the bonds are facing increased scrutiny from investors.  发行债券的发行人正面临着投资者越来越严格的审查。|近义词：publishers, distributors|反义词：recipients, holders

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company issues new shares every year. = 公司每年发行新股。
Who issues these certificates? = 谁发行这些证书？


一般过去时:
The bank issued a statement denying the rumors. = 银行发表声明否认了这些谣言。
They issued a warning about the impending storm. = 他们发布了关于即将到来的暴风雨的警告。


现在完成时:
The government has issued several new regulations. = 政府已经发布了几项新的规章制度。
The court has issued a ruling in the case. = 法院已经对该案作出了裁决。


过去完成时:
The company had issued a press release before the market opened. = 公司在市场开盘前发布了一份新闻稿。
Before the accident, they had issued several safety warnings. = 事故发生前，他们发布过几次安全警告。


将来时:
The company will issue a new report next week. = 公司下周将发布一份新的报告。
They will issue a statement later today. = 他们今天晚些时候将发表声明。


虚拟语气:
If the bank issued more loans, the economy would improve. = 如果银行发放更多贷款，经济就会好转。
I wish they had issued a clearer explanation. = 我希望他们能给出更清晰的解释。


被动语态:
New regulations are issued by the government annually. = 新规章每年由政府颁布。
The bonds were issued at a high interest rate. = 这些债券以高利率发行。


疑问句:
Who issued this order? = 谁发布了这个命令？
Which company issued the bonds? = 哪家公司发行了这些债券？


否定句:
The company did not issue a statement. = 公司没有发表声明。
They have not yet issued the permits. = 他们尚未签发许可证。
