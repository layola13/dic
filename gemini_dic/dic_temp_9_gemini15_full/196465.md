serums:/ˈsɪərəmz/| n.|血清；美容液|A serum is a liquid containing antibodies or other proteins that is used to treat or prevent disease.  It can also refer to a cosmetic product applied to the skin.|例句：The doctor prescribed a serum to boost her immune system.医生开了血清来增强她的免疫系统。

例句：She uses a vitamin C serum every morning as part of her skincare routine. 她每天早上都会使用维生素C美容液作为护肤程序的一部分。|近义词：antiserum,  (for cosmetic use) essence, lotion|反义词：无
