geoengineering:/ˌdʒioʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/ | n. | 地球工程，地球工程学 | The deliberate large-scale manipulation of the Earth's climate system to counteract global warming.  It involves techniques to either remove greenhouse gases from the atmosphere or reduce the amount of sunlight reaching the Earth's surface. | Example:  Scientists are exploring various geoengineering techniques to mitigate climate change. 科学家们正在探索各种地球工程技术以减缓气候变化。

Example: The feasibility of solar radiation management as a geoengineering solution remains highly debated.  作为地球工程解决方案的太阳辐射管理的可行性仍然存在很大争议。


近义词：climate engineering, climate intervention | 反义词：natural climate regulation
