gaiety:/ˈɡeɪəti/ | n. |快乐；欢愉；热闹 |  Gaiety refers to the state of being cheerful and lively; a feeling or atmosphere of fun and enjoyment. It often implies a festive or celebratory mood.  | The party was filled with gaiety and laughter.  派对充满了快乐和笑声。 |  mirth, merriment, jollity, cheerfulness | sadness, gloom, depression, melancholy | 名词
