It implies a second folding action. |Example: The refolded laundry was neatly stacked on the shelf.  重新折叠好的衣物整齐地堆放在架子上。 The refolded map showed a clearer route.  重新折叠的地图显示了一条更清晰的路线。|近义词：re-creased, redoubled (in the context of folding)|反义词：unfolded, flattened|形容词
