cocoonings:/kəˈkuːnɪŋz/| n.|茧状物；隐居；自我保护；|The act of forming or being enclosed in a cocoon, or a similar state of seclusion or protection.  It can refer to the literal process of an insect forming a cocoon, or metaphorically to a period of seclusion, introspection, or self-protection.|例句：The artist retreated into a period of cocoonings, avoiding all social contact.  艺术家退隐一段时间，避免所有社交接触。

例句：Her cocoonings allowed her to focus on her work and recover from burnout.她的隐居让她专注于工作并从倦怠中恢复过来。


近义词：seclusion, isolation, withdrawal, sheltering|反义词：exposure, openness, interaction
