lusus:/ˈluːsəs/| n. |嬉戏；游戏；玩笑；怪诞；异常；畸形|A playful act or remark; a joke; something unusual or abnormal; a freak of nature or a deviation from the norm.  |例句：The child's playful antics were a lusus of youthful energy.  孩子的嬉戏是青春活力的体现。|例句：The birth of a twin is sometimes considered a lusus naturae. 双胞胎的出生有时被认为是自然界的奇观。|近义词：game, prank, sport, oddity, anomaly, freak|反义词：normality, regularity, typicality
