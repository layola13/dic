glow's:/ɡloʊz/ | n. | 光辉；光芒；光泽 | The soft glow of the setting sun.  夕阳柔和的光辉。|近义词：shine, radiance, luminescence | 反义词：darkness, dullness | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun glows brightly today. = 太阳今天明亮地照耀着。
The embers glow in the fireplace. = 余烬在壁炉里发光。


一般过去时:
The city glowed with lights at night. = 夜晚城市灯火辉煌。
Her cheeks glowed with excitement. = 她兴奋得脸颊通红。


现在完成时:
The painting has glowed for centuries. = 这幅画已经闪耀了几个世纪。
The fire has glowed all night. = 火一直燃烧了一整夜。


过去完成时:
The sunset had glowed brilliantly before the storm. =暴风雨来临前，日落辉煌灿烂。
The coals had glowed faintly before we added more wood. = 我们添加更多木柴之前，煤炭微弱地发光。


将来时:
The neon signs will glow all night long. = 霓虹灯将通宵闪亮。
The stars will glow brightly tonight. = 今晚星星将闪耀。


虚拟语气:
If the diamond glowed brighter, it would be more valuable. = 如果钻石更亮，它就会更有价值。
I wish the moon glowed more intensely tonight. = 我希望今晚月亮更亮一些。


被动语态:
The room was glowed with warmth from the fireplace. = 房间被壁炉的温暖照亮了。(此句被动语态略显不自然，更常用的表达是"The room was warm from the fireplace.")
The city is glowed with colorful lights during the festival. = 节日期间，城市被五彩缤纷的灯光照亮了。(此句被动语态略显不自然，更常用的表达是"The city is bright with colorful lights during the festival.")


疑问句:
Does the fire still glow? = 火还在燃烧吗？
Does the lamp glow brightly enough? = 灯够亮吗？


否定句:
The screen doesn't glow anymore. = 屏幕不再发光了。
The stars didn't glow brightly last night. = 昨晚星星没有明亮地闪耀。
