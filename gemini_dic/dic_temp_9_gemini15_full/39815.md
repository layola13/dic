conspectus:/kənˈspektəs/| n.|概要，概述，纲要|A summary or general view of a subject; a synopsis.  It often refers to a comprehensive overview, presenting the main points concisely. |The professor provided a conspectus of the historical context before launching into the detailed analysis.|近义词：summary, synopsis, overview, abstract|反义词：detail, minutiae
