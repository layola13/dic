polyoxyethylene: /ˌpɒliˌɒksɪˈeθɪliːn/| n. |聚氧乙烯|A polymer composed of repeating units of ethylene oxide.  It is a nonionic surfactant and is widely used in various applications due to its water solubility and ability to reduce surface tension.|例句：Polyoxyethylene is a common ingredient in many detergents and cosmetics. 聚氧乙烯是许多洗涤剂和化妆品中的常见成分。|近义词：PEG (polyethylene glycol)|反义词：无|名词
