/ˌoʊvərprəˈvaɪdz/ | v. | 过度提供，供应过多 | To provide more of something than is needed or wanted.  | The government overprovides welfare benefits in some areas.  政府在某些地区过度提供福利。 |  over-supply, surplus | underprovide, undersupply | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company overprovides its employees with benefits. = 公司为员工提供了过多的福利。
They overprovide food at every party. = 他们每次聚会都准备过多的食物。


一般过去时:
The restaurant overprovided food for the event. = 餐厅为活动准备了过多的食物。
Last year, the government overprovided funds for the project. = 去年政府为该项目提供了过多的资金。


现在完成时:
The factory has overprovided parts for the new model. = 工厂为新款车型准备了过多的零件。
They have overprovided for the unexpected guests. = 他们为意外来客准备了过多的东西。


过去完成时:
The organizers had overprovided chairs for the conference. = 组织者为会议准备了过多的椅子。
We had overprovided supplies, so we had leftovers. = 我们准备了过多的物资，所以剩下很多。


将来时:
The school will overprovide textbooks for the new students. = 学校将为新生准备过多的教科书。
They will likely overprovide for the Christmas party. = 圣诞派对他们可能会准备过多的东西。


虚拟语气:
If they overprovided resources, the project would have succeeded. = 如果他们提供了足够的资源，项目就会成功。(This sentence uses overprovide in a contrary-to-fact situation, implying insufficient provision)  如果他们资源充足的话，项目本来会成功的。
I wish they hadn't overprovided so much food; we wasted half of it. = 我希望他们不要准备那么多食物；我们浪费了一半。


被动语态:
The students were overprovided with homework. = 学生们被布置了过多的作业。
The event was overprovided for; there was so much extra food. = 活动准备得过于充分；有很多多余的食物。


疑问句:
Did they overprovide for the picnic? = 野餐他们准备得过多吗？
Does the company overprovide its employees with vacation time? = 公司给员工的假期时间是否过多？


否定句:
The company did not overprovide for the crisis. = 公司没有为危机做好充分准备。(This negates the excessive provision, not necessarily implying sufficient provision) 公司没有为这场危机提供过多的资源。
They didn't overprovide; they had just the right amount. = 他们没有准备过多；数量刚刚好。



PHRASE->
overprovide for: 为...准备过多
overprovide with:  用...过度提供
overprovide resources: 资源供应过多
overprovide services: 服务过度提供
overprovide support: 过度支持
