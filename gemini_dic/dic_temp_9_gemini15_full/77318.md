gathering's:/ˈɡæðərɪŋz/| n.|聚会，集会；聚集的事物|The possessive form of "gathering," indicating something belonging to a gathering.  It refers to the things or aspects related to a gathering.  For example, the gathering's purpose, the gathering's attendees, or the gathering's location.|例句：The gathering's atmosphere was very lively.  这次聚会的氛围非常活跃。

The gathering's success was due to careful planning.  这次聚会的成功是由于周密的计划。|近义词：meeting's, assembly's, conference's|反义词：dispersion's, scattering's|名词
