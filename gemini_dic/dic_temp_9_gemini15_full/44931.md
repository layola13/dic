cryptogamous:/krɪpˈtɒɡəməs/| adj.|隐花植物的；孢子植物的| Relating to or denoting plants that reproduce by spores rather than by seeds, including ferns, mosses, and fungi.|  Cryptogamous plants are characterized by their lack of visible flowers and seeds. They reproduce through spores, which are tiny reproductive units dispersed by wind or water.| Example:  Mosses and ferns are examples of cryptogamous plants.|近义词：spore-bearing, flowerless|反义词：phanerogamous
