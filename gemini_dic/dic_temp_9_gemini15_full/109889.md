liaises:/liˈeɪz/ | v. | 联系，联络，协调 | To act as a link between people or groups of people.  |  例句：The project manager liaises with various departments to ensure smooth operation. 项目经理与各个部门联系以确保顺利运作。


例句：The embassy liaises closely with local authorities. 大使馆与当地政府密切联系。


近义词：connect, coordinate, communicate, collaborate | 反义词：isolate, separate, disconnect | 词性:动词
