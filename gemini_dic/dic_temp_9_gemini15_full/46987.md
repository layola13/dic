darwins:/ˈdɑːrwɪnz/| n.|达尔文（复数）|The plural of Darwin, referring to the evolutionary theories of Charles Darwin or his descendants.  It can also refer to units of evolutionary change.|例句：His research explores the impact of darwins on modern biology. 他的研究探讨了达尔文主义对现代生物学的影响。

例句：The conference celebrated 150 years of darwins and their continuing influence.  此次会议庆祝达尔文主义及其持续影响的150周年。


近义词：Darwinian theories, evolutionary theories


反义词：Creationism (in the context of opposing evolutionary theories)


词性:名词
