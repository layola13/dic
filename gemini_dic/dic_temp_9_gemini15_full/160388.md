popism:/ˈpɒpɪzəm/ | n. | 流行主义；通俗文化崇拜 | The worship of popular culture and its values, often characterized by a rejection of high culture and traditional values.  It emphasizes the transient and ephemeral nature of trends and fashions. | Example:  The rise of popism in the 1960s led to a questioning of established artistic norms.  流行主义在20世纪60年代的兴起导致人们对既定的艺术规范提出了质疑。| 近义词：popular culture, mass culture, commercialism | 反义词：high culture, elitism |  名词
