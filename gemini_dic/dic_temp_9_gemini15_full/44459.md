cross-training:/ˈkrɒsˌtreɪnɪŋ/ | n. |交叉训练，多项目训练 |The practice of engaging in a variety of exercises or activities to improve fitness and overall athletic performance. It helps prevent overuse injuries and promotes well-rounded physical development.  |例句：Cross-training is crucial for athletes to prevent injuries and improve overall fitness. 交叉训练对于运动员预防受伤和提高整体健康水平至关重要。|例句：She incorporates swimming and cycling into her cross-training routine to complement her running. 她将游泳和骑自行车融入她的交叉训练计划中，以补充她的跑步训练。|近义词：varied training, multidisciplinary training |反义词：specialized training, single-discipline training |名词
