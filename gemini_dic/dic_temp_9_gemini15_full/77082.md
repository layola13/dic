garreting:/ˈɡærɪtɪŋ/| n.| 阁楼居住；在阁楼居住|The act of living in a garret; the state of being in a garret.  It often implies poverty or cramped living conditions. |例句：The artist endured years of garreting before achieving recognition.  这位艺术家在获得认可之前忍受了多年的阁楼生活。|近义词：attic living, loft living|反义词：palatial living, luxurious living
