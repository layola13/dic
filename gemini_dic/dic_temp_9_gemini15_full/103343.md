jill:/dʒɪl/| n.|吉尔（女子名）|A female given name, of uncertain origin.  It may be related to the Old French word "Gilles," a variant of "Julius," or it may have a different, less certain etymology.|例句：My friend Jill is a talented artist. 我的朋友吉尔是一位有才华的艺术家。 Jill is going to the party tonight. 吉尔今晚要去参加派对。|近义词：Gill (variant spelling)|反义词：无
