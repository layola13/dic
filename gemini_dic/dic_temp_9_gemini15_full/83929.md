half-century:/ˈhæf ˈsɛntʃəri/| n.|半个世纪；五十年|A period of fifty years.  The term refers to a duration of time, specifically half of a century (100 years).| Example:  The company celebrated its half-century anniversary last year.  这家公司去年庆祝了它的五十周年纪念日。|近义词：fifty years, five decades|反义词：century

词源:
half- + century.  "Half" meaning one of two equal parts, and "century" deriving from the Latin "centuria," meaning "a group of one hundred."  Therefore, "half-century" literally means "half of a hundred years."
