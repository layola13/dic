nonreceiving: /ˌnɒn.rɪˈsiː.vɪŋ/ | adj. | 未接收的；不接收的 | Not receiving something.  This adjective describes something or someone that is not in the process of receiving something, whether it be a signal, information, goods, or anything else. |


例句：

The nonreceiving end of the communication line experienced a complete outage.  = 通信线路的非接收端完全中断。

He reported a nonreceiving status for the package.  = 他报告该包裹未收到。


近义词：unreceived, unreceptive


反义词：receiving, receptive


词性：形容词
