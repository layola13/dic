callback:/ˈkɔːlbæk/ /ˈkɔːlˌbæk/| n. |回调函数；回拨电话|A callback is a function that is executed after another function has completed its execution.  It's often used in asynchronous programming to handle the results of an operation. In telecommunications, it refers to a return phone call.|Example:  The server sent a callback to inform the client of the successful data upload. 服务器发送回调以通知客户端数据上传成功。|近义词：return call, response function|反义词：N/A|名词
