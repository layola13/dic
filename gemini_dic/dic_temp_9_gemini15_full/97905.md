innings:/ˈɪnɪŋz/| n.| 局；回合；比赛阶段 | A period of play in a game, especially in baseball or cricket.  It refers to a turn at batting or bowling. |例句：The home team had a strong innings in the second half of the game. 主队在下半场有一个强势的局。

例句：Our team scored 200 runs in their innings. 我们队在本局比赛中得了200分。


词源:  From Old French *enning, from enner "to enclose, confine".  The modern spelling reflects the influence of the suffix "-ings".


近义词: period, turn, phase


反义词:  None directly applicable.  The concept of an "innings" doesn't have a direct opposite.  Perhaps "break" or "intermission" could be considered in certain contexts.
