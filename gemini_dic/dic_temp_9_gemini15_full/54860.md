disple:/dɪsˈpliːz/| v.|使不高兴，使不悦|To cause someone to be unhappy or dissatisfied.  |例句：Her behavior displeased her parents. 她的行为使她的父母不高兴。|例句：The news displeased him greatly. 这个消息使他非常不高兴。|近义词：annoy, upset, offend|反义词：please, delight, gratify|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His actions displease his boss. = 他的行为让老板不高兴。
The cold weather displeases many people. = 寒冷的天气让很多人不高兴。

一般过去时:
The result displeased the team. = 结果让团队不高兴。
The movie displeased the critics. = 这部电影让影评人不满。

现在完成时:
His constant complaining has displeased everyone. = 他不断的抱怨已经惹恼了所有人。
The new policy has displeased many customers. = 新政策让很多顾客不满。

过去完成时:
He had displeased his teacher by being late. = 他因为迟到而惹恼了他的老师。
They had displeased their parents by not calling. = 他们因为没打电话而惹恼了父母。

将来时:
The changes will displease some of the employees. = 这些改变将会让一些员工不满。
I fear that my decision will displease many people. = 我担心我的决定会让很多人不高兴。

虚拟语气:
I wish my actions hadn't displeased you. = 我希望我的行为没有让你不高兴。
If I had known it would displease him, I wouldn't have done it. = 如果我知道这会让他不高兴，我不会这么做的。

被动语态:
He was displeased by the news. = 他对这个消息感到不高兴。
The manager was displeased with the team's performance. = 经理对团队的表现感到不满。

疑问句:
Does this situation displease you? = 这种情况让你不高兴吗？
Will my request displease the authorities? = 我的请求会让当局不高兴吗？

否定句:
The outcome did not displease the investors. = 结果并没有让投资者不高兴。
I hope my actions will not displease you. = 我希望我的行为不会让你不高兴。


PHRASE->
be displeased with = 对...感到不满
feel displeased = 感到不悦
express displeasure = 表达不满
show displeasure = 表现出不满
cause displeasure = 引起不满
