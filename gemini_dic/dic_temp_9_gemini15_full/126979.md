narwals:/ˈnɑːrwəlz/| n.|独角鲸|A narwhal is an Arctic whale with a long, spiraled tusk that projects from its upper jaw.  It's known for its unique tusk and its ability to dive to great depths.|Example:  The narwhal is a fascinating creature of the Arctic. 独角鲸是北极一种令人着迷的生物。

The pod of narwals migrated south for the winter.  一群独角鲸向南迁徙过冬。|近义词：Arctic whale|反义词：无|词性：名词
