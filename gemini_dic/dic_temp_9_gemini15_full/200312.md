skulked:/skʌlkt/| v.|偷偷摸摸地走；躲藏；|to move secretly and stealthily, typically to avoid being seen or noticed.  |例句：He skulked around the corner, hoping no one would see him. 他偷偷摸摸地走到拐角处，希望没人看见他。

例句：The thief skulked in the shadows, waiting for the right moment to strike. 小偷躲在阴影里，等待着合适的时机下手。


近义词：snuck, slunk, crept,  lurked


反义词：strutted, swaggered, paraded


词源:  The word "skulk" ultimately derives from the Old Norse word *skulk, meaning "to hide" or "to lurk".  Its meaning and usage have remained largely consistent over time, referring to secretive and furtive movement.
