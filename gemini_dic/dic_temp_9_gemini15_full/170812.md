pseudoenthusiastic: /ˌsuːdoʊɛnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/ | adj. | 假装热情的，装腔作势的 |  Seeming enthusiastic but not genuinely feeling that way; showing false or affected enthusiasm.  | 例句：He gave a pseudoenthusiastic speech about the new project, but his lack of eye contact betrayed his true feelings. 他发表了一个关于新项目的假装热情的演讲，但他缺乏眼神交流暴露了他的真实感受。


例句：Her pseudoenthusiastic clapping felt more like a performance than genuine appreciation.  她假装热烈的鼓掌更像是一种表演而不是真正的欣赏。


近义词：affected, insincere, perfunctory, unenthusiastic (反义词)


反义词：genuine, sincere, enthusiastic, passionate


词性：形容词
