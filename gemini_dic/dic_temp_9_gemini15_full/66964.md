experimentally:/ɪkˌsperɪˈmɛntəli/ | adv. | 实验地；凭经验地 |  In a way that relates to or is based on experiments.  It suggests that something is done or happens as a result of or in connection with an experiment. | 例句：The new drug was tested experimentally on animals before human trials. 新药在人体试验前先在动物身上进行了实验。  The hypothesis was experimentally verified.  该假设已通过实验验证。| 近义词：empirically, experimentally | 反义词：theoretically, hypothetically |  词性：副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They experimentally test new materials. = 他们实验性地测试新材料。
We experimentally determine the reaction rate. = 我们通过实验确定反应速率。

一般过去时:
Scientists experimentally verified the theory. = 科学家们通过实验验证了这个理论。
The engineers experimentally analyzed the bridge's stability. = 工程师们通过实验分析了桥梁的稳定性。

现在完成时:
Researchers have experimentally demonstrated the effect. = 研究人员已经通过实验证明了这种效应。
They have experimentally explored various approaches. = 他们已经实验性地探索了各种方法。

过去完成时:
They had experimentally determined the parameters before the main experiment. = 在主要实验之前，他们已经通过实验确定了参数。
Scientists had experimentally shown the phenomenon previously. = 科学家们之前已经通过实验展示了这种现象。


将来时:
We will experimentally investigate the impact of temperature. = 我们将通过实验研究温度的影响。
They will experimentally evaluate the new design. = 他们将实验性地评估新的设计。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would experimentally study it further. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们会进一步进行实验研究。
If they had the equipment, they would experimentally test the hypothesis. = 如果他们有设备，他们就会实验性地检验这个假设。


被动语态:
The new method was experimentally applied to the problem. = 新方法被实验性地应用于该问题。
The data were experimentally obtained through rigorous testing. = 数据是通过严格的测试实验获得的。


疑问句:
Was the experiment conducted experimentally? = 实验是实验性进行的吗？

否定句:

The theory was not experimentally supported. = 该理论没有得到实验的支持。
