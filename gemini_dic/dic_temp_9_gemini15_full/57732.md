dreadnaughts:/ˈdrɛdnɔːts/| n.|无畏舰|A dreadnought is a type of battleship that was dominant in the early 20th century, characterized by its all-big-gun armament and high speed.  These ships rendered older battleships obsolete.|The HMS Dreadnought revolutionized naval warfare.  无畏号战列舰彻底改变了海军作战方式。|battleship, warship|小型舰艇, 旧式战列舰|
