noncrusading: /ˌnɒnˈkruːzeɪdɪŋ/ | adj. |非战斗性的；非改革的；不热衷于改革的 | Not actively engaged in or advocating for a cause or reform.  The term implies a lack of fervent advocacy or missionary zeal. | 例句：His approach to politics was noncrusading, preferring pragmatic compromise to ideological battles. 他的政治方法是非战斗性的，宁愿采取务实的妥协而不是意识形态的斗争。

His noncrusading nature made him a good mediator in disputes.  他非战斗的性格使他成为纠纷中一个优秀的调解人。


近义词：uncrusading, moderate, pragmatic, conciliatory, compromising


反义词：crusading, zealous, militant, activist, reformist


词性：形容词
