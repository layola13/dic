jargoneer:/ˌdʒɑːrɡəˈnɪər/ | v. |使用行话；说行话；说专业术语 | To speak or write in jargon; to use obscure or technical language. |例句：The professor jargoneered throughout the lecture, losing many students in the process.  教授在整个讲座中都在使用行话，导致许多学生听不懂。

例句：He jargoneered so much that nobody understood his explanation. 他说得太过专业，以至于没有人理解他的解释。


近义词：gibber, babble,  waffle,  technicalize


反义词：clarify, elucidate, simplify
