inkwells:/ˈɪŋkwɛlz/| n.|墨水瓶；墨水盒|A small container, typically made of ceramic, glass, or metal, used to hold ink for writing with a pen or quill.  Inkwells were common before the invention of fountain pens and ballpoint pens.|Example: The old desk contained several antique inkwells. 这张旧书桌上摆着几个古董墨水瓶。  The writer dipped his quill into the inkwell. 作家将鹅毛笔蘸进墨水瓶里。|近义词：inkpot, inkholder|反义词：fountain pen, ballpoint pen
