encryptor: /ɪnˈkrɪptər/ | n. | 加密器，加密程序 | A device or program that encrypts data, making it unreadable without the decryption key.  | Example: The encryptor scrambled the confidential documents before transmission. 加密器在传输之前对机密文件进行了加密。 |近义词：cipher, encoder |反义词：decryptor
