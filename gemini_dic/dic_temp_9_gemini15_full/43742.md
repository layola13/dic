creep:/kriːp/| v. n. | 爬行；缓慢移动；令人毛骨悚然的人或事物 | To move slowly and quietly, often in a stealthy or cautious manner;  a person who is unpleasant or causes unease. |例句：The child crept slowly across the room.  孩子慢慢地爬过房间。 The old house had a creepy atmosphere. 这座老房子有一种令人毛骨悚然的气氛。|近义词：crawl, slither,  infiltrate (v.),  creepy, spooky (n.)|反义词：stride, rush,  charming, pleasant (n.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The spider creeps across the ceiling. = 蜘蛛在爬过天花板。
He creeps up on his victims. = 他悄悄地接近他的受害者。


一般过去时:
The cat crept silently into the room. = 猫悄悄地溜进了房间。
She crept away unnoticed. = 她偷偷溜走了，没有被人注意到。


现在完成时:
The plant has crept up the wall. = 植物已经爬上了墙。
Fear has crept into her heart. = 恐惧已经渗入了她的内心。


过去完成时:
He had crept into bed before his parents came home. = 他在父母回家之前已经偷偷溜到床上睡觉了。
The thief had crept away with the jewels. = 小偷已经带着珠宝偷偷溜走了。


将来时:
The ivy will creep over the house. = 常春藤将爬满房子。
I think he will creep up on us. = 我认为他会悄悄地接近我们。


虚拟语气:
If I were a spider, I would creep along the wall. = 如果我是一只蜘蛛，我就会沿着墙爬行。
I wish I hadn't crept into that old house. = 我真希望我没有溜进那座老房子。


被动语态:
The wall was crept over by ivy. = 墙壁被常春藤爬满了。
The plan was crept into cautiously. = 这个计划被谨慎地渗透进去了。


疑问句:
Does the cat creep around at night? = 猫晚上是不是会偷偷摸摸地四处走动？
Is he creeping up on me? = 他是不是在悄悄地接近我？


否定句:
The child did not creep out of bed. = 孩子没有偷偷摸摸地爬下床。
He didn't creep away quietly. = 他没有悄悄地溜走。


PHRASE->

creep out: 令人毛骨悚然
creep along: 缓慢移动
creep up:逐渐增加
