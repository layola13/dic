downloader:/ˈdaʊnˌloʊdər/| n. |下载器，下载程序|A downloader is a program or piece of software that allows users to download files from the internet or a network.|例句：I use a dedicated video downloader to save my favorite clips. 我使用一个专门的视频下载器来保存我喜欢的剪辑。|例句：This software is a powerful file downloader, supporting various formats. 这个软件是一个强大的文件下载器，支持各种格式。|近义词：download manager, client|反义词：uploader
