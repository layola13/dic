septentrion:/sepˈtɛntrɪən/| n. adj.|北方，北风；北方的|The word "septentrion" refers to the north, particularly the northern constellations or the north wind. It evokes a sense of coldness and remoteness.  The word is less common in modern usage, often replaced by simpler terms like "north".|例句：The septentrion winds howled fiercely throughout the night.  北风整夜狂吼。|The septentrion star was used by ancient sailors for navigation. 古代水手用北极星导航。|近义词：north, boreal, arctic|反义词：south, austral, meridional|名词，形容词
