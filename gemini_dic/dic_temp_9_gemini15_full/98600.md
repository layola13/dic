instructor:/ɪnˈstrʌktər/ | n. | 教师，指导者 | A person who teaches or instructs others, especially in a specific subject or skill.  |例句：He is a skilled instructor at the driving school. 他是驾校里一位技艺娴熟的教练。 |例句：The yoga instructor guided the class through a series of poses.  瑜伽教练引导学员们完成一系列体式。|近义词：teacher, tutor, trainer, coach |反义词：student, learner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The instructor teaches grammar. = 教师教授语法。
The instructor explains the lesson. = 教师解释课程。

一般过去时:
The instructor taught us history. = 教师教我们历史。
The instructor explained the concept clearly yesterday. = 教师昨天清晰地解释了这个概念。

现在完成时:
The instructor has taught this course many times. = 该教师已经多次教授这门课程。
The instructor has finished explaining the assignment. = 教师已经完成了对作业的讲解。

过去完成时:
The instructor had already explained the rules before the students arrived. = 学生到达之前，教师已经解释了规则。
The instructor had prepared the materials before the class started. = 课程开始前，教师已经准备好了材料。

将来时:
The instructor will teach a new course next semester. = 教师下学期将教授一门新课程。
The instructor will explain the exam results tomorrow. = 教师明天将解释考试结果。

虚拟语气:
If the instructor were here, he would answer your question. = 如果老师在这儿，他会回答你的问题。
I wish the instructor had given us more time. = 我希望老师给我们更多的时间。

被动语态:
The lesson was instructed by a very experienced teacher. = 这节课是由一位经验丰富的老师教授的。
The students were instructed to complete the assignment by Friday. = 学生们被指示在星期五之前完成作业。

疑问句:
Who is your instructor? = 你的老师是谁？
What does the instructor expect from us? = 教师对我们有什么期望？

否定句:
The instructor didn't explain the assignment clearly. = 教师没有清楚地解释作业。
He wasn't the instructor, he was a student. = 他不是老师，他是一个学生。
