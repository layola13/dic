jalousied:/ˈʒæluːziːd/| n. |百叶窗；嫉妒；屏风| A jalousied window is a window fitted with Venetian blinds or louvers.  The word also refers to a feeling of envy or jealousy, though this usage is less common.  It can also describe a screen or partition, often made of latticework.|例句：The afternoon sun streamed through the jalousied windows. 下午的阳光透过百叶窗照射进来。|例句：Her success filled her colleagues with a degree of jalousied. 她的成功让同事们多少有些嫉妒。|近义词：blind (window covering), shutter, envy, jealousy|反义词：admiration, contentment
