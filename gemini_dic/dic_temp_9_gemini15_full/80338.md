goldfinny:/ˈɡoʊldˌfɪni/|/ˈɡəʊldˌfɪni/| n. |金翅雀|A small, colorful bird with a distinctive red, white, and black face and yellow wings and body.  Goldfinches are known for their cheerful songs and acrobatic feeding habits.|例句：The goldfinny perched on the sunflower, delicately picking out the seeds.  金翅雀停在向日葵上，细致地啄食种子。

例句：We saw a flock of goldfinnies feeding on thistles in the meadow. 我们看到一群金翅雀在草地上吃蓟。


近义词：goldfinch (more common and generally preferred term)


反义词：无


词性: 名词
