platan:/ˈplætən/| n.|悬铃木; 梧桐树 |A platan is a large deciduous tree with broad leaves, belonging to the genus Platanus.  They are known for their distinctive mottled bark and are often planted as ornamentals. |例句：The ancient platan tree shaded the entire park. 这棵古老的悬铃木为整个公园遮荫。|例句： We rested under the shade of a majestic platan. 我们在高大的梧桐树下休息。|近义词：sycamore (especially in North America), plane tree|反义词：无
