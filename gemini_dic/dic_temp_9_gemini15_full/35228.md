cluing:/ˈkluːɪŋ/| n.|线索；提示|The act of providing clues or hints.  It often refers to the process of giving hints or suggestions, especially in a puzzle or game.|例句：The detective work involved careful cluing to solve the mystery.  侦探工作需要仔细地寻找线索来解开谜团。|近义词：hinting, prompting, guiding|反义词：obscuring, concealing|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher is cluing the students about the upcoming test. = 老师正在给学生们提示即将到来的考试。
He is cluing me in on the secret project. = 他正在向我透露秘密项目的信息。


一般过去时:
She clued me in on the surprise party. = 她向我透露了惊喜派对的信息。
The author cleverly clued the reader towards the murderer's identity. = 作者巧妙地向读者暗示了凶手的身份。


现在完成时:
They have clued us in on the new marketing strategy. = 他们已经向我们透露了新的营销策略。
Has she clued you in on what's going on? = 她有没有告诉你发生了什么事？


过去完成时:
He had clued her in on the problem before the meeting. = 他在会议前就已经向她透露了这个问题。
I had clued them in on the secret earlier that day. = 我在那天早些时候就已经向他们透露了秘密。


将来时:
She will clue us in on the details later. = 她稍后会向我们透露细节。
They will be cluing the contestants in on the next challenge. = 他们将向参赛者提示下一个挑战。


虚拟语气:
If he had clued me in sooner, I would have been better prepared. = 如果他早点告诉我，我会准备得更好。
I wish she had clued me in on the changes. = 我希望她能告诉我这些变化。


被动语态:
The contestants were clued in on the rules of the game. = 参赛者们被告知了游戏的规则。
The students are being clued in about the upcoming exam. = 学生们正在被告知即将到来的考试。


疑问句:
Has he clued you in on the latest news? = 他有没有告诉你最新的消息？
Are they going to clue us in on the plan? = 他们会告诉我们计划吗？


否定句:
He didn't clue me in on the important details. = 他没有告诉我重要的细节。
They haven't clued us in on the changes yet. = 他们还没有告诉我们这些变化。


PHRASE->
clue someone in = 告诉某人内情
clue someone up =  给某人提示
clue somebody in to something = 告诉某人某事
a clue to something = 某事的线索
follow a clue = 寻找线索
