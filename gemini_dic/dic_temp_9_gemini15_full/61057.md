ellagic: /ɛˈlædʒɪk/ | n. |鞣花酸| A polyphenol found in many plants, known for its potential antioxidant and anticancer properties.  Ellagic acid is a naturally occurring polyphenol found in various fruits, nuts, and vegetables. It's known for its potent antioxidant properties and has been studied for its potential role in cancer prevention and other health benefits.  |Example:  Studies have shown that ellagic acid may help protect against certain types of cancer.  研究表明，鞣花酸可能有助于预防某些类型的癌症。|近义词:  (no direct synonyms, but other polyphenols with similar antioxidant properties)|反义词: (no direct antonyms)|名词
