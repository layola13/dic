failance:/ˈfeɪləns/| n.|失败，缺点，不足|A failance is a failure or a shortcoming.  It refers to a defect, flaw, or deficiency in something or someone.  It is often used in a more formal context. |例句：The project's failance was attributed to poor planning.  该项目的失败归咎于糟糕的计划。 The main failance of the argument is its lack of evidence.  该论点的主要缺陷是缺乏证据。|近义词：failure, deficiency, shortcoming, flaw, defect|反义词：success, strength, advantage
