cross-sectoral: /ˌkrɒsˈsɛktərəl/| adj. |跨部门的，跨行业的|  Relating to or involving more than one sector of activity or industry.  It suggests collaboration and integration between different sectors to achieve a common goal. |Example: The project requires a cross-sectoral approach, bringing together experts from government, academia, and industry.  这个项目需要跨部门的合作方式，汇聚政府、学术界和产业界的专家。|近义词: intersectoral, multisectoral, interdisciplinary |反义词: sector-specific, single-sector
