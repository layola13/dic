clampdowns:/ˈklæmpdaʊnz/| n. |镇压；压制；严厉措施 |The act of suppressing something, especially the expression of opinions or activities.  It often refers to a sudden and forceful action taken by authorities to control something considered undesirable. |例句：There have been recent clampdowns on illegal immigration. 近期对非法移民进行了严厉打击。

例句：The government imposed a clampdown on dissent after the protests.  政府在抗议活动后对异见进行了压制。


近义词：suppression, crackdown, repression, restrictions


反义词：tolerance, leniency, freedom


词性:名词
