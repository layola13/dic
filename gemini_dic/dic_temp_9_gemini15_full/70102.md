feverous: /ˈfiːvərəs/ | adj. | 发烧的；狂热的；兴奋的 | Characterized by or showing great energy and excitement; feverish activity.  | 例句：The feverish pace of the construction project left workers exhausted.  建筑项目的紧张节奏使工人筋疲力尽。

例句：He was in a feverish state, his temperature soaring. 他处于发烧状态，体温飙升。


近义词：excited, enthusiastic, passionate, fervent, hectic | 反义词：calm, tranquil, apathetic, lethargic


词源:  Derived from "fever" + "-ous" (a suffix indicating full of or characterized by).  The word's evolution reflects the association of fever with intense activity and excitement.
