jambool:/ˈdʒæmbuːl/| n. |香果, 榄仁树果实 |A jambool is the fruit of the Syzygium cumini tree, also known as the Java plum, black plum, or jambolan.  It is a dark purple or black, oval-shaped fruit with a sweet and slightly astringent taste.  |例句：The jambool trees were laden with ripe fruit. 榄仁树上挂满了成熟的果实。|例句：The jambool juice is a popular drink in some regions. 香果汁在一些地区很受欢迎。|近义词：Java plum, black plum, jambolan |反义词:无
