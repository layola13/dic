delegate:/ˈdɛləɡeɪt/| n., vt.|代表，委托人；委派，授权|A person sent or authorized to represent others, especially at a conference.  To entrust (a task or responsibility) to another person, typically one who is less senior than oneself.|例句：She's a delegate to the UN.  她是一位联合国代表。
The manager delegated the task to his assistant.  经理将任务委托给了他的助理。|近义词：representative, envoy, assign, entrust|反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He delegates many tasks to his team. = 他把许多任务委托给他的团队。
She delegates responsibilities effectively. = 她有效地委派职责。


一般过去时:
The committee delegated the responsibility to a subcommittee. = 委员会将责任委托给了一个小组委员会。
He delegated the project to a junior member of staff. = 他将项目委托给了初级员工。


现在完成时:
She has delegated all her work for the week. = 她已经委托了她一周的所有工作。
They have delegated the responsibility to a third party. = 他们已将责任委托给第三方。


过去完成时:
The board had delegated the decision-making power before the meeting. = 董事会在会议之前已经授权了决策权。
He had delegated the task before leaving for vacation. = 他在休假前已经委托了这项任务。


将来时:
She will delegate the task to her most experienced employee. = 她将把这项任务委托给经验最丰富的员工。
They will delegate the responsibility to the project manager.  = 他们将把责任委托给项目经理。


虚拟语气:
If I were the manager, I would delegate more tasks. = 如果我是经理，我会委托更多的任务。
It would be best if he delegated some of his workload. = 最好他能委托一些他的工作量。


被动语态:
The task was delegated to the new employee. = 任务被委托给了新员工。
The responsibilities were delegated fairly among the team members. = 责任在团队成员之间被公平地分配。


疑问句:
Who will you delegate this project to? = 你将把这个项目委托给谁？
To whom was the responsibility delegated? = 责任委托给了谁？


否定句:
She didn't delegate any tasks today. = 她今天没有委托任何任务。
He refused to delegate any of his responsibilities. = 他拒绝委托任何他的责任。
