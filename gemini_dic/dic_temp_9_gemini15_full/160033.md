polythalamous: /ˌpɒliˈθæləməs/ | adj. | 多室的；多心皮的 | Having many chambers or locules, especially referring to the ovary of a flower or the brain.  The term is used primarily in botany and anatomy.  | Example:  The polythalamous ovary of the poppy contains numerous seeds.  罂粟的多室子房包含许多种子。


The polythalamous brain structure is complex and fascinating.  多室脑结构复杂而迷人。|近义词: multilocular, multi-chambered | 反义词: unilocular, unicameral
