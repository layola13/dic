excise:/ˈɛksaɪz/ | vt., n. | 删去；割除；征收消费税 | to remove something, especially by cutting it out; a tax on certain goods produced or sold within a country | 近义词：remove, delete, cut out; levy, duty | 反义词：insert, add


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The editor excises irrelevant details from the manuscript. = 编辑删去了稿件中无关紧要的细节。
The government excises a significant amount of tax on tobacco. = 政府对烟草征收了大量的税。


一般过去时:
The surgeon excised the tumor successfully. = 外科医生成功地切除了肿瘤。
They excised that section of the film. = 他们删掉了电影的那一部分。


现在完成时:
The censors have excised several scenes from the movie. = 审查员已经从电影中删减了几场戏。
The government has excised substantial taxes on alcohol. = 政府对酒精征收了大量的税。


过去完成时:
The author had excised many passages before submitting the manuscript. = 作者在提交稿件之前已经删去了许多段落。
The country had excised heavy taxes on imported goods previously. = 该国之前曾对进口商品征收重税。


将来时:
The editor will excise any inappropriate language. = 编辑将删除任何不合适的语言。
The new law will excise additional taxes on luxury goods. = 新法律将对奢侈品征收额外的税。


虚拟语气:
If they excised that paragraph, the article would be much clearer. = 如果他们删掉那段话，文章就会清晰得多。
I wish they had excised the offensive scene from the movie. = 我希望他们能从电影中删掉那个令人反感的场景。


被动语态:
The offending passages were excised from the book. = 书中冒犯的段落被删掉了。
Heavy taxes are excised on fuel in many countries. = 许多国家对燃料征收重税。


疑问句:
Will the director excise any scenes from the film? = 导演会从电影中删掉任何场景吗？
Does the government excise taxes on gasoline? = 政府对汽油征收消费税吗？


否定句:
The editor did not excise any of the author's original work. = 编辑没有删掉作者的任何原创作品。
The government does not excise taxes on essential goods. = 政府不对生活必需品征收消费税。


PHRASE->
excise tax: 消费税

词源:
From Old French *excider, from Latin excidere "to cut out".
