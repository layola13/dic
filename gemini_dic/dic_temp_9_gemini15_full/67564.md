extrapolatable: /ɪkˈstræpəleɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可外推的，可推断的 |  Capable of being extrapolated; able to be extended beyond known limits by inference.  It refers to data, trends, or models that can be logically extended to predict future outcomes or conditions. |

例句：

The current sales figures are extrapolatable to predict future revenue.  = 目前的销售数据可以外推预测未来的收入。

The research findings are extrapolatable to a wider population. = 研究结果可以推断到更广泛的人群。


近义词：projectable, inferable, predictable

反义词：unpredictable, uninferable
