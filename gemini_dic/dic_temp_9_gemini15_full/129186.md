nobel:/ˈnoʊbəl/ | /ˈnəʊbəl/ | adj. n. |诺贝尔的；高贵的；杰出的 |Relating to Alfred Nobel, the inventor of dynamite, and the Nobel Prizes awarded annually in his honor.  The word can also mean "noble" or "exalted". |例句：The Nobel Prize in Literature is a prestigious award.  = 诺贝尔文学奖是一个极负盛名的奖项。

The Nobel Peace Prize was awarded to Malala Yousafzai. = 诺贝尔和平奖被授予马拉拉·优素福扎伊。

近义词：exalted, distinguished, eminent | 反义词：ignoble, base, common
