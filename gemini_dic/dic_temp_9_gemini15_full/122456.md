mobilised:/ˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/| /ˈməʊbəlaɪzd/ | v. |动员；调动；使…活动起来 | To assemble and prepare (troops, resources, etc.) for action or a specific purpose.  It can also refer to the act of making something active or energetic. |  例句：The army was mobilised to defend the border.  军队已动员起来保卫边境。

例句：The company mobilised its resources to launch the new product. 公司动员了资源来推出新产品。


近义词：muster, rally, activate, marshal

反义词：demobilize, deactivate, disband
