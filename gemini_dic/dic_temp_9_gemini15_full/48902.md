defrayed:/dɪˈfreɪd/| vt.|支付，负担（费用）|to pay (a cost or expense)|The company defrayed the travel expenses of its employees. 公司支付了员工的差旅费。|近义词：pay, cover, meet|反义词：owe, incur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company defrays the travel expenses. = 公司支付差旅费。
He defrays his own college fees. = 他自己支付大学学费。


一般过去时:
They defrayed the cost of the repairs. = 他们支付了修理费用。
The organization defrayed my travel expenses to the conference. = 该组织支付了我参加会议的差旅费。


现在完成时:
We have defrayed all the costs associated with the project. = 我们已经支付了与该项目相关的所有费用。
She has defrayed the expenses from her savings. = 她用积蓄支付了这些费用。


过去完成时:
They had defrayed the initial costs before the project began. = 项目开始前他们已经支付了初始费用。
He had defrayed all his debts before he retired. = 他退休前已经还清了所有债务。


将来时:
The government will defray the cost of the new bridge. = 政府将支付新建桥梁的费用。
We will defray the expenses from the grant. = 我们将用拨款支付费用。


虚拟语气:
If the company defrayed the travel expenses, more employees would attend the conference. = 如果公司支付差旅费，更多员工将参加会议。
I wish the insurance company had defrayed the medical expenses. = 我希望保险公司已经支付了医疗费用。


被动语态:
The costs were defrayed by the government. = 费用由政府支付。
The travel expenses will be defrayed by the company. = 差旅费将由公司支付。


疑问句:
Will the company defray the costs of the training? = 公司会支付培训费用吗？
How will the expenses be defrayed? = 费用将如何支付？


否定句:
The company did not defray the costs of the project. = 公司没有支付该项目的费用。
They could not defray the unexpected expenses. = 他们无法支付意外的费用。


PHRASE->
defray the expenses = 支付费用
defray the cost = 负担成本
defray the charges = 支付费用
defray the bills = 支付账单
fully defray = 全额支付


词源:
From Old French *defreier, from des- (from Latin dis-) + freier "to load, freight, equip," from Frankish *frijon, from Proto-Germanic *frijōn- "to carry, transport."  The modern meaning shifted from "to provide for" to "to pay the cost of."
