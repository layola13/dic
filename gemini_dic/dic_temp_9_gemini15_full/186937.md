riposte:/rɪˈpɒst/| n., v.| 迅速的反击；机智的回答 | A quick, clever reply to an insult or criticism; a quick return of attack. |例句：Her riposte was so sharp that he had no answer. 她的反驳如此尖锐，他无言以对。
He riposted with a witty remark. 他以一句俏皮话进行了反击。|近义词：retort, reply, rejoinder |反义词：submission, surrender


USAGE->

He ripostes with a clever answer. = 他以一个巧妙的答案进行反击。
She ripostes to every criticism. = 她对每一个批评都进行反击。


一般过去时:

She has riposted to her critics many times. = 她多次反驳了批评者。


过去完成时:

She had riposted brilliantly, silencing her opponent. = 她精彩地反驳，让对手哑口无言。
