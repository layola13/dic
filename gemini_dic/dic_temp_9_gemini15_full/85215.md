harkening:/ˈhɑːrkənɪŋ/ | v. |倾听，留心 | To listen attentively; to give heed.  The word implies a careful and often expectant listening. | 例句：He stood harkening to the distant sounds of the city. 他站在那里，仔细聆听着远处城市的声音。


例句：The old woman sat by the fire, harkening to the wind howling outside. 老妇人坐在火炉旁，倾听着屋外呼啸的风声。


近义词：listening, attending, heeding | 反义词：ignoring, disregarding | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harkens to the advice of his elders. 他倾听长辈的建议。
She harkens to the rhythm of the music. 她聆听着音乐的节奏。

一般过去时:
They harkened to the siren's call. 他们听到了警报声。
We harkened to the speaker's words. 我们仔细聆听了演讲者的发言。

现在完成时:
I have harkened to your pleas. 我已经听到了你的请求。
She has harkened to the warnings. 她已经听取了警告。

过去完成时:
He had harkened to the rumors before making a decision. 他在做决定之前已经听取了谣言。
They had harkened to the old tales for hours. 他们已经倾听那些古老的故事几个小时了。

将来时:
We will harken to the judge's ruling. 我们将听从法官的裁决。
They will harken to the advice of their mentors. 他们将听取导师的建议。

虚拟语气:
If I had harkened to your warning, I wouldn't be in this mess. 如果我听了你的警告，我就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had harkened to my intuition. 我真希望我当时听了我的直觉。

被动语态:
His words were harkened to by the crowd.  他的话受到了人群的倾听。 (Note: Passive voice is less common with "harken")
The prophecy was harkened to with great reverence.  人们怀着极大的敬意倾听了预言。(Note: Passive voice is less common with "harken")

疑问句:
Will you harken to my request? 你会听取我的请求吗？
Did they harken to the instructions? 他们听从指示了吗？

否定句:
He did not harken to the warnings. 他没有听从警告。
They did not harken to reason. 他们没有听从劝告。


PHRASE->
harken back to: 回忆起，追溯到
harken unto: 听从，服从
harken to the call of: 响应...的号召
harken to the voice of: 倾听...的声音
to harken and to heed: 倾听并注意


词源:
From Old English *heorcnian, related to *heorcnian "to listen," and ultimately to the Proto-Germanic *haurkōną.  The word retains a strong archaic feel, suggesting a more deliberate and attentive listening than simply "listening."
