jabberer:/ˈdʒæbərər/| n. |喋喋不休的人，唠叨的人 |A jabberer is a person who talks rapidly and incessantly, often incoherently.  They may be excited, nervous, or simply have a habit of talking too much. |例句：He was a nervous jabberer, unable to articulate his thoughts clearly. 他是个紧张的喋喋不休的人，无法清晰地表达自己的想法。

例句：The old woman was a jabberer, filling the room with her endless stories.  这位老妇人是个喋喋不休的人，她的无尽故事充满了整个房间。


近义词：chatterbox, blabbermouth, babbler, talkative person |反义词：silent person, quiet person

词性：名词
