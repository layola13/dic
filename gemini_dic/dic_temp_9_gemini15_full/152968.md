pedigrees:/ˈpedɪɡriːz/| n. |血统；家谱；谱系|A pedigree is a record of the ancestry of a person or animal, showing the line of descent from ancestors.  It often includes information about the characteristics and traits inherited through generations.  The term can also refer to the lineage itself, implying high-quality ancestry. |例句：The dog's pedigree showed it was a champion bloodline. 这只狗的血统证明它来自冠军血统。


The thoroughbred horse has a distinguished pedigree.  这匹纯种马拥有显赫的血统。|近义词：lineage, ancestry, genealogy, family tree |反义词：none (lack of pedigree can be described with terms like "unknown origins" or "common ancestry")
