frothily:/ˈfrɒθɪli/|/ˈfroʊθɪli/| adj.|泡沫状地；轻浮地；轻率地 |Characterized by or producing froth; light and airy; frivolous and superficial.  The word implies a lack of substance or seriousness. |例句：The champagne flowed frothily. 香槟酒泡沫状地流淌着。

The river frothed frothily after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，河水泡沫状地翻滚着。


近义词：frivolously, airily, effervescently, superficially

反义词：seriously, solemnly, gravely, profoundly
