inlist:/ɪnˈlɪst/| v.|登录，入伍，征募|to officially join the army, navy, etc., or to persuade someone to join|Example: He enlisted in the army at the age of 18. 他18岁时参军。  He tried to enlist the help of his friends. 他试图寻求朋友们的帮助。|近义词：enroll, register, recruit|反义词：discharge, demobilize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enlists in the army next month. = 他下个月参军。
They enlist new recruits every year. = 他们每年都招募新兵。

一般过去时:
He enlisted in the navy during the war. = 他在战争期间加入了海军。
She enlisted the support of her colleagues.  = 她争取到了同事们的支持。

现在完成时:
He has enlisted in the air force. = 他已经加入了空军。
She has enlisted the help of several experts. = 她已经寻求了几位专家的帮助。

过去完成时:
He had enlisted before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前他已经参军了。
They had enlisted the services of a top lawyer. = 他们已经聘请了一位顶级律师。

将来时:
He will enlist in the marines after graduation. = 他毕业后将加入海军陆战队。
They will enlist the help of volunteers. = 他们将寻求志愿者的帮助。

虚拟语气:
If he had enlisted earlier, he would have seen more action. = 如果他早点参军，他就会经历更多战斗。
I wish I had enlisted when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻时就参军了。

被动语态:
New recruits are enlisted every year. = 每年都会招募新兵。
His services were enlisted by the company. = 他被公司聘用了。

疑问句:
Will he enlist in the army? = 他会参军吗？
Did she enlist the help of her family? = 她寻求了家人的帮助吗？

否定句:
He did not enlist in the military. = 他没有参军。
They did not enlist the help of professionals. = 他们没有寻求专业人士的帮助。


PHRASE->
enlist in = 加入（军队等）
enlist the help of = 寻求…的帮助
enlist support = 获得支持
enlist oneself = 自愿加入
enlist for = 为…而报名
