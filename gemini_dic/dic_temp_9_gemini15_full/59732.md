eco-chic:/ˈekoʊ ʃik/| adj.|生态时尚；环保时尚|Environmentally friendly and stylish.  This term describes fashion, design, or lifestyle choices that are both stylish and sustainable, minimizing environmental impact.|例句：The eco-chic boutique offered stylish clothing made from recycled materials. 这家生态时尚精品店提供由回收材料制成的时尚服装。
例句：Her eco-chic apartment featured reclaimed wood and energy-efficient appliances.  她的生态时尚公寓采用了再生木材和节能电器。|近义词：sustainable fashion, green fashion, environmentally conscious style|反义词：fast fashion, unsustainable fashion, environmentally damaging style
