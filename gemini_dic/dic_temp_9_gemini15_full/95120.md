inanition: /ˌɪnəˈnɪʃən/ | n. |衰弱；虚脱；空虚；缺乏 | The state of being without strength; emptiness; lack of vitality or substance.  It often refers to a state of physical weakness caused by lack of food or nourishment. |  例句：Prolonged fasting led to a state of inanition. 长期禁食导致虚脱。

例句：The prolonged drought caused widespread inanition among the livestock.  长时间的干旱导致牲畜普遍虚弱。


近义词：debilitation, weakness, exhaustion, emaciation, depletion

反义词：strength, vigor, vitality, fullness
