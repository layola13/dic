/ˈɒlɪɡɪst/ | n. |寡头政治支持者|A person who supports or advocates for oligarchy.  An oligist believes in or promotes a system of government where power is held by a small group of people.  |例句：The oligist argued passionately for the benefits of concentrated power in the hands of a select few.  寡头政治支持者热情地论证了权力集中在少数人手中的好处。|近义词：oligarchist, plutocrat |反义词：democrat, populist
