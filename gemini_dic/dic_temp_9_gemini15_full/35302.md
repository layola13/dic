cmd:/kəˈmænd/| n. vt.|命令，指令；控制；掌握；指挥|英文解析:A command is an instruction given to a computer or other machine to perform a specific task. It can also refer to the power or authority to give orders.  |例句：The captain gave the command to fire. 船长下令开火。|近义词：order, instruction, directive|反义词：obedience, compliance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The general commands the army. = 将军指挥军队。
He commands respect from everyone. = 他赢得所有人的尊重。

一般过去时:
She commanded the troops to advance. = 她命令部队前进。
The king commanded the execution. = 国王下令处决。

现在完成时:
She has commanded the ship for ten years. = 她已经指挥这艘船十年了。
He has commanded great influence in politics. = 他在政治上拥有巨大的影响力。

过去完成时:
The general had commanded the troops before the battle. = 将军在战斗前指挥过部队。
They had commanded the highest respect in their field. = 他们曾在这个领域获得最高的敬意。

将来时:
He will command the next mission. = 他将指挥下一次任务。
She will command the attention of the audience. = 她将吸引观众的注意力。

虚拟语气:

I wish I commanded more resources. = 我希望我拥有更多资源。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
