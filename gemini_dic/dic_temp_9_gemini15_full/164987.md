preharmony:/ˌpriːˈhɑːrməni/| n. |预和谐；先和谐；前和谐|A state or condition that exists before complete harmony is achieved;  a preliminary or anticipatory stage of harmony.|The composer hinted at the preharmony in the introduction, setting the stage for the main theme. 作曲家在引言中暗示了预和谐，为主题曲奠定了基础。|近义词：protoharmony, precursory harmony|反义词：disharmony, discord
