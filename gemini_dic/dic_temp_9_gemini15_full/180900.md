rehypnotizing: /ˌriːˈhɪpnəˌtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 再催眠；重新催眠 |  The act of hypnotizing someone again.  This usually implies a continuation or resumption of a previous hypnotic session, rather than a completely new experience.  | Example: The therapist was rehypnotizing the patient to address lingering anxieties.  = 治疗师正在再次催眠病人以解决持续存在的焦虑。 | 近义词: re-hypnotising,  re-inducing hypnosis | 反义词: awakening, rousing
