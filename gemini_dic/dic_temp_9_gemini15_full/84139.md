half-mumbled:/ˈhæf ˈmʌmbəld/ | adj. |含糊不清地低声说的，含糊不清的低语|Partially mumbled; spoken in a low, indistinct voice.  It describes speech that is not clearly articulated, often due to nervousness, haste, or a low volume.|例句：He gave a half-mumbled apology before rushing off. 他匆匆忙忙地低声说了句含糊不清的道歉就离开了。|例句：The answer was a half-mumbled yes, barely audible. 回答是一个几乎听不见的含糊不清的“是的”。|近义词：indistinct, slurred, muffled, whispered|反义词：clear, articulate, distinct|形容词
