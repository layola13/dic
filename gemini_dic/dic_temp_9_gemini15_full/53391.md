disagrees:/ˌdɪs.əˈɡriːz/| v.|不同意，不一致|To have or express a different opinion.|例句：I disagree with your assessment of the situation. 我不同意你对局势的评估。  They disagree on the best course of action. 他们在最佳行动方案上意见不一。|近义词：differ, dissent, object|反义词：agree, concur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disagrees with the proposal. 他不同意这个提议。
They disagree on many issues. 他们在许多问题上意见不一。


一般过去时:
She disagreed with her boss's decision. 她不同意她老板的决定。
We disagreed about the best way to proceed. 我们对最佳的行动方案意见不一。


现在完成时:
He has disagreed with me on this matter before. 他之前就此事与我意见相左。
They have disagreed on several occasions. 他们已经多次意见相左。


过去完成时:
I had disagreed with him many times before. 我之前已经多次和他意见相左。
She had disagreed with her parents' wishes. 她之前不同意她父母的愿望。


将来时:
I think they will disagree on this point. 我认为他们会在这一点上意见不一。
He will disagree with the findings of the report. 他将不同意报告的调查结果。


虚拟语气:
If he disagreed, he would have voiced his opinion. 如果他不同意，他会表达他的意见。
I wish she didn't disagree with everything I say. 我希望她不要反对我说的一切。


被动语态:
His opinion was disagreed with by many. 许多人不赞同他的意见。(注意：被动语态在此用法相对不常见)
The proposal is disagreed with by the committee.  委员会不同意该提议。(注意：被动语态在此用法相对不常见)


疑问句:
Does he disagree with the plan? 他不同意这个计划吗？
Do they disagree on this topic? 他们在这个问题上有分歧吗？


否定句:
I don't disagree with you entirely. 我并不完全不同意你。
They didn't disagree with the decision. 他们并不反对这个决定。


PHRASE->
disagree with someone: 与某人意见不和
disagree on something: 在某事上意见不一
strongly disagree: 强烈反对
fundamentally disagree: 从根本上反对
disagree vehemently: 激烈地反对
