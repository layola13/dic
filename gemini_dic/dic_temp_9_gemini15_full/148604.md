overtell:/ˌoʊvərˈtɛl/| vt.|说得过多；详述；夸大其词|To tell something more than is necessary or true; to exaggerate.  It implies giving excessive detail or embellishing a story beyond what is required or accurate.|例句：He overtold the story, adding details that weren't true. 他把故事说得太过分了，还加了一些不真实的内容。

例句：Don't overtell the joke; it will lose its impact.  别把笑话说得太长，会失去效果。|近义词：exaggerate, embellish, elaborate|反义词：understate, minimize
