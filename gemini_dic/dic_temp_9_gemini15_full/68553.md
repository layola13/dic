fanfictions:/ˈfænˌfɪkʃənz/ | n. |同人小说|Fictional works written by fans, often based on existing intellectual properties like movies, books, or video games.  These stories explore alternative scenarios, character relationships, or expand upon existing canon.  They are typically shared online through platforms like Archive of Our Own (AO3) or Fanfiction.net. |Example:  She spends hours reading fanfictions about her favorite superhero. 她花好几个小时阅读她最喜欢的超级英雄的同人小说。|近义词: fan fiction, fan-made stories |反义词:  original fiction, canonical work
