horrid:/ˈhɒrɪd/ /ˈhɔːrɪd/| adj.|可怕的；令人厌恶的；糟糕的；讨厌的|Extremely unpleasant; causing horror.  It suggests something that is intensely disagreeable or offensive to the senses or feelings. |例句：The weather was horrid; it rained all day.  天气糟透了；整天都在下雨。

例句：The food was absolutely horrid; I couldn't eat it. 这食物真是太难吃了；我吃不下。

近义词：awful, terrible, dreadful, ghastly, unpleasant, nasty

反义词：delightful, pleasant, wonderful, lovely
