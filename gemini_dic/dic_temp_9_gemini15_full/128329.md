neutered:/ˈnjuːtərd/| adj.|阉割的；中性的；无性别的|  Describes an animal that has had its reproductive organs surgically removed, or something that is neither masculine nor feminine in nature.  |例句：The cat was neutered to prevent unwanted kittens.  这只猫被阉割了以防止不想要的小猫。
The word has a neutral tone in biological contexts, but in other contexts it can be considered harsh.  在生物学语境中，这个词的语气是中性的，但在其他语境中，它可能被认为是苛刻的。|近义词：castrated (for males), spayed (for females), asexual |反义词：intact, fertile
