discharges:/ˈdɪsˌtʃɑːrdʒɪz/ | n. v. | 排放；释放；解雇；出院 |

英文解析：The noun "discharge" refers to the act of releasing something, such as a substance, a person, or an obligation. As a verb, it means to release or let go of something or someone.  It can have various contexts depending on the object.

例句：

The factory discharges pollutants into the river. = 工厂将污染物排放到河里。

The doctor discharged the patient from the hospital. = 医生让病人出院了。


近义词：release, emission, dismissal, expulsion, liberation

反义词：retain, keep, employ, admit, confinement


词性：Noun and Verb
