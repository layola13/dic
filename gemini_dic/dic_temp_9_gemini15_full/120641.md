mis-performed:/ˌmɪs.pɚˈfɔːrmd/| adj.| 表演拙劣的；执行不力的 |  Describes an action or performance that was done incorrectly or inadequately. It implies a failure to meet expected standards or achieve the desired outcome. |Example: The surgery was mis-performed, leading to serious complications.  手术做得很糟糕，导致了严重的并发症。 The orchestra gave a mis-performed rendition of the symphony.  乐团对交响乐的演奏很糟糕。|近义词: poorly executed, badly done, botched, bungled |反义词: well-performed, expertly done, flawlessly executed |形容词
