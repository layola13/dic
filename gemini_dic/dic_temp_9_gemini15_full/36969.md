colstaff:/ˈkɒlstɑːf/|/ˈkɑːlstæf/| n. |警棍；指挥棒；（尤指）警察的警棍|A colstaff is a staff or stick, especially one carried by a police officer as a weapon or symbol of authority.  It can also refer to a conductor's baton.|例句：The police officer used his colstaff to control the unruly crowd.  警察用警棍控制住不守规矩的群众。

The riot police carried colstaffs.  防暴警察携带警棍。


近义词：nightstick, truncheon, baton|反义词：None
