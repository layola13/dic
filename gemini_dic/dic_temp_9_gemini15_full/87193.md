here-below:/hɪər bɪˈləʊ/ | adv. | 在下文中；如下；下面 | This term is used to introduce something that is about to be mentioned or explained. It indicates that further information or details are provided after this point.  | Example: The details of the plan are given here-below.  计划的细节如下所示。 | 近义词：below, hereinafter, subsequently | 反义词：above, previously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The details are given here-below. = 细节如下。
The instructions here-below will guide you. = 下面的说明将指导你。


一般过去时:
The details were given here-below. = 细节如下。
The reasons were explained here-below. = 原因在下文中解释。


现在完成时:
The information has been provided here-below. = 信息已在下文中提供。
The results have been shown here-below. = 结果已在下文中显示。


过去完成时:
The instructions had been laid out here-below. = 说明已在下文中列出。
The evidence had been presented here-below. = 证据已在下文中呈现。


将来时:
The details will be presented here-below. = 细节将在下文中呈现。
The schedule will be outlined here-below. = 时间表将在下文中概述。


虚拟语气:
If the information were included here-below, it would be clearer. = 如果信息包含在下文中，它会更清晰。
I wish the details were given here-below. = 我希望细节在下文中给出。


被动语态:
The rules are clearly stated here-below. = 规则在下文中清楚地说明。
The solution is explained here-below. = 解答在下文中解释。


疑问句:
Is the information here-below accurate? = 下文中信息准确吗？
Are the steps here-below sufficient? = 下文中步骤足够吗？


否定句:
The explanation is not given here-below. = 解释未在下文中给出。
The reasons are not detailed here-below. = 原因未在下文中详细说明。


PHRASE->
There is no common phrasal verb using "here-below".  It functions primarily as a standalone adverbial phrase.  However, it can be used with phrases like:

as detailed here-below:  如下详细说明
as shown here-below:  如下所示
as explained here-below:  如下解释
as provided here-below:  如下提供
as mentioned here-below:  如下所述
