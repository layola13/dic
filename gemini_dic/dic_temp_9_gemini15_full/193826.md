self-destructing: /ˌsɛlf dɪˈstrʌktɪŋ/ | adj. | 自毁的，自动销毁的 | Designed or intended to destroy itself after a certain time or under certain conditions.  Something that is self-destructing will automatically destroy itself.  This can refer to physical objects or systems, or to abstract concepts like relationships or plans. |

例句：

The spy's message was written on self-destructing paper.  间谍的信息写在自毁纸上。

The missile is equipped with a self-destructing mechanism.  这枚导弹配备了自毁装置。


近义词：auto-destructive, self-annihilating

反义词：self-preserving, enduring, permanent

词性：形容词
