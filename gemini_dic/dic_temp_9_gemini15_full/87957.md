high-end:/ˈhaɪ.ɛnd/ | adj. |高端的，高档的 |  Describes products, services, or experiences that are of superior quality, luxury, and often come with a high price tag. They are typically targeted towards affluent consumers. | 例句：This boutique hotel offers high-end amenities and impeccable service. 这家精品酒店提供高端设施和无可挑剔的服务。| 例句：The company launched a new line of high-end smartphones. 这家公司推出了一系列高端智能手机。| 近义词：luxury, premium, upscale, exclusive, top-of-the-line | 反义词：low-end, budget, inexpensive, cheap
