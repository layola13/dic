confirmation: /ˌkɒnfɜːˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 确认；证实；坚定 | The act of confirming something or the state of being confirmed.  It can refer to making something certain, validating something, or strengthening a belief.


例句：

The email served as confirmation of my booking. = 这封邮件证实了我的预订。

We received confirmation that the package had been shipped. = 我们收到包裹已发货的确认。


近义词： verification, validation, affirmation, corroboration

反义词： denial, refutation, disproof


词性：名词
