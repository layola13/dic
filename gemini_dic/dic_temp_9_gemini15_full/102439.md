jackers:/ˈdʒækərz/ | n. |劫车者，抢劫者 | A jacker is someone who steals a car or other vehicle, often using force or threats.  They are criminals involved in carjacking. | Example: The police apprehended the jackers after a high-speed chase.  警方在高速追逐后逮捕了劫车者。  |近义词：carjacker, robber, thief | 反义词：victim, law-abiding citizen | 名词
