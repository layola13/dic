outbuys:/ˌaʊtˈbaɪz/| vt.|超过购买量，购入量超过|To buy more of something than someone else.|The company outbuys its competitors in the raw materials market.|近义词：outpurchase, surpass in buying|反义词：underbuy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outbuy their rivals in the technology sector. = 他们在科技行业的购买量超过竞争对手。
Our company outbuys its competitors for essential supplies. = 我们公司对必需品的购买量超过竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Last quarter, the firm outbought its competitors in the energy market. = 上个季度，该公司在能源市场的购入量超过了竞争对手。
The small business outbought the larger corporation in the auction. = 这家小企业在拍卖会上购入的量超过了大公司。


现在完成时:
They have outbought their competitors consistently over the past five years. = 在过去的五年里，他们一直保持着购入量超过竞争对手的势头。
The investor has outbought everyone else in this stock. = 这位投资者在这只股票上的购入量超过了其他人。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had outbought all other bidders. = 到年底，他们购入的量已经超过了所有其他竞标者。
Before the merger, the company had outbought its rival in key components. = 在合并之前，该公司在关键组件上的购入量已经超过了其竞争对手。


将来时:
We expect that they will outbuy the competition in the next round of bidding. = 我们预计他们在下一轮竞标中将购入量超过竞争对手。
Analysts predict that the company will outbuy its rivals for the next few years. = 分析师预测该公司未来几年将购入量超过其竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If they had a larger budget, they could outbuy their competitors. = 如果他们有更大的预算，他们就能购入量超过竞争对手。
It would be difficult for them to outbuy such a powerful competitor. = 对他们来说，要购入量超过这样一个强大的竞争对手将会非常困难。


被动语态:
The smaller firm was outbought by the larger corporation in the acquisition. = 在这次收购中，这家较小的公司被更大的公司购入量超过。
The market was outbought by institutional investors. = 市场被机构投资者购入量超过。


疑问句:
Did the company outbuy its rivals in the latest acquisition? = 在最近的收购中，该公司是否购入量超过了其竞争对手？
Will they be able to outbuy their competitors in the long term? = 从长远来看，他们能否购入量超过竞争对手？


否定句:
The company did not outbuy its rivals in the recent auction. = 在最近的拍卖会上，该公司购入量并没有超过竞争对手。
They failed to outbuy the other bidders. = 他们未能购入量超过其他竞标者。
