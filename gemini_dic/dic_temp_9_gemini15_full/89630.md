hook-up:/ˈhʊkʌp/| n., v.|连接，搭讪，性关系|

英文解析：As a noun, "hook-up" refers to a connection between two things, often electrical or technological devices. It can also informally refer to a casual sexual encounter. As a verb, it means to connect two things or to meet someone for a casual sexual encounter.

例句：
(Noun - connection)
The electrician quickly hooked up the new washing machine. = 电工迅速连接了新的洗衣机。

(Noun - casual sexual encounter)
They had a one-night hook-up at the party. = 他们在派对上发生了一夜情。

(Verb - to connect)
We need to hook up the speakers to the amplifier. = 我们需要将扬声器连接到放大器上。

(Verb - to meet for a casual sexual encounter)
He tried to hook up with her at the bar. = 他试图在酒吧搭讪她。


近义词：
(Noun - connection) connection, link, attachment
(Noun - casual sexual encounter) rendezvous, fling, hookup
(Verb - to connect) connect, join, attach
(Verb - to meet for a casual sexual encounter) meet, date,  get together


反义词：
(Noun - connection) disconnection, separation
(Verb - to connect) disconnect, detach, separate
