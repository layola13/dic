graying:/ˈɡreɪɪŋ/| v.|变灰，变白，(头发)变灰|The process of becoming gray or turning gray, typically referring to hair.  It can also refer to other things losing their vibrancy and becoming duller.|例句：His hair is graying at the temples.  他的鬓角头发变白了。

例句：The once vibrant colors of the painting are graying with age. 这幅画曾经鲜艳的色彩随着时间的推移而变得暗淡。


近义词：whitening, fading, dulling|反义词：brightening, coloring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building is graying. = 建筑物正在变灰。
His hair is graying rapidly. = 他的头发正在迅速变白。


一般过去时:
The leaves grayed during the autumn. = 叶子在秋天变灰了。
Her once bright eyes grayed with age. = 她曾经明亮的眼睛随着年龄增长而变得暗淡。


现在完成时:
My hair has grayed significantly in the last few years. = 我的头发在过去几年里明显变白了。
The photograph has grayed with time. = 照片随着时间的推移而褪色变灰了。


过去完成时:
The old photograph had grayed considerably before we found it. = 我们找到那张旧照片之前，它已经严重褪色变灰了。
The once colorful tapestry had grayed over the centuries. = 那幅曾经色彩鲜艳的挂毯在几个世纪中已经变灰了。


将来时:
His hair will likely gray completely by the time he's sixty. = 到他六十岁的时候，他的头发很可能会完全变白。
The fabric will gray if exposed to sunlight for too long. = 如果长时间暴露在阳光下，织物会变灰。


虚拟语气:
If he stopped smoking, his hair might not gray so quickly. = 如果他不吸烟，他的头发可能不会那么快变白。
I wish my hair wouldn't gray so fast. = 我希望我的头发不会变白得这么快。


被动语态:
The photo was grayed by the intense sunlight.  = 照片被强烈的阳光晒得变灰了。
The wood was grayed by years of exposure to the elements. = 木材因多年暴露在自然环境中而变灰了。


疑问句:
Is your hair graying? = 你的头发变白了吗？
Are the colors in this painting graying? = 这幅画的颜色正在变灰吗？


否定句:
His hair isn't graying yet. = 他的头发还没变白。
The picture hasn't grayed at all. = 图片一点也没有变灰。
