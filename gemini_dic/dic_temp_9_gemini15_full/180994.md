reimported:/ˌriːɪmˈpɔːrtɪd/| adj.| 再进口的，重新进口的 |  Something that has been imported again after having been exported previously.  It describes goods or products that were initially exported from a country and then later imported back into that same country. |  例句：The company specializes in reimported cars, offering them at competitive prices. 这家公司专门经营再进口汽车，以具有竞争力的价格提供这些汽车。

例句：These reimported textiles are of surprisingly high quality. 这些再进口的纺织品的质量出奇地高。


近义词：re-imported, imported again, re-entered


反义词：exported, originally produced


词性：形容词
