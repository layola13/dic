de-integrating:/ˌdiːˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪŋ/| v. |去整合；取消整合；使分离|The process of separating or dismantling something that was previously integrated.  This often refers to undoing racial or social integration, reversing policies that aimed to bring diverse groups together. |例句：The company is de-integrating its manufacturing processes to improve efficiency. 公司正在取消其制造流程的整合以提高效率。
The government's new policies are leading to the de-integration of the education system. 政府的新政策导致教育体系的分裂。|近义词：disintegrating, separating, disassembling, un-integrating |反义词：integrating, unifying, consolidating
