semi-trailer:/ˈsemiˌtreɪlər/| n. |半挂车 |A semi-trailer is a trailer without a front axle; it is designed to be towed by a tractor unit.  It's a large, heavy-duty vehicle used for transporting goods over long distances. |例句：The semi-trailer carrying the cargo was delayed by a traffic jam. 这辆运送货物的半挂车因交通堵塞而延误。  The company uses a fleet of semi-trailers to deliver goods across the country. 这家公司使用车队半挂车在全国各地运送货物。|近义词：articulated lorry (UK), articulated truck (US), tractor-trailer |反义词：single-axle trailer
