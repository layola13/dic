It describes a person's habitual bad temper, grumbling, and unwillingness to be pleasant.|His curmudgeonliness was legendary in the neighborhood; no one dared to borrow his lawnmower.|近义词：grumpiness, irritability, cantankerousness, ill-temper, surliness|反义词：good nature, amiability, pleasantness, cheerfulness
