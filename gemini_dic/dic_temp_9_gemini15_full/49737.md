demographer:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfər/ | n. |人口统计学家|A demographer is a social scientist who studies the characteristics of human populations, such as size, growth, density, distribution, and vital statistics (births, deaths, marriages, and migrations). They analyze demographic data to understand population trends and their implications for society.  |The demographer presented her findings on population aging to the committee.  人口统计学家向委员会展示了她关于人口老龄化的研究结果。|sociologist, statistician|  (no direct antonym, but perhaps "someone who ignores population trends")|名词
