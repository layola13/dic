camaieu:/kæˈmeɪjuː/| n. |浅色画; 单色画; 柔和的色彩|A camaieu is a painting or drawing executed in monochrome, using only shades of a single color.  It often features subtle gradations of light and shadow to create depth and form.  The effect can be delicate and atmospheric.|Example: The artist created a beautiful camaieu in shades of sepia, capturing the essence of the old town.  艺术家用棕褐色的不同深浅色调创作了一幅美丽的浅色画，捕捉到了老城区的精髓。|近义词：monochrome, monotone, single-color painting|反义词：polychrome, multicolor painting|名词

词源:
The word "camaieu" comes from the French, ultimately deriving from the Greek word "χαμαιλέων" (chamailéon), meaning "chameleon."  This connection arises because the technique of camaieu, using varying shades of a single color, resembles the chameleon's ability to change its color.  The French word then made its way into English.
