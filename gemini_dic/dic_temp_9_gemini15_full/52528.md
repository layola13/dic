difficilitate:/dɪˈfɪkəlɪteɪt/| vt.|使困难；使复杂化 |To make something more difficult.  It implies making something harder to do or understand, often by adding complications or obstacles. |例句：The new regulations are likely to difficilitate the process of obtaining a visa. 新规可能会使获得签证的过程更加困难。|例句：The lack of clear instructions difficilitated the task considerably.  缺乏清晰的指示使这项任务变得相当困难。|近义词：hinder, hamper, obstruct, complicate |反义词：facilitate, simplify, ease
