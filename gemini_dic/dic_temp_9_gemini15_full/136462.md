nonreviewability: /ˌnɒnˌriˌvjuːəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可复查性，不受审查 | The inability to be reviewed or appealed.  This often refers to a decision or action that is final and cannot be challenged.  | 例句：The court ruled that the decision was final and beyond the scope of nonreviewability. 法院裁决该决定是最终的，不受审查。  The administrative agency's decision was characterized by nonreviewability due to its legislative nature. 行政机构的决定由于其立法性质而具有不可审查性。 | 近义词：finality, unappealable, irrevocability | 反义词：reviewability, appealability
