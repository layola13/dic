propositional:/ˌprɒpəˈzɪʃənəl/| adj.|命题的，表述的|Relating to or denoting a proposition.  A proposition is a statement that can be true or false.  The term is often used in logic and philosophy to describe statements that can be analyzed in terms of their truth value.  |例句：The propositional content of the poem is surprisingly complex. 这首诗的命题内容出奇地复杂。

例句：He presented a clear and concise propositional argument. 他提出了一个清晰简洁的命题论证。


近义词：declarative, assertive,  affirmative

反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with terms like "non-declarative" or "implicit")

词性: adjective
