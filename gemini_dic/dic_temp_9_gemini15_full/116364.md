mastication:/ˌmæs.tɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/ | n. |咀嚼；嚼碎 |The process of chewing food, breaking it down into smaller pieces to prepare it for swallowing and digestion.  |Example:  Thorough mastication is essential for proper digestion.  彻底咀嚼对于消化至关重要。|近义词：chewing, grinding |反义词：swallowing (as a direct opposite action)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He masticates his food carefully. = 他仔细地咀嚼食物。
She masticates each bite thoroughly. = 她彻底地咀嚼每一口。

一般过去时:
He masticated his food slowly. = 他慢慢地咀嚼食物。
The baby masticated the teething biscuit with gusto. = 婴儿津津有味地咀嚼着磨牙饼干。

现在完成时:
She has masticated all her food. = 她已经把所有食物都嚼碎了。
He has masticated the food before swallowing. = 他在吞咽之前已经嚼碎了食物。

过去完成时:
He had masticated the tough meat for a long time before swallowing. = 他在吞咽之前已经咀嚼了很长时间的硬肉。
She had masticated the food so well that it was easy to swallow. = 她把食物嚼得很碎，很容易吞咽。

将来时:
He will masticate his food more carefully from now on. = 从现在开始，他会更仔细地咀嚼食物。
They will masticate the food before swallowing it. = 他们会在吞咽食物之前咀嚼它。

虚拟语气:
If he masticated his food properly, he wouldn't have indigestion. = 如果他正确地咀嚼食物，他就不会消化不良。
I wish I had masticated my food more thoroughly. = 我真希望我当时能把食物嚼得更碎。

被动语态:

The food is being masticated by the teeth. = 食物正在被牙齿咀嚼。

疑问句:
