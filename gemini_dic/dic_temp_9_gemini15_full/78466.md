geyser:/ˈɡaɪzər/| n.|间歇泉|A geyser is a spring that throws forth hot water and steam intermittently.|例句：The geyser erupted, sending a plume of hot water high into the air.  间歇泉爆发了，喷出一股热水高高地冲向空中。
The Yellowstone National Park is famous for its geysers. 黄石国家公园以其间歇泉而闻名。|近义词：hot spring, fountain|反义词：无
