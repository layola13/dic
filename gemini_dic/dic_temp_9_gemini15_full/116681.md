maturate:/ˈmætʃəreɪt/ | v. | 使成熟；使完全；发育 | To become or make something fully developed or perfect.  The process of ripening or becoming mature. |例句：The cheese needs to mature for at least six months.  这奶酪至少需要成熟六个月。

The fruit maturated perfectly in the warm sun. 水果在温暖的阳光下完美成熟了。|近义词：ripen, develop, perfect |反义词：underdevelop, spoil


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fruit matures slowly in the cool climate. 水果在凉爽的气候下成熟缓慢。
He matures his ideas before sharing them. 他在分享想法之前会将其完善。


一般过去时:
The wine matured in oak barrels for years. 这葡萄酒在橡木桶中陈酿多年。
The plan matured over several months of discussion.  这个计划经过数月的讨论才最终完善。


现在完成时:
The cheese has matured beautifully. 这奶酪已经完美成熟。

过去完成时:
