rejudge:/ˌriːˈdʒʌdʒ/| vt.|重新审判；重新判断；再评价|To judge or assess again; to reconsider a previous judgment.|例句：The court decided to rejudge the case after new evidence emerged.  法庭决定在出现新的证据后重新审判此案。
例句：The judges agreed to rejudge the competition after complaints about unfairness. 评委们同意在接到关于不公平的投诉后重新评判比赛。|近义词：reassess, reconsider, review|反义词：uphold, confirm
