over-charities: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃærətiz/ | n. | 过度慈善；慈善事业泛滥 |  Excessive charitable activities;  a situation where there are too many charities or where charitable giving is excessive.  This term often implies inefficiency, redundancy, or a lack of impact due to the sheer number of organizations or the amount of money involved. | 例句：The proliferation of over-charities has led to donor fatigue.  过度慈善的激增导致捐赠者疲劳。  The city is struggling with the problem of over-charities competing for limited resources. 这个城市正在努力解决过度慈善组织争夺有限资源的问题。| 近义词：charity overload, excessive philanthropy,  charity saturation | 反义词：charity deficit, lack of philanthropy | 名词
