glosser:/ˈɡlɒsər/| n. |注释者；解释者；（尤指为词典或文本添加注释的人）|A glosser is a person who writes glosses, especially for dictionaries or texts.  They explain or interpret difficult words or passages. |Example: The ancient text was difficult to understand until a skilled glosser provided explanations.  这位古代文本难以理解，直到一位熟练的注释者提供了解释。|近义词：commentator, annotator, interpreter |反义词：obscurer
