misallocated: /ˌmɪsˈæləkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 错误分配的，配置错误的 |  Describes resources, funds, or time that have been assigned or used inappropriately or inefficiently.  It implies a mistake in the allocation process, leading to suboptimal results. | 例句：The company's misallocated funds led to a significant financial loss. 公司资金分配错误导致了严重的财务损失。

The project suffered from misallocated resources; too much time was spent on minor details.  项目因资源分配不当而受损；过多的时间花在了次要细节上。|近义词：improperly allocated, wrongly distributed, inefficiently used | 反义词：properly allocated, efficiently used, optimally distributed |
