differently:/ˈdɪfərəntli/| adv. |不同地，不一样地|In a different way or manner.|The two artists approached the same subject differently.|近义词：otherwise, uniquely |反义词：similarly, identically


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He treats his children differently than his parents treated him. = 他对待孩子的方式与他父母对待他的方式不同。
They handle stress differently depending on their personality. = 他们根据自己的性格以不同的方式应对压力。


一般过去时:
She reacted differently to the news than I expected. = 她对这个消息的反应与我预期的不同。
We approached the problem differently last time. = 我们上次用不同的方法处理了这个问题。


现在完成时:
They have approached the problem differently this time. = 他们这次用不同的方法处理了这个问题。
I have seen the situation handled differently in other countries. = 我在其他国家看到过不同的处理方式。


过去完成时:
He had handled similar situations differently in the past. = 他过去处理类似情况的方式有所不同。
We had approached the task differently before realizing our mistake. = 在意识到错误之前，我们采取了不同的方法来完成这项任务。


将来时:
They will approach the project differently next time. = 下次他们将采用不同的方法来处理这个项目。
I will handle the situation differently in the future. = 我将来会采取不同的方式处理这种情况。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have acted differently. = 如果我知道的话，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had reacted differently to her criticism. = 我希望我对她的批评有不同的反应。


被动语态:
The problem was handled differently by the two teams. = 这个问题由两个团队采用了不同的处理方式。
The situation could have been approached differently. = 这种情况本来可以用不同的方法处理。


疑问句:
Do you think they would react differently? = 你认为他们会采取不同的反应吗？
Would they handle this situation differently? = 他们会用不同的方法处理这种情况吗？


否定句:
They didn't handle the situation differently at all. = 他们根本没有采取不同的处理方式。
I didn't approach the task differently, and I failed. = 我没有采用不同的方法来完成这项任务，所以我失败了。


PHRASE->
think differently = 另类思考
live differently = 过着不一样的生活
work differently = 工作方式不同
act differently = 行为不同
look differently = 看法不同
