possesses:/pəˈzɛsɪz/| vt.|拥有；具有；支配；控制|To have or own something; to have a particular quality or ability.|例句：She possesses a rare talent for painting. 她拥有罕见的绘画天赋。|例句：The museum possesses a vast collection of artifacts. 这个博物馆拥有大量的文物。|近义词：owns, has, controls, commands|反义词：lacks, is without, relinquishes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He possesses a sharp mind. = 他拥有敏锐的头脑。
She possesses great strength of character. = 她具有坚强的性格。

一般过去时:
The king possessed vast territories. = 国王拥有广阔的领土。
He possessed a rare book. = 他拥有一本珍贵的书。

现在完成时:
I have possessed this painting for years. = 我拥有这幅画多年了。
She has possessed that car since 2010. = 她从2010年就拥有那辆车了。

过去完成时:
He had possessed the land for generations. = 他家世世代代拥有这块土地。
They had possessed the secret for many years. = 他们保守这个秘密很多年了。

将来时:
She will possess the necessary skills soon. = 她很快就会掌握必要的技能。
They will possess the property next month. = 他们下个月将拥有这处房产。

虚拟语气:
If he possessed more money, he would buy a house. = 如果他更有钱，他就会买房子。
I wish I possessed the ability to fly. = 我真希望我有飞行的能力。

被动语态:
The painting is possessed by the museum. = 这幅画为博物馆所有。
The land was possessed by the family for centuries. = 这块土地世世代代为这个家族所有。

疑问句:
Does she possess the necessary qualifications? = 她拥有必要的资格吗？
What qualities does he possess? = 他具备什么品质？

否定句:
He does not possess a driver's license. = 他没有驾照。
They do not possess the information we need. = 他们没有我们所需的信息。
