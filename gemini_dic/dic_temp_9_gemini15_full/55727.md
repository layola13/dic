divast:/daɪˈvæst/| n. |女歌星，歌剧女演员，[贬义] 难伺候的歌星|A diva is a famous female singer, especially an opera singer. The term can also be used informally and negatively to describe a woman who is demanding or difficult to please.  | 例句：She's a renowned opera diva. 她是一位著名的歌剧女高音。

例句：She acted like a diva, demanding special treatment. 她表现得像个难伺候的歌星，要求特殊待遇。


近义词：songstress, prima donna, superstar (positive);  prima donna (negative)

反义词：nonentity, nobody


词性: 名词
