fib:/fɪb/ | v. | 撒谎，说谎 | To tell a small, unimportant lie.  | Example:  He fibbed about his age to get into the club. 他谎报年龄以进入俱乐部。 | 近义词：lie, prevaricate | 反义词：tell the truth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often fibs to his mother. = 他经常对他妈妈撒谎。
She fibs about her whereabouts. = 她对自己的行踪撒谎。

一般过去时:
He fibbed to me about the accident. = 他向我撒谎关于那起事故。
She fibbed about her age on her application. = 她在申请表上谎报了年龄。

现在完成时:
I have fibbed to her many times. = 我对她撒了很多次谎。
He has fibbed about his qualifications. = 他谎报了自己的资历。

过去完成时:
She had fibbed about her past before we met. = 我们见面之前，她对她的过去撒了谎。
He had fibbed to his boss about the project's progress. = 他向老板谎报了项目的进展。


将来时:
I will fib if I have to. = 如果必须的话我会撒谎。
She will probably fib to avoid punishment. = 她可能会撒谎以避免惩罚。

虚拟语气:
If I fibbed, I would feel guilty. = 如果我撒谎，我会感到内疚。
I wish I hadn't fibbed to my friend. = 我真希望我没有对我朋友撒谎。

被动语态:
The truth was eventually fibbed about. = 真相最终被隐瞒了。(Note: Passive voice with "fib" is less common and sounds unnatural.)
(A better alternative sentence): The truth was eventually hidden. 真相最终被隐藏了。


疑问句:
Did he fib about the money? = 他关于钱撒谎了吗？
Does she often fib to her parents? = 她经常对她父母撒谎吗？

否定句:
He didn't fib about his intentions. = 他没有隐瞒他的意图。
She wouldn't fib to save her life. = 她不会为了救自己的命而撒谎。


PHRASE->
fib about something = 对某事撒谎
a little white fib = 一个善意的谎言
a harmless fib = 一个无害的谎言
tell a fib = 说谎
a pack of fibs = 一大堆谎话
