exonerates:/ɪɡˈzɒnəreɪt/| vt.|宣告无罪；免除…的责任；使免受指控|To clear (someone) from blame or accusation.  To relieve (someone) from a duty or obligation.|例句：The evidence exonerated the suspect.  证据证明嫌疑人无罪。

The court exonerated her of all charges. 法院撤销了她所有的指控。


近义词：absolve, acquit, clear, vindicate|反义词：convict, incriminate, condemn|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence exonerates the suspect. = 证据证明嫌疑人无罪。
The new findings exonerate the company from liability. = 新的调查结果使公司免于承担责任。


一般过去时:
The jury exonerated the defendant. = 陪审团宣告被告无罪。
The investigation exonerated the police officer. = 调查结果证明警官无罪。


现在完成时:
The court has exonerated him from all charges. = 法院已经撤销了他所有的指控。
Recent evidence has exonerated the wrongly accused. = 最近的证据证明了被冤枉的人的清白。


过去完成时:
By the time the truth came out, the judge had already exonerated him. = 当真相大白的时候，法官已经宣告他无罪了。
They had exonerated the suspect before new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，他们已经为嫌疑人洗脱了嫌疑。


将来时:
The investigation will likely exonerate him. = 调查很可能会证明他的清白。
We hope the appeal will exonerate her. = 我们希望上诉能为她洗清冤屈。


虚拟语气:
If the new evidence were presented, it would exonerate him. = 如果提交新的证据，它将证明他的清白。
I wish the investigation would exonerate my friend. = 我希望调查能够为我的朋友洗清冤屈。


被动语态:
He was exonerated by the court. = 他被法院宣告无罪。
She was exonerated after years of wrongful imprisonment. = 在多年的冤狱之后，她被释放了。


疑问句:
Will the investigation exonerate him? = 调查会证明他的清白吗？
Has the court exonerated the defendant? = 法院是否已经宣告被告无罪？


否定句:
The evidence did not exonerate him. = 证据没有证明他的清白。
The court refused to exonerate the suspect. = 法院拒绝为嫌疑人洗脱嫌疑。


PHRASE->
exonerate someone from blame = 免除某人的责任
be exonerated from responsibility = 被免除责任
fully exonerated = 完全洗脱罪名
exonerate someone of a charge = 为某人洗清一项罪名
exonerate someone from a duty = 免除某人的一项义务
