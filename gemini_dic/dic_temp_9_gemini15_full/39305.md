conifer:/ˈkɒnɪfə(r)/|/ˈkɑːnɪfər/| n. |针叶树；锥果树|A conifer is a tree that bears cones and typically has needle-like or scale-like leaves.  Conifers are typically evergreen, meaning they retain their leaves throughout the year. |例句：Pine trees are a type of conifer. 松树是一种针叶树。

例句：Many conifers thrive in mountainous regions. 许多针叶树在山区茁壮成长。


近义词：evergreen (in the context of trees), needle-leaved tree |反义词：broadleaf tree, deciduous tree |词性：名词
