immunised: /ˈɪmjuːnaɪzd/ | v. | 免疫的；使免疫 | To make someone or something immune to a disease by vaccination or other means.  |例句：The children were immunised against measles. 孩子们接种了麻疹疫苗。 The vaccine immunised her against the flu. 疫苗使她免于患流感。|近义词：vaccinated, inoculated, protected |反义词：susceptible, vulnerable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He immunises his children every year. = 他每年都给他的孩子们接种疫苗。
The doctor immunises patients against various diseases. = 医生为病人接种预防各种疾病的疫苗。

一般过去时:
They immunised the entire population against polio. = 他们为整个人口接种了小儿麻痹症疫苗。
The nurse immunised the baby against diphtheria. = 护士为婴儿接种了白喉疫苗。

现在完成时:
She has been immunised against several childhood diseases. = 她已经接种了几种儿童疾病的疫苗。
The researchers have immunised lab mice against the new virus. = 研究人员已经为实验室老鼠接种了针对这种新型病毒的疫苗。

过去完成时:
The doctor had immunised the patient before the surgery. = 医生在手术前已经为病人接种了疫苗。
They had immunised the animals against rabies several months earlier. = 几个月前他们已经为动物接种了狂犬病疫苗。

将来时:
We will immunise our dog against fleas next week. = 我们下周将为我们的狗接种跳蚤疫苗。
The government will immunise all citizens against the new strain of influenza. = 政府将为所有公民接种新型流感疫苗。

虚拟语气:
If they had immunised the population earlier, there would have been fewer cases. = 如果他们早些为人口接种疫苗，病例就会少得多。
I wish I had been immunised against this disease. = 我真希望我接种过这种疾病的疫苗。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

They did not immunise their pets against rabies. = 他们没有为他们的宠物接种狂犬病疫苗。
