pursuit:/pərˈsuːt/ | n. v. |追求；追捕；职业；嗜好 | The act of pursuing something or someone.  The occupation or activity that someone is engaged in. |例句：His pursuit of knowledge led him to travel the world. 他的求知欲使他周游世界。
He is in pursuit of a better job. 他正在寻求一份更好的工作。


近义词：chase, quest, search, occupation, hobby | 反义词：abandonment, neglect


词性: 名词和动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pursues his dreams relentlessly. = 他坚持不懈地追求他的梦想。
She pursues a career in medicine. = 她追求医学事业。


一般过去时:
They pursued the thief through the streets. = 他们追捕小偷穿过街道。
He pursued his studies diligently. = 他勤奋地学习。


现在完成时:
She has pursued her passion for art for many years. = 她多年来一直追求她对艺术的热情。
They have pursued justice tirelessly. = 他们不知疲倦地追求正义。


过去完成时:
He had pursued a law degree before entering politics. = 他在进入政界之前获得了法律学位。
She had pursued her goals with great determination. = 她以极大的决心追求她的目标。


将来时:
He will pursue his research further next year. = 他明年将进一步进行他的研究。
She will pursue a master's degree in engineering. = 她将攻读工程硕士学位。


虚拟语气:
If I were to pursue my dream, I would move to Paris. = 如果我要追求我的梦想，我会搬到巴黎。
I wish I had pursued my musical talents more seriously. = 我希望我以前更认真地追求我的音乐天赋。


被动语态:
The suspect was pursued by the police. = 嫌疑人被警方追捕。
His ideas were pursued with vigor. = 他的想法被大力推行。


疑问句:
What are you pursuing at the moment? = 你现在在追求什么？
What career are you pursuing? = 你追求什么职业？


否定句:
He did not pursue his education further. = 他没有继续深造。
She did not pursue the matter any longer. = 她不再追究此事。


PHRASE->
in pursuit of = 追求
the pursuit of happiness = 幸福的追求
in hot pursuit = 紧追不舍
a wild goose chase = 徒劳无功的追求
a relentless pursuit = 不懈的追求
