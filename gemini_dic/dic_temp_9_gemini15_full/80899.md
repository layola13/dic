governor-generalships: /ˈɡʌvənərˈdʒɛnərəlʃɪps/ | n. |总督职位；总督任期 |The office or term of office of a governor-general.  It refers to the position and period of time a governor-general holds their office. | Example: The governor-generalships of Canada and Australia are largely ceremonial. 加拿大和澳大利亚的总督职位大多是礼仪性的。  The length of the governor-generalships varied throughout history. 历史上总督任期的长度有所不同。|近义词：governorship (but refers to a governor, not a governor-general)|反义词：无
