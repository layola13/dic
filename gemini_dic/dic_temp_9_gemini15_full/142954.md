outacting:/ˌaʊtˈæktɪŋ/| v.|胜过，超过（在行动或表现上）|To surpass or exceed in action or performance.  It implies a deliberate effort to outperform someone or something.|例句：The new CEO outacted his predecessor in terms of decisiveness and vision. 新任CEO在果断性和远见方面胜过前任。|例句：Her performance on stage completely outacted all expectations. 她在舞台上的表演完全超出了所有人的预期。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass, exceed|反义词：underperform, lag behind|动词


USAGE->

The team outacts its opponents in terms of teamwork. = 这个团队在团队合作方面胜过对手。
