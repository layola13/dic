fetal:/ˈfiːtəl/| adj.|胎儿的，胎内的| Relating to or affecting a fetus.  The word describes something connected to an unborn offspring, from conception to birth.|例句：The doctor monitored the fetal heartbeat. 医生监测胎儿的心跳。

例句：The pregnant woman received regular fetal ultrasound scans. 孕妇定期接受胎儿超声波扫描。


近义词：embryonic (in early stages), unborn|反义词：born, postnatal
