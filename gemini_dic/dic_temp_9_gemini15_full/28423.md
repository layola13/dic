carbonize:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪz/|/ˈkɑːrbənaɪz/| vt. |使碳化；使变成焦炭|To convert (a substance) into carbon or a carbon-containing compound, especially by heating.  This often involves the removal of other elements, such as hydrogen and oxygen.|The wood was carbonized in the intense heat of the fire.  木材在烈火中碳化了。|The process carbonized the organic material, leaving behind only charcoal. 这个过程使有机物碳化，只留下木炭。|char,  coke|decarbonize|动词
