enslave:/ɪnˈsleɪv/ | vt. |奴役；使…成为奴隶 | To make someone a slave; to force someone into a position of servitude.  |例句：The Romans enslaved many people during their conquests. 罗马人在征服期间奴役了许多人。

例句：They were enslaved and forced to work in the fields. 他们被奴役，被迫在田里劳作。


近义词：subjugate, subjugate, ensnare, enthrall, bind


反义词：liberate, free, emancipate


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enslaves the people in his country. 他奴役他国家的人民。
The system enslaves its workers.  这个制度奴役着它的工人。


一般过去时:
The king enslaved his enemies. 国王奴役了他的敌人。
They enslaved the conquered population. 他们奴役了被征服的人民。


现在完成时:
He has enslaved thousands of people. 他已经奴役了成千上万人。
The empire has enslaved countless people throughout its history. 这个帝国在其历史上奴役了无数的人民。


过去完成时:
The plantation owner had enslaved generations of families. 种植园主已经奴役了好几代家庭。
They had enslaved the local tribes before the arrival of the colonists. 在殖民者到来之前，他们已经奴役了当地部落。


将来时:
He will enslave anyone who opposes him. 他将奴役任何反对他的人。
The dictator will continue to enslave his people. 独裁者将继续奴役他的人民。


虚拟语气:
If he were to win, he would enslave the entire nation. 如果他赢了，他会奴役整个国家。
I wish he had not enslaved so many people. 我真希望他没有奴役那么多人。


被动语态:
Many people were enslaved during the war. 许多人在战争中被奴役。
The prisoners were enslaved and forced to work. 囚犯们被奴役并被迫劳动。


疑问句:
Were the people enslaved against their will? 人们是被迫奴役的吗？
Has anyone been enslaved in this region recently? 最近这个地区有人被奴役吗？


否定句:
They did not enslave the captives. 他们没有奴役俘虏。
The government does not enslave its citizens. 政府没有奴役它的公民。


PHRASE->

enslave oneself to:  使自己成为…的奴隶
enslave one's mind: 使思想受束缚
enslave one's body: 使身体受奴役
enslave to habit: 受习惯所支配
