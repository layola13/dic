incertain:/ʌnˈsɜːrtn/| adj.|不确定的，不稳定的，易变的 | Uncertain means not known or definite; subject to change. It implies a lack of clarity or confidence about something.  |例句：The future remains uncertain. 未来依然不确定。

The weather forecast is uncertain. 天气预报不确定。

近义词：doubtful, unsure, questionable, precarious, ambiguous |反义词：certain, sure, definite, stable, reliable |词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The outcome is uncertain. = 结果不确定。
His future plans are uncertain. = 他的未来计划不确定。

一般过去时:
The situation was uncertain. = 当时的情况不确定。
I was uncertain about the answer. = 我对答案不确定。

现在完成时:
Things have become uncertain recently. = 最近情况变得不确定了。
We have been uncertain about his intentions. = 我们一直对他意图不确定。

过去完成时:
I had been uncertain about my decision. = 我之前对我的决定不确定。
They had been uncertain of the outcome. = 他们之前对结果不确定。

将来时:
The results will be uncertain. = 结果将不确定。
The weather will likely be uncertain tomorrow. = 明天天气可能不确定。

虚拟语气:
If the situation were certain, we would act. = 如果情况确定，我们会采取行动。
I wish I were certain about my next step. = 我希望我对我的下一步行动有把握。

被动语态:
The date is uncertain. = 日期不确定。(被动语态在此处不常用，因为uncertain本身就是形容词)
The plan remains uncertain. = 计划依然不确定。(被动语态在此处不常用，因为uncertain本身就是形容词)


疑问句:

Is the future uncertain? = 未来不确定吗？

否定句:
I am not uncertain about my decision. = 我对我的决定很确定。
He is not uncertain of his abilities. = 他对自己有能力很有把握。
