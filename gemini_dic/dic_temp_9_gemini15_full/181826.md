relit:/rɪˈlɪt/| vt.|重新点燃；使…重新活跃|To light (something) again; to kindle anew.  The act of lighting something again.  It can also refer to rekindling something figuratively, such as a relationship or interest.|例句：He relit the candle after it had gone out. 他在蜡烛熄灭后又把它点燃了。

He relit the fire to keep warm. 他重新点燃火堆以保暖。


近义词：reignite, rekindle, reignite, relight|反义词：extinguish, quench|动词
