duskiness:/ˈdʌskɪnəs/| n. |昏暗，朦胧；暮色；暗淡|The quality or state of being dusky; a shadowy or dim appearance.  It often refers to the dim light of twilight. |例句：The duskiness of the forest made it difficult to see the path. 森林的昏暗使难以看清道路。

The duskiness of the room was enhanced by the heavy curtains.  房间的昏暗被厚重的窗帘加重了。|近义词：gloom, dimness, shadowiness, obscurity|反义词：brightness, light, illumination
