perjurous:/ˈpɜːrdʒəriəs/| adj.| 伪证的；作伪证的；不实的 | Relating to or characterized by perjury; containing or involving false statements made under oath.  |Example:  He was accused of perjurous testimony. 他被指控作伪证。

The witness gave perjurous evidence in court.  证人在法庭上提供了伪证。


近义词：false, untrue, mendacious, deceitful

反义词：truthful, honest, veracious


词源:  From Middle English *perjurius, from Old French *perjureus, from Latin perjūrius, from perjūrus “perjured,” from per- “through, thoroughly” + jūrus “sworn.”
