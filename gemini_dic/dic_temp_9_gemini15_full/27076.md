caliber: /ˈkælɪbər/ | n. |口径；直径；才干；素质 |

英文解析:Caliber refers to the diameter of a bullet or the inside diameter of a gun barrel.  It can also refer to the quality, ability, or merit of someone or something.

例句:

The rifle has a caliber of .308.  这支步枪的口径是.308英寸。

He's a man of exceptional caliber. 他是一位才华出众的人。


近义词:diameter, gauge, quality, ability, merit, standard

反义词:inability, incompetence, inferiority
