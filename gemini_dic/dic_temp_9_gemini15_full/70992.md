firewalls:/ˈfaɪərwɔːlz/ | n. | 防火墙 | A firewall is a network security system that monitors and controls incoming and outgoing network traffic based on predetermined security rules.  A firewall typically establishes a barrier between a trusted internal network and an untrusted external network, such as the Internet.  It can be implemented in both hardware and software. | Example: Our company uses a robust firewall to protect our sensitive data from cyberattacks.  我们的公司使用强大的防火墙来保护我们的敏感数据免受网络攻击。  |近义词：security system, network barrier | 反义词：open network, unprotected system | 名词
