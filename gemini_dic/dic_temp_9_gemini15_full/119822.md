/mɪks/ | n. v. |混合物；混合；调配|A mix is a combination of different things.  Mixing involves combining different things together.|例句：The cake mix is easy to use. 蛋糕混合物很容易使用。 The artist mixed the paints to create a new color. 艺术家混合颜料以创造一种新的颜色。|近义词：blend, combination, compound|反义词：separation, segregation|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mixes the ingredients thoroughly. = 他彻底混合了配料。
She mixes the colors beautifully. = 她把颜色混合得很好看。

一般过去时:
I mixed the cement yesterday. = 我昨天拌了水泥。
They mixed the drinks for the party. = 他们为派对调制了饮料。


现在完成时:
She has mixed all the ingredients. = 她已经混合了所有配料。
He has mixed the paint several times. = 他已经混合了几次颜料。


过去完成时:
I had mixed the batter before adding the fruit. = 在加入水果之前，我已经混合好了面糊。
They had mixed the drinks before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经调好了饮料。
