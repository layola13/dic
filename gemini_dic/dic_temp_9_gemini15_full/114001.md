magistrates:/ˈmædʒɪstreɪts/| n.|治安法官；地方行政长官| Magistrates are local judges who deal with less serious crimes.  治安法官是处理较轻微罪行的当地法官。|例句：The magistrates heard the case and imposed a fine. 治安法官听取了此案并处以罚款。

The magistrates' court was busy that day. 那天治安法庭很忙。|近义词：judge, justice of the peace|反义词：defendant, criminal
