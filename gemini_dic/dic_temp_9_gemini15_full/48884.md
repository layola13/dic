deframe:/dɪˈfreɪm/| vt.| 诽谤；陷害；诋毁 | To defame or slander someone; to damage someone's reputation.  |例句：They tried to defame him by spreading false rumors. 他们试图通过散布谣言来诽谤他。  |例句：The journalist was sued for defaming a public figure. 这名记者因诽谤公众人物而被起诉。|近义词：slander, libel, calumniate, vilify |反义词：praise, commend, extol


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defames his rivals regularly. = 他经常诽谤他的竞争对手。
She defames her colleagues to get ahead. = 她诽谤同事以求上位。


一般过去时:
The newspaper defamed the politician in its editorial. = 报纸在其社论中诽谤了这位政治家。
He defamed his neighbor with malicious gossip. = 他用恶意的闲话诽谤了他的邻居。


现在完成时:
They have defamed my character through social media. = 他们通过社交媒体诽谤了我的名誉。
She has defamed several people throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她诽谤过几个人。


过去完成时:
The company had defamed the scientist before he could defend himself. = 在这位科学家能够自卫之前，该公司已经诽谤了他。
They had defamed the artist's reputation before the exhibition. = 在展览之前，他们就已经诽谤了这位艺术家的声誉。


将来时:
They will defame the company if they don't retract the statement. = 如果他们不收回声明，他们将诽谤该公司。
He will defame her again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他会再次诽谤她。


虚拟语气:
If he defamed her, she would sue him. = 如果他诽谤了她，她会起诉他。
I wish he hadn't defamed my family. = 我真希望他没有诽谤我的家人。


被动语态:
He was defamed by the press. = 他被媒体诽谤了。
The politician was defamed in a series of articles. = 这位政治家在一系列文章中受到了诽谤。


疑问句:
Were they defamed by their competitors? = 他们被竞争对手诽谤了吗？
Has he been defamed online? = 他在网上被诽谤了吗？


否定句:
They did not defame him intentionally. = 他们并非故意诽谤他。
She did not defame her boss; she simply reported the truth. = 她没有诽谤她的老板；她只是在陈述事实。


PHRASE->
defame someone's character = 诽谤某人的品格
defame someone's reputation = 诽谤某人的名誉
be defamed by the media = 被媒体诽谤
attempt to defame = 试图诽谤
falsely defame = 恶意诽谤
