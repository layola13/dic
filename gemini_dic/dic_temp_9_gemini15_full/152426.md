pawing:/ˈpɔːɪŋ/ | vt. | 用爪抓；扒；笨拙地摸索 | To strike or scrape with paws or claws; to handle clumsily.  |例句：The cat was pawing at the door.  猫在用爪子抓门。

The dog pawed at the ground excitedly.  狗兴奋地用爪子刨地。

近义词：scratching, scraping, clawing | 反义词： None that directly oppose the action.  | 词性: Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat paws at the curtains every morning. = 猫每天早上都用爪子抓窗帘。
He paws the ground nervously before speaking. = 他在说话前紧张地刨着地。


一般过去时:
The kitten pawed at the ball of yarn. = 小猫用爪子抓着毛线球。
She pawed through her purse looking for her keys. = 她笨手笨脚地在钱包里翻找钥匙。


现在完成时:
The dog has pawed a hole in the garden. = 狗已经在花园里刨了个洞。
He has pawed through all the papers but still can't find it. = 他翻遍了所有的文件，但还是找不到。


过去完成时:
The cat had pawed at the door for hours before someone finally let it in. = 猫在有人最终开门之前，已经用爪子抓了门几个小时了。
She had pawed through the suitcase, but her passport was nowhere to be found. = 她翻遍了手提箱，但护照却不见了。


将来时:
The puppy will paw at your hand if you don't give it attention. = 如果你不理它，小狗会用爪子挠你的手。
I think the bear will paw at the picnic basket. = 我想熊会用爪子扒开野餐篮。


虚拟语气:

I wish the dog hadn't pawed at the new carpet. = 我真希望狗没有抓坏新地毯。


被动语态:
The door was pawed at repeatedly by the cat. = 猫反复地用爪子抓门。

疑问句:
Is the dog pawing at the door? = 狗在抓门吗？
Why is the cat pawing at the carpet? = 猫为什么在抓地毯？


否定句:
The cat didn't paw at the furniture. = 猫没有抓家具。
The dog wasn't pawing at the door; it was barking. = 狗不是在抓门，它在叫。

词源:
From Old English *pāwian, from pāwa "paw".  The word's development reflects the longstanding association of paws with scratching, digging, and manipulating objects.
