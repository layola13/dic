red:/red/| adj.|红色的|The word "red" describes something having the color of blood.  It is a primary color, meaning it cannot be created by mixing other colors.  It is often associated with feelings of passion, danger, or excitement, but its cultural significance can vary. |例句：The apple is red.  这个苹果是红色的。 The traffic light turned red.  交通灯变成了红色。|近义词：crimson, scarlet, ruby|反义词：blue, green, white
