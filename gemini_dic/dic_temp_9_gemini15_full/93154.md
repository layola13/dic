ignigenous:/ɪɡˈnɪdʒənəs/| adj.|火成的，岩浆的|Formed by the solidification of molten rock (magma or lava).  It refers to rocks that were formed from the cooling and solidification of molten rock material.  |例句：The volcanic landscape is largely composed of ignigenous rocks. 这片火山景观主要由火成岩构成。

例句：Many ignigenous rocks contain crystals formed during slow cooling beneath the Earth's surface. 许多火成岩含有在地表下缓慢冷却过程中形成的晶体。


近义词：volcanic, magmatic|反义词：sedimentary, metamorphic|词性:形容词
