gcp:/ˌdʒiːsiːˈpiː/| n.|谷歌云平台|Google Cloud Platform, a suite of cloud computing services offered by Google.  It provides a range of products and services, including computing, storage, databases, networking, and machine learning.  |Example: We're migrating our servers to GCP to improve scalability and reduce costs. 我们正在将服务器迁移到 GCP 以提高可扩展性和降低成本。|近义词：AWS (Amazon Web Services), Azure (Microsoft Azure)|反义词：on-premise infrastructure (本地基础设施)|名词
