glumaceous:/ɡluˈmeɪʃəs/| adj.|颖状的，颖壳的|  Relating to or resembling glumes; having the character of a glume.  Glumes are the bracts at the base of a grass flower.  The term often describes structures that are chaffy or scale-like in appearance.|例句：The glumaceous bracts protected the developing seeds. 颖状苞片保护着发育中的种子。

The inflorescence exhibits glumaceous characteristics. 花序呈现颖状特征。


近义词：scaly, chaffy, bracteate|反义词：petaloid (petal-like)
