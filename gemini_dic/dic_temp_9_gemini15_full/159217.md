poetry:/ˈpoʊətri/ | n. |诗歌，诗作 |The art of writing poems, or a collection of poems.  Poetry often uses figurative language, such as metaphors and similes, to create imagery and evoke emotions. |Example:  She is studying the poetry of Emily Dickinson. 她正在学习艾米莉·狄金森的诗歌。|近义词：verse, rhymes, poems |反义词：prose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He writes poetry every day. = 他每天都写诗。
She reads poetry aloud. = 她朗读诗歌。

一般过去时:
They wrote a poem for their teacher. = 他们为他们的老师写了一首诗。
I read a lot of poetry last night. = 昨晚我读了很多诗歌。


现在完成时:
She has written many poems. = 她已经写了很多诗。
I have never read that poet before. = 我以前从未读过那位诗人的作品。


过去完成时:

They had never seen such beautiful poetry. = 他们从未见过如此美丽的诗歌。


将来时:
