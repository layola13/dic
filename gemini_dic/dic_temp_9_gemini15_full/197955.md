USAGE->
This word is informal and highly negative.  It doesn't have grammatical tenses in the traditional sense as it's a noun describing a state. Therefore, tense conjugations are not applicable.  The word itself is rarely used in formal writing or conversation.


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "shittiness". The word itself is used independently to express a strong negative feeling about a situation.
