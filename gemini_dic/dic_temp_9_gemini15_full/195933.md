sensitivity: /ˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 敏感性；灵敏度；敏感；过敏反应 |

英文解析:The quality or state of being sensitive; the ability to detect or respond to slight changes, stimuli, or influences.  It can refer to physical reactions (like sensitivity to light), emotional responses (like sensitivity to criticism), or the ability of an instrument or device to accurately measure small changes.


例句:

He showed great sensitivity to the suffering of others. 他对别人的痛苦表现出极大的同情心。

The camera's sensitivity to light allows for excellent low-light photography.  相机的感光度使其能够拍摄出优秀的低光照照片。


近义词: responsiveness, susceptibility, delicacy, perceptiveness, acuity


反义词: insensitivity, callousness, apathy, dullness, bluntness


词性: noun

词源:
