intrepidness:/ɪnˈtrɛpɪdnəs/| n. |勇敢无畏；胆量；毅力|The quality of being intrepid; fearless adventurousness.  It suggests a courageous spirit that faces danger or difficulty without fear.  |例句：Her intrepidness in the face of danger was remarkable.  她面对危险时的勇敢令人瞩目。

例句：The climbers demonstrated remarkable intrepidness in conquering the treacherous mountain. 登山者征服险峻山峰展现了非凡的勇气。


近义词：bravery, courage, boldness, daring, valor

反义词：cowardice, timidity, fearfulness


词源：源自于拉丁语 *intrepidus，由 *in- (不) + *trepidus (颤抖的，害怕的)构成。
