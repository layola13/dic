misraise:/ˌmɪsˈreɪz/| vt.|错误地加注赌注；错误地提高赌注|To raise a bet incorrectly, usually by exceeding the allowed increment or by failing to follow the rules of the game.|例句：He misraised his bet by $10, exceeding the maximum increment allowed. 他错误地加注了10美元，超过了允许的最大增量。|例句：She misraised, forgetting to match the previous bet before raising. 她错误地加注了，忘记在加注之前匹配之前的赌注。|近义词：misplace, misjudge|反义词：correctly raise, properly raise|动词
