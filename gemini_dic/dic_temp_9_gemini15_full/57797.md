dredging:/ˈdrɛdʒɪŋ/| n. v.|疏浚；挖掘；拖网捕捞|The process of removing sediment from the bottom of a body of water to maintain or deepen a navigable channel.  It also refers to the act of searching for something, often in a thorough or painstaking manner.|例句：The harbor needs dredging to accommodate larger ships. 港口需要疏浚以容纳更大的船只。

例句：They are dredging the river to improve navigation. 他们正在疏浚河流以改善航运。


近义词：excavation, excavation, deepening, scooping|反义词：filling, sedimentation|词性:noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dredge the harbor regularly. = 他们定期疏浚港口。
The company dredges the riverbed for sand. = 这家公司在河床上挖掘沙子。


一般过去时:
They dredged the canal last year. = 他们去年疏浚了运河。
The archaeologists dredged the seabed for artifacts. = 考古学家在海底挖掘文物。


现在完成时:
They have dredged the channel several times this year. = 今年他们已经疏浚了航道几次。
The authorities have dredged the lake to remove the pollutants. = 当局已经疏浚了湖泊以清除污染物。


过去完成时:
They had dredged the river before the flood. = 洪水来临之前，他们已经疏浚了河流。
The engineers had dredged the area before constructing the bridge. = 工程师们在建造桥梁之前已经疏浚了该区域。


将来时:
They will dredge the port next month. = 他们下个月将疏浚港口。
The government will dredge the river to prevent flooding. = 政府将疏浚河流以防止洪水。


虚拟语气:
If they dredged the river, the boats could pass easily. = 如果他们疏浚了河流，船只就能轻松通行。
I wish they had dredged the lake earlier to prevent the algal bloom. = 我希望他们早点疏浚湖泊以防止藻类大量繁殖。


被动语态:
The harbor is dredged annually. = 港口每年都会进行疏浚。
The riverbed is being dredged to remove the accumulated silt. = 河床正在被疏浚以清除累积的淤泥。


疑问句:
Will they dredge the channel this year? = 他们今年会疏浚航道吗？
Is the lake being dredged to improve water quality? = 为了改善水质，湖泊正在疏浚吗？


否定句:
They did not dredge the canal last year. = 他们去年没有疏浚运河。
The river was not dredged properly, leading to flooding. = 河流没有得到妥善疏浚，导致洪水泛滥。

词源:
源自中古英语 *dredgen，可能源自古荷兰语 *dreggen，意为“拖网捕捞”。  其含义后来扩展到包括用类似工具进行的挖掘和疏浚工作。
