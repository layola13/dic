radioactive:/ˌreɪdiˈoʊæktɪv/| adj.|放射性的，具有放射性的|Emitting or relating to the emission of ionizing radiation or particles.|The radioactive waste needs to be disposed of safely.|近义词：radioactive, radiating|反义词：non-radioactive, stable
