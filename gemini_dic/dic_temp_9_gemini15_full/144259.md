outsoar:/ˌaʊtˈsɔːr/| vt.|超越，胜过；飞越|To fly or soar higher than; to surpass or excel.|例句：The eagle outsoared all other birds.  鹰飞得比其他鸟都高。

例句：Her achievements outsoared all expectations. 她的成就超出了所有人的预期。|近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend, outstrip|反义词：underperform, lag behind|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The eagle outsoars all other birds. = 鹰飞得比其他鸟都高。
He consistently outsoars his competitors in sales. = 他在销售方面始终超越竞争对手。


一般过去时:
The plane outsoared the clouds. = 飞机飞越了云层。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
