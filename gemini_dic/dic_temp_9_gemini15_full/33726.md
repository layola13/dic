circles:/ˈsɜːrkəlz/| n.|圆圈，循环，圈子|A circle is a round plane figure whose boundary consists of points equidistant from a fixed center.  Circles can also refer to a group of people with shared interests or a recurring sequence of events.|例句：The children drew circles on the pavement.孩子们在人行道上画圈。|例句：His social circles are quite broad.他的社交圈子很广。|近义词：rings, rounds, groups|反义词：straight lines, squares
