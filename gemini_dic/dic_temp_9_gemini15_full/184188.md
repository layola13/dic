resoak:/ˌriːˈsoʊk/| vt.|再次浸泡；重新浸湿|To soak again; to soak thoroughly.|例句：I had to resoak the beans because they weren't soft enough. 我不得不再次浸泡豆子，因为它们不够软。|例句：The artist resoaked the canvas to remove the unwanted paint. 艺术家重新浸湿画布以去除不需要的颜料。|近义词：soak again, re-soak, steep again|反义词：dry, drain
