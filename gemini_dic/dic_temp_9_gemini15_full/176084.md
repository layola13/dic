rawest:/ˈrɔːɪst/| adj.|最生的；最粗糙的；最原始的 | Most basic or unrefined; in its earliest or most natural state.  The word implies a lack of processing, refinement, or development.|例句：He ate the rawest oysters he could find. 他吃了能找到的最生的牡蛎。

例句：The rawest form of data is often difficult to interpret. 最原始的数据形式通常难以解释。


近义词：crudest, most primitive, unprocessed, unrefined


反义词：refined, processed, sophisticated, cultured


词性：形容词
