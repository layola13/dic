It can also refer to multiple instances of renewal or regeneration in a broader sense.|Example:  The art historian discussed several historical renascences across different civilizations.  艺术史学家讨论了不同文明中几个历史上的文艺复兴时期。|近义词: revivals, resurgences, regenerations |反义词: declines, decadences, deteriorations |名词
