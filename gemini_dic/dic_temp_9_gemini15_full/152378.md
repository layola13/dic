pave:/peɪv/| vt.|铺设；为…铺路；为…做准备|To cover a surface with hard material such as stones, bricks, or concrete.  To prepare the way for something.|例句：They paved the road with asphalt. 他们用沥青铺了路。

They paved the way for future generations. 他们为后代铺平了道路。|近义词：lay, cover, prepare|反义词：destroy, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The workers pave the road every day. = 工人们每天铺路。
They pave the driveway with bricks. = 他们用砖铺车道。

一般过去时:
They paved the street last week. = 他们上周铺了街道。
The city paved the sidewalks. = 城市铺设了人行道。

现在完成时:
They have paved the entire highway. = 他们已经铺好了整条高速公路。
The construction crew has paved most of the area. = 建设队已经铺设了大部分区域。

过去完成时:
They had paved the path before the festival. = 他们在节日之前铺好了小路。
The builders had paved the square by the time the ceremony began. = 到仪式开始时，建筑商们已经铺好了广场。

将来时:
They will pave the new road next month. = 他们下个月将铺设新路。
The government will pave the streets in the spring. = 政府将在春天铺设街道。

虚拟语气:
If they paved the street, traffic would improve. = 如果他们铺了街道，交通状况就会改善。
I wish they had paved the road sooner. = 我希望他们早点铺路。

被动语态:
The road was paved last year. = 这条路是去年铺的。
The new sidewalk is being paved now. = 新的人行道正在铺设中。

疑问句:
Have they paved the driveway yet? = 他们铺好车道了吗？
When will the road be paved? = 这条路什么时候铺？

否定句:
They didn't pave the road on time. = 他们没有按时铺路。
They haven't paved the path yet. = 他们还没铺好小路。
