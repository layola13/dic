depose:/dɪˈpoʊz/| vt.|罢免；免职；作证；陈述|To remove someone from a high position of power, especially a throne or office. To give evidence or testimony, especially in a formal setting. |例句：The king was deposed in a bloody coup. 国王在一场血腥的政变中被罢免。  The witness deposed that she saw the defendant fleeing the scene. 目击证人证实她看到被告逃离现场。|近义词：dethrone, oust, remove, testify, attest|反义词：appoint, install, enthrone|动词
