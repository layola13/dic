recruitment:/rɪˈkruːtmənt/| n. |招聘，征募|The process of finding and hiring new employees.  It involves identifying needs, attracting applicants, screening candidates, and selecting the best fit. |Example: The company's recruitment process is very thorough. 这家公司的招聘流程非常严格。

The university's recruitment drive attracted many talented students. 大学的招生活动吸引了许多有才华的学生。|近义词：enrollment, enlistment, hiring|反义词：dismissal, layoff


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company recruits new graduates every year. = 公司每年都招聘应届毕业生。
Our team is actively recruiting for a new software engineer. = 我们的团队正在积极招聘一名新的软件工程师。

一般过去时:
They recruited a large number of volunteers for the project. = 他们为该项目招募了大量志愿者。
The army recruited many young men during the war. = 战争期间，军队招募了许多年轻男子。

现在完成时:
We have recruited five new employees this month. = 这个月我们已经招聘了五名新员工。
The company has been recruiting aggressively to expand its workforce. = 公司一直在积极招聘以扩大其员工队伍。

过去完成时:
They had already recruited the team before the project started. = 项目开始前他们就已经招募好团队了。
The company had recruited several candidates before settling on the best one. = 公司在选定最佳人选之前已经招募了几位候选人。

将来时:
We will recruit more staff next year. = 我们明年将招聘更多员工。
The firm will be recruiting extensively for the new project. = 该公司将为新项目进行大规模招聘。

虚拟语气:
If they recruited more experienced staff, the project would be more successful. = 如果他们招募更多经验丰富的员工，项目将会更成功。
I wish they had recruited a more diverse team. = 我希望他们已经招募了一个更加多元化的团队。

被动语态:
Many applicants were recruited for the position. = 许多申请者被录用。
The new employees were carefully recruited and trained. = 新员工经过精心挑选和培训。

疑问句:
How many people did they recruit? = 他们招募了多少人？
Where are you recruiting from? = 你从哪里招聘？

否定句:
They didn't recruit enough people for the event. = 他们为这次活动招募的人不够多。
The company did not recruit anyone without a degree. = 公司没有招聘任何没有学位的人。
