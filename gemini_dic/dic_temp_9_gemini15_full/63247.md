enterpriser:/ˈɛntəpraɪzər/ | n. | 企业家；创业者 | A person who organizes and operates a business or businesses, taking on financial risks in the hope of making a profit.  They are often innovative and willing to take calculated risks. | Example:  He's a successful enterpriser in the tech industry. 他是科技行业里一位成功的企业家。  She's an enterpriser who started her own bakery from scratch. 她是一位白手起家的创业者，自己开了一家面包店。| 近义词：entrepreneur, businessman, businesswoman, innovator | 反义词：employee, worker
