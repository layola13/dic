cyberpunk:/ˈsaɪbərpʌŋk/| n. adj.|赛博朋克；赛博朋克的|A subgenre of science fiction that typically features a dystopian, technologically advanced future characterized by social inequality, cybernetics, and advanced technology.  It often explores themes of alienation, oppression, and the blurring lines between humanity and technology.|例句：Blade Runner is a classic example of cyberpunk cinema.  《银翼杀手》是赛博朋克电影的经典之作。  The novel depicts a gritty cyberpunk world filled with hackers and corporate espionage. 这部小说描绘了一个充满黑客和企业间谍活动的粗犷的赛博朋克世界。|近义词：dystopian fiction, science fiction, techno-thriller|反义词：utopia, pastoral
