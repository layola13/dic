page-through: /peɪdʒ θruː/ | v. | 快速浏览；翻阅 | To look through (a book, magazine, etc.) quickly, often without reading everything in detail.  |例句：I page-through the magazine looking for interesting articles. 我快速翻阅杂志寻找有趣的文章。

例句：She page-through the catalog before making a decision. 她浏览目录后才做决定。


近义词：skim, browse, glance through, leaf through | 反义词：read thoroughly, study carefully | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pages through the newspaper every morning. = 他每天早上都快速翻阅报纸。
They page through the cookbook, searching for a new recipe. = 他们在食谱中翻阅，寻找新的食谱。

一般过去时:
I paged through the book but didn't find anything interesting. = 我翻阅了这本书，但没有发现什么有趣的东西。
She paged through the documents before the meeting. = 会议前她快速浏览了文件。

现在完成时:
I have paged through this report three times, and still haven't found the information. = 我已经翻阅了这份报告三次，但仍然没有找到信息。
He has paged through several books looking for a good story. = 他翻阅了几本书，寻找一个好故事。

过去完成时:
She had paged through the manuscript before she submitted it. = 在她提交手稿之前，她已经浏览过手稿了。
They had paged through the contract several times, before signing it. = 他们在签署合同之前已经翻阅了好几次。

将来时:
I will page through the magazine later tonight. = 我今晚晚些时候会翻阅杂志。
She will page through the application forms tomorrow. = 她明天将浏览申请表。


被动语态:
The book was paged through by many readers. = 这本书被许多读者翻阅过。
The magazine was paged through quickly by the customer. = 顾客快速翻阅了杂志。

疑问句:
Have you paged through the manual yet? = 你已经翻阅过手册了吗？
Did he page through the report before making the presentation? = 他在做演示之前翻阅过报告了吗？

否定句:
I didn't page through the whole document. = 我没有通读整个文件。
She didn't page through the book, she read it cover to cover. = 她没有快速翻阅这本书，而是从头到尾读完。
