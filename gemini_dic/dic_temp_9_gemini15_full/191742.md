scientistic:/ˌsaɪənˈtɪstɪk/| adj.|科学主义的；科学夸夸其谈的|Relating to or characteristic of scientism;  often used negatively to describe an excessive belief in the power of science to solve all problems, or an overly simplistic or reductionist application of scientific methods.|例句：His scientistic approach to social problems ignored the complexities of human behavior.  他的科学主义方法处理社会问题忽略了人类行为的复杂性。

例句：The scientistic claims made by the company were not supported by evidence. 该公司做出的科学主义说法缺乏证据支持。


近义词：reductionist, positivistic, mechanistic

反义词：holistic, humanistic, philosophical


词性:形容词
