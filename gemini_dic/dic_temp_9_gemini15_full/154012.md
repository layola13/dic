perform:/pərˈfɔːrm/| /pɚˈfɔːrm/ | vt. vi. n. |表演；履行；执行；表现 | to carry out, accomplish, or fulfill an action, task, or function; to act out a role or piece of music; to function or operate in a specified manner |例句：The band performed brilliantly. 乐队表演精彩绝伦。  The computer is performing poorly. 这台电脑运行得很差。|近义词：execute, accomplish, achieve, enact, play |反义词：fail, neglect


USAGE->

He performs his duties diligently. 他勤奋地履行职责。
The magician performs amazing tricks. 魔术师表演令人惊奇的戏法。
