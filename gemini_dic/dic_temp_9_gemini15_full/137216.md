nonsuppression:/nɒn səˈprɛʃən/| n. |非压制；不抑制；未经压制|The absence of suppression; the state of not being suppressed.  This term is often used in contexts related to freedom of expression, immune responses, or other processes that might normally be suppressed. |例句：The government's policy of nonsuppression allowed diverse viewpoints to flourish.  政府的不压制政策使得各种观点蓬勃发展。

The study showed nonsuppression of the immune response to the vaccine.  研究表明疫苗对免疫反应没有抑制作用。|近义词：tolerance, permissiveness, freedom |反义词：suppression, repression, inhibition
