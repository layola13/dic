/krʌmpi/| adj.|脾气暴躁的；易怒的；不高兴的|Grumpy describes someone who is in a bad mood and easily annoyed.  It suggests a persistent, rather than temporary, state of irritability.|He's been grumpy all morning.|近义词：irritable, surly, crotchety, bad-tempered|反义词：cheerful, happy, pleasant|


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It describes a state of being.  The tense of the sentence in which it's used will affect the overall meaning, but the word itself doesn't change form.


Example 1 (Present Tense):
The grumpy old man sat alone on the bench. = 脾气暴躁的老人独自坐在长椅上。

Example 2 (Past Tense):
She was grumpy yesterday because she didn't get enough sleep. = 她昨天脾气不好，因为她睡眠不足。


Example 3 (Future Tense):
He'll probably be grumpy if he misses the train again. = 如果他再次错过火车，他可能会很生气。

Example 4 (Present Perfect):
He's been grumpy all week.  = 他整个星期都脾气暴躁。



PHRASE->
There are not many common phrases directly using "grumpy," as it's primarily a descriptive adjective. However, we can create phrases:

a grumpy mood = 坏脾气
feeling grumpy = 感到脾气暴躁
a grumpy disposition = 脾气暴躁的性格
grumpy cat = 脾气暴躁的猫 (popular meme)
grumpy face = 愠怒的脸
