prepolish:/ˌpriːˈpɒlɪʃ/ | /ˌpriːˈpɑːlɪʃ/| v.| 预先抛光；初步抛光 | To polish something lightly or partially before a more thorough polishing.  This is often done to remove surface imperfections before the final polishing stage. | Example:  The mechanic prepolished the car body before applying the final coat of paint. = 技师在涂上最后一层油漆之前先对车身进行了预抛光。 |近义词：rough polish, preliminary polish | 反义词：final polish, high polish
