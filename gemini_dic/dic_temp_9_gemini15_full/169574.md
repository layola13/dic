prophet:/ˈprɒfɪt/ /ˈprɑːfɪt/| n. |预言家，先知 |A prophet is a person regarded as divinely inspired to speak or interpret the will of a god.  They often predict future events or deliver messages from a deity. |The prophet warned the people of the impending earthquake.  这位先知警告人们即将发生地震。|seer, visionary, oracle| deceiver, liar| 名词
