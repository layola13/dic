expressor:/ɪkˈsprɛsər/ | n. | 表达者；表达工具；基因表达子 | A person or thing that expresses something.  This term is often used in the context of genetics to refer to a gene or protein that promotes the expression of another gene. |例句：The artist was a powerful expressor of emotion.  这位艺术家是情感的有力表达者。


The transcription factor acts as an expressor of the target gene.  转录因子充当靶基因的表达子。


近义词： conveyor, transmitter, exponent, articulator | 反义词：suppressor (in the context of gene expression) | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist expresses his feelings through his paintings. =艺术家通过绘画表达他的情感。
The machine expresses the data in a graph. =机器以图表形式表达数据。


一般过去时:
She expressed her concern about the project. =她表达了她对这个项目的担忧。
He expressed his gratitude for their help. =他表达了对他们帮助的感激之情。


现在完成时:
They have expressed their support for the candidate. =他们已经表达了对候选人的支持。
She has expressed her interest in the job. =她已经表达了她对这份工作的兴趣。


过去完成时:
He had expressed his reservations before the meeting. =他在会议前已经表达了他的保留意见。
They had expressed their opinions earlier that day. =他们那天早些时候已经表达了他们的意见。


将来时:
We will express our opinions at the meeting. =我们将在会议上表达我们的意见。
She will express her thanks in a letter. =她将在信中表达她的谢意。


虚拟语气:
If I could express myself better, I would. =如果我能更好地表达自己，我会的。
I wish I could express my feelings more clearly. =我希望我能更清晰地表达我的感受。


被动语态:
His ideas were expressed clearly and concisely. =他的想法表达得清晰简洁。
The results were expressed as percentages. =结果以百分比表示。


疑问句:
How did he express his anger? =他如何表达他的愤怒？
What methods did she use to express her creativity? =她用什么方法表达她的创造力？


否定句:
He didn't express his opinion on the matter. =他对这件事没有表达他的意见。
She couldn't express herself clearly in English. =她无法用英语清晰地表达自己。
