harkens:/ˈhɑːrkənz/| v.| 回溯；追溯；暗示；预示 |To harken back to something is to recall or refer to it.  It often implies a connection to the past. It can also suggest that something foreshadows or alludes to something else.|例句：The novel harkens back to the classic tales of adventure. 这部小说追溯到经典的冒险故事。

The modern art harkens to the styles of the Renaissance. 现代艺术暗示了文艺复兴时期的风格。|近义词：recall, remember, allude, suggest, foreshadow |反义词：ignore, forget, disregard |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harkens back to his childhood memories. = 他回想起童年的回忆。
The design harkens to traditional styles. = 这种设计让人想起传统风格。


一般过去时:
The speaker harkened back to the events of the previous year. = 演讲者回顾了去年的事件。
The painting harkened to the works of the old masters. = 这幅画让人想起旧大师的作品。


现在完成时:
The film has harkened back to the golden age of Hollywood. = 这部电影让人回想起好莱坞的黄金时代。
The author has harkened to the themes of loss and redemption. = 作者已经触及了失去和救赎的主题。


过去完成时:
She had harkened back to her roots before making the decision. = 在做决定之前，她已经追溯到她的根源。
The architect had harkened to the principles of sustainable design. = 建筑师已经遵循了可持续设计的原则。


将来时:
The director will harken back to the original story in the sequel. = 导演将在续集中回顾原来的故事。
The musician will harken to the influences of jazz in his new album. = 音乐家将在他的新专辑中借鉴爵士乐的影响。


虚拟语气:
If the artist harkened to his critics, he might improve. = 如果艺术家听取了评论家的意见，他可能会进步。
I wish the writer had harkened to the advice of his editor. = 我希望作者能听取他编辑的建议。


被动语态:
The play is harkened to as a masterpiece. = 这部剧被认为是杰作。
The tradition is harkened back to by many generations. = 许多代人都追溯到这一传统。


疑问句:
Does the song harken back to any particular genre? = 这首歌是否让人想起任何特定的类型？
Does the artwork harken to any specific movements? = 这件艺术品是否让人想起任何特定的流派？


否定句:
The movie doesn't harken back to any previous installments. = 这部电影并没有让人想起之前的任何一部。
The style doesn't harken to any known historical period. = 这种风格并不让人想起任何已知的历史时期。


PHRASE->
harken back to = 追溯到
harken to = 倾听；听从
harken after = 追求
harken unto = 听从（较为正式）
harken forth = 发出；宣布 (较少用)
