malady:/ˈmælədi/| n. |疾病，弊病|A malady is a disease or ailment.  It can refer to a physical illness or a more general problem or affliction. The term often suggests something serious or persistent.|例句：The doctor diagnosed a rare malady.医生诊断出一种罕见疾病。

The country is suffering from a malady of economic stagnation.  这个国家正遭受经济停滞的困扰。|近义词：disease, ailment, illness, affliction|反义词：health, wellness
