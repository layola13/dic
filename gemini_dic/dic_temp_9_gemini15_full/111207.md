lithophagous:/lɪˈθɒfəɡəs/| adj.|凿石的；食石的|Feeding on or boring into stone;  relating to organisms that erode rock by chemical or mechanical means.  |例句：Certain lithophagous mollusks bore into rocks to create their homes.  某些凿石的软体动物在岩石中钻洞来建造它们的住所。|例句：The lithophagous activity of these bivalves significantly alters the coastal ecosystem.  这些双壳类动物的凿石活动显著改变了沿海生态系统。|近义词：rock-boring, stone-eating |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could include terms describing organisms that feed on soft materials.
