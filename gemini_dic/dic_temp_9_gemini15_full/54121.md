disenchanter: /ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃɑːntər/ | n. | 令人失望的人；使人幻灭的人 |  A person or thing that destroys or diminishes someone's illusions or enthusiasm.  | The new evidence acted as a disenchanter, shattering the hero's carefully constructed image.  新的证据起到了令人失望的作用，粉碎了英雄精心塑造的形象。 | 近义词： disillusioner, debunker | 反义词： inspirer, motivator |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new policy disenchants many voters. = 新政策让许多选民感到失望。
He disenchants his fans with his recent behavior. = 他最近的行为让粉丝们失望了。


一般过去时:
The magician's trick disenchanted the children. = 魔术师的戏法让孩子们大失所望。
Her poor performance disenchanted the audience. = 她糟糕的表现让观众失望了。


现在完成时:
The repeated delays have disenchanted many potential buyers. = 反复的延误已经让许多潜在买家感到失望。
His broken promises have disenchanted his friends. = 他失信的行为让他的朋友们很失望。


过去完成时:
By the end of the war, the soldiers had been disenchanted with the leadership. = 到战争结束时，士兵们已经对领导层感到失望了。
They had been disenchanted with the government's response to the crisis. = 他们对政府应对危机的反应感到非常失望。


将来时:
The upcoming changes will disenchant some employees. = 即将来临的改变将会让一些员工感到失望。
This new evidence will disenchant the public. = 这条新证据将会让公众失望。


虚拟语气:
If the outcome had been different, it wouldn’t have disenchanted him so much. = 如果结果不同的话，就不会让他那么失望了。
I wish the ending of the movie hadn’t disenchanted me so much. = 我真希望电影的结局不会让我这么失望。


被动语态:
Many were disenchanted by the outcome of the election. = 许多人对选举的结果感到失望。
The fans were disenchanted by their team's poor performance. = 粉丝们对他们球队的糟糕表现感到失望。


疑问句:
What disenchanted you about the project? = 项目的什么地方让你失望了？
Has anything disenchanted you lately? = 最近有什么事情让你失望了吗？


否定句:
The speech didn't disenchant the crowd. = 这番演讲并没有让群众失望。
The result did not disenchant the researchers. = 这个结果并没有让研究人员失望。
