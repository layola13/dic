sinistrorse:/sɪˈnɪstrɔːrs/| adj.|左旋的；左向的；（植物）左旋的|Turning or twisting to the left;  in botany, having the parts spirally arranged from right to left (as seen from above).  This is the opposite of dextrorse.|The vine grew sinistrorse around the trellis.|近义词：left-handed, counterclockwise|反义词：dextrorse, right-handed
