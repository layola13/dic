countercast:/ˈkaʊntərkæst/ | n. |反驳；反驳的论点 |A countercast is a statement or argument that contradicts or opposes another. It's often used in legal or academic contexts to refute a previous claim or theory.  |例句：The defense attorney presented a countercast to the prosecution's theory of the crime.  辩护律师对检方的犯罪理论提出了反驳。  |近义词：rebuttal, refutation, counterargument |反义词：confirmation, corroboration |名词
