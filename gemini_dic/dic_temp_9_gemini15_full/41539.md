coroneted:/ˈkɒrəneɪtɪd/|/ˈkɔːrəneɪtɪd/| adj.|加冕的；戴冠的|Adorned with a crown; having a crown.  The word implies a formal bestowal of a crown, often associated with royalty or high status.|例句：The coroneted monarch addressed the nation.  加冕的君主向全国发表讲话。

The coroneted eagle on the banner represented the empire's power.  旗帜上的戴着王冠的鹰象征着帝国的权力。|近义词：crowned, diademed|反义词：uncrowned, unadorned
