dehumidify:/ˌdiːˈhjuːmɪdaɪ/| vt.| 除湿，使干燥 | To remove moisture from something, especially air.  | 例句：The air conditioner dehumidifies the room effectively. 空调有效地使房间除湿。 | 例句：We need to dehumidify the basement to prevent mold growth. 我们需要对地下室除湿以防止霉菌生长。 | 近义词：dry, dehydrate, desiccate | 反义词：humidify, moisten
