pseudo-christianity: /ˌsuːdoʊkrɪʃtʃənˈælɪti/ | n. |伪基督教；似是而非的基督教 |  A system of belief or practice that superficially resembles Christianity but lacks its genuine spirit or essence;  a counterfeit or imitation of Christianity. It often involves selective adoption of Christian terminology or practices without a true understanding or commitment to core Christian tenets. | Example:  Some historians argue that certain ancient cults exhibited aspects of pseudo-Christianity.  一些历史学家认为某些古代邪教表现出伪基督教的某些方面。 | 近义词:  false Christianity, imitation Christianity, ersatz Christianity | 反义词: genuine Christianity, true Christianity | 词性: noun
