comportments:/kəmˈpɔːrtmənts/ | n. |举止；行为；态度 | The word "comportment" refers to a person's behavior or demeanor, especially in a formal or public setting.  It suggests a more deliberate and controlled aspect of behavior than the word "behavior" alone might imply.  |Example:  Her comportment at the royal banquet was impeccable.  她王室宴会上的举止无可挑剔。

Example:  The children's comportment improved significantly after the teacher's talk.  孩子们在老师讲过话后举止有了显著的改善。


近义词：demeanor, behavior, conduct, bearing | 反义词：misbehavior, misconduct | 词性:名词
