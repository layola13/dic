predeterminer: /ˌpriːdɪˈtɜːrmɪnər/ | n. | 前限定词 | A word that precedes and modifies a determiner, such as "all," "both," "half," or "such."  Predeterminers further specify the quantity or type of noun being described.  They are always placed before the determiner. |  例句：All the students passed the exam. (所有学生都通过了考试。)  Both my parents are doctors. (我的父母都是医生。)


例句：
All the books are on the shelf. = 所有书都在书架上。

Half the cake was eaten. =  半个蛋糕被吃掉了。


近义词：multiplier (在某些语境下)

反义词：无直接反义词，其作用是修饰限定词，并非与其形成对立。

词性: 名词
