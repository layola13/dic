shop:/ʃɒp/ /ʃɑːp/| n. v.|商店；购物；买东西|A shop is a place where goods are sold.  It can also refer to the act of buying goods. |例句：I went to the shop to buy some milk. 我去商店买牛奶了。

The baker sold his bread at the local shop. 面包师在当地商店卖他的面包。|近义词：store, store, market|反义词：factory, producer|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I shop at that supermarket every week. = 我每周都在那家超市购物。
He shops for clothes online. = 他在网上买衣服。

一般过去时:
We shopped for hours yesterday. = 我们昨天逛街好几个小时。
She shopped at the mall and bought a new dress. = 她在商场购物并买了一件新裙子。

现在完成时:
I have shopped at this store many times. = 我在这家店买过很多次东西了。
They have shopped for all the ingredients for the cake. = 他们已经买齐了做蛋糕的所有材料。

过去完成时:
I had shopped at the farmer's market before lunch. = 我在午餐前去过农贸市场购物。
She had shopped online before the store opened. = 商店开门前，她就在网上购物了。

将来时:
We will shop for groceries tomorrow. = 我们明天去买杂货。
He will shop around for the best price before buying. = 他会在购买前货比三家。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would shop at more expensive stores. = 如果我有更多钱，我会去更贵的商店购物。
I wish I could shop for new shoes. = 我真希望我能买双新鞋。

被动语态:
The shop is cleaned every night. = 这家商店每天晚上都打扫干净。
The goods are being shopped online. = 货物正在网上销售。

疑问句:
Where do you usually shop? = 你通常在哪里购物？
What time does the shop open? = 商店几点开门？

否定句:
I don't shop at that store anymore. = 我不再在那家店购物了。
He didn't shop for anything today. = 他今天什么都没买。


PHRASE->

online shop: 网上商店
