nonvolitional: /ˌnɑːnvoʊˈlɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非意志的，非自觉的 |  Not done by conscious choice or will; involuntary.  It describes actions or processes that occur without conscious control or intent.  | Example:  The nonvolitional blinking of his eyes showed his nervousness.  他的眼睛不由自主地眨动显示了他的紧张。


Example:  His involuntary twitch was a nonvolitional movement. 他不由自主的抽搐是一个非意志的运动。


近义词: involuntary, unconscious, automatic, reflexive

反义词: volitional, voluntary, deliberate, intentional


词性: adjective
