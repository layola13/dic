open-cast:/ˈoʊpən kæst/| adj. |露天开采的；露天矿的 |Open-cast mining refers to a surface mining technique where the earth is removed to expose the ore body, unlike underground mining.  |例句：The open-cast mine scarred the landscape.  露天矿破坏了景观。  |例句：Open-cast coal mining is a controversial practice. 露天开采煤炭是一种有争议的做法。|近义词：surface, quarry |反义词：underground
