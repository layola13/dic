photointerpretation:/ˌfoʊtoʊɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |照片解释；影像判读|The act of examining photographs or other imagery to extract information.  This involves identifying objects, features, and patterns, and interpreting their significance.  It's commonly used in fields like geography, geology, and intelligence gathering.|例句：The photointerpretation of satellite images revealed the extent of the forest fire.  卫星图像的影像判读揭示了森林火灾的范围。

例句：Careful photointerpretation is crucial for accurate geological mapping.  仔细的照片解释对于精确的地质测绘至关重要。


近义词：image analysis, aerial photographic interpretation, remote sensing interpretation


反义词：None directly applicable, as it's not a concept with a direct opposite.  The closest might be "misinterpretation" or "lack of interpretation".
