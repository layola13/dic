fall-down:/fɔːl daʊn/ | v. |摔倒；倒塌；下降；衰退 | To lose one's balance and fall to the ground; to collapse; to decrease or decline.  |例句：The child fell down and scraped his knee. 孩子摔倒擦破了膝盖。 The old building fell down during the earthquake.  旧楼在地震中倒塌了。|近义词：tumble, collapse, decline |反义词：rise, ascend, improve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He falls down often. = 他经常摔倒。
The stock market falls down. = 股市下跌。

一般过去时:
She fell down the stairs. = 她从楼梯上摔下来了。
The empire fell down. = 帝国衰落了。

现在完成时:
The bridge has fallen down. = 桥梁已经倒塌了。
The sales have fallen down this month. = 这个月的销售额下降了。

过去完成时:
The building had fallen down before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，大楼已经倒塌了。
The company's profits had fallen down significantly before the new CEO took over. = 在新任CEO上任之前，公司的利润已经大幅下降。

将来时:
The tree will fall down in the storm. = 这棵树将在暴风雨中倒下。
The economy is expected to fall down next year. = 预计明年经济将会下滑。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been careful, I would have fallen down. = 如果我不小心，我就摔倒了。
If the government hadn't intervened, the company would have fallen down. = 如果政府不干预，公司就会倒闭。

被动语态:
The vase was fallen down by the cat. = 花瓶被猫打碎了。(注意：被动语态中 "fall down" 常用过去分词作宾语补足语)
The plan was fallen down due to lack of funding. = 计划因缺乏资金而搁置了。(注意：被动语态中 "fall down" 常用过去分词作宾语补足语)

疑问句:
Did he fall down? = 他摔倒了吗？
Has the price fallen down? = 价格下降了吗？

否定句:
He didn't fall down. = 他没有摔倒。
The building didn't fall down. = 大楼没有倒塌。


PHRASE->
fall down on (one's job) = 疏忽职守
fall down flat = 完全失败
fall down stairs = 从楼梯上摔下来
fall down from = 从...摔下来
fall down with = 因...而倒下 (疾病等)
