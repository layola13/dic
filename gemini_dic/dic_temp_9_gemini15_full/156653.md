pictured:/ˈpɪktʃərd/| adj.|描绘的；照片中的；想象中的|Depicted in a picture or photograph; imagined or envisioned.  |例句：The pictured scene was idyllic. 照片中的景色非常优美。
The pictured suspect is described as tall and thin. 图片中的嫌疑犯被描述为高个子且瘦弱。|近义词：depicted, illustrated, envisioned, imagined|反义词：unpictured, undescribed


USAGE->
