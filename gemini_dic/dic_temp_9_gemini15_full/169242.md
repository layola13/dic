prolonging:/prəˈlɒŋɪŋ/| v. |延长；拖延；拉长|The act of making something last longer; extending the duration of something.  |例句：Prolonging the meeting only served to exhaust everyone.  延长会议只会让每个人都筋疲力尽。|例句：The medication helps prolong life for patients with the disease.这种药物有助于延长患有这种疾病的患者的生命。|近义词：extend, lengthen, continue, defer|反义词：shorten, curtail, abbreviate|动词


USAGE->

He is prolonging his stay. = 他正在延长他的逗留时间。
They are prolonging the negotiations. = 他们在拖延谈判。
