mementos:/məˈmɛntoʊz/| n.|纪念品，遗物|Objects that serve as reminders of a person, place, or event.  They often hold sentimental value.|例句：I keep a collection of mementos from my travels. 我收藏着旅行的纪念品。

例句：She gave him a memento of their time together. 她送给他一件他们在一起的纪念品。


近义词：souvenirs, keepsakes, reminders, relics|反义词：None particularly fitting|词性：名词
