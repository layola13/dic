pluck-out: /plʌk aʊt/ | v. |拔出；除去；摘除 | To pull or remove something, usually with a quick, forceful movement. |例句：He plucked out a grey hair from his head. 他从头上拔掉一根白头发。


He plucked out the weeds from the flower bed. 他把花坛里的杂草拔掉了。


近义词：pull out, remove, extract | 反义词：insert, implant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She plucks out her eyebrows every week. = 她每周都修眉。
The farmer plucks out the ripe tomatoes. = 农民摘下成熟的西红柿。

一般过去时:
I plucked out a splinter from my finger. = 我从手指里拔出一根刺。
The dentist plucked out my bad tooth. = 牙医拔掉了我的坏牙。

现在完成时:
She has plucked out all the weeds from the garden. = 她已经把花园里的杂草都拔掉了。
He has plucked out several grey hairs this month. = 这个月他已经拔掉了好几根白头发。

过去完成时:
She had plucked out all the weeds before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，她已经把所有的杂草都拔掉了。
The gardener had plucked out the diseased plants. = 园丁已经把患病的植物拔掉了。

将来时:
I will pluck out the remaining thorns tomorrow. = 我明天会把剩下的刺拔掉。
She will pluck out her eyebrows before the party. = 她会在派对前修眉。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pluck out that annoying hair. = 如果我是你，我会把那根恼人的头发拔掉。
I wish I had plucked out that weed earlier. = 我真希望我早点把那棵杂草拔掉。

被动语态:
The weeds were plucked out by the gardener. = 杂草被园丁拔掉了。
The bad tooth was plucked out by the dentist. = 那颗坏牙被牙医拔掉了。

疑问句:
Have you plucked out all the weeds yet? = 你把所有的杂草都拔掉了吗？
Will he pluck out his eyebrows tomorrow? = 他明天会修眉吗？

否定句:
I didn't pluck out the splinter. = 我没有拔掉那根刺。
She doesn't pluck out her eyebrows often. = 她不经常修眉。


PHRASE->
pluck out one's eyes = 挖掉某人的眼睛
pluck out a tune = 随意弹奏曲调
pluck out a feather = 拔下一根羽毛
pluck out a tooth = 拔牙
pluck out a heart = 挖心


词源:
The word "pluck" comes from the Old English word "pluccian," meaning "to pull." The "-out" suffix indicates a complete removal.  The combination thus signifies a forceful and complete removal by pulling.
