dhaba:/ˈdɑːbɑː/| n. |小餐馆，路边摊|A dhaba is a roadside restaurant, typically found in India and Pakistan, that serves simple, often inexpensive food.  They are known for their quick service and are popular with travelers. |例句：We stopped at a dhaba for lunch and had some delicious dal makhani. 我们在一家小餐馆停下来吃午饭，吃了美味的达尔马卡尼。  The dhaba offered a variety of curries and naan bread. 这家小餐馆提供各种咖喱和馕。|近义词：eatery, restaurant, roadside stall |反义词：fine dining restaurant, upscale restaurant
