etiologists:/ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 病因学者；病原学家 | Scientists who study the causes of diseases.  Etiologists investigate the factors that contribute to the development of illnesses, from genetic predispositions to environmental influences. |  Dr. Smith, a leading etiologist, presented her findings on the causes of the recent outbreak. 史密斯博士是一位领先的病因学者，她介绍了她对最近疫情爆发原因的研究结果。| epidemiologists, pathologists |  (暂无明确反义词) | 名词
