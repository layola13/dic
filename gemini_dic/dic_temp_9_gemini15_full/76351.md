gadder:/ˈɡædər/| n.|闲逛的人，四处游荡的人|A person who goes about from place to place, especially for amusement or pleasure; a frivolous person.|例句：He's a real gadder, always flitting from one social event to another. 他是个十足的闲逛者，总是从一个社交活动飞奔到另一个。|例句：The old woman was a notorious gadder, known for her endless rounds of visits.|那位老妇人是个臭名昭著的闲逛者，以她没完没了的拜访而闻名。|近义词：rover, rambler, wanderer, stroller|反义词：homebody, recluse|名词
