seduce:/səˈduːs/ | vt. | 引诱；诱惑；迷惑 | To persuade (someone) to do something that they would not normally do, especially by being very attractive or charming.  | 例句：He was seduced by the promise of easy money. 他被轻松赚钱的承诺所诱惑。

He tried to seduce her with gifts and flattery. 他试图用礼物和奉承来引诱她。


近义词：tempt, entice, allure, charm | 反义词：repulse, deter


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He seduces women with his charm. = 他用他的魅力诱惑女人。
She seduces her audience with her captivating performance. = 她以迷人的表演吸引观众。


一般过去时:
The siren's song seduced the sailors to their doom. = 海妖的歌声引诱水手走向灭亡。
He was seduced by the glittering lights of the city. = 他被城市耀眼的光芒所诱惑。


现在完成时:
He has seduced many women throughout his life. = 他一生中诱惑过许多女人。
The beauty of the landscape has seduced me into staying longer. = 这美丽的风景让我多留了一段时间。


过去完成时:
She had been seduced by false promises before. = 她以前曾被虚假的承诺所诱惑。
They had seduced him into betraying his friends. = 他们诱使他背叛了他的朋友。


将来时:
I fear he will seduce her with his manipulative tactics. = 我担心他会用他的操纵手段来诱惑她。
The marketing campaign aims to seduce consumers into buying the product. = 这场营销活动旨在诱导消费者购买该产品。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't seduced her, she wouldn't have lost everything. = 如果他没有诱惑她，她就不会失去一切。
I wish I hadn't been seduced by his lies. = 我真希望我没有被他的谎言所迷惑。


被动语态:
Many men have been seduced by her beauty. = 许多男人都被她的美貌所吸引。
The audience was seduced by the actor's powerful performance. = 观众被演员精彩的表演所吸引。


疑问句:
Was he seduced into making that decision? = 他是被诱导做出那个决定的吗？
Has she ever been seduced by someone older? = 她曾经被比她大的男人诱惑过吗？


否定句:
He did not seduce her; she willingly agreed. = 他并没有诱惑她；她是自愿同意的。
The advertisement did not seduce me into buying the product. = 这则广告并没有诱使我购买该产品。


PHRASE->

seduce someone into doing something: 诱使某人做某事
be seduced by: 被……所诱惑
seduce away: 诱走；离间
seduce from: 从……诱走
seduce with: 用……诱惑


词源:

From Old French *seduire, from Latin *ducere "to lead".  The prefix "se-" reflects the reflexive sense of leading oneself astray or being led astray.
