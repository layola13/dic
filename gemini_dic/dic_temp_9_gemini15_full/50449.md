depreciatingly:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪŋli/ | adv. |轻蔑地；贬低地；不以为然地 |Expressing disapproval or lack of respect.  It suggests a condescending or scornful tone. |例句：He looked at her depreciatingly, clearly unimpressed by her efforts. 他轻蔑地看着她，显然对她的努力不屑一顾。

例句：She spoke depreciatingly about his achievements, minimizing their significance. 她贬低地谈论他的成就，淡化了它们的意义。


近义词：contemptuously, disdainfully, scornfully, condescendingly

反义词：respectfully, admiringly, approvingly


词性：副词
