haslet:/ˈhæzlɪt/| n. |内脏，杂碎 |The term "haslet" refers to the edible entrails of a pig, typically including the heart, liver, and lungs.  It is often used in traditional dishes.  The exact composition can vary regionally. |Example: The butcher prepared a delicious haslet for the stew.  屠夫为炖菜准备了一份美味的内脏。  The recipe called for a pound of finely chopped haslet.  食谱需要一磅切碎的内脏。|近义词：offal, entrails, pluck |反义词：无 |名词
