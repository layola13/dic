renews:/rɪˈnuːz/| vt.|更新；续订；恢复；使恢复生机 |To make something new or as if new again; to restore to a former state; to extend the validity of something. |例句：The magazine renews its subscription automatically.  杂志自动续订订阅。|例句：The rain renewed the plants. 雨水使植物恢复了生机。|近义词：refresh, renovate, revive, renew|反义词：terminate, cancel, end


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The magazine renews its subscription automatically. = 杂志自动续订订阅。
He renews his commitment to the project every year. = 他每年都重申他对这个项目的承诺。


一般过去时:
The company renewed its contract with the supplier. = 公司与供应商续签了合同。
She renewed her passport before her trip. = 她旅行前更新了护照。


现在完成时:
They have renewed their vows after 20 years of marriage. = 结婚20年后，他们重温了誓言。
We have renewed our efforts to find a solution. = 我们加倍努力寻找解决方案。


过去完成时:
He had renewed his driver's license before the accident. = 事故发生前，他已经更新了驾照。
She had renewed her faith in humanity after volunteering. = 志愿服务后，她对人性恢复了信心。


将来时:
The council will renew the bridge next year. = 市议会明年将修复这座桥。
I will renew my membership at the gym next month. = 我下个月将续交健身房会员费。


虚拟语气:
If they renewed their contract, we could continue the project. = 如果他们续签合同，我们就可以继续这个项目。
I wish he would renew his efforts to improve. = 我希望他能加倍努力改进。


被动语态:
The contract was renewed last week. = 合同在上周续签了。
His passport will be renewed automatically. = 他的护照将自动续签。


疑问句:
Does the gym renew memberships automatically? = 健身房会自动续签会员吗？
Will they renew the contract next year? = 他们明年会续签合同吗？


否定句:
The company did not renew the contract. = 公司没有续签合同。
He refused to renew his membership. = 他拒绝续交会员费。
