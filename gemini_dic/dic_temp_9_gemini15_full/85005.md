harambee:/hɑːrɑːmˈbiː/|/ˈhæræmbiː/| n. |哈拉姆比（肯尼亚的集体劳动和捐助活动）|A Harambee is a Swahili word meaning "pulling together" or "all pull together". It refers to a self-help effort in Kenya where people work together and pool resources to achieve a common goal, often for community development projects.  The spirit of Harambee emphasizes communal effort and collective responsibility.| The villagers organized a harambee to build a new school. 村民们组织了一次哈拉姆比活动来建造一所新学校。|community effort, collective action|individual effort, selfishness|名词
