monopolistically:/ˌmɒnəˈpɒlɪstɪkli/|/ˌmɑːnəˈpɑːlɪstɪkli/| adj. |垄断地；独占地 |Having the characteristics of a monopoly; exhibiting monopolistic practices or tendencies.  The adjective describes situations, markets, or businesses that operate with the characteristics of a monopoly, often involving control over supply, pricing, or other market factors. |The company was accused of behaving monopolistically by setting artificially high prices.  这家公司被指控通过人为抬高价格而从事垄断行为。

The software company's monopolistic practices were challenged in court. 该软件公司的垄断行为在法庭上受到了挑战。


近义词：exclusively, dominantly, restrictively | 反义词：competitively, openly
