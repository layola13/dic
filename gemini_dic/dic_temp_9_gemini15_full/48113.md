declivitous: /dɪˈklɪvɪtəs/ | adj. | 下坡的；倾斜的 |  Steeply sloping downwards.  Characterized by a downward slope. |

例句：

The declivitous path was difficult to climb. = 下坡的路很难爬。

The declivitous terrain made driving challenging. = 倾斜的地形使驾驶具有挑战性。


近义词：sloping, inclined, steep, descending

反义词：ascending, uphill, rising, level


词源:  From Latin *declivis, from de- ("down") + clivus ("slope").
