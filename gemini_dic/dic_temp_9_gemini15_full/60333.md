egoists:/ˈiːɡoɪsts/| n.|利己主义者，自我中心者|A person who is excessively self-centered and self-absorbed; someone who is primarily concerned with their own interests and feelings, often at the expense of others.  |例句：He's become a bit of an egoist since his promotion.  他自从升职后变得有点自私自利了。|例句：Many egoists fail to recognize the needs and feelings of those around them. 许多利己主义者无法认识到周围人的需求和感受。|近义词：narcissist, self-centered person, individualist (in a negative context)|反义词：altruist, selfless person, humanitarian
