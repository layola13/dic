reformulates:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmjuleɪts/| vt.|重新表述；改写；重新制定|To express something in a new or different way; to restructure or reorganize something.|例句：The company reformulated its marketing strategy after a period of decline. 公司在经历一段时间的衰退后重新制定了其营销策略。

例句：The author reformulated his argument in response to the critics' feedback. 作者根据评论家的反馈重新表述了他的论点。


近义词：rephrase, restate, revise, restructure, recast


反义词：maintain, preserve, keep the same


词性：动词
