overchilling: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃɪlɪŋ/ | v. |过度冷却；冷过头|  The act of chilling something to a temperature lower than is necessary or desirable, potentially damaging or spoiling it.  | 例句：Overchilling the ice cream caused it to become icy and grainy. 过度冷冻冰淇淋导致它变得冰冷且颗粒状。

例句：The rapid overchilling of the engine resulted in engine failure.  发动机快速过度冷却导致发动机故障。


近义词：supercooling, excessive chilling, hypercooling

反义词：overheating, warming


词性: 动词
