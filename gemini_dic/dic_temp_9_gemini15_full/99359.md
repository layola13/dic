interdepended:/ˌɪntərˈdɪˈpendənt/| adj.|相互依赖的，互相依赖的|mutually dependent;  characterized by reciprocal dependence.|The success of the project is interdepended on the cooperation of all team members.  这个项目的成功取决于所有团队成员的合作。|近义词：dependent, reliant, interconnected|反义词：independent, autonomous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The economies of these two countries are interdepended. 这两个国家的经济是相互依赖的。
Their success is interdepended on each other's efforts. 他们的成功相互依赖彼此的努力。


一般过去时:
The two companies were interdepended before the merger. 这两家公司在合并之前是相互依赖的。
Their fates were interdepended until the split. 他们的命运在分裂之前是相互依赖的。


现在完成时:
These industries have become increasingly interdepended over the years. 多年来，这些行业变得越来越相互依赖。
The global economy has become highly interdepended since globalization. 全球经济自从全球化以来变得高度相互依赖。


过去完成时:
The two nations had been interdepended for centuries before the conflict. 这两个国家在冲突之前已经相互依赖了几个世纪。
It was clear that their businesses had been interdepended long before the partnership. 很明显，他们的业务在合作之前就已经相互依赖很长时间了。


将来时:
Their future will be interdepended upon the success of the joint venture. 他们的未来将取决于合资企业的成功与否。
The two countries' economies will become even more interdepended in the coming years. 这两个国家的经济将在未来几年变得更加相互依赖。


虚拟语气:
If the two countries were more interdepended, they would cooperate more effectively. 如果这两个国家更加相互依赖，它们将能够更有效地合作。
If their fates had been less interdepended, the outcome might have been different. 如果他们的命运不那么相互依赖，结果可能会有所不同。


被动语态:
The success of the project was seen as being interdepended on the weather conditions. 项目的成功被认为取决于天气状况。
The outcome of the negotiations was interdepended on several factors outside our control. 谈判的结果取决于我们无法控制的几个因素。


疑问句:
Are the two countries' economies interdepended? 这两个国家的经济是相互依赖的吗？
Is the success of this venture interdepended on external factors? 这个项目的成功是否取决于外部因素？


否定句:
The two companies are not interdepended; they operate independently. 这两家公司并不相互依赖；它们独立运作。
Their businesses are not interdepended in any significant way. 他们的业务并没有以任何重要方式相互依赖。
