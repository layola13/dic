over-read:/ˌoʊvərˈriːd/ | vt. | 过度阅读；误读；略读 | To read something too quickly or carelessly, resulting in a misunderstanding or incomplete comprehension.  | 例句：I over-read the instructions and missed a crucial step. 我读指令太快了，错过了一个关键步骤。 | 例句：He over-read the email and thought it was a rejection letter, when it was actually an acceptance. 他匆匆读完邮件，以为是拒绝信，其实它是录取通知书。| 近义词：misread, skim, scan | 反义词：carefully read, thoroughly read | 动词
