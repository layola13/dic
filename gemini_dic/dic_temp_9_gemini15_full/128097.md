nest:/nest/| n. v.|巢穴；窝；安家；筑巢|A nest is a structure built by a bird or other animal to house its eggs and young.  It can also refer to a comfortable and sheltered place.  As a verb, it means to build a nest or to settle comfortably.|例句：The birds built a nest in the tree. 鸟儿在树上筑巢。 The baby birds were safe in their nest.  小鸟们在巢穴里很安全。|近义词：lair, den, haven|反义词：open, exposed|名词，动词
