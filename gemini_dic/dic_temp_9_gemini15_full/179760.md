reestablishment:/ˌriːɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/ | n. |重建，恢复，再建立 |The act of establishing something again; the process of restoring something to its former state.  It implies a previous existence that has been disrupted or destroyed. |例句：The reestablishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries was a significant event.  两国外交关系的重建是一件意义重大的事件。

The reestablishment of the old order proved impossible.  重建旧秩序证明是不可能的。


近义词：restoration, renewal, reinstatement |反义词：destruction, demolition, abolishment |词性：名词
