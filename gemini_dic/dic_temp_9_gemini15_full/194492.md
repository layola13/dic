词源:

* **self-**:  Old English *self*, meaning "self, same."
* **righteous**: Middle English *riȝtwis*, from Old English *rihtwīs*, combining *riht* ("right") and *wīs* ("wise").  Thus, "righteous" originally meant "acting in accordance with what is right."
* **-ness**: Old English *-nes*, a suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
