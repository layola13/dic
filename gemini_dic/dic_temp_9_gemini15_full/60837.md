electrostrictive:/ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈstrɪktɪv/| adj.| 电致伸缩的 | Relating to or exhibiting electrostriction, the change in the dimensions of a dielectric material in response to an applied electric field.  The effect is independent of the polarity of the field.  | Example: Electrostrictive actuators are used in precision positioning systems. 电致伸缩致动器用于精密定位系统。|近义词:piezoelectric (for certain applications)|反义词: N/A
