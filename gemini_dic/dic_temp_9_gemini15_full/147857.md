overrate:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪt/ | vt. |过高估计；评价过高 | To estimate or rate (someone or something) too highly.  |例句：Don't overrate your opponent; they are capable of surprising you.  不要过高估计你的对手；他们有能力让你大吃一惊。|例句：The critics overrate his early work; his later films are far superior. 批评家们过高评价了他的早期作品；他后来的电影要好得多。|近义词：overvalue, exaggerate, overestimate |反义词：underrate, underestimate
