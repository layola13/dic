overinsures:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈʃʊrz/| v.|过度投保|To insure something for an amount greater than its actual value or risk.  This can be done intentionally or unintentionally, and often leads to unnecessary expense.|例句：He overinsured his classic car, paying far more in premiums than necessary. 他为他的老爷车投保过高，支付的保费远高于必要金额。|例句：The company's risk management team warned against overinsuring the new factory. 公司的风险管理团队警告不要为新工厂过度投保。|近义词：overprotect, excessively insure|反义词：underinsure
