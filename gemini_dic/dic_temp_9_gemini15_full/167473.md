prevention: /prɪˈvɛnʃən/ | n. | 预防，阻止 | The act of preventing something from happening; the state of being prevented.  It often involves anticipating potential problems and taking steps to avoid them.  |例句：Disease prevention is crucial for public health.  疾病预防对公共卫生至关重要。

例句：The prevention of accidents requires careful planning and attention to detail.  预防事故需要仔细的计划和对细节的关注。


近义词：avoidance,  protection, safeguard, preclusion | 反义词：causation, provocation
