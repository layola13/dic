hypes:/haɪps/ | v. n. | 大肆宣传；炒作；使兴奋 | To create excessive or unwarranted enthusiasm for something.  |例句：The media hyped the movie's release, leading to long lines at the theater.媒体对这部电影的上映进行了大肆宣传，导致影院排起了长队。
例句：The hype surrounding the new product was immense.围绕这款新产品的大肆宣传非常巨大。|近义词：promote, publicize, build up,  boost |反义词：downplay, understate, ignore |动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hype their products on social media. = 他们在社交媒体上炒作他们的产品。
The company hypes its new technology constantly. = 这家公司不断地炒作其新技术。

一般过去时:
The campaign hyped the candidate's promises. = 该竞选活动夸大了候选人的承诺。
They hyped the concert relentlessly. = 他们没完没了地炒作这场音乐会。

现在完成时:
They have hyped this event for months. = 他们已经炒作这个活动好几个月了。
The media has hyped the celebrity's new relationship. = 媒体炒作了这位名人的新恋情。

过去完成时:
They had hyped the product before its launch. = 他们在产品发布之前就对其进行了炒作。
The company had hyped the features, creating unrealistic expectations. = 公司夸大了产品的特性，造成了不切实际的期望。


将来时:
They will hype the new game before its release. = 他们将在新游戏发布前进行炒作。
The marketing team will hype the product launch extensively. = 市场营销团队将大力宣传产品的发布。


被动语态:
The product was hyped by the company. = 该产品被公司炒作了。
The concert was hyped to unrealistic levels. = 音乐会宣传到了不切实际的程度。


疑问句:
Are they hyping the new movie too much? = 他们是不是对这部新电影的宣传过度了？
Is the company hyping its product's capabilities? = 公司是否夸大了其产品的性能？


否定句:
They didn't hype the product enough. = 他们对产品的宣传不够。
The company didn't hype the event as much as expected. = 公司对活动的宣传没有预期的那么大。
