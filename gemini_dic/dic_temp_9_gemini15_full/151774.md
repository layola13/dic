pasquiler:/pæsˈkwɪlər/| n. |讽刺诗作者；贴匿名诽谤小纸条的人|A pasquiler is a writer or publisher of pasquils, which are short, satirical, often anonymous writings or drawings that criticize or ridicule someone or something.  They are typically posted in public places. |Example:  The anonymous pasquiler left their scathing critique of the mayor's policies plastered on lampposts throughout the city.  贴在城市路灯上的匿名讽刺诗作者留下了对市长政策的尖刻批评。|近义词：satirist, lampooner, libeler |反义词：praiser, flatterer |名词
