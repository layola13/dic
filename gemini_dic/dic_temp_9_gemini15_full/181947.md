remarketing: /ˌriːˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 再营销，再行销 | The process of marketing to people who have previously interacted with your business.  It involves showing ads to people who have already shown some interest in your products or services.  This is often done through targeted advertising on platforms like Google or social media. |  Example:  Our remarketing campaign significantly increased brand awareness and sales.  我们的再营销活动显著提升了品牌知名度和销售额。 | 近义词：retargeting,  re-engagement marketing | 反义词：first-time marketing, initial marketing
