excursion:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/ | n. |远足；短途旅行；游览 | A short journey or trip, especially one taken for pleasure.  |例句：We took a day excursion to the coast. 我们去海岸一日游。

例句：The school organized an excursion to the museum. 学校组织了一次去博物馆的郊游。


近义词：outing, trip, jaunt, expedition | 反义词：stay, residence


词源:  From Middle French *excursion, from Latin *excurrere "to run out," from ex- "out" + currere "to run."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The family takes an excursion every summer. = 这个家庭每年夏天都去远足。
He often takes excursions to the countryside. = 他经常去乡下远足。


一般过去时:
They took an excursion to the mountains last weekend. = 他们上周末去山里远足了。
We took a memorable excursion to the lake. = 我们进行了一次令人难忘的湖边远足。


现在完成时:
We have taken many excursions this year. = 今年我们已经进行了许多次远足。
She has never taken an excursion abroad. = 她从未出国远足过。


过去完成时:
They had taken several excursions before they decided on their favorite place. = 在他们决定自己最喜欢的地方之前，他们已经进行了几次远足。
Before the accident, he had taken many dangerous excursions. = 事故发生前，他进行过许多危险的远足。


将来时:
We will take an excursion to the beach next month. = 我们下个月将去海滩远足。
They will plan an excursion to celebrate their anniversary. = 他们将计划一次远足来庆祝他们的周年纪念日。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would take an excursion to Europe. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去欧洲远足。
I wish I could take an excursion to the Amazon rainforest. = 我希望我能去亚马逊雨林远足。


被动语态:
The excursion was organized by the school. = 这趟远足是由学校组织的。
The excursion was cancelled due to bad weather. = 由于天气不好，远足被取消了。


疑问句:

否定句:
