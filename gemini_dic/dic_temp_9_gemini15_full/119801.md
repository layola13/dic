migrators:/maɪˈɡreɪtərz/| n. |迁徙者，移居者|People or animals that migrate.  Those who move from one place to another, especially seasonally. |例句：The bird migrators returned to their nesting grounds in the spring.  春回大地，候鸟迁徙者回到了它们的筑巢地。

例句：Millions of migrators flock to the coast during the summer months. 夏季数百万迁徙者涌向海岸。


近义词：migrants, travelers, wanderers, nomads


反义词：sedentaries, residents, locals


词性:名词
