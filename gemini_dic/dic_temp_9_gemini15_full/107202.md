lagune:/ləˈɡuːn/| n. |泻湖；环礁湖 |A lagoon is a shallow body of water separated from a larger body of water by a narrow strip of land, such as a reef or sandbar.  Lagoons are often found along coastlines and are typically calmer than the open ocean. |例句：The calm lagoon was perfect for swimming.  宁静的泻湖非常适合游泳。|例句：The tropical island was surrounded by a beautiful lagoon.  这个热带岛屿被美丽的泻湖环绕着。|近义词：bay, cove, inlet, sound |反义词：ocean, sea
