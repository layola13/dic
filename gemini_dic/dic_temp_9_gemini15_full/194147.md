self-inspections:/ˌsɛlf ɪnˈspɛkʃənz/ | n. | 自我检查；自检 |  The act of conducting inspections on oneself or one's own work, property, or organization.  This often involves a systematic review of processes, procedures, or equipment to identify potential problems or areas for improvement.  | 例句：The company implemented a program of regular self-inspections to ensure compliance with safety regulations.  这家公司实施了定期自我检查计划，以确保遵守安全条例。

The factory workers conduct weekly self-inspections of their machinery. 工厂工人每周对他们的机器进行自我检查。


近义词：self-examination, self-assessment, internal audit | 反义词：external audit, inspection by others | 词性：名词
