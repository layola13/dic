proliferations:/ˌprɒlɪfəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. |增殖；激增；扩散 |The rapid proliferation of new technologies is transforming our lives. 新技术的快速发展正在改变我们的生活。|近义词：increase, growth, expansion |反义词：decline, decrease, reduction

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bacteria proliferate rapidly in warm conditions. = 细菌在温暖的环境中迅速繁殖。
New businesses are proliferating in the city center. = 市中心涌现出许多新企业。

一般过去时:
The weeds proliferated unchecked last summer. = 杂草去年夏天不受控制地疯长。
Rumors proliferated throughout the town. = 谣言在整个城镇蔓延开来。

现在完成时:
The use of social media has proliferated in recent years. = 近年来社交媒体的使用已经普及。
The company's products have proliferated across the globe. = 该公司的产品已遍布全球。

过去完成时:
By the time the disease was discovered, it had already proliferated widely. = 当发现这种疾病时，它已经广泛传播了。
Before the internet, information had not proliferated as quickly. = 在互联网出现之前，信息的传播速度没有这么快。

将来时:
Experts predict that the use of artificial intelligence will proliferate in the coming decade. = 专家预测，人工智能的使用将在未来十年迅速普及。
We anticipate that online shopping will proliferate further. = 我们预计网上购物将会进一步普及。

虚拟语气:
If the virus were allowed to proliferate unchecked, the consequences could be devastating. = 如果病毒不受控制地蔓延，后果可能是灾难性的。
I wish these invasive species wouldn't proliferate so quickly. = 我希望这些入侵物种不会如此迅速地蔓延。

被动语态:
The disease was allowed to proliferate unchecked. = 这种疾病被允许不受控制地蔓延。
The new ideas were quickly proliferated throughout the organization. = 新想法迅速在整个组织中传播。

疑问句:
How can we prevent the proliferation of harmful bacteria? = 我们如何才能防止有害细菌的繁殖？
What factors contribute to the proliferation of misinformation? = 哪些因素导致虚假信息的传播？

否定句:
Efforts to control the proliferation of weapons were unsuccessful. = 控制武器扩散的努力没有成功。
The government tried to prevent the proliferation of fake news. = 政府试图阻止假新闻的传播。
