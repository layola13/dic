pats:/pæts/| n. v.|轻拍；抚摸；少量|A gentle stroke or tap.|例句：She pats the dog on the head. 她轻轻拍了拍狗的头。|近义词：taps, strokes, pats|反义词：punches, hits, slaps|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She pats the cat every day. = 她每天都轻拍猫咪。
He pats his pockets to check for his keys. = 他拍拍口袋检查钥匙。

一般过去时:
I patted my friend on the back. = 我拍了拍朋友的背。
She patted the dough gently. = 她轻轻地拍了拍面团。


现在完成时:
He has patted the dog many times. = 他已经拍过很多次狗了。
She has patted her hair into place. = 她已经把头发拍理整齐了。


过去完成时:
I had patted the horse before mounting it. = 我在骑上马之前拍了拍它。
They had patted the child on his head to comfort him. = 他们拍了拍孩子的头安慰他。


将来时:
I will pat the dog when I see it. = 我看到狗的时候会拍拍它。
She will pat the dough until it is smooth. = 她会一直拍面团直到它变光滑。


虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would pat myself. = 如果我是一只猫，我会自己拍拍自己。
I wish I had patted the dog before it ran away. = 我真希望它跑掉之前我拍拍它。


被动语态:
The dog was patted by the child. = 狗被孩子拍打着。
The dough is patted until it is flat. = 面团被拍打直到变平。


疑问句:
Did you pat the dog? = 你拍狗了吗？
Will you pat the child? = 你会拍拍孩子吗？


否定句:
I did not pat the dog. = 我没有拍狗。
She will not pat the dough again. = 她不会再拍面团了。



PHRASE->
pat on the back:  = 赞扬；鼓励
pat down: = 搜身；摸索
pat oneself on the back: = 自我表扬
give sb a pat on the back: = 给某人鼓励
a pat on the head: = 轻拍头部
