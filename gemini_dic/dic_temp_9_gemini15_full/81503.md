gravestone:/ˈɡreɪvstoʊn/| n. |墓碑|A gravestone is a stone or other material placed on a grave, often inscribed with the deceased person's name and dates of birth and death.  It serves as a marker and a memorial.|例句：The old gravestone was covered in moss.  这块古老的墓碑上长满了苔藓。|例句：We placed flowers on her gravestone. 我们在她墓碑上放了鲜花。|近义词：headstone, tombstone, memorial stone|反义词：无
