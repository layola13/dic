marts:/mɑːrts/| n.|市场，集市|A mart is a market, especially a large retail store.|例句：The farmer's mart was bustling with activity.  农贸市场熙熙攘攘，热闹非凡。|例句：The new shopping mart offers a wide variety of goods. 新建的购物中心提供各种各样的商品。|近义词：market, supermarket, bazaar|反义词：None
