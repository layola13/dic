slitlike:/ˈslɪtlaɪk/| adj.|狭缝状的，裂缝似的|Resembling a slit; long, narrow, and thin.  It often describes something that is very thin and elongated, like a slit or crack.|例句：The eyes of the snake were slitlike and menacing.  蛇的眼睛又细又长，令人恐惧。

The wound was slitlike and deep. 伤口狭长而深。|近义词：fissured, creviced, narrow, elongated |反义词：wide, broad, spacious
