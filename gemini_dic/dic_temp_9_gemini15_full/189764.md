salubrious:/səˈluːbriəs/ | adj. | 有益健康的；清爽宜人的 | Promoting health or well-being.  A salubrious environment or lifestyle is conducive to good health. | 例句：The air in the mountains is remarkably salubrious.  山区里的空气非常清爽宜人。

例句：Regular exercise and a healthy diet contribute to a salubrious lifestyle.  规律的锻炼和健康的饮食有助于健康的生活方式。


近义词：healthy, wholesome, beneficial, healthful, invigorating

反义词：unhealthy, noxious, harmful, deleterious


词源:  From Latin *salubris "healthful," from salus "health," related to salvus "safe," from Proto-Indo-European *sol- "whole, safe, sound."
