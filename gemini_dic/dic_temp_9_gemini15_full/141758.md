orangutan:/ɔːˈræŋʊtæn/| n. |猩猩|A large reddish-brown ape with long arms, found in the rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra.|例句：The orangutan swung effortlessly through the trees.  这只猩猩轻松自如地荡过树丛。|例句：Conservation efforts are crucial for protecting the endangered orangutan population. 保护工作对于保护濒危的猩猩种群至关重要。|近义词：ape, primate|反义词：None
