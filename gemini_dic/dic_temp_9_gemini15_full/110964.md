liquefy:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使液化；液化 | To make (something) liquid.  The process of changing a solid or gas into a liquid state. |例句：Heat will liquefy many solids.  = 热量会使许多固体液化。

The high pressure liquefied the gas. = 高压使气体液化。


近义词：melt, fuse, dissolve | 反义词：solidify, freeze, harden | 词性：动词
