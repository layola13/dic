pleasure:/ˈplɛʒər/| n.|快乐；愉快；乐事|The pleasure is all mine.  这是我的荣幸。| enjoyment, delight, satisfaction | displeasure, unhappiness, misery|名词


英文解析：
A feeling of happiness, enjoyment, or satisfaction.  It can refer to a state of being, an activity, or something that causes happiness.

例句：

It was a pleasure meeting you.  见到你很高兴。

She takes pleasure in gardening. 她以园艺为乐。


近义词：
enjoyment, delight, gratification, satisfaction, fun


反义词：
displeasure, unhappiness, misery, pain, suffering


词性：名词
