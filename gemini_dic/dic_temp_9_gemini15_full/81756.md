greets:/ɡriːts/| vt.|问候；迎接；致意|To express greetings to someone; to welcome someone.  |例句：She greeted her guests warmly. 她热情地迎接她的客人。|例句：He greeted me with a smile. 他笑着向我打招呼。|近义词：salute, welcome, hail|反义词：ignore, snub


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He greets his friends every morning. 他每天早上都向他的朋友们打招呼。
She greets her customers with a smile. 她面带笑容地迎接顾客。


一般过去时:
He greeted his teacher politely. 他礼貌地向老师问好。
She greeted me at the door. 她在门口迎接我。


现在完成时:
He has greeted all the guests. 他已经向所有宾客问好。
She has greeted the new employee. 她已经向新员工表示欢迎。


过去完成时:
He had greeted the boss before the meeting. 他在会议开始前已经向老板问好。
She had greeted her family before leaving. 她离开之前已经向家人问好。


将来时:
He will greet the speaker at the airport. 他将在机场迎接演讲者。
She will greet her parents when they arrive. 他们到达时，她将迎接她的父母。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would greet him. 如果我看到他，我会向他打招呼。
I wish I had greeted her earlier. 我真希望我早点向她打招呼。


被动语态:
The visitors were greeted warmly by the host. 访客受到了主人的热情接待。
The new employees are greeted by the manager. 新员工受到了经理的欢迎。


疑问句:
Did you greet your neighbours? 你向你的邻居们打招呼了吗？
Will you greet the VIP guests personally? 你会亲自迎接贵宾吗？


否定句:
He didn't greet anyone at the party. 他在聚会上没有向任何人打招呼。
She didn't greet me when I arrived. 我到达时她没有向我打招呼。
