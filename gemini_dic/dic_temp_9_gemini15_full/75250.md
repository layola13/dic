from scratch: /frɒm skrætʃ/ | adj. | 从零开始；白手起家 |  Starting from the beginning, without using anything that already exists.  | 例句：She built her business from scratch. = 她白手起家创建了自己的公司。

例句：He learned to code from scratch. = 他从零开始学习编程。


近义词：from the ground up, anew, ab initio

反义词：using existing resources, pre-made


词性：形容词 (作状语)
