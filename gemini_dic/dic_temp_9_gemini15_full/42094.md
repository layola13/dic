cotransferring: /ˌkoʊˈtrænsfɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 共同转移；协同转移 |  The act of transferring something jointly or simultaneously. This term is often used in scientific contexts, particularly in genetics and molecular biology, to describe the simultaneous transfer of multiple genetic elements or molecules.  | Example: The researchers observed cotransferring of antibiotic resistance genes and virulence factors in the bacterial population.  研究人员观察到细菌种群中抗生素抗性基因和毒力因子的共同转移。|近义词: joint transfer, simultaneous transfer, co-transmission | 反义词: independent transfer, sequential transfer | 动词
