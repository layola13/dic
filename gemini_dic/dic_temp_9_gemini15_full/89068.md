holosiderite:/ˌhoʊloʊˈsaɪdəraɪt/| n. |全铁陨石|A type of stony-iron meteorite composed almost entirely of nickel-iron and containing only minor amounts of silicate minerals.  They are relatively rare compared to other meteorite types.  They are believed to originate from the core-mantle boundary of differentiated asteroids.|This holosiderite was found in the Atacama Desert and is currently being studied by scientists.|近义词：stony-iron meteorite (a broader category)|反义词：无
