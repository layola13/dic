readies:/ˈrediz/ | v. |准备好，使准备好 |To make or become ready; prepare.  The verb "ready" is often used with an object, indicating what is being prepared. It can also be used reflexively, meaning to prepare oneself. |例句：He readies the boat for the race. 他为比赛准备船只。

She readies herself for the presentation. 她为演讲做准备。|近义词：prepare, get ready, make ready |反义词：delay, postpone


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He readies his tools every morning. = 他每天早上都准备好他的工具。
She readies the children for school. = 她为孩子们上学做准备。

一般过去时:
They readied the house for the party. = 他们为派对准备了房子。
He readied himself for a long journey. = 他为长途旅行做好了准备。

现在完成时:
She has readied the documents for the meeting. = 她已经为会议准备好了文件。
They have readied themselves for the challenge. = 他们已经为挑战做好了准备。

过去完成时:
He had readied the car before his wife came. = 他在他妻子来之前已经把车准备好了。
The team had readied their strategies before the game. = 球队在比赛前已经准备好了他们的策略。

将来时:
We will ready the equipment tomorrow. = 我们明天将准备好设备。
They will ready themselves for the interview. = 他们将为面试做好准备。

虚拟语气:
If I had readied the materials earlier, I would have finished on time. = 如果我早点准备好材料，我就能按时完成了。
I wish I had readied myself better for the exam. = 我真希望我为考试做了更好的准备。

被动语态:
The room was readied for the guests. = 房间已为客人准备好。
The documents are being readied for publication. = 文件正在准备出版。

疑问句:
Are you readied for the test? = 你准备好考试了吗？
Has the equipment been readied for use? = 设备准备好可以使用了吗？

否定句:
He didn't ready the car in time. = 他没有及时准备好车。
They haven't readied the presentation yet. = 他们还没准备好演示文稿。
