reassurances:/ˌriːəˈʃʊərənsɪz/| n. |保证；安心；慰藉 |The act of reassuring someone; something that reassures.  It refers to words or actions that are intended to alleviate fear or doubt. |例句：Her reassurances calmed his fears.  她的保证平息了他的恐惧。

例句：The doctor's reassurances about her condition helped her relax. 医生对她病情做出的保证帮助她放松了下来。


近义词：comfort, consolation, encouragement, assurance, confidence


反义词：threats, warnings, anxieties


词性:名词
