complaisance:/kəmˈpleɪzəns/ | n. | 顺从；讨好；彬彬有礼 | Willingness to please others; obliging behavior; complaisant manner.  It often implies a yielding disposition, sometimes to an excessive degree. |

例句：

Her complaisance made her a popular guest.  她的顺从使她成为受欢迎的客人。

His complaisance towards his boss was evident in his eagerness to please. 他对老板的顺从在他急于讨好的行为中显而易见。


近义词：obsequiousness, compliance, agreeableness, civility, affability

反义词：defiance, disobedience, resistance, hostility, intransigence


词源:

The word "complaisance" comes from the Old French word "complaisance," which itself derives from the verb "complaire," meaning "to please" or "to gratify."  The prefix "com-" indicates "together" or "with," while "plaire" is related to the Latin word "placere," meaning "to please."
