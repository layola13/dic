glomerule:/ˈɡlɒmərjuːl/| n. |肾小球；小球|A glomerule is a cluster of capillaries forming part of a nephron in the kidney.  It filters blood to produce urine.  The glomerulus is essential for the function of the kidneys. |Example: The glomeruli in the kidneys filter waste products from the blood.  肾脏中的肾小球过滤血液中的废物。|近义词：renal corpuscle|反义词：N/A
