introductory:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌktəri/| adj. |介绍性的，初步的 |serving to introduce; preliminary.  It is used to describe something that serves as an introduction or is of a preliminary nature. |例句：The introductory chapter gave a brief overview of the book's main themes.  介绍性章节简要概述了本书的主要主题。|例句：She gave an introductory speech before the main presentation.  她在主要演示之前做了一个介绍性演讲。|近义词：preliminary, initial, prefatory |反义词：concluding, final
