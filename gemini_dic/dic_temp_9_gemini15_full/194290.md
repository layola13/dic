self-oppressor: /ˌsɛlf əˈprɛsər/ | n. |自我压迫者，自我压抑者 | A person who actively suppresses or hinders their own progress, potential, or well-being, often unconsciously.  This can manifest in various ways, including self-sabotage, negative self-talk, and avoidance of opportunities. |  He was a self-oppressor, constantly undermining his own success through procrastination and self-doubt. 他是一个自我压迫者，总是通过拖延和自我怀疑来破坏自己的成功。|近义词：self-saboteur, self-limiter, self-underminer | 反义词：self-actualizer, self-empowerer |
