remunerability:/ˌriˈmjuːnəreɪbɪləti/| n. |报酬能力；可报酬性 |The ability to be remunerated; the state of being capable of receiving payment or reward.  This term often refers to the potential for a job, position, or task to offer financial compensation.  |例句：The remunerability of this position is dependent upon successful completion of the project. 这份工作的报酬取决于项目的成功完成。

The remunerability of freelance work can be unpredictable.  自由职业工作的报酬可能难以预测。 |近义词：compensability, profitability, rewardability |反义词：unprofitability, unrewardingness
