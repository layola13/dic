punching:/ˈpʌntʃɪŋ/ | v. | 打孔；猛击；冲压 |  To strike forcefully with a fist or other object.  It can also refer to the act of making holes in a material using a machine or tool. |例句：He punched the wall in anger. 他生气地打了墙一拳。|例句：The factory uses machines for punching holes in metal sheets. 工厂使用机器在金属板上冲孔。|近义词：hitting, striking, hammering, stamping |反义词： caressing, stroking


USAGE->

She punches in at 8 a.m. every day. = 她每天早上8点打卡上班。
