mahogany:/məˈhɒɡəni/ | /məˈhɑːɡəni/ | n. adj. |红木；深红褐色 |A reddish-brown hardwood from tropical trees, prized for its beauty and durability.  It is also used to describe a deep reddish-brown color.|例句：The table is made of solid mahogany. 这张桌子是用实木红木做的。

The rich mahogany finish complemented the room's decor.  深红色的红木饰面与房间的装饰相得益彰。|近义词：reddish-brown, rosewood, walnut |反义词：pale, light-colored
