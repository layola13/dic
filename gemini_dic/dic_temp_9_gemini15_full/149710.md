palaeography:/ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfi/|/n.|古文字学|The study of ancient and historical handwriting.  Palaeography involves analyzing the physical characteristics of writing, such as script, handwriting style, and materials used, to understand the historical context and evolution of writing systems. |The palaeographer carefully examined the faded manuscript, deciphering the archaic script.  古文字学家仔细检查了褪色的手稿，破译了古老的文字。|epigraphy, diplomatics|modern calligraphy|
