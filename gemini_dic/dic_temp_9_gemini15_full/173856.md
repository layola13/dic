quasi-provocative: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ prəˈvɒkətɪv/ | adj. | 貌似挑衅的，似是而非地挑衅的 | Seemingly or slightly provocative; tending to excite or stimulate, but in a subtle or indirect way.  It suggests a provocation that is not fully overt or direct. |  例句：His comments, while seemingly innocuous, had a quasi-provocative undertone.  他的评论看似无害，却带有一种似是而非的挑衅意味。


例句：The artist's installation, though not explicitly political, possessed a quasi-provocative quality.  这位艺术家的装置作品，虽然并非明确表达政治立场，却带有一种貌似挑衅的意味。


近义词：slightly provocative, subtly challenging, mildly inflammatory, insinuating


反义词：unprovocative, conciliatory, appeasing, inoffensive


词性：形容词
