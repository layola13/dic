mismatch:/ˈmɪsmætʃ/ | n. |不匹配，不协调 | A mismatch is a situation where two or more things are not suitable for each other.  It can refer to people, objects, or ideas that don't fit together well. | Example: There's a mismatch between his skills and the job requirements.他的技能和工作要求不匹配。  The mismatch of personalities led to conflict. 性格不合导致了冲突。|近义词：inconsistency, discrepancy, incompatibility |反义词：match, compatibility, congruity
