peele:/piːl/| vt.  |剥皮；剥落；揭露；|to remove the skin or outer covering of something, or to come off in this way |He peeled the orange. 他剥了橘子。|He peeled the paint off the wall. 他把墙上的油漆剥掉了。|近义词：pare, skin, strip |反义词：attach, stick


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peels potatoes every evening. 他每天晚上都削土豆皮。
She peels the stickers off her new laptop. 她把新笔记本电脑上的贴纸撕掉了。


一般过去时:
I peeled the banana before eating it. 我吃香蕉前先剥了皮。
They peeled the onions for the soup. 他们为做汤剥了洋葱。


现在完成时:
She has peeled all the carrots. 她已经把所有的胡萝卜都削皮了。
He has peeled the potatoes for the stew. 他已经为炖菜削好了土豆皮。


过去完成时:
I had peeled the apples before the guests arrived. 客人到之前我已经削好了苹果皮。
They had peeled the garlic before starting to cook. 他们开始做饭之前已经剥好了蒜。


将来时:
She will peel the apples for the pie tomorrow. 她明天会为派削苹果皮。
He will peel the oranges for the juice. 他会为果汁剥橘子。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would peel all the potatoes. 如果我有时间，我会把所有的土豆都削皮。
I wish I had peeled the carrots earlier. 我真希望我早点把胡萝卜削皮。


被动语态:
The potatoes were peeled by the chef. 土豆是厨师削的皮。
The oranges were peeled before being juiced. 橘子在榨汁之前已经剥皮了。


疑问句:
Have you peeled the potatoes yet? 你削土豆皮了吗？
Did she peel the bananas this morning? 她今天早上削香蕉皮了吗？


否定句:
He didn't peel the apples. 他没有削苹果皮。
She hasn't peeled the garlic yet. 她还没剥蒜呢。


PHRASE->
peel off: 剥落；脱落
peel away: 逐渐消失；剥去
peel back: 揭开；揭露
peel something from something: 从...剥离...
peel and stick: (指壁纸等) 即撕即贴
