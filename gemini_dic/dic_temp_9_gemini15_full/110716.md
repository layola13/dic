linemen:/ˈlaɪnmən/| n.|架线工人，电力线修理工|Workers who install, maintain, and repair power lines. They often work outdoors in various weather conditions and require specialized skills and training.|例句：The linemen worked tirelessly to restore power after the storm.  架线工人们在暴风雨后不懈努力地恢复电力供应。|例句：A team of linemen climbed the poles to fix the damaged wires. 一队架线工人爬上电线杆修理损坏的电线。|近义词：power line worker, electrical lineman, cable splicer|反义词：无
