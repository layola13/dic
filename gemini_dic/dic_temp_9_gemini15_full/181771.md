religionists: /rɪˈlɪdʒənɪsts/ | n. | 信奉宗教者，宗教人士 | People who practice a religion, often implying a strong adherence to religious beliefs and practices.  The term sometimes carries a connotation of being particularly devout or zealous in their faith. |  The religionists gathered for a week-long prayer retreat.  宗教人士聚集在一起参加为期一周的祈祷静修。

Many religionists believe in the power of prayer. 许多宗教人士相信祈祷的力量。

|近义词: believers, worshipers, devotees, the faithful |反义词: atheists, agnostics, secularists |名词
