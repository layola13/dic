overridden:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdn/| v.|推翻；使无效；压制；凌驾于|To override something means to take control over it and prevent it from having its usual effect.  It often implies a stronger force or authority superseding a weaker one. |The new law overridden the previous regulations.|近义词：supersede, annul, negate, invalidate |反义词：uphold, confirm, ratify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system overrides the default settings. = 系统会覆盖默认设置。
He overrides his assistant's suggestions. = 他否决了助理的建议。

一般过去时:
The judge overridden the jury's verdict. = 法官推翻了陪审团的裁决。
The emergency brake overridden the accelerator. = 紧急刹车系统压制了油门。

现在完成时:
The new software has overridden the old version. = 新软件已经覆盖了旧版本。
The government has overridden the objections. = 政府已经无视了反对意见。

过去完成时:
They had overridden the security protocols before accessing the data. = 他们在访问数据之前已经绕过了安全协议。
The storm had overridden the city's defenses. = 暴风雨已经摧毁了城市的防御工事。

将来时:
The automatic system will override manual controls if necessary. = 必要时，自动系统将覆盖手动控制。
The CEO will override the marketing team's decision. = 首席执行官将否决营销团队的决定。

虚拟语气:
If the safety system hadn't overridden the power, there would have been an explosion. = 如果安全系统没有切断电源，就会发生爆炸。
I wish the committee hadn't overridden my proposal. = 我真希望委员会没有否决我的提案。

被动语态:
The decision was overridden by the board of directors. = 该决定被董事会否决了。
The security settings were overridden by a malicious program. = 安全设置被恶意程序覆盖了。

疑问句:
Was the previous agreement overridden? = 先前的协议被推翻了吗？
Can the parental controls be overridden? = 家长控制可以被绕过吗？

否定句:
The software did not override the existing configurations. = 该软件没有覆盖现有的配置。
The manager didn't override the employee's request. = 经理没有否决员工的请求。


PHRASE->
