kaleidoscopes:/kəˈlaɪdəskoʊps/| n. |万花筒；快速变化的景象|A kaleidoscope is a toy that produces changing patterns of color.  It can also refer to a rapidly changing or diverse range of things. |例句：The child was mesmerized by the kaleidoscope. 孩子被万花筒迷住了。|例句：The city at night was a kaleidoscope of lights and sounds. 夜晚的城市是灯光和声音的万花筒。|近义词：panorama, mosaic, medley|反义词：monotony, uniformity|名词
