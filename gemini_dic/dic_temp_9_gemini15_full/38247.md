computers:/kəmˈpjuːtərz/ | n. |计算机，电脑 |Electronic devices that can be programmed to perform a variety of tasks, including calculations, data processing, and information storage and retrieval.  They are ubiquitous in modern society, used in everything from personal communication to complex scientific research. |This company uses powerful computers for its simulations. 这家公司使用强大的电脑进行模拟。|  Calculators, mainframes, laptops, servers |Manual calculators, abacuses | noun
