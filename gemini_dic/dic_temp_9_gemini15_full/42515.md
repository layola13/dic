counterinvasion:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈveɪʒən/ | n. | 反入侵，反攻 | A military action taken to repel an invasion.  It involves attacking the invading force within the invaded territory.  The term implies a defensive response that becomes an offensive operation. | Example: The counterinvasion successfully pushed back the enemy forces, reclaiming lost territory.  反入侵成功地击退了敌军，收复了失地。 | 近义词：counterattack, offensive, reprisal | 反义词：invasion, occupation
