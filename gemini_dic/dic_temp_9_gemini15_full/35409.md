co-option:/ˌkoʊˈɑːpʃən/| v. | 挪用；采用；吸收；篡夺；|The act of taking something for one's own use, often without permission or acknowledgment; the adoption or appropriation of something for one's own purposes, often with a change in meaning or function.  |例句：The opposition party attempted to co-opt the protesters' message.  反对党试图挪用抗议者的信息。|例句：The company co-opted elements of its competitor's successful marketing campaign. 这家公司采用了其竞争对手成功的营销活动中的某些元素。|近义词：appropriation, adoption, usurpation, exploitation |反义词：rejection, relinquishment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They co-opt new ideas into their existing strategies. = 他们将新的理念融入到他们现有的策略中。
The company co-opts trends to boost sales. = 公司利用潮流来促进销售。


一般过去时:
The government co-opted the revolutionary movement. = 政府吸收了革命运动。
She co-opted his ideas for her own project. = 她把他的想法用于自己的项目。


现在完成时:
They have co-opted several promising young scientists. = 他们已经吸收了几位有前途的年轻科学家。
The corporation has co-opted the language of social justice. = 该公司已经挪用了社会正义的语言。


过去完成时:
The regime had co-opted the opposition before the election. = 该政权在选举前已经收买了反对派。
He had co-opted her research findings without acknowledging her. = 他使用了她的研究成果而没有表示感谢。


将来时:
They will co-opt local leaders into the project. = 他们将吸收当地领导人参与该项目。
The company will co-opt new technologies to improve efficiency. = 公司将采用新技术以提高效率。


虚拟语气:
If they co-opted more diverse voices, the report would be stronger. = 如果他们吸收更多不同的声音，报告会更有力。
I wish they hadn't co-opted our ideas without permission. = 我真希望他们没有未经许可就挪用我们的想法。


被动语态:
The original meaning of the word was co-opted by the new generation. = 这个词的原始含义被新一代人挪用了。
His ideas were co-opted by the mainstream media. = 他的想法被主流媒体采用。


疑问句:
Did they co-opt the design from a competitor? = 他们的设计是从竞争对手那里挪用来的吗？
Will the company co-opt any external expertise? = 公司会吸收任何外部专业知识吗？


否定句:
They did not co-opt any of the protesters' demands. = 他们没有采纳抗议者的任何要求。
The organization will not co-opt any unethical practices. = 该组织不会采用任何不道德的做法。


PHRASE->
co-opt into = 吸纳进…
co-opt for =  为了…而采用
co-opt from = 从…挪用
co-opt by = 通过…来吸收
co-opt with = 与…合作来采用
