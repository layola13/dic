The term often implies a set of instructions or guidelines. | The rubrical notes in the prayer book explained the rituals.  祈祷书中的注释说明了仪式。The manuscript contained rubrical headings in red ink.  手稿中包含用红墨水书写的标题。 | 近义词：explanatory, instructive, directive | 反义词：unmarked, unexplained
