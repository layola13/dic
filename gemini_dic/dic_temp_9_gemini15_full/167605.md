prewrap:/ˈpriːræp/| n.|预包装|Prewrap refers to the process of wrapping something beforehand, typically for protection or preservation.  It also refers to the material used for this type of wrapping.|Example: The prewrap protected the delicate glassware during shipping.  预包装保护了易碎的玻璃器皿在运输过程中。The bakery uses prewrap to keep the bread fresh. 面包店使用预包装来保持面包的新鲜。|近义词：packaging, wrapping, covering|反义词：unwrapping, exposing
