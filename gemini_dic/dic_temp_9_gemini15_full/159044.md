pneumatometry:/njuːməˈtɒmɪtri/ | n. |肺活量测定法；气量测定法 |The measurement of air capacity, especially of the lungs.  Pneumatometry is used to assess lung function and diagnose respiratory disorders. |Example: The doctor ordered a pneumatometry test to evaluate the patient's lung capacity.  医生下令进行肺活量测定以评估患者的肺活量。|近义词：spirometry (for lung function)|反义词：无
