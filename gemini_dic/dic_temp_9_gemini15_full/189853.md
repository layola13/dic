sambaing:/sæmˈbeɪɪŋ/| v.|桑巴舞，跳桑巴舞|The act of performing the samba dance.  It describes the process of dancing the samba.|例句：She was sambaing with incredible energy and grace. 她充满活力和优雅地跳着桑巴舞。

例句：The dancers were sambaing to the rhythmic beat of the drums. 舞者随着鼓的节奏跳着桑巴舞。


近义词：dancing, performing (a samba)

反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with activities that are not dancing)

词性:动词
