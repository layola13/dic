doors:/dɔrz/| n. |门；途径；(尤指)大门 |  A door is an opening in a wall or other structure that allows passage from one place to another.  Doors can be made of various materials and have different designs.  They often have a handle or knob for opening and closing. | 例句：The front door was painted red. 前门漆成了红色。

The doors of opportunity were open to him. 机会的大门向他敞开。


近义词：entrance, gateway, opening | 反义词：wall
