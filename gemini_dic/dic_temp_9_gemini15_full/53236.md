directorate:/dɪˈrektərət/| n.|总局；理事会；指导部门|A directorate is a body of directors or a group of people who manage or supervise a particular area or activity, often within a larger organization.  It can also refer to the office or department headed by such a body.|例句：The finance directorate oversees the budget for the entire company. 财务总局负责整个公司的预算。|例句：The education directorate is responsible for implementing new curriculum reforms. 教育部门负责实施新的课程改革。|近义词：department, administration, authority, board|反义词：subordinate, individual
