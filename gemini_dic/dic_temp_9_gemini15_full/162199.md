pre-owned:/ˌpriːˈoʊnd/| adj.| 二手的，用过的 |Previously owned; not new.  This term is often used in the context of selling used goods, particularly high-value items like cars or jewelry, to imply a higher quality or better condition than simply "used".| 例句：I'm looking for a pre-owned car in good condition. = 我正在寻找一辆状况良好的二手车。

例句：She bought a pre-owned diamond necklace at a fraction of the original price. = 她以原价的一小部分买了一条二手的钻石项链。

近义词：second-hand, used, secondhand

反义词：new, unused


词性：形容词
