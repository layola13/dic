impulsions: /ɪmˈpʌlʃənz/ | n. |冲动；驱使；推动力 |A sudden strong urge or desire to act.  Impulsions are often spontaneous and not carefully considered.  They can be positive or negative, depending on their nature and consequences.| 例句：He acted on a sudden impulse and bought the car. 他一时冲动买了那辆车。  Her impulses often led her into trouble. 她的冲动经常给她带来麻烦。|近义词：urges, drives, instincts, compulsions|反义词：deliberation, reflection, restraint|名词
