satay:/ˈsɑːteɪ/ | n. |沙爹，沙嗲 |Grilled meat (usually chicken, beef, lamb, or seafood) marinated in a spicy peanut sauce and served on skewers.  It's a popular dish in Southeast Asia, particularly in Malaysia, Singapore, and Indonesia. | Example: We had delicious satay at the night market. 我们在夜市吃了美味的沙爹。|近义词：kebab (a broader term)|反义词：无
