gaged:/ɡeɪdʒd/| adj.|已测量，已估量，已订婚|Measured or estimated; betrothed.  The land had been carefully gaged before the construction began. 建筑开始前已经仔细测量了土地。|近义词：measured, assessed, engaged|反义词：unmeasured, unassessed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river is gaged regularly to monitor water levels. = 定期测量河流以监测水位。
The engineer gages the bridge's load-bearing capacity. = 工程师测量桥梁的承重能力。


一般过去时:
They gaged the distance between the two points. = 他们测量了这两点之间的距离。
The architect gaged the amount of material needed. = 建筑师估算了所需材料的数量。


现在完成时:
The team has gaged the public's response to the new policy. = 团队已经评估了公众对新政策的反应。
We have gaged the impact of the advertising campaign. = 我们已经评估了广告宣传的影响。
