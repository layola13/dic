cleft:/klɛft/| adj. n.|分裂的；裂开的；（句法）分裂句的|Split or divided;  a sentence structure that emphasizes a particular part of the sentence by using a "cleft" construction (e.g., "It was John who broke the window").|例句：The cleft in the rock was deep and wide.  岩石上的裂缝又深又宽。The sentence "It was the dog that barked" is a cleft sentence. 句子“是狗叫了”是一个分裂句。|近义词：split, divided, fractured|反义词：united, whole, unbroken
