prayed:/preɪd/| v.|祈祷，恳求|To ask for help or guidance from a god or other supernatural being; to earnestly request or implore.|例句：She prayed for her son's safe return. 她为儿子的平安归来祈祷。|例句：He prayed for forgiveness. 他祈求宽恕。|近义词：besought, implored, supplicated|反义词：cursed, blasphemed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pray every day. = 我每天祈祷。
They pray before meals. = 他们饭前祈祷。

一般过去时:
She prayed for hours. = 她祈祷了好几个小时。
He prayed for rain. = 他祈祷下雨。

现在完成时:
I have prayed for this moment my whole life. = 我一生都在祈祷这一刻的到来。
They have prayed for peace. = 他们一直祈祷和平。

过去完成时:
She had prayed for guidance before making her decision. = 在做决定之前，她已经祈祷寻求指引。
He had prayed to God for help. = 他曾向上帝祈祷寻求帮助。

将来时:
I will pray for you. = 我会为你祈祷。
We will pray for their success. = 我们会为他们的成功祈祷。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pray for a miracle. = 如果我是你，我会祈祷奇迹发生。
I wish I had prayed harder. = 我希望我当时祈祷得更努力。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
Prayers were said for the victims. = 为受害者进行了祈祷。


疑问句:
Do you pray regularly? = 你经常祈祷吗？
Did they pray before the exam? = 考试前他们祈祷了吗？

否定句:
I don't pray often. = 我不经常祈祷。
He didn't pray that night. = 他那天晚上没有祈祷。


PHRASE->
pray for = 为…祈祷
pray to = 向…祈祷
pray that = 祈祷…
pray earnestly = 恳切地祈祷
pray incessantly = 不停地祈祷
