eco-geographic:/ˌekoʊˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪk/| adj. |生态地理的|Relating to the interaction between ecology and geography; concerning the geographical distribution of organisms and ecosystems.  This term encompasses the study of how environmental factors influence the distribution and abundance of species, as well as the impact of organisms on their environment within a geographical context.|Example: The eco-geographic study revealed a strong correlation between altitude and plant species diversity.  生态地理研究揭示了海拔高度与植物物种多样性之间存在很强的相关性。|近义词：biogeographic, ecological, geographical|反义词：N/A
