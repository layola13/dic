heptameters:/ˈheptəˌmiːtərz/| n. |七音步诗|A line of verse consisting of seven metrical feet.|Example:  The line "And the silken, sad, uncertain rustling of each purple curtain" from Edgar Allan Poe's "The Raven" is an example of a heptameter.  这句诗出自爱伦·坡的《乌鸦》，“And the silken, sad, uncertain rustling of each purple curtain”就是一个七音步诗的例子。|近义词：seven-foot line|反义词：monometer, dimeter, trimeter, tetrameter, pentameter, hexameter|无
