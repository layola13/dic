safely:/ˈseɪfli/| adv.|安全地，平安地|In a safe manner; without danger or risk.|例句：He arrived home safely. 他安全到家了。|例句：The package was delivered safely.包裹安全送达了。|近义词：securely, soundly, unharmed|反义词：dangerously, riskily
