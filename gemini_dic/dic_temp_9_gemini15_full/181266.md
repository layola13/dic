reinstituting:/ˌriːɪnˈstɪtuːtɪŋ/| v.|恢复，重建，重新开始|The act of reinstating something involves putting it back into its former place, state, or position.  It implies a previous existence that had been interrupted or ended.|例句：The company is reinstituting its employee training program after a two-year hiatus. 公司在暂停了两年后正在恢复其员工培训计划。

例句：After the storm subsided, the authorities began reinstituting essential services.暴风雨过后，当局开始恢复基本服务。


近义词：restoring, reviving, reestablishing, reinstating

反义词：abolishing, eliminating, terminating, ending

词性:动词


USAGE->

The school reinstitutes the music program. = 学校恢复了音乐课程。
