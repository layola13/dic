词源:
The word "purplishness" is derived from the adjective "purple" and the suffix "-ish," which indicates a slight or somewhat resemblance.  "Purple" itself has a long and complex etymology, tracing back to ancient Greek and ultimately to the Phoenician dye obtained from murex snails.
