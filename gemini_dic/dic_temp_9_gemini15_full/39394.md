conjurement:/kənˈdʒʊərmənt/| n. |咒语；魔法；变戏法；恳求|The act of conjuring; the use of magic or illusion; an invocation or calling upon.  It can also refer to a fervent or earnest request or entreaty.|例句：The magician's conjurement amazed the audience. 魔术师的变戏法让观众惊叹不已。

例句：His conjurement for rain was answered with a downpour. 他祈雨的恳求得到了倾盆大雨的回应。


近义词：incantation, enchantment, spell, invocation, entreaty

反义词：disenchantment, reality, fact


词性：名词
