/nʌθɪŋˈɛəriənɪzəm/| n. |虚无主义；无物论|The philosophical belief that nothing exists or that nothing has meaning.  It's a radical form of nihilism that denies the existence of any objective reality or inherent value.|Example:  His embrace of nothingarianism led him to withdraw from all social interaction.  他的虚无主义让他退出了所有的社交互动。|近义词：nihilism, absurdism|反义词：existentialism, realism|名词
