hour:/ˈaʊər/ /ˈhaʊər/| n. |小时，时刻|A period of time equal to 60 minutes.  It can refer to a specific point in time or a duration. |例句：The meeting lasted for two hours.  会议持续了两个小时。
例句：What time is it?  现在几点钟了？|近义词：period, time|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works eight hours a day. = 他每天工作八小时。
She usually has lunch at noon hour. = 她通常在中午吃饭。

一般过去时:
We spent several hours discussing the project. = 我们花了几个小时讨论这个项目。
The train arrived an hour late. = 火车晚到一个小时。

现在完成时:
They have worked for hours on this problem. = 他们在这个问题上已经工作了好几个小时。
I haven't seen her for hours. = 我好几个小时没见过她了。

过去完成时:
They had already worked for hours before the boss arrived. = 老板到达之前他们已经工作了好几个小时。
We had been waiting for hours before the bus finally came. = 公共汽车终于来了，我们已经等了好几个小时。

将来时:
We will meet in an hour. = 我们一小时后见面。
The film will start in one hour. = 电影一小时后开始。

虚拟语气:
If I had an extra hour, I would finish the report. = 如果我多一个小时，我会完成报告。
I wish I had more hours in a day. = 我希望一天有更多的时间。

被动语态:
The meeting was scheduled for two hours. = 会议安排了两个小时。
The work can be completed within an hour. = 这项工作可以在一小时内完成。

疑问句:
How many hours do you work each week? = 你每周工作多少小时？
What hour does the train leave? = 火车几点钟离开？

否定句:
The meeting didn't last an hour. = 会议没有持续一个小时。
I don't have enough hours in the day. = 我一天的时间不够用。
