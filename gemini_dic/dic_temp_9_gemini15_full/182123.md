remissive:/rɪˈmɪsɪv/| adj.| 懈怠的；宽容的；纵容的 | Neglectful of duty or obligation; lenient or forgiving.  |例句：The teacher was criticized for being too remissive with her students. 老师因对学生过于宽容而受到批评。
例句：His remissive attitude towards his work led to his dismissal. 他对工作懈怠的态度导致了他的解雇。|近义词：negligent, lax, indulgent, permissive |反义词：strict, rigorous, firm, demanding
