holdership:/ˈhoʊldərʃɪp/| n. |持有；所有权；持有人身份|The state or fact of holding something, especially property or a position.  It refers to the right to possess and control something. |例句：The holdership of the land was disputed in court.  土地的所有权在法庭上受到争议。

例句：The company's holdership of the patent is crucial to its success.  该公司对该专利的持有权对其成功至关重要。


近义词：ownership, possession, control, custodianship

反义词：lack of ownership, relinquishment

词性:名词
