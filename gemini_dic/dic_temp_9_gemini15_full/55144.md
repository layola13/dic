dissection:/dɪˈsɛkʃən/| n.|解剖；仔细分析|The careful cutting apart of a body or plant to study its internal parts.  It can also refer to a thorough analysis of a complex subject.|例句：The dissection of the frog was a required part of biology class.  青蛙解剖是生物课的必修部分。

例句：The political scientist offered a detailed dissection of the election results.  这位政治学家对选举结果进行了详细的分析。|近义词：analysis, examination, scrutiny|反义词：synthesis, integration|词性:名词
