committees:/kəˈmɪtiz/ | n. |委员会；全体委员 | A group of people appointed for a specific function, typically within a larger organization.  Committees are formed to make decisions, investigate issues, or oversee projects. | The school board formed a committee to review the curriculum. 学校董事会成立了一个委员会来审查课程。The senate committee is investigating the allegations. 参议院委员会正在调查这些指控。 | councils, boards, panels | individuals, single person | 名词
