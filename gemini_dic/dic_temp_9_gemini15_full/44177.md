critiques:/krɪˈtiːk/| n.| 批评；评论 | A critique is a detailed analysis and assessment of something, especially a literary, philosophical, or artistic work.  It often involves both positive and negative observations and suggestions for improvement. | Example: The professor gave a thorough critique of my essay, pointing out both its strengths and weaknesses.  教授对我的论文进行了彻底的评论，指出了它的优点和缺点。|近义词：review, analysis, evaluation, assessment|反义词：praise, commendation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The professor critiques student papers every week. = 教授每周都评论学生的论文。
She critiques films for a popular website. = 她为一个受欢迎的网站撰写影评。


一般过去时:
He critiqued her performance and offered suggestions. = 他评论了她的表演并提出了建议。
The art critic critiqued the painting's use of color. = 艺术评论家评论了这幅画的色彩运用。


现在完成时:
She has critiqued many works of art throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经评论了许多艺术作品。
He has critiqued the government's policies on several occasions. = 他多次评论了政府的政策。


过去完成时:
They had critiqued the proposal before submitting their feedback. = 他们在提交反馈意见之前已经评论了该提案。
The committee had critiqued the design several times. = 委员会已经多次评论了该设计。


将来时:
She will critique the manuscript next week. = 她下周将评论这份手稿。
The panel will critique the presentations tomorrow. = 专家小组明天将评论这些演示文稿。


虚拟语气:
If I were to critique his work, I would focus on its structure. = 如果我要评论他的作品，我会关注它的结构。
I wish I had critiqued the draft more thoroughly. = 我希望我更彻底地评论了草稿。


被动语态:
The student's essay was critiqued by the professor. = 学生的论文受到了教授的评论。
The new policy is being critiqued by the public. = 新政策正在受到公众的评论。


疑问句:
Will the panel critique the designs? = 专家小组会评论这些设计吗？
Has the article been critiqued yet? = 这篇文章已经被评论了吗？


否定句:
He did not critique her work harshly. = 他并没有严厉地批评她的作品。
They have not yet critiqued the final version. = 他们还没有评论最终版本。
