refresher's:/rɪˈfreʃərz/| n.|复习课；复习材料；提神的东西|A refresher course is a short course designed to update someone's knowledge or skills in a particular area.  A refresher drink is something that revitalizes or invigorates.  The term often implies a brief, focused review to restore or enhance understanding.|例句：The company offered a refresher course on safety regulations. 这家公司提供了一门关于安全条例的复习课程。

例句：I need a refresher;  this coffee is too weak. 我需要提神；这咖啡太淡了。|近义词：review, update, refresher course, revitalizer|反义词：N/A
