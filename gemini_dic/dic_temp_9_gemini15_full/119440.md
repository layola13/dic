microlighting:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌlaɪtɪŋ/| n. |微型滑翔伞运动 |The activity of flying a microlight aircraft.  It's a form of recreational aviation involving lightweight, single-seat or two-seat aircraft. |Example: He took a microlighting lesson and loved the experience. 他上了一节微型滑翔伞课，并且非常喜欢这项体验。|近义词：ultralight flying, hang gliding (though technically different)|反义词：None directly applicable.
