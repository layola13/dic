self-adulatory: /ˌsɛlfˈædjʊlətɔːri/ | adj. | 自我吹嘘的；自夸的 |  Characterized by or given to self-praise; excessively praising oneself.  It suggests an excessive and often unwarranted level of self-praise. | 例句：His self-adulatory speech was met with polite but thinly veiled boredom.  他的自我吹嘘的演讲受到了礼貌但掩饰不住的无聊回应。


例句：The artist's self-adulatory comments about his own work irritated the critics.  这位艺术家对他自己作品的自我吹嘘的评论激怒了评论家们。


近义词：narcissistic, egotistical, self-congratulatory, vainglorious


反义词：self-deprecating, modest, humble


词性: adjective
