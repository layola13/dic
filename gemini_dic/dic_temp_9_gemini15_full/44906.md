cryopreserve:/ˈkraɪoʊprɪˈzɜːrv/| vt.|冷冻保存；低温保存|To preserve (biological material, especially cells, tissues, or organs) by freezing at very low temperatures.|The scientists successfully cryopreserved the embryos for future research. 科学家们成功地冷冻保存了胚胎以备将来研究。|近义词：freeze, refrigerate, store|反义词：thaw, defrost
