perquisite:/ˈpɜːrkɪzɪt/| n. |额外津贴；好处；特权 |A perquisite is a benefit received in addition to one's regular salary or wages.  It is often a privilege or advantage that comes with a job or position. |Example:  Company cars and expense accounts are common perquisites for executives.  公司汽车和费用账户是高管常见的额外津贴。|近义词：benefit, privilege, advantage |反义词：deduction, penalty
