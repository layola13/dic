echinoidea:/ˌɛkɪˈnɔɪdiə/| n. |海胆类|Sea urchins, sand dollars, and heart urchins are all echinoids. They are characterized by their spiny shells and radial symmetry.  They are marine invertebrates belonging to the class Echinoidea within the phylum Echinodermata. |例句：The intertidal zone was teeming with echinoids of various shapes and sizes.  潮间带挤满了各种形状和大小的海胆。|例句：Research on echinoid evolution helps us understand the diversification of marine life.  对海胆进化研究有助于我们理解海洋生物的多样化。|近义词：sea urchin (though not entirely synonymous as echinoidea is a class)|反义词：N/A
