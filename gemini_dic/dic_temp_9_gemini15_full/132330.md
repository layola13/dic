nondeformation:/ˌnɒndeˌfɔːrˈmeɪʃən/| n. |非变形，不变形|The absence of deformation; the state of remaining unchanged in shape or form.  It often refers to a material's or structure's ability to resist changes in shape under stress.|例句：The bridge showed remarkable nondeformation under the heavy load. 这座桥在重压下表现出显著的抗变形能力。
The experiment demonstrated the high nondeformation properties of the new alloy.  实验证明了这种新型合金具有很高的抗变形性能。|近义词：invariance, stability, rigidity|反义词：deformation, distortion, warping|
