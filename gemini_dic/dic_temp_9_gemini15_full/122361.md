mixup: /ˈmɪksʌp/ | n. v. | 混乱；混淆；搞混；误会 |

英文解析:A mix-up is a confusing situation, often caused by a mistake or misunderstanding.  As a verb, it means to confuse or mix things up unintentionally.

例句:
There was a mix-up with the orders, and some customers received the wrong items. = 订单出现混乱，一些顾客收到了错误的商品。

The files were badly mix up, making it difficult to find anything. = 文件被严重弄乱，难以找到任何东西。


近义词:confusion, muddle, jumble, mess, blunder, error

反义词:order, clarity, organization

词性: noun, verb
