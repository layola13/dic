honchos:/ˈhɑːntʃoʊz/| n. |大亨，头目，重要人物|A honcho is a boss or a person of high rank within an organization.  The term is often used informally and carries a slightly playful or ironic tone.|例句：The honchos at the company decided to launch a new marketing campaign. 公司的大亨们决定启动一个新的营销活动。

例句：He's a honcho in the music industry. 他是音乐界的大人物。


近义词：boss, chief, leader, executive, big shot

反义词：subordinate, underling, follower


词性:名词
