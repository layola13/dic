odds:/ɒdz/ /ɑːdz/| n.|可能性；几率；机会；赔率；逆境；差异|The likelihood of something happening; the probability; a chance; the ratio of the stake to the sum won in a bet; unfavorable circumstances; a difference or disparity.|例句：The odds are stacked against him. = 他面临着巨大的困难。

例句：What are the odds of winning the lottery? = 中彩票的几率是多少？|近义词：probability, chance, likelihood, possibility|反义词：certainty, inevitability


USAGE->

The odds are in our favor. = 机会对我们有利。
The odds against success are high. = 成功的机会很渺茫。
