quasi-controlled:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kənˈtroʊld/| adj. | 准控制的，半控制的 |  Approaching or resembling control but not completely or formally so;  partially controlled. This term often describes systems or experiments where some factors are manipulated but not to the extent of a fully controlled experiment.  | Example: The study employed a quasi-controlled design, manipulating one variable while observing others without direct intervention.  这项研究采用了一种准控制设计，操纵一个变量，同时观察其他变量，而无需直接干预。 |近义词:partially controlled, semi-controlled |反义词:fully controlled, uncontrolled
