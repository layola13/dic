primacy:/ˈpraɪməsi/| n. |首要地位；主要地位；优先权 |The state or fact of being primary in importance.  Primacy refers to something that holds the most significant position or importance, often exceeding others in rank or value. It suggests a supreme or leading position.|例句：The primacy of the family is essential to a stable society. 家庭的首要地位对稳定的社会至关重要。

例句：The primacy of education is often emphasized in developing countries.  在发展中国家，教育的首要地位经常被强调。


近义词：superiority, predominance, preeminence, precedence

反义词：subordination, inferiority, insignificance
