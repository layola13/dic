multicelled:/ˈmʌltiˌsɛld/| adj.|多细胞的|Consisting of many cells.  Multicellular organisms are complex life forms composed of numerous cells working together.|例句：Plants are multicellular organisms.  植物是多细胞生物。|例句：Sponges are among the simplest multicellular animals. 海绵是最简单的多细胞动物之一。|近义词：polycellular|反义词：unicellular
