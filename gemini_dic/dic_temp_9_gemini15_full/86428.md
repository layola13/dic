heedy:/ˈhiːdi/| adj.|注意的；留心的；谨慎的|Careful; watchful; heedful.  The heedy driver avoided the accident.  |近义词：attentive, cautious, wary, vigilant|反义词：careless, negligent, reckless, inattentive|


例句：

He gave heedy attention to the speaker's words. 他仔细聆听了演讲者的讲话。

The heedy hiker prepared for the difficult terrain.  这位谨慎的徒步旅行者为艰难的地形做好了准备。


词源:

源自古英语 *hēdan,  意为“to pay attention to”，与“heed” (注意，留心) 词根相关。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is heedy of the dangers. 他注意到了危险。
She is always heedy in her work. 她工作总是很细心。

一般过去时:
He was heedy of the warning signs. 他注意到了警告标志。
She was heedy about crossing the street. 她过马路时很小心。

现在完成时:
He has been heedy about his health. 他一直很注意自己的健康。
She has been heedy of the potential risks. 她一直注意潜在的风险。

过去完成时:
He had been heedy of the approaching storm. 他已经注意到了即将到来的暴风雨。
She had been heedy in her preparations. 她准备工作一直很细致。

将来时:
He will be heedy of the advice. 他会注意这个建议。
She will be heedy when driving in the rain. 她下雨天开车时会很小心。

虚拟语气:
If he were heedy, he wouldn't have made that mistake. 如果他小心点，就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish she had been more heedy in her actions. 我希望她行动更谨慎一些。

被动语态:  (Heedy is rarely used passively)  The warning was heeded.  警告被注意到了。(这里用的是heed,  不是heedy)
The danger should be heeded. 危险应该引起重视。(这里用的是heed,  不是heedy)


疑问句:
Was he heedy enough to avoid the danger? 他是否足够小心以避免危险？
Is she always so heedy? 她总是这么细心吗？

否定句:
He was not heedy enough to see the problem. 他不够细心，没有发现问题。
She wasn't heedy about her finances. 她对自己的财务不够谨慎。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "heedy".  The adjective is used attributively (before a noun) or predicatively (after a linking verb).
