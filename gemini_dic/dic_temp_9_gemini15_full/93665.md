imbuements: /ɪmˈbjuːmənts/ | n. | 灌输；注入；赋予 | The act of imbuing or the state of being imbued; the process of instilling qualities, characteristics, or ideas into something or someone.  | Example: The artist's paintings are imbued with a deep sense of melancholy.  艺术家的绘画作品充满了深深的忧郁。|近义词：infusions, instillations, instillments | 反义词：removal, extraction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist imbues his paintings with vibrant colors. = 艺术家在他的画作中注入了鲜艳的色彩。
This ritual imbues the object with sacred power. = 这种仪式赋予了这个物体神圣的力量。


一般过去时:
The teacher imbued her students with a love of learning. = 老师给她的学生灌输了对学习的热爱。
The experience imbued him with a newfound confidence. = 那次经历赋予了他一种新的自信。


现在完成时:
The ancient traditions have imbued the culture with unique characteristics. = 古老的传统赋予了这种文化独特的特征。
Years of hardship have imbued her with resilience. = 多年的磨难使她变得坚韧。


过去完成时:
She had imbued the story with so much emotion that everyone was moved. = 她赋予了这个故事如此多的情感，以至于每个人都被感动了。
The long journey had imbued them with a sense of exhaustion. = 漫长的旅程使他们感到筋疲力尽。


将来时:
The new program will imbue the workforce with essential skills. = 新的项目将使员工掌握必要的技能。
This training will imbue them with the knowledge they need. = 这项培训将使他们掌握所需的知识。


虚拟语气:
If I could imbue my paintings with life, I would. = 如果我能赋予我的画作生命，我会这么做的。
I wish I could imbue my words with more power. = 我希望我能赋予我的话语更多力量。


被动语态:
The fabric was imbued with a fragrant scent. = 布料被赋予了一种芬芳的香味。
The students were imbued with a strong sense of discipline. = 学生们被灌输了强烈的纪律感。


疑问句:
What qualities did the experience imbue you with? = 那次经历赋予了你什么品质？
How can we imbue our products with more value? = 我们如何才能为我们的产品赋予更多价值？


否定句:
The painting failed to imbue the viewer with any emotion. = 这幅画未能激起观众的任何情感。
He couldn't imbue his speech with the necessary conviction. = 他无法赋予他的演讲必要的信念。
