conventionary:/kənˈvɛnʃənəri/ | adj. | 传统的，习俗的，常规的 | Conventional; adhering to established customs or traditions.  It suggests something is common or ordinary, often implying a lack of originality or innovation. | Example: He had conventionary views on marriage and family. 他对婚姻和家庭持有传统观念。 |近义词：traditional, customary, orthodox | 反义词：unconventional, unorthodox, original
