queller:/ˈkwɛlər/| vt.|压制；平息；镇压；消除|To suppress or extinguish completely; to subdue; to quell a riot.|例句：The police quelled the riot. 警察平息了暴动。

例句：She quelled her fear and faced the challenge. 她压制住恐惧，面对挑战。


近义词：suppress, subdue, crush, extinguish, stifle|反义词：incite, encourage, inflame, provoke|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government quells dissent. = 政府压制异见。
He quells his anger. = 他压制自己的怒火。

一般过去时:
The army quelled the rebellion. = 军队平定了叛乱。
She quelled her anxieties before the speech. = 她在演讲前平息了焦虑。

现在完成时:
The authorities have quelled the unrest. = 当局已经平息了骚乱。
He has quelled his fear of public speaking. = 他已经克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。

过去完成时:
They had quelled the rumors before they spread too far. = 他们在那谣言传播太广之前就将其平息了。
She had quelled her doubts before making the decision. = 她在下决定之前已经平息了自己的疑虑。

将来时:
The police will quell any further disturbances. = 警察将平息任何进一步的骚乱。
He will quell his nervousness before the interview. = 他会在面试前压制住自己的紧张情绪。

虚拟语气:
If only we could quell the conflict peacefully. = 但愿我们能和平地解决冲突。
I wish I could quell my doubts about the project. = 我希望我能消除我对这个项目的疑虑。

被动语态:
The riot was quelled by the police. = 暴乱被警察平息了。
Her fears were quelled by her friend's support. = 她朋友的支持平息了她的恐惧。

疑问句:
Can we quell the rumors effectively? = 我们能有效地平息谣言吗？
How can we quell the spread of misinformation? = 我们如何才能阻止虚假信息的传播？

否定句:
The government failed to quell the protests. = 政府未能平息抗议活动。
He couldn't quell his feelings of sadness. = 他无法压制住悲伤的情绪。


PHRASE->
quell a rebellion = 平息叛乱
quell a riot = 平息暴乱
quell dissent = 压制异见
quell unrest = 平息动荡
quell one's fears = 平息恐惧


词源:
源自古法语queller，意为“to kill”，最终源自拉丁语coactare，“to force together”。  其含义演变为“压制，平息”，反映了对冲突或动乱的压制行为。
