hub:/hʌb/| n. |中心，枢纽，核心 |A central point of activity, interest, or connection.  A hub is a central point of activity, interest, or connection. For example, a city can be a hub for transportation, or a company can be a hub for innovation.| 例句：London is a major hub for international travel.  伦敦是国际旅行的主要枢纽。  The company's headquarters serves as a hub for all its operations. 公司总部是其所有业务的中心。|近义词：center, core, nucleus |反义词：periphery, edge, outskirts |名词
