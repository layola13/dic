mingiest:/ˈmɪndʒɪst/| adj.|最吝啬的，最贪婪的|Most miserly; stingiest.  The word implies an extreme degree of unwillingness to spend money or give anything away.|例句：He was the mingiest man in town, refusing to even share a cup of tea. 他是镇上最吝啬的人，甚至不肯分享一杯茶。He was so mingiest that he wouldn't even give a beggar a penny. 他非常吝啬，甚至不肯给乞丐一个便士。|近义词：stingiest, miserliest, most parsimonious|反义词：most generous, most charitable, most munificent
