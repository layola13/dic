launch:/lɔːntʃ/| /lɑːntʃ/ | vt. n. |发射；启动；开始；上市 | To propel or send off, especially into the air or space; to initiate or begin something; to introduce a new product or service to the market. |例句：The rocket launched successfully. 火箭成功发射了。 The company launched a new marketing campaign. 公司启动了一场新的营销活动。|近义词：initiate, start, commence, introduce |反义词：end, terminate, conclude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They launch a new product every year. = 他们每年都会推出一个新产品。
We launch the boat at dawn. = 我们在黎明时分下水。


一般过去时:
The spaceship launched yesterday. = 宇宙飞船昨天发射了。
She launched her career in finance. = 她开始了她的金融事业。


现在完成时:
They have launched three satellites this year. = 他们今年已经发射了三颗卫星。
The company has successfully launched its new app. = 公司已成功推出了其新的应用程序。


过去完成时:
They had launched the project before the funding was secured. = 在资金到位之前，他们就已经启动了这个项目。
The team had launched several campaigns before this one. = 在这次活动之前，团队已经开展过几次活动。


将来时:
We will launch the new website next month. = 我们下个月将推出新的网站。
They will launch a new line of clothing in the spring. = 他们将在春天推出新的服装系列。


虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, we would have launched the project sooner. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们就会更早启动项目。
I wish we had launched the marketing campaign earlier. = 我希望我们早点启动营销活动。


被动语态:
The rocket was launched at 6 am. = 火箭在早上6点发射。
The new product will be launched globally. = 新产品将在全球上市。


疑问句:
When will the new game be launched? = 新游戏什么时候推出？
Have they launched the new advertising campaign yet? = 他们已经推出新的广告宣传活动了吗？


否定句:
The rocket didn't launch successfully. = 火箭没有成功发射。
They haven't launched the new website yet. = 他们还没有推出新的网站。



PHRASE->
product launch = 产品发布
market launch = 市场发布
missile launch = 导弹发射
campaign launch = 活动启动
software launch = 软件发布
