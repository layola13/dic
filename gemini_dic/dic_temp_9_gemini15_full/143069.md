outboxing: /ˌaʊtˈbɑːksɪŋ/ | v. | 拳击中战胜，技高一筹 |  To defeat someone in boxing by being more skillful and effective.  It implies a superior boxing technique and strategy leading to victory. | Example:  He outboxed his opponent throughout the fight, landing more punches and controlling the distance. 他整场比赛都技高一筹，击中更多拳并控制距离。 | 近义词: outmaneuver, outfight, outsmart | 反义词: be outboxed, lose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outboxes his opponents with superior speed and agility. = 他凭借着更快的速度和灵活性击败对手。
She outboxes most of her competitors in the local boxing club. =  她在当地拳击俱乐部里技压群雄。


一般过去时:
