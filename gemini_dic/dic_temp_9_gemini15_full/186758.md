rikishi:/rɪˈkiːʃi/| n. |力士，相扑手 |A rikishi is a professional sumo wrestler.  Rikishi are known for their immense size and strength, and they participate in highly ritualized matches. |例句：The rikishi bowed deeply before the match began.  这位力士在比赛开始前深深地鞠了一躬。


The rikishi demonstrated incredible power during the bout.  这位力士在比赛中展现了令人难以置信的力量。|近义词：sumo wrestler, wrestler|反义词：None specifically, but words implying weakness or frailty could be considered antonyms in a contextual sense.
