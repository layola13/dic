/pərˈsɪvərɪŋ/ | adj. | 坚持不懈的，锲而不舍的 | Persistent in doing something despite difficulty or delay in achieving success.  It implies a determined effort and continued striving towards a goal. |  She was a persevering student, finally mastering the difficult concept after many hours of study. 她是一位坚持不懈的学生，经过数小时的学习后，最终掌握了这个难题。  He showed persevering dedication to his work, even during challenging times. 他即使在充满挑战的时期也表现出坚持不懈的奉献精神。| persistent, tenacious, steadfast |  giving up, quitting, surrendering |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is persevering in his efforts to learn a new language. 他坚持不懈地努力学习一门外语。
The team is persevering despite the setbacks. 尽管遇到挫折，团队仍在坚持不懈。


一般过去时:
She persevered through many obstacles to achieve her goals. 她克服了许多障碍，最终实现了自己的目标。
They persevered in their search for the lost treasure. 他们坚持不懈地寻找失落的宝藏。


现在完成时:
He has persevered for years in his fight for justice. 他多年来一直坚持不懈地为正义而斗争。
She has persevered despite facing numerous challenges. 尽管面临诸多挑战，她依然坚持不懈。


过去完成时:
They had persevered for months before finally finding a solution. 在最终找到解决方案之前，他们已经坚持了几个月。
She had persevered through many hardships before reaching her success. 在获得成功之前，她已经克服了许多困难。


将来时:
He will persevere in his studies, no matter how difficult they become. 不管学习多么困难，他都会坚持下去。
They will persevere until they reach their destination.  他们将坚持不懈地走到目的地。


虚拟语气:
If I were more persevering, I would have succeeded. 如果我更坚持不懈，我就会成功。
I wish I had been more persevering in my attempts to learn the piano. 我真希望我在学习钢琴的尝试中更坚持不懈一些。


被动语态:
Perseverance is often rewarded. 坚持不懈往往会得到回报。(Note:  "persevering" itself is rarely used passively)
His dedication was persevered in, despite the criticism.  他的奉献精神得到了坚持，尽管受到了批评。(This is an unusual construction)


疑问句:
Is he persevering in his efforts? 他在努力坚持吗？
Are they persevering despite the difficulties? 尽管困难重重，他们还在坚持吗？


否定句:
He did not persevere in his studies. 他没有坚持学习。
They did not persevere in the face of adversity. 面对逆境，他们没有坚持下去。
