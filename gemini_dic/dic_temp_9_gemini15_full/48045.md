decking:/ˈdɛkɪŋ/| n.|甲板；地板；覆盖物|Flooring or covering, typically of wood, for a deck, platform, or floor.  It can refer to the material itself or the process of installing it.|例句：The deck was made of pressure-treated decking.  这甲板是用压力处理过的木板做的。|例句：They are decking the new patio with slate tiles. 他们正在用石板瓦铺设新的露台。|近义词：flooring, platforming, paving|反义词：uncovering, removing
