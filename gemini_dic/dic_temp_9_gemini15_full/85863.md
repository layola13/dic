head-over-heels:/ˌhedˈoʊvərˈhiːlz/| adj.|坠入爱河的；非常；极度|Completely in love; very enthusiastically or intensely.|例句：She's head-over-heels in love with him. 她对他一见钟情。|例句：They were head over heels excited about the trip.他们对这次旅行兴奋不已。|近义词：madly, deeply, passionately|反义词：indifferent, apathetic


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are head over heels in love. = 他们热恋中。
She is head over heels for that new car. = 她对那辆新车爱不释手。


一般过去时:
He was head over heels in love with her. = 他当时对她一见钟情。

被动语态:
