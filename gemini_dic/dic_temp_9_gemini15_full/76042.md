furless:/ˈfɜːrləs/| adj.|无毛的，光秃秃的|Without fur or hair.  The adjective describes something lacking fur or hair. It can be used to describe animals, plants, or even surfaces.|例句：The sphynx cat is a furless breed.  埃及猫是一种无毛猫。

The dog was furless after its fight with a porcupine. 那只狗和豪猪打架后毛都掉了。|近义词：hairless, bald, bare|反义词：furry, hairy
