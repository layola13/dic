gritting:/ˈɡrɪtɪŋ/| v.|咬牙切齿，咯吱作响|The act of grinding or crushing something with a grating sound;  to clench one's teeth, typically in anger or determination.|例句：He gritted his teeth in pain. 他疼得咬牙切齿。

例句：The sound of the gears gritting was irritating. 齿轮咯吱作响的声音很烦人。|近义词：gnashing, grinding, clenching|反义词：relaxing, soothing
