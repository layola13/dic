reproducer:/ˌriːprəˈdjuːsər/| n. |复制者；繁殖者；复制设备|A reproducer is a person or thing that makes copies of something, or produces offspring.|例句：The photocopier is a useful reproducer of documents.  复印机是复制文件的有用设备。|例句：The rabbit is a prolific reproducer. 兔子是多产的繁殖者。|近义词：copier, duplicator, breeder |反义词：destroyer, annihilator
