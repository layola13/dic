nauseousness:/ˈnɔːziəsnəs/ | n. |恶心，想吐的感觉 |A feeling of sickness and the urge to vomit.  It can be caused by various factors, including illness, medication side effects, or food poisoning.  The severity of nausea can range from mild discomfort to intense distress. |例句：The smell of the fish caused a wave of nausea.  鱼腥味引起一阵恶心。

The chemotherapy caused intense nausea and vomiting. 化疗引起了剧烈的恶心和呕吐。|近义词：sickness, queasiness, vomiting |反义词：wellness, well-being
