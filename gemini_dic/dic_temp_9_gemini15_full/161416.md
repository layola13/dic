posture:/ˈpɒstʃə(r)/, /ˈpɑːstʃər/ | n. v. | 姿势；态度；姿态 | The way in which someone holds their body; a particular attitude or feeling. |例句：He sat with perfect posture. 他坐姿端正。

She adopted a defensive posture. 她采取了防御姿态。


近义词：carriage, bearing, stance, attitude | 反义词：slouch, slump


词性: 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maintains good posture. = 他保持良好的姿态。
She often adopts a confident posture. = 她经常摆出自信的姿态。


一般过去时:
He improved his posture with exercises. = 他通过锻炼改善了他的姿势。
She held a regal posture during the ceremony. = 典礼上她保持着高贵的姿态。


现在完成时:
He has developed bad posture from sitting at a desk all day. = 他整天坐在桌子旁，养成了不良的姿势。
She has maintained excellent posture throughout her life. = 她一生都保持着良好的姿态。


过去完成时:
He had already corrected his posture before the photo was taken. = 照片拍摄之前他已经纠正了自己的姿势。
She had held a stiff posture for so long that her muscles ached. = 她保持僵硬的姿势太久了，肌肉都酸痛了。


将来时:

She will need to adjust her posture to avoid back pain. = 她需要调整姿势以避免背痛。
