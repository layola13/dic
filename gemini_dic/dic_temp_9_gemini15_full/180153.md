reforges:/ˌriːˈfɔːrdʒ/| vt.|重铸；重新锻造；改造|To forge (metal) again; to reshape or reconstruct.|The blacksmith reforged the broken sword.  铁匠重铸了那把断剑。|He reforged his career after the scandal.  丑闻后他重塑了自己的职业生涯。|近义词：reshape, remake, reconstruct|反义词：destroy, demolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reforges old tools into new art. 他将旧工具重铸成新的艺术品。
The company reforges its strategy every year.  这家公司每年都会重塑其战略。


一般过去时:
The artist reforged the damaged sculpture.  艺术家重铸了损坏的雕塑。
She reforged her identity after moving to a new country. 她搬到一个新的国家后重塑了自己的身份。


现在完成时:
He has reforged his reputation after years of hard work.  多年努力后，他重塑了自己的声誉。
The government has reforged its relationship with its allies. 政府已经重建了与盟友的关系。


过去完成时:
They had reforged their alliance before the war began.  战争开始前，他们已经重建了他们的联盟。
She had reforged her will to live after overcoming her illness. 她战胜疾病后，重拾了生活的意志。


将来时:
The company will reforge its image through a new marketing campaign.  公司将通过新的营销活动来重塑形象。
He will reforge his commitment to fitness after his injury.  受伤后，他会重新致力于健身。


虚拟语气:
If they reforged their partnership, they could achieve greater success. 如果他们重修伙伴关系，他们就能取得更大的成功。
I wish he had reforged his relationship with his family. 我希望他和家人重修旧好。


被动语态:
The broken vase was reforged by a skilled craftsman.  破损的花瓶被一位技艺精湛的工匠重铸了。
The company's strategy is being reforged to meet the changing market demands.  公司战略正在重塑以满足不断变化的市场需求。


疑问句:
Will the blacksmith reforge the damaged armor?  铁匠会重铸损坏的盔甲吗？
Is the company planning to reforge its product line?  公司计划重塑其产品线吗？


否定句:
He did not reforge the old weapon. 他没有重铸旧武器。
The sculptor could not reforge the damaged statue. 雕塑家无法重铸受损的雕像。
