cladophyll:/ˈklædəfɪl/| n.|叶状枝，假叶|A cladophyll is a flattened stem that resembles a leaf and performs the function of a leaf in photosynthesis.  It is not a true leaf, as it develops from a stem rather than a bud. |Example:  Many cacti species have cladophylls instead of true leaves to reduce water loss.  许多仙人掌物种具有叶状枝而不是真叶以减少水分流失。|近义词：phylloclade|反义词：leaf (true leaf)
