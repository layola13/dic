hot-wiring: /ˈhɑːtˌwaɪərɪŋ/ | n. |  偷接电线；强行启动（汽车）|  The act of bypassing a vehicle's normal starting system by directly connecting the wires to the battery to start the engine. This is often done illegally to steal a car. | The thief gained access to the car through hot-wiring.  小偷通过偷接电线进入了汽车。

The police caught the suspect in the act of hot-wiring a car. 警方当场抓获了正在偷接电线启动汽车的嫌疑人。| 近义词：bypassing the ignition system, illegally starting | 反义词：legally starting, using the key
