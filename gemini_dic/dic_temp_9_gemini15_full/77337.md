gauche:/ɡoʊʃ/| adj.|笨拙的；粗鲁的；不雅观的|Lacking grace, sensitivity, or social skill; awkward.  It often implies a lack of social awareness or tact, leading to embarrassing or inappropriate behavior.|例句：He made a gauche remark about her weight. 他对她体重发表了不雅的评论。|例句：Her gauche attempt at dancing only served to embarrass her further. 她笨拙的舞蹈更让她难堪。|近义词：awkward, clumsy, uncouth, tactless|反义词：graceful, elegant, refined, tactful
