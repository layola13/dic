prediction: /prɪˈdɪkʃən/ | n. |预言；预测；预报 | The act of predicting or foretelling a future event; a statement about what will happen in the future.  |例句：The weather prediction for tomorrow is rain. 明天的天气预报是雨天。|例句：Her prediction about the market crash proved accurate. 她关于市场崩盘的预测被证明是准确的。|近义词：forecast, prophecy, foretelling, prognosis |反义词：retrodiction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist makes predictions about the future. = 科学家对未来做出预测。
He often predicts the outcome of the game. = 他经常预测比赛的结果。


一般过去时:
The fortune teller made a prediction about my future. = 算命先生对我的未来做出了预测。
They predicted a successful launch of the rocket. = 他们预测火箭发射成功。


现在完成时:
Scientists have made many accurate predictions about climate change. = 科学家们已经对气候变化做出了许多准确的预测。
She has predicted the winner of the election several times. = 她已经几次预测了选举的获胜者。


过去完成时:
They had predicted the storm before it hit the coast. = 暴风雨袭击海岸之前，他们就已经预测到了。
The economists had predicted a recession months earlier. = 经济学家们几个月前就预测到了经济衰退。


将来时:
Meteorologists will predict the path of the hurricane. = 气象学家将预测飓风的路径。
Experts will predict the next big technological breakthrough. = 专家们将预测下一个重大的技术突破。


虚拟语气:
If I had listened to his prediction, I wouldn't have lost so much money. = 如果我听了他的预测，我就不会损失这么多钱。
I wish I had known his prediction earlier. = 我真希望我早点知道他的预测。


被动语态:
The earthquake was predicted by scientists a day before. = 地震在一天前被科学家预测到。
The winner of the competition was accurately predicted. = 比赛的获胜者被准确地预测到了。


疑问句:
What is your prediction for the stock market next year? = 你对明年股市的预测是什么？
Can you make a prediction about the weather for the weekend? = 你能预测周末的天气吗？


否定句:
They did not predict the economic downturn. = 他们没有预测到经济低迷。
The forecast did not predict such heavy rainfall. = 预报没有预测到如此大的降雨量。
