deregulating: /ˌdiːˈreɡjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |放松管制；解除管制 |The act of removing or reducing government regulations and controls from a particular industry or sector.  This often aims to increase competition, efficiency, and economic growth. However, it can also lead to negative consequences such as environmental damage or exploitation of workers. |例句：The government is deregulating the telecommunications industry, leading to increased competition and lower prices. 政府正在放松对电信行业的管制，导致竞争加剧和价格下降。

例句：Deregulating the financial markets led to the 2008 financial crisis. 放松金融市场管制导致了2008年的金融危机。


近义词：liberalizing, freeing, unshackling, decontrolling

反义词：regulating, controlling, overseeing, supervising
