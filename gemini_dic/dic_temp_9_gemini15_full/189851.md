samba'd:/sæmˈbɑːd/| v.|跳桑巴舞|The past tense of "samba," meaning to dance the samba.  It describes an action completed in the past.|例句：She samba'd all night at the carnival. 她整个晚上都在狂欢节上跳桑巴舞。 They samba'd with infectious energy. 他们充满感染力地跳着桑巴舞。|近义词：danced (the samba), swayed, moved rhythmically|反义词：remained still, sat


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I samba every Saturday. = 我每个星期六都跳桑巴舞。
He sambas with great passion. = 他充满激情地跳桑巴舞。

一般过去时:
We samba'd until dawn. = 我们跳桑巴舞跳到黎明。
She samba'd beautifully at the party. = 她在派对上跳了一曲优美的桑巴舞。

现在完成时:
I have samba'd many times in Rio. = 我在里约热内卢跳过很多次桑巴舞。
They have samba'd since they were children. = 从小他们就跳桑巴舞。

过去完成时:
We had samba'd for hours before we got tired. = 我们跳了几个小时的桑巴舞才感到疲倦。
She had never samba'd before that night. = 在那天晚上之前，她从未跳过桑巴舞。

将来时:
I will samba at the festival next week. = 我下周将在节日上跳桑巴舞。
They will samba until the music stops. = 他们会一直跳桑巴舞直到音乐停止。

虚拟语气:
If I could samba, I would. = 如果我会跳桑巴舞，我会跳。
I wish I could samba like her. = 我希望我能像她一样跳桑巴舞。

被动语态:
(The verb "samba" is rarely used in the passive voice because it's an intransitive verb.)  A more appropriate example might be: The samba was danced with great enthusiasm. = 桑巴舞跳得非常热情。
The music inspired many to samba. = 音乐激励许多人跳桑巴舞。


疑问句:

Will you samba tonight? = 你今晚会跳桑巴舞吗？

否定句:
I didn't samba last night. = 我昨晚没跳桑巴舞。
He doesn't usually samba. = 他通常不跳桑巴舞。
