explorers:/ɪkˈsplɔːrərz/ | n. |探险家们|People who explore unknown or unfamiliar areas, especially geographically.  Explorers often face challenges and risks in their pursuit of discovery and knowledge. They may be motivated by scientific curiosity, a desire for adventure, or a combination of both.|例句：The early explorers faced many hardships during their voyages. 早期探险家们在航海过程中面临许多苦难。

例句：Many explorers have contributed greatly to our understanding of the world. 许多探险家极大地促进了我们对世界的了解。

近义词：discoverers, adventurers, pioneers, investigators

反义词：settlers (in the sense of establishing permanent settlements rather than exploration)
