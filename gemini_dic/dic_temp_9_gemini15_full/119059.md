metonic:/mɪˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.| 按月球运行周期计算的；梅东尼克的 | Relating to or denoting a cycle of 19 years, in which the phases of the moon recur on approximately the same dates, used in the calculation of the dates of religious festivals.  |The Metonic cycle is used to determine the date of Easter.  梅东尼克周期用于确定复活节的日期。|近义词：lunar, cyclical |反义词：solar
