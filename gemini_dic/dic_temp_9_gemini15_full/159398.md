polaric:/poʊˈlærɪk/| adj.| 极性的；两极的；有极性的 | Relating to or having poles or polarity.  The term often refers to molecules with a positive and negative end, resulting in a dipole moment. | Example: Water is a polaric molecule because of its bent shape and the difference in electronegativity between oxygen and hydrogen.  水是极性分子，因为它的形状弯曲，并且氧和氢的电负性不同。|近义词：dipolar, polar |反义词：nonpolar, apolar
