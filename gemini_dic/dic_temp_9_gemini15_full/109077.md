legates:/ˈlɛɡɪts/| n.|代表；使节；特使|A legate is a person sent by the Pope or another high church official to represent them.  It can also refer to a representative or envoy from one state to another.|例句：The papal legate arrived in Rome to represent the Pope. 教宗特使抵达罗马代表教宗。The ambassador acted as a legate between the two countries. 大使充当了两国之间的代表。|近义词：envoy, ambassador, representative, emissary|反义词：None specifically|名词

一般现在时:
The pope appoints legates to various countries. = 教宗任命特使前往不同的国家。
He serves as a legate for international affairs. = 他担任国际事务特使。


一般过去时:
The legate delivered a message from the Vatican. = 特使传达了梵蒂冈的信息。
A special legate was sent to negotiate a peace treaty. = 特别派遣了一位特使去谈判和平条约。


现在完成时:
The church has sent many legates throughout history. = 教会历史上派遣过许多特使。
The government has appointed a new legate to the United Nations. = 政府任命了一位新的联合国特使。


过去完成时:
Before the war, the legate had already left the country. = 在战争爆发前，特使已经离开了这个国家。
They had discussed the matter with the legate before making a decision. = 在做出决定之前，他们已经与特使讨论过此事。


将来时:
The president will send a legate to attend the summit. = 总统将派遣一位特使参加峰会。
A new legate will be appointed next month. = 下个月将任命一位新的特使。


虚拟语气:
If I were a legate, I would try to promote peace. = 如果我是特使，我会努力促进和平。
I wish the legate had arrived earlier. = 我希望特使早点到达。


被动语态:
The legate was warmly welcomed by the people. = 特使受到了人民的热烈欢迎。
The decision was made by the legate after careful consideration. = 在仔细考虑后，特使做出了决定。


疑问句:
Who is the legate representing the organization? = 代表该组织的特使是谁？
When will the legate return from his mission? = 特使什么时候会完成他的任务返回？


否定句:
He was not a legate of the church. = 他不是教会的特使。
The country did not send a legate to the conference. = 这个国家没有派遣特使参加会议。


PHRASE->
papal legate: 教宗特使
apostolic legate: 宗座特使
legate of the pope: 教宗的特使
special legate: 特别特使
plenipotentiary legate: 全权特使


词源:
From Late Latin *legatus, from legare "to send," "to appoint," "to commission."  The word's meaning evolved from simply "one who is sent" to the more specialized meaning of a diplomatic representative with high authority.
