equities:/ˈɛkwɪtiz/| n.| 股票；权益；公平；公正| Shares representing ownership in a corporation.  Equities are often traded on stock exchanges.  The term also refers to the value of one's assets minus liabilities (net worth).  In a broader sense, it can mean fairness and justice.|例句：He invested a significant portion of his savings in equities. 他把他大部分的积蓄都投资到股票上了。

例句：The court ruled in favor of equities, ensuring a fair settlement. 法院判决支持公平原则，确保达成公平的和解。|近义词：stocks, shares, ownership, fairness, justice|反义词：liabilities, injustice, unfairness|词性:名词
