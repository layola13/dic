jump-aboard:/dʒʌmp əˈbɔːrd/ | phrasal verb | 欣然加入；积极参与 | To enthusiastically join or participate in something.  It implies immediate and eager engagement. | 例句：I'm excited about the new project; I'm jumping aboard! = 我对这个新项目很兴奋；我欣然加入！


例句：Many investors jumped aboard the bandwagon after seeing the company's impressive growth. = 许多投资者在看到公司令人印象深刻的增长后就积极参与进来。


近义词：join in, participate, sign up, get on board


反义词：opt out, withdraw, decline, refuse


词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They jump aboard new trends quickly. = 他们很快就能接受新的潮流。
He jumps aboard any opportunity to learn. = 他抓住任何学习的机会。


一般过去时:
She jumped aboard the campaign immediately. = 她立即加入了这场运动。
We jumped aboard the project last week. = 我们上周加入了这个项目。


现在完成时:
Many people have jumped aboard the sustainability movement. = 许多人已经加入了可持续发展运动。
They have jumped aboard the new technology. = 他们已经采用了这项新技术。


过去完成时:
Before the deadline, they had jumped aboard the project. = 在截止日期之前，他们已经加入了该项目。
I realised I had jumped aboard a sinking ship. = 我意识到我加入了一个正在沉没的船。


将来时:
They will jump aboard the next big thing. = 他们将加入下一个热门事物。
I think many people will jump aboard the new initiative. = 我认为许多人将加入这项新倡议。


虚拟语气:
If he knew about it earlier, he would have jumped aboard. = 如果他早点知道，他会加入的。
I wish I had jumped aboard that investment opportunity. = 我真希望我抓住那个投资机会。


被动语态:
The new policy was jumped aboard by many businesses. = 许多企业都欣然接受了这项新政策。(注意：被动语态略显不自然，很少使用)
The project was quickly jumped aboard by the team. = 该项目很快就被团队欣然接受。(注意：被动语态略显不自然，很少使用)


疑问句:
Will you jump aboard the project? = 你会加入这个项目吗？
Are you going to jump aboard the new initiative? = 你会加入这项新倡议吗？


否定句:
He didn't jump aboard the bandwagon. = 他没有加入潮流。
They chose not to jump aboard the risky venture. = 他们选择不参与那项风险投资。


PHRASE->
jump aboard the bandwagon = 加入潮流

jump aboard the train = 加入运动/趋势
jump aboard the project = 加入项目
jump aboard the new technology = 采用新技术
