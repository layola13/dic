free-market: /ˌfriːˈmɑːrkɪt/ | n. |自由市场|An economic system in which prices are determined by unrestricted competition between privately owned businesses, and by supply and demand.|例句：The free-market principles are often challenged in times of economic crisis. 自由市场原则在经济危机时期经常受到挑战。|近义词：open market, competitive market |反义词：command economy, planned economy
