fossores:/fɒˈsɔːriːz/| n.|穴居动物；掘穴动物|Animals that dig burrows or nests in the ground.  They are often solitary and insectivorous.  The term is commonly used in zoology and entomology.|例句：Many fossorial insects, like certain species of wasps, create elaborate underground nests.|近义词：burrowing animals, digging animals|反义词：arboreal animals (tree-dwelling animals)
