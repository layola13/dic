ringing:/ˈrɪŋɪŋ/ | adj. | 响亮的；嗡嗡作响的 |  Describing a sound that is clear, resonant, and continues for a period of time. It can also refer to a sensation in the ears. |例句：The ringing of the church bells echoed through the valley. 教堂的钟声回荡在山谷中。


The phone was ringing incessantly. 电话不停地响着。|近义词：resonant, sonorous, clanging, buzzing |反义词：silent, muted, dull


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bell is ringing. = 铃声响了。
The phone is ringing constantly. = 电话一直在响。


一般过去时:
The alarm clock rang loudly this morning. = 今天早上闹钟响得很厉害。
The bell rang, signaling the end of class. = 铃声响了，表示下课了。


现在完成时:
The phone has been ringing all morning. = 电话整个上午都在响。
The church bells have rung throughout the ceremony. = 教堂的钟声在整个仪式中都响着。


过去完成时:
The phone had rung several times before I answered it. = 我接电话之前，电话已经响了好几次了。
The bell had rung just before the teacher started speaking. = 老师开始说话之前，铃声刚刚响过。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If the phone rang, I would answer it. = 如果电话响了，我会接。
I wish the ringing would stop. = 我希望铃声能停下来。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
