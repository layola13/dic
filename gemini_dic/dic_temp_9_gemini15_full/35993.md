codeveloped:/ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləpt/| v. |合作开发|To develop something jointly; to create something in collaboration with others.  |Example: The two companies codeveloped a new software application.  两家公司合作开发了一个新的软件应用程序。|近义词：collaborated on, jointly developed, co-created |反义词：developed independently, individually developed |动词
