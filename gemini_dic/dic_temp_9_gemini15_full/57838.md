dressiest:/ˈdrɛsiɪst/| adj. |最时髦的，最讲究穿着的 |Most stylish or elegant in dress.  It is the superlative form of "dressy."|例句：She wore her dressiest outfit to the gala. 她穿着最漂亮的衣服参加了盛典。

例句：He always looks dressiest in a suit. 他穿西装总是最帅气的。


近义词：most stylish, most elegant, most fashionable, most sophisticated

反义词：sloppiest, shabbiest, least stylish


词性：形容词
