oviparous:/oʊˈvɪpərəs/ | adj. | 卵生的；产卵的 |  Producing eggs that develop and hatch outside the body.  Animals that lay eggs are called oviparous.  | 例句：Many reptiles are oviparous, laying their eggs in nests or burying them in the ground. 许多爬行动物是卵生的，它们把蛋产在巢穴里或埋在地下。

例句：Birds are oviparous animals. 鸟类是卵生动物。


近义词：egg-laying


反义词：viviparous, ovoviviparous


词源:  From Latin *oviparus, from ovum "egg" + parere "to bring forth".
