riparian:/rɪˈpeəriən/| adj.| 河岸的；河边的 | Relating to or situated on the banks of a river.  |例句：The riparian vegetation helps to prevent erosion. 河岸植被有助于防止侵蚀。|例句：We walked along the riparian path. 我们沿着河岸小路走。|近义词：riverbank, riverside, littoral |反义词：inland
