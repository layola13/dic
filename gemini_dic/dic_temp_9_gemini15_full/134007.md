nonhospital: /ˌnɑːnˈhɑːspɪtl/ | adj. |非医院的；院外的| Not related to or located in a hospital.  It describes something that exists or occurs outside of a hospital setting. |例句：She received nonhospital care after her surgery. 她手术后接受了非医院护理。

例句：The nonhospital-based physician provided excellent care.  这位非医院的医生提供了极好的护理。


近义词：extra-hospital, out-of-hospital, community-based


反义词：hospital, in-hospital


词性：形容词
