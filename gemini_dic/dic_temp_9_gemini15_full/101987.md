isochoric:/ˌaɪsoʊˈkɔːrɪk/| adj.|等容的，容积不变的|Relating to or characterized by constant volume; occurring at constant volume.  Isochoric processes are those where the volume remains constant throughout the process.  This often happens in a closed, rigid container. |Example:  The isochoric heating of the gas increased its pressure. 等容加热气体增加了它的压力。 The experiment was conducted under isochoric conditions. 实验在等容条件下进行。|近义词：isovolumetric|反义词：isobaric, isothermal

USAGE->
This word is primarily used in physics and chemistry, and doesn't have verb conjugations or tenses like "What" does.  It is an adjective describing a process or system.  Therefore, standard verb tenses are not applicable.
