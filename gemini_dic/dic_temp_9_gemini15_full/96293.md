indigene:/ˈɪndɪdʒən/| adj. n.|土著的；土著居民|Indigenous peoples are those who have a historical connection to a particular place.|例句：The indigenous population of Australia includes Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples.澳大利亚的土著居民包括原住民和托雷斯海峡岛民。|近义词：native, aboriginal, autochthonous|反义词：foreigner, immigrant
