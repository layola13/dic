gibes:/ɡaɪbz/| n. v.|嘲讽；挖苦；戏弄|To make fun of someone in a teasing way; to taunt.|例句：The children gibed at the old man walking down the street. 孩子们嘲笑走在街上的老人。  He gibed at her about her new haircut. 他嘲笑她的新发型。|近义词：taunts, jeers, sneers, mocks|反义词：praises, compliments
