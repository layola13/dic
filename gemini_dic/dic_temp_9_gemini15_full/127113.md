nationalness:/ˌnæʃənəlˈnəs/| n. |民族性；国民性；国家特性|The feeling of belonging to a particular nation; the characteristics of a nation.|例句：The war intensified the nationalness of the people.  战争加剧了人民的民族感情。

例句：Their nationalness is expressed through their unique culture and traditions. 他们的民族性通过他们独特的文化和传统表达出来。


近义词：nationality, patriotism, national identity|反义词：internationalism, cosmopolitanism


词性：名词
