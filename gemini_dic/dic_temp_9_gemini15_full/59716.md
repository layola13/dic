eclectic:/ɛˈklɛktɪk/| adj.|折衷的；兼容并蓄的；选择而成的 |  Deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad and diverse range of sources.  Characterized by a variety of styles or tastes. |例句：Her eclectic taste in music ranged from classical to hip-hop.她的音乐品味兼容并蓄，从古典音乐到嘻哈音乐都有。

例句：The museum's eclectic collection included everything from ancient artifacts to modern art.  博物馆的收藏包罗万象，从古代文物到现代艺术应有尽有。


近义词：diverse, varied, heterogeneous, cosmopolitan
