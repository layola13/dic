prettied:/ˈprɛtid/| v.|使漂亮；打扮漂亮；美化|To make something or someone more attractive or beautiful.  It is often used in the past tense and is less formal than other verbs with similar meanings.|例句：She prettied herself up for the party. 她为了聚会打扮得漂漂亮亮的。|例句：He prettied the garden with colorful flowers. 他用色彩鲜艳的花朵美化了花园。|近义词：beautified, adorned, embellished|反义词：disfigured, marred, uglified|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She pretties her apartment with plants. = 她用植物装饰她的公寓。
He pretties up his presentation with images. = 他用图片美化了他的演示文稿。


一般过去时:
She prettied herself up for the date. = 她为了约会打扮得漂漂亮亮的。
They prettied the room with balloons. = 他们用气球装饰了房间。


现在完成时:
She has prettied the house with new curtains. = 她用新的窗帘装饰了房子。
He has prettied up his resume with impressive achievements. = 他用令人印象深刻的成就美化了他的简历。


过去完成时:

They had prettied the garden with flowers before the party. = 聚会前，他们已经用鲜花装饰了花园。

虚拟语气:
