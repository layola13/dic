herbicides:/ˈhɜːrbɪsaɪdz/| n.|除草剂|Chemical substances used to kill unwanted plants (weeds). Herbicides are widely used in agriculture, horticulture, and landscaping to control weed growth and improve crop yields.  They work through various mechanisms, such as interfering with plant growth hormones or photosynthesis.|例句：Farmers often use herbicides to control weeds in their fields. 农民经常使用除草剂来控制田间的杂草。

The overuse of herbicides can harm beneficial insects and pollute the environment. 除草剂的过度使用会危害益虫并污染环境。|近义词：weed killers, weed control agents|反义词：fertilizers (in the context of plant growth), plant growth stimulants
