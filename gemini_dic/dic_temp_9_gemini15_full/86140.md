hearths:/hɑːrθs/| n. |炉膛；壁炉；家；中心 |The hearth is the central focus of the home, traditionally where the family gathers. 炉膛是家的中心，传统上是家人聚集的地方。|近义词：fireplace, home, center |反义词：exterior, outside
