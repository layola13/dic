reverberant: /rɪˈvɜːbərənt/| adj. | 回响的；余音绕梁的 |  Characterized by reverberation; echoing.  The sound was reverberant, lingering in the large hall.  | The vast cathedral had a deeply reverberant acoustic.  The empty warehouse was reverberant with the sounds of our footsteps. | resonant, echoing | muted, silent
