resilver:/rɪˈsɪlvər/| vt.|镀银，再镀银|To coat (something) again with silver.  This typically refers to re-plating an object that has lost its silver coating. |例句：The antique mirror needed to be resilvered to restore its reflectivity.  这面古董镜子需要重新镀银以恢复其反射率。

例句：We decided to resilver the tea set, as the original plating had worn off. 我们决定重新镀银茶具，因为原来的镀层已经磨损了。


近义词：replate, silver-plate

反义词：desilver (to remove silver coating)

词性：动词
