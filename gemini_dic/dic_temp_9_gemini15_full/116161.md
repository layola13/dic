mascots: /ˈmæskɑːts/ | n. |吉祥物，幸运物 | A mascot is a person, animal, or object believed to bring good luck, or is adopted as a symbol of a group, team, or organization.  Mascots are often used to promote a positive image and build a sense of community. | Example: The Olympic Games always have mascots to represent the host country and the spirit of the games.  奥运会总是有吉祥物来代表主办国和奥运精神。  |近义词：symbol, emblem, talisman |反义词：jinx, ill omen
