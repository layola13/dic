rubbings:/ˈrʌbɪŋz/| n.|拓片，摩擦痕迹|Paper or other material with an impression made by rubbing over a raised surface, such as a stone carving or inscription.  The act of rubbing a surface to clean or polish it, or the marks left by such rubbing.|例句：The museum displayed ancient rubbings of Chinese calligraphy.  博物馆展出了古代中国书法的拓片。|例句：The floor showed rubbings where the furniture had been. 地板上留下了家具移动的摩擦痕迹。|近义词：impressions, copies, tracings|反义词：originals, casts
