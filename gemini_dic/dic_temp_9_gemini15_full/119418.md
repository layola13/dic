microform:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfɔːrm/| n.|缩微胶片；缩微制品|A microform is a miniaturized photographic reproduction of printed or written material, such as a book or document, typically stored on film.  Microforms are used to conserve space and preserve fragile documents. |例句：The library archives many historical documents on microform. 图书馆以缩微胶片的形势保存了许多历史文献。|例句：Researchers used a microform reader to access the archived documents. 研究人员使用缩微胶片阅读器来访问存档文件。|近义词：microfiche, microfilm |反义词：full-size, original
