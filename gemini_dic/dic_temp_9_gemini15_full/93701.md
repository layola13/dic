imitativenesses:/ɪˌmɪtətɪvnəsɪz/| n.|模仿性；模仿行为的复数形式|The plural form of "imitativeness," referring to multiple instances of the tendency or act of imitating.  It describes the various ways in which imitation manifests itself. |例句：The teacher observed several distinct imitativenesses among the students, ranging from mimicking speech patterns to copying artistic styles.  老师观察到学生中有几种不同的模仿行为，从模仿说话方式到复制艺术风格不等。|近义词：mimicries, copies, reproductions|反义词：originality, innovation
