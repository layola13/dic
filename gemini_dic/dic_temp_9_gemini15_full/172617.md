pyrotechnists:/ˌpaɪrəʊˈteknɪsts/| n. |烟火技师，烟火专家|A pyrotechnician is a person who designs, makes, or sets off fireworks.  They are skilled in the art and science of pyrotechnics, understanding the chemical reactions and properties of explosive materials.  Their work requires precision, safety awareness, and a deep understanding of the effects of different pyrotechnic compositions.|例句：The skilled pyrotechnists created a breathtaking fireworks display.  经验丰富的烟火技师创造了一场令人叹为观止的烟火表演。

The pyrotechnists carefully prepared the fireworks for the festival. 烟火技师们仔细地为节日准备烟火。

|近义词：fireworks expert, fireworks technician|反义词：无|词性：名词
