declass:/dɪˈklæs/ | vt. |降低级别；使降级；使失去等级 | To reduce someone or something to a lower rank or class; to demote.  |例句：The general was declassified for his misconduct.  将军因行为不端而被降级。|例句：The company declassified the sensitive information. 公司将敏感信息解密。|近义词：demote, downgrade, reduce, relegate |反义词：promote, upgrade, elevate


USAGE->

They declassify sensitive documents regularly. = 他们定期解密敏感文件。
The government declassifies information when it is no longer a threat. = 当信息不再构成威胁时，政府会将其解密。
