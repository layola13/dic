co-attest:/ˌkoʊ əˈtɛst/ | vt. |共同证明，互相证明 |To jointly attest to the truth or validity of something.  It implies a mutual confirmation or corroboration. |例句：The two witnesses co-attested to the defendant's innocence. = 两名证人共同证明被告无罪。  The documents co-attested the authenticity of the transaction. = 文件共同证明了交易的真实性。|近义词：corroborate, confirm, verify |反义词：deny, refute, contradict |动词
