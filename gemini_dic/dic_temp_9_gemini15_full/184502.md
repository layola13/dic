restates:/ˌriːˈsteɪts/| vt.| 重述；重新声明 | To state something again, often in a different or clearer way.  | The lawyer restated his client's innocence. 律师重申了他客户的清白。 | The teacher restated the instructions more slowly. 老师放慢速度重新说明了指示。 | reiterate, rephrase | contradict, deny | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He restates his position clearly every time. 他每次都清楚地重申他的立场。
She restates the main points at the end of the presentation. 她在演示文稿结尾处重述要点。

一般过去时:
The witness restated his testimony. 证人重述了他的证词。
The politician restated his commitment to the people. 这位政治家重申了他对人民的承诺。

现在完成时:
He has restated his support for the project. 他已经重申了他对这个项目的支持。
She has restated her intentions to run for office. 她已经重申了她竞选公职的意图。

过去完成时:
He had restated his position before the meeting ended. 他在会议结束前已经重申了他的立场。
She had restated her concerns several times. 她已经多次重申了她的担忧。

将来时:
He will restate his argument in the next chapter. 他将在下一章中重述他的论点。
She will restate the key findings in her conclusion. 她将在结论中重述主要发现。

虚拟语气:
If he restated his claim, they might believe him. 如果他重申他的说法，他们可能会相信他。
I wish he had restated his position more clearly. 我希望他更清楚地重申了他的立场。

被动语态:
His position was restated by the spokesperson. 他的立场由发言人重申。
The rules were restated in simpler language. 这些规则用更简单的语言重新说明。

疑问句:
Will he restate his opinion? 他会重申他的意见吗？
Did she restate the facts accurately? 她是否准确地重述了事实？

否定句:
He did not restate his objections. 他没有重申他的反对意见。
She didn't restate the question. 她没有重述问题。
