electrobiologist:/ɪˌlɛktrəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 电生物学家 | A scientist who studies the interaction between electricity and living organisms.  Electrobiologists investigate the electrical properties of biological systems, such as nerve impulses, muscle contractions, and bioelectricity generated by certain organisms.  | Example: The electrobiologist presented her findings on the electrical signals in the brain of a specific species of fish. 电生物学家展示了她对某种鱼类大脑中电信号的研究结果。 | 近义词: biophysicist, neuroscientist (depending on the specific area of research) | 反义词: N/A
