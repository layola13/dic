ovigerons:/ˈoʊvɪdʒərɒnz/| n. | 卵巢, 孵卵器 |  Ovigerons are specialized structures in certain crustaceans, typically female, used for carrying eggs until they hatch. They may be modified appendages or other body parts.  | Example: The female crab protects her eggs within her ovigerons until they are ready to hatch.  = 雌性螃蟹将卵保护在孵卵器中，直到它们准备好孵化。| 近义词：brood pouch, egg sac | 反义词：无
