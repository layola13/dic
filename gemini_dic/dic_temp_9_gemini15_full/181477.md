rejolt:/riˈdʒoʊlt/ | vt. |使再次震惊；再次猛烈摇动|To shock or jolt again; to shake violently again.|例句：The news of the earthquake rejolted the already traumatized community.  地震的消息再次震惊了这个已经受到创伤的社区。
例句：The sudden noise rejolted the sleeping baby. 突如其来的噪音再次惊醒了熟睡的婴儿。|近义词：reshock, shake up, jar |反义词：soothe, calm, tranquilize |动词
