fidgets:/ˈfɪdʒɪts/| n. v.|烦躁不安；坐立不安；拨弄；摆弄|to make small movements, especially because you are nervous or bored|The children fidgeted in their seats, eager for the movie to begin. 孩子们在座位上坐立不安，急切地等待电影开始。|restless, uneasy|calm, still|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He fidgets with his pen during meetings. 他在会议期间不停地拨弄他的钢笔。
She constantly fidgets when she's nervous. 她紧张的时候总是坐立不安。


一般过去时:
He fidgeted nervously during the interview. 他在面试期间紧张地坐立不安。
She fidgeted with her necklace the whole evening. 她整个晚上都在拨弄她的项链。


现在完成时:
He has fidgeted all day because of the exam. 他因为考试而整天都坐立不安。
She has been fidgeting with her hair since she got the bad news. 自从收到坏消息以来，她一直在拨弄她的头发。


过去完成时:
He had fidgeted through the entire presentation before finally speaking up. 他在最终发言前，整个演示过程中一直坐立不安。
She had fidgeted for hours waiting for her flight. 她等航班已经焦急地等待了好几个小时。


将来时:
He will fidget throughout the long flight. 他在漫长的飞行中将会坐立不安。
She will probably fidget during the ceremony. 她很可能在仪式期间会坐立不安。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't so nervous, I wouldn't fidget so much. 如果我不那么紧张，我就不会这么坐立不安了。
I wish I didn't fidget so much during presentations. 我希望我在演示期间不要那么坐立不安。


被动语态:
(The noun form "fidgets" is rarely used in the passive voice.  The verb form can be, but it's uncommon and usually requires a different phrasing.)  His nervousness was evident in the way he fidgeted. 他的紧张从他坐立不安的样子中显而易见。
The audience's restlessness was apparent as they fidgeted in their seats. 观众们在座位上坐立不安，他们的焦躁不安显而易见。


疑问句:
Does he always fidget like that? 他总是那样坐立不安吗？
Why is she fidgeting so much? 她为什么这么坐立不安？


否定句:
He didn't fidget at all during the speech. 他在演讲期间一点也不坐立不安。
She didn't fidget; she remained calm and composed. 她没有坐立不安；她保持冷静和镇定。
