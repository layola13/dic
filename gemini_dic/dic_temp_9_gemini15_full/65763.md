every-man-jack:/ˈɛvri mæn dʒæk/| pron.|每一个人，所有人|This phrase emphasizes inclusiveness, suggesting absolutely everyone without exception. It's an informal and somewhat emphatic way of saying "everyone."  The origin is uncertain but possibly relates to the common usage of "Jack" as a generic name representing a common man.|例句：Every man jack of them agreed to the plan. 他们每一个人都同意了这个计划。|近义词：everyone, everybody, each and every one|反义词：no one, nobody
