princessly: /ˈprɪnsɛsli/ | adj. | 公主般地，像公主一样地 |  Having the qualities or mannerisms associated with a princess; in a princess-like way.  It implies grace, elegance, refinement, and often a touch of privilege or aloofness. |

例句：

She moved princessly across the ballroom floor. = 她像公主一样优雅地穿过舞厅。

She dressed princessly in a flowing gown. = 她穿着飘逸的长袍，打扮得像公主一样。


近义词：royally, regally, elegantly, gracefully, delicately


反义词：crudely, roughly, ungracefully, clumsily


词性：形容词
