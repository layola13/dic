reenvisioning: /ˌriːɪnˈvɪʒənɪŋ/ | v. | 重新构想；重新设想 | The act of reconsidering and changing one's ideas or plans about something.  It involves a fundamental shift in perspective and a creation of a new vision. | 例句：The company is reenvisioning its marketing strategy to attract a younger demographic. = 公司正在重新构想其营销策略以吸引更年轻的人群。

The city council is reenvisioning the downtown area, incorporating green spaces and pedestrian-friendly designs. = 市议会正在重新设想市中心区域，融入绿地和步行友好的设计。


近义词：reimagining, reconceptualizing, rethinking, redesigning | 反义词：maintaining, preserving, sticking to
