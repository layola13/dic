green-thumbed:/ˌɡriːnˈθʌmd/| adj.|  绿拇指的；擅长园艺的 | Having a natural talent for growing plants.  Someone who is green-thumbed is skilled at gardening and has plants that thrive under their care. |  He's incredibly green-thumbed; his garden is always bursting with vibrant flowers. 他非常擅长园艺；他的花园总是盛开着鲜艳的花朵。 |  skilled gardener, adept gardener |  black thumb (反义词,指不擅长园艺)
