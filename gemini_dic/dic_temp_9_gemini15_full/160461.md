populator:/ˈpɑpjuleɪtər/ | n. |填充者；使人口稠密者；定居者 |One who populates; someone or something that fills a place or thing.  A populator can refer to a person who settles in a new area, or something that fills a space or database. |例句：The government implemented policies to attract populators to the sparsely inhabited region. 政府实施政策以吸引居民到人口稀少的地区定居。|例句：The new software acts as a populator, filling the database with relevant information. 新软件充当填充器，用相关信息填充数据库。|近义词：settler, colonizer, filler, inhabiter |反义词：depopulator, evacuator
