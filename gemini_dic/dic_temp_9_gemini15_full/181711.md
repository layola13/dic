relevy:/ˈriːlɛvi/| v.|再征税，重新征税|To levy a tax again; to impose a tax a second time.  This word is relatively rare and often found in legal or historical contexts.|例句：The government decided to relevy the unpopular head tax. 政府决定重新征收不受欢迎的人头税。|例句：The estate was re-levied after an error in the initial assessment.  在最初评估出现错误后，对该遗产进行了重新征税。|近义词：reimpose, reassess|反义词：remove, abolish|动词
