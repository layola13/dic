fleur-de-lis: /ˌflɜːr dəˈliːs/| n. |百合花；鸢尾花；法国王室徽章|The fleur-de-lis is a stylized lily, often used as a symbol of royalty and French culture.  It's a heraldic symbol with a long and rich history, appearing on coats of arms, flags, and various other forms of decorative art.  The design varies slightly depending on the historical period and specific context.|The fleur-de-lis adorned the king's banner.  法国国王的旗帜上装饰着百合花图案。|lily, iris| (no direct antonym, but could be described as opposites of its symbolic meanings:  rebellion, revolution)|名词
