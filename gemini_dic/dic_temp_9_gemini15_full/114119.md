maguey:/ˈmæɡeɪ/| n.|龙舌兰|A type of succulent plant, native to Mexico and Central America, from which tequila and mezcal are made.  The plant is also used for fiber, food, and medicinal purposes.|Example: The maguey plant is a vital part of Mexican culture and economy. 龙舌兰植物是墨西哥文化和经济的重要组成部分。|近义词：century plant, agave (agave is often used interchangeably with maguey, though technically maguey refers to a specific group of agave species)|反义词：无|名词
