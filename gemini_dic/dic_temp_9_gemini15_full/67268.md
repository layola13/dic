exquisitely:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪtli/| adv.|精巧地；精致地；细腻地 |In a very beautiful and delicate way.|The cake was exquisitely decorated.  这个蛋糕装饰得非常精美。|The painting was exquisitely rendered. 这幅画描绘得非常细腻。| beautifully, delicately, finely, perfectly |crudely, roughly, poorly, clumsily |副词
