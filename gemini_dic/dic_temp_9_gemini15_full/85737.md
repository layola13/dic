hawkey:/ˈhɔːki/| n.|鹰眼；(尤指)警惕的人；(美国)爱荷华州居民|A hawkey is a keen-sighted person, often used to describe someone who is vigilant and observant.  The term also refers to a resident of Iowa, USA, due to the state's nickname, the Hawkeye State.|例句：He had the hawkey eye of a seasoned detective. 他拥有经验丰富的侦探那样的锐利目光。|例句：Many hawkeyes came to the state fair. 许多爱荷华州居民来到了州博览会。|近义词：sharp-eyed, vigilant, observant|反义词：blind, unobservant, careless
