prequalifies: /ˌpriːˈkwɒləfaɪz/ | v. |预先鉴定合格；预先批准 | To meet the requirements for something in advance; to be approved or accepted before a formal application or process.  | 例句：The applicant prequalifies for the loan based on her credit score.  基于她的信用评分，申请人预先获得了贷款资格。

The company prequalifies all potential vendors before entering into contracts. 公司在签订合同之前会预先审核所有潜在供应商的资质。


近义词：pre-approve, pre-screen, qualify in advance, vet


反义词：disqualify, reject


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prequalifies for the scholarship. = 他符合奖学金的资格。
She prequalifies for the mortgage. = 她符合抵押贷款的资格。


一般过去时:
The project prequalified last year. = 该项目去年预先获得了资格。
They prequalified all the applicants. = 他们预先审核了所有申请人。


现在完成时:
We have prequalified several candidates. = 我们已经预先审核了几位候选人。
The bank has prequalified her for a loan. = 银行已经预先批准了她贷款。


过去完成时:
They had prequalified the contractors before the bidding process. = 在招标过程之前，他们已经预先审核了承包商。
She had prequalified for the marathon months before the race. = 她在比赛前几个月就已经预先获得了马拉松比赛的资格。


将来时:
They will prequalify the applicants next week. = 他们下周将预先审核申请人。
The committee will prequalify the proposals before the final selection. = 委员会将在最终选择之前预先审核提案。


虚拟语气:
If they prequalified, they would have a better chance of winning the contract. = 如果他们预先获得资格，他们将有更大的机会赢得合同。
I wish I had prequalified for the grant. = 我希望我之前就获得了该助学金的资格。


被动语态:
The applicants were prequalified by the committee. = 申请人由委员会预先审核。
The vendors will be prequalified before the contract is awarded. = 在签订合同之前，供应商将被预先审核。


疑问句:
Does she prequalify for the job? = 她符合这份工作的资格吗？
Have they prequalified the applicants yet? = 他们已经预先审核申请人了吗？


否定句:
He did not prequalify for the loan. = 他没有获得贷款资格。
The project did not prequalify for funding. = 该项目没有获得资金资格。


PHRASE->

prequalify for an award = 预先获得奖项资格


词源:  The word "prequalifies" is formed from the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") and the verb "qualifies" (meaning "to meet the requirements").  Therefore, it literally means "to qualify beforehand."
