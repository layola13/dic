freely:/ˈfriːli/| adv.|自由地，随意地，无拘无束地|In a free manner; without restriction or hindrance.  It can also imply without cost or payment. |例句：The birds flew freely in the sky.  鸟儿自由自在地飞翔在天空。
例句：He gave the book to me freely. 他免费给了我这本书。|近义词：openly, readily, voluntarily, gratis|反义词：constrainedly, reluctantly, unwillingly

USAGE->
一般现在时:

Birds freely fly in the sky. = 鸟儿自由地飞翔在天空。
