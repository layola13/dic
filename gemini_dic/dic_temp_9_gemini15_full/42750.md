countries:/ˈkʌntriz/ | n. |国家们，各国 |Countries are political entities with defined territories and governments.| 例句：Many countries participated in the international conference. 许多国家参加了国际会议。  | 例句：The United Nations comprises nearly two hundred countries. 联合国由近两百个国家组成。|近义词：nations, states |反义词：None (反义词的概念不适用于此)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The United States exports many products to other countries. = 美国向其他国家出口许多产品。
Many countries rely heavily on tourism. = 许多国家严重依赖旅游业。

一般过去时:
Several countries joined forces during the war. = 几个国家在战争期间联合起来。
My family visited three countries last year. = 我家去年访问了三个国家。

现在完成时:
The organization has helped many countries develop their economies. = 该组织帮助许多国家发展了经济。
Several countries have signed the agreement. = 几个国家已经签署了该协议。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, many countries had already submitted their proposals. = 会议之前，许多国家已经提交了提案。
By the time the war ended, several countries had suffered significant losses. = 到战争结束时，几个国家已经遭受了重大损失。

将来时:
We will visit several European countries next year. = 我们明年将访问几个欧洲国家。
Many countries will be affected by climate change. = 许多国家将受到气候变化的影响。

虚拟语气:
If all countries cooperated, we could solve many global issues. = 如果所有国家都合作，我们就能解决许多全球性问题。
I wish all countries had better relations. = 我希望所有国家都有更好的关系。

被动语态:
Many countries were affected by the global financial crisis. = 许多国家受到了全球金融危机的冲击。
The agreement was signed by several countries. = 该协议由几个国家签署。

疑问句:
Which countries are involved in the conflict? = 哪些国家卷入了这场冲突？
How many countries are members of the EU? =有多少个国家是欧盟成员国？

否定句:
Not all countries support the initiative. =并非所有国家都支持这项倡议。
The company does not operate in all countries. =该公司并非在所有国家开展业务。
