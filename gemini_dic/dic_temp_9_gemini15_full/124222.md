mosques:/mɒskz/| n.|清真寺|A mosque is a Muslim place of worship.  It's a building where Muslims gather for prayer, religious instruction, and community events.  Mosques often feature a minaret (a tall, slender tower) from which the call to prayer is made.|例句：Many beautiful mosques are located in Istanbul.  伊斯坦布尔有很多美丽的清真寺。

The Grand Mosque of Mecca is the holiest site in Islam. 麦加大清真寺是伊斯兰教最神圣的场所。|近义词：temple, church, synagogue|反义词：None
