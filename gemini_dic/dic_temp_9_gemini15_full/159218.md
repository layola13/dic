poes:/poʊz/| n.|波斯语；诗歌；诗集|Poems are literary works written in verse.  诗歌是写成诗句的文学作品。|近义词：verse, poetry|反义词：prose


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He writes poems in his spare time. = 他在业余时间写诗。
She reads poems every night before bed. = 她每天晚上睡觉前读诗。


一般过去时:
He wrote a beautiful poem for his wife. = 他为妻子写了一首美丽的诗。
She read several poems aloud at the event. = 她在活动上朗读了几首诗。


现在完成时:
He has written many poems throughout his career. = 他在他职业生涯中写了很多诗。
She has read all of Poe's poems. = 她读过所有爱伦·坡的诗歌。


过去完成时:
He had written several poems before publishing his first collection. = 在出版他的第一本诗集之前，他已经写了几首诗。
She had read that poem many times before. = 她之前已经读过很多次那首诗了。


将来时:
He will write a new poem for the competition. = 他将为比赛写一首新诗。
She will read her poems at the open mic night. = 她将在公开麦克风之夜朗诵她的诗歌。


虚拟语气:
If I could write poems, I would. = 如果我能写诗，我会的。
I wish I could write poems as beautifully as she does. = 我希望我能像她一样写出美丽的诗歌。


被动语态:
His poems were published last year. = 他的诗歌去年出版了。
The poems are being read by students in class. = 学生们正在课堂上朗读这些诗歌。


疑问句:
Do you like his poems? = 你喜欢他的诗吗？
Have you ever read any of her poems? = 你读过她的任何诗歌吗？


否定句:
He doesn't write poems very often. = 他不经常写诗。
She didn't understand the meaning of the poem. = 她不明白这首诗的意思。


PHRASE->
a book of poems = 一本诗集
a collection of poems = 一部诗集
read poems = 读诗
write poems = 写诗
publish poems = 出版诗歌
