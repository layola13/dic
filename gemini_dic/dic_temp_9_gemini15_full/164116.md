predispatches:/ˌpriːˈdɪspætʃɪz/ | n. |预先派遣；预先发送|The act of sending something beforehand;  preliminary dispatches.  It often refers to sending messages, troops, or supplies in advance of a main force or event.|例句：The general ordered predispatches of scouts to secure the route. 将军下令预先派遣侦察兵以确保路线安全。|例句：The company sent predispatches of essential equipment to the construction site. 公司预先将必要的设备运送到施工现场。|近义词：advance dispatch, advance shipment, preliminary sending|反义词：postdispatch, delayed sending|名词
