reinterrogated:/ˌriːɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪtɪd/| v.|再次盘问，重新审问|To question someone again, often more thoroughly or from a different perspective.  The police reinterrogated the suspect after new evidence emerged.|近义词：re-examine, question again, cross-examine |反义词：release, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinterrogate witnesses to clarify inconsistencies. = 他们再次盘问证人以澄清前后矛盾之处。
The detective reinterrogates suspects daily. = 侦探每天都会再次审问嫌疑人。


一般过去时:
The lawyer reinterrogated the witness about his alibi. = 律师再次盘问证人关于他的不在场证明。
The judge ordered the suspect to be reinterrogated. = 法官下令再次审问嫌疑人。


现在完成时:
The police have reinterrogated all the key witnesses. = 警方已经再次盘问了所有关键证人。
They have reinterrogated him three times this week. = 他们本周已经三次再次审问他了。


过去完成时:
The investigators had reinterrogated the suspect before releasing him. = 调查人员在释放嫌疑人之前已经再次审问了他。
Having reinterrogated the witness, the officer found a crucial detail. = 在再次盘问证人后，警官发现了一个关键细节。


将来时:
The authorities will reinterrogate the suspect tomorrow. = 当局将在明天再次审问嫌疑人。
They will reinterrogate him under oath next week. = 他们将在下周在宣誓下再次审问他。


虚拟语气:
If they reinterrogated her, perhaps they would find more information. = 如果他们再次审问她，也许会找到更多信息。
It's possible that if they had reinterrogated him earlier, the case would have been solved sooner. = 也许如果他们早点再次审问他，案件就能早点破了。


被动语态:
The suspect was reinterrogated by the police. = 嫌疑人被警方再次审问了。
The witness was reinterrogated after the court recess. = 证人在休庭后被再次盘问了。


疑问句:
Will the suspect be reinterrogated? = 嫌疑人会被再次审问吗？
Has the witness been reinterrogated yet? = 证人已经被再次盘问了吗？


否定句:
The police did not reinterrogate the witness. = 警方没有再次盘问证人。
The suspect was not reinterrogated after the initial questioning. = 嫌疑人在初步问讯后没有被再次审问。
