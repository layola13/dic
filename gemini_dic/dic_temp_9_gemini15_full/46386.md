dads:/dædz/| n.|爸爸们；父亲们|The plural form of "dad," referring to multiple fathers.  It can also refer to a group of men acting as fathers or father figures.|例句：My dads are taking me to the game.  我的爸爸们带我去看比赛。|例句：The dads in the community organized a fundraising event. 社区里的爸爸们组织了一场筹款活动。|近义词：fathers, papas, pops|反义词：无
