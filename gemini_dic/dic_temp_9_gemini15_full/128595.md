nicer:/ˈnaɪsər/| adj.|更好的，更友好的，更令人愉快的|More pleasant, agreeable, or attractive.  It is the comparative form of "nice".|例句：This cake is nicer than the one I made yesterday.  这个蛋糕比我昨天做的那个更好吃。

例句：She's much nicer to her friends than she is to strangers. 她对朋友比对陌生人好得多。


近义词：better, more pleasant, more agreeable, kinder, sweeter

反义词：worse, unpleasant, disagreeable, unkind,crueler

词性：形容词
