half-done:/ˌhæfˈdʌn/| adj.|半成品的；未完成的；|Partially completed; unfinished.  It describes something that is only partly finished.|例句：The project is half-done, and we need to work harder to finish it on time.  这个项目只完成了一半，我们需要更加努力才能按时完成。|例句：The cake is half-done, but it already smells delicious. 蛋糕只烤了一半，但已经闻起来很香了。|近义词：incomplete, unfinished, partial, uncompleted|反义词：complete, finished, perfected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is half-done. = 项目只完成了一半。
The painting is half-done. = 画只画了一半。


一般过去时:
The report was half-done when the deadline arrived. = 截止日期到达时，报告只完成了一半。
The homework was half-done before he went to bed. = 他睡觉前作业只做了一半。


现在完成时:
The assignment has been half-done for a week. = 作业已经完成了一半，持续了一周了。
The building has been half-done for months. = 这栋建筑已经烂尾好几个月了。


过去完成时:
The manuscript had been half-done before he lost his notes. = 他丢失笔记之前，手稿只完成了一半。
The sculpture had been half-done when the accident happened. = 事故发生时，雕塑只完成了一半。


将来时:
The renovations will be half-done by next week. = 到下周为止，装修将完成一半。
The preparations will be half-done by the time the guests arrive. = 客人到达时，准备工作将完成一半。


虚拟语气:
If the proposal had been half-done, we wouldn't have had to start over. = 如果提案已经完成一半，我们就不用重新开始了。
If the meal were half-done, we could still eat it. = 如果饭菜只做了一半，我们仍然可以吃。


被动语态:
The task was half-done by the team. = 任务由团队完成了一半。
The job was half-done before the budget ran out. = 预算用完之前，工作完成了一半。


疑问句:
Is the work half-done? = 工作完成了一半了吗？
Is the song half-done? = 歌曲完成了一半了吗？


否定句:
The job is not half-done; it's entirely finished. = 工作并非只完成了一半，而是全部完成了。
The painting is not half-done; it's almost complete. = 这幅画并非只完成了一半，而是几乎完成了。
