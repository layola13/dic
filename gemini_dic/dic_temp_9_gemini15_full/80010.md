go-over:/ˈɡoʊ.oʊ.vɚ/ | vt. |仔细检查；复习；重温；讨论；袭击|To examine or check something carefully; to review; to rehearse; to discuss thoroughly; to attack suddenly and violently.|例句：The teacher gave the students a go-over of the lesson before the test.老师在考试前给学生们复习了一遍功课。|例句：The police gave the suspect a thorough go-over.警察对嫌疑犯进行了彻底的搜查。|近义词：review, examine, check, rehearse, discuss |反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goes over his notes every evening. = 他每天晚上都复习笔记。
They go over the project plan before starting. = 他们开始之前会仔细检查项目计划。


一般过去时:
She went over the contract carefully. = 她仔细检查了合同。
We went over the presentation several times. = 我们排练了几次演示文稿。


现在完成时:
I have gone over the report three times. = 我已经检查了三次报告。
They have gone over the safety procedures. = 他们已经复习了安全规程。


过去完成时:
He had gone over the material before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经复习过材料了。
She had gone over the script many times. = 她已经排练过很多次剧本了。


将来时:
We will go over the details tomorrow. = 我们明天将详细讨论。
They will go over the budget again next week. = 他们下周将再次审核预算。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would go over the document again. = 如果我有更多时间，我会再次仔细检查这份文件。
I wish I had gone over the instructions more carefully. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地阅读了说明。


被动语态:
The proposal was gone over by the committee. = 该提案已由委员会仔细审查。
The lesson will be gone over again next week. = 这节课下周将再次复习。


疑问句:
Have you gone over the instructions? = 你仔细看过说明了吗？
Will they go over the plan before they start? = 他们开始之前会检查计划吗？


否定句:
He didn't go over the data before submitting the report. = 他在提交报告之前没有检查数据。
She hasn't gone over her speech yet. = 她还没有复习她的演讲。
