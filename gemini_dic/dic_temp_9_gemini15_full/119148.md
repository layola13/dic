mew's:/mjuːz/| n.| 猫叫声；（尤指小猫的）喵喵叫 |The sound a cat makes, especially a kitten.  |例句：I heard a soft mew's from the basket. 我听到篮子里传来轻柔的猫叫声。  |近义词：meow, purr (if a low sound)|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The kitten mews loudly. = 小猫大声地喵喵叫。
The cat mews for food. = 猫咪喵喵叫着要食物。


一般过去时:
The cat mewed all night. = 猫整夜都在喵喵叫。
The kitten mewed pitifully. = 小猫可怜地喵喵叫。


现在完成时:
The cat has mewed incessantly. = 猫一直在不停地叫。
The neighbor's cat has mewed at my door. = 邻居家的猫在我的门前喵喵叫。


过去完成时:
The cat had mewed before I opened the door. = 我开门之前猫就已经喵喵叫了。
The kitten had mewed until it fell asleep. = 小猫喵喵叫到睡着为止。


将来时:
The cat will mew when it's hungry. = 猫饿了就会喵喵叫。
I think the kitten will mew all night. = 我想小猫会叫一整夜。


虚拟语气:
If the cat mewed, I would give it food. = 如果猫叫了，我会给它食物。
I wish the cat wouldn't mew so loudly. = 我希望猫不要叫那么大声。


被动语态:
(Mew is rarely used in passive voice)  无


疑问句:
Does your cat mew often? = 你的猫经常喵喵叫吗？
Why is the kitten mewing? = 小猫为什么在喵喵叫？


否定句:
The cat didn't mew last night. = 猫昨晚没有叫。
The kitten didn't mew after it ate. = 小猫吃过之后就不叫了。


PHRASE->
a soft mew = 轻柔的喵叫
a loud mew = 大声的喵叫
a pitiful mew = 可怜的喵叫
a persistent mew = 持续的喵叫
a hungry mew = 饿了的喵叫
