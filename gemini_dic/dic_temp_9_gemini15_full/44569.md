crouchant:/ˈkraʊtʃɪŋ/| adj.| 蹲伏的；潜伏的 |  Describes a person or animal in a low, crouching position, often suggesting stealth, readiness, or concealment.  | Example: A tiger crouched in the tall grass, ready to pounce.  一只老虎潜伏在高高的草丛中，准备猛扑。  The cat crouched low to the ground, watching the mouse. 猫低伏在地上，注视着老鼠。|近义词：squatting, stooping, lurking, slinking | 反义词：standing, erect, upright |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat crouches near the mouse. = 猫蹲在老鼠附近。
He crouches down to tie his shoelaces. = 他蹲下系鞋带。

一般过去时:
The lion crouched before it sprang. = 狮子在跳跃之前蹲伏着。
She crouched behind the bush. = 她躲在灌木丛后面。

现在完成时:
The dog has crouched there for hours. = 这只狗在那里蹲了几个小时了。
The burglar has crouched in the shadows. = 窃贼一直潜伏在阴影中。

过去完成时:
The bird had crouched low to the ground before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿已低伏在地面上。
The thief had crouched behind the building when the police arrived. = 当警察到达时，小偷已躲在建筑物后面。

将来时:
The cat will crouch and wait for its prey. = 猫会蹲伏着等待猎物。
He will crouch down to examine the damage. = 他会蹲下检查损坏情况。

虚拟语气:
If I were a ninja, I would crouch silently in the shadows. = 如果我是忍者，我会静静地潜伏在阴影中。
If the hunter had crouched lower, he might have caught the deer. = 如果猎人蹲得更低，他可能就能抓住鹿了。

被动语态:
The figure was crouched behind the fence. = 人影蹲在栅栏后面。
The toy was crouched in a corner of the room. = 玩具蹲在房间的一个角落里。

疑问句:
Is the dog crouching under the table? = 狗是不是躲在桌子底下？
Why is he crouching behind the tree? = 他为什么躲在树后面？

否定句:
The bear did not crouch; it charged. = 熊没有蹲伏；它冲了上来。
She didn't crouch down, she stood tall and proud. = 她没有蹲下，而是昂首挺胸地站着。
