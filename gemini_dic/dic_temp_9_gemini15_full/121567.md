misnavigate:/ˌmɪsˈnævɪɡeɪt/| vt.| 导航错误；迷路；误判方向|To fail to navigate correctly; to go astray or lose one's way.|例句：The ship misnavigated and ran aground. 这艘船导航错误，搁浅了。|例句：He misnavigated the complex political landscape. 他误判了复杂的政治局势。|近义词：err, stray, go astray, lose one's way|反义词：navigate correctly, find one's way


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misnavigates the unfamiliar streets. 他在不熟悉的街道上迷路了。
They misnavigate the negotiation process. 他们在谈判过程中误判了方向。


一般过去时:
The pilot misnavigated the plane, causing a delay. 飞行员导航错误，导致航班延误。
