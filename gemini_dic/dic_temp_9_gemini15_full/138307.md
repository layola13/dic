notebooks:/ˈnoʊtbʊks/ | n. |笔记本，笔记簿 | A notebook is a book with blank pages for writing notes, drawings, or other information.  It can be used for various purposes, such as taking notes in class, keeping a diary, or sketching. | Example: I always carry a notebook and pen to jot down my ideas. 我总是带着笔记本和笔来记下我的想法。  I bought a new notebook for my upcoming trip. 我为即将到来的旅行买了一个新的笔记本。|近义词：journal, diary, notepad | 反义词：None
