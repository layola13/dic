challenging: /ˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 具有挑战性的；要求很高能力的 |  Describing something that is difficult, demanding, or stimulating. It implies a test of one's abilities and often suggests the possibility of success despite the difficulty. |


例句：

The new job is challenging but rewarding.  = 这份新工作很有挑战性，但也很有回报。

Climbing Mount Everest is a challenging feat. = 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项具有挑战性的壮举。


近义词：demanding, difficult, arduous, strenuous, taxing, stimulating


反义词：easy, simple, straightforward, effortless


词性：形容词
