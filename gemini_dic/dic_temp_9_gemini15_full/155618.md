philosophizes:/fɪˈlɒsəfaɪzɪz/| v.|哲学思考；空谈哲学；推理|To think or talk in a philosophical way; to engage in philosophical speculation or reasoning.  |例句：He philosophizes about the meaning of life. 他对人生的意义进行哲学思考。  |例句：She often philosophizes about the nature of reality. 她经常对现实的本质进行哲学思考。|近义词：speculates, ponders, reflects|反义词：acts, does


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He philosophizes about the meaning of life. = 他对人生的意义进行哲学思考。
She philosophizes deeply about ethical dilemmas. = 她对伦理困境进行深入的哲学思考。


一般过去时:
He philosophized about the nature of time. = 他对时间的本质进行了哲学思考。
She philosophized for hours on the subject of free will. = 她就自由意志这个话题思考了几个小时。


现在完成时:
He has philosophized extensively on the subject of consciousness. = 他已就意识这个主题进行了广泛的哲学思考。
She has philosophized throughout her life, seeking answers to profound questions. = 她一生都在进行哲学思考，寻求对深刻问题的答案。


过去完成时:
He had philosophized for years before writing his magnum opus. = 在写出他的巨著之前，他已经思考了很多年。
She had philosophized about the possibility of extraterrestrial life before the discovery. = 在发现之前，她就对外星生命存在的可能性进行过哲学思考。


将来时:
He will philosophize on the topic of existentialism at the conference. = 他将在会议上就存在主义的话题进行哲学思考。
She will philosophize more deeply once she has more time. = 一旦有了更多的时间，她将会更深入地进行哲学思考。


虚拟语气:
If he philosophized more, he might understand the issue better. = 如果他进行更多哲学思考，他可能会更好地理解这个问题。
I wish she philosophized less and acted more. = 我希望她少一些空谈哲学，多一些行动。


被动语态:
The problem was philosophized over for many years. = 这个问题被思考了很多年。
The meaning of life is constantly being philosophized about. = 人生的意义不断地被人们进行哲学思考。


疑问句:
Does he often philosophize about politics? = 他经常对政治进行哲学思考吗？
Does she philosophize from a purely theoretical perspective? = 她是否从纯粹理论的角度进行哲学思考？


否定句:
He doesn't philosophize much anymore. = 他不再进行很多哲学思考了。
She didn't philosophize about the implications of her discovery. = 她没有对她的发现的影响进行哲学思考。
