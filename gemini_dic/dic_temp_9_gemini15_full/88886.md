hokeyness:/ˈhoʊkinis/| n. |做作；矫揉造作；俗套；夸张 |The film was criticized for its excessive hokeyness.  这部电影因其过度做作而受到批评。|近义词：artificiality, cheesiness, corniness, kitsch |反义词：authenticity, genuineness, sincerity

USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't have verb or adjective forms in the way that, say, 'happy' does.  Therefore, verb tenses, passive voice etc. are not applicable to the word 'hokeyness' itself.

例句：

There was a certain hokeyness to the way he presented himself. 他自我介绍的方式有点做作。
