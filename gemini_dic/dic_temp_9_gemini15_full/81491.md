gravels:/ˈɡrævəlz/ | n. |砾石；碎石 | Small stones, especially those forming a layer of soil.  Gravel is often used in construction and landscaping. |例句：The path was covered in loose gravel. 小路覆盖着松散的砾石。

The gardener used gravel to create a decorative border around the flower bed. 园丁用砾石在花坛周围创造了一个装饰性边框。

|近义词：pebbles, stones, shingle |反义词：sand (in terms of particle size), solid rock |词性:名词
