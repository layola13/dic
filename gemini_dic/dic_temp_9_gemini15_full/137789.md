nonvenereal: /ˌnɒnˈvɪnəriəl/ | adj. | 非性病的 | Not transmitted through sexual contact.  This adjective describes diseases or conditions that are not sexually transmitted.  It's the opposite of venereal. |

例句：

The doctor confirmed that the rash was nonvenereal.  医生确认皮疹不是性病。

The symptoms were consistent with a nonvenereal infection.  症状与非性病感染一致。


近义词：nonsexually transmitted, noninfectious (in some contexts)

反义词：venereal, sexually transmitted


词性：形容词
