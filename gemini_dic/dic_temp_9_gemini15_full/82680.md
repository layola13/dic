guested:/ˈɡɛstɪd/| v.|担任嘉宾；客串演出|To appear as a guest on a television program, radio show, or other event.|例句：She guested on several popular talk shows last year.  她去年在几档热门脱口秀节目中担任嘉宾。

例句：The famous actor guested on the sitcom last night.  这位著名演员昨晚在情景喜剧中客串演出。


近义词：appeared, starred, featured


反义词：hosted (in the context of hosting an event)


词性：动词
