disadvantageous:/ˌdɪsˌædvənˈteɪdʒəs/| adj. | 不利的；有害的；有缺点的 | Unfavorable; harmful; having drawbacks.  It describes something that puts one at a disadvantage or is likely to cause harm or hinder progress. |  例句：The current economic climate is disadvantageous to small businesses.  当前的经济环境对小型企业不利。

例句：That strategy is disadvantageous in the long run. 从长远来看，那个策略是不利的。


近义词：unfavorable, detrimental, harmful, unfavorable, undesirable, unhelpful

反义词：advantageous, beneficial, favorable, helpful
