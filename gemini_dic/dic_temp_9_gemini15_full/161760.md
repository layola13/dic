powerplants:/ˈpaʊərpælnts/| n.|发电厂；动力装置|A power plant is a facility that generates electricity or other forms of power.  It can use various sources of energy, such as fossil fuels, nuclear energy, renewable energy (solar, wind, hydro), or geothermal energy.|例句：The new power plant will provide electricity to thousands of homes. 这座新的发电厂将为成千上万的家庭提供电力。|例句：The old power plant was shut down due to environmental concerns.  旧的发电厂由于环境问题而被关闭。|近义词：generating station, power station, energy plant|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with energy consumers.
