dominated:/ˈdɒmɪneɪtɪd/ /ˈdɑːmɪneɪtɪd/| v.|支配；统治；控制；主导；突出 |To have control over a place or person.  To be the most noticeable or important feature of something. |例句：The company dominated the market.  这家公司主导了市场。

例句：Her performance dominated the concert. 她的演出主导了这场音乐会。


近义词：control, rule, govern, influence, overshadow

反义词：submit, obey, yield, be subservient to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dominates the conversation. 他主导着谈话。
The mountain dominates the landscape. 这座山主导着这片风景。

一般过去时:
The army dominated the city.  军队控制了这座城市。
She dominated the chess match. 她赢得了国际象棋比赛。


现在完成时:
They have dominated the sport for years. 他们多年来一直主导着这项运动。
This company has dominated the market since its inception. 自成立以来，这家公司一直主导着市场。


过去完成时:
He had dominated the business world before his retirement. 他退休前一直主导着商界。
The team had dominated the game until the final minutes.  直到最后几分钟，这支球队一直占据主导地位。


将来时:
She will dominate the election. 她将赢得选举。
We predict that this technology will dominate the future. 我们预测这项技术将主导未来。


虚拟语气:
If he dominated the game, we would win. 如果他主导比赛，我们就会赢。
I wish I could dominate my fears. 我希望我能战胜我的恐惧。


被动语态:
The game was dominated by the home team.  比赛被主队控制住了。
The meeting was dominated by his forceful personality. 会议被他强势的个性所主导。


疑问句:
Does he dominate his family? 他是否控制着他的家庭？
Which team dominated the match?  哪支球队在比赛中占据主导地位？


否定句:
He did not dominate the discussion. 他并没有主导讨论。
She didn't dominate the competition. 她并没有在比赛中占据主导地位。


PHRASE->
dominate the conversation 主导谈话
dominate the landscape 占据主导地位的风景
dominate the market 占据市场主导地位
dominate the news 成为新闻焦点
dominate the scene 成为场景焦点

词源:
The word "dominated" comes from the Latin word "dominari," meaning "to be master," "to rule," or "to control."  It entered English through Old French.
