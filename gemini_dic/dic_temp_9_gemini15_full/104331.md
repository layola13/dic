jutty:/ˈdʒʌti/ | n. |突出物；突堤；阳台 |A jutty is a projecting part of a building, structure, or rock face.  It can also refer to a small pier or wharf extending into the water. |例句：The old house had a jutting balcony overlooking the street.  这栋老房子有一个突出阳台俯瞰着街道。

The rocky outcrop jutted out into the sea.  岩石露头伸入大海。|近义词：projection, protrusion, overhang |反义词：recess, indentation
