eruptivity:/ˌɪˈrʌptɪvɪti/| n. |喷发性；爆发性；易爆发性|The tendency or likelihood of something to erupt or burst forth suddenly and violently.  It often refers to volcanic eruptions but can also describe other sudden, forceful events or outbursts.|例句：The eruptivity of the volcano was a major concern for the nearby towns. 火山的喷发性是附近城镇的主要担忧。The eruptivity of his temper was legendary. 他易怒的脾气是出了名的。|近义词：explosiveness, outburst, volatility|反义词：calmness, quiescence, stability
