plums:/pluːmz/| n.|李子，梅子|A plum is a juicy fruit with smooth, purplish-red skin. Plums are often used in jams, pies, and other desserts.|例句：The tree is laden with ripe plums.  这棵树上挂满了成熟的李子。

例句：I bought some plums at the farmers market. 我在农贸市场买了一些李子。


近义词：prune (干李子), damson (一种黑李子)


反义词：无


词性：名词
