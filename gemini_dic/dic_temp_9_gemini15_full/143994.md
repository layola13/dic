outrating:/ˌaʊtˈreɪtɪŋ/| v.|超过，胜过，评价高于 |To surpass or exceed in rating or ranking; to receive a higher rating than.| The new restaurant is outrating its competitors in online reviews. 这家新餐厅在网上评价中超过了竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outscore, outperform |反义词：underrating, underscoring
