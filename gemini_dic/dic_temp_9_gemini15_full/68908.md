fasts:/fæsts/ | n. | 禁食；斋戒 | The act of abstaining from food, often for religious or health reasons.  | 例句：Many religions observe periods of fasting. 许多宗教都有禁食的习俗。 | 例句：She is fasting to lose weight. 她在节食减肥。 | 近义词：abstinence,  diet | 反义词：feasting, gorging | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fasts every Friday. = 他每个星期五都禁食。
She fasts during Ramadan. = 她在斋月期间禁食。


一般过去时:
They fasted for three days. = 他们禁食了三天。
He fasted before the religious ceremony. = 他在宗教仪式前禁食。


现在完成时:
I have fasted for a week. = 我已经禁食一周了。
She has fasted intermittently for months. = 她断断续续地节食几个月了。


过去完成时:
He had fasted for two days before he felt weak. = 他禁食了两天才感到虚弱。
They had fasted before the competition to improve their performance. = 他们为了提高比赛成绩而在比赛前禁食。


将来时:
I will fast next week for religious reasons. = 我下周将因宗教原因禁食。
We will fast before the medical procedure. = 我们将在医疗手术前禁食。


虚拟语气:
If I were to fast, I would drink plenty of water. = 如果我要禁食，我会喝很多水。
I wish I had fasted before the race; I felt sluggish. = 我希望我在比赛前禁食；我觉得很迟钝。


被动语态:
Fasting is practiced by many cultures. = 禁食被许多文化所实践。
The fast was broken after sunset. = 禁食在日落后结束。


疑问句:
Does he often fast? = 他经常禁食吗？
Are you fasting today? = 你今天禁食吗？


否定句:
I do not fast regularly. = 我不经常禁食。
She did not fast before the exam. = 她考试前没有禁食。
