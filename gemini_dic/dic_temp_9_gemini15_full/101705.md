词源:

The word is formed from the prefix "ir-" (meaning "not"), "replace" (meaning "to put back in place"), the suffix "-able" (meaning "capable of"), and another suffix "-ity" (meaning "state or quality").  Therefore, it literally means "the state of not being replaceable".
