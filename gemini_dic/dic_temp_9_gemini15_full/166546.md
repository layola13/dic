presbytic: /prɛzˈbɪtɪk/ | adj. | 老视的，老年性视力的 | Relating to or characteristic of presbyopia (the gradual loss of your eyes' ability to focus on nearby objects).  It is a natural part of aging. | 例句：He's experiencing presbytic symptoms and needs reading glasses. 他正在经历老花眼症状，需要戴老花镜。

例句：Her presbytic vision made it difficult to read small print. 她的老花眼让她难以阅读小字。


近义词：farsighted (in the context of age-related vision loss), hyperopic (though hyperopia can also be present from birth) | 反义词：None (it describes a condition, not a quality with a direct opposite) | 词性：形容词
