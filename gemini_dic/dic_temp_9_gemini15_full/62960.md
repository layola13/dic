ennoblement:/ɪˈnəʊblmənt/ | n. | 提升；封爵；使高贵；崇高化 | The act of ennobling someone or something, raising them to a higher status or moral level.  It often refers to the granting of a noble title, but can also apply to the elevation of something's character or reputation. | Example: The ennoblement of the commoner surprised everyone.  平民的封爵令所有人惊讶。

The ennoblement of his character through years of service was evident. 他多年来服务所提升的品格是显而易见的。 |近义词: elevation, refinement, exaltation |反义词: degradation, debasement, humiliation |
