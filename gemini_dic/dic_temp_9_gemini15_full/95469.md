incitements: /ɪnˈsaɪtmənts/ | n. |煽动；激励；刺激 | Acts or instances of inciting or urging someone to do something, especially something violent or unlawful.  |例句：The police are investigating possible incitements to violence.  警方正在调查可能存在的暴力煽动行为。

例句：His incitements led to a riot in the streets. 他的煽动导致了街头暴乱。


近义词：instigations, provocations, encouragements, goads

反义词：deterrents, discouragements, dissuasions

The government monitors online incitements to hatred. = 政府监控网上煽动仇恨言论。
His speeches contain many incitements to action. = 他的演讲包含许多行动的煽动。


一般过去时:

His aggressive behavior was an incitement to violence. = 他具有侵略性的行为是对暴力的煽动。


现在完成时:
There have been several reports of online incitements to violence. = 有几份报告称网上存在暴力煽动行为。
The authorities have successfully stopped several incitements to riot. = 当局已成功阻止了几起煽动暴乱的行为。


过去完成时:
The police believed the earlier incitements had contributed to the riot. = 警方认为早前的煽动促成了暴乱。
The judge felt the defendant's past incitements were relevant to the current case. = 法官认为被告过去的煽动与当前案件有关。


将来时:
We fear that future incitements will lead to further unrest. = 我们担心未来的煽动会导致进一步的动荡。
The authorities will investigate any incitements to violence immediately. = 当局将立即调查任何暴力煽动行为。


虚拟语气:
If there had been fewer incitements, the violence might have been avoided. = 如果煽动较少，暴力可能就会避免。
I wish there were fewer incitements to hatred in our society. = 我希望我们社会上煽动仇恨的行为少一些。


被动语态:
The riot was believed to have been caused by incitements from extremist groups. = 人们认为暴乱是由极端组织的煽动引起的。
The speeches were criticized for their incitements to hatred and violence. = 这些演讲因煽动仇恨和暴力而受到批评。


疑问句:

What kind of incitements were used to start the riot? = 用什么类型的煽动来发动暴乱？


否定句:
There were no incitements to violence at the rally. = 集会上没有暴力煽动行为。
The speaker denied any incitements to unlawful behavior. = 演讲者否认任何煽动非法行为。

词源:
From Middle English *incitment, from Old French *incitement, from Latin incitamentum, from incitare "to incite," from in- "in" + citare "to summon, rouse."
