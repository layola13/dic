paints:/peɪnts/| v. n.|绘画，油漆；涂抹|To apply paint to a surface;  the act of applying paint or the resulting artwork.|例句：He paints landscapes. 他画风景画。  The house needs painting. 这房子需要油漆。|近义词：colors, decorates, daubs|反义词：erases, removes

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He paints every day. = 他每天都画画。
She paints portraits of animals. = 她画动物肖像。

一般过去时:
I painted a picture yesterday. = 我昨天画了一幅画。
They painted the fence white. = 他们把栅栏漆成白色。

现在完成时:
He has painted many masterpieces. = 他已经画了很多杰作。
She has painted her bedroom a bright yellow. = 她已经把她的卧室漆成了亮黄色。

过去完成时:
He had painted the entire house before it rained. = 在下雨之前他已经把整栋房子都漆好了。
They had painted the mural for weeks before the exhibition. = 他们在展览前已经画了几个星期的壁画。

将来时:
I will paint a new mural next week. = 我下周将画一幅新的壁画。
She will paint the kitchen cabinets tomorrow. = 她明天将粉刷厨房橱柜。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would paint more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常地画画。
I wish I could paint like Picasso. = 我希望我能像毕加索一样画画。

被动语态:
The fence was painted last week. = 栅栏上周被粉刷了。
The mural is being painted by a famous artist. = 壁画正由一位著名的艺术家绘画。

疑问句:
Do you paint often? = 你经常画画吗？
What kind of paints are you using? = 你用的是什么颜料？

否定句:
I don't paint very well. = 我画得不太好。
He didn't paint the door. = 他没有粉刷门。


PHRASE->
paint a picture = 描绘一幅画面
paint the town red = 狂欢
paint oneself into a corner = 陷入困境
a fresh coat of paint = 新的一层油漆
paint by numbers = 按数字绘画
