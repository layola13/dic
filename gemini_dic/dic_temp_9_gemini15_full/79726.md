glues:/ɡluːz/| n. |胶水；粘合剂|A substance used for sticking things together.  Glues are typically viscous liquids or pastes that harden upon drying.|例句：The carpenter used wood glue to join the pieces of furniture. 木匠用木胶粘合家具部件。

例句：She applied a strong glue to seal the envelope. 她用强力胶封住信封。


近义词：adhesives, pastes, cements|反义词：separators, solvents|词性：名词
