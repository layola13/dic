diseducate: /ˌdɪsˈɛdʒʊkeɪt/ | vt. | 使失去教育；使受不良教育；使蒙昧 | To deprive someone of education or to give someone a bad or inadequate education.  |例句：The oppressive regime systematically diseducated the population, preventing them from critical thinking.  专制政权系统性地使人民失去教育，阻止他们进行批判性思考。|The poorly designed curriculum diseducated the students, leaving them unprepared for college. 设计糟糕的课程使学生受了不良教育，让他们为大学做好了准备。 |近义词：misinform, misguide, indoctrinate |反义词：educate, enlighten
