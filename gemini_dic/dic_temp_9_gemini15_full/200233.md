skips:/skɪps/| v.|跳过，略过，遗漏|to move quickly with a light, bouncing step; to omit or leave out something.|例句：He skips breakfast every day. 他每天都跳过早餐。

例句：The report skips over some important details. 报告忽略了一些重要的细节。|近义词：jump, leap, omit, miss|反义词：include, consider, attend to|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skips school every Friday. = 他每个星期五都逃学。
She skips rope every morning. = 她每天早上跳绳。

一般过去时:
I skipped the meeting yesterday. = 我昨天没参加会议。
They skipped dinner last night. = 他们昨晚没吃晚饭。

现在完成时:
He has skipped lunch twice this week. = 他本周已经两次没吃午饭了。
She has skipped several chapters in the book. = 她跳过了书中几章。

过去完成时:
I had skipped breakfast, so I was very hungry. = 我没吃早饭，所以我很饿。
They had skipped the first act of the play. = 他们错过了戏剧的第一幕。

将来时:
I will skip the dessert tonight. = 我今晚不吃甜点了。
She will skip the next meeting. = 她将跳过下次会议。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would skip the meeting. = 如果我有时间，我会跳过会议。
I wish I could skip the exam. = 我希望我能跳过考试。

被动语态:
The meeting was skipped by many people. = 许多人都没参加会议。
Several chapters were skipped in the edited version. = 编辑版跳过了几章。

疑问句:
Did you skip breakfast this morning? = 你今天早上没吃早饭吗？
Will you skip the party? = 你会不去参加聚会吗？

否定句:
I didn't skip class today. = 我今天没有逃课。
She doesn't skip meals regularly. = 她不会经常不吃正餐。
