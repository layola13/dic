disreputes:/ˌdɪsrɪˈpjuːt/| vt.|败坏名誉，使声名狼藉|To damage the reputation or credibility of someone or something.|His actions disreputed the entire organization. 他的行为败坏了整个组织的名誉。|近义词：discredit, defame, slander|反义词：honor, praise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disreputes his colleagues with false accusations. 他用虚假的指控败坏同事的名誉。
The scandal disreputes the company's image. 这场丑闻败坏了公司的形象。


一般过去时:
The politician's lies disreputed his career.  这位政治家的谎言毁了他的事业。
The false news report disreputed the scientist's findings.  那篇虚假的新闻报道败坏了这位科学家的研究成果。


现在完成时:
His reckless behavior has disreputed his family name. 他的鲁莽行为已经败坏了他的家族名声。
The leaked documents have disreputed the government's efforts.  泄露的文件已经败坏了政府的努力。


过去完成时:
They had disreputed the company long before the scandal broke.  在丑闻爆发之前很久，他们就已经败坏了公司的名声。
The rumors had disreputed her reputation before she could defend herself.  谣言在她能够为自己辩护之前就已经败坏了她的名誉。


将来时:
This action will disrepute the whole team.  这个行为将会败坏整个团队的名誉。
Further investigations will likely disrepute the current findings.  进一步的调查很可能会推翻目前的调查结果。


虚拟语气:
If he had not lied, he would not have disreputed his reputation. 如果他没有撒谎，他就不会败坏自己的名声。
I wish they had not disreputed the company's image. 我希望他们没有败坏公司的形象。


被动语态:
The company was disreputed by the negative publicity. 公司因负面宣传而声名狼藉。
His reputation was disreputed by the false accusations.  他的名誉因虚假指控而受损。


疑问句:
Will this decision disrepute the organization?  这个决定会败坏这个组织的名誉吗？
Has the media disreputed the candidate's image? 媒体是否败坏了候选人的形象？


否定句:
The evidence did not disrepute his claims. 证据并没有败坏他的说法。
They tried to disrepute him, but failed.  他们试图败坏他的名誉，但失败了。
