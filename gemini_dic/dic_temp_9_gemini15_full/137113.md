nonsubmission: /ˌnɒnˌsʌbˈmɪʃən/ | n. |未提交；未递交；不提交 | The act or instance of not submitting something; failure to submit.  It often implies a deliberate refusal or neglect to comply with a requirement or expectation. |例句：The nonsubmission of the report led to a delay in the project. 未提交报告导致项目延误。

例句：His nonsubmission of the application resulted in disqualification. 他未提交申请导致资格被取消。


近义词：failure to submit, omission, default, noncompliance, neglect


反义词：submission, compliance


词性：名词
