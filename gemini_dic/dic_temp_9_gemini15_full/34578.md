clearing-out: /ˈklɪərɪŋ aʊt/ | v. | 清理；搬出；清除；解雇 |  The act of removing unwanted or unnecessary items from a place, or of leaving a place.  It can also refer to the dismissal of employees. |

例句：

They spent the weekend clearing-out the attic. = 他们周末花了时间清理阁楼。

The company is clearing-out underperforming employees. = 公司正在解雇表现不佳的员工。


近义词：cleaning, tidying, removing, dismissing, sacking

反义词：cluttering, accumulating, hoarding
