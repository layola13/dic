nonbreeding: /ˈnɒnˈbriːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 非繁殖期的；不繁殖的 | Not breeding; not engaged in reproduction.  The term often refers to a period in an animal's life cycle or to a specific group of animals that are not currently reproducing. |  Many bird species have a nonbreeding season during which they do not reproduce. = 许多鸟类都有一个非繁殖季节，在此期间它们不繁殖。

The nonbreeding population of the species migrates to warmer climates. = 该物种的非繁殖种群迁移到气候温暖的地方。


近义词：nonreproductive, inactive (in terms of breeding) | 反义词：breeding, reproductive
