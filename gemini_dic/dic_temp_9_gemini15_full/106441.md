koshers:/ˈkoʊʃərz/| adj.|犹太教饮食规定的；符合犹太教饮食规定的|Adjective describing food that conforms to kashrut, the Jewish dietary laws.  These laws specify which foods are permitted (kosher) and which are forbidden (treif or non-kosher).|例句：These sausages are kosher, so they're suitable for a kosher meal.|近义词：halal(伊斯兰教饮食规定的), permissible|反义词：treif, non-kosher
