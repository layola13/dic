peppers:/ˈpepərz/| n. |辣椒；胡椒粉|The word "peppers" refers to the fruit of plants in the genus Capsicum, known for their pungent flavor.  They are widely used as a spice and ingredient in various cuisines worldwide.  The pungency is due to the presence of capsaicin. Different types of peppers vary greatly in heat level, size, shape, and color.|例句：I added some bell peppers to the stir-fry. 我在炒菜里加了一些甜椒。

例句：We used chili peppers to make the salsa. 我们用辣椒来制作莎莎酱。|近义词：chilies, pimentos|反义词： (no direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of bland foods)
