nondieter:/ˈnɒnˌdaɪətər/| n. |非节食者，不节食的人 |A nondieter is someone who does not follow a diet or restrict their food intake for weight loss or health reasons. They may eat whatever they want, when they want, without feeling guilty or overly concerned about their weight or health.|例句：She's a nondieter and enjoys a variety of foods without restriction. 她是一个不节食的人，喜欢各种各样的食物，并且不加限制。|近义词：non-restrictive eater, casual eater |反义词：dieter
