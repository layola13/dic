nonwashable: /ˌnɒnˈwɒʃəbəl/ | adj. | 不可洗的 | Not able to be washed without damage.  This adjective describes items that would be ruined or significantly damaged if subjected to standard washing methods.  | Example:  This garment is nonwashable; dry clean only. 这件衣服不可水洗；只能干洗。 | 近义词：unwashable, launder-proof | 反义词：washable
