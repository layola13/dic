communes:/ˈkɒmjuːnz/ /kəˈmjuːnz/| n. |公社；集体农庄；共享社区|A commune is a group of people living together and sharing possessions and responsibilities.  It can refer to a small, self-governing community, often based on socialist or utopian ideals, or a more loosely organized group sharing living space and resources. |Example:  The kibbutzim in Israel are examples of successful communes. 以色列的基布兹是成功的公社的例子。


The early Christian communities were essentially communes. 早期基督教社群本质上是公社。

近义词：collective, community, settlement


反义词：individualism, privatization


词性:名词
