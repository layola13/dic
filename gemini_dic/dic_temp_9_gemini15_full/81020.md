gradually:/ˈɡrædʒuəli/ | adv. |逐渐地，逐步地 | gradually means slowly and steadily. It implies a process of change that happens over time, without sudden or abrupt shifts.  | The sun gradually set, painting the sky with vibrant colors.  日落逐渐西下，天空被染成鲜艳的色彩。| slowly, progressively, steadily | abruptly, suddenly, rapidly | 副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant gradually grows taller. = 植物逐渐长高。
The sun gradually warms the earth. = 太阳逐渐温暖着地球。

一般过去时:
The temperature gradually decreased throughout the night. = 整个晚上气温逐渐下降。

现在完成时:
The company has gradually expanded its market share. = 公司逐渐扩大了市场份额。
My understanding of the subject has gradually deepened. = 我对这个主题的理解逐渐加深了。

过去完成时:
He had gradually lost confidence in himself over the years. = 多年来，他逐渐失去了自信。
The old building had gradually deteriorated over time. = 这座旧建筑随着时间的推移逐渐破败。

将来时:
The city will gradually develop into a major metropolis. = 这个城市将逐渐发展成为一个主要的大都市。
The effects of the medicine will gradually wear off. = 药效将逐渐消失。

疑问句:

否定句:
