/ˈkændɪdeɪt/| n. |候选人，申请者|A person who applies for a job or is nominated for an election.  A candidate for a position must meet certain qualifications.|例句：He is a strong candidate for the job. 他是这份工作的有力候选人。|例句：She's a candidate for mayor. 她是市长候选人。|近义词：applicant, contender, nominee |反义词：voter, elector


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The candidate campaigns tirelessly. = 候选人不知疲倦地竞选。
He is a promising candidate for the scholarship. = 他是获得奖学金的有希望的候选人。

一般过去时:

将来时:
