promontorium:/prɒmənˈtɔːriəm/| n. |海角，岬，突出物 | A promontory is a high point of land that juts out into the sea or a large body of water.  It is typically a rocky headland. |例句：The lighthouse stood on a dramatic promontory overlooking the ocean. 灯塔耸立在一个俯瞰大海的壮观海角上。

例句：From the promontory, we had a breathtaking view of the coastline. 从海角上，我们可以看到令人叹为观止的海岸线景色。


近义词：headland, cape, point, head


反义词：inlet, bay


词性:名词


词源:  From Latin *promontorium, from pro- ("forward") + mons ("mountain").  The word literally means "projecting mountain".


USAGE->
无适用语法变化，promontorium 作为名词，本身不具备动词的语法变化。
