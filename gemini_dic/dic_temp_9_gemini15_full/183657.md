rereleases:/ˌriːˈriːlɪsiːz/| n. |重新发行，再版|The act of releasing something again, typically a product or piece of media that has been previously released.  This often involves new packaging, additional content, or a wider distribution.|例句：The band announced rereleases of their early albums with bonus tracks. 乐队宣布将发行包含额外歌曲的早期专辑的重新发行版。

例句：The remastered rereleases of the classic film sold exceptionally well.  经典电影的重新制作和重新发行版销量非常好。


近义词：reissues, re-releases, relaunches|反义词：initial release, first release|名词
