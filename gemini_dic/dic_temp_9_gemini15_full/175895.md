rather:/ˈrɑːðər/ /ˈræðər/ | adv. |相当，宁可，更确切地说 | Rather means to a significant extent or degree; quite.  It can also mean preferably or instead. |  I'm rather tired today. (我今天相当疲倦。)

He would rather stay home. (他宁愿待在家里。) | fairly, quite, pretty | utterly, completely | 副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am rather busy today. = 我今天相当忙。
She is rather quiet. = 她相当安静。

一般过去时:
He was rather unhappy with the results. =他对结果相当不满。
We were rather surprised by the news. = 我们对这个消息相当惊讶。

现在完成时:
I have been rather unwell lately. = 我最近身体不太舒服。
She has been rather successful in her career. = 她在事业上相当成功。

过去完成时:
They had been rather careless with their belongings. = 他们对自己的东西相当粗心。
He had been rather optimistic about the project. = 他对这个项目相当乐观。

将来时:
I will be rather busy next week. = 我下周会相当忙。
We will be rather tired after the trip. = 旅行后我们会相当疲倦。

虚拟语气:
I would rather go to the beach than stay home. = 我宁愿去海滩也不愿待在家里。
She would rather he hadn't said that. = 她宁愿他没有那样说。

被动语态:
The task was rather difficult to complete. = 这项任务相当难以完成。
The problem was rather easily solved. = 这个问题相当容易解决。

疑问句:
Would you rather have tea or coffee? = 你想喝茶还是咖啡？
Is he rather sensitive? = 他相当敏感吗？

否定句:
I am not rather keen on spicy food. = 我不太喜欢辛辣食物。(此处"rather"使用略显不自然，通常不用否定)
He wasn't rather pleased with the outcome. = 他对结果不太满意。(此处"rather"使用略显不自然，通常不用否定)


PHRASE->
rather than:  而不是
would rather: 宁愿
rather a lot: 相当多
had rather: 宁愿 (过去时态)
rather quickly: 相当快
