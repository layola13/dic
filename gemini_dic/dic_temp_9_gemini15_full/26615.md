The sound is often described as a series of short, sharp bursts of laughter. | Example: Her cachinnation filled the room, a sound both joyous and slightly unsettling.  她的狂笑声充满了整个房间，既令人高兴又略带不安。|近义词：laughter, guffaw, roar, chortle | 反义词：silence, sob, weeping | 名词
