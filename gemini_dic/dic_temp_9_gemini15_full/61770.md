empathizes:/ˈɛmpəθaɪzɪz/| v.|表示同情；感同身受|To understand and share the feelings of another.|She empathizes with the struggles of single mothers. 她同情单身母亲的困境。|sympathizes, understands, relates to|indifference, apathy|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He empathizes with her loss. = 他同情她的损失。
She empathizes with the victims of the disaster. = 她同情灾难的受害者。

一般过去时:
I empathized with his situation. = 我同情他的处境。
She empathized with the character in the book. = 她对书中的人物感同身受。

现在完成时:
He has empathized with many people throughout his career. = 他在职业生涯中同情过很多人。
She has empathized with the pain of others since she was a child. = 自小她就同情他人的痛苦。


过去完成时:
I had empathized with her before, so I understood her reaction. = 我以前就同情过她，所以我理解她的反应。
He had empathized with the plight of the refugees before he met them. = 在他遇到难民之前，他已经同情他们的困境了。


将来时:
I will empathize with you if you tell me your problems. = 如果你告诉我你的问题，我会同情你。
She will empathize with the characters in the play. = 她会对剧中人物感同身受。


虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would empathize more. = 如果我处在他的位置，我会更加同情。
I wish I could empathize better with people who are different from me. = 我希望我能更好地理解与我不同的人。


被动语态:
His suffering was empathized with by many. = 许多人同情他的痛苦。(此用法较少见)
Her plight was empathized with by the whole community. = 整个社区都同情她的困境。(此用法较少见)


疑问句:
Do you empathize with the characters in the story? = 你同情故事中的人物吗？
Does he empathize with the suffering of others? = 他同情他人的痛苦吗？


否定句:
I don't empathize with his actions. = 我不同情他的行为。
She didn't empathize with the villain's motivations. = 她不理解反派的动机。


PHRASE->
empathize with = 同情…
show empathy = 表达同情
deep empathy = 深切的同情
lack of empathy = 缺乏同情心
feel empathy for = 对…产生同情
