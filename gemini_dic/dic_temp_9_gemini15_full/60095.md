eelspear:/ˈiːlspɪər/| n. |鱼叉；鳗鱼叉| A spear used for catching eels.  It typically has multiple barbs to prevent the eel from escaping.|例句：He used an eelspear to catch dinner. 他用鱼叉叉鱼做晚餐。
例句：The eelspear was made of sharpened wood and strong cord. 这支鱼叉是用尖木头和结实的绳子做的。|近义词：spear, trident, fish spear|反义词：fishing net (反义词较为宽泛，因为是不同捕鱼工具)|词性: 名词
