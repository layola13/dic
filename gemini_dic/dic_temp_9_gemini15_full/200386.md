skylarks:/ˈskaɪlɑːks/| n. |云雀|A skylark is a small brown bird that sings beautifully while flying high in the sky.  It is known for its soaring flight and melodious song.|例句：The skylark's song filled the morning air. 云雀的歌声充满了清晨的空气。

例句：We heard the skylarks singing as we walked across the field. 我们穿过田野时听到云雀在唱歌。


近义词：lark (a more general term for the bird)


反义词：None (There isn't a direct antonym for skylark.)


词性:noun
