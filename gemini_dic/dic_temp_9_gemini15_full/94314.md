impetigo:/ɪmˈpɛtɪɡoʊ/| n. |脓疱疮|A highly contagious bacterial skin infection, characterized by blisters that rupture and crust over.|例句：The child developed impetigo after scratching a mosquito bite. 这个孩子抓挠蚊子叮咬后患上了脓疱疮。
|例句：The doctor prescribed antibiotics to treat the impetigo. 医生开了抗生素来治疗脓疱疮。|近义词：skin infection, pyoderma|反义词:|
