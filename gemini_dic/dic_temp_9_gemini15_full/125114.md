multi:/ˈmʌlti/| /ˈmʌltaɪ/| adj.|多样的，多重的，多功能的|Having or involving many parts, elements, or aspects.|The company offers a multi-faceted approach to customer service. 这家公司提供多方面的客户服务。|近义词：multiple, manifold, varied|反义词：single, solitary, one


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This device has multiple functions. = 这个设备有多种功能。
The project involves many different tasks. = 这个项目涉及许多不同的任务。


一般过去时:
The conference covered many topics. = 会议涵盖了许多主题。
She had multiple jobs last year. = 她去年做过几份工作。


现在完成时:
We have completed many projects this year. = 我们今年已经完成了许多项目。
She has had many experiences in her life. = 她一生经历过许多事情。


过去完成时:
They had already completed multiple steps before the delay occurred. = 延迟发生之前，他们已经完成了多个步骤。
He had seen many films before choosing that one. = 他在选择那部电影之前已经看过很多电影。


将来时:
We will have many challenges to overcome. = 我们将面临许多挑战。
The company will offer multiple services in the future. = 公司将来会提供多种服务。


虚拟语气:
If I had multiple choices, I would be happier. = 如果我有更多选择，我会更快乐。
I wish I had more time to handle multiple projects. = 我希望我有更多时间来处理多个项目。


被动语态:
Multiple options were presented to the clients. = 多种方案已提交给客户。
The problem was solved through multiple approaches. = 这个问题通过多种方法解决了。


疑问句:
Does this software have multiple features? = 这个软件有多个功能吗？
Are there multiple ways to achieve this goal? = 实现这个目标有多种方法吗？


否定句:
