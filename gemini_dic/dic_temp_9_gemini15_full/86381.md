hedge:/hedʒ/| n., v.|树篱；避免；对冲；| A hedge is a fence or boundary formed by closely growing bushes or shrubs.  As a verb, it means to avoid giving a direct answer to a question.  It can also refer to financial hedging. |例句：We planted a hedge around the garden. 我们在花园周围种了一排树篱。 He hedged his answer, avoiding a commitment. 他含糊其辞，避免做出承诺。|近义词：fence, barrier, equivocate, evade |反义词：clarify, reveal, direct


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hedges his bets in the stock market. = 他在股市里进行对冲投资。
They hedge their statements to avoid controversy. = 他们含糊其辞以避免争议。


一般过去时:
She hedged around the question, refusing to answer directly. = 她回避了这个问题，拒绝直接回答。
The farmer hedged his field with thorny bushes. = 农民用带刺的灌木丛围住了他的田地。


现在完成时:
They have hedged against inflation by investing in real estate. = 他们通过投资房地产来对抗通货膨胀。
He has hedged his bets on several different projects. = 他在几个不同的项目上进行了对冲投资。


过去完成时:
They had hedged their investments before the market crash. = 他们在市场崩盘之前已经进行了对冲投资。
She had hedged her response, leaving me unsure of her true feelings. = 她含糊其辞，让我不确定她的真实感受。


将来时:
We will hedge our risks by diversifying our portfolio. = 我们将通过多元化投资组合来规避风险。
He will hedge his answer to avoid offending anyone. = 他将含糊其辞以避免冒犯任何人。


虚拟语气:
If he had hedged his bets, he wouldn't have lost so much money. = 如果他进行了对冲投资，他就不会损失那么多钱。
If I were you, I would hedge my answer carefully. = 如果我是你，我会谨慎地含糊其辞。


被动语态:
The garden was hedged with rose bushes. = 花园是用玫瑰花丛围起来的。
His response was hedged to protect his interests. = 他的回答含糊其辞是为了保护他的利益。


疑问句:
Did you hedge your answer to avoid the question? = 你是否回避了这个问题？
Are you hedging your bets on this deal? = 你在这个交易中是否进行了对冲投资？


否定句:
He didn't hedge his bets and lost everything. = 他没有进行对冲投资，结果输掉了所有东西。
She didn't hedge her words, stating her opinion clearly. = 她没有含糊其辞，清楚地表达了自己的意见。
