gulping:/ˈɡʌlpɪŋ/| v.|吞咽；大口喝；狼吞虎咽|The act of swallowing something quickly and noisily.  It often implies a large amount or a feeling of urgency.|例句：He gulped down the water in one go. 他一口喝光了水。|例句：She was gulping her food, clearly nervous. 她狼吞虎咽地吃着食物，显然很紧张。|近义词：swallowing, chugging, guzzling|反义词：sipping, savoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gulps his food every day. = 他每天狼吞虎咽地吃饭。
She gulps water when she is thirsty. = 她渴了就大口喝水。

一般过去时:
He gulped down the medicine. = 他一口吞下了药。
She gulped the news in shock. = 她震惊地吞咽着这个消息。

现在完成时:
He has gulped down three glasses of water. = 他已经喝光了三杯水。
She has gulped her food because she was late for work. = 她因为上班迟到而狼吞虎咽地吃东西。

过去完成时:
He had gulped down a large glass of water before he started the race. = 他在开始比赛前喝了一大杯水。
She had gulped the entire bottle before I could stop her. = 我还没来得及阻止她，她就喝光了整瓶。


将来时:
He will gulp down the entire soda in one sitting. = 他会一口气喝完这瓶苏打水。
She will gulp her coffee before she leaves for work. = 她上班前会快速喝完咖啡。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't gulp down that medicine so quickly. = 如果我是你，我就不会这么快地把药吞下去。
I wish I hadn't gulped down that spicy food so fast. = 我真希望我没有那么快地吃下那辣的食物。

被动语态:
The medicine was gulped down quickly by the patient. = 药物被病人迅速吞下。
The news was gulped down by everyone present. = 在场的所有人都震惊地接受了这个消息。


疑问句:
Is he gulping his food because he is late? = 他是不是因为迟到了所以狼吞虎咽地吃饭？
Why is she gulping down her drink so fast? = 她为什么这么快地喝饮料？


否定句:
He didn't gulp down the entire bottle of wine.  = 他没有喝光整瓶酒。
She didn't gulp her food; she ate slowly and deliberately. = 她没有狼吞虎咽地吃饭；她吃得很慢很仔细。


PHRASE->
gulp down = 快速吞咽
gulp up = 大口吞下
gulp air = 急促地呼吸
a gulp of air = 一口气
gulp something down = 一口吞下某物
