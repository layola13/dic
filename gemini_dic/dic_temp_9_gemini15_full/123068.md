money-spinner:/ˈmʌniˌspɪnər/| n. |摇钱树；生财之道；赚钱的项目|A money-spinner is something that makes a lot of money.|That new restaurant is a real money-spinner.|近义词：cash cow, gold mine|反义词：loss-maker, money pit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This business is a money-spinner. = 这项生意是摇钱树。
That new product is a money-spinner for the company. = 那个新产品是公司的摇钱树。


一般过去时:
The concert was a money-spinner for the charity. = 那场音乐会为慈善机构赚了很多钱。
His invention proved to be a money-spinner. = 他的发明证明是一项赚钱的项目。


现在完成时:
The online store has been a money-spinner since its launch. = 自上线以来，这家网店一直很赚钱。
Their latest film has become a huge money-spinner. = 他们最新的电影已经成为一部非常赚钱的电影。


过去完成时:
The previous product line had been a money-spinner for many years. = 之前的产品线多年来一直很赚钱。
Before the new strategy, the company hadn't found a real money-spinner. = 在新的策略之前，公司还没有找到真正的摇钱树。


将来时:
The new theme park will be a money-spinner, they predict. = 他们预测新的主题公园将是一座摇钱树。
We believe this investment will be a money-spinner in the long run. = 我们相信这项投资从长远来看将是赚钱的。


虚拟语气:
If that project were a money-spinner, we wouldn't be in this situation. = 如果那个项目是摇钱树，我们现在就不会处于这种境地。
I wish this business was a money-spinner. = 我希望这项生意能赚钱。


被动语态:
The new software is being touted as a money-spinner. = 新软件被吹捧为摇钱树。
The idea was initially dismissed, but it later proved to be a money-spinner. = 这个想法最初被否决了，但后来证明它是一个赚钱的项目。


疑问句:
Is this venture a money-spinner? = 这项投资是摇钱树吗？
Will the new product line be a money-spinner? = 新产品线会赚钱吗？


否定句:
The investment wasn't a money-spinner; it resulted in a loss. = 这项投资不是摇钱树；它导致了亏损。
That idea is not a money-spinner; it’s costing us money. = 那个主意不是摇钱树；它正在让我们赔钱。
