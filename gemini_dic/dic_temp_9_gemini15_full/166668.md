preservation: /ˌprɛzərˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 保护；保存；维护 | The act of preserving something.  It involves protecting something from loss or decay.  This can refer to physical objects, environments, or cultural traditions. |例句：The preservation of historical buildings is crucial.  =  保护历史建筑至关重要。

The preservation of endangered species is a global priority. = 保护濒危物种是全球的优先任务。


近义词：conservation, protection, safeguarding | 反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin | 词性：名词
