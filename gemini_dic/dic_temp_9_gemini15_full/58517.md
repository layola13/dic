duet:/ˈduːɛt/| n.|二重奏，二重唱|A duet is a musical composition for two performers, typically vocalists or instrumentalists.| 例句：The soprano and tenor sang a beautiful duet. 女高音和男高音演唱了一首优美的二重奏。

例句：They performed a piano duet at the concert.  他们在音乐会上演奏了一首钢琴二重奏。


近义词：double act, duo


反义词：solo, solo performance


词性:名词
