cherisher:/ˈtʃerɪʃər/| n. |珍爱者，珍惜的人|One who cherishes something or someone.  A person who holds something dear and protects it. |例句：He is a cherisher of old books. 他是一位珍爱旧书的人。She is a cherisher of memories.  她是個珍惜回憶的人。|近义词：protector, preserver, keeper, guardian |反义词：neglecter, abuser, destroyer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She cherishes her family. = 她珍爱她的家人。
He cherishes his freedom. = 他珍惜他的自由。

一般过去时:
They cherished the moment. = 他们珍惜那一刻。
She cherished the gift. = 她珍视这份礼物。

现在完成时:
I have cherished this friendship for years. = 我已经珍视这份友谊多年了。
They have cherished their memories of childhood. = 他们一直珍藏着童年记忆。

过去完成时:
He had cherished the hope of seeing her again. = 他一直怀抱着再次见到她的希望。
She had cherished the thought of traveling to Europe. = 她一直梦想着去欧洲旅行。

将来时:
We will cherish this experience forever. = 我们将永远珍惜这段经历。
They will cherish the opportunity. = 他们会珍惜这个机会。

虚拟语气:
If I had cherished those moments more, I wouldn't regret it now. = 如果我当时更珍惜那些时刻，现在就不会后悔了。
I wish I had cherished my time with my grandparents. = 我真希望我当时能多珍惜和祖父母在一起的时间。

被动语态:
The memory was cherished by all. = 这段记忆被所有人珍藏。
The old photos were cherished by the family. = 这些旧照片被家人珍藏。


疑问句:
Do you cherish your time with loved ones? = 你珍惜和所爱之人在一起的时间吗？
Does he cherish his possessions? = 他珍惜他的财产吗？

否定句:
He doesn't cherish material possessions. = 他不珍视物质财富。
She didn't cherish the opportunity. = 她没有珍惜这个机会。
