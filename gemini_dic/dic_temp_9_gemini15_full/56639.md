donned:/dɒnd/| /dɑːnd/| vt.|穿上，戴上|To put on (a garment, etc.).  It implies a deliberate and often quick action.|例句：He donned his coat and went out. 他穿上外套出去了。|例句：She donned a new dress for the party. 她为了参加聚会穿上了新衣服。|近义词：put on, dressed in, clad in|反义词：doffed, removed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dons his hat every morning. = 他每天早上都戴上帽子。
She dons a new outfit for each performance. = 她每次演出都换一套新服装。

一般过去时:
He donned his helmet before starting the race. = 他在开始比赛前戴上了头盔。
She donned her best gown for the wedding. = 她为了婚礼穿上了她最好的礼服。

现在完成时:
He has donned his winter coat because it's snowing. = 因为他下雪了，所以他穿上了冬衣。
She has donned a disguise to avoid recognition. = 她为了避免被认出来而乔装打扮。

过去完成时:
He had donned his uniform before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前他已经穿上了制服。
She had donned her protective gear before entering the lab. = 进入实验室之前，她已经穿戴好防护装备。

将来时:
He will don his running shoes before the marathon. = 马拉松比赛开始前，他会穿上跑鞋。
She will don a spacesuit before embarking on the mission. = 执行任务之前，她会穿上宇航服。

虚拟语气:
If he were going to a party, he would don his tuxedo. = 如果他要参加聚会，他会穿上他的燕尾服。
If she were a queen, she would don a crown. = 如果她是女王，她会戴上皇冠。

被动语态:
His uniform was donned with pride. = 他的制服是自豪地穿上的。
A protective mask was donned by all workers. = 所有工人都戴上了防护面具。

疑问句:
Has he donned his jacket yet? = 他已经穿上夹克了吗？
Will she don a veil for the ceremony? = 她会在仪式上戴面纱吗？

否定句:
He did not don his glasses because he could see clearly. = 他没有戴眼镜，因为他看得清楚。
She did not don her raincoat because it wasn't raining. = 她没有穿雨衣，因为没有下雨。
