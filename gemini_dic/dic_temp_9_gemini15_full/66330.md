excruciate:/ˈɛkskruʃieɪt/| vt.|使痛苦；使受折磨|To inflict severe suffering or agony on; to torment.|例句：The guilt over his actions began to excruciate him.  他的行为带来的罪恶感开始折磨他。

例句：The intense heat excruciated the workers in the field.  酷热折磨着田里的工人。


近义词：torment, torture, agonize, pain


反义词：soothe, comfort, relieve


词性：动词

词源:
The word "excruciate" comes from the Latin word "cruciatus," meaning "torment" or "cruel infliction of pain," ultimately derived from "crux," meaning "cross."  The prefix "ex-" intensifies the meaning.  The association with crucifixion is evident in its etymology.
