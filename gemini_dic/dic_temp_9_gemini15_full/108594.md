lead-ins:/ˈliːdɪnz/ | n. |开场白；引言；导语 | introductory words or phrases used to begin a conversation, speech, or piece of writing.  They help to smoothly transition into the main topic. |Example:  "As a lead-in to his speech, he told a humorous anecdote."  = “作为演讲的开场白，他讲了一个幽默的轶事。”  |近义词：introduction, preamble, preface, opening remarks |反义词：conclusion, ending
