nonpreferable: /ˌnɒnˈprɛfərəbl/ | adj. | 不受欢迎的；不可取的；非首选的 |  Not preferred; less desirable or suitable.  This adjective indicates something is not the best choice or option, often implying other alternatives are superior. | 例句：That solution is nonpreferable due to its high cost and complexity.  = 由于成本高昂且复杂，该方案不可取。

That approach is nonpreferable to the more efficient method we've developed. = 与我们开发的更高效的方法相比，这种方法不可取。


近义词：undesirable, unsuitable, unacceptable, inferior, less desirable, suboptimal


反义词：preferable, desirable, optimal, superior, best, ideal


词性：形容词
