custom: /ˈkʌstəm/ | n. |风俗；习惯；海关；客户定做 |  The local customs are quite different from ours.当地的风俗习惯和我们的截然不同。|近义词：tradition, habit, practice |反义词：standard, norm


custom: /ˈkʌstəm/ | v. |定制；订做 |I had my wedding dress custom-made. 我定制了我的婚纱。|近义词：tailor, personalize, individualize |反义词：standardize, mass-produce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They follow ancient customs. = 他们遵循古老的习俗。
She often customizes her clothes. = 她经常定制衣服。


一般过去时:
We observed their unique customs. = 我们观察了他们独特的习俗。
He customized his car with special features. = 他用特殊功能定制了他的汽车。


现在完成时:
They have adapted to the local customs. = 他们已经适应了当地的习俗。
She has customized several websites for clients. = 她已经为客户定制了几个网站。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:

词源:
The noun "custom" comes from the Old French word "custume," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "consuetudo," meaning "habit, custom, usage."  The verb form developed later, reflecting the act of creating something according to a customer's specifications.
