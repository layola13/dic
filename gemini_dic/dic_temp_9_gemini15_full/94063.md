impassibility:/ɪmˌpæsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 冷漠；无动于衷；不可通过性 | The inability to feel or show emotion;  lack of feeling; insensibility.  It can also refer to the state of being impossible to pass through. |例句：Her impassibility during the funeral surprised everyone. 她葬礼上的冷漠令所有人惊讶。

例句：The sheer impassibility of the mountain range made travel extremely difficult.  山脉的完全不可逾越性使得旅行极其困难。

近义词：apathy, indifference, insensibility, impenetrability

反义词：sensitivity, empathy, feeling, permeability
