laparoscopically:/ˌlæpəroʊˈskɑːpɪkli/| adv.|腹腔镜地，腹腔镜下| Performed through a small incision using a laparoscope.  This surgical technique minimizes invasiveness.|例句：The surgeon performed the procedure laparoscopically, resulting in a smaller scar and faster recovery.  外科医生采用腹腔镜手术，从而减少了疤痕，加快了恢复速度。

例句：The gallbladder was removed laparoscopically. 胆囊是通过腹腔镜手术切除的。


近义词：minimally invasively


反义词：openly (in the context of surgery)


词性：副词
