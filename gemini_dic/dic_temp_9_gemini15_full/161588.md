pottos:/ˈpɒtoʊz/ | n. |树懒猴|Slow-moving nocturnal primates found in Africa.  They are characterized by their large eyes, slow movements, and prehensile tails. Pottos are arboreal animals, meaning they live in trees. They are omnivores, feeding on fruits, insects, and other small animals.| Example: The potto silently moved through the branches, its large eyes scanning its surroundings.  树懒猴悄无声息地穿梭于树枝之间，它的大眼睛扫描着周围的环境。|近义词：slow loris (but distinct species)|反义词：N/A
