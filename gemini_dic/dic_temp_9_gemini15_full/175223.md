railcards:/ˈreɪlˌkɑːdz/| n.|铁路卡；火车票卡|A railcard is a discount card that allows you to purchase train tickets at a reduced price.  They are often targeted towards specific groups of people, such as students, seniors, or young people.|Example:  I bought a 16-25 Railcard to save money on my train journeys. 我买了一张16-25岁铁路卡来节省火车旅费。|近义词：discount card, travel card|反义词：full-fare ticket
