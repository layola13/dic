multistemmed:/ˌmʌltiˈstemd/| adj. |多茎的，多干的 |Having multiple stems or trunks.  The term typically refers to plants with more than one main stem arising from the base. |例句：The multistemmed rose bush filled the entire garden bed. 这株多茎玫瑰灌木丛占据了整个花坛。  The multistemmed tree provided ample shade. 这棵多干的树提供了充足的树荫。|近义词：poly stemmed, many-stemmed |反义词：single-stemmed, unbranched
