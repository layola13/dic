/kæf/ | n. |咖啡馆，小餐馆 |A small restaurant, typically one that serves coffee and light meals.  |例句：I met my friend at a charming little caf on Bleecker Street. 我在布利克街一家迷人的小咖啡馆遇到了我的朋友。|近义词：coffee shop, bistro, diner |反义词：large restaurant, hotel
