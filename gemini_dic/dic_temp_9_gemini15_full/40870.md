cooked-off: /kʊktˈɔːf/ | adj. | 烧焦的；烤焦的 |  Describes something that has been overcooked to the point of burning or charring, typically referring to food.  | The steak was cooked-off and inedible.  烤肉烧焦了，不能吃了。 The bottom of the cake was cooked-off. 蛋糕底部烤焦了。|  burnt, scorched, charred |  rare, undercooked | 形容词
