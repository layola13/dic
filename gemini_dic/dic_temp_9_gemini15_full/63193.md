entangling: /ɪnˈtæŋɡlɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 纠缠；使纠缠；缠绕；复杂的 |  Entangling refers to the act of becoming or causing something to become entangled, or intertwined in a complicated way.  It can describe physical entanglement (like threads or wires) or metaphorical entanglement (like complex relationships or situations). |

例句：

The fishing net became entangled around the coral. = 渔网缠绕在珊瑚上。

The two countries were entangled in a bitter trade dispute. = 这两个国家卷入了一场激烈的贸易纠纷。


近义词：intertwining, involving, complicating, snarling, confusing


反义词： disentangling, separating, simplifying, clarifying, untangling


词性:动词和形容词
