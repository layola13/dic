counterrally: /ˈkaʊntərˌræli/ | n. | 反抗集会，反示威游行 | A demonstration or protest organized in opposition to another demonstration or event.  It aims to counter the message or impact of the original gathering. | Example:  The mayor's announcement sparked a counterrally from concerned citizens. 市长的声明激起了民众的反示威游行。


The planned peace rally was met with a large counterrally from a group advocating for stricter border controls.  计划中的和平集会被一群主张加强边境管控的人群发起的反示威游行所抵制。| 近义词：counterdemonstration, protest, demonstration | 反义词：rally, demonstration (in the sense of supporting a cause) | 名词
