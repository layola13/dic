ricochetted: /ˈrɪkəʃeɪtɪd/ | v. | 反弹，跳跃 |  The past tense and past participle of "ricochet".  It describes the action of something bouncing off a surface at an angle.  |


例句：

The bullet ricochetted off the wall.  = 子弹击中墙壁后反弹。

The ball ricochetted across the court. = 球在球场上反弹。


近义词：bounce, rebound, carom


反义词：  None directly opposite, but words suggesting a straight trajectory like "penetrated" or "passed through" could be considered antonymic in context.


词性: Verb (过去式和过去分词)


USAGE->

词源:

The word "ricochet" comes from the French word "riochet," which in turn is likely derived from the Old Occitan word "ricot," meaning "to rebound."  The ultimate origin is likely onomatopoeic, imitating the sound of something bouncing.
