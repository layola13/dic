fortifier:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪər/| n. |强化剂；防御工事建造者|A fortifier is a substance added to food or drink to increase its nutritional value or to improve its keeping qualities.  It can also refer to someone who builds or strengthens fortifications.|例句： Vitamin C is often added as a fortifier to fruit juices.  维生素C经常被添加到果汁中作为强化剂。

The ancient Romans were master fortifier, building impressive walls and structures to protect their cities. 古罗马人是防御工事建造的大师，他们建造了令人印象深刻的城墙和建筑物来保护他们的城市。|近义词：reinforcer, strengthener |反义词：weakeners, debilitators
