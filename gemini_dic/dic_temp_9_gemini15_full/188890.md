rustiest:/ˈrʌstiəst/| adj.|最生锈的，最腐蚀的|Most affected by rust; showing the most signs of rust or decay.  The word is the superlative form of "rusty."|例句：The rustiest tools were discarded. 最生锈的工具被丢弃了。


The rustiest part of the ship was below the waterline. 船体最生锈的部分在水线以下。|近义词：most corroded, most oxidized, most deteriorated|反义词：newest, shiniest, pristine
