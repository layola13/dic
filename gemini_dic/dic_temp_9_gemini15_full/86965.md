hendecagonal:/ˌhɛndɪˈkæɡənəl/| adj.| 十一边形的；十一角形的 | Having eleven angles and eleven sides.  A hendecagonal shape is a polygon with eleven sides and eleven angles.  |Example: The mosaic featured a striking hendecagonal tile pattern.  这幅马赛克作品以醒目的十一边形瓷砖图案为特色。|近义词：undecagonal |反义词：triangular, square, pentagonal, etc. (shapes with fewer sides)
