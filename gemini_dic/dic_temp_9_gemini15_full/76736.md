game-changing: /ˈɡeɪmˌtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 改变游戏规则的；具有突破性的；革命性的 |  This adjective describes something that fundamentally alters the existing situation or way of doing things, often in a positive and significant way. It implies a major shift or transformation, making previous approaches obsolete or significantly less effective.  | 例句：The invention of the internet was a game-changing event.  互联网的发明是一个具有突破性的事件。

The new software is game-changing for our workflow. 这款新软件彻底改变了我们的工作流程。


近义词：revolutionary, transformative, groundbreaking, paradigm-shifting, epoch-making | 反义词：insignificant, inconsequential, trivial, minor
