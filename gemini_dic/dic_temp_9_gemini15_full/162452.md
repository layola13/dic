preadopted: /ˌpriːəˈdɑːptɪd/ | adj. | 预先收养的；已被预先收养的 |  Pre-adopted describes a child who has been legally selected for adoption before the finalization of the adoption process.  It implies a period of waiting or preparation before the adoption is officially complete. |  The pre-adopted child was eagerly awaiting his new family.  = 预先收养的孩子正渴望与他的新家庭团聚。 The agency placed several pre-adopted babies in foster care while the paperwork was finalized. = 该机构将几名已被预先收养的婴儿安置在寄养家庭，直到手续办理完毕。| 近义词:  chosen, selected (in the context of adoption) | 反义词: unadopted, orphaned (in the context of adoption)
