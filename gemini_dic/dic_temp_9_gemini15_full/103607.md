jointedly:/ˈdʒɔɪntɪdli/ | adv. | 共同地；联合地 |  done or experienced by two or more people together.  |例句：They jointly own the business. = 他们共同拥有这家企业。|例句：The report was jointly authored by three professors. = 这份报告由三位教授共同撰写。|近义词：together, collectively, collaboratively, in collaboration | 反义词：separately, individually


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They jointly run the company. = 他们共同经营这家公司。
We jointly decide on the budget. = 我们共同决定预算。

一般过去时:
They jointly signed the contract. = 他们共同签署了合同。
We jointly celebrated their birthday. = 我们共同庆祝了他们的生日。

现在完成时:
They have jointly completed the project. = 他们已经共同完成了项目。
We have jointly raised a significant amount of money. = 我们已经共同筹集了一大笔钱。

过去完成时:
They had jointly prepared for the presentation. = 他们已共同为演示做好了准备。
We had jointly agreed on the terms before the meeting. = 会议前我们已共同商定了条款。

将来时:
They will jointly launch a new product. = 他们将共同推出新产品。
We will jointly organize a conference next year. = 我们明年将共同组织一次会议。

虚拟语气:
If they jointly invested, they would have more capital. = 如果他们共同投资，他们就会有更多资本。
I wish they had jointly solved the problem. = 我希望他们共同解决了这个问题。

被动语态:
The decision was jointly made by the committee. = 该决定是由委员会共同做出的。
The award was jointly presented to the winners. = 奖项被共同颁发给获奖者。

疑问句:
Did they jointly agree to the proposal? = 他们是否共同同意了该提案？
Will they jointly fund the research? = 他们是否会共同资助这项研究？

否定句:
They did not jointly undertake the responsibility. = 他们没有共同承担责任。
We did not jointly make that decision. = 我们没有共同做出那个决定。
