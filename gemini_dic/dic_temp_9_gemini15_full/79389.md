glider:/ˈɡlaɪdər/| n.|滑翔机；滑翔者|A glider is an aircraft without an engine that is kept aloft by the force of the wind.  It is often launched by being towed by another aircraft or by being catapulted.|例句：The glider soared gracefully through the air. 这架滑翔机优雅地翱翔在空中。

例句：He is an experienced glider pilot. 他是一位经验丰富的滑翔机飞行员。|近义词：sailplane, hang glider|反义词：powered aircraft|名词
