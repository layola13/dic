drumbeats:/ˈdrʌmˌbiːts/| n. |鼓声；鼓点|The rhythmic sound produced by striking a drum.  It can refer to a single beat or a series of beats, often forming a rhythm or pattern.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe a repetitive or insistent sound or action.|例句：The drumbeats grew louder as the procession approached.  鼓声越来越大，队伍越来越近了。

例句：The steady drumbeats of the rain against the window kept me awake. 雨点持续不断地敲打着窗户，让我无法入睡。


近义词：percussions, rhythms, beats

反义词：silence


词性：名词
