ripeness:/ˈraɪpnəs/| n. |成熟度；成熟；完善|The state of being ripe; the quality of being fully developed or matured.  It can refer to fruit, vegetables, cheese, or even ideas or plans.|例句：The ripeness of the tomatoes indicated they were ready to be picked. 西红柿的成熟度表明它们已经可以采摘了。

例句：The ripeness of his arguments convinced the jury. 他论点的成熟度说服了陪审团。|近义词：maturity, fullness, readiness|反义词：immaturity, greenness, unripeness
