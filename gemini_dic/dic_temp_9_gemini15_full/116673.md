mattress:/ˈmætrəs/| n. |床垫|A mattress is a thick, soft pad filled with various materials, used for sleeping on. It is placed on a bed frame or the floor to provide comfort and support.|例句：I need to buy a new mattress; this one is too old and lumpy. 我需要买个新床垫；这个太旧而且太有疙瘩了。|近义词：bedding, pad|反义词:None.
