paprika:/ˈpæprɪkə/| n.|辣椒粉，红椒粉|A spice made from ground dried red peppers.  The flavor and pungency vary greatly depending on the type of pepper used and how it is processed. It ranges from mild to intensely hot.|例句：This goulash is delicious; the paprika gives it a wonderful rich flavor. 这道匈牙利牛肉汤很好吃；辣椒粉赋予它浓郁的风味。|例句：She sprinkled paprika over the roasted vegetables for extra color and flavor. 她把辣椒粉撒在烤蔬菜上，以增加颜色和风味。|近义词：red pepper flakes, chili powder|反义词：无
