cedarn:/ˈsiːdən/| adj.|雪松木的；雪松色的|Of, relating to, or resembling cedar.  The word often describes something made of cedar wood or having the color of cedar wood.|例句：The cedarn chest held generations of family heirlooms.  这雪松木箱子里装着几代人的传家宝。|例句：The painting featured cedarn hues of deep brown and reddish-brown. 这幅画以深棕色和红棕色的雪松色调为特色。|近义词：cedarwood, cedar-like|反义词：无
