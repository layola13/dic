glove:/ɡlʌv/| n. |手套|A covering for the hand, typically with separate sections for each finger and the thumb.  Gloves are worn to protect the hands from cold, injury, or dirt. |Example: I need to wear gloves when I work in the garden.  我需要戴上手套才能在花园里工作。|近义词：mitten (if fingerless), gauntlet|反义词：None
