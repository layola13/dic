preternatural:/ˌpriːtəˈnætʃərəl/ | adj. | 超自然的；不可思议的 |  Beyond what is natural or normal; supernatural.  It suggests something extraordinary or inexplicable that seems to defy the laws of nature. | 例句：He possessed a preternatural ability to predict the market's fluctuations. 他拥有预测市场波动的神奇能力。

The old house had a preternatural stillness that unnerved the visitors.  这座老房子有一种超乎寻常的寂静，让参观者感到不安。| 近义词：supernatural, paranormal, uncanny, extraordinary | 反义词：natural, normal, ordinary
