disassemble:/ˌdɪs.əˈsɛmbəl/ | vt. |拆卸；分解；解体 | To take something apart, separating it into its constituent pieces.  |例句：The mechanic disassembled the engine to repair it. 技工拆卸了引擎以便修理。
例句：We need to disassemble the furniture before moving. 我们搬家前需要把家具拆卸开来。|近义词：take apart, dismantle, decompose |反义词：assemble, construct, build |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disassembles the computer every year for cleaning. 他每年都会拆卸电脑进行清洁。
They disassemble the machine carefully before inspection. 他们在检查前小心地拆卸机器。

一般过去时:
The technician disassembled the faulty device. 技术人员拆卸了有故障的设备。
She disassembled the old toy and put the parts in a box. 她拆卸了旧玩具并将零件放在盒子里。

现在完成时:
I have disassembled the bike and cleaned all the parts. 我已经拆卸了自行车并清洁了所有零件。
They have disassembled the engine three times this month. 这个月他们已经三次拆卸发动机了。

过去完成时:
He had disassembled the clock before he realized it was antique. 他在意识到那块钟是古董之前就已经把它拆卸了。
We had disassembled the furniture before the movers arrived.  搬家工到达之前，我们已经把家具拆卸好了。

将来时:
