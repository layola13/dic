prerecommend:/ˌpriːˈrɛkəmend/ | vt. |预先推荐，事先推荐 | To recommend beforehand or in advance.  It implies a recommendation made before a formal or official recommendation. |例句：The committee prerecommended the candidate for the position before the final selection process. 委员会在最终甄选过程之前预先推荐了该候选人。

例句：The professor prerecommended several articles for further reading before the next class. 教授在下次课前预先推荐了几篇文章供进一步阅读。


近义词：recommend in advance, suggest beforehand, propose previously
反义词：disapprove beforehand, reject in advance

词性:动词
