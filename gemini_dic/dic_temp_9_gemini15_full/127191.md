natures:/ˈneɪtʃərz/ | n. |天性；自然；性质 |The inherent character or basic constitution of a person or thing.  It refers to the qualities or characteristics that are inherent and fundamental to something.  This word is the plural form of "nature". |例句：The natures of the two brothers were completely different.  两个兄弟的性格完全不同。

例句：His actions revealed his true nature.他的行为暴露了他的本性。


近义词：characteristics, qualities, dispositions, essences |反义词：artificiality, unnaturalness |词性:n.
