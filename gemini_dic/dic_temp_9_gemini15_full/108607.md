leaded:/ˈlɛdɪd/| adj.|含铅的，铅字排印的|Containing lead or made with lead type.  The term often refers to leaded gasoline or leaded type used in printing.|例句：Leaded gasoline is no longer used in many countries.  许多国家不再使用含铅汽油。

例句：The book was printed using leaded type, giving it a distinctive look. 这本书是用铅字排印的，呈现出一种独特的外观。


近义词：plumbed (in the context of containing lead),  typeset (in the context of printing)

反义词：lead-free, unleaded


词性：形容词
