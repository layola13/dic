rudderhead:/ˈrʌdəhed/| n. |舵手；笨蛋；傻瓜 |A rudderhead is the head of a rudder, a steering device on a ship or aircraft.  It is also used informally to refer to a stupid or foolish person. |Example: The captain skillfully maneuvered the ship by using the rudderhead.  船长熟练地利用舵柄操纵船只。  |Example: Don't be such a rudderhead; think before you act! 不要那么傻，行动前先想想！|近义词：helm, tiller (for the ship part); fool, idiot, dunce |反义词：genius, mastermind, intellect
