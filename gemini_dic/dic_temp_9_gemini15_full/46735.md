dandiprat:/ˈdændɪpræt/| n. |小矮子，小不点儿| A diminutive or undersized person.  The word is chiefly dialectal and somewhat archaic, evoking a sense of quaintness or even mild derision.|例句：He was a dandiprat of a man, barely reaching five feet tall. = 他是个小不点儿，身高还不到五英尺。 He called the small child a dandiprat. = 他把那个小孩叫做小不点儿。|近义词：runt, shrimp, midget, little person|反义词：giant, colossus, titan|名词
