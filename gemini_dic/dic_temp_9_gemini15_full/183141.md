repost:/ˌriːˈpoʊst/ | vt. | 再次发布，转发 | To post (something) again, especially on a social media platform.  | Example: I decided to repost her inspiring message on my Instagram. 我决定在我的Instagram上转发她鼓舞人心的信息。 |近义词：reshare, republish, retweet | 反义词：delete, remove


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She reposts interesting articles every day. = 她每天都会转发有趣的文章。
He reposts his friend's photos frequently. = 他经常转发朋友的照片。


一般过去时:
I reposted that video yesterday. = 我昨天转发了那个视频。
They reposted the announcement on their website. = 他们在他们的网站上再次发布了公告。


现在完成时:
She has reposted several times this week. = 她本周已经转发了好几次了。
He has reposted all of his colleague's posts. = 他已经转发了他所有同事的帖子。


过去完成时:
I had reposted the article before he saw it. = 我在他看到那篇文章之前就转发了。
They had reposted the news before the meeting. = 他们在会议前就已经转发了这条新闻。


将来时:
I will repost your photo tomorrow. = 我明天会转发你的照片。
She will repost the update on her blog. = 她将在她的博客上再次发布更新。


虚拟语气:
If I had seen that post earlier, I would have reposted it. = 如果我早点看到那篇文章，我就转发了。
I wish I had reposted that inspirational quote. = 我真希望我转发了那句励志名言。


被动语态:
The post was reposted thousands of times. = 这篇文章被转发了数千次。
The announcement was reposted on all social media platforms. = 公告在所有社交媒体平台上都被再次发布。


疑问句:
Have you reposted my story yet? = 你转发我的故事了吗？
Will you repost this information to your followers? = 你会把这个信息转发给你的粉丝吗？


否定句:
I didn't repost the article because it was controversial. = 我没有转发那篇文章，因为它有争议。
She hasn't reposted anything today. = 她今天还没有转发任何东西。


PHRASE->
repost a photo = 转发照片
repost a video = 转发视频
repost an article = 转发文章
repost someone's tweet = 转发某人的推文
repost on Instagram = 在Instagram上转发
