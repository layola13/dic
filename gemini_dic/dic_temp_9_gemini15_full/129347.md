noisy:/ˈnɔɪzi/| adj.| 吵闹的，喧嚣的 |Making a lot of noise; loud.|The children were very noisy during the movie.  孩子们在电影期间非常吵闹。 The city streets were noisy at night. 城市街道晚上很吵闹。|近义词：loud, clamorous, boisterous |反义词：quiet, silent, peaceful

USAGE->

The market is noisy this morning. = 今早市场很吵闹。
The street is always noisy during rush hour. = 尖峰时间街道总是很吵闹。

一般过去时:
The party was very noisy last night. = 昨晚派对非常吵闹。
