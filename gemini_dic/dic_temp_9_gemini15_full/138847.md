nuring:/ˈnɜːrɪŋ/|/ˈnɝːrɪŋ/| n.| 护理；培育；养育 |The act of caring for someone, especially a sick or injured person.  It can also refer to the process of fostering growth or development. |例句：The nursing staff provided excellent care to the patients.  护理人员为病人提供了优质的护理。

例句：She devoted her life to nursing the sick and needy. 她毕生致力于护理病人和穷人。


近义词：care, tending, fostering, nurturing


反义词：neglecting, ignoring, abandoning


词性：名词
