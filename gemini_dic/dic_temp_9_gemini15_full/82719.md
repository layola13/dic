guidebook:/ˈɡaɪdbʊk/ | n. |指南，旅行指南 |A book giving information and advice about a place, activity, or subject.  It often includes maps, itineraries, and practical tips. |Example: I bought a guidebook to help me plan my trip to Japan. 我买了一本指南来帮助我计划去日本的旅行。|近义词：manual, handbook, directory |反义词：None
