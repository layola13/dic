harpooners:/hɑːˈpuːnərz/| n. |捕鲸叉手；用鱼叉捕鱼的人|People who use harpoons to catch whales or other large fish.  They are skilled in operating harpoons and often work as part of a whaling crew.|例句：The experienced harpooners skillfully launched their harpoons at the whale.  经验丰富的捕鲸叉手熟练地向鲸鱼投掷鱼叉。

The harpooners returned to shore with a bountiful catch of tuna. 捕鲸叉手们满载金枪鱼回到岸上。|近义词：whalers, fishermen|反义词:None specifically contrasting.
