reverendest:/ˈrɛvərəndɪst/| adj.|最尊敬的，最神圣的|Most reverend;  showing the greatest reverence or respect, especially towards a deity or religious figure.  The word is a superlative form of "reverend," implying the highest degree of reverence.|例句：The reverendest of monks offered a prayer for the departed soul. 最受尊敬的僧侣为逝去的灵魂祈祷。The most reverendest priest blessed the congregation. 最受尊敬的牧师祝福了会众。|近义词：most revered, most holy, most sacred|反义词：profane, irreverent
