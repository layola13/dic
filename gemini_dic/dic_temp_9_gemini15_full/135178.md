nonoptimistically:/ˌnɒnˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪkli/|/ˌnɑːnˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪkli/| adj. |不乐观地，悲观地 |Adverb meaning in a way that shows a lack of optimism or hope; pessimistically.  It describes actions or statements made without expecting a positive outcome. |He spoke nonoptimistically about the chances of success. 他不乐观地谈论了成功的几率。  She looked nonoptimistically at the towering pile of work. 她不乐观地看着那堆积如山的待办事项。|pessimistically, gloomily, dejectedly |optimistically, hopefully, confidently|
