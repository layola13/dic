词源:
The word "hard-rocker" is a compound word.  "Hard rock" refers to a genre of rock music characterized by a heavy, distorted guitar sound, strong rhythms, and often aggressive vocals. "-er" is a suffix that forms nouns denoting a person who does something or is of a certain kind.  Therefore, "hard-rocker" literally means a person who plays or is associated with hard rock music.
