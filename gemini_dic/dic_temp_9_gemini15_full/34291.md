clarifications:/ˌklærɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |澄清；解释；说明 |Explanations or statements that make something clearer and easier to understand.  The process of making something clear or easier to understand. |例句：He offered clarifications on the controversial points of his speech. 他对演讲中引起争议的几点作了解释。

例句：Further clarifications are needed before we can proceed. 在我们继续之前，需要进一步的解释。


近义词：explanations, elucidations, interpretations, illuminations |反义词：confusions, ambiguities, obscurations |词性:n.
