floody:/ˈflʌdi/ | adj. | 洪水的；泛滥的；大量的 | Floody refers to something characterized by or resembling a flood; overflowing with water; or excessively abundant.  It suggests a state of being overwhelmed or inundated. |例句：The river became floody after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水泛滥了。

The floody streets made driving impossible. 洪水淹没了街道，使得车辆无法通行。


近义词：inundated, flooded, overflowing, deluge, torrential | 反义词：dry, arid, parched


词性:形容词
