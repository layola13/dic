preabstract:/ˌpriːˈæbstrækt/| adj.| 预摘要的；预先摘要的 |  A preabstract is a summary or abstract prepared before a full text is written. It outlines the main points and arguments that will be developed in the complete work.  This is often used in academic writing and research proposals to give a concise overview of the planned research before undertaking the full project. |Example: The researcher submitted a preabstract of their dissertation before beginning the data collection.  = 研究人员在开始数据收集之前提交了他们的论文预摘要。|近义词： preliminary abstract, advance summary |反义词：full abstract, final summary |
