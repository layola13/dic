electroactive:/ɪˌlɛkˈtroʊæktɪv/ | adj. | 电化学活性的；电活性 | Capable of undergoing a chemical reaction when an electric current or potential is applied.  This typically involves a change in oxidation state.  Electroactive materials are used in various applications, including batteries, sensors, and actuators. | Example:  The electroactive polymer showed a significant change in conductivity upon application of a voltage.  电活性聚合物在施加电压后表现出明显的电导率变化。 | 近义词：electrocatalytic, redox-active | 反义词：electroinactive
