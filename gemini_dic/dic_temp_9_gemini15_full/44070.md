crinkliest: /ˈkrɪŋklɪst/ | adj. | 最皱的，最褶皱的 | Most wrinkled; having many small wrinkles or creases.  The word is the superlative form of "crinkled". It describes a surface that is highly wrinkled or creased. | 例句：The crinkliest page in the book was the oldest. 书中最皱的一页是最旧的。

The crinkliest piece of paper was barely legible. 那张最皱的纸几乎无法辨认。


近义词：most wrinkled, most creased, most rumpled | 反义词：smoothest, flattest


词性：形容词
