decisioning: /dɪˈsɪʒənɪŋ/ | n. | 决策过程；决策制定 | The process of making decisions.  It often involves evaluating options, considering potential consequences, and selecting the best course of action.  This term emphasizes the active process of making decisions, rather than just the decision itself. | Example:  The company's decisioning process is quite rigorous, involving multiple levels of approval. 公司的决策过程非常严格，需要经过多层审批。  The team spent hours in decisioning, weighing the pros and cons of each option.  团队花了几个小时做决策，权衡每个选项的利弊。 | 近义词：decision-making, judgment, deliberation | 反义词：indecision, procrastination | 名词
