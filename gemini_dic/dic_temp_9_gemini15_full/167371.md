pretty penny:/ˈprɪti ˈpɛni/| n.|一大笔钱；相当多的钱 |A considerable sum of money.  It implies a significant expenditure, often more than one would ideally like to spend. |例句：That car cost a pretty penny. = 那辆车花了一大笔钱。

That vacation set me back a pretty penny. = 那次假期花了我一大笔钱。

近义词：a lot of money, a small fortune, a king's ransom

反义词：a pittance, peanuts, cheap
