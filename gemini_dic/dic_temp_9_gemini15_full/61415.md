/ɛmˈbɔːɡ/ | v. | 实施禁运；禁止 | To officially prevent something, especially goods, from being sent to a particular place or from being used.  | The government embargoed all exports of oil. 政府禁止所有石油出口。|近义词：ban, prohibit, blockade | 反义词：permit, allow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government embargoes all arms sales to the warring nations. = 政府禁止向交战国家出售武器。
They embargo all goods from that country. = 他们禁止来自那个国家的所有商品。


一般过去时:
The US embargoed Cuba in 1960. = 美国在1960年对古巴实施了禁运。
They embargoed the shipment of weapons. = 他们禁止了武器的运输。


现在完成时:
The country has embargoed the import of certain chemicals. = 该国已经禁止进口某些化学品。
Many countries have embargoed Russian oil. = 许多国家已经禁止进口俄罗斯石油。


过去完成时:
Before the war, they had embargoed all trade with the enemy. = 在战争爆发前，他们已经禁止了与敌人的所有贸易。
The company had embargoed the release of the product due to safety concerns. = 由于安全问题，该公司已经禁止了该产品的发布。


将来时:
The UN will embargo further military supplies. = 联合国将禁止进一步的军事物资供应。
They will embargo the import of certain fruits if the disease spreads.  = 如果这种疾病蔓延，他们将禁止进口某些水果。


虚拟语气:
If the conflict escalated, they would embargo all trade with the aggressor nation. = 如果冲突升级，他们将禁止与侵略国进行所有贸易。
I wish they had not embargoed the medicine; many people are suffering. = 我真希望他们没有禁止这种药；很多人都在受苦。


被动语态:
All exports were embargoed by the government. = 所有出口都被政府禁止了。
The shipment of goods was embargoed due to violations of international law. = 货物运输由于违反国际法而被禁止。


疑问句:
Was the export of timber embargoed? = 木材出口被禁止了吗？
Will the import of these materials be embargoed? = 这些材料的进口会被禁止吗？


否定句:
The government did not embargo food supplies. = 政府没有禁止食品供应。
They did not embargo the necessary medical equipment. = 他们没有禁止必要的医疗设备。


PHRASE->
trade embargo = 贸易禁运
oil embargo = 石油禁运
arms embargo = 武器禁运
embargo on exports = 出口禁运
embargo on imports = 进口禁运
