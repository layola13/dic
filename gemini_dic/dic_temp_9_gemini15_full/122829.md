moleskin:/ˈmoʊlskɪn/| n. |鼹鼠皮，细棉绒布 |A type of soft, napped fabric, originally made from the skin of moles, now typically made from cotton.  It's known for its durability and softness.|例句：His moleskin trousers were worn but comfortable. 他的鼹鼠皮裤很旧但很舒服。|例句：I prefer to write in a moleskin notebook. 我更喜欢用细棉绒布笔记本写字。|近义词：corduroy, suede|反义词：None specifically, but perhaps coarse fabrics like burlap.
