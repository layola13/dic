overassertiveness:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrtɪvnəs/ | n. | 过分自信；过于强势 |  The act of being excessively assertive; dominating or controlling behavior.  It often involves disregarding the feelings or opinions of others.  | Example: Her overassertiveness in meetings alienated her colleagues.  她的会议上的过分强势疏远了她的同事。  |近义词:  aggressiveness, bossiness, dominance, pushiness | 反义词: submissiveness, passivity, meekness | 名词

词源：
Over- (prefix indicating excess) + assertive (adj. expressing opinions or desires strongly and confidently) + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality)

The word "assertive" comes from the Latin word "assertus," the past participle of "asserere," meaning "to affirm," "to claim," or "to maintain."
