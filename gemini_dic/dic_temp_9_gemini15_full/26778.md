caespitose:/ˈkæs.pɪ.toʊs/ | adj. |丛生的；簇生的；草丛状的 | Growing in dense tufts or clumps, like grass.  The term refers to plants that grow in close clusters, forming a mat-like or turf-like structure. | Example:  The caespitose grasses formed a thick, even carpet across the hillside.  丛生的草形成了覆盖山坡的一层厚厚的、均匀的地毯。|近义词：tufted, clumped, aggregated |反义词：scattered, dispersed
