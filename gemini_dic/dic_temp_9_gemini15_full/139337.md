obrogate:/ˈɒbrəɡeɪt/ | vt. |废除；取消；撤销 | To abolish or annul (a law, custom, etc.) by authority.  To override or disregard (something, especially a rule or principle).|


例句：

The new law obrogated the previous regulations. = 新法律废除了之前的规章制度。

The court decided to obrogate the contract due to a breach of contract. = 法院决定由于违反合同而废除合同。


近义词：abolish, annul, repeal, invalidate, revoke, rescind


反义词：establish, enact, ratify, confirm, institute


词性：动词
