kinesiatrics:/ˌkaɪniːziˈætriks/| n.|运动疗法，肌动学治疗|The study and treatment of movement disorders using therapeutic exercise. Kinesiatrics involves the assessment and treatment of musculoskeletal problems that affect movement.|例句：Kinesiatrics is a valuable approach to treating chronic back pain. 运动疗法是治疗慢性背痛的有效方法。|近义词：physical therapy, physiotherapy, exercise therapy|反义词:None.
