defoliate:/diːˈfoʊliˌeɪt/|/ˈdiːfoʊlieɪt/| vt.|落叶；使落叶；除树叶|To remove leaves from (a tree or plant).  The process can be natural (e.g., due to seasonal changes) or artificial (e.g., caused by herbicides or disease).|例句：The trees were defoliated by the harsh winter.  冬季严寒使树木落叶了。

The storm defoliated the entire orchard.暴风雨使整个果园的树叶都掉光了。


近义词：strip, denude, bare|反义词：leaf out, refoliate|动词
