developer:/dɪˈvɛləpər/ | n. | 开发人员，开发者 | A person who creates or improves computer programs, websites, or other software.  They are also involved in the design and implementation of software systems. | Example:  She's a skilled developer with expertise in Java and Python. 她是一位熟练的开发者，精通Java和Python。 |近义词：programmer, coder, software engineer |反义词：user, consumer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The developer writes clean code. = 开发人员编写整洁的代码。
She develops mobile applications. = 她开发移动应用程序。

一般过去时:
He developed a new algorithm last week. = 他上周开发了一个新算法。
They developed a successful software product. = 他们开发了一个成功的软件产品。

现在完成时:
We have developed several successful games. = 我们已经开发了几款成功的游戏。
The company has developed a new strategy. = 公司制定了一个新战略。

过去完成时:
They had developed the prototype before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前已经开发了原型。
She had developed a strong understanding of the codebase. = 她已经对代码库有了深入的了解。

将来时:
We will develop a new feature next month. = 我们下个月将开发一个新功能。
They will develop a more efficient system. = 他们将开发一个更高效的系统。

虚拟语气:
If I were a developer, I would build a better website. = 如果我是开发者，我会创建一个更好的网站。
It is important that the developers complete the project on time. = 开发人员按时完成项目很重要。

被动语态:
The software was developed by a team of experts. = 该软件由专家团队开发。
The new feature is being developed currently. = 新功能正在开发中。

疑问句:
Who developed this application? = 谁开发了这个应用程序？
What programming languages are you developing in? = 你用什么编程语言进行开发？

否定句:
He did not develop the application alone. = 他没有独自开发这个应用程序。
They haven't developed a solution yet. = 他们还没有开发出解决方案。
