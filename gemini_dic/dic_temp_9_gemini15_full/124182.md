mosaicking:/mɒˈzeɪkɪŋ/| n.|马赛克镶嵌；图像拼接|The process of combining multiple images to create a larger, more comprehensive image.  This is often done to create high-resolution images from smaller ones, or to stitch together overlapping photographs to create a panoramic view.|例句：The software performs mosaicking of the satellite images to create a detailed map.  软件对卫星图像进行拼接以创建详细的地图。


例句：High-resolution mosaicking is crucial for creating detailed maps from aerial photography. 高分辨率图像拼接对于根据航拍照片创建详细地图至关重要。


近义词：image stitching, image compositing, photo stitching


反义词：None  (反义词难以找到，因为这是个描述过程的词)


词性：名词
