kneeling: /ˈniːlɪŋ/ | v.ing | 跪着；跪拜 |  The act of being on one's knees.  It can be an act of prayer, submission, or protest. | 例句：The children were kneeling in prayer. 孩子们正跪着祈祷。  She knelt before the altar. 她跪在祭坛前。 | 近义词：prostrating, genuflecting | 反义词：standing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is kneeling in the church. = 他正在教堂里跪着。
They are kneeling to pray. = 他们在跪着祈祷。


一般过去时:
She knelt down to tie her shoelaces. = 她跪下系鞋带。
He knelt before the king. = 他跪在国王面前。


现在完成时:
She has been kneeling for hours. = 她已经跪了好几个小时了。
They have knelt down in respect. = 他们已经为了表示尊重而跪下了。


过去完成时:
He had knelt before the altar before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前他已经在祭坛前跪下了。
She had been kneeling for so long her legs ached. = 她跪了太久，腿都酸了。


将来时:
They will be kneeling during the wedding ceremony. = 婚礼仪式上他们将会跪着。
He will kneel to propose to her. = 他将要跪下向她求婚。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would kneel and beg for forgiveness. = 如果我是你，我会跪下求饶。
I wish I had knelt down to offer my condolences. = 我真希望我当时跪下表示慰问。


被动语态:
The statue depicts a figure kneeling. = 雕像描绘了一个跪着的人物。
The prayer mat was frequently knelt upon. = 祈祷垫经常被跪在上面。


疑问句:
Is he kneeling or standing? = 他是跪着还是站着？
Were they kneeling when you saw them? = 当你看到他们的时候，他们在跪着吗？


否定句:
He wasn't kneeling; he was sitting. = 他不是跪着，他是坐着。
They did not kneel during the anthem. = 他们国歌期间没有跪下。


PHRASE->
fall to one's knees = 跪下
kneel down = 跪下
kneel before = 跪在...面前
get down on one's knees = 跪下
on bended knee = 跪着
