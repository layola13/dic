moonseed:/ˈmuːnsiːd/| n. |月籽，木防己属植物|A moonseed is a plant of the genus *Menispermum*, having a crescent-shaped seed.  Moonseeds are climbing plants with heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers.  Some species are poisonous. | Example: The moonseed vine climbed high into the trees. 月籽藤蔓攀爬到树上很高的地方。|近义词:  (lacks direct synonyms, needs to be described by characteristics) climbing plant, vine|反义词: (no direct antonyms)|名词
