cinnabars:/ˈsɪnəbɑːrz/| n. |朱砂；辰砂|A bright red mercury sulfide mineral, used as a pigment and in traditional medicine.  It is also known as vermilion when used as a pigment.|例句：The ancient Egyptians used cinnabars in their cosmetics. 古埃及人会在化妆品中使用朱砂。|例句：The vibrant red of the painting was achieved using cinnabars.  这幅画鲜艳的红色是用朱砂实现的。|近义词：vermilion, mercury sulfide|反义词：N/A
