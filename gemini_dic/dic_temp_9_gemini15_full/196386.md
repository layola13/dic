sermonizes:/ˈsɜːrmənaɪzɪz/ | v. |说教；教训；冗长地训斥 | To deliver a sermon; to preach or lecture tediously or moralistically.  |例句：The priest sermonized on the importance of charity.  牧师冗长地训斥了慈善的重要性。 He constantly sermonizes about the dangers of smoking. 他不断地教训吸烟的危害。|近义词：preach, lecture, moralize, preachify |反义词：praise, compliment


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He sermonizes every Sunday. 他每周日都布道。
She sermonizes about the virtues of hard work. 她总是说教勤劳的美德。


一般过去时:
The minister sermonized on forgiveness. 牧师就宽恕问题进行了布道。
My father sermonized to me about my poor grades. 我父亲就我的低分教训了我一顿。


现在完成时:
He has sermonized on this topic many times. 他已经就此主题布道多次了。
She has sermonized to her children about the importance of honesty. 她已经多次告诫她的孩子们诚实的重要性。


过去完成时:
The teacher had sermonized about the dangers of plagiarism before the exam. 老师在考试前就剽窃的危害进行了说教。
He had sermonized extensively on the subject before concluding his speech. 他在结束演讲前对这个主题进行了广泛的阐述。


将来时:
The pastor will sermonize on the parable of the prodigal son. 牧师将就浪子回头的寓言进行布道。
She will sermonize again next week about responsible pet ownership. 她下周将再次就负责任的宠物饲养问题进行说教。


虚拟语气:
If he sermonized less, people might listen more. 如果他少说教一些，人们可能会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't sermonize so much. 我希望他不那么爱说教。


被动语态:
The importance of community was sermonized throughout the meeting.  整个会议都在强调社区的重要性。
The dangers of drug abuse were sermonized to the students. 学生们受到了关于毒品危害的训诫。


疑问句:
Does he often sermonize to his congregation? 他经常向他的会众布道吗？
Will the speaker sermonize for the entire hour? 演讲者会讲满一小时吗？


否定句:
He didn't sermonize at all during the service. 他在仪式上根本没有布道。
She doesn't sermonize unnecessarily. 她不会不必要地说教。


PHRASE->
sermonize on... 就...说教
sermonize about...  关于...说教
sermonize against... 反对...说教
sermonize to... 向...说教
sermonize endlessly  没完没了地说教
