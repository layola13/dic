scatt:/skæt/| v.| 散布，驱散，四散奔逃 | to scatter something means to throw or spread it over a wide area.  |The wind scattered the leaves across the yard. 风把树叶吹散在院子里。| to disperse, spread, fling | gather, collect |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer scatters seeds in the field. = 农民在田里撒种子。
The children scatter when they see the dog. = 孩子们看到狗就四散跑开了。


一般过去时:
He scattered the ashes of his father. = 他撒了他父亲的骨灰。
The crowd scattered after the concert. = 音乐会结束后人群散开了。


现在完成时:
The wind has scattered the papers all over the floor. = 风把纸张吹得到处都是。
The birds have scattered across the sky. = 鸟儿们飞散在天空各处。


过去完成时:
They had scattered before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前他们已经四散逃跑了。
The rain had scattered the picnic-goers. = 雨把野餐的人们打散了。


将来时:
The police will scatter the protesters. = 警察将会驱散抗议者。
I will scatter flower petals at the wedding. = 我会在婚礼上撒花瓣。


虚拟语气:
If I had scattered the seeds earlier, the plants would be taller. = 如果我早点撒种子，植物就会长得更高。
I wish I had scattered the salt more evenly. = 我希望我把盐撒得更均匀一些。


被动语态:
The seeds were scattered by the wind. = 种子被风吹散了。
The crowd was scattered by the police. = 人群被警察驱散了。


疑问句:
Did the wind scatter the leaves? = 风把树叶吹散了吗？
Where did the children scatter to? = 孩子们跑到哪里去了？


否定句:
He didn't scatter the toys after playing. = 他玩完后没有把玩具收拾好。
The rain didn't scatter the audience. = 雨没有驱散观众。
