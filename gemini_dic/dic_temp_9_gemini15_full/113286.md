luteous:/ˈluːtiəs/| adj.|黄色的，土黄色的|Of a yellowish-brown color; resembling the color of lute.  The word evokes a sense of a dull, earthy yellow rather than a bright, sunny yellow.|The luteous fungus grew in the damp corner.  这个土黄色的真菌生长在潮湿的角落。 The bird had luteous plumage.  这只鸟有着土黄色的羽毛。|近义词：yellowish, tawny, ochre, buff|反义词：blue, green, black
