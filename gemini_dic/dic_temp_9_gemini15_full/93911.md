immovableness: /ˌɪˌmuːvəˈbɪlnəs/ | n. | 不动性；坚定；坚决 | The quality or state of being immovable;  firmness; steadfastness.  It refers to something that cannot be moved, either physically or figuratively.  This word emphasizes the resistance to movement or change. |

例句：

The immovableness of the mountain was awe-inspiring.  山峰的巍峨不动令人敬畏。

His immovableness in the face of adversity showed his great strength of character. 他面对逆境时的坚定不移展现了他强大的性格。


近义词：fixity, steadfastness, firmness, stability, rigidity

反义词：mobility, flexibility, changeability, instability, volatility

词性：名词
