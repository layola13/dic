heavily:/ˈhɛvɪli/ | adv. | 大量地；沉重地；严重地 |  Intensifies the meaning of the verb, adjective, or adverb it modifies, indicating a large degree or intensity.  | 例句：It rained heavily yesterday. = 昨天雨下得很大。
It snows heavily in the mountains. = 山区下大雪。| 近义词：intensely, severely, strongly, profoundly | 反义词：lightly, slightly, mildly
