joshing:/ˈdʒɒʃɪŋ/| v.|开玩笑，戏弄|To josh someone means to tease or playfully mock them.  It implies a friendly and lighthearted tone, not intended to cause offense. |例句：He was just joshing you; he didn't mean to hurt your feelings. 他只是跟你开玩笑，并非有意伤害你的感情。|例句：Stop joshing around and tell me what's really going on. 别再开玩笑了，告诉我到底发生了什么。|近义词：tease, kid, joke, banter|反义词：insult, offend, be serious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He joshes his friends all the time. = 他总是跟朋友开玩笑。
She joshes him about his new haircut. = 她取笑他的新发型。


一般过去时:
They joshed him about his clumsy dance moves. = 他们取笑他笨拙的舞姿。
