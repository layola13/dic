exorcise:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ | vt. |驱除（尤指邪灵）；消除（坏念等） | To drive out (evil spirits); to get rid of (bad thoughts, feelings, etc.) |例句：The priest exorcised the demon from the possessed girl.  牧师将附在女孩身上的恶魔驱逐出去了。|例句：He tried to exorcise his fear of failure by working harder. 他努力工作试图消除对失败的恐惧。|近义词：expel, banish, cast out |反义词：summon, invite, harbor
