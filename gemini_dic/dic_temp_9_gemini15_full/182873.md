repents:/rɪˈpents/| v.|后悔，悔改|To feel or express sincere regret or remorse about one's wrongdoing or sin; to change one's mind or behavior.|He repented his hasty decision. 他后悔自己草率的决定。|近义词：regret, rue, atone for|反义词:rejoice, celebrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repents his sins daily. = 他每天都忏悔自己的罪过。
She repents her unkind words. = 她后悔说了那些刻薄的话。


一般过去时:
He repented his actions after the damage was done. = 事情造成损失后，他后悔了自己的行为。
She repented her decision to leave her job. = 她后悔了辞职的决定。


现在完成时:
He has repented his past mistakes and is trying to make amends. = 他已经悔过过去的错误，并试图弥补。
She has repented her youthful indiscretions. = 她已经悔过年轻时的轻率行为。


过去完成时:
He had repented his actions before he confessed to them. = 在他坦白之前，他已经后悔了自己的行为。
She had repented her harsh words before seeing him again. = 在再次见到他之前，她已经后悔了自己说过的刻薄话。


将来时:
He will repent his actions when he sees the consequences. = 当他看到后果时，他会后悔自己的行为。
She will repent her decision if she doesn't find a better job. = 如果她找不到更好的工作，她会后悔自己的决定。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had repented sooner. = 我希望我早点悔过。
If he had repented earlier, things might have been different. = 如果他早点悔过，情况可能就不一样了。


被动语态:
His sins were repented for by the priest. = 他的罪过由牧师代为忏悔。
Her mistakes were eventually repented for. = 她的错误最终得到了忏悔。


疑问句:
Does he truly repent? = 他真的悔过吗？
Will she ever repent her actions? = 她会后悔自己的行为吗？


否定句:
He does not repent his actions. = 他不后悔自己的行为。
She did not repent her decision to move. = 她不后悔搬家的决定。


PHRASE->
repent one's sins = 悔过自己的罪孽
repent in dust and ashes = 痛悔莫及
repent at leisure = 从容悔过
repent of one's folly = 后悔自己的愚蠢
repent one's errors = 后悔自己的错误
