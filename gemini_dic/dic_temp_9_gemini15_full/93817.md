immigrant:/ˈɪmɪɡrənt/| n. |移民| A person who has come to live permanently in a foreign country. |Example:  Many immigrants have enriched the cultural landscape of this city. 许多移民丰富了这座城市的文化景象。|近义词: settler, newcomer |反义词: emigrant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an immigrant from Mexico. 他是一位来自墨西哥的移民。
They are immigrants who seek better opportunities.  他们是寻求更好机会的移民。


一般过去时:
She was an immigrant who arrived ten years ago. 她是一位十年前到达的移民。
My grandparents were immigrants who built a new life here. 我的祖父母是移民，在这里建立了新的生活。


现在完成时:
Many immigrants have faced challenges integrating into society. 许多移民在融入社会方面面临挑战。
He has been an immigrant in this country for over two decades. 他在这个国家做了二十多年的移民。


过去完成时:
Before she immigrated, she had lived in her home country for 25 years. 在移民之前，她在祖国生活了25年。
They had already secured jobs before they immigrated to the US. 他们在移民美国之前已经找到了工作。


将来时:
She will be an immigrant to Canada next year. 她明年将成为加拿大移民。
They will become immigrants once their visa applications are approved. 一旦他们的签证申请获得批准，他们就会成为移民。


虚拟语气:
If I were an immigrant, I would understand their struggles. 如果我是个移民，我会理解他们的挣扎。
I wish I had been an immigrant sooner to experience this culture. 我希望我早点成为移民来体验这种文化。


被动语态:
Many immigrants are often discriminated against. 许多移民经常受到歧视。
The immigrants were welcomed by the local community. 移民受到了当地社区的欢迎。


疑问句:
Is he an immigrant? 他是移民吗？
Are they legal immigrants? 他们是合法移民吗？


否定句:
He is not an immigrant; he is a citizen. 他不是移民；他是公民。
They are not immigrants; they are tourists. 他们不是移民；他们是游客。
