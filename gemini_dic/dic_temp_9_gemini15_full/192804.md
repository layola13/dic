seams:/siːmz/| n.|接缝；接口；外观；迹象|A seam is a line where two pieces of fabric or other material are joined together. It can also refer to a line or crack that appears on a surface.|例句：The seams of the trousers were neatly stitched.  这条裤子的缝线缝得很整齐。|例句：There were cracks appearing in the seams of the old building. 这栋旧建筑的接缝处出现了裂缝。|近义词：joint, juncture, suture|反义词：gap, opening
