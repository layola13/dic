monotone:/ˈmɒnəʊtəʊn/ /ˈmɑːnətoʊn/| adj. n.| 单调的；单音的；无变化的；单调乏味的 | Having or characterized by only one tone; monotonous.  A single, unvaried tone or pitch. |例句：His voice was monotone, lacking any inflection. 他的声音很单调，没有任何抑扬顿挫。

例句：The monotone of the machine made the workers sleepy. 机器单调的声音让工人们昏昏欲睡。

近义词：monotonous, uniform, unvaried, flat

反义词：varied, diverse, colorful, vibrant

词性:形容词，名词


词源:  From Middle French *monotone, from Latin mono- "single" + tonos "tone".
