canorousness:/kəˈnɔːrəsnəs/ | n. |悦耳；响亮；声音洪亮 |The quality of being canorous; melodiousness; resonance.  It refers to the pleasing quality of a sound, particularly a musical or vocal sound, characterized by richness, fullness, and clarity. |例句：The canorousness of the choir filled the cathedral.  教堂里回荡着合唱团悦耳的歌声。

The canorousness of the nightingale's song enchanted the listeners. 夜莺歌声的悦耳动听使听者着迷。


近义词：melodiousness, resonance, euphony, musicality |反义词：harshness, discordance, cacophony


词性：名词


词源：源自于形容词canorous，其词根来自拉丁语 *canō "I sing"。  因此，canorousness 本身体现了“歌唱般”的悦耳性质。
