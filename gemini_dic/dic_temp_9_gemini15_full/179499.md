reeding:/ˈriːdɪŋ/| n.|阅读；朗读|The act of reading or the process of understanding written or printed material.|例句：Reading is a fundamental skill. 阅读是一项基本技能。  He enjoys reading aloud to his children. 他喜欢大声朗读给孩子们听。|近义词：perusal, study, scanning|反义词：writing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reads a book every night. = 他每晚都读一本书。
She reads the newspaper in the morning. = 她早上读报纸。

一般过去时:
I read a great novel last week. = 我上周读了一本很棒的小说。
They read the instructions carefully. = 他们仔细阅读了说明。

现在完成时:
I have read that book twice. = 我已经读过那本书两次了。
She has read many classic novels. = 她已经读过许多经典小说。

过去完成时:
I had read the article before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经读过那篇文章了。
They had read all the documents by the deadline. = 他们截止日期前已经读完所有文件了。

将来时:
I will read that new book tomorrow. = 我明天会读那本新书。
She will have read the entire series by next month. = 她下个月就会读完整个系列了。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would read more books. = 如果我有更多时间，我会读更多书。
I wish I had read that book sooner. = 我真希望我早点读那本书。

被动语态:
The book is read by many people. = 这本书被很多人阅读。
The article was read aloud in class. = 这篇文章在课堂上被大声朗读。

疑问句:
Do you read often? = 你经常阅读吗？
What are you reading? = 你在读什么？

否定句:
He doesn't read much. = 他阅读不多。
She didn't read the email. = 她没有读那封邮件。

PHRASE->
reading comprehension = 阅读理解
speed reading = 快速阅读
reading list = 阅读清单
extensive reading = 广泛阅读
pleasure reading = 休闲阅读
