larum:/ˈlærəm/| n. |警报器；闹钟 | A larum is a device that makes a loud noise to wake someone up or to warn people of danger.  It is often an older term, now largely replaced by "alarm clock" or "alarm".| Example: The larum rang at dawn, jolting him awake.  黎明时分警报器响了，把他惊醒。| 近义词：alarm clock, alarm, warning device | 反义词：silence, quiet
