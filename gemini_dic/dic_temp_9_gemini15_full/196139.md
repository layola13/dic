septfoil:/ˈsɛptfɔɪl/| n. |七叶草；七叶植物|A septfoil is a plant with seven leaflets or leaves.  It often refers to a type of cinquefoil (Potentilla) with seven leaflets, though the term can be used more broadly for any plant with seven-lobed leaves.|Example: The delicate septfoil spread its seven leaflets in the sun.  七叶草在阳光下舒展着七片叶子。|近义词：seven-leaved plant, heptaphyllous plant |反义词：无
