slickers:/ˈslɪkərz/| n.|雨衣，油布雨衣|A slicker is a waterproof coat, typically made of oiled cloth, worn as protection from rain.|例句：He pulled on his slicker before venturing out into the downpour. 他穿上雨衣才冒雨出去。|近义词：raincoat, oilskin|反义词：None specifically.
