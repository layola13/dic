sanguisuge:/ˈsæŋɡwɪsʌdʒ/| n. |水蛭|A bloodsucking annelid worm.  Sanguisuges are known for their ability to attach themselves to a host and feed on its blood. They are often found in freshwater habitats. |例句：The leech, a type of sanguisuge, attached itself to his leg. (这种水蛭吸附在他的腿上。)  The doctor used sanguisuges to remove excess blood from the wound. (医生用水蛭来清除伤口多余的血液。) |近义词：leech, bloodsucker |反义词：N/A
