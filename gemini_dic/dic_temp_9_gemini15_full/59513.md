eastward:/ˈiːstwərd/| adj. adv.|向东的；向东|Moving or directed towards the east.|The sun rose eastward.|近义词：eastbound, easterly|反义词：westward


USAGE->

The plane flies eastward. = 飞机向东飞。
The river flows eastward. = 河水向东流。

The explorers journeyed eastward. = 探险家们向东旅行。

将来时:
