healthiest:/ˈhɛlθiəst/ | adj. | 最健康的 | Most healthy; possessing the best health.  This word is the superlative form of the adjective "healthy". It describes the state of being in the best possible physical and mental condition. | Example:  She chose the healthiest option from the menu. 她从菜单上选择了最健康的食物。

The healthiest lifestyle involves regular exercise and a balanced diet. 最健康的生活方式包括规律的锻炼和均衡的饮食。


近义词: most beneficial, most wholesome, most robust | 反义词: unhealthiest, most unhealthy, sickest
