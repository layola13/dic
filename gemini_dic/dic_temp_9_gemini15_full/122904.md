molotov:/ˈmɒlətɒf/| n. |莫洛托夫鸡尾酒；燃烧瓶|A Molotov cocktail is a type of improvised incendiary weapon.  It consists of a bottle filled with flammable liquid, such as gasoline, with a fuse or improvised igniter.  The bottle is typically thrown at a target, shattering on impact and spreading the burning liquid.|The protesters threw Molotov cocktails at the police station.|近义词:incendiary device, firebomb|反义词:fire extinguisher
