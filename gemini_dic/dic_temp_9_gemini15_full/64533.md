/ˈɛrəbəl/ | adj. | 易错的；易犯错误的 | Liable to err; capable of making mistakes.  The human mind is errable; we all make mistakes from time to time.  | 近义词：fallible, imperfect, flawed | 反义词：infallible, perfect, flawless |


例句：

The witness's testimony was errable due to the passage of time.  证人证词由于时间的推移而容易出错。

He acknowledged that his judgment was errable in that instance. 他承认在那件事上他的判断是错误的。


词源:

From  Middle English *errable, from Old French errable, from Late Latin errabilis, from Latin errare "to wander, stray, err."


用法说明:

errable 主要用作形容词，修饰人、判断、系统等容易出错的事物。  它强调的是一种倾向性或可能性，而不是指某件事一定出错。


无需补充其他语法范例，因为errable本身不是一个动词，无法进行时态变化。  它是一个形容词，只用于描述名词。
