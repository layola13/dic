high-priced:/haɪˈpraɪst/| adj.|价格高的，昂贵的|Expensive; costing a lot of money.|例句：The high-priced restaurant was beyond our budget. 这家高价位的餐厅超出了我们的预算。|近义词：expensive, costly, dear, pricey|反义词：cheap, inexpensive, affordable

These high-priced shoes are made of genuine leather. = 这些昂贵的鞋子是用真皮做的。
