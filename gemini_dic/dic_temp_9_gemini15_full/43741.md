creels:/kriːlz/| n.|鱼篓；(尤指用于捕鱼的)篓子|A creel is a basket, typically made of wicker, used for carrying fish that have been caught.  It's often used in fishing, especially by anglers.|例句：The fisherman proudly showed off his creel full of trout.  渔夫骄傲地展示了他装满鳟鱼的鱼篓。|例句：She carried her catch in a large wicker creel. 她用一个大的柳条鱼篓装着她的渔获。|近义词：basket, hamper, pannier|反义词：无
