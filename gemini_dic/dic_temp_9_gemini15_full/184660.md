restringing:/ˌriːˈstrɪŋɪŋ/ | n. | 重新穿弦；换弦 | The act of replacing the strings on a stringed instrument, such as a guitar, violin, or tennis racket.  It involves removing the old strings and installing new ones, often requiring specific tools and techniques. | Example: The restringing of my guitar improved its sound significantly.  重新为我的吉他换弦显著改善了它的音质。 |近义词: restring, replacing strings | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but the opposite action would be removing strings)
