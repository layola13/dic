howzat:/ˈhaʊzæt/| int.|好球！ (板球术语)|An exclamation used in cricket by a bowler or fielder to appeal to the umpire for a decision, typically that a batsman is out. It is a contraction of "How's that?"|例句：The bowler shouted "Howzat!" as the batsman missed the ball. 投球手在击球手错过球后大喊“好球！”。|近义词：out, appeal|反义词:N/A
