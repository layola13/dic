resultance: /rɪˈzʌltəns/ | n. | 合力；结果；最终效果 | The combined effect of two or more forces, influences, or factors.  It refers to the single force that produces the same effect as the combination of multiple forces acting together. |例句：The resultant of the two forces was a powerful push forward.  两个力的合力是一个强大的向前推力。


例句：The resultant of their efforts was a successful project. 他们努力的结果是一个成功的项目。


近义词：composite, effect, outcome, consequence, product


反义词：cause (in some contexts), antecedent


词性：名词
