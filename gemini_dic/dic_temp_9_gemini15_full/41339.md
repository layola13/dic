coriander: /ˈkɒriændə(r)/, /ˈkɔːriændər/ | n. | 香菜 | The leaves and seeds of an herb, used as a spice and flavoring.  Coriander is widely used in many cuisines around the world, particularly in Asian and Middle Eastern cooking.  The leaves are often called cilantro. |  The fragrant coriander seeds added a warm, citrusy note to the dish.  香菜籽的香味为这道菜增添了一丝温暖的柑橘味。


The fresh coriander leaves brightened up the salad. 新鲜的香菜叶使沙拉更清爽。| cilantro, parsley |  (no direct antonym, as it describes a specific plant) | 名词
