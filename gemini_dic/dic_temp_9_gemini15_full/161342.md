postpsychoanalytic: /ˌpoʊstˌsaɪkoʊæˈnælɪtɪk/ | adj. | 后精神分析学的 |  Relating to or denoting theories and approaches that have developed from or in reaction to psychoanalysis but differ from it in significant ways.  It often involves a critique or revision of Freudian concepts.  | 例句：His work represents a significant contribution to postpsychoanalytic theory.  他的作品对后精神分析理论做出了重大贡献。

例句：The conference featured several presentations on postpsychoanalytic approaches to trauma.  此次会议重点介绍了几种后精神分析学处理创伤的方法。


近义词：post-Freudian, neo-psychoanalytic


反义词：Freudian (strictly adhering to Freud's theories)


词性:形容词
