/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌbld/ | adj. |  过度忧虑的；烦扰不安的 | excessively troubled; worried excessively | He was overtroubled by the news of his friend's illness. 他对朋友生病的消息过度忧虑。|近义词：overwhelmed, anxious, distressed |反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overtroubled by his work. = 他工作压力过大，烦心不已。
She is overtroubled about the upcoming exam. = 她对即将到来的考试过度焦虑。


一般过去时:
He was overtroubled by the loss of his job. = 他因失业而过度忧虑。
She was overtroubled by her relationship problems. = 她因为感情问题而烦扰不安。


现在完成时:
He has been overtroubled by financial difficulties. = 他一直为经济困难而苦恼。
She has been overtroubled since her accident. = 自从事故发生以来，她一直很焦虑。


过去完成时:
He had been overtroubled for weeks before he finally sought help. = 在他最终寻求帮助之前，他已经焦虑了好几个星期。
She had been overtroubled about the project before she found a solution. = 在她找到解决方案之前，她一直为这个项目而烦恼。


将来时:
He will be overtroubled if he doesn't pass the exam. = 如果他考试不及格，他会非常焦虑。
She will be overtroubled if the project is delayed again. = 如果项目再次延期，她会非常担心。


虚拟语气:

I wish I wasn't so overtroubled about this matter. = 我希望我不必为这件事如此烦心。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
