overtightened: /ˌoʊvərˈtaɪtnd/ | adj. | 过度拧紧的；拉得太紧的 |  Describes something that has been tightened too much, beyond the appropriate or recommended level.  This can lead to damage or malfunction. |


例句：

The bolt was overtightened, causing it to snap.  螺栓拧得太紧，导致它断裂了。

The shoelaces were overtightened, cutting off circulation to his feet. 鞋带系得太紧，导致他的脚血液循环受阻。


近义词：too tight, excessively tightened, strained


反义词：loose, slack, undertightened


词性:形容词
