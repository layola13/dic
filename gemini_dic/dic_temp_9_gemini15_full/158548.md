plesiosaurian:/ˌpliːsiəˈsɔːriən/| adj.| 蛇颈龙类的；似蛇颈龙的 | Of or relating to plesiosaurs.  A plesiosaurian reptile is characterized by its long neck, small head, and four flippers. | Example: The plesiosaurian fossils found in the quarry were remarkably well-preserved.  这处采石场发现的蛇颈龙类化石保存得非常好。 | 近义词:plesiosaur-like | 反义词: N/A
