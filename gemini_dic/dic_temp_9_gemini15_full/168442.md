probabilistic:/ˌprɒbəbɪˈlɪstɪk/| /ˌprɑːbəbəlɪˈstɪk/| adj.|概率的；可能的；基于概率的|Relating to or involving probability.  It suggests that something is likely to happen but not certain. |例句：The probabilistic model predicts a 70% chance of rain tomorrow.  概率模型预测明天有70%的降雨概率。

例句：He used a probabilistic approach to solve the problem. 他用概率方法来解决这个问题。


近义词：stochastic, chance-based, likely, possible| 反义词：deterministic, certain, definite| 词性：adj.
