riles:/raɪlz/ | vt. | 激怒；使恼火 | to cause someone to feel annoyed or impatient | His constant complaining riles me. 他不断的抱怨让我很恼火。  | irritate, annoy, exasperate | soothe, calm, please | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His comments rile me every day. = 他的评论每天都让我生气。
That noise riles the neighbors. = 那个噪音惹恼了邻居们。


一般过去时:
The traffic jam riled him yesterday. = 昨天的交通堵塞让他很生气。
Her rudeness riled me greatly. = 她的粗鲁让我非常生气。


现在完成时:
His behavior has riled me for weeks. = 他的行为已经让我生气好几个星期了。
The constant interruptions have riled her to no end. =  不断的打断让她非常生气。


过去完成时:
I had been riled by his actions before the meeting. = 会议之前我就已经被他的行为激怒了。
She had already been riled by his comments before he even started. = 他还没开始说话，她就已经被他的评论激怒了。


将来时:
His actions will surely rile her tomorrow. = 他的行为明天肯定会让她生气。
That news will rile many people. = 那条新闻会激怒很多人。


虚拟语气:
If he knew what he was doing, he wouldn't rile me so much. = 如果他知道自己在做什么，他就不会这么让我生气了。
I wish he wouldn't rile me with his silly jokes. = 我希望他不要拿他的傻笑话来惹我生气。


被动语态:
He was riled by the news. = 他被这个消息激怒了。
She was riled by his constant interruptions. = 她被他不断的打断而激怒了。


疑问句:
Does his behavior rile you? = 他的行为惹你生气吗？
Does loud music rile you? = 吵闹的音乐会让你生气吗？


否定句:
His words didn't rile me at all. = 他的话一点也没让我生气。
The news didn't rile her as much as expected. = 这个消息并没有像预期那样让她生气。


PHRASE->
rile up = 激怒；煽动
rile someone's feathers = 激怒某人
rile sb. into doing sth. = 激怒某人做某事
be riled = 感到生气
get riled up = 变得生气
