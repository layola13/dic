homogenesis:/ˌhoʊmoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs/| n. |同源发生；同源生成|The process by which similar structures or characteristics arise independently in different organisms due to similar environmental pressures or evolutionary pathways.  It's a form of convergent evolution. |Example: The streamlined body shape of dolphins and sharks is an example of homogenesis, as both evolved independently to suit their aquatic environments.  |近义词：convergent evolution, parallel evolution |反义词：divergent evolution, homoplasy
