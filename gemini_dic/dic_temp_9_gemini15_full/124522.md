mountainboarding: /ˈmaʊntɪnˌbɔːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 山地滑板 |  The sport of riding a mountainboard, a type of all-terrain board similar to a snowboard or skateboard but designed for off-road use.  It involves riding downhill or across uneven terrain. | Example: He enjoys mountainboarding down the steep slopes. 他喜欢在陡峭的山坡上玩山地滑板。 | 近义词: all-terrain boarding, off-road boarding | 反义词: None specifically.
