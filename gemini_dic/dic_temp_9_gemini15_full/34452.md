clavichord:/ˈklævɪkɔːrd/ | n. |古钢琴 | A clavichord is a keyboard instrument that produces sound when brass tangents, attached to the keys, strike the strings.  It was popular in the Baroque period and is known for its delicate and intimate sound.  Unlike a harpsichord or piano, the volume is controlled by the pressure applied to the keys. | The clavichord was a popular instrument during the Baroque period, and many composers wrote music specifically for it.  | 近义词：harpsichord, pianoforte  | 反义词：None  | 名词
