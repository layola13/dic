词源:
hyper- (前缀，表示“过度”、“超过”) + fastidious (形容词，表示“讲究的”、“挑剔的”)。  Fastidious itself comes from the Latin *fastidium, meaning "disgust, loathing, weariness."  The sense shifted to "excessive care" or "nicety."
