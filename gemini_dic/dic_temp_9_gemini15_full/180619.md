reglorify:/rɪˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪ/| vt.|重新赞美；再次颂扬|To praise or glorify again; to restore to glory or honor.|例句：The newly renovated church was reglorified with a grand reopening ceremony.  重新装修后的教堂在盛大的重新开放仪式上再次受到了赞扬。|例句：After years of neglect, the old castle was reglorified and opened to the public.  多年被忽视后，古老的城堡得到了修复并对公众开放。|近义词：exalt, glorify, celebrate, honor|反义词：disgrace, dishonor, denigrate|动词
