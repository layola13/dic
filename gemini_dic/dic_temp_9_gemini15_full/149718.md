palaeotype:/ˈpælɪəʊtaɪp/| n. |古文字类型；古音标法|A system of phonetic transcription, devised by Henry Sweet, that uses a modified version of the Roman alphabet to represent sounds. It aimed to provide a more precise and consistent way of representing pronunciation than traditional spelling.  This system was used particularly in the study of historical linguistics and the pronunciation of older texts.|The palaeotype transcription of the Old English word "cniht" is [knixt].|近义词： phonetic transcription,  transcription system |反义词：N/A
