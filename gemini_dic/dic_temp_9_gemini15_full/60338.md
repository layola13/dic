egomaniacal: /ˌiːɡoʊmæˈniːəkəl/ | adj. | 自我中心的，以自我为中心的 | Extremely self-centered and vain; excessively preoccupied with oneself.  |例句：His egomaniacal behavior alienated his colleagues.  他的自我中心行为疏远了他的同事。

例句：Her egomaniacal tendencies made it difficult to work with her.  她以自我为中心的倾向让她很难共事。


近义词：narcissistic, self-absorbed, self-obsessed, vain, conceited

反义词：humble, modest, selfless, altruistic

词源:
The word "egomaniacal" is derived from "egomaniac," which combines "ego" (Latin for "I") and "maniac" (referring to someone with excessive or uncontrolled behavior).  Therefore, it literally means someone who is excessively focused on their own self (ego).
