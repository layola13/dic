in bulk: /ɪn bʌlk/ | adj. | 大量地；批发地 |  指以大量的、非零售的方式购买或销售商品。  | We bought the supplies in bulk to save money. 我们批量购买了物资以节省开支。 | wholesale, in large quantities | retail, individually  |形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They buy coffee beans in bulk. = 他们批量购买咖啡豆。
We purchase stationery in bulk for the office. = 我们为办公室批量采购文具。


一般过去时:
The company bought the materials in bulk last month. = 公司上个月批量购买了材料。
They ordered the books in bulk for the school library. = 他们为学校图书馆批量订购了书籍。


现在完成时:
We have purchased the ingredients in bulk for the upcoming event. = 我们已经为即将到来的活动批量购买了食材。
They have consistently bought their supplies in bulk. = 他们一直批量购买他们的用品。


过去完成时:
The store had already sold out of the items before we arrived, even though they had received a bulk shipment. = 我们到达之前商店就已经卖光了商品，即使他们收到了批量发货。
They had purchased the raw materials in bulk before the price increase. = 他们在大幅涨价之前就已批量购买了原材料。


将来时:
We will order the components in bulk next week. = 我们下周将批量订购组件。
They will buy the furniture in bulk for the new office. = 他们将为新办公室批量购买家具。


虚拟语气:
If we bought in bulk, we would save a considerable amount of money. = 如果我们批量购买，我们将节省大量资金。
I wish we had bought the materials in bulk; it would have been much cheaper. = 我希望我们当时批量购买了材料；那样会便宜得多。


被动语态:
The goods were bought in bulk by the wholesaler. = 这些商品是由批发商批量购买的。
The ingredients are often purchased in bulk for large-scale events. = 大规模活动通常会批量购买食材。


疑问句:
Do you usually buy your groceries in bulk? = 你通常批量购买你的杂货吗？
Did they purchase the paper in bulk or individually? = 他们批量购买还是单独购买纸张？


否定句:
We did not buy the products in bulk this time. = 我们这次没有批量购买产品。
They don't usually buy their supplies in bulk. = 他们通常不批量购买他们的用品。
