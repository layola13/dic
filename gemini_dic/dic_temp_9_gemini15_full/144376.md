outstorm:/aʊtˈstɔːrm/| v.| 猛烈袭击；超越；压倒 |To surpass or overwhelm; to storm out.  |例句：The wind outstormed the small boat. 风猛烈袭击了小船。|近义词：overwhelm, overpower, surpass |反义词：support, aid, assist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind outstorms the small boat. = 风猛烈袭击了小船。
He outstorms his opponents in the debate. = 他在辩论中压倒了对手。


一般过去时:
The storm outstormed the coastal village. = 暴风雨猛烈袭击了沿海村庄。
Her talent outstormed all the other contestants. = 她的才能超过了所有其他参赛者。


现在完成时:
The relentless rain has outstormed the crops. = 持续不断的雨水已经摧毁了庄稼。
He has outstormed all expectations in his career. = 他在事业上超越了所有预期。


过去完成时:
The flood had outstormed the town's defenses. = 洪水已经冲垮了城镇的防御工事。
She had outstormed her fears and finally spoke up. = 她克服了恐惧，最终勇敢地说了出来。


将来时:
The hurricane will outstorm the island's infrastructure. = 飓风将摧毁岛屿的基础设施。
He will outstorm his doubts and achieve his goals. = 他将克服怀疑，实现目标。


虚拟语气:
If the wind outstormed the ship, they would be in danger. = 如果风猛烈袭击了船只，他们将处于危险之中。
If only I had outstormed my hesitation, I would have won the competition. =  如果我当时能克服犹豫，我就能赢得比赛。


被动语态:
The village was outstormed by the flood. = 村庄被洪水冲垮了。
His confidence was outstormed by the pressure. = 他的信心被压力击垮了。


疑问句:

Can his determination outstorm the challenges ahead? = 他的决心能克服未来的挑战吗？


否定句:
The team did not outstorm their opponents. = 这支队伍没有压倒对手。
The small boat did not outstorm the waves. = 小船没有抵挡住海浪。
