scabbarding:/skæˈbɑːdɪŋ/| n. |鞘装;入鞘; |The act of putting a blade into its scabbard.  It can also refer to the process of sheathing something, metaphorically. |例句：The quick scabbarding of his sword prevented further bloodshed.  迅速将剑入鞘阻止了进一步的血流.


例句：The scabbarding of the knife was a swift and silent action.  把刀入鞘的动作迅速而无声。


近义词：sheathing, encasing


反义词：unsheathing, drawing (a sword)


词性：名词
