clarifying: /ˈklærɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. |澄清；阐明；使清晰|Making something clear or easier to understand; removing confusion or ambiguity. |例句：He spent several paragraphs clarifying his position on the issue. 他用几段话来阐明他在这个问题上的立场。 |例句：The teacher's explanation helped clarify the confusing concept. 老师的解释帮助澄清了这个令人困惑的概念。|近义词：explaining, elucidating, illuminating, specifying|反义词：confusing, obscuring, muddying
