pearlish:/ˈpɜːrlɪʃ/| adj.|珍珠似的，略带珍珠色的|Slightly resembling a pearl in color; having a pearly luster.  The adjective describes a color or sheen that is similar to that of a pearl, often implying a soft, iridescent quality.|例句：Her skin had a pearlish glow.  她的皮肤有一种珍珠般的光泽。

例句：The inside of the seashell had a pearlish sheen. 贝壳内侧呈现珍珠般的光泽。


近义词：pearly, opalescent, iridescent

反义词：dull, matte


词性：形容词
