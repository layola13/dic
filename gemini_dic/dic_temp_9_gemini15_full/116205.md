masklike:/ˈmæsklaɪk/| adj.|面具似的；面无表情的；冷漠的|Resembling a mask; devoid of expression; impassive.  It suggests a lack of emotion or feeling, often implying coldness or detachment.|例句：Her masklike face revealed nothing of her true feelings.  她的面具般面孔丝毫没有流露出她的真实感受。

例句：He stared at me with a masklike expression, saying nothing. 他用一种面具般冷漠的表情盯着我，一句话也没说。

近义词：impassive, expressionless, emotionless, vacant, blank

反义词：expressive, animated, emotional, lively
