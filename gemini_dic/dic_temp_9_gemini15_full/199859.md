sittings:/ˈsɪtɪŋz/| n.| 会议；绘画或拍照的时段；连续的会议或工作期间 | Sessions of a meeting, a sitting for a portrait, or a period of continuous work. | 例句：The committee held several sittings before reaching a decision.  委员会在做出决定之前举行了多次会议。

例句：The artist needed three sittings to complete the portrait. 艺术家需要三次绘画才能完成肖像。

反义词：None directly applicable.  The antonym would depend on the context.  For example, if referring to "sittings" as in meeting sessions,  "breaks" or "adjournments" could be considered opposites.
