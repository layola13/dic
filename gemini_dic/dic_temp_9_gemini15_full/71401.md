flagship:/ˈflæɡʃɪp/ | n. |旗舰；佼佼者；代表产品 |  The flagship product of a company; the most important or best product or service offered by a company.  It can also refer to the leading ship in a fleet or a leading business or organization within a larger group. |例句：Apple's iPhone is considered a flagship product. 苹果的iPhone被认为是一款旗舰产品。

例句：Their flagship store is located in the city center. 他们的旗舰店位于市中心。


近义词：leader, vanguard, leading product, premier product | 反义词：also-ran, laggard

词源:
The word "flagship" originates from the nautical term.  A flagship is the ship that carries the commander of a fleet.  The meaning has extended metaphorically to refer to the leading or most important entity within a group.
