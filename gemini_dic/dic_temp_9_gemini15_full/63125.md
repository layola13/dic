enslaver:/ɪnˈsleɪvər/| n. |奴役者，奴隶主 |A person who enslaves others.  Someone who keeps other people as slaves. |The enslavers forced the captured people to work in the fields. 奴隶主强迫被俘虏的人在田里工作。 The history of the enslavers is a dark chapter in human history. 奴隶主的历史是人类历史上黑暗的一章。|近义词：slaveholder, master, captor|反义词：liberator, emancipator
