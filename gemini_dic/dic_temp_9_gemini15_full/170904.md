pseudomedical:/ˌsuːdoʊˈmɛdɪkəl/ | adj. |伪医学的；似是而非的医学的 | Relating to or characteristic of practices or products that are falsely claimed to be medical or that are presented as having medical benefits without scientific evidence.  |Example:  He was selling pseudomedical devices that claimed to cure cancer. 他在卖声称能治愈癌症的伪医学设备。  |近义词：quack, bogus, sham, fake |反义词：genuine, authentic, legitimate
