mortgageor:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɔːr/| n. |抵押人，借款人 |The person who gives a mortgage on property to secure a loan.  A mortgageor is the borrower in a mortgage transaction. |Example: The mortgageor defaulted on their payments, resulting in foreclosure.  抵押人未能按时还款，导致房屋被取消抵押品赎回权。|近义词：borrower, pledgor |反义词：mortgagee
