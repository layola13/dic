quibble:/ˈkwɪbəl/| n., v.| 吹毛求疵；琐碎的争论 | A minor objection or criticism; a trivial argument.  To argue or find fault over petty matters. |例句：He always quibbles over the smallest details. 他总是对最细微的细节吹毛求疵。

例句：They quibbled for hours over the price of the car. 他们就汽车的价格争论了几个小时。


近义词： cavil, carp, nitpick,  fuss


反义词：  accept, agree


词性：名词，动词
