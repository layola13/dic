ruptures:/ˈrʌptʃərz/| n. v.|破裂；断裂；决裂|The sudden rupture of the water pipe caused a flood.  水管突然破裂导致洪水。 |The two countries experienced a diplomatic rupture. 两国经历了外交决裂。|近义词：break, burst, split, fracture|反义词：repair, mend, heal


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The pipe often ruptures under pressure. = 这根管道经常在压力下破裂。
The relationship sometimes ruptures due to misunderstandings. = 由于误解，这种关系有时会破裂。


一般过去时:
The dam ruptured during the storm. = 大坝在暴风雨中决堤了。
Their friendship ruptured after a bitter argument. = 他们友谊在一次激烈的争吵后破裂了。


现在完成时:
The negotiations have ruptured, and no agreement has been reached. = 谈判破裂了，没有达成任何协议。
The aneurysm has ruptured, requiring immediate surgery. = 动脉瘤破裂了，需要立即手术。


过去完成时:
The marriage had ruptured long before they officially separated. = 他们的婚姻早在他们正式分居之前就已经破裂了。
The bridge had ruptured under the weight of the overloaded truck. = 桥梁在超载卡车的重量下已经断裂了。


将来时:
The volcano is expected to rupture soon. = 预计火山很快会爆发。
If the pressure continues to increase, the container will rupture. = 如果压力继续增加，容器将会破裂。


虚拟语气:
If the pipeline had ruptured, it would have caused a major environmental disaster. = 如果管道破裂，就会造成重大的环境灾难。
I wish the peace talks hadn't ruptured. = 我真希望和平谈判没有破裂。


被动语态:
The water main was ruptured by the earthquake. = 自来水干管在地震中破裂了。
The agreement was ruptured by the unexpected announcement. = 协议因意外的声明而破裂了。


疑问句:
Has the contract been ruptured? = 合同破裂了吗？
Did the pipe rupture due to corrosion? = 水管是由于腐蚀而破裂的吗？


否定句:
The agreement did not rupture despite the disagreements. = 尽管存在分歧，协议并没有破裂。
The blood vessel did not rupture during the surgery. = 在手术过程中血管没有破裂。



PHRASE->

rupture a relationship = 破坏一段关系
rupture an aneurysm = 动脉瘤破裂
rupture a contract = 毁约
rupture a dam = 决堤
rupture a peace treaty = 撕毁和平条约

词源:

From Middle English *rupture, from Old French rupture, from Latin ruptura, from rumpere "to break".
