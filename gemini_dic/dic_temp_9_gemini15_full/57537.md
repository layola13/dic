drain:/dreɪn/| vt. n.| 排水；耗尽；使…枯竭；阴沟 |To remove liquid from something.| The plumber came to drain the sink. |近义词：empty, deplete |反义词：fill, replenish| 动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drains the bathtub after every shower. = 他每次洗完澡都把浴缸里的水放掉。
The constant rain drains the soil of its nutrients. =  持续的降雨使土壤失去了养分。


一般过去时:
She drained the pasta before adding the sauce. = 她把意面沥干后才加酱汁。
The long journey drained his energy. = 漫长的旅程耗尽了他的精力。


现在完成时:
They have drained the swamp to build a new housing development. = 他们已经排干了沼泽来建造新的住宅区。
The drought has drained the lake of its water. = 干旱已经使湖泊的水干涸了。


过去完成时:
The team had drained their resources before the final match. = 在决赛之前，球队已经耗尽了资源。
The heavy rains had drained the reservoir before the summer began. =  夏季开始之前，大雨已经排干了水库。


将来时:
We will drain the pool tomorrow. = 我们明天将把游泳池的水排干。
The project is expected to drain the company's funds. =  预计该项目将耗尽公司的资金。


虚拟语气:
If we drained the swamp, we could build a new road. = 如果我们排干沼泽，我们就能修一条新路。
I wish I had drained the battery before storing it. = 我真希望我在储存电池之前把它放电了。


被动语态:
The pool is being drained for maintenance. = 游泳池正在排空进行维护。
The company's resources are being drained by the ongoing project. = 公司的资源正在被正在进行的项目耗尽。


疑问句:
Does this pipe drain properly? = 这根管道排水正常吗？
Will this project drain our savings? = 这个项目会耗尽我们的积蓄吗？


否定句:
The water didn't drain properly. = 水没有排干净。
The company won't drain its resources on this unprofitable venture. = 公司不会在这个无利可图的项目上耗尽资源。


PHRASE->
drain away = 逐渐消失
drain off = 排出；放掉
drain down = 排干；渐渐减少
drain out = 排出；流出
brain drain = 人才外流
