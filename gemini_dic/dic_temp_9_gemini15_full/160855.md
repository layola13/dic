post's:/poʊsts/| n.|帖子，邮件|The plural form of "post," referring to items posted online or through a mail service.|例句：I read all the posts on the forum. 我读了论坛上的所有帖子。|近义词：messages, entries, uploads |反义词: N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The user posts regularly on the forum. = 用户定期在论坛上发帖。
She posts pictures of her cat on Instagram. = 她在Instagram上发布她猫的照片。

一般过去时:
He posted a comment yesterday. = 他昨天发布了一条评论。
They posted the announcement on the website. = 他们在网站上发布了公告。

现在完成时:
She has posted several articles this month. = 这个月她已经发表了几篇文章。
We have posted all the updates on our blog. = 我们已经在博客上发布了所有更新。

过去完成时:
They had posted the results before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经发布了结果。
He had posted the photos before leaving for vacation. = 他在去度假之前已经发布了照片。

将来时:
I will post the report tomorrow. = 我明天将发布报告。
She will post the video on YouTube later. = 她稍后会将视频发布在YouTube上。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would post a longer comment. = 如果我有时间，我会发一篇较长的评论。
I wish I had posted my application earlier. = 我希望我早点提交我的申请。

被动语态:
The news was posted online last night. = 新闻昨晚发布在网上。
The updates are being posted as they come in. = 更新正在陆续发布。

疑问句:
Have you posted the letter yet? = 你寄出信了吗？
When will the results be posted? = 结果什么时候发布？

否定句:
I haven't posted anything today. = 我今天什么都没发。
He didn't post the article on time. = 他没有按时发表文章。
