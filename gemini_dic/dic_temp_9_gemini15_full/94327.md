impetuses:/ˈɪmpɪtəsiːz/| n. |推动力；刺激；动机 |The driving forces or stimuli that cause something to happen or develop. Impetuses can be internal motivations or external influences that initiate action or change.  |例句：The economic downturn was a major impetus for the reforms.  经济低迷是改革的主要推动力。

The discovery of penicillin was a significant impetus for the development of antibiotics. 青霉素的发现是抗生素发展的重要推动力。

|近义词：stimuli, motivations, incentives, impulses, drives |反义词：inhibitions, restraints, deterrents |名词
