fascinates:/ˈfæsɪneɪts/| v.|使着迷；吸引；迷住|To hold the attention of someone completely; to be extremely interesting.|例句：The history of ancient Egypt fascinates me.  古埃及的历史深深吸引着我。  The magician's tricks fascinated the children. 魔术师的戏法迷住了孩子们。|近义词：enthralls, captivates, intrigues, charms|反义词：bores, repels


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum's exhibits fascinate many visitors. = 博物馆的展品吸引了许多游客。
Her stories fascinate her listeners. = 她的故事吸引了她的听众。


一般过去时:
The movie fascinated me. = 这部电影迷住了我。
The performance fascinated the audience. = 这场演出迷住了观众。


现在完成时:
The mystery of the disappearance has fascinated investigators for years. = 失踪的谜团多年来一直吸引着调查人员。
His work has fascinated art critics worldwide. = 他的作品吸引了全世界的艺术评论家。


过去完成时:
I had been fascinated by astronomy since childhood. = 从小我就对天文学很着迷。
She had been fascinated by his stories before she met him. = 在见到他之前，她就已被他的故事迷住了。


将来时:
The new technology will fascinate consumers. = 新技术将吸引消费者。
The upcoming exhibition will fascinate art lovers. = 即将来临的展览将吸引艺术爱好者。


虚拟语气:
If the book were more interesting, it would fascinate me. = 如果这本书更有趣一些，它就会吸引我。
I wish this topic would fascinate me more. = 我希望这个话题能更吸引我。


被动语态:
Many people are fascinated by the beauty of nature. = 许多人被大自然的美丽所吸引。
He was fascinated by her intelligence. = 他被她的才智所吸引。


疑问句:
What fascinates you about this subject? = 你对这个主题什么感兴趣？
Does the idea of space travel fascinate you? = 太空旅行的想法吸引你吗？


否定句:
The lecture didn't fascinate me. = 这场讲座没有吸引我。
The game didn't fascinate the children. = 这个游戏没有吸引孩子们。


PHRASE->
be fascinated by = 对...着迷
be fascinated with = 对...着迷
absolutely fascinated = 非常着迷
utterly fascinated = 完全着迷
deeply fascinated = 深深着迷
