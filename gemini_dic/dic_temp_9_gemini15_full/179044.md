redemonstration: /ˌriːˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 再演示，重新演示 | The act of demonstrating something again; a second or repeated demonstration.  It implies a previous demonstration has already taken place, and this is a repeat performance, perhaps for clarification or to address shortcomings in the original. |例句：The teacher gave a redemonstration of the experiment after several students expressed confusion.  老师在几个学生表示困惑后又演示了一遍实验。

例句：Due to technical difficulties, a redemonstration of the software was scheduled for the following week. 由于技术故障，软件的重新演示安排在下周进行。


近义词：re-demonstration, repeat demonstration, second demonstration | 反义词：initial demonstration, first demonstration | 词性：名词
