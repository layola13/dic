forgerer:/ˈfɔːrdʒərər/| n. |伪造者，造假者 | A forgerer is a person who makes or copies something (like money or documents) illegally.  They create forgeries to deceive others for personal gain. | The forgerer was caught and sentenced to five years in prison.  伪造者被抓获并被判处五年徒刑。| counterfeiter, falsifier | honest person, authenticator | 名词
