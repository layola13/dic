sacked:/sækt/| vt.|解雇；洗劫；装满；|To dismiss someone from their job.  Also, to plunder or loot a place.|例句：He was sacked for incompetence. 他因不称职而被解雇。

例句：The army sacked the city.  军队洗劫了这座城市。


近义词：dismiss, fire, discharge, dismiss, plunder, loot |反义词：hire, employ, recruit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company sacks employees who violate the rules. = 公司解雇违反规定的员工。
He sacks the mail every day. = 他每天都把邮件装满邮箱。


一般过去时:
They sacked him after only six months. = 他们只雇佣了他六个月就解雇了他。
The pirates sacked the coastal village. = 海盗洗劫了沿海村庄。


现在完成时:
The company has sacked several employees this year. = 公司今年已经解雇了几名员工。
They have sacked three towns during their campaign. = 他们在战役中已经洗劫了三个城镇。


过去完成时:
He had been sacked twice before he found his current job. = 在找到现在的工作之前，他已经两次被解雇了。
The invaders had sacked the capital before the king could react. = 在国王做出反应之前，入侵者已经洗劫了首都。


将来时:
They will sack him if he makes another mistake. = 如果他再犯错，他们就会解雇他。
The rebels will sack the city if they succeed. = 如果叛军成功，他们就会洗劫这座城市。


虚拟语气:
If they sacked him, the project would fail. = 如果他们解雇了他，项目就会失败。
If they had sacked the city, the treasure would have been lost. = 如果他们洗劫了这座城，财宝就会丢失。


被动语态:
He was sacked for his bad behavior. = 他因为行为不端而被解雇。
The village was sacked by the enemy army. = 村庄被敌军洗劫了。


疑问句:
Was he sacked for being late? = 他是因为迟到而被解雇的吗？
Was the town sacked during the war? = 城镇在战争期间被洗劫了吗？


否定句:
They didn't sack him, despite his mistakes. = 尽管他犯了错误，他们也没有解雇他。
The invaders did not sack the city, instead they negotiated. = 入侵者并没有洗劫这座城市，而是进行了谈判。



PHRASE->
be sacked from a job = 被解雇
