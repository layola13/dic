fat-cat:/fæt kæt/| n. |富豪；大款；权贵 |A wealthy and influential person, especially one who is considered to be greedy or corrupt.  This term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting that the person has achieved their wealth through questionable means or uses their influence for selfish purposes.|例句：The fat cats on Wall Street are responsible for the economic crisis. 华尔街的富豪们应对经济危机负责。


例句：Those fat cats are always looking for ways to avoid paying taxes. 那些富豪总是在寻找逃税的方法。


近义词：big shot, tycoon, magnate, plutocrat


反义词：pauper, the poor, have-nots


词性:名词
