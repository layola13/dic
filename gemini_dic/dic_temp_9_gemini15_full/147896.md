overreduced:/ˌoʊvərɪˈduːst/| adj.|过度还原的；还原不足的|Excessively reduced;  lacking in reduction.  This term is often used in chemistry, particularly in contexts involving redox reactions (reduction-oxidation reactions) where a substance has been reduced beyond the expected or intended level. It implies an incomplete or excessive reduction process.|The overreduced compound was unstable and quickly oxidized.  过度还原的化合物不稳定，很快被氧化了。|近义词：underoxidized (in some contexts)|反义词：overoxidized, fully reduced
