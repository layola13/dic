heritor:/ˈhɛrɪtər/| n.|继承人，承受者|One who inherits or is entitled to inherit property or a title.|He is the sole heritor of his father's vast estate. 他是他父亲巨额财产的唯一继承人。|heir, inheritor|disinheritor|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heritor receives the inheritance. = 继承人收到遗产。
She is the heritor of a long and proud tradition. = 她是悠久而光荣传统的继承人。

一般过去时:
He became the heritor of his uncle's business. = 他成为他叔叔企业的继承人。
The young girl was the heritor of a fortune. = 这个年轻女孩继承了一笔财富。

现在完成时:
She has become the heritor of her grandmother's house. = 她已成为她祖母房子的继承人。
They have finally found the rightful heritor of the throne. = 他们终于找到了王位的合法继承人。


过去完成时:
Before his death, he had named his son as his heritor. = 他去世前，已指定他的儿子为继承人。
It had been clear that she was the rightful heritor of the title. = 很明显，她是该头衔的合法继承人。


将来时:
He will be the heritor of his father's company. = 他将是他父亲公司的继承人。
She will become the heritor of a substantial legacy. = 她将继承一笔可观的遗产。


虚拟语气:
If he were the heritor, he would manage the estate wisely. = 如果他是继承人，他会明智地管理产业。
I wish I were the heritor of that fortune. = 我希望我是那笔财富的继承人。


被动语态:
The estate was inherited by the heritor. = 产业由继承人继承。
The title was bestowed upon the rightful heritor. = 头衔授予了合法的继承人。


疑问句:
Who is the heritor of this property? = 这处房产的继承人是谁？
Is she the sole heritor of the estate? = 她是否是这笔遗产的唯一继承人？


否定句:
He is not the heritor of the throne. = 他不是王位的继承人。
She was not the heritor of her father's business. = 她不是她父亲企业的继承人。
