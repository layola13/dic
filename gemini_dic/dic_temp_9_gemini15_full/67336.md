extendability:/ˌɪkˌstɛndəˈbɪləti/| n. |可扩展性，可延伸性 |The ability to be extended or expanded.  This refers to the capacity of a system, process, or product to be enlarged, improved, or adapted to meet changing needs or requirements. It often implies scalability and adaptability.  |Example: The platform's extendability allows developers to add new features easily.  平台的可扩展性允许开发人员轻松添加新功能。|近义词：scalability, adaptability, flexibility |反义词：inflexibility, rigidity
