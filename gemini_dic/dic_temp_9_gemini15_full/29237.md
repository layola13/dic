cassava:/kəˈsɑːvə/| n.|木薯|A starchy tuberous root native to South America, widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions as a staple food.  It is also known as manioc or yuca. |例句：Cassava is a major source of carbohydrates for millions of people worldwide.  木薯是全世界数百万人重要的碳水化合物来源。|例句：We used cassava flour to make these delicious pancakes. 我们用木薯粉做了这些美味的薄饼。|近义词：manioc, yuca, tapioca (from cassava)|反义词：无
