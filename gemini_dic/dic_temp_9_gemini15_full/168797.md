producent:/prəˈdjuːsənt/| n.|生产者；制片人|A producent is someone who creates or makes something, especially in the context of agriculture or filmmaking.  In agriculture, it refers to an organism that produces its own food, usually through photosynthesis. In filmmaking, it refers to the person responsible for the financial and organizational aspects of a film's production.|例句：The farmer is a producent of wheat.  这位农民是小麦的生产者。|例句：The film's producent secured funding from several investors. 这部电影的制片人从几家投资者那里获得了资金。|近义词：producer, manufacturer, cultivator|反义词：consumer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer produces wheat. = 农民种植小麦。
She produces high-quality films. = 她制作高质量的电影。


一般过去时:
He produced a successful play last year. = 他去年制作了一部成功的戏剧。
They produced a lot of corn this season. = 他们这个季度生产了很多玉米。


现在完成时:
She has produced three films this year. = 她今年已经制作了三部电影。
The factory has produced thousands of cars. = 工厂已经生产了数千辆汽车。


过去完成时:

将来时:
