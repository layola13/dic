cherry:/ˈtʃɛri/| n.|樱桃|A small, round fruit that is typically red or dark-colored, sweet or tart, and grows on a cherry tree.  Cherries are often eaten fresh, used in desserts, or made into jams and juices.|例句：I love the sweet taste of fresh cherries. 我喜欢新鲜樱桃的甜味。|例句：The cherry pie was delicious.  樱桃派很好吃。|近义词：berry, fruit|反义词：无
