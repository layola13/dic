microlithic:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈlɪθɪk/| adj.| 微石器的；微石器时代的 | Relating to the Microlithic period, a stage in human prehistory characterized by the use of small stone tools.  | Example:  The microlithic tools found at the site provide valuable insights into the lives of early humans.  微石器时代在该遗址发现的工具为了解早期人类的生活提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：small-stone-tool, late-Pleistocene | 反义词：megalithic
