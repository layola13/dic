ride-offs:/ˈraɪdˌɔfs/| n.|冲销；注销；报废；(赛马)取消比赛|The term "ride-offs" refers to the process of removing an asset from the accounting records of a company. This is typically done when an asset has become obsolete, damaged beyond repair, or is no longer useful to the business.  It can also refer to the cancellation of a horse race. |例句：The company took a ride-off on the obsolete equipment.  公司注销了已淘汰的设备。

The insurance company processed a ride-off for the totaled car. 保险公司办理了报废汽车的注销手续。


近义词：write-off, cancellation, disposal |反义词：acquisition, purchase|名词
