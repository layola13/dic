faciae:/ˈfeɪʃiˌaɪ/| n. |面容；外观；表面|The plural of facies, referring to a distinctive aspect or appearance, especially of a rock formation or part of the body.  It often describes a particular characteristic or feature. For example, in geology, facies refers to the characteristics of a rock formation that indicate the environment in which it was formed. In anatomy, it can describe the appearance of a specific part of the body.|例句：The patient's facial facies indicated severe pain.  这位病人的面部表情显示出剧烈的疼痛。|例句：The sedimentary facies of the rock suggested a shallow marine environment. 这块岩石的沉积相表明这是一个浅海环境。|近义词：appearance, aspect, expression, feature|反义词： N/A
