nondependably:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈpɛndəbli/ | adv. |不可靠地，不稳定地 |  Not reliably; inconsistently.  It describes actions or things that are not consistent or trustworthy. |例句：The machine worked nondependably, sometimes failing completely. 这台机器工作不可靠，有时完全失效。

例句：The weather has been nondependably warm this spring.  今年春天，天气暖和得不太稳定。


近义词：unreliably, erratically, inconsistently, capriciously

反义词：dependably, reliably, consistently

词性:副词
