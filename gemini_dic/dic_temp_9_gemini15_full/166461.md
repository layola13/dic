prerevising:/ˌpriːrɪˈvaɪzɪŋ/| v.|预先修改，预先校对|The act of reviewing or revising something before it is finalized or published. It involves checking for errors, inconsistencies, and areas for improvement before the final version is created.  |例句：Prerevising the manuscript allowed the author to catch several grammatical errors. 预先修改手稿使作者能够发现几个语法错误。


例句：The editor spent a week prerevising the chapter before sending it to the printer. 编辑花了一周时间预先修改章节，然后才送去印刷。


近义词：pre-editing, proofreading, reviewing

反义词：post-editing, finalizing

词性：动词
