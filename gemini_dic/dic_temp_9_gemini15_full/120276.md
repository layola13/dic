miniaturizing: /ˌmɪniˈætʃəriŋ/ | v. | 小型化；微型化 | The act of making something smaller, typically to a miniature scale.  It involves reducing the size of an object or system while retaining its essential functions or characteristics. |例句：The company is miniaturizing its new phone to make it more portable.  这家公司正在将新款手机小型化，以使其更便携。

例句：The scientists are working on miniaturizing the complex circuitry for use in microchips. 科学家们正在致力于将复杂的电路小型化，以便用于微芯片。


近义词：downsizing, scaling down, reducing


反义词：enlarging, magnifying, scaling up


词性：动词
