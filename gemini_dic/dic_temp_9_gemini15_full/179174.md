redintegration:/ˌriːɪnˈtɛɡreɪʃən/| n. |复原，重新整合|The process by which a partial stimulus triggers the recall of a complete memory or experience.  It refers to the reconstruction of a whole from its parts.  This is a key concept in Gestalt psychology.|例句：The smell of freshly baked bread triggered a redintegration of childhood memories of my grandmother's kitchen.  闻到新鲜烘焙的面包的味道激起了我对祖母厨房童年记忆的重新整合。|近义词：reconstruction, reconstitution, restoration|反义词：fragmentation, disintegration|名词
