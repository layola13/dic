gripping:/ˈɡrɪpɪŋ/| adj.|扣人心弦的，引人入胜的，令人紧张的|extremely exciting and interesting, holding one's attention completely.  |例句：The movie was so gripping that I couldn't take my eyes off the screen. 这部电影太引人入胜了，我无法移开视线。
例句：The author's gripping narrative kept me up all night. 作者扣人心弦的叙述让我整夜睡不着觉。|近义词：thrilling, captivating, engrossing, compelling|反义词：boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting|
