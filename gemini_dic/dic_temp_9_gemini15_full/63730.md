epibenthic:/ˌɛpɪˈbɛnθɪk/ | adj. | 底栖的，底上生活的 |  Living on the surface of the seabed or lake bed.  | Example: Epibenthic organisms, such as starfish and sea urchins, play a crucial role in the marine ecosystem.  底栖生物，例如海星和海胆，在海洋生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词：benthic (but benthic refers to living *in* the sediment, while epibenthic refers to living *on* the sediment)| 反义词：pelagic (living in the water column) |
