creosol: /ˈkriːsoʊl/ | n. | 木馏油酚；杂酚油 | A mixture of methylphenols (cresols) obtained from coal tar or wood tar.  It's a colorless to yellowish liquid with a strong phenolic odor. It's used as a disinfectant, preservative, and in the production of other chemicals. | Example: Creosol is a common component in wood preservatives.  木馏油酚是木材防腐剂中常见的成分。 | 近义词: cresol, methylphenol | 反义词:  (None readily available;  it's more about its properties than a direct opposite) | 名词
