palped:/pæpt/| v.|触须；触碰|To touch or feel with the palps (sensory appendages of an invertebrate).  The term often refers to the action of an invertebrate, such as an insect or crustacean, using its palps to explore its environment. |例句：The butterfly palped the flower, testing its suitability. 蝴蝶用触须触碰花朵，测试其是否合适。

The crab palped the seabed, searching for food. 蟹用触须探测海底，寻找食物。|近义词：feel, touch, probe|反义词：ignore, neglect|动词
