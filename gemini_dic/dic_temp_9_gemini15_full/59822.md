ecthlipsis: /ɪkˈθlɪpsɪs/ | n. | 音节省略，音节吞音 | The omission of a sound or syllable in pronunciation, especially the omission of a vowel before a consonant or the omission of a consonant before another consonant.  It often occurs in rapid speech.  | Example:  The pronunciation of "I'm going" as "Im goin'" is an example of ecthlipsis.  音节省略的一个例子是将“I'm going”发音为“Im goin'”。| 近义词：elision, slurring | 反义词：  None directly opposite, but the opposite concept would be clear and full pronunciation. |
