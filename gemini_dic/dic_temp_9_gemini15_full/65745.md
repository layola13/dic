everlasting: /ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋ/ | adj. | 永恒的；永久的 |  lasting or intended to last forever.  |例句：Their love was everlasting. 他们的爱是永恒的。

例句：The mountains seem everlasting.群山看起来永恒不变。


近义词：eternal, perpetual, immortal, endless | 反义词：temporary, fleeting, transient, ephemeral | 词性:形容词

词源:
The word "everlasting" comes from the Old English "æfre" (ever) + "læstende" (lasting).  "Læstende" itself is a past participle of the verb "læstan," meaning "to last."  Thus, the word's origins clearly indicate its meaning of enduring forever.
