consular:/ˈkɒnsjʊlə(r)/|/ˈkɑːnsjʊlər/| adj. n.|领事的；领事馆的；|Relating to a consul or a consulate.  A consular official is a representative of a country's government in a foreign country, responsible for protecting the interests of their citizens and assisting them with various matters.|例句：He worked in a consular capacity for many years. 他多年从事领事工作。

例句：The consular office provides assistance to citizens abroad. 领事馆为海外公民提供协助。
