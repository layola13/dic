macrozoospore:/ˌmækroʊˈzoʊəspɔːr/| n. |大游动孢子|A macrozoospore is a relatively large, motile asexual spore produced by some organisms, typically fungi and certain algae. Unlike smaller zoospores, they are often multinucleate and may possess more complex structures. They are involved in asexual reproduction, dispersing to new locations to initiate growth. |Example: The water mold produced numerous macrozoospores, which swam away to colonize new substrates.  水霉菌产生了大量的巨型游动孢子，它们游走以定殖新的基质。|近义词: zoospore, spore |反义词: gamete
