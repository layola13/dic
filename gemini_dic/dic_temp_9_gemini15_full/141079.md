/ˌɒnəʊmætəˈpiːəz/ /ˌɑːnəʊmætəˈpiːəz/ | n. |拟声词；拟声法|The formation of words from sounds associated with what is named.  Onomatopoeia is a figure of speech in which words are formed in such a way that they imitate the sounds associated with the objects or actions they refer to. For example, "buzz," "hiss," and "meow" are onomatopoeic words.|例句：The author used onomatopoeia to vividly describe the sounds of the storm. 作者用拟声词生动地描绘了暴风雨的声音。


例句：The word "splash" is a perfect example of onomatopoeia.  “飞溅”这个词是拟声词的完美例子。


近义词：sound imitation, mimetic word|反义词：无


词性：名词
