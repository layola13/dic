mending:/ˈmɛndɪŋ/ | v. | 修理，缝补；改进，纠正 | The act of repairing something that is broken or damaged;  improving or correcting something. |例句：She is mending her torn dress.  她正在缝补她撕破的衣服。
|例句：The government is trying to mend the relationship between the two countries. 政府正试图修复两国之间的关系。|近义词：repairing, fixing, patching, improving, rectifying |反义词：breaking, damaging, worsening, destroying |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mends his shoes every week. = 他每周修补他的鞋子。
She mends her broken vase. = 她修理她破了的瓶子。


一般过去时:
I mended the fence yesterday. = 我昨天修好了篱笆。

现在完成时:
He has mended the broken chair. = 他已经修好了坏掉的椅子。
She has mended all the holes in her socks. = 她已经补好了袜子上的所有洞。


过去完成时:
He had mended the roof before the rain started. = 下雨前他已经修好了屋顶。
