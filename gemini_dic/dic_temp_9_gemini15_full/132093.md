noncredulous: /ˌnɒnˈkredʒələs/ | adj. |  不轻信的；怀疑的 | Not readily believing; skeptical.  |  He gave her a noncredulous stare. 他给了她一个不相信的眼神。 | skeptical, dubious, incredulous | credulous, gullible |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is noncredulous of her promises. = 他不相信她的承诺。
They are noncredulous about the politician's claims. = 他们对这位政治家的说法表示怀疑。


一般过去时:
She was noncredulous of the story he told. = 她不相信他讲的故事。
The jury were noncredulous of the defendant's alibi. = 陪审团不相信被告的不在场证明。


现在完成时:
I have been noncredulous of such claims for years. = 多年来我一直对这种说法持怀疑态度。
They have remained noncredulous despite the evidence. = 尽管有证据，他们仍然持怀疑态度。


过去完成时:
He had been noncredulous of her honesty until he saw the evidence. = 在他看到证据之前，他一直不相信她的诚实。
We had been noncredulous of the news report until it was confirmed. = 在新闻报道得到证实之前，我们一直对此持怀疑态度。


将来时:
She will be noncredulous of his explanation. = 她将不相信他的解释。
They will likely be noncredulous of any promises made. = 他们很可能会对任何做出的承诺表示怀疑。


虚拟语气:
If I were noncredulous, I would ask for more proof. = 如果我不轻信，我会要求更多证据。
I wish I had been more noncredulous in the past. = 我希望过去我能更怀疑一些。


被动语态:
His story was met with noncredulous stares. = 他的故事受到了怀疑的目光。
The claims were treated with noncredulous skepticism. = 这些说法受到了怀疑的质疑。


疑问句:
Are you noncredulous about the results? = 你对结果是否持怀疑态度？
Is he always so noncredulous of others? = 他总是这么不相信别人吗？


否定句:
He wasn't noncredulous; he simply needed more information. = 他并非不轻信；他只是需要更多信息。
She wasn't noncredulous; she was simply cautious. = 她不是不轻信；她只是谨慎。


PHRASE->
a noncredulous look: 不信的眼神
a noncredulous expression: 不信的表情
a noncredulous audience:  怀疑的观众
remain noncredulous: 保持怀疑
become noncredulous: 变得怀疑


词源:
non- (否定前缀) + credulous (轻信的)  credulous 来自拉丁语 *credulus,  源于 credere (相信)
