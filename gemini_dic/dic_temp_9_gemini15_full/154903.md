peshito:/pɛʃˈiːtoʊ/| n. |叙利亚语圣经|A Syriac translation of the Bible, especially one of the versions used by the Nestorians.|The Peshitta is a Syriac translation of the Bible, highly valued for its antiquity and textual significance. It's considered a crucial text for understanding the development of the Bible's translation and interpretation.|近义词：Syriac Bible, Nestorian Bible|反义词：无
