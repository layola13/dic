pathologists:/pəˈθɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |病理学家| Specialists in pathology, the study of the nature of disease and its causes, processes, development, and consequences. They examine tissues, organs, and bodily fluids to diagnose diseases. |  Pathologists play a crucial role in cancer diagnosis. 病理学家在癌症诊断中发挥着至关重要的作用。|  morphologists, diagnosticians |  general practitioners
