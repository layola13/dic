clarified:/ˈklærɪfaɪd/ | v. |澄清；阐明；净化 | To make something clear or easier to understand; to remove impurities from a liquid. | 例句：He clarified his position on the issue. 他阐明了他在这个问题上的立场。| 例句：The water was clarified by filtering. 水经过过滤后变得清澈。|近义词：explained, elucidated, purified |反义词：confused, obscured, muddied
