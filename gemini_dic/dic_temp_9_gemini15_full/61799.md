emphatical:/ɛmˈfætɪkəl/| adj.|强烈的；强调的；着重的|Emphatical means expressing something forcefully and clearly, leaving no room for doubt or misunderstanding. It suggests a strong conviction or feeling behind the statement.|例句：He gave an emphatical denial of the allegations. 他断然否认了这些指控。|例句：Her emphatical tone left no doubt about her seriousness. 她强硬的语气不容置疑她的认真。|近义词：forceful, strong, emphatic, stressed|反义词：weak, hesitant, uncertain, vague
