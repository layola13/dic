radiuses:/ˈreɪdiˌaɪsɪz/| n.|半径(复数)|The plural of radius.  It refers to multiple distances from a central point to the circumference of a circle or sphere. |例句：The radiuses of the circles were measured precisely.  圆的半径被精确测量。

例句：The architect calculated the radiuses of the arches for the new bridge. 建筑师计算了新桥拱门的半径。


近义词： radii


反义词： diameter (直径)


词性：名词
