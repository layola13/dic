hearkening:/ˈhɑːrkənɪŋ/| v.|倾听，留心，听从|The act of listening attentively; giving heed.  It implies a careful and often expectant listening.|例句：He stood hearkening to the distant sounds of the city. 他站在那里，倾听着远处城市的声音。

例句：The judge hearkened to the witness's testimony.  法官倾听了证人的证词。


近义词：listening, attending, heeding|反义词：ignoring, disregarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hearkens to his grandmother's advice. = 他倾听他祖母的建议。
She hearkens to the wisdom of the ancients. = 她留心古代的智慧。


一般过去时:
They hearkened to the siren's call. = 他们听到了警报声。
He hearkened to the whispers of the wind. = 他倾听风的低语。


现在完成时:
I have hearkened to many wise words in my life. = 我一生中倾听了许多富有智慧的话语。
She has hearkened to the call of adventure. = 她已经听从了冒险的召唤。


过去完成时:
They had hearkened to the rumors for weeks before acting. = 在采取行动之前，他们已经关注了几个星期的谣言。
She had hearkened to the old woman's stories many times. = 她已经多次倾听过老妇人的故事。


将来时:
We will hearken to the instructions of our leader. = 我们将听从我们领导的指示。
They will hearken to the voice of reason. = 他们将倾听理性的声音。


虚拟语气:
If I had hearkened to your warning, I wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果我听了你的警告，我就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had hearkened to my parents' advice. = 我真希望我听了父母的建议。


被动语态:
The speaker's words were hearkened to by the entire audience. = 整个观众都倾听了演讲者的讲话。
His warnings were not hearkened to. = 他的警告没有被重视。


疑问句:
Did you hearken to what I said? = 你有听我说吗？
Will they hearken to the plea for help? = 他们会倾听求助的呼吁吗？


否定句:
He did not hearken to the warnings of the storm. = 他没有理会暴风雨的警告。
They did not hearken to the cries for mercy. = 他们没有理会求饶的声音。


PHRASE->
hearken back to = 回忆起，追溯到
hearken unto = 听从
hearken to the call of = 响应...的号召
to hearken for = 仔细倾听
hearken intently = 专注地倾听


词源:
Old English *hēorcnan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch horen and German hören.  The meaning shifted over time from simply "to hear" to the more deliberate "to listen attentively."
