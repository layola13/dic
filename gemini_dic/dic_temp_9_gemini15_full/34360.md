classicalists:/ˈklæsɪkəlɪsts/| n. |古典学者；古典文学研究者| Scholars specializing in the study of classical antiquity, encompassing ancient Greek and Roman languages, literature, history, philosophy, and culture.  They often possess deep expertise in ancient languages and are engaged in research, teaching, and translation related to the classical world. | The classicalists debated the true meaning of a passage in Homer's Odyssey. 古典学者们就荷马《奥德赛》中一段话的真正含义展开了辩论。| classicists, antiquarians | modernists
