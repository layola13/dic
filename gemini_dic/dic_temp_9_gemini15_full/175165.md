ragga:/ˈræɡə/| n. |雷鬼乐；雷鬼音乐风格|A style of Jamaican popular music characterized by a strong, syncopated rhythm and often featuring toasting (a form of rapping).  It's known for its offbeat rhythms and often features lyrics about social and political issues.|Example:  The band played a lively ragga tune. 乐队演奏了一首欢快的雷鬼乐曲。|近义词：reggae, dancehall|反义词：无
