reinvolvements:/ˌriːɪnˈvɒlvmənts/ | n. | 再参与；重新参与 | The process of becoming involved again in something after a period of disengagement.  It often implies a resumption of a previous activity or relationship. | Example:  The company's reinvolvements in the project after a lengthy hiatus were met with mixed reactions. 公司在长时间中断后重新参与该项目，受到了褒贬不一的反应。|近义词:reengagement, resumption, renewed involvement |反义词: disengagement, withdrawal, abandonment | 名词
