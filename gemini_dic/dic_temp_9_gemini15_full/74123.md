fouler:/ˈfaʊlər/| n. v.|弄脏；犯规者；捕鸟者|to make something dirty; a person who commits a foul; a bird catcher|例句：The oil fouled the engine.  油弄脏了引擎。|近义词：soil, dirty, pollute, offender, poacher|反义词:clean, purify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fouls the basketball game often. = 他经常在篮球比赛中犯规。
The dirty water fouls the river. = 脏水污染了河流。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
They have fouled the air with pollution. = 他们已经用污染污染了空气。
The factory has fouled the river for years. = 这家工厂多年来一直在污染这条河。


过去完成时:
