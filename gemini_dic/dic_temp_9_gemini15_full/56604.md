donatory: /ˈdoʊnətɔːri/ | n. |捐赠者；捐助者| A person or organization that makes a donation.  | The donatory anonymously gave a large sum to the hospital.  捐赠者匿名向医院捐赠了一大笔款项。| donor, contributor, benefactor | recipient, receiver | 名词
