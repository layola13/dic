It can refer to a romantic relationship or a platonic one, and may involve shared living expenses and responsibilities.|例句：The increase in cohabitations reflects changing social attitudes towards marriage. 同居的增加反映了社会对婚姻态度的转变。
例句：Their cohabitation lasted for five years before they decided to marry. 他们同居五年后决定结婚。|近义词：living together, cohabitation|反义词：separation, divorce
