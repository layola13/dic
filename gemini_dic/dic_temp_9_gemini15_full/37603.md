communication: /ˌkəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |交流；沟通；通讯 | The process of conveying information, ideas, or feelings through language or other means.  It involves a sender, a message, a channel, and a receiver. Effective communication requires clarity, conciseness, and consideration of the audience. | Example:  Good communication is essential for a successful team.  良好的沟通对于一个成功的团队至关重要。

Example: The company improved its internal communication through regular meetings and newsletters. 公司通过定期会议和简报改善了内部沟通。


近义词：interaction, transmission, exchange, correspondence | 反义词：silence, isolation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He communicates his ideas effectively. 他有效地传达了他的想法。
We communicate daily via email. 我们每天通过电子邮件沟通。


一般过去时:
She communicated her concerns to the manager. 她向经理表达了她的担忧。
They communicated the news to the public. 他们向公众传达了这个消息。


现在完成时:
They have communicated their decision to us. 他们已经把他们的决定告诉我们了。
I have communicated with him several times. 我已经和他联系过几次了。


过去完成时:
He had communicated his intentions before leaving. 他在离开之前已经表达了他的意图。
She had communicated with the client earlier that day. 她当天早些时候已经与客户联系过了。


将来时:
We will communicate the results next week. 我们下周将公布结果。
He will communicate his findings in the report. 他将在报告中公布他的调查结果。


虚拟语气:
If I could communicate with animals, I would. 如果我能和动物交流，我会的。
I wish I could communicate my feelings better. 我希望我能更好地表达我的感受。


被动语态:
The message was communicated clearly. 信息传达得很清楚。
The decision will be communicated to all employees.  该决定将通知所有员工。


疑问句:
How do you communicate with your family? 你如何与你的家人沟通？
What methods of communication are available?  有哪些沟通方法可用？


否定句:
