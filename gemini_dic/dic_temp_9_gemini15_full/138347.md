noticeable: /ˈnoʊtɪsəbl/ | adj. |显著的，明显的，值得注意的 | Easily seen or noticed; readily apparent.  |例句：There was a noticeable improvement in his work. 他的工作有了显著的改进。

例句：The difference between the two products is noticeable. 这两种产品的区别很明显。


近义词：apparent, obvious, conspicuous, perceptible, marked, evident, detectable | 反义词：inconspicuous, imperceptible, unnoticeable


词源:  From the verb "notice" + "-able" (suffix indicating capability or tendency).  The verb "notice" itself comes from Old French "noticer" meaning "to be aware of," ultimately from Latin "notare" meaning "to mark."
