godforsaken:/ˈɡɑːdˌfɔːrsəkn/| adj. |该死的，荒凉的，糟糕透顶的 | Extremely unpleasant or undesirable; desolate and abandoned.  The word implies a feeling of being forsaken or abandoned by God, hence implying extreme misfortune or unpleasantness. |例句：He lived in a godforsaken shack miles from anywhere. 他住在远离任何地方的破旧小屋里。

例句：This godforsaken project is taking forever.  这个该死的项目永远都做不完。


近义词：cursed, wretched, desolate, awful, terrible, dreadful

反义词：blessed, wonderful, delightful, heavenly
