pailful:/ˈpeɪlfʊl/ | n. |一桶…；满满一桶 |A pailful is the amount that a pail can hold.  It refers to a quantity measured by filling a pail.  The word implies that the pail is full or nearly full. |Example:  He carried a pailful of water. 他提了一桶水。  She poured a pailful of sand into the hole. 她往洞里倒了一桶沙子。|近义词：bucketful |反义词：N/A
