freers:/ˈfriːərz/| n. |解放者；释放者|A person who frees others from oppression or slavery.  Freers often fight for freedom and liberation.|Example:  The abolitionists were freers who fought tirelessly to end slavery.  废奴主义者是为结束奴隶制而不知疲倦地奋斗的解放者。|近义词：liberator, emancipator |反义词：oppressor, enslaver |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The organization frees many prisoners every year. = 这个组织每年释放许多囚犯。
She frees her time for her hobbies. = 她腾出时间来从事她的爱好。


一般过去时:
He freed the bird from its cage. = 他把鸟从笼子里放了出来。
They freed the city from the enemy's control. = 他们解放了这座城市，使其摆脱了敌人的控制。


现在完成时:
They have freed many people from poverty. = 他们已经使许多人摆脱了贫困。
She has freed herself from the constraints of tradition. = 她已经摆脱了传统的束缚。


过去完成时:
He had freed the slaves before the war ended. = 在战争结束之前，他已经解放了奴隶。
The government had freed the political prisoners by then. = 到那时，政府已经释放了政治犯。


将来时:
They will free the hostages soon. = 他们很快就会释放人质。
The new law will free up more resources for education. = 新法将为教育提供更多资源。


虚拟语气:
If only I could free myself from this burden! = 但愿我能摆脱这个负担！
I wish I could free my mind from worries. = 我希望我能摆脱烦恼。


被动语态:
The prisoners were freed yesterday. = 囚犯们昨天被释放了。
The city was freed from the siege.  = 城市解除了围困。


疑问句:
Who freed the animals from the zoo? = 谁把动物从动物园里放了出来？
Will the new policy free up more time for us? = 新政策会为我们腾出更多时间吗？


否定句:
He didn't free the dog from the chain. = 他没有把狗从链子上解下来。
The law did not free them from their debts. = 这项法律并没有使他们免除债务。
