intense:/ɪnˈtɛns/ | adj. | 强烈的；剧烈的；紧张的 |  Characterized by a high degree of strength, force, or feeling; vehement; fervent.  | 例句：The heat was intense.  = 热度很强烈。 | 例句：She felt an intense pain in her leg. = 她的腿感到剧烈的疼痛。 | 近义词：strong, powerful, severe, fervent, passionate | 反义词：mild, weak, gentle, moderate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun is intensely hot today. = 今天的太阳非常热。
He intensely dislikes her. = 他非常讨厌她。
