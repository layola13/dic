pinpoint:/ˈpɪn.pɔɪnt/|/ˈpɪn.pɔɪnt/| vt. n.|精确指出；查明；确定；极小点；微小的部分|to discover or describe the exact location of something or the exact time when something happened|The police were able to pinpoint the location of the suspect using GPS technology.  警方能够利用GPS技术精确查明嫌疑人的位置。

The study pinpointed the exact cause of the disease. 这项研究精确地查明了该疾病的病因。


近义词：locate, identify, determine, specify, isolate

反义词：misidentify, mislocate
