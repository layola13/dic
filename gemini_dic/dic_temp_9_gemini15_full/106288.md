knuckleheads:/ˈnʌkəlˌhedz/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A knucklehead is a foolish or stupid person.  The term is informal and often used playfully or affectionately, though it can also be insulting depending on context.|例句：Those knuckleheads forgot to bring the beer to the party.  那些笨蛋忘了带啤酒去派对。

例句：Don't be such a knucklehead; think before you act!  别那么傻，行动前先想想！|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, dunce, simpleton|反义词：genius, intellectual, mastermind
