self-condemned:/ˌsɛlf kənˈdɛmd/| adj. | 自责的；自我谴责的；被判有罪的 |  Describing someone who feels guilt or shame for their actions, or something that has been judged or found guilty.  | Example: He felt self-condemned after telling the lie. 他撒谎后感到自责。  He was self-condemned for his crimes. 他因罪行而被判有罪。|近义词：guilty, self-reproaching, condemned, culpable |反义词：innocent, guiltless, acquitted
