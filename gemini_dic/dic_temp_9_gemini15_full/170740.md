pseudobacteria:/ˌsuːdəʊbækˈtɪəriə/| n. |假细菌；类细菌|Organisms that resemble bacteria in morphology but differ significantly in their genetic makeup and other fundamental characteristics. They are often found in extreme environments and may play important roles in nutrient cycling and other ecological processes.|Example:  Researchers are studying the unique metabolic pathways of pseudobacteria found in hydrothermal vents. 研究人员正在研究在热液喷口发现的假细菌独特的代谢途径。|近义词：Archaea (in some contexts)|反义词：Eubacteria (true bacteria)|名词
