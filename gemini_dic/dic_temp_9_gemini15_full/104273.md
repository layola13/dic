justice:/ˈdʒʌstɪs/| n.|正义，公平，司法，法官|The quality of being just; righteousness, equitableness, or moral rightness.  It also refers to the system of law, or a judge.|例句：The pursuit of justice is a fundamental human right.  追求正义是基本人权。|例句：She dedicated her life to fighting for social justice. 她毕生致力于争取社会正义。|近义词：fairness, equity, righteousness, right|反义词：injustice, unfairness, inequity
