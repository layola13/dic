seemingness:/ˈsiːmɪŋnəs/| n. |表面现象；貌似；外观 |The seemingness of his calmness belied his inner turmoil.  他的平静外表掩盖了他内心的不安。|  appearance, semblance, show | reality, truth, substance | 名词
