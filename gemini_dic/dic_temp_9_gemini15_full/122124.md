mistranscribing: /ˌmɪsˈtrænskraɪbɪŋ/ | v. | 误抄写；误录；错误转录 | The act of incorrectly writing or recording something.  It implies a mistake in the transcription process, not necessarily a misunderstanding of the original material.  The error could be due to carelessness, poor hearing, or technical difficulties. |


例句：

He was accused of mistranscribing the witness's testimony. = 他被指控误抄了证人的证词。

The software mistranscribed several words in the audio file, resulting in an inaccurate transcript. = 该软件误录了音频文件中的几个单词，导致成绩单不准确。


近义词：miscopying, misrecording, paraphrasing incorrectly, misinterpreting (if the error stems from misunderstanding)


反义词：transcribing accurately, correctly recording


词性：动词
