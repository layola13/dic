misstatement:/ˌmɪsˈsteɪtmənt/| n. |错误陈述，误述 |An inaccurate or untrue statement.  A misstatement of facts can have serious consequences.  |The lawyer's misstatement of the law led to the client's conviction.  律师对法律的错误陈述导致了当事人的定罪。|false statement, inaccuracy, error|accurate statement, truth|名词
