insites:/ˈɪnsaɪts/| n.|洞察力，见解|The ability to have a deep understanding of a person or situation.|例句：Her insights into human nature were remarkable.  她的对人性的洞察力非凡。|例句：The report offers valuable insights into the causes of the crisis.这份报告提供了对危机原因的宝贵见解。|近义词：understanding, comprehension, perception|反义词：misunderstanding, ignorance


USAGE->

She demonstrates keen insights in her work. = 她在工作中展现出敏锐的洞察力。
