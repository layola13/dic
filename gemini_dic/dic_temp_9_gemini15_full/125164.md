multibillionaire:/ˌmʌltiˈbɪljəneər/| n. |亿万富翁，身价数十亿美元的人 | A multibillionaire is an individual whose net worth is in the billions of dollars.  They possess immense wealth and often have significant influence in various sectors. |  Elon Musk is a well-known multibillionaire.  埃隆·马斯克是一位著名的亿万富翁。  Jeff Bezos was once a multibillionaire. 杰夫·贝佐斯曾经是一位亿万富翁。| billionaire, magnate | pauper, beggar | 名词
