rums:/rʌmz/| n.|朗姆酒|Rum is a distilled alcoholic beverage made from sugarcane byproducts such as molasses or sugarcane juice.  It's known for its diverse flavor profiles depending on the ingredients and aging process.|例句：He ordered a glass of dark rum. 他点了一杯黑朗姆酒。|例句：The bartender mixed a rum and coke. 酒保调了一杯朗姆可乐。|近义词：spirits, liquor|反义词：无
