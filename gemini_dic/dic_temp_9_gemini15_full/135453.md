nonpertinently:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːtɪnəntli/ | adv. | 不相关地，不相干地 | Irrelevantly; without relevance.  | Example: He nonpertinently brought up his childhood memories during the serious business meeting. 他在严肃的商务会议上不相关地提起了他的童年回忆。|近义词：irrelevantly, inappropriately, inconsequentially | 反义词：pertinently, relevantly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb,  therefore it doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  It describes how an action is performed.  The tense of the verb it modifies will determine the tense of the sentence.

She often acts nonpertinently. = 她经常做事不相关。
