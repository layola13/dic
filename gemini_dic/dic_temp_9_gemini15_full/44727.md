cruisers:/ˈkruːzərz/| n.|巡洋舰，巡洋车|A cruiser is a type of ship or vehicle designed for long-range patrols or voyages.  Ships are typically warships, while vehicles are usually motorcycles or cars.|例句：The police cruiser sped down the highway. 警车飞驰在高速公路上。

例句：That vintage cruiser is a real head-turner. 那辆老式巡洋车真吸引眼球。|近义词：warship, patrol boat, motorcycle, car|反义词：fighter (for ships),  sports car (for vehicles).
