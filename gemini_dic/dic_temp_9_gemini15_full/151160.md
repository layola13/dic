paraxylene: /ˌpærəˈzaɪliːn/ | n. | 邻二甲苯 | A colorless liquid hydrocarbon, a positional isomer of xylene, used in the production of terephthalic acid, a key component in the production of polyethylene terephthalate (PET).  It's also used as a solvent. | Example: Paraxylene is a crucial raw material in the manufacturing of polyester fibers.  邻二甲苯是生产涤纶纤维的关键原料。 |近义词:dimethylbenzene (less specific)| 反义词:None (positional isomers don't have true antonyms) | noun
