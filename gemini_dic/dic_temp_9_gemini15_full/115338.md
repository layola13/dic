mano-a-mano:/ˈmænə ə ˈmænəʊ/| adj. | 单独面对面地；一对一地 |  A contest or fight between two individuals, directly and without intermediaries. It emphasizes a direct and personal confrontation. |例句：The two rivals finally met mano a mano in the championship game.  两个对手终于在冠军赛中单挑了。
The debate was a mano a mano discussion between the two candidates.  这场辩论是两位候选人之间的直接对话。

近义词：one-on-one, head-to-head, face-to-face, individually

反义词：indirectly, through intermediaries, collectively


词性:形容词 (though sometimes used adverbially)
