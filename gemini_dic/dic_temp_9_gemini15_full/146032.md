overdress:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɛs/| vt.|穿着过度；打扮得太隆重|To wear clothes that are too formal or fancy for an occasion.  |例句：She overdressed for the casual party. 她穿得太隆重去参加那个休闲派对。|例句：Don't overdress for the interview; business casual is fine. 面试时不要穿得太正式；商务休闲装就可以了。|近义词：over-attire, overdo|反义词：underdress
