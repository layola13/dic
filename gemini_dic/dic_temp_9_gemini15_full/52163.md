diaphragm: /ˈdaɪəfræm/ | n. |膈膜；光圈；振膜 | A membranous partition separating the thoracic cavity from the abdominal cavity in mammals.  It plays a major role in breathing.  It can also refer to a device that regulates the aperture of an optical instrument or the vibration of sound in a speaker. | 例句：The diaphragm contracts and relaxes to help with breathing.  膈膜收缩和放松以帮助呼吸。  The photographer adjusted the diaphragm on his camera lens. 摄影师调整了他相机镜头上的光圈。 | 近义词：midriff, membrane | 反义词： N/A
