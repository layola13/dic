gamifies:/ˈɡæmɪfaɪz/ | vt. |使游戏化；赋予游戏特性 | To apply game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts.  Gamification uses game thinking and game mechanics to engage users and solve problems. | Example:  The company gamified its employee training program to increase engagement and improve results.  公司将其员工培训计划游戏化，以提高参与度和改善结果。| 近义词：game-ify, ludify | 反义词：de-gamify


USAGE->

They gamify their marketing campaigns. = 他们将他们的营销活动游戏化。
The app gamifies the learning process. = 这个应用程序使学习过程游戏化。
