defter:/ˈdɛftər/| n. |记事本，账簿|A defter is a type of bound book, often used for recording information, accounts, or notes.  It can refer to a notebook, ledger, or other similar record-keeping device.  The term is often used in historical contexts, particularly relating to Ottoman administration.|Example: The merchant carefully recorded all his transactions in his defter.|近义词：notebook, ledger, journal, register |反义词：None specifically, but words implying lack of organization could be considered opposites (e.g., chaos, disarray).
