consummating:/ˈkɒnsəmeɪtɪŋ/| v.|完成；使完美；性交|To complete or make perfect; to bring to a state of completion;  specifically, to have sexual intercourse to consummate a marriage.|例句：They consummated their marriage with a romantic honeymoon.他们以浪漫的蜜月完成了他们的婚姻。|例句：The deal was consummated after weeks of negotiations.经过数周的谈判，交易终于完成了。|近义词：complete, perfect, fulfill, achieve |反义词：ruin, destroy, spoil
