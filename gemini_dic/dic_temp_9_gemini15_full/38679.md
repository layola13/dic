condemnors:/kənˈdɛmnərz/ | n. |谴责者，定罪者 |Those who express strong disapproval of something, especially a person's actions or behaviour; those who pronounce a sentence of punishment. |例句：The condemnors of the war protested loudly in the streets.  战争的谴责者在街上大声抗议。
The judge, along with the condemnors, felt the sentence was just.  法官和谴责者们都认为判决是公正的。|近义词：critics, censurers, detractors |反义词：supporters,approvers
