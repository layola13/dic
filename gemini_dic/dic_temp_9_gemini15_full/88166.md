hills:/hɪlz/| n. |小山，丘陵|A hill is a raised area of land, smaller than a mountain.|例句：We went for a walk in the hills. 我们去山里散步了。|近义词：knolls, mounds, elevations|反义词：valleys, plains


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hills are green in spring. 春天山峦一片翠绿。
These hills offer stunning views. 这些山丘提供了令人惊叹的景色。

一般过去时:
The hills were covered in snow. 山上覆盖着雪。
We climbed those hills yesterday. 我们昨天爬了那些山。

现在完成时:
We have hiked these hills many times. 我们已经多次徒步穿越这些山丘了。
The rain has eroded the hillsides. 雨水侵蚀了山坡。


过去完成时:
