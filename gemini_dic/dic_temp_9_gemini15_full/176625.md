reaccommodated:/ˌriːəkˈɑːmədeɪtɪd/ | v. |重新安排；重新安置；重新提供住宿 | To provide new or different accommodation for someone.  It implies a previous arrangement has been altered or improved. |例句：The hotel reaccommodated us in a larger room after we complained about the noise.  酒店在我们抱怨噪音后，把我们安排到了一个更大的房间。

例句：The airline reaccommodated the passengers on a later flight after the initial one was cancelled. 航空公司在最初的航班取消后，将乘客安排到了后来的航班上。


近义词：relocate, rearrange, rehouse |反义词：dislodge, displace
