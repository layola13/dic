nonpublishable: /ˌnɒnˈpʌblɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 不可出版的 | Not suitable for publication; likely to cause offense or legal trouble if published.  | Example: The editor deemed the article nonpublishable due to its libelous content.  编辑认为这篇文章因其诽谤内容而不可出版。 | 近义词：unpublishable, unsuitable for publication | 反义词：publishable
