rainmaker:/ˈreɪnˌmeɪkər/ | n. |造雨者；成功人士；影响力大的人 |A rainmaker is someone who is very successful and influential, especially in business.  They are able to bring in a lot of money or achieve great things for their organization.  The term originally referred to someone who could magically produce rain, but now it's used figuratively. |例句：He's a rainmaker in the tech industry, bringing in millions in investment every year. 他是科技行业的成功人士，每年带来数百万美元的投资。  The new CEO is considered a rainmaker, transforming the struggling company into a market leader. 新任CEO被认为是位成功人士，将这家苦苦挣扎的公司变成了市场领导者。|近义词：high-flyer, dealmaker, power broker |反义词：underachiever, failure
