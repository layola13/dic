词源:
The word "reascents" is a combination of the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back") and the noun "ascents" (meaning "a rising or climbing").  Therefore, it literally means "rising again" or "climbing back up".  The word is not as common as "recovery" or "resurgence," but functions similarly.
