nonimpeachabilities:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpiːtʃəbɪlətiz/| n.|不可弹劾性；免于弹劾的特权|The inherent qualities or characteristics that protect someone from impeachment.  This term refers to the legal protections and limitations that prevent someone from being subject to impeachment proceedings.  It often involves aspects of constitutional immunity or procedural safeguards.| Example: The president's nonimpeachabilities were debated extensively during the political crisis.  总统在政治危机期间的不可弹劾性受到了广泛的争论。 |近义词: immunity from impeachment, protection from removal,  safeguard against impeachment|反义词:impeachability, vulnerability to impeachment|名词
