degradement: /dɪˈɡreɪdmənt/ | n. | 降低；降级；堕落；贬低 | The process of reducing something in quality, value, or status; deterioration; humiliation.  |例句：The degradation of the environment is a serious concern. 环境恶化是一个严重的问题。

例句：His public degradation was a shameful spectacle.  他的公开羞辱是一场可耻的闹剧。


近义词：deterioration, decline, downfall, humiliation, debasement

反义词：improvement, elevation, enhancement, upliftment


词源:  From the verb "degrade," which comes from the Old French "degrader," meaning "to lower in rank" or "to debase."  The prefix "de-" means "down" or "away," and the root "-grade" relates to steps or degrees.
