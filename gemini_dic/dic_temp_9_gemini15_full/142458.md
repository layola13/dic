oscinian:/ˈɒsɪniən/| adj.|奥西尼安的；鸣禽类的|Of or relating to the oscines, a suborder of passerine birds characterized by their complex vocalizations.  The term often implies a melodious or musical quality of song.|例句：The oscinian songbirds of the rainforest are incredibly diverse.  亚马逊雨林中的鸣禽种类繁多。|近义词：passerine (in the context of songbirds), melodius|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms describing birds with less developed vocalizations.
