full-length:/ˈfʊlˈleŋθ/| adj.| 全长的；完整的；（电影）完整版的 |  Describes something that is the complete length or duration, without any omissions or abridgments.  It can refer to physical objects or to works of art like films. |例句：The museum displayed a full-length portrait of the queen.  博物馆展出了女王的一幅全身画像。|例句：We watched the full-length version of the movie. 我们观看了电影的完整版。|近义词：complete, entire, unabridged |反义词：abbreviated, shortened, abridged
