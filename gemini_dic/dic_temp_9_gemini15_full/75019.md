friends:/frendz/| n.|朋友们，友人|People you know and like, and with whom you spend time.|例句：I'm going to the park with my friends. 我要去公园和我的朋友们一起玩。|例句：She has many friends all over the world. 她全世界各地都有很多朋友。|近义词：companions, pals, buddies|反义词：enemies, foes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My friends help me a lot. = 我的朋友们帮助我很多。
My friends often visit me. = 我的朋友们经常来看我。


一般过去时:
My friends played games yesterday. = 我的朋友们昨天玩游戏了。
I met my friends at the cafe last night. = 我昨晚在咖啡馆遇到了我的朋友们。


现在完成时:
I have known my best friend for ten years. = 我认识我最好的朋友十年了。
She has made many new friends since moving here. = 她搬到这里之后交了很多新朋友。


过去完成时:
I had already met my friends before the party started. = 在派对开始之前我已经见过我的朋友们了。
They had known each other for years before they started working together. = 他们在开始一起工作之前已经认识很多年了。


将来时:
I will meet my friends tomorrow. = 我明天将要见我的朋友们。
We will go to the cinema with our friends next weekend. = 我们下周末将和朋友们一起去电影院。


虚拟语气:
If I had more friends, I would be happier. = 如果我有更多朋友，我会更快乐。
I wish I had more time to spend with my friends. = 我希望我有更多时间和朋友们一起度过。


被动语态:
My friends are often invited to parties. = 我的朋友们经常被邀请参加派对。
The problem was discussed among friends. = 这个问题在朋友们之间被讨论了。


疑问句:
Do you have many friends? = 你有很多朋友吗？
Who are your friends? = 你的朋友是谁？


否定句:
I don't have many close friends. = 我没有很多亲密的朋友。
She doesn't want to go to the party without her friends. = 她不想没有朋友就参加派对。


PHRASE->
best friends = 最好的朋友
close friends = 亲密的朋友
make friends = 交朋友
friends with = 和...是朋友
a friend of mine = 我的一个朋友
