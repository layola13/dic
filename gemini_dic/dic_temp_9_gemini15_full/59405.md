earthbound:/ˈɜːrθbaʊnd/| adj.|受地球束缚的；思想狭隘的；缺乏想象力的|confined to the earth; lacking imagination or vision; prosaic. |例句：The earthbound astronomer could only dream of space travel.  受地球束缚的天文学家只能梦想太空旅行。

例句：His earthbound perspective prevented him from understanding the broader implications. 他思想狭隘的视角妨碍了他理解更广泛的影响。


近义词：terrestrial, mundane, worldly


反义词：celestial, ethereal, transcendent
