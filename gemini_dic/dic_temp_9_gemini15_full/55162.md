dissembled:/dɪˈsɛmbəl/| vt.|掩饰；伪装；遮掩|To conceal one's true feelings or intentions.|例句：He dissembled his anger, pretending to be calm. 他掩饰了自己的怒火，假装很平静。


例句：She dissembled her true feelings with a forced smile. 她强作欢笑，掩饰了真实的情感。


近义词：conceal, disguise, mask, feign, simulate|反义词：reveal, disclose, expose, confess


词性:动词

词源:
From Old French *desembler, from des- (from Latin dis-) + sembler "to seem, resemble," from Latin similis "like."  The original meaning was "to be unlike, to differ," but it evolved to mean "to disguise, to conceal."
