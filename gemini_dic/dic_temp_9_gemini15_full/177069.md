reapers:/ˈriːpərz/| n.|收割机；收割者|A reaper is a machine or person that harvests crops, especially grain.|例句：The farmers used reapers to harvest the wheat.  农民们用收割机收割小麦。|例句：The reapers worked tirelessly in the hot sun. 收割者们在烈日下辛勤工作。|近义词：harvester, gatherer|反义词：sower (播种者)
