perching:/ˈpɜːrtʃɪŋ/|/ˈpɝːtʃɪŋ/| |v.|栖息；停落；（使）短暂停留 |The act of a bird or other animal resting on something.  It can also refer to the act of a person briefly settling or positioning themselves. |例句：The birds were perching on the telephone wires.  鸟儿栖息在电线上。

The eagle perched high on the cliff. 鹰在悬崖上高高地栖息着。


近义词：resting, roosting, settling, alight


反义词：flying, soaring, departing


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird perches on the branch. = 鸟栖息在树枝上。
He perches himself on the edge of the chair. = 他坐在椅子边上。


一般过去时:
The robin perched on the fence post. = 知更鸟停落在篱笆桩上。
She perched her bag on the table. = 她把包放在桌子上。


现在完成时:
The hawk has perched on that tree for hours. = 鹰在那棵树上栖息了好几个小时。
He has perched there since lunchtime. = 他从午餐时间就一直坐在那里。


过去完成时:
The bird had perched there before the storm arrived. = 在暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿就已经在那里栖息了。
She had perched her hopes on winning the competition. = 她曾寄希望于赢得比赛。


将来时:
The bird will perch on the highest branch. = 鸟儿将栖息在最高的树枝上。
He will perch himself near the window. = 他将坐在窗户附近。


虚拟语气:
If the bird perched closer, I could take a better picture. = 如果鸟儿再靠近一些，我就能拍到更好的照片。
I wish he would perch still for a moment. = 我希望他能安静地待一会儿。


被动语态:
The branch was perched on by many birds. = 许多鸟栖息在树枝上。(此用法较少见，一般主动语态更常用)
The bag was carefully perched on the shelf. = 包被小心地放在架子上。


疑问句:
Where does the bird perch? = 鸟儿在哪里栖息？
Where did she perch her cup? = 她把杯子放在哪里？


否定句:
The bird didn't perch on the branch. = 鸟儿没有栖息在树枝上。
He couldn't perch comfortably on the small stool. = 他不能舒服地坐在那个小凳子上。



PHRASE->
perch on = 栖息于
perch oneself = 使自己坐好
perch up = 爬上；向上爬
perch precariously = 危险地栖息
to perch a hat = 戴帽子
