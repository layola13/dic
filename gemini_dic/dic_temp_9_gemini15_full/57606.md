drapey:/ˈdreɪpi/| adj.| 松垂的；褶皱的；柔软下垂的 | Having or characterized by soft, loose folds; hanging or falling in loose folds.  The fabric has a drapey quality, perfect for flowing gowns.  |The silk scarf had a lovely drapey texture.|近义词：flowing, flowing, loose, limp|反义词：stiff, rigid, taut
