disfrock:/dɪsˈfrɒk/| vt.|剥夺僧侣身份；脱去僧侣长袍；解职|To remove someone from their position or office, especially a religious one; to divest of priestly or monastic robes.|例句：The bishop decided to disfrock the priest for his misconduct. 主教决定因其不当行为而剥夺该牧师的僧侣身份。|例句：The disgraced monk was disfrocked and sent back to his village.  那位声名狼藉的僧侣被剥夺僧侣身份并送回他的村庄。|近义词：defrock, unfrocking, dismiss, degrade|反义词：ordain, invest
