self-build:/ˈsɛlfbɪld/| adj. n. |自己建造的；自己建造|Something that is self-built is constructed by the person or people who will live in or use it, rather than by a professional builder.  It can refer to a house, a piece of furniture, or other projects.|例句：They are proud of their self-build house. 他们为他们自己建造的房子感到骄傲。

例句：He enjoys self-build projects as a hobby. 他喜欢自己动手制作项目作为爱好。


近义词：DIY, home-built, owner-built|反义词：professionally built, commercially built|词性：adj., n.
