high-pitched:/haɪˈpɪtʃt/| adj.|尖声的，高音调的|Having a high pitch or tone.  The sound is high and thin, not low and deep.|例句：Her voice was high-pitched and slightly shrill.  她的声音尖细而刺耳。

例句：The whistle had a high-pitched, piercing sound.  哨子发出尖锐刺耳的声音。

近义词： shrill, treble, acute, piercing

反义词： low-pitched, bass, deep
