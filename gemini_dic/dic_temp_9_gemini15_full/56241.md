dog-ends: /ˈdɒɡˌɛndz/ | n. |香烟屁股；残余物；琐碎的事物 |The small pieces remaining at the end of something, especially a cigarette.  Often used metaphorically to refer to leftover scraps or unimportant details.|

例句：

He picked up the dog-ends from the floor. 他捡起了地上的烟头。

They were left with the dog-ends of the project.  他们只剩下这项工程的残余部分了。


近义词： remnants, scraps, leavings, remains, odds and ends


反义词：  whole, complete, entirety


词性: 名词
