over-designing:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪnɪŋ/ | v. |过度设计|The act of designing something with more complexity or features than necessary, often leading to inefficiency, increased cost, and reduced usability.  It often involves adding features that are rarely or never used.|例句：Over-designing the software resulted in delays and increased development costs.  过度设计软件导致了延误和增加的开发成本。

例句：The bridge was over-designed, making it unnecessarily expensive and complex. 这座桥梁设计过度，使其不必要地昂贵和复杂。


近义词：over-engineering, overcomplicating, gold-plating


反义词：simplifying, streamlining, minimizing


词性:动词
