monotheist:/ˈmɒnəθiːɪst/| /ˈmɑːnəθiːɪst/| n. |一神论者|A monotheist is a person who believes in the existence of only one god.  Monotheism is the belief in a single god.  This contrasts with polytheism, which is the belief in multiple gods.|Many Abrahamic religions, such as Judaism, Christianity, and Islam, are monotheistic.|近义词：unitarian, believer in one god|反义词：polytheist
