disconducive: /ˌdɪskənˈduːsɪv/ | adj. | 不利的；有害的；阻碍的 | Not conducive; unfavorable; hindering.  | The noisy environment was disconducive to studying. 嘈杂的环境不利于学习。 |近义词：unfavorable, detrimental, unhelpful, counterproductive | 反义词：conducive, helpful, beneficial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The noisy environment is disconducive to concentration.  = 嘈杂的环境不利于集中注意力。
This policy is disconducive to economic growth. =这项政策不利于经济增长。


一般过去时:
The lack of communication was disconducive to team success. = 缺乏沟通不利于团队成功。
His negative attitude was disconducive to a productive meeting. = 他的消极态度不利于有效会议的进行。


现在完成时:
The constant interruptions have been disconducive to my work. =  不断的打断已经妨碍了我的工作。
Years of neglect have been disconducive to the building's structural integrity. = 多年的忽视已经损害了建筑物的结构完整性。


过去完成时:
The poor planning had been disconducive to the project's timely completion. =  糟糕的计划已经不利于项目的按时完成。
His previous experience had been disconducive to his adaptation to the new role. = 他之前的经验不利于他适应新的角色。


将来时:
The upcoming changes will be disconducive to employee morale. =  即将到来的变化将不利于员工士气。
Further delays will be disconducive to the project's success. = 进一步的延误将不利于项目的成功。


虚拟语气:
If the conditions were different, it would not be so disconducive to progress. = 如果情况不同，它就不会如此阻碍进步。
I wish the circumstances were not so disconducive to our efforts. = 我希望情况不会如此不利于我们的努力。


被动语态:
The project was hampered by conditions that were disconducive to its success. =  该项目受到不利于其成功的条件的阻碍。
His reputation was damaged by actions that were disconducive to his career. =  他的声誉因不利于他职业生涯的行为而受损。


疑问句:
Are these conditions disconducive to the experiment? = 这些条件不利于实验吗？
Is this approach disconducive to achieving our goals? =  这种方法不利于实现我们的目标吗？


否定句:
The environment was not disconducive to learning; in fact, it was quite stimulating. = 环境并不不利于学习；事实上，它相当有刺激性。
The new system is not disconducive to efficiency; it actually improves it. = 新系统并不妨碍效率；它实际上提高了效率。


PHRASE->

disconducive to success = 不利于成功
disconducive to growth = 不利于成长
