contemptuously: /kənˈtɛmptʃuəsli/ | adv. | 轻蔑地；蔑视地 | Showing contempt; scornfully.  |例句：He contemptuously dismissed their concerns as trivial. 他轻蔑地将他们的担忧斥为琐事。

例句：She looked at him contemptuously, her lips curling into a sneer. 她轻蔑地望着他，嘴唇弯成嘲讽的冷笑。


近义词：scornfully, disdainfully, derisively, sneeringly

反义词：respectfully, admiringly, approvingly


词源:  From contemptuous + -ly.  "Contemptuous" comes from the Latin *contemptus*, the past participle of *contemnere*, meaning "to despise" or "to scorn."  This in turn is composed of *con-* (together, thoroughly) and *temnere* (to disregard, despise).
