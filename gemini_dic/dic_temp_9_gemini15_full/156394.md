physostigmine:/ˌfaɪzoʊˈstɪɡmiːn/ | n. |毒扁豆碱|  A naturally occurring alkaloid found in the Calabar bean (Physostigma venenosum), used as a drug to treat certain conditions such as glaucoma and myasthenia gravis. It acts by inhibiting acetylcholinesterase, an enzyme that breaks down acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter.  | Example: Physostigmine is a cholinergic drug that increases acetylcholine levels in the body. 毒扁豆碱是一种胆碱能药物，可以增加体内乙酰胆碱的水平。|  近义词：neostigmine, pyridostigmine | 反义词：atropine
