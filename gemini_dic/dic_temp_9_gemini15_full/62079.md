encephalitis:/ˌɛnsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs/| n. |脑炎|Inflammation of the brain, usually caused by a viral infection but can also be caused by bacteria, fungi, or parasites.  Symptoms can vary widely depending on the cause and location of the inflammation, but may include headache, fever, confusion, seizures, and weakness.|Example:  The doctor suspected encephalitis after observing the patient's severe headache and seizures.  医生在观察到病人严重的头部疼痛和癫痫发作后怀疑是脑炎。


Example:  Early diagnosis and treatment of encephalitis are crucial to minimize long-term complications.  及早诊断和治疗脑炎对于最大限度地减少长期并发症至关重要。
