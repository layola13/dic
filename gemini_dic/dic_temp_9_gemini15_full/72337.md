flowed:/floʊd/ | v. | 流动；流淌 |  To move smoothly and continuously in a certain direction, like a liquid.  | The river flowed gently to the sea.  这条河缓缓流向大海。| The tears flowed freely down her cheeks. 泪水顺着她的脸颊自由地流淌。|近义词： streamed, poured, coursed | 反义词： stagnated, stopped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water flows smoothly. = 水流得很平稳。
The river flows to the ocean. = 这条河流向大海。

一般过去时:
The river flowed rapidly yesterday. = 河水昨天流得很快。
The honey flowed from the jar. = 蜜糖从罐子里流出来。

现在完成时:
The river has flowed continuously for centuries. = 这条河已经连续流淌了几个世纪。
Much water has flowed under the bridge. = 沧海桑田。

过去完成时:
The river had flowed over its banks before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，河水已经漫过河岸。
The water had flowed away before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，水已经流走了。

将来时:
The river will flow more quickly in the spring. = 春天河水会流得更快。
The lava will flow down the mountainside. = 岩浆将流下山坡。

虚拟语气:
If the river flowed faster, it would erode the banks more quickly. = 如果河水流得更快，它就会更快地侵蚀河岸。
I wish the river flowed clearer. = 我希望河水更清澈。

被动语态:
The water is flowed into the reservoir. = 水被流入水库。 (Note:  This is grammatically awkward;  better phrasing would be "The water flows into the reservoir" or "Water is channeled into the reservoir.")
The dam was flowed over by flood water. = 水坝被洪水冲垮。(Note: This is also grammatically awkward, better to say: "The dam was overflown by flood water" or "Flood water overflowed the dam")


疑问句:
Does the river flow all year round? = 这条河一年四季都流淌吗？
Where does the lava flow? = 岩浆流向哪里？

否定句:
The water didn't flow freely. = 水流得不畅通。
The river doesn't flow in this direction. = 河水不朝这个方向流。


PHRASE->
blood flowed = 血流如注

information flowed = 信息流通
ideas flowed = 思路清晰
