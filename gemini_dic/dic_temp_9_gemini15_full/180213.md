refounder: /ˌriːˈfaʊndər/ | v. | 重新创立者；改革者 | To establish or create something again, often after a period of decline or destruction; to reorganize or reform something significantly. | The company's refounder implemented new strategies that revitalized the business.  这家公司的改革者实施了新的战略，使企业焕发了活力。|近义词：reformer, innovator, restorer |反义词：destroyer, dismantler


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refounds the organization with a new vision. 他以新的愿景重建了这个组织。
She refounds her belief in the project after setbacks.  她在遭遇挫折后重建了她对项目的信心。


一般过去时:
