firebote:/ˈfaɪərbəʊt/| n. |火柴; 燃料木|The right to take firewood from another's land for fuel.  This is an ancient common law right, typically granted to tenants or residents of certain properties.  It is a specific type of estovers. |例句：The ancient agreement granted the villagers firebote rights on the lord's land.  古代协议赋予村民在领主土地上获取燃料木材的权利。|近义词：fuelwood, firewood, estovers |反义词：N/A
