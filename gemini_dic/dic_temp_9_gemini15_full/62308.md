endeared:/ɪnˈdɪrd/| v. adj.|使受喜爱；可爱的；|To cause to be loved;  to make dear or beloved. The verb form describes the action of making someone loved or cherished. The adjective form describes someone or something that is loved or cherished.|例句：Her kindness endeared her to everyone.  她的善良使她受到大家的喜爱。
The child's playful antics endeared him to his aunt.  孩子的顽皮举动让他深受姑姑喜爱。|近义词：beloved, cherished, adored, loved|反义词：disliked, hated, despised|形容词
