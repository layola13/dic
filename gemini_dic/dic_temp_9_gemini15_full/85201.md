harim:/ˈhɑːrɪm/| n. |妻妾，后宫；（尤指穆斯林统治者或富人的）妻妾住所|The word "harim" refers to the women's quarters in a Muslim household, typically including the wives, concubines, and female relatives of the head of the household, often secluded from the outside world.  It can also refer to the women themselves.
