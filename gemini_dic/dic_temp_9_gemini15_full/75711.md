full-cream: /fʊlˈkriːm/ | adj. | 全脂的，全脂牛奶 |  Describes milk or dairy products that contain all the original fat content, unlike skimmed or semi-skimmed milk.  | Example: I prefer full-cream milk in my coffee. 我喜欢在我的咖啡里加全脂牛奶。 | 近义词：whole milk, rich | 反义词：skimmed milk, semi-skimmed milk, low-fat
