heists:/haɪsts/| n.|抢劫；盗窃；|The act of stealing, especially a robbery of a large amount of money or valuables.  It often involves planning and skill.|例句：The police are investigating a series of daring heists across the city.  警方正在调查该市发生的一系列大胆抢劫案。

例句：The museum heist made headlines worldwide. 博物馆的抢劫案占据了全球的头条新闻。

近义词：robberies, burglaries, thefts|反义词：donations, contributions|词性:名词
