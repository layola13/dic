placet:/ˈpleɪs/| n.|场所，地点；位置；职位 |A place is a particular position or area.  It can refer to a location, a position in society or a job. |例句：This is a beautiful place to live. 这是一个美丽的生活场所。

例句：He holds a high place in the company. 他在公司担任要职。

近义词：location, spot, site, position

反义词：nowhere


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is a nice place to relax. = 这是一个放松的好地方。
Where is the best place to eat around here? = 附近哪里是最好的就餐场所？


一般过去时:
We found a quiet place to rest. = 我们找到一个安静的地方休息。
They lived in a small place in the countryside. = 他们住在乡下的一处小地方。


现在完成时:
I have found a new place to work. = 我找到了一份新工作。
She has visited many places in Europe. = 她游览了欧洲许多地方。


过去完成时:
We had already left the place before the storm arrived. = 在暴风雨来临之前，我们已经离开了那个地方。
They had never been to such a beautiful place before. = 他们以前从未去过如此美丽的地方。


将来时:
We will find a better place to live next year. = 明年我们会找到一个更好的居住场所。
They will visit many interesting places during their trip. = 在他们的旅行中，他们将会参观许多有趣的地方。


虚拟语气:
If I had a place of my own, I would be much happier. = 如果我有自己的住所，我会快乐得多。
I wish I could find a peaceful place to escape to. = 我希望我能找到一个宁静的地方来逃避。


被动语态:
The place was cleaned thoroughly. = 这个地方被彻底打扫干净了。
The new office is being prepared for the employees to occupy. = 正在为员工准备新的办公室。


疑问句:
Where is the place? = 地方在哪里？
What kind of place is this? = 这是什么样的场所？


否定句:
There is no place like home. = 没有什么地方比得上家。
I couldn't find a parking place anywhere. = 我到处都找不到停车位。
