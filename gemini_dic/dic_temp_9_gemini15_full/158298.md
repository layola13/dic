playful:/ˈpleɪfəl/| adj.|嬉戏的；顽皮的；爱开玩笑的|Characterized by playfulness; lighthearted and amusing.  It suggests a lighthearted and enjoyable quality, often associated with children or young animals, but can also describe the behavior of adults.|例句：The playful puppy chased its tail endlessly.  这只顽皮的小狗没完没了地追着尾巴跑。

例句：He gave a playful nudge to his friend. 他轻轻地推了推他的朋友，带着开玩笑的意思。


近义词：mischievous, frolicsome, sportive, merry, jocose|反义词：serious, somber, grave, solemn, glum


词源:  The word "playful" originates from the Old English word "plegan," meaning "to play."  Over time, the suffix "-ful" was added, creating "playful," which retains the sense of engaging in play or having the characteristics of play.
