lived:/lɪvd/ | v. |居住；生活；经历|Past tense and past participle of "live."  It describes the state of residing in a place or the experience of a life. |例句：They lived in a small village. 他们住在一个小村庄里。 They lived a happy life together. 他们一起过着幸福的生活。|近义词：resided, dwelt, existed|反义词：died


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I live in London. = 我住在伦敦。
She lives a simple life. = 她过着简朴的生活。

一般过去时:
We lived in Paris for five years. = 我们在巴黎住了五年。
He lived a reckless life in his youth. = 他年轻时过着放荡不羁的生活。

现在完成时:
I have lived here all my life. = 我住在这里一辈子了。
They have lived through many hardships. = 他们经历了许多苦难。

过去完成时:
Before moving to New York, she had lived in California. = 在搬到纽约之前，她住在加利福尼亚。
He had lived in poverty before he became successful. = 在他成功之前，他过着贫困的生活。

将来时:
I will live in a bigger house next year. = 明年我将住在一所更大的房子里。
They will live to regret their decision. = 他们将为他们的决定后悔。

虚拟语气:
If I lived closer, I would visit more often. = 如果我住得近一些，我会经常去拜访。
I wish I lived near the beach. = 我真希望住在海边附近。

被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice, but it's possible in specific contexts):
The house had been lived in for generations. = 这房子已经住了几代人了。  (This implies the house was inhabited, not that someone was *being lived*)


疑问句:
Where did you live before? = 你以前住在哪里？
How long have you lived here? = 你住在这里多久了？

否定句:
They did not live happily ever after. = 他们并没有从此过上幸福的生活。
I have never lived abroad. = 我从未在国外生活过。


PHRASE->
lived experience = 亲身经历
lived reality = 真实生活
lived example = 活生生的例子
lived-in look = 住过的样子（指家具等）
lived-in space = 居住空间
