delinting: /dɪˈlɪntɪŋ/ | v. | 除去棉籽壳；去籽；去除杂质 | The process of removing impurities, especially seeds or hulls, from a substance, typically cotton.  | Example: The factory worker spent hours delinting the cotton before spinning it into thread.  工厂工人花了数小时去除棉花的杂质，然后将其纺成线。 | 近义词：cleaning, purifying, ginning | 反义词：contaminating, polluting
