fibrillary:/fɪˈbrɪləri/| adj.|纤维状的，细丝状的| Relating to or resembling fibrils; composed of or resembling fine fibers.  The term often describes the microscopic structure of tissues or substances.|例句：The biopsy revealed a fibrillary architecture in the tumor.  活检显示肿瘤具有纤维状结构。
例句：The protein formed fibrillary aggregates under certain conditions. 在特定条件下，蛋白质形成了纤维状聚集体。|近义词：fibrous, filamentous, threadlike |反义词：amorphous, structureless
