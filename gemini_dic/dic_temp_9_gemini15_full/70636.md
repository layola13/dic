filterers:/ˈfɪltərərz/| n.|过滤者，筛选器|Those who or that which filters.  People or things that separate or remove unwanted substances or elements.|例句：The kidneys act as efficient filterers of waste products from the blood. 肾脏有效地过滤血液中的废物。

例句：Many water treatment plants use sand filterers to remove impurities. 许多水处理厂使用砂滤器去除杂质。
