photolithography:/ˌfoʊtoʊlɪˈθɑːɡrəfi/ | n. | 光刻术 | The process of creating patterns on a surface using light-sensitive materials and photomasks.  It's a crucial technique in microfabrication, especially in the semiconductor industry for creating integrated circuits.  | Example: Photolithography is used to create the intricate patterns on microchips. 光刻技术用于在微芯片上创建复杂的图案。 | 近义词: microlithography, optical lithography | 反义词: N/A
