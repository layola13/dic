enthused:/ɪnˈθuːzd/| adj.| 热情的；热衷的 | feeling or showing great enthusiasm.  |例句：She was enthused about the new project. 她对这个新项目充满热情。

例句：The children were enthused by the magician's tricks. 孩子们对魔术师的戏法很着迷。


近义词：excited, enthusiastic, passionate, fervent | 反义词：apathetic, unenthusiastic, indifferent


词源:  The word "enthused" originates from the verb "enthuse," which itself comes from the noun "enthusiasm."  "Enthusiasm" comes from the Greek word "enthousiasmos," meaning "inspiration, divine possession." The prefix "en-" intensifies the meaning, while "-thuse" is connected to the root of "enthusiasm."


用法说明:

enthused 主要用作形容词，描述人或其状态，表示对某事或某物充满热情、兴奋。它通常用于非正式场合。


例句:

He was so enthused about his new job. 他对他的新工作非常热情。

She gave an enthused presentation on her research. 她对她的研究作了一个充满热情的陈述。


注意：虽然 "enthuse" 作为动词存在（表示“使…热情；使…兴奋”），但 "enthused" 作为其过去分词形式用作形容词更为常见。  直接用作动词的情况较少见。
