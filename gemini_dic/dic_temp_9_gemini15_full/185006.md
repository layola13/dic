retentivity:/ˌrɛtənˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 保持力；记忆力；滞留性 | The ability of a material to retain its magnetism after the magnetizing force has been removed.  It also refers to the ability to remember things.  |例句：The retentivity of this magnet is excellent.  这个磁铁的保持力极佳。

The high retentivity of the student impressed the teacher. 这个学生的记忆力给老师留下了深刻印象。|近义词：memory, magnetism retention, persistence |反义词：forgetfulness, impermanence
