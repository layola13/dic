concatenation:/ˌkɒŋˌkætɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |连接，串联|The act of linking things together in a series or chain.  It often refers to the joining of strings of characters or data in computer science.  |例句：The concatenation of the two strings resulted in a longer string.  两个字符串的连接产生了一个更长的字符串。

例句：The concatenation of several railway lines created a vast network.  几条铁路的连接创造了一个庞大的网络。


近义词：linking, joining, combination, union|反义词：separation, disjunction, severance|词性：名词


词源：源自拉丁语 *con- (together) + *catenāre (to chain).  字面意思为"链状连接"。
