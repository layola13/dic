overexpectant: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈspɛktənt/ | adj. | 过度期望的，期望过高的 |  Having or showing excessively high expectations. This word is relatively new and not found in older dictionaries, it's a combination of "over" and "expectant".  It describes someone who anticipates more than is realistic or likely.  | Example:  The overexpectant parent pushed their child too hard, leading to burnout.  过度期望的父母逼迫孩子太紧，导致孩子精疲力尽。  | Near synonyms: unrealistic, overly optimistic, demanding, high-expecting | Antonyms: undemanding, realistic, underestimating, complacent | 词性:形容词
