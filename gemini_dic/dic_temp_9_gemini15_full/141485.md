USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your opinions on the matter? = 你对这件事有什么看法？
His opinions are often controversial. = 他的观点常常引起争议。


一般过去时:
What were your opinions before the meeting? = 会议之前你的观点是什么？
Her opinions changed after she heard the evidence. = 在她听到证据后，她的观点改变了。


现在完成时:
Have your opinions on this changed recently? = 你最近对这件事的看法改变了吗？
People have expressed strong opinions on social media. = 人们在社交媒体上表达了强烈的观点。


过去完成时:
I had already formed my opinions before he started speaking. = 在他开始说话之前我已经形成了自己的观点。
They had held opposing opinions for years. = 他们多年来一直持有对立的观点。


将来时:
What will your opinions be after you’ve considered it further? = 在你进一步考虑之后你的观点将会是什么？
I believe his opinions will change after he learns more. = 我相信在他了解更多之后他的观点会改变。


虚拟语气:
If I knew his opinions, I would act differently. = 如果我知道他的观点，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I knew their opinions on the proposal. = 我希望我知道他们对这个提议的看法。


被动语态:
His opinions were widely reported in the media. = 他的观点在媒体上被广泛报道。
The opinions of the minority were ignored. = 少数人的意见被忽视了。


疑问句:
What are the opinions of the experts? = 专家们的意见是什么？
What are your opinions on the new policy? = 你对这项新政策有什么看法？


否定句:
I don't have strong opinions on that topic. = 我对那个话题没有强烈的观点。
They didn't share my opinions. = 他们不认同我的观点。


PHRASE->
in my opinion = 依我看
have an opinion = 有自己的观点
change one's opinion = 改变某人的观点
express an opinion = 表达意见
public opinion = 公众舆论
