self-likings:/selfˈlaɪkiŋz/| n. |自恋；自我欣赏；喜好 |Self-likings refer to one's positive feelings and attitudes towards oneself, including one's traits, abilities, and accomplishments.  It encompasses self-esteem, self-acceptance, and a general sense of self-worth.  However, excessive self-likings can manifest as narcissism or self-obsession. |例句：Her self-likings were evident in her constant boasting.  她的自恋在她不断的吹嘘中显而易见。

例句：He cultivated his self-likings through positive self-talk and achievements. 他通过积极的自我对话和成就来培养他的自我欣赏。


近义词：self-esteem, self-regard, self-love, narcissism (in extreme cases) |反义词：self-loathing, self-deprecation, low self-esteem |词性：名词
