formulating:/ˈfɔːrmjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 制定；构想；表达 | The act of creating or developing something, especially a plan, system, or theory.  It involves careful consideration and organization of ideas or components. | 例句：The team is formulating a new marketing strategy. = 团队正在制定新的营销策略。

例句：She is formulating her thoughts into a coherent essay. = 她正在把自己的想法整理成一篇连贯的论文。


近义词：developing, devising, creating, shaping, expressing


反义词：dismantling, destroying, neglecting, ignoring


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are formulating a response to the crisis. = 他们正在制定应对危机的方案。
The scientist is formulating a new hypothesis. = 科学家正在制定一个新的假设。

一般过去时:
The committee formulated a set of recommendations. = 委员会制定了一套建议。
She formulated her argument carefully before presenting it. = 她仔细地构思了自己的论点后才提出。

现在完成时:
They have formulated a plan to address the issue. = 他们已经制定了一个解决这个问题的计划。
We have formulated a new approach to teaching. = 我们已经制定了一种新的教学方法。

过去完成时:
They had formulated their strategy before the negotiations began. = 谈判开始前，他们就已经制定好了策略。
She had formulated her response before he even finished speaking. = 他还没说完话，她就已构思好了回应。

将来时:
They will formulate a new policy next year. = 他们明年将制定一项新政策。
The government will formulate its response to the report soon. = 政府很快就会制定对这份报告的回应。

虚拟语气:
If they formulated a better plan, they might succeed. = 如果他们制定一个更好的计划，他们可能会成功。
I wish they had formulated a more effective strategy. = 我希望他们当时制定了一个更有效的策略。

被动语态:
A new policy is being formulated by the government. = 政府正在制定一项新政策。
The plan was formulated by a team of experts. = 该计划是由专家团队制定的。

疑问句:
Are they formulating a new product line? = 他们正在制定新的产品线吗？
Has the company formulated a response to the complaint? = 公司对投诉做出了回应了吗？

否定句:
They are not formulating a new strategy at this time. = 他们目前没有制定新的策略。
The committee did not formulate any concrete recommendations. = 委员会没有提出任何具体的建议。


PHRASE->
formulate a plan 制定计划
formulate a theory 提出理论
formulate a response 拟定回应
formulate a question 提出问题
formulate a strategy 制定战略

词源:
From Latin *formulare ("to shape, mold"), from forma ("shape, form").  The meaning evolved to include the creation of something based on a specific form or structure.
