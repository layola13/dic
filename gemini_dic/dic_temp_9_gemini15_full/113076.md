lunacies:/ˈluːnəsiz/ | n. |疯狂行为；愚蠢行为；荒诞想法 |The plural of lunacy, referring to instances of madness, foolishness, or absurdity. It implies a series of irrational or senseless actions or ideas. |例句：His recent business decisions are nothing short of lunacies.  他最近的商业决策简直就是疯了。

例句：The government's policies seem like a series of lunacies. 政府的政策似乎是一系列的荒唐行为。


近义词：follies, absurdities, insanities, extravagances, craziness

反义词：sane actions, rational behavior, wisdom, sensibleness


词源：源自于古法语 *lunatique (adj.)  "lunatic,"  来自拉丁语 *lunaticus,  from luna "moon,"  基于迷信的观念认为月球会影响人的精神状态。
