chutney:/ˈtʃʌtni/| n. |酸辣酱；辣酱；调味酱 |A chutney is a sweet, sour, and spicy condiment, typically made from fruits, vegetables, and spices.  It is a common ingredient in Indian cuisine. |例句：The mango chutney added a delicious sweetness to the curry.  芒果酸辣酱为咖喱增添了美味的甜味。

例句：She served the chicken tikka masala with a side of mint chutney. 她用薄荷酸辣酱搭配鸡肉丁咖喱。


近义词：relish, sauce, condiment |反义词：None (it's a type of food, not easily paired with an antonym)|词性：名词
