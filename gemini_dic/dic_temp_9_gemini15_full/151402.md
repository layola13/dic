parmesan:/ˌpɑːrməˈzæn/|/ˈpɑːrmɪˈzɑːn/| n. |帕尔马干酪，帕玛森干酪 |A hard, granular, dry Italian cheese, typically made from cow's milk. It has a sharp, salty, and slightly nutty flavor.  Parmesan cheese is often grated and used as a topping for pasta dishes, salads, and other foods. |The pasta dish was topped with a generous sprinkling of parmesan. 这份意大利面撒上了大量的帕尔马干酪。

The pizza was delicious, especially with the added parmesan. 这披萨真好吃，尤其加了帕尔马干酪之后。|Asiago, Pecorino Romano|  |n.|
