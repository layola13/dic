scold:/skoʊld/|vt.|责骂，训斥|To rebuke or reprimand sharply.|例句：The teacher scolded the student for misbehaving. 老师因为学生行为不端而责骂了他。|例句：My mother scolded me for coming home late. 我妈妈因为我回家晚了而责骂我。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, chide, reprove|反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She scolds her children often. = 她经常责骂她的孩子。
He scolds his employees for being late. = 他责骂他的员工迟到。

一般过去时:
My father scolded me for breaking the vase. = 我父亲因为我打破了花瓶而责骂我。
The teacher scolded the class for their noisy behavior. = 老师因为班级的吵闹行为而责骂了全班。

现在完成时:
She has scolded him several times this week. = 她本周已经责骂他好几次了。
I have never been scolded by my boss before. = 我以前从未被老板责骂过。

过去完成时:
He had scolded her before, so she was afraid to tell him the truth. = 他以前曾责骂过她，所以她不敢告诉他真相。
The manager had scolded the team for their poor performance before the meeting. = 在会议之前，经理已经因为团队表现不佳而责骂了他们。

将来时:
I think she will scold him if he does it again. = 我认为如果他再这样做，她会责骂他。
The principal will scold the students if they continue to disrupt the class. = 如果学生继续扰乱课堂秩序，校长会责骂他们。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't scold him in front of others. = 如果我是你，我不会当着别人的面责骂他。
I wish I hadn't scolded her so harshly. = 我真希望我没有那么严厉地责骂她。

被动语态:
He was scolded by his teacher for cheating. = 他因为作弊而被老师责骂。
The children were scolded for fighting in the playground. = 孩子们因为在操场上打架而被责骂。

疑问句:
Will you scold him if he breaks the rules? = 如果他违反规章制度，你会责骂他吗？
Did your parents scold you when you were young? = 你小时候父母责骂过你吗？

否定句:
She didn't scold him, even though he deserved it. = 即使他活该，她也没有责骂他。
I will not scold you if you tell me the truth. = 如果你告诉我真相，我不会责骂你。


PHRASE->
scold someone severely = 严厉地责骂某人
scold someone roundly = 狠狠地责骂某人
a scolding look = 责备的眼神
give someone a scolding = 训斥某人
receive a scolding = 受到责骂
