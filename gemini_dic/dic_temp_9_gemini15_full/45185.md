cuisine:/kwɪˈziːn/| n. |烹饪；菜肴；菜系 |The art of preparing food; a style or method of cooking, especially as characteristic of a particular country or region.  |例句：French cuisine is known for its rich sauces and buttery flavors. 法国菜以其浓厚的酱汁和黄油味而闻名。|例句：The restaurant offers a diverse cuisine, ranging from Italian pasta to Thai curries. 这家餐厅提供各种各样的菜肴，从意大利面食到泰国咖喱应有尽有。|近义词：cookery, cooking, gastronomy |反义词：None
