roll-out:/ˈroʊlˌaʊt/|/ˈrəʊlˌaʊt/| n. vt.|推出，展开，公布|The act of making something new available or starting something new;  a public presentation or launch of a new product, service, or plan.|例句：The company is planning a major roll-out of its new software next month. 公司计划下个月推出其新软件。

例句：The roll-out of the new marketing campaign was very successful. 新营销活动的推出非常成功。


近义词：launch, introduction, unveiling, release, debut|反义词：withdrawal, recall, cancellation|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company rolls out a new product every year. = 公司每年都会推出一个新产品。
They roll out the red carpet for VIP guests. = 他们为贵宾铺上红地毯。


一般过去时:
Apple rolled out the iPhone 14 last year. = 苹果去年推出了iPhone 14。
The government rolled out a new economic policy. = 政府推出了一项新的经济政策。


现在完成时:
The company has rolled out three new products this year. = 公司今年已经推出了三款新产品。
They have rolled out the welcome mat for new employees. = 他们为新员工铺上了欢迎垫。


过去完成时:
They had rolled out the plan before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经推出了计划。
The team had rolled out the marketing campaign successfully. = 团队已经成功地推出了营销活动。


将来时:
We will roll out the updated software next week. = 我们下周将推出更新的软件。
They will roll out a new training program for employees. = 他们将为员工推出一个新的培训项目。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would roll out the project earlier. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们就会更早推出这个项目。
I wish they would roll out the new feature soon. = 我希望他们能尽快推出这个新功能。


被动语态:
The new software will be rolled out next month. = 新软件将于下个月推出。
The plan was rolled out without any problems. = 计划顺利推出。


疑问句:
When will the new product be rolled out? = 新产品什么时候推出？
Has the marketing campaign been rolled out yet? = 营销活动已经推出吗？


否定句:
They didn't roll out the update on time. = 他们没有按时推出更新。
The project hasn't been rolled out yet. = 项目尚未推出。


PHRASE->
roll out the red carpet  铺上红地毯 (迎接贵宾)
roll out a plan 制定计划
roll out a product 推出产品
roll out a campaign 展开活动
roll out the welcome mat 欢迎
