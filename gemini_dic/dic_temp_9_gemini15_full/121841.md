misrendered:/ˌmɪsˈrɛndərd/| adj.| 歪曲的；曲解的；翻译错误的 |  Incorrectly represented or interpreted; wrongly translated.  | 例句：The passage was misrendered in the translation, losing its original meaning. 这段文字在翻译中被曲解了，失去了其本来的含义。

The historical context was misrendered in the summary. 摘要中对历史背景的阐述是错误的。| 近义词：misrepresented, misinterpreted, mistranslated, distorted | 反义词：accurately rendered, correctly interpreted, faithfully translated |


词源:

* **mis-**:  A prefix meaning "badly," "wrongly," or "ill."
* **render**: To present, depict, translate, or interpret something.  It originates from Old French *rendre*, meaning "to return, give back, surrender," ultimately from Latin *reddere*.


用法说明:

misrendered 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，例如文本、图像、信息、翻译等，表示这些东西被错误地呈现、解释或翻译。  它强调的是错误的呈现方式导致了理解上的偏差。


例句：

* The artist's intention was misrendered by the critic's interpretation. 艺术家的意图被评论家的解读曲解了。
* The map misrendered the geographical features of the region. 这张地图歪曲了该地区的地形特征。
* The software misrendered the 3D model, resulting in distorted images. 软件错误地渲染了3D模型，导致图像变形。


近义词和反义词已经在上面列出。  由于misrendered本身就包含了错误的含义，所以不需要详细展开各种时态的用法说明（如一般现在时、过去时等），因为这些时态的运用取决于句子整体的时态。
