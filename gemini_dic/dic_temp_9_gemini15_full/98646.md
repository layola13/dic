insue:/ɪnˈsuː/| v.| 随后发生，继起，发生|To happen or occur as a result or consequence.  It often implies an undesirable or unwelcome consequence.|例句：After the argument, a tense silence ensued. 争吵过后，一种紧张的沉默随之而来。|例句：Problems ensued from their failure to plan properly.  他们的计划不周导致了一系列问题。|近义词：follow, result, succeed, come about|反义词：precede, anticipate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A tense silence ensues after the argument. = 争吵后，紧张的沉默随之而来。
Trouble ensues when people ignore warnings. = 当人们忽视警告时，麻烦就会接踵而至。


一般过去时:
A period of uncertainty ensued after the election. = 选举之后，一段不确定的时期随之而来。
Confusion ensued when the instructions were unclear. = 指示不明确导致了混乱。


现在完成时:
Much debate has ensued since the announcement of the new policy. = 自从宣布新政策以来，已经进行了大量的辩论。
Significant changes have ensued in the industry. = 该行业发生了重大变化。


过去完成时:
After the storm had passed, a period of calm ensued. = 暴风雨过去后，平静时期随之而来。
Once the initial excitement had subsided, disappointment ensued. = 一旦最初的兴奋消退，失望就随之而来。


将来时:
Significant challenges will ensue if the project is not properly funded. = 如果项目没有得到适当的资金支持，将会出现重大的挑战。
Further complications will ensue if we don't address this issue immediately. = 如果我们不立即解决这个问题，将会出现进一步的复杂情况。


虚拟语气:
If they continued their reckless behavior, disaster would ensue. = 如果他们继续鲁莽的行为，灾难就会随之而来。
Were the dam to break, widespread flooding would ensue. = 如果大坝决堤，就会发生大范围的洪水。


被动语态:
A state of emergency was ensued after the earthquake. = 地震后宣布进入紧急状态。(此用法不常见，更常用的表达是：A state of emergency was declared after the earthquake.)
Further investigation was ensued by the police. (此用法不常见，更常用的表达是：Further investigation was conducted by the police.)


疑问句:
What will ensue if we fail to meet our deadline? = 如果我们没有按时完成，将会发生什么？
What ensued after the meeting concluded? = 会议结束后发生了什么？


否定句:
No problems ensued from the changes. = 这些变化没有带来任何问题。
No negative consequences ensued from the decision. = 这个决定没有带来任何负面后果。


PHRASE->
ensue from = 源于…
ensue upon = 紧随…之后
ensue in = 在…中发生
ensue with = 以…而告终
ensue after = 在…之后发生
