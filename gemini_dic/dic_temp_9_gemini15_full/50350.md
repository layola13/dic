depolish:/diːˈpɒlɪʃ/ | vt. | 去光, 磨光 | To remove the polish from a surface; to make less shiny or smooth.  |例句：He depolished the surface of the wood to give it a more rustic look. 他把木头的表面磨去了光泽，使其看起来更粗犷。
例句：The constant friction depolished the brass fittings.  不断的摩擦使黄铜配件失去了光泽。|近义词：mat, dull, roughen |反义词：polish, shine, gloss
