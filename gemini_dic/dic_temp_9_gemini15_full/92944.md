ideogeny:/aɪˈdʒɛnədʒi/ | n. |思想起源；观念发生；思想的产生|The study of the origin and development of ideas, especially in relation to their cultural and social context.  It explores how ideas are formed, transmitted, and transformed within societies.|例句：The ideogeny of the concept of democracy is a complex and fascinating topic.  民主概念的思想起源是一个复杂而引人入胜的话题。  |近义词：genesis, origin, development |反义词：destruction, annihilation|名词
