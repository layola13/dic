clastic:/ˈklæstɪk/| adj.|碎屑的；由碎屑构成的| Composed of fragments of pre-existing rocks or minerals.  Clastic rocks are formed from the accumulation and cementation of these fragments.  Examples include sandstone, shale, and conglomerate.| The sandstone is a clastic sedimentary rock. 这砂岩是一种碎屑沉积岩。|  fragmental, detrital | crystalline, massive
