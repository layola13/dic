diarrhoea/diarrhea: /ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ /ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ | n. | 腹泻 | Loose, watery stools.  This is a medical condition characterized by frequent, loose, or watery bowel movements.  The condition can be caused by various factors, including infections, food intolerances, medications, and certain medical conditions. |  He suffered from diarrhoea after eating contaminated food. 他吃了受污染的食物后患上了腹泻。

The child had severe diarrhoea and needed medical attention.  孩子腹泻严重，需要就医。| constipation |  constipation | 名词

词源:

The word "diarrhoea" (British English spelling) and "diarrhea" (American English spelling) both originate from the Greek words "dia" (through) and "rhein" (to flow).  Therefore, the word literally means "flowing through."
