self-administers:/ˌsɛlf ədˈmɪnɪstərz/ | v. |自行管理；自我管理 | To manage or govern oneself; to administer something to oneself.  |例句：The patient self-administers the medication as directed by the doctor. 患者按照医嘱自行服用药物。

例句：The study group self-administers its own meetings and assignments. 学习小组自行管理会议和作业。


USAGE->

He self-administers his medication daily. = 他每天自行服用药物。
She self-administers her time effectively. = 她有效地自我管理时间。
