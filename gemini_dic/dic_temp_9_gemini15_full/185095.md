retieing: /ˌriːˈtaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 重新捆扎；重新系紧 | To tie again; to fasten or secure again.  |例句：He spent the afternoon retying the loose ropes on the boat. 他下午花了时间重新捆绑船上松动的绳索。

例句：After the storm, we had to retie all the packages.暴风雨过后，我们不得不重新捆绑所有包裹。


近义词：refasten, rebind, secure


反义词：untie, loosen


词性：动词
