dissolver:/dɪˈzɒlvər/ | n. |溶解器；解散者 | A device or substance that dissolves something.  Someone or something that causes something to dissolve or disintegrate. |例句：The chemical is a powerful dissolver of organic matter.  这种化学物质是强大的有机物溶解剂。

The factory is a major dissolver of local employment. 这家工厂是当地就业的主要破坏者。|近义词：solvent, disintegrator, liquidator |反义词：solidifier, aggregator
