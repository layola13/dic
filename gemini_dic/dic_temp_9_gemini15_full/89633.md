hookahs:/huˈkɑːz/| n. |水烟，水烟筒 |A hookah, also known as a shisha, is a single- or multi-stemmed instrument for smoking flavored tobacco, sometimes with added fruits or other flavorings.  The smoke is passed through a water basin before inhalation.|例句：Many cafes in the Middle East offer hookahs to their patrons.  许多中东的咖啡馆向顾客提供水烟。

The hookah is a traditional smoking device.|水烟是一种传统的吸烟工具。


近义词：shisha, narghile, hubble-bubble|反义词：无


词源:The word "hookah" is derived from the Hindi word "huqqa," ultimately from the Persian word "hūqqa."  These words refer to the water pipe itself. The word's spread reflects the geographical range of hookah use, originating in the Middle East and South Asia and spreading globally.
