imitated:/ˈɪmɪteɪtɪd/| v.|模仿的，仿效的|To copy someone's behavior or speech, or to make something look, feel, or behave like something else.|例句：He imitated the teacher's accent perfectly. 他完美地模仿了老师的口音。|例句：The fake handbag imitated the real one so well that it was hard to tell the difference.  这个假包模仿真包得如此之好，很难分辨出区别。|近义词：copied, mimicked, duplicated|反义词：originated, innovated


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He imitates his favorite singer. = 他模仿他最喜欢的歌手。
She imitates my mannerisms. = 她模仿我的举止。

一般过去时:
The child imitated the dog's barking. = 孩子模仿狗的叫声。
They imitated the style of the famous painter. = 他们模仿了那位著名画家的风格。

现在完成时:
She has imitated many different artists throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她模仿过许多不同的艺术家。
He has imitated that accent for years. = 他模仿那个口音已经很多年了。

过去完成时:
Before the performance, he had imitated the famous actor’s style for weeks. = 演出前，他模仿那位著名演员的风格已经好几个星期了。
They had imitated the design before realizing it was patented. = 他们在意识到该设计已获得专利之前就进行了模仿。


将来时:
She will imitate the dance moves she saw on TV. = 她将模仿她在电视上看到的舞蹈动作。
He will imitate the sound of a bird. = 他将模仿鸟叫声。

虚拟语气:
If I could imitate her singing voice, I would. = 如果我能模仿她的歌声，我会的。
I wish I could imitate his confidence. = 我希望我能模仿他的自信。

被动语态:
His style was imitated by many artists. = 他的风格被许多艺术家模仿。
The design was imitated by several companies. = 该设计被几家公司模仿。

疑问句:
Did he imitate the famous musician? = 他模仿了那位著名的音乐家吗？
Does she imitate anyone in particular? = 她模仿了哪位特定的人吗？

否定句:
He didn't imitate anyone; his style is original. = 他没有模仿任何人；他的风格是原创的。
She doesn't imitate celebrities. = 她不模仿名人。
