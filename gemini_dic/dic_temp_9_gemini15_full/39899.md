constitutionally:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəli/|/ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəli/| adj.|根据宪法的；本质上地；体质上地 |According to or in a manner consistent with a constitution.  The word implies a connection to fundamental laws or principles, often those of a nation or organization.|


例句：

The court ruled the law unconstitutional. = 法院裁定该法律违宪。

The government acted constitutionally in its response to the crisis. = 政府在应对危机时采取了符合宪法的行动。


近义词：legally, legitimately, rightfully


反义词：illegally, unconstitutionally, unlawfully


词性：形容词
