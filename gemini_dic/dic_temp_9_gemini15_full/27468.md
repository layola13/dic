campaigning:/kæmˈpeɪnɪŋ/| v.|参加竞选；开展运动；宣传 |The act of working to achieve a particular goal, usually involving organized effort and public engagement.  It often involves advocating for a cause, a candidate, or a product.|例句：She's campaigning for a better education system. 她正在为改善教育体制而奔走。|例句：The company is campaigning to raise awareness of its new product. 这家公司正在开展运动以提高其新产品的知名度。|近义词：agitating, advocating, promoting |反义词：opposing, resisting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are campaigning for environmental protection. = 他们在为环境保护而奔走。
He campaigns against poverty every year. = 他每年都从事反贫困运动。

一般过去时:
We campaigned for animal rights last year. = 我们去年为动物权利奔走。
She campaigned tirelessly for social justice. = 她为社会正义不知疲倦地奔走。

现在完成时:
They have been campaigning for years to improve public transportation. = 他们多年来一直在为改善公共交通而奔走。
She has campaigned extensively for women's rights. = 她为妇女权利进行了广泛的宣传。

过去完成时:
They had campaigned for months before the election. = 他们在选举前已经奔走了几个月。
She had campaigned vigorously before the referendum. = 在全民公决前，她进行了强有力的宣传。

将来时:
We will be campaigning for a new library next year. = 我们明年将为新建图书馆而奔走。
He will campaign for the presidency in the upcoming election. = 他将在即将到来的选举中竞选总统。

虚拟语气:
If they campaigned harder, they might win. = 如果他们更努力地奔走，他们可能会获胜。
I wish she had campaigned more effectively. = 我希望她能更有效地开展宣传。

被动语态:
The issue was campaigned for by many groups. = 许多团体都为这个问题奔走。
The new policy is being campaigned against by several organizations. = 几家机构正在反对这项新政策。

疑问句:
Are they campaigning for a specific candidate? = 他们在为某个特定候选人奔走吗？
What are you campaigning for? = 你在为哪个事业奔走？

否定句:
He is not campaigning for reelection. = 他没有竞选连任。
They did not campaign aggressively enough. = 他们的宣传不够积极。


PHRASE->
campaign for = 为……而奔走
campaign against = 反对……而奔走
election campaign = 选举运动
marketing campaign = 营销活动
public awareness campaign = 公众意识宣传活动
