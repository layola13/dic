galliform:/ˈɡælɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 鸡形目的；鸡形类的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Galliformes, an order of birds that includes chickens, turkeys, pheasants, quails, and grouse.  These birds are typically ground-dwelling, have strong legs and feet, and are often sexually dimorphic (males and females look different). | Example:  Many galliform birds exhibit elaborate courtship displays.  许多鸡形目鸟类都表现出复杂的求偶行为。|近义词：fowl-like (in some contexts) |反义词：无
