kickboxer:/ˈkɪkbɒksər/|/ˈkɪkbɑːksər/| n. |踢拳运动员|A person who practices or competes in kickboxing.  Kickboxing is a combat sport that uses a combination of punches and kicks.|He is a highly skilled kickboxer with many championship titles. 他是一位拥有许多冠军头衔的技艺高超的踢拳运动员。|She's training to become a professional kickboxer.  她正在训练成为一名职业踢拳运动员。|martial artist, fighter|—|
