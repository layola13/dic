precompute:/ˌpriːkəmˈpjuːt/ | vt. |预计算，预先计算 | To compute something in advance.  This often involves calculating values that will be needed later and storing them for efficient retrieval.  Precomputation can significantly improve the performance of an algorithm by avoiding redundant calculations. | 例句：The program precomputes the trigonometric functions to speed up rendering. = 程序预先计算三角函数以加快渲染速度。

The game precomputes collision detection data to make gameplay smoother. = 游戏预先计算碰撞检测数据以使游戏运行更流畅。

| 近义词：precalculate, calculate beforehand | 反义词：compute on demand, calculate as needed | 动词
