discourses:/ˈdɪsˌkɔːrsɪz/ | n. |论述；谈话；演讲 |  Formal discussions of a topic, often in a serious and academic manner.  It can also refer to a connected series of utterances or writings. |例句：His discourses on philosophy were both insightful and engaging.  他的哲学论述既有见地又引人入胜。

例句：The professor's discourses covered a wide range of topics. 教授的演讲涵盖了广泛的主题。


近义词：discussions, speeches, lectures, conversations, treatises | 反义词：silence, muteness


词源:  From Old French *discours, from Latin discursus, past participle of discurrere "to run to and fro," from dis- "apart" + currere "to run."  The original meaning implied a running back and forth of ideas or a rambling discussion.
