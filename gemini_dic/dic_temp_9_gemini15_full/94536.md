impossible:/ɪmˈpɒsəbl/ /ɪmˈpɑːsəbl/ | adj. | 不可能的，难以实现的 |  Something that is impossible cannot be done or achieved. It describes something that is beyond the realm of possibility. | It's impossible to be in two places at once.  这不可能同时出现在两个地方。

The task proved impossible to complete within the deadline.  这项任务证明在截止日期前无法完成。


近义词：unachievable, unattainable, impracticable, inconceivable,  out of the question

反义词：possible, achievable, feasible, attainable

词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is impossible for me to finish this work today. = 我今天不可能完成这项工作。
This problem is impossible to solve. = 这个问题不可能解决。


一般过去时:
It was impossible to contact him yesterday. = 昨天联系他是不可能的。
The task was impossible to complete on time. = 这项任务不可能按时完成。


现在完成时:
It has been impossible to reach a consensus. = 一直无法达成共识。
It has been impossible to predict the outcome. = 一直无法预测结果。


过去完成时:
It had been impossible to predict the earthquake. =  预测地震是不可能的。
It had been impossible to warn the citizens in time. =  及时警告市民是不可能的。


将来时:
It will be impossible to reverse the damage. =  扭转损害将是不可能的。
It will be impossible to attend both meetings. =  参加两个会议都是不可能的。


虚拟语气:
It would be impossible to live without water. = 没有水是无法生存的。
It would have been impossible to succeed without your help. =  没有你的帮助，成功是不可能的。


被动语态:
The problem is considered impossible to solve. =  这个问题被认为是无法解决的。
The task was deemed impossible to accomplish. =  这项任务被认为是无法完成的。


疑问句:
Is it impossible to learn a new language in a month? =  一个月内学习一门外语是不可能的？
Is it impossible to get a refund? =  退款是不可能的？


否定句:
It is not impossible to achieve your goals. =  实现你的目标并非不可能。
It wasn't impossible to find a solution. =  找到解决方案并非不可能。


PHRASE->
an impossible task  一项不可能完成的任务
