mentions:/ˈmɛnʃən/| vt. |提及，说起|To speak of or refer to something or someone briefly or casually.|例句：He mentioned the party in passing. 他顺便提到了那个聚会。|例句：She mentioned that she was feeling unwell. 她提到她感觉不舒服。|近义词：refer to, allude to, cite |反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mentions the problem every meeting. 他每次会议都提到这个问题。
She mentions her trip to Paris frequently. 她经常提到她去巴黎的旅行。

一般过去时:
He mentioned his concerns to his boss. 他向老板提到了他的担忧。
She mentioned seeing a strange man near the house. 她提到在房子附近看到一个陌生男子。

现在完成时:
He has mentioned the issue several times. 他已经多次提到这个问题了。
She has mentioned that she's looking for a new job. 她提到过她正在找一份新工作。

过去完成时:
He had mentioned the deadline before the meeting. 他在会议前就提到了截止日期。
She had mentioned her intention to leave earlier that day.  那天早些时候她就提到了她想离开的意图。

将来时:
He will mention the project during the presentation. 他将在演示期间提及该项目。
She will mention the details later in the report. 她将在报告的后面部分提到细节。

虚拟语气:
I wish he had mentioned the risk beforehand. 我希望他事先提到过这个风险。
If she had mentioned the problem earlier, we could have avoided it. 如果她早点提到这个问题，我们就能避免它了。

被动语态:
The problem was mentioned in the report. 这个问题在报告中被提及。
The incident was mentioned briefly by the witness.  目击者简要地提到了这件事故。

疑问句:
Did he mention anything about the meeting? 他有没有提到关于会议的事情？
Has she mentioned when she'll be back? 她有没有提到她什么时候回来？

否定句:
He didn't mention anything about his plans. 他没有提到任何关于他的计划的事情。
She didn't mention seeing anyone suspicious. 她没有提到看到任何可疑的人。


PHRASE->
