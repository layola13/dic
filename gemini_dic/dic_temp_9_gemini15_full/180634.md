regorge:/rɪˈɡɔːrdʒ/ | vt.  |涌出；充满；反刍；重新大量获得|To surge or overflow; to fill abundantly; to vomit; to re-experience or recall vividly.  |例句：The river regorged its banks after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水漫过了河岸。

The volcano regorged molten rock. 火山喷出了熔岩。


近义词：overflow, surge, vomit, disgorge | 反义词：empty, drain, deplete

词源:
From Old French *re-gorger, from re- + gorger "to gorge, swallow," from Vulgar Latin *gurgutiāre, frequentative of *gurgutire "to gurgle," from Late Latin gurgulio "gullet."  The word ultimately derives from the imitative sound of gurgling.
