insomniac:/ˈɪnsɒmniæk/| n. |失眠症患者；经常失眠的人 |A person who has difficulty sleeping.  An insomniac often suffers from insomnia, a condition characterized by difficulty falling asleep, staying asleep, or both. |She's an insomniac and often stays up until dawn.|近义词：sleepless person, night owl|反义词：deep sleeper
