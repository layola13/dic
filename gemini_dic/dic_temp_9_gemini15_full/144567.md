outwandered:/ˌaʊtˈwɒndərd/| v. |超越…漫游，漫游得更远|To have wandered further than someone or something else.|The explorers outwandered all previous expeditions, reaching a previously uncharted territory. 探险家们比以往任何探险队都走得更远，到达了一个以前从未绘制地图的地区。|outwalk, outtravel|stay within bounds|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outwanders everyone in his group. 他比他组里的每个人都走得更远。
They outwander the other hikers every weekend. 他们每个周末都比其他远足者走得更远。


一般过去时:
The seasoned hiker outwandered the novice.  经验丰富的徒步旅行者比新手走得更远。
We outwandered our planned route yesterday. 我们昨天走了超出计划路线的路程。


现在完成时:
She has outwandered all her companions on this journey. 在这次旅途中，她已经走过了所有同伴。
They have outwandered their previous best distance. 他们已经超过了他们之前的最远距离。


过去完成时:
By the time we reached the summit, he had outwandered us by miles. 当我们到达山顶时，他已经比我们多走了几英里。
Before the storm hit, they had outwandered the safe zone. 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经走出了安全区。


将来时:
They will outwander the competition in the upcoming marathon. 他们将在即将到来的马拉松比赛中超过竞争对手。（这里指在比赛中跑得更远）

被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used passively because it implies an active comparison.)  这个动词很少用被动语态，因为它暗示着主动的比较。

否定句:

PHRASE->
