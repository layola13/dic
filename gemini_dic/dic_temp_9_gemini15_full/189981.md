sand:/sænd/| n.|沙子，沙滩|Fine particles of rock produced by the breakdown of larger rocks.  It is often found on beaches, deserts, and riverbeds. |例句：The children built a magnificent sandcastle on the beach.孩子们在沙滩上建了一座宏伟的沙堡。
例句：The desert stretched endlessly, covered in fine sand.沙漠一望无际，覆盖着细沙。|近义词：gravel, grit|反义词：rock, stone
