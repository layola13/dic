crevices:/ˈkrɛvɪsɪz/| n.|裂缝；缝隙 | Narrow openings or cracks, especially in rock or walls.  |例句：Water seeped into the crevices of the rock. 水渗入了岩石的裂缝。

例句：The climber found a handhold in a narrow crevice. 登山者在一个狭窄的裂缝中找到了一个可以抓握的地方。


近义词：crack, fissure, split, chink


反义词：gap (in the sense of a wide opening)


词性：名词


词源:  From Old French *crevisse ("crevice, chink"), from Medieval Latin *crevis ("crack").  The ultimate origin is likely from the Proto-Indo-European root *krep- ("to bend, to crook").
