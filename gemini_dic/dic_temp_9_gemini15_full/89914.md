词源:
From French *hors d'œuvre*, literally "outside the work" (i.e., outside the main course of the meal).  *Hors* means "outside" and *d'œuvre* is from Old French *œuvre*, meaning "work".  Therefore, it originally referred to dishes served outside the main course of a meal.
