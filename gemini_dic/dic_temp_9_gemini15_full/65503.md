europop:/ˈjʊərəʊpɒp/| n. |欧洲流行音乐|A style of pop music that originated in Europe, characterized by catchy melodies, danceable beats, and often incorporating elements of other genres like electronic music, synth-pop, and disco. It often features synthesized instruments and a focus on upbeat tempos and simple song structures.|Example:  Many artists have popularized europop around the world, making it a globally recognized music genre.  许多艺术家在世界各地推广欧洲流行音乐，使其成为一种全球知名的音乐类型。|近义词：dance-pop, synth-pop|反义词：无
