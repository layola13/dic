rendezvoused:/ˌrɒ̃deɪˈvuːzd/ /ˌrɑːndeɪˈvuːzd/ | v. |会合；约会；集合 |  Past tense and past participle of rendezvous.  It describes the act of meeting someone at an agreed time and place.  |例句：The two armies rendezvoused at dawn. = 两军在黎明时分会合。

The spies rendezvoused in a dark alley. = 间谍在一个黑暗的小巷里会合。


近义词：met, assembled, convened, gathered | 反义词：separated, parted, dispersed | 动词
