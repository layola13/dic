postexist:/ˌpoʊstɪˈzɪst/| adj.|后存在主义的；后存在论的|Post-existentialism refers to philosophical perspectives that build upon or critique existentialism, often focusing on aspects not fully addressed by earlier existentialist thinkers.  It explores themes of interconnectedness, technology's impact on existence, and the implications of a posthuman future.|例句：The novel grapples with postexistential themes of technological singularity and the blurring lines between human and machine.  这部小说探讨了技术奇点和人机界限模糊等后存在主义主题。|近义词：post-structuralist, postmodern|反义词：existentialist
