ichthyophthalmite:/ˌɪkθiˌɒfˈθælmaɪt/| n. |鱼眼石|A variety of apophyllite, a hydrated potassium calcium silicate, that is often found in cavities in basaltic rocks and is characterized by its pearly to vitreous luster and its typically white or colorless appearance.  It sometimes exhibits a greenish or yellowish tint. Its name derives from its resemblance to a fish's eye.|The ichthyophthalmite sample displayed a beautiful pearly luster.|近义词：apophyllite|反义词：无
