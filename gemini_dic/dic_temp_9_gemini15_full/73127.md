for their part:/fɔːr ðɛər pɑːrt/ | phrase |就他们而言；就其本身而言| This phrase indicates the perspective or action of a specific group or individual within a larger context. It emphasizes that the statement applies only to that particular group and doesn't necessarily reflect the views or actions of others involved. |例句：The government, for their part, promised to investigate the matter thoroughly.  政府方面承诺将彻底调查此事。

The protesters, for their part, remained peaceful throughout the demonstration.  示威者方面在整个示威活动中都保持了和平。


近义词：as for them, regarding them, in their case, from their perspective

反义词：无


词性：Phrase (短语)
