matinee:/ˌmætɪˈneɪ/| n. |午后演出，日场戏|A theatrical performance, movie, or other entertainment given in the afternoon.  It typically refers to a performance that begins in the afternoon, often around 2 pm or 3 pm.|例句：The matinee performance of Hamlet was sold out.  哈姆雷特的日场演出售罄了。|例句： We decided to attend the matinee showing of the new superhero film. 我们决定去看那部新超级英雄电影的日场。|近义词：afternoon performance, daytime show|反义词：evening performance, night show
