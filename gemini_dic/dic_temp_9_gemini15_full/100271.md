/ˈɪntərˌskoʊps/| n. |内窥镜；内部视野|An interscope is a device used to visually examine the interior of a body cavity or organ.  It typically consists of a thin, flexible tube with a light and camera at the end.|例句：The doctor used an interscope to examine the patient's colon. 医生用内窥镜检查了病人的结肠。|例句：The new interscope technology allows for clearer images and less invasive procedures. 新的内窥镜技术可以获得更清晰的图像，并且侵入性更小。|近义词：endoscope, laparoscope|反义词：无|名词
