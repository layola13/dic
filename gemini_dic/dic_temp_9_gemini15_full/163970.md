predicted:/prɪˈdɪktɪd/ | adj. | 预言的；预测的 |  Something that is predicted is said to be likely to happen in the future, based on information or knowledge that is currently available. It implies a degree of certainty, although not absolute.  | Example: The predicted outcome of the election was a close race.  选举预测的结果是一场势均力敌的比赛。 The weather forecast predicted heavy rain for tomorrow. 天气预报预测明天将有大雨。 |近义词：forecast, foretold, anticipated, foreseen | 反义词：unpredicted, unforeseen

虚拟语气:
