mutationally:/ˌmjuːˈteɪʃənəli/| adv.| 突变地；由于突变 |  Relating to or caused by mutation.  This adverb describes something that happens or exists because of a mutation. | Example:  The protein was mutationally altered to enhance its function.  该蛋白质经过突变改变以增强其功能。  |近义词：mutatively|反义词：normally, naturally


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such, it doesn't have verb conjugations like "is," "was," "will be," etc.  It modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs.


Example sentences demonstrating its usage:

1.  The virus behaved mutationally, evolving rapidly to evade the immune system.  病毒表现出突变性，迅速进化以逃避免疫系统。

2.  The cell line exhibited mutationally altered gene expression. 该细胞系表现出突变改变的基因表达。


PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases directly using "mutationally."  Its usage is largely within scientific or technical contexts.  Phrases related to mutation would include:

1. point mutation: 点突变
2. gene mutation: 基因突变
3. spontaneous mutation: 自发突变
4. induced mutation: 诱导突变
5. somatic mutation: 体细胞突变
