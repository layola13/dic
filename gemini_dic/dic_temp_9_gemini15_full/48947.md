defy:/dɪˈfaɪ/| vt.|反抗；蔑视；挑战；公然违抗|to refuse to obey or show respect for someone or something|The rebels defied the government's orders. 反叛者无视政府的命令。|近义词：resist, oppose, challenge|反义词：obey, comply, submit|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defies authority. = 他蔑视权威。
They defy all attempts to control them. = 他们反抗所有试图控制他们的企图。

一般过去时:
She defied her parents' wishes. = 她违抗了父母的意愿。
The soldiers defied the enemy's attack. = 士兵们抵抗了敌人的进攻。

现在完成时:
He has defied expectations. = 他超出了预期。
They have defied the odds to succeed. = 他们克服困难获得了成功。

过去完成时:
They had defied all predictions. = 他们已经驳斥了所有预测。
She had defied convention all her life. = 她终生都蔑视传统。

将来时:
They will defy the law. = 他们将违反法律。
He will defy all attempts to silence him. = 他将反抗所有让他沉默的企图。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't defy the boss. = 如果我是你，我不会违抗老板。
I wish I had the courage to defy them. = 我真希望我有勇气反抗他们。

被动语态:
The law cannot be defied. = 法律不可违抗。
His authority was defied by many. = 他的权威被许多人蔑视。

疑问句:
Does he dare defy the rules? = 他敢违反规章制度吗？
Will they defy the order? = 他们会违抗命令吗？

否定句:
He did not defy the order. = 他没有违抗命令。
They wouldn't defy their parents. = 他们不会违抗父母。
