frubish:/ˈfruːbɪʃ/| v.|擦亮；使光亮；修饰|To polish or brighten something, especially metal; to renovate or smarten something up.|例句：He frubished up the old brass candlestick. 他擦亮了旧黄铜烛台。  例句：She frubished up the apartment before her guests arrived. 她在她客人到达之前修饰了公寓。|近义词：polish, shine, renovate, refurbish|反义词：tarnish, dull, deteriorate
