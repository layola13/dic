overexercise: /ˌoʊvərˈɛksərsaɪz/ | v. |过度运动；锻炼过度 | To exercise excessively; to engage in strenuous physical activity to an excessive degree, often resulting in negative consequences. |例句：He overexercised and injured his knee. 他过度运动，伤了膝盖。|例句：Overexercising can lead to muscle strain and exhaustion. 过度运动会导致肌肉拉伤和疲惫。|近义词：overtrain, overwork, strain |反义词：undertrain, rest
