checkroll:/ˈtʃɛkroʊl/| n.|点名册，考勤表|A checkroll is a list of names, usually of employees, used to record attendance or payroll.  It can also refer to the act of calling out names to check attendance.|例句：The teacher used the checkroll to take attendance. 老师用点名册来点名。The company's checkroll showed a significant increase in staff this quarter. 公司的考勤表显示本季度员工人数大幅增加。|近义词：attendance list, payroll register, roster|反义词：N/A|名词
