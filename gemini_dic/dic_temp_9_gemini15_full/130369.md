non-united: /ˌnɒn juˈnaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未统一的，未联合的 | Not united; not joined together; separate.  This adjective describes a state of disunity or lack of cohesion among individuals, groups, or entities. It highlights the absence of a unified or combined structure or purpose. |例句：The non-united factions struggled to find common ground.  未统一的派系努力寻找共同点。

The non-united countries formed independent alliances. 未联合的国家组成了独立的联盟。

近义词：divided, separated, fragmented, disunited, unconnected

反义词：united, unified, consolidated, combined, joined
