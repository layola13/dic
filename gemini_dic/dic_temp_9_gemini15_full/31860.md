cheerier:/ˈtʃɪəriər/| adj.| 更快乐的；更愉快的 | More cheerful; more joyful.  It describes something or someone that is more cheerful than before or than something else. | The weather is cheerier today than yesterday.  今天的天气比昨天更晴朗。| The children were much cheerier after receiving their gifts. 收到礼物后，孩子们高兴多了。| cheerful, joyful, merry | gloomy, sad, unhappy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun is cheerier today. = 今天阳光更明媚。
The birds are cheerier in the morning. = 鸟儿清晨叫得更欢快。

一般过去时:
She was cheerier after her promotion. = 她升职后更快乐了。
He felt cheerier after a good night's sleep. = 他睡了个好觉后感觉更精神了。

现在完成时:
The garden has become cheerier since we planted more flowers. =自从我们种了更多花后，花园变得更漂亮了。
Things have been cheerier since the rain stopped. = 雨停后，情况好转了。

过去完成时:
She had been cheerier before the bad news arrived. = 在坏消息传来之前，她一直很开心。
They had felt cheerier after their successful project. = 他们的项目成功后，他们感觉更高兴了。

将来时:
The future will be cheerier if we work together. = 如果我们一起努力，未来会更加光明。
I hope the weather will be cheerier tomorrow. = 我希望明天的天气会更好。

虚拟语气:
If the weather were cheerier, we would go for a picnic. = 如果天气好一点，我们就去野餐。
I wish the situation were cheerier. = 我希望情况能更好一些。

被动语态:
The room was made cheerier with new curtains. = 新窗帘使房间更明亮了。
The atmosphere was made cheerier by the music. = 音乐使气氛更加活跃。

疑问句:
Is the atmosphere cheerier now? =  现在气氛更轻松了吗？
Doesn't she seem cheerier today? = 她今天看起来是不是更开心了？

否定句:
The room wasn't cheerier after the cleaning. = 打扫之后，房间并没有更干净。
His mood wasn't cheerier despite the good news. = 尽管有好消息，他的心情并没有好转。
