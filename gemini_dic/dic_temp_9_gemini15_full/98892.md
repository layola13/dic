intensely:/ɪnˈtɛnsli/ | adv. | 强烈地；剧烈地；紧张地 |  In a very strong or extreme way.  It describes the degree or manner of something. |例句：He stared intensely at the painting. 他专注地凝视着这幅画。

例句：She felt intensely lonely after her husband died. 她丈夫死后感到非常孤独。


近义词：strongly, fiercely, vehemently, powerfully | 反义词：mildly, gently, faintly, weakly | 词性: 副词
