puny:/ˈpjuːni/| adj. |弱小的；微不足道的；可怜的 |Very small and weak; insignificant.  |例句：The puny seedling struggled to survive the harsh winter.  那棵弱小的幼苗在严酷的冬天挣扎求生。

例句：His puny efforts were not enough to win the competition. 他微不足道的努力不足以赢得比赛。


近义词：small, weak, insignificant, feeble, trifling, paltry |反义词：strong, powerful, mighty, significant, substantial


词源：源自古法语 *pune,  意为“小的，微不足道的”，可能与古法语 *poing  “拳头”有关，暗示力量不足。
