gurgling:/ˈɡɜːrɡlɪŋ/ | adj. n. | 汩汩声；咕噜咕噜声 | Making a sound like water running through a narrow passage; a low, bubbling sound.  |例句：The water gurgled down the drain. 水咕噜咕噜地流进下水道。  The baby was gurgling happily.  婴儿高兴地咕噜咕噜地叫着。|近义词：bubbling, murmuring, purling |反义词：silent, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water gurgles down the drain. = 水咕噜咕噜地流进下水道。
The stream gurgles merrily along its course. = 溪流快乐地潺潺流淌。


一般过去时:
The brook gurgled gently last night. = 小溪昨晚轻轻地潺潺作响。
The liquid gurgled as it was poured. = 倒液体时发出咕噜咕噜的声音。


现在完成时:
The pipe has been gurgling all night. = 管子整夜都在咕噜咕噜响。
The baby has gurgled contentedly throughout the afternoon. = 宝宝整个下午都心满意足地咕噜咕噜地叫着。


过去完成时:
The drain had been gurgling for hours before it finally clogged. = 下水道在最终堵塞之前已经咕噜咕噜响了好几个小时。
The tea had gurgled in the kettle before it boiled. = 茶水在水壶里咕噜咕噜响了一会儿才沸腾。


将来时:
The stream will gurgle more loudly after the rain. = 雨后溪流会发出更大的潺潺声。
The pipes will gurgle if the pressure drops too low. = 如果压力下降太低，管道就会发出咕噜咕噜的声音。


虚拟语气:
If the water gurgled louder, we would know there was a problem. = 如果水咕噜咕噜的声音更大，我们就知道了有问题。
I wish the stream gurgled more softly; it's disturbing my sleep. = 我希望溪流的声音能小一点；它扰乱了我的睡眠。


被动语态:
A gurgling sound was heard from the basement. = 从地下室传来咕噜咕噜的声音。
The problem was identified when a gurgling sound was heard. = 当听到咕噜咕噜的声音时，问题就得到了确认。


疑问句:
Is that the water gurgling in the pipes? = 是管道里的水在咕噜咕噜响吗？
Do you hear that gurgling sound? = 你听到那个咕噜咕噜的声音了吗？


否定句:

The liquid didn't gurgle when it was poured. = 倒液体时没有发出咕噜咕噜的声音。
