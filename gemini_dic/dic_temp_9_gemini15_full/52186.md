diarrheal:/ˌdaɪəˈriəl/| adj. |腹泻的；与腹泻有关的| Relating to or characterized by diarrhea.  The adjective form of "diarrhea". It describes something pertaining to or caused by diarrhea.| Example:  He experienced diarrheal symptoms after eating the contaminated food. 他吃了受污染的食物后出现了腹泻症状。|近义词：diarrhetic|反义词：constipated
