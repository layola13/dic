miscomprehension:/ˌmɪskɒmprɪˈhɛnʃən/ | n. |误解，误会 | A failure to understand something correctly.  It often involves a misunderstanding of the intended meaning. | 例句：The miscomprehension between the two parties led to a major conflict.  双方之间的误解导致了重大冲突。


例句：His miscomprehension of the instructions resulted in a flawed product. 他对指令的误解导致产品有缺陷。


近义词：misunderstanding, misinterpretation, confusion


反义词：comprehension, understanding


词性：名词
