compactible:/kəmˈpæktɪbl/ | adj. | 可压缩的；兼容的 |  Able to be compacted; capable of being compressed or reduced in size or volume.  Also, capable of working together without problems; compatible. |

例句：
The soil is compactible, making it ideal for building foundations. = 这土壤易于压实，非常适合建造地基。

The new software is compactible with older systems. = 新软件与旧系统兼容。


近义词：compressible, compatible, congruous

反义词：incompatible, incompressible


词性：形容词
