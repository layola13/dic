mingler: /ˈmɪŋɡlər/ | n. |爱交际的人，爱凑热闹的人 | A person who enjoys socializing and mixing with others.  |He's a real mingler, always chatting with everyone at the party. 他是个真正的社交达人，在派对上总是和每个人聊天。 |近义词：socializer, networker, conversationalist |反义词：loner, recluse, introvert |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mingles with the guests at the party. = 他在派对上与宾客们交往。
She mingles easily with new people. = 她很容易与陌生人打成一片。


一般过去时:
He mingled with the crowd at the concert. = 他在音乐会上与人群混在一起。
She mingled freely at the reception. = 她在招待会上自由地与人交往。


现在完成时:
He has mingled with many influential people in his career. = 他在职业生涯中结识了许多有影响力的人。
She has mingled successfully in various social circles. = 她成功地融入了各种社交圈。


过去完成时:
He had mingled with the protesters before the riot started. = 在暴乱开始之前，他曾与抗议者们混在一起。
She had mingled with the locals before she felt comfortable. = 在她感到自在之前，她已经和当地人混熟了。


将来时:
He will mingle with the celebrities at the award ceremony. = 他将在颁奖典礼上与名人们交往。
She will mingle effortlessly at the upcoming conference. = 她将在即将到来的会议上轻松自如地与人交往。


虚拟语气:
If he mingled more, he would make more friends. = 如果他多与人交往，他会交到更多朋友。
I wish I could mingle more confidently. = 我希望我能更自信地与人交往。


被动语态:
He was mingled amongst the crowd. = 他混在人群中。
The new employee was expected to mingle with the team. = 新员工被期望能与团队成员融洽相处。(被动语态用在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Does he mingle easily at social events? = 他在社交场合容易与人交往吗？
Will she mingle with the other students? = 她会和其他的学生交往吗？


否定句:
He doesn't mingle much at parties. = 他在派对上不太爱与人交往。
She didn't mingle with the other guests. = 她没有与其他客人交往。


PHRASE->
mingling with the crowd = 与人群混在一起
mingling socially = 社交活动
mingling freely = 自由地交往
mingling amongst = 杂处于...之中
mingling in = 参加；加入
