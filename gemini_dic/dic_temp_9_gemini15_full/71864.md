fleshings:/ˈfleʃɪŋz/| n. |皮革下脚料，皮屑|Leather scraps or waste material remaining after the main parts of a hide have been processed.  Fleshings are often used in the production of lower-grade leather products or as a component in other materials.|例句：The tannery recycles the fleshings into fertilizer. 制革厂将皮革下脚料回收制成肥料。

The fleshings were collected and sold to a rendering plant. 皮屑被收集起来卖给了渲染厂。|近义词：scraps, remnants, offal|反义词：finished leather, prime hide
