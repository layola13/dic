/ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度批评；吹毛求疵 | The act of criticizing someone or something excessively or unfairly.  It often implies a fault-finding approach that goes beyond constructive criticism. |  He lost his job because of his manager's overcriticizing. 他因为经理的过度批评而丢了工作。  The teacher's overcriticizing discouraged the students from participating. 老师的过度批评使学生们不敢参与。| criticizing, censuring, fault-finding | praising, commending, approving | 动词
