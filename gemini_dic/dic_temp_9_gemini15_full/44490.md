crossers:/ˈkrɔːsərz/ | n. | 横渡者；越境者；交叉者 | People who cross a boundary, line, or barrier, either physically or metaphorically.  This could refer to people crossing a river, a border, or even a social or cultural divide. |例句：The border patrol stopped several illegal crossers. 边境巡逻队拦住了几个非法越境者。

The hikers were experienced crossers of mountain trails. 这些徒步旅行者是经验丰富的山路穿越者。


近义词：transgressors, trespassers, violators |反义词：stayers, residents, abiders


词性：名词
