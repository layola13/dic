chiliast: /ˈkɪliæst/ | n. |千禧年信徒；相信世界末日后千年王国的人|A person who believes in the imminent establishment of a millennium, a thousand-year reign of Christ on earth, often associated with apocalyptic beliefs.  This belief is rooted in interpretations of the Book of Revelation in the Bible.|  Many chiliasts believe that the Second Coming of Christ is near and will usher in a period of peace and prosperity.  许多千禧年信徒相信基督的第二次降临即将到来，并将开启一个和平与繁荣的时期。| 近义词：millennialist, apocalypticist | 反义词：skeptic, rationalist | 名词
