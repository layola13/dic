physiology:/ˌfɪziˈɒlədʒi/|/ˈfɪziˈɑːlədʒi/| n. |生理学；生理机能|The study of the functions and mechanisms of living organisms and their parts.  It explores how living things work at all levels, from the molecular to the whole organism.|Example:  She's studying physiology at university. 她正在大学学习生理学。
The physiology of the heart is complex. 心脏的生理机能很复杂。|anatomy, biology|pathology, disease|名词
