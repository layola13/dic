dendrochronologist:/ˌdɛndrəʊˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪst/| n. |树轮年代学家|A dendrochronologist is a scientist who studies tree rings to determine the age of wood and to learn about past climates and environmental conditions.  They analyze the width, density, and isotopic composition of tree rings to reconstruct past events. |The dendrochronologist carefully examined the tree rings to determine the age of the ancient artifact.  树轮年代学家仔细检查树木年轮以确定古代文物的年代。|  paleoclimatologist,  historical geologist|  (无明确反义词)|名词
