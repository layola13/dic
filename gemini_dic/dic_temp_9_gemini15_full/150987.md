paramo:/ˈpærəmoʊ/| n.|帕拉莫（南美洲高海拔草原）|A high-altitude grassland ecosystem, typically found in the Andes Mountains of South America, characterized by a unique flora and fauna adapted to harsh conditions.  It is characterized by low temperatures, high winds, and poor soil. |The paramo ecosystem is incredibly diverse and important for water regulation. |highland, moor, tundra |desert, rainforest |名词
