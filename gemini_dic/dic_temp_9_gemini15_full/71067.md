firsthand:/ˈfɜːrstˌhænd/| adj.|第一手的；直接的|obtained or experienced directly; not secondhand or from another person.  It emphasizes direct experience or knowledge without intermediaries.|例句：I have firsthand experience of the difficulties involved. 我有亲身经历过其中的困难。

例句：She gave a firsthand account of the accident. 她对事故作了第一手的叙述。


近义词：direct, immediate, personal, original

反义词：secondhand, indirect


词性:形容词
