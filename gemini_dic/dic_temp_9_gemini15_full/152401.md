pavilions:/pəˈvɪljən/| n.|凉亭；展馆；大型帐篷|A pavilion is a light, usually open structure, often used for shelter or display.  It can be a temporary structure, like a tent at a fair, or a more permanent building, like a gazebo in a garden.  Pavilions are often associated with elegance and sophistication.|例句：The park boasts several beautiful pavilions where people can relax.|近义词：gazebo, kiosk, booth, tent|反义词：building, house
