journalary:/ˈdʒɜːrnəlri/| n.|日记；日记簿|A journalary is a record of daily events, thoughts, or feelings, typically kept in a diary or journal.  It can also refer to the style or practice of keeping such a record.|Example: She maintained a meticulous journalary throughout her travels. 她旅行期间一直认真地写日记。|近义词：diary, journal, logbook |反义词：None
