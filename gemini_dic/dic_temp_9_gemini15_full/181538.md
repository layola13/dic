rel:/rɛl/| adj. |相关的；相对的；家属的 | relating to, connected with, or referring to something else;  a family member |例句：The two events are closely related. 这两件事密切相关。|例句：She is a close relative. 她是我的近亲。|近义词：relevant, pertinent, kindred |反义词：unrelated, irrelevant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two ideas are related. = 这两个想法是相关的。
This problem is related to the previous one. = 这个问题与先前的问题相关。

一般过去时:
The incident was related to the media. = 这起事件被告知媒体。
The two events were closely related. = 这两件事密切相关。

现在完成时:
The police have related the two crimes. = 警方已将这两起罪行联系起来。
I have related my experience to my friend. = 我把我经历的事情告诉了我的朋友。

过去完成时:
He had related the story to me before. = 他以前已经把这个故事讲给我听了。
She had related the events in chronological order. = 她已经按时间顺序叙述了这些事件。

将来时:
I will relate the details of the meeting later. = 我稍后会详细说明会议的情况。
He will relate his experiences in his book. = 他将在他的书中讲述他的经历。

虚拟语气:
If I were related to him, I would help him. = 如果我和他沾亲带故，我会帮助他。
If the two events were related, it would be a coincidence. = 如果这两件事有关联，那将是一种巧合。

被动语态:
The two points are related in the report. = 这两点在报告中是相关的。
The news was related to the public. = 消息已告知公众。

疑问句:
Are these two concepts related? = 这两个概念有关联吗？
Is he related to the victim? = 他与受害者有关系吗？

否定句:
The two factors are not directly related. = 这两个因素没有直接关系。
The information is not related to the case. = 此信息与案件无关。
