griping:/ˈɡriːpɪŋ/ | v. | 抱怨；牢骚；抓紧；紧握 |  to complain constantly in an irritating way; to hold something firmly. | 例句：He's been griping about his job all week. 他整个星期都在抱怨他的工作。  | 例句：The climber gripped the rock face tightly. 登山者紧紧抓住岩石表面。 | 近义词：complaining, grumbling, whining, complaining, grasping | 反义词：praising, complimenting, releasing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is constantly griping about the weather. = 他一直在抱怨天气。
They are griping about the long hours. = 他们在抱怨漫长的工作时间。

一般过去时:
She gripped the steering wheel tightly during the storm. = 暴风雨期间她紧紧抓住方向盘。
He gripped my hand and wouldn't let go. = 他紧紧抓住我的手，不肯放开。

现在完成时:
He has been griping about his boss all morning. = 他整个上午都在抱怨他的老板。
She has gripped the opportunity to advance her career. = 她抓住了提升职业生涯的机会。

过去完成时:
I had been griping for weeks before I finally quit my job. = 在我最终辞职之前，我已经抱怨了好几个星期了。
They had gripped the rope firmly before descending the cliff. = 他们在下降悬崖之前紧紧抓住了绳索。

将来时:
I will be griping if the weather doesn't improve. = 如果天气不好转，我会抱怨的。
She will grip the microphone tightly before giving her speech. = 她将在发表演讲前紧紧抓住麦克风。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't have to work so hard, I wouldn't be griping so much. = 如果我不必这么努力工作，我就不会抱怨这么多了。
I wish he would stop griping all the time. = 我希望他能停止一直抱怨。

被动语态:
The problem was gripped by many people. = 这个问题被许多人关注。 (This is a less common passive use, focusing on the "gripped" meaning of holding firmly)


疑问句:
Is he still griping about the meeting? = 他还在抱怨会议吗？
What are you griping about now? = 你现在在抱怨什么？

否定句:
He didn't grip the rail hard enough and nearly fell. = 他没有紧紧抓住扶手，差点摔倒。
She doesn't grip her pen properly, so her writing is messy. = 她握笔姿势不正确，所以字迹很乱。


PHRASE->
griping about something = 抱怨某事
griping session = 抱怨会
griping constantly = 不停地抱怨
to have a grip on something = 控制某事
to lose one's grip = 失去控制
