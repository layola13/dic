collateral:/kəˈlætərəl/| n.|抵押品；担保品；附带的；次要的|Something pledged as security for repayment of a loan, to be forfeited in case of default.|The bank required collateral before granting the loan.  银行在批准贷款前要求提供抵押品。|Security, pledge, guarantee|Primary, principal|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank requires collateral for all loans. = 银行对所有贷款都要求提供抵押品。
This agreement lists all the collateral involved. = 这份协议列出了所有相关的抵押品。

一般过去时:
He used his house as collateral for the business loan. = 他用自己的房子作为商业贷款的抵押品。
The company provided insufficient collateral for the investment. = 公司提供的投资担保品不足。

现在完成时:
They have already secured the necessary collateral for the project. = 他们已经为该项目获得了必要的抵押品。
The borrower has failed to provide sufficient collateral. = 借款人未能提供足够的抵押品。

过去完成时:
Before the loan was approved, they had already gathered the collateral. = 在贷款获批之前，他们已经收集好了抵押品。
The investor had insisted on additional collateral before investing. = 投资者在投资前坚持要求提供额外的抵押品。

将来时:
She will use her stocks as collateral for the upcoming loan. = 她将用她的股票作为即将到来的贷款的抵押品。
We will need to find suitable collateral before applying for the loan. = 我们需要在申请贷款之前找到合适的抵押品。

虚拟语气:
If I had more collateral, I could get a larger loan. = 如果我有更多抵押品，我就能获得更大的贷款。
I wish I had sufficient collateral to secure the investment. = 我希望我有足够的抵押品来担保这笔投资。

被动语态:
The loan was secured by collateral from the borrower. = 这笔贷款由借款人的抵押品担保。
Collateral is often required by lenders to mitigate risk. = 贷款人通常要求提供抵押品以降低风险。

疑问句:
What kind of collateral do they accept? = 他们接受什么类型的抵押品？
Is the property sufficient collateral for the loan? = 这处房产是否足以作为贷款的抵押品？

否定句:
He didn't have enough collateral to secure the loan. = 他没有足够的抵押品来担保贷款。
The bank refused the loan application because of insufficient collateral. = 由于抵押品不足，银行拒绝了贷款申请。
