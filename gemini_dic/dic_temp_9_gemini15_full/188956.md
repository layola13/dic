/rwænˈdɑːn/| adj. |卢旺达的；卢旺达人的|Of or relating to Rwanda, its people, or its culture.  The word derives from Rwanda, the country in East Africa.  It is used as an adjective to describe things originating from or associated with Rwanda.|

The Rwandan government implemented new policies. = 卢旺达政府实施了新的政策。

The Rwandan people are known for their resilience. = 卢旺达人民以其韧性而闻名。


近义词：Rwandese (often used interchangeably)
反义词：无


词性：形容词
