shoeing:/ˈʃuːɪŋ/ | n. | 钉马掌；给马钉掌 | The act of fitting horseshoes onto a horse's hooves.  This involves shaping and attaching the horseshoe to protect and support the hoof. | Example: The farrier was skilled in shoeing horses of all sizes.  钉马掌师傅擅长为各种尺寸的马钉马掌。

|近义词: horseshoeing |反义词:  removing horseshoes |词性:名词

The farrier shoes horses every day. = 钉马掌的师傅每天都给马钉掌。
He shoes his own horse regularly. = 他定期给自己的马钉掌。
