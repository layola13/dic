laments:/ləˈments/| v. n.| 哀悼；悲叹；抱怨 | To express sorrow, regret, or disappointment.  |例句：She lamented the loss of her childhood home. 她为失去童年故居而悲伤。

例句：He lamented his inability to help. 他为无法提供帮助而感到惋惜。


近义词：mourn, grieve, bewail, deplore, complain


反义词：celebrate, rejoice, exult


词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He laments the state of the world. = 他哀叹世界现状。
She frequently laments her bad luck. = 她经常抱怨自己的运气不好。


一般过去时:
They lamented the death of their leader. = 他们为领导人的去世而哀悼。
He lamented his lost opportunity. = 他为失去的机会而惋惜。


现在完成时:
She has lamented the decision for weeks. = 她已经为此决定哀叹了好几个星期了。
He has lamented his mistakes many times. = 他多次为自己的错误感到懊悔。


过去完成时:
They had lamented the lost battle before the news arrived. = 在消息传来之前，他们就已经为这场失败的战役感到悲痛了。
She had lamented her poor choices for years. = 她多年来一直为自己的糟糕选择而后悔。


将来时:
He will lament his actions later. = 他以后会为自己的行为后悔。
They will lament the consequences of their decisions. = 他们将为其决定的后果而懊悔。


虚拟语气:
I would lament the loss if it were my child. = 如果是我孩子的话，我会为这损失感到悲痛。
She wished she hadn't lamented so loudly. = 她希望自己没有大声抱怨。


被动语态:
The loss was lamented by everyone. = 这损失受到了所有人的哀悼。
His mistakes were lamented by his colleagues. = 他的错误受到了同事们的指责。


疑问句:
Why does he lament so much? = 他为什么如此悲伤？
What are they lamenting about? = 他们在哀叹什么？


否定句:
He did not lament the outcome. = 他并不为结果感到悲伤。
She doesn't lament her past decisions. = 她并不为过去的决定而后悔。


PHRASE->
lament over = 为...哀叹
lament for = 为...而悲伤
lament the loss of = 为...的损失而悲伤
a lament for = 为...而作的挽歌
make a lament = 吟诵挽歌
