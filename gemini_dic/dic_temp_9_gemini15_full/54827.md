dispersion:/dɪˈspɜːrʒən/| n. |分散；弥散；传播；色散|The scattering or spreading of something over a wide area.  It can refer to the distribution of things in space, the spreading of a substance, or the scattering of light. |例句：The dispersion of seeds by the wind helped the plant to colonize new areas. 风力传播种子帮助植物在新区域生长。

The dispersion of the crowd after the concert was surprisingly quick. 音乐会结束后，人群散得令人惊讶地快。

|近义词：scattering, dissemination, spread, diffusion |反义词：concentration, aggregation, unification |名词
