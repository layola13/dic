hexagrams:/ˈhɛksəˌɡræmz/| n. |卦；六十四卦；八卦|Six-line figures used in divination, especially in the I Ching.  Each hexagram is composed of six horizontal lines, each line either solid (yang) or broken (yin).|例句：The I Ching uses hexagrams to provide guidance and insight.  易经用卦来提供指导和洞察力。

The study of hexagrams is a complex undertaking.  对卦的研究是一项复杂的工作。|近义词：divinations, symbols, figures|反义词：None.
