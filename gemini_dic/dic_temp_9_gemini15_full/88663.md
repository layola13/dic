hoarse:/hɔːrs/| /hɔːrs/ | adj. |嘶哑的，沙哑的 | Having a rough voice, typically as a result of illness or overuse.  |His voice was hoarse from shouting. 他喊哑了嗓子。|  The speaker's voice became hoarse during the long lecture. 演讲者在长时间的演讲中声音变得嘶哑。|  rough, raspy | clear, smooth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sounds hoarse today. = 他今天声音嘶哑。
Her voice is usually hoarse after singing. = 她唱歌后声音通常会嘶哑。


一般过去时:

She spoke in a hoarse whisper. = 她嘶哑地低语。
