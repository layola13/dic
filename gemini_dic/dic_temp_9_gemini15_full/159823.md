polygrooved:/ˌpɒliˈɡruːvd/| adj.|多槽的，多沟的|Having multiple grooves.  The term is often used in engineering and manufacturing to describe surfaces with many grooves, often for increased friction or other functional purposes. |Example: The polygrooved roller increased traction significantly.  多槽滚轮显著增加了牵引力。  The polygrooved surface improved the grip of the tire. 多槽表面提高了轮胎的抓地力。|近义词：multi-grooved, channeled, furrowed|反义词：smooth, ungrooved
