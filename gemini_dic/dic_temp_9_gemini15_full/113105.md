lunchless: /ˈlʌntʃləs/ | adj. | 不吃午饭的；没有午饭的 |  Without lunch; lacking lunch.  The word is formed by adding the prefix "lunch-" to the suffix "-less," indicating the absence of something.  |  例句：He felt lunchless and irritable by the afternoon.  下午他感到没吃午饭，脾气暴躁。 He went on a lunchless diet to lose weight. 他为了减肥进行了一段不吃午饭的节食。 | 近义词： without lunch,  unfed (at lunchtime) | 反义词： having lunch, fed (at lunchtime)
