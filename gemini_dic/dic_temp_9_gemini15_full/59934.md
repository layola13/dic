edgewise:/ˈɛdʒwaɪz/ | adv. |  侧着；边缘朝上；顺利地 |  In a position with the edge foremost or upward; successfully or smoothly. | 例句：He couldn't get the board edgewise. 他无法把木板侧着放。

例句：
The carpenter placed the plank edgewise against the wall. 木匠把木板侧着靠在墙上。

例句：
The ship sailed edgewise through the narrow channel. 船侧着身穿过狭窄的海峡。


近义词：sideways, laterally, on edge


反义词：flatwise, broadside
