outinfluencing:  /ˌaʊtˈɪnflʊənsɪŋ/ | v. | 超越影响；过度影响 |  The act of exceeding or surpassing the influence of something or someone. It implies an influence that goes beyond the normal or expected level.  This word is not a standard dictionary entry and is likely a newly coined or rarely used word.  It is formed by combining "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) with "influencing". |


例句:

The celebrity's outinfluencing of public opinion on the political issue was remarkable.  = 名人在政治问题上的公众舆论影响力超乎寻常。

The company's marketing campaign was successful in outinfluencing its competitors. = 公司的营销活动成功地超越了竞争对手的影响力。


近义词: overshadowing, surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, dominating


反义词:  undermining, diminishing, lessening, weakening


词性:动词
