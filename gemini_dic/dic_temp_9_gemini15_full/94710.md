improper fraction:/ɪmˈprɑːpər frækʃən/| n. |假分数|A fraction where the numerator is greater than or equal to the denominator.  For example, 5/4, 7/3, and 11/11 are improper fractions.  An improper fraction represents a value greater than or equal to one.|The pizza was cut into 8 slices, and I ate 11/8 of it.  (比萨饼被切成8块，我吃了11/8块。)  |近义词:top-heavy fraction|反义词:proper fraction
