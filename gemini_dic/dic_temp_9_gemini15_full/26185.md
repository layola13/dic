butlerage:/ˈbʌtlərɪdʒ/ | n. |管家费；酒税 |A fee paid to a butler, historically a tax on wine or beer.  The term originates from the role of butlers in managing household finances and supplies, including alcoholic beverages. |The manor collected a butlerage on all the wine sold within its boundaries.|近义词：tax, levy, duty |反义词：None specifically.
