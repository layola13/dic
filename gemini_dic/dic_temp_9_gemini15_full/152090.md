pathetic:/pəˈθɛtɪk/| adj. |令人同情的；可怜的；拙劣的；低劣的 | evoking pity or sadness; causing one to feel sympathy.  Also,  of very low quality; worthless. | 例句：His pathetic attempt to fix the car only made things worse.  他的拙劣的修车尝试只会让情况更糟。

His pathetic story moved everyone to tears. 他可怜的故事让每个人都感动得流泪。


近义词：pitiful, lamentable, miserable, wretched, sorry, poor, inadequate, feeble

反义词：admirable, excellent, superb, outstanding, magnificent
