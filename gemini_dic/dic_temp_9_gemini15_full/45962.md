cya:/saɪˈɑː/| int.|再见|See you again.  It's a shortened, informal way of saying "see you."|例句：Cya later!  一会儿见！|近义词：see you, bye, later|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't conjugate like verbs or nouns.  Therefore, typical tense conjugations are not applicable.  It's used as a standalone farewell.


PHRASE->
Cya soon =  很快再见
Cya later = 一会儿见
Cya tomorrow = 明天见
Catch ya later =  一会儿见 (slightly more informal)
See ya = 再见 (similar meaning, slightly more formal)
