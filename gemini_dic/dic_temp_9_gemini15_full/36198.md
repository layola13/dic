cogent: /ˈkoʊdʒənt/ | adj. | 有说服力的；令人信服的；合理的 |  Convincing or believable by virtue of strong evidence or clear logic.  A cogent argument is one that is clear, logical, and persuasive. |

例句：

His cogent arguments swayed the jury.  = 他有说服力的论点说服了陪审团。

The evidence presented was cogent and irrefutable. =  提交的证据令人信服且无可辩驳。


近义词：persuasive, convincing, compelling, forceful, sound, valid

反义词：unconvincing, weak, flimsy, illogical, unsound, invalid

词性：形容词
