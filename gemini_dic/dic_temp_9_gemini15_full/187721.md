roost:/ruːst/| n., v.|栖息处；鸟窝；（尤指鸟类的）夜栖；栖息；聚居|

英文解析:
Noun: A roost is a place where birds or other animals rest or sleep, especially at night.  It can also refer to a group of birds resting together.

Verb: To roost means to settle down to rest or sleep, especially for birds perching on a branch or other support.


例句：
The chickens went to their roost at sunset.  鸡在日落时回到它们的栖息处。

The birds were roosting in the trees.  鸟儿们栖息在树上。


近义词：
nest (as a noun), perch (as a noun and verb), settle, rest


反义词：
(There isn't a direct antonym, but words suggesting activity or movement could be considered opposites.)  fly, soar, take flight


词性：
Noun, Verb
