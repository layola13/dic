mollify:/ˈmɑːlɪfaɪ/| vt.|安慰；抚慰；缓和；平息|To appease the anger or anxiety of (someone). To lessen the severity of (something).|例句：He tried to mollify his critics with a conciliatory speech. 他试图通过一篇调解性讲话来安抚他的批评者。

例句：The government attempted to mollify public anger over the new tax. 政府试图平息公众对新税的愤怒。


近义词：soothe, appease, pacify, placate|反义词：aggravate, exacerbate, inflame, provoke
