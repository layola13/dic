/ˌoʊvər kəˈrɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 过度纠正；矫枉过正 | The act of correcting something to an excessive degree, often resulting in a worse outcome than the original problem.  It implies going beyond what is necessary or appropriate in an attempt to fix something. | Example: Overcorrecting the steering wheel after a skid can cause the car to spin out.  过度纠正方向盘会导致汽车甩尾。 |近义词: overcompensating, overdoing, going too far | 反义词: undercorrecting, neglecting
