shola:/ˈʃoʊlə/| n.|沼泽林，湿地森林|A shola is a patch of forest found in the high altitude regions of the Western Ghats mountain range in southern India.  These forests are typically evergreen and are characterized by their unique flora and fauna. They are islands of forest surrounded by grassland or other vegetation.|例句：The shola forests of the Western Ghats are biodiversity hotspots. 西高止山脉的沼泽林是生物多样性热点地区。|近义词：forest patch, woodland island|反义词：desert, grassland
