hippopotamus:/ˌhɪpəˈpɒtəməs/|/ˈhɪpəpɑːtəmʌs/| n. |河马|A large, mostly herbivorous, semiaquatic mammal of Africa, having thick skin, short legs, and a large head with a wide mouth.|例句：The hippopotamus opened its massive mouth, revealing rows of formidable teeth.  河马张开巨大的嘴巴，露出几排可怕的牙齿。|例句：We saw a family of hippopotamuses basking in the mud. 我们看到一群河马在泥里晒太阳。|近义词：river horse|反义词：无

词源:
The word "hippopotamus" comes from the Ancient Greek word ἱπποπόταμος (hippopótamos), which is a compound of ἵππος (hippos), meaning "horse," and ποταμός (potamos), meaning "river."  Thus, literally, "river horse."
