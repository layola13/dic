premaintains:/ˌpriːmeɪnˈteɪnz/| v.| 预先维护；提前保养 |To perform maintenance or upkeep on something before it is needed or before a problem arises.| The technician premaintains the machinery every month to prevent breakdowns. 技术人员每月对机器进行预先维护，以防止故障。|近义词：preventative maintenance, proactive maintenance|反义词：reactive maintenance


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They premaintain the aircraft regularly. = 他们定期对飞机进行预先维护。
The company premaintains its computer systems. = 公司对它的计算机系统进行预先维护。


一般过去时:
The engineer premaintained the engine before the long journey. = 工程师在长途旅行之前对发动机进行了预先维护。
We premaintained the equipment last week. = 我们上周对设备进行了预先维护。


现在完成时:
They have premaintained the building's electrical system. = 他们已经对建筑物的电力系统进行了预先维护。
The mechanic has premaintained the car's brakes. = 技工已经对汽车的刹车进行了预先维护。


过去完成时:
They had premaintained the servers before the data migration. = 他们在数据迁移之前已经对服务器进行了预先维护。
She had premaintained her bicycle before the race. = 她在比赛前已经对她的自行车进行了预先维护。


将来时:
We will premaintain the generators before the storm. = 我们将在暴风雨来临之前对发电机进行预先维护。
They will premaintain the network infrastructure next month. = 他们下个月将对网络基础设施进行预先维护。


虚拟语气:
If they premaintained the equipment properly, this wouldn't have happened. = 如果他们正确地进行了设备预先维护，就不会发生这种情况了。
It would be wise if the company premaintained its assets more frequently. = 如果公司更频繁地对资产进行预先维护，那就明智了。


被动语态:
The equipment is premaintained every six months. = 设备每六个月进行一次预先维护。
The system was premaintained before the upgrade. = 系统在升级之前进行了预先维护。


疑问句:
Does the factory premaintain its machinery? = 工厂是否对它的机器进行预先维护？
How often is the software premaintained? = 软件多久进行一次预先维护？


否定句:
They don't premaintain the vehicles often enough. = 他们对车辆的预先维护不够频繁。
The company didn't premaintain its database properly. = 公司没有正确地对数据库进行预先维护。
