overreliant: /ˌoʊvərɪˈlaɪənt/ | adj. | 过分依赖的，依赖性过强的 | Excessively reliant; depending too heavily on someone or something. |例句：The company became overreliant on a single supplier, leaving it vulnerable to supply chain disruptions.  这家公司过度依赖单一供应商，使其容易受到供应链中断的影响。


例句：He's overreliant on his parents for financial support. 他过度依赖父母的经济支持。


近义词：dependent, reliant, heavily reliant, unduly reliant | 反义词：independent, self-reliant


词源：over- (表示“过度”) + reliant (依赖的)  reliant 源自 rely (依赖) 的形容词形式。rely 的词源追溯到古法语 relier 和古英语 relyan，最终源自拉丁语 religare (to bind together, tie up)
