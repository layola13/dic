overtalk:/ˈoʊvərtɔːk/| vt.|喋喋不休地谈论；打断；喧宾夺主|To talk over or beyond a certain point; to talk too much or too long; to interrupt or drown out someone else's speech. |例句：He overtalked his welcome at the party. 他在聚会上话太多，不受欢迎了。

例句：She constantly overtalks her colleagues in meetings, making it difficult for others to contribute.  她在会议上总是喋喋不休地打断同事，使其他人难以发言。


近义词：interrupt, dominate, monopolize, gabble


反义词：listen attentively, be silent, be concise


词性：动词
