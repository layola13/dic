orientally:/ˌɔːriˈentəli/ | adv. |  东方地；东方风格地 | In a manner characteristic of the Orient or East Asia;  in an Eastern style.  The word often carries a dated or even stereotypical connotation. | 例句：The garden was decorated orientally, with pagodas and cherry blossoms.  花园装饰得很有东方特色，有宝塔和樱花。

例句：The restaurant served food that was described as "orientally spiced." 餐厅供应被描述为“东方香料”的食物。


近义词：easternly (less loaded), in an Eastern style,  in the manner of the East


反义词： occidentally (in a Western style)


词性：副词
