chances:/tʃɑːnsɪz/| n. |机会，可能性，概率 |The chances of winning are slim.  赢得的可能性很小。|There are many chances for advancement in this company.  在这家公司有很多晋升的机会。| opportunities, possibilities | impossibilities, certainties | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chances are high that it will rain. = 下雨的可能性很高。
What are the chances of success? = 成功的机会有多大？


一般过去时:
The chances of survival were low. = 生存的机会很低。
His chances improved after the surgery. = 他手术后机会增加了。


现在完成时:
My chances have improved significantly. = 我的机会已经大大提高了。
What chances have you had so far? = 到目前为止你有哪些机会？


过去完成时:
The chances of winning had diminished before the final game. = 在决赛之前获胜的机会已经减少了。
I realized my chances had already been missed. = 我意识到我的机会已经错过了。


将来时:
The chances of a recession will increase if inflation remains high. = 如果通货膨胀居高不下，经济衰退的可能性将会增加。
What are the chances that she will accept the job? = 她接受这份工作的可能性有多大？


虚拟语气:
If I had had more chances, I would have succeeded. = 如果我有更多机会，我就成功了。
I wish I had more chances to travel. = 我希望我有更多旅行的机会。


被动语态:
His chances were increased by the new evidence. = 新证据增加了他的机会。
The chances of success are being carefully considered. = 成功的机会正在被仔细考虑。


疑问句:
What are the chances of getting a promotion? = 得到晋升的机会有多大？
What are my chances of winning the lottery? = 我中彩票的机会有多大？


否定句:
There are no chances of success without hard work. = 没有努力就没有成功的机会。
She had little chance of winning the race.  她赢得比赛的机会很小。


PHRASE->
take a chance: 冒险
