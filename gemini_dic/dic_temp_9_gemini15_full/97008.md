infatigable:/ɪnˈfæɡɪtəbl/ | adj. |不知疲倦的；不屈不挠的 |  tireless; persistent; incapable of being fatigued.  The word describes someone or something that shows an inability to become tired or weary. This characteristic often manifests as unwavering persistence in pursuing a goal or task. |


例句：

He was an indefatigable worker, putting in long hours every day.  = 他是一位不知疲倦的工作者，每天工作很长时间。

Her indefatigable spirit helped her overcome many obstacles. = 她不屈不挠的精神帮助她克服了许多障碍。


近义词：tireless, untiring, tireless, persistent, assiduous, unflagging

反义词：tired, weary, fatigued, lazy, indolent


词源:

The word "infatigable" comes from the Latin prefix "in-" (meaning "not"), "fatigāre" (meaning "to tire" or "to weary"), and the suffix "-able" (meaning "capable of").  Therefore, the word literally means "not capable of being tired."


用法说明:

infatigable 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，例如人、动物或机器。它通常用于描述那些在面对困难或挑战时仍然坚持不懈的人或事物。
