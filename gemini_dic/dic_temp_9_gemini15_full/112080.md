longingness:/ˈlɒŋɪŋnəs/ | n. |渴望；思慕；依恋|A feeling of intense longing or yearning.  It often implies a deep and persistent desire for something or someone that is absent or unattainable.  The word emphasizes the intensity and duration of the feeling.|例句：The longingness for her childhood home overwhelmed her.  她对童年故乡的渴望压倒了她。

例句：His longingness for his lost love was palpable. 他对失去的爱人的渴望是显而易见的。


近义词：yearning, yearningness, wistfulness, pining


反义词：contentment, satisfaction, indifference


词性：名词
