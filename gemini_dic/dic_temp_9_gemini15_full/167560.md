prewashes:/ˌpriːˈwɒʃɪz/ | n. | 预洗；预先清洗 | Prewashes are a preliminary cleaning step before the main wash cycle. 预洗是主要洗涤循环之前的初步清洁步骤。|近义词：pre-cleaning, preliminary cleaning | 反义词：final wash, post-wash |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine automatically prewashes the clothes. = 机器自动预洗衣服。
She always prewashes her delicates before washing them. = 她总是在洗涤衣物之前预洗她的精致衣物。


一般过去时:
I prewashed the shirts before putting them in the washing machine. = 我把衬衫预洗后才放入洗衣机。
They prewashed the heavily soiled towels separately. = 他们将严重弄脏的毛巾分开预洗。


现在完成时:
She has prewashed all the white clothes. = 她已经预洗了所有白色衣物。
We have prewashed the muddy clothes to remove the excess dirt. = 我们已经预洗了泥泞的衣服以去除多余的污垢。


过去完成时:
He had prewashed the delicate fabrics to protect them from damage. = 他已经预洗了精致的面料以保护它们免受损坏。
They had prewashed the curtains before hanging them up to dry. = 他们已经预洗了窗帘，然后才挂起来晾干。


将来时:
I will prewash the clothes before I go to work. = 我上班前会预洗衣服。
She will prewash the heavily stained shirts tonight. = 她今晚将预洗那些严重污渍的衬衫。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would prewash the clothes. = 如果我有时间，我会预洗衣服。
If she prewashed the clothes, they would be cleaner. = 如果她预洗了衣服，衣服会更干净。


被动语态:
The clothes were prewashed before being put in the dryer. = 衣服在放入烘干机之前已被预洗。
The delicate fabrics are prewashed to protect their colors. = 精致的面料经过预洗以保护其颜色。


疑问句:
Did you prewash the clothes before washing them? = 你在洗衣服之前预洗了吗？
Have they prewashed the delicate items? = 他们预洗了那些精致的物品了吗？


否定句:
I didn't prewash the clothes because I was in a hurry. = 我因为赶时间所以没有预洗衣服。
She didn't prewash the clothes, and they ended up with more stains. = 她没有预洗衣服，结果衣服上留下了更多的污渍。
