daydreaming: /ˈdeɪdriːmɪŋ/ | n. | 白日梦；幻想 | The act of letting your thoughts wander while you are awake, often with pleasant or fanciful images.  It's a state of mind characterized by inattention to one's immediate surroundings. | 例句：He was daydreaming during the lecture. 他在课堂上做白日梦。

例句：She spent the afternoon daydreaming about her upcoming vacation. 她下午都在做关于即将到来的假期的白日梦。


近义词：fantasizing, woolgathering, reverie, brown study


反义词：concentration, focus, attention


词性：名词
