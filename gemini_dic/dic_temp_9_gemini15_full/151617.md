participating:/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 参与的；参加的 |  Describes someone or something actively involved in an activity, event, or process.  |  The participating teams showed great sportsmanship.  参与的队伍展现了极好的体育精神。|  involved, engaging, taking part |  uninvolved, passive, abstaining |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is participating in the marathon. 他正在参加马拉松比赛。
The students are participating actively in class discussions. 学生们积极参与课堂讨论。


一般过去时:
She participated in the science fair last year. 她去年参加了科学展览会。
They participated enthusiastically in the fundraising event. 他们热情地参加了募捐活动。


现在完成时:
He has participated in numerous charity events. 他参加过许多慈善活动。
We have participated in the project from the beginning. 我们从一开始就参与了这个项目。


过去完成时:
They had participated in several meetings before making the final decision. 他们在做出最终决定之前已经参加了几次会议。
She had participated in many competitions before winning the championship. 在赢得冠军之前，她已经参加过许多比赛。


将来时:
I will be participating in a conference next month. 我下个月将参加一个会议。
They will participate in the debate tomorrow. 他们明天将参加辩论。


虚拟语气:
If I were participating, I would have a better understanding of the project. 如果我参与其中，我会对这个项目有更好的了解。
I wish I had been participating in the discussion earlier. 我希望我早点参与讨论。


被动语态:
Many students were participating in the school play. 许多学生都参加了学校的戏剧表演。(被动语态较少用于此词)
The company's employees are actively being encouraged to participate in training programs. 公司积极鼓励员工参加培训项目。(强调被动参与)


疑问句:
Are you participating in the upcoming festival? 你会参加即将到来的节日吗？
Is he participating in the research study? 他参与这项研究吗？


否定句:
She is not participating in the competition this year. 她今年不参加比赛。
He did not participate in the meeting yesterday. 他昨天没有参加会议。



PHRASE->
participating in: 参与...
actively participating: 积极参与
fully participating: 全力参与
willingly participating: 乐意参与
meaningfully participating: 有意义地参与
