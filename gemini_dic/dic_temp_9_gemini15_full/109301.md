lend:/lend/| vt.|借出，贷款|To give something to someone for a period of time, expecting it to be returned.|例句：Can I lend you my pen? 我可以把我的钢笔借给你吗？|例句：The bank lent him a large sum of money. 银行借给他一大笔钱。|近义词：loan, borrow|反义词：borrow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I lend my car to my brother sometimes. = 我有时把我的车借给我的弟弟。
She lends books to the library. = 她把书借给图书馆。

一般过去时:
He lent me his bicycle last week. = 他上周把他的自行车借给了我。
They lent money to the struggling business. = 他们借钱给这家苦苦挣扎的企业。

现在完成时:
I have lent him money several times. = 我已经借给他几次钱了。
She has lent her expertise to many projects. = 她已经把她的专业知识借给了许多项目。

过去完成时:
He had lent his car to his friend before the accident. = 事故发生前，他已经把车借给了他的朋友。
They had lent the company significant funds before it went bankrupt. = 在公司破产之前，他们已经借给了公司大量的资金。

将来时:
I will lend you my notes for the exam. = 我会把我的考试笔记借给你。
She will lend her support to the campaign. = 她将支持这项运动。

虚拟语气:
If I had money, I would lend it to you. = 如果我有钱，我会借给你。
I wish I could lend you more money. = 我希望我能借给你更多钱。

被动语态:
Money was lent to the farmers by the government. = 政府把钱借给了农民。
His car was lent to a friend for the weekend. = 他的车被借给了一个朋友一个周末。

疑问句:
Will you lend me your phone? = 你会把你的手机借给我吗？
Can you lend me some sugar? = 你能借我一些糖吗？

否定句:
I cannot lend you any more money. = 我不能再借给你任何钱了。
She did not lend him her car. = 她没有把她的车借给他。


PHRASE->
lend a hand = 帮忙
lend itself to = 适合于
lend an ear = 倾听
lend credence to = 使…更可信
lend weight to = 加强…的力度
