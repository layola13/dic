granger:/ˈɡreɪndʒər/| n. |格兰杰；乡绅；土地拥有者 |A Granger is a person who owns or manages a large farm, especially one that is self-sufficient and produces a variety of crops and livestock.  The term often evokes images of a prosperous and independent rural landowner.| Example:  He was a Granger, known throughout the county for his bountiful harvests. 他是一位格兰杰，以其丰收而闻名全郡。|近义词：landowner, farmer, yeoman |反义词：tenant, renter
