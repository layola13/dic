over-plowing: /ˌoʊvərˈplaʊɪŋ/ | n. |过度耕作|The act of plowing land too frequently or too deeply, leading to soil degradation.|例句：Over-plowing depleted the soil of its essential nutrients.  过度耕作耗尽了土壤中的必需营养物质。|近义词：overcultivation, excessive plowing|反义词：conservation tillage, no-till farming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer over-plows his fields every year. = 这位农民每年都过度耕作他的田地。
They over-plow the land, damaging the soil structure. = 他们过度耕作土地，破坏了土壤结构。


一般过去时:
The farmers over-plowed their land last season. = 农民们在上个季度过度耕作了他们的土地。
He over-plowed the field, resulting in poor crop yields. = 他过度耕作了田地，导致收成不好。


现在完成时:
They have over-plowed this land for decades. = 几十年来他们一直过度耕作这块土地。
Farmers have over-plowed the fields, leading to erosion. = 农民们过度耕作了田地，导致了侵蚀。


过去完成时:

将来时:

If they continue this practice, they will over-plow the land to its detriment. = 如果他们继续这种做法，他们将会过度耕作土地，从而对其造成损害。


虚拟语气:
