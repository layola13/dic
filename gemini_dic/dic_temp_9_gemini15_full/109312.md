lends:/lɛndz/ | vt. |借出；给予；使……具有(某种性质)| to give something to someone for a period of time, expecting it to be returned |例句：The library lends books to students. 图书馆把书借给学生。|例句：His experience lends weight to his argument. 他的经验使他的论点更有分量。|近义词：loan, give, provide |反义词：borrow, receive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lends me his car every weekend. = 他每个周末都把他的车借给我。
The bank lends money to businesses. = 银行向企业贷款。


一般过去时:
She lent me her bike last week. = 她上周把她的自行车借给了我。
They lent their support to the project. = 他们支持了这个项目。


现在完成时:
I have lent him my notes several times. = 我已经好几次把我的笔记借给他了。
The government has lent significant financial aid to the disaster victims. = 政府已经向灾民提供了大量的财政援助。


过去完成时:
He had lent his tools to his neighbor before he needed them himself. = 他在他自己需要之前已经把他的工具借给了他的邻居。
They had lent their full support to the cause before the controversy began. = 在争议开始之前，他们已经全力支持这项事业。


将来时:
I will lend you my umbrella if it rains. = 如果下雨，我会把我的雨伞借给你。
The company will lend its expertise to the development of the new product. = 公司将把它的专业知识借给新产品的开发。


虚拟语气:
If I had lent him the money, he would have been able to buy the house. = 如果我借给他钱，他就能买下房子了。
I wish I had lent him my car; he wouldn't have been late for work. = 我真希望我把车借给他；他上班就不会迟到了。


被动语态:
Money was lent to the company at a high interest rate. = 以高利率向该公司贷款。
Support was lent to the campaign by several celebrities. = 几位名人都支持了这场运动。


疑问句:
Does he lend his books to others? = 他把他的书借给别人吗？
Will you lend me your pen? = 你能把你的钢笔借给我吗？


否定句:
I didn't lend him my car because I needed it. = 我没有把我的车借给他，因为我需要它。
The bank refused to lend them any more money. = 银行拒绝再借给他们任何钱。


PHRASE->
lend a hand = 帮忙
lend itself to = 适合于
lend an ear = 倾听
lend credence to = 使…更可信
lend weight to = 增添分量
