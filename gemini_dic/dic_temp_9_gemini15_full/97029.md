infectiously:/ɪnˈfɛkʃəsli/ | adv. |  有感染力地；极易传染地 | In a way that is likely to spread to others; contagiously.  |例句：The laughter spread infectiously through the room.  笑声在房间里传染性地蔓延开来。

例句：His enthusiasm was infectiously inspiring. 他的热情极具感染力，令人振奋。


近义词：contagiously, communicably, catching


反义词：uncontagiously, uncommunicably


词性:副词
