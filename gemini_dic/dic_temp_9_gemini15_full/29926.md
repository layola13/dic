cavalierly:/ˌkævəˈlɪərli/| adv.|漫不经心地；傲慢无礼地；轻率地 |In a casual and offhand manner; disdainfully.  Showing a lack of proper concern; nonchalantly. |例句：He cavalierly dismissed her concerns. 他漫不经心地驳回了她的担忧。

例句：She treated the serious matter cavalierly. 她轻率地对待了这个严重的问题。


近义词：disdainfully, nonchalantly, carelessly, contemptuously, superciliously

反义词：carefully, respectfully, seriously, thoughtfully, attentively

词源：源自法语单词 *cavalier*,  意为“骑士”，后来引申出“傲慢的；轻率的”的意思。  This reflects the historical association of knights with a certain air of nonchalant disregard for the concerns of commoners.
