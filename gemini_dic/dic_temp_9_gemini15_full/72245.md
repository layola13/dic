floriferous:/flɒrɪfərəs/|ˈflɔːrɪfərəs/| adj.|开花的；多花的；繁茂的|Producing many flowers; full of flowers.|The garden was floriferous, a riot of color and fragrance.  花园里鲜花盛开，色彩芬芳，一片繁华景象。|The hillside was a floriferous spectacle in spring. 春天山坡上花团锦簇，蔚为壮观。|abundant, profuse, blossoming|barren, sterile, flowerless
