roadblocking: /ˈroʊdˌblɑːkɪŋ/ | n. | 道路封锁；阻碍；障碍 | The act of blocking a road, typically to impede traffic or protest.  It can also refer to figurative obstacles or impediments. |例句：The protestors engaged in roadblocking to disrupt the summit.  抗议者封锁道路以扰乱峰会。

The construction work caused significant roadblocking during rush hour.  施工导致高峰期严重堵塞道路。

近义词：obstruction, impediment, blockage, barrier | 反义词：clear passage, open road | 词性：名词
