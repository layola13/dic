/prɪˈriːvɜːrsz/ | v. |预先反转；提前逆转| To reverse something before it happens or before it is normally done.  This is a relatively uncommon word, often used in technical contexts or in situations where an action is reversed proactively.  It implies a deliberate and anticipatory action. |The system automatically prereverses the transaction to prevent errors. = 系统自动预先反转交易以防止错误。 The engineer prereversed the engine's polarity to test a new safety mechanism. = 工程师预先反转了发动机的极性以测试一种新的安全机制。|近义词：preempt, counteract, anticipate, forestall |反义词：postpone, delay,  reinforce
