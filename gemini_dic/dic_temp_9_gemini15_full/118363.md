meritmonger:/ˈmɛrɪtˌmʌŋɡər/| n. |沽名钓誉者，只顾个人利益的人| A person who excessively emphasizes or boasts about their merits or achievements, often to gain personal advantage.  They may be self-righteous and focus on their own accomplishments while disregarding the contributions of others.|例句：He's a real meritmonger, always talking about his awards and achievements. 他是个十足的沽名钓誉者，总是吹嘘自己的奖项和成就。


例句：The meritmonger took all the credit for the team's success, ignoring the hard work of his colleagues.  这个沽名钓誉者把团队成功的全部功劳都揽到了自己身上，忽略了同事们的辛勤工作。


近义词：boaster, braggart, self-promoter, egotist, show-off


反义词：humble, modest, selfless, altruistic


词性：noun
