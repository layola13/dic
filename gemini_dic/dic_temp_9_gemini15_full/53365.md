disaffiliates:/dɪs.əˈfɪl.i.eɪts/| v.|使脱离关系；使脱钩；使不再隶属|To formally withdraw from an affiliation or association; to sever a connection or relationship.|The university disaffiliated itself from the controversial research group.  大学与该有争议的研究小组脱离了关系。|The smaller company disaffiliated from the larger corporation after a dispute.  小型公司在发生纠纷后与大型公司脱钩。|近义词：separate, sever, withdraw from|反义词：affiliate, associate, connect

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disaffiliates from the trade association annually. = 这家公司每年都脱离该行业协会。
He disaffiliates himself from any political party. = 他与任何政党都保持距离。

一般过去时:
The club disaffiliated from the national organization last year. = 俱乐部去年脱离了全国性组织。
She disaffiliated herself from the project due to ethical concerns. = 由于伦理方面的顾虑，她退出了该项目。

现在完成时:
The school has disaffiliated from the governing body. = 该学校已经脱离了管理机构。
They have disaffiliated themselves from the campaign. = 他们已经与该运动脱钩了。

过去完成时:
The group had disaffiliated before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，该组织就已经脱离了。
He had disaffiliated himself from the company long before the bankruptcy. = 早在破产之前，他就已经与该公司脱离了关系。

将来时:
The organization will disaffiliate from the parent company next year. = 该组织明年将脱离母公司。
She will disaffiliate herself from the project after completing her report. = 完成报告后，她将退出该项目。

虚拟语气:
If the team disaffiliated, they would lose funding. = 如果球队脱离关系，他们将失去资金。
I wish the organization had disaffiliated sooner. = 我希望该组织早点脱离关系就好了。

被动语态:
The branch office was disaffiliated by the headquarters. = 分公司被总部取消了隶属关系。
The student was disaffiliated from the university for violating the code of conduct. = 该学生因违反行为准则而被大学开除。


疑问句:
Will the organization disaffiliate soon? = 该组织很快就会脱离关系吗？
Has the club disaffiliated yet? = 俱乐部已经脱离关系了吗？

否定句:
The union did not disaffiliate from the parent organization. = 工会没有脱离母组织。
The team will not disaffiliate despite the pressure. = 尽管面临压力，该团队也不会脱离关系。
