sleeking:/ˈsliːkɪŋ/| v.|使光滑；使顺滑；打扮； |To make something smooth and shiny, or to make oneself look neat and stylish.  |例句：She spent hours sleeking her hair before the party. 她花了几个小时在派对前梳理她的头发。
例句：He sleeked down his unruly hair with gel. 他用发胶把蓬乱的头发梳理服帖。|近义词：smoothing, polishing, grooming |反义词：roughening, tousling, disheveling
