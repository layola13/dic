drawing-boards: /ˈdrɔːɪŋbɔːrdz/ | n. |画板；制图板 | A flat, usually rectangular surface used for drawing or drafting.  It often has a smooth surface and may be adjustable in angle. | Example: The architect carefully sketched the building plans on the drawing-boards. 建筑师仔细地在画板上勾勒出建筑图纸。The engineers used large drawing-boards to create detailed blueprints.工程师们使用大型画板来创建详细的蓝图。|近义词：drafting board, drawing table |反义词：None
