coluber:/ˈkoʊlʊbər/| n. |蛇（尤指游蛇科的蛇）|A coluber is a genus of non-venomous snakes, mostly found in Europe, Asia, and Africa. They are known for their slender bodies and smooth scales.  Many species are harmless to humans. |Example: The researcher observed a coluber basking in the sun. 研究人员观察到一条游蛇在阳光下晒太阳。|近义词：snake, serpent |反义词：无
