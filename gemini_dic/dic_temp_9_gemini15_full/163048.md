preclinical:/ˌpriːˈklɪnɪkəl/| adj.|临床前的；临床试验前的| Relating to or denoting the stage of research and development before clinical trials begin on humans.  This includes in vitro studies (test tube experiments) and in vivo studies (animal testing).|  例句：Preclinical studies showed promising results for the new drug. 临床前研究显示这种新药的结果很有希望。

例句：The company is currently conducting preclinical trials on the new cancer drug.  这家公司目前正在对这种新型抗癌药物进行临床前试验。


近义词：preliminary, prior to clinical, experimental


反义词：clinical, postclinical


词性：形容词
