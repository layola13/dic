harassed:/ˈhærəst/ | v. adj. | 骚扰的；受折磨的；使烦恼 |  To worry, disturb, or annoy someone persistently.  To subject someone to aggressive pressure or intimidation. |例句：She felt harassed by the constant phone calls. 她被不断的电话骚扰得受不了了。 He was harassed by debt. 他被债务缠身，苦不堪言。|近义词：pestered, bothered, tormented, stressed, overwhelmed |反义词：comforted, relaxed, soothed, calm


USAGE->

He constantly harasses his colleagues. = 他不断骚扰他的同事。
They harass her with rude comments. = 他们用粗鲁的言辞骚扰她。
