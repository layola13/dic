latinos:/læˈtiːnoʊs/| n. |拉丁美洲人（复数）|People of Latin American descent.  The term is often used in the United States to refer to people of Hispanic or Latino origin, though it can be a source of debate due to its broad inclusivity and potential exclusion of certain groups. |Example: Many Latinos celebrate Cinco de Mayo. 许多拉丁美洲人庆祝五一节。 The event attracted a large number of Latinos from across the state. 此活动吸引了来自该州各地的大量拉丁裔人士。|近义词：Hispanics (often used interchangeably, but with nuances in meaning), Latinx (a more inclusive term)|反义词：None directly applicable, as it describes a group, not a characteristic.
