preemotionally: /ˌpriːɪˈmoʊʃənəli/ | adv. | 预先情感地，情感预先地 |  Describes actions or states influenced by emotions felt before the event or situation itself. It suggests a proactive emotional response rather than a reactive one.  | 例句：He preemotionally prepared for the rejection, stockpiling self-esteem boosters. 他预先为被拒绝做好了准备，储备了增强自尊心的方法。  He preemotionally judged the situation, letting his fear cloud his judgment. 他预先对情况做出了判断，让恐惧蒙蔽了他的判断。 | 近义词：proactively emotionally, anticipatorily emotionally | 反义词：reactively, unemotionally
