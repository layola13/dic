rorqual:/ˈrɔːrkwɒl/| n. |须鲸；鳁鲸|A rorqual is a baleen whale characterized by its pleated throat grooves, which expand greatly when the whale feeds. Rorquals are known for their impressive size and their method of lunge feeding, where they scoop up large amounts of water containing krill or other small prey.  |Example: The blue whale is the largest rorqual and the largest animal on Earth.|近义词: baleen whale |反义词:toothed whale
