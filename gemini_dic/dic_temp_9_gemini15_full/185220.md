retracted:/rɪˈtræktɪd/ | adj. v. |收回的；撤回的；缩回的 | Withdrawn or taken back; pulled back or in. | The retracted article was criticized for its factual inaccuracies.  这篇被撤回的文章因其事实上的不准确而受到批评。The snake retracted its fangs. 蛇缩回了它的毒牙。| withdrawn, canceled, rescinded | published, extended, advanced | adj. v.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The scientist retracts his claims. = 科学家撤回了他的说法。
He retracts his hand from the hot stove. = 他把手从热炉子上缩了回来。


一般过去时:
The journal retracted the paper after discovering errors. = 期刊在发现错误后撤回了论文。
The turtle retracted its head into its shell. = 乌龟把头缩进了壳里。


现在完成时:
The company has retracted its offer. = 公司已经撤回了它的提议。
She has retracted her statement about the accident. = 她已经收回了她关于事故的声明。


过去完成时:
They had retracted their support before the vote. = 他们在投票前就已经撤回了他们的支持。
The cat had retracted its claws after catching the mouse. = 猫在抓住老鼠后已经缩回了爪子。


将来时:
The newspaper will retract the story if proven false. = 如果被证明是假的，报纸将撤回这篇报道。
He will retract his arm if it gets too close to the fire. = 如果他的手臂离火太近，他会缩回去。


虚拟语气:
If he had known the truth, he would have retracted his statement. = 如果他知道真相，他就会收回他的声明。
If the evidence had been stronger, they would have retracted their decision. = 如果证据更充分，他们就会撤回他们的决定。


被动语态:
The article was retracted due to plagiarism. = 这篇文章由于剽窃而被撤回。
His hand was retracted quickly from the hot surface. = 他的手迅速从热表面缩了回来。


疑问句:
Has the statement been retracted? = 声明已被撤回了吗？
Was his hand retracted in time? = 他的手及时缩回去了吗？


否定句:
The author did not retract his controversial claims. = 作者没有收回他引起争议的说法。
The dog did not retract its teeth during the play. = 狗在玩耍时没有缩回它的牙齿。
