plumpest:/ˈplʌmpɪst/| adj.|最丰满的，最圆润的|Most plump; fullest; roundest.  The adjective "plump" describes someone or something as pleasantly fat or rounded. "Plumpest" is the superlative form, indicating the highest degree of plumpness.|例句：She has the plumpest cheeks I've ever seen. 她拥有我见过的最丰满的脸颊。

例句：Of all the apples, this one is the plumpest. 在所有苹果中，这个最丰满。


近义词：fullest, roundest, fattest, chubbiest

反义词：thinnest, skinniest, leanest

词性：形容词
