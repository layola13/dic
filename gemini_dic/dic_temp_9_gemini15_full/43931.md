crewmanship:/ˈkruːmənʃɪp/| n. |船员技艺；团队合作能力|The skill and efficiency of a crew in working together, especially on a ship or aircraft.  It encompasses teamwork, coordination, and the ability to handle emergencies effectively.|例句：The captain praised the crew's exceptional crewmanship during the storm. 船长称赞船员在暴风雨中的出色船员技艺。


例句：Their excellent crewmanship ensured a smooth and efficient voyage. 他们出色的团队合作能力确保了航行的顺利和高效。|近义词：teamwork, coordination, collaboration|反义词：disorganization, incompetence, discord
