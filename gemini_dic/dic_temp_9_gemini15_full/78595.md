gibaro:/ˈxɪbərəʊ/| n. |吉巴罗人（波多黎各山区农民）|A Gibaro is a person from the mountainous regions of Puerto Rico, traditionally a farmer or rural inhabitant.  They are known for their resilience, strong cultural identity, and often marginalized status. The term can sometimes carry a connotation of being rustic or unsophisticated, but it is increasingly reclaimed as a source of pride.|The Gibaro farmers worked tirelessly to cultivate their land. 吉巴罗农民辛勤耕作土地。| campesino, peasant |  (no direct antonym, but terms like "city dweller" or "elite" contrast)|名词
