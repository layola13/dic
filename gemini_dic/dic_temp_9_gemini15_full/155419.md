pheasant:/ˈfɛzənt/| n. |雉鸡，环颈雉|A pheasant is a type of game bird known for its colorful plumage.  They are often hunted for sport and their meat is considered a delicacy. |例句：The hunter shot a magnificent pheasant in the woods.  猎人射杀了一只美丽的雉鸡。|The farmer saw a flock of pheasants near his field. 农夫在田地附近看到一群雉鸡。|近义词：game bird,fowl|反义词：无|名词
