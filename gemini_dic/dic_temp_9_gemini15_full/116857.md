maximizers:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzərz/| n. |最大化者；追求完美者|People who strive for the absolute best option available, even if it means sacrificing efficiency or satisfaction. They tend to compare and contrast extensively before making decisions.|例句：Maximizers often experience decision paralysis because they're constantly searching for the perfect choice. 最大化者经常会经历决策瘫痪，因为他们一直在寻找完美的选择。|例句：Unlike satisficers, maximizers spend considerable time evaluating all possible options. 与满足者不同，最大化者会花费大量时间评估所有可能的选项。|近义词：perfectionists, optimizers|反义词：satisficers
