logged:/lɒɡd/ /lɑːɡd/| v. adj.|记录的；登录的；砍伐的|To record (information) in a systematic way; to enter (a computer system) by providing a username and password;  to cut down (trees).|例句：I logged the details of the meeting in my notebook.  我把会议的细节记录在我的笔记本上了。  I logged into my email account. 我登录了我的邮箱账户。|近义词：recorded, registered, signed in, felled|反义词：unlogged, unregistered, unsigned out


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I log my expenses every day. = 我每天记录我的开支。
He logs into the system twice a day. = 他一天登录系统两次。

一般过去时:
She logged the incident in the report. = 她将事件记录在报告中。
They logged out of the system after work. = 他们下班后退出系统。


现在完成时:
I have logged all the data. = 我已经记录了所有数据。
He has logged in successfully. = 他已经成功登录。


过去完成时:
She had logged the information before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，她已经记录了信息。
They had logged off before the system crashed. = 系统崩溃前，他们已经注销了。


将来时:
I will log the results tomorrow. = 我明天将记录结果。
He will log in from home. = 他将在家里登录。


虚拟语气:
If I logged the data correctly, the analysis would be accurate. = 如果我正确记录了数据，分析就会准确。
I wish I had logged the call. = 我真希望我记录了这个电话。


被动语态:
The data was logged automatically. = 数据被自动记录。
The system is logged into remotely. = 系统正被远程登录。


疑问句:
Have you logged the changes? = 你记录了这些更改了吗？
Did he log in this morning? = 他今天早上登录了吗？


否定句:
I didn't log the call. = 我没有记录这个电话。
She hasn't logged in yet. = 她还没登录。


PHRASE->
logged in = 登录
logged out = 注销
logged on = 登录 (同义于logged in)
logged off = 注销 (同义于logged out)
fully logged = 完全记录
