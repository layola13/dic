epidemic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk/| n. |流行病，疫病 |A widespread occurrence of an infectious disease in a community at a particular time.  |例句：The flu epidemic swept through the city.  流感疫情席卷了这座城市。|例句：The country is facing a serious epidemic of drug abuse. 这个国家正面临严重的吸毒疫情。|近义词：outbreak, plague, pandemic |反义词：endemic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
An epidemic spreads rapidly. = 流行病传播迅速。
The epidemic affects many people. = 流行病影响很多人。

一般过去时:
A severe epidemic occurred last year. = 去年发生了一次严重的疫情。
The epidemic killed thousands of people. = 疫情导致数千人死亡。

现在完成时:
The government has controlled the epidemic. = 政府已经控制住了疫情。
Many measures have been taken to prevent the epidemic. = 已经采取了许多措施来预防疫情。

过去完成时:
Before the vaccine was developed, the epidemic had already spread widely. = 在疫苗研制出来之前，疫情已经广泛传播了。
By the time help arrived, the epidemic had claimed many lives. = 当援助到达时，疫情已经夺走了许多人的生命。

将来时:
Experts predict another epidemic will occur next year. = 专家预测明年还会发生另一场疫情。
The new virus may cause a global epidemic. = 新病毒可能会引发全球性疫情。

虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, the epidemic might have been less severe. = 如果我们早点采取行动，疫情可能就不会那么严重了。
I wish the epidemic would end soon. = 我希望疫情能尽快结束。

被动语态:
The epidemic was quickly contained. = 疫情很快得到了控制。
The spread of the epidemic is being monitored closely. = 疫情的蔓延正在受到密切监控。

疑问句:
Is there an epidemic in the region? = 这个地区有疫情吗？
What caused the epidemic? = 导致疫情的原因是什么？

否定句:
There is no epidemic in this area. = 这个地区没有疫情。
The government claims there is no epidemic. = 政府声称没有疫情。
