readjusting:/ˌriːəˈdʒʌstɪŋ/| v.|重新调整；重新调整；校正 |The process of making something fit or work correctly again after it has been changed or disturbed.  It implies a return to a previous state or a new state of equilibrium. |例句：He was readjusting his tie. 他正在重新整理领带。


例句：The company is readjusting its marketing strategy. 公司正在重新调整其营销策略。


近义词：realigning, recalibrating, readapting


反义词：disrupting, deranging, disorganizing


词性：动词 (现在分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are readjusting the schedule. = 他们正在重新调整日程安排。
The mechanic is readjusting the brakes. = 技工正在重新调整刹车。


一般过去时:
She readjusted her glasses. = 她重新调整了眼镜。
We readjusted the furniture in the living room. = 我们重新调整了客厅的家具摆放。


现在完成时:
He has readjusted his expectations. = 他已经重新调整了他的期望。
The team has readjusted its game plan. = 球队已经重新调整了比赛计划。


过去完成时:
They had readjusted the budget before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经重新调整了预算。
She had readjusted her position before the photo was taken. = 在拍照之前，她已经重新调整了姿势。


将来时:
We will readjust the equipment next week. = 我们下周将重新调整设备。
They will readjust their approach to the problem. = 他们将重新调整解决问题的方法。


虚拟语气:
If they readjusted their strategy, they might succeed. = 如果他们重新调整策略，他们可能会成功。
I wish I had readjusted my plans earlier. = 我真希望我早点重新调整我的计划。


被动语态:
The system is being readjusted. = 系统正在被重新调整。
The prices were readjusted after the investigation. = 调查之后，价格被重新调整了。


疑问句:
Are they readjusting the timetable? = 他们正在重新调整时间表吗？
Is the machine being readjusted correctly? = 机器正在被正确地重新调整吗？


否定句:
He did not readjust his thinking. = 他没有重新调整他的想法。
They haven't readjusted the software yet. = 他们还没有重新调整软件。


PHRASE->
