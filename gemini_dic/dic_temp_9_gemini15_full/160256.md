pontlevis:/pɒ̃tleviː/| n.|吊桥|A pontlevis is a type of drawbridge that is raised and lowered vertically.  It consists of a heavy platform or gate that is hinged at one end and lifted by ropes, chains, or a counterweight.  Pontlevis were commonly used in medieval fortifications.| Example: The soldiers raised the pontlevis to prevent the enemy from entering the castle.  The old castle was famous for its well-preserved pontlevis.|近义词：drawbridge|反义词：fixed bridge
