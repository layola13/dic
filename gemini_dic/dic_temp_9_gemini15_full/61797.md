emphasizing: /ˈɛmfəsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 強調，著重 |  The act of giving special importance or prominence to something.  It highlights or stresses a particular point or aspect. |例句：He was emphasizing the importance of punctuality. 他正在强调准时的重要性。
例句：The teacher emphasizing the key concepts in the lesson.老师强调了课堂上的重点概念。|近义词： stressing, highlighting, underscoring, accentuating |反义词： downplaying, minimizing, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is emphasizing the need for change. 他正在强调改变的必要性。
She emphasizes the importance of education. 她强调教育的重要性。


一般过去时:
The speaker emphasized the urgency of the situation. 演讲者强调了局势的紧迫性。
They emphasized the need for immediate action. 他们强调需要立即采取行动。


现在完成时:
She has emphasized this point repeatedly. 她已经反复强调了这一点。
He has emphasized the importance of teamwork throughout the project. 他在整个项目中都强调了团队合作的重要性。


过去完成时:
The report had emphasized the risks involved. 报告已经强调了所涉及的风险。
They had emphasized the need for caution before the experiment. 他们在实验前强调了谨慎的必要性。


将来时:
She will emphasize the benefits of the new program. 她将强调新项目的益处。
He will emphasize the importance of customer satisfaction. 他将强调客户满意度的重要性。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would emphasize the positive aspects. 如果我是你，我会强调积极的方面。
I wish he had emphasized the safety procedures more clearly. 我希望他更清楚地强调安全程序。


被动语态:
The need for reform was emphasized by the committee. 改革的必要性受到了委员会的强调。
The importance of detail is emphasized in this course. 这门课程强调细节的重要性。


疑问句:
What are you emphasizing in your presentation? 你在你的演示文稿中强调什么？
What point is being emphasized here? 这里强调的是什么观点？


否定句:
He didn't emphasize the negative aspects. 他没有强调消极的方面。
The report did not emphasize the long-term consequences. 报告没有强调长远的影响。
