encrypts:/ɪnˈkrɪpts/| v.|加密，编码|To convert (information or data) into a code, especially to prevent unauthorized access.|The software encrypts all sensitive data before transmission.  软件在传输之前对所有敏感数据进行加密。|The government encrypts classified documents to protect them from leaks. 政府对机密文件进行加密以防止泄露。|decode, cipher, code|decrypt|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The system encrypts data automatically. = 系统自动加密数据。
She encrypts her emails before sending them. = 她发送电子邮件之前会对其进行加密。

一般过去时:
He encrypted the file using a strong password. = 他使用强密码加密了文件。
They encrypted the message to keep it secret. = 他们加密了信息以使其保密。

现在完成时:
We have encrypted all the customer data. = 我们已经加密了所有客户数据。
She has encrypted the document multiple times to enhance security. = 她已经多次加密了该文档以增强安全性。

过去完成时:
They had encrypted the data before the hacker attempted to access it. = 黑客试图访问数据之前，他们已经对其进行了加密。
The company had encrypted its financial records for years. = 这家公司多年来一直对其财务记录进行加密。

将来时:
We will encrypt the database before launching the application. = 我们将在启动应用程序之前加密数据库。
He will encrypt the message using a new algorithm. = 他将使用一种新的算法来加密信息。
