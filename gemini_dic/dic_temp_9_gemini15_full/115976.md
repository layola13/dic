mars:/mɑːrz/|n.|火星|The fourth planet from the sun, a cold, desert world.  Mars is known for its reddish appearance due to iron oxide on its surface.  It is a subject of ongoing scientific exploration, with missions searching for evidence of past or present life.|例句：NASA is planning another mission to Mars. 美国宇航局正计划另一次火星任务。

例句：Scientists are studying Martian soil samples to search for signs of life. 科学家们正在研究火星土壤样本以寻找生命迹象。|近义词：the red planet|反义词：无
