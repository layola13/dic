sakura:/sɑːˈkuːrɑː/| n. |樱花；樱花树|Japanese cherry blossom;  a tree of the genus Prunus, especially the Japanese cherry, prized for its profuse display of delicate pink or white flowers.  The word often refers to the flowers themselves. |例句：The sakura trees were in full bloom, a breathtaking sight. 樱花盛开，景色令人叹为观止。  The delicate petals of the sakura drifted down like pink snow. 樱花的娇嫩花瓣像粉红色的雪一样飘落下来。|近义词：cherry blossom|反义词：无
