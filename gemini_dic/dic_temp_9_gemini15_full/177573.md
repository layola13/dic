rebrew:/ˌriːˈbruː/| vt.|再酿造；重新酿造|To brew again; to brew something again.  This word is a relatively recent coinage, combining "re-" (again) with "brew".|例句：We're going to rebrew the award-winning stout for next year's festival. 我们打算为明年的节日再次酿造屡获殊荣的世涛啤酒。

例句：The microbrewery decided to rebrew their popular IPA after it sold out quickly. 这家小型啤酒厂决定在广受欢迎的印度淡色艾尔啤酒售罄后再次酿造。|近义词：remake, redo|反义词：discard, dispose of|动词


USAGE->

He rebrews his best-selling beer every month. 他每个月都重新酿造他最畅销的啤酒。
They rebrew the ale using a new recipe. 他们用新的配方重新酿造这种麦芽酒。
