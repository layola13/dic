domesday:/ˈduːmzdeɪ/| n.|世界末日；大审判日；大规模调查；|The noun "domesday" refers to the Day of Judgment in Christian theology, but it also refers to a comprehensive survey.  The most famous usage is the Domesday Book, a record of land ownership in England commissioned by William the Conqueror.|例句：The Domesday Book provides invaluable information about medieval England.  都斯伯里书提供了关于中世纪英格兰的宝贵信息。The apocalyptic movie depicted a vivid domesday scenario. 这部末日电影描绘了一个生动的世界末日场景。|近义词：judgment day, apocalypse, survey, census|反义词：creation, beginning, genesis
