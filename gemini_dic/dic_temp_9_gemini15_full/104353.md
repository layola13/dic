juxtaposition:/ˌdʒʌkstəˈpəʊʒən/| n.|并置；并列；对比|The act or instance of placing two or more things side by side, often to compare or contrast them.  It often highlights the differences between the elements, creating a surprising or thought-provoking effect. |例句：The juxtaposition of the ancient temple and the modern skyscraper was striking. 古老的寺庙和现代摩天大楼并置在一起，景象令人惊叹。

例句：The painting uses juxtaposition of light and dark to create a dramatic effect. 这幅画运用明暗对比来营造戏剧性的效果。


近义词：contrast, comparison, apposition

反义词：separation, isolation, disjunction

词性: noun
