memorialising: /məˈmɔːriəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 纪念；使不朽；记录；铭记 |  The act of commemorating or preserving the memory of someone or something.  It often involves creating a lasting record or tribute. |


例句：

The city is memorialising the victims of the earthquake with a new park. = 城市正在用一个新的公园来纪念地震的受害者。

They are memorialising the event in a special exhibition. = 他们正在一个特别的展览中纪念这一事件。


近义词：commemorating, celebrating, remembering, recording, preserving


反义词：forgetting, ignoring, neglecting


词性: 动词
