nonspontaneousness:/ˌnɑːnˌspɑːnˈteɪniəsnəs/| n. |非自发性，不自发性 |The lack of spontaneity; the quality or state of not being spontaneous.  It often refers to processes or reactions that require an external input of energy to occur. |例句：The reaction exhibits a high degree of nonspontaneousness, requiring significant energy input to proceed.  反应表现出高度的非自发性，需要大量的能量输入才能进行。

The crystallization of a supersaturated solution is an example of nonspontaneousness under certain conditions.  在某些条件下，过饱和溶液的结晶就是一个非自发性的例子。|近义词：non-spontaneity, involuntariness |反义词：spontaneity
