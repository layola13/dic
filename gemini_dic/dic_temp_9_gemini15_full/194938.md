semi-liquid: /ˈsɛmiˈlɪkwɪd/ | adj. | 半液体的，半流动的 |  Having the consistency of a substance that is between a liquid and a solid; neither completely liquid nor completely solid.  | 例句：The mud was semi-liquid, making it difficult to walk through.  泥浆是半流动的，很难走过去。 The lava flowed as a semi-liquid mass down the volcano's side.  熔岩以半液态的形态流下山。 | 近义词：viscous, pasty, fluid | 反义词：solid, rigid
