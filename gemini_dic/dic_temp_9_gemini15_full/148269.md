oversmoked:/ˌoʊvərˈsmoʊkt/| adj.|烟熏过度的；熏过头了的|Excessively smoked, often resulting in a bitter or unpleasant taste or smell.  This usually refers to food that has been exposed to too much smoke during the smoking process. |The salmon was oversmoked and tasted bitter.  熏鲑鱼熏过头了，尝起来很苦。 The ribs were oversmoked, rendering them inedible.  排骨熏过头了，变得不能吃了。|undersmoked|well-smoked|形容词
