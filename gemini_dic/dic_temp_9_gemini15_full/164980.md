prehardening:/ˌpriːˈhɑːrdənɪŋ/| n. | 预硬化 | The process of partially hardening a metal before its final heat treatment.  This increases its strength and hardness while maintaining some ductility.  | Example: Prehardening the steel improved its machinability and reduced distortion during the final hardening process. 预硬化钢材提高了其可加工性和减少了最终硬化过程中的变形。 | 近义词:pre-strengthening, preliminary hardening | 反义词:softening, annealing | 名词
