forgivenesses:/fərˈɡɪvnəsɪz/| n. |宽恕；赦免（复数）|The plural form of forgiveness, referring to multiple acts of forgiving or instances of being forgiven.  It emphasizes the repetition or multiplicity of the act of forgiving.|例句：His numerous forgivenesses of her mistakes eventually wore him down.  他多次原谅她的错误最终让他精疲力尽。|例句：The forgivenesses offered by the community helped the victims heal. 社区的多次原谅帮助受害者治愈。|近义词：pardons, remissions, absolutions|反义词：condemnations, censures, punishments|名词
