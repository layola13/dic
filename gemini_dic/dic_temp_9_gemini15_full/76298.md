gabbing:/ˈɡæbɪŋ/| v.|喋喋不休地谈话；闲聊 |  To talk casually and continuously, often about unimportant things.  |They were gabbing away happily about their weekend plans.  他们兴高采烈地聊着他们的周末计划。| chatting, gossiping, yakking | listening,  quiet | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are gabbing on the phone. = 他们在电话里喋喋不休。
We gab every day at lunch. = 我们每天午餐时间都会聊天。

一般过去时:
They gabbed about their favorite movies. = 他们聊着他们最喜欢的电影。
She gabbed with her friends for hours. = 她和朋友们聊了几个小时。

现在完成时:
We have been gabbing for hours. = 我们已经聊了几个小时了。
They have gabbed about this topic before. = 他们之前就讨论过这个话题了。

过去完成时:
They had gabbed for a while before I joined them. = 我加入他们之前，他们已经聊了一会儿了。
She had gabbed with her sister on the phone before going to bed. = 她睡觉前和姐姐在电话里聊了一会儿。

将来时:
We will be gabbing all night long. = 我们将整夜聊天。
They will gab about the party later. = 他们稍后会聊派对的事。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would gab with you. = 如果我有时间，我会和你聊聊。
I wish I could gab with my old friends. = 我真希望我能和我的老朋友们聊聊天。

被动语态:
(Gabbing is rarely used in the passive voice.)  (喋喋不休很少用被动语态。)


疑问句:
Are they gabbing again? = 他们又在喋喋不休了吗？
Were they gabbing during the meeting? = 会议期间他们一直在聊天吗？

否定句:
They weren't gabbing; they were working. = 他们不是在聊天；他们在工作。
We don't usually gab during meetings. = 我们通常不会在会议期间聊天。


PHRASE->

gabbing away: 喋喋不休地说着
gabbing incessantly: 不停地闲聊
gabbing about: 闲聊关于...
gabbing over: 因为...而闲聊
gabbing with: 和...闲聊
