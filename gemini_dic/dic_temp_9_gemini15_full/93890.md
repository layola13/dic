immortalising: /ɪˈmɔːrtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使不朽；使永垂不朽 |  The act of making someone or something immortal or famous for a long time; making something last forever in memory or history. | 例句：His heroic actions immortalized him in the hearts of his people.  他的英勇事迹使他在人民心中永垂不朽。

His paintings immortalise the beauty of the Tuscan countryside. 他的画作使托斯卡纳乡村的美景永垂不朽。


近义词：memorializing, perpetuating, preserving


反义词：obliterating, forgetting, destroying


词性：动词 (现在分词)
