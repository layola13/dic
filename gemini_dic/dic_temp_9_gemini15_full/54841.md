displaced:/dɪsˈpleɪst/| adj., v.|流离失所的；移位的；取代的；|removed from one's usual place of residence; moved from its usual or proper place; replaced;  |例句：The war displaced thousands of people from their homes.  战争使成千上万的人流离失所。|近义词：removed, relocated, replaced, ousted |反义词：settled, established, in place


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flood displaces many families from their homes. = 洪水使许多家庭流离失所。
The new manager displaces the old one. = 新经理取代了旧经理。


一般过去时:
The earthquake displaced thousands of people. = 地震使数千人流离失所。
The old system was displaced by a new one. = 旧系统被新系统取代了。


现在完成时:
Millions have been displaced by the conflict. = 数百万人在冲突中流离失所。
The traditional methods have been displaced by modern technology. = 传统方法已被现代技术取代。


过去完成时:
They had been displaced from their land for generations. = 他们世世代代都被迫离开家园。
The old building had been displaced by a modern skyscraper. = 旧建筑已被一座现代摩天大楼取代。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will displace coastal communities. = 海平面上升将使沿海社区流离失所。
The new software will displace the older version. = 新软件将取代旧版本。


虚拟语气:
If the dam broke, it would displace millions of people. = 如果大坝决堤，将会有数百万人流离失所。
If we had acted sooner, the old system wouldn't have been displaced so quickly. = 如果我们早点采取行动，旧系统就不会这么快被取代。


被动语态:
Many people were displaced by the hurricane. = 许多人被飓风迫使离开家园。
The old chair was displaced by a new one. = 旧椅子被新椅子取代了。


疑问句:
Were many people displaced by the storm? = 暴风雨中很多人流离失所了吗？
Will the old technology be displaced soon? = 旧技术很快会被取代吗？


否定句:
The new policy did not displace the old one. = 新政策并没有取代旧政策。
The refugees were not displaced by choice. = 难民并非自愿流离失所。


PHRASE->
internally displaced person (IDP) = 国内流离失所者
displaced persons camp = 流离失所者营地
emotionally displaced = 情感错位
socially displaced = 社会地位下降
geographically displaced = 地理位置改变
