precociously:/prɪˈkoʊʃəsli/| adv.|早熟地；过早地；早慧地 |In a way that shows precocity; prematurely.  It implies an advanced development or maturity beyond what is typical for one's age.|例句：The child precociously mastered calculus at age eight.  八岁的孩子过早地掌握了微积分。

例句：She precociously began writing poetry at the age of ten. 她十岁时就过早地开始写诗了。


近义词： prematurely, ahead of time, early, forwardly


反义词： belatedly, tardily, late


词性：副词
