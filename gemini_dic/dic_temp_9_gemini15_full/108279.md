laundry:/ˈlɔːndri/ /ˈlɔːnəri/ | n. | 洗衣，洗衣店，待洗的衣物 |  Laundry refers to clothing and other fabric items that need to be washed, the act of washing these items, or the place where washing is done.  |  I have a big pile of laundry to do this weekend. 我这周末有一大堆衣服要洗。  The laundry is overflowing. 洗衣篮都溢出来了。|  wash, washing |  cleanliness (反义词更偏向于状态而不是具体行为)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I do laundry every Saturday. = 我每个星期六都洗衣服。
She always does her laundry at the laundromat. = 她总是去自助洗衣店洗衣服。

一般过去时:
I did laundry yesterday. = 我昨天洗了衣服。
They did the laundry before going out. = 他们出门前洗了衣服。

现在完成时:
I have done the laundry already. = 我已经洗完衣服了。
She has not done her laundry this week. = 她这周还没洗衣服。

过去完成时:
I had done the laundry before he arrived. = 他来之前我已经洗完衣服了。
They had done their laundry before the party. = 他们在派对前洗完了衣服。

将来时:
I will do the laundry tomorrow. = 我明天会洗衣服。
She will have her laundry done at the cleaners. = 她会把衣服送到干洗店去洗。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would do the laundry. = 如果我有更多时间，我会洗衣服。
I wish I had already done the laundry. = 我真希望我已经洗完衣服了。

被动语态:
The laundry is done every week. = 洗衣每周都做。
The laundry was done by the maid. = 洗衣是由女佣做的。

疑问句:
Have you done the laundry yet? = 你洗衣服了吗？
Where do you do your laundry? = 你在哪里洗衣服？

否定句:
I didn't do the laundry today. = 我今天没洗衣服。
She hasn't done the laundry yet. = 她还没洗衣服。
