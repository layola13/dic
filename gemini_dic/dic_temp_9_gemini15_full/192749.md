seafaring:/ˈsiːfɛərɪŋ/| adj.|航海的；从事航海的|Relating to or involved in seafaring; nautical.|例句：The museum has a fascinating exhibit on seafaring history. 这个博物馆有一个关于航海史的迷人展览。
例句：His seafaring grandfather told many tales of adventure at sea. 他从事航海的祖父讲述了许多海上冒险的故事。|近义词：maritime, nautical, naval|反义词：terrestrial

一般现在时:
He is seafaring most of the year. 他一年大部分时间都在航海。
Seafaring traditions are still strong in this coastal community.  这个沿海社区的航海传统仍然很强大。

My ancestor was a seafaring captain in the 18th century. 我的祖先是一位18世纪的航海船长。

现在完成时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
