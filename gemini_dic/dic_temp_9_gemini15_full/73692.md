forgiveness:/fərˈɡɪvnəs/| n. |宽恕，原谅 |The act of forgiving or being forgiven.  It implies ceasing to feel resentment or anger toward someone for an offense.  Forgiveness can be offered or received, and it often involves a process of letting go of negative emotions. |例句：He asked for her forgiveness. 他请求她的原谅。

例句：The forgiveness of sins is a central tenet of many religions.  宽恕罪孽是许多宗教的核心信条。


近义词：pardon, absolution, leniency, mercy

反义词：vengeance, retribution, punishment
