filoplume:/ˈfɪləpluːm/| n. |细羽；绒羽；纤细的羽毛|A filoplume is a type of feather found in many birds, characterized by its thin, hair-like shaft with a few barbs near the tip.  They are often sensory in function, helping the bird detect air currents or touch. |Example: The bird's filoplumes were barely visible against its downy plumage.|近义词：hair-like feather, bristle|反义词：None
