intumescence: /ɪnˈtjuːməs(ə)ns/ | n. |肿胀；膨胀；隆起 | The swelling or enlarging of a part of the body; a process of swelling or becoming larger.  It often refers to a gradual increase in size or volume, not necessarily due to inflammation. | Example: The intumescence of the river after the heavy rain caused flooding.  暴雨过后河流的膨胀导致了洪水。 The intumescence of the bread dough was evident as it rose in the oven. 面包面团在烤箱里膨胀得很明显。 | 近义词：swelling, distension, inflation, tumefaction | 反义词：deflation, shrinkage, reduction
