These sources replenish themselves naturally, unlike fossil fuels. | Example: The government is investing heavily in renewabilities to reduce carbon emissions.  政府正在大力投资可再生能源以减少碳排放。 | 近义词: renewable energy sources, sustainable energy | 反义词: non-renewable resources, fossil fuels | 名词
