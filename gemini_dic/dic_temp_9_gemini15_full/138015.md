noon:/nuːn/| n.|正午，中午|The time of day when the sun is highest in the sky, twelve o'clock in the day.|例句：We had lunch at noon. 我们中午吃的午饭。|例句：The meeting is scheduled for noon tomorrow. 明天中午举行会议。|近义词： midday, high noon|反义词：midnight


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is noon. = 现在是中午。
Noon comes after eleven o'clock. = 十一点之后是中午。

一般过去时:
It was noon when we arrived. = 我们到达时是中午。
The sun was high at noon. = 中午太阳很高。

现在完成时:
Noon has already passed. = 中午已经过去了。
We have just passed noon. = 我们刚刚过了中午。

过去完成时:
It had been noon when the accident occurred. = 事故发生时已经是中午了。
We had already eaten lunch before noon. = 我们在中午之前已经吃过午饭了。

将来时:
It will be noon soon. = 马上就到中午了。
We will have lunch at noon tomorrow. = 我们明天中午吃午饭。

虚拟语气:
If it were noon, we would start our meeting. = 如果是中午，我们就开始会议。
I wish it were noon already. = 我希望现在已经是中午了。

被动语态:
Noon is often considered the hottest time of the day. = 中午通常被认为是一天中最热的时候。
(Noon itself cannot be acted upon passively in a meaningful way.)

疑问句:
Is it noon yet? = 现在是中午了吗？
What time is noon? = 中午几点？


否定句:
It is not yet noon. = 现在还没到中午。
It wasn't noon when the incident happened. = 事件发生时还不是中午。
