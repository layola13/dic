raccoon:/ræˈkuːn/|/ˈrækuːn/| n. |浣熊|A raccoon is a small North American mammal with grayish-brown fur, a bushy tail, and a mask-like marking around its eyes.  They are known for their intelligence and dexterity.  They are omnivores with a diet that includes fruits, nuts, insects, and small animals.|Example: I saw a raccoon rummaging through the trash last night.  昨晚我看到一只浣熊在垃圾桶里翻找东西。|近义词：coati, bandit (informal)|反义词:  N/A|名词
