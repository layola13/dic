mambos:/ˈmæmboʊz/| n.|曼波舞；曼波舞曲|A mambo is a type of dance music and dance that originated in Cuba.  It is characterized by its fast tempo, syncopated rhythms, and complex steps.  The music often incorporates elements of Afro-Cuban rhythms and jazz. The dance itself is known for its energetic and passionate movements.|例句：We learned to dance the mambo at the salsa club. 我们在萨尔萨舞俱乐部学会了跳曼波舞。

例句：The band played a lively mambo that got everyone on the dance floor.乐队演奏了一首欢快的曼波舞曲，让每个人都上了舞池。|近义词：rumba, cha-cha, salsa|反义词：无
