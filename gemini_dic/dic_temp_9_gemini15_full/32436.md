chimeras:/kaɪˈmɪrəz/| n.|嵌合体；幻想；妄想|A chimera is something that is unreal or imaginary, or a creature in Greek mythology that is made up of parts from different animals.  It can also refer to an organism containing cells from two or more distinct individuals or species. |例句：The project was a chimera from the start, doomed to failure. 这个项目从一开始就是一个幻想，注定要失败。

例句：Genetic engineering can create chimeras with different types of cells. 基因工程可以创造具有不同类型细胞的嵌合体。


近义词：fantasy, illusion, delusion, hybrid

反义词：reality, truth


词性:名词
