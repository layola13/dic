gagers:/ˈɡeɪdʒərz/| n. |测量员；估价者；（液体）流量计|A gager is a person who measures or assesses something, often liquids or the flow of liquids, or who assesses the value of something.|The customs gager checked the contents of the shipping containers.|近义词：measurer, assessor, evaluator|反义词：N/A
