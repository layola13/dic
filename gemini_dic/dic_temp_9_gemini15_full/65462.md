/ˈjʊərəʊˌɛmpɪ/| n. |欧洲议会议员|Member of the European Parliament.  A Member of the European Parliament (MEP) is an elected representative in the European Parliament, the legislative branch of the European Union.  MEPs are directly elected by citizens of the EU member states. |Example:  She's a Euro-MP representing the south of France. 她是一位代表法国南部的欧洲议会议员。|近义词: MEP (Member of the European Parliament)|反义词:  None  |名词
