retributory:/rɪˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ | adj. | 报应的；惩罚性的 |  Giving or receiving punishment; relating to or characteristic of retribution.  It implies a deserved punishment for wrongdoing. |


例句：

The judge handed down a retributive sentence, reflecting the severity of the crime. = 法官作出了惩罚性的判决，反映了罪行的严重性。

His actions had retributive consequences, leading to his downfall. = 他的行为带来了报应，导致了他的垮台。


近义词：punitive, avenging, vengeful

反义词：forgiving, merciful, lenient


词性：形容词
