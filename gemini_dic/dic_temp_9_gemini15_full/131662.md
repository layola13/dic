nonconcentrative:/ˌnɒnkɒnsɛnˈtreɪtɪv/| adj.|非浓缩的，非主动转运的|Not requiring energy to move substances across a cell membrane.  This is in contrast to active transport, which does require energy. Nonconcentrative transport mechanisms typically move substances down their concentration gradient (from an area of high concentration to an area of low concentration).|例句：Nonconcentrative transport plays a significant role in the absorption of certain nutrients.  非浓缩转运在某些营养物质的吸收中起着重要作用。

The movement of water across a cell membrane through aquaporins is an example of nonconcentrative transport. 水通过水通道蛋白穿过细胞膜的运动是非浓缩转运的一个例子。|近义词：passive, facilitated|反义词：active, concentrative
