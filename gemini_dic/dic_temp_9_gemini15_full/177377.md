reauthenticates:/ˌriːɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪts/| vt.|重新验证；重新认证|To verify or authenticate something again; to confirm its authenticity a second time.|例句：The system reauthenticates the user every hour to ensure security.  系统每小时重新验证用户身份以确保安全。

例句：The museum reauthenticates its collection periodically to prevent forgeries. 博物馆定期重新验证其藏品以防止赝品。


近义词：reverify, revalidate, reaffirm


反义词：invalidate, discredit


词性：动词
