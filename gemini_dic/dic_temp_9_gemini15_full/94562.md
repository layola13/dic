impotently:/ɪmˈpɑːtəntli/ | adv. | 无力地；无效地 | Ineffectively; without power to produce any effect.  |例句：He struggled impotently against the current. 他无力地挣扎着对抗水流。

例句：She watched impotently as the fire spread. 她无力地看着大火蔓延。


近义词：powerlessly, ineffectually, fruitlessly, weakly

反义词：effectively, powerfully, successfully
