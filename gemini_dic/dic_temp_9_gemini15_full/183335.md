reprobate:/ˈrɛprəbeɪt/| n., adj., v.| 堕落者；谴责；责备 | A reprobate is a person who is morally depraved or wicked.  The word can also be used as an adjective to describe something morally corrupt, or as a verb meaning to disapprove of or condemn. |例句：He was a confirmed reprobate, given to drink and debauchery. 他是个十足的堕落者，沉迷于酒色。  The judge reprobated the defendant's actions. 法官谴责了被告的行为。|近义词：scoundrel, villain, rascal, depraved, wicked|反义词：saint, angel, virtuous, righteous| noun, adjective, verb
