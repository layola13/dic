debutant:/ˈdɛbjuːtɑːnt/| n.|初次登台者；初露头角者；（尤指）首次参加社交活动的年轻女子|A debutant is a person, especially a young woman, making their first appearance in society or a particular field.  The term often refers to a young woman making her debut at a formal social event. |例句：She was a beautiful debutante, much admired at the ball. 她是一位美丽的初次登台的女子，在舞会上备受赞赏。

例句：His debut novel was an immediate bestseller. 他的处女作一上市就成了畅销书。


近义词：newcomer, novice, newcomer, initiate


反义词：veteran, expert
