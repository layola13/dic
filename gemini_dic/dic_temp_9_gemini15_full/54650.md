disobedience:/dɪs əˈbiːdiəns/| n. |不服从；违抗；违逆|The refusal to obey rules or someone in authority.  It often implies a conscious and deliberate act of defiance. |例句：Her disobedience led to a suspension from school.  她的不服从导致她被学校停学。

例句：The soldiers' disobedience of orders resulted in a court-martial. 士兵们违抗命令导致了军事法庭的审判。


近义词：defiance, insubordination, rebellion, noncompliance |反义词：obedience, compliance, conformity


词性：名词
