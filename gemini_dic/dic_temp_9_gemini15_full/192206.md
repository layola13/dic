scratcher: /ˈskræʧər/ | n. | 抓挠器，抓痒器 | A tool or device used for scratching something, often the skin to relieve itching.  It can also refer to someone or something that scratches. | 例句：The cat loves its scratching post. = 猫喜欢它的抓挠柱。  My back is itchy; I need a scratcher. = 我的背痒；我需要一个抓痒器。 | 近义词：scraper, scratcher post, itcher | 反义词: smoother


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scratches his head constantly. = 他不停地挠头。
The cat uses the scratcher every day. = 猫每天都用抓挠器。

一般过去时:
She scratched her arm on a thorn. = 她的胳膊被刺划破了。
The dog scratched at the door. = 狗抓挠着门。

现在完成时:
I have scratched my car. = 我把我的车刮花了。
The cat has destroyed the old scratcher. = 猫已经毁坏了旧的抓挠器。

过去完成时:
He had scratched the paint before I noticed it. = 我注意到之前他已经刮花了油漆。
The dog had scratched at the door for hours before someone let it in. = 狗在有人开门之前已经抓挠门几个小时了。

将来时:
I will scratch the lottery ticket. = 我将刮开彩票。
She will buy a new scratcher for her cat. = 她将为她的猫买一个新的抓挠器。

虚拟语气:
If I had a scratcher, I would relieve the itch. = 如果我有一个抓痒器，我会缓解瘙痒。
I wish I hadn't scratched the table. = 我真希望我没有刮坏桌子。

否定句:
