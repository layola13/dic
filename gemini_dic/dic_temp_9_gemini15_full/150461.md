pantheist:/ˈpænθiɪst/| n. |泛神论者|A pantheist believes that God is everything and everywhere, that the universe is identical with God.  Pantheists don't believe in a personal God separate from the universe. |Example:  Many ancient cultures held pantheistic beliefs. 许多古代文化持有泛神论的信仰。  He identified himself as a pantheist, finding divinity in the natural world. 他认为自己是一个泛神论者，在自然世界中找到了神性。|近义词：panentheist (泛神论者，但与泛神论略有不同)|反义词：atheist (无神论者), theist (有神论者)
