sanderling:/ˈsændərliŋ/| n. |矶鹬|A small, sand-colored shorebird with a short, slightly upturned bill, found on beaches worldwide.  It is known for its distinctive, fast running movements along the shoreline. |Example:  We spotted a flock of sanderlings scurrying along the water's edge as the waves receded. 我们看到一群矶鹬在波浪退去时沿着水边飞快地跑动。  |近义词：dunlin, stint|反义词： (None directly opposite, but larger shorebirds could be considered contrasting)|名词
