recarrying:/ˌriːˈkæriɪŋ/| v.|重新携带，再次搬运 |The act of carrying something again or repeatedly.  It implies a previous instance of carrying the same item or a similar task.|例句：The heavy boxes required recarrying after the initial delivery went wrong.  重新运送沉重的箱子，因为第一次送货出了问题。 The hikers found themselves recarrying their supplies after a landslide blocked the trail.  山体滑坡阻塞了小路后，远足者发现自己不得不再次搬运他们的物资。|近义词：retransporting, conveying again, carrying anew|反义词：unloading, leaving behind, discarding
