rousement:/ˈraʊzmənt/ | n. | 唤醒；激励；骚动 | The act of rousing someone from sleep or inactivity; a state of being roused or excited.  It can also refer to a commotion or disturbance. | 例句：The sudden rousement startled the sleeping child.  = 突然的吵闹声惊醒了熟睡的孩子。 The rousement of the troops before the battle was inspiring. = 战前对士兵的激励鼓舞人心。 | 近义词：awakening, stimulation, excitement, commotion, upheaval | 反义词：calm, tranquility, quiet, slumber  | 名词
