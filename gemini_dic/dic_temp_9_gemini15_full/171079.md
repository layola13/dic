It is primarily transmitted through contact with infected birds, their droppings, or contaminated environments. Symptoms can range from mild flu-like illness to severe pneumonia.  Early diagnosis and treatment with antibiotics are crucial for a favorable outcome. |  The parrot owner developed psittacosis after handling an infected bird.  鹦鹉主人在接触受感染的鸟类后患上了鹦鹉病。|  ornithosis, chlamydiosis |  healthy |  n.
