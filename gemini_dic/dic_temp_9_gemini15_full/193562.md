This often involves internal mechanisms that detect deviations from the desired state and then implement corrective actions.  Self-alignments are crucial in many technological applications, ensuring optimal performance and accuracy.  | Example: The gyroscope in the drone provides continuous self-alignments, keeping it stable during flight.  The self-alignments of the telescope's mirrors are essential for achieving high-resolution images. | 近义词: self-adjustment, self-calibration, auto-alignment | 反义词: misalignment, disorientation | 名词
