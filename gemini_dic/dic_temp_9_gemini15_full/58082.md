drooped:/druːpt/| v.|下垂；萎靡；沮丧 |To bend or hang downwards; to become less firm or upright; to lose energy or become depressed.|例句：The flowers drooped in the heat.  花朵在酷暑中下垂了。

The branches drooped under the weight of the snow. 树枝在雪的重量下下垂了。

近义词：sag, slump, wilt, droop, sink| 反义词：rise, lift, perk up| 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flowers droop in the sun. = 花朵在阳光下下垂。
He droops his head in shame. = 他羞愧地低下了头。

一般过去时:
The leaves drooped after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，树叶下垂了。
She drooped with exhaustion. = 她累得无精打采。

The plant has drooped since I forgot to water it. = 自从我忘记浇水以来，植物就枯萎了。
His spirits have drooped since he lost his job. = 自从他失业后，他的精神就萎靡不振了。

过去完成时:
The flowers had drooped before the rain came. = 下雨之前花朵就已经下垂了。
He had drooped visibly after the news. = 听到那个消息后，他明显地萎靡了。

将来时:

I think she will droop under the pressure of her new job. = 我认为她会在新工作的压力下变得萎靡不振。

虚拟语气:
