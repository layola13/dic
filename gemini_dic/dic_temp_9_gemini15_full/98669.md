insulate:/ˈɪnsəleɪt/ | vt.  | 隔离；使绝缘；使隔音 | To protect something from the passage of heat, electricity, or sound.  |  We insulated the attic to keep the house warmer in winter. 我们给阁楼做了隔热处理，以使房子在冬天更暖和。  The electrician insulated the wires to prevent a short circuit. 电工给电线做了绝缘处理以防止短路。 | protect, shield, buffer | conduct, expose | 动词
