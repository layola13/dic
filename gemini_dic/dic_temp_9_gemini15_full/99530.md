intergenerative:/ˌɪntərˌdʒɛnəˈreɪtɪv/ | adj. |跨代的，世代间的|  Relating to or involving more than one generation.  This term emphasizes the interaction and influence between different generations. |例句：The intergenerative project brought together young people and senior citizens to share their experiences.  跨代项目将年轻人和老年人聚集在一起分享他们的经验。


例句：Intergenerative learning models foster mutual respect and understanding between generations. 跨代学习模式促进不同世代之间的相互尊重和理解。


近义词：multigenerational, transgenerational


反义词：intragenerational (within one generation)


词性：形容词
