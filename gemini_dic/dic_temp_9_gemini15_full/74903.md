freshment:/ˈfrɛʃmənt/ | n. | 新生，新来者 | A newcomer, especially a new student at a school or college.  | Example: The freshmen were given a tour of the campus. 新生们被带去参观了校园。 | 近义词：newcomer, recruit, beginner | 反义词：veteran, senior


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The refreshment stand is open now. = 茶点摊现在开门了。
He is a freshman this year. = 他今年是新生。


一般过去时:
Many freshmen attended the orientation last week. = 许多新生上周参加了迎新会。
The refreshment stand closed early yesterday. = 茶点摊昨天早早关门了。


现在完成时:
She has been a freshman for only one month. = 她才当了一个月新生。
They have already finished the refreshments. = 他们已经吃完了点心。


过去完成时:
The orientation had already started before I arrived. = 我到达之前迎新会已经开始了。
They had consumed all the refreshments by the time the party ended. = 派对结束时，他们已经吃掉了所有的点心。


将来时:
He will be a sophomore next year. = 他明年将是大二学生。
The refreshment stand will open at noon tomorrow. = 茶点摊明天中午开门。


虚拟语气:
If I were a freshman again, I would study harder. = 如果我再当一次新生，我会更努力学习。
If there were more refreshments, everyone would be happier. = 如果有更多点心，大家都会更开心。


被动语态:
The freshmen were given a warm welcome. = 新生们受到了热烈的欢迎。
The refreshments were prepared by the catering company. = 点心是由餐饮公司准备的。


疑问句:

否定句:

There were no refreshments at the meeting. = 会议上没有点心。
