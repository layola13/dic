fell-down:/fɛl daʊn/| adj. n. v.|摔倒的；倒塌的；下降的|It describes something that has fallen to the ground or a state of decline.  It can be used as an adjective, noun, or verb, depending on the context.| 例句：The vase fell down and shattered. 花瓶摔碎了。 The old building had a fell-down roof. 那座旧建筑的屋顶已经倒塌了.  |近义词：collapsed, tumbled, dropped, plummeted|反义词：standing, upright, elevated|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price of oil falls down. = 油价下跌。
The leaves fall down in autumn. = 秋天树叶落下。


一般过去时:
He fell down the stairs. = 他从楼梯上摔下来了。
The empire fell down. = 帝国灭亡了。


现在完成时:
The old house has fallen down. = 老房子已经倒塌了。
The stock market has fallen down sharply. = 股市暴跌。


过去完成时:
The bridge had fallen down before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，桥已经垮塌了。
The company had fallen down on its promises. = 公司没有遵守承诺。


将来时:
The tree will fall down in the storm. = 这棵树将在暴风雨中倒下。
The economy might fall down further. = 经济可能进一步下滑。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't intervened, the economy would have fallen down. = 如果政府不干预，经济就会崩溃。
I wish the prices hadn't fallen down so much. = 我真希望价格不要跌得这么厉害。


被动语态:
The building was fallen down by the earthquake. = 建筑物在地震中倒塌了。(注意：此句法略显不自然，更常用被动语态表达为：The building was destroyed by the earthquake.)
The plan was fallen down due to lack of funding. = 计划由于缺乏资金而失败了。( 同样，此句法略显不自然，更常用被动语态表达为：The plan was abandoned due to lack of funding.)


疑问句:
Did the statue fall down? = 雕像倒了吗？
Has the sales figures fallen down? = 销售额下降了吗？


否定句:
The building didn't fall down. = 建筑物没有倒塌。
The plan didn't fall down. = 计划没有失败。


PHRASE->
fall down on (one's job/duties) = 未尽职；失职
fall down with = 因…而倒下
fall down to = 降至
fall down flat = 完全失败
fall down from = 从…跌落
