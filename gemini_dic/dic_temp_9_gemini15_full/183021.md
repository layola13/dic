replicant:/ˈrɛplɪkənt/| n. |复制人，仿制品| A replicant is a being, usually artificial, that is made to closely resemble a human or another organism.  In science fiction, replicants are often bioengineered beings with advanced capabilities. |Example: The film Blade Runner features replicants who are almost indistinguishable from humans.  这部电影《银翼杀手》中出现了与人类几乎无法区分的复制人。|近义词：clone, copy, imitation, android |反义词：original, authentic
