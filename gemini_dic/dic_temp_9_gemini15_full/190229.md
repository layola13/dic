sapodilla:/ˌsæpəˈdɪlə/| n. |番石榴|A tropical evergreen tree (Manilkara zapota) bearing a brown, edible fruit with a sweet, custard-like flesh.|The sapodilla tree is native to southern Mexico and Central America.  番石榴树原产于墨西哥南部和中美洲。|近义词：chicle (referring to the latex)|反义词：无
