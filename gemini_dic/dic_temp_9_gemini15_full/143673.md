outlasts:/ˌaʊtˈlæsts/| vt.|持续时间比…长；比…更持久|to last longer than (someone or something)|The quality of this furniture outlasts its price. 这款家具的质量超过了它的价格。|近义词：survive, endure, outlive|反义词：fail, perish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This material outlasts all others. = 这种材料比其他所有材料都耐用。
His love for her outlasts all trials. = 他对她的爱经受住了所有的考验。


一般过去时:
The old house outlasted several owners. = 那座老房子经历了好几任主人。
Her determination outlasted the difficulties. = 她的决心战胜了困难。


现在完成时:
This engine has outlasted all expectations. = 这台发动机已经超出了所有预期。
Their marriage has outlasted many others. = 他们的婚姻比许多其他的婚姻都长久。


过去完成时:
The empire had outlasted many dynasties. = 这个帝国比许多王朝都存在的时间长。
He had outlasted all his rivals. = 他比所有竞争对手都活得长。


将来时:
This product will outlast its competitors. = 此产品将比竞争对手更持久。
I believe this friendship will outlast time itself. = 我相信这段友谊将超越时间本身。


虚拟语气:
If only this feeling could outlast the summer. = 如果这种感觉能够持续到夏天就好了。
I wish my enthusiasm could outlast this project. = 我希望我的热情能持续到这个项目结束。


被动语态:
The old bridge was outlasted by the new one. = 老桥不如新桥坚固耐用。
The original design was outlasted by newer models. = 原来的设计被更新的型号所取代。


疑问句:
Will this battery outlast the trip? = 这块电池够用整个旅程吗？
Does his influence outlast his lifetime? = 他的影响力能超越他的一生吗？


否定句:
His patience didn't outlast the long wait. = 他的耐心没能坚持住漫长的等待。
The old building didn't outlast the earthquake. = 那座旧建筑没能经受住地震。


PHRASE->
outlast the competition = 战胜竞争对手
outlast the storm = 渡过难关
outlast the others = 比其他人坚持更久
outlast the warranty = 超过保修期
outlast the test = 经受住考验
