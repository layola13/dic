choices:/ˈtʃɔɪsɪz/ | n. | 选择，挑选 | Choices refer to the act of selecting or the things that can be selected from a set of options.  | Example:  The store offers a wide range of choices. 这家商店提供各种各样的选择。|  options, alternatives, selections | limitations, restrictions | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your choices? = 你的选择是什么？
What choices do we have? = 我们有什么选择？


一般过去时:
What were his choices? = 他的选择是什么？
What choices did she make? = 她做了什么选择？


现在完成时:
What choices have you made? = 你做了什么选择？
What choices have they considered? = 他们考虑过什么选择？


过去完成时:
What choices had he already made? = 他之前已经做了什么选择？
What choices had they considered before the deadline? = 截止日期前他们考虑过什么选择？


将来时:
What choices will you have? = 你将有什么选择？
What choices will they make tomorrow? = 他们明天会做什么选择？


虚拟语气:
If I had more choices, I would be happier. = 如果我有更多选择，我会更快乐。
I wish I had more choices. = 我希望我有更多选择。


被动语态:
Several choices were presented to the committee. = 向委员会提出了几个选择。
The best choices are being considered by the team. = 团队正在考虑最佳选择。


疑问句:
What choices are available? = 有哪些选择？
What choices are there? = 有什么选择？


否定句:
There are no good choices. = 没有好的选择。
I have no other choices. = 我没有其他选择。


PHRASE->
a choice of = 可以选择的
