change your mind: /ˌtʃeɪndʒ jɔːr maɪnd/ | v. phr. |改变主意|To alter one's opinion or decision.  |例句：He was going to quit his job, but he changed his mind. 他本来打算辞职，但他改变主意了。|近义词：have a change of heart, reconsider, rethink |反义词：remain steadfast, persist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He changes his mind frequently. = 他经常改变主意。
She changes her mind about the color of the wall. = 她改变了对墙面颜色的想法。


一般过去时:
I changed my mind about going to the party. = 我改变了去参加聚会的想法。
They changed their minds after hearing the new evidence. = 他们听到新的证据后改变了主意。


现在完成时:
She has changed her mind several times this week. = 她本周已经几次改变主意了。
Have you changed your mind about the vacation? = 你改变度假计划了吗？


过去完成时:
I had changed my mind before he even finished speaking. = 他还没说完话我就已经改变主意了。
They had changed their minds about the investment by the time the deadline arrived. = 截止日期到来时，他们已经改变了投资想法。


将来时:
I think I will change my mind about that later. = 我想我以后会改变对这件事的看法。
He will probably change his mind once he sees the results. = 他看到结果后可能会改变主意。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the consequences, I wouldn't have changed my mind. = 如果我知道后果，我就不会改变主意了。
I wish I hadn't changed my mind about going to college. = 我真希望我没有改变上大学的想法。


被动语态:
His mind was changed by the compelling arguments. = 令人信服的论点改变了他的想法。(被动语态较少用于此短语)
The decision was changed; the plan was revised after they changed their minds. = 决定被更改了；在他们改变主意后，计划被修订了。(被动语态较少用于此短语，通常用主动语态表达)


疑问句:
Have you changed your mind yet? = 你改变主意了吗？
Did he change his mind about the offer? = 他改变对这个提议的想法了吗？


否定句:
He didn't change his mind, despite the pressure. = 尽管有压力，他也没有改变主意。
She refused to change her mind, no matter what I said. = 无论我说什么，她都不肯改变主意。


PHRASE->
change your mind about something  = 改变对某事的看法
change your mind on something = 改变对某事的决定
make up your mind = 下定决心 (反义)
to have a change of heart = 改变想法 (近义)
think twice = 三思而后行 (近义，暗含改变主意的可能性)
