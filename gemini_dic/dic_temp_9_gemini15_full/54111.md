词源:
The word "disembrangle" is formed from the prefix "dis-" (meaning "apart" or "away"), the verb "embangle" (meaning "to tangle" or "to involve"), and the suffix "-le".  The word "embangle" is now largely obsolete, but its meaning is clearly seen in the construction of "disembrangle".  Therefore, "disembrangle" literally means "to untangle" or "to free from entanglement".
