occluder:/əˈkluːdər/| n. |遮挡物，闭塞物 |Something that blocks or obstructs, especially light or vision.  An occluder is a device or structure that prevents something from passing through or being seen.  |例句：The clouds acted as an occluder, blocking the sun. 云彩充当了遮挡物，遮住了太阳。

The surgeon used an eyelid occluder during the operation. 外科医生在手术过程中使用了眼睑遮挡器。


近义词：obstruction, barrier, screen, block |反义词：opener, revealer

词性：名词
