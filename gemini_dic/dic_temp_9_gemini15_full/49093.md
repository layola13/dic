deified:/ˈdiːɪfaɪd/| adj.|神化了的；被奉为神明的| Treated as or believed to be a god.  The ancient Egyptians deified their pharaohs.|例句：Many ancient cultures deified their rulers. 许多古代文化都将其统治者神化。
例句：The hero was deified after his death for his selfless deeds.这位英雄死后因其无私的行为而被神化。|近义词：idolized, divinized, apotheosized|反义词：denigrated, vilified, condemned
