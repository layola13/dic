hogwashes:/ˈhɒɡwɒʃɪz/ | n. |胡说八道，废话 |Nonsense, rubbish, or lies.  The word often implies a deliberate attempt to deceive or mislead. |例句：Don't give me that hogwash; I know you're lying.  别跟我说那些胡话，我知道你在撒谎。

例句：His explanation was pure hogwash; it didn't make any sense. 他的解释纯属胡说八道，毫无道理。


近义词：balderdash, bunkum, claptrap, drivel, gibberish, twaddle


反义词：truth, fact, reality, sense


词性: 名词
