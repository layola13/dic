detraining:/ˌdiːˈtreɪnɪŋ/ | n. |  退化；训练减少；运动量减少 | The process of losing the physiological benefits of training, such as strength, endurance, or cardiovascular fitness, due to a reduction or cessation of training.  | Example:  Detraining is a common occurrence in athletes who take extended breaks from their sport. = 退化是运动员长时间停止运动后常见的现象。  The effects of detraining on muscle mass were significant. = 退化对肌肉质量的影响是显著的。| 近义词：regression, decline, atrophy | 反义词：training, conditioning
