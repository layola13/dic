The term is deeply offensive and should be avoided. |Example:  The use of the word "kanacka" is considered highly offensive and racist.  使用“kanacka”一词被认为是极其具有冒犯性和种族歧视的。|近义词：Abo (highly offensive), Aussie (neutral)|反义词：无|名词
