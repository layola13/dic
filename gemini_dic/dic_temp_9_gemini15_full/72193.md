flopping:/ˈflɑːpɪŋ/|/ˈflɒpɪŋ/| v. |扑腾；拍打；（尤指因失败而）突然倒下；|The sound of a fish flopping around in the bucket was quite distressing.  水桶里鱼扑腾的声音令人不安。


The bird flopped down onto the branch, exhausted from its flight.  鸟儿筋疲力尽地扑腾着落到树枝上。


近义词：thumping, flapping, plunging, collapsing|反义词：soaring,  rising, succeeding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is flopping the fish onto the ice. 他正在把鱼拍打到冰上。
The bird flopping its wings  is trying to fly. 那只扑腾翅膀的鸟儿正试图飞起来。


一般过去时:
The comedian's new act flopped.  喜剧演员的新节目失败了。
The exhausted bird flopped to the ground. 精疲力尽的鸟儿扑通一声落到地上。


现在完成时:
The sales team has flopped this quarter. 销售团队本季度业绩不佳。
The actor has flopped in several movies.  这位演员在几部电影中都失败了。


过去完成时:
The project had flopped before they even finished the first phase.  项目在他们完成第一阶段之前就已经失败了。
The bird had flopped down before I could help it.  在我能帮它之前，鸟已经扑通一声倒下了。


将来时:
I think the new product will flop. 我认为新产品会失败。
The politician fears his campaign will flop.  这位政治家担心他的竞选会失败。


虚拟语气:
If the play flopped, they would lose a lot of money.  如果戏剧失败了，他们会损失很多钱。
I wish the movie hadn't flopped.  我希望这部电影没有失败。


被动语态:
The plan was flopped by the unexpected circumstances. 计划由于意外情况而失败了。
The project was flopped because of the lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，项目失败了。


疑问句:
Did the business flop?  生意失败了吗？
Will the new restaurant flop?  新餐馆会失败吗？


否定句:
The show didn't flop; it was a huge success.  演出没有失败；它非常成功。
The plan didn't flop; it was carefully executed.  计划没有失败；它被仔细执行了。


PHRASE->

flop on the floor  倒在地上
a flop  一个失败
