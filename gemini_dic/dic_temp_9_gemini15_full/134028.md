nonhunters:/ˈnɑːnˌhʌntərz/ | n. |非狩猎者，不狩猎的人 |People who do not hunt.  This term often refers to those who oppose hunting or choose not to participate in it for ethical, environmental, or personal reasons. |Example:  The nonhunters organized a protest against the new hunting regulations.  非狩猎者组织了一场反对新的狩猎规定的抗议活动。|近义词：anti-hunters, non-sportsmen |反义词：hunters, sportsmen |名词
