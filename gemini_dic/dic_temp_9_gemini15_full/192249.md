screeched:/skriːtʃt/| v.|尖声叫喊，刺耳地摩擦|To make a loud, harsh, high-pitched sound,  or to cause something to make such a sound.  It can refer to a sound made by a person or an object.|例句：The brakes screeched as the car came to a sudden stop.  汽车突然刹车，刹车发出刺耳的声音。

The girl screeched in terror when she saw the snake. 女孩看到蛇后吓得尖叫起来。|近义词：shriek, shrieked, yell, squeal|反义词：whisper, murmur

The tires screech when the car turns sharply. = 汽车急转弯时轮胎会发出尖锐的摩擦声。
He screeches insults at his opponents. = 他向对手尖声叫骂。


一般过去时:

She screeched with laughter. = 她尖声大笑。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
