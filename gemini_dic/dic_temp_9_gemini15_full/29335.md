castillo:/kɑːˈstiːjoʊ/| n. |城堡，宫殿|A castillo is a large building, typically fortified, historically occupied by a ruler or other person of high rank.|例句：The ancient castillo still stands proudly on the hill.  古老的城堡依然自豪地矗立在山上。|例句：They spent the afternoon exploring the magnificent castillo.  他们下午花时间探索了宏伟的城堡。|近义词：fortress, stronghold, palace|反义词：hut, shack
