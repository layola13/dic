put-to-sleep:/pʊt tə sliːp/| vt.|使入睡；处死；使停止运转|to cause someone or something to fall asleep or become inactive.  This phrase can have both literal and figurative meanings.|例句：The vet put the dog to sleep.  兽医让狗安乐死。|例句：The heavy rain put the fire to sleep. 大雨扑灭了大火。|近义词：euthanize, kill, deactivate|反义词：wake up, activate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They put the dog to sleep every night. = 他们每晚都让狗睡觉。
She puts her worries to sleep by reading. = 她通过阅读来让自己忘记烦恼。

一般过去时:
The farmer put the chickens to sleep in the coop. = 农民把鸡关进鸡舍睡觉。
The mechanic put the engine to sleep after the repair. = 修理完毕后，技工让发动机停止运转。


现在完成时:
The doctor has put many animals to sleep. = 这位医生已经安乐死过很多动物。
We have put the project to sleep for now. = 我们目前已经暂停了这个项目。


过去完成时:
They had put the horse to sleep before the ceremony. = 他们在仪式之前已经让马安乐死了。
The manager had put the old system to sleep before implementing the new one. = 经理在实施新系统之前已经停用了旧系统。


将来时:
The vet will put the cat to sleep tomorrow. = 兽医明天将对猫实施安乐死。
We will put the machine to sleep after the experiment. = 我们实验结束后将让机器停止运转。


虚拟语气:
If I were a vet, I would not put a healthy animal to sleep. = 如果我是兽医，我不会安乐死健康的动物。
I wish I could put my anxieties to sleep easily. = 我希望我能轻松地忘记我的焦虑。


被动语态:
The old car was put to sleep last year. = 那辆旧车去年报废了。
The server was put to sleep remotely. = 服务器被远程关闭。


疑问句:
Will they put the horse to sleep? = 他们会让马安乐死吗？
Should we put this project to sleep? = 我们应该暂停这个项目吗？


否定句:
They did not put the dog to sleep. = 他们没有让狗安乐死。
We will not put the computer to sleep. = 我们不会让电脑休眠。


PHRASE->
