preaccomplishments:/ˌpriːəˈkɑːmplɪʃmənts/| n.|预先取得的成就；预先完成的工作|Achievements or tasks completed before a specific point in time or before a main accomplishment.  It refers to things done in preparation for or leading up to a larger goal. |例句：Her preaccomplishments in research laid the groundwork for her groundbreaking discovery.  她的前期研究成果为她突破性的发现奠定了基础。
例句：The team's preaccomplishments included securing funding and assembling the necessary equipment. 团队的预先成就包括获得资金和组装必要的设备。|近义词：preparatory work, preliminary achievements, groundwork, groundwork, prior achievements|反义词：subsequent accomplishments, post-accomplishments|名词
