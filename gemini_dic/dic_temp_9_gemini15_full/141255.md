opaque:/oʊˈpeɪk/ | adj. | 不透明的；难以理解的；迟钝的 |  Not allowing light to pass through; not transparent.  Also used figuratively to describe something difficult to understand or someone who is mentally slow. | 例句：The glass is opaque, so I can't see through it. = 这玻璃是不透明的，所以我无法看透。

The professor's lecture was opaque; none of the students understood it. = 教授的讲座晦涩难懂；没有一个学生理解。


近义词：nontransparent, impenetrable, obscure, obtuse | 反义词：transparent, clear, lucid, intelligible
