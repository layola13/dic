delusions:/dɪˈluʒənz/| n.|妄想，错觉|A delusion is a false belief that is strongly held despite evidence to the contrary.  It is often a symptom of a mental illness. |例句：His delusions of grandeur led him to believe he was a king.  他的夸大妄想让他相信自己是一位国王。

例句：She suffers from delusions that she is being followed. 她患有被跟踪的妄想症。


近义词：hallucinations, fantasies, illusions, false beliefs|反义词：reality, truth, facts|词性：名词

She experiences delusions regularly. 她经常经历妄想。
