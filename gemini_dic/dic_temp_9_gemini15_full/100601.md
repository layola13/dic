intimacy:/ˈɪntɪməsi/| n. |亲密；隐私；私密关系 |The state or feeling of being intimately acquainted with someone or something; closeness.  It can refer to physical closeness, emotional closeness, or both.  |例句：Their intimacy grew over the years. 他们的亲密关系随着岁月的流逝而加深。

例句：The intimacy of their conversation was evident. 他们谈话的亲密程度显而易见。


近义词：closeness, familiarity, nearness, rapport|反义词：distance, estrangement, alienation
