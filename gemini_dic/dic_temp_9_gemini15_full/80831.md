gouged:/ɡɑʊdʒd/| v.| 挖出；敲诈；欺骗 | To make a hollow or groove in a surface by cutting or scooping; to force someone to pay an excessive amount of money. |例句：The sculptor gouged out the details of the face.  雕塑家挖出了脸部的细节。|例句：The landlord gouged his tenants with exorbitant rent. 房东向他的房客收取过高的租金，敲诈他们。|近义词：excavate, carve, overcharge, swindle |反义词：fill, smooth, fairly price

一般现在时:
He gouges the wood with a chisel. = 他用凿子凿木头。
They gouge prices to maximize profit. = 他们抬高价格以最大化利润。

一般过去时:
The strong winds gouged deep holes in the sand. = 强风在沙地上挖出了深深的洞穴。
The company gouged customers with hidden fees. = 公司通过隐藏费用敲诈顾客。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

Before the investigation, the company had gouged its customers for years. = 在调查之前，该公司多年来一直在敲诈其客户。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
