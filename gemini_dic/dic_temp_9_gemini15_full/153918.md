perdu:/pɛrˈduː/ | adj. | 迷路的；失去的；绝望的 | Lost; missing; forlorn.  The word evokes a sense of being lost and abandoned, often with an implication of sadness or hopelessness.  | 例句：The little boy was perdu in the woods.  那个小男孩在树林里迷路了。

The dog, perdu and whimpering, was found huddled under a bush.  那只迷路且呜咽的狗被发现蜷缩在灌木丛下。


近义词：lost, missing, astray, forlorn, desperate | 反义词：found, recovered, safe, hopeful


词源:  From Old French *perdu, past participle of perdre "to lose," from Vulgar Latin *perdēre, alteration of Latin perdere "to destroy, lose."  The sense development is from "lost" to "lost and abandoned," hence the emotional connotation.
