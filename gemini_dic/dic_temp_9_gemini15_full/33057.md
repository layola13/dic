chowder:/ˈtʃaʊdər/ | n. |浓汤；杂烩汤 |A chowder is a hearty, often creamy soup or stew, typically containing seafood, potatoes, and onions.  It can also include other ingredients depending on the region and recipe.  |Example:  The clam chowder was rich and flavorful. 这份蛤蜊浓汤浓郁美味。|近义词：stew, soup, broth |反义词：N/A
