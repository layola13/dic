mass-produce:/ˈmæs prəˈduːs/| vt.|大规模生产，批量生产|To manufacture something on a large scale using standardized methods.|例句：The company mass-produces toys for the holiday season.  这家公司为假日季大规模生产玩具。|例句：They mass-produced cars using assembly lines. 他们使用装配线大规模生产汽车。|近义词：manufacture, produce in large quantities|反义词：handcraft, custom-make|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mass-produce clothing in that factory. = 那个工厂大规模生产服装。
The company mass-produces electronic components. = 这家公司大规模生产电子元件。

一般过去时:
Ford mass-produced the Model T. = 福特公司大规模生产了T型车。
The factory mass-produced shoes for export. = 这个工厂大规模生产鞋子用于出口。

现在完成时:
They have mass-produced millions of units. = 他们已经大规模生产了数百万个单元。
The company has mass-produced this product for years. = 这家公司多年来一直在大规模生产这种产品。

过去完成时:
Before the war, they had mass-produced weapons. = 在战争爆发前，他们已经大规模生产武器了。
By 2000, they had mass-produced millions of cars. = 到2000年，他们已经大规模生产了数百万辆汽车。

将来时:
They will mass-produce the new model next year. = 他们明年将大规模生产新款车型。
The factory will mass-produce solar panels. = 工厂将大规模生产太阳能电池板。

虚拟语气:
If they had mass-produced it earlier, they would have made more profit. = 如果他们早点大规模生产它，他们本可以赚更多钱。
If they were to mass-produce this item, the cost would be significantly lower. = 如果他们要大规模生产这个项目，成本将大大降低。

被动语态:
Millions of cars are mass-produced each year. = 每年都有数百万辆汽车被大规模生产。
The product is mass-produced using advanced technology. = 该产品采用先进技术进行大规模生产。

疑问句:
Are they mass-producing the new phone? = 他们正在大规模生产新手机吗？
Does the company mass-produce any sustainable products? = 这家公司是否大规模生产任何可持续产品？

否定句:
They don't mass-produce that model anymore. = 他们不再大规模生产那个型号了。
The company doesn't mass-produce handcrafted goods. = 这家公司不生产手工制品。

词源:
mass- (表示“大量的”) + produce (表示“生产”)  组合而成。 "Mass"源于拉丁语"massa"，意为“块，团”。 "Produce"源于拉丁语"producere"，意为“生产，带来”。
