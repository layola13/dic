quipped:/kwɪpt/| vt.|俏皮地说；机智地说；(迅速)说；|To say something witty or clever.|He quipped that the meeting was as exciting as watching paint dry. 他俏皮地说，这个会议就像看油漆变干一样令人兴奋。|近义词：remarked, observed, joked, said|反义词：yelled, screamed
