divertive: /daɪˈvɜːrtɪv/ | adj. | 消遣的；转移注意力的 | Providing amusement or entertainment; diverting.  Something that is divertive serves to distract or amuse, offering a break from routine or seriousness. |

例句：

The comedian's jokes were highly divertive, keeping the audience entertained throughout the show.  喜剧演员的笑话非常有趣，让观众在整个演出过程中都兴高采烈。

Reading a good book can be a divertive activity after a long day of work.  读一本好书可以在漫长的一天工作后成为一种消遣活动。


近义词：amusing, entertaining, diverting, recreational, playful

反义词：boring, tedious, dull, monotonous


词性：形容词
