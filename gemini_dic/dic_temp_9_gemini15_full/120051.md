millponds:/ˈmɪlpɑːndz/ | n. |磨坊水池；平静的池塘 |Still, quiet water, often associated with a mill.  The term evokes a sense of tranquility and stillness. |例句：The ducks swam peacefully on the millponds.  鸭子平静地游在磨坊水池里。

例句：The reflection of the trees in the millponds was breathtaking. 磨坊水池中树木的倒影令人叹为观止。
