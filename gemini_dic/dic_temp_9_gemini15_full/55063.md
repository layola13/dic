disregard:/ˌdɪs.rɪˈɡɑːrd/|/ˈdɪs.rɪ.ɡɑːrd/| vt. n.|忽视；不理会；漠视|To ignore something or someone; to treat something as unimportant.|例句：He disregarded the doctor's advice and continued smoking. 他无视医生的建议继续吸烟。|例句：The company's disregard for safety regulations led to a serious accident. 公司对安全规定的漠视导致了一起严重的事故。|近义词：ignore, neglect, overlook, dismiss|反义词：heed, attend to, consider, respect|动词
