entrochal: /ˈɛntroʊkəl/ | adj. |  似海胆的；海胆状的 | Relating to or resembling an entroch, a fossil crinoid stem.  The term often describes structures with a columnar or radial arrangement, reminiscent of the skeletal structure of a crinoid (sea lily).  | Example: The rock formation exhibited a striking entrochal pattern. 这块岩石的构造展现出令人惊叹的海胆状图案。 | 近义词:  crinoidal, columnar, radial | 反义词: amorphous, shapeless

USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't lend itself to typical verb conjugations or the creation of phrases in the same way as common verbs or nouns.  The usage is limited to descriptive contexts.
