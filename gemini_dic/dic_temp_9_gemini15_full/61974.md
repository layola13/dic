enable:/ɪˈneɪbl/ | vt. |使能够；使可能；授权|To give someone or something the authority or power to do something.  It also means to make something possible.|例句：The new software enables users to edit videos easily. 新软件使用户能够轻松编辑视频。
例句：His experience enabled him to succeed in the difficult job. 他的经验使他能够胜任那份艰难的工作。|近义词：empower, authorize, facilitate |反义词：disable, prohibit, hinder |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The key enables access to the building. = 这把钥匙可以进入大楼。
This software enables efficient data processing. = 这个软件可以高效地处理数据。

一般过去时:
The training enabled them to perform better. = 培训使他们能够做得更好。
The new law enabled businesses to expand. = 新法律使企业能够扩张。

现在完成时:
Technological advancements have enabled incredible progress. = 技术进步已经带来了令人难以置信的进步。
The new policy has enabled greater transparency. = 新政策促进了更大的透明度。

过去完成时:
Having studied hard, she enabled herself to pass the exam. = 因为努力学习，她能够通过考试。
The previous investment had enabled the company's growth. = 先前的投资促进了公司的发展。

将来时:
This invention will enable faster communication. = 这项发明将使通信更快。
The new bridge will enable easier access to the island. = 新桥将使更容易到达该岛。

虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could enable further research. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们就能进行进一步的研究。
I wish this technology enabled more sustainable practices. = 我希望这项技术能够支持更可持续的实践。

被动语态:
Access to the system is enabled by a password. = 通过密码可以访问系统。
The project was enabled by a generous grant. = 该项目得到了慷慨的资助得以实现。

疑问句:
What enabled you to achieve this success? = 是什么使你取得了这样的成功？
Does this tool enable remote control? = 这个工具可以远程控制吗？

否定句:
The lack of funding disabled the project. = 缺乏资金导致项目无法进行。
His injury prevented him from enabling himself to participate. = 他的伤势阻止了他参与。


PHRASE->
enable someone to do something = 使某人能够做某事
enable access to = 启用访问
enable features = 启用功能
enable-bodied = 健全的
enable-to-disable switch = 启用/禁用开关
