circulator:/ˈsɜːrkjuleɪtər/| n. |循环器，循环装置|A device or system that circulates something, such as blood, air, or water.  It can refer to a person or thing that causes circulation or movement. |例句：The hospital uses a sophisticated circulator to maintain a steady flow of oxygen throughout the building. 医院使用复杂的循环器来维持整个建筑物中稳定的氧气流量。


例句：The new circulator in the heating system improved efficiency significantly.  新的供暖系统循环器显著提高了效率。


近义词：pump, conveyor, distributor |反义词：obstructor, blocker |词性：名词


词源:  From "circulate" (to move in a circle or circuit) + "-or" (agent suffix).  The root "circulus" is Latin for "circle".
