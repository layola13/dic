flosh:/flɒʃ/| n.|冲洗；大量；洪水|A sudden rush of water; a flood.  It can also refer to a large quantity of something.|例句：The heavy rain caused a flosh of water down the street. 大雨导致街道上洪水泛滥。|例句：There was a flosh of papers on the desk. 桌子上有一大堆文件。|近义词：flood, deluge, rush, torrent|反义词：drought, trickle
