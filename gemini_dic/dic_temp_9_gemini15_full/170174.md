protoplasm:/ˈproʊtoʊˌplæzəm/| n. |原生质；细胞质 |The living substance of a cell, including the cytoplasm, nucleus, and other organelles.  It is a complex mixture of water, proteins, lipids, carbohydrates, and nucleic acids. |Example:  The protoplasm of the cell is responsible for its metabolism and growth.  原生质负责细胞的新陈代谢和生长。|近义词：cytoplasm, cell substance |反义词：nonliving matter
