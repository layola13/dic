It is a complex oxide mineral with a chemical formula that varies depending on the relative amounts of niobium and tantalum present.  It is an important ore of niobium and tantalum. |The sample contained a significant amount of fergusonite.  这个样本含有大量的铁铌矿。|  Near synonyms include: niobite, tantalite (though these are distinct minerals with different compositions).
