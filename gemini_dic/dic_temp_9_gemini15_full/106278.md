knoxvillite:/ˌnɒksˈvɪlaɪt/| n. |诺克斯维尔人；诺克斯维尔市居民|A Knoxvillite is a resident of Knoxville, Tennessee.  The term often implies a sense of belonging or connection to the city's culture and community.|Example:  Many Knoxvillites are proud of their city's vibrant arts scene. 许多诺克斯维尔人都为他们城市充满活力的艺术氛围感到自豪。|近义词：Knoxville resident,  Knoxvillian|反义词：无
