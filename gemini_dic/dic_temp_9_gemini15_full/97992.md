inoffensive: /ˌɪnəˈfɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 无害的；不得罪人的；温和的 | Not likely to offend or provoke; harmless.  |  例句：He tried to be as inoffensive as possible in his comments. 他尽量使自己的评论不冒犯任何人。

例句：Her jokes were inoffensive, even to those easily offended. 她的笑话即使对那些容易生气的人来说也是无伤大雅的。


近义词：harmless, innocuous, mild, unoffensive | 反义词：offensive, insulting, aggressive, provocative
