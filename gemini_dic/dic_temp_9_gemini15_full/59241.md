e-card:/ˈiːkɑːrd/ | n. | 电子贺卡 | An electronic greeting card sent via email or the internet.  It often includes animations, sounds, and personalized messages. | Example: I sent my friend an e-card for her birthday. 我给我朋友发了一张电子贺卡祝她生日快乐。 | 近义词：electronic greeting card, online greeting card | 反义词：paper card, physical card
