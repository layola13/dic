diastolic: /daɪəˈstɒlɪk/ /daɪəˈstɑːlɪk/ | adj. | 舒张期的；舒张压的 | Relating to the phase of the heartbeat when the heart muscle relaxes and allows the chambers to fill with blood.  It is the lower number in a blood pressure reading. | Example:  His diastolic blood pressure was consistently high. 他的舒张压持续偏高。 |近义词:relaxation (in the context of the heart)| 反义词:systolic | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The diastolic pressure is usually lower than the systolic pressure. = 舒张压通常低于收缩压。
Her diastolic blood pressure remains stable. = 她的舒张压保持稳定。


一般过去时:
His diastolic pressure was elevated after the exercise. = 他运动后舒张压升高了。
The doctor checked her diastolic and systolic blood pressure. = 医生检查了她的舒张压和收缩压。


现在完成时:
She has had consistently high diastolic pressure for years. = 她多年来舒张压一直很高。
The patient has experienced a decrease in diastolic pressure since starting medication. = 自从开始服药以来，病人的舒张压有所下降。


过去完成时:
His diastolic pressure had been normal before the recent illness. = 他在最近生病之前舒张压一直正常。
They had noted a gradual increase in her diastolic pressure over the past few months. = 在过去的几个月里，他们注意到她的舒张压逐渐升高。


将来时:
His diastolic pressure will likely improve with lifestyle changes. = 通过改变生活方式，他的舒张压可能会改善。
The doctor believes her diastolic pressure will stabilize with the new medication. = 医生相信她的舒张压服用新药后会稳定下来。


虚拟语气:
If his diastolic pressure were lower, he would feel better. = 如果他的舒张压低一些，他会感觉更好。
I wish my diastolic pressure was consistently normal. = 我希望我的舒张压一直正常。


被动语态:
His diastolic pressure was carefully monitored during the surgery. = 在手术期间，他的舒张压被仔细监测。
The diastolic pressure reading was recorded by the nurse. = 舒张压读数由护士记录。


疑问句:
What is your diastolic blood pressure? = 你的舒张压是多少？
Was his diastolic pressure measured accurately? = 他的舒张压测量准确吗？


否定句:
His diastolic pressure was not abnormally high. = 他的舒张压并非异常高。
She did not experience any significant changes in her diastolic pressure. = 她的舒张压没有出现任何显著变化。



PHRASE->

词源:
From diastole (the relaxation phase of the heart) + -ic (adjective suffix).  Diastole itself comes from Greek διαστολή (diastolē), meaning "dilation, expansion".
