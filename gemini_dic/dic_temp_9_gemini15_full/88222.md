hinderling: /ˈhɪndərliŋ/ | n. | 障碍物；阻碍；妨碍者 | A hinderling is something that hinders or obstructs; an obstacle or impediment.  It can also refer to a person who hinders or obstructs. The word is now largely archaic or found in specialized contexts. | Example: The overgrown weeds were a hinderling to our progress.  杂草丛生阻碍了我们的前进。  The constant complaints of the employee proved a hinderling to team productivity. 员工不断的抱怨妨碍了团队的生产力。| 近义词：obstacle, impediment, obstruction, hindrance | 反义词：help, aid, assistance |  词性: 名词
