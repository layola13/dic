indict:/ɪnˈdaɪt/| vt.|控告；起诉|To formally accuse someone of a crime.|The grand jury indicted him on charges of murder. 大陪审团以谋杀罪起诉他。|accuse, charge|acquit, exonerate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The grand jury indicts the suspect. = 大陪审团起诉了嫌疑人。
They indict him for fraud. = 他们以诈骗罪起诉他。

一般过去时:
The court indicted the defendant last week. = 法院上周起诉了被告。
The prosecutor indicted the man for theft. = 检察官以盗窃罪起诉了该男子。

现在完成时:
The authorities have indicted several individuals in the case. = 当局已在该案中起诉了几个人。
They have indicted him on multiple counts of bribery. = 他们以多项贿赂罪名起诉了他。

过去完成时:
The judge had already indicted the criminal before the evidence was presented. = 在证据提交之前，法官已经起诉了罪犯。
The committee had indicted the mayor for corruption before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，委员会已经以腐败罪起诉了市长。


将来时:
The government will indict the CEO next month. = 政府下个月将起诉首席执行官。
They will indict him for tax evasion. = 他们将以逃税罪起诉他。


被动语态:
He was indicted on charges of conspiracy. = 他被指控犯有共谋罪。
The suspect was indicted by the grand jury. = 嫌疑人被大陪审团起诉。


疑问句:
Has the suspect been indicted yet? = 嫌疑人已经被起诉了吗？
Will they indict him for perjury? = 他们会以伪证罪起诉他吗？


否定句:
The jury did not indict the defendant. = 陪审团没有起诉被告。
They did not indict him because of lack of evidence. = 由于证据不足，他们没有起诉他。


PHRASE->
be indicted for = 因…而被起诉
indict on charges of = 以…罪名起诉
indict somebody for something = 因某事起诉某人
a formal indictment = 正式起诉书
to face indictment = 面临起诉
