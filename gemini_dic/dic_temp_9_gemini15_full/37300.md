commandments:/kəˈmændmənts/ | n. |诫命；戒律 | Rules or laws, especially those given by a god or authority figure.  The Ten Commandments are a prime example. |例句：The Ten Commandments are fundamental to many religions. 十诫是许多宗教的基础。

例句：Following the commandments is crucial for spiritual growth. 遵守诫命对灵性成长至关重要。


近义词：precepts, rules, laws, ordinances, dictates

反义词：disobedience, transgression, violation


词源：源自古英语 *commendment,  由 *com- (together) 和 *mandment (command) 组成，意为“共同命令”。  "Commandment" itself evolved from Old French commandement and ultimately from Latin *commendare (to entrust, to commend).
