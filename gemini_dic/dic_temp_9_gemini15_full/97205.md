inflammably: /ɪnˈflæməbli/ | adj. | 易燃的，煽动性的 | Easily ignited or set on fire; tending to arouse strong emotions, especially anger or hostility. |例句：The curtains were made of highly inflammable material.  窗帘是用易燃材料制成的。|例句：His inflammable speech angered the crowd. 他煽动性的演讲激怒了人群。 |近义词：flammable, combustible, incendiary |反义词：fire-resistant, fireproof, nonflammable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gas is inflammable. =  这种气体易燃。
This topic is inflammable in this current political climate. = 在当前的政治气候下，这个话题很容易引起争议。

一般过去时:
The old building was highly inflammable. = 这座旧建筑物极易燃烧。
His words were inflammable and caused a riot. = 他的话具有煽动性，并引发了暴乱。


现在完成时:
The factory has been deemed inflammable and shut down. = 该工厂已被认定为易燃且已关闭。
His actions have proven to be highly inflammable, leading to widespread unrest. = 他的行为已被证明极具煽动性，导致了广泛的不满。


过去完成时:
The wood had been inflammable, so the fire spread quickly. = 木材易燃，因此火势迅速蔓延。
The situation had become inflammable due to the irresponsible reporting. = 由于不负责任的报道，局势变得紧张。


将来时:

His words will likely prove inflammable and cause further conflict. = 他的话很可能会被证明具有煽动性并导致进一步的冲突。


虚拟语气:

If his speech had been less inflammable, the protests might not have occurred. = 如果他的演讲不那么煽动性，抗议活动可能就不会发生。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Is this substance inflammable? = 这种物质易燃吗？
Is his behavior likely to be inflammable and provoke a response? = 他的行为可能会煽风点火并激起回应吗？


否定句:
The material is not inflammable. = 该材料不易燃。
His speech was not inflammable, it was quite calming. = 他的演讲并非煽动性，而是相当平静的。
