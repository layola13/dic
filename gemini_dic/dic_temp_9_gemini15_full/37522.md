common nouns:/ˈkɑːmən naʊnz/| n. |普通名词 |A common noun is a noun that refers to a general person, place, thing, or idea, not a specific one.  Unlike proper nouns, common nouns are not capitalized.  Examples include "dog," "city," "happiness," and "teacher."|例句：The dog chased the ball.  那条狗追着球跑。  The city is known for its beautiful parks.  这座城市以其美丽的公园而闻名。|近义词：general nouns|反义词：proper nouns|名词
