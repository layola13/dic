prolapse:/ˈproʊlæps/ | n. v. |脱垂，内脏下垂| The falling or slipping down of an organ or part of the body from its normal position.  A prolapse can occur in various organs, such as the uterus, rectum, or bladder.  |例句：She experienced a uterine prolapse after childbirth. 她分娩后经历了子宫脱垂。

The doctor diagnosed a rectal prolapse. 医生诊断为直肠脱垂。


近义词：descent,  lapse,  hernia (in some contexts)|反义词：elevation,  support,  retention|名词，动词
