redemocratizing: /ˌriːdɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使重新民主化 | The process of restoring democratic principles and practices to a country or society after a period of authoritarian rule or instability.  It involves establishing or strengthening institutions that promote participation, accountability, and the rule of law. | Example:  The country is undergoing a difficult process of redemocratizing after decades of dictatorship. = 这个国家正在经历一个艰难的重新民主化过程，此前经历了几十年的独裁统治。  The international community is supporting the redemocratizing efforts in the region. = 国际社会正在支持该地区的重新民主化努力。 | 近义词: democratizing anew, restoring democracy, revitalizing democracy | 反义词: authoritarianizing, dictatorializing
