coaptation:/ˌkoʊæpˈteɪʃən/| n. | 挪用；适应；配合；合作；兼并|The process by which existing structures or processes are modified to serve new functions.  This can occur through evolutionary adaptation, where a trait originally serving one purpose is repurposed for another, or through the combination of pre-existing components into a novel system. |例句：The evolution of feathers is a classic example of co-option, initially used for insulation, later for flight.  羽毛的进化是挪用的一个经典例子，最初用于保温，后来用于飞行。

例句：The company achieved success through the coaptation of existing technologies. 公司通过对现有技术的整合取得了成功。


近义词：adaptation, exaptation, appropriation, modification |反义词：creation, invention
