internists:/ɪnˈtɜːrnɪsts/| n.|内科医生|A physician specializing in internal medicine, which focuses on the diagnosis and nonsurgical treatment of diseases of internal organs.|例句：Many internists work in hospitals and clinics, providing comprehensive care to patients.|近义词：physician, doctor, general practitioner|反义词：surgeon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Internists diagnose and treat a wide range of illnesses. = 内科医生诊断和治疗各种疾病。
She regularly consults with internists on complex cases. = 她定期就复杂病例咨询内科医生。


一般过去时:
The internist examined the patient thoroughly. = 内科医生仔细检查了病人。
