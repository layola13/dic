farmhouse:/ˈfɑːrmhaʊs/| n. |农舍，农家|A farmhouse is a house on a farm.  It is typically a relatively simple dwelling, often built of wood, stone, or brick, and located in a rural area.  Farmhouses can range in size and style depending on the size and prosperity of the farm. |例句：We spent the weekend at a charming farmhouse in the countryside. 我们在乡下的一座迷人的农舍里度过了周末。

例句：The old farmhouse was surrounded by fields of wheat.  那座老农舍被小麦田包围着。


近义词：farm dwelling, country house, rural home |反义词：city house, apartment, urban dwelling |词性：名词
