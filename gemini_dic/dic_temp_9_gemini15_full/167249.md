preterperfect:/ˌpriːtərˈpɜːrfɪkt/| adj.|过去完成时|The term "preterperfect" refers to a grammatical tense that describes an action completed before another action in the past.  It's less commonly used than "past perfect," which is its more prevalent synonym.  The preterperfect emphasizes the completion of the prior action, often highlighting its relevance to a subsequent event.|例句：He had already eaten before she arrived. = 他在她到达之前就已经吃过了。

She had finished her work before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前完成了她的工作。|近义词：past perfect, pluperfect|反义词：future perfect, present perfect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I eat breakfast every morning. = 我每天早上吃早餐。
She works at a bank. = 她在银行工作。


一般过去时:
I ate breakfast this morning. = 我今天早上吃了早餐。
She worked late last night. = 她昨晚工作到很晚。


现在完成时:
I have eaten breakfast. = 我已经吃过早餐了。
She has worked hard this week. = 她这周工作很努力。


过去完成时:
I had eaten breakfast before I went to work. = 我去上班之前已经吃过早餐了。
She had worked for the company for ten years before she retired. = 她退休前在该公司工作了十年。


将来时:
I will eat breakfast tomorrow. = 我明天将吃早餐。
She will work from home next week. = 她下周将在家里工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam. = 如果我学习更努力，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had known the truth sooner. = 我希望我早点知道真相。


被动语态:
The car was repaired yesterday. = 汽车昨天修好了。
The report was completed before the deadline. = 报告在截止日期前完成了。


疑问句:
Did you eat breakfast? = 你吃早餐了吗？
Where did she work? = 她在哪里工作？


否定句:
I did not eat breakfast. = 我没有吃早餐。
She did not work late last night. = 她昨晚没有工作到很晚。


PHRASE->
There is no common phrase directly related to "preterperfect" as it's a grammatical tense, not a word or phrase itself.  However, the past perfect tense (its synonym) is used in many phrases expressing prior actions or states.  Examples would be contextual phrases involving "after," "before," "already," "yet," and "by the time."
