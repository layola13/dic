halfway:/ˈhæfweɪ/| adj. adv.|中途的，半路上的；部分地|Halfway means in the middle of a journey or process.  It can be used as an adjective to describe a location or a state of completion, or as an adverb to modify a verb.|例句：We are halfway through the project. 我们已经完成了项目的一半。

They stopped halfway up the mountain. 他们在半山腰停了下来。|近义词：midway, partially|反义词：fully, completely
