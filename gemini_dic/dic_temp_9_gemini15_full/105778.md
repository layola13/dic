kirsch:/kɪrʃ/| n.|樱桃白兰地|A strong, colorless fruit brandy distilled from fermented morello cherries.  It is typically served as a digestif.|例句：He finished his meal with a glass of kirsch. 他用一杯樱桃白兰地结束了他的饭菜。|例句：The kirsch added a fruity complexity to the dessert. 樱桃白兰地为甜点增添了水果的复杂风味。|近义词：cherry brandy, cherry liqueur|反义词：无
