nepenthes:/nɪˈpɛnθiːz/| n. |猪笼草属植物；忘忧草 |Nepenthes is a genus of carnivorous plants, also known as pitcher plants.  They are characterized by their unique pitcher-shaped traps, which they use to lure, trap, and digest insects and other small creatures.  These plants are found in tropical regions of Southeast Asia, Australia, and other parts of the world. |Example:  Many species of Nepenthes are cultivated as ornamental plants due to their striking appearance.  许多猪笼草品种因其醒目的外观而被作为观赏植物种植。|近义词：pitcher plant (猪笼草)|反义词：无
