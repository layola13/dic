countenances:/ˈkaʊntənənsɪz/ | n. |面容；表情；容貌 | The appearance, especially the expression, of a person's face.  It often reflects their mood or inner state. |例句：Her countenance showed her concern.  她的面容显示出她的担忧。 |例句：He tried to maintain a calm countenance despite the chaos. 尽管一片混乱，他还是努力保持冷静的面容。|近义词：expression, visage, features |反义词：无  | 名词
