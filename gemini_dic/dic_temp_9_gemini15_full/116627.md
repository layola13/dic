matronymic:/məˈtrɒnɪmɪk/ | n. | 母系姓氏；沿袭母系氏族的名字 | A matronymic is a name derived from the name of one's mother or a female ancestor.  It's the female equivalent of a patronymic.  Matronymics are less common than patronymics in many cultures. | Example:  A woman named Anya whose mother's maiden name was Petrova might give her children the matronymic Petrova.  | 近义词：maternal name, mother's name | 反义词：patronymic
