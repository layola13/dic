quasi-political:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈpɒlətɪkəl/|/ˈkwɑːzi ˈpɒlɪtɪkəl/| adj. |准政治的；似政治的；带有政治色彩的 |Something that is quasi-political has some characteristics of politics but is not fully or officially political.  It often refers to activities or organizations that have political implications or influence but do not operate within the formal structures of government. | The student organization, while not officially affiliated with any political party, engaged in quasi-political activities, advocating for environmental protection.  学生组织虽然没有正式加入任何政党，但从事准政治活动，倡导环境保护。|近义词：semi-political, politically tinged, politically motivated |反义词：apolitical, non-political
