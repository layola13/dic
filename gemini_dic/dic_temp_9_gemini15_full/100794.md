intricate:/ˈɪntrɪkət/| adj.|复杂的，错综复杂的| having many small parts or details that are arranged in a complicated way; difficult to understand or follow.  |例句：The plot of the novel was incredibly intricate and kept me guessing until the very end. 这部小说的情节非常复杂，让我一直猜到最后。

例句：The intricate design of the watch captivated her. 这款手表的复杂设计迷住了她。

近义词：complex, complicated, convoluted, elaborate

反义词：simple, straightforward, uncomplicated

词源:
From Old French *entrecruiser ("to interweave, interlace"), from entre ("between") + croiser ("to cross").  The modern meaning developed through the idea of something being interwoven or interlaced in a complex way.
