invertebrata: /ɪnˌvɜːrtɪˈbreɪtə/ | n. | 无脊椎动物 |  Invertebrates are animals lacking a backbone or spinal column. They represent the vast majority of animal species on Earth, encompassing a wide range of phyla, from insects and crustaceans to mollusks and echinoderms.  | Example:  Sponges, insects, and jellyfish are all examples of invertebrates.  无脊椎动物的例子包括海绵、昆虫和水母。|近义词: invertebrate animals | 反义词: vertebrate
