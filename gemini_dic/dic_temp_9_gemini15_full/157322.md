pioneered:/ˌpaɪəˈnɪrd/| v.|开创，倡导，率先发展|To have been the first to use or develop something.|The company pioneered the use of computer technology in the 1980s. 这家公司在20世纪80年代率先使用了计算机技术。|initiated, introduced, developed|hindered, obstructed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pioneers new techniques in surgery. 他在外科手术中开创了新技术。
She pioneers innovative solutions to environmental problems. 她率先提出了解决环境问题的创新方案。


一般过去时:
Marie Curie pioneered research in radioactivity. 居里夫人开创了放射性研究。
The Wright brothers pioneered manned flight.  莱特兄弟开创了载人飞行。


现在完成时:
Scientists have pioneered many life-saving medical treatments. 科学家们已经开创了许多挽救生命的医疗方法。
They have pioneered a new approach to teaching English. 他们已经开创了一种新的英语教学方法。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of the internet, many researchers had pioneered the development of computer networks. 在互联网发明之前，许多研究人员已经开创了计算机网络的发展。
By the time she retired, she had pioneered several groundbreaking discoveries in her field.  在她退休时，她已经在自己的领域开创了几项具有开创性的发现。


将来时:
We will pioneer new ways to generate clean energy. 我们将开创新的清洁能源生产方式。
They believe they will pioneer a new era in space exploration. 他们相信他们将开创一个太空探索的新时代。


虚拟语气:
If she had pioneered this technology earlier, the results would have been even better. 如果她早些开创这项技术，结果会更好。
I wish I had pioneered a more effective approach to problem-solving. 我希望我曾经开创了一种更有效的解决问题的方法。


被动语态:
New methods of farming were pioneered in the 1950s.  20世纪50年代开创了新的农业方法。
The use of renewable energy sources is being pioneered worldwide.  世界各地都在开创可再生能源的使用。


疑问句:
Who pioneered the use of anesthesia in surgery? 谁在外科手术中率先使用了麻醉？
What company pioneered the development of the personal computer?  哪家公司率先开发了个人电脑？


否定句:
They did not pioneer the use of this technology.  他们并没有开创这项技术的使用。
The project failed because they did not pioneer a new approach.  该项目失败是因为他们没有开创一种新的方法。
