commodity:/kəˈmɒdəti/|n.|商品，日用品|A raw material or primary agricultural product that can be bought and sold, such as copper or coffee.|例句：Oil is a valuable commodity. 石油是一种宝贵的商品。|例句：The price of commodities fluctuates constantly. 商品价格不断波动。|近义词：goods, wares, merchandise|反义词：service, intangible asset


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price of this commodity is high. = 这种商品的价格很高。
This company trades in various commodities. = 这家公司经营各种商品。

一般过去时:
The price of this commodity was lower last year. = 去年这种商品的价格较低。
They sold a large quantity of commodities last month. = 上个月他们卖出了大量的商品。

现在完成时:
The price of this commodity has risen sharply. = 这种商品的价格已大幅上涨。
We have imported many commodities from China. = 我们从中国进口了许多商品。

过去完成时:
The price of this commodity had already fallen before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，这种商品的价格就已经下跌了。
They had sold all their commodities before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经卖掉了所有的商品。

将来时:
The price of this commodity will likely increase next year. = 这种商品的价格明年可能会上涨。
They will import more commodities from overseas. = 他们将从海外进口更多商品。

虚拟语气:
If this commodity were cheaper, I would buy more. = 如果这种商品更便宜，我会买更多。
I wish this commodity were more readily available. = 我希望这种商品更容易买到。

被动语态:
This commodity is widely traded globally. = 这种商品在全球广泛交易。
Many commodities are affected by weather conditions. = 许多商品受天气条件的影响。

疑问句:
What is the price of this commodity? = 这种商品的价格是多少？
What commodities does this company produce? = 这家公司生产什么商品？

否定句:
This commodity is not easily available. = 这种商品不容易买到。
They do not trade in this type of commodity. = 他们不交易这种类型的商品。
