idiopathical:/ˌɪdiəʊˈpæθɪkəl/ | adj. | 特发性的，原因不明的 | Of unknown cause; occurring spontaneously.  The term is often used in medicine to describe diseases or conditions with no identifiable cause. | 例句：The doctor diagnosed her condition as idiopathic epilepsy.  医生诊断她的病症为特发性癫痫。

The patient presented with idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura. 病人出现特发性血小板减少性紫癜。


近义词：unexplained, spontaneous, primary, essential | 反义词： symptomatic, secondary


词源:  From Greek *idios ("one's own") + pathos ("suffering, feeling").  The term implies a disease originating from within the patient, without an external cause.
