platformless:/ˈplætˌfɔːrmləs/ | adj. | 无平台的，非平台式的 | Lacking a platform; not based on or using a platform.  This term often refers to decentralized systems or applications that don't rely on a central platform or server. | Example: The platformless application runs directly on the user's device, eliminating the need for a central server.  无平台应用程序直接在用户设备上运行，无需中央服务器。| 近义词： decentralized, serverless | 反义词：platform-based, centralized
