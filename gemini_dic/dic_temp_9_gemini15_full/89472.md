honeycombs:/ˈhʌnikəʊmz/ | n. |蜂巢；蜂窝状结构 |A honeycomb is a mass of hexagonal cells built by honeybees to store honey and raise their young.  Honeycombs are also used metaphorically to describe things with a similar structure. |例句：The bees diligently built their honeycombs.  蜜蜂勤奋地建造它们的蜂巢。

The architect designed a building with a honeycomb structure for maximum efficiency.  建筑师设计了一座蜂窝状结构的建筑，以达到最高的效率。|近义词：cells, lattice |反义词：无
