sidekick:/ˈsaɪdˌkɪk/| n. |助手，伙伴，副手 |A sidekick is a person who assists or accompanies another person, often a more important or powerful one.  They are usually a close friend or associate.  |例句：Batman's sidekick, Robin, always helps him fight crime.  蝙蝠侠的助手罗宾总是帮助他打击犯罪。

例句：The comedian's sidekick provided many funny lines during the show.  这个喜剧演员的助手在演出中提供了许多有趣的台词。


近义词：assistant, companion, partner, associate, helper |反义词：leader, boss, superior
