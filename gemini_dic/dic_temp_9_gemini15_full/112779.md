lubricates:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪts/ | vt. |润滑；使润滑 | To make (something) smooth and slippery, typically by applying a lubricant.  |例句：The mechanic lubricates the engine parts regularly. 机械师定期润滑发动机零件。
例句：Oil lubricates the moving parts of a machine, reducing friction. 油脂润滑机器的活动部件，减少摩擦。|近义词：oil, grease, smooth |反义词：dry, rough


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lubricates the machine every week. = 他每周都润滑机器。
The oil lubricates the engine smoothly. = 机油使发动机平稳运转。

一般过去时:
She lubricated the hinges with oil. = 她用油润滑了铰链。
The mechanic lubricated all the moving parts. = 机械师润滑了所有活动部件。

现在完成时:
They have lubricated the car's engine. = 他们已经润滑了汽车的发动机。
We have lubricated the bicycle chain. = 我们已经润滑了自行车的链条。

过去完成时:
He had lubricated the bike before the race. = 他在比赛前已经润滑了自行车。
She had lubricated the lock, so it opened easily. = 她已经润滑了锁，所以它很容易打开。

将来时:
I will lubricate the door hinges tomorrow. = 我明天会润滑门铰链。
They will lubricate the machinery before starting it. = 他们在启动机器之前会润滑它。

虚拟语气:
If I lubricated the gears, the machine would run more smoothly. = 如果我润滑齿轮，机器就会运转得更平稳。
I wish I had lubricated the hinges earlier; now they squeak. = 我真希望我早点润滑铰链；现在它们吱吱作响。

被动语态:
The engine is lubricated automatically. = 发动机是自动润滑的。
The parts were lubricated before assembly. = 零件在组装前已过润滑处理。

疑问句:
Do you lubricate your car regularly? = 你定期润滑你的车吗？
How often should this machine be lubricated? = 这台机器多久应该润滑一次？

否定句:

She doesn't lubricate the machine often enough. = 她润滑机器的频率不够高。
