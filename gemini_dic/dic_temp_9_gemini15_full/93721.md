immanency: /ˈɪmənənsi/ | n. | 内在性；内在存在；临在 | The inherent presence or existence of something, especially a divine being, within the material world.  It suggests a close connection or even identification between the divine and the world. |例句：The immanency of God was a central theme in his theology.  上帝的内在性是他神学中的核心主题。

例句：The artist sought to capture the immanency of nature in her paintings.  这位艺术家试图在她的绘画中捕捉自然的内在性。


近义词：inherence, indwelling, presence, immediacy | 反义词：transcendence, externality


词源：源自拉丁语 *immanēre，意思是“to remain within”。前缀“im-”表示“in”或“into”，后缀“-ency”表示“state or quality”。
