michaela:/mɪˈkeɪlə/| n. |米凯拉（女子名）|A female given name, of Hebrew origin. It is a variant of Michaela, which itself is a feminine form of Michael.  The name Michael derives from the Hebrew name מִיכָאֵל (Mikha'el), meaning "Who is like God?".|My friend Michaela is a talented artist.  我的朋友米凯拉是一位有天赋的艺术家。|近义词: Michelle, Micaela|反义词: (无)|名词
