slippier:/ˈslɪpəriər/| adj.|更滑的，更狡猾的 | More slippery; more prone to slipping or to being deceptive.  It is the comparative form of "slippery".|例句：The roads are much slippier after the rain. 雨后道路更滑了。
The salesman was slippier than I thought; he tricked me into buying a faulty product.  这个推销员比我想象的更狡猾；他骗我买了一个有缺陷的产品。|近义词：slicker, more treacherous, more elusive |反义词：less slippery, stable, reliable
