frowners:/ˈfraʊnərz/| n. |皱眉的人；爱皱眉的人|People who frequently frown.  The word is a plural noun formed by adding the suffix "-ers" to the verb "frown," indicating those who perform the action. It lacks a widely established single-word antonym.  |例句：The frowners in the audience made the speaker nervous.  观众中那些皱眉的人让演讲者紧张起来。  The group of frowners sat silently, their displeasure evident.  那一群皱眉的人默默地坐着，他们的不满显而易见。|近义词：glowerers, scowlers|反义词:smilers
