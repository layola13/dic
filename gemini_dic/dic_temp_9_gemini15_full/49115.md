deinternationalize:/ˌdiˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. |使不再具有国际性；取消国际地位 | To remove the international character of something; to make something less international.  |The government decided to deinternationalize the airport, focusing on domestic flights instead. 政府决定取消该机场的国际地位，转而专注于国内航班。|nationalize, localize |internationalize, globalize |动词
