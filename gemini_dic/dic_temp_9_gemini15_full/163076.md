precocities: /prɪˈkɒsətiz/ | n. | 早熟；早慧；天赋早现 |  The early development of abilities or talents, especially in a child.  It refers to instances of exceptional ability or talent shown at a young age. |

例句：Her precocities in mathematics were evident from a young age.  = 她从很小的时候就展现出在数学方面的早慧。

例句：The child's musical precocities astonished everyone. =  孩子的音乐天赋早现令所有人惊讶。


近义词：precosity, early talent, giftedness, aptitude


反义词：backwardness, retardation, dullness


词性：名词
