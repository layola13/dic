hominins:/ˈhɒmɪnɪnz/|/ˈhɑːmɪnɪnz/| n. |人类（包括现代人和已灭绝的直立行走物种）|Hominins are a group of extinct and extant bipedal primates that includes modern humans and all species more closely related to humans than to chimpanzees.|The discovery of new hominin fossils is constantly reshaping our understanding of human evolution.  = 新的人类化石的发现不断重塑我们对人类进化的理解。

The oldest known hominin fossils date back millions of years. = 已知最古老的人类化石可以追溯到数百万年前。|近义词：human ancestors, bipeds|反义词：non-hominins, primates (excluding hominins)
