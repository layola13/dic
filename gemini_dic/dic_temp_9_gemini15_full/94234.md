imperiled:/ɪmˈperɪld/| adj.| 处于危险中的；濒临灭绝的 | exposed to danger; threatened with ruin or destruction. | 例句：The imperiled species needs our protection.  濒危物种需要我们的保护。| 例句：The imperiled economy needs a stimulus package. 岌岌可危的经济需要一揽子刺激计划。| 近义词：endangered, threatened, jeopardized, at risk | 反义词：safe, secure, protected

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fragile ecosystem is imperiled by pollution. = 脆弱的生态系统正受到污染的威胁。
The endangered species are imperiled by habitat loss. = 濒危物种因栖息地丧失而面临危险。

一般过去时:
The company was imperiled by the economic downturn. = 公司因经济低迷而陷入困境。
The project was imperiled by a lack of funding. = 该项目因缺乏资金而面临危险。

现在完成时:
The rainforest has been imperiled by deforestation. = 雨林因森林砍伐而受到了威胁。
The river has been imperiled by industrial pollution. = 河流因工业污染而受到了威胁。

过去完成时:
The negotiations had been imperiled by a lack of trust. = 谈判因缺乏信任而陷入了困境。
The peace talks had been imperiled by the recent violence. = 和谈因最近的暴力事件而面临危险。

将来时:
The coastal communities will be imperiled by rising sea levels. = 沿海社区将面临海平面上升的威胁。
The ancient monuments will be imperiled by neglect. = 古迹将因疏于保护而面临危险。

虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, the environment will be imperiled. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，环境将受到威胁。
If the dam had broken, the town would have been imperiled. = 如果大坝决堤，小镇就会面临危险。

被动语态:
The wildlife sanctuary was imperiled by the wildfire. = 野生动物保护区受到了野火的威胁。
The peace process is being imperiled by political instability. = 和平进程正受到政治动荡的威胁。

疑问句:
Is the project imperiled by the budget cuts? = 该项目是否因预算削减而面临危险？
Are the coral reefs imperiled by climate change? = 珊瑚礁是否因气候变化而面临危险？

否定句:
The situation is not imperiled, despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，但形势并未危及。
The company's future is not imperiled, thanks to the new investment. = 多亏了新的投资，公司的未来没有受到威胁。
