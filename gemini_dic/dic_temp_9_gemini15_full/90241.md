houseboat: /ˈhaʊsboʊt/ | n. | 房屋船，水上房屋 | A houseboat is a boat that has been designed or modified to be lived in as a permanent or temporary residence.  It typically includes living spaces, sleeping areas, and kitchen and bathroom facilities.  |例句：They live on a houseboat on the river.  他们住在河上的一艘房屋船上。

They spent their summer vacation cruising around the canals on their luxurious houseboat. 他们在豪华的房屋船上度过了在运河上巡游的暑假。|近义词：floating home, river home |反义词：land-based house, apartment
