mycoprotein:/ˌmaɪkoʊˈproʊtiːn/| n. |菌丝蛋白，真菌蛋白|A protein derived from the fungus Fusarium venenatum, used as a meat substitute.  It's a sustainable and low-fat source of protein.|Example:  Many vegetarian and vegan products utilize mycoprotein as a meat alternative. 许多素食和纯素产品都使用菌丝蛋白作为肉类替代品。|近义词：fungal protein, single-cell protein|反义词：animal protein
