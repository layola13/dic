handcuffing:/ˈhændˌkʌfɪŋ/| v.|上手铐；束缚|The act of fastening handcuffs on someone's wrists.  It can also refer to the feeling of being restricted or constrained.|例句：The police officer proceeded with the handcuffing of the suspect. = 警官着手给嫌疑犯戴上手铐。

例句：The strict rules felt like a form of constant handcuffing. = 严格的规定感觉像是持续不断的束缚。

近义词：restraining, shackling, binding |反义词：freeing, liberating
