lame:/leɪm/| adj.|无力的；瘸的；乏味的；蹩脚的|lacking in force or effectiveness; weak; unsatisfactory; crippled|例句：His excuse was lame. 他的借口很牵强。
例句：The comedian's jokes were lame.  这位喜剧演员的笑话很糟糕。|近义词：weak, feeble, unsatisfactory, poor, crippled|反义词：strong, powerful, excellent, superb
