pentile:/ˈpɛntaɪl/| n.| 戊烷；五角形图案 | A pentile is a type of display technology that uses a pentagonal arrangement of pixels to achieve higher resolution and better color reproduction than traditional rectangular pixel arrangements.  It is often used in mobile phone displays. | Example: The new phone boasts a pentile display for sharper images.  这款新手机拥有戊烷显示屏，图像更清晰。|近义词：display technology (显示技术) |反义词：无
