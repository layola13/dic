massicot:/ˈmæsɪkɒt/| n.|黄丹，氧化铅|A bright yellow pigment made from lead monoxide. It was historically used as a paint pigment and in glazes.  Massicot is a naturally occurring mineral, but it's also produced synthetically. |例句：The ancient vase was decorated with a vibrant massicot glaze.  古代花瓶装饰着鲜艳的黄丹釉。|The artist used massicot to create a sunny yellow hue in the painting.艺术家用黄丹在画中创造出阳光般的黄色色调。|近义词：lead monoxide, yellow lead|反义词：N/A
