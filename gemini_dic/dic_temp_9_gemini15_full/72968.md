fool:/fuːl/| n., v.|傻瓜；愚弄|

英文解析：A fool is a person who lacks good judgment or sense.  As a verb, it means to trick or deceive someone.

例句：
He was a fool to believe her lies. = 他真傻，居然相信她的谎话。

Don't be fooled by his charm; he's a scoundrel. = 不要被他迷人的外表所迷惑；他是个恶棍。


近义词：idiot, moron, simpleton, dupe (n.) ; trick, deceive, hoax (v.)

反义词：wise person, genius, sage (n.); enlighten, inform (v.)

词性：Noun, Verb
