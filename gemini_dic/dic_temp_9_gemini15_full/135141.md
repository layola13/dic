nonofficials:/ˌnɒnəˈfɪʃəlz/| n.|非官方人士，非官方机构|People or organizations not officially involved in something.  This term often refers to individuals or groups expressing opinions or engaging in actions outside of established official channels. |例句：The nonofficials voiced concerns about the new policy.  非官方人士对新政策表示担忧。

例句：Several nonofficials attended the meeting to provide input.  一些非官方人士出席了会议，以提供意见。


近义词：unofficial, outsiders, private citizens, laypeople

反义词：officials, authorities, government representatives


词性:名词
