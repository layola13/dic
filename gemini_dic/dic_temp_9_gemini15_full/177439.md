rebarbarization: /ˌriːˌbɑːrbəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |野蛮化，倒退，复归野蛮状态 | The process of reverting to a barbaric or uncivilized state; a return to primitive or savage conditions.  It often implies a decline in societal order, culture, or morality. |Example: The rebarbarization of society after the collapse of the government was evident in the rising crime rates and breakdown of social norms.  社会的野蛮化在政府垮台后的犯罪率上升和社会规范的崩溃中显而易见。|近义词：degeneration, devolution, barbarization |反义词：civilization, refinement, progress |  词性:名词
