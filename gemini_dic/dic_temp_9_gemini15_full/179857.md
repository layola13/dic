reexploration:/ˌriːˌɛksploʊˈreɪʃən/| n. |重新探索，再探索|The act of exploring something again; a renewed examination or investigation.|例句：The reexploration of the cave revealed new passages.  重新探索这个洞穴发现了新的通道。

例句：The team undertook a reexploration of the historical data to verify their findings.  团队对历史数据进行了重新探索以验证他们的发现。


近义词：reinvestigation, reexamination, review


反义词：initial exploration, first exploration


词性:名词
