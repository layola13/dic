midi:/ˈmɪdi/| n.|中音，中音域，MIDI|A MIDI (Musical Instrument Digital Interface) is a technical standard that allows electronic musical instruments, computers, and other related devices to connect and communicate with one another.|Example:  He used a MIDI keyboard to control the synthesizer. 他用MIDI键盘控制合成器。|近义词：medium|反义词：high, low
