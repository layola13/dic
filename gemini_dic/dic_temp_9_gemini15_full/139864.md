oculars:/ˈɑːkjʊlərz/ | n. | 目镜；(复数)眼镜 | Eyepieces of an optical instrument, such as a microscope or telescope.  Also can refer to eyeglasses. | Example: The microscopist adjusted the oculars to improve the image clarity. 显微镜操作者调节目镜以提高图像清晰度。  |近义词：eyepieces, lenses (in this context) | 反义词：objectives (in the context of microscopes and telescopes)
