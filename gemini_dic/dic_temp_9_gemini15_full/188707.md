runch:/rʌntʃ/| n. |用力咀嚼；猛吃；（非正式）简易午餐|A quick, informal meal, often eaten hurriedly.|例句：I only had time for a quick runch before work. 我上班前只来得及匆匆吃了一顿简易午餐。|近义词：lunch, snack, bite|反义词：formal dinner, feast


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He crunches the numbers every day. = 他每天都仔细核算数字。
They crunch the data to find the answer. = 他们分析数据以找到答案。


一般过去时:
She crunched the carrots before eating them. = 她吃胡萝卜之前先把它们咬碎了。
He crunched the leaves under his feet. = 他踩碎了脚下的树叶。


现在完成时:
I have crunched all the data for the report. = 我已经完成了报告所需的所有数据分析。
She has crunched the numbers and found the solution. = 她已经计算过数字并找到了解决方案。


过去完成时:
He had crunched the numbers before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就已经完成了数字的计算。
They had crunched all the evidence before reaching a verdict. = 他们在做出判决之前已经分析了所有证据。


将来时:
She will crunch the data next week. = 她下周将分析数据。
He will crunch the numbers to get a result. = 他将计算数字以获得结果。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had crunched the data more thoroughly. = 我希望我更彻底地分析了数据。


被动语态:

疑问句:
