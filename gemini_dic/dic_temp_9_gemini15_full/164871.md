/ˌpriːˈfreʃmən/| n. |预备新生，大学预科生 |A prefreshman is a student who is preparing to enter freshman year of college or university, often completing preparatory courses or programs.  They are typically in their final year of high school or a similar pre-university program. |Example: Many prefreshmen attend summer bridge programs to help them transition to college life.  许多预科生参加暑期桥梁课程来帮助他们过渡到大学生活。|近义词：prospective freshman, pre-college student |反义词：graduate, alumnus
