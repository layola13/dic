half-contentedness: /ˈhæf kənˈtɛntɪdnəs/ | n. | 半心半意；不甚满意；勉强满足 | A state of being only partially satisfied or pleased; a feeling of mild contentment mixed with dissatisfaction.  It suggests a lack of full commitment or enthusiasm. |


例句：

He felt a sense of half-contentedness with his job, knowing he could do better but lacking the motivation to change. = 他对自己的工作感到半心半意，他知道自己可以做得更好，但却缺乏改变的动力。


Despite achieving a certain level of success, she experienced half-contentedness, always yearning for more. = 尽管取得了一定的成功，但她仍然感到不甚满意，总是渴望更多。


近义词：

ambivalence, dissatisfaction, restlessness, complacency, lukewarmness


反义词：

full contentment, complete satisfaction, enthusiasm, zeal


词性: 名词
