carpentering:/ˈkɑːrpəntəriŋ/ | n. |木工，木匠技艺 |The art and skill of cutting, shaping, and joining timber to construct buildings, furniture, etc.  木工技艺，指切割、塑造和连接木材以建造建筑物、家具等的艺术和技巧。|例句：He learned carpentry from his father. 他向他父亲学习木工。

例句：The intricate carpentry in the cathedral is breathtaking. 教堂里精细的木工工艺令人叹为观止。


近义词：joinery, woodworking |反义词：无
