reveled:/rɪˈviːld/| vt.|揭露；展现；显示|To make (something previously unknown or secret) known.  To show or display (something) proudly or conspicuously.|例句：The journalist revealed the politician's secret affair.  记者揭露了这位政治家的秘密恋情。

例句：She revealed her beautiful new dress at the party.  她在派对上展示了她漂亮的连衣裙。


近义词：disclosed, unveiled, uncovered, exposed|反义词：concealed, hidden, obscured|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reveals his secrets to his best friend. 他向他最好的朋友透露他的秘密。
The painting reveals a hidden message. 这幅画揭示了一个隐藏的信息。


一般过去时:
She revealed her true feelings in her diary.  她在日记中表达了她真实的想法。
The investigation revealed a shocking truth. 调查揭示了一个令人震惊的事实。


现在完成时:
Scientists have revealed new evidence about the origin of the universe. 科学家们已经公布了关于宇宙起源的新证据。
The company has revealed its new product line. 公司已经发布了它的新产品线。


过去完成时:
The trial had revealed the defendant's guilt. 审判已经揭露了被告的罪行。
The study had revealed that the drug was ineffective. 研究已经表明该药物无效。


将来时:
The research will reveal the answers to many unanswered questions. 这项研究将揭示许多悬而未决问题的答案。
The upcoming report will reveal the company's financial status.  即将发布的报告将揭示公司的财务状况。


虚拟语气:
If she revealed her identity, she would be in danger. 如果她泄露身份，她将处于危险之中。
I wish he hadn't revealed my secret. 我真希望他没有泄露我的秘密。


被动语态:
The truth was revealed to the public. 事实真相被公之于众。
The secret was revealed by an anonymous source. 这个秘密被一个匿名消息来源透露了。


疑问句:
When will the results of the experiment be revealed? 实验结果何时公布？
What did the investigation reveal? 调查发现了什么？


否定句:
He did not reveal his plans to anyone. 他没有向任何人透露他的计划。
The company refused to reveal its financial data. 公司拒绝透露其财务数据。
