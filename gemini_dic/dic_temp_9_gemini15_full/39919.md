constrainingly:/kənˈstreɪnɪŋli/ | adv. | 限制地；约束地；局促地 | In a way that restricts or limits; in a manner that feels confined or oppressed.  | The artist felt constrainedly bound by the client's strict specifications.  艺术家感到受到客户严格规范的限制。 | restrictively, limitingly, oppressively | freely, openly, expansively | 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such, it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  Its usage is primarily descriptive of the manner in which an action is performed or a state exists.

Example sentences illustrating its use:

He spoke constrainingly, as if afraid to voice his true opinions. 他说话吞吞吐吐的，好像害怕说出自己的真实想法。

She moved constrainingly, her stiff joints hindering her graceful movements. 她行动迟缓，僵硬的关节阻碍了她优雅的动作。


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases directly using "constrainingly".  Its usage is more integrated into sentences to describe the manner of an action or state.  Examples could be constructed, but they wouldn't be common idiomatic phrases.  For example:

"To act constrainingly" (to act in a restricted manner).  "To speak constrainingly" (to speak hesitantly or cautiously).  These are descriptive uses, not fixed phrases.
