illusional: /ɪˈluʒənəl/ | adj. | 虚幻的；不真实的；错觉的 |  Relating to or characterized by illusion; unreal or imaginary.  It suggests something that is deceptive or based on a false perception of reality.  |

例句：His hopes of winning the lottery were purely illusional.  他的中彩票的希望完全是幻想。

例句：The magician created an illusional world on stage. 魔术师在舞台上创造了一个虚幻的世界。


近义词：illusory, unreal, imaginary, fanciful, deceptive

反义词：real, actual, factual, genuine, true


词性：形容词
