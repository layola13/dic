rhetor: /ˈrɛtər/ | n. | 修辞学家；雄辩家；修辞法 | A rhetor is a skilled speaker or writer who uses language effectively and persuasively.  Rhetoric refers to the art of effective or persuasive speaking or writing, especially the use of figures of speech and other compositional techniques. | Example:  Cicero was a renowned rhetor of ancient Rome.  西塞罗是古罗马著名的雄辩家。 |近义词: orator, speaker, writer |反义词: inarticulate, uncommunicative |名词
