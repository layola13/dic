farmlands:/ˈfɑːrmlændz/| n.|农田，耕地|Areas of land used for farming, typically including fields, pastures, and orchards.  Farmlands are crucial for food production and often characterize rural landscapes.|例句：The farmlands stretched as far as the eye could see.  这片农田一眼望不到边。|例句：The fertile farmlands produced abundant harvests. 肥沃的农田带来了丰收。|近义词：cultivated land, farmland, agricultural land|反义词：urban areas, wilderness, undeveloped land|名词
