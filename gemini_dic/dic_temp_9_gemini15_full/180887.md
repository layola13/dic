词源:

The word "rehumanizations" is derived from the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back"), the word "human" (referring to a person or mankind), and the suffix "-ization" (indicating the process of making something into something else).  Therefore, it literally means the process of making something human again or restoring human qualities.  The plural form "-izations" indicates multiple instances or processes of rehumanization.
