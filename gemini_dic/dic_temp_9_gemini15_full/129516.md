non-attachment: /ˌnɒnəˈtætʃmənt/ | n. | 不执着；超然；淡泊 | The state of being free from attachment to material possessions or worldly concerns; detachment.  It often refers to a philosophical or spiritual concept, particularly in Buddhism, emphasizing freedom from craving and aversion. | 例句：Practicing non-attachment can lead to greater inner peace.  练习不执着可以带来更大的内心平静。
He achieved non-attachment through years of meditation. 他通过多年的冥想实现了不执着。| 近义词：detachment, disengagement, indifference | 反义词：attachment, obsession, craving |  名词
