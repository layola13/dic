houses:/ˈhaʊzɪz/| n.|房屋，住宅|Buildings used as dwellings.|例句：He owns several houses in the city. 他在城里拥有几栋房子。|例句：They are building new houses in the suburbs. 他们在郊区建造新房子。|近义词：homes, dwellings, residences|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He houses his tools in the shed. = 他把工具放在棚子里。
The hotel houses over 200 guests. = 这家酒店住着超过200位客人。

一般过去时:
They housed the refugees in a temporary shelter. = 他们把难民安置在一个临时住所。
The old building housed a library many years ago. = 这座旧建筑物很多年前曾是一家图书馆。

现在完成时:
The organization has housed thousands of homeless people. = 这个组织已经为数千名无家可归者提供了住所。
This house has housed three generations of my family. = 这栋房子住过我家的三代人。

过去完成时:
They had housed the animals before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把动物安置好了。
The museum had housed the artifacts for decades before they were moved. = 在文物被搬走之前，博物馆已经收藏了这些文物几十年了。

将来时:
They will house the exhibition in the main hall. = 他们将在主厅举办展览。
The new building will house a school and a community center. = 新大楼将容纳一所学校和一个社区中心。

虚拟语气:
If they had housed the animals properly, they wouldn't have escaped. = 如果他们当初妥善安置了动物，它们就不会逃跑了。
I wish we had housed the guests in a more comfortable hotel. = 我真希望我们把客人安排在一个更舒适的酒店。

被动语态:
The refugees were housed in temporary tents. = 难民被安置在临时帐篷里。
The art collection is housed in a secure vault. = 艺术收藏品被保存在一个安全的保险库里。

疑问句:
Where are the supplies housed? = 这些物资放在哪里？
How many people does this building house? = 这栋建筑物住着多少人？

否定句:
The old building doesn't house anyone anymore. = 这座旧建筑物不再住人了。
They didn't house the animals in the barn. = 他们没有把动物放在谷仓里。
