reimplied:/ˌriːɪmˈplaɪd/| v.|再次暗示，重新暗示|To imply something again or in a different way.  It suggests a previous implication that is being reinforced or clarified.|例句：The lawyer reimplied the defendant's guilt during his closing argument. 律师在结案陈词中再次暗示了被告的罪行。

例句：The professor reimplied the importance of regular study through additional examples. 教授通过额外的例子再次强调了定期学习的重要性。


近义词：reiterate, suggest, insinuate, hint at


反义词：explicitly state, directly declare


词性：动词
