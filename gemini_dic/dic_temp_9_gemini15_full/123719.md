moonlights: /ˈmuːnlaɪts/ | v. |兼职做…；月光族|To have a second job in addition to one's regular employment.  |例句：He moonlights as a bartender to supplement his income. 他兼职做调酒师以补贴家用。|例句：She moonlights as a writer to pursue her passion. 她兼职写作来追求自己的爱好。|近义词：works a second job, has a side hustle |反义词：works full-time, has only one job


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moonlights as a taxi driver. = 他兼职做出租车司机。
She moonlights as a singer in a local bar. = 她在当地酒吧兼职唱歌。

一般过去时:
He moonlighted as a waiter during college. = 他大学期间兼职做过服务员。
She moonlighted as a tutor to save money for her trip. = 她兼职做家教来攒钱旅行。

现在完成时:
He has moonlighted as a freelance writer for several years. = 他已经兼职自由撰稿人好几年了。
She has moonlighted at various jobs to pay off her student loans. = 她做过各种兼职来偿还学生贷款。

过去完成时:
He had moonlighted as a security guard before he got his current job. = 在找到现在的工作之前，他做过兼职保安。
She had moonlighted at a bakery before she started her own business. = 在自己创业之前，她曾在面包店兼职。

将来时:
He will moonlight as a photographer this summer. = 他今年夏天将兼职摄影师。
She will moonlight at a bookstore during the holiday season. = 她将在节日期间在书店兼职。

I wish I could moonlight as a musician, but I don't have the time. = 我真希望我能兼职当音乐家，但我没有时间。
