dicer:/ˈdaɪsər/| n. |切丁机；骰子玩家|A dicer is a machine used to cut food into small cubes, or a person who plays dice games.|例句：The chef used a dicer to quickly chop the vegetables.  厨师用切丁机快速切碎蔬菜。
例句：He's a skilled dicer, always winning at the casino. 他是个熟练的骰子玩家，总是在赌场赢钱。|近义词：cube cutter (for machine), gambler (for person)|反义词：None specifically applicable|名词
