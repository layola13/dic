pay-up:/peɪ ʌp/| v.|付清；全部付清|To pay a debt or bill completely.|例句：You need to pay up your outstanding balance by the end of the month.  你需要在本月底前付清欠款。|例句：He finally paid up all his gambling debts. 他终于还清了他所有的赌债。|近义词：settle, clear, liquidate|反义词：owe, default


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pays up his bills on time every month. = 他每月按时付清账单。
They pay up their debts promptly. = 他们迅速偿还债务。


一般过去时:
She paid up her loan last year. = 她去年还清了贷款。
We paid up all our expenses before the trip. = 我们在旅行前付清了所有费用。


现在完成时:
I have paid up all my credit card bills. = 我已经付清了所有的信用卡账单。
They have paid up the entire amount. = 他们已经付清了全部款项。


过去完成时:
He had paid up his dues before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经付清了会费。
She had paid up the rent before moving out. = 她在搬出去之前已经付清了房租。


将来时:
I will pay up the balance next week. = 我下周将付清余额。
They will pay up the debt as soon as possible. = 他们会尽快还清债务。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would pay up the debt immediately. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会立即还清债务。
I wish I could pay up all my bills right now. = 我希望我现在能付清所有账单。


被动语态:
The debt must be paid up by next Friday. = 债务必须在下周五前付清。
The bill should have been paid up a long time ago. = 账单很久以前就应该付清了。


疑问句:
Have you paid up the rent yet? = 你付清房租了吗？
Will he pay up the money he owes me? = 他会还我欠我的钱吗？


否定句:
He didn't pay up his taxes on time. = 他没有按时纳税。
She hasn't paid up her medical bills yet. = 她还没有付清医疗费。


PHRASE->
pay up in full = 全额付款
pay up front = 预付
pay up to date = 支付到期款项
pay up on time = 按时付款
pay up the arrears = 付清欠款
