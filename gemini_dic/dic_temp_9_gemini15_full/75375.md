frowner:/ˈfraʊnər/| n. |皱眉的人，愁眉苦脸的人 |A frowner is a person who habitually frowns.  They often express displeasure, displeasure, or unhappiness through their facial expression. |Example: He was a frowner, his face etched with lines of perpetual discontent. 他是个皱眉的人，脸上刻着永久的不满。|近义词：grumbler, pessimist |反义词：smiler, optimist |名词
