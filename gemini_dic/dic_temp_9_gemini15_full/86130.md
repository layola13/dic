hearteningly: /ˈhɑːrtənɪŋli/ | adv. |令人鼓舞地；令人振奋地 |  In a way that gives encouragement or hope.  It describes something that is encouraging and uplifting. |例句：Hearteningly, the economy showed signs of recovery.  令人鼓舞的是，经济显示出复苏的迹象。

例句：Hearteningly,  many people volunteered to help after the disaster. 令人鼓舞的是，灾难发生后许多人自愿帮忙。


近义词：encouragingly, reassuringly, hopefully, promisingly


反义词：discouragingly, depressingly, dishearteningly


词源：源自动词hearten (使振奋；使高兴)，加上副词后缀-ingly构成。  Hearten itself comes from the Old English word "heortan," meaning "to encourage" or "to make happy," related to the word "heart."
