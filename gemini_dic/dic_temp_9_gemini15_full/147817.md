overprovoke:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvoʊk/| vt.|过度激怒，过度刺激|To provoke excessively or beyond a reasonable limit.  This implies going beyond the point of simply irritating someone and causing a much stronger, potentially negative reaction.|例句：His constant criticism overprovoked her, leading to a major argument.  他的不断批评过度激怒了她，导致了一场大争吵。|例句：The inflammatory rhetoric overprovoked the already tense situation.煽动性的言论过度刺激了本已紧张的局势。|近义词：exasperate, infuriate, enrage|反义词：soothe, appease, calm
