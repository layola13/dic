intermetropolitan:/ˌɪntərˌmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtən/| adj.|大都市间的，大都市范围内的| Relating to or connecting several metropolitan areas.  The term suggests a level of interaction and connection beyond a single city. |例句：The intermetropolitan rail system connects several major cities.  大都市间的铁路系统连接着几个主要城市。


例句：The intermetropolitan highway system facilitates trade and travel between cities. 大都市间的公路系统促进了城市之间的贸易和交通。


近义词：regional,  multinational (in the context of connections between cities)


反义词：local, intraurban


词性：形容词
