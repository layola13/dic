guelderrose:/ˈɡɛldərˌroʊz/| n. |接骨木属植物，荚蒾属植物|A guelder-rose is a shrub with white flowers, belonging to the Viburnum genus.  It is also known as snowball tree or cramp bark.  The name derives from Gelderland, a region in the Netherlands where it was once common.|The guelder-rose bushes were in full bloom, creating a stunning display of white. 接骨木属植物正盛开着，展现出一片令人惊叹的白色景象。|  snowball bush, Viburnum opulus |  (no direct antonym)|名词
