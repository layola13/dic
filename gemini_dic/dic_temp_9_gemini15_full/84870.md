hanging in there: /ˈhæŋɪŋ ɪn ðɛr/ | phrasal verb | 坚持下去；挺住；保持坚强 |  This phrase means to persevere despite difficulties or challenges. It expresses encouragement and resilience.  |  例句：Despite all the setbacks, she's hanging in there. 尽管遭遇了所有挫折，她仍然坚持着。

例句：He's been through a lot lately, but he's hanging in there. 他最近经历了很多，但他坚持了下来。


近义词：persevere, endure, persist, keep going, stay strong | 反义词：give up, surrender, quit, collapse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are hanging in there despite the difficult circumstances. = 尽管情况艰难，他们仍在坚持。
We are hanging in there, waiting for the results. = 我们在坚持，等待结果。


一般过去时:
She hung in there until the very end. = 她坚持到了最后。
They hung in there through the storm. = 他们在暴风雨中坚持了下来。


现在完成时:
He has been hanging in there for months. = 他已经坚持了好几个月了。
We have hung in there through thick and thin. = 我们风雨同舟，坚持了下来。


过去完成时:
They had hung in there for years before finally giving up. = 他们在最终放弃之前坚持了好几年。
She had hung in there despite facing numerous obstacles. = 尽管面临诸多障碍，她仍然坚持了下来。


将来时:
I will hang in there no matter what happens. = 无论发生什么，我都会坚持下去。
We will hang in there until we succeed. = 我们会坚持到成功为止。


虚拟语气:
If I were in your shoes, I would hang in there. = 如果我是你，我会坚持下去。
I wish I could hang in there a little longer. = 我希望我能再坚持一会儿。


被动语态: (This phrase is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Are you hanging in there? = 你还好吗？/ 你坚持得怎么样？
Is he still hanging in there? = 他还在坚持吗？


否定句:
They are not hanging in there anymore. = 他们不再坚持了。
He didn't hang in there long enough. = 他坚持的时间不够长。
