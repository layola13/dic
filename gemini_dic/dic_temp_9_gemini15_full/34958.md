clod:/klɑːd/ | n. |土块；笨蛋；傻瓜 | A lump of earth or clay.  A stupid or insensitive person. |例句：He tripped over a clod of earth. 他被一块土块绊倒了。


例句：He's such a clod; he doesn't understand the situation. 他真是个笨蛋，不明白情况。

近义词：lump, clodhopper, oaf, dolt, blockhead

反义词：genius, intellectual, bright, clever

词性:名词
