finan:/ˈfaɪnæn/| n. |财政，金融；资金|Finance is the management of money.  |例句：The company's finances are in a precarious state. 这家公司的财政状况岌岌可危。|近义词：funds, resources, capital|反义词:debt, deficit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Finance affects every aspect of our lives. = 金融影响着我们生活的方方面面。
The company finances its operations through loans. = 公司通过贷款为其运营提供资金。


一般过去时:
They financed the project with a bank loan. = 他们通过银行贷款为项目融资。
The government financed the construction of the new highway. = 政府资助了新建高速公路的建设。


现在完成时:
We have financed several successful startups. = 我们已经为几家成功的初创企业提供了资金。
The government has financed numerous research projects. = 政府已经资助了许多研究项目。


过去完成时:
They had already financed the acquisition before the market crashed. = 市场崩盘之前，他们已经为收购提供了资金。
The company had financed its expansion through internal resources. = 公司通过内部资源为其扩张提供了资金。


将来时:
We will finance the new factory next year. = 我们明年将为新工厂提供资金。
The government will finance the development of renewable energy. = 政府将资助可再生能源的开发。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would finance a larger project. = 如果我们有更多的钱，我们会资助一个更大的项目。
I wish we could finance the research project. = 我希望我们能够为这个研究项目提供资金。


被动语态:
The project was financed by a private investor. = 该项目由一位私人投资者提供资金。
The construction of the bridge is being financed by the government. = 大桥的建设由政府提供资金。


疑问句:
How will you finance your education? = 你将如何资助你的教育？
Who financed the research? = 谁资助了这项研究？


否定句:
They did not finance the project. = 他们没有为该项目提供资金。
The company could not finance its expansion. = 公司无法为其扩张提供资金。
