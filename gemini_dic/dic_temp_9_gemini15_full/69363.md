federator:/ˈfɛdəreɪtər/| n. |联合者；联合组织；联盟成员|A person or thing that federates or unites; a member of a federation.|The software acts as a federator, consolidating data from multiple sources.  该软件充当联合者，整合来自多个来源的数据。|近义词：uniter, consolidator, integrator |反义词：separator, divider
