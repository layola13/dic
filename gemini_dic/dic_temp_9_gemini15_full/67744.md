eyeballers:/ˈaɪˌbɔːlər/| n.|  盯着看的人；（尤指）密切关注某事物的人 |A person who stares intensely, especially someone closely monitoring something.  |例句：The players were constantly eyeballed by the coach during practice.  球员在训练期间一直受到教练的密切关注。|近义词：watchers, observers, monitors |反义词：ignorer, overlooker |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The coach eyeballs the players constantly. = 教练一直盯着球员看。
They eyeball the market trends carefully. = 他们仔细观察市场趋势。


一般过去时:
The detective eyeballed the suspect for any suspicious movements. = 侦探密切注视着嫌疑人的任何可疑举动。
She eyeballed him across the crowded room. = 她隔着拥挤的房间盯着他看。


现在完成时:
The investors have eyeballed the company's financial reports for weeks. = 投资者们已经关注这家公司的财务报表几周了。
He has eyeballed that property for months, hoping the price will drop. = 他已经关注那处房产几个月了，希望价格会下降。


过去完成时:
They had eyeballed the situation for a long time before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经观察了很久的情况。
She had eyeballed the painting for years before she finally bought it. = 她关注那幅画多年后才最终买下它。


将来时:
We will eyeball the progress of the project closely. = 我们将密切关注项目的进展。
They will eyeball the competition's new product launch. = 他们将密切关注竞争对手的新产品发布。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would eyeball the contract carefully before signing. = 如果我是你，我会在签字之前仔细阅读合同。
I wish I had eyeballed that investment opportunity more closely. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地关注那个投资机会。


被动语态:
The suspect was eyeballed by the police throughout the interrogation. = 嫌疑人在整个审讯过程中一直受到警方的密切监视。
The company's performance is being eyeballed by potential investors. = 公司的表现正受到潜在投资者的密切关注。


疑问句:
Are they eyeballing the competition's strategies? = 他们在密切关注竞争对手的策略吗？
Was the suspect being eyeballed by the security cameras? = 嫌疑人被安全摄像头监视了吗？


否定句:
The manager didn't eyeball the employees' work closely enough. = 经理没有足够仔细地检查员工的工作。
They didn't eyeball the risks involved before making the investment. = 他们在投资前没有仔细评估风险。
