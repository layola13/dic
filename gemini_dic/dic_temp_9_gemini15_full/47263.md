de:/də, deɪ/| prep.|从；自；关于；由于；在…方面|英文解析：A preposition indicating origin, source, position, or cause.  It can also show a relationship or connection.|例句：He came from Germany. 他来自德国。|近义词：from, of, about, concerning, regarding|反义词：to, towards


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The book is from the library. = 这本书来自图书馆。
The letter is from my mother. = 这封信是我母亲写的。


一般过去时:
She came from a small town. = 她来自一个小镇。
The news was from a reliable source. = 这个消息来自可靠的消息来源。


现在完成时:
The information has come from several sources. = 信息来自多个来源。
We have learned a lot from this experience. = 我们从这次经历中学到了很多。


过去完成时:
He had already left before I got there. = 我到那里之前他已经离开了。
She had heard about it from a friend. = 她已经从一个朋友那里听说过这件事了。


将来时:
The package will arrive from China next week. = 包裹下周将从中国到达。
She will graduate from university next year. = 她明年将从大学毕业。


虚拟语气:
If I were from a wealthy family, I would have more opportunities. = 如果我来自富裕家庭，我会拥有更多机会。
I wish I were from a different country. = 我希望我来自不同的国家。


被动语态:
The decision was made from the board of directors. = 这个决定是由董事会做出的。
The information was obtained from reliable sources. = 信息是从可靠的来源获得的。


疑问句:
Where are you from? = 你是哪里人？
What is this made from? = 这是什么做的？


否定句:
This painting is not from the Renaissance. = 这幅画不是文艺复兴时期的。
The problem doesn't stem from a lack of effort. = 问题并非源于缺乏努力。


PHRASE->
from time to time = 偶尔
from now on = 从现在开始
from scratch = 从零开始
from top to bottom = 从上到下
from head to toe = 从头到脚
