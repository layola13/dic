inevasible:/ɪnˈevəsəbl/ | adj. | 不可避免的，无法逃避的 | Incapable of being avoided or escaped.  The word suggests a situation or consequence that is unavoidable, often implying a sense of inevitability or doom. | Example: The economic downturn was inevasible; no amount of government intervention could have prevented it.  经济衰退是不可避免的；任何政府干预都无法阻止它。 |近义词： unavoidable, inescapable, inevitable, certain | 反义词： avoidable, escapable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The consequences of this decision are inevasible. = 这个决定的后果是不可避免的。
This problem is inevasible, no matter what we do. = 无论我们做什么，这个问题都是不可避免的。

一般过去时:
The war was inevasible despite peace talks. = 尽管进行了和平谈判，战争还是不可避免的。
The accident was inevasible given the poor road conditions. = 鉴于糟糕的路况，事故是不可避免的。

现在完成时:
The crisis has proven inevasible, despite our best efforts. = 尽管我们尽了最大努力，这场危机已被证明是不可避免的。
The damage has become inevasible after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，损失已无法避免。


过去完成时:
It had become inevasible that the company would go bankrupt. = 公司破产已成为不可避免的事实。
By then, it had become inevasible that they would lose the election. = 到那时，他们输掉选举已成为不可避免的事实。

将来时:
Further conflict is inevasible unless a compromise is reached. = 除非达成妥协，否则进一步的冲突是不可避免的。
The increase in taxes is inevasible next year. = 明年税收增加是不可避免的。

虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, the disaster might have been inevasible. = 如果我们早点采取行动，灾难可能就不可避免了。(这句话表达的是虚拟语气，表示对过去情况的假设，如果早点行动，灾难可能无法避免，因此用might have been inevasible而不是was inevasible)
If only we had known, the tragedy would have been inevasible.  = 如果我们早知道的话，悲剧就不可避免了。


被动语态:
The outcome was inevasible, as demonstrated by the evidence. = 证据表明，结果是不可避免的。
The failure was inevasible, given the lack of resources. = 鉴于资源匮乏，失败是不可避免的。

疑问句:
Is this outcome truly inevasible? = 这个结果真的不可避免吗？
Is there any way to avoid this inevasible consequence? = 有什么办法可以避免这个不可避免的后果吗？

否定句:
The situation is not inevasible; we can still find a solution. = 情况并非不可避免；我们仍然可以找到解决方案。
The defeat was not inevasible; they could have won if they had tried harder. = 失败并非不可避免；如果他们更努力一些，他们本来可以获胜的。
