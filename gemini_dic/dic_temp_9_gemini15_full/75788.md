fumaroles:/ˈfjuːməroʊlz/ | n. | 喷气孔；火山喷气口 | openings in the earth's surface that emit steam and gases | The fumaroles on the volcano's slopes hissed and steamed.  火山斜坡上的喷气孔发出嘶嘶声和蒸汽声。| vents, geysers |  (无明确反义词，可考虑cold springs) | 名词
