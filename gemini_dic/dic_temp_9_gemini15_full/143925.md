outpromises: /ˌaʊtˈprɑːmɪsɪz/ | v. |  许诺过多；言过其实 | To promise more than one can deliver; to make excessive or unrealistic promises. |  例句：The government outpromised itself on tax cuts and now faces a budget crisis. 政府在减税问题上许诺过多，现在面临预算危机。  | 近义词：overpromise, exaggerate, oversell | 反义词：underpromise, undersell | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outpromises his abilities regularly. = 他经常言过其实，超过自己的能力。
The company outpromises its customers on delivery times. = 公司在交货时间上对客户许诺过多。

一般过去时:
They outpromised themselves at the negotiation table. = 他们在谈判桌上许诺过多。
The candidate outpromised what he could realistically deliver. = 候选人许诺了他实际上无法兑现的事情。

现在完成时:
The project manager has outpromised the resources available. = 项目经理已经许诺了超出可用资源的承诺。
The company has outpromised its capacity for several years running. = 这家公司连续几年来都许诺了超出其能力的承诺。

过去完成时:
They had outpromised on the project, leading to delays. = 他们在项目上许诺过多，导致了延误。
The team had outpromised their capabilities before the deadline approached. = 团队在截止日期临近之前就许诺了超出其能力的承诺。

将来时:
He will outpromise himself again if he's not careful. = 如果他不小心，他还会言过其实。
The politician will likely outpromise during the election campaign. = 这位政治家在竞选期间很可能会言过其实。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't outpromised, the project would have been completed on time. = 如果他没有许诺过多，项目就能按时完成了。
If only they hadn't outpromised the clients! = 但愿他们没有对客户许诺过多！


被动语态:
The promises were outpromised. = 这些承诺是言过其实的。（此用法较少见，通常用被动语态表达结果）
The team felt that their capabilities were outpromised by the management. = 团队感觉他们的能力被管理层夸大了。

疑问句:
Did the CEO outpromise the company's capabilities? = 首席执行官是否言过其实，超过了公司的能力？
Does he often outpromise what he can achieve? = 他经常言过其实，超过他所能达到的吗？

否定句:
He didn't outpromise on this occasion. = 他这次没有言过其实。
The company tried not to outpromise its production capacity. = 公司试图避免许诺超出其生产能力的承诺。
