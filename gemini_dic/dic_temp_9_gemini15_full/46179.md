cyphering:/ˈsaɪfərɪŋ/ | v. | 密码编写；计算；暗中策划 | The act of writing in or using a cipher; the process of solving a cipher;  secret or coded communication.  It can also refer to the act of making calculations, often implying a complex or tedious process. | 例句：She spent hours cyphering the secret message. 她花了几个小时破译秘密信息。

The team was cyphering the complex data to find the solution.  团队正在计算复杂数据以找到解决方案。 | 近义词：deciphering, encoding, calculating, computing | 反义词：decoding (in the context of ciphers), revealing (in the context of secrets)
