consolidated:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪtɪd/ | adj. v. |巩固的；合并的；统一的 | Strengthened; combined; unified.  |The consolidated report showed a significant increase in profits. 巩固后的报告显示利润显著增长。|近义词：strengthened, combined, unified, merged |反义词：weakened, fragmented, separated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consolidates his power. 他巩固他的权力。
The company consolidates its operations. 这家公司合并其业务。

一般过去时:
They consolidated their resources. 他们整合了他们的资源。
The government consolidated its control. 政府巩固了其控制权。

现在完成时:
She has consolidated her position in the company. 她巩固了她在公司的地位。
The team has consolidated its lead in the game. 球队巩固了它的领先优势。

过去完成时:
The army had consolidated its defenses before the attack. 军队在进攻前已经巩固了防御工事。
He had consolidated his notes before the exam. 他考试前已经整理好了笔记。

将来时:
They will consolidate their efforts to achieve the goal. 他们将团结一致以实现目标。
The company will consolidate its branches. 公司将合并其分支机构。

虚拟语气:
If they had consolidated their resources earlier, they would have succeeded. 如果他们早点整合资源，他们就会成功。
I wish we had consolidated our position before the market downturn. 我希望我们在市场低迷之前巩固了我们的地位。

被动语态:
The accounts were consolidated into a single report. 这些账目被合并成一份报告。
The two companies are being consolidated. 这两家公司正在合并。

疑问句:
Have they consolidated their holdings? 他们已经合并了他们的资产吗？
Will the government consolidate its power? 政府会巩固其权力吗？

否定句:
They did not consolidate their gains. 他们没有巩固他们的成果。
The company hasn't consolidated its market share. 这家公司没有巩固其市场份额。


PHRASE->
consolidated financial statements 综合财务报表
consolidated debt 合并债务
consolidated assets 合并资产
consolidated results 综合结果
consolidated market 合并市场


词源:
From Latin *consolidatus, past participle of consolidare "to make solid, strengthen," from con- "together" + solidus "solid."
