infringes:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒ/| v. |侵犯；违反；触犯 |To act in a way that breaks a law, rule, or agreement.  It also suggests a gradual or subtle encroachment on something. |例句：The company infringed the patent by copying its design.  这家公司通过抄袭其设计侵犯了专利。

例句：Their actions infringed on my privacy. 他们的行为侵犯了我的隐私。


近义词：violate, encroach upon, trespass on, breach, contravene |反义词：respect, uphold, comply with


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He infringes the law. = 他违反法律。
The new regulations infringe on our rights. = 新规侵犯了我们的权利。


一般过去时:
They infringed copyright law. = 他们侵犯了版权法。
The construction project infringed on the neighboring property. = 建设项目侵犯了邻近的房产。


现在完成时:
She has infringed upon his intellectual property rights. = 她侵犯了他的知识产权。
The company has infringed the contract terms repeatedly. = 这家公司多次违反合同条款。


过去完成时:
They had infringed the agreement before the dispute arose. = 争议发生之前，他们就已经违反了协议。
The government had infringed on citizens' freedoms for many years. = 政府多年来一直侵犯公民自由。


将来时:
He will infringe the rules if he is not careful. = 如果他不小心，他将违反规则。
The development project will infringe on the local ecosystem. = 开发项目将侵犯当地生态系统。


虚拟语气:
If they infringed the law, they would be punished. = 如果他们违反法律，他们将受到惩罚。
I wish they hadn't infringed on my personal space. = 我真希望他们没有侵犯我的私人空间。


被动语态:
The patent was infringed by a competitor. = 专利被竞争对手侵犯了。
Their rights were infringed by the new legislation. = 他们的权利被新法规侵犯了。


疑问句:
Does this action infringe on any laws? = 这个行为是否侵犯了任何法律？
Did their actions infringe on your property rights? = 他们的行为是否侵犯了你的财产权？


否定句:
He did not infringe any copyright laws. = 他没有侵犯任何版权法。
The project will not infringe on the environment. = 该项目不会影响环境。


PHRASE->
infringe upon/on = 侵犯；违反
infringe a copyright = 侵犯版权
infringe a patent = 侵犯专利
infringe the law = 违法
infringe someone's rights = 侵犯某人的权利

词源:
From Old French *enfringre, from en- "in, on" + fringre "to break".  The "fringre" root is related to the Old High German *bringan "to break", ultimately from a Proto-Germanic root *breŋk-.
