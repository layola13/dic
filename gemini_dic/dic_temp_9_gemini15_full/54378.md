dishwashers:/ˈdɪʃˌwɒʃərz/ | n. | 洗碗机；洗碗工(复数) | A machine for washing dishes.  Also refers to people who wash dishes. | 例句：Our new dishwashers is so quiet. = 我们的新洗碗机非常安静。

例句：Many restaurants employ dishwashers to clean up after meals. = 许多餐馆雇佣洗碗工来清理餐后餐具。


近义词：dishwashing machine


反义词：无


词性:名词
