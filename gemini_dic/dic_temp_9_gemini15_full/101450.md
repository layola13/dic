irc:/ˈaɪ.ɑːrˈsiː/|/ˈaɪ.ɑːrˈsiː/| n. |互联网中继聊天|Internet Relay Chat, a system for exchanging messages in real time over the Internet.  It's a text-based communication protocol that allows users to communicate with each other in chat rooms or through private messages.|Example:  I met my best friend on IRC. = 我在IRC上认识了我最好的朋友。|近义词：chat, online chat, messaging|反义词：face-to-face communication


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, typical verb tenses are not applicable.  Instead, we can show examples of how it's used in different sentence structures.
