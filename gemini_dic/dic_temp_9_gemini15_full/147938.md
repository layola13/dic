overreporting:/ˌoʊvərˈriːpɔːrtɪŋ/ | n. | 过度报告，夸大报告 | The act of reporting something as being larger, more significant, or more frequent than it actually is.  This can be intentional or unintentional.  It often involves exaggerating figures, inflating statistics, or misrepresenting data. |例句：The study found evidence of overreporting of injuries in the accident report. 这项研究发现事故报告中存在夸大受伤情况的证据。

The company's overreporting of profits led to an investigation by the SEC.  该公司的利润夸大报告导致了美国证券交易委员会的调查。|近义词：exaggeration, inflation, misrepresentation |反义词：underreporting, downplaying, minimizing |
