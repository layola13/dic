parkway:/ˈpɑːrkeɪ/ | n. | 公园路，林荫大道 | A wide road, often landscaped, designed for pleasant driving, usually with a central reservation or median.  | Example: We drove along the scenic parkway, enjoying the beautiful scenery. 我们沿着风景优美的林荫大道行驶，欣赏美丽的风景。 | 近义词：boulevard, avenue | 反义词：alley, lane
