dells:/delz/| n.|山谷，峡谷|A dell is a small valley, typically one that is wooded and secluded.  It often implies a sense of beauty and tranquility.|例句：The sunlight dappled through the trees in the secluded dell.  阳光透过树林洒落在隐蔽的山谷里。

例句：We hiked down into the dell to find a hidden waterfall. 我们徒步走到山谷里，发现了一个隐藏的瀑布。


近义词：valley, dale, glen, ravine|反义词：mountain, peak, summit|词性:名词
