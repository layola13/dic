embed:/ɪmˈbed/ | vt. |嵌入，植入，嵌进|To fix something firmly in a surrounding mass.  To incorporate something within something else.|例句：The journalist embedded herself with the troops.  记者随部队一起行动。

例句：The stone was embedded in the concrete.  石头嵌在水泥里。


近义词：insert, implant, integrate, fix


反义词：remove, extract, dislodge


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They embed the video in their website. = 他们将视频嵌入他们的网站。
The artist embeds precious stones in her paintings. = 艺术家在她的画作中镶嵌宝石。


一般过去时:
She embedded the quote in her essay. = 她把引言嵌入她的论文中。
He embedded the chip in the device. = 他将芯片嵌入设备中。


现在完成时:
They have embedded the tracker in the package. = 他们已将追踪器嵌入包裹中。
We have embedded several links in this document. = 我们已在此文档中嵌入多个链接。


过去完成时:
The spy had embedded himself in the organization for years. = 间谍已潜伏在该组织多年。
She had embedded the secret message in the song lyrics. = 她已将秘密信息嵌入歌曲歌词中。


将来时:
They will embed the sensor in the soil. = 他们将传感器嵌入土壤中。
We will embed the map in the presentation. = 我们将在演示文稿中嵌入地图。


虚拟语气:
If they embedded the code correctly, the program would work. = 如果他们正确地嵌入代码，程序就会运行。
I wish I had embedded the image before submitting the report. = 我真希望我在提交报告前已经嵌入图片。


被动语态:
The video was embedded in the blog post. = 视频被嵌入博客文章中。
The data will be embedded in the microchip. = 数据将被嵌入微芯片中。


疑问句:
Is the software embedded in the hardware? = 软件是嵌入硬件中的吗？
Have they embedded the subtitles in the video? = 他们是否已将字幕嵌入视频中？


否定句:
They did not embed the correct driver. = 他们没有嵌入正确的驱动程序。
She didn't embed the image properly. = 她没有正确地嵌入图像。


PHRASE->
embed in = 嵌入到...中
embed with = 与...一起行动（记者）
embed code = 嵌入代码
deeply embedded = 深度嵌入
be embedded in = 嵌入在...中
