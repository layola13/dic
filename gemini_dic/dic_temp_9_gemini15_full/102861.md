jaundice:/ˈdʒɔːndɪs/|/ˈdʒɔːnɪs/| n. adj.|黄疸病；黄疸的；（尤指因嫉妒或不满而产生的）愤恨，不快|A yellowish discoloration of the skin, whites of the eyes, and mucous membranes caused by an accumulation of bile pigment in the blood.  Jaundice can be a symptom of various liver or blood disorders.|例句：The baby developed jaundice shortly after birth. 婴儿出生后不久就患上了黄疸病。|例句：His words were tinged with jaundice, born of envy. 他的话语中带着嫉妒的怨恨。|近义词：yellowing, icterus|反义词：healthy, rosy
