ossify:/ˈɑːsɪfaɪ/| vt. vi.|使骨化；使僵化；顽固化|To turn into bone; to become hardened or inflexible.|例句：The injury caused the tissue to ossify.  受伤导致组织骨化。|例句：His ideas have ossified over the years.  他的想法多年来已经僵化了。|近义词：harden, petrify, calcify|反义词：soften,  flexibilize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bone ossifies gradually. = 骨骼逐渐骨化。
He ossifies his beliefs. = 他使自己的信仰僵化。

一般过去时:
The tissue ossified after the injury. = 受伤后组织骨化了。
The government ossified its policies. = 政府使它的政策僵化了。

现在完成时:
The injury has ossified the joint. = 受伤已使关节骨化。
His views have ossified over time. = 他的观点随着时间的推移而僵化了。

过去完成时:
