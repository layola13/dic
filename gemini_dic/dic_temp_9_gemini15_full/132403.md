nondenominational:/ˌnɒnˌdɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəl/ | adj. |非教派的；无宗派的 |Not associated with or restricted to any particular religious denomination.  This term typically refers to religious organizations or institutions that do not adhere to a specific sect or creed within a broader religion, or to activities that are inclusive of people from various religious backgrounds. |Example:  The school is nondenominational, welcoming students of all faiths. 这所学校是非教派的，欢迎所有信仰的学生。 The nondenominational church held a community service. 这家非教派教堂举办了一场社区服务。|近义词：interfaith, ecumenical, nonsectarian |反义词：denominational, sectarian
