piezoelectric:/ˌpiːzoʊɪˈlɛktrɪk/| adj. |压电的；压电效应的|  relating to or denoting the production of an electric charge in certain crystals in response to mechanical stress, or the converse effect.  压电的；指某些晶体在受到机械应力时产生电荷，或反之亦然。| Example: Piezoelectric crystals are used in many electronic devices. 压电晶体被用于许多电子设备中。 The piezoelectric effect is used to generate high-voltage pulses. 压电效应用于产生高压脉冲。|近义词：electromechanical (in the context of the effect)|反义词：无|词性:形容词
