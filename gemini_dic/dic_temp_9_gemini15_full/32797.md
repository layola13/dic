choirboy:/ˈkwɑːrˌbɔɪ/|/ˈkwʌɪrˌbɔɪ/| n. |唱诗班男孩；天真无邪的男孩|A choirboy is a boy who sings in a choir, often associated with innocence and purity.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe someone who is innocent or naive. |例句：He was a choirboy in the local church. 他是当地教堂的唱诗班男孩。|例句：Despite his criminal record, he maintains a choirboy image. 尽管有犯罪记录，他仍然保持着天真无邪的形象。|近义词：chorister, boy soprano, innocent, naive |反义词：delinquent, villain, hardened criminal
