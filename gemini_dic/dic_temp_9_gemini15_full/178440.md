reconsolate:/ˌriːˈkɒnsəleɪt/| adj.|无法安慰的，无法慰藉的|inconsolable;  unable to be comforted;  extremely sad and unable to accept consolation. |例句：She was inconsolable after the death of her husband. 她丈夫死后悲痛欲绝，无法安慰。 |例句：The news left him in a reconsolate state of despair. 这个消息使他陷入无法安慰的绝望状态。|近义词： inconsolable, disconsolate, comfortless |反义词：consolable, comforted
