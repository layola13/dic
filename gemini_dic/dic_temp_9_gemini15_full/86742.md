help's:/hɛlps/ | n. |帮助（help的第三人称单数现在时）|The verb "help" in its third-person singular present tense form.  It indicates assistance or aid provided by a single person or thing.|He helps his mother with the housework. 他帮他妈妈做家务。|assists, aids, supports |hinders, obstructs, impedes |v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He helps me with my homework every day. = 他每天都帮我做作业。
The teacher helps students understand difficult concepts. =老师帮助学生理解复杂的概念。

一般过去时:
She helped me when I was in trouble. = 我遇到麻烦时，她帮助了我。
They helped build the new school. =他们帮忙建造了新学校。

现在完成时:
He has helped many people in need. = 他已经帮助了许多需要帮助的人。
She has helped to improve the situation. =她帮助改善了现状。

过去完成时:
I had helped him before he asked for help. =在他求助之前，我已经帮助过他了。
They had helped prepare the food before the guests arrived. =客人们到达之前，他们已经帮忙准备好了食物。

将来时:
She will help you with your project tomorrow. =她明天会帮你做项目。
He will help us move the furniture. =他会帮我们搬家具。

虚拟语气:
If he helped me, I could finish the work on time. =如果他帮我，我就能按时完成工作。
I wish he helped me more often. =我希望他更经常地帮助我。

被动语态:
I was helped by a kind stranger. =我得到了一位好心人的帮助。
The problem was helped by a new solution. =这个问题通过一个新的解决方案得到了解决。

疑问句:
Does he help you with your studies? =他帮你学习吗？
Can she help me with this task? =她能帮我完成这项任务吗？

否定句:
He doesn't help with the chores. =他不帮忙做家务。
She didn't help him at all. =她根本没帮他。


PHRASE->
help oneself to = 自取；随便吃
help out = 帮助；援助
can't help but = 不禁；忍不住
help somebody out = 帮助某人摆脱困境
with the help of = 在…的帮助下
