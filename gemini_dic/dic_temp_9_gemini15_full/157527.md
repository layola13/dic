pistol-whipping:/ˈpɪstəlˌwɪpɪŋ/| n. v.|用枪托击打|The act of hitting someone with the butt of a pistol.  It is a violent and potentially lethal assault.|例句：The mugger pistol-whipped the victim before stealing his wallet.  歹徒用枪托击打受害者后偷走了他的钱包。

The suspect was charged with assault and battery after pistol-whipping the store clerk. 嫌疑人因用枪托击打店员而被指控犯有攻击和殴打罪。|近义词：bludgeoning, clubbing, beating|反义词：healing, comforting

词源:

The word is a compound of "pistol" and "whipping."  "Whipping" refers to the act of striking repeatedly with a flexible object, here, the butt of a pistol. The term emerged in the 20th century as a descriptive term for this specific act of violence.
