curt:/kɜːrt/| adj.|简略的；粗鲁的；唐突的 |characterized by shortness of speech or abruptness of manner; rudely brief.  |例句：He gave a curt nod. 他简短地点了点头。  |例句：Her curt reply showed her displeasure. 她简短的回答显示了她的不满。|近义词：rude, abrupt, brusque, blunt, short|反义词：polite, courteous, affable, gracious

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is curt with his customers. = 他对顾客很粗鲁。
Her responses are always curt. = 她的回答总是很简短。

一般过去时:
He was curt in his dismissal of the suggestion. = 他粗鲁地拒绝了这个建议。
She gave a curt answer and walked away. = 她简短地回答了一句，然后走开了。

现在完成时:
He has been curt with me all day. = 他一整天都对我态度粗鲁。
She has always been curt in her communications. = 她一直以来沟通方式都很简短。

过去完成时:
He had been curt with her before, so she was not surprised. = 他以前对她就很粗鲁，所以她并不惊讶。
She had been curt in her previous emails, so he wasn't expecting a lengthy response. = 她之前的邮件都很简短，所以他没有期待冗长的回复。

将来时:
He will be curt with anyone who challenges him. = 他会对任何挑战他的人都很粗鲁。
She will likely be curt if you interrupt her. = 如果你打断她，她可能会很简短地回应。

虚拟语气:
If he were not so curt, people would like him better. = 如果他不那么粗鲁，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she hadn't been so curt in her response. = 我希望她回复时不要那么简短。

被动语态:
His responses were often perceived as curt. = 他的回答经常被认为是粗鲁的。
The message was curt and to the point. = 这条信息简短而直接。

疑问句:
Was his response curt? = 他的回答很简短吗？
Are you always so curt? = 你总是这么粗鲁吗？

否定句:
He was not curt; he was simply busy. = 他并不粗鲁；他只是很忙。
Her manner was not curt, but rather reserved. = 她的态度不是粗鲁的，而是比较含蓄的。


PHRASE->
a curt nod = 简短的点头
a curt reply = 简短的回答
a curt dismissal = 粗鲁的解雇
a curt refusal = 简短的拒绝
curt and rude = 简短且粗鲁的

词源:
The word "curt" comes from the Middle English word "curt," meaning "short" or "brief." This, in turn, is derived from the Old Northern French word "court," also meaning "short."  The modern meaning, implying rudeness, developed later.
