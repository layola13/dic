helminth:/ˈhɛlmɪnθ/ | n. | 蠕虫；寄生虫 | A parasitic worm; a general term for various worm-like parasites, including nematodes, cestodes, and trematodes.  | Example:  The doctor diagnosed the patient with a helminth infection.  医生诊断病人感染了蠕虫。 | 近义词：worm, parasite | 反义词：N/A
