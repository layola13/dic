presenters:/prɪˈzɛntərz/| n.|主持人；演讲者；介绍者|People who introduce or give a presentation.  They can be speakers at conferences, hosts of television shows, or anyone who presents information to an audience.|例句：The presenters at the conference were all experts in their fields.  会议上的演讲者都是各自领域的专家。

例句：The television presenters kept the audience entertained throughout the show. 电视节目主持人让观众在整个节目中都感到很开心。

近义词：speakers, hosts, announcers, commentators

反义词：audience, listeners, viewers

词性：名词
