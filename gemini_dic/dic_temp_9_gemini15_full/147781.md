overpromptly: /ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmptli/ | adv. | 过分迅速地，过早地 | Excessively or prematurely prompt; too quickly. |


例句：

He overpromptly answered the question before I had even finished asking it.  = 他在我还没问完问题就过早地回答了。

The flowers bloomed overpromptly due to the unusually warm weather. = 由于天气异常温暖，花朵过早地开了。


近义词：prematurely, hastily, rashly, too quickly

反义词：slowly, deliberately, tardily, late


词性: 副词
