kittiwake:/ˈkɪtɪweɪk/| n. |海鸥的一种|A small white gull (Rissa tridactyla) with black markings on the wings, found in the northern Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.  Kittiwakes are known for their nesting habits on cliffs. |例句：The kittiwakes nested on the sheer cliffs, their white bodies contrasting sharply against the dark rock.  海鸥在陡峭的悬崖上筑巢，它们白色的身体与黑暗的岩石形成鲜明对比。

例句： We watched a colony of kittiwakes diving for fish in the turbulent waters. 我们观察到一群海鸥在汹涌的海水中捕鱼。


近义词：gull (a more general term) |反义词：无
