rejectionism:/rɪˈdʒɛkʃənɪzəm/| n. |排斥主义；拒绝主义|The refusal to accept or consider something, often a particular idea, belief, or system.  This often involves actively opposing or fighting against it.  It can range from mild skepticism to outright hostility.|例句：The rise of rejectionism in the scientific community hampered progress in the field.  科学界排斥主义的兴起阻碍了该领域的进步。

例句：His rejectionism towards new technologies made him seem outdated. 他对新技术的排斥让他显得过时了。


近义词：opposition, denial, repudiation, resistance


反义词：acceptance, embrace, adoption


词性: 名词
