particularizer: /pərˈtɪkjʊləraɪzər/ | n. | 详述者；细节说明者 | One who particularizes; one who specifies details. A particularizer focuses on specific details and examples to support a claim or argument, rather than relying on general statements or broad generalizations.  | Example:  The professor acted as a particularizer, providing numerous case studies to illustrate his points. 教授充当了详述者的角色，提供了许多案例研究来说明他的观点。 | 近义词:specifier, detailer, preciser | 反义词:generalizer, summarizer | 名词
