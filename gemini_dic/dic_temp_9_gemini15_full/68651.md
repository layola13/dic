far-sighted:/fɑːrˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. |有远见的；远视的 | Having or showing a long-range perspective; able to see distant objects clearly.  |例句：He was a far-sighted leader who anticipated the future needs of the country. 他是一位有远见的领导人，预料到了国家未来的需求。

例句：She is far-sighted and needs glasses to see things up close. 她有远视，需要戴眼镜才能看清近处的东西。

近义词：foresighted, visionary, prescient, long-sighted | 反义词：short-sighted, myopic, nearsighted

词源:
The word "far-sighted" is a compound word.  "Far" means distant or remote, and "sighted" relates to sight or vision. The combination implies having a vision that extends far into the future.  The "sighted" part is related to the Old English word "siht," meaning sight.
