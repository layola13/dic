grocer:/ˈɡroʊsər/| n. |杂货商；食品商 |A grocer is a person who owns or works in a grocery store, selling food and household goods.  |The local grocer always had the freshest produce. 当地杂货店的农产品总是最新鲜的。|merchant, retailer|producer, wholesaler|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The grocer sells fresh bread daily. = 杂货商每天都卖新鲜面包。
He stocks his shelves with groceries every morning. = 他每天早上都给货架上补货。

一般过去时:
The grocer closed early yesterday. = 杂货商昨天早早关门了。
She bought her groceries from the same grocer for years. = 她多年来一直从同一家杂货店买东西。

现在完成时:
The grocer has increased his prices recently. = 杂货商最近提高了价格。
We have been shopping at this grocer for five years. = 我们在这家杂货店购物五年了。

过去完成时:

将来时:
The grocer will open a new store next month. = 杂货商下个月将开一家新店。
He will deliver the groceries to our house tomorrow. = 他明天会把杂货送到我们家。

虚拟语气:
