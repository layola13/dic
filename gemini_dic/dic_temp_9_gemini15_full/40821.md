convoyed:/kənˈvɔɪd/| v.|护送；护航；护 convoy  |护送；护航；护送的队伍|The ships were convoyed by destroyers.  军舰由驱逐舰护航。|The troops were convoyed through enemy territory. 部队护送通过敌方领土。|近义词：escort, guard, shepherd|反义词：abandon, desert


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The navy convoys merchant ships across the Atlantic. = 海军护送商船横渡大西洋。
He convoys his children to school every day. = 他每天护送他的孩子们去上学。

一般过去时:
The army convoyed the refugees to safety. = 军队护送难民到安全地带。
The captain convoyed his fleet through the storm. = 船长护送他的舰队穿过暴风雨。

现在完成时:
The government has convoyed hundreds of thousands of people to safety. = 政府已经护送成千上万的人到安全地带。
They have convoyed supplies to the disaster area. = 他们已经护送物资到灾区。

过去完成时:
The ships had been convoyed through the dangerous waters. = 船只已经通过危险水域被护送。
The general had convoyed his troops across the mountains. = 将军已经护送他的部队越过山脉。

将来时:
The merchant ships will be convoyed by warships. = 商船将由军舰护送。
The refugees will be convoyed to a safer location. = 难民将被护送至更安全的地点。

虚拟语气:
If we had been convoyed, we would have arrived safely. = 如果我们当时有护送，我们就会安全到达。
