gongerine:/ˈɡɒŋɡərɪn/| n. |锣声；（尤指）中国锣声|A gongerine is the sound of a gong, especially a Chinese gong.  The word evokes the resonant, often metallic and reverberating sound of a gong. It is less common than simply using the word "gong" but adds a more descriptive and evocative element.|例句：The gongerine echoed through the temple courtyard.  锣声回荡在寺庙庭院里。|The resonant clang of a large gong. |The quiet murmur of a small bell.|无
