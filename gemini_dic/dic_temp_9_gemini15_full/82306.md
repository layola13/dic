grouse:/ɡraʊs/| n. v.|松鸡；抱怨；牢骚|A grouse is a type of game bird.  The verb "to grouse" means to complain or grumble. |例句：He shot a grouse. 他打到一只松鸡。He was constantly grousing about his job. 他一直在抱怨他的工作。|近义词：complaint, grumble, grumbling, moan, protest|反义词：praise, compliment, approval


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often grouses about the weather. = 他经常抱怨天气。
They grouse about their workload every day. = 他们每天都抱怨他们的工作量。

一般过去时:
She groused about the long lines at the store. = 她抱怨商店的长队。
He groused to his friends about his boss. = 他向朋友们抱怨他的老板。

现在完成时:
I have groused to him many times about this problem. = 我已经多次向他抱怨这个问题了。
We have groused about the poor service for weeks. = 我们已经抱怨糟糕的服务几周了。

过去完成时:
They had groused about the unfair treatment before they quit their jobs. = 在他们辞职之前，他们已经抱怨过不公平的待遇。
She had groused to her family about her boyfriend's behavior for months. = 她已经向家人抱怨男友的行为几个月了。

将来时:
He will grouse about the traffic again tomorrow. = 他明天还会抱怨交通。
They will grouse about the new rules when they are announced. = 当新规公布时，他们会抱怨。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I wouldn't grouse so much. = 如果我是他，我不会抱怨那么多。
I wish he wouldn't grouse all the time. = 我希望他不总是抱怨。


被动语态:
