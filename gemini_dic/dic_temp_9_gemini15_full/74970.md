fricasseeing:/ˌfrɪkəˈsiːɪŋ/| v. |炖，焖（尤指鸡肉）|To cook (food, especially meat) slowly in a rich sauce in a covered dish.|The chef was fricasseeing the chicken in a creamy mushroom sauce.  厨师正在用奶油蘑菇酱炖鸡肉。|She fricasseed the rabbit with herbs and wine. 她用香草和葡萄酒炖兔肉。|近义词：stew, braise, simmer|反义词：fry, grill
