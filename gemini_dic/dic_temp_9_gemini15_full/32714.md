chlorinations:/ˌklɔːrɪˈneɪʃənz/| n.|氯化（过程）；氯化作用|The process of treating something with chlorine.  This often refers to water purification, but can also apply to other substances.|例句：The water treatment plant uses chlorinations to kill harmful bacteria.  该水处理厂使用氯化法杀死有害细菌。

例句：The chlorinations of the swimming pool are routinely checked. 游泳池的氯化情况定期检查。


近义词：chlorination (单数形式),  disinfection (消毒)


反义词：dechlorination (脱氯)
