plottingly: /ˈplɒtɪŋli/ | adv. | 阴谋地；策划地 | In a way that suggests plotting or scheming.  It implies secrecy and intention to deceive or manipulate. | 例句：He looked at them plottingly, a glint in his eye. 他阴险地望着他们，眼里闪着光芒。  She spoke plottingly, hinting at secrets she wouldn't reveal. 她阴谋般地说着话，暗示着一些她不会透露的秘密。 | 近义词：schemingly, conspiratorially, deviously, insidiously | 反义词：openly, honestly, frankly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such does not have verb conjugations.  It modifies verbs, describing *how* an action is performed.

一般现在时:
He acts plottingly. = 他行动诡秘。
They speak plottingly. = 他们阴谋地说着话。

一般过去时:
He acted plottingly. = 他行动诡秘。
They spoke plottingly. = 他们阴谋地说着话。

He has spoken plottingly. = 他一直阴险地说话。


过去完成时:
They had acted plottingly before the meeting. = 会议前他们就一直在秘密行动。

被动语态:
(Plottingly is an adverb and cannot be used in the passive voice directly. The passive voice would apply to the verb it modifies.)
The plan was plotted plottingly. = 这个计划是阴险地策划的。(Note the awkwardness; better phrasing is usually possible)
His actions were interpreted plottingly. = 他的行为被解读为阴险的。(This illustrates the use, though it is not a perfect passive construction)


疑问句:
Did he act plottingly? = 他行动诡秘吗？
Were they speaking plottingly? = 他们当时阴险地说话吗？


否定句:
He did not act plottingly. = 他没有诡秘行事。
They did not speak plottingly. = 他们没有阴谋地说话。


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases using "plottingly".  Its usage is primarily as a single adverb to modify verbs describing secretive or manipulative actions.  Examples of verbs it could modify include:  look, smile, whisper, plan, scheme, act, behave, speak.
