imputable:/ˈɪmpjʊtəbl/| adj.| 可归咎的，可归责的 | Attributable; able to be blamed or credited on someone or something.  |例句：His actions were imputable to his lack of judgment.  他的行为是由于他缺乏判断力造成的。 |例句：The damage was imputable to negligence.  损害是由于疏忽造成的。|近义词：ascribable, attributable, accountable |反义词：unaccountable, inexcusable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mistake is imputable to him. = 这个错误归咎于他。
His success is imputable to hard work. = 他的成功归功于努力工作。

一般过去时:
The accident was imputable to driver error. = 事故归咎于驾驶员的错误。
The loss was imputable to poor management. = 损失是由于管理不善造成的。


现在完成时:
The problem has been imputable to faulty design. = 问题一直以来都归咎于设计缺陷。
The delay has been imputable to unforeseen circumstances. = 延误是由于不可预见的情况造成的。


过去完成时:
The failure had been imputable to a lack of funding. = 失败是因为资金不足造成的。
The damage had been imputable to the storm. = 损害是由于暴风雨造成的。


将来时:
The consequences will be imputable to their decisions. = 后果将归咎于他们的决定。
The success will be imputable to their hard work and dedication. = 成功将归功于他们的辛勤工作和奉献精神。


虚拟语气:
If the error were imputable to the software, we would have to rewrite it. = 如果错误归咎于软件，我们就必须重写它。
If the failure had been imputable to negligence, the company would have faced legal action. = 如果失败是由于疏忽造成的，公司将面临法律诉讼。


被动语态:
The blame was imputed to him. = 责任归咎于他。
The responsibility was imputed to the team leader. = 责任被归咎于团队领导。


疑问句:
Is the mistake imputable to anyone in particular? = 这个错误可以归咎于任何特定的人吗？
To whom is the responsibility imputable? = 责任应归咎于谁？


否定句:
The failure is not imputable to any single cause. = 失败并非由于单一原因造成的。
The damage is not imputable to natural causes. = 损害并非由于自然原因造成的。


PHRASE->
be imputable to = 归咎于
hold someone imputable = 认为某人有责任
impute something to someone = 将某事归咎于某人
impute blame to = 将责任归咎于
find someone imputable = 认定某人有责任
