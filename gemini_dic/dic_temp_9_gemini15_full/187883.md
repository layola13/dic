rosta:/ˈrɒstə/| n. |花名册；值班表；轮值表|A roster is a list of names, often of people who are available to do a particular job, or who are scheduled to do something at particular times.|The teacher keeps a roster of students' names.|近义词：list, schedule, register|反义词：N/A

The hospital uses a roster system for nurses. = 医院对护士采用轮值表制度。
