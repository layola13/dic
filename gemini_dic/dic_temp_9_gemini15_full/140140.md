off-year:/ˈɔːf jɪr/| adj.|非选举年；间歇年|A year in which no general elections are held.|The town council held a special meeting during the off-year.|近义词：non-election year, intervening year|反义词：election year


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is an off-year for elections. = 今年是非选举年。
The farmers have an off-year for their harvest. = 农民们今年收成不好。


一般过去时:
Last year was an off-year for the state legislature. = 去年是州议会休会年。
The company experienced an off-year in sales. = 公司去年销售业绩不佳。


现在完成时:
We have had several off-years in a row. = 我们连续好几年都处于非选举年。
The market has experienced several off-years recently. = 近期市场经历了好几年的低迷时期。


过去完成时:
They had already planned their off-year activities before the election. = 他们在选举前就已经计划好了休假年的活动。
The business had already faced several off-years before it finally closed. = 这家企业在最终倒闭之前已经经历了好几年的不景气。


将来时:
Next year will be an off-year for the Senate. = 明年是参议院的休会年。
The company projects that next year will be an off-year for profits. = 公司预测明年利润将会下降。


虚拟语气:
If this were an off-year, we could relax. = 如果今年是非选举年，我们就可以放松一下。
I wish this were an off-year, so I could take a break. = 我希望今年是非选举年，这样我就可以休息一下。


被动语态:
Off-years are often used for planning and reorganization. = 非选举年经常被用于规划和重组。
The off-year budget was criticized for its cuts. = 非选举年的预算因其削减而受到批评。


疑问句:
Is this an off-year for local elections? = 今年是地方选举的非选举年吗？
Will next year be an off-year for the company? = 明年公司会是低迷期吗？


否定句:
This is not an off-year for presidential elections. = 今年不是总统大选的非选举年。
