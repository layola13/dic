nontangible: /ˌnɑːnˈtændʒəbl/ | adj. | 无形的；难以捉摸的 | Not able to be touched or perceived by the senses; intangible.  |例句：Goodwill is a nontangible asset. 商誉是一种无形资产。

例句：The company's greatest asset is its nontangible reputation for quality. 公司最大的资产是其高质量的无形声誉。


近义词：intangible, immaterial, incorporeal, abstract


反义词：tangible, concrete, material


词源：non- (否定前缀) + tangible (可触摸的，有形的)
