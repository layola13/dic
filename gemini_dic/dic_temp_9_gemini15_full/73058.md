footnoted:/ˈfʊtˌnoʊtɪd/ | adj. |脚注的，加了脚注的 | Having footnotes; containing or marked with footnotes.  | Example: The footnoted text clarifies the author's ambiguous statements.  脚注文本阐明了作者模棱两可的陈述。|近义词：annotated, referenced | 反义词：unreferenced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The document is footnoted extensively. = 这份文件有很多脚注。
This article is footnoted throughout. = 这篇文章通篇都有脚注。

一般过去时:
The professor footnoted the student's paper. = 教授给学生的论文加了脚注。
The editor footnoted several passages. = 编辑给几段文字加了脚注。

现在完成时:
They have footnoted all the relevant passages. = 他们已经为所有相关的段落加上了脚注。
She has footnoted the bibliography. = 她已经为参考文献加上了脚注。

过去完成时:
He had footnoted the manuscript before submitting it. = 他在提交手稿之前已经加上了脚注。
The researcher had footnoted her findings extensively. = 研究人员已经为她的研究结果做了大量的脚注。

将来时:
We will footnote the chapter tomorrow. = 我们明天将为这一章添加脚注。
They will footnote the entire book. = 他们将为整本书添加脚注。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would footnote every important point. = 如果我有时间，我会为每一个要点添加脚注。
I wish I had footnoted that quote properly. = 我真希望我当时正确地为那段引用加上了脚注。

被动语态:
The text was footnoted by the editor. = 这段文字是由编辑添加脚注的。
The references were footnoted meticulously. = 参考文献被一丝不苟地加上了脚注。

疑问句:
Is the text footnoted? = 这段文字有脚注吗？
Are all the sources footnoted? = 所有来源都加了脚注吗？

否定句:
The article was not footnoted properly. = 这篇文章的脚注不规范。
The essay is not footnoted at all. = 这篇文章根本没有脚注。
