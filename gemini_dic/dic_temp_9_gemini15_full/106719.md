kyloes:/ˈkaɪloʊz/| n.|凯洛斯牛(苏格兰的一种牛)| A breed of cattle originating in the Kyloe district of Scotland, known for their hardiness and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.  They are typically black or dark-brown in color and have long horns.|Example: The farmer raised a herd of Kyloes on his highland farm.  这位农民在他的高地农场饲养着一群凯洛斯牛。|近义词: Highland cattle, Galloway cattle|反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it refers to a specific breed)|名词
