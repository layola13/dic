proslavery:/ˈproʊˌsleɪvəri/| adj. | 支持奴隶制的；赞成奴隶制的 |  Supporting or advocating for the institution of slavery.  The proslavery arguments often centered on economic benefits and biblical justifications.  | 例句：The proslavery South fought to preserve its way of life. = 支持奴隶制的南方为维护其生活方式而战。

The proslavery advocates believed that slavery was divinely ordained. = 支持奴隶制的拥护者认为奴隶制是上帝预定的。

| 近义词：anti-abolitionist, slavery-supporting | 反义词：abolitionist, anti-slavery |  adj.
