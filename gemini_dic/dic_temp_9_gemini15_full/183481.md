repugnant:/rɪˈpʌɡnənt/| adj. |令人厌恶的，令人反感的| Extremely distasteful; repulsive.|The smell of rotting meat was repugnant.|令人作呕；令人反感| offensive, disgusting, repulsive, abhorrent |pleasant, agreeable |形容词


例句：

The idea of eating insects is repugnant to many Westerners.  许多西方人觉得吃昆虫令人作呕。

His behavior was so repugnant that everyone avoided him. 他的行为如此令人反感，以至于每个人都躲着他。


近义词：

offensive, disgusting, repulsive, abhorrent, hateful, loathsome


反义词：

pleasant, agreeable, attractive, appealing, delightful


词源：

From Latin *repugnare "to fight back, resist," from re- "back" + pugnare "to fight."  The sense shifted to "be contrary to" and then to "be distasteful."


用法说明：

repugnant主要用作形容词，修饰事物或想法，表示它们令人厌恶、反感或令人作呕。它通常用于描述强烈的不喜欢或反感，比"disgusting"或"offensive"更强调内心的反感和排斥。  它也可以用于更抽象的概念，例如一个想法或行为。


PHRASE->

find something repugnant: 发现某事令人厌恶
be repugnant to someone: 对某人来说是令人厌恶的
a repugnant smell/taste/sight: 令人作呕的气味/味道/景象
utterly repugnant: 完全令人厌恶的
deeply repugnant: 深恶痛绝的
