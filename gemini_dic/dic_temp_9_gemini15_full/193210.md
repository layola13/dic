seducing: /səˈduːsɪŋ/ | v. | 引诱；诱惑；迷惑 |  The act of tempting or persuading someone to do something, especially something morally wrong or unwise, through charm or flattery.  It often implies a gradual process of persuasion. | 例句：His seductive charm easily won her over. = 他迷人的魅力轻易地赢得了她的芳心。

The marketing campaign was seductive, promising unrealistic results. = 这场营销活动极具诱惑力，承诺了不切实际的结果。


近义词：tempting, alluring, enticing, captivating, charming

反义词：repelling, disgusting, off-putting
