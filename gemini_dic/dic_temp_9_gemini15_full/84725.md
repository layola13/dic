handing:/ˈhændɪŋ/| v.|递交，移交，处理|The act of giving or transferring something to someone;  the act of managing or dealing with something.|例句：He was handing out flyers at the concert. 他在音乐会上分发传单。

例句：The teacher is handing back the exam papers.老师正在发回试卷。


近义词：passing, delivering, managing, dealing with|反义词：receiving, taking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is handing me the book. 他正在把书递给我。
She hands out the papers every morning. 她每天早上分发文件。

一般过去时:
I handed him the letter yesterday. 我昨天把信交给了他。
They handed in their assignments on time. 他们按时提交了作业。

现在完成时:
She has handed in her resignation. 她已经递交了辞呈。
He has handed out all the prizes. 他已经分发了所有奖品。

过去完成时:
I had handed the report to my boss before the meeting. 我在会议前已将报告提交给了我的老板。
They had handed over the responsibility to the new team. 他们已将责任移交给新团队。

将来时:
I will hand you the documents tomorrow. 我明天会把文件交给你。
She will be handing out the questionnaires next week. 她下周将分发问卷。

虚拟语气:
If I had handed in my application earlier, I would have got the job. 如果我早点提交申请，我就拿到这份工作了。
I wish I had handed the project over sooner. 我真希望我早点把项目移交出去。

被动语态:
The documents were handed to the committee. 文件被提交给了委员会。
The case is being handed over to the police. 此案正在移交给警方。

疑问句:
Are you handing out the invitations? 你正在分发请柬吗？
Who is handing the parcel? 谁在递送包裹？

否定句:
I didn't hand in my homework. 我没有提交我的作业。
She wasn't handing out the flyers. 她没有分发传单。


PHRASE->
handing over: 移交
handing down: 传递（传统、遗产等）
handing out: 分发
handing in: 提交
handing off: 交付
