freestyling:/ˈfriːstaɪlɪŋ/| v. |自由发挥，即兴表演 |The act of performing or creating something without premeditation or planning; improvising.  It often refers to artistic expression, particularly in music, dance, or sports. |例句：He was freestyling on the guitar, creating a unique and spontaneous melody. 他在吉他上即兴演奏，创造出一段独特而自发的旋律。

例句：The dancers were freestyling, their movements flowing effortlessly from one to the next. 舞蹈演员们在自由发挥，他们的动作流畅地从一个动作过渡到另一个动作。


近义词：improvising, ad-libbing, extemporizing |反义词：preplanning, rehearsing, scripting |词性:动词
