plaguing:/ˈpleɪɡɪŋ/| v. |困扰；折磨；使受疾病侵袭 |  to cause repeated trouble or suffering to someone or something.  |例句：Financial problems have been plaguing the company for months.  财务问题几个月来一直困扰着这家公司。

例句：The drought is plaguing the farmers. 旱灾正在折磨着农民们。


近义词：tormenting, harassing, afflicting, besetting, troubling

反义词：soothing, comforting, alleviating, relieving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant noise is plaguing me. = 持续的噪音困扰着我。
He is plaguing his sister with questions. = 他用问题烦扰着他妹妹。


一般过去时:
Droughts plagued the region last year. = 去年干旱困扰着这个地区。
The insects plagued the crops. = 昆虫侵害了庄稼。


现在完成时:
Financial worries have been plaguing her for years. = 多年来，经济上的担忧一直困扰着她。
The team has been plagued by injuries this season. = 这个赛季球队一直饱受伤病困扰。


过去完成时:
The company had been plagued by scandals before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司一直受到丑闻的困扰。
They had been plagued by doubts about the project's success. = 他们一直对项目的成功心存疑虑。


将来时:
Climate change will plague future generations. = 气候变化将困扰后代。
We fear that the disease will plague the country for years to come. = 我们担心这种疾病将在未来几年困扰这个国家。


虚拟语气:
If only this headache would stop plaguing me! = 但愿这个头痛能停止折磨我！
If this problem hadn’t plagued us, we would have finished on time. = 如果这个问题没有困扰我们，我们就能按时完成了。


被动语态:
The city was plagued by rioting. = 这个城市受到暴乱的困扰。
The project has been plagued by delays. = 这个项目一直被延误所困扰。


疑问句:
What is plaguing you today? = 你今天怎么了？
Is anything plaguing your mind? = 有什么事困扰着你吗？


否定句:
Nothing is plaguing me at the moment. = 目前没有什么困扰我。
The problems didn't plague them as much as expected. = 问题并没有像预期的那样困扰他们。


PHRASE->
be plagued by = 受…的困扰
plagued with = 充满…的；受…折磨的
plaguing question = 令人苦恼的问题
plaguing doubt = 挥之不去的疑虑
plaguing thought = 挥之不去的想法
