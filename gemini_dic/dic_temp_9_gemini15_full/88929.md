holders:/ˈhoʊldərz/ | n. | 持有人，持有者，支架 |  A person or thing that holds something.  This word refers to someone who possesses or owns something, or something that supports or contains something else. |  The bondholders are anxiously awaiting the company's announcement.  债券持有人正焦急地等待着公司的公告。

The museum displays the ancient artifacts in protective holders. 博物馆将古代文物保存在保护性支架中。 | owners, possessors, keepers |  losers, relinquishers | 名词
