It can also refer to a small ice cap. |例句：The archaeologist carefully examined the calotte of the ancient skull. 考古学家仔细检查了古代头骨的头盖骨顶部。|例句：The climber's hat had a small calotte to protect his head from the sun. 登山者的帽子有一个小帽盖以保护他的头部免受阳光照射。|近义词：skullcap, cap, dome |反义词：base, bottom
