mag:/mæɡ/ | n. |杂志；魔力；<非正式>有魅力的人| A magazine is a periodical publication containing articles and illustrations, often on a particular subject or for a particular readership.  The word also refers to a strong attractive force, often associated with witchcraft or magic, or informally, a person with a strong personal magnetism. |例句：I enjoy reading magazines about fashion. 我喜欢读时尚杂志。|近义词：periodical, journal, publication, charm, allure |反义词：dullness, unattractiveness
