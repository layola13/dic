gearing:/ˈɡɪərɪŋ/| n. |传动装置；齿轮传动；准备；装备|The process of adjusting or equipping something for a particular purpose or task;  the system of gears in a machine. |例句：The car's gearing was smooth and efficient. 这辆车的传动装置平顺而高效。|例句：The company is gearing up for a major expansion. 公司正在为大规模扩张做准备。|近义词：preparation, equipment, mechanism|反义词：disengagement, dismantling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gearing system is working well. = 传动系统运转良好。
He is gearing up for the marathon. = 他正在为马拉松做准备。


一般过去时:
The old car's gearing was noisy. = 那辆旧车的传动装置很吵。
They geared down before climbing the hill. = 他们在爬坡前减速了。


现在完成时:
We have geared our marketing efforts towards young adults. = 我们已经将我们的营销工作重点放在年轻人身上。
The company has geared up for the product launch. = 公司已为产品发布做好了准备。


过去完成时:
They had geared down before entering the sharp turn. = 他们在进入急转弯前已经减速了。
The factory had geared its production to meet the increased demand. = 工厂已经调整了生产以满足增长的需求。


将来时:
We will gear the presentation towards the investors. = 我们将把演示重点放在投资者身上。
They will be gearing up for the next project soon. = 他们很快就会为下一个项目做准备。


虚拟语气:
If we geared the campaign differently, we might have seen better results. = 如果我们采用不同的竞选策略，可能会看到更好的结果。
I wish we had geared up earlier for the unexpected surge in demand. = 我希望我们早点为需求的意外激增做好准备。


被动语态:
The machinery is being geared towards increased productivity. = 机器设备正在调整以提高生产力。
The presentation was geared to the needs of the audience. = 演示内容是根据观众的需求而设计的。


疑问句:
Is the gearing ratio correct for this application? = 这个应用的齿轮比正确吗？
How is the company gearing up for the competition? = 公司是如何为竞争做准备的？


否定句:
The engine wasn't geared for high speeds. = 这台发动机不适合高速运转。
They didn't gear their efforts towards the right target market. = 他们的努力并没有针对正确的目标市场。
