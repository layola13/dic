nonacceleration:/ˌnɒnæksɛləˈreɪʃən/ | n. |非加速；不加速；匀速 |The lack of acceleration; a state or period of not accelerating.  It often refers to a constant speed or velocity. |例句：The car maintained a state of nonacceleration down the long, straight road. 这辆车在长长的直路上保持匀速行驶。


例句：The experiment showed nonacceleration of the particle, indicating a stable energy level.  实验表明粒子没有加速，表明能级稳定。


近义词：constant speed, uniform motion, steady state


反义词：acceleration


词性: 名词
