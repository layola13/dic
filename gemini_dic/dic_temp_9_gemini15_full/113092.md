lunch:/lʌntʃ/| n. |午餐，午饭 |The midday meal.  It is typically eaten between breakfast and dinner. |例句：We had lunch at a nice restaurant. 我们在一家不错的餐厅吃了午餐。|例句：What's for lunch today? 今天午餐吃什么？|近义词：midday meal, luncheon |反义词：breakfast, dinner

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have lunch at noon every day. = 我每天中午吃午饭。
She usually eats a light lunch. = 她通常吃清淡的午餐。


一般过去时:
We had lunch together yesterday. = 我们昨天一起吃了午饭。
He ate a quick lunch before the meeting. = 他在会议前快速吃了个午餐。


现在完成时:
I haven't had lunch yet. = 我还没吃午饭。
They have already finished lunch. = 他们已经吃过午饭了。


过去完成时:
I had already eaten lunch when he called. = 他打电话的时候我已经吃过午饭了。
She had finished her lunch before the movie started. = 电影开始之前她已经吃完了午饭。


将来时:
We will have lunch at the new cafe tomorrow. = 我们明天将在新的咖啡馆吃午餐。
They are going to have lunch with their clients. = 他们将与客户共进午餐。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would have lunch with you. = 如果我有时间，我会和你一起吃午饭。
I wish I had packed a lunch. = 我真希望我带了午饭。


被动语态:
Lunch is served at 12 noon. = 中午12点供应午餐。
The meeting was interrupted by lunch. = 会议被午餐打断了。


疑问句:
What time is lunch? = 午餐时间是什么时候？
What are you having for lunch? = 你午餐吃什么？


否定句:
I didn't have lunch today. = 我今天没吃午饭。
They won't have lunch until 2 pm. = 他们直到下午两点才吃午饭。


PHRASE->
have lunch = 吃午饭
skip lunch = 午饭不吃
pack a lunch = 准备午饭
lunch break = 午休
lunchtime = 午餐时间
