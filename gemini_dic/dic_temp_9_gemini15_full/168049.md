privatest:/ˈpraɪvətɪst/| adj.| 最私人的，最隐秘的 |Most private; most secret.  The word is the superlative form of "private". It emphasizes the highest degree of privacy or secrecy.|例句：This is my privatest thought, never to be shared. 这是我最私密的想法，永远不会与人分享。|近义词：most confidential, most intimate, most secluded |反义词：public, open, exposed
