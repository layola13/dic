rechromatographing:/ˌriːkrəˈmætəɡræfɪŋ/| v.|再色谱分析，重新色谱分离|The process of subjecting a substance to chromatography a second time, often to improve separation or purification.  This technique is used to further refine the results of an initial chromatographic run.|例句：The scientist decided to rechromatograph the sample to obtain a better separation of the components. 科学家决定对样品进行再色谱分析，以获得更好的组分分离。

例句：Rechromatographing the mixture yielded a purer product.  重新色谱分离混合物得到了更纯净的产物。


近义词：re-chromatography, secondary chromatography


反义词：initial chromatography (first chromatography)


词性：动词
