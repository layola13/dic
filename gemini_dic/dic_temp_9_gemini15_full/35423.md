co-ordinative:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnətɪv/ | adj. | 协调的；并列的 | Relating to or involving coordination; characterized by coordination.  The term often refers to grammatical structures where two or more clauses are joined together without one being subordinate to the other. | Example: The sentence "The dog barked, and the cat ran away" is a co-ordinative sentence.  这个句子“狗叫了，猫跑开了”是一个并列句。|近义词：coordinating, conjunctive, parallel | 反义词：subordinate, dependent
