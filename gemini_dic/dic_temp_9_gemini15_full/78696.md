gigafactory:/ˈɡɪɡəˌfæktəri/| n. |超级工厂|A gigafactory is an extremely large factory, typically used for the mass production of batteries, electric vehicles, or other advanced technology products.  It's characterized by its massive scale and automation. |Tesla's Gigafactory in Nevada produces batteries for electric vehicles.|近义词：massive factory, large-scale manufacturing plant|反义词：small workshop, cottage industry
