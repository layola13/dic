dialectic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktɪk/ | n. |辩证法；辩论；对立统一 |The process of arriving at the truth by stating a thesis, then an antithesis, and finally a synthesis that unites the two.  It's a method of philosophical argument that involves some kind of contradictory process between opposing sides.  |Example:  He engaged in a lively dialectic with his opponent. 他与对手进行了激烈的辩论。

Example:  The philosopher's work is a study in dialectic, moving from opposing viewpoints to a synthesis.  这位哲学家的作品是对辩证法的研究，从对立的观点过渡到综合。


近义词：debate, discussion, argument, discourse | 反义词：monologue, agreement, consensus | 词性：名词
