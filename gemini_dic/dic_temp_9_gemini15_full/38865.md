confederationism:/ˌkənˌfɛdəˈreɪʃənɪzəm/| n. |邦联主义；联盟主义|The political ideology advocating for a confederation or federation of states or groups.  It emphasizes decentralized governance, regional autonomy, and cooperation among constituent units while maintaining a degree of overall unity.|Example:  The success of the Swiss Confederation demonstrates the viability of confederationism as a system of government.  瑞士邦联的成功证明了邦联主义作为一种政府体制的可行性。|近义词：federalism, decentralization|反义词：centralization, unitary government|名词
