deejayed:/ˈdiːdʒeɪd/| v.|担任DJ，播放音乐|Past tense and past participle of "to deejay".  It means to act as a disc jockey, playing music for an audience.  The term often implies a specific style or skill in selecting and mixing music.|例句：He deejayed at the club all night. 他整晚都在俱乐部做DJ。
The DJ deejayed a mix of house and techno music.  这位DJ播放了一系列浩室和迷幻电子音乐。|近义词：spun, mixed (music), emceed|反义词：None specifically.
