in relation to: /ɪn rɪˈleɪʃən tuː/ | prep. |关于；涉及；与…有关 |  This preposition indicates a connection or relevance between two things. It emphasizes the context or perspective from which something is being considered.  | Example:  The study examined the impact of social media in relation to teenage mental health. 这项研究考察了社交媒体对青少年心理健康的影响。  | 近义词: concerning, regarding, respecting, with regard to, in terms of | 反义词: unrelated to, independent of, separate from


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project's success is in relation to its funding. = 项目的成功与它的资金有关。
Her happiness is in relation to her family. = 她的幸福与她的家庭有关。

一般过去时:
His decision was in relation to the new policy. = 他的决定与新政策有关。
The argument was in relation to the distribution of resources. = 争论是关于资源分配的。

现在完成时:
The changes have been in relation to the new regulations. = 这些变化与新的规章制度有关。
His improved performance has been in relation to his increased effort. = 他表现的提高与其付出的努力有关。

过去完成时:
The investigation had been in relation to a previous incident. = 调查与之前的事件有关。
Their concerns had been in relation to the safety procedures. = 他们的担忧与安全程序有关。

将来时:
The announcement will be in relation to the company's future plans. = 公告将与公司的未来计划有关。
His next move will be in relation to the market trends. = 他的下一步行动将与市场趋势有关。

虚拟语气:
If the decision were in relation to fairness, it would be different. = 如果决定与公平有关，情况就会不同。
I wish the report were in relation to the actual data. = 我希望报告与实际数据有关。

被动语态:
The decision was made in relation to the budget constraints. = 决定是根据预算限制作出的。
The problem is being addressed in relation to the current situation. = 正在针对当前情况解决这个问题。

疑问句:
Is your question in relation to the meeting? = 你的问题与会议有关吗？
Is this information in relation to the ongoing project? = 这个信息与正在进行的项目有关吗？

否定句:
The statement was not in relation to the current events. = 这份声明与当前事件无关。
His comments were not in relation to the topic under discussion. = 他的评论与正在讨论的话题无关。
