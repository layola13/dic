frater:/ˈfreɪtər/| n. |兄弟；会员（尤指兄弟会会员）|A frater is a member of a fraternity, especially in a college or university.  The word often implies a strong bond of brotherhood and shared experiences.| Example: He's a frater at Sigma Alpha Epsilon. 他是西格玛·阿尔法·爱普西隆兄弟会的成员。|近义词：brother, member, comrade|反义词：enemy, opponent
