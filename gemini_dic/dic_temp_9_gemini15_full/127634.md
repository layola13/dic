needlessly:/ˈniːdləsli/| adj.|不必要地；无谓地 | Unnecessarily; without need.  It implies that something was done or happened without a good reason, and could have been avoided. |例句：He needlessly complicated the issue. 他不必要地使问题复杂化了。

例句：She needlessly worried about the exam. 她不必要地为考试担忧。


近义词：unnecessarily, pointlessly, fruitlessly, superfluously

反义词：necessarily, usefully, purposefully
