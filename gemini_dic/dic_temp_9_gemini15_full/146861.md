overindustrialize:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪz/| vt.|过度工业化|To industrialize to an excessive degree, often resulting in negative consequences for the environment and society.  The process involves expanding industrial capacity beyond what is sustainable or beneficial.|例句：Overindustrializing the region led to severe air pollution and water contamination.  过度工业化该地区导致了严重的空气污染和水污染。
例句：The rapid overindustrialization of the country depleted its natural resources.  该国的快速过度工业化耗尽了其自然资源。|近义词：overdevelop, hyperindustrialize|反义词：deindustrialize, underdevelop
