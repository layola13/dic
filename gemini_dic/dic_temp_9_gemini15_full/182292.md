remunerate:/rɪˈmjuːnəreɪt/| vt.|酬谢；报酬；补偿|To pay (someone) for work or services; to compensate.  The company remunerates its employees fairly.|The company remunerates its employees fairly. = 公司公平地支付员工报酬。|They remunerated him generously for his services. = 他们慷慨地报答了他的服务。|compensate, reward, recompense|underpay, shortchange|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company remunerates its employees fairly. = 公司公平地支付员工报酬。
We remunerate our consultants for their expertise. = 我们为顾问的专业知识支付报酬。


一般过去时:
The client remunerated the contractor promptly. = 客户迅速支付了承包商的报酬。
They remunerated him handsomely for his work. = 他们为他辛勤的工作支付了丰厚的报酬。


现在完成时:
The company has remunerated all its employees for their overtime. = 公司已经支付了所有员工的加班费。
She has been adequately remunerated for her years of service. = 她多年来的服务得到了充分的报酬。


过去完成时:
The board had already remunerated the CEO before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，董事会已经支付了首席执行官的报酬。
He had been fully remunerated for the damages before filing the lawsuit. = 在提起诉讼之前，他已经完全获得了损害赔偿。


将来时:
The company will remunerate the workers for their lost time. = 公司将赔偿工人们损失的时间。
They will be remunerated according to their performance. = 他们将根据他们的表现获得报酬。


虚拟语气:
If the company remunerated its employees better, they would be more productive. = 如果公司更好地支付员工报酬，他们就会更有生产力。
I wish they had remunerated me fairly for my work. = 我希望他们能公平地支付我的工作报酬。


被动语态:
The workers were remunerated for their extra hours. = 工人们得到了加班费。
The consultant was fairly remunerated for his services. = 顾问的服务得到了公平的报酬。


疑问句:
How will the company remunerate its employees for the holiday? = 公司将如何支付员工的假期工资？
Will the project manager be adequately remunerated for his efforts? = 项目经理的努力会得到足够的报酬吗？


否定句:
The company did not remunerate the employees for their extra work. = 公司没有支付员工的额外工作报酬。
They were not remunerated appropriately for their contributions. = 他们的贡献没有得到适当的报酬。


PHRASE->
remunerate fairly=公平地报酬
remunerate adequately=充分地报酬
remunerate handsomely=慷慨地报酬
remunerate promptly=迅速地报酬
remunerate fully=全额报酬


词源:
From Middle French *rémunérer, from Latin remunerāri "to reward," from remunerāre "to pay back, reward," from re- "again" + munus "gift, service, duty."
