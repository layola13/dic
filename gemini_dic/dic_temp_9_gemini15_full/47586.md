debasing:/dɪˈbeɪsɪŋ/| v. |贬低；降低；使堕落 |To lower the value, quality, or status of something or someone.  |The constant criticism was debasing her confidence.  持续的批评正在贬低她的自信。|近义词：degrading, lowering, undermining |反义词：elevating, enhancing, uplifting |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is debasing the currency. = 他正在贬低货币。
They are debasing the moral standards. = 他们正在贬低道德标准。

一般过去时:
The government debased the currency through inflation. = 政府通过通货膨胀贬低了货币。
He debased himself by lying. = 他通过说谎而使自己堕落。

现在完成时:
They have debased the reputation of the company. = 他们已经败坏了公司的声誉。
She has debased herself by her actions. = 她因为自己的行为而使自己堕落。

过去完成时:
They had debased the value of the artifacts before selling them. = 他们在出售文物之前已经贬低了文物的价值。
He had debased his principles to achieve his goal. = 为了达到目标，他已经放弃了自己的原则。

将来时:
The new policy will debase the education system. = 新政策将贬低教育体系。
He will debase himself if he continues this behavior. = 如果他继续这种行为，他会使自己堕落。

虚拟语气:
If he continued this behavior, he would debase himself. = 如果他继续这种行为，他会使自己堕落。
I wish they hadn't debased the quality of the product. = 我真希望他们没有降低产品的质量。

被动语态:
The currency was debased by the government's policies. = 货币因政府的政策而贬值。
The artist's work was debased by poor reproduction techniques. = 艺术家的作品因劣质的复制技术而贬值。

疑问句:
Is he debasing the meaning of the word? = 他是否在贬低这个词的意思？
Are they debasing the integrity of the process? = 他们是否在败坏这个过程的完整性？

否定句:
He did not debase the honor of his family. = 他没有败坏他家庭的荣誉。
They would not debase their values for money. = 他们不会为了钱而放弃自己的价值观。
