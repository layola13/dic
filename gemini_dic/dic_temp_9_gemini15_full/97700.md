enigma:/ˈɛnɪɡmə/| n. |谜；谜题；难以理解的事物 |Something that is mysterious, puzzling, or difficult to understand.  An enigmatic person or thing is mysterious and difficult to understand. |The origin of the universe remains an enigma.  宇宙的起源仍然是一个谜。|puzzle, mystery, riddle |solution, clarity |名词


例句：

The disappearance of the painting remains an enigma.  这幅画的失踪仍然是一个谜。

His behavior was an enigma to everyone.  他的行为对每个人来说都是一个谜。


近义词：

mystery, puzzle, riddle, conundrum, paradox


反义词：

clarity, solution, explanation
