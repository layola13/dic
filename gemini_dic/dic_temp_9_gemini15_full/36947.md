colourful: /ˈkʌləfl/ /ˈkʌlərfl/ | adj. |色彩鲜艳的，五彩缤纷的，多姿多彩的 |  Having many bright colors; full of variety and interest.  | The garden was colourful with a variety of flowers. 花园里五彩缤纷的花朵盛开。The book presents a colourful account of his travels. 这本书生动地记述了他的旅行经历。 | vibrant, vivid, bright, varied | monochrome, dull, drab, monotonous | 形容词

词源:

来自colour (颜色) + -ful (形容词后缀)，表示“充满…的”。  "Colour" itself originates from the Old French "color," ultimately from the Latin "color," meaning "appearance, hue."
