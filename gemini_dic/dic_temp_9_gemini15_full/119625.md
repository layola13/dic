middle-agedness:/ˌmɪdlˈeɪdʒɪdnəs/| n. |中年；中年期|The state or quality of being middle-aged; the period of life between youth and old age.  It often encompasses the challenges, experiences, and perspectives associated with this life stage. |例句：He was grappling with the anxieties of middle-agedness. 他正在努力应对中年期的焦虑。|例句：Her novel explored the complexities of middle-agedness and relationships. 她的小说探讨了中年期和人际关系的复杂性。|近义词：midlife, maturity|反义词：youth, adolescence
