pyrolator:/ˈpaɪrəleɪtər/| n. |热解器，热裂解器|A pyrolator is a device used to perform pyrolysis, a chemical process in which organic substances are decomposed by heat in the absence of oxygen.|例句：The pyrolator heated the sample to a high temperature, breaking down the organic molecules. 热解器将样品加热到高温，分解有机分子。  The researchers used a specialized pyrolator to analyze the composition of the ancient wood. 研究人员使用专门的热解器来分析古代木材的成分。|近义词：pyrolyzer, thermal cracker|反义词：N/A
