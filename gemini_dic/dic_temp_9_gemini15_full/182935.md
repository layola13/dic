rephrase:/ˌriːˈfreɪz/ | vt. | 改述；改写；重新表达 | To express something in different words, often to make it clearer or more concise.  | Example: Could you rephrase that question?  It's a little confusing.  你能重新表达一下那个问题吗？有点令人困惑。|近义词：paraphrase, restate, reformulate | 反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to leave something un-rephrased.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rephrases his arguments to make them more persuasive. = 他改述了他的论点，使它们更有说服力。
She rephrases every sentence to ensure clarity. = 她改写每一个句子以确保清晰。


一般过去时:
The teacher rephrased the complex sentence for the students. = 老师为学生们改写了那个复杂的句子。
He rephrased his answer to avoid offending her. = 他重新表达了他的答案以避免冒犯她。


现在完成时:
She has rephrased the essay several times to improve its flow. = 她已经多次改写了这篇论文以改善其流畅性。
I have rephrased my request to make it more polite. = 我已经重新表达了我的请求，使其更礼貌。


过去完成时:
He had rephrased his email before sending it. = 他在发送邮件之前已经重新表达了他的邮件内容。
They had rephrased the contract to address the concerns. = 他们已经改写了合同以解决这些担忧。


将来时:
I will rephrase my comment to be more specific. = 我将重新表达我的评论，使其更具体。
She will rephrase the instructions to make them easier to understand. = 她将改写说明，使它们更容易理解。


虚拟语气:
If I were to rephrase it, I would say this. = 如果我要重新表达它，我会这么说。
I wish I had rephrased my response more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地重新表达我的回应。


被动语态:
The sentence was rephrased to make it more concise. = 句子被改写得更简洁。
The instructions will be rephrased for better clarity. = 说明将被改写以获得更好的清晰度。


疑问句:
Should I rephrase my statement? = 我应该重新表达我的陈述吗？
How would you rephrase this sentence? = 你将如何改写这个句子？


否定句:
He didn't rephrase his question, despite the confusion. = 尽管很困惑，他并没有重新表达他的问题。
She refused to rephrase her controversial remarks. = 她拒绝重新表达她有争议的言论。
