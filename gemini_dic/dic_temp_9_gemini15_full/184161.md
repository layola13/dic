resize:/ˌriːˈsaɪz/| vt.|重新调整大小，改变尺寸|To change the size of something, typically an image or window.|例句：I need to resize this image to fit the frame. 我需要调整这张图片的大小以适合相框。
例句：Please resize the window to make it bigger. 请调整窗口大小使其更大。|近义词：scale, adjust, alter|反义词：maintain the same size
