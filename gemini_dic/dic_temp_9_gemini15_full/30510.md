centurist:/ˈsɛntʃʊrɪst/| n. |百年主义者，世纪主义者|A person who focuses on the achievements and events of a particular century, often expressing strong opinions about it, sometimes with a sense of superiority or nostalgia.|例句：He's a bit of a 20th-century centurist, always talking about how much better things were back then. 他有点20世纪的百年主义者，总是说过去的事情有多好。|近义词：nostalgist, traditionalist |反义词：modernist, futurist
