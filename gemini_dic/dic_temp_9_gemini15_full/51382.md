determinants:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnənts/ | n. |决定因素；行列式|Factors that decisively influence the nature or outcome of something.|The main determinants of success in business are hard work and innovation.  商业成功的决定性因素是努力工作和创新。|The economic determinants of poverty are complex and multifaceted. 贫困的经济决定因素复杂且多方面。|近义词：factors, influences, causes |反义词：indeterminates, variables


USAGE->

一般现在时:
What determinants influence the price of oil? = 什么决定因素会影响石油价格？
The determinants of his success are many and varied. = 他成功的决定因素有很多，而且多种多样。


一般过去时:
What were the determinants of the war? = 战争的决定因素是什么？
The determinants of the company's failure were poor management and lack of innovation. = 公司失败的决定因素是管理不善和缺乏创新。


现在完成时:
What determinants have shaped the modern world? = 哪些决定因素塑造了现代世界？
The determinants of his success have been his hard work and dedication. = 他成功的决定因素一直以来都是他的努力工作和奉献精神。


过去完成时:
What determinants had already been established before the experiment began? = 在实验开始之前已经确立了哪些决定因素？
The determinants of the conflict had been simmering for years before it erupted. = 冲突的决定因素在爆发前已经酝酿多年了。


将来时:
What determinants will affect the future of the planet? = 什么决定因素将影响地球的未来？
The determinants of the election will likely be the economy and social issues. = 大选的决定因素可能是经济和社会问题。


虚拟语气:
If we knew the determinants, we could predict the outcome. = 如果我们知道决定因素，我们就能预测结果。
I wish we understood the determinants of human behavior better. = 我希望我们能更好地理解人类行为的决定因素。


被动语态:
The outcome was largely determined by external determinants. = 结果很大程度上取决于外部决定因素。
The results are determined by a variety of factors. = 结果由多种因素决定。


疑问句:
What are the key determinants of this problem? = 这个问题的关键决定因素是什么？
Are there any other significant determinants we should consider? = 我们是否应该考虑任何其他重要的决定因素？


否定句:
There are no single determinants for this phenomenon. = 这种现象没有单一的决定因素。
The success of the project was not determined by external determinants alone. = 项目的成功并非仅仅由外部决定因素决定。
