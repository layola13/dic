frankensteins:/ˈfræŋkənstaɪnz/| n.|弗兰肯斯坦们，弗兰肯斯坦式的创造物|The term "Frankensteins" refers to something monstrous or terrifying, often a creation that has unforeseen and negative consequences. It originates from Mary Shelley's novel "Frankenstein," where Victor Frankenstein creates a creature that turns against him.|例句：Some people view genetically modified foods as modern-day Frankensteins.|近义词：monsters, abominations|反义词：blessings, miracles|名词
