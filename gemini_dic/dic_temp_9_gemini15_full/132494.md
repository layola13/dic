nondesignate:/ˌnɒnˈdɛzɪɡneɪt/| adj.|未指定的；未被指定的|Not designated; not appointed or assigned to a particular purpose or role.|The nondesignate area was left undeveloped.  未指定的区域未开发。|The nondesignate employees were given general tasks. 未被指定职位的员工被分配了一般性任务。|designated, appointed|designated, assigned|


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses like past, present, future etc.  The usage is always the same: describing something as not having been designated.
