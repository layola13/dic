patrilineal:/ˌpætɹɪˈlaɪnɪəl/| adj. |父系的；父系社会的；沿父系继承的|Relating to or based on kinship with the father or descent through the male line.|Many societies are patrilineal, tracing lineage through the father's side of the family.  许多社会是父系的，通过父亲一方的家庭追溯血统。|The inheritance was patrilineal, passing only to the eldest son.  继承是父系的，只传给长子。|matrilineal,  uterine|matrilocal
