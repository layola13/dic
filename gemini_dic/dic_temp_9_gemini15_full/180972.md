reimmerse:/ˌriːɪˈmɜːrs/| vt.|重新沉浸；再次沉浸|To immerse again; to submerge again.  It implies a return to a previous state of immersion, often implying a deeper or more complete immersion than before.|例句：After a break, she decided to reimmerse herself in her research.  休息过后，她决定重新投入她的研究。|例句：He reimmersed himself in the cool water after a short rest on the beach.他在沙滩上短暂休息后再次沉浸在凉爽的水中。|近义词：submerge, immerse, engulf|反义词：emerge, extract
