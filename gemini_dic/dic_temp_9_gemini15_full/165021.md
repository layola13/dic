prehistory:/ˈpriːhɪstəri/| n. |史前时期，史前时代 |The period of time before written records.  The study of this period relies on archaeology, anthropology, and geology to reconstruct past events and cultures.|例句：The discovery of ancient tools shed light on the prehistory of the region.  这项古代工具的发现揭示了该地区史前时期的信息。

例句：Much of human prehistory remains a mystery.  人类史前史的大部分仍然是个谜。


近义词：antiquity, primeval times


反义词：history


词性：名词
