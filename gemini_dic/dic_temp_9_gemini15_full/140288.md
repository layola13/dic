oftentimes:/ˈɔːftənˌtaɪmz/ | adv. | 经常；很多时候 |  Oftentimes means frequently or many times. It is a more emphatic and slightly more formal version of "often."  |  Oftentimes, I go for a walk in the park. = 我经常去公园散步。  They oftentimes forget their keys. = 他们经常忘记带钥匙。 | often, frequently, many times | rarely, seldom, infrequently | 副词
