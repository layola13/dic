necklaces:/ˈnekləsɪz/ | n. |项链；项圈 | Necklaces are adornments worn around the neck, often made of precious metals, stones, or beads. They can be simple or elaborate, and are often used as jewelry. |例句：She wore a beautiful diamond necklace to the party. 她戴了一条美丽的钻石项链去参加派对。

例句：Many cultures have traditions associated with wearing necklaces. 许多文化都有佩戴项链的传统。

反义词：None (no direct antonym)
