dumpiest:/ˈdʌmpiɪst/| adj.|最矮胖的，最笨拙的 | Most clumsy and stout.  It's the superlative form of "dumpy," suggesting the highest degree of being short, stout, and clumsy. |例句：The dumpiest of the puppies waddled after its mother. 最矮胖的小狗摇摇晃晃地跟在它妈妈后面。 The dumpiest person in the room struggled to fit through the narrow doorway.房间里最矮胖的人费力地穿过狭窄的门道。|近义词：stockiest, chubbiest, most squat |反义词：tallest, slimmest, most graceful
