prothallus:/ˈproʊθæləs/| n. |原叶体，配子体|The prothallus is the small, flat, green, gametophyte stage in the life cycle of ferns and other pteridophytes.  It develops from a spore and produces the sex organs (antheridia and archegonia).|The fern prothallus is heart-shaped and typically less than a centimeter in diameter.|近义词：gametophyte|反义词：sporophyte
