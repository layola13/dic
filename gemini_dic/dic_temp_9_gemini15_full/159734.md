polycotyledon: /ˌpɒliˈkɒtɪliːdən/| n. |多子叶植物|A plant having more than two cotyledons (seed leaves) in its embryo.  Polycotyledons are relatively rare, primarily found in certain conifers and some unusual flowering plants. They represent a deviation from the typical dicotyledonous or monocotyledonous patterns.|Example:  The unusual seed structure of this plant, with its multiple cotyledons, identifies it as a polycotyledon. = 这株植物的种子结构异常，具有多个子叶，这表明它是一种多子叶植物。|近义词：multicotyledon (less common)|反义词：monocotyledon, dicotyledon|名词
