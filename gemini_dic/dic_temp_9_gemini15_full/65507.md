euroscepticism:/ˈjʊərəʊˈsɛptɪsɪzəm/| n. |欧洲怀疑论|Opposition to the European Union and its policies.  It encompasses a range of views, from those who simply question certain EU policies to those who advocate for leaving the EU entirely. |The rise of euroscepticism in several European countries led to referendums on EU membership. 欧洲怀疑论在一些欧洲国家的兴起导致了关于欧盟成员国身份的公投。|EU-scepticism, anti-Europeanism| pro-Europeanism, pro-EU
