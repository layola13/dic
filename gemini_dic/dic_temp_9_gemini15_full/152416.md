pavone:/pəˈvoʊni/ | n. |孔雀，自夸的人|A pavone is a peacock, or someone who shows off ostentatiously.  The word often implies a vain or boastful display.|例句：He pavoned around the room, showing off his new suit. 他在房间里趾高气扬地走来走去，炫耀他的新西装。  The peacock pavoned its magnificent tail feathers. 孔雀炫耀着它华丽的尾羽。|近义词：peacock, show off, swagger, strut|反义词：humble, modest, shy

词源:
The word "pavone" comes from the Italian word "pavone," meaning "peacock."  It entered English relatively recently, reflecting the adoption of the peacock's ostentatious display as a metaphor for human vanity.
