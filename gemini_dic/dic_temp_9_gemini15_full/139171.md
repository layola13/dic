obituarily:/əˈbɪtʃuərili/| adv.| 死后地，在讣告中 | In a manner characteristic of an obituary; as if in an obituary.  It describes something written or said as if it were in an obituary. |例句：The article obituarily recounted the deceased's life achievements. 这篇文章如同讣告般地叙述了死者的生平成就。


The local newspaper reported the incident obituarily, focusing on the tragic loss. 当地报纸以讣告的方式报道了这一事件，重点关注了这场悲惨的损失。


近义词：funereally, posthumously


反义词：vivaciously, joyfully


词性:副词
