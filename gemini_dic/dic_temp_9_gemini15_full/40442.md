contrastimulant: /ˌkɒntrəˈstɪmjələnt/ | n. | 对抗兴奋剂；抑制兴奋剂 | A substance that counteracts or inhibits stimulation; an agent that reduces excitation or activity.  It often refers to a drug that reduces the effects of a stimulant. | Example:  While caffeine is a stimulant, certain medications act as contrastimulants to counteract its effects.  某些药物作为对抗兴奋剂来抵消咖啡因的效果。 |近义词: depressant, inhibitor, sedative | 反义词: stimulant, excitant | 名词
