grails:/ɡreɪlz/| n.|格莱尔斯（苏格兰威士忌）, 魔杖, 魅力, 成功|A type of Scotch whisky;  a magical staff or wand; something that brings charm or success.  The word often refers to the Scotch whisky, but its broader meaning encompasses magical objects and elements of success.|例句：He brought out a collection of rare grails. 他拿出了一套珍稀的格莱尔斯威士忌。


例句：The Grail represents the ultimate goal. 圣杯象征着最终目标。


近义词：chalice, cup, achievement, success, prize|反义词：failure, defeat, disappointment|词性：名词
