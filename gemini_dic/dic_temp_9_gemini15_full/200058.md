sketched:/skɛtʃt/ | v. | 描绘，速写，勾勒 | To make a quick, rough drawing of something.  |  She sketched a quick portrait of the child. 她快速地为孩子画了一幅肖像。|  draw, outline, draft |  painted, detailed |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sketches every day. = 他每天都画速写。
She sketches beautiful landscapes. = 她画美丽的风景画。

一般过去时:
I sketched a quick map. = 我快速地画了一张地图。
The artist sketched the model's pose. = 艺术家速写了模特儿的姿势。

现在完成时:
She has sketched many portraits. = 她已经画了许多肖像。
He has sketched the main ideas for his novel. = 他已经勾勒了他小说的主要构思。

过去完成时:
I had sketched the design before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经画好了设计图。
They had sketched out a plan before starting the project. = 他们在开始项目之前已经制定了一个计划。

将来时:
I will sketch a design for the new building. = 我将为新建筑设计一个草图。
She will sketch the scene from memory. = 她将凭记忆画出这个场景。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would sketch more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地画速写。
I wish I could sketch as well as he can. = 我希望我能像他一样画得好。

被动语态:
The scene was sketched in detail. = 场景被详细地勾勒出来。
The building plans were sketched by the architect. = 建筑图纸由建筑师绘制。

疑问句:
Did you sketch the building? = 你画了那栋建筑吗？
Have you ever sketched a person from life? = 你曾经现场速写过人吗？

否定句:
I didn't sketch the scene. = 我没有画那个场景。
She couldn't sketch the details accurately. = 她无法准确地勾勒细节。


PHRASE->
