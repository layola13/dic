commanderships:/kəˈmændəʃɪps/ | n. |指挥权；指挥职位 | The responsibilities and authority associated with being a commander.  It refers to the position itself, rather than the act of commanding. |例句：He held several important commanderships during his military career. 他在军旅生涯中担任过几个重要的指挥职务。

例句：The commanderships of the various battalions were rotated annually. 各个营的指挥权每年轮换一次。


近义词：command, leadership,  authority


反义词：subordination, followership


词性：名词
