pro-Americanism:/ˌproʊ əˈmɛrɪkənɪzəm/ | n. |亲美主义，亲美倾向 |Support for or advocacy of the policies or interests of the United States.  It often involves a positive view of American culture, values, and political system. |例句：The politician's pro-Americanism was evident in his speeches.  这位政治家的亲美立场在他的演讲中显而易见。|近义词：Americanophilia, phil-Americanism |反义词：anti-Americanism
