harmonist:/ˈhɑːrmənɪst/ | n. | 和谐主义者，调音师 | A person who believes in or promotes harmony, especially in social or political affairs; also, a person skilled in tuning musical instruments. | 例句：He was a staunch harmonist, always seeking common ground in debates. 他是一位坚定的和谐主义者，总是在辩论中寻求共同点。  | 例句：The harmonist meticulously tuned the piano before the concert.  这位调音师在音乐会前仔细地调音了钢琴。 | 近义词：pacifist, peacemaker, tuner | 反义词：discordist, troublemaker
