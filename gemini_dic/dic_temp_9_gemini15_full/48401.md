dedicatee:/ˌdɛdɪˈkeɪtiː/| n. |献词的对象，被献给的人|A person to whom something, such as a book or a musical work, is dedicated.  It refers to the recipient of a dedication.|The author dedicated the book to his dedicatee, his beloved grandmother.  作者将这本书献给了他的献词对象——他亲爱的祖母。|recipient, dedicatory |undeserving, unworthy |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The author dedicates his new novel to his wife. = 作者将他的新小说献给他的妻子。
This song is dedicated to all the mothers in the world. = 这首歌献给世界上所有的母亲。


一般过去时:
He dedicated his life to serving others. = 他将一生奉献于服务他人。
She dedicated her award to her mentor. = 她把奖项献给了她的导师。


现在完成时:
They have dedicated their time and resources to this project. = 他们已经将时间和资源投入到这个项目中。
He has dedicated many years to the study of ancient history. = 他多年来一直致力于古代史的研究。


过去完成时:
She had already dedicated the painting to her friend before the exhibition. = 展览之前，她已经把画献给了她的朋友。
They had dedicated their efforts to finding a solution before giving up. = 在放弃之前，他们已经尽力寻找解决方案。


将来时:
I will dedicate my next speech to the memory of my father. = 我将在我的下次演讲中缅怀我的父亲。
She will dedicate her future work to advancing women's rights. = 她将把未来的工作献给推进妇女权利的事业。


虚拟语气:
If I were to write a book, I would dedicate it to my parents. = 如果我要写一本书，我会把它献给我的父母。
I wish I could dedicate more time to my family. = 我希望我能多花些时间陪家人。


被动语态:
The song was dedicated to the memory of the fallen soldiers. = 这首歌是献给阵亡士兵的。
The building was dedicated to the cause of world peace. = 这座建筑物是献给世界和平事业的。


疑问句:
To whom is this book dedicated? = 这本书献给谁？
Who was the poem dedicated to? = 这首诗献给谁？


否定句:
He did not dedicate his work to anyone in particular. = 他没有特别把他的作品献给任何人。
The artist did not dedicate the sculpture to a specific person. = 艺术家没有将雕塑献给特定的人。


PHRASE->
dedicate to = 献给…
be dedicated to = 致力于…
a dedication to = 对…的奉献
dedicated effort = 尽心尽力的努力
dedicated worker = 尽职尽责的员工
