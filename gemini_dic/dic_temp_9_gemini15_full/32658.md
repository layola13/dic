chiton:/ˈkaɪtən/| n. |多板纲动物；石鳖 |A chiton is a marine mollusk with a shell composed of eight overlapping plates. Chitons are found in intertidal zones and shallow waters worldwide.  They graze on algae and other organisms using a radula, a rasping tongue-like structure.|例句：The rocky intertidal zone was teeming with chitons clinging tightly to the rocks.  岩石潮间带挤满了紧紧依附在岩石上的石鳖。|近义词：polyplacophoran |反义词：N/A
