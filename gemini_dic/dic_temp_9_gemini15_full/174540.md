quintiles:/ˈkwɪntaɪlz/ | n. | 五分位数 | Five equal groups into which a population can be divided, based on the distribution of a particular variable, such as income or wealth.  Each quintile represents 20% of the population.  For example, the top quintile represents the wealthiest 20% of the population. | The study divided the participants into quintiles based on their age.  这项研究根据参与者的年龄将他们分成五个等级。 The bottom quintile experienced the highest rate of unemployment. 最低收入的五分之一人口的失业率最高。| deciles, percentiles |  (no direct antonym, but the opposite concept would be to group data into unequal parts) | 名词
