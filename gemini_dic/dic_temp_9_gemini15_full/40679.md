conversationally:/ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənəli/ /ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənəli/ | adj. |口语化的，会话式的 |In a manner characteristic of conversation; informally.  It describes something said or done in a casual, informal way, typical of everyday conversation rather than formal speech or writing. |例句：He explained the concept conversationally, using simple terms and avoiding jargon. 他用简单的术语解释了这个概念，避免使用术语，以口语化的方式进行。

例句：She conversationally mentioned her upcoming trip, as if it were no big deal. 她漫不经心地提到了她即将到来的旅行，好像没什么大不了的。


近义词：informally, casually, colloquially, familiarly |反义词：formally, officially, ceremoniously


词性：形容词
