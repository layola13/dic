buyable: /ˈbaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可买的，值得买的 | Able to be bought; worth buying.  Something that is buyable is available for purchase and considered worthwhile to acquire. |

例句：

The tickets for the concert are buyable online. = 音乐会的门票可以在网上购买。

That vintage car is quite buyable, given its condition and rarity. = 考虑到其状况和稀有性，那辆老爷车相当值得购买。


近义词：purchasable, obtainable, affordable (if referring to price)

反义词：unobtainable, unpurchasable, unaffordable (if referring to price), overpriced (if referring to value)

词性：形容词
