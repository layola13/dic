inspirer:/ɪnˈspaɪrər/| n. |鼓舞者，激励者 |A person or thing that inspires. Someone or something that gives you new ideas and enthusiasm.|  Her teacher was a great inspirer.  她的老师是一位伟大的激励者。| motivator, stimulator, influencer |  depressor, discourager | 名词


USAGE->

The artist inspires many people. =这位艺术家激励了许多人。
This book inspires me to write. =这本书激励我去写作。


一般过去时:
