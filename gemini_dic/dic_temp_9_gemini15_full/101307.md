inwardly:/ˈɪnwərdli/| adv.|内在地，内心深处地|  Internally; within the mind or spirit.  It describes something happening or felt inside, rather than outwardly expressed.|例句：She inwardly rejoiced at the news, though she showed no outward sign. 她内心为这个消息而高兴，尽管她没有表现出来。

例句：He inwardly cursed his own clumsiness. 他内心咒骂自己的笨拙。


近义词：internally, mentally, secretly, privately|反义词：outwardly, openly, externally|词性:副词
