intracultural: /ˌɪntrəˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. |内部文化的；文化内部的 | Relating to or occurring within a single culture.  It emphasizes the interactions, variations, and complexities within a particular culture, rather than comparing it to others. | Example: Intracultural communication studies focus on the nuances of communication within a specific cultural group.  内部文化交流研究侧重于特定文化群体内部交流的细微之处。  | 近义词：intrasocietal, internal (in a cultural context) | 反义词：intercultural, cross-cultural
