reputedly: /rɪˈpjuːtɪdli/ | adv. |据称；据说；名誉上地 |  According to what people say; reputedly.  The adverb "reputedly" indicates that something is believed to be true based on reputation or hearsay, but not necessarily proven fact.  | 例句：He is reputedly the richest man in town. = 他据说是镇上最富有的人。


The company is reputedly developing a new type of engine. = 这家公司据说正在开发一种新型发动机。


近义词：allegedly, supposedly, reportedly | 反义词：certainly, undeniably, provably


词性: 副词
