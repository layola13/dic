codirector:/ˌkoʊdaɪˈrɛktər/ | n. | 联合导演 | A codirector is a person who shares the directorial responsibilities of a film, play, or other production with another person.  They work together to make creative decisions and oversee the production process. | Example:  The film was directed by a team, with Jane Doe and John Smith serving as codirectors.  这部电影由一个团队执导，Jane Doe 和 John Smith 担任联合导演。 | 近义词：joint director, associate director | 反义词：sole director
