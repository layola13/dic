multitools:/ˈmʌltiˌtuːlz/ | n. |多功能工具 |A multitool is a hand tool that combines several different tools into a single, compact unit.  Common tools found in multitools include pliers, screwdrivers, knives, saws, and scissors.  They are often used for repairs, outdoor activities, and emergency situations. |Example: He used his multitool to tighten a loose screw on his bike. 他用他的多功能工具拧紧自行车上松动的螺丝。  She always keeps a multitool in her backpack for hiking trips. 她在背包里总是放一个多功能工具用于徒步旅行。|近义词：utility knife, pocket tool, combination tool |反义词：specialized tool
