nonexhaustible: /ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɔːstɪbl/ | adj. | 不竭的；用之不竭的 | Not capable of being exhausted or used up.  It implies a resource that is essentially limitless or replenishes faster than it is consumed.  | Example: Solar energy is a nonexhaustible resource.  太阳能是一种取之不尽的资源。 The ocean's vastness feels nonexhaustible. 海洋的广阔无垠令人感觉用之不竭。 | 近义词: inexhaustible, unlimited, boundless, infinite | 反义词: exhaustible, limited, finite
