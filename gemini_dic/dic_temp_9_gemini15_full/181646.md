relaying: /rɪˈleɪɪŋ/ | v. | 转播；传递；依靠；重复 |  The act of transmitting something, such as a message or signal, from one point to another; or the act of repeating or reiterating something.  |

例句：

The news channel was relaying live coverage of the storm. = 新闻频道正在转播暴风雨的实时报道。

The runner relayed the baton to the next participant in the race. = 跑步运动员将接力棒传递给了比赛中的下一位参与者。


近义词：transmitting, forwarding, retransmitting, repeating, conveying

反义词：receiving, intercepting, stopping


USAGE->

They are relaying the information to the authorities. = 他们正在向当局传递信息。
The radio station is relaying a concert from the city center. = 收音机电台正在转播来自市中心的音乐会。

现在完成时:
They have relayed the emergency broadcast to all regions. = 他们已将紧急广播转播到所有地区。

过去完成时:

The team had relayed the plan to their colleagues before the meeting. = 团队在会议前已将计划转达给了他们的同事。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
