denominations:/ˌdɛnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 面额；教派；命名；名称 |  The word "denominations" refers to the different values of money, such as coins or bills.  It can also refer to different branches or sects within a religion, or to the act of giving a name or designation to something. | Example: The bank teller counted the denominations of the money.  银行出纳员清点钱的币值。  The country has many Christian denominations. 这个国家有很多基督教教派。|近义词：sects, sects, values, names |反义词：unification, whole
