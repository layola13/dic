pulverised: /ˈpʌlvəraɪzd/ | v. | 粉碎；磨成粉末；彻底击败 | To reduce to powder or dust; to completely defeat or overwhelm. |

例句：

The rock was pulverised by the explosion.  =  岩石被爆炸炸成了粉末。

The army was completely pulverised in the battle. =  军队在战斗中被彻底击败。


近义词：crushed, ground, smashed, obliterated, annihilated

反义词：consolidated, unified, intact
