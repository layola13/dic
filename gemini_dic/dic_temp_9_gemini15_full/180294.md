refrigerants:/rɪˈfrɪdʒərənts/| n. |制冷剂|Substances used in refrigeration systems to absorb heat and lower the temperature.  Refrigerants are typically volatile liquids or gases that undergo a phase change during the refrigeration cycle. They are chosen for their thermodynamic properties, environmental impact, and safety.|Example:  Many modern refrigerators use environmentally friendly refrigerants. 许多现代冰箱使用环保制冷剂。

The development of new refrigerants is an active area of research, driven by the need to reduce the environmental impact of traditional refrigerants. 新型制冷剂的研发是一个活跃的研究领域，其动力在于减少传统制冷剂对环境的影响。|近义词：coolants, refrigerating agents|反义词：heaters
