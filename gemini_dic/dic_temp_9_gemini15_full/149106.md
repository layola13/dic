oxford-cloth: /ˈɒksfəd klɒθ/ | n. |牛津布|A tightly woven, durable cotton fabric, typically plain weave, known for its smooth surface and strength. It's often used for shirts and other clothing items. |例句：His shirt is made of fine oxford cloth. 他的衬衫是用精细的牛津布做的。  The oxford cloth button-down shirt is a classic style. 牛津布纽扣衬衫是一款经典款式。|近义词：broadcloth, poplin|反义词：None specifically, but fabrics with looser weaves or different textures could be considered opposites.|名词
