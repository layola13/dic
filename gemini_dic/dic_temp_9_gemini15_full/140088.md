off-air: /ˌɔːfˈɛər/ | adj. | 停播的；不在播出的 | Not being broadcast; not on the air.  |例句：The radio station went off-air during the storm.  =暴风雨期间，广播电台停止播音。

例句：The TV show is off-air for the summer. = 这个电视节目夏季停播。


近义词：unbroadcast, not on the air | 反义词：on-air, broadcasting


词源:  The term is a compound of "off" (meaning not in operation or not active) and "air" (referring to the broadcast medium).  It directly describes the state of a broadcast not being transmitted.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The show is off-air right now. = 节目现在停播了。
The radio station is usually off-air overnight. = 广播电台通常在夜间停播。


一般过去时:
The broadcast went off-air unexpectedly. = 节目意外停播了。
The channel was off-air for several hours. = 频道停播了好几个小时。


现在完成时:
The station has been off-air since the power outage. = 自停电以来，电台一直停播。
The program has been off-air for a long time. = 这档节目已经停播很久了。


过去完成时:
The program had been off-air for a week before they fixed the problem. = 他们在解决问题之前，节目已经停播了一周。
The network had been off-air several times that month. = 那一个月网络已经停播过几次了。


将来时:
The show will be off-air next week for maintenance. = 节目下周将停播进行维护。
The station will go off-air at midnight. = 电台将在午夜停播。


虚拟语气:
If the transmitter failed, the broadcast would go off-air. = 如果发射机发生故障，广播将会停播。
If they hadn't fixed the equipment, the show would have been off-air all day. = 如果他们没有修理设备，节目就会停播一整天。


被动语态:
The broadcast was taken off-air due to technical difficulties. = 由于技术故障，广播被停播了。
The show is being taken off-air temporarily. = 节目正在暂时停播。


疑问句:
Is the program still off-air? = 节目还在停播吗？
Was the station off-air yesterday? = 电台昨天停播了吗？


否定句:
The show is not off-air yet. = 节目还没有停播。
The channel wasn't off-air during the emergency. = 紧急情况下频道没有停播。


PHRASE->
go off-air = 停播
take something off-air = 将……停播
remain off-air = 保持停播状态
return to the air/be back on air = 恢复播出
off-air recording = 停播录音
