shouldering:/ˈʃoʊldəriŋ/| v. |承担；扛；肩负|Supporting or carrying something on one's shoulders; also figuratively, taking on a responsibility or burden. |例句：He was shouldering a heavy backpack. 他背着一个沉重的背包。|例句：She shouldered the responsibility of caring for her aging parents. 她承担起了照顾年迈父母的责任。|近义词：bearing, carrying, supporting, undertaking |反义词：avoiding, shirking, neglecting |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shoulders the blame for the accident. = 他承担了事故的责任。
They are shouldering the burden of debt. = 他们正在承担债务的重担。

一般过去时:
She shouldered the heavy box with ease. = 她轻松地扛起了那个沉重的箱子。
He shouldered his way through the crowd. = 他挤过人群。

现在完成时:
We have shouldered many challenges in our business. = 在我们的业务中，我们已经克服了许多挑战。
She has shouldered the responsibility for years. = 她已经承担了多年的责任。

过去完成时:
They had shouldered the financial burden before the project started. = 在项目开始之前，他们已经承担了经济负担。
He had shouldered the responsibility of leading the team. = 他已经承担了领导团队的责任。

将来时:
She will shoulder the responsibility of managing the department. = 她将承担管理部门的责任。
They will shoulder the costs of the renovation. = 他们将承担装修的费用。

虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I could shoulder that heavy load. = 如果我更强壮，我就能扛起那个重物。
I wish I could shoulder the burden of their problems. = 我希望我能承担他们问题的负担。

被动语态:
The responsibility was shouldered by the project manager. = 责任由项目经理承担。
The extra work was shouldered by the team. =  额外的任务由团队承担。

疑问句:
Who shouldered the responsibility for the mistake? = 谁承担了这个错误的责任？
Are they shouldering the cost of the repairs? = 他们承担维修费用吗？

否定句:
He did not shoulder the blame. = 他没有承担责任。
They refused to shoulder the burden of the task. = 他们拒绝承担这项任务的重担。


PHRASE->

shoulder one's way through: 挤过
shoulder to shoulder with: 与…肩并肩
