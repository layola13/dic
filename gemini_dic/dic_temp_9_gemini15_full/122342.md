mixen:/ˈmɪksən/| n.|混合物，粪堆|A mixen is a dung heap, typically one that is composed of a mixture of animal dung and other organic matter.  It is often found on farms and in rural areas.  The term is primarily used in British English.|例句：The farmer spread the mixen on the fields to fertilize the soil. 农民将粪堆撒在田里给土壤施肥。
The smell of the mixen was strong and pungent. 粪堆的气味又浓又刺鼻。|近义词：dung heap, compost heap|反义词：cleanliness, purity
