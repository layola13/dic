ripeners: /ˈraɪpənərz/ | n. |催熟剂，促熟剂 | Substances used to accelerate the ripening process of fruits.  They can be natural or synthetic chemicals that stimulate the production of ethylene, a plant hormone that triggers ripening. | Example: Farmers sometimes use ripeners to ensure that their fruits reach the market at the optimal stage of ripeness.  农民有时使用催熟剂以确保他们的水果在最佳成熟度时上市。|近义词：maturation agents, ripening agents |反义词：retardants (in the context of ripening)
