muting:/ˈmjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 使沉默；使消音；减弱 | The act of making something quieter or less noticeable; silencing or dampening a sound.  |例句：He was muting the microphone during the online meeting. 他在网上会议期间正在消音麦克风。

例句：The musician muted the strings to create a softer sound.  音乐家使琴弦消音以创造更柔和的声音。


近义词：silencing, dampening, softening, quieting | 反义词：amplifying, intensifying | 词性：动词


USAGE->
