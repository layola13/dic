radder:/ˈrædə(r)/| n.|舵，方向舵|A rudder is a flat, vertical piece of equipment at the back of a boat or aircraft that is used to control its direction.|例句：The sailor adjusted the rudder to steer the boat around the rocks.  舵手调整舵的方向，使船绕过岩石。|近义词：helm, sternpost|反义词：N/A
