perfuming: /pərˈfjuːmɪŋ/ | v. | 使充满香味；喷洒香水 | The act of adding fragrance, usually with perfume.  |例句：She was perfuming the room with lavender. 她正在用薰衣草使房间充满香味。 |例句：He perfumed his handkerchief with a delicate scent. 他用一种淡雅的香味使手帕充满香味。 | 近义词：scenting, fragrancing | 反义词：deodorizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She perfumes her room every morning. = 她每天早上都给她的房间喷洒香水。
They perfume their candles with essential oils. = 他们用精油给蜡烛增添香味。
