pseudoskeletal:/ˌsuːdoʊˈskɛlətəl/ | adj. | 假骨骼的；似骨骼的 |  Relating to or resembling a skeleton, but not being a true skeleton; often used to describe structures that provide support or framework but lack the typical characteristics of a true skeleton.  | Example: The insect's pseudoskeletal exoskeleton provides structural support.  昆虫的假骨骼外骨骼提供了结构支撑。 | 近义词: skeletal-like, quasi-skeletal | 反义词: true skeletal

词源:
The word "pseudoskeletal" is a combination of "pseudo-" (meaning false or resembling) and "skeletal" (relating to a skeleton).  Therefore, it describes something that appears to be or functions like a skeleton, but isn't a true, bone-based skeleton.
