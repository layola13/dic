offspring:/ˈɒfsprɪŋ/ /ˈɔːfsprɪŋ/ | n. | 子孙，后代 | The descendants of a person, animal, or plant.  |例句：The couple had three offspring. 这对夫妇有三个孩子。|例句：The oak tree produced many offspring. 这棵橡树繁殖了许多后代。|近义词：children, progeny, descendants |反义词：ancestor, forebear
