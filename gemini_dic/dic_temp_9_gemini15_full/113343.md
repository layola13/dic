luxuriation:/ˌlʌɡʒʊˈreɪʃən/| n. |尽情享受；奢侈；挥霍 |The act of indulging in luxury or pleasure; the state of being luxurious.  It implies a lavish and often excessive enjoyment of pleasures. |例句：He indulged in the luxuriation of a long hot bath after a hard day's work. 他在辛苦工作一天后尽情享受了长时间的热水浴。

例句：The luxuriation of the palace was breathtaking; gold leaf adorned every surface. 宫殿的奢华令人叹为观止；每个表面都装饰着金箔。


近义词：indulgence, self-indulgence, revelry, extravagance

反义词：austerity, frugality, self-denial, deprivation


词性：名词
