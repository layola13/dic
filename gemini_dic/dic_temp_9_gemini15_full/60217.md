effluvial:/ɪˈfluːviəl/| adj.|恶臭的；散发恶臭的 | Relating to or resembling an effluvium; foul-smelling.  |The effluvial smell coming from the drain was unbearable.  下水道散发出的恶臭令人难以忍受。|fetid, malodorous, noisome| fragrant, sweet-smelling|形容词
