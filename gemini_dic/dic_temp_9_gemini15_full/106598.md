kudos:/ˈkuːdoʊz/| n.|荣誉；赞扬；赞赏|Praise and honor received for an achievement.|例句：She received kudos for her outstanding work. 她因其杰出的工作而获得赞扬。|近义词：praise, acclaim, commendation, tribute|反义词：criticism, condemnation, censure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team receives kudos for their victory. = 团队因他们的胜利而获得赞扬。
She gets kudos for her innovative ideas. = 她因其创新的想法而获得赞扬。

一般过去时:
He received kudos for his bravery. = 他因其勇敢而获得赞扬。
They were given kudos for their hard work. = 他们因辛勤工作而获得赞扬。

现在完成时:
The project has earned kudos from the critics. = 该项目已获得评论家的好评。
She has received much kudos for her contribution. = 她因其贡献而获得了许多赞扬。

过去完成时:
He had already received kudos for his earlier work. = 他之前的工作已经获得赞扬。
They had earned kudos for their efforts before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，他们已经因他们的努力而获得赞扬。

将来时:
She will receive kudos for her performance. = 她将因其表现而获得赞扬。
The company will likely earn kudos for its new product. = 这家公司可能会因其新产品而获得赞扬。

虚拟语气:
If she had finished the project, she would have received kudos. = 如果她完成了项目，她就会得到赞扬。
I wish I had received kudos for my efforts. = 我希望我的努力得到赞扬。

被动语态:
Kudos were given to the team for their success. = 团队因其成功而获得赞扬。
The award was given as a token of kudos. = 该奖项作为赞扬的象征而颁发。

疑问句:
Will she receive kudos for her presentation? = 她会因她的演讲而获得赞扬吗？
Did he get kudos for his achievement? = 他因其成就而获得赞扬了吗？

否定句:
He didn't receive kudos for his work. = 他的工作没有得到赞扬。
The team didn't get the kudos they deserved. = 团队没有得到应得的赞扬。
