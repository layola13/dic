haemorrhoids:/ˈhiːmərɔɪdz/| n. |痔疮|Swollen veins in the anus and rectum that can cause pain, itching, and bleeding.  Haemorrhoids are a common condition, affecting many people at some point in their lives.  They can be internal (inside the rectum) or external (outside the anus). Treatment options range from lifestyle changes to surgery.|The doctor diagnosed him with haemorrhoids and recommended some lifestyle changes.|piles, anal varices|healthy anal veins.|
