memoryless:/ˈmɛmərilɛs/| adj.|无记忆的；无记忆性的|lacking memory; not retaining or recalling past events or information.  The term often describes systems or processes where the future state depends only on the present state, not on past states.|例句：The Markov chain is a memoryless process.  = 马尔可夫链是一个无记忆性的过程。

例句： This algorithm is memoryless, meaning its output depends solely on the current input. = 这个算法是无记忆的，这意味着它的输出仅仅取决于当前的输入。

近义词：amnesiac, forgetful (in some contexts)

反义词：memorable, mindful, retentive
