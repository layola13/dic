macaroon:/ˌmækəˈruːn/| n. |马卡龙|A small, sweet meringue-based confection made with egg whites, sugar, and almond flour, often sandwiched together with buttercream or ganache.  Macarons are known for their delicate texture and vibrant colors.| Example: The bakery showcased a beautiful array of macarons in various pastel shades.  这家面包店展示了一系列色彩鲜艳的马卡龙。|近义词：meringue, cookie, confection|反义词：None
