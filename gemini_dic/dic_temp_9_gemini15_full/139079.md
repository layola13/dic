oaty:/ˈoʊti/| adj.|燕麦味的，像燕麦的|Having the taste or texture of oats.  The word evokes the flavor and consistency of oats, often used to describe foods containing oats or having a similar feel.|例句：The porridge had a lovely oaty flavour. 这粥有很棒的燕麦味。|例句：This granola bar has a wonderfully oaty texture. 这格兰诺拉麦片棒有很棒的燕麦口感。|近义词：oatmeal-like, oat-flavored|反义词：smooth, creamy
