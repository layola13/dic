misrouting:/ˌmɪsˈraʊtɪŋ/| n. |错误路由，路径错误|The act of sending something, especially data or communication signals, to the wrong destination.  This often occurs in computer networks or telecommunications systems due to errors in routing protocols or network configurations. |例句：The misrouting of the package resulted in a significant delay.  包裹的错误路由导致了严重的延误。

例句：The network experienced a period of misrouting, causing several websites to be inaccessible. 网络经历了一段时间的错误路由，导致多个网站无法访问。


近义词：malrouting, wrong routing, incorrect routing |反义词：correct routing, proper routing |名词
