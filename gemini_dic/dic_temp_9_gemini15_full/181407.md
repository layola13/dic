reitemize:/ˌriːˈaɪtəmaɪz/| vt.|重新列举，重新分条列举|To list or enumerate again, often with changes or additions.|The professor reitemized the syllabus to include the new assignments. 教授重新列举了教学大纲，以包含新的作业。|近义词：relist, reiterate, reenumerate|反义词：summarize, consolidate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reitemizes his expenses every month. 他每月都重新列举他的开支。
She reitemizes the grocery list after finding a better deal.  她在找到更好的交易后重新列举了购物清单。


一般过去时:
They reitemized the budget after reviewing the project costs.  他们在审查项目成本后重新列举了预算。
The committee reitemized the proposals before voting. 委员会在投票前重新列举了提案。


现在完成时:
She has reitemized her tax deductions several times this year.  她今年已经几次重新列举了她的税收抵扣。
He has reitemized the shipment contents for clarity. 他为了清晰起见重新列举了货件内容。


过去完成时:
They had reitemized the evidence before presenting their case.  他们在陈述案情之前已经重新列举了证据。
The lawyer had reitemized the witness statements to highlight inconsistencies. 律师已经重新列举了证词以突出不一致之处。


将来时:
We will reitemize the inventory after the stock arrives.  货物到达后，我们将重新列举库存。
She will reitemize the agenda before the meeting starts. 会议开始前，她将重新列举议程。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reitemize the entire report. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新列举整份报告。
It would be helpful if they reitemized the instructions more clearly. 如果他们更清晰地重新列举说明，将会很有帮助。


被动语态:
The report was reitemized to reflect the latest data. 报告被重新列举以反映最新数据。
The items were reitemized in alphabetical order. 项目按字母顺序重新列举。


疑问句:
Will the contract be reitemized before signing? 合同在签署前会被重新列举吗？
Have the expenses been reitemized for the audit? 为了审计，开支已经被重新列举了吗？


否定句:
He did not reitemize the accounts receivable. 他没有重新列举应收账款。
They did not reitemize the list of attendees. 他们没有重新列举与会人员名单。
