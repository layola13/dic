intraop:/ˈɪntrəɑːp/| adj.|手术中的，术中的|Relating to or occurring during a surgical operation.  The term combines "intra-" (within) and "-op" (short for operation).|例句：Intraoperative complications delayed the surgery. 手术中并发症延误了手术。

例句：Intraoperative monitoring is crucial for patient safety.  术中监测对患者安全至关重要。


近义词：surgical, operative, perioperative (broader term encompassing before, during, and after surgery)

反义词：postoperative (after surgery), preoperative (before surgery)

词性：形容词
