preconfess:/ˌpriːkənˈfɛs/| v.| 预先承认，预先坦白 | To confess beforehand; to admit something before it is discovered.  |例句：He preconfessed his involvement in the scheme to avoid further investigation. 他预先承认参与了该计划以避免进一步调查。


例句：She preconfessed her mistakes to her boss, hoping to mitigate the consequences. 她预先向老板承认了自己的错误，希望能减轻后果。


近义词：pre-admit, anticipate confession, forewarn


反义词：conceal, hide, deny


词性:动词
