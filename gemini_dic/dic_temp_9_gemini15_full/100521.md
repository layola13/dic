interviews:/ˈɪntərˌvjuːz/| n. |面试；采访 | Interviews are a crucial part of the hiring process. 面试是招聘过程中至关重要的部分。 |近义词：auditions, consultations |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He interviews candidates every Tuesday. 他每周二面试候选人。
The journalist interviews celebrities regularly.  这位记者定期采访名人。


一般过去时:
She interviewed for a marketing position last week. 她上周面试了一个市场营销职位。
They interviewed several witnesses to the accident.  他们采访了几位事故目击者。


现在完成时:
I have interviewed over fifty applicants this month. 这个月我已经面试了五十多名申请人。
The police have interviewed all the suspects. 警方已经讯问了所有嫌疑人。


过去完成时:
The company had interviewed all the candidates before making a decision. 公司在做出决定之前已经面试了所有候选人。
Before the trial, the lawyer had interviewed all the potential jurors. 在审判之前，律师已经面谈了所有潜在的陪审员。


将来时:
They will interview the new CEO next Monday. 他们下周一将面试新任首席执行官。
She will be interviewed for a television program tomorrow. 她明天将接受电视节目的采访。


虚拟语气:
If I were to interview for that job, I would be very nervous. 如果我去面试那份工作，我会非常紧张。
I wish I had interviewed more thoroughly before hiring him. 我真希望在雇佣他之前更仔细地面试过他。


被动语态:
The applicants were interviewed by a panel of experts. 申请人由专家小组进行面试。
The witnesses were interviewed by the police.  证人被警方问话。


疑问句:
When will the interviews be conducted? 面试将在什么时候进行？
Have all the candidates been interviewed yet? 所有候选人都已经面试过了吗？


否定句:
They did not interview any qualified candidates. 他们没有面试任何合格的候选人。
The company hasn't yet interviewed all the applicants. 公司还没有面试所有申请人。


PHRASE->
job interview: 工作面试
conduct an interview: 进行面试
interview process: 面试流程
press interview: 新闻采访
panel interview: 小组面试
