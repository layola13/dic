craters:/ˈkreɪtərz/| n.|陨石坑，火山口|A crater is a large, bowl-shaped cavity in the ground or on a celestial body, typically formed by an impact or explosion.|The moon is covered in craters formed by meteorite impacts. 月球表面布满了陨石撞击形成的陨石坑。|The volcano erupted, leaving a massive crater behind. 火山爆发，留下了一个巨大的火山口。|pit, cavity|mountain, plain
