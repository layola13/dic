pay-as-you-go: /ˈpeɪ əz juː ɡoʊ/ | adj. | 预付费的；按次付费的 |  A system where you pay for a service only when you use it, rather than paying in advance for a subscription or contract.  This term is often used for mobile phone plans, utilities, and other services. | 例句：Many mobile phone companies offer pay-as-you-go plans. 许多手机公司提供预付费套餐。  | 例句：The pay-as-you-go electricity meter allows you to control your energy consumption.  按次付费的电表让你能够控制你的能源消耗。 | 近义词：prepaid, on-demand | 反义词：subscription-based, contract-based
