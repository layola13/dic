shuttler:/ˈʃʌt.lə(r)/| n. |穿梭者；（尤指）羽毛球运动员|A person who travels regularly between two places, especially a person who plays badminton regularly.|例句：He's a dedicated shuttler, traveling between London and Paris for work. 他是一位尽职的穿梭者，往返于伦敦和巴黎之间工作。  例句：She's a talented shuttler and a promising athlete. 她是一位才华横溢的羽毛球运动员，也是一位很有前途的运动员。|近义词：commuter, traveler, badminton player|反义词：stay-at-home


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shuttle bus shuttles between the airport and the city center. =机场和市中心之间有穿梭巴士往返。
He shuttles between his office and his home every day. = 他每天往返于办公室和家之间。

一般过去时:
The family shuttled back and forth between their two homes. = 这家人在他们两个家之间来回奔波。
She shuttled the children to school and various activities. = 她把孩子们送去学校和参加各种活动。


现在完成时:
They have shuttled goods across the country for over 20 years. = 他们已经跨国运输货物20多年了。
She has shuttled between different departments in the company. = 她一直在公司不同的部门之间穿梭。


过去完成时:
He had shuttled between several jobs before finding his current position. = 在找到现在的工作之前，他曾做过几份工作。
They had shuttled the injured to the hospital before the ambulance arrived. = 救护车到达之前，他们已经把伤员送到了医院。


将来时:
We will shuttle the delegates to the conference center. = 我们将把代表们送到会议中心。
The company will shuttle its employees to the new office building. = 公司将把员工送往新的办公大楼。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would shuttle my children to their activities more easily. = 如果我有车，我会更容易地把我的孩子们送到他们的活动场所。
I wish I could shuttle between my two favourite cities more often. = 我希望我能更频繁地在两个最喜欢的城市之间穿梭。


被动语态:
The passengers were shuttled to their hotels by bus. = 乘客们被巴士送到了他们的酒店。
The documents were shuttled between the two offices electronically. = 文件通过电子方式在两个办公室之间传递。


疑问句:
Does the hotel shuttle run regularly to the airport? = 酒店的穿梭巴士定期往返机场吗？
How often do you shuttle between your home and your office? = 你多久往返于家和办公室之间一次？


否定句:
The company doesn't shuttle its employees to the office anymore. = 公司不再把员工送到办公室了。
He didn't shuttle between cities as much as he used to. = 他不像以前那样经常在城市之间穿梭了。
