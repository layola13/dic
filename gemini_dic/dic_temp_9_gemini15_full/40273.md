continuant:/ˈkɒntɪnjuənt/ /ˈkɑːntɪnjuənt/ | adj. |持续的，连续的|  A continuant sound is one that can be continued without interruption.  It contrasts with a stop consonant, which is produced by stopping the airflow completely and then releasing it.  Continuant sounds include fricatives (like /s/, /f/, /θ/), affricates (like /tʃ/, /dʒ/), approximants (like /l/, /r/, /j/, /w/) and vowels. | The humming sound is a continuant.  持续的嗡嗡声。|  continuous, sustained | stop consonant, transient. |形容词
