outwandering: /ˌaʊtˈwɒndərɪŋ/ | v. | 漫游，四处游荡 |  The act of wandering far and wide; going beyond usual limits in exploration or travel.  It implies a more extensive and purposeful wandering than simply "wandering". | 例句：The explorers spent months outwandering in the vast wilderness.  探险家们在广阔的荒野中花了几个月的时间四处游荡。  The children were outwandering in the woods, far from the house. 孩子们在树林里四处游荡，远离了家。 | 近义词：roaming, rambling, ranging, exploring | 反义词：staying put, remaining stationary | 动词
