/riːdiŋ/| n. |再读，复习 |The act of reading something again; a second reading.  This word is not commonly used and may be considered a misspelling or an uncommon variant of "rereading".  Example:  She gave the text a readding before submitting it. 她提交之前又读了一遍文本。(This sentence is more naturally expressed as "She reread the text...")|近义词：rereading, review |反义词：first reading, initial reading |名词


USAGE->
This word lacks common usage and doesn't typically appear in different grammatical tenses.  The examples below use the more common "rereading":

一般现在时:
She is rereading the chapter. = 她正在重读这一章。
He rereads the email every day. = 他每天都重读这封邮件。

一般过去时:
I reread the book last night. = 我昨晚又读了一遍这本书。
They reread the instructions carefully. = 他们仔细地又读了一遍说明书。

现在完成时:
I have reread that article several times. = 我已经重读那篇文章好几次了。
She has reread the contract before signing. = 她签字前已经重读了合同。

过去完成时:
He had reread the letter before he replied. = 他回复信件之前已经重读了信。
They had reread the script before the performance. = 他们演出前已经重读了剧本。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I had reread the instructions more carefully. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地重读了说明书。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
