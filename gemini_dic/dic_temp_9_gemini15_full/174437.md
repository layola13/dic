quilting:/ˈkwɪltɪŋ/| n. v.|缝被;绗缝;被子;棉被|The process of sewing together layers of fabric, typically with a layer of batting in between, to create a warm and often decorative textile.  It can also refer to the textile itself.|例句：She enjoys quilting as a hobby. 她喜欢绗缝作为一种爱好。


The intricate quilting on the antique bedspread was stunning. 这床古董床罩上复杂的绗缝令人惊叹。|近义词：patchwork, stitching|反义词:None
