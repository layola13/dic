powderers: /ˈpaʊdərərz/ | n. |  炮制者；（尤指）制粉者 |  A person or thing that powders something, especially one that grinds or prepares substances into a powder.  This word often refers to those who prepare ingredients, such as spices or medicines, into powdered form.  | 例句：The ancient powderers meticulously ground the herbs into a fine powder for the potion. 古代的制粉者小心翼翼地将草药磨成细粉，用于配制药剂。  The confectioners are also powderers, as they powder sugar for icing. 糖果师也是制粉者，因为他们将糖粉用于糖霜。 | 近义词：grinders, pulverizers, millers | 反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑与“lumpers” (成块者)相对比 |  n.
