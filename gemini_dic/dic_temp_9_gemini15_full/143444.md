/ˌaʊtˈflaɪɪŋ/| v. |超越，胜过；飞越|To fly faster or further than (someone or something).  This word describes the act of exceeding another in flight, either in speed or distance.|例句：The new jet outflew all previous models. = 新型喷气式飞机的速度超过了所有以前的型号。

The experienced pilot easily outflew the novice. = 经验丰富的飞行员轻松地超过了新手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词：underperform, lag behind|动词
