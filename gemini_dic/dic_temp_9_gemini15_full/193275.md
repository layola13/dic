seek:/siːk/ | vt. | 寻找；寻求；追求 | To attempt to find (something); to try to obtain or achieve (something).  | 例句：They are seeking justice. 他们在寻求正义。
They are seeking a solution to the problem. 他们在寻求解决问题的方案。 |近义词：search for, pursue, hunt for, strive for | 反义词：ignore, neglect, abandon | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I seek knowledge every day. = 我每天都在寻求知识。
She seeks happiness in her work. = 她从工作中寻求快乐。

一般过去时:
He sought help from his friends. = 他向朋友们寻求帮助。
They sought refuge from the storm. = 他们躲避暴风雨寻求庇护。

现在完成时:
I have sought answers to many questions. = 我已经寻找过许多问题的答案。
She has sought her fortune in a foreign land. = 她一直在异国他乡寻求财富。

过去完成时:
He had sought advice from his mentor before making the decision. = 在做决定之前，他已经向他的导师寻求过建议。
They had sought employment for months before finding a job. = 他们找工作找了几个月才找到。

将来时:
I will seek employment upon graduation. = 我毕业后会去找工作。
She will seek a better life for her children. = 她将为她的孩子寻求更好的生活。

虚拟语气:
If I sought help earlier, I wouldn't be in this situation. = 如果我早点寻求帮助，我就不会陷入这种境地。
I wish I had sought medical attention sooner. = 我真希望我早点就医。

被动语态:
Help was sought from the local authorities. = 向当地政府寻求了帮助。
A solution is being sought to the energy crisis. = 正在寻求解决能源危机的方案。

疑问句:
What are you seeking? = 你在寻找什么？
What did they seek to achieve? = 他们试图取得什么成就？

否定句:
He did not seek revenge. = 他没有寻求报复。
They did not seek permission before entering. = 他们在进入之前没有寻求许可。


PHRASE->
seek out = 找出；搜寻
seek after = 追求；追寻
seek to = 试图；企图
in seek of = 寻找；寻求
seek and find = 寻找并找到
