globin:/ˈɡloʊbɪn/ | n. | 球蛋白 | A family of heme-containing globular proteins found in red blood cells and muscle tissue, responsible for transporting oxygen (hemoglobin) or storing oxygen (myoglobin).  | Example: Hemoglobin, the protein that carries oxygen in the blood, is a type of globin.  血红蛋白，一种在血液中携带氧气的蛋白质，是一种球蛋白。 | 近义词：hemoprotein | 反义词：无
