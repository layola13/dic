encrypt:/ɪnˈkrɪpt/ | vt. |加密，编码 |To convert (information or data) into a code, especially to prevent unauthorized access.  |The company encrypts all sensitive data before transmission. 公司在传输所有敏感数据之前都会对其进行加密。 | 近义词：encode, cipher, scramble | 反义词：decrypt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encrypts his emails every day. 他每天都加密他的电子邮件。
We encrypt sensitive data before sending it. 我们在发送敏感数据之前对其进行加密。


一般过去时:
She encrypted the file before sending it. 她发送文件之前对其进行了加密。
They encrypted the message to protect it from eavesdroppers. 他们加密了信息以保护其免受窃听。


现在完成时:
I have encrypted all my important documents. 我已经加密了我所有重要的文件。
They have encrypted the data to ensure its security. 他们已经加密了数据以确保其安全。


过去完成时:
He had encrypted the hard drive before the hackers accessed it. 黑客访问硬盘之前，他已经将其加密了。
She had encrypted the files, so the thief couldn't read them. 她已经加密了文件，所以窃贼无法读取它们。


将来时:
We will encrypt the data before uploading it to the cloud. 我们将在将数据上传到云端之前对其进行加密。
They will encrypt all communications using a new protocol. 他们将使用新的协议加密所有通信。


虚拟语气:
It would be safer if we encrypted all our communications. 如果我们加密所有通信，将会更安全。
If I had known how to encrypt, I wouldn't have lost my data. 如果我知道如何加密，我就不会丢失我的数据了。


被动语态:
The data is encrypted using a strong algorithm. 数据使用强算法进行加密。
All emails are encrypted automatically. 所有电子邮件都会自动加密。


疑问句:
Is the data encrypted? 数据是否已加密？
Does the software encrypt files automatically? 该软件是否自动加密文件？


否定句:
The message was not encrypted. 信息未加密。
He didn't encrypt the file before sharing it. 他在分享文件之前没有对其进行加密。


PHRASE->
encrypt data: 加密数据
encrypt a file: 加密文件
encrypt communication: 加密通信
encrypt a message: 加密信息
encrypt with a key: 用密钥加密
