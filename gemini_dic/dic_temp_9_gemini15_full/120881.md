misbrew:/ˈmɪsbruː/| v.|酿造失败；冲泡失败；弄糟（饮料）|To prepare (a drink, especially beer) badly or incorrectly.  This word is a relatively informal neologism, a combination of "mis-" (wrongly) and "brew" (to prepare a drink).|例句：He misbrewed the coffee, making it too bitter. 他把咖啡冲泡坏了，冲得太苦了。  The brewer misbrewed the batch, resulting in a sour taste. 酿酒师酿造失败，导致啤酒有酸味。|近义词：spoil, ruin, botch|反义词：brew perfectly, masterfully brew
