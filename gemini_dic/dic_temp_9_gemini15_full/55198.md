dissepiment:/dɪˈsɛpɪmənt/| n.|隔膜；隔板|A dissepiment is a partition, especially a wall or membrane separating cavities or locules, as in a fruit or ovary.|例句：The ovary of this plant has many dissepiments, creating numerous compartments for seeds.  这个植物的子房有很多隔膜，为种子创造了许多隔间。

The dissepiments in the fruit helped to protect the seeds from damage. 果实中的隔膜有助于保护种子免受损害。|近义词：partition, septum, diaphragm|反义词：opening, aperture|词性 n.
