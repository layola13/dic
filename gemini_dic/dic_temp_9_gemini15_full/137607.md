nontronite:/ˈnɒntrɒnaɪt/ | n. | 诺特罗石 | A hydrous phyllosilicate mineral of the smectite group, typically greenish-grey in colour and occurring in clay deposits.  It is a type of swelling clay, meaning its structure can expand when water is absorbed. | Example: The soil sample contained a significant amount of nontronite, contributing to its high water retention capacity.  土壤样本含有大量的诺特罗石，这使其具有很高的保水能力。| 近义词：smectite, montmorillonite (部分情况下) | 反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与非膨胀性粘土矿物对比，例如高岭石kaolinite |  名词
