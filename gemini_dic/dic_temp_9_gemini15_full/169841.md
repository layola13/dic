proselytised:/ˈprɒsəlaɪzd/ | /ˈprɑːsəlaɪzd/| v. | 劝诱…改宗；积极宣传（信仰） | To try to persuade someone to convert to one's religion or belief.  | The missionaries proselytised to the villagers, hoping to convert them to Christianity. 传教士向村民传教，希望能将他们改信基督教。 | convert, evangelize, preach |  persecute, suppress | 动词
