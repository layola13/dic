got along:/ɡɑːt əˈlɒŋ/ | v. |相处融洽，进展顺利|To have a good relationship; to progress satisfactorily. |例句：We got along well despite our differences. 我们尽管有分歧，但相处得很好。|例句：The project is getting along nicely.  项目进展顺利。|近义词：get on, agree, proceed |反义词：clash, disagree, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They get along well. = 他们相处融洽。
We get along fine. = 我们相处得很好。

一般过去时:
They got along well during the trip. = 他们在旅行期间相处融洽。
The negotiations got along smoothly. = 谈判进展顺利。

现在完成时:
We've gotten along well for years. = 我们多年来相处融洽。
The construction has gotten along faster than expected. = 建设进展比预期快。

过去完成时:
They had gotten along well before the argument. = 争吵之前，他们相处融洽。
The project had gotten along well until the funding was cut. = 直到资金被削减之前，项目进展顺利。

将来时:
We will get along fine, I'm sure. = 我确信我们会相处融洽。
The meeting will get along efficiently. = 会议将有效率地进行。

虚拟语气:
If they got along better, there would be less conflict. = 如果他们相处更好，冲突就会减少。
I wish we had gotten along better in the past. = 我希望我们过去相处得更好。

被动语态: (This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice)

疑问句:
Do you get along with your coworkers? = 你和你的同事相处得好吗？
How are they getting along? = 他们相处得如何？

否定句:
They didn't get along at all. = 他们根本相处不好。
The project isn't getting along as planned. = 项目进展不如计划顺利。
