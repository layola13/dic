pyaemic:/paɪˈiːmɪk/| adj.|败血性的，败血症的| Relating to or characterized by pyaemia (a severe form of septicemia characterized by the presence of pus-forming bacteria in the blood).  It describes a condition where pus-forming bacteria spread through the bloodstream, causing multiple abscesses in various organs.| Example: The patient presented with pyaemic symptoms, including high fever and multiple abscesses.  患者出现了败血症症状，包括高烧和多个脓肿。|近义词：septic, septicaemic|反义词：aseptic, sterile|
