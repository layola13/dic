cantic:/ˈkæntɪk/| n.|圣歌；赞美诗|A cantic is a sacred song or hymn, especially one sung in a religious service.|例句：The choir sang a beautiful cantic during the mass.  唱诗班在弥撒中唱了一首优美的圣歌。|近义词：hymn, anthem, psalm|反义词：profane song, secular music


USAGE->

Part of a noun phrase:
