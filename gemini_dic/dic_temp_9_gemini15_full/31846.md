cheeping:/tʃiːpɪŋ/| v.|唧唧叫声；吱吱叫|The sound of small birds making a series of short, high-pitched sounds.|例句：I woke up to the cheeping of birds outside my window. 我被窗外鸟儿的唧唧喳喳声吵醒了。  例句：The baby birds were cheeping loudly for food. 小鸟们大声地唧唧叫着要食物。|近义词：chirping, twittering, peeping|反义词：roaring, booming

The birds are cheeping merrily in the trees. = 鸟儿们在树上快乐地唧唧喳喳叫着。
The crickets are cheeping throughout the night. = 蟋蟀整夜都在唧唧叫。
