fearfuler:/ˈfɪrfʊlər/ | adj. | 更可怕的，更令人恐惧的 | More fearful; more inspiring fear.  This word is a comparative form of "fearful," and its usage is somewhat uncommon in modern English. It is generally considered less elegant than using "more fearful." |  The storm grew fearfuler by the minute.  暴风雨每分钟都变得更可怕。 The deeper we went into the forest, the fearfuler the silence became. 我们越深入森林，寂静就越令人恐惧。 | more frightening, terrifying, dreadful | fearless, safe, calm | 形容词
