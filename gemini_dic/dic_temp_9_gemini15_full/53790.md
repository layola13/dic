discomposing: /ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊzɪŋ/ | v. |使不安；使不适；使不协调 |  To disturb the composure or tranquility of; unsettle; to cause to lose its composure or self-possession; to disturb the harmony or order of; to decompose. |


例句：
The news discomposing her greatly. = 这个消息让她非常不安。

The unexpected guest discomposing the carefully planned party. = 不速之客扰乱了精心策划的聚会。


近义词：disturb, upset, unsettle, discompose


反义词：calm, soothe, reassure, compose


词性：动词
