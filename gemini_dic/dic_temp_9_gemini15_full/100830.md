introductor:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌktər/| n. |介绍者，引介者 |A person who introduces someone or something.  An introductor is someone who formally presents a person or thing to others.  |例句：The introductor spoke briefly before calling the main speaker to the stage. 介绍人在请主要发言人上台之前简短地发言了。|例句：He acted as the introductor for the new product launch. 他担任了新产品发布会的介绍人。|近义词：introducer, presenter |反义词：concluder
