letting-out: /ˈlɛtɪŋ aʊt/ | v. | 出租；放出；泄露；使变松 |

英文解析:  Letting-out refers to the action of renting out a property, releasing something from confinement, revealing a secret, or loosening something tight.  The specific meaning depends heavily on context.

例句:

They are letting out their spare room to students. = 他们把空余的房间出租给学生。

The prisoner was letting out a strangled cry. = 囚犯发出一声嘶哑的喊叫。


近义词: renting out (出租), releasing (释放), disclosing (泄露), loosening (放松)

反义词: renting in (租入), keeping in (关着), concealing (隐藏), tightening (收紧)


USAGE->

The landlord lets out several apartments. = 房东出租几套公寓。
She lets out a sigh of relief. = 她舒了一口气。

He let out his dog in the park. = 他在公园里放了他的狗。

现在完成时:

将来时:

被动语态:
