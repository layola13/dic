phrenologist:/frɪˈnɒlədʒɪst/|/ˈfrɛnɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |颅相学家|A phrenologist is a person who practices phrenology, the now-discredited study of the shape and size of the skull as a supposed indication of character and mental abilities.|例句：The phrenologist examined the bumps on the patient's head to determine his personality traits. 颅相学家检查病人头上的隆起以确定他的性格特征。|近义词：cranioscopist (较少使用)|反义词：N/A
