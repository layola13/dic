klipfish:/ˈklɪpfɪʃ/| n. |盐渍鳕鱼|Salt-cured cod, typically dried and sometimes smoked. Klipfish is a traditional food in many parts of the world, particularly in the Caribbean and some parts of Europe.  It's known for its firm texture and salty flavor.  |The recipe calls for klipfish, which adds a unique savory depth.|近义词：salt cod, dried cod|反义词：fresh cod
