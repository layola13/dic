quasi-diplomatic:/ˈkwɑːzaɪˌdɪpləˈmætɪk/ | adj. |准外交的；半外交的 |  Relating to or having the characteristics of diplomacy but not officially or fully diplomatic.  It suggests actions or interactions that resemble diplomatic efforts but lack the formal status or authority of official diplomatic channels.  | Example: The meeting between the two leaders, though informal, was considered a quasi-diplomatic initiative to ease tensions.  这次两位领导人的会面虽然非正式，但被认为是缓和紧张局势的准外交举措。|近义词：semi-diplomatic, unofficial diplomatic |反义词：fully diplomatic, official diplomatic |
