mellic:/ˈmɛlɪk/| adj.|蜜的；甜美的；悦耳的|Sweet, honey-like; melodious.  The word evokes a sense of sweetness and pleasantness, often associated with honey or a honey-like quality of sound.|例句：The mellic tones of the singer's voice captivated the audience. 歌唱家甜美的嗓音迷住了观众。 The dessert had a mellic sweetness. 甜点有着蜂蜜般的甜味。|近义词：honeyed, sweet, melodious, dulcet|反义词：harsh, discordant, acrid
