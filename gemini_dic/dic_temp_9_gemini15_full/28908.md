carriage:/ˈkærɪdʒ/| n.|四轮马车；车厢；运费；<古>运送|A carriage is a wheeled vehicle, typically horse-drawn, for carrying passengers or goods.  It can also refer to a compartment in a train, or the cost of transporting goods.|例句：They rode in a horse-drawn carriage. 他们乘坐一辆马车。

The train carriage was crowded. 火车车厢很拥挤。|近义词：cart, wagon, coach, compartment|反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "carriage" originates from the Old French word "cariage," meaning "load, burden," which itself comes from the Latin word "carrus," meaning "wagon" or "cart."  The evolution reflects the historical use of carriages for transporting goods and people.
