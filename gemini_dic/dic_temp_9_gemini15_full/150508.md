pantomime:/ˈpænəmaɪm/|/ˈpæntəmaɪm/| n. v.|哑剧；模仿；做哑剧表演|A pantomime is a theatrical entertainment, mainly for children, which involves music, topical jokes, and slapstick comedy, and traditionally includes a fairy tale or similar narrative.  It often features performers miming actions and exaggerated gestures. |The children enjoyed the Christmas pantomime.孩子们很喜欢圣诞哑剧。He expertly pantomimed the scene. 他熟练地模仿了那个场景。|mime, charade, skit|drama, play|n.
