effectively:/ɪˈfektɪvli/ | adv. |有效地；实际上 |In an effective way; in a way that produces a desired result.  The new marketing campaign effectively increased sales. 新的营销活动有效地增加了销售额。|近义词：efficiently, successfully, powerfully |反义词：ineffectively, unsuccessfully, weakly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He effectively manages his time. = 他有效地管理他的时间。
She effectively communicates her ideas. = 她有效地传达了她的想法。


一般过去时:
The teacher effectively explained the concept. = 老师有效地解释了这个概念。
The medicine effectively relieved his pain. = 药物有效地缓解了他的疼痛。


现在完成时:
They have effectively solved the problem. = 他们已经有效地解决了这个问题。
We have effectively completed the project. = 我们已经有效地完成了项目。


过去完成时:
She had effectively prepared for the exam. = 她已经有效地准备了考试。
He had effectively finished his work before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经有效地完成了他的工作。


将来时:
We will effectively utilize the new technology. = 我们将有效地利用这项新技术。
They will effectively address the concerns. = 他们将有效地解决这些担忧。


虚拟语气:
If we worked effectively, we would finish on time. = 如果我们有效地工作，我们就能按时完成。
If she had studied effectively, she would have passed the test. = 如果她有效地学习了，她就会通过考试。


被动语态:
The problem was effectively addressed by the team. = 这个问题由团队有效地解决了。
The message was effectively communicated to the audience. = 信息有效地传达给了观众。


疑问句:
Did he effectively present his argument? = 他有效地陈述了他的论点了吗？
Does this method effectively achieve the goal? = 这种方法能有效地达到目标吗？


否定句:
The plan did not effectively address the issue. = 该计划未能有效地解决这个问题。
He didn't effectively manage his resources. = 他没有有效地管理他的资源。
