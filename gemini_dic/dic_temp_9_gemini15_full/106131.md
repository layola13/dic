knits:/nɪts/ | v. | 编织；缝合；(使)皱缩 | To interloop yarn or thread to create fabric.  Knitting can be done by hand or machine. | 例句：She knits sweaters for her grandchildren. = 她为她的孙子孙女织毛衣。

She knitted a beautiful scarf. = 她织了一条漂亮的围巾。


近义词：weave, crochet, stitch | 反义词：unravel, separate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She knits every evening. = 她每天晚上都织毛衣。
He knits a complex pattern. = 他织着一个复杂的图案。

一般过去时:
My grandmother knitted me a blanket. = 我奶奶为我织了一条毯子。
They knitted all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在织毛线。

现在完成时:
I have knitted three scarves this month. = 这个月我已经织了三条围巾。
She has knitted for over fifty years. = 她已经织了五十年多了。

过去完成时:
She had knitted the sweater before the baby was born. = 在婴儿出生之前，她已经织好了毛衣。
They had knitted many items for the charity shop. = 他们已经为慈善商店织了很多东西。

将来时:
I will knit a new hat for winter. = 我将为冬天织一顶新帽子。
She will knit a dress for her daughter's wedding. = 她将为女儿的婚礼织一件礼服。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would knit a quilt. = 如果我有更多时间，我会织一床被子。
I wish I could knit as well as my aunt. = 我希望我能像我阿姨一样擅长织毛衣。

被动语态:
The sweater was knitted by hand. = 这件毛衣是手工编织的。
The scarves are being knitted for a charity event. = 这些围巾正在为慈善活动而编织。

疑问句:
Do you knit? = 你会织毛衣吗？
Does she knit often? = 她经常织毛衣吗？

否定句:
He doesn't knit. = 他不会织毛衣。
They didn't knit that sweater. = 他们没有织那件毛衣。


PHRASE->
knit together:  团结一致 (e.g., The team knitted together to win the game. 团队团结一致赢得了比赛。)
knit one's brows: 皱眉 (e.g., He knitted his brows in concentration. 他聚精会神地皱起了眉头。)
knitting needle: 编织针 (e.g., She used bamboo knitting needles. 她用的是竹编织针。)
tight knit:  紧密的；关系密切的 (e.g., They are a tight-knit community. 他们是一个紧密的社区。)
knitting pattern: 编织图案 (e.g., I'm following a new knitting pattern. 我正在按照一个新的编织图案进行编织。)
