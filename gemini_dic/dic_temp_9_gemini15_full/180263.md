reframe:/ˌriːˈfreɪm/| vt.|重新构建，重新阐述，改变框架|To present or consider something in a new or different way.  To change the frame or structure of something. |例句：Let's reframe the discussion to focus on solutions.  让我们重新构建讨论，专注于解决方案。

例句：She reframed the painting to fit the new wall. 她重新装裱画作以适应新的墙壁。


近义词：rephrase, reformulate, reinterpret, restructure|反义词：maintain, preserve, keep,  leave unchanged|动词
