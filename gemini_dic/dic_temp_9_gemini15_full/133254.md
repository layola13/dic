nonexertion:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɜːrʃən/| n. |不费力；不努力；缺乏努力|The state or quality of not exerting oneself; lack of effort.|例句：His success was attributed to nonexertion of his talent.  他的成功归因于没有发挥他的才能。

例句：The project failed due to a period of prolonged nonexertion from the team. 项目失败是因为团队长期缺乏努力。


近义词：inactivity, idleness, sloth, laziness|反义词：exertion, effort, diligence, industry|词性:名词
