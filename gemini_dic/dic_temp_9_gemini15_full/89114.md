hombre:/ˈɑːmbrɛ/| n.|男人，家伙，人|A man, especially a Spanish-speaking man.  The word is often used informally and can sometimes carry connotations of machismo or a certain type of masculinity. |例句：He's a real hombre. 他是个真男人。|例句：That hombre over there looks suspicious.  那边那个家伙看起来很可疑。|近义词：man, fellow, guy, dude|反义词：woman
