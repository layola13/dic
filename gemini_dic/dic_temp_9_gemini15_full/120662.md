/mɪsˈækt/| v.|行为不当，犯错|To act wrongly or improperly; to make a mistake in one's actions.|例句：He misacted in his dealings with the client, leading to a loss of business. 他在与客户打交道时行为不当，导致业务损失。

例句：The actor misacted several lines during the crucial scene, ruining the dramatic effect.  这位演员在关键场景中念错了几行台词，破坏了戏剧效果。


近义词：err, blunder, misbehave,  offend,  fault


反义词：act correctly, behave properly, do right


词性：动词
