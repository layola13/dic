grate:/ɡreɪt/ | vt. n.|磨碎；感激；摩擦声|To rub against a surface with a scraping sound; to express gratitude; to reduce to small particles by rubbing.|例句：He grated the cheese onto the pasta. 他把奶酪磨碎撒在意大利面上。|近义词：grind, shred, rasp, appreciate|反义词：smooth, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grates the cheese every morning. 他每天早上都磨碎奶酪。
She grates her knuckles against the rough wood. 她的指关节摩擦着粗糙的木头。


一般过去时:
I grated the carrots for the salad. 我把胡萝卜磨碎了做沙拉。
The old door grated as it opened. 旧门打开时发出摩擦声。


现在完成时:
He has grated the ginger for the stir-fry. 他已经把姜磨碎了准备做炒菜。
She has grated her knee on the pavement. 她的膝盖蹭破了路面。


过去完成时:
I had grated the cheese before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前我已经把奶酪磨碎了。
The rusty hinges had grated loudly all night. 生锈的铰链整夜发出刺耳的摩擦声。


将来时:
I will grate the potatoes for the hash browns. 我将把土豆磨碎做土豆饼。
The wheels will grate against the rocks if you're not careful. 如果你不小心，轮子会摩擦到石头上。


虚拟语气:
If I had a grater, I would grate the vegetables myself. 如果我有一个擦菜器，我会自己把蔬菜磨碎。
I wish I hadn't grated my elbow on that rough wall. 我真希望我的胳膊肘没有蹭到那堵粗糙的墙上。


被动语态:
The cheese was grated finely. 奶酪被细细地磨碎了。
The metal was grated by the rough surface. 金属被粗糙的表面磨损了。


疑问句:
Have you grated the nutmeg yet? 你磨碎肉豆蔻了吗？
Did the brakes grate when you applied them? 你踩刹车时刹车片有摩擦声吗？


否定句:
He didn't grate the carrots; he chopped them. 他没有磨碎胡萝卜；他把它们切碎了。
The wheels didn't grate on the smooth road. 轮子在平坦的道路上没有发出摩擦声。
