copywriter:/ˈkɒpiˌraɪtər/ /ˈkɑːpiˌraɪtɚ/ | n. | 广告文案撰写人，文案策划 | A person who writes the text of advertisements or publicity material. |  She's a talented copywriter who can craft compelling ad campaigns. 她是一位才华横溢的文案撰写人，能够策划引人入胜的广告活动。 |  advertiser, writer, wordsmith |  (no direct antonym)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The copywriter writes compelling headlines. = 文案撰写人撰写引人注目的标题。
He creates engaging content for social media. = 他为社交媒体创作引人入胜的内容。

一般过去时:
The copywriter wrote a successful campaign last year. = 文案撰写人去年创作了一个成功的广告活动。
She revised the script based on client feedback. = 她根据客户反馈修改了脚本。

现在完成时:
The copywriter has written numerous award-winning ads. = 该文案撰写人撰写了许多屡获殊荣的广告。
He has completed the draft of the brochure. = 他已经完成了宣传册的草稿。
