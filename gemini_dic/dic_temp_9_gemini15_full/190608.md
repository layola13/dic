saturates: /ˈsætʃəreɪts/ | vt. | 使饱和；浸透；充满 | To fill something completely so that no more can be added.  |例句：The sponge saturates the water. 海绵吸饱了水。

The heavy rain saturated the ground. 大雨使地面湿透了。|近义词：soak, impregnate, permeate |反义词：dehydrate, dry


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soil saturates with water after a heavy rain. = 大雨过后土壤饱和了水分。
He saturates the market with his new product. = 他用他的新产品充斥市场。


一般过去时:
The fabric saturated quickly with the dye. = 布料很快就被染料染透了。
The news saturated the media for days. = 这条新闻占据了媒体几天的报道。


现在完成时:
The market has been saturated with similar products. = 市场已经被类似的产品充斥了。
The sponge has saturated completely; no more water can be absorbed. = 海绵已经完全饱和了；无法再吸收更多水分。


过去完成时:
The ground had already saturated before the second downpour. = 在第二次暴雨来临之前，地面已经饱和了。
The company had saturated the local market before expanding nationally. = 公司在扩展到全国市场之前已经饱和了当地市场。


将来时:
The new advertising campaign will saturate the airwaves next month. = 下个月新的广告宣传活动将充斥广播电波。
The heavy rain is expected to saturate the riverbanks, leading to flooding. = 预计大雨将使河岸饱和，导致洪水。


虚拟语气:
If we saturated the market, we would dominate the industry. = 如果我们使市场饱和，我们将主导行业。
I wish I had saturated the market earlier; I would have made more profits. = 我希望我早点让市场饱和；我会赚更多钱。


被动语态:
The market was saturated by the influx of cheap imports. = 市场被大量廉价进口商品充斥。
The fabric is saturated with dye in the process. = 布料在这个过程中被染料浸透。


疑问句:
Has the market been saturated with this type of technology? = 市场已经被这种技术饱和了吗？
Is the soil saturated enough for planting? = 土壤是否足够饱和以适合种植？


否定句:
The solution did not saturate the mixture completely. = 溶液并没有完全使混合物饱和。
The market isn't yet saturated with electric vehicles. = 市场还没有被电动汽车饱和。


PHRASE->
market saturation 市场饱和
saturated fat 饱和脂肪
saturated solution 饱和溶液
colour saturation 颜色饱和度
saturated steam 饱和蒸汽


词源:
From Latin *saturare ("to fill, stuff, cram"), from satur ("full").  The meaning evolved from a sense of filling to the point of no more capacity.
