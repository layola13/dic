dud's:/dʌdz/| n.|次品，废物，失败之作|A dud is something that fails to function or perform as expected; a failure.|例句：The firework was a dud and didn't go off. 这支烟花是次品，没有爆炸。  The experiment was a complete dud.  实验完全失败了。|近义词：failure, flop, bomb|反义词：success, triumph
