roamed:/roʊmd/ | v. | 漫游；游荡；闲逛 | to travel or walk about over a wide area without a definite destination | The lion roamed the savanna.  狮子在热带草原上漫游。| The children roamed the streets after school.放学后孩子们在街上闲逛。| wandered, rambled, strolled | confined, stayed, remained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He roams the countryside every weekend. 他每个周末都在乡下漫游。
They roam the streets at night. 他们晚上在街上游荡。

一般过去时:
We roamed the forest for hours. 我们在森林里漫步了几个小时。
She roamed the streets looking for her lost dog. 她四处寻找她丢失的狗。

现在完成时:
They have roamed the world for many years. 他们已经环游世界多年了。
The nomads have roamed these lands for centuries. 游牧民族几个世纪以来一直在这些土地上游牧。


过去完成时:
Before settling down, they had roamed the plains for years. 在定居之前，他们在平原上游荡了很多年。
After he had roamed the city for days, he finally found a hotel. 在城里游荡了几天后，他终于找到了一家旅馆。

将来时:
We will roam the beaches tomorrow. 我们明天将在海滩上漫步。
They will roam the streets until they find a place to stay. 他们会在街上游荡，直到找到住处。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would roam the countryside freely. 如果我有车，我会自由地漫游乡村。
I wish I could roam the world without a care in the world. 我希望我能无忧无虑地环游世界。


被动语态:
The country was roamed by many tourists last summer. 去年夏天，许多游客游览了这个国家。(此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The land has been roamed by wild animals for ages. 这片土地上世代居住着野生动物。(此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Did they roam the mountains? 他们漫游了山区吗？
Where do the elephants roam? 大象在哪里游荡？


否定句:
The sheep did not roam freely in the field. 绵羊没有在田野里自由地游荡。
He doesn't roam the streets anymore. 他不再在街上游荡了。


PHRASE->

roam at will 随意游荡
