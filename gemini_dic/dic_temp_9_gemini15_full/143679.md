outlawed:/ˈaʊtlɔːd/| adj. |被宣布为非法的；被禁止的| declared illegal; prohibited. | Example:  Gambling was outlawed in that state.  那个州赌博被禁止了。  |近义词：forbidden, illegal, prohibited, banned |反义词：legal, permitted, allowed |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government outlaws the production of certain drugs. = 政府禁止生产某些毒品。
This activity is outlawed in many countries. =这项活动在许多国家是被禁止的。


一般过去时:
They outlawed the sale of alcohol during Prohibition. =他们在禁酒时期禁止了酒的销售。
The practice was outlawed many years ago. =这项活动在很多年前就被禁止了。


现在完成时:
Many countries have outlawed the use of certain pesticides. =许多国家已经禁止使用某些杀虫剂。
The substance has been outlawed since 1980. =这种物质从1980年以来就被禁止了。


过去完成时:
They had outlawed the import of those goods before the crisis. =在危机爆发前，他们已经禁止了这些商品的进口。
The law had been outlawed long before I was born. =那项法律在我出生很久以前就被废除了。


将来时:
They will outlaw the use of plastic bags next year. =他们明年将禁止使用塑料袋。
The new legislation will outlaw smoking in public places. =新法案将禁止在公共场所吸烟。


虚拟语气:
If they outlawed that product, the company would lose profits. =如果他们禁止生产那个产品，公司将会损失利润。
I wish they outlawed those harmful chemicals. =我希望他们能禁止那些有害的化学物质。


被动语态:
The practice was outlawed by the government. =这项活动被政府禁止了。
The new rule will be outlawed by the next session of parliament. =新规将在下一届议会中被废除。


疑问句:
Has the substance been outlawed in your country? =这种物质在你们国家被禁止了吗？
Was the practice ever outlawed in the past? =这项活动过去曾经被禁止过吗？


否定句:
The activity was not outlawed until recently. =这项活动直到最近才被禁止。
That type of hunting is not outlawed in this region. =这种类型的狩猎在这个地区并没有被禁止。
