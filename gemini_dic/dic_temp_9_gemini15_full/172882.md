quagmires:/ˈkwæɡmaɪrz/| n.|泥潭；困境；复杂的问题 |A quagmire is a soft, wet area of ground that is difficult to walk across. It can also refer to a difficult or complicated situation. |例句：The project quickly became a quagmire of delays and cost overruns.  这个项目很快变成了一个充满延误和成本超支的困境。


例句：The negotiations were bogged down in a political quagmire.  谈判陷入了政治困境。


近义词：morass, bog, mire, predicament, mess


反义词：easy situation, clear path, simple solution


词性：名词
