jambul:/ˈdʒæmbəl/| n.|香榄，印度紫檀|A jambul is a tropical evergreen tree that produces a dark purple, almost black, fruit.  The fruit is edible and is often used in jams, jellies, and other food products. The tree itself is also valued for its timber.|Example:  The local market was brimming with fresh jambul fruit.|近义词：Java plum, black plum|反义词：无
