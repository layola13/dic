drowsier:/ˈdraʊziər/| adj.|困倦的，想睡的，瞌睡的 |More sleepy; feeling a greater need for sleep.  It often implies a gradual increase in sleepiness.|例句：After a long day, I felt drowsier than usual.  一天之后，我感到比平时更困倦。|例句：The warm fire made me feel even drowsier.|温暖的炉火让我感觉更困了。|近义词：sleepier, sluggish, lethargic|反义词：wide-awake, alert, energetic|形容词


USAGE->

He is drowsier than usual today. = 他今天比平时更困。
The baby is drowsier after her nap. = 婴儿午睡后更困了。

一般过去时:
I was drowsier after the long drive. = 长途开车后我更困了。

现在完成时:

将来时:

The students will likely be drowsier by the end of the semester. = 到学期末，学生们可能会更困。
