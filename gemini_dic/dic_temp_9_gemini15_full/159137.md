pod-caster:/ˈpɑːdkæstər/|/ˈpɒdkɑːstə(r)/ | n. |播客制作人，播客主持人 |A podcaster is a person who creates and hosts a podcast.  They are responsible for all aspects of podcast production, from planning and recording to editing and distribution. |Example:  She's a successful podcaster with millions of listeners. 她是一位拥有数百万听众的成功播客制作人。

Example: He is a new podcaster and is still learning the ropes. 他是一位新手播客制作人，仍在学习技巧。


近义词:podcast host, audio show host |反义词:listener, audience member
