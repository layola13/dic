optometry:/ɒpˈtɒmətri/|/ˈɑːptɑːmətri/| n. |验光学；视光学|The science of vision, visual systems, and vision correction.  It involves the examination, diagnosis, treatment, and management of vision-related problems. Optometrists are healthcare professionals who provide eye care services.| 例句：She's studying optometry at university. = 她正在大学学习验光学。

Many people visit an optometrist for regular eye exams. = 许多人定期去看验光师进行眼睛检查。
