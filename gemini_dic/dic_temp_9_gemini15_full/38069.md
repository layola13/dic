compositing:/kəmˈpɑːzɪtɪŋ/ | n. |合成，合成影像|The process of combining multiple images or video streams into a single image or video.  This is often used in filmmaking, video games, and graphic design to create complex visual effects.  For example, compositing might involve layering a CGI character onto a live-action background.|近义词：layering, combining, merging |反义词：separating, isolating
