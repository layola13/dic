catches:/ˈkætʃɪz/| v.|抓住；赶上；感染；理解；吸引；录制|To seize or grasp something with the hands or other parts of the body.  To overtake or come up with someone or something. To become infected with a disease. To understand or comprehend something. To attract or fascinate someone. To record something.|例句：The cat catches mice. 猫抓老鼠。
The bus catches the train. 公车赶上了火车。


He catches a cold easily. 他很容易感冒。

I didn't catch what you said. 我没听懂你说什么。

Her beauty catches the eye. 她的美貌引人注目。

The concert was caught on camera. 音乐会被录了下来。

|近义词：grasp, seize, apprehend, trap, capture, get, understand, attract, record|反义词：miss, lose, fail to understand, repel|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He catches the ball every time. = 他每次都能接住球。
She catches the bus to work. = 她搭公车去上班。

一般过去时:
I caught a cold last week. = 我上星期感冒了。
The police caught the thief. = 警察抓住了小偷。

现在完成时:
He has caught many fish today. = 他今天钓了很多鱼。
She has caught the flu. = 她得了流感。


过去完成时:
I had caught the train before the storm began. =暴风雨开始之前我已经赶上了火车。
They had caught the criminal before he escaped the country. = 他们在罪犯逃出国境之前就抓住了他。

将来时:
I will catch the early train tomorrow. = 我明天将搭乘早班火车。
We will catch you later. = 我们稍后再找你。

虚拟语气:
If I caught a big fish, I would be very happy. = 如果我钓到一条大鱼，我会非常高兴。
If I had caught that bus, I wouldn't have been late. = 如果我赶上了那班车，我就不会迟到了。


被动语态:
The thief was caught by the police. = 小偷被警察抓住了。
The ball was caught by the player. = 球被运动员接住了。


疑问句:
Did you catch the game last night? = 你昨晚看比赛了吗？（看比赛= 抓住比赛的精彩）
Did she catch the meaning of the poem? = 她理解这首诗的意思吗？


否定句:
I didn't catch your name. = 我没听清你的名字。
He didn't catch the ball. = 他没接住球。
