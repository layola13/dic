redialed:/ˌriːˈdaɪld/| v. |重拨（电话）|To dial a telephone number again.  This usually implies that the initial call was unsuccessful.|例句：I redialed the number several times but still couldn't get through.  我重拨了好几次号码，但仍然无法接通。
例句：After the line went dead, she redialed immediately.  线路中断后，她立即重拨了电话。|近义词：recalled, tried again|反义词：disconnected, hung up|动词
