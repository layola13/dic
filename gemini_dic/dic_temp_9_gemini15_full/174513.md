quinquereme:/ˈkwɪŋkwɪriːm/| n. |五桨战船|A quinquereme was an ancient Roman and Greek warship with five banks or tiers of oars.  These large vessels were powerful and played a significant role in naval warfare during their time. They required a large crew and were difficult to maneuver. |Example: The Roman navy relied heavily on quinqueremes for its dominance in the Mediterranean.  罗马海军非常依赖五桨战船来维持其在地中海的统治地位。|近义词：warship, galley (though less specific)|反义词：none specifically, but perhaps small boat or sailing vessel.
