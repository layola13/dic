instigations:/ˌɪnstɪˈɡeɪʃənz/| n. |煽动；教唆；挑衅|The act of instigating or provoking something, typically something bad or violent.  It refers to the actions or processes that lead to the beginning of an event, especially a negative one. |例句：The police are investigating the instigations that led to the riot.  警方正在调查导致暴乱的煽动行为。

例句：His instigations were the cause of much unhappiness. 他的煽动导致了许多不幸。


近义词：incitements, provocations, instigations, goadings |反义词：pacifications, appeasements, conciliations |词性：名词
