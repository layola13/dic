buzzard:/ˈbʌzərd/| n.|秃鹰，鹰；讨厌鬼|A buzzard is a large bird of prey, typically with brown plumage.  It is known for its scavenging habits and circling flight.  The term can also be used informally to describe an unpleasant or annoying person.|例句：A buzzard circled lazily overhead.  一只秃鹰懒洋洋地在头顶盘旋。|例句：He's a real buzzard; always complaining. 他真是个讨厌鬼，总是抱怨。|近义词：vulture, hawk (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a helpful or pleasant person.|名词
