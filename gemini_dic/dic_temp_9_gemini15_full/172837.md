quadrophonic:/ˌkwɑːdrəˈfɒnɪk/| adj.|四声道立体声的；四声道的|Relating to or using a four-channel sound reproduction system.  Quadrophonic sound uses four speakers to create a more immersive and realistic listening experience than stereo sound. |Example: The concert was recorded in quadrophonic sound, offering a truly enveloping auditory experience.  这场音乐会是用四声道立体声录制的，提供了真正身临其境的听觉体验。|近义词：four-channel, quad|反义词：monophonic, stereophonic
