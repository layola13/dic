overdrying: /ˌoʊvərˈdraɪɪŋ/ | v. |过度干燥|The act of drying something excessively, resulting in damage or undesirable effects.  It often refers to fabrics, foods, or other materials that become brittle, cracked, or otherwise compromised due to excessive dryness.|例句：Overdrying the laundry caused the towels to become stiff and scratchy. = 洗衣物过度干燥导致毛巾变得僵硬粗糙。
例句：The overdrying of the clay led to cracks in the finished pottery. = 粘土过度干燥导致成品陶器出现裂缝。|近义词：desiccation, dehydration|反义词：underdrying, dampening
