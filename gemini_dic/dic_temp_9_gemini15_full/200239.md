skirmished:/ˈskɜːrmɪʃt/ | v. | 小规模冲突；轻微争执 | To engage in a brief, minor fight or conflict; a brief and usually minor fight.  |例句：The two armies skirmished for several days before the main battle.  两军在主要战役前进行了数日的零星冲突。

The rebels skirmished with government troops near the border.  叛军在边境附近与政府军发生小规模冲突。


近义词：scolded, quarreled, fought, clashed, scuffled | 反义词：cooperated, collaborated


USAGE->

The soldiers skirmish near the border. = 士兵们在边境附近发生小规模冲突。

一般过去时:

过去完成时:

词源:
源自中古英语 * skirmisch，最终源自中古荷兰语 * schermischen “战斗，格斗”，可能与古高地德语 * scermen “切割，削减”有关。
