clunking:/ˈklʌŋkɪŋ/| adj.| 沉重的；笨重的；发出沉闷声响的 |  Describing a sound or movement that is heavy, dull, and somewhat clumsy. It suggests a solid, heavy object hitting another, making a thudding or resonant noise.  |例句：The old door closed with a loud clunking sound.  = 那扇旧门发出沉重的砰的一声关上了。

The engine made a worrying clunking noise. = 发动机发出令人担忧的沉闷声响。

近义词： thudding, bumping, banging, jarring | 反义词： light, smooth, silent, delicate | 词性:形容词
