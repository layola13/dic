foghorns:/ˈfɔːɡhɔːrnz/ | n. |雾号，雾笛 | a horn sounded in foggy conditions to warn ships of danger.  雾中用于警告船只危险的号角。| The foghorns blared incessantly throughout the night.  雾号整夜不停地鸣叫着。|  warning signals, sirens | (无明确反义词，可考虑quiet, silence等作为相对词)| 名词

The foghorn sounds intermittently. = 雾号间歇地鸣响。
Foghorns warn ships of danger. = 雾号警告船只危险。
