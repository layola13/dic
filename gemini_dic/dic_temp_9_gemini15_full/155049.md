petitions:/pəˈtɪʃənz/| n.|请愿书；请求|A formal written request, typically one signed by many people, appealing to authority with respect to a particular cause.|例句：Thousands signed the petition to save the local library.数千人签署了请愿书以拯救当地图书馆。|例句：The government received numerous petitions against the new tax law. 政府收到了许多反对新税法的请愿书。|近义词：requests, appeals, supplications|反义词：denials, rejections|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They petition the government for change. = 他们向政府请愿要求改变。
Many people petition for better public transport. = 许多人请愿要求改善公共交通。


一般过去时:
The citizens petitioned the mayor to reconsider his decision. = 市民向市长请愿要求他重新考虑他的决定。
She petitioned the court for a new trial. = 她向法院请愿要求重新审判。


现在完成时:
They have petitioned the council several times this year. = 他们今年已经向议会请愿了几次。
Activists have petitioned for stricter environmental regulations. = 活动家们一直在请愿要求更严格的环境法规。


过去完成时:
They had petitioned the king before, but to no avail. = 他们以前曾向国王请愿过，但没有结果。
The workers had petitioned for better wages for months. = 工人们几个月来一直在请愿要求更高的工资。


将来时:
They will petition the court again next week. = 他们下周将再次向法院请愿。
We will petition the school board to reconsider their decision. = 我们将向学校董事会请愿要求他们重新考虑他们的决定。


虚拟语气:
If they petitioned the government, maybe they would get a response. = 如果他们向政府请愿，也许他们会得到回应。
I wish they had petitioned the authorities earlier. = 我希望他们早点向当局请愿。


被动语态:
The petition was submitted to the parliament. = 请愿书已提交给议会。
The issue was petitioned about by many concerned citizens. = 许多关心此事的市民对此问题进行了请愿。


疑问句:
Will they petition the council? = 他们会向议会请愿吗？
Have they petitioned the government yet? = 他们已经向政府请愿了吗？


否定句:
They did not petition the authorities. = 他们没有向当局请愿。
The workers chose not to petition for better conditions. = 工人们选择不为争取更好的条件而请愿。
