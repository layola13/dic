promotion:/prəˈmoʊʃən/| n. |晋升；促销；推广；宣传 |The act of raising someone to a higher position or rank; the advancement of someone to a more important position.  Also refers to the advertising and marketing of a product or service. |例句：He received a promotion to senior manager. 他被提升为高级经理。

例句：The company launched a new promotion for its summer clothing line. 公司为其夏季服装系列推出了一项新的促销活动。

近义词：advancement, elevation, upgrade, publicity, marketing

反义词：demotion, relegation, downgrade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She gets a promotion every year. = 她每年都升职。
The company runs many promotions during the holidays. = 公司在节日期间开展许多促销活动。

一般过去时:
He received a promotion last month. = 他上个月升职了。
They launched a successful promotion for their new product. = 他们为他们的新产品成功地开展了一次促销活动。

现在完成时:
She has had three promotions since joining the company. = 自加入公司以来，她已经升职三次了。
The marketing team has completed several promotions this year. = 营销团队今年已经完成了多次促销活动。

过去完成时:
He had already received two promotions before he was offered the CEO position. = 在他被提名为首席执行官之前，他已经升职两次了。
They had run several unsuccessful promotions before they finally found one that worked. = 在他们最终找到一个有效的促销活动之前，他们已经开展过几次不成功的促销活动。

将来时:
She will likely get a promotion next year. = 她明年很可能会升职。
The company will launch a new promotion next month. = 公司下个月将推出一个新的促销活动。

虚拟语气:
If I worked harder, I might get a promotion. = 如果我更努力工作，我可能会升职。
I wish I had applied for that promotion. = 我真希望我申请了那次晋升。

被动语态:
He was given a promotion. = 他被提升了。
The new product was promoted through various channels. = 新产品通过各种渠道进行推广。

疑问句:
Did he get a promotion? = 他升职了吗？
Is there a promotion on this product? = 这个产品有促销活动吗？

否定句:
He didn't get the promotion. = 他没有得到晋升。
The promotion didn't generate much interest. = 促销活动没有引起太多兴趣。
