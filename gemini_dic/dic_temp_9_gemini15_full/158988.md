plutonic:/pluːˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.|深成的；冥府的；| relating to or denoting igneous rock that has solidified at considerable depth below the surface of the earth.  Plutonic rocks are typically coarse-grained. |例句：Granite is a plutonic rock. 花岗岩是一种深成岩。|例句：The plutonic formations of the region are of great geological interest. 该地区的深成岩层具有极大的地质学意义。|近义词：intrusive, abyssal|反义词：volcanic, extrusive
