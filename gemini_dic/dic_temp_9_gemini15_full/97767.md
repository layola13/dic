inject:/ɪnˈdʒekt/ | vt. |注射；注入；引入 | To force or introduce (a liquid, gas, or other substance) into something, typically with a syringe or other instrument.  |例句：The doctor injected the medicine into his arm. 医生将药注射到他的手臂里。

例句：The artist injected vibrant colors into the painting.艺术家在画中注入了鲜艳的色彩。


近义词：insert, introduce, administer | 反义词：extract, withdraw, remove | 词性:动词

词源:
The word "inject" comes from the Latin word "injectare," which is a combination of "in-" (into) and "jactare" (to throw or cast).  Therefore, the literal meaning is "to throw into."
