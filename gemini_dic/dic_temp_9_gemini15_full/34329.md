clashed:/klæʃt/ | v. |冲突，抵触；不协调；发出撞击声 |To come into violent conflict; to be in disagreement or opposition; to make a loud, harsh noise. |例句：The two armies clashed on the battlefield.  两军在战场上交战。

The colors in her outfit clashed horribly. 她的衣服颜色搭配得很糟糕。


近义词：collided, conflicted, clashed, disagreed,  |反义词：harmonized, agreed, concurred |动词


USAGE->

过去完成时:
