/ˌpriːˈrɛkənˌsaɪlz/| vt. |预先调节；预先协调；预先对账|To reconcile (accounts, etc.) in advance.  This term is primarily used in accounting and finance, referring to the process of comparing and adjusting financial records before they are officially reconciled.|The accountant prereconciles the bank statements each month to identify potential discrepancies before the formal reconciliation process.|近义词：pre-adjust, pre-balance|反义词：post-reconcile


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company prereconciles its accounts every week. = 公司每周预先对账。
The team prereconciles the data before submitting the report. = 团队在提交报告之前预先协调数据。


一般过去时:
They prereconciled the figures last night. = 他们昨晚预先对账。
The department prereconciled the budget before the meeting. = 部门在会议前预先协调了预算。


现在完成时:
We have prereconciled the transactions for this quarter. = 我们已经预先协调了本季度的交易。
She has prereconciled the accounts for the past three months. = 她已经预先对账了过去三个月的账目。


过去完成时:
They had prereconciled the data before the system crash. = 系统崩溃之前，他们已经预先协调了数据。
The auditor had prereconciled the accounts before the audit began. = 审计员在审计开始之前已经预先对账。


将来时:
We will prereconcile the financial statements next week. = 我们下周将预先对账。
The team will prereconcile the project budget before the final presentation. = 团队将在最终演示文稿之前预先协调项目预算。


虚拟语气:
If we prereconciled the accounts earlier, we would have found the error sooner. = 如果我们早点预先对账，就能更快发现错误。
It would be helpful if they prereconciled the data before analysis. = 如果他们在分析之前预先协调数据，将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The accounts are prereconciled by the accounting team. = 账目由会计团队预先对账。
The data will be prereconciled before being uploaded to the system. = 数据将在上传到系统之前进行预先协调。


疑问句:

否定句:
They did not prereconcile the accounts. = 他们没有预先对账。
The system does not automatically prereconcile the transactions. = 系统不会自动预先协调交易。
