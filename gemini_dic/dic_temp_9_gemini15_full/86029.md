health:/helθ/| n. |健康，卫生，保健|The state of being free from illness or injury.|例句：She is in good health. 她身体健康。|例句：The health of the nation is important.  国家的健康很重要。|近义词：wellness, well-being |反义词：illness, sickness, disease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maintains good health through regular exercise. = 他通过定期锻炼保持健康。
Good nutrition contributes to good health. = 良好的营养有助于健康。


一般过去时:
She enjoyed excellent health until her seventies. = 直到七十多岁，她都享有极好的健康。
His health deteriorated rapidly after the accident. = 事故后他的健康迅速恶化。


现在完成时:
I have maintained my health through a balanced diet. = 我通过均衡饮食保持了健康。
She has struggled with her health for many years. = 多年来她一直与健康作斗争。


过去完成时:
He had been in poor health for years before his death. = 他去世前多年身体状况一直不好。
They had neglected their health for too long. = 他们长期忽视了自己的健康。


将来时:
I will prioritize my health in the coming year. = 我将在来年优先考虑我的健康。
He hopes to improve his health with a new diet and exercise program. = 他希望通过新的饮食和锻炼计划来改善他的健康。


虚拟语气:
If I had taken better care of my health, I wouldn't be so ill now. = 如果我更好地照顾自己的健康，我现在就不会这么病了。
I wish I had paid more attention to my health in my younger years. = 我希望我在年轻的时候更关注我的健康。


被动语态:
Public health is often overlooked. = 公共卫生常常被忽视。
His health was severely impacted by the stress of his job. = 他的健康受到了工作压力严重的影响。


疑问句:
How is your health? = 你的健康状况如何？
What can I do to improve my health? = 我能做些什么来改善我的健康？


否定句:
He doesn't pay much attention to his health. = 他不太注意自己的健康。
She didn't realize the importance of good health until she got sick. = 直到生病后，她才意识到良好健康的重要性。
