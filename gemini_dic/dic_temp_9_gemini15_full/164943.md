pregrowth:/ˈpriːɡroʊθ/ | adj. | 预生长；生长前的 |  Describing the period or stage before significant growth begins; preparatory to growth.  It often refers to the initial phase of development, before exponential increase or expansion. | Example: The pregrowth phase of the bacteria was characterized by slow cell division.  细菌的预生长阶段的特点是细胞分裂缓慢。  |近义词: preparatory, preliminary, antecedent, initial | 反义词: postgrowth, mature, developed | 词性: adjective
