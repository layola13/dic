nonroutine:/ˌnɑːnruːˈtiːn/| adj. |例外的；非常规的；非日常的 |Not part of the usual routine or procedure; unusual or unexpected.  |例句：The unexpected power outage caused a nonroutine shutdown of the system.  意外的停电导致系统非正常关闭。

例句：This project requires nonroutine problem-solving skills. 这个项目需要非常规的解决问题的能力。


近义词：unusual, exceptional, extraordinary, irregular, atypical |反义词：routine, regular, usual, ordinary, typical |词性：形容词


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".  (否定前缀)
* **routine**:  from Old French *routine*, ultimately from Latin *ruta* ("a track, a road"). (来自古法语，最终源于拉丁语，意为“小路，道路”，引申为常规)
