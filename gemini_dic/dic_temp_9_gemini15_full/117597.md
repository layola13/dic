megaly:/ˈmɛɡəli/| n.|巨大型，肥大|Enlargement or abnormal increase in size of an organ or part, usually due to an increase in the size of the cells themselves (hypertrophy).  The term is often used as a combining form in medical terminology to indicate enlargement of a specific organ or body part.| 例句：Hepatomegaly is an enlargement of the liver.  肝肿大是肝脏增大。 Cardiomegaly refers to an enlarged heart. 心脏肥大指的是心脏增大。|近义词：hypertrophy, enlargement, distention |反义词：atrophy, hypoplasia
