habiting:/ˈhæbɪtɪŋ/| v. |居住，栖息|The act of living in or occupying a place.  It implies a more permanent or regular residence than simply "living" in a place.  It is often used in the context of animals or plants inhabiting a particular environment.|例句：Many species of birds are inhabiting the forest.  许多鸟类栖息在森林里。

例句：The family is inhabiting a small cottage by the sea.  这家人住在一间海边的简陋小屋里。


近义词：dwelling, residing, occupying, inhabiting

反义词：evacuating, leaving, deserting

词性：动词
