reballoting: /ˌriːˈbælətiŋ/ | n. | 重新投票；再次投票 | The act of voting again, typically after an initial vote has been deemed invalid or inconclusive.  It often involves a repeat of the voting process with the same or a modified set of options. | Example: The reballoting process was necessary after irregularities were discovered in the initial election.  重新投票过程是在最初选举中发现违规行为后才必要的。 | 近义词：revoting, second ballot, re-election | 反义词：final vote, decisive vote | 名词
