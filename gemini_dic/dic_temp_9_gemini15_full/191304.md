scenarios:/səˈnærioʊz/| n. |可能发生的情况；设想；方案 | A description of a possible course of events.  It often involves a hypothetical situation or a prediction of future events. | Example: We need to prepare for several different scenarios, including a complete power outage. 我们需要为几种不同的情况做好准备，包括完全停电。|近义词：situations, possibilities, hypotheses, eventualities |反义词：realities, certainties


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scenarios they present are quite realistic. = 他们提出的设想相当现实。
This scenario plays out often in real life. = 这种情况在现实生活中经常发生。


一般过去时:
The emergency scenarios we practiced last year proved useful. = 我们去年练习的紧急情况预案证明很有用。
The worst-case scenario unfolded exactly as we feared. = 最糟糕的情况正如我们所担心的那样发生了。


现在完成时:
We have considered all possible scenarios. = 我们已经考虑了所有可能的情况。
They have run through several scenarios to prepare for the launch. = 他们演练了几种方案来为发射做准备。


过去完成时:
By the time the crisis hit, they had already prepared several scenarios. = 当危机来临时，他们已经准备了几种方案。
We had anticipated this scenario and had a plan in place. = 我们预料到了这种情况，并制定了计划。


将来时:
We will explore several different scenarios in the next meeting. = 我们将在下一次会议上探讨几种不同的方案。
The report will outline several possible scenarios for the future. = 该报告将概述未来几种可能的情况。


虚拟语气:
If this scenario were to occur, we would be prepared. = 如果这种情况发生，我们将做好准备。
I wish this scenario had never happened. = 我希望这种情况从未发生过。


被动语态:
Several scenarios were presented to the board. = 向董事会提出了几种方案。
The best-case scenario is being considered by the team. = 团队正在考虑最乐观的情况。


疑问句:
What are the possible scenarios? = 可能的情况有哪些？
What scenario are we preparing for? = 我们正在为哪种情况做准备？


否定句:
That scenario is not likely to happen. = 这种情况不太可能发生。
This scenario does not take into account external factors. = 这种情况没有考虑外部因素。
