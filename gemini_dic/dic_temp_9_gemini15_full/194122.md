self-impulsions: /ˌsɛlfɪmˈpʌlʃənz/ | n. | 自发冲动；自我驱动 |  The inherent drive or motivation within an individual to act or behave in a certain way, often without external prompting. It refers to actions stemming from internal desires, needs, or instincts. |

例句：

His self-impulsions led him to quit his stable job and start his own business.  他的自发冲动促使他辞去稳定的工作，自己创业。

The artist's self-impulsions fueled her creativity, resulting in a prolific body of work. 艺术家的自我驱动激发了她的创造力，创作出了大量的作品。


近义词：self-motivation, inner drive, initiative, impetus

反义词：passivity, inertia, inaction, compulsion (in the sense of being driven by external forces)
