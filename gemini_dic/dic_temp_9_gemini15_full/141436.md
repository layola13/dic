ophthalmoscope:/ɒfˈθælməskoʊp/| n. |检眼镜|An ophthalmoscope is an instrument used by ophthalmologists and other healthcare professionals to examine the interior of the eye, particularly the retina, optic disc, and blood vessels.  It allows visualization of the structures within the eye to diagnose various eye conditions.|Example: The doctor used an ophthalmoscope to examine the patient's retina for signs of diabetic retinopathy.  医生用检眼镜检查患者的视网膜是否有糖尿病视网膜病变的迹象。|近义词：opthalmic instrument|反义词：无
