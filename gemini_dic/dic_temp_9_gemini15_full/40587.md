convalesce:/ˌkɒnvəˈles/| /ˌkɑːnvəˈlɛs/ | vi. |恢复健康；康复|To recover health and strength after illness; recuperate.  |例句：He is convalescing at home after his surgery. 他手术后在家休养。|例句：She spent several weeks convalescing in the countryside. 她花了几周时间在乡下休养。|近义词：recover, recuperate, rehabilitate |反义词：worsen, deteriorate |动词
