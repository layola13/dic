fraction:/ˈfrækʃən/| n.|分数；部分；少量|A fraction is a numerical representation of a part of a whole, expressed as a ratio of two integers.  It can also refer to a small portion or part of something.|Example: One-half (1/2) is a fraction.  She only ate a fraction of the cake.|近义词：part, portion, piece|反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The recipe calls for a fraction of a cup of sugar. = 食谱需要一小部分杯子的糖。
A small fraction of the population supports this policy. = 只有很小一部分人口支持这项政策。


一般过去时:
Only a fraction of the students passed the exam. = 只有极少部分学生通过了考试。
He gave a fraction of his earnings to charity. = 他把一部分收入捐给了慈善机构。


现在完成时:
A large fraction of the work has been completed. = 大部分工作已经完成了。
Scientists have identified only a fraction of the existing species. = 科学家们只识别出极少部分现存物种。


过去完成时:
By the end of the day, a fraction of the original supplies remained. = 到一天结束时，只剩下少量原先的物资。
He had already spent a fraction of his inheritance. = 他已经花掉了一部分遗产。


将来时:
A larger fraction of the population will be elderly in the future. = 未来会有更大比例的人口是老年人。
Only a fraction of the project will be finished by next week. = 到下周只有项目的一小部分将会完成。


虚拟语气:
If only a fraction of the predictions were true, it would be catastrophic. = 如果只有一小部分预测成真，那将是灾难性的。
I wish a larger fraction of my time were spent on personal hobbies. = 我希望我的时间有更大一部分花在我的个人爱好上。


被动语态:
A significant fraction of the budget was allocated to research. = 一大部分预算被分配给了研究。
Only a small fraction of the profits are being distributed to shareholders. = 只有极少部分利润被分配给股东。


疑问句:
What fraction of the budget is allocated to education? = 预算中有多少比例分配给了教育？
What fraction of the population is unemployed? = 有多少比例的人口处于失业状态？


否定句:
More than a fraction of the students did not complete their assignments. = 不止一小部分学生没有完成作业。
Not a fraction of the evidence supports his claim. = 没有一点证据支持他的说法。


PHRASE->

a fraction of: 一小部分
a small fraction of: 极小一部分
a large fraction of: 大部分
a significant fraction of: 相当一部分
a considerable fraction of: 相当大的一部分
