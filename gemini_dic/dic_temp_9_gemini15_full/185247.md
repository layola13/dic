retranscribing: /ˌriːˈtrænskraɪbɪŋ/ | v. |重新抄写；重新转录 | To write or record something again, especially in a different form.  |例句：The secretary is retranscribing the minutes of the meeting. 秘书正在重新抄写会议记录。

例句：The researcher is retranscribing the audio interview into a written transcript. 研究人员正在将音频访谈重新转录成文字记录。


近义词：rewriting, recopying, transcribing again

反义词：None (no direct opposite, but the opposite action would be destroying the original transcription)

词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retranscribes the old records every week. 他每周都重新抄写旧记录。
She retranscribes the audio files for her research. 她为研究重新转录音频文件。
