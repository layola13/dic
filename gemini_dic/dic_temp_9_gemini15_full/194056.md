self-harmer:/ˈsɛlfˈhɑːrmər/| n. |自残者|A person who intentionally injures themselves, often as a coping mechanism for emotional distress.  This can involve a wide range of behaviors, from cutting to burning to hitting.  The term often implies a pattern of repetitive behavior rather than a single incident.|例句：The therapist worked with the self-harmer to develop healthier coping strategies.|近义词：self-mutilator, person with self-harm behaviors|反义词：N/A
