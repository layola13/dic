nonperformer:/ˌnɒn pərˈfɔːrmər/| n. |未达标者，表现不佳者，失败者|A nonperformer is someone or something that fails to achieve the expected results or standards.  It can refer to an employee who doesn't meet performance expectations, a product that doesn't sell well, or an investment that doesn't yield returns.|例句：The company decided to lay off several nonperformers. 公司决定解雇几名表现不佳的员工。

例句：That marketing campaign was a complete nonperformer; sales actually decreased. 那次营销活动完全失败了；销量反而下降了。|近义词：underachiever, failure, slacker |反义词：high performer, top performer, achiever
