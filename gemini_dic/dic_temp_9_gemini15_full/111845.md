logicality: /ˌlɒdʒɪˈkælɪti/ | /ˌlɑːdʒɪˈkælɪti/ | n. |逻辑性，合理性 |The quality of being logical; soundness of reasoning.  It refers to the degree to which something conforms to the principles of logic, exhibiting coherence, consistency, and validity in its structure or argument. |例句：The logicality of his argument was undeniable.  他的论证的逻辑性是不可否认的。

The report lacked logicality; its conclusions didn't follow from the evidence presented.  这份报告缺乏逻辑性；它的结论与所提供的证据不符。|近义词：reasonableness, rationality, coherence, consistency |反义词：illogic, irrationality, inconsistency, incoherence
