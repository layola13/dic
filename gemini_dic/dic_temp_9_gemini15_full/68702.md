farer:/ˈfɛərər/| n. |旅行者，旅客|A person who travels, especially on foot.  A farer is someone who journeys, often over a long distance, especially on foot.  The word emphasizes the act of traveling itself, rather than the method of travel.|例句：The weary farer finally reached the inn.  疲惫的旅行者终于到达了客栈。
The lone farer walked for miles across the desolate landscape.  孤独的旅行者在荒凉的景色中走了数英里。|近义词：traveler, journeyer, wayfarer|反义词：stay-at-home
