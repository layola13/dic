self-reforms:/ˌsɛlfˈriːfɔːrmz/| n.|自我改革，自我改进|The process of improving oneself through one's own efforts.  It emphasizes internal change and improvement driven by the individual.|例句：The company underwent significant self-reforms to improve efficiency.  这家公司进行了重大的自我改革以提高效率。


例句：Her self-reforms included a commitment to healthier eating and regular exercise. 她的自我改革包括承诺健康饮食和定期锻炼。


近义词：self-improvement, self-amendment, personal growth


反义词：self-destruction, stagnation, complacency


词性:名词
