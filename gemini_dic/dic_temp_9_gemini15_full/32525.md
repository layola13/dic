chinwag: /ˈtʃɪnwæɡ/ | v. | 闲聊，聊天 | An informal conversation; a chat.  | Example: Let's have a chinwag about the weekend.  我们来聊聊周末的事情吧。 |近义词: chat, gossip, natter, confab |反义词:  silence, formal discussion | 动词


USAGE->

词源:

The word "chinwag" is believed to be of British origin, emerging in the 20th century.  Its etymology is uncertain, but it's likely a blend of "chin" (referring to the lower part of the face, where conversation often takes place) and "wag" (meaning to move or shake, suggesting lively conversation).  The exact origins remain speculative, but the informal and playful nature of the word aligns with its use as a descriptive term for casual conversation.
