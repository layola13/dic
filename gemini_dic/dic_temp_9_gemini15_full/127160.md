natural numbers:/ˈnætʃrəl ˈnʌmbərz/| n. |自然数|The natural numbers are the positive integers, starting from 1 and increasing infinitely. They are the numbers we use for counting.  They are often denoted by the symbol ℕ. |Example: The set of natural numbers includes 1, 2, 3, 4, and so on. 自然数集合包括1, 2, 3, 4等等。|近义词：positive integers, counting numbers |反义词：negative integers, zero
