leavening:/ˈlevənɪŋ/| n.| 发酵剂；使…蓬松的东西 | A substance, such as yeast or baking powder, that causes dough or batter to rise.  | Example: The baker added leavening to the bread dough to make it light and fluffy.  烘焙师在面包面团中加入了发酵剂，使它变得轻盈蓬松。|近义词：raising agent, yeast, baking powder |反义词：flattening agent
