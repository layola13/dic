preaches:/ˈpriːtʃɪz/| v.|布道；宣扬；竭力主张|To deliver a sermon or religious address; to advocate or promote (an idea or cause) earnestly.|例句：The priest preaches every Sunday.  牧师每周日布道。|例句：He preaches the importance of education. 他极力主张教育的重要性。|近义词：sermonizes, advocates, proclaims|反义词：remains silent, ignores


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preaches every Sunday. = 他每周日布道。
She preaches tolerance and understanding. = 她宣扬宽容和理解。

一般过去时:
The minister preached a powerful sermon. = 牧师发表了一篇有力的布道。
He preached against violence and hatred. = 他谴责暴力和仇恨。

现在完成时:
He has preached in this church for twenty years. = 他在这座教堂布道已有二十年了。
She has preached the gospel to many people. = 她向许多人宣讲了福音。

过去完成时:
Before the war, he had preached peace and reconciliation. = 在战争爆发前，他一直宣扬和平与和解。
They had preached the importance of unity for years. = 他们多年来一直宣扬团结的重要性。

将来时:
He will preach on the theme of forgiveness next Sunday. = 他下周日将以宽恕为主题布道。
She will preach against injustice and inequality. = 她将谴责不公正和不平等。

虚拟语气:
If he preached with more passion, more people would listen. = 如果他布道更热情一些，就会有更多人倾听。
I wish he preached less about politics and more about faith. = 我希望他少谈论政治，多谈论信仰。

被动语态:
The sermon was preached to a large congregation. = 布道向众多会众宣讲。
The importance of hard work is often preached by our teachers. = 努力工作的重要性经常被我们的老师宣扬。

疑问句:
Does he preach every Sunday? = 他每周日都布道吗？
What does the preacher preach about today? = 传教士今天讲了什么？

否定句:
He doesn't preach hatred or division. = 他不宣扬仇恨或分裂。
She didn't preach to the unconverted. = 她没有向不信教的人布道。


PHRASE->
preach to the choir = 对牛弹琴
preach about = 宣扬…
preach at = 对…说教
preach against = 反对…
preach tolerance = 宣扬宽容
