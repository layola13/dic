curator: /kjʊˈreɪtər/ | n. | 馆长；策展人；管理人 | A curator is a person who is in charge of and responsible for the collection and care of items in a museum or other institution.  They also often organize exhibitions and events. |  The museum curator gave a fascinating lecture on the newly acquired artifacts.  博物馆馆长就新获得的文物做了一个引人入胜的讲座。|  conservator, keeper, custodian |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境使用例如：destructor) | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The curator selects artworks for the exhibition. = 馆长为展览挑选艺术品。
He curates a collection of antique maps. = 他管理着一批古地图收藏。


一般过去时:
The curator organized a successful retrospective. = 馆长组织了一次成功的回顾展。
She curated the museum's new wing.  = 她负责博物馆新展馆的策划。


现在完成时:
The museum has hired a new curator. = 博物馆已经聘请了一位新的馆长。
She has curated several award-winning exhibitions. = 她策划过几次屡获殊荣的展览。


过去完成时:
The curator had already chosen the paintings before the board meeting. = 在董事会会议之前，馆长已经选好了画作。
They had curated the collection for over a decade. = 他们已经管理这个收藏超过十年了。


将来时:
The museum will appoint a new curator next year. = 博物馆明年将任命一位新的馆长。
He will curate a major exhibition on Impressionist art. = 他将策划一个关于印象派艺术的重要展览。


虚拟语气:
If I were a curator, I would focus on contemporary art. = 如果我是馆长，我会专注于当代艺术。
I wish I could have curated that exhibition. = 我希望我能策划那个展览。


被动语态:
The exhibition was curated by a renowned expert. = 这个展览是由一位著名的专家策划的。
The artifacts are carefully curated by the museum staff. = 文物由博物馆工作人员仔细保管。


疑问句:
Who is the curator of this museum? = 这个博物馆的馆长是谁？
Will the curator be attending the opening ceremony? = 馆长会参加开幕式吗？


否定句:
He is not a curator, he's a conservator. = 他不是馆长，他是保管员。
The museum didn't have a curator for several months. = 博物馆好几个月都没有馆长。
