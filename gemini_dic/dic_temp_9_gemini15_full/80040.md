go-without-saying: /ˌɡoʊ wɪˈðaʊt ˈseɪɪŋ/ | adj. phr. | 不言而喻的，显而易见的 |  It is self-evident or obvious; needing no explanation.  | 例句：It goes without saying that you should be punctual for the interview.  = 面试准时赴约，这是不言而喻的。


例句：His dedication to his work goes without saying. = 他对工作的奉献精神是不言而喻的。


近义词：obvious, self-evident, apparent, clear, understandable


反义词：unclear, ambiguous, debatable, questionable


词性：形容词短语 (Adjective Phrase)


USAGE->
(由于go without saying本身是一个短语，不涉及各种时态变化，这里不列举时态例句)
