camped:/kæmpt/| v.|露营，扎营|To set up camp; to stay in a tent or other temporary shelter.  |例句：We camped by the lake for a week. 我们在湖边露营了一周。|例句：The family camped out under the stars.  这家人在星空下露营。|近义词：pitched, bivouacked|反义词：moved on, departed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They camp in the mountains every summer. = 他们每年夏天都在山上露营。
We usually camp near a river. = 我们通常在河边露营。


一般过去时:
They camped in the forest last weekend. = 他们上周末在森林里露营。
We camped under the stars last night. = 我们昨晚在星空下露营。


现在完成时:
They have camped in this spot many times. = 他们在这个地方露营过很多次。
We have never camped in such a beautiful place before. = 我们以前从未在如此美丽的地方露营过。


过去完成时:
They had camped there for a week before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临前，他们已在那里露营了一周。
We had already camped before the sun set. = 太阳下山之前，我们已经扎营了。


将来时:
They will camp near the beach next month. = 他们下个月将在海滩附近露营。
We will camp in the national park next summer. = 我们明年夏天将在国家公园露营。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would camp for a longer time. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会露营更长时间。
I wish we could camp by the ocean. = 我希望我们能在海边露营。


被动语态:
The campsite was camped on by many people during the festival. = 节日期间，许多人在营地露营。
The area was already camped out before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，那个地区已经被占领了。


疑问句:
Did they camp in the woods? = 他们在树林里露营了吗？
Where will you camp this year? = 今年你们会在哪里露营？


否定句:
They didn't camp near the river. = 他们没有在河边露营。
We won't camp in that area because of the wild animals. = 我们不会在那个地区露营，因为那里有野生动物。
