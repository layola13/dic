cowboys:/ˈkaʊˌbɔɪz/ | n. |牛仔，牧童 | Cowboys are often associated with the American West and a romanticized image of rugged individualism and skill in horseback riding and cattle herding.  They played a crucial role in the development of the American West, driving cattle across vast distances.  |例句：The cowboys skillfully herded the cattle across the plains. 牛仔们熟练地将牛群赶过平原。

例句：Many Hollywood films depict cowboys as brave and independent figures. 许多好莱坞电影将牛仔描绘成勇敢和独立的人物。


近义词：ranch hands, cattlemen, vaqueros (Spanish) |反义词：city dwellers, urbanites |词性：名词
