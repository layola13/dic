semihumanitarian:/ˌsɛmiˌhjuːmænɪˈtɛəriən/| adj.|半人道主义的；似人道主义的|Partially or seemingly humanitarian; exhibiting some but not all characteristics of humanitarianism.  It suggests an action or policy that has elements of humanitarian concern but is also motivated by other, potentially less altruistic factors.|例句：The government's response to the crisis was criticized as semihumanitarian, prioritizing political stability over immediate relief efforts.  政府对危机的应对被批评为半人道主义的，优先考虑政治稳定而不是立即救济工作。|近义词：quasi-humanitarian, partly humanitarian|反义词：inhumanitarian, anti-humanitarian
