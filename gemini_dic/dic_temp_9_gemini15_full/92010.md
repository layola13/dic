hyperorthodoxy:/ˌhaɪpərˈɔːrθədɒksi/ | adj. | 极端正统；过分正统 | Excessively orthodox; going beyond orthodoxy.  It implies an adherence to established doctrines or beliefs to an extreme or rigid degree, often to the point of intolerance or inflexibility. | The hyperorthodoxy of the religious sect led to their isolation from the wider community.  极端正统的宗教教派导致他们与更广泛的社会隔绝。 |近义词：extremism, fundamentalism, rigorism | 反义词：liberalism, modernism, progressivism
