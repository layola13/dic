monophonic:/ˌmɒnəˈfɒnɪk/|/ˌmɑːnəˈfɑːnɪk/| adj. |单音的，单声道|Having or using only one part; single-voiced; relating to sound reproduced from a single channel.  It refers to music or sound that uses only one melodic line at a time, without harmony or accompaniment. |例句：The recording is monophonic, lacking the richness of stereo sound. 这段录音是单声道，缺乏立体声的丰富感。
The early phonograph produced only monophonic sound. 早期的留声机只能产生单声道声音。

|近义词：single-voiced, unaccompanied, solo|反义词：polyphonic, stereophonic, multi-channel|形容词
