reaffirming:/ˌriːəˈfɜːrmɪŋ/ | v. | 重申；再次确认 |  To state again or emphasize one's commitment to or belief in something.  The act of reaffirming something involves restating a belief, commitment, or promise to reinforce its importance or validity. | 例句：The president reaffirmed his commitment to peace.  总统重申了他对和平的承诺。

例句：The treaty reaffirmed the borders between the two countries.  该条约再次确认了这两个国家之间的边界。


近义词：reassert, reiterate, restate, confirm, assure


反义词：deny, refute, contradict


词性:动词
