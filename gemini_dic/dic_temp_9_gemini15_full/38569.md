concocts:/kənˈkɒkts/| vt.|调制；捏造；虚构|To concoct something, typically a drink or dish, means to prepare it by mixing different ingredients together.  It can also mean to invent or devise something, especially a story or plan, often in a deceptive way.|例句：She concocted a delicious soup from leftover vegetables. 她用剩下的蔬菜调制了一道美味的汤。

She concocted a story to explain her lateness. 她编造了一个故事来解释她的迟到。|近义词：prepare, mix, brew, invent, fabricate, devise|反义词：destroy, dismantle, deconstruct

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef concocts a new dish every week. = 厨师每周都会调制一道新菜。
She concocts elaborate excuses for her mistakes. = 她为自己的错误编造了复杂的借口。
