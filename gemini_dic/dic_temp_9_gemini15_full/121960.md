missioning:/mɪˈʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 任务分配；委派；启用；调试 | The act of assigning a mission or task; the process of commissioning something, such as a piece of equipment or a work of art.  | Example: The missioning of the new satellite is scheduled for next week.  新卫星的启用计划在下周进行。 | 近义词：commissioning, assignment, delegation | 反义词：decommissioning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is missioning new projects constantly. = 公司不断地分配新项目。
He is missioning his team to solve the problem. = 他委派他的团队去解决这个问题。


一般过去时:
They missioned the troops to the front line. = 他们把军队派往前线。
The artist was missioned to create a mural. = 该艺术家受委托创作一幅壁画。


现在完成时:
We have missioned several teams to different regions. = 我们已经向不同的地区派遣了几个团队。
They have missioned a new study into climate change. = 他们已经委托进行一项关于气候变化的新研究。


过去完成时:
The general had missioned the scouts before the attack. = 将军在进攻之前已经派遣了侦察兵。
The company had missioned the product before launching its marketing campaign. = 公司在启动营销活动之前已经完成了产品的调试。


将来时:
They will mission the ship to explore the ocean depths. = 他们将派遣这艘船去探索海洋深处。
We will mission the new software to our clients next month. = 我们下个月将把新软件交付给我们的客户。


虚拟语气:
If we missioned more resources, we could finish the project sooner. = 如果我们投入更多资源，我们就能更快地完成项目。
I wish we had missioned a specialist earlier. = 我希望我们早点委托一位专家。


被动语态:
The team was missioned to investigate the accident. = 该团队受命调查事故。
The new equipment is being missioned this week. = 新设备本周正在调试中。


疑问句:
Who was missioned to lead the project? = 谁被委派领导这个项目？
What tasks have been missioned to the junior staff? = 分配给初级员工的任务是什么？


否定句:
They did not mission enough personnel for the task. = 他们没有为这项任务派遣足够的人员。
The project was not missioned properly, leading to delays. = 项目的启动不当，导致延误。
