salmagundi:/ˌsælməˈɡʌndi/| n.|什锦菜；大杂烩；混合物 |A salmagundi is a heterogeneous mixture of various ingredients, often food, but can also refer to a collection of miscellaneous items or ideas.  It implies a lack of uniformity or organization, but not necessarily in a negative way.  The term often suggests a lively and interesting, if somewhat chaotic, mix.|例句：The chef prepared a delicious salmagundi of seafood, vegetables, and pasta.  厨师准备了一道美味的海鲜、蔬菜和意大利面的什锦菜。

The artist's portfolio was a salmagundi of styles and techniques. 艺术家的作品集是各种风格和技巧的大杂烩。|近义词：hodgepodge, medley, mixture, farrago|反义词：uniformity, homogeneity, order
