repinning:/ˌriːˈpɪnɪŋ/| v.|重新别上，重新固定|The act of pinning something again;  fastening something with pins a second time, often implying a previous pinning that may have come loose or needs readjustment.|例句：She spent the afternoon repinning her collection of vintage scarves. 她花了整个下午重新别上她收藏的复古围巾。|例句：The tailor was repinning the lining of the jacket.  裁缝正在重新钉上夹克的衬里。|近义词：refastening, reattaching|反义词：unpinning, removing
