crushing:/ˈkrʌʃɪŋ/| adj., v.|压碎的；令人沮丧的；压倒性的；碾碎|The act of crushing something involves applying great force to it, resulting in its deformation or destruction.  It can also refer to something overwhelming or depressing. |例句：The weight of the rocks was crushing.  岩石的重量令人难以承受。|例句：The news of her failure was absolutely crushing. 她失败的消息非常令人沮丧。|近义词：overwhelming, devastating, depressing, pulverizing|反义词： uplifting, encouraging, exhilarating|形容词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The machine crushes stones. = 机器碾碎石头。
He crushes the can. = 他压扁易拉罐。


一般过去时:
The earthquake crushed the buildings. = 地震摧毁了建筑物。
She crushed the flower in her hand. = 她把花捏碎了。


现在完成时:
The pressure has crushed the pipe. = 压力已经压坏了管道。
The news has crushed his spirit. = 这个消息击垮了他的精神。


过去完成时:
The weight had crushed the car before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，重量已经压垮了汽车。
The bad news had crushed her hopes. = 坏消息已经粉碎了她的希望。


将来时:
The new policy will crush small businesses. = 新政策将摧毁小型企业。
The weight of the snow will crush the roof. = 雪的重量将压垮屋顶。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the truth, it would have crushed me. = 如果我知道真相，它会让我崩溃的。
If they had been there, the experience would have crushed them. = 如果他们在那儿，那经历会压垮他们的。


被动语态:
The grapes were crushed to make wine. = 葡萄被压碎以酿酒。
His spirit was crushed by the criticism. = 他的精神被批评压垮了。


疑问句:
Is the machine crushing the rocks effectively? = 机器是否有效地碾碎岩石？
Was the building crushed by the earthquake? = 建筑物是被地震压垮的吗？


否定句:
The machine doesn't crush stones. = 机器不碾碎石头。
He didn't crush the can. = 他没有压扁易拉罐。


PHRASE->

a crushing defeat: 一场惨败
crushing blow: 致命打击
crushing weight: 巨大的重量
crushing disappointment: 极度失望
crushing boredom: 令人难以忍受的无聊
