gamification:/ˌɡeɪmɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |游戏化；游戏机制应用|The application of game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts.  Gamification is used to engage users, increase motivation, and encourage participation.  It often involves using points, badges, leaderboards, and other game mechanics to reward desired behaviors.|例句：The company used gamification to improve employee productivity. 这家公司利用游戏化来提高员工生产力。

例句：Gamification techniques are increasingly used in education to motivate students. 游戏化技术越来越多地用于教育领域，以激励学生。


近义词：game mechanics application, playful engagement, interactive learning


反义词：de-gamification (a hypothetical antonym suggesting removal of game elements)


词性:名词
