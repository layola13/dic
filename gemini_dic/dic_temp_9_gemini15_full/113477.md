lyrically:/ˈlɪrɪkli/| adj.|抒情地，优美地，诗意地|In a way that is expressive and melodious, like a lyric or poem.  It suggests beauty, emotion, and often a musical quality.|例句：The song was lyrically beautiful, evoking strong emotions in the listener. 这首歌词优美动听，唤起了听众强烈的情感。

例句：She described the sunset lyrically, painting a vivid picture with her words. 她抒情地描述了日落，用文字描绘出一幅生动的画面。

近义词：poetically, musically, melodiously, expressively

反义词：prosaically, bluntly, harshly, directly
