mistranscribe:/ˌmɪsˈtrænskraɪb/| vt.|误抄，误写，歪曲|To write or record something incorrectly.  This often implies a misunderstanding or misinterpretation of the original material, leading to an inaccurate representation.|例句：He mistranscribed the address, leading to the delayed delivery of the package. 他地址抄错了，导致包裹延迟送达。

例句：The student mistranscribed the complex equation, resulting in an incorrect answer.  学生误抄了复杂的方程式，导致答案错误。|近义词：miscopy, misrepresent, misinterpret|反义词：transcribe accurately, correctly record
