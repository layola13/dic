recolonize:/ˌriːˈkɒlənaɪz/ | vt. |重新殖民；再次殖民 | To establish control over (a territory) again after a period of not having control.  This often implies a return to a previous colonial system or a similar form of dominance.  The term often carries negative connotations due to the historical injustices associated with colonialism. | Example:  The company attempted to recolonize the market after a period of decline.  这家公司试图在一段时间的衰退后重新控制市场。


Example:  Some historians argue that certain economic practices amount to a form of recolonization of developing nations.  一些历史学家认为某些经济行为相当于对发展中国家的重新殖民。


近义词: reoccupy, reconquer, reassert control over | 反义词: decolonize, liberate
