downscaled:/ˈdaʊnˌskeɪld/| adj.|缩减规模的，小型化的|Reduced in size or scale;  made smaller or less extensive.|The company downscaled its operations after the economic downturn.  经济衰退后，公司缩减了业务规模。|近义词：reduced, minimized, scaled-down|反义词：upscaled, enlarged, expanded|
