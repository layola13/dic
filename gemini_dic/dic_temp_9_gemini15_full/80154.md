goddam: /ˈɡɑːdæm/ | interj. adj. | 该死的，可恶的 |  A profane exclamation of anger, frustration, or annoyance;  also used as an intensifier or adjective. | 例句：Goddam it! That's infuriating! 该死！这太令人恼火了！

例句：The goddam car won't start. 这该死的车发动不了。


近义词：damn, darn, blasted, cursed, hellish


反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，因为它是表达负面情绪的词)


词性:  感叹词，形容词


USAGE->
(goddam is primarily used as an interjection and adjective,  grammatical tense usage is not applicable in the same way as verbs.)


PHRASE->

goddam right = 完全正确
goddam fool = 该死的傻瓜
