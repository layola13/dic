clogging:/ˈklɒɡɪŋ/| v. |堵塞；阻塞；(使)阻塞；妨碍|To block something, preventing liquid, gas, or movement.  |例句：The drain is clogged with hair.  排水管被头发堵住了。|例句：The heavy snow clogged the roads. 大雪堵塞了道路。|近义词：obstruct, block, impede, jam |反义词：clear, unblock, open


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pipes are clogging the system. = 管道正在堵塞系统。
Leaves are clogging the gutters. = 叶子堵塞了排水沟。


一般过去时:
The heavy rain clogged the streets. = 大雨堵塞了街道。
The snowstorm clogged the airport. = 暴风雪堵塞了机场。


现在完成时:
The constant use has clogged the engine. =  持续使用已经堵塞了发动机。
Years of neglect have clogged the pipes. = 多年的忽视已经堵塞了管道。


过去完成时:
The pipe had clogged before we even noticed. =  在我们注意到之前，管道就已经堵塞了。
The drain had clogged, causing a backup. = 排水管堵塞了，导致了堵塞。


将来时:
The predicted snowstorm will clog the highways. = 预计的暴风雪将堵塞高速公路。
Heavy rain will clog the drainage system. = 大雨将堵塞排水系统。


虚拟语气:
If the pipes clogged, we would have a problem. = 如果管道堵塞了，我们会遇到问题。
I wish the drain hadn't clogged. = 我真希望排水管没有堵塞。


被动语态:

The system was clogged by debris. = 系统被碎片堵塞了。


疑问句:
Is the sink clogged? = 水槽堵了吗？

否定句:
The pipes aren't clogged. = 管道没有堵塞。
The drain isn't clogged yet. = 排水管还没有堵塞。
