seismographic:/ˌsaɪzˈmɒɡræfɪk/ | adj. |地震仪的；地震图的 | Relating to or characteristic of a seismograph or seismogram.  The word describes something connected to the recording or study of earthquakes. | Example:  The seismographic readings showed a significant earthquake. 地震仪读数显示发生了强烈地震。  |近义词：seismic,earthquake-related |反义词：None.

Seismographic data helps scientists understand plate tectonics. = 地震数据帮助科学家了解板块构造。
