lordlet:/ˈlɔːdlət/ | n. | 小领主，小贵族 | A person of low rank in the nobility; a petty lord.  The word suggests someone with limited power and influence compared to a full lord. | Example: He was merely a lordlet, holding sway over a small, insignificant fiefdom. 他只是一个地位低下的领主，只统治着一个小而无关紧要的领地。 | 近义词：minor noble, petty lord | 反义词：king, emperor, sovereign
