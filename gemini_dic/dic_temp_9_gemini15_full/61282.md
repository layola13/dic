embalmed:/ɪmˈbɑːmd/ | vt. | 经过防腐处理的；保存完好的 | To preserve (a body) from decay by embalming.  This typically involves treating the body with chemicals to prevent decomposition. | 例句：The ancient Egyptian pharaohs were embalmed to preserve their bodies for the afterlife.  = 古埃及法老的遗体经过防腐处理以保存他们的遗体以备来世。

The body was carefully embalmed before the funeral. = 在葬礼之前，遗体被仔细地进行了防腐处理。


近义词：preserve, conserve | 反义词：decay, decompose | 词性: 动词
