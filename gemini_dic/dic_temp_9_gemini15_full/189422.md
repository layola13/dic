sailboard:/ˈseɪlbɔːrd/| n. |帆板；风帆滑板 | A sailboard is a board used for sailing on water, propelled by wind power using a sail.  It's also known as a windsurfer.  | Example: He learned to sailboard in the summer. 他在夏天学会了帆板运动。|近义词：windsurfer |反义词:None.
