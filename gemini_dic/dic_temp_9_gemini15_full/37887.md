complaining:/kəmˈpleɪnɪŋ/| v. |抱怨，投诉 |The act of expressing dissatisfaction or discontent, often about something that is wrong or unsatisfactory.  It can involve stating a grievance or making a formal protest. |例句：He spent the whole evening complaining about his job. 他整个晚上都在抱怨他的工作。

例句：She complained to the manager about the poor service. 她向经理投诉服务差。


近义词：grumbling, grumbling, protesting, objecting |反义词：praising, complimenting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is constantly complaining about the noise. = 他一直在抱怨噪音。
They complain about the long hours. = 他们抱怨工作时间太长。

一般过去时:
She complained to the teacher about the unfair test. = 她向老师抱怨考试不公平。
He complained that the food was cold. = 他抱怨食物是冷的。

现在完成时:
I have been complaining about this problem for weeks. = 我已经抱怨这个问题好几个星期了。
They have complained several times about the poor quality of the product. = 他们已经多次抱怨产品的质量差。

过去完成时:
He had complained numerous times before they finally took action. = 在他们最终采取行动之前，他已经抱怨过很多次了。
She had complained about the lack of support before quitting her job. = 在辞职之前，她已经抱怨过缺乏支持。

将来时:
I will complain to the authorities if the problem isn't solved. = 如果问题得不到解决，我将向有关部门投诉。
They will be complaining again if the service doesn't improve. = 如果服务没有改善，他们还会再次抱怨。

虚拟语气:
I wish he wouldn't complain so much. = 我希望他不要抱怨那么多。
If I were you, I wouldn't complain about such a small thing. = 如果我是你，我不会为这么小的事情抱怨。

被动语态:
The poor service was complained about by many customers. = 许多顾客都抱怨服务差。
The issue has been complained about repeatedly. = 这个问题已经被反复抱怨。

疑问句:
What are you complaining about? = 你在抱怨什么？
Why is he complaining? = 他为什么抱怨？

否定句:
He didn't complain about the long journey. = 他没有抱怨漫长的旅程。
She doesn't complain often. = 她不经常抱怨。


PHRASE->
complain about = 抱怨…
complain to sb about sth = 向某人抱怨某事
lodge a complain = 正式投诉
file a complain = 提交投诉
make a complain = 提出投诉
