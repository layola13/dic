compounds:/ˈkɒmpaʊndz/ /ˈkɑːmpaʊndz/| n.|化合物，复合词，复合体|A compound is a substance formed when two or more chemical elements are chemically bonded together.  It can also refer to a word formed by combining two or more words, or a thing made up of several parts.| Examples: Water (H₂O) is a compound of hydrogen and oxygen.  "Bookstore" is a compound word. The building is a compound of several smaller structures.|近义词：mixture, complex, composite|反义词：element, simple substance|名词
