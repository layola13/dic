moody:/ˈmuːdi/ | adj. | 喜怒无常的；阴沉的；忧郁的 |  Characterized by frequent changes of mood; sullen or gloomy.  | 例句：He's been rather moody lately.  他最近情绪不太稳定。

He was in a moody silence. 他沉默不语，显得闷闷不乐。

| 近义词：temperamental, sulky, sullen, glum, morose | 反义词：cheerful, sunny, happy, jovial | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is moody today. = 他今天情绪不好。
She is always moody in the afternoon. = 她下午总是情绪低落。


一般过去时:
He was moody yesterday. = 他昨天情绪不好。
She was moody after the argument. = 争吵后她情绪低落。


现在完成时:
He has been moody all week. = 他整个星期都情绪低落。
She has been moody since she lost her job. = 自从失业后，她就一直情绪低落。


过去完成时:
He had been moody for days before he finally snapped. = 他闷闷不乐了好几天，最后终于爆发了。
She had been moody all morning before the good news arrived. = 在好消息传来之前，她一上午都情绪低落。


将来时:
He will probably be moody again tomorrow. = 他明天可能还会情绪低落。
She will be moody if she doesn't get her way. = 如果她不能如愿以偿，她就会情绪低落。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so moody, he would have more friends. = 如果他不那么情绪化，他就会有更多朋友。
I wish she weren't so moody. = 我希望她不要那么情绪化。


被动语态:  (Moody is rarely used in the passive voice.)  


疑问句:
Is he moody today? = 他今天情绪不好吗？
Why is she always so moody? = 她为什么总是情绪低落？


否定句:
He is not usually moody. = 他通常不会情绪低落。
She wasn't moody at all during the party. = 她在派对上一点也不情绪低落。


PHRASE->
a moody teenager = 一个情绪化的青少年
moody blues = 忧郁的蓝调
in a moody state = 处于情绪低落的状态
moody atmosphere = 阴沉的气氛
a moody disposition = 喜怒无常的性情
