helter:/ˈheltər/| adj.|混乱的，杂乱的；仓促的 |A helter-skelter state or condition is one that is disorganized and chaotic.  It often implies a hurried or rushed manner.|例句：The aftermath of the storm left the town in a helter-skelter state.暴风雨过后，小镇一片混乱。|近义词：tumultuous, chaotic, disordered|反义词：orderly, organized, systematic|
