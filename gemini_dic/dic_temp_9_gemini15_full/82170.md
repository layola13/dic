grope:/ɡroʊp/| /ɡroʊp/ |v.|摸索， groping  |摸索；探索；尝试；性骚扰 |例句：He groped around in the dark for his glasses. 他在黑暗中摸索着找眼镜。|例句：She groped for the right words to express her feelings. 她努力寻找合适的词语来表达她的感受。|近义词：feel, fumble, search |反义词：see, find clearly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gropes for his keys in the dark. = 他在黑暗中摸索着找钥匙。
They grope for solutions to the problem. = 他们摸索着解决问题的办法。

一般过去时:
She groped for the light switch. = 她摸索着找电灯开关。
He groped his way through the dense fog. = 他在浓雾中摸索着前进。

现在完成时:
I have groped for understanding in this complex issue. = 我一直在努力理解这个复杂的问题。
They have groped their way to success. = 他们摸索着取得了成功。

过去完成时:
She had groped for her phone before realizing it was in her pocket. = 她摸索着找手机，然后才意识到它在口袋里。
He had groped around in the dark for a long time before finding the exit. = 他在黑暗中摸索了很久才找到出口。

将来时:
