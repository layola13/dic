digger:/ˈdɪɡər/| n. v.|挖掘者；挖掘机；（尤指）袋鼠；挖掘|A digger is a person or machine that digs.  The word often refers to a type of heavy machinery used for excavation, but can also refer to a person who digs or a marsupial, such as a kangaroo.|例句：The construction worker used a digger to create the foundation. 建筑工人用挖掘机挖地基。

例句：The kangaroo is a powerful digger, able to create burrows in hard earth. 袋鼠是强壮的挖掘者，能够在地面上挖洞穴。|近义词：excavator, excavator, burrower|反义词：filler, builder.
