detect:/dɪˈtɛkt/ | vt. | 发现；探测；查明 | To discover something not easily noticed, or to discover the presence or existence of something. | 例句：The police detected a small amount of explosives. 警方发现少量炸药。

The machine detected a fault in the engine. 机器探测到发动机故障。

近义词：discover, find, identify, discern | 反义词：miss, overlook, ignore | 词性：动词

We detect a problem with the system. = 我们发现系统有问题。

词源:
The word "detect" comes from the Latin word "detegere," meaning "to uncover" or "to expose."  The prefix "de-" means "off" or "away," and "-tegere" is related to the word "tegmen," meaning "covering."  Therefore, the original meaning implied removing a covering to reveal something hidden.
