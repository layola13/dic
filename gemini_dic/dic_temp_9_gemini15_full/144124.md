outsourcing:/ˈaʊtsɔːrsɪŋ/| n. |外包；外判|The process of contracting a task or function to an external provider.|例句：Many companies outsource their customer service to call centers in other countries.  许多公司将其客户服务外包给其他国家的呼叫中心。|例句：Outsourcing manufacturing has reduced costs for many businesses. 将制造业外包降低了许多企业的成本。|近义词：contracting out, subcontracting|反义词：insourcing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company outsources its IT services. = 这家公司将其IT服务外包。
We outsource our accounting to a specialist firm. = 我们将我们的会计工作外包给一家专业公司。

一般过去时:
They outsourced the project to a third-party vendor. = 他们将项目外包给了一个第三方供应商。
The company outsourced its production to China. = 这家公司将其生产外包到中国。

现在完成时:
We have outsourced several tasks this year. = 今年我们已经外包了几项任务。
They have outsourced their marketing efforts to a digital agency. = 他们已经将其营销工作外包给了一家数字代理公司。

过去完成时:
They had outsourced the software development before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经将软件开发外包出去了。
The company had outsourced its customer support before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经将其客户支持外包出去了。

将来时:
We will outsource the data entry work next month. = 我们下个月将把数据录入工作外包出去。
They will outsource their logistics to a more efficient provider. = 他们将把物流外包给一个更高效的供应商。

虚拟语气:
If we outsourced the project, we would save money. = 如果我们将项目外包出去，我们将节省资金。
It would be beneficial if we outsourced our recruitment. = 如果我们将招聘外包出去，将是有益的。

被动语态:
The work was outsourced to a cheaper supplier. = 这项工作被外包给了一个更便宜的供应商。
The project is being outsourced to a reputable company. = 该项目正在被外包给一家信誉良好的公司。

疑问句:
Will you outsource this job? = 你会把这份工作外包出去吗？
Is the company considering outsourcing its manufacturing? = 公司是否正在考虑将其制造业外包出去？

否定句:
We do not outsource our core business functions. = 我们不会将我们的核心业务职能外包出去。
The company did not outsource its research and development. = 这家公司没有将其研发工作外包出去。
