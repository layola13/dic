USAGE->
一般现在时:
The carpet covers the entire floor. = 地毯覆盖了整个地板。
We clean the carpets every week. = 我们每周都清洁地毯。


一般过去时:
They installed a new carpet in the living room. = 他们在客厅铺上了新的地毯。
She spilled coffee on the carpet. = 她把咖啡洒在地毯上了。


现在完成时:
We have just cleaned the carpets. = 我们刚清洁完地毯。
They have had that carpet for ten years. = 他们已经有那块地毯十年了。

将来时:
