radishes:/ˈrædɪʃɪz/| n.|小萝卜，萝卜|Small, typically red, round roots eaten raw as a salad vegetable.  They have a pungent, peppery taste.|例句：I added some sliced radishes to my salad. 我在我的沙拉里加了一些切好的小萝卜。|例句：The farmer grew a variety of radishes in his field. 农民在他的田里种植了各种各样的萝卜。|近义词：root vegetables, turnips (部分)|反义词: None (反义词的概念在此处不适用)|名词
