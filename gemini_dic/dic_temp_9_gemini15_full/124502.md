moulten:/ˈmoʊltən/| adj.|熔化的，熔解的|Molten describes something that has been melted by heat, typically a metal or other solid material.  It emphasizes the liquid state resulting from the melting process.|例句：The molten lava flowed down the volcano's side.  熔化的岩浆流下山坡。|例句：The goldsmith poured the molten gold into the mold.  金匠将熔化的黄金倒入模具中。|近义词：melted, fused, liquefied |反义词：solid, frozen, solidified
