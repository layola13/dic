growers:/ˈɡroʊərz/| n. |种植者，栽培者|People or companies that cultivate plants, especially on a large scale for commercial purposes. They are involved in the entire process from planting seeds or seedlings to harvesting and selling the produce.  |Example:  The apple growers are concerned about the late frost. 苹果种植者担心晚霜。

The orange growers are expecting a bumper crop this year. 橘子种植者预计今年将获得丰收。|近义词：cultivators, farmers, planters|反义词：consumers
