outwaving:/ˌaʊtˈweɪvɪŋ/| v.|超越；胜过；超过|The meaning of "outwaving" is less common and primarily refers to surpassing or exceeding something else, particularly in terms of wave-like motion or influence.  It implies a greater extent or intensity.|例句：The enthusiasm of the crowd outwaved the initial skepticism.  人群的热情盖过了最初的怀疑。The new campaign's impact outwaved that of the previous one. 新的活动的影响力超过了之前的活动。|近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, overshadowing|反义词：undermining, being surpassed by
