handkerchiefs:/ˈhæŋkətʃiːfs/| n. |手帕（复数）|Handkerchiefs are small squares of cloth or paper used to wipe the nose or eyes. They can be made of various materials like cotton, linen, or silk.|例句：She pulled out a handkerchief and dabbed her eyes. 她拿出了一块手帕擦了擦眼睛。

例句：He always carries a few handkerchiefs in his pocket. 他总是口袋里带着几块手帕。

反义词：无
