corona:/kəˈroʊnə/ /kəˈruːnə/| n. |冠，冕；日冕；光环；(Corona)人名；[植] 冠状病毒 |A corona is a crown-like structure, typically associated with the sun (solar corona) or a metaphorical representation of glory, power, or achievement.  It also refers to a type of virus. |例句：The sun's corona is visible during a solar eclipse. 日食期间可以看到太阳的日冕。|例句：The king wore a golden corona. 国王戴着金色的皇冠。|近义词：crown, halo, aureole |反义词：N/A
