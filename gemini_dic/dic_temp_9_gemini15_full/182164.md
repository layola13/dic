remodelling:/ˌriˈmɒdəlɪŋ/|/ˈriˌmɑːdəlɪŋ/ | n. | 改建；改型；翻新 | The process of altering or renovating something, especially a building or structure, to improve its appearance or functionality.  It involves significant changes to the existing design and structure. | Example: The remodelling of the old factory into a modern apartment complex was a huge undertaking.  老工厂改建成现代化的公寓大楼是一项巨大的工程。  The kitchen remodelling took longer than expected. 厨房改造比预期的要花更长时间。 |近义词：renovation, refurbishment, rebuilding, restructuring |反义词：demolition, destruction


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company is remodelling several of its stores. = 公司正在对其几家门店进行改建。
He is remodelling his bathroom. = 他正在改造他的浴室。

一般过去时:
They remodelled their house last year. = 他们去年翻修了他们的房子。
We remodelled the garden. = 我们重新设计了花园。

现在完成时:
She has remodelled her website. = 她已经重新设计了她的网站。
They have remodelled the entire building. = 他们已经改造了整栋楼。

过去完成时:

The company had remodelled its strategy before the crisis hit. = 公司在危机爆发前已经调整了战略。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
