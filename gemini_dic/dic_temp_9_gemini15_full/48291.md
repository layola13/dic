decreasingly:/dɪˈkriːsɪŋli/ | adv. | 逐渐减少地；越来越少地 | Gradually becoming less; to a progressively lesser extent.  | The population is decreasingly reliant on agriculture.  人口越来越少地依赖农业。 | increasingly | increasingly |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The demand for fossil fuels is decreasingly important. = 对化石燃料的需求越来越不重要。
His interest in the project is decreasingly apparent. = 他对这个项目的兴趣越来越不明显。


一般过去时:
The company's profits decreased increasingly last year. = 公司去年的利润越来越少了。(注意：例句使用了increasingly，反义词，为了说明用法对比)
His enthusiasm decreased increasingly as the project progressed. = 随着项目的进展，他的热情越来越低。


现在完成时:
The impact of the news has decreased increasingly over time. = 这则新闻的影响随着时间的推移越来越小。
The number of participants has decreased increasingly since the beginning. = 自开始以来，参与者的数量越来越少。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the effectiveness of the medicine had decreased increasingly. = 到年底，这种药的疗效越来越差了。
Before the intervention, their confidence had decreased increasingly. = 干预之前，他们的信心越来越低。


将来时:
The importance of this issue will decrease increasingly in the coming years. = 未来几年，这个问题的重要性将越来越低。
The effectiveness of the treatment will decrease increasingly with time. = 治疗的效果会随着时间的推移而越来越差。


虚拟语气:
If the funding decreased increasingly, the project would fail. = 如果资金越来越少，项目就会失败。
I wish the noise decreased increasingly. = 我希望噪音越来越小。


被动语态:
The support for the policy has been decreasing increasingly over the past few months. = 在过去的几个月里，对这项政策的支持越来越少。
The problem has been decreasing increasingly addressed by the government. = 这个问题越来越受到政府的重视。(注意：例句使用了increasingly addressed，为了说明用法对比，并非完全被动语态)


疑问句:
Is the demand for this product decreasing increasingly? = 对这个产品的需求是否越来越少？
Are the effects of the drug decreasing increasingly? = 药物的效果是否越来越差？


否定句:
The problem is not decreasing increasingly; it's getting worse. = 问题并没有越来越少；它正在恶化。
The pollution level is not decreasing increasingly; it remains high. = 污染水平并没有越来越少；它仍然很高。


PHRASE->
decreasingly less: 越来越少
decreasingly important: 越来越不重要
decreasingly effective: 越来越无效
decreasingly popular: 越来越不受欢迎
decreasingly frequent: 越来越不频繁
