preconfessing: /ˌpriːkənˈfɛsɪŋ/ | v. | 预先承认；事先坦白 |  The act of confessing something before it is discovered or asked for.  It implies a proactive admission of guilt or wrongdoing, often to mitigate potential consequences. |


例句：

He preconfessed his involvement in the scheme to avoid further investigation. = 他预先承认参与了该计划以避免进一步调查。

She preconfessed her mistakes to her boss, hoping for leniency. = 她向老板预先承认了自己的错误，希望能得到宽大处理。


近义词：anticipatory confession, preemptive confession

反义词：concealing, hiding, denying


词性:动词
