prerighteously:/ˌpriːˈraɪtʃəsli/| adv. |自以为义地，假道学地 |Acting in a self-righteous or sanctimonious manner;  displaying excessive moral superiority.  |例句：He prerighteously condemned the actions of the other drivers, ignoring his own reckless driving. 他自以为义地谴责其他司机的行为，却忽略了自己的鲁莽驾驶。

例句：She prerighteously lectured me on the importance of charity, despite her own stinginess. 她自以为义地教训我慈善的重要性，尽管她自己很吝啬。


近义词：self-righteously, sanctimoniously, hypocritically, holily


反义词： humbly, modestly, unassumingly


词性: 副词
