dehumidifier:/diːˈhjuːmɪdaɪfər/| n. |除湿机|A dehumidifier is an appliance that removes moisture from the air.  It works by drawing in humid air, cooling it below its dew point, and then collecting the condensed water.  The dry air is then released back into the room. |例句：We bought a dehumidifier to combat the dampness in our basement. 我们买了一台除湿机来解决地下室潮湿的问题。

例句：The dehumidifier runs constantly during the rainy season. 雨季除湿机一直开着。


近义词：humidistat (湿度控制器, indirectly), dryer (干燥器，范围更广)|反义词：humidifier (加湿器)|词性:名词

词源:
The word "dehumidifier" is a compound word.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "to remove or reduce," "humid" refers to moisture or humidity, and "-ifier" is a suffix indicating a device or agent that performs a specific action.  Therefore, a dehumidifier is a device that removes humidity.
