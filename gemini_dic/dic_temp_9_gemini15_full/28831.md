carpals:/ˈkɑːrpəlz/ | n. |腕骨 |The carpals are the eight small bones that make up the wrist. They are arranged in two rows, and they articulate with the radius and ulna of the forearm and the metacarpals of the hand.  |例句：The fracture involved several carpals in her left wrist. 她左腕的骨折涉及几块腕骨。|  The doctor examined the patient's carpals for signs of injury. 医生检查了病人的腕骨是否有受伤迹象。|近义词：wrist bones|反义词：无
