It refers to the angle between two lines or surfaces that is not a right angle (90 degrees). | Example: The nonperpendicularity of the walls caused structural problems.  非垂直的墙壁导致了结构问题。The slight nonperpendicularity of the lines was barely noticeable.  线条轻微的非垂直性几乎难以察觉。| 近义词: obliqueness, slant, deviation from perpendicularity | 反义词: perpendicularity
