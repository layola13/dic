disimmure:/ˌdɪsɪˈmjʊər/| vt.|使…显露；使…公开；摆脱隐藏|To remove from concealment; to uncover; to disclose. The act of disimmurring something is the process of bringing it out from a hidden or secluded place and making it visible or known.  This often implies a previous state of confinement or secrecy.|例句：The archaeologist carefully disimmured the ancient artifact from its earthen tomb. 考古学家小心翼翼地将古代文物从泥土坟墓中挖掘出来。

The artist disimmured her masterpiece after years of keeping it hidden.  艺术家在多年隐瞒之后公开了她的杰作。


近义词：uncover, reveal, expose, disclose, unearth|反义词：conceal, hide, bury, secrete, obscure|动词
