freshest: /ˈfreʃɪst/ | adj. | 最新鲜的，最新鲜的 |  The most fresh;  possessing the highest degree of freshness. This word is the superlative form of the adjective "fresh." It implies a superior level of freshness compared to other items or instances.  | 例句：The freshest fruit is always found at the farmer's market. 最新鲜的水果总是在农贸市场能找到。

The bakery sells the freshest bread in town. 这家面包店卖城里最新鲜的面包。


近义词：newest, most recent, most pristine | 反义词：oldest, stale, spoiled, rotten | 词性：形容词
