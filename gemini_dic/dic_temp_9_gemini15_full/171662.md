pulsing:/ˈpʌlsɪŋ/| adj.|跳动的；脉冲的；有节奏地跳动的|Characterized by or producing a rhythmic beat or pulsation; throbbing.  The pulsing light of the disco ball filled the room.  |近义词：throbbing, beating, throbbing, vibrating|反义词：still, stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The light is pulsing rhythmically. = 灯光有节奏地跳动着。
His heart is pulsing rapidly. = 他的心跳很快。


一般过去时:
The engine pulsed powerfully before it stalled. = 发动机在熄火前强劲地跳动着。
The ground pulsed beneath our feet. = 地面在我们脚下震动。


现在完成时:
The pain has been pulsing in my head all day. = 我头痛了一整天，痛感一阵一阵的。
The city has pulsed with life and energy for centuries. = 这个城市几个世纪以来一直充满活力。


过去完成时:
The machine had pulsed erratically before it finally stopped. = 机器在最终停止前一直不稳定地跳动着。
My heart had pulsed with excitement before the race. = 比赛前我的心跳加速。


将来时:
The lights will pulse to the music. = 灯光将随着音乐节奏跳动。
The city will pulse with activity during the festival. = 节日期间，城市将充满活力。


虚拟语气:
If the machine were pulsing correctly, it would work. = 如果机器正常跳动，它就能工作。
I wish my heart wasn't pulsing so fast. = 我希望我的心跳不要这么快。


被动语态:
The signal was pulsed repeatedly. = 信号被重复地脉冲发送。
The light is pulsed by an electronic device. = 灯光由电子设备控制脉冲。


疑问句:
Is the light pulsing? = 灯光在跳动吗？
Is your heart pulsing too fast? = 你的心跳太快了吗？


否定句:
The machine isn't pulsing. = 机器没有跳动。
The light doesn't pulse steadily. = 灯光不稳定地跳动。


PHRASE->
pulsing light = 跳动的灯光
pulsing pain = 一阵一阵的疼痛
pulsing energy = 充满活力的能量
pulsing rhythm = 跳动的节奏
pulsing beat = 跳动的心跳


词源:
From the verb "pulse," ultimately from Latin "pulsare" (to beat, strike).  The adjective form "pulsing" developed naturally to describe something exhibiting the action of pulsing.
