for-all-intents-and-purposes:/fɔːr ɔːl ɪnˈtents ænd ˈpɜːrpəsɪz/| adj. phrase|实际上；事实上；大体上；名义上|This phrase means "in every practical sense" or "virtually".  It indicates that something is true or accurate enough for all practical purposes, even if not completely or technically true.|例句：For all intents and purposes, the project is finished. = 实际上，这个项目已经完成了。

例句：For all intents and purposes, she’s retired, although she still does some consulting work. = 虽然她还在做一些咨询工作，但实际上她已经退休了。


近义词：effectively, practically, virtually, essentially, in effect

反义词：theoretically, nominally, technically

词性:形容词短语 (Adjective Phrase)
