pros:/prɒz/|ˈproʊz/| n.|优点，好处；赞成；正面评价|advantages, benefits, strengths, pluses; arguments or opinions in favor of something; favorable comments or reviews|The pros of working from home include flexibility and less commuting.  在家工作的优点包括灵活性和减少通勤。|The pros and cons of the proposal need to be carefully considered.  这项提案的利弊需要仔细权衡。|cons, disadvantages, drawbacks|


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The pros outweigh the cons in this case. = 在这种情况下，优点超过缺点。
What are the pros of this new technology? = 这项新技术的优点是什么？


一般过去时:
The pros of the old system were clear. = 旧系统的优点很明显。
He listed the pros and cons before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前列出了利弊。


现在完成时:
We have weighed the pros and cons carefully. = 我们已经仔细权衡了利弊。
The company has already highlighted the pros of their new product. = 公司已经强调了其新产品的优点。


过去完成时:
They had already considered the pros and cons before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经考虑了利弊。
She had listed all the pros before presenting her argument. = 她在陈述论点之前已经列出了所有优点。


将来时:
The project will have many pros for the community. = 该项目将为社区带来许多好处。
We will discuss the pros and cons of the different options. = 我们将讨论不同选择的利弊。


虚拟语气:
If the pros were more significant, we would have proceeded. = 如果优点更显著，我们就会继续进行。
I wish the pros of this plan were clearer. = 我希望这个计划的优点更清晰。


被动语态:
The pros of the new policy were widely publicized. = 新政策的优点被广泛宣传。
The pros and cons are being carefully analyzed. = 利弊正在被仔细分析。


疑问句:
What are the pros of this approach? = 这种方法的优点是什么？
What were the main pros of the previous design? = 以前设计的优点是什么？


否定句:
There were few, if any, pros to the plan. = 这个计划几乎没有优点。
The disadvantages far outweighed the few pros. = 缺点远远超过了为数不多的优点。
