sickling:/ˈsɪklɪŋ/| n. v.|镰刀状细胞形成；镰刀状；|The process by which red blood cells assume a sickle shape, characteristic of sickle cell anemia.|例句：Sickling of red blood cells can lead to vaso-occlusion. 镰刀状红细胞可导致血管阻塞。|近义词：sickle-cell formation|反义词:normal red blood cell shape
