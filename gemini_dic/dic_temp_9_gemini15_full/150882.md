parakite:/ˈpærəkaɪt/| n. |滑翔伞；动力伞|A parakite is a type of kite that is used for recreational flying.  It is typically larger and more robust than a standard kite, and often incorporates a control system that allows the pilot to steer it.  Parakites can be flown solo or in tandem, and are capable of carrying a significant amount of weight.|例句：He took his parakite to the beach and spent the afternoon soaring through the air. 他带着他的滑翔伞去海滩，下午在空中翱翔。|近义词：kite, paraglider|反义词:|无|名词
