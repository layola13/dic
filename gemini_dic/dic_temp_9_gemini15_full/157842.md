plagiostomatous: /ˌplædʒiˈɒstəməs/ | adj. | 具有板鳃的；软骨鱼类的 |  Having or relating to placoid scales (dermal denticles).  The term refers to cartilaginous fishes, such as sharks, rays, and chimaeras, characterized by their placoid scales. | Example:  Plagiostomatous fishes are known for their cartilaginous skeletons and placoid scales.  = 软骨鱼类以其软骨骨骼和盾鳞而闻名。 | 近义词: cartilaginous (referring to skeleton) | 反义词: osteichthyan (referring to bony fish)
