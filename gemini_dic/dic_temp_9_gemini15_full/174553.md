quintuplets:/ˈkwɪntʌplɛts/| n. |五胞胎|Five babies born at the same time to the same mother.  Quintuplets are a rare occurrence. |The McCaughey septuplets were born in 1997, but the Dionne quintuplets, born in 1934, were the first set to survive infancy.|近义词：multiple birth, five babies|反义词：single birth
