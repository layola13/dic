pleuro:/ˈplʊəroʊ/| adj. n.|肋骨的；胸膜的；侧面的| Relating to the pleura, a serous membrane that lines the chest cavity and covers the lungs.  The term is often used in anatomical and medical contexts. |例句：Pleural effusion is a buildup of fluid in the pleural space.  胸腔积液是胸膜腔积液的积聚。|近义词：costal, thoracic |反义词：无
