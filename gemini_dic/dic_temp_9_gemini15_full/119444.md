microlith:/ˈmaɪkrəʊlɪθ/| n. |微石器|A small stone tool, typically less than 2.5 centimeters in length, characteristic of the Mesolithic period.  Microliths were often hafted onto larger tools or weapons.|例句：The archaeologist carefully examined the microliths found at the dig site. 考古学家仔细检查了在挖掘现场发现的微石器。 The discovery of numerous microliths indicated a significant Mesolithic settlement.  大量微石器的发现表明这是一个重要的中石器时代聚落。|近义词：small stone tool, flake|反义词：megalith
