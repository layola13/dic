concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ | vt. |调制；编造；策划 | to make or prepare a mixture or dish, especially one in a complicated way; to invent or devise something, typically a story or plan. |例句：She concocted a delicious meal from the few ingredients she had. 她用仅有的几样食材调制出一顿美味佳肴。


She concocted a story to explain her lateness. 她编造了一个故事来解释她迟到的原因。|近义词：brew, devise, fabricate, invent |反义词：destroy, demolish, undo


USAGE->
