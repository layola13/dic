raiders:/ˈreɪdərz/| n.|袭击者，侵略者，海盗|People who raid or attack a place, often violently, to steal something.  They can be pirates, bandits, or even groups engaged in military actions. |例句：The raiders stole all the gold from the village.  袭击者从村庄偷走了所有的金子。

例句：Viking raiders terrorized the coastal towns. 维京海盗袭击了沿海城镇。


近义词：attackers, invaders, plunderers, bandits, pirates


反义词：defenders, protectors


词性:名词
