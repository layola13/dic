registers:/ˈrɛdʒɪstərz/| n. v.|登记；注册；记录；寄存器|The word "registers" can function as both a noun and a verb. As a noun, it refers to a record or list of names, items, or events.  As a verb, it means to record or enter something into a register. |例句：The hotel registers all its guests. (名词) 旅馆登记所有入住的客人。The students registered for the course last week. (动词) 学生们上周注册了这门课程。|近义词：records, enrollments, entries |反义词：deregisters, unregisters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school registers all new students. = 学校登记所有新生。
He registers his car every year. = 他每年登记他的车。


一般过去时:
She registered for the marathon last month. = 她上个月报名参加了马拉松比赛。
They registered their complaint with the manager. = 他们向经理登记了他们的投诉。


现在完成时:
I have registered for the online course. = 我已经注册了在线课程。

过去完成时:
They had registered their protest before the meeting started. = 他们会议开始前就已登记了他们的抗议。
She had registered her interest in the position months ago. = 她几个月前就对这个职位表示了兴趣。


将来时:

They will register their votes in the upcoming election. = 他们将在即将到来的选举中登记投票。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had registered for that workshop. = 我真希望我报名参加了那个研讨会。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
