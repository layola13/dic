compress:/kəmˈprɛs/ | vt. n.|压缩，浓缩；压紧|To reduce in size or volume; to condense.|例句：The data was compressed before being sent. 数据在发送前被压缩了。|例句：The sponge compressed easily. 这块海绵很容易压缩。|近义词：condense, squeeze, contract|反义词：expand, enlarge
