pay-down:/ˈpeɪ daʊn/| v.|偿还（债务）, 减少（数量）|To reduce a debt or amount gradually by making regular payments. |例句：We're working hard to pay down our mortgage. 我们正在努力偿还抵押贷款。|例句：The company is paying down its inventory.  公司正在减少库存。|近义词：reduce, decrease, diminish|反义词：increase, accrue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pay down their debt each month. = 他们每个月都偿还债务。
The company pays down its inventory gradually. = 公司逐渐减少库存。


一般过去时:
We paid down a significant portion of our loan last year. = 去年我们偿还了一大部分贷款。
She paid down her credit card debt diligently. = 她勤奋地偿还了信用卡债务。


现在完成时:
They have paid down most of their student loans. = 他们已经偿还了大部分学生贷款。
The government has paid down the national debt slightly. = 政府略微减少了国债。


过去完成时:
Before the sale, they had paid down their inventory to a manageable level. = 在出售之前，他们已将库存减少到可控水平。
By the end of the year, they had paid down a considerable amount of their debt. = 到年底，他们已经偿还了相当数量的债务。


将来时:
We will pay down our car loan over the next five years. = 我们将在未来五年内偿还汽车贷款。
The company plans to pay down its debt significantly next quarter. = 公司计划在下个季度大幅减少债务。


虚拟语气:
If they had paid down their debt earlier, they would be in a better financial position. = 如果他们早点偿还债务，他们的财务状况就会更好。
I wish I had paid down my credit card debt sooner. = 我真希望我早点偿还信用卡债务。


被动语态:
The debt is being paid down slowly but surely. = 债务正在缓慢但肯定地被偿还。
The inventory is being paid down through increased sales. = 库存通过增加销售额而减少。


疑问句:
How long will it take to pay down the mortgage? = 偿还抵押贷款需要多长时间？
Are they paying down their debt effectively? = 他们是否有效地偿还债务？


否定句:
They did not pay down their debt as quickly as they had hoped. = 他们没有像希望的那样快速偿还债务。
The company hasn't paid down its debt enough to meet its targets. = 公司减少的债务不足以达到其目标。


PHRASE->
pay down a loan = 偿还贷款
pay down debt = 偿还债务
pay down a mortgage = 偿还抵押贷款
pay down inventory = 减少库存
pay down the principal = 偿还本金
