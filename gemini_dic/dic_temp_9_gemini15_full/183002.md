replete:/rɪˈpliːt/| adj.| 充满的；装满的；丰富的 | full or well-supplied with something.  |The basket was replete with fresh fruit.  篮子里装满了新鲜水果。| abundant, full, brimming | empty, deficient, lacking |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The barn is replete with hay. = 谷仓里装满了干草。
The garden is replete with colorful flowers. = 花园里充满了五颜六色的花朵。

一般过去时:
The room was replete with antique furniture. = 房间里摆满了古董家具。
Her speech was replete with interesting anecdotes. = 她的演讲充满了有趣的轶事。

现在完成时:
The coffers have been replete with donations. = 国库已装满了捐款。
My heart has been replete with joy. = 我的心充满了喜悦。

过去完成时:
The well had been replete with water before the drought. = 干旱之前，井里曾装满了水。
The store had been replete with customers before the sale. = 销售之前，商店里曾挤满了顾客。

将来时:
The stadium will be replete with cheering fans. = 体育场将挤满欢呼的球迷。
The shelves will be replete with new books next week. = 下周货架上将摆满新书。

虚拟语气:
If the bowl were replete with cherries, I would be very happy. = 如果碗里装满了樱桃，我会非常高兴。
I wish my life were replete with adventure. = 我希望我的生活充满冒险。

被动语态:
The document was replete with technical jargon. = 这份文件充满了专业术语。
The report was replete with statistical data. = 报告中充满了统计数据。

疑问句:

Is the jar replete with homemade jam? = 罐子里装满了自制果酱吗？

否定句:
The cupboard is not replete with food. = 食品柜里没有装满食物。
The explanation was not replete with details. = 解释中缺乏细节。
