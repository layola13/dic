prated:/ˈpreɪtɪd/| v.|喋喋不休地谈论，夸夸其谈 |To talk at length about something, especially in a way that is dull or boring.  |例句：He prated endlessly about his achievements. 他没完没了地夸耀他的成就。

例句：She prated on about her trip to Europe, boring everyone to tears. 她喋喋不休地谈论她的欧洲之旅，把大家都烦死了。


近义词：babbled, chattered, gabbled, rambled


反义词：kept silent, remained mum


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prates about politics all day. = 他整天都在大谈政治。
She prates incessantly about her children. = 她没完没了地谈论她的孩子。


一般过去时:
He prated about his new car for an hour. = 他夸耀他的新车夸了一个小时。
She prated on about her vacation until everyone fell asleep. = 她一直喋喋不休地讲她的假期，直到大家都睡着了。


现在完成时:
He has prated about this subject many times. = 他已经多次谈论过这个话题了。
She has prated endlessly about her problems. = 她没完没了地抱怨她的问题。


过去完成时:
He had prated for hours before anyone stopped him. = 他已经说了好几个小时，才有人阻止他。
She had prated about her success before we even began the meeting. = 在我们开始会议之前，她就一直在夸耀她的成功。


将来时:
He will prate about his accomplishments at the ceremony. = 他将在仪式上夸耀他的成就。
She will prate endlessly during the dinner party. = 她将在晚宴上喋喋不休。


虚拟语气:
If he prated less, people would listen more. = 如果他少说一些，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't prate so much. = 我希望他不那么喋喋不休。


被动语态:
His achievements were prated about by the media. = 他的成就被媒体大肆宣传。
Her problems were prated about by her friends. = 她的问题被她的朋友们喋喋不休地谈论着。


疑问句:
Does he often prate about his work? = 他经常夸耀他的工作吗？
Will she prate about her trip again? = 她还会再谈论她的旅行吗？


否定句:
He didn't prate about his failures. = 他没有谈论他的失败。
She didn't prate during the important meeting. = 她在重要的会议上没有喋喋不休。



PHRASE->
prate on = 喋喋不休
prate endlessly = 没完没了地谈论
prate about = 谈论…
prate nonsense = 说废话
prate away = 滔滔不绝地说


词源:
The word "prate" comes from the Middle English word "praten," which in turn has roots in the Old French word "parler" meaning "to speak."  Over time, the meaning shifted from simply "to speak" to its current connotation of idle or excessive talk.
