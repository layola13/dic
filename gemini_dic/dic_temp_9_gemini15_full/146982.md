overjudicious: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒuːdɪʃəs/ | adj. | 过分谨慎的；过于审慎的 | Excessively cautious or careful; showing excessive prudence.  It implies an over-emphasis on caution to the point of hindering action or progress. |The judge's overjudicious approach delayed the trial.  法官过于谨慎的做法拖延了审判。  His overjudicious nature prevented him from taking risks necessary for business success. 他过分谨慎的性格妨碍了他冒险取得商业成功的必要性。 |近义词：overcautious, excessively careful, scrupulous (to a fault) |反义词：reckless, impetuous, rash
