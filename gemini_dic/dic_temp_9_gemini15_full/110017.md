libratory:/laɪˈbrɛəri/| adj.|图书馆的；书房的|relating to a library or libraries; of or pertaining to a library or study.|The libratory resources were extensive. 图书馆的资源非常丰富。|近义词：library, bookish|反义词：unlearned, ignorant
