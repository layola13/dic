quasi-calm: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ kɑːm/ | adj. | 几乎平静的；相对平静的 |  Relatively calm; seemingly calm but potentially unstable.  It suggests a state of calmness that is not entirely complete or stable, hinting at underlying tension or potential for disruption. |例句：The situation remained quasi-calm for a few hours before the protests erupted again.  情况在抗议再次爆发前的几个小时里保持着相对平静。

The sea remained quasi-calm after the storm, with a slight swell still present. 风暴过后，大海依然相对平静，但仍有轻微的涌动。|近义词：tranquil, placid, still, peaceful |反义词：turbulent, chaotic, stormy, agitated
