pluviometer:/pluːˈvɪɒmɪtər/| n. |雨量器，测雨器|A pluviometer is an instrument used to measure the amount of rainfall over a period of time. It typically consists of a container with a known cross-sectional area that collects rainwater, allowing the depth of rainfall to be calculated.|The pluviometer recorded 2 inches of rain in the last 24 hours. 雨量器记录了过去24小时下了2英寸的雨。|rain gauge, ombrometer|  |n.
