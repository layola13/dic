impious:/ˈɪmpiəs/| adj.|不虔诚的；不敬神的；不孝的|lacking piety or reverence for God; irreverent; disrespectful towards God or sacred things.  It often implies a deliberate or defiant disregard for religious or moral principles.|例句：His impious remarks offended many worshippers.  他的不敬言论冒犯了许多信徒。

例句：The impious king ignored the warnings of the priests.  这位不虔诚的国王无视了祭司的警告。


近义词：ungodly, irreverent, profane, sacrilegious, godless, blasphemous

反义词：pious, devout, reverent, holy


词源：源自于拉丁语“impius”，由“in-”(否定前缀)和“pius”(虔诚的)构成。
