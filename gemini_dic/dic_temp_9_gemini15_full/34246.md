clap-stick:/klæpˌstɪk/| n.|拍打棒，拍板|A clap-stick is a percussion instrument consisting of two sticks that are clapped together to produce a rhythmic sound. It is often used in traditional music and dance.  |Example: The dancers used clap-sticks to keep the rhythm of their performance.  舞者用拍打棒来保持他们表演的节奏。|近义词：rhythm stick, percussion stick |反义词：无
