self-helper:/ˈsɛlf ˈhɛlpər/| n. |自助者，自助人士|A person who relies on their own efforts and resources to achieve their goals, often without seeking external help.  This term often refers to someone who uses self-help techniques or resources to improve their mental or physical well-being.|例句：Many self-helpers find success through consistent effort and self-discipline. 许多自助者通过持续的努力和自律获得成功。  She's a true self-helper; she overcame her challenges without relying on anyone else. 她是一个真正的自助者；她在没有依赖任何人的情况下克服了她的挑战。|近义词：self-reliant, independent, autonomous|反义词：dependent, reliant
