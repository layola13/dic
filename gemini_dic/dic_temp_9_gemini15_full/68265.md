fake:/feɪk/| adj., n., v.|假的；伪造；伪装|Something fake is not genuine; it is an imitation or a copy.  It can also refer to someone who is deceitful or dishonest.  As a verb, it means to create something fake or to pretend.|例句：The painting was a fake.  这幅画是假的。

That diamond is a fake.  那颗钻石是假的。


近义词：false, artificial, counterfeit, sham, bogus, phony, imitation|反义词：real, genuine, authentic, true|词性：adj., n., v.
