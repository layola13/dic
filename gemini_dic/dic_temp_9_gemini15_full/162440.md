preadmitted:/ˌpriːədˈmɪtɪd/| adj.|预先录取的，预先准许的|Pre-admitted refers to having been accepted or admitted beforehand, typically to a program, institution, or event.  It implies prior approval or acceptance.|例句：He was pre-admitted to the university's summer program. 他已被预先录取到大学的暑期项目。|例句：The pre-admitted students received early access to orientation materials. 预先录取的学生提前获得了迎新材料。|近义词：pre-approved, accepted, admitted beforehand|反义词：rejected, denied
