condemn:/kənˈdem/| vt.|谴责，判刑，宣告…有罪|to express strong disapproval of; to sentence to punishment|例句：The government condemned the attack.  政府谴责了这次袭击。

例句：The judge condemned him to life imprisonment. 法官判处他无期徒刑。

近义词：criticize, denounce, censure, convict|反义词：praise, commend, approve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The council condemns the proposed development.  市议会谴责拟议中的开发项目。
He condemns violence in all its forms. 他谴责一切形式的暴力。


一般过去时:
The court condemned him to death. 法院判他死刑。
They condemned the actions of the police. 他们谴责了警方的行为。


现在完成时:
The international community has condemned the use of chemical weapons. 国际社会已谴责使用化学武器。
The media has condemned the company's unethical practices. 媒体谴责了该公司的违反道德的行为。


过去完成时:
The judge had already condemned him before the evidence was presented.  在证据出示之前，法官已经判了他罪。
The public had condemned the policy long before it was repealed. 公众早在政策被废除之前就谴责了它。


将来时:
The government will condemn the act of terrorism. 政府将谴责恐怖主义行为。
The world will condemn this injustice. 世界将谴责这种不公正行为。


虚拟语气:
If they knew the truth, they would condemn his actions. 如果他们知道真相，他们会谴责他的行为。
I wish the world condemned such cruelty more often. 我希望世界能更频繁地谴责这种残酷行为。


被动语态:
His actions were condemned by many. 他的行为受到许多人的谴责。
The plan was condemned as impractical.  该计划因不切实际而遭到谴责。


疑问句:
Does the public condemn this decision? 公众是否谴责这项决定？
Will the court condemn him to prison? 法院会判他入狱吗？


否定句:
They did not condemn the protesters. 他们没有谴责抗议者。
The judge did not condemn him to death. 法官没有判他死刑。


PHRASE->
condemn to death: 判处死刑
condemn someone to:  判处某人…
condemn something as:  谴责某事为…
condemn sb/sth outright:  公开谴责某人/某事
condemn sb/sth implicitly:  含蓄地谴责某人/某事

词源:
源自中古英语 condemn，源自古法语 condamner，源自拉丁语 con- ("together") + damnare ("to judge, condemn").  字面意思为“一起判断”。
