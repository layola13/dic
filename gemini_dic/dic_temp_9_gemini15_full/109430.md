leopardess:/ˈlepərdɛs/| n.|雌豹|A female leopard.  The word is formed by adding the feminine suffix "-ess" to "leopard".  It specifically refers to a female of the leopard species, Panthera pardus.|例句：The leopardess stalked her prey with deadly grace.  这只雌豹优雅地潜伏着，准备捕猎。|例句：We spotted a leopardess and her cubs near the watering hole. 我们在水坑附近看到了一只雌豹和她的幼崽。|近义词：female leopard|反义词：leopard (male)|名词
