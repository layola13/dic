nonreactionary:/ˌnɒnˌriˈækʃənəri/ | adj. |非反动的；进步的 | Not reactionary; progressive or liberal in outlook.  It describes someone or something that does not oppose progress or reform, and may even actively support it. The term emphasizes a lack of opposition to change, unlike "reactionary" which implies active resistance to change. |The government's policies were described as nonreactionary, aiming for gradual social and economic improvements.  政府的政策被描述为非反动的，旨在逐步改善社会和经济状况。| progressive, liberal, forward-looking | reactionary, conservative, traditional |
