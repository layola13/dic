scaffoldings:/ˈskæfəʊldɪŋz/ | n. | 脚手架；支架；临时结构 | Temporary structures, usually made of wood or metal poles and planks, used to support workers and materials during construction or repairs.  Scaffoldings provide a temporary platform for accessing high places. | The workers erected scaffolding around the building before starting repairs. 工人们在开始修理之前在建筑物周围搭起了脚手架。


The scaffolding was removed once the painting was finished. 油漆完成后，脚手架就拆除了。 | staging, framework | ground, foundation | 名词
