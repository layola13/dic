run-ups:/ˈrʌnʌp/| n.|预备阶段；准备工作；助跑；增长；上涨|The period of time leading up to an important event or action.  It can also refer to the act of running up to something, or a period of growth or increase.|例句：The run-up to the election was filled with intense campaigning. 选举前的准备阶段充满了激烈的竞选活动。

例句：The athlete took a long run-up before his jump.  运动员在跳跃前做了很长的助跑。|近义词：preparation, buildup, approach, prelude|反义词：conclusion, aftermath, decline|词性:名词
