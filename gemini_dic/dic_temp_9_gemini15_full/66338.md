exculpated:/ˈɛksʌlpeɪtɪd/| vt.|宣告无罪；开脱；辩护|To clear from blame or guilt; to exonerate.  The evidence exculpated the suspect. 证据证明嫌疑人无罪。|近义词：acquit, exonerate, absolve|反义词：convict, incriminate, inculpate|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The court exculpates innocent people. = 法院宣告无辜者无罪。
He exculpates himself from all responsibility. = 他为自己开脱一切责任。


一般过去时:
The jury exculpated the defendant. = 陪审团宣告被告无罪。
The new evidence exculpated her from any wrongdoing. = 新证据证明她没有做错任何事。


现在完成时:
The investigation has exculpated the mayor. = 调查已经证明市长无罪。
They have exculpated themselves through their testimony. = 他们通过证词为自己辩护。


过去完成时:
The lawyer had exculpated his client before the trial. = 律师在审判前已经为他的当事人辩护。
It was revealed that the police had exculpated the real culprit. = 据透露，警方已经为真正的罪犯开脱。


将来时:
The judge will exculpate the accused if the evidence supports it. = 如果证据支持，法官将宣告被告无罪。
We believe the new findings will exculpate him completely. = 我们相信新的发现将完全为他洗清罪名。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, the court would exculpate him. = 如果证据更充分，法院就会宣告他无罪。
I wish the investigation had exculpated him sooner. = 我希望调查能早点为他洗清罪名。


被动语态:
He was exculpated after a long and difficult trial. = 在漫长而艰难的审判后，他被宣告无罪。
The suspect was exculpated by the lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，嫌疑人被宣告无罪。


疑问句:
Will the court exculpate the defendant? = 法院会宣告被告无罪吗？
Has the investigation exculpated all the suspects? = 调查是否已经为所有嫌疑人洗清了罪名？


否定句:
The court did not exculpate the accused. = 法院没有宣告被告无罪。
The evidence failed to exculpate him completely. = 证据未能完全为他洗清罪名。


PHRASE->
be exculpated from = 从...中被免除罪责
exculpate oneself = 为自己辩护
exculpate someone of = 为某人开脱某事
fully exculpated = 完全被宣告无罪
successfully exculpated = 成功地被宣告无罪
