ledgement:/ˈlɛdʒmənt/| n.|承认；认可；声明；题词|The act of acknowledging something, or a statement acknowledging something.  It can also refer to an inscription or endorsement.|例句：The acknowledgement of his contribution was long overdue.  他的贡献得到认可已经很久了。

例句：The document bears the ledgement of the witness. 这份文件有证人的签署。


近义词：acknowledgment, recognition, affirmation, avowal, declaration|反义词：denial, refutation, disavowal|词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acknowledges his mistake. = 他承认自己的错误。
She affirms her commitment. = 她肯定她的承诺。


一般过去时:
They acknowledged the receipt of the goods. = 他们承认收到了货物。
He affirmed his loyalty to the king. = 他肯定他对国王的忠诚。


现在完成时:
She has acknowledged her debt to her teachers. = 她已经承认了她对老师的感激之情。
They have affirmed their support for the project. = 他们已经肯定了他们对这个项目的支持。


过去完成时:
He had acknowledged his error before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经承认了他的错误。
She had affirmed her belief in God. = 她已经肯定了她对上帝的信仰。


将来时:
He will acknowledge his responsibility. = 他将会承认他的责任。
She will affirm her intention to leave. = 她将肯定她离开的意图。


虚拟语气:
If he acknowledged his fault, he would be forgiven. = 如果他承认他的错误，他就会被原谅。
If she affirmed her love, he would be happy. = 如果她肯定她的爱，他会很高兴。


被动语态:
His contribution was acknowledged by the committee. = 他的贡献得到了委员会的认可。
Her loyalty was affirmed by the government. = 她的忠诚得到了政府的肯定。


疑问句:
Did he acknowledge his mistake? = 他承认他的错误了吗？
Will she affirm her commitment? = 她会肯定她的承诺吗？


否定句:
He did not acknowledge the letter. = 他没有承认这封信。
She did not affirm her allegiance. = 她没有肯定她的效忠。


PHRASE->
in acknowledgement of = 为了表彰；以示答谢
acknowledge receipt of = 确认收到
ledgement of receipt = 收据
make acknowledgement = 承认；表示感谢
mutual acknowledgement = 相互承认
