scampered:/ˈskæmpərd/ | v. |飞跑，快跑，轻快地跑 | To run quickly and lightly, especially in a playful way. |例句：The children scampered across the playground. 孩子们在操场上飞跑。

例句：The rabbit scampered into its burrow when it saw the fox. 兔子看到狐狸后，飞快地跑进洞里。


近义词：dashed, hurried, scurried, darted | 反义词：crawled, crept, inched


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mouse scampers across the floor. = 老鼠在房间里飞跑。
He scampers to catch the bus every morning. = 他每天早上都飞快地跑着去赶公共汽车。


一般过去时:
The dog scampered after the ball. = 狗追着球跑。
She scampered up the stairs. = 她飞快地跑上楼梯。


现在完成时:
The children have scampered all over the house. = 孩子们在房子里跑来跑去。
The cat has scampered up the tree. = 猫已经窜上树了。


过去完成时:
The thief had scampered away before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经逃跑了。
They had scampered into the woods before the storm hit.  =暴风雨来临之前，他们已经跑进了树林。


将来时:
The kids will scamper down to the beach. = 孩子们将飞快地跑到海滩。
He will scamper off as soon as he sees me. = 一看到我，他就会跑掉。


虚拟语气:
If the rabbit had not scampered away, it would have been caught. = 如果兔子没有跑掉，它就被抓住了。
I wish I could scamper through the fields like a child again. = 我希望我还能像孩子一样在田野里飞奔。


被动语态: (此词较少用于被动语态)
The ground was scampered across by the playful puppies. =  顽皮的小狗在草地上跑来跑去。(较为不自然)
(更好的表达：Playful puppies scampered across the ground.)


疑问句:
Did the squirrel scamper up the tree? = 松鼠跑上树了吗？
Will the children scamper through the park? = 孩子们会在公园里跑来跑去吗？


否定句:
The cat did not scamper away. = 猫没有跑掉。
He didn't scamper across the road. = 他没有跑过马路。


PHRASE->
scamper away = 跑掉
scamper off = 跑开
scamper about = 到处跑
scamper up = 跑上去
scamper down = 跑下来

词源:
The word "scamper" likely originated from the Middle English word "scamperen," possibly a combination of "scamp" (to run quickly) and a frequentative suffix "-er."  Its exact etymological roots are uncertain but its meaning reflects a light, quick running style.
