preset:/ˈpriːsɛt/ | adj., n. |预设的；预置的；预设值；预设 |Pre-existing setting or configuration; something set in advance.  A preset is a configuration or setting that is already established before use.  It can refer to a specific value or a complete set of parameters. |例句：The camera has several preset options for different shooting conditions. 这台相机有几个预设选项，适用于不同的拍摄条件。

The software comes with a range of preset filters. 这个软件自带一系列预设滤镜。|近义词：predetermined, default, preconfigured |反义词：custom, user-defined |词性：adj., n.
