leagues:/liːɡz/| n.|联盟，社团，等级，英里|a group of people, countries, or organizations that have joined together for a particular purpose; a particular level of skill or ability in a sport or game; a unit of measurement equal to three miles|例句：The team plays in the top leagues. 这支球队在顶级联赛中比赛。|近义词：association, federation, alliance, division|反义词：individual, isolation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team plays in several leagues. = 这支球队参加几个联赛。
Many countries belong to international leagues. = 许多国家属于国际联盟。

一般过去时:
He played in the minor leagues. = 他在小联盟打球。
She joined several women's leagues. = 她加入了几个女子联盟。

现在完成时:
They have played in many leagues this season. = 这个赛季他们参加了很多联赛。
The organization has formed several leagues across the country. = 该组织在全国各地组建了多个联盟。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, they had competed in different leagues. = 在合并之前，他们在不同的联赛中竞争。
The team had won many games in the lower leagues. = 这支球队在低级别联赛中赢得了许多比赛。


将来时:
She will join the professional leagues next year. = 她明年将加入职业联赛。
They will compete in the highest leagues in the world. = 他们将在世界上最高的联赛中竞争。


虚拟语气:
If I were better, I would play in the higher leagues. = 如果我更好，我会在更高的联赛中打球。
I wish I could join the leagues next year. = 我希望明年能加入联盟。


被动语态:
The games are organized by different leagues. = 比赛由不同的联盟组织。
The rules are set by the governing leagues. = 规则由管理联盟制定。


疑问句:
Which leagues are you interested in? = 你对哪些联盟感兴趣？
What leagues does the team play in? = 这支球队参加哪些联赛？


否定句:
He didn't play in the professional leagues. = 他没有在职业联赛中打球。
She doesn't belong to any leagues. = 她不属于任何联盟。
