classification:/ˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 分类，分类法 | The act or process of classifying; arrangement into classes or categories.  |例句：The classification of plants is a complex process. 植物的分类是一个复杂的过程。|例句：His classification of the data was very helpful. 他对数据的分类非常有帮助。|近义词：categorization, grouping, sorting |反义词：randomization, mixing
