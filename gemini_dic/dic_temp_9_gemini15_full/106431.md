korma:/ˈkɔːrmə/| n.|印度咖喱菜|A rich, creamy curry dish from the Indian subcontinent, typically made with yogurt, spices, and nuts.  The specific ingredients and preparation methods vary widely depending on the region and the cook.|Example:  I ordered the chicken korma for dinner. 我晚餐点了鸡肉咖喱。  The lamb korma was incredibly flavorful.  这道羊肉咖喱味道棒极了。|近义词：curry, stew|反义词：无
