enlivening: /ɪnˈlaɪvənɪŋ/ | adj. | 使活跃的；使振奋的；使生动的 | Making something more lively, interesting, or exciting.  | 例句：The lively music provided an enlivening atmosphere. 活泼的音乐营造了活跃的气氛。
The comedian's jokes had an enlivening effect on the audience.  喜剧演员的笑话对观众产生了振奋人心的效果。 | 近义词： invigorating, stimulating, exciting, animating | 反义词： dulling, depressing, boring, monotonous | 词性：形容词


USAGE->

The music is enlivening the party. = 音乐使派对活跃起来。
This game is enlivening the children. = 这个游戏让孩子们活跃起来。
