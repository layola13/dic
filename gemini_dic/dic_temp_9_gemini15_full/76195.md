fustet:/ˈfʌstɛt/| n.| 黄栌木，苏木精| A yellow dye obtained from the wood of the fustet tree (Cotinus coggygria), also known as Venetian sumac.| The fustet dye was traditionally used to color fabrics a rich yellow or yellowish-brown.|  Fustet dye is now less common due to the availability of synthetic dyes.|近义词：yellow dye,  dye wood|反义词：无
