herpetic:/hɜːrˈpɛtɪk/| adj. |疱疹的，由疱疹引起的| Relating to or caused by herpes.  The term typically refers to infections caused by the herpes simplex virus (HSV) or the varicella-zoster virus (VZV).|The doctor diagnosed her rash as herpetic in nature.|近义词：herpesviral|反义词：non-herpetic
