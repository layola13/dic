endocarditis:/ˌɛndoʊkɑːrˈdaɪtɪs/| n. |心内膜炎|Inflammation of the inner lining of the heart (the endocardium).  This inflammation is usually caused by an infection, often from bacteria.  It can damage the heart valves and lead to serious complications.|例句：The patient was diagnosed with infective endocarditis after experiencing fever and shortness of breath.  这位病人经历发烧和呼吸急促后被诊断为感染性心内膜炎。|近义词：valvulitis (if valves are primarily affected) |反义词：None specifically, but healthy heart lining could be considered an antonym. |名词

词源:
The word "endocarditis" is derived from the Greek words "endon" (within) + "kardia" (heart) + "-itis" (inflammation).  Therefore, it literally means "inflammation within the heart."
