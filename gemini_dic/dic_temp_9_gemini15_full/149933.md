palliation:/ˌpæliˈeɪʃən/| n. |缓解；减轻；姑息治疗|The act of alleviating pain or distress, especially without curing the underlying disease.  It often refers to providing comfort and support to patients with incurable illnesses. |例句：The treatment focuses on palliation of symptoms rather than a cure.  治疗的重点是缓解症状而不是治愈。

例句：Palliative care aims to improve the quality of life for patients with serious illnesses.姑息治疗旨在提高患有严重疾病的患者的生活质量。


近义词：relief, mitigation, alleviation, comfort |反义词：aggravation, exacerbation

词源:
From Late Latin *palliatio, from Latin pallio "to cloak, cover," from palla "cloak."  The original sense was to cover or conceal, which evolved into the sense of masking or alleviating symptoms.
