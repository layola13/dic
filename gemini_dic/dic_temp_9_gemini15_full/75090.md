frilliest:/ˈfrɪliɪst/| adj.| 最华丽的；最褶边的；最装饰的 | Most ornamented with frills; excessively ornate.  The frilliest dress in the shop was a vibrant pink.  |The most ornate and decorative. | plain, simple |  形容词


例句：

Her frilliest dress was perfect for the wedding. = 她最华丽的裙子非常适合婚礼。

The frilliest curtains in the room completely obscured the view. = 房间里最华丽的窗帘完全遮挡了视线。


词源：

frilliest是frilly的最高级形式。frilly源于frill，意为“褶边；装饰”。Frill 最初来自古法语 *frille，与现代法语的 froufrou （褶边）有关。最终的词源可能与古高地德语的 *frilla （边缘，褶边）有关。


近义词：

ruffled, flounced, ornate, embellished, decorated, fussy


反义词：

plain, simple, undecorated, austere
