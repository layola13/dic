polemicist:/pəˈlɛmɪsɪst/| n. |论战者；好辩者|A polemicist is a person skilled in or given to polemics; a controversialist.  They are often involved in debates and arguments, usually on a particular topic. They are known for their skillful use of rhetoric and argumentation to defend their position. |例句：He is a renowned polemicist, known for his sharp wit and ability to dismantle opposing arguments. 他是一位著名的论战者，以其敏捷的才智和驳倒对方论点的能力而闻名。

例句：The online polemicist engaged in a heated debate about the merits of artificial intelligence.这位网络论战者就人工智能的优缺点展开了激烈的辩论。


近义词：debater, arguer, controversialist, disputant

反义词：peacemaker, conciliator


词性:名词
