hofbräu:/ˈhoːfbroɪ/| n.|霍夫布劳啤酒|Hofbräu is a German word meaning "court brewery".  It refers to a brewery that was originally owned or operated by a royal court or other governing body.  The most famous example is the Hofbräuhaus in Munich.|例句：The Hofbräuhaus is a world-renowned beer hall in Munich.  慕尼黑霍夫布劳豪斯是一家世界著名的啤酒馆。|近义词：brewery, beer hall|反义词：N/A
