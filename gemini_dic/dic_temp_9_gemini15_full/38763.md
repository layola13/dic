condoning:/kənˈdoʊnɪŋ/| v.|宽恕，纵容|To accept or allow (something that is wrong or considered wrong) to continue.|The government was accused of condoning violence. 政府被指控纵容暴力。|近义词：overlook, excuse, tolerate|反义词：condemn, denounce, censure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He condoned his son's bad behavior. = 他纵容了他儿子的不良行为。
They condone corruption within the organization. = 他们纵容组织内的腐败行为。


一般过去时:
She condoned his actions, even though she knew they were wrong. = 即使她知道他的行为是错的，她也原谅了他。
The principal condoned the students' pranks. = 校长纵容了学生们的恶作剧。


现在完成时:
The company has condoned this unethical practice for years. = 这家公司多年来一直纵容这种不道德的行为。
We have condoned too much violence in our society. = 我们社会已经纵容了太多的暴力行为。


过去完成时:
The court had condoned similar actions in the past. = 法院过去曾纵容过类似的行为。
She realized that she had condoned his lies for too long. = 她意识到她已经纵容了他的谎言太久了。


将来时:
They will condone this behavior no longer. = 他们将不再纵容这种行为。
I fear that the government will condone the use of force. = 我担心政府会纵容使用武力。


虚拟语气:
If they condoned such behavior, the consequences would be severe. = 如果他们纵容这种行为，后果将不堪设想。
I wish they hadn't condoned his actions. = 我希望他们没有纵容他的行为。


被动语态:
Such behavior should not be condoned. = 这种行为不应该被纵容。
His actions were condoned by his superiors. = 他的行为得到了上司的纵容。


疑问句:
Does the law condone this type of activity? = 法律是否纵容这种活动？
Will the company condone this employee's misconduct? = 公司会纵容这位员工的不当行为吗？


否定句:
We will not condone such behavior in the workplace. = 我们不会纵容工作场所的这种行为。
The judge refused to condone the defendant's actions. = 法官拒绝纵容被告的行为。


PHRASE->
condone violence = 纵容暴力
condone cheating = 纵容作弊
condone corruption = 纵容腐败
condone wrongdoing = 纵容不法行为
condone injustice = 纵容不公


词源:
From Old French condoner, from Latin condonare, from con- ("together") + donare ("to give").  The original meaning was to forgive a debt, which evolved to the modern sense of overlooking or excusing wrongdoing.
