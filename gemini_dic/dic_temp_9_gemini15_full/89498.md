honeysuckle:/ˈhʌnɪˌsʌkəl/| n. |忍冬，金银花|A climbing plant with fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers.  The flowers are often used to make perfumes and teas.|The honeysuckle climbed the trellis, its sweet scent filling the air.  忍冬爬上了凉棚，它的甜香充满了空气。|jasmine, woodbine|poison ivy (in terms of being a climbing plant that can be harmful)|noun
