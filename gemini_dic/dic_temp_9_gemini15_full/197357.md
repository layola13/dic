sheaf:/ʃiːf/| n. |捆，束；（麦等的）禾束；（数学）层；|A collection of things, especially stalks of grain, bound together.  It can also refer to a collection of mathematical objects.|例句：The farmer gathered a sheaf of wheat.  农民收割了一捆麦子。

例句：The mathematician worked on the properties of a sheaf. 数学家研究层的一些属性。


近义词：bundle, bunch, collection | 反义词：scattering, dispersion | 词性：名词
