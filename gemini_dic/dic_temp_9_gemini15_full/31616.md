chastity: /ˈtʃæstɪti/ | n. |贞洁；纯洁；禁欲| The state or practice of refraining from all sexual activity; purity.  |例句：She vowed to live a life of chastity. 她发誓要过贞洁的生活。

例句：The monk's chastity was unquestioned.  这位僧侣的贞洁毋庸置疑。


近义词：purity, abstinence, continence, virtue |反义词：lechery, lasciviousness, debauchery

词源:
