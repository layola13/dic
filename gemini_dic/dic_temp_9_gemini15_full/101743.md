irresolute: /ɪˈrezəluːt/ | adj. |优柔寡断的，犹豫不决的 | Unable to make decisions; wavering.  |例句：He stood irresolute, unsure of which path to take. 他犹豫不决地站在那里，不知道该走哪条路。

例句：Her irresolute manner frustrated her colleagues.  她优柔寡断的态度让同事们很沮丧。


近义词：wavering, hesitant, indecisive, vacillating, uncertain, doubtful


反义词：decisive, resolute, determined, firm


词源:

* **ir-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **resolut-**: from Latin "resolutus," past participle of "resolvere," meaning "to loosen, untie, solve, resolve."  This relates to the sense of having one's mind made up, a firmness of purpose.
