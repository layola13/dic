rendezvous's:/ˈrɑːndeɪvuːz/| n.|约会地点；集合点；会合|A rendezvous is a meeting at an agreed time and place.  It can refer to a romantic meeting, a military gathering, or any planned meeting.|例句：The lovers arranged a secret rendezvous by the lake.  恋人安排在湖边秘密约会。|近义词：meeting, appointment, assignation|反义词：separation, parting
