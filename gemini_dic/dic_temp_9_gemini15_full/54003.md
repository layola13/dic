discriminations:/ˌdɪskrɪmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |歧视，区别对待 |The act of making a distinction; the practice of unfairly treating a person or group of people differently from other people or groups of people.  |例句：Racial discriminations are illegal in many countries. 种族歧视在许多国家都是非法的。  |例句：The company was accused of gender discriminations in its hiring practices.  该公司被指控在其招聘实践中存在性别歧视。 |近义词：prejudice, bias, bigotry, unfairness |反义词：fairness, equality, impartiality
