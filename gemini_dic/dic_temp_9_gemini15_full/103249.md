jewelers:/ˈdʒuːələr/| n.|珠宝商；珠宝制造商|A jeweler is a person who makes, repairs, or sells jewelry.  Jewelers often work with precious metals and gemstones.|例句：My aunt is a jeweler and she makes beautiful necklaces. 我阿姨是位珠宝商，她制作精美的项链。  The jeweler expertly repaired my grandmother's antique brooch. 珠宝商熟练地修理了我祖母的古董胸针。|近义词：goldsmith, silversmith|反义词：N/A
