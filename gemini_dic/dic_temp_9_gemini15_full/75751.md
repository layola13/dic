fulmar:/ˈfʊlmɑːr/| n. |海燕|A fulmar is a seabird of the family Procellariidae, characterized by its tube-like nostrils and gliding flight.  Fulmars are found in the North Atlantic and North Pacific Oceans. They are known for their ability to survive in harsh conditions and their distinctive projectile vomiting defense mechanism. |例句：The fulmar soared effortlessly over the turbulent waves. 海燕毫不费力地翱翔在汹涌的波浪之上。

例句：We saw several fulmars circling the fishing boat. 我们看到几只海燕在渔船周围盘旋。
