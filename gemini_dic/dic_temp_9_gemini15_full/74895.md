freshly:/ˈfreʃli/| adv.|新鲜地，最近地，刚|Recently, in a fresh or new condition.  It describes something that has just been done or made.|例句：The bread smells freshly baked. 这面包闻起来很新鲜。|例句：I freshly painted the walls. 我刚粉刷了墙壁。|近义词：recently, newly, just|反义词：stale, old, long ago
