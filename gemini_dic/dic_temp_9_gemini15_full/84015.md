half-dram:/hɑːfˈdræm/| n. | 半打兰姆酒(一种古老的计量单位)|A half-dram is a unit of weight equal to half a dram, an archaic unit of weight, approximately 1.77 grams.  It was often used in medicine and pharmacy in the past to measure dosages of liquid medications.  The term is now largely obsolete, replaced by metric units.| Example: The old apothecary's recipe called for a half-dram of laudanum.  (老药剂师的处方需要半打兰姆酒的鸦片酊。) |近义词:half an apothecary's dram|反义词:a full dram
