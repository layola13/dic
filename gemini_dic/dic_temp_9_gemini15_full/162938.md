prechilling: /ˈpriːtʃɪlɪŋ/ | v. |预冷；预先冷却 | To cool something down beforehand, typically food or a substance, to a lower temperature before further processing or storage.  | Example: We prechilled the ingredients before adding them to the ice cream maker. 我们在把配料加入冰淇淋机之前先进行了预冷。|近义词：precooling, chilling beforehand |反义词：preheating, warming beforehand
