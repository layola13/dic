lairdocracy:/leərˈdɒkrəsi/| n. | 庸医统治；江湖郎中统治 | Rule by incompetent or unqualified doctors or medical practitioners.  It is a blend of "lair" (suggesting a den or hideout of questionable individuals) and "democracy."  The term is used satirically or critically to highlight situations where medical decisions are dominated by those lacking proper expertise or ethical standards. | The recent healthcare scandal revealed a disturbing lairdocracy, with unqualified practitioners making life-or-death decisions.  庸医当道，最近的医疗丑闻暴露了令人不安的事实，一些无资格的行医者正在做出生死攸关的决定。|  oligarchy, plutocracy | meritocracy | n.
