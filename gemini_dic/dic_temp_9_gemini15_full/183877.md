resembled:/rɪˈzɛmbəld/| v.|类似，相似|to be like or similar to someone or something else|The twins resembled each other so much that it was difficult to tell them apart. 这对双胞胎长得非常相似，很难区分他们。|近义词：resemble, look like, be similar to|反义词：differ from, be unlike


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resembles his father. = 他长得像他父亲。
The two paintings resemble each other in style. = 这两幅画在风格上很相似。

一般过去时:
The painting resembled a famous masterpiece. = 这幅画酷似一件著名的杰作。
She resembled her mother in both appearance and personality. = 她在外貌和性格上都像她母亲。

现在完成时:
The building has resembled a castle since it was built. = 这座建筑自建成以来一直像一座城堡。
The situation has resembled a crisis for weeks. = 好几个星期以来，情况一直像一场危机。

过去完成时:
He had resembled his grandfather in his youth. = 他年轻的时候长得像他的祖父。
The town had resembled a ghost town before the new industry arrived. = 在新兴产业到来之前，这个城镇就像个鬼城。

将来时:
The new product will resemble its predecessor in many ways. = 新产品在许多方面将与其前身相似。
The renovated house will resemble a modern villa. = 翻修后的房子将像一座现代别墅。

虚拟语气:
If he resembled his father more, he would be more popular. = 如果他更像他父亲，他会更受欢迎。
I wish the plan resembled reality more. = 我希望这个计划更接近现实。

被动语态:
The child was resembled by his father. = 这个孩子长得像他父亲。（被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态）
The new design was resembled by the old one. = 新设计与旧设计相似。（被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态）

疑问句:
Does he resemble his mother? = 他像他母亲吗？
Does the sculpture resemble a human being? = 这座雕塑像人吗？

否定句:
The two houses do not resemble each other at all. = 这两所房子一点也不相似。
The weather today does not resemble what was predicted. = 今天的天气与预报的大相径庭。


PHRASE->
resemble in appearance = 在外貌上相似
resemble in character = 在性格上相似
closely resemble = 酷似
strongly resemble = 非常相似
bear a resemblance to = 与…相似
