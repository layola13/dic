lilliputians:/ˌlɪləˈpjuːʃənz/| n. |小人国居民；身材矮小的人| Inhabitants of Lilliput, the fictional island in Jonathan Swift's *Gulliver's Travels*, characterized by their diminutive size; figuratively, people of small stature or insignificant importance.  |Example: The task seemed gigantic to the lilliputians working on it.  小人国居民们觉得这项任务无比巨大。

The children, dwarfed by the enormous castle, felt like lilliputians. 孩子们被巨大的城堡比下去，感觉自己像小人国人一样渺小。|近义词：dwarfs, midgets, pygmies|反义词：giants, titans
