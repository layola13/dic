over-mixes:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪksɪz/| v.|过度混合，搅拌过度|To mix something for too long, resulting in a less desirable texture or consistency.  This often applies to baking, where overmixing can develop gluten excessively, leading to tough baked goods.|例句：Don't over-mix the cake batter; it will become tough. 不要过度搅拌蛋糕糊；它会变得很硬。|例句：Over-mixing the cookie dough resulted in flat, chewy cookies.  过度搅拌饼干面团导致饼干扁平且耐嚼。|近义词：overbeat, overwhisk|反义词：undermix
