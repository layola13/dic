midgut:/ˈmɪdɡʌt/| n. |中肠|The midgut is the central section of the alimentary canal in many animals, including humans.  It develops from the embryonic midgut and extends from the pylorus of the stomach to the beginning of the transverse colon.  It's responsible for the majority of digestion and absorption of nutrients.|Example: The midgut is responsible for absorbing most of the nutrients from food.  中肠负责吸收食物中的大部分营养物质。 The doctor examined the patient's midgut during the surgery.  医生在手术过程中检查了病人的中肠。|近义词：mesentery, intestinal tract |反义词：foregut, hindgut |名词
