chuckles:/ˈtʃʌkəlz/ | n. |轻声笑，咯咯笑 | a soft laugh; a series of quiet laughs.  |例句：She let out a few chuckles at the joke. 她听到笑话后轻轻地笑了几声。|例句：The children were giggling and chuckling. 孩子们咯咯地笑着。|近义词：giggles, snickers, titters |反义词：sobbing, weeping


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chuckles at the comedian's jokes. = 他对喜剧演员的笑话咯咯地笑。
She chuckles softly to herself. = 她轻声自嘲地笑着。

一般过去时:
He chuckled at the funny story. = 他听到那个有趣的故事笑了。
She chuckled nervously before answering. = 她紧张地笑了笑才回答。

现在完成时:
She has chuckled at that joke many times. = 她已经多次听到那个笑话而笑了。
He has chuckled throughout the entire movie. = 他在整部电影中一直咯咯地笑。

过去完成时:
He had chuckled at the same joke before. = 他以前就听过同样的笑话而笑了。
She had chuckled quietly throughout the presentation. = 在整个演示过程中，她一直轻轻地笑着。

将来时:
I think he will chuckle at my story. = 我认为他会听我的故事而笑。
She will chuckle when she hears the news. = 她听到这个消息后会咯咯地笑。

虚拟语气:
If I told that joke, he would chuckle. = 如果我讲那个笑话，他会咯咯地笑。
I wish she would chuckle at my attempts at humor. = 我希望她能对我的幽默尝试一笑。

被动语态:
(This word is rarely used passively)  The joke was chuckled at by everyone. =  这个笑话让每个人都笑了。(很少用)
His attempts at humor were chuckled at by few. = 很少有人被他试图幽默逗笑。(很少用)

疑问句:
Did he chuckle at my joke? = 他听我的笑话笑了吗？
Will she chuckle at the punchline? = 她会对妙语连珠的笑点笑吗？

否定句:
He didn't chuckle at the joke. = 他没有因为那个笑话而笑。
She didn't chuckle; she remained serious. = 她没有笑；她保持严肃。


PHRASE->
a chuckle of amusement =  愉悦的轻声笑
a nervous chuckle =  紧张的轻声笑
a hearty chuckle =  爽朗的笑声
let out a chuckle = 发出一阵轻笑
suppress a chuckle = 忍住笑声
