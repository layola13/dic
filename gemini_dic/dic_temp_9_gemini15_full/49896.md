demurred:/dɪˈmɜːrd/| v.|反对，表示异议，犹豫|To raise objections; to hesitate or delay|He demurred at the suggestion that he was at fault. 他对暗示他犯错的建议表示反对。|近义词：object, protest, hesitate, balk|反义词：agree, accept, concur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demurs to any changes to the plan. 他反对计划的任何更改。
She demurs when asked to speak in public. 当被要求在公共场合发言时，她犹豫了。


一般过去时:
They demurred at the high price. 他们对高价表示异议。
He demurred politely but firmly. 他礼貌但坚定地表示反对。


现在完成时:
She has demurred on several occasions. 她已经多次表示反对。
He has demurred to the proposal. 他对这个提议表示了异议。


过去完成时:
They had demurred at the initial proposal. 他们对最初的提议表示了反对。
She had demurred before, but this time she agreed. 她以前曾表示反对，但这次她同意了。


将来时:
He will likely demur if we ask him to do that. 如果我们要求他这样做，他很可能会反对。
They will demur at the cost of the project. 他们会反对这个项目的成本。


虚拟语气:
If he had not demurred, the project might have been successful. 如果他没有反对，这个项目可能已经成功了。
I wish he hadn't demurred; his input would have been valuable. 我真希望他没有反对；他的意见本来会很有价值。


被动语态:
The proposal was demurred to by several members of the committee. 该提案遭到委员会多名成员的反对。
His suggestions were demurred at by the board. 他的建议遭到董事会的反对。


疑问句:
Did anyone demur to the decision? 有人反对这个决定吗？
Will they demur if we suggest a different approach? 如果我们建议不同的方法，他们会反对吗？


否定句:
He did not demur at the extra work. 他并不反对额外的工作。
She did not demur to his request. 她没有反对他的要求。


PHRASE->
demur to: 对…表示异议
demur against: 反对
demur at: 对…表示反对
demur upon: 仔细考虑
without demur:  毫无异议地
