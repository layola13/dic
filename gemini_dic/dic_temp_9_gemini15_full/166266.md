prerecite:/ˌpriːrɪˈsaɪt/| v. |预先背诵，预先朗读 |To recite or rehearse something beforehand.  It implies preparation and practice before a public performance or presentation. |例句：The students prerecited their lines before the school play. 学生们在学校戏剧演出前预先背诵了他们的台词。

例句：She prerecited her speech several times to ensure a smooth delivery. 她预先朗读了好几次她的演讲稿，以确保流畅的表达。


近义词：rehearse, review, practice


反义词：improvise, ad-lib


词性:动词
