editing:/ˈedɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 编辑；修改 | The process of preparing written material for publication, including correcting errors, improving style, and organizing content.  It can also refer to the process of modifying audio or video recordings. |例句：The editing process took several weeks.  编辑过程花了几个星期。|例句：The film's editing was masterful. 这部电影的剪辑非常出色。|近义词：revision, correction, amendment |反义词：creation, original writing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The editor is editing the manuscript. = 编辑正在编辑手稿。
She edits articles for a living. = 她靠编辑文章为生。

一般过去时:
He edited the book last year. = 他去年编辑了这本书。
They edited the video before releasing it. = 他们在发布视频之前对其进行了编辑。

现在完成时:
I have edited this document three times. = 我已经编辑了这份文档三次。
She has edited many books in her career. = 她在她职业生涯中编辑过许多书籍。

过去完成时:
He had edited the chapter before submitting it. = 他在提交章节之前已经编辑过了。
They had edited the film several times before the premiere. = 首映前他们已经多次剪辑了这部电影。

将来时:
We will edit the video tomorrow. = 我们明天将编辑视频。
She will be editing the manuscript all day. = 她将一整天都在编辑手稿。

虚拟语气:
If I were editing the novel, I would change the ending. = 如果我编辑这本小说，我会改变结局。
I wish I had edited the essay more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地编辑过这篇文章。

被动语态:
The article was edited by a professional. = 这篇文章由一位专业人士编辑。
The video is being edited right now. = 视频正在编辑中。

疑问句:
Are you editing the report yet? = 你编辑报告了吗？
Has the manuscript been edited? = 手稿编辑了吗？

否定句:
I didn't edit the document. = 我没有编辑这份文档。
He hasn't finished editing the video. = 他还没有完成视频编辑。
