outstinking:/ˌaʊtˈstɪŋkɪŋ/| adj.|极其恶臭的，令人作呕的|extremely unpleasant and foul-smelling;  overpowering in its bad smell. This word is informal and humorous, often used hyperbolically.|例句：The garbage outstinking the entire neighborhood.  垃圾的气味熏得整个街区都臭气熏天。

例句：That old cheese is outstinking the refrigerator.  那块老奶酪把冰箱都熏臭了。


近义词：stinky, foul-smelling, reeking, fetid, rank, putrid


反义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling, aromatic


词性:形容词
