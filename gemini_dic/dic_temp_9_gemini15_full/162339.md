preachify:/ˈpriːtʃɪfaɪ/| vt.|说教；教训；喋喋不休地劝诫|To talk in a tedious or pompous way, especially on moral matters; to deliver a sermon or lecture excessively or tiresomely.  |例句：He was constantly preaching about the importance of hard work. 他不断地宣讲努力工作的重要性。

例句：Stop preachifying to me; I know what I'm doing. 别对我唠叨了；我知道我在做什么。


近义词：lecture, sermonize, moralize, pontificate |反义词：listen, understand, empathize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preachifies to his children about good manners. 他对孩子们说教良好的礼仪。
She preachifies about the dangers of smoking. 她喋喋不休地谈论吸烟的危害。


一般过去时:
He preachified to the congregation for an hour. 他向会众布道了一个小时。
She preachified at her brother about his messy room. 她教训她弟弟房间乱糟糟的。


现在完成时:
He has preachified to me about this topic many times. 他已经多次向我宣讲这个话题。
She has preachified endlessly about the importance of saving money. 她没完没了地劝诫要存钱的重要性。


过去完成时:
He had preachified to them about responsibility before the accident. 事故发生前，他已经对他们进行了关于责任的训诫。
She had preachified to her students about the exam before they took it. 考试前，她已经对学生们进行了考试的训诫。


将来时:
He will preachify to his class about the importance of education. 他将向他的班级宣讲教育的重要性。
She will preachify to her children about the dangers of drugs. 她将告诫她的孩子们毒品的危害。


虚拟语气:
If he preachified less, people would listen more. 如果他少说教，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't preachify so much. 我希望他不那么爱说教。


被动语态:
His views were preachified to the audience. 他的观点被传达给了听众。(Note:  Passive voice is less common with this verb)
The importance of discipline was preachified by the teacher. 老师反复强调纪律的重要性。(Note:  Passive voice is less common with this verb)


疑问句:
Does he often preachify to others? 他经常对别人说教吗？
Will she preachify at the meeting? 她会在会议上说教吗？


否定句:
He didn't preachify at all during the meeting. 他在会议期间根本没有说教。
She doesn't preachify unnecessarily. 她不会无谓地说教。
