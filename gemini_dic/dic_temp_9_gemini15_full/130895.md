nonarticulately:/ˌnɒnɑːˈtɪkjʊlətli/| adj. |含糊不清地；不清晰地|In a manner that is not clear or distinct in speech or expression.  It implies a lack of articulation or clarity in communication, whether spoken or written.|例句：He mumbled nonarticulately, making it difficult to understand him. 他含糊不清地咕哝着，很难听懂他在说什么。

例句：The baby cried nonarticulately, expressing its discomfort.  婴儿含糊不清地哭着，表达着它的不适。


近义词：inarticulately, indistinctly, unclearly, vaguely

反义词：clearly, distinctly, articulately


词源：
* **non-**:  Prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".
* **articulate**:  From Latin *articulatus*, past participle of *articulare* "to furnish with joints," from *articulus* "joint, knuckle, small joint," diminutive of *artus* "joint, limb."  The sense of "expressing oneself clearly" developed later.
