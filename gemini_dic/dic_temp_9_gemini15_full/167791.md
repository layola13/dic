primer:/ˈpraɪmər/| n.|初级读本；入门书；引信；底漆；|A primer is a book for teaching children to read, or a book that introduces a subject. It can also refer to an explosive charge used to detonate a larger explosive, or a type of paint used as a base coat. |例句：She used a primer before painting the wall. 她粉刷墙壁前先上了底漆。|近义词：introduction, textbook, handbook, base coat |反义词：advanced text, finishing coat
