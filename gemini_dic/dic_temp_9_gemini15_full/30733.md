cervus:/ˈsɜːrvəs/| n. |鹿|The word "cervus" is the Latin word for "deer," specifically referring to the genus Cervus, which includes various species of deer such as red deer, sika deer, and wapiti.  It's a term often used in zoological contexts.  |Example:  The majestic cervus elaphus (red deer) roamed the forest.  雄伟的赤鹿在森林里漫游。|近义词：deer, stag (male deer)|反义词：无
