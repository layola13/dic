reside:/rɪˈzaɪd/| v.|居住，存在，属于|To live in a place; to be present in something.|例句：They reside in a small village. 他们住在一个小村庄。|例句：The problem resides in a lack of communication. 问题在于缺乏沟通。|近义词：live, dwell, inhabit, exist|反义词：depart, leave


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resides in London. = 他住在伦敦。
The power resides in the people. = 权力属于人民。

一般过去时:
She resided in Paris for five years. = 她曾在巴黎住了五年。
The fault resided in the faulty wiring. = 故障在于线路故障。

现在完成时:
They have resided in this house for twenty years. = 他们在这栋房子里住了二十年。
The issue has resided in the company’s poor management for years. =  这个问题多年来一直存在于公司糟糕的管理中。

过去完成时:
He had resided in New York before moving to California. = 他在搬到加利福尼亚之前住在纽约。
The problem had resided in the software for quite some time. = 这个问题在软件中存在了一段时间。

将来时:
We will reside in a new apartment next month. = 我们下个月将住进一间新公寓。
The solution will reside in further research. = 解决方法在于进一步的研究。

虚拟语气:
If I resided in a bigger house, I would have more space. = 如果我住在一间更大的房子里，我就会有更多空间。
If the problem resided in the hardware, we would have replaced it by now. = 如果问题出在硬件上，我们现在就应该更换了。

被动语态:
The decision was resided in by the committee. = 这个决定是由委员会做出的。（此用法较少见，被动语态通常用其他动词替代）
The responsibility was resided in the manager. = 责任由经理承担。（此用法较少见，被动语态通常用其他动词替代）


疑问句:
Where do they reside? = 他们住在哪里？
Where does the difficulty reside? = 困难在哪里？

否定句:
He does not reside in this city. = 他不住在这个城市。
The answer does not reside in simple solutions. = 答案并不在于简单的解决方法。


PHRASE->
