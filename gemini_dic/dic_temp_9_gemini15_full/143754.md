outmanoeuvred:/ˌaʊt.məˈnuː.vɚd/| v.|巧妙战胜，智胜，以策略取胜|To defeat or gain an advantage over (someone) by skillful maneuvering or planning.  It implies a strategic outsmarting rather than simply being stronger.|例句：The general outmanoeuvred the enemy and won the battle.  将军巧妙地战胜了敌人并赢得了战争。

The experienced politician outmanoeuvred his opponent in the debate.  经验丰富的政治家在辩论中智胜了他的对手。|近义词：outwit, outsmart, surpass, defeat|反义词：be defeated by, be outdone by
