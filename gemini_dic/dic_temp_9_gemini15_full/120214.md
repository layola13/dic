mineralogy:/ˌmɪnəˈrælədʒi/| n. |矿物学|The science that deals with the identification, classification, and description of minerals.  It encompasses their chemical composition, crystal structure, physical properties, and origin.|Example:  She studied mineralogy at university and became a skilled geologist.|近义词：geochemistry (部分重叠)|反义词：无
