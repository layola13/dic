preponderatingly:/prɪˈpɒndəreɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 主要地；压倒性地；极大地 |  Overwhelmingly; to a very great extent.  It describes a situation where something is significantly more prominent or influential than other factors. |例句：The evidence preponderatingly suggests his guilt.  证据压倒性地表明他有罪。

例句：The meeting was preponderatingly dominated by the CEO's opinions. 会议主要由首席执行官的意见主导。


近义词：overwhelmingly, predominantly, mainly, largely, significantly


反义词：minimally, slightly, marginally, insignificantly


词性：副词
