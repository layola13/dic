slithers:/ˈslɪðərz/| v.|蜿蜒移动，悄悄移动 |To move smoothly and silently along a surface, like a snake.|The snake slithered through the grass.  蛇蜿蜒穿过草地。 The thief slithered away unnoticed.  小偷溜走了，没有被发现。| creep, crawl, glide | stand still, remain stationary | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The snake slithers through the grass. = 蛇蜿蜒穿过草地。
He slithers into the room unnoticed. = 他悄悄地溜进了房间。


一般过去时:
The snake slithered through the grass yesterday. = 昨天蛇蜿蜒穿过草地。
She slithered out of the room quietly. = 她悄悄地溜出了房间。


现在完成时:
The snake has slithered away. = 蛇已经溜走了。
The thief has slithered into the shadows. = 小偷已经潜入阴影中。


过去完成时:
The snake had slithered away before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，蛇已经溜走了。
The burglar had slithered out of the building before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，窃贼已经溜出了大楼。


将来时:
The snake will slither into its hole. = 蛇将蜿蜒进入它的洞穴。
He will slither away when he sees me. = 当他看到我时，他会溜走。


虚拟语气:
If the snake slithered closer, I would scream. = 如果蛇靠近，我会尖叫。
I wish the snake hadn't slithered into my house. = 我真希望蛇没有溜进我的房子。


被动语态:
The snake can't be slithered. (This is grammatically possible but unnatural.  Slither is rarely used passively.) = 蛇无法被（使）蜿蜒移动。（这在语法上是可能的，但不自然。slither很少用被动语态。）
(A more natural passive would use a different verb, e.g., The snake was moved slowly.)= 蛇被慢慢地移动了。


疑问句:
Does the snake slither quickly? = 蛇爬得快吗？
Will the thief slither away silently? = 小偷会悄悄溜走吗？


否定句:
The snake doesn't slither very fast. = 蛇爬得不快。
The burglar didn't slither into the house undetected. = 窃贼并没有未被察觉地溜进房子。


PHRASE->
slither away = 溜走
slither into = 悄悄溜进
slither out = 悄悄溜出
slither through = 蜿蜒穿过
slither down = 滑落
