cubemap:/ˈkjuːbˌmæp/| n.|立方体贴图，六面图|A cubemap is a collection of six images, each representing one face of a cube, used in computer graphics to represent an environment map.  It's a way to create realistic reflections and environment lighting in 3D scenes. |Example:  The game engine uses cubemaps to render realistic reflections on the car's surface.  游戏引擎使用立方体贴图来渲染汽车表面的逼真反射。|近义词：environment map, skybox|反义词：无
