leeway:/ˈliːweɪ/| n. |余地；自由；让步| Freedom to move or act within certain limits; latitude; allowance.  It refers to the amount of freedom available to someone to make choices or take actions within a set of boundaries or constraints.|The captain had some leeway in choosing his route. 船长在选择航线方面有一定的自由。|There's some leeway in the deadline. 最后期限有一定的余地。|近义词：latitude, scope, margin, room|反义词：restriction, constraint, limitation
