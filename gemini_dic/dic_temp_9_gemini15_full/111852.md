logician:/ˈlɒdʒɪʃən/| n. |逻辑学家|A person skilled in logic; someone who studies or is expert in logic.  A logician is trained in the principles of reasoning and argumentation, and can analyze and evaluate arguments for validity and soundness. |例句：The logician meticulously dissected the argument, revealing its flaws.  逻辑学家一丝不苟地剖析了这个论证，揭示了其缺陷。

例句：Many logicians have contributed to the field of artificial intelligence. 许多逻辑学家为人工智能领域做出了贡献。


近义词：reasoner, philosopher (in some contexts), analyst


反义词：irrationalist (in a broad sense)


词性:名词
