factorising: /ˈfæktəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 因式分解 | To express (a number or algebraic expression) as a product of factors.  | Example:  Factorising the expression x² - 4 gives (x - 2)(x + 2).  因式分解表达式 x² - 4 得到 (x - 2)(x + 2)。 | 近义词: factoring, resolving into factors | 反义词: expanding, multiplying out
