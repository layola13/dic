pantiling:/ˈpænˌtaɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 铺瓦，盖瓦 | The act of covering a roof with tiles.  This word refers specifically to the process of installing tiles on a roof, often involving overlapping the tiles for proper water runoff. | Example: The pantiling of the new roof took several days.  铺设新屋顶的瓦片花了几天时间。| 近义词：tiling, roofing | 反义词：removing tiles, unroofing
