contraindicates: /ˌkɒntrəɪndɪkeɪt/ /ˌkɑːntrəɪndɪkeɪt/ | v. |禁忌，表明…不宜；提示…不适宜 | To suggest or indicate that a particular treatment or procedure is inadvisable or likely to be harmful in a particular situation.  |  The patient's allergy to penicillin contraindicates its use.  这位病人的青霉素过敏反应表明不宜使用青霉素。|  discourage, advise against | indicate, suggest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This medication contraindicates alcohol consumption. = 这种药物禁忌饮酒。
Her age contraindicates participation in the marathon. = 她的年龄表明不宜参加马拉松比赛。


一般过去时:
The doctor contraindicated surgery due to the patient's weak heart. = 由于病人心脏虚弱，医生禁忌进行手术。
The high fever contraindicated any strenuous activity. = 高烧表明不宜进行任何剧烈活动。


现在完成时:
Recent studies have contraindicated the use of this drug in pregnant women. = 最近的研究表明这种药物不宜用于孕妇。
His history of heart problems has contraindicated strenuous exercise. = 他有心脏问题的病史，表明不宜进行剧烈运动。


过去完成时:
The doctor had contraindicated the treatment after reviewing the patient's medical history.  = 医生在审查病人的病史后，已禁忌这种治疗方法。
The previous test results had contraindicated further investigation. = 之前的测试结果表明无需进一步调查。


将来时:
The doctor will contraindicate the procedure if the patient's condition worsens. = 如果病人的病情恶化，医生将禁忌该手术。
The results of the upcoming tests will likely contraindicate further treatment. = 即将进行的测试结果很可能会表明不宜进一步治疗。


虚拟语气:
If the patient's blood pressure were higher, the surgery would be contraindicated. = 如果病人的血压更高，手术将是禁忌的。
If I had known that the medicine contraindicated alcohol, I wouldn't have drunk any. = 如果我知道这种药禁忌饮酒，我就不会喝酒了。


被动语态:
The use of this drug is contraindicated in children under five. = 五岁以下儿童禁忌使用这种药物。
Further treatment was contraindicated after the patient experienced an adverse reaction. = 病人出现不良反应后，禁忌进一步治疗。


疑问句:
Does the patient's history contraindicate the use of this medication? = 病人的病史是否禁忌使用这种药物？
Does this allergy contraindicate the planned surgery? = 这种过敏反应是否禁忌计划中的手术？


否定句:
The test results did not contraindicate the proposed treatment. = 测试结果并未禁忌建议的治疗方案。
Nothing in his medical history contraindicated the surgery. = 他的病史中没有任何内容禁忌进行手术。
