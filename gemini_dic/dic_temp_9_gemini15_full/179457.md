redundantly:/rɪˈdʌndəntli/| adv. | 多余地；冗余地 | In a redundant manner; unnecessarily or excessively.  It implies repetition or superfluity. |例句：He redundantly repeated the same point three times. 他冗余地重复了同一点三次。

例句：The report was redundantly long, containing much unnecessary detail. 这份报告冗长累赘，包含许多不必要的细节。


近义词： unnecessarily, superfluously, excessively, repetitively


反义词： concisely, succinctly, briefly


词性: 副词
