gen:/dʒɛn/ | /dʒen/ | n. |基因；起源；种类|The basic unit of heredity that is transferred from a parent to offspring and is held to determine some characteristic of the offspring.| 例句：Genes determine many of our physical characteristics. 基因决定了我们的许多生理特征。  Genes are segments of DNA. 基因是DNA片段。|近义词：gene, origin, type |反义词：  N/A
