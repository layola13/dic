overexploit:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪt/| vt.|过度开发；过度利用 |To exploit to such a degree as to cause damage or exhaustion.  It implies using a resource beyond its sustainable capacity, leading to depletion or harm.|例句：The fishing industry has overexploited the cod population in the North Atlantic.  北大西洋的鳕鱼种群因捕捞业的过度捕捞而数量锐减。

例句：The company was accused of overexploiting its workers by making them work excessive overtime without proper compensation. 这家公司被指控过度压榨员工，强迫他们加班加点而不给予适当的补偿。


近义词：overuse, deplete, exhaust, drain


反义词：conserve, preserve, protect, sustain


词源: over- (prefix indicating excess) + exploit (verb meaning to make full use of a resource, often to the point of abuse)
