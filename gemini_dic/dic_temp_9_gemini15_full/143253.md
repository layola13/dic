outdespatched:/ˌaʊtˈdɛspætʃt/| v.|发出；发出(货物)；派遣出去|The word "outdespatched" is a less common variant of "despatched,"  meaning something has been sent out, typically goods or people. It emphasizes the completion and finality of the sending.  It's formed by adding the intensifying prefix "out-" to "despatched."|例句：The goods were outdespatched yesterday. 货物昨天已经发出。The emergency team was outdespatched to the disaster zone. 紧急救援队已被派往灾区。|近义词：sent, dispatched, shipped, forwarded|反义词：received, returned
