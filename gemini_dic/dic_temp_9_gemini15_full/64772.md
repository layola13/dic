eserine:/ˈɛzərɪn/| n. |毒扁豆碱|Eserine is a naturally occurring alkaloid found in the Calabar bean (Physostigma venenosum). It acts as a cholinesterase inhibitor, meaning it prevents the breakdown of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter. This leads to increased acetylcholine levels in the body, which can have various effects depending on the dosage and administration method.  It has been used medically to treat glaucoma and myasthenia gravis, but its use is limited due to its potential side effects.|例句：The ophthalmologist prescribed eserine eye drops to lower the patient's intraocular pressure. 眼科医生开了毒扁豆碱滴眼液来降低病人的眼内压。|近义词：physostigmine (化学名称)|反义词：Atropine (has opposite effects)|名词
