differs:/ˈdɪfərz/| v.|不同；相异|To be unlike or dissimilar; to have points of unlikeness.|The two plans differ significantly in their approach. 这两个计划在方法上大相径庭。| varies, contrasts, distinguishes | resembles, matches,  agrees |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His opinion differs from mine. = 他的意见与我的不同。
The two books differ in style. = 这两本书的风格不同。

一般过去时:
Their accounts of the event differed. = 他们对事件的描述不同。
The results differed significantly from the predictions. = 结果与预测大相径庭。

现在完成时:
The situation has differed greatly since then. = 从那时起情况已经大不相同了。
Their attitudes have differed throughout the project. = 他们在整个项目中的态度一直不同。

过去完成时:
The evidence differed from what he had previously stated. = 证据与他之前所说的不同。
His story differed from what we had heard before. = 他的说法与我们之前听到的不同。

将来时:
The outcome will likely differ depending on the conditions. = 结果可能会根据条件而有所不同。
Their approaches will differ significantly in the future. = 他们未来的方法将大相径庭。

虚拟语气:
If the facts differed, the outcome would be different. = 如果事实不同，结果也会不同。
I wish my opinion differed from his. = 我希望我的意见与他不同。

被动语态:
The two approaches are differed in their methods. = 这两种方法在方法上有所不同。(此句被动语态略显不自然，一般不用被动)
The results were differed in the experimental group. = 实验组的结果有所不同。（此句被动语态略显不自然，一般不用被动）

疑问句:
How do the two proposals differ? = 这两个提案有何不同？
In what ways do their opinions differ? = 他们的意见在哪些方面不同？

否定句:
The twins don't differ much in appearance. = 这对双胞胎外表差别不大。
The two samples didn't differ significantly in their composition. = 这两个样本的成分没有显著差异。


PHRASE->
differ from = 不同于
differ in = 在...方面不同
differ with = 与...意见相左
differ widely = 大相径庭
differ substantially = 大体不同
