nigher:/ˈnaɪər/| adj.|更近的，更接近的|Nearer; closer.  The meaning is essentially the same as "nearer," but "nigher" is considered archaic or poetic.|例句：The stars seemed nigher than ever before.  星星似乎比以往任何时候都更近了。

例句：Draw nigher, and let me see your face. 靠近些，让我看看你的脸。

近义词：nearer, closer

反义词：farther, further

词性：形容词
