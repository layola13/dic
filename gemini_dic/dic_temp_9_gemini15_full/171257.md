pteridophyta:/ˌtɛrɪˈdɑɪfɪtə/| n. |蕨类植物门|The Pteridophyta are a division of vascular plants that reproduce via spores, rather than seeds. They include ferns, horsetails, and whisk ferns.  These plants lack flowers and seeds, and typically have prominent fronds. |Example:  The lush rainforest is home to a diverse array of pteridophytes.  热带雨林是多种蕨类植物的家园。|近义词: ferns (as a general term)|反义词: Spermatophyta (seed plants)|名词
