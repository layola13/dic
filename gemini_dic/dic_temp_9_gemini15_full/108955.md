leeriness:/ˈlɪəriɪnəs/| n. |警惕；怀疑；不信任 |A feeling of doubt or suspicion; distrust.  It often implies a cautious reluctance to trust someone or something. |例句：Her leeriness of strangers was understandable, given her past experiences.  她的警惕性是可以理解的，考虑到她过去的经历。

例句：The leeriness in his eyes told me he wasn't being entirely truthful. 他眼中的警惕告诉我他并非完全说实话。


近义词：suspicion, distrust, skepticism, apprehension, wariness

反义词：trust, confidence, faith, belief
