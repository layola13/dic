drainer:/ˈdreɪnər/| n.|排水器；滤水器；下水道|A drainer is a device or container used to drain liquids from something, such as a sink drainer for dishes or a kitchen drainer for vegetables.|例句：The kitchen drainer was full of wet dishes. 厨房的滤水器里装满了湿盘子。  The sink drainer is clogged.水槽的排水器堵塞了。|近义词：strainer, filter|反义词：None
