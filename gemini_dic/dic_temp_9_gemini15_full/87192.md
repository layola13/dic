here-and-there:/ˌhɪr ænd ðɛr/| adv.|到处，这里那里|The word "here-and-there" is an adverb that indicates something is scattered or located in various places, not concentrated in one spot. It suggests a lack of specific location or organization.|例句：Books were scattered here and there on the floor.书散落在地板上。|例句：The children were playing here and there in the garden.孩子们在花园里到处玩耍。|近义词：everywhere, sporadically, randomly|反义词：everywhere, concentrated, together


USAGE->
