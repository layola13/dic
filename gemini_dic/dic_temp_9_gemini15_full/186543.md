ridding:/ˈrɪdɪŋ/| v.|摆脱，除去，清除|The act of getting rid of something unwanted or undesirable.  It implies a complete or thorough removal.|例句：He's ridding his house of clutter. 他正在清理他家里的杂物。

例句：The farmer is ridding the field of weeds. 这位农民正在清除田里的杂草。


近义词：removing, eliminating, getting rid of, clearing


反义词：adding, accumulating, collecting


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am ridding my desk of paperwork. = 我正在清理我桌子上的文件。
She rids her garden of pests regularly. = 她定期清除花园里的害虫。

一般过去时:
He ridded his life of negativity. = 他摆脱了生活中的消极情绪。
They ridded the ship of rats. = 他们清除船上的老鼠。

现在完成时:
I have ridded myself of that bad habit. = 我已经改掉了那个坏习惯。
She has ridded the house of the infestation. = 她已经除掉了房子里的虫害。

过去完成时:
He had ridded the car of its rust before selling it. = 他在卖车之前已经除掉了车身上的锈迹。
They had ridded the area of dangerous materials. = 他们已经清除了该区域的危险物品。

将来时:
I will rid my home of clutter this weekend. = 我这个周末将会清理家里的杂物。
She will rid herself of her anxieties. = 她将会摆脱她的焦虑。

虚拟语气:
If I could rid myself of this debt, I would be happy. = 如果我能摆脱这笔债务，我会很高兴。
I wish I could rid myself of this headache. = 我真希望我能摆脱这个头痛。

被动语态:
The town is being rid of its old infrastructure. = 这个城镇正在清除其旧的基础设施。
The problem is being rid of gradually. = 这个问题正在逐渐得到解决。

疑问句:
How do you rid yourself of stress? = 你如何缓解压力？
How can we rid the world of poverty? = 我们怎样才能消除世界贫困？

否定句:
He couldn't rid his mind of the thought. = 他无法摆脱这个想法。
She hasn't rid the garden of all the weeds yet. = 她还没有清除掉花园里所有的杂草。


PHRASE->
rid oneself of = 摆脱…
rid...of = 清除…
rid the world of = 消除…
be rid of = 摆脱…
get rid of = 除去…
