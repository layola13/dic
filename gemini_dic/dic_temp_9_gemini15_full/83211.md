guzzling:/ˈɡʌzəlɪŋ/| v.| 狂饮；狼吞虎咽 | To drink large quantities of liquid rapidly; to eat greedily. |例句：He was guzzling beer all afternoon. 他整个下午都在狂饮啤酒。

例句：The dog guzzled down his food in seconds.  这条狗几秒钟就狼吞虎咽地吃完了食物。


近义词：gorging, swilling, downing, consuming

反义词：sipping, nibbling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He guzzles beer every night. = 他每晚都狂饮啤酒。
She guzzles her food down quickly. = 她狼吞虎咽地吃东西。

一般过去时:
They guzzled the champagne at the party. = 他们在派对上狂饮香槟。
He guzzled his dinner before rushing off to work. = 他匆匆忙忙地吃完晚饭就去上班了。

现在完成时:
He has guzzled two liters of soda already. = 他已经喝了两升苏打水。
They have guzzled all the snacks. = 他们已经把所有的零食都吃光了。

过去完成时:
She had guzzled a whole bottle of wine before driving home. = 她开车回家前已经喝了一整瓶酒。
They had guzzled all the beer before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经把所有的啤酒都喝光了。

将来时:
He will guzzle that whole pizza in minutes. = 他几分钟内就会把整个披萨吃光。
They will guzzle all the punch at the wedding reception. = 他们会在婚礼招待会上把所有的宾治酒都喝光。

虚拟语气:
If he had known the consequences, he wouldn't have guzzled so much. = 如果他知道后果，他就不会喝这么多。
I wish I hadn't guzzled that whole cake. = 我真希望我没有把整个蛋糕都吃掉。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The beer was guzzled quickly by the thirsty crowd. = 啤酒被口渴的人群迅速喝光。
All the food was guzzled in a few minutes. = 所有食物在几分钟内就被吃光了。

疑问句:
Is he guzzling again? = 他又在狂饮了吗？

否定句:
He doesn't usually guzzle his food. = 他通常不会狼吞虎咽地吃饭。
They didn't guzzle the wine, they sipped it slowly. = 他们没有狂饮葡萄酒，而是慢慢地品尝。
