marsala:/mɑːˈsɑːlə/| n. adj.|马萨拉酒；马萨拉色的|A fortified wine from Sicily, typically dark reddish-brown in color.  It is often used in cooking.|例句：The chef used a generous splash of marsala in the chicken dish.  厨师在鸡肉菜肴中加入了大量的马萨拉酒。The dress was a beautiful marsala color. 这件衣服是美丽的马萨拉色。|近义词：wine, sherry|反义词:N/A
