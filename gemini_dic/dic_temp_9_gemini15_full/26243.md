butterweed: /ˈbʌtərwiːd/ | n. |黄花蒿,油蒿 | A type of weed, typically with yellow flowers, belonging to the genus *Senecio*.  It is often considered a nuisance in gardens and fields.  The name likely refers to its buttery yellow flowers or a butter-like substance associated with the plant. |例句：The butterweed was spreading rapidly across the field.  油蒿在田野里迅速蔓延。|例句： We spent hours pulling butterweed out of the garden. 我们花了数小时从花园里拔除油蒿。|近义词：groundsel, ragwort (depending on the specific species)|反义词: (None directly applicable;  one might contrast it with a desirable plant)|词性:名词
