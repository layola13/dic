heroically:/hɪˈroʊɪkli/| adv.|英勇地；豪迈地；勇敢地 |In a heroic manner; bravely.  The firefighters heroically battled the blaze.  |例句：The soldiers fought heroically to defend their country.  士兵们英勇作战保卫他们的国家。


例句：She heroically faced her fears and spoke up. 她勇敢地面对恐惧，勇敢地说了出来。


近义词：bravely, courageously, valiantly, gallantly


反义词：cowardly, timidly, fearfully
