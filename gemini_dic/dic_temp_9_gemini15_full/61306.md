embarking:/ɪmˈbɑːrkɪŋ/ | v. | 开始；着手；乘船（飞机） | 开始从事某项工作或活动；登上（船、飞机等） |例句：He is embarking on a new career. 他正在开始一份新的职业。


例句：They are embarking on a long journey. 他们正在开始一次长途旅行。


近义词：commencing, starting, beginning, setting out


反义词：concluding, finishing, ending


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are embarking on a new project. = 他们正在着手一个新项目。
We embark on a new adventure every year. = 我们每年都开始一次新的冒险。


一般过去时:
She embarked on a writing career after college. = 她大学毕业后开始了写作生涯。
He embarked on a journey to find himself. = 他踏上了寻找自我的旅程。


现在完成时:
They have embarked on a ambitious plan to clean the ocean. = 他们已经开始了一个雄心勃勃的计划来清洁海洋。
We have embarked on a long and difficult process. = 我们已经开始了一个漫长而艰难的过程。


过去完成时:
Before he died, he had embarked on a final painting. = 他去世前，已经开始创作最后一幅画作。
She had embarked on a rigorous training program before the competition. = 比赛前，她已经开始了严格的训练计划。


将来时:
They will embark on a world tour next year. = 他们明年将开始世界巡演。
He will embark on a new chapter in his life. = 他将开始人生的新篇章。


虚拟语气:
If I had the courage, I would embark on that challenging project. = 如果我有勇气，我会着手那个具有挑战性的项目。
I wish I could embark on a journey around the world. = 我真希望我能环游世界。


被动语态:
The project was embarked on last month. = 该项目上个月启动了。
The mission was embarked upon with great enthusiasm. = 这项任务以极大的热情开始了。


疑问句:
When are they embarking on their trip? = 他们什么时候开始他们的旅行？
Are you embarking on a new business venture? = 你要开始新的商业冒险吗？


否定句:
He did not embark on the risky investment. = 他没有着手那项风险投资。
They are not embarking on this project without careful planning. = 他们不会在没有仔细计划的情况下开始这个项目。


PHRASE->
embark on a journey = 开始一段旅程
embark on a career = 开始职业生涯
embark on a project = 开始一个项目
embark on a new adventure = 开始新的冒险
embark upon a task = 着手一项任务
