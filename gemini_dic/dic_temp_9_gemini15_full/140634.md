olympian:/əˈlɪmpiən/ | n. | 奥运选手；卓越的人 | A person who competes in the Olympic Games; a person of exceptional qualities or abilities. | Example:  She's a true Olympian, both in her athletic achievements and her unwavering dedication. 她是一位真正的奥运选手，无论是她的运动成就还是她坚定不移的奉献精神。


Example: Many consider him an Olympian in the field of mathematics, given his groundbreaking contributions.  鉴于他在数学领域的开创性贡献，许多人认为他是一位杰出的数学家。


近义词: athlete, champion, star, virtuoso


反义词: amateur, nobody, insignificant



USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an Olympian. = 他是一位奥运选手。
She competes as an Olympian. = 她作为一名奥运选手参赛。
