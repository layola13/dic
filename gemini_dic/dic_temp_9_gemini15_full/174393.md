quieted:/ˈkwaɪətɪd/| v.|使平静；使安静；平息|To make quiet or still; to calm or pacify; to suppress or subdue.|例句：The teacher quieted the noisy classroom.老师使吵闹的教室安静下来。|例句：The storm eventually quieted down.暴风雨最终平息了。|近义词：silenced, calmed, hushed, subdued|反义词：excited, agitated, roused, stimulated|动词
