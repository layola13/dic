overcautiously:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəsli/ | adj. | 过分谨慎地；过于小心地 | Excessively cautious; showing too much care to avoid potential danger or risk.  |  He overcautiously avoided any risky investments, missing out on potential profits. 他过于谨慎地避免任何风险投资，错过了潜在的利润。  | cautiously, carefully, prudently | recklessly, carelessly, rashly | 形容词
