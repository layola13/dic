presoliciting:/ˌpriːˈsɒlɪsɪtɪŋ/ | v. |预先征求；提前招标 | The act of soliciting bids or proposals before officially announcing a project or need.  It is often done to gauge interest or obtain preliminary information.  | Example: The government was accused of presoliciting bids for the construction project, giving an unfair advantage to certain companies.  政府被指控在建设项目招标前预先征求意见，给了某些公司不公平的优势。 |近义词：pre-tendering, advance solicitation, preliminary bidding |反义词：post-soliciting,  late bidding
