firebombed:/ˈfaɪərˌbɒmbd/|/ˈfaɪərˌbɑːmbd/| vt.|纵火袭击；用燃烧弹袭击|To attack with firebombs.  The act of attacking a place or thing with firebombs.|例句：The building was firebombed during the riots.  暴乱期间，这座建筑物遭到燃烧弹袭击。
The protesters firebombed the police station in a show of defiance.  抗议者用燃烧弹袭击警察局以示反抗。|近义词：bombard, attack with incendiaries|反义词：extinguish, protect
