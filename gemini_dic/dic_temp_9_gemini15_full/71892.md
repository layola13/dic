flew-out: /fluː aʊt/ | v. | 飞出，飞离 | The verb "flew out" is the past tense of the phrasal verb "fly out."  It describes something or someone departing rapidly by air. It can refer to a literal flight, or a metaphorical departure. | 例句：The bird flew out of the cage.  鸟从笼子里飞了出来。 The angry words flew out before he could stop himself.  愤怒的话脱口而出，他来不及阻止。 | 近义词：departed, took off, escaped, zoomed out  | 反义词：flew in, arrived, landed
