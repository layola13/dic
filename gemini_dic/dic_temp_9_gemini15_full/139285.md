obliteration:/ˌɒblɪtəˈreɪʃən/| /ˌɑːblɪtəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |消灭；涂抹；忘却 | The act of obliterating or the state of being obliterated; complete destruction or removal of something.  It can refer to physical destruction, the erasure of something written or recorded, or the removal of something from memory or consciousness. |例句：The bombing caused the complete obliteration of the city.  轰炸导致了城市的彻底毁灭。

The earthquake resulted in the obliteration of several villages. 地震导致了几个村庄的消失。

近义词：destruction, annihilation, eradication, extinction, erasure |反义词：creation, preservation, construction, establishment
