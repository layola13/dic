exultingly:/ɪɡˈzʌltɪŋli/ | adv. | 狂喜地；得意洋洋地 |  Expressing or characterized by exultation; triumphantly joyful.  | 例句：He shouted exultingly, "We won!" 他高兴地喊道：“我们赢了！”

例句：She danced exultingly after hearing the good news.  她听到好消息后欣喜若狂地跳起了舞。


近义词：triumphantly, jubilantly, joyfully, gleefully


反义词：sadly, dejectedly, sorrowfully, miserably


词性：副词
