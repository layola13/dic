endpoint:/ˈɛndpɔɪnt/ | n. | 终点；端点；接口；终结点 | A point at which something ends;  the final point in a process, journey, or sequence.  It can also refer to a communication endpoint in a network or system, representing a device or application capable of sending and receiving data. |例句：The endpoint of the journey was a breathtaking view. 这段旅程的终点是一个令人叹为观止的景色。  The server acts as an endpoint for client requests. 服务器作为客户端请求的终结点。|近义词：terminus, extremity, conclusion, interface |反义词：beginning, start, origin
