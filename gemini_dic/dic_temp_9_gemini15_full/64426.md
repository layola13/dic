ergotin:/ˈɜːrɡəʊtɪn/| n.|麦角菌素，麦角碱|A complex mixture of alkaloids derived from ergot, a fungus that infects rye and other grains.  It has historically been used in medicine, but is potent and dangerous if not properly handled.  Ergotin's effects are due to its various constituent alkaloids, such as ergotamine and ergometrine.|The doctor prescribed ergotin to control postpartum bleeding.  医生开了麦角菌素来控制产后出血。|近义词：ergotism (the condition caused by ergot poisoning),  ergot alkaloids|反义词：无
