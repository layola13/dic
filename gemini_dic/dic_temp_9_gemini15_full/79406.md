glimpser:/ˈɡlɪmpsər/| n.|一瞥；短暂的体验|A person who glimpses something; one who catches a brief view.  A glimpse is a brief, incomplete view of something.  The word "glimpser" emphasizes the person experiencing this brief view.|例句：He was a glimpser of the future, catching fleeting visions of possibilities. 他是一位对未来的短暂预见者，捕捉到转瞬即逝的可能性景象。

例句：The mountain climber was a glimpser of breathtaking scenery. 这名登山者得以一瞥令人惊叹的景色。


近义词：viewer, observer, witness|反义词：无|词性:n.
