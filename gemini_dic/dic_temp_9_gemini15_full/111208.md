lithophane:/ˈlɪθəfeɪn/| n. |透光石雕;影透雕|A lithophane is a type of decorative object made of translucent porcelain or other material, where a relief image is created so that when backlit, the image is visible.  The variations in thickness of the material control the amount of light that passes through, creating a shaded image. |Example: The museum showcased a collection of antique lithophanes depicting scenes from classical mythology.  博物馆展出了一个收藏的古代透光石雕，描绘了古典神话中的场景。|近义词:relief sculpture, cameo|反义词:N/A
