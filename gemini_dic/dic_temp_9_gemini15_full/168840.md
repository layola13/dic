proempiricist: /ˌproʊɛmˈpɪrɪsɪst/ | adj. | 倾向经验主义的；拥护经验主义的 |  A proempiricist perspective emphasizes empirical evidence and observation in forming beliefs and theories.  It is a supporter of empiricism, which is a philosophical stance that emphasizes the role of experience and observation in the formation of knowledge. |  The proempiricist scientist insisted on rigorous experimentation before accepting any new theory. | 近义词：empiricist, experientialist | 反义词：rationalist, a priorist
