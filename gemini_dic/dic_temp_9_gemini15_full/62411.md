endogeny:/ɛnˈdɒdʒəni/|/ɪnˈdɒdʒɪni/| n. |内生性；内源性；内生作用|The origination or development of something from within an organism or system.  It refers to processes that originate internally, rather than externally.| Example: The study focused on the endogeny of the disease, exploring its internal causes rather than external factors.  这项研究侧重于该疾病的内生性，探索其内在原因而非外部因素。|近义词：intrinsicality, inherent nature|反义词：exogeny
