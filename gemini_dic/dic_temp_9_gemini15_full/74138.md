foundationer:/faʊnˈdeɪʃənər/ | n. | 基金会成员；（尤指）牛津大学或剑桥大学的捐赠生 | A foundationer is a student at a college or university who receives financial support from a foundation or endowment.  This term is particularly associated with Oxford and Cambridge Universities, where it traditionally referred to students who received financial aid to attend. | Example: He was a foundationer at Christ Church, Oxford, benefiting from a generous scholarship. 他是牛津大学基督教堂学院的捐赠生，受益于一笔丰厚的奖学金。 | 近义词：scholar, bursar (in some contexts) | 反义词：paying student, independent student | 名词
