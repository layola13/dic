recontends:/ˌriːkənˈtɛndz/| vt.|再争辩，再竞争|To contend again; to renew a contest or argument.|例句：He recontends the point, arguing that the evidence is insufficient. 他再次争辩这一点，认为证据不足。|例句：The two companies recontend for market share after a period of truce. 两家公司在休战一段时间后再次争夺市场份额。|近义词：reargue, dispute, contest|反义词：concede, agree, accept
