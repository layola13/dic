luxuriate:/ˈlʌkʃəriˌeɪt/| v.|尽情享受；沉溺于；豪华地装饰|To take great pleasure in something; to indulge oneself; to make something luxurious.|例句：She luxuriated in the warmth of the sun. 她尽情享受阳光的温暖。|例句：They luxuriated in their newfound wealth. 他们沉溺于他们新获得的财富。|近义词：indulge, revel, wallow|反义词：abstain, deny, deprive

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He luxuriates in his success. = 他沉溺于他的成功。
They luxuriate in the beautiful scenery. = 他们尽情享受美丽的景色。

一般过去时:
She luxuriated in a long hot bath. = 她痛痛快快地洗了个热水澡。
We luxuriated in the comfort of our hotel room. = 我们享受着酒店房间的舒适。

现在完成时:
He has luxuriated in his retirement. = 他一直享受着退休生活。
She has luxuriated in the peace and quiet of the countryside. = 她一直沉醉于乡村的宁静与安详。

过去完成时:
They had luxuriated in the sunshine all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都享受着阳光。
She had luxuriated in the thought of her upcoming vacation. = 她一直沉浸在即将到来的假期的憧憬中。

将来时:
We will luxuriate in the spa tomorrow. = 我们明天将在水疗中心尽情享受。
They will luxuriate in the comfort of their new home. = 他们将享受他们新家的舒适。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would luxuriate in a long holiday. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会享受一个长假。
I wish I could luxuriate in a quiet evening at home. = 我希望我能在家享受一个宁静的夜晚。

被动语态:
The room was luxuriated with expensive furniture. = 房间里摆满了昂贵的家具，显得十分豪华。 (This usage is less common and often implies passive embellishment rather than active enjoyment.)


疑问句:
Do you ever luxuriate in a good book? = 你经常沉醉于阅读好书吗？
How often do you luxuriate in a long soak in the bath? = 你多久享受一次长时间的泡澡？

否定句:
He didn't luxuriate in the attention he received. = 他并不享受他所受到的关注。
They couldn't luxuriate in their success because of the looming deadline. = 由于迫在眉睫的截止日期，他们无法尽情享受他们的成功。


PHRASE->
luxuriate in comfort = 享受舒适
luxuriate in silence = 享受宁静
luxuriate in the sunshine = 享受阳光
luxuriate in the beauty = 享受美丽
luxuriate in idleness = 沉溺于懒散


词源:
源自拉丁语 *luxuriāri，意为“茂盛生长，繁荣”。  The sense of "to grow luxuriantly" evolved into the current meaning of enjoying oneself richly.
