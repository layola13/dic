celebrious:/səˈlɛbriəs/| adj.|著名的，驰名的|Famous; renowned; celebrated.  The word suggests a level of fame associated with widespread recognition and admiration, often for achievements or qualities that are considered exceptional or noteworthy. |例句：His celebrious achievements in the field of medicine earned him numerous awards. 他在医学领域的著名成就为他赢得了无数奖项。|近义词：famous, renowned, celebrated, illustrious, eminent|反义词：obscure, unknown, anonymous
