dept:/dɛpt/ | n. | 部门，系；深度 | A dept is a division or section of a larger organization, such as a university department or a government department.  It can also refer to the depth of something. |例句：The marketing dept is responsible for advertising. 市场部负责广告宣传。  The dept of the wound was alarming. 伤口深度令人担忧。|近义词：division, section, branch, unit |反义词：surface (in the context of depth)


USAGE->
