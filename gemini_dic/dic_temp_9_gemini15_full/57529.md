dragster:/ˈdræɡstər/| n.|竞速赛车，拖曳赛车|A dragster is a type of race car designed for short, straight-line races (drag racing). They are characterized by their powerful engines, lightweight construction, and streamlined bodies optimized for maximum acceleration.  |例句：The dragster roared down the track, leaving a trail of smoke. 这辆竞速赛车轰鸣着冲下赛道，留下了一条烟雾轨迹。

The dragster reached speeds exceeding 300 mph. 这辆竞速赛车达到了超过300英里的时速。|近义词：race car, hot rod|反义词：无|名词
