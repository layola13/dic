prechilled:/ˌpriːˈtʃɪld/| adj.|预冷的，预先冷却的|Pre-cooled beforehand; already chilled.|The prechilled soup was served immediately. 预先冷却的汤立即上桌了。|近义词：chilled, cooled, refrigerated|反义词：heated, warmed
