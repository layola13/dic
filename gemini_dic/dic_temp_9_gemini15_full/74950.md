friable:/ˈfraɪəbəl/| adj.|易碎的；易粉碎的；易崩解的|easily crumbled or pulverized; readily breaking into small pieces.|The friable soil crumbled easily under the pressure.|近义词：crumbly, brittle, powdery|反义词：solid, hard, tough


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The soil is friable. = 土壤易碎。
The cake is friable and delicious. = 蛋糕松软可口。


一般过去时:
The cookie was friable after it cooled. = 饼干冷却后变得易碎。
The clay was friable, making it easy to mold. = 粘土易碎，易于成型。


现在完成时:

The old building's foundation has become friable over time. = 老建筑的地基随着时间的推移变得脆弱。


过去完成时:
The bread had become friable before we could eat it. = 我们还没来得及吃面包就变碎了。
The ancient pottery had become friable due to weathering. = 古代陶器由于风化而变得易碎。


将来时:

The rock will become more friable after the freeze-thaw cycles. = 经过冻融循环后，岩石会变得更容易破碎。


虚拟语气:
If the soil were friable, planting would be easier. = 如果土壤松软，种植就会更容易。
I wish the cookie wasn't so friable; it broke when I picked it up. = 我真希望饼干不要这么易碎；我拿起来的时候它就碎了。


被动语态:
The sample was easily crumbled because it was friable. = 样本很容易碎，因为它很易碎。
The material is considered friable because it breaks down easily. = 这种材料被认为是易碎的，因为它很容易分解。


疑问句:
Is the ground friable enough to plant seeds? = 地面够松软可以播种吗？
Is the sandstone friable and easy to carve? = 砂岩易碎且易于雕刻吗？


否定句:
