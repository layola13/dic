overdogmatically:/ˌoʊvərˌdɒɡˈmætɪkli/|/ˌoʊvərˌdɑːɡˈmætɪkli/| adj.| 过于教条地；武断地 | Excessively dogmatic; asserting opinions in a rigid and inflexible way.  It implies a stubborn adherence to beliefs without considering alternative viewpoints or evidence. |The professor presented his theory overdogmatically, refusing to entertain any counterarguments. 教授武断地提出了他的理论，拒绝接受任何反驳意见。|近义词：dogmatically, rigidly, inflexibly, stubbornly, imperiously|反义词：open-mindedly, flexibly, tolerantly, reasonably
