dumb-ass: /ˌdʌmˈæs/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜 |  A derogatory term for a stupid or foolish person. It combines "dumb" (meaning stupid) and "ass" (a rude term for a foolish person).  The term is highly offensive and should be avoided in formal settings. | Example: "Don't be such a dumb-ass!" = "别那么傻！" | 近义词：idiot, moron, fool, jerk | 反义词：genius, intelligent, clever |  名词
