packager:/ˈpækɪdʒər/| n. |打包者；包装商；软件包管理器|A person or company that packages goods or software.  It can also refer to a software tool that manages software packages.|例句：The packager carefully sealed the fragile items. 打包者小心地密封了易碎物品。

The software packager made it easy to install the new application. 软件包管理器使安装新应用程序变得容易。|近义词：wrapper, assembler, bundler |反义词：unpackager (less common)|名词
