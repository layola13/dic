coatis:/ˈkoʊ.ə.tiːz/| n. |卷尾猴；科阿蒂|Coatis are small to medium-sized mammals belonging to the family Procyonidae, which also includes raccoons and kinkajous.  They are characterized by their long, ringed tails and their elongated snouts.|Example: We saw a family of coatis foraging for food in the rainforest. 我们在雨林里看到一群卷尾猴在觅食。|近义词：ringtail (referring to the tail)|反义词：无
