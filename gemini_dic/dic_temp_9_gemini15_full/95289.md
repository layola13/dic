incarcerations:/ɪnˌkɑːrsəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. |监禁；关押；拘留 |The act of imprisoning someone.  The plural form refers to multiple instances of imprisonment. |例句：The high rate of incarcerations in the US is a matter of serious concern.  美国的监禁率之高令人担忧。

例句：Recent studies show a correlation between poverty and increased incarcerations. 最近的研究表明贫困与监禁率增加之间存在相关性。


近义词：imprisonments, confinements, detentions |反义词：releases, liberations


词源:  From the verb "incarcerate," which comes from Late Latin *incarcerare, from Latin carcer "prison."  The prefix "in-" indicates "into" or "in."
