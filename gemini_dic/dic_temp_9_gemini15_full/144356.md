outstaying:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪɪŋ/| adj. | 优秀的，杰出的；逾期的；持续存在的 | Outstanding work deserves recognition. 杰出的工作应该得到认可。|近义词：excellent, exceptional, prominent | 反义词：inferior, mediocre, insignificant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His work is outstanding. = 他的工作很出色。
The view is outstanding. = 景色很出色。


一般过去时:
Her performance was outstanding. = 她的表现非常出色。
The project was outstanding. = 这个项目很优秀。


现在完成时:
He has produced outstanding results. = 他取得了显著的成果。
She has an outstanding loan. = 她有一笔未偿还的贷款。


过去完成时:
The problem had been outstanding for months. = 这问题已经存在好几个月了。
They had already completed the outstanding tasks. = 他们已经完成了未完成的任务。


将来时:
The results will be outstanding. = 结果将会很出色。
The payment will be outstanding until next month. = 付款将拖延到下个月。


虚拟语气:
If the work were outstanding, it would be awarded. = 如果工作出色，它将获奖。
If only the results were outstanding. = 但愿结果出色。


被动语态:
The bills are outstanding. = 账单尚未支付。
The matter is outstanding. = 这件事尚未解决。


疑问句:
Are there any outstanding issues? = 还有什么未解决的问题吗？
Is the payment still outstanding? = 付款是否仍然未付？


否定句:
His performance was not outstanding. = 他的表现并不出色。
There are no outstanding problems. = 没有未解决的问题。
