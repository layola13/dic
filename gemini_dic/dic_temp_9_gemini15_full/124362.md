motivate:/ˈmoʊtɪveɪt/| vt.|激励，激发，驱动|to provide someone with a reason for doing something:|The teacher motivated her students to learn more.|近义词：inspire, encourage, stimulate|反义词：discourage, demotivate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He motivates his team to achieve their goals. = 他激励他的团队实现他们的目标。
This new strategy motivates employees to work harder. = 这一新策略激励员工更加努力工作。

一般过去时:
The speech motivated the audience to take action. = 这篇演讲激励听众采取行动。
The reward motivated him to finish the project on time. = 奖励激励他按时完成项目。

现在完成时:
His passion has motivated countless people. = 他的热情激励了无数人。
The success of the project has motivated the team to take on new challenges. = 项目的成功激励团队承担新的挑战。

过去完成时:
Having been motivated by her teacher, she pursued her dream. = 在老师的激励下，她追逐着自己的梦想。
The previous success had motivated them to try again. = 之前的成功激励他们再次尝试。

将来时:
The upcoming competition will motivate them to train harder. = 即将来临的比赛将激励他们更加努力训练。
This new policy will motivate employees to be more productive. = 这项新政策将激励员工提高生产力。

虚拟语气:
If only I had been motivated earlier, I would have achieved more. = 如果我早点被激励的话，我会取得更大的成就。
I wish my teacher had motivated me more during high school. = 我希望我的老师在高中时能更多地激励我。

被动语态:
The students were motivated by the teacher's inspiring words. = 学生们被老师鼓舞人心的言语所激励。
The team was motivated to improve their performance. = 团队被激励去提高他们的表现。

疑问句:
What motivates you to work so hard? = 什么激励你如此努力工作？
What motivated her to make such a bold decision? = 什么激励她做出如此大胆的决定？

否定句:
Nothing motivated him to change his ways. = 没有什么能激励他改变他的方式。
The lack of recognition demotivated the employees. = 缺乏认可使员工失去了动力。


PHRASE->

highly motivated = 高度积极主动的
self-motivated = 自我激励的
stay motivated = 保持动力
motivated employee =  积极主动的员工
