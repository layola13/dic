re-booked:/ˌriːˈbʊkt/| v.|重新预订|To book something again, typically after a previous booking has been cancelled or changed.  This implies a prior booking existed.|例句：I had to re-book my flight after the airline cancelled my original reservation. 我不得不重新预订我的航班，因为航空公司取消了我的原预订。

例句：We re-booked the restaurant table for a later time. 我们将餐馆的桌子重新预订到了稍晚的时间。


近义词：re-schedule, re-arrange

反义词：cancel


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-book their holiday every year. = 他们每年都重新预订他们的假期。
We re-book the same hotel every summer. = 我们每年夏天都重新预订同一家酒店。


一般过去时:
She re-booked her appointment for next week. = 她将她的预约重新安排到下周。
He re-booked the tickets after missing the first flight. = 他在错过第一次航班后重新预订了机票。


现在完成时:
They have re-booked their tickets several times due to flight delays. = 由于航班延误，他们已经几次重新预订了机票。
I have re-booked the concert tickets after they were sold out. =  演唱会门票售罄后，我已经重新预订了。


过去完成时:
I had re-booked the hotel room before the cancellation notice arrived. = 在收到取消通知之前，我已经重新预订了酒店房间。
She had re-booked the flight because the original one was overbooked. = 她已经重新预订了航班，因为原来的航班超员了。


将来时:
We will re-book the table if the weather is bad. = 如果天气不好，我们将重新预订桌子。
They will re-book their vacation if they find a better deal. = 如果他们找到更好的交易，他们将重新预订他们的假期。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the flight was cancelled, I would have re-booked earlier. = 如果我知道航班被取消了，我会早点重新预订。
I wish I had re-booked my hotel sooner; now all the rooms are full. = 我真希望我早点重新预订酒店；现在所有的房间都满了。


被动语态:
The flight was re-booked for the following day. = 航班被重新预订到第二天。
The tickets were re-booked without any extra charge. = 机票被重新预订，没有任何额外费用。


疑问句:
Have you re-booked your appointment yet? = 你已经重新预订你的预约了吗？
Will they re-book the venue for the conference? = 他们会为会议重新预订场地吗？


否定句:
I did not re-book the tickets because the price was too high. = 我没有重新预订机票，因为价格太高了。
They couldn't re-book their trip at short notice. = 他们无法短时间内重新预订他们的旅行。
