controvert:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːt/ /ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrt/ | vt. | 反驳；驳斥；争论 | To deny or oppose (something, especially a statement or theory)  |  He controverted the witness's testimony. 他反驳了证人的证词。 |  dispute, challenge, contradict |  confirm, support, agree | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He controverts the scientist's findings. 他反驳了这位科学家的发现。
They controvert the proposed legislation.  他们反对拟议的法案。


一般过去时:
She controverted his claims in the debate.  她在辩论中反驳了他的说法。
The lawyer controverted the evidence presented by the prosecution.  律师反驳了检方提供的证据。


现在完成时:
Scientists have controverted the theory for years. 科学家们多年来一直反驳这一理论。
He has controverted every point made in the article. 他反驳了文章中提出的每一个论点。


过去完成时:
They had controverted the agreement before the final signing. 他们在最终签署之前就已反驳了该协议。
She had controverted his explanation several times. 她已经几次反驳了他的解释。


将来时:
He will controvert the accusations in court. 他将在法庭上反驳这些指控。
They will controvert the decision at the next meeting. 他们将在下次会议上反驳这一决定。


虚拟语气:
If he controverted the evidence, the case would be dismissed. 如果他反驳了证据，案件就会被驳回。
I wish he had controverted the accusations earlier. 我希望他早点反驳这些指控。


被动语态:
His arguments were controverted by the opposing team. 他的论点被对方团队反驳了。
The new theory is being controverted by many experts.  许多专家正在反驳这一新理论。


疑问句:
Does he controvert the findings of the study? 他是否反驳了这项研究的结果？
Will they controvert the proposal at the meeting? 他们会在会议上反驳这个提议吗？


否定句:
He did not controvert the evidence presented. 他没有反驳所提供的证据。
She does not controvert the general consensus. 她不反驳普遍的共识。


PHRASE->
controvert a claim  反驳一项说法
controvert a theory 反驳一种理论
controvert an argument 反驳一个论点
controvert a statement 反驳一个说法
controvert the evidence 反驳证据
