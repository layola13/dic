hippiedoms:/ˈhɪpiˌdəmz/| n. |嬉皮士文化，嬉皮士社会|The social and cultural phenomenon of the hippie movement, characterized by its countercultural values, ideals, and lifestyle.  It often refers to the collective aspects of hippie culture rather than individual hippies.|例句：The remnants of the 1960s hippiedoms are still visible in some parts of San Francisco.  六十年代嬉皮士文化的残余在旧金山的一些地方仍然可见。|近义词：hippie culture, counterculture, bohemianism |反义词：establishment, mainstream culture |名词
