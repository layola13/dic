repellents:/rɪˈpɛlənts/| n.|驱虫剂，防腐剂|Substances that repel or prevent the approach of something unwanted, typically insects or pests.|Example:  Mosquito repellents are essential during the summer months.  夏季蚊虫驱蚊剂必不可少。

Example: We used a wood repellent to protect the fence from rot. 我们用木材防腐剂来保护栅栏免受腐烂。|近义词：deterrents, repulsants|反义词：attractants, lures|名词
