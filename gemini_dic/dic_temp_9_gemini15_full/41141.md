copublished:/ˌkoʊˈpʌblɪʃt/| adj. |合作出版的；联合出版的|Published by two or more publishers jointly.  This term describes a book or other publication that has been produced through a collaborative effort between two or more publishing houses. This often happens for books with a wider appeal or those requiring specialized expertise from different publishers.|例句：This book was copublished by Oxford University Press and Cambridge University Press. 这本书是由牛津大学出版社和剑桥大学出版社合作出版的。|近义词：jointly published, collaboratively published |反义词：solely published, individually published |形容词
