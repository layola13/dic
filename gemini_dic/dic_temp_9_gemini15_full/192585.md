sculptured:/ˈskʌlptʃərd/| adj.|雕刻的；雕塑的|Shaped or formed by carving or molding; having the appearance of being carved or molded.  The adjective describes something that resembles or has been created through the process of sculpting.|例句：The sculptured marble statue stood proudly in the garden.  花园里骄傲地矗立着一尊雕刻的大理石雕像。
The walls were adorned with intricately sculptured reliefs. 墙壁上装饰着精美的浮雕。

近义词：carved, molded, modeled, chiseled
反义词：unshaped, unformed, plain
