envisions:/ɪnˈvɪʒən/| vt.|想象；预想；设想|To form a mental picture of (something) yet to happen; to imagine or conceive of as existing in the future.|例句：The architect envisioned a building that would be both beautiful and functional. 建筑师设想了一座既美观又实用的建筑物。

例句：She envisioned a future where everyone had access to clean water. 她设想了一个未来，每个人都能获得清洁用水。


近义词：imagine, visualize, foresee, contemplate, picture

反义词：ignore, disregard, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He envisions a bright future for his company. = 他设想公司拥有光明的未来。
They envision a world without poverty. = 他们设想一个没有贫困的世界。


一般过去时:
The artist envisioned a breathtaking landscape. = 艺术家想象出一幅令人叹为观止的风景画。
She envisioned her wedding day in great detail. = 她详细地想象着她的婚礼。


现在完成时:
We have envisioned a new strategy for marketing. = 我们已经设想了一个新的营销策略。
They have envisioned a sustainable solution to the problem. = 他们已经设想了一个可持续的解决方案。


过去完成时:
He had envisioned a successful career before the accident. = 事故发生前，他曾设想过一个成功的职业生涯。
She had envisioned a different life for herself. = 她曾设想过另一种人生。


将来时:
They will envision a new product line next year. = 他们明年将构想一条新的产品线。
We will envision a better way to achieve our goal. = 我们将设想一个更好的方法来实现我们的目标。


虚拟语气:
If I had envisioned the consequences, I would have acted differently. = 如果我能预见后果，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had envisioned the difficulties involved. = 我希望我能预见到所涉及的困难。


被动语态:
A brighter future is envisioned by many people. = 许多人设想了一个更光明的未来。
A new solution is being envisioned by the team. = 团队正在设想一个新的解决方案。


疑问句:
What do you envision for the future? = 你对未来有什么设想？
What did they envision when they started the project? = 他们开始这个项目时设想的是什么？


否定句:
He did not envision the difficulties he would face. = 他没有预见到他将面临的困难。
She didn't envision such a dramatic change. = 她没有预料到如此巨大的变化。
