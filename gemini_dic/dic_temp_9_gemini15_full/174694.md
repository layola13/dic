quotations:/ˌkwoʊˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 引语；报价；引文 |  A quotation is a group of words taken from a text or speech and repeated by someone else.  It can refer to a passage from a book, article, speech, etc., or to a price given for something. |  Example 1: The essay included several quotations from Shakespeare.  这个论文包含了几段莎士比亚的引语。| Example 2:  We received several quotations for the repair work. 我们收到了几份维修报价。 |近义词：citation, excerpt, quote | 反义词：original text, paraphrase
