overfeeding: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːdɪŋ/ | n. | 过度喂养；喂食过多 | The act of feeding someone or something excessively.  It often leads to negative consequences, such as obesity, health problems, and behavioral issues. |例句：Overfeeding your pet can lead to obesity and health problems. 过度喂养你的宠物会导致肥胖和健康问题。

例句：The overfeeding of livestock contributed to the environmental pollution.  牲畜的过度喂养导致了环境污染。


近义词：overeating, gorging, stuffing


反义词：undernourishment, starvation, malnourishment


词性：名词
