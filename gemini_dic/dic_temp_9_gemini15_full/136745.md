nonsensuous:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnʃuəs/| adj. |非感官的；不感性的；无感觉的 |Not relating to or affecting the senses; lacking sensuousness or appeal to the senses.  The abstract painting was described as nonsensuous, lacking the tactile and visual richness of more traditional styles.|例句：The architecture of the building was purely functional, almost nonsensuous. 这座建筑的建筑风格纯粹是功能性的，几乎没有感官上的吸引力。

例句：The mathematical proof, while elegant, was completely nonsensuous to those without a strong background in the subject.  这个数学证明虽然很优雅，但对于那些没有扎实学科背景的人来说完全没有感官上的理解。


近义词：insensible, unfeeling, unemotional, abstract, intellectual


反义词：sensuous, sensual, emotional, passionate


词性:形容词
