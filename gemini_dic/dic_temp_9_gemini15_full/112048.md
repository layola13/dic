longboats:/ˈlɒŋˌboʊts/ | n. | 长船 | A large open boat propelled by oars or sails, typically used for carrying cargo or people.  Longboats were common in Viking times and later periods.  |例句：The Vikings used longboats to raid coastal settlements.  维京人用长船袭击沿海定居点。

The crew rowed the longboat ashore. 船员们把长船划到岸边。|近义词：galley, barge, whaler |反义词：canoe (in terms of size), dinghy (in terms of size)
