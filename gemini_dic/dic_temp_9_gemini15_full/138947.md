nutty:/ˈnʌti/ | adj. | 疯狂的；古怪的；略带疯狂的；味道像坚果的 |

英文解析:  Describes someone who is eccentric, slightly crazy, or behaving in an unusual way.  It can also describe something that tastes like nuts.

例句:

He's a bit nutty, always doing unexpected things.  = 他有点古怪，总是做一些出乎意料的事情。

That cake has a nutty flavor. = 那块蛋糕有一种坚果的味道。


近义词: crazy, mad, eccentric, whimsical, insane(for the "crazy" meaning); nutty,  nuty(for the "nut-flavored" meaning)


反义词: sane, rational, normal, sensible (for the "crazy" meaning); bland, tasteless (for the "nut-flavored" meaning)


词性: adjective
