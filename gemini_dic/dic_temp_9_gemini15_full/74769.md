freeze-frame:/friːz freɪm/| n. |冻结画面，定格画面 |A freeze-frame is a still image taken from a video or film.  It's a moment paused in time, often used for emphasis or to highlight a particular detail. |Example: The director used a freeze-frame to emphasize the character's shocked expression.  导演用冻结画面来强调人物震惊的表情。|近义词：still image, paused image |反义词：moving image, continuous shot |名词
