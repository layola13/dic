counterargument:/ˈkaʊntərˌɑːrɡjumənt/| n. |反驳，反论，对立派论点 |A counterargument is a statement or argument that contradicts or opposes another argument.  It's used to challenge or refute a claim made in the original argument. |Example:  The author presents a compelling argument for stricter gun control, but a strong counterargument could be made about the Second Amendment rights of individuals.  作者提出了加强枪支管制的令人信服的论点，但可以提出关于个人第二修正案权利的有力反驳。|近义词：rebuttal, refutation, objection |反义词：support, confirmation, corroboration |名词
