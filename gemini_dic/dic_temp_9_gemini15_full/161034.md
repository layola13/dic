postcode:/ˈpoʊstkoʊd/ /ˈpəʊstkəʊd/ | n. |邮政编码|A postcode is a series of letters and/or numbers used to identify a specific postal area for the purpose of efficient mail sorting and delivery.  It's crucial for ensuring mail reaches the correct address. |Example: My postcode is SW1A 2AA. 我的邮政编码是SW1A 2AA。|近义词：zip code (US)|反义词：无
