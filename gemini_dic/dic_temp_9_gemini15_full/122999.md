monarchic:/məˈnɑːrkɪk/| adj.|君主制的；君主统治的| Relating to a monarch or monarchy; characterized by the rule of a monarch.  |例句：The country has a long and proud monarchic tradition. 这个国家有着悠久而自豪的君主制传统。|例句：The monarchic system was eventually replaced by a republic. 君主制最终被共和国取代。|近义词：regal, royal, kingly, queenly|反义词：republican, democratic

一般过去时:

Several countries have transitioned away from monarchic rule. = 几个国家已经从君主统治过渡到其他统治形式。

虚拟语气:
If the country were monarchic, the situation might be different. = 如果这个国家是君主制国家，情况可能会有所不同。
I wish this nation hadn't abandoned its old monarchic traditions. = 我希望这个国家没有抛弃它古老的君主制传统。


被动语态:
