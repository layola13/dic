outfrothing:/aʊtˈfroʊθɪŋ/ | v. | 溢出泡沫；大量涌出 |  The overflowing abundance or profusion of something, especially foam or froth;  to overflow with foam or froth.  It implies a vigorous and abundant outpouring.  | 例句：The beer was outfrothing from the overflowing glass.  啤酒从溢出的杯子里大量涌出。 The river, swollen with rain, was outfrothing at the edges.  雨水使河水暴涨，河边泛起大量泡沫。 | 近义词：overflow, foam over, surge, bubble over | 反义词：subside, settle, calm
