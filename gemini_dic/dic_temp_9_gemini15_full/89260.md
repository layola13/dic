hominoids:/ˈhɒmɪnɔɪdz/ /ˈhɑːmɪnɔɪdz/ | n. |类人猿；人猿总科动物 |  Hominoids are a superfamily of primates that includes humans, apes, and their extinct ancestors.  They are characterized by their relatively large brain size, lack of a tail, and adaptations for upright posture and bipedal locomotion (in some species). |  The great apes, including gorillas, chimpanzees, orangutans, and humans, are all hominoids. |近义词：apes (less precise)|反义词：无
