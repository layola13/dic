lardier:/ˈlɑːrdiər/| n. |猪油桶；盛猪油的容器|A lardier is a container, traditionally made of wood, used for storing lard.  It's often characterized by its size and capacity to hold a significant quantity of lard.|The old farmhouse kitchen still housed a large lardier, filled with the rendered fat from last autumn's pig.|近义词：lard tub, lard crock|反义词：无
