negative:/ˈneɡətɪv/| adj., n.|消极的；负面的；否定的；负数 |  relating to or expressing denial, rejection, or opposition; characterized by or expressing negativity; less than zero.  | 例句：He has a negative attitude towards work. 他对工作态度消极。  | 例句：The test result was negative. 检测结果呈阴性。|近义词：pessimistic, unfavorable, adverse |反义词：positive, affirmative, favorable |形容词，名词
