reopening:/ˌriˈoʊpənɪŋ/| n. |重新开放；再次营业|The act of opening something again after it has been closed.  It can refer to a business, a facility, or an event. |例句：The theater's reopening was a huge success. 剧院的重新开放非常成功。

例句：After renovations, the museum's reopening attracted many visitors.  装修后，博物馆的重新开放吸引了众多游客。


近义词：reactivation, resumption, recommencement |反义词：closure, shutdown, closing |词性：名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop is reopening next week. = 商店下周重新开业。
The school is reopening after the holiday. = 学校假期后重新开学。


一般过去时:
The restaurant reopened last month. = 餐厅上个月重新开业了。
The factory reopened after the strike. = 罢工后工厂重新开工了。


现在完成时:
The store has reopened since the renovations. = 商店自从装修后就重新开业了。
They have reopened the investigation into the case. = 他们已经重新对该案进行了调查。


过去完成时:
The museum had reopened before the anniversary. = 博物馆在周年纪念日之前已经重新开放了。
They had reopened the discussion after a long break. = 他们经过长时间的休息后重新开始讨论。


将来时:
The cinema will reopen next spring. = 电影院将于明年春天重新开放。
The bridge will reopen to traffic next month. = 这座桥将于下个月重新通车。


虚拟语气:
If the funding were approved, the project would reopen. = 如果资金得到批准，项目就会重新启动。
I wish the restaurant would reopen soon. = 我希望这家餐厅能尽快重新开业。


被动语态:
The road was reopened after the repairs. = 道路维修后重新开放了。
The case is being reopened for further investigation. = 此案正在重新调查。


疑问句:
When is the shop reopening? = 商店什么时候重新开业？
Has the school reopened yet? = 学校重新开学了吗？


否定句:
The business didn't reopen after the pandemic. = 这家企业疫情后没有重新开业。
The investigation wasn't reopened. = 调查没有重新启动。


PHRASE->

reopening ceremony:重新开业仪式
