centurys:/ˈsɛntʃəriːz/| n. |世纪（复数）| Centuries are periods of 100 years.  The word refers to multiple periods of 100 years. |Example: Many significant events have occurred throughout the centuries.  几个世纪以来发生了许多重要事件。|近义词：ages, eras |反义词：None (as it's a plural form)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Centuries pass slowly. = 世纪缓慢流逝。
The centuries bring changes. = 世纪带来变化。


一般过去时:
Several centuries passed before the discovery. = 发现之前过去了几个世纪。
Past centuries witnessed great empires. = 过去的几个世纪见证了伟大的帝国。


现在完成时:
Many centuries have passed since then. = 从那时起已经过去了许多个世纪。
Several centuries have witnessed technological advancements. = 几个世纪以来见证了技术的进步。


过去完成时:
Centuries had passed before the technology was developed. = 这项技术开发之前已经过去了几个世纪。
By the time the war ended, centuries had passed since the conflict began. = 当战争结束时，自冲突开始以来已经过去了几个世纪。


将来时:
Future centuries will face new challenges. = 未来几个世纪将面临新的挑战。
The coming centuries will bring about unforeseen changes. = 未来几个世纪将带来不可预见的变化。


虚拟语气:
If centuries could speak, they would tell amazing stories. = 如果几个世纪能说话，它们会讲述惊人的故事。
I wish I could have lived through several centuries. = 我希望我能经历几个世纪。


被动语态:
The events of past centuries are often misinterpreted. = 过去几个世纪的事件常常被误解。
The impact of those centuries is still being felt today. = 那些世纪的影响至今仍在感受到。


疑问句:
How many centuries have passed since the Roman Empire? = 自罗马帝国以来过了多少个世纪？
Which centuries were most significant for scientific discovery? = 哪些世纪对科学发现最重要？


否定句:
Not all centuries were peaceful. = 并非所有世纪都是和平的。
The following centuries did not bring an end to conflict. = 接下来的几个世纪并没有结束冲突。
