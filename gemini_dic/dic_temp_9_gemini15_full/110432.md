likelihood:/ˈlaɪklihʊd/ | n. |可能性，概率 | The likelihood of success is high.成功的可能性很高。| probability, chance, possibility | impossibility, unlikelihood | 名词

英文解析:The likelihood of something is the chance or probability that it will happen.  It refers to the degree to which something is likely to occur.

例句：

The likelihood of rain is high today.  今天下雨的可能性很大。

There's a high likelihood that the project will be completed on time.  该项目按时完成的可能性很高。


近义词：probability, chance, possibility, prospect


反义词：impossibility, unlikelihood


词性：名词
