coatrooms:/ˈkoʊtruːmz/| n.|衣帽间，行李寄存处|A room or area where coats, bags, and other belongings can be left for safekeeping, often found in public places such as theaters, restaurants, or schools.|Example:  Please leave your coats in the coatrooms before entering the theater. 请在进入剧院之前将外套放在衣帽间。|近义词：cloakroom, checkroom|反义词：无
