forging:/ˈfɔːrdʒɪŋ/ | n. v. |锻造；伪造；创建；形成 |The creation of something, typically metal, by heating and hammering; the act of making something fake or false; the process of gradually developing something. |例句：The blacksmith was skilled in forging swords.  铁匠擅长锻造宝剑。  He was accused of forging documents. 他被指控伪造文件。|近义词：fabrication, counterfeiting, shaping, molding |反义词：destruction, dismantling, authenticating
