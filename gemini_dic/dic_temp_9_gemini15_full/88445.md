hist:/hɪst/ | n. |历史，历史学|The study of past events, particularly in human affairs.|例句：History is a vast and complex subject. 历史是一个浩瀚而复杂的学科。|例句：We learned about ancient history in school. 我们在学校学习了古代史。|近义词：past, chronicle, annals |反义词：future, present


USAGE->
一般现在时:
History repeats itself. = 历史总是重演。
History teaches us valuable lessons. = 历史教会我们宝贵的教训。

一般过去时:
The historian studied the ancient civilization. = 历史学家研究了古代文明。
The battle of Waterloo was a significant historical event. = 滑铁卢战役是一场重要的历史事件。

现在完成时:
Historians have uncovered new evidence. = 历史学家们发现了新的证据。

过去完成时:
By the time the war ended, much history had already been made. = 到战争结束时，许多历史已经创造了。

将来时:
Future historians will study our time. = 未来的历史学家将研究我们的时代。
We will learn more about the history of this region. = 我们将学习更多关于这个地区的历史。

虚拟语气:
If I had studied history, I would know more about this topic. = 如果我学习过历史，我会对这个话题了解更多。
I wish I had more time to study history. = 我希望我有更多时间学习历史。

被动语态:
