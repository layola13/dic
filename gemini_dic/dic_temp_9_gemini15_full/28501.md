cardamoms: /ˈkɑːdəməmz/ | n. |豆蔻(复数) |The dried seed pods of several plants in the ginger family, used as a spice.| 例句：The aroma of cardamoms filled the air. 豆蔻的香味充满了空气。


The recipe calls for a generous amount of cardamoms. 这个食谱需要大量的豆蔻。


近义词：spice, aromatic seeds | 反义词：无


词源:  The word "cardamom" comes from the Latin word "cardamomum," which in turn is derived from Greek κάρδαμον (kárdamon) and ultimately from an ancient Semitic root.  The precise origin within Semitic languages is debated but it likely refers to the plant itself.
