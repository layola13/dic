overdedicating: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |过度投入；过度奉献|  To dedicate excessively; to give more time, effort, or resources to something than is necessary or reasonable.  It implies a level of commitment that is potentially harmful or unproductive. |例句：He was overdedicating himself to work, neglecting his health and family. 他过度投入工作，忽略了健康和家庭。

例句：She overdedicated to the project, burning herself out before the deadline. 她过度投入项目，在截止日期前把自己累垮了。


近义词：overcommitting, overinvesting, overdoing,  excessive dedication


反义词：neglecting, ignoring, undercommitting
