counterrevolutionary: /ˌkaʊntərˌrevəˈluːʃənəri/ | adj., n. | 反革命的；反革命分子 |

英文解析:  Opposed to or seeking to overthrow a revolution or revolutionary government.  The adjective describes someone or something that opposes a revolution. The noun refers to a person who actively works against a revolution.

例句:

The government cracked down on counterrevolutionary activities.  政府镇压了反革命活动。

He was labeled a counterrevolutionary and imprisoned. 他被贴上反革命分子的标签并被监禁。


近义词:  reactionary, anti-revolutionary, subversive

反义词: revolutionary


词性: adjective, noun
