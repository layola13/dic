chlorimetry:/klɔːˈrɪmətri/| n. |比色测定法|The quantitative determination of a substance by measuring the intensity of its color.  This is typically done by comparing the color of a solution of the substance to the color of a series of standard solutions of known concentration.| 例句：Chlorimetry is a widely used technique in analytical chemistry for determining the concentration of various substances. 比色测定法是分析化学中广泛使用的测定各种物质浓度的技术。

The results of the chlorimetry experiment showed a high concentration of the target compound. 比色测定实验的结果显示目标化合物的浓度很高。|近义词：colorimetry, spectrophotometry|反义词：None|
