propitiator: /prəˈpɪʃieɪtər/ | n. | 息事宁人者；抚慰者；奉献祭品者 | One who propitiates; one who seeks to appease or conciliate.  A propitiator tries to win favor by making offerings or gestures of appeasement. | Example:  The king sent a propitiator to the angered gods with gifts of gold and incense.  国王派了一个使者带着金银香料去安抚愤怒的神明。 |近义词: appeaser, conciliator, pacifier | 反义词: antagonizer, provoker
