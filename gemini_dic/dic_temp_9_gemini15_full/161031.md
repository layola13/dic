postchlorination:/ˌpoʊstˌklɔːrɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |次氯消毒|The process of adding chlorine to water after other treatment processes have been completed. This is done to ensure that the water remains disinfected until it reaches the consumer.  |例句：Postchlorination is a crucial step in ensuring the safety of drinking water. 次氯消毒是确保饮用水安全的重要步骤。|例句：The water treatment plant utilizes postchlorination to maintain disinfection throughout the distribution system.  该水处理厂采用次氯消毒来维持整个配水系统的消毒效果。|近义词：disinfection, sterilization |反义词：dechlorination
