screenwriter:/ˈskriːnˌraɪtər/| n. |编剧|A person who writes scripts for films, television programs, or video games.| 例句：The screenwriter crafted a compelling narrative that kept the audience engaged.  这位编剧精心打造了一个引人入胜的故事，让观众始终保持兴趣。

例句：The award-winning screenwriter is known for her unique style.  这位屡获殊荣的编剧以其独特的风格而闻名。


近义词：scriptwriter, scenario writer | 反义词：director (in the sense of being the opposite role in film production)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The screenwriter writes a new script every year. = 编剧每年都写一个新的剧本。
She often works with a director to develop the script. = 她经常与导演合作开发剧本。

一般过去时:
He wrote the screenplay for a famous movie. = 他为一部著名电影创作了剧本。
The screenwriter adapted the novel into a successful film script. = 编剧将这部小说改编成一部成功的电影剧本。

现在完成时:
She has written several award-winning screenplays. = 她已经创作了几部获奖的剧本。
The studio has hired a new screenwriter for their next project. = 制片厂为他们的下一个项目聘请了一位新的编剧。

过去完成时:
Before the filming, he had already rewritten the script several times. = 在拍摄之前，他已经修改了剧本好几次。
They had completed the first draft of the screenplay before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经完成了剧本的第一稿。

将来时:
She will write the screenplay for the sequel next year. = 她明年将为续集创作剧本。
The studio will hire a team of screenwriters for the ambitious project. = 制片厂将为这个雄心勃勃的项目聘请一个编剧团队。

虚拟语气:
If I were a screenwriter, I would write a story about my hometown. = 如果我是一个编剧，我会写一个关于我家乡的故事。
I wish I had the talent to be a successful screenwriter. = 我希望我有能力成为一名成功的编剧。

被动语态:
The screenplay was written by a famous author. = 剧本是由一位著名作家创作的。
The script is being rewritten by the director. = 剧本正在由导演改写。

疑问句:
Who wrote the screenplay for this movie? = 这部电影的剧本是谁写的？
Is the screenwriter still working on the project? = 编剧还在参与这个项目吗？

否定句:
He didn't write the script on time. = 他没有按时写完剧本。
The studio didn't hire the screenwriter they initially wanted. = 制片厂没有聘用他们最初想要的编剧。
