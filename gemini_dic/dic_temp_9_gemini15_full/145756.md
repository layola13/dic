overcultivations:/ˌoʊvərˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃənz/| n.|过度耕作；过度栽培|The excessive cultivation of land, leading to soil degradation and depletion.  Overcultivations can result in decreased yields and environmental damage.|例句：The overcultivations of the farmland led to soil erosion and reduced crop production.  过度耕作导致土壤侵蚀和作物减产。

The continuous overcultivations without proper soil management practices have severely depleted the nutrients in the soil.  持续的过度耕作而没有适当的土壤管理措施严重耗尽了土壤中的养分。|近义词：overfarming, excessive cultivation|反义词：fallow, crop rotation
