price:/praɪs/| n. |价格，价钱，代价|The price of gasoline has increased recently.  汽油价格最近上涨了。  The price of freedom is eternal vigilance. 自由的代价是永恒的警惕。|近义词：cost, charge, fee, valuation|反义词：value (in the sense of intrinsic worth)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price of milk is high. = 牛奶的价格很高。
What is the price of this car? = 这辆车的价格是多少？


一般过去时:
The price of gold was lower last year. = 去年金价更低。
What was the price of that house? = 那栋房子的价格是多少？


现在完成时:
The price of oil has risen sharply. = 油价已大幅上涨。
Has the price of tickets changed? = 门票价格变了没有？


过去完成时:
The price had already fallen before we bought it. = 我们买之前价格已经下跌了。
What had the price been before the sale? = 折价前价格是多少？


将来时:
The price will likely increase next month. = 价格可能下个月会上涨。
What will the price be tomorrow? = 明天的价格是多少？


虚拟语气:
If the price were lower, I would buy it. = 如果价格更低，我会买它。
I wish the price wasn't so high. = 我希望价格不要这么高。


被动语态:
The price is determined by supply and demand. = 价格由供求关系决定。
The price was unfairly inflated. = 价格被不公平地抬高了。


疑问句:
What's the price? = 价格是多少？
How much is the price? = 价格是多少？


否定句:
The price is not reasonable. = 价格不合理。
The price isn't affordable for me. = 这个价格我买不起。


PHRASE->
at a price = 付出代价
at any price = 不惜任何代价
for the price of = 以...的价格
price war = 价格战
fixed price = 固定价格
