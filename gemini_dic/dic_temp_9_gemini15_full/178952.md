redecline:/ˌriːdɪˈklaɪn/| v.|重新下降；再次衰退；再次倾斜|To decline again; to decrease or deteriorate once more.  The word implies a previous decline that has been followed by a further downturn.|例句：After a brief period of improvement, the economy redeclined. = 在短暂的改善之后，经济再次衰退。

例句：The patient's condition redeclined after a period of apparent recovery. = 病人在一段时间的明显康复后，病情再次恶化。


近义词：decline again, deteriorate further, worsen, relapse|反义词：improve, recover, rise, ascend|词性:动词
