outsworn:/aʊtˈswɔːrn/| adj.|宣誓否认的；被宣誓驳斥的|  Having been sworn against or denied under oath.  This word is primarily used in a legal or formal context, referring to something that has been officially refuted or denied through a sworn statement.|例句：The witness's testimony was outsworn by the defendant's alibi.  证人的证词被被告的不在场证明所驳斥。

The evidence presented was outsworn by the opposing counsel’s arguments.  提出的证据被对方律师的论点所驳斥。|近义词：refuted, denied, contradicted|反义词：confirmed, verified, substantiated|词性：形容词
