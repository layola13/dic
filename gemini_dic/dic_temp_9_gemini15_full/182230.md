remorseless: /rɪˈmɔːrsləs/ | adj. | 无情的；冷酷的；不后悔的 |  Without pity or compassion; merciless.  Showing no regret or remorse. | 例句：The remorseless killer showed no sign of guilt.  这名冷酷无情的杀手没有表现出任何内疚的迹象。

例句：He delivered his criticisms with remorseless honesty. 他以无情的诚实提出了他的批评。


近义词：merciless, pitiless, ruthless, relentless, unrepentant, callous

反义词：merciful, compassionate, repentant, remorseful
