jurists:/ˈdʒʊərɪsts/| n.|法学家；法律专家|Experts in law, especially those who are scholars or practitioners of law.  They often interpret and apply legal principles.|例句：The jurists debated the implications of the new law.  法学家们辩论了新法律的含义。|例句：Many renowned jurists contributed to the development of legal theory. 许多著名的法学家为法律理论的发展做出了贡献。|近义词：legal scholars, lawyers, judges, attorneys|反义词：layperson (in legal matters), layman
