ringside:/ˈrɪŋsaɪd/| n.|拳击台边；最佳位置|The area immediately surrounding a boxing ring; a position affording the best view or opportunity.|例句：The ringside seats offered an unparalleled view of the fight. 拳击台边的座位提供了无与伦比的比赛视野。

例句：He secured a ringside seat to witness the historic event. 他获得了观看这一历史性事件的最佳位置。

近义词：front row, best seat, prime location

反义词：back row, distant view
