morphinated:/ˈmɔːrfɪneɪtɪd/| adj.|吗啡化的；受吗啡影响的 | Affected by or treated with morphine.  The term often implies a state of being heavily influenced by morphine, leading to sedation, analgesia, and potentially addiction.|例句：The patient was morphinated to alleviate severe post-surgical pain. 患者被注射吗啡以减轻严重的术后疼痛。

The morphinated soldier drifted in and out of consciousness.  吗啡注射的士兵时而清醒时而昏迷。|近义词：drugged, narcotized, opiated|反义词：sober, undrugged
