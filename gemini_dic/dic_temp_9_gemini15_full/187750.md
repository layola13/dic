roots:/ruːts/|ˈruːts/| n.|根；根源；祖先；词根|The origins or fundamental causes of something.  The part of a plant that attaches it to the ground and absorbs water and nutrients.|例句：The roots of the tree are deep in the ground. 这棵树的根扎得很深。 The roots of the problem are complex.  这个问题的根源很复杂。|近义词：origin, source, base, foundation|反义词：top, surface, outcome, result


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The tree has strong roots. = 这棵树根系发达。
What are the roots of this conflict? = 这场冲突的根源是什么？


一般过去时:
The ancient civilization traced its roots back thousands of years. = 这个古老的文明追溯其起源于数千年前。
We discovered the roots of the plant were damaged. = 我们发现植物的根系受损了。


现在完成时:
They have finally found the roots of the problem. = 他们终于找到了问题的根源。
The investigation has uncovered the roots of the corruption. = 调查揭露了腐败的根源。


过去完成时:
By then, the roots of the plant had already rotted. = 到那时，植物的根系已经腐烂了。
Before the discovery, scientists had not understood the roots of the disease. = 在这项发现之前，科学家们并不了解这种疾病的根源。


将来时:
The project will explore the roots of social inequality. = 这个项目将探究社会不平等的根源。
Scientists believe they will soon identify the roots of the illness. = 科学家们相信他们很快就能确定这种疾病的根源。


虚拟语气:
If the roots were deeper, the tree would survive the drought. = 如果根扎得更深，这棵树就能挺过干旱。
I wish I knew the roots of his anger. = 我真希望知道他生气的原因。


被动语态:
The roots of the building were damaged by the earthquake. = 建筑的地基在地震中受损了。
The origins of the tradition are thought to be rooted in ancient mythology. = 这种传统的起源被认为根植于古代神话。


疑问句:
What are the roots of your family history? = 你家族历史的根源是什么？
Where do the roots of this plant go? = 这棵植物的根系延伸到哪里？


否定句:
The plant didn't have strong enough roots to survive the storm. = 这棵植物的根系不够发达，无法抵御暴风雨。
Their argument didn't have any real roots. = 他们的争论没有真正的根源。
