krakowiak:/kraˈkɔvjæk/| n.|克拉科维亚克舞曲|A lively Polish folk dance in 2/4 or 4/4 time, characterized by its vigorous and sometimes acrobatic movements. It is often accompanied by music played on fiddles, and is traditionally performed in pairs.|例句：The dancers performed a spirited krakowiak, their feet moving with incredible speed and precision.  舞者们表演了一支充满活力的克拉科维亚克舞曲，他们的脚步移动得令人难以置信的快速而精准。|近义词：polonaise (a different Polish dance)|反义词：无|名词
