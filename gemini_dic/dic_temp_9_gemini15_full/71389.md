flagpole:/ˈflæɡˌpoʊl/| n. |旗杆 |A tall pole from which a flag is flown.  A flagpole is typically made of wood or metal and is often seen in front of government buildings, schools, or private homes. |Example: The American flag was proudly waving from the flagpole. 美国国旗骄傲地飘扬在旗杆上。|近义词：flagstaff|反义词：无
