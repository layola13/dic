handcuffed:/ˈhændˌkʌft/| adj. |上手铐的，被铐住的 |  Being restrained with handcuffs.  |例句：The suspect was handcuffed and taken to the police station. 嫌疑犯被戴上手铐带到了警察局。  |近义词：restrained, shackled, manacled |反义词：unhandcuffed, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police officer handcuffs the suspect. = 警官给嫌疑犯戴上手铐。
He is handcuffed to the chair. = 他被铐在椅子上。

一般过去时:
The thief was handcuffed after the robbery. = 抢劫后，小偷被戴上手铐。
They handcuffed him to the other prisoner. = 他们把他和另一个囚犯铐在一起。

现在完成时:
The police have handcuffed several protesters. = 警方已经逮捕了几名抗议者。
He has been handcuffed for hours. = 他已经被铐了几个小时了。

过去完成时:
The suspect had been handcuffed before they arrived. = 嫌疑犯在他们到达之前就已经被戴上手铐了。
She had handcuffed herself to the gates. = 她把自己铐在了大门上。

将来时:
The police will handcuff the criminal. = 警方将逮捕罪犯。
They will be handcuffed and transported to jail. = 他们将被戴上手铐并押送监狱。

虚拟语气:
If they had handcuffed him earlier, he wouldn't have escaped. = 如果他们早点把他铐起来，他就不会逃跑了。
I wish I hadn't been handcuffed. = 我真希望我没有被铐起来。

被动语态:
The prisoner was handcuffed to the wall. = 囚犯被铐在墙上。
The suspects are being handcuffed by the officers. = 嫌疑犯正在被警官们戴上手铐。

疑问句:
Were the suspects handcuffed? = 嫌疑犯被戴上手铐了吗？
Is he handcuffed to the bed? = 他被铐在床上吗？

否定句:
The criminal wasn't handcuffed properly. = 罪犯的手铐没有戴好。
They weren't handcuffed during the interrogation. = 他们在审讯期间没有被戴上手铐。


PHRASE->
