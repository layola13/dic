conformal:/kənˈfɔːrməl/ | adj. | 保形的，符合的 | Conforming in form or shape;  corresponding in shape or form.  It describes something that preserves angles and shapes during a transformation.  | 例句：The map projection is conformal, preserving the angles between intersecting lines.  该地图投影是保形的，保持相交线之间的角度。


例句：A conformal mapping transforms shapes without distorting angles. 保形映射变换形状而不扭曲角度。


近义词：isometric, isogonal | 反义词：non-conformal


词源：From Latin *conformis ("of like form"), from com- ("together") + forma ("form", "shape").
