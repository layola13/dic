nests:/nestz/| n.|鸟巢；巢穴；藏匿处|A nest is a structure built by birds, insects, or other animals to hold their eggs and young.  It can also refer to a place where something is hidden or sheltered.|例句：The birds built a nest in the tree.  鸟儿在树上筑巢。

例句：The police searched the house for clues, suspecting it to be a nest of criminals. 警方搜查了这栋房子寻找线索，怀疑它是一个罪犯窝点。


近义词：lair, haven,  hideout|反义词：none|词性：名词
