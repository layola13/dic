mummification: /ˌmʌmɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 木乃伊化；防腐处理 | The process of embalming and drying a body to prevent decay, especially as practiced by the ancient Egyptians.  | Example: The mummification process involved removing internal organs and applying resins.  木乃伊化过程包括移除内脏和涂抹树脂。 | 近义词：embalming | 反义词：decomposition, decay | 名词
