siphuncular:/sɪˈfʌŋkjʊlə(r)/| adj.|  虹吸管的；似虹吸管的 | Relating to or resembling a siphon.  The term often describes structures or processes that involve the movement of fluids in a manner similar to a siphon. | Example: The siphuncular canal in the nautilus shell facilitates gas exchange.  鹦鹉螺壳中的虹吸管有助于气体交换。|近义词：siphon-like, tube-like |反义词：None specifically defined.
