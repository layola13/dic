encurtain:/ɪnˈkɜːrtn/|v.|幕布拉上；遮蔽；掩盖|To cover or hide something with a curtain or something similar.  The word is rarely used and often considered archaic or literary.|例句：The play ended, and the stage was encurtained. 剧终，舞台上幕布落下。  The memories were encurtained by the passage of time.  时间冲淡了记忆。|近义词：conceal, obscure, shroud, veil|反义词：reveal, uncover, expose|动词
