sanctums:/ˈsæŋktəmz/| n. |圣所；密室；隐秘处 |A sanctum is a private place where one can be alone and undisturbed; a holy place.  It often implies a place of refuge, peace, or serious work. |例句：He retreated to his sanctum to write his novel. 他退回到自己的密室去写小说。

例句：The library was his personal sanctum, a place of quiet contemplation. 图书馆是他个人的圣所，一个安静沉思的地方。


近义词：retreat, refuge, sanctuary, haven

反义词：public place, open space

词性:名词
