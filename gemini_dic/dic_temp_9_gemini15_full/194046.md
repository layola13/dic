self-glorying: /ˈsɛlf ˈɡlɔːriɪŋ/ | adj. | 自吹自擂的；自负的 |  Self-glorying refers to the act of excessively praising oneself or one's achievements, often in a boastful or arrogant manner. It implies a preoccupation with one's own importance and a disregard for the contributions of others. | 例句：His self-glorying speech alienated many of his colleagues.  他的自吹自擂的演讲疏远了他的许多同事。

His self-glorying posts on social media annoyed his friends. 他在社交媒体上自吹自擂的帖子惹恼了他的朋友们。


近义词：vain, boastful, self-aggrandizing, narcissistic, egotistical | 反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, self-effacing


词性：形容词
