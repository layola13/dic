词源:

The word "chromoleucite" is a combination of two parts:

* **chromo-**: From Greek *chroma* (χρῶμα), meaning "color". This prefix indicates the presence of color.
* **leucite**: From Greek *leukos* (λευκός), meaning "white".  This refers to the base mineral leucite, which is typically white or colorless.


因此，“chromoleucite”字面意思是“有色白榴石”，指因杂质而呈现颜色的白榴石。
