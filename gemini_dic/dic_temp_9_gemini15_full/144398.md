outstripped:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪpt/| vt.|超过，胜过 |To surpass or exceed (someone or something) in performance or achievement. |例句：Our sales outstripped those of our competitors. 我们的销售额超过了竞争对手。|例句：The demand for the product quickly outstripped supply. 产品的需求迅速超过了供应。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outpace, transcend|反义词：lag behind, fall short of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outstrips his rivals in the race. 他在比赛中超过了他的对手。
The company outstrips its competitors in innovation. 这家公司在创新方面超过了竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outstripped all expectations. 她超出了所有人的预期。
The team outstripped their previous record. 这个团队超过了他们之前的纪录。

现在完成时:
The growth of the economy has outstripped inflation. 经济的增长已经超过了通货膨胀。
Technological advancements have outstripped our ability to regulate them. 技术进步已经超过了我们监管它们的能力。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, their profits had outstripped those of the previous year. 到年底，他们的利润已经超过了前一年。
Before the merger, one company had already outstripped the other in market share. 在合并之前，一家公司已经在市场份额上超过了另一家。

将来时:
We expect that our production will outstrip demand next year. 我们预计明年的产量将超过需求。
Experts predict that artificial intelligence will outstrip human capabilities in some areas. 专家预测人工智能将在某些领域超过人类的能力。

虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we might outstrip our competitors. 如果我们更努力工作，我们可能会超过我们的竞争对手。
If only we had planned better, we could have outstripped the competition. 如果我们计划得更好，我们本可以超过竞争对手。


被动语态:
They were outstripped by the newcomer. 他们被新来者超过了。
Our efforts were outstripped by the scale of the problem. 我们的努力被问题的规模所超过。

疑问句:
Did he outstrip the other runners in the marathon? 他在马拉松比赛中超过其他跑步者了吗？
Will our new product outstrip the existing ones on the market? 我们的新产品会超过市场上现有的产品吗？

否定句:
We did not outstrip our sales targets this quarter. 本季度我们没有超过我们的销售目标。
Their performance did not outstrip expectations. 他们的表现没有超出预期。


PHRASE->
outstrip the competition  超过竞争对手
outstrip expectations 超出预期
outstrip the demand 超过需求
outstrip previous records 超过以往的记录
outstrip the growth of  超过...的增长
