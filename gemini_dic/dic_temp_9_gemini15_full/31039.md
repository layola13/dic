chamberlains:/ˈtʃeɪmbərˌleɪnz/ | n. |侍从长；大臣；（尤指）宫廷大臣 |A chamberlain is a high-ranking official, especially one in a royal court, responsible for managing affairs of the royal household.  The term can also refer to officials in other contexts, such as in a city or organization. |例句：The king's chamberlain oversaw the running of the royal palace. 国王的侍从长负责管理皇宫的运作。

The chief chamberlain of the city council presented the budget. 市议会的首席大臣提交了预算。|近义词：official, minister, steward |反义词：subject, commoner
