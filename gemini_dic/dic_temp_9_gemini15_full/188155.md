routed:/ˈraʊtɪd/ | v. | 打败；使溃败；规划路线；|to defeat decisively; to plan a route; to send (data) along a particular path in a network.| The army was routed by the enemy.  军队被敌人打败了。|近义词：defeated, overwhelmed, vanquished, routed | 反义词：victorious, successful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The software routes all incoming traffic to the server. = 软件将所有传入流量路由到服务器。
He carefully routes his daily commute to avoid traffic. = 他仔细规划他的日常通勤路线以避免交通拥堵。


一般过去时:
The general routed the enemy forces in a decisive battle. = 将军在一场决定性战役中击败了敌军。
They routed the package through a different shipping company. = 他们通过不同的运输公司运输包裹。
