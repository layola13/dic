nobility:/nəʊˈbɪləti/ | n. |贵族；高贵；高尚 | The quality of being noble in character, mind, birth, or rank;  the class of people holding hereditary titles.  |例句：The nobility of his character shone through in his actions.  他的高尚品格在他的行动中显露无遗。

例句：The British nobility once held immense power.  英国贵族曾经拥有巨大的权力。


近义词：aristocracy, gentry, high-mindedness, elevation

反义词：commonality, vulgarity, baseness


词源：From Old French *nobilité, from Latin nobilitas "nobility, fame, distinction," from nobilis "well-known, famous, distinguished," from noscere "to know."
