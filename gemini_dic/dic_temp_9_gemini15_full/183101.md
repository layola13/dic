reporter:/rɪˈpɔːrtər/| n. |记者，通讯员|A person who reports news for newspapers, television, or radio.  A reporter gathers information and writes or broadcasts news stories.|Example: The reporter interviewed the eyewitness to the accident.  这位记者采访了事故的目击者。

The reporter filed her story before the deadline.  这位记者在截止日期前提交了她的报道。|journalist, correspondent, newsman| censor, editor (in some contexts)| noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The reporter writes a news story every day. = 记者每天写一篇新闻报道。
The reporters interview many people for their stories. = 记者们为他们的报道采访很多人。


一般过去时:
The reporter interviewed the mayor yesterday. = 记者昨天采访了市长。
The reporters covered the election campaign extensively. = 记者们广泛报道了竞选活动。


现在完成时:
The reporter has written several articles on climate change. = 这位记者已经撰写了几篇关于气候变化的文章。
The reporters have already filed their reports. = 记者们已经提交了他们的报告。


过去完成时:
The reporter had already left before the press conference began. = 在新闻发布会开始之前，记者已经离开了。
The reporters had gathered all the necessary information before writing the story. = 记者们在撰写报道之前已经收集了所有必要的信息。


将来时:
The reporter will cover the upcoming summit. = 记者将报道即将召开的峰会。
The reporters will be live broadcasting from the scene. = 记者们将从现场进行现场直播。


虚拟语气:
If the reporter were here, he would ask some tough questions. = 如果记者在这里，他会问一些尖锐的问题。
I wish the reporter had more time to investigate the story. = 我希望记者有更多时间调查这个故事。


被动语态:
The story was written by a young reporter. = 这篇文章是一位年轻记者写的。
The press conference was covered by many reporters. = 新闻发布会受到了许多记者的报道。


疑问句:
Is the reporter still working on the story? = 记者还在写报道吗？
What did the reporter say in her report? = 记者在她的报道中说了什么？


否定句:
The reporter didn't have time to finish the story. = 记者没有时间完成报道。
The reporters didn't reveal the source of their information. = 记者们没有透露他们信息来源。
