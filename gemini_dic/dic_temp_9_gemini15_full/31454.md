charlatanically:/ˌʃɑːrləˈtænɪkli/| adv.|江湖骗子般地，骗人的，装腔作势地|In a manner characteristic of a charlatan; fraudulently or deceptively.  It implies a pretense of knowledge or skill that one does not possess.|例句：He charlatanically claimed to cure cancer with a simple herbal remedy. 他江湖骗子般地声称可以用简单的草药治疗癌症。


例句：The politician charlatanically promised economic prosperity without offering any concrete plans.  这位政客装腔作势地承诺经济繁荣，却没有提供任何具体的计划。


近义词：fraudulently, deceptively, deceptively, impostrously, shamly


反义词：honestly, genuinely, authentically, truthfully


词性：副词
