seismographs:/ˈsaɪzməɡræfs/| n. |地震仪|An instrument that measures and records ground motion, especially that caused by earthquakes.|例句：Scientists use seismographs to detect and study earthquakes.  科学家们用地震仪探测和研究地震。|近义词：seismometer|反义词:None
