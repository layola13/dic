cirriferous:/sɪˈrɪfərəs/| adj.|具卷须的；有卷须的|Bearing or producing cirri (slender, thread-like appendages).  The word describes organisms or structures that possess these delicate, hair-like or thread-like projections.|Example: The cirriferous plant displayed its delicate, thread-like tendrils.|近义词：cirrate, tendriled|反义词：acirrate
