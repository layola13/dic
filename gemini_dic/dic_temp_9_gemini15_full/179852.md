reexplanations:/ˌriːˌɛkspləˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |重新解释；再次解释 | Repeated explanations or clarifications of something previously explained.  It involves going over the explanation again, often with more detail or from a different perspective. |  The professor offered reexplanations of the complex theorem to ensure everyone understood. 教授对复杂的定理进行了重新解释，以确保每个人都理解。 The student's reexplanations were still unclear.  学生的再次解释仍然不清楚。| clarifications, elaborations, reinterpretations | misunderstandings, misinterpretations |  n.
