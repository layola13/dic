obliquing:/əˈbliːkɪŋ/| v.|倾斜；歪斜；不正视 | To move or lie at an angle; to slant.  It also implies a subtle or indirect approach or manner. |例句：The tower is obliquing slightly to the north. 塔略微向北倾斜。

The politician obliqued the question, avoiding a direct answer.  这位政客回避了这个问题，没有直接回答。|近义词：slanting, tilting, skewing, angling |反义词：straightening, vertical, direct

词源:
