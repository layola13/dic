make a dent in: /meɪk ə dent ɪn/ | phrasal verb | 对…造成影响；对…有所削减；取得进展 | To make a noticeable difference or reduction in something; to make progress in something. | 例句：We hope to make a dent in the national debt. 我们希望能够减少国家债务。

例句：Their fundraising efforts made a significant dent in the hospital's operating deficit.  他们的筹款努力显著减少了医院的运营赤字。


近义词：reduce, decrease, diminish, impact, affect,  lessen


反义词：increase, augment, enlarge, expand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They make a dent in their savings every month. = 他们每个月都在减少储蓄。
We make a dent in the workload by working overtime. = 我们通过加班来减少工作量。


一般过去时:
The new marketing campaign made a dent in sales figures. = 新的营销活动对销售数据有所影响。
She made a dent in her to-do list by finishing three tasks. = 她完成了三项任务，并在待办事项清单上取得了进展。


现在完成时:
We have made a dent in the problem, but more work is needed. = 我们已经对这个问题有所改善，但还需要做更多工作。
They have made a dent in the mountain of paperwork. = 他们已经处理了一部分堆积如山的文书工作。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had made a dent in their debt. = 到年底，他们已经减少了债务。
Before the sale, they had made little dent in their inventory. = 在促销之前，他们的库存几乎没有减少。


将来时:
We will make a dent in the project by next week. = 我们下周将在这个项目上取得进展。
This new strategy will make a dent in our market share loss. = 这项新战略将减少我们市场份额的损失。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we could make a bigger dent in the project. = 如果我们更努力工作，我们就能在这个项目上取得更大的进展。
I wish we could make a dent in the poverty problem. = 我希望我们能够对贫困问题有所改善。


被动语态:
A significant dent was made in the unemployment rate. = 失业率显著下降。
Little dent has been made in the environmental problem. = 环境问题几乎没有改善。


疑问句:
Will this new policy make a dent in crime rates? =这项新政策会降低犯罪率吗？
Has our marketing campaign made a dent in brand awareness? = 我们的营销活动是否提高了品牌知名度？


否定句:
The new law hasn't made a dent in the problem of drug abuse. = 新法律并没有解决吸毒问题。
Their efforts did not make a dent in the overall situation. = 他们的努力并没有改变整体局面。
