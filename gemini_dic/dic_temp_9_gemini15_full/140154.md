offender:/əˈfɛndər/ | n. | 罪犯，冒犯者 | A person who has committed a crime or offense.  |Example: The police apprehended the offender after a lengthy investigation.  警察在经过长时间的调查后逮捕了罪犯。|近义词：criminal, culprit, perpetrator |反义词：victim, innocent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The offender often commits petty crimes. = 这个罪犯经常犯轻罪。
He offends people with his rude remarks. = 他用粗鲁的言辞冒犯他人。


一般过去时:
The offender escaped from prison last night. = 罪犯昨晚越狱了。
He offended his friend with his insensitive joke. = 他用不敏感的笑话冒犯了他的朋友。


现在完成时:
The police have apprehended the offender. = 警方已经逮捕了罪犯。
He has offended many people with his behavior. = 他因为自己的行为冒犯了许多人。


过去完成时:
The judge had already sentenced the offender before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，法官已经判决了罪犯。
He had offended her deeply before he apologized. = 在道歉之前，他已经深深地冒犯了她。


将来时:
The police will arrest the offender soon. = 警方很快就会逮捕罪犯。
He will offend more people if he doesn't change his attitude. = 如果他不改变态度，他会冒犯更多的人。


虚拟语气:
If he were not an offender, he wouldn't be in jail. = 如果他不是罪犯，他就不会在监狱里。
I wish I hadn't offended him. = 我真希望我没有冒犯他。


被动语态:
The offender was arrested by the police. = 罪犯被警方逮捕了。
Many people were offended by his speech. = 许多人被他讲话冒犯了。


疑问句:
Who is the offender? = 罪犯是谁？
Was anyone offended by my comment? = 我的评论有冒犯到任何人吗？


否定句:
He is not an offender; he is innocent. = 他不是罪犯；他是无辜的。
I didn't mean to offend anyone. = 我无意冒犯任何人。
