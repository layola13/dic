palpate:/ˈpælpeɪt/| vt.|触诊，摸诊|To examine (a part of the body) by touch, especially to detect abnormalities.|例句：The doctor palpated her abdomen to check for any tenderness. 医生触诊了她的腹部以检查是否有压痛。|近义词：feel, examine, touch|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor palpates the patient's abdomen. = 医生触诊病人的腹部。
She palpates her breast regularly for lumps. = 她定期触摸她的乳房检查肿块。

一般过去时:
He palpated the patient's pulse. = 他触诊了病人的脉搏。
The nurse palpated the injured area gently. = 护士轻轻地触诊了受伤的部位。

现在完成时:
I have palpated several patients today. = 我今天已经触诊了几位病人。
She has never palpated a human heart before. = 她以前从未触诊过人的心脏。

过去完成时:
