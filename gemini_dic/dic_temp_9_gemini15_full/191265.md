scattergraphs:/ˈskætərɡræfs/| n. |散点图 |A scatter graph (also called a scatter plot, scatter diagram, or scatter chart) is a type of chart used to visually represent the relationship between two sets of data.  Each data point is represented as a dot on the chart, and the position of the dot is determined by its values on the two axes.  Scatter graphs are useful for identifying trends, correlations, and outliers in data. |The scatter graph clearly showed a positive correlation between ice cream sales and temperature. 散点图清晰地显示了冰淇淋销量和温度之间的正相关关系。|chart, diagram, plot |(none specifically antonymic)|名词
