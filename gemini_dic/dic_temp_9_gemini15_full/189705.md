salsify:/ˈsælsɪfaɪ/| n.|  菜蓟, 旱芹| A plant with a long taproot that is eaten as a vegetable.  The taproot is often long and fleshy, and the leaves are long and lanceolate. | Salsify is a delicious root vegetable that is often roasted or sautéed.  烤或炒菜蓟根是一种美味的根茎类蔬菜。| 近义词：oyster plant, vegetable oyster | 反义词：无
