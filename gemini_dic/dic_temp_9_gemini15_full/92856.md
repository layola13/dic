icy:/ˈaɪsi/| adj.|冰冷的，冰冷的，结冰的|Covered with or containing ice; extremely cold.  It can also describe a person's manner as cold and unfriendly.|例句：The roads were icy and dangerous.  道路结冰且危险。|例句：Her icy stare made him uncomfortable. 她冰冷的目光让他感到不舒服。|近义词：frosty, frigid, glacial|反义词：warm, balmy, friendly
