/ˈriːtiən/| adj.|莱蒂期（地质年代）的|Of or relating to the Rhaetian, the uppermost stage of the Late Triassic Epoch, characterized by distinctive fossils and geological formations.  The Rhaetian is a geological age and stage spanning from approximately 208.5 to 201.3 million years ago.  It represents a transition period between the Triassic and Jurassic periods. |The Rhaetian beds in this region are rich in plant fossils.|近义词：Late Triassic|反义词：无
