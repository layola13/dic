procuring: /prəˈkjʊərɪŋ/ | v. | 获取；获得；采购 | Obtaining or acquiring something, especially with care or effort. |例句：The company is procuring new equipment. 这家公司正在采购新设备。

The government is procuring essential supplies for the disaster victims. 政府正在为灾民采购必需品。

近义词：obtaining, acquiring, securing, getting, purchasing | 反义词：losing, relinquishing, surrendering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are procuring new software for the office. = 他们正在为办公室采购新的软件。
The company procures its supplies from various vendors. = 这家公司从不同的供应商那里采购供应品。


一般过去时:
She procured a rare book for her collection. = 她为自己的收藏购得一本珍稀书籍。
We procured the necessary permits before starting the project. = 我们在项目开始前获得了必要的许可证。


现在完成时:
They have procured all the materials needed for the construction. = 他们已经采购了建筑所需的所有材料。
The organization has procured funding for its new program. = 该组织已经为其新项目筹集到了资金。


过去完成时:
They had procured the land before they began building the house. = 他们在开始建造房子之前就已经获得了土地。
She had procured a visa before her trip to Europe. = 她去欧洲旅行之前已经办好了签证。


将来时:
We will procure the necessary equipment next week. = 我们下周将采购必要的设备。
The company will procure the services of a consultant. = 公司将聘请一位顾问的服务。


虚拟语气:
If they could procure the necessary funds, they would start the project. = 如果他们能够筹集到必要的资金，他们就会启动项目。
I wish I could procure a ticket to the concert. = 我真希望我能弄到一张音乐会的票。


被动语态:
The materials were procured from a local supplier. = 材料是从当地供应商那里采购的。
The necessary permits are being procured by the city council. = 必要的许可证正在由市议会办理。


疑问句:
Where did you procure that antique vase? = 你从哪里买到那个古董花瓶的？
How will the company procure the needed resources? = 公司将如何获取所需的资源？


否定句:
They could not procure the necessary components for the machine. = 他们无法为机器采购必要的部件。
He failed to procure a loan for his business. = 他未能为他的生意获得贷款。


PHRASE->
procure funds = 筹集资金
procure evidence = 获取证据
procure supplies = 采购物资
procure a visa = 获得签证
procure a job = 找到工作


词源:
From Old French *procurer, from Latin procurare ("to manage, take care of"), from pro- ("forth, forward") + curare ("to care for").
