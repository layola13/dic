sabbath:/ˈsæbəθ/| n.|安息日，星期六|The Sabbath is a day of rest and religious observance for Jews, Christians, and some others.  It is a time for spiritual reflection and worship. |例句：The Jewish Sabbath begins at sundown on Friday and ends at sundown on Saturday. = 犹太人的安息日从星期五日落开始，星期六日落结束。

例句：Many Christians observe the Sabbath on Sunday. = 许多基督徒在星期日遵守安息日。|近义词：rest day, holy day|反义词：workday
