by-lines:/ˈbaɪˌlaɪnz/| n.|作者署名行；（报刊文章等的）作者姓名栏|Lines at the beginning of a newspaper article or other written work that identify the author.  They often include the author's name and sometimes their title or affiliation.|例句：The by-lines in the newspaper indicated that several different journalists contributed to the story.  报纸上的作者署名行表明几位不同的记者都参与了这篇报道。|近义词：author's names, credits|反义词：无
