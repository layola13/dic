pickpocketing: /ˈpɪkˌpɒkɪtɪŋ/ | n. |扒窃；偷窃（尤指在拥挤的地方）|The act of stealing from someone's pockets, typically in a crowded place.  |例句：Pickpocketing is a common problem in tourist areas.  扒窃在旅游区是一个常见问题。

例句：There have been several reports of pickpocketing on the subway recently. 近期地铁上发生了好几起扒窃事件。


近义词：theft, larceny, pilfering

反义词：giving, donating
