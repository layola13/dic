foresights:/ˈfɔːrsaɪts/| n.|远见；预见；先见之明|The ability to foresee future events and to prepare for them.  It often involves careful consideration of potential outcomes and planning accordingly.|例句：Her foresights in investing secured her financial future.  她的投资远见确保了她的未来财务安全。


例句：The company's success is largely due to the management's foresights.  公司的成功很大程度上归功于管理层的远见卓识。


近义词：forethought, foresight, prescience, prevision


反义词：shortsightedness, myopia, lack of foresight


词性: 名词
