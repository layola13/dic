couche:/kuʃ/| n.|长沙发；卧榻|A long upholstered couch or sofa.|例句：The living room has a comfortable couche where we can relax. 客厅里有一张舒适的长沙发，我们可以放松一下。|近义词：sofa, settee, divan|反义词：chair, stool
