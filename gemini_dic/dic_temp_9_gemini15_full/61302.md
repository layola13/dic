embark:/ɪmˈbɑːrk/| vt.|开始；着手；乘船（飞机）|to begin a journey, especially a long or important one, or to put someone or something on a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.|例句：He is about to embark on a new career. 他正要开始新的职业生涯。|例句：The passengers embarked the plane.乘客登上了飞机。|近义词：begin, commence, board|反义词：disembark, end, finish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embarks on a new project every year. = 他每年都会开始一个新项目。
They embark on a long journey to the mountains. = 他们开始了一段漫长的山路旅程。

一般过去时:
She embarked on a writing career after graduation. = 她毕业后开始从事写作事业。
The ship embarked all the passengers before setting sail. = 船在起航前载上了所有乘客。

现在完成时:
We have embarked on a challenging fundraising campaign. = 我们已经开始了一场具有挑战性的筹款活动。
They have embarked on a new phase of their research. = 他们已经开始了他们研究的新阶段。

过去完成时:
He had embarked on his journey before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他已经开始了他的旅程。
They had embarked on the project months ago. = 几个月前他们就已经开始了这个项目。

将来时:
She will embark on a solo trip around the world next year. = 她明年将开始环游世界的单独旅行。
We will embark on a new strategy to improve sales. = 我们将开始一项新的策略来提高销售额。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would embark on that adventure. = 如果我是你，我会开始那次冒险。
I wish I had embarked on that business opportunity sooner. = 我真希望我早点抓住那个商机。

被动语态:
The plan was embarked on without sufficient preparation. = 该计划在没有充分准备的情况下就开始了。
The new policy will be embarked upon next month. = 新政策将于下个月启动。

疑问句:
When will they embark on their honeymoon? = 他们什么时候开始蜜月旅行？
Has he embarked on his new job yet? = 他开始他的新工作了吗？

否定句:
He did not embark on his journey until the next morning. = 直到第二天早上他才开始他的旅程。
They have not yet embarked on the ambitious project. = 他们还没有开始那个雄心勃勃的项目。
