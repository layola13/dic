nunciate:/ˈnʌnsieɪt/| vt.|宣告；正式宣布；清晰地发音|To pronounce or articulate (words or sounds) clearly and distinctly; to announce formally. |例句：The president will enunciate his policy on immigration tomorrow. 总统将于明天正式宣布其移民政策。
例句：He enunciated each word carefully so that everyone could understand him. 他仔细地逐字清晰地发音，以便每个人都能理解他。|近义词：pronounce, articulate, announce, proclaim|反义词：mumble, slur

She enunciates every syllable with precision. 她精确地发出了每一个音节。

词源:
From Latin *enunciāre "to utter, declare," from ex- "out" + nuntiāre "to announce," from nuntius "messenger."  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *new- "to announce, to call."
