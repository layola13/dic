renewer: /rɪˈnuːər/ | n. | 更新者；续约者；复兴者 | One who renews something.  A person or thing that renews or causes renewal. |


例句：

The policy renewer received a discount for prompt payment. = 保单续保人因及时付款而获得折扣。

He is a renewer of old traditions. = 他是旧传统复兴者。


近义词：reviver, restorer, renovator

反义词：destroyer, terminator, ender


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The policy renewer pays annually. = 保单续保人每年支付保费。
He is a renewer of old traditions. = 他是旧传统复兴者。


一般过去时:
The software renewer updated the system last night. = 软件更新者昨晚更新了系统。
She was a renewer of hope in a difficult time. = 在困难时期，她是希望的复兴者。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
Before the renovation, the building had been a renewer of its former glory. = 在翻新之前，这座建筑曾恢复了它昔日的辉煌。(The building, acting as a renewer...)
They had been renewers of the old contract before the new one was signed. = 在签订新合同之前，他们已经续签了旧合同。(They, acting as renewers...)


将来时:
The subscription renewer will automatically renew next month. = 订阅续订者将在下个月自动续订。
She will be a renewer of hope for the community. = 她将成为社区希望的复兴者。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
