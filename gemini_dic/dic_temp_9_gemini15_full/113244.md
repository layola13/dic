lustier:/ˈlʌstiər/| adj.|更强壮的；更健康的；更充满活力的|More vigorous or robust; stronger and healthier.  The lustier plants thrived in the sun.|The lustier athlete easily won the race.|近义词：stronger, healthier, more vigorous, robust|反义词：weaker, sicker, less vigorous

词源:
From Middle English *lusti, from Old English *lust, meaning "desire, pleasure, strength."  The modern sense evolved from the older association of strength and vigor with pleasure and vitality.
