eyed:/aɪd/| adj.| 眼睛的；有……眼睛的 | Having eyes or an eye; relating to the eye.  |例句：The eyedrops helped soothe my irritated eyes. 眼药水帮助舒缓了我发炎的眼睛。

例句：The eyed potato was carefully selected for its size and shape.  这个带芽的土豆因为大小和形状被仔细挑选出来。


近义词：ocular, visual | 反义词：blind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eyed the suspicious package cautiously. 他谨慎地注视着那个可疑的包裹。
She eyed her reflection in the mirror critically. 她批判性地审视着镜子里的自己。

一般过去时:
The cat eyed the mouse before pouncing. 猫在猛扑之前注视着老鼠。
He eyed the menu with interest. 他饶有兴致地查看菜单。

现在完成时:

They have eyed several properties, but haven't made a decision yet. 他们已经看中了几个房产，但还没有做出决定。

过去完成时:

She had eyed the opportunity for weeks, waiting for the right moment. 她已经关注这个机会几周了，等待着合适的时机。

将来时:
He will eye the competition closely during the race. 他将在比赛中密切关注竞争对手。
