columella:/ˌkɒljʊˈmɛlə/| n. |小柱，中柱；耳柱|A small column or pillar, especially the central pillar in a plant stem or the bony core in the cochlea of the ear.|The columella of the snail shell was broken.  蜗牛壳的中心柱断了。|The columella of the ear helps transmit sound vibrations. 耳柱帮助传递声音振动。|pillar, column, axis| (no direct antonym, depends on context)|名词

fungal columella: The central column in some fungi.
