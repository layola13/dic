renounce:/rɪˈnaʊns/| vt.|放弃；抛弃；否认；拒绝；宣布放弃|To give up or reject something, especially a claim, right, or belief.  It implies a formal or deliberate act of relinquishing something. |例句：He renounced his claim to the throne. 他放弃了他的王位继承权。

例句：She renounced her citizenship. 她放弃了她的国籍。


近义词：abandon, relinquish, forsake, repudiate, disown

反义词：claim, embrace, accept, acknowledge, retain

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He renounces his belief in God. 他放弃了他对上帝的信仰。
She renounces her inheritance. 她放弃了她的遗产。


一般过去时:
He renounced his membership in the club. 他放弃了他俱乐部的会员资格。
She renounced her American citizenship. 她放弃了她的美国国籍。


现在完成时:
He has renounced violence. 他已经放弃了暴力。
She has renounced her past life. 她已经放弃了她过去的生活。


过去完成时:
He had renounced his faith before he met her. 在他遇到她之前，他已经放弃了他的信仰。
She had renounced her family before she moved away. 在她搬走之前，她已经与家人断绝了关系。


将来时:
He will renounce his title. 他将放弃他的头衔。
She will renounce her position. 她将辞去她的职务。


虚拟语气:
If he renounced his ambition, he would be happier. 如果他放弃他的野心，他会更快乐。
If she renounced her beliefs, she would have an easier life. 如果她放弃她的信仰，她将会过得更容易。


被动语态:
His claim was renounced by the court. 他的要求被法院驳回了。
Her citizenship was renounced. 她的国籍被放弃了。


疑问句:
Will he renounce his inheritance? 他会放弃他的遗产吗？
Has she renounced her faith? 她已经放弃她的信仰了吗？


否定句:
He did not renounce his position. 他没有放弃他的职位。
She would not renounce her beliefs. 她不会放弃她的信仰。
