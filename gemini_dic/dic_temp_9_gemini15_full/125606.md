multivarious:/ˌmʌltiˈveriəs/| adj. | 多种多样的；各种各样的 | Having or showing great variety; diverse.  | The museum boasts a multivarious collection of artifacts from around the world.  博物馆拥有来自世界各地的各种各样的文物。 | diverse, varied, manifold, heterogeneous | uniform, homogeneous, monotonous


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "what" does.  It describes nouns.
