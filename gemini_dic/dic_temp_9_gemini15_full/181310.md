reinterrogate: /ˌriːɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪt/ | vt. | 再次审问；重新盘问 | To question (someone) again.  This often implies a more thorough or focused questioning than the initial interrogation.  | 例句：The police reinterrogated the suspect after new evidence emerged. = 新证据出现后，警方再次审问了嫌疑人。

例句：The detective decided to reinterrogate the witness to clarify some inconsistencies in their testimony. = 侦探决定再次盘问证人，以澄清证词中的一些不一致之处。


近义词：re-examine, question again, interview again, cross-examine


反义词：release, free


词性：动词
