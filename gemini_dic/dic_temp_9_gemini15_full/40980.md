coordinated:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 协调的；配合的；统一的 |  Characterized by the harmonious functioning of parts; working together efficiently.  | The team worked in a coordinated effort to complete the project.  团队协同努力完成了项目。 | synchronized, integrated, unified | uncoordinated, disorganized, chaotic | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dancers' movements are coordinated. = 舞者的动作是协调的。
Their efforts are coordinated to achieve the goal. = 他们的努力协调一致以实现目标。


一般过去时:
The event was carefully coordinated. = 活动被精心策划协调。
The soldiers' actions were coordinated by the general. = 士兵的行动由将军协调。


现在完成时:
The team has coordinated its strategy. = 团队已经协调了其战略。
Everything has been coordinated for the conference. = 会议的一切都已协调妥当。


过去完成时:
The plan had been coordinated before the meeting. = 计划在会议之前就已经协调好了。
All the details had been coordinated by the time the guests arrived. = 客人到达时，所有细节都已协调完毕。


将来时:
The project will be coordinated by a project manager. = 项目将由项目经理协调。
We will coordinate our schedules for the trip. = 我们将协调我们的行程安排。


虚拟语气:
If the tasks were better coordinated, we would finish earlier. = 如果任务协调得更好，我们会早点完成。
It would be helpful if all departments were coordinated. = 如果所有部门都能协调一致，那就太好了。


被动语态:
The activities were coordinated by the committee. = 这些活动由委员会协调。
The response was coordinated by the emergency services. = 紧急服务部门协调了应对措施。


疑问句:
Are the schedules coordinated? = 时间表协调了吗？
Have all the departments coordinated their efforts? = 所有部门都协调了他们的努力吗？


否定句:
The efforts were not coordinated. = 努力没有协调好。
The team's actions were not coordinated effectively. = 团队的行为没有得到有效的协调。


PHRASE->
coordinated effort  协调一致的努力

poorly coordinated  协调不良的

词源:
From "coordinate" (verb), meaning "to bring into a common action, movement, or condition," ultimately derived from Latin "co-" (together) + "ordinatus" (ordered).  The adjective form "coordinated" emerged later, reflecting the state of being harmoniously organized.
