forgetfulness: /fərˈɡɛtflnəs/ | n. | 健忘；遗忘；疏忽 | The state or condition of being forgetful; the inability or failure to remember something.  It can also refer to an instance of forgetting something. |例句：His forgetfulness was becoming a problem at work.  他的健忘在工作中越来越成问题了。

例句：The forgetfulness of the witness made it difficult to reconstruct the events.  证人的健忘使得重建事件变得困难。


近义词：oblivion, amnesia, lapse of memory, absentmindedness

反义词：remembrance, recollection, memory


词性：名词
