certifications:/ˌsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 证明；证书；认证 | Official documents or statements confirming the truth of something or the existence of a particular quality.  They are often required for professional licensing or to demonstrate competence in a specific area. |  例句：He needs several certifications to qualify for the job. 他需要几张证书才能胜任这份工作。

例句：The company requires all employees to obtain food safety certifications. 公司要求所有员工获得食品安全认证。


近义词：credentials, attestations, accreditations, endorsements

反义词：disproof, refutation
