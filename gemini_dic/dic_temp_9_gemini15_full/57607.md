draping:/ˈdreɪpɪŋ/| n. v.|悬垂；褶皱；披挂；装饰；|The act of arranging fabric in graceful folds; the way fabric hangs or falls.  It can also refer to the decorative arrangement of fabric on a person or object.|例句：The draping of the curtains was exquisite. 窗帘的悬垂方式非常精美。
The sculptor skillfully draped the fabric over the statue. 雕塑家熟练地将布料披在雕像上。|近义词：hanging, cascading, flowing|反义词：stiff, taut


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The fabric drapes beautifully. = 这布料垂坠得很漂亮。
She drapes the scarves around her neck. = 她把围巾披在脖子上。


一般过去时:
The artist draped the model in silk. = 艺术家给模特披上了丝绸。
