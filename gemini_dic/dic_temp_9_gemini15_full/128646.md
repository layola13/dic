nicking:/ˈnɪkɪŋ/| v.|轻微切割；偷窃；(非正式)批评；|To make a small cut or scratch in something; to steal something; (informal) to criticize.|例句：He was nicking the paintwork of the car. 他在划坏汽车的油漆。|例句：The thief was nicking wallets from tourists. 小偷正在偷游客的钱包。|近义词：cutting, stealing, criticizing|反义词：repairing, restoring, praising


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is nicking the apples from the tree. = 他正在偷树上的苹果。
She nicks the corners of her photographs. = 她修剪照片的角。


一般过去时:
They nicked my bike last night. = 他们昨晚偷了我的自行车。
The chef nicked the vegetables before cooking. = 厨师在烹饪前切碎了蔬菜。


现在完成时:
He has nicked several items from the store. = 他已经从商店偷了几件东西。
She has nicked her finger while slicing the bread. = 她切面包时割伤了手指。


过去完成时:
The thief had nicked the jewels before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，小偷已经偷走了珠宝。
They had nicked the painting from the museum years ago. = 他们几年前就从博物馆偷走了那幅画。


将来时:
I will nick the branches of that tree to make a sculpture. = 我将剪掉那棵树的树枝来做一个雕塑。
He will nick his finger if he's not careful. = 如果他不小心，他会割伤手指。


虚拟语气:
If he nicked the car, he would be in trouble. = 如果他划伤了汽车，他会有麻烦。
If she had nicked less of the fabric, it would have been better. = 如果她剪掉的布料少一些，效果会更好。


被动语态:
The painting was nicked from the gallery. = 那幅画是从画廊偷走的。
The apples were nicked from the orchard. = 苹果是从果园里偷走的。


疑问句:
Did he nick the money? = 他偷了钱吗？
Has she nicked the scissors? = 她拿走了剪刀吗？


否定句:
He didn't nick the vase. = 他没有偷花瓶。
She doesn't nick the corners of the pages. = 她不剪页角。
