rhizomatous:/ˌraɪzəˈmeɪtəs/| adj. | 根茎的；具根茎的 | Having or relating to a rhizome. A rhizome is a continuously growing horizontal underground stem which puts out lateral shoots and roots at intervals.  Plants with rhizomes spread vegetatively, meaning they can form new plants from the rhizome itself. | Example:  Many ferns have rhizomatous growth habits.  许多蕨类植物具有根茎生长习性。 The ginger plant is rhizomatous. 姜是根茎植物。|近义词：rhizomatic|反义词：non-rhizomatous
