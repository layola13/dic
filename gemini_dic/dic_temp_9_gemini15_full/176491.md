re-practices:/ˌriːˈpræktɪsɪz/ | v. |反复练习，再练习 |To practice something again; to engage in repeated practice.  |例句：She re-practices her piano sonata every day. = 她每天都反复练习她的钢琴奏鸣曲。
例句：The team re-practices the play until they get it perfect. =  球队反复练习这个战术，直到做到完美。 |近义词：rehearse, review, drill |反义词：neglect, abandon

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-practices his speech every evening. = 他每天晚上都反复练习他的演讲。
They re-practice the dance routine before every performance. = 他们在每次演出前都会反复练习舞蹈套路。

一般过去时:
She re-practiced her lines until she felt confident. = 她反复练习台词，直到感觉自信。
The band re-practiced the song after the feedback from the producer. = 乐队在收到制作人的反馈后反复练习了这首歌。

现在完成时:
He has re-practiced the difficult passage many times. = 他已经反复练习了那段困难的乐句很多次。
They have re-practiced the choreography since the last rehearsal. = 自从上次排练以来，他们一直在反复练习舞蹈编排。

过去完成时:
She had re-practiced the song several times before the audition. = 在试镜之前，她已经反复练习了那首歌好几次。
The actors had re-practiced their scenes before the opening night. = 在首映之夜之前，演员们已经反复排练了他们的场景。

将来时:
He will re-practice the piece for the upcoming competition. = 他将为即将到来的比赛反复练习这首乐曲。
They will re-practice their presentation before the conference. = 他们将在会议之前反复练习他们的演示。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would re-practice my guitar skills. = 如果我有更多时间，我会反复练习我的吉他技巧。
I wish I had re-practiced my French before the trip. = 我真希望我在旅行前能反复练习我的法语。

被动语态:
The routine was re-practiced by the dancers until it was flawless. = 舞蹈演员们反复练习了这个套路，直到它完美无瑕。
The speech was re-practiced countless times before the big event. = 在大型活动之前，演讲稿被反复练习了无数次。

疑问句:
Will you re-practice the song before the concert? = 你会在音乐会前反复练习这首歌吗？
Have they re-practiced their lines for the play? = 他们已经为这部戏反复练习台词了吗？

否定句:
He did not re-practice the difficult section of the music. = 他没有反复练习这首乐曲中困难的部分。
They did not re-practice their presentation before the meeting. = 他们在会议前没有反复练习他们的演示。
