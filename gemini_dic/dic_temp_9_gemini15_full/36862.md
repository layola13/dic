color:/ˈkʌlə(r)/, /ˈkʌlər/ | n., adj. |颜色，色彩；有…颜色的 |  Color is a fundamental aspect of visual perception.颜色是视觉感知的一个基本方面。The walls are painted a pale color. 墙壁漆成浅色。|近义词：hue, shade, tint |反义词：monochrome, colorless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sky is blue. = 天空是蓝色的。
Her dress is a vibrant color. = 她的裙子颜色鲜艳。

一般过去时:
The leaves turned brown in autumn. = 秋天树叶变黄了。
The car was originally red. = 这辆车原本是红色的。

现在完成时:
She has dyed her hair blonde. = 她把头发染成了金色。
The artist has used many colors in this painting. = 这幅画用了很多颜色。

过去完成时:
The walls had been painted before we moved in. = 我们搬进去之前墙已经刷过漆了。
He had already chosen the color for his new car. = 他已经选好了新车的颜色。

将来时:
We will paint the house a light green. = 我们将把房子漆成浅绿色。
The flowers will bloom in vibrant colors next spring. = 明春鲜花将以鲜艳的颜色盛开。


虚拟语气:
If the walls were a different color, the room would feel brighter. = 如果墙壁颜色不同，房间会感觉更明亮。
I wish my car was a more exciting color. = 我希望我的车颜色更鲜艳些。

被动语态:
The fabric is colored with natural dyes. = 织物是用天然染料染色的。
The picture was colored with crayons. = 这幅图是用蜡笔涂色的。

疑问句:
What color is your favorite? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What color are the curtains? = 窗帘是什么颜色？

否定句:
The room isn't the right color. = 这个房间的颜色不对。
Her eyes are not blue; they're green. = 她的眼睛不是蓝色的，是绿色的。
