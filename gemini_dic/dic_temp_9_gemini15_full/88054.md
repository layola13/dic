highfield:/ˈhaɪfiːld/ | n. | 高地；高田；高地地区 | A highfield is an area of land that is higher than the surrounding land, often used for farming or grazing.  It can also refer to a specific named location. | Example: The sheep grazed peacefully in the highfield.  绵羊在高地上平静地吃草。 The family lived on a small farm nestled in a picturesque highfield.  这家人住在一个风景如画的高地上的一个小农场里。|近义词：upland, highland, elevated land |反义词：lowland, valley, depression |名词
