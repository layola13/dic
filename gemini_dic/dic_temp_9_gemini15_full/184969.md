reteamed:/ˌriːˈtiːmd/| v.|重新组队，重组团队 |To form a team again, often with the same members.  This implies a previous team that has been disbanded or temporarily separated and is now being reconstituted. |例句：The band reteamed after a five-year hiatus to record a new album. 乐队在五年休整后重新组队录制新专辑。

例句：The project team reteamed to address the unexpected challenges. 项目团队重新组队以应对意外挑战。


近义词：reformed, regrouped, reunited


反义词：disbanded, separated, dissolved


词性:动词
