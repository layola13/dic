pleiad:/ˈpliːæd/| n.|七姐妹星团；一群杰出人物|A cluster of seven stars in the constellation Taurus, also known as the Seven Sisters; a group of eminent people.  |例句：The Pleiades are a beautiful sight in the night sky.  七姐妹星团是夜空中美丽的景象。

例句：Shakespeare, Milton, and Chaucer are often cited as members of a pleiad of great English poets. 莎士比亚、弥尔顿和乔叟经常被认为是伟大英国诗人中的佼佼者。|近义词：cluster, group, constellation|反义词：individual, singleton
