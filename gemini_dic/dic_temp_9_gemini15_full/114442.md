makeovers:/ˈmeɪkˌoʊvərz/| n.|改头换面；彻底改造；大变样 |A makeover is a significant change or improvement to the appearance or style of something or someone.  It often involves a transformation that enhances attractiveness or functionality.|例句：She gave her kitchen a complete makeover. 她彻底改造了她的厨房。

例句：The old house underwent a stunning makeover. 这栋旧房子经历了令人惊艳的改造。


近义词：transformation, revamp, renovation, refurbishment|反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin
