mail-bombed:/ˈmeɪlˌbɒmbd/ | v. |邮件轰炸，邮件攻击 | To overwhelm someone with a large number of emails, often unsolicited or unwanted, typically as a form of harassment or protest.  This is often done using automated scripts or bots. | Example: The company was mail-bombed with thousands of angry emails after announcing the price increase. 公司在宣布涨价后收到了数千封愤怒的邮件。 | 近义词：spammed, flooded (with emails), bombarded (with emails) | 反义词：None (the opposite would be a lack of emails)
