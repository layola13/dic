digitalised: /ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪzd/ | v. | 使数字化；将…转换成数字形式 | To convert information or media into a digital format, suitable for use by computers or other digital devices.  | The library has digitalised its entire collection of books. 图书馆已将其所有藏书数字化。  The company is planning to digitalise its operations. 公司计划将其运营数字化。 | computerized, digitized | analogized  | 动词
