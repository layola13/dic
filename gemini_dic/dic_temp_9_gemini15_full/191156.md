scare:/skɛər/| vt. n.| 恐吓；使害怕；惊吓；受惊吓的事物| to frighten someone suddenly; something that frightens someone. |例句：The loud noise scared the children.  大声的噪音吓坏了孩子们。|例句：The sudden storm was a real scare. 突如其来的暴风雨真是令人害怕。|近义词：frighten, terrify, intimidate |反义词：comfort, reassure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog scares the cat. = 狗吓唬猫。
Loud noises scare me. = 大声的噪音吓到我了。


一般过去时:
The movie scared me. = 这部电影吓到我了。
The thunder scared the horse. = 雷声吓到了马。


现在完成时:
Nothing has scared me like that before. = 以前没有什么事情像那样吓到过我。
That experience has scared her for life. = 那次经历把她吓怕了。


过去完成时:
I had never been so scared before. = 我以前从未如此害怕过。
She had already been scared by the previous incident. = 她已经被之前的事件吓到了。


将来时:
The unexpected sound will scare you. = 意外的声音会吓到你。
I think the dog will scare the burglar away. = 我想狗会把窃贼吓跑。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been scared, I would have gone. = 如果我不害怕，我就去了。
I wish I hadn't been scared to ask. = 我真希望我不害怕问。


被动语态:
The children were scared by the loud noise. = 孩子们被大声的噪音吓到了。
She was scared stiff by the horror movie. = 她被恐怖电影吓得僵住了。


疑问句:
What scares you? = 什么吓到你？
Are you scared of spiders? = 你怕蜘蛛吗？


否定句:
The noise didn't scare me. = 噪音没有吓到我。
I wasn't scared at all. = 我一点也不害怕。
