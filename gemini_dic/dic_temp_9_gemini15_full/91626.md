hyperbaric:/ˌhaɪpərˈbærɪk/| adj.|高压的；高气压的| relating to or denoting increased pressure, especially of air.  The term is often used in the context of medical treatment involving increased atmospheric pressure.|例句：Hyperbaric oxygen therapy is used to treat decompression sickness. 高压氧疗法用于治疗减压病。
例句：The hyperbaric chamber simulates the pressure at great depths. 高压舱模拟深海压力。|近义词：high-pressure|反义词：hypobaric|形容词
