slitty:/ˈslɪti/| adj.| 狭长的；眼裂的；（尤指眼睛）细长的，眯着的 | having narrow slits, especially eyes;  narrow and elongated.  |例句：He had slitty eyes, making him look suspicious. 他有着狭长的眼睛，让他看起来很可疑。

例句：The slitty windows let in very little light. 狭长的窗户只透进很少的光线。


近义词：narrow, elongated, thin,  (for eyes) narrowed, squinted


反义词：wide, broad, open (for eyes) wide-open


词性：形容词
