readapts:/ˌriːˈæpt/| v.|重新适应，改编|To adapt again; to modify or adjust something to suit a new purpose or situation.|例句：The company readapted its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic.  公司重新调整了其营销策略以针对更年轻的人群。|例句：The novel was readapted into a successful film. 这部小说被改编成了一部成功的电影。|近义词：revise, modify, adjust|反义词：maintain, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He readapts his teaching methods every semester. 他每个学期都会调整他的教学方法。
The company readapts its products to meet changing consumer demands. 公司调整产品以满足不断变化的消费者需求。
