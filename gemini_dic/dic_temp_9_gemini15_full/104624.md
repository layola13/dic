/kɑːr/ | n. | 车，汽车 | A motor vehicle with four wheels; an automobile. |  例句：He bought a new car last month. 他上个月买了一辆新车。 |  例句：My car is parked outside. 我的车停在外面。|近义词：automobile, vehicle, motorcar | 反义词：None | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car runs smoothly. = 这辆车运行平稳。
He drives a red car. = 他开一辆红色的车。

一般过去时:
She drove her car to work yesterday. = 昨天她开车去上班。
They bought a used car last year. = 他们去年买了一辆二手车。

现在完成时:
I have driven this car for five years. = 我已经开这辆车五年了。
He has not had any car accidents. = 他没有发生过任何车祸。

过去完成时:
She had driven the car for ten years before selling it. = 她开了十年车之后才卖掉它。
They had already left in the car before I called. = 我打电话之前他们已经开车离开了。

将来时:
I will buy a new car next year. = 我明年会买一辆新车。
He will drive us to the airport tomorrow. = 他明天会开车送我们去机场。

虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would drive to the beach. = 如果我有一辆车，我会开车去海滩。
I wish I had a faster car. = 我真希望我有一辆更快的车。

被动语态:
The car was repaired last week. = 这辆车上周修好了。
The car is being washed now. = 车现在正在清洗。

疑问句:
Do you have a car? = 你有车吗？
What kind of car is that? = 那是什么样的车？

否定句:
He doesn't own a car. = 他没有车。
They didn't drive to the party. = 他们没开车去参加派对。
