contours:/ˈkɒntʊərz/ /ˈkɑːntʊrz/| n. |轮廓；曲线；等高线 |The outlines or shapes of something.  Contours can refer to the shape of a landscape, a drawing, or even a person's face.  |例句：The contours of the hills were clearly visible. 山丘的轮廓清晰可见。

例句：The artist skillfully captured the contours of the model's face.艺术家巧妙地捕捉到了模特的面部轮廓。


近义词：outline, profile, shape, form |反义词：flatness, uniformity


词源:  From Middle French *contour, from Old French conturer "to outline, draw the outline of" (12c.), from Latin con- "together" + tornāre "to turn, shape on a lathe".  The modern English word reflects the sense of a line that outlines a shape.
