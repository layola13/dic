interstimulus:/ˌɪntərˈstɪmjʊləs/| n. |刺激间隔；刺激间期|The time interval between two stimuli.  This term is primarily used in experimental psychology and neuroscience to describe the duration between two distinct sensory inputs or events.  The length of the interstimulus interval can significantly impact the response of an organism or subject. |Example: The researchers manipulated the interstimulus interval to study its effect on reaction time. 研究人员操纵刺激间隔来研究其对反应时间的的影响。|近义词：stimulus interval, intertrial interval|反义词：无|名词
