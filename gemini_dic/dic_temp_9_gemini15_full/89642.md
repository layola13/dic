hookey:/ˈhʊki/| n.|旷课，逃学|The act of staying away from school or work without permission.  It often refers to a single instance of skipping school or work, rather than a prolonged absence.|例句：He played hookey from school to go fishing. 他逃学去钓鱼了。|例句：The kids were caught playing hookey and sent to the principal's office. 孩子们被抓到逃学，被送到校长办公室。|近义词：truancy, absence, skipping, playing hooky|反义词：attendance, presence
