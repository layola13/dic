coprophagan:/kɒˈprɒfəɡæn/| n. |食粪动物；食粪者 |An animal that eats feces.  Coprophagy is a common behavior in many animal species, serving various purposes such as nutrient recycling and gut microbiome regulation. |Example: Rabbits are well-known coprophagans, consuming their own soft cecotropes for optimal nutrient absorption. 兔子是著名的食粪动物，它们会吃掉自己柔软的盲肠粪便以获得最佳的营养吸收。|近义词： dung eater, scatophage |反义词： carnivore, herbivore
