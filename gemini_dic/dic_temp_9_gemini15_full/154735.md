persuadable: /pərˈsweɪdəbl/ | adj. | 可劝服的，易受影响的 | Easily persuaded or influenced; open to suggestion. |  He was surprisingly persuadable, easily swayed by his friend's arguments. 他出奇地容易被劝服，很容易被朋友的论点所左右。 |近义词： susceptible, amenable, tractable | 反义词： intractable, stubborn, obstinate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is persuadable if you approach him gently. = 如果你温柔地接近他，他是可以被劝服的。
She is usually persuadable when it comes to helping others. = 在帮助别人方面，她通常是很容易被劝服的。

一般过去时:
The jury was persuadable after hearing the new evidence. = 陪审团在听到新的证据后是可以被劝服的。
He was more persuadable before the deadline. = 截止日期之前，他更容易被劝服。
