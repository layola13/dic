mutiny:/ˈmjuːtəni/|/ˈmjuːtɪni/| n. v.|哗变；叛变；反抗|An open rebellion against the person or authority in power, especially by sailors against their officers.|The sailors staged a mutiny after weeks of harsh treatment. 水手们在遭受数周虐待后发动了哗变。The soldiers threatened a mutiny if their demands were not met. 如果他们的要求得不到满足，士兵们威胁要发动兵变。|rebellion, insurrection, revolt|obedience, submission, compliance|名词，动词
