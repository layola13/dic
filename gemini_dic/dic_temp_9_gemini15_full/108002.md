lasso:/ˈlæsoʊ/| n., v.|套索；套住|A lasso is a rope with a loop at one end, used for catching cattle or horses.  It can also refer to the act of catching something with a lasso.|例句：The cowboy threw the lasso and caught the calf.  牛仔扔出套索套住了小牛。  He lassoed the runaway horse. 他用套索套住了逃跑的马。|近义词：rope, snare, noose|反义词：release, free
