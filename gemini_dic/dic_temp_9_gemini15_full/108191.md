latticing: /ˈlætɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 格子状结构；格子花纹；（尤指）棚架结构 | The arrangement of something in a lattice pattern, especially a framework of crossed strips or bars.  It often refers to a structure used for support or decoration. |例句：The garden featured a beautiful latticing over the rose bushes. 花园里玫瑰丛上有一个美丽的格子架。

The intricate latticing of the ceiling was a masterpiece of craftsmanship. 天花板复杂的格子状结构是工艺的杰作。

近义词：trelliswork, lattice, framework, grating | 反义词：solid, unbroken


词源:  Derived from "lattice," which comes from Old French "lattis" and ultimately from Medieval Latin "latus" meaning "lath" or "strip of wood".  The "-ing" suffix indicates a gerund or noun of action.
