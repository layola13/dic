indicated:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtɪd/ | v. |指示；表明；象征；暗示 |to show, point out, or make clear |例句：The arrow indicated the direction we should go.  箭头指示了我们应该去的方向。

The study indicated a strong correlation between smoking and lung cancer.  研究表明吸烟与肺癌之间有很强的相关性。


近义词：show, suggest, signify, imply, point to | 反义词：conceal, hide, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The arrow indicates the path. = 箭头指示着道路。
The symptoms indicate a serious illness. = 症状表明是一种严重的疾病。

一般过去时:
The map indicated the location of the treasure. = 地图指示了宝藏的位置。
He indicated his agreement by nodding his head. = 他点头表示同意。

现在完成时:
All the evidence has indicated his guilt. = 所有证据都表明他有罪。
Recent studies have indicated a rise in sea levels. = 最近的研究表明海平面上升了。

过去完成时:
The broken glass indicated that a fight had taken place. = 打碎的玻璃表明曾发生过争斗。
The doctor indicated that the patient had suffered a stroke. = 医生表明病人曾中风。

将来时:
The forecast indicates that it will rain tomorrow. = 预报显示明天会下雨。
The rising inflation indicates that prices will continue to climb. = 通货膨胀上升表明物价将继续上涨。

虚拟语气:
If the results indicated a problem, we would have taken action. = 如果结果表明存在问题，我们会采取行动。
I wish the map indicated a clearer route. = 我希望地图能指示更清晰的路线。

被动语态:
The direction was indicated by a signpost. = 方向由路标指示。
His guilt was indicated by the evidence. = 他的罪行由证据表明。

疑问句:
Does this sign indicate the way to the station? = 这个标志指示去车站的路吗？
What does the research indicate about the effects of climate change? = 这项研究表明气候变化的影响是什么？

否定句:
The map didn't indicate any alternative routes. = 地图没有指示任何替代路线。
The initial tests didn't indicate any serious problems. = 初步测试没有显示任何严重问题。


PHRASE->

indirectly indicated = 间接地表明
