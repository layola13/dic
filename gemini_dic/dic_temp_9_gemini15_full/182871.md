repentingly:/rɪˈpentɪŋli/| adj. |悔恨地；懊悔地 | Showing or feeling regret for one's wrongdoing.  It implies a feeling of sorrow and remorse for past actions. |例句：He looked repentingly at his wife, admitting his mistake. 他悔恨地望着妻子，承认了自己的错误。


例句：She spoke repentingly about her past actions, wishing she could undo them. 她懊悔地谈论起过去的行径，希望能够弥补。


近义词：contritely, remorsefully, regretfully


反义词：unrepentantly, remorselessly


词性:形容词
