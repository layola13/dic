paring:/ˈpɛərɪŋ/| n.|削皮；配对；成对的东西|The act of peeling fruit or vegetables, or the pieces of fruit or vegetables that have been peeled.  It can also refer to a pair or pairs of things.|例句：The paring of apples was a tedious task. 苹果削皮是一项乏味的工作。
例句： He enjoyed the sweet paring of the pears. 他喜欢吃甜梨的果皮。|近义词：peeling, pairing off, couple|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be leaving the skin on.

He is paring the potatoes. = 他正在削土豆皮。
She pares apples every morning. = 她每天早上削苹果。
