Sentence Showing Contrast:

WORD ORIGIN->
The word "co-ordinately" derives from the word "coordinate," which itself comes from the Latin words "co-" (meaning "together") and "ordinatus" (meaning "ordered" or "arranged").  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "coordinate" into an adverb, indicating "in a coordinate manner".
