columbin:/ˈkʌləmbɪn/| n. |紫花地丁；(戏剧中的)哥伦拜恩|A columbine is a type of flowering plant, typically with bell-shaped flowers.  In drama, especially Commedia dell'arte, Columbine is a stock character, usually a clever and charming servant girl.|The garden was full of vibrant columbines.|近义词：aquilegia|反义词：无
