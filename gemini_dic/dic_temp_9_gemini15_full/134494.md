nonleague:/ˈnɒnliːɡ/| adj.|非联盟的；非联赛的|Not belonging to a league or association, especially in sports.  Often refers to teams or competitions outside the main professional leagues.|例句：Our team plays in the nonleague; we're not part of the professional football league. 我们的球队参加非联赛；我们不是职业足球联赛的一部分。


例句：The nonleague football club achieved an unexpected victory against a top-tier team. 这家非联赛足球俱乐部对顶级球队取得了意外的胜利。


近义词：independent, unaffiliated


反义词：league, affiliated
