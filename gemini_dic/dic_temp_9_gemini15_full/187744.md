rootlessly:/ˈruːtləslɪ/ | adj. | 没有根的；无根的；漂泊无定的；没有归属感的 | Lacking roots or a sense of belonging; unsettled; adrift.  |  He felt rootless after moving to a new city and leaving all his friends behind. 他搬到一个新的城市，离开了他所有的朋友后感到无所适从。  | 近义词：unrooted, unsettled, adrift, homeless, wandering | 反义词：grounded, settled, rooted, established


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He feels rootless in this new environment. 他在这个新环境中感到无所适从。
They live rootlessly, moving from place to place. 他们过着漂泊的生活，四处迁徙。


一般过去时:
She felt rootless after the death of her parents. 她父母去世后感到无所适从。
He lived rootlessly for many years after leaving home. 他离家后漂泊了很多年。


现在完成时:
He has felt rootless ever since he graduated from college. 从大学毕业后，他就一直感到无所适从。
They have lived rootlessly for decades, never settling down. 几十年来，他们一直过着漂泊的生活，从未安定下来。


过去完成时:
She had felt rootless for years before she finally found a place to call home. 在她最终找到一个称之为家的地方之前，她已经漂泊多年了。
He had lived rootlessly since his childhood, never knowing where he truly belonged. 从小他就过着漂泊的生活，从未知道自己真正属于哪里。


将来时:
She will feel rootless if she moves to a foreign country without any support. 如果她搬到国外而没有任何支持，她将会感到无所适从。
They will continue to live rootlessly unless they find a stable job. 除非他们找到一份稳定的工作，否则他们将继续过着漂泊的生活。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't left his hometown, he wouldn't feel so rootless. 如果他没有离开家乡，他就不会感到如此无所适从。
I wish I didn't feel so rootless and alone. 我希望我不那么无所适从和孤独。


被动语态:
His feelings of rootlessness were exacerbated by the constant travel. 他那种无所适从的感觉因不断的旅行而加剧。
(Note:  Rootlessness itself is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Do you ever feel rootless in your life? 你在生活中有没有感到过无所适从？
Why do so many young people feel rootless today? 为什么今天这么多年轻人感到无所适从？


否定句:
He does not feel rootless anymore since he started a family.自从组建家庭后，他已经不再感到无所适从了。
She didn't feel rootless in her childhood because she had a strong family support system. 因为她有一个强大的家庭支持系统，所以她在童年时期并没有感到无所适从。
