good-hearted:/ˈɡʊdˈhɑːtɪd/ | adj. |心地善良的，好心的 | Kind and compassionate; having a generous and sympathetic nature.  |He's a good-hearted man who always helps others in need. 他是一个好心人，总是帮助需要帮助的人。|近义词：kind, benevolent, compassionate, charitable |反义词：cruel, heartless, unkind, malevolent


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  The usage changes depending on the sentence structure and what it modifies.

Example 1 (Simple Sentence):
The good-hearted volunteer spent hours helping the homeless.  = 心地善良的志愿者花了几个小时帮助无家可归的人。

Example 2 (Complex Sentence):
Because of her good-hearted nature, she donated a significant portion of her earnings to charity. = 由于她心地善良，她捐出了大部分收入给慈善机构。


Example 3 (Question):
Is he a truly good-hearted person, or is he just pretending? = 他真的是个好心人吗，还是只是装装样子？

Example 4 (Negative Sentence):
Despite his outward appearance, he wasn't actually a good-hearted individual. = 尽管外表看起来不错，但他实际上并非一个好心人。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrasal verbs or idioms directly using "good-hearted". However,  it can be part of longer phrases:

1. a good-hearted soul: 一个善良的人
2. a good-hearted gesture: 一个好心的举动
3. good-hearted intentions: 好心肠
4. seemingly good-hearted: 表面上善良的
5. truly good-hearted: 真正善良的
