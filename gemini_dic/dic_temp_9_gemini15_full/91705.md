It represents a more intense and inflexible form of conservatism than traditional conservatism. | The rise of hyperconservatism in the country led to increased social division and political gridlock.  极端保守主义在该国的兴起导致了社会分裂和政治僵局的加剧。 | 近义词：reactionarism, ultraconservatism, right-wing extremism | 反义词：radicalism, progressivism, liberalism
