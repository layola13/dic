deadheads:/ˈdedˌhedz/| n.| 死忠乐迷；（尤指）Grateful Dead乐队的歌迷；无业游民；无能的人 |People who are extremely devoted fans, especially of the Grateful Dead band.  The term can also refer to someone unemployed or incompetent.| 例句：Many deadheads followed the Grateful Dead on their tours. 许多死忠乐迷跟随Grateful Dead乐队巡演。

The term originally referred to people who, metaphorically, had their heads “dead,” suggesting a state of being unmotivated or unproductive. Over time, the term's connotation shifted to describe dedicated fans, particularly of the Grateful Dead.  |近义词：fanatics, enthusiasts, groupies (for musicians)|反义词：casual fans, critics|
