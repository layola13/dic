dearest:/ˈdɪərɪst/| adj.|最亲爱的，最心爱的|Most beloved; most precious.  It is a superlative form of "dear," expressing the highest degree of affection or fondness. |例句：My dearest friend, I miss you terribly. 我最亲爱的朋友，我非常想念你。

例句：To my dearest mother, thank you for everything. 致我最亲爱的母亲，感谢您的一切。


近义词：beloved, darling, loved, precious|反义词：unloved, hated, despised|词性：形容词
