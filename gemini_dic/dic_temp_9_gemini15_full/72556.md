fluttered:/ˈflʌtərd/ | v. | 扑动；飘动；激动；慌乱 | To move lightly and quickly; to flap (wings, etc.); to be agitated or nervous. |例句：The butterfly fluttered its wings. 蝴蝶拍打着翅膀。


The leaves fluttered in the breeze. 叶子在微风中飘动。


近义词：flapped, fluttered, trembled, quivered | 反义词：remained still, stayed calm

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flag flutters in the wind. = 旗帜在风中飘扬。
Birds flutter their wings before taking off. = 鸟儿在起飞前拍打翅膀。

一般过去时:
Her heart fluttered when she saw him. = 当她看到他时，她的心跳加速了。
The bird fluttered down to the ground. = 鸟儿扑腾着落到地上。

现在完成时:
The butterflies have fluttered around the flowers all day. = 蝴蝶整天都在花朵周围飞舞。
My heart has fluttered with excitement. = 我的心因兴奋而怦怦直跳。

过去完成时:
The leaves had fluttered to the ground before the storm arrived. = 暴风雨来临之前，树叶已经飘落到地面。
She had fluttered nervously before the interview. = 面试前她紧张地颤抖。

将来时:
The flags will flutter proudly in the breeze. = 旗帜将在微风中骄傲地飘扬。
I think my heart will flutter when I see her. = 我想当我看到她的时候我的心会怦怦乱跳。

虚拟语气:
If the bird fluttered its wings harder, it might fly away. = 如果鸟儿更用力地拍打翅膀，它可能会飞走。
If I hadn't fluttered so much, I might have passed the test. = 如果我不那么紧张，我可能会通过考试。

被动语态:
The flag was fluttered by the strong wind. = 旗帜被强风吹得飘动。
His feelings were fluttered by her words. = 他的感情被她的话所触动。

疑问句:
Did the bird flutter its wings? = 鸟儿拍打翅膀了吗？
Does your heart flutter when you're nervous? = 你紧张的时候心跳会加速吗？

否定句:
The flag didn't flutter in the still air. = 旗帜在静止的空气中没有飘动。
My heart didn't flutter; I remained calm. = 我的心跳没有加速；我保持冷静。
