precoracoid:/ˈpriːkɔːrəkɔɪd/| n. |前乌喙骨|A bone in the pectoral girdle of some vertebrates, lying in front of the coracoid.  It is particularly prominent in some extinct reptiles and amphibians. |Example: The precoracoid is a key feature in the study of early amniote evolution. 前乌喙骨是早期羊膜动物进化研究中的一个关键特征。|近义词:coracoid (部分情况下)|反义词:None|名词
