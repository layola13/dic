gangways:/ˈɡæŋweɪz/| n.|舷梯；通道；走道|A gangway is a passageway, especially a temporary or portable one, such as a walkway or ramp, for people to walk along, typically on board a ship or aircraft, or used for access to something.|例句：The passengers disembarked the ship via the gangway. 乘客通过舷梯下船。|例句：The workers used a temporary gangway to access the roof. 工人们使用临时走道登上屋顶。|近义词：passage, walkway, ramp, corridor|反义词：dead end
