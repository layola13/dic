megadeath:/ˈmɛɡəˌdɛθ/| n.|百万死亡；大规模死亡|A megadeath is a unit of measurement representing one million deaths.  It is often used in discussions of the potential casualties of nuclear war or other catastrophic events. The term is less frequently used in formal contexts due to its bluntness and the ethical implications of quantifying human lives.|例句：The simulation predicted a megadeath toll in the event of a large-scale nuclear conflict.  模拟预测，大规模核冲突将造成百万人的死亡。|近义词：massacre, carnage, holocaust|反义词:survival, salvation
