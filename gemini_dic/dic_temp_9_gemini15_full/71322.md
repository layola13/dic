fizzy:/ˈfɪzi/| adj.| 起泡的，嘶嘶作响的 | Containing bubbles; effervescent.  |例句：The fizzy drink was refreshing on a hot day.  炎热的一天，这种汽水很清爽。|例句：The champagne was delightfully fizzy. 香槟酒的气泡令人愉快。|近义词：effervescent, bubbly, sparkling |反义词：flat, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soda is fizzy. = 这苏打水很起泡。
This lemonade tastes fizzy. = 这柠檬水尝起来很气泡。

一般过去时:
The drink was very fizzy. = 这饮料非常起泡。
The water was fizzy after the treatment. = 处理后，水变得很起泡。

现在完成时:
The bottle has gone fizzy. = 这瓶汽水已经变没气了。(Note:  This uses "fizzy" in a slightly different way, implying a loss of fizziness)
The champagne has become less fizzy. = 香槟酒的气泡变少了。

过去完成时:
The soda had gone flat; it wasn't fizzy anymore. = 苏打水已经没气了；它不再起泡了。
The beer had lost its fizzy quality. = 这啤酒已经失去了起泡的特性。

将来时:
The drink will be fizzy when it's cold. = 这饮料冷了之后就会起泡。
The soda will be less fizzy after it's been open for a while. = 这苏打水开封一段时间后气泡会减少。

虚拟语气:
If the drink were fizzy, I would drink it. = 如果这饮料是起泡的，我会喝它。
I wish this juice were more fizzy. = 我希望这果汁更起泡一些。

被动语态:
The drink is made to be fizzy. = 这饮料是特意做成起泡的。
The soda is described as fizzy on the label. = 苏打水的标签上写着“起泡的”。

疑问句:
Is the water fizzy? = 这水是起泡的吗？
Is the lemonade still fizzy? = 这柠檬水还起泡吗？

否定句:
The drink isn't fizzy. = 这饮料不起泡。
The beer isn't very fizzy. = 这啤酒不太起泡。


PHRASE->
fizzy drinks = 汽水
fizzy water = 汽泡水
a fizzy feeling =  一种兴奋的感觉 (Figurative use)
fizzy wine = 气泡酒
get fizzy = 变的兴奋（口语化用法）
