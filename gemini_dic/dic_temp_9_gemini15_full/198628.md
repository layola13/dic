shunting:/ˈʃʌntɪŋ/| n. v.|调车；转移；分流|The act of moving railway cars from one track to another, or the act of diverting something from its usual course.|例句：The shunting yard was busy with the movement of freight cars. 调车场里货车川流不息。The project was shunted aside due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目被搁置一旁。|近义词：switching, diverting, shifting|反义词：maintaining, continuing
