roseless:/ˈroʊzlis/| adj. |无玫瑰的；缺乏玫瑰般美好的|Lacking roses; without the beauty or qualities associated with roses.  The adjective "roseless" describes something that is devoid of roses or lacks the qualities typically associated with roses, such as beauty, fragrance, or romance.  It can be used literally or figuratively. |例句：The roseless garden felt desolate.  =  没有玫瑰的花园显得荒凉。The roseless winter brought a sense of bleakness. =  没有玫瑰的冬天带来一种凄凉的感觉。|近义词：rose-less, unromantic, bleak, barren |反义词：rose-filled, romantic, beautiful, fragrant
