pointlike:/ˈpɔɪntlaɪk/| adj.|点状的；类似点的|Resembling a point; very small and precise.  It is often used to describe something that is small, sharp, or focused, similar to a point.|例句：The distant stars appeared pointlike through the telescope. 遥远的星星在望远镜里看起来像一个个点。  The laser beam was incredibly pointlike and accurate. 激光束非常细小而精准。|近义词：punctiform, minute, tiny|反义词：diffuse, widespread, large
