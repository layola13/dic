eriometer:/ɪəriˈɒmɪtə(r)/| n. |爱里奥米特(测量纤维直径的仪器)|An eriometer is an instrument used to measure the diameter of fibers, especially wool fibers.  It works by measuring the diffraction patterns produced by light passing through the fibers. |The eriometer gave a precise measurement of the wool fiber diameter.|近义词：fiber diameter measuring device|反义词: N/A
