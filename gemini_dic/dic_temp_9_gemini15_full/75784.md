fumarate:/ˈfjuːməreɪt/| n.|富马酸盐|Fumarate is the salt or ester of fumaric acid.  It's an important intermediate in the citric acid cycle (also known as the Krebs cycle), a crucial metabolic pathway in cells.  Fumarates are also used in various industrial applications.|The enzyme fumarase catalyzes the reversible hydration of fumarate to malate. |近义词：fumaric acid salt|反义词：无
