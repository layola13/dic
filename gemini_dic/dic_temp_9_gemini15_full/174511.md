quinquennium:/kwɪŋˈkwɛniəm/| n. |五年期；五年节|A period of five years.  The word is derived from the Latin words "quinque" (five) and "annum" (year). It is often used in official or formal contexts to refer to a specific five-year period, such as a parliamentary term or a development plan.|The quinquennium of the project saw significant progress.|近义词：five-year period, lustrum|反义词：无
