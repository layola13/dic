siphonous:/ˈsaɪfənəs/| adj. |虹吸管状的，具虹吸管的；管状的|Having a siphon or siphons; tubular.  Referring to algae that lack cellular septation and have a multinucleate cytoplasm. |例句：The siphonous algae exhibit a unique cellular structure.  虹吸管状藻类表现出独特的细胞结构。  The researcher observed the siphonous growth pattern in the specimen. 研究人员观察到了样本中的虹吸状生长模式。|近义词：tubular, canalicular |反义词：septate (referring to algae)
