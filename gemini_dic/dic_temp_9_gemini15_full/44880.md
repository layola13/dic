crybaby:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/| n. |爱哭鬼，爱哭的人 |A crybaby is a person, especially a child, who cries frequently or easily.  They often complain or whine excessively. |例句：He's such a crybaby; he cries every time he doesn't get his way. 他是个爱哭鬼；每次没遂了他的愿就哭。

例句：Don't be such a crybaby! Just get over it. 别那么爱哭！克服它。


近义词：whiner, complainer, weakling, milksop

反义词：tough, stoic, brave, resilient

词性:名词
