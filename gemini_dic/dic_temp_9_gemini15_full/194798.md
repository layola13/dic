semafor:/ˈsɛməˌfɔːr/| n. |信号灯；信号机；信号|A semafor is a signaling device, typically a visual one, used to give instructions or warnings.  It is often used to control traffic, railway signals, or other movements.  |例句：The traffic semafor turned red, so I stopped my car. 交通信号灯变红，所以我停下了车。

The railway semafor indicated that the train could proceed. 铁路信号灯指示火车可以继续行驶。|近义词：signal, light, indicator |反义词：None.
