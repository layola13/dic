grudgingness:/ˈɡrʌdʒɪŋnəs/| n. |勉强，不愿，不情愿 |Reluctant or unwilling agreement or compliance; resentment.  It implies a feeling of being forced into something against one's will, often accompanied by resentment or ill will. |例句：He admitted his mistake with grudgingness. 他勉强承认了自己的错误。


例句：Her grudgingness was evident in her terse reply.  她的不情愿从她简短的回答中显而易见。


近义词：reluctance, unwillingness, resentment, begrudging, unwillingness


反义词：willingness, eagerness, enthusiasm
