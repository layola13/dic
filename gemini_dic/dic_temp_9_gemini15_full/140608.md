olivine:/ˈɒlɪviːn/ | n. |橄榄石|A silicate mineral, typically olive-green in color, consisting of magnesium and iron silicate.  It is a common mineral in the Earth's mantle and is often found in igneous and metamorphic rocks. | Example: Olivine is a common mineral in basalt.  橄榄石是玄武岩中常见的矿物。|近义词：chrysolite (古称)|反义词：无
