postcretaceous: /ˌpoʊstkrɪˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 白垩纪后的 | Postcretaceous refers to the geological period and events that occurred after the Cretaceous period, which ended approximately 66 million years ago. It encompasses the Cenozoic Era, including the Paleogene, Neogene, and Quaternary periods.  The term is primarily used in geology and paleontology. | The postcretaceous extinction event led to the rise of mammals. 白垩纪后的灭绝事件导致了哺乳动物的兴起。 |  Cenozoic, Tertiary | Precambrian  | adj.
