facsimile's:/ˈfæksɪmɪliz/ | n. | 传真；复制品 | A facsimile is an exact copy, especially of a document.  It's often used in the context of a fax machine. | Example: The museum displayed a facsimile of the ancient scroll.  博物馆展出了古代卷轴的复制品。 |近义词:copy, reproduction, duplicate |反义词:original
