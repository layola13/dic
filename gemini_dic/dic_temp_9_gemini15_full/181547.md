relace:/rɪˈleɪs/ | vt. |替换，更换|To replace something with something else.  It implies a substitution of one thing for another.|例句：He relaced the worn-out shoelaces with new ones. 他用新的鞋带替换了磨损的鞋带。|近义词：replace, substitute, exchange |反义词：retain, keep
