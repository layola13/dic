mini-cab: /ˈmɪniˌkæb/ | n. |小型出租车，迷你出租车 | A small taxi, typically privately owned and operated, often unlicensed or operating outside the framework of a larger taxi company.  Mini-cabs are usually cheaper than licensed taxis but may not offer the same level of regulation and safety. |  例句：I took a mini-cab to the airport. 我坐小型出租车去了机场。


例句：The mini-cab driver knew a shortcut through the back streets. 小型出租车司机知道一条穿过后街的捷径。


近义词：private hire car, unlicensed taxi


反义词：licensed taxi, black cab (in the UK)


词性：名词
