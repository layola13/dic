lutheranism:/ˈluːθərənɪzəm/| n. |路德宗，路德教|The theological system and practices of the Lutheran Church, based on the teachings of Martin Luther.  Lutheranism emphasizes salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, as revealed in the Bible.  It is a major branch of Protestantism. |Example:  Lutheranism spread rapidly throughout Northern Europe after the Reformation. 路德教在宗教改革后迅速传播到北欧各地。|近义词：Protestantism, Reformation |反义词：Catholicism
