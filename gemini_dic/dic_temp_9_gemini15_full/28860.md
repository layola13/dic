carpetbaggeries:/ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbæɡəriːz/ | n. | 骗子行为；投机行为；（尤指内战后南方重建时期）北方人到南方发财的投机行为 | The term "carpetbagger" originally referred to Northerners who went South after the Civil War seeking personal gain, often exploiting the instability and poverty of the region.  The term carries a strong negative connotation, implying dishonesty and opportunism.  Carpetbaggeries, therefore, refers to the actions and practices of these individuals. | The Reconstruction era was rife with carpetbaggeries, as unscrupulous individuals exploited the South's vulnerability.  | 近义词：exploitation, profiteering, graft, racketeering | 反义词：altruism, philanthropy, integrity | 名词
