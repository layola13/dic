chondrule:/ˈkɒndrʊl/| n. |球粒|A small, spherical grain in a chondrite meteorite, believed to have formed in the early solar system before the accretion of the meteorite.  Chondrules are typically composed of silicate minerals. |例句：The meteorite sample contained numerous chondrules, indicating its extraterrestrial origin.  这块陨石样本包含许多球粒，表明其起源于地球以外。|近义词：spherule (in this context)|反义词：N/A
