rotisserie:/roʊˈtɪsəri/ | n. |旋转烤肉架；旋转烤肉 |A rotisserie is a device for roasting meat by rotating it on a spit over a heat source.  It often refers to the cooked meat as well.  The even cooking produces juicy and flavorful results. | Example:  He bought a new rotisserie to cook a whole chicken. 他买了一个新的旋转烤肉架来烤整鸡。  The restaurant is famous for its rotisserie chicken. 这家餐厅以其旋转烤鸡而闻名。 |近义词:spit, grill, barbecue |反义词:boiler, steamer
