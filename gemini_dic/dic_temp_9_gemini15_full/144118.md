outscores:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːrz/| vt.|胜过；得分超过|To achieve a higher score than (someone or something).|例句：Our team outscored their team by ten points. 我们的队伍以十分之差胜过他们。|例句：He consistently outscores his classmates on math tests. 他在数学考试中总是比他的同学们得分高。|近义词：surpass, exceed, beat, top|反义词：underscore, lose to
