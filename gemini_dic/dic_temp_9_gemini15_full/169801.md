proscenium:/proʊˈsiːniəm/| n. | 前台;舞台前沿 | The proscenium is the part of a stage that is in front of the curtain.  It's the architectural frame separating the audience from the stage. | Example: The actors took their bows at the edge of the proscenium.  演员们在舞台前沿谢幕。  |近义词:stage front, arch |反义词:backstage, auditorium
