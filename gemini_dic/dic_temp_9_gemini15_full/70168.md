fibers:/ˈfaɪbərz/| n.|纤维，纤维状物质|Slender, threadlike structures that are the fundamental units of many natural and synthetic materials.  Fibers can be natural, such as cotton, wool, or silk, or synthetic, such as nylon or polyester. They are often used in textiles, but also have applications in other industries like composites and insulation.|例句：Cotton fibers are used to make clothing. 棉花纤维用于制作衣物。

The fibers in this carpet are very durable. 这地毯的纤维非常耐用。|近义词：threads, filaments, strands|反义词：solids, aggregates
