omnivorisms:/ˌɒmnɪˈvɔːrɪzəm/ | n. |杂食性；杂食动物习性 | The practice or characteristic of eating both plants and animals.  Omnivorism is a dietary habit common among many animal species, allowing for adaptability to various food sources. | Example: The omnivorism of humans has allowed us to thrive in diverse environments. 人类的杂食性使我们能够在不同的环境中繁荣发展。|近义词：omnivorousness |反义词：herbivorism, carnivorism
