paradisically:/ˌpærəˈdaɪsɪkli/| adj.|天堂般地，极乐地|Extremely pleasant and delightful; like paradise.  The word evokes a sense of idyllic beauty, peace, and happiness.|例句：The island was paradisically beautiful, with white sand beaches and crystal-clear water.  这个岛屿美得像天堂一样，拥有白色的沙滩和清澈见底的海水。

例句：The couple spent their honeymoon in a paradisically secluded villa. 这对夫妇在一片与世隔绝的天堂般的别墅里度过了蜜月。


近义词：heavenly, idyllic, utopian, blissful, enchanting

反义词：hellish, dreadful, awful, miserable, nightmarish

词性：形容词
