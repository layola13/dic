clean-and-jerk: /kliːn ænd ˈdʒɜːrk/ | n. |挺举 | A weightlifting exercise consisting of two movements: the clean and the jerk.  The clean involves lifting a barbell from the ground to the shoulders, and the jerk involves pushing the barbell overhead. | 例句：He performed a clean-and-jerk of 150 kilograms. 他完成了150公斤的挺举。 | 近义词：overhead lift, weightlifting exercise | 反义词：N/A | 名词
