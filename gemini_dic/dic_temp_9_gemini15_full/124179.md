mosaiced:/mɒˈzeɪkt/| adj.|马赛克的；用马赛克装饰的|Decorated or inlaid with mosaics.  The adjective describes something that is made of or resembles a mosaic, characterized by a combination of diverse elements forming a unified whole.|例句：The bathroom floor was beautifully mosaiced with colorful tiles.  浴室地板用色彩鲜艳的瓷砖漂亮地装饰成马赛克图案。

The church's ceiling is mosaiced with gold leaf and precious stones. 教堂的天花板镶嵌着金箔和宝石，呈现马赛克图案。|近义词：mosaic, tessellated|反义词：uniform, plain
