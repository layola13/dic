intermediates:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiəts/| n.|中间体，中间产物|Substances formed during a chemical reaction before the final product is produced.  They are often unstable and reactive.|例句：The reaction proceeds through several intermediates before yielding the desired product.  反应经过几个中间体才生成所需的产物。

例句：Many organic reactions involve unstable intermediates that are difficult to isolate. 许多有机反应涉及难以分离的不稳定中间体。


近义词：intermediaries,  transitional products|反义词：final products, end products|名词
