paso:/ˈpɑːsoʊ/| n. |台阶；步骤；步伐；山口；通行证|a step, especially one in a series or process; a stage in a journey or process; a mountain pass. |例句：Take one step at a time. = 一步一步来。
The project is in its final stages. = 这个项目处于最后阶段。|近义词：step, stage, phase |反义词：leap, jump


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He takes a slow paso. = 他迈着缓慢的步伐。
She follows each paso carefully. = 她仔细地按照每一步步骤进行。

一般过去时:
He took a large paso forward. = 他向前迈了一大步。
They followed the pasos in the recipe. = 他们按照食谱上的步骤进行。

现在完成时:
She has completed all the pasos. = 她已经完成了所有步骤。
We have taken many pasos toward our goal. = 我们朝着目标迈出了许多步。

过去完成时:
He had already taken several pasos before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经迈出了好几步。
They had carefully reviewed all the pasos before starting. = 他们开始之前已经仔细检查了所有步骤。

将来时:
She will take the next paso tomorrow. = 她明天将采取下一步行动。
We will follow the pasos outlined in the manual. = 我们将按照手册中列出的步骤进行。

虚拟语气:
If I knew the next paso, I would proceed. = 如果我知道下一步是什么，我就会继续。
I wish I had known the pasos beforehand. = 我真希望我事先知道这些步骤。

被动语态:

疑问句:
What is the next paso? = 下一步是什么？
What pasos are involved in this process? = 这个过程中涉及哪些步骤？

否定句:

PHRASE->
paso a paso = step by step (逐步地)
dar un paso = to take a step (迈出一步)
paso firme = firm step (坚定的步伐)
primer paso = first step (第一步)
en paso = in step (同步)
