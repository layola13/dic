self-enjoyments:/selfɪnˈdʒɔɪmənts/| n. | 自娱自乐；个人享受 | Activities or experiences that bring pleasure or satisfaction to oneself.  It often implies activities done alone or independently for personal gratification. | Example:  Her self-enjoyments included reading, gardening, and painting. 她的自娱自乐包括阅读、园艺和绘画。  |近义词：pleasures, diversions, recreations, indulgences | 反义词：self-denial, hardship, suffering | 名词
