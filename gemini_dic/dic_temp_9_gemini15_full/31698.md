chauvinists:/ˈʃoʊvənɪsts/| n. |沙文主义者；盲目爱国者 |A chauvinist is a person who believes in the superiority of their own country, group, or sex and shows contempt for others.  They are excessively patriotic and often prejudiced against those they consider inferior. |Example:  His chauvinistic views were evident in his derogatory comments about women. 他沙文主义的观点在他对女性的贬低性评论中显而易见。

Example: Some argue that national chauvinism hinders international cooperation. 有人认为民族沙文主义阻碍了国际合作。|近义词：jingoist, bigot, nationalist |反义词：internationalist, cosmopolitan, pacifist
