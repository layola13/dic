patellar:/pəˈtɛlə(r)/| n. |髌骨的；膝盖骨的 | Relating to or denoting the patella (kneecap).  The patellar tendon connects the patella to the tibia.  |例句：He suffered a patellar fracture during the game. 他在比赛中遭受了髌骨骨折。

例句：The doctor examined his patellar reflex. 医生检查了他的膝反射。


近义词：kneecap (informal)

反义词：无

词性：形容词
