kathetometer:/ˌkæθəˈmɪtər/| n. |卡尺测高仪，测高计 |A kathetometer is a precision instrument used to measure vertical distances or differences in height with high accuracy. It typically consists of a vertically mounted telescope that can be moved up and down along a precisely calibrated scale.  |The scientist used a kathetometer to precisely measure the height of the liquid column in the experiment. 科学家用卡尺测高仪精确测量实验中液柱的高度。|vernier caliper, micrometer |  |n.
