larded:/ˈlɑːrdɪd/ | v. | 填满…；在…中加入肥肉；(用肥肉)使肥沃 | To insert (something, such as fat) into something else; to enrich or embellish excessively. |例句：The speech was larded with quotations from famous authors. 这篇演讲充斥着名家名言。  |例句：The rich soil was larded with manure. 肥沃的土壤里掺杂着肥料。|近义词：intersperse, enrich, embellish |反义词：simplify,  reduce


USAGE->

He lards his speeches with jokes. = 他在演讲中穿插笑话。
She lards the cake batter with butter. = 她往蛋糕糊里加黄油。
