reanalyzer:/ˌriːˈænəlaɪzər/| n. |重新分析器，再分析器 |A reanalyzer is a tool or system that performs a second analysis of data or information, often to refine or correct a previous analysis or to extract additional insights.  It may involve revisiting previous steps, using different algorithms or techniques, or incorporating new data. | Example: The research team used a reanalyzer to further investigate the anomalies detected in the initial data analysis.  重新分析小组使用重新分析器来进一步调查在初始数据分析中检测到的异常现象。|近义词：re-examiner, validator, verifier |反义词：analyzer (in the sense of a primary analysis)
