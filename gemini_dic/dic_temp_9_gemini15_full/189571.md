salespersons:/ˈseɪlzˌpɜːrsənz/ | n. |销售人员，售货员 | A salesperson is a person who sells goods or services.  Salespersons work in various settings, from retail stores to online platforms. They are responsible for interacting with customers, understanding their needs, and persuading them to make a purchase.  | Example: The salespersons at that store were very helpful.  那些商店的销售人员非常乐于助人。

The company hired several new salespersons to boost its sales figures.  公司雇佣了几名新的销售人员来提高销售额。|近义词:sales representatives, vendors, retailers |反义词:customers, buyers

词源:
