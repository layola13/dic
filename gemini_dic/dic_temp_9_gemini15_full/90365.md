how:/haʊ/| adv.|如何；多么；到什么程度|英文解析：How is used to ask questions about the manner, method, or degree of something.  It can also express the extent or degree of something.|例句：How did you do that? 你是怎么做到的？

How are you feeling today? 你今天感觉如何？


近义词：in what way, to what extent|反义词:N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
How do you feel? = 你感觉如何？
How does she get to work? = 她怎么上班？


一般过去时:
How did you get here? = 你是怎么到这里来的？
How did the accident happen? = 事故是怎么发生的？


现在完成时:
How have you been? = 你最近好吗？
How has the project progressed? = 项目进展如何？


过去完成时:
How had he managed to do that? = 他是怎么做到那件事的？
How had they solved the problem before? = 他们以前是怎么解决这个问题的？


将来时:
How will you get there? = 你将如何到达那里？
How will the weather be tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
I don't know how I would react. = 我不知道我会如何反应。
I wish I knew how to fix it. = 我真希望知道怎么修理它。


被动语态:
How is the problem being solved? = 这个问题是如何被解决的？
How was the decision made? = 这个决定是如何做出的？


疑问句:
How old are you? = 你几岁了？
How much does it cost? = 它多少钱？


否定句:
I don't know how to help. = 我不知道该如何帮助。
She didn't explain how to do it. = 她没有解释怎么做。


PHRASE->
How about...? = ...怎么样？
How come...? = 怎么会...？
How much...? = ...多少钱？
How many...? = ...有多少？
How often...? = 多久一次？
