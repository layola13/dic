semi-professional: /ˌsɛmiprəˈfɛʃənəl/ | adj. | 半专业的；业余的 |  Relating to or denoting a level of skill or activity that is not fully professional but is more advanced than amateur.  It suggests a level of commitment and skill above amateur but below fully professional.  | 例句：He plays semi-professional football on weekends. 他周末踢半职业足球。  He's a semi-professional photographer, taking commissions but not relying solely on photography for income. 他是一位半职业摄影师，接一些订单，但并不完全依赖摄影收入。 | 近义词：part-time professional, near-professional | 反义词：professional, amateur
