shrimping:/ˈʃrɪmpɪŋ/| v.|捕虾；从事捕虾业|The act of catching shrimp, either commercially or recreationally.  It involves various methods, from trawling to hand-picking.|例句：He spent his summer shrimping off the coast of Louisiana. 他在路易斯安那州海岸捕虾度过了夏天。
例句：Shrimping is a major industry in this region. 捕虾业是该地区的主要产业。|近义词：fishing, trawling |反义词：None specifically, but activities like farming (if contrasting with wild shrimping) could be considered opposite.
