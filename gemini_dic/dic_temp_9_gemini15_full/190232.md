saponacity: /ˌsæpəˈnæsɪti/ | n. |皂化性，皂化能力|The ability of a substance to undergo saponification (the process of soap making).  It refers to the ease and extent to which a substance reacts with an alkali to form soap. |The saponacity of the oil determines the quality of the soap produced.  这种油的皂化能力决定了所生产肥皂的质量。|近义词：soap-making ability, saponifiable property |反义词：insaponifiability
