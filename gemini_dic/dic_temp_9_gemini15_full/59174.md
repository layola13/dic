dysfunctioned:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənd/| v.|功能失调；发生故障；运转不良 |  Past tense and past participle of "dysfunction."  It describes a state where something is not working properly or as intended.  |例句：The machine dysfunctioned during the critical experiment.  机器在关键实验中发生了故障。

The software dysfunctioned, causing a system crash. 软件发生故障，导致系统崩溃。

|近义词：malfunctioned, failed, broke down |反义词：functioned, worked, operated
