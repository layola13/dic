disturbances:/dɪˈstɜːrbənsɪz/ | n. | 扰乱；骚乱；障碍 |  A disturbance is an interruption of peace and quiet or of the normal course of events. It can refer to a variety of situations, from minor annoyances to major upheavals.  | Example 1: The loud music caused a disturbance in the library.  喧闹的音乐扰乱了图书馆的秩序。| Example 2:  Civil disturbances broke out in the city after the election. 选举后，该市爆发了民变。 |近义词：disruptions, troubles, turmoil, unrest | 反义词：peace, calm, tranquility, order |

过去完成时:
