horseshoes:/ˈhɔːrsʃuːz/| n. |马蹄铁|U形的金属片，钉在马蹄上以保护马蹄免受磨损和损伤。Horseshoes are typically made of iron or steel and are shaped to fit the contours of the horse's hoof. They are nailed onto the hoof with special horseshoe nails.  | Example: The blacksmith carefully shaped the horseshoe to fit the horse's hoof.  铁匠小心地将马蹄铁塑造成适合马蹄的形状。|近义词：horse shoe (单数)|反义词：无|名词
