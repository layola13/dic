It implies a deliberate act of copying before the original is even presented or known.  This word is relatively rare and often found in specialized contexts. | The preimitated designs were quickly discovered to be derivative. = 预先模仿的设计很快就被发现是抄袭的。  The artist's style, while seemingly original, was subtly preimitated from his mentor. = 艺术家的风格，虽然看起来很原创，但却微妙地模仿了他的导师。|近义词:copied, duplicated, plagiarized | 反义词:original, innovative, unprecedented |
