categorizing: /ˈkætəɡəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 分类，归类 | The act of assigning something to a category or class.  This involves identifying shared characteristics and grouping items accordingly.  It's a fundamental process in organization and understanding information. | Example:  He is categorizing the books by author. 他正在按作者分类书籍。  The librarian is categorizing the new arrivals. 图书管理员正在对新书进行分类。|近义词：classifying, sorting, grouping, arranging | 反义词：randomizing, mixing, jumbling | 动词
