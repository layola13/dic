mendaciously:/mənˈdeɪʃəsli/| adv.| 虚伪地；说谎地 |  Characterized by or given to falsehood or deception; lying.  | 例句：He mendaciously claimed to have witnessed the crime. 他虚伪地声称目击了这起犯罪。

例句：She mendaciously denied any involvement in the scandal. 她虚伪地否认与这起丑闻有任何牵连。


近义词：deceitfully, deceptively, falsely, dishonestly

反义词：truthfully, honestly, sincerely


词性：副词
