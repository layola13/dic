cauterizes:/ˈkɔːtəraɪzɪz/| vt.| 烧灼；止血；使…失去活力 |To burn the surface of (tissue) with a hot instrument or caustic substance, typically to stop bleeding or prevent infection.  It implies a searing or burning effect to destroy tissue or stop bleeding. |例句：The surgeon cauterized the wound to stop the bleeding. = 外科医生用烧灼法止住了伤口出血。

The doctor cauterized the small cut to prevent infection. = 医生烧灼了小伤口以防止感染。


近义词：sear, burn, sear, coagulate |反义词：heal, soothe
