millennial:/mɪˈleniəl/| n. adj.| 千禧一代；千禧年的； |A millennial is a person who reached adulthood around the turn of the 21st century.  Millennials are often characterized by their use of technology and their focus on social and environmental issues.|例句：Many millennials are entrepreneurs. 许多千禧一代是企业家。
The millennial generation is known for its tech-savviness. 千禧一代以其精通技术而闻名。|近义词：Gen Y, Generation Y|反义词：Baby Boomer
