entreatance:/ɪnˈtriːtəns/ | n. |恳求；请求；哀求 |The act of entreating; earnest or humble request.  It implies a strong plea, often made with humility and desperation.  |例句：He made a heartfelt entreatance for mercy. 他恳切地请求宽恕。

例句：Her entreatance to her father was met with a stern refusal.  她向父亲的恳求遭到了严厉的拒绝。


近义词：supplication, plea, petition, appeal


反义词：demand, command, order


词性：名词


词源：源自动词entreat (恳求，哀求)，加上-ance构成名词后缀，表示“行为”或“状态”。Entreat本身源自古法语entreter，最终源自拉丁语interrogare（询问）。
