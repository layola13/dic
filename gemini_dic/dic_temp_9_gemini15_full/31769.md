check-outs:/ˈtʃekˌaʊts/| n.|结账处；退房；签出|The process of leaving a hotel or other accommodation, or the process of paying for goods at a store.  It can also refer to the act of borrowing library books or similar items.|例句：The check-outs were long lines at the supermarket during the holiday rush. 节日期间超市结账处排起了长队。|例句：We had a smooth check-out from the hotel. 我们顺利办理了酒店退房手续。|近义词：check-out counters, departures, exits|反义词：check-ins
