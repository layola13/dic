leafs:/liːvz/| v.|叶子；树叶|The plural form of "leaf".  It describes the action of leaves falling or the act of turning over pages.  |The leaves are changing color. 叶子正在变色。|The wind leafs through the pages of the book. 风翻动着书页。|falls, flutters, drops|remains, stays|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind leafs through the pages. = 风翻动着书页。
He leafs through the magazine quickly. = 他快速翻阅杂志。

一般过去时:
She leafed through the book looking for a specific passage. = 她翻阅书籍寻找特定的段落。
The leaves leafed down from the tree. = 叶子从树上飘落下来。

现在完成时:
The gardener has leafed through all the seed catalogs. = 园丁已经翻阅了所有的种子目录。
The wind has leafed the pages of the book. = 风已经翻动了书页。

过去完成时:
He had leafed through the newspaper before he saw the article. = 他看完报纸后才看到那篇文章。
The children had leafed through the coloring book before dinner. = 孩子们在晚餐前已经翻阅了涂色书。

将来时:
She will leaf through the contract carefully. = 她会仔细地翻阅合同。
The leaves will leaf down in autumn. = 秋天树叶会飘落。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would leaf through that interesting novel. = 如果我有更多时间，我会仔细阅读那本有趣的书。
I wish I had leafed through the manual before attempting the repair. = 我希望在尝试修理之前先翻阅一下说明书。

被动语态:
The pages were leafed through by the librarian. = 书页被图书管理员翻阅了。
The book was leafed through carelessly. = 书被粗心地翻阅了。


疑问句:
Did he leaf through the report? = 他翻阅了报告吗？
Has she leafed through all the documents? = 她翻阅了所有文件了吗？

否定句:
He didn't leaf through the book properly. = 他没有好好翻阅这本书。
She didn't leaf through the magazine at all. = 她根本没有翻阅杂志。
