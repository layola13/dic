neutralising: /ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 中和；使无效；抵消 | To make something ineffective or harmless; to counteract the effect of something.  | The antidote neutralized the poison.  解毒剂中和了毒药。 | The government's new policy aims to neutralize the opposition. 政府的新政策旨在中和反对派。 | counteract, offset, cancel out | exacerbate, intensify, strengthen | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He neutralizes the acid with an alkali. = 他用碱中和酸。
The drug neutralizes the effects of the venom. = 这种药物能中和毒液的毒性。

一般过去时:
The scientist neutralized the harmful bacteria. = 科学家使有害细菌失效。
She neutralized the threat by calling the police. = 她通过报警来消除威胁。

现在完成时:
The medication has neutralized the pain. = 药物已经缓解了疼痛。
They have neutralized the enemy's attack. = 他们已经化解了敌人的攻击。

过去完成时:
He had neutralized the bomb before it exploded. = 他在炸弹爆炸前已经将其化解了。
The army had neutralized the rebel forces. = 军队已经平定了叛军。

将来时:
We will neutralize the threat before it escalates. = 我们会在威胁升级之前将其消除。
The new system will neutralize the negative effects. = 新系统将抵消负面影响。

虚拟语气:
If we had neutralized the virus earlier, fewer people would have been infected. = 如果我们早点消灭病毒，感染的人就会少一些。
I wish I could neutralize the bad feelings between them. = 我希望我能消除他们之间的隔阂。

被动语态:
The harmful substance was neutralized by the antidote. = 有害物质被解毒剂中和了。
The enemy's attack was neutralized by our forces. = 敌人的攻击被我军击退了。

疑问句:
How can we neutralize the negative impact? = 我们如何才能抵消负面影响？
Will the treaty neutralize the conflict? = 这项条约能否化解冲突？

否定句:
The treatment didn't neutralize the infection. = 治疗没有消除感染。
He couldn't neutralize the opponent's argument. = 他无法反驳对手的论点。
