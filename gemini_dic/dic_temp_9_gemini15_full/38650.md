concurrently:/kənˈkʌrəntli/ | adv. |同时地，并发地 | happening or existing at the same time.  The two events happened concurrently.  |例句：The two projects were run concurrently. 这两个项目同时进行。 |例句：The symptoms appeared concurrently with the onset of the disease. 症状与疾病发作同时出现。|近义词：simultaneously, at the same time, together |反义词：sequentially, successively
