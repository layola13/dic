cohosted:/koʊˈhoʊstɪd/| v. |共同主持|To host an event or program together with someone else.  This implies a shared responsibility and equal participation in the hosting duties.|例句：The conference was cohosted by the university and a local business. 这场会议由大学和一家当地企业共同主办。|近义词：jointly hosted, hosted together|反义词：solely hosted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cohost a weekly podcast. = 他们共同主持一个每周播客。
We cohost the charity gala every year. = 我们每年共同主持慈善晚会。

一般过去时:
She cohosted the awards ceremony with her colleague. = 她和她的同事一起主持了颁奖典礼。
They cohosted a successful fundraising event last month. = 他们上个月共同举办了一场成功的筹款活动。

现在完成时:
They have cohosted several events this year. = 今年他们已经共同主持了几场活动。
We have cohosted this show for five years. = 我们已经共同主持这个节目五年了。


过去完成时:
Before the show, they had cohosted a similar event in another city. = 节目之前，他们在另一个城市共同主持过类似的活动。
He had cohosted several podcasts before starting his own. = 在开始自己的播客之前，他已经共同主持过几个播客。
