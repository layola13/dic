overresponding:/ˌoʊvərɪˈspɑːndɪŋ/ | adj. |反应过度，过度反应|Responding excessively or disproportionately to a stimulus or situation.  It implies a reaction that is stronger or more intense than what is considered appropriate or necessary.|例句：His overresponding to criticism led to unnecessary conflict. 他的过度反应批评导致了不必要的冲突。The market's overresponding to the news caused a sharp drop in prices. 市场对新闻的过度反应导致价格暴跌。|近义词：overreacting, hypersensitive, overly sensitive |反义词：underreacting, indifferent, unresponsive
