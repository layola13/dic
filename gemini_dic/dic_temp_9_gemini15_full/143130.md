outcarolling:/ˌaʊtˈkærəlɪŋ/| v.|胜过…的歌唱|To surpass in singing; to outsing.|The choir outcarolling all others in the competition.  合唱团在比赛中胜过所有其他合唱团。|近义词:outsing, outdo, surpass|反义词:be outsung by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outcarol the other singers every year. = 他们每年都胜过其他歌手的歌唱。
The birds outcarol each other in the morning. = 鸟儿们早晨互相竞相歌唱，彼此胜过。


一般过去时:
She outcaroled her rival in the final round. = 她在决赛中胜过了她的对手的歌唱。
The soloist outcaroled the chorus during the performance. = 独奏者在演出中胜过了合唱队的歌唱。


现在完成时:
He has outcaroled everyone he's ever competed against. = 他已经胜过所有和他比赛过的歌手。
The choir has outcaroled many other groups in recent years. = 近年来，这个合唱团已经胜过许多其他团体。


过去完成时:
By the end of the concert, she had outcaroled all the other performers. = 音乐会结束时，她已经胜过了所有其他表演者的歌唱。
Before the competition, he had outcaroled his practice partners many times. = 比赛前，他已经多次胜过他的练习伙伴。
