neckplate:/ˈnekˌpleɪt/| n. |护颈板；颈板|A neckplate is a plate, typically made of metal, attached to the neck of a musical instrument, such as a guitar or bass, to reinforce the neck joint and provide stability.  It often has screws that attach the neck to the body of the instrument. |Example: The guitar's neckplate was loose, causing buzzing sounds.|近义词：neck reinforcement plate|反义词: (无明确反义词)|名词
