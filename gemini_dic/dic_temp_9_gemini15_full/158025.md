planter:/ˈplæntər/| n.|种植者；花盆；播种机|A planter is a person who plants, or a container used for planting.  It can also refer to a machine used for planting seeds or seedlings.|例句：He is a skilled planter of rice. 他是一位熟练的种稻人。

例句：She bought a beautiful ceramic planter for her orchids. 她为她的兰花买了一个漂亮的花盆。

近义词：cultivator, sower, pot

反义词：harvester (in the context of a person)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plants tomatoes every year. = 他每年都种植西红柿。
She waters the plants in her planter. = 她浇灌花盆里的植物。


一般过去时:
They planted a tree yesterday. = 他们昨天种了一棵树。
He used a planter to sow the seeds. = 他用播种机播种。


现在完成时:
I have planted all the seeds. = 我已经把所有的种子都种上了。
She has just bought a new planter for her balcony. = 她刚为她的阳台买了一个新的花盆。


过去完成时:
They had planted the flowers before the rain started. = 在下雨之前他们已经种好了花。
She had already filled the planter with soil. = 她已经把花盆装满了土。


将来时:
We will plant more trees next spring. = 我们明年春天将种植更多的树木。
He will buy a larger planter for his growing plants. = 他将为越来越多的植物购买一个更大的花盆。


虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger planter, I would plant more flowers. = 如果我有一个更大的花盆，我会种更多的花。
I wish I had planted the seeds earlier. = 我真希望我早点播种。


被动语态:

疑问句:
