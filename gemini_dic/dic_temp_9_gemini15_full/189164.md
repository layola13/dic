sackfuls: /ˈsækfulz/ | n. | 一袋一袋的；大量的 |  A large quantity of something, typically measured in sacks.  It implies a considerable, possibly overflowing, amount.  The word combines "sack" (a bag) and the plural suffix "-fuls," indicating multiple sacks. |


例句：

He carried sackfuls of potatoes from the field. 他从田里扛来了一袋又一袋的土豆。

The children brought sackfuls of leaves for their art project. 孩子们带回了一大堆树叶用于他们的美术项目。


近义词：loads, heaps, piles, quantities


反义词：少量 a small amount, handful


词性: 名词
