morbidly:/ˈmɔːrbɪdli/| adv.|病态地；令人毛骨悚然地；异常兴奋地|In a morbid or gruesome manner;  showing an unhealthy interest in death or disease.  It suggests an excessive or unhealthy fascination with unpleasant or disturbing subjects, especially death and decay.|例句：He morbidly contemplated the details of the accident. 他病态地思考着事故的细节。

例句：She morbidly collected antique medical instruments. 她病态地收集着古董医疗器械。


近义词：gruesomely, gruesomely, macabrely, unhealthily, unwholesomely


反义词：healthily, wholesomely, cheerfully, joyfully


词性: 副词
