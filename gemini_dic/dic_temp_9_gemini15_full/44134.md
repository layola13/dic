cristae:/ˈkrɪstiː/| n.|嵴，褶皱|The cristae are the folds of the inner mitochondrial membrane, increasing the surface area available for ATP synthesis.  They are characteristic of mitochondria and are crucial for cellular respiration. |The cristae of the mitochondria are highly folded.|近义词：fold, ridge, pleat|反义词：smooth surface
